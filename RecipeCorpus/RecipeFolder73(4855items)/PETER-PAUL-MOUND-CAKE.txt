Title: PETER PAUL MOUND CAKE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Box chocolate cake mix
1 cn Chocolate frosting.
20    Marshmallows
1 pk Coconut
1 c  Evaporating milk
1 c  Sugar
Make cake according to directions on box. Heat milk, sugar,
marshmallows. When marshmallows melt, add coconut. Split cake into 4
layers. Space layers with coconut mixture. Frost with chocolate
frosting.
