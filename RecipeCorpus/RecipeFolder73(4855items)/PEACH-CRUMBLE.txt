Title: PEACH CRUMBLE
Categories: Desserts, Fruits, Microwave
Yield: 6 Servings
6 c  Peaches;peeled,thin sliced
1 tb Lemon juice
6 tb Butter
3/4 c  Flour
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Rolled oats
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg;freshly grated
1/2 c  Waltnuts;chopped coarsely
place peache slices in 8-1/2 inch round or 8 inch square microwave
proof dish. Stir in lemon juice and toss lightly. cut butter into
flour in medium mixing bowl until mixture resembles coarse cornmeal.
Stir in sugars, oats, cinnsmon, nutmeg and waltnuts. Sprinkle over
peaches. Microwave on high(100% power) until fruit is tender 12 to 14
minutes rotating dish once. or bake in oven at 350 for 20 minutes
