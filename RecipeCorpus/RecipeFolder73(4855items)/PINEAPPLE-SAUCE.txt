Title: PINEAPPLE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Pineapple juice
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Flour
1 c  Diced pineapple
12    Marshmallows, diced
2    Lemons
Combine flour and sugar.  Add pineapple juice, lemon juice, and grated
lemon rind.  Cook over hot water, stirring constantly, until thick.
Add pineapple and marshmallows. Mix thoroughly. Serve with pudding or
frozen desserts.

Wilma Onstott, Gering, NE.
