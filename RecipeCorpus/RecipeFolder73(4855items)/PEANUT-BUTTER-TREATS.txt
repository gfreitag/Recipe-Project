Title: PEANUT BUTTER TREATS
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 24 Servings
12 oz BUTTERSCOTCH MORSELS
1 c  PEANUT BUTTER
6 c  KELLOGGS RICE KRISPIES
COMBINE MORSELS AND PEANUT BUTTER IN A SAUCE PAN. STIR OVER LOW HEAT
UNTIL SMOOTH. REMOVE FROM HEAT AND ADD CEREAL. STIR WELL TO COAT.
PRESS MIXTURE INTO A BUTTERED 9 X 13" PAN. CHILL TO FIRM MIXTURE. CUT
INTO 2" SQUARES.
