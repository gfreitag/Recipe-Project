Title: PLUM CHUTNEY #1
Categories: Relishes, India
Yield: 5 Servings
3 lb Plums; pitted/quartered
1 lb Apple, granny smith
1    Onion, large; chopped
2    Garlic clove; minced
2 c  Brown sugar
2 c  Vinegar
1 tb Salt
1 tb Ginger, ground
2 tb Cloves, ground
1/2 ts Red pepper
Pit and quarter the plums.  Peel, core, and quarter the apples.
Combine all ingredients in a large Dutch oven; bring to a boil. Cook,
uncov- ered, over medium heat 2 hours or until thickened, stirring
occasionally.

Quickly spoon chutney into hot sterilized jars, leaving 1/4"
head-space; cover at once with metal lids, and screw bands tight.
Process 10 minutes in boiling-water bath.
--- Southern Living magazine
