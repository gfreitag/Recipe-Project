Title: PEAR RELISH
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Peck pears
2 lb Brown sugar
3    Onions, chopped
1 lb Raisins
2 c  Vinegar
Spices
Pare and shop pears.  Combine with other ingredients, adding spices to
taste.  Mix thoroughly.  Cook slowly, stirring frequently, until
thick. Ruby C. Babcock, Battle Creek, MI.
