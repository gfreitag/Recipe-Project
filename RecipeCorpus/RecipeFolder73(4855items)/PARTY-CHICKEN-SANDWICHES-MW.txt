Title: PARTY CHICKEN SANDWICHES-MW
Categories: Appetizers, Miraclewhip
Yield: 3 Dozen
(From Joni's Kitchen:)
1 1/2 c  Chicken; cooked chop fine
1 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
4 oz Can Green Chillies; drain
3/4 c  Sharp Cheddar cheese
-shredded ( 3 oz's)
1/4 c  Onion; chopped fine
36    Slices Party Rye bread or
-pumpernickel slices
Combine chicken, salad dressing,chilies, cheese and onions; mix
lightly. Cover bread with chicken mixture. Broil 5 minutes or until
lightly browned. Serve Hot. Garnish as desired. Makes 3 dozen.
Preparation time: 15 minutes. Broiling time: 5 minutes.

Source: Kraft Miracle Whip Cookbook 1989
