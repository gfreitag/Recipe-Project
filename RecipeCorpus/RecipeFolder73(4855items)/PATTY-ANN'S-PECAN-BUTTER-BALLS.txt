Title: PATTY ANN'S PECAN BUTTER BALLS
Categories: Christmas, Cookies
2 c  Flour -- sifted
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Granulated Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Butter
2 ts Vanilla Extract
2 c  Pecans -- finely chopped

Oven 325=B0 F.

Combine dry ingredients and work in butter and vanilla. Add nuts and
mix well.  Shape into 1" balls. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet while
warm.

Store in tin box between layers of wax paper.zo
