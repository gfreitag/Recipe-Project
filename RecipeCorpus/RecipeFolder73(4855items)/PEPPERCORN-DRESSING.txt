Title: PEPPERCORN DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Finely chopped shallots
2 tb Vinegar
1 1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Sour cream
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Cream or half-and-half
3/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 ts Finely ground pepper
2 1/2 ts Cracked peppercorns
1/4 c  Freshly grated Parmesan
-Cheese
Contributed to the echo by: BOYD NARON PEPPERCORN DRESSING Combine all
ingredients and refrigerate in jar at least 24 hours.

Makes 1 pint.
