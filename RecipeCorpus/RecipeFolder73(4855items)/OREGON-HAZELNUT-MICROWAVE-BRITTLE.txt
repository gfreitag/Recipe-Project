Title: OREGON HAZELNUT MICROWAVE BRITTLE
Categories: Candy, Microwave
Yield: 1 Pound
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  White corn syrup
1 c  Oregon hazelnuts
1 ts Butter
1 ts Vanilla extract
In a 1-1/2 quart casserole dish, stir together sugar and syrup.
Microwave on HIGH for 4 minutes. Stir in the hazelnuts and microwave
on HIGH for 3 to 5 minutes. Add butter and vanilla, blending well.
Microwave on HIGH for 1 to 2 minutes. Pour mixture onto a lightly
buttered warm cookie sheet. Let cool for 1/2 to 1 hour. Break into
small pieces and drizzle with white and milk chocolate. Store in an
airtight container in a cool place.
