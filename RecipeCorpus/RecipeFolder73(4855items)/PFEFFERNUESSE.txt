Title: PFEFFERNUESSE
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Eggs
250 g  Sugar
250 g  All purpose flour
50 g  Cornstarch
1 ts Baking-powder
1 tb Rum
1/2    Grated citron skin
Trace of clove, allspice and
-nutmeg (for each)
1 pn Pepper
1 ts Cinnamon
75 g  Chopped candied lemon-peel
Icing
beat eggs and sugar until fluffy, pour in gradually flour mixed with
cornstarch and baking- powder, rum and the various spices. last knead
in the candied lemon-peel. let rest dough for about one hour then
roll out (approx. 1 cm) and cut out small round biscuits, lay on a
greased baking-sheet. bake in a preheated oven for about 10-15 -
190-200 C i. e. ???. let cool down and cover with icing
