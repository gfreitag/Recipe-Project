Title: PLASTIC BAG ICE CREAM
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Vanilla
1 ea Pint size Zip-lock Bag
-Heavy duty
1 ea Gallon size Zip-lock Bag
-Heavy duty
7 tb Salt (Rock?)
Ice cubes
Put milk, sugar, and vanilla in the pint size bag and close. Put this
bag inside the inside the gallon size bag. Fill large bag with ice
cubes and 6 - 7 tablespoons salt. Close tightly and shake vigorously
for about 5 minutes or until ice cream is done.

From: Theresa Hart, 7/5/96 Press Enterprise

Note: Fruit and/or syrup can be added to the ice cream.
