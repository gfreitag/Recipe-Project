Title: PICKLED VIDALIA & RED ONIONS - MARTHA STEWA
Categories: Pantry, Soup/stews, Pickles
Yield: 1 Half-gallo
3 c  Vinegar
6 tb Kosher salt
6 tb Sugar
2    Vidalia onions, peeled and
-sliced into 1/4-inch-thick
-rings
2    Red onions, sliced into
-1/4-inch-thick rings
1    Garlic shoot (optional)
1. Wash 1 glass half-gallon jar with lid in hot, soapy water and rinse
well.

2. Combine 1/4 C water, vinegar, salt, and sugar in a saucepan and
bring to a boil.

3. Meanwhile, fill jar halfway with either Vidalia or red onion
slices. Add garlic shoot; cover with remaining onion slices, packing
them with the back of a clean spoon. Leave 1/4 inch of space beneath
the rim. Pour hot liquid over onions, covering them by 1/4 inch and
leaving 1/2 inch of space beneath the rim. Place lid on jar and let
stand until cool. Store in the refrigerator; serve within a week.
