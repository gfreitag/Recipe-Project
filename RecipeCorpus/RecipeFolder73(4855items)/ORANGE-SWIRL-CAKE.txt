Title: ORANGE SWIRL CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 pk Orange gelatin powder
1/2 ts Orange extract
Powdered sugar
Prepare cake mix according to package directions. Remove 1 1/2 cups
of the batter and set aside.  Pour remaining batter into a greased
and floured Bundt pan. Stir the gelatin (dry) and extract into the
reserved batter. Drop orange batter by generous tablespoonfuls
randomly in 6 to 8 moundsonto batter in pan.  Cut through batter with
knife in S-shaped curves in one continuous motion for swirled effect.
Bake 45 minutes. Cool 10 minutes, then remove from pan.  Cool
completely and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
