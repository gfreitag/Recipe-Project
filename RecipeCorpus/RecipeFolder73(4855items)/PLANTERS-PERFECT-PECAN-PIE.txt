Title: PLANTERS PERFECT PECAN PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
3 ea EGGS
1 c  LIGHT CORN SYRUP
1 c  SUGAR
2 tb MARGARINE
1/8 ts SALT
1 ts VANILLA
1 c  PECAN HALVES, CHOPPED
1 ea 9" UNBAKED PIE SHELL
IN A MEDIUM BOWL, BEAT EGGS SLIGHTLY, STIR IN CORN SYRUP, MARGARINE,
VANILLA AND SALT. STIR IN PECAN HALVES. POUR INTO PIE SHELL. THEN:
BAKE AT 3500F. FOR 25 TO 30 MINUTES MORE OR UNTIL CRUST IS GOLDEN
