Title: OREO SPEEDWAGON
Categories: Alcohol, Desserts, Ice cream, Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
2 oz Kahlua
2    Scoops Vanilla ice cream
3    Oreo Cookies
Combine 2 oz. of Kahlua, 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream, and 3 crushed
oreo cookies. Blend until smooth. This drink was invented in Fairmont
B.C. Chef, Pierre

Recipe by: Warren Hall (whall@eagle.wbm.ca)
