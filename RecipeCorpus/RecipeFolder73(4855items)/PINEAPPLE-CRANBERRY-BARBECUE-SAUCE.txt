Title: PINEAPPLE CRANBERRY BARBECUE SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 2 Cups
1 ea 12 oz jar of pineapple
-preserves
1/2 c  Cranberry sauce, whole,
-canned
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/3 c  Vinegar
Combine all ingredients. Use to baste pork, poultry or sweet potatoes
last 15 minutes of grilling. Heat and pass remainder.
