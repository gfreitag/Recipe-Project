Title: PIE CRUST (STAR 6/13)
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  FLOUR
1 3/4 c  SHORTENING
1 tb SUGAR
1 ts SALT
1 tb VINEGAR
1 ea EGG
1/2 c  WATER
MIX THE FLOUR, SHORTENING, SUGAR AND SALT WITH A FORK. IN A SMALL
BOWL MIX THE VINEGAR, EGG AND WATER. ADD TO THE FLOUR MIXTURE WITH A
FORK UNTIL MOISTENED. MOLD INTO A BALL AND CHILL. ALLOW TO CHILL FOR
AT LEAST 15 MINUTES BEFORE USING AND KEEP REMAINING IN THE
REFRIGERATOR FOR UP TO 2 WEEKS.
