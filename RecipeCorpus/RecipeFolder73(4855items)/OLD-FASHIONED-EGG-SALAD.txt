Title: OLD-FASHIONED EGG SALAD
Categories: Salads, Sandwiches, Eggs
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
Yield: 6 Servings
2 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Dried minced onion
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
6    Hard-cooked eggs -- chopped
1/2 c  Finely chopped celery
Lettuce leaves or bread
:

In a bowl, combine mayonnaise, lemon juice, onion, salt and pepper.
Stir in eggs and celery.  Cover and chill. For each serving, spoon
about 1/2 cup onto a lettuce leaf or spread on bread.
