Title: PEAR CONSERVES
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
5 c  Pared, chopped pears
2    Lemons
5 c  Sugar
1    Orange
2 c  Raisins
Remove seeds from lemons and orange.  Grind pulp and rind using the
coarse knife of food chopper.  Combine fruits and sugar. Cook slowly,
stirring frequently, until thick. Mrs. Leroy Ellis, Clarksville, OH.
