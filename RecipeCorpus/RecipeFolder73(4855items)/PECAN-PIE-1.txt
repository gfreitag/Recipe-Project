Title: PECAN PIE 1
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 ea Butter; stick
3 ea Egg
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Karo; white
3 tb Corn meal
1 ts Vanilla
1 ea Salt; pinch
1 c  Pecans; chopped
1 ea Pie shell
per serving: 250 Fat grams    per serving: 30 Approx.
Calories
Cook Time: 1:30 Cholesterol  per serving: 20 Add all ingredients
together in bowl, and mix well. Pour into pie shell, and bake 1 hour
at 300 degrees. Allow to cool and serve.
