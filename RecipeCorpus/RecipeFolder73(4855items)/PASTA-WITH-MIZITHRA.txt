Title: PASTA WITH MIZITHRA
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
4 x  Garlic cloves, chopped fine
1 lb Dry pasta, cooked/drained
1/2 c  Parmesan, freshly grated
1/2 c  Mizithra cheese,fresh grated
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 c  Parsley, chopped for garnish
Bring the water for the pasta to boil and prepare everything else.
It's important to never overcook pasta. Heat the olive oil in a small
frying pan and add the chopped garlic. Saute for just a moment and
set aside. Boil the pasta until just barely tender. Drain and toss
with all ingredients. Serve as a side dish in the place of another
starch.
