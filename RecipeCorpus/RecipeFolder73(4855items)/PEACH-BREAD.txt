Title: PEACH BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 1/2 c  Fresh peaches, peeled and
-sliced
1/2 c  Unsweetened applesauce
1 tb Apple juice concentrate,
-thawed
1 tb Molasses
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Ground cardamom
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Rolled oats
1 1/2 c  Whole wheat bread flour
2 ts Active dry yeast
1/2 c  Chopped dried peaches
You can substitute one 16-ounce can sliced peaches for the fresh
peaches and applesauce.
