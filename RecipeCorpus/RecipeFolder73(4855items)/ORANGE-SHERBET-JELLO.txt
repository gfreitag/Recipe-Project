Title: ORANGE SHERBET JELLO
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lg Package of orange Jello
Boiling water
1 pt Orange sherbet
Colored marshmallows
1 sm Can of pineapple tidbits
1 sm Bottle of 7 UP
1 sm Can mandarin oranges, drain
Prepare Jello, using juice from pineapple plus boiling water to make
1 cup of liquid for Jello.  Add orange sherbet as well as 7 UP and
oranges. Top with marshmallows and put in the refrigerator to set.
Randy Rigg
