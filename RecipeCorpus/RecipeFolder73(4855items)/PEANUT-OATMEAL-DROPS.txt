Title: PEANUT-OATMEAL DROPS
Categories: Cookies, Travel, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Peanut Butter
3/4 c  Brown Sugar; Packed
1/2 c  Sugar; Granulated
3 ea Eggs; Large
1/4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Flour; Unbleached
1 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Slat
1 c  Oats, Quick-cooking Rolled
1 c  Peanuts; Chopped
Cream together shortening, peanut butter, and sugars until light and
fluffy; add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each. Blend in
milk and vanilla. Thoroughly stir together flour, soda, and salt;
stir into creamed mixture.  Stir in oats and peanuts.  Drop from
teaspoon 2 inches apart on greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees
f. for 10 to 12 minutes.

Makes 6 dozen.
