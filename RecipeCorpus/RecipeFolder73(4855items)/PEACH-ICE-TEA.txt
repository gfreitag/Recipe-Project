Title: PEACH ICE TEA
Categories: Diabetic, Beverages
Yield: 4 Nice folks
4    Regular tea bags;
3 ts Peach syrup; such as Monin
-=OR=-
3 ts Mango syrup; DaVinci or
-Torani
Sugar to taste
(sugar sub)
Brew tea.  Let cool.  Add flavoring.  Add ice. Sweeten to taste.
