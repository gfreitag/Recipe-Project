Title: PARTY PARSNIPS & CARROTS
Categories: Cooking liv, Vegetables
1 lb each carrots and parsnips,
peeled and cut
into 3 x 1/4 x 1/4-inch
sticks
Yield: 4 Servings
:
:
:
1 ts olive oil

Preheat oven to 400 degree F. Spread vegetable sticks in single layer
on nonstick baking sheet. Sprinkle vegetables with olive oil. Toss
until well coated.

Bake, tossing vegetables frequently, 1 to 1 1/2 hours or until crisp
and golden.

Yield: 6 serving
