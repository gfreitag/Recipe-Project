Title: ORANGE ARUGULA SALAD WITH VANILLA-INFUSED OIL
Categories: New imports, Salads
2 c  washed arugula leaves
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  thinly-sliced red onion
1    orange
1 ts white wine vinegar
1 TB vanilla oil
Salt and pepper
:

In a salad bowl toss arugula and red onion. Segment orange over a
small bowl to catch juice. Add vinegar and vanilla oil to orange
juice and season with salt and pepper. Pour dressing over greens and
toss. Garnish with orange segments.

Yield: 1 servin
