Title: PICKLED HERRING
Categories: Christmas, Seafood, Ethnic, Holiday, Polish
Yield: 6 Servings
Herring fillets
Onions, sliced
1    Bay leaf
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 ts Sugar
1/2    To 3/4 cup vinegar
1 c  Cold water
Soak herring for 3 or 4 hours in cold water. Wash in cold water and
cut into cubes. Prepare a marinade with the remaining ingredients and
marinate for 2 days. Drain and serve.
