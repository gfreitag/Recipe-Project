Title: PEANUT BUTTER COOKIE POPS (WILTON)
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 ea Egg
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
Wilton dark or light cocoa
Flavored candy melts
preheat oven to 375 degrees

Thoroughly cream butter, peanut butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla.
Toss together dry ingredients; blend into creamed mixture. Shape
dough into ball. Roll dough approximately 1/8 inch thick. Dip cutters
into flour before each use. Bake 10-12 minutes. Cool. Dip in
chocolate or or make peanut butter chocolate sandwiches. Makes 4 dozen

Place one or two wafers of candy melts on warm cookie. Sandwich two
cookies together on lollipop stick.
