Title: OYSTERS BINGO (FRIED OYSTERS)
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
1 oz Butter or Vegetable oil
6    Fresh Oysters
1 c  Flour ( seasoned with salt
-and pepper)
1 tb Oyster Juice
1 tb White Wine
1 tb Shallots
1 tb Real Lemon Juice
Oysters Bingo from the Ships Cabin Seafood Restaurant in Norfolk,
Virginia

Lightly  bread 6 oysters and saute on one side until golden brown,
then saute the other side until same color appears. Remove oysters
from pan. Turn  off heat and add the wine, oyster juice, lemon juice,
and shallots to the butter that the oysters were sauted in. This will
make a creamy sauce to pour over the oysters.
