Title: OPRAH'S MOCK CAESAR SALAD (LACTO)
Categories: Salads, Rosie, Prodigy, Dec.
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Garlic cloves; minced
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Low-sodium soy sauce
Fresh cracked black pepper
3 c  Romaine lettuce; torn
3 c  Endive; torn
1 lg Tomato; chunked
1 tb Parmesan cheese
Mix garlic, lemon juice, soy sauce and pepper for the dressing. Toss
with lettuces and tomatoes.
