Title: ORANGE-CINNAMON RICE
Categories: New text im, Cooking rig
1 TB olive oil or unsalted
butter
Yield: 6 Servings
:
1/3 c  onion -- finely chopped
1 c  basmati or other long-grain
rice
:
2 ts grated orange zest
1    4-inch piece cinnamon stick
1 1/4 c  water
1/2 c  fresh orange juice
1/2 ts salt

In a saucepan, heat the oil or butter and saute the onions over
medium heat until soft but not brown. Add the rice and saute 2
minutes longer, stirring occasionally. Add the remaining ingredients
and bring to a boil, stirring once or twice. Reduce the heat to a
simmer, cover, and cook 15 minutes or until all the liquid is
absorbed. Remove the cinnamon stick. Let the rice stand 5 minutes,
partially covered, before serving.
