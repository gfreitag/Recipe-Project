Title: PEANUT SAUCE (RICE)
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Creamy peanut butter (Don't
-use old fashioned "home
-style" or fresh ground)
1/2 c  Of hot water
3 tb Reduced-sodium soy sauce
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 tb Dark molasses
1 ts Garlic Powder Granules
1/2 ts Red Pepper Flakes
In small bowl, whisk peanut butter into water. Add remaining i
ingredients and whisk until smooth. Cover and refrigerate until
serving time. Bring to room temperature before serving

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
