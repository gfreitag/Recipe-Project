Title: PASTA WITH WHITE SAUCE
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 3 Servings
1 c  EDENsoy Creamy White Sauce
1 pk EDEN Organic Pasta
-- (any variety)
2 tb EDEN Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1    Red pepper; sliced into
- thin long pieces
1 tb Oregano or basil
-- fresh or dried
Cook pasta according to Package directions. Drain. Toss with olive
oil. Add peppers and herbs to white sauce recipe while it is
simmering. Turn off heat, mix in pasta and serve.

Prep Time: 15-20 minutes * Cooking Time: 10 minutes * Yield: 3-4
servings
