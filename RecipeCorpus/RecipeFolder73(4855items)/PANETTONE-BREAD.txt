Title: PANETTONE BREAD
Categories: Bh&g, Fruits, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 pound loaf
3/4 c  Milk
1    Egg
3 tb Applesauce
3 c  Bread flour
2 tb Honey
1 1/2 ts Anise seed, crushed
3/4 ts Salt
1 ts Yeast
1/3 c  Light raisins
1/3 c  Currants
1/3 c  Chopped candied citron
Use light setting.  103 cal.
