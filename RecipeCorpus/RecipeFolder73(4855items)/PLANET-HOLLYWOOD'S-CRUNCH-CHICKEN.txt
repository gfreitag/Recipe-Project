Title: PLANET HOLLYWOOD'S CRUNCH CHICKEN
Categories: Chicken, Dkuhnen msn
Yield: 4 Servings
5 oz Cap'n Crunch Cereal
4 oz Corn flakes
1/4 c  Flour
1/4 ts Onion powder
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Black pepper
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Milk
24    1-0z chicken tenderloins or
Chicken breasts cut into
Fingers
Place cereal in a food processor.  Mix well until crumbs are formed.
(There should be some chunks of cereal in the mixture.) Transfer to a
mixing bowl. Combine flour, onion and garlic powder and pepper in a
second mixing bowl. Combine eggs and milk in a third mixing bowl.
Place chicken in seasoned flour, one at a time, dip and coat evenly
in egg wash, then coat evenly with Crunch mixture.  Using a candy
thermometer, heat oil in large frying pan until temperature reaches
325f. Fry tenders for 3 1/2 minutes or until golden brown.  Remove
and drain on paper towels. Source: TV Guide, December 30th, 1995.
