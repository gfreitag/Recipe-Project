Title: PARTY CHEESE BALL
Categories: Appetizers, Spreads
Yield: 2 Cups
2 pk (8oz each) Cream cheese,
Softened
2 c  Shredded Sharp Natural
Cheddar Cheese
1 tb Chopped pimento
1 tb Chopped green pepper
1 tb Finely chopped onion
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ds Ground red pepper
1 ds Salt
Chopped pecans
Combine cream cheese and cheddar cheese, mixing at medium speed on
electric mixer until well blended. Add all remaining ingredients
except pecans; mix well. Chill several hours. Shape into ball; roll
in pecans. Serve with crackers. Makes approximately 2 cups.
