Title: PACIFIC RIM GREEN BEANS
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Green beans, cut in half
2 tb Oil or margarine
1    Garlic clove, finely chopped
1 sm Red onion, finely chopped
2 tb Red bell pepper, chopped
1    Serrano chile
;seeded and minced
1 ts Fresh ginger, grated
3/4 c  Chicken broth
2 ts Soy sauce
In a medium saucepan, heat oil or margarine.  Add garlic, onion, red
pepper, chile and ginger; saute until softened. Add string beans,
tossing until coated. Stir in chicken broth and soy sauce. Cover and
cook on moderate heat just until beans are tender-crisp.

From 1993 "Shepherd's Garden Seeds Catalog," pg. 6. Posted by Cathy
Harned.
