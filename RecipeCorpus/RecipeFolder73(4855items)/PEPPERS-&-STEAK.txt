Title: PEPPERS & STEAK
Categories: Crockpot, Main dish, Low-cal, Beef
Yield: 6 Servings
- kxmt69b
2    Green peppers, chopped
1 c  Beef bouillon
1/2 ts Ground ginger
2 lb Lean round steak
2 tb Minced dried onion
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 ts Garlic powder
MMMMM-------------------NUTRI INFO PER SERVING------------------------
106 x  *Calories
Cut steak into serving size portions.  Place half the steak in
Crockpot. Arrange green peppers on steak.
Place remaining steak on top.  Mix remaining ingredients and pour
over meat.  cover and cook on Low setting for 8 to 10 hours, or high
for 4 to 5 hours.
Each serving:  186 calories.
