Title: PASTA W/TUNA & FRESH BASIL
Categories: Fish, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Tuna -- ( 7 1/2) undrained
4 c  Cooked ziti (about 1 lb)
1/2 c  Diced sweet red pepper
1/4 c  Chopped fresh basil
Lemon juice to taste
Combine all ingredients together in a medium bowl and toss well.
Chill at least 1 hour before serving. Serves 4 as a main course.
