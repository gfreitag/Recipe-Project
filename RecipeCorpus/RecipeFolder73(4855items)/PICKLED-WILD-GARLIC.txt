Title: PICKLED WILD GARLIC
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
Wild garlic
1 part vinegar
1 part water
1/2 tsp. sieved alum
wash garlic well and cut off the green part which can be used in
soups. Pack white parts in mason jars and to each add 1/2 tsp. alum
and the one part vinegar and one part water. Seal and refrigerate and
these are ready after two to three weeks to make your meals taste
like a feast. They will keep like this all winter.
