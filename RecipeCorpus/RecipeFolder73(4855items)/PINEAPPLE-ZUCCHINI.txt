Title: PINEAPPLE ZUCCHINI
Categories: Bread, Size
Yield: 12 Servings
3    Egg whites -- whipped
1 c  Applesauce
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Zucchini -- shredded
8 oz Can pineapple -- crushed
-drained
3 c  Unbleached flour
2 c  Granulated sugar
2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Raisins
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour. In
a mixing bowl, combine egg whites, applesauce, vanilla, zucchini , and
pineapple. In another mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking soda,
salt, baking powder, cinnamon, and raisins. Mix wet ingredients with
dry ingredients just until moistened. Pour into prepared baking
pan(s). Bake for
1    hour depending on pan size.
