Title: ORANGE MARMALADE SAUCE
Categories: Fish, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Orange marmalade
-juice of 1 large sweet
-orange
-pinch of ground ginger
1 ts Prepared horseradish
Put the above ingredients in the container of a food processor or
blender and blend well for about 10 seconds. From the files of Al
Rice, North Pole Alaska.
Feb 1994
