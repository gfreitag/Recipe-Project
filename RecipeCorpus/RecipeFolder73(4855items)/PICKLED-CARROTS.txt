Title: PICKLED CARROTS
Categories: Pickles
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Vinegar
2 c  Water
1 tb Whole mixed spices
Small carrots
Salt
Scrape and wash carrots.  Boil until tender in water to which 1/2
teaspoon salt has been added per quart. Pack in sterilized jars. Fill
jars to within 1/4 inch of top with sirup made by boiling together
vinegar, water, sugar, and spices. Mrs. Harry R. Baer, Meyersdale, PA.
