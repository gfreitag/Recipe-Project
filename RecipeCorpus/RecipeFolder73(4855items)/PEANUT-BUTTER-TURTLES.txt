Title: PEANUT BUTTER TURTLES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
Apple
2 tb Peanut Butter
5 x  Grapes
Slice an apple in half. Make several slits in each half. Fill with
peanut butter or sesame butter (available in health stores.) Attach
seedless grapes with toothpicks (remove before giving tio very young
children) for the head and legs and stick a carrot shaving on for a
tail (tuck it in 1 of the slits) Nice to serve as a lunch treat.
