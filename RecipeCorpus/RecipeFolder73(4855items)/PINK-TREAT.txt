Title: PINK TREAT
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 50 Servings
3    (4/5 quart) bottles of
Rose, chilled
(use your favorite
Inexpensive
Wine)
1    (6 oz can frozen lemonade
Concentrate
2 qt Raspberry sherbert
1    (4/5 quart) bottle pink
Champagne or
Sparkling burgundy
Ice
Pour into a punch bowl the rose (or your favorite wine), and lemonade
concentrate. Stir in raspberry sherbet. Mix thoroughly. Pour in
sparkling wine. Add ice and serve immediately.
