Title: ORANGE - HONEY - MUSTARD SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Orange juice concentrate
- thawed
1/2 tb Wine vinegar
1/2 tb Honey
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/8 ts Salt
1/3 c  Safflower or sunflower oil
Combine ingredients and whisk until emulsified. Makes
1/2    cup, enough for 4. For Creamy Orange Honey Mustard
Dressing add 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 1/4 cup yogurt and 1
tablespoon mayonnaise to **1/4** cup of above dressing. Toss with
DRY, mixed, chilled 4 to 6 servings.
