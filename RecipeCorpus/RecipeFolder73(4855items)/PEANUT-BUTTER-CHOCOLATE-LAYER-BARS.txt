Title: PEANUT BUTTER CHOCOLATE LAYER BARS
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts, Kathy's
Yield: 24 Bars
2 c  Peanutbutter sandwich cookie
Crumbs(about 20 cookies)
3 tb Butter
1 1/4 c  Peanuts, chopped
1 c  Semi-sweet choc.morsels
(about 6 oz.)
1 c  Coconut, flaked
1 1/4 c  Sweetened condensed milk
combine crumbs and butter in small bowl. Press onto bottom of greased
13 x 9 inch baking pan. Layer peanuts, morsels, and coconut over
crumb mixture. drizzle sweeten condenced milk evenly over top. Bake
in preheated 350 degrees oven for 20 to 25 min. or until coconut is
golden brown. cool completely on wire rack.
