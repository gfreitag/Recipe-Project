Title: PEAR & GINGER JAM
Categories: Condiments, Fruits
Yield: 1 Serving
2 lb Pears
4 oz (1/2 cup) preserved ginger
2 lb (5 1/3 cups) sugar
1 1/4 c  Water
1 oz (1") fresh ginger
Juice of 2 lemons
Makes 2 lbs Peel, core and dice the pears. Cut the preserved ginger
nto small chunks. Put all the ingredients into a preserving kettle
and stir over a gentle hea t until the sugar has dissolved. Bring to
a boil and boil rapidly for abou t 10 minutes, stirring occasionally,
or until setting point is reached. Rem ove the piece of fresh ginger,
lift out the fruit with a slotted spoon and place in hot clean jars.
Rapidly boil the syrup to reduce for a few minute s, then pour over
the fruit to cover. Cover and process, then complete sea ls and cool.
