Title: PIZZA CUPS
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Hot or mild pork sausage
1    Jar (14 ounces) pizza
-sauce
2 tb Ketchup
1/4 ts Garlic powder
2 cn (10 ounces each)
-refrigerated biscuits
-Shredded mozzarella
-cheese
-Grated Parmesan cheese
In a skillet, cook sausage over mediun heat; drain. Stir in pizza
sauce, ketch up and garlic powder; set aside. Press biscuits into 20
well-greased muffin . cups. Spoon 1 or 2 tablespoons of the meat
sauce into each biscuit; top with mozzarella cheese and sprinkle with
Parmesan cheese. Bake at 350! for 10-15 minutes or until golden
brown. (Refrigerate or freeze any remairling meat sauce.)

Yield: 20 pizzas.
