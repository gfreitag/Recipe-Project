Title: PINEAPPLE VINAIGRETTE (BON APPETIT-JAN 94)
Categories: Sauces, Preserves
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Chopped Fresh Pineapple
1 c  Chopped Yellow Bell Pepper
1/3 c  Rice Vinegar
1 tb Minced Seeded Jalapeno
Chili
1/2 tb Curry Powder
1/2 tb Ground Pepper
1 tb Vegetable Oil
Puree pineapple in processor until smooth.  Add bell pepper, vinegar,
chili, curry powder and ground pepper; process until smooth. With
machine running, gradually add oil.
