Title: PEAR WALDORF SALAD
Categories: Fruits, Misc
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Pears, coarsely chopped
-(about 2 cups)
2 md Stalks celery, chopped
-(about 1 cup)
1/2 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/3 c  Coarsely chopped nuts
Toss all ingredients. Serve on salad greens if desired. 4 SERVINGS;
360 CALORIES PER SERVING.
