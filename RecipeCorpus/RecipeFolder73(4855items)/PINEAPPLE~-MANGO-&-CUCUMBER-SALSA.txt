Title: PINEAPPLE~ MANGO & CUCUMBER SALSA
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 Recipe
-JUDI M. PHELPS
1/2 c  Pineapple; diced in
-small pieces
1/2 c  Firm ripe mango
1/2 c  Cucumber; diced & peeled
1/3 c  Red bell pepper; diced
1/3 c  Tomato; diced
3 tb Green onion or chives,
-finely chopped
3 tb Cilantro or
1 ts Mint
Juice of 2 limes
Hawaiian chili pepper or
Jalapeno; to taste
Salt to taste
Cut all ingredients in small diced; mix well, adjust seasoning. Chill
to marinate and marry flavors.  Good with white fish.
