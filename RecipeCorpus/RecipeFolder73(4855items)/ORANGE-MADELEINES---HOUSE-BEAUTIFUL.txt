Title: ORANGE MADELEINES - HOUSE BEAUTIFUL
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 60 Madeleines
Cooking oil spray
1 c  Whole wheat pastry flour
1 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking soda
2 c  Orange juice
2 ts Orange zest
4 tb Unsalted butter, room temp.
1 c  Sugar
3 md Eggs, room temperature
Spray 60 Madeleine molds with cooking spray.

In a medium mixing bowl, mix flours and baking soda together and set
aside.

In a medium saucepan bring orange juice to a boil, remove pan from
heat and allow to cool. In a small bowl mix orange juice and zest
together and set aside.

In a large mixing bowl cream the butter and sugar together. Beat in
eggs one at a time. Beat in flour mixture in thirds alternately with
the orange-juice mixture.

Spoon 1 T  batter into each prepared Madeleine mold, and bake in a
preheated 350'F oven for 10 to 15 minutes. Makes 60.
