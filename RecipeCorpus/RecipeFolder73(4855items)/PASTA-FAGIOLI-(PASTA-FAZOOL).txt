Title: PASTA FAGIOLI (PASTA FAZOOL)
Categories: Pasta, Soups, Italian
Yield: 4 Servings
Beans, kidney, blackeye
Onions
Garlic
Black pepper
Ham pieces
1 md Can tomatoes
Ditilini pasta
Saute onions, garlic, ham pieces in peanut oil; add beans and
simmer 1/2 hour to 45 minutes. oil ditilini pasta, add the fazool and
enjoy. Serve with garlic bread.
