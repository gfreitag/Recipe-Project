Title: PIZZA GREEN BEANS
Categories: Diabetic, Vegetables, Side dish, Beans
Yield: 5 Servings
2 c  Green Beans;
1 tb Lemon juice;
1/4 ts Oregano;
1 ts Pimiento; chopped
ds Garlic powder;
ds Salt;
Cook green beans in boiling water until tender; drain. Combine lemon
juice, oregano, pimiento, garlic powder and salt. Pour over beans;
toss Food Exchange per serving: 1 VEGETABLE EXCHANGE CAL; 32

Source: The Complete Diabetic Cookbook by Mary Jane Finsand. Brought
