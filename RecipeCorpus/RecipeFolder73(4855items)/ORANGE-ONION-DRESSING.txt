Title: ORANGE-ONION DRESSING
Categories: Fruits, Misc, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 ts Grated onion
1 tb Honey or sugar
1 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Shake all ingredients in tightly covered container. ABOUT 1 CUP
DRESSING; 70  CALORIES PER TABLESPOON.
