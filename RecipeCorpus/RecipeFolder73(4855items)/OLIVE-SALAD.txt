Title: OLIVE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Appetizers
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  Black olives
1 c  Green olives
1    Lemon; juice of
2 tb Parsley; chopped
1 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Chili powder
1    Garlic clove; crushed
1/2 ts Cumin, ground
2 tb Oil
In a bowl, mix the oil with the cumin, chili powder, paprika,
garlic and lemon juice. Then pour over the dressing over the olives
in the bowl and stir around so that they are well coated.
Put in the salad in the fridge to chill before serving. SERVES: 2-4
