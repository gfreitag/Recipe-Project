Title: PEANUT BUTTER CHEWS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Corn syrup
Bring to a rolling boil.
-Remove from heat and add:
2 tb Butter
1 c  Peanut butter
3 c  Corn flakes
Mix all ingedients and drop by teaspoonfulls on waxed paper. Work
Quickly before it cools.

From: Lancaster Farming Shared By: Pat Stockett
