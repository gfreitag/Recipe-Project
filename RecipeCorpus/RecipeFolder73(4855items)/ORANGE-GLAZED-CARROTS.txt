Title: ORANGE GLAZED CARROTS
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 4 Servings
6 md Carrots, peeled and
-thinly sliced
3 tb Dark brown sugar,
-firmly packed
3 tb Butter or margarine
3 tb Orange juice
1 ts Lemon peel
1/4 ts Salt
1. Combine all ingredients in a deep, 1-quart, heat-resistant,
non-metallic casserole. 2. Heat, covered, in Microwave Oven 6 to 8
minutes or until carrots are tender. Stir occasionally so that
carrots are well glazed.
