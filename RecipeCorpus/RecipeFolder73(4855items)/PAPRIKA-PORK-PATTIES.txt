Title: PAPRIKA PORK PATTIES
Categories: Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
6 ea Servings
6 lg Garlic cloves
1/4 lb Bacon, diced
6 tb Ice water
4 ts Sweet Hungarian paprika
1 ts Hot Hungarian paprika
3/4 ts Coarse salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Ground allspice
1 lb Ground pork
1/2 c  Chopped drained sauerkraut
With processor running, drop garlic through feed tube and mince. Add
next 7 ingredients and process until thick paste forms. Add pork and
combine, using on/off turns Add sauerkraut and just mix in. With
moistened hands, shape sausage into six 3/4-inch-thick patties;
arrange on plate. Heat large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add
sausage patties and cook until brown and just cooked through, about 6
minutes per side. PAPRIKA PORK PATTIES
