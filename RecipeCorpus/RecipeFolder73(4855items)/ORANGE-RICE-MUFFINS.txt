Title: ORANGE RICE MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins, Brunch, Grains, Quick
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Mashed banana
1/2 c  Orange juice concentrate
2 tb Oil
1 1/4 c  Rice flour
1 1/2 ts Cornstarch-free baking powde
In large bowl mix, bananas, orange juice concentrate, and oil. Add
flour and baking powder. Blend well. Fill six 2 1/2 inch nonstick
muffin cups 2/3 full. Bake at 375 for 20-25 minutes. Turn out onto a
wire rack to cool. Variations: add 1/2 cup raisins, 1/2 cup sliced
almonds, 1/2 cup chopped dates (no sugar), or 1/2 cup well drained
crushed pineapple. For apple rice muffins, substitute apple juice
concentrate for orange juice concentrate.
