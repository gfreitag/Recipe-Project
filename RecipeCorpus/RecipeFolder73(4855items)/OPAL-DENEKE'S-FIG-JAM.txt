Title: OPAL DENEKE'S FIG JAM
Categories: Jams
Yield: 1 Recipe
6 1/2 lb Ripe figs (See note #1)
1 cn #2 1/2 crushed pineapple
4 1/2 lb Sugar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Cinnamon
1 tb Cloves
1 tb Allspice
NOTE #1:  Weigh figs after peeling, removing only the peeling that .
easily pulls off with the stem. Mash peeled figs to fine pulp, add
other ingredients. Mix thoroughly and cook until thick, stirring
constantly. Pour into hot sterile jars and seal.
