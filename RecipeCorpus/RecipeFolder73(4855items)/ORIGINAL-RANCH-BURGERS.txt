Title: ORIGINAL RANCH BURGERS
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Kathy's
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk (1 oz) Hidden Valley Ranch
Milk recipe Original Ranch
Salad dressing
1 lb Ground beef
Combine dressing and ground beef, mix well. form into 4 patties and
grill or broil until done.
