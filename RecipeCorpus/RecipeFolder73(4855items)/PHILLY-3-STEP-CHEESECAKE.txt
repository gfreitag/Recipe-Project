Title: PHILLY 3-STEP CHEESECAKE
Categories: Cyberealm, Mom's best, Cheesecakes
Yield: 8 Servings
2    8 oz packages Philadelphia
Cream cheese, softened
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
1    Keebler ready crust graham
Cracker pie crust
1. Mix cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla at medium speed with elec-
tric mixer until well blended. Add eggs, mix until well blended.

2. Pour into crust

3. Bake at 350F 40 minutes or until center is almost set. Cool.
Refrigerate 3 hours or overnight. Top with fresh fruit; drizzle with
2 Tb. melted strawberry jelly.
