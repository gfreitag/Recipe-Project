Title: PARM-A-CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  PARMESAN CHEESE
1/4 c  FLOUR
1 ts PAPRIKA
1/2 ts SALT
1 ds PEPPER
1 ea EGG, SLIGHTLY BEATEN
1 tb MILK
4 ea CHICKEN BREASTS, CLEANED
1/4 c  MARGARINE OR BUTTER
COMBINE CHEESE, FLOUR, PAPRIKA, SALT AND PEPPER. IN ANOTHER DISH
COMBINE THE EGG AND MILK. DIP EACH CHICKEN PIECE IN THE MILK AND THEN
IN THE CRUMB MIXTURE. PLACE IN A BAKING DISH AND POUR MARGARINE ALL
OVER THE PIECES. BAKE AT 350 DEG F FOR ABOUT 1 HOUR. IF DESIRED,
SRINKLE ADDITIONAL CHEESE OVER THE CHICKEN PIECES BEFORE SERVING.
