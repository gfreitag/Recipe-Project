Title: PICKLED VEGETABLES
Categories: Salads, Thai, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Boiling water
1/4 c  White vinegar
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Cauliflower, coarsely chop
1 c  Cabbage, shredded
1 c  Carrot, shredded
2 ea Serrano chilies, stemmed &
-- thinly sliced lengthwise
Combine water, vinegar, sugar & salt.  Stir until the sugar is
dissolved. Let the mixture cool to room temperature. Add the
vegetables to the liquid & let them marinate, covered, for 1 day at
room temperature, or 2 to 3 days in the refrigerator.
