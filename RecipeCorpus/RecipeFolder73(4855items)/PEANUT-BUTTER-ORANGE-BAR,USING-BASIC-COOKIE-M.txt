Title: PEANUT BUTTER ORANGE BAR/USING BASIC COOKIE M
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 16 Servings
2 c  Basic cookie mix
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 ts Orange peel -- grated
1 c  Peanut butter chips --
Reese's
1 c  Confectioners' sugar
1 1/2 ts Orange juice
Combine cookie mix, sour cream and orange peel in a small bowl. Stir
in peanut butter chips.  Spread in a greased 9-inch square baking
pan. Bake at 350 F. for 20 to 25 minutes. Combine confectioners'
sugar and orange juice; drizzle over cooled mixture.  Cut into bars.
Yield: 16
