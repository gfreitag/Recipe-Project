Title: PLAY DOUGH #4
Categories: Kids, Crafts, Play dough
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Salt
6 ts Cream of tartar
3 c  Water
3 tb Oil
Food coloring
Combine ingredients and cook over low heat until it comes away from
the pan and appears like the stuff we know and love!
