Title: ORANGE OAT CREAM
Categories: Grains, Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 1 Batch
1 c  Steel-cut oats
1 c  Whipping cream
1 tb Sour cream
2 tb Honey
1/4 c  Frozen undiluted orange
-juice
1 ts Grated orange peel
Put oats on a cookie sheet and toast at 350 F. for 20 minutes. Remove
from oven and leave to cool.  Whip cream until it stands in soft
peaks. Stir in all remaining ingredients. Refrigerate. Use as a
topping for pies, desserts and cakes.

On back of 24 oz. bag of Arrowhead Mills brand steel-cut oats.
