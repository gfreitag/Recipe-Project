Title: ORANGE DREAM CAKE
Categories: Box cakes, Bundt
Yield: 10 Servings
3/4 c  Miracle Whip
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 pk Dream Whip
3/4 c  Orange juice
3    Eggs
2 ts Grated orange peel
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
2 tb Milk
Preheat oven to 350~. Beat miracle whip, cake mix, whipped topping,
juice, eggs and peel at medium speed with electric mixer for 2
minutes. Pour into a greased and floured 10" bundt pan. Bake 35-40
minutes or until wooden pick inserted near center comes out clean.
Cool 10 minutes; remove from pan. Cool completely. Stir together
powdered sugar and milk until smooth. Drizzle over cake.
