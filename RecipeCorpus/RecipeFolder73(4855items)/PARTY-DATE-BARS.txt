Title: PARTY DATE BARS
Categories: Cookies, Bar
Yield: 24 Bars
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
2 c  Pitted dates
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Orange; grated peel
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Chopped nutmeats
MMMMM-------------------------CRUMB BASE------------------------------
1 c  Sifted all-purpose flour
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Quaker Oats, uncooked
-- (quick or old-fashioned)
1 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
1/2 c  Butter or margarine; melted
For filling, combine dates, sugar, orange peel and orange juice in
medium-sized saucepan.  Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally
until thickened.  Cool. Stir in nutmeats.

For base, sift together flour, soda and salt.  Add oats and sugar; mix
well.  Stir in butter; mix until crumbly. Firmly press 2/3 of mixture
into greased 8-inch square baking pan. Spread filling evenly over
crumb base. Top with remaining crumb mixture; pack lightly. Bake in
preheated moderate oven (350 F.) 30 to 35 minutes. Cool; cut in bars.
