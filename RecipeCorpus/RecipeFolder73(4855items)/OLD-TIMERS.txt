Title: OLD-TIMERS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 48 Cookies
3/4 c  Lard
3/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Mild molasses
3/4 c  Sour cream
4 1/2 c  Sifted all-purpose flour
2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Ginger
Cream lard and sugar together. Add molasses and cream, then the eggs.
Stir in the sifted dry ingredients. Dough will be soft. Chill in
refrigerator overnight. Use half of dough at a time. Roll out
lightly, 1/4-inch thick on a floured cloth. Cut rounds of 2 1/2 to 3
inches. Place on greased cookie sheets and bake 15 to 20 minutes.
Source: The Chatelaine Cookbook
by Elaine Collett From the collection of K. Deck
