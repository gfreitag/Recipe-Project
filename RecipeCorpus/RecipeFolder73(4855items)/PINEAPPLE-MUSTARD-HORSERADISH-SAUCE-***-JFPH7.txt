Title: PINEAPPLE-MUSTARD-HORSERADISH SAUCE *** JFPH7
Categories: Sauces, Fruits, Condiment
Yield: 1 Servings
10 oz Pineapple preserves
10 oz Apple jelly
6 oz Horseradish
1 1/2 oz Dry mustard
1 ts Black pepper
Mix all ingredients and let sit in refrigerator for 1 hour. Hope you
enjoy. VICKI PHILLIPS   (JFPH78B) Source: "Welcome to Our Family
Dinner Table"
