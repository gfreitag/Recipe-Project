Title: PEANUT LOAF
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
4 tb Margarine
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Peanuts chopped fine
Cream margarine and sugar, add egg. Mix flour & baking powder and add
alternately with milk, mix in peanuts. Pour in greased & floured loaf
pan and bake in 350 F oven 50 - 60 minutes.
