Title: PICO DE GALLO SALSA  PUERTO NUEVO
Categories: Salsa, Mexican
Yield: 2 Servings
3    Serrano peppers -- finely
Diced
(or jalapenos)
2    Tomatoes -- diced
1    Onion -- finely chopped
Chopped cilantro -- see
Note
1    Clove garlic -- chopped
1    Lemon -- halved
~ CILANTRO: "1 handful (no more than that)" - Combine chilies,
tomatoes, onion, cilantro and garlic. - Mix and squeeze lemon halves
over mixture, making sure no seeds get in salsa. - Serve ar room
temperature.

recipe for more information.
