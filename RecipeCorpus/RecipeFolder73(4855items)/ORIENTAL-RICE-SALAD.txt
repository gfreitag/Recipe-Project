Title: ORIENTAL RICE SALAD
Categories: Chinese, Rice
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn Chicken Broth
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Diced Cooked Poultry or Pork
1/4 lb Fresh Mushrooms
2 oz Jar Pimento, sliced or diced
1 c  Rice, raw
2 tb Soy Sauce
2 tb Toasted Sesame Seeds
1/4 lb Fresh Bean Sprouts
4    Green Onions with tops
Slice mushrooms and onions.  Drain pimento.  Add enough water to
broth to make 2 1/2 cups.  Bring to a boil in a medium saucepan. Stir
in rice. Cover tightly and simmer for 20 minutes. Remove from heat
and let stand, covered, until all liquid is absorbed; about 5
minutes. Transfer rice to large bowl. Combine oil, soy sauce, salt
and sesame seeds (optional); mix well.  Stir into hot, cooked rice.
Cover and chill. Stir in remaining ingredients before serving.
