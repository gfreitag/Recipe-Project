Title: PISTACHIO SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Pistachio pudding
1 cn Crushed pineapple and juice
(Large can)
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
1 lg Carton of Cool Whip
Nuts
Mix pudding, crushed pineapple and juice together. Add marshmallows.
Add Cool Whip.  Add sufficient nuts to topping. Mix well and
refrigerate. Randy Rigg
