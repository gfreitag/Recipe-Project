Title: PEACH & CHAMPAGNE SPECIAL
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pt Frozen vanilla yogurt
1    Split chilled champagne (2
Cups)
1/2 c  Fresh peaches*
1    Peeled and sliced kiwi
Mix yogurt, champagne and peaches together until smooth and creamy.
Pour into wine glasses and serve immediately garnished with kiwi
slices. * You can use any other seasonal fruit. Recipe By
: DESSERT SHOW #DS3041

Date: 09/26/96
