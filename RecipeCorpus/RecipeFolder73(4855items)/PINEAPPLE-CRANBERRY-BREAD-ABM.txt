Title: PINEAPPLE CRANBERRY BREAD ABM
Categories: Abm, Posted-mm
Yield: 8 Servings
2 ts Yeast
3 c  Flour
1 c  Oats
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
2/3 c  Pineapple -- crushed
1 c  Cranberry juice
Add at beep
Chopped nuts
Cranberries -- dried
Put all ingredients in bread machine and turn on.

Recipe By: "The Bread Machine Cookbook," Donna German
