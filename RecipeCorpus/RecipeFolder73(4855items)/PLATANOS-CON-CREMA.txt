Title: PLATANOS CON CREMA
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Bananas
1 c  Butter
1/4 c  Sweet sherry
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Whipping cream
1/2 ts Vanilla
Bananas with Cream xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

Melt butter in microwave 30 seconds.   Peel bananas, slice legthwise
or crosswise, add to melted butter and heat 2 minutes. Place on
serving platter, discarding butter, sprinkle with sherry and 1/4 cup
sugar.

Whip cream, add vanilla and remaining sugar.  Cover bananas with
cream.
