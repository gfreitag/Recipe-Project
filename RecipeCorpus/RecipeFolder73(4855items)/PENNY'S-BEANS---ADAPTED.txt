Title: PENNY'S BEANS - ADAPTED
Categories: Beans, Diabetic, Vegetarian
Yield: 2 Cups
1 lb Beans; cooked
1/2    Onion; diced
1 tb Vegetable oil
2 cl Garlic; crushed
Lemon pepper
1 c  Brown rice; cooked
1 cn Mushrooms
2 c  Vegetable stock
3 tb Lemon juice
Saute onions in oil until clear with garlic, add mushrooms
(undrained), broth, lemon juice, lemon pepper, and rice to beans.
Cook over low heat for about 1/2 hour.
