Title: ORIENTAL STYLE DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
4 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Honey
1/2 ts Five spice powder
3 tb Garlic oil
3 tb Sesame oil
Mix all ingredients in a shaker jar. Secure cap tightly and shake
vigorously. Shake again just prior to use.
