Title: PLAIN CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 3/4 c  Sifted flour
1/4 ts Salt
3 ts Baking powder
3/4 c  Milk
Cream butter and sugar.  Beat eggs and stir in.  Sift together flour,
salt and baking powder.  Add alternately with milk. Bake in a 9 x 9"
pan at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes.

Use this recipe for strawberry shortcake.

From Mrs. Alvin Funk, Hillsboro, KS
