Title: OLD-FASHIONED BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Mustard, ground
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/8 c  Honey
1/2 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Tabasco sauce
1/2 c  Horseradish
2 ts Salt
1 ts Black pepper, fresh ground
1 tb Sugar
1 ea Garlic, clove, crushed
1 ts Sage
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan.  Bring to a boil while stirring
constantly.  Set aside and let cool to room temperature. Use for
basting spareribs and as a general-purpose barbeque sauce for
hamburgers, pork, etc.

Source:  Mesquite Cookery by John 'Boog' Powell
