Title: OREGON HAZELNUT ENERGY BALLS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 20 Pieces
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/3 c  Honey
3/4 c  Dry milk
1/4 c  Grated cheddar cheese
1 c  Muesli
1 c  Oregon hazelnuts, chopped
Mix together peanut butter, honey, dry milk, cheese and Muesli; let
rest for 5 minutes and roll into approximately 20 1-inch balls. Roll
in chopped Oregon hazelnuts.
