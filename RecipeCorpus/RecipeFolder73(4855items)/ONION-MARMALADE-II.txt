Title: ONION MARMALADE II
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 1 Cup
4    Red onions (~1.5 lb)
2 tb Butter
2 tb Red wine vinegar
2 tb Honey
Cut onions crosswise in half, those into quarters, those into cubes.
Heat butter in a heavy saucepan, add onions, cover, cook medium high
~15 min until they start to brown, stirring occasionally. Uncover,
add vinegar, cook briefly until almost evaporated, then stir in
honey. Cover tightly, cook 10 min.
