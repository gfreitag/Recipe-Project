Title: PANOCHA
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 ds Salt
1 tb Corn Syrup
1 tb Butter
3/4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Nutmeats
Combine sugar, syrup, salt, milk and butter. Cook, stirring only until
sugar  dissolves, to soft ball stage (238 degrees). Cool without
stirring, then add vanilla and heat until thick. Add nuts, and spread
in greased pan.

From Alvadore Church of Christ Cookbook, by Violet Chambers
