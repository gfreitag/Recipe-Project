Title: OVEN GLAZED CHICKEN
Categories: Chicken, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Chicken breast (skinned)
1 cn Tomato soup
1 tb Packed brown sugar
1 tb Water
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
Hot cooked rice or noodles
1.In 11"x7" baking dish, arrange chicken. Bake at 375F for 30
minutes. 2.In a small bowl, combine soup, brown sugar, water, vinegar
and worcestershire sauce; spoon over chicken. 3.Bake 30 minutes or
until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear. Stir sauce
before serving. Serve with rice or noodles. Preparation: 5 min.
Cooking time: 1 hour
