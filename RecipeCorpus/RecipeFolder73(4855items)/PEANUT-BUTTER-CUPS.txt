Title: PEANUT BUTTER CUPS
Categories: Candies, Desserts
Yield: 40 Servings
1/2 c  BUTTER
1 c  PEANUT BUTTER
2 c  POWDERED SUGAR
1/2 lb WHITE CHOCOLATE
1/2 lb CHOCOLATE, DARK
MIX INGREDIENTS TOGETHER WELL. MELT TOGETHER 1/2 LB. WHITE CHOCOLATE
AND 1/2 LB. DARK CHOCOLATE. MIX UNTIL SMOOTH. DIP EACH PEANUT BUTTER
BALL IN CHOCOLATE AND PLACE ON WAXED PAPER, IN REFRIGERATER, TO FIRM.
