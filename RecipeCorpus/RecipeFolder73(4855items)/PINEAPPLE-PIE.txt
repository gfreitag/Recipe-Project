Title: PINEAPPLE PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
14 oz. can
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 cn Crushed pineapple, drained
20 oz. can
1 ct Frozen whipped topping
8 oz. size thawed
1 lg Graham cracker crust
Combine condensed milk and lemon juice.  Stir well. Fold in pineapple
and whipped topping.  Spoon mix into crust and chill before serving.
Randy Rigg
