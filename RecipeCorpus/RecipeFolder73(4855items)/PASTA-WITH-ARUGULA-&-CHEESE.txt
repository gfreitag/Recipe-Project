Title: PASTA WITH ARUGULA & CHEESE
Categories: Italian/tus, Main dishes, Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 3 Servings
1 bn Arugula
4    Tomatoes -- chopped
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 lb Thin spaghetti
Parmesan cheese
Combine arugula, tomatoes and oilve oil. Let stand for 1 hour. Toss
with cooked spaghetti and garnish with shaved Parmesan cheese.
