Title: PESTO & PASTA
Categories: New, Text, Import
1/4 lb Rotelle pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
6 TB pesto
2 c  tomato sauce
1/4 c  cream

Cook Rotelle according to package directions. Drain and add 3
tablespoons of pesto. Toss to coat. Set aside. In saucepan combine
remaining pesto and tomato sauce. Stir until mixed. Add cream and
stir well. Pour over pasta and serve.
