Title: PIGS IN A BLANKET
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Hot dogs
1 pk Crescent rolls
5 sl American cheese
Split hot dogs lengthwise.  Add small strips of cheese on each hot
dog. Wrap hot dog and cheese in crescent roll, rolling wide end
first. Bake on ungreased pan according to crescent roll package, or
until golden brown. Randy Rigg  The Pinnacle Club BBS  812-963-9139
