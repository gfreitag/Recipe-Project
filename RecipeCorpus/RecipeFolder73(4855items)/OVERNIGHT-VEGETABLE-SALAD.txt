Title: OVERNIGHT VEGETABLE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Peas, Beans, Greens, Celery
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Tiny green peas, drained (16
-oz.)
1 cn French-style green beans,
-drained (16 oz.)
1 cn Shoe peg white corn, drained
-(11 oz.)
1 md Onion, finely chopped
3/4 c  Finely chopped celery
2 tb Chopped pimentos, optional
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  White wine vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
In a large bowl, combine peas, beans, corn, onion, celery and
pimentos. In a large saucepan, combine remaining ingredients; heat
and stir until sugar dissolves. Pour over vegetables. Cover and
refrigerate overnight. Yield: 10-12 servings.

Note: Salad will keep for several days stored in a covered container
in the refrigerator.

SOURCE:*Jackie Carlile, Bakersfield, CA, Reminisce Sept./Oct. 93
