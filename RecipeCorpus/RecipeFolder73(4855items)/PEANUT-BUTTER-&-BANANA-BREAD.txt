Title: PEANUT BUTTER & BANANA BREAD
Categories: Bread, Machine
Yield: 14 Servings
1 pk Yeast
2 1/2 c  Bread flour
1/2 c  Wheat flour
3 tb Gluten
2 tb Brown sugar -- packed
1 ts Salt
1    Banana
1    Sliced directly into
1    The inner pan
3/4 c  Peanut butter, creamy or
Chunky
1    Egg
1 c  Very warm water
Add all ingredients into the pan in the order listed. Select white
bread and push "Start."
