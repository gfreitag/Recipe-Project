Title: PEACH COLADA
Categories: Alcoholic, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Drink
1 oz Dark rum
1 oz Light rum
4 oz Pineapple juice
1 oz Cream of coconut
1/2 c  Peaches; fresh; peeled
- (more or less, to taste)
1 c  Ice; crushed
Combine all ingredients in blender and blend well. Makes one drink.

A winning recipe from the recent Peach Dessert Contest at the State
Farmers Market in Raleigh, NC.
