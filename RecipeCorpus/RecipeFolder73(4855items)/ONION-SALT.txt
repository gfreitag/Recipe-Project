Title: ONION SALT
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Dried onion
(rounded tb)
1 tb Unrefined coarse salt
(rounded tb)
Measure the onion and salt into the blender and process at high speed
until fine. If the onion has any moisture in it at all, some of the
mixture will have to be scraped    from the sides of the blender with
a rubber spatula. Store in a small jar, tightly sealed. YIELD 1/2
shakerful. SOURCE: Blend It Splendid (pg. 138)
