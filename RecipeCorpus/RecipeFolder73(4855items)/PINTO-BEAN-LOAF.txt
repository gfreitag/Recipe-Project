Title: PINTO BEAN LOAF
Categories: Main dish, Mcd/c
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Pinto Beans, cooked/mashed
1 c  Tomato sauce
1 c  Bread crumbs, finely ground
1/4 c  Minced onion
1/4 c  Quick oatmeal
3 ts Egg replacer mixed with
4 tb Water, beat til frothy
Fresh ground pepper to taste
1/4 c  Catsup or barbeque sauce
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine all ingredients, except
catsup, in a large bowl.  Mix well. Turn into a non-stick loaf pan,
9-1/4 x 5-1/4 x 3, and flatten.
Spread catsup or BBQ sauce over the top.  Bake for 45 minutes. NOTE:
Makes a good sandwich spread when cold.
