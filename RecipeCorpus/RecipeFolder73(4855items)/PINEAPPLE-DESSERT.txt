Title: PINEAPPLE DESSERT
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 16 Servings
1 lg Container Cool  Whip
1 lg Can (20 oz) crushed
Pineapple, drained.
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 c  Chopped pecans
2    Graham cracker pie shells
Mix pineapple and sweetened condensed milk .  Fold in cool whip until
thoroughly combined. Add lemon juice and fold in pecans. Pour into
two pie shells and chill about 4 hours. Serves 16
