Title: OREGON HAZELNUT MACAROONS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 30 Cookies
1 c  Oregon hazelnut paste
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Orange peel
1/8 ts Salt
2    Egg whites; beaten stiff
Blend paste, sugar, orange peel and salt. Fold in egg whites. Drop by
teaspoons onto lightly-greased cookie sheet. Bake in a 350 oven 15 to
17 minutes, until golden brown. Cool.
