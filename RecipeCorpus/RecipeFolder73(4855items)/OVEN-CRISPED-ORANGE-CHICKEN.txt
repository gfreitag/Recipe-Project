Title: OVEN-CRISPED ORANGE CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Egg; beaten
1/3 c  Frozen orange juice concentr
2 tb Soy sauce
1/2 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
1 ts Paprika
3 tb Unsalted butter; melted
Chicken parts
Preheat oven to 375.

Combine the egg, orange juice concentrate, and soy sauce; stir to mix
well. In a small bowl, combine the bread crumbs and paprika. Put
melted butter in a 13 x 9 x 2 baking pan. Dip chicken pieces in the
orange-soy mixture, then coat with crumbs.  Place chicken skin side
up in baking pan. Sprinkle with any remaining crumbs. Bake uncovered
for 50 minutes. Do not turn.
