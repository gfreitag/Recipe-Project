Title: PEANUT BUTTER APPLE DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Fruit
Yield: 2 Cups
1 pk (8 oz.) cream cheese,
-softened
1 c  Peanut butter
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Milk
3    To 4 apples, cut into wedges
In a mixing bowl, combine the first four ingredients; mix well. Serve
with apples.  Store in the refrigerator. Yield: 2 2/3 cups. Typed in
