Title: ORANGE CHOCOLATE MOUSSE (VEG TIMES)
Categories: Desserts, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Brown rice syrup
2 ts Vanilla
1 lb Firm tofu, cut into chunks
1/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 ts Grated orange zest
Water as needed
Place rice syrup & vanilla in a food processor. Process, adding the
tofu a few chunks at a time. Then add cocoa & orange zest. Add water
to thin as needed.

Pour into serving dishes & chill.

"Vegetarian Times" November, 1991
