Title: PINEAPPLE DIP
Categories: Desserts, Osg, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ea Pineapple; run through food
-processor, chopped
2 c  Corn syrup; light
4 c  Water
6 c  Sugar
Boil sugar, water, syrup to soft ball stage. Add pineapple and what
juice may be from grinding and cook to desired thickness.

Source: Mrs. Frank Walter, Pleasent Grove Grange, Senece County, OH
