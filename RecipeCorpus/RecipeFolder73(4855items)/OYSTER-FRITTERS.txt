Title: OYSTER FRITTERS
Categories: Shellfish, Cajun
Yield: 20 Servings
1 c  Pancake mix
1 pt Oysters & juice
2 tb Onion, finely chopped
1 ts Baking powder
1    Egg
Salt & pepper to taste
1/4 c  Cooking oil
Combine pancake mix, oysters, onion, baking powder, egg, salt and
pepper.  Drop by tablespoonsful into hot oil. Brown on both sides.
Drain on paper towels. Courtesy Telephone Pioneers BillSpalding *P
