Title: PAPAS CON TOMATES
Categories: Peppers, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Dried mirasol chilies, stems
-and seeds removed, crushed
2    Meduim tomatoes, peeled and
-diced
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1    Large potato, cooked,peeled,
-and diced
1    Medium sweet potato, cooked,
-peeled and diced
Chopped fresh cilantro for
-garnish
Simmer the chiles, tomatoes and cumin for 15 minutes to form a thick
sauce. Pour sauce over potatoes, toss to coat, garnish with cilantro,
and serve.
Heat index: 6 on a scale of 1-10
Source: Whole Chile Pepper Book
