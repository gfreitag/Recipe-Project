Title: ORANGE JULIUS COPYCAT RECIPE
Categories: Beverages, Fruits, Shrimp
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  Orange juice
1 c  Water
2    Eggs
3/4 ts Vanilla extract
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Ice -- heaping cup
Combine all of the ingredients in a blender set on High speed for
exactly one minute.
