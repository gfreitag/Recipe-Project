Title: POACHED PEARS WITH CHOCOLATE SAUCE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sugar
2 c  Water
1    3"cinnamon stick
3    Whole cloves
4 lg Bose pears with stems
Attached, peeled
Great Chocolate Sauce
In med saucepan, dissolve sugar in water; heat to boiling. Add spices;
cover. Set aside. Core pears from bottom; add to liquid. Simmer pears
partially covered, basting & gently turning, 30 to 45 minutes,
according to ripeness, until tender when tested with wooden pick.
Cool pears in poaching liquid. Chill thoroughly; drain. Serve with
sauce. Submitted By THESERVS@GATE.NET (THOMAS E. HAUG)  On   TUE, 14
