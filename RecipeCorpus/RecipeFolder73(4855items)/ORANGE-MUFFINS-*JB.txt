Title: ORANGE MUFFINS *JB
Categories: Muffins, Breakfast
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Cooking oil
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Fresh orange juice
2 tb Grated orange rind
Combine oil, sugar and eggs. Mix well. Add remaining ingredients.
Blend. Pour into muffin pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes.
