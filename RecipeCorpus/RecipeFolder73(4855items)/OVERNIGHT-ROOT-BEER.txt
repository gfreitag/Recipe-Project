Title: OVERNIGHT ROOT BEER
Categories: Mixed, Drinks
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb Root beer extract
1 tb Dry yeast
Lukewarm water
In a gallon jug with a tight-fitting lid, combine sugar, root beer
extract and yeast. Add enough lukewarm water to fill gallon jug. Mix
well. Let stand at room temperature for 6 hours. Place in
refrigerator overnight or until completely chilled.
