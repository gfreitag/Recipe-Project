Title: ORANGE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
3/4 c  Orange Juice
1 tb Lemon Juice
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Nutmeg, Ground
2 tb Butter
2 tb All-Purpose Flour
In a measuring cup, combine orange juice, lemon juice, salt and
nutmeg; set aside.  In a 1 quart saucepan over medium heat, melt
butter. Add flour and cook, stirring constantly, until sauce is
thickened. This sauce is very good on baked fish or chicken.  From:
Syd's Cookbook.
