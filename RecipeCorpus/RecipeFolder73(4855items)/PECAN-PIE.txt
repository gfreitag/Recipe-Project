Title: PECAN PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM----------------------------INCH---------------------------------
3 ea Eggs
2/3 c  Sugar
1/3 ts Salt
1/3 c  Melted butter
1 c  Dark corn syrup
1 x  Mix in:
1 c  Pecans
MMMMM----------------------------INCH---------------------------------
2 ea Large eggs
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Melted butter
3/4 c  Dark corn syrup
1 x  Mix in:
3/4 c  Pecans
Pour into pastry shell.Bake @ 375 degrees 40 to 50 minutes.
