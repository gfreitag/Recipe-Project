Title: PICADILLO (BEEF HASH)
Categories: Beef
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Oil
1 ea Onion
1 ea Green pepper
1 ea Clove garlic
1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 lb Ground pork
1/4 lb Ground ham
3/4 c  Capers
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1/4 c  Dry wine
Heat oil and saute minced onion, crushed garlic and minced green
pepper. Add the meat and cook a few minutes, stirring constantly to
avoid sticking. Add the remaining ingredients and cook over medium
heat for about 20 minutes. Serves 6. Picadillo (beef hash)
