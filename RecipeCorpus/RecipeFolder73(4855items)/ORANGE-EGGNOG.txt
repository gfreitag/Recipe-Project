Title: ORANGE EGGNOG
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 16 Servings
2 qt Milk
Orange sections
6 oz Orange juice concentrate
1 1/2 c  Sugar
12    Eggs; separated
Heat milk in large heavy saucepan. Beat egg yolks with 1 cup sugar.
Stir in about 1 cup hot milk. Quickly stir into remaining hot milk
and cook, stirring constantly, over very low heat until mixture
thickens and coats metal spoon. Remove from heat and add vanilla.
Chill. Stir in undiluted orange concentrate. Pour into punch bowl.
Beat egg whites until foamy. Add remaining 1/2 cup sugar, 2
tablespoons at a time, and continue beating until mixture stands in
stiff peaks. Float heaping tablespoons of meringue on top of eggnog.
Garnish with orange sections.
