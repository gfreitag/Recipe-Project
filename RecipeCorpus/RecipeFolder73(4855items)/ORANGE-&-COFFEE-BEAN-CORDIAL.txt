Title: ORANGE & COFFEE BEAN CORDIAL
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 1 Pint
1    Orange, extra large
44    Coffee beans
44    Sugar cubes
1    Vanilla bean (1 inch)
1 pt Cognac*
Wash orange and make as many small slits in it as possible.Stick a
coffee bean in each slit and place the rest of the beans in the jar.
Add the sugar cubes and the orange. Pour in cognac and add vanilla
bean. Let the drink mature for 1 1/2 to 2 months. Strain, squeezing
juice from orange, and filter. Ready to serve. *Vodka or rum may also
be used. Yield: 1 pint Container: Very wide mouth quart jar
