Title: PATRIDGEBERRY PUNCH
Categories: Canadian, Beverages
Yield: 8 Servings
1 qt Partridgeberries
3 T  Lemon juice
1 c  Orange juice
2 c  Sugar
6 c  Water
1 qt Ginger ale
Cook partridgeberries in 4 cups of water until soft.Crush and drain
through a cheese cloth.Boil sugar and remaining 2 cups of water for 5
Min.Add to Partridgeberry juice and chill.Add fruit juices.Before
serving add ginger ale.
