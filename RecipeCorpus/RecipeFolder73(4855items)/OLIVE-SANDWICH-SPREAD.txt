Title: OLIVE SANDWICH SPREAD
Categories: Misc
Yield: 4 Servings
2 pk Cream cheese, softened
(3 oz. each)
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Chopped green olives
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1 tb Olive juice
In a small bowl, combine all ingredients. Spread on crackers or bread.
Yield: 1 1/2 cups.

Source: Taste of Home magazine
