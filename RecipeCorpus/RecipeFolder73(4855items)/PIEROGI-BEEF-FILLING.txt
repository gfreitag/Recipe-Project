Title: PIEROGI BEEF FILLING
Categories: Beef
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lg Onion -- sliced
2 tb Margarine
1 3/4 c  Ground beef
3/4 c  Cooked rice
2 ts Beef broth
3 tb Hot water
1 tb Parsley -- chopped
Saute onion in margarine.  Stir in meat and rice. Dissolve bouillon
in hot water.  Add to meat mixture with parsley and salt & pepper to
taste.
