Title: PACE CREAM CHEESE DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Cream cheese (8 oz)
4 tb PACE Picante Sauce
Soften cream cheese in a bowl. Stir in PACE Picante Sauce. Add lemon,
salt and pepper to taste. Blend well. Serve with corn chips or potato
chips. Add more PACE Picante Sauce for a hotter taste.
