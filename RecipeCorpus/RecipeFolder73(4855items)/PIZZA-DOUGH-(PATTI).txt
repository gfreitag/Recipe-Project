Title: PIZZA DOUGH (PATTI)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Yeast
2 c  115 degrees water
1 1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Olive oil
5 c  High gluten flour
-(white bread)
mix together till makes a ball...  if feels to wet add flour.. place
in bowl with plastic ontop.  let rise 4-6 hours make pizza....cook
450 for 15-25 min.

Note:  I also add garlic, rosemary, herbs to dough at start... makes
things tasty...

Patti
