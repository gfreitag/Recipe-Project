Title: PICKLED ROSEBUDS
Categories: Preserves, Relishes, Flowers
Yield: 1 Servings
40    Baby rosebuds
1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  White wine vinegar
Was the rosebuds well.  Place rosebuds in a 1-quart jar. Combine
sugar and vinegar; pour over rosebuds. Seal with paraffin and store
in warm, dark place for about one month. Buds may be used in salads
or with sandwiches.
