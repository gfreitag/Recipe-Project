Title: PAPAS CON CREMA VERDE
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
1 bn Cilantro -- stems included
6 sm Yellow chiles -- seeded
1 lg Pale green (Hungarian) chile
Seeded
1 bn Green onions -- white and
Green
Parts included
1 c  Mexican Crema
1/2 c  Olive oil
1    Lime -- juiced
Place all of the above ingredients in a blender. Blend until very
smooth. Use as a sauce over very hot, boiled, and skinned potatoes.
