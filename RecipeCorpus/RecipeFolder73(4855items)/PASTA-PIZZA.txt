Title: PASTA PIZZA
Categories: Pasta, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Angel hair spaghetti
1 tb Olive oil
1 ts Olive oil
1/3 c  Chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 1/4 c  Low-fat pasta sauce
1/4 c  Sliced black olives
1/2 c  Shredded non-fat mozzarella
1/4 ts Italian seasoning
Prepare angel hair pasta as directed on package. Rinse and drain. Set
aside.  In 6-inch nonstick skillet, combine 1 teaspoon olive oil,
green pepper and onion. Cook over medium high heat for 3 to 5 minutes
or until vegetables are tender, stirring frequently. Set aside. Het
remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil over medium high heat in 10-inch
nonstick skillet. Spread angel hair pasta in skillet.  Cook for 4 to
6 minutes or until lightly browned on underside. Turn pasta crust
onto plate. Slip crust back into skillet browned-side-up. Reduce heat
to medium. Spoon sauce evenly over crust.  Top evenly with prepared
vegetables and the olives. Sprinkle with cheese and italian
seasoning. Cover and cook for 4 to 5 minutes or until cheese is
melted.  Cut pizza into wedges and serve.
