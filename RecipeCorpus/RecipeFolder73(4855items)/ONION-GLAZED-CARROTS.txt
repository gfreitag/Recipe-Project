Title: ONION GLAZED CARROTS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Lipton onion butter*
1/2 c  Brown sugar
4 c  Cooked sliced carrots
In Medium skillet, melt Lipton onion butter; blend in brown sugar. Add
carrots and cook, stirring constantly, 5 minutes or until carrots are
evenly glazed.

*Lipton onion butter: Thoroughly blen 1 envelope lipton onion recipe
secrets soup mix with 1 container (8 oz.) whipped butter or soft
margarine, or 1/2 pound butter or margarine, softened. Store covered
in refrigerator.
