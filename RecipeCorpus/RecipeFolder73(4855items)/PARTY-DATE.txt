Title: PARTY DATE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 25 Servings
3 c  Dates, cut up
1 1/2 c  Shortening
3 c  Boiling water
6    Eggs
3 ts Baking soda
3 tb Cocoa
4 c  Sugar
4 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Chopped nuts
2 c  Chocolate bits
Mix dates and water and allow to cool. Combine 3c sugar, shortening,
eggs, cocoa and flour. Add baking soda to date mixture and combine.
Pour into 2 greased loaf pans and sprinkle with topping of 1c sugar,
nuts and chocolate bits. Bake at 350 degrees for 45-50 minutes. Slice
and serve hot. Garnish with whipped cream. Mrs. Henry D. Chaplin
