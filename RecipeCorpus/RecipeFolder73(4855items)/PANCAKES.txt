Title: PANCAKES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Flour
2 tb Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
3 ts Baking powder
2 ea Eggs
1 1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Salad oil or melted crisco.
Sift to-gether flour,sugar,salt and baking powder.Beat eggs and add
milk.Combine with dry ingredients,mixing smooth.Add shortening or
salad oil.Drop by spoonfuls on hot griddle.
