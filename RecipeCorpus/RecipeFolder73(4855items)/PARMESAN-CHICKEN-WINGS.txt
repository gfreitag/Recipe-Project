Title: PARMESAN CHICKEN WINGS
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Freshly grated parmesan
Ches
1 tb Heaping -- finely chopped
Fresh
1    Parsley
1 tb Fresh -- oregano or marjam
Choppedor 1 ts.of either
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Fresh ground pepper
2 lb Chicken wings cut up
1/2 c  Butter melted
Preheat oven to 375 deg.Mix cheese,parsley,oregano,salt and pepper
together and place on a dry tray.Dip each piece of chicken into the
butter,then roll in the cheese mixture,coating well.Place on a
greased baking sheet.Bake for 45 minutes,turning when brown.Wings
will freeze well.Thaw in refrigerator,and heat in a 375 deg.
oven.Makes 4 main course or 8 appetizer servings...
