Title: PLUM RELISH
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Apples
4 md Onions
1 qt Vinegar
2 tb Grated ginger root
2 tb Cloves
6 lb Plums, pitted
1 ts Garlic salt
2 tb Salt
1/2 ts Red pepper
2 lb Brown sugar
Pare and core apples.  Chop apples, onions, and plums. Combine all
ingredients.  Cook slowly, stirring frequently, until thick. Mrs. J.F.
Mabie, Happy Hollow, WA.
