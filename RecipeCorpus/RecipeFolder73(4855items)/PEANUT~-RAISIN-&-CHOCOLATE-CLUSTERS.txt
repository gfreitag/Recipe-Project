Title: PEANUT~ RAISIN & CHOCOLATE CLUSTERS
Categories: Candies, Holiday
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Egg white
1/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Raisins
3/4 c  Raisins
3/4 c  Chocolate chips
Beat egg white till frothy, add sugar gradually; continue beating till
light. Stir in raisins, peanuts and chocolate chips. Place in mounds
on cookie sheet lined with parchment (or buttered and floured). Bake
in preheated 375F oven 8 to 10 minutes or till browned. Cool. Makes:
24 candies
