Title: PARTY APPETIZER
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 15 Servings
1    8 OZ
2 tb Milk
1    3 oz
1/4 c  Green pepper
2 tb Dehydrated minced onion
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/3 c  Chopped pecans
Cream cheese
Sliced dried beef
BLEND CHEESE AND MILK IN BOWL.  ADD  DRIED BEEF(CUT INTO SMALL
PIECES), GREEN PEPPERS, ONIONS, GARLIC SALT AND PEPPER. MIX WELL.
STIR IN SOUR CREAM AND SPREAD INTO BAKING DISH AND BAKE AT 350 FOR 20
MINUTES, PLACE PECANS ON TOP .   SERVE WARM WITH CRACKERS FOR DIPPING.
