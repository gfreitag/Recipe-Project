Title: PAULINE'S SLAW VARIATIONS
Categories: Salads, Family
Yield: 1 Batch
SLAW #1:
Cabbage, shredded
Apples, chunked, covered w/
Lemon juice
-(so apples won't brown)
Golden raisins
SLAW #2:
Cabbage, shredded
Carrots, grated
Radishes, grated
Parsley flakes
Zucchini, diced (optional)
Combine with mayonnaise and a spoonful of sour cream; chill.
