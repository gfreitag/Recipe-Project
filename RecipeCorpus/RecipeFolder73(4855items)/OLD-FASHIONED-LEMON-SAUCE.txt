Title: OLD-FASHIONED LEMON SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 1/3 cups
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Water
1    Egg well beaten
3 tb Lemon juice (1 lemon)
Grated rind of 1 lemon
Combine in saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly,
