Title: OLIVE CURRIED-EGG SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches, Eggs
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Hard-boiled eggs; coarsely
- chopped
1/3 c  Salad dressing
1 1/2 ts Curry powder
10    Olives; chopped
1/4 ts Sakt
8 sl Whole-wheat bread
Butter or margarine; soften
Tomato slices
Fresh lettuce
Combine egg; salad dressing, curry powder, olives and salt in a
medium size bowl and mix thoroughly.  Spread bread slices and butter.
Divide egg salad mixture equally on 4 of the bread slices. Top each
with thin slice of tomato and lettuce leaves and place top bread
layer on sandwich. Makes 4 sandwiches.  Formatted by Mary Wilson,
BWVB02B.
Date: 07/02/96
