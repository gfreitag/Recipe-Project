Title: PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES-MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 36 Servings
1 1/2 c  Bake it all mix
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla extract
Combine all ingredients and shape into balls 1 inch in diameter. Place
balls on cooky sheet. Press with fork dipped in sugar. Bake in a 350
F. oven 10 to 12 minutes. When cool, store in a coverd container.
Yield: 3 dozen
