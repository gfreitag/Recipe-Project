Title: OVEN BAKED BEANS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Navy Beans
1 ea Onion
1/4 lb Salt pork
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Molasses
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1/4 ts Dry mustard
Soak beans overnight covered with water. In morning put on stove and
bring to simmer and simmer 15 min. In casserole put whole onion, add
beans and other ingredients, pour water beans were simmered in over
all. Cover and bake in 275 F oven for 4 or 5 hours.
