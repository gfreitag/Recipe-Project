Title: ORANGE BREAD (MCGEE)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Orange peels
1 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg; well beaten
1 c  Milk
3 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
Boil orange peel in salt water, 10 minutes. Drain and cut fine. Add
water, sugar and cook until thick and tender. Add egg, milk, flour
and baking powder. Bake in moderate oven 3/4 hour. (Can use sour milk
with 1/2 t. baking soda and 1 t. baking powder).

NOTE: Moderate oven is 350 - 400 F.

Source: SIS McGee, Richfield Grange, Summit County, OH Lowell Grange,
Washington County, OH
