Title: ONION SOUP MIX
Categories: Soups/stews
Yield: 10 Servings
6 tb Onion flakes
2 tb Parmesan cheese; (optional)
2 ts Corn starch
(optional)
2 tb Beef bouillon
1 tb Soybean flour
2    - 3 tabsp vegetable protein
2 tb Instant mashed potatoes
Blend all of the above ingredients together until well blended. Shelf
life-- 2 months. TO RECONSTITUTE:  Use 2 Tablespoons in 1-1/2 cups
boiling water. Source: Travis County Agricultural Service, Austin, TX
