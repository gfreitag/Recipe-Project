Title: PAPAYA & PINEAPPLE CHICHA
Categories: Beverage, Fruit, Latin, Panama
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Papaya -- Cubed
1 c  Pineapple -- Cubed
2 tb Lemon Juice
1/2 c  Sugar -- Or More To Taste
Ice -- Crushed
Put the papaya and lemon juice in the blender and blend. Add the
sugar, pineapple and lastly the crushed ice. Serve in a tall glasses
and top with cherries.
