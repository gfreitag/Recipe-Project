Title: PECAN MERINGUES
Categories: Christmas, Cookies, Nuts
2 c  pecans -- chopped
Yield: 1 Servings
1 TB flour
1 TB corn starch
2 lg egg whites
pn salt
:
1 c  light brown sugar -- packed

Chop the nut medium fine.  Mix the nuts , flour and cornstarch in a
small bowl.  Beat the egg whites until stiff, adding sugar gradually.
Fold in the flour/nut mixture. Drop by spoonfulls onto parchment
paper. Bake in preheated 300 oven for about 18-20 minutes.
