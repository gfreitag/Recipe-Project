Title: PINEAPPLE ICEBOX PIE
Categories: Fruits, Pies & past
Yield: 8 Servings
4 tb Flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs -- beaten
3/4 c  Water
20 oz Crushed pineapple in juice
Vanilla wafers
Mix flour and sugar in a pot.  Add eggs and water. Add pineapple.
Cook till thick.  Alternate layers of wafers and fillinf in pie p an
till used up. Chill.
