Title: PHILLY CHEESE STEAK SANDWICH
Categories: Sandwiches, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
Van Geffen VGHC42a
2 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 ts Garlic Powder
1/8 ts Ground Black Pepper
8 oz Flank Steak
1 c  Red Onions -- Thinly Sliced
4    Hard Rolls (6") -- halved
Toasted
1/2 c  Pasteurized Process Cheese
Spread
Preheat broiler. In a small bowl combine Worcestershire sauce, garlic
powder and black pepper. Place steak on a rack in a broiler pan; brush
sauce mixture over sides of steak. Top steak with onions. Broil 4 to
5" from heat until onions are nearly crisp-tender, 3 to 5 minutes.
Remove onions from meat, placing to one side of broiler pan. Turn
steak; broil until done as desired,, 3 to 5 minutes longer. Place
meat on cutting board; thinly slice diagonally across the grain.
Place bottom halves of rolls on broiler pan, cut sides up; top with
beef slices and onions. Spoon cheese spread over onions. Broil until
melted, about 30 seconds. Cover with remaining roll halves.
