Title: PEANUT GARLIC SAUCE
Categories: Poultry, Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
2 ts Creamy peanut butter
2 tb Fish sauce
2 tb Chicken stock
1 ts Minced garlic
1 ts Red curry paste
Put the peanut butter into a blender,and add the fish sauce and
stock.Blend well.Add the garlic and curry paste,and blend until
smooth.
