Title: PIERRE FRANEY'S BROILED TOMATOES
Categories: Side, Dish
6 md tomatoes
Yield: 6 Servings
24    slivers garlic
salt to taste if desired
freshly ground pepper to
taste
:
:
:
3 TB olive oil
4 TB Parmesan cheese -- grated
4 TB fresh basil -- chopped

1.  Preheat the broiler to high, 500 degrees.

2.  Cut the tomatoes in half and arrange, cut side up, in a baking
dish. Insert 2 garlic slivers in each half.

3.  Sprinkle with salt, pepper and olive oil. Sprinkle with the
cheese.

4.  Place under the broiler for about 5 minutes until bubbling and the
garlic tips start to brown.  Remove garlic, sprinkle with basil and
serve.

Yield:  6 servings.
