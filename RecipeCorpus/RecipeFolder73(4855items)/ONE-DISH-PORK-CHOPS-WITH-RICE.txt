Title: ONE-DISH PORK CHOPS WITH RICE
Categories: Pork, Casseroles
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
3 tb Pepper, green; chopped
2 tb Oil, vegetable;
1 c  Rice, white; raw
2 c  Tomatoes, stewed; chopped
2 ts Sugar;
1/2 c  Cheese, Parmesan; grated
4 ea Pork chops;*
*Use 3/4 lb hot sausage instead of pork chops if you prefer. Heat oil
in a skillet that can go into oven; add onions, green peppers, and
rice. Saute until rice is translucent. Add sugar and tomatoes, blend.
Top with pork chops and then cheese.  Bake, covered, in preheated
350^F oven for about 1 hour.  Uncover and carefully stir rice mixture
after 50 minutes. Yield: 4 servings.
