Title: PEANUT BUTTER & JAM BARS
Categories: Dessert, Suzy, Posted-mm
Yield: 32 Bars
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Unsalted butter
1/2 c  Chunky peanut butter
1 lg Egg
1 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Strawberry preserves
Glaze
2 tb Unsalted butter
1 c  Powdered sugar
4 ts Hot water - + or -
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Well blend sugars, butter, peanut butter and egg in a large mixing
bowl. By hand stir in flour, baking soda and baking powder until well
mixed. Set aside 1 cup of the dough.

Press remaining dough onto the bottom of an ungreased 13x9x2-inch
baking pan. Spread strawberry preserves over dough.

Crumble the reserved dough, them generously sprinkle over the layer of
preserves.

Bake 20-25 minutes or until golden brown; cool completely. Drizzle
with the glaze. Cut into 2 x 1 1/4-inch bars.

GLAZE: Melt margarine in a double boiler over low heat. Stir in
powdered sugar and vanilla extract. Add water, 1 tsp at a time,
beating until smooth with a wish until thin enough to drizzle over
the bars.
