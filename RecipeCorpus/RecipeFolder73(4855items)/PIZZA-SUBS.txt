Title: PIZZA SUBS
Categories: Easy, Snacks
Yield: 6 Servings
1    French bread stick
1 c  Tomato sauce (7 1/2 oz) or:
1 c  Tomato sauce
2 ts Dried oregano
1 1/2 c  Mushrooms; thin sliced
1    Green pepper; chopped
2 c  Shredded mozzarella
Approx. Cook Time:
Fat grams    per serving:
:02ea
Halve bread lengthwise, cut each half in 3 pieces. Mix tomato sauce
with oregano. Spread 2 Tbsp over each piece. Top with mushrooms, then
green pepper. Sprinkle with cheese. Microwave, one at a time HIGH
(100%) for 2 minutes. Source: Canadian Living magazine March 1990
