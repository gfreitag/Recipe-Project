Title: PEANUT BUTTER FLUFF
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Peanut butter
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Sugar
Salt
Evaporated milk
:    Mix together the peanut butter, lemon juice, and sugar. Salt to
taste and add enough evaporated milk to make a apreading consistancy.
Whip until light and fluffy.
Serve on raisin bread.
This will keep for one week and makes 1-1/2c. Jeannie Allen
