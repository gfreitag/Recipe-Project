Title: PATTY PAN LASAGNA
Categories: Low-fat, Vegetables
1 lg squash, scallop -- sliced
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4    " thick
8 oz spaghetti sauce
8 oz cottage cheese, 2% fat
1/2 c  shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 c  shredded parmesan cheese

Slice patty pan or scalloped squash 1/4 inch thick. Lightly salt to
reduce water in squash at least an hour a head of making this dish,
then drain. Spray 1 1/2 qt. square baking dish lightly with Pam-type
spray. Layer sliced squash in baking dish. Spoon spaghetti sauce on
top of the squash. Top with cottage cheese, sprinkle of mozzarella &
parmesan cheese. Repeat 2 more times ending with cheeses. Bake at
350F for 1 hour. 205 calories per serving with 10.1 grams fat.
