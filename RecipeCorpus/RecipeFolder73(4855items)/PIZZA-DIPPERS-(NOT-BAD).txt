Title: PIZZA DIPPERS (NOT BAD)
Categories: Appetizers, Sausage, Shara
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Italian sausage
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1/3 c  Cold water
2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
2/3 c  Shortening
Brown sausage, crumbling, and drain.  Combine flour, cheese, salt.
Cut in shortening until mixture resembles peas. Stir in the sausage.
Add water a bit at a time until the mixture can be shaped into balls.
Bake 10-12 minutes @ 450~.  Serve with toothpicks and PIZZA SAUCE.
