Title: PARTY PUNCH
Categories: Punch, Apples
Yield: 30 Servings
1 c  Orange juice
2 qt Gingerale; chilled
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 pt Cranberry juice cocktail
1 c  Pineapple juice
1 pt Apple cider
Combine all ingredients except gingerale; mix well. Just before
serving, pour over ice in punch bowl; add gingerale. serves about 30
