Title: OLD-FASHIONED BUTTER DROP COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 3/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 375F. Sift together the flour, baking powder, and
baking soda.  Set aside Cream together teh butte and sugar until very
light. Add egg and blend well; mix in vanilla. Gradually add dry
mixture to creamed mixture.  Slowly blend in milk. Drop cookies on a
greased cookie sheet, placing each 2" apart. Bake 8 to 10 minutes.

Source: Dining In Shared by: Sharon Herrington
