Title: PISTACHIO FLUFFY FROSTING
Categories: Frostings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Milk; cold
1 sm Pistachio instant pudding
8 oz Cool Whip
Pour milk into deep, narrow-bottom bowl and add Cool Whip & pudding.
Beat slowly to blend. Gradually increase beating speed to high & whip
until mixture will form soft peaks, 4-6 minutes. 3 cups (Keep
refrigerated)
