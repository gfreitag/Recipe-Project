Title: ORANGE-PINEAPPLE BREAKFAST SHAKE
Categories: Beverages, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Unsweetened pineapple juice
1/2 c  Plain yogurt
1 ts Honey
In a blender, combine juices, yogurt, and honey. Blend until
ingredients are combined well. Pour the shake into a glass, and
garnish with an orange twist or fresh mint sprig, if desired.

As printed in American Baby-April 1997
