Title: PIZZA HOT DOGS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Hot Dog buns
4    Hot Dogs
12    Slices mozzarella cheese
1    Jar pizza sauce
Grated parmesan cheese
Split hot dogs and hot dog buns lengthwise.  Place buns on a cookie
sheet. Sprinkle mozzarela cheese over buns. Place split hot dog on
each bun. Spread pizza sauce over the hotdogs, sprinkle with parmesan
cheese and broil until cheese bubbles and the hot dogs are hot.
