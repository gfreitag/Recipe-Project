Title: PAUL PRUDHOMME'S CAJUN MEAT SEASONING
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Salt
3/4 c  Granulated garlic
3/4 c  Black pepper
1/2 c  Cayenne pepper
1/4 c  Ground cumin
In bowl combine salt, garlic, black pepper, cayenne and cumin. Mix
thoroughly. Store in large glass jar with tight fitting lid. Keeps
indefinitely. Makes 3
3/4    cups.
