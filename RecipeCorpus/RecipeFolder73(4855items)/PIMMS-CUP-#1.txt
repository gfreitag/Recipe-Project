Title: PIMMS CUP #1
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 4 Servings
-JUDI M. PHELPS
3 oz Pimm's No.1
1    Twist of cucumber peel
1 sl Lemon
Few borage leaves
Plenty of ice
Fizzy lemonade
1 oz London Dry or Plymouth gin
(optional)
1 ts Cointreau (optional)
Stir and serve in a half or one-pint glass mug, then sit back and
enjoy!
