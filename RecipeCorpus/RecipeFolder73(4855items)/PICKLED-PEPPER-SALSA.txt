Title: PICKLED PEPPER SALSA
Categories: Mexican, Sauces
Yield: 5 Servings
5 c  Tomatoes,seeded,peeled>>>
Finely chopped
1/2 c  Pickled jalapenos including>
Some pickling juice
1    Onion, finely chopped
1    Green onion,finely chopped
2 ts Cilantro, finely chopped
Chop each ingredient by hand, or separately in food processor.
Combine all the ingredients, adding salt to taste. Allow to stand for
abt. 1 hr to allow flavors to blend. Stir well before serving. Yield:
Approx. 5 cups
