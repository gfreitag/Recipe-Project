Title: PINEAPPLE GLAZED YAMS
Categories: Vegetarian, Side dish
Yield: 6 Servings
4 lg Yams
1/2 c  Brown sugar or natural
--sweetener
1 ts Orange rind; grated
2 ts Cornstarch
4 tb Margarine, non-dairy
1 ts Salt
1 c  Pineapple; crushed
Peel the yams, slice them thickly, and boil them in salted water for
about 10 minutes. Melt the margarine in a large, heavy skillet and
stir in the brown sugar, salt, orange rind and pineapple.

Dissolve the cornstarch in about 2 Tbs of cold water and stir it
into the pineapple mixture. When the mixture is well blended and
thickened, add the yams and turn them over until all the slices are
coated evenly. Continue cooking over a medium flame until the yams
are quite tender and well-glazed. Serve hot.
Serves 6.

Recipe by Susan D. Gregory
