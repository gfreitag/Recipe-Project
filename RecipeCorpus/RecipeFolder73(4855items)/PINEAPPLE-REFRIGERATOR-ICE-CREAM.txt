Title: PINEAPPLE REFRIGERATOR ICE CREAM
Categories: Desserts, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Cream
2 c  Pineapple; crushed
Or
2 c  Mixed fruit
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ea Egg whites
1 ts Vanilla
Whip cream, add sugar, fruit and fold in egg whites last. Note: Whip
the egg whites until stiff BEFORE folding in. Source: Mrs. Amy
Morrison, Durthick Grange, Licking County, OH
