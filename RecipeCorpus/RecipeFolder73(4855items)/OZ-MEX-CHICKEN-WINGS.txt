Title: OZ-MEX CHICKEN WINGS
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
3 tb Vegetable oil
1 ts Ground  red  chile powder, s
2 ts Paprika
1 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Crushed dried oregano
3 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
2 lb Chicken wings; cut at the jo
Recipe by: Sally Hammond Place all the ingredients, except the chicken
wings, in a large glass dish bowl and mix well. Add the chicken and
turn to coat with the marinade. Cov

Preheat the oven to 375 F.

Drain the chicken wings and arrange in a large baking tray.

Bake 30-40 minutes or  until the wings are tender and the juices run
clear when pierced at the thickest part.
