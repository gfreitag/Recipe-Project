Title: ORANGE CHICKEN - BSNX01A
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
Talkofsky-BSNX01A
Orange rind -- grated
4    Chicken Breast Cutlets
1 tb Corn starch (potato starch-
1 c  Orange juice
Brown sugar (syrup-Passover)
1/4  cup

Cook all ingredients for the sauce over medium heat, stirring
constantly until thick and glossy.  Pour sauce over frozen chicken
breast cutlets, bake uncovered approximately 35 minutes at 350~. This
sauce may also be used for broiled or microwaved cutlets.

From bag of Empire Individualy Quick Frozen Chicken Breast Cutlets.
