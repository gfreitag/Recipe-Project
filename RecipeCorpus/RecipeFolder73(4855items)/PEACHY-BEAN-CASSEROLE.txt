Title: PEACHY BEAN CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Brown sugar beans(16oz)
1/4 c  Peach preserves
1/4 ts Soy sauce
2 tb Tomato catsup
2 tb Onion,chopped
4    Chicken thighs or breasts
1. Combine beans, catsup, preserves, onion and soy sauce in a
10x6x2-inch baking dish.
2. To coat chicken pieces evenly with sauce, nestle chicken in bean
mixture, skin-side down; then turn pieces skin-side up.
3. Cover and bake in preheated 350'F. oven 1 hour.
4. Uncover and bake 30 minutes longer, basting chicken with sauce
occasionally.
