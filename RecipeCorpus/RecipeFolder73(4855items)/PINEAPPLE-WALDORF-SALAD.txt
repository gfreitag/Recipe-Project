Title: PINEAPPLE WALDORF SALAD
Categories: Salads, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn (20 oz) can pineapple
-tidbits
1    Red apple, chunked
1    Tart apple, chunked
1 1/2 c  Walnuts
1 c  Chopped celery
3/4 c  Plain yogurt
3 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Lemon peel
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Cinnamon
Drain pineapple. Combine with apples, walnuts and celery. Combine
remaining ingredients and stir into pineapple mixture. Chill.
