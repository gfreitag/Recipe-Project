Title: PEAR HONEY
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Pears
2    Apples
1    Orange
1 1/2 lb Sugar
Pare apples and pears.  Remove seeds.  Peel orange. Remove seeds.
Grind pears, apples, and orange.  Add sugar and cook 20 minutes,
stirring frequently.  Add grated orange rind. Cook until thick. Elma
Hinkle, Chilo, OH.
