Title: PLANTATION STEW (NBHX19B) 1I21
Categories: Meats, Beef, American
Yield: 7 Servings
3 1/2 lb Lamb-stew meat
1/2 c  Olive oil
6 ea Potatoes-cubed
2 ea Red bell peppers,diced
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Thyme
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Orange juice
1 tb Lea & perrins
1 tb Parsly-chopped
1/4 c  Sherry
2 qt Chicken stock
Heat oil and saute lamb till brown on all sides add the stock and
simmer for 2 hours add the remaining ingredients,expect for the shery
simmer for 1/2 hour more,add the sherry just before serving 09/19
