Title: PISTACHIO CAKE W/CREAM CHEESE FROSTING -XWCG8
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 8 Servings
1 pk White cake mix
5 x  Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Oil
2 pk 4oz. instant pistachio pudd.
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts (Optional)
Combine all ingredients. Bake at 350 for 45-60 minutes in greased
tube pan. Remove from oven; let cool and remove from pan. CREAM
CHEESE FROSTING: 3 tsp butter or marg., softened; 6 oz. cream cheese,
softened; 1 1/4 cups powdered sugar; 1/4 tsp vanilla.  Combine all
ingredients and frost cooled cake.
