Title: PERSIMMON PUDDING 5
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Egg
3 oz Butter
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Persimmon pulp
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Milk
Cream butter and sugar, add egg, blend well. Add dry ingredients and
milk. Stir in persimmon pulp. Pour into greased pan (9x13) bake 1
hour at 325 degrees.
