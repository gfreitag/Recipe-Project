Title: PLAIN FRIED RICE
Categories: Side dish, Thai, Rice, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
3 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Onion, chopped
1 tb Garlic, chopped
5 c  Cooked long-grain rice
1 c  Tofu, diced
3 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts White pepper
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
1 tb Cilantro leaves, chopped
1 c  Cucumber, sliced
2 tb Red & green chilies, chopped
3 ea Green onions, chopped
1 ea Lime, cut into wedges
Heat the oil in a wok over medium-high heat.  Add the onion &
stir-fry for 3 minutes.  Add the garlic & stir-fry until both are
golden. Add the rice & the tofu & stir-fry for 3 minutes. Sprinkle
with soy sauce & white pepper. Remove from heat & place in a serving
dish surrounded with garnishes.
