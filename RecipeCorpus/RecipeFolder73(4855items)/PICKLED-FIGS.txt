Title: PICKLED FIGS
Categories: Pickles
Yield: 10 Pints
7 lb Figs
4 lb Sugar
1 pt Vinegar
1 pt ;Water
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Allspice
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Mace
Blanch figs 5 to 10 minutes. Heat sugar and water and add vinegar and
spices (either ground or whole). Boil 5 minutes. Remove spice bag.
Add figs and cook until the fruit can be pierced with a toothpick and
until penetration of syrup is complete. (This may take a half hour at
a slow boil.) Fill sterilized jars and seal at once.
