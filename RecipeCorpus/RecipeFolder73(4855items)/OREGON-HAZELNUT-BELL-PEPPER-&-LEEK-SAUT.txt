Title: OREGON HAZELNUT BELL PEPPER & LEEK SAUT
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Red bell peppers
2    Green bell peppers
2    Leeks
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Roasted & chopped hazelnuts
-(Oregon hazelnuts)
Salt & pepper to taste
Yield: 4 to 6 servings.

Cut the peppers into thin strips. Slice the leeks on the diagonal into
1/2-inch pieces. Melt butter in a medium saut pan. Add the peppers
and the leeks; cook over medium heat for a minute or two. Add
hazelnuts, and continue cooking for another five minutes. Add salt
and pepper, to taste.
