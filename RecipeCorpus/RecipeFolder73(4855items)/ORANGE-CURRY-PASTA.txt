Title: ORANGE CURRY PASTA
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  All purpose flour
1/4 c  Frozen orange juice
-- concentrate, thawed
1 tb Curry powder
1 pn Salt
1 pn Pepper
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients until dough forms a ball.
Turn dough out onto a floured board & knead until smooth & pliable
like a firm bread dough. Place dough in a bowl & cover with plastic
wrap & let rest for at least 30 minutes.  Shape & cook dough as
desired.
