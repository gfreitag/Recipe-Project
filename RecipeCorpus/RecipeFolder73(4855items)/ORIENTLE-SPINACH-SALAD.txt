Title: ORIENTLE SPINACH SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Bunch of spinach
1 cn Water crestnuts(sliced)
1 cn Bean sprouts(drained)
2    Hard boiled eggs
1/4 lb Bacon (fried and crumbled)
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Oil
1/8 c  White vinegar
1/2 c  Med. onion
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
3 ts Ketchup
Blend dressing one day in advance.
