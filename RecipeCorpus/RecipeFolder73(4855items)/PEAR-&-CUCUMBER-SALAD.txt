Title: PEAR & CUCUMBER SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Fruits
Yield: 2 Servings
2 lg Pears; peeled and sliced
4 ts Lemon juice
1 tb Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
1    Cucumber; unpeeled
Lettuce
Mayonnaise; or French Drssng
Combine pears, lemon juice, sugar, and salt.  Slice cucumber paper
thin, and toss lightly with pears. Arrange on crisp lettuce; serve
with mayonnaise.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy
Coleman.
