Title: PEANUT SAUCE (RICKERT)
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
14 oz Cans coconut milk
1/2 tb Red curry paste.
1 tb Chili paste in bean oil
2 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
3/4 c  Peanut butter
Heat coconut milk until boiling.   Add chili and curry pastes, and
blend. Boil again, and add the rest of the ingredients and mix
thoroughly. Cook for 5 minutes and remove from heat. This is often
served with sate; its also nice on toast or on fresh apple slices.
