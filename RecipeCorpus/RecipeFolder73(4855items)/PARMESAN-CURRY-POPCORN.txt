Title: PARMESAN-CURRY POPCORN
Categories: Appetizers, Crocker
Yield: 12 Cups
1/2 c  Margarine or butter, melted
1/3 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Curry powder
12 c  Popcorn (Already Popped)
Mix margarine, cheese, salt and curry powder. Pour over Popcorn;
toss. 12 cups popcorn;170 calories per cup.

Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
