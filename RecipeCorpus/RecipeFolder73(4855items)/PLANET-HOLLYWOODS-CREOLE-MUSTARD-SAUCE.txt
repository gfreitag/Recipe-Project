Title: PLANET HOLLYWOODS CREOLE MUSTARD SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Cup
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Creole style mustard or
DiJon mustard
1 tb Yellow mustard
1 tb Horseradish
1/2 ts Cider vinegar
ds Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Red wine vinegar
1 ts Water
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Green onions, sliced into
1/4" pieces
1 tb Crushed garlic in oil
1 ts Finely chopped green peppers
1 ts Finely chopped celery
1 ts Finely chopped onion
mix all ingredients together.

Serve with Planet Hollywood Chicken.
