Title: PEANUT BUTTER SCOTCH BARS...MAGAZINE
Categories: Party, Candies, Desserts
Yield: 3 Servings
1 c  Crunchy peanut butter
3/4 c  Margarine
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Domino liquid brown sugar
3 ea Eggs
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Rolled oats
1/2 ts Salt
6 oz Butterscotch morsels
DIRECTIONS ~------------------------------------------------------
~--- COMBINE PEANUT BUTTER, MARGARINE, VANILLA AND LIQUID BROWN
SUGAR. MIX UNTIL SMOOTH. BEAT IN EGGS. STIR IN REMAINING INGREDIENTS.
MIX WELL. SPOON BATTER INTO A GREASED 13X9X2 INCH BAKING PAN. SPREAD
SMOOTH. BAKE IN A 350F OVEN 35 MINUTES OR UNTIL DONE. MAKES 36
SQUARES.
