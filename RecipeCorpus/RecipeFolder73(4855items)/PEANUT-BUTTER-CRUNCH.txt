Title: PEANUT BUTTER CRUNCH
Categories: Candies, Snacks, Microwave
Yield: 24 Servings
1 c  Light corn syrup
1 c  Granulated sugar
12 oz Crunchy peanut butter
6 c  Crisp rice cereal
In a 2 quart microwavable container, stir together syrup and sugar;
cook on High 100% power about 1 1/2 to 2 minutes, until sugar is
dissolved. Stir in peanut butter until well blended. Mix in cereal.
Pour into an 8 x 12 inch butter pan.  When mixture is cool, cut into
squares.
