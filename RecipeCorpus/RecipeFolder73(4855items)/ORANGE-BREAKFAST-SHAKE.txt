Title: ORANGE BREAKFAST SHAKE
Categories: Snacks, Syd's book, Breakfast
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  Fresh Orange Juice
1/2 c  Skim Milk
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Vanilla Extract
Place all ingredients in a blender bowl and process until smooth.
Pour into glasses and serve.  93 calories per serving. From: Syd's
Cookbook.
