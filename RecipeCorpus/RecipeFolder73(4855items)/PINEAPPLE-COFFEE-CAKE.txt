Title: PINEAPPLE COFFEE CAKE
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 9 Servings
1    Quick coffee cake recipe
1/2 c  Flour
1/3 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Pineapple tidbits*
*Drained and cut in half. Spread Quick Coffee Cake batter into a 9 by
9 by 2-inch pan, bottom rubbed with shortening. Make topping by
combining flour, sugar, cinnamon, and butter. Spread pineapple over
batter. Sprinkle topping over pineapple.  Bake in a 400 F. oven about
25 minutes. Yield: One 9-inch square cake, about 9 servings
