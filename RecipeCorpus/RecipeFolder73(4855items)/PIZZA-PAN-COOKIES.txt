Title: PIZZA PAN COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 24 Servings
1 c  BUTTER
3/4 c  SUGAR
3/4 c  PACKED BROWN SUGAR
1 ts VANILLA
2 1/4 c  FLOUR
1/4 ts SALT
1 ts BAKING SODA
12 oz CHOCOLATE CHIPS
2 x  EGGS
LIGHTLY GREASE 2 PIZZA PANS ( 12 INCH ). PREHEAT OVEN TO 375 DEG. F.
CREAM SUGARS, BUTTER AND VANILLA IN A MIXER BOWL. ADD EGGS AND MIX.
COMBINE SALT, FLOUR AND BAKING SODA IN A SMALL BOWL. ADD TO CREAMED
MIXTURE. STIR IN CHIPS. ADD NUTS IF DESIRED. DIVIDE DOUGH IN TWO
PARTS. PRESS EACH PART INTO A PIZZA PAN EVENLY. BAKE AT 375 DEG F.
FOR 20 MINUTES.
