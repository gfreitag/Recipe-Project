Title: ORANGE CHICKEN (CHAT)
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Chicken Portions
1    Onion, Peeled and Thinly
-Sliced
1/2 ts Dried Rosemary
1/2 ts Ground Ginger
pn Turmeric or Paprika
1 pt Unsweetened Orange Juice
2 ts Orange Marmalade
MMMMM--------------------------TO SERVE-------------------------------
Boiled Rice, Noodles or
-Potatoes.
Preheat oven to 180C, 350F, Gas Mark 4.

Put chicken portions into casserole dish. Sprinkle over onion,
rosemary, ginger and turmeric or paprika. Pour over orange juice and
stir in marmalade.

Cover and cook for about 45 minutes until the chicken is tender.
Serve with boiled rice, noodles or potatoes.

Source: CHAT Magazine
