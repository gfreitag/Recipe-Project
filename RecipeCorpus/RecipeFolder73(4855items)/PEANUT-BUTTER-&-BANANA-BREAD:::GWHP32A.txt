Title: PEANUT BUTTER & BANANA BREAD:::GWHP32A
Categories: Breads, Dak
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Yeast
2 1/2 c  Better for Bread flour
1/2 c  Wheat flour
3 tb Gluten
2 tb Brown sugar; packed
1 ts Salt
1 ea Banana;6 oz.peeled
1 x  -sliced directly into
1 x  -the inner pan
3/4 c  Peanut butter, creamy or chu
1 ea Egg
1 c  Very warm water
Add all ingredients into the pan in the order listed. Select white
bread anpush "Start." Childhood's favorite sandwich auto-baked into a
funtastic bread - for a special treat, spread toasted slices with a
mixture of 1 part chocolate sauce to 2 parts peanut butter. Shared by
Barb Day
