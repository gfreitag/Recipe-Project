Title: OLIVE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Microwave, Condiments, Dairy
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 tb All-pourpose flour
1 c  Milk
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sliced pimiento-stuffed
Green olives
1. Melt butter in a 1-quart glass measuring pitcher in microwave oven
(about 30 secones at High).  Add flour and blend to a smooth paste.
Add milk gradually, stirring constantly.

2.  Cook uncovered in microwave oven 4 minutes at High, or until
thickened; stir vigorously after 2 minutes, then every 30 seconds.

Yield: About 1 cup.
