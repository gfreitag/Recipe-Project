Title: PEPPY SEA-FOOD SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Chili sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Prepared horseradish
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 dr Tabasco or other bottled hot
Pepper sauce
Combine ingredients and chill thoroughly.  Serve with shrimp. Makes
about 1/2 cup.

Source:  BH & G Barbecue Book, 1965
