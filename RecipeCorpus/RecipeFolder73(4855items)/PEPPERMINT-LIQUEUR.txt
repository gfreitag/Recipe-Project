Title: PEPPERMINT LIQUEUR
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 1 Quart
2 ts Peppermint extract(or 3 ts)
3 c  Vodka
1 c  Sugar syrup (see recipe)
Combine all ingredients and stir. Let stand for 2 weeks. Use 3
teaspoons of extract for a stronger mint taste and additional sugar
syrup for a sweeter thicker liqueur. Yield: 1 quart Container: Quart
jar
