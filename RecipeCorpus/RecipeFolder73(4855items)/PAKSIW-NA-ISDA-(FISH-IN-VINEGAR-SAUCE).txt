Title: PAKSIW NA ISDA (FISH IN VINEGAR SAUCE)
Categories: Fish, Phillipines
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM---------------------------YIELD--------------------------------
2 lb Fish slices (rainbow trout,
-salmon
- milkfish)
1/2 c  Vinegar
3 ea - 4 clove garlic; minced
2 ts Ginger; sliced
1 ea Hot chili pepper; optional
1/4 c  Water
Salt and pepper to taste
Combine all ingredients together in a glass cooking pan or a stainless
steel pan.  Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce heat to
medium low. Cover and simmer for 10 - 15 minutes. Transfer to a
serving dish. Serves 4.
