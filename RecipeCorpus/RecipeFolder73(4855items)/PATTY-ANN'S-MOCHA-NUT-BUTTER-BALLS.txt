Title: PATTY ANN'S MOCHA NUT BUTTER BALLS
Categories: Christmas, Cookies
1 c  Butter -- softened
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Granulated Sugar
2 ts Vanilla Extract
2 ts Instant Coffee
1/4 c  Cocoa Powder
1 3/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Walnuts Or Pecans -- finely
chopped
:

Oven 325=B0 F.

Cream butter, sugar, and vanilla until light.  Add next four
ingredients. Mix well.  Add nuts.  Shape in 1" balls on ungreased
cookie sheets. Bake at 325=B0 for about 17-20 minutes. Roll in 4x
sugar while warm.
