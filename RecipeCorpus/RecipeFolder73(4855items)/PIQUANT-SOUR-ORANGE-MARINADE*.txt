Title: PIQUANT SOUR ORANGE MARINADE*
Categories: Marinades, Sauces
Yield: 15 Servings
1 c  Olive oil
4    Garlic cloves finely minced
1/2 ts Salt
1 t  Black pepper
1 c  Juice & pulp of sour oranges
1 t  Ground oregano
1 t  Basil fresh & chopped
3 T  Chopped onion
Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Pour over pork, venison, or poultry.
Cover and refrigerate for 12-24 hours. When cooking the meat use this
as a basting suace as well.
