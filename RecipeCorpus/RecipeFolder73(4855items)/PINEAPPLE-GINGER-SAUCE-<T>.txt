Title: PINEAPPLE GINGER SAUCE <T>
Categories: Digest, August
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Minced garlic
1 ts Minced ginger
1 c  No-sugar-added pineapple
Juice
1/4 c  Low sodium tamari or soy
Sauce
1 tb Cornstarch dissolved in 1
tb Cold water
Saute garlic and ginger in a little water in a medium non-stick
skillet. After about 3 minutes add juice and soysauce. Heat on medium
high until it boils, then stir in cornstarch. Heat until it gets to
your desired consistency.
