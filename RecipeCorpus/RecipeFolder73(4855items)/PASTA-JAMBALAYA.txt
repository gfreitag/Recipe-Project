Title: PASTA JAMBALAYA
Categories: Pasta, Casseroles, Wrv
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Chicken; diced
1/2 lb Tasso or smoked sausage;chop
1/4 c  Green pepper; chopped
1/4 c  Red onion; chopped
1 tb Minced garlic
x  Creole seasoning
1/4    Stick butter
x  Grated provolone
x  Grated gouda
1 lb Three-colored pasta; cooked
Saute meats in oil. Add pepper, onion, garlic, seasoning and butter.
Heat. Toss with pasta and serve with grated cheeses.
