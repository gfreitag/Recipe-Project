Title: PEACH & POPPY SEED MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Fruits
Yield: 12 Servings
2/3 c  Peach; pur√©ed*
1 ts Baking soda
10 T  Butter; softened
1 c  Sugar
2    Egg
1 1/4 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
3 T  Poppy seeds
*use "Junior" baby food peaches if you can't get good fresh peaches
Preheat oven to 350 degrees (F).  Grease muffin tins for a dozen
muffins. Stir baking soda into pureed peaches.  They will foam up.
Cream butter with sugar.

When mixture is smooth, add eggs, one at a time.  Alternately add
flour and peach puree, then salt, vanilla extract, and poppy seeds.
Fill each muffin cup nearly to the top.  Bake 20 to 25 minutes or
until a toothpick or cake tester inserted in one of the muffins comes
out clean. Makes 12 large muffins.
