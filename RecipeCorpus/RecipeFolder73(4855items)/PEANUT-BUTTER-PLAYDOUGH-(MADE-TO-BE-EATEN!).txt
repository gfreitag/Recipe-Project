Title: PEANUT BUTTER PLAYDOUGH (MADE TO BE EATEN!)
Categories: Kids, Play dough, Crafts
Yield: 1 Batch
12 oz Peanut butter
6 tb Honey
1/2 c  (to 1 1/2 cups) nonfat dry
-milk powder
Combine all ingredients, add more dry milk if needed to keep it
from being sticky.
