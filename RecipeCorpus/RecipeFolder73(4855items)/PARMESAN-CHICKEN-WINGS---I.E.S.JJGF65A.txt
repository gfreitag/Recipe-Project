Title: PARMESAN CHICKEN WINGS   I.E.S.JJGF65A
Categories: Chicken, Italian, Cheese, Wings, Appetizers
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PHILLY.INQUIRER---------------------------
MMMMM----------------------NATHALIE DUPREE---------------------------
1 c  Freshly grated parmesan ches
1 tb Heaping,finely chopped fresh
1    Parsley
1 tb Fresh,oregano or marjam
Choppedor 1 ts.of either
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Fresh ground pepper
2 lb Chicken wings cut up
1/2 c  Butter melted
Preheat oven to 375 deg.Mix cheese,parsley,oregano,salt and pepper
together and place on a dry tray.Dip each piece of chicken into the
butter,then roll in the cheese mixture,coating well.Place on a greased
baking sheet.Bake for 45 minutes,turning when brown.Wings will freeze
well.Thaw in refrigerator,and heat in a 375 deg. oven.Makes 4 main
course or 8 appetizer servings...
