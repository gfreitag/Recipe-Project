Title: ORANGE WAKAME SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
3 tb EDEN Wakame Flakes
1/4 c  EDEN Mirin
1 1/2 ts LIMA Sea Salt
1    Cucumber; thinly sliced
2    Oranges or tangerines
-- peeled and sliced
5    Red radishes
-- cut in half and sliced
1    Green onion; sliced
2 tb Sesame seeds, toasted
-- (use black sesame seeds
-- if available)
2 tb EDEN Brown Rice Vinegar
Soak wakame in 2 cups of hot water for 10 minutes. Drain and add
remaining ingredients. Chill and serve.

Prep Time. 10 minutes Yield: 4 servings
