Title: PARMESAN MASHED POTATOES
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/4 ts EACH salt and pepper
2 2/3 c  Frozen mashed potatoes
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Freshly grated parmesan
Cheese
Heat milk, salt and pepper in a medium saucepan over medium heat until
steaming hot, about 6 minutes.

Add potatoes and cook, stirring constantly, until smooth and hot,
about 6 minutes.  Add butter and cheese; stir until butter melts.
