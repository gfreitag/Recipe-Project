Title: ORANGE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Party, Low-cal
Yield: 8 Servings
1    Box tapioca pudding
2 cn Mandarin oranges, drained
3 3/4    Box instant vanilla pudding
9 oz Container Cool Whip
1 - 3 oz box orange gelatin Mix first four ingredients together and
boil for 10 minutes. Cool completely. Add oranges and Cool Whip. Pour
into bowl and refrigerate. Sounds easy enough, yes? Talk to ya later.
