Title: PAM'S RICE (SIDE DISH)
Categories: Side dish, Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 ts Thyme
3/4 c  Chopped onion
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Chopped celery
1/2 ts Finnes herbs
1 c  Uncooked long-grain rice
1 ts Salt
1    Envelope Lipton chicken
-noodle soup mix
2 1/2 c  Water
Brown celery, onion, and rice in butter (15-20 min) on med heat. Stir
in all other ingredients (can keep in frying pan if enough room or
use a sauce pan), cover, simmer 15 min or until moisture is soaked
up. Remove from heat, toss with spoon, cover and let stand 5-10 min.
