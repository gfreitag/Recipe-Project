Title: PEANUT BUTTER MARINATED RIBS
Categories: Meats, Barbecue, Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Soy sauce
3 tb Honey
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Minced ginger
1 ts Minced garlic
1/8 ts Crushed red pepper
1 lb Pork ribs
In bowl, combine peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, lemon juice, ginger,
garlic and red pepper; stir to blend. Place ribs is shallow baking
pan; pour marinade evenly over meat. Cover and refrigerate 6 to 8
hours, turning meat once. Prepare grill for cooking. Wipe excess
marinade from meat. Cook turning frequently, 45-60 minutes. Source:
Woman's Day Meals in Minutes. Typed in MMFormat by Cindy Hartlin.
