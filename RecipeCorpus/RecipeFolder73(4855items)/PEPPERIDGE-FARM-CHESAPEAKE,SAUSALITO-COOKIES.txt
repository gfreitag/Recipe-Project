Title: PEPPERIDGE FARM CHESAPEAKE/SAUSALITO COOKIES
Categories: Desserts, Cookies, Cake mix
E  *****
Yield: 1 Servings

simply follow the recipe for the keebler soft batch cookie with these
exceptons: 1. omit the water 2. omit the molasses 3. add 3 cups of
chopped pecans or if you are making sausalito cookies use macadamia
nuts. Bake at the same temp but for 10-11 minutes rather than 8
minutes. This will make the cookies crisper.
