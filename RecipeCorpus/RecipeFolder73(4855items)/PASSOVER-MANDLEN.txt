Title: PASSOVER MANDLEN
Categories: Kosher, Breads, Passover
Yield: 1 Servings
3    Eggs, beaten
2/3 c  Matzo cake meal
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 ts Potato flour
Beat eggs, cake meal, potato floour and salt.  Knead well. Roll out on
floured board to 1/4 inch thickness. Cut into 1/4 inch cubes. Fry in
deep fat heated to 375 F. until brown. Drain--serve in soup.
