Title: ONION & TOMATO RAITA
Categories: Indian, Sauces and
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz Plain Yogurt
1 sm Onion
1/2 ts Salt
1 sm Tomato
1/2 ts Chat masala (opt)
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
1/4 c  Milk
1. Beat yogurt and milk till smooth 2. Chop onion and tomatoes and
add to Yogurt. 3. Add salt and pepper and sprinkle the chat masala
over, and serve.
