Title: PESTO 2
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Packed fresh basil leaves
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1/4 c  Pine nuts
1 1/4 c  Extra-virgin olive oil
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1/2 c  Romano cheese
3/4 ts Salt
Place basil and garlic in a blender or food processor and puree. Add
oil and mix thoroughly, then blend in parmesan and romano cheeses.
Fold in pine nuts with a spatula.

Enjoy!

Chris
