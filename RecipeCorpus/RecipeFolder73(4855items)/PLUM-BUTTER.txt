Title: PLUM BUTTER
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
Text Only
Pare and quarter apples.  Remove seeds.  Combine with an equal amount
of plums.  cover with water and cook until fruit is soft. Rub through
a sieve. Add 1/2 as much sugar as fruit pulp. Simmer slowly, stirring
frequently, until thick. The Household Searchlight
