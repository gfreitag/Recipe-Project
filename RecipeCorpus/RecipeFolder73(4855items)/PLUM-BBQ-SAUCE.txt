Title: PLUM BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Onion -- choppewd
1/4 c  Chili sauce
16    1/2 oz
Pitted, -- fine chop
1/4 c  Butter
2 ts Dijon mustard
1    6 oz
Purple plums, drained,
cn Frozen lemonade
Cook  onions in butter in 2 quart pan stirring occasionally, until
tender, about 2 min.  Stir in remaining ingredients. Heat to boiling,
reduce to low heat. Simmer uncovered 15 min. stirring occasionally.
Makes 2 cups.
