Title: OREO COOKIE BREAD
Categories: Breads, Abm, Misc
Yield: 14 Servings
1 lb. loaf
3/4 c  Milk
1    Egg
3 tb Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
3/4 c  Oreo cookies,crushed
1 1/2 lb loaf
1 c  Milk
1 1/2    Eggs
5 tb Butter
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
3 c  Bread flour
2 1/2 ts Yeast
1 c  Oreo cookies,crushed
Bake according to manufacturer's instructions, adding the Oreos
during the raisin-bread cycle, or five minutes before the final
kneading is finished.
