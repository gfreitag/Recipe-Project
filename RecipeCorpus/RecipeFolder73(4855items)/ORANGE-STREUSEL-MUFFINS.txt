Title: ORANGE STREUSEL MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 3/4 c  All purpose flour
1/4 c  Sugar
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg
3/4 c  Milk
1/3 c  Oil
1/4 c  Orange marmalade
MMMMM----------------------STREUSEL TOPPING---------------------------
2 tb All purpose flour
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Softened butter
1 ts Ground cinnamon
2 tb Chopped pecans
Grease muffin cups or line with paper baking cups. Preheat oven to 400
degrees.  Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the
center. In another mixing bowl combine egg, oil and milk. Add all at
once to the flour mixture stirring just until moistened. Batter will
be slightly lumpy.  Spoon about 1 tablespoon of the mixture into
muffin pan and top with 1 teaspoon of marmalade. Spoon another
tablespoon of batter on top of the marmalade.  Sprinkle a tablespoon
of streusel topping over this. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 to 25
minutes.
