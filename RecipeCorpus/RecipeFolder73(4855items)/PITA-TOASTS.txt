Title: PITA TOASTS
Categories: Breads, Appetizers
Yield: 12 Servings
4    Pita bread rounds
1/4 c  Butter, softened
Take 4 rounds of pita bread and spread them with 1/4 c softened
butter. Cut each round in half and cut each half into 8 triangles.
Place on greased baking sheet. Bake at 350F (180C) for 15 minutes or
until browned and crisp. Makes 64 triangles.
