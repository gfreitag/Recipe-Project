Title: PIZZA SAUCE #3
Categories: Pizza, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Tomato paste
1/4 pt Marjoram
3 c  Water
1/4 pt Oregano
3 pt Cooking oil
1/2 pt Brown sugar
1/4 c  Raw onions (chopped)
1 pt Black pepper
1    Clove garlic (chopped)
1 pt Parmesan cheese (grated)
Combine ingredients in a 1 qt sauce pan and cook for 30-40 minutes.
Makes enough sauce for 4 12" Pizzas.
