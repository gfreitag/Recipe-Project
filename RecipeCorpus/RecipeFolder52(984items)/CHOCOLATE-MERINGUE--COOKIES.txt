Title: CHOCOLATE MERINGUE  COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Diabetic
Yield: 30 Cookies
2    Egg whites
3 pk Sweet and low
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Alba chocolate dry skim milk
Beat whites stiff gradually adding sweetener and vanilla. Carefully
fold in ( dry ) milk. Drop by teaspoonful onto nonstick baking sheet.
Bake 275 for 25 minutes or crisp. Submitted By LIR119@DELPHI.COM On
