Title: CHOCOLATE PASSION DESSERT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 16 Servings
24    Fudge brownies
2 c  Sliced strawberries
2    Bananas
2    Tubs (8 oz ea) Cool Whip
Chocolate Non-Dairy Topping
-thawed
1. Cute brownies into 1/2 inch cubes. 2. Layer in a 3 quart serving
bowl; 1/2 of the brownies, 1 cup of the strawberries, 1 sliced
banana, and 1 tub of the Cool Whip Chocolate Whipped topping. 3.
Repeat layers. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
