Title: CHOCOLATE COCONUT BREAD
Categories: Brd. machin
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Heavy cream
1 1/2    Eggs
3 tb Fruit juice concentrate
3 tb Honey
1 1/2 tb Unsweetened cocoa
1/3 ts Salt
1/3 c  Coconut flakes
3 tb Vital gluten; optional
3/4 c  Wheat flakes
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1/3 c  Chopped nuts
Recipe by: Donna German - The Bread Machine Cookbook III Use
Raisin/Mix Cycle and add Chocolate Chips and Chopped nuts when
indicated; or 5 minutes prior to the end of the second kneading cycle
