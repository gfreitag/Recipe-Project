Title: CHOCOLATE ALMOND TORTE WITH STRAWBERRY SAUCE
Categories: Passover, Desserts, Fruits, Chocolate, Nuts
Yield: 16 Servings
-SHERI KONOPKO
1 1/4 c  Sugar; (Domino)
1 c  Margarine; softened
4    Eggs; separated
2 1/4 c  Almonds; sliced; blanched
7 oz Unsweetened chocolate
-preheat oven 300
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
2 c  Strawberries; frozen;
-unsweetened
1 c  Sugar
Line the bottom of 9 inch springform pan with wax paper; grease paper.

With blender; mix sugar and margarine, until fluffy. Add egg yolks.
In a food processor, process almonds and chocolate until finely
ground. Stir into sugar mixture.

In separate bowl beat egg whites to stiff peaks. Mix 1/3 whites into
batter. Gently fold in remaining whites. Spread batter into pan,
smoothing top. Bake about 1 1/2 hours,until top springs back. Cool
for 10 min. before releasing.
