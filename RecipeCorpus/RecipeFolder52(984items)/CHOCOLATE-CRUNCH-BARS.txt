Title: CHOCOLATE CRUNCH BARS
Categories: Bars, Chocolate
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Honey
1/3 c  Margarine
1/4 c  Cocoa Powder, Sweetened --
1 c  Granola
1 c  Dry Milk
1. Blend together everthing except the granola to a stiff dough. 2.
Knead in the granols, or roll the shaped bars in granola.

* Carob powder may be used also.
