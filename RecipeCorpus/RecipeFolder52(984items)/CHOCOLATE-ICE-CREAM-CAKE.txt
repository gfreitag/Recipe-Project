Title: CHOCOLATE ICE CREAM CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts, Bundt
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Chocolate cake mix
1 pt Chocolate ice cream, soften
3 lg Eggs
1 c  Water
Combine ingredients and beat for 4 min. Pour batter into a well
greased and floured 12-cup, 10-inch Bundt pan and bake at 350 degrees
for 45 min. Cooled cake can be frosted with coconut fudge frosting or
glazed with chocolate icing. Source: Unusual Old World and American
Recipes from Nordic Ware. Formatted Karen Adler FNGP13B.
