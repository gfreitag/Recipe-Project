Title: CHOCOLATE MINT ICE CREAM PIE
Categories: Ice cream, Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
1    Chocolate cookie crumb pie
- crust
1 qt Mint chocolate chip ice
- cream; softened
8    Thin mints
Whipped cream
Fill pie crust with softened mint chocolate chip ice cream. Re-freeze
until firm. Garnish before serving with whipped cream and thin
chocolate covered mints.
