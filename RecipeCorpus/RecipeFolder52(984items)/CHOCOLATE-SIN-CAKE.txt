Title: CHOCOLATE SIN CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
2    Sticks butter
1 1/2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
6 oz Pk semisweet chocolate chips
1 1/2 c  Cholcolate syrup
1 1/2 c  Applesauce
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Almond extract -- pure
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Chopped nuts -- optional
Preheat oven to 325^.  Cream butter and sugar well. Add eggs 1 at a
time. Beat until light and fluffy. Melt the chocolate morsels with
the syrup in a double boiler.  Add to thebutter and egg mixture.  Add
remaining ingredients, and blend well. Pour batter into a well
greased 10-inch bundt pan or a 10-inch round pan.  Bake for 1 hour or
until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Cool well before turning
out of pan. This cake is very moist and requires no frosting, but it
is delicious with whipped cream.
