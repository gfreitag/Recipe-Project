Title: CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES - SWEET LIFE
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Butter; softened
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Nutmeats; chopped
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
Recipe by: Sweet Life chocolate chip bag Preparation Time: 0:30 1.
Preheat oven to 375F 2. Cream butter. Gradually add sugars, beating
continuously, until
creamy. 3. Beat in egg and vanilla. 4. Sift together and stir in
flour, salt, and baking soda to butter
mixture. 5. Stir in nutmeats and chocolate chips. 6. Drop batter
from teaspoon, well apart, on a greased cookie
sheet.  Bake about 10 minutes.
