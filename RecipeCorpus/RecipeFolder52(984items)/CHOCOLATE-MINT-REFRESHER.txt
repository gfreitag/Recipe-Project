Title: CHOCOLATE MINT REFRESHER
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Cold freshly brewed coffee
4 tb Creme de cacao
4 tb White creme de menthe
1 1/2 c  Light cream (half-and-half)
Blend ingredients until frothy. Pour over ice.

Nutritional analysis per serving: 244 calories; 10.5 grams total fat
(28 percent of calories from fat); 33.4 milligrams cholesterol; 42.8
milligrams sodium.
