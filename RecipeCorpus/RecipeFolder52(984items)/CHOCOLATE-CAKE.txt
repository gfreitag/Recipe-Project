Title: CHOCOLATE CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 8 Servings
4 tb Cocoa
1/2 c  Boiling water
1/4 c  Margarine
1 1/2 c  Flour
3/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1/4 ts Salt
3 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
2/3 c  Milk or water
Cream margarine and sugar and add the egg. Alternately add the
flour,baking powder & salt mixture with the milk or water.Lastly add
the cocoa and hot water mixture which had been mixed at the start and
set aside.Bake 30 min. in a 350 oven.
