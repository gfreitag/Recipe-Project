Title: CURRY SOUP WITH BEANS
Categories: Soups, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 lb Dried lima beans, soaked
4 ea Whole cloves
4 ea Whole peppercorns
1 ea Bay leaf
1/2 ts Chile powder
Salt, to taste
2 ea Onions, chopped
1 c  Carrots, sliced
3 tb Oil
2 tb Curry powder
1/2 ts Paprika
Cook beans in water to cover with cloves, peppercorns, bay, chile
powder, salt, onions and carrots.  Cook until tender, about 1 1/2
hours. In a small skillet, heat the oil.  Add curry powder and
paprika, and stir for a minute to develop the flavors. Add to soup
and simmer together for a few minutes before serving.
