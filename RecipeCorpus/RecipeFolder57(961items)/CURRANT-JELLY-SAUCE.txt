Title: CURRANT JELLY SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Scallions -- chopped
1 tb All-purpose flour
1 c  Chicken stock
3 tb Currant jelly
1 tb Creme de cassis
Melt butter and saute scallions for two minutes. Stir in flour and
cook, stirring, one minute more. Gradually add stock, stirring
constantly, and bring to a boil. Turn off heat. Add currant jelly and
creme de cassis, and stir until smooth.

: Elizabeth Powell
