Title: CYRIL'S EGGNOG
Categories: Beverages, Eggs
Yield: 24 Servings
24    Egg; separated
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3 c  Whiskey
1 pt Cream, whipping
Separate eggs.  Beat yolks until creamy.  Add sugar and beat well. Add
whiskey, 2 tablespoons at a time, beating between each addition.

Beat whites very stiff and fold into the yolks.  Whip one pint cream
very stiff and fold into mixture.

--- Cyril Greene
Comanche, TX
