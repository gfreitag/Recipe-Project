Title: CRUNCHY CHOCOLATE CHIPSTERS
Categories: Cookies, Crisco, Chocolate
Yield: 48 Cookies
1/2 c  Butter Flavor Crisco
1/2 c  Sugar, granulated
1/2 c  Sugar, brown, firmly packed
2 tb Milk
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/4 c  Flour, all purpose
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Crisp rice cereal
1 c  Chocolate chips, semi-sweet
- miniatures
Bake Time:
Preparation Time: 20 Minutes
9    Minutes

1. Heat oven to 350 F. Grease baking sheet with Butter Flavor Crisco.
Set aside.

2. Cream Butter Flavor Crisco, granulated sugar, brown sugar and milk
in large bowl at medium speed of electric mixer until well blended.
Beat in egg and vanilla.

3. Combine flour, baking soda and salt. Add to creamed mixture. Mix
until blended. Stir in creeal and chocolate. Drop level measuring
tablespoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto baking sheet.

4. Bake at 350 for 9 minutes. Remove to cooling rack.

Makes 4 dozen 2 inch cookies.
