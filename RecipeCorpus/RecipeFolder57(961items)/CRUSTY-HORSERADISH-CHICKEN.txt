Title: CRUSTY HORSERADISH CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Tried and t, M. c.
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Boned and skinned chicken
-breast halves
3/4 c  Breadcrumbs, wholewheat
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Freshly ground pepper
2 tb Prepared horseradish
1 tb Dijon mustard
1/4 c  White wine
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cut meat into 4 portions. On a plate mix
together the bread crumbx, salt and pepper. In a bowl stir together
the horseradish, mustard and wine. Dip chicken in the horseradish
mixture until well coated. You can marinade for up to 1 hour for a
stronger flavor. Roll the chicken in the breadcrumbs to coat evenly.
Place pieces in a casserole (not glass). Don't let the pieces touch.
Bake uncovered for 25-30 mins. until firm to the touch. Posted to
