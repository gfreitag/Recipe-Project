Title: CURRIED BREAD
Categories: Bh&g, Spicy, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pound loaf
1/3 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Curry powder
1 tb Applesauce
3/4 c  Milk
2 c  Bread flour
2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Yeast
1/4 c  Coconut
1/4 c  Chopped peanuts
1/4 c  Raisins
Cook onion & curry powder until tender. Cool slightly. Add ing to
machine. 103 cal; 3 g pro; 17 g car Better Homes & Gardens pg 24
