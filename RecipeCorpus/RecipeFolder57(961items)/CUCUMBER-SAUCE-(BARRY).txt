Title: CUCUMBER SAUCE (BARRY)
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 150 Ml
1/2 lg Cucumber
4 oz Fromage Frais
Seasoning
Grate the cucumber, sprinkle with salt, then leave for 15 minutes to
allow excess water to drain. Rinse and pat dry with kitchen paper.
Place the fromage frais in a bowl and stir in the cucumber.
