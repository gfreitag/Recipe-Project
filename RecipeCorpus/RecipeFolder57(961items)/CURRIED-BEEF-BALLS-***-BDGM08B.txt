Title: CURRIED BEEF BALLS *** BDGM08B
Categories: Appetizers, Beef, Microwave, Indian
Yield: 24 Servings
1/2 c  Herb-seasoned stuffing mix
1/3 c  Evaporated milk
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Curry powder
1 lb Ground beef
In large mixng bowl, thoroughly combine crumbs, milk, salt & curry
powder. Add beef, blend well.  Shape into 48 1" balls.
TO MICROWAVE 12 MEATBALLS IN CIRCLE ON PAPER PLATE: Cover plate
w/wax paper.  Microwave at HIGH 2-3 minutes, rotating dish 1/4 turn
after 1 minute. Serve with Sweet-Tart Sauce
