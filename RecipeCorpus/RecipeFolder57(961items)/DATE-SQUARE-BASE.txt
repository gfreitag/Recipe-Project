Title: DATE SQUARE BASE
Categories: Cakes, Squares, Cookies
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  Soft margarine
3/4 c  Brown sugar
3 tb White sugar
1 c  Flour
1/4 ts Baking soda
1 c  Rolled oats
Mix all to-gether and put half of mixture in 9x9 pan and put date
filling on top then the rest of mixture on top of that. Press down a
little and bake in 350 oven 18 to 20 min.
