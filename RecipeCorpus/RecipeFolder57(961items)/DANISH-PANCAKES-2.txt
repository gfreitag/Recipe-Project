Title: DANISH PANCAKES 2
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  All-purpose biscuit mix
1    Egg, slightly beaten
3/4 c  Beer
1/3 c  Milk
Jam, jelly or preserves
Sifted confectioners sugar
Traditionally a dessert, these delicious filled pancakes may also be
served at brunch.

Combine biscuit mix, egg, beer, and milk, stirring just until blended.
Batter will be slightly lumpy. Using about 1 TBS batter per pancake,
cook on a very lightly greased griddle or skillet until lightly
browned, turning once. Spread with jam and roll up. Sprinkle with
confectioners sugar. Serve warm. Makes 20 small pancakes, about 4-6
servings.
