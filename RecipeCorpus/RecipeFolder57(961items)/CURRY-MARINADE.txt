Title: CURRY MARINADE
Categories: Sauces, Grill
Yield: 1 Cervings
2 tb Safflower oil
1/4 c  Dry white wine
Juice of 1/2 a lemon
1 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Shallot, minced
2 ts Curry powder
Combine all the ingredients in a container.  Swirl often when
brushing over foods to prevent separation.

Use to marinate cubed tempeh for 1 hour.  Or brush over grilling
eggplant, onions or potatoes.  (To grill potatoes, try parboiling
them first).

Nava Atlas, "Vegetarian Celebrations"
