Title: CUMQUAT SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
12    Cumquats
1 1/2 c  Orangejuice
1 1/2 oz Sugar
1. Wash and slice cumquats into fine, thin slices; add the orangejuice
and the sugar and cook for about 10 minutes. 2. Let cool and serve
with mousse au chocolat
