Title: CRUNCHY NACHO DOGS
Categories: Appetizers, Dips, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
8 oz Can Pillsbury Crescent
Dinner Rolls
4 sl (4 oz.) American Cheese
4    Wieners
1 tb Milk
1/2 c  Nacho cheese-flavored
Totilla chips -- crushed
Ketchup, salsa or dairy sour
Cream -- if desired
Heat oven to 375F.  Lightly grease cookie sheet. Separate dough into 4
rectangles; firmly press perforations to seal. Place cheese slices on
rectangles; place wiener in centre of each cheese. Roll up each
rectangle; press edges to seal.  Brush rolls with milk; roll in
tortilla chips. Place rolls, seam side down, on greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375F for 15 to 22 minutes or until deep golden brown. Serve
with ketchup, salsa or sour cream. 4 servings
