Title: CURRY BLEND
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Ground cumin
4 ts Ground coriander
4 ts Powder mustard
2 tb Ground tumeric
1 ts Ground allspice
1 ts Cayenne (ground red pepper)
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground ginger
1    In
recipes calling for curry powder, use in same amount. 2. Sprinkle over
poultry before baking.
3.  Season filling for deviled eggs. While you're
at it, keep these in mind for gifts. Just put them in a pretty jar,
and attach a few recipe suggestions with a ribbon. Makes a scant 1/2
cup.
