Title: CURRIED CHUTNEY SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
Jim Vorheis
16 oz Cream cheese
1/2 c  Major Grey's chutney
1/2 c  Chopped almonds, toasted
1 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Dry mustard
Bring cream cheese to room temperature.  Mix all ingredients together
well. Pack in a crock.  Chill. Serve with crackers or use spread to
stuff dates or celery.

Colorado Cache Cookbook (1978) From the collection of Jim Vorheis
