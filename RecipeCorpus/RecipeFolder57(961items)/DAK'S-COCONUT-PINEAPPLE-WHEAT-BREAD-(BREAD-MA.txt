Title: DAK'S COCONUT-PINEAPPLE WHEAT BREAD (BREAD MA
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1 tb Oil
1/2 c  Crushed pineapple, packed in
It's own juice, drained
3 tb Pineapple juice (liquid
Drained from can)
3/4 c  Plus 3 tb water
1/2 c  Shredded coconut (optional,
Add at the start of second
Mix)
Put all the ingredients into pan in the order listed, select "whole
wheat" and push "start."
