Title: CURRY VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Vegetarian, Sauces, Dressings
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM------------------FOR HARVEST GRAPE SALAD-----------------------
1/4 c  Vinegar, white wine
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Garlic; minced
1/4 ts Dill weed, dried
1/8 ts Pepper, ground
ds Hot pepper sauce
In small bowl or jar, combine all ingredients; mix or shake well.
Makes about 1/2 cup
