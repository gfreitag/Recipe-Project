Title: CURRIED CHICKPEAS WITH VEGGIES
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Easy
Yield: 1 Servings
3 oz Turnips; sliced 1/4-inch
-thick
3 oz Broccoli flowerets; in bite-
-size pieces
3 oz Chickpeas; cooked
3 oz Barley; cooked
2 tb Fresh chives; minced
2 tb Soy oil
1/2 ts Curry
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Steam turnips and broccoli for 8 minutes. Combine with remaining
ingredients. Mix well. Serve warm.
