Title: DAK'S SHERRY-POPPY SEED WHEAT BREAD (BREAD MA
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Whole wheat flour
4 tb Poppy seeds
3 tb Canola oil
3 tb Creamed sherry, or use
Water
1/2 c  Sour cream, warmed
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Plus 2 tb warm water
Put all the ingredients into pan in the order listed, select "whole
wheat" and push "start."
