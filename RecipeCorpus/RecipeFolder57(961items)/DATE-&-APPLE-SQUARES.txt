Title: DATE & APPLE SQUARES
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine,softened
3/4 c  Sugar
1 x  Egg
2 x  Apples, peeled, finely chopp
1/2 c  Chopped Nuts
1/2 c  Chopped Dates
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1 ts Baking Powder
Preheat oven to 350 deg F.
Blend butter and sugar, then beat in egg.
Stir in apples, dates, and nuts. In a separate bowl, combine flour,
baking powder and baking soda. Gradually stir into apple mixture.
Spread in a greased 9"x9" pan and bake 30 minutes.
