Title: DAD'S FUDGE
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  White sugar
1/4 lb Margarine
1/2 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Oatmeal
1 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Walnuts
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Coconut
Boil the brown sugar, white sugar, margarine and milk for 2 1/2
minutes. Remove from heat and add the remaining ingredients. Mix and
pour into square buttered pan.
