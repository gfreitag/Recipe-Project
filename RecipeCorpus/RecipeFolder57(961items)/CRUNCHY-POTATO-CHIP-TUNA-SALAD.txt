Title: CRUNCHY POTATO CHIP TUNA SALAD
Categories: Salads, Fish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Vinegar
2 c  Cabbage; finely shredded
6 1/2 oz Tuna; drained & flaked
1/3 c  Carrot; shredded
1/3 c  Bell pepper, green; chopped
2 tb Onion; minced
2 1/2 c  Potato chips
In a medium bowl, combine mayonnaise and vinegar.  Stir in cabbage,
tuna, carrot, green bell pepper and onion.  Cover; chill.  Just
before serving add potato chips; toss lightly.
