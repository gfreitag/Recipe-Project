Title: CUCUMBER~ TOMATO~ SCALLION~ & YOGURT RELISH
Categories: Vegetables, Preserves, East/orient
Yield: 1 Servings
1 md Cucumber
1 c  Yogurt
1 ts Sugar
1/4 c  Chopped scallions
1/4 ts Cumin
1/2 c  Peeled, seeded, cubed tomato
1. Peel cucumber. Cut lengthwise in half and scrape out center seeds
2. Cut cucumber into 1 1/2 inch lengths. Cut into coarse shreds with
a hand grater or food processor. There should be about 1 1/2 cups. 3.
Combine yogurt, cucumber and remaining ingredients. Refrigerate and
allow flavors to blend for an hour or more before serving. Submitted
