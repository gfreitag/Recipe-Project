Title: CUT-OUT SUGAR COOKIES (MACM)
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Mix
1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Vanilla
1 ea Egg
Preheat oven to 400 F.  In medium bowl, combine all ingredients
except 1 cup mix; blend until smooth. Stir in remaining mix. On
floured surface, knead about 20 strokes until smooth. Roll out dough
1/8-inch thick; cut into desired shapes. Place on lightly greased
cookie sheets. Bake 5 to 8 minutes until edges are light golden
brown. Cool 1 minute.; remove from cookie sheet. Frost and decorate
as desired. Makes about 2 dozen assorted size cookies. Source:
Pillsbury, Festive Baking
