Title: DATE-APPLE BREAD/BAKE IT ALL MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 8 Servings
3 c  Bake it all mix*
1    Egg
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Orange juice
1 ts Grated orange peel
3/4 c  Applesauce
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 c  Dates -- chopped
*Do not pack. Combine all ingredients except Mix. Add Mix and beat to
blend thoroughly.  Turn into a 9 by 5 by 3-inch loaf pan, bottom
rubbed with shortening. Bake in a 350 F. oven, about 50 minutes. Let
stand about 10 minutes,  then turn out on a rack to cool. Twelve
hours' storage improves flavor and texture.
