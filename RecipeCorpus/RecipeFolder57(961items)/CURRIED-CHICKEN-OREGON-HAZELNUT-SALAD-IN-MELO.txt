Title: CURRIED CHICKEN-OREGON HAZELNUT SALAD IN MELO
Categories: Salads, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
3 sm Melons; halved & seeded
-- (drain upside down
-- on towel)
MMMMM----------------------CHICKEN FILLING---------------------------
2 c  Cubed, cooked chicken
1/2 c  Diced celery
1    Apple (tart) cored, chopped
1 tb Green onion, finely chopped
1 tb Capers, drained
2/3 c  Oregon hazelnuts, roasted
-- and coarsely chopped
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Sour cream
2 ts Curry powder
Currants for garnish
Combine first six ingredients, except apples. Mix mayonnaise, sour
cream and curry together and stir into chicken mixture. Cover and
refrigerate 4 hours or overnight. Add chopped apples that have been
dipped in lemon juice and water. Fill cavity of melons. Garnish with
sprinkle of currants over top.
