Title: DEB'S CHICKEN CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Souped up, Left chick
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Kraft Mac & Cheese dinner
1 c  Leftover cooked chicken
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 tb Onion
1 1/2 c  Chicken broth
1 tb Green pepper, opt
2 tb Butter
1 cn French fried onion rings
Use a 2 qt. casserole. Mix everything together but onion rings.
Microwave on high 16 minutes, covered, stirring halfway through. When
done, put onion rings over top and let stand covered a few minutes
before serving.
