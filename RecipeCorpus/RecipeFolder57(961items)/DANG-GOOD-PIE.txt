Title: DANG GOOD PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 4 Servings
3/4    Stick butter
3    Eggs
3 tb Flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Crushed pineapple, drained
1 c  Coconut flakes
9-inch unbaked pie shell
Melt butter and mix with remaining ingredients.  Pour filling into
unbaked pie shell and bake 1 hour at 350 degrees or until set and
brown.
