Title: CUSTARD FILLING
Categories: Desserts, Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
2 1/4 c  EDENsoy
-OR- any EDEN food beverage
1/8 c  Honey
1 ts Vanilla
-(if not using
-Vanilla flavor)
2 1/2 tb EDEN KUDZU; (dissolved in...
1/4 c  EDENSOY)
Heat 2 cups EDENSOY, honey and vanilla (if using) in a saucepan. Add
dissolved kudzu and cook until mixture thickens. Great as a dessert
filling, with fruit, or as a quick pudding. Variation: Add 2
teaspoons of finely grated lemon zest and mix in well.

Cooking Time: 5 minutes Prep Time: 5 minutes Yield: 4 servings
