Title: CUCUMBER SAUCE
Categories: Digest, Dec.
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz Tofu
2 tb Capers
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 pn Black pepper
Whirl in blender until smooth. grate 1 medium cucumber (no, you don't
need to remove seeds) and squeeze out the excess liquid. Stir into
the tofu mixture.  Add a small amount of chopped fresh mint if
available. Let stand for a while so the flavors blend.
