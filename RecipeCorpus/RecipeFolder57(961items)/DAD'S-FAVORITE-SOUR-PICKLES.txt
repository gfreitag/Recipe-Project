Title: DAD'S FAVORITE SOUR PICKLES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 24 Servings
36 ea SMALL CUCUMBERS
1 qt CIDER VINEGAR
2 qt WATER
1 c  CANNING SALT
1 x  DILL
1 x  GARLIC
WASH CUCUMBERS. PLACE IN QUART JARS WITH TWO BUDS OF GARLIC AND
SEVERAL SPRIGS OF DILL. COMBINE VINEGAR, SALT AND WATER. BRING TO A
BOIL AND POUR OVER CUCUMBERS IN THE JARS TO WITH IN 1/2" OF THE TOP.
