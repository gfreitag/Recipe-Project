Title: CRUNCHY STUFFED EGGS
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Eggs, hard-cooked
1/4 c  Commercial sour cream
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper, coarsely ground
1 ts Parsley flakes
2    Bacon slices, cooked and
-crumbled
Paprika
6    Pimiento-stuffed olives,
-halved
Peel eggs; slice in half lengthwise, and carefully remove yolks. Mash
yolks and stir in sour cream, salt, pepper, and parsley flakes; mix
until smooth. Stir in bacon. Stuff egg whites with yolk mixture;
sprinkle with paprika, and top with an olive half.

Shared by: June Hoffman, 8/93
