Title: CRUNCHY SWEET & SOUR FETTUCCINE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Pasta, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
5 c  Honey-Mustard Chicken
-with Fettuccine (chilled)
-(see separate recipe)
1    1-inch piece ginger
-- peeled and minced, OR...
1 ts -Dried ginger
1 c  Diced celery
1 md Cucumber; peeled,
-- chopped and seeded
2 md Apples; cored and chopped
Toss all ingredients together and serve.

Each serving provides: 455 Calories; 31.3 g Protein; 72.5 g
Carbohydrates; 5.8 g Fat; 127 mg Cholesterol; 311 mg Sodium. Calories
from Fat: 11%
