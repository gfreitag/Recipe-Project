Title: CURRY PIZZA APPETIZERS
Categories: Appetizers, Jaw
Yield: 1 Party
1    Package English muffins
1 c  Black olives, chopped
1/2 c  Green onions, chopped
1 1/2 c  Sharp cheddar cheese,
Grated
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Curry powder
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Split muffins and lay in a single layer
on baking sheet. Mix remaining ingredients and spread on muffin
halves. Bake in preheated oven for about 15 minutes until spread is
hot and bubbly. Cut each half into fourth or sixths and serve.
