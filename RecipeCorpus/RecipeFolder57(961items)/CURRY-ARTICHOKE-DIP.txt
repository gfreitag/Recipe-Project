Title: CURRY ARTICHOKE DIP
Categories: Digest, Sept.
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Artichoke hearts, drained
And squeezed dry
1/2    Box Mori Nu Lite Tofu
1/4 c  Rice Dream (rice milk)
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1/2 ts Ground coriander
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Turmeric
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1/8 ts Hot mustard
1/8 ts Ground cloves
1/8 ts Black pepper
Combine all in food processor and blend.  Put in microwave safe dish
and heat for 2-4 minutes.  Serve hot. Better the second day.
