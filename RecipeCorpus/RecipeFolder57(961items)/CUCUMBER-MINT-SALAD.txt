Title: CUCUMBER-MINT SALAD
Categories: Salads, Herbs
1 lg cucumber
Yield: 4 Servings
1 sm onion  -- chopped fine
1 TB fresh mint  -- chopped
1/4 c  white vinegar
1 ts sugar
1/2 ts salt
1/2 ts lemon pepper seasoning

Peel cucumber and chop into 1/4-inch cubes. Combine all ingredients
in bowl and mix. Serve at room temperature. Serves 4.

: Newsday, 5/17/94
