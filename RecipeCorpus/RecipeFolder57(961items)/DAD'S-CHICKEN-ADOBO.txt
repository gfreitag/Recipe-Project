Title: DAD'S CHICKEN ADOBO
Categories: Filipino, Chicken, Pork
Yield: 8 Servings
6    Pork Chops
4    Chicken Breast Halves
Without Skin
1 ts Pepper
2 ts Ginger Powder
3 lb Fresh Spinach
4 ts Sherry
2 ts Salt
1 ts Monosodium Glutamate
8 tb Vinegar
4    Bay Leaves
4    Cloves Garlic -- Minced
1 tb Shoyu
1 1/2 tb Sugar
Leave chops and chicken whole. Wash and rain spinach and set aside.
Make marinade with rest of ingredients and marinate meats for an hour
in a skillet. Bring to near boil, then simmer for 1 hour. Remove
meat. Add spinach to hot marinade, cover and simmer for 5 mins. serve.
