Title: DAK'S MINT ZUCCHINI OAT BRAN BREAD (BREAD MAC
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads, Vegetables, Squash
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Yeast
1/4 ts Ginger
1 1/4 c  Whole wheat flour
1 1/4 c  Better for Bread white flour
1/2 c  Rolled oats (Quaker Oats)
1/2 c  Oat bran
1 ts Salt
3 tb Gluten
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Sugar
2    Egg whites,room temperature
1 c  Water, warmed
1 c  Grated zucchini, warmed
2 tb Mint leaves, chopped
Set browning control to light. Put all ingredients into pan in order
listed, select "Oat/French" and push "Start."
