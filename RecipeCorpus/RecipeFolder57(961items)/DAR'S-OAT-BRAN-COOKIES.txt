Title: DAR'S OAT BRAN COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 10 Servings
1 c  Oat bran
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
Egg
2/3 c  Butter flavored
short ning

Mix all ingredients together.  Roll into 1 inch balls and roll in
powdered sugar.  Place about  2 inch apart on greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees for 13 minutes.
