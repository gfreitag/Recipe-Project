Title: CRUNCHY SPINACH SALAD
Categories: Salads, Dressings
Yield: 8 Servings
2 qt Spinach; fresh torn
2 c  Bean sprouts; fresh OR
1 cn Bean sprouts; drained (16oz)
1 cn Water chestnuts; sliced,
-drained (8 oz can)
4    Eggs; hard-cooked, chopped
6    Bacon strips; cooked and
-crumbled
1 sm Onion; thinly sliced
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Brown sugar; packed
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/3 c  Vinegar
1/3 c  Ketchup
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
In a large bowl, combine spinach, bean sprouts, water chestnuts,
eggs, bacon and onion.  In a bottle or jar, combine all dressing
ingredients. Cover and shake well to mix. Just before serving, pour
dressing over salad and toss.
