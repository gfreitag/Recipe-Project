Title: CURRIED CORN SALAD
Categories: Low fat, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Corn (fresh or canned)
1/2 c  Diced red bell pepper
1    2 scallions, chopped
1/2    Cucumber, peeled, seeded,
Chopped
Spinach leaves (I didn't
Have any, used red leaf
Lettuce instead)
Dressing:
1 tb Cider vinegar
1 ts Curry spices (turmeric,
Cumin, coriander, mixed)
1 ts Mustard
Tabasco or cayenne pepper to
Taste
Mix dressing and pour over salad, allow to marinate a few minutes.
