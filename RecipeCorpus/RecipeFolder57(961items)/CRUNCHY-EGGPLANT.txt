Title: CRUNCHY EGGPLANT
Categories: Digest, August
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Eggplant
x  Eggwhite
x  Lemon pepper
x  Oregano
x  Red pepper
x  Crushed corn flakes
x  Pam
Slice the eggplant, peel or not as pleases you. Dip slices in
eggwhite with lemon pepper, oregano, red pepper to taste. Coat with
crushed corn flakes and place on baking sheet sprayed with pam. Bake
in hot oven for about 20 minutes.  Hot these are yummy. cold they
make a crispy satisfying toothsome treat.
