Title: DAK'S WALNUT BLUE WHEAT BREAD (BREAD MACHINE)
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Margarine
2 oz 2-4 oz crumbled blue cheese
1 c  Plus 2 TBS warm water
1/2 c  Toasted walnuts(add at beep
Put all ingredients into pan in the order listed, select "whole
wheat" and push "start"
