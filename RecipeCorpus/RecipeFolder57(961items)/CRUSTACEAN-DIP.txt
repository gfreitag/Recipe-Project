Title: CRUSTACEAN DIP
Categories: Chinese, Condiments, Ceideburg 2
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Chopped ginger
1/4 c  Vinegar
3 tb Light soy sauce
1 1/2 tb Sugar
This is a classic Chinese dip for things like shrimp balls. Real
simple, basic and good.

Mix an serve.
