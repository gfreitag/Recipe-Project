Title: CUCUMBER DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dressings
Yield: 2 Cups
1 md Cucumber
1 sm Onion
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Lemon juice; plus
1 ts Lemon juice
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
ds Garlic powder
1 1/3 c  Mayonnaise
Grate cucumber and onion; then drain well.  Pat dry with paper towels.
Combine remaining ingredients; stir until blended. Add cucumber and
onion, stirring well.

Yield: about 2-2/3 cups

Source: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in 1980. Typed for you by
Nancy Coleman
