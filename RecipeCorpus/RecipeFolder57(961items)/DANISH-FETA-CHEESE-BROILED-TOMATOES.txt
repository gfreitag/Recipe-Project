Title: DANISH FETA CHEESE-BROILED TOMATOES
Categories: Tomatoes, Cheese
Yield: 6 Servings
3 lg Tomatoes, cut in half
Dash of pepper
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Danish Feta cheese, finely
Crumbled
1 tb Chopped green onion
1/8 ts Dried thyme
Core tomatoes slightly, then sprinkle tomatoes with pepper. In a bowl,
blend together mayonnaise, Feta cheese, green onion and thyme. Spoon
into halves. Broil for about 5 minutes or until tops are golden
brown. Source: Creative Home Cooking #244
by Mona Brun From the collection of K. Deck
