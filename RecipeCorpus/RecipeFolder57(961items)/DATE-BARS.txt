Title: DATE BARS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
Salt
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Butter
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Lemon
3 tb Water; hot
1 c  Dates
1 c  Pecans
2 ea Eggs; well beaten
Cream sugar and butter. Add extracts, hot water, dates and nuts. Add
well beaten eggs, dry ingredients, sifted together. Spread in pan 8 x
10 x 12. Bake 30 minutes at 325 F.

Source: Mrs. Edith Gosnell, Chillicothe Grange, Ross County, OH
