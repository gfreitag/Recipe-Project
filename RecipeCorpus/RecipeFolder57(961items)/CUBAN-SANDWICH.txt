Title: CUBAN SANDWICH
Categories: Sandwiches, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Hoagie roll or French bread
Mustard
Butter
3/4 lb Baked ham,thinly sliced
1/2 lb Barbecued or roast pork
1/4 lb Thin sliced Swiss cheese
1/4 lb Thin sliced Italian salami
Dill pickle,slice lengthwise
Cut bread into six pieces,8" long.Split lengthwise;spread mustard
on one side and butter on other.Divide ham,pork,Swiss cheese,salami
and pickle among the six sandwiches,arranging in layers on the
bread.Warm in oven just before serving.To serve,wrap each in a paper
napkin and secure with a toothpick.
