Title: DAISY SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Each salad greens
1/2 c  Green peas
1    Red onion rings
1 c  Alfalfa sprouts
1/2 c  Fresh parsley
3    Hard cooked eggs
1 c  Dressing
dressing ; 1 c mayonnaise, 1 clove garlic choppod, 1/2 tsp. each salt
and pepper, 1 tsp. tarragon. mix well and spread on top of salad
ingredients except eggs. slice eggs and arrange on top, hence the
name daisy salad. refrigerate  for 3 - 5 hours  for flavors to blend.
