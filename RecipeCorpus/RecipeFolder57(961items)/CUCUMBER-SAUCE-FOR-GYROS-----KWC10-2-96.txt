Title: CUCUMBER SAUCE FOR GYROS
Categories: Sauces, Main dish, Sides
Yield: 75 Servings
10 ea Cucumbers(peeled)
1 ea Small Yellow Onion
5 lb Sour Cream
2 ts Salt
1 ts Black Pepper
2 tb Parsley Flake
3 tb Dill Weed
Peel and finely dice the cucumbers.  Dice the onion the same size.
Mix with the rest of the ingredients. Taste and let set overnight.
Taste again and make any neccessary adjustments.

Serve with gyros.
