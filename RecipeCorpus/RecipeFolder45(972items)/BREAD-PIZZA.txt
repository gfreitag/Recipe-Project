Title: BREAD PIZZA
Categories: Italian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ea White Bread Slice
1 x  Ketchup
1 ea Hot Dog
1 pn Oregano
1 ea American Cheese Slice
Spread Ketchup on Bread, drop on a pinch or two of Oregano. Place one
slice of American Cheese on top. Cut Hot Dog into little round pieces
1/8 to 1/4 inch , and place 5 or 6 pieces per slice of bread. Bake in
toaster oven until the cheese melts.
