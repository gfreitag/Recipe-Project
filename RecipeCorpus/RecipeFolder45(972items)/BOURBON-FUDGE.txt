Title: BOURBON FUDGE
Categories: Fudge, Chocolate, Alcohol, Candies
Yield: 64 Pieces
2 c  Semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 cn 14oz sweetened condensed
-milk
1/4 c  Maker's Mark
1/2 ts Orange extract
1 pk 3/4oz slivered almonds
Combine chocolate chips and milk in large bowl and cover loosely.
Cook in microwave for 3 minutes on High. Remove from microwave and
stir until smooth. Add bourbon and orange extract; mix well. Grease
an 8 inch square pan and sprinkle bottom of pan with almonds. Pour
mixture in pan; let stand until firm or chill in refrigerator.
