Title: BOLOGNA STRATA
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
12    Slices firm white bread,
-crusts removed
1/2 lb Sliced American cheese
2 1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Minced onion
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 lb Sliced bologna
4    Eggs
1 ts Mustard
1 ts Salt
Grease a 13x9-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 slices bread in one layer
across the bottom of the baking dish. Cover with bologna slices,
cheese slices, and top with remaining 6 slices bread. Beat eggs well.
Add remaining ingredients and beat again. Pour egg mixture over the
layers in baking dish.  Let stand 1 hour or refrigerate overnight.
Bake at (350F) uncovered for about 50 minutes until puffed and
lightly browned.
