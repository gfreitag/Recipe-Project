Title: BOLOGNA SOUP
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Ring bologna, chopped
4 c  Water
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Chopped carrots
1    Onion, chopped
2 md Potatoes, diced
1 c  Chopped cabbage
2    Green peppers, chopped
1 c  Canned or fresh tomatoes,
-peeled and chopped
Cook bologna in water for 30 minutes. Add remaining ingredients. Cook
for an additional 30 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
