Title: BOURBON BALLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 42 Servings
1 c  Finely chopped pecans
2 c  Crushed vanilla wafers
1 c  Confectioner's sugar
1 1/2 tb Unsweetened cocoa powder
3 tb Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Bourbon
Confectioner's sugar
1.  Combine pecans, vanilla wafers, sugar, cocoa powder, corn syrup,
and bourbon, mixing together well. 2. Using slightly dampened
hands, shape mixture into 3/4" balls. Roll
candy balls in sugar. 3. Store in a tightly covered container.
