Title: BREAKFAST RICE
Categories: Breakfast, Mcdougall, Prodigy, Dec.
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Long-grain brown rice
1 c  Unsweetened apple juice
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
Recipe by: The New McDougall Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:05
:    Combine all fo the ingredients in a sacuepan. Bring to a boil,
cover, reduce the heat, and cook for 40 mintues. Let rest for 10
minutes before serving.    This may also be made in a slow cooker
overnight. Combine all of the ingredients and cook on low for 8 to 10
hours. 170 calories, 1.0 grams fat per serving.
