Title: BOILED FROSTING 1
Categories: Candies
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  White sugar
1/3 c  Water
1 ts Vinegar
2 ea Egg whites
1 ts Vanilla
Boil to-gether sugar, water and vinegar to 238 F or until syrup spins
when dropped from tip of spoon. Beat egg whites stiff, gradually add
syrup, beating constantly until frosting holds shape. Add vanilla.
tip of spoon.
