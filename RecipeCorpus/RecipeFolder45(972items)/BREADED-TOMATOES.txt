Title: BREADED TOMATOES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 lb Tomatoes, or
1    Tin crushed with juice
2 tb Sugar (or to taste)
1 tb Flour
1    To 2 slices of bread torn
-into pieces
Cream (tin or fresh)
Cook tomatoes 5 to 10 minutes. Mix sugar and flour together and add to
tomatoes to thicken.  Add bread pieces and cream. Serve
