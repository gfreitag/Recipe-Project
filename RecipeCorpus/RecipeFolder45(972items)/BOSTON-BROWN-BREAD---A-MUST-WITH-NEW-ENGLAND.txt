Title: BOSTON BROWN BREAD - A MUST WITH NEW ENGLAND
Categories: Breads
Yield: 10 Servings
1/2 c  Flour
3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Corn Meal
3 c  Whole wheat flour
3/4 c  Dark molasses
1    Egg
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Melted shortening
Sift flour, baking soda, sugar, and salt together. Mix in corn meal
and whole wheat flour.  Add remaining ingredients, mixing only until
all flour is moistened. Pour into 2 greased 9 X 5 X 3 inch loaf pans.
Bake at 300Ã¸F for 1 hour and 15 minutes.  Remove from pans and cool.
