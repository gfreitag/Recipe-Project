Title: BONELESS CHICKEN WITH PIMINTO'S
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Cream of chicken soup
2 tb Oil
1/2 cn Milk
1/2 c  Grated cheese
2 tb Mustard
1    Box of brocoli spears
2 tb Chopped piminto
Brown boneless chicken in oil. Add soup, milk, grated cheese,
mustard. Then addd brocoli and piminto Cook 2 minutes. Serve over
rice.
