Title: BODY SCRUB
Categories: Gifts, Holiday, Personal
Yield: 1 Scrub
2 oz Apricot kernel oil
1 oz Sunflower oil
1 oz Jojoba oil
1 oz Rice bran or oat bran
6    Drops essential oils of
Lavender or rosemary
Heat plant oils together.  Mix in bran and essential oils. Stand on a
large towel and gently rub mixture into the skin in small, circular
motions. Work from the toes to the neck, avoiding the face. Rinse in
a lukewarm shower, without soap.  Give a blast of cold water and
towel dry. Submitted By ARLENES@HOLLY.COLOSTATE.EDU  On
