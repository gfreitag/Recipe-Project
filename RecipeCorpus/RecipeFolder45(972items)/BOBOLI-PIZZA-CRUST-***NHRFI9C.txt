Title: BOBOLI PIZZA CRUST ***NHRFI9C
Categories: Main dish, Appetizers, Italian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Yeast
1/4 c  Water
2 1/4 c  Warm water
6 tb Olive oil; plus extra for p
5 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt- optional
Topping:
Kosher salt
4 tb Olive oil
2 tb Water
Ground pepper
Rosemary
Thyme
Mix together first six ingredients and let rise 30-40 minutes. Divide
dough into 3 parts and place in 3 olive -oiled pie pans. Dimple dough
with fin- gers.
Mix olive oil and water,  brush tops with this mixture. Sprinkle on
top: sea salt, pepper, rosemary and thyme. Let rise 50 to 60 minutes.
Bake at 35 degrees for 25 minutes.  May dip in olive oil. Have
