Title: BOXTY
Categories: Vegan, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Potato; raw, grated
1 c  Mashed potatoes
1 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
2 ts Salt
2    Eggs
Milk; to mix (about 1/4 cu
Recipe by: unknown Squeeze the grated raw potatoes in a cloth to
remove as much moisture as possible. Sift the flour with the salt and
baking powder. Mix all potatoes with the dry ingredients and add the
beaten eggs. Mix well, and add sufficient milk to make a batter. Drop
by tablespoons onto a hot buttered frying pan and cook over moderate
heat until browned (4 minutes per side) Serve hot and well buttered
with or without sugar.
