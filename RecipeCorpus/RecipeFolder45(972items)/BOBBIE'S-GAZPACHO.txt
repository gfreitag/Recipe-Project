Title: BOBBIE'S GAZPACHO
Categories: Tomatoes, Vegetables, Soups, Healthwise
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lg Can vegetable juice cocktail
1    Clove garlic -- minced
2    Carrots -- finely chopped
1    Cucumber -- finely chopd
1    Onion -- minced
1 tb Wine vinegar
1 tb Olive oil
2    Radishes -- diced
3    Stalks celery -- finely
Chopd
1/2    Green pepper -- diced
1    Tomato -- peeled & chopped
Mix in large deep bowl: juice, garlic, carrot, cucumber, onion,
vinegar, olive oil, radishes, celery and green pepper. Chill. Serve
cold. Can add chopped, peeled tomato.
