Title: BRIE WALNUT SPREAD
Categories: Breadmakers, Breads, Spreads
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Brie
1/2 c  Cream cheese
2 tb Kirsch liqueur
1/2 c  Walnut pieces
Blend cheese and Kirsch together in food processor. Stir in nuts.
Store in refrigerator.
