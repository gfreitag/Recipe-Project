Title: BOILED BEEF PIZZAIOLA
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Tomatoes, drained
- reserve juice
1    Garlic clove, chopped
2 ts Parsley, chopped
1 ts Oregano
Salt
Pepper
1 lb Sliced boiled beef
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Reserved juice
Grease shallow baking dish with oil. Mix together tomatoes, garlic,
parsley, oregano, salt, and pepper. Line baking dish with half the
mixture. Place sliced beef over this in a single layer and cover with
rest of mixture. Sprinkle with olive oil and reserved juice. Bake in
moderate oven (375 deg F) about 30 minutes.
