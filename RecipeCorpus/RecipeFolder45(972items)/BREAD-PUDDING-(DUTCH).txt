Title: BREAD PUDDING (DUTCH)
Categories: Penndutch, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 qt Cake
1 x  *or:
1 qt Bread, stale
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ea Egg, well beaten
1/4 c  Raisins
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Beat the eggs and add the sugar, milk and nutmeg. Butter a baking
dish. Arrange bread or cake in dish and pour liquid over it. Let
stand until bread has been thoroughly soaked. Add the raisins and
bake at 350-F for 25 minutes. Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book -
Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
