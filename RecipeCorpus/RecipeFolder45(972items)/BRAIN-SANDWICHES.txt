Title: BRAIN SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Pork brains
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Soak brains in salt water a short time.  Cover with clear water and
remove membrane.  Drain; beat in other ingredients with spoon. If too
thin, add a small amount of flour; if too thick, add small amount of
milk. Fry on griddle until well done, turning once. Serve on buns, of
course. Ramdy Rigg
