Title: BRAISED CELERY #1
Categories: Ireland, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ea Head celery
1 md Onion
1 ts Chopped parsley
2 sl Bacon
10 ea Fl Stock
Salt/pepper to taste
1 oz Butter
Clean celery, cut into one-inch pieces and place in a casserole dish.
Finely chop bacon and onion and sprinkle over celery along with
chopped parsley.  Pour on stock. Dot with knobs of butter. Cover dish
and bake in a moderate oven for 30-45 minutes.
