Title: BREAD MACHINE CORNMEAL BREAD
Categories: Breads, Abm
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM------------------------MEDIUM LOAF-----------------------------
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Vegetable Oil
1    Egg
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 c  Yellow Cornmeal
2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
This loaf rises very high and is suprisingly good.  We have tried
other breadmaker cornmeal breads but none have equaled this one.
