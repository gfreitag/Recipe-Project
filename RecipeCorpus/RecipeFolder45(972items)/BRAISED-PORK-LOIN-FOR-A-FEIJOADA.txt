Title: BRAISED PORK LOIN FOR A FEIJOADA
Categories: Pork
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Pork loin (10" cut), about 4
-lbs.
1    Lemon
1    Clove garlic
Tabasco sauce
Salt
1/4 c  Shortening
1    Bay leaf
Have the butcher bone the loin & reserve the bone rack. Rub the meat
with lemon juice, garlic, Tabasco sauce & salt. Brown the loin in the
shortening, turning to brown on all sides. Replace meat in bone rack &
stand in a Dutch oven. Add the bay leaf & a little water to the pot,
cover & braise until tender, or about 1 1/4 hours.
