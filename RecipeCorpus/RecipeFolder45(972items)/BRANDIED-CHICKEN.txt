Title: BRANDIED CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
2 1/2 lbs. chicken, cut for
-frying, or boned chicken
-breasts
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1/2    Stick butter
1 c  Heavy cream
3 oz Brandy
1 tb Curry powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Parsley
Paprika
Saute chicken and garlic in butter.  Transfer to a casserole with
juices from pan.

Mix cream, brandy, curry powder, salt, and pepper and pour over
chicken. Cover and bake at 350Ã¸F for 35-40 minutes. Sprinkle with
finely chopped parsley and paprika. Yield: 4 servings. Typed in
