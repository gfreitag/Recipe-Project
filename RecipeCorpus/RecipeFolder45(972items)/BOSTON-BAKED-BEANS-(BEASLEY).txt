Title: BOSTON BAKED BEANS (BEASLEY)
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Navy beans
1    Onion, chopped
1 tb Dry mustard
3 ts Salt
4 tb Brown sugar
1/2 lb Salt pork
Soak beans overnight. Drain, cover with fresh water. Bring to boil,
simmer until tender, drain. Pour 1/2 of beans into bean pot. Combine
remaining ingredients and add to pot. Pour in remaining beans. Cover
with boiling water and bake 8 hours at 250 degrees. Watch & add more
water if necessary.

Mrs. Jack Beasley
