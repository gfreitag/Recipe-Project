Title: BREAD PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Bread cubes
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 ea Eggs
2 tb Butter
Heat the milk and pour over the bread cubes. Add the eggs beaten with
the sugar and butter cut in small pieces. Add 1 tsp. vanilla. Bake at
350 until pudding is firm.
