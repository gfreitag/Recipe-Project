Title: BRAN CANDY
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
1/8 ts Baking soda
1 tb Butter or butter substitute
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Bran
Boil all ingredients except bran to soft ball stage (234 - 238 F).
Cool to room temperature.  Add bran. If desired, 1/2 cup raisins,
dates, or nuts may be added. Beat until stiff enough to knead. Turn
onto board dusted with powdered sugar. Knead until smooth. Shape into
a roll and slice. Margaret E. Johnson, Kent, WA.
