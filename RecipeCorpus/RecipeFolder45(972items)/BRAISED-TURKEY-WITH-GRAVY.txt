Title: BRAISED TURKEY WITH GRAVY
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 2 Servings
1 tb Flour
1 tb Water
1/2 c  Turkey cooking liquid
-(from Braised Turkey
- Drumsticks)
6 oz Braised Turkey
2 servings of about 3 ounces turkey and 1/4 cup gravy each 186
calories per serving

1. Mix flour and water until smooth. Stir into turkey cooking liquid

2. Cook stirring constantly, until thickened--about 2 minutes.

3. Serve over braised turkey.
