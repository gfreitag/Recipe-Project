Title: BREAST OF CHICKEN IN JALAPENO-CITRUS MARINADE
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Boneless skinless chicken br
2    Jalapeno; seeded
1/4 c  Chopped cilantro; or italian
Juice of 1 lemon
4 md Garlic cloves
1 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Kosher salt; (i use regular)
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
2 tb Olive oil
Recipe by: Kay Hinga <HINGA@IPFWCVAX.BITNET> Combine marinade
ingredients in blender. Marinate chicken in refrigerator u 24 hours.
Remove chicken from marinade. Do not wipe. Bake, saute, broil or
grill. Enjoy!

Note: I don't like this very much baked, but it's great grilled.
