Title: BOURBON ICE CREAM
Categories: Desserts, Beverages
Yield: 999 Servings
4    Eggs
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Bourbon
14 oz Eagle milk
6 c  Half & Half
1    Mint sprigs
Beat eggs at medium speed, gradually add sugar. Add bourbon & Eagle.
Mix well.  Pour mixture into freezer can for a 1 gallon ice cream
freezer. Freeze according to directions. Let ripen at least one hour.
Serve with mint sprigs.
