Title: BRAUNSCHWEIGER SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 lb Braunschweiger
Onion, diced
2 tb Pickle juice
1 1/2 pk Cream cheese
4    Sweet pickles, diced
3 tb Salad dressing
1/2 pk Cream cheese
Salad dressing
Milk
Mix all ingredients for Braunschweiger with electric mixer; form into
a ball.  Chill for 1 hour.  Frosting: Cream cheese together with
small amount of milk and salad dressing to spreading consistency.
Spread over ball and chill for 24 hours.  Serve with crackers. Randy
Rigg
