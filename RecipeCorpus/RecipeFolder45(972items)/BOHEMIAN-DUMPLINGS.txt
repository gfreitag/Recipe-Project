Title: BOHEMIAN DUMPLINGS
Categories: Favorites, Side dish
Yield: 4 -6 serving
2 c  Flour
2    Eggs
A little water
Mix together. Plop mixture onto a cutting board. Using the back of a
butter knife, scrape amounts of about a tablespoonful into boiling
water. Boil 8-10 minutes.
