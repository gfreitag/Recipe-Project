Title: BRENDA'S BROWNIES
Categories: Bar, Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1 c  Semi-sweet chocolate
Morsels
1 cn Eagle condensed milk
1 c  Butterscotch morsels
Mix all ingredients until well blended. Press into well greased 9-inch
square pan. Bake 25-30 minutes at 350. Cool for 45 minutes.
