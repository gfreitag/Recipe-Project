Title: BOLLOS (USING COOKED PEAS)
Categories: Snacks, Cuban, Vegetarian, Vegetables
Yield: 30 Fritters
1 1/2 c  Dried black-eyed peas
1 md Onion; sliced
2    Garlic cloves; bruised
2 lg Garlic cloves; crushed
2    Chili peppers
-- seeded and chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Flour
Oil for frying
Soak the peas in water to cover overnight.  Drain and place in
saucepan with fresh water to cover.  Add the sliced onion and bruised
garlic. Bring the water to a boil and simmer for about 2 hours or
until the peas are soft.  Drain well.  Puree in a food mill or food
processor. Add the crushed garlic, chili peppers, and salt; mix well.
Form into 1-inch balls and roll in the flour, shaking off any excess.
Heat the oil to 350 degrees and carefully drop the balls in, a few at
a time. Fry for about 1 minute or until golden brown. Drain on paper
towels and serve.
