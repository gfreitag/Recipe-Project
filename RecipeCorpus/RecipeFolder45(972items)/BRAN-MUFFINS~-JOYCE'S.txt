Title: BRAN MUFFINS~ JOYCE'S
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Shortening
1/4 c  Molasses
1    Egg
1 c  All bran
3/4 c  Milk
1 c  Flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
Mix well shortening and sugar, add egg and beat well.Stir in all bran
and milk and let set until most of moisture is taken up.Sift
to-gether flour salt and baking powder and add to first mixture and
stir only until combined. Put in muffin pans and bake in 400 F oven
30 min.
