Title: BOSS PIT DRY BARBECUE SEASONING
Categories: Rub, Bbq
Yield: 1 Servings
13 oz Salt
1 1/2 oz Black pepper
1 oz Chili powder
1 tb Celery salt
1    Small can allspice
1    Small can onion powder
2 oz Red pepper
1 oz Garlic powder
1 oz MSG (optional)
1    Small can nutmeg
1    Small can ground cloves
Combine all ingredients and rub into meat prior to placing on the
pit. This seasoning is excellent on shoulders and ribs. Recipe posted
by: Earl Shelsby Via: RFIX_S 09-28-1994
