Title: BOUILLABAISSE #3
Categories: Soups, Fish, Shellfish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Mussels, cut in pieces
1 c  White wine
1 1/2 lb Sole, cut in 1" pieces
1 1/2 lb Snapper, cut in 1" pieces
2 ts Ground saffron
4 tb + 2 tsp butter
5    Garlic cloves, crushed
2    Onions, sliced
1    Carrot, sliced
2    Stalks celery, sliced
1 ts Grated nutmeg
1 ts Basil
1 ts Thyme
1    Bay leaf
2 tb Soy sauce
2 qt Water
12    Slices french bread
Steam mussels 3-4 minutes.  Dissolve saffron in warm wine; set aside.
Melt butter; add garlic, onion, carrot, celery, seasonings, and soy
sauce. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes. Add water and wine; bring to
boiling; add fish. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Serve with
toasted bread.
