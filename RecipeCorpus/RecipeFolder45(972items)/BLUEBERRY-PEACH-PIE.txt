Title: BLUEBERRY PEACH PIE
Categories: Home cookin
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sugar
3 tb Cornstarch
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Lemon jello mix
4 c  Unpeeled sliced peaches
(fresh
Or frozen and thawed)
3/4 c  Blueberries (fresh or
Frozen
And thawed)
9    " pie shell
Whipped Cream (or
Substitute) to top
From Errin Dillon: My great-aunt made this pie for a reunion last
year and it was incredible!

Combine first sugar, cornstarch and water in a sauce pan. Bring to
a boil over medium heat.  Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat.  Add jello mix; stir until dissolved.
Cool.
Mix peaches and blueberries in a bowl.  Add jello mixture; stir.
Spoon into shell.
Cover; chill for 1 hour.
Serve with whipped cream or substitute.
