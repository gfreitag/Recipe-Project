Title: BLUEBERRY SURPRISE
Categories: Desserts, Fruits, Cakes
Yield: 10 Servings
2 c  Blueberries
1/3 c  Sugar
1 cn Crushed pineapple in juice
Undrained
1 pk White cake mix (not with
Pudding)
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
3/4 c  Butter -- melted
Layer first 5 ingredients in a greased 9x13" pan. Dribble margarine
over all.

Bake 1 hour at 350F.
