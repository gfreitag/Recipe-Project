Title: BMM HERB BREAD
Categories: Pam vkbb14a, Breads, Bmm, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 pound loaf
3 tb Applesauce (butter)
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Milk
3 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Dried dill
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/2 ts Dried rosemary
1 1/2 ts Yeast
Place all ingred. including onion mix in pan. select light crust &
press start.
