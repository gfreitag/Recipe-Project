Title: CREAMY CHOCOLATE SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 12 Servings
1 1/2 c  Milk
2 oz Baking chocolate
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb All-purpose flour
2 tb Butter
2 tb Vanilla extract
1 ds Salt
Heat milk and chocolate in saucepan until chocolate melts. Beat with
whisk until smooth. Mix sugar, flour, and salt and stir slowly into
chocolate mixture. Cook and stir 5 minutes. Add butter and vanilla.
