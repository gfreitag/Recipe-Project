Title: CREAM OF ZUCCHINI SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lg Onion, chopped
1/4 c  Butter
3 c  Chicken broth
6 md Zucchini, diced
1 tb Parsley, chopped
1/4 ts Thyme leaves
Salt
Pepper
1 1/2 c  Half-and-half
1 c  Dry white wine
Saute onion in butter until soft. Add broth, zucchini, parsley,
thyme, salt and pepper. Simmer 10 minutes, or until zucchini is
softened. Puree in blender. Return to pan, add half-and-half and wine
and heat through.

SOURCE: Akron Beacon Journal staff writer Jane Snow; Wednesday
08/18/93
