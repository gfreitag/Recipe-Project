Title: CRUMBY PORK CHOPS
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Pork Chops
1 1/2    Crushed Saltine crackers
Garlic Powder
Salt
Pepper
Preparation: Rinse chops and pat dry. Press all sides of chops into
cracker crumbs. Place on a foiled line cookie sheet, and sprinkle
each chop with garlic powder, salt & pepper.

Bake: 350 degrees for 20 minutes on one side; then flip and bake for
15 more minutes. Time will be longer for extra thick cut slices.

Serving suggestion: Serve with warmed apple sauce spiced with
cinnamon and cloves.
