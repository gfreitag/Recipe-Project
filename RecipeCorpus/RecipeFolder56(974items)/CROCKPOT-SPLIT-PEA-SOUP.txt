Title: CROCKPOT SPLIT PEA SOUP
Categories: Casseroles, Soups, Pulses, Meats, Crockpot
Yield: 8 Servings
1    (1-lb) package split peas
1    Ham bone (with some meat
Left on)  or
2    Ham hocks
1    Carrot; diced
1    Onion; diced
1    Stalk celery; diced
2 qt Water
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Combine all ingredients in slow-cooking pot. Cover and cook on LOW
for 8 to 10 hours. Remove ham bone; cut meat off, dice, and return
meat to soup.

Makes 8 servings Source: Crockery Cookery by Mabel Hoffman From the
collection of Karen Deck
