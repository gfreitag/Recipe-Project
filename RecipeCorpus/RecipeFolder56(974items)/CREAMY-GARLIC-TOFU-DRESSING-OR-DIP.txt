Title: CREAMY GARLIC TOFU DRESSING OR DIP
Categories: Vegetarian, Appetizers, Dips, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Tofu, firm or soft; patted
-dry
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Oil
4 tb ;water (use only 2 for the
-dip or spread)
1 tb Tamari
3    Garlic cloves; chopped
Place all of the ingredients in a blender or food processor andpuree
until very smooth. Use more or less garlic depending on your taste.
You may need to turn off the machine and scrape down the sides a few
times.

If too thick for salad dressing, then add a little more water until
desired consistency is reached. May be kept covered in the
refrigerator for up to 4 days.
