Title: CROCKPOT CORN
Categories: Side dish, Crockpot, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lg Bag frozen corn
8 oz Pkg. cream cheese
1    Stick margarine
Salt and pepper to taste
Melt cream cheese and margarine in microwave.  Spray crockpot with
vegetable spray.  Put melted cream cheese and margarine in crockpot.
Add corn, salt and pepper. Cook in crockpot for two hours on low.
