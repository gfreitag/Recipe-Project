Title: CREAM OF VIDALIA ONION SOUP
Categories: Vegetables, Soups
Yield: 1 Servings
6 tb Margarine
4 tb Flour
4 c  Milk
2 c  Chicken stock
1 c  Chopped onions
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Finely cut green onions
4 tb Cream
Melt 2 tb of margarine in top of double boiler. Add flour and mix
well. Add milk and chicken stock. Beat with wire whip to blend
together and keep smooth. Saute onions in 4 tb of margarine, 3
minutes. Add to soup mixture and cook 15 minutes. Add salt, pepper,
green onions and cream. Stir to blend and serve.
