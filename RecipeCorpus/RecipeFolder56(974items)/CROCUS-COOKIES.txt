Title: CROCUS COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Lard
1 ts Baking soda; heaping
8 ea Eggs; well beaten
5 c  Flour
Take out 2 T. of egg white to wash top of cookies. Mold in hand, do
not roll on bake board.

Note: No time or temperature given. Assume a 425 F. oven.

Source: Mrs. Willis Wentz, Bucyrus Grange, Crawford County, OH
