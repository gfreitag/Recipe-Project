Title: CROWD CHICKEN CASSEROLE
Categories: Big recipe, Main dish, Chick:cookd
Yield: 24 Servings
10 c  Diced cooked chicken
10 c  Celery, chopped
2 bn Green onions with tops
- sliced
8 oz Chopped green chilies,canned
5 3/4 oz Pitted ripe olives, canned
- drained and sliced
2 c  Slivered almonds
20 oz Shredded cheddar cheese
- divided
2 c  Mayonnaise
16 oz Sour cream
5 c  Potato chips, crushed
Combine the first six ingredients.  Add 2 cups cheese. Mix mayonnaise
and sour cream; add to chicken mixture and toss. Spoon into two
greased 13-in X 9-in X 2-in baking dishes. Sprinkle with chips. Top
with remaining cheese. Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees for 20-25
minutes or until hot.

Source:  Taste of Home Collectors Edition
