Title: CROSTINI (GARLIC FLAVORED TOAST)
Categories: Appetizers, Side dishes
Yield: 6 Servings
6    White bread slices; day-old
4    Olive oil; or butter
2    Cl Garlic; split lenghtwise
Cut each slice of bread in 4 squares or triangles. It is not
necessary to remove the crusts. Heat the oil in a small skillet or
saucepan and saute the garlic over medium-low heat. When the garlic
is deep gold in color but not browned, discard it. Brush the flavored
oil on both sides of the bread and set the slices on a baking sheet.
Toast lightly on both sides in a 375 F oven. Serve hot.

Serves 6.
