Title: CROATIAN GARABIJE
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 1 Batch
5 c  Sifted flour
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter
1 c  Shortening
6    Egg yolks
2 tb Sour cream
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
Make as for pie crust; roll out and cut with cookie cutter or cookie
press. Bake 12 to 15 minutes at 350 F.
