Title: CREAMY MUSTARD SAUCE 2
Categories: Sauces, Mine
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
1/2 c  Nonfat yogurt
1/2 c  Nonfat sour cream
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1 tb (to 2 tbs)
-red wine vinegar
1 tb Spicy brown mustard
1 ts Soy sauce
3/4 ts Dried marjoram
1/8 ts Pepper
1    Garlic clove(s), minced
Combine all the ingredients and stir well. Cover and chill if desired.
Serve with beef, pork, lamb, or poultry.
