Title: CRETON
Categories: Pork
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Minced ,pork
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
1 ea Onion grated
1/2 ts Each salt & pepper
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Milk
place in saucepan all ingredients and stir well. Cover and cook over
low heat 1 hour, stirring once or twice during cooking. Put into bowl
and refrigerate.
