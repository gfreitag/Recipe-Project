Title: CREAMY LO-CAL MAYONNAISE
Categories: Sauces, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  White flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Onion powder
1/8 ts Garlic powder
2 tb Oil
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
Simmer together all ingredients except oil & lemon juice. Scrape the
simmered ingredients into a blender. While blending, drizzle in the
oil. Stir in the lemon juice. Chill. Alternative: Replace oil with 3
tb tahini OR use 1/4 c cashews.
