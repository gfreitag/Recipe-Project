Title: CREAMY PEPPERCORN DRESSING
Categories: Cajun, Dressings
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
3/4 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
Combine buttermilk, mayonnaise, black pepper, garlic powder, onion
powder and cayenne pepper in a medium-sized mixing bowl. Mix well.
Cover and refrigerate until ready to use.  Serve as a dressing for
you favorite salad.

Prep. time: 30 minutes
