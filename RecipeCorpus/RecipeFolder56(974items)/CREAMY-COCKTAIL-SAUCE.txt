Title: CREAMY COCKTAIL SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Cup
1/2 c  Plain low-fat yogurt
1/2 c  Reduced-fat mayonnaise
2 tb Seafood cocktail sauce
Directions: Combine all ingredients in a bowl; stir with a wire whisk
until blended. Cover and chill. Yield: 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons
(serving size: 1 tablespoon).

Nutritional Info: CALORIES 24 (71% from fat); PROTEIN 0.4g; FAT 1.9g
(sat 0.3g, mono 0.6g, poly 0.9g); CARB 1.3g; FIBER 0g; CHOL 3mg; IRON
0mg; SODIUM 74mg; CALC 12mg
