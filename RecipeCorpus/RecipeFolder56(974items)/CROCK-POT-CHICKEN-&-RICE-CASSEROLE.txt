Title: CROCK-POT CHICKEN & RICE CASSEROLE
Categories: Crockpot, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
4 lg Chicken breasts
1 cn Cream of chicken soup (small
1 cn Cream of clery soup (small)
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup (sm)
1/2 c  Diced celery
1 c  Minute rice
Mix in crock-pot the 3 cans of soup and rice. Place the chicken on
top of the mixture, then add the diced celery. Cook for 3 hours on
high or 4 hours on low. Makes 4 servings. More rice, about 1/2 cup,
and 2 other chicken breasts may be added to make 6 servings. Linda
