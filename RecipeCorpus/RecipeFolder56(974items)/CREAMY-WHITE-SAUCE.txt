Title: CREAMY WHITE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Recipe
2 tb EDEN SaffloWer Oil
2 tb Unbleached white flour
1 c  EDENsoy
-OR- any EDEN food beverage
1/2 ts LIMA Sea Salt
2 ts EDEN Mirin
2 lg Cloves garlic, pressed
1/8 c  Fresh parsley
-- (chopped finely)
Mix oil and flour in a heavy saucepan. Heat over a low flame stirring
constantly for 3-5 minutes. Slowly add EDENSOY, Stirring constantly as
sauce thickens. Add salt, garlic, and mirin. Simmer on low heat
uncovered, stirring occasionally for 5-10 minutes. Remove from heat
and stir in parsley. This is great on vegetables, tofu, seafood, or
pasta.

Prep Time: 5 minutes * Cooking Time: 10 minutes * Yield: 1-1/2 cups
