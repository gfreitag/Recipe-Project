Title: CRITTER PB&J SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches, Party
Yield: 8 Servings
16 sl Whole wheat bread
8 tb Peanut butter
8 ts Jelly
Cut bread with 3 to 4-inch wide open-topped animal-shaped cookie
cutters (freezing the bread makes it easier to cut). You will need 2
of each shape to make each sandwich. Spread peanut butter on 8 animal
cutouts; spread jelly on reverse side of corresponding cutouts.
Assemble sandwiches.

Source:  All New Party Cookbook
