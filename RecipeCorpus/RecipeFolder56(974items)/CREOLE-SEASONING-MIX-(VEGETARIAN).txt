Title: CREOLE SEASONING MIX (VEGETARIAN)
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
4 ts Salt
4 ts Paprika -- * see note
1 tb Garlic powder -- or
Granulated
1 tb Black pepper
2 1/4 ts Onion powder -- or
Granulated
1 1/2 ts Thyme
1 1/2 ts Oregano
1 1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container. NOTES
: * using sweet or hot will make a big difference
