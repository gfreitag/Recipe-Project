Title: CREAMY CARAMEL DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 3 1/2 cups
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
3/4 c  Brown Sugar, firmly packed
8 oz Sour Cream
2 ts Vanilla
2 ts Lemon juice
1 c  Milk (cold)
1 pk (3.4 oz) Instant Vanilla
-pudding mix
Fresh Fruit (assorted)
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and brown sugar until smooth. Add
the sour cream, vanilla, lemon juice, milk and pudding mix, beating
well after each addition.

Cover and chill for at least 1 hour.
