Title: CROCKPOT CHICKEN PARISIENNE
Categories: None, Crockpot
Yield: 1 Servings
6 md Chicken breasts
Salt & pepper
Paprika
1/2 c  Dry white wine or vermouth
-(opt)
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
4 oz Mushrooms, sliced; drained
1 c  Sour cream; mixed with
1/4 c  Flour
Sprinkle chicken breasts lightly with salt, pepper, and paprika.
Place chicken in Crock-Pot.  Mix white wine, soup, and mushrooms
until well combined. Pour over chicken. Sprinkle with a little more
paprika. Cover and cook on low for 7 to 9 hours. (On high: 3 to 4
hours) Remove chicken and stir in sour cream mixture during last 30
minutes. Serve sauce over chicken with rice or noodles.
