Title: CROCKPOT ORANGE GLAZED CARROTS
Categories: Vegetables, Crockpot
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Thinly sliced carrots
3 tb Butter or margarine
2 c  Water
3 tb Orange marmalade
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Chopped pecans
Combine carrots, water, and salt in crock-pot. Cover and cook on high
2 to 3 hours or until the carrots are done. Drain well; stir in
remaining ingredients. Cover and cook on high 20-30 minutes. Makes 5
to 6 servings....
