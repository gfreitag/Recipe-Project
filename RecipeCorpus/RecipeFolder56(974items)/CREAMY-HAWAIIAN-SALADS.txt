Title: CREAMY HAWAIIAN SALADS
Categories: Salads, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
-MARY WILSON   BWVB02B
1/2 c  Walnut halves
1 c  Sour cream (regular, light
- or no-fat)
1 cn (20oz) pineapple chunks in
- in juice; drained
2    Stalks (1 cup) celery; chop
1 md Banana; sliced
1 md Papaya; peeled, pitted,
- cubed
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Lemon juice
Chopped walnuts, if desired
In large bowl gently toss together all ingredients except chop
walnuts. Sprinkle with chopped.  Serve immediately. Yield: 6 servings.

Nutrition Information (1 serving):  Calories 220; Protein 4g;
Carbohydrate 24g; Fat 14g; Cholesterol 20mg; Sodium 45mg.
