Title: CREOLE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Sliced okra
1 x  Oil
1 c  Drained, canned It. tomatoes
1 x  Garlic
2 c  Finely chopped onions
1 1/2 c  Chili sauce
1 x  Louisiana hot sauce
3 ea Medium Green Peppers
1 1/2 c  Sherry
Combine the sliced okra with the tomatoes, chopped onions, and the
green peppers chopped and saute' for a few minutes in garlic-flavored
oil. Add a dash or two of Louisiana hot sauce, the chili sauce, and
the sherry (ordinary).  Simmer over low heat for 30 minutes. Cool
slightly and blend in a blender.
