Title: CREAMY PUMPKIN SOUP
Categories: Soups, Cuban
Yield: 6 Servings
3 tb Olive oil
1 lg Onion, chopped
2 ea Garlic cloves, chopped
2 lb Butternut squash, diced
4 c  Stock
1 c  Water
1 ds Tabasco
Salt to taste
Pimento strips to garnish
Heat olive oil overl ow heat & add onion & garlic. Cook, stirring till
tender.  Add squash & stock, cover & simmer for 40 to 50 minutes or
till the squash is very soft.

In a food prcessor, process soup till its very smooth & return to
pot. Thin with water & season.  Pour into a tureen & garnish with
pimento.

Adapted from Randelman & Schwartz, "Memories of a Cuban Kitchen"
