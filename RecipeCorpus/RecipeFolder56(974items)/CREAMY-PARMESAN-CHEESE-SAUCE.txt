Title: CREAMY PARMESAN CHEESE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Cheese/eggs, Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
8 oz Pkg Cream Cheese, cubed
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan Cheese
3/4 c  Milk
Dash of ground nutmeg
-and pepper
Microwave cream cheese, milk and parmesan cheese in 1-quart measure.
Microwave on MEDIUM (50%) 6 to 8 minutes or until sauce is smooth,
stirring every 2 minutes. Stir in seasonings. Toss with hot cooked
pasta. (1 1/2 cups)
