Title: CROCKPOT TROPICAL TEA WARMER
Categories: Beverages, Crockpot
Yield: 10 Servings
6 c  Boiling water
1 1/2 c  Orange juice
6 ts Bags
1 1/2 c  Pineapple juice
1/3 c  Sugar
1    Orange sliced (unpeeled)
2 tb Honey
Warm your crock-pot first with some hot tap water. Then pour boiling
water over the tea bags in the  >> crock-pot. Cover and let stand 5
minutes. Remove the tea bags. Stir in the sugar, honey, orange juice,
pineapple juice, and orange slices. Cover and heat on low for 2-3
hours; serve from pot.
