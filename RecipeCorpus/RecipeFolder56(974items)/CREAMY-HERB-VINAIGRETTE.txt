Title: CREAMY HERB VINAIGRETTE
Categories: S. dressing
Yield: 1 1/3 cups
1 ts Mayonaise
1 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3/4 c  Spanish olive oil
3 tb Flavored vinegar
1 ts Parsley; minced
1/2 ts Thyme; minced
1/2 ts Oregano; minced
Place the mayonaise, mustard, salt, pepper, oil and vinegar in the
food processor and pulse until smooth. Add the minced herbs and stir
to mix. Refrigerate.

Nutritional info per tablespoon: 69 cal; trace pro, .1g carb, 8g fat
(99%) Source: Miami Herald 12/21/95
