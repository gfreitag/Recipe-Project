Title: CROCKPOT BARBECUE
Categories: Meats, Bbq, Crockpot
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Boneless chuck steak, 1 1/2"
-thick
1    Clove garlic peeled & minced
1/4 c  Wine vinegar
1 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Paprika
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Ketchup
1 ts Salt
1 ts Dry or prepared mustard
1/4 ts Black pepper
Cut the beef on a diagonal, across the grain into slices 1" wide.
Place these in the crockpot. In a small bowl, combine the remaining
ingredients. Pour over the meat and mix. Cover and cook on low for 3
to
5    hours.
