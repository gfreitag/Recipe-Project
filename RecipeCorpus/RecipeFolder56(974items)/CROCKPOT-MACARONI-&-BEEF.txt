Title: CROCKPOT MACARONI & BEEF
Categories: Crockpot
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
2 c  Uncooked macaroni
1 md Onion -- chopped
1 cn Mushroom pieces -- drained
1 cn Corn -- drained
2 cn Tomato soup
Salt and pepper -- to taste
Brown ground beef, drain fat; put in crockpot  Cook macaroni
according to package directions till barely tender, drain well. (I
find that cooking slightly less than half the time given works OK).
Put macaroni and blend Cook on low 7 to 9 hours or on high for 3 to 4
hours.
