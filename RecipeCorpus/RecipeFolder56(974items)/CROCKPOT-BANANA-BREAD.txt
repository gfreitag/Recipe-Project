Title: CROCKPOT BANANA BREAD
Categories: Bananas, Crockpot, Fruits
Yield: 8 Servings
1 3/4 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Shortening
2/3 c  Sugar
2    Eggs -- well beaten
1 1/2 c  2 To 3 Bananas -- mashed
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped/opt
Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. With the
electric beater on low, fluff the shortening in a small bowl, until
soft and creamy. Add the sugar gradually. Beat in the eggs in a slow
stream. With a fork, beat in 1/3 of the flour mixture, 1/2 the
bananas another 1/3 of the flour mixture, the rest of the bananas
then the last of the flour mixture.  Fold in the walnuts.  Turn into
a greased and floured baking unit or a 2 1/2 quart mold and cover.
Place on a rack in the slow cooker. Cover the cooker, but prop the
lid open with a toothpick or a twist of foil to let the excess steam
excape.  Cook on high for 4 to 6 hours. Cool on a rack for 10
minutes. Serve Warm.

Makes 4 to 6 servings
