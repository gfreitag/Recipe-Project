Title: CRISPY OAT HERRINGS & APPLE RINGS (IRISH)
Categories: Fish, Fruits
Yield: 2 Servings
3 x  Whole herrings
2 oz Medium-ground oatmeal
1    Egg
2 x  Red apples
1    Parsley sprig to garnish
Salt and pepper
Melted fat or oil to fry
Clean and scale herrings, remove fins, wash in cold water. Dry well.
Season with salt and pepper.  Dip herrings in beaten egg, and coat
with oatmeal. Fry in hot fat for 4-5 minutes on each side. Drain well
on paper towels and keep hot.  Wash apples, core, cut in slices and
fry in hot fat for a few seconds, turning once. Serve herrings with
apple rings on a hot dish. Garnish with parsley sprigs.  Serve with a
cider sauce.
