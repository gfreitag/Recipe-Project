Title: CREAM OF MUSHROOM SOUP #2
Categories: Soups/stews
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Cornstarch
3 1/2 c  Water
3    Low Sodium Chicken
Bouillon Cubes
1 c  Finely Chopped Fresh
Mushrooms
3/4 c  Skim Milk
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ts Onion Powder
Stir Cornstarch Into Water & Heat Until Boiling.  Stir in Bouillon
Cubes Until Dissolved.  Add Chopped Mushrooms To Mixture & Simmer For
20 Minutes. Add Skim Milk, Pepper & Onion Powder. Heat Only Until
Hot. (Fat 0.2. Chol. 1.)
