Title: CROCKPOT SPANISH RICE
Categories: Vegetarian, Crock pot, Mershon
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Onion, diced
1    Green pepper, minced
2    Tomatoes, peeled, quartered
15 oz Tomato Sauce
1 1/2 c  Water
2 ts Chili powder
2 ts Worchester sauce
1 1/2 ts Garlic powder
3/4 c  Brown rice
2 tb Salsa
Stir all ingredients together.  Cover and cook on low 7-10 hours.
From: Joan Mershon
Date: 22 Mar 95
