Title: CROCKPOT SEASONED BEEF IN SAUCE
Categories: None, Crockpot
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Round steak
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Onions
1 tb Worchestershire sauce
1 ts Curry powder
1 ts Molasses
1/2 ts Powdered ginger
1 c  Water
1 c  Sour cream
2 tb Horseradish sauce
Combine flour, salt and pepper.  Cut round steak into cubes. Toss meat
cubes with flour mixture.  Brown in a little hot oil. Put in
crockpot, add sliced onions, Worchest. sauce, curry, molasses, ginger
and water. Cover and slow cook 6 hours.  Just before serving add sour
cream mixed with horseradish.

Serve with rice, peas and cottage cheese/pear salad.
