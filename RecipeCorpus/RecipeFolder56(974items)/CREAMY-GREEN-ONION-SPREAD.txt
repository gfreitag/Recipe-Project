Title: CREAMY GREEN ONION SPREAD
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk (8 ounces) cream cheese,
-softened
2 tb Milk
2    Green onions with tops,
-chopped
-Crackers
In a small bowl, beat cream cheese and milk until smooth. Stir in
onions. Serve with crackers

Yield: 1 cup.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
