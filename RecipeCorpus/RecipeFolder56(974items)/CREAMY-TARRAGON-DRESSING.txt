Title: CREAMY TARRAGON DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 1 Cup
1/2 c  Nonfat yogurt
1/2 c  Reduced-calorie mayonnaise
1 tb Chopped fresh tarragon
-OR
1 1/2 ts Dried tarragon, crumbled
1/4 ts Freshly ground black pepper
In a small bowl, whisk all ingredients together until blended, or
combine in a blended until smooth.

Nutritional information per tablespoon: 25 calories, 2 grams fat, 46
milligrams sodium, 3 milligrams cholesterol.
