Title: CREAMY BALSAMIC DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces
Yield: 10 Oz
1 c  Mayonnaise
3 tb Balsamic Vinegar
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 ts Worchestershire sauce
2 tb Extra virgin olive oil
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Pepper
Salt to taste
Combine all ingredients in 2-cup bowl and whisk until smooth. Chill
several hours before serving over salad greens. Makes enough for 8-10
people.

SERVING SUGGESTION: For a truly Italian entree, serve shredded
chicken over iceberg lettuce topped with this dressing.
