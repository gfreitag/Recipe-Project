Title: CROAKER CROQUETTES TWO
Categories: Wildgame, Fish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pt Potatoes, mashed
1 ts Onion juice
1    Egg
1 c  Fish, seasoned, flaked
Mix all together. Form into croquettes or patties and dip into egg
prepared as for fillets. Roll in crumbs and fry in deep fat at 365-F.
Serve with tomato sauce. Suggestions: Substitute Duchesse potato for
mashed; add one Tbsp fat, one egg yolk slightly beaten and 1/3 tsp
salt to each cup of hot riced potato. Generalized recipe made
specific for Croaker. Source: FISHES AND FISHING IN LOUISIANA
published 1933 Recipe date: 01/17/33
