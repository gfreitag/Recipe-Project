Title: CREAMY SPAM MUSHROOM SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  SPAM Luncheon Meat, finely
-cubed (12 oz)
1 cn 99% fat-free condensed cream
-of mushroom soup (10 3/4oz)
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4 c  Chopped onion
12 sl Bread, toasted
In bowl, combine SPAM, soup, cheese, and onion. Spread mixture on
toasted bread. Place on baking sheet. Broil 4-5 minutes or until
cheese is melted and bubbly.
