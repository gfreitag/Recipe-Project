Title: CREAMY QUINOA PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Eden Quinoa; rinsed
2 1/2 c  Edensoy
-OR- any Eden food beverage
1/8 ts Lima Sea Salt
1 ts Sesame butter or tahini
2 tb Eden Barley Malt Syrup
-OR- maple syrup or honey
2 tb Eden Kudzu; dissolved in...
2 tb Cold water
1 tb Vanilla
1/2 ts Fresh grated nutmeg
-OR- ground cinnamon
Put Quinoa, Edensoy and salt in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Cover
and simmer for 20 minutes.  Add sesame butter and Barley Malt Syrup.
Mix well. Add Kudzu, stirring constantly until mixture thickens. Add
vanilla and spices.  Top with your choice of nuts or fruit. Delicious
warm or chilled.

Prep Time: 15 min. * Cooking Time: 20 min. * Yield: 4 servings
