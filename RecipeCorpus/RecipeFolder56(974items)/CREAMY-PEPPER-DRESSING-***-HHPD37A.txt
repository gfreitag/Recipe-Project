Title: CREAMY PEPPER DRESSING *** HHPD37A
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Mayo
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Parmesan, grated cheese
1 tb Pepper; freshly grated grou
1 tb Cider vinegar
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Onion;  finely chopped
1 ts Garlic salt
Whisk all ingredients until well combined and chill thoroughly before
serving.  Makes 3 cups. FROM:    KIM GEORGE (HHPD37A)
