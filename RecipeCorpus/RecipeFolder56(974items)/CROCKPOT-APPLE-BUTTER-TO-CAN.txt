Title: CROCKPOT APPLE BUTTER TO CAN
Categories: Crockpot, Canning
Yield: 4 Servings
7 c  Applesauce; unsweetened
2 c  Apple cider
1 1/2 c  Honey
1 ts Ground cinnamon; or more
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Allspice
In a crockpot, mix all ingredients.  Cover and cook on low heat for
14 to 15 hrs., or until the mixture is deep brown.
Pack while hot into 4 hot pint jars.  Process in hot water bath for
10 mins., counting the time after the jars have been immersed and the
water comes again to a rolling boil.
