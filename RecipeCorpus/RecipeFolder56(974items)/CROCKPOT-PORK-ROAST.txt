Title: CROCKPOT PORK ROAST
Categories: Crockpot, Pork
Yield: 4 Servings
3    To 4 lb
1    Env Lipton "Golden Onion
Soup Mix"
1    Env Water
lg Pinch garlic powder
Pork roast -- (not chops)
Place the roast in your crockpot.  Pour in soup, water and garlic
powder. Cook all day on low.  Thicken juice to make gravy and serve
over potatoes, rice or noodles.
