Title: CREAMY DATE SHAKE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 3 Servings
2 1/4 c  Vanilla nonfat frozen yogurt
3/4 c  Skim milk
1/2 c  Chopped pitted dates
1 ts Vanilla extract
5    Ice cubes
Combine first 4 ingredients in blender, and process until dates are
finely chopped. With blender on, add ice cubes,1 at a time, and
process until smooth. Yield: 3 cups (serving size: 1 cup). - - - - -
~ - - - ~ - - - - - - - - Serving Ideas
: Serve immediately.
