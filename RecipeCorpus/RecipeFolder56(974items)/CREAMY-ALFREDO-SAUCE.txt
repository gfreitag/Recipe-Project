Title: CREAMY ALFREDO SAUCE
Categories: Ethnic, Sauces, Pasta
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Butter/margarine
1 c  Light cream
1/3 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1/8 ts Pepper
-Pinch ground nutmeg
In medium saucepan over medium low heat, melt butter. Add cream;
cover and simmer 2 minutes. Stir in cheese, pepper and nutmeg. Serve
over hot cooked pasta.
