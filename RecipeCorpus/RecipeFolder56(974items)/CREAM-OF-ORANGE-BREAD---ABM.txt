Title: CREAM OF ORANGE BREAD - ABM
Categories: Abm, Bread, Suzy, Posted-mm, Tried
Yield: 1 Loaf
MMMMM-----------------------1 1/2 LB. LOAF----------------------------
1 pk Active Dry Yeast
Or Quick Rise
3 c  Bread flour
3 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Salt
2 tb Grated orange peel
1 ts Grated lemon peel
1/8 ts Ginger or cardamom
2 tb Cool butter,cut in pieces
2/3 c  Light cream
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Undiluted,concentrated
Frozen orange juice,thawed
Place all ingredients into pan in the order suggested by the
manufacturer. Process accordingly.

Note: I've made this on the manual/dough cycle and shaped   the dough
into rolls, did an additional rise and    proceeded with standard
oven baking.
