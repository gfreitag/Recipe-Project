Title: COFFEESHAKE
Categories: Coffee, Shara
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/4 oz Fresh brewed coffee
2 ts Cocoa powder
2 tb Sugar
1/4 qt Milk
1/8 qt Fresh cream
Grated chocolate
Prepare coffee and allow to cool.  Add cocoa powder, sugar and milk;
blend well.  Fill 6 glasses, top with fresh cream and sprinkle with
chocolate.

Source: A bag.
