Title: CORNED BEEF #1
Categories: Crockpot, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 1 Cornedbeef
-SUEANN LARUE
4 lb Corned beef brisket
-Water
1/2 c  Onion;chopped
2    Garlic cloves; minced OR
Garlic powder
2    Bay leaves
1    Head cabbage;cut into wedges
Placed corned beef in crockpot, barely cover with water. Add onion,
garlic and bay leaves. Cover and cook on LOW for 9 to 10 hours. If
cabbage is desired, lift corned beef out of pot, turn to HIGH and
drop wedges of cabbage into broth. Cover and cook 15 to 20 Mins.
Remove and serve.
