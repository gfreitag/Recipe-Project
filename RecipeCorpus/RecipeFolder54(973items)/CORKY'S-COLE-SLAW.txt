Title: CORKY'S COLE SLAW
Categories: Sides
Yield: 6 Servings
1 md Head of green cabbage --
Shredded
2 md Carrots -- grated
1    Green pepper -- finely
Diced
2 tb Onion -- grated
2 c  Mayonnaise
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Dijon mustard
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
2 tb Celery seeds
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts White pepper
Mix vegetables in a bowl. Mix remaining ingredients in another. Mix
together and toss well. Cover and refigerate 3-4 hours.

: Kit Anderson
