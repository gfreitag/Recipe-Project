Title: COCONUT MILK/MAKE YOUR OWN
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
See directions
Mix 2 cups shredded coconut, either fresh or packaged, with 2 cups of
light cream.  Heat to just below simmering point, then remove from
heat and let steep for 30 minutes. Strain through cheesecloth or a
wire strainer, pressing or squeezing out all the liquid.
Makes 2 cups of coconut milk--or cream.
