Title: CORIANDER SPARERIBS
Categories: Pork
Yield: 8 Servings
5 lb Pork spareribs
1/4 c  Sugar
3 tb Garlic; minced
3 tb Lemon juice
2 1/2 tb Coriander; ground
1 ts Dried red hot chilies; crush
1 ts Salt
1/3 c  Water
Recipe by: Sunset Annual 1989 - Page 14 Trim excess fat off ribs. Cut
ribs apart between bones.  In a 14- by 17-in Bake, covered, in a 400
degree oven until meat is almost tender when pierce
