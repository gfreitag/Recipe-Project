Title: COCOA BROWNIES (DERSKIN)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Melted butter or margarine
2    Eggs
2 c  Brown sugar
6 tb Cocoa
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
Mix sugar and cocoa together. Add butter (or margarine) and eggs. Mix
well. Add the rest of the ingredients. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes.
