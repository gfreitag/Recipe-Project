Title: COOKIE PIE CRUST
Categories: Pastries
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Margarine -- melted
3 tb Confectioner's sugar
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 c  All-purpose flour
In a small mixing bowl, mix together all ingredients. Press into a
9-inch pie dish. Prick with a fork.

Bake at 475 degrees for 8 to 10 mintues.
