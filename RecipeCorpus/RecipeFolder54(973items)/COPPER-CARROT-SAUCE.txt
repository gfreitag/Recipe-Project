Title: COPPER CARROT SAUCE
Categories: Miamiherald, Sauces, Vegetarian, Vegan
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 cn Tomato soup (10 3/4oz)
1/2 c  Corn oil
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Cider vinegar
1 ts Yellow mustard
1 ts Worchestershire sauce (veg)
Salt and pepper to taste
Mix all ingredients in a bowl.  Set aside.

Nutritional info per 1/4 recipe: 475 cal; 1g pro, 61g carb, 28g
fat(51%)

Source: Kathy Allsworth, Davie, Fl Miami Herald, 2/15/96 format by
LIsa Crawford, 7/7/96
