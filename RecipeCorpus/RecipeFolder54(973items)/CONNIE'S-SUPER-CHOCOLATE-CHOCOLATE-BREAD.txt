Title: CONNIE'S SUPER CHOCOLATE CHOCOLATE BREAD
Categories: Abm, Favorites
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Tepid water, PLUS 1
-Tablespoon
2 3/4 c  Bread flour
2 tb Hot cocoa mix
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Butter, or margarine
2 ts Active dry yeast
1 1/4 c  Chocolate chips, added at
-the *beep

manufacturer.... Use SWEET CYCLE.
