Title: CORNED BEEF & SWISS APPETIZERS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 36 Servings
1 pk Cream cheese, softened
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 lb Corned beef, chopped
1/2 c  Shredded swiss cheese
2 tb Chopped green onion
36    Slices cocktail rye bread,
(toasted)
Mix cream cheese and mustard with electric mixer on medium speed until
smooth. Blend in meat, Swiss cheese and onion. Spread on toast slices.
Place on cookie sheet. Broil 2 to 3 minutes or until lightly browned.

To Make Ahead:  Prepare as directed except for broiling. Place on
cookie sheet.  Freeze 1 hour or until firm. Place in freezer-safe
zipper-style plastic bags. Freeze up to 1 month. When ready to serve,
thaw
10    minutes. Broil as directd.ch
