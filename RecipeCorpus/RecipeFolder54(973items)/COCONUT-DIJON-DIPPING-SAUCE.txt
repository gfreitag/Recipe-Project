Title: COCONUT DIJON DIPPING SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Seafood, Condiments
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Coco lopez cream of coconut
1/2 c  Dijon-style mustard
In small bowl, combine ingredients; mix well. Serve with your favorite
appetizers. Regrigerate leftovers.
