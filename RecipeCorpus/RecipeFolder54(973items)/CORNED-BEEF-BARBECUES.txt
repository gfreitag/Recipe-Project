Title: CORNED BEEF BARBECUES
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Water
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Worchestershire sauce
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Mustard
1/2 c  Ketchup
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
1    Small onion (chopped)
1 cn Canned corned beef
Put all ingredients in a fry pan and simmer for at least an hour or
until done to your liking. When done; serve on hamburger buns.
These are simple to make and taste good too.
