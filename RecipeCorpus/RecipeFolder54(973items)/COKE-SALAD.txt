Title: COKE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 pk Cherry Jello
1 cn Dark sweet cherries
1 lg Can of crushed pineapple
1 c  Pecans
2 sm Cokes
Drain juice off of the cherries and pineapple and add enough water to
make 2 cups of liquid and heat until boiling. Pour liquid over Jello
and stir until well dissolved, then add cherries, pineapple, nuts and
Cokes. Chill. Randy Rigg
