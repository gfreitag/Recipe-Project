Title: CORIANDER COHORT
Categories: Seasoning
Yield: 8 Servings
1 ts Cumin -- toasted
1 ts Coriander seed
1 ts Black peppercorns
An all-purpose seasoning with a Near East accent combines equal parts
toasted cumin, coriander seed and black peppercorns in a pepper
grinder. Place it on your table and serve over rice or couscous.
TIPS: grind some on baked or mashed potatoes.
