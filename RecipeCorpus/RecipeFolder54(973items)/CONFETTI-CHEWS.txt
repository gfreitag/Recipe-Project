Title: CONFETTI CHEWS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Pillsbury Funfetti white
Cake mix
1 ct Cool Whip (8oz)
1    Egg
Confections sugar
Oven should be preheated to 350 F. degrees. Reserve candy bits. Then
combine Cool Whip, and egg. Beat at low speed until moistened. Fold
in the candy bits by hand, using a rubber spatula. Drop by
teaspoonsful into confectioners' sugar. Roll into balls. On an
ungreased cookie sheet, place them 2 inches apart. Bake for 8-10
minutes.
