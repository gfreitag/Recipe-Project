Title: COFFEE APPLE CAKE
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  All purpose flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Oil
2 ea Eggs
1/4 c  Coffee
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
2 1/2 c  Sliced peeled apples
1 c  Chopped nuts
Mix all the ingredients together and bake at 350 degrees for 60
minutes
