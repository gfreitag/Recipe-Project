Title: CONFETTI DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Salad Oil
3/4 c  Vinegar
1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
2    Green Onions W/Tops; Sliced
1/4 c  Pimiento Stuffed Olives
1/2 sm Green Pepper;Cut Into Pieces
1 lg Celery Stalk; Sliced
Put all of the ingredients into a blender container and blend until
the vegetables are finely chopped. Chill the mixture before serving.
Stir the mixture before spooning the dressing over salad greens.

Makes approximately 2 1/3 cups of dressing.
