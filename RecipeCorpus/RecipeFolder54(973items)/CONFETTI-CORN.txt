Title: CONFETTI CORN
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Olive oil
3 c  Frozen corn kernels
1/2 c  Diced red bell pepper
3/4 ts Chili powder
3    Scallions -- thinly sliced
1/4 ts Salt
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  Add corn, bell pepper
and chili powder. Increase heat to medium-high and cook, stirring
often, until corn is hot, about 2 minutes. Stir in scallions and
salt; cook
1    minute.
