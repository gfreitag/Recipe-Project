Title: COFFEE LIQUEUR ZESTY SEAFOOD MARINADE/SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Alcohol, Marinades
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Coffee liqueur
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Onion, finely chopped
6 ds Hot pepper sauce
1/2 ts Honey
1/8 ts Ginger root, grated
1/4 c  Lime juice
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Dill weed
1/4 ts White pepper
Shake all ingredients together well in covered jar. Let stand 1
hour or longer to blend flavors. Shake well before using. Pour over
fish and marinate 30 minutes. Grill fish, basting often..
