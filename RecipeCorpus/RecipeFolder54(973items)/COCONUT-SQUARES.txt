Title: COCONUT SQUARES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 1/2 c  Graham wafer crumbs
1 ts Sugar
1/2 c  Melted butter
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 cn Eagle bran milk
2 c  Coconut
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
Combine crumbs,sugar and melted butter & press into pan, cook 10
minutes in 350 oven. Cool. Combine milk, coconut, vanilla and salt,
cover first mixture & bake 15 min in 350 oven, When cool ice with
chocolate icing.
