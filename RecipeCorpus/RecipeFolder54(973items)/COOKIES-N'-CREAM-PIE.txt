Title: COOKIES N' CREAM PIE
Categories: Desserts, Pies
Yield: 1 Pie
1 1/2 c  Cold half/half
1 pk Chocolate instant pudding
4 serving size
8 oz Whipped topping, thawed
1 c  Crushed tagalong cookies
1    Ready made chocolate pie
Crust
Pour half and half into large bowl. Add pudding mix, beat with wire
whisk until well blended, 1 minute. Let stand 5 minutes. Fold in
whipped topping and crushed cookies. Spoon into crust. Freeze until
firm, about 6 hours or overnight. Remove from freezer and let stand
10 minutes to soften before serving. Store leftover pie in freezer.
