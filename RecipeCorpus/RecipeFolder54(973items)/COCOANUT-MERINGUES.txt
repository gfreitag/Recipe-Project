Title: COCOANUT MERINGUES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Egg whites
1/4 c  Chopped Walnuts
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Cocoanut
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Orange extract
3/8 c  Chopped Dates
Beat egg whites stiff and beat sugar into them. Fold in other
ingredients. Drop from teaspoon on greased tin. Bake in a moderate
oven.

Time in oven, 30 minutes. Temperature, 300 degrees.
