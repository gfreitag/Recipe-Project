Title: CORN BREAD STICKS
Categories: Breads, Misc
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Corn-meal
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Sour milk
2    Eggs, well beaten
3 tb Melted shortening
Sift flour, measure, and sift with salt, baking soda, baking powder,
and sugar.  Combine eggs, milk, and shortening. Add dry ingredients.
Beat thoroughly. Fill well-oiled corn-stick pans 2/3 full. Bake in
hot oven (425Ã¸ F) 20-25 minutes. 12 servings. The Household
