Title: CORN TOMATILLIO SALSA
Categories: Appetizers, Low-cal
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Roasted corn kernels
2    Tomatillios, husked &
Coarsley chopped
1 tb Red onion, finely diced
1 1/2 ts Jalapeno, minced
2 tb Lime juice
2 tb Cilantro, chopped
1/2 ts Honey
Combine all the ingrediants in a bowl & mix well. Season to taset
with salt and pepper.
