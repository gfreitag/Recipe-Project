Title: CATTLE RANCH BAR-B-QUE SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Butter
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 lb Bacon grease (1 cup Wesson
- oil)
1 tb Black pepper
1 tb Red (Cayenne) pepper
3 ea Onions, grated
10 oz Worcestershire sauce
24 oz Ketchup
1 tb Salt
1 tb Celery salt
1 ts Garlic salt
Combine all ingredients and simmer on very low fire for at least 20
minutes.

Source:  Best Barbeque Recipes, by Mildred Fischer
