Title: CAVALIER DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads, Cheese
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  Mayonnaise Or Salad Dressing
2 tb Blue Cheese; Crumbled
1 tb Catsup
1 tb Vinegar
1 sm Onion; Piece
Put all of the ingredients in a blender container and blend until
smooth. Allow to chill at least 30 minutes before serving.

Makes 1 cup.
