Title: CATTLE KINGS' RANCH HAND CHICKEN SOUP
Categories: Soups, Poultry, Rice, Tex-mex
Yield: 4 Servings
6 c  Strong flavor chicken stock
2 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Chopped carrots
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Chopped red onion
1/2 c  Chopped zucchini
1/2 c  Chopped yellow squash
1 c  Cooked rice
Salt, to taste
Black pepper,to taste
4    Sprigs of chopped cilantro
- leaves for garnish
Bring stock to a boil in a large pot. In a separate large pot, heat
oil and saute carrots, celery, onion, zucchini and yellow squash. Add
boiling stock to vegetables, then stir in rice; season with salt and
pepper. Ladle into bowls and top with cilantro.
