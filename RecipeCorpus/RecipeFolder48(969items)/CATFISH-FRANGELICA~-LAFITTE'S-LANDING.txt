Title: CATFISH FRANGELICA~ LAFITTE'S LANDING
Categories: Creole, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Catfish fillets(5-7oz ea)
1 x  Cayenne pepper to taste
1/2 c  Butter or margarine,melted
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Butter
1 x  Salt to taste
1 x  Paprika for color
1/4 c  Dry white wine
1/4 c  Pecan halves
MMMMM------------------FRANGELICA BUTTER SAUCE-----------------------
1 c  Heavy cream
1 c  Butter,cold chipped
3 tb Frangelica liqueur
1. Thaw frozen fish according to package directions.
2. Prepare Frangelica Butter Sauce.
3. Season catfish with salt and cayenne, sprinkle with paprika and
place in baking dish.
4. Add melted butter, wine and lemon juice.
5. Bake uncovered in preheated 450'F. oven 5 to 7 minutes (do not
overcook).
6. Place on serving platter and top with Frangelica Butter Sauce and
pecans sauteed in 2 Tablespoons butter.
*** FRANGELICA BUTTER SAUCE ***
1. In medium saucepan, slowly reduce cream and Frangelica together
until approximately 6 tablespoons remain.
2. Slowly add butter, a few chips at a time, swirling pan constantly
to incorporate butter into cream (do not stir); continue swirling
until all butter has been added.
