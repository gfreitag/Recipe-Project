Title: CHARLEY BRENON III'S SWEDISH APPLE PIE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Egg, well beaten
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Chopped apples
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Mix the stuff together and spread it into an 8 x 8 x 2 greased pan.
Bake for 30 minutes at 350F. Sprinkle with confectioners sugar when
done (if desired).
