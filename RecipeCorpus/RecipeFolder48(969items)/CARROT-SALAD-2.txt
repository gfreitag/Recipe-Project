Title: CARROT SALAD 2
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
5 c  Carrots, sliced
1 c  Tomato soup
3/4 c  Vinegar, white
1/2 c  Oil
1 ts Mustard, dry
1 ts Salt
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1    Onion, cut in rings
1    Green pepper, large
3/4 c  Sugar, white
Parboil carrots for about 12 - 15 minutes.  Add rest of ingredients
and marinate in refrigerator at least
24    hours.  Serve chilled
