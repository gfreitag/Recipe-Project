Title: CARMEL APPLE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Container (8-oz) frozen
Whipped topping thawed
1 pk Dry instant butterscotch
Pudding
1 cn (8-oz) crushed pineapple
With juice
3 c  Diced apples
1 c  Dry roasted nuts
1 c  Marshmallows
Mix cool whip, butterscotch pudding and crushed pineapple with juice.
Add apples, roasted nuts and marshmallows. Keep in refrigerator.
