Title: CARROT FLUFF
Categories: Passover, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
-SHERI KONOPKO (GXCH63B)
1 c  Cake meal
3 lb Carrots; -cooked and mashed
3    Eggs; separated
1 ts Salt
3/4 c  Sugar
1    Stick margarine; melted
Preheat oven-375 Combine cake meal and salt.
In large bowl combine carrots; sugar, margarine, and yolks Mix well
Make a well in center and add dry ingredients Stir until well blended
Beat egg white until still, but not dry fold into carrots Lightly
spoon into greased baking dish

Bake for 45 min.
