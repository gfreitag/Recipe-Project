Title: CHAR'S PASTA SALAD
Categories: Pasta, Easy, Quick
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Pasta Shapes (any)
4    Hard Cooked Eggs
1 c  Cubed Ham (or Hormel can)
1/2 c  Hellman's Mayonaise
1 ts Dijonaise
ds Garlic Powder
ds Salt and Pepper to taste
Boil pasta, chop hard cooked eggs, mix with the remainder of the
ingredients.
