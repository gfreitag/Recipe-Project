Title: CHARCOAL GRILLED SHIITAKES
Categories: Side dish, Grill, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
8 oz Shiitakes
1 tb Olive oil
1 tb Tamari
1 tb Garlic, crushed
1 ts Rosemary, minced
Salt & black pepper
1 ts Maple syrup
1 ts Sesame oil, otional
Rinse mushrooms.  Remove & discard stems.  Toss mushrooms with
remaining ingredients & marinate for 5 minutes. Grill caps over coals
until lightly charred. Serve hot.
