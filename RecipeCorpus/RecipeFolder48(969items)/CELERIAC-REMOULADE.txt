Title: CELERIAC REMOULADE
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Dijon mustard
2 tb Finely minced parsley
2 tb Finely minced tarragon
1    Garlic clove; finely minced
2    Sweet pickles (optional)
-- finely chopped
2    Young celeriac knobs
-- peeled & coarsely grated
In a medium sized bowl, mix together mayonnaise, mustard, parsley,
tarragon, garlic and sweet pickles (if used). Add celeriac and toss
until completely coated. Cover and chill until ready to serve.

From: The Cook's Garden catalog - Spring/Summer 1993 (page 13)
