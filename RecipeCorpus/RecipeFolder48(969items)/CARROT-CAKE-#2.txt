Title: CARROT CAKE #2
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Soda
1 ts Cinnamon
3    Beaten eggs
1 1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 c  Grated carrots
1 sm Cn crushed pineapple
2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Chopped pecans
Glaze:
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
1 ts Butter
1 ts Vanilla
Into a large mixing bowl sift dry ingredients.  Add eggs, oil,
carrots, & pineapple.  Mix well.  Add vanilla & pecans. Bake in tube
pan or 6 1/2x 10 1/2-inch oblong cake pan for one hr. at 350 degrees.
Glaze with the following, if desired.

Glaze:

Combine sugar & milk in saucepan.  Bring to a boil & cook over low
heat until mixture forms a soft ball in water. Cool. Add butter &
vanilla; beat until creamy. Pour over cake.
