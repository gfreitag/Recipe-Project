Title: CATTLE RANCH BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Savory, Sauces
Yield: 1 Serving
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 lb Bacon grease or 1 cup wesson
- oil
1/2 lb Butter
1 tb Pepper, black,freshly ground
1 tb Cayenne
3 ea Onion; chopped
1 ea Worcestershire sauce  10 oz
1 ea Ketchup, 24 oz
1 tb Salt
1 tb Celery salt
1 ts Garlic salt
Combine all ingredients and simmer on very low fire for at least 20
minutes.  Makes sauce for 25 lbs of meat. Excellent for spareribs.
