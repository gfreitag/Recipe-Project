Title: CASERA SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Vegetables, Mexican, Tomatoes
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Tomatoes; Finely Chopped
1/2 c  Onion; Chopped
1 ea Clove Garlic; Finely Chopped
1 ea Jalapeno Chile; Canned, *
1/2 ts Jalepeno Chile Liquid
1 tb Cilantro; Fresh,Snipped Fine
1 tb Lemon Juice
1/2 ts Oregano Leaves; Dried
1 1/2 ts Vegetable Oil
*  Jalapeno Chile should be seeded and finely chopped.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Mix all ingredients in glass or plastic bowl.
Cover and refrigerate up to 7 days. Makes about 2 cups of sauce.
