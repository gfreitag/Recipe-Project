Title: CARROT PINEAPPLE BREAD
Categories: Donna germn, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Can crush pineapple juice
3/4 c  Crushed pineapple
1/2 c  Raw, grated carrots
1 1/2 tb Applesauce
3/4 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Brown sugar
3/4 c  Oats
3/4 c  Whole wheat flour
1 1/2 c  Bread Flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
Put in breadmaker as per usual you machine. I baked it on quick, but
regular should work fine.  The recipe was adapted from one in Donna
German's book II.
