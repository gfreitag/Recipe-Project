Title: CARIBBEAN RUB OR JERK RUB
Categories: Rubs
6 TB garlic -- minced
Yield: 1 Servings
6 TB onion -- minced
6 TB dried minced onion
2 TB allspice
1 TB dried ground chipotle
2 TB Hungarian paprika
2 TB brown sugar
4 1/2 ts fresh thyme -- minced
4 1/2 ts cinnamon
1 1/2 ts nutmeg
1/2 ts ground habanera
2    lemons -- zest

In a bowl combine all ingredients.
