Title: CARROTS (GLAZED STYLE)
Categories: Side dish, Very low fa
8 Lg Carrots -- thinly sliced
Yield: 8 Servings
1/4 c  Apple Juice, Frozen
Concentrate -- thawed
:
1 TB Orange Rind -- grated
1 ts Cornstarch
1/8 ts Ground Cloves

Steam carrots for 15 min or until tender.

Combine apple juice, orange rind, cornstarch and cloves in a lg
saucepan.

Mix until smooth, then cook and stir constantly until mixture has
thickened and cleared.

Add cooked carrots to the sauce.

Serve hot.
