Title: CHEDDAR CHEESE BREAD (AUTOMATIC BREAD MAKER)
Categories: Breadmaker, Cheese, Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
1 pk Yeast (active dry)
3 c  Bread flour
1/4 c  Nonfat dry milk
1 tb Butter, soft
1 ts Salt
2 tb Sugar
1 1/4 c  Water, warm
1 1/2 c  Cheddar Cheese, grated
Extra-sharp
Note:  Use all ingredients at room temperature. This recipe was
provided with the DAK automatic bread machine. Place all ingredients
into bread pan in the order listed. Select "white bread" setting.
Push start!!!!
