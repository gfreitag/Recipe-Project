Title: CHEDDAR & BACON BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Ounces
2 1/2 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
1 1/3 tb Butter
1 c  Milk
MMMMM-----------------ADD FOLLOWING 1ST KNEADING----------------------
2/3 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
2/3 c  Cooked, crumbled bacon
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order. Set "baking control" at 10 o'clock. Select "white bread" and
push Start. In hot & humid weather, use 1/8 c less water.
