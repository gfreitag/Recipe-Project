Title: CARROT CAKE #1
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
1 tb Cinnamon
2 ts Soda
2/3 c  Cooking oil
2 c  Grated carrots
4    Eggs
1 c  Chopped nuts
Frosting:
1 sm Pk cream cheese
1/4    Stick margarine
1 ts Vanilla
2 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
Mix all ingredients in order; beat well.  Pour into 2 9x5x2-inch loaf
pans. Bake at 350 degrees one hr., or until done. Frost with the
following frosting, if desired.

Frosting:

Mix all ingredients well.  Spread frosting over cooled cake.
