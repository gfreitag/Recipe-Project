Title: CELERY SALT
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Celery seed
(heaping tb)
2 tb Unrefined coarse salt
(heaping tb)
Spoon the seeds into the blender and process at highest speed until
ground quite fine--probably about 30 seconds. When fine, add the salt
and process at high speed until the salt is as fine as you want it.
Store in a small jar with a tight lid. SOURCE: Blend It Splendid (pg.
135)
