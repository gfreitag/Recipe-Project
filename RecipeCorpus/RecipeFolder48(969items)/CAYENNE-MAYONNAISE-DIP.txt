Title: CAYENNE MAYONNAISE DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Dips
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour Cream
1 x  Cayenne Pepper; To Taste
Blend all of the ingredients together until smooth. Cover and chill.
Makes about 2 cups of dip SUGGESTED DIPPERS: Seafood Chunks,
Pineapple, Cherry Tomatoes, Rye Or Cheese Crackers
