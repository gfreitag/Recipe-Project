Title: CAVIAR KISSES
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 2 Servings
1 sm Cucumber, scrubbed and
-trimmed
1/3 c  Sour Cream
1 ts Dried dill weed
Freshly ground black pepper
-to taste
1    Jar red salmon caviar
Fresh dill sprigs
8    Thin slices whole-wheat
-bread
Butter or margarine
Serves 2

Slice cucumber into 1/4-inch rounds.  In a small bowl, combine sour
cream, dried dill and pepper.  Place one teaspoon of the sour cream
mixture on each cucumber slice. Garnish each with about 1/2 teaspoon
caviar and a dill sprig.  Cut bread slices with heart-shaped cookie
cutter. Toast and butter. Place cucumber slices in center of serving
plate and surround with toast hearts.

From: Military Lifestyle magazine, Feb 90. Posted by: Bill McGimpsey
