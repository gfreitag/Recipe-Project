Title: CAVIAR POTATOES
Categories: Appetizers, Potatoes, Fish/shellf
Yield: 4 Servings
7 sm New potatoes
2 1/2 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Plus 1 Tbsp. commercial sour
Cream
About 2-1/2 tsp. black
Caviar
About 2-1/2 tsp. gold caviar
Drained
Cook potatoes in boiling water for 15 minutes or until tender.  Drain
and chill.

Slice potatoes crosswise into 1/4 inch slices.  Place potato slices
on a serving platter.  Top each slice with about 3/4 tsp. sour cream
and about 1/2 tsp. caviar.  Yield: about 3 dozen
