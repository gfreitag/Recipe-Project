Title: CARIBBEAN RUB OR JERK RUB (G&C)
Categories: Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
6 tb Garlic -- minced
6 tb Onion -- minced
6 tb Dried minced onion
2 tb Allspice
1 tb Dried ground chipotle
2 tb Hungarian paprika
2 tb Brown sugar
4 1/2 ts Fresh thyme -- minced
4 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Ground habanera
2    Lemons -- zest
In a bowl combine all ingredients.
