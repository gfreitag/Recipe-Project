Title: CARROT SALAD (SATTERLY)
Categories: Salads, Appetizers, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
4 md Carrots, shredded
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1 tb Basil
1 tb Scallions
3 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Safflower oil
Salt & pepper
Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl, toss & serve.
