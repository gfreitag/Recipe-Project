Title: CARROT & RAISIN MUFFINS 1
Categories: Muffins, Quickbreads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/4 c  Flour
3/4 c  Creamota
-(quick cooking
-rolled oats)
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
3/4 c  Milk
1 c  Grated carrot
3/4 c  Raisins
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1    Egg
3 tb Oil
Mix flour, creamota, baking powder & salt. Stir in carrot, raisins &
cinnamon. Beat egg, milk & oil. Make a well in centre of dry
ingredients, pour in liquid. Mix lightly. 3/4 fill greased muffin
pans & bake at 375oF for 15-20 mins. From: Ricardo Landrau Date:
09-15-93
