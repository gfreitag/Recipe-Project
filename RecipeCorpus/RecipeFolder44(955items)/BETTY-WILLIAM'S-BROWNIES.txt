Title: BETTY WILLIAM'S BROWNIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 8 Servings
4    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1    Stick butter
1 c  Flour
1 cn Hersher chocolate syrup --
1    #
1 c  Nuts -- chopped
Mix eggs, sugar and butter well in large bowl of electric mixer. Add
remaining ingredients and mix well. Bake 30 minutes at 350 F in
greased 9 x 13 pan. Cool and frost with: 1 Cup sugar, 6 Tablespoons
butter, 6 Tablespoons milk.  Mix and bring to boil. Boil 1 minute.
Remove from heat and add 1 cup chocolate chips . Beat with a spoon
until thickened. Spread and cut in bars
