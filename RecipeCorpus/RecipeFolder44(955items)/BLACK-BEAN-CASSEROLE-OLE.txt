Title: BLACK BEAN CASSEROLE OLE
Categories: Vegetarian, Tex-mex, Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Olive oil
1 c  Onions; chopped
3/4 c  Cornmeal, yellow, + 2 tbs
2 ts Chili powder
1 3/4 c  Soymilk, lite
1 lb Beans, black, canned; rinsed
-and drained
1 lb Corn, whole kernel, canned;
-drained
1 lb Tomatoes, stewed, canned
4 oz Chilies, green, canned;
-chopped
1/4 c  Nutritional yeast cheesy
-sauce
Preheat oven to 350.

Lightly oil an 8-inch square baking pan or spray with a nonstick
cooking spray.

In a large bowl, combine all ingredients, except cheesy sauce. Mix
well. Place in prepared pan. Drop cheesy sauce on top by spoonfuls.

Bake, uncovered, 45 minutes.
