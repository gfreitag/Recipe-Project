Title: BEST EVER FUDGE CAKE
Categories: Weight watc, Cakes
Yield: 16 Servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/4 c  Diet margarine
2 tb Diet margarine
2 tb Margarine
1 ts Margarine
2 lg Eggs
2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla extract
8 ts Powdered sugar
1. Preheat oven to 350ยก.  Spray Bundt pan with Pam. 2. Cream
margarines and sugar.  Add eggs. 3. Combine dry ingredients and add
to mixture. 4. Add buttermilk. Mix well and pour into sprayed Bundt
pan. 5. Bake 40 minutes at 350ยก.  Cool 15 minutes and then remove
from pan. 6. Sprinkle with the powdered sugar before serving.
