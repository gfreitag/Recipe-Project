Title: BEST EVER PLAIN OLE PEAS (BLACK-EYES)
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
-JUDY GARNETT PJXG05A
16 oz Pkg. dried black-eyed peas
6 c  Water
4 sl Hickory-smoked bacon
1 tb Salt
1 tb Sugar
1 tb White wine vinegar
1/4 ts -Black pepper
1/4 ts Garlic salt
Sort and wash peas; place in a large Dutch oven. Cover with water 2
inches above peas; let soak 8 hours. Drain. Add 6 cups water and
remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and
simmer 1 1/2 hours. Yield: 6 cups.
Source: Southern Living
