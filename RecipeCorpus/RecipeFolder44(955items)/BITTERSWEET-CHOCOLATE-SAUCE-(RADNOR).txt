Title: BITTERSWEET CHOCOLATE SAUCE (RADNOR)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Whipping cream
2 tb Sugar
4 oz Bittersweet Hawaiian
-chocolate, chopped
1 tb Unsalted butter
1. Combine the cream and sugar in a small saucepan and heat to a
boil. Pour the hot mixture over the chocolate and butter in a medium
bowl. Stir until completely melted and smooth. Strain through a fine
wire mesh strainer into a serving bowl. Serve warm. Reheat in a
double boiler if made in advance and refrigerated.
