Title: BLACK & TAN
Categories: Alcohol, Beers, Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2    Bass Ale
1/2    Guiness Stout
Fill glass half full with Harps, then dribble Guiness ever so
carefully so that the beers don't mix (much). A drink that takes its
name from the uniforms worn by the British Troops that occupied
Ireland.
