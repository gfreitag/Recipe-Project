Title: BIG BUCKET IN THE SKY CHICKEN
Categories: Salads, Meats, Main dishes
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Chicken cutup
3 c  Selfrising flour
1 ts Salt
2    Envelopes tomato cup of soup
Or
2    Envelopes cream of soup
And
1 tb Paprika
2 pk Good seasons Italian
Dressing mix(dry packets)
1/4 lb Butter
place all ingredients in a bowl and stir then add some of the mix to a
plastic bag add chicken and shake. then put butter in a pan and place
chicken on top bake 350 for 40 minutes. this was on Phil's show
recipe from Gloria the copycat lady.
