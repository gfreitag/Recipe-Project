Title: BLUE CHEESE DIPPING SAUCE
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Bowl
1/4 lb Blue, Roquefort or
Gorgonzola cheese
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Wine vinegar
ds Hot pepper sauce, to taste
In a small bowl, mash the blue cheese, leaving some small lumps,
Whisk in the mayonnaise until blended. Add the remaining ingredients
and whisk to blend well. Cover and refregerate until serving time.
