Title: BLACK WALNUT TAFFY
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1 tb Butter or butter substitute
1/2 c  Sweetened condensed milk
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Molasses
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Water
Boil molasses, sugar, salt, milk, water, and butter to hard ball
stage (265 ~ 270 F).  Add flavoring.  Spread nuts over the bottom of
a well-buttered pan.  Pour taffy over nuts. Cool. Pull until stiff
and creamy. Cut in 1-inch pieces. Isabel Tovill, Lancaster, OH.
