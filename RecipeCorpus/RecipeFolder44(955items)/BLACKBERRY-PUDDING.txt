Title: BLACKBERRY PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Butter
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
pn Salt
1/2 c  Milk
Sweetened blackberries or
- cherries
Cream 1/2 c sugar and butter; blend in flour, baking powder and salt.
Stir in milk; pour into greased 8 x 8 inch baking dish. Cover with
blackberries. Bake at 325 to 350 degrees for about 45 minutes.
Sprinkle top with remaining sugar; bake for 5 to 10 minutes longer.

SOURCE: 1979 New Orleans Times-Picayune Recipe Contest Cookbook Typed
for you by Nancy Coleman
