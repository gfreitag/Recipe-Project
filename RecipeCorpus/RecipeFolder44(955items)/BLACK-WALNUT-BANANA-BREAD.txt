Title: BLACK WALNUT-BANANA BREAD
Categories: Bread machi, Fruits
Yield: 14 Servings
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
2 tb Unsalted butter
1 sm Banana -- sliced
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
2 tb Water
2 tb Milk
1 lg Egg
1/2 c  Chopped black walnuts or
Pecans
Add the flour, butter, banana,sugar,salt,yeast,water, milk,and egg to
bread machine. Process the ingredients according to your
manufacturer's

instructions for a basic bread setting or a raisin bread setting. Add
the nuts to the dough at the beginning of the second kneading cycle
of the machine.
