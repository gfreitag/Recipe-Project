Title: BISQUICK FUDGE BROWNIES
Categories: Chocolate, Brownies, Bisquick, Impossible
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Chocolate chips
1/4 c  Margarine
2 c  Biscuit baking mix
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
1    Eggs; beaten
1 ts Vanilla
Preheat oven to 350~F. In large saucepan, over low heat, melt 1 cup
chips with margarine; remove from heat. Add biscuit mix, condensed
milk, egg and vanilla. Stir in remaining chips. Turn into
well-greased 13x9" pan. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until brownies begin
to pull away from sides of pan. Cool. Garnish as desired. Cut into
bars.
