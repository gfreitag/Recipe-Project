Title: BLACKEN MIX
Categories: Condiments, Prodigy, Cajun
Yield: 1 /4 cup  Use about 1-2 tsp mix per serving. 1 tsp each:
onion powder
garlic salt
ground red papper
dried basil, crushed 1/2 tsp each: ground white pepper ground black
pepper crushed dried thyme 1/4 tsp ground sage For fish, brush fish
with melted margarine or salad oil; then coat both sides with Blacken
Mix. For beef or chicken, do the same without oil.Cook in UNOILED
iron skillet at medium-high heat or broil.
