Title: BITTERSWEET CHOCOLATE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate, Upload
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Pudding mix, chocolate
-(6 serving size)
1 c  Water
6 1/2 oz Cola beverage
Whipped topping (optional)
Combine pudding mix, water and cola in saucepan. Cook and stir over
medium heat until mixture comes to full boil. Remove from heat, pour
int individual dessert dishes. Chill, serve plain or with whipped
topping, if desired.
