Title: BIG BOY'S TOMATO & SPICE DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads, Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
1/2 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
2 tb Ketchup
1 tb Heinz 57 Sauce
1 tb Light Karo Corn Syrup
Combine salad dressing, ketchup, Heinz 57 and corn syrup. Stir it with
rubber bowl scraper till thoroughly blended. Keep refrigerated up to
30 days. Source: Gloria Pitzer's MakeAlike Recipes
