Title: BHINDI MASALA
Categories: Vegetables, Ethnic
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Oil
1 lg Onion, chopped
10 ea Garlic cloves, minced
2 ea Fresh green chilies, chopped
1 ts Turmeric
1 tb Cumin, ground
3 lb Okra
Salt
Heat oil in large pot.  Add onion, garlic & chilies. Fry over medium
heat till browned.  Stir in turmeric & cumin & fry for 2 minutes. Mix
in okra & salt. Cover, reduce heat & simmer for 8 to 10 minutes,
stirring occasionally to prevent burning.  Serve hot with plain rice
or chapatis.

Rani, "Feast of India"
