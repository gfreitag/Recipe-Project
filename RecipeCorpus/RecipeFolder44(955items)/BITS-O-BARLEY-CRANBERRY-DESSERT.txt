Title: BITS-O-BARLEY CRANBERRY DESSERT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 1/2 c  Cranberry juice
2/3 c  AM Bits-O-Barley
1 pn Salt
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Lemon peel
1/2 c  Honey
Bring first six ingredients to a boil, then cover, remove from heat
and let stand until all the juice is absorbed. Add honey, blend well.
Serve warm or cold.
