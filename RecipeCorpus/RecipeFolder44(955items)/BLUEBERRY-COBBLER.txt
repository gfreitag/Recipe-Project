Title: BLUEBERRY COBBLER
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1    Egg
1    Stick of butter
16 oz Blueberries
Melt butter in a 9x13 inch baking dish.  Add all ingredients except
the blueberries in a large mixing bowl. Mix well; then, when
margarine is melted, pour the ingredients into the baking dish. Add
the blueberries. Don't mix them in; just add them.  Bake at 350
degrees. Randy Rigg
