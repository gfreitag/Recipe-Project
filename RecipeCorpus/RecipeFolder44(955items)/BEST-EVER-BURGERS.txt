Title: BEST EVER BURGERS
Categories: Quick meals
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Bread crumbs, fine, dry
2 tb Salsa, pizza sauce, etc.
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 lb Lean ground beef
4    Cheese slices, mozzarella, -
-monterey jack,cheddar,etc.
4    Kaiser rolls or buns
4    Lettuce leaves
Set oven to broil. Stir together the bread crumbs, salsa, salt, and
pepper. Add ground beef. Mix well. Shape into four patties about 3/4
inches thick. Place patties on broiler rack and broil 4 to 5 inches
from heat for 12 to 14 minutes, turning once. Top with cheese slice
and broil one minute more. Serve on kaiser roll with a lettuce leaf
and additional salsa if desired.

473 calories, 34 g protein, 34 g carbs, 22 g fat
