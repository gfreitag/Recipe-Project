Title: BLOODY MARY MARINADE
Categories: Marinades, Bar-b-q
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM------------------FOR POULTRY, PORK, FISH-----------------------
3/4 c  V-8 juice
1/4 c  Vodka
1 ts Prepared horseradish
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Soy sauce
1 ds Hot pepper sauce
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Makes about 1 cup
