Title: BEURRE MAITRE D'HOTEL
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 lb Sweet butter
1 ts Fresh lemon juice
- (amount may be doubled)
1 tb Freshly minced parsley
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
This basic recipe is very good as it is, but variations using
different fresh herbs (minced shallots or garlic, mustard,
horseradish, and so forth) are just as wonderful. SOFTEN THE SWEET
butter. Mix in fresh lemon juice, freshly minced parsley, salt and
freshly ground pepper. Makes 1/2 Cup
