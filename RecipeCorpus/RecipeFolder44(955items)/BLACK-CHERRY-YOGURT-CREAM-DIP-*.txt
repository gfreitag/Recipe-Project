Title: BLACK CHERRY YOGURT CREAM DIP *
Categories: Dips, Fruits
Yield: 1 Cups
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
8 oz Black cherry yogurt
3 oz Cream cheese; softened
1 tb Powdered sugar
1/4 ts Vanilla
In blender, combine all ingredients; process 1-2 minutes or until
smooth. Pour into small serving bowl; refrigerate 2 hours or until
slightly thickened. Serve with fresh fruit dippers.

NOTE: Can make this any flavor you want by changing the yogurt.
