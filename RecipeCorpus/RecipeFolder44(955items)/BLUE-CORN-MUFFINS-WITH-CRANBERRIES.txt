Title: BLUE CORN MUFFINS WITH CRANBERRIES
Categories: Muffins, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Finely ground blue cornmeal
1 c  Unbleached white flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
pn Salt
1    Egg
1    Egg white
3/4    Skim milk
1/3 c  Canola oil
1/2 c  Honey or maple syrup
3/4 c  Cranberries
Preheat oven to 350.  Stir together dry ingredients. In separate
bowl, stir together liquid ingredients. Mix liquid and dry
ingredients until just combined; batter will be lumpy. Stir in
cranberries. Fill oiled muffin tin (I use a veg. spray) about 3/4
full with batter. Bake until toothpick comes clean, about 18-20 min.
Makes 10 muffins  (I got 11).

Per serving:  208 cal, 4g protein, 8g fat (!), 30g carbo, 17mg
cholesterol, 211mg sodium, 1g fiber.

Source: Vegetarian Times
