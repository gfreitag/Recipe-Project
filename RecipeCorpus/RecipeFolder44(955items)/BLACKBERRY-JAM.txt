Title: BLACKBERRY JAM
Categories: Condiments, Jams
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Blackberries
2 c  Water
1 pk Powdered fruit pectin
5 c  Sugar
Crush fruit thoroughly.  Add water and fruit pectin. Stir until
pectin is dissolved.  Heat to boiling. Boil 5-10 minutes. Add sugar.
Stir until dissolved. Boil 3-5 minutes, stirring frequently, or until
thick. The Household Searchlight
