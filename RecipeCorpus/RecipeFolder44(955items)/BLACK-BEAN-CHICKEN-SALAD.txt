Title: BLACK BEAN CHICKEN SALAD
Categories: Poultry, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
Can black beans
Cooked chicken
Whole kernel corn
Chopped tomatoes
Chopped scallions
Lime juice
Olive oil
Cilantro
Mixed rinsed and drained canned black beans with cubes of cooked
chicken, thawed whole kernel corn, chopped tomatoes and chopped
scallions. Moisten with lime juice and a small amount of olive oil.
Season to taste with fresh chopped cilantro.
