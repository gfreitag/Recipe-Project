Title: BILLIE~S POTATOES
Categories: Potatoes
Yield: 1 12
1 lg Sour cream
1 pk Dry ranch dip
1 pk Dry italian mix
1    Bottle italian dressing
1 lb Bacon
1 lg Onion
5 lb Potatoes
1 tb Garlic powder
1 pk Shredded chedder cheese
Fry bacon and remove,in grease fry sliced potatoes till almost
done.Add garlic,onion and fry all till done.Add rest of
ingredients(except bacon)cook till bubbles.top with crumbled bacon
