Title: BLUE-CHEESE SAUCE
Categories: Chicken, Sauces, Cheese, Four star
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Blue cheese -- crumbled
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Plain low-fat yogurt
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
1/4 ts Coarsely ground pepper
In a medium bowl, stir together all of the ingredients. Makes about 1
1/2 cups. Serve with chicken. Each tablespoon: About 55 calories, 1 g
protein, 0 g carbohydrate, 5 g total fat (2 g saturated), 6 mg
cholesterol, 95 mg sodium.
