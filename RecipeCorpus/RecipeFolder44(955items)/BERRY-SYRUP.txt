Title: BERRY SYRUP
Categories: Sauces, Fruits, Mcdougall
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz Frozen unsweetened berries
12 oz Frzn apple juice concentrate
1 tb Cornstarch
1/4 c  Cold water
Combine berries of your choice and undiluted apple juice concentrate
in cause pan. Mix cornstarch in water until dissolved Slowly add a
little of the cornstarch mixture to berry mixture while it cooks over
medium temperature Continue adding cornstarch until syrup is desired
thickness Serve over waffles or pancakes Makes about 2 1/2 cups
