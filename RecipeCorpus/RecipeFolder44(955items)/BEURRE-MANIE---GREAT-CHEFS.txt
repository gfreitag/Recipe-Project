Title: BEURRE MANIE - GREAT CHEFS
Categories: None
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Flour
2 tb Butter
For the beurre manie, simply melt the butter in a saute pan, add
the flour and then cook over medium heat for a minute or two to take
the "floury" flavor out of the mix.

Pour the sauce over the rabbit and serve.

Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions
:    Box 71112, New Orleans, Louisiana - 1983
:    Chef Gerard Crozier, Crozier's Restaurant, New Orleans
