Title: BLUE CORN BLUEBERRY MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Quick
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 c  AM Blue Cornmeal
1 c  AM Unbleached White Flour
2 ts Non-alum baking powder
1/2 ts Sea salt (optional)
1 1/2 c  Water
2 ts Honey (may be doubled)
1/4 c  AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Fresh or frozen blueberries
Stir dry ingredients together.  Combine liquid ingredients. Mix
liquid and dry ingredients and stir until just mixed. Gently stir in
blueberries. Fill oiled muffin tins 2/3 full.  Bake in a preheated
oven at 400 F. for 20 minutes or until done.
