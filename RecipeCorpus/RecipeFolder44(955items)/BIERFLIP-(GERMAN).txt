Title: BIERFLIP (GERMAN)
Categories: Cyberealm, Beverages, German
Yield: 2 Servings
1 3/16 qt Beer
8 oz Sugar
1 pn Salt
1 pn Cinnamon
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Milk or Cream
1/2 c  Rum
1.Mix all ingridients in a high pot and beat with mixer until foamy.
2.Serve immediately

Out of "1 X 1 Der guten Kueche"
