Title: BIG JOHN'S MARINADE
Categories: Sauces, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/4 c  Salad oil, olive oil, or mix
Two
3/4 c  Soy sauce
2 1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1 ts Crushed garlic
3/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Dry mustard
1 tb Fresh ground black pepper
1 ts Dried parsley flakes
1/3 c  Lemon juice
Prepare this marinate at least 6 hours in advance of use and always
marinate meat for 6 -8 hours or overnight. Baste during the
grilling/smoking process. Mop it on before serving.
