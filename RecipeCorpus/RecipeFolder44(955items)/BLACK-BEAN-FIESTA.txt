Title: BLACK BEAN FIESTA
Categories: Beans, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
6 tb Fresh lime juice
1/4 c  Chopped cilantro
1 ts Ground cumin
4 c  Cooked black beans
2 md Red bell peppers diced
1 md Red onion diced
1 1/2 c  Cooked corn kernals
1    Or 2 jalepeno peppers
Seeded -- diced ( opt )
Salt and pepper
In a large bowl mix everything except beans and vegetables. add beans
and vegetables and toss well. taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
Garnish with extra cilantro.
