Title: BEST SOURDOUGH BREAD
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  Sourdough Starter
2/3 c  Milk
2 tb Butter
2 tb Sugar (natural)
1 tb Salt
3 c  Bread Flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
Put everything into breadmachine in order listed.  Set machine for
White bread, light crust and regular or delayed cycle. Enjoy! For 1
pound loaf. revised by "kingman"
