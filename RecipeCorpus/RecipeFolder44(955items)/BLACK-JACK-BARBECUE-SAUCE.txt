Title: BLACK JACK BARBECUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Strong black coffee
1 c  Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
3 tb Chili powder
2 ts Salt
2 c  Chopped onions
1/4 c  Minced hot chili peppers
6    Cloves garlic - minced
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and simmer 25 minutes. Strain or
puree in a blender or food processor. Refridgerate between uses.
Makes 5 cups.
