Title: BESAN BURFI
Categories: Desserts, Indian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Besan
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
4    Seeds Cardamom
Nuts (optional)
1. melt shortening in a pan. 2. Turn down heat and add cardamom and
Besan. 3. Fry, stirring constantly to prevent burning till it has
changed color to brown and smells done.  (Test: a few drops of water
sprinkled on it splutters instantly). 4. Turn off heat and stir in
sugar. 5. Spread on a platter 1/2" thick. 6. Cut into diamond shapes
after it has cooled down.
