Title: BLUE-CHEESE-STUFFED BURGERS
Categories: Picnic, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Egg, beaten
1/4 c  Bread crumbs, dry
2 tb Onion, finely chopped
2 tb Ketchup
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1    Pepper (dash)
1 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Blue-cheese
4    Buns
Instead of just plain burgers on the grill, try our Midwest Living
creation, flavored with Worcestershire sauce and seasoned salt.

1.  In a mixing bowl, combine egg, bread crumbs, onion, ketchup,
worcestershire sauce and pepper.

2.  Add ground beef and mix well.

3.  Shape beef into eight 4-inch-round patties (1/4" thick). Spoon 1
tb
crumbled blue cheese in the center of 4 of the patties. Top with
remaining meat patties.  Press to seal the edges.

4.  Grill the meat over medium coals for 14 to 16 minutes, turning
once.
Top with crumbled blue-cheese if you like.

5.  Serve on buns and top as you like.
