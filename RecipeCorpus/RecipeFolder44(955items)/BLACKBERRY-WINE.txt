Title: BLACKBERRY WINE
Categories: Alcohol, Southern, Wine
2 qt berry juice
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/3 pt water
3 c  sugar
1/4 c  french brandy

Combine juice, water, and sugar, let stand 3 days in a cool place Skim
well.  Let stand to ferment 6 days longer. Remove froth and add
brandy. Close tightly. In 2 days, draw off sediments. Bottle tightly
and seal. Wine improves with age...
