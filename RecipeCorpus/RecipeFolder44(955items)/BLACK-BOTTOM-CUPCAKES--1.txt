Title: BLACK BOTTOM CUPCAKES -1
Categories: Cupcakes, Chocolate
Yield: 48 Servings
Cream Cheese Mixture-----
8 oz Cream Cheese
1    Egg
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Chocolate Chips --
Miniature
Chocolate Cake Mixture-----
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking Soda
1/4 c  Cocoa Powder
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Oil
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Vanilla
Mix together cream cheese mixture. In a separate bowl, mix together
chocolate cake mixture. Place one tablespoon cake mixture, then one
teaspoon cheese mixture into small cupcake papers. Bake 350 degrees
for 20 minutes.

Yield:  48 small cupcakes.
