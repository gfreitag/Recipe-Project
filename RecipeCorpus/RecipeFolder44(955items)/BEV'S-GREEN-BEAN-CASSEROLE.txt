Title: BEV'S GREEN BEAN CASSEROLE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Shoe-peg corn
1 cn Green beans french cut
1 cn Cream of celery
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Grated cheese
1    Stick of butter
1    Roll of Ritz crackers
1/2 c  Slivered almonds
Mix forst two ingredients.  Add to next 4 ingredients.
Melt butter to crushed Ritz crackers for topping. Top with slivered
almonds.  Grease casserole dish and bake
350    degrees for 45 min.
