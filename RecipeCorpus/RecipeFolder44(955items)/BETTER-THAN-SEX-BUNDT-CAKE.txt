Title: BETTER THAN SEX BUNDT CAKE
Categories: Bundt, Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
1    Box yellow cake mix
1    Box instant vanilla pudd mx
4    Eggs
8 oz Sour cream
1/2 c  Oil
1/2 c  Water
1    Bar german sweet choc
Grated
6 oz Pk chocolate chips
1 c  Pecans -- chopped
Mix first 6 ingredients together and beat with electric mixer until
smooth; add next 3 ingredients. Pour batter into greased and floured
Bundt pan. Bake at 325 degrees for 55 minutes. Use toothpick to check
for doneness. Let cool; remove from pan. Keep tightly covered for
freshness.
