Title: BLACKBERRY COBBLER (FRUIT SWEET)
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
1/3 c  Butter
1 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Fruit Sweet; plus
1/4 c  Fruit Sweet (adjust to
-sweetness of fruit)
3/4 c  ;Water
2 c  Blackberries or other fruit
2 ts Almond extract
Preheat the oven to 350 F.  Melt the butter in a 9" square pan.
Combine the flour and baking powder.  Add Fruit Sweet and water. Mix
well. Pour over the butter. Combine fruit, Fruit Sweet, and almond
extract. Blend together then pour over batter.  Bake 25 minutes.

Fruit sweet is made of peach and pear juices and pineapple syrup. It
is available from Wax Orchards, RR 4 Box 320, Vashon Island, WA 98070
