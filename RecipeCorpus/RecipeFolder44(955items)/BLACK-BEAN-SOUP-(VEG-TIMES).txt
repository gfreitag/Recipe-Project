Title: BLACK BEAN SOUP (VEG TIMES)
Categories: Soups, Appetizers, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Black beans
4 c  Water
3    Bay leaves
4    Cloves
2    Onions, chopped
2    Garlic cloves
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
Salt to taste
Add all ingredients except salt to boiling water. Simmer till tender.
Puree.  Add salt to taste & serve.
