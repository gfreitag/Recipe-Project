Title: BLUE RIBBON FRENCH TOAST (STAR 11/23/92)
Categories: Breakfast, Snacks
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Eggs
1 c  Flour
1 c  Milk
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Cinnamon
8    Slices firm bread, white or-
Wheat bread
1/2 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients except for the bread. Blend well. Pour into a
shallow dish or pan. Place bread slices into the dish. Turn to coat
bread on both sides. Cook on griddle or in a skillet, in oil,
margarine or butter, until golden, turn and cook until second side is
golden. Serve with syrup or confectioners sugar.
