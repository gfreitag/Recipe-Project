Title: BLOND BROWNIES - BEVERLY'S
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
2 2/3 c  Flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Butter
1 lb Box light brown sugar
3    Eggs
6 oz Pkg chocolate chips
6 oz Pkg butterscotch chips
1 c  Pecans
Sift flour, baking powder and salt together and set aside. Melt
butter and brown sugar in saucepan; cool.
When cool, add eggs, one at a time. Add flour mixture; add chips and
nuts last.  Spread in a jelly roll pan; bake at 350 degree for 25-30
minutes. Do not overcook.  Batter is very thick.  Cut in squares when
cool.
