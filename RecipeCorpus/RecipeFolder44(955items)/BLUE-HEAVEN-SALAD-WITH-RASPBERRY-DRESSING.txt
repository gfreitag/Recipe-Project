Title: BLUE HEAVEN SALAD WITH RASPBERRY DRESSING
Categories: Wasnone
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Head lettuce, butter or
Boston bib
1/2 c  Crumbled blue cheese
1/2 c  Pecan halves -- fried
Lightly in
Olive oil -- as needed
1/2 c  Blueberries
DRESSING--
1/2 c  Raspberies, whole -- fresh
Or frozen
1/2 c  Raspberry vinegar
1/2 c  Honey
1 c  Olive oil
Wash, drain and break apart the lettuce.  Be sure to use the tiny
leaves in the middle.  Top with remaining ingredients and serve with
raspberry dressing.

Place raspberries, vinegar and honey in the food processor. Turn on
and slowly add olive oil. The dressing will be slightly thick.
