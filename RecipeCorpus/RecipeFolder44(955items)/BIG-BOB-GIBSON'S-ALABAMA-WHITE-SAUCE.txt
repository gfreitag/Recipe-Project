Title: BIG BOB GIBSON'S ALABAMA WHITE SAUCE
Categories:
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Apple cider vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 tb Pepper (black); cracked
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper (red); ground
Combine in a plastic container. Refrigerate.

SOURCE:  "The Ultimate Barbecue Sauce Cookbook" by Auchmutey and
Puckett (ISBN 1-56352-201-2)

Contributor:  Kit Anderson
