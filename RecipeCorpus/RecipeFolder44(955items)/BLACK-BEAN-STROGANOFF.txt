Title: BLACK BEAN STROGANOFF
Categories: Beef, China, Main dish, Submitted
Yield: 6 Servings
12 oz Egg noodles, medium
2 tb Butter or Margarine
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 lb Beef, boneless
- cut in thin strips
1 sm Onions
- chopped
1 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Black Bean Sauce
1/4 ts Black pepper, ground
1/8 ts Salt
1/4 c  Parsley
- minced
Cook noodles as package directs; drain and keep warm. Meanwhile,
heat 1 tablespoon butter and vegetable oil in large skillet over high
heat. Add beef; saute 2 minutes or until browned. Remove from
skillet. Melt remaining 1 tablesppon butter in same skillet. Add
onion; suate 2 minutes, or until tender. Mix in beef, sour cream,
black bean suace, pepper and salt. Heat through. Remove from heat;
stir in parsley. Serve over noodles.
