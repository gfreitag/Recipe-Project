Title: BLACK BEANS SOUP
Categories: Beans, Mexican, Soups & ste, Mc
Yield: 1 Servings
1 l  Black beans broth
1 md Onion, chopped
1 c  Tomato sauce
1 ts Salt or chiken bouillon cube
100 g  Pasta (fideo)
Side Dish:
1 md Onion, chopped
Cilantro, chopped
Chiles serranos, chopped
In a sauce pan with a little amount of vegetable oil fry the onion,
fry the pasta and add the tomato sauce, fry 10 min and add the black
beans broth, add salt or chicken bouillon cube. cook 30 min. serve
with chile, cilantro and onion chopped. Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1
