Title: BLACKENED SPICES
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts White pepper
1 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Dried thyme leaves
1/2 ts Dried oregano leaves
Mix spices.  Store in an empty spice jar labeled "Blackened Spices".
This one I use is fairly simple and we like it. Marybeth Decker
