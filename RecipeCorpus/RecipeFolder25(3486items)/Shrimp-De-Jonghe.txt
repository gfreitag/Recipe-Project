Title: Shrimp De Jonghe
Categories: Main dish, French, Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Paprika
ds Cayenne
2 lb Shrimp;uncooked
1/2 c  Sherry
1/2 c  Coconut;flaked
1/4 c  Bread Crumbs;dry
1/3 c  Peanut Oil
3 tb Parsley;chopped
1 tb Garlic;minced
Combine oil, coconut, bread crumbs, chopped parsley, paprika,
cayenne, salt and minced garlic. Reserve 1/4 cup of mixture for
topping.
Toss shelled and deveined shrimp lightly in remaining mixture until
shrimp is well coated.
Turn into lightly oiled casserole Pour sherry over shrimp and
sprinkle with reserved mixture.
Bake, uncovered, in moderate oven (375 F) until shrimp is tender,
about 40 minutes.
