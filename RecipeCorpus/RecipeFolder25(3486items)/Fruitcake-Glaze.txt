Title: Fruitcake Glaze
Categories: Dessert, Holiday, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Water
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Corn syrup
Boil sugar, syrup and water.  Simmer 2 minutes.  Brush over cakes.

Serve over Fruitcake.
