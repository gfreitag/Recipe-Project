Title: American Farm Style Sausage #1
Categories: Sausage, Pork, Homemade, Veal
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Ground cloves
1 t  Ground mace
1 t  Ground sage
1/2 c  Fine, dry bread crumbs
1 c  Water
3 lb Medium ground pork butt
2 lb Medium ground veal
1 T  Salt
1 T  Pepper
1 t  Ground Nutmeg
Mix all ingredients together and stuff into sheep casing or make into
patties. SOURCE: The Sausage Making Cookbook, by Jerry Predika
Collection of Clarence Fontish
