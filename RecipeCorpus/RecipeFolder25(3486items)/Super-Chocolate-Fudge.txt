Title: Super Chocolate Fudge
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
pn Salt
1 t  Vanilla
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
350 g  Semi sweet chocolate chips
1 cn EAGLE brand sweetened
-condensed milk
1 1/4 c  Icing sugar
In heavy saucepan, over low heat, melt chocolate chips with sweetened
condensed milk.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Spread evenly in
waxed paper line 8 inch square pan.  Chill 2-3 hour or until firm.
Turn duge onto cutting board.  Peel off paper and cut into squares.
Store loosely covered at room temperature.  Makes about 2 lbs.
Origin: Eagle Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk ad, Chatelaine, Dec/89.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
