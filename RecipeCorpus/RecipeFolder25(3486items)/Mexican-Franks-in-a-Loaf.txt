Title: Mexican Franks in a Loaf
Categories: Hot dogs, Cheese/eggs, Main dish, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 folks
-Original
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
-(2 oz)
1/2 c  Salsa;
1    Egg;
6    Frankfurters; hot dog hotdog
-weiner, franks
2 tb Salsa; -=OR=-
2 tb Ripe olives; chopped -=OR=-
2 tb Green bell pepper;
1 1/2 c  Bisquick baking mix;
Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Grease loaf pan; 9x5x3".  Split
frankfurters lengthwise, not cutting completely through.  Fill each
with 1 ts salsa. Mix remaining ingredients with fork; beat 30
strokes. Spread in pan. Arrange frankfurters crosswise on dough in
pan, curving slightly and pressing into dough slightly.  Bake 25 to
30 minutes or until center is set. To serve, cut bread between
frankfruters. CAL: 325; PRO: 11g; CAR: 21; FAT: g; CHO: 70; SOD:
