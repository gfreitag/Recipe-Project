Title: Homemade Caramel Sauce with Variations
Categories: Dessert, Ice cream, Holiday
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM---------------------MAKES: 2 1/4 CUPS--------------------------
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
2/3 c  Whipping cream.
3/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
3/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
Combine all ingredients except whipping cream in a saucepan. Cook over
medium heat, stirring, until mixture comes to a full boil. Cool 5
minutes; stir in whipping cream. Serve warm or divide into 3 3/4 cup
portions to prepare variations. Variations: Rum-Raisin: While still
warm, stir 1/4 cup raisins and 1/4 tsp rum extract into 3/4 cup sauce
Banana: Cool sauce completely. Cut a banana into cubes; stir into
sauce. Source: Land O Lakes Treasury of country Recipes Carolyn
Shaw's Collection 11-2-94
