Title: Mushrooms Magnifique
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Lemon juice
1 md Onion, chopped
1 lb Fresh mushrooms, thin sliced    1 1/2 c  Sour cream
6 sl Canadian bacon
3 ea English muffins, halved and
-toasted
2 T  Butter
Chopped parsley
Paprika
Saute onion and mushrooms in butter until tender.  Add salt, pepper,
parsley paprika, lemon juice and sour cream; place over low heat
until warm (do not allow to boil). Fry canadian bacon, and place over
muffin halves. Spoon sauce over bacon. Serve immediately.
