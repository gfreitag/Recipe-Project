Title: Marinated Baby Carrots
Categories: Appetizer, Sthrn/livng, Vegetable, Marinade
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 ts Seasoned salt
1/4 ts Salt
Parsley, fresh; minced
8 sm Carrots
3 tb Vinegar
3 tb Oil, salad
1 sm Garlic clove; crushed
Peel and cut carrots into short, 3" stickes.  Place in shallow dish;
combine other ingredients except parsley and pour over carrots,
spooning marinade over carrots so all will be coated.  Cover tighlty
and refrigerate overnight, turning carrots occasionally.  Drain off
marinade; sprinkle carrots with parsley.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, March 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
