Title: Quick Zippy Cheese Dip
Categories: Diabetic, Cheese/eggs, Microwave, Dip
Yield: 1 cup
1 T  Jalapeno relish
8 oz Processed cheese
Cut cheese into small cubes and place in small glass dish. Cook on
medium high (80%) 1 minute. Stir in the relish and cook an additional
minute. Stir and cover with waxed paper 2-3 minutes to carry over
cook and continue melting the cheese. If cheese is not completely
melted cook an additional minute. For a thinner dip add 1 tablespoon
skim milk with the relish. Let rest 5 minutes before serving. Serve
warm.

Makes 1 Cup 8 Servings 2 Tablespoons = 75 calories
1 medium fat-meat exchange
Carbohydrate 0
Protein 7 g
Fat 5.5 g
