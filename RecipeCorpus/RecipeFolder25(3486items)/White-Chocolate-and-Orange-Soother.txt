Title: White Chocolate and Orange Soother
Categories: Alcohol, Beverage, Chocolate, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
-chocolate
1 T  Orange liqueur
1 ea Thin orange slice
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1 c  Whole milk
5 ea Squares white chocolate,
-preferably Israeli white
Heat the milk and cream in a small bowl over low heat. Add the
chocolate and mix gently in circular motion, until melt. Don't let it
boil. Add the orange liqueur and orange slice, and serve. Makes about
2 cups.
