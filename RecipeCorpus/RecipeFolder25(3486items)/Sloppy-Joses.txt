Title: Sloppy Joses
Categories: Sandwich, Ground beef
Yield: 8 servings
8    Buns, sliced
Finely chopped lettuce
Non-fat sour cream
1 cn La Casita Salsa (32 oz)
2 lb Extra lean ground beef
-or ground turkey
Brown 2 pounds of beef or turkey over low heat. Drain fat. Add jar of
salsa. Simmer 10-15 minutes on low. Serve on toasted buns with
lettuce and sour cream.
