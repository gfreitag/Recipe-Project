Title: Cranberry Glazed Pork Chops
Categories: Fruit, Main dish, Pork, Meat
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------MAKES: 4 SERVINGS--------------------------
1/2 c  Jellied cranberry sauce
1 ea Tb honey
1/8 t  Ginger
1/8 t  Cinnamon
4 ea Boneless loin pork chops,
-trimmed of fat
1/4 t  Pepper
1/2 c  Chicken broth or water
Sprinkle both sides of pork chops with pepper. Spray heavy nonstick
skillet with cooking spray; place over medium heat until hot. Add
pork chops. Cook three minutes each side or until browned. Add
chicken broth; cover and cook 12-15 minutes or until tender, turning
once. In small bowl, combine remaining ingredients; blend well. Pour
over chops. Cook 1 minute, turning chops to glaze. Place chops on
platter; spoon cranberry mixture over top. Per serving: 230 cal., 23g
pro., 18g carb., 8g fat, 150mg sodium, 63mg chol., 370mg potassium.
Exchanges: 3 Lean meat, 1 fruit. Source: Fast and Healtyh Magazine
