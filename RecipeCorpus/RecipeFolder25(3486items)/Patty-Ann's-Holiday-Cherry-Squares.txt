Title: Patty Ann's Holiday Cherry Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 pan
1 1/3 c  Flaked coconut
3/4 c  Marachino cherries--chopped
1 cn Eagle Brand Condensed Milk
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 1/2 c  Cornflake crumbs
1/2 c  Soft margarine
4 tb Sugar
2 c  Miniature marshmallows
In a 13 x 9 x 2" baking pan, combine cornflake crumbs, butter and
sugar. Press down firmly with back of spoon. Sprinkle coconut,
marshmallows and cheries evenly over crumb crust. Pour Eagle Brand
milk over all evenly. Bake at 350 F. for abt. 25 min.  Cool
completely before cutting into squares.  Store in tin box between
layers of wax paper. Submitted By BILL SPALDING   On   12-18-94
