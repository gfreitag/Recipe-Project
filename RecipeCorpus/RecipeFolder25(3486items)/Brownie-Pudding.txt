Title: Brownie Pudding
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Pudding, Chocolate
Yield: 1 cake
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Semisw. choc chips
1/2 c  Brown sugar; firm. packed
1 3/4 c  Boiling water
Peppermint, coffee, or
-vanilla flavored ice cream
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa; divided
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Margerine; melted
As the cake bakes, a gooey pudding forms on bottom like magic.
Preheat oven 350.

Combine flour, sugar, 1/4c cocoa, baking powder and salt in bowl.
Stir in milk, butter and vanilla until smooth; stir in chocolate
chips. Spread in ungreased shallow 1 1/2qt casserole.

Sprinkle brown sugar and 1/4c cocoa on top. Place in oven; pour
boiling water over top. Bake 35 mins. Cool 10 mins after serving.

Serve with ice cream
