Title: Zippy's Grilled Cheese
Categories: Main dish, Sandwich, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
8    Bacon;slices
8    Tomato;slices
4    Wheat Bread;slices
4    Cheddar Cheese;slices
1    Avocado;sliced;
Heat oven to broil.
Fry bacon 3/4 done, drain on paper towels. Toast one side of Bread
in broiler.
On untoasted side of each bread slice, put 1 slice cheese, avocado
slices, 2 slices tomato. Secure 2 slices of bacon in an 'X' across
the top of each sandwich with toothpicks.
Broil until the cheese melts and bacon is finished cooking.
