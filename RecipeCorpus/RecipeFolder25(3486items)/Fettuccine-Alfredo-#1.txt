Title: Fettuccine Alfredo #1
Categories: Italian, Pasta
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 lb Sweet butter
1/2 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1 pk (12-oz) extra long
-fettuccine
2/3 c  Light cream
Cook noodle according to package directions. WHile noodles are
cooking, melt butter. Place drained, hot noodles in warm serving bowl
or platter. Pour cream over the noodles. Then add melted butter and
about half the cheese. Toss noodles with a fork and spoon until well
blended, adding balance of the cheese a little bit at a time while
tossing. Top with additional grated cheese, if desired.
