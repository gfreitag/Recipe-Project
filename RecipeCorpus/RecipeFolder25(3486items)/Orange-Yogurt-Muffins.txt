Title: Orange Yogurt Muffins
Categories: Cake/cookie, Muffin, Fruit
Yield: 11 servings
1    Egg
1 c  Low-fat yogurt
1/4 c  Margarine or butter,melted
2 tb Orange rind strips
1/4 c  Orange juice
2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Currants
In large bowl, stir together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda
and salt.  Mix in currants. In separate bowl, beat egg; blend in
yogurt, margarine or butter, orange rind and juice. Stir into flour
mixture just until moistened. Spoon into large greased or paper-lined
muffin cups, filling to top. Bake in 350 oven for 25 minutes or until
firm to the touch. Makes 11 muffins.
