Title: Hearty Halibut
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Dry white wine
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Dillweed
1/8 ts Pepper
1    Lemon, cut into wedges for
-garnish
2/3 c  Thinly sliced onion
2 lb Halibut steaks, cut into 6
-portions
1 1/2 c  Chopped mushrooms
1/3 c  Chopped tomato
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
3 tb Chopped pimento
Preheat oven to 350. Arrange onion in bottom of greased 12X8X2" baking
dish. Place fish on top of onion. Combine mushrooms, tomato, green
pepper, parsley, and pimento; spread over fish. Combine wine, lemon
juice, salt, dill weed, and pepper, pour over vegetables. Bake 25-30
minutes, until fish tests done with fork. Garnish with lemon wedges.
