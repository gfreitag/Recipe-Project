Title: Hamburger Corn Bake
Categories: Casserole, Vegetable
Yield: 3 servings
1/3 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Salt
1 pk Corn muffin mix
1 1/2 lb Ground chuck
1 cn Whole kernal corn
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1 cn Tomatoes, drained
Brown the meat in a skillet, with a little oil added, which can be
placed in the oven later or transferred to a baking dish.  Add green
pepper, tomatoes, corn, onion and salt.  Prepare corn muffin mix and
spread over the casserole.  Bake for 25 minutes in a 350 degree oven.
