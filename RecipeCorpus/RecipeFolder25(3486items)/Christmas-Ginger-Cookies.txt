Title: Christmas Ginger Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Molasses
1 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/2 c  Water
1 lg Egg
1 t  Vanilla
Mix flour, soda, spices and
-salt.
6 c  Flour
1 T  Baking soda
1 T  Ginger
1 t  Cloves
1 t  Cinnamon
1 t  Nutmeg
1/2 t  Salt
1 c  Solid shortening, Crisco
Beat shortening, molasses, sugar, water, egg and vanilla until well
blended. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Will be soft. Divide into
fourths.  Pat each 1/4 into 1" thick round.  Wrap in plastic and
refrigerate at least 3 hours or up to 1 week. Roll on lightly floured
surface to 1/8" thick, cut with ginermen cutters and bake at 350
degrees for 10-12 minutes.  Cool slightly on cookie sheet, then
transfer to wire racks to cool completely.  Decorate with Royal
Icing. ROYAL ICING: 3 cups confectioners sugar 1/4 tsp cream of
tartar 2 large egg whites food colors In large bowl, mix all
ingredients until stiff.  Color and use to decorate cookies. Note:
GREAT RECIPE! Makes lots of cookies. Dough is easy to handle and can
be made ahead.  Lots to decorate! - Debbie Carlson From: Linda Glover
