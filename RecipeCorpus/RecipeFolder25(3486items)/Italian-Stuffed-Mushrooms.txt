Title: Italian Stuffed Mushrooms
Categories: Meat, Pork, Cheese/eggs, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
-croutons, crushed
1 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
-(4 oz.)
1 md Tomato, finely chopped
1 1/2 t  Minced fresh oregano OR
1/2 t  Dried oregano
4 ea Strips Bacon, diced
24-30 Large Fresh mushrooms
1/4 lb Ground fully cooked ham
2 T  Minced fresh parsley
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 c  Onion and garlic salad
In a skillet, cook the bacon until crisp. Meanwhile, remove mushroom
stems from caps; set caps aside. Mince half the stems and discard the
rest. Add minced stems to bacon and drippings, saute 2-3 minutes.
Remove from the heat and stir in remaining ingredients. Firmly stuff
into mushroom caps. Place in a greased jelly roll pan. Bake at 425
degrees for 12-15 minutes or until the mushrooms are tender. Yield: 2
1/2-3 dozen. SOURCE:* Taste of Home Magazine Aug./Sept. 94 POSTED BY:
Jim Bodle 10/94
