Title: Beef Drumsticks
Categories: Beef, Ground beef
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Mustard
1/4 c  Fine crumbs
1 lb Ground beef
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1    Egg
1/4 c  Chopped onion
Combine all ingredients.  Shape 1/3 to 1/2 cup of meat mixture around
metal or wood skewers.  Roll drumsticks in 1/2 cup of fine crumbs.
Fry in heavy frying pan turning to brown all over; cover and cook
about 15 minutes or until cooked through. Randy Rigg
