Title: Charles' Beef-Vegetable Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Beef, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Diced potatoes
1 c  Mushrooms
2 1/2 c  Canned tomatoes
1 cn Stewed tomatoes (14 oz)
1 c  Red wine
1 T  Sugar
Water, as needed
2 qt Beef stock
2 lb Beef, cubed
1/2 c  Onions, chopped
1/2 c  Fresh parsley, chopped
1 1/2 c  Celery
1 1/2 c  Carrots
1 c  Shredded cabbage
Precook beef (broil or bake).  Microwave celery, carrots, shredded
cabbage, and potatoes for 4 minutes on high.  Saute mushrooms and
onions. Combine all in pot, cook on low for at least one hour.  Eat.

original recipe from Charles Bolliger, North Bennington, Vermont.
