Title: Tex-Mex Mix
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Tex/mexican, Snack, Mixes
Yield: 2 quarts
2 ts Ground cumin
1 c  Cubed Monterey Jack cheese
-- (in 1/4" cubes)
2 qt Popcorn popped in oil
2 ts Ground chili powder
2 ts Paprika
Keep popped corn warm.  Mix seasonings together and toss with
popcorn. Add cheese and mix thoroughly.

Nutritional info per serving (based on 3-cup servings): Total
calories 402, 29 g fat (18 g saturated), 16 g carbohydrates, 0.9 g
fiber, 22 g protein, 888 mg sodium, 66 mg cholesterol. For a
lower-fat version of Tex-Mex Mix, use low-sodium, low-fat cheese for
22 g of fat (12 g saturated).
