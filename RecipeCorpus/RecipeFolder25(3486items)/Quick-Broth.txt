Title: Quick Broth
Categories: Soup/stew, Chicken, Vegetable, Herbs/spice
Yield: 3 quarts
-coarsely chopped
1    Bouquet garni
-(thyme, bay leaf,
-and parsley sprigs)
13 c  Water
1    Chicken (3 1/2 to 4 lb)
-cut into 10 pieces
-plus neck and gizzards
1 md Onion; coarsely chopped
1 md Carrot; coarsely chopped
1 md Celery stalk
1. Bring all ingredients to simmer, skimming foam and fat as it rises
to surface. continue to simmer until chicken is tender, about 40
minutes. Remove and reserve chicken pieces for another use. Simmer
broth to blend flavors, about 20 minutes longer.

2. Strain and skim remaining fat from broth and continue with any soup
recipe, or cool to room temperature and refrigerate. Remove congealed
fat before reheating. You can refrigerate this broth for up to 5 days
(or longer, if you bring it to a boil every few days), or freeze it
for several months.
