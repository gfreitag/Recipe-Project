Title: Pecan Puffs
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 22 puffs
2 tb Granulated sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
Confectioners' sugar
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Pecans
1/2 c  Butter or margarine (not
-spread); at room temp.
1.  Heat oven to 300F.  Have ungreased cookie sheets ready.

2.  Process flour and pecans in a food processor or blender until
nuts are very finely chopped.

3.  Beat butter, sugar and vanilla in a large bowl with electric mixer
until pale and fluffy.  With mixer on low speed, gradually beat in
flour mixture just until blended.

4.  Roll level tablespoonfuls dough into balls.  Place about 1 inch
apart on cookie sheets.

5.  Bake 45 minutes or until golden.  While warm, roll in
confectioners' sugar.  Place on wire rack to cool completely, then
roll in confectioners' sugar again.  Store airtight up to 2 weeks or
freeze.
