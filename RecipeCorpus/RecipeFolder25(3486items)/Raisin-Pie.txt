Title: Raisin Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Butter
3    Eggs, separated
1 tb Jack Daniels whiskey
1 c  Water
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Brown sugar
3 tb Flour
Beat egg yolks, add water, raisins, flour and sugar.  Cook until
thick; add butter and whiskey.  Pour in a baked pie crust.  Beat egg
whites for meringue and brown. Randy Rigg
