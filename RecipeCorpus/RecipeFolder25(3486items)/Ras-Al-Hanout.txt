Title: Ras Al Hanout
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 /2 cup
1 ts Coriander seeds
1/8 ts Red pepper flakes
1/8 ts Mace
1 tb Ground ginger
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1/2 ts Anise seeds
1 ts Fennel seeds
8    Allspice berries
8    Cardamon pods
15    Black peppercorns
1    1/2" long cinnamon stick
Combine all ingredients in a spice grinder; grind to a fine powder.
Transfer to a small bowl to use or store in a airtight container.
