Title: Oven Shrimps and Cheese Fondue
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Salt
2 c  Shredded sharp cheese
3 tb Chopped onion
2 c  Shrimp
10 sl White bread
5    Eggs
2 1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Parsley
1 ts Dry mustard
Remove crusts from the bread; cut into cubes.  Beat eggs, milk and
seasonings.  Add bread cubes, onion, cheese and shrimp.  Pour into
ungreased baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes, uncovered.
Serve immediately. Randy Rigg
