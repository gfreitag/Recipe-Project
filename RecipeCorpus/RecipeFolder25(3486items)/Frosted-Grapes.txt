Title: Frosted Grapes
Categories: Fruit, Holiday, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Water
Equal, sugar substitute
Seedless red or green grapes
Add distinction to any
-occasion with these
-frosted jewels.
1 ea Egg white, lightly beaten
In small bowl, combine egg white and water.  Dip grapes in egg white,
rool in Equal.  Let dry at toom temperature.  Use as a garnish for
fancy desserts or on a fruit party tray. Origin: Equal's 10th
Anniversary Recipe Book, Collector's Edition. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens.
