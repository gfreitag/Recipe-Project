Title: Pistachio Cream
Categories: Diabetic, Ice cream
Yield: 6 servings
Pudding mix
12 oz Can evaporated skim milk
13 oz Sugar-free instant pistachio
Chill evaporated milk thoroughly.  Pour into 1 qt. measuring cup. Add
enough cold water to make 3 c. liquid. Next, add pudding mix. With a
wire whisk, beat until well blended. Pour into ice-cream maker.
Freeze as directed by manufacturer.  Serve immediately or pack in
freezeer container for later use.
