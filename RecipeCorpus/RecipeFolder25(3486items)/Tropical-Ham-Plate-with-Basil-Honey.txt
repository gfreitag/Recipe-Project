Title: Tropical Ham Plate with Basil Honey
Categories: Pork, Meat, Fruit, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 4 SERVINGS--------------------------
1/4 c  Lime juice
2 ea Kiwi fruit
2 T  Honey
1 T  Salad oil
5 1/2 t  Basil
6 oz Thinly sliced Canadian bacon
1 ea Peeled and cored pineapple
Heat oil in a wide frying pan over medium-high heat. Add bacon and
cook turning once, until lightly browned (about 3 minutes).
Meanwhile, mix lime juice, honey, and basil; set aside. Slice
pineapple crosswise about 1/3" thick. Peel kiwi fruit and slice
crosswise about 1/4" thick. Arrange pineapple, kiwis, and bacon on
individual plates. Drizzle with honey mixture.
