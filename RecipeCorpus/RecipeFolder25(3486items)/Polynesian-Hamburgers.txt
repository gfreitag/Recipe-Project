Title: Polynesian Hamburgers
Categories: Main dish, Burgers, Ground beef
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Sugar, brown; firmly packed
2 tb Water
1/2 ts Mustard, dry
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 lb Beef, ground
2 c  Breadcrumbs, soft
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Water; ice
1 ts Salt, seasoned
2 md Onions; cut in thick slices
Combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, chopped onion, water, and seasoned
saut. Shape into 4 patties.  Saute onion in butter; remove.  Brown
hamburgers. Return onion to skillet.  Combine remaining ingredients;
add to hamburgers. Cover; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; simmer 30
minutes.
