Title: Newer Ambrosia
Categories: Fruit, Salad
Yield: 8 servings
8 oz Sour cream
6 ea Maraschino cherries, cut in
Half
Crusted nuts for garnish
1 1/2 c  Miniature marshmallows
1 cn 20 oz pineapple chunks
Drained
1 cn 11 oz mandarin oranges,drain
1 c  Shredded coconut
In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients and toss gently.
Refrigerate for 2 to 3 hours to marry the flavors. Over night is even
better. Sprinkle on some crushed nuts, if you'd like.
