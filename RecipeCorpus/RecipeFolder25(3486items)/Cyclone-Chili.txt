Title: Cyclone Chili
Categories: Chili, Beef, Chilies
Yield: 8 servings
Seeded and chopped
3 c  Tomatillos (about 10)
1 1/2 ts Ground coriander
5 ts Ground cumin
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
6 oz Tomato paste
3 c  Beef stock
3 x  Cloves of garlic, chopped
1/3 c  Lard
4 lb Beef chuck
Cut into 1/2" cubes
Onion, chopped
1 c  Canned nopalitos (cactus
Pieces)
12 x  Serano peppers
Heat lard in kettle.  Brown beef a few pieces at a time. Remove all
beef, and saute onion and garlic till soft. Add beef to kettle. Rinse
cactus pieces in cold water; drain and add to beef. Add the rest of
the ingredients; cover and simmer for 2 1/2 hours. Makes 2 1/2
quarts. Recipe from Kathy Pole
