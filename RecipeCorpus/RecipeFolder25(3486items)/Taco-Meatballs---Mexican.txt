Title: Taco Meatballs - Mexican
Categories: Tex/mexican, Ground beef, Meatballs
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Onion
1/4 c  Celery
1    Eggs; beaten
8 oz Taco sauce
11 oz Chedder cheese soup
1 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Green pepper
1 c  Rice; cooked or
Wide noodles
2 ts Garlic salt
Mix all but last two ingredients. Form meat balls and place in 2- 1/2
qt. dish. Bake at 350~ for 30 minutes. While baking, heat taco sauce
and soup on stove. Pour over meatballs and bake another 30 minutes.
