Title: Ginger Spice Muffin Mix with Recipe
Categories: Muffin, Mixes, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Cloves
-MUFFINS:
1 pk Mix
1/4 c  Butter, melted
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Milk
1 3/4 c  Flour
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Brown sugar
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
Mix: Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.

Ginger Spice Muffins: Preheat oven to 400. Combine all ingredients
and stir just till moistened. Batter will be lumpy. Fill greased
muffin cups 2/3 full and bake 15 minutes until golden.
