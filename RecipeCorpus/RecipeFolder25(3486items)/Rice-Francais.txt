Title: Rice Francais
Categories: Side dish, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
8 c  Water
1 c  Rice;white
1/2 ts Salt
A never fail approach to cooking rice:
Bring water and salt to a rolling boil in a large pot. Add rice
and cook 15 - 20 minutes at low boil, stirring occasionally. When
rice is just tender, drain in colander. Serve.
