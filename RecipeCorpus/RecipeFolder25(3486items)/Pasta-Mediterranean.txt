Title: Pasta Mediterranean
Categories: Pasta, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Chicken broth
1 cn PRECIOUS Ricotta Cheese
8 oz PRECIOUS Mozzarella Cheese,
-shredded
1/2 c  Sliced ripe olives (opt)
1 lb Hot cooked fettuccine
1/8 c  STAR Olive Oil
1 sm Zucchini, sliced (~1 cup)
1 c  Cut-up plum tomatoes
2 tb Fresh basil
-OR
1 t  Dried basil
2    Garlic cloves, minced
In a large saucepan over medium heat, heat oil; add zucchini,
tomatoes, basil and garlic and cook until tender-crisp, stirring
often. Add broth; stir in ricotta and mozzarella cheese and heat
until cheese is melted. Add olives. Toss with fettuccine.
