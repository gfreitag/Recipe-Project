Title: Maple Glazed Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Syrup, Vegetable, Sweetpotato
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Maple syrup
1/4 c  Apple cider
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 t  Salt
2 1/2-3 Lbs. Sweet
-potatoes, peeled
1 3/4-2 Lbs. Apples,
-peeled and cored
Cut the sweet potatoes and apples into 1/4 inch slices. Layer in a
greased 13" x 9" x 2" baking dish. In a saucepan, bring syrup, cider,
butter and salt to a boil. Pour over potatoes and apples. Cover and
bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour. Uncover and bake 10-15 minutes more
or until tender. Serves 8-10. SOURCE:* Country Magazine
