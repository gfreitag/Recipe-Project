Title: Western Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Olive Oil
1 ds Garlic Powder
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Mustard;dry
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Egg;hard boiled;
1    Lettuce;Boston;head
1/2 lb Spinach;washed
1    Red Onion;sliced;
2 tb Parsley;chopped
1/4 c  Vinegar
Remove egg yolk and mash with garlic. Blend in salt, paprika,
pepper and mustard.
Add olive oil, vinegar; beat well. Chop egg white and parsley and
stir in. Combine lettuce, spinach and onion in salad bowl.
Add dressing. Toss lightly.
