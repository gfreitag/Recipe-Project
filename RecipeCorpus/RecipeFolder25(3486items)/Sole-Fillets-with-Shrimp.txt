Title: Sole Fillets with Shrimp
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Fish, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
10    Shrimp, cleaned
1/2 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1 tb Chopped parsley
1/2    Lemon, juice of
2 tb Vegetable oil
3 tb Butter
4    Sole fillets
-Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Flour
Heat oil and 2 tb butter in large fry pan over low heat. Sprinkle
fish with salt and pepper; coat with flour. Fry over low heat 3
minutes. turn and cook another 3 minutes or until done. Remove to
serving platter; keep warm. Add shrimp. mushrooms and parsley to same
fry pan. Cook for 3 minutes, shaking pan frequently. Stir in
remaining butter, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Pour over fish and
serve.
