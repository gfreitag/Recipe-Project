Title: Hash-Stuffed Peppers
Categories: Casserole, Meat
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Corned beef,diced,cooked
1 c  Potatoes,diced,cooked
1/2 c  Bread crumbs,soft
1 cn Tomato sauce(8oz)
1 T  Worcestershire sauce
6    Green peppers,medium-size
1 c  Water
1/2 t  Salt
2 T  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Onion,minced
1. Cut a thin slice from the stem of each green pepper; scoop out
seeds.

2. Bring water and salt to a boil in a large saucepan; add peppers.

3. Simmer, covered, 5 minutes; remove peppers and drain.

4. Melt butter in a medium-size saucepan; add onion and saute 2
minutes.

5. Stir in remaining ingredients.

6. Spoon mixture into pepper shells.

7. Place in a greased 10x6x2-inch baking pan; bake, uncovered, in a
preheated 350'F. oven until hot, about 30 minutes.
