Title: Clam Savory
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Clam/oyster
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Tomato paste
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Sherry
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
4 sl Buttered toast
3 tb Butter
1 sm Onion, chopped
1/2    Green pepper, seeded and
-finely chopped
6 1/2 oz Can chopped clams, drained
1 c  Grated Swiss cheese
Melt butter in frying pan. Add onion and green pepper; saute for three
minutes. Add clams, cheese, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce,
sherry and cayenne pepper; cook, stirring constantly, until cheese
melts. Pile on toast and serve hot.
