Title: Garlic-Sauced Vegetables
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Pepper - sweet, cut in strip
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 ea Garlic clove, quartered
1/8 t  Pepper
1 ds Salt
2 c  Broccoli florets
1 c  Cauliflower florets
2 tb Water
1 md Zucchini, sliced 1/4" thick
1 c  Mushrooms - fresh
On a large plate place broccoli and caiflower around outer edges.
Sprinkle with water.  Cover with microwave-safe plastic wrap. Vent by
leaving a small area unsealed at the edge.  Nuke on high for 2
minutes. Place Zucchini, mushrooms and pepper in center. Nuke,
covered, on high for 3 to 5 minutes more or until vegetables are
crisp-tender. Carefully drain liquid by tilting the covered platter
in direction of the vent. In a 1 cup microwave measure stir together
margarine, garlic, pepper and salt. Nuke on high about 1 minute or
until margarine is melted. Discard margarine. Drizzle over vegies.
