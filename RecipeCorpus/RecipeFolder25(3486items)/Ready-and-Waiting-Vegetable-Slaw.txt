Title: Ready and Waiting Vegetable Slaw
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Vegetable, Salad
Yield: 30 servings
1 1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Canola oil
2 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1 lb French-style green beans
10 oz Corn
10 oz Peas
1 lb Cabbage/coleslaw mix
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped celery
Steam frozen vegetables(first group of 3 veggies) until crisp-tender,
approx 3 minutes.  Combine with fresh veggies (second group of 3
veggies) In saucepan, combine dressing ingredients (vinegar, water,
sugar, oil, salt, pepper) and bring to boil. Cool for 5 minutes. Pour
over vegetables. Mix well.  Cover and refrigerate overnight. Will
keep in frig several weeks.  Makes approx. 30 1/2cup servings.

Calories:  81 Fat: 2.1g Sat Fat 0.2g
