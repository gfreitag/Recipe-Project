Title: Squid with Tomatoes
Categories: Seafood, Stove top
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 T  Tomato paste
2 ea Fresh tomatoes, peeled,
-seeded and finely chopped
-or- 1 cup drained,
-chopped, canned tomatoes
1 T  Chopped parsley
2 c  Cooked rice
2 lb Cleaned squid
3 T  Olive oil
2 ea Minced garlic cloves
1/2 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
1/8 t  Oregano
1/2 c  Dry sherry
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Clean squid, pat dry and cut into small pieces. Fry garlic in hot oil
one or two minutes. Add squid and simmer covered for five minutes.
Stir in the remaining ingredients except 2 tsp parsley and the cooked
rice. Simmer uncovered 15 minutes, stirring occasionally until squid
is tender. Serve with rice and garnish with the remaining parsley.
