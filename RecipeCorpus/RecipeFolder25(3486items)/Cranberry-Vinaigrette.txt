Title: Cranberry Vinaigrette
Categories: Fruit, Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------MAKES: 1 CUP-----------------------------
1/4 c  Oil
1 ea Tb Dijon mustard
1/2 c  Jellied cranberry sauce
1/4 c  Raspberry or red wine
-vinegar
Combine all ingredients in blender container; blend until smooth.
Pour into serving container. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Per
Tablespoon: 45 cal., 0g protein, 4g carb, 4g fat, 25mg sodium, 1 fat
