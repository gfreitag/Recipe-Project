Title: Egyptian Shrimp Pate
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, African, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 10 servings
2    Greek olives, finely
-chopped
-Salt and pepper to taste
1    Lemon, cut into wedges for
-garnish
-Whole Greek olives for
-garnish
1/2 lb Cooked shrimp
1/4 c  Chopped, toasted almonds
1 tb Freshly grated ginger
1/4 ts Curry powder
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
2 tb Finely chopped parsley
2 tb Melted butter
Puree all ingredients, form into a loaf and refrigerate for 12 hours.
When ready to serve, place loaf on serving dish. Garnish with lemon
wedges and olives. Serve with crackers or pita bread.
