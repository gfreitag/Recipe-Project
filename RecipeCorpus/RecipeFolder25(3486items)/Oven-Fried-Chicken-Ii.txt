Title: Oven-Fried Chicken Ii
Categories: Diabetic, Low-cal/fat, Main dish, Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Ground thyme
6    Chicken breast halves
-- skinned
1/4 c  Buttermilk
1 c  Corn flake crumbs
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Red pepper
Combine first 5 ingredients in plastic bag.  Shake to mix well.
Brush both sides of chicken with buttermilk.  Place in bag of crumbs
and shake to coat.  Place chicken on broiler pan which has been
sprayed with vegetable spray.  Bake at 400 F. for 40 minutes or until
done.

Per servings:  200 calories; 75 mg cholesterol; 3 gm fat; 110 mg
sodium. ADA Exchange Value = 3 meats (You can also use this as a WW
exchange.)

Source:  Magazine clipping.  Shared by Judi M. Phelps, who wrote:
"Here is an easy low-cal dish that is great with minimal effort.
Those on a Weight Watchers diet as well as on a diabetic diet can use
the exchanges interchangeably."  Date: 22 Sep 94.
