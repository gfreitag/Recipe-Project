Title: Deviled Crab Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Dip, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
-Salt and pepper to taste
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
-Parsley, for garnish
6 oz Can crabmeat, drained and
-flaked
2    Hard boiled eggs, chopped
1/2 ts Prepared mustard
Combine all ingredients; garnis with parsley and serve.
