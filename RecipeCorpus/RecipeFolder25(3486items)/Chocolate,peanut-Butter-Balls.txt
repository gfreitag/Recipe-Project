Title: Chocolate/peanut Butter Balls
Categories: Candy, Snack, Chocolate
Yield: 12 servings
1/2    Block Gulf was
12 oz Bag chocolate chips
1 md Jar of peanut butter
1    Box 10x confectioners sugar
1    Stick Blue Bonnet marg.
Mix peanut butter, margarine and sugar into balls. Freeze on cookie
sheet

Melt chocolate and Gulf Wax

Dip balls into chocolate (use toothpick).    Return to freezer.
