Title: Pit Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Sthrn/livng, Bbq/grill
Yield: 2 quarts
1 tb Sugar, brown
1 tb Onion juice
1 1/2    Garlic cloves; minced
2 tb Salt
2 tb Pepper
2 c  Catsup
1/2 lb Butter (or marg.)
2 c  Vinegar
1 tb Tabasco sauce
4 oz Worcestershire sauce
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil.  Remove
from heat, cool, and store in jars for use as needed to mop on beef,
chicken, or pork on barbecue grill.
