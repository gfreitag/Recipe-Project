Title: Buttermilk Dressing - Martha Stewart Living
Categories: Dressing, Salad
Yield: 8 servings
3 T  Extra-virgin olive oil
Salt & freshly ground pepper
1 T  Minced fresh chives
1/2 c  Buttermilk
3 1/2 oz Soft goat cheese
2 T  Lemon juice
In the bowl of a food processor, combine buttermilk, goat cheese,
lemon juice, olive oil, and salt and pepper to taste. Process until
smooth. Stir in chives, and serve with the salad.
