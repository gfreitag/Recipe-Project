Title: Barbecued Curried Fresh Fruit
Categories: Fruit, Curries, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
-about 1/2 melon each)
1 c  Pineapple cubes, fresh or
-canned, drained
2 T  Butter
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1 T  Curry powder
This sweet, ho sensation is
-wonderful served over
-frozen yogurt or
Vanilla ice cream
1 c  EACH, cantaloupe and
-honeydew melon balls,
Arrange melon balls and pineapple chunks in 8 inch square foil pan.
Pour 1 cup water into another 8 inch square foil pan.  Place pan
containing fruit into pan containing water.  In small saucepan, melt
butter over medium low heat, stir in sugar and curry poder.  Spoon
butter mixture evenly over fruit.  Place pans on barbecue grill.
Grill over medium low heat 10-15 minutes or until sauce is bubbly.
Serve at once. Per serving: 139 calories, 5 g fat. Origin:
Homemaker's Recipe Collection of the Year, Sizzling Barbecues. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens.
