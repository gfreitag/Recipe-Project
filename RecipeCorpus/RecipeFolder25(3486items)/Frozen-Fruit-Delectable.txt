Title: Frozen Fruit Delectable
Categories: Salad, Fruit, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 9 servings
20 oz Pineapple, crushed; drained
1/4 c  Pecans; chopped
Parsley
Maraschino cherries
1 c  Cream, whipping
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
16 oz Fruit cocktail; drained
Chill cream; whip until almost stiff.  Beat in sugar, lemon juice, and
mayonnaise.  Fold in drained fruits and chopped pecans.

Freeze in an 8-inch square pan.  Cut into squares.  Garnish with
sprig of parsley or maraschino cherry.
