Title: Instant Chocolate Syrup
Categories: Diabetic, Syrup, Dessert, Chocolate
Yield: 1 cup
1 t  Vanilla
Sweetener = 1/2 cup sugar
3/4 c  Dry unsweetened cocoa
pn Cinnamon
1 1/4 c  Water
In a heavy saucepan, combine cocoa, cinnamon and water; stir or whisk
until there are no dry lumps of cocoa. Stir and cook over medium heat
until mixture comes to a boil. Reduce heat; boil gently, stirring
often for five minutes or until mixture is thick and smooth. Cool
slightly, stir in vanilla and sweetener. Pour into a container or jar
with lid. Cover and store in refrigerator up to three weeks.

Makes 1 cup syrup 1 serving = 1 tablespoon
1 extra choice
2 g carbohydrate
1 g protein
50 kilojoules
12 calories
