Title: Poultry Seasoning Gift Basket with Stuffing Recipe
Categories: Mixes, Poultry, Stuffing, Herbs/spice, Holiday
Yield: 1 basket
MMMMM------------------------HERB PLANTS-----------------------------
1    Marjoram plant
1    Thyme plant
1    Rosemary plant
1    Sage plant
1    Summer savory plant
MMMMM----------------OLD FASHIONED TURKEY STUFFIG---------------------
3    Ribs celery, chopped
2 1/2 c  Chicken broth
8 c  Stale French bread cubes
1/2 c  Butter
1 tb EACH snipped herbs
1 lg Onion, chopped
Basket: Arrange plants in a plastic lined basket.

Stuffing; Melt butter. Add herbs, onion and celery. Saute until limp.
Add broth and bring to a boil. Pour over bread cubes and mix well.
Bake in turkey or a greased casserole in a slow oven 1 hour, basting
with turkey drippings or additional butter.
