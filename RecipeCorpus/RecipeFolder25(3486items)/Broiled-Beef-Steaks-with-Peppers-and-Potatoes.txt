Title: Broiled Beef Steaks with Peppers and Potatoes
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Vegetable, Potato
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 t  Dried thyme
2 lg Baking potatoes - large (do
-not pare), cut into 8
-lengthwise wedges
1 lg Red pepper, cut into 8
-lengthwise wedges
2 ea Beef top loin steaks cut 1"
-thick
2 tb Olive oil
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1 t  Garlic salt
1 t  Dried rosmary
1/2 t  Allspice
Place frozen beef top loin steaks on rack in broiler pan so surface
of meat is 4 to 5 inches from heat.  Broil first side od steaks 15
minutes; turn steaks.  Combine oil, vinegar, garlic salt, rosemary,
thyme, and allspice; brush on all sides of potatoes and pepper
wedges. Place peppers around steak on broiler pan; broil peppers and
second side of steaks 13-15 minutes to desired degree of doneness
(rare or medium), adding potatoes to broiler pan during last 5 to 7
minutes of broiling time. Carve steak into thick strips and serve
with broiled vegetables. Submitted By JEFF THOMAS   On   11-21-94
