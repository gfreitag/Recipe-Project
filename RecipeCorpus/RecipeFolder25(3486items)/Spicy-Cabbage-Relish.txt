Title: Spicy Cabbage Relish
Categories: Diabetic, Relish, Microwave
Yield: 4 cups
2 t  Prepared mustard
1 t  Worcestershire sauce
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Hot tabasco sauce
1/8 t  Pepper
4 t  Butter or margerine
2 c  Finely shredded cabbage
1 md Green pepper, chopped
1 sm Onion, chopped
2 md Tomatoes, chopped
2 T  Wine vinegar
Place butter or margerine in glass 2 quart casserole. Place in micro
and cook on high until melted - about 45 seconds. Add remaining
ingredients to butter and mix well. Cover and microwave on high 7-9
minutes, stirring after 4 minutes. Serve hot over hot dogs, with
polish ring or your favourite sausage. Refrigerate leftovers and use
hot or cold.

If cooking sausage, remember to price and let steam escape.

Makes 4 cups 1/2 cup serving = 1/2 fat exchange
1 vegetable exchange
48 calories
5 g carbohydrate
2 g protein
2.5 g fat
