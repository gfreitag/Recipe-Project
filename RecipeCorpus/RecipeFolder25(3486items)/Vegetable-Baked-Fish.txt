Title: Vegetable Baked Fish
Categories: Main dish, Fish, Seafood, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Water
1 tb Butter
1/8 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Dill Weed
1/4 ts Savory
1/2 c  Zucchini;sliced
1    Green Pepper;sliced;
1    Tomato;sliced;
1    Onion;sliced;
1 lb Fish;fillet
1/4 ts Salt
Place 1/2 of vegetables in baking dish.  Season fish with herbs,
salt and pepper and place on top of the vegetables.
Cover with remaining vegetables. Add water, dot with butter and
sprinkle with paprika.  Bake at 350F for 20 - 25 minutes.
