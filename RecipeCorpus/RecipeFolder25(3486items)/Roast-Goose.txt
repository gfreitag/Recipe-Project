Title: Roast Goose
Categories: Poultry, Stuffing, Holiday, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
14 lb Goose
MMMMM--------------------------STUFFING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Pine nuts
1 c  Chestnuts, shelled &
-quartered, optional
1/2 c  Celery, diced
1/2    Cube of butter
2 c  ;water
1/2 c  Wild rice
1 c  White rice
1/2 c  Onion, diced
1 cn Mandarin oranges
Goose- Clean out excess fat (there will be a lot!) and rinse well.
Stuff, salt & pepper skin, prick well, & tent w/tin foil. Roast at
375F for 4 hrs. Remove tent & cook 30 mins more.  (Approx 25 mins/lb)

Stuffing- Cook rices until done. Saute celery & onion in butter. Mix
all ingreds & stuff goose.
