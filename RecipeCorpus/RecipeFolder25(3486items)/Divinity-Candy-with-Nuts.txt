Title: Divinity Candy with Nuts
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Nut meats
3 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Water
2 ea Egg whites
1/2 c  Corn syrup; light
Cook sugar, syrup and water to boiling point. Caver and let boil 3
minutes without stirring. Remove cover, cook unitl hard ball stage or
255 F. Add salt and vanilla to egg whites, beat until still. Pour
syrup from a height of 1 foot, slowly into whites. When thick, drop
by teaspoonful on waxed paper, after adding nuts.

Source: Mrs. Wilma J. Short, West Newton Grange, Allen County, OH
