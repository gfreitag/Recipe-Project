Title: Dulse Fried Oyster Mushrooms
Categories: Side dish, Mushroom
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-----------------------FLOUR MIXTURE----------------------------
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
Salt & black pepper
1 ts Dulse flakes, pulverized
1/2 ts Garlic powder
MMMMM-----------------------LIQUID MIXTURE----------------------------
1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Dulse flakes
1/4 ts Garlic powder
MMMMM--------------------BREAD CRUMB MIXTURE-------------------------
1 1/2 c  Safflower oil
4 oz Oyster mushrooms, rinsed
2 c  Fresh breadcrumbs
4 ts Dulse flakes
1/2 ts Garlic powder
Combine the ingredients for the three mixtures separately & set aside.

Line a baking sheet with absorbent towels.  Heat oil to 350F in a
medium-sized saucepan, about 5 minutes over medium heat.

Dredge mushrooms in flour mixture.  Shake off excess flour & dunk in
the liquid, before rolling in the breadcrumbs.  Fry in the hot oil
for 45 seconds each side, they should be golden brown.  Drain & serve.
