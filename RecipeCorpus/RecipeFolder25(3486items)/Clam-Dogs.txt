Title: Clam Dogs
Categories: Can/preserv, Fish, Clam/oyster
Yield: 2 pounds
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Basil
1 ts Lemon juice
2    Six oz cans chopped clams,
-drained
4    Feet small hog casings
1 1/2 lb Whitefish fillet, cubed
1    Egg, beaten
2 tb Chopped parsley
1 ts Onion powder
1 ts Garlic powder
Prepare casings. Process fish in food processor just until broken. Add
remaining ingredients except clams, process until blended. Transer to
a bowl and mix with clams. Stuff into casings and twist off into 3-4"
links.
