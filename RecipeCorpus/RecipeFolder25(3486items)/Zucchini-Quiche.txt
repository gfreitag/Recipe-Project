Title: Zucchini Quiche
Categories: Pie/tart, Vegetarian, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Onion;chopped
1/4 c  Onion;chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Bisquick
3    Zucchini;sliced;small
c  Parmesan Cheese;grated
9    Egg;
1/2 ts Oregano;dried
5 tb Butter;melted
Cook zucchini and onions in 1/2 of the butter with oregano and salt
till tender - about 5 minutes.
In blender or processor, mix eggs, milk, Parmesan, Bisquick, salt,
and remaining butter. Pour into a buttered 13 inch dish or pie plate.
Spread zucchini all over top.
Bake in a pre-heated 375F oven 30 - 40 minutes.
