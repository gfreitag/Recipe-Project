Title: Spiced Apple Cider
Categories: Apples, Beverage, Herbs/spice
Yield: 6 servings
1    Cinnamon stick (3" long)
1/8 c  Brown sugar
1 qt Apple cider
2    Whole allspice
2    Whole cloves
Mix in a saucepan: cider, allspice, cloves, and cinnamon. Boil 5
minutes. Add brown sugar. Boil 5 minutes longer.
