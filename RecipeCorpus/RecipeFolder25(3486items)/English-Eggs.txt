Title: English Eggs
Categories: Cheese/eggs, British
Yield: 2 servings
4 lg Eggs
Salt and pepper
4 oz Freshly grated sharp
-cheddar cheese, about 3/4
-cup
**From Roger Moore...  007**
2 T  Unsalted butter
1 md Tomato, peeled, seeded,
-chopped
2 ea Green onions, thinly sliced
Melt 1 tbsp of the butter in a small skillet over high heat.  Add the
tomato and green onions and cook until onions have softened, about 3
minutes.  Transfer to a bowl.  Melt the remaining tbsp of butter in
the same pan.  Beat the eggs until well blended in a small bowl.  Add
salt and pepper to taste. Pour the egg into the hot butter, reduce
heat to medium and scramble by folding gently-not stirring-until the
eggs have set, about 3 minutes.  Gently fold in the tomato mixture.
Transfer to a servingplatter and sprinkle with cheese.  SErve
immediately.  Makes 2 servings. Origin: Newspaper article about Robin
Leach gathering recipes. Shared by: Sharon Stevens.
