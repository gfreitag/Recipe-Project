Title: Cake Mix Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 32 servings
1/2 t  Flavoring extract
1 c  Chopped nuts, raisins, chips
1 ea Pk Cake mix, your choice
2 ea Eggs
1/4 c  Oil
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Combine cake mix with eggs, oil and
extract to complement cake.  Beat until smooth.  Fold in chips,
raisins or nuts to complement flavor of cake mix. Drop by teaspoonful
about 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake for 10-12
minutes. Center will be soft. Allow to cool slightly before removing
from pan.
