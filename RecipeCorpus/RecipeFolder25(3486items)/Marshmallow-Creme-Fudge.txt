Title: Marshmallow Creme Fudge
Categories: Candy, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chopped nuts
12 oz Chocolate chips
1 ts Vanilla
4 c  Sugar
14 oz Can evaporated milk
1/2 c  Butter
1 pt Marshmallow creme
Bring sugar, milk and butter to a boil. Boil rapidly for 11 minutes,
stirring occasionally. Add marshmallow creme, chopped nuts, chocolate
chips and vanilla; stir until marshmallow creme and chips are melted.
Pour into a 9 x 12-inch buttered pan. Let cool and mark into squares.
