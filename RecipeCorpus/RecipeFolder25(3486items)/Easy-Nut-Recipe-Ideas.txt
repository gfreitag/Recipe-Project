Title: Easy Nut Recipe Ideas
Categories: Snack, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 batch
MMMMM-----------------CUMIN-FLAVORED PISTACHIOS----------------------
2 ts Ground cumin
2 c  Pistachios; shelled
2 ts Melted butter
MMMMM----------------------HOLIDAY NUT MIX---------------------------
1/4 c  Chopped crystallized ginger
1 tb Melted butter
1/2 ts Coarse salt
1 c  Roasted walnuts
1 c  Roasted pecans
1/2 c  Roasted blanched almonds
1/2 c  Dried cranberries
MMMMM----------------CHOCOLATE-EXPRESSO HAZELNUTS---------------------
-roasted and skinned
1/2 c  Ground roasted hazelnuts
6 oz Chocolate; bittersweet
-or semisweet
1 1/2 c  Hazelnuts
MMMMM----------------------SZECHUAN CASHEWS---------------------------
2 ts Melted butter
1/2 ts Coarse salt
2 ts Szechuan peppercorns
2 c  Cashews, raw, shelled
Cumin-Flavored Pistachios: Toss all ingredients together. Roast at
275ø for 20 minutes. Makes 2 cups.

Holiday Nut Mix: Toss all ingredients together. Makes 2 1/2 cups.

Chocolate-Expresso Hazelnuts: Melt the chocolate and stir in the
hazelnuts until all the nuts are coated with chocolate. Using a
spoon, drop 3 or 4 nuts in clusters onto a sheet of wax paper.
Sprinkle the clusters with the ground hazelnuts. Let the clusters sit
until the chocolate has hardened. Makes about 2 cups.

Szechuan Cashews: Crush the peppercorns and run through a fine sieve.
Toss the cashews with the melted butter, the crushed peppercorns and
the coarse salt. Bake in a 275ø oven for 30 minutes. Makes 2 cups.
