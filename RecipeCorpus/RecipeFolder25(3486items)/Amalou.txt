Title: Amalou
Categories: Condiment, Dressing, Moroccan, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Thick honey
1/2 lb Blanched almonds
Salad oil
1/2 c  French walnut oil
Brown the almonds in a little hot salad oil.  Drain and pulverize
them in a blender with the walnut oil and salt.  When the mixture is
smooth and creamy, spoon the honey into the blender jar and continue
blending 20 seconds.  Pour the amalou into a stoneware crock and
store in a cool place. Amalou is spread on Moroccan bread or fried
breads.

Yield: About 1 1/2 cups.

From "Couscous and Other Good Food from Morocco" by Paula Wolfert. New
York: Harper & Row, Publishers, 1987.  ISBN 0-06-091396-7.  Pg. 36.
