Title: Home Made French Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Salad, French, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Sugar
1 t  Salt (optional)
1 c  Oil
1 c  Vinegar
1 t  Dry mustard
1 sm Onion, chopped finely
Mix all together in a glass quart bottle that has a tight fitting lid.
Store in the fridge.  Keeps for weeks and weeks!! TO MAKE RUSSIAN:
Add a couple of good shakes of worchestershire and about a cup of
mayonnaise. Put mayo in a bowl, and slowly add the dressing, whisking
constantly. Then pour back into bottle and store in fridge. Origin:
My mom Shared by: Sharon Stevens
