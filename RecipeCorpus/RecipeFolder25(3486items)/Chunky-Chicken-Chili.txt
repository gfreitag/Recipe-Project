Title: Chunky Chicken Chili
Categories: Chicken, Chili
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM--------------------CONNIE RIZZO GTBK24A-------------------------
1 ts Cumin
1 tb Oragano
4 ds Tabasco
1 ds Worcesthire
2/3    Bag garbanzo beans (soaked)
2    Green pepper;chopped
8    Chicken boullion cubes
<<<OR>>>
4 cn Chicken broth
-salt/pepper to taste
2 lb Chicken breast meat; cut
-into bite-sized pieces
2 c  Onion; chopped
1 c  Celery; sliced
1 c  Carrots; sliced
4    Cloves garlic; minced
2 c  Pace salsa
56 oz Can tomatoes
56 oz Can water
2 tb Chili powder
Brown chicken, onions, garlic. Add all tomatoes, water, and beans and
simmer 30 min. Add all other ingredients and simmer until beans and
vegetables are tender. MM Format Norma Wrenn npxr56b NOTE: I did not
use the tomatoes or water and I used 1 can of beans. 10/08/94
