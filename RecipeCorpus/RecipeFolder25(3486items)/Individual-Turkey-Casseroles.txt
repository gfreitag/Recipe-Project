Title: Individual Turkey Casseroles
Categories: Main dish, Casserole, Sthrn/livng, Turkey, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Milk
1 tb Soy sauce
1 lb Chinese vegetables; drained
2 c  Turkey; sliced chunks
2 c  Rice; cooked
3 oz Chow mein noodles
1/3 c  Butter
1/2 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 1/2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Ginger, ground
Melt butter in saucepan.  Blend in flour, salt, pepper, curry powder,
and ginger.  Add milk and soy sauce; cook, stirring constantly, until
sauce is thickened and smooth.  Fold in vegetables and turkey.  Place
an equal amount of rice in six 10-ounce individual casseroles; top
each with an equal amount of turkey mixture and noodles.  Bake at 375
degrees until hot and bubbly, about 15 minutes.
