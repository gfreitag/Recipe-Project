Title: Marinated Mushrooms and Peppers
Categories: Appetizer, Holiday
Yield: 3 servings
MMMMM-----------------------MAKES: 3 CUPS----------------------------
8 oz Pkg mushrooms, halved
1/4 c  Thinly sliced green onion
1 ea Green pepper, cut in 1"
-pieces
1 ea Red bell pepper, cut in 1"
-pieces
1/3 c  White wine or cider vinegar
3 ea Tb oil
1 t  Sugar
1 t  Dried basil leaves
1/2 t  Salt
1/8 t  Garlic powder
In nonmetal container or ziploc bag, combine vinegar, oil, sugar,
basil, salt, and garlic powder; mix well. Add mushrooms and peppers;
stir or shake gently to coat. Seal and refrigerate at least 1 hour to
blend flavors. Stir before serving. Per 1/4 cup serving: 25 cal, 1g
pro, 2g carb, 0g fiber, 2g fat, 5mg sodium, 95mg potassium, 1.2 fat
exchange. Source: Fast and Healthy Nov/Dec 92 Carolyn Shaw's
