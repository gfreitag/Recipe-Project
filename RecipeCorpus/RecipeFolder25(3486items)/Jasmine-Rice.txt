Title: Jasmine Rice
Categories: Rice, Information
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/3 c  Water
Posted by Gaye Levy DTXT63A
2 c  Jasmine Rice
Put rice in pot and cover with water.  Stir well to release starch
then drain.  Put rice in back and add the amount of water indicated
in the ingredients.  Cover and bring to boil for 1 minute.  Reduce to
low and cook 10 minutes. Turn off heat and allow to sit for at least
5 minutes. Gaye Levy
