Title: Turkey Melts
Categories: Turkey, Sandwich, Holiday, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Turkey stuffing
6 oz Cooked turkey, chopped
4 ea Green onions, chopped
1 T  Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper
1 1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
These are also very good
-made with mozzarella
-cheese.
4 ea Onion buns
4 t  Butter, softened
2/3 c  Cranberry sauce
Split onion buns and butter.  Broil until just golden.  Spread
cranberry sauce over buns.  Crumble stuffing over cranberry sauces.
In bowl, combine the turkey, onion, mustard and season with salt and
pepper to taste. Fill buns and sprinkle cheese over top.  Broil until
cheese is melted and bubbling.  Makes 4 servings. Origin: Canadian
Living, December 1994. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
