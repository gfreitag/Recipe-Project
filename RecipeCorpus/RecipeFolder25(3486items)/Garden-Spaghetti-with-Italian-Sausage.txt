Title: Garden Spaghetti with Italian Sausage
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Pasta
Yield: 8 servings
1 md Red bell pepper, sliced
-into thin strips
1 md Yellow bell pepper, sliced
-into thin strips
16 oz AMERICAN BEAUTY Spaghetti,
-uncooked
Grated Parmesan cheese (opt)
1 tb Olive oil
1 lb Sweet Italian sausage,
-sliced
1 cn Ragu Chunky Gardenstyle
-Super Mushroom Pasta Sauce
-(28 oz)
2 c  Fresh broccoli florets
In a large skillet, heat olive oil; add sausage. Cook over medium
heat, stirring frequently, until thoroughly cooked. Drain; stir in
pasta sauce, broccoli and peppers. Cover, simmer 10-15 minutes,
stirring occasionally or until vegetables are tender. Meanwhile, cook
pasta according to package directions; drain. Spoon sausage mixture
over hot pasta. Sprinkle with cheese, if desired. Preparation time:
25 minutes. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   10-24-94
