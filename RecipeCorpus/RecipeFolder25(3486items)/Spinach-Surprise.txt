Title: Spinach Surprise
Categories: Side dish, Casserole, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Nutmeg, ground
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Breadcrumbs; fine
3    Eggs; beaten
20 oz Spinach, chopped; thawed
1 md Green onion; chopped
1 md Green pepper; chopped
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
2 tb Margarine; melted
1 ts Salt
Press water from spinach; add remaining ingredients, blending well.
Spoon into a lightly greased 2-quart casserole.  Bake at 300 degrees
for 45 to 50 minutes.
