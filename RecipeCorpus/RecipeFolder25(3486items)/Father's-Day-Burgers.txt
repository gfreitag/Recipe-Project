Title: Father's Day Burgers
Categories: Burgers, Ground beef, Main dish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Seasoned salt mix
2 lb Ground sirloin
1/2 lb Ground chuck
Combine ground meat and spice mix; form into 6 patties. Chill until
ready to cook. Preheat grill and cook 3-4 minutes per side.
