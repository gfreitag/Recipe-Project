Title: Caper Vinaigrette
Categories: Salad, Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Capers;finely chopped
3/4 c  Olive Oil
1/4 c  Wine Vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
In a screw top jar, combine all ingredients. Shake well to blend.
Make dressing well in advance to blend seasonings. Store tightly
capped at room temperature.
Good over cold chicken salad.
