Title: Turkey Noodle Soup Mix with Recipe
Categories: Soup/stew, Pasta, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
-SOUP:
1 pk Soup mix
8 c  Water
2    Carrots, diced
2    Stalks celery, diced
1/4 c  Minced onion
3 c  Cooked diced turkey
1 c  Fine egg noodles, uncooked
1 1/2 tb Chicken flavored bouillon
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
1/4 ts Dried whole thyme
1/8 ts Celery seeds
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1    Bay leaf
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Turkey Noodle Soup: Combine soup mix and water. Add vegetables and
bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 15 minutes. Discard
bay leaf, add turkey and heat through. 8 servings.
