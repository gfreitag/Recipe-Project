Title: Bagna Cauda
Categories: Appetizer, Dip, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Olive Oil
Salt;
Pepper;
2 oz Anchovies;rinsed
10    Garlic;minced;clove
1/4 c  Butter
3/4 c  Cream;whipping
Heat oil in saucepan, add garlic and 1/4 cup cream.
Reduce heat to low, cook 1/2 hour.
Add anchovies, cook 3 minutes.
Add the butter and the rest of the cream, cook 5-8 more minutes, or
until slightly thickened. Salt and pepper to taste.
Serve with sliced raw veggies or french bread.
