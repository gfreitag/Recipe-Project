Title: Peas and Onion Bake
Categories: Side dish, Casserole, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 8 servings
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 cn Green peas; drained 16 oz ea
3 1/2 oz French-fried onions; canned
Paprika
2 tb Flour, all-purpose
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 tb Butter (or marg.); melted
1 1/2 c  Milk
Stir flour, salt, and pepper into butter.  Gradually add milk and
Worcestershire sauce.  Bring to a boil, sitrring constantly; cook
until smooth and thickened.  Place peas in a shallow 2-quart
casserole; add sauce.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 20
minutes. Top with onions, and sprinkle with paprika; bake an
additional 5 minutes.
