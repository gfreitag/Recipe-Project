Title: Quick Mussel Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Seafood, Vegetable, Clam/oyster
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Chopped parsley
2 c  Shelled mussels, drained,
-reserving liquid
1 1/4 c  Dry white wine
2 c  Fish broth
1 tb Butter, softened
1 tb Flour
1    Onion, chopped
1    Leek, finely sliced
1    Stakl celery, fiely chopped
2 tb Butter
3    Cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 ts Sage
1/2 ts Salt
In large pot, brown onion, leek, and celery in 2 tb butter. Add
garlic, sage, salt, and parsley. Stir in reserved mussel liquid, wine
and broth; bring to a boil, then remove from heat. Mold softened
butter and flour together to form a ball; add this in small bits to
soup, stirring constantly until soup is smooth. Add mussels; heat
thoroughly. Serve.

Source: Creative Cooking: Fish and Other Seafood Carolyn Shaw's
