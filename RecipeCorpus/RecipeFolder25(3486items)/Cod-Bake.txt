Title: Cod Bake
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
1    Clove garlic, minced
1    Bay leaf
16 oz Can stewed tomatoes
1 1/2 lb Cod fillets
2 tb Olive oil
1    Spanish onion, chopped
1    Green pepper, seeded and
-chopped
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
Saute onion, green pepper, garlic, and mushrooms in oil until soft.
Add bay leaf and tomatoes; simmer 30 minutes. Preheat oven to 350.
Arrange cod in greased baking dish. Remove bay leaf from sauce; pour
over fish. Bake 40 minutes; or until fish flakes easily when tested
with a fork.

Source: Creative Cooking: Fish and Other Seafood Carolyn Shaw's
