Title: Kahlua Sauce  (La Mansion Del Rio)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Dessert, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 t  Vanilla
1/2 c  Heavy Cream
1/4 c  Kahlua
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Sugar
1 T  Cornstarch
1 1/2 t  Butter, unsalted, melted
Over medium heat, bring milk to boil.  Add sugar and cornstarch,
stiffing constantly until thick.  Remove from heat and stir in butter
and vanilla. Cool.  Refrigerate at least 2 hours or overnight. Before
serving, stir in heavy cream and Kahlua.  Ladle on unmolded Flan.
Recipe of La Mansion del Rio, from Arlene Lightsey's column, Chefs'
Secrets, S.A. Express-News.
