Title: Mckiel Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 loaf
1 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
4 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
2 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter
5    Eggs
2 c  Raisins (if desired)
Cream butter and sugar well.  Add eggs one at a time, beating very
well after each addition.  Add other ingred.  Bake in moderate (325)
oven about 1 hour. Divide batter to make different types. Floured
cherries = cherry pound cake.  Caraway seeds for caraway cake.
Glaced fruits = fruit pound cake.

Note:  This cake is very much like a pound cake.  Excellent recipe
given to me by my mother-in-law.  I have been using it for years.
