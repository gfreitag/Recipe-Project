Title: Philly Brunch Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Breakfast
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  MILNOT
Topping:
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Cake flour
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Margarine
1/2 c  Shortening
8 oz Philadelphia cream cheese
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Cake flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Soda
Topping-combine brown sugar, 1/3 cup of flour, cinnamon and 2
tablespoons of margarine, mixing until crumbly.  Set aside.  Cream
the 1/2 cup of margarine, cream cheese and 1 1/4 cups of sugar.  Add
eggs and vanilla; beat well.  Sift together the 2 cups of flour,
baking powder, soda and salt; add alternately with MILNOT, mixing
well after each addition. Pour into a greased and floured 13 x 9 inch
pan. Sprinkle with the crumb mixture.  Bake in moderate oven, 350
degrees, for 35 to 45 minutes. Randy Rigg
