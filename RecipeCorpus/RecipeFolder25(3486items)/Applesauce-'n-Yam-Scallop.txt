Title: Applesauce 'n Yam Scallop
Categories: Side dish, Sweetpotato, Apples, Fruit, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
ds Nutmeg, ground
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Milk, evaporated
1    Egg; beaten
4 tb Butter; melted, divided
3/4 c  Bread cubes, soft
1 c  Applesauce
3/4 c  Yams; cooked, mashed
2 tb Sugar, brown; firmly packed
Pour 2 tablespoons melted butter over bread cubes; toss lightly and
set aside.  Combine remaining ingredients; pour into a greased 3-cup
casserole. Top with bread cubes.  Bake at 375 degrees for 20 to 25
minutes; allow to stand 10 minutes before serving.
