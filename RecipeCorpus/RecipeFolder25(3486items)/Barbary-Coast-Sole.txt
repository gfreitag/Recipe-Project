Title: Barbary Coast Sole
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Butter
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper;white
1 tb Flour;white
1 ds Nutmeg
1 ds Paprika
2 lb Sole;fillet
1 oz Gooseberries;can
1/2 c  Onion;chopped
1/4 c  White Wine
3/4 c  Cream;heavy
1    Egg;beaten yolk;
Drain liquid from gooseberries, reserving 1/4 cup.
Sprinkle both sides of skinless, boneless fillets with salt and
pepper; roll them up lengthwise, securing with toothpicks.
In a skillet, fry onions in butter till translucent. Add wine,
gooseberry liquid and fish. Simmer covered for 10 minutes; remove
fish.
Combine egg yolks, cream, salt, nutmeg and flour. Add this to
cooking liquid and stir over high heat until thickened. Add the
gooseberries; warm through.
Remove toothpicks from fish and place fish on platter, Cover with
gooseberry sauce and sprinkle with paprika. Brown under broiler for 5
minutes.
