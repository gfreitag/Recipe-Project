Title: Kahlua Chili
Categories: Chili, Alcohol, Ground beef, Pasta
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
2 tb Tomato paste
4 ts Salt
2 ts Red cayenne pepper
2 ts Cumin powder
2 ts Dried marjoram; crushed
2 ts Dried oregano; crushed
Spaghetti; cooked
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
4    Onions; chopped
4    Cloves onion; minced
4 lb Ground beef
1 c  Green pepper; chunked
1 c  Kahlua
1/2 c  Parsley; chopped
6 tb Chili powder
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPINGS-------------------------------
Onion; chopped
Cheddar cheese; grated
In large pot, heat oil. Add onions and garlic; saute until
transparent. Add meat; brown. Add remaining ingredients except cheese
and onions; mix well. Bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer,
partially covered, for 45 minutes. To serve, spoon chili on top of
cooked, drained spaghetti in individual bowls and top with cheese and
onions. Yields: 5 quarts.
