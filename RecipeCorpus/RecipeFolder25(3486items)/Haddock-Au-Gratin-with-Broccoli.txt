Title: Haddock Au Gratin with Broccoli
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
5 tb Finely shredded leek -OR-
5 tb Finely chopped chives
3 tb Milk -OR-
3 tb Sour cream -OR-
3 tb Light cream
10 oz Pkg frozen broccoli
1 1/2 lb Haddock fillets
1 ts Salt
1 c  Coarsley grated Parmesan
-cheese
3 tb Bread crumbs
Preheat oven to 425. Prepare broccoli until just tender. Place in
greased ovenproof dish. Place fish fillets on top; sprinkle with
salt. Mix grated cheese, bread crumbs, and leeks or chives together.
Blend in milk, sour cream, or cream. Dab cheese mixture over fish;
bake 20-30 minutes or until fish tests done with fork. Serve with
mashed potatoes.
