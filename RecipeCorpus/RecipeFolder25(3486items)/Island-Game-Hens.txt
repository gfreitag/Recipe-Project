Title: Island Game Hens
Categories: Poultry, Game, Fruit, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Garlic Powder
1 ts Sesame Oil
2 ts Honey
Peanut Oil;
;
3    Game Hens;
1 tb Ginger;grated
1/4 c  Sherry
1/2 c  Pineapple Juice;unsweetened
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
Split hens in half, pat dry.
Combine all other ingredients except peanut oil, pour over game
hens, refrigerate overnight.
Heat oven to broil. Adjust broiler rack to 4 inches below heating
element. Coat rack with peanut oil and broil hens skin side down till
brown. Turn hens over and broil other side till brown, basting
occasionally with peanut oil. Internal temperature of hens should be
170F.
Serve with rice.
