Title: Pesto-Chevre Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Italian, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Parsley;fresh
2    Garlic;peeled;cloves
3/4 c  Parmesan Cheese;grated
3/4 c  Olive Oil
Cornmeal;
1    PIZZA DOUGH;recipe
Goat Cheese;chunked;
1    Red Onion;sliced;
4 c  Mozzarella Cheese;grated
1 c  Basil;fresh
In a food processor, blend the basil, parsley, garlic, Parmesan,
and olive oil.
Form the PIZZA DOUGH into 2 crusts; place on two oiled pizza pans
in which a handful of cornmeal has been scattered.
Set the PIZZA DOUGH into a 425F oven for 10 minutes. Spread the
basil sauce on the pizzas, add the goat cheese, onion and mozzarella.
Cook for 10 - 15 minutes more, until cheese bubbles.
