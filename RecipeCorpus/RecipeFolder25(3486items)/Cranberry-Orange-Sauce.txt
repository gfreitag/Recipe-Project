Title: Cranberry-Orange Sauce
Categories: Diabetic, Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 16 - 2 tb ser
1 ts Raspberry conserve; no-sugar
-added
1 c  Orange; cut in a 8 sections
12 oz (1 pk) fresh cranberries;
3 tb Orange juice concentrate;
-frozen
Rinse cranberries and place in a medium-size saucepan with a small
amount of water.  Cook over medium heat, stirring in next 2
ingredients, until berries are soft.  In a food processor four orange
sections. Repeat with remaining cranberries and oranges.  Chill
before serving. Makes 2 cups. Food Exchanges per serving: FREE; CAL:
21; CHO: Omg; CAR: Og; PRO: Omg; SOD: 0mg; FAT: 0g; Source: The Light
