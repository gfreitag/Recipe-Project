Title: Kathy Hirdler's Fire Camp Chili (Serves 1200)
Categories: Chili, Meat, Main dish, Soup/stew
Yield: 1200 servings
-bacon ends, ground beef,

4 c  Chili powder
Salt to taste
100 lb Pinto beans
48 lg Onions, chopped
4 c  Jalapeno chilies with juice
40 lb Meat (ham, sausage, pork,
Soak beans overnight, then raise to a boil on high heat.

Add all ingredients and simmer until tender (about 6 hours).

Add water as necessary. Stir occasionally. Makes 60 gallons.

Serves 1200 approximately.
