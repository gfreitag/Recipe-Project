Title: Tears on Your Pillow Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Cheese/eggs, Dessert
Yield: 6 servings
1 T  All purpose flour
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
1-9 inch unbaked pie shell
A sweet tooth pleaser
1/3 c  Butter, melted
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 ea Eggs
In a large bowl beat together the butter, borwn sugar, eggs, flour,
and milk until well blended.  Pour the filling into the pie shell.
Bake in a 350F oven for 35 minutes.  Turn off the oven and leave the
pie for 45 minutes to 1 hour.  Serves 6-8 Submitted by: Sherry Hyatt
~ Kingsport, Tennessee Origin: Aunt Bee's Mayberry Cookbook. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
