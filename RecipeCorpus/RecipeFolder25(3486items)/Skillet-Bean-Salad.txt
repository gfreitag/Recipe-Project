Title: Skillet Bean Salad
Categories: Salad, Sthrn/livng, Vegetable, Beans
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Butter (or marg.); melted
2 cn Pork and beans; 16 oz. ea.
1/4 c  Raisins
1 c  Cucumber; thinly sliced
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1 lg Garlic clove; minced
Saute cucumber, onion, and garlic in butter until tender.  Add
remaining ingredients.  Heat, stirring occasionally.
