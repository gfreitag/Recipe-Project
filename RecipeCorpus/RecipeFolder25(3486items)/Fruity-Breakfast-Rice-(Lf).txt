Title: Fruity Breakfast Rice (Lf)
Categories: Fruit, Rice, Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-----------------ONE MEAL AT A TIME; KATAHN----------------------
1 md Apple; chopped (or other
-fruit of choice)
3 tb Raisins;
2 c  Cooked brown rice*; heated
1 c  Plain, nonfat yogurt;
1 tb Honey or brown sugar;
1 ts Cinnamon;
*Delicious idea for using leftover rice

Combine rice, yogurt, honey or brown sugar, and cinnamon in a bowl.
Stir in chopped apple and raisins and reheat.

per serving: 213 calories, 1/2 gm fat, 1 mg chol, 4 gm fiber, 50 mg
sodium, 2% of calories from fat
