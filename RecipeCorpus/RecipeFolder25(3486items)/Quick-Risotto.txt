Title: Quick Risotto
Categories: Rice, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A----------------------
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Chives
1/2 c  Dry white wine
1/3 c  Romano cheese
Tomatoes; chop, optional
1 c  Arborio rice
2 1/2 c  Chicken broth; heated
1 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Quick cooking rice
2    Shallots; chopped
1 ts Basil
Saute shallots in oil; add rice and stir a few minutes. Add warm
broth and wine; stir. Add herbs and cheese. Remove from heat and let
sit. Can add tomatoes, if using.
