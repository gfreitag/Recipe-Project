Title: Heath Bar Dessert
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 24 servings
2 c  Milk
1 qt Butter pecan ice cream
8 oz Cool whip
6    Heath candy bars
16    Squares white crackers
16    Squares graham crackers
1    Stick margerine
2    Instant vanilla pudding box
Mix white crackers, graham crackers, and crush.  Mix with margerine,
melted to make a crust.  Put in bottom of 9 x 13 pan.

Mix pudding, milk and soft ice cream thoroughly and pour over crust.
Top with cool whip.  Crush heath bars and sprinkle over top.
Refrigerate. Submitted By LISA GREENWOOD   On   10-18-94
