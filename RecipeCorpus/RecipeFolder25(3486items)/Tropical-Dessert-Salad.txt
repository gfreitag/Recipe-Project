Title: Tropical Dessert Salad
Categories: Salad, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Kiwi (about 2 large)
1/2 c  Fresh orange juice
1/4 c  Unsweetened flaked coconut
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped unsalted
-peanuts
1 1/2 c  Mango (about 1 1/2 mangos)
3/4 c  Fresh pineapple
1 1/2 c  Melon (1/2 of a medium
-cantaloupe or 1/3 of a
-small honeydew)
All the fruit must be peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces. Combine
with the orange juice and coconut. Add the peanuts right before
serving. Garnish each portion with a dab of Banana Whipped Cream, if
you like.

Per serving, without whipped cream: 133 calories, 3 grams protein, 21
grams carbohydrates, 4 grams fiber, 5.5 grams fat (2 grams
saturated), 8 milligrams sodium. From: Michael Orchekowski
