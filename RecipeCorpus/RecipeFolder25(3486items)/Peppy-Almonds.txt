Title: Peppy Almonds
Categories: Appetizer, Nuts/grains, Holiday
Yield: 1 batch
1 ts Chili powder
1/8 ts Cayenne
1 1/2 c  Shelled almonds
2 tb Margarine, melted
1 ts Salt
1 ts Celery salt
In baking pan, combine margarine, salt, celery salt, chili powder and
cayenne.  Stir in almonds.  Bake in 375 degree oven for 15 minutes,
stirring occasionally.  Cool
