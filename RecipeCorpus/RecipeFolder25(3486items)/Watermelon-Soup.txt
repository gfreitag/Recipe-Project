Title: Watermelon Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 T  Gin (optional)
1/2 t  Rose water (optional)
1 pn Salt
----- GARNISH ---------
Chilled watermellon balls
Finely chopped fresh mint
6 c  Very cold watermelon juice
- made fron ripe, chilled,
- seeded watermelon
1 3/4 c  Buttermilk, preferably
- Bulgarian style, or plain
- yogurt
Combine all the ingredients except the garnish and chill deeply.
Serve in large chilled goblets and garnish with the water melon balls
and fresh mint.
