Title: Cornbread & Sausage Stuffing
Categories: Side dish, Bread, Stuffing, Meat
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Melted butter
Salt and pepper
1 1/2 c  Sausage
3    Dozen chestnuts (optional)
4 c  Boiling water
4 c  Cornbread, broken up
1    Onion, grated
1 c  Diced celery
2    Eggs, beaten
Pour boiling water over bread and squeeze out fairly dry. Add
remaining ingredients except sausage and mix well. Brown sausage and
add to stuffing. Use for stuffing pork, lamb, or as a side dish for
you main meal. Submitted By MICHELLE BRUCE   On   11-22-94
