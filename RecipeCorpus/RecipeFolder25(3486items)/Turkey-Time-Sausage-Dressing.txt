Title: Turkey-Time Sausage Dressing
Categories: Side dish, Sthrn/livng, Sausage, Dressing
Yield: 1 unknown
-according to pkg. direction
1 c  Cheese, Parmesan; grated
8 oz Stuffing mix
1/2 ts Sage, rubbed
1    Garlic clove; chopped
1 c  Turkey broth
2 tb Oil, olive
1 tb Butter
1 lb Sausage, pork
1/2 lb Veal, ground
1/2 lb Pork, ground
3/4 c  Onion; chopped
1 1/4 c  Celery; chopped (and leaves)
1/4 c  Parsley; chopped
4    Eggs
10 oz Spinach, chopped; cooked
Cook sausage until cooked through; break apart and add ground veal and
pork; cover and cook 15 minutes.  Add onion, celery, and parsley;
cook, covered, 20minutes longer.  Stir to mix thoroughly.

Meanwhile, beat eggs lightly; add spinach and Parmesan cheese.  Place
stuffing mix in bowl; add meat mixture, egg mixture, sage, garlic,
broth, olive oil, and butter.  Mix carefully with fork.  Spinach may
be onitted and additional celery added.

Yield: dressing for a 15 to 20 pound turkey.
