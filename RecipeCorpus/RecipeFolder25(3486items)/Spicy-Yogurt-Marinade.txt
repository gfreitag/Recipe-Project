Title: Spicy Yogurt Marinade
Categories: Marinade, Asian, Herbs/spice
Yield: 2 cups
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1/2 ts Coriander seeds, ground
1/2 ts Cardamom, ground
pn Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Kosher salt
1 1/2 c  Yogurt, plain
1/4 c  Mint, fresh, finely chopped
Ginger root, minced (one
-inch cube)
2    Garlic cloves, minced or
-pressed
1/2 ts *Garam Marsala* or curry
-powder
Combine the ingredients in a nonreactive bowl.

Source: "Dry Rubs, Pastes & Marinades for Poultry, Meat, Seafood,
Cheese & Vegetables" by Jim Tarantino, 1992.  The Crossing Press,
Freedom, CA 95019. Submitted By JIM MOREY   On   12-06-94
