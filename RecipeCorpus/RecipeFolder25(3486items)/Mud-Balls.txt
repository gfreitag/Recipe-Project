Title: Mud Balls
Categories: Dessert, Snack
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 pk Miniature marshmallows
1    Candy cane; crushed
1 pk Oreo cookies
1 c  Whipping cream
1 tb Powdered sugar
Crush oreos in the blender till fine. Place half of the crushed oreos
in bottom of 9x13" pan. Beat whipping cream, adding sugar and
vanilla, to taste. Add marshmallows and crushed candy to whipped
cream. Put cream mixture in pan on cookies, spreading evenly. Top
with remaining cookie crumbs. Refrigerate for 2-3 hours. Cut into
squares to serve. Submitted By ELIZABETH WOOD   On   12-19-94
