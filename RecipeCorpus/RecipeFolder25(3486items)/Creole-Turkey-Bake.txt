Title: Creole Turkey Bake
Categories: Main dish, Vegetable, Turkey, Cajun/creol
Yield: 4 folks
-stewed and drained
1 1/2 ts Creole seasoning; (see note
-below)
4 oz Fettucine; cooked
2 c  Cooked turkey; cut into 1/2"
-pieces
Vegetable cooking spray;
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese;
2/3 c  Onoion; chopped
2/3 c  Celery; chopped
1/3 c  Green peppers; chopped
1 cl Garlic; peeled minced
1 tb Margarine;
1/4 c  Mushrooms; sliced
4 oz Cream cheese; softened
8 oz Low-sodium tomatoes;
In medium nonstick skillet, over medium-high heat, saute onion,
celery, green bell pepper and garlic in margarine 4-5 minutes, or
until vegetables are tender-crisp.  Add mushrooms and saute 2
minutes. Remove from heat. In medium bowl, blend cream cheese,
tomatoes and creole seasoning. Fold in vegetable mixture , fettuccine
and turkey. Pour mixture into 9" square pan that has been sprayed.
Sprinkle cheese over top. Bake in 325 degrees oven 30 minutes or
until bubbly. NOTE: Creole seasoning is a blend of salt, garlic
powder, black and cayenne peppers, paprika and dried oregano and
thyme.  If you perfer, you can blend a version of your own for this
dish. Per serving: 380 calories, 14g: fat; 691mg: sod; 100mg: cho:
