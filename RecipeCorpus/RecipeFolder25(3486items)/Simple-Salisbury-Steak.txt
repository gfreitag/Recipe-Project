Title: Simple Salisbury Steak
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Ground beef
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 ea Egg, beaten
1 1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms
1 cn Cream of Mushroom Soup, div.
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1.  In bowl, mix thoroughly 1/4 cup of the soup, beef, bread crumbs,
egg, and onion.  Shape firmly into 6 patties. 2. In skillet over
medium-high heat, cook patties, a few at a time,
until browned on both sides.  Spoon off fat. 3. Stir in remaining
soup and mushrooms; return patties to skillet.
Reduce heat to low.  Cover; simmer 20 min. or until done, turning
patties occasionally.
