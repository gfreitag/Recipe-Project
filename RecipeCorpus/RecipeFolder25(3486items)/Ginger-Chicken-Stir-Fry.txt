Title: Ginger Chicken Stir-Fry
Categories: Main dish, Stir fry, Asian, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
Diced
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Oyster sauce
2 tb Chopped fresh coriander,
Mint, or green onion
1 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Clivered gingerroot
1    Clove garlic, minced
2    Boneless skinless chicken
Breasts, cubed
2    Onions, chopped
1    Sweet red or green pepper
In skillet or wok, heat oil over high heat; stir-fry ginger and
garlic for 30 seconds or until fragrant. Add chicken; stir-fry for 2
or 4 minutes or until lightly browned. Add onions, sweet pepper and
mushrooms; stir-fry for 1 to 3 minutes or until softened. Stir in soy
and oyster sauces, tossing to coat.  Sprinkle with coriander. Makes 4
servings.
