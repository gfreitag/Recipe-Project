Title: Spicy Ham and Eggs
Categories: Breakfast, Meat, Cheese/eggs, Pork
Yield: 4 servings
ds Pepper
2 tb Butter or margarine
6    Eggs
1 c  (4 1/2 oz.)Deviled Ham
2 ts Instant minced onion
1. Beat eggs with a fork until well mixed. Then stir in ham, onion,
pepper, and 2 Tb water, mixing well. 2. Slowly heat butter in 9- or
10-inch skillet. Pour in egg mixture; cook,
covered, and over low heat, until eggs are firm - 15 to 20
minutes. 3. With a spatula, loosen edge of eggs; cut into quarters.
