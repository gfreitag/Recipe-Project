Title: Oven-Barbecued Venison Ribs
Categories: Game, Meat, Main dish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 8 servings
1 x  Vegetable oil
2 T  Vinegar
1 ts Prepared mustard
2 lb Venison ribs
1 x  Salt & Pepper
2 T  Brown sugar
16 oz Tomato sauce
In a 2-qt. baking dish or roaster arrange ribs. Brush lightly with
cooking oil & sprinkle with salt & pepper. Bake at 325-350 degrees
until fairly well done, turning once. Pour off any juices. Combine
vinegar, brown sugar & mustard with tomato sauce & pour over ribs.
Bake until done, basting occasionally with sauce.
