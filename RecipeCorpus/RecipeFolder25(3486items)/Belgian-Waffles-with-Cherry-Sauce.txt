Title: Belgian Waffles with Cherry Sauce
Categories: Breakfast, Pancakes, Salsa/sauce, Fruit, Waffles
Yield: 1 batch
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
-pitted halved
1 ts Grated orange peel
Sweetened whipped cream
-GARNISH
1/4 c  Sugar
2 ts Cornstarch
1/8 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 c  Sweet cherries
MMMMM--------------------------WAFFLES-------------------------------
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Melted butter/margarine
4    Egg separated
2 c  Flour
2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
SAUCE: Combine sugar, cornstarch and cinnamon; add orange juice,
cherries and peel. Bring to a boil over medium high heat; boil until
thickened. Serve warm cherry sauce over waffles; top with sweetened
whipped cream.

WAFFLES: Combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Combine milk,
melted butter/margarine and egg yolks. Add to dry ingredients; stir
just to moisten. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Bake in waffle
iron according to manufacturer's directions.

TIP: Belgian waffles can be made ahead and frozen in freezer proof
plastic bags or aluminum foil. Toast frozen waffles in toaster oven
or bake at 325 degrees for 12-15 minutes.
