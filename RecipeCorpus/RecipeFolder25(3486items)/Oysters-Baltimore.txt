Title: Oysters Baltimore
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Clam/oyster
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 sl Hot buttered toast,
-quartered
4 sl Bacon
1 1/2 pt Oysters, undrained
3 tb Chili sauce
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
6 tb Heavy whipping cream
1/2 ts Tarragon
Cook bacon in frying pan until crisp. Drain on paper towels, then
crumble and set aside. Pur off all but 1 tb bacon fat from pan. Add
oysters with their liquid. Cook over medium heat until most pan
juices have been absorbed. In bowl, combine remaining ingredients;
add to oysters. Simmer for 3-5 minutes. Serve over hot, buttered
toast, garnished with crumbled bacon.
