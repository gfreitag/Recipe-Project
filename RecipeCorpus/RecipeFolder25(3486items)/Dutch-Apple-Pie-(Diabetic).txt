Title: Dutch Apple Pie (Diabetic)
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 12 servings
For Topping
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Quick-cooking oats
Sweetener equal to 1/2 c
-brown sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1 pk Sugar-free instant vanilla
-pudding mix
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Quick-cooking oats
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Sweetener equal to 1/2 c
-sugar
5 c  Sliced baking apples
1/2 c  Unsweetened frozen apple
-juice concentrate
Pie crust for 1 crust pie
----------------------------
Mix flour, oats, cinnamon, and sweetener with a fork.  Pour over
sliced apples and mix well.  Pour apple juice over top of apples and
mix well again.  Put apple mixture in pie crust; set aside.
For topping, mix dry ingredients with fork.  Add butter and cut
into dry indredients with a pastry blender or two knives until it
resembles coarse crumbs.  Pour mixture over pie filling and press
down evenly over apples.  Bake at 400 degrees for 45-55 minutes.
Makes 12 servings. 2 bread, 3 fat, and 1 fruit exchange. 251
calories, 12 grams of fat, 283 mg. sodium, 5 mg. cholesterol in each.
