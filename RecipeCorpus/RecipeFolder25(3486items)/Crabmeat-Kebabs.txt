Title: Crabmeat Kebabs
Categories: Main dish, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Tarragon
1 lb Bacon slices, halved
2 c  Crabmeat
1 c  Bread crumbs
2 tb Dry sherry
Preheat broiler. Mix all ingredients except bacon. Roll into small
ball. Wrap a bacon slice around each ball; place on slewers. Broil 15
minutes, turning once halfway through cooking time.
