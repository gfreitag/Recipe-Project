Title: Butterscotch Pudding Pralines
Categories: Candy, Pudding
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Margarine
1/2 c  Brown sugar
Mix together in a deep
Saucepan:
1 pk Regular butterscotch pudding
1/2 c  MILNOT
Cook over high heat until sugar dissolves and mixture boils.  Stir
constantly.  Lower heat and continue stirring until it reaches 234
degrees. Remove from heat.  Add pecans.  Stir by hand until mixture
begins to thicken and lose some gloss.  Drop on waxed paper. Randy
