Title: Barbecued Beans
Categories: Side dish, Beans, Sthrn/livng, Ground beef, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Catsup
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Vinegar
1/4 ts Hot sauce
1 lb Beef, ground
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
28 oz Pork and beans
Brown beef and onion; pour off drippings.  Add remaining ingredients;
mix well.  Pour into a 1-1/2 quart casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees
for 30 minutes.
