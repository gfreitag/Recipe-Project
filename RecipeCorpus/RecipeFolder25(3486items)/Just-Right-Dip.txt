Title: Just Right Dip
Categories: Dip, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Dried dill weed
1 t  Seasoned salt
14 1/2 oz Can of asparagus spears
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 t  Hot sauce
In a blender, combine all of the ingredients, and whip until smooth.
Adjust the seasonings to taste.  Serve with corn chips.  Serves 6-8.
Origin: Aunt Bee's Mayberry Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
Nov/94.
