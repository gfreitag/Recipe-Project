Title: Dixie Pork Chops
Categories: Main dish, Pork, Sthrn/livng, Apples
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Sugar, brown
2 tb Flour
1 c  Water; hot
Vinegar; few drops
1/2 c  Raisins, seedlesss
8    Pork chops
2 tb Shortening
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sage, ground
4    Apples; cored & cut in rings
Brown chops in hot shortening.  Remove chops and save drippings.  Put
chops in a shallow baking dish; sprinkle with salt and sage.  Top
with apple rings and sprinkle with sugar.  In skillet blend
drippings, flour, water, and vinegar; cook until thick. Add raisins
and pour over chops. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 1 hour.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
