Title: Cajun Seasoning Mix with Recipes
Categories: Cajun/creol, Mixes, Herbs/spice, Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Seasoning mix
1/3 c  Olive oil
2 lb Salmon steaks or fillets
-SHRIMP:
4 c  Water
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Seasoning mix
1 lb Large shrimp, cleaned
-Cocktail sauce
3 tb Salt
1 tb Paprika
1 tb Onion powder
1 tb Garlic powder
1 tb Cayene pepper
1/2 ts White pepper
1 1/2 ts Whole thyme
3/4 ts Black peppr
1/2 ts Whole oregano
-SALMON:
Combine all seasoning ingredients and store in an airtight container.

Barbecued Cajun Salmon: Marinate the salmon in combined seasoning mix
and olive oil about 20 minutes. BBQ over hot coals 10 minutes total
for each inch thickness. Makes 4 servings.

Spiced Shrimp: Boil water, lemon juice and seasoning mix. Add shrimp,
return to boil. remove from heat; let stand 5 minutes. Drain and
refrigerate. Serve cold with cocktail sauce
