Title: Micro-Poached Chicken Breasts
Categories: Poultry, Microwave, Chicken
Yield: 3 cups
1/2 c  White wine or water
White pepper
1 lb Boneless chicken breast
-halves
Rinse chicken and place around sides of an 8" square glass dish. Pour
wine over chicken and sprinkle with pepper. Cover with vented plastic
wrap and microwave on high 6-7 minutes, or until juice from chicken
is no longer pink. Let stand, covered. 30 minutes. Drain liquid and
let stand until cool enough to handle. Coarsely chop. Use in any
recipe calling for cooked chicken meat. Yields 2 1/2 cups
