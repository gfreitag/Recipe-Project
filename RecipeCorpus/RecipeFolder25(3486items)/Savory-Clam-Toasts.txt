Title: Savory Clam Toasts
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Clam/oyster
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Tomato poaste
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
-a tb dry sherry
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1    Dill pickle, thinly sliced
4 sl Hot buttered toast
3 tb Butter
1 sm Onion, finely chopped
1/2    Green pepper, seeded and
-finely chopped
6 1/2 oz Can chopped clams, drained
1 c  Grated Cheddar cheese
Melt butter, add onion and green pepper; saute for 3 minutes. Add
clams, grated cheese, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, sherry and
cayenne pepper. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until cheese
melts. Place a pickle slice on each slice of toast; spoon clam
mixture on top.
