Title: Aunt Bee's Kerosene Cucumbers
Categories: Can/preserv, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
6 t  Whole spices
6 ea Lumps alum
1 qt Cider vinegar
2 qt Water
1 c  Salt
Cucumbers
1 bn Dill
6 ea Hot peppers
6 ea Cloves garlic
6 sl Onion
Wash and dry enough cucumbers for six sterilized 1 quart jars.  In the
bottom of each jar place a portion of the dill, 1 hot pepper, 1 clove
of garlic, 1 slice of onion, 1 tsp whole spices, a small lump a lum,
and cucumbers.  In a saucpean combine the vinegar, water, and salt.
let the mixture come to a rolling boil, then pour into the jars.
Seal immediately. Makes 6 quarts. Submitted by: Alice and Jim
Schwenke - Houston, Texas Origin: Aunt Bee's Mayberry Cookbook.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
