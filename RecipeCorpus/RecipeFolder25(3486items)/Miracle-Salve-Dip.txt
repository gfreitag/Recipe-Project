Title: Miracle Salve Dip
Categories: Dip, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
3 t  Prepared mustard
1/4 t  Tabasco sauce
1 1/2 c  Almonds
3 T  Chopped green onions
Crackers
Tested and approved by Dr.
-Pendyke.
9 sl Bacon
3 sm Tomatoes, quartered
8 oz Cream cheese, softened and
-cubed
In a large skillet, fry the bacon.  Drain on paper towels, crumble,
and set aside.  IN a blender or food processor blend the tomatoes,
cream cheese, mustard, and tabasco sauce.  Add the almonds, onions,
and bacon blend until the almonds are chopped.  Refrigerate for 2
hours or up to 2 days. Serve with crackers.  Serves 10-12. Origin:
Aunt Bee's Mayberry Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
