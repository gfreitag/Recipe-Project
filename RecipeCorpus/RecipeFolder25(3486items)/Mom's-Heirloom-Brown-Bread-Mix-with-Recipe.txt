Title: Mom's Heirloom Brown Bread Mix with Recipe
Categories: Mixes, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Raisins
-BROWN BREAD:
1 pk Mix
2 c  Uttermilk
1/2 c  Molasses
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Chopped dates
1 ts Salt
Mix: Combine all ingredients until well blended. Store in an airtight
container.

Mom's Heirloom Brown Bread: Preheat oven to 350 and grease loaf pan.
Combine all ingredients, let stand 1/2 hour. Pour into loaf pan and
bake 45-50 minutes.
