Title: Deviled Crab/shrimp Dip
Categories: Diabetic, Appetizer, Seafood, Microwave, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 32 servings
1/2 t  Horseradish
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Pepper
1/4 t  Dry mustard
8 oz Cream cheese
1 c  Crab/Shrimp or combination
2 T  Chopped sweet onion
2 T  Skim milk
Soften cream cheese in micro on high for 2 minutes unwrapped in a
glass dish. Mix all remaining ingredients into cheese and bake on
medium high for 4-6 minutes or until hot.

Serve with vegetable sticks, french bread cubes or crackers.

32 servings 1 Tablespoon = 50 calories
1/2 fat-meat exchange
1/2 fat exchange
Carbohydrates 0
Protein 4 g
Fat 4 g
