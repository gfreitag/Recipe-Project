Title: Butternut Squash Pasta
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 pn Black pepper
1 pn Nutmeg
1 pn Garlic powder
1 sm Butternut squash
1 3/4 c  All purpose flour
1 ts Hazelnut butter
Preheat oven to 350F.

Cut squash into quarters, remove seeds & place in a baking dish with
1/2 c water.  Cover & bake til ltender (50 to 60 minutes).  Let cool
& mash flesh.  Set aside.

In a large bowl, combine 3/4 c squash with remaining ingredients to
form a soft dough.  Turn out onto a floured board & knead until
smooth & pliable like a firm bread dough.

Place in a bowl, cover with plastic wrap & let rest for at least 30
minutes.  Shape & cook dough as desired.
