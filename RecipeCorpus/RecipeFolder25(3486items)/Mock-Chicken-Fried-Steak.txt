Title: Mock Chicken Fried Steak
Categories: Main dish, Ground beef
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Worcestershire
1 lb Ground Beef;lean
2 tb Olive Oil
1    Egg;beaten;
1 c  Crackers;coarsely crushed
1/4 c  Milk
2 tb Onion;finely chopped
1 ts Chili Powder
Combine egg, 1/2 cup cracker crumbs, milk, onion, chili powder,
salt and worcestershire. Add ground beef and mix well.
Shape into six patties 1/2' thick. Coat with remaining cracker
crumbs.
Fry in hot olive oil over medium-high heat about 3 minutes each
side.
Serve with milk gravy.
