Title: Carolina Hash
Categories: Dessert, Sthrn/livng, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Almond extract
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Sherry
1/2 pt Cream, whipping; whipped
1 c  Pineapple, crushed
8 oz Marshmallows, miniature
2/3 c  Almonds, slivered
1/4 c  Vanilla wafers; crushed
Combine all ingredients except whipped cream; mix well.  Fold in
whipped cream.  Pour into an 8" square pan and freeze.  Serve as
salad or dessert.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, March 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
