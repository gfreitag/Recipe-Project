Title: Hot Pepper Oil
Categories: Condiment, Can/preserv
Yield: 3 servings
-(actually, 4 to 6 leaves)
Mild olive oil [use
-extra-virgin]
2 oz Dried red chiles (about 2
-cups)
4 ea Imported bay leaves
Drop the chiles 1 at a time alternately with the bay leaves into a
clean dry bottle.  Fill the bottle with oil and cork or cap securely.
Prepare oil early as the flavors take at least a month to develop.
Oil will keep at room temperature for about 3 months. Store in a cool
dark place. Source: Food & Wine - December, 1993
