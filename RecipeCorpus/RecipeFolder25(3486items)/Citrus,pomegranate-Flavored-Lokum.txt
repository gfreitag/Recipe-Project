Title: Citrus/pomegranate-Flavored Lokum
Categories: Dessert, Middle east
Yield: 1 batch
1 c  Citrus/pomegranate juice
Food coloring
Icing sugar to coat
3    Envelopes unflavored gelatin
1 1/2 c  ;Water
2 c  Sugar
Bring gelatin, water, and sugar to boil and simmer 30 minutes.  Add
fruit juice and food coloring.  Simmer another 5 minutes.  Pour into
oiled pan and chill until firm.  Use sharp, wet knife to cut into 1"
cubes, and drop onto waxed paper covered with icing sugar.  Toss to
coat well.
