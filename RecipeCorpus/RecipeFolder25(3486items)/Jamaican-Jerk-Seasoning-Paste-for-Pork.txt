Title: Jamaican Jerk Seasoning Paste for Pork
Categories: Condiment, Chilies, Caribbean, Pork
Yield: 1 batch
3 tb Lime juice
2 tb Chopped scallions
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground nutmeg
4    Habaneros; seeded & chopped
-or-
7    Jalapenos; seeded & chopped
2 oz Allspice berries OR
1/4 c  Powdered allspice
Pound or puree all the ingredients to make a thick paste. Spread
paste over meat and marinate for 1 hour or longer before grilling.
