Title: Fruit Sandwich
Categories: Diabetic, Sandwich, Fruit, Kids
Yield: 1 sandwich
2 tb Pineapple crushed
-in natural juice
2 sl Bread (any kind)
3 tb Sm-curd cottage cheese;
2 tb Apple, or pear, or apricot
-chopped
2 tb Slivered almonds;
In a small bowl, mix all ingredients (except the bread) together. You
may need to add a little more cottage cheese to bind the ingredients.
Spread the mixture on the bread.  This sandwich is especially good
when served on some quick beards such as banana bread, zucchini bread
or any other quick breads. Source: The Creative Lunch Box by Ellen
