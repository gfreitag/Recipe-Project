Title: Roman-Style Chicken Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Chicken, Poultry, Italian
Yield: 4 pounds
2 ts Coarse grind black pepper
1 c  Finely chopped onion
1/2 c  Grated Romano cheese
1/2 c  Finely chopped green pepper
4    Feet small hog or sheep
-casings
4 lb Chicken meat-15% fat
2 ts Salt, or to taste
Prepare casings. Grind chicken through the coarse disk, mix with
remaining ingredients and grind again. Stuff into casings and twist
off into 4" links. To cook, saute in oil unti browned and cooked
through.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
