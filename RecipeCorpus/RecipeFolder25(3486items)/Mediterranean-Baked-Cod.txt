Instructions:

Preheat oven to 375F.  Rinse cod and pat dry.  Arrange fillets in a
large baking dish, folding thin tail ends under each to give fillets
even thickness.

Pour tomatoes and their juice over fish.  Sprinkle with capers and/or
olives, followed by Parmesan cheese.

Bake fish uncovered in heated oven until just opague in center, 20-25
minutes for fillets about 1 inch thick. (The pan juices are tasty
poured over rice or couscous.)
