Title: Vanishing Oatmeal Raisin Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 48 cookies
1 t  Baking soda
1 t  Ground cinnamon
1/2 t  Salt (opt)
3 c  QUAKER Oats (quick or
-old-fashioned, uncooked)
1 c  SUN-MAID Raisins
1 c  Margarine or butter,
-softened (2 sticks)
1 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
2    Eggs
1 t  Vanilla
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1. Heat oven to 350'F.

2. Beat margarine and sugars until creamy. Add eggs and vanilla; beat
well.

3. Combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt; add to margarine
mixture, mixing well.

4. Stir in oats and raisins; mix well.

5. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet.

6. Bake at 350'F. for 10-12 minutes or until light golden brown.

7. Cool 1 minute on cookie sheet; remove to wire rack. Cool
completely. Store in airtight container.
