Title: Skillet Spanish Rice
Categories: Side dish, Rice, Ground beef
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper, ground black
8 oz Tomato sauce
1 1/2 c  Water
1 lb Beef, ground
1 md Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Pepper, green; diced
1 c  Rice; uncooked
1 ts Chili powder
Cook beef, onion and green pepper in large skillet over medium heat;
drain fat.  Stir in rice, chili powder, salt, pepper, tomato sauce
and water; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 to 20
minutes or until rice is tender.

Each serving provides 237 calories, 16 g. protein, 11 g. fat, 27 g.
carbohydrate, 2 g. dietary fiber, 44 mg. cholesterol and 462 mg.
sodium.

SOURCE: Stretching your Budget with Rice, USA Rice Council pamphlet.
