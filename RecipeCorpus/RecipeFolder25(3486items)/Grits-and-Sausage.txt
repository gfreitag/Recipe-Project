Title: Grits and Sausage
Categories: Nuts/grains, Sausage, Meat, Breakfast
Yield: 8 folks
Black pepper to taste;
1 lb Tenderloin pork sausage;*
4 c  Boiling water;
1 ts Salt;
1 c  Grits;
* There was another recipe calling for summer sausage in this
cookbook. Bring to boil 4 c of salted water, add grits and cook for
3-1/2 minutes. Add 1/2 cup cornmeal and black pepper and mix through.
Cut up pork sausage; add and mix with grits and cornmeal mixture. Put
in loafpan and chill overnight in refrigerator.  Cut in slices about
3/4" thick and fry on both sides until brown. Source: Southern
Living, Our Best Recipes. Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion
and her Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On   10-30-94
