Title: Sauerkraut Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 sm Onion, Chopped
1/2    Bell Pepper, Chopped
1    Carrot, Chopped
1 cn Sauerkraut, Shredded
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Oil
1/3 c  Vinegar
Drain the Kraut, mix the oil and sugar and vinegar together, stir
until the sugar is melted, add the rest and stir well, allow to set
in refrigerator for several hours.
