Title: Caesar Dressing - Bon Appetit
Categories: Salad, Dressing
Yield: 2 cups
1 c  (scant) olive oil
-(preferably extra-virgin)
2 T  Fresh lemon juice
1/8 T  Or more hot pepper sauce
-(such as Tabasco)
3 lg Eggs, room temperature
3 T  Dijon mustard
5 md Garlic cloves, pressed
1 T  Worcestershire sauce
3/4 T  Anchovy paste
Whisk first 5 ingredients in small bowl. Gradually whisk in oil, lemon
juice and hot pepper sauce. (Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover and
refrigerate. Rewhisk before serving.)
