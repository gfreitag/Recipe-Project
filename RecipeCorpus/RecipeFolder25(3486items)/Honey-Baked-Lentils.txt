Title: Honey-Baked Lentils
Categories: Side dish, Casserole, Beans, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1 c  Water
6 sl Bacon
1/2 c  Honey
16 oz Lentils
1 ts Mustard, dry
1/4 ts Ginger, ground
1 tb Soy sauce
Simmer lentils 30 minutes in enough water to cover.  Combine mustard,
ginger, soy sauce, onion, and 1 cup water; stir into cooked lentils.

Cut bacon into 1-inch pieces.  Stir two-thirds of bacon into lentils;
sprinkle remainder over the top.  Pour honey over all.  Cover
tightly. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  Uncover last few minutes to
brown the bacon.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, March 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
