Title: Pineapple Shake
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Vanilla extract
1 cn (20 oz.) pineapple chunks in
-unsweetened juice
1 md Banana, peeled, cut into
-chunks
1 1/2 c  Skim milk
Drain pineapple, reserving 3/4 cup juice.  Measure 1/2 cup pineapple
chunks and save remainder for other use.  Place all ingredients in
blender and process until smooth.  Pour into glasses.  Makes 4
servings. Submitted By NANCY VAINE   On   11-08-94
