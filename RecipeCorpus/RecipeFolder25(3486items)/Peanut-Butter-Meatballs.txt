Title: Peanut Butter Meatballs
Categories: Main dish, Ground beef, Meatballs
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Creamy peanut butter
1 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
-FHMN87A Phill Bower
1 lb Ground beef
1    Egg
1 sm Onion; chopped
1 ts Garlic salt
Combine ground beef, egg, onion, garlic salt and pepper. Mix well.
Shape into 1 inch balls.  Place into an 8x8-inch square pan. Set
aside. In a small bowl, add peanut butter, milk and salt. Mix well.
Pour over meatballs.  Bake in 325 oven for 35 to 40 minutes.

From  the Ultimate Peanut Butter Cookbook   by Arthur Boze
