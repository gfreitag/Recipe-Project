Title: Sunny Peanut-Oat Cookies
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2 c  Rolled Oats
1/2 c  Sunflower Seed;unsalted
1/2 c  Peanut Butter
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 ts Salt
In a medium-sized bowl stir together peanut butter, honey, and salt
until well mixed. Stir in oats and seeds.
Drop by teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350F for 10
minutes or until brown.
