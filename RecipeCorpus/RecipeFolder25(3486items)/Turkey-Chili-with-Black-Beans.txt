Title: Turkey Chili with Black Beans
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Beans, Turkey, Chili
Yield: 4 folks
1/2 ts Dried marjoram;
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes;
1/4 ts Cinnamon;
16 oz Can black beans; rinsed &
-drained
2 c  Cooked turkey; cut into 1/2"
-cubes
1/2 c  Cilantro; coarsely chopped
-stems removed
4 tb Cheddar cheese; shredded
-Reduced-fat
1 c  Onions; chopped
1    Red bell pepper; cut into
-1/4" pieces
2 cl Garlic; peeled & minced
2    Jalapeno peppers; seeded
-minced
28 oz Can tomatoes;
1 tb Chili powder;
1 1/2 ts Ground cumin;
1 1/2 ts Ground coriander
1/2 ts Dried oregano;
In a 3 quart microwave-safe dish, combine onion, bell pepper, garlic,
jalapeno and tomatoes.  Stir in chili powder, cumin, coriander,
oregano, marjoram, red pepper flakes and cinnamon; cover dish.  Cook
in mmicrowave oven on high for 10 minutes; stir halfway through. Stir
in beans and turkey; recover dish.  Cook in microwave oven on High
for 4 minutes; stir in cilantro.  Ladle into bowls, and garnish with
cheese. NOTE: for fulling blending of flavors, prepare a day before
serving. Source: The San Diego Union Food Section, Nov. 24, 1994
