Title: Red Apple Glazed Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Main dish, Fruit, Poultry, Apples
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM---------------------MAKES: 6 SERVINGS--------------------------
MMMMM--------------------------STUFFING-------------------------------
-juice or water
1/2 t  Salt
pn Pepper
pn Allspice
4-5 lb whole roasting
-chicken
16 oz Jar spiced apple rings,
-reserve juice
1 1/2 c  Dry bread cubes
1 c  Sliced 1/2" celery
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/3 c  Butter or margarine, melted
2 ea Tb reserved spiced apple
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
2 ea Tb water
1/4 c  Reserved spiced apple juice
2 ea Tb light corn syrup
Heat oven to 350~. Cut 5 spiced apple rings into eights; reserve
remaining apple rings. Combine with remaining stuffing ingredients.
Stuff chicken; secure to body of chicken. Place on rack in roasting
pan. In saucepan, combine glaze ingredients. Cook, stirring
occasionally, until heated through. Spoon glaze over chicken. Bake,
basting occasionally, for 1 hour. If needed, add 1/4 cup water to
basting juices. Loosley cover chicken with aluminum foil. Continue
baking, basting occasionally, for 1-1 1/2 hours or until chicken is
fork tender. Garnish with reserved apple rings. Source: Land O Lakes
Treasury of Country Recipes Carolyn Shaw's Collection 11-1-94
