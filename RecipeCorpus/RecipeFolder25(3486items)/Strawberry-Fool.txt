Title: Strawberry Fool
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, British
Yield: 1 servings
2 dr Lemon Juice
1 c  Cream;whipping
1 1/2    Strawberry;fresh hulled;pint
1/2 c  Sugar
Puree strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice in food processor fitted
with steel blade.
Whip cream till it holds stiff peaks; stir the puree into it till
the mixture is smooth. Adjust for sweetness, if desired.
Refrigerate for several hours before serving.
