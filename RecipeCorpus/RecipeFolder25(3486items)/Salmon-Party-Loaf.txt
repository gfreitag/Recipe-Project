Title: Salmon Party Loaf
Categories: Appetizer, Seafood
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Pecans
8 oz Cream cheese
1 cn Salmon
1 tb Horseradish
2 tb Minced onion
Soften cheese; clean salmon.  Mix cheese, salmon, horseradish, onion,
lemon juice and salt together in a bowl.  Form into oval shape; coat
with nuts. Serve with Sociable crackers or my favorite Ritz, etc. Let
chill several hours before serving. Randy Rigg
