Title: Big Bucket in the Sky Fried Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 1 batch
1/3 c  Club soda or beer
3 lb Chicken fryer parts
1 1/2 pt Vegetable oil
2 c  Dry pancake mix
2 pk Good Seasons Italian
Dressing mix (0.9 oz)
1 lg Egg
In a roomy bowl, combine the pancake mix with package of salad
dressing mix.  Combine remaining dressing package with egg and club
soda. Dip each piece of chicken into egg/soda mix then into pancake
mixture. Place pieces on rack or toweling, without touching each
other, to dry about five minutes.

Meanwhile, pour enough oil into heavy 2 1/2 quart saucepan or deep
fryer to give you a depth of at least 2 1/2".  Heat oil to about 425
F. Brown coated chicken pieces in hot oil for 5-6 minutes.  Reduce
heat as necessary to regulate temperature recovery.  Spear each piece
with the tip of a sharp knife, removing to baking sheet.  Arrange
pieces on sheet so that they aren't touching.

Bake, uncovered, in preheated 350 F. oven about 30 minutes.
