Title: Stir-Fry Green Beans
Categories: Side dish, Beans, Vegetable, Stir fry, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Soy Sauce
2 tb Peanut Oil
2 tb Water
3 c  Green Beans;trimmed
2    Garlic;minced;cloves
2 tb Ginger;grated
Heat oil in a wok or skillet.
Add green beans, ginger and garlic. Stir fry for 2 minutes at high
heat.
Add soy sauce and water and cook, covered for 3 - 5 more minutes,
until beans are done.
