Title: Master Mix Recipe
Categories: Bread
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Baking Powder
4 c  Shortening
3 tb Salt
20 c  All-Purpose flour
2 tb Cream of Tartar
2 1/2 c  Dry Milk
1/2 c  Sugar
Measure all ingredients accurately. Stir the flour, dry milk, salt,
baking powder, cream of tartar and sugar together until well blended
in a large bowl. Cut in the shortening with a pastry blender or your
hands until evenly distributed and resembles coarse meal. Store this
mix in an air-tight container such as a plastic ice cream container.
Keep at room temperature in a dark place. Keeps 4 weeks at cool
temperature.
Use this mix in place of Bisquick in any recipe.
