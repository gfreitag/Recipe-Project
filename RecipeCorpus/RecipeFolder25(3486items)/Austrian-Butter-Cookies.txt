Title: Austrian Butter Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, European
Yield: 3 dozen  1 c flour 1 c sugar 2/3 c nuts; chopped 1/2 c butter; softened

Sift the flour and sugar together.  Add the chopped nuts. Blend in
the butter.  Stir with a spoon until a soft dough is formed. Chill
for 1-2 hours.

Roll the dough out on a floured board to 1/8" thickness.  Cut into
rounds with a biscuit cutter and place rounds on an ungreased cookie
sheet. Bake at 375 (191C) for 7-10 min.

Yields about 3 dozen.

Can be made into a sandwich cookie.  Traditional Austrian manner is to
spread thinly with jam, cover with a second cookie, then cover top
with chocolate frosting and a pecan or walnut half.

CHOCOLATE FROSTING

1  TB butter 1/3 c powdered sugar 1 egg yolk 1 sq unsweetened
chocolate,
-melted and cooled.

Cream butter and sugar.  Add egg yolk and chocolate.  Blend well.
