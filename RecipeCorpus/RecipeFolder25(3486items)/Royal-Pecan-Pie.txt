Title: Royal Pecan Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 servings
2 T  Butter, melted
1 c  Pecans, chopped
Crunch Topping
3 T  King white syrup
1/3 c  Brown sugar, packed
3 T  Butter
1 1/2 c  Pecans halves
Pecan Filling
1 c  King Golden Syrup
4 ea Eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
1 t  Vanilla
Mix all filling ingredients and pour into pie shell.  Bake for 50-55
minutes at 350.  Cover edge of pie shell for last 30 minutes.

Combine all topping ingredients in medium saucepan.  Cook 2 to 3
minutes over low heat, stirring constantly.  Spread evenly over top
of pie for last 10 minutes of baking.
