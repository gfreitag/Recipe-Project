Title: Crab Louis
Categories: Appetizer, Salad, Seafood, Cajun/creol, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Lettuce;chopped
Lettuce;leaves;
1 c  Sauce Louis
2 c  Crab;cooked
2    Egg;hard boiled;
2 tb Chives;chopped
Slice the hard-boiled  eggs. Arrange lettuce leaves in bottoms of
individual salad bowls (four bowls for salad, six for appetizer).
Heap chopped lettuce on the lettuce leaves, the crab meat on the
chopped lettuce, and a few slices of egg to one side in the bowls.
Pour the Sauce Louis over the crab, sprinkle with chives, and serve.
