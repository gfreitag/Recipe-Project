Title: Mexicali Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Chili powder
1    Garlic clove; peeled
1    Egg yolk; hard-cooked
3/4 c  French dressing
1 1/2 tb Worcestershire sauce
Sieve the egg yolk into the French dressing.  Blend Worcestershire
sauce and chili powder together and stir into dressing.  Jab a
toothpick into the garlic clove and drop into the dressing.  This
dressing is best made several hours in advance so it willhave time to
"ripen." Remove the garlic before serving.
