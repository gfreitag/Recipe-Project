Title: Mexican Fiesta Dip Mix with Dip Recipe
Categories: Tex/mexican, Mixes, Herbs/spice, Dip
Yield: 7 servings
-DIP:
3 tb Dip mix
1 c  Regular or lowfat
-mayonnaise
1 c  Sour cream -OR-
1 c  Lowfat yogurt
1/2 c  Dried parsley
1/3    Dried minced onion
1/4 c  Dried chives
1/3 c  Chili powder
1/4 c  Ground cumin
1/4 c  Salt
Combine all ingredients; refrigerate 2-4 hours. Serve with tortilla
chips or fresh veggies. Makes 2 cups dip.
