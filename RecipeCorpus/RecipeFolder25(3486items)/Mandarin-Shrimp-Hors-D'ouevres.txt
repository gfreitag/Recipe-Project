Title: Mandarin Shrimp Hors D'ouevres
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Stir fry, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 6 servings
-Salt to taste
1 tb Sherry
6 tb Oil
1 lb Small shrimp, cleaned
Heat oil in wok or frying pan. Add shrimp and stir fry until pink;
drain. Sprinkle with salt and sherry and serve.
