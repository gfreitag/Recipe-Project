Title: Curried Rice with Fruit Mix With Recipe
Categories: Curries, Mixes, Fruit, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 tb Instant minced onion
3 tb Golden raisins
-RICE:
2 3/4 c  Water
3 tb Butter
1 pk Rice mix
1 1/2 c  White rice
2 1/4 ts Curry powder
2    Chicken bouillon cubes,
-crumbled
1/3 c  Mixed dried fruits
1/2 c  Slivered almonds
Mix: Layer in order given in an airtight container.

Curried Rice with Fruit: Boil water and butter. Add rice mix, cover,
reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes. 6 servings.
