Title: Cranberry Nut Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert, Holiday, Fruit
Yield: 1 pie
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1/3 c  Margarine; melted
Ice cream, vanilla
1 1/4 c  Cranberries
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Walnuts; chopped
1    Egg
Preheat oven to 325F.  Butter a 9-inch pie pan and layer cranberries
on bottom of pan.  Sprinkle with brown sugar and nuts.

In a bowl, beat egg until thick.  Gradually add sugar, beating until
well blended.  Stir in flour and butter and blend well.  Pour over
cranberries. Bake 45 min.  Serve with vanilla ice cream.
