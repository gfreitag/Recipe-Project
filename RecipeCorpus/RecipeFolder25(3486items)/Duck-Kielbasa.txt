Title: Duck Kielbasa
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Poultry
Yield: 4 pounds
1 ts Marjoram
1 ts Summer savory
1/2 ts Allspice
1 tb Sweet paprika
4    Feet small hog casings
4 lb Domestic duck
2 ts Salt, or to taste
2 ts Black pepper
4 cl Very finely minced garlic
Prepare casings. Grind duck through the fine disk; combine with
remaining ingredients and grind again. Stuff casings and twist off
into 3-4" links. Saute in oil until evenly browned and cooked through.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
