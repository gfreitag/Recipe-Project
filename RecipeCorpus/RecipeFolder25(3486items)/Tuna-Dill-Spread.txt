Title: Tuna Dill Spread
Categories: Seafood, Fish, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
-cucumber
2 T  Lemon juice
1-2 T Minced fresh dill
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1 cn Tuna, drained and flaked (6
-1/8 oz.)
1 pk Cream cheese, softened (3
-oz.)
1/3 c  Finely chopped seeded
In a bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well. Use as a sandwich
filling or spread on crackers. Yield 1 1/4 cups. SOURCE:* Taste of
