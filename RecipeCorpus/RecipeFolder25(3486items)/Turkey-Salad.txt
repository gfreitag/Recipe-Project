Title: Turkey Salad
Categories: Salad, Turkey, Vegetable, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  Dijon mustard
1/4 t  Salt
pn Pepper
2 ea Tart apples, upeeled and
-cubed
2 ea Stalks celery, chopped
2 c  Cubed cooked turkey
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts, toasted
Lettuce leaves
Here's a novel way to use
-up your turkey
-leftovers... in a crunchy
Salad.
1/4 c  Light sour cream
1/4 c  Light mayonnaise
2 T  Chopped fresh parsley
2 T  Lemon juice
1 T  Liquid honey
Blend sour cream, mayonnaise, parsley, lemon juice, honey, mustard,
salt and pepper.  Toss with apples, celery, turkey and half of the
walnuts. Mound onto lettuce lined plates and sprinkle with remaining
walnuts. Makes 4 servings. Origin: Canadian Living, December 1994.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
