Title: Toffee Squares
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Dessert, Holiday
Yield: 6 dozen
2 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk
12 oz Chocolate chips
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Butter
1 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg yolk
1 ts Vanilla
350  20-25 min
Cream butter, sugar egg yolk, and vanilla. Stir in flour throughly.
Spread on rectangle sheet to a 14 x 10 shape. Dough will be soft.
Spread choc. chips on top and let stand till soft. Spread over top
and sprinkle with nuts. cut into small squares while warm. Typed by
