Title: Tangy Ginger Dressing - Bon Appetit
Categories: Salad, Dressing
Yield: 1 cup
3 T  Vegetable oil
2 T  Finely chopped lemon peel
-(yellow part only)
2 T  Tomato puree
1/2 t  Sugar
1/4 c  Chopped peeled fresh ginger
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Water
3 T  Distilled white vinegar
Combine all ingredients in blender. Blend on high until smooth, about
2 minutes. Season dressing to taste with salt and pepper.

The restaurant serves this dressing over a salad of shredded iceberg
lettuce, sliced mushrooms and red bell pepper strips.
