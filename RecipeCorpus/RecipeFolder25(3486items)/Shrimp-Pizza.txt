Title: Shrimp Pizza
Categories: Main dish, Pizza, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Parsley; chopped
1 1/2 ts Oregano
3    Pizza crusts (9"); unbaked
3/4 lb Cheese, Mozzarella; thinly
-sliced
3 cn Shrimp; 4-1/2 to 5 oz. ea.
1/3 c  Onion; chopped
3    Garlic cloves; finely choppd
1/2 c  Shortening (or oil); melted
18 oz Tomato paste, Italian style
Drain shrimp and rinse with cold water.  Cook onion and garlic in
shortenign until tender.  Add tomato paste and simmer for 5 minutes.
Remove from heat; add parsley and oregano.  Place pizza crusts on
well-greased baking pans.  Cover each crust with one-third of the
sauce, one-third of the shrimp, and one-third of the cheese.  Bake at
425 degrees for 20 minutes or until crust is brown and cheese melts.
