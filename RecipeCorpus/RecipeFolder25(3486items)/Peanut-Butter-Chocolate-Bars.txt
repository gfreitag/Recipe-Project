Title: Peanut Butter Chocolate Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 30 cookines
3    Eggs
1 c  All purpose flour
2 c  Milk Chocolate morsels
1 c  Colored sugar or small
Colored candies
1 c  Peanut Butter, smooth or
Chunkie
6 tb Butter or margarine
3/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 ts Vanilla extract
Beat peanut butter, butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, and
vanilla in large mixer bowl until creamy.  Beat in eggs; beat in
flour. Stir in 3/4 cup morsels.  Spread into greased 13 x 9 inch
baking pan.

Bake in preheated oven 350 overn for 20 to 25 minutes or until edges
are lighly browned.  Remove from over; immediately sprinkle with
remaining morsels.  Let stand for 5 minutes; spread.  Sprinkle
colored sugar over melted chocolate.  Cool in pan on wire rack
