Title: Gwynne's Hot Dogs and Noodles
Categories: Hot dogs, Pasta, Cheese/eggs, Casserole
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Cheddar Cheese Soup,
-undiluted (10 3/4 oz.)
1 md Onion, Chopped
1/2 cn Water
1/4 c  Cracker Crumbs
2 c  Cooked Medium Noodles
4 ea Hot Dogs, Cubed
2 T  Butter
1 T  Mustard
1 ea Clove Garlic, Minced
1 cn Tomato Soup, undiluted (10
-3/4 oz.)
Brown hot dogs, onion, and garlic. Mix soups, water, and mustard and
heat. Stir in hot dog mixture then add cooked noodles. Put this
mixture in a 1 1/2 quart casserole, top with crumbs. Bake at 350
degrees for 30 minutes. SOURCE:*Gwynne Bodle SHARED BY:Jim Bodle
