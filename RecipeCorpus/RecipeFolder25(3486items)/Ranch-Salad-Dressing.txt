Title: Ranch Salad Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing, Homemade
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 t  Pepper
1/4 t  Paprika
1/2 t  Onion powder
1 t  Dried parsley flakes
1/4 t  Dried dill weed
1/2 t  Celery salt
Makes 2-1/4 cups
2 c  Plain low fat yogurt
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1/4 t  Msg
1 t  Dried minced onion
Mix all ingredients together.  Refrigerate for 1 hour to blend
flavors. Use as dip for raw vegetables, or salad dressing. Origin:
Women's Circle, for cooks on the go. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/94
