Title: Broiled Catfish Fillets
Categories: Main dish, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Lemon juice
1 c  Dry white wine
6    Catfish fillets
Paprika
1 sm Onion; chopped
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Garlic salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
In a 1-1/2 quart saucepan bring onion, mustard, salt, pepper,
Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, lemon juice and wine to a boil;
remove from heat.  Place catfish fillets on a foil-lined cookie
sheet; cover with sauce and sprinkle with paprika.  Broil 7 inches
from heat at 450 degrees about 20 minutes.
