Title: Caribbean Pepper Mix
Categories: Caribbean, Mixes, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Malaar green peppercorns
2 tb Whole Jamacian allspice
1/2 c  Muntok white peppercorns
1/2 c  Tellicherry black
-peppercorns
Mix all ingredients and store in an airtight container.
