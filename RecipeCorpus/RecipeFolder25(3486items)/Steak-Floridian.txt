Title: Steak Floridian
Categories: Main dish, Sthrn/livng, Fruit, Vegetable, Beef
Yield: 6 servings
1    Orange; unpeeled, thinly
-sliced
1 md Onion; thinly sliced
1    Lemon; unpeeled, thinly
-sliced
6    Cloves, whole
2 lb Steak, round; cut 1/2" thick
1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
3 tb Shortening
1 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Water
Cut steak into 6 serving-size pieces.  Combine flour, salt, and
pepper; pound into steak.  Brown steak in hot shortening.  Arrange
steak in baking dish.  Combine catsup and water and pour over meat.
Place orange slices on top of meat; top with onion slices, then lemon
slices. Stick a whole clove in each stack.  Cover dish and bake at
350 degrees for 1 hour or until meat is tender.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
