Title: Raisin Fudge
Categories: Candy, Fruit, 1941, Chocolate
Yield: 6 servings
2    Squares chocolate
2 tb Raisins
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 ts Vanilla
2 tb Butter or butter substitute
2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
Melt butter; add sugar, milk, molasses, and chocolate.  Heat gently
and stir until chocolate is melted.  Bring to the boiling point and
boil to soft ball stage (234 - 238 F).  Remove from fire. Cool to
room temperature. Beat until creamy.  Add raisins, nuts, and
flavoring. Continue beating until mixture will hold its shape. Pour
into well-buttered pan. Cut in squares. Mrs. H.J.T., Tulsa, OK.
