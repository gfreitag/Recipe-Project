Title: Pseudo-Salisbury Steaks
Categories: Ground beef, Beef, Microwave, Burgers
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM--------------------SOURCE: MAINBEEF.ZIP-------------------------
1/2 ea - small onion, sliced and
-separated into rings
2 ea - Tbsp thinly sliced celery
(MICROWAVE)
1 ea - beaten egg
3 ea - Tbsp fine dry bread crumbs
1 1/2 ea - tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ea - lb ground beef
1/2 ea - 1 oz pkg brown gravy mix
Combine eggs, crumbs, Worcestershire sauce and dash of pepper. Add
beef; mix well. Shape into two 1/2" thick patties. Place in a
microwave safe 10 x 6 x 2" baking dish. Cover with waxed paper.
Microwave, loosely covered, on 100%(high) for 2 minutes, rotating
baking dish a half turn once. Turn patties over. Cook, loosely
covered, for 1 to 2 minutes more or until done. Remove patties,
reserving drippings in dish. Keep patties warm. Stir gravy mix,
onion, celery and dash of pepper into reserved drippings. Stir in 1/2
cup water. Cook, uncovered, for 3 to 4 minutes or until thickened and
bubbly, stirring every minute. Skim off fat. Spoon over patties.
Makes 2 servings.
