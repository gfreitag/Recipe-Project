Title: Cranberry Glazed Rolls
Categories: Fruit, Bread, Muffin
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Brown sugar
6 ea To 8 brown 'n serve rolls
1/4 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Jellied cranberry sauce
Set oven at 400 F.  Grease muffin pans or custard cups.  Sprinkle a
few chopped nuts into each.  Combine cranberry sauce that has been
crushed with a fork and brown sugar.  Put a Tbsp. of the mixture in
each muffin cup. Then turn brown 'n serve roll upside down and press
into each muffin cup. Bake at 400 F. for 12 to 15 minutes.  Let cool
for 4 to 5 minutes. Invert pans and gently remove rolls. From: 101
