Title: Popeye Pesto
Categories: Diabetic, Vegetable, Salsa/sauce, Kids
Yield: 2 cups
2 tb Dried leaf basil;
1/4 c  Almonds; chopped
2 cl Garlic;
1/4 c  Fresh parsley; stems
1 lb Fresh spinach; trimmed
1/2 c  Plain low-fat yogurt;
1/2 c  Low-fat cottage cheese; (1%)
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
removed

Steam spinach lightly in just the water remaining on leaves after
rinsing. Drain in a blender or food processor fitted with the metal
blade, combine spinach and remaining ingredients and process until
smooth. Makes about 2 cups. Use as a salad dressing or serve over
pasta, warm or chilled Food Exchange per serving: FREE; CAL: 16; CHO:
1mg; CAR: 1g; PRO: 1g; SOD: 38mg; FAT: 1g; Source: Light & Easy
