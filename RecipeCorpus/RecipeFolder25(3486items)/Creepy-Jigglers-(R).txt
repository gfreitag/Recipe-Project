Title: Creepy Jigglers (R)
Categories: Snack
Yield: 24 pieces
1 pk JELL-O Brand Orange Flavor
2 1/2 c  Boiling water
3 pk JELL-O Brand Grape Flavor
STIR boiling water into gelatins in large bowl 3 minutes or until
completely dissolved. Pour into 13x9" pan.

REFRIGERATE at least 3 hours or until firm. Dip bottom of pan in warm
water about 15 seconds. Cut into Halloween shapes with cookie cutters
all the way through gelatin. (To cut Halloween shapes without cookie
cutters, place paper or cardboard patterns on gelatin. Cut around
patterns on knife.) Lift from pan.
