Title: Chicken a L'orange
Categories: Main dish, Sthrn/livng, Fruit, Chicken
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Orange peel; grated
1 c  Orange juice
1 ts Sugar
1    Orange; sectioned
2 1/2 tb Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 lb Fryer; cut into pieces
3 tb Butter (or marg.); melted
Combine flour, chili powder, and salt; dredge chicken pieces,
reserving remaining flour mixture.  Brown chicken in melted butter
over medium heat.

Remove chicken from skillet; stir in remaining flour mixture, orange
peel, orange juice, and sugar.  Return chicken to skillet; cover.
Simmer 45 minutes or untl chicken is tender.  Remove cover; add
orange sections and heat 5 minutes.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, March 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
