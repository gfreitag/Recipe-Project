Title: Baked Rice Pudding
Categories: Rice, Low-cal/fat, Pudding
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Honey
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Rice; cooked
1 1/2 c  Milk, 1% low fat
1 c  Raisins
Combine all ingredients.  Pour into a casserole, cover and bake at 325
degrees for 45 minutes.  May be served hot or cold.
