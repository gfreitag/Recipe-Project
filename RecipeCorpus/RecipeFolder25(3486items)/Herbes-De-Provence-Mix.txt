Title: Herbes De Provence Mix
Categories: Herbs/spice, Mixes, French
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Whole dried oregano
2 tb Whole dried rosemary
1/2 c  Dried whole thyme
1/4 c  Whole dried basil
Mix Spices,  together thoroughly. Store in an airtight container.

Source: The Perfect Mix by Diane Phillips ISBN 0-688-12104-7 Typed by
