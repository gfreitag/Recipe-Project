Title: Acadian Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Tabasco Sauce
1 ts Worcestershire
1/2 ts Thyme;dried
Salt;
Sugar;
tb Butter
1/2 c  Green Pepper;chopped
2    Garlic;crushed;cloves
1 c  Onion;chopped
1/2 c  Celery;minced
16 oz Tomato;can undrained
1    Bay Leaf;
Chop tomatoes, set aside. Reserve liquid from can.
Saute all remaining vegetables in butter till tender.
Add herbs and spices, cook 1 more minute.
Add tomatoes and reserved liquid from can, cook about 10 minutes
more.
Discard bay leaf before serving.
