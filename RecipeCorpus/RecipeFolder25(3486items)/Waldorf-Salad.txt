Title: Waldorf Salad
Categories: Salad, Side dish, Fruit, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Mustard;Dijon
1 c  Walnuts;toasted chopped
Lettuce;chopped Romaine;
1 c  Apple;skinned diced
1/2 c  Apple;unskinned diced
1 1/2 c  Celery;diced
2/3 c  MAYONNAISE
Combine the diced apples, celery, the mayonnaise, and the mustard.
Arrange the salad on the chopped lettuce and top with the walnuts.
Serve at once, as the apples will discolor if left out.
