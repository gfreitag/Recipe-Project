Title: Cranberry Corn Muffins
Categories: Diabetic, Muffin, Bread, Fruit
Yield: 12 sweet ones
1    Egg; beaten
1 c  Skim milk;
1/4 c  Walnut oil;
1 c  Fresh cranberries;
1 c  Yellow cornbread;
1/2 c  Unbleached a-p flour;
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour;
4 ts Baking powder;
1 c  Skim milk;
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Coat a 12-cup muffin pan with non-stick
cooking spray.  In a medium-size bowl, combine cornmeal, flours and
baking powder.  In another bowl, mix milk, egg and oil; stir into dry
ingredients. Fold in cranberries.  Spoon batter into muffin cups (2/3
full). Bake 15 to 20 minutes, until golden-brown. Food Exchanges per
serving: 1 STARCH/BREAD EXCHANGE + 1 FAT EXCHANGE; CAL: 143; CHO:
23mg; CAR: 20g; PRO: 3g; SOD: 121mg; FAT: 6g; Source: Light & Easy
