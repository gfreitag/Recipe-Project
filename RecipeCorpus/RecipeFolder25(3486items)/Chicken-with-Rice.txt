Title: Chicken with Rice
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Crockpot, Rice, Chicken
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Minced parsley
1/8 t  Saffron
1 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Uncook rice
1 cl Garlic, crushed
1 lg Chicken, 2-5 lbs, cut in pcs
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Paprika
1/8 t  Pepper
1/2 lg Onion, chopped
1 lg Tomato, peeled and chopped
Season chicken with salt, paprika, and pepper. Put in crock pot. Add
other ingedients, except rice. Cook 6 - 8 hours. Add rice and stir.
Cook on high 1 hour.
