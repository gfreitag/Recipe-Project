Title: Zucchini Mandarin Ada
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish, Diabetic
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 t  Nutmeg
1/4 c  Slivered almonds, toasted
1 lb Zucchini
8 oz Canned mandarine oranges
Cut the zucchini into slices. Steam until tender. Add the orange
slices and the nutmeg. Sprinkle with the almonds just before serving.
From the ADA Holiday Cookbook by Dr. Betty Wedman 1 serving = 1/2
fruit exchange + 1/2 fat exchange = 60 Cal, 8 CHO, 2 PRO, 3 Fat, 1
Na, 239 K, 0 Cholest Posted by PHILLIP BOWER.
