Title: Aioli Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    French Bread;crustless;slice
1/4 c  Milk
2    Egg;yolk of;
5    Garlic;crushed;cloves
1 ts Mustard;prepared
1 tb Lemon Juice
3/4 c  Olive Oil
Sauce for seafood, beef.
Soak bread in milk, wring dry. Place all ingredients except olive
oil in blender or processor; blend until smooth, slowly adding oil.
