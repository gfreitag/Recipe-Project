Title: Iowa Pork Tenderloin Roast
Categories: Pork, Main dish, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Cinnamon
1 tb Orange juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Ground cloves
3 lb Pork tenderloin roast
1 c  Cranberry sauce
1 tb Grey Poupon mustard
2 tb Brown sugar
Mix all ingredients together and spread on top of roast. Bake
uncovered at 325 degrees for 2 hours.

Recipe by Helen Burmeister; as published in the Cedar Rapids Gazette
