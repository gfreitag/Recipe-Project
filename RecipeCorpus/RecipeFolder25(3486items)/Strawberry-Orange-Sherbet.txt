Title: Strawberry-Orange Sherbet
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Frozen orange juice
-concentrate
2 tb Liquid (orange juice, rum
-or water)
16 oz Frozen unsweetened
-strawberries
1/2 c  Plain non-fat yogurt
2 tb Honey
Put all in a food processor or a powerful blender; puree. Serve
immediately or store in freezer (if frozen hard, put in refrigerator
30 minutes to soften).

Per half-cup: 40 milligrams vitamin C, 78 calories, 2 grams protein,
16 grams carbohydrates, 2 grams fiber, 0.1 grams fat, 17 milligrams
sodium. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   10-23-94
