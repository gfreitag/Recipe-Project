Title: Cooked Turkey Giblets and Broth
Categories: Poultry, Soup/stew, Turkey, Holiday
Yield: 3 cups
Parsley
1    Bay leaf
2 ts Salt
1 qt Water
Turkey neck
Turkey giblets
-reserve liver
1 lg Onion; cut
1    Celery with leaves; cut
Place all ingredients (except liver) in saucepan.  Cover and bring to
boil. Lower heat and simmer 2 hours.  Add liver for last 15 minutes.
Strain. Use broth as desired. Chop giblets. Use as desired.
