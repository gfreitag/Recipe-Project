Title: Crab-Stuffed Avocados
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Vegetable, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Worcestershire
1 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 tb Onion;grated
1/4 ts Celery Salt
1 ds Cayenne
1/2 c  Cheddar Cheese
3    Avocado;
2 c  Crab;cooked
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1 c  Milk
6 tb Vinegar
3    Garlic;cloves
Peel avocados and cut in half. Place 1/2 clove peeled garlic and 1
tablespoon vinegar in each half.
Melt butter in saucepan; add flour, stir. Slowly add milk, stirring
constantly. Add all remaining ingredients except cheese and heat
through.
Remove garlic and vinegar from avocado halves; fill with crab
mixture. Sprinkle with cheese and place in pan with 1/4 inch water in
the bottom. Bake for 15 minutes at 350 F.
