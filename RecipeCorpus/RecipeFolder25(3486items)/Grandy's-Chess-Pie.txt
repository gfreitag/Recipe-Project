Title: Grandy's Chess Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert
Yield: 1 pie
1 ts Corn meal
3 ea Eggs
2 dr Almond flavor
1 ea Unbaked pie shell
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Flour
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 c  Butter
1 ts Vanilla
Mix ingreds. together.  Pour into pie shell.  Bake at 350 F. abt.
35 min. or until knife inserted comes out dry. If oven is too hot,
set pan of water beneath pie.
