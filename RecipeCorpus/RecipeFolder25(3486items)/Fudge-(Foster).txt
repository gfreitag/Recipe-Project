Title: Fudge (Foster)
Categories: Candy, Dessert, Snack, Chocolate
Yield: 5 servings
1 ea 5oz jar marshmallow creme
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Nuts (or more)
5 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Butter
1 c  Milk
24 oz Chocolate bits
Heat the sugar, butter and milk to a full rolling boil, about 6
minutes, stirring. Add the chocolate bits and the marshmallow creme,
stir in. Then add the vanilla and nuts, stir in. Pour in large pan or
cookie sheet.
