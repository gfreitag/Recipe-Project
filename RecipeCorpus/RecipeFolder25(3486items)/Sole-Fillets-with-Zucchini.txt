Title: Sole Fillets with Zucchini
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Fish, Vegetable, Mushroom
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1 sm Zucchini, thinly sliced
1 tb Capers
1 tb Chopped parsley
1/2    Lemon, juiced
4    Sole fillets (6 oz)
-Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Flour
2 tb Vegetable oil
3 tb Butter
Sprinkle fish fillets with salt and pepper; coat with flour. Heat oil
and 2 tb butter in large fry pan. Add fish fillets and cook for 3
minutes. Turn and cook 3 minutes more. Transfer fish to serving
platter; keep warm. Add mushrooms, turn heat to high. Add zucchini
and cook for 5 minutes, shaking pan frequently. Add capers and
chopped parsley; stir to mix well. Add remianing butter to pan; cook,
stirring, 2-3 minutes. Stir in lemon juice. Pour mixture over fish
and serve.

Source: Creative Cooking: Fish and Other Seafood Carolyn Shaw's
