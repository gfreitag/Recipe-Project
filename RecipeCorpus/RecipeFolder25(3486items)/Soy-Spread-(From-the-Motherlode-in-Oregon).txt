Title: Soy Spread (From the Motherlode in Oregon)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Sandwich, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 quart
1 tb Honey
2 tb Tamari
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Soy flour
2 1/2 c  ;Water
2 1/2 c  Oil
1    Lemon; juice of
4    Garlic cloves (up to 5)
-- mashed
"This glorious, original spread from the Motherlode in Oregon can be
served by itself as a sandwich spread or used as a vegetable
substitute for mayonnaise."

Combine soy flour and water in a heavy saucepan.  Bring to a boil over
medium heat, stirring frequently.  Then lower heat, and simmer 20
minutes, stirring occasionally.  Cool.  Add remaining ingredients.

From _Country Commune Cooking_ by Lucy Horton.  New York: Coward,
