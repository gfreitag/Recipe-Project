Title: Supreme Broccoli
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
3    Eggs; beaten
8 oz Cheese spread; jar
1/2 c  Cracker crumbs
Butter (or marg.)
20 oz Broccoli, chopped; frozen
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/4 c  Butter (or marg.); melted
2 tb Flour, all-purpose
1/2 c  Water
Cook broccoli according to package directions; drain.  Saute onion in
1/4 cup butter until tender; blend in flour.  Stir in water, and cook
until smooth and thickened; remove from heat.  Stir in eggs and
cheese, blending welll add broccoli.

Spoon mixture into a greased 2-quart shallow casserole; sprinkle with
cracker crumbs, and dot with butter.  Bake at 325 degrees for 30 to 40
minutes.
