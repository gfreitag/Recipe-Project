Title: Camembert Spread
Categories: Bread, Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Spread
Yield: 16 servings
1/4 ts Worcestershire
1/2 c  Pecans;ground
8 oz Camembert
8 oz Cream Cheese;softened
3 tb White Wine
In mixing bowl, combine Camembert cheese and cream cheese until
smooth. Gradually beat in wine. Add steak sauce or Worcestershire
sauce.
Gradually add ground pecans.  Mix well.  Fill pastry bag with cheese
mixture and spread mixture over melba toast, crackers or on French
bread.
If you don't have a pastry bag, use knife to spread.  There is
enough mixture for 7 to 8 dozen canapes.
