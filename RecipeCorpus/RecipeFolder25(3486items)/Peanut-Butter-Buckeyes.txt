Title: Peanut Butter Buckeyes
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Candy, Holiday, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Rice crispies
12 oz Chocolate chips
1 1/2    Sticks melted butter
2 c  Chunky peanut butter
1 lb Powdered sugar
mix butter,peanut butter and sugar. add rice crispies roll into balls
and dip in melted chocolate.
