Title: Un-Fried Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Non Fat Yougart
Chicken Breasts, Thighs or
-Drumsticks.  Skin Removed
MMMMM----------------FOR BREADING:  MIX TOGETHER---------------------
1/4 t  Oregano
1/4 t  Tyme
1 T  Creole Seasoning
1 T  Old Bay Seasoning
1 c  Italian style bread crumbs
1 c  All purpose Flour
ds (or more) Cyaenne pepper
-(I like more)
1/4 t  Basil
Preheat oven to 400  Spray metal pan w/nonfat Vegetable spray. Dip
skinless chicken in yougart then dip into breading mixture. Place
breaded chicken in pan, not toughing. Spray tops of chicken ligltly
with the nonfat Vegetable spray. Cook 40-50 minutes turning chicken
over 1/2 way (or until done, be careful not to burn it) From Wendy
Mohaske, Mich State Police Northville Regional Dispatch Fat content,
Breasts 2.2g, Dark meat 4.2 meat
