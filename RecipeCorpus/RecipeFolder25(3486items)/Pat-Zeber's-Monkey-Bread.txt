Title: Pat Zeber's Monkey Bread
Categories: Breakfast, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Stick Margerine
Cinnamon Sugar mix
3 cn Pillsbury Buttermilk
-Biscuits
1 c  Brown Sugar
Melt together the brown sugar and margerine, heat and stir until
blended. quarter 15 biscuits, roll each quarter into a ball, roll in
cinnamon sugar. place balls in buttered bundt pan.  Pour the
butter/brown sugar mixture over balls.  Continue with the rest of the
biscuits. Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes.  Invert on plate
immediately after removing from oven.
