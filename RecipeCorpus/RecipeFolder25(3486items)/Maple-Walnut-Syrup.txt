Title: Maple-Walnut Syrup
Categories: Can/preserv, Syrup, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  (120 mL) sugar
2 c  (480 mL) walnut pieces
1 1/2 c  (300 mL) corn syrup
1 c  (240 mL) maple syrup
1/2 c  (120 mL) water
Combine corn syrup, maple syrup, water and sugar in a saucepan over
medium heat.  Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.  Stir in nuts,
reduce heat and simmer syrup about 15 minutes or until thick.  Pour
hot, into hot jars, leaving 1/4 inch (6 mm) head space.  Adjust caps.
Process 10 minutes in boiling water bath.  Yield: about 4 half pints.
