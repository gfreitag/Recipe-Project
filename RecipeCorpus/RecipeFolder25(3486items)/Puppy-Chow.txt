Title: Puppy Chow
Categories: Kids, Snack, Microwave
Yield: 6 cups
1/2 c  Margerine
5 c  To 6 c  Oatbake -=OR=-
5 c  To 6 c  Crispix cereal
2 1/2 c  Powdered sugar (optional)
1 lg Pk chocolate -=OR=-
1 lg Pk butterscotch chips
1/2 c  To 3/4 c. peanut butter
1    Stick butter -=OR=-
Melt (microwave is easiest) chips, peanut butter and butter. Mix in
cereal. Stir quickly and well, to coat. Place powdered sugar in
plastic bag, put cereal in bag, shake well to coat.

Six cups for all those hungry pups around.
