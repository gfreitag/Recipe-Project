Title: Jalapeno Corn Bread Sticks
Categories: Bread, Tex/mexican
Yield: 12 servings
375 ea Oven. Grease corn stick
-pans.
1 T  Baking powder
1 t  Salt
2 T  Jalapeno pepper; minced
1 T  Red bell pepper; minced
1 T  Green onion; minced,
-part only
-Belle Bestor
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 ea Egg
1 1/4 c  Milk
1 T  Louisiana Hot Sauce
Green
1 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 c  Flour
Mix together oil, egg, milk, and hot sauce. Stir in other ingredients
in order listed. Do not overmix. Spoon into corn stick pans. Bake
until golden, about 12 to 15 minutes. "Lee Bailey's New Orleans"
