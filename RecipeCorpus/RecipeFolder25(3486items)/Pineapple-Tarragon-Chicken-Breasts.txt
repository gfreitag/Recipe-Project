Title: Pineapple-Tarragon Chicken Breasts
Categories: Chicken, Poultry, Bbq/grill, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM---------------------MAKES: 6 SERVINGS--------------------------
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/2 t  Salt
pn Pepper
6 ea Split chicken breasts
6 oz Can frozen pineapple juice
-concentrate, thawed
1/4 c  Honey
1 t  Tarragon leaves
Prepare grill placing coals to one side; heat until coals are ash
white. Make aluminum foil drip pan; place oppisite coals. in
saucepan, stir together all sauce ingredients. Cook over medium heat,
stirring occasionally until heated through. Grill, turning
occasionally and basting, 25-35 minutes or until fork tender. To
serve, cook remaining sauce until heated through. Serve sauce over
chicken. Tip: Orange juice concentrate can be substituted for the
pineapple. Source: Land O Lakes Treasury of Country Recipes Carolyn
Shaw's Collection 11-1-94
