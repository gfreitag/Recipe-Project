Title: Fresh Tomato Scallop
Categories: Vegetable, Microwave, Casserole, Tomato, Clam/oyster
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Chopped toasted pecans
5 md Tomatoes
1 T  White wine
Worcestershire sauce
2 T  Sliced green onion
1 T  Margarine or butter
1/3 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
2 T  Grated Parmesan cheese
In a 2-cup glass measure micro-cook onion and margarine or butter,
uncovered, on 100% power (high) 1 minute or till crisp-tender.  Add
crumbs, cheese and pecans; toss to mix.  Set aside. Peel tomatoes, if
desired, cut into 1/2 inch slices.  In an 8-inch microwave-safe
quiche dish arrange tomatoes in spiral.  Sprinkle with Worcestershire
sauce. Cover loosley with waxed paper.  Micro-cook on 100% power
(high) for 2 to 3 minutes (low wattage ovens; 2-1/3 to 3-1/2 minutes)
or till almost heated through, giving plate a half-turn once.
Sprinkle crumb mixture over tomatoes. Cook, uncovered, on high 30 to
60 seconds (low wattage; 1 to 2 minutes) or till heated through.
Makes 4 to 6 side dish servings. From: 1990 Best-Recipes Yearbook,
Better Homes and Gardens Shared By: Pat Stockett
