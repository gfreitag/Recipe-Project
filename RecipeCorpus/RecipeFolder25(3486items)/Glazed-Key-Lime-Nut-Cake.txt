Title: Glazed Key Lime-Nut Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: THE NATIONAL CULINAR---------------------
-substitute)
1 c  Milk
8 t  Grated lime rind
2 c  Chopped pecans
6 T  Lime juice
3 c  Sifted flour
2 t  Baking powder
1 ea 1 /2 teaspoons salt
3 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Unsalted butter or margarine
4 ea Eggs (or 1 cup egg
Method: Sift flour, baking powder and salt.  Cream 2 cups sugar with
butter; beat in eggs.  Add flour mixture and milk alternately, beating
after each addition.  Stir in rind and nuts.  Turn into greased tube
pan. Bake at 350' till done, about 1 hour.  Remove cake from pan as
soon as baked.  In small pan, stir and cook remaining 1 1/2 cups
sugar and juice till sugar is dissolved.  A little at a time, pour
over cake till all glaze is absorbed.  Cool and slice.
