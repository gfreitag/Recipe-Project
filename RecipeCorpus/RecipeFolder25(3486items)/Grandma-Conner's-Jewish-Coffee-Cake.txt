Title: Grandma Conner's Jewish Coffee Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Jewish, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------------YIELD: 1 CAKE----------------------------
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
3 c  Flour
1/2 t  Salt
3 t  Baking powder
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter
3 ea Eggs
2 t  Almond flavoring
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Chopped nutmeats
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
Combine sugar, butter, eggs, almond flaovring, and sour cream. Beat
until Smooth. Sift dry ingredients together and combine with wet
mixture. Grease a 10" tube pan and line bottom with waxed paper. Pour
half of the batter in and sprinkle with combined filling ingredients.
Pour remaining batter over. Bake in a preheated 350 oven until
toothpick comes out clean. Let stand 15 minutes before removing cake
from pan and cooling on wire rack. Source: Grandma Conner Posted by
Carolyn Shaw 12-13-94
