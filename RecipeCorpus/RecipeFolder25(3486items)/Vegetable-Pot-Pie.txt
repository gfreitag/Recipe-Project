Title: Vegetable Pot Pie
Categories: Main dish, Pie/tart, Vegetarian, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1    PIE DOUGH;recipe(s)
1/4 c  Parmesan Cheese
1/2 ts Marjoram
1 ts Sage
1/2 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Potato;chunked
1/2 c  Carrot;chopped
1/4 c  Celery;chopped
1/2 c  Peas
1/4 c  Mushrooms;sliced
1/2 c  Onion;chopped
1/2 c  Tofu;chopped
2 c  Mushroom Gravy
Heat 1/4 cup olive oil in a large skillet. Saute' first 6
ingredients over medium heat till onion is translucent. Add tofu and
herbs; cook 2 minutes more. Add salt, pepper, Parmesan.
Place vegetable mixture in deep pie dish, add gravy till it
reaches the top of veggie mixture. Cover with rolled PIE DOUGH; prick
the crust.
Bake at 350 F for 35 - 45 minutes, until crust is golden brown.
