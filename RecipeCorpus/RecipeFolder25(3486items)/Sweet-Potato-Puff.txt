Title: Sweet Potato Puff
Categories: Vegetable, Casserole, Side dish, Sweetpotato
Yield: 6 servings
2 t  Almond extract
3    Egg whites
2 tb Almond slivers, toasted
-lightly
Non-stick cooking spray
3 1/2 lb Sweet potatoes, (4 large)
1/2 c  Orange marmalade or apricot
-preserved
1/4 c  Brown sugar, or to taste
1/2 c  Orange juice
Cook sweet potatoes until tender (15-20 minutes in a microwave, 1
hour in a preheat 450'f. oven, or boiled for 25 minutes). Cool and
peel. In a large bowl, mash. Add marmalade, sugar, orange juice and
almond extract; beat with mixer until smooth. In a separate bowl,
beat egg whites to stiff peaks. Fold into potato mixture. Coat a
6-cup casserole with non-stick spray. Add mixture, sprinkle with
almonds. Bake at 350'F. until puffy and set, about 40 minutes.

Per serving: 340 calories, 6 grams protein, 76 grams carbohydrates, 6
grams fiber, 2 grams fat (0.2 saturated), 71 milligrams sodium.
