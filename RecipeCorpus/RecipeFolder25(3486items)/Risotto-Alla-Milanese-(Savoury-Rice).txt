Title: Risotto Alla Milanese (Savoury Rice)
Categories: Italian, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
Salt and pepper
2 oz Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 lb Italian rice
3 T  Olive oil
2 pt Beef stock
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
pn Of saffron
Extra butter
2 oz Butter
1 ea Onion finely chopped
Heat the butter and oil in a saucepan, slowly fry the onion without
letting it brown.  Add the rice, and stir over heat for a few
minutes. Add the stock, then cover the pan and let the contents boil
for 10 minutes. Add a pinch of saffron, salt and peper, stir and boil
for a further 5-6 minutes. Remove from heat, stir in some butter and
the cheese, and serve. Makes 6 helpings.
