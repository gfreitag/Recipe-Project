Title: Derby Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1 t  Vanilla
1 T  Bourbon
1/2 c  Butter
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Flour
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Whipped cream (optional)
9 ea Inch pie shell
Beat together the butter, eggs, sugar and flour. Add the walnuts,
chocolate chips, vanilla and bourbon and stir. Pour into an unbaked
pie shell. Bake 40 minutes at 325-F. Cool, so the pie will set. Warm
slightly just before serving. Top with whipped cream. Source:
Favorite Recipes from Boder's On-the-River, Mequon, WI and The Woolen
Mill Inn, Cedarburg, WI. From: Sallie Krebs
