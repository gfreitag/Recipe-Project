Title: Orange Julius
Categories: Beverage, Cheese/eggs, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 t  Vanilla extract
1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  (heaping) ice
1 c  Orange juice
1 c  Water
2 ea Eggs
Combine all of the ingredients in a blender set on high speed for
exactly 1 minute. Makes: 2 cups From: "Top Secret Recipes" Cookbook
