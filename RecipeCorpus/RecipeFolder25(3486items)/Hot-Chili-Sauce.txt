Title: Hot Chili Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetable, Tex/mexican, Chilies
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Dry Mustard
1 ea Clove Garlic
1/4 c  Olive Oil
2 c  Water
1 x  Chilies; *
1/4 c  Red Wine Vinegar
*  You should use 6 to 8 dried Cascabel chilies in this recipe. If
they can't be found, then use a 1/2 of a medium Ancho Chile. But the
result will not be as hot.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Heat water to boiling; stir in chilies. Boil
uncoverd 5 minutes; drain. Remove stems. Place chilies, vinegar,
mustard and garlic in a blender container; cover and blend until the
chilies are finely chopped. Gradually pour in oil, blending until
smooth. Makes about 1/2 cup of sauce. From: Carl Berger (Cooking Echo)
