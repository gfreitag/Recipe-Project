Title: Squash or Pumpkin Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pumpkin, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Cinnamon
2 ea Eggs, beaten
2 T  Flour
2 c  Sweet milk
Pumpkin Pie
1 c  Sifted flour
l  Salt
1 c  Brown sugar
Bake 3/4 hour, placing pie in very hot oven for 15 minutes.  Then
reduce heat. Squash Pie: Same as above using ginger instead of
cinnamon. From: A Rhode Island Rule Book Shared By: Pat Stockett
