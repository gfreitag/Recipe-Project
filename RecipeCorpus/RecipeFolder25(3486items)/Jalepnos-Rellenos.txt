Title: Jalepnos Rellenos
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Tex/mexican, Side dish, Chilies
Yield: 12 servings
-cheese (2 ounces)
1/4 c  Sliced green onion
12 ea Pimiento strips
12 ea Pickled jalapeno peppers
1 ea 3 ounce package cream
-cheese, softened
1/2 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
Rinse and drain jalapeno peppers.  Slit lengthwise on one side, remove
seeds and veins, leaving stem attached.  Beat cream cheese till
fluffy. Beat in cheddar cheese and green onion.  Stuff each pepper
with part of the cheese mixture.  Arrange on heatproof serving plate
or baking sheet, bake in 350 F. oven about 10 minutes or till cheese
melts.  Top each pepper with a pimiento strip.  Makes 12 From: Better
Homes and Gardens, Mexican Cookbook Shared By: Pat Stockett
