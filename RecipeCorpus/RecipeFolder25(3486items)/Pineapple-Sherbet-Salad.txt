Title: Pineapple Sherbet Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit, Sthrn/livng, Ice cream
Yield: 6 servings
1    Banana
1/4 c  Almonds, slivered; toasted
Salad greens
8 3/4 oz Pineapple tidbits
Water
3 oz Gelatin, raspberry-flavored
1/2 pt Sherbet, raspberry
Drain syrup from pineapple; add enough water to make 1 cup liquid.
Heat to boiling; add gelatin and stir until dissolves.  Stir in
sherbet and cool. When mixture begins to thicken, add peeled diced
banana, almonds, and pineapple.  Chill until firm.  Serve on salad
greens.
