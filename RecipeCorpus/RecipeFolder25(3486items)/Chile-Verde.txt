Title: Chile Verde
Categories: Main dish, Tex/mexican, Pork, Vegetable, Chilies
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Green Chiles;chopped mild
2 tb Cilantro;chopped fresh
Salt;
Pepper;
1    SALSA FRESCA;recipe
2 lb Pork;lean cubed
1/2 c  Water
2    Garlic;cloves
1    Onion;chopped;
1 c  Tomatillos;canned whole
1 ts Coriander;ground seeds of
Cook pork and garlic in 1/2 cup water until all the water is
absorbed.
Discard garlic; let the pork brown in its fat for a few minutes.
Add the onion and saute till translucent.
Add the tomatillos and the chiles, cover and simmer for 30-40
minutes, until pork is tender.
Just before serving, add the chopped cilantro, salt and pepper to
taste, stir well.   Serve with SALSA FRESCA.
(NOTE: If tomatillos are not available, substitute canned whole
tomatoes).
