Title: Cranberry Glazed Yams
Categories: Side dish, Fruit, Vegetable, Sweetpotato
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 t  Grated orange peel
1 T  Butter or margarine
8 md Yams, cooked and halved, or
-1 (16 oz) can whole packed
-yams, drained
1 c  Whole-berry cranberry sauce
Arrange yams in a shallow, buttered baking dish.  Combine cranberry
sauce, water, brown sugar, and orange peel in a saucepan; mix well,
and bring to a boil.  Cook gently 5 minutes.  Add butter and pour
mixture over potatoes. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, basting
frequently.  Yield: 4 to 6 servings. From: Sweet Potato Recipes with
that Old Fashioned Flavor Shared By: Pat Stockett
