Title: Mandalay Salad
Categories: Salad, Side dish, Asian, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Rice Wine Vinegar
3 tb Sesame Oil;dark
1 lb Spinach;cleaned
1/4 c  Sesame Seeds;toasted
Clean, dry and tear spinach into bite-sized pieces. Arrange in a
salad bowl and sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds.
Mix oil and vinegar and pour over spinach. Toss and serve.
