Title: Fruit Cookie Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pizza, Fruit, Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
1/8 t  Salt
1 T  Domino Granulated Sugar
1 1/2 ea To 2 cups prepared vanilla
-pudding
1 1/2 c  Each, sliced kiwi, sliced
-strawberries, mandarin
-orange
Sections and raspberries
3/4 c  Butter or margarine,
-softened
1/4 c  Firmly packed Domino Light
-Brown Sugar
1/2 c  Domino Granulated Sugar
1 ea Egg
1 t  Vanilla
2 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 t  Baking powder
In a medium bowl, beat together butter and sugars until light and
creamy. Add egg and vanilla; beat well.  Combine flour, baking powder
and salt. Gradually blend into butter mixture until just moistened.
Press dough evenly into a 12-inch pizza pan.  Sprinkle with 1 Tbsp.
sugar. Bake in 375 oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until lightly
browned. Remove and cool. Spread with pudding, leaving 1 inch all
around.  Arrange fruit on pudding. Makes 8 servings. From: Domino Ad
