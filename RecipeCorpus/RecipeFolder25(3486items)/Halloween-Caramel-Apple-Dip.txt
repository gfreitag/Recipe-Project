Title: Halloween Caramel Apple Dip
Categories: Fruit, Holiday, Candy, Apples
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Caramel Flavoring
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
1/2 c  Brown sugar
Blend all ingredients with mixer.  Serve with apple slices.  Tastes
like caramel apples, but doesn't stick to the roof of you mouth or
your teeth. Submitted By DOROTHY THOMPSON   On   10-23-94
