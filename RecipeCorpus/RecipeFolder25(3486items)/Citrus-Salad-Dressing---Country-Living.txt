Title: Citrus Salad Dressing - Country Living
Categories: Fruit, Holiday, Salad, Dressing
Yield: 1 cup
1 t  Dry mustard
1/4 t  Salt
Ribbon (opt.)
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1/3 c  Orange juice
1/3 c  Olive oil
2 T  Honey
1. In 2-C measuring cup or small howl, with wire whisk, combine lemon
juice, orange juice, oil, honey, mustard, and salt until well mixed.

2. Pour salad dressing through a funnel into a bottle. Cover, label,
and store in the refrigerator. For a gift, tie bottle with a ribbon
and bow, if desired.
