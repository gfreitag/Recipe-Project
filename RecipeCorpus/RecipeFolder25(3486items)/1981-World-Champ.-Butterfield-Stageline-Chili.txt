Categories: Chili, Beef, Pork, Main dish
Yield: 25 servings
1 tb Cumin
1 ts Salt

1 tb Dry mustard
1 oz Tequila
1 cn Beer
2 x  (3oz) chili powder
2 x  Beef bouillon cubes
From: S. Van Bibber XWCG89a
4 x  Medium onions, minced
10 lb Lean beef brisket, finely ch
1/4 c  Oil
1 1/2 x  Cloves garlic, minced
2 lb Ground pork
1 cn (7oz) whole green chiles, mi
1 cn (15oz) tomato sauce
1 lb Whole tomatoes, finely chopp
Brown onions and beef in oil. Stir in garlic, pork, chiles, cumin,
oregano, salt, dry mustard, tomato sauce, tomatoes, tequila, beer,
chili powder and bouillon cubes. Bring to boil, then reduce heat and
simmer 2-3 hours. Stir occasionally. Do not stir the last 30 min
before serving.
