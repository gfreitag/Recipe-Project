Title: Swiss Cheese Salad
Categories: Salad, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Cumin;ground
1/2 c  Sour Cream
Lettuce;
1/2 lb Swiss Cheese;cubed
6    Egg;hard - boiled;
1 1/2 ts Mustard
1 ts Horseradish
1/2 ts Salt
Chop eggs. Mix cheese and eggs in a bowl. Combine sour cream,
mustard, horseradish, salt, pepper, and cumin. Fold in cheese and egg
mixture.
Refrigerate 1 hour. Serve on a bed of lettuce.
