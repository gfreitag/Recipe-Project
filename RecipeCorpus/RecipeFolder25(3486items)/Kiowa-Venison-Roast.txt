Title: Kiowa Venison Roast
Categories: Game, Main dish, Native amer
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 t  Salt
1 T  Chopped onion
Slab of venison, about 2
-inches thick
4 t  Bacon drippings
1/4 t  Pepper
1 T  Chopped celery
Lay venison on board and pound flour into it. Melt drippings in a
large frying pan; brown roast in it.  Add seasonings and one half of
the water. Cover and let simmer for 55 minutes.  Pour in rest of
water and simmer until meat is done. From: Recipes and Remembrances
