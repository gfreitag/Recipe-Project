Title: Pistachio Raita (Piston-Walla Raita)
Categories: Indian, Nuts/grains, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
36    Unsalted pistachios
-- shelled and chopped
1 pn Ground saffron (opt'l.)
3 1/4 c  Plain yogurt
4 tb Rose water
2 tb Clear honey
Stir the yogurt, rose water, honey and pistachios together and chill
the mixture.  Sprinkle with saffron, if desired, and serve.

Merchant writes: "This is more an accompaniment to spicy Indian
dishes than a salad, but it is a good one as it cools the palate."

From _Ismail Merchant's Indian Cuisine_ by Ismail Merchant.  New York:
Simon & Schuster, Inc., 1986.  ISBN 0-671-68477-9.  Pg. 175.  Posted
by Cathy Harned.
