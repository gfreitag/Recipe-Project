Title: Zucchini Me Yaurti
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Greek
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Dill Weed;chopped fresh
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
2/3 c  Yogurt;plain
3 tb Olive Oil
2 lb Zucchini;sliced
1/2 c  Onion;chopped
1 tb Lemon Juice
Saute onions and zucchini in olive oil over medium heat 5 minutes.
Cover and cook 2 - 3 more minutes, or until tender.
Combine last 5 ingredients, pour over squash; heat. Serve
immediately.
