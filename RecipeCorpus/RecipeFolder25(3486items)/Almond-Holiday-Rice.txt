Title: Almond Holiday Rice
Categories: Side dish, Holiday, Rice, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
2 cn Mushrooms, drained and
-chopped (2.5 oz)
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
1/2 c  Whipping cream
Cayenne pepper
1 c  Mahatma (r) Rice
1 md Onion, chopped
1    Garlic clove, minced
4 t  Bbutter, divided
1/2 c  Chopped almonds, toasted
1 cn Chopped pimientos (1 oz)
Preheat oven to 350'F.

Prepare rice according to package direction. Set aside. In a medium
skillet saute onion and garlic in butter. Add almonds, pimientos,
mushrooms, salt and pepper. Fold in cooked rice.

Fold mixture into an oiled 2-quart baking dish. Pour whipping cream
over rice mixture. Bake at 350'F. for about 15 minutes, or until
thoroughly heated. Sprinkle with cayenne pepper.
