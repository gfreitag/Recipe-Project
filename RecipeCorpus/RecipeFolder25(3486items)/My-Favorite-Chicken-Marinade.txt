Title: My Favorite Chicken Marinade
Categories: Bbq/grill, Marinade, Chicken, Poultry
Yield: 12 servings
3 tb Cavender's Greek Seasoning
1 c  Oil
6    Chicken whole breasts
1 c  Cooking sherry
3 tb A-1 steak sauce
3 tb Worchestshire sauce
Mix together.  marinate chicken overnight.  Cook anyway, best on the
grill. Change the wine and meat to taste.
