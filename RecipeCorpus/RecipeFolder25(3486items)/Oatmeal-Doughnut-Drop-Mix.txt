Title: Oatmeal Doughnut Drop Mix
Categories: Nuts/grains, Mixes, Breakfast, Doughnut
Yield: 1 servings
-DOUGHNUTS:
1 pk Mix
1 c  Milk
2 tb Butter, melted
1    Egg
8 c  Oil, heated to 360
-Cinnamon sugar
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Salt
3/4 c  Flour
1 tb Dry yeast
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Quick rolled oats
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Oatmeal Doughnut Drops: Combine mix with milk, butter and egg. Stir
until well blended. Refrigerate 2 hours or overnight. Drop dough by
tablespoonfuls into hot oil and cook until browned on all sides.
