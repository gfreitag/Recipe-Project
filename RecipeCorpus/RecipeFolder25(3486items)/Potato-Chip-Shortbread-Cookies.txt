Title: Potato Chip Shortbread Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Snack, Dessert
Yield: 84 servings
1 c  Pecans, chopped
2 c  Potato chips, crushed
2 c  Confectioners' sugar or more
2 c  Margarine, softened
1 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla extract
3 c  Flour
Cream margarine, sugar and vanilla in mixer bowl until light and
fluffy. Add flour; mix well.  Stir in pecans and porato chips.  Drop
by teaspoonfuls onto nonstick cookie sheet.  Bake at 350Ã¸ for 10 to
15 minutes or until golden.  Sprinkle with confectioners' sugar.
Cool on wire rack.

Prep Time:  10 minutes plus baking
