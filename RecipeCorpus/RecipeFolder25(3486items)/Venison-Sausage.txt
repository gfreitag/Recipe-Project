Title: Venison Sausage
Categories: Game, Sausage, Homemade, Pork
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Coriander
1 1/2 t  Mustard Seed
6 ea Cloves Pressed garlic
2 T  Black pepper
1 c  Cold water
4 lb Coarse Ground Venison
1 lb Fine ground bacon
1 T  Salt
1 T  Sage
1 t  Allspice
2 T  Sugar
Combine all ingredients, mix thoroughly and stuff into hog casings. To
cook, boil, bake or fry. SOURCE: The Sausage Making Cookbook, by Jerry
Predika Collection of Clarence Fontish
