Title: Linda's Microwave Candy
Categories: Candy, Holiday, Microwave, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Salted peanuts
10 1/2 oz Mini marshmallows
12 oz Chocolate chips
6 oz Butterscotch chips
1 c  Peanut butter
In the microwave, melt the chips and peanut butter on high for 3
minutes (stir after 1 1/2 minutes).  Stir in other ingredients and
pour into a buttered 13x9" pan. Refrigerate for 1 hour, then cut into
squares and put back in refrigerator. From: Linda Rehberg Posted by:
Debbie Carlson
