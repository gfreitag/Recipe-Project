Title: Country Oven-Fried Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM--------------------MAKES: 4-6 SERVINGS-------------------------
1/4 t  Rosemary leaves, crushed
1/4 t  Paprika
2 1/2-3 1/2 lb chicken,
-cut into eight pieces.
1/3 c  Butter or margarine, melted
1/3 c  Flour
3/4 t  Salt
1/2 t  Pepper
1/4 t  Thyme leaves
Heat oven to 375~. In roasting pan, melt butter in oven. Combine
remaining ingredients except chicken. Dip chicken in melted butter,
then coat with flour mixture. In same pan, place chicken, skin side
down. Bake 25-30 minutes, turn chicken over. continue baking 30-35
minutes until fork tender. Source: Land O Lakes Treasury of Country
Recipes Carolyn Shaw's Collection 11-1-94
