Title: Coca-Mint Kisses
Categories: Dessert, Chocolate
Yield: 24 kisses
1 t  Vanilla extract
1/2 t  Peppermint extract
2 lg Egg whites, room temperature
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Unsweetened cocoa powder
Preheat oven to 250'F. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper. In
small bowl, using electric mixer on high speed, beat egg whites until
foamy. One tablespoon at a time. Beat in six tablespoons sugar; each
spoonful of sugar should be completely dissolved and incorporated
before next addition. Continue beating until soft peaks form when
beaters are lifted. In cup, stir cocoa powder with remaining two
tablespoons sugar. One tablespoon at a time, beat mixture into
batter. Add vanilla and peppermint extracts and beat until shiny and
stiff peaks form when beaters are lifted. Drop batter by heaping
teaspoons onto prepared baking sheets and bake for 45 minutes or
until kisses are firm on outside. Carefully transfer kisses to rack
to cool completely; store in airtight container.

Each kiss contains: 18 calories, 0 fat, 0 cholesterol, 5 mg sodium.
