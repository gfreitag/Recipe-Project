Title: The "I Can't Believe It's This Easy To Make" Fudge
Categories: Candy, Chocolate
Yield: 32 servings
16 oz Semi sweet chocolate squares
2/3 c  Corn syrup
Microwave together on high for 2-3 minutes until melted.  Stir.  Cool
for 5 minutes.  Pour into a 8x5 inch lightly greased loaf pan lined
with waxed paper.  Refrigerate 3 hours or overnight to set.  Cut into
1 inch squares. Makes 32 squares. Can add chopped nuts if desired.
Origin: Kraft Wha't Cooking Magazine, 10th issue. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Dec/94.
