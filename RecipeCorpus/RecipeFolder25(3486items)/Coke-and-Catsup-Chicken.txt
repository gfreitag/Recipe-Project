Title: Coke and Catsup Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Poultry, Salsa/sauce, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Catsup
4 lb Frying chicken, cut up
1 c  Cola beverage
Mix cola and catsup in bowl.  Pour over chicken pieces in buttered
baking dish.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper and bake in 350F oven for
about 1 hour, uncovered, or until chicken is tender and has a nice
glaze. Source: No-More-Than-4 Ingredient Recipes by Joy Gallagher
Douglas shared by Elizabeth Rodier
