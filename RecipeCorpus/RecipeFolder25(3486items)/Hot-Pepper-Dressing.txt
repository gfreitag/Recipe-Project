Title: Hot Pepper Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing
Yield: 2 servings
1 t  Hot sauce
1 t  Garlic powder
1/2 t  Black pepper
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
2 T  Olive oil
1 t  Hot pepper flakes
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl or cruet. Mix well. Yield: 1
cup. Source: More Cajun Cooking, Modern Publishing, New York, NY
