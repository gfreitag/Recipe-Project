Title: Fruity Banana Loaf
Categories: Bread, Fruit, Holiday
Yield: 2 loaves
3    Ripe bananas (1 cup)mashed
1/4 c  Milk
1 c  Candied cherries
1/4 c  Candied pineapple
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Raisins
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
2    Eggs
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
In a bowl, cream sugar and shortening until blended.  Add eggs, beat
well. Combine flour, soda and salt.  Combine bananas and milk.  Add
flour, alternately with banana mix, to creamed mixture.  Stir in
fruit and nuts. Pour into 2 greased pans (7 1/2 X 3 1/2).  Bake in
350 degree oven for 55/60 minutes.  Cool.  Remove from pans and cool.
Wrap and store overnight or freeze.
