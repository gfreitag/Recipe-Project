Title: Shrimp Arnaud
Categories: Appetizer, French, Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Chili Sauce
2 ts Mustard;hot
1    Garlic;clove;medium
1/4 ts Paprika
4 lb Shrimp;cooked
1/4 c  Peanut Oil
1/4 c  Vinegar;white
1 tb Chives;chopped
Combine oil, white vinegar, chives, chili sauce, hot mustard,
garlic and salt. Add cooled shrimp; Toss well.
Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours, tossing occasionally.
To serve, drain, sprinkle lightly with paprika.
