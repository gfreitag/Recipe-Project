Title: Chicken Mushroom Quiche
Categories: Pie/tart, Cheese/eggs, Chicken
Yield: 2 servings
3 ea Eggs
1 cn Evaporated milk
Cayenne pepper
Nutmeg
1 c  Chopped cooked chicken
1 ea Unbaked pie shell
1 cn Mushrooms (4 Oz.)
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
1 1/2 c  Shredded Swiss cheese
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Add cayenne pepper to taste. Bake the pie
shell for 5minutes. Put half of the cheese into the bottom. Sprinkle
in the chicken, mushrooms, onions and the rest of the cheese.
Slightly beat the eggs, then add milk and pepper. Pour the mixture
over the filling. Sprinkle the top with nutmeg. Bake 10 minutes.
Lower oven temperature to 325 degrees. Bake 20 minutes more.
