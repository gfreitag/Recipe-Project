Title: Chili - Mexican
Categories: Tex/mexican, Chili, Ground beef, Meat, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
Garlic to taste
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
12 oz Can stewed tomatoes
2 lb Ground beef
1 lb Chorizo, sliced
1 c  Green chiles, diced
1    Diced onion
MMMMM-------------------PINTO BEANS IF NEEDED------------------------
3 c  Cooked
Brown beef and chorizo. Drain fat and add all ingredients to large
pot. Simmer 2 or 3 hours. Kay Doner *P JWPC45A 3/23/92 7:47pm
