Title: Housekeeping Ideas 3
Categories: Information
Household sponges stay fresh when soaked regularly in cold
salt water.
Yield: 1 info  :
:
:
If you have age spots on linen which has been stored away
for a long time, sponge the spots with full strength
peroxide, then rinse in cold water and wash as usual. More
than one application of the peroxide may be necessary for
very stubborn spots.
:
:
:
:
:
:
Recycle old powder puffs. Wash them in soapy water, rinse
well and dry thoroughly; use for polishing silverware,
copper and brass.
:
:
:
:
Want to change those hard-to-remove decals in the bathtub?
Peel them off, then to the remaining hardened adhesive,
apply a generous coating of petroleum jelly. Allow to soften
for a while and all the unsightly residue will be easy to
remove.
:
:
:
:
:
:
Dampen your broom bristles or the inside of your dustpan
before sweeping to keep the dust from flying when you're
house cleaning.
:
:
:
:
Apply a non-flammable wax or polish to the inside of
ashtrays. Ashes won't cling, odors won't linger and paper
towels will wipe them clean in a jiffy.
:
:
:
:
To clean those small hard-to-get-to crevices on carved
furniture, dip a small cotton swab into furniture polish and
run it inside the difficult places.
:
:
:
:
Cut your utility bill by opening the dishwasher door and
letting the dishes air dry when the machine reaches the
drying cycle.
:
:
:
:
Fill all cracks with steel wool to keep mice out.
:
:
Ball point pen ink can be easily removed from clothes if you
spray with hair spray then wash as usual.
:
:
:
Blood stains on clothes wash out after a good soaking in
cold water. For more stubborn blood stains, add a cup of
vinegar to the water.
:
:
:
:
To get rid of iron rust stains, boil garments in a solution
of four teaspoons cream of tartar to one pint of water or
rub with diluted peroxide or lemon juice.
:
:
:
:
You won't slip on them if you sew rubber fruit jar rings
under each corner and under the center of any scatter rugs
in your home.
:
:
:
:
Give new life to feather pillows and dusty drapes; put them
into the clothes dryer on warm or air dry setting for 10-15
minutes.
