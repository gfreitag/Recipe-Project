Title: Banana Pineapple Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1    Ripe banana
2 sl Canned pineapple
Salad greens
Place a ring of pineapple around each of a peeled banana.  Garnish
with crisp greens and berries, if desired.  Serve with mayonnaise or
cream dressing. Randy Rigg
