Title: Cajun Corn & Shrimp Soup Mix with Recipe
Categories: Cajun/creol, Mixes, Soup/stew, Vegetable, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
-SOUP:
1/2 c  Butter
1 md Onion, chopped
1/4 c  Flour
1 tb Soup mix
3 c  Boiling water
3 c  Freshly scraped or frozen
-corn kernals
3/4 lb Shrimp, cleaned
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 tb Paprika
2 3/4 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Black pepper
1 1/2 ts Onion powder
1 1/2 ts Oregano leaves
1 1/2 ts Thyme leaves
5    Vegetable bouillon cubes,
-crumbled
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Soup: Saute onion in butter 3 minutes. Add flour and cook, stirring
constantly. Stir in the soup mix and gradually add the boiling water,
whisking until the mixture is thick. Add the corn and shrimp, simmer 5
minutes, or until shrimp are pink. Stir in heavy cre
