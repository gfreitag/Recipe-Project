Title: Peter Island Banana Pancakes
Categories: Breakfast, Pancakes
Yield: 6 grandsons
3 c  Flour;
2 tb Baking powder;
2 ts Butter; melted
2    Whole bananas; ripe chopped
4    Whole eggs;
2 c  Milk;
1 c  Sugar;
1 tb Vanilla extract;
In a large bowl, combine eggs, milk and sugar.  Mix well.  Add vinilla
extract, flour, baking powder, melted butter and bananas.  Mix well.
Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake on hot grill and pan. When done.
Serve hot with fruit garnish and maple syrup flavored with a
tablespoon of Caribbean Rum. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune Food
Section, Nov. 3, 1994 & Chef Conrad "Spinks" Henley. Brought to you
and your via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
