Title: New York Pushcart Hot Dog Onion Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Hot dogs
Yield: 8 servings
1/8 t  Chili powder
ds Hot pepper sauce
ds Salt
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 md Onions, cut into 1/4" slices
1/4 c  Ketchup
pn Ground cinnamon
From MR. FOOD COOKS REAL AMERICAN

In a medium-sized skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat;
add the onion slices and saute for about 7 minutes, until golden and
limp. Mix in the ketchup, then add the cinnamon, chili powder, hot
pepper sauce and salt. Pour in the water; stir. Bring to a boil,
reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for about 10 minutes. Serve over
hot dogs. Serves 6-8. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   10-23-94
