Title: Crockpot Polish Sausage and Cabbage
Categories: Main dish, Crockpot, Sausage, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1    Onion; large; cut 1" slices
1 1/2 lb Polish sausage; cut 1" piec
14 oz Broth; chicken
1/2    Cabbage head; course sliced
1    Potato; small; peeled/diced
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Caraway seed
~-----LIZ JONES   JONES------------------------- Place sliced cabbage
in crockpot. Toss with diced potato, salt and caraway seed. Add
sliced onion and Polish sausage. Pour chicken broth over all; stir
lightly. Cover and cook on Low setting for 8 to 10 hours (on HIGH
setting for 2 to 4 hours). This dish is delicious served with
mustard, horseradish and boiled new potatoes. Makes 4 servings (about
3 1/2 quarts). Formatted from Rival Crock-Pot Cooking, March 1992...
