Title: Veal Parmesan
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Italian, Veal, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Oregano;dried
1 c  White Wine
3/4 c  Olive Oil
;OPTIONAL:;
1 lb Mozzarella Cheese
1 1/2 c  Tomato Sauce
lb Veal
1/2 c  Bread Crumbs
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2    Egg;beaten;
1/2 c  Parmesan Cheese
Pound veal till thin. Pat dry, cut into pieces. Combine bread
crumbs, Parmesan, spices, and salt. Set aside.   Beat eggs in a
shallow dish. Dip veal into eggs, then crumbs, coating on both sides.
Heat oil in skillet. Saute veal till golden brown, about 3 minutes
per side. Remove to a warm platter.
Drain fat, stir in wine, scraping up brown residue till boiling.
Pour over veal.
OPTIONAL:
Cover veal with tomato sauce. Top with mozzarella slices. Broil till
cheese melts.
