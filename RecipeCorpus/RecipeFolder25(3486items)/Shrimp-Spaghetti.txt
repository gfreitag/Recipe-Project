Title: Shrimp Spaghetti
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Sthrn/livng, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 6 servings
16 oz Tomato sauce
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Shrimp, fresh; or frozen,
-cooked
Cheese, Parmesan; grated
1/2 lb Spaghetti
3 tb Butter (or marg.)
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1    Garlic clove; minced
2/3 c  Mushrooms, sliced
1 c  Water
Cook spaghetti according to package directions.  Melt butter and saute
onion, celery, garlic, and mushrooms until tender.  Add water, tomato
sauce, seasonings, and shrimp.  Simmer for 25 minutes.  Pour over
cooked spaghetti and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
