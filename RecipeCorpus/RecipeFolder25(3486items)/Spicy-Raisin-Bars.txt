Title: Spicy Raisin Bars
Categories: Herbs/spice, Fruit, Vegetable, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Cloves
1 c  Shredded unpeeled zucchini
1/2 c  Raisins
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
%%%%% FROSTING  %%%%%
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
1 1/2 c  Sifted icing sugar
1/4 c  Shortening (crisco)
1/4 t  Cinnamon
1-2 tbsp milk
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
%%%%% BAR  %%%%%
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
1/4 c  Shortening (crisco)
2/3 c  Lightly packed brown sugar
1 ea Egg
1 t  Vanilla
1 c  All purpose flour
1 t  Baking soda
1/2 t  Cinnamon
BAR:  Cream shortening, brown sugar, egg and vanilla in large bowl on
medium speed of electric mixer until light and creamy.  Add flour,
baking soda and spices.  Mix well. Stir in zucchini and raisins.
Spread evenly in greased 9 inch square cake pan.  Bake at 350F for
25-30 minutes, or until cake tester inserted in centre comes out
clean.  Cool completely. FROSTING: Beat all ingredients together
adding enough milk to make a smooth spreading consistency.  Spread
evenly over bars. Origin: Baking Festival 1994 Recipes, by Robin Hood
and Baker's. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
