Title: Braised Red Cabbage
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, European
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ts Sugar
2 tb Butter
2 tb Red Wine
1/2    Red Cabbage;shredded;head
2    Pippin Apples;peeled, cored;
1/2    Onion;chopped;
1 1/2 tb Wine Vinegar
1 1/2 tb Water
Place the cabbage in a heavy enameled pot with the water, vinegar,
onion, salt, and pepper.
Cover pot and cook at low heat for 1 hour. Add the sugar and
apples. Cook another hour and 15 minutes.
Just before serving, add the butter and red wine. Excellent with
Danish Pork Roast.
