Title: Grandmas Chicken N Dumplings
Categories: Bread, Pasta, Stove top, Chicken
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
DUMPLINGS
2 ea Eggs, beaten
1/2 c  Broth from chicken
CHICKEN
1 ea Stewing chicken, in pieces
1/2 ea Onion, chopped
2 ea Carrots, chopped
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
2 c  Flour
2 T  Baking powder
1 ea Celery rib, chopped
Salt/pepper to taste
Stew the chicken with chopped onion, carrots, celery and salt/pepper
until chicken is done and tender. Take 1/2 cup of the broth for
making dumplings. In a bowl, mix 2 beaten eggs, the broth, flour, and
baking powder. roll out the dumplings and cut into pieces, not too
thin nor fat. Drop into the simmering stew and cook, covered tightly,
for about 15 minutes.
