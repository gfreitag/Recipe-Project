Title: Hot & Spicy Chex (R) Party Mix - Microwave Version
Categories: Snack, Nuts/grains, Cheese/eggs, Mixes, Microwave
Yield: 10 cups
8 c  Chex cereal
1 c  Planters Mixed nuts or
-Peanuts
1 c  Pretzels
1 c  Bite-size cheese crackers
1/4 c  Margarine or butter, melted
2 1/2 t  Lea & Perrins Worcestershire
-Sauce
2 t  TABASCO Pepper Sauce
1 1/4 t  Lawry's Seaoned Salt
1. Combine margarine, Worcestershire sauce, pepper sauce and seasoned
salt; mix well. Pour cereals, nuts, pretzels and cheese crackers into
large GLAD-LOCK Zipper Bag.

2. Pour margarine mixture over cereal mixture inside GLAD-LOCK Zipper
Bag. Seal top of bag securely. Shake bag until all pieces are evenly
coated.

3. Pour contents of bag into large microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on
HIGH 5-6 minutes, stirring thoroughly every 2 minutes. While
stirring, make sure to scrape sides and bottom of bowl. Spread on
absorbent paper to cool. Store in airtight container.
