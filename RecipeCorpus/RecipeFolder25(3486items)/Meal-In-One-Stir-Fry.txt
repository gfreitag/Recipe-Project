Title: Meal-In-One Stir-Fry
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Rice, Stir fry, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
8 1/2 oz Peas, green; drained
3 c  Rice; cooked
2 oz Pimentos, diced; opt.
1 ts Pepper, ground black
1 sm Onion; chopped
2 tb Oil, vegetable
10 oz Chicken, chunk; drained and
-flaked
4 oz Mushrooms, sliced; drained
Cook onion in large skillet until transparent.  Add chicken,
mushrooms, peas, rice, pimentos and pepper; stir well and heat
thoroughly.

Each serving provides 250 calories, 18 g. protein, 8 g. fat, 25 g.
carbohydrate, 1 g. dietary fiber, 42 mg. cholesterol and 119 mg
sodium.

SOURCE: Stretching your Budget with Rice, USA Rice Council pamphlet.
