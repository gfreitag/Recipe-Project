Title: Chinese Vermicelli
Categories: Chinese, Pasta, Vegetable
Yield: 8 cups
1 1/8 lb Fresh vermicelli
1/2 c  Seasame seeds, toasted
1 c  Green onions, thinly sliced
- on the diagonal, for
- garnish
1/4 c  Seasame oil
1/4 c  Black souy sauce
2 T  Sugar
2 T  Hot chili oil
2 T  Black vinegar or balsamic
In a large bowl, whisk together sesame oil, soy sauce, sugar,
chili oil, and vinegar.  Cook vermicelli in boiling water until
barely tender (probably less than a minute).  Drain, rinse with cold
water, and drain again.  With you hands, immediately toss the pasta
with the dressing, making sure pasta is completeely and evenly
coated.  Add seeds and continue folding dressing throught the pasta,
literally "whiping the bowl with the vermicelli.  Remove to a shallow
platter, top with green onions, and serve at room temperature.
