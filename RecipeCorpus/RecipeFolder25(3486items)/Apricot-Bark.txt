Title: Apricot Bark
Categories: Chocolate, Holiday, Fruit, Candy
Yield: 1 servings
6 ea Squares white chocolate
1 c  Dried apricots, chopped
6 ea Squares bittersweet
-chocolate
Chope both types of chocolate and place in separate bowls.  Partially
melt chocolates over hot water. (or microwave) Remove from heat and
continue stirring until completely melted.  Stir 1/2 the apricots
enot each chocolate.  Drop alternate spoonfuls onto waxed paper lined
baking sheet. Using the end of a knife, draw the white and dark
chocolate together to give a marbled effect.  Chill until firm.
Break into pieces. Origin: Kraft What's Cooking magazine, 6th issue
