Title: Peanut Butter Dessert
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Milk
8 oz Whipped topping
3 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 c  Confectioner's sugar
1/4 c  Peanut butter
Cream the cream cheese.  Add sugar, peanut butter and milk.  Fold in
Cool Whip.  Pour into baked pie shell or graham crust. From:
Lancaster Farming Shared By: Pat Stockett
