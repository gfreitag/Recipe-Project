Title: Dressed Crab
Categories: Main dish, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Dry mustard
3 dr Tabasco sauce
-Salt and pepper to taste
2 tb Melted butter
2 tb Parsley flakes
-Paprika to taste
2 tb Butter
1 lb Crabmeat
1 c  Fresh bread crumbs
1/2 c  Heavy whipping cream,
-lightly whipped
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Finely chopped scallion
Preheat oven to 375. Melt 2 tb butter in heavy skillet. Add crabmeat;
toss well to coat. Add bread crumbs, cream, lemon juice, scallion,
Worcestershire sauce, sry mustard, Tabasco sauce, salt, and pepper to
crab. Mix lightly but thoroughly. divide among 4 lightly greased
ramekins. Drizzle butter over crab mixture; sprinkle with parsley and
paprika. Place on baking sheets and bake 15 minutes. or until lightly
browned.
