Title: Salisbury Steak
Categories: Beef, Ground beef, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 lb Round steak, ground
2 tb Onion juice
Combine meat, onion juice, salt, and pepper.  Mix thoroughly. Form in
small flat cakes.  Broil until well browned.  Serve at once with the
following sauce:

3  Tbsp bread crumbs, 1/8  tsp dry mustard, 1  Tbsp vinegar 1/3 cup
sweet cream, 3  Tbsp grated horseradish, Salt and pepper

Combine cream, crumbs, horseradish, and mustard.  Season to taste.
Heat over hot water.  Add vinegar just before serving. 4 servings.

Myrtle Magnusen, Loma, NE.
