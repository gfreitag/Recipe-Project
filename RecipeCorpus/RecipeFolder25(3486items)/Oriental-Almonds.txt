Title: Oriental Almonds
Categories: Appetizer, Nuts/grains, Holiday
Yield: 2 cups
1/8 ts Chili powder (or to taste)
ds Hot sauce
2 c  Blanched whole almonds
1 1/2 tb Butter or margarine
1 1/2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cinnamon
Melt butter in two quart baking dish in 300 degree oven.  Stir in
spice ingredients, mix well, add almonds, stir until completely
coated. Bake stirring occasionally for 15 mins or until almonds are
crisp.
