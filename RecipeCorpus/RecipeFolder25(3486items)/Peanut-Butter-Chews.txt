Title: Peanut Butter Chews
Categories: Candy, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
Bring to a rolling boil.
-Remove
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Corn syrup
MMMMM---------------------FROM HEAT AND ADD--------------------------
3 c  Corn flakes
2 T  Butter
1 c  Peanut butter
Mix all ingredients and drop by teaspoonfuls on wax paper.  Work
quickly before it cools. From: Lancaster Farming Shared By: Pat
