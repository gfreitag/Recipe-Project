Title: Shrimp Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Fish, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 2 pounds
1 ts Sweet paprika
1 ts Lemon juice
2 tb Chopped parsley
1/2 lb Tiny shrimp, cleaned*
4    Feet small hog casings
1 1/2 lb White fish fillet, cubed
1    Egg, beaten
1 ts Onion powder
*If using canned shrimp, rinse very well under cold running water to
get rid of the tinny taste.

Prepare casings. Process fish just until broken in food processor. Add
remaining ingredients except shrimp. Process until well blended.
Transfer to mixing bowl and mix in shrimp. Stuff casings and tie off
into 3-4" links.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
