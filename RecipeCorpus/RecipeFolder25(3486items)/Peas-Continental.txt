Title: Peas Continental
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Marjoram;dried
2 tb Sherry
2 c  Peas;cooked
2 tb Butter
1 c  Mushrooms;sliced
1/4 c  Onion;minced
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
Heat butter in skillet. Saute mushrooms and onions about 5 minutes
or until tender.
Add salt and pepper, nutmeg, marjoram, sherry and peas. Warm peas
through and serve.
