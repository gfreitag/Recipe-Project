Title: Pineapple Orange Nut Loaf
Categories: Bread, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Frozen orange juice, thawed
8 3/4 oz Can of crushed pineapple,
Drained
1/2 c  Whole nutmeats
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Shortening
1    Egg
2 c  Flour
1 ts Soda
Cream together sugar and shortening; add egg.  Beat well.  Add flour,
soda and salt, alternately with orange juice.  Stir in pineapple and
nuts. Turn into a well greased loaf pan or 3 small loaf pans.  Bake
at 350 degrees for 50 to 60 minutes.  Cool. Randy Rigg
