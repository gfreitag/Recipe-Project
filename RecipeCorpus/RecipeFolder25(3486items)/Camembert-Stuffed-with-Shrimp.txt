Title: Camembert Stuffed with Shrimp
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Appetizer, Snack, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Bread crumbs
2 1/4 tb White sesame seeds
2 1/2 c  Oil
1    Camembert cheese
1/4 c  Cooked small shrimp
1    Egg, beaten
Cut cheese into 4 wedges; make slit in center of each wedge and insert
several small shrimp. Close up and pinch to reshape. Dip cheese into
egg and coat lightly with bread crumbs, then dip into egg again and
coat with sesme seeds. Place on tray and refrigerate for 1 hour. Heat
oil in deep skillet. Fry Camambert uneill golden brown. Drain and
serve at once on folded paper napkins.
