Title: Leek and Ham Quiche
Categories: Main dish, Pork, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Cheddar or swiss cheese,
-grated
3 ea Eggs, beaten
1 1/4 c  Milk or light cream
1/8 t  Nutmeg
Salt and pepper to taste
1 ea 9 or 10 in. pastry shell
1/2 lb Leeks, white part only
-(about 2 cups) washed and
-thinly sliced
2 T  Butter
1 c  Ham, cubed
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Saute' leeks in butter until golden
brown. Add ham. Place in pastry shell and sprinkle with cheese. Beat
together eggs, milk, nutmeg, salt and pepper. Pour over leek and ham
mixture. Bake 35 to 45 minutes. SOURCE:*Know Your Onions SHARED BY:
Jim Bodle 8/92, 12/94
