Title: Pecans Worcestershire
Categories: Appetizer, Nuts/grains, Holiday
Yield: 1 batch
Salt to taste
2 c  Large pecan halves
2 tb Butter
1/3 c  Worchestershire sauce
Tabasco Sauce to taste
Melt butter, add other ingredients.  Remove from heat; add pecans,
stir and mix five minutes or more so that each nut is coated and all
sauce is absorbed.  Line cookie sheet with paper towels, pour out and
spread evenly. Bake at 300 for 15 minutes.
