Title: Cinnamon and Spice Raisin Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Herbs/spice, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
1 2/3 c  All-purpose flour
1 t  Ground cinnamon
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Baking soda
1/2 t  Ground nutmeg
1/2 c  Walnuts
1/2 c  Finely chopped raisins
1 pk Active dry yeast
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Cooking oil
1 ea Egg
2 ea Egg whites
1/2 c  Molasses
In a large mixing bowl soften yeast in 1 cup warm water(105 to 115);
stir in the 1-1/2 cups flour till smooth.  Cover and let stand in a
warm place for 30 minutes.  In a large mixing bowl mix together sugar
and oil. Add egg and egg whites.  Beat with an electric mixer on low
speed till combine. Add molasses and yeast mixture; beat on low till
combined. Stir together the 1-2/3 cups flour, cinnamon, salt, soda,
and nutmeg; stir into yeast mixture.  Stir in nuts and raisins.  Turn
into a greased and floured 13x9x2-inch baking pan.  Let stand,
covered in a warm place for 1 hour. Bake in a 350 oven for 40 minutes
or till toothpick inserted comes out clean.  Cool.
per serving: 470
