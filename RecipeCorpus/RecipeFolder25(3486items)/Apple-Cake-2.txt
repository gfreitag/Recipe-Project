Title: Apple Cake 2
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Apples
Yield: 1 cake
1 ts Baking Soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
3 c  Diced Apples
1 c  Walnuts
3    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Oil
2 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Sifted Flour
Mix first four ingredients at high speed for 3 min.  Add dry
ingredients slowly.  Fold in Apples and Nuts, pour into a greased and
floured tube pan. Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
