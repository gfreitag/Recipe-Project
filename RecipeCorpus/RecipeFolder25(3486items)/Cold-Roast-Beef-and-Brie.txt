Title: Cold Roast Beef and Brie
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Breakfast, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
4    Lettuce;leaves
Mayonnaise;
6    Croissant;sliced;
8 oz Brie
1 1/2 lb Roast Beef;cooked, sliced
Set Brie out to reach room temperature. Slice croissants
lengthwise, toast lightly.
Spread Brie on halved croissant bottoms; spread mayonnaise on the
other halves.
Place the (rare, thin sliced) roast beef on Brie halves, add a
leaf of lettuce to each, top with mayonnaise halves.
