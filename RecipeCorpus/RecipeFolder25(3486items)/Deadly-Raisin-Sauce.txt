Title: Deadly Raisin Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  EACH, salt, cinnamon,
-cloves, dry mustard
1 T  Vinegar
1 c  Raisins
1 3/4 c  Water
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 T  Cornstarch
Boil the raisins in the water for 5 minutes.  In a small bowl, mix the
brown sugar, cornstarch, salt, cinnamon, cloves and dry mustard
together. Bring raisins back up to boil, add the sugar/spice mixture,
stirring until thickened.  Blend in the vinegar.  Makes 1-1/2 cups.
Origin: My Mom, Shirley Whittaker Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
