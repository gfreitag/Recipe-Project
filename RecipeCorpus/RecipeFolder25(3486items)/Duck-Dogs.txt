Title: Duck Dogs
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Poultry
Yield: 2 pounds
1 ts Coriander seed
1/4 ts Ground mace
1/2 ts White pepper
1 ts Sweet paprika
1    Egg white
2    Or 3 feet small hog or
-sheep casings
2 lb Fresh duck meat
2 tb Finely chopped fresh chives
2 cl Garlic, finely chopped
Prepare casings Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate 30
minutes. Grind through fine disk, refrigerate until firm. Stuff
casings and tie off into 4" lengths. Boil a pot of water, add links,
reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes. The sausages may be eaten
immediately ot refrigerated and served cold later or browned in a
little oil.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
