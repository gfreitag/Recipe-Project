Title: Cornish Hens with Dijon Walnut Sauce
Categories: Poultry, Rice, Main dish, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Chicken broth
2 tb White wine
1 tb Honey
1/3 c  Walnuts, chopped
Wild rice, cooked
2    Cornish hens
1 lg Garlic clove, split
1    Lemon, juice of
1 tb Flour
1/4 c  Grey Poupon Dijon Mustard
Rub hens inside and out with garlic. Sprinkle inside and out with
lemon juice. Bake in 400 deg F oven 1 hour or until tender. Remove
hens to warm platter. Stir flour into drippings in pan. Cook 2-3
minutes. Combine mustard, broth, wine and honey. Gradually add to
flour mixture, stirring constantly. Bring to boil. Mix in nuts. Split
hens. Place on a bed of wild rice surrounded by lettuce leaves. Spoon
sauce over hens. Garnish with fresh strawberry and diamond-cut peeled
kiwi.
