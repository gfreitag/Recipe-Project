Title: Microwave Orange Cranberry Sauce
Categories: Microwave, Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 cups
1 tb Orange zest;
1 lb Fresh cranberries;
2 c  Sugar;
1 c  Orange juice;
In a microproof 3-quart casserole, combine sugar and orange juice.
Microwave, uncovered on High 3 to 4 minutes to dissolve sugar and
orange juice; mix well.  Add orange zest and cranberries; cover with
lid or vented plastic wrap on high for 8 to 10 minutes, covered.
Sauce will thicken when chilled. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune
Food Section, Nov 20, 1994 Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion
