Title: Mark Janssen's Green Chile and Spam Stew
Categories: Chili, Spam, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 t  Chili powder
2 T  Vegetable oil
2 cups potatoes, diced 2 cups
1/2 cup water 1 medium onion, diced
2 cn Spam (8 oz.), rinsed and
-diced  1 tsp. onion powder
Salt & pepper to taste
carrots, diced
1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper 3 cloves garlic, minced 2 stalks
1/4 cup
1 cup milk 1
celery, chopped 1 (4 oz.) can chopped green chile
picante sauce 1 to 2 cups water
tbsp. cornstarch grated cheddar cheese chopped green onion
Directions: Heat the vegetables in a large cast-iron skillet.  Add
diced Spam, onion powder, chili powder, salt and pepper.  As Spam is
browning, microwave diced carrots, potatoes and i/2 cup water for 8
to 10 minutes, or until tender. While carrots and potatoes are
cooking, add the bell pepper, celery, green chile and picante sauce
to browned Spam, and simmer for 10 minutes. Add potatoes and carrots,
and 1 to 2 cups water (enough to almost cover other ingredients).
Stir to deglaze pan, then simmer for 1 hour. Mix cornstarch and milk.
Add to Spam-vegetable mixture, and bring almost to a boil to thicken
sauce. Sprinkle with grated cheddar cheese and onion, and serve with
warmed flour tortillas.
