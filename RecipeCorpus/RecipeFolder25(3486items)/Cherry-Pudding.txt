Title: Cherry Pudding
Categories: Pudding, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Cold water
1/4 ts Salt
Mix and let simmer:
1 c  Cherries
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Butter
1/2 c  Hot water
Combine:
Pour batter into 8 x 8 inch baking pan, greased.  Pour cherry liquid
over top.  The batter will come to the top through liquid.  Bake for
20 minutes at 350 degrees.  Serve topped with ice cream or whipped
cream. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   10-21-94
