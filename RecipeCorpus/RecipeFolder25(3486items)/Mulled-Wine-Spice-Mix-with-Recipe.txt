Title: Mulled Wine Spice Mix with Recipe
Categories: Alcohol, Mixes, Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
-WINE:
1/4 c  Mix
1 qt Dry red wine
1 qt White grape juice
-Orange slices
10    Sticks cinnamon, broken in
-pieces
1/3 c  Whole cloves
1/3 c  Whole allspice
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
2 tb Dried orange peel
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Mulled Wine: Combine mix, wine and grape juice and bring to a boil.
Simmer 30 minutes. Strain spices, and serve in mugs. Float one slice
orange in each mug for garnish.
