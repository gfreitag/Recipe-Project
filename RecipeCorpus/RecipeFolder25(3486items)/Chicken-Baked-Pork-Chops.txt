Title: Chicken Baked Pork Chops
Categories: Pork, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn (10 1/2 oz.) Cream of
-chicken soup
1 c  Milk
8 ea Pork chops
1 t  Salt
2 T  Drippings or oil
1 c  Instant rice
Using a flameproof-ovenproof covered casserole, brown the salted
chops in drippings (or oil.) Cover with rice. Combine soup and milk,
and pour over rice, making sure all rice is moistened. Cover tightly.
Bake in 350 degree oven for one hour.
