Title: Curried Chicken Salad
Categories: Salad, Chicken, Fruit, Curries
Yield: 6 servings
Grapefruit sections
Apples
Pear halves
Cherries
Ginger, ground
3 c  Chicken; cooked, diced
2 c  Celeyr; diced
1/2 c  Chutney
1 ts Salt
Salad greens
Orange sections
MMMMM-------------------CURRY FRENCH DRESSING------------------------
1/8 ts Pepper, white
1 ts Curry powder
2/3 c  Oil, salad; (or olive)
3 tb Vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
Combine chicken, celery, chutney, salt, and Curry French Dressing.
Chill for several hours.  Serve on salad greens surrounded with
fruits. Sprinkle pear halve with ground ginger.

Curry French Dressing: Beat ingredients together until well blended.
Yield: 3/4 cup.
