Title: Onion-Cheese Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin
Yield: 1 servings
-french-fried onions,
-crumbled
1 ea Egg
1 c  Milk
3 c  Biscuit mix
1 t  Onion salt
3/4 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 ea (3 1/2 oz.) can
Combine all ingredients in large bowl; beat 1 minute.  Fill medium
sized, greased muffin tins 2/3 full.  Bake at 400 about 15 minutes.
Serve Warm.
