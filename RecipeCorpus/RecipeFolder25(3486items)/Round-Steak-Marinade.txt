Title: Round Steak Marinade
Categories: Marinade, Sthrn/livng, Beef
Yield: 2 3/4 cups
1    Garlic clove; minced
1/4 c  Ginger, candied; chopped
1/2 ts Black pepper; coarsely grnd
1/2 ts Mustard, dry
1 c  Oil, salad
1 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Vinegar, wine
1/2 c  Onion, minced
Combine all ingredients and pour over steak in a flat dish.  Cover
dish and leave in refrigerator overnight.  To grill meat, remover
from marinade and drain well.  Use marinade to baste meat as it cooks.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
