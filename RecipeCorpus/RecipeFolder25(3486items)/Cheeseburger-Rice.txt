Title: Cheeseburger Rice
Categories: Casserole, Main dish, Ground beef, Cheese/eggs, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 1/2 c  Minute rice, uncooked
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1 1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Catsup
2 tb Prepared mustard
Brown meat and onion in large skillet on medium high heat; drain fat.
Stir in water, catsup, mustard, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil.
Stir in rice; cover.  Remove from heat. Let stand five minutes. Fluff
with fork. Sprinkle with cheese; cover.  Let stand 3 minutes or until
cheese melts. Garnish with lettuce leaves, pickle and tomato slices.
