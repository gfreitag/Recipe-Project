Title: Lime Jello Dessert
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 cn MILNOT, well chilled
1 pk Lime Jello
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
3/4 c  Sugar
Dissolve Jello, sugar in the hot water then add lemon juice.  Chill
until partially set and whip.  Whip the MILNOT and fold all together.
Roll 16 graham crackers and add 1/2 stick of butter and 1/4 cup of
sugar. Pat in a 9x13 inch pan.  Save a few crumbs for the top.  Pour
the Jello mixture over the graham cracker crumbs.  (Look gang!
another MILNOT recipe) Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On
