Title: Celery and Crabmeat Cocktail
Categories: Appetizer, Sthrn/livng, Vegetable, Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 3 1/2 cups
2 tb Lemon juice; freshly squeezd
2 tb Onion; minced
1 1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper, black
1 1/2 c  Celery; diced
2 c  Crabmeat; cooked, chilled
3 tb Mayonnaise
2 tb Chili sauce
Combine all ingredients.  Toss lightly.  Serve on lettuce as the first
course of serve as a salad.  Yield: 3-1/2 cups (6 servings as a
salad; 8 servings as a cocktail).

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
