Title: Pineapple-Cocoa Icing
Categories: Diabetic, Cake/cookie, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 1 cup
2 T  Unsweetened cocoa
1/4 c  Instant powdered milk
1 t  Vanilla extract
3 T  Butter
1/4 c  Unsweetened frozen pineapple
Juice concentrate, thawed
In a small saucepan combine butter and pineapple juice concentrate.
Bring to a boil over medium heat. Remove from heat and immediately
stir in cocoa and powdered milk until smooth. Stir in vanilla
extract. Allow to cool 10 minutes before spooning over desert. Allow
icing to set on dessert at room temperature, and store refrigerated.
