Title: Martha's Special Apple-Cinnamon Raisin Scones
Categories: Breakfast, Muffin, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Apples
Yield: 8 wedges
6 tb Milk
1/4 c  Butter or margarine, melted
Softened butter (opt) and
Sugar (opt)
2 pk Martha White Muffin Mix
-(7 oz ea; Apple Cinnamon)
1/2 c  Raisins or currants
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
Preheat oven to 400'F. Combine Muffin Mix, raisins, walnuts, milk and
melted butter; stir until mixture forms a soft dough. Turn onto
lightly floured board or pastry cloth and knead gently, just until
smooth. Pat out into a 6" round. Brush with softened butter and
sprinkle with sugar. Cut into 8 wedges with sharp knife. Place on
greased baking sheet about 1" apart. Bake 14-16 minutes or until
golden brown. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   10-31-94
