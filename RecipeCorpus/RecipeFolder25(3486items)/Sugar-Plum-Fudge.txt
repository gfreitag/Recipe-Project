Title: Sugar Plum Fudge
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
4 oz Mixed candied fruits
1/2 c  Walnuts (chopped)
1 c  Sugar
1 1/3 c  Evaporated milk
12 oz Chocolate chips
Combine sugar and milk; stir well. Boil until full rolling boil; boil
7 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and add chocolate
chips quickly; stir until smooth. Quickly pour into an 8 or 9-inch
buttered pie plate. Sprinkle over the top the fruits and nuts and
press into fudge. Cut into small pie-shaped pieces. Keep in the
refrigerator. Yield: 2 pounds. Submitted By LAWRENCE KELLIE   On
11-12-94
