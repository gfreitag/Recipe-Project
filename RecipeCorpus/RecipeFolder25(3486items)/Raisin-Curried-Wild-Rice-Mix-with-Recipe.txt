Title: Raisin Curried Wild Rice Mix with Recipe
Categories: Curries, Mixes, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Curry powder
-RICE:
4 c  Water
3 tb Butter
1 pk Rice mix
1 c  Wild rice
4    Chicken bouillon cubes,
-crumbled
3 tb Dreid minced onion
1/2 c  Raisins
Mix: Layer ingredients in jar in order given.

Rice: Boil water and butter. add rice mix; reduce heat, cover and
simmer 50 minutes, adding more water if needed. 8 servings.
