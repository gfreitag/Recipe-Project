Title: Dilled Crab Chowder
Categories: Seafood, Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 10 servings
-soup
16 1/2 oz Can cream style corn
3 tb Diced pimento
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Dill weed
2    Bay leaves
1/2 c  Dry vermouth
1 lg Onion, minced
4    Stalks celery, minced
1/2 c  Butter
1 lb Crabmeat
5 c  Milk
1 c  Half and half or light
-cream
2 cn Condensed cream of potato
Saute onion and celery in butter until soft. Stir in crabmeat. Add
milk, half and half, potato aoup, corn, pimento, salt, dill, and bay
leaves. Mix well and cook over medium heat 20 minutes; do not boil.
Add vermouth, cook for 5 more minutes. Remove from bay leaves and
serve.
