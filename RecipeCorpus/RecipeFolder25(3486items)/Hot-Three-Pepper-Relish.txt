Title: Hot Three Pepper Relish
Categories: Can/preserv, Relish, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Apple Cider Vinegar
1 ea Box pectin crystals
3 1/4 c  Sugar, divided
4 c  Finely chopped, seeded,
-fresh peppers (about 3
-red, 3 green, and
5 ea Jalapeno's)
In large saucepan, combine chopped peppers and vinegar.  In small
bowl, mix pectin crystals with 1/4 cup of the sugar.  Slowly stir
pectin mixture into peppers.  Cook and stir over high heat until
mixture comes to a full boil. Stir in remaining sugar.  Cook and stir
until mixture returns to a boil. Continue to stir and boil hard for 1
minutes.  Skim off foam. Stir and skim for 5 minutes to cool
slightly.  Pour quickly into sterilized jars. Seal while hot with new
2 piece lids or 1/8 inch hot paraffin. Makes about 5 cups. Origin:
Robin Hood, Recipes Plus, 1992. Shared by: Sharon Stevens.
