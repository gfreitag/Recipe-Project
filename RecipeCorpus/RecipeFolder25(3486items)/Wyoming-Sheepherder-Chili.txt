Title: Wyoming Sheepherder Chili
Categories: Chili, Main dish, Ground beef
Yield: 8 servings
Salt
Black pepper
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Vinegar
1 cn Stewed tomatoes
1 cn Tomato sauce
Water as necessary
3 lb Ground beef
4 tb Dried onions
1/4 c  Chili powder - mild, medium
Or hot, to suit your taste.
1 tb Cumin
2 tb Worcestershire
1 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
Brown the meat in a heavy pot, drain off the fat and add all the other
ingredients. Simmer for at least 2 hours, adding water as needed.
Thicken with flour if desired (preferably Masa Harina or ground-up
corn tortillas). Serve over rice.  SJC
