Title: Country Chicken Nuggets
Categories: Chicken, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 30 servings
MMMMM--------------------MAKES: 30-40 NUGGETS-------------------------
2 ea Boneless chicken breasts,
-cut in 1" pieces
1/2 c  Butter, melted
1 c  Corn flake crumbs
1 1/2 t  Oregano leaves
1 1/2 t  Thyme leaves
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1 ea Tb milk
1 c  Sour cream
2 ea Tb. Dijon mustard
Heat oven to 425~. In small bowl, stir together corn flake crumbs,
oregano, and thyme. Dip chicken pieces in melted butter, then coat
with crumb mixture. Place chicken 1/2" apart on a 15X10X1" jelly roll
pan. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until fork tender and crisp.
Meanwhile, in small bowl, stir together all sauce ingredients. Serve
nuggets with sauce. Tip: Nuggets can be baked ahead of time and
reheated at 350~ for 10 minutes. Source: Land O Lakes Treasury of
Country Recipes Carolyn Shaw's Collection
