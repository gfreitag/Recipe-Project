Title: Impossible Fruit Pie
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Cooking Oil
1    Egg
3/4 c  Water
-Waldine Van Geffen, VGHC42A
1 cn Fruit Pie Filling 16oz
1 3/4 c  Bisquick
Preheat oven to 425~.  Mix together the Bisquick, oil, egg and water.
Pour the resulting batter into a 9" pie plate. Pour pie filling into
the center of the batter. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes. Good eaten warm
topped with ice cream.
