Title: Chon Buri Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Asian, Chicken
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Bamboo Shoots;chopped
1    Scallion;chopped;
6 dr Chili Oil
2    Chicken Breast;skinned, bone
5 c  Chicken Broth
1 c  Coconut Milk;unsweetened
2 tb Cilantro;chopped, fresh
In a large saucepan, cook chicken in broth until done. Remove
chicken and tear into small chunks.
Add coconut milk and bamboo shoots to broth, heat.
Just before serving, add coriander, scallions and chicken to broth.
Add chili oil to taste, if desired.
