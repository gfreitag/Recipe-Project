Title: Sweet and Sour Meatloaves with Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Beef, Vegetable, Main dish, Potato, Asian
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Dry bread crumbs
3 T  Teriyaki sauce
1 T  Oriental sesame oil
1/2 t  Minced garlic
1/3 c  Sweet and sour stir fry
-sauce
1 1/2 lb Sweet potatoes, cut in 1/4
-inch thick rounds
1/4 c  Water
1 lb Lean ground beef
10 ea Ounces chopped spinach,
-thawed and squeezed dry
1/2 c  Chopped scallions
Overlap potato slices in a shallow 9-10 inch microwave dish.  Add
water, cover and microwave on high for 9-10 minutes. Turning to
insure even cooking.  Cook until almost tender.  Meanwhile mix beef,
spinach, scallions, bread crumbs, teriyaki sauce, sesame oil and
garlic.  Shape into 4 small loaves.  Place spoke fashion in another
shallow 9-10 inch microwave dish.  Remove potatoes from microwave and
let stand, covered on a heat proof surface.  Cover meat loaves with
waxed paper and microwave on high for 5 minutes.  Rotate meat loaves
in dish and spoon on 3 tbsp of the stir fry sauce.  Cook another 2-3
minutes or until meat is no longer pink. Drain any water from sweet
potatoes.  Spoon on remaining stir fry sauce. When meatloaves are
done, uncover potatoes and microwave 1-2 minutes on high to heat
through. Serves 4. Per serving: 601 cal, 33 g pro, 60 g carbo, 28 g
fat, 85 mg chol, 814 mg soduim. Origin: Women's Day Magazine, October
1994. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
