Title: No Salt Seasoned Salt Mix with Recipe
Categories: Mixes, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
-EALTHY HEART CHICKEN:
2 tb Olive oil
2    Boned, skinned chicken
-breasts
1 ts No salt mix
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Snipped parsley
-Cooked Rice or pasta
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Thyme leaves
1/4 ts Onion powder
1/4 ts Paprika
1/8 ts Celery seed
1/4 ts White pepper
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Dried lemon peel
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
SEASONED SALT:

Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.

HEALTHY HEART CHICKEN:

Cut chicken breasts in half. Season and saute in oil over medium high
heat about 4 minutes per side. Stir in th elemon jiuce, scraping up
any brown bits. Add parsley and serve over rice or pasta.
