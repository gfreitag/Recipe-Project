Title: Red Snapper with Shrimp Stuffing
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Fish, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
3 sl Cooked bacon, crumbled
1 c  Fresh bread crumbs
1/2 c  Chopped cooked shrimp
1    Egg
-Pepper to taste
2 tb Butter, melted
4 lb Red snapper, cleaned and
-boned
-Salt to taste
1/4 c  Chopped celery
2 tb Chopped onion
2 tb Butter
Preheat oven to 350. Rub fish inside and out with salt. Sauce onion
and celery in butter until soft. Add bacon, bread crumbs, shrimp,
egg, slat and pepper; mix well. Stuff fish; close with toothpicks.
Place in baking pan, brush with melted butter. Bake 40 minutes or
till fish tests done with fork.
