Title: Ginger Cranberries
Categories: Relish, Salad, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Honey
3/4 c  Sugar, white
1 1/2 ts Ginger
per serving: 496 Fat grams per serving: 1 Approx. Cook
1 lb Cranberries
1    Orange; quartered
1 c  Raisins
Calories
Time: 0:45

Grind oranges and cranberries with the coarsest blade of a sausage
grinder. Mix with all other ingredients.  Chill at least 2 hours
before serving.
