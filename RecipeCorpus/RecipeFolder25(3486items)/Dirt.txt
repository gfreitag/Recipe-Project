Title: Dirt
Categories: Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs, Chocolate, Pudding
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/4    Bag Oreo Cookies
4    -5 Gummy Worms
1    Plastic flower pot 8"
-across 7" deep
1    Plastic garden trowel
1    Silk Flower
1/4 c  Soft margarine or butter
8 oz Cream Cheese
1 c  Confectionary Sugar
3    To 3 1/2 Cups of Milk
2 pk Instant Chocolate Pudding
12 oz Cool Whip
Directions

In large mixing bowl combine margarine, cream cheese, and
confectionary sugar.

In second bowl combine mix milk starting with 3 cups (adding the
other half if necessary, Thicker is better), and cool whip. Mix this
mixture very well with first mixture.

crush the oreos in a food processor.

Place the Cool Whip lid on the bottom of the flower pot and add
alternating layers of the pudding mixture and cookie mixture making
the cookie mixture the last one into the flower pot.

add gummy worms partially burying them in the cookie mixture, add
flower and refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving.

Serve with plastic trowel.
