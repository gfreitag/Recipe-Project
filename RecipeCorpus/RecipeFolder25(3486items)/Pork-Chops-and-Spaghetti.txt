Title: Pork Chops and Spaghetti
Categories: Main dish, Pork, Pasta, Vegetable, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 batch
1/4 t  Oregano
2    Gloves garlic, smooshed
1 lg Jar Ragu (i like mushroom
-or simmered beef)
6    To 8 pork chops
1 qt Canned tomatoes
1 c  Chopped onion
1 t  Basil
3    Bay leaves
1/2 t  Parsley
1/2 t  Thyme
Bake pork chops in 350degF oven for about 45 min.  Or fry in skillet
until nice browned.  Err on the side of a bit crispy.

Meanwhile, start the sauce.  Combine tomatoes, onions, seasonings, and
garlic together in kettle.  Bring to a simmer.  Simmer for 30 minutes,
stirring occasionally.  Add prepared spagetti sauce.  (not sure of
exact size, around 1 1/2 to 2 qts.)  Bring to a simmer.  Add slightly
crispy pork chops and simmer until pork chops are falling apart.
Remove bay leaves and bones (if you like).  Serve with spagetti.
