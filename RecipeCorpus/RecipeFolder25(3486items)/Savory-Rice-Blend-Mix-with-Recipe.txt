Title: Savory Rice Blend Mix with Recipe
Categories: Herbs/spice, Mixes, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Dried minced onion
-RICE:
2 1/2 c  Water
2 tb Butter
1 pk Rice mix
1 1/2 c  White rice
3    Crumbled beef bouillon
-cubes
3/4 ts Dried rosemary
3/4 ts Dried marjoram
3/4 ts Dried thyme
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Savory Rice Blend: Bring water and butter to a boil. Add mix, reduce
heat, cover and simmer 20 minutes until rice is tender and liquid is
absorbed. 6 servings.
