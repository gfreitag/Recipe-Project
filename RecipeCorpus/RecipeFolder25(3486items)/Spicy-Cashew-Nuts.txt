Title: Spicy Cashew Nuts
Categories: Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------------YIELD: 2 CUPS----------------------------
1 t  Curry powder or garam masala
1 t  Cumin seeds, ground
1/4 t  Cayenne pepper
2 c  Raw cashew nuts
2 ea Tb butter
1 t  Salt
Melt the butter in a saucepan, and saute nuts until golden brown.
Stir in the seasonings and cook for a few more minutes. Remove with a
slotted spoon, drain on kitchen paper. When cool, store in a clean
screw top jar. Decorate the lid and label. Source: The Country
Kitchen: Gifts Homemade by Anne Chapman ISBN: 1-875410-61-9 Typed by
Carolyn Shaw 12-94.
