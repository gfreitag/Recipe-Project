Title: Sausage Burgers
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Sthrn/livng, Burgers
Yield: 8 sandwiches
1 md Onion; cut in 8 slices and
-separated into rings
8    Buns, hamburger; warmed
2 lb Sausage, bulk pork
3 tb Water
2 lg Tomatoes; cut in 3 slices ea
Shape sausage into 8 patties, 1/2 to 3/4 inch thick, 3 to 4 inches in
diameter.  Place in a cold frying pan; add water, cover, and cook
over low heat for 5 minutes.  Pour off drippings.  Cook patties
slowly on both sides until well done.  Place patties, tomato slices,
and onion rings between halves of warmed hamburger buns.
