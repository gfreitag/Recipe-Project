Title: Indiana-Style Corn Dogs
Categories: Hot dogs, Meat, Snack, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Egg, beaten
1/4 t  Paprika
1/2 t  Dry mustard
ds Of pepper
10-16 hot dogs
Vegtable oil for deep frying
Ingredients
1/2 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 T  Baking powder
1 t  Salt
1 T  Sugar
1 c  Evaporated  milk
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
In bowl, mix cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt, sugar, milk, egg,
paprika, mustard and pepper. Pour mixture into tall gglass. Skewer
hot dogs with wooden skewers; dip in mixture. Deep-fry at 375 degrees
until golden brown (about 2 minutes). Drain on paper towels. Recipe
sent in by Sally Denney, Warsaw, Indiana. Kim Smith
