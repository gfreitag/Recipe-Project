Title: Cappuccino Mix
Categories: Mixes, Beverage
Yield: 28 servings
-CAPPUCCINO:
2 tb Mix
6 oz Boiling water
-Whipped Cream
-Shaved Chocolate
-Frangelico liqueur,
-optional
1 c  Powdered instant nondairy
-creamer
1 c  Chocolate flavored drink
-mix
3/4 c  Instant coffee
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Cappuccino: Pour boiling water over mix in cup. Garnish with whipped
cream and chocolate. Add 1 tb frangelico if desired.
