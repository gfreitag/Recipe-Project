Title: Spanish Style Chorizo
Categories: Pork, Sausage, Homemade
Yield: 10 pounds
1 ts Crushed oregano
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Fennel seed
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
3/4 c  Brandy
1/2 ts Ascorbic acid
1 ts Saltpeter
6    Feet medium hog casings
8 lb Lean pork, cubed
2 lb Pork fat, cubed
5 tb Salt
2 tb Fine grind black pepper
3 tb Cayenne
1 tb Coarsley crushed red pepper
2 tb Finely minced garlic
1 ts Cumin seed
Grind meat and fat separately through the coarse disk and mix
together. Sprinkle remaining ingredients on the meat and mix
thoroughly. Cure in the refrigerator 24 hours. Prepare casings, stuff
and tie off into 4" links. Hang to dry for about 8 weeks.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
