Title: Chinese-Style Crab Soup
Categories: Chinese, Soup/stew, Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 6 servings
2    Eggs, beaten
1 1/2 tb Vinegar
1 1/2 tb Sherry
1 1/2 tb Soy sauce
3    Scallions, sliced
2 tb Oil
1/2 lb Crabmeat
1/4 ts Salt
2 md Tomatoes, coarsley chopped
1 1/2 ts Chopped fresh ginger
5 c  Chicken broth
Saute crabmeat, salt, tomatoes, and ginger in oil for 5 minutes. Add
chicken broth and cook over low heat for 10 minutes. Combine eggs,
vinegar, sherry, and soy sauce in a bowl; mix well. Pour egg mixture
into soup slowly. Stir in scallions; simmer for 3
