Title: Tofu & Mushrooms in Mustard Sauce
Categories: Main dish, Thai, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
2 ea Vegetable bouillon cubes
-- dissolved in 1/2 c water
3 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Pepper
2 tb Gray poupon mustard
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 lb Tofu, cubed
1/2 lb Button mushrooms
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Flour
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
1 tb Cilantro leaves, chopped
3 ea Green onions, chopped
In a wok, heat 2 tb oil to medium hot, add the tofu & fry until they
change colour.  Remove the tofu & lightly saute the mushrooms. Remove
& add the rest of the oil.  Blend in the flour, dissolved bouillon,
soy sauce, pepper & mustard.

Put tofu & mushroms back in the wok, cover & cook over very low heat
for 15 minutes.  Garnish & serve.

Puangkram C. Schmitz & Michael J. Worman, "Practical Thai Cooking"
