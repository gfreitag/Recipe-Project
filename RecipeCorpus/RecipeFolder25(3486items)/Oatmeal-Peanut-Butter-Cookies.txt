Title: Oatmeal Peanut-Butter Cookies
Categories: Nuts/grains, Cake/cookie, Dessert, Holiday
Yield: 16 servings
1    Portion Vanilla Dough
-Basic Cookie Dough
-recipe
1/2 c  Uncooked old-fashioned, OR
-quick-cooking oats
1/4 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Creamy peanut butter
Mix ingredients throughly into dough.  Roll into an 8-inch log and
slice into 16 pieces.  roll each round into a ball and put on
ungreased cookie sheets 1 inch apart.  Flatten each with a fork,
making crisscross marks. Bake 10 to 12 min. at 350degF, until golden
on bottom. Remove to a wrie rack to cool.

Per cookie: 93 cal, 2 g pro, 10 g car, 5 g fat, 11 mg chol with
butter, 3 mg chol with margarine, 35 mg. sod Exchanges: 1/4
starch/bread, 1/4 fruit, 1 fat.
