Title: Black Bean Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Beans, Pork
Yield: 1 servings
-in fat and
Excellent when served with
-just a dollop of yogurt
-and a slice of
Lime.
This is a hearty stew that
-has almost no fat.
-Tomatoes and pumpkin
Give it a rich flavor.  It
-is very high in fiber, low
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 T  Cumin
1 1/2 T  Balsamic (or red wine)
-vinegar
8 oz Fat-free ham, cut into
-small squares
Freshly ground black pepper
1 c  Dry sherry
Salt to taste
1 c  Low-fat yogurt
sl Green onion for garnish
10 ea Lime slices
1 pk (12 oz) black beans, washed
-and picked over
No stick cooking spray
1 1/2 c  Chopped green onions or
-scallions, including
-tender tops
3 ea Cloves garlic, minced
1 cn (26 oz) tomatoes
1 c  Fresh or canned unseasoned
-pumpkin puree
1 1/2 c  Beef stock
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Place the beans in a large saucepan and cover with boiling water to a
level of 3" above the beans.  Simmer until the beans are tender,
about 3 hours. Drain. Spray the large saucepan thoroughly with no
stick cooking spray. Saute the onion and garlic until just soft.  Add
the tomatoes, pumpkin puree, beef stock, cumin, vinegar, and black
beans.  Simmer for 25 min. Add the ham, black pepper, and sherry and
heat thoroughly. Salt to taste. Serve in large bowls with a dollop of
low-fat yogurt, a sprinkle of green onions, and a slice of lime.
