Title: Ko Chu Jung
Categories: Asian, Condiment, Rice
Yield: 4 gallons
40 oz Honey
2 1/4 c  Salt
1 1/2 lb Paprika
1/2 lb Cayenne (more if desired!)
3 c  Soy sauce
15 c  Dry rice
-- (1/2 can be sweet rice)
10 lb Miso
2 lb Yellow soy bean flour
5 lb Honey; plus...
Cook rice.

Bake flour in 325 F oven until lightly browned, about 1/2 hour.  Stir
often.

Combine all ingredients.  Stir down several times a day until it stops
rising.  Do not put in jars for at least a month.  Refrigerate or put
in freezer.
