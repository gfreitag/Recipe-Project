Title: Bagels with Crab Spread
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 24 servings
MMMMM-------------------------MAKES: 24------------------------------
1 t  Horseradish
6 1/2 oz Can crabmeat, drained,
-flaked with cartilage
-removed
Snipped chives or parsley
12 ea Mini bagels, split
8 oz Container soft-style cream
-cheese with olives and
-pimiento
1 ea Tb chili sauce
Toast bagels lightly under the broiler 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
Meanwhile, for spread, stir together the cream cheese, chili sauce,
and horseradish in a mixing bowl till combined. Stir in the crab.
Transfer mixture to a serving bowl. Garnish with snipped chives.
Serve crab mixture with toasted bagel halves. Source: BH&G Holiday
