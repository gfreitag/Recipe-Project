Title: Crystallized-Ginger Sauces
Categories: Dessert, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 batch
1    Lemon; grated zest of
1/2 c  Butter
1 tb Finely chopped crystallized
-ginger or
1 ts Ground ginger
2 c  Ginger ale
1 c  Sugar
3 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Lemon juice
-- (takes 2 large lemons)
Let the ginger ale stand for a few minutes, then bring to a full boil
in a saucepan.

Thoroughly blend together the sugar and cornstarch and stir gradually
~ or better, sift - into the boiling ginger ale.  Cook, stirring
constantly with a whisk, over medium heat until the mixture begins to
thicken. Add remaining ingredients and cook for 2 minutes, stirring
constantly.

Serve hot over gingerbread.

Kluger writes: "Having no rum on hand for rum-butter sauce, I
concocted this sauce to go with my gingerbread recipe.  Very tangy
and tasty!"

From Special Writer Marilyn Kluger's 10/21/92 "Gingerbread: When the
