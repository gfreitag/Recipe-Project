Title: Ambrosia 4
Categories: Fruit, Salad
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
2 md Bananas; sliced
1/3 c  Sugar rolled dates cut up
11 oz Can mandarin orange segments
-drained
1 md Apple, chopped
MMMMM----------------------PIQUANT DRESSING---------------------------
1/2 ts Grated onion
1    Garlic cloved -split - then
-later removed
1/2 c  French dressing
2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Celery seed
Piquant Dressing Mix all ingredients except garlic. Cut clove of
garlic crosswise into halves, let stand in dressing one hour, then
remove garlic.

Salad Toss orange segments, apple, bananas, dates and piquant
dressing. Spoon into lettuce cups; sprinkle with coconut.
