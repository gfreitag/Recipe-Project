Title: Amaretto Parfait
Categories: Ice cream, Alcohol, Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 servings
4-6 crushed amaretti
-cookies
2 1/8 c  Container coffee ice cream
2 T  Almond or coffee flavored
-liqueur
Scoop coffee ice cream into 4 individual parfait dishes.  Drizzle with
almond or coffee flavored liqueur, then sprinkle with crushed cookies.
Serves 4. Origin: Chatelaine Magazine, December 1989 Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Dec/94.
