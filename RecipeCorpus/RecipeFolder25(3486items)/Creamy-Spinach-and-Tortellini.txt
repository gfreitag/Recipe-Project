Title: Creamy Spinach and Tortellini
Categories: Main dish, Vegetable, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Tomato; seeded and cubed
1/4 c  Basil leaves; fresh, chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Whipping cream
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
- or Romano cheese
1 lb Fresh cheese tortellini;
- or frozen
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
3    Garlic cloves; minced
2 tb Olive oil; or oil
9 oz Pkg Frozen chopped spinach;
- thawed; Green Giant
Cook tortellini to desired doneness as directed on package.  Drain;
rinse with hot water.

Meanwhile, in large skillet over medium heat cook onion and garlic in
oil until lightly browned, about 4 minutes.  Add spinach, tomato,
basil, salt and pepper; cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Stir
in whipping cream and Parmesan cheese; cook until mixture just comes
to a boil. Reduce heat to low.  Add tortellini; mix well.  Cook an
additional 4 to 5 minutes or until thoroughly heated.  Serve with
additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Note: I sub 4 tsp dried basil for the real thing.
