Title: Two-Flavor Fudge
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
6 oz Chocolate chips
6 oz Butterscotch chips
1 c  Walnuts (chopped)
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  White sugar
1 c  Evaporated milk
1/2 c  Butter
1 pt Marshmallow creme
Combine in a saucepan brown suar, white sugar, evaporated milk and
butter. Bring to full boil over moderate heat, stirring frequently.
Boil for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally; remove from heat. Add the
marshmallow and chocolate and butterscotch chips; stir until chips
are melted and mixture is smooth. Blend in walnuts and vanilla; pour
into greased 9-inch square pan. Chill until firm; cut in small
squares. Submitted By LAWRENCE KELLIE   On   11-12-94
