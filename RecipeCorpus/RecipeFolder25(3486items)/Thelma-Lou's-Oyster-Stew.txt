Title: Thelma Lou's Oyster Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Main dish, Clam/oyster
Yield: 6 servings
In a 2 quart saucepan heat
-the butter over low heat
-until it begins to
Bubble. Add the chopped
-celery, saving some of the
-green leaves.  Stir
And cook for a few minutes.
- Add the oysters and cook
-for 3-4 minutes,
Or until the edges of the
-oysters curl.  Remove from
-the heat and add
1 pt Jar oysters
sm White celery stalks with
-leaves from the center of
-the stalk,
Chopped
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
3/4 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
1/4 t  Paprika
1 qt Milk
Paprika
Butter
Oyster crackers
the milk, salt, pepper, and paprika.  Return to the heat, and cook
until amost boiling, about 10 minutes.  During the last few minutes,
add the reserved celery leaves.  Serve in hot bowls with a lump of
butter and a dash of paprika in each bowl.  Serve the crackers on the
side. Serves 6. Submitted by: Betty Lynn - Cast member, Mayberry RFD
