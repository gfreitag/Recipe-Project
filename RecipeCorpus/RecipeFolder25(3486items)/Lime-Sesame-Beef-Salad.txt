Title: Lime-Sesame Beef Salad
Categories: Beef, Main dish, Microwave, Salad
Yield: 4 servings
1 sm Onion; cut lengthwise
-- into thin wedges
1 md Red bell pepper; julienned
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped fresh
-cilantro
Boston lettuce leaves
12 oz Deli roast beef
-- sliced 1/4" thick
1 sm Cucumber
-- cut lengthwise in half &
-- crosswise in thin slices
1 md Carrot; julienned
MMMMM--------------------LIME-SESAME DRESSING-------------------------
1 tb Soy sauce
1    Garlic clove; crushed
1/8 ts Crushed red pepper
3 tb Fresh lime juice
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Dark sesame oil
Stack beef slices; cut lengthwise in half and then crosswise into
1/2" wide strips.  In medium bowl, combine beef, cucumber, carrot,
onion, bell pepper and cilantro; set aside.

In 1 cup microwave-safe glass measure, combine dressing ingredients.
Cook on high (100 percent power) for 30 seconds or just until bubbly,
stirring until sugar is completely dissolved.  Add dressing to beef
mixture; toss to coat.

To serve, arrange lettuce on serving platter.  Spoon beef mixture over
lettuce.

Nutritional info per serving: 261 calories, 26 g protein, 18 g
carbohydrates, 10 g fat, 3.2 mg iron, 329 mg sodium, 69 mg
cholesterol.
