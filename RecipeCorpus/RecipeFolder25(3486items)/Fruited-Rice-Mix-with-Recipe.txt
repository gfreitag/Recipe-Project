Title: Fruited Rice Mix with Recipe
Categories: Fruit, Mixes, Nuts/grains, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Dried orange rind
1/2 ts Onion powder
-RICE:
2 1/2 c  Water
2 tb Butter
1 pk Rice mix
1 c  Long grain rice
1/2 c  Finely chopped apricots
1/4 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Slivered almonds
1    Chicken bouillon cube,
-crumbled
2 ts Parsley flakes
Mix; Combine and store in an airtight container.

Fruited Rice:

Bring water nad butter to a boil. Add rice mix. Reduce heat; cover and
simmer 2-25 minutes until rice is tender and water is absorbed. 6
servings.
