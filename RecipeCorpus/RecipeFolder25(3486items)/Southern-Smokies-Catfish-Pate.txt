Title: Southern Smokies Catfish Pate
Categories: Cajun/creol, Appetizer, Fish
Yield: 16 servings
1/2    Clove garlic,crushed
1/8 t  Black pepper
Toast fingers
Lemon wedges
1/3 c  Flaked smoked catfish
1 pk Cream cheese,softened(8 oz)
1/2 c  Butter or margarine,softened
2 T  Red wine
1 T  Lemon juice
1. Combine all ingredients except toast and lemon wedges in a
processor bowl; process until ingredients are mixed well.

2. Chill in molds or crocks.

3. Serve on toast fingers; squeeze lemon wedges over pate.
