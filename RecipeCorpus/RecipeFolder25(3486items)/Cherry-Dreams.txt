Title: Cherry Dreams
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie
Yield: 16 servings
1/4 ts Salt
2    Eggs well beaten
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts (optional)
1/2 c  Shredded coconut
1/2 c  Chopped maraschino cherries
3/4 c  Soft butter
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Icing sugar
1 c  Granulated sugar
1/4 c  All purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
Preheat oven to 350, grease an 8 inch square pan. Cream butter blend
in vanilla. Sift together 1 1/2 c. flour and icing sugar; blend into
butter mixture. Press into prepared pan; bake in preheated oven 20
minutes.

Sift together granulated sugar, 1/4 c. flour, baking powder and salt.
Blend in eggs and vanilla.  Add walnuts and/or cocanut, cherries; mix
well.

Pour over baked mixture and bake 30 minutes longer.  Cool and cut
into 2 inch squares.
