Title: Smoked Salmon Rolls with Oysters
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Clam/oyster, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
4 ea Peppercorns crushed
2 ea Cloves whole
2 t  Tomato paste
1/3 c  Swiss cheese grated
4 ea Salmon smoked large slices
1 c  Stock fish
1 t  Parsley
1 t  Onion chopped
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 ea Garlic clove chopped
3/4 c  Cream or cream sauce
4 ea Mushroom caps large
20 ea Oysters small raw
1 c  Wine white
1 t  Shallots
1 t  Chives
Rinse salmon slices in hot water, drain and dry. At end of each slice
place 5 oysters then roll salmon over them. Arrange rolls on a
shallow, greased baking pan. In a saucepan combine wine, stock,
parsley, shallots, onion, peppercorns, garlic and cloves. Bring to a
boil then simmer until sauce is reduced to 1/2 cup. Strain sauce into
another pan then add cream or cream sauce and tomato paste. Bring
this to a boil then remove from heat. Saute mushroom caps in butter
until done then place on each salmon roll. Pour sauce over salmon
rolls, sprinkle with grated swiss cheese and broil until cheese
melts. Serve immediately.
