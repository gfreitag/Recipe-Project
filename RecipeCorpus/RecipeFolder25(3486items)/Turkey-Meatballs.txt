Title: Turkey Meatballs
Categories: Ground beef, Poultry, Meatballs
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Fennel seed
1 ds Black pepper
1 ts Crushed red pepper
1/2 ts Minced garlic
6 tb Parmesan cheese
2 lb Ground turkey
2    Beaten eggs
2/3 c  Fresh bread crumbs
1 ts Grated lemon peel
3 tb Parsley
In a bowl, combine all of the above. Shape into large walnut sized
meatballs. Place in baking dish. Bake at 400'F for about 30 minutes or
untill crispy and brown. Serve with spaghetti sauce on spaghetti. Lisa
