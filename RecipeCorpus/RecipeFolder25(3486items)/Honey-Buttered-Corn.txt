Title: Honey Buttered Corn
Categories: Microwave, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 t  Lemon-pepper seasoning
1/2 t  Seasoned salt
4 ea Fresh medium ears of corn
1/3 c  Margarine or butter,
-softened
1 T  Honey
Stir together margarine, honey, lemon pepper, and seasoned salt.
Remove husks from corn.  Scrub corn with a stiff brush to remove
silks. Rinse. Wrap each ear of corn in waxed paper.  Micro-cook on
100% power (HIGH) 9 to 11 minutes or until tender, turning over once.
Serve with 2 tsp. honey-butter mixture per ear.  Store any remaining
mixture, covered in refrigerator.  Serves 4 Per serving: 143 cal., 3
g pro., 17 g carbo., 9 g fat, 0 mg chol., 261 mg sodium, and 2 g
dietary fiber. From: Take Pride (Supermarket magazine) Shared By: Pat
Stockett
