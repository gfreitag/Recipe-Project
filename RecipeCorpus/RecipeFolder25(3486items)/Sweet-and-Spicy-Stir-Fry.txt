Title: Sweet and Spicy Stir-Fry
Categories: Stir fry, Poultry, Chicken, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
4    Half boneless chicken
-breasts, skinned, cooked
-and cubed (from
-MEDITERRANEAN CHICKEN)
3/4 c  Sweet and sour sauce
1/2 t  Crushed red pepper flakes
4 c  Cooked rice
1 cn DEL MONTE Pineapple Chunks
-In Its Own Juice, undrained
2 t  Vegetable oil
1 lg Red or green pepper, cut
-into strips
1 cn DEL MONTE Cut Green Beans,
-drained (8 oz)
Prep time: 5 minutes. Cook time: 5 minutes.

1. Drain pineapple, reserving 1/3 cup juice.

2. In large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add pepper
strips; stir-fry 2 minutes.

3. Add green beans, chicken, sweet and sour sauce, red pepper flakes,
pineapple, and reserved juice; stir-fry 3 minutes or until heated
through.

4. Meanwhile, place rice in microwavable dish; sprinkle with 2
teaspoons water. Cover with plastic wrap; slit to vent. Microwave on
HIGH 4 minutes or until heated through. Spoon onto serving plate; top
with chicken mixture.
