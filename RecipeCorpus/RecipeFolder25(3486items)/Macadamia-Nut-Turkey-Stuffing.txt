Title: Macadamia-Nut Turkey Stuffing
Categories: Holiday, Stuffing, Poultry, Turkey, Nuts/grains
Yield: 12 lb turkey
1/2 ts Dried sage;
1/2 ts Dried thyme;
1 1/2 ts Salt;
1/2 ts Black pepper;
3 1/2 oz (1 jar) macadamia nuts;
-chopped
1/4 c  To 1/2 c chicken broth;
8 c  Bread Cubes
6 tb Butter -=OR=-
6 tb Margarine;
1 c  Onion; chopped
1 md Clove garlic; minced
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1/2 c  Celery leaves; chopped
Place the bread cubes in a large bowl.  Melt the butter in a skillet;
saute the onion, garlic, celery and celery leaves 10 minutes.  Add
vegetables to bread cubes and toss with sage, thyme, salt and pepper,
and nuts. If stuffing seems very dry, moisten with broth.  Cool
stuffing before putting into turkey.  Place excess buttered casserole
dish and bake at 325 degrees for 45-60 minutes. Source: The San Diego
Union-Tribune Food Section, Nov. 20, 1994. Brought to you and your
via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION
On   11-20-94
