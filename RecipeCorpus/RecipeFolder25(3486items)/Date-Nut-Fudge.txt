Title: Date-Nut Fudge
Categories: Candy, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Butter
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Nuts (chopped)
4 c  Sugar
1 c  Dates (chopped)
1 c  Milk
Mix sugar, dates, milk and butter; boil until a soft ball is formed
when dropped into cold water. Add vanilla and nuts. Beat until
mixture begins to thicken; pour into greased pan. Yield: 2 pounds.
