Title: Seasoned Pretzels
Categories: Holiday, Bread, Snack
Yield: 6 servings
-Original Party Dip Mix
1 t  Worcestershire sauce
1/2 t  Seasoned salt
6 c  Pretzels
1/2 c  Butter or
-margarine, melted
1 ea Pk Hidden Valley Ranch
Preheat oven to 250F.  Place pretzels in a large shallow baking pan.
In a small bowl, combine butter, dip mix, Worcestershire, and salt.
Pour butter mixture over pretzels; stir well.  Bake 1 hour, stirring
every 15 minutes. Pour onto waxed paper to cool.  Store in airtight
container.
