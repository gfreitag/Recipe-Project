Title: Confetti Mushroom and Rice Side Dish
Categories: Side dish, Rice, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
-rice
1/2 t  Dried thyme
2 c  Chicken broth
2 cn Green Giant Sliced
-Mushrooms, drained
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Diced red bell pepper
3 tb Sliced green onions
-including tops
1 c  Uncooked long grain white
Melt margarine in medium saucepan. Add bell pepper, onions and
uncooked rice. Saute for 2-3 minutes until vegetables become tender
and rice begins to brown. Add thyme, chicken broth and mushrooms;
bring to a boil. Cover; reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or
until liquid is absorbed. Let stand 5 minutes.
