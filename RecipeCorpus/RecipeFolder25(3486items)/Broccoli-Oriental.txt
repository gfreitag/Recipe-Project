Title: Broccoli Oriental
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Asian
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Margarine or butter
4 ts Soy sauce
ds Ground red pepper
1 lb Broccoli, cut up OR
1    10-oz package frozen cut
-broccoli
1/2 c  Sliced water chestnuts
In a medium saucepan cook fresh broccoli and water chestnuts,
covered, in a small amount of boiling water for 9 to 11 minutes or
till crisp-tender. (Or cook frozen broccoli and water chestnuts
according to broccoli package directions.)  Drain; transfer to a
bowl. Keep warm

In the same saucepan melt margarine or butter.  Stir in soy sauce and
red pepper.  Heat through.  Pour over broccoli.
