Title: Hot Beer Chili
Categories: Ground beef, Sausage, Alcohol, Chili
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
2 ds Tabasco sauce or
-more
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb White vinegar
1/2 ts Crushed red pepper
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Bay leaves; crushed
12 oz Beer
1 lb Ground beef
1 lb Bulk Hot sausage
1    Onion; chopped
1 sm Garlic clove; chopped
2 tb Butter
20 oz Chopped tomatoes; can of
12 oz Tomato paste
1/4 c  Chili powder
Saute the onion and garlic in butter until tender. Add the ground
beef and sausage and cook over medium heat until browned. Add
tomatoes, tomato paste, chili powder, sauces, vinegar, peppers and
bay leaves. Stir in the beer and simmer for 2 hours.
