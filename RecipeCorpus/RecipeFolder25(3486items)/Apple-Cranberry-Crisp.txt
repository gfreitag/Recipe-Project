Title: Apple-Cranberry Crisp
Categories: Dessert, Holiday, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Cinnamon
4 c  Apples; peeled, sliced 1/4in
-thick, (abt 4 medium)
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Cranberries; fresh
2/3 c  Rolled oats; (NOT instant)
3 tb Flour
1/3 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Margarine; melted
1 ts Margarine; melted
1/3 c  Brown sugar
Preheat oven to 375F.  In a small bowl, combine rolled oats, flour
and 1/3 cup brown sugar.  Stir in margarine and set aside.

In a large bowl, mix remaining 1/3 cup brown sugar with cornstarch and
cinnamon.  Blend well.  Add apples, cranberries and lemon juice and
toss well.

Place fruit mixture in a 2-qt baking dish.  Sprinkle with rolled oat
mixture.  Bake 35-45 minutes or until apples are tender, but not
mushy, and the top is lightly browned.  Serve warm or at room
temperature.
