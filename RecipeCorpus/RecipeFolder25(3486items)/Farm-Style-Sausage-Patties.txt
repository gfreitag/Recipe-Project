Title: Farm-Style Sausage Patties
Categories: Sausage, Pork, Homemade
Yield: 6 lbs
1 T  Coarse black pepper
1 1/2 t  Ground cloves
1/2 t  Thyme leaves
1/2 t  Ground allspice
2 md Onions, minced
2    Cloves garlic, minced (opt.)
5 lb Boneless pork butt
Or shoulder, cubed
1 lb Salt pork or unsalted side
Pork, skinned and cubed
1 T  Salt up to 2 Tablespoons
2 T  Rubbed sage
1. Put pork and salt pork through food chopper with coarse blade in
place. 2. Blend in remaining ingredients thoroughly, adding just 1
tablespoon of salt at first.  make a small patty of the meat and cook
in skillet; taste; add more salt if desired.3. Shape into patties and
fry in large skillet until well browned on both sides and cooked
through or wrap and reftigerate, or freezeThis is an irresistible
breakfast sausage! Submitted By BRENT WILLIAMS   On   11-02-94
