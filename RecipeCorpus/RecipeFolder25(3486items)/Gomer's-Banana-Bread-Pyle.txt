Title: Gomer's Banana Bread Pyle
Categories: Bread, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Cake/cookie, Muffin
Yield: 2 servings
2 1/2 c  Sifted all purpose flour
1 t  Salt
2 t  Baking soda
1/2 c  Chopped macadamia nuts
1 c  Butter or oil
2 c  Sugar
4 ea Eggs, beaten well
6-7 bananas, mashed
Grease 2 large loaf pans or 5 small ones.  Cream the butter with the
sugar. Add the eggs and beat well.  Add the mashed bananas, stirring
to blend. n a separate bowl, sift together the dry ingredients.  Fold
the dry ingredients into the batter, mixing well.  Add the macadamia
nuts. Pour the batter into the pans.  Bake in a 350F oven for 50-60
minutes, or until done. For muffins, fill 12 muffin cups 3/4 full and
bake in a 350F oven for 35-45 minutes.  Makes 2 larger loaves, 5
smaller loaves, or 12 large muffins. Submitted by: Jim Nabors - cast
member Mayberry RFD (Played Gomer Pyle) Origin: Aunt Bee's Mayberry
Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
