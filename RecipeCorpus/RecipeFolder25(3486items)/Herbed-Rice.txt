Title: Herbed Rice
Categories: Side dish, Rice, Sthrn/livng, Herbs/spice
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Rosemary
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/2 ts Thyme
1/4 c  Butter (or marg.)
1 c  Rice, regular; uncooked
2 c  Broth, chicken
3 tb Onion, instant minced
Combine all ingredients in saucepan.  Heat to boiling; stir once.
Cover; simmer for 20 minutes or until tender.
