Title: Couscous Dressing - Martha Stewart Living
Categories: Pasta, Dressing
Yield: 1 cup
1 1/2 t  Salt
1 1/2 t  Cinnamon
1 1/2 t  Nutmeg
1/2 t  Ground cloves
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
3/4 c  Olive Oil
3 t  Sugar
1 1/2 t  Freshly ground pepper
1 1/2 t  Ground ginger
Whisk together all ingredients in a bowl.
