Title: Stuffed Zucchini (Microwave)
Categories: Vegetable, Microwave, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Munster cheese, shredded
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
1/4 c  Butter
Mushrooms, chopped
Stuffed zucchini
-(microwave)
2 lg Zucchini
2 ea Chopped scallions
1 t  Parsley
Cut zucchini in half, scoop out pulp. Place butter in cooking dish,
nuke on HIGH for 2 minutes*.  Add onions, and garlic to butter, nuke
on HIGH for 3 minutes. Add mushrooms and parsley, nuke n HIGH for 3
minutes. Drain cooked veggies, but in mixing bowl with zucchini pulp.
Add cheese and mix well. Fill each 1/2 zucchini with mixture and cook
on HIGH fr 6 minutes. *seems to me time is too long for melting
butter.. SC From Sandy Colby
