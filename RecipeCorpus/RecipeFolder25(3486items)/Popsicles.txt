Title: Popsicles
Categories: Dessert, Kids, Snack
Yield: 1 popsicles
1 c  Sugar
1 pk Koolaid; unsweetened
3 oz Jello;pkg, any flavor
Dissolve the above in 2c boiling water.  Be SURE it's dissolved, it
does take a while.  Add 2c cold water, mix well.

Pour into molds, freeze til firm. Posted on cooking echo by Jeanne
