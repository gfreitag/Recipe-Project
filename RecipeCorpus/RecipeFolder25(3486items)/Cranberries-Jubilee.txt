Title: Cranberries Jubilee
Categories: Fruit, Alcohol, Ice cream
Yield: 1 servings
3 T  Grand marnier
Vanilla ice cream
1 ea Cranberry sauce
1/4 c  Rum or brandy
Combine cranberry sauce and 1/4 cup rum in a wide frying pan.  Place
over mediumlow heat.  heat, stirring often, until fairly smooth and
boiling gently.  Pour Grand Marnier into a measuring cup.  Turn off
heat. Quickly pour all the grand marnier over sauce andimmediately
bring a lighted match nearsurface to flame.  Spoon hot sauce over
vanilla ice cream. Makes 1 cup. Origin: Chatelaine Magazine,
December, 1989. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
