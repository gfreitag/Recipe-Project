Title: Linguine Primavera Della Santa Maria
Categories: Italian, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Dry white wine
1/2 t  Salt (opt)
2 tb Chopped fresh basil
-OR
1 t  Dried basil, crushed
1 pk CONTADINA Refrigerated
-Linguine (9 oz)
Sliced ripe olives (opt)
Chopped fresh basil (opt)
2 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Chopped shallots
1 tb Chopped garlic
1 c  Sliced zucchini (1 small)
1 c  Sliced yellow squash (1 med)
1 c  Bell pepper strips
1 cn CONTADINA Pasta Ready Chunky
-Tomatoes with Crushed Red
-Peppers (14.5 oz)
1 cn CONTADINA Tomato Paste (6oz)
In medium skillet, heat oil; saute shallots and garlic for 1 minute.
Add zucchini, yellow squash and bell pepper; saute for 3-4 minutes.
Stir in CONTADINA Pasta Ready Chunky Tomatoes, paste, wine and salt;
simmer for 5-8 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Stir in basil;
simmer for 1 minute. Prepare pasta according to package directions.
Pour sauce over pasta; garnish with olives and basil.
