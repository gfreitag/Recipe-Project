Title: Moose Steak Supreme
Categories: Main dish, Game
Yield: 1 servings
Salt and pepper
1 ea Egg
3 ea Moose steaks
1/4 c  Milk or goats milk
Cracker crumbs
Cut moose steak very thin. Pound on bolth sides to tenderize, beat
egg, milk and seasoning in a small bowl.  Dip meat in egg
mixture,then dip in cracker crumbs.  Fry meat in medium hot pan.
Origin: Anna Heppner
