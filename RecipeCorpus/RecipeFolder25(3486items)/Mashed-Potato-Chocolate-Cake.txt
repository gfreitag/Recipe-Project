Title: Mashed Potato Chocolate Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Potato
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Margarine
4    Eggs, separated
2 c  Sifted flour
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Mashed potatoes
1/8 ts Salt
3    Squares of unsweetened
Chocolate
1 c  Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Milk
Heat milk and chocolate in a saucepan, stir until melted and then
cool. Cream 1 3/4 cups of the sugar with margarine.  Add egg yolks
and mashed potatoes; mix well.  Beat egg whites until stiff but not
dry. Add 1/4 cup of sugar to egg whites and beat.  Fold into cake
mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Randy Rigg
