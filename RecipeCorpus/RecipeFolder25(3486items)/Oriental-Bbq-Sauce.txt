Title: Oriental Bbq Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Condiment, Asian
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM---------------------MAKES: 2 1/2 CUPS--------------------------
4 t  Oriental sesame oil
2 t  Rice vinegar
2 t  Finely chopped garlic
1/4 t  Crushed red pepper flakes
1 c  Soy sauce
1 c  Water
6 ea Tb brown sugar
1/4 c  Grated, pared fresh
-gingerroot
Mix together and store in refrigerator for up to 6 weeks. Per
Tablespoon: 17 calories, 0g protein, 1g fat, 3g carb., 532mg sodium,
0mg chol. Source: Family Circle 12/2/86
