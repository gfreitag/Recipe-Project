Title: Short-Cut Chili Con Carne
Categories: Crockpot, Main dish, Chili
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts (2) chili powder
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 cn (8oz) tomato sauce
2 cn (16oz) kidney beans, drained
1 lb Lean ground beef
1    Small onion, chopped
1 ts Salt
1    Bay leaf
In skillet or slow-cooking pot with browning unit, break up beef with
fork and cook until lightly browned. Pour off excess fat. In
slow-cooking pot, combine meat with onion, salt, chili powder, bay
leaf, Worcestershire sauce, tomato sauce, and kidney beans. Cover and
cook on high for 2 to 3 hours. Remove bay leaf. Makes 6 to 7 servings.
