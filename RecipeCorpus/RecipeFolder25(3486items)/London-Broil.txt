Title: London Broil
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Bbq/grill, Meat
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ts Mustard;dry
2 ts Worcestershire
1 ds Cayenne
dr Tabasco sauce
2 lb Steak;flank
1    Garlic;sliced;clove
1 c  Salad Oil
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 ts Salt
Remove excess fat from steak then score on both sides. Place all
the ingredients in a shallow pan.
Place steak in pan, then turn over. Marinate at least 3 hours or
over- night, turning several times.
Place steak in broiler 3 inches from heat. Broil each side 4
minutes.
Slice thin across grain diagonally.
Serve with Bearnaise Sauce.
