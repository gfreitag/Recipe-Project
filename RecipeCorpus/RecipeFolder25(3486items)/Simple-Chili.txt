Title: Simple Chili
Categories: Soup/stew, Meat, Ground beef, Chili
Yield: 4 servings
15 oz Can Tomato Sauce
15 oz Can Whole Tomatoes
7 oz Can Tomato Paste
15 oz Water
OPTIONAL -  -
1/2 tb Cornstarch, for thickening
3 lb Coarse ground chili meat
1 tb Onion salt
2 tb Garlic powder
3 tb Chili powder
2 tb Ground Cumin
3 tb Tabasco sauce
Brown chili meat, then pour off fat/grease.  Run hot water over meat
to remove additional grease.  (Using a strainer, collander or sieve
helps)

Add remaining ingredients.  Bring to boil, stirring.  Reduce to low
heat and simmer 2 hours covered.  Chop whole tomatoes as they cook.
Add the cornstarch during the last 1/2 hour to thicken the liquid if
needed.

Serve with saltine crackers.

Enjoy! - Jeff Duke
