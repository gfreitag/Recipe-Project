Title: Pumpkin Bread in a Coffee Can
Categories: Dessert, Bread, Pumpkin
Yield: 3 cans
1 c  Pumpkin; regular can
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
3 x  Coffee cans; 13 oz size
4 x  Eggs
3 c  Sugar
3 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Oil
1/2 c  Water
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix all the wet ingredients in one
bowl, and all dry ingredients in another bowl.  Then mix both bowl's
ingredients together.  Pour into 3 greased "1 pound" (13 ounce)
coffee cans. Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees.
