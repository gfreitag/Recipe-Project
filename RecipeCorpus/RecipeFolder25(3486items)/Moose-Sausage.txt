Title: Moose Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Game, Beef
Yield: 5 pounds
2 ts Coarse grind black pepper
2 cl Finely minced garlic
1/4 c  Onion, finely minced
1/2 c  Sweet red pepper, minced
1 ts Crushed red pepper
5    Feet medium hog casings
5 lb Moose with fat
-OR_
4 lb Moose meat and an equal
-amount of beef fat, cubed
2 ts Salt
Prepare casings. Combine remaining ingredients, grind through the
coarse disk and stuff into casings. Twist off into 4" links. Let cure
in the refrigerator two days before cooking.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
