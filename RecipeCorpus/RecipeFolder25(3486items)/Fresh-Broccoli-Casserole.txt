Title: Fresh Broccoli Casserole
Categories: Vegetable, Casserole
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Light sour cream
1/3 c  Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese
1/4 c  Crumbled herb-seasoned bread
-crumbs
9/16 lb Broccoli, trimmed and cut
-into spears
1/4 c  Grated onion
1 cn 10.3/4 oz can cream of
-mushroom soup, undiluted
Steam broccoli until just barely fork tender; rinse in cold water and
drain well. Place well-drained broccoli in a flat 1.1/2-2qt
casserole. Combine next four ingredients and spread over broccoli;
top with herb-seasoned bread crumbs. Bake in a preheated 350 degree
oven for 30-35 minutes or until broccoli is tender and crumbs are
golden brown.

Serves 6-8
