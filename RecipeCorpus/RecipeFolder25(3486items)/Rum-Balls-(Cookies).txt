Title: Rum Balls (Cookies)
Categories: Candy, Alcohol, Cake/cookie, Holiday, Nuts/grains
Yield: 12 servings
-crumbs, 1 cup powdered
-sugar,
1/2 c  White corn syrup
1/4 c  Rum
1 c  Powdered sugar
2 1/2 c  Vanilla wafers; crushed
1 c  Powdered sugar
2 ea Tb Cocoa; unsweetened
1 c  Nuts; finely chopped
Pecan halves
Combine vanilla wafer
and cocoa, mixing well. Add the nuts, syrup and rum, stirring until
well blended. Shape into 1" balls and place on waxed paper. Let stand
1 hour, then roll in powdered sugar to coat and press a pecan half in
each ball. Store in tightly covered container for three days.
