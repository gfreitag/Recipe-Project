Title: South India Chutney
Categories: Indian, Relish, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 servings
1/8 ts Ginger
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Flaked coconut
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
2 tb Grated radish
1/3 c  Finely chopped green pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1. Combine all ingredients, tossing to mix well. 2. Serve with meat
dishes or any curry. MAKES 1 1/3 CUPS
