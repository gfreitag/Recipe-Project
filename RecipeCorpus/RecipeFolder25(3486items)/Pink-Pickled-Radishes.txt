Title: Pink Pickled Radishes
Categories: Vegetable, Sthrn/livng, Appetizer
Yield: 3 cups
1/4 c  Oil, olive
1/4 c  Water; cold
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper, white
2 bn Radishes, red round
2 c  Water
1/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Vinegar, champagne wine
Wash radishes and cut off roots; trim stem ends.  Boil radishes in 2
cups water, to which 1/4 teaspoon salt has been added, until they
lose their color, about 10 minutes.  Drain and cool.

Mix vinegar, oil, 1/4 cup cold water, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and white
pepper. Marinate radishes in this mixture for at least 24 hours
before serving.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
