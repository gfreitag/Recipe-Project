Title: Firehall Chocolate Chip Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 2 dozen
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
12 oz Chocolate chips
1 c  Chopped nuts
2 1/4 c  All purpose four
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 c  Butter, softened
3/4 c  Sugar
Combine dry ingredients in small bowl.

Cream together butter and sugars until creamy.  Add vanilla and
continue beating, adding eggs, one at a time until smooth.  Gradually
add flour mixture, mixing well.

Stir chocolate chips and nuts into batter.  Drop by the tablespoonful
onto a cool cookie sheet (about 6 per sheet) and back in 375F oven
for 8-10 minutes.  Do not overbake.

From: Firehall Favourites Cookbook by John Pentland University
Endowment Lands Fire Department. ISBN 0-919431-09-7
