Red Beans and Rice: Soak the beans overnight in water to cover. Drain;
combine with remaining ingredients, except the rice and 4 1/2 cups
water. Bring to a boil; recuce heat, cover and simmer until beans are
tender, about 2 hours. Remove the ham hock, cool slightly. Remove m
