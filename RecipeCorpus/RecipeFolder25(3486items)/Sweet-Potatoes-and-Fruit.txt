Title: Sweet Potatoes and Fruit
Categories: Vegetable, Fruit, Apples, Side dish, Sweetpotato
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Cinnamon
4 tb Sugar
Butter or Margarine
2 c  Sweet Potatoes, sliced and
-cooked
2 c  Apples, Oranges,or Pineapple
-fresh and sliced
Arrange alternating layers of sweet potatoes and fresh fruit.
Sprinkle with mixture of sugar and cinnamon.  Dot with butter or
margarine. Bake at 350Ã¸ for 25 to 30 minutes.  When serving, drizzle
syrup from the baking pan over the top of each portion.
