Title: Summertime Chicken Salad
Categories: Salad, Chicken, Low-cal/fat, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ts Curry powder (optional)
2 c  Cubed, cooked chicken
4    Peaches or nectarines; cubed
1 c  Sliced celery (2 ribs)
1/4 c  Sliced green onions
1/4 c  Toasted slivered almonds
-(optional)
1/2 c  Mayonnaise Dressing
-BEST FOODS Reduced Fat,
-Cholsterol Free
-or Light Mayonnaise
2 tb Lemon or lime juice
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Salt
1. In large bowl whisk together mayonnaise, lemon or lime juice,
sugar, ginger, salt and pepper (for curried chicken salad whisk in
curry powder).

2. Stir in chicken, peaches, celery and green onions.

3. Cover and chill.  Before serving, stir in almonds if desired.

Nutrition information per serving (with Reduced Fat): 180 Calories,
15g Protein, 17g Carbohydrates, 6g Fat, 40mg Cholesterol, 300mg
Sodium.
