Title: Veal Scallopine
Categories: Main dish, Italian, Meat, Veal, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Oregano
1 c  White Wine
3/4 c  Olive Oil
;OPTIONAL:;
1 lb Mozzarella Cheese;sliced
1 1/2 c  Tomato Sauce
1 3/4 lb Veal
1/2 c  Bread Crumbs
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper;ground
2    Egg;beaten;
1/2 c  Parmesan Cheese
Pound veal till thin. Pat dry, cut into pieces. Combine bread
crumbs, cheese, spices and salt. Set aside.
Beat eggs in a shallow dish. Dip veal into eggs, then crumb
mixture, coating on both sides.
Heat oil in skillet. Saute veal until golden brown. About 3
minutes per side. Remove to warm platter.
Drain fat, stir in wine, scraping up brown residue till
boiling.Pour over veal.
Serving hint: Cover with tomato sauce. Top with mozzarella cheese
slices. Broil till cheese melts.
