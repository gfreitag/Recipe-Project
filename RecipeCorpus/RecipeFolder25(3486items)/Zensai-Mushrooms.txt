Title: Zensai Mushrooms
Categories: Side dish, Mushroom, Sthrn/livng, Asian
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Mushrooms, fresh; cleaned
-and stemmed
Carrot curls
Parsley
2/3 c  Vinegar, wine
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Soy sauce
1/4 ts Salt
Combine vinegar, sugar, soy sauce, and salt.  Pour over mushrooms;
marinate about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Remove from
marinade, and drain on paper towels.  Before serving, garnish with
carrot curls and parsley.
