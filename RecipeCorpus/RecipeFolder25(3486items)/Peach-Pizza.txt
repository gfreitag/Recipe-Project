Title: Peach Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Fruit, Cheese/eggs, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
-time dessert is
Heavenly tasting.
Impressive yet quick and
-easy to make, this party
MMMMM-------------------PREP TIME: 20 MINUTES------------------------
MMMMM---------------------BAKING: 15 MINUTES--------------------------
MMMMM-----------------------CHILL: 1 HOUR----------------------------
28 oz (2-14oz cans) canned sliced
-peaches in juice, drained
1/2 c  Apricot jam
1/4 c  Sliced almonds, toasted
1/2 pk Frozen puff pastry, thawed
1 ea Lkg 250g light cream cheese
1/3 c  Granulated sugar
1 t  Grated lemon rind
1/2 c  Plain yogurt
Roll pastry on lightly floured board to fit a 12 inch round pizza pan.
Place in pan and prick with a fork.  Bake at 425F for 8-10 minutes or
until lightly browned.  Cool.  Beat cream cheese, sugar and lemon
rind until smooth.  Blend in yogurt.  Spread evenly over crust,
leaving a 1/2 inch border.  Place drained peaches on paper towels to
dry thoroughly. Arrange on top of filling.  Melt apricot jam and
brush over fruit. Chill about 1 hour.  Garnish with almonds before
serving. Origin: Canadian Reader's Digest, October, 1993 Shared by:
Sharon Stevens
