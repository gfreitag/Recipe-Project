Title: Hot Green Pepper Jelly
Categories: Appetizer, Can/preserv, Chilies
Yield: 3 servings
1    Bottle of Certo
3 tb Cayenne pepper
1 c  Green pepper
6 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Cider vinegar
Remove seeds from the green peppers.  Grind in your blender.  Combine
with sugar and vinegar and bring to a full boil.  Continue cooking 10
minutes. Cool for 5 to 10 minutes.  Add Certo and Cayenne pepper.
Cool. Add several drops of green food coloring for dark green color.
Pour into prepared jars; seal with paraffin.  Serve as appetizers.
Spread Ritz crackers with cream cheese, then jelly. Randy Rigg
