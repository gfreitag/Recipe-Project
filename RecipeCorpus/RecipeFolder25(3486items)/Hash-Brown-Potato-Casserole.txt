Title: Hash Brown Potato Casserole
Categories: Potato, Cheese/eggs, Casserole, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 t  Salt
1/2 t  Garlic salt
1 c  Corn flakes
1/4 c  Melted margarine
1 ea (32oz) package frozen hash
-brown potatoes
1/2 c  Melted margarine
8 oz Shredded cheddar cheese
1 c  Onions, chopped
1 pt Sour cream
Parially defrost hash browns.  Mix potatoes, 1/2 cup margarine,
cheese, onions, sour cream, soup, and spices.  Put into greased 9x13
inch dish. Sprinkle corn flakes on top of potatoes, pour the 1/4 cup
margarine over corn flakes.  Bake uncovered in 350F oven for 1 hour
and 15 minutes. Origin: Women's Circle, for cooks on the go. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens.
