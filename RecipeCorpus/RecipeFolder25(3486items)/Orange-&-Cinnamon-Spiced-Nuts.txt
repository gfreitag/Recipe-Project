Title: Orange & Cinnamon Spiced Nuts
Categories: Holiday, Snack
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-----------------------MAKES: 6 CUPS----------------------------
1/4 t  Nutmeg
pn Salt
2 ea Egg whites
1 ea Tb grated orange peel
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
2 c  Whole blanched almonds
1 1/2 c  Pecan halves
1 1/2 c  Filberts
1 c  Sugar
1/4 t  Cinnamon
Heat oven to 325~. Spread nuts in a 15X10X1" jelly roll pan. Bake,
stirring occasionally, 20-25 minutes or until lightly toasted. In
small bowl stir together sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt. In small
mixer bowl beat egg whites at high speed until soft peaks form;
gradually add sugar mixture and continue to beat until stiff peaks
form. By hand, fold in nuts and orange peel. In same jelly roll pan
melt butter in oven. Spread nut mixture over butter. Bake, stirring
every 10 minutes or so, for 25-30 minutes or until nuts are brown and
no butter remains. Cool completely. Store in airtight container.
