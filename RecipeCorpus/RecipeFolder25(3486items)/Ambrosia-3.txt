Title: Ambrosia 3
Categories: Salad, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Orange juice
1/2 c  Sugar
3 lg Seedless oranges
3 md Bananas
1/2 pk Flaked coconut
Peel oranges and bananas. Section oranges over bowl (to catch extra
juice), slice bananas into  quarter inch slices. Mix with the
coconut, orange juice and sugar.  Allow to stand in refrigerator
several hours before serving. Submitted By DALE SHIPP   On   12-16-94
