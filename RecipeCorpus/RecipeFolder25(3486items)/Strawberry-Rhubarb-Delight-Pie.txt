Title: Strawberry-Rhubarb Delight Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Egg yolks, beaten
1 tb Flour
1 tb Butter
8 oz Frozen rhubarb
8 oz Frozen strawberries
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Water (or 1/2c)
Stir all ingredients together.  Bring to a boil for a few minutes.
Place in unbaked 9 inch pie crust.  Bake at 400 degrees for 35 to 40
minutes. Remove from oven and cover with a meringue topping and cook
until golden. (Jenneifer Pucilowski, Abilene, TX)
