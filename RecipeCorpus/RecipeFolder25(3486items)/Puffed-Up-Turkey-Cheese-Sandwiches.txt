Title: Puffed-Up Turkey-Cheese Sandwiches
Categories: Poultry, Sandwich, Turkey, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1/3 c  Fat-free mayonnaise;
2 sl Whole wheat bread; toasted
-On one side
1    Egg white;
2 ts Parmesan cheese;
2/3 c  Turkey; cooked chopped
3 tb Celery;
1 tb Sweet green peppers; chopped
1 tb Apples; chopped
1/4 c  Fat-free egg substitute;
Combine the turkey, celery, onions, pepper, apples, egg substitute, 1
tb of the cheese and the mayonnaise.  Place bread on the baking sheet,
toasted-side down.  Spread the untoasted side with the turkey
mixture. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.  Meanwhile, beat the egg white
until stiff but not dry.  Fold the remaining cheese into the egg
white, and top each sandwich with a dollop of the mixture.  Sprinkle
each wtih Parmesan. Increase the oven temperature to 450 degrees and
bake until the topping is puffy and golden brown.  Servings: 2. Pre
serving: 237 cal; 6.4g fat; 47mg; chol; 658mg sod; Source: The
