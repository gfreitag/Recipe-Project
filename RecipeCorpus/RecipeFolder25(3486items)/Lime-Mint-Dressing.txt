Title: Lime Mint Dressing
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 1 cup
2 tb Peanut or vegetable oil
5 t  Sugar
1 tb Chopped mint leaves
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
3 tb Fish sauce
-OR
3 tb Wite wine Worcestershire
-sauce
In a jar with tight-fitting lid, combine lime juice, fish sauce, oil,
sugar, mint, garlic and salt. Shake until sugar dissolves. Makes 3/4
cup. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   11-25-94
