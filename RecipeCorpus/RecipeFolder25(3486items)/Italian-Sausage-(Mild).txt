Title: Italian Sausage (Mild)
Categories: Sausage, Italian, Pork, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 t  Crushed hot pepper
1 1/2 t  Black Pepper
1 c  Water
5 lb Coarse ground pork butt
5 t  Salt
5 t  Fennel Seed
Combine all ingredients, mix well and stuff into hog casing or make
patties. To cook, fry or bake. SOURCE: The Sausage Making Cookbook, by
Jerry Predika Collection of Clarence Fontish
