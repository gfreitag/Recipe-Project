Title: Chili Cheese Ball
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Chili, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 ball
1/2 ts Garlic powder;
1 ds Salt;
Chili powder;
2 lb Velvetta cheese;
2 pk (8 oz) cream cheese;
1 c  Nuts; chopped
Place velveeta cheese and cream cheese in a large bowl; let to come
to room temperature.  Mix cheese well.  Add nuts, garlic powder and
salt; mix well. Shape into ball; roll in chili powder.  Wrap in foil;
chill until firm. Source: The Holiday Cookbook; Favorite Recipes of
