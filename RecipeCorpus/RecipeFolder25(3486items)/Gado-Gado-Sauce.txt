Title: Gado Gado Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
2 tb Soy sauce
2 lg Garlic cloves; minced
1/2 ts Lemon juice; freshly squeezd
1/2 c  Peanut butter
3/4 c  Water; hot; to 1 cup
1 ts Red chili pepper; crushed
2 tb Molasses, dark
Combine peanut butter and hot water in small saucepan.  Stir in chili
pepper, molasses, soy sauce, garlic, and lemon juice; simmer 10
minutes. This is excellent as a dip or can be served over vegetables.
