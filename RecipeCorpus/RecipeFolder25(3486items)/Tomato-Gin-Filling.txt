Title: Tomato Gin Filling
Categories: Vegetable, Alcohol, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Minced pickled jalapeno
-peppers
1/2 t  Grated lime rind
pn EACH, salt, pepper and sugar
6 sm Cherry tomatoes, chopped
1 T  Finely chopped fresh
-coriander
1 T  Dry gin
Im medium bowl, combine all ingredients.  Let stand 30 minutes.  With
slotted spoon, divide evenly among 8 mini tart shells, serve at once.
Origin: Recipe Booklet that came in the mail. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Oct/94.
