Title: Outdoor Pilaf
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Rice
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Salt
ds Pepper
1 cn (8 1/2 oz) peas, undrained
1    Chicken-bouillion cube
1/2 c  Packaged precooked rice
1. In small saucepan, combine peas and bouillion cube; bring to
boiling. 2. Stir in rice; remove from heat. Cover and let stand for 5
minutes. 3. Add remaining ingredents. Toss gently until butter is
melted. Submitted By MICHELLE BRUCE   On   11-22-94
