Title: French Cabbage Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Pork, Potato, Vegetable, French
Yield: 8 servings
6 ea Parsley sprigs
1 ea Bay leaf
1/2 t  Ground thyme
1/2 t  Marjoram
2 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
2 ea Onions
2 ea Carrots, quartered
2 ea Turnips, peeled & chopped
Salt
3 c  Potatoes, peeled & chopped
1 lb Lean bacon or ham
;in 1 piece
3 qt Water
2 lb Cabbage, roughly sliced
6 ea Peppercorns, crushed
2 ea Celery stalks, sliced
1 1/2 c  Red or white beans, canned
;drained
Put potatoes and bacon in deep kettle.  Add water.  Bring to boil.
Simmer, covered, 2 hours, or until meat is tender.  Remove meat;
slice into serving pieces.  Skim off excess fat from soup.  Return
meat to soup.  Add beans. Season to taste with salt.  Heat
thoroughly.  Serve with French bread. Recipe in "A Taste of West
Virginia: A Book of Favorite Recipes." Leawood, KS: Circulation
Service, Inc., 1991.  Pg. 15.  Posted by Cathy Harned.
