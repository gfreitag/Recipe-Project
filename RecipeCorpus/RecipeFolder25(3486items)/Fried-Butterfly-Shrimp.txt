Title: Fried Butterfly Shrimp
Categories: Main dish, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
1    Egg
1 c  Up water
2    Rsp oil
1/2 ts Salt
-Oil for frying
2 lb Large shrimp, cleaned,
-leaving tail intact
1 c  Sifted flour
1/2 ts Sugar
-Pinch curry powder
Slit shrimp down back and spread apart butterfly fashion. Pull tail
thru slit. Combine flour, sugar, and curry powder. Add egg, water,
and 2 tsp oil; beat well. Dip shrimp in batter. Heat oil to 375 in
deep skillet or deep fryer. Fry, a few at a time, until golden brown.
Drain on paper towels.
