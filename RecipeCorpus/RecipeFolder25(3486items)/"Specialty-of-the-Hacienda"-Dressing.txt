Title: "Specialty of the Hacienda" Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
-(your choice)
1/2 c  Pace Picante Sauce
1/2 c  Bottled dressing
Mix together equal parts of Pace Picante Sauce and your favorite
fat-free, light or regular CREAMY bottled dressing - ranch, creamy
garlic, creamy Italian or creamy cucumber. Toss with green salads,
bean salads, pasta salads or coleslaw. Or drizzle over sliced
tomatoes, cucumbers or avocados or a combination of all three. This
version makes about 1 cup dressing. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI
