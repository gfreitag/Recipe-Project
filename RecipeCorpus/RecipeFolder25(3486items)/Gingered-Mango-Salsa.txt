Title: Gingered Mango Salsa
Categories: Fruit, Herbs/spice, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 sm Garlic clove, finely chopped
1 tb Fresh ginger, finely chopped
1 pn Salt
1/4    Habanero chile
-or-
1    Whole jalapeno
;seeded and finely chopped
3/4 ts Soy sauce
1 c  Mango, peeled and diced
Combine and mix prepared ingredients in a small bowl.  Salsa texture
should be coarse.
