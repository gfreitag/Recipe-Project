Title: Kris Kringles
Categories: Dessert, Holiday, Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 servings
6 c  Rice krispies
1 pk 6 oz semi/sweet choc chips
1 pk 6 oz butterscotch chips
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Light corn syrup
1 c  Peanut butter
Mix sugar and corn syrup in 3 qt saucepan. Boil over moderate heat,
remove. Stir in peanut butter and cereal.  Press into greased 13" x
9" pan. Melt choc chips and butterscotch chips in a double boiler
over hot, not boiling water.  Spread over cereal mix.  Chill for five
minutes, remove from pan, and cut. Francis R. (Sonny) Martin, Sr.
