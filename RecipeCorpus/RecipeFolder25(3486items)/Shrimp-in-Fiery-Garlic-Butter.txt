Title: Shrimp in Fiery Garlic Butter
Categories: Herbs/spice, Seafood, Main dish, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
Bolillos or crusty French
-bread
1 ea Heat oven to 400F. Shell
-and devein shrimp, leaving
-tails attached; rinse
And drain well.
2 ea Combine butter and oil in
-8- to 9-inch skillet; heat
-over medium heat
Until
1 1/2 lb Fresh medium shrimp, in
-shells
1/2 c  Butter
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
8 ea Cloves garlic, finely
-chopped
1 ea To 3 dried de arbol
-chilies, coarsely crumbled
1 T  Fresh lime juice
1/4 t  Salt
butter is melted and foamy. Add garlic, chilies, lime juice and salt;
cook and stif 1 minute. Remove from heat. 3. Spread shrimp in even
layer in shallow 2-quart gratin pan or baking dish or in shallow
2-cup gratin pans. Pour hot butter mixture over shrimp. 4. Bake
shrimp, stirring once or twice, just until opaque throughout and
sizzling hot, 10 to 12 minutes. (Do not overcook or shrimp will be
dry and tough.) Garnish with green onions; serve immediately. Use
Bolillos to  blot up butter sauce. Makes 4 servings
