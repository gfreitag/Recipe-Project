Title: Basic Baked Macaroni & Cheese
Categories: Pasta, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
2 1/2 c  Milk
2 c  (8 oz.) shredded sharp
-Cheddar
2 c  Uncooked elbow macaroni,
-cooked & drained
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1 ts Dry mustard
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In medium saucepan, melt butter; stir in
flour, mustard, & salt. Gradually stir in milk. Cook & stir until
mixture thickens slightly (should coat the spoon). Remove from heat.
Add 1 1/2 cup cheese; stir until melted. Stir in cooked macaroni.
Turn into greased 1 1/2 qt. shallow baking dish. Top with remaining
cheese. Bake 20-25 minutes or until bubbly. 6- 8 servings. From: Earl
