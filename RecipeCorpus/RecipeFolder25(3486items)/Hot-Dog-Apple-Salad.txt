Title: Hot Dog Apple Salad
Categories: Kids, Salad, Main dish, Hot dogs, Fruit
Yield: 6 grandsons
1 ts Celery seed;
1/4 c  Parsley; finely chopped
1 c  Dairy sour cream;
1/2 ts Salt;
1 ts Prepared mustard;
6    Lettuce cups;
2 c  Elbow macaroni; cooked
-according to package
-directions
6    Hot dog;
4 md -sized McIntosh Apples;
2 tb Lemon juice;
4    Stacks pascal celery;
Drian the macaroni and set aside.  Cut the hot dogs up into 1/4"
cubes. Add the macaroni and mix well.  Peel and core the apples.  Cut
into 1/2" cubes. Drench with lemon juice and set aside for 5 minutes;
then add to the marconi mixture.  Cut the celery diagonally into
1/16" slices. Add to the macaroni mixture.  Add the chopped onion,
celery seed and the parsley. Mix all well.  In a separate bowl, mix
the sour cream, salt and mustard until well blended. Pour this
mixture over the hot dog-mixture. Turn the mixture over several times
until all is well mixed and evenly flavored. Chill in the
refrigerator for at least 2 hours.  Serve in lettuce cups. Source:
