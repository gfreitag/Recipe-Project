Title: Salad with Raspberry Vinaigrette
Categories: Salad
Yield: 8 servings
Mixed salad greens (bite-
-size pieces)
Thinly sliced onion
PRECIOUS Mozzarella Cheese,
-shredded
1/4 c  STAR Red Wine Vinegar
1/4 c  Strained pureed raspberries
1 tb Honey
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 t  Dried oregano leaves,crushed
1/2 c  STAR Olive Oil
In a small bowl mix vinegar, raspberries, honey, garlic and oregano.
Gradually whisk in oil. Place greens and onion in large bowl. Toss
with enough dressing to coat. Reserve remaining dressing for another
use. Sprinkle salad with mozzarella cheese.
