Title: Pizza Pancakes
Categories: Snack, Meat, Cheese/eggs, Main dish, Pizza
Yield: 14 pancakes
1/2 c  Pepperoni; chopped
1/2 c  Tomato; chopped
1/4 c  Green bell pepper,
-chopped
2 ts Italian seasoning
2 c  Bisquick baking mix;
-original
1 c  Milk;
2    Eggs;
1/2 c  Mozzarella cheese; (2 oz)
-shredded
Heat griddle or skillet; grease if necessary.  Beat baking mix, milk,
and eggs with wire whisk or hand better until well blended.  Stir in
remaining ingredients.  (batter will be thick.)  Pour batter by scant
1/4 cups onto hot griddle, spread slightly.  Cook until edges are
dry. Turn; cook until golden until golden brown.  Serve topped with
warm pizza or spaghetti sauce, grated Parmesan cheese, pepperoni,
tomato and bell pepper if desired. CAL: 120 (1 PANCAKE).  PRO: 4g;
CAR: 12; FAT: 6g; SOD: 370mg; Source: Bisquick Makes it Eazy, by WHOM
