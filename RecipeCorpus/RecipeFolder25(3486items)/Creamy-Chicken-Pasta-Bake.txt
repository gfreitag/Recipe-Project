Title: Creamy Chicken Pasta Bake
Categories: Chicken, Pasta, Low-cal/fat, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
-(8 oz.), shredded, divided
8 oz Spinach OR plain fettuccini
-cooked, drained
6    Chicken breast halves
-boneless, skinless
-about 2 lb.
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
1 cn Condensed cream of mushroom
-with reduced fat soup
-10-3/4 oz. can
1 c  Fat Free Sour Cream
-KNUDSEN FREE
1/2 c  Chopped red pepper
1 pk Part skim Mozzarella cheese
-KRAFT 1/3 Less Fat Natural
1. Heat oven to 350-degrees F.

2. Mix soup, sour cream, pepper and 1 cup of the cheese.  Toss with
pasta. Spoon into 13x9-inch baking dish.

3. Arrange chicken breasts over pasta mixture.  Sprinkle with
remaining 1 cup cheese and seasoning.

4. Bake 40 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.  Makes 6
servings.

Nutritional Information: 480 Calories, 12g Total fat, 115mg
Cholesterol.

From an insert in a carton of Knudsen Free Fat Free Sour Cream. APB
