Title: Corn-Nutmeg Miniature Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1 ea Egg, beaten
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Salad oil
1 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 c  Flour
1 1/2 t  Ground nutmeg
1/4 c  Sugar
4 t  Baking powder
Combine cornmeal, flour nutmeg, sugar, baking powder and salt.  Add
egg, milk and oil; beat about 1 minute or until smooth. Fill lightly
greased miniature muffin pans 3/4 full.  Bake at 425 for 10-12
minutes. Remove from pan while warm.
