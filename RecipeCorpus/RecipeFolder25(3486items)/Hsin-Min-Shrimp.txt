Title: Hsin-Min Shrimp
Categories: Appetizer, Asian, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Lemon Juice
1    Garlic;crushed;clove
1/4 c  Peanut Oil
1 lb Shrimp;raw
1/2 c  Soy Sauce
1/2 c  Sherry
Peel and de-vein shrimp. Mix ingredients well, pour over shrimp and
refrigerate 4 to 6 hours.
Remove shrimp and broil about 2 minutes each side.
