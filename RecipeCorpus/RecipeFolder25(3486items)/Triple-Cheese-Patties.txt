Title: Triple Cheese Patties
Categories: Meat, Ground beef, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 patties
1/4 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Garlic salt
6 tb Creamed cottage cheese
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
8 oz Tomato sauce
6 sl Mozzarella cheese; or Swiss
-3x3 inches
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Bread crumbs; dry
1 sm Onion; chopped (1/4 cup)
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Basil leaves
Mix meat, bread crumbs, onion, egg, and seasonings.  Shape into 12
thin patties, about 4 inches in diameter.

Top each of 6 patties with 1 tablespoon cottage cheese, spreading
within 1/2 inch of edge; sprinkle with 2 teaspoons Parmesan cheese.
Cover each with a remaining patty, sealing edges firmly.

Brown patties in large skillet over medium-high heat, turning once.
Drain off fat.  Pour tomato sauce over patties; cover and simmer 15
minutes. Place a cheese slice on each patty; cover and heat until
cheese in melted, about 2 minutes.  Serve sauce over patties
