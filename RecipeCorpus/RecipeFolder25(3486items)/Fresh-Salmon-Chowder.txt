Title: Fresh Salmon Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Water
-Salt and freshly ground
-pepper to taste
1 1/2 lb Fresh salmon fillets
1 c  Milk
2 md Onions, sliced
2    Carrots, sliced
2    Stalks celery wit tops,
-diced
3    Potatoes, peeled and cubed
Bring onion, carrots, celery and potatoes to a boil with water, salt,
and pepper; reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes. Add salmon amd milk to
saucepan. Cook over low heat for 20 minutes or until fish is done.
Serve.
