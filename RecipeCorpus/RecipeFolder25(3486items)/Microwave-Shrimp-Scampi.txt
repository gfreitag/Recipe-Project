Title: Microwave Shrimp Scampi
Categories: Microwave, Main dish, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 t  Salt
Paprika
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
2 T  Lemon juice
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 lb Medium shrimp, shelled and
-cleaned
2 ea Cloves garlic, finely
-chopped
1 T  Parsley flakes
Place butter and garlic in 8 inch round dish. Cook on High 3 to 4
minutes or until butter is melted. Add lemon juice, parsley and salt
stir in shrimp. Cover with plastic wrap. Cook at Medium 6 to 7
minutes, or until shrimp is tender stir once. Let stand, covered, 5
minutes sprinkle with paprika before serving. Yield: 4 servings
