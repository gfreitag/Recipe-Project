Title: Chocolate Surprise Truffles
Categories: Candy, Chocolate
Yield: 36 truffles
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/4 c  Whipping cream
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Unsalted butter (1 stick);
;softened
2 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
MMMMM--------------------------CENTERS-------------------------------
Whole almonds, pecan or
-walnut halves
After-dinner mints
Whole candied cherries
MMMMM--------------------------COATING-------------------------------
Chopped nuts
Cocoa
Powdered sugar
Flaked coconut
In large mixer bowl, beat butter. Stir together powdered sugar and
cocoa; add to butter mixture alternately with whipping cream and
vanilla, blending well. Refrigerate until firm. Shape small amount of
mixture around desired center; roll into 1-inch balls. Drop into
desired coating, turning until well covered. Refrigerate until firm.

Variation: Add 1/2 ts rum extract, decrease vanilla to 1 ts.
