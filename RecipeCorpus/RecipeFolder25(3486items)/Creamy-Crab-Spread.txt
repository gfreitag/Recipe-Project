Title: Creamy Crab Spread
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizer, Seafood, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------MAKES: 1-------------------------------
2 t  Lemon juice
1/8 t  Onion powder
6 oz Crab, coarsely chopped
Fancy crackers
8 ea 0z tub light cream cheese,
-softened
1/4 c  Light mayonnaise
2 ea Tb ketchup
1 ea Tb horseradish
Combine all ingredients except crab, blend until smooth. Gently fold
in crab. Cover and refrigerate at least one hour before serving.
Carolyn's note: The only nutrition information I wrote on this one
was that it has about 1 gram of fat per tablespoon... Source: hand
