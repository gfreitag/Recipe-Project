Title: Stuffed Figs and Dates
Categories: Snack, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
Unsweetened shredded
-- coconut
Figs
Pitted dates
Shelled walnut halves
Cut out the centre of each fig & place a walnut half inside.  Cut
open the adtes & do the same.  Roll each stuffed fruit in shredded
coconut. Make as many as you want.
