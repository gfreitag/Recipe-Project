Title: Low Fat/low Cal Chicken Cacciatore
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Low-cal/fat, Chicken, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
Dash of pepper
2 md Whole chicken breasts,
Skinned, boned & split
Paprika
2 t  Cornstarch
2 tb Cold water
4 oz Spaghetti, cooked
1/2 cn (7-1/2 oz) tomatoes
3/4 c  Sliced fresh mushrooms
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper, chopped
3 tb Dry red wine
1    Garlic
1/2 t  Dried oregano, crushed
1/4 t  Salt
In a medium skillet cut up the undrained tomatoes.  Add
:
mushrooms, onions, green peper, wine, garlic, oregano, salt & pepper.
Place chicken pieces on the tomato mixture in skillet.  Bring to a
boil; reduce heat; cover and simmer for 25 minutes.  Keep warm.
Combine constarch & cold water; stir into a skillet mixture.
:
Cook and stir until mixture is thickend bubly.  Cook and stir 2
minutes more. Arrange chken and spagetti on platter; spoon sauce over
