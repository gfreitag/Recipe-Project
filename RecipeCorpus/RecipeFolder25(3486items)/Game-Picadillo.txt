Title: Game Picadillo
Categories: Game, Pork, Main dish, Vegetable, Beef
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------MAKES:  6 SERVINGS--------------------------
2 T  Ground cummin
4 c  Water
1 c  Chopped Onion
1/2 c  Celery
1/2 c  Bell pepper
4 c  Potatoes, cut into cubes
2 c  Frozen corn kernels
1 lb Ground venison
1 lb Ground beef or pork
3 T  Flour
6 oz Tomato paste
2 T  Salt Or To Taste
1 T  Black pepper
1 1/2 T  Granulated garlic
Place venison, beef or pork in large deep pot or Dutch oven over
medium heat.  Cook until meat is brown; drain any excess fat. 2. Stir
in flour, tomato paste, salt, pepper, garlic and ground cumin to coat
meat evenly.  Add water and vegetables.  Bring liquid to a boil,
reduce heat and simmer for about 45 minutes.  Serve over rice, it
desired. Origin: Kelowna Daily Courier Shared by: Bob Shiells
