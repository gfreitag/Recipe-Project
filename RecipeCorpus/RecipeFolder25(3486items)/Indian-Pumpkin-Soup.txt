Title: Indian Pumpkin Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Native amer, Pumpkin
Yield: 6 servings
ds Salt
1 c  Milk or light cream
1 tb Curry powder or to taste
ds Fresh or dried mint
2 1/2 c  Mashed cooked pumpkin
3 c  Water (approximately)
1 tb Chopped onion
1 tb Shortening
Saute onion until transparent.

Combine pumpkin and water, stir to form thin puree.  Add onion.  Boil
for three minutes.  Add milk or cream, salt to taste and curry
powder. Heat through, stirring constantly.

Sprinkle mint over the top before serving if desired.
