Title: Baked Halibut
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Canned tomatoes
1/2 ts Sugar
1 md Onion, thinly sliced
1/2 c  Heavy whipping cream
4    Halibut steaks
1 ts Salt
-Freshly ground pepper to
-taste
3 tb Butter, melted
Preheat oven to 400. Place halibut in buttered baking dish; sprinkle
with salt and pepper. Brush with melted butter. Crush tomatoes with
sugar; spread over fish. Cover with onion. Bake for 20 minutes. Pour
cream over fish; bake for 10 minutes more.
