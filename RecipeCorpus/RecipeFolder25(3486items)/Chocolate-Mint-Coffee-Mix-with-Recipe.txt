Title: Chocolate Mint Coffee Mix with Recipe
Categories: Mixes, Beverage
Yield: 3 servings
-COFFEE:
1 1/2 tb Mix
6 oz Boiling water
-Whipped Cream
-Candy Cane
3/4 c  Nondairy powdered creamer
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Instant coffee
1/4 c  Cocoa
6    Peppermint candies, crushed
Mix: In blender, process all ingredients until candies are pulverized.
Store in an airtight container.

Chocolate Mint Coffee: Pour boiling water over mix in cup. Garnish
with whipped cream and stir with candy cane.
