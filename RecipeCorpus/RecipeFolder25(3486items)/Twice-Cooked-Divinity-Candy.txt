Title: Twice-Cooked Divinity Candy
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 servings
1    Dash salt
2    Stiffly beaten egg whites
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Corn syrup
1/2 c  Water
Stir sugar into syrup, water and salt until dissolved; boil to medium
ball stage or 240-degrees. Slowly pour 1/3 of mixture over egg
whites, beating constantly. Cook remaining syrup to very hard ball
stage or 265 degrees. Beat syrup into candy mixture. Continue beating
until mixture holds its shape; stir in vanilla. Drop from teaspoon
onto greased cookie sheet; swirl each piece to a peak.
