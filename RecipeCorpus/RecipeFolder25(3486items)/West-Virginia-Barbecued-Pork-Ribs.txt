Title: West Virginia Barbecued Pork Ribs
Categories: Pork, Bbq/grill, Meat, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 T  Garlic salt
1 T  Celery salt
1 T  Chili powder
1/2 t  Ground allspice
1/2 t  Ground ginger
1/2 t  Ground cloves
1/2 t  Dry mustard
1/2 c  Drained sweet pickle relish
6 lb Fresh pork ribs
4 ea Onions, chopped
2 c  Water
2 ea Beef bouillon cubes
1/2 c  Dark brown sugar
1 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1 lb Tomato puree
Brown ribs in heavy kettle.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and
simmer for 2 hours, or until tender.  Makes 8 servings. Origin:
Woman's Day Encyclopedia of Cookery, Volume 1, West Virginia Section.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, May/94.
