Title: California Mashed Potatoes
Categories: Vegetable, Potato, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
-(optional)
1    Envelope Onion Soup Mix
-Lipton Recipe Secrets
3/4 c  Milk; heated to boiling
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 lb All-purpose potatoes
-peeled, if desired
-and cut into chunks
Water
2 tb Chopped fresh parsley
In 3-quart saucepan, cover potatoes with water.  Bring to a boil over
high heat.  Reduce heat to low and simmer 20 minutes or until
potatoes are very tender; drain.  Return potatoes to saucepan.  Mash
potatoes. Stir in parsley and onion soup mix blended with hot milk
and sour cream.

Makes about 8 servings.

From a Lipton advertisement, USA Weekend magazine, November 18-20,
