Title: Sausage and Pineapple Tidbits
Categories: Appetizer, Sausage, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1    Stick of margarine
1 ds Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Lemon juice
1 ts Mustard
1 pk Ecrich smoky links
1 cn Large pineapple slices
1 c  Pineapple juice
1    Box of brown sugar
1 ds Hot sauce
Cut each sausage into 8 pieces.  Cut each pineapple slice into 7 or 8
pieces.  Alternate 2 sausage and 1 pineapple and 2 pineapple and 1
sausage on toothpicks.  In a saucepan, bring to a boil the remaining
ingredients and add sausage and pineapple toothpicks.  Boil for a
couple of minutes, remove from heat and let sit for 2 to 3 hours.
Before serving reheat and serve warm.  A fondue pot is real nice or a
crockpot, to keep your tidbits warm. Randy Rigg
