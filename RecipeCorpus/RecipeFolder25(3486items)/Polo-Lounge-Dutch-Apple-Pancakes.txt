Title: Polo Lounge Dutch Apple Pancakes
Categories: Breakfast, Kids, Pancakes
Yield: 6 grandsons
1/4 ts Almond extract;
4 md Apples; cut into thin wedges
1 tb To 2 tb cinnamon;
2 tb Powdered sugar;
2 c  Milk;
1 c  Flour;
6    Eggs;
1/4 c  Granulated sugar + 3 tbs
-(divided use)
Combine milk, flour, eggs, 3 tb granulated sugar and almond extract
in a bowl.  Mix to blend well.  Pour into hot, well-greased 10"
skillet, tilting pan so that batter forms collar around sides of pan.
Arrange apple wedges in layer over batter.  Combine remaining 1/4 c
granulated sugar and cinnamon.  Sprinkle evenly over apples.  Bake a
350 degrees until batter is set and surface is browned, about 45
minutes. Sprinkle with powdered sugar. Serve with jams and preserves
if desired. Source: The San Diego Union-Trubine Food Section, Nov. 3,
1994 and Beverly Hills Hotel Brought to you and yours via Nancy
O'Brion and her Meal-Master on behalf of our grandsons.
