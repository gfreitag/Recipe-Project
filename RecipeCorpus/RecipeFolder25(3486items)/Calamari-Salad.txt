Title: Calamari Salad
Categories: Seafood, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Lemon Peel shredded
1/4 c  Olive Oil
2 ea Clove Garlic Crushed
1/2 t  Oregano
1/2 lb Mushrooms, sliced thin
1 lb Cleaned Squid
1 md Onion slice thin
1/4 c  Lemon Juice
1/2 t  Basil
1/2 t  Rosemary
Salt and Pepper to taste
Cook squid with lemon peel and onion 5 minutes. Drain and chill.
Combine oil, lemon juice and spices and pour over mushrooms. Chill
several hours. Combine squid and mushrooms, toss. Serve on salad
greens. POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 10/94
