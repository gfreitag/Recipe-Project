Title: Maple Cream
Categories: Candy, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Butter
3/4 c  Pecan meats cut into small
-pieces
1/2 t  Vanilla
1/2 c  Sweetened condensed milk
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Granulated sugar
3/4 c  Maple sugar
Blend milk, water, sugar and syrup, dissolve thoroughly, heat slowly
to boiling point, cook slowly to soft ball stage when tested in cold
water. Remove from fire, drop in butter and flavoring, do not stir.
When lukewarm beat until creamy, ad nuts, spread in buttered pan.
