Title: Chilled Shrimp Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Appetizer, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Prepared mustard
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
4 c  Buttermilk
1 md Cucumber, peeled, seeded,
-and finely diced
3/4 c  Cooked shrimp, chopped
1 tb Fresh dill -or-
1 ts Dried dill
Combine all ingredients; mix well. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or
until well chilled. Serve cold.
