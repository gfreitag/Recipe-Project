Title: Sweet Potato Pecan Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Vegetable, Nuts/grains, Sweetpotato
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Salt
1 1/2 c  Scalded milk
2 ea Well beaten eggs
1 1/2 c  Cooked mashed sweet potatoes
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 t  Ground ginger
1 t  Ground cinnamon
Beat all ingredients together.  Pour in unbaked pie shell and bake
for 20 minutes in 350 F. oven.  Then sprinkle on topping and continue
to bake until set; about 25 minutes longer. Topping: 3/4 cup chopped
pecans 1/2 stick butter 1/4 cup brown sugar Mix until crumbly. From:
