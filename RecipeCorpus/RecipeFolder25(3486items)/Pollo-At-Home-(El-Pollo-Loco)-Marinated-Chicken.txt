Title: Pollo At Home (El Pollo Loco) Marinated Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Main dish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
Color (powder found at
-Mexican markets)
1/4 t  Ground cumin
2 t  Lemon juice
3 lb Chicken pieces
1/4 c  Corn oil
1/4 c  Melted butter
1/2 t  Onion powder
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1 t  Annatto powder or a few
-drops yellow food
Combine all except chicken in a shallow pan.  Add chicken, turning to
coat. Cover and marinate several hours or overnight.  Remove chicken
from marinade, cook over medium coals on BBQ grill or 4" from source
of heat under broiler, until browned on both sides and done, turning
and basting frequently, about 30-45 minutes. Serves: 4 From: Judy
