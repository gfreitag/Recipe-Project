Title: Stuffed Game Hens
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Game, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Sherry
4 tb Butter;melted
Salt;
Pepper;
4    Game Hens;
1/2    WILD RICE STUFFING;recipe
1/2 c  Dried Apricots;chopped
1/2 c  Pecans;chopped
Prepare WILD RICE STUFFING, omitting the olives. When cool, add
apricots, pecans, and sherry.
Pat game hens dry inside and out, sprinkle with salt and pepper,
stuff with rice mixture.
Place hens in a pre-heated 375 degree oven and bake 45 minutes to an
hour, basting frequently with melted butter and pan juices.
