Title: Sweetly Spiced Walnuts
Categories: Appetizer, Nuts/grains, Holiday
Yield: 5 cups
2 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Salt
5 c  Walnut halves
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Water
2 ts Ground cinnamon
Combine first 6 ingredients in a 3 quart casserole, stirring well.
Cover with wax paper and microwave on High for 4 minutes; stir well,
cover and microwave on High for 3 1/2 to 4 1/2 minutes or until
mixture reaches soft ball stage (234 degrees) or until a small amount
of mixture dropped into cold water forms a soft ball but flattens
when removed from water. Add walnuts; stir until coated.  Spread
walnuts on wax paper separating with a fork.  Let stand until cool
and dry. Store in an airtight container
