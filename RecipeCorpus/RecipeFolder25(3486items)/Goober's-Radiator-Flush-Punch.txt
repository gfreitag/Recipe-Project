Title: Goober's Radiator Flush Punch
Categories: Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 5 servings
Juice of 1 lemon
1 c  Ginger ale
Orange and lemon slices
2 c  Prepared tea
Juice of 2 oranges
12 oz Can orange soda
In a pitcher combine all of the ingredients.  Serve over ice.  This
is best when served with comic books.  Makes 5 cups. Submitted by:
Bill Flynn - High Point, North Carolina Origin: Aunt Bee's Mayberry
Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
