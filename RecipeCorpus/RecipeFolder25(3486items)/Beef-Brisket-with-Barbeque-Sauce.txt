Title: Beef Brisket with Barbeque Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Crockpot, Main dish, Meat, Beef
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Red pepper; ground
2    Garlic cloves; minced
2 1/2 lb Beef brisket
1/2 c  Catsup
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Margarine; or butter
3/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Vinegar
2 ts Beef bouillon; granules
1 ts Mustard; dry
1 ts Chili powder
For cooking liquid, in a bowl combine water, worcestershire sauce,
vinegar, bouillon, mustard, chili powder, red pepper, and garlic;
reserve 1/2 c. liquid for sauce.  Trim fat from brisket.  If
necessary, cut brisket to fit crockery.  Cover; cook on low-heat
setting for 10-12 hours or high-heat setting for 5-6 hours.

For sauce, in a small sauce pan combine 1/2 c reserved liquid, catsup,
brown sugar, and margarine.  Heat through.  Pass sauce with meat.

Source- Better Homes & Gardens New Crockery Cooker Cookbook, page 15.
