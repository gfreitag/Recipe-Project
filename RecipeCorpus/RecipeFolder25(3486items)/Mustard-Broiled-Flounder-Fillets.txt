Title: Mustard Broiled Flounder Fillets
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
-Freshly ground pepper to
-taste
1 1/2 lb Flounder fillets
2 tb Oil
2 tb Mayonnaise
1 tb Dijon mustard
Preheat broiler. Place fish in a single layer on lightly oiled broiler
rack. Brush lightly with oil. Combine mayonnaise, mustard, parsley and
pepper; spread over fish. Broil 3-8 minutes or till topping is
browned and fish flakes easily when tested with fork. The cooking
time depends on the thickness of the fish. Place on heated platter;
serve atonce.
