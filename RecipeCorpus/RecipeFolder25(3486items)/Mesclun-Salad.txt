Title: Mesclun Salad
Categories: Salad, French, Appetizer, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
1 ts Dijon
Salt & pepper
1 lb Mesculun*
3/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1 tb Parsley, minced
1 tb Chives, minced
In a small bowl, whisk together the juice, vinegar, parsley, chives,
garlic, mustard, salt & pepper.  Set aside.

Arrange mesculun on individual salad plates.  Just before serving,
re-whisk the vinaigrette & dribble a tb or so on each serving.

*Mesculun is simply a generic name for an assortment of wild or baby
lettuces.  If it's not available, mix several varieties together
ensuring that you include arugula (rocket) or watercress for a
characteristic spicy taste.
