Title: Bouquet Garni Mix
Categories: French, Mixes, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sage
1/2 c  Rosemary
1/2 c  Parsley
1/2 c  Dried thyme
1/2 c  Bay leaves
Crush herbs. Make bundles using 1 tb mix wrapped in cheesecloth and
tied with twine. Store in an airtight container.

Source: The Perfect Mix by Diane Phillips ISBN 0-688-12104-7 Typed by
