Title: Anchovy Cheese Sauce for Vegetables
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 1 2/3 cups
1 c  Cheese, Cheddar; shredded
2 oz Anchovie fillets; drained
-and finely chopped
2 tb Butter (or marg.)
2 tb Flour, all-purpose
1 c  Milk; hot
Melt butter in saucepan; stir in flour very carefully so that it does
not lump.  Remove from heat, and gradually stir in milk.  Cook over
medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens.  Stir in
cheese and anchovies, reduce heat to low, and cook until cheese
melts. Serve over hot cooked spinach, cauliflower, or asparagus.
