Title: Curried Shrimp
Categories: Main dish, Curries, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Stock, chicken
2 c  Milk
1 ts Lemon juice; freshly squeezd
4 c  Shrimp; cooked
1/4 c  Butter (or marg.)
1/4 c  Onion flakes; crushed
1/3 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Salt
3 ts Curry powder
Melt butter, add crushed onion flakes, and cook until soft.  Stir in
flour and seasonings.  Add stock; cook until thick, stirring
constantly. Add milk, lemon juice, and shrimp.  Heat thoroughly.
