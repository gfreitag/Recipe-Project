Title: Gyoza (Crispy Won Ton)
Categories: Appetizer, Asian, Pork
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Salt
2 ts Soy sauce
1/2 ts MSG
1 pn Pepper (even less)
60    Gyoza skins(or wonton)
1/2 lb Ground pork
8    Water chestnuts
1 ts Garlic juice
1/4 c  Green onion; chopped
Peel chestnuts (if necessary) and mince. Combine pork, nuts, garlic
juice and onions. Mix well. Add soy sauce, msg, pepper. Spread skins
out and place 1 tsp. mix in the center. Fold and crimp. Deep fry
until crisp.
