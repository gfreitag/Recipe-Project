Title: Herbed Hummus with Pistachios
Categories: Appetizer, Middle east, Snack, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 cups
1/2 c  Cilantro, finely chopped
1/2 c  Pistachios
2 c  Hummus bi tahini
1/2 c  Watercress, finely chopped
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Pita wedges
Olive oil
Pistachio nuts
Place hummus in a bowl & fold in watercress & cilantro.  Taste to
correct seasonings.  Cover & chill in the refrigrator.  Before
serving, add chopped pistachios, drizzle with olive oil & garnish
with additional nuts. Serve with pita wedges.

Virginia Habeeb, "Pita the Great"
