Title: Mexican Fried Rice
Categories: Side dish, Bbq/grill, Tex/mexican, Stir fry, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Salsa
1/2 c  Chopped green bell pepper
1 sm Onion, chopped
1    Garlic clove, minced
3 tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Long-grain rice
1 pk Frozen rak shrimp, shelled
-and deveined (opt; 8 oz)
Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add rice; cook until
golden brown, stirring frequently. Stir in shrimp, salsa, bell
pepper, onion, garlic and 3 cups water. Bring mixture to a boil;
reduce heat to low. Cover; simmer 15-20 minutes or until rice is
tender. Season to taste; serve with additional salsa, if desired.
