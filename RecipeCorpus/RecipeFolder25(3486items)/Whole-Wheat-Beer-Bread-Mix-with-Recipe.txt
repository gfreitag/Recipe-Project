Title: Whole Wheat Beer Bread Mix with Recipe
Categories: Mixes, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
-Bread Mix
12 oz Can beer -OR-
12 oz Bottle unflavored seltzer
1/2 c  Butter, melted
2 1/2 c  Self rising flour
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
3 tb Sugar
-BREAD:
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Whole Wheat Beer Bread: Preheat oven to 375. Combine bread mix and
beer. Blend together using a wooden spoon. The batter will be lumpy.
