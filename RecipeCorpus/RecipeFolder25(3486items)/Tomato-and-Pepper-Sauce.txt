Title: Tomato and Pepper Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Tomato, Vegetable
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  Tomatoes
2 tb Sugar
3/4 c  Green pepper
3/4 c  Onions
Saute pepper and onions in small amount of shortening.  Heat through
but do not brown.  Add tomatoes and sugar, cover and cook for about 2
and 1/2 hours or until dark color.  Serve with any of your favorite
meats. Randy Rigg
