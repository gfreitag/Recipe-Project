Title: Crab & Spinach Supreme
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 12 servings
-Dash tabasco
1 lb Crambeat, flaked
2 pk Frozen chopped spinach(10
-oz), cooked and drained
1 c  Minced scallions
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1/2 c  Butter
1 tb Grated Parmesan
-Salt and pepper to taste
Saute scallion and garlic in butter; remove from pan. Combine with
remaining ingredients; mix well. Place in chafing dish; serve hot with
crackers or party bread
