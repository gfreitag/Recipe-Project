Title: My Spanish Rice
Categories: Ground beef, Rice, Tomato
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM--------------------MM BY HELEN PEAGRAM-------------------------
2/3 c  Rice, raw
1/2 sm Onion
1 lb Ground beef
1 cn Tomato soup (10 oz)
Brown onion in fat, add meat and brown.  Strain off any grease.  Add
tomato soup and 1 can water.  Add rice and simmer 45 minutes.  Serve
with vegetable of choice.
