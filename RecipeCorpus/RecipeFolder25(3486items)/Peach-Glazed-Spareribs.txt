Title: Peach Glazed Spareribs
Categories: Pork, Bbq/grill, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Ground ginger
4 lb Iowa spareribs
Pepper to taste
1 cn 16oz sliced peaches, drained
2 tb Soy sauce
1 ea Garlic glove, minced
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Catsup
Sieve peaches or puree in blender. Add remaining ingredients, except
ribs. Cut ribs in serving size portions, rub with additional salt and
pepper. Grill, bone side down, over slow coals for 20 minutes. Turn
again, grill 20 minutes brushing with peach mixture.
