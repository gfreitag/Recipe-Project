Title: Turkey Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Poultry, Turkey, Pasta, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Mushrooms;sliced
1/2 ts Basil
1    Bouillon;chicken;cube
3 c  Linguini;cooked
Salt;
Pepper;
1 c  WHITE SAUCE
2 1/2 c  Turkey;cooked
1 c  Celery;diced
1    Green Pepper;diced;
2 tb Olive Oil
1    Garlic;crushed;clove
1 1/2 c  Tomato Puree
Cube cooked turkey. Cook linguini according to directions.
Make WHITE SAUCE.
In medium saucepan heat oil, add garlic, celery, green pepper,mush-
rooms and cook until tender. Add tomato puree, bouillon, basil, salt
and pepper to taste, reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes.
In casserole dish spread half the WHITE SAUCE. Add half the
spaghetti and tomato mixture plus turkey. Repeat layers. Bake at 350
degrees for 30 minutes.
