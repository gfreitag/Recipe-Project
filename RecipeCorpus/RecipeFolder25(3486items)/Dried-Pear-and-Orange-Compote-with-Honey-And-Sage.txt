Title: Dried Pear and Orange Compote with Honey And Sage
Categories: Fruit, Herbs/spice, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 quart
-sliced very thin
2 c  Dried pears (12 oz)
-cut in 1/4" strips
2 tb Sage leaves; minced
2 tb Grand Marnier or other
-orange-flavored liqueur
1/2 c  Honey
1 lg Navel orange; ends removed
-fruit and zest
-sliced very thin
1/2    Lemon; ends removed
-fruit and zest
Bring honey and 2 cups water to boil in a large, heavy-bottomed
saucepan over medium heat, stirring frequently. Add orange, lemon and
pears; reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered, stirring often until
pears plump, about 20 minutes. Stir in sage and liqueur; simmer until
alcohol has evaporated, 1 to 2 minutes. cool, then refrigerate until
citrus peels soften, about 1 week. Serve chilled or at room
temperature.
