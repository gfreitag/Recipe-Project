Title: Maple Raspberry Dressing
Categories: Syrup, Salad, Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Grey Poupon Mustard
2 t  Tarragon Leaves, crumbled
1 t  Salt
4 T  Raspberry Vinegar
4 T  Olive Oil
4 T  Vegetable Oil
4 T  Maple Syrup
Combine all ingredients in a small jar and pour over greens or your
favorite salad. SOURCE: Jennie Wendt, Rome NY Published in Syracuse
