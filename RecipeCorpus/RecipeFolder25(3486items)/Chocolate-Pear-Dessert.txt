Title: Chocolate Pear Dessert
Categories: Diabetic, Dessert, Fruit, Chocolate, Pudding
Yield: 8 sweet ones
1/2 ts Orange extract -=OR=-
1/2 ts Almond extract;
4    Fully ripe pears; halved
2 ts Walnuts; chopped finely-=OR=
2 ts Almonds; chopped finely;
2 c  Cold skim milk;
1 pk Jell-O chocolate flavor
-fat-free Sugar Instant
-Reduced Calorie Pudding &
-Pie Filling
Pour milk and extract in medium bowl.  Add pudding mix.  Beat with
wire whish 1 minute.  Let stand 5 minutes.  Place pear halves in 8
dessert dishes.  Spoon pudding evenly over pears.  Sprinkle with 1/4
ts walnut over each serving.  Refrigerate until ready to serve. Makes
servings. Food Exchange per serving: 1 STARCH EXCHANGE + 1/2  FRUIT
EXCHANGE; CAL: 100, FAT: 1g; CHO: 0mg; SOD: 180mg; CAR: 22g; PRO: 3g;
