Title: Apricot Chews
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 40 cookies
2/3 c  Sweetened condensed milk;
-NOT evaporated milk
2 c  Sweetened flaked coconut
1 pk Dried apricots (6 oz);
-finely chopped (1 cup)
1.  Heat oven to 350F.  Grease cookie sheets.

2.  Mix all ingredients until well blended.  Roll rounded
teaspoonfuls into balls.

3.  Place 1 inch apart on cookie sheets.  Bake 12 minutes or until
set.

4.  Remove to wire rack to cool.  Store in airtight containers up to 2
weeks.
