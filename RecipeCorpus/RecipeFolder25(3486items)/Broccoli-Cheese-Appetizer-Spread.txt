Title: Broccoli-Cheese Appetizer Spread
Categories: Appetizer, Sthrn/livng, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
-chopped
1/2 c  Parsley, fresh; chopped
1 tb Basil, dried
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Chili powder
10 oz Broccoli, chopped; thawed
-and drained
1 c  Mayonnaise
2/3 c  Cheese, Parmesan; grated
1/2 c  Chives, fresh or frozen;
Combine all ingredients in a lightly greased, 1-quart baking dish.

Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until thoroughly heated.  Spoon
into a serving bowl, if desired, and serve with crackers and turnips
or other vegetable sticks.
