Title: Chicken Chinoise
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Chinese, Stir fry, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chicken Stock
2 tb Cornstarch
6 tb Water;cold
2 tb Soy Sauce
1 tb Ginger;fresh, grated
tb Oil;peanut
3    Chicken Breast;skinned, bone
1/2 c  Celery;diced
1 c  Green Beans;blanched
1/2 c  Scallion;chopped
1 cn Water Chestnut;drained, chop
1 c  Mushrooms;chopped
Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces.
Heat oil to very hot in wok or large skillet; add chicken, cook 1
minute. Add the rest of the vegetables and the ginger, cook 2 more
minutes. Add the chicken stock.
Mix the cornstarch with the water, add to the chicken, stirring
constantly.
Add the soy sauce, cook 1 more minute.
Serve over rice.
