Title: Stuffed Lamb Chops
Categories: Main dish, Lamb, Meat
Yield: 1 servings
ds Salt
ds Pepper
1/2 c  Bread Crumbs
4 tb Lamb Chops;thick
4 tb Butter
1    Garlic;minced;clove
2 tb Parsley;chopped
Make a pocket in the thickest part of the chop. Mix remaining
ingredients, add salt and pepper to taste. Insert mixture in pocket;
secure chops with tooth picks.
Broil 8 minutes for rare, 14 minutes for medium rare, 18 minutes
for medium.
Variations: Omit parsley, add
tarragon or rosemary.
