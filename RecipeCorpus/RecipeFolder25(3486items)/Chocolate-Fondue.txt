Title: Chocolate Fondue
Categories: Chocolate, Salsa/sauce, Dessert
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM---------------------------
1/2 ts Vanilla or almond extract
1/2 c  Granulated white sugar twin
2/3 c  Dry unsweetened cocoa
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Skim milk
In a heavy saucepan, combine cocoa, cinnamon and milk; stir or whisk
until there are no dry lumps of cocoa. Stir and cook over medium heat
until mixture comes to a boil. Reduce heat; boilgently, stirring
often for 5 minutes or until mixture is thick and smooth. Cool
lsightly. Stir in vanilla and Sugar Twin. Pour into a small enamelled
fondue pot or heat-proof ceramic bowl. Makes 8 servings of 1/4 cup
each. Approximate calories:124/serving.
