Title: Bourbon or Rum Balls
Categories: Cake/cookie, Alcohol, Holiday, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 dozen
1/4 c  Bourbon *OR* Rum
1 1/2 tb Light corn syrup (karo)
Powdered sugar for rolling
1 c  Vanilla wafer crumbs
1 c  Finely chopped pecans
1 c  Powdered sugar
2 tb Cocoa
Mix crumbs, pecans, powdered sugar and cocoa; blend bourbon (or rum)
with the corn syrup.  Combine the two mixtures.

Shape into one inch balls, roll in the additional powdered sugar;
refrigerate.
