Title: Fruit Ambrosia
Categories: Salad, Fruit, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
2    Bananas; sliced
1/2 c  Coconut, flaked
Mint leaves
1 c  Orange sections
1 c  Grapefruit sections
1/2 lb Grapes, green; halved
Combine orange and grapefruit sections, grapes, and bananas.  Add
coconut and toss gently.  Garnish with mint leaves.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, March 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
