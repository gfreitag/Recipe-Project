Title: Crema De Abacate (Cream of Avocado Dessert) (Brazil)
Categories: So. america, Dessert, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Lime juice; fresh
6 tb Sugar, confectioners'
1/2    Lime; cut into 4 thin wedges
2 lg Avocados; ripe, chilled,
;peeled, halved, seeded and
;diced
With the back of a spoon, force the diced avocado through a sieve
set over a bowl.  Stir in the lime juice and sugar.  Serve from
chilled parfait glasses or dessert dishes, and garnish each serving
with a lime wedge.
