Title: Miss Allie's Broccoli and Rice Casserole with Cheese
Categories: Vegetable, Rice, Casserole, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Cream of chicken soup (10oz)
1 pk Cheese Whiz
2 1/2 c  Rice,cooked
1    Onion,medium-sized,chopped
2 T  Butter
2 pk Broccoil,frozen,chopped*
* - cooked according to package directions and drained.

1. Preheat oven to 350'F.

2. Saute onion in butter until soft and add to broccoli. Add remaining
ingredients and blend well. Pour into well-buttered casserole and bake
until firm and lightly browned on top, about 30 minutes.
