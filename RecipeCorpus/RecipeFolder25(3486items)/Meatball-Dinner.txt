Title: Meatball Dinner
Categories: Meatballs, Ground beef, Vegetable, Main dish, Meat
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
(10 3/4 oz)
3/4 c  Milk
1 pk Frozen mixed vegetables
(10 oz)
1 lb Ground beef
1    Egg
1 sm Onion; chopped (1/4 c)
1/3 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/4 c  Milk
3/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Mix all ingredients.  Shape mizture by tablespoonsful into 1 1/2-inch
balls.  (For ease of shaping meatballs, occasionally wet hands with
cold water.)

To cook in skillet:  Heat 1 tablespoon salad oil in large skillet;
cook meatballs over medium heat until brown, about 20 minutes.  Drain
off fat.

To cook in oven:  Place meatballs in lightly greased baking pay,
13x9x2 or 15x10x1 inch; bake uncovered in 400F oven until light
brown, about 20 minutes.  Drain off fat.

Mix soup and milk in large skillet.  Stir in vegetables; heat to
boiling. Reduce heat; cover and simmer until vegetables are tender,
about 10 minutes.  Add cooked meatballs; heat through.
