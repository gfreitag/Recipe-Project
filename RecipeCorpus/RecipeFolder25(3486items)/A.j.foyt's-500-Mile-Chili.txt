Title: A.j.foyt's 500-Mile Chili
Categories: Chili, Beef, Pork, Sausage
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Cayenne
3 tb Chili powder
3 cn Tomato sauce (15-oz each)
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Water
1 cn Mexican beer or any kind
3 lb Stewing beef, cubed
1 1/2 lb Cubed pork
1 lb Chorizo (Spanish sausage)
Sliced
2    Large onions, diced
5    Cloves garlic, minced
3    Jalapeno pepper, peel+diced
Brown Meat, onions, garlic and jalapenos together in olive oil. While
mixture is browning, add cayenne and chili powder. Stir often. Add
tomatoe sauce, salt, water and beer. Cover and cook on low heat for
30 minutes, stirring often. Uncover and simmer for 2 hours. From
*Prodigy's Food and Wine Bulletin Board.
