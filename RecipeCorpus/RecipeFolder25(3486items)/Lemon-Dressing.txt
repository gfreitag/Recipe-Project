Title: Lemon Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Japanese
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Grated lemon peel
1 c  Vegetable oil
1 lg Egg, room temp
1 tb Rice Vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
Process egg in blender for 15 seconds.  Add vinegar, salt and lemon pe
process 5 seconds.  With blender running, add the oil in a slow steady
stream.  Dressing will become thick and mayonnaise-like.  Use it to bi
minced items in sushi filling.  Keeps in refrigerator for about 1.5
