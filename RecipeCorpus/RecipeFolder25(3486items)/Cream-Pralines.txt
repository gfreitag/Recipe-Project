Title: Cream Pralines
Categories: Candy, Nuts/grains
Yield: 25 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 25 SERVINGS--------------------------
1 cn Milk, evaporated (5 oz can)
2 c  Pecan halves
1 1/4 c  Sugar, brown - packed
1 1/4 c  Sugar
Combine sugars and milk in heavy saucepan.  Cook over medium heat,
stirring constantly, to soft-ball stage on candy thermometer.  Cool 5
minutes, without stirring.  Add pecan halves; beat with wooden spoon
until mixture begins to thicken.  Drop quickly from teaspoon onto
waxed paper. Makes 25 pieces. Christmas 1980
