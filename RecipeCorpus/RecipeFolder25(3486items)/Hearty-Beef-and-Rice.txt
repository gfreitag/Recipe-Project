Title: Hearty Beef and Rice
Categories: Main dish, Burgers, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
-and chopped
7 oz Corn, whole kernel
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Chili powder
1 c  Cheese, Cheddar; shredded
1 lb Beef, ground
1 sm Onion; chopped
3 c  Rice; cooked
1 cn Soup, cream of mushroom
8 oz Tomatoes, stewed; undrained
Cook beef and onion in large skillet over medium-high heat; drain
fat. Stir in rice, soup, tomatoes, corn, garlic powder and chili
powder. Pour mixture into greased 2-quart baking dish; bake at 350
degrees for 20 minutes. Top with cheese; bake an additional 5 to 10
minutes.

Each serving provides 414 calories, 22 g. protein, 21 g. fat, 34 g.
carbohydrate, 2 g. dietary fiber, 65 mg cholesterol and 665 mg sodium.

SOURCE: Stretching your Budget with Rice, USA Rice Council pamphlet.
