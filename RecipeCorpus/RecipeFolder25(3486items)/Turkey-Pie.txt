Title: Turkey Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Turkey, Vegetable
Yield: 1 pie
1 c  Frozen peas
1 cn 3 oz mushrooms, drained
1 cn Refrigerator biscuits or
Home made
4 c  Cut up turkey
1/2 c  Leftover gravy
1 tb Minced onion
1/2 ts Rosemary
1 cn 10 oz Cream of Chicken soup
Mix turkey and gravy, seasonings and soup; add peas and mushrooms.
Heat slowly, stirring occasionally until bubbling hot.

Turn into 1 1/2 quart casserole.  Top with turkey shaped biscuits by
rolling out and cutting with a turkey shaped cutter.  Bake at 450
oven for 15 mins or until biscuits are done.
