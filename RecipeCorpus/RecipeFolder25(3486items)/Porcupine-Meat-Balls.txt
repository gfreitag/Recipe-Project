Title: Porcupine Meat Balls
Categories: Beef, Ground beef, Meatballs, Rice, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Condensed tomato soup
1/2 c  Water
1 1/2 lb Ground Beef
1/2 c  Uncooked rice
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Combine meat, rice, salt and onion.  Shape into small balls.  Blend
soup and water until mixture begins to simmer.  Add meat balls.  Cook
about 30 minutes.
