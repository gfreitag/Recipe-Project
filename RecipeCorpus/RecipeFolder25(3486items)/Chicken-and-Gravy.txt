Title: Chicken and Gravy
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Crockpot, Chicken
Yield: 1 servings
4 tb Flour
4 c  Potatoes, sliced
1 md Chicken, cut up or 3 breasts
And 3 leg quarters.
4 c  Of chicken boullion
Coat chicken in flour. Put in crock pot or slow cooker. Add 4 tb
flour, chicken boullion. Put in 4 cups of diced potatoes and cook.
