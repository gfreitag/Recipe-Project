Title: Moroccan Saffron Rice
Categories: Side dish, Moroccan, Rice, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Saffron
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 md Yellow onion, chopped
1 ea Green bell pepper, chopped
1 c  Long-grain rice
Heat oil in a large skillet.  Stir in the onion, pepper & rice.  Fry
over moderate heat, stirring frequently, until the rice becomes
golden, about 15 minutes.  Add spices & 2 c water & bring to a boil.
Reduce heat & simmer about 20 minutes until liquid is absorbed.
