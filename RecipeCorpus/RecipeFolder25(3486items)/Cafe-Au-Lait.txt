Title: Cafe Au Lait
Categories: Cajun/creol, Beverage
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Louisiana coffee w/chicory
2 c  Milk
Sugar
1. Put milk in saucepan; bring to a boil.

2. Pour hot freshly brewed coffee and milk simutaneously into cups;
sweeten with sugar to taste.
