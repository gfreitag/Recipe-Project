Title: Scone Mix with Recipe
Categories: Mixes, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Mix
3 tb Butter, softened
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1    Egg
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
2 ts Cream of tartar
-SCONES:
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Scones: Preheat oven to 375. Blend butter into mix until it is well
distributed. Stir in egg and buttermilk. Knead 1 minute. Quarter
dough, form into a 6" round and 1/2" thick. Cut each circle in
quarters. Place on greased cookie sheet and bake 15 minutes or unti
