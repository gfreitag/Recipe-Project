Title: Ribbon Sandwich
Categories: Bread, Appetizer, Seafood, Sandwich
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 ts Mustard
ds Salt
ds Pepper
1    White Bread;unsliced;loaf
8 oz Tuna;can drained
1/4 c  Celery;minced
1 tb Sweet Pickle;chopped
4    Egg;hard boiled;
Shell and chop eggs;  mix with 1/4 cup mayonnaise, mustard, salt
and pepper. Drain tuna;  mix with 1/4 cup mayonnaise, celery and
sweet pickle. Refrigerate in separate bowls.
Trim crusts from each loaf. Slice each loaf into 5 to 7 horizontal
slices. Alternate spreading tuna and egg mixtures on each horizontal
slice. Wrap in foil or plastic and refrigerate overnight.
Cut this loaf of alternating bread and fillings into 15 to 20
crosswise slices. Each slice can be halved.  Keep refrigerated until
served.
