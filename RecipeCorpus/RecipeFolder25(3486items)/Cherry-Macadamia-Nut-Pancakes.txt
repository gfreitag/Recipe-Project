Title: Cherry Macadamia Nut Pancakes
Categories: Kids, Breakfast, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Pancakes
Yield: 4 grandsons
3 c  Buttermilk;
4 tb Butter; melted
1 c  Sundried cherries; soaked in
1 c  Water; 10 minutes & drained
1/2 c  Macadamia nuts;
1 1/2 c  Whipped cream;
3 c  Flour;
1/2 ts Salt;
6 tb Sugar;
1/2 ts Baking soda;
1 tb Baking powder;
3    Eggs;
Sift flour, salt, sugar, baking soda and baking powder into a bowl.
Beat eggs, buttermilk and butter in a second bowl.  Mix together wet
and dry ingredients and stir until sightly lumpy.  Dab 1/4 cup on
heated, oiled griddle.  Sprinkle 1/2 of cherries and nuts over
pancakes before turning on the second on the second side.  Serve
sprinkled with remaining cherries and nuts, topped with 2 oz whipped
cream per serving. Source: The San Diego Union Food Section, Nov. 3,
1994 and Chef Michal Viasich, Miramar Sheration Hotal. Brought to you
and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY
