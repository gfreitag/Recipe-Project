Title: Lobster Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 2 pounds
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts White pepper
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 lb Coarsley chopped lobster met
4    Feet small hog casings
1 1/2 lb Whitefish fillet, cubed
1/2 ts Ground mustard seed
1/2 ts Ground coriander
1 ts Paprika
Prepare casings. Pulse the fish in food processor just until it is
broken, 3-4 times.. Add the mustard, coriander, paprika, lemon juice,
pepper and egg. Process until blended. Put the mixture in a mixing
bowl and add the lobster meat; blend well. Stuff casings and twist
off into 3-4" links.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
