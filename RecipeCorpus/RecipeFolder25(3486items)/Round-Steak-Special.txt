Title: Round Steak Special
Categories: Main dish, Sthrn/livng, Beef
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Wine, Burgundy
Salt; to taste
Pepper; to taste
1 c  Tomato sauce
Cheese, Parmesan; grated
1 lb Steak, round
3 tb Oil, olive
1/2 c  Tomatoes; chopped
1/2 c  Green peppers; chopped
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Mushrooms; drained, chopped
Cut steak into 4 serving-size pieces; brown in hot olive oil.  Add
tomatoes, peppers, onion, and mushrooms, and cook 4 minutes.  Put
mixutre into a 3-quart casserole dish; add Burgundy, salt, pepper,
and tomato sauce and top with grated Parmesan cheese.  Cover dish and
bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
