Title: Egg Curry
Categories: Indian, Cheese/eggs, Curries, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
Dissolved in 1 1/2 cups
Boiling water
6    Hard-cooked eggs, halved
Lengthwise
1 ts Salt
1 c  Cooked peas, drained
1 ts Lemon juice
4 1/2 c  Cooked white rice, drained
2 tb Salad oil
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1 ts Crushed coriander seed
1/2 ts Ground turmeric
1/4 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Ginger
1/8 ts Cayenne
2    Chicken-bouillon cubes,
1. In hot oil in medium skillet, saute onion and garlic until
tender-about
5 minutes.
2. Add coriander, turmeric, cumin, ginger, and cayenne; cook
stirring, 2
minutes.
3. Add bouillon; simmer slowly 10 minutes, or until mixture is
slightly
thickened.
4. Add eggs, salt, and peas; simmer gently until thoroughly
heated-about
5 minutes. Add lemon juice. Serve over hot rice.
