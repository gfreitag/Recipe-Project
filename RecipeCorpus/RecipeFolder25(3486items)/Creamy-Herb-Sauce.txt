Title: Creamy Herb Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 20 ounces
1/4 t  Pepper
1    Garlic clove, minced
4 T  Milk
1 ct Daisy or Daisy Light (16 oz)
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 T  Herb soup mix
Warm Daisy Light, mayonnaise, herb soup mix, garlic and milk, stirring
constantly.
