Title: Brick Flambe
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Alcohol, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Caraway seeds
3 T  Butter
2 T  Brandy, heated
1/2 ea Fresh lemon
1/2 lb Brick cheese, cut in half
-horizontally
1 ea Egg, beaten
2/3 c  Dry bread crumbs
Dip the cheese into the egg and coat with bread crumbs that have been
mixed with caraway seeds.  Melt the butter in a frying pan and sear
the cheese until brown on all sides.  Place cheese on a very hot
ceramic dish, pour brandy over top and ignite. Squeeze the lemon over
the flaming cheese to extinquish.  Serve with cocktail bread squares.
Makes 6 servings. Origin: KRAFT What's Cooking Magazine, 10th issue.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
