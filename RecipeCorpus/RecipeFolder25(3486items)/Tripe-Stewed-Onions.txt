Title: Tripe Stewed Onions
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Meat
Yield: 6 servings
Salt and pepper
Butter
2 lb Tripe
2    Onions
1 c  Hot milk
Simmer the tripe and onions in salted water for three or four hours.
Drain. Chop the cooked onions very fine, place them in hot milk, and
season with salt, pepper and butter.  Pour lthis over the tripe and
serve at once. Submitted By BRENT WILLIAMS   On   11-08-94
