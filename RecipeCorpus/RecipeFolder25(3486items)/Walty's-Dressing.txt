Title: Walty's Dressing
Categories: Condiment, Salad, Dressing
Yield: 1 recipe
2 ts Fresh thyme, minced
2 ts Chili sauce
Fresh pepper, to taste
6 lg Garlic cloves
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Olive oil
3/8 c  Red wine vinegar
*Heaping teaspoons.

Mash garlic and salt to a paste with a mortar and pestle.  Combine
with other ingredients.  Let mixture stand a few hours or overnight
in the refrigerator to blend flavors.  Stir before using.

The editors wrote: "This dressing is excellent on a salad of fresh
lettuce, radishes, onions, and drained and rinsed cooked or canned
beans."

Ted Waltersdorf "The Kitchen Table: Where Herbs and Spices Make a
Difference" in "The Herb Companion."  October/November, 1992. Posted
by Cathy Harned.
