Title: Fruited Rice Mix
Categories: Fruit, Rice, Mixes, Low-cal/fat, Snack
Yield: 5 servings
MMMMM-----------------------MAKES: 5 CUPS----------------------------
3 ea Tb chicken flavored instant
-bouillon
1 T  Dried grated orange peel
1 t  Seasoned salt
1/2 t  Allspice
4 c  Uncooked regular rice
1/2 c  Dried apricots, finely
-chopped
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Slivered almonds
1/4 c  Dried minced onion
In large bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well. Store in an airtight
container at room temperature. To Prepare 4 servings: Place 1 cup
rice mix in a saucepan with 1 3/4 cups water and mix well. Bring to a
boil, reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 14-18 minutes or until
rice is tender and liquid is absorbed. Remove from heat; let stand
five minutes. Fluff with fork before serving. Carolyn's note: the
only nutritional information I saved on this is that it has 1 gram of
fat per 2/3 cup serving. Source: Handwritten in "the big pile"
