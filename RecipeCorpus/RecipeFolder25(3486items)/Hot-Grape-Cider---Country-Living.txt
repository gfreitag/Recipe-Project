Title: Hot Grape Cider - Country Living
Categories: Apples, Beverage, Fruit, Herbs/spice
Yield: 8 cups
1 t  Whole cloves
Additional short cinnamon
-sticks (opt.)
4 c  Grape juice
4 c  Apple cider
2    4-inch cinnamon sticks
1 T  Whole allspice
1. In 4-quart saucepan, heat grape juice. apple cider, cinnamon
sticks, allspice and cloves just to boiling over high heat. Reduce
heat to low, cover, and simmer 5 minutes.

2. To serve at home, ladle grape cider, excluding spices, into cup.
Place a cinnamon stick in each cup, if desired. Or ladle cider into
heatproof beverage container to transport to picnic.
