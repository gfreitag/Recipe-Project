Title: Milwaukee Pizza
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Pizza, Spread
Yield: 3 servings
Parsley flakes
Crackers
8 oz Cream cheese
6 oz Can of shrimp
12 oz Heinz chili sauce
Spread cream cheese on serving plate to 1/8 inch thick; then drain
shrimp and crumble on top of cream cheese; then spread chili sauce on
top of meat and sprinkle with parsley.  Serve spread on your favorite
crackers. Randy Rigg
