Title: Mulled Cider - Country Living
Categories: Beverage, Apples, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 serving
Pinch of ground cloves
1 c  Apple cider
1    2 1/2-inch cinnamon stick
In microwave-safe mug or cup, combine cider, cinnamon, and cloves.
Microwave on high (100 percent) 3 min- utes. Let cider stand 5
minutes and serve warm.
