Title: Fiesta Dressing
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 20 ounces
2 t  Taco seasoning
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 ct Daisy or Daisy Light (16 oz)
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 T  Salsa
Combine Daisy or Daisy Light, mayonnaise, salsa, taco seasoning and
minced garlic. Garnish with a dollop of Daisy.
