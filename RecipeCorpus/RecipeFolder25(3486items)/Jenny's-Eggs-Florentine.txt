Title: Jenny's Eggs Florentine
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Mustard
1 tb Lemon Juice
1/8 ts Cayenne
1/2 c  Spinach;cooked,squeezed dry
4    Egg;poached;
4    English Muffin;toasted;slice
4    Cheddar Cheese;slices
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
3/4 c  Sour Cream
Blend last 6 ingredients in a blender or processor.
Gently heat resultant sauce in sauce pan. Place cheese slices on
toast, broil them till cheese melts.
To serve, place poached eggs on the muffins and top with sauce.
