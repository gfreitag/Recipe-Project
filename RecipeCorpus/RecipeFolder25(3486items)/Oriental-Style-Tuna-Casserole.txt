Title: Oriental Style Tuna Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Casserole, Asian
Yield: 2 servings
2 c  Bean sprouts
1/2 c  Diced green pepper
-Soy Sauce to taste
6 1/2 oz Can tuna fish, drained and
-flaked
1/2 c  Diced onion
1/2 c  Diced celery
Preheat oven to 350. In bowl, combine tuna, onion, celery, bean
sprouts, green pepper and soy sauce. Place in casserole dish and bake
for 30 minutes. Serve hot with additional soy sauce if desired.
