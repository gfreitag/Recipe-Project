Title: Stuffed French Loaf
Categories: Ground beef, Pork, Sausage, Bread, Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Oregano
2 tb Melted butter
Garlic salt
1/2 lb Ground beef and pork sausage
1 md Onion, chopped
2    Loaves of brown and serve
French bread
1/3 c  Water
1    Egg
Cook pork sausage, beef and onion until brown.  Cut off end of the
bread, hollow inside (save the ends).  Toss bread crumbs with other
ingredients including meat except for garlic and butter.  Stuff
loaves. Sprad garlic and butter over bread.  Bake in a 400 degree
oven for 15 to 20 minutes. Randy Rigg
