Title: Shrimp Salad
Categories: Salad, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  French dressing
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
-Salt and pepper to taste
-Lettuce
1 lb Shrimp, cleaned
1/2    Lemon, juiced
1 c  Chopped celery
2 tb Capers
1/4 c  Chopped green peppers
Combine all ingredients except lettuce. Mix well. To serve, line
salad bowl with lettuce leaves; pile shrimp salad on top.
