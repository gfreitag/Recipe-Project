Title: Mile High Meringue
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 1 serving
1/4 ts Vanilla
3 tb Granulated sugar or icing
- sugar
3    Egg whites
2 tb Cold water
Pinch of salt
1/4 ts Cream of tarter
Combine egg whites and cold water in large bowl. Beat until frothy.
Add slt, cream of tarter, and vanilla. Beat until stiff. Add sugar
and beat to stiff peaks. Pile on to hot pie, tarts or cake and bake
at 325 deg F. or until golden.
