Title: Chocolate Crinkles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Holiday
Yield: 6 servings
2 t  Vanilla
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
1 c  Confectioners' sugar
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
4 ea Sq. unsweetened chocolate
4 oz Melted
2 c  Granulated sugar
4 ea Eggs
Mix oil, chocolate and granulated sugar.  Blend in 1 egg at a time
until well mixed.  Add vanilla.  Stir flour, baking powder, and salt
into oil mixture.  Chill several hours or overnight. Heat oven to
350. Drop teaspoonfuls of dough into confectioners' sugar. Roll in
sugar; shape into balls.  Place 2" apart on greased baking sheet.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes, or until almost no imprint remains when
touched lightly in center. Makes 6 dozen cookies.
