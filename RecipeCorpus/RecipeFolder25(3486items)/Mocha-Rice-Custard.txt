Title: Mocha Rice Custard
Categories: Dessert, Sthrn/livng, Rice, Chocolate, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
4    Eggs; slightly beaten
1/3 c  Rice, instant
Whipped cream (opt.)
Chocolate curls (opt.)
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Chocolate milk mix, instant
1 tb Coffee, instant
1 qt Milk; divided
Combine first 4 ingredients; gradually stir in 3/4 cup milk and blend
well. Add remaining milk, eggs, and rice; mix well.  Pour into an 8"
square baking dish (1-1/2 qt. capacity).  Set in a pan of hot water 1
inch deep. Bake at 350 degrees about 50 minutes.

Stir mixture twice during first 30 minutes of baking.  Remove from
oven when a knife inserted 1 inch from edge of dish comes out clean.
Set on wire rack to cool.  Chill; serve plain or garnish with whipped
cream and chocolate curls.
