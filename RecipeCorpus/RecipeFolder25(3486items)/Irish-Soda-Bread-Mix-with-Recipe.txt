Title: Irish Soda Bread Mix with Recipe
Categories: Fruit, Mixes, Bread
Yield: 5 servings
-SODA BREAD:
1 pk Bread mix
1/4 c  Butter, softened
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Buttermilk
2 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Dried currants
1 c  Light brown sugar
1 tb Baking powder
1 ts Baking powder
Mix: Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.

Irish Soda Bread: Preheat oven to 350. Grease and flour a 9"
springform pan. Cut butter into mix. Add eggs and buttermilk, stir
until smooth. Pour into pan and bake 45 minutes or until a toothpick
inserted in center comes out clean. Serve warm.
