Title: Frozen Yogurt
Categories: Homemade, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Lemon juice
Sugar (to taste)
1 ea Bag frozen fruit OR fruit
-frozen at home
1/2 c  No fat plain yogurt
Place all indgredients in the blender and blend well.  Makes homemade
frozen yogurt without using an ice cream machine! (Saw on Low
Cholesterol Gourmet) from the editor of Eating Well magazine.
Contains virtually NO FAT!
