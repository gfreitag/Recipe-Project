Title: Banana Crunch Salad
Categories: Kids, Salad, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
6 tb Peanut Butter;softened
1/3 c  Sunflower Seed;hulled
6    Lettuce;leaves
1/2 c  Peanuts
2    Apple;sliced;
2    Banana;sliced;
1/4 c  Lemon Juice
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Wash, core and slice the apples. Quickly dip them into the lemon
juice and let drain.
Peel and slice bananas.  Quickly dip them into the lemon juice and
let drain.
In a medium sized mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise and softened
peanut butter until well mixed.
Stir the sunflower seeds into the mayonnaise mixture.
Stir the apple slices into the mayonnaise mixture until all slices
are well coated.
Put lettuce leaves on to small plates.
Spoon the banana mixture on to the lettuce leaves and sprinkle with
peanuts.
