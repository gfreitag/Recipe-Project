Title: Cider_glazed Cornish Hens
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Sweet butter
2 tb Honey
1 tb Dijon-style mustard
1 tb Wine vinegar
Wild Rice Mix
6    Perdue cornish gane hens
Salt; to taste
Pepper; to taste
1 qt Apple cider; or juice
1    Cinnamon stick
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.  Wash hens and pat dry..

Season inside and out with salt and pepper.  Tie legs together and
fold back wings.

For glaze, place cider vinegar with cinnamon stick in a medium
saucepan; boil until reduced to 1 cup.  Remove cinnamon stick and
stir in butter, honey, mustard and vinegar.  Boil until thick,
syrupy, and translucent.

Place hens in a shallow roasting pan.  Brushing occasionally with
glaze, cook 55-60 minutes or until golden and tender.

Serve over Wild Rice Mix.
