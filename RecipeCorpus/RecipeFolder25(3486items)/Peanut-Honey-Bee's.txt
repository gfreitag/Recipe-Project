Title: Peanut Honey Bee's
Categories: Nuts/grains, Holiday, Dessert
Yield: 28 servings
1 1/2 c  Graham wafer crumbs
1 oz Semi sweet chocolate, melted
Almond flakes
1 c  Smooth peanut butter
1/4 c  Butter
1 c  Icing sugar
Combine peanut butter, butter and sugar.  Blend in crumbs thoroughly.
Shape into 1 inch oval.  Drizzle stripes of chocolate across tops of
bees. Insert almond flakes on either side of 'bee body' to make
wings. Store in refrigerator.  Makes 28-30 bee's. Origin: Kraft,
What's Cooking Magazine, 10th issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
Dec/94.
