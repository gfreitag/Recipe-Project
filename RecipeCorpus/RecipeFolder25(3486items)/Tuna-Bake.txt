Title: Tuna Bake
Categories: Main dish, Casserole, Sthrn/livng, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Celery; finely chopped
1 cn Soup, golden mushroom
1/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper; finely chopped
2 tb Oil, salad
3 oz Noodles, chow mein
6 1/2 oz Tuna; drained and flaked
2 oz Pimiento; drained and choppd
Saute onion and green pepper in salad oil.  Combine remaining
ingredients; add onion and green pepper, and mix well.  Place in a
greased 1-1/2 qt. casserole.  Cover and bake at 375 degrees for 25
minutes. Remove cover; bake an additional 10 minutes.
