Title: Macadamia Nut Fudge
Categories: Candy, Nuts/grains, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
-coarsely chopped
3/4 c  Sweetened condensed milk
1 t  Vanilla
1 c  Chopped macadamia nuts
The dark chocolate inthe
-fudge contrasts
-beautifully with the rich
Macadamia nuts.
8 oz Semisweet chocolate,
In double boiler set over hot water and heat chocolate with the
condensed milk until melted.  Stir in vanilla, and the nuts.  Spread
fudge into foil lined 8x4 inch loaf pan.  Refrigerate for at least 3
hours or until firm. Remove from pan and peel off foil.  Cut into
small pieces. Makes about 1-1/4 lb. Origin: Canadian Living, December
1994. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
