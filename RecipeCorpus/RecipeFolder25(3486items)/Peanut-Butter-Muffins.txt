Title: Peanut Butter Muffins
Categories: Muffin
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Peanut butter
1    Egg
1 c  Milk
2 tb Melted shortening
2 c  Flour
4 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
Mix and sift dry ingredients.  Work in peanut butter.  Combine egg
and milk and stir in flour.  Add shortening and bake in well greased
tins about 20 minutes at 400 degrees. Randy Rigg
