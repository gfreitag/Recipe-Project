Title: Ornamental Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Cream of tartar
2 ea Egg whites
2 1/2 c  Icing sugar
Put egg whites and 1/2 cup icing sugar into a large bowl.  Beat 10
minutes. Add 1/2 cup icing sugar and beat again.  Add cream of
tartar. Continue adding icing sugar and beating until mixture holds
its shape. You can use this for decorating special cakes.  Can be
used in decorating tubes. This is the icing that hardens after it has
sat for a while. Origin: Sharon Stevens Shared by: Sharon Stevens
