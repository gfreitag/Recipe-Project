Title: Oven-Fried Chicken Iii
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 1 batch
1    3 lb. chicken; cut up
1/4 c  Butter; melted
3 c  Corn flakes
1 ts Basil
1 ts Oregano
Crush corn flakes finely.  Mix flakes with seasonings.  Brush chicken
with melted butter.  Roll chicken in corn flake mixture.  Drizzle any
remaining butter on top.  Bake at 375 F. for 45 to 55 minutes.
