Title: Oat and Brown Sugar Crumb Topping
Categories: Cake/cookie, Breakfast
Yield: 12 muffinss
1 t  Ground cinnamon
Pinch of salt
1/2 c  (1 stick) cold, unsalted
Butter cut into 1/4 in.
1 c  Unbleached or whole wheat
Flour
3/4 c  Rolled oats (not instant)
2/3 c  Packed light brown sugar
pcs

Mix the flour, oats, brown sugar, cinnamon and salt in a large bowl.
Add the butted and rub into the dry ingredients until you have a
uniform, dampish crumbs. If it seems overly clumpy, rub in a tbs. or
two of more flour. Store whatever you aren't using right away in a
sealed plastic bag in the freezer. Makes about 2 1/2 cups.

From the Boston Globe, Oct. 19, 1994
