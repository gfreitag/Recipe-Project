Title: Zucchini with Peanuts
Categories: Main dish, African, Nuts/grains, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Unsalted peanuts, raw or
-- roasted, coarsely ground
4 sm Zucchini
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Oil
Simmer the zucchini whole in about 1/2 c salted water until very
tender, about 10 minutes.  Combine the zucchini & oil & mash into a
smooth consistency.  Top with the nuts.

VARIATION: When zucchini is tender, drain & slice it, gently stir in
the oil & top with nuts.
