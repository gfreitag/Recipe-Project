Title: Milano Figs in Cocoa
Categories: Candy, Fruit, Chocolate, Alcohol
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Orange liqueur
2 tb Confectioner's sugar
Cocoa
12    Ripe figs--peeled
1 c  Ricotta cheese
1/4 c  Heavy cream
Cut the figs twice through the center but DO NOT cut through the base.
Stand them on a serving platter. Spread them open. Place the ricotta
in a bowl and thin with the cream.  Add the orange liqueur and sugar.
Spoon the mixture neatly over the figs. Dust each fig with a tiny bit
of cocoa. Chill 15 minutes.
