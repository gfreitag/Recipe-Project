Title: Quick Tomato Aspic
Categories: Salad, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng, Tomato
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Water; cold
Salt; to taste
Lettuce
3 oz Gelatin, lemon
1 c  Water; boiling
8 oz Tomato sauce with onions
MMMMM----------------CUCUMBER-MAYONNAISE DRESSING---------------------
1 tb Parsley; chopped
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Cucumber; chopped
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water; add tomato sauce.  Rinse out tomato
sauce can with cold water and add to gelatin mixture.  Add salt.
Pour into ring mold and chill until firm.

Unmold on lettuce and fill center with Cucumber-Mayonnaise Dressing.

Cucumber-Mayonnaise Dresing: Combine ingredients, and serve with
tomato aspic.
