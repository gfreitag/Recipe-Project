Title: Coffee Liqueur (Fake Kahlua)
Categories: Mixes, Alcohol, Beverage
Yield: 7 cups
2/3 c  Dry instant coffee
1 ts Vanilla -or-
1    Vanilla bean
3 c  Vodka
2 c  Water
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Packed brown sugar
Mix all ingredients in a 2-quart jar. Cover tightly and let stand at
room temperature two weeks. Remove vanilla bean. Pour into bottles
and seal. Makes about 7 1/2 cups.
