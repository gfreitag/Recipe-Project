Title: Luscious Berries & Cream
Categories: Dessert, Low-cal/fat, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Fat Free Sour Cream
-KNUDSEN FREE
2 ts Brown sugar
2 c  Halved strawberries
1 c  Raspberries
1 c  Blueberries
Place fruit in bowl; top with sour cream and brown sugar.  Serve
immediately.  Makes 4 servings.

Suggestion: Serve over shortcake.

110 Calories, 1g Total fat, 3mg Cholesterol
