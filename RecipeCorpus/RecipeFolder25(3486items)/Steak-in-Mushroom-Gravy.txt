Title: Steak in Mushroom Gravy
Categories: Main dish, Sthrn/livng, Beef
Yield: 4 servings
1 sm Onion; chopped
1 ts Salt
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 lb Round steak
2 tb Oil; or shortening
1 c  Soup, cream of mushroom
Brown meat on both sides in hot oil in large fry pan.  Combine
remaining ingredients and pour over meat.  Cover; simmer 1-1/2 to 2
hours until meat is tender.

TIP: Meat may be cooked whole or cut into serving-size pieces.
