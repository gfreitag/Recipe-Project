Title: Meatballs in Sour Cream
Categories: Main dish, Meatballs, Ground beef
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Finely Chopped Onion
2 tb Flour
1    10 1/2 oz. Condensed Beef
Broth
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1 c  Sour Cream
Hot Cooked Rice Or
Mashed Potatoes
-Jacqueline Dunn  JWPF94B
1 c  Crushed Corn Chips
1 c  Milk
1    Clove Crushed Garlic
1 tb Parsley Flakes
1 ts Salt
ds Pepper
2 lb Lean Ground Beef
3 tb Butter
Combine corn chips, milk, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper. Add meat
and mix well. Shape into small balls and place in large shallow pan.
Bake in moderate oven 375Ã¸ for 20 minutes. Melt butter in 3 quart
sauce pan. Add onion and cook until tender, but do not brown. Blend
in flour. Add broth, water, and worcestershire sauce. Cook, stirring
until mixture comes to a boil. Reduce heat and blend in sour cream.
Heat but do not boil. Add meatballs and stir gently to avoid breaking
them. Serve over rice or potatoes. ENJOY!!!
