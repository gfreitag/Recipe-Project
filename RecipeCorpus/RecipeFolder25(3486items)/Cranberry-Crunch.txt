Title: Cranberry Crunch
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 5 - 6 folks
4 tb Butter -=OR=-
4 tb Margarine;
Whipped cream -=OR=-
Vanilla Ice Cream;
16 oz Can whole cranberry sauce;
1/2 c  Quick-cooking rolled oats;
1/4 c  All-purpose flour;
1/4 c  Brown sugar;
Preheat oven to to 350 degrees.  Spread cranberry sauce in a 9" pie
plate. Combine oats, flour and brown sugar, cut in butter until
crumbly. Sprinkle over the cranberries.  Bake for 25 minutes.  Top
with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream. (How about french vanilla
ice cream?) Source: San Diego Union-Tribune Food Section, Nov 20,
