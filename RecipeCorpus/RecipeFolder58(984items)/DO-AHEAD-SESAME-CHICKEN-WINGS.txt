Title: DO-AHEAD SESAME CHICKEN WINGS
Categories: Chicken, Wings
Yield: 1 Servings
20    Chicken wings (about 4
-pounds)
2 tb Margarine or butter;
-melted
-l 1/2 cups Bisquick baking
-mix
1/2 c  Sesame seed
2 ts Paprika
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Salt
2    Eggs
2 tb Milk
1/4 c  Margarine or butter;
-melted
Separate chicken wings at joints; discard tips. Spread 1 tablespoon
margarine in each of 2 rectangular pans, 13 x 9 x 2 inches. Mix
baking mix, sesame seed, paprika, mustard and salt. Beat eggs and
milk with fork. Dip chicken into egg mixture; coat with sesame seed
mixture. Arrange close together in pans. Cover and refrigerate up to
6 hours (or bake immediately). Heat oven to 425!. Drizzle 1/4 Cup
margarine over chicken. Bake uncovered 35 to 40 minutes or until
brown and crisp. 40 chicken wing pieces.
