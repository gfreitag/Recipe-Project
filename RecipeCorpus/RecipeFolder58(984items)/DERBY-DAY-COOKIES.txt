Title: DERBY DAY COOKIES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 Dozen
1    16 oz.box confectioners'
-sugar
1 c  Finely chopped pecans
1/2 c  Butter, room temperature
1/4 c  Green creme de menthe
1    6 oz. pkg. semisweet choc.
-chips, melted
Mix confectioners' sugar, pecans, butter and creme de menthe in
medium bowl until well blended.  Cover and refrigerate until firm,
about 30 minutes.

Line a large baking sheet with foil.  Shape pecan mixture into 1 1/4
inch round balls and place on sheet. Refrigerate until firm, about 15
minutes. Dip half of each ball into melted chocolate; return to
sheet. Refrigerate until set, about 1 hour.  (Can be prepared ahead.
Place in airtight container and refrigerate five days or freeze up to
one month.) Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Quick 'n
Easy.
