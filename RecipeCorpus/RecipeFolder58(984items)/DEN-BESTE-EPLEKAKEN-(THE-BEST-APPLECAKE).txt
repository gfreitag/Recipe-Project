Title: DEN BESTE EPLEKAKEN (THE BEST APPLECAKE)
Categories: Cakes, Ethnic
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Butter, clarified
2 ts Cardamom, ground
1 c  Sugar, brown
2 c  Persimmon pulp
1 ts Juice, lemon
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Walnuts, chopped
Butter an ovenproof dish. Fill almost to the brim with peeled apple
wedges and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon. In a small bowl crumble
together equal parts of margarine, flour and sugar. Sprinkle over
apples. Bake about 40 minutes at 375 degrees. Serve warm with chilled
cream.

Source: Ekte Norsk Mat
