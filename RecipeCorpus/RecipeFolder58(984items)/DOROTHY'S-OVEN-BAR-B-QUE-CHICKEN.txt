Title: DOROTHY'S OVEN BAR-B-QUE CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 md Chicken cut into pieces
1 md Onion
2 tb Oil
2 tb Vinegar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Brown sugar
1 c  Catsup
3 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tb Mustard, prepared
1 c  Water
Salt and Pepper
Brown the chicken in the oil, remove and brown the onion. Remove all
but 2 tablespoons of the oil.  Add remaining ingredients. Simmer 30
minutes.

Place chicken in oven dish and pour sauce over chicken. Bake
uncovered in slow oven (325%) for one hour. Sauce is also excellent
for meat.
