Title: DEVILED CHEESE FILLING
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Grated cheese ( any kind of
Hard grating cheese )
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Vinegar
1 ts Peanut butter
1/2 ts ( or more ) prepared mustard
Few grains cayenne
:    Combine and blend throughly. Spread between two slices of any
kind of bread. Jeannie Allen
