Title: DELI STYLE SANDWICHES
Categories: Meats, Sandwiches
Yield: 2 Servings
4 sl Deli rye bread
4 oz Thinly sliced roast beef,
-Or boiled ham
4 tb Whipped cream cheese with
-Onion and chives
Quick lunch with interesting twist in flavor Toast bread, spread both
sides of bread generously with cream cheese, top with warm ham or
roast beef and enjoy and enjoy with crisp deli - dill pickle.
