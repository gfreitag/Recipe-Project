Title: DISGUSTINGLY RICH BROWNIES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
3/4 c  Cocoa
1 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Butter
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Walnuts
Preheat oven to 350.

Melt butter; stir cocoa and sugar together, then stir in melted
butter. Add eggs and vanilla. Add flour and salt. Stir until smooth.
Add walnuts.

Grease 8 or 9-inch baking pan; spread mixture into pan. Bake 40 to 50
minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. Cool.
