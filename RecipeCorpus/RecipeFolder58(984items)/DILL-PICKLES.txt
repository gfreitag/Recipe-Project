Title: DILL PICKLES
Categories: Pickles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ea 6 quart basket of small cumb
1 ea Fresh dill
2 c  White wine vinegar
1 c  Pickling salt
10 c  Water
Wash jars and scrub cumbers. Cut off stems and blossom ends and prick
cucumbers with a fork. Pack in jars and pour boiling water over
cumbers and let stand for 3 minutes. Drain off water. Combine
vinegar, 10 cups of water and salt. Bring to a boil. Pour over
cucumbers in jars, add a sprig of dill and seal immediately. Leave
for several weeks before using. NOTE: A little clove of garlic can be
added with the dill to create a different taste to your pickles.
