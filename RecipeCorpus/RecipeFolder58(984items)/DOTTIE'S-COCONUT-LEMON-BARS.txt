Title: DOTTIE'S COCONUT-LEMON BARS
Categories: Blank
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Butter
1/4 c  Brown sugar
3    Eggs, beaten
2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Shredded unsweetened
Coconut
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Walnuts, chopped
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Lemon rind, grated
Mix flour, butter, and 1/4 c sugar. Press firmly into the bottom of a
greased 9x13" pan. Bake 10 min at 350. Cool.

Mix all the remaining ingredients. Spread over the crust layer. Bake
20    min at 350.

Yankee Magazine 7/81, Great New England Cooks: Mary Bosqui

From: Michael Loo
