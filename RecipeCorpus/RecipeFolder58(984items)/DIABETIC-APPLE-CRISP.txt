Title: DIABETIC APPLE CRISP
Categories: Desserts, Diabetic, Apples, Bars
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Cooking apple(peel & slice)
1 ts Orange juice
2 ts Granulated sugar replacement
2 tb Cornflakes (crushed)
1 ts Margarine
Place apple slices in small baking dish.  Sprinkle with orange juice,
sugar replacement and crushed cornflakes; top with margarine. Bake at
350F for 20-25 minutes. MICROWAVE: Cook on high for 3-4 minutes. Hold
2 minutes. 1 serving = 1 fruit, 1 fat, 1/3 bread, calories = 109 Pat
