Title: DOTT KREMER'S ROLLED COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 15 Servings
1 c  SUGAR
1/2 c  CRISCO
2 tb MILK
1 x  EGG
1 tb BAKING POWDER
1 ts VANILLA
MIX ALL INGREDIENTS WELL - CHILL - ROLL TO 1/4" THICK CUT AS DESIRED
AND BAKE AT 350 DEG F FOR 15 TO 20 MINUTES. SPRINKLE WITH SUGAR AND
SERVE OT STORE.
