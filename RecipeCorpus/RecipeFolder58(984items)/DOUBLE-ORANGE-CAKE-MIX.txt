Title: DOUBLE ORANGE CAKE-MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 9 Servings
2 c  Bake it all mix
2    Eggs
2/3 c  Buttermilk or sour milk*
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Orange peel -- ground**
1/2 c  Raisins -- chopped
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/3 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Sugar
* To make sour milk, place 2 teaspoons vinegar in measuring cup and
fill to 2/3 cup mark with milk.
**Or finely chopped.    Break eggs into a bowl; beat well. Stir in
buttermilk, 1/2 cup sugar, orange peel and raisins. Add Mix and soda.
Beat well.  Pour into an 8 by 8 by 2-inch pan, bottom rubbed with
shortening. Bake in a 350 F. oven about 30 minutes. Combine orange
juice and 1/4 cup sugar, stirring to dissolve sugar. Pour over hot
cake. Cool. Yield: One 8-inch square cake
