Title: DILLED CUCUMBER SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches, Cucumber
Yield: 4 Servings
2 oz Nuefchatel cheese; softened
1 tb Fat-free or reduced-calorie
-mayonnaise, salad dressing
1 ts -2 tsp. dried dill weed
1 ts Skim milk
1    Garlic clove; finely chopped
8 sl Whole-grain bread; toasted
-if desired
2 sm Cucumber; thinly sliced
Freshly ground pepper
Mix Neufchatel cheese, mayonnaise, dill weed, milk and garlic. Spread
cheese mixture on bread,  Place cucumber slices on 4 slices bread.
Sprinkle with pepper. Top with remaining bread. Makes 4 sandwiches.
