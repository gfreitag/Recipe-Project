Title: DRIED FRUIT BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
-MARLENE SINYARD   (HFWK05A)
1 1/8 c  Water
1 tb Butter; or margarine
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Dry milk solids
1 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1 1/4 ts Yeast
1 c  Mixed dried fruit bits
