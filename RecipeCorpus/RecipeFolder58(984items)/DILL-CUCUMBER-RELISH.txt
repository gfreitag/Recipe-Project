Title: DILL CUCUMBER RELISH
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 2 Servings
3    Medium cucumbers
1/4 c  Onion; grated
1/2 ts Pepper
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1 ts Dill seed
Put cucs through food chopper, drain.  Add remaining ingredients. Mix
well. Before using, chill several days to let flavors blend. Makes 2
c. LISA CRAWLEY TSPN00B
