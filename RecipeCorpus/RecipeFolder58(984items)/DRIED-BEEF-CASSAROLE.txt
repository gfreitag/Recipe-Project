Title: DRIED BEEF CASSAROLE
Categories: Beef, Casseroles, Pasta
Yield: 8 Servings
3 oz Dried beef (Armour)
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 pk Small sour cream
1    Onion, chopped
2 c  Macaroni, cooked & drained
1/2 lb Cheddar, grated
Chop beef into small pieces. Mix beef, soup, sour cream and onion
together. Add macaroni.  Mix well. Pour into greased 13x9 pan. top
with cheese. cover with foil & bake @ 350F for 45-55 min.
