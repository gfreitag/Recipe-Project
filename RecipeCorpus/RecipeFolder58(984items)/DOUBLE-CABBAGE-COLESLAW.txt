Title: DOUBLE CABBAGE COLESLAW
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Honey-mustard
2 tb Golden brown sugar
2 ts Celery seeds
1 1/2 ts Pepper
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream or plain yogurt
1 md Head green cabbage, shredded
1 md Head red cabbage, shredded
1 bn Green onions, chopped
1/2 c  Dried currants
Whisk first 6 ingredients together.  Add may and sour cream and whisk
until smooth.  Add both cabbages, green onions and currants and toss
to coat (I'd use a large bowl here). Cover and refrigerate at least 4
hours.
