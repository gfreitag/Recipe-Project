Title: DOUBLE CHOCOLATE BARS
Categories: Snacks, Desserts, Microwave
Yield: 4 Dozen
1 pk 16 oz. cream filled choc.
-sandwich cookies, crushed
3/4 c  Butter or margarine,melted
1 cn 14 oz. sweetened cond.milk
2 c  12 oz. miniature semisweet
-choc. chips, divided
Combine cookie crumbs an butter; pat onto the bottom of an ungreased
13 inch x 9 inch x 2 inch baking pan. Combine milk and 1 cup of chips
in a microwave-safe bowl. Cover and microwave on high for 1 minute or
until chips are melted; stir until smooth.  Pour over crust. Sprinkle
with remaining chips.  Bake at 350Ã¸F for 10-12 minutes or until chips
are melted.  Cool. Yield: about 4 dozen. This recipe was tested in a
