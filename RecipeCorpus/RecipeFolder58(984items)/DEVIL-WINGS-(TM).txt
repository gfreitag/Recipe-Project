Title: DEVIL WINGS (TM)
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 8 Servings
12 oz RED DEVIL Cayenne Pepper
-Sauce
8 oz Italian salad dressing
5 lb Chicken wings or drumettes,
-rinsed
1    2-gallon resealable freezer
-bag
In a freezer bag, mix RED DEVIL with salad dressing. Keep 1 cup for
basting and dripping. Add chicken wings to bag, seal and refrigerate
at least 2 hours, turning bag several times. Grill wings over low
fire or bake at 350'F. for 45 minutes or until done, basting with
reserved sauce.
