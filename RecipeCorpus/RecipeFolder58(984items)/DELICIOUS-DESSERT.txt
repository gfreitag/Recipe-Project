Title: DELICIOUS DESSERT
Categories: Bars
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
8 oz Cream cheese
1 c  Confectioner's sugar
1 c  Cool Whip
1 ea Instant vanilla pudding
1 ea Instant butterscotch
-pudding
2 3/4 c  Milk
In a mixing bowl, cut margarine into flour.  Add 1/4 cup walnuts.
Then pat into an ungreased 9 by 13 pan. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
Cool. Now mix cream cheese and sugar. Add 1/2 cup cool whip to
mixture. Spread on to cool crust.  Mix both pudding mixes with milk.
Mix until semi-firm. Spread on top of crust and cheese mixture.
Spread remaining Cool Whip on top and add remaining 1/4 cup of nuts
as garnish. Refrigerate for an hour. Walt MM
