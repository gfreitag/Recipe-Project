Title: DIABETIC COOKIES
Categories: Diabetic, Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Shortening/promise
-margarine
1    Egg
2 tb Sweet 'n' low
1 tb Butter
1/2 c  Buttermilk/sour cream
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Grated apple
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 tb Vanilla
Cream shortening, egg, sugar substitute and butter; add buttermilk
and mix in flour and baking soda, apple, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg,
soda, cloves and vanilla.  Drop on greased cookie sheet. Bake 7-8
minutes at 375 degrees.

Date: 11-18-93
From: Jeffrey Dean
