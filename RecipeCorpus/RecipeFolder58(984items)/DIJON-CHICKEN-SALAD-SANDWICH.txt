Title: DIJON CHICKEN SALAD SANDWICH
Categories: Sandwiches, Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  BEST FOODS mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sliced green onions
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 ts Dried dill
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Cubed chicken
Lettuce
Tomato
Combine mayonnaise, green onions, Dijon mustard, dill, salt, and
chicken. Fill croissants with lettuce, tomato, and chicken salad.
