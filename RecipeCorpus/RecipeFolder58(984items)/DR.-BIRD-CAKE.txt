Title: DR. BIRD CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Mjl
Yield: 1 Cake
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Oil
1 cn Crushed pineapple (18oz)
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
3    Eggs
2 c  Ripe bannanas;diced
Measure dry ingredients and sift together. Add banannas, oil,
vanilla, eggs and pineapple with juice. Stir, do not beat. Pour into
greased 9 inch tube pan Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 20
minutes. Cool before removing from pan
