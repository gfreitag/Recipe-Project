Title: DILL CORNMEAL DUMPLINGS
Categories: Magazine, Breads, Soups/stews
Yield: 8 Dumplings
1 1/4 c  Flour; all-purpose
1/3 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 tb Dill; finely chopped
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Vegetable shortening; chilld
1 c  Milk
In a medium bowl, combine the first 5 ingredients. With 2 knives or
your fingers, cut in the shortening until the mixture resembles
coarse crumbs. Add the milk, stirring with a fork, just until the
mixture holds together.

With a large spoon, drop dumpling batter in 8 spoonfuls over the stew.
Simmer over medium-low heat, covered, about 16 minutes, or until the
dumplings are done.
