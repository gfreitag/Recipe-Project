Title: DOGGY TREATS
Categories: Pets
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Jar baby food meat (any
- flavor)
7 tb Wheat germ
4 tb Nonfat powdered milk
Combine all ingredients. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie
sheets. Flatten slightly with fork. Bake in 350 degree oven until
lightly browned. Let cool, then serve.
