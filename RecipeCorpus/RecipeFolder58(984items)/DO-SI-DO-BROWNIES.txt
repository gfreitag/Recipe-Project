Title: DO-SI-DO BROWNIES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Pan
BROWNIES
1/2 c  Butter
2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/3 c  Cocoa
1 c  Chopped peanuts
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Marshmallows
1/2 c  Peanut butter chips
1 c  Crushed Do-Si-Do cookies
ICING
1/2 c  Butter
1 lb Powdered sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa
1 c  Chopped peanuts
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
BROWNIES: Preheat oven to 350F. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs. Sift
together flour and cocoa; add gradually until blended. Add peanuts and
vanilla. Spread into greased and floured 9 x 13 inch pan. Bake 30-35
minutes. Remove from oven. Mix together marshmallows, peanut butter
chips and crushed do-si-do cookies. Top brownies with marshmallow
mixture. Bake 10 more minutes. Cool completely and ice.

ICING: Melt butter. Sift cocoa and powdered sugar together; add
butter. Stir in milk and peanuts.
