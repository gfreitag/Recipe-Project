Title: DEVILLED CRAB IN TOMATO.
Categories: Appetizers, Thai
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Cooked crab meat.
1/2 c  Shallots, peeled, chopped.
1/2 c  Lemon grass, sliced thin.
1/4 c  Nam-prik Pao.
1/4 c  Fish Sauce.
1/4 c  Lime juice.
2 tb Sugar.
1/2 c  Mint leaves, chopped fine.
1/4 c  Scallions, sliced fine.
1/4 c  Coriander leaves, chopped.
1 tb Chilli peppers, chopped.
30 ea Italian tomatoes.
Wash the tomatoes and dry.  Cut in half lengthwise, and scoop out
seeds and pulps.
Mix all remaining ingredients and fill the tomato halves. Arrange on a
platter, on a bed of lettuce leaves, and decorate with mint leaves.
