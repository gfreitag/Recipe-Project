Title: DOUGH ART (MICROWAVE)
Categories: Microwave, Christmas
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Salt
1 1/2 c  Hot water (instant coffee
Added to water gives dough
"browned" coror)
Knead dough 6 - 8 minutes.  Roll out dough and cut with cookie
cutters into desired shapes.

Place on wax paper on glass tray; with a toothpick, make several
holes in each piece to let air escape, then microwave on high for
about 2 minutes. Time will vary according to size of "cookies."

When baked, allow to cool before  decorating.
