Title: DONNA GERMAN'S TURKEY STUFFING
Categories: Side dish, Holiday, Poultry, German
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
3 tb Butter or margarine
3 1/2 c  Cubed or crumbed bread; use
- Turkey Stuffing Bread
- (recipe)
3/4 c  Chicken or turkey broth
1/4 c  Walnuts (optional)
Salt, to taste
Pepper, to taste
Saute celery and onion in the butter/margarine until soft; toss wiht
bread in a bowl.  Add warmed broth and season and mix until well
belnded. Stuff bird or bake in a covered casserole at 350 F. for 20
minutes.

From:  The Bread Machine Newsletter, Vol. I, No. 5, by Donna German
