Title: DO-IT-YOURSELF PEANUT BUTTER
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Freshly roasted peanuts
Peanut oil
1/2 ts Salt
In the container of a blender put the peanuts and 1-1/2 tablespoons
peanut oil. Blend the mixture until it is smooth, adding another
1-1/2 tablespoons oil gradually, or enough to make the peanut butter
the proper consistency. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt if the peanuts are
unsalted.
