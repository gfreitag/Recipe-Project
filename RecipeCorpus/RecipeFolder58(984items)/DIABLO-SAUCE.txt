Title: DIABLO SAUCE
Categories: Barbecue, Chicken, Pork, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 qt Chicken stock
1/2 c  Ketchup
3/4 c  Chili sauce
1 ts Liquid barbecue smokeae
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Salt; to taste
Pepper; to taste
1/2 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Sweet pickle relish
Parsley; chopped
In a large saucepan, mix frist 7 ingredients. Bring to boil, in a
small pan , melt margarine and gradually add flour, stir until
smooth. Add gradually to sauce mixture until desired thickness is
reached.
