Title: DIJON POTATO SALAD
Categories: Potsalads
Yield: 4 Guessed
MMMMM----------------FOOD IN A FLASH, TVFN AIKENS---------------------
8 md Potatoes, quartered, cooked
1 ts Chicken bouillon (liquid)
2 ts Warm water
2    Hard cooked eggs, sliced
1/2 c  Mayo
Cayenne
1/4 c  Dijon
2 tb Tarragon vinegar
2 ts Onion, minced
2 ts Parsley
1 ts Dried tarragon
Salt and pepper
Lettuce leaves
Combine mayo, bouillon, cayenne, tarragon, onion, dijon and parsley;
whisk. Add vinegar; whisk. Pour over potatoes; toss. Place on
individual plates and top with egg.
