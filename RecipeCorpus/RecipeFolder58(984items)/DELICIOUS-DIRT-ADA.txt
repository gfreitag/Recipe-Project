Title: DELICIOUS DIRT-ADA
Categories: Cakes, Desserts, Diabetic
Yield: 12 Servings
DevilsFood cakemix-sugarfree
8 oz Low fat cream cheese
1/8 lb Margarine
3 1/2 c  Skim milk
2 x  Sugarfree inst. choc pudding
12 oz Sugarfree whipped topping
20 oz Dark brown sugarfree cookies
Bake sugar free devils good cake mix according to directions and
cool. Mix low far cream cheese or Neufchatel cheese, margarine, milk
and pudding packets.  Stir until thickened and fold in prepared
sugarfree whipped topping.  Crush cookies into crumbs.
