Title: DOUBLE APPLE SALAD  (TASTE OF HOME)
Categories: Salads, Fruits
Yield: 10 - 12 serv.
1 lg Golden Delicious Apple
- diced
1 lg Red Delecious Apple; diced
1 ts Lemon juice
20 oz Can Pineapple Chunks
- drained
1 c  Miniature Marshmallows
2/3 c  Coconut; flaked
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1/4 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Celery; thinly sliced
In a bowl, toss apples with lemon juice. Add remaining ingredients
and mix well. Cover and chill for at least
1    hour.
