Title: DICED PEPPER & TOMATO SALAD
Categories: Side dish, Moroccan, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lg Green bell peppers, grilled
-- & skinned
6 ea Tomatoes, skinned & seeded
2 ea Garlic cloves, chopped
Salt
Rinse & drain the peppers.  Remove the core & seeds & cut the flesh
into small dice.  Dice the tomatoes to the same size. Combine
tomatoes & peppers & add the chopped garlic. Season with salt to
taste & chill. Drain off excess liquids before serving.
