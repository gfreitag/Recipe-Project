Title: DOLLY PARTON'S FIVE LAYER DINNER
Categories: Casseroles, Main dishes, Potatoes
2 c  potatoes -- sliced
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  ground beef, extra lean
1 c  green pepper
1 c  onions -- sliced
2 c  tomatoes -- cooked
salt and pepper
green pepper -- sliced
:
:

Layer potatoes, beef, onions, and tomatoes in a 2 quart casserole.
Season each layer with salt and peppr to taste. Garnish with sliced
green peppers, cover and bake for 2 hours at 350.
