Title: DOT'S BANANA PUDDING
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
-====================
Ingredients: 3 small boxes instant vanilla pudding mix 5 cups cold
milk 12 oz whipped topping (Cool Whip) 8 oz sour cream 1 large box
vanilla wafers 56 large bananas. Mix pudding mix with milk. Fold in
half of the whipped topping, all of the sour cream.  In large bowl,
layer wafers, bananas, pudding mixture. Top with remaining whipped
topping. Refrigerate. Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.40
