Title: DILLED EGG SALAD
Categories: Salads, Eggs
Yield: 4 Servings
3 tb Dill, fresh; minced
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Pimentos, minced
1 ts Dijon mustard
6    Egg; hard-cooked, peeled
-and diced
Blend the first four ingredients together. Mix in the diced
hard-cooked eggs. Refrigerate for several hours to allow the flavors
to blend.

Source: The Green House - Encinitas, CA
