Title: DICED WINTER MELON SOUP B1
Categories: Chinese, Fruits, Soups
Yield: 4 Servings
5 c  Soup stock or chicken broth
1/2 lb Winter melon; diced
1/4 c  Chicken, diced
4    Water chestnuts; diced
Salt
MSG
Place stock or broth in a pot. Cover & bring to a boil. Add winter
melon, chicken & water chestnuts. Simmer 10 minutes. Season to taste
with salt and MSG.
