Title: DENNIS'S BLARNEY STONE BREAD
Categories: Oat, Bmm, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Oats,Quaker Quick
1 1/4 c  Milk
1    Egg Beaters
3 c  Flour,White Bread
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Applesauce
3 tb Honey
3/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Ginger,Ground
1/8 ts Cloves
2 ts Yeast
Crust - light Place all in bread pan, select Light Crust setting, and
press start.
