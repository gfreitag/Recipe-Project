Title: DOMESTIC BOURSIN CHEESE
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
Jim Vorheis
3 oz Cream cheese
4 tb Butter
1/4 ts Garlic powder
2 tb Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese
1 tb Dry white wine
1 tb Minced parsley
1 pn Of thyme
1 pn Of marjoram
Cream all ingredients thoroughly or use food processor with steel
blade. Chill for at least four hours. Serve with crackers.

Colorado Cache Cookbook (1978) From the collection of Jim Vorheis
