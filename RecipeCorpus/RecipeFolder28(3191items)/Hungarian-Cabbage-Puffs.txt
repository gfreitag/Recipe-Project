Title: Hungarian Cabbage Puffs
Categories: Snacks, Beef
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 t  Salt
Few grains of nutmeg
8 lg Cabbage leaves
1 ea Egg
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1/4 t  Pepper
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 c  Cooked rice
Cook cabbage leaves in saucepan in salted water for 5 minutes.  Drain.
Combine ground beef, egg, rice, salt, pepper, onion and nutmeg in
mixing bowl.  Shape meat mixture into balls and wrap each ball in a
cabbage leaf. Fasten each with a wooden toothpick.  Place the cabbage
rolls in a sauce made of 1 cup of cooked tomatoes, 1/4 cup of chopped
onion, 1/4 cup margarine, 3/4 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper.  Cover and
simmer for 45 minutes.
