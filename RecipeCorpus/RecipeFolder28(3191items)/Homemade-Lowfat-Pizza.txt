Title: Homemade Lowfat Pizza
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 8 ----------
1/2 t  Dried basil
1/4 t  Oregano
1/2 t  Dried thyme
**TOPPINGS**
1 cn (8oz) mushroom stems and
-pieces, rinsed and drained
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1 1/2 c  Grated nonfat mozzarella
1/4 c  Nonfat Alpine Lace or
-Weight Watchers Parmesan
-cheese
1 ea Pk yeast
1 c  Warm water
1 t  Sugar (do not use S&L in
-this recipe)
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 t  Lite salt, optional
**BASIC SAUCE**
1 cn (263/4oz) Hunt's light
-spaghetti sauce
1 cn (6oz) tomato paste
1/2 lb Skinless white meat turkey,
-cooked and drained
1/2 t  Garlic powder
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
Dissolve yeast in 1/4 cup water, and sugar and let stand 10 minutes.
Add remaining water, flour and salt and beat very well until dough is
completely smooth. Dough will be sticky but no kneading is required.
Place in a bowl that has been sprayed with Pam. cover and let rise
until double, about 1 hour. Punch down, divide in half and let stand
10 minutes. dough will be very elastic. Roll out into 2 8" circles.
Pizza: Place dough on a cookie sheet that has been sprayed with Pam.
Spoon sauce on crust. Top with any combination of the above choices.
Sprinkle your choice of cheese on top. Bake at 450F. 8-10 minutes.
Per serving: 256 cal., 1/3g fat (4%), 18mg chol., 4g fiber, 18g pro.,
