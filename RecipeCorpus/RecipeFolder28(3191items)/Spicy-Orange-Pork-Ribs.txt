Title: Spicy Orange Pork Ribs
Categories: Pork, Meats, Fruit, Syrups, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Bay leaf
3 lb Pork side ribs
1 ea Onion, chopped
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1 t  Five spice powder
1 ea Clove garlic
1 c  Orange juice
3 T  Liquid honey
1 T  Soy sauce, and minced ginger
Place cut ribs, bay leaf and onion in large dutch oven.  Cover with
cold water and bring to boil.  Simmer for about 30-45 minutes until
cooked and tender.  Drain.  In seperate pot, bring all the sauce
ingredients to a boil and cook for about 7-10 minutes.  Cool
slightly.  Layer ribs in baking dish and spoon sauce over top turning
ribs to coat completely. Save about 1/4 cup sauce for basting while
baking.  Bake in 350F for 30-40 minutes or until sauce has glazed and
thickened.  Makes 4 servings. Origin: Adapted from a recipe in
Canadian Living, March/95. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, March/95.
