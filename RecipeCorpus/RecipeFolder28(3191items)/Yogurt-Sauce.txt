Title: Yogurt Sauce
Categories: Seafood, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts White pepper
3 tb Lumpfish or black caviar
2 c  Plain yogurt
1/4 bn Chives, minced
3    Green onions, minced
Combine all ingredients but caviar. Fold in caviar. Chill. Makes
about 2 cups. From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska. Feb 1994
