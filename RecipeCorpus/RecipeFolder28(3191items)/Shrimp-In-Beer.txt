Title: Shrimp In Beer
Categories: Seafood, Beer
Yield: 1 servings
1    Bay leaf
6 tb Butter
3 tb Flour
2 lb Shrimp
-salt and pepper
2    Bottles beer
3    Leeks chopped
2    Onions, chopped
3    Sprigs parsley
1    Stalk celery
Simmer beer with leeks, onions, parsley, bay leaf and celery 15
minutes. Strain out vegetables. Melt butter; blend in flour. Add beer
slowly, stirring constantly over low heat until thickened. Add
shrimp; simmer 5 minutes until fish turns pink; season to taste with
salt and pepper.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
