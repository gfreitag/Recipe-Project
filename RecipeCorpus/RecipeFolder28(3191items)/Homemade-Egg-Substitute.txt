Title: Homemade Egg Substitute
Categories: Healthy, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Oil
6    Egg whites
1/4 c  Powdered nonfat milk
Recipe by: The New American Diet - ISBN 0-671-66375-5

Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl and blend until smooth.
Store in jar in refrigerator up to 1 week. Also freezes well.

To prepare as scrambled egg. Fry slowly over low heat heat in a non
stick fry pan.

Makes 1 cup (1/4 cup of mix = 1 whole egg)

(Author's Note): Use instead of commercial egg substitutes. Much
cheaper.
