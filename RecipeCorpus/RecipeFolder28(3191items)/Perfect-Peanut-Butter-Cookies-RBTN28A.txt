Title: Perfect Peanut Butter Cookies RBTN28A
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 42 cookies
1 ea Large egg
3/4 ts Vanilla extract
1 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Creamy or chunk-style peanut
Butter
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Light brown sugar, firmly
Packed
Heat oven to 375 F. In large bowl with electric mixer at medium
speed, beat butter, peanut butter, granulated sugar and 1/4 cup of
the brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in egg and vanilla until
thoroughly blended. In small bowl, combine flour, baking soda and
salt. With mixer at low speed, beat into butter mixture until
blended. Sprinkle remaining 1/4 cup brown sugar on top of dough.
Using rubber spatula, lightly fold in sugar. (Sugar granules will
still be visible.) Roll heaping teaspoonfuls of dough into balls;
place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheets. Using bottom of
glass, press each ball into 1/4 inch-thick circle. Bake 5-8 munutes
until lightly browned. Remove cookies to wire rack to cool...
