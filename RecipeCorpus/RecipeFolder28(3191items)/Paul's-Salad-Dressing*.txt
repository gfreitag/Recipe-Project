Title: Paul's Salad Dressing*
Categories: Salads
Yield: 12 ounces
1 tb Tamari soy sauce
1 tb Tahini (heaping)
3/4 c  Sesame oil
1    Orange, juice only (about
-1/3 cup
Blend all the ingredients together in a blender. If the oil seperates
blend in 1 tablespoon of water.
