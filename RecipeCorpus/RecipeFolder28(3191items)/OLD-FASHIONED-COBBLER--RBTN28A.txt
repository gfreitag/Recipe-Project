Title: OLD FASHIONED COBBLER  RBTN28A
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
2/3 c  Milk
1 ea Can pie cherries
1/2 c  1 stick of oleo
1 c  Sugar
Melt oleo in a baking dish over low flame. Mix remaining ingredients
(except Cherries) and pour slowly over oleo. Spoon cherries evenly
over this batter. Add sugar if desired. Bake 30 Minutes or until done.
