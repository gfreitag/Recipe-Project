Title: Old-Fashioned Microwave Chocolate Pudding
Categories: Chocolate, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
2 oz Unsweetened chocolate square
-- cut-up
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb Cornstarch
1/8 ts Salt
2 1/2 c  Milk
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:20 1. In a 2-qt glass bowl, mixtogether sugar,
cornstarch, and salt until well blended. Gradually whisk in milk
until cornstach is completely dissolved. Add chocolate.

2. Cook in microwave on High 7 to 9 mins, whisking every 2 mins, until
mixture is cooked through, smooth, and thickened. Whisk in vanilla.
Pour into 4 individual serving dishes. Cover with plastic wrap to
prevent skin from forming on top. Refrigerate until cold.

NOTES : Just like pudding from a mix without the long cook times,
constant
stirring and scorched pans. Tested in 600-700 watt micro.
