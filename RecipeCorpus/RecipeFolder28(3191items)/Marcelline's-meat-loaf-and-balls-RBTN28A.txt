Title: Marcelline's meat loaf and balls RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
x  1/2 jar picante sauce
1 ea Egg
1 ds Salt and pepper
1 cn Sauerkraut for balls
1/2 lb Sausage
1 lb Hamb
1 ea Diced onion
x  Crackers to hold mix togethr
Mix together all ingredients except kraut. Bake at 300 degrees for 1
hr. for balls make meat mixture into balls and wrap firmly with kraut
and place in a baking dish. Bake same as loaf.
