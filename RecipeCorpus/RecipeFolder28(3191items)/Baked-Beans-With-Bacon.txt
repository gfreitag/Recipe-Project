Title: Baked Beans With Bacon
Categories: Crockpot, Healthy, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
-- cut into thin strips
2 tb Light molasses
1 tb Light brown sugar; packed
1 1/2 tb Apple cider vinegar
1 ts Powdered mustard
1 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/8 ts Ground ginger
1 lg Onion; finely chopped
1    Garlic clove; minced
4 cn (16 oz) vegetarian beans,
-- low-sodium w/sauce
-- or regulr vegetarian
2/3 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Long-grain rice; uncooked
1 pk (6 oz) canadian bacon
Recipe by: Skinny One-Pot Meals - ISBN 0-940625-75-X Combine all
ingredients in large crock pot. Cover and cook on high 1 hour. Stir
mixture. Turn heat to low and cook an additional 6 to 7 hours or until
flavors are well blended.
