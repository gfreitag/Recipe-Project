Title: 231307 Boterkoek (Butter Cake From Holland)
Categories: West europe, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 t  Salt
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 t  Baking powder
1 c  Margarine
3    Eggs
2 c  Sugar
1    2 tsp. almond extract
Beat margarine and sugar.  Add eggs and beat well.  Add remaining
ingredients; dough will be fairly stiff.  Pat into two 9-inch round
cake pans and bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes until golden
brown. Cut into wedges.
