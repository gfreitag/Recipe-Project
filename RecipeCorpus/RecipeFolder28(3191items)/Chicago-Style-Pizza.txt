Title: Chicago Style Pizza
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
10    Refrigerated biscuits
1/4 c  Mushroom stems and pieces,
Drained
1    Tomato, sliced
1 c  Mozzarella cheese, grated
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
6 oz Can of tomato paste
2 tb Parmesan cheese
1 ts Oregano
1 1/4 ts Salt
Grease a 9 inch pan.  Brown in a skillet the ground beef and onion.
Drain beef and stir in tomato paste, 1 tb Parmesan cheese, oregano,
salt and pepper.  Simmer while preparing crust.  Separate biscuits.
Arrange biscuits in a prepared pan and press over bottom and up sides
to form crust. Spoon hot meat mixture into crust.  Arrange on top;
mushrooms, tomato slices, 1 tb Parmesan cheese and Mozzarella cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes or until the crust is a deep
golden brown. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   03-09-95
