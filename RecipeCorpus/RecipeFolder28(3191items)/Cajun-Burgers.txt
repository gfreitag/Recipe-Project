Title: Cajun Burgers
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Cajun's Blackened Seasoning
1 lb Lean ground beef
2 tb Worchestershire Sauce
Mix the Worchestershire sauce and the Blackened seasoning with the
ground beef (mix well). Shape into 4 patties, and grill.

From the Kitchen of: Gary & Margie Hartford, Eugene OR  (1:152/19)
