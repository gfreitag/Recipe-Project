Title: Orange Tangerine Beef
Categories: Meats
Yield: 3 servings
3/4 lb Flank steak; sliced
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
1 ts Sesame oil
1/3 c  Peanut oil
2    Red chilies; dried  halved
5 pn Orange peel; dried  soaked
2 ts Soy sauce; dark
2 ts Rice wine
1 ts Ginger; chopped fine;
1 ts Cornstarch
MMMMM-------------------------SEASONING------------------------------
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Sesame oil
1/2 ts Szechuan peppercorns; roaste
2 ts Soy sauce; dark
1/4 ts Salt
Combine beef with marinade; let stand 20 min. Heat oil in a wok or
large skillet until very hot. Remove bbef from marinade and add to
oil; stir-fry until brown (abt. 2 min). Remove and drain. Remove all
but 1 Tbs oil, reheat il and add chili peppers; stir-fry 10 sec. Add
beef, orange peel and seasonings. Stir-fry 4 min, mixing well. Serve
at once.
