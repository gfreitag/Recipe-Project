Title: Mud Pie
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 6 servings
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
3 tb Milk
1 tb Corn syrup
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/4 c  Roasted almonds; diced
2 c  Chocolate wafer cookie crumb
4 tb Butter; melted
1 1/2 qt Favorite chocolate or
Coffee ice cream; softened
-- slightly
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:37 1. In a medium bowl, mix together chocolate
cookie crumbs and butter. Press into bottom and up sides of a 9-inch
pie pan. Freeze 1/2 hour. Spread softened ice cream evenly over
crust, rounding off top slightly. Freeze until ice cream is firm, at
least 2 hours.

2. Meanwhile, in a 1-qt glass bowl, combine chocolate chips and milk.
Heat in microwave on High 1 to 1 1/2 mins, or until melted and smooth
when stirred. Stir in corn syrup and vanilla. Refrigerate until
chilled but not set, stirring a couple of times.

3. Spread cool chocolate mixture over top of frozen pie. Sprinkle
almonds on top and return to freezer until firm, about 2 hours.
