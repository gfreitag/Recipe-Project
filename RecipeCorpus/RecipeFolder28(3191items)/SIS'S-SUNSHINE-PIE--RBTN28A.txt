Title: SIS'S SUNSHINE PIE  RBTN28A
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
Drained
1 ct Small whipped topping
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
1 cn Small frozen lemonade(thawed
1 cn Small crushed pineapple
Mix milk, pineapple, and lemonade together until smooth. Fold in
whipped topping. Pour into baked 9-inch pie crust. Chill. "note"
Graham cracker crust is almost as good.
