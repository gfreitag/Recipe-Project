Title: Oven Stew  RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 12 servings
1 ea Large onion sliced & seperat
2 c  Celery-large pieces
4 ea Potatoes-cut in 1" cubes
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ea Salt & pepper as needed
2 lb Stew meat
1/4 c  Flour
1/4 c  Oil
1 ea Clove minced garlic
1/2 c  Chili sauce
28 oz Sliced stewed tomatoes
Dredge meat in flour and saute in oil with garlic in a large dutch
oven. Add rest of the ingredients, stir well. Cook covered at 325*
for 1 1/2 hrs. Remove lid and bake 1 hr. more.
