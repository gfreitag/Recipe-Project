Title: Quick Khoya
Categories: Condiments, Indian
Yield: 3 ounces
2 tb Lukewarm milk
1/2 c  Full cream milk powder
2 ts Ghee, melted
Combine all ingredients in a bowl until a soft bowl forms.

Note: I don't know the US equivalent of khoya or full cream milk.
