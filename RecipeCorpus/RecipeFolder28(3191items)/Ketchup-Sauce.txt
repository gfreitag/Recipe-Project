Title: Ketchup Sauce
Categories: Condiments, Dress/stuff
Yield: 2 servings
2 ts Tamari soy sauce; low-sodium
1/4 ts Onion powder
1/8 ts Ground oregano
1 c  Tomato sauce
6 oz Tomato paste
1 1/2 tb Cider vinegar
Recipe by: McDougall Plan Preparation Time: 0:05 Mix all ingredients
in a jar. Store covered in the refrigerator. Will keep for several
weeks. From the collection of Sue Smith, S.Smith34, Uploaded June 16,
1994
