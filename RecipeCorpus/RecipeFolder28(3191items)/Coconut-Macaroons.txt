Title: Coconut Macaroons
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
16 oz Shredded coconut
15 oz Milnot
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
Mix Milnot and coconut thoroughly.  Add vanilla.  Bake 1 inch apart
on a well greased cookie sheet for 8 to 10 minutes at 350 degrees
until lightly brown.  Let cool; remove to wire rack. Randy Rigg
