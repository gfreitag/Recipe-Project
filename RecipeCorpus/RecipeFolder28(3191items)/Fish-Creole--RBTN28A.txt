Title: Fish Creole  RBTN28A
Categories: Fish
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Instant chicken bouillon gra
1 x  Dash bottled hot pepper sauc
1/2 c  Water
1 tb Cornstarch
3 tb Cold water
3 c  Hot cooked rice
16 oz Fresh or frozen fillets
1/3 c  Chopped onion
1/3 c  Chopped green pepper
1 ea Clove garlic minced
2 tb Water
16 oz Can tomatoes, cut up
2 tb Parsley
Thaw fish if frozen. Cut into 1-inch cubes. Combine onion, green
pepper, garlic and 2 tablespoons water. Cook, covered, until tender.
Add undrained tomatoes, parsley, bouillon granules, hot pepper sauce,
and 1/2 cup water. Simmer, covered, 10 minutes. Blend cornstarch and
3 tablespoons cold water. Stir into tomato mixture. Cook and stir
until thickened. Stir in fish. Simmer, covered, 5 to 7 minutes. Serve
over rice. Makes 6 servings...
