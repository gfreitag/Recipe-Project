Title: Nacho Skillet Casserole
Categories: Beef, Tex/mexican, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Chili Powder
1 cn Kidney Beans, drained (15oz)
1 cn Tomato Sauce (15oz)
1 cn Mexicorn, undrained (12oz)
2 c  Tortilla Chips
4 oz Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 lb Hamburger
1 md Onion, chopped
1 md Green Pepper, chopped
3    Garlic Cloves, chopped
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Oregano Leaves
In large skillet, brown hamburger and onion; drain well.  Stir in
sugar, oregano, chili powder, kidney beans, tomato sauce and corn.
Simmer 10-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until most of the
moisture is absorbed. Sprinkle tortilla chips evenly over meat
mixture; top with cheese. Cover, simmer 2-3 minutes or until cheese
is melted. Serve immediately. Submitted By JIM MOREY   On   03-08-95
