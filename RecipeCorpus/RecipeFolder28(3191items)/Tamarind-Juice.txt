Title: Tamarind Juice
Categories: Fruit, Indian
Yield: 1 cup
1 c  Warm water
1    Lemon-sized piece tamarind
Soak tamarind in warm water for about 10 minutes. Squeeze and extract
as much juice as possible from the pulp, adding a little extra warm
water if necessary.
