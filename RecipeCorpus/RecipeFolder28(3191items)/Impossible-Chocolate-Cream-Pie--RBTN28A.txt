Title: Impossible Chocolate Cream Pie  RBTN28A
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Bisquick
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Margarine
2 ea Unsweetened chocolate square
Melt 2 chocolate squares slowly on top of range or Microwave. Blend
all together in blender about 1 minute. Pour in a greased pie plate.
Bake in 350* oven for 1/2 hour or until set. After cool top with whip
cream, chocolate sprinkles or what ever you like...
