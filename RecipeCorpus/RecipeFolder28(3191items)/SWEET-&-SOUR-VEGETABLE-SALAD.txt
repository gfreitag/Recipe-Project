Title: SWEET & SOUR VEGETABLE SALAD
Categories: Main dish, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 99 servings
1 c  FRENCH STYLE GREEN BEANS
1 x  WATER CHESTNUTS
1 x  ENGLISH PEAS
1 x  BELL PEPPER
1 c  CHOPPED CELERY
1/2 ts DILL SEED
3/4 c  WESSON OIL
1 ts COARSE BLACK PEPPER
1 c  SHOE PEG.CORN
1 x  SLICED CARROTS
1 x  SMALL JAR PIMENTOS
1 x  BEAN SPROUTS
1 x  MED ONION
1 c  VINEGAR
1 1/2 c  SUGAR
2 ts SALT
6 x  TABASCO SAUCE
NOTE: X MEANS AS NEEDED::: BRING TO A BOIL, vinegar, dill seed, sugar,
wesson oil, salt, black pepper, tabasco & POUR OVER VEGETAABLES AND
REFREGERATE..
