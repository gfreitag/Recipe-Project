Title: No-Toil Devilish Pineapple Dessert
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2    Sticks butter; melted
1 c  Blanched almonds; slivered
Whipped cream or vanilla
-- ice cream
1 cn (20 oz) crushed pineapple
-- in unsweetend juice
1 pk Devil's food cake mix
(no pudding incl.)(18.25 oz)
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:40 1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Spread undrained
pineapple evenly in a 9 x13-inch baking pan. Sprinkle dry cake mix on
top. Drizzle melted butter evenly over cake. Sprinkle almonds on
surface.

2. Bake 25 to 30 mins. Cut into squares. Serve warm or cold topped
with whipped cream or ice cream.
