Title: Horseradish Turkey and Ham Melt
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
6 sl Cooked ham
6 sl Ripe tomato
1 md Green pepper;sliced
6 sl Cheddar cheese
6 sl Rye bread
1/3 c  Butter or margarine;softened
2 tb Prepared horseradish
6 sl Cooked turkey
Heat broiler. Toast bread. In small bowl stir together butter and
horseradish; spread butter mixture on toast. Layer each slice toast
with 1 slice turkey, 1 slice ham, 1 slice tomato and 1 slice green
pepper. Place sandwiches on cookie sheet. Broil 3 to 4 inches from
heat for 3 to 4 minutes or until sandwiches are heated through. Top
with cheese; broil 2 to 3 minutes or until cheese is melted.
