Title: Potato Pancakes^
Categories: Healthy
Yield: 16 servings
1/4 c  Flour
1 tb Low sodium soy sauce
3 lb Potatoes, grated
4    Egg whites, beaten
1 lg Onion, grated
Grate potatoes into cold water so they don't turn brown. then squeeze
water out quickly. Add eg whites, mix well to coat; then add onion,
flour and soy sauce. Spray a nonstick skillet with Pam. Drop
spoonfuls of mixture into skillet; cook until crispy. makes 16
pancakes.

Calories: 17 Protein: 6g Carbohydrates: 20g Fat: 0g Sodium: 37mg
Cholesterol: 0mg
