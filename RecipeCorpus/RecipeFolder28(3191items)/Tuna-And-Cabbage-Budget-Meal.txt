Title: Tuna And Cabbage Budget Meal
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Tuna
-Rice or noodles
-Salt and pepper to taste
1    Head cabbage
1    Onion
1    Tomato
Put 1 to 2 tablespoons oil in large skillet.  Brown sliced onion in
oil and add chopped tomato.  Cook until tomato begins to fall apart
and then add large sliced cabbage.  When cabbage begins to soften,
add can of tuna fish and cook an additional 5 to 10 minutes. Serve
over platter of rice or noodles.
