Title: Quick Lunch: Vegetable Saute
Categories: Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------SAUTE IN VEGETABLE STOCK-----------------------
2 ts {or more} chilli
2 ts Minced garlic
1 ea Taco seasoning mix
2    Larges onions, diced
1    Carrot, grated
1    Green pepper, diced
2 ts Cumin
MMMMM--------------------------THROW IN-------------------------------
1 cn Tomatoes
1 c  Leftover rice & peas.
1/4 c  Cracked wheat
1    To 2 cups frozen corn
1 cn Kidney beans, drained &
Rinsed
It made a huge amount, so will no doubt be eating for rest of week.

Source: apparent improvisation
