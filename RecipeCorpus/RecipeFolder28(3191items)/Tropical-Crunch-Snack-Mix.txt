Title: Tropical Crunch Snack Mix
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 11 cups
8 oz Dried pineapple pieces
6 oz Coconut chips
12 oz Honey roasted peanuts
10 oz Salted cashews
10 oz Dried banana chips
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.  Store in airtight container.
