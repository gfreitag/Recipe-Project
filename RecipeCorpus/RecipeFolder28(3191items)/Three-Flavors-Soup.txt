Title: Three Flavors Soup
Categories: Chinese, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
8    Water chestnuts
2    Green onions
1 ts Salt
5 c  Light, clear chicken stock
10    Fresh medium shrimp (OR
1/4 lb Cooked baby shrimp)
Preparation:  Wash, shell & devein shrimp.  Slice water chestnuts
into thin circles.  Mince entire green onions. Bring stock to boil
with onions & water chestnuts. Add shrimp & salt. Taste & correct
salt if necessary. Return soup to boil.  Serve.
