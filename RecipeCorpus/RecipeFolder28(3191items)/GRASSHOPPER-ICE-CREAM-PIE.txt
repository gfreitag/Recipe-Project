Title: GRASSHOPPER ICE CREAM PIE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb White creme de cacao*
1 ea 9 inch chocolate crumb crust
1 pt Vannila ice cream,softened
2 tb Green creme de menthe'
3 c  (8oz)Cool whip, thawed
*or use 6 drops green food coloring, 1 teaspoon vanilla and 1/4 t.
peppermint extract.

Combine ice cream, lemon juice, creme de menthe and creme de cacao in
bowl. Fold in whipped topping, blending well.

Freeze, if necessary, until mixture will mound. Spoon into crust.
Freeze until firm, at least 4 hours. Garnish with chocolate curls, if
desired.

Store any leftover pie in freezer.
