Title: Broccoli Bacon Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 servings
-and crumbled
-DRESSING:
3 tb Vinegar
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/3 c  Sugar
1 lg Bunch broccoli, separated
-into florets
1 sm Red onion, coarsely
-chopped
1 c  Raisins
10    To 12 bacon strips, cooked
In a large serving bowl, combine the broccoli, onion, raisins and
bacon, set aside. In a mixing bowl, combine dressing ingredients.
Just before serving, pour dressing over broccoli mixture; toss to
coat. Yield: 6-8 servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
