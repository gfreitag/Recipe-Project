Title: Zucchini-corn Skillet
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1 x  Large onion, chopped
1 x  Clove garlic,minced
8 oz Egg beater with cheese
1/4 ts Ground red pepper
1 tb Sweet Unsalted Margarine
4 c  Thinly sliced zucchini
8 3/4 oz Cream style corn
1 tb Chopped parsley
In large skillet, over medium heat, melt margarine. Add onion,zuchini
and galic; cook, stirring occasionally, until zucchini is tender,
about 8 to 10 minutes. Stir in corn, Egg beater with cheese, parsley
and red pepper until blended. Reduce heat; cook, uncovered, until
set, about 30 minutes, shaking pan occasionally.
