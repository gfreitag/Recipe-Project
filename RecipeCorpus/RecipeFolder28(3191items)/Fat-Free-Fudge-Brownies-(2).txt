Title: Fat-Free Fudge Brownies (2)
Categories: Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
3    Egg whites or equivalent
1 t  Vanilla
1/4 ts Baking soda
3/4 c  Whole wheat pastry flour
1 c  Sucanat
1/4 t  Salt
1/3 c  Unsweetened applesauce
Combine dry ingredients.  Beat wet ingredients.  Combine and beat wet
and dry ingredients. Pour into an 8x8 in pan sprayed lightly with
nonstick cooking spray. Bake at 325 F. for 25-30 min., or just until
the edges are firm and the center is set. Cool and cut into squares.

Source: a slight modification of the Brownie recipe posted a couple of
weeks ago.

Posted by Cindy Tobias <CLTOBIAS@CCIT.ARIZONA.EDU> to the Fatfree
Digest [Volume 15 Issue 14] Feb. 14, 1995.
