Title: German-Style Dinner
Categories: Crockpot, Healthy
Yield: 6 servings
-- small pieces
2    Bay leaves
1/8 ts Black pepper
3/4 c  Cranberry juice cocktail
-- or apple cider
1 1/2 tb Light brown sugar; packed
2 c  Sauerkraut, fresh
2 lg Apples, cored & thinly slice
1 lg Onion; finely chopped
2 c  Potatoes, peel & thinly slic
1 1/2 c  Rutabaga cubes; peeled
6 oz Ham steak, reduced-sodium
-- or regular; cut in
Recipe by: Skinny One-Pot Meals - ISBN 0-940625-75-X In large crock
pot, combine sauerkraut, apples, onion, potatoes, rutabaga and ham.
Add bay leaves and pepper. In small bowl, mix together cranberry
juice and brown sugar. Pour over sauerkraut mixture.

Cover and cook on high heat 1 hour. Stir mixture. Reduce heat to low,
and cook an additional 6 to 7 hours.
