Title: Chili Taco Salad  RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Guacamole
1/2 c  2oz.shredded cheddar cheese
2 c  Corn chips
1 cn Wolf brand plain chili 15oz.
2 1/2 c  Shredded lettuce
1/2 c  Ripe olive slices
1 ea Medium-sized tomato, chopped
In 2-qt. salad bowl, layer hot chili, lettuce, olives and tomato.
Spoon guacamole over tomato, top with cheese and corn chips. Serve
immediately..
