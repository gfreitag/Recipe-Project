Title: Cream Gravy RBTN28A
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 x  Salt & pepper
2 ea Bacon slices crumbled
4 tb Drippings (bacon etc.)
4 tb Flour
2 1/2 c  Whole milk
Add the flour to drippings & cook, stirring to keep from burning & is
a rich brown color. Pour milk in all at once & add crumbled bacon,
cook, stirring until of desired thickness.
