Title: Corn Vegetable Medley RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM---------------------PREP TIME 10 MINS--------------------------
MMMMM---------------------COOK TIME: 25 MINS--------------------------
1 c  Sliced carrots
1 c  Cauliflowerets
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese(opt)
1 cn 10 3/4ozCampbells New Golden
Corn Soup
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Broccoli flowerets
1. In saucepan, heat soup and milk to boiling, stirring often. Stir in
vegetables. 2. Return to boiling. Cover; cook over low heat 20
min. or until
vegetables are tender, stirring often. Stir in cheese. Heat
through.

Note: If desired, substitute 1 bag (16oz.) frozen broccoli, carrots
and cauliflower for fresh vegetables. Reduce cookin time to 15 min.
