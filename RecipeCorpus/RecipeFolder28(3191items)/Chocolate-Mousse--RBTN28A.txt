Title: Chocolate Mousse  RBTN28A
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
8 oz Cool whip topping
1 cn Evaporated skim milk
7 oz Pkg.Choc. covered Caramel
1/4 c  Heavy Cream (whipping)
1/4 ts Rum extract
Put candy & heavy cream in non-stick sauce pan over low low heat &
stir until smooth. Stir in rum, let cool 4 or 5 minutes then fold in
cool whip until smooth. Put in serving dishes & top with whipped
evaporated milk...
