Title: Pineapple Barbecue Glaze
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 c  Orange marmalade
2 tb A-1 (tm) Steak Sauce
3 oz Pineapple juice, frozen
-concentrate
In a saucepan, combine all ingredients. Bring to boiling. Use to baste
poultry or pork, last 10 minutes of grilling.
