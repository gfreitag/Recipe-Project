Title: ONION ROASTED POTATOES   RBTN28A
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1/3 c  Olive oil or vegetable oil
1 ea Envelope onion soup mix
2 lb Potatoes cut in large chunks
Preheat oven to 450 F. In a large plastic bag, add all ingredients.
Close bag and shake until potatoes are evenly coated. Empty potatoes
into shallow baking or roasting pan; discard bag. Bake, stirring
occasionally, 40 minutes or until potatoes are tender and golden
brown. Garnish, if desired with chopped parsley. Makes about 8
servings.
