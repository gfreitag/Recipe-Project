Title: Smash Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 20 servings
8 oz Package cream cheese
1 lb Box powdered sugar
1 T  Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Margarine
3    Eggs
1 pk Duncan Hines yellow cake mix
1 c  Pecans, chopped
Dump cake mix, softened margarine and 1 egg in bowl.  Mix well and
gently press in a 9x13 inch pan.  Take hands and press in cup of
chopped pecans; set aside.  Mix cream cheese, powdered sugar and 2
eggs with mixer until creamy.  Add vanilla and pour over cake
mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.

Note:  Use a piece of Saran wrap to cover cake when pressing in the
cake mixture and the nuts. The nuts will float, if not pressed in
well, when you pour in the cheese mixture.
