Title: Anna M. Stamplecoskie's Potato Dumplings
Categories: Vegetables, Canadian
Yield: 15 servings
Salt to taste
Cream
1 1/2 c  Raw potatoes
1 c  Flour
4 1/2 qt Water
Grate potatoes finely.  Squeeze dry in a clean cloth.  Add flour and
mix well with potatoes.  Bring water to the boil and add salt.  Make
little balls of the flour/potato mix.  Drop into the boiling water
and boil for 16 minutes.  At the last minute, add cream.

From: Macdonald Was Late for Dinner: A Slice of Culinary Life in
Early Canada by Patricia Beeson
