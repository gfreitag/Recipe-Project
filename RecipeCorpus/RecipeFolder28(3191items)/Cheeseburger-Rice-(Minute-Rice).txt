Title: Cheeseburger Rice (Minute Rice)
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Rice, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
1 1/2 c  Minute Rice, uncooked
1 c  Cheddar Cheese, shredded
1 lb Ground beef
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Catsup
2 tb Mustard, prepared
Brown meat and onion in large skillet.  Drain off fat.  Stir in
water, catsup, mustard, salt and pepper.  Bring to a boil.
Stir in rice; cover.  Remove from heat.  Let stand 5 minutes.
Fluff with fork.  Sprinkle with cheese; cover.  Let stand 3 minutes
or until cheese melts.
Garnish with lettuce leaves, pickle and tomato slices.
