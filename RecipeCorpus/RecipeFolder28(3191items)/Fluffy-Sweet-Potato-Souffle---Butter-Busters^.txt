Title: Fluffy Sweet Potato Souffle - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 6 ----------
1/4 c  Light rum
**OR**
1 t  Rum extract
1 t  Cinnamon
3 c  Mashed, peeled, cooked
-sweet potatoes
1/4 c  Brown sugar*
1 c  Egg Beaters, beaten
*Or 2 tablespoons brown sugar and 1/2 teaspoon Sweet 'n Low Brown
Preheat oven to 350F. Combine all ingredients and beat well with
mixer. Fold into a 2-qt. baking dish that has been sprayed with a
nonfat cooking spray. Bake 45-50 minutes or until a knife inserted in
the center comes out clean and top is firm and golden brown. Per
sugar serving: 204 cal., 4g fat (2%), 0mg chol., 4g fiber, 5g pro.,
37g carb., 63mg sod. Per S&L serving: 193 cal., 34g carb. Butter
Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw
2-95.
