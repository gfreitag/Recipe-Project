Title: Crab-Stuffed Avocado
Categories: Vegetables, Seafood, Dips/spread, Mom's, Salads
Yield: 4 servings

1/8 t  Worcestershire
2 ea Ripe avocados
Lemon juice
Salt
1 1/2 c  Chilled, cooked or canned
-crab or lobster meat
1/2 c  Mayonnaise or cooked salad
-dressing
1/2 c  Minced celery
1/4 c  Minced pimento
2 t  Lemon juice
ds Tabasco
Combine first 6 ingredients. Halve avocados lengthwise; remove pits;
peel, sprinkle with lemon juice and salt. Arrange each half on bed of
crisp greens; fill halves with crab meat; then top each with some of
dressing. Makes 4 servings.
