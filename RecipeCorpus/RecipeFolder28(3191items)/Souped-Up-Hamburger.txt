Title: Souped Up Hamburger
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Mushroom soup
1 cn Onion rings
8 oz Mozzarella or cheddar cheese
2 lb Ground beef
1 pk Fresh or frozen broccoli
1 cn Chicken and rice soup
While browning ground beef, cook broccoli.  Mix soups together and
heat. Place half of browned beef into an 8 x 10 inch casserole dish,
covering bottom of dish.  Layer in half the broccoli, chopped onion
rings, cheese and soup.  Repeat layers.

Bake in a 350 F oven until mixture bubbles.

Serves 4 to 6.
