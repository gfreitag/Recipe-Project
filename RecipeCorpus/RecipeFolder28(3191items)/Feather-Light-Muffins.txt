Title: Feather-Light Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Milk
-TOPPING:
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Around cinnamon
1/2 c  Butter or margarine,
-melted
1/3 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 1/2 c  Cake flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
In a mixing bowl, cream shortening, sugar and egg. Combine dry
ingredients; add to creamed mixture alternately with milk. Fill
greased muffin tins 2/3 full. Bake at 325! for 20-25 minutes or until
golden. Let cool for 3-4 minutes. Meanwhile, combine sugar and
cinnamon in a small bowl. Roll warm muffins in melted butter, then in
sugar mixture. Serve warm.

Yield: 8-10 muffins. From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.
Feb 1994
