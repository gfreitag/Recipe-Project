Title: Stir-Fried Cucumbers and Beef
Categories: Asian, Stir fry, Beef, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Salt
1 tb Salad oil
4 sm Cucumbers
5 1/4 oz Lean beef
MMMMM-------------------------MIXTURE A------------------------------
Salt and pepper
1/4 ts Crushed garlic
MMMMM-------------------------MIXTURE B------------------------------
1/2 ts Soy sauce
1/2 ts Ground chili pepper
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Finely chopped green onion
1 ts Roasted sesame seeds
1 ts Sesame oil
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Chili pepper; shredded
Cut off both ends of cucumbers and slice thinly.

In a bowl place the cucumbers and sprinkle with 1 teaspoon salt; let
stand 5-10 minutes.

When cucumbers are flexible, wrap in a cloth or paper towel and
squeeze out water.

Cut beef into julienne strips.

Heat salad oil.  Quickly stir-fry beef over high heat and season with
Mixture A.

Add cucumber slices and stir-fry briefly.  Season with Mixture B.
Transfer to serving plate and garnish with shredded chili pepper.
