Title: Oyster Crackers
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 cups
1/2    Envelope original ranch
- dressing mix
- equals 1 tablespoon
1 ts Dill weed
1    Box oyster crackers
- approx 14 oz
- generic brand is fine
1/2 c  Oil
Preheat oven to 250 degrees. Mix together oil, ranch dressing powder,
and dill weed in a glass measuring cup.  Place crackers in a bowl.
Pour mixture over crackers and stir well.  Spread crackers in a
single layer on a cookie sheet with sides and bake for 20 minutes.

Source: Pat Bowman - brought this home from visiting Pat Kratka in
North Carolina 3/95.  MM format April Bowman-Fox

Comments: I thought these crackers would be tasty - especially in
cream soups, but we ended up eating a whole
batch just as a snack. I'm sure the sodium content
is astronomical.
