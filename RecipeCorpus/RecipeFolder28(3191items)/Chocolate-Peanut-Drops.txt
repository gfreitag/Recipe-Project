Title: Chocolate Peanut Drops
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 48 servings
1/3 c  Peanut butter
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
2/3 c  Unsalted peanuts, chopped
1 c  Chocolate chips
1 1/4 c  Flour
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
8 tb Unsalted butter
1 c  Brown sugar, lightly packed
Recipe by: Melanie Bernard and Brooke Dojny

Sift together flour, soda, and salt.  Cream butter and brown sugar.
Add peanut butter and mix well. Add egg and vanilla.  Add dry mixture
and mix until blended. Stir in peanuts and chocolate chips.

Drop by rounded tablespoonsful onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake in
center of 375 degree oven 10-12 minutes, until set.
