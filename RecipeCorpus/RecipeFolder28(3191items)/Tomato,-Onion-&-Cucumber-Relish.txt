Title: Tomato, Onion & Cucumber Relish
Categories: Relishes, Indian, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cayenne
Freshly ground black pepper
1/2 ts Cumin seeds, roasted
1 md Onion
1 md Tomato, sliced thin
2 md Cucumbers, sliced thin
2 tb Lemon juice
Peel onion, slice thinly & soak in iced water for 30 minutes.  Drain
& pat dry.  Separate rounds into rings.

In a serving plate, arrange tomato slices in a single layer, slight
overlap is okay.  Arrange cucumbers over the top.  Scatter onion
rings over the cucumbers.  Sprinke with lemon juice, salt, cayenne,
pepper & cumin. Toss just the onion rings, leaving everything else
undisturbed.

Madhur Jaffrey, "A Taste of India"
