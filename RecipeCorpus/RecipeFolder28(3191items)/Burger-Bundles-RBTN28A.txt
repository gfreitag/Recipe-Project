Title: Burger Bundles RBTN28A
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 24 servings
1/3 c  Dry bread crumbs
3/4 lb Velveeta cut into 1/4"slices
24 ea Dinner rolls (2 Packages)
x  Dill pickle slices
2 lb Lean ground beef
1 pk Onion soup mix (1 1/4 oz)
1 ea Egg
1/2 ts Pepper
2 tb Water
Heat oven to 400 F. Mix ingredients (except cheese) & press onto
bottom of 15x10x1 inch baking pan. Prick meat mixture with fork. Bake
10-15 minutes or to desired doneness. Drain. Place Velveeta slices
over meat. Bake 1 minute or until Velveeta begins to melt. Cut into
24 squares. For each sandwich, cover bottom half of dinner roll with
burger square, pickle slice and remaining dinner roll half. Makes
24...
