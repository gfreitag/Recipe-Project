Title: 632766 Individual Pork Tenderloin
Categories: Pork
Yield: 4 servings
8 sl Bacon
1    To 2 c. Russian dressing
2    Boneless pork tenderloins,
-(1 to 1 1/2 lbs. each)
-halved
Cut each tenderloin in half.  Wrap bacon around each portion; secure
it with a toothpick.  Marinate in Russian dressing for 24 hours.
Place on a roasting pan and cook 50 minutes at 375 degrees or until
the internal temperature is 160 degrees.  Do not overcook. Also
delicious cooked on grill.
