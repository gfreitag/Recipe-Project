Title: 521186 Fruit Jewels
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Snacks, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Orange peel, grated
1/2 c  Evaporated milk, undiluted
2 c  Mixed candied fruit,
-chopped
1 c  Walnuts, coarsely chopped
6 oz (1 c.) semi-sweet chocolate
-pieces
1 c  Seedless raisins
1 c  Regular all-purpose flour,
-sifted
1 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
2 t  Ground cinnamon
1/2 t  Ground nutmeg
1/4 t  Ground cloves
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
Sift the flour, baking powder, salt and spices together; set aside.
Cream butter or margarine; gradually add brown sugar, creaming well.
Mix in orange peel.  Alternately add dry ingredients in thirds and
evaporated milk in halves, mixing until blended after each addition.
Stir in remaining ingredients. Drop by heaping teaspoonfuls onto
lightly greased cookie sheets.  Bake at 375 degrees about 12 minutes.
Cool cookies on wire rack. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
03-03-95
