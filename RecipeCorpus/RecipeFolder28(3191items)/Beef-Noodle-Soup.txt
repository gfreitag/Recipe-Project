Title: Beef Noodle Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Beef, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
-(16 oz.)
1 t  Dried oregano
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1 c  Uncooked medium egg noodles
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 cn Italian stewed tomatoes (14
-1/2 oz. ea.)
2 cn Beef broth (10 1/2 oz. ea.)
1 cn Mixed Vegetables, drained
In a Dutch oven or soup kettle, brown ground beef and onion; drain.
Add tomatoes, broth, vegetables and seasonings. Bring to a boil; add
noodles. Reduce heat to medium-low; cover and cook for 10-15 minutes
or until noodles are done. Serves 6-8 (2 quarts) SOURCE:* Taste of
Home Magazine, Feb./Mar. 1995 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 2/95
