Title: Thai Beef Curry
Categories: Thai, Beef, Curries
Yield: 2 servings
-inch lengths (fresh or
-frozen)
2 md Mushrooms
3 tb Dry roasted peanuts
1    Fresh green chili, seeded
-and chopped
1/4 c  Water
1 tb Fish sauce
2 ts Brown sugar
2 tb Peanut oil
1 ts Red Curry Paste
12 oz Lean beef cut into finger
-thin slices
1    Stalk lemon grass finely
-chopped
-OR-
-(zest of 1/2 lemon)
4 oz Green beans cut into 1 1/2
cut beef into finger thin slices.

In wok, heat oil, add curry paste, stir 30 seconds.  Add beef and
lemon grass (or lemon zest) and stir fry for 1 min or until lightly
browned. Remove beef.

Add a little oil if needed, Add beans and mushrooms, stir fry 3 mins.
Add Peanuts and chili.

Stir in water, fish sauce and sugar, cook about 2 mins or until beans
are crisp tender.

Put beef back in wok, stir and serve.

Serve with noodles or rice -

2 servings
