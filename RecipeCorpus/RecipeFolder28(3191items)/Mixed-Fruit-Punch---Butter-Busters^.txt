Title: Mixed Fruit Punch - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Beverages
Yield: 50 ----------
3 c  Sugar
OR
2 c  Sugar
AND
12 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
5 c  Water
6 ea Bananas
1 cn (12oz) lemonade
2 cn (12oz) frozen orange juice
1 cn (46oz) pineapple juice,
-unsweetened
6 qt Diet Sprite
Mash bananas and place in blender with water and sugar. Blend until
smooth. Combine with orange juice, lemonade and pineapple juice. Add
sprite just before serving. Rum can be added if desired. Per sugar
serving: 109cal., 0.1g fat (1%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 1g pro., 27g
carb., 27mg sod. Per S&L serving: 95 cal., 23g carb. Butter Busters
by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 3-95.
