Title: Meat Cholent
Categories: Jewish, Beef, Main dish
Yield: 8 - 10 folks
1/4 ts Pepper;
1/4 ts Ginger;
1 c  Pearl barley;
2 tb Flour;
2 ts Paprika;
2 c  Dried lima beans;
3 lb Brisket;
3    Onion; diced
3 tb Fat;
2 ts Salt;
Soak the beans overnight in water to cover.  Drain.  Use a heavy
saucepan or dutch oven and brown the meat and onions in the fat.
Sprinkle with the salt, pepper and ginger.  Add the beans and barley
and sprinkle with the flour and paprika.  Add enough boiling water to
cover one inch above the mixture.   Cover tightly.  Cholent may be
baked for 24 hours in a 250 degrees oven or, for quicker cooking in a
350 degrees oven 4-5 hours. Slice the meat and serve with the barley
and beans. Serves 8-10. Source: The Art of Jewish Cooking, Jennie
Grossinger, 1958 Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her
Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On   03-02-95
