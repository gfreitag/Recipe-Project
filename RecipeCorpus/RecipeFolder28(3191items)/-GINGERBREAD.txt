Title:  GINGERBREAD
Categories: Desserts, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Water
1/3 c  Shortening, melted
1 c  Molasses
1    Egg, beaten
-Glaze (below)
2 3/4 c  Flour
1 1/2 t  Baking soda
1/2 t  Salt
1 t  Ground cinnamon
1 1/2 t  Ground ginger
1/4 t  Ground cloves
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine first 6 ingredients in a large
bowl; mix well.  Combine water, shortening, molasses and egg.  Mix
well, and stir into flour mixture. Pour batter into a greased 9"
square pan.  Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, or until a wooden pick
inserted in center comes out clean. Spread glaze on warm gingerbread,
if desired, and serve.
