Title: 661705 Maandazi  (East African Doughnut)
Categories: African, Cake/cookie, Doughnut
Yield: 4 servings
-Pinch of salt
1    Egg
1/4 c  Milk
-Oil for frying
1 1/4 c  Flour
2 T  Sugar
1 t  Baking powder
1 t  Ground cardamon
Mix together the dry ingredients.  Mix in egg and milk until well
blended. Roll out as for a jelly roll to about 3/4 inch thickness.
Cut using a doughnut cutter with center ring removed.  Fry in hot
(365 degree) oil until golden brown.  May roll in confectioners'
sugar before serving. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-04-95
