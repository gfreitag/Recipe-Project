Title: Minted Prunes
Categories: Beverages, Herbs/spice, Mom's, Fruit, Salads
Yield: 2 servings

1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/8 t  Salt
3/4 ea To 1 teasp. mint extract
2 1/2 c  Cooked prunes
1 1/2 c  Liquid drained from prunes
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
1 c  Brown sugar, packed
In saucepan, combine prunes, liquid, vinegar, sugar, cinnamon, salt.
Boil gently, covered 15 min. Remove from heat; stir in extract.
Cover; let cool. Use to garnish fruit salads. Or pit and stuff prunes
with cream cheese; serve on lettuce; pass French dressing. Makes 2
1/2 cups.
