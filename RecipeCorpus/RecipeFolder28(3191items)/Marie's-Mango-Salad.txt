Title: Marie's Mango Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
Juice and zest of 1 lime
1 t  Honey
Salt to taste
2    Medium mango; chunked
1/2    Vidalia onion; diced
1/2 c  Cilantro; chopped
1    Hot pepper; diced
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Stir gently.  Refrigerate until
ready to serve.  Source: Store ad
