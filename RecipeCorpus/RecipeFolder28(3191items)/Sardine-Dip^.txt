Title: Sardine Dip^
Categories: Healthy
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  "Our" sour cream
1 cl Garlic, minced
1/4 ts Fresh lemon juice
2 cn (3 3/4oz) sardines,
-boneless, skinless in
-water
Blend all ingredients in a bowl, cover and refrigerate until well
chilled. Makes 1 cup.

Per tablespoon: Calories: 36 Protein: 3g Carbohydrates: 1g Fat: 2g,
50%!!!!!! Sodium: 64mg Cholesterol: trace
