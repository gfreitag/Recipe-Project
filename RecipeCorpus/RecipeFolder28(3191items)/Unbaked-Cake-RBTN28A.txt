Title: Unbaked Cake RBTN28A
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
1 qt Pecans
13 oz Vanilla wafers
1 cn Eagle brand milk
8 oz Flake coconut
MMMMM-------------------FRUIT, DATES, OPTIONAL------------------------
Crunch wafers real fine & mix with pecans, Coconut & fruit. Pour milk
into mixture. Mix well, press into a pan. The harder it is pressed
the better. Cool in refrigerator, cover. Cut into squares.
