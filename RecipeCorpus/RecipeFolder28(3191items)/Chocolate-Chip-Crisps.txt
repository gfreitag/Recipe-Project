Title: Chocolate Chip Crisps
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 doz
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
12 oz Chocolate chips (1 bag)
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Shortening
2    Eggs
Cream sugar, shortening and eggs thoroughly.  Add dry ingredients to
creamed mixture.  Mix well.  Stir in chocolate chips.

Drop by teaspoon onto baking sheet.

Bake at 350 for 15 to 18 minutes.

Notes: We used butter flavored Crisco and sprayed Pam on the baking
sheets. We also double stacked the sheets (placed an empty under the
full one. Our cookies were done in about 13 minutes, so watch 'em.
