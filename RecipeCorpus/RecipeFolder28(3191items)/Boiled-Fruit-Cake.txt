Title: Boiled Fruit Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Baking powder
1 1/2 t  Mixed spices
2 ea Eggs
Nuts and flavouring
-(optional)
2 oz Brandy (optional)
1 lb Mixed dried fruit
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Margarine
1 t  Baking soda
1/2 c  Sugar
ds Of vinegar
2 c  Flour
In saucepan, mix together the first 6 ingredients.  Bring to a boil
and simmer for about 15 minutes.  Remove from heat and allow to cool.
When cool, add the remaining ingredients and mix until blended.
Spoon into pans and bake at 300F for about 1-1/2 hours.  Will fill an
8 inch square pan, or loaf pans. Origin: Kathy Davis (my Avon lady)
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
