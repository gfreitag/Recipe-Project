Title: Microwaved Chili^
Categories: Healthy, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 tb Low sodium soy sauce
1 tb Chili powder or to taste
1 ts Dried minced onion
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Tabasco sauce
OR
1 tb Hot salsa
1 lb Lean flank steak, ground
1 cn (15 1/2oz) kidney beans,
-rinsed and drained
1 cn (14 1/2oz) no salt added
-tomatoes, chopped, with
-juice
1 cn (8oz) no salt aded tomato
-sauce
Cook flank steak in a nonstick skillet until browned; drain fat.
Place in a 3-qt. microwavable dish. Add all remaining ingredients and
cook, covered on high for 5 minutes; stir. Cook on medium 10 minutes;
stir. Uncover and cook on medium 10 minutes. Serve on rice. Makes 4
servings.

Per serving without rice: Calories: 225 Protein: 23g Carbohydrates:
19g Fat: 5g Sodium: 338mg Cholesterol: 47mg
