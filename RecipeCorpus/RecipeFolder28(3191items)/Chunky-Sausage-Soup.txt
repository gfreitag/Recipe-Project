Title: Chunky Sausage Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Water
14 oz Tomatoes; diced in juice
2 cn 14 oz beef broth
7 oz Swift Brown n Serve Turkey
-Sausage
1/2 c  Elbow macaroni
1 c  Carrot; sliced
1 c  Celery; sliced
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Parsley; chopped
1/2 t  Chili powder
1/4 t  Salt
Combine all ingredients except sausage and macaroni in large saucepan
or dutch oven.  Bring to a boil over high heat. Cut sausage links into
fourths.  Stir in sausage and macaroni. Reduce heat and simmer
covered 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Source:  Swift
