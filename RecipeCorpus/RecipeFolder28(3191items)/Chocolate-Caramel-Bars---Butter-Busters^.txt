Title: Chocolate Caramel Bars - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 ----------
1/2 c  Kraft fatfree Miracle Whip
1 t  Vanilla
1 t  Bakinf soda
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Mrs. Richardsons or Kroger
-fatfree caramel topping
1 c  Braum's or Mrs.
-Richardson's fatfree fudge
-topping
1 c  Grape Nuts cereal
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
3/4 c  Sugar
OR
1/2 c  Sugar
AND 3 pk Sweet 'n Low
3/4 c  Brown sugar
OR
1/2 c  Brown sugar
AND
2 t  Sweet 'n Low Brown
Heat oven to 350F. Cream Egg Beaters, sugars, Miracle Whip and
vanilla. Add flour and baking soda. Set aside 1 cup of the batter.
Spread remaining batter into a 13 x 9" baking dish that has been
sprayed with Pam. Bake 12-15 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool
10 minutes. Spread with carames and fudge toppings. Sprinkle Grape
Nuts over this. Spoon reserved 1 cup batter by teaspoonfuls over the
Grape Nuts. continue baking 12-15 minutes or until evenly browned.
Cool; cut into squares. These can be served alone as cookie bars or
warm with fatfree ice cream or nonfat frozen yogurt on top for a
delicious dessert. Per sugar serving: 98 cal., 0.1g fat (1%), 0mg
chol., 0.4g fiber., 2g pro., 24g carb., 189mg sod. Per S&L serving:
87 cal., 21g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5
