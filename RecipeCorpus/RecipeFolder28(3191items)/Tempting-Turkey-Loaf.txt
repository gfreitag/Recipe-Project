Title: Tempting Turkey Loaf
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 7 servings
MMMMM----------------------PHILLY.INQUIRER---------------------------
1 tb GROUND THYME
2 c  FINE DRY BREAD CRUMBS
SALT AND  PEPPER TO TASTE
1/2 c  CHOPPED MUSHROOMS
MILK OR POULTRY STOCK AS NEE
2 lb GROUND TURKEY
1 tb WORCHESTERSHIRE SAUCE
1    LARGE EGG
1 tb CHOPPED PARSLEY
1    LARGE ONION
PREHEAT OVEN TO 400 DEGREES.IN MIXING BOWL,COMBINE GROUND TURKEY
WITH EGG.SAUTE ONIONSIN NON STICK SKILLET UNTIL LIMP.ADD TO TURKEY
MIXTURE ALON WITH THE BREAD CRUMBS,MUSHROOMS,WORCHESTERSHIRE
SAUCE,PARSLEY,THYME,SALT A PEPPER;BLEND WELL.THE FAT AND MOISTURE
CONTENT OF GROUND TURKEY CAN VARY.I THE MIXTURE IS DRY,MOISTEN WITH
MILK OR BROTH AS NEEDED.PLACE IN A NON STICK,OR LIGHTLY OILED 9 X 5 X
3 INCH LOAF PAN.TOP LOAF WITH A PIECE OF LIGHTLY OILED WAX PAPER,AND
COVER PAN WITH FOIL.PLACE LOAF PAN IN A LARGER PAN WITH BOILING WATER
TO ABOUT 1/2" DEEP.BAKE AT 400 DEGREES FOR TEN MINUTES;REDUCE HEAT TO
350 AND CONTINUE BAKING FOR ONE HOUR MORE OR UNTIL FIRM. REMOVE LOAF
FROM PAN;LET STAND FOR 10 MINUTES BEFORE UNMOULDING.SLIC AND SERVE
PLAIN OR,IF DESIRED,WITH A FRESH TOMATO SAUCE OR MUSHROOM GRAVY.
MAKES SIX TO EIGHT SERVINGS.
