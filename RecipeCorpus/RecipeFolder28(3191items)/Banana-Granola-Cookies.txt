Title: Banana Granola Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 5 dozen
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Granola
1/2 c  Butter/Margarine, softened
1 c  Brown Sugar, firmly packed
1    Egg
1/2 ts Vanilla Extract
1 c  Bananas, mashed
Cream butter or margarine and sugar in a large bowl.  Add the egg,
vanilla, and bananas, beating until well blended.  Add the flour,
cinnamon, baking soda and salt, and mix until blended.  Stir in
granola.

Drop by heaping teaspoons onto a greased cookie sheet, placing cookies
about 2" apart.

Bake at 375Ã¸F for 10 - 12 minutes.  Cool on a wire rack.  Makes 4-5
Dozen.
