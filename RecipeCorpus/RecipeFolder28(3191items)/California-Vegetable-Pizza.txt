Title: California Vegetable Pizza
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Artichoke hearts, chopped
1/4 c  Olives, sliced
1 ea Tomato, sliced
6 ea Green bell pepper rings
1 ea Ball wholewheat pizza dough
-- see recipe
1/2 c  Pizza sauce, see recipe
1/2 c  Broccoli, lightly steamed
Preat oven to 500F & oil a 12" pizza pan.

Place dough on a lightly floured surface & press to flatten.  Lightly
roll out dough into a 12" circle, place on pizza pan & pinch the edge
to form a rim.

Spread the sauce on the dough to within 1" of the rim & arrange the
vegetables on the sauce, ending with the tomatoes & peppers.

Bake until the crust is brown & crisp, about 15 minutes.  Remove from
oven & let cool for 5 minutes.  Slice & serve hot.
