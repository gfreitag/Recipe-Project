Title: Quick Beef Vegetable Soup
Categories: Meats, Microwave, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Marjoram leaves
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Onion salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/2 lb Package spaghetti broken
-into thirds and uncooked
Grated parmesanc cheese
-optional
1 lb Ground beef
1    (16-oz) package frozen
-mixed soup vegetables
4 c  Vegetable/cocktail/tomato
-juice
4 c  Water
2 tb Beef flavor instant
-bouillon
1 ts Basil leaves
In ducth oven, brown ground beef; drain. Stir in next ten
ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer 30 minutes.
Prepare spaghetti according to package directions; drain. Add
to soup mixture; heat through. Serve with parmesan cheese.
Refrigerate or freeze leftovers.
