Title: Cheesy Roast Beef Hash
Categories: Beef, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Chili sauce
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Shredded Cheddar or Swiss
-cheese
2 tb Thinly sliced green onion
4 c  Idaho frozen O'Brien
-potatoes
1/2 lb Cooked roast beef, cubed
1 c  Frozen sliced carrots
1    Jar (12 ounces) beef or
-mushroom gravy
In 2-quart microwave-safe casserole combine frozen potatoes, roast
beef, carrots, gravy, chili sauce and Worcestershire sauce. Cover
loosely with plastic wrap; cook on High 12 to 15 minutes or until
heated through, stirring occasionally. Top with cheese and green
onion; re-cover and let stand 2 minutes or until cheese is melted.

Makes 4 servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
