Title: Beef Supreme RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 x  Salt & pepper to taste
8 oz Carton sour cream
6 oz 2 3-ounce cubes cream cheese
6 ea Green onions, chopped
1 x  Mozarella cheese
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
14 1/2 oz Can whole tomatoes drained
10 1/2 oz Can pizza sauce
1 pk Frozen egg noodles
1 tb Sugar
Brown ground beef and drain. Add tomatoes, pizza sauce, sugar, salt
and pepper. Simmer, uncovered 10 minutes. Prepare noodles according
to package directions. Combine noodles, sour cream, cream cheese and
green onions, stirring well. Spoon half of noodles mixture into
greased baking dish, top with half the meat mixture. Repeat the
layers. Cover and bake in 350F oven for 35 minutes. "I also like to
add mozarella cheese on top about 5 minutes before it is done baking."
