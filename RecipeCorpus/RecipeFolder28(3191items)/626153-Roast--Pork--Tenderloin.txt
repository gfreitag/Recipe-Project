Title: 626153 Roast  Pork  Tenderloin
Categories: Pork
Yield: 3 servings
-Bacon strips
-Seasonings
1    Whole pork tenderloin
-Onions, sliced
-Tomatoes, sliced
Preparation : Season tenderloin with salt, pepper, and marjoram as
desired. Place onion slices the length of tenderloin; top with tomato
slices. Wrap with bacon slices secured with toothpicks.  Bake
approximately one hour at 350 degrees.  (Bacon should be crisp.)
Carve in slices to serve. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   02-25-95
