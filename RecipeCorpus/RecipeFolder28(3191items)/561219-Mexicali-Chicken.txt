Title: 561219 Mexicali Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Tex/mexican, Low-cal/fat, Main dish
Yield: 5 servings
1    Bottle chunky salsa
1/2 c  Water
1/2 t  Oregano
1/4 c  Instant minced onion
-Pam cooking spray
5    Skinned, boneless chicken
-breasts
1 t  Salt
1/2 t  Garlic powder
5 T  Sharp cheddar cheese,
-grated
Brown chicken breasts quickly, in 2 sprays of Pam.  Set aside. Mix
together remaining ingredients except cheese.  Pour over chicken
placed in single layer in baking dish.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Cover
and refrigerate overnight.  Bake at 400 degrees covered for 20
minutes. Remove cover and bake for 20 minutes more. 206 calories
serving. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   02-26-95
