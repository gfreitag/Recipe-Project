Title: Crabmeat Supreme
Categories: Seafood, Beer
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Cold water
2 c  Flaked crabmeat
1 c  Buttered bread crumbs
1 cn Tomato soup
1/2 c  Beer
1 c  Grated cheddar cheese
1 tb Cornstarch
Combine tomato soup, beer and cheese. Stir over low heat until cheese
melts. Blend cornstarch with cold water; stir constantly until
thickened. Add crabmeat; mix well. Pour into casserole or individual
casseroles. Top with crumbs and additional grated cheese if desired.
Brown under broiler. From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.
Feb 1994
