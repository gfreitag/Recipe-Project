Title: Sauerkraut Rolls
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Sauerkraut
1/2 c  Meat stock
4    Pieces of steak(Elk or deer)
2    Slices bacon
1/2    Chopped onion
2 ts Sugar
Pound meat quite thin and cut into pieces about 3 by 4 inches.  Dice
bacon and fry; add onion and cook 5 minutes.  Add sugar, pepper and
sauerkraut. Heat thoroughly.  Place a portion of saurerkraut mixture
in center of each piece of meat.  Roll and tie securely with tread or
fine string.  Place rolls in a greased casserole and add meat stock.
Bake in a moderate oven (350 degrees F.) about 1 hour or until meat
is tender.
