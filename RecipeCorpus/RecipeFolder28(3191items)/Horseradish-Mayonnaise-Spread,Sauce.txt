Title: Horseradish-Mayonnaise Spread/Sauce
Categories: Condiments, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Onion powder
1 c  Mayonnaise
3 ts Prepared horseradish
2 ts Dijon mustard
Recipe by: The Cook's Book of Uncommon Recipes - ISBN 0-940367-14-9

Mix all ingredients together and store in a covered container in your
refrigerator. Use as a sauce with roast beef or cold cuts. Also as a
spread on bread used to make meat or cheese sandwiches in place of
butter or plain mayonnaise.
