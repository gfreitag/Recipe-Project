Title: Happy Home Recipe
Categories: Information
Yield: 1 servings
3    -spoons of hope
2    -spoons of tenderness
4 qt Faith
1    -barrel of laughter
4 c  Love
2 c  Loyalty
4 c  Forgiveness
1 c  Friendship
Take love and loyalty; mix thoroughly with faith.  Blend with
tenderness, kindness, and understanding.  Add friendship and hope.
Sprinkle abundantly with laughter.  Bake with sunshine.  Serve
generous helpings daily.

Marie Harper  Tangipahoa Parish (Hammond)
