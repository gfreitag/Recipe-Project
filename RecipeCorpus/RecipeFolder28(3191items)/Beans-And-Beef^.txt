Title: Beans And Beef^
Categories: Healthy, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Canned no salt added
-tomatoes
1 c  Cooked kidney beans
1/2 ts Chili powder or to taste
1 lb Ground beef
1 lg Onion, diced
2 cl Garlic, minced
1/2    Green bell pepper, diced
1/2    Red bell pepper, diced
Preheat oven to 350F. Spray a 1 1/2-qt. baking dish with Pam. Brown
meat in a medium size skillet; drain off fat. Ad onion, garlic, bell
peppers and tomatoes; cook 10 minutes. Add beans and chili powder.
Spoon mixture into baking dish. Cover and bake 1 1/2 hours. Remove
cover. Bake 20 minutes more. Serve hot. Makes 6 servings.

Per serving without rice: Calories: 194 Protein: 23g Carbohydrates:
12g Fat: 5g Sodium: 123mg Cholesterol: 47mg
