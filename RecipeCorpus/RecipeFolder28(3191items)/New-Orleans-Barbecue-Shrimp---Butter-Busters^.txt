Title: New Orleans Barbecue Shrimp - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 10 servings
1 1/2 t  Lite salt, optional
1 1/2 t  Garlic powder
1 1/2 t  Onion powder
3/4 t  Oregano
3/4 t  Basil
1/2 t  Thyme
1/2 t  Black pepper
1/2 t  White pepper
1/2 t  Cayenne pepper
1 1/2 t  Paprika
2 c  Liquid Butter Buds
6 t  Ts seasoning blend (below)
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
10 oz Lite or nonalcholic beer
1 t  Minced garlic
2 t  Lemon juice
3 lb (or 4) shrimp
1 ea Loaf French or sourdough
-bread
SEASONING BLEND
Combine all seasonings together. Pour Butter Buds in a nonstick
skillet and saute garlic, shrimp and seasoning 2-3 minutes. Add beer,
Worcestershire and lemon juice. Cook 3-6 minutes until shrimp are
cooked and liquid thickens. Serve with plenty of French bread to sop
up sauce. Reserve remaining seasoning for a later use. Per serving:
314 cal., 3.4g fat (10%), 209mg chol., 1g fiber, 32g pro., 32g carb.,
876mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered
