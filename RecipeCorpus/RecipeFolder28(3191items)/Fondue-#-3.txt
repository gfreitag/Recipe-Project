Title: Fondue # 3
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
Bread, cubed
1 1/2 c  Milk
Pepper to taste
2 ts Garlic, finely chopped
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1 lb Gouda cheese
1/4 c  White wine, dry
2 tb Butter
2 ts Kirsch
1/4 c  Flour
1. Grate the cheese.
2. Melt the butter in a saucepan and add the flour, stirring with a
wire whisk. Add the milk, stirring rapidly with whisk. When blended
and smooth, add the garlic, pepper, nutmeg and wine. Add the kirsch,
if desired. 3. Serve hot with bread cubes for dipping. If the sauce
is to be used over vegetables, omit the kirsch.
