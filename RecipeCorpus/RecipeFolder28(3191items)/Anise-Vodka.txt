Title: Anise Vodka
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 batch
2 oz Sugar
1    Bottle vodka
2 oz Star anise
Put the anise into the vodka, breaking up the pieces if necessary.
Add the sugar.  Replace the top and give the bottle a good shake
every day until the sugar is dissolved. Keep for a month or more
before using.

from Edible Gifts by Claire Clifton and Martina Nicolls typed by
