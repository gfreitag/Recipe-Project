Title: Mango Remoulade
Categories: Seafood, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2    Egg yolks, room
-temperature
1/2 bn Cilantro
1    Very ripe mango, peeled and
-pureed
2    Limes, seeded
1 1/4 c  Olive oil
Blend all ingredients in a food processor until smooth. From the
files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
