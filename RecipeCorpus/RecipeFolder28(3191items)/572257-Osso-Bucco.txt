Title: 572257 Osso Bucco
Categories: Pasta, Veal
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 t  Basil
1 t  Sugar
2 T  Grated Romano cheese
6    Veal shanks
3 T  Olive oil
1 c  White wine
1 c  Chicken broth
1 cn Whole tomatoes
1 cn Tomato sauce
1 cn Pizza sauce
1 md Onion, chopped
1 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Chopped carrots
1    Bay leaf
4 T  Butter
1 t  Grated lemon rind
1/4 c  Flour
1/4 t  Black pepper
1/4 t  Thyme
1/4 t  Oregano
1/4 t  Garlic powder
Saute onion, celery, carrots and bay leaf in butter until onion is
translucent, about 5 minutes.  Stir in lemon rind.  Remove from heat
and set aside.  Combine flour with salt and pepper.  Roll meat in
flour and shake off excess.  Brown meat on both sides in olive oil in
Dutch oven, with tight fitting lid, over medium heat.  Add tomatoes,
sauces, wine, broth and seasonings.  Add sugar and cheese. Add shanks
and cook covered on low heat for about 1 hour.  Serve over angel hair
or thin spaghetti. Serves 4.
