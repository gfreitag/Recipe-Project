Title: Cheese Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Crushed cornflakes
8 oz Package of wide noodles
1 1/4 c  Milk
3    Eggs
1 1/3 c  Grated Cheddar cheese
Mix cheese, salt, pepper and cooked noodles in a greased casserole.
Beat eggs; add to milk and pour over noodles.  Sprinkle cereal over
the top. Bake at 325 degrees for 40 minutes, until crusty. Randy Rigg
