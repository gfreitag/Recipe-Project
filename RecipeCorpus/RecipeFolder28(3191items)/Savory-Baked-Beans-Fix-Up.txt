Title: Savory Baked Beans Fix-Up
Categories: Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Catsup
2    Bacon strips,cut/1" pieces
1 cn Pork and beans(16-18oz)
2 T  Brown sugar
1/4 t  Mustard,dry
1. Combine all ingredients.~ 2. Bake in a greased casserole in
preheated 350'F. oven 20 minutes.~ 3. Uncover; bake 20 minutes
longer.~ Recipe courtesy of:  Michael Orchekowski, 01 Feb 93  04:21:46
