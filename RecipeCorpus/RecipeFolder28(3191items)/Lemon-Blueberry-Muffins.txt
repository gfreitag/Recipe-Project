Title: Lemon Blueberry Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Breads
Yield: 12 muffins
1 c  Milk
1/3 c  Margarine/shortening, melted
1 1/2 t  Grated lemon peel
1 1/2 c  Post Blueberry Morning
1 1/3 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1 T  Baking powder
1/4 t  Salt
1    Egg
Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in large bowl. Beat egg in
small bowl; stir in milk, margarine and lemon peel. Add to flour
moisture; stir just until moistened. Batter will be lumpy. Stir in
cereal. Spoon batter into greased or paper lined muffin tins. Fill
each 2/3 cup full. Bake 20 minutes.  Serve warm.
