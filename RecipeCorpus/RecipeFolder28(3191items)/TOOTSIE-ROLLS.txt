Title: TOOTSIE ROLLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings  1 T margarine 2 1-oz squares unsweetened chocolate, melted 6 T
powered milk 1/4 cup white corn syrup 1/2 tsp vanilla 2 cups powered
sugar

Blend margarine and syrup together until smooth.  Add melted
chocolate to moxture; blend.  Add vanilla, powered sugar and dry
milk. Knead until the mixture is workable; shpae to look like
Tootsies. Chill. Wrap individually in plastic wrap.
