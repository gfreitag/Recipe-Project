Title: 293638 Lazy Pierogi Casserole
Categories: East europe, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Onion, chopped
1/4 t  Paprika
1/4 t  Onion salt (2)
12    Lasagna noodles
2 c  Cottage cheese
1    Egg
1/4 t  Onion salt
1 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
2 c  Potatoes, mashed
Cook and drain noodles.  Line bottom of 9 x 13-inch pan (4 noodles).
Mix together: cottage cheese, egg and onion salt.  Spoon over
noodles. In same bowl, mix: cheese, potatoes, salt and onion salt.
Spread over noodles. Cover with layer of noodles.  Melt butter; saute
onions. Add paprika. Pour over the top.  Bake 350 degrees for 30
minutes. Let stand 10 minutes. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
02-27-95
