Title: Marmalade Muffins
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/4 c  All-fruit marmalade spread
-(12.5 calories per tsp.)
2 1/4 c  Buttermilk baking mix
1 c  ;water
2 tb Granulated sugar
1/4 c  Egg substitute
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.  Spray twelve 2-3/4-inch muffin cups
with nonstick cooking spray.

In large bowl, combine baking mix and sugar.  Add 1 cup water, the egg
substitute, and vanilla; mix until just blended.  Spoon 1/4 cup
batter into each muffin cup; top each with 1 teaspoon orange
marmalade. Spoon remaining batter evenly over marmalade.

Bake muffins 12-15 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center
comes out clean.  Remove; place on rack to cool.

Each serving provides: 1 B, 50 optional calories.  Per serving: 112
cal, 2 g pro, 3 g fat, 20 g car, 271 mg sod, 0 mg chol.
