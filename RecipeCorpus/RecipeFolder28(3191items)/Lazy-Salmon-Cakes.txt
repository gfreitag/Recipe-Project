Title: Lazy Salmon Cakes
Categories: Fish
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 ts Dry cilantro /fresh
-parsley
1/4 md Onion, chopped
7 1/2 oz Can salmon, no skin, bones
1    Egg
1/2 c  Mashed potato, 1 medium
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Lemon or orange peel dry
1/2 ts Dry dill or 1/4 c fresh
-dill
Mash potato and allow it to cool.  Mix with lemon juice, seasonings,
onion and then mix in the salmon.  Mix in beaten egg.

Form the mixture into 4 patties or 6 smaller ones.  Heat a large
saute pan over medium heat.  Spray or wipe with canola.  Fry the
cakes for 5 minutes on each side until golden brown.  Serve with
lemon wedge.

Makes 2 servings for hungry eaters, 3 smaller ones.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
