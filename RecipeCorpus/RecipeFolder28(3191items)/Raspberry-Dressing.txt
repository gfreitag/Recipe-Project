Title: Raspberry Dressing
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
-vinegar
2 ts Sugar
1 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
1/2 c  Raspberries*
1 tb Raspberry or red wine
Place all ingredients in blender. Cover and blend on high speed
about 15 seconds or until smooth.

*Frozen unsweetened loose-pack raspberries, thawed and drained,  can
be substituted for the fresh raspberries.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
