Title: 279706 Tuna Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Cheddar cheese
1 cn LeSueur very young sm.
-early peas
-Bread crumbs
2 1/2 c  Elbow macaroni
2 cn Tuna
3/4 c  Milk
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
Cook macaroni in water until tender.  Drain in colander.  Grate
cheese. Combine macaroni, tuna (drained), soup, milk, cheese and peas
(drained) into greased casserole dish; mix well.  Sprinkle bread
crumbs on top. Bake in 375 degree oven for approximately 20 minutes.
