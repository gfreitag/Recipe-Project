Title: Strawberry Blintz - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Desserts
Yield: 10 ----------
1 t  Sugar
AND
1 ea Pk Seweet 'n Low
10 ea Prepared crepes
2 ea Pk (oz) fatfree cream cheese
1/4 c  Egg Beaters
1 ea Tb sugar
OR
MMMMM----------------------STRAWBERRY SAUCE---------------------------
OR
1 ea Tb sugar
AND
1 1/2 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
1 ea Pk (16oz) frozen
-strawberries, thawed and
-strained
2 ea Tb sugar
Combine cream cheese, Egg Beater and sugar; spread about 2
tablespoons down the center of each crepe. Fold up like an envelope.
In a nonstick skillet that has been sprayed with Pam, cook over
medium heat, seam side down about 4 minutes or until golden brown.
Turn and cook another 4 minutes. Serve with strawberry sauce Sauce:
Puree berries. Stir in sugar and serve over blintzes. Per sugar
serving: 101 cal., 0.2g fat (2%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 7g pro., 17g
carb., 184mg sod. Per S&L serving: 94cal., 15g carb. Butter Busters
by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 3-95.
