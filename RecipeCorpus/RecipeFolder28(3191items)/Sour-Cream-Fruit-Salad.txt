Title: Sour Cream Fruit Salad
Categories: Fruit, Nuts/grains, Desserts, Mom's
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------KEYWORDS: PINEAPPLE, SALADS,---------------------
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: MOM'S RECIPE CLIPPIN---------------------
1 c  Shredded coconut
1 c  Small marshmallows
1 c  Commercial sour cream
This amount serves four
-generously.
1 c  Drained mandarin oranges
1 c  Drained crushed pineapple
Combine all ingredients. Spoon into ring mold or loaf pan. Let stand
at least 12 hours. Serve on crisp greens. Garnish with maraschino
cherries.
