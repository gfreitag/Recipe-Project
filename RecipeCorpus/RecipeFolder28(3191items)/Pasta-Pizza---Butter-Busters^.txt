Title: Pasta Pizza - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 12 ----------
-chopped, optional
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 cn (8oz) sliced mushrooms,
-rinsed and drained
1 t  Italian seasoning
2 c  Grated nonfat mozzarella
-cheese
1/2 lb Skinless white turkey meat,
-ground, cooked and drained
1 ea Pk (7oz) elbows, cooked and
-drained
1/2 c  Skim milk
1/4 c  Egg beaters, beaten
1/4 t  Lite salt, optional
1 1/2 c  Hunt's light spaghetti sauce
1 cn (10oz) Rotel tomatoes and
-green chiles, cut up and
-drained
1 sm Green pepper, finely
Blend skim milk, Egg Beater and salt. Add macaroni and mix well.
Spread evenly in a 15 x 10" baking sheet that has been sprayed with
Pam. Combine Rotel tomatoes with spaghetti sauce and turkey; spread
on macaroni. Top with bell pepper, onion, mushroom and Italian
seasoning. Top with cheese. Bake at 350F. 30 minutes or until the
macaroni mixture is set. Let stand 5 minutes before cutting. Per
serving: 124 cal., 0.8g fat (6%), 13mg chol., 2g fiber, 11g pro.,
