Title: Calamari and Shrimp with Linguine - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 12 servings
-fatfree chicken broth
1/4 c  (to 1/2) dry white wine or
-cooking wine
2 ea Tb Hunts tomato paste
1 t  Oregano
1 t  Basil
1/2 t  Black pepper
1/2 t  (to 1) crushed red pepper
l  Salt to taste, optional
1 ea Pk (16oz) linguine, cooked
1 lb Squid, cleaned and cut in
-1/4" rings (calamari)
2 t  Minced garlic
1 lb Cooked and pelled shrimp
12 oz Canned chopped clams,
-rinsed and drained
1 cn (28oz) Italian tomatoes,
-pureed in a blender
2 ea Tb liquid Butter Buds or
In a large pot, combine tomatoes, 1 tablespoon Butter Buds, clams,
shrimp, wine, tomato paste, basil, oregano, slat, black pepper and
red pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce het and simmer 30 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a nonstick skillet, saute garlic and calamari in Butter
Buds about 5 minutes or until done. Add to the tomato mixture. Simmer
15 minutes. Serve over linguine. Per serving: 189 cal., 1.9g fat
(9%), 181mg chol., 1 g fiber, 24g pro., 18g carb., 162mg sod. Butter
