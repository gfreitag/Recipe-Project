Title: Brown Sugar Frosting
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 ts Vanilla
2    Egg whites
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
ds Salt
1/4 c  Cold water
Boil Sugar and water until it threads from the end of a fork.  Cool
and pour onto the stiffly beaten egg whites, add salt and vanilla and
beat until cold.
