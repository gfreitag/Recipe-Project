Title: Rosemary Garlic Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Italian, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 14 servings
1 1/2 ts Garlic ; minced in oil or 1-
; cloves crushed
1 tb Dried parsley
1 ts Dried rosemary
1 1/2 ts Dried oregano
1 1/4 c  Warm water ; very
1 pk Active dry yeast
1 c  Whole-wheat flour
2 c  Flour ; unbleached
1 ts Salt
1 ts Ground pepper ; freshly
2 tb Oil
(adapted from Bernard Clayton's _Complete Book of Bread_, p. 464)
