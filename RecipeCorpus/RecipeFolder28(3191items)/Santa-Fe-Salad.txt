Title: Santa Fe Salad
Categories: Salads, Main dish, Side dish, Vegetarian, Rice
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Vinegar
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Snipped fresh cilantro
1    Jalapeno pepper; minced
1 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Cooked brown rice; cooled
1 c  Canned black beans
-OR- canned pinto beans
-- rinsed and drained
8 3/4 oz Canned whole kernel corn
-- drained
2 tb Minced onion
Combine rice, beans, corn and onion in medium bowl.  Whisk remaining
ingredients in small bowl; pour over rice mixture.  Chill 2 to 3
hours so flavors will blend.  Stir before serving.

Each serving provides: * 316 calories * 11.1 g. protein * 8.0 g. fat
* 53.4 g. carbohydrate * 697 mg. sodium * 0 mg. cholesterol
