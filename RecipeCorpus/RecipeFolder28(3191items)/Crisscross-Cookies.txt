Title: Crisscross Cookies
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 5 dozen
1    Egg
1 1/2 c  Flour, all-purpose
3/4 ts Soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Peanut butter; smooth or
-crunchy
1/2 c  Butter (or marg.)
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Sugar, brown; firmly packed
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Cream peanut butter and butter.  Add sugar gradually; cream until
light and fluffy.  Add vanilla and egg, beating well.  Combine flour,
soda, baking powder, and salt; blend into creamed mixture.  Chill.

Shape dough into 1-inch balls and place about 2 inches apart on
ungreased cookie sheets.  Flatten with a fork in crisscross pattern.
Bake at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.
