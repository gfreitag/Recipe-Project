Title: Traditional Hopping John
Categories: Holiday, Rice, Pork
Yield: 10 servings
2 pk (10 oz) frozen blackeyed
-peas
1/2 c  Uncooked white rice
2 c  Water
Salt & pepper, to taste
Louisiana Hot Sauce
4    Bacon strips
1/4 c  Onion, diced
1/2    Bell pepper, diced
1/2    Red bell pepper, diced
2 c  FRESH Blackeyed peas
-or purple hull peas
-OR-
Dice bacon. brown in dutch oven with onion and pepeprs, until bacon is
crisp and vegetables are soft.  Add peas and rice. then water.  Cover
and simmer over very low heat about 20 minutes, until the rice is
tender. Salt & pepper to taste.  Add a dash of hot sauce (to taste).

This recipe came to America from Africa though the slave trade.  It
is now the traditional dish served by most Southerners on New Year's
Day. It is reported to bring good luck.
