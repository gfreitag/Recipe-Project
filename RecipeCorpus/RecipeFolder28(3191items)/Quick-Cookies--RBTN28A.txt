Title: Quick Cookies  RBTN28A
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 servings
5 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
Cream Butter, (with beater) gradually add sugar & when it is light add
flour & salt. Beat until dough is a good consistency. Divide dough in
two pieces. Roll each piece until thin , cut for cookies & place on a
cookie Sheet. Bake 300F 25 min.
