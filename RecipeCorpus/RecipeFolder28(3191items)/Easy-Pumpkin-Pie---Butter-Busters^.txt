Title: Easy Pumpkin Pie - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Desserts
Yield: 16 ----------
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/4 t  Ground cloves
1 cn (12oz) evaporated skim milk
1 ea Pk (1.3oz) prepared Dream
-Whip
Pastry Pie crust, separate
-recipe
OR
Basic Graham Cracker Crust,
-separate recipe
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
1 cn (16oz) solid pack pumpkin
3/4 c  Sugar
OR
1/2 c  Sugar
AND
3 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
1 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Ground ginger
Preheat oven to 425F. Mix filling in order given. Pour filling into
crust and bake 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 350F. and bake 40-45
minutes more or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
Garnish with Dream Whip. If using pastry crust, use as you would a
regular pie crust. There is enough filling for 2 pies. Per sugar
serving: (graham cracker crust) 135 cal., 0.1g fat (0%), 0mg chol.,
1g fiber, 4g pro., 29g carb., 103mg sod. Per S&L serving: (graham
cracker crust) 118 cal., 24g carb. Pastry crust sugar serving: 126
cal., 0.4g ft (3%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 4g pro., 27g carb., 85mg
sod. Pastry crust S&L serving: 115 cal., 24g carb. Butter Busters by
Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 3-95.
