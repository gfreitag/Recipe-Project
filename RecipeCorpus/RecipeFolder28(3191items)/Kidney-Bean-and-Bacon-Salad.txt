Title: Kidney Bean and Bacon Salad
Categories: Vegetables, Mom's, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: MOM'S RECIPE CLIPPIN---------------------
1/3 c  French Dressing made with
Basil Wine Vinegar
3 c  Canned kidney beans
4 sl Of bacon, diced
1/2 ea Chopped cucumber
Drain the beans, crisp the bacon and chop the cucumber. Combine and
mix with dressing. Arrange on crisp romaine and serve with additional
French Dressing.
