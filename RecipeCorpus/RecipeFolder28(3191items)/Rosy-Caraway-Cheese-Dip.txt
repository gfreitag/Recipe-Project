Title: Rosy Caraway Cheese Dip
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Dips/spread, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 batch
1 lg Shallot, minced
4 ts Caraway seeds
4 ts Paprika
4 ts Capers, drained
8 oz Low-fat cream cheese
;at room temperature
1 tb Butter, at room temperature
1 tb Dijon mustard
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
2 tb Chives, chopped
In a food processor or a bowl, combine all ingredients except capers.
Process or mix until very well blended.  Add capers and blend in for
a few seconds.  Chill.  Best if made several days ahead.

Serve in a bowl, sprinkled with chives.  Pumpernickel, black bread or
other dark breads go well with this.
