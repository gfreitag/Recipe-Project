Title: Ham and Lima Bean Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
28 oz Chicken broth
2 c  Ilk
10 oz Frozen Fordhook lima beans
8 oz Cooked smoked ham; cut up pi
2 tb Butter
12 oz Red-skinned potatoes*
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Mixture of green and red pep
* Scrubbed and cut into 1/4 inch pieces. Melt butter in 5 qt pot over
medium-high heat.  Add potatoes, onion and peppers. Cook about 8
minutes until onion and peppers are tender. Stir in remaining
ingredients, except ham; bring to boil.  Reduce heat to medium. Add
ham and simmer 10 to 15 minutes until hot and lima beans are tender.
**SuSan/San Diego**
