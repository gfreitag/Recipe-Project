Title: Tuna And Cabbage Quick Dish
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Soy sauce
1 t  Sugar
-Salt
1 T  Minced parsley
1 cn Tuna
3    Leaves cabbage
2/3 c  Chicken broth
1 T  Sake
Open can of tuna, drain water and break into pieces.  Cut cabbage in
bite size.  Boil soup, sake, soy sauce, sugar and salt.  Add tuna and
cabbage. Cook 4 minutes.  Put everything in a dish, sprinkle parsley
on the top. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-03-95
