Title: Low-fat tortilla chips
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizers
Yield: 48 chips
Garlic powder, opt.
Chili powder, opt.
Vegetable cooking spray
8    6-in flour or corn tortillas
Salt, opt.
spray baking sheets with spray.  cut each tortilla into six wedges;
arrange wedges in single layer on baking sheet.  spray lightly with
cooking spray, then sprinkle to taste with salt, garlic powder or
chili powder.

bake 10 to 12 min in preheated 350degF oven.  or until crisp.

per serving (8 chips): 91 cal., 2 g pro., 15 g car, 2 g fat, 25% cal
from fat, 0 mg chol, 127 mg sod.
