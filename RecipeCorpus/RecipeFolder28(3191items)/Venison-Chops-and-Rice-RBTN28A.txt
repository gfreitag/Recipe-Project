Title: Venison Chops and Rice RBTN28A
Categories: Alcohol, Rice, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
6 ea Slices tomato
1 cn Chicken broth
6 ea Chops
6 tb Uncooked rice
6 ea Slices onion
Brown chops on both sides.  Grease casserole dish well. Put 1 tb
rice for each chop in bottom of dish.
Place chops on rice.  Put slice of onion, tomato & pepper on each
chop. Cover with chicken broth.  Bake 1 hr. at 350 degrees.
