Title: Sour Soup with Rice Noodles
Categories: Chinese, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1    Green onion
1/2 c  Rice stick noodles, soaked
1 ts Thin soy sauce
1 pn Sugar
2 ts White vinegar
1/2 ts Sesame oil
5 c  Chicken stock, strained
And fairly clear
1/8 lb Boneless pork butt
4    Black mushrooms
1/3 c  Dried bamboo shoot tips,
Soaked and shredded
Preparation:  Wash and soak mushrooms in warm water for 30 minutes;
shred. Wash and soak dried bamboo shoots in warm water for 1 hour;
shred in 1" pieces. Slice pork butt into 1" matchsticks. Wash and
trim green onion, shred on the bias into 1" pieces.  Soak rice stick
noodle in warm water until soft.

Cooking Soup:  In sauce pan, heat broth.  When it reaches gentle
boil, add pork, mushrooms, bamboo shoots, soy sauce and sugar; simmer
10 minutes. Add soaked rice stick noodles; simmer for another 10
minutes. A minute before removing from heat, add vinegar and sesame
oil; stir. Remove to serving bowl; garnish with shredded green
onions. Serve.
