Title: Creamy Chocolate Frozen Yogurt - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Desserts
Yield: 8 ----------
1 cn (12oz) evaporateed skim milk
1/4 c  Egg Beaters, beaten
2/3 c  Hershey's Chocolate syrupp
1 t  Vanilla
1 c  Nonfat vanila yogurt,
-stirred
2 t  Cornstarch
1/2 c  Sugar
OR
1/4 c  Sugar
AND
3 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
In a medium saucepan, combine cornstarch and sugar. Stir in milk and
Egg Beaters. Cook and stir over low heat 6-8 minutes or until mixture
coats a metal spoon. Remove from heat. Add chocolate syrup and cool.
Stir in vanilla and yogurt. Freeze in an ice cream freezer acccording
to manufactureers directions. Per sugar serving: 160 cal., 0.4g fat
(2%), 2mg chol., 0g fiber, 6g pro., 35g carb., 106mg sod. Per S&L
serving: 137 cal., 29g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN
