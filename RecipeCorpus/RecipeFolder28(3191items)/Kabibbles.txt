Title: Kabibbles
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 serving<g>
2 ts Celery Salt
1 ts Sweet Paprika
1 pk Garlic and Onion Croutons
1/2 c  Melted Butter
2 tb Sesame Seeds
Pour croutons into large bowl.  Combine remaining ingredients and
toss with croutons.  Place onto cookie sheet and toast in 275F oven
for 20 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes.  Allow to cool.
