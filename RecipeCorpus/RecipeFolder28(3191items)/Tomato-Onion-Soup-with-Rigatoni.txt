Title: Tomato-Onion Soup with Rigatoni
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Rigatoni
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1    Bay leaf
1/2 ts Dried basil
1 tb Soy sauce
1 lg Onion; chopped
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 c  Water
28 oz Canned tomatoes; chopped, w/
1 tb Dried parsley
Recipe by: The New McDougall Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:10
Saute the onion and garlic powder in the water for 15 minutes. Add
the remaining ingredients, except the soy sauce. Simmer, covered, for
1 hour. Add the soy sauce and remove the bay leaf just before serving.

150 calories, 1.0 gram fat per serving. From the collection of Sue
Smith, S.Smith34, Uploaded June 16, 1994
