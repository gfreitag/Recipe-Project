Title: Chunky Granola Bars
Categories: Snacks, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Honey
2 c  Granola
1    Egg
1/2 c  Peanut Butter
Combine egg, peanut butter and honey in a small saucepan. Cook over
medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture starts to bubble.
Remove from heat and add granola.  Mix well.  Spread evenly in a
greased 8" square pan. Chill until firm.  Cut into bars.  Store in
refrigerator.

SOURCE:  S.H.A.R.E Colorado Jan./Feb 1995 submitted by Kristi
Zimmerman - Englewood, Colo.
