Title: Avocado-at-Sea
Categories: Question?
Yield: 1 serving
MMMMM----------------DRINK TO YOUR HEALTH; WOLFOR---------------------
1/2 c  Light cream
1/2    Avocado; mashed
10 3/4 oz Chicken broth, canned
1 c  Clam chowder, condensed
Good source of protein, calcium, vitamin A

Blend all ingredients in blender until smooth.  Heat in saucepan.
