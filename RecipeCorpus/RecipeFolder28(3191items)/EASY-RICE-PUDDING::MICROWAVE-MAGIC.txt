Title: EASY RICE PUDDING::MICROWAVE MAGIC
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk (4 SERV)VAN.PUDDING & PIEMIX    2 1/2 c  MILK
1/2 c  QUICK-COOKING RICE
1/2 c  RAISINS
In 4-cup glass measure or casserole combine all ingredients. Cook
uncovered 6 minutes or until mixture boils, stirring occasionally
during last half of cooking time. Serve warm or cool. 4 to 6 servings.
