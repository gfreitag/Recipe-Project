Title: Vegetable Paella
Categories: Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
8    Sprigs fresh mint; minced
3 tb Parsley; fresh minced
1/2 ts Turmeric
3 tb Tomato sauce
1 c  Rice
1 c  (to 2 c) Water or Chicken
-stock
1 cn Peas
1 lg Leek; quartered, sliced thin
1/2 lg Green pepper; chopped
2    Carrots; chopped
2    Celery; chopped
2    Green onion; chopped
1 lg White onion; chopped
8    Sprigs fresh thyme; leaves
-only
In a large pot or wok, saute leek, green pepper, carrots, celery,
green onion and white onion... about 2-3 minutes. Add fresh thyme,
mint and parsley. Add turmeric and tomato sauce. Add 1 cup rice and
saute another minute or two. Add 1 to 1 1/2 c water or chicken stock,
cover and simmer 15 minutes. Add peas, cover and simmer another 5
minutes or until all moisture is absorbed by rice. Mmmmmm, delicious,
simple and quick.

(Happy Eating from Michele & Peter, 1993)
