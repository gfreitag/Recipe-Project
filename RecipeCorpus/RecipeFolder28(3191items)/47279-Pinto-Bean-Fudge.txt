Title: 47279 Pinto Bean Fudge
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
6 oz Unsweetened chocolate
1 T  Vanilla
2 lb Powdered sugar
1 c  Pinto beans, drained &
-mashed
1/4    To 1/2 c. milk
6 T  Butter
Melt chocolate and butter.  Add mashed pinto beans mixed with milk.
Add 1 tablespoon vanilla.  Stir until slightly thickened. Gradually
work in the powdered sugar.  Spread in buttered pan. Refrigerate. You
can add raisins, nuts, etc.
