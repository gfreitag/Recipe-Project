Title: Nutty Chocolate-Covered Potato Chips
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 36 servings
2 c  Crushed potato chips
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Coconut; flaked or shredded
12 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
2 tb Cooking oil or solid vegetab
-- shortening
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:12 1. In a 2-qt glass bowl, combine chocolate
chips and oil. Heat in microwave on High about 1 1/2 mins, until
melted and smooth when stirred.

2. Stir in potato chips, nuts, and coconut until well coated. Drop by
teaspoonfuls onto 2 wax paper-lined baking sheets. Refrigerate until
set, about 1 hour. Store, covered, in refrigerator.
