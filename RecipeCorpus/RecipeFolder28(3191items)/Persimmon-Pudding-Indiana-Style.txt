Title: Persimmon Pudding Indiana Style
Categories: Puddings
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Persimmon pulp
1 c  Buttermilk
2    Eggs
1 c  Sugar, white
1 c  Sugar, dark brown, packed
2 1/4 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
1 ts Soda
1 ts Cinnamon
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a large bowl, measure all
ingredients. With mixer at low speed, beat ingredients just until
batter is smooth. Pour batter into two 9 x 9 inch pans; level batter
with spatula. Bake for 50 minutes or until toothpick inserted in the
center comes out clean. Serve with whipped cream. Randy Rigg
