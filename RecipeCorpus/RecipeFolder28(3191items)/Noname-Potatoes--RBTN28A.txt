Title: Noname Potatoes  RBTN28A
Categories: Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Cream of chicken soup
1 cn Evaporated milk
2 lb Frozen hash browns
1 pk Dry onion soup mix
1/2 c  Stick oleo
Melt stick of oleo in 9x13" pan. Put potatoes in. Mix soup, milk &
onion mix and pour over top. Bake 350F one hour.
