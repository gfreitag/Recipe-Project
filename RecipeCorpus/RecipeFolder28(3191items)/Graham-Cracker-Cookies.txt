Title: Graham Cracker Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
3/4 c  Chopped pecans
1 ts Vanilla
20    Graham crackers, separate
1/2 c  Sugar
2    Sticks of butter
Separate the graham crackers and put on a cookie sheet.  Put sugar and
butter in a pan, heat and boil for 2 minutes; then add the pecans and
vanilla.  Pour over crackers.  Bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees.
Remove from pan immediately. Randy Rigg
