Title: Raspberry Brandy
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 batch
1 lb Raspberries
3 oz Sugar
1    Bottle Spanish brandy (or
-vodka)
Put the raspberries and spirit into a wide-necked jar.  Stir and
bruise the fruit well.  Cover tightly and leave to macerate for 2
weeks. Then add the sugar, shake to dissolve and leave for another 2
weeks. Filter through double muslin into clean bottles.
