Title: Paella
Categories: Seafood, Pasta
Yield: 8 servings
1 lb Shrimp
1 qt Mussels
1 ts Oregano
1    Clove garlic, peeled
6 tb Olive oil
2 oz Ham, cut in thin strips
1 oz Salt pork, finely chopped
1    Green pepper, seeded and
-chopped
1 ts Capers
2 1/4 c  Rice, washed and drained
1 ts Saffron (or tumeric)
1 cn Pimentos
1    1 1/2 lb cooked lobster
12 sm Clams
1    1 1/2 lb chicken
2    Peppercorns
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Vinegar
1    Chorizo (hot Spanish
-sausage), sliced
1    Onion, peeled and chopped
1/2 ts Ground coriander
3 tb Tomato sauce
4 c  Boiling water
1 cn Peas, drained
Remove meat from the lobster. Shell and devein shrimp. Scrub mussels
and clams. Cut chicken into medium sized serving pieces.

Combine oregano, peppercorns, garlic, salt, two tbs of the olive oil
and the vinegar and mash with back of spoon or with a mortar. Rub
chicken with the mixture.

Heat remaining olive oil in a deep, heavy skillet and brown chicken
lightly over moderate het. Add ham, chorizo, salt pork, onion, green
pepper, coriander and capers. Cook ten minutes over low heat.

Add tomato sauce and rice and cook 5 minutes. Add boiling water,
saffron and shrimp. Mix well and cook rapidly, covered, until liquid
is absorbed, about 20 minutes. With a large spoon, turn rice from top
to bottom. Add lobster meat and peas; cover and cook 5 minutes longer.

Steam mussels and clams in a little water until their shells open.
Heat the pimientos and drain.

Use the mussels, clams and pimientos as a garnish.

Yield: 6-8 servings
