Title: Arroz Con Leche
Categories: Desserts, Tex/mexican, Puddings, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
ds Salt
14 oz Can condensed milk
Powdered cinnamon
1 c  Rice
1 ea (2") cinnamon stick
3 c  Water
Place rice, water, and cinnamon stick in a pot and cook until water is
consumed and rice is soft.  Add salt, condensed milk, and about 1 c
water. Simmer about 10 mins.  Should be smooth, not watery. Sprinkle
with cinnamon when serving.
