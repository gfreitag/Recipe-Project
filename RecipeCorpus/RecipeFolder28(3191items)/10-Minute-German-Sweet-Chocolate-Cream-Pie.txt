Title: 10 Minute German Sweet Chocolate Cream Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
-thawed
1 ea Graham cracker crust
Chocolate curls, for
-Garnish
3 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 ea Pk 4 oz Sweet baking
-Chocolate
1/3 c  Milk, divided
2 T  Sugar
8 oz Frozen whipped topping,
Combine sweet baking chocolate and 2 tablespoons milk in top of a
boiler; bring water to a boil.  Reduce heat to low; cook until
chocolate melts, stirring occasionally.  Set aside. Beat cream cheese
at high speed of an electric mixer until creamy. Gradually add sugar,
beating well. Add reserved chocolate mixture and remaining 3
tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon milk, beating until smooth. Fold in
whipped topping; spoon into crust. Garnish, if desired. Cover and
chill at least 8 hours.
