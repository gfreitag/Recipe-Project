Title: Caramel Apple Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Desserts
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
1/2 c  Country Morning Blend
1/4 c  Whipping cream
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
In 1 quart saucepan combine all dip ingredients except whipping
cream. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until mixture
comes to a full boil (7 to 8 minutes). Boil 1 to 2 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Dip will thicken as it cools.  Serve warm dip with
cut-up fruit. Store leftovers in fridge in covered container. Source:
