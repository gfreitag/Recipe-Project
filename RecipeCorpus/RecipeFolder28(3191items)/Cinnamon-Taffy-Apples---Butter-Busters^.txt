Title: Cinnamon Taffy Apples - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Snacks
Yield: 6 ----------
2 t  Red food coloring
1/2 t  Cinnamon
6 md Red delicious apples
6 ea Wooden skewers
2 c  Sugar (Do not use Sweet 'n
-Low in this recipe)
2 c  Light corn syrup
1/3 c  Cinnamon candies
1 c  Cold water
In a medium saucepan combine sugar, corn syrup and cinnamon candies
with cold water. Stirring constantly, cook just until sugr and
candies are dissolved; do not boil. Add food coloring and cinnamon;
stir to mix well. Bring syrup to a boil; continue cooking, without
stirring, to 300F. on candy thermometer. Meanwhile, wash apples and
dry well. Remove stem and place a wooden skewer in far enough to hold
apple firmly. remove syrup from heat. Working quickly, dip each apple
into syrup to coat caompletely. Place, skewer end up, on a cookie
sheet that has been sprayed with Pam. Let taffy harden. Let stand at
room temperature until they are ready to serve. Per sugar serving:
495 cal., 0.6g fat (1%), 0mg chol., 4g fiber, 0g pro., 127g carb.,
70mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered
