Title: Hearty Tuna Macaroni Salad
Categories: Salads, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 t  Pepper
2 c  Finely shredded cabbage
1/4 c  Julienne green pepper
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 t  Salt
2 t  Vinegar
1 c  Elbow macaroni
1/2 c  Grated carrot
2 T  Minced onion
1 cn (7 oz.) tuna, drained and
-flaked
2 T  Prepared mustard
Cook macaroni in boiling water until tender, drain and rinse.  In a
large bowl combine macaroni and next five ingredients.  In a small
bowl combine mayonnaise with remaining ingredients.  Pour over
macaroni tuna mixture. Mix well and chill.  Serve on lettuce.
