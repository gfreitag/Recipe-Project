Title: Graham Cracker Bars  RBTN28A
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 servings
1 c  Chopped pecans etc.
1 c  Real butter
1/2 c  Sugar
Mix butter & sugar. Boil 2 min. Line cookie sheet with aluminum foil
(shiny side up) spread with Graham crackers. Pour syrup over crackers
& sprinkle with chopped nuts. Bake 350F 8 minutes. Cool & break into
bars.
