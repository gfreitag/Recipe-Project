Title: Mixed Fruits with Grass Jelly
Categories: Chinese, Desserts, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Grass jelly
1/2 cn Lychee fruit with juice
1/2 c  Canned mandarin oranges
Have all ingredients well chilled.  Open both ends of grass jelly
can; push out gelatin; slice, then cut into 1/2" cubes. Place grass
jelly cubes in serving bowl with lychee fruit and mandarin oranges.
Cover and keep refrigerated until ready to serve.
