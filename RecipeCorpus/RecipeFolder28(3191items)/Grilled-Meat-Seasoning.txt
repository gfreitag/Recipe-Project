Title: Grilled Meat Seasoning
Categories: Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 cup
1 t  Garlic powder
1 t  Onion powder
1 t  Salt
1/2 t  Ground red pepper
1/2 t  Ground black pepper
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 T  Ground cumin
2 T  Chili powder
1 t  Ground cinnamon
1 t  Ground nutmeg
In a small bowl, combine all ingredients until well blended. Store in
airtight container.  Give with serving instructions. To serve:
Generously sprinkle seasoning on raw meat about 2 hours before
cooking; return meat to refrigerator to allow flavors to be absorbed.
Grill or broil as desired. Especially good with pork and poultry.
