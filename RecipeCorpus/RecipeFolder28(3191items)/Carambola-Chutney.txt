Title: Carambola Chutney
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 cup
1 tb Ginger, peeled
-finely chopped
1/4 ts Ground cloves
2 tb White wine vinegar
2 c  Carambola (star fruit)
-cubed (3/4 lb)
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Dry red wine
Combine all ingredients in medium saucepan and stir well. Bring to a
boil over medium-high heat and cook for 25 minutes or until slightly
thickened.
