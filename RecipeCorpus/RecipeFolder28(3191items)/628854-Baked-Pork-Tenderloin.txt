Title: 628854 Baked Pork Tenderloin
Categories: Pork
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Meat stock or broth
2 T  Flour
3 t  Salt
1/2 t  Pepper
3 T  Light wine, optional
2    Whole pork tenderloins
1/4 c  Margarine
1 c  Chopped celery
1/2 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1 lg Onion, sliced
Preparation : Saute pork in margarine. Remove from pan. Add celery,
mushrooms and onion. Cook about 5 minutes. Mix meat stock and flour
until smooth.  Add to vegetables with seasonings, cook until gravy is
thickened. Add wine.  Place in casserole and bake covered, 325
degrees for 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 hours.  Serves 4-6.
