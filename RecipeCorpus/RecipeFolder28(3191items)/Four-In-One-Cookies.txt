Title: Four-In-One Cookies
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 5 1/2 dozen
1 1/4 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
3 c  Oats; uncooked (quick or
- old-fashioned)
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Sugar, brown; firmly packed
2    Eggs
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Combine shortening, sugar, eggs, and vanilla; beat until creamy.  Sift
together flour, soda and salt.  Add to creamed mixture; blend well.
Stir in oats.

Shape dough into 1" balls.  Place about 2 inches apart on greased
cookie sheets.  Bake at 350 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.  Cool about
2 minutes before removing from cookie sheets.

NOTE: For variety, add one of the following ingredients with the
oats: 1 cup raisins, 1 cup salted peanuts, 1 (6 oz.) package sem-swet
chocolate pieces, or a cup flaked coconut.
