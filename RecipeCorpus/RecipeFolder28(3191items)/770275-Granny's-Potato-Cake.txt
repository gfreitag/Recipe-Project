Title: 770275 Granny's Potato Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chocolate (Nestle's powder)
1 c  Milk
2 c  Flour
4 t  Baking powder
1 c  Chopped walnuts
4    Egg whites
2 c  Sugar
4    Egg yolks
1 c  Crisco (thick stuff)
2 t  Vanilla
1 c  Mashed potatoes (salted &
-buttered)
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
-powder
1 pk Or more powdered sugar
6 T  Water
3 T  Melted margarine or butter
2 T  Nestle's Quik chocolate
CAKE:  Cream together the sugar, egg yolks, Crisco and vanilla. Mix
mashed potatoes, milk and Nestle's chocolate together, then add to
first mixture. Add flour, baking powder and nuts.  Beat egg whites
until thick and fold into mixture.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bake
for 25 minutes. Let cool. Empty one pan onto cake plate, frost top.
Put next cake on top of first one, frost top. Put last cake on top
and frost top and sides. Grease well 3 cake pans and dust with flour.
