Title: Kartoffelpuree Meerrettich (Mashed Potatoes/horseradish C
Categories: Ethnic, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 x  Pepper; Freshly Ground
1/2 c  Sour Cream
1 T  Horseradish
2 t  Parsley; Minced
5 ea Potatoes; Medium
1 x  ;Boiling Water
1/2 t  Salt
2 T  Butter
Peel and quarter potatoes.  Cook in boiling salted water in
medium-size saucepan until tender, drain.  Mash, adding 1 T butter
and the pepper. Add sour cream, horseradish, and minced parsley. Whip
as for mashed potatoes. Place in serving bowl; top with 1 T melted
butter and serve. Submitted By LAWRENCE KELLIE   On   03-04-95
