Title: Poached Salmon In Orange Sauce^
Categories: Healthy, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Orange juice
1 ts Grated prange zest
1 ts Cornstarch
-MIXED WITH
2 tb Water
3/4 c  Fish stock
1/2 c  White wine
1 ts Dried leaf thyme
-Dash pepper
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 lb Salmon fillet
Preheat oven to 400F. Mix stock, wine, thyme, pepper and lemon juice
in a saucepan large enough to hold fish in a single layer. Add fish;
poach until fish is opague, about 8 minutes. Simmer 5 minutes. Remove
fish and place in a baking dish. Mix orange juice, zest and
cornstarch mixture in a small saucepan. Cook until thickened,
stirring. Pour over fish. Heat in oven 5 minutes. Cut into 4 serving
pieces.

Calories: 183 Protein: 24g Carbohydrates: 68g Fat: 8g, 39%!!! Sodium:
79mg Cholesterol: 40mg
