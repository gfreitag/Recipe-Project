Title: Olive Relish Molds

Yield: 8 servings

These pretty little molds make zippy accompaniment for seafood. 1 inch
stick cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon whole cloves 1/2 teaspoon whole allspice
1 1/3 cups sugar 1 1/3 cups water 2/3 cup vinegar 1 package
lemon-flavored gelatin 1 cup stuffed green-olive slices 1/2 cup
chopped California walnuts 1/2 cup chopped maraschino cherries
Combine spices, sugar, water, and vinegar. Bring to boiling, reduce
heat and simmer 5 minutes; strain. Add gelatin to hot mixture; stir
to dissolve. Chill until partially set. Add remaining ingredients and
pour into tiny individual molds or 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 x 2 1/2-inch loaf
pan. Chill till firm. Makes 8 to 12 servings
