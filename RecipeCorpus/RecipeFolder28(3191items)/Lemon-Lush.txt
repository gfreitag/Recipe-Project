Title: Lemon Lush
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Powdered suar
3 c  Milk
2 pk Instant lemon pudding
1/4 c  Chopped walnuts
1    Stick of margarine
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1 pk Philadelphia cream cheese
1 ct Cool Whip
Mix the first 3 ingredients and press into a 9 x 13 inch cake pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.  Cool.  Mix Philadelphia Cream
Cheese, 1 cup of Cool Whip, and powdered sugar.  Spread over baked
crust. Mix milk and pudding until slightly thick.  Pour over top. Top
with remaining Cool Whip and 1/4 cup of chopped walnuts.  Chill.
