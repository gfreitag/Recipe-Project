Title: Crabmeat Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Seafood
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 ts Mustard
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
Dash of pepper
Dash of paprika
2 sl White bread, cubed
2 oz Cheddar cheese, cubed
6 oz Crabmeat, well drained
4 oz Canned mushrooms, drained
2    Eggs
1 c  Skim milk
Arrange bread, cheese, crabmeat and mushrooms in Pam sprayed 1 1/2
quart casserole.  Beat eggs with milk and seasonings.  Pour egg
mixture over other ingredients and let stand, covered, in
refrigerator overnight. Sprinkle with paprika and bake at 350 degrees
for 45 minutes. If after 45 minutes, casserole looks too juicy, cook
it uncovered for a while longer. Randy Rigg
