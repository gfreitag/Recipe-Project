Title: Nonfat Egg Nog - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Beverages
Yield: 8 ----------
3 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
1 t  Vanilla
1 t  Rum extract
1/4 c  Brandy, optional
Cinnamon and nutmeg for
-garnish
6 c  Skim milk
1 c  Egg Beaters
1/2 c  Sugar
OR
1/4 c  Sugar
ANd
In a large saucepan over medium heat, heat 5 cups milk, Egg Beaters
and sugar until thickened, stirring constantly. Remove from heat;
stir in vanilla and rum extracts. cover and chill. Just before
serving, stir in brandy and thin to desired consistency with
remaining milk. Sprinkle with cinnamon and nutmeg. Per sugar serving:
129 cal., 0.3g fat (2%), 4mg chol., 0g fiber, 9g pro., 22g carb.,
141mg sod. Per S&L serving: 108 cal., 16g carb. Note: If you use the
brandy, add 17 calories per serving. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie
ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 3-95.
