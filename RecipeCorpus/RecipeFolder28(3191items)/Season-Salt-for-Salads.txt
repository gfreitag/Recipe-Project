Title: Season Salt for Salads
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Sesame seeds
1/4 ts Dried lemon peel
1 ts Coarse salt
1 ts Grated parmesean cheese
3/4 ts Tarragon
1/2 ts Freeze dried chives
Blend ingredients well. Use on salads
