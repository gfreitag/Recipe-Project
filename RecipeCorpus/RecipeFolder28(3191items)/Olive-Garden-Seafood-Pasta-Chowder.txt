Title: Olive Garden Seafood Pasta Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Pasta, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
2 pk (1oz each) newburg sauce mix
3 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Dry white wine
1/4 c  Sliced green onions
-----angela trusty dwtj64a--
6 oz Small shells or bowties
3 oz Crab meat
6 tb Butter
1/2 lb Sliced fresh mushrooms
Cook pasta according to package directions. Sort crab meat to remove
any shell pieces. Melt butter in 3-quart non aluminum saucepan. Add
mushrooms; saute for 3 minutes. Add sauce mix; stir well. Add milk,
water and wine; stir well with a wire whisk until mixture comes to a
boil. Reduce heat; simmer 5-8 min; stirring constantly. Add green
onions, pasta and crab; stir to combine. Heat through. Spoon into
serving bowls and garnish each serving with chopped parsley.
