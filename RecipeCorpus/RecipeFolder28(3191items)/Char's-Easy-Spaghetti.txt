Title: Char's Easy Spaghetti
Categories: Pasta, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Margarine
2 c  Shredded Mozarella Cheese
ds Salt and Pepper
1 pk Angel Hair Pasta
1 cn Pasta Ready Tomatoes
1 c  Diced Ham or Pepperoni
Cook pasta, drain and add the remainder of the ingredients.
