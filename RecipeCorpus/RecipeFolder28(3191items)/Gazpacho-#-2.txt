Title: Gazpacho # 2
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
3 x  Garlic, cloves
1/2 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Chili powder
4 tb Olive oil
12 oz V8 or tomato juice
Zucchini
Green pepper, small
Onion, small
Cucumber
Tomato, large
Finely chop vegetables and garlic ( or run through food processor or
blender for 10 seconds ).  Add vegetables and spices to juice and
refrigerate. THE ANDERSONS' FOOD GARDEN
