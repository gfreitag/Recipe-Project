Title: TUNA CASSEROLE "VIOLET'S"
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 c  Cheddar cheese
1 c  TUNA
1 x  Regular milk
1 pk Wide noodles
2 c  Cream of celery soup
1 c  Evaporated milk
1 x  Cracker crumbs
In one pan cook & drain noodles, while in another pan combine, mix
remaining ingredients & heat to a thin consistency & pour over
noodles. Mix well & top with cracker crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees 45
to 60 minutes.
