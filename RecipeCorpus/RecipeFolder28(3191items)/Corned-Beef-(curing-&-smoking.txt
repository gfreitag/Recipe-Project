Title: Corned Beef (curing & smoking
Categories: Beef, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------SWEET PICKLING BRINE-------------------------
1 ea Recipe sweet pickling brine
Cure beef brisket in Sweet Pickle Brine at 35 F. for 2 days per
pound. After curing, process just the same as beef roast. A meat
thermometer will show when cooking is complete.
