Drain grapefruit and add
cherries between each segment. Carefully pour half the gelatin
mixture over fruits in mold; chill till firm. Meanwhile chill
remaining gelatin mixture till partially set; add remaining
grapefruit segments and carefully pour mixture over firm gelatin in
mold. Chill till set. Unmold on platter; line center with endive and
fill with Cream-cheese Balls: Soften one 8-ounce package cream
cheese; add 1/2 cup chopped California walnuts. Form in 3/4-inch
balls; chill. Makes 8 to 10 servings.
