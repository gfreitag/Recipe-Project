Title: 28085 Chicken Tacquitos
Categories: Tex/mexican, Chicken
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Chunky salsa (Hot)
2 c  Shredded Cheddar
1 cn Corn & red pepper
-Sour cream
-Tortilla chips
2 T  Vegetable oil
3/4 lb Chicken breasts, cut into
-1/2" pieces
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1/2 c  Chopped red pepper
Heat oil and cook chicken 2 minutes.  Add onion and peppers. Cook
until tender.  Drain if necessary.  Add chunky salsa and drained corn
and 1/2 of cheese.  Simmer over low heat until cheese is melted. Just
before serving sprinkle rest of cheese over top and add dollop of
sour cream. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   02-26-95
