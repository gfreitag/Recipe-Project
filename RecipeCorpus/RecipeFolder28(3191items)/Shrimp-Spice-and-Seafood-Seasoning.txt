Title: Shrimp Spice and Seafood Seasoning
Categories: Seafood, Herbs/spice
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: "MAKE YOUR OWN GROCE---------------------
MMMMM------------------FROM :  THERESA MERKLING-----------------------
1/2 t  Whole allspices
1/2 t  Black peppercorns
1/2 t  Ground ginger
Makes 1/2 cup.
2 T  Mustard seed
2 T  Dill seed
2 T  Hot red pepper flakes
2 lg Bay leaves, crumbled
1/2 t  Whole cloves
MMMMM------------------SHRIMP SPICE (CRAB BOIL-----------------------
Combine all ingredients; add to water when boiling seafood. SEAFOOD
SEASONING: Replace whole spices with ground where possible; combine
all ingredients in a blender or food processor and whirl into a
powder; mix in 1/2 c salt. Sprinkle over seafood and sauces. Makes 1
cup.
