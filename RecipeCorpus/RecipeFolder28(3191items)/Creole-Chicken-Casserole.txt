Title: Creole Chicken Casserole
Categories: Chicken, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Finely chopped fully cooked
-smoked ham
1/4 c  Tomato paste
1 sm Green bell pepper; chopped
-(about l/2 cup)
16 oz Whole tomatoes, undrained
6 1/2 oz Shrimp, rinsed and drained
2 c  Uncooked instant rice
2 c  Chicken broth
1/2 ts Red pepper sauce
-medium onion, chopped
-(about 1/2 cup)
1    Clove garlic, crushed
2 c  Cut-up cooked chicken
Mix rice, broth, pepper sauce, onion and garlic in 3-quart
microwavable casserole. Cover tightly and microwave on high 9 to 11
minutes or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed. Stir in
remaining ingredients; break up tomatoes. Cover tightly and microwave
8 to 10 minutes, stirring after 5 minutes, until hot.

6 servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
