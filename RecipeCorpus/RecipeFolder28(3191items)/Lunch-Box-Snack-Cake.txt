Title: Lunch Box Snack Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 15 servings
2 t  Baking soda
1 t  Cinnamon
1 t  Nutmeg
1/2 t  Salt
1 1/2 c  Apples; chopped
1/2 c  Country Morning Blend
3/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Brown sugar
2 1/4 c  Flour
1 c  Buttermilk
2    Eggs
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/2 c  Salted peanuts; chopped
1/4 c  Sugar
Heat oven to 350.  In large mixer bowl combine margarine, sugar and
brown sugar.  Beat at medium speed until well mixed. Add all
remaining cake ingredients except apples.  Beat at medium speed,
scraping bowl often, until well mixed (1 to 2 minutes). By hand, fold
in apples. Pour into greased and floured 13 x 9 pan. In small bowl
stir together all topping ingredients.  Sprinkle over cake batter.
Bake for 35 to 45 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center
comes out clean. Source: Land o Lakes ad
