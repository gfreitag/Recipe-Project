Title: 626944 Chicken Kiev
Categories: Chicken
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Salt
2    Chicken breasts, split
1/2 c  Dry white wine
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 t  Chopped parsley
2/3 c  Butter
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs
2 T  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 t  Basil
1 t  Oregano leaves
1/2 t  Garlic salt
In heavy 2 quart saucepan, melt butter.  Meanwhile, on piece of wax
paper, combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, basil, oregano, garlic
salt, and salt.  Dip chicken breasts in melted butter then roll in
crumbs to coat. Place in ungreased baking pan.  Bake at 375 degrees
for 50-60 minutes or until golden brown.  Pour wine into remainder of
melted butter, add onion and parsley.  When chicken is golden brown,
pour butter sauce mixture over chicken.  Return to oven for 5-10
minutes more or until sauce is hot. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
03-02-95
