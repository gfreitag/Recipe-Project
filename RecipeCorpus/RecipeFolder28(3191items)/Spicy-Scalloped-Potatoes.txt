Title: Spicy Scalloped Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Thinly sliced Onion
1 ea Pepper
1/2 cn 1/4 to 1/2 cn. Roi-tel Tomat
4 c  Thinly sliced Potatoes
1 ea Salt
3 tb Margarine
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In lightly oiled casserol dish place
layer of potatoes, salt, pepper & lightly sprinkle with flour, then
place layer of onions. Alternate potatoes, onions until all are used.
Heat milk, margarine & chopped Roi-tel tomatoes & green chiles. Pour
liquid over potatoes, cover with foil & bake one hour. Remove foil &
bake additional 30 minutes.
