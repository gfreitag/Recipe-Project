Title: Apple Pin-Wheel Salads
Categories: Fruit, Candies, Dips/spread, Mom's
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM-----------------KEYWORDS: WALDORF, WALNUTS----------------------
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: MY MOTHER'S RECIPE C---------------------
-70's

MMMMM--------------------FROM : SALLIE KREBS-------------------------
1 c  Celery slices
3/4 c  Tiny marshmallows
1/2 c  Coarsely broken California
-walnuts
1/3 c  Seedless raisins
1/2 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
8 ea Lettuce cups
Traditional Waldorf salad-
-only this time add tiny
-marshmallows and some
Raisins.
6 ea Unpared red medium apples
Lemon juice, fresh, frozen,
-or canned
Core just 4 of the apples and dice; sprinkle with lemon juice to keep
color bright. Combine with celery, marshmallows, nuts, raisins and
mayonnaise. Spoon mixture into lettuce cups. Core remaining apples;
cut in 1/2-inch slices and brush with lemon juice. Form in pin wheel
atop each salad. Center with additional raisins. Makes 8 servings.
