Title: Poor Man's Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 3 servings
1/2    Stick of margarine, melted
1/3 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Flour, heaping
1 pn Salt
Mix in a small bowl in the order given with a fork.  Pour into a 8 or
9 inch shallow pie crust and bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35
minutes. Randy Rigg
