Title: Lime Broiled Fish^
Categories: Healthy, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
ds Pepper
4    (4oz) boned fish fillets
OR
4    (5 1/2-6oz) fish steaks with
-bone
1 ts Dijon mustard,
-no salt added
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
2 ts Freshly grated gingerroot
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Apple juice concentrate
Combine all ingredients except fish in a shallow bowl. Add fish to
lime mixture; marinate at least 1 hour, turning occasionally. Preheat
broiler. Place fish on broiler rack. Broil 5 minutes, basting with
marinade. Turn carefully; broil 5-7 minutes longer until fish is
opague. Makes 4 servings.

Calories: 89 Protein: 17g Carbohydrates: 3g Fat: 1g Sodium: 80mg
Cholesterol: 67mg
