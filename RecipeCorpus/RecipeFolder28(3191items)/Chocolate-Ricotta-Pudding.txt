Title: Chocolate Ricotta Pudding
Categories: Chocolate, Healthy
Yield: 8 servings
3 1/2 ts Aspartame sweetener
lg Pinch unsweetened dutch
-- cocoa powder
lg Pinch aspartame sweetener
1 1/2 tb Unsweetened dutch cocoa
-- powder
3 tb Rum
2 2/3 c  Ricotta cheese, low-fat
Recipe by: Skinny Chocolate - ISBN 0-940625-80-6 Combine cocoa and
rum and stir until smooth. Add mixture to ricotta. Add aspartame and
stir together well.

Spoon pudding into glass bowl. Cover and chill 3 hours or more. Mix a
large pinch each of cocoa and aspartame and sprinkle over pudding.
Divide pudding among 8 dessert bowls.
