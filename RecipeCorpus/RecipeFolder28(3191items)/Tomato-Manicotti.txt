Title: Tomato Manicotti
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Cottage cheese
2 cl Garlic
1 pk Manicotti
2 c  Grated Monterey Jack Cheese
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1 lg Can of tomato sauce
1/2 lb Ground beef
2 tb Parsley flakes
1 ts Instant onion
1/4 ts Oregano
Pour 1/2 cup of sauce in a baking dish.  Set aside.  Mix beef,
parsley, spices, cottage cheese and garlic.  Stuff cooked shells.
Roll in sauce and pour over rest of the sauce.  Cover with foil. Bake
at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.  Remove and sprinkle on Jack and
Parmesan cheeses. Return to the oven, uncovered, for 10 minutes more.
When using a whole box of noodles, double filling mix. Randy Rigg
