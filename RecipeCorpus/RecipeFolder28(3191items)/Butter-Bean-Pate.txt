Title: Butter Bean Pate
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks, Dips/spread
Yield: 10 servings
1/3 c  Parsley, minced
1 1/2 c  Butter beans, cooked
1 tb Nutritional yeast
1 ts Tamari
1 ts Olive oil
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1 c  Onion, minced
1/3 c  Carrots, minced
Heat oil over medium heat in a large skillet.  Saute garlic, onion,
carrot & parsley for 5 minutes.  Puree the beans with remaining
ingredients until smooth.  Add sauteed vegetables.

Transfer to an oiled decorative serving dish & refrigerate for at
least 1 hour before serving.
