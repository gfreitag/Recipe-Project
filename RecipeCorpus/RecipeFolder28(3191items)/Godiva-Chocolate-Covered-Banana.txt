Title: Godiva Chocolate Covered Banana
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Godiva
Yield: 2 servings
1/2    Banana, Sliced
1/2 c  Vanilla Ice Cream
2 oz Godiva Liqueur
1/2 oz Myers's Rum
Pour Godiva into blender.  Add rum, banana and ice cream. Blend until
smooth. Pour into serving glass. Garnish with banana slice. Makes 2
drinks. Submitted By CHARLENE DEERING   On   03-13-95
