Title: Quick Chocolate Banana Bundt Cake
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 12 servings
4    Eggs
1 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
2    Bananas, very ripe; mashed
1 pk Devil's food (no pudding)
-- cake mix; (18.25 oz)
1 pk Vanilla or chocolate pudding
(3.5 oz)
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 1:15 1. Preheat oven to 350 F. In a large bowl
combine bananas, cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, sour cream, oil, and
1/4 cup water. Beat with electric mixer on low until combined.
Increase speed to medium and beat 2 mins. By hand, mix in nuts.

2. Turn batter into a well-greased and floured 12-cup bundt or tube
pan. Bake 50 to 60 mins, or until a cake tester inserted in center
comes out clean. Let cool in pan 10 to 15 mins before removing from
pan to cool completely.
