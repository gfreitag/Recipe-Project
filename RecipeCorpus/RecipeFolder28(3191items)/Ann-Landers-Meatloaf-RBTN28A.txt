Title: Ann Landers Meatloaf RBTN28A
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Accent
1/2 c  Warm water
1 pk Lipton's onion soup mix
2 lb Ground round steak
2 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  Bread crumbs
3/4 c  Ketchup
Beat thoroughly. Put into loaf pan. Cover with two of strips bacon,
if you like that flavor. Pour one 8-ounce can of tomato sauce over
all. Bake one hour at 350 degrees...
