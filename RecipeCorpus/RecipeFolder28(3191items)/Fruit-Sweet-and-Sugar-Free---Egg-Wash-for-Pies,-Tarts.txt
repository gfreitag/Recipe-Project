Title: Fruit Sweet and Sugar Free - Egg Wash for Pies, Tarts
Categories: Diabetic, Desserts
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 c  Cream or milk
1    Egg or 2 yolks
pn Salt
In a cup or small bowl, mix the egg and salt together with a fork
until well mixted. Stir inthe cream or milk. Apply the egg wash to
the crust using a brush. Egg wash can be stored in the refrigerator
for a couple of days if kept well covered.
