Title: Pork Loin With Potato And Cabbage
Categories: Crockpot, Healthy
Yield: 5 servings
2 ts Balsamic vinegar
2 ts Dried thyme
1 lg Bay leaf
1/4 ts Salt, or to taste; optional
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 lb Pork loin, trimmed of all fa
-- sliced
1 cn Tomato sauce, low-sodium or
-- regular; divided
1 lb Boiling potatoes, peeled
& grated or shredded
1 cn Stewed tomatoes low-sodium
-- or reg w/juice; 14 1/2
-- oz can
3 c  Cabbage, coarse ribs removed
-- thinly sliced
1 lg Onion; finely chopped
2    Garlic cloves; minced
1 tb Light brown sugar; packed
Recipe by: Skinny One-Pot Meals - ISBN 0-940625-75-X In large crock
pot, combine potatoes, tomatoes, cabbage, onion, garlic, brown sugar,
vinegar, thyme, bay leaf, salt, if desired, and pepper. Stir to mix
well. Add pork slices on top of vegetables. Pour one-half of tomato
sauce over all. Reserve remaining tomato sauce in non-reactive
container in refrigerator.

Cover pork mixture, can cook on high setting 1 hour. Stir. Reduce
heat to low, and cook an additional 5 to 6 hours. Stir in remaining
tomato sauce and cook an additional 5 to 10 mins.
