Title: Chocolate Liqueur Sundaes - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Desserts
Yield: 6 ----------
6 ea Tb Cointreau
6 ea Tb Grape Nuts Cereal
1 qt Frozen nonfat vanilla
-yogurt or fatfree ice cream
3/4 c  Braum's or Smucker's
-fatfree fudge topping
1 ea Pk (1.3oz) prepared Dream
-Whip
6 ea Tb kahlua
Spoon 2 tablespoons fudge topping into each of 6 brandy snifters.
Place a scoop of frozen yogurt in each; top with dream whip. Drizzle
1 tablespoon of each liqueur over each snifter. Sprinkle with Grape
Nuts. Per sugar serving: 393 cal., 1.8g fat (4%), 0mg chol., 1g
fiber, 9g pro., 72g varb., 208mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie
ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 3-95.
