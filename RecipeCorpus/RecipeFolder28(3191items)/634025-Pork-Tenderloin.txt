Title: 634025 Pork Tenderloin
Categories: Pork
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 T  Honey
1/2 t  Ground ginger
1/2 t  Salt
1    Garlic clove, crushed
2    Pre-packaged tenderloins
-Apple juice
-Salt & pepper if desired
--MARINADE:--
1/2 c  Orange marmalade
Combine all ingredients well.  Mix marinade and pour over meat. This
can be done in the morning for dinner that night or can be marinated
overnight. Follow directions on package to cook.  Use apple juice in
pan to keep meat moist while cooking.  When done cover with tin foil
to prevent meat from drying out.  Slice into 1/2 inch slices to serve.
