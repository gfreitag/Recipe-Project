Title:  GIBLET GRAVY
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 5 servings
-or
Pieces of chicken or turkey
3    Hard-cooked eggs, chopped
Pepper to taste
3 c  Chicken or turkey broth
1/4 c  Flour
10 3/4 oz Can cream of chicken soup,
Undiluted
1/2 c  (to 3/4 cup) chopped giblets
"This makes enough for a family turkey dinner, with leftovers for
sandwiches the next day, and revives not only the turkey, but your
appetite."

Mix broth and flour until smooth in a medium saucepan. Stir in
remaining ingredients; cook, stirring constantly, until hot and
thickened.

Yields:  5 cups From: "Original Whistle Stop Cafe Cookbook" Posted by:
