Title: Uncooked Plum Pudding
Categories: Desserts, 1941
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Finely shredded citron
1 c  Raisins
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Cold water
1/2 ts Allspice
1 ts Cinnamon
2    Egg whites, stiffly beaten
1 c  Cooked prunes, finely
-chopped
1 c  Grapenuts or rolled graham
-crackers
2 c  Boiling water
1/2 ts Salt
4 tb Gelatin
2 tb Corn sirup
4 tb Fruit juice
Soften gelatin in cold water.  Dissolve in boiling water.  Add all
ingredients except egg whites.  Mix thoroughly.  Chill until
partially set. Fold in egg whites.  Pour into long shallow pan. Chill
until firm. Cut in squares with sharp knife.  Serve with whipped
cream. 8 servings.

Mrs. E.B. Douglas, Anderson, IN.
