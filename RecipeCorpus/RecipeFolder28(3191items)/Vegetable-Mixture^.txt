Title: Vegetable Mixture^
Categories: Healthy, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
4 cl Garlic, minced
1/2 c  Chicken or vegetable stock
2 t  Dried leaf basil
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1/2 t  Onion powder
1 ea Tb low sodium soy sauce
2 ea Zucchini, shredded
4 ea Carrots, shredded
1 ea Onion, finely chopped
2 c  Finely chopped broccoli
2 c  Finely chopped cauliflower
2 ea Plum tomatoes, chopped
1 ea Green bell pepper, chopped
Combine all ingredients and saute in stock until soft. Add seasonings
and soy sauce; cook 5-10 minutes over medium heat. Makes about 1 1/2
quarts cooked vegetables. Note: This is very adaptable....use what
you have on hand. Per tablespoon: Calories: 5 Protein: trace
Carbohydrates: 1g Fat: 0g Sodium: 9mg Cholesterol: 0mg Cooking for
