Title: Super Split Pea Soup
Categories: Vegetables, Soup/stew, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
3 lg Carrots, peeled and wedged
2    Bay leaves
Salt
Pepper
2 lb Dried split green peas
6 qt Water
1 lg Onion, minced
2 lg Potatoes, cut into chunks
Combine all ingredients in deep soup pot; bring to a boil.  Reduce
heat and simmer for 2 hours.  Adjust seasonings and serve.
