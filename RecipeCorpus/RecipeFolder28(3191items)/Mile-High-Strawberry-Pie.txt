Title: Mile-High Strawberry Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit, Puddings
Yield: 8 - 10 folks
1    10" deep dish pastry shell;
1 c  Alta Dena heavy cream;
1 1/2 c  Instant vanilla pudding mix;
3 qt Fresh strawberry; divided
1 1/2 c  Sugar;
6 tb Cornstarch;
2/3 c  Water;
In a large bowl, mash enough strawberries to equal 3 cups.  In a
saucepan, combine sugar and cornstarch.  Stir in the mashed
strawberries and water; mix well.  Cook and stir for 2 minutes.
Remove from heat. Pour into a large bowl. Chill 2-3 hours.  In a
small mixing bowl whip cream and until soft pecks form.  Sprinkle
pudding over cream and whip until stiff. Pipe around edge of pie or
doing on individual. Serves 8 to 10.
