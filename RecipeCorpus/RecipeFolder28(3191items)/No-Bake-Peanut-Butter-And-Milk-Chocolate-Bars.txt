Title: No-Bake Peanut Butter And Milk Chocolate Bars
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 20 servings
1/3 c  Heavy cream or half-and-half
1 c  Milk chocolate chips
1 tb Vegetable oil or solid short
1/3 c  Choc. wafer cookie/graham
-- cracker crumbs
1 1/2 c  Chunky peanut butter
1 1/4 c  Powdered sugar
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:47 1. Sprinkle cookie crumbs evenly over bottom
of foil-lined 8-inch square baking pan.

2. In a medium bowl, mix together peanut butter, powdered sugar, and
cream with a fork until well blended. Carefully spread evenly over
cookie crust. Refrigerate until firm, about 30 mins.

3. In a 1-cup glass measure combine chocolate chips and oil. Heat in a
microwave on Medium 1 1/2 to 2 mins, or until mixture is melted and
smooth when stirred.

4. Spread evenly over peanut butter layer. Refrigerate until
chocolate is set, about 1 hour. Cut into 20 bars. Keep refrigerated.
