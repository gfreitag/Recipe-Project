Title: Italian Zucchini Cauliflower Soup^
Categories: Healthy, Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
3 lg Tomatoes, quartered*
1/2 c  Sliced okra
1 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Very thin noodles, cooked
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1 tb Low sodium soy sauce
2 md Onions, finely diced
2 md Turnips, finely diced
4 md Carrots, finely diced
6 c  Chicken stock
1 sm Cauliflower, cut into tiny
-florets
*Or 2 1 lb. cans Saute onions, turnips and carrots in 1 cup of the
chicken stock in a large saucepan 20 minutes or until tender. Add
remaining ingredients except noodles, cheese and soy sauce. Simmer 25
minutes more. Add noodles, cheese and soy sauce. Simmer 10 minutes
and serve. Makes 8 servings.

Calories: 107 Protein: 6g Carbohydrates: 18g Fat: 1g, 0.08% Sodium:
169mg Cholesterol: 1mg
