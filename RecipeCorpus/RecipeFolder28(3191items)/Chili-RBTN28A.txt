Title: Chili RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1 pk Williams chili seasoning
1 ts Garlic salt
2 tb Picante sauce (hot)
1 x  Salt to taste
2 lb Lean ground beef
1 ea Large onion
2 cn Ranch style beans
1 cn Tomatoes (chopped)
Place over low heat and simmer about an hour. This is so simple but
sooooo good. We can hardly wait for winter. Eating with family and
friends is a great treat...
