Title: Tempura Batter for Deep Frying  *****
Categories: Question?
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Cornstarch
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2    Eggs,beaten
1 c  Cold water
3/4 c  All-purpose flour
Directions Mix all ingredients with fork just until blended. ( Batter
will be thin and slightly lumpy. Typed by G.Major 01\27\95
