Title: Spanish Corn with Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Frozen corn
1    Onion; chopped
1/2    Green pepper; chopped
1/2    Red pepper; chopped
1/2 t  Salt
3/4 lb Chicken breast; boneless
-and cut into strips
2 T  Margarine
1 t  Chili powder
1/2 t  Ground cumin
Brown chicken in margarine with chili powder and cumin, about 4
minutes. Add corn, onion, peppers and salt; cook and stir about 4
minutes until vegetables are tender. 210 calories and 8 grams fat.
