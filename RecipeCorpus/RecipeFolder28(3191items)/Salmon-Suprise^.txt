Title: Salmon Suprise^
Categories: Healthy, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
6    (3oz) slamon fillets
1 c  Marinara sauce (seperate
-recipe)
6    Green lasagna noodles
2 tb Lowfat buttermilk or skim
-milk
Preheat oven to 350F. Cook noodles until tender but firm. Drain. Mix
milk with Marinara sauce and pour 1/4 cup on bottom of a baking dish.
Place 1 fillet in center of 1 cooked noodle and roll up. Place roll
in sauce, seam side down. Repeat with remaining fillets and noodles.
cover rolls with remaining sauce. Bake 20-25 minutes, until bubbly
and fish is cooked. Makes 6 servings.

Variation: Add 1 tablespoon chopped spinach to center of lasagna with
salmon.

Calories: 210 Protein: 20g Carbohydrates: 24g Fat: 4g, 17% Sodium:
67mg Cholesterol: 28mg
