Title: Taco soup rbtn28A
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 10 servings
1 1/2 c  Water
1 pk 1 oz ranch dressing mix
1 cn 14 1/2 oz. hominy drained
3 cn 14 1/2 oz ea stewed tomatoes
1 cn 16 oz red kidney beans drain
x  Shredded cheddar cheese
x  Tortilla chips
2 lb Ground beef
1 sm Chopped onion
3 cn 4 oz. chopped green chilies
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1 cn 16 oz. pinto beans
1 cn 16 oz. lima beans drained
1 pk 1 1/4 oz. taco seasoning
In a large Dutch oven or kettle, brown beef and onion. Drain any fat.
Add all remaining ingredients except last two-bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes.  Top with cheese and serve with
chips, if desired.
