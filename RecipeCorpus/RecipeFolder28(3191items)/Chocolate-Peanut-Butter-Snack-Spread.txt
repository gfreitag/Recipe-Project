Title: Chocolate Peanut Butter Snack Spread
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Peanut butter, chunky,
-- creamy,or honey-nut
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
1/4 c  Milk
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:07 In a 1-qt glass measure, combine chocolate
chips and milk. Heat in microwave on High 1 to 1 1/2 min, or until
chocolate is melted and mixture is smooth when stirred. Sti r in
peanut butter until well blended. Store, covered, in refrigerator up
to 1 month. Remove from refrigerator 1 hour before serving for easier
spreading.
