Title: Favorite Broccoli Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped seeded tomatoes
2    Hard-cooked eggs, sliced
1 c  Mayonnaise or salad
-dressing
2 tb Vinegar
1 bn Broccoli, separated into
-florets
1    Head cauliflower, separated
-into florets
8    Bacon strips, fried and
-crumbled
In a large salad bowl, combine broccoli, cauliflower, bacon, onion,
tomatoes and eggs; set aside. In another bowl, combine mayonnaise,
sugar and vinegar; mix until smooth. Just before serving, pour
dressing over salad and toss.

Yield: 6-8 servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
