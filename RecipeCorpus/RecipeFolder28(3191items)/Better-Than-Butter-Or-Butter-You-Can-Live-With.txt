Title: Better Than Butter Or Butter You Can Live With
Categories: Delete
Yield: 1 servings
2 pk "BUTTERBUDS" NATURAL BUTTER
-FLAVORED MIX
1    -3 PACKAGES "SWEET'N LOW"
1 pt LOWFAT (1-2% MILKFAT)
-COTTAGE CHEESE
1/4 c  WATER
4 tb NONFAT DRY MILK
Put all ingredients in blender. Blend on low and increase speed until
smooth. Pour contents back into cheese container and refrigerate.

There are 7 calories per TABLESPOON, UNMEASURABLE FATS & CHOLESTEROL
