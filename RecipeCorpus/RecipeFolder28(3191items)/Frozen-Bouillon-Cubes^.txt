Title: Frozen Bouillon Cubes^
Categories: Healthy
Yield: 16 servings
1 ea Bay leaf, broken in half
1 cl Garlic, minced
2 c  Chicken or beef stock
1/2 c  White wine
Boil all ingredients in a saucepan until reduced by half. Cool. Pour
into ice cube trays and freeze until solid. Remove from trays and pl
Add 1 cube to wok or skillet for sauteing or stir frying. Per cube:
Calories: 8 Protein: 0.5g Carbohydrates: 0.5g Fat: trace Sodium: 6mg
