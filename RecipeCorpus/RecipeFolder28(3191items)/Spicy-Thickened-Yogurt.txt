Title: Spicy Thickened Yogurt
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 3 /4 cup
1 ts Pure maple syrup
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Yogurt
1/2 ts Pure chile powder
1/2 ts Ground coriander seeds
1. Put the yogurt in a stainless steel strainer set over a bowl and
let drain for 3-4 hours at room temperature.

2. In a small skillet, toast the chile powder and coriander over
moderately high heat, stirring until slightly darkened and fragrant,
about 40 seconds. Scrape toasted spices into a small bowl to cool.
Stir in the drained yogurt, maple syrup and salt. Can be kept in the
refrigerator for up to 1 week. Stir well and bring to room
temperature before serving.
