Title: Creamy Tofu Tomato Soup^
Categories: Healthy, Soup/stew
Yield: 2 servings
3    Tomatoes, diced
12 oz Tofu
2 tb Minced parsley
1    Onion, diced
1 cl Garlic, crushed
1 c  Skim milk
-Dash Tabasco sauce
Spray a saucepan with Pam. Saute onion and garlic over medium heat 3-4
minutes or until soft. Add milk, Tabasco and tomatoes, stirring
constantly. Cool 10 minutes. Pour into food processor, add 10 ounces
of the tofu and process until smooth. Serve hot or cold. Cut
remaining tofu into small cubes. Sprinkle with tofu and parsley.
Makes 2 servings.

Calories: 119 Protein: 10g Carbohydrates: 12g Fat: 3g, 23% Sodium:
43mg Cholesterol: 1mg
