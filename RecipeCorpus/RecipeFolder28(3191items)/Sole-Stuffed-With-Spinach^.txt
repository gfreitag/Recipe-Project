Title: Sole Stuffed With Spinach^
Categories: Healthy, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
-fillets
1/2    Lemon, juice of
1 c  Marinara sauce (separate
-recipe)
3 tb Lowfat buttermilk
1 c  Chopped fresh spinach
1/2 c  Lowfat cottage cheese
2 cl Garlic, minced
2 tb Xhopped fresh parsley
4    (4oz0 sole or flounder
Preheat oven to 350F. Spray an ovenproof dish with Pam. Cook spinach
in a small amount of water until just wilted, about 2-3 minutes. Mix
spinach, cheese, garlic and parsley in a bowl. Place 2 tablespoons
mixture in center of each fillet. Roll fillet and place in dish, seam
side down; pour lemon juice over rolled fillets. Mix sauce and milk
together and pour over fish. Bake about 15 minutes or until fish
turns opague. Makes 4 srevings.

Variations: Sprinkle 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese evenly over fish
when done and place under broiler 2 minutes. Spray with Pam before
broiling to keep it from becoming too dry.

Calories: 200 Protein: 37g Carbohydrates: 8g Fat: 2g, 9% Sodium: 297mg
Cholesterol: 56mg
