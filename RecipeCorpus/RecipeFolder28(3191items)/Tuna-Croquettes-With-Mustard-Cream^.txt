Title: Tuna Croquettes With Mustard Cream^
Categories: Healthy, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
-canola oil, lightly flaked
-with fork
-pepper to taste
2    Egg whites, lightly beaten
1/4 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 c  Mashed potatoes
1 md Onion, finely chopped
1 tb Plain nonfat yogurt
1/4 c  Chopped dill, lightly
-packed
2 ts Fresh lemon juice
1 cn (9 1/4oz) tuna packed in
Combine potatoes, onion and yogurt until smooth. Stir in dill, lemon
and pepper. Lightly fold in tuna. Pour in egg whites and mix. Divide
misture into 6 croquettes. Spray a large nonstick skillet with butter
flaovred Pam. Heat skillet over medium heat until hot. Cook 1 1/2
minutes on each side or until nicelybrowned and heated through.
Combine yogurt and mustard. Blend until creamy. Place croquettes on
whole grain buns and top each with 2 tablespoons of mustard cream on
each, lettuce and tomato. Serve.

Calories: 94 Protein: 12g Carbohydrates: 7g Fat: 2g, 20% Sodium: 18mg
Cholesterol: trace
