Title: Spicy Rice Casserole
Categories: Rice, Casseroles, Sausage
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Boiling water
1    To 2 jalapeno peppers,
-seeded and finely minced
1 pk (6-1/4 ounces) long grain
-and wild rice
1 lb Mild bulk pork sausage
1 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Garlic powder
2 md Onions, chopped
2 md Green peppers, chopped
2    Beef bouillon cubes
In a large skillet, cook sausage, cumin and garlic powder, stirring
often. Drain. Add onions and green peppers; saut until crisp-tender.
Dissolve bouillon in water; add to skillet. Stir in jalapenos, rice
and rice seasoning packet; bring to a boil. Reduce Yield: 4-6
servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
