Title: Hoppin' John Salsa
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1 T  Vegetable oil
1 t  Sugar
Salt
Black pepper
1 cn (15oz) black-eyed peas
-drained/rinsed
1/2 c  Chopped carrots
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Chopped red onion
2 T  Chopped cilantro
Combine first five ingredients.  In small bowl, combine remaining
ingredients.  Pour over vegetable mixture and combine.

per serving (1/2 cup): 104 cal, 4 g pro, 15 g car, 4 g fat, 27% cal
from fat, 0 mg chol, 270 mg sod.

original recipe from Woman's Day Low-Fat Meals Charlotte Welch
