Title: Crab Stuffed Manicotti - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 9 servings
Nonfat cooking spray
1 cn (15oz) tomato sauce
1 cn (6oz) tomato paste
1/2 t  Dried whole oregano
1/4 t  Whole dried thyme
1/2 t  Dried whole basil
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1 t  Low sodium Worcestershire
8 oz Nonfat mozzarella cheese,
-grated
1 ea Pk (8oz) imitation king
-crabmeat
18 ea Manicotti shells, cooked
1 1/2 c  Nonfat ricotta cheese
1 cn (8oz) mushroom pieces,
-rinsed and drained
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
1/4 c  Dry white wine or cooking
-wine
1 ea Tb lemon juice
Combine crabmeat, ricotta, mushrooms, onions, wine and lemon juice
until blended. Stuff shells. Place in a 13 x 9 x 2" casserole that
has been sprayed with a nonstick cooking spray. Combine tomato sauce,
tomato paste, spices and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a boil;
reduce heat and simmer about 5 minutes. Spoon tomato sauce mixture
over the shells. Sprinkle with mozzarella. Cover and bake at 350F.
35-40 minutes. Per serving: 242 cal., 1.2g fat (4%), 8mg chol., 3g
fiber, 17g pro., 41g carb., 821mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie
ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 2-95.
