Title: Fruit And Oat Snack Mix
Categories: Snacks, Mixes, Kids
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 pk (5.4oz) Betty Crocker Fruit
-Wrinkles Strawberry Fruit
-Bits
3 c  Cheerios cereal
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Salted peanuts
Mix all ingredients and store in an airtight container for up to 5
days.

Makes 5 cups snack mix
