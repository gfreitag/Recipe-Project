Title: Pineapple Carob Cashew Brownies
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Vegetarian
Yield: 12 brownies
1/2 c  Unsweetened applesauce
1/4 c  Canned pineapple pieces
-- (Unsweetened)
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Raw cashew pieces
2 c  Whole wheat pastry flour
2/3 c  Carob powder
2 ts Baking powder
1 pn Salt
1/2 c  Honey or apple juice
Preheat oven to 350 F.  In a large mixing bowl combine flour, carob,
baking powder and salt.

Blend honey, applesauce, juices, pineapple and vanilla.

Add dry ingredients to wet mixture and stir in nuts.  Pour batter
into an oiled 9" x 13" pan.  Bake 1 hour or until brownies pull away
from sides of pan.

Calories per serving: 172 Grams of Fat: 4 % Fat calories: 20
Cholesterol: 0 mg Grams of Fiber: 3.7
