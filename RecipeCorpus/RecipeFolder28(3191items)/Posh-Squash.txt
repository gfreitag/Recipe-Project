Title: Posh Squash
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Parmesan cheese
Salt and pepper to taste
Buttered bread crumbs
2 lb Yellow squash
2    Eggs; beaten
1/4 c  Bell pepper; finely
-- chopped
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
Recipe by: Come and Get It! by Jr. Welfare League of Talladega, Al

Cook squash, drain. Combine other ingredients in a bowl. Add squash.
Pour into buttered casserole dish. Top with crumbs. Bake at 350
degrees for 30 mins.

Note: This can be frozen, but defrost thoroughly before cooking.

Originally submitted to book by Beth Ralston of Charlotte, N.C.
