Title: Macaroni Salmon Loaf^
Categories: Healthy, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
-crumbs
3    Egg whites
3/4 c  Skom milk
2 cl Garlic, minced
1 ts Dried dill
1/4 ts Dried rosemary
4 oz Elbows, cooked al dente
1 cn (1lb0 pink or red salmon
2 tb Onion, finely minced
1 tb Chopped parsley
-Dash pepper
1 c  Coarse whole wheat bread
Preheat oven to 350F. Spray a 9 x 5" loaf pan with Pam. Combine all
ingredients in a medium size bowl with a fork. Place in loaf pan and
bake 35-40 minutes. Makes 4 servings.

Variation: Heat 1/2 cup Marinara sauce with 2 tablespoons lowfat
buttermilk. Use as a sauce for the salmon loaf.

Per serving without sauce: Calories: 245 Protein: 25g Carbohydrates:
29g Fat: 5g, 19% Sodium: 444mg Cholesterol: 36mg
