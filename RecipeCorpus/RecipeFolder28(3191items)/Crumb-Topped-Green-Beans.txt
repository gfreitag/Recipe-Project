Title: Crumb-Topped Green Beans
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Italian-seasoned
-dry breadcrumbs
1 1/4 lb Green beans
1 tb Butter
1/2 c  Onion(s), chopped
Steam the green beans until crisp-tender.

Melt butter in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add chopped
onion and breadcrumbs and saut for 1 minute. Add green beans and
saut for 4 minutes or until tender.
