Title: Tabbouleh With Meat
Categories: Asian, Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Bulgur wheat
1/2 c  Celery
1/2 c  Carrot
1/2 c  Parsley
1/4 c  Green pepper
1/4 c  Green onion
1 1/4 c  Cucumber; chopped
2    Tomato; chopped
8 oz Chicken, cubed; cooked
1/3 c  Oil
1/3 c  Lemon juice
2 ts Mint; dried
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2    Garlic clove
2 c  Water; boiling
Mix oil, lemon juice, mint, salt, pepper and garlic and pour over
meat. Marinate 30 minutes. Pour water over bulgur and soak 20
minutes. Drain and squeeze out excess water. Add remaining
ingredients except cucumber and tomato and chill for 3 to 5 hours.
Add rest before serving.
