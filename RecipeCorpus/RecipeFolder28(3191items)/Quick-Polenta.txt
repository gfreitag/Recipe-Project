Title: Quick Polenta
Categories: Italian
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Butter
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
1 1/2 qt Water
2 ts Salt
2 c  Instant polenta
Recipe by: 365 Easy Italian Recipes - ISBN 0-06-016310-0 Preparation
Time: 0:15

1. In a large saucepan, bring water and salt to a boil over high heat.
Stir in polenta in a slow steady stream. Reduce heat to medium and
continue stirring 5 mins, or until polenta is thickened to
consistency of hot cereal.

2. Remove polenta from heat and stir in butter, pepper, and Parmesan
cheese.
