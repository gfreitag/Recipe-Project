Title: Bacon and Cheese Puff Pie
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
1 ds Pepper
1/2 c  Flour
3/4 c  Sour cream
1/2 ts Salt
Paprika
1 cn Crescent rolls
13 sl Cooked and crumbled bacon
2 md Tomatoes, sliced
6 sl Velveeta cheese
3    Eggs, separated
1/2 ts Salt
Unroll dough and form a crust with rolls in 9 inch piepan.  Sprinkle
bacon over crust.  Top with tomato slices, season with salt and
pepper. Place cheese slices over the top to form a cover.  Beat egg
whites until stiff. In a large bowl, mix yolks, sour cream, flour,
1/2 tsp salt and the dash of pepper.  Blend well; gently fold into
egg whites. Pour mixture over cheese layer.  Sprinkle with paprika.
Bake at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes. Randy Rigg
