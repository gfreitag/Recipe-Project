Title: Chocolate Cherry Bars (1974)
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 36 bars
MMMMM---------------------------BARS:--------------------------------
-filling
1 t  Almond extract
2    Eggs, beaten
1 pk Pillsbury Plus Devil's Food
-cake mix
21 oz Can cherry fruit pie
MMMMM-------------------------FROSTING:------------------------------
6 oz Pkg semi-sweet chocolate
-chips
1 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Milk
5 T  Butter
1974 Pillsbury Bake Off Winner Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and
flour 15 x 10 x 1 inch pan or 13 x 9 inch pan. In large bowl, combine
all bar ingredients; stir until well blended. Spread in greased and
floured pan. Bake at 350 degrees until toothpick inserted in center
comes out clean. For 15 x 10 x 1 inch pan, bake 20 to 30 minutes; for
13 x 9 inch pan, bake 25 to 30 minutes.  In small saucepan, combine
sugar, milk and butter. Bring to a boil; boil 1 minute, stirring
constantly. Remove from heat; stir in chocolate chips until smooth.
Pour and spread over warm bars. Cool completely. Cut into bars.
