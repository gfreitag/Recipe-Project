Title: Ravioli Con Pesto
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 5 servings
1 1/2 c  Basil leaves
1/2 t  Salt and pepper
1/2 c  Romano; grated
2    Tomatoes; diced
1/2 pk Cheese ravioli
1 c  Olive oil
3 T  Finely chopped walnuts or
-pine nuts
3 cl Garlic; finely chopped
In blender, combine basil, salt, pepper, garlic, walnuts, cheese and
olive oil at high speed until smooth and thin enough to run easily
off spatula. Pour mixture over hot cooked ravioli, add tomatoes and
serve. Source: Rosetto Pkg
