Title: Red Chick
Categories: Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Tomato sauce
1 cn Water
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Prepared mustard
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Vinegar
Chicken pieces (of your
-choice)
1/2 c  Onion; sliced
1/2 c  Bell pepper; chopped
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1    Bay leaf
1/2 ts Oregano
Recipe by: Come and Get It! by Jr. Welfare League of Talladega, Al

Place chicken pieces side by side in 13 x 9 casserole dish. Mix
together remaining ingredients and pour over chicken. Cook at 350 F
for 1 1/2 hours.

Turn pieces midway through the cooking time. Yield: 4 to 6 servings

Original submitted to book by Mrs Glen Gaines (Laura)
