Title: Orange Baked Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Healthy
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Parsley, or 1 tsp dried
-- chopped
1    Cl Garlic; minced
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Chicken, skinless
-- cut in pieces
1 cn (6 oz) frozen orange
Juice concentrate
-- or;
1 1/2 c  Orange juice
1 tb Orange rind; grated
2 tb Soy sauce, low sodium
1 tb Onion; minced
Recipe by: The New American Diet - ISBN 0-671-66375-5

Preheat oven to 400 F. Combine all ingredients except chicken. Place
chicken in a 9 x 12-inch pan. Pour marinade over chicken. Cover pan
(foil) and bake for 45 mins. Uncover and continue baking until
tender. Baste several times with sauce. If desired, thicken remaining
sauce in pan and serve as gravy.

Makes 6 servings.

(Author's Note): Very easy to prepare. Sauce is great over rice or
potatoes. You may also marinate the chicken in sauce 8 hours or
overnight in refrigerator.
