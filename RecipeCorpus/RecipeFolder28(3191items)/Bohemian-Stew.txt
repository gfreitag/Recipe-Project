Title: Bohemian Stew
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Vegetables
Yield: 10 servings
1 cn Tomatoes (28 oz size)
1 c  Diced potatoes
2    Bay leaves
1/2 c  Frozen peas
1 tb Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
3/4 c  Hot water
2 1/2 lb Beef stew meat
Oil for frying
2 c  Chopped carrots
2 c  Chopped celery
3    Onions, cut up
3 tb Tapioca
1 tb Sugar
Preheat oven to 250 deg. Brown stew meat in hot oil. Remove from oil
and combine with all other ingredients into heavy pot. Bake covered
at 250 degrees F for five hours, stirring occasionally. Serve with
dumplings or boiled potatoes or buttered noodles.
