Title: Quick Coconut Milk
Categories: Nuts/grains, Indian
Yield: 1 cup
10 c  Lukewarm water
1 c  Shredded coconut
Blend coconut and 1 cup of water to a smooth paste in a blender or
food processor. Put the paste in a cheesecloth bag and squeeze out as
much coconut milk as possible. Place the remaining water in a bowl
and knead the coconut bag in the water to squeeze out any remaining
milk.
