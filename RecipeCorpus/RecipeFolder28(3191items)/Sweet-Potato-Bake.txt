Title: Sweet Potato Bake
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Coconut milk
-(or whole milk)
2    Eggs, beaten
2 lb Sweet potatoes or yams
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Ground allspice
1/4 ts Cayenne (more to taste)
1. Preheat oven to 375øF. Roast sweet potatoes on a baking sheet for
30-45 minutes, or until soft. Cool slightly, then peel.

2. Mash sweet potatoes to a smooth puree with the sugar. Season with
allspice, cayenne and salt. Can be prepared to this point up to 2
days in advance; refrigerate. Bring to room temperature before
proceeding.

3. Preheat oven to 375øF. Stir the milk and eggs into the sweet
potatoes, blending well. Scrape the mixture into a 4-cup souffl dish
or a 9-inch square baking dish and bake until set, about 30 minutes.
