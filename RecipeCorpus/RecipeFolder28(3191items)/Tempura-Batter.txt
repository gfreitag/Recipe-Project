Title: Tempura Batter
Categories: Appetizers, Chinese
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Cornstarch
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2    Eggs; beaten
1 c  Water; cold
3/4 c  Flour
Mix all ingredients with a fork JUST UNTIL BLENDED (batter will
be thin and slightly lumpy).  Be sure all items are chilled. Keep
mixed batter on ice water. Batter does not keep well; use immediately.
