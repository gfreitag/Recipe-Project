Title: Muesli
Categories: Breakfast, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 1 recipe
4 oz Mixed nuts, chopped
4 oz Mied dried fruit, chopped
1 lb Rolled oats
1 lb Barley flakes
Put the oats into a bowl & stir in the remaining ingredients until
evenly mixed.  Store in an airtight container.

Serve with soymilk & sweeten with maple syrup or brown sugar if
desired. Also serve garnished with sl;ices of fresh fruit in season.

Mary Norwak, "Grains, Beans & Pulses"
