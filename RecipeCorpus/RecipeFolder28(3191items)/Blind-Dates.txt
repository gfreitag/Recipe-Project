Title: Blind Dates
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 1 batch
-roasted)
Granulated sugar
1    Packet dried dates
Salted peanuts (not dry
Cut a slit in each date and remove the stone.  Fill the dates with the
salted peanuts and roll in granulated sugar.  Pack in layers of waxed
or greaseproof paper, tin foil or paper cases in tins or boxes.
