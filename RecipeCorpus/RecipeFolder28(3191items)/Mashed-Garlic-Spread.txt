Title: Mashed Garlic Spread
Categories: Relishes, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
12 x  Garlic cloves
1 tb Vinegar(white)
Place all ingredients in a blender or food processor bowl and chop
to a fine mash. Place in a tightly sealed glass jar and store in
refrigerator. Keeps well. Great for garlic bread, or any recipe
calling for mashed garlic.
