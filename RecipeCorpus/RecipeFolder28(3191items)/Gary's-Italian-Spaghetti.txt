Title: Gary's Italian Spaghetti
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 5 servings
1 3/4 c  Water (1 sauce can full)
1/2 T  Sugar
1/4 t  Oregano
1/4 t  Basil
1    Garlic clove, crushed
1    Bay leaf, small
16 oz Spaghetti
1/2 lb Beef, extra lean, ground
1/2 lb Italian sausage *
1/2    Onion, medium, chopped
1 cn Diced tomatoes (14oz.)
1 cn Tomato sauce (15oz.)
1 cn Tomato paste (12oz.)
1 pk Spaghetti sauce mix **
* Cut into 3/4" pieces ** Italian-style with mushrooms

Cook Italian sausage over medium-high heat until nicely browned.
Remove sausage from pot, leaving drippings. Cook onions in hot
drippings for three minutes; add ground beef. Cook and stir ground
beef and onion until meat is brown and onion is tender. Return
sausage to pot; drain grease from meat mixture. Stir in remaining
ingredients, except spaghetti. Cover; Simmer 1 1/2 hours, stirring
occasionally.
Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Serve meat sauce
over hot spaghetti; top with parmesan cheese, if desired.
