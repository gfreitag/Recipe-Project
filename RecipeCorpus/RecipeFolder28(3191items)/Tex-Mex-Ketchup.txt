Title: Tex Mex Ketchup
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 5 cups
1 t  Dried minced onion
32 oz Ketchup
1 c  Med thick and chunky salsa
1 T  Dried cilantro
1 t  Ground cumin
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients; stir until well blended.
Store in airtight container in refrigerator.
