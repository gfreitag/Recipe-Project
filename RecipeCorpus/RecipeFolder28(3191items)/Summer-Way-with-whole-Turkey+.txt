Title: Summer Way with whole Turkey+
Categories: Turkey
Yield: 1 sm. turkey
Boiling water
1 sm Turkey, thawed
Cut off wings and legs of turkey. Put the carcass into a pot of
BOILING WATER  to cover. Cover and Simmer for 45 minutes. Add wings
and legs and simmer for another 45 minutes. Allow to cool in broth,
covered. Debone when cool enough to handle.
