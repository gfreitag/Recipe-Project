Title: Hamburger Casseole
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk Macaroni & Cheese
1 x  Picante sauce
1 lb Hamburger Meat
1 cn Tomatoe Paste
1 x  Cheddar cheese etc.
Brown & drain Hamburger meat. At the same time prepare the Macaroni &
Cheese as directed. Mix together these ingredients plus Tomatoe
Paste. Add Picante Sauce to taste. Pour into 8x10 (whatever) bake
dish & top with shredded cheese. Put in preheated 350 degree oven
until cheese is melted.
