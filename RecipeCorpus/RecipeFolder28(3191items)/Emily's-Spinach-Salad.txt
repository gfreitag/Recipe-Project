Title: Emily's Spinach Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Seasoned pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 pk (10 ounces) fresh spinach,
-torn into bite-size
-pieces
5    Bacon strips, cooked and
-crumbled
2    Hard-cooked eggs, sliced
2/3 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
2 ts Lemon juice
2 ts Soy sauce
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Salt
Combine first 10 ingredients in a jar, cover tightly and shake until
well mixed; set aside. Place spinach in a large salad bowl. Just
before serving, pour dressing over spinach and toss gently. Garnish
with crumbled bacon and egg slices. Yield: 6-8 servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
