Title: 631229 Oven Bbq'd Pork Tenderloin
Categories: Pork
Yield: 4 servings
1    Green pepper, minced
1    Clove garlic, minced
2    Pork tenderloins (1 1/2 lb.
-total)
-Oil
MARINADE:--1/4 c. soy sauce
1/4 c  Red wine
1 T  Brown sugar
1 T  Honey
1/2 t  Cinnamon
Preparation : Combine marinade ingredients in non-aluminum dish. Add
pork, turning to coat.  Cover and refrigerate 3 hours or overnight.
Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking.  Add meat to
oven-proof skillet and brown.  Transfer skillet to oven and roast 325
degrees for 30 minutes. Cool to room temperature and slice thin.
