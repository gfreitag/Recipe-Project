Title: MUSHROOM SALAD (Hungarian Style)
Categories: Salads, East europe
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
3/4 c  Sour cream or plain yogurt
-salt & pepper (to taste)
1 T  Parsley, finely diced
2 lb Firm fresh mushrooms
1 ea Onion, diced
1 T  Vinegar
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 T  Lemon juice
1 t  Paprika
1 c  Bell peppers, diced
2 ea Cloves of garlic, minced
Clean and trim the mushrooms, blanch for 1-2 minutes in boiling salted
water. Drain mushrooms and set aside. Mix the onion, garlic, green
pepper, lemon juice and vinegar. Season to taste with salt & pepper.
Blend in the sour cream and stir until smooth. Add in the mushrooms.
Note: if the mushrooms are large half or quarter them. Stir until the
mushrooms are well coated with the dressing. Sprinkle with parsley
and paprika when serving.
