Title: Broccoli Soup RBTN28A
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Onion
1 ea 16 oz. pkg frozen broccoli
2 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 lb Velvetta cheese cubed
5 c  Water
4 ea Chicken bullion cubes
Dissolved in water
2 c  Cubed potatoes
1 c  Chopped celery
In large pan add water, dissolve boullion cube, potatoes, celery,
onion, cook until tender, add broccli, cook until tender, add cream
of chicken soup & Velvetta, cook some more & serve.. This is
Melvina's special....
