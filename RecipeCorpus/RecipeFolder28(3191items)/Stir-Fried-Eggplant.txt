Title: Stir-Fried Eggplant
Categories: Side dish, Indian, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Cayenne
1/4 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Lemon juice
6 tb Mustard oil
1 ts Panchporan powder
1/8 ts Asafetida
1 md Eggplant, cubed
2 ts Coriander, ground
Heat oil in a wok until it is very hot & smoking.  Put in panchphoran
& asafetida.  A second or two later, put in the eggplant, stir for 1
minute & reduce heat to medium.  Add corinader, cayenne, turmeric &
salt. Kep stirring & frying for 15 minutes, adding 1 tb water every
minute or so, until the eggplant is cooked.  Sprinkle with lemon
juice & stir again.

Madhur Jaffrey, "A Taste of India"
