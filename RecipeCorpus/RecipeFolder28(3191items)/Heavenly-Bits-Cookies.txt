Title: Heavenly Bits Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Chopped pecans
2 c  Plain flour
1/2 lb Margarine
5 tb Powdered sugar
Cream margarine and sugar.  Add flour, pecans and vanilla.  Mix
together well.  Roll into small oval shaped balls.  Bake for 15
minutes in a 350 degree oven on a greased cookie sheet.  Cool and
roll in powdered sugar. Randy Rigg
