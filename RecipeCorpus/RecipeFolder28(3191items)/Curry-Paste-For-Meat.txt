Title: Curry Paste For Meat
Categories: Curries, Indian, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Cayenne Pepper
2 T  Ground Coriander
1/2 t  Ground Caraway Seeds
1 1/2 t  Paprika
2 T  Anchovy Paste
2 t  Vinegar
1 T  Freshly Ground Nutmeg
1/2 t  Ground Cloves
1 t  Mace
10 ea Cardamom Pods ( Remove
-Seeds To Grind) OR
1 t  Cardamom Powder
Combine all of the dry ingredients.  Add the anchovy paste and
vinegar, blend well.  Keep in an airtight jar. From How To Make Good
Curries by Helen Lawson  Copyright 1973
