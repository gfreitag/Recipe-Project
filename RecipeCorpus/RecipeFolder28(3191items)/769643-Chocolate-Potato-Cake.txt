Title: 769643 Chocolate Potato Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Ground chocolate (prefer
-Ghirardeli's)
1 t  Ground cinnamon
1 t  Ground cloves
1/2 t  Ground nutmeg
1 c  Chopped walnuts
4    Eggs
2/3 c  Shortening or butter
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Mashed potatoes
1/2 c  Sweet milk or coffee
2 c  Flour
2 t  Baking powder
1/4 t  Salt
Cream butter and sugar.  Add beaten egg yolks and hot mashed
potatoes. Sift together 3 times, the flour, baking powder, salt,
chocolate, spices; add nuts. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Bake
in three (a must) greased 8-inch layer pans at 350 degrees for 30
minutes. Cool for 30 minutes. Spread on marshmallow frosting and
sprinkle with nuts. (Cake is also very good with a cream cheese icing
and nuts.) Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-04-95
