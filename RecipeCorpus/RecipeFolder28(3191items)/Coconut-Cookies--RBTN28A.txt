Title: Coconut Cookies  RBTN28A
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Coconut extract
1 c  Bisquick
1 c  Instant potato flakes
1/3 c  Margarine (softened)
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
Cream margarine, sugar until fluffy. Add egg & extract. Stir until
creamy. Stir in flakes & bisquick. Roll in 3/4" balls. Bake at 350*
8-10 minutes on ungreased cookie sheet.
