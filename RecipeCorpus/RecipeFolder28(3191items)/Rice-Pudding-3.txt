Title: Rice Pudding 3
Categories: Puddings, Rice
Yield: 12 servings
1 t  Cinnamon
1 t  Vanilla
2 ea Eggs, beaten
2 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Cooked rice
1/2 c  Raisins, optional
1/3 c  Sugar
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.. In medium  saucepan, heat milk to very
warm. * DO NOT BOIL *. Remove from heat; add remaining ingredients;
mix well. Pour into ungreased 1 1/2 quart casserole. Place casserole
in baking pan with about 1 inch hot water. Bake at 350 for 30
minutes. Carefully stir pudding; bake an additional 15 to 20 minutes
or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Serve warm or
cold with cream if desired. Makes 12 servings.
