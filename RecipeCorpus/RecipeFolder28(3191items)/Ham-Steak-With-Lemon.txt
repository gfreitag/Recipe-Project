Title: Ham Steak With Lemon
Categories: Pork, Beef
Yield: 2 servings
3 T  Brown sugar
2 t  Prepared mustard
2 sl Fully cooked ham (1/2 inch
-thick)
1 ea Lemon, halved
Broil ham slices 5 inches from the heat for 5 minutes. Meanwhile,
slice one lemon half; set aside. Grate peel from the other half and
squeeze 1 tablespoon of juice into a bowl. Add peel, brown sugar and
mustard to juice. Turn ham over; brush with the lemon mixture. Top
with lemon slices. Broil 3-4 minutes more or until ham is heated
through. Serves 2. SOURCE:* Taste of Home Magazine, Feb./Mar. 1995
