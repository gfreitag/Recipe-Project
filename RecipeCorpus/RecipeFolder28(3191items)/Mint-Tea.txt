Title: Mint Tea
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Sugar
3 c  Boiling water
4    Tea bags of green tea
1 c  Packed mint leaves
Place tea bags, mint and sugar in teapot. Pour boiling water over and
stir to dissolve sugar. Steep for 4 minutes and serve.
