Title: Pollo Loco Marinade (2nd)
Categories: Marinades, Salsa/sauce, Poultry, Bbq/grill, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Lime;  squeezed.
1 x  Cilantro
1 ea Lemon;  squeezed
1/4 c  Orange juice
Pour over raw chicken and let soak for up to 4 hours and grill on the
bbq.. also you can add tequilla and its wonderful.
