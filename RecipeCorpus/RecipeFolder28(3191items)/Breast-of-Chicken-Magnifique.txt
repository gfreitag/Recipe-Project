Title: Breast of Chicken Magnifique
Categories: Chicken, Turkey, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1 cn Cream of Chicken soup
1/2 lg Clove of garlic, minced
Generous dash crushed thyme
1 pn Rosemary, crushed
1/3 c  Half and half
Cooked noodles
(You can serve this as a
-fancy dinner to your
-friends and they
Will think you worked all
-day.  It is really great.)
4 ea Chicken breasts
1 T  Butter or margarine
Brown chicken in butter; remove.  Brown mushrooms.  Stir in soup,
garlic and seasonings.  Add chicken.  Cover and cook over low heat 45
minutes, stirring now and then.  Blend in cream; heat slowly. Serve
with noodles. Serves 4
