Title: Frozen Strawberry-Yogurt Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Desserts
Yield: 16 servings
16 oz Cool Whip Topping thawed
2 ea Graham Cracker Crust
16 oz Vanilla Yogurt
2 c  Strawberrys sweetened frozen
Fold Yogurt into whipped topping, blend well; fold in strawberries.
Spoon into crust Freeze until firm, 4 hrs. or overnight. Remove from
freezer 30 minutes before serving and keep chilled in refrigerator.
Garnish with additional whole strawberries, if desired. Store any
leftover pie in freezer.
