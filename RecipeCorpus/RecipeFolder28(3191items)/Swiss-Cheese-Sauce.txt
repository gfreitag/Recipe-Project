Title: Swiss Cheese Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
-Dash of ground nutmeg
1    L/2 Cups milk
1/3 c  Dry white wine
1/2 c  Shredded Swiss cheese 6
-ounces)
3 tb Margarine or. butter
3 tb Gold Medal all-purpose
-flour
1/4 ts Salt
-Dash of pepper
Heat margarine in I l/2-quart saucepan until melted. Stir in flour,
salt, pepper and nutmeg until blended. Cook over low heat, stirring
constantly, until smooth and bubbly; remove from heat. Stir in milk
and wine. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir I
minute; remove from heat. Stir in cheese until melted.
