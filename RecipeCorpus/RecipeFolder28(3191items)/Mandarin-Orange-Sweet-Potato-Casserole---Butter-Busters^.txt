Title: Mandarin Orange Sweet Potato Casserole - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 12 ----------
2 t  Grated orange peel
2 ea Tb liquid Butter Buds
1/2 c  Grape-Nuts cereal
2 c  (8oz) mandarin oranges,
-drained
3 lb (to4) sweet potatoes
1 c  Packed brown sugar*
1 1/2 ea Tb cornstarch
1/4 t  Lite salt, optional
1 1/2 t  Cinnamon
1 c  Apricot nectar
Boil potatoes and set aside. combine sugar, cornstarch, lite salt and
cinnamon in a saucepan. Add apricot nectar and orange rind; bring to a
boil, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Stir in Butter Buds. Pour
sauce over the sliced, peeled sweet potatoes and mandarin orange
sections that have been placed in a 9 x 13" casserole that has been
sprayed with a nonstick cooking spray. Sprinkle Grape-Nuts on top and
bake at 350F. 25 minutes. Per sugar serving: 161 cal., 2g fat (1%),
0mg chol., 2g fiber, 2g pro., 40g carb., 65mg sod. Per S&L serving:
128 cal., 32g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5
