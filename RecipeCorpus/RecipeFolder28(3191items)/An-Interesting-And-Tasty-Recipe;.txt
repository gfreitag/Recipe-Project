Title: An Interesting And Tasty Recipe;
Categories: Potato
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Of All-Purpose Potatoes
1/3 c  Olive Oil Or Vegetable Oil
1    Envelope Of Lipton Onion
-Recipe Soup Mix
Preheat Oven To 450 Degrees

Cut Potatoes Into 1/2 Inch Chunks

Place All Ingredients Into Plastic Bag Close, Seal And Shake Bag To
Mix Well And Coat Potatoes

Place Contents Of Bag Into Shallow Pan Bake 40 Minutes, Stirring
Occasionally,  Or Until Potatoes Are Tender And Brown From the files
of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
