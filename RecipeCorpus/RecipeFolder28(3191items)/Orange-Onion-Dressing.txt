Title: Orange-Onion Dressing
Categories: Fruit, Vegetables, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 ts Grated onion
1 tb Honey or sugar
Shake all ingredients in tightly covered container. ABOUT 1 CUP
DRESSING; 70  CALORIES PER TABLESPOON.
