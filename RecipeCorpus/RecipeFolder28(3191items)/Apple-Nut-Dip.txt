Title: Apple Nut Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 1/4 cups
8 oz Neufchatel cheese; softened
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon;
2 tb Brown sugar;
1    Apple; seeded cored shredded
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
1 c  Nonfat light sour cream;
-=OR=-
1 c  Regular sour cream
8 oz Light cream cheese; fat-free
8 oz Regular cream cheese -=OR=-
In a medium bowl, beat together sour cream, cinnamon and brown sugar.
Cover and chill for at least 1 hour.  Stir shredded apple and nuts
into the cheese mixture just before serving.  Serve with bagels.
