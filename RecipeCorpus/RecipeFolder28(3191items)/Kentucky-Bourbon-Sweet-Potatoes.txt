Title: Kentucky Bourbon Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Bourbon
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Margarine
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Vanilla
4 c  Sweet potatoes; cooked
OR
2 cn Sweet potatoes, 16 oz
-- well drained
1 c  Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Margarine
3/4 c  Pecans; chopped
3/4 c  Brown sugar
Recipe by: Come and Get It! by Jr. Welfare League of Talladega, Al

Mash sweet potatoes. Add sugar, bourbon, eggs, margarine, nutmeg, and
vanilla. Beat until well blended. Turn into a 2 qt shallow baking
dish. Topping: In a saucepan, stir margarine, nuts, and brown sugar
together. When margaine is melted, spoon on top of potatoes. Bake
uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 mins.

YIELD: 6 servings.

Originally to book submitted by Hilda Fannin.
