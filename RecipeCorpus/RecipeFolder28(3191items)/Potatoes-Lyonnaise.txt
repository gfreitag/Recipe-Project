Title: Potatoes Lyonnaise
Categories: Potato
Yield: 1 servings
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Paprika
3    Fresh Idaho potatoes, cut
-into 1-inch pieces
2 md Onions, sliced
2 tb Butter or margarine
In 3-quart microwave-safe casserole combine all ingredients. Cover
loosely with plastic wrap; cook on High 15 to 20 minutes or until
potatoes and onions are tender, stirring several times. Let stand 5
minutes. Makes 6 servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
