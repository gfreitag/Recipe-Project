Title: Sweet and Sour Vegetables
Categories: Diabetic, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
4 ts Vinegar;
2 ts Soysauce;
Salt and pepper; to taste
1 ts Brown sugar;
2 ts Oil;
2    Onions;
2    Carrots; thinly sliced
2    Stacks celery; diced
1    Green pepper; chopped
(1).  Heat the oil in a saucepan and saute the onions until
transparent. (2).  Add the carrots, celery and green peppers and
saute for five minutes over a low heat.  Cover and cook for ten
minutes. (3). Add the vinegar, soy sauce, salt and pepper to taste
and sugar. Mix well.
