Title: Clove Brandy
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 ts EACH whole cloves and whole
-coriander; slightly bruised
1/2    Bottle Spanish brandy
1 oz Sugar
Put the sugar, cloves and coriander in the brandy bottle.  Cap firmly,
shake well and leave in a warm place for a few days to dissolve the
sugar. Infuse for a month, then strain through double muslin and
rebottle.

This can be drunk on its own or used for flavoring dessert sauces.
