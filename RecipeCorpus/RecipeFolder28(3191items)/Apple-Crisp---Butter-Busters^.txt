Yield: 9 ----------

Heat oven to 275F> Mix together apple pie filling, water and
granulated sugar in a bowl. In another bowl, combine all dry
ingredients. Fold the two together and spoon into a square baking
dish that has been sprayyed with Pam. Bake 30-40 minutes until set
and golden brown ontop. This is great served warm with Dream Whip on
top or frozen nonfat yogurt. As a variation you can substitute
cherry, blueberry, strawberry or raspberry pie filling. Per sugar
serving: 219 cal., 0.5g fat (2%), 0mg chol., 2g fiber, 2g pro., 53g
carb., 126 mg sod. Per S&L serving: 194 cal., 45g carb. Butter
