Choose a salad- Crunchy Tuna, Tennessee Chicken, Chunky Egg, Sea-Food
Supreme, etc. Serve in or on one of these: Frankfurter, Crisp Hard, or
Finger Roll. Hollow out; fill. If you wish, sprinkle with cheese;
toast in broiler. Hamburger Bun or Frankfurter Roll. Split; toast.
Serve topped with filling; or spread filling between halves.
Cream-Puff Shells. Split and fill. If small, serve two or three, each
with different filling.
