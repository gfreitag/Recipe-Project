Title: Apple Cake Pudding
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
Water and let stand
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Chopped pecans
1/2 c  Margarine
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 c  Flour
2 c  Chopped apples
1 ts Soda, in small amount of
Sift dry ingredients.  Cream butter, eggs, sugar, apples, pecans and
soda water.  Mix well.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  Serve plain
or with Cool Whip topping. Randy Rigg
