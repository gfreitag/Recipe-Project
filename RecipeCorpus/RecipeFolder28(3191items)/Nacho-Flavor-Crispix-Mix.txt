Title: Nacho Flavor Crispix Mix
Categories: Snacks, Nuts/grains
Yield: 10 cups
1 c  Corn chips
1 c  Cheese puff balls
3 T  Oil
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
3 T  Dry cheese sauce mix
1 T  Dry taco seasoning mix
8 c  Crispix cereal
Combine parmesan cheese, cheese sauce mix and taco seasoning mix.  Set
aside.  In 2 gallon bag, combine crispix, corn chips and cheese
puffs. Pour oil over cereal mixture, tossing gently to coat.  Add
parmesan cheese mixture, tossing gently until cereal mixture is
thoroughly coated. Store in airtight container.  Per 1/2 cup serving:
100 calories and 4 grams fat. Source:  Kelloggs ad
