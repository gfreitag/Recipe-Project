Title: 697909 Low-Fat Lasagna  (Turkey)
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Poultry, Pasta
Yield: 10 servings
1 t  Fennel seed, crushed
1/8 t  Ground red pepper
8 oz Lasagna noodles
1    Beaten egg
2 c  Low-fat cottage cheese
3/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 T  Dried parsley flakes
1/2 t  Pepper
8 oz Mozzarella cheese
1 lb Ground lean turkey
1 c  Chopped onion
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1    (16 oz.) can tomatoes, cut
-up
1    (8 oz.) can tomato sauce
1    (6 oz.) can tomato paste
2 t  Dried basil, crushed
1 t  Dried oregano, crushed
In a large skillet, cook meat, onion, and garlic until meat is brown
and onion is tender.  Drain off fat.  Stir in the next 7 ingredients.
Cover and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring mixture often. Meanwhile,
cook noodles according to package directions, except omit oil. Drain.
Stir together egg, cottage cheese, 1/2 cup of the Parmesan cheese,
parsley, and pepper. In a 9x13x2 inch baking dish, layer half of the
noodles, half of the cottage cheese mixture, half of the Mozzarella,
and half of the meat sauce. Repeat layers; sprinkle with remaining
Parmesan cheese. Bake in a 375 degree oven for 30 to 35 minutes or
until heated through. Let stand for 10 minutes. Makes 10 servings.
