Title: BINGO BARS
Categories: Candies, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 20 servings
1 1/4 c  Salad oil
1 1/2 ts Soda
1 ea Applesauce,apricots,carrots*
1 c  Pecans, chopped
2 c  Powdered sugar
4 tb Stick margarine, melted
3 ea Eggs
2 c  Flour
3 ea (4 3/4oz)jars baby food *
2 ts Cinnamon
3 oz Cream cheese, softened
2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
Beat eggs, gradually add sugar & mix well. Add salad oil & blend.
Sift dry ingredients & add to egg mixture. Blend in baby food & add
nuts. Bake in a 10- by 15-inch greased & floured jelly roll pan for
20 minutes at 350 degrees. FROSTING Mix & spread on bars: cream
cheese, powdered sugar, margarine & vanilla.
