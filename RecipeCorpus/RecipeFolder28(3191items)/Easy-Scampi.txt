Title: Easy Scampi
Categories: Seafood, Italian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
-deveined
1/4 c  Dry white wine
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
Salt, to taste
Pepper, to taste
Warm rice
3/4 c  (1 1/2 sticks) unsalted
-butter
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
4    Medium cloves garlic,crushed
4    Sprigs parsley, minced
1 lb Medium size uncooked shrimp
Melt butter in medium skillet over low heat. Add onion, garlic, and
parsley and saute till golden; about 10 minutes. Add shrimp and stir
just till pink. Remove shrimp and place in ovenproof dish. cover
tightly and keep warm. Add wine and lemon juice to skillet and simmer
about 2 to 3 minutes. season to taste with salt and pepper and pour
over the shrimp.

Serve over warm rice.
