Title: Mock Sukiyaki
Categories: Beef, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
-mushrooms.
1/2 c  Sliced green onions,1 inch
-pieces.
1 cn Beef broth(or Bullion).
1 T  Soy sauce.
1/4 c  Water.
2 T  Cornstarch.
4 c  Hot cooked rice.
1 lb Sliced round steak.
2 T  Salad oil.
1 1/2 c  Sliced celery.
1 md Green pepper,sliced in
-rings.
1 lg Onion,thinly sliced in
-rings.
1 1/2 c  Sliced fresh,or 1(6oz)can
Brown meat in salad oil,add vegetables,beef broth,and soy sauce.
Cover,cook over low heat 10min.or until vegetables are tender. Stir
often. Combine water and cornstarch,add to meat mixture. Stir until
thickened. Serve over hot rice. Serves:4-6 "Rural Montana"by RaNae
Bramlett,Columbia Falls,Mt.
