Title: 631004 Grilled Pork Tenderloin
Categories: Pork
Yield: 4 servings
-salad
-dressing
2    (2 lb.) pork tenderloins
1    (8 oz.) bottle Zesty Italian
Preparation : In large covered bowl pour salad dressing over
tenderloins and marinate for 8 to 12 hours in refrigerator, turning
often. Remove from dressing and place on grill.  Cook until meat
thermometer reaches 160 to 170 degrees.  Slice and serve.
