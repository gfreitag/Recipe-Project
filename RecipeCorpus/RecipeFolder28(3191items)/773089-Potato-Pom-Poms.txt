Title: 773089 Potato Pom-Poms
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 48 servings
1 t  Almond extract
-Dash of salt
-Chocolate bark
1 c  Mashed potatoes
2 c  Shredded coconut
4 c  Powdered sugar
Mix mashed potatoes and coconut.  Blend in powdered sugar, 1 cup at a
time. Add almond extract and salt.  Chill overnight.  Use teaspoon to
form small balls, dip in melted chocolate bark.  Makes about 4 dozen.
