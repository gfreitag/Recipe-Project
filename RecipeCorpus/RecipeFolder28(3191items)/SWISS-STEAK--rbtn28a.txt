Title: SWISS STEAK  rbtn28a
Categories: Meats
Yield: 2 servings
Peeled & sliced
1 x  Medium bell pepper
Seeded & sliced
2 c  V-8 or tomatoe juice
1 x  Swiss steak cut into
Serving pieces
1/2 c  Flour
1 x  Medium onion
Place onions and bell peppers in bottom of crockpot. Pound flour in
the steak (seasoned with salt and pepper) with a meat mallet until
meat is tender. Brown in hot oil, and place in crock pot over the
onions and peppers. Cover with the V-8 juice or tomato juice. Set on
low and cook until done (it is OK if it cooks all day). Serve over
cooked rice...
