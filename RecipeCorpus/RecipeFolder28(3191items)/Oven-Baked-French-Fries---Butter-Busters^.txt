Title: Oven Baked French Fries - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 4 ----------
l  Salt to taste
Pam spray
4 ea Potatoes
1/2 c  Liquid Butter Buds
Cut potatoes into strips. Arrange on a Teflon baking sheet, after
dipping or brushing with Butter Buds. You can also try other
seasonings such as garlic powder, onion powder or chile powder. bake
at 425F. 15-20 minutes or until golden brown. Turn; bake 15-20
minutes more or until tender. to shorten baking time, parboil
potatoes 15 minutes; slice and bake as directed. Per serving: 83
cal., 0.2g fat (2%), omg chol., 2g fiber, 2g pro., 18g carb., 154mf
