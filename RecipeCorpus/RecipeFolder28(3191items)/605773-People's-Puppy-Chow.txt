Title: 605773 People's Puppy Chow
Categories: Snacks, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Oleo
1    Box Crispix
6 oz Milk chocolate chips
6 oz Semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 c  Peanut butter
Melt above ingredients and pour over 1 box Crispix.  Toss until
coated. Put coated cereal and 2 cups powdered sugar in a brown paper
bag and shake until coated.  Cool.  Store in an airtight container.
