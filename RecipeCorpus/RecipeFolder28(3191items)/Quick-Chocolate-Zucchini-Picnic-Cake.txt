Title: Quick Chocolate Zucchini Picnic Cake
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 10 servings
3 c  Zucchini; packed shredded
1 tb Cinnamon
1 tb Orange zest; grated
1/2 c  Coconut; flaked or shredded
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
Powdered sugar
1 pk Devil's food (no pudding)
-- cake mix; (18.25 oz)
1 pk (4 oz) instant chocolate/
-- chocolate fudge pudding
-- mix
4    Eggs
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:55 1. Preheat oven to 350 F. In a large bowl,
beat cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, oil, zucchini, cinnamon, and orange
zest with an electric mixer on medium, 3 to 4 mins, until fluffy and
thoroughly blended. Stir in coconut and walnuts. Turn into a greased
and floured 9 x 13-inch baking pan.

2. Bake 40 to 45 mins, or until a cake tester inserted in center
comes out clean. Let cake cool in pan. Sprinkle powdered sugar over
top before serving.
