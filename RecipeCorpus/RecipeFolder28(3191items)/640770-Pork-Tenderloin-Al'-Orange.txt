Title: 640770 Pork Tenderloin Al' Orange
Categories: Pork
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Lemon juice (optional)
1 t  Prepared horseradish
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1    (10 oz.) can mandarin
-orange slices, drained
1 lb Pork tenderloin
1 t  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Orange marmalade
1 T  Cornstarch
Slice tenderloin crosswise into eight pieces.  Using meat mallet,
flatten each piece slightly.  Heat oil in large non-stick skillet
over medium-high heat.  Cook pork slices quickly about 1 minute per
side, until browned. In a small bowl, thoroughly mix remaining
ingredients, except mandarin oranges.  Add to skillet and cook over
medium-high heat, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens; simmer
an additional 3 to 4 minutes. Transfer to serving platter and garnish
with mandarin orange slices. Makes 4 servings.
