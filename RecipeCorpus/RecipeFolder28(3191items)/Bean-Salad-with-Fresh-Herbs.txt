Title: Bean Salad with Fresh Herbs
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
-marjoram or basil
2 T  Olive oil
2 t  Dijon mustard
1/2    Lemon
Salt and pepper to taste
1 lb Snap beans; green and/or
-yellow
2    Green onions; finely chopped
2 T  Or more of fresh savory,
-sage, tarragon, thyme,
Trim beans and cut into 2" pieces.  Place in microwave bowl and add
1/4 cup water.  Cover and microwave on HIGH about 10 minutes or until
beans are crisp tender.  Drain and toss immediately with onion,
herbs, oil, mustard, juice of lemon, salt and pepper.  If cooling to
room temperature, add lemon juice right before serving.  Each serving
88 calories and 6 grams fat. Source: Grocery ad
