Title: 564393 Burke's Greek Spaghetti
Categories: Greek, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
-Med. can mushrooms
1 lg Stewed tomatoes
8 oz Grated Old English cheese
1 lb Spaghetti, cooked & drained
1 lg Green pepper, chopped
1 lg Onion, chopped
1    Stick butter
-Sm. can ripe olives
Saute peppers and onions in melted stick of butter.  Add olives,
mushrooms and stewed tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes.  Layer
spaghetti, sauce and cheese; repeat layers.  Top with mushrooms soup
diluted with 1/2 can of milk.  Bake for 35--45 minutes at 350 degrees
until bubbly. '50 Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-03-95
