Title: Veggie-Bean Sloppy Joes
Categories: Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Barbeque sauce
x  Buns, rolls, corn bread -
What ever you'd like to put
Underneath
1 md Onion chopped
1 md Green pepper chopped
3 c  Cooked brown rice
2    16 oz. cans Mexican style
Pinto beans
Saute onions and green peppers until tender crisp.  Add rice, beans,
and barbeque sauce.  Simmer 10-15 minutes until heated through.
Serve over your choice of rolls/bread.
