Title: Fruit-Filled Acorn Squash
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
16 oz Can whole-berry
-cranberry sauce
3 md Acorn squash (1 lb ea)
1 tb Butter
1 1/2 c  Granny smith apples
Place squash in a 13x9-inch baking dish. Bake at 400øF for 45 minutes
or until tender. Or pierce squash with a fork and arrange on paper
towels in microwave oven. Microwave on high 9-10 minutes or until
tender.

Melt butter in medium nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add apple;
saut for 5 minutes. Stir in cranberry sauce; cook for 2 minutes or
until apple is tender, stirring occasionally.

Cut squash in half lengthwise and discard seeds and membrane. Spoon
1/3 cup of fruit filling into each squash half.
