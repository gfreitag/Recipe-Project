Title: Garlic Chicken Drummies
Categories: Chicken
Yield: 1 servings
1    Clove garlic, finely
-chopped
8    Chicken drumsticks (about
5 1/2    Pounds)
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Chopped fresh or 1 teaspoon
-dried oregano leaves
Mix all ingredients except chicken drumsticks. Cover and grill
chicken 5 to 6 inches from medium coals 15 to 20 minutes; turn
chicken. Cover and grill 20 to 40 minutes longer, turning and
brushing frequently with garlic mixture, until done.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
