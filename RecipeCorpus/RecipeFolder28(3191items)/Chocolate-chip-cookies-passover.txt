Title: Chocolate chip cookies passover
Categories: Jewish, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chopped nuts
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Mothers margarine melted
1 c  Matzo meal
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Farfel
6 oz Choc. chips for passover
Chocolate chip cookies

Combine matzo meal, sugar, farfel, chocolate chips, nuts. Beat eggs
with melted margarine, pour over dry ingredients and mix well. Drop
by teaspoon onto cookie sheet 1" apart. Bake at 350 for 20 min.
