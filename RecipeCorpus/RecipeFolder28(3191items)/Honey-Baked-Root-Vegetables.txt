Title: Honey-Baked Root Vegetables
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
-cut in 4x«-inch sticks
1 sm Rutabaga (1 lb)
-cut in 4x«-inch sticks
Cooking spray
2 tb Honey
1 tb Melted butter
1/4 ts Salt
1 lb Carrots
Combine honey, butter and salt in a large bowl; stir well. Add carrot
and rutabaga; toss to coat vegetables.

Arrange vegetables in a single layer in a 15x10-inch jelly roll pan
coated with cooking spray. Bake at 375øF for 1 hour or until
vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally.
