Title: Easy Ham Glaze  RBTN28A
Categories: Meats
Yield: 12 servings
3 tb Prepared mustard
1 x  Dash ground cloves
1 c  Karo corn syrup light/dark
1/2 c  Brown sugar "packed"
In 2-qt saucepan combine all ingredients. Stirring constantly, bring
to boil over medium heat and boil 5 min. Brush frequently on ham
during last 30 min. of baking. Makes about 1 cup. MICROWAVE
DIRECTIONS: In 1 1/2-qt. microproof bowl stir all ingredients.
Microwave at high (100%) 6 min. Glaze ham as above.
