Title: 790702 Chicken Marinara
Categories: Italian, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Grated Parmesan
-cheese
3    To 3 1/2 lb chicken, cut up
1/2 c  Seasoned flour
1/2 c  Melted butter or oleo
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/2 t  Basil
1/4 t  Pepper
1 T  Parsley
15 oz Tomato sauce
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2    Garlic cloves, crushed
2 T  Oil (olive oil preferred)
1 t  Salt
1 t  Oregano
Dredge chicken in seasoned flour.  Place in 13x9x2-inch pan in which
butter has been melted.  Bake in very hot oven (450 degrees) 30-40
minutes. Meanwhile, saute onion and garlic in oil in small pan until
soft but not brown.  Stir tomato sauce and spice into sauce mixture.
Boil gently, uncovered, 15 minutes.  Remove chicken from oven; reduce
heat to moderate (350 degrees).   Turn chicken over and top with
sauce and 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese.  Return to oven; bake 30 minutes
or until chicken is tender. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
03-03-95
