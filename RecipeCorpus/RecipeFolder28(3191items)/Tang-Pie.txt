Title: Tang Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Desserts
Yield: 1 pie
1    10-inch graham cracker crust
Mandarin oranges and banana
-slices (optional)
Additional thawed Cool Whip;
(optional)
1    14-oz can sweetened
-condensed milk
1    8-oz carton sour cream
1/4 c  Tang instant breakfast drink
1    9-oz carton Cool Whip;
-thawed
Combine milk and sour cream; mix well.  Stir in Tang (this will
congeal rather quickly).  Fold in Cool Whip.  Add fruit, if desired.
Pour into graham cracker crust.  Refrigerate overnight.  Garnish with
additional Cool Whip, if desired.
