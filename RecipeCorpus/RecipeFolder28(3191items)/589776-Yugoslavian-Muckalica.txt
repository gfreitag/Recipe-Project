Title: 589776 Yugoslavian Muckalica
Categories: East europe, Main dish, Pork
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Paprika
1/4 t  Coarsely ground black
-pepper
1/4 t  Crushed red pepper
1 lg Green pepper, cut into 1/4"
-x 1 1/2" strips
2 oz Feta cheese, grated
1 1/2 lb Boneless pork chops, cut
-into 1/2" x 1/2" strips
1/2 c  Water
3 md Sized onions, sliced
2 md Tomatoes, diced into 3/4"
-cubes (or 1 pt., 16 oz.,
-canned tomatoes)
1 1/2 t  Salt
Spices can be mixed together in a cup while the pork cooks.  Heat 2
tablespoons cooking oil in large skillet.  Add pork strips, cook over
medium heat until browned, about 15 minutes.  Stir frequently. Add
water, onions, tomatoes, salt, paprika, black pepper and red pepper.
Cover and simmer 30 minutes.  Stir occasionally.  Add green pepper,
replace cover, simmer 8 minutes.  Stir occasionally.  Serve over
rice, pasta of choice or Ramen noodles.  Top each serving with feta
cheese (optional). Serves 4. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
03-05-95
