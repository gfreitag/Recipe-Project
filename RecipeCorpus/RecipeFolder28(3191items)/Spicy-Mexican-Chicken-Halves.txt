Title: Spicy Mexican Chicken Halves
Categories: Chicken, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Red pepper sauce
2    Broiler fryer chickens (2
-to 2 1/2 pounds each), cut
-in half
8 oz Tomato sauce
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Chili powder
Mix all ingredients except chicken. Grill chicken halves, bone sides
down, 5 inches from medium coals 20 to 30 minutes; turn chicken.
Grill 30 to 40 minutes longer, turning and brushing Frequently with
tomato mixture, until done.

4 servings.
