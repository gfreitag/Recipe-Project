Title: Banker's Pineapple Cheese Ball
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 cups
1 t  Salt
2 c  Pecans; chopped
Assorted crackers
2    8 oz cream cheese; softened
8 1/2 oz Crushed pineapple; drained
1/4 c  Green pepper; finely chopped
2 T  Onion; chopped
In medium bowl combine the cream cheese, pineapple, green pepper,
onion, salt and 1 cup of pecans.  Mix well.  Refrigerate until firm.
Roll the cheese ball in the remaining pecans before serving.  Source:
Beverly Hillbillies Cookbook by Jim Clark
