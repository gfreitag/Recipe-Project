Title: Brownie Pizza
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Light corn syrup
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
1 c  Walnuts; chopped
1    Butter
3/4 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
2 oz Unsweetened chocolate square
-- cut-up
2    Eggs
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:45 1. Preheat oven to 350 F. In a 2-qt glassbowl,
combine butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and unsweetened
chocolate. Heat in microwave on High 1 1/2 to 2 mins, or until butter
and chocolate are melted and smooth when stirred. Let cool 5 mins.

2. With a whisk or fork, beat in eggs, corn syrup, vanilla until well
blended. Add flour and baking powder; mix well. Stir in 1/2 cup
chocolate chips.

3. Line a 12-inch pizza pan with a 1/2-inch-high rim with foil.
Grease foil well. Spread evenly over pan. Sprinkle nuts and remaining
1/2 cup chocolate chips over batter. Bake 25 to 28 mins, or until
set. Do not overbake.

4. Let cool, then refrigerate until chilled. Cut pizza into 12 to 14
wedges. Peel off foil before serving.
