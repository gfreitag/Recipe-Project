Title: Eggless "Egg" Sandwich Spread
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks, Dips/spread
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Parsley, chopped
1 ts Nutritional yeast
1/2 ts Mustard powder
Salt, as required
1/2 lb Tofu
1/4 ts Turmeric
1 ea Celery stalk
1 sm Onion
1 tb Vegan mayonnaise
Mash tofu.  Finely chop the onion & celery (use a food processor if
you have one handy).  Combine all ingredients & chill well.

Leah Leneman, "365 Plus One Vegan Recipes"
