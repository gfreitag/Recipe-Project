Title: Breakfast-In-A-Dish
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
6 sl Schwan's American Processed
-Cheese
1/2 c  Celery, diced
1/2 c  Onion, diced
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ct Elite Egg egg Substitute
8 sl Haugin's Farm Brand Thick
-Sliced Bacon, crumbled*
6    Frozen Gourmet's Choice
-Hash Brown Patties
2 c  Water
1 tb Chicken flavored Bouillon
-Granules
1/4 c  Margarine
Preheat oven to 350Ã¸F.  Prepare bacon in skillet over medium heat;
drain. In large saucepan or stock pot combine hash brown patties,
bouillon granules and water.  Cover and place over medium-low heat
until patties are thawed and crumbled.  Remove from heat.  Add bacon,
margarine, cheese, celery, onion, salt and pepper.  Mix ingredients
together until margarine and cheese have melted.  Gradually add egg
substitute to mixture, stirring constantly.  Pour mixture into a
2-qt. casserole dish. Bake 1 hour or until mixture is firmly set in
center.

Makes 6-8 servings.

NOTE: *6 Haugin's Farm Brand Whole Hog Country Sausage Patties,
cooked, drained and crumbled may be substituted for the bacon.
