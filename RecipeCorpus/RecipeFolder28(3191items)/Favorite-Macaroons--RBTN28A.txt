Title: Favorite Macaroons  RBTN28A
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 5 dozen
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Almond extract
1 pk 14oz flake coconut
1 cn 14oz sweetened condense milk
Combine all ingredients, mixing well. Drop from teaspoon 1 inch apart
on a well-greased cookie sheet. Bake in preheated 350-degree oven for
10-12 minutes or until very lightly golden. Remove at once from
baking sheet using a moistened spatula. ;;
