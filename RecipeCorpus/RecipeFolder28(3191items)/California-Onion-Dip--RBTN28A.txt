Title: California Onion Dip  RBTN28A
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 10 servings
1 ea 16 oz. sour cream
1 pk Campbells dry onion soup mix
In medium bowl, blend 1 pouch Campbell's dry Onion Soup and Recipe
Mix and 1 container (16 oz.) sour cream. Cover; refrigerate at least
2 hr. Serve with chips or fresh vegetables for dipping. Makes about 2
cups. VARIATION: Prepare as above with 1/2 Yogurt and 1/2 sour cream.
