Title: Pimento Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Butter
8 oz Mushrooms and juice
2 c  Cooked chicken, diced
3 tb Cream
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup; undi
5 oz Pimento cheese
3    Hard cooked eggs; chopped
Salt
Pepper
Blend soup and cheese and heat; add eggs salt, pepper and butter.
Then add mushrooms with juice and chicken. Bo not boil. Add cream
shortly before serving. Serve over chinese noodles, in pastry shells
or anything you prefer.
