Title: Taco Casserole  RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 10 servings
2 cn Rotel Tom.& Green Chiles
1 cn Milnot
1 cn Creamed chicken soup
1 x  Grated cheese to cover top
1 pk Lg.pk.Taco flav.Doritos
1 ea Crushed
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 ea Onion chopped
Put crushed taco chips in a well buttered large oven usable casserole
dish. Brown meat and onions. Add next 3 ingredients and heat. Pour
meat over tacos and top with grated cheese. Cover with foil and bake
45 minutes at 350F. Remove foil the last 10 minutes. Makes large
casserole.
