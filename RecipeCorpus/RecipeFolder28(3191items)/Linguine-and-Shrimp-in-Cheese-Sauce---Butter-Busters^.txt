Title: Linguine and Shrimp in Cheese Sauce - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Pk (q0oz) frozen peas,
-thawed
1/2 t  Lite salt, optional
1/8 t  Nutmeg
2 ds (or 3) Taasco sauce
1/2 c  Nonfat mozzarella cheese,
-grated
12 oz Linguine, cooked
1 t  Crushed garlic
1/4 t  Crushed red pepper flakes
1 ea Tb liquid Butter Buds or
-fatfree chicken broth
3/4 c  Nonfat ricotta cheese
1/2 c  Skim milk
8 oz Small cooked frozen shrimp,
-thawed
Saute garlic and pepper flakes in Butter Buds 1-2 minutes. Add milk,
ricotta, shrimp and peas. Cook until smooth. Add nutmeg and Tabasco.
Add salt if desired. Cover and cook on low 4-5 minutes. Stir in
cheese. Pour sauce over cooked linguine, tossing to coat. Per
serving: 144 cal., 0.7g fat (4%), 56mg chol., 2g fiber, 14g pro., 19g
carb., 171mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5
