Title: Mustard Horseradish Butter for Fish
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
5 tb Butter; room temperature
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tb Dijon-style mustard
2 tb Prepared horseradish
BLEND ALL INGREDIENTS TOGETHER, scrape into a serving bowl and serve
at room temperature for dolloping over fish.
