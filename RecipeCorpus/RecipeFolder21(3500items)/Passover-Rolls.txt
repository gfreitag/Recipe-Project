Title: Passover Rolls
Categories: Jewish, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Matzo meal
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
3/4 c  Water
3    Eggs
Bring oil and water to a boil.  Quickly add dry ingredients and mix
until all are evenly dampened.  Remove from heat and let cool a bit,
at least 1/2 half hr.  Beat eggs and add to matzo meal mixture,
beating throughly. Drop by tablespoonsful onto greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 400 F. for 45 min. to 1 hr. or until nicely puffed and
browned.
