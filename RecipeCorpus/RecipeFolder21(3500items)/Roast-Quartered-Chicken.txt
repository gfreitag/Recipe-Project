Title: Roast Quartered Chicken
Categories: Jewish, Main dish, Poultry, Appetizer, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1    Roasting chicken, quartered
1    Large diced onion
Garlic, minced
Salt, pepper, paprika
Season each quarter well and place in roasting pan over diced onions
and garlic.  Add about 1/2 cup of water and cover.  Roast at 350 F.
until almost tender.  Remove cover, lower heat to 325 F. and bake
uncovered, turning once until each piece is golden brown.
