Title: Crumb Topping for Fruit Buckle
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 c  Butter
Contributed to the echo by: Pat Knox Crumb Topping Mix all ingredients
together of topping with fork until fine crumbles. Put on top of
fruit and bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
