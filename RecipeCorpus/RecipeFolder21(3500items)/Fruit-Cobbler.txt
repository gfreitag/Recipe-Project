Title: Fruit Cobbler
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
3/4    Stick butter
3/4 c  Flour
3/4 c  Milk
1 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
2 c  Sweetened fruit
Contributed to the echo by: Pat Knox Fruit Cobbler Melt butter in deep
casserole dish. Mix sugar, flour, baking powder, and milk together;
pour over melted butter. Don't stir. On top, pour 2 cups sweetened
fruit. Bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour at 325 degrees.
