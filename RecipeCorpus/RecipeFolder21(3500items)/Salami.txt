Title: Salami
Categories: Meat
Yield: 1 servings
5 lb Hamburger meat
5 ts Hickory smoked salt
2 1/2 ts Whole mustard seed
1/2 ts Garlic salt
2 1/2 tb Morton's Tender Quick salt>>
NO SUBSTITUTIONS==
2 1/2 tb Whole peppercorns
Mix all ingreds. well.  Refrigerate for 24 hrs; mix 5 times during
the 24 hrs.  Divide into 3 equal parts.  Shape into rolls.  Place on
broiler rack with drip pan.  Bake at 150F. for 8 hrs.  Will keep 3-4
weeks in 'fridge, or freeze for longer storage. Courtesy Telephone
