Title: Quick Apple Bread
Categories: Bread, Cajun/creol, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
2 tb Milk
1 c  Chopped apples, unpeeled
1/4 c  Chopped nuts
2 c  Sifted flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Cinnamon
Cream shortening, sugar, salt, vanilla until light and fluffy.
Add eggs and beat well.  Add milk, apples and nuts, beat.  Stir in
flour, baking powder, cinnamon.  Blend.  Pour into greased loaf pan.
Bake at 350 F. for about 50 min. Courtesy Telephone Pioneers
