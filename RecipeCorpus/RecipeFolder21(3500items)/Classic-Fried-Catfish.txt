Title: Classic Fried Catfish
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
-Waldine Van Geffen VGHC42A
3/4 c  Yellow Cornmeal
1/4 c  Flour
2 ts Salt
1 ts Cayenne Pepper
1/4 ts Garlic Powder
4    Farm-raised Catfish Fillets
-or whole catfish
x  Vegetable Oil
Combine cornmeal, flour, salt, cayenne and garlic powder. Coat
catfish with mixture, shaking off excess. Add 1" layer of oil to a
large skillet. Heat to 350~. Add catfish in single layer and fry
until golden brown, about 5 to 6 minutes depending on size. Remove
and drain on paper towels. Source: The Catfish Institute. (wrv)
