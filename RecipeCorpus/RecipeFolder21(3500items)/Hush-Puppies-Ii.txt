Title: Hush Puppies Ii
Categories: Bread, Main dish, Cajun/creol
Yield: 20 servings
2 2/3 c  Cold water
1 lg Onion, chopped fine
1 1/2 c  Yellow corn meal
1 ts Baking powder
3 tb Butter or oleo
1/4 c  Parsley flakes
2 2/3 tb Plain flour
1 ts Salt
Add butter to water and bring to a boil.  Add onion and parsley
flakes to boiling water.  Sift together corn meal, flour, baking
powder and salt.
Stir this into the boiling water mixture and cook 5 minutes.  Stir
well. Spread on large cookie sheet and cool.  Roll into small balls
and freeze on a cookie sheet.  Fry in deep fat. Courtesy Telephone
