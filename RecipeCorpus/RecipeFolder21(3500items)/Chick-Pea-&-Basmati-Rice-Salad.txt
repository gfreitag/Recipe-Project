Title: Chick Pea & Basmati Rice Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Cooked chick peas
1 c  Cooked basmati rice
1/2 ea Red bell pepper, diced
1/2 ea Green bell pepper, diced
1/2 ea Yellow bell pepper, diced
4 ea Scallions, sliced
Salt to taste
--------VINAIGRETTE---------
2 ts Toasted sesame seeds
1 ts Sesame oil
1/4 ts Cumin
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Olive oil
+++++  ts Salt

Toss together chick peas, rice, bell peppers, scallions in a large
bowl. Whisk together all the ingredients for the vinaigrette.  toss
with salad & season to taste.

"The Hamilton Spectator" August, 1993
