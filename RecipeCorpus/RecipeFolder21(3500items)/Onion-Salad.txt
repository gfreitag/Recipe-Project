Title: Onion Salad
Categories: Salad, Appetizer
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Chopped sweet onions
16 oz Cooked red kidney beans
1 c  Green bell pepper, diced
1 c  Yellow bell pepper, diced
1 tb Minced jalapeno
1/2 c  White wine vinegar
2 tb Vegetable oil
3 tb Minced coriander, fresh
1 ts Chili powder
Combine onions, kidney beans & the peppers in a large shallow dish.
Toss & set aside.  Combine vinegar, cilantro & chili powder in a
small bowl; stir well.  Pour over vegetables & toss to coat.  Cover &
refrigerate for at least 8 hours, or overnight, stirring
occasionally.  Toss before serving.

"The Hamilton Spectator" May 26, 1993
