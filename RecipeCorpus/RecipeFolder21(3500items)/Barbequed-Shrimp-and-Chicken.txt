Title: Barbequed Shrimp and Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Bbq/grill, Chicken, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Minced Garlic
1/2 ts Ground Cumin
1/2 lb Medium Shrimp
1/2 ts Onion Powder
1/4 c  Melted Butter
1 lb Boneless Chicken Breast
powder, cumin and melted butter.  Marinate shrimp and chicken while
preparing grill.  String shrimp and chicken onto separate skewers.
Cook about 5 inches above hot coals, allowing 5 minutes for shrimp
and 10 minutes for chicken.  Turn once or twice while cooking and
baste often with remaining marinade.
