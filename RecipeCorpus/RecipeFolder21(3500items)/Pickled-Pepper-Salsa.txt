Title: Pickled Pepper Salsa
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 5 servings
5 c  Tomatoes,seeded,peeled>>>
Finely chopped
1/2 c  Pickled jalapenos including>
Some pickling juice
1    Onion, finely chopped
1    Green onion,finely chopped
2 ts Cilantro, finely chopped
Chop each ingredient by hand, or separately in food processor.
Combine all the ingredients, adding salt to taste.  Allow to stand
for abt. 1 hr to allow flavors to blend.  Stir well before serving.

Yield:  Approx. 5 cups
