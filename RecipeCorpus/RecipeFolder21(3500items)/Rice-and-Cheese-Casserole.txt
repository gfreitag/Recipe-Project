Title: Rice and Cheese Casserole
Categories: Rice, Cheese/eggs, Side dish
3 green onions, chopped 1 cup low-fat
1 tsp dill weed 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan
Yield: 6 servings  2 1/2 cup cooked brown rice
cottage cheese
1/2 cup low-fat milk 1/2 tsp Dijon-style mustard Nonstick vegetable
spray Directions: Combine all but the last ingredient in a mixing
bowl. Pour into a casserole dish coated with nonstick vegetable
spray. Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
