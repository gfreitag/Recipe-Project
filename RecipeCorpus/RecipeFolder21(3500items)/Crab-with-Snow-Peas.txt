Title: Crab with Snow Peas
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 lb Fresh crab meat, diced
2    Cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 lb Fresh snow peas
1 cn (8 oz) water chestnuts (OR
1 c  Jicama, cut in 1/2" cubes)
1/4 c  White wine
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Arrowroot
1/4 c  Water
4 c  Rice, freshly cooked
Heat oil.  Add crab and simmer, stirring, 2 minutes.  Add garlic,
snow peas and water chestnuts; simmer 5 minutes.  Stir together wine,
soy sauce, arrowroot and water until arrowroot is dissolved.  Pour
into cooking crab mixture and simmer another 2 minutes.  Serve
immediately over hot rice.
