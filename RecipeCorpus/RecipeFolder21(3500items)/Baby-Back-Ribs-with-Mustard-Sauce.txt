Title: Baby Back Ribs with Mustard Sauce
Categories: Appetizer, Cajun/creol, Meat
Yield: 20 servings
10 lb Baby back pork ribs,separatd
Salt to taste
Cayenne pepper to taste
MMMMM-----------------------MUSTARD SAUCE----------------------------
1 c  Mustard-your choice
1/2 c  Honey
1 c  Dry white wine (opt)or stock
Preheat oven to 300 F.  Sprinkle salt and pepper on the ribs, the
place them in a large baking pan, and bake in oven until tenter,
about 1 hr. Drizzle with the mustard sauce.

Yield: 10-20
