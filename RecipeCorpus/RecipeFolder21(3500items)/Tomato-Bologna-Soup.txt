Title: Tomato Bologna Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Microwave elbows uncooked
1 cn (10.5-oz) condensed tomato
Soup
Hot water
3    Slices bologna cut up
In 2 quart microwave safe casserole, stir together pasta, soup, 1 1/2
soup cans hot water and bologna. Cover; microwave at high 13 minutes,
or until pasta is tender. Stir.
