Title: Nut Kolachky Filling
Categories: Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Ground walnuts
3/4 c  Sugar
3    Egg whites
2 tb Melted butter
1    Egg
Contributed to the echo by: Leti Labell Originally from: "The Best of
Boris" by the St. Mark Women's Guild of Bethesda, Md. Nut Kolachky
Filling wites, sugar and melted butter to the ground walnuts and mix
well. Makes approximately 10 dozen.
