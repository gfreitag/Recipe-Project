Title: Brunch Eggs
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
8    Slices bread,cubed
1/2 lb Cheddar cheese,cubed
1 lb Bacon,crisp fried,
Crumbled
1 cn (4 oz) mushrooms, optional
3    Eggs, beaten with:
2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dried mustard
Dash pepper
Dash paprika
In casserole dish,layer bread,cheese and bacon.Beat others and pour
over top.If using mushrooms,drain and pour over top.Bake @ 350
degrees for 50 minutes.

* Best if left in refrigerator overnight and baked in the morning.
