Title: Hearty Pasta and Red Bean Salad
Categories: Salad, Pasta, Italian
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Raw, med-size shaped pasta
2 c  Cooked/canned red beans OR
2 c  Kidney beans
1 c  Diced zucchini (@ 1 med)
1    Sm green pepper, fine chop
1    Med ripe tomato, chopped
1/3 c  Chopped green olives
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 c  Plain yogurt
1/2 ts Chili powder r more to taste
1/2 ts Ground coriander
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Dried sage
Cook the pasta al dente and rinse with cool water.  Drain the pasta
well and put it in a mixing bowl.  Add the remaining ingredients and
mix thoroughly.  Serve at room temperature or chilled.
