Title: Garlic & Chickpea Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
8 oz Chickpeas, soaked & cooked
2 ea Garlic bulbs, boiled whole
-- for 20 minutes, drained
4 oz Olive oil
2 ea Juice of lemons
3 oz Tahini
Black pepper
4 tb Fresh chopped parsley
Tomatoes to garnish
Blend the chickpeas till well pureed.  Squeeze the garlic cloves from
their skins & add to the chickpeas.  Put in the oil, lemon juice &
tahini. Blend again.  Add pepper & parsley & mix well.

Put the mixture into an oiled mould & chill for 2 hours.  Turn it
onto a plate & garnish with tomato wedges or slices.

Gail Duff, "A Book of Herbs & Spices"
