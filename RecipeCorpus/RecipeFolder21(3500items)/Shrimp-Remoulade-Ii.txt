Title: Shrimp Remoulade Ii
Categories: Appetizer, Salsa/sauce, Cajun/creol, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
6 tb Oil
1 tb Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
1/2    Heart of celery, fine chop
2 tb Vinegar
1/2 ts Creole mustard
1/2 ts White pepper
1/2    White onion, chopped finely
Chopped parsley
Mix all ingredients well and chill.  Serve on cold boiled shrimp.
