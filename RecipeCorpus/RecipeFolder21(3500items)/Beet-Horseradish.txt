Title: Beet Horseradish
Categories: Jewish, Relish
Yield: 1 servings
1    Fresh horseradish, grated
1    Fresh beet, grated
1 tb Sugar (approx)
1 pn Salt
Vinegar & water to cover
Place grated horseradish and beets in jar.  Combine vinegar diluted
with a little cold water.  Add sugar and salt and add combined
mixture to horseradish.  Cover tightly.
