Title: Vegetable Soup with Tilsit Toast
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
4    Sliced diced cooked bacon
1 lb Fresh/frozen mixed chopped
Vegetables
5 c  Beef broth
6    Slices white/whole wheat
Bread
Shredded tilsit
Chopped fresh parsley for
Garnish
Cook diced bacon in 3 quart saucepan 3-4 minutes or until crisp. Add
vegetables such as carrots, cauliflower, broccoli and peas. Add beef
broth. Cover and bring to a boil. Uncover and simmer 5-10 minutes or
until vegetables are tender. Season to taste with salt and pepper and
ladle into 6 heatproof bowls. Float 1 slice white or whole wheat
toast in each and sprinkle each slice with shredded tilsit. Broil
briefly until cheese is golden brown. Sprinkle with chopped fresh
parsley.
