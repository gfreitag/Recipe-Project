Title: Creole Tomatoes
Categories: Jewish, Vegetable, Cajun/creol
Yield: 6 servings
6    Med.size Creole tomatoes
1/2 c  Fine dry bread crumbs/flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
Shortening
Cut tomatoes crosswise into 1/2" thick slices.  Dip each slice in
combined crumbs, salt and pepper.  Fry in small amt. of shortening
until brown on both sides.

Variation:  Dip in cornmeal instead of crumbs or flour.

EXCELLENT!!
