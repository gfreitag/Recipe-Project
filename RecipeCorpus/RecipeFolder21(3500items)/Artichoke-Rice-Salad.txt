Title: Artichoke Rice Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
1 pk Rice-a-Roni, chicken flavor
12 sm Stuffed olives, sliced
1/2    Green pepper, chopped fine
4    Green onions, chopped fine
2    Jars marinated artichokes>>
Including olive oil.
Cook Rice-a-Roni as directed.  Add remaining ingredients and toss.
May be eaten hot as avegetable, or chilled and served on a lettuce
leaf as a salad.
