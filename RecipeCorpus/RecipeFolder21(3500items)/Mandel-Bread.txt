Title: Mandel Bread
Categories: Jewish, Bread, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
3    Eggs
3/4 c  Oil
2 tb Potato starch
3/4 c  Finely chopped nuts
2 tb Matzo meal
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Cake meal
Sift potato starch, cake meal, matzo meal, salt and cinnamon
together. Beat eggs and sugar together.  Add oil, beating well.  Add
sifted dry ingredients and nuts and blend well.  Refrigerate for a
while.  Lightly oil a cookie sheet.  Shape dough on sheet into 3 x 1
inch rolls.  Bake at 350 F. for 30 min., or until lightly browned.
Cool slightly.  Slice rolls and place, cut side up, on cookie sheet.
Return to hot oven after turning heat opff.  Let stand in oven until
lightly browned and crisp.  One tablespoon lemon or orange rind may
be added instead of cinnamon.
