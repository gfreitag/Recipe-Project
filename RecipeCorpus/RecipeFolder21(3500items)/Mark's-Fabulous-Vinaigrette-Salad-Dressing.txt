Title: Mark's Fabulous Vinaigrette Salad Dressing
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Salad, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Extra Virgin olive oil
1/2 c  Malt vinegar
1 lg Garlic clove, crushed
1/4 ts Dijon mustard
2 ts Tarragon
1 ts Dillweed
Salt & pepper
Combine all ingredients in a salad bottle.  Shake well.  If keeping
in the fridge, remember to warm up to room temperature about an hour
before serving.
