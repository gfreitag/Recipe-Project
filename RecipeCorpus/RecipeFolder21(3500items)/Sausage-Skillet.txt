Title: Sausage Skillet
Categories: Pork, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
4    Red potatoes, cut in 1/2"
Cubes
4    Italian-style sausages, cut
Into 1/4" cubes
1    Onion, diced
1    Bell pepper, into strips
1    Red pepper, into lengths
In sauce pan, cover potatoes and sausage with water; simmer, covered,
until sausage is fully cooked, about 20 minutes.  Drain off water;
add vegetables to potatoes and sausage in fry pan.  Simmer 10
minutes, stirring occasionally.  Serve immediately.
