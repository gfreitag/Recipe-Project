Title: Strawberry Sherbet
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Jewish
Yield: 4 servings
-JOYCE MYERS (FTTT35B)
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Oil
1    Banana
1 pk Frozen strawberries
Blend well at high speed for 2 to 3 mins. Transfer to a mold and
freeze.
