Title: Banana Cake
Categories: Jewish, Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
7    Eggs, separated
1/4 c  Potato starch
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
3/4 c  Cake flour
1 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Mashed bananas
Beat yolks with 1/2 cup sugar, add bananas and sifted flour and
starch. Beat whites, andd 1/2 cup sugar and fold into yolks.  Fold in
nuts.  Pour batter into ungreased tube cake pan.  Bake in preheated
oven at 325 F. for about 40-50 min.  Remove, invert and hang pan
until cool.
