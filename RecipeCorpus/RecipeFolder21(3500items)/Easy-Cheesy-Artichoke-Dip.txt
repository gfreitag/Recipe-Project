Title: Easy Cheesy Artichoke Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Artichoke hearts (14 oz)
1/2 c  Pace Picante Sauce
-OR
1/2 c  Pace Thick & Chunky Salsa
3/4 c  Mayonnaise (light or reg)
1 c  Freshly grated Parmesan
Drain and chop artichoke hearts. Mix with Pace, mayonnaise and
Parmesan cheese. Spread into a pie plate or ovenproof casserole. Bake
at 350'F. for 20 minutes or until hot. For a festive touch, sprinkle
with chopped red bell peppers and fresh cilantro. Place on a tray and
surround with crackers, chips or vegetable dippers.
