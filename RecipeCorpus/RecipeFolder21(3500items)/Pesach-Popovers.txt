Title: Pesach Popovers
Categories: Jewish, Bread
Yield: 8 servings
-RHONA ALTOMARI (KXPV54B)
2 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Oil
1 1/2 c  -water
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Matzoh meal
1/2 ts Cinnamon
4    Eggs
Blend until mixture forms ball. Cool (not long). Add one at a time;
beating well-4 eggs

Grease muffin tins. Moisten hands, shape. Sprinkle with cinnamon &
sugar. Bake 375 for 45 minutes. Makes 12 Formatted by Elaine Radis;
3/92
