Title: Bacon Potato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Diced potatoes
1    Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped celery
1    Sliced carrot
1    Jar (12-oz) bacon pieces
4 tb Butter/margarine
1 c  Chicken broth
2 c  Milk
In 3 quart casserole, combine 1st 7 ingredinets. Cover loosely with
plastic wrap. Microwave on high 13-15 minutes or until potatoes are
tender; stirring once. Stir in milk, salt and pepper. Recover. Cook
at high 8 minutes or until heated through.
