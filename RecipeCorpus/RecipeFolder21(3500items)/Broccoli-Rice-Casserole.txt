Title: Broccoli-Rice Casserole
Categories: Vegetable, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 servings
2 pk Frozen, choppped broccoli
1 cn Cream mushroom soup
1    Jar Cheez-whiz (small)
1 cn Water chestnuts, chopped
4 c  Cooked rice
1 cn Cream chicken soup
Combine and mix all ingredients, heat in 350 F. oven until hot and
bubbly.

J.R.--Monroe, La.
