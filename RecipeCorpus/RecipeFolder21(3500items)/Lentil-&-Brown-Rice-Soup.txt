Title: Lentil & Brown Rice Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Appetizer, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Lentils
1/3 c  Brown rice
2 tb Olive oil
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
2 tb Soy sauce
2 ea Bay leaves
1 sm Onion, chopped
2 md Carrots, sliced
1 lg Celery stalk, chopped
14 oz Can tomatoes, chopped
1/2 c  Tomato juice
1/4 c  Sherry
1 ts Basil
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/2 ts Thyme
Salt & pepper
Place first 6 ingredients in large soup pot.  Cover with 3 cups water
& bring to a boil.  Cover & simmer for 7 to 8 minutes.  Add 2 more
cups of water along with the rest of the ingredients.  Cover & simmer
for 25 to 30 minutes.  Serve hot.

Nava Atlas, "Vegetariana"
