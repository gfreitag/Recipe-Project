Title: Sauteed Mushrooms
Categories: Indian, Mushroom, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
6 tb Ghee
3 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Cayenne
1/2 ts Cumin seeds
6 oz Button mushrooms, sliced
Melt ghee in a skillet overl ow heat.  Add lemon juice, cayenne &
cumin seeds.  Cook for 3 to 4 minutes.  Add mushrooms & saute for 4
to 6 minutes.

Adapted from Ismail Merchant, "Indian Cuisine"
