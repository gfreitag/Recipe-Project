Title: Tagliatelle with Caviar and Cream***
Categories: Italian, Pasta, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
- G. Granaroli XBRG76A
- MM:MK VMXV03A
1 1/4 c  Heavy cream
1 sm Jar caviar
2 tb Butter
A couple of sage leaves or
-1 ts dried sage powder
1 lb Thin noodles, Pref. fresh
1/4 c  Cognac
Grated cheese
Pour cream into saucepan, add caviar and simmer over low heat 10 min.
DO NOT BOIL!Add butter,sage and cognac and cook for 2 min on low.
Serve over hot cooked pasta with cheese on the side for topping. This
is a great starter for a fish dinner or used as a side dish.
