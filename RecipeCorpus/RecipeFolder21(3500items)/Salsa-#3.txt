Title: Salsa #3
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Pasteurized process cheese
Spread cubed
1    (8-oz) jar salsa OR
1 cn (10-oz) diced tomatos and
Green chilies drained
2 tb Chopped cilantro opt
Microwave process cheese spread and salsa or tomatos and green
chilies in 1 1/2 quart bowl on high 5 minutes or until thoroughly
heated, stirring after 3 minutes. Mix in cilantro. Serve hot with
tortilla chips. CONVENTIONAL: Stir process cheese and salsa or
tomatos and green chilies over low heat until smooth. Mix in cilantro.
