Title: King's Brown Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Main dish, Fish, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 servings
3 sm White onions, minced
1    Winesap apple, grated OR>>>
3 tb Mincemeat
3 oz Kikkoman soy sauce
2    Cloves garlic, crushed
1    Bottle (5 oz.) Picappeppa>>>
Sauce--NO SUBSTITUTIONS.
4 oz Olive oil
Red & black pepper, salt
Saute onion and garlic in oil, add remaining ingredients and
simmer 30 minutes. Use for crab legs or cook shrimp in sauce.  Serve
shrimp in brown sauce with toasted hard rolls and garlic butter.
