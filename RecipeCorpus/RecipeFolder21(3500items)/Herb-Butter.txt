Title: Herb Butter
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Butter, softened
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Basil
1 ts Garlic powder
Combine in a medium bowl and beat with an electric mixer on high speed
until light and fluffy. Use on hot biscuits, vegetables, fish,
poultry, and steaks.

Source: Quick 'n Easy Country Cookin From: Jon Judd, Cooking Echo,
2/93
