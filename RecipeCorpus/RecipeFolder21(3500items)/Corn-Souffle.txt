Title: Corn Souffle
Categories: Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 cn Cream style corn
1 c  Cornbread mix
2 ts Garlic salt
Grated cheese
4    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Wesson oil
1 sm Jar pimentos
Combine and mix corn, eggs, oil, garlic salt, pimentos, corn bread
mix. Pour into casserole; bake at 325 F. for 45-50 minutes.  Sprinkle
grated cheese over casserole just before corn gets done. Courtesy
