Title: Castle Casserole
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
16    White Castle hamburgers
4    White Castle onion chips
12    Slices American cheese
1/4 c  Milk
Layer 8 White Castle hamburgers in the bottom of a 9" x 13" pan,
right side up. Cover with cheese and onion chips. Pour milk over all.
Place 8 more White Castles upside down, layer with cheese and onion
chips. Bake at 300 deg F. until well done (about 25 minutes).

Alaine Lee Cone, Indianapolis, Indiana
