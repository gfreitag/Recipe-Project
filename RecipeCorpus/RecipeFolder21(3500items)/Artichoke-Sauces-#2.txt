Title: Artichoke Sauces #2
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Non-fat yogurt
1 tb Prepared horseradish
1 pn Powdered mustard
Mix yogurt with horseradish and mustard. Use other seasoning to
taste. Mix well, refrigerate while artichoke is steaming.

SOURCE: National Cooking Echo 04/16/90 Contributed to the echo
by: Blanche Nonken & Ellen Cleary
