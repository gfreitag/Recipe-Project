Title: Apple Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Cajun/creol, Chicken
Yield: 2 servings
1/2    Small onion, sliced
2    Chicken breasts
1 tb Oil
2 ts Lemon juice
Salt & pepper
1/2 ts Cumin
1/2 c  Apple juice
1/2 c  Water
Flour
Saute onion in oil.  Salt, pepper and flour chicken, add to oonion
and brown quickly.  Add cumin and stir.  Add water and juices.
Cover, reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes.  Serve with some of the
sauce and onion over chicken.  Recipe may be doubled (4 breasts), but
use only HALF again the sauce ingredients. Courtesy Telephone
