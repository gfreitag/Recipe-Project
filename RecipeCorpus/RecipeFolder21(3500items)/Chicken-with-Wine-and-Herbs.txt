Title: Chicken with Wine and Herbs
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
Frying Chicken
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Garlic Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Oregano
1 c  Dry White Wine
1/2 ts Salt
pieces on all sides.  Pour off excess oil.  Add wine and seasonings.
Cover and simmer for 30 to 40 minutes or until chicken is tender.
