Title: Passover Beet Preserves
Categories: Jewish, Relish
Yield: 1 servings
3 lb Fresh beets   OR
2 cn Shoestring beets (12 oz.)
1 c  Sugar
4 oz Blanched,shredded almonds
1 1/2    Lemons
1 1/4 c  Honey
1 ts Ginger (or less to taste)
Boil, peel and grate fresh beets on large grater, or use shoestring
beets. Bring sugar and honey to a rolling boil; add beets, lemon
juice and rind. Cook 30 min.  Add ginger and cook until thick, about
30 min. longer.  Add almonds, cook for 5 min. stirring often.
