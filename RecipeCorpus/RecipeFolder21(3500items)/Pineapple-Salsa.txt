Title: Pineapple Salsa
Categories: Salad
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn (20-oz) crushed drained
Pineapple
1 sm Red bell pepper seeded
And chopped
2 tb Red onion
1    Fresh jalapeno pepper
Seeded and minced
1 1/2 tb Chopped cilantro
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
In medium serving bowl, combine all ingredients. Cover and chill until
ready to serve.
