Title: Mexican Chicken Casserole
Categories: Tex/mexican, Poultry, Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 8 servings
5    Chick. breast, boned,cut up.
1 pk Corn tortillas (12 in pk)
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
2 cn Green chile salsa (7 oz.ea)
1 c  Milk
1    Onion, chopped
1 lb Cheddar cheese, grated.
Cut tortillas in 1" squares.  Combine all other ingredients except
cheese. Butter casserole; layer tortillas, chicken, and soup mixture.
Top each layer with grated cheese.  Let stand in refrigerator for 24
hrs.  Bake at 300 F. for 1 1/2 to 2 hr. uncovered.
