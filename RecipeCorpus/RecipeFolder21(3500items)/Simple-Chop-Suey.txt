Title: Simple Chop Suey
Categories: Side dish, Chinese, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 sm Onion, sliced
2 ea Carrots, julienned
8 oz Can bamboo shoots
12 oz Can bean sprouts
1 tb Sunflower oil
1 sm Garlic clove, crushed
3 tb Soy sauce
Drain the bamboo & sprouts.  Remove as many of the green pods as
possible. Heat the oil in a wok & crush the garlic into it.  Add the
onion & carrots. Stir fry for a few minutes or until the onion has
softened slightly. Stir in the bamboo shoots & cook for a minute.
Add the bean sprouts & cok for another minute.  Add soy sauce.  Serve
immediately.

Bridget Jones, "Wok Cookery"
