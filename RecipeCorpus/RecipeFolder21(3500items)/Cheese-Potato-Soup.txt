Title: Cheese Potato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1/2    Stick oleo
1/4 c  Flour
2 c  Mashed potatoes
1/2    Stick oleo
1/4 c  Onion
2 c  Milk
1 sm Jar Cheese Whiz
Salt and pepper to taste
Simmer 1/2 stick oleo and onion until done the way you want. Sprinkle
flour to thicken. Add milk, 1/2 stick oleo, cheese, potatoes, salt
and pepper. Heat until cheese melts, stirring constantly to prevent
scorching.
