Title: Egg Casserole
Categories: Pork, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 lb Pork sausage (bulk)
9    Eggs
3 c  Milk
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Salt
4    Slices white bread, cubed
In 1/4" squares
2 c  Grated cheddar cheese
Brown sausage and crumble.Drain well. Mix
eggs,milk,mustard,salt,cheese Pour over bread in greased 9 x 13 pan
Cover - let sit overnight Uncover - bake @ 350 degrees for 1 to 1 1/2
hours
