Title: Fruit Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Raisins
2 c  Water
2 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Shortening
1 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
4 c  White flour
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 ts Allspice
1 ts Cloves
1 c  Mixed fruit
1 c  Currants
In a pot, combine the first four ingredients.  Bring to a boil &
simmer for 1 minute.  Set aside to cool.

Sift together dry ingredients.  Make a well in the centre & fold in
the boiled ingredients.  Mix well.  Bake at 300F for 60 minutes.
Test to see if done all the way through.  If not, return to oven for
a few more minutes.  Cool on a wire rack.
