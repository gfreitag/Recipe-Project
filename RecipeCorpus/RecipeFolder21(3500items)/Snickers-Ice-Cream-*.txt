Title: Snickers Ice Cream *
Categories: Dessert, Ice cream
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
12 oz Vanilla ice cream
1/4 c  Crunchy peanut butter
1 c  Cool whip
1 pk Instant chocolate pudding
Mix all ingredients well. Pour into square pan and freeze. Serves 8.
