Title: Shiitake Saute
Categories: Vegetable, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 servings
8 lg Fresh shiitake mushrooms
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Mirin or sweet sherry
1 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Sugar
Cut off and discard tough mushroom stems and cut caps into wedges.
Heat oil in skillet. Add mushrooms and stir-fry until almost tender.
Add mirin, soy sauce and sugar and toss to coat mushrooms.
