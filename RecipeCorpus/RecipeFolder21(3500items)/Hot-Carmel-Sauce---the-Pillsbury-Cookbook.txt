Title: Hot Carmel Sauce - the Pillsbury Cookbook
Categories: Dessert, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Half-and-half
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
6 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Vanilla

corn
