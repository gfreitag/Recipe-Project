Title: Bluenose Strawberry Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Sliced strawberries
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Half and half
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Brandy
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Extra sliced strawberries
And mint sprigs
Blend strawberries, sour cream, half and half, sugar, brandy and
vanilla until smooth, about 30-45 seconds. Serve in chilled cups and
garnish with sliced strawberries and mint sprigs.
