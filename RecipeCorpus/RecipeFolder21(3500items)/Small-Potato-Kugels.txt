Title: Small Potato Kugels
Categories: Jewish, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
4    Potatoes, peeled
2 tb Chicken fat
1    Small onion
2 tb Matzo meal
1    Egg, well beaten
1 ts Water
Grate potatoes into bowl of ice water.  Drain all liquid through
collander..  Add rest of ingredients and mix well.  Put batter into
greased cupcake tins and bake until browned in 350 F. oven.
