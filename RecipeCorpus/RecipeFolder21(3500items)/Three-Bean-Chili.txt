Title: Three Bean Chili
Categories: Tex/mexican, Chili, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1    Jar-prepared brown gravy
1 cn Whole tomatoes, chopped(1#)
1 tb Chili powder
1 cn Spicy chili beans (15 oz.)
1 cn Garbanzo beans (15 oz.)
1 cn Pinto or Kidney beans
1 cn Chopped green chiles (4 oz.)
MMMMM-------------------------GARNISHES------------------------------
Yogurt or sour cream
Grated cheddar cheese
Sliced green onions
Drain all canned ingredients before adding;  Combine gravy, chopped
tomatoes and chili powder in 3 qt. saucepan.  Bring to boil and stir
in beans and chiles.  Cover; simmer 15 min. to blend flavors, stirring
occasionally.  Serve with desired toppings.

Variation:  brown 1 lb. coarsely ground beef in skillet.  Drain well.
Add to chili mixture.
