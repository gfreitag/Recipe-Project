Title: Spam Meat Spread
Categories: Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Spam
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Prepared mustard
1/4 lb Cubed Velveeta cheese
2 tb Melted butter
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Catsup
1 ts Minced onion
Potato roll buns
Spread the above ingredients on potato roll buns. Cover tightly with
foil and heat at 350 degrees.
