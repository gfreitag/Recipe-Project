Title: Chili Bean Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
12 oz Low fat creamy cottage
Cheese
2 cn (about 1 lb each) kidney
Beans drained
1    Packet chili seasoning mix
Or to taste
MMMMM--------------------------DIPPERS-------------------------------
Raw vegetables or
Tortilla chips
