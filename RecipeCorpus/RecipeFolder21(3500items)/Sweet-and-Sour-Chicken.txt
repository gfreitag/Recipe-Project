Title: Sweet and Sour Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 6 servings
4    Chick. breast,halved,skinned
Boned.
1/2 c  Flour with salt & pepper
1/3 c  Oil
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1 cn 13 oz. pineapple tidbits
1 1/4 c  Juice(water + pineapple juic
2/3 c  Cider  vinegar
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Ginger
1    Chicken bullion cube
2 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Soy sauce
1 cn Mandarin oranges (10 oz.)
1/2 c  Marachino cherries-halved
Shake chicken in bag with flour, salt and pepper; brown well both
sides in hot oil.  Put in 9 x 13 inch baking pan.  Bake at 350 F. for
20 Min.

FOR SAUCE:  Mix all ingreds. except pineapple, oranges, and cherries.
Boil for 2 min., stirring.  Pour sauce over chicken.  Add pineapple,
oranges, cherries.  Baste and bake 20 min. more.  Accompany with hot
cooked rice.
