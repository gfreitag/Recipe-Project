Title: Spicy Corn Relish
Categories: Relish, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1 qt Whole fresh kernel corn OR>>
Equal amt. frozen or canned.
1 c  Celery, chopped
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Salt
1 c  Pimento, chopped coarse
1 c  Green pepper, chopped coarse
1/2 c  Onion, chopped med.
2 c  Vinegar (1 pt. bottle)
1 ts Celery seed
Mix sugar, salt, celery seed and vinegar together. Place in
enameled, or pyrex saucepan and boil with vegetables for 20 min. Can
and process in usual manner.
