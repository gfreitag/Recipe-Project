Title: Pork Fried Rice
Categories: Pasta, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
4    Pork chops or steaks
1/2    Med. onion, chopped
2 1/4 c  Water
4 tb Soy sauce
1/2    Med. bell pepper, chopped
1 1/2 c  Long grain rice
4 tb Oil
Brown pork in oil.  Chop in bite size pieces.  Add onion and bell
pepper to oil.  Stir, add rice and let fry until light golden brown.
