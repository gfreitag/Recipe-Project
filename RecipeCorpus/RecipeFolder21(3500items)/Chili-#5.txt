Title: Chili #5
Categories: Chili
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Bacon/small ham chunks
Fried crisp
2 qt Tomato sauce
1 sm Chopped onion
5 md Chopped shallots
1 c  Fresh diced artichokes
1 cn (15-oz) kidney beans opt
2 c  Potato puree
2 c  Michigan dry wine
2 tb Dried parsley
1 tb Thyme
1 tb Oregano
3 tb Chili powder
4 tb Maple syrup
Combine bacon, tomato sauce, onion, shallots, artichokes, beans,
potato puree, wine, parsley, thyme, oregano, chili and maple syrup in
a pot. Heat to boiling, then simmer for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
