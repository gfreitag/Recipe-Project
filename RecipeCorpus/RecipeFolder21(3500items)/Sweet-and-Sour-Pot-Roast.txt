Title: Sweet and Sour Pot Roast
Categories: Jewish, Meat, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/2 lb Pot roast
2    Large onions
1    Clove garlic
1/2 c  Clear stock
1/3 c  Raisins
1    Bay leaf
2 tb Vinegar
1 tb Dark brown sugar
2 tb Ketchup
Brown beef on all sides in some fat in heavy pan.  Add onions and
garlic, diced, and brown.  Add stock and bay leaf and simmer 1 hr ,
covered tightly.  Add more stock if necessary.  Add ketchup, vinegar
and sugar and simmer unil tender, adding raisins the last 15 minutes.
