Title: Enchiladas
Categories: Tex/mexican, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Ricotta
1    Egg
1 c  Green onions
2 tb Chopped chilies
1 ts Cumin
1 c  Jack cheese
8    Tortillas
10 oz Enchilada sauce
2 c  Cheddar cheese
1    8 x 12in. glass pan
Contributed to the echo by: BOYD NARON ENCHILADAS Stir first six
ingredients together and fill tortillas. Put in greased 8 x 12 glass
pan. Pour sauce over. Microwave on high for 11 to 13 minutes,
rotating once. Cover with cheddar cheese and cook on high 1 to 2
minutes.
