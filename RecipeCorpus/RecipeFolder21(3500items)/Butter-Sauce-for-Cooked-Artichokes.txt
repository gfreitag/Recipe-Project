Title: Butter Sauce for Cooked Artichokes
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  (2 sticks) melted butter
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dry mustard
Hope you all like these, and hope you can use them, Stephen.
Contributed to the echo by: Michelle Bass Originally from: "The Best
of Bon Appetit Cookbook", Knapp Press, 1979 BUTTER SAUCE FOR COOKED
ARTICHOKES Combine all ingredients and simmer 5 minutes. Serve warm
as dipping sauce with hot artichokes.

Servings: 4
