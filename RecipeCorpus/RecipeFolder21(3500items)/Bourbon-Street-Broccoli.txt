Title: Bourbon Street Broccoli
Categories: Jewish, Cajun/creol, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
2 pk Frozen broccoli (10 oz.)
1 cn Tuna fish (9 1/2 oz.)
1 cn Cream celery soup,undiluted
1 c  Seasoned bread crumbs
3 tb Melted butter or margarine
2 c  Water
1    Egg
Cheddar cheese strips
Mix ingreds. together, blending well.  Place mixture in individual
ramekins and put cheese strips on top.  Bake for about 1 hr. at 350
F. Sprinkle with additional bread crumbs, if desired.

May also be made as casserole in single pyrex dish.
