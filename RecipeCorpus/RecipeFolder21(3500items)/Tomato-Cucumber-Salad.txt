Title: Tomato-Cucumber Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 4 servings
1    Tomato, cut into about 18
Pieces (cut in half, then
Each half in thirds
Sideways and lengthwise)
1    Cucumber, peeled and thinly
Sliced
1/3 lg Onion, thinly sliced
3 tb Vinegar
3 tb Sugar
3 tb Water
Salt and pepper
Contributed to the echo by: Ellen Cleary The weather here is feeling
like spring and inspired me to make this salad. I know it's simple,
but I LOVE simple food and there seem to be lots of beginning cooks
on the echo now. TOMATO-CUCUMBER SALAD

Combine ingredients and marinate at least 1 hour.
