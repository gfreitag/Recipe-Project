Title: Famous Durgin-Park Corn Bread
Categories: Bread
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Flour
1 c  Cornmeal
1 tb Baking Powder
1 tb Melted Butter
Mix sugar with beaten eggs. Sift flour, salt and cornmeal together.
Add melted butter and milk. Beat up very quickly and bake in a large
buttered pan in a very hot oven (450 degrees).

SOURCE: *Dexters Grist Mill, Sandwich, Cape Cod, MA SHARED BY: Jim
Bodle 2/93
