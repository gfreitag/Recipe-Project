Title: Oyster Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Cajun/creol
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped fresh parsley
2 tb Oil or shortening
1 c  Cooked giblets
2 c  Giblet stock
2 c  Cooked rice
3 c  Crumbled dry bread
1 pt Chopped oysters & juice
Salt to taste
Cayenne pepper to taste
Preheat oven to 350 F.  In a lge. saucepan, saute celery, onions, and
parsley in the oil until the onions are clear.  In a large mixing
bowl, combine the giblets and stock with the cooked rice, bread, and
oysters w/juice; mix well.  Add the sald and pepper. Pour into a
greased medium-sized baking dish and bake for 45 minutes, or stuff a
large bird and bake until the bird is done.

Yield: about 10 cups, or enough to stuff a large hen.
