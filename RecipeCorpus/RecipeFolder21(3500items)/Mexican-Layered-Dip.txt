Title: Mexican Layered Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn (30 oz) refried beans
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Picante sauce
2 c  Shredded cheese
1 cn (4-oz) black olives
Tortilla chips
Spread beans in 9 x 13 inch baking dish. Combine sour cream and
mayonnaise; spread over bean dip. Cover with picante sauce. Sprinkle
with cheese and top with black olives. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees
for 30 minutes. Serve with tortilla chips.
