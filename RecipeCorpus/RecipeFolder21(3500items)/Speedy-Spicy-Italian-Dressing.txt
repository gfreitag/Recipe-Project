Title: Speedy-Spicy Italian Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Pace Picante Sauce
1/3 c  Bottled Italian Dressing
Combine ingredients; mix well. Serve as a dressing for favorite
salads or use as a marinade for fresh vegetables, bean salads or
cooked shrimp.

Makes about 3/4 cup.
