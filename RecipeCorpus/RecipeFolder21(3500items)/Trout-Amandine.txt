Title: Trout Amandine
Categories: Main dish, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
6    Fillets of trout
1/3 c  Flour
1/4 c  Butter
3/4 c  Milk
Salt & pepper
1/2 c  Almonds, chopped
Dip trout in milk.  Season with salt & pepper, roll in flour.  Cook
trout in butter until evenly browned on both sides.  Remove fish and
saute shopped almonds in drippings.  Sprinkle over fish. Courtesy
