Title: Hamburger Hot Dish
Categories: Beef
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Celery,diced
1/2 c  American cheese,diced
1/4 c  Ripe olives,cut up
2 c  Uncooked noodles
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Onion salt
3 tb Ketchup OR
1 c  Canned tomatoes
Brown hamburger.Add remainder of ingredients.Cover tightly.Bring to a
boil.Simmer for 30 minutes,stirring occasionally.Add water as needed.
