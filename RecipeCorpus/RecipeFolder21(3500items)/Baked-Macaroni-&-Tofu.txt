Title: Baked Macaroni & Tofu
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Cooked elbow macaroni
1 c  Mashed tofu
3 c  Cheesy gravy
Preheat oven to 350F.  Combine tofu & macaroni.  Place in a baking
dish. Pour gravy over the mixture & bake 20 minutes.
