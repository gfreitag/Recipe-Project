Title: Mock Whipped Cream
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Fresh tofu
3 tb Natural syrup
1/2 ts Vanilla extract or flavoring
-of choice
3 tb Almond buter or sesame
-tahini
Soymilk (add as needed)
Bill tofu in water for 10 minutes. Drain the tofu and let it cool.
Blend the tofu and the rest of the ingredients into a blender or food
processor until smooth. If the "whipped cream" is too thick, add
soymlk sparingly.
Chill at least 1 hour before serving. Makes 1 cup.
