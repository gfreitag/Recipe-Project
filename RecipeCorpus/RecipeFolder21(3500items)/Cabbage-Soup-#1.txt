Title: Cabbage Soup #1
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn (48-oz) tomato juice
3 c  Water
1/2    Head cabbage shredded
4    Sliced carrots
1    Diced onion
6    Beef bouillon cubes
1    Bay leaf
In a large pot, combine tomato juice, water, cabbage, carrots, onion,
bouillon and bay leaf, and simmer for an hour. Remove bay leaf before
serving.
