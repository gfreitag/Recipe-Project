Title: Salsa Borracho for Enchiladas
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 3 servings
3 tb Butter
4    Clove garlic, minced
4 tb Chili powder
4    Jalapenos, diced
2 c  Diced tomatoes
2 tb Tomato paste
1/2 c  Beer
In nonaluminum pan, melt the butter over medium low heat.  Add the
garlic, chili powder, and jalapenos.  Saute until the chili powder
begins to foam. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.
Simmer for 5 min. Remove from heat.

Yield:  approx. 3 cups.
