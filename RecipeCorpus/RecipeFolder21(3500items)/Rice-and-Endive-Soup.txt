Title: Rice and Endive Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Appetizer, Italian, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Uncooked rice
1 1/2 c  Chopepd onions
2 ea Celery stalks, sliced
2 sm Carrots, peeled & cut into
-- half moons
3 tb Olive oil
3 c  Vegetable stock
10 ea Ripe plum tomatoes, chopped,
-- or 28 oz can whole plum
-- tomatoes, crushed
1 ea Head curly endive, chopped
1/2 c  Chopped fresh parsley
1 1/2 tb Chopped fresh basil
Salt & pepper
Cook the rice.

Meanwhile, saute the onions, celery & carrots in the olive oil till
tender. Add the stock & tomatoes & endive.  Simer for about 15
minutes. Stir in the cooked rice.  Add parsley & basil.  Season with
salt & pepper to taste.
