Title: Lemon Chicken Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
3 cn Chicken broth
1/4 c  Rice
4 c  FRESH lemon juice
Contributed to the echo by: Marge Clark Cook rice in broth for
approx. 40 mins.

Add the warm broth slowly to the beaten egg yolks, beating constantly.
