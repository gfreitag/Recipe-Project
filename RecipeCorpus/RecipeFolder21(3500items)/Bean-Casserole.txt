Title: Bean Casserole
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Pork and beans
1 cn Red beans, drained
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Mustard
1/4 c  Water
1 cn White lima beans, drained
1 lg Onion chopped fine
3 tb Bacon drippings
3    Cloves garlic, chopped
1 tb Syrup
Salt & Pepper to taste
Fry onion and garlic in bacon fat until limp.  Mix with remaining
ingredients.  Pour into casserole dish; bake 1 hr. at 350 F.
