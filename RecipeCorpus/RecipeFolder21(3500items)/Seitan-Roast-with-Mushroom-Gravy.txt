Title: Seitan Roast with Mushroom Gravy
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Seitan
2 c  Sliced mushrooms
2 c  Sliced onions
1 1/3 oz Tofu scrambler
4 c  Water
1 tb Chopped fresh basil leaves
1 tb Chopped fresh sage leaves
Slice seitan into 1/2" slices & place in a Dutch oven.  Layer
mushrooms & onions on top.  In a bowl, combine dry tofu scrambler
with water, basil & sage.  Pour over seitan & vegetables.  Bring to a
boil & simmer gently for 30 minutes, till gravy has thickened.

"Vegetarian Times" November, 1991
