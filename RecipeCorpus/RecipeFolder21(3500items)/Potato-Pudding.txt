Title: Potato Pudding
Categories: Jewish, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
6    Potatoes, drained but not>>
Dry.
1    Onion, grated
1 ts Salt & pepper (to taste)
1 tb Matzo meal or cream wheat
3    Eggs, beaten
6 tb Oil, margarine or crisco
Grate potatoes, drain but not dry.  Put back starch.  Add grated
onion, 3 eggs, 1 tablespoon cream of wheat, matzo meal, or flour and
salt.  Melt shortening.  Add 4 tbs. to potatoes and leave 2 tbs.
inside baking pan. Bake for 350 F. for abt. 1 hr.
