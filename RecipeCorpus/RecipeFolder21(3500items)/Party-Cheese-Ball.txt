Title: Party Cheese Ball
Categories: Spread
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Philidelphia cream cheese
8 oz Cracker Barrel cheese
1    Jar pimento cheese
1    Chopped onion
1    Chopped green pepper
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Lemon juice/real lemon
1 pk Ground pecans/almonds/etc
Have cheese at room temperature. In a mixing bowl; shred cracker
barrel cheese. Mix with pastry blender. Mix jar of pimento cheese
with blender. Add other ingredients and mix into bowl. Form into a
ball and put on foil covered dish - cover ball also. Refrigerate for
3-4 hours. Roll in ground nuts.
