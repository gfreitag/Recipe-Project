Title: Royal Street Jambalaya
Categories: Jewish, Cajun/creol, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1    1 lb. jar gefilte fish
2 c  Cooked rice
2 tb Butter or pareve margarine
1/4 c  Bell pepper, chopped
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Thyme
1 cn Condensed tomato soup>>>
Undiluted.
1 c  Milk
Paprika
Saute onion and green pepper in butter until tender but not brown.
Add salt, pepper, thyme, soup, and milk.  Mis with rice Pour into
greased 1 1/2 qt. baking dish.

Drain gefilte fish and arrange on top; press into rice mixture and
sprinkle with paprika.  Bake in  350 F. oven for 30 min.
