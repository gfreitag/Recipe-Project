Title: Easy Hodge Podge Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Hamburger
1    Chopped onion
Salt and pepper
1 cn (10.75-oz) tomato soup
1 cn (16-oz) mixed vegetables
1 cn (16-oz) kidney beans
1    Box (14.75-oz) spaghetti
Brown hamburger and onions. Drain excess grease. Add tomato soup and
half a can of water. Stir in vegetables. Run knife through spaghetti
to cut strands. Add to soup mixture. Bring to boil, simmer 15 minutes.
