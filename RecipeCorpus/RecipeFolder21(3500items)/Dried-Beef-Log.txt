Title: Dried Beef Log
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 2 servings
1 pk (8-oz) cream cheese softened
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1 tb Prepared horseradish
1/3 c  Chopped stuffed olives
2 1/2 oz Dried beef
Blend together the cream cheese, parmesan cheese and horseradish.
Stir in stuffed olives. On waxed paper, shape mixture in two 6 inch
rolls, 1 1/2 inches in diameter. Wrap and chill several hours or
overnight. Roll each log in finely snipped dried beef. Serve with
crackers.
