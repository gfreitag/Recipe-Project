Title: Pumpkin Bread or Muffins
Categories: Bread
Yield: 3 servings
3 1/2 c  Pumpkin, pureed
1 c  Butter
2    Eggs
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 1/2 c  Pastry flour
6 ts Baking powder
1/4 c  Molasses
1 ts Cloves
2 ts Nutmeg
2 c  Walnuts, broken
2 c  Honey
1 c  Cream
4 tb Vanilla
Combine pumpkin, butter, and eggs.  Add remaining ingredients and
stir. Pour into oiled loaf pans or muffin tins. Bake at 350 degrees
for 30 minutes or until tops brown. To give as gifts, bake in oiled
empty coffee or tea tins.
