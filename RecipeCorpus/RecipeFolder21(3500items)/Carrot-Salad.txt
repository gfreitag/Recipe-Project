Title: Carrot Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Carrots, shredded
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1/2 c  Apples, chopped
1/3 c  Seedless raisins
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
Combine carrots, apples, raisins and mix well.  Add the salt and
mayonnaise.  Chill and serve on nest of lettuce.

C.C.--Lake Charles, La.
