Title: Shrimp Paste
Categories: Appetizer, Cajun/creol, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Can cooked shrimp
1 pk Cream cheese (8 oz.)
1    Very samll onion (grate>>>>
Until juicy).
1 1/2 tb Chili sauce
1 1/2 ts Horseradish
Rinse and clean shrimp, breaking it into small pieces. Begin mixing
softened cream sheese with other ingredients, except the shrimp. Mix
well, then add shrimp and continue mixing, leaving the shrimp in
chunky bits. This is not a dip, but it is used as a spread for
crackers or potato chips. It should be made several hours ahead of
serving time and refrigerated.
