Title: Pico De Gallo
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1    Tomato, chopped
1    Onion, chopped
5    Cilantro sprigs, chopped
1    Jalapeno pepper, chopped
1    Lemon, cut in half
Salt, to taste
Contributed to the echo by: Mike Avery Originally from: "Austin
American-Stateman, May 24, 1990 Pico De Gallo

Mix tomato, onion, cilantro, and jalapeno in a serving dish. Squeeze
lemon juice over mixture and add salt to taste and serve. From
"Fajita Festival."

{Lime juice may be used instead of lemon, depending on what's
available and in season.}
