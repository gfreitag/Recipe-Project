Title: Lima Bean Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Frozen lima beans
3 1/2 c  Sliced carrots
3 tb Olive oil
5 ea Green onions, finely chopped
5 c  Chopped mushrooms
1 ds Basil
1 ds Black pepper
1 ds Cayenne
3/4 c  Soy sauce
Cook lima beans in water according to packet instructions.  Steam
carrots till tender.  Drain reserving cooking water.

Heat oil in a skillet & saute green onions & mushrooms until tender.
Add spices & saute for a further 1 minute.

Combine all ingredients (except milk) along with 1 cup of bean &
carrot water.  Using a blender, blend in batches till smooth.  Pour
mixture into a large soup pot.  Add milk. Cook slowly for 15 to 20
minutes.
