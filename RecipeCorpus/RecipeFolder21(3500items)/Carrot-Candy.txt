Title: Carrot Candy
Categories: Jewish, Candy
Yield: 1 servings
1 pt Grated carrots
3/4 ts Ginger
1 tb Orange rind, grated
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 lb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Nuts
3/4 ts Lemon juice
Combine carrosts, sugar, orange rind and juice.  Add salt and cook,
stirring often.   After 5 or 10 min. add ginger and lemon juice.
Continue cooking until a few dorps of the syrup form a firm ball in
cold water. Remove from fire, add nuts and spread on wet board.  Cut
candy into squares when cold.
