Title: Tofu Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetarian
Yield: 2 servings
1 lb Firm tofu
1/4 c  Tahini
1 ts Mustard
1 tb Tamari
1 tb Eggless mayo
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
1 sm Garlic clove; minced
1/4 c  Shredded carrots
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
Mash tofu.  Add other ingredients and mix well.  Spread on toast,
pita, crackers, or roll up in lettuce leaves.
