Title: Tropic Sun Spareribs
Categories: Main dish, Pork, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
4 lb Spareribs, or country-style
- pork ribs
3 lg Cloves garic, pressed
1 lg Can (20-oz.) Dole crushed
- pineapple, undrained
1 lg Onion
1    Bottle (12-oz.) chili sauce
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 ts Ground giner
1/2 ts Dry mustard
Rub ribs with garlic.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Arrange onion
in large baking pan.  Place ribs on top.  Add 1/4 cup water to pan.
Cover with foil.  Bake in 350-degrre F. oven 1/2 hour.  Combine
remainig ingredients.  Spoon over ribs.  Bake, uncovered, one hour
longer.  S

Serves 4

SOURCE: Dole's Quick 'N Easy Recipes.
