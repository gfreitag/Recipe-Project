Title: Delicious Party Spread
Categories: Spread
Yield: 1 servings
12 oz Softened light cream cheese
1 cn (4.25-oz) deviled ham
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
Dash cayenne pepper
1/3 c  Finely chopped green bell
Pepper
1/3 c  Finely chopped red bell
Pepper
In small mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, deviled ham,
worcestershire sauce and cayenne pepper. Beat on medium speed until
smooth. Stir in green and red peppers. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour
or until ready to serve. Serve with crackers or vegetables.
