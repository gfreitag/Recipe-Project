Title: Sally's Remoulade Sauce
Categories: Appetizer, Salsa/sauce, Cajun/creol, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
2    Cloves garlic, pressed
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Black pepper
1/2 c  Catsup
1 ts Lemon juice
Dash of Tabasco
Horseradish to taste
1 tb Vinegar
1 sm White onion, grated
1/2 c  Wesson oil
1/2 c  Chili sauce
Dash of paprika
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Mustard
Blend all ingreds. together in blender--serve over cold boiled
shrimp.
