Title: Overnight Cinnamon Coffeecake
Categories: Breakfast, Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
1 x  -Dorothy Cross TMPJ72B
1 pk Frozen dinner rolls
1 pk (3-1/2 oz) instant
1 x  -butterscotch pudding
1 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
2 tb Cinnamon
1/2 c  Butter; (1 stick), melted
1/2 c  Pecans
Layer in a greased bundt cake pan in the follow order: frozen rolls,
powdered pudding, brown sugar, cinnamon, melted butter, and pecan.
Cover the pan with foil topped with clean towel and allow to sit
overnight. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes. Serves 8 to 10.
Source: Country Inn and Bed and Breakfast Cookbook (Served at The
Hoosier Hospitality B&B in Bloomington, IN) Reformatted for
