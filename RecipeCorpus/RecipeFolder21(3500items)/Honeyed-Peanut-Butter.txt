Title: Honeyed Peanut Butter
Categories: Sandwich, Snack
Yield: 1 servings
-From the Kitchen of
-Lawrence & Cindy Kellie
1 c  Butter; softened
1 c  Peanut butter
1/4 c  Honey
MMMMM--------------------------OPTIONAL-------------------------------
2 tb Toasted sesame seeds
Beat ingredients together for several minutes.  If you have a food
processor, let it do the work for you.

Option:  After spreading on bread, cover withhalved, seedless grapes,
or banana penneis, or crisp apple slices.  The fruie will make the
sandwich delightfully moist and be a lunchtime surprise.
