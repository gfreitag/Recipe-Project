Title: Kreplach
Categories: Jewish, Bread, Meat
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------DOUGH--------------------------------
1    Egg
1/2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Flour
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/2 lb Beef, cooked
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1    Onion
1 tb Fat or crisco
Fry onions in fat, grind meat, beat in egg and seasoning.   Beat egg,
salt and add flour.  Knead dough to a smooth ball.  Roll out on board
to 1/8th inch thickness and cut into  2" squares.  Put a small ball
of filling into center of each square, fold one corner over
diagonally to form a triangle and press edges firmly together.  Let
stand for 10 min. and then cook in boiling salted water for 30 min.
Drain and serve either in chicken soup or browned in the oven with
fat as a side dish.
