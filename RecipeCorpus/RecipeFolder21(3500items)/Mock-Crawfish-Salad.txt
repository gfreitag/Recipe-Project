Title: Mock Crawfish Salad
Categories: Jewish, Cajun/creol, Salad, Fish
Yield: 8 servings
3 lb Red snapper fillets
4    Slices lemon
1 sm Onion sliced
1 c  Celelry, chopped fine
1    Large carrot, grated
2 qt Water
1 sm Onion minced
3/4 c  Chili sauce
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Mayonnaise
Bring water to boil in large kettle.  Add red snappes, lemon, sliced
onion, sald, and pepper.  Continue to boil for 20 min.  Drain fish;
cool and flake.

Toss fish with minced onion, celery, carrot, chili sauce, and
mayonnaise. Adjust salt and pepper to taste.  Chill overnight.
