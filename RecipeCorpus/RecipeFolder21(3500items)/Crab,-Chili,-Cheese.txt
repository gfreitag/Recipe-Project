Title: Crab, Chili, Cheese
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk 8-oz cream cheese
1 cn Crabmeat
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1 ts Horseradish
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Tobasco sauce
1 ts Chopped chives
1/4 ts Parsley
1 ts Salt
Slice cream cheese in half and cover with crabmeat. Just before
serving top with chili sauce mixture. Serve with crackers.
