Title: Salad Dressing
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 tb Minced onion
1 1/3 tb Accent
2/3 c  Vinegar
Dash of Tabasco sauce
3 tb Yellow mustard
1 1/3 tb Salt
1 ts Lea & Perrins sauce
2 1/3 c  Salad oil
Place onion, mustard, Accent, salt and vinegar in mixer.  Mix
until well blended.  Add remaining ingredients and stir in gradually.
Mix well and place in 1-quart container.  Store refrigerated.
