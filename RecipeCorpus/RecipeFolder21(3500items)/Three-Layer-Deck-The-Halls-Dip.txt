Title: Three-Layer Deck-The-Halls Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Guacamole
8 oz Sour cream
8 oz Pace Picante Sauce
-OR
8 oz Pace Thick & Chunky Salsa
In a shallow, clear glass bowl (straight sides are best), layer
guacamole, sour cream and Pace. Place the bowl on a tray and surround
it with chips or colorful vegetable dippers.
