Title: Savory Egg Filling
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
-From the Kitchen of
Lawrence & Cindy Kellie
1    Onion; finely chopped
2 tb Parsley; finely chopped
4 tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Tomato; fresh or canned
1 tb Flour
6    Egg; lightly beaten
Seasonings to taste:
-salt, season salt
-George Washington Broth
Suate ontions in oil.  Add tomatoes (chopped or mashed) and stir in
flour to make a thick paste.  Turn down heat before adding eggs,
parsley and seasonings.  Cook like scrambling eggs, stirring
constantly.  Remove from heat when set.  Cool and add mayonnaise to
make a soft spread.
