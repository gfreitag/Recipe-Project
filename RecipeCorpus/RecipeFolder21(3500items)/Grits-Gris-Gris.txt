Title: Grits Gris-Gris
Categories: Jewish, Cajun/creol
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Grits (not quick cooking)
3/4 ts Salt
3 1/3 c  Boiling water OR >>>>

Boil.
Stir grits slowly into boiling salted water in heavy saucepan.
Return to boil; reduce heat and cover.   Cook slowly for 25 to 30

breakfast as an accompaniment to smothered liver. (If served with
