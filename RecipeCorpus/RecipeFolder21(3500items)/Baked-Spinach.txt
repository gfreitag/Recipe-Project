Title: Baked Spinach
Categories: Vegetable, Appetizer, Casserole
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Butter
8 oz Cream Cheese
2 pk Frozen Spinach
1 pk Lipton Onion Soup Mix
Breadcrumbs
Melt butter in a large frying pan.  Add 2 packages of spinach.  Break
up and stir.  Add cream cheese and onion soup mix.  Stir well.  Place
in a casserole.  Top with breadcrumbs and bake at 300oF for 45
minutes.
