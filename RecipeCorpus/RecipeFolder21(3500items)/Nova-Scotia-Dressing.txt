Title: Nova Scotia Dressing
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 9 servings
6 c  Potato, cubed
1 1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Poultry seasoning (to
Taste) OR
1 ts Sage
1/2 c  Melted margarine
1/2 c  Onion, chopped (to taste)
Cook potatoes about 20 min or until soft enough to mash.  Combine all
ingredients in large bowl and blend well.  TASTE.  Divide into
meal-sized portions (extra may be frozen).  Re-heat in microwave or
oven a few minutes before serving.
