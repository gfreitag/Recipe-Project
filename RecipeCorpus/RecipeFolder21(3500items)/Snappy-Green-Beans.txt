Title: Snappy Green Beans
Categories: Vegetable, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Hot sausage, cut thin
2 cn Cut green beans (#303)
2    Green onions, sliced thin
1/2 ts Parsley
1/2 ts Thyme
1 ts Oil or shortening
Salt to taste
Fry sausage in oil until slightly browned.  Add green onions.
Cook 1 minute longer.  Add liquid from string beans, thyme, salt and
parsley. Cook until sausage is very tender.  Add green beans and cook
5 minutes longer. Fresh green beans may be used if desired.  Boil
beans until tender. Use water from boiled beans and prepare as for
canned beans.
