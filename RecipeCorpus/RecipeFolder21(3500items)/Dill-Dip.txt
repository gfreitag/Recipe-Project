Title: Dill Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Sour cream
1 tb Dried parsley
1 tb Minced dried onions
1 1/2 tb Dill weed
3/4 tb Seasoned salt
You may use mayonnaise or salad dressing. Blend all ingredients
together and chill before serving. May be made several days ahead.
