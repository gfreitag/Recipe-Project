Title: Cheese-Beef Casserole Pie
Categories: Beef, Casserole, Cheese/eggs, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 sm Package cream cheese
1    Can mushroom soup
1 c  Canned corn with pimento
1 pk Refrigerated bisquits
Brown ground beef in skillet; drain. Mix all but biscuits. Pour in 2
quart casserole. Top with bisquits. Bake at 350 for about 20 minutes.
Source: Nancy Alexander (PBJF04B)
