Title: Delicious Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Cottage cheese
2 tb Grated cheese
1/4 ts Dill weed
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Salt
In a bowl mash with a fork the cottage cheese and grated cheese. Add
the dill weed, salt and worcestershire sauce. Mix well. Dip raw
vegetables and eat.
