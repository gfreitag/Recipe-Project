Title: Italian Style Meat Loaf
Categories: Beef
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Lean ground beef
6 oz Italian sausage or spicy
Bulk sausage
1 cn (14 1/2 oz) Italian style
Stewed tomatoes
1 c  Fresh bread crumbs
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped green bell pepper
1    Egg, beaten
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients;mix well.Place in a 4 1/2 x
8" loaf pan.Bake @ 350 degrees for 1 hour;drain.Makes 6 servings.
