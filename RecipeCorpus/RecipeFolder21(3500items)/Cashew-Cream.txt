Title: Cashew Cream
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetarian, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Raw, unsalted cashews
1/4 c  Water, approximately
2    -to
3 tb Maple syrup
1 ts Vanilla
Coarsely chop the nuts in a blender or food processor.  With the motor
running, gradually add the water, 2 tablespoons maple syrup, and
vanilla.

Taste and add additional maple syrup, if desired.  Continue processing
until the mixture achieves a consistency similar to whipped cream.
Add a bit more water if needed.

Makes about 3/4 cup
