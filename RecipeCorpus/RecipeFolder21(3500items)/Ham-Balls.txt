Title: Ham Balls
Categories: Pork
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-------------------------HAM BALLS------------------------------
1 lb Ground ham
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs
1 1/2 lb Sausage
2 c  Bread crumbs
Onions
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
3/4 c  Brown sugar (packed)
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 ts Dry mustard
Mix and form into balls the size of golf balls. Pour sauce over balls
and bake about 1 hour at 350F.
