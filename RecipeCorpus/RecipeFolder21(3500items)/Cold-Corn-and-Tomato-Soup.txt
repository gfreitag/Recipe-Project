Title: Cold Corn and Tomato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Chicken stock/broth
1 1/2 c  Peeled, seeded, chopped
Tomatos
1 md Chopped onion
1    Bay leaf
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
1    Pinch sugar
Combine all ingredients in a saucapan and bring to a boil, lower heat
and simmer, partially covered for 20 minutes. Cool slightly, then
puree in a blender. You may have to do it in two batches.
