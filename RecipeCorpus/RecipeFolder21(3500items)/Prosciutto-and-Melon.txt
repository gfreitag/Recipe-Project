Title: Prosciutto and Melon
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1    Ripe cantaloupe sliced or
Cubed and peeled
4 oz Thin sliced prosciutto
For a sit down first course, put 2-3 thin slices of peeled melon on
each plate and drape 2-3 slices of prosciutto over them. For finger
food, cut melon in cubes and wrap prosciutto around them, holding in
place with a toothpick.
