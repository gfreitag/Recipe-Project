Title: Aphrodisiac Bread
Categories: Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Dry yeast
1/2 c  Warm water
1 1/2 c  Unbleached white flour
2 tb Oil
1/2 ts Curry powder
2 pn Parsley
3/4 ts Salt
1 ts Garlic minced
In a large bowl, sprinkle yeast into water & dissolve.  Add the rest
of the ingredients in order & beat well.

On a floured surface, knead dough lightly for 5 minutes.  Form into a
round bread & set aside until its has doubled.

Preheat oven to 350F & bake for 45 minutes till light brown.

Anne Lerner, "Breads You Wouldn't Believe"
