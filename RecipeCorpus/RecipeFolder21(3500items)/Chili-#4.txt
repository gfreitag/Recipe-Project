Title: Chili #4
Categories: Chili
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Ground round
1 sm Chopped onion
1/2 ts Chili powder
3/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Catsup
Salt and pepper to taste
Mix all ingredients well. Place in a crockpot or slow cooker. Cook
for 4 hours, stirring often until done. A condiment for hot dogs or
hamburgers.
