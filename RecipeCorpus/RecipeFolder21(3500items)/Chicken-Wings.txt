Title: Chicken Wings
Categories: Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Corn syrup
5 oz Soy sauce
30    Chicken wings
Contributed to the echo by: BOYD NARON CHICKEN WINGS Simmer first 3
ingredients for 30 minutes. Cut wings in half and arrange in 9 x 13
pan. Pour sauce over and bake covered at 250 degrees for 1-1/2 hours.
Uncover and bake another 30 minutes.
