Title: Chicken a la King
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Cajun/creol, Chicken
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Oleo, melted
2 c  Chicken broth
1 ts Salt
2 ts Minced onion
1    Cooked fryer--boned
1 cn Cream mushroom soup
2/3 c  Flour
2 c  Milk
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Bell pepper, diced
2    Pimentos, diced
Fresh mushrooms, sliced
Add flour to melted oleo and stir over low heat.  Add broth and
milk all at once.  Cook and stir until thickened.  Add remaining
ingredients, and heat.  Serve on toast, rice or in patty shells.
