Title: Domatosoupa Me Spaghetti
Categories: Soup/stew, Greek, Vegetarian
Yield: 2 servings
455 g  Tinned tomatoes, sieved
2    Celery sticks, chopped
1    Onion, chopped
1    Large carrot, chopped
1    Medium potato, diced
60 ml Olive oil
Sea Salt
Black pepper
22 g  Wholemeal spaghetti 1" bits
Put the tomatoes in a saucepan Add 1.8l of water Add the celery,
onion, carrot, oil, salt and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes then add
the spaghetti Serve when the spaghetti is cooked (about 10-15 minutes)
