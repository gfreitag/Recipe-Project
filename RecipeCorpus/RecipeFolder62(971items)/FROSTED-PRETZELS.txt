Title: FROSTED PRETZELS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 84 Servings
1    Recipe
Heat contents of 1 tub Creamy Deluxe vanilla or chocolate
ready-to-spread frosting in 1 qt. saucepan over low heat until
melted. Keep frosting over low heat, stirring occasionally; dip small
pretzel twists into frosting to coat.  Place on waxed paper; let
stand until dry, about 8 hours.
