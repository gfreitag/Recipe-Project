Title: FRIENDSHIP TEA MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
18 oz Tang orange powder drink
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Sweetened lemonade mix
1/2 c  Instant tea
3 oz Apricot flavor Jello
2 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Ground cloves
Water -- boiling
Combine all ingredients, except boiling water, in a large bowl,
stirring well. Store mix in an airtight container. To serve, place
1-1/2 tbls mixture in a cup. Add 1 cup boiling water. Makes about 50
servings.
