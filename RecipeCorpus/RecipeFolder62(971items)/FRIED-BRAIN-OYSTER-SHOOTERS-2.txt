Title: FRIED-BRAIN OYSTER SHOOTERS 2
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Seafood, Weird, New (to be
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Vodka
1    Shucked Oyster
Tabasco
Cocktail Sauce

cocktail sauce with a few drops of tabasco. Bottoms up!
