Title: FRESCO MARINATED CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Lipton Recipe Secrets Savory
-Herb with Garlic Soup Mix
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Olive or vegetable oil
1 ts Lemon juice or vinegar
1 lb Boneless skinless chicken
-breast halves
In non aluminum baking dish, blend first four ingredients; add
chicken and turn to coat. Cover and marinate in refrigerator at least
1 hour or overnight. On a broiler pan or grill lined with foil
sprayed with no stick cooking spray, arrange chicken, pour 1/2 of
marinade over chicken. Grill or broil, turning once and pouring
remaining marinade over chicken, until done.
