Title: FROSTED CHOCOLATE DROPS
Categories: Cookies, Drop
Yield: 48 Cookies
1/2 c  Butter or margarine; soft
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
1    Egg
3 oz Unsweetened chocolate
-- melted and cooled
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Quaker Oats, uncooked
-- (quick or old-fashioned)
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
2 tb Butter; soft
3 c  Sifted confectioners' sugar
1 ts Vanilla
3 tb Milk
MMMMM-----------------------FOR DECORATION----------------------------
Pecan halves
Beat butter and sugars together until creamy.  Blend in egg and melted
chocolate.  Sift together flour, soda and salt. Add to creamed mixture
alternately with sour cream and milk. Stir in oats. Drop by
teaspoonfuls onto lightly greased cooky sheets.  Bake in preheated
moderate oven (375 F.) 10 to 12 minutes; cool.

For frosting, combine all ingredients.  Beat until smooth and of
spreading consistency.  Frost cooled cookies. Top each cooky with a
pecan half.
