Title: FRENCH HONEY BREAD
Categories: Breads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/8 c  Water
1 tb Honey
1 tb Olive oil
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
3 c  Bread flour
1 pk Yeast
I made this in the bread machine,rolled it out and let rise for
about 20 min.,put on sauce and toppings then baked at 350 for 20 min.
If using soy cheese,add the last 5 min. This makes excellent pizza
dough.
