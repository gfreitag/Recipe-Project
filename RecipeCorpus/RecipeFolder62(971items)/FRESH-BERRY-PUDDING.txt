Title: FRESH BERRY PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
1 sm Banana
1 c  Sliced strawberries
1/2 c  Raspberries
1 tb Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Plain yogurt
1/2 c  Blueberries
In food processor or blender, puree together banana, strawberries,
raspberries and sugar until smooth; transfer to bowl. Fold in yogurt.

Spoon into serving dishes; sprinkle with blueberries. Cover and
refrigerate for at least 1 hour or until chilled. Makes 4 servings
