Title: FRIED RICE 3
Categories: Oriental, Rice
Yield: 6 Servings
8    Bacon slices, cut into piece
1 c  Carrot, shredded or finely c
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Celery
1/2 c  Peas, cooked
3 c  Rice, cold, cooked
3 ts Soy sauce
1/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Green onion, chopped
Put bacon, carrot, onion and celery into frying pan. Saute until
onion is soft and clear. If it appeard dry, add some cooking oil, 1
tbsp at a time. Add green onion, Saute another 1 or 2 minutes. Add
remaining ingredients. Saute until heated through.  Check for salt.
If you are not using soy sauce, you may need more salt.
