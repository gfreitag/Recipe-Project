Title: FRIED SHREDDED CARROTS
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 2 Servings
2 tb Oil
2    Garlic cloves
4    Carrots, shredded
2 tb Tamari
4 tb Nutritional yeast
1/4 ts Fresh ginger, minced
Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.  Saute garlic for 1 minute.
Stir in carrots & remaining ingredients. Stir fry for a few minutes,
until carrots are just tender. Serve.
