Title: FRIED EGG
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  MIlk
2 tb Buttermilk
4    Peach Halves
Put milk in jar and add the 2 T buttermilk. Stir well and set aside
for 24 hours.It will thicken.To make dessert put a couple of
spoonfuls onto a plate and flatten a bit. Put a peach half on top of
this, thus the name of fried egg.
