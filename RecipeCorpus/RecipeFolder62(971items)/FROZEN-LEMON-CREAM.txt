Title: FROZEN LEMON CREAM
Categories: Desserts
1/2 c sugar 2 lemons - peel
Yield: 10 Servings  2 c nonfat milk
finely grated & 1/2 c juice

Combine milk, sugar, lemon juice and peel and stir until sugar is
dissolved.  Pour into shallow pan and freeze. Spoon frozen mixture
into processor and mix until smooth. Return to freezer until
partially frozen & serve. ~--
