Title: FROZEN WHITE RELISH
Categories: Relishes, Condiments
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Heavy cream
1 c  Prepared horseradish
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Minced parsley
1 tb Chopped pimiento
Whip cream until stiff.  Fold in other ingredients. Place in
freezer and freeze, stirring once during process. Serve with baked
fish or ham.

Recipe from : Mrs. Alex Retzlaf
