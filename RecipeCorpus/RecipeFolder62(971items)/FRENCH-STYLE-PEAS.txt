Title: FRENCH-STYLE PEAS
Categories: French, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 6 Servings
3    Leaves Lettuce
1 tb Butter
18 oz Peas -- Frozen
1/2 ts Salt And Pepper
1/4 ts Sugar
1/8 ts Pepper
In large skillet with tight-fitting lid melt 1 tbsp. butter Spread 2
or 3 lettuce leaves in skillet.  Add 18 oz. peas. Dot with 3 Tbsp.
butter sprinkle with 1/2 tsp. salt 1/4 tsp. sugar and 1/8 tsp.
pepper. Cook over low heat 30 min. til tender.
