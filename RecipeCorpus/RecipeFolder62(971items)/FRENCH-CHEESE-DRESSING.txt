Title: FRENCH-CHEESE DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads, Cheese, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Salad Oil
1/3 c  White Wine Vinegar
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Paprika
1 ds Pepper
1/2 md Garlic Clove
1/4 sm Onion
3 oz Blue Cheese
Put all of the ingredients, except the blue cheese, into a blender
container and blend until the onion is finely chopped. Crumble the
blue cheese into the mixture and blend until the cheese is chopped.
Store in the refrigerator.  Shake the salad dressing mixture before
serving over the salad.

Makes 2 cups.
