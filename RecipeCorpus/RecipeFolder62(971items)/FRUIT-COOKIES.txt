Title: FRUIT COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Lard; or butter
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Milk; sour
1 ts Baking soda; in milk
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 lb Raisins
1 c  Nuts
Knead like bread until stiff enough not to stick to board. Roll thin.
Cut and bake.

Note: No temperature given. Assume a 425 F. oven.

Source: Bloom Community Grange, Seneca County, OH
