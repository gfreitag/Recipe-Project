Title: FRENCH TOAST WITH FRESH BLUEBERRIES
Categories: New, Text, Import
3    eggs
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ts vanilla
Water
Cinnamon, to taste
:
:
2 TB butter
1    loaf white bread or challah
cut into 1-inch slices
:
2 c  fresh blueberries, --
rinsed
Powdered sugar
Maple syrup, warmed in
microwave
:
:
:
:

In a shallow dish beat together the eggs, vanilla, splash of water,
and cinnamon. In a non-stick skillet or griddle melt butter over
moderate heat. Dip bread slices into egg mixture and transfer to hot
skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown.
Transfer French toast to a plate, sprinkle with blueberries and
powdered sugar. Serve with warm maple syrup.

Yield: 4 servings
