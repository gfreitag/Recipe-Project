Title: FRESH STRAWBERRY LAYER PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1 lb Confectioners' sugar
2    Eggs
1/2 pt Whipping cream, whipped or
8 oz Cool Whip
1 qt Strawberries
1 lb Vanilla wafers
Cream together first 3 ingredients.  Roll vanilla wafers into not too
fine crumbs.  Place 1/2 in bottom of pan (9x13). Cover with layer of
creamed butter, sugar and eggs. Then cover with strawberries whole or
sliced. Cover with whipped cream - then top with rest of vanilla
crumbs. Garnish with whole berries and chill. May be chilled
overnight.
