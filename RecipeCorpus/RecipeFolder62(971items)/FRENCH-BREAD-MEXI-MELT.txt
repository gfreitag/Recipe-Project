Title: FRENCH BREAD MEXI-MELT
Categories: Mexican, Hamburger, French, Beef
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Loaf of French Bread, split
- lengthwise
1 lb Ground beef
1 pk Taco seasoning
2    Tomatoes, slice
2    Onions, slice
8 sl American Cheese, halved
- triangles
1 cn Tomato sauce
Brown beef, add tomato sauce, and taco seasoning. Simmer 5 to 20 min.
Spread mixture on bread.  Top with layer of sliced tomatos and
cheese. Bake at 375 for 8 ~ 12 min. until cheese melts. Cut into
pieces to serve. This can be broiled if you like it crispier.
