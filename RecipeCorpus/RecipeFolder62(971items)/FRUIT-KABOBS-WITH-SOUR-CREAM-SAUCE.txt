Title: FRUIT KABOBS WITH SOUR CREAM SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 4 Servings
3/4 c  Cantaloupe cubes
3/4 c  Sliced bananas
3/4 c  Whole strawberries
3/4 c  Pineapple chunks
3/4 c  Peach chunks
3/4 c  Nonfat plain yogurt
1/4 c  Sour cream
2 ts Freshly grated nutmeg
1 tb Maple syrup
1 tb Honey
Fresh mint leaves -- for
Garnish
1. Skewer chunks of fruit onto 12 bamboo or metal shish kabob skewers,
alternating colors and types of fruit. Arrange on a platter or in a
flat basket.

2. Mix together yogurt, sour cream, nutmeg, maple syrup, and honey,
and pour into a small serving bowl. Garnish with mint leaves. Serve
sauce with fruit kabobs.

Makes 1 dozen skewers.
