Title: FROZEN BANANA SMOOTHIE
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Frozen bananas
Vanilla extract
Sliced seasonal fruit
Cut frozen banana into 4 pieces and cut away peel and discard. In
blender, blend banana until thick and smooth. Add a dash of vanilla
extract and blend again. Scoop out and serve with fresh sliced
seasonal fruit.
