Title: FROSTING CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Box Duncan Hines Yellow cake
Mix
4    Eggs
1 c  Oil
1 c  Water
1 cn Pillsbury Creamy Deluxe
Coconut Pecan Frosting
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
Mix cake mix, eggs, oil, & water well.  Add pecans & mix well. Spray &
flour bundt cake pan, & bake on top rack of oven at 350 degrees for
45 to 60 min.  Remove from pan while hot.
