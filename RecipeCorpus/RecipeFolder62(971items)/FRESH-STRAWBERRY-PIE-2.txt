Title: FRESH STRAWBERRY PIE 2
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Fresh strawberries, washed
1 1/2 c  Water
4 tb Corn starch
And stemmed.
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Red food coloring.
In a glass mixing bowl, mash up strawberries.  Add and mix well
together; water, sugar, corn starch, and food coloring. Microwave on
high for 5 to 6 min. or until thickened & clear, stirring often. Then
stir in 2 c. whole fresh strawberries, pour into prepared pie crust
and chill for 2 hours, or until set. Serve with whipped cream or ice
cream.
