Title: FRITO PIE
Categories: Beef, Pies
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1    Chopped onion
1    Green pepper
1/2 lb Velveeta cheese
1 cn Chili beans
1 cn Tomato soup
1    Jar taco sauce
1 sm Bag Fritos
Brown hamburger, onion and green pepper; drain.  Add 1/4 lb cheese.
Stir until melted.  Remove from stove. Add chili beans, soup, taco
sauce and crushed Fritos. Put in 11 x13 pan. Top with remainder of
cheese. Bake at 350 until cheese melts through.  A package of chili
mix or taco mix may be added for additional seasoning.
