Title: FRENCH CHOCOLATE PRALINE
Categories: Sauces, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
-Waldine Van Geffen VGHC42A
1    Recipe of French Praline
-Topping
4 oz Semi-sweet chocolate
1 1/2 c  Heavy cream
Melt the chocolate in the cream, stirring until smooth. Add the
praline at the last moment or sprinkle on top of each serving.
