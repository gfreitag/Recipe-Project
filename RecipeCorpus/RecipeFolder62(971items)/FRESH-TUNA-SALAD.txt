Title: FRESH TUNA SALAD
Categories: Seafood, Salads, Fish, Wrv
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
2 lb Fresh tune
1 1/2 ts Liquid crab boil
6    Eggs; hard-boil, chop
1 c  Onion; chop fine
1/2 c  Celery; chop fine
1/2 c  Dill pickle relish
Mayonnaise
Creole seasoning; to taste
Boil tuna in a pot of water seasoned with crab boil until it flakes
apart. Remove from heat and soak for 15 to 20 minutes. Drain, cool
and break into small pieces. Mix tuna with all ingredients to desired
consistency. Serve on sandwich or with crackers. Source:
Times-Picayune, N.O.LA
