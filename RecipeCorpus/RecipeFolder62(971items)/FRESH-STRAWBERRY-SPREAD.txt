Title: FRESH STRAWBERRY SPREAD
Categories: Desserts, Low-cal, Genie
Yield: 2 Servings
-Press Enterprise
2 pt Fresh, ripe strawberries
2 tb Water
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Granulated gelatin
1/4 c  Cold water
6    Packets of artificial
-sweetener, equivalent to
-12 tsp of sugar
Measure 2 cups of berries, wash and cut into small, bite-sized
pieces. Put berry bits in saucepan with 2 TAB water and lemon juice.
Partially crush berries. Bring to a boil. Stir and cook rapidly about
5 minutes until berries are just cooked. Meanwhile, soak gelatin in
the cold water. Remove berries from heat. Add gelatin and atificial
sweetener; stir to disolve and blend. Remove any foam from surface
and discard. Pour into hot, small jars; cover lightly and cool. When
completely cooled, cover tightly and store in refrigerator. 2-1/4
cups.
