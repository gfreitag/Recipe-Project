Title: FRENCH CREAM PIE
Categories: Desserts, Heirloom
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  White sugar
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Sour cream
1 ts Flour
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1    Egg, beaten
Mix first five ingredients together, add beaten egg. Cook in double
boiler till thick, remove from stove. Add 1 tsp. soda. Spread in a
baked pie shell.  Chill. Cover with whipped cream. Source: Heritage
Recipes ch.
