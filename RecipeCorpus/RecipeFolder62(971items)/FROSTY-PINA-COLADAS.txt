Title: FROSTY PINA COLADAS
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
9 oz Cream of coconut
8 oz Crushed pineapple; undrained
1/4 c  Light rum
1/4 c  Milk
1 tb Powdered sugar
Ice cubes
Combine all ingredients, except ice cubes, in container of electric
blender; process until smooth. Gradually add enough ice to make
mixture measure 4 to 5 cups in blender; process until mixture is
smooth and thickened.
