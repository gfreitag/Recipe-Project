Title: FROZEN CHOCOLATE MOUSSE
Categories: Chocolate, Mousses
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Margarine -- or butter
2 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa
4    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
Graham cracker crumbs
Beat together margarine and sugar and cocoa.  Beat in eggs and
vanilla. Beat well. Mixture may look curdled.

Put a layer of crumbs in the bottom of an 8" square pan. Spread
chocolate mixture over.  Top with more crumbs. Freeze,

Cut into rectangles to serve.
