Title: FRUIT GOOPS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
5 qt Popped popcorn
2 c  Sugar
1    (6 oz) can frozen grape
-juice concentrate
3/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1 ts Vinegar
1/2 ts Slat
Preheat oven 250.  Place popcorn in a large 4 inch butttred baking
pan. Keep warm in oven.  Combine all ingredients except popcorn in a
large saucepan. Bring to a boil. Lower head and cook until mixture
reaches 250 on a candy thermometer. Mixture will bubble so watch
closely to keep it from boiling over.Remove popcorn from oven and
pour syrup
