Title: FRESH GRAPEFRUIT COTTAGE BREAD:::GWHP32A
Categories: Breads, Dak
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Better for Bread flour
1/4 c  Sugar
1/8 ts Baking soda
1 tb Grapefruit peel;grated
1/2 c  Low-fat cottage cheese
2 ea Egg whites
1 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Grapefruit juice;fresh/heat
Add all ingredients into the pan in the order listed, set darkness
control at 11 o'clock.Select white bread and push "Start." This
palate-refreshing mealtime accompaniment, this tart/sweet bread
offers a taste of English elegance when toasted slices are topped
with citrus marmalade. Recipe shared by Barb Day
