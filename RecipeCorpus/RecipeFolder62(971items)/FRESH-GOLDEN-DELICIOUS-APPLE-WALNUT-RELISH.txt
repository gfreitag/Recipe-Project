Title: FRESH GOLDEN DELICIOUS APPLE-WALNUT RELISH
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Shelled walnuts
2    Golden delicious apples,
-peeled, cored and diced
1/2 c  Sweet wine
Preheat oven to 350F. Place walnuts in a pan and toast for 5 minutes
until dark brown and aromatic. Cool and chop. Combine all
ingredients. Best when served the next day.

From the cookbook "Apples" by Robert Berkley
