Title: FRESH TOMATO SALSA (SALSA DE JITOMATE CRUDA)
Categories: Relishes, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 md Tomatoes, diced
1 md White onion, diced
8    Green onions, finely chopped
12    Sprigs cilantro, finely
- chopped
3 to 6 chilies serranos  OR
- 2 jalapenos, minced
Salt to taste
1/3 c  Grapefruit or lime juice
1 md Avocado, diced
1 ts Oregano, crumbled
1/2 c  Cilantro, chopped
Combine all ingredients.  If prepared ahead of time, combine all the
ingredients except the avocado, which should be added just before
serving.
