Title: FRENCH TOASTED TUNA SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches, Fish
Yield: 6 Sandwiches
9 1/4 oz Canned tuna
1/4 c  Celery, finely chopped
1/4 c  Onion, finely chopped
1/4 c  Sweet pickle relish
1/4 c  Salad dressing (mayonnaise)
12 sl Bread, dry
2    Eggs; beaten
1/3 c  Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
2 tb Oil or fat (approximately)
Drain and flake tuna.  Mix with celery, onion, relish, and salad
dressing. Spread tuna mixture on 6 slices of bread; top with
remaining slices. Mix eggs, milk, and vanilla. Dip sandwiches into
egg mixture to coat each side. Brown in fat on a hot griddle or in a
frypan, about 3 to 4 minutes on each side.

Calories per sandwich: About 360
