Title: FRIED PEA FRITTERS
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Field peas (cooked)
1 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Curry powder
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Milk
Mix all dry ingredients.  Beat eggs and milk.  Add to the flour
mixture. Gently stir in cooked peas.  Drop from spoon into 3/4 inch
hot fat. Fry until light brown. Serves 4 to 5.
