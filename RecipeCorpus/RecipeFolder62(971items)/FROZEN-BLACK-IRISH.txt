Title: FROZEN BLACK IRISH
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Vodka
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Part Coffee Liqueur
1    Part Irish Cream
1    Part Vodka
1    To 2 Scoops Chocolate Ice
-Cream
Mix ingredients in a blender and then add ice.  Blend until smooth.
Yields a mocha milk shake that sneaks up on you. Very rich.
