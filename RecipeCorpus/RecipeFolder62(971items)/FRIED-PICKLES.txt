Title: FRIED PICKLES
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM---------------------SEASONED EGG WASH--------------------------
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
1/3 c  Milk
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/3 c  Worcestershire Sauce
1/3 ts Tabasco
MMMMM-----------------------SEASONED FLOUR----------------------------
2 c  Flour
1/2 tb Paprika
1 tb Garlic salt
1/4 ts Poultry seasoning
1/2 tb Black pepper
Oil for deep frying
Klauson Whole Pickles
Whip together all ingredients for seasoned egg wash, set aside. Mix
ingredients for flour mixture in a shallow dish. Slice pickle 1/8"
thick.
Dip into flour mix, then egg wash, then flour mix again. Fry until
golden brown, about 1 minute.  Drain on paper towels. Serve with
dipping sauce of ranch dressing, ketchup or horseradish sauce.
