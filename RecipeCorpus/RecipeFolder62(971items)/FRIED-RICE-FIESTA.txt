Title: FRIED RICE FIESTA
Categories: Vegetarian, Side dish
Yield: 6 Servings
4    Radishes, trimmed, sliced
1 ts Rice wine vinegar
3    Cloves garlic, minced
1 ts Sesame oil
4    Scallions
4 oz Mushrooms, thinly sliced
3 c  Cooked brown rice
3 oz Frozen peas, defrosted
2 oz Water chestnuts, sliced
1 tb Soy sauce
Cook rice according to package directions. Combine radishes with rice
wine vinegar in a small bowl and reserve. Place garlic and sesame oil
in a saucepan. Add scallions and mushrooms and saute until tender.
Add brown rice, radishes and remaining ingredients. Combine. Serve
hot. Serves 6.
