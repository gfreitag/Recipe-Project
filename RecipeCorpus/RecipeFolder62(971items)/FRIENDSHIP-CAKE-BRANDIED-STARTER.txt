Title: FRIENDSHIP CAKE BRANDIED STARTER
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Pineapple; canned -- cubed
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Brandy
1 c  Maraschino cherries
And juice
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Brandy
1 c  Peaches; and juice --
Sliced
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Brandy
In a large one gallon jar, combine pineapple, 1 cup sugar and 2
tablespoons brandy.  Let these ingredients sit in the jar for two
weeks and stir daily. At the end of two weeks, add cherries, 1 cup
sugar and 2 tablespoons brandy. Let the mixture sit for two more
weeks, stirring daily. The fourth week, add peaches, 1 cup sugar and
2 tablespoons brandy. Let the mixture sit for two weeks, stirring
daily. Separate the liquid from the fruit. The liquid is your starter
and you can use the fruit on ice cream or cake. Now you are ready to
prepare the fruit for the cake. Do not refrigerate the starter liquid.
