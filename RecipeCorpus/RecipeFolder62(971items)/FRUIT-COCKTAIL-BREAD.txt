Title: FRUIT COCKTAIL BREAD
Categories: Breads, Machine
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 pk Yeast
1/2 ts Ginger
3 1/4 c  Bread flour
2 tb Gluten
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Macadamia nuts/halved pecans
1 tb Honey
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Grated tangerine rind
15 1/4 oz Dole Tropical Fruit Salad
Remove 2 T. of fruit salad juice.  Warm remaining fruit salad and
juice. Add ingredients in order listed at room temperature. Select
white bread and push start.
