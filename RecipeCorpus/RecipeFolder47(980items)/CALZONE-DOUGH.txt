Title: CALZONE DOUGH
Categories: Today's, Imports
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2    Yeast -- pkg.
4 c  Flour -- white
1/8 ts Salt
2 tb Olive Oil
1 1/2 c  Water
Fit metal blade into food processor; add all dry ingredients and
olive oil. Slowly add water while processing on KNEAD, till dough
forms a ball. Place dough in an oiled bowl and let rise 1 - 1 1/2
hours, covered. Fill with Calzone Filling and bake at 400F for 30
minutes.
