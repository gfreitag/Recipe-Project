Title: CAKE IN ICE CREAM CONES: HELOISE'S EDIBLE CUP
Categories: Cakes, Kids
Yield: 24 Servings
1 pk Cake mix
24 ea Flat-bottom ice-cream cones
See note
Make the cake mix batter as directed on the box. Put flat-bottomed
ice-cream cones in muffin tins. Fill each cone about half-way with
the mix. Bake the cupcakes according to t he directions on the box.
When done, let them cool, then add your favorite frosting. Jazz them
up with sprinkles, jimmies, gumdrops, cherries . . . Note: check box
for the number of cupcakes your mix normally makes.

: Heloise (1996)
