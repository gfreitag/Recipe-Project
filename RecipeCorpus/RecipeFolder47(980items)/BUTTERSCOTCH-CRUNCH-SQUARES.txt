Title: BUTTERSCOTCH CRUNCH SQUARES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Pan
1 c  Flour
1/4 c  Quaker oats
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Nuts
1    12oz jar butterscotch OR
Caramel topping
1 qt Ice cream
Combine flour, oats and brown sugar, cut in butter until it is like
coarse crumbs.  Stir in nuts, pat into 9x13 inch pan and bake 15
minutes at 400 degrees. Stir while still warm to crumble. Cool.
Spread half in 8 inch pan. Drizzle 1/2 topping over crumbs. Spoon
softened ice cream into pan, drizzle remaining topping, sprinkle
remaining crumbs..freeze.
