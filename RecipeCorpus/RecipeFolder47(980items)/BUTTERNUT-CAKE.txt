Title: BUTTERNUT CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
2 x  Sticks Butter
3 c  Sifted Flour
1 ts Baking Soda
3    Eggs
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Buttermilk
3 ts Butternut Flavoring
1/4 ts Salt
8 oz Cream Cheese
Stick Butter
Box Powdered Sugar
2 ts Butternut Flavoring
1 c  Chopped Nuts
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Grease and flour three 9-inch cake pans of
butter.  Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Mix soda and salt into buttermilk until foamy. Add alternately with
flour. Add 3 teaspoons flavoring.  Pour into prepared pans and bake
until done, 20 to 25 minutes. teaspoons flavoring. Add nuts. Spread
on cooled cake.
