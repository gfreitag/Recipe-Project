Title: CAPPUCINO POUND CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Alcohol
Yield: 12 Servings
3/4 c  Oil
1/2 c  Honey
1 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb Creme de Cacao
1 tb Kahlua
DRY INGREDIENTS:
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Cocoa
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 tb Instant coffee
2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Cloves
COFFEE KAHLUA CREAM GLAZE:
1 c  Cream
1 ts Instant coffee
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Kahlua
Mix all wet ingredients together and beat for 4 minutes with electric
mixer.  Mix all dry ingredients together and add to wet mixture, mix
well. Spread into greased and floured tube pan.  Bake at 325 degrees
F. for 1 hour 10 minutes.

Coffee Kahlua Cream Glaze:  Beat all ingredients until stiff and
spread over cooled cake.
