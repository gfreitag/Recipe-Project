Title: CARAWAY RYE ONION BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Water
1 tb Vegetable Oil
1 tb Honey
2 tb Dried Onion
1 1/4 c  Bread Flour
1/2 c  Whole Wheat Flour
1/4 c  Rye Flour
1 1/2 tb Powdered Milk
3/4 ts Salt
2 ts Caraway Seeds
2 ts Yeast
Put ingredients in breadmaker in order listed, unless using a Welbilt
or DAK (reverse them, in that case).
