Title: CAJUN BARBEQUE BUTTER
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 12 Servings
1 1/2 lb Unsalted butter -- softened
1 tb Cayenne
1 tb Black pepper
2 ts Salt
1 tb Crushed red pepper
1 tb Dried thyme
1 tb Dried basil
1 ts Dried oregano
1 1/2 oz Fresh garlic -- minced
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
NOTE: Use "Cajun Barbeque Butter" to make "Cajun Barbeque Shrimp." See
recipe in this cookbook.

Whip butter until smooth. Add remaining ingredients and blend
completely.

Put into a storage container and chill until needed.

: Travis Henderson II of Newport's Seafood, Dallas, TX
