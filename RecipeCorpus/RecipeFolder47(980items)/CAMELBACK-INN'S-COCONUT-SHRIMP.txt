Title: CAMELBACK INN'S COCONUT SHRIMP
Categories: Seafood, Sauces
Yield: 2 Servings
12    Shrimp, peeled and deveined
4 tb Flour
4    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Coconut, shredded
Oil, for deep-frying
MMMMM-----------------HONEY MUSTARD HORSERADISH----------------------
1 tb Dry mustard, Coleman's
2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Horseradish
Dredge shrimp in flour, then dip them in egg and roll in shredded
coconut. Deep-fry shrimp at 350 degrees until golden brown in color.
Serve with Honey Mustard Horseradish Sauce Sauce directions: Combine
all ingredients and mix together thoroughly.
