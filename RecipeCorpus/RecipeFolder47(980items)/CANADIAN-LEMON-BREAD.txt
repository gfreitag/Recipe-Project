Title: CANADIAN LEMON BREAD
Categories: Breakfast, Breads, Tried
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Lemon (large)
2 ea Eggs, beaten
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Nuts, chopped (optional)
1/4 c  Confectioners' sugar
Cream together butter and sugar until fluffy. Grate peel from lemon
and add to creamed mixture. Squeeze juice from lemon and set aside.
Add eggs, flour, baking powder, salt and milk to creamed mixture and
blend well. Stir in nuts.
Turn batter into greased 9x5-inch loaf pan and bake at 350 degrees one
hour. Combine lemon juice and confectioners' sugar and mix well. Pour
over hot loaf and allow syrup to sink into bread. Turn loaf out onto
a rack and cool.
