Title: CABBAGE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
4 c  Chopped cabbage
1 ea Medium onion, chopped fine
1 pk Chinese noodles, discard fla
1 cn Baby shrimp - drained (optio
1/2 c  Oil
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 x  Dash of soy sauce (optional)
1/4 c  HONEY
1/4 c  Sun flower seeds
Wisk together oil, vinegar, and honey.  Pour over all other
ingredients. Mix well.  Cover and let stand in refrigerator 24 hours.
