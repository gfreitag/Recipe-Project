Title: BUTTERY MELT-A-WAYS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 42 Cookies
1 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  C and H Powdered Sugar
2 1/4 c  Sifted cake flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
C and H Powdered Sugar
Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Blend in
flour, salt, and vanilla.  Drop by teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart on
ungreased cookie sheet. Bake in 375 degree oven until set but not
brown 12 to 15 minutes. Remove to rack and sprinkle with powdered
sugar.
