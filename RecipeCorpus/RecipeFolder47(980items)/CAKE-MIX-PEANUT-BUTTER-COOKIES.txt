Title: CAKE MIX PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Cakes
Yield: 36 Servings
1 ea Yellow or White Cake Mix
1 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Oil (1/3 c works okay)
2 tb Water
2 ea Eggs
Mix well by hand.  Drop by teaspoon 1 inch apart on an ungreased cooky
sheet. Criss cross with a fork dipped in water. Bake 12 to 15 minutes
at 350~.  Remove immediately. Makes 3 to 4 dozen.
