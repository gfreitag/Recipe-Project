Title: CALIFORNIA CALZONE
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Pizza dough
2    Whole avocados
2    Whole Roma tomatoes
1 oz Garlic
2 oz Ricotta cheese
2 oz Chevre cheese
1 oz Provolone cheese
1 oz Spinach
1 pn Parsley
1 pn Thyme
Tabasco sauce -- to taste
Salt -- to taste
Pepper -- to taste
Preheat oven to 500F. Mix all ingredients together and place in a pie
pan. Put into oven and warm. Roll out dough into a circle. Place
mixture on pizza dough. Fold pizza dough into the shape of a half
moon. Seal tightly and prick with a fork. Tim and place on pizza
stone in oven. Bake until golden brown.
