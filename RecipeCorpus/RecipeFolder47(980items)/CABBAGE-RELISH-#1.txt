Title: CABBAGE RELISH #1
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 1 Recipe
-JUDI M. PHELPS
3 lg Cabbage
2 qt Onions; grated
2 qt Vinegar
3 tb Salt
6 tb Mustard
3 tb Turmeric
1 c  Flour
Cook first 7 ingredients for 1 1/2 hours until tender. Mix flour with
water to make a paste.  Then add flour paste to other ingredients.
Stir and cook 10 minutes. Put in sterilized jars and seal. Serve with
hamburgers or hot dogs.
