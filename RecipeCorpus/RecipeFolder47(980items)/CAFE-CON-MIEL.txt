Title: CAFE CON MIEL
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 3 Servings
2 c  Prepared coffee,
1 x  (fresh, instant, or decaf)
1/2 c  Milk
4 tb HONEY, more or less to taste
1/8 ts Cinnamon
1 x  Dash nutmeg or allspice
1 x  Dash vanilla
Heat ingredients in a saucepan, but do not boil.  Stir well to
combine. Serve as a light dessert. From Connecticut Honey by Eastern
Connecticut Beekeepers Association
