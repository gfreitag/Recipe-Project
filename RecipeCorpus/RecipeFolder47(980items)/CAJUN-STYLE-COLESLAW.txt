Title: CAJUN STYLE COLESLAW
Categories: Cajun, Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  Cabbage, shredded
1 c  Carrots, sredded
3/4 c  Salad dressing
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Dried chives
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 ts Paprika
Combine cabbage, carrots, salad dressing, garlic powder, chives and
black pepper in a large mixing bowl. Mix well. Cover and refrigerate
for 30 minutes.  Sprinkle with paprika.

Prep. time: 1 hour
