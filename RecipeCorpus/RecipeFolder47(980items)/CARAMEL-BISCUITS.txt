Title: CARAMEL BISCUITS
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz Butter
1 tb Condensed milk
1 ts Vanilla
2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Coconut
1 3/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
2 c  Cornflakes
Cream butter and sugar and condensed milk.  Add egg and essence and
mix in all dry ingredients.  Put in 3/4 tsp lots on a cold tray. Bake
at 325 - 350 F.  for
15    mins.
