Title: BUTTERSCOTCH PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Butter, melted
3 tb Cornstarch
4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Dissolve 1 cup milk with cornstarch. Add to the melted brown sugar and
butter. Add remainder of milk; cook in double boiler until thick. Add
vanilla and salt.
