Title: CARBONATED BEVERAGE JELLY
Categories: Condiments, Jams
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Carbonated beverage
3 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Water
1/2    Bottle fruit pectin
Combine sugar, beverage, and water.  Mix.  Heat rapidly to boiling.
Add fruit pectin at once.  Stir constantly before and while boiling.
Heat to full rolling boil. Boil hard 1/2 minute. Remove from fire.
Skim. The Household Searchlight
