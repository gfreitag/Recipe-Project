Title: BUTTERSCOTCH SAUCE ALA CYBEREALM
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
8 oz Sugar
1/2 c  Cream
1 1/16 oz Butter
2 tb Maplesyrup
1. Mix sugar, cream,butter and syrup and on medium heat bring to a
boil,
stirring often. 2. Cool and serve with pudding or icecream.
