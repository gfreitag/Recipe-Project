Title: CANNON'S TWO-BOWL ICING
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 10 Servings
1 tb Meringue powder
1/2 c  Water
3/4 c  Granulated sugar
In first bowl, beat meringue powder and water together until
fluffy. Then add the granulated sugar. Beat together til well
blended. In a second bowl, cream together: 1 lb Crisco or Alpine
shortening 2 lb Confectioner's sugar 2 T flavoring 1 T water
(optional)
Pinch of salt (optional)
Slowly add the first bowl to the second bowl, make sure to scrape
the bowl often. Never needs refrigerated. Source: newsletter Jan '90
and Cannons Dolores, Ohio 04/29 07:24 pm
