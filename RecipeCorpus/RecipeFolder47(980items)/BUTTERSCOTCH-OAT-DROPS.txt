Title: BUTTERSCOTCH OAT DROPS
Categories: Cookies, Posted to m
Yield: 6 Dozen
1 c  Butter or marg; softened
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown sugar; packed
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
2 c  Rolled oats
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
2 c  Butterscotch chips
1 c  Chopped nuts
1.  Cream together butter and both sugars.  Beat in eggs 1 at a time.
Add vanilla.

2.  Measure in remaining ingredients and mix well.  Drop by spoonfuls
onto ungreased baking sheet.  Bake at 350F for 8-10 mins.
