Title: CANLIS SHRIMP
Categories: Main dish, Shellfish, Cajun, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Large shrimp, shelled
1 oz Butter
1 oz Olive oil
1/4 ts Fresh groun pepper
1/4 ts Salt
2 oz Dry vermouth
Juice of 2 lemons
1    Small garlic clove, crushed
Into large skillet, place olive oil.  When simmering, add shrimp;
allow to cook until golden brown. Reduce heat; add butter, salt
pepper. When you think you have too much salt, add more.  This is one
dish you cannot oversalt.  When well blended, raise heat to very hot.
Add lemon juice and dry vermouth and cook for about 1 minute,
constantly stirring. Courtesy Telephone Pioneers
