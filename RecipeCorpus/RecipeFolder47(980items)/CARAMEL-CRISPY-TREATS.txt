Title: CARAMEL CRISPY TREATS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Butter
2 1/2 c  Marshmallows;miniature
1 cn Chow mein noodles; 4 oz or
-113 g.
12    Caramels
1 tb -Cold water
2 tb Peanut butter; smooth or
-crunchy
Melt butter with marshmallows over low heat or in microwave at Medium
for 2 minutes. Stir until smooth. Add noodles; toss until well
coated. Drop spoonfuls of noodle mixture onto greased baking sheet.
Melt caramels with water over low heat or in microwave at Low for 2
minutes. Stir until smooth. Add peanut butter; mix well. Drizzle over
treats. Chill. MAKES:
24    TREATS
