Title: CANDIED TOMATOES
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
Stephen Ceideburg
28 oz Can tomatoes
2 c  Cubed white bread
2 tb Brown or granulated sugar
2 tb Cider vinegar
1 ts Dried basil, optional
Tabasco sauce or pepper, to
-taste
2 ts Butter or margarine
Cut whole tomatoes into pieces, combine with bread cubes, sugar,
vinegar, basil and Tabasco or pepper in 6-cup casserole. Dot with
butter and bake in 400-degree oven until bubbling hot throughout and
lightly browned, 30 to 45 minutes. Or, bring combined ingredients to
a boil in a saucepan on the stovetop and lower heat and simmer. Cook
uncovered on low heat until slightly thickened, about 20 minutes.

Per serving: 80 calories, 2 grams protein, 15 grams carbohydrate, 2
grams fat, 3 milligrams cholesterol,
246    milligrams sodium.

Francis Price writing in the Oregonian's FOODday, 1/12/93.
