Title: BUTTERSCOTCH SUPREME
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
Crust:
1 c  Flour, all-purpose
1/4 lb Butter
1 c  Pecans -- Finely Chopped
Cheese Layer:
8 oz Cream Cheese -- Softened
1 c  Cool Whip
1 c  Confectioner's sugar
Butterscotch Layer:
2 lg (or 4 small) Butterscotch
Instant
Pudding mix
3 c  Milk, Cold
Topping:
Remainder lg Container Cool
-Whip
Crust: Mix and press in 9x12 pan. Bake 15 minutes at
300    deg. Let cool.

Cheese Layer: Blend well and spread over cooled crust.

Butterscotch Layer: Mix well and spread over Cheese Layer.

Topping: Spread remainder of 1 large cool whip over last layer and
sprinkle with finely chopped nuts.

Keep refrigerated or on ice in cooler for a picnic!
