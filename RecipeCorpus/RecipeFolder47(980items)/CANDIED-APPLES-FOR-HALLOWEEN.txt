Title: CANDIED APPLES FOR HALLOWEEN
Categories: Candies
Yield: 10 Servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Vinegar
2/3 c  Water
1 pk Cherry Kool-Aid
10    Apple
Mix sugar, vinegar and water. Boil until mixture reaches 300-degrees;
cool. Stir in Kool-Aid. Put apples on sticks and dip into mixture
quickly. Yield:
10    Candied Apples.
