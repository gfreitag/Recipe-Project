Title: CANDY JELLO SALAD
Categories: Desserts, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Cinnamon candy pieces
1 sm Raspberry jello
1 c  Boiling water
3/4 c  Ice cold water
1 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Chopped Apples
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
Add 1 c boiling water to contents of jello.  Add cinnamon candies and
stir until dissolved.  Add cold water, chopped celery, chopped
apples, and nuts. Refrigerate until set.
