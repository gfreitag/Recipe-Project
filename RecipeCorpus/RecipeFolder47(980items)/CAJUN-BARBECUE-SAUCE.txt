Title: CAJUN BARBECUE SAUCE
Categories: Cajun, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 tb Paprika
4 tb Light brown sugar
1 md Onion -- minced
1 c  Tomato puree
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Boiling water
2 ds Worcestershire sauce
Combine salt, pepper, paprika, brown sugar, onion, tomato puree,
butter, water and Worcestershire sauce in a saucepan. Heat to
boiling. Reduce heat and simmer for 2 hours.
