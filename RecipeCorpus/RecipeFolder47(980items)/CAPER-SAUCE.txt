Title: CAPER SAUCE
Categories: Beef, Sauces, Vegetables
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Capers *
1/4 c  Dairy Sour Cream
1 tb Dijon-Style Mustard
1 tb Horseradish
1 tb White Vinegar
1/2 ts Sugar
Chopped Dill Pickle can be substituted for Capers.
*
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Combine mayonnaise, capers, sour cream, mustard,
horseradish, vinegar and sugar.  Mix well. Serve with Beef With Caper
Sauce or with any roast beef.
