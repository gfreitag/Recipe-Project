Title: CAFE REMY
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 1 Serving*
Amaretto coffee beans
1 tb Vanilla extract
1 ts Almond extract
1 ts Cocoa powder
1 ts Sugar
*  No serving size was provided in the recipe, however, I would
estimate it to be at least 8 to 12 servings.

Brew coffee.  Add flavorings, 1 tsp. chocolate and sugar per cup.
Garnish with whipped cream, chocolate and red candy sprinkles, and a
chocolate- covered strawberry on top.
