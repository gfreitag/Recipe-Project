Title: HARISSA SAUCE #2 (FRESH)
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 12 Servings
20    Whole red chili peppers --
Fresh
4    Whole plum tomatoes
2    Cloves garlic
1 ts Ground cumin
1 ts Coriander
4 ts Red cayenne pepper --
Optional
Vinegar
remove stem and seeds from peppers. Put everything into blender or
Mortar & pestle and process till smooth. add vinegar to reach desire
consistancy.
