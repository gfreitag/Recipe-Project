Title: HAM & CHEESE BALLS]
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 30 Servings
1/4 lb Finely ground ham
1/4 lb Cream cheese
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Catsup
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Salt
Blend ingredients thoroughly and form into tiny balls. Roll in chopped
chives. Serve speared on tooth picks. Makes about 30 balls.
