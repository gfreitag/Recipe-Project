Title: HAM~ BROCCOLI~ & VIDALIA ONION QUICHE
Categories: Quiche
Yield: 2 Quiches
-Sue Woodward
MMMMM----------------VIDALIA SWEET ONION COOKBOOK---------------------
2    Pie crusts; unbaked
1 c  Ham; cooked, chopped
2    Eggs; beaten
3/4 c  Cheddar cheese; grated
3/4 c  Swiss cheese; grated or thin
-slices
1 pk 10-oz. froz. choppd broccoli
Vidalia onion; chopped, to
-taste
1 sm Can chopped mushrooms;
-drained (or small jar)
1/3 c  Milk
Salt
Pepper
Cook broccoli accdg. to directions, drain and pour in bowl. Add
remaining ingredients except pie crusts. Mix well. Pour into both pie
crusts. Bake at 375~ for about 50 mins.
