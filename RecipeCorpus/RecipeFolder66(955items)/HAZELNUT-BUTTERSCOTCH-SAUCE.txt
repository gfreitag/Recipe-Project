Title: HAZELNUT BUTTERSCOTCH SAUCE
Categories: Desserts, Sauces
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 c  Dark brown sugar
-(firmly packed)
1 ds Salt
1/3 c  Milk
2 tb Light corn syrup
3 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Oregon hazelnuts
-roasted & coarsely chopped
1 ts Vanilla
Yield: about 1-1/3 cups.

Combine sugar, salt, milk, corn syrup and butter in saucepan. Bring
to a boil, stirring constantly; cook about 5 minutes, or until
thickened. Remove from heat and add hazelnuts and vanilla. Serve warm
over ice cream.
