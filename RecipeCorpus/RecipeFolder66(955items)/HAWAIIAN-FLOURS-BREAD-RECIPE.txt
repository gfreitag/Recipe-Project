Title: HAWAIIAN FLOURS BREAD RECIPE
Categories: Breadmach, Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 c  White cake mix (no pudding)
1/2 c  Whole wheat bread flour
1 c  White bread flour
1 c  Regular unbleached flour
2 ts Yeast
2 tb Margarine
3 tb Gluten flour
1 tb Dough Conditioner (optional)
3/4 tb Sugar
1 1/4 c  Water or milk
6 tb Nine grain mix (optional but
-recommended)
This rises better than the stock recipe, is a bit more firm, and is
great for French Toast!
