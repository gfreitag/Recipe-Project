Title: HAM BAKE AMSTERDAM
Categories: Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 c  Diced cooked ham
2 tb Chopped onion
1/8 ts Tarragon
2 tb Margarine
10 1/2 oz Can condensed cream of
-chicken soup
1/2 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Cooked noodles
1/2 c  Cooked & sliced green beans
2 tb Fine dry bread crumbs
1/2 sm Clove garlic, minced
1/4 ts Paprika
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Butter a 1 quart baking dish. Saute ham,
onion and tarragon in 1 tablespoon margarine until ham is browned and
onion is tender. Stir in soup, water, noodles and green beans. Turn
into a baking dish.  Lightly brown crumbs, garlic and paprika in
remaining tablespoon margarine; sprinkle over top of ham mixture.
Bake 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
