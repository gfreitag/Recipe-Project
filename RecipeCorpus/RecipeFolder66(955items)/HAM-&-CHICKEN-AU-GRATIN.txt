Title: HAM & CHICKEN AU GRATIN
Categories: Casseroles, Ham, Rice, Left chick
Yield: 4 Servings
1/3 c  Butter
1/8 ts Nutmeg
2/3 c  Onions; chopped
1/8 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Flour
3 c  Ham; cooked, diced
3 c  Milk
2 c  Chicken; cooked, diced
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Sharp cheddar cheese; cut up
2 c  Rice; cooked
1/2 c  Buttered fresh breadcrumbs
Preheat oven to 350~. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat; saute
onions til tender. Stir in flour; slowly add milk, stirring
constantly til thickened. Combine with remaining ingredients except
crumbs in casserole. Top with bread crumbs. Bake at 350~ for 30 to 35
minutes or til brown. Makes 4-6 servings.
