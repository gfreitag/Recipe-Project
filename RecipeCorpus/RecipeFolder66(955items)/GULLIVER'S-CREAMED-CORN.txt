Title: GULLIVER'S CREAMED CORN
Categories: None
Yield: 8 Servings
20 oz Frozen Kernel Corn
8 oz Whipping Cream
1/2 pt Homogenized Milk
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts MSG (Accent) - Optional
6 ts Sugar
White or Cayenne Pepper
2 tb Melted Butter
2 tb Flour
Combine all ingredients except last two in a pot and bring to a boil.
Simmer 5 minutes.  Blend butter with flour, add to corn, mix well and
remove from heat.

VARIATION:

Put finished corn in heat proof casserole, sprinkle with Parmesan
cheese and place under a broiler until evenly browned.

SERVES 8
