Title: HAM~ EGG & CHEESE CASSEROLE
Categories: Brunch, Dairy, Casseroles
Yield: 12 Servings
5 c  Plain bread cubes or crouton
1 lb Ham; chopped*
2 c  Cheddar cheese; cubed small*
4 c  Milk **
1/4 ts Pepper**
6    Eggs**
1/3 c  Corn oil**
1 ts Salt**
1 ts Dry mustard**
1/3 c  Flour**
* Place these in the bottom of a greased 9 X 13 pan. ** Pour this
mixture over crouton mixture and refrigerate overnight. Bake at 300
for 1 1/2 hours. Let sit for 10 to 15 minutes before serving. Cut
into squares. Serves 12 to 15 (not at our house!!)
