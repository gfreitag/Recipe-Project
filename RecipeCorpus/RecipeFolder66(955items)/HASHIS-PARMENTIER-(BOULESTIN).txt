Title: HASHIS PARMENTIER (BOULESTIN)
Categories: Beef
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Cold boiled beef
2    Rashers bacon
1 ts Chopped parsley
Slat & pepper
1 1/2 oz Butter
3 sm Onions
1 sm Shallot
Worcestershire sauce
1 lb Mashed potatoes
1/4 pt Stock
Mince the beef and rashers of bacon very finely, add salt, pepper,
chopped parsley.  Melt the butter in a saucepan or a frying pan, and
fry the chopped onions and shallots until golden brown. Then stir in
the meat and fry it lightly, add about 1/4 pt stock, and a few drops
Worcestershire sauce.  Well butter a fireproof dish or pie dish, put
in the meat mixture, well cover with finely mashed potatoes.  These
should be prepared with a little butter and milk and carefully
seasoned. Brown in the oven.
