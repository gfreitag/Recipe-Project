Title: HAMBURGER-CABBAGE STEW
Categories: Stews
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 1/2 lb Hamburger
1 sm Onion
Salt
4 c  Water
1 qt Tomatoes
3    Beef bouillon cubes
1 sm Head cabbage shredded
1 c  Celery with leaves, cut up
2    Or 3 carrots, cubed
1    Turnip, cubed
2 md Potatoes, cube
1 tb Basil
1 tb Sugar
Brown and season hamburger and onion.  After the seasoned hamburger
and onion are browned add the ten ingredients that come after salt.
Simmer until vegetables are tender and flavors are well mixed. Adjust
seasoning.
