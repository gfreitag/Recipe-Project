Title: HAWAIIAN HAM SALAD
Categories: Salads, Pork
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Ham, smoked; cubed, cooked
8 oz Pineapple chunks
2    Celery rib; sliced
1/2 c  Olive, black; halved
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Peanuts
Combine ham, pineapple, celery, and olives.  Mix mayonnaise and lemon
juice; stir into ham mix.  Chill at least an hour. Stir in peanuts
before serving.
