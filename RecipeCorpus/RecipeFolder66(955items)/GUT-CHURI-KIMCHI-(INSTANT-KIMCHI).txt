Title: GUT CHURI KIMCHI (INSTANT KIMCHI)
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Chinese cabbage
2    Garlic cloves
1 ts Hot pepper
1 ts Soy sauce
1 ts Vinegar
1 tb Salt
1 tb Sugar, granulated
Chop the cabbage into pieces about 1 1/2 inches long by 1 1/2 inches
wide. Three cups of chopped cabbage are needed.
Crush the garlic and blend with the hot pepper, soy sauce and
vinegar. Add the cabbage and mix well. Add the salt and sugar, mix
and cover until ready to serve.
