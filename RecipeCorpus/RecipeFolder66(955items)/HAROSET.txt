Title: HAROSET
Categories: Jewish
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 lb Pitted dates
1/4 lb Raisins
4    Dried figs
1 1/2 c  Sweet kosher wine
1/4 lb Walnuts
1/4 lb Alonds
2 tb Sesame seeds
1/2 ts Cumin
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cardamon
Place the dried fruits in a food processor or blender; chop fine. Add
1/2 cup of the wine. Mix again at low speed. Add the remaining
ingredients and mix at low speed until nearly smooth. Makes 8
servings.

Origin: Journal American, March 24, 1993 Shared by: Sharon Stevens
