Title: HAM BAKED IN MILK
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
-Sue Woodward
MMMMM-----------------RUMFORD COMPLETE COOK BOOK----------------------
1 sl Ham; very thick slice
1 ts Dry mustard
Pepper
2 c  Milk
1 tb Butter; or drippings
Rub mustard and pepper into surface of ham.  Place in baking pan, add
milk, dot with butter, cover and bake about 45 mins. in moderate oven
(350~). Any liquid remaining in the pan after baking may be slightly
thickened and served as sauce or gravy.

Author - Lily Haxworth Wallace, c. 1947
