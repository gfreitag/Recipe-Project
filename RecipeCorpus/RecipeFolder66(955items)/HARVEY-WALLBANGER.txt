Title: HARVEY WALLBANGER
Categories: Mixed drink, Vodka, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Vodka
4 oz Orange juice
1/2 oz Galliano
Pour vodka and orange juice into a collins glass filled with ice
cubes and stir. Float galliano on top by pouring slowly over a
teaspoon turned bottom-side up.
