Title: HAM & BROCCOLI CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Pork
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Frozen broccoli -- 10 oz.
1 pk Frozen french fries -- 1#
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
3/4 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Ham -- cubed
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- gr
Spray dish with Pam.  Place fries in 9x13 dish. Sprinkle broccoli over
fries.  Blend soup and milk. Add ham. Pour mixture over broccoli.
Sprinkle cheese on top. Bake at 350 F 25 minutes.
