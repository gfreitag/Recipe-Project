Title: HARVEST BEAN CHOWDER
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Idaho Great Northern beans
Water
Meaty ham bone
-OR- smoked ham hock
1 md Onion; chopped
1    Clove garlic; crushed
1 md Potato; diced
2    Carrots; sliced
1 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
3 tb Flour
3 c  Milk
Salt and pepper
Soak beans according to directions, using either method. Drain beans
and measure liquid. Add water to make 1-1/2 quarts. Place beans,
liquid, ham bone, onion and garlic in large kettle. Simmer 1-1/2 to 2
hours, or until tender. Add potato, carrots, celery and green pepper.
Simmer 30 minutes longer. Remove ham bone. Cut up meat and return to
soup. Thicken with flour blended with a little cold water. Add milk
and heat gently. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Makes 6 to 8
servings.
