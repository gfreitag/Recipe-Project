Title: HAWAIIAN  BANANA  NUT  BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Flour
3/4 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Chopped pecans
3    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Vegetable oil
2 c  Mashed bananas
1 ea 8 oz. can of crushed
Pineapple
And juice
2 ts Vanilla
NOTE:  Combine ingredients and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 10
minutes. Cool in pan for 10 minutes.
