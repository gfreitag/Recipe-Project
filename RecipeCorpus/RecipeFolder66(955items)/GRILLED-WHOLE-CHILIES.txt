Title: GRILLED WHOLE CHILIES
Categories: Sides
Yield: 1 Servings
Fruit - Select firm ripe
Fruit in season,
110 ml Plain yoghurt
1 ts Honey
Fresh mint chopped
Mint sprigs
1    Slice the fruit.
2.  Serve with a dollop of yoghurt mixed with honey and mint. Garnish
with mint sprigs.
