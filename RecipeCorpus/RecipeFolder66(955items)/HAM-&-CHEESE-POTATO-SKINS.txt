Title: HAM & CHEESE POTATO SKINS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 12 Servings
1 x  Basic Potato Skins recipe
1 ts Dijon or spicy mustard
1/4 lb Thinly sliced ham
2 oz Roasted red peppers -or-
2 oz Whole pimentos
1/2 c  Monterey Jack cheese shreds
1 x  Chopped parsley (optional)
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Follow the recipe for Basic Crispy Potato
Skins.
Brush insides of potato skins with mustard. Drain and slice peppers or
pimientos. Top with slices of ham and a few strips of pepper/pimiento.
Sprinkle with cheese.
Bake about 10 minutes on baking sheet, or until cheese is melted and
bubbling. Sprinkle with parsley if you like.
Makes about 2 skins per serving.
