Title: HAZELNUT BUTTER CUPS
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 Batch
1 1/2 lb Milk chocolate coating
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
3/4 c  Hazelnut butter
1 ds Salt
1/4 lb Margarine
1 c  Powdered sugar
2 tb Graham cracker crumbs
Combine filling ingredients in the food processor. Store in airtight
container. Melt chocolate coating in top of double boiler over hot
water. When melted, spoon into small candy mold or paper cup. While
chocolate is still soft, place a small spoonful of filling mixture
into center of cup. Push one roasted hazelnut into top of filling,
allowing chocolate to fill in around sides of paper cup. Refrigerate
in tightly covered container.
