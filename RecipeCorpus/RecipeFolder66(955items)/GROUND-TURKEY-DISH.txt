Title: GROUND TURKEY DISH
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Turkey -- ground
1/2 c  Onions -- chopped
1 tb Chili powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Stewed tomatoes -- (16 oz)
1 cn Kidney beans -- (15 oz)
3/4 c  Rice -- uncooked
3/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Bell peppers -- chopped
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
In a skillet cook onion and peppers until tender.  Add meat until no
pink remains. Add chili powder and salt. Stir in tomatoes, beans,
rice, and water.  Cover and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 20
minutes. Serve with cheese on top.
