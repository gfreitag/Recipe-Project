Title: HAMBURGER PACIFIC
Categories: Casseroles, Hamburger
Yield: 8 Servings
2 tb Cooking oil
2 lb Ground beef
1 md Chopped onion
1/4 ts Garlic powder or
Minced garlic clove
2 ts Salt
2 ts Pepper
10 oz Sliced mushrooms, drained
10 oz Cream of tomato soup
14 oz Cream corn
2 c  Fresh chop suey vegetables
3 c  Cooked noodles
Paprika

Heat oil in frying pan. Add next 5 ingredients. Saute until no trace
of pink remains in meat. Turn off heat. Drain and discard fat.

Add mushrooms, soup and corn to meat. Stir. Empty in 2 quart
casserole.

Spread chop suey vegetables over meat mixture.

Prepare noodles as directed on package. Drain. Arrange over
vegetables. Sprinkle with Paprika for colour. Cover. Bake in 350 F
oven for 40 minutes until hot. Serves 8.
