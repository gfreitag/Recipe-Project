Title: HAWAIIAN MACADAMIA NUT BITES
Categories: Cookies, Chocolate, Snacks
Yield: 60 Servings
1/2 c  Softened Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Vanilla Extract
1/8 ts Salt
5 oz Whole Macadamia Nuts
1/4 c  Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips
1 ts Shortening
1 lg Egg
1 1/2 c  All Purpose Flour
Preheat oven to 350Ã¸F.  In a large mixer bowl at low speed, beat
butter and sugar until blended.  Increase speed to medium-high, beat
until fluffy. Beat in egg and vanilla. Beat in flour and salt. Shape
1 ts of dough around each Macadamia nut.  Place on ungreased baking
sheets. Bake for 10 to 13 minutes or until golden around base of each
cookie. Cool on wire racks. In a small saucepan over low heat, melt
chocolate chips and shortening. Drizzle over cookies. Store in a
tightly covered container. Yields 5-1/2 dozen.
