Title: HARDEE'S RAISIN BISCUITS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 c  Bisquick
2    Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
2/3 c  Milk
2/3 c  Powdered sugar
1    Water
1/4    Vanilla
1/2 c  Raisins
Recipe by: Vicki Phillips (Kountry Cook) Blend Bisquick, sugar and
raisins. Add milk and stir.  Add cinnamon and roll out to 1/2 inch
thickness and cut with a biscuit cutter. Bake on a lightly greased
pan for 15 minutes at 350 degrees.  Blend rest of ingredients
together and spread over the biscuits while hot. Posted to MC-Recipe
