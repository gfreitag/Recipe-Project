Title: GRUNCH
Categories: Camping, Outdoors, Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 2 Cups
1 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Crushed graham crackers
1/4 c  Powdered skim milk
3 tb Cinnamon
1 tb Powdered cloves
At home, mix ingredients.  Store in plastic container and place in the
refrigerator to harden overnight. Store in a plastic squeeze tube for
easy carrying. Makes two cups. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com
Source: Trail Food.
