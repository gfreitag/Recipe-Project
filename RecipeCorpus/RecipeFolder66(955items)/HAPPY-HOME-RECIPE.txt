Title: HAPPY HOME RECIPE
Categories: Diabetic, Main dish, Info
Yield: 1 Family
4 c  Of love
2 c  Of loyalty
3 c  Forgiveness
1 c  Friendship
5    Spoons of hope
2    Spoons of tenderness
4 qt Of faith
1    Barrel of laughter
Take love and loyalty, mix them throughly with Faith. Blend it with
tenderness, kindness and understanding. Add friendship and hope,
sprinkle abundantly with laughter. Bake it with sunshine. Serve daily
with generous helpings
