Title: HAMBURGER & NOODLES
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Hamburger
1 lg Onion
1 ts Garlic
3 c  Macaroni
1 qt Tomato juice
1 cn Tomato soup
1 cn Tomato sauce
1/2 ts Liquid smoke flavoring
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
Salt and pepper to taste
Recipe by: Verla Furniss, Rigby, ID
Cook macaroni according to package directions. Combine with the
rest of ingredients.
