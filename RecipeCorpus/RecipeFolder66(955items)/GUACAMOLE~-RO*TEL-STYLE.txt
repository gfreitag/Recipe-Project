Title: GUACAMOLE~ RO*TEL STYLE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Quart
3    Ripe avocados, peeled and
-mashed
1 cn RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and
-Green Chilies, drained
-(10 oz)
1 md Onion, chopped
ds Hot pepper sauce
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Combine all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours.
