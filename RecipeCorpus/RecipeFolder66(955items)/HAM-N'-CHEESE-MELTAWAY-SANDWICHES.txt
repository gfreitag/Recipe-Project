Title: HAM N' CHEESE MELTAWAY SANDWICHES
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 12 Servings
1 lb Chipped ham
1    Onion, minced
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1    Doz. hot dog buns
6    Hard boiled eggs
1/2 c  Chili sauce
Cheese
Mix all ingredients, except cheese and buns, in large container.
Fill hot dog buns with mixture.  Add strip of cheese to each bun.
Wrap each bun in foil. Heat in 425 F. oven to warm thoroughly and
melt cheese. These sandwiches can be frozen. Allow approximately 20
minutes in oven when taken from freezer.

P.P.--Gretna, La.
