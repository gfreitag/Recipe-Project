Title: HAM & BEAN SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Ham (diced)
1 md Onion
1    Celery stick
1 md Carrot (grated fine)
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 c  Catsup
Hot peppers (optional)
Keep seeds in for FIRE!
Mix ingredients in saucepan.  Cover with water and cook for about 45
minutes.  Then add 2 cans (16 ounces) of great northern beans, and
cook for
15    minutes.
