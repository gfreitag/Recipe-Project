Title: HAMBURGER STEW
Categories: None
Yield: 10 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1    Can condensed tomato soup
1    Can Zesty Tomato or Italian
-Herb Tomato soup
1    Can condensed cream of
-mushroom soup
2 c  Water
1 ts Instant beef bouillon
2 c  Grated carrots
1 1/2 c  Raw elbow macaroni, cooked
-according to package
-directions
In large saucepan or Dutch oven over medium heat, brown ground beef,
onion and celery; drain.  Stir in remaining ingredients. Cover;
simmer over low heat 5 ~ 10 minutes, or until carrots are tender and
stew is thoroughly heated.  Serves 10.
