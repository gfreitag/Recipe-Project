Title: HARDY VEGETABLE SOUP
Categories: Soups, Vegetables
Yield: 10 Servings
4 qt Stock
3/4 c  Celery, chopped
3/4 c  Onion, chopped
2    Garlic cloves, chopped
1 c  Carrots, sliced
2 c  Potatoes, diced
2 tb Oil
1/2 c  Tomato paste
1/2 c  Green pepper, diced
1 c  Mixed vegetables
1/2 tb Sweet basil
Salt & pepper to taste
1/2 c  Macaroni
1/2 c  Pot barley
2 c  Coarsley chopped cabbage
1    Tomato, chopped
In a soup pot saute the celery, onion and garlic in oil for 5
minutes. Add water, vegetables, pot barley, meats, seasonings. Bring
to the boil and simmer for 1 1/4 hours. For last 1/4 hour add cabbage
and macaroni.
