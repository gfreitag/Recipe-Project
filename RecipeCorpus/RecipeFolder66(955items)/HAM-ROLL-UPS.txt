Title: HAM ROLL-UPS
Categories: Appetizers, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 24 Servings
8 oz Sliced ham (1 package)
1 bn Green onions, whole
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
- (1 package)
Spread sliced ham with cream cheese.  Lay 1 onion along each slice
edge and roll up.  Chill until cheese is firm. Slice into 3/4-inch
pieces and arrange upright on plate. Recipe can be doubled or
tripled. Makes 24 appetizer servings.
