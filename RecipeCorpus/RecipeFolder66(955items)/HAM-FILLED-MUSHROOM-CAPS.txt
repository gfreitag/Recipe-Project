Title: HAM-FILLED MUSHROOM CAPS
Categories: Spanish, Mushrooms
Yield: 4 Servings
3 tb Cured ham, finely diced
2 ea Cl Garlic, minced
2 tb Parsley, minced
1/2 ea Dried red chili pepper, seed
1 x  1/4 teaspoon crushed red pep
2 ts Fruity olive oil
12 ea Medium mushroom caps, brushe
In a bowl mix together the ham, garlic, parsley, chili pepper, and
olive oil. Fill the mushroom caps with this mixture, arrange in a
baking dish [may be prepared ahead], and bake at 350ø F for 15
minutes. (Champi¤ones Rellenos De Jamon). From "Tapas: The Little
Dishes of Spain" by Penelope Casas. Fat grams per serving: Approx.
