Title: GROUND BEEF GOULASH RBTN28A
Categories: Beef
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 ea Medium onion
1 ea Clove garlic minced
1 pk 4 oz uncooked noodles
1/2 c  Water
1 c  10 1/2 oz beef broth
2 1/2 c  Tomatoe juice
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 1/2 ts Celery salt
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/3 c  Chopped green pepper
1 c  Reduced-fat sour cream
1 c  4oz sliced mushrooms
Drained
Brown beef, onion and garlic in large skillet. Add noodles. Combine
water, broth, tomato juice and seasonings; pour over noodles and meat
mixture. Cover, simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add
green pepper; cover and cook until noodles are tender. Add mushrooms
and sour cream; heat but do not boil....
