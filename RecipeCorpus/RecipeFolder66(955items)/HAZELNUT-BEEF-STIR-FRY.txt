Title: HAZELNUT BEEF STIR FRY
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Stir-fry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Beef top round
-- cut in thin strips
3 tb Teriyaki sauce
2 tb Oil (divided)
2 ts Cornstarch
1    Green pepper
-- cut into 3/4-inch slices
2    Carrots; sliced
-- pre-cooked tender crisp
6    Green onions
-- cut into 2-inch slices
3/4 c  Roasted & chopped hazelnuts
-- (Oregon hazelnuts)
Combine teriyaki sauce, 1 tablespoon oil and cornstarch. Add beef
strips and marinate 30 minutes. Drain beef (reserve marinade), and
add to hot oil in skillet. Add vegetables and cook until meat is done
and vegetables are tender crisp. Add rest of marinade and cook until
thickened. Top with hazelnuts and serve.
