Title: HALIBUT BROCCOLI PIE
Categories: Main dish, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 1 Recipe
3 c  Grated sharp cheddar cheese
1 1/2 c  Flaked halibut
1 c  Finely chopped onion
1 pk Frozen chopped broccoli
-- thawed and drained
1 1/3 c  Milk
3    Eggs
3/4 c  Bisquick baking mix
1/2 ts Lemon pepper
1/4 ts Minced garlic
1/8 ts Dried thyme leaves
Mix 2 cups cheese, halibut, onion and broccoli together and place in
a 10" greased pie plate. Beat milk, eggs, Bisquick, lemon pepper,
garlic and thyme. Pour over halibut-broccoli mixture. Bake 400
degrees F. for 25-35 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes
out clean. Top with remaining cheese and bake 1-2 minutes longer to
melt cheese. Cool 5 minutes and cut into wedges. Garnish with tomato
and parsley.

Source: Alaska Seafood Cookbook Reprinted by permission from the
Alaska Seafood Marketing Institute
