Title: HARDTACK
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Level teaspoons baking
-powder
1 pn Salt
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs, well beaten
1    Jelly glass orange marmalade
1 lb Finely chopped walnuts
1 lb Finely chopped dates
**** HARDTACK ****

Sift ingredients together. Add the remainder of ingredients; mix
well. Bake about 1 inch thick on a cookie sheet in a moderate oven at
375 degrees (F), for about 45 minutes. Cut into squares while warm.
