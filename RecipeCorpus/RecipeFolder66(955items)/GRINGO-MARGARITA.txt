Title: GRINGO MARGARITA
Categories: Alcohol, American, Beverages, Mexican, New (to be
Yield: 4 Servings
6 oz Can Limeade -- Frozen
-Concentrate
6 oz Gin
6 oz Club Soda -- or seltzer
Orange Slices -- or lime
Slices
Cherries
Put the frozen limeade concentrate and gin in the container of a
blender. Process until smooth. Add seltzer and stir gently. Serve in
stemmed glasses whose rims have been dipped in lime juice, then in
salt (optional) or over ice cubes in a tall glass. Garnish tall
glasses with fruit slices and cherries.

Serves 4.
