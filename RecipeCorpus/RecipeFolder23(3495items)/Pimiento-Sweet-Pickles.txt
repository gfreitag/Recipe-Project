Title: Pimiento Sweet Pickles
Categories: Can/preserv
Yield: 6 servings
1 sm Piece ginger root
1 tb Whole mustard seed
4 c  Vinegar
7 c  Sugar
24    Red sweet peppers
1 tb Whole cloves
1    Blade mace
3    Inch stick cinnamon
1 tb Allspice
Wash peppers, remove seeds and membranes.  Soak overnight in a brine
made in proportion of 1 tablespoon salt to 1 quart water.  Drain and
chop peppers. Heat spices, vinegar, and sugar.  Add peppers.  Simmer
1/2 hour. Strain. Pack peppers in freshly sterilized jars.  Boil down
the pickle sirup until thick and pour over peppers until jar is
filled.  The Household Searchlight
