Title: Hot Cashew Chicken Salad
Categories: Chinese, Chicken, Salad
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Vegetable oil
1 1/4 lb Boneless chicken breasts,
-diced
3 tb Chopped scallions
1    Garlic clove, minced
1/4 c  Cashews
2 c  Chopped romaine lettuce
1/2 lb Chopped spinach
11 oz Mandarin oranges, drained
3 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Honey
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1. In a large salad bowl, combine lettuce, spinach & oranges.

2. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, honey, ginger & 3 tbsp. water.
Set sauce aside.

3. In a wok or large frying pan, heat oil over medium high heat. Add
chicken & stir fry until barely opaque throughout, about 5 minutes.
Add scallions & garlic & cook 1 more minute. Add sauce & cook,
tossing, for 30 seconds.

4. Pour chicken & sauce over greens & oranges & toss. Sprinkle
cashews on top.
