Title: Turkey Zanzibar
Categories: Poultry, Turkey, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
Salt, to taste
Ground pepper, to taste
1/2 sm Lemons or limes
Mango chutney, Optional
1 lb Turkey breast tenderloin,
-boneless and skinless
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Cumin, ground
Brush turkey breast tenderloins on both sides with oil and rub with
seasonings.  Squeeze lemon (or lime) over turkey; set aside.  Prepare
outdoor grill for cooking or pre-heat broiler.  Grill or broil
tenderloins 5 to 6 inches from heat source 5 to 8 minutes on each
side until cooked through.  Serve with mango chutney, if desired.
