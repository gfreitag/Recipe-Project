Title: Lighthouse Flounder
Categories: Fish, Crab/shrimp, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Canned newburg sauce
-salt
-paprika
1 1/2 lb Flounder fillets
1 lb Crabmeat
1/2    Cub dry bread crumbs
1/4 c  Margarine or butter
Cut fish into  1 1/2 to 2 inch strips. Mix crabmeat, bread crumbs and
half cup newburg sauce to make stuffing. Place spoon full of stuffing
on each fillet; roll up firmly and place, small end down on a
foil-lined baking pan. Sprinkle each fillet with salt and paprika;
dot with margarine or butter. Bake at 350 degrees for about 20
minutes or until fish flakes. Heat remaining newburg sauce and pour
over fish. Note: You could improve this recipe with your own newburg
sauce.
