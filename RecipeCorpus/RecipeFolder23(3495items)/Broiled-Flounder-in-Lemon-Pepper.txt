Title: Broiled Flounder in Lemon Pepper
Categories: Fish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
2 T  Margarine, softened
1 t  Lemon juice
1 lb Flounder fillets
1/8 t  Grated lemon peel
1/8 t  Black pepper
Place fillets on lightly greased broiler pan. Mix remaining
ingredients and spread on fillets. Broil five inches from heat for
ten minutes or until meat flakes easily.
