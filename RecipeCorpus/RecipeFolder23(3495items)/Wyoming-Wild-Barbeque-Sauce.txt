Title: Wyoming Wild Barbeque Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
-chopped
2 tb Dry mustard
1 ts Tabasco pepper sauce
1 tb Dark molasses
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1 c  Chili sauce
1 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Steak sauce
1 tb Worchestershire sauce (Lea &
-Perrins recommended)
1 tb Garlic cloves, finely
In a medium sized bowl, combine all ingredients.  Whisk until sauce is
well blended.

Use with beef, pork, or game.
