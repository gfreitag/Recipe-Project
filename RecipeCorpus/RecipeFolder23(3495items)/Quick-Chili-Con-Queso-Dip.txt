Title: Quick Chili Con Queso Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn (#2 ?) ROTEL chopped chilies
1    Electric slow cooker
2lb. velveeta cheese log
Put cheese log in cooker.  Dump in ROTEL.  Heat.  Stir chilies into
melted cheese.  Serve with 'crunchies' such as chips and veggie
sticks, etc. .
