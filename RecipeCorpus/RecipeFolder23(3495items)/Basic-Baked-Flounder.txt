Title: Basic Baked Flounder
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1    Onion chopped
1 cn Cheese soup
1 lb Flounder fillets
1/2    Bell pepper chopped
-paprika
Dry fish in a paper towel. Place in single layer in baking dish.
Sprinkle onions and  pepper over fish.  Spread soup over all.
sprinkle with paprika. baked uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
