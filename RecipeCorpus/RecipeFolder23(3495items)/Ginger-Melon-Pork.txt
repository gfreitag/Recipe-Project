Title: Ginger Melon Pork
Categories: Chinese, Pork
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Dry sherry
2 tb Wine vinegar
1 tb Cornstarch
3 c  Melon cubed - honeydew or
-cantaloupe
1/2 c  Pickled watermelon rind
-diced
1 tb Cooking oil
1 lb Fresh pork leg cubed-fresh
-ham
1/2    Thinly sliced onion
1    Minced clove garlic
1 tb Grated fresh ginger OR 1
-teaspoon dry ginger
2 tb Soy sauce
Heat oil in large skillet over medium high heat. Brown pork cubes,
stirring until lightly browned, about 4 to 5 minutes. Stir in onion,
garlic and ginger; stir and cook for 2-3 minutes.

Mix together soy sauce, sherry, vinegar and cornstarch. Add to
skillet. Cook and stir until sauce thickens. Stir in melon and
watermelon rind. Heat through.
