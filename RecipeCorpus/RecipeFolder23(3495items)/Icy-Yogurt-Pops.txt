Title: Icy Yogurt Pops
Categories: Snack, Kids
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Low-fat or Skim Milk
1 c  Low-fat Plain Yogurt
3/4 c  Frozen Fruit Juice
Servings:  6

* You can use pureed fresh fruit or frozen fruits instead juice. In a
4 cup glass measure, combine yogurt, fruit juice concentrate and
milk. Pour into pop molds or use small paper cups and insert a wooden
stick in the centre of each. Freeze until firm, about 2 - 3 hours. To
serve, peel off paper cups.

From The Gazette, 91/07/24.
