Title: Tarragon Sauce for Fish
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
3 t  Lemon juice
3 t  Flour
1    Hard-boiled egg
1 c  Milk
2 t  Capers
1/2 t  Tarragon
-salt and pepper to taste
Stir flour into 1/4 cup milk until well blended, add the remaining
milk. Add tarragon, capers, lemon juice, salt, pepper. Heat over slow
fire, stirring constantly, until sauce is a smooth consistency.  Chop
hard-boiled egg and blend with sauce.  Serve with boiled or sauteed
fish; especially good with haddock, cod or halibut.
