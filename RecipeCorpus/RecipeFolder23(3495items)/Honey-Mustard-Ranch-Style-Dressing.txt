Title: Honey Mustard Ranch Style Dressing
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 2 servings
-Dressing Mix
1 ts Dijon mustard
2 tb To 4 tb honey
1 c  Milk
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 pk Hidden Valley Ranch Style
Servings: 2 cups

Measure all ingredients into a small bowl.  Whisk with a wire whisk
until smooth.  Taste and adjust the amount of honey to your
preference.  The Dijon mustard has a bit of a bite, so you may need
more if you prefer a smoother dressing.
