Title: Mexican Chicken Wings
Categories: Chicken, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
12 oz Tortilla corn chips
1 lb Chicken wings, disjointed &
-tips discarded
1/2 c  Corn oil
1/4 c  Chili powder
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Ground cumin
1. Preheat oven to 350Ã¸. In a small bowl, whisk together the oil,
chili powder, oregano & cumin to blend well.

2. Pulverize the tortilla chips in a food processor. Pour into a
shallow bowl.

3. Dip the chicken pieces in the seasoned oil; then dredge in the
ground chips until coated. Set on a foil-lined baking sheet & bake
for 45 minutes until browned & crisp outside & tender inside. Serve
hot.
