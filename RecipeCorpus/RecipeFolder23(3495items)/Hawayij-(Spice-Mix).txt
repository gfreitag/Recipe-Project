Title: Hawayij (Spice Mix)
Categories: Middle east, Misc/food, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Saffron threads
1 ts Cardamom seeds
2 ts Tumeric
Karen Mintzias
6 ts Black peppercorns
3 ts Caraway seeds
Pound peppercorns, caraway seeds, saffron and cardamom seeds in a
mortar, or grind in a blender.

Stir in tumeric and store spice mix in a sealed jar.  Use according to
recipes.

Source: The Complete Middle East Cookbook by Tess Mallos Typed for
you by Karen Mintzias
