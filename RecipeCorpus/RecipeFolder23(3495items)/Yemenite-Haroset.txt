Title: Yemenite Haroset
Categories: Jewish, Dessert
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Sesame seeds
1/2 ts Cumin
1/2 ts Ginger, ground
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cardomom
1/2 lb Pitted dates
1/4 lb Raisins
4    Figs, dried
1 1/2 c  Sweet wine
1/4 lb Walnuts
1/4 lb Almonds
Place fruits in food processor or blender.  Finely chop.  Add 1/2 cup
of the wine.  Mix again, at low speed.  Add remaining ingredients and
mix at low speed until nearly smooth.
