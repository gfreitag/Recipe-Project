Title: Tortellini Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Italian, Pasta
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Water
9 oz Fresh cheese tortellini
1 c  Peas, frozen
2 tb Parsley, snipped
1 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Onion, finely chopped
1 c  Mushrooms, sliced
1 ea Garlic clove,minced
14 1/2 oz Can chicken broth
In a large saucepan or Dutch oven heat oil.  Add onion, mushrooms and
garlic.  Cook, stirring occasionally, until onion is tender.  Add
chicken broth and water.  Bring to a boil.  Add tortellini and peas.
Return to a boil and cook for 6 to 12 minutes or until tortellini is
tender. Stir in parsley.

Makes 8 servings.
