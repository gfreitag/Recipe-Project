Title: Broiled Flounder with Thyme
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 T  Butter or margarine
2 T  Lemon juice
1 T  Chopped parsley
1 lb Flounder fillets
-salt
1/4 t  Thyme
Place fillets skin down on lightly greased broiler pan. Mix remaining
ingredients, except parsley, and spread on fillets. Broil 4 inches
from heat for 10 minutes per inch of thickness or until it flakes
easily. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
