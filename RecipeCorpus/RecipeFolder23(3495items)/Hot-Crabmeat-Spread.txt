Title: Hot Crabmeat Spread
Categories: Seafood, Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
1 ds Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Lemon juice
8 oz Cream cheese
1 cn Crabmeat
Soften cream cheese.  Drain crabmeat and break into small pieces.
Mix all ingredients together.  Bake at 350 degrees (F) until cream
cheese is "goey", about 20 to 25 minutes. Serve hot with crackers.
