Title: Kadju Badun (Deviled Cashewnuts)
Categories: Appetizer, Indian
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Water
250 ml Oil
1 ts Chilli powder
225 g  Cashewnuts
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Salt
Mix  the  cashewnuts,  paprika,  turmeric,  salt  and water together
in a bowl and allow to stand for 1 hour. Heat the oil and deep fry the
cashewnuts until evenly browned. Remove from the oil, drain well and
place on serving dish. Sprinkle with chilli powder and mix well.
From: "A taste of Sri Lanka" by Indra Jayasekera, ISBN 962 224 010 0
