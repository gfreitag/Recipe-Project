Title: Creole Style Flounder
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1/4    Green peppers chopped
8 oz Can tomato sauce
1/4 t  Salt
1 lb Flounder fillets
1/4 c  Chopped celery
1 T  Cooking oil
1/4 t  Black pepper
Saute vegetables in oil. Add tomato sauce and seasonings. Simmer for
ten minutes. Oil a baking dish and lay fillets in a single layer.
Cover with sauce. Bake at 450 degrees for ten minutes or until fish
flakes easily. Serve over rice.
