Title: Dutch Pickles
Categories: Can/preserv, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
1 qt Chopped onions
1 sm Cabbage, chopped
1 tb Mustard
1 c  Flour
3 c  Sugar
1 qt Chopped green tomatoes
1 lg Cauliflower, chopped
1 c  Salt
1 tb Turmeric
2 qt Vinegar
Combine vegetables and salt.  Cover with hot water.  Let stand 30
minutes. Drain.  Combine turmeric, mustard, flour, and sugar. Add
vinegar. Cook until thick and smooth.  Add vegetables.  Boil slowly,
stirring constantly, 15-20 minutes. The Household Searchlight
