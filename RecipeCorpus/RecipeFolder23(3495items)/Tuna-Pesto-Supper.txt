Title: Tuna Pesto Supper
Categories: Stove top, Pasta, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Pesto
2 T  Lemon juice
1 pk Egg noodles
1 cn Tuna
1 lb Asparagus, cut into 2-3 in
Cook the noodles as directed.  Steam the asparagus until just tender.
In a small saucepan, heat the pesto, tuna, and lemon juice.  Toss the
noodles with the sauce and asparagus.
