Title: Swiss Cheese Vegetable Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Milk or light cream
6 oz Swiss cheese
1 ea Swiss Vegetable Soup Mix *
3 c  Hottest tap water
Servings:  4

*Use Knorr or Maggi dry soup mix.

Combine soup mix and water in 2 quart glass bowl.  Cover, microcook
on HIGH for 15 minutes.  Stir in milk and cheese.  Microcook on
MEDIUM- HIGH (70%) for 5 minutes.  Stir, cook for 1 minute more or
until heated through.

Serve with soft biscuit breadsticks.
