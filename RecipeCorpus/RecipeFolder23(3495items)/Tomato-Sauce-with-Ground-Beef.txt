Title: Tomato Sauce with Ground Beef
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Italian
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Chopped onion
1 lb Ground beef
1    Can Tomato sauce 15 oz
2 tb Chopped Italian parsley
1 ts Dried Basil
1/4 ts Black pepper
2 tb Olive oil
1    Garlic clove minced
1    Can Italian Tomatoes 28oz
1    Can Tomato Paste 6 oz
1    Salt to taste
1/2 ts Dried Oregano
1    Whole Bay Leaf
Heat oil in a large heavy saucepan; add onion; saute over low heat,
stirring, until tender and golden, about 5 min.  Add the garlic;
saute 1 min. Meanwhile, brown the ground beef in a skillet, stirring
to crumble meat; drain off fat; add to the onion mixture. Add the
tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tomato sauce can filled with water, the
tomato paste, parsley, 1 tsp salt, the basil, oregano, pepper, and
bay leaf. Heat over medium-low heat to simmering. Simmer, uncovered,
over low heat, stirring occasionally until sauce is thickened and
flavorful, about 1 hour and 15 minutes.
