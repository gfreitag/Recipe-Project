Title: Ergolavi
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Greek
Yield: 1 servings
3 ea Egg whites
2 ts Vanilla
6 c  Almonds
3 c  Sugar
Blanch almonds.  Dry in oven, mash with mortar and pestle, or
pulverize in blender or food processor or grinder.  Place sugar, egg
whites, and vanilla in bowl; add the almonds.  Blend, using your
fingers, into a thick dough or mass.  Shape into narrow "fingers,"
and place on a buttered baking sheet.  Bake in preheated 300 F. oven
for about 20 min..
