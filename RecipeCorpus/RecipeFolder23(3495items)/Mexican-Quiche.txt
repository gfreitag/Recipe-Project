Title: Mexican Quiche
Categories: Pork, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Shredded swiss cheese
Cayenne pepper
5    Eggs
4    Tostaco shells
4 oz Chopped green chilies
2 c  Light cream
6 sl Cooked crumbled bacon
1/4 ts Salt
Put cheese into shells.  Mix bacon with drained chilies and put into
shells.  Beat eggs, cream and salt.  Pour into shells. Sprinkle
cayenne over top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes. Cool 5
minutes. Makes 4 servings.
