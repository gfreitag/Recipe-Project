Title: Frankfurter Crown with Potato Dressing
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Mennonite
Yield: 8 servings
3    Slices bacon
1 1/2 lb Frankfurters
MMMMM----------------------POTATO DRESSING---------------------------
1/8 ts Pepper
1 qt Bread cubes
1    Egg
2 c  Mashed potatoes
4    Slices bacon, diced
1 tb Onion, chopped
1 tb Parsley, chopped
1 ts Salt
Thread frankfurters through center on a string.
Tie ends of string and stand frankfurters in shape of a crown on a
rack in a roasting pan.
Fill center with Potato Dressing made as follows:
Brown bacon, add onions and cook slowly.
Add seasoning and combine with bread, beaten egg and mashed potatoes.
Toss together and add water until mixture is desired consistency.
Lay 3 strips of bacon over the top of the dressing.
Bake at 375 degrees until bacon browns, add 1/2 cup water, cover and
cook 25 minutes.
