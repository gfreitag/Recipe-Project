Title: Indonesian Chili Peanut Sauce
Categories: Asian, Condiment
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Karo, Light Or Dark
1 tb Black Soy Sauce
2 tb Sesame Oil
Red Pepper To Taste
2 tb Oil
1 ts Garlic, Minced
1/2 c  Peanut Butter
1/2 c  Chicken Stock Or Broth
2 tb Cider Vinegar
