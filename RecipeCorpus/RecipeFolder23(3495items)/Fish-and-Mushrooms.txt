Title: Fish and Mushrooms
Categories: Seafood, Diabetic, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
-Dash of salt
-Dash of pepper
1    Stalk celery, sliced thin
1 T  Soy sauce
1 T  Dry sherry
1    Onion, sliced thin
1 T  Oil
1 1/2 c  Mushrooms, sliced thin
1 lb Fish fillets (cod, halibut,
-sole) cut in 1" slices
Preheat skillet and saute onion in oil. Add mushrooms and saute about
2 minutes, stirring constantly, until mushrooms wilt. Spread half of
fillet slices on mushrooms; sprinkle with salt and pepper; add
remaining fillets; sprinkle these with salt and pepper. Add celery,
soy sauce, and sherry; cook gently, covered, for ten minutes.
