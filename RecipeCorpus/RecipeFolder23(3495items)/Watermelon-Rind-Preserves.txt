Title: Watermelon Rind Preserves
Categories: Can/preserv
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Sugar
1/2    Sliced lemon
2    Sticks ginger
1    Watermellon
1 qt Water
1 tb Lime
1 qt Water
Pare green and pink from inch cubes of 1 melon.  Soak overnight in 1
quart water and 1 tbsp lime  Rinse well.  Boil cubes in clear water
for 15 minutes. Drain.  Boil 1 quart water, 2 cups sugar, 1/2 sliced
lemon, 2 sticks ginger, for 5 minutes.  Add rind and cook until
clear.  Let stand overnight.  Repeat, and seal in sterilized jars.
