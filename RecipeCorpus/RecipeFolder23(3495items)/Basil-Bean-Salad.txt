Title: Basil Bean Salad
Categories: Salad, Italian, Vegetable, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Dried basil
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/8 ts Pepper
Salt to taste
1    10oz. pkg frozen green beans
1/2 c  Onions; very thinly sliced
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1 tb Olive oil; plus 1 tsp.
3 tb Water
Cook beans according to directions, cooking until just
tender-crisp. Drain. Combine beans and onions in a large bowl.
Combine remaining ingredinets and pour over beans. toss until well
blended. Chill several hours or overnight, stirring occasionally.
**More Lean and Luscious** Info per serving: 78 calories 2g protein
5g fat 1mg cholesterol
