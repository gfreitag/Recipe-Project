Title: Hot Dippity Dogs
Categories: Snack, Cheese/eggs, Kids
Yield: 6 servings
2 oz Velveeta cheese
4    Hot dogs
From: Healthy Meals and More

toothpicks

Melt cheese. Slice hot dogs into bite size pieces. Put one piece of
hot dog on each toothpick. Let children dip hot dogs in cheese. HOT
DIPPITY DOG!
