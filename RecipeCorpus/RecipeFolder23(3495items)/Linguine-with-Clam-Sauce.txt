Title: Linguine with Clam Sauce
Categories: Pasta, Italian
Yield: 2 servings
13 oz Chopped Clams *
8 oz Cooked, Drained Linguine
3/4 c  Heavy Cream
3/4 ts Onion Salt
1/2 ts Thyme Leaves
* Include the juice from one can of chopped clams.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream, onion salt, thyme leaves and
chopped clams.  Cook 8 ounces of linguine as package directs, drain.
Toss with sauce and serve.
