Title: Beer Sauce for Baked Fish
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Mustard
1 c  Beer
-t tbs capers
1 c  Medium white sauce
1 c  Grated cheddar cheese
2 t  Worcestershire sauce
1 t  Paprika
Combine white sauce, cheese, worcestershire sauce, paprika and
mustard. Stir over low heat until cheese melts. Add beer slowly while
stirring; add capers. Serve hot with baked fish fillets
