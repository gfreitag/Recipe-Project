Title: Garam Masala #3
Categories: Indian, Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Black peppercorns
2 ts Cumin seeds
1/2 ts Coriander seeds
2 sm Dried red chilies, seeded
10    Green cardamoms, pods
-cracked, seeded
-OR
6    Black cardamoms, pods
-cracked, seeded
Using a blender, process all ingredients until finely ground. Store
in an airtight jar up to 3 months.
