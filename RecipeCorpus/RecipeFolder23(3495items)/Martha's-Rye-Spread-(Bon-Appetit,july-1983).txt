Title: Martha's Rye Spread (Bon Appetit/july 1983)
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Dill seed
1    To 2 ts Beau Monde seasoning
1    Small rye loaf
Cocktail rye slicews
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
6 oz Chopped corned beef
2 tb Minced onion
2 tb Minced fresh parsley
Combine first seven ingredients in large bowl and mix thoroughly.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.  Spoon dip into hollowed loaf and
serve with cocktail rye slices.
