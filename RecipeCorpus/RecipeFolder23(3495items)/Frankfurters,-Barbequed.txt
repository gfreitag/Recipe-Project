Title: Frankfurters, Barbequed
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meat, Mennonite
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Vinegar
3 ts Worchestershire sauce
1/2 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 c  Celery, diced
12    Frankfurters
1/4 c  Onions, chopped
2 tb Brown sugar
1 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Fat
Brown onions in hot fat.
Add remaining ingredients and cook together several minutes.
Split frankfurters and put into sauce.
Bake at 375 degrees for 50 to 60 minutes.
