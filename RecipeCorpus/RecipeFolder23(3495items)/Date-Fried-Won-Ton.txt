Title: Date Fried Won Ton
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
Won Ton Wrappers
1 lb Pitted dates, cut fine
1/2 c  Walnut meats, cut fine
1)    Combine dates and walnuts. 2) Place 1 TEASpoon of mixture on one
corner of Won Ton wrapper 3) Wrap as for coconut candy won ton and
fry in deep hot oil until golden brown. 4) An american version is to
roll date'filled Won ton while hot in powdered sugar. 5) A delicious
Won Ton wrapper may be made by mixing together 1 pound of cooked
mashed sweet potatoes, cooled, and 1 cup of flour. 6)  Work into a
stiff dough.  Pinch off a piece of dough the size of a walnut and
roll very thin.
