Title: Kanape Me Kasseri
Categories: Appetizer, Greek
Yield: 1 servings
Chopped.
Chopped olives or pickles>>>
Or relish.
1/2 lb Kasseri cheese
Sliced white bread
6 sl Mortadella or other salami>>
Trim crust from bread and cut into desired shapes with canape
cutter or sharp knife.  Top each pc. w/thin slice of cheese cut to
fit the sape of the bread.  Granist edges w/mortadella or other
salami, and the chopped olives, pickles, or relish.
