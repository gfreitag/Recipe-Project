Title: Tzatziki (Greek Cucumber and Yogurt Salad)
Categories: Salad, Greek
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1 ts Salt
1 pt Plain Yogurt
1    Unpeeled cucumber, chopped
-fine
1    Clove garlic, crushed
parsley

To the yogurt add the cucumber, garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, and
salt. Blend well with fork. Top with parsley. Refrigerate. Serve with
Melba toast as first course or as a side dish during dinner.
