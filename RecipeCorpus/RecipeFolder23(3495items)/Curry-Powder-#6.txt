Title: Curry Powder #6
Categories: Indian, Condiment, Curries
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Ground Allspice
1 tb Salt
1 tb Ground Black Pepper
1/2 tb Dry Mustard
1/4 ts Saffron
2 1/2 tb Coriander Seeds
2 ts Garlic Powder
1 tb Ground Cumin
2 ts Ground Tumeric
1 ts Ground Ginger
1 ts Chili Seasoning
Grind the coriander seeds, (sieve if any large husks remain). Blend
all of the ingredient together and store in an airtight jar. This
recipe makes 3 ounces of curry powder.

NOTE:  THIS IS MADRAS STYLE CURRY POWDER AND IS VERY HOT.

From How To Make Good Curries by Helen Lawson  Copyright 1973
