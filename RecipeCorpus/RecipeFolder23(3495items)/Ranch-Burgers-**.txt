Title: Ranch Burgers **
Categories: Ground beef, Bbq/grill, Burgers
Yield: 6 burgers
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
1/2 ts Black pepper
Sesame seed buns
Lettuce, tomatoes, onion
- slices (opt)
Ranch salad dressing
1 1/4 lb Ground beef
3/4 c  Ranch salad dressing
3/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/4 c  Onion; minced
1 ts Salt
In large bowl, combine ground beef, 3/4 cup salad dressing, bread
crumbs, onion, salt & pepper. Shape into 6 patties. Grill over medium
hot coals for 4-5 minutes for medium doneness. Place on sesame seed
buns with your choice of additions. Serve with generous amount of
additional salad dressing.
