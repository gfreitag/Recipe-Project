Title: Lemon Fish
Categories: Seafood, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Mild white fish
1/2 t  Italian herb seasoning
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Combine herb seasoning and lemon juice in a saucepan and bring to a
boil. Lower heat to simmer, add fish and cook 5-8 minutes. Do not
overcook.

~Trim & Thin 4-Ingredient Cookbook
