Title: Garlic Peanuts
Categories: Snack
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Garlic Powder
1 c  Raw Peanuts
For each cup of raw peanuts, start with a small amount of garlic
powder, about 1/4 teaspoon, stirring well to blend.  Increase garlic
powder to taste as the peanuts warm.  Spread out on a cookie sheet
