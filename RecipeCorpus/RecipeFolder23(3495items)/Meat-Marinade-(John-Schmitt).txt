Title: Meat Marinade (John Schmitt)
Categories: Condiment, Marinade
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 ts Parsley
1 tb Pepper
1/2 c  Wine vinegar
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  Oil
3/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Wostershire sauce
2 tb Dry mustard
2 1/2 ts Salt
Meat Marinade (John Schmitt)

No written directions, verbal = mix, spread on meat, let sit until
ready. Cook.
