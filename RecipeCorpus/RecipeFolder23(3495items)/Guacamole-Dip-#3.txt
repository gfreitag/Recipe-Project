Title: Guacamole Dip #3
Categories: Dip
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Diced Green Chilies, Drained
1 tb Lemon Juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
1 lg Avocado, Peeled And Mashed
1 sm Chopped Tomato
1/4 c  Minced Onion
Combine all ingredients and mix until blended.  Cover and chill until
serving time.  Makes 2 cups.  From: Los Angeles Times.
