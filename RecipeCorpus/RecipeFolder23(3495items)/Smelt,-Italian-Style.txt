Title: Smelt, Italian Style
Categories: Fish, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Shredded parmesan cheese
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1 lg Can Italian tomatoes (28
1 1/2 t  Oregano
2 t  Sugar
1/4    Chopped parsley
1 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
2 c  Sliced onions
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
-oz)
1    6 oz can tomato paste
1 1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
2 lb Smelt
Saute onions and garlic in oil until soft. Add tomatoes, tomato paste,
oregano, 1 tsp salt, sugar and pepper; mix well. Cover and cook
slowly, about 30 minutes, until slightly thickened and flavors blend.
Stir often during cooking. Spread sauce over bottom of 3- quart
baking dish. Arrange smelt in single layer on sauce. Sprinkle with
remaining 1/2 tsp salt and cheeses. Bake at 450 degrees for 15
minutes.
