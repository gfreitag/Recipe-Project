Title: Kansas City Burgers **
Categories: Ground beef, Bbq/grill, Burgers
Yield: 4 burgers
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
4    Buns; split, butter, toast
4 sl Swiss cheese
4 sl Bacon; cooked, crumbled
2    Green onions; sliced
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Frozen hash brown potatoes;
- thawed
2 tb Barbecue sauce
Add thawed hash brown potatoes and barbecue sauce to ground beef. Mix
well. Shape into 4 patties. Grill or broil to desired doneness. Place
each cooked burger on bun; top with cheese slice, crumbled bacon and
sliced green onion.
