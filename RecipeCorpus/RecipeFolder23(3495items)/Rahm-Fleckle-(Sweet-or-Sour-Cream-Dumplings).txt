Title: Rahm-Fleckle (Sweet or Sour Cream Dumplings)
Categories: German
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 l  Cream or sour cream (1/2 cup
-plus 1/2 Tbsp)
A few tart apples, peeled
-and thinly sliced
80 g  Butter (1/3 cup)
5    Potatoes, boiled in their
-jackets and then grated
5 tb To 6 tb flour
1    Egg
1 ds Salt
Mix the grated potatoes, flour, egg, and salt, and knead into a
dough. Roll out to 1/5 of an inch thickness, and put into a greased
pan.  Spread the cream our sour cream over the top, then cover with
the sliced apples. Bake at medium heat.  Cut into squares and serve
hot, covered with cream.

Serves 4.

From:  D'SCHWAEBISCH' KUCHE' by Aegidius Kolb and Leonhard Lidel,
Allgaeuer Zeitungsverlag, Kempten.  1976. (Translation/Conversion:
Karin Brewer) Posted by:  Karin Brewer, Cooking Echo, 8/92
