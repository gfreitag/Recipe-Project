Title: Basque Style Steamed Clams (Almejas Guisadas)
Categories: Seafood, European, Main dish, Clam/oyster
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Dry white wine
1/2 c  Water (or additional clam
-liquid)
1/4 ts Black pepper, freshly ground
4 qt Cherrystone clams
4    Cloves garlic, minced
1    Onion, about 1" in diameter,
-minced
1 tb Parsley, minced
Wash clams thoroughly and place in a large kettle.  Add remaining
ingredients.  Cover and cook over medium heat for 20 minute.  Serve
hot in individual bowls, accomapanied by thick slices of french bread
and a green salad.
