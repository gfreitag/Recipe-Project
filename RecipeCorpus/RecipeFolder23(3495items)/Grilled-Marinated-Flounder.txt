Title: Grilled Marinated Flounder
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Vegetable oil
2    Cloves minced garlic
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 T  Soy sauce
Mix all ingredients and use to marinate 1 1/2 lb of fish for 1 to 2
hours. Grill in a basket over charcoal until fish flakes easily.
Serve with lemon slices.
