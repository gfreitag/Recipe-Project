Title: Carrots in Vinegar
Categories: Japanese, Condiment
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 lb Carrots thinly sliced (or
-broccoli or cauliflower)
Place carrots in a small saucepan with the vinegar.  Bring vinegar to
a boil; cover pan and reduce heat.  Cook at a simmer for about 8-10
min or until carrots are tender and liquid is absorbed.  Serve at
room temperature or chilled.  Serve with a dressing such as
mayonnaise or garnish with salted black sesame seeds.
