Title: Gado-Gado (Mixed Vegetables in Peanut Sauce)
Categories: Asian, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Bean Sprouts
1 lb New Potatoes
3    Eggs, Hard Boiled
1/2 lb Green Beans
20 oz Spinach, Fresh
1/2    Cabbage, Sliced Fine
Cook potatoes with the skins on. Blanch vegetables until tender-crisp.
Chill all cooked vegetables; arrange attractively.  Serve with Saus
Kacang.
