Title: Meatloaf Ole
Categories: Ground beef, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
1 md Tomato; Chopped
1/2 c  Green Onions; Sliced
2 tb Ketchup
1 tb Salsa
1/2 ts Seasoned Salt
1 1/2 lb Lean Ground Beef
3/4 c  Unseasoned Dry Bread Crumbs
1 lg Egg; Beaten
4 oz Green Chiles; Diced, 1 Can
1/2 c  Cheddar Cheese; Grated, 2 Oz
1 pk Taco Seasoning Mix; 1.25 Oz
Combine the first 6 ingredients in a large bowl, blending well.  Pat
the meat mixture into a 9 X 5 X 3-Inch loaf pan.  Bake, uncovered, in
350 Degree F. oven for about 1 hour or until the meat is cooked
through.  Let stand for 10 minutes before draining the fat and
removing the meat loaf from the pan.  Combine the remaining
ingredients in a small bowl.  Slice the meat loaf into 1/2-inch
slices and spoon the tomato mixture over the slices.  Serve with
Mexican Rice.
