Title: Mixed Italian Olives
Categories: Appetizer, Condiment
Yield: 6 servings
3    Stalks celery, chopped
1    Green pepper, chopped
1    Red pepper, chopped
1    Garlic clove, crushed
1/2 lb Green olives
1/2 lb Black olives
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Olive oil
Mixed Italian Olives

Freshly ground pepper to taste Oregano to taste

Crack the olives with a hammer or mallet until the pits show.
Combine the olives with the remaining ingredients. Let stand at room
temperature for 2 days. Store in refrigerator in sealed sterilized
jars.
