Title: Bulgogi (Marinated Sirloin Steak)
Categories: Asian, Beef
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Soy Sauce
1 lg Onion
2    Or 3 Cloves Garlic
1 ts Pepper
1 lb Sirloin Steak (cut into thin
-strips)
2 tb Sesame Oil
2 tb Cooking Sherry
2 tb Sugar
Mince garlic and chop onion (finely).  Mix all ingredients with the
steak strips.  Let marinate for 1 hour room temp. or 2 hours
refrigerated. Broil until done to your liking.  Serve over rice.

It says it makes 4 servings, but it's never enough for us.  I always
double it.
