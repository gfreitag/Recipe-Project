Title: Fried Bread Crumbs
Categories: Misc/food
Yield: 2 servings
2 c  Fine soft bread crumbs
INGREDIENTS:
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
Servings: Makes 2 cups

DIRECTIONS: Melt butter over high heat. Saute crumbs until golden
brown, stirring occasionally. Drain.

Source: The Best of Bon Appetit, Vol. 1 (1970)

From: Sallie Krebs
