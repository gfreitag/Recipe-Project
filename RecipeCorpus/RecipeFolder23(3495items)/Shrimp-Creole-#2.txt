Title: Shrimp Creole #2
Categories: Main dish, Vegetable, Cajun/creol, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Water
1    Bay leaf, crushed
1 ts Minced fresh parsley
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 lb Salad shrimp
* Serve over bed of rice*
3/4 c  Chopped onion
1    Clove garlic, pressed or
- minced
1 md Green pepper, finely chopped
1/2 c  Finely chopped celery
2 tb Butter
1 cn Tomato sauce
In medium skillet, saute' onion, garlic, green pepper, and celery in
butter, about 5 minutes, or until tender.  Remove from heat; stir in
tomato sauce, water, bay leaf, parsley, salt, and pepper.  Add
shrimp. Bring mixture to a boil; cook covered over medium heat 5
minutes.  Serve over fluffy white rice.

Yield: 2 servings.
