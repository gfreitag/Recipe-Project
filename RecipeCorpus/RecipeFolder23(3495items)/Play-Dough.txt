Title: Play Dough
Categories: Kids
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Salt
2 ts Cream of tartar
2 tb Cooking oil
1 ds Food colouring
1 c  Water
1 c  Flour
In a heavy saucepan, stir food colouring into water.  Add all
remaining ingredients.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly
with a wooden spoon until mixture thickens and draws away from the
sides of the pan. Form into a ball and cool. Pack play dough in a
zip-lock bag or air-tight container.

NB: Can also be used as a treat for birthday parties, just pack it
into individual decorative containers.

Source: Modern Woman magazine, June 1993 issue Shared by: Sharon
Herrington
