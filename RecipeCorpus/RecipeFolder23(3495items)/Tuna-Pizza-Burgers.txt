Title: Tuna Pizza Burgers
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 cn Diced celery
2    Flat cans tuna fish in water
8 oz Tomato sauce
1    Grated cheese of choice
6    Bread
1/3 cn Mayonnaise
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Oregano
1/4 t  Basil
1 T  Minced onion
Drain tuna. Blend mayonnaise, salt, basil, and oregano. Toss with
celery, tuna, and onion. Toast 6 slices bread or 6 English muffin
halves, or 6 hamburger bun halves. Pile tuna mixture on bread. Spoon
on tomato sauce. Sprinkle with cheese. Broil about 10 minutes.
