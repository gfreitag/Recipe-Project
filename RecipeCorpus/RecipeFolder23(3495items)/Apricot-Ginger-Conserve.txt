Title: Apricot Ginger Conserve
Categories: Condiment, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Grated orange rind
1 ts Grated lemon rind
2 1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
3 c  Dried apricots
3/4 c  Sliced, candied ginger
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
5 c  Cold water
Wash apricots.  Add water, ginger, orange and lemon juice, grated
rind, and sugar.  Stir until well mixed.  Simmer slowly, stirring
frequently, until thick and clear.  Add nuts and cook 5 minutes. The
Household Searchlight
