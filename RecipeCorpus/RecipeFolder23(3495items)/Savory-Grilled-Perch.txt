Title: Savory Grilled Perch
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Onion powder
1/4 t  Garlic powder
4 t  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 t  Salt
2 1/2 T  Worcestershire sauce
4 sm Fish, cleaned
Combine oil, onion powder, salt, garlic, Worcestershire sauce and
lemon juice. Brush fish inside and out with mixture. Place on grill
six inches from hot coals. Cook for five minutes. Turn over and brush
with remaining mixture. Cook three more minutes or until flesh flakes
at touch of fork.
