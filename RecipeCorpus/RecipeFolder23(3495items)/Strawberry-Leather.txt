Title: Strawberry Leather
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Sugar
2 c  Fresh strawberry puree
1 c  Applesauce
Combine pureed berries and applesauce with lemon juice and sugar.
Line a 10x15 in jelly roll pan with plastic wrap.  Pour fruit into
pan and spread evenly.  Bake about 5 hours at 200F or until leather
pulls away from wrap.

Flip leather onto counter, peel away plastic and remove any mixture
that has not "set."  Cut into slices and wrap each in plastic.

Source:  Sun Newspaper clipping June 28/93 from The Garden (U-pick
farm) booklet, shared but not tested by Elizabeth Rodier, Calgary,
Alberta
