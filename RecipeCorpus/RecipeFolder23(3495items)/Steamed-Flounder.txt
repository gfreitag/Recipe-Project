Title: Steamed Flounder
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Hot, cooked rice
1 t  Salt
2 T  Dry sherry
3 T  Vegetable oil
4    Scallions cut into 2-inch
1 1/2 lb Flounder fillets
1 t  Sugar
1 T  Soya sauce
1 T  Grated ginger
-lengths
Place salted fish in a steamer. Either one of those oriental basket
steamers that fit on top of a kettle or a small canner or other pot
with a basket will do. In either case the fish should be placed on a
layer of aluminum foil. Get a small amount of water under the steamer
boiling/ Steam covered for 10 minutes. Transfer to serving platter
and serve with hot rice and additional soya sauce if desired.
