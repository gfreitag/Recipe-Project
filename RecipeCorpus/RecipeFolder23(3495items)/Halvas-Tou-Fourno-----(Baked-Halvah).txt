(Baked Halvah)
Title: Halvas Tou Fourno
Categories: Dessert, Greek
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-------------------------THE HALVAH------------------------------
4 ea Eggs, separated
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Slivered almonds, blanched
3 c  Farina (Cream of Wheat)
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Butter
MMMMM-------------------------THE SYRUP------------------------------
1 x  Piece of stick cinnamon
2 or 3 whole cloves
2 1/2 c  Sugar
3 c  Water
Mix farina with cinnamon and set aside.  Cream butter in a bowl.
In another bowl, beat egg yolks with the sugar until thick.  Add to
the butter and beat very well.  Beat egg whites until stiff.  Then
fold alternately with the farina into the butter-egg mixture.  Add
almonds; mix gently. Put into a buttered pan.  Bake in preheated 350
F. oven for 45 min.

Prepare syrup by boiling all of the ingreds. for abt. 8 min.
Remove and discard cinnamon and cloves.  Pour syrup over hot halvah.
Cool, serve.
