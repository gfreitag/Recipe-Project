Title: Scalloped Fish
Categories: Fish, 1941
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Buttered bread crumbs
-Salt, pepper, and paprika
1 1/2 c  Medium white sauce
1 T  Chopped pimento
2 c  Cooked flaked fish
Combine fish, white sauce, pimento, and seasonings.  Pour into
well-oiled baking dish.  Cover with crumbs.  Bake in moderate oven
(400 F) 30 minutes, or until sauce bubbles through the crumbs. 6
servings. The Household Searchlight
