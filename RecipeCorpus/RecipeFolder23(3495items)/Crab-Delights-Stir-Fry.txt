Title: Crab Delights Stir Fry
Categories: Chinese, Seafood, Stir fry
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Ground ginger
Soy sauce optional
8 oz Crab delights flakes
-or chunks
10 oz Frozen oriental style
-vegetables with seasoning
Combine crab delights, vegetables with seasoning, garlic and ginger.
Heat according to directions on vegetables package. Stir and serve.
Season with soy sauce, if desired.
