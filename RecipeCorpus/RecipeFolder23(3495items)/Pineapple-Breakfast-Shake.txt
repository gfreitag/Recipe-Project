Title: Pineapple Breakfast Shake
Categories: Snack
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Skim Milk
1/4 ts Vanilla Extract
20 oz Pineapple Chunks *
1 md Banana, Cut Into Chunks
* Pineapple chunks in unsweetened juice.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Drain pineapple, reserving 3/4 cup juice.  Measure 1/2 cup
pineapple chunks and save the remainder for another use.  Place all
ingredients in a blender bowl and process until smooth.  Pour into
glasses and serve..  85 calories per serving.  From: Syd's Cookbook.
