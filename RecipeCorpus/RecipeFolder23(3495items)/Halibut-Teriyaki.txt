Title: Halibut Teriyaki
Categories: Fish, Japanese
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Soya sauce
1    Clove garlic
1 lb Halibut steaks
1/2 c  White wine
1 t  Sugar
Mash garlic with side of knife. Mix soya sauce. wine, garlic and
sugar. Heat to boiling and pour over fish. Marinate 15 to 20 minutes.
Broil fish 4 inches from heat for 10 minutes or until flesh flakes
easily.
