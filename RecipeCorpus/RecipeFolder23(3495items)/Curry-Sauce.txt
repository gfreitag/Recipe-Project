Title: Curry Sauce
Categories: Asian, Condiment, Curries
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Celery
2 tb Coconut
4 ts Chutney
1    Clove garlic
1    Bay leaf
1/4 ts Seasoned slat
Pinch dried thyme
1 tb Butter
4 tb Curry powder
14 1/2 oz Chicken broth
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Sliced carrot
1    Sm tomato, seeded, chopped
1    Sm Apple, peeled, chopped
Cook butter and curry powder 1 to 2 min.  Add chicken broth, onion,
carrot, tomato, apple, celert, coconut, chutney, garlic, bay leaf,
salt and thyme. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 1 hr.
Remove bay leaf. Processs or blend the mixture till smooth.  Makes
about 1 2/3 cups
