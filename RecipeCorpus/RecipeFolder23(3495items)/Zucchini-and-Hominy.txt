Title: Zucchini and Hominy
Categories: Tex/mexican, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
20 oz Hominy; Drained, 1 cn
2 tb Lime Juice
1 tb Chile Powder
1 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1/4 c  Onion; Chopped, 1 Sm
2 tb Margarine Or Butter
2 tb Vegetable Oil
3    Zucchini; Md., *
2 c  Tomatoes; Chopped, 2 Md
*    Cut the zucchini into 1/2-inch pieces.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~- Cook and stir the onion in the margarine and oil in a 10-inch
skillet over medium heat until tender.  Stir in the remaining
ingredients and cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the
zucchini is tender, about 10 to 15 minutes.
