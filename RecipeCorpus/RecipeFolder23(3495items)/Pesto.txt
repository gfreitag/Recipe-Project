Title: Pesto
Categories: Italian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Pine nuts
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
More olive oil (for
- storage purposes)
1 1/2 c  Packed fresh basil leaves
3    Cloves garlic; peeled
1 tb Romano; grated
2 tb Parmesan; grated
1/2 c  Olive oil
In a food processor, combine the basil and garlic and process to a
coarse puree.  Add the cheese and process to blend. With the motor
running, add the olive oil in a thin steady stream through the feed
tube. Add the pine nuts and blend the mixture until smooth. Season
with salt and pepper if you wish.

To store, transfer to a jar and pour a thin layer of olive oil over
the top to preserve the pesto. Keep refrigerated.
