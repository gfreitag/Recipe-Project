Title: Dry-Fried Shrimp with Ginger
Categories: Chinese, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
- Dry sherry
2 tb Light soy sauce
1/2 ts Dark soy sauce
1 pn Salt
1/4 c  Chicken broth
4 tb Peanut oil
2 lb Cleaned, unshelled shrimp
2 tb Minced garlic
1 ts Minced fresh ginger root
4 tb Coarsely chopped scallions
2 tb Chinese rice wine, or:
HEAT A WOK over a high flame. Add the oil, and when it is hot,
stir-fry the shrimp quickly until they turn pink. Add the remaining
ingredients. Continue stirring until the shrimp are cooked (about 3
minutes).
