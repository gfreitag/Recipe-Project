Title: Orange Honey Mustard
Categories: Herbs/spice, Condiment, Salsa/sauce, Spread
Yield: 48 servings
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Honey
2 c  Cider vinegar
1    Grated rind of large orange
1/2 c  Flour,all-purpose
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Mustard powder
2 ts Turmeric,ground
1. Combine flour, sugar, mustard powder and turmeric in small bowl.
Stir in water. Let stand for 2 hours.

2. Combine mustard mixture, honey and vinegar in top of double boiler.
Cook, whisking often, over boiling water until mixture just begins to
thicken, about 3 minutes. Cook 2 minutes longer, whisking constantly,
until slightly thickened. Stir grated orange rind into just-cooked
mustard mixture (Mixture will thicken completely on standing.) turn
into clean storage jar. Cover and refrigerate at least overnight or
forup to 1 month.
