Title: Grilled Pizza Sandwiches (Sandwich Machine)
Categories: Sandwich, Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
4    Slices cheese
1    Garlic powder
1    Butter or margarine
2/3 c  Pizza sauce (canned)
8    Slices bread
4    Large slices salami
Spread pizza sauce on one side of each bread slice.  Top one slice
with salami slice then cheese slice.  Sprinkle with garlic powder.
Top with second slice of bread, sauce side down.  Butter both sides.

Grill

From Better Homes & Gardens, April 1959, by Mrs. J. C. Baker, Solon,
Ohio Posted by: Sheila Exner - October 1991
