Title: Chicken in Spicy Brown Sauce
Categories: Tex/mexican, Chicken
Yield: 6 servings
4 ts Unsweetened cocoa powder
1 ts Ground cumin
1 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Garlic salt
3    Whole chicken breasts,split
-,boned and skinned
2 tb Vegetable oil
1    15 oz can tomato sauce
1/2 c  Picante sauce
Dash EACH: cloves,nutmeg,ground allspice

Pound chicken to 1/2" thick.Lightly brown in oil in large skillet,
about 2 minutes on each side;drain off fat.Combine remaining
ingredients;mix well.Pour over chicken in skillet.Bring to
boil.Reduce heat;cover and simmer,gently,10 minutes.Remove chicken to
serving platter;keep warm.Cook and stir sauce until slightly
thickened,about 3 to 5 minutes.Spoon sauce over chicken.

Makes 6 servings.
