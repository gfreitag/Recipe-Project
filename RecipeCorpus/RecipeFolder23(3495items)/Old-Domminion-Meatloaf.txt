Title: Old Domminion Meatloaf
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Meatloaf
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Finely chopped green pepper
2 ea Medium eggs
1/8 ts Dried basil
2 sl Dry bread
1/4 c  Catsup
2 lb Lean ground beef
1 c  Yellow onion
1 c  Shredded potato
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Campbell's Vegetable Soup
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Tear dry bread into pieces. Shred 2
or 3 medium potatoes. Mix ingrediants, except catsup, in large mixing
bowl, until thoroughly combined and mixture holds together. Spray
9x24 ovenproof dish with Pam or some other vegegetable oil spray.
Place meatloaf mixture in dish trying to leave 1/2-inch space around
edges.  Put catsup on top of mixture.  Place in oven and bake for
approximately 1 hour.
