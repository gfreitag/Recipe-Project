Title: Lamb Marinade and Barbecue
Categories: Marinade, Main dish, Meat, Bbq/grill
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Soy sauce; light
2 tb Dry mustard
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1 1/2 tb Parsley, chopped
1/3 c  Lemon juice
-LOIS' KITCHEN
8 lb Lamb leg; boned and
-butterflied
1 1/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Worchestershire Sauce
2    Garlic clove; diced
Mix ingredients together. Marinate butterflied lamb roast overnight,
basting as needed. Cook over hot grill, baste as needed. Best served
medium to rare. Makes enough marinade for about 6-8lbs of lamb.

This is Lois' recipe and is our annual July 4th BBQ; right after the
CLYDE BEATTY CIRCUS/still under a tent!!!!
