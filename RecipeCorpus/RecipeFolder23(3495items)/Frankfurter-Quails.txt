Title: Frankfurter Quails
Categories: Meat, Snack, Mennonite, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings  Make a lengthwise slit in the frankfurter.
Fill with a stick of sharp cheese 1/4-inch thick.
Wrap each frankfurter in spiral fashion with a whole slice of bacon.
Fasten each with a toothpick.
Place split side up in a baking dish.
Bake at 425 degrees for 20 minutes, turn if necessary to cook the
bacon.
