Title: Texas Whoppers
Categories: Snack, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Sweet Pickle
1/4 c  Sour Cream
1/4 c  Cheddar Cheese, grated
4    Rolls, Large, split
4    FRANKS
1/2 c  Kidney Beans, drained
1    Onion, small
2 tb Green Pepper
1 tb Hot Sauce
Chop FRANKS. Add kidney beans, onion, green pepper and pickle. Mix
with sour cream and hot sauce. Chill. Spoon mixture on bottom half of
roll; sprinkle with cheese; replace top roll. Serve with dill pickles.
