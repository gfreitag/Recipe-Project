Title: Onion Patties
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
2 tb Cornmeal
1 ts Pepper
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Parsley
3/4 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
3/4 c  Milk
1 tb Pepper sauce
1 c  Finely chopped onions
Mix all ingredients. Drop by teaspoon into hot oil. Fry each side
about two minutes.  Drain on paper towel

Source: Rock Springs Sweet Onion Festival Cook Book 1992
