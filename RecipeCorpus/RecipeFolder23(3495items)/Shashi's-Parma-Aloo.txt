Title: Shashi's Parma Aloo
Categories: Potato, Snack, Appetizer
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Cumin, ground
Juice of 1/2 small lemon
1 T  Tomato paste
8 sm New potatoes
1 ts Vegetable oil
1/2 ts Turmeric
pn Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Garam Masala
Preheat the oven to 350 Combine all the ingredients, except the
potatoes in a bowl. Mix to a paste. Peel the potatoes. Cut a 1/2 inch
wedge out of each potato. Rub the space with spice paste. Reinsert
the wedge and rub the potatoes all over with the remaining spice.
Place on a baking sheet and bake for 30 - 40 minutes, turning every
10 minutes, until tender. Serve at once.
