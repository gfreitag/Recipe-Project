Title: Broiled Sole with Mushroom Sauce
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Salsa/sauce, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1    Minced green onion
1 T  White wine
1 lb Sole fillets
2 T  Margarine or butter
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
Place fillets on lightly greased broiler pan.  Dot with 1 tablespoon
margarine or bautter.  Broil 4 inches from heat for 10 minutes per
inch of fish.  Heat remaining margarine or butter in small skillet.
Saute mushrooms and onions.  Add wine and heat.  Pour over broiled
fish when serving.
