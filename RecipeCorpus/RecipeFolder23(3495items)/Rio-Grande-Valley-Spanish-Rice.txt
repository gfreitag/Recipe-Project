Title: Rio Grande Valley Spanish Rice
Categories: Vegetable, Tex/mexican, Tomato
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 ts Black pepper (chopped)
1/2 c  Bell pepper (chopped)
1/2 c  Onion
1 c  Tomato sauce
3 c  Boiling salted water
2 tb Bacon grease or oil
1 1/2 c  Rice
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Cumin seed
1/2 ts Oregano (Mexican)
In hot bacon grease, cook 1 1/2 Cups rice til golden brown. Add
seasonings, chopped pepper and onion, and tomato sauce. Pour all this
into boiling water. Reduce heat, cover and cook 12-15 minutes. Remove
from heat, leave covered and  let steam  a few  minutes. Doubles
nicely
