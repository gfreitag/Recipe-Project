Title: Baked Stuffed Herring (Irish)
Categories: Fish, European
Yield: 4 servings
1    Salt and pepper
8    Herrings, cleaned
300 ml Hard cider
1    Bay leaf, well crumbled
1    Fresh ground pepper
4 tb Breadcrumbs (heaping)
1 ts Parsley, chopped
1 sm Egg, beaten
1    Juice and rind of lemon
1 pn Nutmeg
Directions: First make the stuffing by mixing the breadcrumbs,
parsley, beaten egg, lemon juice and peel, and salt and pepper. Stuff
the fish with the mixture. Lay in an ovenproof dish, close together;
add the cider, crumbled bay leaf and salt and pepper.  Cover with
foil and bake at 350F for about 35 minutes.  Serves 4.
