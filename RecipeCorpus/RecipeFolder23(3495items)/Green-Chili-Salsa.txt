Title: Green Chili Salsa
Categories: Tex/mexican, Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
1    Clove Chopped Garlic
5    Tomatoes
1 tb Oil
1    Chopped Onion
1 cn Chopped Green Chili
Heat oil, add onion and simmer for a couple of minutes.  Add chili
and simm a couple of more minutes.  Add garlic and tomatoes, simmer
10 more minutes. Add salt and pepper to your taste.  Now blend if you
used natural chilies. Blend anyways LA RA HOT
