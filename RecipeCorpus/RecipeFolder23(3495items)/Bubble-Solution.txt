Title: Bubble Solution
Categories: Kids
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Sugar
1    Food coloring
1/3 c  Dish soap or baby shampoo
1 1/4 c  Water
Servings:  1

Combine ingredients and pour into an unbreakable bottle. To blow
bubbles, experiment using plastic straws, pipe cleaners formed into
loops, strawberry baskets from grocery store, spoons with holes in
them and other items you think of.
