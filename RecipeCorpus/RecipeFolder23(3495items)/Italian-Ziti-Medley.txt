Title: Italian Ziti Medley
Categories: Italian, Pasta, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  All-purpose flour
8 oz Cheddar cheese shredded
1/2 c  Grated parmesan cheese
8 oz Cut Ziti - uncooked
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 lb Italian sausage *
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1/3 c  Chopped onion
2 1/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Green pepper strips
* Sliced into coins

In 4-quart saucepan, brown sausage.  Remove from pan and drain off
fat. Melt butter and cook mushrooms, pepper and onion until tender
but not brown.  Blend flour into vegetable mixture.  Gradually stir
in milk. Cook, stirring consstantly over med-low heat until mixture
begins to boil.
Boil and stir 1 minute.  Blend in 1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese, parmesan
cheese and pepper until cheeses are melted and mixture is smooth.
Cook ziti according to package directions, and drain.  Stir hot
cooked ziti and sausage into vegetable cheese mixture.  pour into
buttered 2-quart casserole.  Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 Cup cheddar
cheese.  Bake covered at 350 for 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
