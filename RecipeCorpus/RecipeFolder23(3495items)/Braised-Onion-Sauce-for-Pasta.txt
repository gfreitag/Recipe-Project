Title: Braised Onion Sauce for Pasta
Categories: Pasta, Italian
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 lb Onions, peeled, sliced
1/4 c  Madeira grated cheese
1/2 lb Butter
1 tb Sugar
Saute onions in butter. Stir in sugar, lower heat and cook very
slowly one hour.  Stir in the wine, cook briefly, and pour over a
pound of freshly cooked pasta. Pass the Parmesan.
