Title: Grilled Flounder with Lemon & Thyme
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Salt
1/2 t  Dried thyme
2 T  Butter
3    Lemons
1/2 t  Pepper
1 1/2 lb Fish fillets
Squeeze 1/2 cup lemon juice from lemons. Combine the juice, salt
pepper and thyme in a shallow dish.  Add the fish & turn to coat.
Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, turning 2 or 3 times.
Drain the fish and brush with melted butter and place on preheated
grill. Broil until opaque throughout.
