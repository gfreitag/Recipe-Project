Title: Trout Almandine
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Milk
1/8 t  Black pepper
2    Sticks of butter or
-margarine
4    Fillets of trout
1 t  Salt
1/2 c  Flour
-
1/2 c  Chopped almonds
Dip fillets in milk. Season with salt & pepper. Coat with flour. Melt
butter or margarine in skillet and cook fillets, browning evenly on
both sides. Remove fish from skillet and saute almonds for about 10
minutes. Serve almonds over trout fillets.
