Title: Micro Poached Flounder
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Butter
-a few dros of honey
2    Flounder fillets
1    Yellow onion, sliced thin
1/4 c  White wine
-a sprinkle of nutmeg
Cook onions in butter about one minue  on high in microwave. Stir in
wine and spieces. including salt and pepper if desired. Add fillets,
turning to coat. Cover with wax paper and cook on high for 8 minutes,
basting and turning pan after 4.
