Title: Honey Cure for Ham
Categories: Pork, British
Yield: 6 servings

You will need a salting pan.  Rub the ham all over with the salt,
saltpeter, and pepper.  Put it in a salting pan.  Turn and rub it
well with the mixture every day for 4 days.  Then pour the honey over
the ham and rub it in.  Rub the ham with the pickle twice a week for
a month before you hang it up to dry.

Time:  1 month plus.

From:  "The Old World Kitchen - The Rich Tradition of European Peasant
