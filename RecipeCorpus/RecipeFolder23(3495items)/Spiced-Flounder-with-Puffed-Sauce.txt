Title: Spiced Flounder with Puffed Sauce
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Old Bay Seasoning
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
-juice of 1/2 lemon
1 lb Flounder fillets
3    Egg whites
1 T  Fresh parsley
-pinch salt
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. In a greased baking dish, place fish and
sprinkle with Old Bay Seasoning. Bake for 10 minutes, remove and
cool. Beat egg whites stiff. Fold in mayonnaise, parsley, lemon juice
and salt. Spread mixture over fish and return to oven for 15 minutes.
Note: Trout, perch, striped bass or other firm fish can be used in
this recipe.
