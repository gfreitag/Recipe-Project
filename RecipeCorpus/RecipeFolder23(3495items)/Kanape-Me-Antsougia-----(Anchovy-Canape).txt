(Anchovy Canape)
Title: Kanape Me Antsougia
Categories: Appetizer, Greek
Yield: 1 servings
5 ea Hard cooked egg whites, chop
Chopped olives
Capers
1 cn Flat anchovy fillets
2 ea Cooked beets, chopped
Bread, trimmed and cut>>>>
Into rectangles.
Place an anchovy fillet lengthwise down the center of each piece of
bread.  On one side of it, put the chopped egg whites topped with a
few capers; on the other side, put the chopped beets topped with the
chopped olives.
