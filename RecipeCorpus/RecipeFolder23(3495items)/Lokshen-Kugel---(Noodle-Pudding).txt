Title: Lokshen Kugel - (Noodle Pudding)
Categories: Dessert, Pasta, Jewish
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Vegetable Oil
1 c  Unsweetened Apple Juice
1/2 c  Raisins, dark or golden
1/4 c  Walnuts -or- Pecans, chopped
2    Eggs
1 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Ground Nutmeg
2 1/2 c  Cooked Broad Noodles
Keywords: Vegetarian, Ovo

Beat the eggs and sugar until fluffy.  Add the remaining ingredients,
except the nuts. Pour into a well-oiled 2-quart casserole dish or an
8-inch baking pan. Sprinkle on the nuts.

Bake in a 350-degree oven for 40 to 50 minutes, or until browned.

Serves 6

One Serving = Calories: 225 Carbohydrates: 33 Protein: 6 Fat: 8
Sodium: 27 Potassium: 215 Cholesterol: 88

Exchange Value: 1-1/2 Bread Exchanges + 1-1/2 Fruit Exchanges 1 1/2
Fat Exchanges

Source: Holiday Cookbook, American Diabetes Association, ISBN
0-13-024894-0, by Betty Wedman, M.S.,R.D.
