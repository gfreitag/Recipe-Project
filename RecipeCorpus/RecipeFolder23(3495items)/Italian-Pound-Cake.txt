Title: Italian Pound Cake
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Italian
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Salt
Grated semi-sweet chocolate
-or sliced strawberries
1 ts Vanilla
FROSTING
8 oz Semi-sweet chocolate bits
1/2 c  Strong brewed coffee
10 tb Unsalted butter, chilled
1/2 lb Butter
2/3 lb Ricotta cheese
1 2/3 c  Sugar
8 ts Sugar
5    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Cake flour
4 ts Whipping cream
CAKE/FILLING

Cake:  Cream butter, slowly adding sugar until light.  Add eggs one
at a time.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Bake in buttered and
floured 9 x 5 loaf pan at 325 degrees for 1-1/4 to 1 1/2 hours or
until done. Cool in pan for 5 minutes before turning onto rack. Slice
horizontally into three sections.

Filling:  Combine first four ingredients in blender until smooth.
Fold in grated chocolate or strawberries.  Spread evenly between
layers.

Frosting:  Melt chocolate with coffee over very low heat. Beat in
butter a small amount at a time.  Continue beating until thick and of
spreading consistency.  Frost cake and garnish.
