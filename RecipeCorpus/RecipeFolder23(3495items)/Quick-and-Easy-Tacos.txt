Title: Quick and Easy Tacos
Categories: Tex/mexican, Snack
Yield: 5 servings
1 c  Lettuce
3/4 c  Tomato
1 c  Velvetta salsa Dip
1/2 lb Ground beef, cooked drained
10    Taco shells
Stir velveeta cheese spread salsa dip into cooked meat. Fill taco
shells with meat mixture,  Top with lettuce and tomato
