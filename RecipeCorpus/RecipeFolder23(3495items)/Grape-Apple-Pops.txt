Title: Grape Apple Pops
Categories: Kids, Snack, Apples
Yield: 9 servings
23 oz Jar MOTTS Chunky Apple Sauce
9    (3 oz) cold drink cups
9    Wooden sticks
1/2    (12 oz) can (3/4 cup) frozen
-grape juice concentrate,
-thawed
In medium bowl, combine juice concentrate and apple sauce; blend
well. Fill each cup with about 1/3 cup juice mixture. Place cups in
freezer until partially frozen, about 1 hour. Insert sticks; freeze
until firm, about 2 hours. To serve, remove drink cups. Makes 9 pops.
