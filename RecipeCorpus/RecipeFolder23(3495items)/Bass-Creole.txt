Title: Bass Creole
Categories: Fish, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Tabasco
1    Bay leaf
2 T  Lemon juice
1/4 lb Sliced mushrooms
1 lb Bass fillets
2 T  Butter or margarine
1 md Chopped onion
1/4 t  Tarragon leaves
1 c  Tomatoes, peeled and drained
1    Green pepper chopped
Melt butter in skillet. Add onion and green peppers and saute until
tender. Add mushrooms and cook another 3 minutes. Add tomatoes, lemon
juice. tarragon, bay leaf, tabasco and salt. Cover and simmer 20
minutes.Add fish and simmer until fish flakes easily with fork, about
12 to 15 minutes.
