Title: Five Spice Baked Chicken
Categories: Chinese, Chicken
Yield: 1 servings
1    Carrot, chopped
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 tb Soy sauce
Pepper, dash
3 tb Rice
2    Green onion, chopped
1/3 c  Water
1/4 ts Five spice
1/2    Chicken breast
Place first four ingredients on foil with dull side down. Place
chicken breast on top. Mix together remaining ingredients and pour
over breast. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 to 50 minutes.
