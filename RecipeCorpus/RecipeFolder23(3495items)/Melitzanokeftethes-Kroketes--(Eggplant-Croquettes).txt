Title: Melitzanokeftethes Kroketes  (Eggplant Croquettes)
Categories: Vegetable, Main dish, Greek
Yield: 1 servings
Oil for frying
Eggplants
Cracker meal
MMMMM------------------------THE MIXTURE-----------------------------
1 tb Baking powder
2 tb Parsley, chopped
1 ts Onion, chopped
Salt & pepper to taste
1 1/2 c  Bread crumbs
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Kefalotiri cheese, grated OR
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese, grated
Wash the eggplants and pierce in several places with a fork.  Boil
in lightly salted water until tender; drain well.  Remove skin and
mas the pulp; measure it.  Mix 2 c. of eggplant pulp with the amount
of ingredients listed above and blend well.  Shape into croquettes or
patties. Roll in cracker meal, and fry until golden brown.
