(Liver)
Title: Tziyerosarmas
Categories: Meat, Main dish, Greek
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Milk
Salt & pepper to taste
5 ea Eggs
1 ea (or 2) Sausage casings
4 tb Butter, melted
1 ea Lamb's liver (or calf)
1/2 c  Raw rice
1 bn Scallions, chopped
2 ts Dill, chopped
1 ts Mint, chopped
For stuffing, skin the liver and cut it into small pcs. with
scissors; place in a bowl.  Add rice, scallions, dill, mint, salt &
pepper; mix well.

Wash the casing and dip it into warm water, to open well.  Place
the stuffing in the casing (as you would stuff a sausage).  Fold it
carelully and place on a buttered pan with the folded side down.
Pour the melted butter over this and roast in a 325 F. oven for 45
min.
