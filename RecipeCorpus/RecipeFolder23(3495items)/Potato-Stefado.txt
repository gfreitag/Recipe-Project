Title: Potato Stefado
Categories: Potato, Main dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1    Bay leaf
1 ts Oregano , dried
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Cumin, ground
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1 c  Stock
Crumbled Feta, fresh parsley
... chopped walnuts for
... garnish
3/4 lb Mushrooms, quartered
3 lg Round red potatoes, cut into
... 1 1/2 inch chunks
8    Shallots, quartered
4    Garlic, crushed
1 lg Cauliflower flowerets
1 cn Tomato paste (6 oz)
1 T  Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 c  Parsley, chopped
Preheat oven to 350 Combine all ingredients in a flameproof casserole.
Bring to a boil, then remove from the heat. Cover tightly and bake
for 1 to 1 1/4 hours. Garnish each serving with some Feta, Parsley,
and Walnuts.
