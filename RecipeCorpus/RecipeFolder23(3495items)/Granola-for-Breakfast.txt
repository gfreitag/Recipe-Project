Title: Granola for Breakfast
Categories: Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 ts Cinnamon
1 1/4 c  Honey
3/4 c  Sterling oil (vegetable oil)
1 1/2 c  Assorted nut, chopped
1 qt Rolled oats
1 c  Rolled wheat
1 c  Rolled rye
1 c  Rolled barley
1 ts Nutmeg
Toss grains and spices, mix well. Mix honey and oil thoroughly and
blend in the mixture of grains & spices. Spread on a sheet pan and
toast in a 300 degree oven. (times vary-toast until a light-dark
golden brown) Let cool on the pan thoroughly and store in an air
tight container. While the mixture is cooling toss in raisins  &/or
other dried fruit like dates, or what ever you would like.
