Title: Garlic Mackerel
Categories: Fish, British
Yield: 1 servings
1    Clove garlic
-butter for frying
4 lg Mackerel (or 8 small)
-Seasoned flour
-Lemon juice
Mince garlic very finely  Divide between the fish and rub in well.
Roll mackerel in beaten egg and then in flour.  Fry in butter for 4-5
minutes each side.  Sprinkle with lemon juice and serve.
