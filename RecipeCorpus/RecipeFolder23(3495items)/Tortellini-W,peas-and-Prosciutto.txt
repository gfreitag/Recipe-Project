Title: Tortellini W/peas and Prosciutto
Categories: Italian, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
6 tb Grated parmesan
3/4 c  Peas *
1 1/2 oz Prosciutto (cut)
15 oz Tortellini (cheese)
1 1/2 c  Whipping cream
1 pn Nutmeg
* Frozen tiny, thawed and drained.

Cook tortellini in a large pot of boiling water until barely tender,
stirring occasionally.  Drain.  Meanwhile bring cream to boil in large
saucepan.  Reduce heat.  Add nutmeg and simmer until slightly
thickened, about 8 minutes.  Return tortellini to pot.  Add warm
cream, parmesan, peas & prosciutto.  Simmer over low heat until
tortellini are tender and sauce thickens, stirring occasionally.
About 4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
