Title: Green Mayonnaise
Categories: Fish, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Parsley chopped
1 t  Tarragon leaves chopped
1 t  Dill weed chopped
2 c  Mayonnaise
1 t  Chives chopped
1 t  Chervil chopped
All seasonings should be chopped fine. Combine all ingredients and mix
well. Let stand in refrigerator for 2 hours before serving. Best with
salmon.
