Title: Pear Cranberry Conserve
Categories: Condiment, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Cranberries
3 c  Sugar
2 c  Dried pears
3 c  Water
Remove cores from pears.  Wash and chop.  Combine pears, cranberries,
water, and sugar.  Simmer until thick.  Stir frequently to prevent
burning. The Household Searchlight
