Title: Tamer Bi Sumsum - Dates with Sesame Seeds
Categories: Snack, Middle east
Yield: 10 servings
2 tb Sesame seeds
20    Dates stuffed with almonds
-(Madinah dates)
Place the sesame seeds in a small frying pan over low heat and roast
until golden, stirring constantly.  Remove and cool.  Roll each date
in the seeds to coat and arrange on a tray.  Serve with Gahwa (Arabic
Coffee). Serves 10 people.
