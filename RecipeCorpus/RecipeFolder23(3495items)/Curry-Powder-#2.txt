Title: Curry Powder #2
Categories: Condiment, Curries, Indian
Yield: 6 servings
6    Green cardamoms
6    Cloves
6    Fresh curry leaves
1 ts Cayenne
1 oz Coriander seeds
1/2 oz Cumin seeds
1 tb Fennel seeds
1 ts Fenugreek seeds
Small piece of cinnamon
Dry roast the whole spices in a heavy frying pan over medium heat
until they turn dark brown, stirring and shaking frequently. Let
cool. Combine with remaining ingredients and grind into a powder.
Gives a darker taste than Indian curry.
