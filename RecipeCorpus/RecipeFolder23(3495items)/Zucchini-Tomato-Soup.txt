Title: Zucchini-Tomato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 8 servings
-lengthwise and sliced (2
-cups)
1 cn (8-ounce) whole kernel corn,
-drained
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Water
1 cn (18-ounce) (2 1/4 cups)
-tomato juice
2    Med. zucchini, quarted
In 2-quart saucepan cook onion and green pepper in water, covered,
about 5 minutes or till vegetables are tender. Add tomato juice,
zucchini, corn, and salt. Simmer covered, 30 minutes. Cool. Stir into
buttermilk.

Cover; chill.

Makes 8 to 10 servings.
