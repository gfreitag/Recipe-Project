Title: Tomato Bean Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Cooked tomatoes
Salt
2 c  Navy beans
1/4 lb Diced bacon or salt pork
Wash beans.  Cover with cold water.  Add bacon or salt pork. Cover.
Heat to boiling.  Simmer until tender, but not mushy, adding water as
needed. Season to taste.  Add tomatoes.  Simmer 30 minutes. Serve
hot. 6 servings.

Mrs. Charles T. O'Connor, Homer, NE.
