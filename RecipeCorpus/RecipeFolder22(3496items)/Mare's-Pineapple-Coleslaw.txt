Title: Mare's Pineapple Coleslaw
Categories: Salad
Yield: 10 servings
1 sm Onion, or 2 tsp onion powder
2 tb Wine vinegar
2 ts Lemon pepper
1    Cabbage
20 oz Pineapple, canned, crushed
1/2 c  Raisins
Shred cabbage and place in large bowl.  Add raisins. Pour pineapple
juice into blender jar.  Spoon crushed pineapple into jar until it
measures 2 cups.  (Add any remaining pineapple to cabbage and
raisins) Add remaining ingredients to blender jar.  (If using whole
onion, cut into 1/8's) Blend until smooth.  Pour over cabbage,
raisins, and pineapple. Toss until dressing and slaw are mixed well.
Refrigerate at least one hour before serving.  Toss again before
serving.

Makes 10 - 12 cups
