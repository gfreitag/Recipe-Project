Title: Applesauce Bread
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 pn Nutmeg
2/3 c  Warm applesauce
1/2 c  Warm applejuice
1/2 c  Grated fresh apple
1 pk Yeast
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
3/4 c  Wheat flour
1/2 ts Salt
4 tb White sugar
Put all ingredients into the pan, select white bread, and push start.

From Loafing It by DAK
