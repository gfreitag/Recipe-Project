Title: Vegetable Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Italian, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1    Large can pillsbury crescent    1 1/2 ts Dill
1 ts Garlic salt
2    Medium carrots chopped
1 c  Cauliflower chopped
1/2 c  Green peppers chopped
1 c  Broccoli chopped
1 c  Mushrooms chopped
-rolls
1    Small can pillsbury crescent
-rolls
1    Large softened cream cheese
1    Small softened cream cheese
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 1/2 ts Onion flakes
Lay our on pizza pan the pillsbury crescent roll mix and
push edges together and bake according to directions or
until golden brown. Spread the softened cream cheese,
mayonnaise, onion flakes, dill and garlic salt over the
baked crust. Chopp the carrots, cauliflower, green
peppers, broccoli and mushrooms fine. Mix the vegetables
together, press onto cheese mixture. Cut into small bit
size pieces. Serve cold.
