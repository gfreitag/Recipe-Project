Title: Pumpernickel Bread - Pan-1
Categories: Breadmaker, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Butter
3 tb Cornmeal
2 tb Cocoa
2 tb Molasses
15/16 c  Water (7 1/2 fl.oz)
1 ts Dry yeast
XKGR41A Don Fifield
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
1/4 c  Rye flour (1 oz)
1/4 c  Whole wheat flour(1 1/3 oz)
1 tb Dry milk
1 ts Salt
Place all ingredients (except liquids and yeast) inside the bread
pan. Add liquid ingredients. Close cover and place dry yeast into the
yeast holder. Press start.
