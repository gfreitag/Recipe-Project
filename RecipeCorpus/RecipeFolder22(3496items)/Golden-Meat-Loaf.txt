Title: Golden Meat Loaf
Categories: Meatloaf, Beef, Mennonite
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 1/2 ts Worchestershire sauce
3    Hardcooked eggs
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 c  Rolled oats
1 c  Raw, grated carrots
1/2    Minced onion
2    Eggs, beaten
Combine all ingredients, except hard-cooked eggs, and mix thoroughly.
Place half of mixture in a loaf pan 4 X 9 inches.
Cut eggs in half lengthwise and arrange in row through center of the
meat. Place remainder of the meat on top.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 1 1/4 hours.
