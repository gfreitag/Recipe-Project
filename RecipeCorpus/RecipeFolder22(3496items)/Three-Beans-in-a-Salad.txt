Title: Three Beans in a Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Natural rice vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
4 ts Olive oil
1/4 ts Each salt and freshly ground
Pepper
Lettuce leaves
2/3 c  Cooked, drained pinto beans
2/3 c  Cooked, drained garbanzo bns
1 1/2 c  Sliced, steamed green beans
1/2 c  Diced celery
1/4 c  Diced red onion
1/4 c  Diced pimentos
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1.  In salad bowl combine all ingredients, except lettuce. Chill

2.  Serve on lettuce leaves.

DEAL-MEAL CARDS USED:  1 meat 1 vegetable 1 fat 155 Calories
