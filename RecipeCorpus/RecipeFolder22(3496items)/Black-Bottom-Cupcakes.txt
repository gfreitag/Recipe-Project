Title: Black Bottom Cupcakes
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 18 servings
1/3 c  Oil
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Water
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Soda
Pecans
Chocolate chips
8 oz Cream cheese
1    Egg
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ds Salt
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Cocoa
Combine, by hand, the first 4 ingredients in a small bowl.  Do not
overmix. The cream cheese will be lumpy.  Combine the next 8
ingredients in a larger bowl, stirring until smooth.  Pour batter
into muffin tins about 1/2 full. Add 1 heaping teaspoon of cheese
mixture to center of each cupcake. Top with chocolate chips and
pecans.  Bake for 20 minutes in 350 oven. Makes 18 regular or 30 mini
cupcakes.
