Title: Garlic Gazpacho
Categories: Soup/stew, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Lemon juice
3 c  Tomato juice
(salt)
Cayenne pepper
2    Cloves garlic
6 lg Tomatoes
1 lg Onion
1    Green pepper
2 sm Cucumber
Peel the tomatoes and blend with the garlic in a blender.  Add 1/4 of
the green pepper, 1/4 of the onion, and 1/2 of the cucumber.  Chill.
Chop remaining vegetables and put in separate serving dishes in the
refrigerator.  Put tomato juice in refrigerator.  Just before serving,
blend olive oil, lemon juice, salt, cayenne and tomato juice.  Add to
blender mixture.  Ladle into soup dishes.  Pass chopped vegetables
and a dish of croutons as garnish.
