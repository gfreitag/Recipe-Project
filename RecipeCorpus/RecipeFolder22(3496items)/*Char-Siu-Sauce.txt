Title: *Char Siu Sauce
Categories: Chinese, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  Salt
1 t  Chinese five-spice powder
3 ea Quarter-size slices fresh
-ginger, crushed
1/4 c  Reduced sodium soy sauce
2 T  Honey
2 T  Sugar
2 T  Dry sherry
Extracted from a Char Siu Ding (Pork) recipe. Should be tested.

In a pan, combine soy, honey, sugar, sherry, salt, five-spice and
ginger. Heat for one minute to dissolve sugar.

From the Sunset Chinese Cookbook.
