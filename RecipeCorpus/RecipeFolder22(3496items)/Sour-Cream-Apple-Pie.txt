Title: Sour Cream Apple Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 4 servings
Pinch   salt
Pie shell (9-inch)
-TOPPING
1/4 c  Butter or margarine;softened
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Almonds;sliced
4    Cooking apples;medium-tart
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Light sour cream
2    Eggs;lightly beaten
1/2 ts Vanilla
Preheat oven to 350F.  Peel and core the apples; thinly slice. In a
large bowl, combine the apple slices, sugar, lemon juice, sour cream,
eggs, vanilla, and salt.  Mix well and set aside.
Bake empty pie shell for 5 minutes, then fill with apple mixture and
bake for 35 minutes.
Meanwhile, mix margarine or butter with sugar and almonds until
smooth. Set aside.
Remove pie from oven.  Spread the topping on pie and put back in
oven for 20 minutes.  Remove pie from oven and let cool on rack
before serving.

Makes 8 servings.

Total fat: 24.3 g Cholesterol, using
Calories per serving: 444
margarine: 66 mg Sodium: 321 mg Fiber: 2.6 g
