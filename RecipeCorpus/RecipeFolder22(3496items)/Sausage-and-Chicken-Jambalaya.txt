Title: Sausage and Chicken Jambalaya
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Cajun/creol, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Rice
1 ea Onion, sliced
1 lb Hot sausage
4 ea Chicken breasts
1 ea Bell pepper, sliced
2 cn Beef broth
2 tb Butter or oleo
Saute sliced pepper, onion and hot sausage in butter for 5 min.
In 9 x 13-inch pan, mix consomme and rice.  Add sausage mixture.
Place chicken breasts on top and cover with foil.  Bake at 350 F. for
1 hr., uncover and bake add'tl. 30 min.

Yield:  4 servings
