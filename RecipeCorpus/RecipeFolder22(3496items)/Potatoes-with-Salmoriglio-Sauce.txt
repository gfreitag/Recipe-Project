Title: Potatoes with Salmoriglio Sauce
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Freshly ground black pepper
-to taste
2 T  Minced fresh oregano or 1
-ts dried oregano crushed
2 lb Small red new potatoes,
-scrubbed
3 T  Olive oil
2 T  Lemon juice
1 lg Clove garlic, peeled and
-forced through a press
Cut the potatoes in halves and place in a steamer basket.  Steam in a
covered pan with water at a low boil. Steam about 20 minutes, until
the potatoes are tender. Drain and transfer to a bowl.

While the potatoes are cooking combine the olive oil, lemon juice,
garlic, salt, pepper, and oregano in a small jar.

Pour the sauce over the potatoes and stir well to coat.  Let sit 5
minutes and serve.

Menu suggestion: Serve this side-dish with baked chicken or a simply
prepared fish Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.50
