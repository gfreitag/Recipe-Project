Title: Quick French Onion Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Black Pepper
4 x  French Bread Slices
6 oz Jarlsberg Cheese
1 lb Yellow Onions
1 ts Sugar
2 tb Olive Oil
5 c  Beef Broth, Strained
Thinly slice onions and cheese.  Cut bread into 1 inch slices and
toast. In dutch oven, cook onions and sugar in hot oil over medium
heat for 15 to 20 minutes or until deep golden brown, stirring
frequently to prevent burning. Slowly add broth and pepper; bring to
a boil.  Reduce heat, cover, and cook slowly for 20 minutes.  Ladle
into ovenproof container or individual ovenproof bowls.  Place bread
slices on top and add cheese. Place under broiler 3 to 4 inches from
heat and cook 3 to 5 minutes until cheese is hot and bubbly.
