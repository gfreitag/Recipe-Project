Title: Mexican-Style Creamed Corn
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Chopped Green Pepper
1/4 c  Chopped Celery
1/4 c  Diced green Chili Peppers
10 oz Pkg frozen whole kernel Corn
1/4 c  Chopped sweet Red Pepper
1/4 c  Cream Cheese (reduced cal.)
1 T  Skim Milk
In a medium saucepan combine corn, green pepper, red pepper, celery,
and 1/2 cup water. Cook about 5 minutes or till corn is tender. Drain.
Stir in cream cheese, chili peppers, milk, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and
dash pepper. Heat through.
********************************************************* Per
serving: 94 calories, 4 g protein, 17 g carbohydrates, 3 g fat, 0 mg
cholesterol, 215 mg sodium, 144 mg potassium.
