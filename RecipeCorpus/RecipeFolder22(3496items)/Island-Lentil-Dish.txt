Title: Island Lentil Dish
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
-bite-size pieces
3 tb Sunflower oil
1 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Salt
6 oz Lentils; cooked
6 oz Brown rice; cooked
6 oz Celery; chopped
6 oz Cauliflower flowerets; in
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Lightly grease 4 x 8 baking pan with
sunflower oil. Combine all ingredients.Toss and mix well. Transfer to
baking dish and bake for 15 minutes.
