Title: Orange Barbeque Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Vinegar, Chinese black
2 tb Onion, grated
1/2 ts Gingerroot, grated
2 ts Sauce, hot pepper
6 oz Chili sauce (Heinz)
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Molasses
Combine all ingredients in a large saucepan. Stir to blend then bring
to a boil and let it cool. Makes about 1 1/4 cups. Will keep for a
brief period if refrigerated. Serve with Barbequed Ribs, roast
chicken or beef.
