Title: Chicken & Spaghetti, Iv
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Rice, Cajun/creol, Chicken
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Celery, chopped
2 ea Cubes chicken bouillon
1 lb Velveeta cheese w/peppers
1 ea Chicken
3/4 c  Bell pepper, chopped
2 qt Chicken broth
1 pk Spaghetti
Boil chicken in water.  Remove and debone.  Add chicken cubes to
broth: should be 2 qts. water.  Cook spaghetti in broth until most of
liquid is absorbed.

Add remaining ingreds.

Yield:  6 to 8 servings
