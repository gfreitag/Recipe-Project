Title: Cottage Dill Bread for Bread Machine
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 8 servings
-Electric Bread
MMMMM------------------------REGULAR LOAF-----------------------------
1 1/4 c  Cottage cheese
1/2 tb Dry onion
1/2 tb Dill seed
1/2 tb Dill weed
1 ts Fast rise yeast; **OR**
2 ts -Active dry yeast
1/2 c  Water
2 c  White bread flour
1 tb Dry milk
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
MMMMM-------------------------LARGE LOAF------------------------------
3/4 c  Cottage cheese
1 tb Dry onion
1 tb Dill seed
1 tb Dill weed
2 ts Fast rise yeast;  **OR**
3 ts -Active dry yeast
3/4 c  Water
3 c  White bread flour
1 1/2 tb Dry milk
2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Butter
+++ servings are based on 8 for regular loaf and 12 for large loaf.
Hints: Use small curd, lowfat cottage cheese. This recipe can be made
with the regular or rapid bake cycles. Be sure to follow loading
instructions for your particular bread machine.
