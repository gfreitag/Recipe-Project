Title: Broccomole
Categories: Vegetarian, Snack, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Tomato; diced
1    Onion, green; sliced
1    Chili pepper, green; chopped
1 1/2 c  Broccoli stems; well-cooked
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Cumin
1/8 ts Garlic powder
Place broccoli stems, lemon juice, cumin and garlic in a blender or
food processor and puree until smooth.
Transfer to a bowl. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. For best
flavor, chill before serving.

Makes 2 cups
