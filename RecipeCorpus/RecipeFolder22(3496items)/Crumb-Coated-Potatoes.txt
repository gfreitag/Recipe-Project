Title: Crumb-Coated Potatoes
Categories: Vegetarian, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Margarine
3 cn (16-oz) whole white potatoes
1/2 c  Dried bread crumbs
In 12-inch skillet over medium heat, stir dried bread crumbs, salt
and pepper into hot margarine. Add potatoes, shaking skillet to
coatpotatoes evenly with bread crumbs. Cook until potatoes are golden
and heated through, about 15 minutes, shaking skillet occasionally.
Makes 8 servings.
