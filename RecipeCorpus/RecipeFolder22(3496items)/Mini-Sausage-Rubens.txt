Title: Mini-Sausage Rubens
Categories: Pork, Appetizer
Yield: 44 servings
1 c  Thousand island dressing
1 ea (16oz) saurkraut
10 ea Slices of swiss cheese
1/2 lb Smoked sausage
1 ea Loaf party rye bread
4 tb (1/2 stick) butter
Slice  sausage  into  1/4 inch pieces.  Drain  the  saurkraut.
Cut
cheese  slices  into  1/2" x 2 1/2" strips.  Melt  butter  in
small
skillet.   Brush  rye bread with butter on both  sides.    Place
on
baking  sheet.  Toast 400 degrees for 5 minutes. Turn.  Remove
from
oven  and  build rubens ending with criss-crossed cheese  slices
on
top.   Bake 5 to 10 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly.
Notes : Yields approximately 44 appetizers.
