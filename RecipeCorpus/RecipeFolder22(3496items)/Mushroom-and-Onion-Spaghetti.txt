Title: Mushroom and Onion Spaghetti
Categories: Vegetarian, Pasta, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Olive oil
6 oz Spaghetti; cooked
3 oz Mushrooms; sliced
3 oz Onions; sliced
3 oz Tomato; chopped
1/2 ts Basil
Saute mushrooms, onions, and tomato with basil, oregano and salt in
olive oil for 5 minutes. Combine with spaghetti and toss gently.
Serve warm.
