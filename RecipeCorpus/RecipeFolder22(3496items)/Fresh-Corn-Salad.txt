Title: Fresh Corn Salad
Categories: Vegetarian, Salad
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Parsley, fresh; finely
-chopped
2 tb Olive oil, extra virgin
2 tb Vinegar, apple cider
1/4 ts Salt
1/8    Black pepper
3 c  Corn kernels; steamed 10
-minutes
1 c  Bell pepper, red; chopped
1 c  Fennel; chopped; steamed
-15 to 20 minutes
1 tb Coriander, fresh; finely
-chopped
Combine all of the ingredients in a large bowl, toss, and serve cold
or at room temperature.

Yield: 2 servings
