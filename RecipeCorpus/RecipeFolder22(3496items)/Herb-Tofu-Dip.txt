Title: Herb Tofu Dip
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Parsley, chopped
2 ts Herb salt
1/4 ts Onion powder
2 cl Garlic, pressed
1/2 lb Tofu (drained)
1/2 c  Green onion chopped
1 1/4 ts Celery salt
1/2 ts Dill weed
Blenderize first four ingredients until smooth Add remaining
ingredients and blend again Serve as a dip for a variety of fresh
vegetables Makes 1 1/2 cups **Natures Panntry Cookbook**
