Title: Wild Rice/pine Nut Stuffing
Categories: Stuffing, Nuts/grains, Vegetable, Diabetic
Yield: 2 servings  1/4 c  Wild Rice, uncooked 1 c Water 1 Green Onion (scallion) 1 sm
Garlic Clove, minced 1 ts Vegetable Oil 1/4 c Pine Nuts, lightly
toasted 1/2 ts Dried Thyme

Cook the wild rice in the water until tender, about 1 hour.  Add more
water, if needed.

Meanwhile, saute the onion, garlic, pine nuts and thyme in the oil.
Add the wild rice. Simmer for 10 minutes to blend the flavors. Cool
enough to stuff into quail or one Cornish hen.
