Title: Corny Frank Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1 oz Can cream style corn
1 c  Milk
3/4 lb Velveeta processed cheese,
-cubed
1 ds Pepper
1 lb Franks, cut into 1-inch
-pieces
1/2 c  Chopped green peppers
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Butter or margarine
Saute the frankfurters, peppers, onion in margarine in a 3-qt.
saucepan. Add the remaining ingredients.  Stir until the cheese
spread is melted.

Source: Velveeta, Gram's Recipes
