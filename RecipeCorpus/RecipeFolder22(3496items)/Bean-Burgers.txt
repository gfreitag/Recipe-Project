Title: Bean Burgers
Categories: Misc/food
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Salt (opt)
1 tb Arrowroot (cornstarch)
3 ts Minced onion/and parsley
(optional)
4    Slices whole wheat bread
16 oz No-fat refried beans
1 c  Cooked brown rice or millet
1/4 c  Oats
1/4 c  Tomato paste
Process bread, one slice at a time, in a bry blender to form soft
curmbs. Mix all ingredients together well. Form into 8 patties. Brown
on each side in a vegetable oil sprayed non-stick skillet. Seve as is
or on whole wheat buns with the works. (I substituted 1 cup of bread
crumbs for the bread, 1/4 cup ketchup for the tomato paste.)
