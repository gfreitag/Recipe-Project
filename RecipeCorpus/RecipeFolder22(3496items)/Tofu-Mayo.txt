Title: Tofu Mayo
Categories: Vegetarian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 ts Rice syrup
1 c  Soft silken tofu; mashed
1 tb Olive oil
1    Garlic clove; crushed
Drain tofu for 15 to 29 minutes and squeeze out any excess water.

Blend all ingredients untilsmooth and creamy. If mixure seems too
thick, add one teaspoon of water.

Per serving (1 tbs): 33 cal; 3 g prot; 7 mg sod; 1 g carb; 2 g fat;
0 mg chol; 33 mg cal
