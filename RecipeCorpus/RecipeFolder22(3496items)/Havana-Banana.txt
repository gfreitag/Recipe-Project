Title: Havana Banana
Categories: Beverage, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
Ice cubes
1    Maraschino cherry
1 sl Orange
3/4 oz Light rum
3/4 oz Banana liqueur
1/2 c  Pineapple-coconut drink
Combine rum and banana liqueur. Add pineapple-coconut drink and stir
to blend. Pour over ice cubes in large or small brandy snifter.
Garnish with maraschino cherry and orange slice.
