Title: Tofu South of the Border Enchiladas
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Tex/mexican
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Dried cilantro
12 oz Silken firm tofu, drained
-and mashed
2 c  Diced tomatoes, drained
2 c  Thick tomato salsa
1/2 c  Low fat cheddar, shredded
8    8" whole wheat tortillas
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
3/4 c  Green peppers, chopped
4 oz Green mild chilies, chopped
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/2 ts Cumin seed
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Lightly spray a 9"x13" baking pan with
no-stick vegetable spray.

In bowl, combine all ingredients except tortillas, tomato salsa and
cheese. Place 1/2 cup of mixture in center of each tortilla and roll.
Place in baking dish, seam side down.  Poru salsa over enchiladas.
Sprinkle with shredded cheese.  Cover pan with aluminum foil and bake
for 35-30 minutes.

Per serving: calories 199 Total fat 6.5g protein 8.8g caloeies from
28g
fat 29% carbohydrate
