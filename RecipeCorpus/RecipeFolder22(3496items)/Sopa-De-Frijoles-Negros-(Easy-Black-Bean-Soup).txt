Title: Sopa De Frijoles Negros (Easy Black Bean Soup)
Categories: Vegetable, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts CUMIN
1 ts CILANTRO (OPTIONAL)
1/2 ts SALT
1 c  CHICKEN BROTH
8 oz CAN TOMATO SAUCE
1 c  SOUR CREAM
15 oz CAN BLACK BEANS
2 tb BUTTER
1    STALK OF CELERY
1    GARLIC CLOVE,MINCED
1    ONION
1 ts CHILI POWDER
PLACE CANNED BEANS IN BLENDER A SMALL AMOUNT AT A TIME TILL BLENDED
COMPLETELY.  IN SAUCEPAN MELT BUTTER, ADD ONION, GARLIC, CILANTRO,
CUMIN, CHILI POWDER, CELERY AND SALT. COOK TILL ONION IS TRANSPARENT.
ADD TOMATO SAUCE, BLENDED BLACK BEANS, AND CHICKEN BROTH. SERVE HOT
AND TOPPED WITH SOUR CREAM.
