Title: Sloppy Joes
Categories: Main dish, Meat
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/8 ts Pepper
6    Hamburger buns, toasted
1 lb Ground beef
1 cn Italian style tomato soup
1/4 c  Water
In skillet, cook beef until browned, stirring to separate meat.
Spoon off fat.

Add soup, water, Worcestershire and pepper.  Heat through, stirring
often.

Serve on buns.

Yield:  6 sandwiches
