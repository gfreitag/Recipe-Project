Title: Spunky Monkey Snack Mix *
Categories: Snack, Nuts/grains
Yield: 7 cups
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
1 c  Dried banana chips
1 c  Honey roasted peanuts
3 c  Graham squares & crispy
- mini grahams cereal
2 c  Malted milk balls
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients; toss gently. Store in
tightly covered container.
