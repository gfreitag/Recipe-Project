Title: Elegant Green Beans
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
Pepper to taste
15 1/2 oz Green beans, can
1 sm French fried onions, can
1    Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c  Milk
1 ts Soy sauce
In 1/2 quart casserole, combine soup, milk, soy sauce, and pepper;
stir in green beans and 1/2 onions. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes;
stir. Top with onions, bake 5 minutes more. Makes 4 cups
