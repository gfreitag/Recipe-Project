Title: Spanish Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Stewed tomatoes
8 oz Tomato sauce
1    Green pepper
1 c  Fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 tb Honey
SLICE green pepper SAUTE green pepper and mushrooms ADD tomatoes,
tomato sauce and honey SIMMER for 30 minutes or longer SERVE over
your favorite loaf

**Modified from Natures Pantry Cookbook**
