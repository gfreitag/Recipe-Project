Title: Jellied Beef Broth
Categories: Soup/stew, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Chopped parsley
Salt and pepper
1 tb Cold water
1 ts Celery salt
1/2 c  Concentrated meat broth
2 c  Boiling water
2 ts Gelatin
1/2    Bay leaf
1    Clove
Combine broth, water, bay leaf, clove, celery salt, and Worcestershire
sauce.  Season to taste.  Simmer 10 minutes. Strain. Add gelatin
which has been softened in cold water.  Stir until dissolved. Pour
into bouillon cups.  Chill until firm. Garnish with chopped parsley.
6 servings.
