Title: Potato Chowder
Categories: Casserole, Main dish, Vegetable
Yield: 10 servings
4 ts Chicken-flavor vegetarian
-bouillon
6 c  Soy milk
1/2 c  Flour, all-purpose
Paprika; sprinkle
4 c  Potatoes; peel & dice
1/2 c  Onion; finely chopped
1 c  Carrot; grated
1 ts Salt; optional
1/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Dried parsley; flakes
Recipe adapted from: The Mormon Diet Cookbook Preparation Time: 1:00

In lg Dutch oven or kettle, combine potatoes, onion, carrot, salt,
pepper, parsley flakes and bouillon.

Add enough water to just cover veggies; cook until veggies are
tender, about 15 - 20 min.

Do not drain.

Measure 1 1/2 C milk and add flour to milk, stirring with wire whisk.

Stir until blended.

Simmer for 15 min on low heat and thicken.

Garnish with paprika.
