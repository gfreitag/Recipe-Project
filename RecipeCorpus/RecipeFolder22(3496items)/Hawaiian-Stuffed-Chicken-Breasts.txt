Title: Hawaiian Stuffed Chicken Breasts
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1    Green pepper, chopped OR>>>>
1/2    Red pepper, chopped
1/2    Green pepper, chopped
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Vinegar
1/4 ts Ground ginger
2/3 c  Hot water
2 tb Margarine or butter
2 c  Stuffing mix, canister type
1 cn Crushed pineapple in syrup>>
(8 1/4 oz. size).
4    Boneless chicken breast>>>>
Halves, pounded 1/4" thick.
Heat oven to 400 F.  Mix hot water and margarine in bowl.  Stir in
stuffing mix, pepper, half the pineapple and syrup.

Spoon stuffing evenly on chicken; roll tightly.  Secure with
toothpicks. Place in 9" square pan with any remaining stuffing in
center.

Mix remaining pineapple and syrup, sugar, vinegar and ginger.
Spoon over chicken.  Bake 30 minutes or until thoroughly cooked.
