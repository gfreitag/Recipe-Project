Title: Cream of Peanut Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
1    Qt. Chicken stock
1 ds Pepper
1 ts Salt
1 c  Cream
1/4 c  Butter
1 tb Minced onion
1 tb Flour
1 c  Peanut butter
Melt butter. Add onion. Simmer until tender but not brown. Add flour
and peanut butter and stir to a smooth paste. Add stock gradually. Add
seasonings. Cook twenty minutes in a double boiler, stirring
constantly, until thickened. Strain and add cream. (Serves 8)
