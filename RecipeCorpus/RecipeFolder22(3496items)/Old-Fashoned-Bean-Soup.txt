Title: Old-Fashoned Bean Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable, Mennonite
Yield: 8 servings
2 qt Milk
3 tb Butter
1/4 c  Cream
1 1/2 c  Navy beans
Salt and pepper
3 c  Toasted bread cubes
Soak beans and cook until soft.
Add milk and butter, salt and pepper
Bring almost to a boil; and just before serving add cream and pour
over bread cubes.
