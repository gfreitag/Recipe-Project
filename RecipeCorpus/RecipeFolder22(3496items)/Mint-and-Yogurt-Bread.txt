Title: Mint and Yogurt Bread
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
-fresh lemon
2 ts Honey
1 ts Melted butter
1/4 c  Plus 1 Tbsp. warm water
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1 c  Plain yogurt; room temp.
3 tb Fresh mint; chopped
Grated yellow rind of 1
Add all of the above ingredients, select white bread and push start.

From Loafing It by DAK
