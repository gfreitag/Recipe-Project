Title: Tantalizing Tempeh Dinner
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
-cubes
2 tb Soy oil
1 ts Onion; minced
1 ts Fresh chives; chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 oz Dulse, dry
3 oz Chickpeas; cooked
3 oz Soybean sprouts
3 oz Broccoli flowerets; in bite-
-size pieces
3 oz Tempeh; cut into 1/2-inch
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease a 4 x8 baking pan with
soy oil. Soak and rinse dulse 2 or 3 times in cold water. Combine
with the remaining ingredients and mix well. Transfer to baking pan
and bake for 20 minutes.
