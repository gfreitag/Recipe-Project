Title: Kima -  Janet
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
Few green peas
1    Med. chopped onion
1 lb Can stewed tomatoes
Cooked rice
1 lb Ground beef
1 tb Curry
1 tb Chile powder
1 ts Seasoned salt
Brown meat and add spices.  Add chopped onion and cooked till wilted.
Add tomatoes.  Cook a while.  When ready to serve add a handful or
two of green peas.  Cook till done.  Serve over rice.
