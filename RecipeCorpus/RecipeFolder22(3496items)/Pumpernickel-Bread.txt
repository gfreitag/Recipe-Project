Title: Pumpernickel Bread
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Unsweetened cocoa
3 tb Molasses
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 1/4 c  Warm water
1 1/2 pk Yeast
2 c  Rye flour
1/2 c  Unprocessed whole-bran
-cereal
1 3/4 c  Bread flour
2 ts Caraway seed
Add all ingredients in the order listed, select white bread and push
start.
