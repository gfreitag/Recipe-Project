Title: Sausage and Bean Casserole
Categories: Pork, Vegetable, Casserole, Mennonite
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Tomato juice
1 lb Sausage, ground
3 1/2 c  Navy beans, cooked
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
Brown sausage and onion together.
Drain off excess fat.
Combine beans and add seasoning.
Place in greased casserole and pour tomato juice over mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 to 50 minutes.
