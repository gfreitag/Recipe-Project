Title: Pepper Spice Bread
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
2 tb Corn syrup
1/4 c  Pecans (or other nuts)
1/2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1 ts Whole anise seed
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Allspice
3/4 c  Very warm water
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Nonfat dry milk powder
1/4 c  Soft butter
1    Egg
2 tb Honey
Put in all ingredients in the order listed, select white bread and
push start.

NOTE: This recipe produces a light brown loaf with a unique spicy
taste.

From Loafing It by DAK
