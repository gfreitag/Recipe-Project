Title: Soup for the Gods - Shen-Hsien-T'ang
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
In 1/8 sections
1/2 oz Finely chopped Ginseng root
1 ts Sesame oil
1 ts Salt
6 c  Boiling water
2 tb Soy sauce
12    Half-inch sections of garlic
-shoots or 1 scallion cut
Put seasonings in a bowl and pour the boiling water into it. This is
a good drinking soup that goes well with a heavy dinner.
