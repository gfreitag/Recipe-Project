Title: Chewy Macaroons
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Flaked coconut
2    Egg whites; room temperature
1/2 ts Vanilla (or almond) extract
2 tb Flour
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Lightly grease and flour 2 large cookie
sheets, set aside.
In large mixing bowl, beat egg whites until stiff but not dry.
Beat in vanilla.  Mix together remaining ingredients, fold into egg
whites. Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto prepared cookie sheets 2
inches apart. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown.  Cool
slightly, then remove.
