Title: Brown Rice Pilaf
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sliced fresh Mushrooms
1/2 c  Shredded Carrot
1/4 c  Thinly sliced Green Onion
1/2 t  Instant chicken bouillon
3/4 c  Brown Rice, quick cooking
1/4 t  Dried Marjoram, crushed
2 T  Snipped fresh Parsley
In a medium saucepan stir together bouillon granules and 1 cup water.
Bring to boiling. Stir in mushrooms, brown rice, carrot, marjoram,
and dash pepper. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 12 minutes.
Remove from heat; let stand for 5 minutes. Add green onion and
parsley; toss lightly with a fork. Serve immediately.
************************************************* Per serving: 104
calories, 3 g protein, 21 g carbohydrates, 1 g fat, 0 mg cholesterol,
105 mg sodium, 205 mg potassium.
