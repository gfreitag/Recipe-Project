Title: Chicken Stir-Fry
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Asian, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Cream of broccoli soup
2/3 c  Water
2 ts Soy sauce, lo-sodium
1/4 ts Ginger, ground
4 c  Rice, cooked, no salt
1 lb Chicken breasts, skinned>>>
Cut into strips
1 c  Broccoli flowerets
1 c  Carrot strips, cut>>>>
Matchstick size.
1/2 c  Onion, cut in wedges
Spray nonstick skillet with cooking spray.  Heat 1 min.  Stir-fry
chicken in 2 batches until browned.  Remove.

Remove skillet from heat, spray skillet with cooking spray.
Stir-fry vegetables until tender-crisp.

Add soup, water, soy sauce and ginger; heat to boiling.  Return
chicken to skillet.  Heat through.  Serve over hot cooked rice.

Nutritional values per serving:  Calories 466, Protein 34g, Fat 5g,
Cholesterol 70mg, Sodium 464mg, Carbohydrate 69g
