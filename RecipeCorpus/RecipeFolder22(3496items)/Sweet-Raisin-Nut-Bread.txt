Title: Sweet Raisin Nut Bread
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
4 tb Melted butter; cooled
1/4 c  Honey
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Raisins
1/3 c  Golden raisins
3/4 c  Nuts *
1 pk Yeast
3 tb Sugar
3 c  Bread flour
3/4 c  Warm milk
1/4 c  Warm water
2    Eggs; room temperature
* Pistachios or peanuts

Simply dump in all ingredients in the order listed, select white
bread and push start.

From Loafing It by DAK
