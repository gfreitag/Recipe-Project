Title: Quick and Easy Couscous
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
-tomatoes
1 cn Minestrone soup
-garlic
-olive oil
1 x  Box couscous
2 c  Chicken broth, canned or
-fresh
2 sm Zucchini/courgettes
-handful  of cherry
Bring the stock to a boil in a saucepan.  Add the couscous, stir.
Cover. Turn off heat.

Saute garlic in olive oil in a frying pan.  Add
zucchini/courgette,saute for 23 minutes.  Add sliced tomatoes. Blend.
Add minestrone, heat through.

Place couscous in a bowl, add vegetables.  Add left over meat, if
desired (leftover buffalo chicken wings, sliced, is especially good.
