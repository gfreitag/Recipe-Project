Title: Lobster Stew
Categories: Seafood, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 pn White Pepper
4 tb Butter
1 ts Salt
1 1/4 lb Boiled Lobster
5 c  Milk
1/2 c  Heavy Cream
Remove meat from lobster body and claws.  Cube.  Scald milk with
lobster shells in it.  Discard shells.  Slightly sautÂ‚ lobster in
butter; add the milk and bring to boil.  Remove from heat, add cream,
season to taste and serve with Oyster crackers.
