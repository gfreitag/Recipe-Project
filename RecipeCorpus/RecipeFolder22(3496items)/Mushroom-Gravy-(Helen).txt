Title: Mushroom Gravy (Helen)
Categories: Vegetarian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 12 servings
4 t  Thyme
4 T  Arrowroot
1/2 c  Water
2 ea Dash pepper
8 T  Barley miso
3 1/2 c  Water or stock
2    Onion, diced
3 c  Sliced mushrooms
2 t  Sage
Spoon miso into 1/2 cup boiling liquid and stir to dissolve.  Add
onions, mushrooms, sage and thyme.  Cook for 5 minutes.  Add 1 1/4
cups more water or stock and continue cooking. Dissolve arrowroot in
1/4 cup water and add to mixture. Stir untill thickened, about 5
minutes. Add dash of fresh pepper and more miso is stronger gravy
tast is desired. Spoon over Holiday Loaf.
