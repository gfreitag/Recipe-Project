Title: Honey-Mustard Dressing Ddnn79c
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------GARY & MARY WEYBACK DDNN79C---------------------
1 t  Dijon mustard
1 t  Honey
Pepper; ground
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Vinegar; white, wine
2 T  Lemon juice; fresh
2 t  Parsley; finely chopped
2 t  Onion; chopped
Combine all ingredients in electric blender.  Cover; whirl until
smooth, scraping down sides as necessary.
Refrigerate until ready to use. Posted by Gary & Mary & formatted by
Liz Jones (vxrf36b) 5/92.
