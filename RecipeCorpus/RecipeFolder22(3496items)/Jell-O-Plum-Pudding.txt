Title: Jell-O Plum Pudding
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Currants
1/2 c  Chopped dates
1 c  Drained, crushed pineapple
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 sm Package raspberry JELL-O
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Ginger, powdered
1/2 ts Cloves
1 1/2 c  Boiling eater
Combine JELL-O, salt and spices; dissolve in boiling water. Stir in
raisins and currants. Chill until slightly thickened. Fold in remaing
ingredients. Pour into a 5-cup mold and chill until firm. Unmold by
briefly dipping mold or bowl in warm water just up to the rim. Invert
onto serving plate.
