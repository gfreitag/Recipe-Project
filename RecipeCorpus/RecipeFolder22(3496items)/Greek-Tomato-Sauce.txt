Title: Greek Tomato Sauce
Categories: Vegetarian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Parsley,fresh;finely chopped
2 ts Basil, fresh; finely chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 c  Onions, yellow; chopped
1/2 c  Bell peppers, green; chopped
1/4 c  Vegetable broth
16 oz Tomatoes, canned, whole
Saute the onions and peppers in the broth in a large saucepan for 5
minutes over medium heat. Add the remaining ingredients and cook over
medium-low heat for 25 minutes. Serve hot over pasta, vegetables, or
crepes.

Yield: 3 cups
