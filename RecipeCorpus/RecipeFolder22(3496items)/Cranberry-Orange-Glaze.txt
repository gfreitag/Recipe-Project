Title: Cranberry-Orange Glaze
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Orange Juice
1 tb Lemon Juice
3 oz Dried Cranberries
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
In a small saucepan, combine ingredients and mix well.  Heat over low
heat until the sugar is dissolved.  Use to flavor ham, poultry or
pork chops. Typed by Syd Bigger.
