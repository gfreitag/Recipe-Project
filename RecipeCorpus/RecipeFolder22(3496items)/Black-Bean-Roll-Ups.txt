Title: Black Bean Roll-Ups
Categories: Tex/mexican, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
4    Flour tortillas
1/2 c  Jack cheese, shredded>>>>>
Jalapeno flavored.
1    Recipe: Tomatillo guacamole
1 1/2 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Onion, finely diced
1    Clove garlic, minced
1 cn Black beans, drained (15oz)
Salt and pepper to taste
Heat oil in large skillet.  Add onion and garlic and saute until
onion is tender, about 5 minutes.  Add beans.  Season to taste with
salt and pepper.  Cook and stir mixture over low heat, while mashing.

When hot and pulpy, remove beans from heat.  Spread 1/4 of mixture
on each of 4 tortillas.  Top each with 2 tbs. shredded cheese.
Arrange tortillas on baking sheet.  Bake at 350 F. until cheese
melts, about 5 minutes.  To serve, top each tortilla with 1/4 of
Tomatillo guacamole and roll up tortillas and serve.
