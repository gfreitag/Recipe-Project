Title: Gumbo Vegan
Categories: Vegetarian, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Italian-flavored crushed
-tomatoes
1 ts Garlic powder
ds Tabasco sauce
1 c  Whole kernel corn, fresh or
-frozen
1 md Onion; chopped
1 md Green pepper; diced
1    Stalk celery; chopped
2 c  Okra; sliced (fresh or
-frozen)
Salt and pepper to taste
Saute onion, green pepper and celery in a small amount of water until
soft. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Simmer for 10
minutes and serve. May be served over rice for a main dish.
