Title: Cheddar Cheese Bread
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
2 tb Sugar
1 1/4 c  Warm water
1 1/2 c  Grated sharp cheddar; (6 oz)
-at room temperature
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1/4 c  Nonfat dry milk
1 tb Soft butter
1 ts Salt
Put all ingredients into the pan, select white bread, and push start.

From Loafing It by DAK
