Title: Peanut Butter and Jelly Tamales
Categories: Vegetarian, Dessert, Tex/mexican
Yield: 9 servings
-papers
1    Basic masa dough recipe
18    Corn husks or parchment
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 c  Peanut butter, unsweetened
1 c  Jam, fruit-sweetened
To make filling:

Mix jam and peanut butter.

Fill and cook tamales according to "Tamales: Basic Procedure."

Per serving (2 tamales): 554 cal; 8 g prot; 267 mg sod; 89 g carb;
17 g fat; 0 mg chol; 19 mg calcium
