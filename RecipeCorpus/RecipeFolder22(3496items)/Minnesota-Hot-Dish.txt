Title: Minnesota Hot Dish
Categories: Casserole, Main dish, Meat
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Celery, chopped
3 tb Low sodium soy sauce
2 ts Low solt beef bouilon
1 1/2 c  Water
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 c  Wild rice uncooked
1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms,sliced
1/2 c  Green pepper, chopped
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1. Rinse and soak wild rice in hot water for up to 3 hours. Drain. 2.
In a 4 quart saucepan, cover rice with water, Bring to a boil and
simmer for 20 minutes. Drain any excess water. 3. Brown ground beef
in a nonstick skillet. Drain any fat or juices 4. Combine cooked
ground beef with remaining ingredients in a large casserole dish. Mix
thoroughly. 5. Cover and bake 1 hour at 350* F. Uncover and bake 15
additional minutes.
