Title: Refried Bean Burgers
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 10 servings
2 tb Nutritional yeast
1/4 c  Tamari
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Thyme
1/2 c  Raw grated carrots
2 c  Rolled oats or bread crumbs
2 c  Fantastic Foods instant
Refried Beans, prepared
According to package
Directions
1/2 c  Stock or water, if needed
1 c  Cooked rice
1 c  Toasted sesame seeds
Mix all ingredients together and form into patties.
Freeze, then take out and fry or bake. Mustard can be added for a
different taste.

Makes 10-12 burgers.

Submitted by Jo Ann L. Fogler, Wilmington, NC, for the "Build a
Better Burger Contest" sponsored by the North American Vegetarian
Society

From _Vegetarian Voice_
