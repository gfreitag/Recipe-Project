Title: Stir-Fried Spinach with Ginger and Almonds
Categories: Vegetable, Asian, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Almonds, sliced
1/2 tb Oil, peanut
1/4 ts Ginger, ground
1 lb Spinach, fresh
1/2 tb Miso
1 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Vinegar, wine
1/4 ts Oil, sesame
Trim, wash, and drain spinach.  Cut into 1" to 2" widths. Mix miso,
soy sauce, vinegar, and sesame oil; set aside.

Heat wok over high heat.  Add almonds and stir-fry until fragrant and
edges turn golden, about 45 seconds. Transfer to plate.

Heat peanut oil in wok.  Add spinach and ginger and stir-fry just
until tender, about 3 minutes.  Drizzle with bean mixture. Serve
immediately!

From _Bon Appetit_, October 1987. MM by Sylvia Steiger, GEnie
