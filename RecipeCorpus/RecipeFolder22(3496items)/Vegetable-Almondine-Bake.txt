Title: Vegetable Almondine Bake
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Sage
1/4 ts Curry
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Sesame oil
3 oz Wheat flakes
3 oz Onion; chopped
3 oz Tomato; chopped
3 oz Green pepper; chopped
1 1/2 oz Scallions; chopped
1 1/2 oz Almonds; blanched, slivered
Preheat oven to 375 deg. Lightly grease 4 x 8 baking dish with
sesame oil. Cook wheat flakes in 12 ounces of water for 10 to 12
minutes. Saute vegetables, almonds, thyme, sage, curry and salt in a
skillet with sesame oil for 5 minutes. Combine with wheat flakes and
transfer to baking dish and bake for 15 minutes.
Serves 2.
