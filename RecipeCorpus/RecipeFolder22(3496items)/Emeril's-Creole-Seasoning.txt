Title: Emeril's Creole Seasoning
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Thyme
1 tb Oregano
1 tb Onion, dried
2 1/2 tb Paprika
2 tb Garlic powder
2 tb Black pepper
1 tb Red pepper, ground
In a small bowl, mix together all ingredients.
Transfer to a small jar and store in a cool, dry place. Makes about
2/3 cup.

Vegetarian Times, December 1992/MM by DEEANNE
