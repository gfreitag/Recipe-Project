Title: Beef and Vegetable Stew
Categories: Beef, Meat, Soup/stew, Mennonite
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Diced carrots
4    Large potatoes
1/2 lb Green beans
1 c  Tomatoes
1/2 c  Water
2 lb Beef, cubed
1    Large onion
4 tb Fat
OR
1 1/2    Inch cube suet
1/8 ts Pepper
Melt suet or fat in hot pan, add cubed beef and chopped onion and
brown thoroughly.
Add green beans first, then diced carrots, potatoes, tomatoes and
water. Cook slowly for 3 to 3 1/2 hours in a Dutch oven or 15 minutes
in a pressure cooker.
