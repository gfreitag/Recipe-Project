Title: Reuben Meatloaf
Categories: Meat, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
Pepper
Filling:
1 cn Sauerkraut; 8 oz
1 oz Corned beef (from can), chop
1/2 c  Cheese; swiss, shredded
Approx. Cook Time: :20 Combine
1 lb Ground beef, crumbled
1 c  Breadcrumbs; soft, preferab
1/2 c  Thousand island dressing
1    Egg
1/2 ts Caraway seed (optional)
Fat grams    per serving:
all meat mixture ingredients in medium bowl. Press 1/2 of mixture in
bottom of loaf pan. Make slight indentation, lengthwise down centre
of meat loaf. Combine all filling mixture and put in indentation. Top
with remaining meat mixture, pressing to enclose filling and sealing
edges. Cook at HIGH (100%) 13-18 minutes or till firm and temperature
is 145F. Let stand covered for 5 minutes. posted by Anne MacLellan
