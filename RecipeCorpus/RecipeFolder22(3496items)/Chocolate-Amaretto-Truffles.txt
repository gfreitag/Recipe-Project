Title: Chocolate Amaretto Truffles
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Alcohol
Yield: 15 servings
1 tb Amaretto liqueur
1/2 c  Nuts
-finely chopped
4 oz Ghirardelli semi-sweet
-chocolate
1/4 c  Butter
Melt broken chocolate, remove from heat. Stir in Amaretto. Add
butter, a tablespoon at a time, beating with a wire whip until smooth
and creamy.
Chill 5-10 minutes to firm mixture. Drop by teaspoon and roll into
nuts.
Keep truffles refrigerated until ready for use.
Source: Recipes from Ghirardelli Chocolate Company of San Fransciso
