Title: Asian Coleslaw
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Pepper, cayenne; -=or=- more
-if desired
6 c  Red cabbage; thinly sliced
3/8 c  Parsley; chopped
3 tb Cilantro; chopped
3/8 c  Onions, red; minced
1 1/2 tb Oil, olive;
3 tb Lemon juice;
1 1/2 tb Balsamic vinegar;
1/2 c  Rice vinegar;
1 1/2 ts Maple syrup;
3/4 ts Salt;
1/4 ts Pepper, black; ground
MMMMM------------------------PER SERVING-----------------------------
6 x  *gm carbo
207 x  *mg sodium
1 x  *gm fiber
50 x  *cals
1/2 x  *gm protein
3 x  *gm fat
IN a large bowl, combine oil, lemon juice, vinegars, maple syrup,
salt, black pepper, and cayenne.  Toss with cabbage; let stand at room
temperature for 1 hour. Taste for seasoning and adjust if necessary,
toss in parsley, cilantro and red onion.  Serve at room temperature.
Makes 8 servings.
