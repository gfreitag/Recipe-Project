Title: Rhubarb Bread
Categories: Bread, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Nutmeg
2 1/2 c  Rhubarb,diced
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Oil
2    Eggs
1 c  Sour milk
1 ts Salt
Mix well and pour into 2 greased 9x5 inch loaf pans. Before baking,
top each loaf with 1 tsp melted margarine and sprinklw with 1
tablespoon sugar over each loaf. Bake at 350 F for about 1 hour
