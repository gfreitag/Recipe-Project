Title: Esau's Pottage
Categories: Vegetarian, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 cl Garlic minced or pressed
1 ts Salt
1 tb Soy sauce
6 c  Water
1 c  Uncooked barley
1 c  Uncooked lentils
1 lg Onoin chopped
1/2 c  Celery shopped
COMBINE all ingredients in a large covered saucepan BRING TO A BOIL,
then reduce heat nd simmer until done OR BAKE at 350 for 45-50
minutes SPRINKLE with chopped fresh parsley and serve over cooked
brown rice ** Natures Pantry Cookbook**
