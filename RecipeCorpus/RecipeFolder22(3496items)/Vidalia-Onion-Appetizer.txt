Title: Vidalia Onion Appetizer
Categories: Appetizer, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Vinegar
4 tb Mayonnaise, eggless
1/2 ts Salt, celery
1 lg Onions, sweet
1/2 c  Water
4 tb Sugar
Thinly slice onions.  Combine onions, water, sugar, and vinegar;
marinate overnight.

Before serving, drain and pat dry.  Toss with mayonnaise and salt.
Serve on party rye.
