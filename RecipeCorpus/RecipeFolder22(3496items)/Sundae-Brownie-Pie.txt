Title: Sundae Brownie Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract or flavor
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1/2 c  Peanut Butter chips
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Ice Cream
Chocolate Topping
Chocolate Kisses
1    Unbaked 9" pie shell
2    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter (1 stick), melted
1/2 c  A.P. flour
1/3 c  Cocoa powder
1/4 ts Salt
Heat oven to 375 F.  Generously prick bottom and sides of pie
crust with fork.  Bake crust 10 minutes, remove from oven.
Meanwhile, in lge. bowl, beat eggs; stir in sugar and butter.  Stir
together flour, cocoa and salt; add to butter mixture.  Stir in
vanilla, chocolate chips, peanut butter chips and nuts.  Pour into
pie crust; return to oven.  Bake additional 25 to 30 minutes or until
almost set.  (Pie will NOT test done in center.) Serve warm or cool
with ice cream; drizzle with topping. Garnish with KISS.

Yield:  8 to 10 servings or one 9" pie
