Title: "No Bake" Cookie Crumble Cheesecake
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Mini-chocolate chips
1    Deep dish graham cracker>>>>
Crust (9oz. size).
1 c  Your favorite cookies,>>>>>
Coarsely crushed.
1    Envelope plain gelatine
1/4 c  Cold milk
1 c  Milk, heated to boiling
2 pk Cream cheese, 8 oz. ea.
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract or flavor
In blender, sprinkle gelatine over cold milk; let stand 2 min.
Add hot milk and process at low until dissolved, about 2 min.  Add
cream cheese, sugar and vanilla and process until blended.  Arrange
chocolate in bottom of crust.  Pour in gelatine mixture; sprinkle
with crushed cookies.  Chill until firm, about 2 hrs.

Yield: 8 to 10 servings
