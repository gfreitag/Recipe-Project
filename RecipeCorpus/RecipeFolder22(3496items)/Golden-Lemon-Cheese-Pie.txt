Title: Golden Lemon Cheese Pie
Categories: Dessert, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  ReaLemon juice
1 ts Vanilla flavor or extract
1    9" graham cracker pie shell
1 pk Cream cheese (8oz),softened
1 cn Eagle brand milk (14oz)
MMMMM--------------------GOLDEN LEMON TOPPING-------------------------
1/4 c  ReaLemon juice
1    Egg yolk
1/3 c  Sugar
2 1/2 ts Cornstarh
1/2 c  Water
In mixer bowl, beat cheese until fluffy.  Gradually beat in Eagle
Brand until smooth.  Stir in ReaLemon and vanilla.  Pour into
prepared crust. Top with Golden Lemon Topping.   Chill 3 hrs.
Garnish as desired. Refrigerate leftovers.

FOR GOLDEN LEMON TOPPING:  In sauce pan combine sugar and
cornstarch. Add lemon juice, water and egg yolk; mix well.  Over med.
heat, cook and stir until thickened.  Remove from heat; stir in
margarine or butter until melted.

Yield: 1-Nine inch pie
