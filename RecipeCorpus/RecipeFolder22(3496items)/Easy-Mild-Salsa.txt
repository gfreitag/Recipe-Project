Title: Easy Mild Salsa
Categories: Vegetarian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Cilantro; minced (optional)
3 tb Olive oil, extra virgin
Chili powder
Cayenne
1 1/2 c  Tomatoes; chopped; peeling
-optional
1/2 c  Onion, red; minced
1/2 c  Bell pepper, red; minced
1/2 c  Bell pepper, green; minced
Combine the tomatoes, onion, peppers, cilantro, and garlic in a
medium bowl. Stir in oil, chili powder, and cayenne to taste. Yields
2 1/2 to 3 cups.
