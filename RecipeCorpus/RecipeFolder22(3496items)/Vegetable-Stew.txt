Title: Vegetable Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped green bell
-pepper
1/2 c  Chopped seeded tomato
1/4 c  Chicken broth
1/8 t  Fennel seeds
-freshly ground black
-pepper
2 t  Reduced-calorie margarine
1/4 c  Chopped onion
3 oz Pared all-purpose potato,
-in 1/2-inch cubes
1/2 c  Trimmed green beans, in
1    -inch pieces
1 c  Coarsely chopped yellow
-bell pepper
Spray a medium skillet with nonstick cooking spray.  Melt margarine
over medium heat.  Add onion and cook until tender, stirring
frequently, about 3 minutes.

Add potato, beans, and bell peppers; reduce heat to low and cok,
stirring constantly, for 5 minutes. add remaining ingredients; cover
and simmer for 10 minutes.

Makes 1 serving.
