Title: Red Onion Salsa
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce, Vegetable, Vegetarian
Yield: 7 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Minced parsley (packed)
Black pepper to taste
2 c  Chopped red onion
1/2 c  Minced fresh cilantro (pack)
2 c  Minced fresh ripe tomatoes
Combine all ingredients and mix well. For a finer consistency, give
the mixture a brief whirl or two in a food processor or blender.
