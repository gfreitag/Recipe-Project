Title: Sausage Ratatouille
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1    Onion; diced (1 cup)
2 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 lb Hot italian sausage
3/4 c  Chunky spaghetti sauce
1/2 lb Eggplant; cut into
-1/2 inch cubes
1/2 lb Mushrooms; quartered
1    Summer squash; diced 3/4in
1    Zucchini; diced 3/4 inch
1    Red pepper; diced 3/4 inch
1    Yellow pepper; diced 3/4 in
MMMMM----------------NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION/SERV---------------------
18 x  G fat
35 x  Mg cholesterol
757 x  Mg sodium
282 x  Calories
13 x  G protein
20 x  G carbohydrate
1.  Preheat oven to 400F.  In large, shallow roasting pan, combine
vegetables; mix.  Drizzle with oil; sprinkle with the salt and
pepper. Stur until vegetables are coated.  Bake 30 minutes, stirring
once.

2.  In large nonstick skillet, combine sausage and 1 cup water; boil.
Cover; simmer 8 minutes orr until firm.  Drain; cool on plate.  Cut
crosswise into 1/2-inch slices.  In skillet, over medium-high heat,
lightly brown sausage 5 minutes.  Add sauce; mix.  Add to vegetables;
bake 15 minutes or until vegetables are tender.

From:  McCalls September 1993
