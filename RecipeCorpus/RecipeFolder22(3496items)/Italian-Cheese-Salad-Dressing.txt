Title: Italian Cheese Salad Dressing
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Seasoned pepper
1 ts Italian herb seasoning
2 c  Chef-Mate Italian Cheese
- Sauce
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Milk
1.  Mix all ingredients thoroughly with a wire whip. 2. Chill several
hours. serve over tossed green or pasta salads.

Makes 1 quart.
