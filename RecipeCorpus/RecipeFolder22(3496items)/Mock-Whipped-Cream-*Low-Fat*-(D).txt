Title: Mock Whipped Cream *Low-Fat* (D)
Categories: Dessert, Low-cal/fat, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
-diane crhn32b
MMMMM----------------USE WITH PUMPKIN SPICE CAKE---------------------
1 ts Orange rind; grated
x  Dash freshly grated nutmeg
8 oz Fresh soft TOFU;drained well
2 tb Maple syrup; or to taste
1 ts Vanilla extract
MMMMM----------------------DATA PER TBSPOON---------------------------
x  *mg Chol
*mg Sodium
x  *gm Fiber
19 x  *Calories
*gm Protein
1/2 x  *gm Fat
*gm Carbo
Puree all ingredients in a blender or food processor until very
smooth and creamy.  Adjust sweetener to taste. Cover and chill 20
minutes before serving.
Makes about 1 cup.

SOURCE: Vegetarian Times Mag, Dec '92 Mary Carroll, author The No
