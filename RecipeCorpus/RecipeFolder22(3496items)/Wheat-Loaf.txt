Title: Wheat Loaf
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Carrots; finely grated
1/4 c  Tomato paste
2 tb Tomato paste
2 c  Ground seitan mix (see
-recipe)
1/2 c  TVP
Egg replacer for 2 eggs
Prepare the egg replacer according to package directions.

Mix together all the ingredients except for the 2 Tbs of tomato
paste.

Pack the mixture into a loaf pan and apread the top of the loaf with
the 2 tbs tomato paste.

Bake at 350 deg for 35 minutes, or until firm.

Serve with a whole grain bread, a cooked vegetable and a salad.

Difficult to tell it's not meat.
