Title: Poppy-Seed Dressing 2
Categories: Dressing, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Onion juice (Make by gratin
-onions and catching juice
-in a bowl.
3 tb Poppy seeds
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Salt
2 ts Dry mustard
2/3 c  Vinegar
2 c  Salad oil
Mix sugar, salt, mustard and vinegar. Add onion juice and stir
thoroughly. Add oil, slowly, beating constantly until completely
combined and thick. Add poppy seeds and beat for a few minutes.
Refrigerate.
