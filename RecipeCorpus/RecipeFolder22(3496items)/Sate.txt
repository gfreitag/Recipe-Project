Title: Sate
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Vietnamese hot sauce
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1/8 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Fruit jelly
2 tb Peanut butter (chunky)
1/8 ts Rice vinegar (white)
6 tb Water
1/2 ts Garlic
MIX IT ALL UP! Use this to dip already broiled, marinated meat,
shrimp etc. on skewers.
