Title: Amish Bread W/oatmeal
Categories: Bread
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM-------------------NANCY HAGFORS GXDB48A------------------------
3    Eggs
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Sourdough starter
1/2 c  Oil
1 c  Light brown sugar
3/4 c  Flour
3/4 c  Oatmeal (quick)
Mix all ingredients together.  Bake at 350-degrees for 40 minutes.
