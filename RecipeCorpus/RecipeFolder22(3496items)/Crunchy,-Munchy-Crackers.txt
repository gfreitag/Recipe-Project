Title: Crunchy, Munchy Crackers
Categories: Snack, Vegetarian, Cracker
Yield: 30 servings
1/2 c  Water
1/3 c  Oil
1/2 c  Sesame seeds
2 1/2 c  Rolled oat or barley flakes
1/2 c  Barley flour
1/2 ts Salt
Heat oven to 300 degrees.

Combine oats, barley flour, salt, water, and oil in a large bowl; mix
throroughly.

Shape the dough into a rectangle and roll it paper-thin on a lightly
greased, rimless baking sheet.

Moisten the surface of the rolled dough with a damp pastry brush, and
sprinkle seeds on the dough. Roll again to press seeds into the
surface.

Using a knife or pizza cutter, cut the dough into 1-inch squares.

Bake until thoroughly dry and slightly brown, about 40 minutes.
