Title: Unfried French Fries
Categories: Vegetable, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
Salt to taste (optional)
6 lg Potatoes
Boil potatoes 20 minutes (Peel them or leave them unpeeled) Cut as
fries. Place on non-stick cookie sheet (or spray cookie sheet with
PAM), one layer thick. Lightly sprinkle with salt if desired, or your
favorite seasoning. Bake at 400 degrees for 45-60 minutes. Turn with
spatula after 25 minutes. Serve === From Fabulous Foods for Friends
and Family by Caviness===
