Title: Bean and Pistou Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Pesto sauce
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
Freshly ground black pepper
2 c  Chicken broth
2 cn White beans, rinsed and
-drained (19-ounce cans)
Put the broth and beans in a saucepan over moderate heat and bring to
a boil.  Reduce heat to low and stir in the remaining ingredients.
Simmer for about 1 minute.

Makes four 1-cup servings.

Nutrition information per serving: Calories: 496 Protein: 29.4 grams
Fat: 11.5 grams Colesterol: 3.9 mg Fiber: 22.1 grams Sodium: 483 mg
