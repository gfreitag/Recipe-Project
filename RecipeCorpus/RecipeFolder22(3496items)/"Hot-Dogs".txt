Title: "Hot Dogs"
Categories: Vegetarian, Appetizer
Yield: 20 servings
1/4 ts Ground red peppers; diced
1 1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Oil for deep frying
1 cn Chickpeas (1 lb)
3    Slices white bread; trimmed
Egg replacer equal to 2 eggs
1/2 ts Garlic; minced
In blender grind chick peas and bread together. Mix in egg
substitute, garlic, red peppers, salt and pepper.
Shape into finger length rolls and chill 1 hour. Heat the oil to 370
deg in deep fryer and carefully drop the "hot dogs" into it. Fry
until browned; drain.
Serve with mustard or hot chili sauce. Makes about 20.
