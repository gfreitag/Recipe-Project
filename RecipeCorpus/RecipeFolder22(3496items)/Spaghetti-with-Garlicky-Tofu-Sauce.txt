Title: Spaghetti with Garlicky Tofu Sauce
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Pasta
Yield: 2 servings
Oregano
Pepper, black
Basil
1 lb Spaghetti, whole wheat;
--cooked
2    Garlic cloves; crushed
1    Onion; chopped
1    Bell pepper, green; chopped
1 lb Tofu, firm; cubed
2 ts Oil, canola
26 oz Sauce, tomato or spaghetti
Saute garlic, onion, pepper and tofu in oil.

Add tomato or spaghetti sauce and heat thoroughly.
Season to taste.

Serve over whole grain spaghetti.

Makes 4 cups.

Per cup: 437.8 cal. (24% from fat), 28 g protein, 12 g fat, 54 g
carb, 82 mg sod, 0 mg chol, 10 g fiber
