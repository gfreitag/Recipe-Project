Title: Classic Salsa (Hot!)
Categories: Vegetarian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
ds Honey or alternative
Chili powder
Salt
Wine vinegar or lemon juice
2 c  Tomatoes; peeled, chopped
2 tb Cilantro; minced
2 tb Onion, red; minced
1 ts Chilies, hot; minced
Combine tomatoes, cilantro, onion and hot pepper.
Season to taste with sweetener, chili powder, salt, and wine vinegar.
Yields 2 1/2 cups.
