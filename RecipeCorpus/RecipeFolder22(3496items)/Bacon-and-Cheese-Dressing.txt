Title: Bacon and Cheese Dressing
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1 1/4 c  Milk
1 1/2 oz Bacon,cooked crisp, crumbled
1 qt Chef-Mate Bacon 'n Cheddar
-Cheese Sauce
1 qt Mayonnaise
1.  Mix ingredients throughly. Chill. 2. Serve over salad greens.

Makes 2 quarts 1 1/2 cups.
