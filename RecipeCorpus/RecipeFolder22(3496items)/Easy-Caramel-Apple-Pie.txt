Title: Easy Caramel Apple Pie
Categories: Dessert, Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
2/3 c  Caramel ice cream topping
2 ts Lemon juice
1    (9 inch) Pet Ritz pie crust
1/2 c  Pecan pieces
1 pk Caramel candies
1/3 c  Flour
3 c  Chopped Jonathan or Rome
Apples
Combine 10 caramel candies, each cut into 4 pieces, and flour. Add
chopped Jonathan or Rome apples, caramel ice cream topping, and lemon
juice. Mix well. Pour mixture into a frozen, regular 9" Pet Ritz pie
crust and sprinkle with pecan pieces.  Bake at 375Ã¸F on a preheated
baking sheet for 40-45 minutes.  Store at room temperature.
