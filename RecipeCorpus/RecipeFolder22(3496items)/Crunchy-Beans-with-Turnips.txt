Title: Crunchy Beans with Turnips
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Soy oil
1 1/2 oz Walnuts; chopped
3 oz Soybean sprouts
3 oz Turnips; sliced 1/4-inch
-thick
3 oz Lima beans; cooked
2 tb Chives; minced
1 ts Curry
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Lightly grease 4 x 8 baking pan with
soy oil. Steam turnips. Combinelima beans, chives, curry, soy sauce,
salt andsoy oil in a blender with 2 ounces water. Pureeuntilsmooth.
Combine all ingredients together and mix well. Transfer to baking pan
and cook for 15 minutes.
