Title: Ginger Salad Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Diced fresh ginger
2 ts Sugar
1 1/2 ts Grated lemon peel
1/2 ts Catsup
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 c  Sesame or soy oil
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/3 c  Diced Onion
1/4 c  Diced celery
3 tb Rice wine vinegar; plus---->
1 ts Rice wine vinegar
Place all ingredients into blender or food processor and blend until
vegetables are pureed. Store in covered jar. Shake thoroughly before
using. Karen Mintzias TCVC49A
