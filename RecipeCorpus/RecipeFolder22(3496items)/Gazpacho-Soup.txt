Title: Gazpacho Soup
Categories: Vegetarian, Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb White horseradish
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ts Salt
1 cn Tomato juice (46 oz)
1 sm Celery stalk
1 bn Green onions
1    Cucumber, unpeeled
3 md Tomatoes
1 lg Green pepper
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Wine vinegar
Grate or chop vegetables fine by hand or use a food processor.Mix
remaining ingredients thoroughly and combine with vegetables.
Refrigerate several hours or overnight. Serves 8.

Source: Suncoast Seasonings, by the Dunedin Youth Guild, Inc.,
Dunedin, FL; received in November Cookbook Swap from Sue Woodward/MM
by DEEANNE
