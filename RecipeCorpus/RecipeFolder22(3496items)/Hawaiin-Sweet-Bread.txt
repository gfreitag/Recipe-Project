Title: Hawaiin Sweet Bread
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Lemon extract
1/4 ts Vanilla extract
2    Eggs
4 tb Butter
1 c  Warm water
3 ts Dry yeast
5 tb Sugar
3 c  Bread flour
3/4 ts Salt
2 tb Dry milk
2 tb Instant potato flakes
This is from "Great Bread Machine Recipes" by Norman A. Garrett.
