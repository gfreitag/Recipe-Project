Title: Chocolate Rum Balls
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 tb Light corn syrup
-- (to 2 tbs)
1/3 c  Chocolate shots (to
-- 1/2 cup)
2 c  Chocolate cake crumbs,
-- stale
1 tb Butter; melted
1 tb Light rum
1 1/2 ts Rum extract
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:15 1. In a medium bowl, combine cake crumbs,
melted butter, rum, and rum extract. Mix well. Add 1 1/2 Tbs corn
syrup and blend until mixture holds together. Add remaining 1/2 Tbs
corn syrup if needed to bind. 2. Form into 8 or 9 (1 3/4-inch) balls
by rolling mixture between palms of hands. Then roll balls in
chocolate shots to coat completely. Store in a tightly covered
container in refrigerator. Serve in paper candy cups.

Note: Recipe may be doubled.
