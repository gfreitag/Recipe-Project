Title: Waimea Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit
Yield: 8 -10 servin
1 ts Mint
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/4 ts Salt
Crisp salad greens
4    Bananas, sliced
2 cn Pineapple chunks (20 oz can)
2 cn Oranges, drained (11 oz can)
4 c  Strawberries, halved
3 tb Lime juice, fresh
3 tb Lemon juice, fresh
3 tb Vegetable oil
Drain pineapple, reserve 3/4 cup juice.  Chill pineapple, orange, and
strwberries.  Blend reserved pineapple juice, lime juice, oil, mint,
curry powder, and salt in a small bowl and refrigerate covered.  When
ready to serve salad, arrange crisp greens on large serving platter.
Add bananas to other fruits.  Attractively arrange fruits on greens.
Serve with dressing. Submitted By FLEURETTE DELISLE   On   02-06-95
