Title: Freezer Mix Goulash
Categories: Meat
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Brown sugar
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
3 oz Noodles
1 pk Beef-Tomato Freezer Mix
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Catsup
1 c  Celery; sliced
1 cn Mushroom;stems/pieces(4oz)
Dip container of frozen mix into hot water just to loosen. Place
frozen mix and remaining ingredients except noodles in 2-quart
saucepan; heat to boiling. Reduce heat; cover and simmer, stirring
frequently, until mix is thawed and celery is tender, about 40
minutes. While meat mixture is simmering, cook noodles as directed on
package; drain. Stir noodles into meat mixture and simmer until hot.
