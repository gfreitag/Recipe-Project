Title: Kettle Goulash
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
3 lb Beef, stewing; in 1" cubes
Pepper; to taste
Salt; to taste
6 oz Tomato paste
Sour cream; at room temp.
4 tb Bacon fat
5 lg Onions; coarsly chopped
2 lg Pepper, green bell; chopped
3    Garlic clove; minced
1 1/2 tb Paprika, Hungarian
Preheat oven to 325Ã¸F.  Heat fat in a deep heavy pot. Cook the onions,
peppers, and garlic until the onions are limp and transparent. Add
paprika and cook until it has lost its raw taste. Add beef and
remaining ingredients except sour cream.  Stir well to combine.
Simmer in preheated oven for 1 1/2 to 2 hours or until the meat is
tender. Adjust oven temperature during cooking time so contents of
pot remain at a simmer. Serve in shallow soup bowls with a tablespoon
of sour cream atop each serving.
