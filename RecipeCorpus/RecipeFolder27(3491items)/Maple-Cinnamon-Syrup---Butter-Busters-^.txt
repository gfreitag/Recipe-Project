Title: Maple Cinnamon Syrup - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Tb skim milk
1 ea Tb liquid Butter Buds
1/4 t  Maple flavoring
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1 t  Cornstarch
1/4 t  Cinnamon
1/2 c  PLUS see next line
Combine sugar, cornstarch and cinnamon in a small saucepan, stirring
well. Gradualy stir in milk. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer
until slightly thickened, stirring constantly. Remove from heat; add
liquid Butter Busters and maple flavoring stirring until mixed.
Serving size is 2 tablespoons Per sugar serving: 35 cal., og fat
(0%), 0.5mg chol., 0g fiber, 0.5g pro., 8g carb., 26mg sod. Per S&L
serving: 22cal., 4.5g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN
