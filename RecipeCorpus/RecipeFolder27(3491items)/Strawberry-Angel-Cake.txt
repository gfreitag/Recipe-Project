Title: Strawberry Angel Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 pk Strawberry Jello
1 c  Hot water
2 c  Frozen strawberries
1 sm Container of Cool Whip
3/4    Angel food loaf cake
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding
1 c  Milk
1 pt Vanilla ice cream
Mix milk, pudding and melted ice cream, pour over Angel food cake,
that has been torn into small pieces.  Spread into a 8 x 12 inch pan
and chill for 1/2 hour.  Thaw strawberries and add Jello dissolved in
hot water, chill until it starts to set and pour over cake.  Spread
top with Cool Whip. Randy Rigg
