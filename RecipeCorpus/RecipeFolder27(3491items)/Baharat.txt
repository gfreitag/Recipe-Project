Title: Baharat
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------AFRICA - THE GULF STATES-----------------------
1 sm Piece of cinnamon
Seeds from 6 green cardamons
2 tb Paprika
1 ts Ground chili
1/2    Nutmeg, grated
1 tb Black peppercorns
1 tb Coriander seeds
1 tb Cumin seeds
1 tb Cloves
Grind all the ingredients together. Use to spice meats and
vegetables. Can be kept stored in an airtight container for 3-4
months.
