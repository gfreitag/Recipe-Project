Title: SHERBAT (LEMON DRINK COOLER)
Categories: Indian, Vegetarian, Beverage
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea Lemons
2 tb Rose water
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Ice, crushed
4 c  Water
Squeeze juice from the 2 lemons and reserve juice.

In a saucepan, combine sugar, crushed ice, 4 cups water, lemon juice
and rose water.

When sugar is completely dissolved, strain and serve the Sherbat in
frosty glasses.
