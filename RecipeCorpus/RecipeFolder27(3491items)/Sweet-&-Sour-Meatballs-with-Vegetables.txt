Title: Sweet & Sour Meatballs with Vegetables
Categories: Main dish, Vegetable, Low-cal/fat, Rice dishes, Turkey
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Lite soy sauce;
1/4 ts Ground ginger;
16 oz Frozen oriental/mixed
Vegetables;prepared to
Package directions
1 c  Prepared sweet & sour sauce;
Hot cooked rice;(optional)
1 lb Ground turkey;
3/4 c  Multi-bran Chex crushed;
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1/4 c  Cholesterol-free egg product
Or 1 egg beaten
2 tb Fresh parsley; chopped
1    Clove garlic;minced
In medium bowl combine turkey6, cereal, onion, egg, parsley, garlic,
soy sauce and ginger.  Mix well.  Using 1 rounded tablespoon meat
mixture for each, shape into 2-inch balls.  Brown meatballs in
lightly greased skillet over medium heat, turning often add water;
cover and cook over low heat 8 to 10 minutes or until no longer pink,
stirring gently until meatballs are coated and sauce and vegetables
are warm. Serve over rice if desired.
