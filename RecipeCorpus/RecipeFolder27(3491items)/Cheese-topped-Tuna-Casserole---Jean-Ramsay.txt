Title: Cheese-topped Tuna Casserole - Jean Ramsay
Categories: Casserole, Main dish, Fish
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Cooked rice
Salt to taste
Pepper to raste
1/3 c  Cheddar cheese/grated
1/3 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Butter/margarine
1 cn Cream of celery soup
2 ts Lemon juice
2/3 c  Tuna, drained & flaked
Cook onion in butter/margarine until tender but not brown.   Stir
in remaining ingredients except cheese.   Turn into a buttered,
shallow 6-inch casserole or individual casseroles;  bake in preheated
350 degree F oven for 20 minutes, or until heated through.   Top with
cheese; bake 5 minutes longer
