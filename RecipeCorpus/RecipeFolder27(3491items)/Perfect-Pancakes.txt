Title: Perfect Pancakes
Categories: Mixes, Breakfast, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 12 pancakes
1 c  Water
3 T  Oil
1 1/2 c  Pancake mix
1    Egg, slightly beaten
Put pancake mix in medium bowl.  Combine egg, water and oil in a small
bowl.  Add egg mixture to pancake mix. Add more water for thinner
pancakes. Blend well. Let stand 5 minutes. Cook on a hot oiled
griddle about 3 to 4 minutes, until browned on each side.
