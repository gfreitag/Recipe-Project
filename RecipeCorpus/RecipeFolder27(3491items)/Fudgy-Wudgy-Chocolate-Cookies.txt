Title: Fudgy Wudgy Chocolate Cookies
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 45 servings
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk,
(14 oz can)
2 c  Pecans; chopped
12 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
2 tb Butter
3/4 c  Flour
2 ts Vanilla extract
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:20 1. Preheat oven to 350 F. In a 2-qt glass
bowl, cook chocolate with butter in a microwave on High 1 to 2 mins,
or until melted and smooth when stirred. Mix in flour until well
blended. Add vanilla and sweetened condensed milk and mix well. Stir
in pecans. 2. Drop dough by tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart into 2
greased foil-lined cookie sheets. Bake 9 to 10 mins (cookies will be
dull on top and soft). Let cookies cool on baking sheets. Carefully
remove from foil.
