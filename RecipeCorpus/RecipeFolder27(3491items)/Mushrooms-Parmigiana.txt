Title: Mushrooms Parmigiana
Categories: Appetizer, Italian, Mushroom
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
Salt & pepper
Water
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 lb Lg fresh mushrooms
2    Cloves garlic, minced
2 T  Italian parsley, chopped
3 T  Parmesan cheese, grated
1 ts Oregano
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Wash mushrooms and remove stems. Chop
the stems and mix with the garlic, parsley, cheese, oregano, bread
crumbs, and salt and pepper to taste.  Place the mushroom caps cavity
side up in an oiled shallow baking dish.  Fill the cavities with the
chopped mixture and fill the the bottom of the baking dish with 1/4"
water. Pour the olive oil evenly over the caps and bake 30 minutes.
Serve hot. Submitted By KRISTIN TREMAYNE   On   02-12-95
