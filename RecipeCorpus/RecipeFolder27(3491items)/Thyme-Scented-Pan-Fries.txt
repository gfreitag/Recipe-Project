Title: Thyme-Scented Pan Fries
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
5 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Salt
Pepper to taste
6    Baking potatoes
-cut into 1/2" wedges
2 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Thyme, chopped or
SautÂ‚ potatoes in oil in large heavy skillet over medium heat for
about 3 minutes, stirring frequently.

Add remaining ingredients, reduce heat to medium-low and cook for 5
minutes. Turn potatoes and cover. Cook until tender, about 12 minutes.

Uncover and raise heat to medium-high. Cook until potatoes are
browned, turning occasionally. Drain on paper towels. Serve hot.
