Title: Bhoona Curry
Categories: Main dish, Curries
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-------------------------SPICE MIX------------------------------
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Turmeric
1 ts Garam masala
1 ts Ground coriander
MMMMM---------------------OTHER INGREDIENTS--------------------------
2    Cloves garlic
1 tb Vinegar
2 oz Coconut; creamed
-or dessicated equiv
Salt to taste
1 1/2 lb Cubed lamb or beef
-or pork or chicken
2 tb Ghee or vegetable oil
1 md Onion
2 md Tomatoes
Bhoonas are dry curries with a rich, creamy coconut sauce.

1. Heat oil or ghee. Fry meat for 5 to 10 minutes.

2. Blend the onion, garlic, tomatoes and vinegar.

3. Stir-fry the blend, spice mixture and coconut for 5 minutes.

4. Combine the meat and the fried blend. Simmer until the meat is
tender (or cook in 375F oven). The dish should be quite dry with a
little bit of gravy. Stir frequently, adding a little water if it
sticks. Add salt to taste.

Source: The Curry Club Blended Spice Mix
