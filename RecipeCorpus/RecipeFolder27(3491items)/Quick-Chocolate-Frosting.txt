Title: Quick Chocolate Frosting
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Powdered sugar
6 tb Milk
6 oz Semisweet chocolate
Chips
2 tb Butter
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:04

1. In a 1-qt glass bowl, combine chocolate chips and butter. Heat in a
microwave on High 1 to 1 1/2 mins, or until melted and smooth when
stirred.

2. Beat in powdered sugar and milk.
