Title: Spam Lasagna
Categories: Pasta, Main dish, Spam, Tomato, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 - 8 folks
1    Bay leaf;
6 oz Lasagna noodles; cooked
1 1/2 c  Mozzarella cheese; diced
-(6-oz)
1 1/2 c  Romano cheese; freshly
-grated
1 1/2 c  Cottage cheese; -=OR=-
1 1/2 c  Ricotta drained
2 c  Diced spam;
1 cn (16-oz) tomatoes; cut up
1 c  (15-oz) tomatoe sauce;
1 c  Mushrooms; fresh chopped
1/2 c  Marsala
1/2 c  Broth;
1 cl Garlic; minced
1 ts Dried rosemary leaves;
-crushed
1/2 ts Sage leaves; crushed
In a large skillet combine first 9 ingredients.  Simmer, uncovered,
till slightly thickened, 15 to 20  minutes. Remove bay leaf.  Spoon A
THIRD of sauce into a 9x9x2" baking pan.  Place 3 or 4 lasagna
noodles over it. Combine 3 cheese.  Layer HALF the cheeses and the
noodles atop the noodles. Spoon 1/3 more sauce over. Repeat noodle,
cheese, spam and sauce. Cover, leave vent open. Bake for 45 minutes.
Uncover; let stand for 10 to 15 minutes before serving. Serves 6 to 8.
