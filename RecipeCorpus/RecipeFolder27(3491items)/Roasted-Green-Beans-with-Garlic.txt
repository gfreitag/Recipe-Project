Title: Roasted Green Beans with Garlic
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
Salt and pepper
3    Anchovy fillets; mashed
2 ts To 3 ts lemon juice
Zest of 1 lemon,
-finely grated
1 lb Green beans; stemmed
3    Garlic cloves; smashed
3    Sprigs of thyme
-coarsely chopped
1/4 c  Extra-virgin olive oil
1. Preheat oven to 450Ã¸F. In a large baking dish, toss the green
beans with the garlic, thyme and olive oil and season with salt and
pepper. Spread the beans and seasonings in a single layer and roast
for about 15 minutes, tossing occasionally, until tender and lightly
browned. Discard the thyme.

2. Transfer the beans to a bowl. Add the anchovies, lemon juice and
lemon zest and toss well to coat. Serve warm or at room temperature.
