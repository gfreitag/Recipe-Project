Title: Italian Spring Bread
Categories: Bread, Fruit, Italian
Yield: 1 loaf
1/4 c  Anise
3 c  Bread flour
2 1/2 t  Yeast
2/3 c  Golden raisins
1 c  Water
2 T  Oil
2 t  Honey
2/3 t  Salt
Use raisin bread or mix cycle.  Raisins are to be added at beep.
