Title: Grandma's Picnic Cake
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 cake
1 t  Cinnamon
1 t  Vanilla
3/4 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Chopped nuts
3/4 c  Chocolate chips
1 c  Chopped dates
1 t  Baking soda
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
4 c  Basic cake mix
2    Eggs, beaten
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease a 13 x 9 pan.  In a small bowl, combine
dates, baking soda and boiling water. Set aside to cool. In a large
bowl, combine cake mix, eggs, cinnamon and vanilla. Beat at medium
speed 2 to 3 minutes. Scrape beaters and bowl and beat 2 more
minutes. Add date mixture. Beat 2 more minutes. Pour into prepared
pan. Top with brown sugar, nuts and then chocolate chips.  Bake 30 to
35 minutes. Cool in pan 10 minutes, then cool on a wire rack.
