Title: Bugs on a Log
Categories: Kids, Snack
Yield: 1 recipe
Chocolate chips
Celery stalks
Peanut butter
Take a stalk of celery and fill center with peanut butter. Place
pieces of chocolate chips on top of "log."

Variations: cream cheese or Cheez Whiz, raisins

from: The Daily Herald, 2/14/95
