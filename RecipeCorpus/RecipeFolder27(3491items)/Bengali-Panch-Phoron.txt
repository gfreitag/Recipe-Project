Title: Bengali Panch Phoron
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------INDIA--------------------------------
1 tb Nigella seeds
-(black onion seeds)
1 tb Fenugreek seeds
1 tb Cumin seeds
1 tb Fennel seeds
1 tb Black mustard seeds
Mix all the spices together. Use to flavor pulses and vegetable
dishes. Stored in an airtight container, it will keep for 3-4 months.
