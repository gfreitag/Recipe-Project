Title: Caramelized Sweet Onion Slices
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, West europe
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Packed brown sugar
2 tb Dried thyme, crushed
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Posted by Stu Hopper
3 ea Large Spanish Sweet Onions
-(about 3 lbs)
1/4 c  Butter
3/4 c  Granulated sugar
Peel and cut onions into 3/4 to 1" slices.  Microwave slices at high
(100%) 5 minutes.  Heat butter in large skillet; add onion slices.
Combine sugars, thyme, salt and pepper.  Sprinkle 1 tablespoon sugar
mixture over each slice; cook over medium-low heat about 10 minutes
on first side. Turn slices, sprinkle each slice with 1 tablespoon
sugar mixture and cook 10 more minutes.  Turn slices and cook 1
minute to caramelize sugar topping. Keep warm and repeat procedure
for remaining onion slices. Submitted By STEWART HOPPER   On
02-19-95
