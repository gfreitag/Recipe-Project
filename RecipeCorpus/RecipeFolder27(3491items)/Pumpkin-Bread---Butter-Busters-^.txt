Title: Pumpkin Bread - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Bread
Yield: 36 servings
1 t  Salt
1 cn (16oz) pumpkin
2/3 c  Water
2 t  Baking soda
3 c  Flour
1 c  Liquid Butter buds or
-Promise Ultra fatfree
3 c  Sugar*
1 c  Egg Beaters
1 t  Nutmeg
1 t  Cinnamon
*Or 1 1/2 cups sugar and 18 packets Sweet 'n Low Beat first seven
ingredients until smooth. Add the water, flour, baking soda. Pour into
three loaf or 1 pound coffee cans that have been sprayed with Pam.
Bake 55 minutes at 350F. Serve with Dream Whip on top. Per sugar
serving: 116 cal., 0.4g fat (3%), 0mg chol., 0.5g fiber, 2g pro., 26g
carb., 108mg sod. Per S&L serving: 85 cal., 18g carb. Butter Busters
by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 2-95.
