Title: Tomato and Fennel Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Tomato, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
-1/2 a small bulb
1 ts Fennel seeds, crushed
Salt and pepper
1 1/2 c  Prepared spaghetti sauce
3    Plum tomatoes
-seeded, chopped (1 cup)
1 c  Fennel, thinly sliced
Combine spaghetti sauce, tomatoes, sliced fennel and fennel seeds in
saucepan. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat to low and cook, stirring
occasionally, until fresh fennel is tender, about 5 minutes. Season
with salt and pepper to taste. SErve over cooked pasta.
