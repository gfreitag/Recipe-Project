Title: Flour and Corn Tortillas
Categories: Bread
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Water
1 3/4 c  Masa
1/3 c  Flour
Mix thoroughly.  Shape into 12 balls.  Roll out and bake on lightly
greased griddle until light brown.
