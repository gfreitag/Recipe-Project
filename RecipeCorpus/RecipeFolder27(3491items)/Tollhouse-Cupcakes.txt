Title: Tollhouse Cupcakes
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Plus 2 tb of flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter
6 tb Granulated sugar
6 tb Brown sugar
1    Egg
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
6 oz Chocolate chips
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
1    Egg
1/8 ts Salt
Combine and beat first four ingredients.  Sift together and add the
last three ingredients.  Spoon by rounded tb into cupcake papers.
Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes.

Beat until creamy first three ingredients.  Add chips, nuts and
vanilla. Stir.  Spoon 1 tb over each cupcake.  Bake at 375 degrees
for 15 minutes. Randy Rigg
