Title: Zucchini-Mushroom Cheese Melt - Butter busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 4 ----------
1/4 t  Garlic powder
1/8 t  Oregano
1 c  Nonfat mozzarella cheese,
-grated
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
1/4 lb Sliced fresh mushrooms
1 ea Tb liquid Butter Buds or
-fatfree chicken broth
2 c  Grated zucchini
1/4 t  Lite salt, optional
Brown mushrooms in Butter Buds in a nonstick pan. Stir in all the
rest of the ingredients except cheese. Pout into a 1-qt. casserole
dish that has been sprayed with a nonstick spray. Sprinkle cheese on
top and stir into mixture. Bake at 350F. for about 25 minutes. Per
serving: 48 cal., 0.2g fat (4%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 7g pro., 5g
carb., 133mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5
