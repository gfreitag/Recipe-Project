Title: Black Pepper-Mustard Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Condiment
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Liquid used for steaming
-or fish stock
1/4 c  Light cream
2 tb Dijon mustard
1/4 ts Pepper
Whisk cream and mustard together in small saucepan; stir in pepper
and set aside. Add steaming liquid to pan. Bring sauce to a boil,
remove from heat and serve.
