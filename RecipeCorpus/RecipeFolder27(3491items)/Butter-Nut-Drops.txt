Title: Butter-Nut Drops
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains, So. america, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Lemon juice
2 T  Grated orange rind
1 T  Grated lemon rind
1/2 c  Brazil Nuts, ground fine
cn Cherries
1/2 c  Butter
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg, separated
1/2 t  Vanilla
1/4 t  Salt
1 c  Cake flour
Cream butter and sugar well.  Add egg yolk and flavoring.  Beat well.
Sift dry ingredients together and add with lemon juice and rinds.  Mix
thoroughly and place in a covered bowl.  Chill.  Roll into tiny balls
(1/2 tsp. of dough per ball).  Dip balls into slightly beaten egg
white. Roll in ground nuts.  Place on greased cookie sheet 1 inch
apart.  Press 1/2 candied cherry on top of each and bake at 350 F. 25
minutes.  Makes 40. From: Culinary Art's Institute Encyclopedic
CookBook Shared By: Pat Stockett
