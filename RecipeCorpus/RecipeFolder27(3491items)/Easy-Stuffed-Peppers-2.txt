Title: Easy Stuffed Peppers 2
Categories: Beef, Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
Dash of pepper
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1 ts Onion, minced
1 cn Tomato soup
6 md Green peppers
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Bread crumbs
1/2 ts Salt
Remove tops & seeds from peppers; steam shells about 5 min.  Combine
remaining ingredients using only 1/2 can of soup.  Mix well.  Fill
peppers with stuffing.  Bake in a moderate oven , 375 degrees, for 30
min. Pour remaining 1/2 can of soup over peppers.  Continue baking 15
min. Serves 6 Submitted By SUE LASTER   On   02-08-95 (1745)
