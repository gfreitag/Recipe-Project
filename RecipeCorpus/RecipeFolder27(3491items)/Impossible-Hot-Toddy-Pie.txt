Title: Impossible Hot Toddy Pie
Categories: Dessert, Cheese/eggs, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Rum or
1 ts Rum Flavoring
ds Ground Nutmeg
1 1/2 c  Canned Eggnog
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Bisquick
4    EGGS
Heat oven to 350.  Grease 9x1-1/4" pie plate.  Place all ingredients
except nutmeg in blender.  Cover and blend on high 15 seconds. Pour
into plate; sprinkle with nutmeg. Bake until knife inserted in center
comes out clean, about 40 minutes.  Cool 5 minutes. Serve with spices
Whipped Cream.
