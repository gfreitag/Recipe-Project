Title: Broccoli Cheese-Rice Casserole - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 14 ----------
-chestnuts, rinsed and
-drained
2 c  Grated nonfat Cheddar cheese
1 c  Homemade lowfat croutons or
-Pepperidge Farms seasoned
-stuffing cubes
1 t  Mrs. Dash salt-free extra
-spicy seasoning(to11/2tsp)
2 ea Boxes Uncle Ben's Llong
-Grain and Wild Rice*
2 ea Pk frozen chopped broccoli,
-prepared as directed
1 cn (14oz) reduced sodium
-chicken broth
2 cn (8oz) chopped mushrooms,
-rinsed and drained
2 cn (8oz) whole water
*the 5 minute variety, prepared with liquid Butter Buds. Prepare rice
as directed on package. Add the cooked broccoli, mushrooms, water
chestnuts and chicken broth to the rice mixture. Add seasoning and
cheese. Spoon into a large casserole that has been sprayed with
nonstick spray. Top with croutons. Bake at 350F. 30-35 minutes
covered. Remove the cover the last 10 minutes. Per serving: 172 cal.,
0.9g fat (5%), 0mg chol, 2g fiber, 8g pro., 33g carb., 305mg sod.
