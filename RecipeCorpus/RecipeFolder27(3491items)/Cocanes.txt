Title: Cocanes
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
3 ea Eggs
2 1/2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Butter
5 1/2 c  Flour
This mixture requires no liquid. Mix with hands until it will hold
together. Boll one-half inch thick and cut into shape of fingers.
Bake in hot oven.

Note: Hot oven is 400 - 450 F.

Source: Birds Run Grange, Gurnsey County, OH
