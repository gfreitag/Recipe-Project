Title: Barbecued Beef short Ribs
Categories: Bbq/grill, Beef, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 ts Garlic salt
1 1/2 ts Prepared mustard
2    Lemons, sliced thinly
1 md Onion, sliced thinly
4 1/2 lb Beef short ribs, cut into
-serving-size pieces
1 1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1 ts Beef bouillon granules
1/3 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
Preheat oven to 350 deg f.  Place short ribs in a deep roasting pan.
In small bowl, combine tomato sauce, bouillon granules, red wine
vinegar, brown sugar, worcestershire sauce, garlic salt, and prepared
mustard. Pour sauce over ribs; place lemon and onions slices over
sauce.  Bake ribs, covered, until tender, 1 1/2 to 2 hours. Serve
ribs with sauce. Submitted By FLEURETTE DELISLE   On   02-06-95
