Title: Peachy Spiced Chicken
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Plus 1 ts vegetable oil
1/2 ts Imitation butter flavor
1 tb Firmly packed brown sugar
1 1/4 lb Boneless skinless chicken
1 1/2 c  Peach slices, drained
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 tb Vinegar
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
Several hours before baking, place chicken in an 8" square pan that
has been sprayed with a nonstick cooking spray. Combine peaches with
the remaining ingredients, pour over chicken, and chill several
hours. Return to room temperature before baking. Preheat oven to 350.
Cover and bake 1 hour.

Per serving: 36g protein, 9g fat, 15g carb., 115mg sodium, 99mg
chol., 282 calories
