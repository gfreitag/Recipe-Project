Title: Layered Nutty Chocolate Squares
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 45 servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
9 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
2 c  Peanuts or pecans, salted
-- coarsely chopped
1 1/2    Sticks butter or margarine,
-- (plus 1 tbs); softened
3/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
1    Egg
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:40 1. Preheat oven to 350 F. In a medium bowl,
combine 1 1/2 sticks butter and brown sugar. Beat with an electric
mixer on medium until light and fluffy, 2 to 3 mins. Beat in egg and
1 tsp vanilla until blended. Add flour and beat until well blended.
Press dough evenly in bottom of a foil-lined 9 x 13-inch baking pan.
Bake 16 to 18 mins, or until light golden. Let cool 10 to 15 mins. 2.
Meanwhile, in a 1-qt glass bowl, combine chocolate chips and corn
syrup. Heat in microwave on High 1 to 1 1/2 mins, stirring until
smooth. Stir in remaining 1 Tbs butter and remaining 1/2 tsp vanilla
until blended. 3. Spread chocolate mixture evenly over baked crust.
Sprinkle nuts over top; press in gently. Refrigerate until set, 2 to
3 hours. Cut into 45 squares.
