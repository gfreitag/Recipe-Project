Title: Spicy Chili Dip - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizer, Pickles
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn (4oz) chopped green
-chilies, drained
1 sm Tomato, chopped
1 c  Grated nonfat Cheddar cheese
1/2 lb Lean ground white meat
-turkey w/o skin
1 ea Pk taco seasoning mix, (any
-brand)
1 sm Onion, chopped
Cook turkey meat and chopped onion in a bowl in the microwave with a
piece of waxed paper on it. Cook on high2-3 minutes. Check and stir.
Cook longer if needed. Remove turkey and onion from bowl and drain
off fat. Prepare taco mix with the microwave instructions that are on
the package, using only 1/4 cup water. Stir in the chiles and tomato.
Sprinkle grated cheese on top. Serve hot with Smart Temptations low
fat tortilla chips. Per 1/4 cup serving: 84 cal., 1g fat (11%), 19mg
chol., 0.5g fiber, 12g pro., 6g carb., 566mg sod. Butter Busters by
