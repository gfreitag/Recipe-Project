Title: BLACK FOREST BROWNIE PIE
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 cn Cherry pie filling
1/4 c  Butter or margarine,melted
2 oz Unsweetened chocolate,
-melted
2/3 c  Sugar
1 lg Egg
MMMMM----------------------CHOCOLATE CRUST---------------------------
3 tb Butter or margarine,melted
24    Chocolate wafers
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
2 ts Whipping cream
1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips,
Combine butter, chocolate, sugar, egg, milk, and vanilla; beat well.
Add flour and nuts; beat well. Spread batter into crust. Bake in a
350'F. oven until top looks dry and feels firm when gently touched,
18-20 minutes. cool; spread top with frosting. When frosting is cool,
transfer cherries from can with a slotted spoon to top dessert.

*** CHOCOLATE CRUST *** Finely crush chocolate wafers to make 1 1/4
cups crumbs; pour into a 9" pie pan. Mix with 3 tablespooons melted
butter or margarine and press firmly over pan bottom and up side.
Bake in a 350'F. oven until darker brown at rim, 8-10 minutes, cool.

*** FROSTIMG *** In a 1- to 1 1/2 quart pan over low heat, stir 1/2
cup semisweet chocolate baking chips with 2 tablespoons whipping
cream just until snooth. Use warm.
