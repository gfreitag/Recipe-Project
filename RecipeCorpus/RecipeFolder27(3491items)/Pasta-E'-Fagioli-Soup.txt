Title: Pasta E' Fagioli Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
2 3/4 qt (88 oz) beef stock
3 ts Oregano
2 1/2 ts Pepper (black)
5 ts Parsley (fresh chopped)
1 1/2 ts Tabasco sauce
3 ts Oil
2 lb Ground beef
12 oz Chopped onion
14 oz Slivered carrots
14 oz Diced celery
1 1/2 qt (48-oz) tomatoes (diced-cann    1 1/2 qt (48-oz) spaghetti sauce
Shell or elbow macaroni nood
2    15oz. cans of red kidney bea
1    48 oz. jar white kidney bean
Saute beef in oil in a large 10-qt. pot until beef starts to brown.
Add onions, carrots, celery and tomatoes and simmer for about 10
minutes. Drain and rinse beans and add to the pot. Also add beef
stock, oregano, >> pepper, Tabasco, spaghetti sauce, and noodles.
Simmer until celery and carrots are tender, about 45 minutes. This
recipe will make 9 quarts of soup!!! Great for guests!!!
