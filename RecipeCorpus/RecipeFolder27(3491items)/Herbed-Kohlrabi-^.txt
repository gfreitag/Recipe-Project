Title: Herbed Kohlrabi ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Dijon mustard
1 tb Snipped fresh parsley
1/2 ts Dried savory, crushed
1 lb Kohlrabi, peeled and cubed
-(2 1/2 cups)
1 tb Margarine or butter
Cook kohlrabi, covered, in enough boiling water to cover in a medium
saucepan for about 10 minutes or till crisp tender. Drain well. Add
the remaining ingredients. Cook and stir over low heat till kohlrabi
is coated. 4 servings.

Microwave directions: Place the kohlrabi in a microwave safe 1 quart
casserole. Add 2 tablespoons water. Microwave, covered, on high 4-6
minutes or till crisp tender, stirring once. Drain. Add reamining
ingredilents. Cook 45-60 seconds unril margarine melts. Stir to coat.

Per serving: 57 cal., 3g fat, 0mg chol., 117mg sodium, 7g carb., 2g
fiber, 2g pro., 1 vegetable and 1/2 fat exchanges.
