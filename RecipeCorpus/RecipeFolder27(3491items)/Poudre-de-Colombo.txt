Title: Poudre de Colombo
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------MARTINIQUE AND GUADELOPE-----------------------
1 ts Ground coriander seeds
1 ts Ground mustard seeds
3    Garlic clove(s)
2    Hot red chilies
1/8 ts Turmeric
Peel and crush the garlic; seed and mash the chilies. combine all the
ingredients and mix to a paste. Can be stored in the refrigerator for
up to 6 weeks.
