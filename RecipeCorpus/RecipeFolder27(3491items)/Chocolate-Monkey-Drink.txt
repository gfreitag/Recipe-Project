Title: Chocolate Monkey Drink
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 2 servings
3 tb Banana liqueur
2 c  Crushed ice
Maraschino cherries
Banana slices
Whipped cream
2    Scoops chocolate ice cream,
-- (generous scoops)
1/2 c  Chocolate syrup
1    Banana; cut-up
3 tb Creme de cacao
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:10 1. In a blender or food processor, blend
together ice cream, chocolate syrup, banana, creme de cacao, banana
liqueur, and crushed ice until smooth. 2. Pour into 2 or 3 stemmed
glasses. Garnish each glass with a maraschino cherry, banana slice,
and whipped cream. Serve immediately.

Note: For a non-alcoholic version, simply omit the liqueurs. Kids
will love it.
