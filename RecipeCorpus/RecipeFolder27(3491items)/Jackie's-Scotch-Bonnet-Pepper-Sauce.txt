Title: Jackie's Scotch Bonnet Pepper Sauce
Categories: Caribbean, Condiment, Hot/spicy, Chilies
Yield: 2 pints
Rind of One Lime
1/2 c  Lime Juice
1 1/2 c  Malt Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Prepared Yellow Mustard
1    Green Papaya, Peeled, Seeded
--and Roughly Chopped
10    Scotch Bonnet Peppers,
--Seeded
2    Onions, Quartered
3    Cloves Garlic
Puree the papaya, Scotch Bonnet peppers, onions, garlic, lime rind,
and lime juice in a food processor.  Transfer to a medium saucepan
and stir in the vinegar, salt and mustard.  Simmer the mixture over
low heat for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Bottle the sauce in
hot sterilized jars.

From Sugar Reef Caribbean Cookbook.
