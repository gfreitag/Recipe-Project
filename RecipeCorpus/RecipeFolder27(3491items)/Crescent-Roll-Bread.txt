Title: Crescent Roll Bread
Categories: Bread
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 ea Stick margarine or butter
3 cn Crescent rolls (large)
Melt butter in Bundt pan. Remove rolls from cans but do not unroll or
separate. Preheat oven to 325~F. Bake about 25 - 35 minutes or until
golden brown. To remove from pan, invert immediately on plate, being
careful of any leftover butter.
