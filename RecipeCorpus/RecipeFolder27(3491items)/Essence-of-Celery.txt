Title: Essence of Celery
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 batch  The authors note that they found this recipe in the 113th edition of
"Enquire Within", published in 1923:

This is prepared by soaking for a fortnight half an ounce of the
seeds of celery in a quarter of a pint of brandy.  A few drops will
flavour a pint of soup or broth equal to a head of celery.

from Edible Gifts by Claire Clifton and Martina Nicolls typed by
