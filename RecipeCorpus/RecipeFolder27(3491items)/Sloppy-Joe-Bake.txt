Title: Sloppy Joe Bake
Categories: Casserole, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Butter/margarine, softened
1    Egg
4    Slices American cheese,
Cut in half diagionally
1 lb Lean ground beef or ground
Turkey
1 cn Sloppy joe sauce or your own
Homemade sauce
2 c  Bisquick baking mix
Heat oven to 375 degrees F.  Grease a 9x1 1/2 inch pan heavely.
Brown Meat and drain of any excess grease.  Add sauce and heat, being
careful not to let it stick to the pan.

Mix milk, butter/margarine, egg and baking mix, mixture will be
slightly lumpy.  Spread batter in pan, spreading it up the sides
about 1/4. Smooth over bottom.  Pour beef and sauce evenly over the
batter being careful not to puncture the batter.

Bake for 25 minutes, put american cheese over beef point to point
or make your own design.  Bake until cheese melts about 5 minutes or
so. Submitted By LOU RAMSAY   On   02-12-95
