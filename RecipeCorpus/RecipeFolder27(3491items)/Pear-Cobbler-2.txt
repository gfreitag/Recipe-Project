Title: Pear Cobbler 2
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Raisins
Cinnamon
3 c  Pears, diced and mixed with
-sugar to taste
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Bisquick
1 c  Milk
Melt butter in baking dish.  Mix sugar, bisquick, milk, nuts and
raisins together.  Spread in pan with butter.  Evenly spread pears
over batter. Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Bake at 400 degrees for 40
minutes.
