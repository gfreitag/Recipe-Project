Title: Frozen Bananas
Categories: Kids, Snack
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Oil
3/4 c  Peanuts or walnuts; chopped
3/4 c  Coconut
3    Bananas
6    Popsicle sticks, wooden
1 c  Orange or lemon juice
1 c  Chocolate chips
Cut bananas in half and insert popsicle stick lengthwise through
center. Roll in orange or lemon juice and place on waxed paper. Place
in freezer until frozen firm. Melt chocolate chips and oil on top of
double boiler. Spoon chocolate over frozen bananas and then roll in
nuts and coconut. Work fast as the chocolate dries fast.
