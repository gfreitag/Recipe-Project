Title: Stroganoff For Six
Categories: Main dish, Beef
Yield: 6 servings
1 T  Lemon juice
1/8 t  Ground nutmeg
1 c  Low fat yogurt
Salt and pepper to taste
4 T  Butter
2 T  Onions, minced
2 lb Steak-Umm steak sandwich
-slices
1/2 lb Mushroom, thinly sliced
1.  Prepare beef and vegetables. 2. Heat 2 tbsp butter in skillet.
Saute onion until transparent, set aside. Saute beef strips until
brown, set aside. 3. Add remaining 2 tbsp butter to pan. Saute
mushrooms - 3 minutes. Seasoning with salt and pepper to taste and
nutmeg. 4. Whisk yogurt with lemon juice and add to the mushrooms.
Heat, do not boil. Return beef strips and onions to the pan.  Heat
thorough and serve over noodles or rice. Source: Philly-Gourment
