Title: Zucchini Bread - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Bread
Yield: 24 servings
1 c  Brown sugar**
1 c  Liquid Butter Buds or
-Promise Ultra fatfree
-margarine
2 t  Vanilla
2 c  Grated zucchini or zucchini
-and carrots, mixed
3 c  Flour
1 ea Tb cinnamon
1 t  Lite salt, optional
1 t  Baking soda
1 t  Baking powder
3/4 c  Egg Beaters, beaten
1 c  Sugar*
*Or 1/2 cup sugar and 6 packets Sweet 'n Low **Or 1/2 cup brown sugar
and 2 teaspoons S&L Brown Preheat oven to 350. Spray two loaf pans
with Pam. Sift flour, cinnamon, salt, baking soda and baking powder;
set aside. Mix Egg Beaters, sugars, liquid Butter buds and vanilla.
Add sifted dry ingredients and blend in grated zucchini. Pour into
loaf pans and bake 60-70 minutes. Per sugar serving: 137 cal., 0.4g
fat (3%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 3g pro., 31g carb., 137mg sod. Per S&L
serving: 107 cal., 23g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN
