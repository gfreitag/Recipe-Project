Title: Gardem Scramble - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Breakfast
Yield: 2 servings
1 ea Tb fatfree chicken broth
1 c  Grated nonfat Cheddar cheese
2 ea Sl white or whole wheat
-bread, toasted
Nonfat cooking spray
1 ea Tb chopped chives
1 c  Egg Beaters
1/8 t  Black pepper
1/8 t  Marjoram leaves
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
1/4 c  Finely chopped red pepper
1/4 c  Finely chopped green pepper
In a bowl, combine Egg Beaters, pepper and marjoram; set aside. In a
nonstick skillet sprayed with nonstick spray, over medium heat, cook
celery, red and green peppers and chives in chicken broth until
tender. Add Egg Beaters and grated cheese to mixture. Cook, stirring
occasionally, until set. Serve immediately over toast halves if
desired or plain. Per serving: 181 cal., 0g fat (0%), 20mg chol., 4g
fiber, 30g pro., 12g carb., 774mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie
