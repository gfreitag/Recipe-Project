Title: Broiled Salmon with Spicy Maple Basting Sauce
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2    Garlic clove(s), minced
1 ts Dried hot red pepper flakes
-or to taste
1/4 ts Salt
4    1" thick salmon fillets
-(6 oz ea)
6 tb Maple syrup (Grade B) or
Grade A maple syrup
-flavored with
3    Drops maple extract
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Ginger; peeled and minced
In small heavy saucepan combine maple syrup, water, ginger, garlic,
red pepper flakes, and salt and simmer until reduced to about Â« cup.
Cool basting sauce.

Preheat broiler.

Arrange salmon, skin side down, on oiled rack of broiler pan and
season with salt. Brush with sauce and broil until just cooked
through, about 6 minutes more.
