Title: California Bisque
Categories: Soup/stew, Tomato, Microwave
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Condensed Tomato Bisque
2 t  Lemon juice
Ingredients
1 T  Butter or margarine
1/2 t  Chili powder
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 ea Soup can water
1/2 c  Chopped avocado
1/4 c  Thinly sliced celery
Generous dash ground
-cinnamon
In 1-1/2 qt. microwave-safe casserole, combine butter, celery, chili
powder and cinnamo n.  Cover with lid; microwave on HIGH 3 min. or
until celery is tender, stirring once during cooking. Stir in soup,
water, and lemon juice. Cover; microwave on HIGH 3 min. or until hot
and bubbling. Stir in avocado. Serves 3
