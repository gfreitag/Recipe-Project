Title: Open Face Hamburgers
Categories: Beef, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
pn Salt; to taste
pn Pepper; to taste
1 1/2 lb Beef,GROUND ROUND
8    Buns
1 lg Onion, spanish; sliced
Do not butter buns.  Toast buns under broiler - watch carefully.
Remove buns from oven when nicely toasted and let cool. Thinly spread
raw meat over entire top of bun Be sure to cover edges of bun. Place
under broiler. Watch carefully. Cook to desired doneness.

Since this is using the oven, plan stove top extras.  Good with potato
salad, pasta salad, etc.
