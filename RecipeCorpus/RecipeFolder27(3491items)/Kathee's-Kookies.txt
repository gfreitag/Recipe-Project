Title: Kathee's Kookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 48 squares
1 1/2 c  Flaked coconut
5 1/2 oz Evaporated milk
1 c  C and H Brown Sugar
-- firmly packed
1/2 c  Butter or margarine; melted
1 3/4 c  Graham cracker crumbs
-- (11 double crackers)
1 c  Coarsely chopped walnuts
6 oz Chocolate pieces
Pour melted butter in bottom of 13 x 9-inch pan.  Sprinkle crumbs
evenly over melted butter.  Sprinkle walnuts over crumbs, chocolate
pieces over walnuts and coconut over chocolate pieces.  Combine
evaporated milk with brown sugar.  Stir until smooth.  Pour evenly
over layers.  Bake in 350 degree oven 25 minutes.  Cool one hour. Cut
into 48 squares (1-1/2 x 1-1/2 inches).
