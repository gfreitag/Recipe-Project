Title: Superbowl Chili
Categories: Soup/stew, Low-cal/fat, Chili
Yield: 4 servings
2 cn Fat free black beans, drain
2 tb Chili powder
1 tb Cumin
1/4 c  Water
1/8 c  Flour
8 oz Tomato sauce
1/4 c  Jalapeno peppers, chopped
1 c  Chopped onions
1/2 c  Chopped bell pepper
1/4 c  Chopped celery
2 cl Garlic, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
Combine jalapeno peppers, onion, celery, bell pepper, garlic, salt and
pepper in saucepan and simmer for 45 minutes. Add remaining
ingredients and simmer 15 minutes until thick.
