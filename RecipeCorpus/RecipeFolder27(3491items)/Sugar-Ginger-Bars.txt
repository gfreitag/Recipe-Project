Title: Sugar-Ginger Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 64 1x2" bars
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
2 ts Ginger
2 tb Buttermilk
1 1/4 c  C and H Granulated Sugar
-- (divided)
1 c  Butter or margarine
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
Cream together 1 cup sugar and butter.  Add eggs one at a time,
beating well after each addition.  Combine flour, soda, salt and
ginger.  Stir in dry ingredients and then buttermilk.  Spread in
greased 15 x 10-inch pan. Sprinkle batter with remaining 1/4 cup
sugar.  Bake in 400 degree oven 20 minutes.  Cool and cut into 64 bar
cookies (1 x 2 inches).
