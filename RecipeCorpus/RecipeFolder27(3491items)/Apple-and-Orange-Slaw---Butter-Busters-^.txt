Title: Apple and Orange Slaw - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Kraft fatfree mayonnaise or
-Miracle Whip
1 ea Tb brown sugar
**OR**
1/4 t  Sweet 'n Low Brown
1 1/2 c  Finely shredded cabbage
1 cn (10 1/4oz) mandarin
-oranges, drained
1 md Red delicious apple, cored
-and diced
Place the first three ingredients in a bowl tossing to combine.
Combine mayonnaise and brown sugar. Add this mixturer to cabbage
mixture and coat well. Cover and chil one hour. Per sugar serving: 90
cal., 0.2g fat (2%), 0mg chol., 2g fiber, 1g pro., 23g carb., 289mg
sod. Per S&L serving: 80 cal., 20g carb. Butter Busters by Pam
