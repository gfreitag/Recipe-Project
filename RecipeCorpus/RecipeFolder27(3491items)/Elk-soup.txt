Title: Elk soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Game
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 1 SERVINGS--------------------------
-several pieces.
2 qt Water
3/4 c  Diced celery
4 t  Salt
1/3 c  Rice
3 lb Knuckle bone and meat.
1 lg Onion,quartered
1 c  Diced carrots
1/4 t  Pepper
2 t  Parsley
Saw or crack bone in
Wipe pieces clean with damp cloth. Put into soup kettle and add
water, Heat to boil,reduce heat and simmer gently for 2 to 21/2
hours, Remove bone, cut off meat and dice,Return meat and marrow to
soup. Add remaining ingredients and simmer 20 to 30 minutes untill
rice and vegetables are done. Once in a while i will add potatoes if
the kid's don't want rice. Origin: Anna Heppner
