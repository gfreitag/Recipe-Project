Title: Bread Stuffing for Turkey - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 12 ----------
-fatfree Bread)
1 1/2 t  Lite salt, optional
1 1/2 t  Crushed sage leaves
1 t  Thyme leaves
1/2 t  Pepper
2 ea Tb liquid Butter Buds or
-fatfree chicken broth
3/4 c  Minced onion
1 1/2 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Liquid Butter buds
9 c  Soft bread cubes (Wonder
In large skillet in 2 tablespoons Butter Buds or chicken broth, cook
and stir onion and celery until onion is tender. Stir in about 1/3 of
the bread cubes. Turn into a deep bowl. Add remaining ingredients and
toss. Bake in the turkey or a casserole dish that has been covered.
If you bake in a casserole, bake at 325F. for 30-40 minutes.
Variations: Apple Raisin Stuffing: Decrease bread cubes t 7 cups and
increase lite salt to 1 teaspoon. Add 3 cups of finely chopped apples
and 3/4 cup raisins with the remaining ingredients. Corn Bread
Stuffing: Omit soft bread cubes and substitute corn bread cubes.
Oyster Stuffing: Decrease bread cubes to 8 cups and add 2 8oz cans
oysters, drained and chopped, with the remaining ingredients. Per
serving: 55 cal., 0.1g fat (2%), omg chol., 3g fiber, 2g pro., 10g
carb., 269mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5
