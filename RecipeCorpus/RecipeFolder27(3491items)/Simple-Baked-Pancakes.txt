Title: Simple Baked Pancakes
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Mild olive oil
Pinch ground nutmeg
Posted by Stu Hopper
----------------------------
2 ea Large eggs
1/2 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  Whisk together eggs and milk.  Beat in
flour, salt and nutmeg.  Heat oil in oven for 5 minutes in 9" pie
plate. When oil is hot, pour in batter.  Bake uncovered for 18-20
minutes until edges are puffed and crispy, and center is golden
brown. DO NOT OPEN OVEN DOOR DURING BAKING TIME.
