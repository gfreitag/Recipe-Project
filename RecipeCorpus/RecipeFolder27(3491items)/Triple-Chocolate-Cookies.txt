Title: Triple Chocolate Cookies
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 48 servings
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
6 oz White chocolate, imported
-- break in 1/2" chunks
1 1/2 c  Oats, quick-cooking
2 1/2    Sticks butter; softened
2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 tb Vanilla extract
ds Salt
3/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:30 1. Preheat oven to 350 F. In a food processor,
grind oats to a fine powder; set aside. 2. In a large bowl, beat
butter and sugar with an electric mixer on medium until light and
fluffy, 2 to 3 mins. Beat in eggs, vanilla, and salt until well
blended. Add cocoa, flour, ground oatmeal, baking soda, and baking
powder and beat until well blended, 2 to 3 mins. Mix in chocolate
chips and white chocolate pieces. (suggest by hand). Batter will be
stiff. 3. With a 1 1/4-inch ice cream scoop or with heaping
tablespoonfuls, place batter 3 inches apart on lightly greased cookie
sheets. With fingertips, press tops down slightly. Bake 10 to 11
mins, or until set but still soft. Let cookies cool on pans 3 mins,
then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
