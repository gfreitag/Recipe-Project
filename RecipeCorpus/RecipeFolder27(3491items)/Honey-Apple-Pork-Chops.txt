Title: Honey Apple Pork Chops
Categories: Pork, Fruit, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Honey
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
1/4 t  Ground pepper
Posted By Stu Hopper
----------------------------
4 ea Iowa Pork Chops (1" thick)
1 1/2 c  Apple cider
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Combine all ingredients, mix well and pour over chops arranged in
shallow dish.  Cover and refrigerate overnight, turning meat
occasionally. Remove chops and place on grill (bone side on grill:
chops are not laying flat on the grill) about 6" above medium coals).
Grill for 40 to 50 minutes, basting every 10-15 minutes.

Enjoy!
