Title: Juniper BBQ Spice
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------AMERICA-------------------------------
3    Cloves
3    Bay leaves
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Juniper berries
1 ts Black peppercorns
6    Allspice berries
Grind the spices, finely crumble the bay leaves, and combine
everything.

An aromatic blend that goes well with dense-textured fish such as
monkfish, tuna and swordfish, and with steaks and lamb.

The Complete Book of Spices by Jill Norman ISBN 0-140-23804-2 pg 106
