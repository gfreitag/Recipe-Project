Title: Hot Corned Beef Sandwiches
Categories: Main dish, Sandwich, Sthrn/livng, Beef
Yield: 10 sandwiches
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
Seasoned pepper
10    Hamburger buns
12 oz Corned beef
1 c  Celery; finely chopped
3 tb Hot dog relish
2 tb Pimiento; chopped
Chop corned beef; stir in celery, relish, pimiento, and mayonnaise.
Add seasoned pepper to taste. Fill buns with mixture and put on
baking sheet (place buns filled side up).  Cover with foil; heat at
300 degrees about 35 minutes.
