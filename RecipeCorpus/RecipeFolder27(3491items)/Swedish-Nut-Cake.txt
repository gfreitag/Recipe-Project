Title: Swedish Nut Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Raisins
8 oz Cream cheese
1    Stick of butter
1    Box of powdered sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Nuts
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Flour
2    Eggs
2 ts Baking soda
20 oz Can of crushed pineapple
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Nuts
Hand mix sugar, flour, eggs, baking soda, pineapple, 1 ts vanilla,
nuts and raisins.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes in a 9 x 13
inch pan.

Icing: Let cheese and butter soften, then mix in with remaining
ingredients. Randy Rigg
