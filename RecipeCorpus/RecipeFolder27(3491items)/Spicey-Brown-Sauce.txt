Title: Spicey Brown Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings  Prepare Brown Sauce as directed except stir in 2 tbs chopped onion, 1
tbs snipped parsley, 1 tbs vinegar, 1/4 ts dried tarragon leaves,
crushed, and 1/4 ts dried thyme leaves, crushed, with broth. Good
with chicken or pork.

Note: Acquired from Jean Allen (GRDG72B) Prodigy December 29, 1991.
