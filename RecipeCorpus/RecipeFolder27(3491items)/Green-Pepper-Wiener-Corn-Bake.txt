Title: Green Pepper Wiener Corn Bake
Categories: Casserole, Vegetable, Sausage
Yield: 4 servings
1    Green pepper, chopped
1 pk (10 oz.) weiners
2 cn Creamed corn
Slice weiners thinly and saute along with green pepper till slightly
browned. Combine with corn and heat thoroughly and serve.
