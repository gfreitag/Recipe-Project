Title: Pumpkin Bran Muffins - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Bread
Yield: 12 ----------
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
1 c  Canned pumpkin
1 c  Raisin Bran or Shredded
-Bran Flake cereal
3/4 c  Skim milk
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar*
2 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Baking soda
1/2 t  Lite salt
1 t  Cinnamon
*Or 1/4 cup sugar and 3 packets Sweet 'n Low. Spray 12 muffin cups
with nonstick spray. In a medium bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking
powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon. In a large bowl, using a wire
whisk, beat Egg Beaters. Stir in pumpkin, cereal, milk and corn
syrip. Add to flour mixture; stir until well blended. Spoon into
prepared muffin cups. Bake at 400F. 18-20 minutes. Cool in pan 5
minutes. Per sugar serving: 153 cal., 0.7g fat (4%), 0mg chol., 2g
fiber, 4g pro., 38g carb., 168mg sod. Per S&L serving: 137 cal., 34g
carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by
