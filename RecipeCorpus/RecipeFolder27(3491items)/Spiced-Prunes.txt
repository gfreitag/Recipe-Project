Title: Spiced Prunes
Categories: Fruit, Condiment
Yield: 1 batch
20    Fluid ounces wine vinegar
8 oz Brown sugar
Several cracked prune stones
-per jar (optional)
1 tb Brandy or vodka per jar
2 lb Large prunes
Scrape of nutmeg or a blade
-of mace
Splinter of stick cinnamon
1 ts Ground black pepper
Soak the prunes overnight in cold water.  Drain them well and pack
them firmly into jars.  Boil the vinegar with the spices, sugar and
cracked stones and pour over the prunes.  Make sure the fruit is
completely covered.  Add a tablespoon of brandy or vodka to each jar,
screw the lids on and keep for several months before opening.

Note:  The authors say that this is delicious served with chilli as
well as cold meat or game.
