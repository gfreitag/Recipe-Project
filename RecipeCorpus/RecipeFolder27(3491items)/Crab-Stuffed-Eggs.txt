Title: Crab-Stuffed Eggs
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Sthrn/livng, Seafood
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Green pepper; finely chopped
1 tb French salad dressing mix
1/3 c  Sour cream
12    Eggs, hard-cooked
8 oz Crabmeat; drained and flaked
1 c  Celery; finely chopped
Slice eggs in half lengthwise; remove yolks and mash.  Combine yolks,
crabmeat, celery, green pepper, salad dressing mix and sour cream;
blend well.  Stir until well mixed.  Stuff egg whites with crabmeat
mixture. Chill untl serving time.
