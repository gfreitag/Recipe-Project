Title: EASY TO MAKE BROWNIES
Categories: Dessert, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
2/3 c  Sifted flour
2/3 c  Cut up nuts
2    Sq melted chocolate
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
Cream butter, add sugar, eggs, vanilla. Then flour, chocolate and
nuts. Line 9 x 12 pan with waxed paper. Bake 25 min at 325 degrees.
These will be soft and chewy.
