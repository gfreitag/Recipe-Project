Title: Basic BBQ Spice Mix
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------AMERICA-------------------------------
2 ts Paprika
1 tb Mustard powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Black peppercorns
1 ts Celery seeds
1/2 ts Cayenne
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Dried marjoram
Crush the peppercorns and celery seeds in a mortar and mix all the
ingredients together. Medium-hot blend. Rub on meat before grilling.
