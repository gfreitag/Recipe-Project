Title: Lowfat Refried Beans - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 6 ----------
2 ea Tb chile powder
2 t  Ground cumin
1/2 t  Lite salt, optional
1/8 t  Ground pepper
1/2 c  Salsa
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Liquid Butter Buds or
-fatfree chicken broth
2 c  Canned pinto beans, rinsed
-and drained, or fresh
-cooked
Heat liquid Butter Buds in a 10" skillet over medium heat until hot.
Add chopped onion and cook until soft. Add pinto beans; cook, stirring
occasionallly, 5 minutes. Mash beans; stir in chile powder, salsa,
cumin, salt and pepper. Add more Butter Buds or chicken broth to
skillet if necessary. Cook and stir until a smooth paste forms, about
5 minutes. Garnish with nonfat grated cheddar cheese melted on top.
Per serving: 86 cal., 0.8g fat (8%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 4g pro.,
