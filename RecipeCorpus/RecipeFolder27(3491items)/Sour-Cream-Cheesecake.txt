Title: Sour Cream Cheesecake
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
Microwave 1-1/2 minutes
1 1/4 c  SUGAR
1 tb VANILLA
1/4 ts ALMOND EXTRACT
5    JUMBO EGGS, unshelled and
-warmed in microwave
25    Seconds.
4 c  SOUR CREAM
1/4 ts SALT
2 c  GRAHAM CRACKER CRUMBS
1/4 c  NUTS, finely chopped
1 ts CINNAMON
6 tb BUTTER, melted
Mix well and pat into bottom
-and sides of 10" springform
Pan.
CAKE
24 oz CREAM CHEESE, unwrapped and
-softened in
** CRUST
