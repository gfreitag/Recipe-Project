Title: Cinnamon Candy Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  ;boiling water
1 c  Apples; chopped
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
1 pk Gelatin, cherry; 3-oz pkg
1/4 c  Cinnamon candies
1 c  ;boiling water
1 c  Celery; chopped
Dissolve gelatin in 1 cup boiling water. Add cinnamon candies to the
1/2 cup boiling water and stir to dissolve. Add enough water to make
1 cup liquid and stir into dissolved gelatin. Cool till partially set
and add remaining ingredients. Pour into mold or flat dish and
refrigerate until set.

per serving: 210Cal, 11gFat, 29gCarb, 37mgNa, 0mgChol approximate
cost: $1.75
