Title: Broccoli Corn Bread
Categories: Bread, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine,
-melted, divided
2 pk Corn bread/muffin mix (8
-1/2 oz. ea.)
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Cottage cheese
1 pk Frozen chopped broccoli,
-thawed (10 oz.)
3/4 c  Chopped onion
In a mixing bowl. lightly beat eggs. Add cottage cheese, broccoli,
onion, 6 tablespoons butter and muffin mix; beat well. pour remaining
butter into a 10-inch ovenproof skillet; pour batter into skillet.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes or until golden. Cut into
wedges and serve warm. Serves 12-16. SOURCE:* Taste of Home Magazine,
Feb./Mar. 1995 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 2/95
