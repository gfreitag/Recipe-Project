Title: Banana Walnut Snack Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Snack
Yield: 9 servings
1 ea EGG
1/2 c  MASHED RIPE BANANAS
1/2 c  CHOPPED WALNUTS
1 pk SNACK CAKE MIX  (2 1/4 CUPS)
1/3 c  OIL
1/2 c  BUTTERMILK, MILK OR WATER
PREHEAT OVEN TO 350.  POUR SNACK CAKE MIX INTO AN UNGREASED 9" SQUARE
PAN. IN SMALL BOWL, COMBINE REMAINING INGREDIENTS, BEATING WITH A
FORK TO BLEND. STIR INTO SNACK CAKE MIX UNTIL BLENDED. BAKE 30 TO 35
MINUTES UNTIL DONE. COOL.
