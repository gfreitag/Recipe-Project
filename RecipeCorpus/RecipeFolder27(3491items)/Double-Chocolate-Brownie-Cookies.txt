Title: Double Chocolate Brownie Cookies
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 36 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Flour
1/4 ts Baking powder
1 c  Pecans; chopped
2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
4 tb Butter
1/3 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
-- firmly packed
2    Eggs
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:27 1. Preheat oven to 350 F. In a 2-qt glass
bowl, combine 1 cup chocolate chips and butter. Heat in a microwave
on High 1 to 1 1/2 mins, until chocolate is melted and smooth when
stirred. 2. Stir in cocoa until blended. With a fork, beat in eggs,
vanilla, and sugar until well mixed. Stir in flour and baking powder
until well blended. Stir in pecans and remaining 1 cup chocolate
chips. 3. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart onto
foil-lined cookie sheets. Bake 10 to 12 mins, or until tops are
cracked slightly. Let cookies cool on pans 5 mins, then remove to
wire racks to let cool completely.
