Title: Fudge Frosting
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Heavy cream
4 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
4 oz Stick butter or margarine
-- softened
6 oz Bittersweet chocolate
-- melted
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:10

1. In a medium bowl, beat together butter and melted chocolate with an
electric mixer on medium speed until combined, 1 min. Beat in cream.

2. Add powdered sugar and vanilla and beat on high until frosting is
smooth and fluffy.
