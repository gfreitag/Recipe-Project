Title: Kahlua Fudge Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Cold water
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Kahlua or coffee liqueur
Confectioner icing, optional
1    18 1/2 oz pkg chocolate cake
-ix
1    3 3/4 oz pkg chocolate insta
-t pudding and pie fil
4    Eggs, at room temperature
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients
and mix well. Pour into greased 12 cup Bundt or tube pan. Bake 50 to
60 minutes. Cool. Invert onto a serving plate. Drizzle icing over top
and sides, if desired.
