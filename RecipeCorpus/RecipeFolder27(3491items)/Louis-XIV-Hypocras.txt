Title: Louis XIV Hypocras
Categories: Beverage, Ancient
Yield: 1 batch
1/4 ts Mace
12    Cloves
1/2 ts Almond extract
1    Russet apple; sliced
1 qt Good claret
1/2 c  Warm honey
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1/4 ts Ginger
Blend honey into claret until it doesn't settle to the bottom.  Add
all other ingredients and let stand overnight.  Strain twice through
4 layers of cheesecloth.  Bottle and cork lightly.
