Title: Punch Bowl Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
2 qt Strawberries, sliced
1 lg Size of Cool Whip
Filling:
2    Sticks of butter
2    Eggs
2 c  Powdered sugar
1    Box of yellow cake mix,
Prepared and baked
1 lg Can of crushed pineapple,
Drained
4    Bananas, sliced
1 lg Box instant vanilla pudding
For filling, combine 3 ingredients and beat on high for 15 minutes.
Alternate layers of cake, filling, strawberries, pineapple, and
bananas. Sprinkle pudding over bananas.  Top with Cool Whip,
strawberries, and nuts. Prepare in a punch bowl. Randy Rigg
