Title: Potatoes Mary Margaret
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
Paprika
2    Cloves garlic, minced
5    Large red potatoes
Salt and pepper, to taste
2 1/2 c  Whipping cream
2 tb Butter
Peel and slice potatoes very thin. Layer in a small buttered
casserole; potatoes, cheese, salt and pepper. Simmer cream, butter
and garlic until reduced by half. Pour over potatoes, sprinkle with
paprika. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 1 1/2 hours. Enjoy, Sheryl BTW
M.M. said you may need to add more cream.
