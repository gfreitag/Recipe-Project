Title: Baked Mustard Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Water
2 tb Oil, salad
1 ts Thyme, dried leaf
1/4 ts Ginger, ground
2    Chickens, broiler-fryer;
-quartered
2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Mustard, prepared
2 tb Vinegar
Sprinkle salt on both sides of chicken.  Place skin side up in
foil-lined shallow baking pan.  Combine other ingredients and spoon
over chicken. Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees for 50 to 60 minutes,
basting occasionally.
