Title: Brazilian Milk Shake
Categories: Beverage, Fruit, Ice cream
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Dutch processed cocoa;
2 tb Sugar;
1 c  Milk;
1/2    Avocado;
Vanilla ice cream; equal
-amount
Place all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth.  Add up 1/2
cup more milk, if desired, for a thinner shake.  Pour into glasses
and serve. Makes 2 servings. Nutrition value per serving: 257 cal;
6.6g pro; 26.4g carb; 13.5g fat; 26.4gm chol; 85mg sod; Source: Peter
De Bean, Escondido, CA. The Friendly Exchange, Spring 1995 Brought to
you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
