Title: Sweet & Spicy Cookies
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 cookies
1/2 t  Ginger
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Allspice
Sugar
2 c  Basic cookie mix
4 T  Molasses
1/2 t  Vanilla
1    Egg, beaten
Preheat oven to 375.  Lightly grease baking sheets. In medium bowl,
combine cookie mix, molasses, vanilla, egg, ginger, cinnamon and
allspice. Stir until blended. Drop by tsp 2" apart on prepared baking
sheets. Flatten with the bottom of a glass dipped in sugar. Bake 8 to
10 minutes. Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   02-10-95
