Title: Creamy Turkey Vegetable Soup ^
Categories: Soup/stew, Turkey, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Diced potatoes
1 c  Diced carrots
2 1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Flour
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 sm Onion, chopped
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 c  Water
2    Chicken bouillon cubes
2 c  Diced cooked turkey
1/2 c  Chopped celery
Saute onion in butter until clear. Add water, bouillon, turkey and
vegetables. Cover and boil gently until vegetables are tender. Add a
small amount of milk to the flour, stir until smooth. Add remaining
milk, salt and pepper. Stir into soup. Simmer, stirring occasionally,
until soup thickens slightly. Serve.
