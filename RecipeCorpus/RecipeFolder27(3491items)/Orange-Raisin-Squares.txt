Title: Orange Raisin Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Raisins
1 ea Orange; small - rind of
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Sour cream
1/4 ts Salt
Add soda to cream. Grate orange peel. Pour hot water over raisins,
then grind. Bake in large flat pan. Roll in powdered sugar or use
orange icing.

Note: No temperature give. Assume a slow 325 F. oven.

Source: Mrs. W. D. Wareham, Union Grange, Richland County, OH
