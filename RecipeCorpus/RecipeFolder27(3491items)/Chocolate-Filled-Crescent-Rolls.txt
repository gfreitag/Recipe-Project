Title: Chocolate-Filled Crescent Rolls
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1/3 c  Walnuts; chopped
1 pk (8 oz) quick refrigerated
Crescent dinner rolls
1 ts (to 2) milk
2 tb Butter
3 tb Brown sugar
2 ts Orange zest
-- grated
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:28 1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. In a small glass
bowl, melt butter in a microwave on High 20 to 30 secs. Stir in brown
sugar, orange zest, and cinnamon. Let cool; then mix in chocolate
chips and walnuts. 2. Unroll dough and separate into 8 triangles.
Spread about 1 heaping Tbs chocolate mixture over each triangle. Roll
up from wide end (opposite the point), stretching dough a little as
you roll. Place rolls, point side down, on an ungreased baking sheet.
Curve into crescent shapes. Brush tops with a little milk. 3. Bake 10
to 12 mins, or until golden brown. Wait 2 to 3 mins, then transfer to
a rack to cool. Serve warm or at room temperature.
