Title: Shrimp Salad - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 6 servings
*OR**
1 t  Capers
2 ea Tb Kraft fatfree mayonnaise
-or Miracle Whip
1/2 t  Worcestershire sauce
1 l  Salt and pepper to taste
1 lb Cooked peeled shrimp
1 cl Garlic, minced
1 c  Chopped celery
2 ea Hard cooked egg whites,
-chopped
1 ea Sweet pickle, finely chopped
Cut each shrimp into two or three pieces. Combine all ingredients.
Serve on lettuce or as a stuffing for tomatoes. Per serving: 88 cal.,
0.8g fat (8%), 147mg chol., 0.3g fiber 17g pro., 2g carb., 294mg sod.
