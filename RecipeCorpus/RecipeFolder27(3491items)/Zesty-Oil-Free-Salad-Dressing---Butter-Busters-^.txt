Title: Zesty Oil Free Salad Dressing - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 6 servings
1 t  Dijon mustard
1/4 t  Lite salt, optional
1/4 t  Pepper
2 t  Finely chopped parsley
1/2 c  Evaporated skim milk
2 ea Tb cider or wine vinegar
1 ea Tb lemon juice
1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1 t  Chopped onion
Combine all ingredients in a blender. Process until thoroughly mixed.
Serve on your favorite salad ingredients. Per 1/4 cup serving: 26
cal., 0.1g fat (3%), 0mg chol., 0.4g fiber, 2g pro., 5g carb., 173mg
