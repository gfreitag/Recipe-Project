Title: Vanilla Banana Bread
Categories: Bread, Fruit, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
2    Eggs
10 tb Butter
1 c  Bananas
1/2 c  Nuts
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 c  Flour
1/4 c  Brown sugar
2 ts Baking powder
3 ts Pure vanilla
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Sugar
Combine dry ingredients.  Combine 2 Tbsp. butter, nuts, brown sugar,
and 1 tsp vanilla.  Set aside. Cream butter and sugar. Combine eggs,
bananas, cream, and remaining vanilla. Blend in dry ingredients.
Measure out 1/4 cup nut mix.  Place rest in greased pan, cover with
batter, top with reserved nut mix..  Bake at 350o for 50 min. Cool 10
min.
