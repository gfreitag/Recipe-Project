Title: Tuna with Oriental Sauce
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 t  Sugar
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
1 c  Snow peas, trimmed
1/8 c  Hoisin sauce
1 t  Sesame oil
4 ea Tuna steaks or fillets
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
To make sauce, combine first four ingredients in a small saucepan and
simmer over low heat for a couple of minutes. To barbecue tuna, brush
steaks with sauce and place on greased grill over hot coals. Cook
tuna 4 to 5 minutes, brush steaks with sauce and turn. Continue
grilling 2 to 3 minutes or until fish separates easily when tested
with a fork. Tuna is done, as is roasted beef, when it's slightly
pink in the centre. Place on individual plates, with snow peas for
garnish.
