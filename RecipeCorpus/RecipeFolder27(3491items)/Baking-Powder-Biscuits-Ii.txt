Title: Baking Powder Biscuits Ii
Categories: Bread
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Milk
3 c  Ready-mixed flour
7 tb Shortening
Sift flour, measure, and sift.  Cut in shortening with 2 spatulas. Add
milk.  Turn onto lightly floured board.  Knead lightly and pat into
sheet 1/2 inch thick.  Cut with floured cutter. Bake in hot oven
