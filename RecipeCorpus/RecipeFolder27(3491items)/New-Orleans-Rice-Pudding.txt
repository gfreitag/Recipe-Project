Title: New Orleans Rice Pudding
Categories: Cajun/creol, Dessert, Rice dishes, Pudding
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Raisins
5    Eggs (beaten)
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Uncooked rice
1 qt Milk
8 tb Butter
3/4 c  Sugar
Combine rice and milk. Bring to a boil, cover and cook over low heat
until rice is tender and has absorbed most of the milk. Add butter,
sugar, vanilla, raisins and eggs. Turn into a buttered 2-quart
casserole dish. Sprinkle with cinnamon. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes.
Serves 6-8.

From the cookbook Recipes and Reminiscences of New Orleans
