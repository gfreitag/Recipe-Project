Title: Cream Cheese Apple Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 12 servings
3 ts Vanilla
6    Apples; sliced
24 oz Cream cheese
6 tb Milk
2 1/4 c  Brown sugar
Blend all ingredients together.  Dip sliced granny smith apples in
the dip, then into crushed peanuts.
Enjoy! Makes  a great fruit dish for a party or buffet or you can
just enjoy it  at home like we do.
Hope you like it!
