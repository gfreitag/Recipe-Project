Title: Apple Corn Johnny Cake
Categories: Muffin, Pork, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea Eggs, separated
2 ea Tb Canola oil
1 3/4 c  Applesauce (14 oz can)
6 ea Bacon strips,cooked,crumbled
1 c  Corn meal
3/4 c  Rice flour
4 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
2 ea Tb Sugar
Breakfast idea without gluten or dairy.  Serve warm with maple syrup
or as a quickbread with soup or salad. In a large mixing bowl combine
cornmeal, rice flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Beat egg whites
until stiff. To the dry ingredients, add egg yolks, oil, applesauce
and bacon.  Stir well. Fold in egg whites.  Pour batter into greased
8x8 inch baking pan (or prepared muffin pan).  Bake in 425 F oven for
45 min or until an inserted knife comes out clean.  Serves 4-5.
Source: Co-op advertising recipe Sept 93 Shared but not tested by
