Title: Pork Chops with Fruit
Categories: Pork, Fruit, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Prune juice
6 ea Stewed prunes
1/4 c  Apricot juice
Salt to taste
Paprika to taste
Posted by Stu Hopper
____________________________
4 ea Iowa Pork Chops (1" thick)
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Apricots halves
Sear chops in hot skillet.  Season with salt and paprika.  Cover
chops with fruit.  Add juice and cover pan tightly.  Cook 30 minutes
over medium heat. (chops an be transferred to a casserole dish with
tight lid and placed in a 350 degree oven for 30 to 40 minutes)

Enjoy
