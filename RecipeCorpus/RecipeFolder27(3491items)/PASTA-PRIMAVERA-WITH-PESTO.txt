Title: PASTA PRIMAVERA WITH PESTO
Categories: Pasta, Salsa/sauce, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/3 c  Pine nuts or walnuts (or
-any combination)
4 ea Cloves garlic
1/3 c  Extra virgin olive oil
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese, freshly
-grated
3-4 cups of broccoli
-flowerets and carrots
-sliced at an angle
12 oz Pasta (rotini-red, green
-and white)
Approx 2/3 cup fresh basil
-leaves
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Add carrots and pasta to boiling water. Add broccoli after 5 minutes.
While pasta/vegetables are cooking, add basil, nuts and garlic to
food processor and pulse until finely chopped. With the machine
running, pour in the olive oil in a thin stream. Add the cheese, salt
and pepper to taste and process briefly to combine. Thin with 2 Tbs
HOT water. This will make it easier to toss. Toss pesto with hot
pasta and vegetables.
