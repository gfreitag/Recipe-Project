Title: Christmas Hard Candy
Categories: Candy, Holiday
Yield: 2 lbs
(to 1/2 tsp)(Available in
-cake decorating and candy
-supply stores)
1 ts Red or green food coloring
3 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Light brown syrup
1 c  Water
1/4 ts Cinnamon or peppermint oil,
In a large heavy saucepan, combine sugar, corn syrup and water.  Cook
on medium-high heat until candy thermometer reads 300 degrees (hard
crack stage), stirring occasionally.  Remove from the heat; stir in
oi and food coloring, keeping face away from mixture as order is very
strong. immediately pour onto an oiled cookie sheet.  Cool; break
into pieces. Store in airtight containers.  Yield: about 2 lbs.
