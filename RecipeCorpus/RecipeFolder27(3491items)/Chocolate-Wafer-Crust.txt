Title: Chocolate Wafer Crust
Categories: Chocolate, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Sugar
4 tb Butter; melted
1 1/2 c  Chocolate wafer cookies
Crumbs
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:18
