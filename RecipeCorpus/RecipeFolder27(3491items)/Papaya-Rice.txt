Title: Papaya Rice
Categories: Rice dishes, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
-or mango juice
1 1/4 c  Long-grain white rice
1/2 ts Salt
1    Papaya; peeled
-seeded, cut in 1/2" dice
-(about 1 3/4 cups)
2 ts Vegetable oil
1 tb Ginger; finely chopped
1 sm Garlic clove(s)
-finely chopped
1/2 ts Ground cardamon
1 c  Papaya nectar
In a heavy saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Add ginger and garlic;
cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Stir in cardamon. Combine papaya nectar
with 1Â« cups water and add to pot along with rice and salt; bring to
a rolling boil, stirring occasionally. Add diced papaya, bring to a
simmer, then turn heat down to lowest point.

Cover pan and cook for 20 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand,
covered, for 15 minutes. Fluff rice with a fork and serve.
