Title: Fruity Chicken Salad ^
Categories: Salad
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Mayonnaise
Lettuce Leaves
Sliced cantaloupe, peaches
-or strawberries for
-garnish
3 c  Diced cooked chicken
1 c  Halved seedless green
-grapes
1 c  Diced celery
1 1/2 c  Blanched, salted almonds
1 c  Diced green pepper
1/2 c  Sour cream
Combine all ingredients and chill for several hours. Serve on lettuce
and garnish with cantaloupe, peaches, or strawberries.
