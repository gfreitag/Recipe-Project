Title: Basic Garlic Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 /3 cup
1 ts Dried parsley
1 1/2 tb Oregano, minced or
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1 pk Butter Buds Mix, dry
1/4 c  Hot water
1 lg Garlic clove(s), minced
1 tb Parsley, minced or
In a small bowl, dissolve Butter Buds in the hot water; stir in the
remaining ingredients.

Savor the Flavor by Gail L. Becker ISBN 0-517-10026-6 pg 86
