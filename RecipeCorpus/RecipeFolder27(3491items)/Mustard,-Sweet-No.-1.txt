Title: Mustard, Sweet No. 1
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 32 servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Chopped herbs, optional
1 c  Dry mustard
1 c  White or malt vinegar
2    Eggs
Beat together the mustard and vinegar until smooth.  Refrigerate,
covered, overnight.

Place the vinegar mixture in a bowl and beat in the eggs and sugar.
Cook in a double boiler over boiling water until thickened,about 10
min. Cool before stirring in the herbs. Store in a covered jar in the
refrigerator. Submitted By JIM WELLER   On   02-20-95
