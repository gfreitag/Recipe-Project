Title: Cheese Buns
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
Oil; for deep frying
1 c  Samsoe cheese; grated
2    Egg whites
1 tb Celery salt
1 tb Paprika
WHIP EGG WHITES UNTIL STIFF.FOLD IN CHEESE AND PAPRIKA. HEAT THE
OIL.FORM BUNS WITH A TEASPOON AND FRY UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN. 5 TO 8
MINUTES. DRAIN ON PAPER TOWELS AND SPRINKLE WITH CELERY SALT. SERVE
HOT.
