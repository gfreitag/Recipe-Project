Title: Creamettes Mexi-Mac Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Chili, Casserole, Chilies
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Sliced green onions
1 1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
2 c  Tortilla chips, divided
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 md Tomato, chopped
1 tb Chopped cilantro
1 pk Creamettes Elbow mararoni
(7 oz)
1 cn Hormel No Beans Chili
(15 oz)
1/4 c  Tomato juice
1 cn Diced green chilies
(4 oz)
Prepare macaroni according to package directions; drain. In medium
saucepan heat chili, tomato juice, green chilies, and green onions.
Combine macaroni, chili mixture and 1/2 cup cheese. Crush 1 cup
tortilla chips and spread on bottom of a 3 quart baking dish. Spoon
in macaroni mixture. Top with remaining tortilla chips and cheese.
Bake in 350o oven for 30 minutes. Garnish top with sour cream, tomato
and cilantro. Refrigerate leftover.
