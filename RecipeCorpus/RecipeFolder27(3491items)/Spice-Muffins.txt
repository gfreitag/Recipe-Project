Title: Spice Muffins
Categories: Muffin
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Ginger
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
2 c  Flour
1    Egg, well beaten
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Melted shortening
Sift flour, measure, and sift with spices, baking powder, and salt.
Combine egg, sugar, shortening, and milk.  Add dry ingredients. Beat
only until smooth.  Fill well-oiled muffin tins 2/3 full. Bake in hot
oven (425Ã¸ F) 15-20 minutes.  12 servings. Mrs. Charles Colgrove,
Jersey City, NJ.
