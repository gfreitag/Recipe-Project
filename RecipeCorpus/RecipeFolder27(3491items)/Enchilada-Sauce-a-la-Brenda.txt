Title: Enchilada Sauce a la Brenda
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Garlic powder
16 oz Can of tomato sauce
2 tb Chili powder
1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Boullion
Mix the chili powder with just enough water to make a smooth paste.
Then mix in the remaining ingredients and simmer.  Easy!

This recipie came from Brenda Frandsen's church cook book which she
compiled.
