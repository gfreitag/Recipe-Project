Title: Honey-Cream Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin
Yield: 12 muffins
1/3 c  Butter, melted
1 c  Light cream
1/2 c  Honey
1 c  Chopped dates (optional)
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg, beaten
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

Sift the flour, baking powder and salt together.  Stir in the egg,
butter, cream and honey, just until moistened.  Fold in dates if
desired. Fill paper-lined muffin tins full, and bake approximately 20
minutes.
