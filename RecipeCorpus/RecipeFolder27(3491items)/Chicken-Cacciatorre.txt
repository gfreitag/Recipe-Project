Title: Chicken Cacciatorre
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Dried oregano
1/2 ts Celery seed
1    Bay leaf
1 1/4 lb Boneless skinless chicken
1/4 c  Dry white wine
2 c  Cooked noodles
2 ts Vegetable oil
1 c  Sliced onions
1 cl Garlic, minced
16 oz Can chopped tomatoes
8 oz Can tomato sauce
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Heat oil in large saucepan. Add onion and garlic and cook until
tender, about 10 minutes. Add tomatoes, sauce, and spices, stirring
to blend. Add chicken, cover and simmer 1 hour, stirring
occasionally. Add wine. Cook, uncovered, 15 minutes. Remove and
discard bay leaf before serving. Serve over noodles.

Per serving: 36g protein, 13g fat, 33g carb., 1100mg sodium, 124mg
chol., 395 calories.
