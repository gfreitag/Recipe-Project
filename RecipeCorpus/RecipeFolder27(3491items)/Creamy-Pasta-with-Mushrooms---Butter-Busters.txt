Title: Creamy Pasta with Mushrooms - Butter Busters
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 9 ----------
1/2 c  Egg Beaters, beaten
2 cn (8oz) sliced mushrooms,
-rinsed and drained
1/2 c  Alpine Lace or Weight
-Watchers fatfree Parmesan
-cheese
1 lb Fettuccine, uncooked
-(Eggless variety)
1/2 c  Evaporated skim milk
1/3 c  Fatfree mayonnaise or
-Miracle Whip
1 t  Minced garlic
Prepare fettuccine as directed on package. Drain and set aside.
Gradually add skim milk to fatfree mayonnaise and garlic in a small
saucepan. Heat thoroughly, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat;
blend in Egg Beaters. toss with fettuccine and mushrooms until well
coated. Add Parmesan cheese and mix well. Per serving: 164 cal., 0.7g
fat (4%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 7g pro., 31g carb., 264mg sod. Butter
Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw
2-95.
