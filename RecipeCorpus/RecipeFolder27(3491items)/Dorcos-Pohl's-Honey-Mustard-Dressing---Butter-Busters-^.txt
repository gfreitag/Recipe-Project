Title: Dorcos Pohl's Honey Mustard Dressing - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 10 servings
1/3 c  Apple cider vinegar
1/4 t  Cayenne pepper
1/2 c  Water
1 cl Minced garlic
1 1/4 c  Kraft fatfree mayonnaise or
-Miracle Whip
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Prepared yellow mustard
Place all ingredients in a bowl and whisk together. Per 1/4 cup
serving: 56 cal., 0.3g fat (5%), 0mg chol, 0g fiber, 0g pro., 14g
carb., 372mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-446-67040-5
