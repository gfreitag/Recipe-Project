Title: Kentucky Corn Pones
Categories: Bread
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Whipping cream
1 tb Butter
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Cornmeal
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Boiling water
Combine cornmeal and salt; add boiling water and stir well. Add
remaining ingredients.  Drop mixture by tablespoonfuls onto a greased
griddle. Bake at 450 for 15 minutes or until edges are lightly
browned.
