Title: Cajun Tuna Steaks 2
Categories: Cajun/creol, Fish, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Dill, dried
1 ts Onion powder
1 ts Black pepper
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Salt
Tuna steaks
2 tb Paprika
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1 1/2 ts Oregano, dried
1 1/2 ts Thyme, dried
Mix together all ingredients until well combined.  Dry rub approx. 1
tsp spice mixture over the surface of each tuna steak and wait about
1/2 hour to set the flavor before broiling or gas grilling. [ Save
remaining mixture in airtight container for future use.]
