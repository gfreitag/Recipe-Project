Title: Aloha Loaf
Categories: Bread, Nuts/grains, Fruit, Dessert
Yield: 1 loaf
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Orange juice
1    Orange rind, grated
1/2 c  Coconut, flaked
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Margarine
1/2 c  Sugar
Preheat oven to 350 deg f.  Shift together flour, baking powder, and
salt onto a sheet of waxed paper; set aside.  In medium mixing bowl,
cream magarine; add sugar gradually, beating well after each
addition.  Add eggs one at the time; continue beating until mixture
is light and fluffy.  Add orange juice to milk.  Add liquid
ingredients to creamed mixture alternately with dry ingredients,
beginning and ending with dry ingedients.  Fold in grated rind and
coconut.  Pour batter into greased and floured loaf pan.  Bake until
bread test done and is golden brown, about 1 hour.  Remove from pan
to cooling rack. Submitted By FLEURETTE DELISLE   On   02-06-95
