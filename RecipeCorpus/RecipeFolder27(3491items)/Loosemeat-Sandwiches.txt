Title: Loosemeat Sandwiches
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Lawry's Seasoned Salt
1/2 ts Kitchen Bouquet
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1    Garlic clove, minced
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 md Onion, chopped
1 lb Ground chuck
1/2 c  Tomato juice
In a large skillet over medium heat, saute garlic in oil until int
begins to brown. Add onion and saute until soft. Add meat, stirring
constantly with a fork to crumble as finely as possible. When meat is
brown, add all remaining ingredients and simmer, uncovered, stirring
occasionally until the liquid cooks out (about 15-20 minutes). Spoon
onto hamburger buns. (Makes 4 regular or 8 small sandwiches)
