Title: Mock Hard Cooked Eggs - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizer, Pickles
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Ct (8oz) Egg Beaters
In a nonstick 8" skillet, pour Egg Beaters. Cover tightly; cook over
low heat 10 minutes or until just set. Remove from heat; let stand,
covered, 10 minutes. Remove from skillet and cool completely. Chop
and use in your favorite egg salad recipe or sprinkle over salads or
casseroles. Makes 1 cup chopped mock eggs. Per serving: 100 cal., 0g
fat (0%), 0mg chol., 0g fiber, 20g pro., 0g carb., 320mg sod. Butter
