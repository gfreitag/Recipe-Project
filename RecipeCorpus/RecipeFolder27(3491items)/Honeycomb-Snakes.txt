Title: Honeycomb Snakes
Categories: Kids, Snack, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------PREP TIME: 10 MINUTES------------------------
Tie a knot in the end of a piece of shoestring licorice.  Thread on
pieces of honeycomb cereal alternating with dried apricots.  Finish
with an apricot, strung lengthwise.  Decorate with currants for eyes,
and split licorice end to make forked tongue. Origin: What's Cooking
Magazine, by Kraft, 8th issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, May/94.
