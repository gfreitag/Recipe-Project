Title: Zhug
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------YEMEN--------------------------------
6    Garlic clove(s)
Seeds from 6 green cardamons
1 ts To 2 ts lemon juice
2 sm Mild red chili peppers
2    To 3 red chilies
Handful cilantro leaves
1 1/2 tb Ground coriander seeds
Seed and chop the red peppers and chilies. chop the cilantro leaves.
Blend or pound all the ingredients to a paste. Use as a table
condiment. Can be kept stored in an airtight container in the
refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
