Title: Chinese Ice Cream
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Cool Whip
1 t  Vanillia
4 c  Cooked white rice
1 cn Fruit cocktail, well drained
1/8 c  White sugar
Mix all ingredients in large serving bowl.

Decorate with maraschino cherries
