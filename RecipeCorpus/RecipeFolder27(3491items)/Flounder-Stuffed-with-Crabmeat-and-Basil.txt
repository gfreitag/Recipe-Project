Title: Flounder Stuffed with Crabmeat and Basil
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
-1/8 tspn salt
1/4 lb Crabmeat
4 ea Flounder fillets
1/2 c  Basil leaves, packed
1 T  Reduced-calorie mayonnaise
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1/8 t  Paprika
1/4 c  Bread crumbs
1 sm Garlic clove
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Wash the flounder fillets and pat dry
with paper towel.  Coat with breadcrumbs and set aside. In the food
processor, combine and puree together the basil, garlic, mayonnaise,
and salt. Transfer to a bowl and stir in the crabmeat. Spoon 2
Tablespoons of basil-crabmeat filling on each fillet, then roll the
fish over the filling to create open-sided roulades. Lay the flounder
rolls ona bake-and-serve casserole, sprinkle lightly with paprika,
and bake for 8 minutes. TOT SAT UNSAT CAL PROT CARB FIBR FAT FAT FAT
