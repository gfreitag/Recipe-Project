Title: Granny's Country Chili
Categories: Meat, Vegetable, Chili
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Black pepper
2 tb Garlic salt
2 tb Chili powder
8 oz Dried blackeyed peas
1 lb Sausage
3 cn Tomatoes
2 c  Water
Cover the blackeyed peas in water and soak overnight. Drain peas.
Saute sausage meat until done. Add tomatoes, water, spices. Simmer
one hour.

Note:  This recipe appeared in the Waycross (Georgia) Journal-Herald
17th Annual Cookbook, November 16, 1990 and was submitted by Ms.
Edith King of Blackshear, Georgia.
