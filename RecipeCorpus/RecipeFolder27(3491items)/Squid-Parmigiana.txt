Title: Squid Parmigiana
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 lb Provolone or romano cheese,
-thinly sliced
1 ea Recipe Squid Steak a' la
-Anthony's Fish Grotto
1 c  Tomato sauce
After the fillets are browned, place them in a baking dish and coat
with your favorite tomato sauce. Top with slices of cheese. Broil
under high heat until the cheese melts. Serve immediately.
SOURCE:*The International Squid Cookbook, by Isaac Cronin,
