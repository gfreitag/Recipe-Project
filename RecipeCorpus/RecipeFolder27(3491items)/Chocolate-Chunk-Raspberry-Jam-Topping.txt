Title: Chocolate Chunk Raspberry Jam Topping
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
6 oz Bittersweet, semisweet or
-- white chocolate; cut
-- in 1/2" chunks
16    Red raspberry jam, seedless
1/3 c  Chambord, grand marnier,
-- or dark rum
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:10 In a small bowl, stir together jam and
Chanbord until well blended and smooth. Stir in chocolate chunks.
Store in a tightly covered container in refrigerator. Serve spooned
over ice cream, cake slices, or chocolate mousse.
