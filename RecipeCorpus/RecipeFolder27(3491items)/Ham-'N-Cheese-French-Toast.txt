Title: Ham 'N Cheese French Toast
Categories: Breakfast, Sthrn/livng, Pork, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 sandwiches
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Mustard, dry
Butter (or marg.)
4 sl Ham
4 sl Cheese
8 sl Bread, raisin (day-old)
2    Eggs; beaten
Place a slice of ham and a cheese slice between 2 slices raisin bread.
Combine eggs, milk, salt, and dry mustard in a bowl.  Heat butter on
griddle; dip each sandwich in egg mixture and cook on hot griddle
until bread is lightly browned on both sides and cheese has melted.
