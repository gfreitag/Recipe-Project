Title: Peanut Butter Swirl Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 18 bars
1 c  Flour
1 t  Baking powder
1/4 t  Salt
4 oz Peanut Butter Chips
6 oz Chocolate chips
1/2 c  Crunchy peanut butter
1/3 c  Butter, softened
3/4 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs
2 t  Vanilla
Beat Peanut butter, Butter, and two sugars until creamy. Add Eggs and
Vanilla and beat until mixed. Blend Flour, Baking powder and Salt
with the peanut butter mixture. Mix in Peanut butter chips. Spread in
a greased 13x9x2 inch pan.  Sprinkle with chocolate morsels.  Bake 5
minutes at 350 degreees, run knife through to marbleize, Bake 20 to
25 minutes longer. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   02-13-95
