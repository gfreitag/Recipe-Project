Title: Asparagus Potato Quiche
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
3    Eggs;
3 tb Diced ham;
3 tb Cheddar cheese; grated
1 tb Green onions; chopped
1    6-1/2 oz pasty crust;
-prepared
6    Asparagus sprigs, diced
-discard tough ends)
2 md Potatoes; peeled grated
3 tb Butter -=OR=-
1 sm Onion; diced
1 cl Garlic; crushed
Melt butter or margarine, add onion and garlic and fry until onions
are transparent.  Add diced asparagus and fry 2 more minutes.  Add
grated potatoes, whisk in eggs, ham, cheese and chives.  Add salt and
pepper if desired.  Pour into prepared pastry crust and bake 350
degrees for 1 hour, garnish with diced tomatoes.  Served 6. Source:
