Title: 3 Beans Salad
Categories: Vegetable, Salad
Yield: 6 -8  servin
2 sm Green peppers, seeded and
-chopped
1 1/4 ts Salt
Dash pepper
1/3 c  Red wine vinegar
1 ts Sugar
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1 1/2 c  Red kidney beans, canned,
-drained
1 c  Chick peas, canned, drained
1 c  Green beans, canned, drained
1/2 c  Onion, finely chopped
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 1/2 tb Parsley, chopped
Using a strainer or colander, rinse drained kidney beans and
chick-peas under cold running water.  Pat dry with paper towels.  In
large bowl, combine kidney beans, chick-peas, green-beans, onion,
garlic powder, green pepper, and salt and pepper; mix well.  In small
bowl, combine vinegar, sugar, and oil.  Pour dressing over beans and
toss.  Let salad stand for an hour before serving. (If salad must
stand longer, refrigerate.) Submitted By FLEURETTE DELISLE   On
02-06-95
