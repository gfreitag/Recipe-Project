Title: Fantastic Layered Salad(Seven-Layer Salad)
Categories: Salad, Rice dishes, Vegetable, Side dish, Main dish
Yield: 8 nice folks
-thawed
1 c  Red kidney beans; drained
1 c  Good Seasons Italian;
-prepared -=OR=-
1 c  Cheese Italian Salad
-Dressing
1 1/3 c  Water
1/2 c  Salt;
1 1/2 c  MINUTE Rice;
3 1/2 c  Iceberg lettuce(1 head);
1 sm Red onion; thinly sliced
1 pk BIRDS EYE green peas;
Bring water and salt to a full boil in medium saucepan. Stir in rice.
Cover; remove from heat.  Let stand 5 minutes.  Fluff with fork; cool.
Place 1 1/2 cups of the lettuce in 2-quart straight-sided bowl. Add
half of the onion and top with half of the rice.  Add a layer peas.
Repeat lettuce, onion and rice layers.  Top with kidney beans and add
remaining lettuce. Cover and chill. About 1 hour before serving, pour
dressing evenly over salad and cover and chill. Store any leftover
salad in refrigerator. Makes 8 servings.
