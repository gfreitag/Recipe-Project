Title: Fruit-Nut Snack
Categories: Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 cups
4 oz Dates, chopped
6 1/2 oz Spanish peanuts, salted
1 c  Raisins
Mix ingredients.  Serve in small bowls.

Calories per 1 tablespoon serving: About 35
