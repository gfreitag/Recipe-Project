Title: Frozen Marshmallow & Pineapple Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 6 servings
-maraschino cherries
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 c  Coarsely chopped nuts
6 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 c  Drained crushed pineapple
2 c  Miniature marshmallows
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped
Blend cheese and pineapple; mix in remaining ingredients and spoon
into a refrigerator tray.  Freeze until firm, cut in squares and
serve.
