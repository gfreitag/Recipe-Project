Title: Garlic and Bean Soup
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 lg Clove garlic
2 ts Parmesan cheese
Salt
Pepper
16 oz Cooked white kidney beans
1 pk Instant chicken broth mix
1 1/2 c  Water
1 tb Dried parsley flakes
In a blender, combine all ingredients. Blend until smooth. Pour into
saucepan, heat and serve.

Per Serving: 10g protein, 1g fat, 25g carb., 229mg sodium, 1mg chol.,
143 calories.
