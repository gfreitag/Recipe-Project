Title: CLINTON'S CHIPS
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 90 servings
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Solid vegetable shortening
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Light brown sugar, packed
12 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
1 1/2 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
1 ts Salt
1 c  Granulated sugar
2    Eggs
2 c  Old-fashioned rolled oats
Preheat oven to 350F. Grease baking sheets. Combine flour, salt, and
baking soda on waxed paper. Beat together shortening, sugars, and
vanilla in large bowl with electric mixer until creamy. Add eggs,
beating until light and fluffy. Gradually beat in flour mixture and
rolled oats. Mix in chocolate chips.  Drop batter by well-rounded
measuring teaspoonfuls onto greased baking sheets. Bake in preheated
350F oven for 8 to 10 minutes or until golden. Cool cookies on sheets
on wire rack 2 minutes. Remove to wire rack to cool completely.
