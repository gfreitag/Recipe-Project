Title: Apple Butter Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Low-cal/fat, Fruit
Yield: 60 cookies
1 c  Dates, quartered
1 c  Seedless Raisins
1/2 c  Margarine
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg Substitute
1 c  Apple Butter
2 c  Flour
1 t  Salt
1 t  Baking Soda
1/4 t  Cloves
1/4 t  Nutmeg
1 t  Cinnamon
[PAUSE] Combine dry ingredients. In another bowl, cream butter &
sugar; stir in egg, dates & raisins. Stir in dry ingredients
alternately w/apple butter. Drop by rounded teaspoons onto greased
baking sheet 2" apart. Bake in preheated 375 deg oven for about 12
minutes. Submitted By MICHELLE BRUCE   On   02-04-95
