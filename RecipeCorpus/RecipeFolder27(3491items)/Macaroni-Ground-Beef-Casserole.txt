Title: Macaroni Ground Beef Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Meat
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Beef consomme
Salt & pepper
Peppers & celery, chopped
-optional
3 c  Uncooked macaroni
2 lb Hamburg
1 lg Onion
1 cn Tomatoes (large)
1 cn Tomatoe soup
Fry Hamburg & onions until brown. Add other ingredients. Salt and
pepper to taste. Bake about 1 hour at 400 F
