Title: Calico Cole Slaw - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 6 servings
**OR**
1 1/2 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
3 ea Tb wine vinegar
1/2 t  Lite salt, optional
1 t  Celery seed
3 c  Chopped cabbage
1/4 c  Minced onion
2/3 c  Fatfree Kraft mayonnaise or
-Miracle Whip
1 ea Tb sugar
Stir al of the ingredients together and chill for at least one hour.
the longer it chills, the better the flavor. Per sugar serving: 33
cal., 0.1g fat (3%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 1g pro., 8g carb., 202mg
sod. Per S&L serving: 28cal, 7g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie
