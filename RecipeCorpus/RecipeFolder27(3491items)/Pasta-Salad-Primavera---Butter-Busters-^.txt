Title: Pasta Salad Primavera - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 10 servings
**OR**
1 c  Fatfree Italian salad
-dressing
1 t  Dried basil
1 c  Nonfat mozzarella cheese,
-grated
1/3 c  Fatfree Parmesan cheese
-(Alpine Lace or Weight
-Watchers)
3/4 ea Pk (8oz) Skinner Trio
-Italiano noodles
2 lg Tomatoes, coarsley chopped
1 1/2 c  Cooked broccoli florets
3/4 c  Frozen peas, thawed
**OR**
3/4 c  Cooked sliced carrots
1 c  Fatfree Weight Watchers
-Ceaser dressing
Cook noodles according to package directions, drain and rinse with
cold water until cool. In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, broccoli,
peas or carrots (or both), dressing and basil. Add cheese and
noodles; toss lightly and chill. Per serving: 77 cal., 0.5g fat (6%),
2mg chol., 2g fiber, 8g pro., 12g carb., 310mg sod. Butter Busters by
