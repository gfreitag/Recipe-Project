Title: Kikkoman Beef & Tomato Stir-Fry Salad
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Stir fry, Tomato, Asian
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Onion powder
4 c  Shredded iceberg lettuce
2 ea Stalks celery, cut diagonal
Into thin slices
1 ea Green pepper, julienned
2 ts Minced fresh ginger root
15 ea Cherry tomatoes, halved
1 lb Boneless beef steak
1 tb Cornstarch
4 tb Kikkoman Soy Sauce, divided
1/2 ts Sugar
1 ea Clove garlic, pressed
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1 tb Tomato catsup
3 tb Vegetable oil
Cut beef into thin strips. Combine cornstarch, 2 tablespoon soy sauce,
sugar and garlic; stir in beef. Let stand 15 minutes. Meanwhile,
combine remaining soy sauce with vinegar, catsup, 1 tablespoon oil,
onion powder and 2 tablespoons water; set aside. Line large platter
or bowl with lettuce. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in hot wok or large
skillet over high heat. Add beef and stir-fry 1 minute; remove. Heat
remaining oil in same pan. Add next 3 ingredients; stir-fry 1 minute.
Remove pan from heat; stir in tomatoes beef and soy sauce mixture.
Spoon mixture over lettuce; toss well to combine.
