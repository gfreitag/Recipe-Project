Title: Impossible No-Crust Sweet Potato Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert, Holiday, Sweetpotato
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine;softened
1    Egg
1/2 ts To 1 tsp almond extract
Whipped cream
DEIDRE ANNE PENROD   (FGGT98
4    Sweet potatoes; *
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
[PAUSE] * Medium size\peeled\cooked\mashed

Combine all the ingredients except whipped cream in a large mixing
bowl; beat at medium speed of electric mixer until well blended.
Spoon mixture into a 9-inch pie plate. Bake at 450^ for 25 minutes or
until lightly browned.  Serve warm or cool with a dollop of whipped
cream.

YIELD: One 9-inch pie.
