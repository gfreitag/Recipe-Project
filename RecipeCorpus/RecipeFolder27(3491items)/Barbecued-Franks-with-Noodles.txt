Title: Barbecued Franks with Noodles
Categories: Sausage, Pasta, Kids, Main dish
Yield: 1 casserole
1 lb All beef franks;
3 c  Flat egg noodles; cooked
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/2 ts Salt;
1 ts Paprika;
1/2 c  Water;
1 c  Chili sauce;
1/4 c  Oil;
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1 tb Worcestershire sauce;
1 tb Brown sugar;
Into 2 quart baking dish put the cooked noodles; arrange franks to
top. Saute onion in oil; cook until golden brown.  Add remaining
ingredients and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Remove
from heat and pour over noodles and franks.  Bake at 350 degrees for
30 minutes. Source: Jean Swede, Our Favorite Recipes, Walker Scott
