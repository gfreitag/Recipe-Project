Title: Adobong Okra
Categories: Vegetable, Filipino
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Patis (fish sauce)
4 cl Garlic,crushed
1    Onion, chopped
1 ts Pepper
20    Okra, fresh young ones
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Pork, diced
Wash and parboil the okra in the water. Set aside. Fry the pork. Push
to one side of the pan. Saute garlic, onion and okra. Season with
vinegar, patis, & pepper. Cook until okra is tender crisp. (Vinegar
helps control the sliminess of the okra.) From: Let's Cook with Nora,
Nora V. Daza Typed by: Joell Abbott 9/94
