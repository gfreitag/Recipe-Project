Title: Winter's Day Stew
Categories: Meat, Main dish, Game, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Ground Cumin
3/4 c  Cider
3/4 c  Dark Beer
4 c  Winter Squash, diced
-(Acorn or banana are best)
Salt
Pepper
1 c  Green Pepper, chopped
2 c  Apples, chopped
(SAUTE TOGETHER):
2 lb Venison steak or roast cut
-to 1" pieces
2 tb Olive Oil
2 c  Onions, diced
2    Cloves garlic, minced
(ADD):
2 tb Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
Transfer sauted ingredients to 2 qt. (or larger) crock pot or Dutch
oven.

Slow cook 5-6 hours on low or until tender.
