Title: Peanut Crisps
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1 t  Vanilla
1 c  Crispie rice cereal
1 c  Crunchy peanut butter
1 ea Egg
3/4 c  Sugar
In a large mixing bowl stir together peanut butter, egg, sugar and
vanilla. When well blended, quickly stir in cereal. Roll into 1 inch
balls. Place on a cookie sheet and flatten with tines of a fork. Bake
at 350 F for about 10 minutes.  Do not remove from pan until slightly
cooled. Source: Co-op grocery store advertising, September 1993
Shared and tested by Elizabeth Rodier
