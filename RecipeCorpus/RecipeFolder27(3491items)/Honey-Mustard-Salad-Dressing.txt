Title: Honey Mustard Salad Dressing
Categories: Salad
Yield: 4 servings
1 pt Mayo
2 pt Sour cream
1 pt Dijon
1 pt Honey
Wisk first 2 ingredients together.  Blend honey and mustard. Adjust
the seasoning to taste with last 2 ingredients. May be used as a
salad dressing or as a dipping sauce. source: Howard Radis, Chef,
JAKE'S MARKETPLACE CAFE, Londonderry,   Vermont. It's super.
The 'pt' in this recipe should be thought of as parts. that way you
can make as much (or little) as you want)
