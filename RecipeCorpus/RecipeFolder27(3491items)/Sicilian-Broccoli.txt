Title: Sicilian Broccoli
Categories: Italian, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 5 servings
2 c  Mozzarella or sharp cheddar
Cheese, shredded
Fresh ground pepper
*Optional*
4    Anchovies, finely chopped
1/2 c  Sliced black olives
2 lb Broccoli
2 T  Butter
1    Green onion, chopped
(including green)
1    Clove garlic, minced
1 1/2 T  Flour (all purpose)
1 c  Chicken stock or broth
Cook broccoli in salted water until tender. As the broccoli cooks,
melt butter in saucepan and add the onion and garlic. Cook, stirring,
about 3 minutes. Do not brown! Sprinkle with the flour and add the
stock, stirring vigorously with a wire whisk. When the mixture is
thickened, simmer 5 min. Add the anchovies and olives (if desired),
pepper to taste, and cheese, and stir until the cheese melts. Serve
over the hot, drained broccoli. Submitted By KRISTIN TREMAYNE   On
02-12-95
