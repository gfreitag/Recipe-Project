Title: Spicy Chicken Salad - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn (8oz) water chestnuts,
-drained and chopped
1 ea Tb mustard
1/2 c  Kraft fatfree Miracle Whip
1 ea Bottle fatfree Thousand
-Island dressing
1 t  Hickory salt seasoning
12 oz Cooked white chicken meat,
-boneless and skinless
4 ea Hard boiled egg whites,
-chopped
1/4 c  Sweet pickle relish
1 ea Tb chopped onion
1/4 c  Chopped celery
Bake the chicken after you sprinkle it with seasoning on it for about
20-30 minutes covered at 325F. In a medium sized bowl combine all of
the other ingredients. Remove the cooked chicken and chop into cubes.
Fold it into the dressing mixture. Chill about 30 minutes. This is
great served with nonfat saltine crackers or on a sandwich. It can
also be served on a lettuce leaf with some fresh fruit. Per serving:
158 cal., 2g fat (11%), 36mg chol., 0g fiber, 16g pro., 18g carb.,
685mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-446-67040-5 Entered
