Title: Gourmet Green Beans
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs, Mushroom
Yield: 1 servings
1    Jar cheez whiz (8-oz)
1    Jar sliced mushrooms;
-drained (2-1/2-oz)
6    Bacon; slices
1/4 c  Onion; chopped, may use
-whole one
2 pk (9-oz) Cut green beans;
Fry bacon until crisp. Remove from skillet.  Drain fat, reserving 1
tablespoon. Crumble bacon.  Saute onion in bacon fat until tender. Add
bacon, green beans, chiz whiz, and mushrooms.  Heat stirring
occasionally. Makes 6-8 servings. Source: Pan Sykes, Cooking with
