Title: Shredded Wheat Bread (Machine)
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1/4 c  Light molasses
1 c  Mini-shredded wheat biscuits
(2 ounces)
1 1/4 c  Plus 1 T plus 1 t warm
(105ø-115ø) water
2 1/4 ts Dry yeast
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
3 tb Butter/margarine
Add ingredients in the order listed. All ingredients must be at room
temperature, unless otherwise noted. Select whole grain and baking
control for light. Press start. Tent a piece of aluminum foil, shiny
side down, over glass dome at start of baking. Remove when cooling
begins. Bread will be ready in 4 hours and 10 minutes.

Connoisseurs of shredded wheat know their favorite cereal has no added
sugar or salt. It's just pure, wholesome goodness in a delightfully
crunchy form. The idea of basing a bread on a breakfast cereal was
intriguing, as is the spicy hint imparted by the light molasses.

From "Easy Baking" by Claudia Burns and Tom Lacalamita for Welbilt
