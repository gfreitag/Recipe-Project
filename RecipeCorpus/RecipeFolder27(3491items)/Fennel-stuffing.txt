Title: Fennel stuffing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped fennel stalk
1/4 t  Salt
1 x  Sprig chopped fennel
1 1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1/4 c  Chopped celery
1 x  Egg, beaten
1/4 t  Paprika
leaf

Combine bread crumbs, chopped vegs and egg. Sprinkle seasonings and
toss. Moisten with a little milk or water if necessary and use
dressing to stuff a fish cavity before baking.
