Title: Curried Turkey Canapes ^
Categories: Appetizer, Curries, Turkey, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Curry powder
Crackers
Hard boiled egg slices
Shredded coconut
Chopped peanuts
1 c  Finely chopped cooked
-turkey
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Finely chopped chutney
1 tb Chutney juice
Blend turkey, mayonnaise, chutney, chutney juice and curry powder
well. Spread on crackers. Top some with each of the remaininging
ingredients. Arrange on a platter and serve.
