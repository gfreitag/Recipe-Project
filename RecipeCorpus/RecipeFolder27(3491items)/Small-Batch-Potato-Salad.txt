Title: Small Batch Potato Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable, Pork
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1 cn Sliced potatoes, drained
-(15 oz.)
Minced fresh parsley
4 ea Strips Bacon
1 T  Cornstarch
1/3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Vinegar
In a skillet, cook bacon until crisp. Drain, reserving 2 tablespoons
of drippings. Crumble bacon and set aside. Add cornstarch and sugar to
drippings; stir until smooth. Add water, vinegar, salt and pepper;
cook and stir over medium heat for 3-4 minutes or until thickened and
bubbly. Stir in potatoes and bacon. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until
heated through. Serve warm or at room temperature. Garnish with
parsley. Serves 2. SOURCE:* Taste of Home Magazine, Feb./Mar. 1995
