Title: Irresistible Chocolate Cake
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 1 cake
2    Eggs, well beaten
2 1/2 T  Butter or margarine, melted
3 1/3 c  Basic cake mix
9 T  Cocoa
1 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 375.  Lightly grease and flour two 8" round pans.
Combine basic cake mix and cocoa in a large bowl. Add 1/2 cup of the
milk and beat at medium speed for 2 minutes. Add remaining 1/2 cup
milk, eggs and melted butter or margarine. Beat 2 more minutes. Pour
into prepared pans. Bake 25 to 30 minutes. Cool in pans 10 minutes,
then cool on wire racks. Frost as desired.
