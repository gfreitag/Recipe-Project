Title: Warm Apple Topping - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
**OR**
1/4 t  Sweet 'n Low brown
1/4 t  Almond extract
3/4 c  Sliced green apples
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/8 t  Nutmeg
2 t  Brown sugar
Bring to a boil the apples, orange juice and nutmeg. Cover and simmer
5 minutes. Remove from heat and mash apples until smooth. Stir in
sugar and almond extract. Serving size is 2 tablespoons. Per sugar
serving: 14 cal., 0g fat (0%), 0mg chol., 0.5g fiber, 0g pro., 3.5g
carb., 0mg sod. Per S&L serving: 10 cal., 2.5g carb. Butter Busters
