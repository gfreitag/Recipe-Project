1 cn (8oz) sliced mushrooms

-fatfree chicken broth
1 ea Pk (16oz) nonfat cottage
-cheese or ricotta
1 ea Pk (10oz) chopped spinach,
In a nonstick ckillet, over medium heat, cook mushrooms, onion,
garlic, Italian seasoning and pepper in Butter Buds until tender,
about 4 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in cottage cheese,
Parmesan cheese, spinach and Egg Beaters. Spoon into cooked shells.
Spread 1/2 cup spaghetti sauce on bottom of 13 x 9 x 2" baking pan
that has been sprayed with nonstick cooking spray. Arrange shells in
sauce and a layer of grated mozzarella. Pour the rest of the sauce
over shells and sprinkle with remaining mozzarella. Bake at 350F. for
35 minutes or until heated through and cheese is melted. Per serving:
