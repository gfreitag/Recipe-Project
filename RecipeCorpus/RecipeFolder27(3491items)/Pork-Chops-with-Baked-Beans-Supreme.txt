Title: Pork Chops with Baked Beans Supreme
Categories: Pork, Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Brer Rabit green label
-molasses
1 cn Pork and beans (31oz size)
Salt and pepper to taste
Posted by Stu Hopper
____________________________
6 ea Pork chops
2 tb Catsup
2 tb Brown sugar
Mix beans, molasses, brown sugar, and catsup together and place in
7"x11" baking pan.  Salt and pepper pork chops and place on beans in
baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour, turning pork chops after
1/2 hour of baking.

Enjoy
