Title: White Trash *
Categories: Snack, Nuts/grains, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
12 oz Chocolate chips
12 oz Raisins (opt)
1 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
12 oz Golden graahams cereal
24 oz Peanuts
2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Butter
Mix golden grahams and peanuts. Add raisins or another type nut. Melt
together peanut butter, butter and chocolate chips. Coat with powdered
sugar.
