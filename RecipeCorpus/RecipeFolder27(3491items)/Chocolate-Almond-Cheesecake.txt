Title: Chocolate Almond Cheesecake
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
Microwave 2 minutes.
5    JUMBO EGGS, out of shells
-and microwave on
High 25 seconds to WARM them
3 oz UNSWEETENED CHOCOLATE or
-CHOCOBAKE
2 ts VANILLA
1/2 c  HEAVY CREAM
1/4 c  CORNSTARCH
1 lb OREOS, crushed
6 tb BUTTER, melted
1 c  ALMONDS, chopped
Mix and pat onto bottom and
-sides of 10" springform
-pan.
CAKE
1 1/4 c  SUGAR
32 oz CREAM CHEESE, unwrapped and
-softened in
** CRUST
