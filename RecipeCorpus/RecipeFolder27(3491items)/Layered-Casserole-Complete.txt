Title: Layered Casserole Complete
Categories: Mixes, Beef, Casserole
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Mashed potatoes
1 c  Grated cheddar cheese
1 pt Meat sauce mix
16 oz French cut green beans
Preheat oven to 350.  Lightly butter a 1 1/2 qt casserole. Layer
casserole with meat sauce mix, green beans and mashed potatoes.
Sprinkle grated cheese on top. Bake 25 to 30 minutes, until bubbly.
