Title: Filipino Bean, Shrimp and Spinach
Categories: Filipino, Seafood, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
To taste
12 oz Cleaned shrimp
1 c  Cooked fresh lima beans
1 c  Cleaned spinach
2 1/2 c  Water
1 md Tomato, sliced
4 oz Onion, finely diced
1 sm Garlic clove, minced
Salt and pepper or soy sauce
In saucepan combine water, tomato, onion, garlic, salt and pepper.
Bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer for 15 minutes. Add shrimp,
beans, and spinach and return to simmer for 5 to 8 minutes more or
until shrimp are pink and firm. *From Weight Watchers International
Cookbook* Submitted By MICHELLE BRUCE   On   01-31-95
