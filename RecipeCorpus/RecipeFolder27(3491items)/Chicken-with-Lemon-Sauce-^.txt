Title: Chicken with Lemon Sauce ^
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Chicken, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Minced onion
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 c  Butter
2    Broiler chickens, halved
1 cl Garlic, crushed
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Salad oil
Melt butter in heavy frying pan wit lid. Add chicken and brown well
on all sides. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over chicken.
Cover tightly. Cook over low heat about 40 minutes, or until chicken
is tender. Transfer to serving dish and serve.
