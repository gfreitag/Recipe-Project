Title: Halfway House Beans
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Pork, Tomato
Yield: 8 servings
4 md Tomatoes;
2 tb To 3 tb sugar;
1 ds Salt & pepper;
3 pk (10-oz) green beans; frozen
-(french style)
4 sl Bacon;
1    Onion;
Cook green beans and drain.  Fry bacon and drain.  Chop onion and
saute in fat.  Add tomatoes, sugar salt, and pepper.  Cover and
simmer until soft. about 20 minutes.  Add beans and crumbled bacon
and stir together. Serves 8. Source: Mrs. J. B. Hall Jr. (Sue), The
