Title: Brown Sugar Icing
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
3 tb Cream
6 tb Butter
Mix and bring to a boil.  Remove from fire and add baking powder and
vanilla.  Beat until you can spread on cake. Randy Rigg
