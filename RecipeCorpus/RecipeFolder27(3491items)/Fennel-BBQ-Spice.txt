Title: Fennel BBQ Spice
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------AMERICA-------------------------------
1/2 ts Coriander seeds
1 ts Grated lemon peel
1 ts Dried thyme.
1    Garlic clove(s)
1 ts Black peppercorns
2 ts Fennel seeds
Crush the  garlic in a mortar and mix everything together.

An excellent blend for fish.
