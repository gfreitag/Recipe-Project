Title: Poppy Seed Dressing - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 20 servings
2/3 c  White vinegar
2 c  Liquid Butter Buds
2 ea Tb poppy seeds
1/3 ea Onion, grated
1 1/2 c  Sugar*
2 t  Dry mustard
1 t  Papa Dash lit salt, optional
Place all ingredients in the blender and blend until smooth. Serve
over fresh fruit. 2 1/2 cups. *Or use 1 cup sugar and 6 packets Sweet
'n Low Per two tablespoon serving: 76 cal., 0.5g fat (65), 0mg
choll., 0g fiber, 0g pro., 18g carb., 176mg sod. Per S&L serving: 56
cal., 13g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-446-67040-5
