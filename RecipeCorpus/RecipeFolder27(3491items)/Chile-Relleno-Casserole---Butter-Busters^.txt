Title: Chile Relleno Casserole - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 9 ----------
1/2 lb Nonfat grated mozzarella
1 cn (14 1/2oz) Del Monte chunky
-Mexican style stewed
-tomatoes
1 c  Evaporated skim milk
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
1/3 c  Flour
3 cn (4oz) whole green chiles
Preheat oven to 350F. spray a 1 1/2-qt. size casserole dish with
nonfat cooking spray. Beat evaporated skim milk, Egg Beaters, and
flour until smooth. split open chiles and rinse to remove seeds.
Drain chiles on a paper towel. Mix cheeses together and reserve one
half cup for topping. Alternate layers of chiles, cheese and Egg
Beaters mixture in a casserole dish. Pour chunky tomato sauce over
top layer and sprinkle reserved cheese on top. bake one hour or until
done in the center. Per serving: 83 cal., 0.3g fat (3%), 3mg chol.,
1g fiber, 9g pro., 12g carb., 300mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam
Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 2-95.
