Title: Layered Twenty Four Hour Salad - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 16 servings
1 cn (8oz) sliced wate
-chestnuts, drained
1/2 c  Fatfree Parmesan cheese
-(Alpine Lace or Weight
-Watchers)
1 ea Pt Kraft fatfree mayonnaise
-or Miracle Whip
1/2 c  Fatfree sour cream
3 c  Shredded combination of
-lettuces
2 c  Grated carrots
1 ea Stalk celery, diced
1 1/2 c  Frozen Bird's Eye green peas
1 ea Salt and Pepper to taste
2 c  Nonfat mozzarella cheese,
-grated
In a very large salad bowl, line the bottom with lettuce. Cover the
lettuce with carrots, celery, onions, water chestnuts, and peas.
Spread the pint of mayonnaise mixed with the sour cream as you would
ice a cake. Sprinkle mozzzarella and Parmesan cheese on top. cover
and chill 24 hours. Per serving: 89 cal., 0.1g fat (1%), 2mg chol.,
2g fiber, 4g pro., 19g carb., 675mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam
