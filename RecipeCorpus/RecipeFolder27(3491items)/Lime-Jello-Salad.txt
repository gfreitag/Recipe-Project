Title: Lime Jello Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 10 servings
1 cn Crushed pineapple, including
Juice
1 c  Chopped pecans
1    (8-oz) carton frozen whipped
Topping
1 pk (3-oz) lime jello
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
1 sm Cream cheese, cut in small
Pieces
1 c  Boiling water
Combine lime jello, marshmallows, cream cheese and water until
marshmallows and cream cheese dissolve.  Add crushed pineapple and
pecans. Put in refrigerator until it sets, then fold in whipped
topping.  Pour into molds or large dish.
