Title: Layered Mexican Dip - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizer, Pickles
Yield: 32 servings
1 c  Grated nonfat Cheddar cheese
1 ea Jar (16oz) taco sauce
1 cn (4oz) chopped green chilies
1 c  Fatfree sour cream
3 ea Tb fatfree chicken broth
1 lg Onion, diced
2 cn (1lb) Old el Paso fatfree
-refried beans
1/2 lb Ground white tenderloin
-skinless turkey, cooked
-and drained
3 c  Nonfat mozzarella cheese
Brown diced onion in chicken broth. Add cooked ground turkey. Stir
refried beans into turkey mixture. Spread into a 9x13" casserole dish
that has been sprayed with a nonfat cooking spray. Sprinkle green
chiles over top of turkey bean mixture. Spread half of cheese over
this. Pour taco sauce over cheese. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top
and bake at 400F 20-25 minutes. Cool slightly and spoon sour cream to
make a cross on top of casserole. Serve with Smart Temptations lowfat
tortilla chips or homemade lowfat chips. Per 1/4 cup serving: 109
cal., 0.3g fat(2%), 12mg chol., 1g fiber, 18g protein, 7g carb.,
487mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered
