Title: Heather's Chocolate Eclair Ecstasy
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 12 servings
8 oz Cool Whip; thawed
1 lb Graham crackers
3 c  Milk
6 oz Pudding, french vanilla inst
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
1 ts Vanilla
3 tb ;hot water
3 tb Margarine; melted
3 tb Cocoa
1 1/2 c  Sugar, powdered
Mix together well the pudding mix and milk. Add Cool Whip. In the
bottom of a 9x13-inch pan, put a layer of whole graham crackers. Pour
half of the pudding mix over the graham crackers. Add another layer
of graham crackers over the mix, then pour remaining pudding mixture
over crackers. Top with remaining layer of crackers. Mix all glaze
ingredients together until smooth. Drizzle glaze over top of graham
crackers and refrigerate for 24 hours.

per serving: 391Cal, 13gFat, 65gCarb, 493mgNa, 4mgChol
