Title: Onion Slim Dip - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizer, Pickles
Yield: 8 servings
1 ea Pk (ab 1 1/2oz) dry onion
-soup mix, (any brand)
1/4 c  Skim milk
2 c  Nonfat cottage cheese,
-drained
Mix skim milk and cottage cheese in blender. Blend on high speed until
smooth and creamy, about 3 minutes. Stir in onion soup mix. Chill one
hour. Serve with fresh vegetables. Note: You can make this with
fatfree sour cream in place of the cottage cheese and skim milk. Per
1/4 cup serving: 47 cal., 0g fat (0%), 0mg chol., 0g fiber, 8g pro.,
3g carb., 460mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN
