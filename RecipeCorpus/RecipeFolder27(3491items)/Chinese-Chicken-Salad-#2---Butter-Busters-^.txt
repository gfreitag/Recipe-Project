Title: Chinese Chicken Salad #2 - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 8 servings
-(Weight Watchers or Kraft)
1 t  Soy sauce
1 ea Tb fatfree ranch dressing
-(Weight Watchers or Kraft)
1 ea Tb fatfree sour cream
1 ea Tb lemon juice
1 c  Cooked chicken breast, cubed
1/2 c  Water chestnuts, drained
1/2 c  Bean sprouts
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Chopped green onion
1 c  Cooked brown or white rice
2 ea Tb fatfree Italian dressing
In large bowl, mix chicken, water chestnuts, sprouts, celery and
onion. In a separate bowl, pour Italian dressing over cooked rice and
stir until absorbed then add to the chicken mixture. Mix together the
soy sauce, ranch dressing sour cream and lemon juice and toss with
the other ingredients. Per serving: 89cal., 1g fat (10%), 12mg chol.,
2g fiber, 6g pro., 14g carb., 205mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam
