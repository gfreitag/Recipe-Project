Title: Scotch Shortbread
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 35 cookies
1/2 ts Almond extract
2 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Butter
3/4 c  C and H Powdered Sugar
-- unsifted
Beat butter until soft.  Add sugar and almond extract and beat until
smooth.  Add flour and salt, and mix well.  Press dough evenly in 11
x 7 x 2-inch pan.  Mark in 1-1/2 inch squares with a fork.  Bake in
375-degree oven 25 minutes.  Cut along markings while hot.  Cool in
pan.
