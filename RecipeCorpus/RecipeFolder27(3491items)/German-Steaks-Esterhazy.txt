Title: German Steaks Esterhazy
Categories: Main dish, Aust/german, Beef, Meat
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Sour Cream
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
4    Servings Of Steak; **
1/4 lb Mushrooms; Diced
1 sm Carrot; Diced
Shallot Or Green Onion; *
2 tb Butter
1 ts Paprika
*    Shallot or Green Onion should be minced ** Steaks should be one
of the following:  Sirloin, T-bone, Or Fillet.

Saute mushrooms, carrot, and shallot or green onion in butter. Add
paprika, salt, sour cream, and Worcestershire sauce. Simmer for 2
minutes, but DO NOT boil. Broil steaks and top with sauce.
