Title: Date Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Nuts
1 c  Dates; seeded - chopped
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Sugar; white
3 ea Eggs
4 c  Flour; sifted
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cream tartar
3/4 c  Lard and butter
Mix flour, cream tartar and soda. Combine remaining ingredients, mix.
Let stand over night. Slice and bake in morning.

Note: No time or temperature given. Assume a 375 F. oven.

Source: Mrs. S. A. Schlup, Mifflin Grange, Wyandot County, OH
