Title: Irish Cream (Extra Rich)
Categories: Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Evaporated milk
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
6    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Blended Irish whiskey
1 ts Pure vanilla extract
1 tb Instant coffee
Combine whiskey, vanilla, and coffee and blend in blender. Add milks
and blend for 1 minute. Add eggs and pulse stir (use lowest speed on
blender and turn on-off about eight times) just until smooth and not
thick. Makes about 4 cups.
