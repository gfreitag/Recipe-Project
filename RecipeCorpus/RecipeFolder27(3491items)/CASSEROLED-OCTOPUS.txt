Title: CASSEROLED OCTOPUS
Categories: Seafood, Fish, Casserole, Mushroom
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Olive Oil
2 c  Julienned Mushrooms
1 ea Clove Garlic pressed
1 T  Fresh Chervil chopped
1 ea Bay Leaf
6 sm Octopus allow 1/2 lb. per
-person
1/2 c  Dry Wine
1 c  Chopped Onions
1 T  Parsley chopped
1 T  Basil
Cover and bring just to a boil. Reduce heat and cover tightly and
simmer 2-1/2 to 3 hours. You may add ink to the pan drippings, just
before serving. DO NOT BOIL POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 2/95
