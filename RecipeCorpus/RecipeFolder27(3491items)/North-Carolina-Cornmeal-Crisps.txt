Title: North Carolina Cornmeal Crisps
Categories: Bread
Yield: 18 servings
2 tb Lard, melted
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Cornmeal
1 1/4 c  Boiling water
Combine all ingredients, mixing well.  Spoon batter by T onto a
lightly greased baking sheet.  Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes or
until edges are browned.
