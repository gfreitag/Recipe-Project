Title: Wine Barbecued Chicken
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Dried marjoram
1/4 ts Dried rosemary
1 1/4 lb Boneless chicken parts
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Dry white wine
1 tb Plus 1 tsp minced onion flak
1/2 ts Salt
Place chicken in a shallow baking pan. Combine remaining ingredients
and pour over chicken. Marinate several hours. Preheat oven to 350.
Bake, uncovered, one hour, basting frequently.

Per serving: 30g protein, 11g fat, 2g carb., 559mg sodium, 99mg
chol., 238 calories.
