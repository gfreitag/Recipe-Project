Title: Cabbage Surprise
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 - 6 folks
3 tb Butter  -=OR=-
3 tb Margarine;
10    Ritz crackers, crumbled
1 md Cabbage; cut in wedges
2 oz Sharp cheese; grated
1/2 cn Mushroom soup;
Cook cabbage in water until tender, but not soggy.  Drian well.  Mix
together the cheese, soup, butter or margarine and crackers.  Return
cabbage to pan.  Pour soup mixture over cabbage and heat.  Serves 4
to 6. Source: Mrs. W.J. O'Donohue (Cynthia), The Stuffed Cabbage,
Richmond, VA Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her
