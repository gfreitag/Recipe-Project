Title: Cinnamon Nut Raisin Bread (Machine)
Categories: Bread, Nuts/grains, Breadmaker, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
2 tb Nonfat dry milk
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Walnuts, chopped (optional)
2 tb Butter/margarine
1 3/4 ts Active dry yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
1 tb Dark brown sugar, packed
1/2 tb Granulated sugar
Add ingredients in the order listed. All ingredients must be at room
temperature. Press Select for Yeast bread. Press baking control for
medium. Press start. Bread will be ready in 4 hours and 4 minutes.

From "Easy Baking" by Claudia Burns and Tom Lacalamita for Welbilt.
