Title: Golden French Toast
Categories: Mixes, Breakfast, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 10 slices
1 c  Pancake mix
10 sl Bread
2    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Milk
In medium shallow bowl, combine eggs, milk and pancake mix. Blend
well. Dip bread slices into the batter and cook on a hot buttered
griddle for about 4 minutes, until lightly browned on both sides.
