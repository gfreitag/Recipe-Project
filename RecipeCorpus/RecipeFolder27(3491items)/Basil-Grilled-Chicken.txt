Title: Basil Grilled Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Chicken, Herbs/spice, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Minced fresh basil
1 T  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Fresh basil sprigs (opt.)
3/4 ts Coarsely ground pepper
4 ea Chicken breast halves,
- skinned
1/3 c  Butter/margarine, melted
1/4 c  Chopped fresh basil
1/2 c  Butter/margarine, softened
Press 3/4 teaspoon pepper into meaty side of chicken breast halves.
Combine 1/3 cup melted butter and 1/4 cup chopped basil; stir well.
Brush chicken lightly with melted butter mixture.

Combine 1/2 cup softened butter, 2 tablespoons basil, Parmesan cheese,
garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.  Beat at low speed
of an electric mixer until mixture is well blended and smooth.
Transfer to a small serving bowl; set aside.

Grill chicken over medium coals 8 to 10 minutes on each side, basting
frequently with remaining melted butter mixture.

Serve grilled chicken with basil-butter mixture.  Garnish with fresh
basil sprigs, if desired.
