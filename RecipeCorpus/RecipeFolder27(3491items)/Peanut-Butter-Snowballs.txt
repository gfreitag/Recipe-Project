Title: Peanut Butter Snowballs
Categories: Candy, Nuts/grains
c  confectioners' sugar
Yield: 2 dozen  1
1/2   c  creamy peanut butter
tb butter/margarine, softened
lb white confectionery coating
3
1

In a mixing bowl, combine sugar, peanut butter and butter; mix well.
Shape into 1 inch balls and place on waxed paper lined cookie sheet.
Chill for 30 minutes or until firm.  Meanwhile, melt the white
coating in a double boiler or microwave-safe bowl.  Dip balls and
place on waxed paper to harden.  Yield:  2 dozen
