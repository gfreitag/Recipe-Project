Title: Brown Bread (ABM)
Categories: Bread
Yield: 1 loaf
3/4 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 c  Oatmeal (rolled oats)
2 T  Milk powder
1 1/2 t  Salt
2 1/2 t  Dry yeast
1 c  Water
2 T  Water
1 1/2 T  Vegetable oil
2 T  Molasses
2 1/4 c  White flour
For 1 1/2 lb. Loaf:

Put ingredients in the bread case in the order listed. Bake on the
basic white bread mode (3:50 on our machine).
