Title: Coconut Dream Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Coconut
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 1/4 c  Sifted flour
1 1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Butter
2    Eggs
Combine 1 cup of flour and 1/4 cup of sugar.  Add butter and mix.
Press in an ungreased 9 inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15
minutes. Beat eggs. Add gradually; cup sugar, until fluffy.  Sift 1/4
cup of flour and baking powder.  Fold into egg mixture.  Add
remaining ingredients. Spread on baked mixture.  Bake 20 to 25
minutes. Cut into small squares while warm. Randy Rigg
