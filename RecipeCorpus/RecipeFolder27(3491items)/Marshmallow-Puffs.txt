Title: Marshmallow Puffs
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 dozen
1/2 c  Chunky peanut butter
2 tb Butter/margarine
36 lg Marshmallows
1 1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
Line a 9 inch square pan with foil; butter the foil.  Arrange
marshmallows in pan.  In a double boiler or microwave-safe bowl, melt
chocolate chips, peanut butter and butter.  Pour over the
marshmallow. Chill completely. Cut between marshmallows.   Yield:  3
dozen Submitted By MARIE CULVER   On   02-05-95 (1324)
