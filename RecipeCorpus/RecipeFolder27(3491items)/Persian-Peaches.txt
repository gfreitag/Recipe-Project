Title: Persian Peaches
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 5 servings
2 tb Finely chopped
Candied ginger
1 ds Salt
4 c  Sliced peaches
1/2 c  Orange juice
3 tb Honey
Candied ginger adds delightful zingo. Serve plain or over vanilla ice
cream.

Combine all ingredients, mixing gently. Cover; chill thoroughly.
Spoon into chilled sherbet glasses. Makes 5 servings.
