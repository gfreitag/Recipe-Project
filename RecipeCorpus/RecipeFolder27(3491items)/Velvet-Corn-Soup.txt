Title: Velvet Corn Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
1    Egg, beaten
1 1/4 t  Salt
1/2 t  White pepper
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
3 c  Fresh or frozen corn
4 c  Half and half
Melt butter, add onion.  Saute until soft, not brown.  Add corn, cook
5 minutes.  Do not brown!  Add cream and beaten egg.  Cook until
mixture begins to boil, stirring constantly for 3 minutes. Add
seasoning. Put in blender, whip until creamy.  Strain and reheat - or
serve cold! Its delicious!  Makes 8-10 servings.
