Title: Snack Cake Mix
Categories: Cake/cookie, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
2 T  BAKING SODA
6 c  SUGAR
8 c  FLOUR
1 T  SALT
IN LARGE BOWL, COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS.  STIR WITH A WIRE WHISK UNTIL
BLENDED. DIVIDE MIXURE EVENLY INTO SIX 2 1/2 CUP CONTAINERS WITH TIGHT
FITTING LIDS, ABOUT 2 1/4 CUP EACH. LABEL AND STORE IN COOL DRY
PLACE. USE WITHIN 12 WEEKS.
