Title: Black Olive Paste
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 1 cup
2    Anchovy fillets (opt)
1 tb Capers
1/2 ts Thyme leaves, minced
1 pn Cayenne
3 tb Olive oil
1 c  Olives,
-such as Kalamata
-or Nicoise, pitted
1 lg Garlic clove(s)
-coarsely chopped
Place all ingredients in food processor; process to a paste. Scrape
into a small bowl. Can be refrigerated in an airtight container for
at least 1 month.

Cook's Illustrated March/April 1995
