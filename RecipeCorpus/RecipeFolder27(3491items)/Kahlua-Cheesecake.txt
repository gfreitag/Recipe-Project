Title: Kahlua Cheesecake
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
Bake 8-10 minutes. Cool.
CAKE
2    Envelopes UNFLAVORED GELATIN
1/2 c  KAHLUA
1/2 c  WATER
3    EGGS, separated
1/4 c  SUGAR
1/8 ts SALT
16 oz CREAM CHEESE, softened
1 c  WHIPPING CREAM
1 1/2 c  ZWIEBACK COOKIES, crushed
1/3 c  SUGAR
1/3 c  BUTTER, melted
Preheat oven to 350-degrees
-F.
Blend all ingredients
-together. Press firmly on
-bottom and
Up sides halfway of a 9-inch
-springform pan.
** CRUST
