Title: Ruby Grapefruit
Categories: Fruit, Low-cal/fat, Dessert
Yield: 8 servings
4 tb Sugar
6    Grapefruit
10 oz Raspberries, frozen
Heat raspberries and sugar in small saucepan until berries are thawed
and juice flows. Puree in blender or food mill; strain to remove
seeds. Peel and section grapefruit. Pour raspberry sauce over
grapefruit sections. Chill and serve.

per serving: 123Cal, 0gFat, 30gCarb, 5mgNa, 0mgChol approximate cost:
$3

Evaluation by Deseret News: Refreshing and different- a wonderful
change from sweet stuff!  Your family will love it, especially if you
section the grapefruit for them!
