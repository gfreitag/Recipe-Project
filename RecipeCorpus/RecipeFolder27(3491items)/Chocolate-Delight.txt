Title: Chocolate Delight
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Creme de cacao or other
-- chocolate liqueur
2 tb Brandy
Whipped cream
2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Sweet ground chocolate
-- & cocoa mix; (try
-- ghirardelli)
2 tb Coffee-flavored liqueur
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:09 In a 1-qt glass measure, heat milk in a
microwave on High 3 to 4 mins, or until boiling. Whisk in cocoa mix,
coffee liqueur, creme de cacao, and brandy until well mixed. Serve in
2 or 3 coffee cups or mugs. Garnish with whipped cream. Serve
immediately.
