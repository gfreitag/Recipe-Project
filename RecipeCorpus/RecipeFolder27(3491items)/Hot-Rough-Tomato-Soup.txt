Title: Hot Rough Tomato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Indian, Appetizer, Vegetarian, Tomato
Yield: 6 servings
1 pn Thyme
2 sm Dried red chilies, chopped
6 ea Whole cloves
12 ea Peppercorns
3 ea Bay leaves
Salt
3 lg Ripe tomatoes
2 tb Ghee
1 lg Onion, coarsely chopped
1 sm Potato, cubed
3/4 c  Tomato paste
7 1/2 c  Vegetable stock
Skin the tomatoes & cut into chunks.  Melt ghee in pot over
medium-low heat & cook onion till it turns golden.  Add the rest of
the ingredients, adding enough salt to taste.  Simmer for 45 minutes.

Adapted from Ismail Merchant's "Indian Cuisine"
