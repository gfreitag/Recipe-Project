Title: Lemon Curry Chicken Soup ^
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/2 ts Curry powder
1 tb Parsley
6 tb Rice
8 c  Chicken stock
4    Eggs
Bring rice and chicken stock to a boil in a large pot. Reduce heat and
simmer 20 minutes, or until rice is tender. Beat eggs, lemon juice and
curry powder together. Add 3 tablespoons hot stock to egg mixture,
stir into remaining stock. Simmer, stirring constantly, 5 minutes.
Ladle soup into serving bowls, sprinkle with parsley and serve.
