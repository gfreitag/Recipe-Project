Title: Polo Loco Marinade III
Categories: Chicken, Marinade, Bbq/grill, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Orange juice
Mexican spice seasoning
1    Lemon; squeezed
1    Lime; squeezed
Cilantro
Pour over raw chicken and let soak for up to 4 hours and grill on the
bbq.. also you can add tequilla and its wonderful.
