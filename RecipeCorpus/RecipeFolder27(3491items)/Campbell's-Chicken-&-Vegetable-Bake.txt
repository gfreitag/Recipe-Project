Title: Campbell's Chicken & Vegetable Bake
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Casserole, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
2 cn Swanson Premium chicken
(5 oz each)
1 ds French's Worcesteshire sauce
1 cn Durkee French Fried onions
(2.8 oz)
1 cn Campbell's cream of chicken
Soup
1/2 c  Milk
3 c  Cooked cut up vegetables *
1/4 c  Cooked red pepper strips
(optional)
1. In 1 1/2 quart casserole dish, mix soup, milk, vegetables, chicken,
Worcestershire sauce and 1/2 can onions.

2. Bake at 350o for 25 minutes or until hot.

3. Stir; sprinkle top with remaining onions

4. Bake 5 minutes or until done.

*  Mixture of broccoli, caulflower, sliced carrot, green beans

Source: Campbell's ad
