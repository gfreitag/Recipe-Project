Title: *Low-fat Fettucini Alfredo
Categories: Pasta, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 2 servings
3 tb Grated parmesan cheese
1/8 c  Chopped fresh parsley
1 x  Pepper
6 oz Dry fettucini
1 c  Evaporated Skim milk
2 ea Garlic cloves
Cook pasta according to directions.  Drain.  Place pasta in a large
nonstick saute' pan along with milk and garlic.  Bring to a simmer,
stirring frequently.  Add cheese. Continue cooking until cheese melts
and sauce thickens.  Stir in parsley and black pepper. 179 Cal, 15g
Protein, 4g Fat, 23g Carb, 11mg Chol; and 320mg Sodium From: Lawrence
