Title: Magic Granola Bars
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 24 cookies
3/4 c  Buterscotch chips
1 c  Granola mix
15 oz Sweetened condensed milk
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 1/2 c  Crushed graham crackers
1 1/3 c  Coconut
3/4 c  Chocolate chips
Preheat oven to 350.  Put butter or margarine in a 13 x 9 baking pan.
Melt in preheating oven.  Sprinkle crushed graham crackers evenly
over melted butter or margarine. Sprinkle coconut evenly over crumbs.
In a small bowl, combine chocolate chips and butterscotch chips.
Sprinkle over coconut. Sprinkle with granola mix. Drizzle with
sweetened condensed milk. Bake about 25 minutes.  Cool.  Cut into 2"
squares. Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   02-11-95
