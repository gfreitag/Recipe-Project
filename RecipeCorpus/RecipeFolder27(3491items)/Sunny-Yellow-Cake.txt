Title: Sunny Yellow Cake
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 cake
2    Eggs, well beaten
1 t  Vanilla
3 1/3 c  Basic cake mix
3/4 c  Milk
MMMMM----------------ORANGE BUTTER CREAM FROSTING---------------------
1 1/2 t  Dry grated orange peel
3 T  Orange juice
1/3 c  Soft butter or margarine
3 c  Sifted powdered sugar
Preheat oven to 375.  Grease and lightly flour two 8" round cake mix.
In a large bowl, combine cake mix and milk. Beat at medium speed 2
minutes. Scrape bowl and beaters. Add eggs and vanilla. Beat 2 more
minutes. Pour into prepared pans.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes.  Cool in
pans 10 minutes, then cool on wire racks.  Prepare orange frosting.
Frost cake when cooled. VARIATION:  To make orange cake, add 1 tsp
dry grated orange peel to basic cake mix.  Substitute 1/2 tsp lemon
extract for vanilla. Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   02-09-95
