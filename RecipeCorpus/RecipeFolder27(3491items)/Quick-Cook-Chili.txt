Title: Quick-Cook Chili
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
10 3/4 oz Tomato soup, condensed
1 tb Chili powder; or as desired
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
16 oz Canned pinto beans
Heat beef and onion in a frypan until beef is browned and onion is
tender. Drain off excess fat.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Cover.
Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Calories per 3/4 cup serving: About 270
