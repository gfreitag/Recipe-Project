Title: Light Spinach Dip with Water Chestnuts - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizer, Pickles
Yield: 12 servings
-Miracle Whip
1/4 c  Dry vegetable soup mix,
-(Knorr 1.4oz)
2 t  Grated fresh onion
1 t  Lemon juice
1 cn (8oz) chopped and drained
-water chestnuts
1 ea Ct (12oz) carton nonfat
-cottage cheese
**OR**
1 ea Ct (12oz) fatfree sour cream
1 ea Pk (10oz) chopped spinach,
-thawed and drained
1 c  Kraft fatfree mayonnaise or
Process cottage cheese in food processor or blender until smooth.
Place in medium bowl and set aside. Press spinach between paper
towels until barely moist. Add spinach and remaining ingredients to
cheese mixture; stir well. Cover and refrigerate about 3 hours. Serve
with nonfat crackers or fresh vegetables. Per 1/4 cup serving: 61
cal., 0.4g fat (6%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 5g pro., 11g carb., 620mg
sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-446-67040-5 Entered by
