Title: Date and Nut Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chopped dates
3/4 c  Chopped pecans or walnuts
1 ts Vanilla
Confectioners sugar
2    Eggs
1 c  Confectioners sugar
1 tb Shortening, melted
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
The dates give a special flavor to this easy to make dessert.

Preheat oven to 325Ã¸. In a bowl, beat eggs until light. Add sugar and
shortening; blend well.

Sift flour and baking powder together and blend well. Stir in dates,
nuts and vanilla. Blend well.

Pour into greased 8 x 8-inch pan. Bake for 25 minutes. Cool on wire
rack. Cut in small squares or oblongs. Roll in confectioners sugar.

Store in covered container.

Yield: 18-24. Lois Boudreau, St. Louis, MO
