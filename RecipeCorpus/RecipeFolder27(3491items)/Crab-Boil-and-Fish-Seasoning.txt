Title: Crab Boil and Fish Seasoning
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------AMERICA-------------------------------
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Allspice berries
1 sm Piece of dried ginger
3    Bay leaves
1 ts Black peppercorns
1 ts Yellow mustard seeds
1 ts Dill seeds
1 ts Coriander seeds
Tie the spices in a muslin bag and add to the water for boiling
crabs. Or grind to a powder and use to spice fish.

The Complete Book of Spices by Jill Norman ISBN 0-140-23804-2 pg 109
