Title: Plain Muffins Ii
Categories: Muffin
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Milk
1    Egg, well beaten
2 c  Ready-mixed flour
3 tb Melted shortening
4 tb Sugar
Sift prepared flour, measure, and sift.  Combine egg, shortening,
milk, and sugar.  Add sifted flour and beat only until smooth. Fill
well-oiled muffin tins 2/3 full.  Bake in hot oven (425Ã¸ F) 15-20
