Title: Mashed Potatoes Diablo
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 ts Turmeric
1 ds Cayenne pepper or to taste
2 tb Parsley, chopped
8    Potatoes, peeled
1 c  Milk (soy or low-fat)
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 ts Lemon juice
Place potatoes in large saucepan with enough salted cold water to
cover. Boil until tender when pierced with a fork, about 30 minutes.
Drain.

Mash potaoes. mix in enough milk to make potatoes moist and fluffy.

Stir in remaining ingredients. Serve warm.
