Title: Whole Wheat Pancakes - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Breakfast
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Egg Beaters
1/2 c  Vanilla nonfat yogurt
1/4 c  Water
1 ea Tb liquid Butter Buds
Nonfat cooking spray
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
2 t  Baking powder
2 t  Sugar or 1 packet Sweet 'n
-Low
Combine flours, baking powder and sugar in a large bowl; make a well
in center of mixture. Combine Egg Beaters, yogurt, water and liquid
Butter Buds. Add to dry ingredients and mix until just moistened. For
each pancake, pour 3 tablespoons batter onto a onstick skillet that
has been sprayed with nonstick spray. Turn pancakes when tops are
covered with bubbles and edges look cooked. Per sugar serving: 60
cal., 0.2g fat (0%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 3g pro., 11g carb., 114mg
sod. Per S&L serving: 58cal., 10.8g carb. Butter Busters by Pam
Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 2-95.
