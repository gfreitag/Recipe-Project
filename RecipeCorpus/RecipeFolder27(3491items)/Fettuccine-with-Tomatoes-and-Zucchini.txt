Title: Fettuccine with Tomatoes and Zucchini
Categories: Beef, Pasta, Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 4 -6 serving
-Tomatoes (14 1/2 oz)
1 cn Tomato sauce (8 oz)
1 md Carrot, julienne cut
1 md Zucchini, cubed
6 oz Fettuccine pasta
1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2    Onion, chopped
1 ts Basil, crushed
1 cn Italian Recipe Stewed
Cook pasta as package directs; drain. In skillet, brown meat with
onion and basil. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, and carrots. Cook,
uncovered, over medium head 8 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add
zucchini; cover and cook 7 minutes or until zucchini is tender. Just
before serving, sppon sauce over hot pasta. Garnish with chopped
parsley, if desired. 4 to 6 servings.

Prep. time 6 min. Cook time 20 min.  (Del Monte is the source of this
recipe)

NOTES: When I made it I left out the carrots and zucchini. It was
DELICIOUS. My family loved it. They even found it would be wonderful
as a topping on pizza. You know what your family likes and can add or
detract like we did. Enjoy. Serve with french bread and a salad.
