Title: Avocado Salad
Categories: Vegetable, Salad, Fruit
Yield: 6 -8  servin
1/2 c  Pomegranate seeds (optional)
3 c  Mixed salad greens
2    Avocados
1/3 c  Walnuts, chopped
1 lg Pink grapefruit (or 1 cup
-canned grapefruit sections)
2 lg Navel oranges
1 c  Green grapes
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 c  Salad oil
1/4 c  Lemon juice or vinegar
1 ts Paprika
Section grapefruit and oranges; set aside.  Wash grapes and drain
well. Remove seeds from pomegranate; set aside.  In lrg salad bowl or
on individual salad plates, arrange salad greens.  Top with orange and
grapefruit sections.  Peel and slice avocado.  Arrange avocado slices
over citrus fruits.  Sprinkle with grapes, pomegranate seeds and
walnuts. To prepare dressing, combine all ingredients in a glass jar
or small mixing bowl.  Shake dressing well before serving with salad.
