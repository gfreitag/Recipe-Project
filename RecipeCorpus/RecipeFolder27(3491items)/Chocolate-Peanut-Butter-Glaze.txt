Title: Chocolate Peanut Butter Glaze
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
6 oz Semisweet chocolate
-chips
1/4 c  Powdered sugar;
-sifted
2 tb Butter; cut-up
2 tb Creamy peanut butter
3 tb Light corn syrup
1 ts Vanilla extract
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:07 In a 1-qt glass bowl, combine butter, peanut
butter, corn syrup, vanilla, and 2 Tbs water. Heat in a microwave
oven on High 1 to 1 1/2 mins, until butter is melted and mixture is
smooth when stirred. Add chocolate chips and stir until completely
melted and smooth. Stir in powdered sugar until blended. Refrigerate
10 mins, or until glaze is of spreading consistency.

From:  Dan Klepach
