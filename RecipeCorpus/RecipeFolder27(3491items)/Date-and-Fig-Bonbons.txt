Title: Date and Fig Bonbons
Categories: Candy, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Nuts
1 ea Powdered sugar
1 lb Prunes or raisins
1 lb Figs
1 lb Dates
Wash fruit thoroughly; put all ingredients through a meat chopper.
Shape mixture into balls; roll in powdered sugar.
