Title: Easiest Ever Peanut Butter Fudge
Categories: Microwave, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 16 servings
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
12 oz Semisweet chocolate morsels
12 oz Crunchy peanut butter
Put chocolate chips and peanut butter into a microwave-safe dish.
Microwave on high 1-2 minutes until chips have completely melted.
Add sweetened condensed milk; stir until mixture is of a uniform
consistency. Pour into a 8"x8" square pan lined with wax paper.
Refrigerate until firm. Cut into 1" pieces.
