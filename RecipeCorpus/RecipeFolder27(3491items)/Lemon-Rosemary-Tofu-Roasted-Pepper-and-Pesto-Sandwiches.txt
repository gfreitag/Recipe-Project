Title: Lemon-Rosemary Tofu-Roasted Pepper and Pesto Sandwiches
Categories: Sandwich, Vegetable, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
8 sl Whole grain bread
1    Recipe Lemon-Rosemary Tofu
1 lg Red pepper, roasted, peeled,
...and seeded
1/4 c  Pesto
Cut pepper into four large pieces.  Spread pesto on four slices of
bread, and top each with one piece of pepper.  Place one slice of
Lemon-Rosemary Tofu on top.  Cover each with a slice of bread.  Serve
with a crisp green salad.
