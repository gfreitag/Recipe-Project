Title: Elegant Low Calorie Flounder
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Onion powder
1 ts Lemon-pepper seasoning
Paprika
Lemon
8    Cherry tomatoes
4    Flounder fillets (4 oz each)
3 c  Chicken broth
4 c  Dry white wine
Shallots, finely chopped
Wrap filets around cherry tomatos, secure with toothpicks.  Poach
flounder filets in chicken broth and wine.  Sprinkle remaining
ingredients on filets.  Fish is done when flakes with fork, about 10
minutes.  Serve with lemon wedges.  Sprinkle with paprika.  Serves 4.
125 calories per serving. Submitted By PAT STOCKETT   On   02-09-95
