Title: Chicken Wings in Oyster Sauce ^
Categories: Appetizer, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Oyster sauce
1 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Water
3 tb Peanut oil
2 cl Garlic, halved
8    Chicken wings
2 tb Soy sauce
Preheat wok or frying pan; coat bottom and sides with oil. Rub bottom
and sides with garlic, then discard garlic. Disjoint wings into three
pieces. Add middles and tips of wings to pan and brown on both sides.
Add remaining pieces and brown also. Mix soy and oyster sauces
together and add to wings. Stir in sugar and water. Cover and cook
over medium low heat for 15 minutes. Serve.
