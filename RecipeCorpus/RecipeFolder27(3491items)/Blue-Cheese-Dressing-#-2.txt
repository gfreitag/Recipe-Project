Title: Blue Cheese Dressing # 2
Categories: Salad
Yield: 1 servings
1    Bunch scallions
1    Clove garlic
1 tb White vinegar
1 tb Olive oil
1 ts Worchestershire sauce
6 oz Cream cheese
1 ds Tabasco or cayenne
Black pepper to taste
4 oz Blue Cheese
1 c  Sour cream
Puree all, except cream cheese and blue cheese, in processor till
smooth. Combine this mixture with room temp cream cheese. Add blue
cheese without combining too throughly so large chunks remain intact.
Maria/Ho-Ho-Kus, N.J.
