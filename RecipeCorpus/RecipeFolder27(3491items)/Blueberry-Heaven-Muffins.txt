Title: Blueberry Heaven Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin, Fruit
Yield: 12 muffins.
1/3 c  Butter, melted
2 ts Lemon juice (fresh if avail)
1 ts Grated lemon rind (fresh if
-available)
1 1/2 c  Fresh blueberries (frozen ok
-too)
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Sour cream
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/4 ts Cinnamon
3 tb Butter
1/3 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

Sift together the first 4 ingredients.  Stir in the next 6 ingredients
until just moistened.  Fold in the blueberries.  Mix the topping
ingredients until crumbly.  Fill paper lined muffin tins ful with the
batter and sprinkle liberally with the topping.  Bake appx. 20
minutes.

You might want to make a double batch of topping for better coverage.
