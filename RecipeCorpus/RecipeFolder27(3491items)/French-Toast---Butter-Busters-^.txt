Title: French Toast - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Breakfast
Yield: 5 servings
2 ea Tb liquid Butter Buds
10 ea Sl white bread (Wonder
-fatfree)
l  Maple syrup
1 c  Egg Beaters
1/3 c  Skim ilk
1 t  Ground cinnamon
1 t  Vanilla
In a shallow pan or bowl, combine Egg Beaters, milk, cinnamon and
vanilla. In a nonstick skillet over medium heat in liquid Butter
Buds, place the pieces of bread that have been dipped in Egg Beaters
mixture. Brown 3 minutes on each side. Add more liquid Butter Buds if
needed. Serve with lite maple syrup. Per serving: 112 cal., 0g fat
(0%), 0mg chol., 6g fiber, 8g pro., 14g carb., 347mg sod. Butter
Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw
2-95.
