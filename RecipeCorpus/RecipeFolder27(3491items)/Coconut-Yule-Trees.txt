Title: Coconut Yule Trees
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains, Holiday
Yield: 2 dozen
2 oz Dark chocolate confectionary
-coating (to 4 oz.)
Green sugar and red-hot
-candies
3 c  Coconut, flaked
2 c  Confectioners' sugar
1/4 c  Butter/margarine, softened
1/4 c  Light cream
1 ts Almond extract
In a large bowl, combine the first 5 ingredients; mix well.  Drop by
tablespoonfuls onto a waxed paper-lined cookie sheet; chill for 1
hour. shape into trees.  In a double boiler or microwave safe bowl,
melt chocolate coating.  Dip trunks of trees and set on waxed paper
to harden. Decorate tops of trees with green sugar and red-hots.
