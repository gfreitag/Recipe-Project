Title: Soft Bean Tacos
Categories: Vegetable, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Chopped red onion
1/4 c  Picante sauce
2 tb No-oil Italian dressing
6    Whole grain flour tortillas
16 oz Black beans, canned or
Dried beans cooked and
Seasoned
1/2 c  Chopped celery
Mix together all ingredients, except the tortillas and chill. Preheat
oven to 250 degrees. Scoop 1/3 cup beans into flour tortilla and
roll. Place in baking dish and heat for 20 minutes. Serves 6
Calories: 186 Fat: 1.3 gm 6% fat
