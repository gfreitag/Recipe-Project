Title: Tomato and Broccoli Casserole - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 8 ----------
-chopped
1 c  Snipped parsley
1 t  Instant chicken bouillon
1/4 t  Lite salt, optional
1/4 t  Crushed oregano
1/4 t  Crushed basil
1 c  Grated nonfat Cheddar cheese
2 c  Frozen cooked chopped
-broccoli, drained
1 1/2 c  Cooked elbow macaroni*
1 md Onion, chopped
1 t  Minced garlic
2 ea Tb liquid Butter buds or
-fatfree chicken broth
5 md Tomatoes, peeled seeded and
Set aside the broccoli. In a 3-qt. saucepan, cook onion and garlic in
Butter Buds olr chicken broth. Add tomatoes, parsley, bouillon, salt,
oregano and basil. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and cover, simmering 3
minutes. Stir in pasta and 1/2 cup of the cheese. Add broccoli. Pour
into an 8" square pan that has been sprayed with a nonstick spray.
Bake uncovered at 375F. for 15 minutes. sprinkle with remaining
cheese and bake 5 minutes longer. Per serving: 82 cal., 0.8g fat
(8%), 0mg chol., 3g fiber, 5g pro., 16g carb., 178mg sod. Butter
