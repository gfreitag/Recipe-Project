Title: Steamed Leeks with Mustard Vinaigrette
Categories: Vegetable, Herbs/spice, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1    Recipe Steamed Leeks
MMMMM--------------------MUSTARD VINAIGRETTE-------------------------
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ts Sugar
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 tb Cider vinegar
1 tb Dijon mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1. Whisk vinegar, mustard, salt, pepper and sugar together in a small
bowl until well-blended; slowly whisk in olive oil so the vinaigrette
emulsifies.

2. Arrange leeks (warm or chilled) on plates, drizzle with
vinaigrette and serve.
