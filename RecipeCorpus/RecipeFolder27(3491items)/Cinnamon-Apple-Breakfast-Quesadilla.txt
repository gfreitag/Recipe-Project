Title: Cinnamon-Apple Breakfast Quesadilla
Categories: Kids, Breakfast, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Chunky applesauce, divided
1/4 c  Shredded Monterey Jack
Cheese
2    6-inch flour tortillas
1 ts Cinnamon-sugar, divided
2 tb Vanilla yogurt (optional)
1. Preheat oven to 400F. 2. Place one tortilla on ungreased baking
sheet. Spread half applesauce over tortilla; reserve half for
garnish. Sprinkle half of cinnamon-sugar over applesauce. Top with
cheese and second tortilla. Sprinkle remaining cinnamon-sugar on top.
3. Bake at 400F for 6-8 minutes or until golden brown. Cool 2
minutes. 4. Cut into squares and serve with reserved applesauce and
vanilla yogurt. *From September 1994 issue of Parenting magazine*
