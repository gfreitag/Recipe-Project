Title: Easy White Chocolate Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/4 c  Water
1 c  White chocolate; melted
1 pk White cake mix w/ pudding
4    Egg whites
Mix first 3 ingredients as usual. Measure chocolate and then melt.
Add to cake batter. Then follow directions on cake box as usual. This
is great with raspberry filling.
