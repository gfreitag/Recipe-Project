Title: Turkey Salad - Jeam Ramsay
Categories: Side dish, Salad, Fruit, Poultry
Yield: 2 servings
1 md Bread & butter pickle,
Finely chopped
2 tb Onions. red or white,
Chopped coursely
1/2 c  Salad dressing
1 c  Turkey, cooked and cubed
2 c  Iceburg lettuce, in bite-
Sized pieces
1/4 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Seedless grapes, halved
1/4 c  Pecans, finely chopped
Combine all ingredients except salad dressing in a large salad
bowl. make sure the cheese is mixed in and not in a large lump.

Add salad dressing and mix well.  Chill for a least 20 minutes
before serving.
