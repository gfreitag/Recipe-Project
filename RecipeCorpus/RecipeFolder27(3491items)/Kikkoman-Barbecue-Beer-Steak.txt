Title: Kikkoman Barbecue Beer Steak
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Onion, finely chopped
1/2 c  Kikkoman Soy Sauce
1/4 ts Black pepper
1 1/2 lb Beef sirloin steak
About 3/4" thick
1 cn Beer (7 oz)
Place steak in plastic bag. combine remaining 4 ingredients; pour over
steak. Press air out of bag; tie top securely. Refrigerate 8 hours or
overnight; turn bag over occasionally. Place steak on grill 4 to 5
inches from hot coals. Cook 8 to 10 minutes, for rare or to desired
doneness.

Source:"Eating Light with Kikkoman Soy Sauce"; a P.O.P. tag
