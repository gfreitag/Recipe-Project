Title: Baked Macaroni/Micro
Categories: Casserole, Cheese/eggs, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Mozerello cheese..Optional
1 1/2 c  Elbow macaroni
Salt & pepper and marg.
2    Eggs
Milk to 2 cups (with eggs)
4 oz Cheddar cheese..grated
Beat eggs in 2 cup measuring cup - add milk to make 2 cups.

In Corning ware dish...layer noodles, grated cheese, salt/pepper and
2 or 3 pats of margarine.   Add milk/egg mixture.

Bake on high for 20 minutes.   Stop at 10 mins and pull away from
sides -
so mixture will cook evenly.
