Title: SEE'S FUDGE
Categories: Candy, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Vanilla
2 c  Walnuts; chopped
4 1/2 c  White sugar
1 cn Evaporated milk
-ANDY MONDAY-CWMR26A
3 pk Chocolate chips
1 pt Marshmellow cream
1/2 lb Butter
Place in large bowl first five ingredients. Set aside.

Boil briskly for 9 minutes after coming to a boil, the sugar and
evaporated milk. Stirring, add to the first mixture and mix well. Put
in buttered shallow pan and refrigerate. Slice into squares and
serve. It will freeze and keep for months.
