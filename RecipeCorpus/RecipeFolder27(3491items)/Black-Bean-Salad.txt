Title: Black Bean Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 12 servings
2    Green onions, thin sliced
2 c  Oil
1/2 c  Honey
2    Cloves garlic, pressed
1    Lemon, juiced
1 c  White wine
1 ts Oregano
1 tb Parsley
2 c  French-cut green beans
3 c  Black beans, cooked
1 c  Garbanzo beans, cooked
1 c  Adzuki beans (or red
Beans), cooked
2    Onions, sliced
2    Green peppers, sliced
2    Stalks celery, sliced
Combine all ingredients in large bowl, mixing thoroughly.  Chill
overnight.
