Title: Velveeta Classic Nacho Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Salsa
2 tb Chopped cilantro
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
2 tb Margarine
1 lb Velveeta; cubed
Saute onions in margarine; reduce heat to low. Add remaining
ingredients; stir unti process cheese spread is melted. Serve hot
with tortilla chips or vegetable dippers, if desired.
