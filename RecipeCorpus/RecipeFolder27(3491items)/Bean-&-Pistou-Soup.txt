Title: Bean & Pistou Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 4 SERVINGS--------------------------
2 T  Pesto sauce
2 T  Parmesan; grated
Pepper, black
2 c  Chicken broth
2 cn White beans, 19 oz; rinsed
-& drained
Put broth and beans in a saucepan over moderate heat and bring to a
boil. Reduce heat to low and stir in the remaining ingredients.
Simmer about 1 minute. Nutrition information per serving: Calories:
496 Protein: 29.4 grams   Fat: 11.5 grams Cholesterol: 3.9 mg Fiber:
22.1 grams Sodium: 483 mg [ MODERN MATURITY; April/May 1990 ] per
Fred Peters
