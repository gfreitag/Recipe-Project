Title: Mounds Cheesecake
Categories: Cheesecakes
Yield: 6 servings
Large bowl, microwave on
-high for 2 minutes
1 c  SUGAR
5    JUMBO EGGS, shelled and
-warmed 25 seconds in
Microwave
1/2 c  HEAVY CREAM
1/4 c  CORNSTARCH
1 ts COCONUT EXTRACT
1 c  ALMONDS, coarsely chopped
2 c  VANILLA WAFER CRUMBS
1 1/2 c  COCONUT
5 tb BUTTER, melted
Mix and press into bottom
-and sides of 10-inch
-springform
Pan.
CAKE
2 tb CREAM CHEESE, remove from
-wrapper, put in
** CRUST
