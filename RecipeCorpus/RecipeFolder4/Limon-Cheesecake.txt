Title: Limon Cheesecake
Categories: Cheesecakes
Yield: 6 servings
4    (8 oz) pkg CREAM CHEESE,
-softened
4 lg EGGS
13    LEMON SANDWICH COOKIES (or
-vanilla)
3 tb BUTTER, melted
Crush cookies, mix well with    1 3/4 c  SUGAR
3 tb FLOUR
3 oz LEMON JUICE (fresh)
3 oz LIME JUICE (fresh)
-butter and pat firmly into
-10"
Springform pan.
FILLING
** CRUST
