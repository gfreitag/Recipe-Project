Title: Fast Meat Loaf Patties
Categories: Meatloaf, Ground beef, Beef
Yield: 8 servings
1    Egg
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1    Dash salt & pepper
2 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Diced onions
1/2 c  Oat meal
1/2 c  Milk
Mix into patties of desired size (at least 1/2 inch thick). Place to short
strips of bacon on top and bake @ 375 degrees for about 25 minutes.
