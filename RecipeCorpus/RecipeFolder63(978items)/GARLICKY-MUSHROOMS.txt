Title: GARLICKY MUSHROOMS
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Sliced mushrooms
4 tb Olive oil
7 lg Cloves fresh garlic, sliced
3 tb Chopped parsley
Salt and pepper to taste
Saute mushrooms in olive oil, covered, about 15 minutes. Uncover; add
garlic, parsley, salt and pepper. Simmer until liquid evaporates. Do
not let garlic brown. Serve and enjoy.
