Title: GERI'S HONEY CURRY CHICKEN WINGS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Chicken wings
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Prepared mustard
1 ts Salt
1 tb Curry (mild or hot)
place chicken in shallow baking pan, skin side up. Combine butter,
honey, mustard, salt, & curry powder and mix well. Pour over chicken
and bake at 350F for 1 1/4 hour's basting every 15 min's.
