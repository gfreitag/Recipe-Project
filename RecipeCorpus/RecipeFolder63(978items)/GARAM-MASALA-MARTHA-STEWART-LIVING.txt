Title: GARAM MASALA-MARTHA STEWART LIVING
Categories: Herb/spice, Soup/stews
Yield: 1 /4 cup
1 ts Coriander seeds
1 1/2 ts Cumin seeds
1 ts Cardamom seeds
2    Cinnamon sticks
3    Bay leaves
1 1/2 ts Peppercorns
1/2 ts Whole cloves
1 ts Ground mace
1. Place all the seeds together in a saute pan over medium heat and
dry-roast, shaking pan often, until they give. off an aroma, about 5
minutes.

2. Combine all ingredients in a mortar or a clean coffee grinder and
grind to a powder. Store in an airtight container.

Martha Stewart Living/Feb. & March/94 Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
