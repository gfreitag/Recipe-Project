Title: GAJAR HALVA
Categories: Desserts, Indian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Carrots
1 pt Half and Half
1 1/2 c  Sugar
4    Cardamom
Raisins; handful
Cashew nuts; handful
Add a little butter to a frying pan and heat to coat the pan. Roast
cashew nuts until golden brown and add the raisins to the pan for a
few seconds. Remove the cashews and raisins and keep aside.

Grate the carrots and add to the pan.  Add half and half, and heat for
about an hour, starting with high heat, stirring, and lowering the
heat after the mixture starts boiling. Heat until almost dry.

Add sugar, mix, and continue to cook until damp/dry.

Remove from stove and add cashews and raisins and cardamoms.
