Title: FRUIT SYRUP
Categories: Breakfast, Sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Dried apples
1 c  Brown sugar
1/4 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Margarine
1 c  Water
Recipe by: "Backpacker's Cookbook" by Margaret Cross and Jean Fiske 1.
Simmer the apples in the water until they are rehydrated. 2. Add
remaining ingredients and simmer until sugar is mellted and thickened.
