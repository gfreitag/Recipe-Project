Title: GADO GADO
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz White cabbage
1/4 c  Sesame oil
1 lg Onion, cut in fourths,
-thinly sliced
1    Green bell pepper, seeded,
-thinly sliced
6 oz Fresh bean sprouts
1    Fresh green chili, seeded,
-finely chopped
1    Garlic clove, crushed
2    Shallots, finely chopped
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/3 c  Smooth peanut butter
3 tb Lemon juice
3 dr Hot-pepper sauce
1/3 c  Water
Red bell pepper strips (opt)
Finely shred cabbage, discarding stalk.

Heat 2 tablespoons of sesame oil in a skillet. Add cabbage, onion,
thinly sliced bell pepper, bean sprouts and chili and fry over fairly
high heat 3-4 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat, spoon
mixture into a serving dish; cool.

To make sauce, heat remaining sesame oil in saucepan. Add garlic,
shallots and cumin and fry gently 5 minutes. Add peanut butter and
cook gently 2 minutes. Stir in lemon juice, hot-pepper sauce and
water and heat through gently to form a fairly thick sauce. Garnish
sauce with bell pepper strips, if desired, and serve with cooled
vegetables.
