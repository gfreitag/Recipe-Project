Title: GARDEN BEAN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Beans, Vegetables
Yield: 16 Servings
1 cn Green beans; cut (16 oz can)
2 cn Lima beans; (17 oz ea)
1 cn Kidney beans; (16 oz)
1 cn Wax beans; (16 oz)
1 cn Garbanzo beans; (15 oz)
1 lg Green pepper; chopped
3    Celery stalks; chopped
2 oz Green pimiento;
-sliced, drained
1    Green onion bunch; sliced
2 c  Vinegar
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
Drain all cans of beans; place in a large bowl.  Add green pepper,
celery, pimiento and green onions; set aside. Bring remaining
ingredients to a boil in a heavy saucepan; boil for 5 minutes. Remove
from heat and immediately pour over vegetables. Refrigerate several
hours or overnight.
