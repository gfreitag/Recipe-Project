Title: FUZZY FRUIT SODAS
Categories: Beverages, Better
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM---------------------CHEAPER AND BETTER--------------------------
1 tb Frozen juice concentrate
8 oz Club soda
** The healthiest alternative possible to soda. Pour club soda into a
glass and swirl in a T of fruit juice concentrate. Taste and add a
bit more concentrate if necessary. Big savings.
