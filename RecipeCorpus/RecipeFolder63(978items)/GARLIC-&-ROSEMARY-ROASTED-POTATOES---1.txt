Title: GARLIC & ROSEMARY ROASTED POTATOES - 1
Categories: Side dishes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb New potatoes
1 tb Fresh rosemary
1 ts Salt
3    Cl Garlic; chopped
Olive oil cooking spray
Recipe by: George McTyre <MCTYREG@baylor.edu> Wash and srub potatoes.
Cut in potatoes in half. Spray a baking dish with t with cooking
spray. Sprinkle potatoes with garlic, rosemary, and salt and stir to
mix well. Bake uncovered for 20 to 35 minutes at 400 degrees,
stirring occasionally.
