Title: FUDGE CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 c  Brown Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Salt
2    Eggs
5 tb Cocoa
5 tb Milk
1 c  Flour
In saucepan put the 5 tbs. cocoa and 5 tbs milk and bring to a boil
stirring constantly, remove from heat and cool. Cream butter and
sugar, add beaten eggs. Add the 1/2 cup milk and 1/2 cup flour. Add
the cocoa mixture then the flour, baking powder and salt. Add
vanilla. Pour into greased and floured 9 in. layer cake pans. Bake in
350 oven 30 - 35 min. FILLING:
1/2 c  white sugar
1 1/2    tbs. cocoa
1    heaping tbs. flour
1 c  hot water
1    tbs. butter Cook until thick and cool. Cut each cake layer
in two and put some filling between each. On top sprinkle some icing
sugar.
