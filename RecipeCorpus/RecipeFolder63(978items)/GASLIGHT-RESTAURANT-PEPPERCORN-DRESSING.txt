Title: GASLIGHT RESTAURANT PEPPERCORN DRESSING
Categories: Salads
Yield: 10 Servings
1 oz Parmesan cheese
3/4 tb Black pepper
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/2    Lemon, juiced
1/2 tb MSG
1/4 tb Tabasco sauce
1/2 tb Worchestershire sauce
3/4 tb Vinegar
1 qt Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Cold water
Mix all together well, and refrigerate.
