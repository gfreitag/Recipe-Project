Title: GARLIC SHRIMP AU GRATIN
Categories: Garlic, Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Raw shrimp
1 1/2    Sticks butter
2 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
1/2 c  Finely chopped parsley
4    Cloves fresh garlic, pressed
Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Dry sherry
Shell and devein shrimp.  Toss into boiling water, return to boil and
cook for about 2 minutes until shrimp turn all pink. Drain. In large
skillet melt 1 stick butter (1/2 cup) over low heat. Add bread
crumbs, parsley, garlic and salt and pepper.  Stir a few minutes over
low heat; pour in sherry and cook 1 minute more. Place alternate
layers of shrimp and bread crumbs in well-buttered gratin dish,
ending with bread crumbs. Dot with remaining butter.  Bake at 350
degrees for 10 to 15 minutes.
