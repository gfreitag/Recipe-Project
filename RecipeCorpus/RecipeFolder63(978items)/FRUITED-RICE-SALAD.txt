Title: FRUITED RICE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Uncooked long grain rice
1 cn (11-oz) mandarin oranges
Juice reserved
1 c  Seedless red grapes each
Cut in half
1/3 c  Golden raisins
1/3 c  Sliced natural almonds
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Cider vinegar
1 ts Dijon style mustard
1 tb Chopped parsley
Lettuce leaves
Prepare rice according to package directions for microwave cooking.
Refrigerate rice uncovered until cooled, about 15 minutes. Meanwhile,
in large serving bowl, combine orange segments, grapes, raisins and
almonds. In small bowl, whisk together 2 Tb reserved juice, oil,
vinegar, mustard, parsley, salt and pepper. Add rice to fruit
mixture. Pour salad dressing over salad; toss. Garnish with lettuce
leaves.
