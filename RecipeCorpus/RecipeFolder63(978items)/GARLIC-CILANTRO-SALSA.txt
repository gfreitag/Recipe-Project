Title: GARLIC CILANTRO SALSA
Categories: Salsa, Vegetables, Mexican
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Cloves Garlic, chopped
3    Jalapeno Chiles, chopped
4    Tomatoes, chopped
1 ts Cumin
1/2 c  Cilantro, chopped
1 ds Lemon Juice
1 ds Salt
Combine all ingredients and whirr until smooth in a blender or food
processor. Makes 2/3 cup.

Source: San Francisco Chronicle
