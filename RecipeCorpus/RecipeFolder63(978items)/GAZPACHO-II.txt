Title: GAZPACHO II
Categories: Soups
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ea Garlic clove, mashed
1 ea Onion, peeled chopped
5 ea Tomatoes, ripe, peeled
1 c  Beef bouillon
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Vinegar
2 tb Parsley, chopped
1 ds Paprika
1 ea Green pepper, medium  choppe
Combine garlic and onion in blender and blend till smooth. Add the
remaining ingredients; cover the container and blend the mixture till
it is smooth. Chill soup thoroughly. With the soup, serve in separate
dishes: cucumber, green pepper, tomato, onion (all finely chopped)
and croutons.
