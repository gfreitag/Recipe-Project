Title: FUN CLAY
Categories: Crafts, Kids, Play dough
Yield: 1 Batch
-Susan Klapper KCXJ08A
1 lb Baking Soda
1 1/4 c  Water
1 c  Cornstarch
Combine ingredients in saucepan.  Using medium heat and stirring
constantly, cook until mix is doughlike. Refrigerate until cool; then
knead a few minutes. Roll on floured waxed paper .
