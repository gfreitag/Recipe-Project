Title: GARLIC SAFFRON MAYONNAISE
Categories: New, Text, Import
1 sm clove garlic
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 ts saffron dissolved in rice
vinegar
:
1 TB pimientos
1/2 c  mayonnaise, -- to taste
Rice vinegar, to taste
Salt
Crushed red pepper
Water, optional
:
:
:
:

In a food processor or blender puree clove garlic, saffron dissolved
in some rice vinegar;, and pimientos. Add mayonnaise to taste. Add
more rice vinegar to taste and season with salt and crushed red
pepper. Thin with water if you wish.
