Title: GARDEN-GOOD GREEN BEANS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Green Beans
1 md Onion-sliced
3 tb Butter
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ts Chicken Bouillion
1/4 c  Water
Snip ends off green beans; halve lenghtwise, then crosswise.
Saute onion in butter until soft in a medium saucepan. Stir in
beans; toss until shiny moist.
Stir in remaining ingredients; heat to boiling; cover.
Cook 10 minutes or until beans are just crisply tender and serve.
