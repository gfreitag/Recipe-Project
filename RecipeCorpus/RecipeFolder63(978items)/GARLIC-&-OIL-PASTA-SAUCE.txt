Title: GARLIC & OIL PASTA SAUCE
Categories: French, Garlic, Sauces, Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Olive oil
15 lg Garlic cloves; chopped
1/4 ts Hot red pepper flakes
1/2 c  Parsley, fresh; finely
-chopped
Salt
Pepper, black
1/4 c  Parmesan; grated
Heat oil in skillet.  Add garlic, pepper flakes, and parsley and cook
until garlic is tender, about 4 mins. Stir in salt and pepper and
toss with hot pasta in a serving bowl. Mix in cheese, if used.

Recommended pasta:  3/4 pound spaghetti or spaghettini
