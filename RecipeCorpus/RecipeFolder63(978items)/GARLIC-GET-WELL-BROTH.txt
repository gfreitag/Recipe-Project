Title: GARLIC GET WELL BROTH
Categories: Soups, Garlic
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Onion; chopped
4    Garlic clove; peeled
1/2 ts Ginger; grated
1/2 c  ;Water
Bouquet garni
Salt; to taste
Pepper; to taste
Boil all until garlic and onion are soft.  Strain and serve.
