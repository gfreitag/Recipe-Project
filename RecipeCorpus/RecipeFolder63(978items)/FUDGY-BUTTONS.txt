Title: FUDGY BUTTONS
Categories: Snacks, Desserts
Yield: 1 Dozen
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 1/2 ts Baking cocoa
1/2 c  Confectioners' sugar
1/2 ts Milk
2 tb Creamy peanut butter
In a small saucepan, melt the butter; remove from the heat. Add cocoa
and mix well.  Stir in sugar.  Add milk and stir until smooth. Add
peanut butter and mix well. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper;
flatten tops and shape into 1 inch patties.  Yield about 1 1/2 dozen.
