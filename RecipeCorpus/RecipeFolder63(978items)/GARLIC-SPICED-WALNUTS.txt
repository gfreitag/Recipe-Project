Title: GARLIC SPICED WALNUTS
Categories: Grains
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Allspice
5 c  Water
1 lb Walnut halves
4 tb Melted butter
3/4 ts Garlic salt
Add ginger and allspice to water and bring to a boil. Drop in walnuts
and boil about 3 minutes.  Drain well. Spread in a shallow pan and
bake in 350 degree oven for 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
Remove from oven and toss with melted butter and garlic salt.
