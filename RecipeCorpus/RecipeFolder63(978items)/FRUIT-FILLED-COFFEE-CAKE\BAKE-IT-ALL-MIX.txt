Title: FRUIT-FILLED COFFEE CAKE\BAKE IT ALL MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Bake it all mix
1    Egg
1/2 c  Milk
3 tb Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Marmalade -- * see note
1/4 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
*Or apricot or berry jam. Do not pack mix when measuring. Break egg
into bowl; beat.  Add milk, Mix and granulated sugar. Mix thoroughly.
Spread 1/2 of the batter in an 8-inch round layer cake pan, bottom
rubbed with shortening. Spread marmalade over batter. Combine brown
sugar, raisins and cinnamon and sprinkle on top of marmalade. Drop
remaining batter by teaspoonfuls around outer edge of pan.  Bake in a
350 F. oven 35 to 40 minutes.    Yield:  One 8-inch round cake
