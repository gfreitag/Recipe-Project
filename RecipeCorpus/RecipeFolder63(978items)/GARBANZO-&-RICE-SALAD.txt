Title: GARBANZO & RICE SALAD
Categories: Main dish, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Drained garbanzos
2 c  Diced grn. peppers
1/2 c  Red onion, diced
1/2 ts Dillweed
2 c  Cooked brown rice
2 md Tomatoes, coarsely diced
4 tb Wine vinegar
-salt and pepper, optional
Combine all ingredients.  Toss well and refrigerate overnight. Toss
several times during refrigeration period and just before serving.
