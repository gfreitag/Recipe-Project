Title: FRUIT SMOOTHIES
Categories: Digest, Mar95
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lg Can of diced pineapples in
Their own juice
1 c  Finely sliced strawberries
1 ea Crushed ice as you wish (I
Like 3or4cups)
Put all ingredients in the blender and blend on high for 3 to 4
minutes or until smooth.  I use this recipe almost every other day
substituting all different types of fruit. Try it first thing in the
morning, it is a great pick - me - up!!
