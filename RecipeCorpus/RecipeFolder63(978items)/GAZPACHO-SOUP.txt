Title: GAZPACHO SOUP
Categories: Soups, Vegetarian
Yield: 5 Servings
15 oz EDEN Crushed Tomatoes
14 1/2 oz EDEN Diced Tomatoes
1 c  Water
2 tb Onion, minced
1    Green pepper; diced
1    Red pepper; diced
2    Cucumbers; peeled, seeded
-- and cut in chunks
1 bn Green onions; thinly sliced
2    Cloves garlic
-- peeled and pressed
3 tb EDEN Red Wine Vinegar
2 tb EDEN Shoyu
1 ts Cumin
3 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Lime juice
2 ts Basil
2 ts Dill
Combine all ingredients and chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
Great with cornbread!
