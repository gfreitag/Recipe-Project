Title: FRUIT-BOWL DRINK
Categories: Fruit, Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/4 c  Orange juice
1 c  Watermelon Chunks, seeded
1/2 c  Fresh raspberries
1/2 c  Fresh strawberries
1/2 c  Fresh pineapple chunks
1/4 c  Unsweetened applesauce
1    Kiwi fruit, peeled
1/2 md Pear
Combine all of the ingredients in a food processor or blender and
process until thick and smooth.  Serve the drink over ice in 4 tall
glasses.

Source: Time Life Books
