Title: FRUIT SALSA
Categories: Sauces, Mexican, Lowcal
Yield: 12 Servings
1 sm Ripe papaya, peeled seeded
Diced
1    Firm small banana diced
2    Green onions minced
3 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
3 tb Lime juice
2    Jalapeno pepper minced
Combine all ingredients and serve at room temperature Light Cooking
