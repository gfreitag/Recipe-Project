Title: GARLIC PASTA CHICKEN SALAD *
Categories: Salads, Pasta, Chicken
16 oz Garlic seasoned pasta accent
Yield: 2 Servings
- Green Giant frozen veggies
1 1/2 c  Chicken; cooked, cubed
1/4 c  Italian salad dressing
1/3 c  Croutons

Cook vegetables as directed on package till vegetables are
crisp-tender. Cover; refrigerate about 1 hour. In large bowl, combine
chicken, dressing, and pasta-vegetable mix; toss to coat. Garnish
with croutons
