Title: FUDGE (MICROWAVE)
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts
Yield: 32 Servings
2 c  Miniature marshmallows
14 oz Can condensed milk
1 ds Salt
12 oz Semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 c  Milk chocolate chips
1/2 c  Nuts
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
In  2 qt. glass measure, combine marshmallows, milk, and  salt.
Microcook on HIGH 3-4 minutes.  Stir until  marshmallows  melt and
mixture is smooth. Add chips and stir until melted.   Stir in nuts
(may use up to 1 cup) and vanilla.

Line  8  or  9 inch square pan with wax  paper.   Spread  fudge
evenly in pan.  Chill 2 hours, until firm.  Turn on to  cutting
board, peel off paper and cut into squares.

Serves 32
