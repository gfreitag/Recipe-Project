Title: GATOR JUICE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
4 ea Peeled kiwi
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Lime juice
9 oz Unsweetened pineapple juice
2 c  Sparkling water
Place all ingredients into blender with crushed ice. Blend for 1
minute. Makes 4 servings.

Source: Adapted from Just For Kids by Jen Bays Avis, R.D. and Kathy F
Ward, R.D.; as published in the Oregonian FoodDay; typos by Dorothy
Flatman 1996
