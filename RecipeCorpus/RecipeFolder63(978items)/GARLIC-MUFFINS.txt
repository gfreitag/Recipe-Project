Title: GARLIC MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins, Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Milk, 1 egg, 3 Tbsp melted
-margarine, 2-3 cloves
-crushed garlic
2 tb Chopped chives, 1 Tbsp
-sugar, 4 tsp baking powder,
-2 cups flour
Reheat oven to 375oF, prepare pans. Put the first six ingredients
into a bowl & mix well. Add the remaining ingredients & blend until
just mixed. Spoon into pans and bake for 15-20 mins. Makes 12
muffins. Note: Use these muffins in place of garlic bread. Lovely
with soups etc.
