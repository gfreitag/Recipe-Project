Title: GANACHE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Cream
8 oz Semi-sweet chocolate, chop
4 tb Butter, softened
Bring cream to a boil. Transfer to medium bowl and immediately add
choc. l smooth. Let cool slightly. Whisk in butter by teaspoonfuls.
Source: newsletter Feb '90, newspaper Dolores McCann
