Title: GARLIC & CHEESE TOAST (PARMESAN AIOLI)
Categories: None
Yield: 16 Slices
1 c  Light mayonnaise
2 md Cloves garlic, peeled,
-forced through a press
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
16    Slices ( 1/4-inch thick)
-French or Italian bread
1. Combine the mayonnaise, garlic and Parmesan.

2. Lay the bread slices on a baking sheet and toast on one side under
a hot broiler.

3. Turn and spread the garlic mayonnaise on the toast and broil until
golden and bubbly.

Nutrition information per slice: 156 calories, 4 g protein, 6 g fat
(2 g saturated), 19 g carbohydrate,
345 mg sodium, 1 mg cholesterol.
