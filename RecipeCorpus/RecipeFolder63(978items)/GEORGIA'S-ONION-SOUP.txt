Title: GEORGIA'S ONION SOUP
Categories: New, Text, Import
4    onions, -- sliced
Yield: 4 Servings
4 TB butter
4 c  vegetable stock
1 c  white wine
Salt
Pepper
:
:
2 TB fresh herbs, -- chopped
Cornbread
Cheese
:
:

In large sauce pan place onion and butter. Saute until soft. Add
stock, wine, salt, pepper and herbs. Simmer for about 35 minutes. In
serving bowl place cornbread and sprinkle with cheese. Pour over
soup. Serve.
