Title: GERMAN BEEF
Categories: Beef, German
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Stew beef
1/4 c  Vinegar
2 tb Sugar
1    Onion -- chopped
1 cn Mushroom
1 cn Beef broth
Brown the stew beef.  Add remaining ingredients except mushrooms.
Simmer for 2 hours. Add mushrooms. Thicken and serve over noodles.
From Deb Bisson-Devost
