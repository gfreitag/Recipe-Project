Title: BAKED CHICKEN & POTATOES
Categories: Poultry, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1    3-4 pound fryer, cut into
-serving pieces
6    Potatoes, peeled & quartered
1 c  Crushed tomatoes
2 tb Parsley
1 ts Basil
2    Cloves garlic, minced
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
3 tb Vegetable oil
Wash chicken and wipe dry with paper toweling.  Put enough oil in a
large roasting pan to cover bottom of the pan. Place chicken in p;an
and place potatoes between the chicken pieces.

Sprinkle tomatoes, parsley, basil, garlic, salt and pepper over the
chicken and potatoes.  Lastly, sprinkle with the grated cheese and
the oil.
