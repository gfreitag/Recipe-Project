Title: BAILEY'S CHOCOLATE CHIP CHEESECAKE
Categories: Cheesecakes, Chocolate, Cakes
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  GRAHAM CRACKER CRUMBS
1/4 c  SUGAR
6 tb BUTTER
Preheat oven to 325-degrees.
Combine crumbs and sugar in
-pan. Stir in butter. Press
-into
Bottom and 1" up sides of
-pan.
Bake 7 minutes.
Maintain oven temperature at
-325-degrees.
FILLING
2 1/2 lb CREAM CHEESE, room
-temperature
1 2/3 c  SUGAR
5    EGGS, room temperature
1 c  BAILEY'S IRISH CREAM
1 tb VANILLA
1 c  CHOCOLATE CHIPS
** CRUST
