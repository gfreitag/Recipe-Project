Title: B-B-Q MEATBALLS & SMOKIES (GREAT)
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 10 Servings
1 1/2 lb Hamburger
5 sl Bread
1/2 c  Water
2 ts Chives
1 tb Salt
Pepper to taste
1/4 ts Celery salt
1 ts Garlic salt
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
1/2 ts Onion salt
6 ds Worcestershire sauce
4 ds Hot sauce
2    Eggs
1 1/2 lb Cocktail smokies(or more)
MMMMM------------------------B-B-Q SAUCE-----------------------------
16 oz Tomato sauce
1/4 c  Butter
1 md Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Catsup
3 ts B-b-q spice
5 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Vinegar
10 ds Hot sauce
2 ts Liquid smoke
1 1/2 c  Chili sauce
Combine hamburger and the next 12 ingredients.  Shape into small
meatballs and saute until done.  Set aside

Combine Sauce ingredients and cook until thick.
