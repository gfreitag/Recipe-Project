Title: BACON & CHEESE EGGS
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Hard cooked eggs
3 tb Mayonnaise
2 sl Crisp bacon
2 ts Vinegar
1/4 c  Grated cheddar
1/8 ts Salt
Mash egg yolks and stir with bacon, cheese, mayonnaise, vinegar and
salt. Fill egg white halves. Top with crumbled bacon.
