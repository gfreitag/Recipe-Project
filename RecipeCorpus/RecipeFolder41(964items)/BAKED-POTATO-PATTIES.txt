Title: BAKED POTATO PATTIES
Categories: Diabetic, Vegetarian, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
6 md Potatoes, scrubbed
2 tb Onion; minced
1 tb Fresh parsley; chopped
1/8 ts Freshly ground pepper
2 tb Whole wheat pastry flour
Preheat the oven to 375.

Shred the potatoes coarsely.  Mix with the onion, parsley, pepper, and
flour.  Shape into 4 patties and place on a nonstick baking sheet.
Bake for 30 minutes. If you like, place the patties under the broiler
during the last few minutes for browning.
