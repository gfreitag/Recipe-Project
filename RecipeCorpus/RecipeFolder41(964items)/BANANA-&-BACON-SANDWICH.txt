Title: BANANA & BACON SANDWICH
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Crushed bananas
1    Apple
4    Bacon slices
1    Salt and lettuce
Crush banana, chop crisped bacon and mince apple. Mix, add salt,
spread between buttered slices of bread and add lettuce leaf.
