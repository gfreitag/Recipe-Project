Title: BAKED HUSH PUPPIES
Categories: Breads
Yield: 24 Servings
2/3 c  Yellow Cornmeal
1/3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking Powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Minced Onion
1/3 c  Skim Milk
1 tb Vegetable Oil
1/8 ts Pepper
1    Egg Beaten
Combine Cornmeal, Flour, Baking Powder & Salt in A Medium Bowl; Make
A Well in The Center Of Mixture. Combine Onion, Milk, Oil, Pepper &
Egg.Add To Dry Ingredients, Stirring Just Until Moistened. Spoon 1 T.
Batter Into Minature Muffin Pans Coated With Cooking Spray. Bake At
450 For 10 Minutes OR Until Lightly Browned. Remove From Pan
Immediately.
(Fat 1. Chol. 11.)
