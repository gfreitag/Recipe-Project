Title: BANANA BREAD DIP
Categories: Desserts, Dips, Fruits, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Ripe Bananas; Mashed
1 ts Lemon Juice
1 ts Vanilla Extract
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon; Ground
1/4 ts Nutmeg; Ground
1/2 c  Quick Oatmeal; Uncooked, *
*   Do NOT use the instant packets for the oatmeal. Use the Quick
Oats. +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++ Mix the bananas and lemon juice, blending well.
Add all of the other ingredients, blending well. Serve immediately.
Makes about 2 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS:  Fresh Or Dried Fruit,
Chocolate Wafers, Pound Cake Cubes, Vanilla Wafers
