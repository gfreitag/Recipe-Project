Title: BACON DROP BISCUITS+++FGGT98B
Categories: Master mix, Biscuits, Jewel
Yield: 9 Servings
1 1/2 c  Bake it all mix*
1/2 c  Milk
2    Or 3 strips bacon
*Do not pack
Cut 2 to 3 strips of bacon into small pieces. Panfry. Drain. Add
to Mix of Baking Powder Drop Biscuit recipe before blending in milk.
Yield: 9 to 10 biscuits
