Title: BAKED CHICKEN COATING
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Cornmeal
2 tb Dry mustard
2 tb Paprika
1 tb Garlic powder
1 tb Celery salt
1 tb Pepper
1 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Tyme
1/2 ts Oragano
1    Broiler chicken cut up
Mix all dry ingredients and put in bag. Coat pieces of chicken in this
mixture. Place on greased shallow baking pan and bake in 350 oven
45-60 minutes.
