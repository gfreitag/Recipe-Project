Title: BAKED 7 BEANS & BARLEY
Categories: Main dish, Side dish
Yield: 1 Recipe
3 c  Cooked AM 7 Bean & Barley
-- (drained)
1/2 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Tomato puree
1/2 c  Water
3 tb Honey
1/4 c  Diced onion
1 ts Sea salt (optional)
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Turmeric
1 ds Black pepper (optional)
Combine all ingredients and mix well.  Pour into a baking dish and
bake in a preheated 350 F. oven for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
