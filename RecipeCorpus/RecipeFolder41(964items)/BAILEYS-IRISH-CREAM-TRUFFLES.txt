Title: BAILEYS IRISH CREAM TRUFFLES
Categories: Chocolate, Candies, Alcohol
Yield: 16 Servings
12 oz Semi-sweet choc morsels
1/4 c  Heavy cream
1 tb Sweet Butter
2    Egg yolks
1/4 c  Baileys Irish Cream
Melt chocolate, Baileys and heavy cream together over very low heat.
Whisk in yolks, one at a time; mixture will thicken. Whisk in butter.
Refrigerate overnight, or until firm. With spoon make small balls.
Roll in powdered sugar, cocoa, chopped nuts, sprinkles, etc.
