Title: BAKED DOUGH - FOR MOLDING
Categories: Kids, Crafts
Yield: 1 Batch
4 c  Flour
1 c  Salt
1 1/2    To 2 cups water
Condensed milk
SOURCE:  Posted on NVN - Food & Wine Board by Syd Kerr - note
#18580.. MM format by Ursula R. Taulor.
This is for molding and then saving.
Combine flour, salt, and water.  Shape.  Brush with condensed milk.
Bake at 250~ for 1 hour.
