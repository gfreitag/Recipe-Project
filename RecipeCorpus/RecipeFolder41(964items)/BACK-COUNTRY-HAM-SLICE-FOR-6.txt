Title: BACK COUNTRY HAM SLICE FOR 6
Categories: Main dish, Meats
2 lb ham slices -- 3/4 inch
thick
Yield: 6 Servings
:
3/8 c  fat
1/4 c  prepared mustard
1/2 c  brown sugar
1 ts salt
2 ts paprika
1 c  water

Fry ham in fat. Remove ham slice(s0 from skillet. Pour all the other
ingredients into skillet, cook 5 minutes, place ham slice(s) back into
sauce and cook for 5 minutes more. Serve. Serves 3.
