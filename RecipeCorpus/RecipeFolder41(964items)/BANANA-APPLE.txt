Title: BANANA APPLE
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Applesauce; at room temp
2    Egg whites; whipped
3 tb Fat-free sour cream
1 c  Bananas; mashed
1 ts Vanilla
2    Apples; chopped
2 c  Unbleached flour
1/2 c  Brown sugar; packed
1/2 c  Granulated sugar; room temp
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
Recipe by: Jo Anne Merrill Preparation Time: 0:15 Preheat oven at 350.
Prepare a pan(s) with cooking spray and flour. In a mixing bowl,
combine applesauce, egg whites, sour cream, bananas, vanilla, and
apples. In another mixing bowl, combine flour, sugars, baking powder,
baking soda, and cinnamon. Mix wet ingredients with dry ingredients
just until moistened. Pour into prepared pan(s).  Bake about 1 hour
depending on pan size.
