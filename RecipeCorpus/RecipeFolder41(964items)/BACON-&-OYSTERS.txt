Title: BACON & OYSTERS
Categories: Wildgame, Seafood
Yield: 1 Servings
12    Oysters, selected
12    Bacon slices, thin
Wrap each oyster, well-drained, in bacon. Skewer with wooden
toothpick. Bake on a pan in a medium oven until bacon browns. Source:
FISHES AND FISHING IN LOUISIANA, publ 1933 Recipe date: 01/17/33
