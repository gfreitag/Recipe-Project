Title: BAKING POWDER BISCUITS I
Categories: Breads, Biscuits
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Flour
4 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
6 tb Shortening
1 c  Milk
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder and salt. Cut in
shortening with 2 spatulas.  Add milk. Turn onto lightly floured
board. Knead lightly and pat into sheet 1/2 inch thick. Cut with
floured cutter. Bake in hot oven (450Ã¸ F) 12 minutes. 16 servings.
