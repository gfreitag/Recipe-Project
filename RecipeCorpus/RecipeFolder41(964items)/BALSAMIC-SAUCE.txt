Title: BALSAMIC SAUCE
Categories: Italian, Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Balsamic vinegar
4    Cl Garlic; sliced
4 pn (2-inch) fresh rosemary
2 tb Butter; optional
Put all the ingredients except the butter into a saucepan and bring
to a boil.  Reduce the heat and simmer until it is reduced by half,
about 10 minutes. The sauce is ready when it coats the back of a
spoon. Add the optional butter and stir well.
