Title: BAKED ONIONS
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 12 Servings
6    Red onions, halved
1    Rind of lemon,cut in strips
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Port wine
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1 c  Raisins
1 tb Brown sugar
Pepper
In large saucepan, cover onions and lemon rind with water; bring to
boil and boil for 5 minutes. Drain; refresh under cold running water
and drain again. Discard rind. Peel onions and arrange in 13 x 9 inch
casserole dish; pour olive oil over.

Bake, uncovered in 375Ã¸F oven, basting occasionally, for 40 minutes.
Add port, vinegar, raisins, sugar, and pepper to taste. Bake for 30
minutes longer or until juices are syrupy. Makes 12 servings. Source:
