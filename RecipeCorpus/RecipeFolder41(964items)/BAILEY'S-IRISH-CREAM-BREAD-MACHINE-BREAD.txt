Title: BAILEY'S IRISH CREAM BREAD-MACHINE BREAD
Categories: Irish, Breadmaker, Low-fat
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/4 c  Water ; very warm
1 pk Yeast
1 c  Oats
3 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
1/3 c  Honey
2 tb Bailey's irish cream ; or to
Recipe by: "Aine.McManus" <mcmanus@maths.anu.edu.au>, Gaelic List Put
ingredients  in pan in order listed, Select "white bread" setting on
machine and press Start. The bread doesn't rise much, but the texture
is great.
