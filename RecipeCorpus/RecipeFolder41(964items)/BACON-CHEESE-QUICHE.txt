Title: BACON CHEESE QUICHE
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Recipe
4    Eggs
3/4 c  Cream
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Grated cheese
3/4 c  Chopped bacon
1 c  Packet pastry mix (dry)
1 tb Melted butter
1    Chopped onion
1 ts Chopped parsley
Lightly beat the eggs, then add all other ingredients and beat
together. Place into a large greased pie dish and bake 35-40 minutes
at 320 degrees F (160 degrees C).
