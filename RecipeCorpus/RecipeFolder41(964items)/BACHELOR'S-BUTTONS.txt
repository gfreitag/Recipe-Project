Title: BACHELOR'S BUTTONS
Categories: Cookies, Tested
Yield: 36 Servings
2/3 c  Butter; or margarine
1 3/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs; well beaten
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
3 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
Glace cherries
Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.  Stir in eggs; add
vanilla. Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt; stir into
creamed mixture. Mix thoroughly. Drop by teaspoons onto greased
baking sheet. Put half a cherry on top of each "button". Bake 8-10
minutes at 425F.
