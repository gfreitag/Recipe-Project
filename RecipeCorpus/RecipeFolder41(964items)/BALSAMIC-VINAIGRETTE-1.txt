Title: BALSAMIC VINAIGRETTE 1
Categories: Salad, Dressings
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Balsamic vinegar
1 1/2 c  Salad oil
3    Shallots, finely chopped
2 tb Dijon mustard
Salt and freshly ground
-pepper to taste
Juice of 1 lemon
Whisk together the vinegar, shallots, mustard and lemon juice. Then
add the oil and adjust the seasoning.
