Title: BAKED CREAMED POTATOES
Categories: Potato cass
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM-----------------FARM J AMERICA'S BEST VEGS----------------------
4 c  Potatoes, diced
Salt and pepper
1 tb Fresh dill, minced
1 c  Heavy cream
1 c  Milk
Good with fish. Place potatoes in shallow baking dish; sprinkle with
salt, pepper, and dill. Combine cream and milk; pour over potatoes.
Bake 350 F, 30 mins
