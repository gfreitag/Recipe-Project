Title: BAKED BEAN POT LENTILS
Categories: Appetizers, Pulses and
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Lentils
2 1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Chopped Onion
1 ts Salt
2 tb Dark Brown Sugar
2 tb Catsup
2 tb Molasses
1/2 ts Dry Mustard
4 sl Bacon -- diced and cooked
In saucepan, combine lentils, water, onion, and salt. Bring to a boil;
cover and simmer 45 minutes.  Stir in remaining ingredients. Turn into
1-quart casserole dish and bake at 350 for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Stir
occasionally.
