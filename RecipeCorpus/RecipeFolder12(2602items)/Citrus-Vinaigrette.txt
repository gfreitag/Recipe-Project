Title: Citrus Vinaigrette
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
16 oz Orange juice, reduced by 1/2
In volume
Juice of one lemon
1/2 c  Peanut oil
1/4 c  Rice wine vinegar
Place juice in a blender or food processor. Add vinegar and slowly add
peanut oil to emulsify. Salt and pepper to taste.

Yield: 1 1/2 cups
