Title: Fried Sage Leaves
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Loosely packed fresh sage
Leave
Coarse salt to taste
In a heavy saucepan heat 1-inch oil to 365 degrees on a deep-fat
thermometer. Working quickly, fry sage leaves, 5 at a time, 2 seconds,
transferring with a slotted spoon to paper towels to drain.
Immediately sprinkle sage leaves with coarse salt.

Yield: 1/2 cup
