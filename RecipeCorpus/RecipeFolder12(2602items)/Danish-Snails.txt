Title: Danish Snails
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
1/2    Batch Danish pastry
1/2    Stick butter
1/2 c  Light brown sugar
3/4 c  Chopped pecans or walnuts
Cinnamon
Egg wash
Water icing
Roll dough to a 12 by 20-inch rectangle. Spread with soft butter and
sprinkle with brown sugar, pecans, and cinnamon. Roll up from 20-inch
side, and cut into 12 pieces. Place pieces, cut side up, in muffin
pans lined with paper muffin cups. Proof 50% and egg wash. Bake at
375 degrees about 25 minutes. Cool and drizzle with water icing.

Yield: 12 snails
