Title: Papaya Tomatillo Salsa
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1 lg Papaya, peeled, seeded and
Coarsely chopped
4 md Tomatillos, husked and
Coarsely chopped
2 tb Finely diced red onion
1 tb Minced jalapeno
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped cilantro
1 ts Honey
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
Combine the papaya, tomatillos, onions, jalapeno, lime juice,
cilantro, and honey in a bowl and season to taste with salt and
pepper.

Yield: about 3 cups
