Title: Lyonnaise Mirlitons
Categories: Emeril, Sides
Yield: 4 servings
2    Tablespooons vegetable oil
1 c  Thinly-sliced onions
2 c  Peeled, seeded and
Thinly-sliced mirlitons
(chayotes)
1 tb Chopped garlic
2 tb Chopped green onion
1 tb Chopped parsley
Salt and freshly-ground
Black pepper
In a medium saute pan heat oil over low heat, add onions and cook,.
stirring, until golden brown and sweet, about 10 minutes. Add garlic
and continue cooking 2 minutes. Add mirlitons and sautJ, tossing and
mixing, 3 minutes. Add green onion and parsley, and season to taste
with salt and pepper.

Yield: 3 to 4 servings
