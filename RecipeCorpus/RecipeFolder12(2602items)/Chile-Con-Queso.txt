Title: Chile Con Queso
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/2 c  Grated Mexican Asadero or
Manchego cheese
1 c  Roasted Jalapeno Salsa,
Recipe follows
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. To prepare the Chile con Queso, mix
the cheese and Roasted Jalapeno Salsa in a cazuela or an ovenproof
serving dish and bake until the cheese has melted, about 10 minutes.
Serve immediately.

Yield: 6 to 8 servings
