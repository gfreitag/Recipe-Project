Title: Vinaigrette
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper
1 sm Clove garlic, chopped
2 tb Finely chopped chives
1 tb Vinegar
3 tb Extra virgin olive oil
1 tb Chopped shallots
In a bowl, whisk together the vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper. Then
whisk in the olive oil, garlic, chives and shallots until smooth.
