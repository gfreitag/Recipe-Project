Title: Chicken Trifolata
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
1    Chicken, cut up into small
Pieces
Flour
6 tb Virgin olive oil
4    Cloves garlic, thinly
Sliced
1 lb Portobello mushrooms, caps
Cut into 1/4inch strips
4 oz Black truffles, thinly
Sliced
1 c  Dry white wine
2 tb Tomato conserva or paste
1 c  Chicken stock
1 bn Italian parsley, finely
Chopped to yield 1/4 cup
Wash and pat chicken dry and dredge in flour.

In a 14 inch frying pan, heat olive oil until just smoking.  Brown
pieces of chicken until golden, 3 or 4 at a time.  Remove to a plate.
Continue until all the pieces are done.

Add garlic and cook until light brown.  Add Portables and truffles and
saute until soft, about 4 to 5 minutes.  Add wine and tomato conserva
and stir through.  Add broth and chicken pieces and bring to a boil.
Lower heat and simmer 20 minutes or until the juices of a thick piece
of chicken run clear.  Add parsley and serve immediately.

Yield: 4 servings
