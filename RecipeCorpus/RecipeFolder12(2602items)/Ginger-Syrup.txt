Title: Ginger Syrup
Categories:
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------ESSENCE OF EMERIL #EE2188----------------------
1 c  Sugar
1 1/4 c  Water
1 oz Ginger root stem, peeled and
Rough chopped
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and simmer for 15 minutes. Cool
and use the syrup to moisten cakes, sweeten tea, or over your ice
cream.
