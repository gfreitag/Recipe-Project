Title: Spicy Tomato Relish
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
2 md Ripe tomatoes 2 tablespoons
Finely diced Spanish onion
2 tb Chopped parsley
1 ts Red pepper flakes
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and season with salt and pepper to
taste. Serve at room temperature.

Yield: 4 servings
