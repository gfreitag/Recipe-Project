Title: Flaky Pastry
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Cold unsalted butter, cut
Into bits
1/2 c  Cold vegetable shortening,
Cut into bits
1/4 c  Ice water
1    Egg lightly beaten with 1
TB water, for egg wash
Into a mixing bowl sift flour and salt. Add butter and shortening and
blend with a pastry blender or fingertips until it resembles coarse
meal. Add water, stirring with a fork until dough comes together. If
too dry, add a little more water. Gather dough into a ball, wrap in
plastic wrap and flatten into a disk. Chill at least 30 minutes, or
may be prepared a day ahead.
