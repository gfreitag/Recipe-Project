Title: Caramelized Pears
Categories: Baker
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Unsalted butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Brandy
1 ts Lemon juice
2    Pears, peeled, halved, cored
Sliced
In a saute pan on medium, melt the butter then stir in the sugar.
Simmer, stirring occasionally until it begins to turn golden brown
and caramelized. Add brandy and lemon juice to the pan and simmer to
incorporate. Add pear slices and cook slowly to tenderize and
caramelize.

Yield: 4 servings
