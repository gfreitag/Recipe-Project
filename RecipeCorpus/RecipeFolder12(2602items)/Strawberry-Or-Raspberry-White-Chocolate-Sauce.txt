Title: Strawberry Or Raspberry White Chocolate Sauce
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb White chocolate
1/2 c  Water
5    Berries of choice
Over a double boiler melt white chocolate.  Add water and mix until no
lumps remain.

Roughly chop berries and add to sauce.  Reserve.
