Title: Cabbage Slaw
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Thinly sliced cabbage
2    Thinly sliced medium onions,
About 2 cups
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Celery seed
2 tb White vinegar
1 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Diced red bell pepper
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
Salt and pepper
1 ts Tabasco
Combine all and mix well. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
