Title: Grilled Rib Eye Steaks With Dipping Sauce
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Beef rib eye
Marinade:
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1 tb Fish sauce
1 tb Brown sugar
Dipping Sauce:
5 sm Dried red chiles
1/4 c  Thai fish sauce
Juice of 2 limes
1 ts Palm or brown sugar
1 ts Soy sauce
1 tb Roasted rice powder
2    Scallions, finely chopped
Trim steaks of fat.  Combine marinade ingredients in a large
container. Add the beef, cover, and refrigerate 1 to 2 hours. Remove
meat from refrigerator 30 minutes before grilling to enhance flavors.
Meanwhile, combine all the dipping sauce ingredients in a small bowl
and set aside. Preheat grill.  Grill steak, turning occasionally, 5
to 10 minutes for mediumrare.  Transfer meat to a cutting board and
set aside 10 minutes before slicing crosswise into thin slices.
Transfer meat and juices to a serving platter and serve with the
dipping sauce and Sticky Rice.

Yield: 4 servings
