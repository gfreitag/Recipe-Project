Title: Salsa Verde
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Packed fresh parsley leaves,
Washed well and spun dry
1 sm Garlic clove
1/3 c  Extra-virgin olive oil
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1/2 ts Anchovy paste
In a blender blend salsa ingredients until smooth. Salsa may be made
1 day ahead and chilled, covered. Bring salsa to room temperature
before serving.

Yield: about 1 cup
