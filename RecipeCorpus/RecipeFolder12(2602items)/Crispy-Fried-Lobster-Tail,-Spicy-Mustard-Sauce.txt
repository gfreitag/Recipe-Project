Title: Crispy Fried Lobster Tail, Spicy Mustard Sauce
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
5    Raw lobster tails, cut into
Eighths
Tempura batter:
8 oz Warm beer
1/2 c  Cornstarch
1/2 c  Flour
Salt and white pepper to
Taste
Place flour and cornstarch in a bowl. Add beer and mix well until all
ingredients are combined. Season with salt and pepper. Let mixture
rest for one hour.

Dip raw lobster tail pieces into tempura batter. Deep-fry at 340
degrees F until golden brown (approximately 1 1/2 to 2 minutes).
Serve with Mustard Sauce.

Yield: 4 servings
