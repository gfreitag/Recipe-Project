Title: Stuffed Baked Pork Chops
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Golden raisins, roughly
Chopped
1/2 ts Grated lemon peel
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Finely diced peeled apple or
Pear
Salt and pepper
4    Very thick loin pork chops
Flour for dusting chops
Vegetable oil for skillet
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.

Combine the raisins, grated lemon peel, lemon juice and apple and
season with salt and pepper. Make a pocket in the side of each pork
chop and stuff some of the mixture inside.. Close each chop with
toothpicks. Season with salt and lightly dust with flour.

Heat the oil in an iron skillet. Add the chops and brown them brief
on each side. Season with fresh pepper. Cover skillet with foil and
bake for 30 to
40    minutes.

Yield: 4 servings
