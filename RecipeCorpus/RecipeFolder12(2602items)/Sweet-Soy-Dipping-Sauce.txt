Title: Sweet-Soy Dipping Sauce
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Mirin
3/4 c  Defatted chicken or fish
Stock
1/4 c  Dark soy sauce
2 tb Each finely slivered ginger
And lemon zest
2 ts Sugar or to taste
Combine all ingredients and stir to dissolve sugar. Allow to sit for
at least 2 hours for flavors to develop. Store covered in
refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.

(Recipe courtesy of John Ash)
