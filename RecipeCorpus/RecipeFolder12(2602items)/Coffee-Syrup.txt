Title: Coffee Syrup
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Strong coffee
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
In a small saucepan heat coffee just to simmering. Stir in sugar and
vanilla and simmer 8 minutes, stirring until sugar dissolves; cool
before using.

Yield: 1 to 1 1/2 cups
