Title: Fire Roasted Chicken Skewers With Tangerine-Cascabel Glaz
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Olive oil
2    Shallots, finely diced
pn Cascabel powder
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1 c  Red wine
1 c  Tangerine juice
1 ts Sugar
Lemon juice to taste
Honey to taste
1 tb Olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
1/4 c  Fresh orange juice
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Ancho chile powder
1 tb Pasilla pepper
2 tb Paprika
1/2 ts Cayenne
1/4 c  Olive oil
8    Boneless, skinless chicken
Thighs, cut in half
Lengthwise
16    Bamboo skewers, soaked in
Water for 2 hours
Heat the olive oil in a medium sauce pan over mediumheat. Add the
shallots and cascabel powder and cook until soft. Raise the heat to
high, add the vinegar, red wine, tangerine juice and sugar and reduce
to a syrup. Strain, return to a small saucepan and season with lemon
juice, honey to taste. Add the olive oil and season with salt and
pepper. Let cool. Mix together the juices, chile powders and oil. Add
the chicken thighs and let marinate for 2 hours in the refrigerator.
Preheat grill. Remove chicken from marinade and thread each chicken
half onto 2 skewers so that the meat stays flat on the grill. Grill
for 4 minutes on each side or until cooked through. Remove chicken
from the grill and brush liberally with the tangerine glaze.

Yield: 4 servings
