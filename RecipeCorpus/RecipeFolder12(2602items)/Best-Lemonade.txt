Title: Best Lemonade
Categories: Try it
Yield: 10 servings
MMMMM----------------COOKING MONDAY TO FRIDAY #MF---------------------
2 ts Grated fresh lemon peel
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Juice, lemon
Bring lemon peel, sugar, and water to a boil; boil for 30 seconds.
Remove from heat and add the lemon juice. Cool to room temperature
and store syrup in the refrigerator.

For each glass of lemonade use 1/4 cup of syrup with a cup of fizzy
water or plain water. To make lemonade ice cubes, stir 2 tablespoons
of syrup per cup of water and freeze as lemonade ice cubes.
