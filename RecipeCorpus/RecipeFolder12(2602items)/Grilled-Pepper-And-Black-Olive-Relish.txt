Title: Grilled Pepper And Black Olive Relish
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Pitted black olives,
Chopped
1/4 c  Grilled red bell peppers,
Seeded and diced
1/4 c  Grilled yellow peppers
Seeded and diced
2 tb Crushed red pepper flakes
1 tb Fresh garlic
2 tb Fresh thyme
1/4 c  Chopped fresh parsley
2 tb Sherry vinegar
1 tb Honey
3 tb Olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper to taste
In a mixing bowl combine all ingredients and season to taste with
salt and freshly ground pepper. Serve with grilled chicken or fish.
