Title: Adobo Marinade
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Drained canned tomatoes
3 tb Ancho chile powder
3 tb Pasilla chile powder
1    Canned chipotle, seeded and
Diced
1 tb Honey
1 tb Dark brown sugar
3 tb Red wine vinegar
1 ts Cayenne
1/4 c  Chopped garlic
1/4 c  Olive oil
Puree the marinade ingredients in a food processor.

Yield: 3 cups
