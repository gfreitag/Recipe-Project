Title: Seasoning Mix
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 ts Dried oregano
1 1/2 ts Dried thyme
1 1/2 tb Paprika
3 tb Onion powder
1 tb Garlic powder
1 1/2 ts Black pepper
1 1/2 ts Cayenne
1 tb Salt
Blend all ingredients. Keeps well tightly sealed in a jar.
