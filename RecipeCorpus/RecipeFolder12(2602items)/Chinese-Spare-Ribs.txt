Title: Chinese Spare Ribs
Categories: Taste
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Soy sauce
1 1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
4 ts Hoisin sauce
4 ts Minced garlic
2 lb Spare ribs
Mix together the first five ingredients in a bowl. Fit the ribs
snugly into a bowl or pan and pour the marinade over. Cover and
refrigerate for 6 hours to overnight.

Grill or broil spareribs, turning once until done, about 15 minutes.
Serve.
