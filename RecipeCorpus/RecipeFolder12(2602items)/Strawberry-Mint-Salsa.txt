Title: Strawberry Mint Salsa
Categories: Baker
Yield: 4 servings
2 pt Strawberries
8 oz Strawberry puree
1    Lime, juiced
2    Leaves fresh mint,
Julienned
Cut off strawberry tops and cut up. Toss into strawberry puree then
add lime juice. Wrap and refrigerate. To serve, spoon salsa on plate
and sprinkle with mint.

Yield: 12 servings
