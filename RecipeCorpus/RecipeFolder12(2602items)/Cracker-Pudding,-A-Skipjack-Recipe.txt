Title: Cracker Pudding, A Skipjack Recipe
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
30    Unsalted soda crackers
1 c  Water
2    Eggs, beaten
pn Salt
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1    (13-ounce) can evaporated
Milk
1/4 c  Butter melted
Put crackers in a bowl and soak in 1 cup water until soft. Add eggs,
slat, sugar, and vanilla. Sit to make mixture uniform. Add evaporated
milk and whole milk. The pudding is very thin, as it is supposed to
be. Pour butter into a 9 by 13-inch baking dish, and pour pudding
over it. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes to one hour.
