Title: Bourbon Sauce
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1/2    Stick (4 tablespoons)
Unsalted butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Bourbon
4    Egg yolks
Melt the butter in a double boiler over simmering water. Add the
sugar and whisk to dissolve. Add the bourbon and whisk for 1 minute.
Remove from the heat and drizzle in the egg yolks, whisking
constantly.

Return the double boiler to the heat and continue whisking until the
sauce is pale and slightly thickened, 3 to 4 minutes.

Spoon the sauce over the pudding and serve immediately.
