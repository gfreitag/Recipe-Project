Title: Puttanesca Sauce
Categories: Try it, Sauces
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM------------------------TASTE #4893-----------------------------
1/3 c  Oil, olive
1 tb Garlic cloves, crushed
4 c  Canned plum tomatoes;
Drained and chopped
1 tb Oregano, fresh; minced
1/2 ts Red pepper flakes
2 tb Capers; drained
12    Olives, black; halved
2 oz Anchovies; drained and
Minced
Salt
Pepper
2 tb Parsley, fresh; minced
2 tb Basil, fresh; minced
1 lb Pasta; cooked
To a large saucepan add the olive oil and minced garlic. Saute it
briefly. Then add the plum tomatoes, fresh oregano, red pepper
flakes, capers, and black olives. Cover the pan and simmer the sauce
for 10 minutes, then uncover and simmer 10 more minutes, stirring
occasionally.

To the reduced sauce add the anchovies, and season to taste with salt
and pepper. Simmer 5 minutes longer to heat through and blend the
flavors.

Sauce the pasta in a large pasta bowl. Sprinkle on the parsley and
basil.
