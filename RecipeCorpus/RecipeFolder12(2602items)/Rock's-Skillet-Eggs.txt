Title: Rock's Skillet Eggs
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
6 oz Bacon, chopped
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1/2 c  Chopped green peppers
Salt freshly ground black
Pepper
1 ts Chopped garlic
2 c  Small diced Idaho potatoes,
Peeled and blanched
1 c  Fresh tomatoes, peeled,
Seeded and chopped
4 oz Cheddar cheese
8 lg Eggs
In a large nonstick pan, over medium heat, render the bacon until
crispy, about 6 minutes. Add the onions and peppers. Season with salt
and freshly ground black pepper. Add the garlic, potatoes, and
tomatoes. Saute for 3 to 4 minutes. Sprinkle the top of the potatoes
with the cheese. Drop whole cracked eggs into the pan a couple of
inches apart. Cover and allow the eggs to poach to desired doneness.
Remove the pan from heat and cut into pie-shaped pieces. Serve with
hot French bread.

Yield: 4 servings
