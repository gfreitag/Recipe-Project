Title: Grilled Corn Niblets With Lime Butter And Cracked Black P
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
4    Ears of corn
2 tb Olive oil
Salt
3 tb Unsalted butter
Juice of 1 lime
1 tb Coarsely cracked black
Pepper
Brush corn with olive oil, season with salt, place on the grill and
grill until lightly golden on all sides. Heat the butter in a medium
skillet and add the lime juice. Add the corn niblets and cook for one
minute. Add the black pepper.

Yield: 4 servings
