Title: Smoothie Martini
Categories:
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------CHEF DU JOUR DEB STANTON #DJ---------------------
2    Bananas
1 pt Strawberries
2 c  Vanilla yogurt
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Pineapple juice
Whipped cream for garnish
Martini glasses
In a blender combine sliced bananas, sliced strawberries, yogurt and
juices. Pour into martini glasses and top with whipped cream.
