Title: Fruit Swirls
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Orange juice, frozen
1 c  Cranberry juice, frozen
1 c  Apple cider, frozen
1 c  Pink lemonade, frozen
In a blender puree each of the frozen juices separately. Layer them
using equal amounts in each of 4 glasses. Place the glasses in the
freezer in-between layers. When all of the frozen fruit juice has
been pureed and layered, remove the glasses from the freezer. Place a
spoon in each glass then remove it. Serve immediately.

Yield: 4 servings
