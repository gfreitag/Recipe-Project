Title: Blueberry Lemonade
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Blueberries
3/4 c  Sugar
2 c  Freshly squeezed lemon
Juice
3 c  Cold water
Ice cubes
Place the blueberries and sugar in a blender and puree. Place the
lemon juice and water in a pitcher and strain the pureed blueberries
through a mediummesh sieve into the pitcher. Stir well and pour into
tall icefilled glasses.

Yield: 4 servings
