Title: Barbecued Ribs With Peanut-Chipotle Sauce And Tomatillo C
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Soy sauce
1 c  Water
4 tb Chopped ginger
3    Cloves garlic coarsely
Chopped
2    Racks of pork ribs
Combine the soy sauce, water, ginger and garlic in a medium saucepan
and bring to a boil. Turn off heat and let cool. Place ribs in a
large roasting dish. Cover with the marinade and let marinate in
refrigerator for 24 hours.

Yield: 4 servings
