Title: Coleslaw (Live)
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
3 1/2 c  Medium grated cabbage, red
Or green or a combination of
Both
1 c  Grated carrot
2 tb Cider vinegar
1 tb Superfine sugar
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 c  Plus 1 tablespoon olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
Black pepper
2 ts Celery seeds
In a large bowl combine the grated cabbage and carrot. In a small bowl
combine the vinegar and sugar, stirring until the sugar dissolves.
Mix in the mustard, olive oil, salt and pepper. Mix in

celery seeds. Pour the dressing over the cabbage, tossing to coat.
Cover and chill for 2 hours before serving.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings
