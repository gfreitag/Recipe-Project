Title: Pollo In Escabeche (Pickled Chicken)
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
1    Fryer chicken
6    Cloves garlic, peeled,
Whole
12    Black peppercorns
4    Sprigs thyme
1 tb Toasted cumin seeds
1 md Spanish onion, cut into
1/4    Inch slices
2 tb Salt
1 tb Sugar
4 c  White wine
1 c  Cider vinegar
1    Lemon, thinly sliced
1    Orange, thinly sliced
Cut chicken into 10 pieces and remove skin. Place pieces in a 4quart
saucepan and add remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil, lower heat,
cover and simmer for 1 hour and 20 minutes. Remove chicken carefully
and place in a shallow crock. Pour cooking liquid and solids over
chicken and allow to cool. Serve chilled with chicory salad and lemon
wedges.

Yield: 4 servings
