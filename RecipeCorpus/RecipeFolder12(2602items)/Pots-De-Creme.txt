Title: Pots De Creme
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Swiss chocolate
2 c  Warm milk
6    Egg yolks, beaten
1 tb Bourbon whiskey
Melt chocolate in warm milk. Stir until it boils and the chocolate
coats the spoon. Remove from heat. Stir into the egg yolks and add
the Bourbon. Pour into the pots de creme cups.

Use the leftover egg whites to make small meringues to serve with the
pots de creme and a slivered orange rind for the garnish.

Yield: 8 servings
