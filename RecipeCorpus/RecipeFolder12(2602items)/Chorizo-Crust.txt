Title: Chorizo Crust
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Finely chopped chorizo
Sausage
1/2    To 1 cup bread crumbs
16 lg Sea scallops
Essence
1/2 c  Flour
1    Egg, slightly beaten plus
TB milk
Olive oil for pan-frying
In a hot saute pan, render the chorizo for 2-3 minutes. Remove from
the heat. In a food processor, pulse the chorizo with the bread
crumbs until the mixture binds together. Season with Essence. Season
the scallops with Essence. Dredge the scallops in the flour. Dip the
scallops in the egg wash, removing any excess. Dredge the scallops in
the chorizo crust. In a saute pan, heat the olive oil. When the oil
is hot, pan-fry the scallops for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until
they are golden. Drain on a paper-lined plate. Season with Essence.

Yield: 4 servings
