Title: Broccoli Pesto
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Tightly packed fresh basil
Leaves 3/4 cup extra-virgin
Olive oil
1/2 c  Freshly grated
Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
1/2 c  Tightly packed broccoli
Florets
1/2 c  Blanched almonds
1/4 c  Chicken stock
6    Garlic cloves, chopped
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
1/4 ts Freshly ground black pepper
Combine all the ingredients in a food processor and blend until you
have a smooth paste.

Yield: about 2 cups
