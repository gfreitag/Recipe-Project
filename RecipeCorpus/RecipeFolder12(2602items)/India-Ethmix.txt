Title: India Ethmix
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
5 ts Turmeric
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
5 ts Ground cumin
5 ts Ground coriander
1 1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
2 1/2 ts Decorticated cardamom seeds
2 1/2 ts Fenugreek seeds
Grind to a fine powder in a small electric coffee grinder designated
for this use.  Shake through a sieve to remove coarse pieces or
debris.

Yield: 1/2 cup
