Title: Thousand Island Dressing
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Chili sauce
1 tb Minced green bell pepper,
Optional
1 tb Minced onion, scallion, or
Chives
1    Hard boiled egg, peeled and
Finely chopped
1/4 c  Minced pimiento stuffed
Green olives
1/4 ts Hot red pepper sauce
In a bowl, mix the dressing ingredients well, cover and chill.

Yield: 1 1/2 cups
