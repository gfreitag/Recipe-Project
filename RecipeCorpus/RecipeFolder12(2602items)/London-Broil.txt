Title: London Broil
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Red wine
3    Garlic cloves, smashed
1 tb Salt and 1 teaspoon
Coarse-cracked black pepper
1 1/2 lb Flank steak
In a nonreactive bowl large enough to fit flank steak combine oil,
lemon juice, wine, garlic, salt and pepper. Add flank steak and
marinate 4 hours, turning once after 2 hours. Drain flank steak, pat
dry and place on a broiler pan. Broil under preheated broiler, 10
minutes per side, until well-browned. Transfer to cutting board and
slice against the grain. Save leftovers for sandwiches.

Yield: 6 servings
