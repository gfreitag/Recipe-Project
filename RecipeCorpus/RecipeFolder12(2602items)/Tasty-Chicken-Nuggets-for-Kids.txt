Title: Tasty Chicken Nuggets for Kids
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  A seasoning or flavoring
Your kids love; ketchup
Barbecue sauce,
Soy or steak sauce
Salt and freshly ground
Black pepper
1/2 lb Skinless boneless, chicken
Breasts or chicken "tenders"
Cut
Into 1/2 inch cubes or very
Thin strips
2 tb Unsalted butter
1/2 c  Dried bread crumbs
Transfer the seasoning or flavoring of choice in a shallow dish and
season with salt and pepper. Marinate the chicken in the mixture for
as long as possible. Heat the butter in a medium skillet, over medium
high heat. Dip the chicken in bread crumbs and saute slowly on all
sides until cooked through.

Yield: 2 servings
