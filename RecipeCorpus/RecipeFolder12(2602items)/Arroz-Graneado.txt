Title: Arroz Graneado
Categories: Toohot, Hot
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Lard or vegetable oil
2    Cloves garlic
2 c  Rice, washed and drained
3 1/2 c  Water or chicken stock
1 ts Coarse salt
1/4 c  Chopped parsley leaves
Heat lard in a heavy saucepan.  Add garlic and saute 1 minute.  Add
rice, chicken stock and lower the heat, cover tightly and cook for 15
to 20 minutes, or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.  Fluff
with a fork and add chopped parsley. Serve on a platter with shrimp
in the middle.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings
