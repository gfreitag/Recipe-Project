Title: Macaroons
Categories: Baker
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Finely ground almonds
1/2 lb Sugar
1/2 ts Pure almond extract
3    Unbeaten egg whites
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Combine almonds, sugar and almond
extract. Add egg whites until all ingredients are thoroughly mixed.
Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper. Put the batter into a
pastry bag and squeeze out small thick drops about the size of a half
dollar, 2 inches apart. Bake 25 to 30 minutes. Cool.

Yield: About 2 Dozen Macaroons
