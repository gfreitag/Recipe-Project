Title: Cinnamon Chicken
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Dry sherry
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Lemon juice
4    Cloves garlic, minced
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1    Frying chicken, 2 1/2 to 3
Pounds, cut into pieces
2 tb Vegetable oil
In a mediumsize bowl, mix the dry sherry, cinnamon, honey, lemon
juice, garlic, salt and pepper.  Add the chicken and toss to a evenly
coat. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator 8 hours, or overnight.

To cook, preheat the oven to 350 degrees.

Remove the chicken, shaking off excess marinade.  Pour the marinade
into a small saucepan and bring to a boil.  Boil until it begins to
thicken and about 1 cup remains, 5 to 10 minutes.  Heat the oil in an
ovenproof skillet over mediumhigh heat.  Sear the chicken until
golden on both sides. Pour the reduced marinade over the chicken and
place in the oven.

Bake about 20 minutes and serve.

Yield:  4 servings
