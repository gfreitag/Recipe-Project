Title: Emeril's Homemade Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1    Onion, minced
2    To 3 jalapeno peppers,
Minced
3 tb Vegetable oil
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1 c  Water
2 c  Ketchup
2 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
Salt and black pepper
In a saucepan saute onion and peppers in vegetable oil fo-r 2 to 3
minutes, until softened. Add garlic, water, ketchup, brown sugar and
Worcestershire sauce and stir well to combine. Cover and simmer 10 to
15 minutes, just until flavors are melded.

Yield: About 2 cups
