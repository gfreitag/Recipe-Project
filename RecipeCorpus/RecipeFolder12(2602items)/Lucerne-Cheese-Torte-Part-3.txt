Title: Lucerne Cheese Torte Part 3
Categories: Baker
Yield: 4 servings
---Soaking Syrup---
1 1/4 c  Water
2 1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice, strained
Pour the water into a 1 1/2 quart saucepan.  Add the sugar; stir to
blend; then bring to a boil over medium heat.  Remove from heat and
cool. Pour into a container with a lid and refrigerate up to 6 months.

Combine the Soaking Syrup and lemon juice in a liquid cup measure. Set
aside.
