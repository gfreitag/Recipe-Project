Title: Pickled Onions
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2 lg Red onions, peeled and
Thinly sliced
1 tb Olive oil
3 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Sugar
6    Whole black peppercorns
4    Sprigs fresh thyme
Garnish: 2 tablespoons
Finely chopped thyme
Saute the onions over low heat in 1 tablespoon of olive oil until
soft. Place the vinegar, 1/4 cup of olive oil, sugar, peppercorns and
thyme in a large noncorrosive saucepan and bring to a boil. Add the
onions to the hot liquid and simmer for 5 minutes. Remove the pot
from the heat and allow the onions to steep for at least 2 hours or
overnight. (if storing overnight, place in a bowl and cover.) When
ready to serve, drain the liquid and top with thyme.

Yield: 4 servings
