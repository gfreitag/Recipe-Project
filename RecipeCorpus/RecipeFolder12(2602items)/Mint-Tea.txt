Title: Mint Tea
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Chinese Gunpowder Tea
5 c  Boiling water
15    Fresh mint sprigs
Sugar to taste
Place the tea in a teapot and fill it with boiling water. Let the tea
steep for 2 minutes. Rinse the mint under running water and pack it
into the teapot. Stir in the sugar. Serve hot.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings
