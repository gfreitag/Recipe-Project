Title: Melon Liquado
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
1    Cantaloupe or honeydew melon
Peeled, seeds removed,
Chopped
1 1/2 c  Cold milk
2 c  Chopped ice
3 tb Honey
Combine the fruit with the milk, ice and honey in a blender or food
processor and puree until smooth. Pour into tall glasses and serve
immediately.

Yield: 1 quart
