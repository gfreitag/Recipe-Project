Title: She-Crabs Marinara
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
6 tb Extra virgin olive oil
4    Cloves of garlic, peeled and
Chopped
pn Crushed red pepper flakes
1 1/2 lb Ripe plum tomatoes, peeled,
Seeded and chopped
Salt and freshly ground
Black pepper
10    Sprigs Italian parsley,
Leaves only, chopped
12    Crabs, about 3 pounds,
Cleaned
Start by placing a large skillet over medium heat, and add the olive
oil to the pan. Start to saute the garlic and red pepper for 2
minutes or until golden, add the tomatoes, season with salt and
pepper and allow to simmer for 15 minutes. If the sauce gets too
thick you can add a little water.

Add the parsley and crabs and allow them to simmer for an additional
15 minutes. Taste for seasoning and serve hot.

Yield: 6 servings
