Title: Pineapple Sunrise
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
6 oz White tequila
12 oz Pineapple juice
2 oz Lime juice
2 ts Grenadine
Cracked ice
Pineapple slices for
Garnish
Combine liquid (in two batches) in a cocktail shaker with cracked ice
and shake well. Strain into glasses and garnish with pineapple.
