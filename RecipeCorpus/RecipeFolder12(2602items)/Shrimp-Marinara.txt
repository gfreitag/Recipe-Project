Title: Shrimp Marinara
Categories: Taste
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Large shrimp (16-20)
1    Heaping tablespoon finely
Minced garlic
3 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Salt
4 c  Mariner Sauce (see previous
Recipe)
2 tb Chicken stock
Crushed red pepper
(optional)
Salt and pepper
Finely-chopped parsley, for
Garnish
Peel, define and butterfly shrimp. In a bowl, mix shrimp with garlic,
1 tablespoon of the oil and salt. Marinate, refrigerated, 1 hour.

Heat 2 tablespoons of the oil in a large sauté pan over high heat. Add
shrimp, spreading them so cut surface lies flat. Weight shrimp with a
slightly smaller sauté pan or other weight, press and sear 30
seconds, and remove to a plate. Add mariner and chicken stock to pan.
Reduce rapidly over high heat 5 minutes, or until liquid has
evaporated. Return shrimp to pan, toss well and cook until shrimp are
warmed through. Season to taste with crushed red pepper, salt and
pepper. Serve immediately, sprinkled with chopped parsley.

Yield: 4 servings
