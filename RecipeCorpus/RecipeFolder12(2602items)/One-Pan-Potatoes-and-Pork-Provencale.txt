Title: One-Pan Potatoes and Pork Provencale
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
4 ea Medium potatoes, cut
-3/4-inch cubes
Microwaved 6-10 minutes
-until tender
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 lb Lean pork, cut into thin
-strips
1 c  Cherry tomatoes
1/3 c  Orange marmalade
2 tb Dij
While potatoes cook, heat oil in large skillet over high heat. Add
pork and toss until opaque. Drain. Add potatoes and tomatoes; saute
and toss 3 minutes. Add marmalade and mustard; toss until marmalade
is melted. Season with salt and pepper. Recipe by: The Potato Board
