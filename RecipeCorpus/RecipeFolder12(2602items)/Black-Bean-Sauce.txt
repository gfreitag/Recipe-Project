Title: Black Bean Sauce
Categories: Emeril, Hot
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1 tb Olive oil
1/3 c  Chopped onions
1 tb Minced garlic
1 tb Seeded, chopped jalapeno
1 c  Black beans, soaked in water
To cover, at least 8 hours
Or
Overnight
6 c  Chicken stock
1 tb Lime juice
1 ts Southwest Seasoning, or to
Taste
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Salt
Black pepper
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped cilantro
Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat.  Add onions, garlic,
jalapeno and drained, rinsed beans; cook, stirring, 2 minutes.  Add
stock, lime juice, Southwest seasoning, chili powder, cumin, salt and
3 turns of black pepper.  Bring to a boil and simmer 30 minutes.
Reduce heat to medium and simmer 15 minutes.  Stir in cilantro and
simmer until beans are tender, about 5 minutes longer.
