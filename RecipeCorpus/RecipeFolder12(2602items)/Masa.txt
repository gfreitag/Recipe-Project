Title: Masa
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Finely ground deep yellow
Masa harina
2 3/4 c  Cold water
1 ts Salt
In a large bowl combine all ingredients and stir until smooth. The
dough should be slightly sticky and form a ball when pressed
together. To test, flatten a small piece of dough between your palms.
If the edges crack, add water to the dough, a tablespoon at a time,
until a test piece does not crack.
