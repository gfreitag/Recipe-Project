Title: Confit Of Apples
Categories: Baker
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Unsalted butter
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Brandy
2 ts Lemon juice
4    Granny Smith apples
In a saute pan on medium, melt the butter then stir in the sugar.
Simmer, stirring occasionally until it begins to turn golden brown
and caramelized. Add brandy and lemon juice to the pan and simmer to
incorporate. Peel, half and core apples. Place apples in caramel and
cook slowly to caramelize and tenderize. Serve warm.

Yield: 8 servings
