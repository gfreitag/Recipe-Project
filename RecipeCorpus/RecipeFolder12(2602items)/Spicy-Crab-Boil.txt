Title: Spicy Crab Boil
Categories: Taste
Yield: 4 servings
24 oz Beer (2 bottles)
1    Lemon, quartered
1 1/2 tb Black peppercorns
1 sm Dried red chili
1 ts Cayenne
1 tb Coriander seeds
2 ts Brown mustard seeds
1/4 ts Whole allspice
4    Cloves
4    Bay leaves
1 tb Celery seed
2 ts Dill seed
1    Dozen blue crabs (such as
Maryland Blues)
In a very large pot combine beer and lemon with 3 cups water. In a
coffee grinder or spice mill, grind spices to a coarse powder; add to
pot. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, reduce heat to medium and
simmer vigorously, 10 minutes. Add crabs and cover pot tightly. Bring
to a boil and simmer 8 minutes. Serve on lots of newspapers with a
small hammer, picks and lemon.

Yield: 2 servings
