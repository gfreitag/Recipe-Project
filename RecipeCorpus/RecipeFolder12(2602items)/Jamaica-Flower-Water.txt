Title: Jamaica Flower Water
Categories: Toohot, Hot
Yield: 4 servings
2 qt Water
3/4 c  Dried Jamiaca or hibiscus
Flowers
1/2 c  Sugar or honey
Orange slices for garnish
Bring the water to a boil.  Add the flowers and return to a boil.
Reduce to a simmer and cook 10 minutes.  Stir in the sugar or honey,
strain into a pitcher and refrigerate.  Serve cold over ice with
slices of orange as garnish.

Variation: Before serving, stir in 2 cups of orange juice.

Yield: 1 1/2 quarts
