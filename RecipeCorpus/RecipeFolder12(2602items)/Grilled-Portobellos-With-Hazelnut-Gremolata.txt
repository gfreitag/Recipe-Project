Title: Grilled Portobellos With Hazelnut Gremolata
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
4 md Portobello mushrooms, stems
Removed
Olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
Brush mushrooms on both sides with olive oil and season with salt and
pepper to taste. Grill for 5 to 6 minutes on each side until just
cooked through. Serve each person a mushroom cap and sprinkle with
the gremolata.
