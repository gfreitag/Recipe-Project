Title: Grilled Chicken W\ Grilled Pepper And Black Olive Relish
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
Garlic and Fresh Oregano
Marinade-
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Coarsely chopped garlic
1/2 c  Coarsely chopped fresh
Oregano leaves
1 tb Sherry vinegar
2 tb Lime juice
1 tb Honey
2 ts Ancho powder
2    Chickens, quartered
1 c  Grilled Pepper and Black
Olive Relish Marinade,
Recipe follows
Combine all marinade ingredients in a mixing bowl. Mix until
thoroughly combined in a mixing bowl. Marinate chicken for 1 hour.
Prepare a wood or charcoal grill and let it burn down to embers.

Grill chicken for 4 minutes on each side or until done. Serve with the
relish spooned over it.

Yield: 4 servings
