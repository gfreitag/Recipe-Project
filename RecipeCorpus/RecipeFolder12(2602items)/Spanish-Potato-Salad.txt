Title: Spanish Potato Salad
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1 tb Honey
1 pn Saffron threads
1 c  Good quality mayonnaise
1 tb Minced garlic
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
2 lb New potatoes
1 lg Tomato, coarsely chopped
1/2 c  Finely diced Spanish onion
1 tb Finely chopped fresh thyme
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped flatleaf
Parsley
Combine the vinegar, honey and saffron in a small pot. Bring to a
boil over high heat; immediately remove from the heat and let sit
until cool to room temperature. Combine the mayonnaise and garlic
with the saffron mixture in a medium mixing bowl and season to taste
with salt and pepper.

Cook the potatoes in a large pot of boiling, salted water until
tender. Drain and slice 1/2-inch thick. Place in a large serving bowl
and immediately fold in the mayonnaise mixture, tomatoes, onion,
thyme and parsley. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Yield: 4 servings
