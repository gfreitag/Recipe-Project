Title: Horseradish Cream
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Chopped shallots
1/4 c  Chopped garlic
1 tb Olive oil
2 c  Veal stock
3 c  Cream
1/2 ts Cayenne
1/2 c  Horseradish
Salt to taste
Sweat shallots and garlic in olive oil. Add stock and reduce until
almost dry.

Add cream, cayenne and horseradish, season with salt.
