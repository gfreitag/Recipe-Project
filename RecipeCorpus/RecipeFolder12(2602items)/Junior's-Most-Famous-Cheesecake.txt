Title: Junior's Most Famous Cheesecake
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
7/8 c  Sugar
3 tb Cornstarch
1 lb 14 ounces cream cheese
1    Extra large egg
1/2 c  Heavy cream
3/4 ts Vanilla
Mix 7/8 cup sugar with 3 tablespoons corn starch. Add to 1 pound 14
ounces cream cheese and mix. Add 1 extra large egg and blend. Then
add 1/2 cup heavy cream a little at a time and mix. Then add 3/4
teaspoons vanilla and mix.

Now for the baking. Add ingredients to well greased 7-inch pan with
sponge cake on bottom of pan. Bake at 450 degrees for approximately
40 to 45 minutes until top is golden brown. Cool for about 3 hours.

(recipe courtesy of Alan Rosen, owner, Junior's, Brooklyn, NY)
