Title: MELON SMOOTHIES
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings  1 1/2 cups seeded and chopped watermelon
1 1/2 cups seeded and chopped honeydew melon
Juice of 2 limes
1 cup vanilla lowfat yogurt
1 cup ice cubes

Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Pour into
glasses.

Yield: 4 servings
