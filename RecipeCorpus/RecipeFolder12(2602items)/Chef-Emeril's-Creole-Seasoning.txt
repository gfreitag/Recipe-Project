Title: Chef Emeril's Creole Seasoning
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/2 tb Paprika
2 tb Salt
2 tb Garlic powder
1 tb Onion powder
1 tb Black pepper
1 tb Cayenne pepper
1 tb Dried leaf oregano
1 tb Dried leaf thyme
Combine all ingredients thoroughly and store in an airtight container.
Makes about 2/3 cup.
