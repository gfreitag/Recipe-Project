Title: Bbq Whole Chicken
Categories:
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------GRILLIN' & CHILLIN' #GR3613---------------------
2    4 pound chickens
MMMMM----------------------SOUTHERN BBQ RUB---------------------------
2 tb Salt
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Ground cumin
1 tb Ancho chile powder
1 tb Pasilla chile powder
1 tb Cracked black pepper
1 tb Cayenne
4 tb Paprika
Prepare a wood or charcoal grill and let it burn down to embers. Rub
the chickens with the barbecue rub. Let sit in the refrigerator for 1
hour.

On the grill place the chickens, on the side with no fire. Cover the
grill and vent slightly. Cook for 3 hours or until chickens are
cooked through.

Southern Rub: Mix all ingredients together well.
