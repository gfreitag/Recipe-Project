Title: Fried Plantains
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
4    Ripe plantains
Oil for frying
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
Preheat the fryer. Peel the plantains. Thinly slice the plantains,
crosswise. Fry the plantains in batches until golden brown, about 2
minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from the oil and drain on paper
towels. Season with salt and pepper.

Yield: 6 to 8 servings
