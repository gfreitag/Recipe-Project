Title: Sour Cream Dipping Sauce
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sour cream
2 ts Dijon-style mustard
2    Scallions, minced
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
In a small serving bowl combine the sour cream, the mustard, the
scallions, and the Worcestershire sauce.

Yield: 1 cup
