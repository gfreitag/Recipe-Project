Title: Crema
Categories:
Yield: 2 Cups
MMMMM------------------TOO HOT TAMALES #TH6105-----------------------
2 c  Heavy cream
1/4 c  Buttermilk
Whisk the cream and buttermilk together. Cover and set in a warm
place for 8 hours. Crema may be kept in the refrigerator as long as a
week.
