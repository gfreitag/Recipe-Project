Title: Passion Fruit Sangria
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
2    Bottles dry white wine
3/4 c  Brandy
1/2 c  Triple sec
3/4 c  Simple syrup (equal parts of
Sugar and water, heat until
Sugar
Dissolves and cool)
3/4 c  Passion fruit puree
3    Oranges, sliced into thin
Rounds
3    Green apples, cored and
Sliced thin
2    Lemons, sliced into thin
Rounds
Combine all ingredients in a large pitcher. Refrigerate covered, 3
hours or up to 2 days. Serve over ice.

Yield: 4 servings
