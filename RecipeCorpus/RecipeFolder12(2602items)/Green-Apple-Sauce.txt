Title: Green Apple Sauce
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
8    Green apples, peeled and
Cored
1/2 c  Sugar
2    Shots grappa
1 pn Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
Juice of 1 lemon
1/4 c  Fresh chives
Quarter apples and place them in a saucepan over medium heat with the
sugar, grappa, cinnamon, salt and lemon juice. Cook until soft and
broken down, about 10 to 15 minutes. Remove from heat, stir in chives
and serve immediately.

Yield: 4 servings
