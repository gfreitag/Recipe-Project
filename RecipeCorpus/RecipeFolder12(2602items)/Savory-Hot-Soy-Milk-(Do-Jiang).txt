Title: Savory Hot Soy Milk (Do Jiang)
Categories: Try it
Yield: 2 Servings
-Taiwan, North China
MMMMM----------------FLATBREADS & FLAVORS; ALFORD---------------------
2 c  Soy milk; unsweetened
1 ts Vinegar, cider
1 ts Shrimp, dried; finely
-chopped
1 tb Pork, dried; finely
-shredded
1/4 c  Croutons; small, or bread
-cubes
2    Onions, green; finely
-chopped
1 ts Cilantro, fresh; coarsely
-chopped
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
1/4 ts Oil, chile; to taste
Heat soy milk over medium-high heat until boiling.  Reduce heat to
low and stir in vinegar.  Pour into one soup bowl per serving.  Add
remaining ingredients.  Drizzle chile oil on top and serve with
flatbread for dipping.
