Title: Magic Cupcakes
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
Chocolate cupcakes (see
Chocolate cake recipe)
Cream filling (see recipe)
Ganache (see recipe, or
Other fudgy chocolate
Icing)
Cut cone-shaped hole in top of cupcake. Remove cone and cut off tip.
Fill cavity in cupcake with cream filling. Replace cone top. Ice with
soft ganache or icing.
