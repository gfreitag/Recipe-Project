Title: Grinder
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
French loaf, split
3 sl Salami
3 sl Mortadella
3 sl Provolone cheese
3 sl Swiss cheese
10    Rings pickled banana
Peppers
Olive oil
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Open up loaf and layer all the goodies
inside. Drizzle with olive oil and bake in oven until warm and crispy.

Yield: 1 serving
