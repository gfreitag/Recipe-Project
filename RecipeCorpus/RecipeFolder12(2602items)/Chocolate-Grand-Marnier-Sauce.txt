Title: Chocolate Grand Marnier Sauce
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Heavy cream
1 tb Butter
1/4 c  Grand Marnier
4 oz Semisweet chocolate, finely
Chopped
In a small saucepan, heat the cream and butter.  Add the Grand
Marnier. Whisk then bring to a boil.  Remove from the heat and whisk
in chocolate pieces until all the chocolate is incorporated. Serve
with the souffle.

Yield:  about 2 1/2 cups
