Title: Barbecued Oysters And Razor Clams With Spicy Chili Vinega
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
2    Dozen oysters
2    Dozen Razor clams
2 tb Chopped fresh jalapeno
Peppers
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
2 tb Finely chopped red onion
6    Mint leaves, chiffonade
Preheat grill or barbecue.

Scrub and clean oysters and razor clams and drain.

In a small mixing bowl, place chopped peppers, vinegar, sugar, salt,
onion and mint and stir together. Place shellfish on grill and cook
until shells open. remove and place on platter covered with rock
salt. Place dipping sauce in center and serve with cocktail forks.

Yield: 4 servings
