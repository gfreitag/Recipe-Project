Title: Champagne Sabayon 1
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
6 lg Egg yolks
1 tb Minced shallots
1/2 ts Pure vanilla extract
1/2 c  Champagne
Salt
Freshly ground white pepper
2 ts Snipped fresh chives
Combine all of the ingredients in a stainless-steel bowl. Set the
bowl over a pot of simmering water and whisk until the mixture begins
to thicken, about 2 minutes. Remove from the heat and serve. Yield: 1
1/2 cups
