Title: Sauce For Vegetables (Recipe Courtesy Of Lidia Bastianich
Categories: Taste
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Grated Pecorino Romano
1/4 c  Extra virgin olive oil
1    Cooked carrot, from stock
1 c  Vegetable stock
1 ts Fresh lemon juice
In a blender, combine all ingredients, slowly adding the oil. Blend
until smooth, adding extra stock if too dense. The texture should be
that of hollandaise sauce.
