Title: Mango Margaritas
Categories: 2hot
Yield: 4 servings
1    Banana, peeled, sliced
1    10 ounce mango, peeled,
Pitted, sliced
1/3 c  Tequila
2 tb Triple Sec
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Fresh lime juice
1 c  Ice cubes
Combine all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth and frothy.
Pour into chilled glasses and serve immediately.

Yield: 4 servings
