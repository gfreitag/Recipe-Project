Title: Berry French Toast With Fois Gras
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Whole milk
1    Egg
1 ts Grated lemon zest
1 ts Grated orange zest
4    (3-inch) slices of brioche
2 tb Butter
4    (2 ounce) slices of fois
Gras
1/2 c  Raspberries
1/2 c  Blueberries
1/2 c  Blackberries
1 c  Raspberry coulis
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
Black pepper
1 c  Caramel sauce in squeeze
Bottle
Powdered sugar
In a mixing bowl, whisk the milk, eggs and zest together. Soak the
bread in the milk egg mixture. In a sauté pan, melt the butter. When
the butter is hot, pan-fry the bread for 2 minutes on each side or
until golden brown. Remove from the pan. In a hot sauté pan, sear the
fois for 30 seconds on each side. Remove from the pan and sauté the
berries in the fat. Add the coulis. Stir in the balsamic vinegar.
Season with black pepper. Place the sauce in the center of the plate.
Pile the French toast in the center of the sauce. top with the fois
gras. Garnish with caramel sauce and powdered sugar.

Yield: 4 servings
