Title: Fresh Herb Salad
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Fresh purslane, washed and
Patted dry
1/4 c  Fresh chervil sprigs, washed
And patted dry
1/4 c  Lovage leaves, washed and
Patted dry
1/4 c  Tarragon leaves, washed and
Patted dry
1/4 c  Viola flowers, washed and
Patted dry
1/4 c  Small basil leaves, washed
And patted dry
1/4 c  Snipped chives
Drizzle of extra virgin
Olive oil
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
Combine all of the herbs in a mixing bowl. Toss the herbs lightly in
the oil. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immedietly.

Yield: 1 3/4 cup
