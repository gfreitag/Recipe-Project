Title: Salmon Croquettes
Categories: Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lg Potato
1 lg Can salmon, drained
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
1    Egg, beaten
Salt and pepper
Cracker meal
Peel and boil potatoes 'til soft enough to mash.  Combine with salmon,
onion, egg and seasoning in a large bowl.  Beat on low speed with
mixer until well blended.  Roll in cracker meal.  Shape into any size
you want and fry in 1/2 inch oil in heavy skillet until golden brown.
