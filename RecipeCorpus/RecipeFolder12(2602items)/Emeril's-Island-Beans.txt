Title: Emeril's Island Beans
Categories: Ethnic, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 8 Servings
1 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 lb Chorizo; dried and chopped
2 c  Chopped onions
1 sm Chili pepper; finely minced
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
2 tb Chopped garlic
1 lb Dried black beans
10 c  Chicken stock
1 tb Butter
In a large saucepan, over medium heat, add the oil. When the oil is
hot, add the sausage and brown for 2 minutes. Add the onions and
peppers. Season with salt and pepper. Saute for 3 to 4 minutes or
until the vegetables are wilted. Stir in the garlic and black beans.
Saute for 1 minute. Add the stock and bring to a boil, reduce the
heat to medium-low and simmer until the beans are creamy and tender,
about 2 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally. Stir in the butter and
reseason wit salt and pepper.
