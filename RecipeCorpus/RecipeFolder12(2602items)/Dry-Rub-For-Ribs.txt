Title: Dry Rub For Ribs
Categories: Taste
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Paprika
2 tb Celery salt
2 tb Salt
2 tb Ground black pepper
2 tb Cumin
2 tb Packed dark brown sugar
1 tb White sugar
1 tb Ground oregano
1 tb Cayenne pepper
2 ts Grated lemon peel
2 ts Sage leaves or rosemary,
Crumbled
2    Bay leaves, crumbled
1 ts Dry mustard
Combine all ingredients. Store in a jar with tightfitting lid. Makes
enough to coat 3 to 4 slabs of ribs.

Yield: Makes about 1 1/2 Cups
