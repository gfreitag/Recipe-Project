Title: Gnocchi In Creamy Red Salsa
Categories: Hot
Yield: 4 servings
Finished Gnocchi cooked as
Above
2 c  Red Tomato Salsa
1/2 c  Crema, creme fraiche, or
Sour cream
1/2 c  Grated Manchego (Mexican
Melting cheese) or Muenster
Cheese
1/2 bn Fresh cilantro, washed and
Chopped
Bring salsa and cream to boil in medium ovenproof skillet or
casserole. Add cheese and gnocchi. Return to a boil and transfer to
oven. Bake until sauce thickens and is bubbly and a slight crust
forms on top, about 7 minutes. Garnish with cilantro and serve
immediately.

Yield: six appetizers or four entrees
