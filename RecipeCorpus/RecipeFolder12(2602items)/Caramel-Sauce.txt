Title: Caramel Sauce
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Heavy cream
In a heavy-bottomed saucepan combine sugar with 1/4 cup water and
bring to a boil, stirring often.  Cook, stirring occasionally, until
mixture is a deep nutty-brown color and consistency of thin syrup,
about 10 minutes. Remove from heat.

Carefully stir in cream and return sauce to high heat.  Boil, stirring
often, 2 minutes.  Best if used immediately.

Yield: about 3/4 cup
