Title: Jamaican Spiced Vegetables
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Cayenne pepper
1 ts Cinnamon, ground
1 ts Nutmeg, ground
1 ts Allspice, ground
2 ts Chives, dried
2 ts Thyme, dried
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Dried minced onion
1 tb Salt
2    Ears sweet corn, broken in
1    Plantain, green, quartered
In skin
1    Sweet potato, slice 1/4-inch
Thick and blanched
Oil to drizzle
Combine spices in a bowl by hand or in food processor. Store in an
airtight container.

Sprinkle vegetables with spice and drizzle with oil. Grill 5 minutes
per side. Serve with Jamaican Marinated Pork.
