Title: Coleslaw
Categories: Taste
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sour cream
4 ts Cider vinegar
4 ts Fresh lemon juice
1 tb Brown mustard
2 ts Drained horseradish
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Celery seed
Salt and fresh black pepper
5 c  Shredded cabbage
In a large bowl whisk together all ingredients except cabbage; season
to taste with salt and pepper. Add cabbage and toss to combine. Chill,
covered, for at least 6 hours or overnight.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings
