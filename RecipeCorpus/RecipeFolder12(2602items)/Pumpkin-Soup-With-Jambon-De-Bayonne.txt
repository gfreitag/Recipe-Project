Title: Pumpkin Soup With Jambon De Bayonne
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Duck fat
8    Spanish onions, chopped into
1/4    Inch dice
1    Turnip, chopped into 1/4inch
Dice
10    Cloves garlic, finely
Chopped
4 c  Fresh pumpkin, peeled,
Seeded and cut into 1/2inch
Cubes
4 oz Jambon de Bayonne, sliced
Thin, then cut into 1/4inch
Julienne
1/2 c  Creme fraiche plus 1/2 cup
6 c  Chicken stock
3 tb Whole fresh tarragon leaves
In a 6 quart soup pot, heat oil until just smoking. Add onions,
turnip and garlic and cook until light golden brown, about 8 to 10
minutes. Add pumpkin and cook until softened, about 5 to 8 minutes.
Add Jambon, creme fraiche and chicken stock and bring to boil. Reduce
heat and simmer 1 hour. Season with salt and pepper. Soup should be
almost a loose puree. Serve in a warm, hollowed out pumpkin, ladling
into smaller bowls made of pumpkins. Add dollop of creme fraiche and
sprinkle with tarragon. Yield: 6 servings
