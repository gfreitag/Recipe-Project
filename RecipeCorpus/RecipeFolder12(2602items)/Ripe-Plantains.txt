Title: Ripe Plantains
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2 lg Ripe plantains
Vegetable oil as needed
Salt and freshly ground
Black pepper
Cut plantains in half and peel. Slice them crosswise on the diagonal
into 1/4inch ovals. In a 10inch iron skillet heat oil over moderate
heat until hazy. Fry the plantains for about 4 minutes a side,
turning them once. They should look golden brown. Drain on paper
towels and season well with salt and pepper.

Yield: 4 servings
