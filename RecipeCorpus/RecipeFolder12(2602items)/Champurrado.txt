Title: Champurrado
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
10 1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Masa harina
2 oz Mexican chocolate or
Bittersweet chocolate,
Chopped
1    Cinnamon stick
2 tb Brown sugar
Mix 1 cup water with masa harina. Let stand 15 minutes. Bring
remaining 1 1/2 cups water to boil in heavy medium saucepan. Strain
masa harina mixture into boiling water. Add chocolate and stir until
melted and smooth. Add cinnamon stick. Stir over low heat until
mixture thickens or whip to frothy consistency with a molinillo,
about 5 minutes. Stir in brown sugar.

Yield: 4 servings
