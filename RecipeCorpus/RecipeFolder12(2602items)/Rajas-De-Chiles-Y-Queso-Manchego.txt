Title: Rajas De Chiles Y Queso Manchego
Categories: 2hot
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Peanut oil
1/2 c  Whole epazote leaves
9    Poblano or Anaheim chiles,
Roasted, peeled, seeded, and
Cut in strips
1 ts Sea salt, or to taste
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1 c  Milk
12 oz Queso Manchego or Muenster
Cheese, cut into thick
Slices
Over a mediumhigh heat, in a mediumsize cast iron skillet, heat the
oil until it smokes. Add the whole epazote leaves and fry until they
wilt, stirring all the time, about 2 minutes. Add the chile strips
and salt and fry about 3 minutes, stirring all the time.

Reduce the heat to low. Stir in the cream and milk. Add the pieces of
cheese and cook until the cheese melts.

Serve hot, over Arroz Blanco.

Yield: 6 servings
