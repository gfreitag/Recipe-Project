Title: Emeril's Southwest Seasoning (Southwest Spice)
Categories: Emeril, Spices
Yield: 1 /2
2 tb Chili powder
2 ts Ground cumin
2 tb Paprika
1 tb Dried oregano
1 tb Ground coriander
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1 tb Garlic powder
1 ts Crushed red pepper
1 tb Salt
1 ts Black  pepper
Combine all ingredients thoroughly.
