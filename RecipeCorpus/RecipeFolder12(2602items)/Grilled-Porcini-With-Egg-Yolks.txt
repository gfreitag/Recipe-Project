Title: Grilled Porcini With Egg Yolks
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Fresh porcini or cepes
3 tb Extra virgin olive oil plus
2    Tablespoons
4    Eggs, jumbo
Preheat grill.

Slice mushrooms 1/4-inch thick and drizzle with oil and salt and
pepper. Place mushrooms on grill and cook until tender and juicy,
about 2 minutes per side. Meanwhile, heat remaining oil in a
non-stick 12-inch pan until just smoking. Crack eggs, eareful to
maintain unbroken yolds, into pan and cook eggs until whites have
set. Remove pan from heat and allow to rest 3 minutes. Remove
mushrooms to serving plater. Cut away whites of eggs and gingerly
place egg yolks on top of mushrooms and serve immediately.

Yield: 4 as tapas
