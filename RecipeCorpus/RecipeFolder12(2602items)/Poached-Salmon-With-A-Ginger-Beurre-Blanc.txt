Title: Poached Salmon With A Ginger Beurre Blanc
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
1    Inch piece of peeled fresh
Gingerroot, sliced thinly,
1 lg Garlic clove, mashed with
Flat side of a knife
2    Lemon slices
2 tb Soy sauce
1 c  Water
Two 1/2-inch thick salmon
Steaks (about 6 ounces
Each)
In a straight sided saute pan, combine the ginger, garlic, lemon
slices, soy sauce, and water.

Bring to a boil, add the salmon and poach until done, about 5 minutes.
Transfer the salmon to a serving plate and serve with the ginger
beurre blanc.

Yield: 2 servings
