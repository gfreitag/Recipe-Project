Title: Roasted Garlic Vinaigrette
Categories:
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------TOO HOT TAMALES #TH6315-----------------------
12    Garlic cloves, roasted and
Peeled
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1 tb Honey
1    Limes; Juice of
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 ts Coarse salt
1/2 ts Black pepper, ground
Combine the garlic, vinegar, honey and lime juice in a blender and
puree until smooth. With the motor running, slowly add the oil until
emulsified. Season with salt and pepper.

To roast garlic: Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Drizzle a tiny bit of
olive oil over unpeeled garlic cloves, and wrap in aluminum foil.
Place on a cookie sheet and bake about 1 hour, or until soft
throughout. Set aside to cool, then peel.
