Title: Skirt Steak Brochettes
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
2 tb Minced shallots
4 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Cracked black pepper
Juice and zest of 1 lemon
1/2 lb Flank steak, cut with the
Grain into 1/2-inch thick
Slices
12    Wood skewers, soaked 30
Minutes in warm water to
Prevent burning
Frisee or other salad greens
For garnish
Light tomato vinaigrette,
For serving
In a shallow bowl whisk together vinegar, shallots, olive oil, pepper,
lemon juice and zest. Thread meat on skewers and add to marinade; set
aside for 1 hour. Grill skewers on a preheated grill, a few minutes
per side. Serve 3 per person, garnished with salad greens and a
spoonful of vinaigrette.

Yield: 4 servings
