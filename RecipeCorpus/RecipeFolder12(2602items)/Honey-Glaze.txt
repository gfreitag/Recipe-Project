Title: Honey Glaze
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Confectioners' sugar
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 ts Vanilla
In a mixing bowl whisk sugar with honey and vanilla to a maple sugar
consistency. Add about 3 tablespoonfuls hot water, a spoonful at a
time, until thinned to glazing constintency.

Yield: enough to glaze about 1 1/2 Dozen Doughnuts
