Title: Toasted Almond Brittle
Categories: Baker
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Sugar
Water to dissolve
3 tb Butter
3 c  Toasted almonds
Bring sugar and water to a boil in a heavy sauce pan. Brush down
sides of pan with a water to prevent sugar from crystallizing. Cook
until amber in color and water has evaporated. Carefully stir in
butter with a wooden spoon until completely blended. Stir in almonds.
Pour onto a greased 1/2-inch sheet pan. Cool. Yield: 3 cups
