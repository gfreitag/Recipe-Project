Title: Pan Con Tomatoes
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
1    Loaf crusty baguette, cut
Into 12 slices
6    Ripe plum tomatoes, sliced
In half
1/4 c  Extra virgin olive oil
Salt and pepper
Preheat grill.

Place bread on grill and cook until lightly toasted. Remove and
squeeze juice and seeds from half a tomato on to each piece of bread.
Drizzle each slice with oil, season with salt and pepper and serve
warm.

Yield: 4 as tapas
