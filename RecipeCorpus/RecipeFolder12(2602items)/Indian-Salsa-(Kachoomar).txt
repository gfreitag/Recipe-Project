Title: Indian Salsa (Kachoomar)
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Chopped tomatoes
1 c  Chopped onions
1 c  Chopped cucumber
1 c  Chopped cilantro leaves and
Tender stems
2 tb Thinly-sliced green chilies,
Seeds discarded
1/2 ts Roasted cumin seeds
Juice of 1 lemon
Coarse salt to taste
Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and serve immediately with
Tandoori Chicken or cover and refrigerate. Keeps for 5 days.

Yield: 4 servings
