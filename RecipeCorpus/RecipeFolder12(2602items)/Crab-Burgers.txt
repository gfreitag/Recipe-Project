Title: Crab Burgers
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Crabmeat, backfin or
Special
1/2 sm Onion, finely diced
1/4    Green pepper, finely diced
1 tb Mayonnaise
ds Old Bay Seasoning
8 sl Mozzarella cheese
8    Hamburger rolls
Cut hamburger rolls in half.  Butter each half and toast lightly in
broiler.  In a bowl combine crabmeat, onion, pepper, mayonnaise, and
Old Bay Seasoning.  Cover each of the 8 rolls thickly with crabmeat
mixture, and top with a slice of cheese.  Place 8 burgers on a cookie
sheet and bake in a 350 degree oven, until crabmeat is heated through
and cheese is melted.  Remove from oven, top with other half of roll,
and serve.
