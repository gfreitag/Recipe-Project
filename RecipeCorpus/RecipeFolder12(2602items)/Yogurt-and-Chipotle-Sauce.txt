Title: Yogurt and Chipotle Sauce
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 sm Can of chipotle in adobo
1 c  Plain yogurt
In a blender, combine 2 tablespoons of chipotle with 1 cup yogurt. If
you want to blend in an entire can you will need to increase yogurt
to 4 cups. Sauce is spicy unless you reduce amount of pepper for a
milder sauce.
