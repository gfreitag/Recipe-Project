Title: Coconut Ice
Categories: 2hot
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
4 c  Coconut milk, preferably
Fresh
2 ts Vanilla
Place the sugar and water in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil.
Cool, then whisk in the coconut milk and vanilla. Pour into a
container and refrigerate until cold. Transfer to an ice cream maker
and mix according to machine's directions. Yield: 1 quart
