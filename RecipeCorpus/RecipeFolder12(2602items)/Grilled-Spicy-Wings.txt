Title: Grilled Spicy Wings
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
3    Cloves garlic, chopped
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice
2 tb Olive oil
1 tb Chipotle puree
1 tb Finely chopped parsley
1 tb Finely chopped oregano
20    Chicken wings
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
Mix together all ingredients in a medium bowl. Add the chicken wings
and toss to coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours. Grill
wings.

Yield: 4 servings
