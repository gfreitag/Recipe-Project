Title: Cafe De Olla
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
8 c  Water
2 sm Cinnamon sticks
3    Whole cloves
4 oz Dark brown sugar, or
Piloncillo
1    Square semisweet chocolate
Or Mexican chocolate
4 oz Ground coffee
Bring the water to a boil, the add the cinnamon, cloves, sugar, and
chocolate. When the liquid comes to a boil again, skim off any foam.
Reduce the heat to low and make sure the liquid does not boil. Add
the coffee, and let it steep for 5 minutes.

Serve the coffee in an earthenware pot with a ladle.

Yield: 8 servings
