Title: Shrimp Sauce Piquante
Categories: Shrimp
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Cooking oil
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 cn Tomato paste
1 cn Tomato sauce
1 md Bell pepper, chopped
1 cn Rotel tomatoes, diced
4    6 c. water
Salt to taste
Cayenne pepper to taste
1 md Onion, chopped
2    Stalks celery, chopped
3 c  Rice, cooked
Make roux with oil and flour; brown flour until it turns a medium
brown. Add onion, bell pepper, and celery; cook until mixture sticks
to pot. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste and Rotel&#174; tomatoes; cook
about 1 hour. Add salt and cayenne pepper to taste. Add water to
ingredients, and cook another 2-3 hours.  During the last 30 minutes,
add 1-1/2 pounds deveined, cleaned shrimp.  Serve over rice.
