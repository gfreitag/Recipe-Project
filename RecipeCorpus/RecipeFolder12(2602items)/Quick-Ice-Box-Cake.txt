Title: Quick Ice Box Cake
Categories: Monday
Yield: 4 servings
sl Almond pound cake
Liqueur like Kirsch to taste
Sugared fruit like berries
Or kiwi, peaches, plums to
Taste
1 c  Whipped cream
Layer some cake slices in the bottom of a loaf pan; sprinkle with
liqueur and top with fruit and whipped cream.  Repeat with another
layer at least, ending with cake on top.  Cover and refrigerate until
cake is moistened.
