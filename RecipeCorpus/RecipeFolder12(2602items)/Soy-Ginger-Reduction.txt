Title: Soy Ginger Reduction
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 tb Light soy sauce
1/2 ts Each minced garlic and
Ginger
2 ts Sugar
2 tb Rice vinegar
Salt and pepper
Set ingredients in a small saucepan, heat and reduce until syrupy;
drizzle through a strainer, over tuna.
