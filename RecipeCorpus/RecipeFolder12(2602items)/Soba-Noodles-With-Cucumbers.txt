Title: Soba Noodles With Cucumbers
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Soba noodles
1/4 c  Soy sauce
4 dr Hot pepper oil
2 tb White vinegar
1/2 ts Finely minced fresh ginger
2 tb Sesame oil
1 lg Seedless cucumber, quartered
And diced
Black sesame seeds
Cook the noodles in a large pot of boiling water until tender. Drain
well. Whisk together the soy sauce, hot pepper oil, vinegar, ginger
and sesame oil and toss with the warm noodles. Add the cucumber and
top with the sesame seeds. Serve at room temperature.

Yield: 4 servings
