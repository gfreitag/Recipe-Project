Title: Glossy Chocolate Sauce
Categories: Baker
Yield: 4 servings
3 oz Unsweetened chocolate
7 oz Semisweet chcocolate
3 oz Corn syrup
1/2 c  Hot water
Combine chocolate, place over water bath, stir until melted. Whisk in
syrup and water, incorporating well. Serve warm.

Yield: 2 cups
