Title: Garlic Chips
Categories: Toohot, Hot
Yield: 4 servings
15    20 large cloves garlic,
Thinly sliced
3 tb Olive oil
1 ts Coarse salt
Heat olive oil in a medium skillet over moderate heat.  Add garlic
slices and sautJ until lightly browned, about 12 minutes.  Drain
garlic on paper towels.  Sprinkle with salt and serve.
