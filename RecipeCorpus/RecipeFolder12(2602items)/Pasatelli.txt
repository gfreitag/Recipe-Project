Title: Pasatelli
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Fresh bread crumbs
2 c  Grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
Plus 1/2 cup
3    Eggs
6 tb Unsalted butter, melted
1/2 ts Grated nutmeg
1/2 ga Boiling chicken stock
In a mixing bowl, stir together bread crumbs, 2 cups cheese, eggs,
butter and nutmeg until a ball is formed. Feed ball into meat grinder
set over boiling chicken stock and grind noodles into broth. They
will set as they hit the stock. When all the noodles are in broth,
lower heat to simmer and cook 5 to 6 minutes. Serve in deep bowls
with plenty of grated cheese.

Yield: 4 servings
