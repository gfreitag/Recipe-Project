Title: Softshell Crab Sandwich With Toasted Paprika Aioli
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Prepared mayonnaise
6    Cloves roasted garlic
2 ts Spanish paprika, toasted
pn Cayenne pepper
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
4    Softshell crabs, cleaned
2 tb Olive oil
2 c  Watercress, washed
4 lg Crusty rolls
Place the mayonnaise, garlic, toasted paprika, cayenne and lemon
juice in a blender and blend until smooth. Preheat grill. Brush the
crabs with the olive oil and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Grill. Place 1 crab on each roll, spread with aioli and top with
watercress.

Yield: 4 servings
