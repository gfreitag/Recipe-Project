Title: Minted Honeydew Limeade
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
3 lb Honeydew melon
1 1/2 c  Superfine granulated sugar
1 3/4 c  Fresh lime juice
3 tb Chopped fresh mint leaves
3 c  Cold water 1 cup cold ice
Cubes
Remove rind from melon, discarding it, and cut enough fruit into
1-inch pieces to measure 6 cups. In a blender puree melon in batches
with sugar, lime juice, and mint until completely smooth and pour
into a thermos or other container with water and ice. Shake or stir
limeade before serving.

Yield: about 9 cups
