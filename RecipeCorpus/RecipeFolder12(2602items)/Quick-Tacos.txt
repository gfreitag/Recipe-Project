Title: Quick Tacos
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Black beans, drained and
Rinsed
3/4 c  Sour cream
3/4 c  Salsa
Lime juice to taste
Guacamole
Grated cheddar cheese
Shredded lettuce
8    Taco shells
In the food processor, combine the beans, sour cream, and salsa. Puree
until smooth and transfer to a bowl and mix in lime juice to taste.

Put out remaining ingredients in bowls and let diners assemble their
own tacos.

Yield: 4 servings
