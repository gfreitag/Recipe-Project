Title: Chocolate Mexicana
Categories: Hot
Yield: 4 servings
3    Squares sweetened Mexican
Chocolate grated
8 c  Milk
Put the milk and chocolate into an earthenware pot and cook over a
low heat until the chocolate has melted. Stir constantly so that it
does not stick to the pan.

When the chocolate comes to a boil, lower the heat and beat
vigorously with a molinillo until a thick foam rises to the top.

Serve hot in earthenware mugs.

Yield: 8 servings
