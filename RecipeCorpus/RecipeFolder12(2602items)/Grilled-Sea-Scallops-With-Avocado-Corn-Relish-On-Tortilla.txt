Title: Grilled Sea Scallops With Avocado-Corn Relish On Tortilla
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
Avocado-Corn Relish-
2    Haas avocado, peeled, seeded
And coarsely chopped
1 c  Roasted corn kernels
3 tb Finely diced red onion
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped cilantro
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
2 tb Sour cream
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
12    Sea scallops
Olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
1    Bag baked or fried flour
Tortilla chips
1/4 c  Finely chopped parsely
Preheat grill. Brush scallops with oil and season with salt and
pepper to taste. Grill the scallops for 2 minutes on each side.
Arrange tortillas on a serving platter, top each with a layer of
relish and 1 scallop. Garnish with parsley.

Yield: 4 servings
