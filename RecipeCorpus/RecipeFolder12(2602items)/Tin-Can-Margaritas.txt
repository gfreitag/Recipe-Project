Title: Tin Can Margaritas
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1    Lime, cut in wedges
Coarse salt, for glasses
1 lg Can frozen limeade
1    Full can good-quality
Tequila
1/2 cn Triple-sec
1/2 cn Lemon juice
Ice
Rub rims of glasses with a lime wedge; place salt in a saucer and dip
rims. In a pitcher combine remaining ingredients, stir well and fill
up with ice. Serve in salt-rimmed glasses with lime wedges.

Yield: 1 pitcher
