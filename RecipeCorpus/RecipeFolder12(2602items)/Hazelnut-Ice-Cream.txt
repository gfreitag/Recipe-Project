Title: Hazelnut Ice Cream
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Chopped roasted hazelnuts
2 pt Vanilla ice cream, softened
1/4 c  Frangelico
Garnish: whipped cream,
Powdered sugar and mint
In a mixing bowl, combine the hazelnuts, ice cream, and Frangelico
together. Mix until fully incorporated. Spoon the mixture into a
freezer safe container with a lid. Place in the freezer and allow to
freeze, about 1 hour. Place a slice of the tart in the center of a
plate. Drizzle with the caramel sauce. Place a scoop of the ice cream
on the side of the tart. Garnish with whipped cream, powdered sugar
and mint.

Yields: 2 pints
