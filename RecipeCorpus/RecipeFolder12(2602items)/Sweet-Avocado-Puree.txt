Title: Sweet Avocado Puree
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2    Ripe chilled avocados,
Seeded, diced
1/4 c  Fresh lime or lemon juice
1/3 c  Confectioner's sugar or
More
Raspberries
Fresh mint sprigs or chopped
Pistachio nuts
With the back of a spoon, press the avocados through a sieve into a
bowl. Add the lime juice and sweeten with sugar.

Transfer the puree to 4 chilled wine glasses and garnish with
raspberries, mint and/or pistachio nuts.

Yield: 4 servings
