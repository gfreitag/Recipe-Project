Title: Tomato Vinaigrette
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
3 lb Ripe fresh tomatoes, peeled
And seeded and finely
Chopped
4 tb Olive oil
2 tb Champagne vinegar
2 tb Snipped chives
1 tb Chopped fresh tarragon
Salt and freshly ground
Black pepper
Combine tomatoes with olive oil and vinegar, chives and tarragon and
season with salt and pepper.

Yield: 2 cups
