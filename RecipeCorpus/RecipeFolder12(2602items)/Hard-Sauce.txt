Title: Hard Sauce
Categories: Ash
Yield: 4 servings
6 tb Unsalted butter (3/4 stick)
Softened
1 c  Sifted powdered sugar
1 tb Finely grated orange zest
1 tb Orange juice
Cream butter with a whip attachment with an electric mixer.  Add
sugar a little at a time beating continuously.  Beat in orange zest
and juice and continue beating until mixture is light and fluffy.
Transfer to a serving bowl and serve with warm steamed pudding.

Yield: 8 to 10 servings
