Title: Warm Haricots Verts Relish
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
6 oz Bacon, chopped
1 c  Julienne red onions
1 lb Haricots verts, blanched
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
1 tb Chopped garlic
In a large saute pan, over medium heat, render the bacon until
crispy. Pour off half of the grease. Add the onions and saute for 2
minutes. Add the haricots verts. Season with salt and pepper. Saute
for 2 minutes. Add the garlic and continue to cook for 1 minute.
Remove from the heat.

Yield: 6 to 8 servings
