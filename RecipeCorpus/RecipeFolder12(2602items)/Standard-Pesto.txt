Title: Standard Pesto
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Fresh basil leaves
2    Garlic cloves, chopped
1/4    (2 ounces) pine nuts
Salt and pepper
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1/2 c  Olive oil
Place the basil, garlic, pine nuts and 1/2 teaspoon salt in the food
processor, grind into a paste. Add the cheese and grind until
thoroughly blended. With the machine running slowly add the oil.
Adjust the seasonings. Pour into a bowl.

Yield: 1 generous cup
