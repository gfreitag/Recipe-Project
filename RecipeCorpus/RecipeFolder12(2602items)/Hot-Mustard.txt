Title: Hot Mustard
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Powdered hot mustard
1 ts White vinegar
1/2 ts Sesame oil
2 tb Water
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl, adding more or less water
depending on desired thinness of sauce. Let stand for 10 minutes, then
serve as a dipping sauce for Egg Rolls.

Yield: about 1/4 cup
