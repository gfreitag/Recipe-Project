Title: Romanoff
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Grand Marnier
1 c  Sour cream
2 c  Sweeten whipped cream
1 pt Raspberries
1 pt Blueberries
1 pt Blackberries
1 pt Loganberries
1 pt Strawberries, halved
2 tb Chiffonade of mint
Powdered sugar in shaker
In a mixing bowl, whisk the Grand Marnier and sour cream together.
Fold in the whipped cream. Arrange the berries on a platter. Pour the
cream mixture over the top of the berries. Garnish with the mint and
powdered sugar.

Yield: 4 servings
