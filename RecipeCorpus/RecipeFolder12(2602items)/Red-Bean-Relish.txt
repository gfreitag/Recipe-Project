Title: Red Bean Relish
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Diced andouille sausage
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
1/4 c  Chopped celery
2 tb Minced red bell peppers
2 tb Peeled chopped tomatoes
2 ts Minced garlic
1 ts Creole Seasoning
1 c  Cooked red kidney beans
Salt and black pepper
Heat oil in a medium skillet over medium-high heat. When oil is hot,
add sausage, green onions, celery, bell peppers, tomatoes, garlic and
Creole seasoning; sautJ 2 minutes. Stir in beans, salt, and 3 turns
of pepper; sautJ 1 minute more.

Yield: about 2 cups
