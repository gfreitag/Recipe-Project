Title: Bordelaise Sauce
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Minced shallots
2 tb Minced garlic
1 ts Creole Seasoning, or to
Taste
1 c  Dry red wine
1/2 ts Salt
Black pepper
1 1/2 c  Reduced veal or beef stock
1 tb Chopped green onion
Combine shallots, garlic and Creole seasoning in a small saucepan
over high heat and cook 1 minute.  Add wine and bring to a boil. Add
salt and 6 turns pepper.  Stir in stock and bring back to a boil.
Reduce heat to simmering and cook, skimming off fat and impurities
several times, about 10 minutes. Turn up heat to high, skim any
remaining impurities from the top of the sauce and cook 1 1/2 minutes
longer. Add green onions before serving.

Yield: 1 1/2 cups
