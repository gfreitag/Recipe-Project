Title: VEGETABLE CASSEROLE
Categories: Vegetables, Ada
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM-------------------LISA CRAWLEY  TSPN00B------------------------
1 c  Peas
1 c  Green Beans
1 c  Carrots; sliced
1 c  Mushrooms
1    Egg
1 ts Margarine; melted
1/2 c  Milk
Salt and Pepper; to taste
Veg. Cooking Spray
Cook veg. in sm. amt. of boiling salted water til crisp-tender;
drain. Chop veg. fine.  Whip egg til lemon colored; add margarine and
milk. Blend well. Add chopped veggies, salt and pepper. Pour into
baking dish coated w/ veg. spray. Cover and bake at 350 for 45 min.
or til set. Yield: 8 servings Exchange/serving: 1 vegetable
Calories/serving: 36
