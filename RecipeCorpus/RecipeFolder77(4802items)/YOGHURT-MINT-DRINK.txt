Title: YOGHURT MINT DRINK
Categories: Beverages, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
150 g  Full Cream Yoghurt
1/2    Lemon (The Juice)
1    Mint Sprig
1 tb Honey
1    Egg Yolk
1    Scoop Lemon Sorbet
Place yoghurt, lemon juice, washed and chopped mint, honey, egg yolk
and sorbet in an electric blender and whip together until frothy.
Serve the drink in a longdrink glass garnished with a lemon slice.
Approx
280    cal.
