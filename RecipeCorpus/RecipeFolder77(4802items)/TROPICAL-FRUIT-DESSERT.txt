Title: TROPICAL FRUIT DESSERT
Categories: Fruit
1/2 lb storebought fruit salad from
salad bar
Yield: 1 Servings
:
1    star fruit or carambola --
peeled and sliced
:
1    kiwi -- peeled and sliced
1 TB sugar
3 TB grand marnier
fresh mint leaves
:

Drain off any excess liquid from storebought fruit salad. In a large
bowl toss together all of the fruit, sugar and the grand marnier.
Serve mixed fruit with mint leaves as garnish.
