Title: TUNA-CELERY CHOWDER
Categories: Soups, Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Celery -- chopped
1    Onion, small -- chopped
Fine
1 1/2 c  Water
1 cn Cream of celery soup
1 cn Tuna -- preferably chunky
1/4 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
Boil celery and onion together in water until tender. Add cream of
celery soup, stir well. Drain and add tuna, paprika and salt. Heat
together under low flame for 5 minutes.
