Title: ZUCCHINI HEALTH BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
3    Eggs
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Honey
1/4 c  Wheat germ
2 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Bran
1 c  Oil
2 c  Grated unpeeled zucchini
1/4 ts Baking powder
3/4 c  Chopped nuts
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Raisins
3 1/2 ts Cinnamon
Mix in order given.  Bake in 2 greased loaf pans at 325 degrees for
60 to 70 minutes.
