Title: WHEAT BREAD-BREAD MACHINE
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
Pat Ballard (MGFN03A)
1 1/8 c  Water
3 tb Honey
1/3 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
Put liquid ingredients in first, with yeast in last. Bake on light
setting, if you have one.  Makes a 1 lb. loaf.
