Title: TOFU BURGERS (HOWARD)
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 8 Servings
1    Onion finely chopped
24 oz Tofu; crumbled & cool
2 c  Water
1/2 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/8 ts Pepper
1. Saute Onion in Small Amount Of Water Until Tender & Transparent.
Remove >From Heat & Cool.2.  Mix Onion With Rest Of Ingredients &
Form Into 8 Patties.3. Heat A Nonstick Skillet & Coat With Cooking
Spray.4. Add 4 Patties, Cover & Cook Over Low Heat, About 5 Min. Per
Side.Repeat With Remaining Patties. (Fat 3.9. Chol. 0. Per Patty.)
