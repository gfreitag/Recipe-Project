Title: YOGURT CILANTRO SAUCE
Categories: New, Text, Import
2 c  plain yogurt
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  chopped fresh cilantro
2 TB minced fresh garlic
1 TB lime juice
1 TB honey
Salt and freshly ground
white pepper to taste
:
:

In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients and mix to blend. Season to
taste with salt and pepper. May be made one day ahead, covered and
stored in the refrigerator.
