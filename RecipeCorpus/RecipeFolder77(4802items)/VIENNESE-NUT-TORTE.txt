Title: VIENNESE NUT TORTE
Categories: Cakes, Chocolate, Tortes
Yield: 12 Servings
7    Eggs -- separated
2/3 c  Sugar
5 oz Bittersweet chocolate --
Grated
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 c  Nuts -- ground
Chocolate icing
Bear yolks and sugar till light.  Add chocolate, cinnamon and nuts.
Fold in stiffly beaten whites.  Put into a tube pan and bake 45 min
at 325F or till done.
