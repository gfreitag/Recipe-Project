Title: WARM CHIPPED BEEF DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Dips, Holiday
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ea 8 oz. pkgs. cream cheese
4 tb Milk
1 c  Sour cream
4 tb Minced onions
4 tb Chopped green pepper
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ea 3 oz. pkgs. chipped beef,
1 x  Chopped
Put in pan and bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Keep warm when
serving.

From "Hostess Delights", undated.  Entered 11-93.
