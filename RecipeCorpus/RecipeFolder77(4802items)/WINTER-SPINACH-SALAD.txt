Title: WINTER SPINACH SALAD
Categories: Autumn, Salads/dres, Winter
Yield: 4 Servings
1 bn Spinach -- roughly chopped
1/3 c  Dried sour cherries
3    Scallions -- thinly sliced
1    Green apple -- cored and
Sliced
1/2 c  Seedless red grapes --
Halved
1/4 c  Walnuts -- coarsely chopped
2 ts Apple cider vinegar
2 tb Olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
1/3 c  Goat cheese -- crumbled
Combine spinach, cherries, scallions, apple slices, grapes and
walnute. Whisk together vinegar and olive oil; seasion to taste with
salt and pepper. Pour vinaigrette over salad; toss in cheese and
arrange on salad plates.
