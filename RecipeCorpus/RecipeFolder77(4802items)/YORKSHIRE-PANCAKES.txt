Title: YORKSHIRE PANCAKES
Categories: Breakfast, Special
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Flour
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
1/8 lb Butter (1/2 stick)
1 x  Lemon juice
Heat oven to 450 degrees. Let eggs warm to room temperature. Beat
slightly. Add flour and milk to eggs, mix thoroughly. Melt butter in
long, over-proof baking dish. Pour batter. Bake 15 minutes until
pancake rises. Serve sprinkled with lemon juice and powdered sugar.
