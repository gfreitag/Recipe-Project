Title: VENISON & 4-BEANS
Categories: Wild game
Yield: 5 Servings
2 lb Venison
1 lb Bacon
1 cn Pork and beans
1 cn Lima beans
1 cn Kidney beans
1 cn Navy beans
1/2    Onion, cut up
1    Green pepper, cut up
1 c  Mustard
1 c  Catsup
1 ts Brown sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
Brown venison and bacon.  Put all ingredients in crock pot and crook
for 4 hours on high temperature setting.
