Title: WELSH CAKES(WELSH)
Categories: Cakes, Welsh
Yield: 12 Cakes
1 lb Flour
1/2 ts Mixed spice
4 oz Margarine
1 ts Baking powder
4 oz Lard
1    Egg
4 oz Currants
Pinch salt
6 oz Sugar
2 tb Milk
(PICAU AR Y MAEN) Sift the flour, baking powder and salt together.
Rub in the fat and add the other dry ingredients. Beat the eggs
lightly and add with enough milk to make a firm paste. Roll out on a
floured board to a thickness of 4 inch and cut into rounds. Cook on a
greased griddle for about 3  minutes on  each side. until
golden-brown. Cool and sprinkle with sugar. Serve alone or with
butter.
