Title: VINAIGRETTES & VARIATIONS
Categories: Salad dres
Yield: 1 /2 c
MMMMM------------------------SMALL FEASTS-----------------------------
MMMMM------------------BASIC VINAIGRETTE, 1/2C-----------------------
2 tb Red OR white wine vinegar
6 tb Olive oil, to 8T
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Dijon
2 ts Shallots, minced, opt
MMMMM---------------------VARIATION: CHIVES--------------------------
1 tb Chives, minced
1 tb Shallots
1 c  VINAIGRETTE
MMMMM--------------------VARIATION: CUCUMBER-------------------------
3 tb Cucumber, grated
1 tb Chives, chpd
1 c  VINAIGRETTE
MMMMM---------------------VARIATION: GARLIC--------------------------
1 cl Garlic, processed into
1 c  VINAIGRETTE
MMMMM----------------------VARIATION: HERBS---------------------------
3    Sprigs parsley, minced
1 ts Dried tarragon
1 ts Fresh basil OR 1/2t dried
1 c  VINAIGRETTE
MMMMM--------------------VARIATION: TARRAGON-------------------------
Substitute tarragon vinegar
-for red or white in master
-recipe
Blend all until well combined.
