Title: TIDEWATER COLESLAW
Categories: Slaw, Thrill of t
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  White vinegar
1/3 c  Sugar
1 tb Celery seed
Salt and pepper -- to taste
1    Head green cabbage -- finely
Shredded
2    Carrots -- finely grated
In a small bowl, blend the mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, celery seed,
and salt and pepper to taste, and mix well.

In a large bowl, combine the cabbage and carrots. Pour the dressing
over the mixture and blend well. Refrigerate until serving time.
