Title: TOMATO SOUP CAKE (GERTRUDE INGRAHAM)
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Shortning
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Tomato soup
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon,nutmeg and cloves
1/2 c  Nuts (chopped)
1/2 c  Raisins (optional)
1 pk Cream cheese
1 1/2 c  Confectionary sugar
