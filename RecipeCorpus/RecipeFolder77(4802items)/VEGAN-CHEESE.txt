Title: VEGAN CHEESE
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz Firm tofu
1/3 c  Tahini
2 ts Umeboshi paste
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Oregano, dried
1/2 c  Water
Blend all of the ingredients until smooth, creamy, but thick. For a
creamier cheese, use up to a full cup of water. The creamier cheese
will be a little runny.

Source: "Friendly Foods" by Brother Ron Pickarski
