Title: TUNA FISH CURRY
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
2 ts Oil
2 ts Curry powder
1    Apple; peeled & chopped
1 cn Tuna; drained - 8 oz
1 cn Tomato sauce (8 oz) or 1 cup
2 tb Apple juice
2 c  Hot cooked rice
Approx. Cook Time:
Fat grams    per serving:
:10
Heat oil in wok . Add apple and stir fry over medium heat, 1-2
minutes. Stir in curry powder. Add tomato sauce. Bring to a boil, add
juice and tuna. Stir till heated. Serve over hot cooked rice.
