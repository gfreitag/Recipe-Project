Title: WALNUT PENUCHE DROPS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 30 Servings
1 c  Light brown sugar, packed
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/4 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Light cream
1 tb Butter
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 1/2 c  Coarsely chopped walnuts
DIRECTIONS: Mix first 4 ingredients in saucepan. Cook, stirring,
until a small amount of mixture forms a soft ball when dropped in
cold water (238-F). (This takes 25 to 30 minutes.) Remove from heat;
add butter and vanilla. Beat until blended; add walnuts, and stir
until coated. Drop quickly by dessert-spoonfuls onto waxed paper.
