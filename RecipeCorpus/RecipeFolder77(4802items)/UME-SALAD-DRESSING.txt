Title: UME SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Sauces, Dressings, Salads
Yield: 1 Recipe
2/3 c  EDEN Olive Oil
1/3 c  EDEN Ume Plum Vinegar
1    Garlic clove; peeled
1/2    Onion
Mix all ingredients in a blender until smooth.  Store, tightly
capped, in refrigerator.  Add more garlic if desired.
