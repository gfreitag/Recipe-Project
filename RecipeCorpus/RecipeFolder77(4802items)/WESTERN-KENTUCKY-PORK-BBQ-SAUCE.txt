Title: WESTERN KENTUCKY PORK BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
3 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Ketchup
3/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 ts Paprika
1 1/2 ts Black pepper
1 1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
2 ts Dry mustard
5 ts Onion powder
3 tb Salt
Mix all ingredients and boil for five minues, stirring frequently.

Source: Best Barbeque Recipes by Mildred Fischer
