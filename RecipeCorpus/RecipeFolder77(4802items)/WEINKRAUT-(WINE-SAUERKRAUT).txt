Title: WEINKRAUT (WINE SAUERKRAUT)
Categories: Side dish, White wine
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 1/3 c  Dry white wine
2 cn Sauerkraut,drained(16oz)
Melt butter in 10-inch skillet over low heat; add sauerkraut. Cover
and cook, stirring occasionally, 30 minutes; add wine. Heat to
boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer until liquid is absorbed,
about 45 minutes.
NOTE: This is a mild variation of sauerkraut. Fresh sauerkraut can be
used. Follow directions except - simmer until liquid is absorbed,
about 1 hour.
