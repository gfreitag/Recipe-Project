Title: VEGETABLE RICE
Categories: Rice
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
3 tb Butter
1 1/2 c  Mushroom, sliced
2 c  Broccoli, chopped
1 c  Tomato, diced
1 c  Rice, uncooked
2 c  Water
2 ea Chicken bouillon cubes
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 c  Cheese, grated
Cook onions in butter until golden; Add mushrooms, broccoli and
tomatoes and cook about 4 minutes. Add water and bouillon cubes and
bring to a boil. Stir in rice, salt and oregano. Cover and cook over
low heat, 20 minutes or until water is absorbed. Stir in parmesan
cheese, serve hot or cold.
