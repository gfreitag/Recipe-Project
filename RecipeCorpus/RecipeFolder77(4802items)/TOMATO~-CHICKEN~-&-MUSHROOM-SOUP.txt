Title: TOMATO~ CHICKEN~ & MUSHROOM SOUP
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Mushrooms -- sliced
2 tb Butter
55 oz Chicken broth
4 c  Cooked chicken -- diced
29 oz Stewed tomatoes
16 oz Tomato sauce
2    Envelopes
Dressing mix
1 1/2 c  Minute rice
Good seasons italian salad
Cook and stir mushrooms in hot butter; gradually stir in broth, then
add chicken, tomatoes, tomato sauce, and salad dressing mix. Bring to
a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 10 minutes. Stir in rice; cover
and remove from heat.  Let stand at least 5 minutes.
