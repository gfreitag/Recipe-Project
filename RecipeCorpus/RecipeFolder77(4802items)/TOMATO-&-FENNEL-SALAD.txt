Title: TOMATO & FENNEL SALAD
Categories: Salad
1 lg fennel bulb -- core and
thinly
slice
Yield: 6 Servings
:
:
4 md tomatoes -- cut in wedges
and seeded
:
1 ts Dijon mustard
3 TB red wine vinegar
3/4 c  olive oil
Salt and pepper
:
2 TB fennel frond -- or
cilantro -- finely chopped
:

Combine the fennel and the tomatoes. Mix together the Dijon mustard,
vinegar, and oil. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Pour over the
dressing the fennel and tomatoes. Top with the chopped fennel. See
Strawberry mouse. [patH mcRecipe]
