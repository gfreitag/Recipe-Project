Title: YELLOW PEA BROTH WITH TOMATOES
Categories: Soups, Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
2/3 c  Yellow split peas
1/2 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Coriander
8 c  Water
1/2 tb Peanut oil
1 ts Mustard seeds
3 lg Ripe tomatoes, peeled,
- seeded & chopped
1 tb Corn oil
Salt & pepper
2 tb Cilantro, chopped
Combine spolit peas, turmeric, coriander, water & oil in a large pot.
Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally, reduce heat, partially cover
& cook until peas are soft & break down when crushed.  Puree till
smooth.

Heat clean pot over moderately high heat, add mustard seeds & fry
till they begin to pop.  Add tomato & cook until it softens, 2 to 3
minutes. Add pureed split pea mixture. Stir in corn oil & season.
Serve hot or chiled, garnished with cilantro.

Adapted from Yamuna Devi, "Yamuna's Table"
