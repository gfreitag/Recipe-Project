Title: TOMATO & AVOCADO PINWHEEL SALAD
Categories: Salads, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
4 ea Tomatoes, cut into wedges
2 ea Avocadoes, peeled & pitted,
-- cut into thin wedges
2 tb Lime juice
MMMMM---------------------SWEET VINAIGRETTE--------------------------
1/2 c  Balsamic vinegar
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
3 tb Onion, grated
1 tb Sweetener
1/4 c  Olive oil
Sprinkle tomato & avocado wedges with lime juice.  On a serving
platter, arrange laternate slices of tomato & aovcado in a pinwheel
fashion. VINAIGRETTE: Whisk together vinegar, garlic, salt, onion &
sweetener. Gradually pour in the oil & whisk to blend. Pour this
vinaigrette over the wedges. Serve.
