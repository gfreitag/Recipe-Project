Title: TOFU FRENCH TOAST
Categories: Breakfast, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Soft or medium tofu
1 c  Water
4 tb Oil
1 pn Salt
1 ts Vanilla extract
3 ts Sugar
8 sl Wholewheat bread
Margarine or oil for frying
Put the tofu, water, oil, salt, vanilla extract & sugar in blender or
food processor & blend thoroughly. Pour the mixture into a shallow
bowl & dip in the slices of bread. Heat the margarine or oil in a
skillet & fry the dipped bread over a moderate heat until browned on
both sides. Serve with maple syrup or jam. Or, serve with a mixture
of sugar & cinnamon powder.
