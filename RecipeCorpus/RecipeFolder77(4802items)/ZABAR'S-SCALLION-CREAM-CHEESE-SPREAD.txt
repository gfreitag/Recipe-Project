Title: ZABAR'S SCALLION CREAM CHEESE SPREAD
Categories: New york, Breakfast
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Cream cheese
1/2 c  Sour Cream
pn Salt
pn Garlic powder
1/2 c  Chopped scallions
Combine Cream cheese, sour cream, salt and garlic powder in a large
bowl and stir until well mixed and smooth. Stir in scallions. Serve
on crackers or toasted bagels. Makes about 4 cups
