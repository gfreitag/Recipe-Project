Title: VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PHILLY.INQUIRER---------------------------
2 tb DIJON MUSTARD
1/4 c  RED VINEGAR
LIVE OIL
DROP OR 2 WORCESTERSHIRE SA
MAKE A SIMPLE VINAIGRETTE BY DISSOLVING 2 TABLESPOONS DIJON
MUSTARD IN 1/4 CUP RED VINEGAR.BEAT IN A GOOD QUALITY OLIVE OIL UNTIL
THE VINAIGRETTE THICKENS.YOU CAN FLAVOR THIS BASE WITH GARLIC,GRATED
CHEESE,SHALLOTS,EVEN DROP OR TWO OF WORCESTERSHIRE
SAUCE...............
