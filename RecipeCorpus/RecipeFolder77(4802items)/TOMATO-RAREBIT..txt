Title: TOMATO RAREBIT.
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz 30 g mature cheddar cheese
Finely grated.
1 ts Worcestershire sauce.
pn Cayenne pepper.
1 sl 1 oz 30 g wholemeal bread.
1 md Tomato sliced.
Sprig of parsley to garnish
1. In a small bowl mix together the cheese, Worcestershire Sauce and
cayenne pepper. 2. Toast the bread lightly and arrange the sliced
tomato on top. Spoon the cheese mixture over the surface and grill
until bubbling and golden. 3. Garnish with a sprig of parsley and
serve at once. Preparation 2 minutes, cooking 5 minutes.
