Title: TROUT IN ALFOIL - OZ
Categories: Fish, Oz
Yield: 2 Servings
2 ea Trout
1 tb Butter
1 tb Lemon juice
Clean trout then butter liberally both sides. Lay on alfoil and
squeeze lemon juice over fish. Wrap the fish in the foil and place on
a few coals to cook slowly.

Source: Bush Cooking by Max Bryant Published by the Kangaroo Press
ISBN 0 86417 230 3
