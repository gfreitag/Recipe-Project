Title: TINY  CHEESE CAKES
Categories: Desserts
1    whole vanilla wafer cookies
Yield: 22 Servings
2 pk ( 8 oz ea)  no fat cream
cheese
:
3/4 c  sugar
2 ea eggs or egg substitute

Place vanilla wafer in bottom of paper muffin cup liner. Mix cream
cheese, sugar, eggs.  Garnish with red cherry or green and red
candied cherries for Christmas.

Pour into muffin cups.  Bake 15 min at 375 degrees.
