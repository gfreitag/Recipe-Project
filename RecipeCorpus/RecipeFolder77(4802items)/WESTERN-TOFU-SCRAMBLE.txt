Title: WESTERN TOFU SCRAMBLE
Categories: Vegetarian, Breakfasts, Breakfast
Yield: 2 Servings
1 pk Mori-Nu silken firm tofu;
-crumbled (10.5 oz)
2 ts Margarine
1/4 c  Green onion; chopped
3/4 c  Red pepper; in julienne
-strips
1 c  Mushrooms; sliced
1 1/2 md Potato
3 tb Picante sauce
Turmeric
Microwave potato for 5-6 minutes or use baked potato.
Cool and slice thin.

Add crumbled tofu to melted margarine in teflon skillet. Add 2
dashes of turmeric and cook on high for
5    minutes.

Add vegetables and potato and cook 5 minutes or until vegetables are
tender crisp.

Stir in picante sauce and serve.

Makes two 1 1/3 cup servings.
