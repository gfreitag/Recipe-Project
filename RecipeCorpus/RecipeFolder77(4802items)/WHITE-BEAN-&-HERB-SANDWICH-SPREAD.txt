Title: WHITE BEAN & HERB SANDWICH SPREAD
Categories: Lacto
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Cooked and drained white
Beans
2 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1 tb Snipped chives
1 tb Chopped fresh dill (or 1
ts Dried dill)
1/4 ts Salt (can eliminate this if
Using canned beans)
1/8 ts Pepper
2 oz Non fat cream cheese
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
8 sl Favorite bread (recipe
Recommends dark rye)
1    Cucumber, peeled and thinly
Sliced
x  Lettuce
x  Sprouts
Combine first 8 ingredients in a food processor and process until
smooth. Taste for seasonings and add more herbs or lemon juice if
necessary. Spread mixture on bread, top with cucumber, lettuce and
sprouts. Put sandwiches together and slice.

From my "Best of Vegetarian Times" collection.
