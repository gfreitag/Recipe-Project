Title: TUNA NOODLE CASSEROLE.  VERY SIMPLE.
Categories: Maindish
1 lg Can tuna
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Bag wide egg noodles
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Cream of celery soup
Half cup grated cheddar
Cheese
:
:
1 tb Grated onion.

Cook noodles and drain tuna. Mix all ingredients together in a
buttered casserole. Top with a little of the cheese and some cracker
crumbs. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes or until bubbly.
