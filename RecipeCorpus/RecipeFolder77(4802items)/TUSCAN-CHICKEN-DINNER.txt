Title: TUSCAN CHICKEN DINNER
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Skinned and boned chicken
-breast halves
4    Garlic cloves, minced
1 tb Minced fresh rosemary
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn (15-ounce) cannellini beans,
-rinsed and drained
1/2 c  Dried tomatoes in oil,
-drained and chopped
1/2 c  Chopped purple onion
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Lemon juice
Fresh rosemary sprigs
Place each chicken breast half in center of a sheet of 14- x 12-inch
aluminum foil. Combine garlic, rosemary, and salt; rub evenly over
chicken. Stir together cannellini beans and next 4 ingredients, and
spoon evenly over chicken. Bring edges of foil together; fold down
loosely in locked folds. Crimp edges to seal. Place foil packets on a
baking sheet. Bake at 350 B0 for 35 to 40 minutes or until chicken is
done. Garnish, if desired.
