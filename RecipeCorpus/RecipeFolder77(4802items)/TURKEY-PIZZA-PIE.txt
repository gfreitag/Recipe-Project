Title: TURKEY PIZZA PIE
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Spaghetti sauce mix(1.5oz)
Water
1 cn Mushrooms,sliced(2.5oz)
1/4 c  Milk
1 ts Garlic salt
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese,grated
1 cn Tomato paste(6oz)
1/2 ts Oregano,crushed
1 lb Turkey,ground
1/4 c  Bread crumbs,seasoned
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Mozzarella,cheese,shredded
1. Combine in a saucepan the spaghetti sauce mix, tomato paste and
half a tomato paste can of water. 2. Add oregano and mushrooms; bring
to a boil. 3. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, 10 minutes, stirring
occasionally. 4. Combine turkey, milk, bread crumbs, garlic salt and
pepper. 5. Press turkey mixture evenly over bottom and sides of a
9-inch pie plate. 6. Spoon spaghetti sauce evenly into meat crust;
sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. 7. Bake in preheated 350'F. oven 30
minutes. 8. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese; bake 15 minutes longer.
9. Cool slightly; cut into wedges to serve.
