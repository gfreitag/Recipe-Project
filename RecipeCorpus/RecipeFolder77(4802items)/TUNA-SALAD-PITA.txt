Title: TUNA SALAD PITA
Categories: Sandwiches, Fish, Mexican
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2    Large pita bread
3 1/2 oz Water packed tuna fish
3/4 c  Lettuce
1 ds Celery seed
1 ds Black pepper
1 ds Onion powder
Line pita bread with lettuce.Mix spices with tuna.Stuff tuna
mixture into pita bread.NOTE: You can add mayonnaise,but this will
take more time and add more calories.
