Title: WALDORF SALAD (DISNEY KIDS)
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 7 Servings
2 1/2 c  Chopped apples
1 c  Cheddar cheese
1 c  Red or green grapes
1/2 c  Diced celery
1/2 c  Walnuts
1/2 c  Raisins
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
In a large bowl, toss all the ingredients and stir well to coat with
the mayonnaise and lemon juice. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
Serves 6 to 8.

~ - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

NOTES : Reviewed in "Vegetables your kids will eat," Family Fun
Magazine, May 1996.  This salad dresses up a plate nicely. Use fresh
ingredients and toss well to coat the apples and prevent them from
browning.
