Title: TOMATO & CORN SALSA
Categories: Salsa, Vegetables, Mexican
Yield: 4 Servings
1 sm Onion, chopped
3    Tomatoes, chopped
1    Jalapeno Pepper
1/2 c  Cooked Corn
1 tb Corn Oil
1 ts Chili Powder
Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor and whirr
until well blended.

Source: San Francisco Chronicle
