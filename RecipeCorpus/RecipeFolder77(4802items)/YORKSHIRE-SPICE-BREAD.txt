Title: YORKSHIRE SPICE BREAD
Categories: Gbmr, Spicy, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 pound loaf
1/2 c  Raisins
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 tb Maple syrup
2 tb Applesauce (or butter)
1    Egg
2 1/2 c  Bread flour + 2T
1/4 c  Powdered sugar
1 tb Orange peel, dried
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 ts Yeast
You may hold out the rasins and put them in when the machine beeps if
you use the raisin setting on the machine. Adapted from Gread Bread
Machine by PNewton vkbb14a
