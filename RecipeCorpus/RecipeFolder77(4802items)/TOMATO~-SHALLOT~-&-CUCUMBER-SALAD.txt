Title: TOMATO~ SHALLOT~ & CUCUMBER SALAD
Categories: New, Text, Import
6    peeled shallots, -- cut in
half if large
Yield: 4 Servings
:
1/2 c  olive oil
Salt and pepper
:
1/2 lb tomatoes (2 medium tomatoes)
cut into wedges
:
1 lg cucumber, peeled, seeded and
sliced -- into 1/2-inch pie
:
2 TB balsamic vinegar
1 TB chopped tarragon

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Toss the shallots with 1 tablespoon
olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Roast for 12 minutes.
Meanwhile, place tomatoes and cucumbers in a mixing bowl. Place
shallots in a small bowl. Mix in vinegar, tarragon, remaining olive
oil and season. Toss with the tomatoes and cucumbers.
