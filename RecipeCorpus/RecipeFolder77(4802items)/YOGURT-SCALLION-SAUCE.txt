Title: YOGURT SCALLION SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 8 Servings
1/4 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
2 tb Finely minced scallions
1/4 c  Sour cream
1 tb Minced fresh cilantro
1    Garlic, clove -- minced
Salt and black pepper
Combine all the sauce ingredients in a bowl and whisk to blend. Taste
and adjust seasonings.  Set aside.
