Title: WALDORF ASTORIA STEW
Categories: Stews
Yield: 1 Recipe
3 lb Stew meat, cut small
2 md Onions, chopped
1 c  Celery, chopped
4    Potatoes, large diced
2 c  Carrots, chopped
2 tb Tapioca
1 tb Sugar
1 cn Tomato soup
1    Clove garlic
Salt & pepper to taste
Mix, cover and bake 5 hours @ 250 degrees

Options:You can use a can of stewed tomatoes and a can of mushrooms
and cook for a shorter period of time in a 325 degree oven. Submitted
