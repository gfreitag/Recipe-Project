Title: WHITE SANGRIA
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Limes -- sliced
2    Lemons -- sliced
2 lg Red Delicious apples --
Sliced
2    750 milliliter bottles
Chablis
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Brandy
2    33 8 oz. bottles club soda
Combine sliced fruit, wine, sugar and brandy;  chill at least 2 hours.
Gently st ir in club soda and serve sangria over ice.
