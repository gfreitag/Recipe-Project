Title: TINA'S MEATLOAF
Categories: Beef
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Freshly made bread crumbs
1    Onion, chopped fine
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
2 lb Ground Beef
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
3/4 c  Milk
Preheat the oven to 350. Butter a loaf pan. Combine all the
ingredients in a large bowl; your freshly washed hands are best tools
for the job. Pat into the loaf pan and bakefor 45 minutes.
