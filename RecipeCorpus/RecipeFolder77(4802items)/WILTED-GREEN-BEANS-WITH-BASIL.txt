Title: WILTED GREEN BEANS WITH BASIL
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Low-fat
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Green beans, trimmed
1 ts Olive oil
2 ts Balsamic vinegar
1 tb Basil, minced
Salt & pepper
Bell peppers, red & yellow,
-- for garnish
Preheat oven to 500F.  In a large bowl, toss all the ingredients
until the beans are well coated.  Place on a non-stick baking sheet
in one layer (use more than one sheet if necessary). Roast for 10
minutes, stirring once. Serve at room temperature garnished with the
bell pepper slices.
