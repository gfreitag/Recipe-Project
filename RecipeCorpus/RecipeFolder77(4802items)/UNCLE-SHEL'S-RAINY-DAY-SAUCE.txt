Title: UNCLE SHEL'S RAINY DAY SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
35 oz Tomato sauce
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Molasses
1 lg Clove
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 ts Cayenne
1/2 ts Red pepper flakes
1 ts Sweet Hungarian paprika
1/8 ts Cloves -- ground
1    Chipotle pepper -- minced
1/8 ts Celery seed
1/8 ts Coriander
1 ts Liquid Barbecue SmokeAE
1/4 ts Red,white,green and black
Peppercorns -- crushed
1    Lemon juice
ds Ginger
Garlic -- minced
Pour the tomato sauce into a heavy 3 qrt saucepan. Bring the heat up
to medium and start the sauce warming. Add the minced garlic, sugar,
molasses, balsamic vinegar, and spices and let the sauce warm until
it just bubbles. You may up the heat a little but don't let the sauce
boil. When sauce starts to bubble add the liquid smoke and the
squeeeze of fresh lemon juice and let sauce sit on low or warm until
ready to use.
