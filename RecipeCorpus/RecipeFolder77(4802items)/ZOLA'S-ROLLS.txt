Title: ZOLA'S ROLLS
Categories: Bread, Misc
Yield: 20 Servings
1 tb Yeast
1 1/2 c  Warm water
3 c  Flour
2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Veg. oil
Put the yeast in the lukewarm water and let it dissolve. Mix the
flour, sugar and salt to-gether and when the yeast is ready pour into
flour mixture along with the oil, mix well until the dough is smooth
and elastic. Cover and let rise 1 hour. Cut into 1 oz. pieces and
roll into balls and put on greased cookie sheet, let rise another 15
minutes. Bake in 325 oven about 20 minutes.
