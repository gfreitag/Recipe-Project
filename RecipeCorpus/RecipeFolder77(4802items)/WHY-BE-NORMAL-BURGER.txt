Title: WHY BE NORMAL BURGER
Categories: Poultry, Rice
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Ground turkey
2 c  Wild rice -- cooked
1/4 c  Cooking wine
3/4 c  Cranberry sauce -- whole
1 1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1 c  Onions -- chopped
Combine all ingredients.  Shape into patties and grill. (Take about 7
minutes a side)
