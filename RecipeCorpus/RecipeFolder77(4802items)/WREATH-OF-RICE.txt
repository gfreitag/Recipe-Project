Title: WREATH OF RICE
Categories: Grains, Christmas
Yield: 1 Wreath
2 c  Rice
3 cn Beef consomme
2    Soup cans water
8    Green onions; chopped
1 lg Can mushrooms
1    Stick butter; melted
1 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients; spoon into greased 3-quart ring mold. Cover
with foil. Bake at 450 degrees for 1 hour. Let stand for 10 minutes.
Remove foil; press rice mixture down with spoon. Invert carefully on
serving platter.  Garnish with holly leaves if desired. MM Wrenn

Source:  Military Officers' Wives:  Holiday Recipes
