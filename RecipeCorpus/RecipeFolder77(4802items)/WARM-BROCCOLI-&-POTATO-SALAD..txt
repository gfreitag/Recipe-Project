Title: WARM BROCCOLI & POTATO SALAD.
Categories: Salads, Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
6 md Potatoes, cubed
2 c  Broccoli florets
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/4 c  Orange juice
3 tb Olive oil
3 tb White wine vinegar
2 ts Basil
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
1/4 ts Hot pepper sauce
2 tb Parsley, chopped
2 ea Green onions, thinly sliced
Salt & white pepper
Preheat oven to 150F.

Place potatoes in a pot with enough water to cover & parboil for 10
minutes.  Remove with a slotted spoon & save the water. Transfer
potatoes to a baking sheet & place in oven. Meanwhile, blanch the
broccoli in the reserved water for one minute only.  Remove & add to
the potatoes in the oven.

DRESSING:  Combine the first five ingredients in a small saucepan &
bring to a boil.  Remove from the heat & stir in the remaining
ingredients.

Remove vegetables from the oven & toss with the dressing ensuring
that they are thoroughly coated. Serve immediately.
