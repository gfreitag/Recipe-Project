Title: TOFU-BASIL DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2    Silken (creamy) tofu; (about
2 tb Cider vinegar
2 tb Unsweetened apple; or orange
1/2 ts Dijon mustard
1 sm Minced or pressed
2 tb Fresh basil; chopped
1/4 ts Salt
Recipe by: Felicia Pickering <MNHAN063@SIVM.SI.EDU> Place all of the
ingredients in a blender or food processor and puree until smooth.
Covered and refrigerated, Tofu-Basil Dressing will keep for about a
week. Use to dress tossed salads, chopped tomatoes, steamed green
beans, asparagus, or little new potatoes
