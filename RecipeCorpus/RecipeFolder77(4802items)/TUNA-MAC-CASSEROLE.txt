Title: TUNA-MAC CASSEROLE
Categories: Low fat, Casseroles, Freezes wel
Yield: 7 Servings
4 c  Cooked elbow macaroni --
About 7 oz dry
1 cn Reduced-fat cream of
Mushroom soup
1 c  Skim milk
12 1/4 oz Water-packed tuna
1 c  Mushroom pieces -- raw
2 tb Green onion tops
1/4 ts Celery seed
1/4 ts Lemon pepper
Salt -- (optional)
Mix altogether.  Pour into two 1 1/2 quart casseroles coated with
cooking spray.  (One may be lined with heavy duty foil before
spraying. Then freeze before baking.) Bake at 350B0 F for 25-30 min.
(Bake FROZEN casserole for 50-60 min or until hot.)

per serving:  211 Kcal   2.1g Fat   9.3% CFF   371mg sodium
