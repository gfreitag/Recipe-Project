Title: ZIPPY CRISP CHICKEN BAKE *** SCPF53A
Categories: Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
1 c  Corn flake crumbs
1/2 c  Kraft Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 x  Salt and pepper
lb chicken;* *cut up & skinned/I use all breasts 1. Preheat
2,3
oven to 350 2. Brush chicken with salad dressing: coat with combined
crumbs and cheese. Sprinkle with salt and pepper 3. Place in 13 x 9
baking dish 4. Bake 1 hour or until tender. Makes 3 to 4 servings
This is good and has a little "zing" to it. Hope you all enjoy it.
