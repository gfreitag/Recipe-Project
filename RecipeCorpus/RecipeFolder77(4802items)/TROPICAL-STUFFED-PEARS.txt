Title: TROPICAL STUFFED PEARS
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn (8oz.) sour cream
1 cn (8oz.) crushed pineapple,
-drained
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
1 c  Flaked coconut
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1 cn (29 oz.) pear halves,drained
Lettuce leaves, optional
In a bowl, combine the sour cream, pineapple, marshmallows, coconut
and pecans.  Place pears on lettuce if desired; spoon pineapple
mixture into the center of each. Yield 6-7 servings. Typed in
