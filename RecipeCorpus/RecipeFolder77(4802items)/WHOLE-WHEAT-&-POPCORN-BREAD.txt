Title: WHOLE WHEAT & POPCORN BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Lg. loaf
MMMMM-------------------LISA CRAWLEY/TEASPOON------------------------
-The Bread Machine Gourmet
2 2/3 c  Whole Wheat Flour
1/2 c  Popcorn Flour
1/4 c  Gluten Flour
1 1/2 tb Dry Milk
1 1/2 ts Sea Salt
1 1/4 c  Water
3 tb Soy Margarine
1 1/2 tb Molasses
1 tb Honey
4 ts Active Dry Yeast
Put ingred. in pan according to mfr. directions. Program for whole
wheat mode if your machine has the capability. Invert bread on wire
rack after baking cycle is finished. Allow to cool completely.
