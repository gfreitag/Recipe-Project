Title: VEGETABLE BROTH MIX (VEGETARIAN)
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Onion powder
1 tb Celery salt
1 tb Dried parsley
1 1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground savory
1/2 ts Dried marjoram
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Turmeric
1/4 ts Ground sage
Combine all ingredients and mix well. Mix again before using. Dried
vegetable flakes can also be added for a flavor bonus.
