Title: TUMBLEWEED
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 oz Kahlua
3/4 oz Dark creme de cacao
3 oz Light cream or milk
2    Scoops vanilla ice cream
Preparation : Combine the ingredients and blend until creamy. Pour
into stemmed glasses to 1 inch from the top. Top with whipped cream
and sprinkle with chocolate shavings.
