Title: WEBER TANGY BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Butter
3 tb Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Celery
2 tb Granulated sugar
2 tb Vinegar
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 ts Dry mustard
1 c  Ketchup
-salt and pepper to taste
Melt butter in a skillet.  Saute onions and celery until tender. Add
remaining ingredients and cook about 15 to 20 minutes so flavors
blend. (Makes 1 1/2 cups)

Source:  Weber-Stephen Products Company
