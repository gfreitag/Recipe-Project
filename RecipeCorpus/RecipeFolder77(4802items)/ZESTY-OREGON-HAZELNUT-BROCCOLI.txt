Title: ZESTY OREGON HAZELNUT BROCCOLI
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lg Garlic clove; minced
1 tb Oil
1 1/2 lb Broccoli, chopped
1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 ts Horseradish
1/4 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Marjoram
Salt & pepper to taste
1/2 c  Roasted & chopped hazelnuts
-(Oregon hazelnuts)
SautÂ‚ garlic in oil. Add broccoli and water. Cover and steam until
tender crisp over medium heat. (Most of the liquid should be gone.)
Add sour cream and seasonings. Heat until hot and sauce slightly
thickens. Add nuts, toss and serve.
