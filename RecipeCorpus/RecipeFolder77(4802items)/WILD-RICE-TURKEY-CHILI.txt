Title: WILD RICE TURKEY CHILI
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
1 tb Oil
1 md Onion -- chopped
10 ml Garlic -- minced
2 c  Turkey; cooked -- cubed
2 c  Cooked wild rice
15 oz Can Great Northern beans --
Drained
11 oz Can white corn
2 cn Diced green chilies -- 4oz
Ea
14 1/2 oz Chicken broth
1 ts Ground cumin
Hot pepper sauce to taste
6 oz Monterey Jack -- shredded
Heat oil in large pan over medium heat; add onion and garlic. Cook
until tender. Add turkey, rice, beans corn, chilies, broth and cumin.
Cover and simmer over low heat to serving temperature. Stir in hot
pepper sauce. Serve with cheese.
