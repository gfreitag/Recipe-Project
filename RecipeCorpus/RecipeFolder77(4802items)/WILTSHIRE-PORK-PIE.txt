Title: WILTSHIRE PORK PIE
Categories: Pork, Ethnic
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM----------------DAILY TELEGRAPH FAV. RECIPES---------------------
1 lb Pork
1/2 lb Onions
3/4 lb Apples
Salt
Black pepper
1/4 ts Sage
1    Pie crust
Cut up the meat, chop the onions, slice the apples. Arrange layers of
pork, onion and apple with seasoning and sage in a deep pie dish.
Repeat layers till full. Put on pastry lid, bake 1.1/2 hours at 350
degrees F
