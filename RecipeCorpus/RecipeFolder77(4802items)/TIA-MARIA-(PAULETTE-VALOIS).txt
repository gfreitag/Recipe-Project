Title: TIA MARIA (PAULETTE VALOIS)
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 72 Servings
26 oz Brandy (el cheapo brand)
1    Vanilla bean
1 1/4 qt Water; boiling
1 1/4 qt Sugar
2 oz Antiqua coffee(Spice Island)
Mix coffee and sugar well.  Pour in boiling water, mix well, let stand
until cool.  Add brandy, cut vanilla bean lengthwise and drop into
bottle. Let stand 30 days. Make approx 3 1/2 bottles (90 oz)
