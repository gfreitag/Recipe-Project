Title: WALNUT FILLING FOR NUT & POPPYSEED ROLLS
Categories: Kosher, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Ground walnuts
1    Med. apple, grated
2 c  Sugar (or a little less)
Mix together and add enough cold water to moisten, about 1 to 2 tbs.
Mixture should be moist--not wet.

Will fill 1/2 of #164 dough.
