Title: VANILLA-SCENTED PINEAPPLE
Categories: Low-fat, Vegetarian, Fruits, To try
Yield: 8 Servings
1 md Pineapple, fresh;
1/4 c  Agar-agar flakes;
1 1/2 c  ;water;
2 ts Vanilla extract;
1/2 c  Pineapple juice;
3 tb Honey;
MMMMM------------------------PER SERVING-----------------------------
74 x  *cals
3/8 x  *gm protein
3/16 x  *gm fat
17 x  *gm carbo
1 x  *mg sodium
1 x  *gm fiber
Peel and core pineapple, then slice into 8 rings. Place one ring in
each of 8 shallow dessert dishes. IN a saucepan, combine remaining
ingredients; bring to a boil. Simmer 5 minutes, stirring, until agar
has completely dissolved.  Divide sauce among dessert dishes. Chill
until sauce if firm, about 12 minutes. Serves 8.
