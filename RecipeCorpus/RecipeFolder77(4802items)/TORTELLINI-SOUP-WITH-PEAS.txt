Title: TORTELLINI SOUP WITH PEAS
Categories: Soups, Pasta
Yield: 3 Servings
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1/2 lb Frozen cheese tortellini
1 cn Chicken broth (10 oz.)
1 c  Frozen peas
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Freshly grated parmesan
-cheese
In large pot, bring 3 cups water and garlic to boil. Add frozen
tortellini; return to boil.  Reduce heat to medium-high; cook,
stirring occasionally, for 15 minutes.

Add chicken broth, 1 1/4 cups water and peas; return to boil. Reduce
heat to medium; cook for 5 minutes or until pasta is tender but firm.
Stir in pepper. Serve sprinkled with Parmesan. Makes 3 servings.
