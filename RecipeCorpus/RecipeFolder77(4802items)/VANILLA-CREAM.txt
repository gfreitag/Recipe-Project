Title: VANILLA CREAM
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 3 Cups
2    Eggs
3 ts Vanilla
12 oz Can condensed milk
1 1/2 c  Brandy
1/2 c  Vodka
Beat all ingredients together. Refrigerate for 2 weeks before serving.
Results in a rich after dinner drink for a "sweet tooth" or in coffee.
Yield: 3 cups
Container: Quart jar
