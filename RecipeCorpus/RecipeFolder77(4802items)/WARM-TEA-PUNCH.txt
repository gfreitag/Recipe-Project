Title: WARM TEA PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Tea
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 c  Water
6    Orange herbal tea bags
10    Whole cloves
2    Cinnamon sticks
2 c  Cranberry juice
1 1/2 c  White grape juice
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
Orange slices/whole cloves--
--optional for garnish
In a medium saucepan, bring water to a boil.  Turn off heat; add tea
bags, cloves and cinnamon sticks.  Cover and steep for 5 minutes.
Remove tea bags.  Stir in the juices and brown sugar; heat through.
Remove spices. Garnish with clove-studded orange slices if desired.
Source: Taste of Home mag, June/July 1996
