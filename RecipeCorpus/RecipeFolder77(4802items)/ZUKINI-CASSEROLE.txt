Title: ZUKINI CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
3    Peeled diced tomatoes
1    Large zukini sliced
1    Layer of onion rings
1/2 ts Each salt & pepper
3 tb Brown sugar
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 c  Cracker crumbs
1/2 c  Grated cheese
Layer zukini and peeled and diced tomatoes and then onion rings.
Sprinkle brown sugar and salt, pepper and basil on top then cracker
crumbs and grated cheese . Cover with foil and bake 1/2 hour in 350
oven then remove foil and continue baking 45 min.
