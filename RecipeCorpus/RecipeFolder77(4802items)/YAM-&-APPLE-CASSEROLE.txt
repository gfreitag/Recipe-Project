Title: YAM & APPLE CASSEROLE
Categories: Mcdougall, Casseroles, Vegetables, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Sweet potatoes, medium
1    Apple, baking, large
1/4 c  Applesauce
1/2 c  Water
Peel the sweet potatoes and slice thinly.  Core the apple and cut
into thin slices.  (May peel, if desired). Layer the apples and
sweetpotatoes into a square baking dish.  Combine applesauce and
water and pour over the layered ingredients.  Sprinkle with cinnamon,
if desired. Cover and bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes. May be
served hot or cold.

From: The McDougall Health-Supporting Cookbook, Volume One, Mary
McDougall
