Title: TWO BERRY YOGURT SHAKE
Categories: Snacks, Syd's book
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  Skim Milk
1/2 c  Fresh Strawberries, Mashed
1/3 c  Fresh Raspberries, Mashed
1/3 c  Plain Low Fat Yogurt
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla Extract
Blend all ingredients in a blender bowl until thick and frothy. 121
calories per serving.  From: Syd's Cookbook.
