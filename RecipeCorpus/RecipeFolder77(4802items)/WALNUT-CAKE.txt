Title: WALNUT CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
10    Eggs, separated
1 2/3 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 c  Matzo cake flour
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Orange peel; grated (option
-l)
1 1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form; set aside. Beat egg yolks and
powdered sugar. Sift flour; add to egg yolk mixture. Add vanilla,
salt and orange peel, if desired. Stir in walnuts. Fold in egg
whites. Spoon into 10" tube pan. Bake @ 350 degrees for 50 minutes.
Invert and cool before removing from pan.
