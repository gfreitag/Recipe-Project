Title: TUNA & WATERCRESS SANDWICHES
Categories: Seafood, Cold sandwi
6 oz Starkist Solid White Tuna --
drained, chunked
Yield: 12 Servings
:
1/2 c  Butter -- softened
1/2 c  Watercress leaves -- firmly
pkd
:
2 TB Lemon juice
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts White pepper
24 sl Thin sandwich bread --
crusts
:
:
Additional watercress --
garnish
:
:

1. In food processor bowl with metal blade, place tuna, butter, 1/2
cup watercress, lemon juice, salt and white pepper. 2. Pulse on and
off until watercress is finely chopped and mixture is blended. 3.
Spread tuna on half the bread slices; top with remaining slices. 4.
Cut into squares or triangles. Serve or refrigerate up to 2 hours. 5.
Garnish with additional watercress. Makes 12 servings
