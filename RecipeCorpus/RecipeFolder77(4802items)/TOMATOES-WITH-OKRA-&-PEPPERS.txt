Title: TOMATOES WITH OKRA & PEPPERS
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
12    Okra pods
1    Green pepper; finely chopped
1/4 c  Breadcrumbs; dry, fine
1 tb Butter (or marg.); melted
1 ts Onion; minced
Salt
Pepper
Paprika
6 md Tomatoes
Breadcrumbs; buttered
Wash okra and slice.  Parboil 5 minutes; drain.  Mix with green
pepper, dry breadcrumbs, butter, and onion. Season to taste with
salt, pepepr, and paprika.

Core tomatoes, and remove tops.  Fill tomatoes with okra mixture.
Place in a greased baking dish; sprinkle with buttered breadcrumbs,
and bake at 375 degrees for
20    minutes.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy
Coleman.
