Title: WHAT'S IN THE FRIDGE? HAM GLAZE
Categories: Sauces, Ham, Pork
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Strawberry jam
1/2 c  Apple jelly (or apple-mint)
2 tb Mustard, brown
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Lemon juice
In a small saucepan, warm the jam and the jelly until they have
melted. Stir in the mustard, soy sauce and lemon juice. Heat gently
until blended; use as a sauce or glaze over ham.
