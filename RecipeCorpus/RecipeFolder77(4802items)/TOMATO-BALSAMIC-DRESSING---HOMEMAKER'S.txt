Title: TOMATO BALSAMIC DRESSING - HOMEMAKER'S
Categories: Salads, Sauces/dips
Yield: 6 'servings'
2    Cloves garlic
2 md Tomatoes
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Fresh basil; chopped
1.  In blender, chop garlic.

2.  Cut tomatoes in half and squeeze out seeds.  Add to garlic and
blend until pureed.

3.  Add vinegar, salt and pepper and blend a few seconds.  Blend in
oil. Stir in basil.
