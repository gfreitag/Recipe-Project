Title: VIDALIA ONION SALAD*
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Vidalia onions;thinly sliced
1 x  Lettuce;torn in bite size-
-pieces - icebox,Romaine etc
8 x  Eggs;hard boiled
DRESSING
1/2 c  Mustard
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Salt to taste
1/4 ts Lemon-pepper seasoning
2 ts White vinegar
In a seperate bowl, mix the dressing ingredients. To assemble: make
layers of lettuce, onions, eggs and dressing. Repeat and end with
dressing. Cover and chill for at least 3 hours. This is great with
steak and potatoes!
