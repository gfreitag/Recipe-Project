Title: WELSH SODA CAKE(WELSH)
Categories: Cakes, Welsh
Yield: 1 Cake
1 lb Flour
4 oz Butter
8 oz Castor sugar
8 oz Cunants
1    Egg
1/2 c  Sour milk
1 ts Bicarbonate of soda
(TEISEN LAETH ENWYN) Rub the butter into the flour and add the sugar
and fruit. Beat the egg well and add to the mixture. Dis- solve the
bicarbonate of soda in 1 tablespoon of milk. Mix in the rest of the
milk. Add the bicarbonate in the milk just before putting the cake in
the oven. Place rn a greased tin and cook in a hot oven (425/F. or
Mark 7) for 30-40 minutes.
