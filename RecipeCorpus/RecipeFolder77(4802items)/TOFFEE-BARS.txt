Title: TOFFEE BARS
Categories: Desserts, Candies, Nuts, Chocolate
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1    Egg yolk
1 c  Flour
1/4 c  Nuts; chopped
1/2 ts Vanilla
POWER: 50% .... TURN: Yes .... TIME: 8 min. per 6 servings Cream
butter and brown sugar. Add egg yolk, flour, vanilla and salt. Spread
in 8x8 glass baking dish. Microwave at 50% power for 7 to 8 minutes.
Give 1/2 turn twice during cooking. Sprinkle chocolate chips over
top. Let chips soften for 2 minutes. Spread with knife over bars.
Sprinkle with nuts. Cut into bars.
