Title: WESTERN SKILLET RICE
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Old
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
16 oz Cn stewed tomatoes
1 c  Small sweet peas
1 c  Sharp cheddar, shredded
1    Pkg onion soup mix
1 1/2 c  Water
3/4 c  Brown rice, uncooked
1/2 tb Oil
In a skillet on high heat add the oil and brown the meat, stirring
frequently. Drain any excess fat. Stir in the onion soup mix, water,
rice, tomatoes and peas. Simmer 25 minutes until rice is tender; top
with cheese.
