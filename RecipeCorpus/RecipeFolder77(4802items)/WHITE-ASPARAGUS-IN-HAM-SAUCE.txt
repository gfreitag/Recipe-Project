Title: WHITE ASPARAGUS IN HAM SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 4 Servings
LISA CRAWLEY  TSPN00B-----
2 cn White Asparagus 14 1/2 oz
Ea
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1/2 c  Reserved Cooking liquid from
Asparagus
1/2 c  Milk
4 oz Cooked Lean Ham --
Julienned
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Salt
Drain asparagus; reserve 1/2 c liquid.  Heat butter in saucepan. Add
flour; blend.  Gradually pour in asparagus liquid and milk; stir
constantly over low heat until sauce thickens and bubbles. Add ham
and seasonings. Gently stir in asparagus; heat through, do not boil.
Serve in preheated serving dish.
