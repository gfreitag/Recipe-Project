Title: TOMATO BREAD (ABM)
Categories: Digest, August, Abm, Ovo
Yield: 12 Servings
6    Tomato paste
1    Egg white
2    Bread flour
1/4    Salt
2    Applesauce or olive oil
2    Sugar
1/2    Italian seasoning
1 1/2    Dried minced onion
1/4    Garlic powder
1/4    Nutmeg
1 1/2    Dry yeast
Combine all ingredients according to manufacturer's directions.
Process in regular, delayed, or rapid bake cycle.
