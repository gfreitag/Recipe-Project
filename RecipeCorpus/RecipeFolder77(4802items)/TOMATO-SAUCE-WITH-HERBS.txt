Title: TOMATO SAUCE WITH HERBS
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Olive oil
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1/2 c  Bell peppers -- chopped
2 c  Tomatoes -- cored/halved/see
Ed
2 tb Basil
2 tb Parsley
2 ts Oregano
2 ts Marjoram
1 ts Rosemary
In a skillet, heat oil over moderate heat.  Add garlic and peppers
until tender.  Add tomatoes, basil, parsley, oregano, marjoram, and
rosemary. Cook just until tomatoes' juices thicken, 7 to 10 minutes.
Pour over cooked pasta.
