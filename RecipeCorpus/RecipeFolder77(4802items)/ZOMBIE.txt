Title: ZOMBIE
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Measure dark rum
1    Measure light rum
1    Measure apricot brandy
1/2    Measure lemon juice
1/2    Measure pineapple juice
1/2    Measure orange juice
Place ingredients in a blender with ice and blend until smooth. Pour
into a large goblet or tankard and top with crushed ice. Decorate
with slices of pineapple, orange, lemon, lime and a marachino cherry.
