Title: UNCOOKED TOMATO SAUCE WITH OLIVES & MOZZARE
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
3    Tomatoes; medium
2    Garlic cloves; large, mince
3 tb Capers; drained
1 1/2 ts Oregano; dried
1/3 c  Oil; olive
18    Olives; black pitted & slic
6 oz Mozzarella cheese; diced
Approx. Cook Time: none
Fat grams    per serving:
Nobake but requires time to marinate. Combine sauce ingredients in
noncorrosive bowl, set aside for 30 minute or more, up to 2 hours.
Cook pasta and drain. Return empty pot to turned off burner, pour in
drained pasta and toss. Cover with lid for 1 minute to let cheese
soften. Uncover and serve.  Good for the height of tomato season on
hot days.
