Title: VEGETABLE CLAM CHOWDER
Categories: Soups, Seafood, Microwave
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Margarine or butter
1 pk (16 oz.) frozen mixed
Vegetables (such as Broccoli
Corn and Red Peppers mix)
2 cn (6 1/2 ounces each) minced
Clams, drained
3 c  Milk
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Place margarine and frozen vegetables in 3-quart microwavable
casserole. Cover tightly and microwave on high 8 to 10 minutes or
until hot. Stir in remaining ingredients. Cover tightly and microwave
on medium- high (70%) 10 to 12 minutes, stirring after 5 minutes,
until hot.
