Title: WALNUT CREAMS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 24 Servings
2/3 c  Sweetened condensed milk
4 1/2 c  Sifted confectioners' sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/8 ts Salt
Food coloring
Walnut pieces
DIRECTIONS: Mix well first 4 ingredients. Knead until smooth. Divide;
color half pink and half green. Shape in 3/4" balls, and press a
piece of walnut on each. Let stand until firm.

Source: Mom's old magazine clippings- 1940's to 1970's
