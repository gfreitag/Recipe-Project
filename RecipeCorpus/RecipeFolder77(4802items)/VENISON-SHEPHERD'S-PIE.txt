Title: VENISON SHEPHERD'S PIE
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Ground venison cooked
1 sm Onion
1 ts Shortening
1 cn Beef gravy
1/2 c  Diced cooked carrots
1/2 c  Diced lightly cooked celery
1/2 c  Peas
3 c  Mashed potatoes
1 ts Chili pepper (opt.)
Slightly brown onion in shortening and then add gravy, venison,
carrots, celery and peas.  Let simmer for 2 min. and then pour into a
2-qt casserole dish.  Spread mashed potatoes on top. Bake at 450F for
about 25 min or until lightly browned.
