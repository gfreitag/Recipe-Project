Title: TOMATO ARUGULA SALSA
Categories: Frugal, Gourmet
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Plum tomatoes -- seeded and
Chopped
1 c  Arugula leaves -- chopped
1 tb Minced red onion
1/2 c  Extra virgin olive oil
1 ts Dijon mustard
2 tb White wine vinegar
2 tb Lemon juice
Salt and pepper
You can use basil, italian parsley or watercress instead of the
arugula.

Chop by hand and combine all ingredients.
