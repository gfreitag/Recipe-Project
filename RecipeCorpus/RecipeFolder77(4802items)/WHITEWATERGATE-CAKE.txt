Title: WHITEWATERGATE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Cake
MMMMM----------------------------CAKE---------------------------------
1 pk White cake mix
1 c  Salad oil
1/2 c  Choppen pecans
1 pk Instant pistachio pudding
3 ea Eggs
1 c  Club soda
MMMMM---------------------PISTACHIO FROSTING--------------------------
2    Env. Dream Whip
1 1/2 c  Cold milk
1 pk Instant pistachio pudding
Combine ingreds., beat 4 min. at med. speed of elec. mixer. Pour
batter into prepared 10" tube pan. Bake at 350 F. for 45-50 min. Turn
out on wire rack to cool. Frost with Pistachio Frosting, garnish with
pecans.

PISTACHIO FROSTING:  Combine whipped topping mix and milk, beat at
high speed of elec. mixer until peaks form. Add pudding mix and beat
until fluffy.
