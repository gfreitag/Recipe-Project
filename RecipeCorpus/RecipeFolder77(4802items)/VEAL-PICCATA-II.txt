Title: VEAL PICCATA II
Categories: Veal
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Veal scallops; pounded thin
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Dry white wine
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1    Garlic clove
Salt & pepper
1/2 lb Mushrooms; fresh; sliced
3 tb Capers
1/2 c  Green onions; sliced
Dredge veal slices in flour. Saute veal in butter, add wine, lemon
juice, garlic, salt & pepper. Simmer one minute. Remove meat slices
and place in casserole; top with sauce, uncooked mushroom slices,
capers and green onions. You can bake at 350~ for 30 minutes or cover
and refrigerate and cook later. Bring to room temperature before
baking. Garnish with fresh parsley.
