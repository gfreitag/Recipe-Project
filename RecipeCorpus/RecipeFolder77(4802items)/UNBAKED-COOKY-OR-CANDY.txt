Title: UNBAKED COOKY OR CANDY
Categories: Cookies, Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1    Stick oleo
1/2 c  Peanut butter
3 c  Quick oats
1/3 c  Cocoa
1/2 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
From: Marilla Ransdell

Put butter, sugar, milk and cocoa in pan. Boil 1 or 2 minutes (she
boils 2 minutes) then add peanut butter, oats, vanilla. Drop on wax
paper, cool.
