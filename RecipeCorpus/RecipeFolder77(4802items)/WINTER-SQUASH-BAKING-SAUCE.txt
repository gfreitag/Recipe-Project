Title: WINTER SQUASH BAKING SAUCE
Categories: Herbs, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Squash
2 tb Melted butter
1 tb Chervil
1 tb Sage
1 tb Ginger
1 tb Honey
1.Combine all ingredients. 2.Cut a winter squash (acorn, banana,
buttercup, butternut, hubbard, spaghetti, or turk's turban) in half
and scoop out seeds. 3.Spoon the sauce into the center of each squash
half and bake as usual.
