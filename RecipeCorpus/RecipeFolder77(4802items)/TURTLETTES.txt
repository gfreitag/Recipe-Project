Title: TURTLETTES
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Doz  lg. pecans,
-halved lengthwise
-(legs)
2    Doz  lg. pecans,
-halved crosswise
-(head)
2    Doz  caramel candies
-(body)
1 sm RECIPE
-CHOCOLATE  CHIP COATING
1 c  Chocolate chips
2 tb Vegetable shortening
Microwave caramels to soften (not melt) to form turtles. Microwave 2
to 4 minutes until chips soften. Stir well. Use to dip or frost
turtle candies. Makes
2    dozen. From: Eldon Morris
