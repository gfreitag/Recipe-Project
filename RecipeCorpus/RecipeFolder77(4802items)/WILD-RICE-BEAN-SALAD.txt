Title: WILD RICE BEAN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Main dish
Yield: 1 Recipe
1    Box Wild Quick Brown Rice
2 c  Cooked beans
2/3 c  Diced onions
2 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Tamari soy sauce
Cook contents of box according to package directions, cool. Add
remaining ingredients, and toss.
