Title: TOFU LASAGNA
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 8 Servings
10 oz Drained Tofu
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan
2 ea Garlic cloves
1/2 lb Mushrooms, Sliced & Sauteed
1/4 c  Fresh Parsley
2 1/2 c  Sauce
2 c  Grated Mozz, Swiss or M.Jac
12 ea Lasagne Noodles
Mix mashed tofu with parmesan and garlic. Oil pan -- layer noodles
with sauce, cheese, mushrooms & tofu mix.
top layer should be noodle with sauce & cheese. Bake @ 350 for 45
minutes (or 400 for 35 minutes)
