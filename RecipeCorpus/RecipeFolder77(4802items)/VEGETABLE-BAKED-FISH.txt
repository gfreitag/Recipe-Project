Title: VEGETABLE BAKED FISH
Categories: Fish
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Zucchini, sliced
1 ea Green pepper, sliced
1 ea Tomato, sliced
1 ea Onion, sliced
1 lb Fish fillet
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Water
1 tb Butter
1/8 ts Paprika
Place 1/2 of vegetables in baking dish.  Season fish with salt and
pepper and place on top of the vegetables. Cover with remaining
vegetables. Add water, dot with butter and sprinkle with paprika.
Bake at 350 deg for 20-25 minutes.
