Title: WARM TOMATO-PEPPER SAUCE~ CALIFORNIA STYLE PA
Categories: Pasta, Sauce, Catering
Yield: 24 Servings
2 1/2 qt Red bell peppers -- chopped
5 c  Thinly sliced green onions
3 tb Minced garlic
1/3 c  Olive oil
8 qt Tomatoes -- seeded and
Diced
1 2/3 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1 c  Black pepper
2 1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
3 lb Cooked pasta -- penne
SautÂ‚ peppers, onions and garlic in oil in a large skillet over
medium high heat until vegetables are tender-crisp. Stir in tomatoes;
cook over high heat until sauce thickens, 10-15 minutes. Stir in
remaining ingredients except pasta. Serve.
