Title: WATER CHESTNUT HORS D'OEUVRES
Categories: Appetizers, Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Bacon
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
16 oz Whole water chestnuts
2 ts Butter
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Soy sauce
1 ts Mustard
Recipe by: Joel Ehrlich <Joel.Ehrlich@SALATA.COM> Preheat the oven to
375 degrees. Wrap each water chestnut in 1/2 strip of bacon. Secure
each with a toothpick. Bake until the bacon is done (about 40
minutes). Remove to a chafing dish. Combine the remaining ingredients
in a small saucepan over low heat. Pour over the chestnuts. Keep
warm. Serve
