Title: TORTELLINI TOSS
Categories: Quick meals, Cheese/eggs, Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb OLIVE OIL
1 md ONION  CHOPPED
2 md ZUCCHINI  CHOPPED
2 md YELLOW SQUASH  CHOPPED
1 md RED BELL PEPPER CHOPPED
2 c  MUSHROOMS SLICED
2 cl GARLIC MINCED
SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE
1/4 ts OREGANO
1 lb COOKED CHEESE TORTELLINI
1/4 c  GRATED PARMESAN CHEESE
HEAT OLIVE OIL IN A LARGE SKILLET OVER HIGH HEAT. ADD ONION, ZUCCHINI,
SQUASH, AND BELL PEPPER. STIR IN MUSHROOMS, GARLIC, OREGANO, SALT AND
PEPPER. COOK AND STIR 7 MINS. OR UNTIL VEGETABLES ARE CRISP-TENDER.
TOSS WITH TORTELLINI AND PARMESAN CHEESE.
