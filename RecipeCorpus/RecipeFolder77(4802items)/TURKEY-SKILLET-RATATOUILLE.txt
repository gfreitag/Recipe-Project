Title: TURKEY SKILLET RATATOUILLE
Categories: Poultry, Quick
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Eggplant; small/pared
1    Zucchini; small, diced
1    Onion; halved/thinly sliced
1    Garlic clove; minced
2 c  Tomatoes; broken/w juice
Salt & pepper to taste
1 1/2 ts Oregano
3 c  Turkey; roasted/cubed
Spray skillet with cooking spray.  Combine all ingredients except
turkey. Cover and simmer 10 minutes, stirring frequently, until
vegetables are tender-crisp.  Stir in cubed turkey,  Cook and stir
uncovered until nearly all the liquid is evaporated and the turkey
cubes are white (about 3 to 4 minutes).
