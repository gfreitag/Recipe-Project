Title: WAFFLES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Flour
3 ts Baking Powder
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Sugar
2 ea Egg yolks
1 1/4 c  Milk
6 tb Salad oil or melted  crisco
2 ea Egg whites
Sift flour,baking powder,salt and sugar.Beat egg yolks;add milk.
Combine with dry ingredients mixing smooth.Add shortening or salad
oil.Beat egg whites stiff and fold in.
