Title: TURKEY SLICES WITH GREEN PEPPERCORN SAUCE
Categories: Creole, Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/4 lb Turkey breast,thinly sliced
Salt
Black pepper
2 tb Oil
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 c  White wine,dry
3    Green onions,minced
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 tb Green peppercorns
1. Season turkey slices.
2. In large skillet, heat oil and butter; saute turkey 2 minutes on
each side, then remove and keep warm.
3. Add remaining ingredients to skillet and simmer 5 minutes.
4. Serve sauce over turkey slices.
