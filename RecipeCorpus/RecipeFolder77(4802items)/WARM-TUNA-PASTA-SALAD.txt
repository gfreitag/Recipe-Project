Title: WARM TUNA-PASTA SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 Servings
1/4 c  Water
16 oz Frozen broccoli, -- (1
Package)
Rotini pasta, peas, and red
Peppers
With Parmesan cheese
Seasoning
1/3 c  Nonfat sour cream
Alternative
6 1/2 oz 60%-less-salt tuna in water
(1 can) drained
Combine water and broccoli mixture in a medium saucepan. Bring to a
boil; cover, reduce heat to medium-high, and cook 6 minutes, stirring
once.

Remove from heat. Add nonfat sour cream alternative and tuna to
broccoli mixture, and toss well. Yield: 3 servings (serving size: 1
cup).
