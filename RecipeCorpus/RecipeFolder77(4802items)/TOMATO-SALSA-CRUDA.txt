Title: TOMATO SALSA CRUDA
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 1 Cup
4    Italian Roma tomatoes,,
-cored and chopped
12    Serrano chiles,, chopped
1/4 sm Onion,, chopped
Juice of 1 lime
1 ts Sea salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
In a blender, place the tomatoes, chiles, onion, lime juice, salt, and
pepper. Blend briefly, making sure the salsa remains rough. Store in a
covered container, in the refrigerator, no more than 1 day.
