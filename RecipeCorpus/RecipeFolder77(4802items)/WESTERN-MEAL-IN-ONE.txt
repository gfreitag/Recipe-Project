Title: WESTERN MEAL IN ONE
Categories: Beef grnd, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------KAREN GODFREY----------------------------
1 lb Ground beef or turkey
1 tb Oil
1    Clove garlic
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper; chopped
1 ts Salt
1 ts Chili powder
16 oz Red beans; drained
2 1/2 c  Tomatoes w/juice; chopped
3/4 c  Long grain rice
3/4 c  Cheddar cheese
1/4 c  Black olives;, sliced
Brown meat in oil with minced garlic. Add onion and green pepper; cook
until onion is transparent.  Drain off fat. In a 2 qt. casserole,
combine the meat mixture, salt, chili powder, beans, tomatoes, and
rice. Bake, covered, at 350 degrees F. for 30 minutes. Uncover and
sprinkle with cheese and olives; bake for
15    minutes more.
