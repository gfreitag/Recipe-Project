Title: TURKISH PILAF
Categories: Grains, Vegan, Vegetarian, Turkish, Ethnic
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Brown rice
4 c  Water
2 tb Almonds; slivered
2 tb Pistachio nuts; shelled
1/4 c  Dried prunes; chopped
1/4 c  Dried apricot; chopped
1 ts Cinnamon
Cook the brown rice in boiling water in a covered pot for 45 minutes.
Stir in remaining ingredients and serve warm.

Cal per serving: 203 Fat: 4 grams
