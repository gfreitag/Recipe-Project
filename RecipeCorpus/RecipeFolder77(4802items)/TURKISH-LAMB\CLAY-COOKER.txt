Title: TURKISH LAMB\CLAY COOKER
Categories: Clay cooker, Lamb
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Cut-up green beans
2    Large onions, sliced
1    Large Idaho potato, peeled
And sliced
4    Large peeled tomatoes
1 lb Lamb, cubed
Salt, caraway seeds
1    Clove of garlic
1    Bouillon cube
Soak lid of glazed clay cooker. Mix all ingredients in a bowl,
transfer to cooker. Cover and put into cold oven. Turn temperature to
425F and bake for 80 minutes. From Schlemmertopf Recipe Book.
