Title: TROUT BAKED IN WINE
Categories: Fish
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Rainbow trout (about 2.5 lb)
2 c  White wine
1 pn Each of herbs*
*Whichever ones you like parsley, thyme, herbes de provence....&
Garlic.

Scale and gut the trout if not cleaned already.  You can filet it, or
not: it hardly matters.  Cut the trout in half the long way and lay
in a baking dish. Barely cover with white wine. Add herbs, if you
like them, or garlic. Bake at 350 F for half an hour.
