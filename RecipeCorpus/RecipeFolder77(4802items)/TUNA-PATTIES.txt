Title: TUNA PATTIES
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
2 cn Tuna; drained, finely flaked
1 c  Soft bread crumbs; about
-3 slices
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Chopped green onion
3    Egg; well beaten
4 tb Shortening
MMMMM-----------------------CUCUMBER SAUCE----------------------------
1/2 c  Sour cream
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1    Clove garlic; fine minced
1 sm Cucumber; peeled and
-finely chopped
MMMMM----------------------SOUR CREAM SAUCE---------------------------
2 tb Chopped green onion
1 tb Butter
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb Lemon juice
Salt and pepper to taste
Mix ingredients, except shortening, together and form into patties.
Melt shortening in heavy skillet and saute the patties until golden
brown. Drain on absorbent paper. Serve hot with sauce or chill for
several hours or overnight and serve cold with Cucumber sauce.

Cucumber sauce:  Mix all ingredients well and chill.

Sour Cream Sauce:  In small saucepan saute onion in butter. Lower
heat and stir in sour cream and lemon juice. Salt and pepper to
taste. Serve over hot patties.

From:  The Tuna Cookbook
