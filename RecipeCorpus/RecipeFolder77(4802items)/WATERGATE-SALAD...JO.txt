Title: WATERGATE SALAD...JO
Categories: Party, Desserts, Side dish
Yield: 1 Servings
9 oz Cool whip
1 pk Instant pistachio pudding
1 lb Crushed pineapple; w juice
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
DIRECTIONS ~------------------------------------------------------
~--- FOLD PUDDING (DRY) INTO WHIPPED TOPPING. ADD REST OF INGREDIENTS.
REFRIGERATE AT LEAST 1 HOUR.
