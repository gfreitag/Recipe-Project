Title: UNCOOKED CHOCOLATE ROLL~ MRS. LYON'S RECIPE
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 8 Servings
1 pk Semi-sweet chocolate
1 pk Colored marshmallows
1/2 lb Cherries
1 c  Walnuts
Melt the chocolate and add the cherries & marshmallows. Mix well and
put on waxed paper and roll up like jelly roll. On another piece of
waxed paper put some cocoanut and roll the roll in it. Refrigerate
and slice as needed.
