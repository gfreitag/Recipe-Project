Title: TONGDAK JUK (ROASTED CHICKEN)
Categories: Korean, Chicken
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lg Roasting chicken
2 tb Sesame seed oil
3 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Soy sauce
3 tb Minced onion
2 ts Minced garlic
1 ts Minced gingerroot
2 ts Pepper
1 tb Ground toasted sesame seed
Directions: Steam chicken for 1 hour. Combine all other ingredients
to make basting sauce. Pour some sauce over chicken. Skewer chicken
and grill over charcoal, turning and basting often, for 1 hour.  A
rotisserie can also be used, or roast on rack in preheated slow oven
(325 degrees) for 3 1/2 hours, turning and basting frequently. Before
serving, brush with sesame seed oil to give chicken a good color and
a special flavor.
