Title: WAKEUP SHAKEUP
Categories: Beverages, Breakfast
Yield: 1 Serving
Orange juice
1    Banana
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
Fill your glass or mug with as much orange juice as you wish. Pour
this into blender; add vanilla, egg and banana broken into small
pieces. Whirl until smooth and foamy.

Variations: Substitute apple or other juice for orange; Substitute
milk for juice; Add 1 tb strawberry jam; Add 1/2 ts instant coffee;
