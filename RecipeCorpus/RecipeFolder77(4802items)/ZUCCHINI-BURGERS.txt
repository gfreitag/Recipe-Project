Title: ZUCCHINI BURGERS
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Shredded zucchini
1/2 c  Diced cheddar cheese
2    Eggs, beaten
1 sm Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Crushed crackers, bread
-crumbs, or croutons
1/3 c  Wheat germ
Mix together. Drop by Tablespoonfuls on non-stick skillet in which
one T margarine has been melted. Brown each side about 8 minutes on
med. heat.
