Title: UNBELIEVABLE OATMEAL COOKIES
Categories: New, Text, Import
10 TB softened sweet butter
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  sugar
1    egg, -- lightly beaten
1/2 ts grated orange zest
1 ts almond or vanilla extract
3 c  rolled oatmeal -- (not
instant)
:

Lightly butter a cookie sheet. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.

Beat together the butter, sugar, egg, orange zest and almond extract.
With= =7F your fingers press the oatmeal into the butter until the
dough just holds together. Form the dough into tiny balls and with
the tines of a fork press them down. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes or
until golden brown.

Yield: 2 1/2 dozen
