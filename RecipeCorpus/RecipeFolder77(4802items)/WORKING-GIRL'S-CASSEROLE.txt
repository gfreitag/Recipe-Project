Title: WORKING GIRL'S CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Ground beef
Yield: 4 Servings
6 x  Medium potatoes
1 c  Grated cheese
1 lb Hamburger
1/2 c  Chopped onion
Cook and mash potatoes till creamy.  Put in a casserole and using
spoon hollow out hole in center about the size of a saucer. Brown
loose hamburger and onions. Fill hole in potatoes with hamburger and
onions. Cover with grated cheese.  Place in oven at 450 degrees until
cheese melts and is bubbly and you're ready to eat. It travels good
too.

Cookbook:  Sinkin Spells, Hot Flashes, Fits and Cravins by Ernest
Matthew Mickler.
