Title: WALNUT BUTTER SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
3 tb Madeira wine
In skillet, saute walnuts in 2 Tablespoons of the butter until golden
and fragrant. Reduce heat and add remaining 6 Tablespoons butter;
stir until melted. Stir in Madeira. Serve warm. Serve with grilled
trout
