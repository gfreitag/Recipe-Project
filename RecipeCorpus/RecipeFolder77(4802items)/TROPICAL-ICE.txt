Title: TROPICAL ICE
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Gin
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Mashed Bananas
2 cn Crushed Pineapple --
Undrained (20 oz.)
10 oz Can Maraschino Cherries --
-drained and chopped
4 c  Orange Juice
2 tb Lemon Juice
1/2 c  Sugar
Ginger Ale
White Wine
Combine first 6 ingredients in blender and freeze until firm. To serve
punch, partially thaw fruit mixture. Place in punch bowl and break
into chunks. Add ginger ale and white wine to taste.
