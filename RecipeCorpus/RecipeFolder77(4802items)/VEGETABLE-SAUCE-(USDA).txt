Title: VEGETABLE SAUCE (USDA)
Categories: Freezes wel, Usda
Yield: 6 Servings
3 tb Butter or margarine
3 tb Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Mixed vegetables -- cooked
Melt butter.  Stir in flour and salt.  Add milk slowly. Cook until
thickened, stirring constantly. Stir in vegetables. Heat to serving
temperature.

~ - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Serving Ideas : Serve over Ham Turnovers. (separate recipe)

NOTES : This recipe is for 6 servings (about 1/4 cup each) to
accompany Ham Turnovers, a freeze ahead meal. The sauce is made at
the time of serving. You will want to have the ingredients on hand
when you are ready to use the frozen food.
