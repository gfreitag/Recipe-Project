Title: WALLPAPER CLEANER (1909 PRESIDENTIAL COOKBOOK
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flour -- (heaping)
1 tb Kerosene
2 tb Ammonia
1/2 c  Warm water
CLEANER #2-
1    Sal Ammoniac
4    Parts rye flour
Water
CLEANER #3-
1 tb Salt
1 tb Kerosene
2 tb Vinegar
2 tb Ammonia
1/2 c  Warm water
1/2 c  Flour
Mix above and boil til flour is thoroughly scalded stirring
constantly. Then knead w/ hands and it is ready for use. Use as if it
were an eraser. CLEANER #2 Add enough water to the rye flour and
ammoniac that it will form a dough, then use on soiled parts as if
the mixture was a sponge. Turn soiled part of dough into itself and
work out a clean part of the mixture. Never continue to rub the
soiled surface of the cleaner into the wall. CLEANER #3 Cook while
stirring. Knead into a smooth dough. Use pieces as though it were an
eraser to clean wallpaper. SOURCE:Handwritten notes glued into the
Presidential Cookbook of 1909.
