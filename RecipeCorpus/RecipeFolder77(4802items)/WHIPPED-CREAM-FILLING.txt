Title: WHIPPED CREAM FILLING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Whipping cream
Few grains salt
2 tb Powdered sugar
1/4 ts Vanilla
Sift sugar.  Whip cream until stiff.  Add sugar, salt, and flavoring.
Pile lightly between layers of well-cooled cake. Serve at once. If
desired, coconut may be sprinkled over the cream.
