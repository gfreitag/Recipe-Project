Title: ZUCCHINI NAPOLEON
Categories: Kosher, Cajun, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Zucchini
1/4 c  Chopped onions
3 tb Margarine, pareve
4    Med.tomatoes,peel,quarter
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Oregano
1 ds Pepper
1 tb Flour--subs. potato starch>>
For Passover.
Cut zucchini into 1/2" slices.  Saute onions in margarine until
tender. Add tomatoes and cook 5 min. Then add zucchini, salt,
oregano, and pepper. Cook covered over low heat until zucchini is
tender (abt. 12 to 15 min.) If a thicker sauce is desired, sprinkle
in flour and cook a few minutes longer.
