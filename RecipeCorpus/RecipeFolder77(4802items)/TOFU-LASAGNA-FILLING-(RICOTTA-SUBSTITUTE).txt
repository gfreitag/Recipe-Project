Title: TOFU LASAGNA FILLING (RICOTTA SUBSTITUTE)
Categories: Vegetables, Sept.
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Tofu
1 tb Garlic powder
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Basil
1 ts Lemon juice
1 tb Olive oil
Blend all the ingredients well.
