Title: WAKES CAKES(ENGLISH)
Categories: English
Yield: 4 Servings
12 oz Flour
8 oz Butter
6 oz White sugar
1    Egg well beaten
1/4 ts Baking powder
3 oz Currants
1/2 oz Caraway seeds
Grated rind of 1 lemon
Sugar to sprinkle
Set oven to 375/F or Mark 5. Cream the butter and sugar together in a
bowl, add the beaten egg and mix in all the other igredients to make
a firm dough. Roll out thinly on a floured surface, cut into rounds
with a 2 1/2 inch cutter sprinkle with sugar and place on a greased
baking tray. Bake for  10-15  minutes until lightly browned. They
should be crisp and sweet like biscuits.
