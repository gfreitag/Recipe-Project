Title: WHITE SPAM & GARLIC PIZZA
Categories: Pizza, Spam, Cheese, Provolone, Ricotta
Yield: 1 Servings
Start with Boboli brand
Premade pizza crust or other
Favorite crust.
Layer on Provalone cheese.
Layer on Mozzerella cheese.
Dollop on spoonfuls
Ricotta cheese. Mince Spam
And garlic in food
Processors until pasty.
Sprinkle Spam and garlic
Paste over pizza. Sprinkle
On grated Romano
Cheese  Bake in 450 degree F
Oven until done.
