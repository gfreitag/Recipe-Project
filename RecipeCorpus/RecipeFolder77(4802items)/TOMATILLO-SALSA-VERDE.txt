Title: TOMATILLO SALSA VERDE
Categories: Sauces, Vegetables
Yield: 2 Cups
1 lb Tomatillos
3 ea Serrano chiles, with seeds
3/4 c  Cilantro leaves
2 tb Lime juice
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
Husk & rinse the tomatillos.  Coarsely chop them & place them in a
food processor or blender.

Add the remaining ingredients to the food processor & puree. Transfer
to a serving dish but let it sit for about 30 minutes before serving.

This is a medium salsa that goes well with tortilla chips.

Mark Miller, "The Great Salsa Book" reprinted in "Vegetarian Gourmet",
Summer 1995
