Title: TING A LINGS
Categories: Desserts, Biscuits, Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
6 oz Chocolate Chips
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Cornflakes
1/2 c  Chopped Filberts
1/4 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Coconut
1/4 c  Chopped Dates Or Dried Fruit
Melt the chocolate over hot (not boiling) water in a double boiler.
Add the remaining ingredients.  Mix well and drop by spoonfuls onto
waxed paper. Allow to harden before serving. From: Syd's Cookbook.
