Title: TORTELLINI & SPINACH STEW
Categories: Stews
9 oz tortellini, cheese-filled
fresh, uncooked
Yield: 2 Servings
:
2 c  spinach leaves  -- packed
2 md tomatoes  -- or 4 plum
tomatoes
:
2 TB olive oil
2 ts garlic  -- minced
1 c  chicken broth  -- or beef or
vegetable
:
2 TB fresh basil  -- minced, or 1
ts dri
:
1/3 c  Asiago cheese  -- grated or
Parmesan c
Freshly ground black pepper
for garnish
:
:
:

Cook tortellini according to package directions. While tortellini is
cooking, remove stems from spinach leaves and set aside. Seed and chop
tomatoes and set aside. Heat oil in a large deep skillet over medium
high heat.  Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.  Add broth and boil
gently, uncovered, for 5 minutes.  Add spinach, tomato, and basil and
heat through just until spinach wilts. Drain tortellini and add to
skillet. Cook for 1 minute or until hot.  Sprinkle with cheese and
serve with pepper.
