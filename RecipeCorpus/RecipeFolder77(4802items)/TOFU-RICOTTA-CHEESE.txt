Title: TOFU RICOTTA CHEESE
Categories: Vegetarian, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Firm tofu
1/3 c  Lemon juice
4 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
4 tb Oil
2 ts Basil
1 ts Garlic
Grind tofu through a food mill or mash with fork. Mix in other
ingredients. Use in recipes that call for a Ricotta style filling.
