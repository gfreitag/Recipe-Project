Title: WINDS IN THE WILLOWS
Categories: Casseroles, Beans
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Hamburger
1 cn Refried beans
2 ea Hot dogs
1 pk Hash browns
Season salt
Paperika
1 cn Mixed vegs
1 cn Tomato soup
Method:
In a casserole dish put browned hamburger on bottom. Then the
:
refried beans , then sliver hotdogs length wise and place on top of
beans. Then add can of mixed vegs and a can of tomato soup. Then put
hash browns on top and sprinkle with season salt and paperika. Bake
at 350 " F for 25-30 min.

"YOU WILL HAVE WIND IN THE WILLOWS"
