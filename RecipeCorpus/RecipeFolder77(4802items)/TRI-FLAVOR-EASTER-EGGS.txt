Title: TRI-FLAVOR EASTER EGGS
Categories: Holidays, Candies, Easter
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1 c  Flaked coconut
3 c  Confectioners sugar
1 c  Crunchy peanut butter
1/2 c  Butter or margarine, melted
1 ts Vanilla
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 lb Flavored compound counting
*NOTE* Can use either butterscotch or chocolate flavored compound
counting. MELTED. Mix crumbs, coconut and 1 1/2 cups confectioners
sugar together in bowl. Work in peanut butter, butter, vanilla and
salt. Work in enough remaining confectioners sugar by hand to shape
centers easily. Shape into small eggs; dip in melted compound
coating. From Sweet Thing @5211 March 1994
