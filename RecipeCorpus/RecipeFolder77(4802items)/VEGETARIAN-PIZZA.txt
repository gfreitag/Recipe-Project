Title: VEGETARIAN PIZZA
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1    12 inch Boboli crust
1    Tomato, cut into thin wedges
Halved
1 c  Broccoli flowerets
1/2 c  Red onion rings
Dried basil leaves
2 c  (8oz) shredded low-moisture
Part-skim mozzarella cheese
Heat oven to 350F. Place crust on cookie sheet. Top with vegetables.
Bake 10-15 minutes or until cheese is melted and topping thoroughly
heated.
