Title: WESTERN SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ea Egg, cooked
1 ea Lettuce, Boston, head
1/2 lb Spinach
1 ea Onion, sliced
2 tb Parsley, chopped
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Salad oil
1 ds Garlic powder
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Remove egg yolk and mash with garlic. Blend in salt, paprika, pepper
and mustard. Add salad oil, vinegar; Beat well. Chop egg white and
parsley and stir in. Combine lettuce, spinach and onion in salad
bowl. Add dressing. Toss lightly.
