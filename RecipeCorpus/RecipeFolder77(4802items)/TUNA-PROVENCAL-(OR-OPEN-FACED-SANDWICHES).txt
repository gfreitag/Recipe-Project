Title: TUNA PROVENCAL (OR OPEN FACED SANDWICHES)
Categories: Cold sands, Greensalads, Seafood
Yield: 2 Servings
MMMMM------------------HOW TO BOIL WATER, TVFN-----------------------
1 cn White tuna, drained
1 tb Capers
12    Cured black olives, sliced
1/2    Red onion, minced
2    Plum tomatoes, seeded, chpd
1 tb Olive oil
2 tb Red wine vinegar
Salt and pepper
1 bn Watercress
6 sl Whole grain bread
MMMMM----------------------SALAD VARIATION---------------------------
Mixed greens
Boiled new potato, quartered
Hard cooked egg, quartered
Cooked green beans
In lg bowl, mix tuna, capers, olives, onion, and tomatoes. Pour in
oil and vinegar; toss. Season and serve with watercress on whole
grain bread. Salad Variation: Arrange mixed greens on plate, top with
potato, egg and green beans. Place a scoop of tuna salad in center of
