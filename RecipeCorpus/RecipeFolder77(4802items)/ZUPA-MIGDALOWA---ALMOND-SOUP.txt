Title: ZUPA MIGDALOWA - ALMOND SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
5 c  Milk
1/2 lb Almonds, blanched, and
-finely ground
1 ts Almond extract
2 c  Cooked riced
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Raisins or currants
1. Heat milk just to simmering in a large saucepan. 2. Add all the
ingredients; stir until well mixed. Cook over low heat 3 to 5
minutes. 3. Serve hot as is traditional for Christmas, or chill
before serving.
