Title: WASP CATCHER
Categories: Non-edible
Yield: 1 Portion
1 ea Plastic box w/lid
1 ea Sweet juice
1 ea Washing-up liquid
1 ts Marmelade
Cut 4 holes in the lid of the plastic box and fill it with sweet
juice and a few drops of washing-up liquid. Place some marmelade on
top of the lid. A cheap and effectiv wasp catcher that should keep
the wasps away from you.

From the Danish Consumer Institute.
