Title: TURKEY PASTA SUPREME
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
3/4 lb Uncooked turkey breast
2    Cl Garlic; minced
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 1/4 c  Whipping cream
2 tb Fresh basil or 2 tsp dried -
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
ds Pepper
3 c  Hot cooked pasta; 3-4 cups
Recipe by: TASTE OF HOME DEC/JAN 1996 Cut turkey into 2x1/4" pieces.
In a skillet, saute turkey and garlic in butter until turkey is
browned and no longer pink, about 6 min. Add cream, basil, Parmesan
and pepper; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer for 3 minutes,
stirring freq. Stir in pasta and toss to coat. Serve immediately.
