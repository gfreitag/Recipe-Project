Title: TUNA POT PIE
Categories: Dinner, Fish, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
2 cn Drained tuna
12 oz Mixed vegetables
2 oz Butter melted
1 T  Flour
2 ea Pie crusts 8 inch
1/2 c  Milk
2 oz Parmesean cheese
Melt butter in large pot.  When melted whisk in flour to form a light
rue. Then whisk in milk to make a sauce.  Stir in tuna and
vegetables. Heat through.  Add parmesean cheese and stir in.  Put
mixture into pie crust and cover with other crust.  Cook 1/2 hour in
oven at 350 degrees. Let cool for 10 minutes then serve.
