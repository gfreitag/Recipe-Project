Title: TOFU WHIPPED CREAM TOPPING
Categories: Vegetarian, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
10 1/2 oz Firm tofu
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Confectioners sugar
1/2 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Vanilla
Combine all ingredients and blend in blender until smooth. Serve
immediately or chill.
