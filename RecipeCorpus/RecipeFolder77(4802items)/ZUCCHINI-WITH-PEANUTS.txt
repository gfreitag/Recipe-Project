Title: ZUCCHINI WITH PEANUTS
Categories: Main dish, African, Nuts, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
4 sm Zucchini
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Oil
1 c  Unsalted peanuts, raw or
-- roasted, coarsely ground
Simmer the zucchini whole in about 1/2 c salted water until very
tender, about 10 minutes.  Combine the zucchini & oil & mash into a
smooth consistency.  Top with the nuts. VARIATION: When zucchini is
tender, drain & slice it, gently stir in the oil & top with nuts.
