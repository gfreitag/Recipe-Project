Title: TREACLE TOFFEE
Categories: Candies, Welsh
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 lb Demerara sugar
1/4 pt Water
4 oz Butter
1/4 ts Cream of tartar
3 tb Black treacle
3 tb Golden syrup
2    Drops vinegar
(LOSIN DU (TAFFI TRIOG OR CYFLAITH) Dissolve the sugar in the water.
Add the buner   , cream of tartar, treacle and syrup. Add 2 drops
vinegar. Bring to the boil and heat to 270/F. or 131/C. Do not stir.
Turn into a buttered tin. After 5 minutes cut into squares. Leave to
set.
