Title: TUNA SALAD VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Cajun, Salads
Yield: 3 Cups
3 c  Green Salad
2 cn Tuna (6 1/2 oz) each
1 c  Vinaigrette dressing
Place Green Salad in a large bowl.  Add tuna, and mix well. Pour
dressing over salad.  Toss lightly to distribute.

Prep. time: 30 minutes
