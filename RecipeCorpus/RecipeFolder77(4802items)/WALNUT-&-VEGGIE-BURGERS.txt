Title: WALNUT & VEGGIE BURGERS
Categories: Burgers, Rice, Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Good guess
MMMMM----------------MEALS WITHOUT MEAT, TV FOOD---------------------
2 ts Veg oil, divided
1/2    Red onion, chpd
1    Rib celery, chpd
1    Carrot, chpd
1/2    Red bell pepper, chpd
Garlic, opt
1 c  Walnuts, toasted, ground
1/2 c  Breadcrumbs
1/2 c  Cooked brown rice OR orzo
-pasta
2    Eggs
Salt and pepper
Buns
MMMMM--------------------------FIXINGS-------------------------------
Avocado slices
Swiss cheese slices
Red onion slices
Catsup
Mustard
Saute onion celery, carrots and red bell pepper in 1t oil until soft.
Cover if desired. Garlic may be added if desired. Add nuts, crumbs,
and rice. Form into patties. Fry in 1t oil until golden. Place on bun
and assemble.
