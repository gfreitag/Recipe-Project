Title: TOFU PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Tofu
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Tofu
1/2 c  Oil
1 1/4 c  Sugar or
1/2 c  Honey
1/3 c  Cocoa
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/4 ts Salt
Put  all  ingredients  in blender or food  proceesor. Blend until
smooth. Pour into serving dishes and chill well, at least 2 hrs.
