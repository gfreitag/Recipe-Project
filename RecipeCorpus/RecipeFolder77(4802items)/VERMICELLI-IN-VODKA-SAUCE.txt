Title: VERMICELLI IN VODKA SAUCE
Categories: Alcohol, Pasta, Italian, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings  Ingredients: 2 dried red peppers 3/4 cup vodka Soak red peppers in
vodka for 24 hours

10 Tbsp butter 6 oz tomatoes,
1 lb Vermicelli 3/4 cup freshly grated
2-2/3 cup whipping cream
chopped
Parmesan cheese   3 Tbsp minced fresh parsley Freshly ground pepper
to taste Directions: Combine cream, butter and tomatoes in heavy
large saucepan. Simmer until reduced by 1/3, about 12 minutes. Cook
pasta according to directions. Drain. Add to sauce. Boil 1 minute,
stirring constantly. Discard red pepper add vodka to pasta. Simmer
until sauce thickens, stirring constantly, about 3 minutes. Mix in
3/4 cup Parmesan and parsley. Serve immediately, passing additional
Parmesan and pepper separately.
