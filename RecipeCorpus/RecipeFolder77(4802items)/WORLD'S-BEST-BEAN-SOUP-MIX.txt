Title: WORLD'S BEST BEAN SOUP MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 40 Servings
1 lb Navy beans
1 lb Great northern beans
1 lb Pinto beans
1 lb Red beans
1 lb Dried soybeans
1 lb Yellow split peas
1 lb Green split peas
1 lb Black eyed peas
1 lb Dried lentils
1 lb Dried limas -- Large
1 lb Dried baby limas
1 lb Barley, pearled
Combine all the ingredients in a large pan or bowl. Measure out 2-cup
gift packages and store in airtight containers.

: Diane Phillips, The Perfect Mix
