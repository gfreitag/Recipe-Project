Title: WINTER SQUASH WITH MADEIRA
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Serves
1 1/4 lb Acorn or butternut squash
2 tb Butter
2 tb Corn oil
1 c  Heavy cream
1/4 c  Madeira wine
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
2 tb Melted butter
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs
Prep time: 10 mins   Cook time: 50 mins   Difficulty: *

Peel, quarter and seed squash. Cut into 1/4 inch slices. Saute in
butter and oil until lightly browned (2 - 3 minutes). Butter a 1
quart baking dish. Place half of squash in dish. Sprinkle with salt
and pepper. Pour on half of a mixture of the cream and Madeira. Top
with remaining squash and season. Pour on remaining cream and Madeira
mixture. Combine bread crumbs and nuts with melted butter and
sprinkle on top. Bake at 325 F for 45 minutes or until squash is done.
