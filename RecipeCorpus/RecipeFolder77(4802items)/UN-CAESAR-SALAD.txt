Title: UN-CAESAR SALAD
Categories: Digest, Nov.
Yield: 1 Servings
1    To 4 cloves garlic, to
Taste, chopped
4 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 tb Capers including the brine
4 tb Chickpeas
4 tb Additional chickpea can or
Cooking liquid
2 ts Tamari, or to taste -
Depends on salt in beans
x  Additional capers for
Garnish
x  Pepper
x  Croutons (optional)
x  Romaine lettuce
Blend everything except the last three items until only tiny pieces of
chickpea are visible.  (I used a Braun hand-blender). Toss Romaine
lettuce with mixture. Garnish with the capers and fresh ground black
pepper and croutons.  Makes somewhere around 1 cup.
