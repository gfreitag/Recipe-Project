Title: WOMACK'S HOUSE COBBLER
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 lb Butter
1 qt Prepared fruit
Mix flour, sugar, cinnamon, cut in butter until coarse and lumpy.
Arrange fruit in 8 inch square baking pan. Top with flour mix. Bake
until golden brown and crisp,
350    degrees for 30 minutes.
