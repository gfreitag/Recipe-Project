Title: TWO HOUR SPAGHETTI SAUCE
Categories: Beef, Pasta, Sauces and
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1 tb Salad Oil
1 lb Ground Beef
1 ds Garlic Salt
29 oz Can Whole Tomatoes
8 oz Can Tomato Sauce
6 oz Can Tomato Paste
1 cn Cream Of Mushroom Soup
1 1/2 ts Oregano
1 ts Salt
1 tb Chili Powder
ds Pepper
2 c  Water
1    Bay Leaf
Brown onion in oil; add meal and seasonings; cook until meat is
brown. Add soup, paste, water and all other ingredients. Simmer,
uncovered, for 2 to 2 1/2 hours.

NOTE:  The ninth ingredient is 1 can mushrooms-sorry for the typo
