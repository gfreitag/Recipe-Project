Title: TIPSY CHEESE SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Sharp cheddar cheese; shredd
2    Cl Garlic; crushed
1/2 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 c  Beer
Beat cheese (at room temperature) until creamy.  Add garlic,
Worcestershire sauce, and mustard.  Gradually add beer.
