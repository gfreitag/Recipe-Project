Title: WENDY'S FROSTY
Categories: Copycat, Desserts
Yield: 2 Servings
3/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Chocolate drink powder
-(Hershey or Nestle)
4 c  Vanilla ice cream
Combine all of the ingredients in a blender.  Blend on medium speed
until cream.  Stir if necessary.

If too thin, freeze the mixture in the blender or in cups until
thicker.

Source: Top Secret Recipes by Todd Wilbur
