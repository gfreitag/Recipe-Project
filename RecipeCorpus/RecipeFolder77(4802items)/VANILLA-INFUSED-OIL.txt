Title: VANILLA-INFUSED OIL
Categories: New imports, Seasonings
3    vanilla beans, -- split
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  light olive oil

In a small saucepan top vanilla beans with oil. Slowly heat until hot
and vanilla is aromatic. Cool, strain and store refrigerated
