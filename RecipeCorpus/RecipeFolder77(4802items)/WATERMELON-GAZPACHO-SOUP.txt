Title: WATERMELON GAZPACHO SOUP
Categories: New text im, Cooking rig
Yield: 1 Servings    2 1/2 c  fresh watermelon juice
2    tomatoes -- chopped
1 sm cucumber -- peeled and
diced
:
3    scallions
Salt and pepper
Crostini for garnish
:
:

In a blender combine watermelon juice, tomatoes, cucumber, scallions.
and salt and pepper.

Serve in bowls garnished with crostini.
