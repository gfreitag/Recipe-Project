Title: WHEATEN MILK BISCUITS
Categories: Petfood, Misc.
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Wheat germ
1/2 c  Powdered milk
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Whole-wheat flour
6 tb Butter-flavored shortening
1    Egg
1 ts Brown sugar
1/2 c  Water
Preheat oven to 325Ã¸F.  Combine dry ingredients.  Cut in shortening
until crumbly.  Beat egg and combine with brown sugar. Add to flour
mixture. Gradually add water until a stiff dough is formed. Knead
until smooth and pliable.  Roll dough to 1/2 inch thickness and cut
into the classic bone shape.  Bake for 30 minutes. Store in
