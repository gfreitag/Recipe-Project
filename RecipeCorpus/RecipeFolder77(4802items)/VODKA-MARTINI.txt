Title: VODKA MARTINI
Categories: Mixed drink, Vodka, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 oz Vodka
1/2 oz Dry vermouth
1    Green olive
Cracked ice
Fill a mixing glass with ice. Pour in vodka and dry vermouth. Stir (or
shake) and strain into a martini glass. Garnish with an olive.
