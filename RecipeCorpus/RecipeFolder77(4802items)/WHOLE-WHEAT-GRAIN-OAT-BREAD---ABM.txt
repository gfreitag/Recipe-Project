Title: WHOLE WHEAT GRAIN OAT BREAD - ABM
Categories: Bread, Abm
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 1/8 c  Water, at 105-110 degrees F
1 tb Butter or margarine
2 tb Molasses or honey
1 1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 c  White flour
1/2 c  Rolled oats
3/4 c  7-grain cereal
1/4 c  Nonfat dried milk powder
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
3 tb Gluten
2 ts Yeast
Place ingredients in bread machine in order given (or in reverse
order if your machine takes the dry ingredients first).

To make in bread machine: process on whole wheat cycle.

To bake in oven: process on dough cycle.  Remove dough, punch down and
shape into loaf.  Place in greased bread loaf pan and let rise until
doubled, about one hour. Bake in 400 degree F oven for 20 minutes.
Remove loaf to cool.

Note: Instead of using 7 grain cereal, you can use 8 grain, 9 grain,
12 grain, etc. -- whatever is available where you shop. You can
usually find this stuff in grocery stores where they have bins with
grains, flours, etc.

Option: Add 1/4 sunflower seeds.
