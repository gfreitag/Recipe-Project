Title: TURKISH HAZELNUTS
Categories: Candy, Nuts
Yield: 4 Cups
4 c  Oregon hazelnuts
2    Egg whites
1 c  Sugar
1 ds Salt
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
In a large shallow baking pan, toast hazelnuts in 325 oven, 15
minutes, stirring occasionally. Meanwhile, beat egg whites until
foamy; gradually add sugar and salt, beating until stiff. Fold in
toasted hazelnuts. Melt butter in same pan; spread nut mixture on
top. Bake 30 to 40 minutes at 325 or until nut are browned and butter
is absorbed. Stir nuts thoroughly every 10 minutes during baking.
