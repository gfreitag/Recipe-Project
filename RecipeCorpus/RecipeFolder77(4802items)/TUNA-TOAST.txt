Title: TUNA TOAST
Categories: Cyberealm, Appetizers, Ethnic
Yield: 4 Servings
2 cn Tuna in Water,drained
6    Anchovies, chopped finely
5 1/2 oz Butter, soft
1    Peel of a Lemon
Salt
2    Pickles,cubed finely
8 sl Whole Wheat Bread
1 .Mix the anchovies, butter, lemonpeel and tuna together and mix
very good. 2. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 3. Toast the
bread and spread the tunamix on it. 4. Cut toast diagonally in half
and garnish with pickles.
Out of "D'Guchi"magazine
Typed by Brigitte Sealing
