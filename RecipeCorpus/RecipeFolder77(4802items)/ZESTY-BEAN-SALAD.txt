Title: ZESTY BEAN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Vegetables,mixed,drained(16
1 cn Kidney beans,drained(15oz)
1 c  Swiss cheese,julienne strips
1 tb Parsley,chopped
1    Garlic clove,minced
1 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Basil
1/8 ts White pepper
1/4 c  Caesar dressing,prepared
1 pk Croutons
Combine all ingredients except croutons in large bowl. Refrigerate
several hours. Sprinkle with croutons before serving.
