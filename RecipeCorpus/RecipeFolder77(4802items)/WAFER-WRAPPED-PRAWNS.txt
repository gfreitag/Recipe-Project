Title: WAFER WRAPPED PRAWNS
Categories: Fondue, Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
12 oz Prawns -- peeled & chopped
1    Green chile -- seeded and
Chopped
2 ts Oyster sauce
2    Sheets filo pastry
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
4 tb Tomato sauce
1 ts Chilli sauce
1/2 ts Sesame oil
In a bowl mix together prawns, chilli and oyster sauce.

Cut filo pastry into 4 inch squares. Place a heaped teaspoon of prawn
filling in the centre of each square, draw the corners of the pastry
together and twist to form little bundles. Place on a floured serving
plate and chill until required.

To make sauce, pour tomato sauce, chilli sauce, 2 tablespoons water
and sesame oil into a saucepan and heat gently for 3-4 minutes. serve
hot with the prawn bundles cooked in hot oil.
