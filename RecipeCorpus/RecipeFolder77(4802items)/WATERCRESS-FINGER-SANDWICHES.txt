Title: WATERCRESS FINGER SANDWICHES
Categories: Salads, French, Vegetables, Microwave, Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
4 sl White bread
1    Bunch watercress
2 tb Mayonnaise
Salt and pepper
Trim crust off bread, then butter each slice. Wash watercress, then
chop very fine. Add mayonnaise and seasoning to watercress; mix
thoroughly. Spread watercress mixture on two slices of the bread, and
top with remaining slices. Cut sandwiches in half, serve garnished
with watercress.
