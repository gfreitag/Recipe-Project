Title: TOLL HOUSE COOKIES
Categories: Desserts, Cookies
Yield: 1 Batchngs
1/2 c  Butter
1/4 c  Shortening
1/2 c  White sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Semisweet chocolate pieces
Make 8" foil "pans" so cookies will hold shape.

Mix all in order given.  chill dough 3 hours - 3 days.
From Marie Campbell
