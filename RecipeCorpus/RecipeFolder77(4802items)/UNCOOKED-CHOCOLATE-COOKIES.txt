Title: UNCOOKED CHOCOLATE COOKIES
Categories: Chocolate, Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  White sugar
1/2 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Cocoa
2 1/2 c  Oatmeal
1 1/2 c  Cocoanut
1/2 ts Salt
Boil first 4 ingredients to-gether for 2 min. no longer,cool and add
vanilla. In large bowl put oatmeal and cocoanut and pour cocoa mixture
over. Press in pan or drop by tsps on waxed paper. Refrigerate.
Instead of cocoanut you can use 1/2  cup peanut butter.
