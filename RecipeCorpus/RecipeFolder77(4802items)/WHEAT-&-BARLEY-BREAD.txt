Title: WHEAT & BARLEY BREAD
Categories: Abm, Breads
Yield: 1 Lg. loaf
MMMMM-------------------LISA CRAWLEY/TEASPOON------------------------
1 1/8 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Bread Flour
1 1/2 c  Whole Wheat Flour
1/2 c  Barley Flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Butter or Margarine
1 1/2 tb Nonfat Dry Milk Powder
2 ts Active Dry Yeast
Place all ingred. in breadmachine according to mfg. instructions.
Start.
