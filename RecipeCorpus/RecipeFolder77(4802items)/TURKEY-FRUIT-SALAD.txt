Title: TURKEY FRUIT SALAD
Categories: Poultry, Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Honey
1/8 ts Ground ginger
2 c  Cubed cooked turkey
11 oz Mandarin oranges; drained
1 c  Unpeeled apple; chopped
1 c  Grape halves
8 1/4 oz Pineapple chunks; drained
1/2 c  Pecan halves; toasted
Recipe by: TASTE OF HOME - FEB/MARCH 1996 In a large bowl, combine
mayonnaise, honey and ginger. Stir in turkey, oranges, apple, grapes
and pineapple. Refrigerate for 1 hour. Sprinkle with pecans just
before serving.
