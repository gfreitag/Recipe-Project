Title: ZUCCHINI CHUTNEY
Categories: Relishes, Indian
Yield: 1 Pint
3 md Zucchini
1    Onions
1/2 ts Hing
1/2 ts Tamcon
2    Green chillies
Fry cut zucchini, onions, and green chillies. Add turmeric, salt,
cook on low flame for 5 to 10 minutes. Boil tamcon, add to mix above.
Pulverize the whole thing in blender.

Seasoning:    Thalshi Kottify with mustard seeds, urad dal.
