Title: TOFU "CHEESE" SAUCE
Categories: Vegetarian, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Tofu, firm; crumbled
1/3 c  Lemon juice,freshly squeezed
1/3 c  Nutritional yeast flakes
1/3 c  Tahini, raw
3 tb Tamari
3 tb ;water
1 ts Basil, dried
1/8 ts Turmeric
1    Garlic clove; peeled
1/4 ts Pepper
Put all of the ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
Serve this sauce warm or at room temperature.
