Title: WATERCRESS SALAD WITH LEMON DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz Watercress; stemmed
1/2 c  Monterey jack cheese; grated
1/2 c  Fresh blueberries; -or-
1/2 c  Seedless red grapes
1/4 c  Pecans; chopped & toasted
-----lemon dressing-----
1    Garlic clove
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
2 tb Sour cream
1 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Oil
