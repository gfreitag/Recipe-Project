Title: VEGETABLE SUNBURGERS
Categories: Main dish, Nuts
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Grated carrots
1/2 c  Finely chopped celery
2 tb Chopped onions
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1 tb Chopped green peppers
1 ea Egg, Beaten
1 tb Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Tomato juice
1 c  Ground Sunflower Seeds
2 tb Wheat Germ
1/8 ts Dried Basil
Preheat oven to 350. In a medium-size bowl combine all ingredients,
and shape into patties. Arrange in an oiled shallow baking dish. Bake
until brown on top, about 15 minutes. Turn patties and bake until
reverse sides are brown 10-15 m.
