Title: TURKEY SAUSAGE-FETTUCINE ALFREDO
Categories: Pasta, Meat, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Italian Turkey Sausage
6 c  Cooked fettucine, warm
1/2 c  Evaporated skim milk
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
3 tb Margarine, softened
2 tb Minced fresh parsley
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 sm Tomato, chopped
Spray large skillet with no-stick cooking spray. Heat over
medium-high heat about 30 seconds. Add lean turkey Italian sausage.
Cook, uncovered, 14 to 16 min. or until lightly browned & no longer
pink in center, turning occasionally.

Combine fettucine with all remaining ingrediants except tomato. Toss
untill fettucine is well coated. Spoon onto large platter. Top with
sausages. Sprinkle with chopped tomato.

Source: The Turkey Store. 25-Aug-96
