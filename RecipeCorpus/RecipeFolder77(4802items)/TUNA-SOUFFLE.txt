Title: TUNA SOUFFLE
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1/3 ts Pepper
4 tb Flour
4 tb Milk
1 1/4 c  Flaked tuna, drained
4 ea Egg whites, well beaten
Pinch of thyme
Parmesan cheese
Mix together the first 4 ingredients; slowly add milk; then tuna.
Fold in beaten egg whites, then thyme. Pour into a well-buttered 2
quart souffle dish. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Bake at 350F for
45    minutes.
