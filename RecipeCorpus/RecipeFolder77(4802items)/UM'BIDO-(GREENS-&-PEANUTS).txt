Title: UM'BIDO (GREENS & PEANUTS)
Categories: Side dish, Nuts, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Spinach, cooked
1 c  Peanuts, coarsely ground
2 tb Oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Cook spinach in enough water to cover.  After 4 or 5 minutes,
sprinkle in the peanuts & stir well. Continue cooking over medium
heat for about 30 minutes, adding more water if necessary to prevent
burning. Just befroe serving, drain & mix in the oil, salt & pepper.
