Title: TOMATO & CASHEW SAUCE
Categories: Sauce, Gravy
1 TB butter
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  tomatoes -- peeled and
chopped
:
1/2 c  cream
1/4 c  ground cashews
salt and pepper
:

To make 2 cups of sauce for chicken, fish, pork or vegetables: melt
butter, add tomatoes and cook until soft, (about 5 minutes). Add
cream and nuts and season to taste. Reheat and serve.
