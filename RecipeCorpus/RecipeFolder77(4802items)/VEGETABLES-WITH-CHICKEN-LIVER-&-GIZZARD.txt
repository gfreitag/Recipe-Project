Title: VEGETABLES WITH CHICKEN LIVER & GIZZARD
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Chicken livers & gizzards; s
18    Snow peas
14    Cauliflower; chopped
1    Onion; chopped
1    Carrot; chopped
1    Salt
2    Cornstarch
14    Chicken stock
1    Soy sauce
14    Cooking oil
2    Rice wine
Recipe by: Nila Sweeney Preparation Time: 0:35 1. Soak livers &
gizzards in mixture of cornstach, wine and soy. Let stand 15min.

2. Fry onion and vegetables in oil with 1 tsb salt. Cook for 5min.
Set as

3.  Fry gizzards until tender and then add liver. When done put back
vegetables.

4. Add cornstarch mix and stock and finish cooking (about 10-15min)
