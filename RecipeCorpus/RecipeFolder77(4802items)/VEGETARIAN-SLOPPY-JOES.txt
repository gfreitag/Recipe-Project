Title: VEGETARIAN SLOPPY JOES
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 md Onion; chopped
1 md Green pepper; chopped
3 c  Cooked brown rice
32 oz Mexican-style pinto beans
-- (canned)
3/4 c  Hickory smoke barbecue sauce
4    Whole grain buns
Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.  Add onions and green
pepepr; cook 2 to 3 minutes.  Add rice, beans and barbecue sauce.
Simmer 10 to 15 minutes, until heated through. Serve on buns.

Each serving provides: * 542 calories * 19.2 g. protein * 9.4 g. fat
* 96.4 g. carbohydrates * 3.2 g. dietary fiber * 17 mg. cholesterol *
1637 mg. sodium.
