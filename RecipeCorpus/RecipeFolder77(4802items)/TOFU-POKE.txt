Title: TOFU POKE
Categories: Main dish, Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
16 oz Tofu, firm
1 md Onion, sliced thinly
6 md Tomatoes, julienned
1 ts Ginger, fresh minced
3 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Sesame seeds, toasted
Lemon juice to taste
Drain and rinse the tofu. Cut it into 1/2 inch cubes and place the
cubes in a mixing bowl. Add the tomatoes and onions. In a separate
container, mix the ginger, soy sauce, sesame seeds and lemon juice.
Pour this mixture over the tofu and vegetables. Turn gently and allow
to marinate in the refridgerator for a few hours before serving.
Serve over cous cous or pasta.
