Title: UNCLE TAI'S SPECIAL DRINK B1
Categories: Beverages, Chinese, Side dish
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Jigger dark rum
1    Jigger light rum
1    Jigger apricot brandy
1 ts Orgeat almond syrup
2 ds Triple sec
Orange juice
Pineaple juice
Combine rums, apricot brandy,almond syrup & Triple Sec. Fill glass
with equal portions orange juice & pineapple juice. Orgeat almond
syrup is a flavored sugar syrup which is available at liquor stores.
This may be decorated with miniature paper parasols.

Temperature(s): COLD Effort: EASY Time: 00:05 Source: UNCLE TAI'S
Comments: SOUTH POST OAK; HOUSTON
