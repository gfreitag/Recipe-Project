Title: TURKEY CHILI ADIOS
Categories: Turkey, Lefto over, Chili, Stove top, Turkeychili
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Cooked, minced turkey
1 md Onion chopped
1 c  Canned kidney beans, drained
1    4 oz. can green chiles
2 ts Chili powder
1 ts Cumin powder
2    Ribs celery, chopped
3 c  Turkey or chicken broth
1 c  Raw rice
1    6 oz. can tomato paste
1 ts Prepared mustard
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and cover and simmer, stirring
occasionally until rice is tender, about 35 minutes. For thicker,
richer chili, puree one small can of kidney beans in a blender or
food processor and add to chili. From: Pat Stockett Date: Sat, 10-2
