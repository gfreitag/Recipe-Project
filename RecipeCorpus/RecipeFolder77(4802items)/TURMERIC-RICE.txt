Title: TURMERIC RICE
Categories: Indian, Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Converted Rice
1 ts Ground turmeric
1 pn Of chili powder
1/2    Bay leaf
Bring water to boil in medium sauce pan.  Add rice with spices, stir
gently for 10 seconds. Cover pan, lower heat to low - cook covered
for 30 minutes. Stir when finished.
