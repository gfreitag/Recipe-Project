Title: W.E DEBOE'S MOTHER'S BROWNIES
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate
Yield: 1 Recipe
2    Sticks oleo
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour
3 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Melt the oleo and mix with sugar and
cocoa. Add the rest of the ingredients, finally stirring in the
chopped nuts. Pour into a 9x13-inch greased and floured pan. Bake 30
to 35 minutes.
