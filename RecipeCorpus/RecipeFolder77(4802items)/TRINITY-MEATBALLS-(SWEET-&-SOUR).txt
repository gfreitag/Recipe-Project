Title: TRINITY MEATBALLS (SWEET & SOUR)
Categories: Meatballs
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Hamburger
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1    Egg
Spices
~~~~~~~~~SAUCE~~~~~~~~~~~~~
1 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 c  Water
1 ts Lemon juice
Mix and shape into meatballs. Brown in fry pan or oven. Place in
casserole dish. Dice 1 onion and sprinkle over meatballs. Pour sauce
over top and bake 1 hour at 350. Submitted By BILL AND TRACY SWARTZ
