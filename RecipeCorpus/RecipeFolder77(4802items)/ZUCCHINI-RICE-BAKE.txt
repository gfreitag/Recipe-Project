Title: ZUCCHINI RICE BAKE
Categories: Vegetarian, Casseroles, Main dish, Brown rice
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Cooked brown rice
1 lb Zucchini, cooked, drained,
-mashed
2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
2 lg Green onions sliced thin
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
3    Eggs, beaten (can use 4 egg)
1/4 c  Butter, melted
Salt and pepper to taste
*You can use up to 1 1/2 cups cooked brown rice. Combine rice,
zucchini, cheese, onion and parsley in bowl and mix well. Stir in
eggs, butter, salt and pepper. Spoon into lightly greased 9" baking
dish; sprinkle with paprika. Bake at 350 for 1 hour. This recipe is
easily doubled. Use at least 2 lb zucchini and can take up to 3 cups
cooked brown rice. Use about 4 or 5 large green onions,a nd 4 cups
cheese. You can also use a combination of Cheddar and Jack cheese if
you like. If you double this, bake in a 9 X 13 casserole.
