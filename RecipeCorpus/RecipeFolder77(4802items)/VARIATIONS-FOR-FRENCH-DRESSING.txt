Title: VARIATIONS FOR FRENCH DRESSING
Categories: Diabetic, Dressings, Salads, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------TO 1/2 C OF FRENCH DRESSING---------------------
-lo-cal
MMMMM--------------------------AVOCADO-------------------------------
2 tb Avocado; mashed
MMMMM---------------------------CHEESE--------------------------------
2 tb Blue cheese;mashed
-=OR=-
2 tb Roquefort cheese; mashed
MMMMM----------------------------EGG---------------------------------
1    Hard-cooked egg yolk;
-crumbled & combined with
ds Hot pepper sauce;
MMMMM---------------------------LEMON--------------------------------
1 tb Lemon juice;
MMMMM-------------------------SOY SAUCE------------------------------
1 tb Soy sauce;
Food Exchanges per serving: Avocado Dressing: 1 FAT EXCHANGE Cheese
Dressing: 1/4 MEAT EXCHANGE Egg dressing: 1 MEAT EXCHANGE Lemon
Dressing: Free Soy Sauce Dressing: Free (I used salt free) CAL: 1/2
C=100
