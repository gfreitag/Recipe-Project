Title: WALNUT COTTAGE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg, well beaten
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Milk
2 c  Flour
3 ts Baking-powder
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 ts Vanilla
Cream butter with sugar.  Add egg.  Beat thoroughly. Sift flour,
measure, and sift with baking-powder and salt. Add alternately with
milk to first mixture. Add nuts and flavoring. Mix thoroughly. Pour
into shallow, well-oiled baking pan.  Bake in moderate oven (375 F)
30 minutes. Serve with vanilla or hard sauce.
6    servings.
