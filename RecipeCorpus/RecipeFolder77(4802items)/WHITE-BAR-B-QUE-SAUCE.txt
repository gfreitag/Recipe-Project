Title: WHITE BAR-B-QUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Barbacue, Honey
Yield: 1 Servings
6 tb Mayonnaise
3 tb Honey
1 ts Salt
1 ts Black pepper
2 tb Lemon juice
3 tb White wine vinegar
Whick ingredients together until smooth. Brush on chicken near the
end on broiling or baking.
