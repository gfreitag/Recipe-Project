Title: ZUCCHINI BREAD (M_C-TX) (05/10/93)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 20 Servings
3    Eggs
3/4 c  Oil
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Raw zucchini
2 c  Flour
1 c  Chopped nuts
1/4 ts Baking powder
2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
3 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg
3 ts Vanilla
Beat eggs, add sugar, oil and zucchini. In separate bowl add al dry
ingredients and sift into sugar mixture. Add vanilla and nuts. Pour
into 2 greased loaf pans and bake 45 minutes at 350 . (Fills 3 mini
pans - bake same length of time).
