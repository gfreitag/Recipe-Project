Title: VANILLA EXTRACT 1
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Whole vanilla beans
1 1/2 c  Vodka
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Granulated sugar
In a glass 3 cup container with a tight fitting lid, combine vanilla
beans with the vodka. Cap and allow to sit at room temperature for
three weeks. Remove vanilla beans and strain liquid through a double
into a bowl, if necessary. In a medium
layer of cheesecloth
saucepan, combine water and sugar over medium heat. Bring to a boil,
dissolves. Remove from heat and
stirring constantly, until sugar
cool. Stir into vanilla mixture. Pour mixture into a bottle; cap and
allow to sit at room temperature one month. Yield: 2 cups of extract.
