Title: TUNA PESTO & PASTA
Categories: Pasta, Rice, Couscous
Yield: 2 Servings
1 2/3 c  Frozen peas -- thawed
3 c  Twist pasta -- cooked
6 1/8 oz Tuna in water -- drained and
Flaked
1/4 c  Fat-free mayonnaise
4 tb Pesto
4 tb Fat-free Parmesan cheese
Prepare vegetables and twist pasta according to package directions.
In a skillet, combine vegetables, pasta, tuna, mayonnaise and pesto;
heat through.
