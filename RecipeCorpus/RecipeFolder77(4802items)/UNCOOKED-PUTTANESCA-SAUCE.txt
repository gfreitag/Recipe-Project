Title: UNCOOKED PUTTANESCA SAUCE
Categories: Brenda, Cookbooks, Pasta, Sauces and, Vegetarian
2 lg cloves  garlic -- chopped
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb tomatoes -- ripe, seeded,
coarsely chopped **
:
1/4 c  olive oil
1/2 ts dried basil -- or oregano
1/4 ts hot red pepper flakes
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
:
:
10    Kalamata olives -- pitted,
cut into
strips
:
:
2 TB capers -- drained
1/4 c  flat-leaf parsley --
chopped
:
2 TB freshly grated Parmesan
cheese -- plus extra for
serving
:
:

** Note: Full flavored tomatoes are very important in this recipe.

COMBINE GARLIC, tomatoes, oil, basil, red pepper flakes, salt, pepper,
olives, capers, parsley, and cheese in large serving bowl. Add
drained hot pasta and toss until combined. Serve with extra cheese
and pass the peppermill.

Recommended Pasta: 1/2 pound penne rigate or spaghetti
