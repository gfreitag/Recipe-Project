Title: VARIETY COLESLAW
Categories: Salads, Low-cal
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Shredded cabbage
1 md Carrot, chopped or grated
2 tb Green pepper, chopped
1/4 c  Boiled dressing, diet*
2 tb Plain yogurt
CHOICE OF ONE
1/2    Apple, chopped or
1/3 c  Drained, unsw. pineapple ch
2 tb Raisins
* refers to a dressing made with artificial sweetener Combine cabbage,
carrot and green pepper in a bowl. Combine dressing with yogurt. Add
apple or pineapple or raisins to cabbage mixture. Pour on dressing
and mix gently.
