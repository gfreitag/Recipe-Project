Title: TUILES NOISETTE
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Recipe
3    Egg whites
1 1/2 oz Sugar
1 tb Vanilla
1 1/2 oz Roasted & ground hazelnuts
-(Oregon hazelnuts)
Heat egg whites and sugar in double boiler. Blend in vanilla and
hazelnuts. Form oval tongues 3" long onto a buttered cookie sheet.
Bake at 280 until edges only are light brown, about 8 to 10 minutes.
While hot, put tongues over bottles or rolling pin to make slightly
rounded wafers.
