Title: VODKA PUNCH
Categories: P* best, Holiday, Family, Beverages
Yield: 28 Servings
1 qt Cranberry juice
2 c  Vodka
1 cn 6 oz. frozen lemonade
1 1/2 qt 7-up; chilled (48 oz.)

28 - 4 oz. drinks.   This one can "sneak up on you." Joyce
