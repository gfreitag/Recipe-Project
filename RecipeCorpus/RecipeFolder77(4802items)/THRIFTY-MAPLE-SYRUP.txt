Title: THRIFTY MAPLE SYRUP
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Brown sugar --  packed
1/2 c  Water
1/4 ts Maple flavoring
1 tb Butter or margarine
In a small saucepan, combine the corn syrup, brown sugar andwater;
cook, stirring constantly, until the sugar is dissolved. Add the
maple flavoring and butter. Serve warm or cool. Makes 1 3/4 cups.
Microwave Directions: Combine   the corn syrup, brown sugar and water
in a 1-quart glass measurer. Microwave on 100 percent power for 3 to 4
minutes, stirring once.  Add the maple flavoring and butter.
Serve warm or cool. Penny Halsey (ATBN65B).

: Country Folk Art Magazine, February 1994
