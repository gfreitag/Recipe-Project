Title: TURKEY CREOLE
Categories: Poultry, Rice
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Onion, large diced
1 1/2    Bell Pepper, diced
3/4 c  Celery, diced
24 oz Stewed Tomatoes
4 1/2 ts Chili powder
3/4 ts Salt (to taste)
4 c  Turkey, cooked & cut up
6 c  Rice, cooked
Put everything except rice in crockpot.  Leave for most of the day.
Serve over cooked rice. <How's this for simple?>
