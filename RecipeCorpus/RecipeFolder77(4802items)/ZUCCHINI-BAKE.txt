Title: ZUCCHINI BAKE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Shredded zucchini (3-4 cups)
3    Eggs
1 sm Onion, grated
1/3 c  Buttermilk
2 tb Canola oil
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 c  Bisquick or similar biscuit
Mix
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Spray a 9-inch pan or 11-by-7-inch
baking dish with a nonstick vegetable coating; set aside. Combine all
ingredients in a large bowl and mix well. Pour into the prepared pan.

Bake in the preheated oven for about 1 hour or until lightly browned.
Let rest 5 minutes before cutting into 6 servings.
