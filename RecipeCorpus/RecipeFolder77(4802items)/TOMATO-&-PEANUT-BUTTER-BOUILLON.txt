Title: TOMATO & PEANUT BUTTER BOUILLON
Categories: Soups
Yield: 15 Servings
4 c  Tomato Juice
32 oz Beef Bouillon
1 tb Lemon Juice
2 c  Peanut Butter, Smooth
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 c  Sherry
Combine and warm all ingredients but the sherry in a saucepan. Blend
thoroughly. Once thoroughly blended, cook slowly for about 30 minutes,
stirring frequently. Stir in the sherry and serve.
