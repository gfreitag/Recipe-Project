Title: TRIPLE CHEESE DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Cottage Cheese
3 pk Cream Cheese (8 Oz Each) --
Softened
6 oz Blue Cheese
2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1 ts Seasoned Salt
1/4 ts Red Pepper Sauce
3/4 c  Fresh Parsley -- snipped
Combine all ingredients in large bowl. Mix until thoroughly blended.
Chill several hours before serving. Serve with fresh vegetables and
crackers.
