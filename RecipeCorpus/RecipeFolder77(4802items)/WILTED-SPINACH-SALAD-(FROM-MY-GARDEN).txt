Title: WILTED SPINACH SALAD (FROM MY GARDEN)
Categories: Wasnone
Yield: 1 Servings    2 1/2 TB walnut or almond oil
1/4 lb nuts
1/2 c  mandarin orange segments
1 bn spinach -- washed and dried

In saucepan heat oil with nuts. Add oranges and pour over spinach.
Toss and serve.
