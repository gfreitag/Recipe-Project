Title: VEGGIES IN A DIP
Categories: Advantage, Pickell
Yield: 1 Recipe
8 oz Soft Cream Cheese
1/2 c  Mayonnaise or Miracle Whip
1 pk Ranch Dressing (DRY)
1 1/4 c  Broccoli (chopped)
1 1/4 c  Cauliflower (chopped)
1 pk Pillsbury Crescent Rolls
1    Red Pepper (chopped)
Heat oven to 375F. Spray pan with pam. Unroll dough and spread over
pan. Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden brown. Cool. Mix first 3
ingredients together. Add next 2 and mix. Spread on baked crust. Top
with choped red peppers.
