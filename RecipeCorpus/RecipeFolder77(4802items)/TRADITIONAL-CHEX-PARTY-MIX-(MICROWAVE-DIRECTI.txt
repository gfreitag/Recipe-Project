Title: TRADITIONAL CHEX PARTY MIX (MICROWAVE DIRECTI
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 8 Servings
1/4 c  BUTTER
1 1/4 ts SEASONED SALT
4 1/2 ts WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE
8 c  CHEX CEREAL
1 c  SALTED NUTS
1 c  PRETZEL STICKS
IN A LARGE BOWL, MELT BUTTER, HEATING FOR 1 MINUTE ON HIGH POWER.
STIR IN SEASON SALT AND WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE. ADD CEREAL SLOWLY
STIRRING TO COAT ALL PIECES WELL. MICROWAVE ON HIGH FOR 5 - 6
MINUTES. STIR EVERY TWO MINUTES.
