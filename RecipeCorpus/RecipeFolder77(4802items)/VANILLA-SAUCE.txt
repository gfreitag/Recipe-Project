Title: VANILLA SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 14 Servings
2 c  Nonfat Milk -- or skim
1    Egg Beaters. 99% Egg
Substitute -- 1/4 C
3/4 c  Sugar -- or 1/2 C + 3 pkg
Sweet 'N Low
1 ts Vanilla
ds Nutmeg
1 tb Flour -- or 2 tsp
Cornstarch
Boil milk and beat in sugar, flour, and Egg Beater that have been
mixed together.  Simmer until it thickens, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat adding vanilla and nutmeg.

It is very good.
