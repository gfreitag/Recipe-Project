Title: WARMED TOMATOES WITH BASIL & BRIE
Categories: Side dish, Appetizer, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
4 lg Tomatoes
2 tb Olive oil
6 lg Basil leaves, shredded
8 oz Brie cheese
Core and cut tomatoes into thick slices; arrange on ovenproof platter.
Drizzle with oil; sprinkle with basil.

Remove rind from Brie.  Chop cheese and scatter over tomatoes.
(Tomatoes can be prepared to this point and set aside for up to 1
hour.) Broil for 1 to 2 minutes or just until cheese has melted.
Makes 6 servings. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source:
