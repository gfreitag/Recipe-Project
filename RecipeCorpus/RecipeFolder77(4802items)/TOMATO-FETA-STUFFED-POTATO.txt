Title: TOMATO-FETA STUFFED POTATO
Categories: Baked, Potatoes
Yield: 1 Servings
10 oz Idaho potatoes
1/2 c  Tomato -- chopped
3/4 oz Crumbled feta cheese
1 tb Nonfat sour cream
pn Dried dill
In small bowl combine potato flesh, tomato, feta, sourcream and dill.
Bake at 375 for 15 minutes or until hot.
