Title: TOFFEE ** JO
Categories: Candies, Mw
Yield: 1 Servings
10 x  Graham crackers
1/2 c  Butter
3/4 c  Brown sugar (well packed)
1/4 c  Walnuts (chopped)
1 pk Choc chips (semi-sweet)
Place crackers on bottom of buttered 12 x 7 glass pan. Set this aside.
Combine butter and brown sugar in a 4 cup glass measure cup and micro
on Roast (Med Hi) for 2 minutes or until butter is melted. Stir in
nuts and pour this mixture over the crackers. Micro on Roast for 4 -
5 minutes or until bubbly. Top with Choc chips and continue cooking
on Roast for 1 1/2 minutes until chocolate is softened. Spread across
the top. Chill 30 min. and cut or break into eating pieces.
