Title: TOFU MILK
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
4 oz Tofu
1/2    To 2/3 c water
2 ts Maple syrup
Combine all ingredients in blender and puree until smooth. Use the
lesser amount of water for a light cream consistency, the full amount
for milk.

Makes about 1 cup per "serving."
