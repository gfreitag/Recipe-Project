Title: WALNUT-RAISIN BAKED APPLES
Categories: Fruits, Microwave
Yield: 1 Recipe
4    Apples, peeled and cored as
...directed in Basic Baked
...Apples recipe
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Apple juice
1/4 c  Honey
1 tb Unsalted butter
Fill cavities of prepared apples with raisins and chopped walnuts.
Place apples in 8-inch square baking dish.  Pour water and apple
juice over all. Brush apple tops with honey and dot with butter. Bake
according to directions in Basic Baked Apples recipe.

FOR GROWN-UPS: For a romantic touch, pour * cup warmed Calvados or
applejack over baked apples and ignite with a match.  Serve with
vanilla ice cream or frozen yogurt.

Source: Mommy Made and Daddy Too by Martha and David Kimmel with
Suzanne Goldenson.
