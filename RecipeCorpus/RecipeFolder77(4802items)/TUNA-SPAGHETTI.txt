Title: TUNA SPAGHETTI
Categories: Casseroles, Fish
Yield: 5 Servings
7 oz Thin spaghetti
2    Garlic cloves; chopped fine
1/4 c  Margarine or butter
3/4 c  Half-and-half
1 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Oregano
9 1/4 oz Tuna; drained
1/2 c  Pimiento-stuff olives; slice
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
Cook spaghetti as directed on package; drain.  Cook garlic in
margarine in 2-quart saucepan until garlic is golden brown. Stir in
half-and-half, basil and oregano. Heat to boiling. Stir in tuna,
olives, and cheese. Boil and stir 1 minute.  Pour over hot spaghetti.
Sprinkle with snipped parsley if desired. 5 servings: 375 calories
per serving. From Betty Crocker's Cookbook
