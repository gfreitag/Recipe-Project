Title: VEGETABLE & HERB CURRY
Categories: Main dish, Side dish
Yield: 1 Recipe
1    Box Quick Brown Rice
-- (Vegetable and Herb)
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 md Onion; thinly sliced
2    Celery stalks; chopped
1    Green bell pepper; chopped
1    Fresh apple; chopped  OR...
1/2 c  -Chopped dried apples
1/4 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Water
2 ts Curry powder
Cook contents of box according to package directions. While rice is
cooking, saute vegetables until barely tender (3 to 5 minutes). Stir
in the apples, raisins and water. Cover and steam for a few minutes
until the raisins are softened.  When the rice is done, add it and
the curry powder to the vegetables.  Stir gently until well mixed.
Let sit a few minutes for the flavors to mingle. Add salt or tamari
soy sauce to taste.
