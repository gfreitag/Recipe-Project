Title: YUMMY IRISH CREME [A]
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 8 Servings
1 3/4 c  Whiskey
14 oz Can sweetened condensed milk
1 c  Heavy cream
4    Eggs
2 tb Chocolate syrup
2 ts Instant coffee
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Almond extract
Process all ingredients in blender until mixed; chill. Garnish
servings with edible flowers if desired.

Source: Great Recipes form Great Gardeners
