Title: WHITE BEAN SANDWICHES
Categories: Beans, Sandwiches, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Sandwiches
15 oz Can small white beans,
Rinsed and drained
1 lg Stalk celery, diced
2    Green onions, thinly sliced
1/2 ts Dijon mustard
1 ts Wine vinegar
1 tb Mayonnaise
Salt and pepper to taste
4    Sandwich rolls
Optional: lettuce & tomato
Combine beans, celery, onions, mustard, vinegar, amyonnaise, salt and
pepper in a bowl.  Divide white bean filling evenly among rolls. Top
with lettuce and tomato, if desired. (The filling can also be served
with hearty wheat crackers as a dip)
