Title: TROPICAL TURKEY SALAD
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
5 c  Torn fresh spinach
3 c  Torn lettuce
2 c  Cooked turkey; cubed
2 sl Red onion; separated into ri
1/2 c  Green pepper; chopped
1/2 c  Each: mandarin oranges, slic
1/2 c  Pineapple chunks
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Raspberry syrup
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1 1/2 ts Honey
1/2 ts Celery seed
1/2 c  Sliced almonds; toasted
1/4 c  Flaked coconut; toasted
Recipe by: TASTE OF HOME DEC/JAN 1996 Line a large salad bowl with
spinach and lettuce Combine the next six ingredients; spoon into
bowl. In a jar with tight-fitting lid, combine oil, syrup, vinegar,
honey and celery seed; shake well. Pour over the salad. Top with
almonds and coconut. serve immediately.
