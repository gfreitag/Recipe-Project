Title: WELCOME BACK BAKED BEANS
Categories: Side dishes
Yield: 24 Servings
1    #10 can pork and beans
1 c  Catsup
1 c  Dark corn syrup
1/4 c  Mustard
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
1 lb Bacon
1 md Onion, chopped fine
combine beans and seasonings in a large baking pan. Top with bacon
strips. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes, or until bacon is crisp.
Serve warm.
