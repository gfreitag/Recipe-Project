Title: TWO POTATO TOPPED COTTAGE PIE
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
5 md Kohlrabi bulbs, peeled &
-- chopped
1/2 c  Pearl barley
1 ea Onion, chopped
1 ea Celery rib, sliced
1 ea Carrot diced
1/2 c  Mushrooms, chopped
2 ts Basil
2 ts Parsley
Salt & pepper
1/2 c  Red wine
1 c  Stock
2 c  Mashed potatoes
2 c  Mashed sweet potatoes
Place all ingredients, except the potatoes, in a large saucepan &
bring to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat & simmer until the vegetables &
barley are tender, about 45 minutes. Preheat the oven to 350F.
Transfer the cooked mixture to a lightly oiled casserole dish & top
with alternate diagonal rows of both kinds of potato. (For a more
decorative look, try piping the potatoes). Bake for 35 to 40 minutes
or until the edges bubble & the potatoes are golden brown.  Serve
immediately.
