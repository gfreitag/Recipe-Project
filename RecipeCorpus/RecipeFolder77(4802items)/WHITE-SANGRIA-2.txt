Title: WHITE SANGRIA 2
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Spanish, New (to be
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Limes -- sliced
2    Lemons -- sliced
2 lg Red Delicious Apples --
Sliced
1 1/2 l  Chablis
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Brandy
2    Bottles Club Soda(33.8 oz.
-each)
Combine sliced fruit, wine, sugar and brandy;  chill at least 2 hours.
Gently stir in club soda and serve sangria over ice.
