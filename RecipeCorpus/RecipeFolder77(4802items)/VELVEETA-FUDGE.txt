Title: VELVEETA FUDGE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Margarine or butter
1 lb Velveeta cheese
1 c  Cocoa
4 lb Icing sugar
2 c  Chopped nuts
2 ts Vanilla
Melt margarine and cheese to-gether over low heat. Mix in remaining
ingredients. Spread in greased pan and cool. Cut in squares and keep
in fridge. Makes enough for a big party. I cut recipe in half and its
just right.
