Title: VANILLA PUDDING & PIE FILLING MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/3 c  Granulated sugar
1 3/4 c  Cornstarch
3/4 ts Salt
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients. Stir with a wire whisk until
blended.  Pour into a 4-cup container with a tight-fitting lid. Seal
container. Label with date and contents. Store in a cool, dry place.
Use within 4 months. Makes about 4 cups mix.
