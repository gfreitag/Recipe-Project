Title: TURKEY STROGANOFF (ADMISTON)
Categories: Entree, Charleston
2 TB olive oil
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb ground turkey
1 md onion -- chopped
1 pk sliced mushrooms
1/3 c  dry white wine
1 ds nutmeg
Salt and pepper
:
1 1/2 c  sour cream
Your favorite pasta --
cooked
Paprika
:
:
:

Heat oil in heavy skillet over medium heat. Brown turkey and onion.
Mix in mushrooms, wine and nutmeg. Season with salt and pepper.
Reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes. Add sour cream. Do not boil. Place
pasta on a platter. Pour turkey mixture over top and sprinkle with
paprika.
