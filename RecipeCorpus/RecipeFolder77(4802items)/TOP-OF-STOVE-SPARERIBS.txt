Title: TOP OF STOVE SPARERIBS
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Cajun
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lb Spareribs or pork chops
3/4 c  Water
2 tb Vinegar
2    Onions, sliced
1 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
3/4 c  Catsup
2 tb Brn. sugar, packed
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 tb Oil
Season meat, brown in oil.  Add sliced onions.  Mix other ingreds.
Cover and let simmer abt. 3 hrs, or until tender. Courtesy Telephone
