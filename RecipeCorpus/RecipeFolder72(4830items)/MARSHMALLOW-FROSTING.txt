Title: MARSHMALLOW FROSTING
Categories: Frostings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Water
3/4 ts Vinegar
3    Egg whites
15 lg Marshmallows; chopped
Combine sugar, water and vinegar in a heavy saucepan. Cook over medium
heat, stirring frequently until mixture comes to a boil and sugar
disolves. Continue cooking, stirrin frequently until mixture reaches
soft ball stage (240~). Beat egg whites until foamy. While beating at
medium speed, slowly pour hot syrup in a thin stream over egg whites.
Turn mixer to high speed and continue beating until stiff peaks form
and frosting is thick enough to spread. Fold in marshmallows. Enough
to cover a 3-layer cake.
