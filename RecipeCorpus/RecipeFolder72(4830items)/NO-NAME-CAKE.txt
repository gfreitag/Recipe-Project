Title: NO-NAME CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Of flour
1 ts Of baking soda
1/2 ts Of salt
3 ts Of cinnamon
3    Eggs beaten
2 c  Of sugar
1/2 c  Of salad oil
1/2 ts Of vanilla
5 c  Of apples (peeled and sliced
1/2 c  Of chopped walnuts (optional
Combine eggs, oil, sugar, & vanilla. Add flour, salt, cinnamon, &
baking soda. Fold in apples and nuts. Bake at 350 degrees in a
greased 13x9 pan for 1 hour. Let cool then serve!
