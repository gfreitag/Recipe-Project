Title: MARINATED JALAPENOS-CH
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 Servings
Whole jalapenos -- cut in
Half,seeded
Whole lime juice
Lots salt
Whole onion rings
Mix all ingredients and let sit for 5 minutes.  Eat.

They are delicious. The lime and the salt gets rid of the hotness (if
can't still handle it cut them and put them is salt and water for 1-2
hours to remove some hotness before you put the lime).
