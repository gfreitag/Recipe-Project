Title: NUT LOAF
Categories: Breads, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Nuts
2 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
1 ea Egg
Beat egg in cup, then fill cup with milk. Add to above mixture. Bake
35 to 40 minutes.

Note: Not temperature given. Suggest moderate (350 F) oven.

Source: Timber Run Grange, Muskingum County, OH
