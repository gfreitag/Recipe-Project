Title: MOLHO MALAGEUTA (MALAGUETA SAUCE)
Categories: Sauce/gravy, Brazil, Upload
Yield: 1 Bottle
1 c  Chiles, malagueta; stemmed
1 c  Vinegar
1    Wine bottle, 750ml
Oil, olive
MMMMM----------------------HEAT SCALE - HOT---------------------------
Place the chiles in the wine bottle, add the vinegar and olive oil
to fill. Cork the bottle securely. Place bottle in refrigerator and
let steep for at least 2 weeks, shaking the bottle infrequently.
Substitute tabascos or piquins if malagueta chiles are unavailable.
