Title: MOROCCAN GLAZED CARROTS
Categories: Appetizers, Moroccan, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Carrots, peeled
Salt
4 tb Olive oil
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Cilantro, chopped
Hot red pepper, to taste
Sweet red pepper, to taste
Slice the carrots fairly thin lengthwise.  Cut slices into 3/4"
lengths. Cook segments in boiling, salted water until just tender.
Drain. Toss in pot with remaining ingredients. Serve hot as a side
dish or cold as an appetizer accompanying other vegetables.
