Title: MR. FOOD'S CUCUMBER SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 14 Servings
1 c  White vinegar
1 c  Water
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
8 c  (about 3 pounds) peeled and
- thinly sliced Kirby
- cucumbers
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a medium-sized bowl, combine the vinegar,
water, sugar, and salt until well blended. Add the cucumbers and toss
until well coated. Cover and chill for at least 2 hours, or
overnight. This will keep in a sealed container in the refrigerator
for several NOTE: If you'd like, you can add a couple tablespoons of
chopped fresh dill to give these even more fresh flavor.
