Title: MR. FOOD'S SHORTCUT A LA KING
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Regular or low-fat
- sour cream
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
16 oz Frozen mixed vegetables
1/2 lb Deli chicken or turkey
- breast, cubed
10 1/2 oz Condensed cream of
- chicken soup
3    English muffins, split
- in half
