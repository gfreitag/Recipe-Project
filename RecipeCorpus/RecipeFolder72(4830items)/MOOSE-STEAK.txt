Title: MOOSE STEAK
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Onions, chopped fine
2 tb Butter
2 lb Moose Steak
1 c  Chopped Mushrooms
1/2 c  Sweet or Sour Cream
2 tb Flour
Fry onion, brown in butter. Sear steak on both sides in butter with
onion. Cover and let simmer for 1/2 hour. When meat is almost tender,
add mushrooms and the flour stirred into the cream. Cover and let
simmer
20    minutes.
