Title: MINTED MELON
Categories: Fruits, Harned 1994, Herb/spice, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Orange-fleshed melon; seeded
-- flesh in lg. cubes
1 ts Grated orange rind
1/4 c  Orange juice
2 tb Sugar
1/3 c  Raspberry vinegar
1/4 c  Fresh mint; finely chopped
Put melon cubes in serving bowl.  Combine other ingredients; mix well
to dissolve sugar.  Pour over melon. Chill, covered, for an hour to
let flavors blend.

From 1991 "Shepherd's Garden Seeds" catalog.  Pg. 40. Electronic
format by Cathy Harned.
