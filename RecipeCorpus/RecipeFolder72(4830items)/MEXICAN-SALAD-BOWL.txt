Title: MEXICAN SALAD BOWL
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
8 c  Mixed Greens; Bite-Size
1/4 c  Green Onion/Tops; Chopped
1/4 c  Green Pepper; Chopped
1/4 c  Ripe Olives; Sliced, Pitted
2 lg Tomatoes; Cut Into Wedges
MMMMM-------------------AVOCADO SALAD DRESSING------------------------
1 lg Avocado, Pitted
2 tb Lemon Juice
1/2 c  Dairy Sour Cream
1/4 c  Salad Oil
1 ts Seasoned Salt
1/4 ts Chili Powder
MMMMM-------------------------GARNISHES------------------------------
1/4 c  Cheddar Cheese; Shredded
1 c  Corn Chips; Coarsely Crushed
Combine the greens, green onions, green pepper, olives and tomatoes
in a salad bowl.  Cover and refrigerate. Prepare the Avocado Salad
Dressing.

TO SERVE:  Sprinkle the salad ingredients with the cheese, the the
corn chips.  Top with the Avocado Salad Dressing and toss lightly to
coat.

AVOCADO SALAD DRESSING:

Mash the avocado and lemon juice together.  Add the rest of the
ingredients, blending well, and cover. Refrigerate for several hours
to blend the flavors before serving.
