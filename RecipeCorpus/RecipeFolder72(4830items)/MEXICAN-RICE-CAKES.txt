Title: MEXICAN RICE CAKES
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Rice cakes
1/4 c  Picante sauce
1/4 c  Refried beans
1/3 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4 c  Sliced jalapeno peppers
Place rice cakes on baking sheet.  Spread refried beans evenly on
each rice cake; top with picante sauce, cheese, then with peppers.
Bake at 400 degrees
10    minutes.  Serve immediately.

Microwave Oven Instructions: ============================ Prepare rice
cakes as directed on microproof baking sheet. Cook uncovered on HIGH
(maximum power) 1-1/2 minutes; rotate after 1 minute. Serve
immediately.

Each serving provides: * 87 calories * 3.7 grams protein * 3.4 grams
fat * 9.4 grams carbohydrate * 310 milligrams sodium * 10 milligrams
cholesterol
