Title: MARCEPAN - MARZIPAN
Categories: Candies
Yield: 2 Pounds
1 lb Almonds, blanched
- finely ground
1 lb Sugar, powdered
2 tb Orange water or rose water
-(get at drug store)
Food coloring
Decorations
1. Combine ground almonds, sugar and flavouring in a saucepan. Cook
until mixture leaves side of pan. 2. Roll almond mixture on a flat
surface to a 1/2" thickness. Cut into shapes, mold, paint with food
colouring, decorate, etc. 3. Place on wax paper to dry for 2 hours.
