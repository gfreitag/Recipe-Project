Title: NO FAT BANANA MUFFINS
Categories: Low fat, Breads, Low cal, Fruits, Muffins
Yield: 12 Servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts -salt -- optional
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2    Egg whites
2 lg Ripe bananas -- mashed
2/3 c  Skim milk
1/3 c  Corn Syrup
Spray 12 (2 1/2-inch) muffin cups with cooking spray. In medium bowl
combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon.
In large bowl, using a fork or wire whisk, beat egg whites lightly.
Stir in mashed bananas, milk and corn syrup.  Add flour mixture; stir
until well blended.  Spoon into prepared muffin cups. Bake in 400
deg. oven 22 to 25 minutes or until firm to touch. Cool in pan 5
minutes. Remove: cool on wire rack.  Makes 12 muffins.  Per serving:
0g fat, 0 mg cholesterol, 130 calories, 200 mg sodium (less if you
omit salt)
