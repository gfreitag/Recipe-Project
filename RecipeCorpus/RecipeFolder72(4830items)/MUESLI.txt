Title: MUESLI
Categories: Breakfast, Nuts, Fruits
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 lb Rolled oats
1 lb Barley flakes
4 oz Mixed nuts, chopped
4 oz Mied dried fruit, chopped
Put the oats into a bowl & stir in the remaining ingredients until
evenly mixed.  Store in an airtight container. Serve with soymilk &
sweeten with maple syrup or brown sugar if desired. Also serve
garnished with sl;ices of fresh fruit in season.
