Title: MINCEMEAT ( U.K.)
Categories: Fruits, Preserves
Yield: 12 Servings   125g		suet shredded ( or melted margarine) 125g		Apple grated
500g		Mixed dried fruit 125g		demerara sugar 125g		Mixed peel
125g		almonds blanched and well dried 1		Rind & juice lemon
1tsp		Mixed spice 0.5tsp	cinnamon 0.5tsp	grated nutmeg 4tbs		Brandy
or whisky or Rum Mix all the ingredients thoroughly pot in dry jars
and seal. Keep in a cold dry place. This mincemeat also improves with
age.
