Title: MOCHI CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 24 Servings
1 lb Mochiko
2 1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Butter or margarine; melted
3 c  Island Fresh milk
5    Island Fresh eggs; beaten
2 ts Lemon extract
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Shredded coconut
Preheat electric oven to 350  F.  Grease a 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan. In a
large bowl, combine mochiko, sugar, and baking powder. Add remaining
ingredients; mix well. Pour into prepared pan. Bake for 1 hour; cool.
Makes 24 servings.
