Title: MY CEASAR SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Garlic clove
6 tb Oil
2 tb Wine vinegar
1 tb Dijon mustard
1    Egg yolk
2 dr Worcestershire
2 dr Tobasco sauce
Salt
Pepper
Peel and bruise garlic clove.  Rub a wooden bowl with garlic. Mince
garlic finely.  Beat oil and vinegar. Add mustard and beat well. Add
tobasco and worcestershire sauces and beat well.  Mix greens and
veggies of your choice; lettuce, onions, mushrooms, croutons, etc.
Pour dressing over salad just before serving and toss.
