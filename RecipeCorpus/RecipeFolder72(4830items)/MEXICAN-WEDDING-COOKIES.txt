Title: MEXICAN WEDDING COOKIES
Categories: Mexican, Cookies
Yield: 36 Servings
1 c  Butter, softened
1 c  Powdered sugar
2 c  Sifted flour
1 c  Ground nuts
1 ts Vanilla
Combine all ingredients. Form into 1 1/2" balls. Bake on cookie sheet
at 350 for about 10-15 minutes or until set. Roll in powdered sugar
while still warm.
