Title: MRS. WHITE'S DOUBLE BAKED POTATO
Categories: Stuft spuds
Yield: 1 Servings
1 md Potato
1/2 ts Onion, minced
1/4 ts Horseradish
1 tb Milk
1/8 ts Pepper
Paprika & parsley
Bake potato in 400 degree oven for 1 hour. With sharp knife, cut area
off top of potato large enough to scoop out the inside of the skin.
Set shell aside. Mash potato with onion, horseradish, milk and
pepper. Return to shell and sprinkle with paprika and parsley. When
ready to serve, return to oven and heat thoroughly. ABOUT THE SAME
CALORIE CONTENT AS AN APPLE!
