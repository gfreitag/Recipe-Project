Title: OLD ENGLISH COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ea Eggs
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Coffee; cold
3 c  Flour
2 c  Raisins
1 c  Lard
1/2 c  Nuts
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cloves
Mix well, drop from spoon on cookie sheet. Bake in medium hot oven.
Note: Assume a 425 F. oven. Source: Birds Run Grange, Gurnsey County,
OH
