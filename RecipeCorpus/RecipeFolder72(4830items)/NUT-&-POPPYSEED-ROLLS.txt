Title: NUT & POPPYSEED ROLLS
Categories: Kosher, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Butter
1 c  Lukewarm milk
1    Cake of yeast
2 tb Sugar
4    Egg yolks
4 c  Flour
Dissolve yeast in milk.  Sift dry ingredients; cut butter into
mixture as you would for pie dough.  Toss in liquids. Knead until
smooth. Let rise about 2 hrs. Divide into 4 pcs. Roll each pc. very
thin. Spread with filling and roll as for a jelly roll. Bake on a
lightly greased sheet at 350 F. until brown, abt. 30 to 45 min. Slice
into pieces of desired size.

Filling recipes to follow: Nos. 165, 166, 167
