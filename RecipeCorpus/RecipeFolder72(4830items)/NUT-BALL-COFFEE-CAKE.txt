Title: NUT BALL COFFEE CAKE
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Bake it all mix
3 tb Granulated sugar
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/3 c  Milk
1    Egg
2 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
1/3 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/3 c  Nuts -- finely chopped
Mix first 6 ingredients together, beat until well mixed Dip a
spoonful of the dough first into melted butter, then into brown sugar
and 1 teaspoon cinnamon mixed together, then into nuts. Place balls
close together in an 8-inch pie pan or individually in muffin cups.
Bake in a 375 F. oven for 18 to 20 minutes. Yield: 12 Nut Balls
