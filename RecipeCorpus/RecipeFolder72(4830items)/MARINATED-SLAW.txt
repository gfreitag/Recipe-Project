Title: MARINATED SLAW
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
8 c  Shredded cabbage 1 1/2 to
2 lbs
2 tb Chopped pimientos
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
3/4 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Water
1 tb Mustard seed
In a large bowl, combine the cabbage, pimientos, green pepper and
onion. Toss lightly; set aside. Combine remaining ingredients in a
medium saucepan; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered for
20-25 minutes or until slightly thickened.  Pour over cabbage
mixture. cover and regrigerate 4 hours or overnight. Slaw will keep
in the refrigerator for several days.
