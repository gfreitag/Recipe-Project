Title: MOLDED CRUNCH SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 12 Servings
1 lb CAN GRAPEFRUIT SECTIONS
3 oz LEMON GELATIN
1 c  BUTTERMILK
1/2 c  WALNUTS
1/2 c  CELERY - CHOPPED FINE
11 oz MANDARIN ORANGES
DRAIN GRAPEFRUIT SECTIONS, SAVING THE JUICE. ADD WATER TO THE JUICE
TO MAKE 1 CUP OF LIQUID. HEAT TO THE BOILING POINT, ADD GELATIN AND
DISSOLVE THOROUGHLY. COOL. STIR IN BUTTERMILK, AND FOLD IN THE
DRAINED FRUIT. FOLD IN THE ORANGES, CELERY AND NUTS. SPOON INTO A
RING MOLD AND CHILL.
