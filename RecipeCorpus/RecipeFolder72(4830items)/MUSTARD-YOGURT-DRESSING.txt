Title: MUSTARD YOGURT DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces, Dressings
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Plain, low-fat yogurt
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Paprika
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
Combine seasonings with 1/4 cup of the yogurt; mix thoroughly. Add to
remaining yogurt.  Mix well, turn into a small jar, cover, and chill
several hours before use. Values per serving: CHO 2 gm., PRO 1 gm.,
FAT 0, Calories 16, Sodium 148 mg. with salt. Food Exchange per
serving: Up to 2 TBSP may be considered "free." (Right, even with the
sugar.)
