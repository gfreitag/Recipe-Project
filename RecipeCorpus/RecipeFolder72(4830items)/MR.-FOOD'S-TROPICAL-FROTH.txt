Title: MR. FOOD'S TROPICAL FROTH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 Servings
6 oz Frozen limeade concentrate,
- thawed
6 oz Pineapple juice
1    Litre ginger ale
2 c  Rainbow sherbet
8    Ice cubes
2    Lemons, sliced
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a large punch bowl, combine the limeade
concentrate, pineapple juice, and ginger ale. Add the sherbet and ice
cubes just before serving. Garnish with the lemon slices. NOTE: If
you don't have rainbow sherbet on hand, try substituting vanilla ice
cream.
