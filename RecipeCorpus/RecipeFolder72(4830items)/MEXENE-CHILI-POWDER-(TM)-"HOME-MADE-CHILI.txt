Title: MEXENE CHILI POWDER (TM) "HOME-MADE CHILI
Categories: Chili, Ground beef, Mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Onion
1    Garlic clove, minced
2 tb Mexene Chili Powder
1 tb Flour
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Sugar
1 cn Tomato sauce (8 oz.)
1 c  Water
Brown the ground beef, onion, and garlic.  Drain excess fat. Stir in
remaining ingredients.  Mix well.  Cover and simmer 30 minutes.

From the label on Mexene brand Chili Powder
