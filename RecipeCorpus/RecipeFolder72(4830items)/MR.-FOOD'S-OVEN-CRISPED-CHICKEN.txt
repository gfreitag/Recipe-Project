Title: MR. FOOD'S OVEN-CRISPED CHICKEN
Categories: Meats, Chicken
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ea Chicken (2 1/2 to 3 pounds),
- cut into eighths and skin
- removed
2 ts Salt
1 ts Black pepper
2/3 c  All-purpose flour
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Milk
6 c  Toasted rice cereal,
- coarsely crushed
1 ts Garlic powder
=======================================================

chicken with the salt and pepper. Place the flour in a shallow dish.
