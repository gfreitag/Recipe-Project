Title: MY CHRISTMAS PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Peagram, Puddings
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Grated carrot
2 c  Grated potato
2 1/2 c  Soft bread crumbs
2 c  Raisins
2 c  Currants
2 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Suet OR
1 c  Margarine
4 tb Sour milk
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Flour
2 ts Salt
2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Allspice
Mix in order given. Turn into well greased heat proof bowl; cover and
steam about 3 hours. Takes a medium sized bowl. Serve with brown
sugar sauce, or custard, or whatever you like.
