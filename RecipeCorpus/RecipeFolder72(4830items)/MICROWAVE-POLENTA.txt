Title: MICROWAVE POLENTA
Categories: Italian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Cornmeal
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Olive oil
3 1/2    Q water
Mix all ingredients in a 3-quart microwave-safe dish. Cover and cook
on high power for 5 minutes.  Stir and cook on high power until
thickened, about 5 minutes longer.

Makes 4 servings.
