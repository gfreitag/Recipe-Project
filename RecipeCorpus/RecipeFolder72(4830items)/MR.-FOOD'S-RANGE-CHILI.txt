Title: MR. FOOD'S RANGE CHILI
Categories: Casseroles, Chili
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 lb Kielbasa sausage,
- casing removed
2 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion, chopped
1/4 ts Minced garlic
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 tb Red wine vinegar
29 oz Diced tomatoes
32 oz Pinto beans, drained
2 tb Chili seasoning mix
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a soup pot, combine the sausage, ground
beef, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper and cook over medium-high heat
for 7 to 9 minutes, or until no pink remains in the meat, stirring
frequently. Add the vinegar, tomatoes, beans, and seasoning mix to
the meat NOTE: Use whatever toppers you like or try some of these:
shredded cheese or chopped onions and peppers, sour cream, tortilla
chips or bread.
