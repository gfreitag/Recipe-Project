Title: NUOC CHAM (VIETNAMESE MULTI-PURPOSE SAUCE)
Categories: Sauce/gravy, Vietnam, Update
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ea Cloves garlic
1 ea Fresh hot chili pepper
-finely chopped
4 tb White sugar
2 tb Fish sauce
5 tb Water
1 tb Lemon juice
Combine ingredients in a clean jar. Mix until sugar is dissolved. This
sauce will keep in the refrigerator for about a month or so. Good
sprinkled as a condiment on fish, chicken, pork or as a dipping sauce
for lumpia, eggrolls, wonton, etc. I've also used this as a dressing
for a basil, mint and beansprout salad. Nuoc Cham (Vietnamese
"multi-purpose" sauce)
