Title: OAT BREAD
Categories: Oats, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
-1 large loaf
1 1/3 c  Water
2 1/2 tb Applesauce
1/4 c  Sugar (I use fructose)

1 ts Salt
2/3 c  Oat bran

1/4 c  Nonfat dry milk
1/3 c  Shreaded carrots

1/3 c  Sunflower seeds
