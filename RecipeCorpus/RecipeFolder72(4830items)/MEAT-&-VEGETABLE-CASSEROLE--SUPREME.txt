Title: MEAT & VEGETABLE CASSEROLE  SUPREME
Categories: Beef, Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
3    Medium baking potatoes;
-peeled and sliced
3    Medium onions; peeled,
-sliced
Salt and pepper to taste
1    Stewed tomatoes
Preliminary: Preheat oven to 350.

1. Fry beef in a large skillet, breaking up with a spatula, until no
pink color remains.  Drain.

2. Arrange a layer of potatoes, a layer of meat and a layer of onions
in a 2-1/2 quart casserole dish.  Season with salt and pepper; repeat
layering until all ingredients are used (except the tomatoes).

3. Top with tomatoes.  Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour.
