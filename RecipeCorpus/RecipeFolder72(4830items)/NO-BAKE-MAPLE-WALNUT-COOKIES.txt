Title: NO-BAKE MAPLE WALNUT COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 2 Dozen
1/2 c  Butter
2 c  Sugar, granulated
1 c  Sugar, brown
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Marshmallow creme
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Black walnuts
1 ts Maple flavoring
3 c  Rolled oats
Mix the butter, sugars and milk; boil two minutes. Add the remaining
ingredients; drop by spoonfuls onto waxed paper. Let stand to harden.
