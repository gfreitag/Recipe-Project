Title: OATMEAL  FILLED  COOKIES
Categories: Cookie
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Melted shortening
2 c  Quick cooking oatmeal
2 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Soda
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
3/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 tb Butter
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Chopped pineapple
2 tb Flour
1 ts Vanilla
Blend shortening with brown sugar.  Sift flour and soda together and
mix with oatmeal.  Blend the dry mixture and vanilla into brown sugar
and shortening mixtures, working into dough with hands. Press half
this mixture on bottom of well greased pan.  Add filling and then
press other half on top.    Cool until thick, add vanilla when partly
cooked. Bake at
375    degrees for 25 minutes.
