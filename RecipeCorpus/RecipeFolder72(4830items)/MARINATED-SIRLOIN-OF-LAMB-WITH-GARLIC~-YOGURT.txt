Title: MARINATED SIRLOIN OF LAMB WITH GARLIC~ YOGURT
Categories: Lamb
2    sirloins of lamb
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz plain yogurt
4    cloves garlic
1/2 c  Poblano peppers -- chopped
1/4 c  fresh mint -- chopped
mint sprigs
:

Mix the yogurt, cloves, peppers and mint. Spread this mixture all
over the lamb in a mixing bowl and let sit in refrigerator overnight.
Grill over high heat 5 minutes on first side and 4 minutes on reverse
side. Remove from heat and let sit 5 minutes. Slice and serve with
fresh mint sprigs.
