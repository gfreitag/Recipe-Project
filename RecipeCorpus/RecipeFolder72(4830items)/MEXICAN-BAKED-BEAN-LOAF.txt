Title: MEXICAN BAKED BEAN LOAF
Categories: Beans, Mexican
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Baked beans
1    Green pepper -- chopped
3    Onion -- chopped
2 tb Cooking oil
4 dr Tabasco sauce
1 c  Tomato sauce
1 c  Bread crumbs
Saute the pepper and onions in oil until lightly browned. Stir in
Tabasco. Add beans and tomato sauce. Cook 5 minutes. Turn into a
greased baking dish.  Top with crumbs. Dot with butter. Bake at 350F
until crumbs are brown, about 15 minutes.  Add more tomato sauce if
necessary.
