Title: MINCEMEAT SQUARES
Categories: Desserts, Bars
Yield: 16 Bars
1 c  Rolled oats
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
1 1/4 c  Mincemeat
1 tb Rum
Combine rolled oats, flour, baking soda, salt and sugar. Cut in
shortening until mixture is crumbly. Firmly press half of this
mixture into an 8 inch square pan. Spread evenly with mincemeat,
flavoured with rum. Top with remaining crumb mixture, packing down
firmly with fingers. Bake at 350 F for 25 to 30 minutes.
