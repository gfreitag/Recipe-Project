Title: MEXICAN PITA CHIPS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Lite margarine softened
1    Crushed garlic clove
1 tb Dried oregano
1 tb Chopped parsley
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
4 lg Pita breads
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. In small bowl, combine first 6
ingredients. Cut each pit bread in half; spread each half with
margarine mixture, then cut each into quarters. Place on baking
sheet; bake 3-5 minutes until browned and crisp.
