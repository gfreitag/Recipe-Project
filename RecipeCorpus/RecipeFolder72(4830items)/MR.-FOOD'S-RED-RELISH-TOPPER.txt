Title: MR. FOOD'S RED RELISH TOPPER
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 99 Servings
About 5 cups
24 oz Chili sauce
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Chopped garlic
1 md Green bell pepper, chopped
1/4 c  Pickle relish
