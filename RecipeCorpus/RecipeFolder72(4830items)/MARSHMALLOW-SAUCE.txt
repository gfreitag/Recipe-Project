Title: MARSHMALLOW SAUCE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
8 oz Marshmallows
2 tb Water
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Milk
1 tb Light corn syrup
1 ts Vanilla extract
In top of double boiler, melt marshmallows with water. Leave in double
boiler and set aside.

In a heavy saucepan, combine sugar, milk, and corn syrup. Bring to a
boil, reduce heat, and simmer 5 minutes. Pour this liquid over
marshmallows and, with a hand-held mixer, whisk, or egg beater, beat
until smooth. Sauce will thicken as it cooks.  Remove from heat. Add
vanilla. Serve at room temperature.  Makes enough for 4 to 6 sundaes.

From:  "Square Meals Cookbook", Courtesy Debbie Carlson - Cooking Echo
