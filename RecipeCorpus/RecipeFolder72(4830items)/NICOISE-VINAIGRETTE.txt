Title: NICOISE VINAIGRETTE
Categories: June 1996 i, Marinades, Sauces, Salsas, Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
1 tb Red wine vinegar
2    Cloves garlic -- coarsely
Chopped
1/2 c  Watercress leaves and some
Stems
3 tb Fresh lemon juice
2    Whole cherry tomatoes --
Quartered
1    Whole scallion -- cut in
1/2    " lengths
4    Whole Nicoise olives --
Pitted
3    Whole anchovy fillets --
Rinsed and dried
1 ts Capers
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1/2 c  Extra virgin olive oil
Combine all ingredients except oil in a blender; finely chop. With
blender running, slowly add oil until completely incorporated.
