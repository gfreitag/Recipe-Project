Title: MEXICALI MACARONI
Categories: Main dish, Crockpot, Meats, Microwave
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 tb Minced Onions
1 cn Tomatoes (16 oz)
1 cn Green beans (drained)
3/4 c  Macaroni
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Cumin
1 ds Pepper
For Microwave Brown beef with onion & drain Cook macaroni first, then
add remaining ingred and cook on high for 7-10 minutes.

For Crockpot Brown beef with onion & drain combine remaining ingred
and cook on low for 3-4 hours.

For Stovetop Brown beef with onion & drain combine remaining ingrd and
simmer until macaroni is tender.
