Title: NO-BAKE COOKIES (BY MARY BAKER)
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Plus peanut butter
2 c  Plus oatmeal
plus means a hefty, overflowed cup) Cook sugar, milk, cocoa, and salt
in saucepan. Bring to boil and boil for 1 min. Remove from heat, mix
in vanilla, mix in peanut butter, and mix in oats. Pour in buttered
pan and let harden.
