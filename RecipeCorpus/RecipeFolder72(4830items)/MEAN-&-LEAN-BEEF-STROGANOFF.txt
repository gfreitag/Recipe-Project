Title: MEAN & LEAN BEEF STROGANOFF
Categories: Beef
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Beef stew meat; cut into
-1" cubes
1 md Onion; sliced
1 sm Garlic clove; chopped fine
1/4 c  Shortening
1 1/2 c  Water
3/4 c  Ketchup
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Sugar, brown
2 ts Salt
2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Mustard, dry
1/4 c  Water, cold
2 tb Flour
Cook and stir beef, onion, and garlic in shortening until beef is
brown; drain.  Stir in 1 1/2 cups water, ketchup, Worcestershire,
brown sugar, salt, paprika and mustard. Heat to boiling; reduce heat.
Cover and simmer 2 to 2 1/2 hours until beef is tender.

Shake 1/4 cup water and flour; stir gradually into beef mixture. Heat
to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir one minute until
thickened.
