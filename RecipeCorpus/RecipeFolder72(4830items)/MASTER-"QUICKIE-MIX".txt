Title: MASTER "QUICKIE MIX"
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 14 Servings
7 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
3 c  Instant non-fat dry milk
Powder
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Wheat germ
5 tb Baking powder
1/2 tb Salt
1 1/2 c  Vegetable oil
Combine flour, dry milk powder, wheat germ, sugar, baking powder and
salt in a large bowl.  Make well in center; pour vegetable oil. Using
a pastry blender, 2 knives or clean fingers, mix in oil with dry
ingredients. Mixture will be crumbly.   Store in tightly covered
plastic or glass container with wide top. Keep in refrigerator up to
4 weeks or in freezer for 3 months.  Makes about 14 cups mix. Note:
A nutritious mix you can use to make biscuits, muffins, pancakes,
cakes, coffee cakes and quick breads. See variations with a "Quickie
Mix" title for other recipes. Source: Family Circle/September 1980.
