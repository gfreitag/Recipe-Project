Title: NORA MILL BUCKWHEAT BUTTERMILK PANCAKES
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 c  NORA MILL buckwheat flour
3 tb NORA MILL whole wheat flour
1 ts Dry yeast
1 c  Lukewarm water
1 c  Buttermilk
1/2 ts Salt
Dissolve dry yeast into lukewarm water.  Add buttermilk, both flours,
and salt, then mix well.  Let set overnight. Bake the next day on a
hot griddle.
