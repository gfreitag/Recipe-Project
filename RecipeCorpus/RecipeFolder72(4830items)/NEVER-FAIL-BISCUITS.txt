Title: NEVER FAIL BISCUITS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
5 tb Shortening
3/4 c  Milk
2 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
Sift dry ingredients, Cut in shorteneing very finely. Add milk. Toss
on a floured board. Knead lightly, roll, cut and put on greased
pan.Bake in 400 F. oven for 20 minutes.
