Title: OAT BREAD VARIATION
Categories: Breadmaker, Low fat
Yield: 14 Servings
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 c  Oat flour
1 pk Active dry yeast
1 ts Sugar ; brown, white or
; honey
1 tb Butter
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/4 c  Warm water
1/2 c  Mashed potato flakes ; insta
Recipe by: m2xenix!uunet!telesoft!kami (Kami Olsson-Tapp @day) 1/2c
instant mashed potatoes added to white or oat bread make it
moister and make the little bit that is left better the next day.
I put the flakes in below the flour if I am using the timer.
