Title: MEXICAN CHICKEN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Mexican, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Cooked diced chicken
1/4 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Finely chopped carrot
2 tb Cilantro
2 tb Capers (drained)
2 tb Red Bell Pepper -
-finely chopped
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1/4 c  Tabasco Brand Jalapeno Sauce
1    Avocado, peeled and wedged
Lettuce leaves
Paprika
Toss all ingredients except avocado, lettuce and paprika. When well
mixed refrigerate, 30 minutes. Serve on lettuce leaves with avocado
wedges. Sprinkle with paprika.
