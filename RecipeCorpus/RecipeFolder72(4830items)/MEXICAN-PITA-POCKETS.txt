Title: MEXICAN PITA POCKETS
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
16 oz Van Camp's Pork and Beans
-- undrained
2 c  Shredded lettuce
1 c  Chopped tomato
4 oz Shredded cheddar cheese
Plain yogurt
Brown ground beef with onion; drain.  Add beans and salsa, heat
through. Spoon 1/2 cup mixture into each pita pocket half. Top with
lettuce, tomato, cheese, yogurt and additional salsa, if desired.

Nutritional Analysis per serving: * calories 329 * carbohydrates 33 g
* protein 20 g * fat 13 g * calcium 198 mg * sodium 610 mg *
cholesterol 45 mg * dietary fiber 11 g
