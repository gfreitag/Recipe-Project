Title: MOLLY'S STUFFED FRENCH BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 20 Servings
1/2 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
-or miracle whip light
1 pk Liver sausage
1/3 c  Finely chopped walnuts
1 tb Each chopped - onion,
-parsley
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1    Baguette loaf
Combine all ingredients except for bread.  Blend well. Slice off ends
of bread.  Cut into four.  Scoop out bread leaving 1/2 inch shell.
Tear removed bread into small pieces. Blend into filling. Fill each
shell with 1/3 cup mixture.  Wrap securely in plastic wrap;chill at
least 1 hour. Cut into 1/2 inch slices to serve. Makes 20 slices.

Origin:  June Reader's Digest (Canadian) Shared by: Sharon Stevens.
