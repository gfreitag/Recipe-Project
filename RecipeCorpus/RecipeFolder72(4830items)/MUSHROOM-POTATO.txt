Title: MUSHROOM POTATO
Categories: Potato/rice, Vegetables, Carnation
Yield: 1 Servings
10 oz Potato; baked, split and
- fluffed
1 1/2 oz Mushrooms; sliced, sauteed
1 1/2 oz Chef-Mate Sharp Cheddar
- Cheese Sauce; heated
1/2 ts Parsley; chopped
1.  In split, fluffed, baked potato, place mushrooms. 2. Ladle Sharp
Cheddar Cheese Sauce over potato. 3. Garnish with parsley and serve.

Makes 1 stuffed potato.

Source: Carnation Company
