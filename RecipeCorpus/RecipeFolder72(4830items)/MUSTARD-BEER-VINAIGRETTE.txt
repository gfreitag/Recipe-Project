Title: MUSTARD-BEER VINAIGRETTE
Categories: New text im, Cooking rig
5 TB olive oil
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  onions -- finely chopped
2 ts garlic -- chopped
1 c  ale
3 TB white wine vinegar
2 ts honey
1 1/2 TB whole grain mustard
Salt and freshly ground
pepper
:
:

Heat 3 tablespoons olive oil in a small saute pan and saute onions and
garlic until very soft but not brown. Add the ale, vinegar and honey
and simmer for 4-5 minutes or until mixture is lightly reduced. Cool
slightly and place mixture in a blender with the mustard. With motor
running add remaining 2 tablespoons olive oil and reserved
tablespoons bacon fat if using. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Yield: 6-8 servings
