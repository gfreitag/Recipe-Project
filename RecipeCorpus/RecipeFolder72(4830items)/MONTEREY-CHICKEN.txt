Title: MONTEREY CHICKEN
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Banana,mashed
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Salad oil
2 tb Orange juice concentrate
1/4 ts Ginger
1    Chicken,broiler-fryer,cut up
1. Stir together banana, orange juice concentrate, soy sauce, ginger
and oil in a shallow baking dish.
2. Dry chicken with paper towel; dip in banana mixture.
3. Arrange chicken, skin-side up, in a single layer in an oblong glass
3-quart 13x9x2-inch baking dish; spoon leftover banana mixture in top
of chicken.
4. Bake in preheated 350'F. oven until tender and browned, 50 to 60
minutes.
