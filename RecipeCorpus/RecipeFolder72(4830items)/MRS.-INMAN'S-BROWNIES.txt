Title: MRS. INMAN'S BROWNIES
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate
Yield: 1 Recipe
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
3    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Plain flour
2    Sqares unsweetened chocolate
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Pecans, chopped
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cream butter or oleo together with
sugar, add well beaten eggs, then sifted flour, baking powder, nuts,
vanilla and chocolate. (If squares are not available, use 7
tablespoons cocoa). Pour into well greased 9x13-inch pan.  Bake at
350 degrees for 25 minutes and cut into squares.
