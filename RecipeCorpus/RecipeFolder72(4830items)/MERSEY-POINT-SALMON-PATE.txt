Title: MERSEY POINT SALMON PATE
Categories: Seafood, Meats, Canadian
Yield: 1 Batch
1/4 lb Smoked salmon
1/2 lb Smoked mackerel fillets
8 oz Light cream cheese
2 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Horseradish
Fresh ground pepper
Remove skin and bones from smoked mackerel. Puree all ingredients in
a food processor. Package for refrigerator.
