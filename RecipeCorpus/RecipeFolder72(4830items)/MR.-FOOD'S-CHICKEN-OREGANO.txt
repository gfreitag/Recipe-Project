Title: MR. FOOD'S CHICKEN OREGANO
Categories: Meats, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ea Whole chicken; 3 to 3 1/2
- lbs cut into 8 pieces
1 ea Juice of 2 lemons
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter, melted
1/4 c  Water
1 ts Dried oregano
=======================================================
============== ==== == Place the chicken in a 9 X 13 baking dish that
has been coated with nonstick cooking spray. Pour the lemon juice
over the chicken and sprinkle with the salt and pepper. Cover tightly
and chill for 4 to 6 hours, or overnight. Preheat the oven to 350F.
Drizzle the chicken with the melted butter and add the water to the
bottom of the baking dish. Sprinkle with the oregano. Cover tightly
with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes; uncover and bake for 25
to 30 more minutes, or until no pink remains and the juices run clear.
