Title: MR. FOOD'S SOUTHWESTERN STUFFED CHICKEN
Categories: Meats, Chicken
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Crushed cheese crackers
2 tb Dry taco seasoning mix
6    Boneless, skinless chicken
- breast halves (1½ to 2
- pounds total)
4 oz Mexican-flavored processed
- cheese spread, cut into 6
- cubes 1/3 cup butter,
- melted
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 350°F. In a shallow bowl,
combine the cracker crumbs and taco seasoning mix; set aside. With a
kitchen mallet or rolling pin, gently pound the chicken to ¼-inch
thickness between 2 pieces of waxed paper. Place a cube of cheese in
the c NOTE: Any type of cheese will work well as a filling,
especially Monterey Jack-pepper cheese if you want that extra touch
of spiciness.
