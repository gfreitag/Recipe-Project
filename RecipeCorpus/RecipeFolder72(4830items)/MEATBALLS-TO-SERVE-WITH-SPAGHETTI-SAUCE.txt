Title: MEATBALLS TO SERVE WITH SPAGHETTI SAUCE
Categories: Meats, Italian
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 lb Ground beef
1/4 lb Ground pork
1 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
1/2 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1    Sprig parsley chopped or
1 Tbls parsley flakes
1 cl Garlic, minced
1/2 c  Milk
2    Well-beaten eggs
Salt and pepper
Combine meats, crumbs, cheese, parsley, garlic, milk, eggs, and
seasonings; mix throughly. Form into small balls; brown in hot fat.
Add to sauce and cook over low heat 15 minutes. Serve over spaghetti.
