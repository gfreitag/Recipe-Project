Title: MUSTARD POTATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Eggs, Potatoes

Yield: 6 Servings

1    Recipe Old-Fashioned Egg --
Salad (see previous
Recipe)

1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Dried minced onion
1/4 ts Salt
Lettuce leaves
Sliced radishes -- optional
:
:

In a saucepan, cover potatoes with water and cook until tender but
firm. Drain and cool.  In a bowl, combine egg salad, mayonnaise,
mustard, onion and salt. Stir in cooled potatoes. Cover and chill.
Serve on lettuce leaves; garnish with radishes, if desired.
