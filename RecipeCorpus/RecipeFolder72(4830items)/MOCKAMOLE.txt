Title: MOCKAMOLE
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetarian
Yield: 2 Cups
1    Ripe avocado
1    Block ( 10 0z ) silken tofu
5 tb Fresh lemon juice
2    Cloves garlic
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Fresh chopped parsley
Place skinned avocado in food processor with remaining ingredients and
process smooth, Good as a stuffing in pita or a side dish with spicy
foods.When processing may need to add a tb of water for smooth
consisitency.
