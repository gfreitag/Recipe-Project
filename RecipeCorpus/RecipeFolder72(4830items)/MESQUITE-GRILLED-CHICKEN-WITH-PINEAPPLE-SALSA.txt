Title: MESQUITE GRILLED CHICKEN WITH PINEAPPLE SALSA
Categories: Poultry, Bar-b-q, Salsa, Marinades
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Chicken Breast, skinless
- and boneless
1 pk Chicken Mesquite Marinade
- McCormick/Schilling
MMMMM----------------------PINEAPPLE SALSA---------------------------
1 cn Pineapple, DOLE
-crushed and drained
1/4 c  Red bell pepper, chopped
1 ea Green onion, sliced
1 tb Lime juice, fresh
1 tb Soy Sauce, Chun King Lite
1/4 ts Hot Shot Black & Red Pepper
-McCormick/Schilling blend
- up to 1/2 ts, as desired
Prepare 1 pkg (1.37 oz) Chicken Mesquite Marinade according to
package directions, using 1 lb, boneless, skinless chicken breast.
Grill Chicken. Serve with Pineapple Salsa.
PINEAPPLE SALSA: Combine pineapple, red bell pepper, green onion,
lime juice, soy sauce and black & red pepper blend.
