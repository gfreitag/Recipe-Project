Title: MORI-NU "LITE" EGGLESS EGG SALAD
Categories: Vegetarian, Salads, Sandwiches
Yield: 3 Servings
1 pk Mori-Nu Lite Tofu, firm
-(10.5 oz); drained
1 ts Each: apple cider vinegar
-and honey or other natural
-sweetener
2 ts Mustard
1/2 ts Turmeric
2 tb Each: diced celery, onion
1 ts Parsley; chopped
ds Paprika
ds Pepper
Crumble tofu into a small bowl. In a separate bowl, combine vinegar,
mustard, honey and turmeric. Mix thoroughly and pour over crumbled
tofu. Add celery, onion, parsley, paprika and pepper. Mix
thoroughly.Refrigerate approximately 30 minutesto allow flavors to
meld.

Makes three 1/2 cup servings.

Source: Mori-Nu advertisement, Vegetarian Times, Jan 94/MM by DEEANNE
