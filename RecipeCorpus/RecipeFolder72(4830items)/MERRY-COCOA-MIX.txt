Title: MERRY COCOA MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 3 Servings
Makes: 3 1/4 cups mix-----
2 1/2 c  Instant nonfat dry milk
3/4 c  Powdered chocolate flavor --
Drink mix
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Combine and store in an --
Airtight container.
To make: Place 3-4 heaping Tablespoons in a mug and fill with boiling
water. Carolyn's note: The only nutrition information I noted was
that it has no fat. Source: Hand written in the "big pile" Carolyn
