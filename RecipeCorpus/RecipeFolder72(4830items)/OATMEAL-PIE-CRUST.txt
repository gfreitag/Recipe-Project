Title: OATMEAL PIE CRUST
Categories: Low-cal, Pies
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Quick rolled oats
1/2 ts Salt
4 tb Vegetable oil
3 tb Ice water (3 to 4 tb)
Combine flour, rolled oats and salt in mixing bowl. Slowly drizzle in
oil, mixing with a fork until mixture resembles fine crumbs. Add ice
water slowly until mixture begins to form a ball. Pat into 9 inch pie
plate. Bake according to filling recipe.

For baked pastry shell:  Prick pastry with fork in several places.
Bake at 400F 10 min until light golden brown.

1/6 pie crust without filling 1 starch choice, 2 fats & oils choices
15 g carbohydrate, 3 g protein, 10 g fat (162 cal)
