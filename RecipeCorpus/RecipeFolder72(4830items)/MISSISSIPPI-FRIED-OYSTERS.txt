Title: MISSISSIPPI FRIED OYSTERS
Categories: Seafood, Southern
Yield: 1 Servings
Raw oysters
2    Eggs -- beaten
2 c  Cornmeal
1 ts Sugar
2 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
2 tb Flour
Drain oysters and soak in eggs.  Mix cornmeal, sugar, salt, pepper and
flour. Dip oysters in cornmeal mixture and fry in deep shortening
until golden brown.  Drain on paper towels.
