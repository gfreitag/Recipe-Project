Title: NOT-REALLY-TAMARIND SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Fruits
1/4 c  (packed) brown sugar
3 tb fresh lemon juice
1/2 ts molasses
Yield: 1 Servings  :
:
:

Combine all ingredients in a very small saucepan over low heat,
stirring until the sugar melts.  Or, microwave it in a small
microwave safe container on full power for 30 seconds. Walt MM
