Title: MOLASSES GINGERBREAD MEN
Categories: Cookies, Breads
1/3 c Brown sugar 1 ts
1/3 c Dark molasses 1 ts Salt
Yield: 48 Servings    2 3/4 c  Sifted flour
Baking soda
1    Egg, well beaten 2 ts Cinnamon 1/3 c Melted shortening or
butter 1 1/4 ts Ginger, ground

Sift flour, soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger and sugar into bowl. Add
brown sugar, molasses, egg, and melted shortening. Stir to mix
thoroughly. Chill dough for at least one hour. Roll on floured board
to 1/4" thickness. Cut with floured cutters and bake on a greased
cookie sheet at 375 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes.
