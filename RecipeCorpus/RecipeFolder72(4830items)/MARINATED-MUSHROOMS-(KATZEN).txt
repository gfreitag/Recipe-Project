Title: MARINATED MUSHROOMS (KATZEN)
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Small mushrooms, cleaned
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Thyme
2 md Garlic cloves, chopped
Black pepper
2 tb Parsley
Slice mushroom stems, leave the caps whole.  Place in a pot with a
small amount of water & cook gently for 15 minutes. Reserve the stock
for soups. Combine the remaining ingredients. Place mushrooms in the
marinade & let sit for at least 2 hours, either in the fridge or at
room temperature, stirring ocassionally.

Adapted from Mollie Katzen, "Moosewood Cookbook"
