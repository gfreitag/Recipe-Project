Title: MARGARITA SUNRISE
Categories: Beverages, Fruits, Mexican
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ea Lime; Cut Into Halves
1 x  Granulated Sugar
3 c  Cracked Ice
1/4 c  Powdered Sugar
1 c  Tequila
6 oz Frozen Orange Juice; *
*  Use 1 6-ounce can of thawed frozen concentrate.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Rub rims of 4 stemmed glasses with 1 line half;
dip rims of glasses in granulated sugar. Squeeze juice from both lime
halves into blender container.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and
blend on high speed until foamy.
