Title: MR. FOOD'S LEMONY TARTAR SAUCE - 8/2/95
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
3 tb Pickle relish -- drained
1 tb Scallions -- chopped
4 ts Fresh lemon juice
In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well. Cover and
chill until ready to serve.
