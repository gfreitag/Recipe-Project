Title: MY CHOCOLATE CHIP NUT BARS
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Margarine
2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 c  Chipits
1 c  Nuts
Melt margarine in large pot.  Remove from heat and add sugar and
vanilla. Stir well.  Add eggs, one at a time. Stir together flour,
baking powder, and salt. Add to pan and mix well. Spread in pan 9X13.
Sprinkle with nuts and chipits over surface and press down lightly.
Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes. Cool and cut into bars.
