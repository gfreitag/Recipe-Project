Title: MARKET SALAD WITH CAPERS
Categories: Salads
1 Sm Tomato -- diced
Yield: 2 Servings
1    Ear Corn
2 md Red Potatoes -- cooked
2 ts Capers -- drained
1/4 ts Paprika
2 TB Olive Oil
1/2 TB Red Wine Vinegar
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
:

Peel potatoes if desired. Dice.  Cook corn and remove from cob.
Combine tomato, corn, potatoes, and capers in salad bowl. Stir
together paprika, oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper in small bowl. Pour
over vegetables and toss well.  Let stand 5 minutes for flavors to
blend.
