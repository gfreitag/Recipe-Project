Title: OATMEAL  HAWAIIAN  BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 1/2 c  (20 oz.) crushed pineapple,
Drained
3 c  (10 oz.) coconut
2 ts Soda
1 1/2 c  Oats
2 ts Salt
Combine eggs, sugar, and beat until light.  Sift flour, salt and
soda; add to egg mixture and beat until smooth. Add rest of
ingredients and mix well. Spoon into greased and floured 9x5 loaf
pan. Bake at 325 degrees 1 hour. Remove from pans immediately.
