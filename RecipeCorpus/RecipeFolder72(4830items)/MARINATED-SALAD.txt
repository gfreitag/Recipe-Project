Title: MARINATED SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Can French-style green beans
1 x  Drained
1 ea Small jar chopped pimento
2 ea Small onions, sliced &
1 x  Separated into rings
3/4 c  Chopped celery
1 cn Shoe Peg corn, drained
1 ea Small can tender green peas,
1 x  Drained
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1/2 c  Sugar
DRESSING: In a small saucepan, mix above ingredients. Bring to boil;
stir until sugar is dissolved.  Pour over vegetable mixture;
