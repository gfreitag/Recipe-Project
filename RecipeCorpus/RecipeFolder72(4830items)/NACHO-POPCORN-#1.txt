Title: NACHO POPCORN #1
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 10 Servings
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Crushed red pepper
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
10 c  Warm popped popcorn
1/3 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
In a small bowl, stir paprika, red pepper, and cumin into melted
butter or mar garine. Gently toss butter mixture with popcorn,
coating evenly. Sprinkle wit h Parmesan cheese and toss till coated.
Makes 10 cups.
