Title: MIX & MATCH CASSEROLES
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM-------------------CHOOSE ONE SAUCE MAKER------------------------
1 cn (10 3/4-oz) cream of
-mushroom soup, undiluted
1 cn (10 3/4-oz) cream of celery
-soup, undiluted
1 cn (10 3/4-oz) cream of chicken
-soup, undiluted
1 cn (10 3/4-oz) Cheddar cheese
-soup, undiluted
2 cn (14 1/2-oz) diced tomatoes
-with basil, garlic, and
-oregano, undrained
MMMMM--------------------CHOOSE ONE VEGETABLE-------------------------
1 cn (14 1/2-oz) cut green
-Italian beans, drained
1 cn (15 1/4-oz) sweet peas,
-drained
1 cn (10 1/2-oz) asparagus cuts
-and tips, drained
1 cn (15 1/4-oz) whole kernel
-corn, drained
1 cn (13 1/2-oz) chopped spinach
MMMMM-------------------CHOOSE ONE PASTA/RICE------------------------
2 c  Uncooked elbow macaroni
1 c  Uncooked regular rice
4 c  Uncooked wide egg noodles
3 c  Uncooked small pasta shells
CHOOSE ONE
-MEAT/FISH/POULTRY:
2 cn (6-oz) solid white tuna in
-spring water, drained and
-flaked
2 c  Chopped cooked chicken
2 c  Chopped cooked ham
2 c  Chopped cooked turkey
1 lb Ground beef, browned and
-drained
CHOOSE ONE OR MORE EXTRAS
-(OPTIONAL):
1 cn (3-oz) sliced mushrooms,
-drained
1/4 c  Sliced ripe olives
1/4 c  Chopped red or green bell
-pepper
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Chopped celery
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1 cn (4.5-oz) chopped green
-chiles
1 cn (1 1/4-oz) envelope taco
-seasoning mix
MMMMM-----------------CHOOSE ONE OR TWO TOPPINGS----------------------
1/2 c  (2 oz) shredded mozzarella
-cheese
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 c  (2 oz) shredded Swiss cheese
1/2 c  Fine, dry breadcrumbs
1 c  Round buttery crackers,
-crushed
1 c  Herb-seasoned stuffing mix
Combine 1 (8-ounce) container sour cream, 1 cup milk, 1 cup water, 1
teaspoon salt, and 1 teaspoon pepper with Sauce Maker (omit sour
cream and milk when using tomatoes). Stir in Vegetable, Pasta/Rice,
Meat/Fish/Poultry, and, if desired, Extras. Spoon into a lightly
greased 13- x 9-inch baking dish. Bake, covered, at 350 B0 for 1 hour
and 10 minutes. Uncover and sprinkle with Toppings; bake 10 more
minutes.
