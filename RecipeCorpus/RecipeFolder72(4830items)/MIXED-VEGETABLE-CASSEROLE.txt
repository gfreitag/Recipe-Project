Title: MIXED VEGETABLE CASSEROLE
Categories: Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
2 cn Veg-All, drained
1 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Grated cheese
1    Stick oleo, melted
1 cn Kernel corn, drained
1 c  Chopped onion
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
Ritz crackers, crushed
Combine vegetables, cheese and mayonnaise.  Place in casserole.
Top with Ritz crackers and drizzle oleo over all. Bake at 350 F. for
30 min. Courtesy Telephone Pioneers BillSpalding *P CRBR 38 A
