Title: MARSHMALLOW FUDGE (EDWARDS)
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Powdered sugar
2 oz Chocolate
Cream of tartar; a pinch
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Marshmallows
Cook all ingredients, except marshmallows to soft ball stage. Remove
from fire and beat until creamy. Add marshmallows cut up. Pour into
buttered pan, mark in squares.

Source: Ethel Edwards, Hillgrove Grange, Darke County, OH
