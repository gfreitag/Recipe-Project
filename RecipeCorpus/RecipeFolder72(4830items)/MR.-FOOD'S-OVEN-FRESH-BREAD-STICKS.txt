Title: MR. FOOD'S OVEN-FRESH BREAD STICKS
Categories: Grains, Bread stick
Yield: 10 Servings
1 lb Frozen bread dough, thawed
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1    Egg, beaten
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
3 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Sesame seeds
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 350 F. Cut the thawed
dough into ten 1/2 inch thick slices. Lightly flour a cutting board
and, with your fingers, roll each slice into a 12 inch rope. Place on
a cookie sheet that has been coated with nonstick vegetable spray.
Brush each with beaten egg. In a small bowl, combine the remaining
ingredients and sprinkle on top. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until
golden brown.
