Title: MERRY BARS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Icing sugar
1    Egg yolk
1/2 c  Nuts
1/2 c  Marachino cherries cut up
1/2 c  Mashed banana
1 c  Shredded cocoanut
1 ts Vanilla
Line a 8-8 pan with waxed paper. Cover bottom with layer of graham
wafers. Mix above ingredients and put on the crackers, layer the top
with crackers,then cover with an icing and refrigerate.
