Title: NOTHING PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flour
1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Boiling water
2 ts Butter
1 c  Mixed fruit
1 ts Spice
1 ts Baking soda
Mix boiling water, soda and butter together.  Mix dry ingredients
together add water and mix well.  Tie in a cloth, boil 2 hours. Looks
sloppy but turns out well.

*******************************

This measurements are the old standard measures. This good served with
custard.
