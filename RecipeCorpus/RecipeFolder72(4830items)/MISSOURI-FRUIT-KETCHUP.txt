Title: MISSOURI FRUIT KETCHUP
Categories: Preserve, Ketchup, Grape, Plum, Crabapple
Yield: 1 Servings
3 lb Tart fruit (grapes,
Crabapples and red plums)
1 c  Water
4 c  Cider vinegar
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Ground allspice
Clean fruit, removing stems, cores, and pits.  Add water to fruit and
simmer, covered, for 30 minutes, or until fruit is tender. Press fruit
through a sieve. Pour fruit pulp into a large kettle, add remaining
ingredients.  Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until
smooth and very thick.  Spoon mixture into hot sterilized glasses.
Seal tightly. Cool and store.  Makes three 8oz glasses.

Origin: Women's Day Encyclopedia of Cookery, Vol 1, Missouri Section.
