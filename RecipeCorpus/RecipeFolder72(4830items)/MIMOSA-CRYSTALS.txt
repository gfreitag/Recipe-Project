Title: MIMOSA CRYSTALS
Categories: Flowers, Candies, Holiday, Info/tips
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flowers
1 oz Gum arabic
1 c  Water
1 tb Corn syrup
1 c  Sugar
Dry the flowers. Dissolve gum arabic in 1/2 cup hot water and cool.
Carefully coat the petals with this mixture. Put them on wax paper so
they will not touch and mix the corn syrup and sugar in 1/2 cup water
and cook to a soft ball.  Dip petals into syrup and dry. Sprinkle
with finely granulated sugar.

* Source: Flower Cookery (The Art of Cooking with Flowers) * by Mary
