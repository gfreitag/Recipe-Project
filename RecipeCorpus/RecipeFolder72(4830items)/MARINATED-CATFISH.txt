Title: MARINATED CATFISH
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Catfish fillets
Melted butter; or margarine
-----lemon marinade-----
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Chopped onion
1 ts Salt substitute; (mrs. dash)
1 ts Tabasco sauce
----italian marinade-----
1 c  Italian dressing; (regular o
2 1/2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
----wine marinade----
1/2 c  Dry white wine
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Fresh parsley; chopped
1    Lemon; thinly sliced
Combine ingredients from one of the recipes.  Marinate fillets 1-2
hours turning occasionally.

Broil fillets until fish flakes easily with a fork. Baste with
marinade and/or melted margarine.

Broil 6-8" from heat for 5 minutes.
