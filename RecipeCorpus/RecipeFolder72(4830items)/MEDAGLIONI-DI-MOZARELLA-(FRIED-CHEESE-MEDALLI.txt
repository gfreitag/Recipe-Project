Title: MEDAGLIONI DI MOZARELLA (FRIED CHEESE MEDALLI
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Mozzarella cheese; to 1 1/2
Flour; for dredging
2    Eggs; well beaten
Dry bread crumbs; for dredgi
Oil
Cut the cheese into the shape of medallions about 1/2" thick. Roll
the medallions in the flour, dip in egg, and coat with bread crumbs.
Heat enough oil to come up 1/4" on the sides of a heavy skillet and
fry the medallions over medium-high heat until golden brown on both
sides. Drain on paper towels and serve immediately.

Serves 6-8.
