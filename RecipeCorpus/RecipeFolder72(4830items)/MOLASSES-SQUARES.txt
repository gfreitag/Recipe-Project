Title: MOLASSES SQUARES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Butter or butter substitute
1/3 c  Molasses
1/3 c  Water
Few grains salt
Combine ingredients and boil to soft crack stage (275 ~ 280 F). Pour
into well-buttered, shallow pan.  Cut in squares when nearly cold.
Mrs. J.M. Novin, Stoneboro, PA.
