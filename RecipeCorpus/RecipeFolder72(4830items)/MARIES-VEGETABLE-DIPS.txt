Title: MARIES VEGETABLE DIPS
Categories: Dairy, Dips, Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
1    12 oz jar Maries Blue Cheese
Dressing
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
1/4 c  Finely chopped sweet green
Pepper
1/4 c  Finely chopped sweet red
Pepper
1 tb Chopped fresh dill weed
OR
1/2 ts Dried dill weed, crushed
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients.  Cover, refrigerate until
serving time, at least 2 hours. Garnish with additional diced sweet
red peppers.

Serve with sliced red or green apples for dipping.

Makes 1 1/2 cups
