Title: MINI SUBMARINE SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches, Party
Yield: 8 Servings
8 ts Mayonnaise or salad dressing
8    Hot dog buns, split
3 oz (4 slices) American cheese,
-cut in half
4 oz Thinly sliced salami, cut
-in half
4 oz Thinly sliced cooked turkey,
-cut in half
1 c  Shredded lettuce
Spread 1/2 teaspoon mayonnaise on each cut surface of each bun. Layer
1/8 of cheese, salami, turkey, and lettuce on bottom half of each
bun. Top each with other half of bun.

Source:  All New Party Cookbook
