Title: MR. FOOD'S SMORGASBURGERS
Categories: Meats, Hamburger
Yield: 8 Servings
2 tb Vegetable oil, divided
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
1 md Onion, chopped
1    Egg
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 c  Applesauce
1 ts Dried sage
1 lb Ground turkey
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Cooked brown rice
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 tb Ketchup
8    Hamburger buns
=======================================================

oil over medium-high heat and add the onion and garlic; saut√© for
about 5 minutes, until the onion is tender, and place in a large
