Title: MIDORI MACAROONS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 36 Servings
1    8-oz can almond paste
1 c  Granulated sugar
2    Egg whites
MMMMM------------------------MIDORI ICING-----------------------------
1 c  Powdered sugar
1/8 c  Midori
1 tb Butter
In a mixing bowl crumble almond paste.  Gradually add sugar. Add 2 egg
whites to almond paste mixture. Beat until smooth (dough will be
stiff). Insert large star tube in pastry bag. Fill with dough. Using
a slight swirling motion, squeeze dough onto well-greased cookie
sheet. Lift tube 1/4-inch from pan until dough breaks easily. Or,
drop dough from teaspoon on well-greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 F
for 15-18 minutes. Immediately remove from pan, cool on rack, and ice
with Midori Icing. Makes 3 dozen cookies. Midori Icing:  Mix icing
ingredients together until spreadable.
