Title: MIRIAM'S ESCALLOPED CORN
Categories: Vegetables, Great, Kids love, Marie
Yield: 10 Servings
1/2 c  Butter, melted
1 cn Cream style corn  16 oz
1 cn Whole kernel corn  16 oz
8 oz Sour cream
1 pk Jiffy corn muffin mix
Combine all ingredients.  Place into a well-greased 2 quart casserole.

Bake at 350~ for 1 hour.
