Title: MINT & CELERY LEAF VINEGAR
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Fresh mint leaves
1/2 c  Fresh celery leaves (yellow
Leaves)
3 c  Champagne vinegar or mild
White vinegar
In a heavy bottomed glass jar, crush the mint and celery leaves with
a wood spoon. Store for 1 month in a cool, dark place. Strain through
cheesecloth. Bottle the vinegar with sprigs of fresh mint and celery.
