Title: MINT CHIP BROWNIES
Categories: Desserts, Cookies
Yield: 24 Servings
2 c  SUGAR
1 1/4 c  FLOUR
3/4 c  COCOA
1/2 ts BAKING SODA
1/2 ts SALT
1 c  MELTED MARGARINE
4 ea EGGS
2 ts VANILLA
1 1/2 c  MINT CHOCOLATE CHIPS (9oz.)
1 c  CHOPPED WALNUTS
IN A LARGE BOWL MIX TOGETHER SUGAR, FLOUR, COCOA, BAKING SODA AND
SALT. COMBINE MARGARINE, EGGS, AND VANILLA. COMBINE WITH FLOUR
MIXTURE. FOLD IN CHIPS AND NUTS. TURN INTO A 9" X 13" GREASED BAKING
PAN. BAKE AT 350 DEG F FOR 30 MINUTES. DO NOT OVER BAKE.
