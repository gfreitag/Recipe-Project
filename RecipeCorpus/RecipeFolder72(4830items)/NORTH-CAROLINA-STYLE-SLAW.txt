Title: NORTH CAROLINA STYLE SLAW
Categories: Sides
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Head cabbage -- shredded
2    Red and green bell peppers
Finely chopped
1 c  Cider vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1 ts Ground red pepper
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Ketchup
mix everything but cabbage and peppers and taste!!!!! Add whatever you
think you might want more of ( ketchup and sugar reduce bitterness).

Dump it in with the vegs and mix well.  Let sit in frige to chill and
allow the flavor to shgoot throughout.

En joy on a sandwich next to it or witha pile of Q.
