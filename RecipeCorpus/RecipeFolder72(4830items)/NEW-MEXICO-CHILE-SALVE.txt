Title: NEW-MEXICO CHILE SALVE
Categories: Marinades, Bar-b-q
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-----------------FOR BEEF, POULTRY, & PORK----------------------
3 tb Mild ground chiles,
-=OR=- chili powder
1 ts Hungarian hot paprika
Pinch of coriander
2 tb -=OR=- 3 Tbsp. vegetable oil
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mash until smooth. Makes anout 1/3
cup
