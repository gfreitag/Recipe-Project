Title: MOCHA COFFEE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Batch
1 c  Instant coffee crystals
1 c  Hot chocolate or cocoa mix
1 c  Non-dairy creamer
1/2 c  Sugar
Combine all ingredients; mix thoroughly. Store in a tightly- covered
jar. To serve; put 1-1/2 to 2 tablespoons into a cup or mug. Stir in
boiling water to fill cup. Makes 3-1/2 cups coffee mix or about 25 or
more servings.
