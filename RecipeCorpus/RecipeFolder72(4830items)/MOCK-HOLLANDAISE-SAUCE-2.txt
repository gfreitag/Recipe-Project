Title: MOCK HOLLANDAISE SAUCE 2
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Butter or margarine
3 tb Flour
1 c  Milk
2    Egg yolks -- slightly
Beaten
2 tb Lemon juice
Salt and pepper
Heat butter. Stir in flour. Cook for 1 minute. Add milk. Whisk until
smooth. Remmove form heat. Whisk in egg yolks. Cook for 1 minute.
Stir in lemon juice. Season to taste. Serve with asparagus, broccoli
or spinach.
