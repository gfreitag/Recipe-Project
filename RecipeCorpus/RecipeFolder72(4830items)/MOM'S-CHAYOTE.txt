Title: MOM'S CHAYOTE
Categories: Vegetables, Mom
Yield: 1 Batch
Chayote, skinned and sliced
Salt
MSG
In bottom of pressure cooker, layer skinned slices. Cover with salt
and a touch of MSG.  Another layer of chayote...

Put in just enough water to cover the bottom.  Cover with 15 lb.
weight. After it starts popping, cook 2 minutes.
