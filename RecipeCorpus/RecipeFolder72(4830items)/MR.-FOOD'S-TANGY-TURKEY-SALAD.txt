Title: MR. FOOD'S TANGY TURKEY SALAD
Categories: Salads, Turkey
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Diced cooked turkey
6    Hard-boiled eggs, diced
1 c  Finely chopped celery
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Prepared white horseradish
1/2 ts Black pepper
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients;
mix well. Cover and chill until ready to serve.
