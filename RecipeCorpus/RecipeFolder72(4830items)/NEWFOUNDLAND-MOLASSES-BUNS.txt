Title: NEWFOUNDLAND MOLASSES BUNS
Categories: Breads, Grains, Nfld
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1    Well-beaten egg
1 tb Milk
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Diced pork
1 ts Soda
Cream butter & sugar.  Add egg and vamilla and molasses. Sift
together dry ingredients with milk. Bake in cup cake pans at 350f for
15-20 minutes. Raisins may be added if desired.
