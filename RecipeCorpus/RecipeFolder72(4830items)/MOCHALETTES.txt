Title: MOCHALETTES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 40 Servings
1 c  Vanilla(graham) wafer crumbs
1 1/4 c  Icing sugar (powdered)
1/3 c  Nuts, chopped
1 tb Cocoa
1/2 tb Instant coffee
1/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Rum extract (optional)
Coconut or nuts for rolling
Combine crushed wafers, sugar, cocoa, nuts and coffee. Add milk and
mix well.  Add extract and mix.

Form into small balls (about 1 tsp each) and roll in coconut or
chopped nuts.  Chill.

Source:  Fill the Cookie Jar, City of Calgary Electric System, undated
Shared and tested by Elizabeth Rodier Aug 93.
