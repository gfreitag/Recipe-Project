Title: MOM'S ROAST CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Mom
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Chicken
Lemon juice, bottled
Sherry
Garlic powder
Salt
Pepper
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Clean and dry the chicken. Then wet
the skin with bottled lemon juice. Sprinkle the top with salt,
pepper, garlic powder & MSG. Roast with water and sherry in the
bottom of the pan. Baste occasionally (with water and sherry).

After one & a half hours, slice between the thigh and body on both
sides. Continue to roast till done (about two hours; check charts for
bird's weight).

Sprinkle with paprika (for color) before serving.
