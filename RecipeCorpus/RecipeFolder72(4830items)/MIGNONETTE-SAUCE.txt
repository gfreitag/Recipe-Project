Title: MIGNONETTE SAUCE
Categories: Seasonings, Sauces
Yield: 1 Batch
1    Part white wine
1    Part champagne vinegar or
--  white wine vinegar
A little minced shallot
Lots of freshly cracked
-- black pepper
Combine all ingredients.  Excellent served with fresh oysters.

From Susan Belsinger and Carolyn Dille's "Peppercorns Around the
World" article in "The Herb Companion." Dec. 1992/Jan. 1993, Vol. 5,
No. 2. Pg. 46.  Posted by Cathy Harned.
