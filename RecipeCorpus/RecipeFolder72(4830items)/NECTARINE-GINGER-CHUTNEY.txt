Title: NECTARINE GINGER CHUTNEY
Categories: Oriental
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 lb Fresh ginger -- peeled and
Minced
6 c  Granulated sugar
3 c  Cider vinegar
6    Cloves garlic -- peeled and
Mashed
8 lb Nectarines (about 24 medium)
Pitted and cubed
2 md Onions -- chopped
1 1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 c  Golden raisins
1 c  Raisins
Cover ginger with water in a saucepan and bring to a boil, turn down
heat and simmer until tender, 35-40 minutes. In a large non-aluminum
pot, mix ginger water, ginger, sugar, vinegar, and garlic. Bring to a
boil and add remaining ingredients. Cook until thick, about 1 1/2
hours.
