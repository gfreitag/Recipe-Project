Title: MARINATED FLANK STEAK (M_C-TX) 05/11/93
Categories: Meats, Beef, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Flank steak
3/4 c  Soy sauce
3 tb Honey
2 tb Vinegar
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Ginger
3/4 c  Salad oil
Minced onion
Marinate steak in mixture of next seven ingredients a few hours or
overnight in the refrigerator.  Broil to desired doneness turning
once.
