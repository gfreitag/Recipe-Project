Title: MULTI-GRAIN SOYBEAN BREAD (1 1/2 LB. BREAD MA
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker, Bean/legume
Yield: 1 Loaf
2 c  Bread flour
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 c  Soybean flour
1 1/2 ts Nonfat powdered milk
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Cornmeal
1/3 c  Bran flakes
1 ts Flax seeds
1 ts Whole millet
1/2 c  Soybeans; soaked overnight
- and drained
1 1/2 ts Soy oil
1 1/2 ts Honey
1 1/2 c  Warm water
1 1/2 ts Dry yeast
Add ingredients to bread machine according to manufacturer's
directions.

Bread Machine Tips: All ingredients should be at room temperature,
unless otherwise noted in the recipe.

Ingredients should be added to your bread machine according to the
specific directions given inthe owner's manual.

Always use powdered milk when using the timer on your machine.

Yield: One 1 1/2 pound loaf of delicious and nutritious bread.
