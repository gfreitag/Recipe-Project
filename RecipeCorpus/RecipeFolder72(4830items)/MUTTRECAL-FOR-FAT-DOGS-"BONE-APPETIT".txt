Title: MUTTRECAL FOR FAT DOGS "BONE APPETIT"
Categories: Pets
Yield: 1 Batch
1 c  Soy Beans
2 dr Soy sauce
1 tb Oil, Vegetable
ds Salt, sea
Combine ingredients. Spread in a single layer on a greased baking
sheet. Bake, stirring occasionally, for 25 minutes at 325 degrees.
Serve warm and crunchy. Said to have only 25 calories per mouthful.
stiff. Chill for a half hour. Roll rounded teaspoonfuls into balls.
