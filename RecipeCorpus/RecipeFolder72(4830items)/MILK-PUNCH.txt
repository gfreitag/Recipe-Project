Title: MILK PUNCH
Categories: Essence, Of, Emeril
Yield: 4 Servings    1 1/2 oz brandy
1/2 ts vanilla extract
1/2 oz simple syrup
1 oz half and half
2 oz milk
Freshly grated nutmeg
:

In a cocktail shaker combine brandy, vanilla, syrup, half-and-half,
and milk; fill with ice cubes. Shake vigorously until chilled; strain
into a cocktail glass, dust with nutmeg and serve.

Yield: 1 drin
