Title: OLD FASHIONED CHOCOLATE BUTTER ROLLS
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Flour
1/3 c  Cocoa powder
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 ea 'gobs' of margerine
1 ea Rolled out pie crust
1 ea Milk
For one butter roll:

Mix all ingredients.  Spread on pie crust.  Roll up like a jelly
roll. Put into a greased pan to prevent sticking. Dab margerine on
top and pour milk on unitl it is about 1/4 inch deep in pan. Cook
until crust is brown and crispy and milk is absorbed.  About one hour
on 275-300 degrees.

This was taught to a friend of mine by her great grand father.
