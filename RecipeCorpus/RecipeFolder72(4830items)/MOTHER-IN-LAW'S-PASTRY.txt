Title: MOTHER IN LAW'S PASTRY
Categories: Desserts, Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Water
The above ingredients will make 3 crusts.

Cut shortening into flour and salt mixture until mealy. Add water and
mix until dough pulls away from sides of bowl.
