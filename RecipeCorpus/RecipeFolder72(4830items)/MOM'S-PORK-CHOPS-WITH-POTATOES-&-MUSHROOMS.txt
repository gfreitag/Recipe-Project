Title: MOM'S PORK CHOPS WITH POTATOES & MUSHROOMS
Categories: Casseroles, Pork, Meats
Pork Chops
Potatoes
Salt
Yield: 1 Servings  :
:
:
1 cn Cream Of Mushroom Soup
Water
:

Brown pork chops in skillet with small amount of oil. Slice raw
potatoes into baking dish, Sprinkle lightly with salt. Place chops on
potatoes. Mix 3/4 can of water with soup and pour mixture over chops
and potatoes. Cover with foil. Bake at 350 degrees for
1-1/2    hours.
