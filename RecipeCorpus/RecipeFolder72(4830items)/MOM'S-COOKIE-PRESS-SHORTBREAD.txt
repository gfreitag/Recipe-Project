Title: MOM'S COOKIE PRESS SHORTBREAD
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 2 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar, sifted
4 c  Butter
8 c  Flour
2 ts Salt (omit ?)
1 c  Cornstarch
1 c  Icing sugar
Directions: Mix well, put in cookie press and put cookies on ungreased
cookie sheet. Bake at 325 approx 20 minutes. Cookies should not brown
too much.
