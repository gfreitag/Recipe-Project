Title: MARTHA'S BUTTERMILK POUND CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 16 Servings
MMMMM----------------------------CAKE---------------------------------
1 c  Margarine
2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
1 c  Buttermilk
1/2 ts Baking soda
3 c  All purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Vanilla extract
2 tb Lemon extract
1 ts Almond extract
MMMMM------------------------LEMON GLAZE-----------------------------
1 c  Powdered sugar
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Lemon juice
******Cake:*********** Cream margarine and sugar together until light
and fluffy.  Add eggs one at a time beating well after each addition.
Mix together the baking soda and buttermilk. Combine flour and salt
and add to creamed mixture 1 cup at a time alternating with the
buttermilk. Beat well after each addition. Stir in the flavorings and
bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes at 350{F in a 10 inch Bundt pan. Cool
10    minutes and remove from pan. ******Lemon glaze:********
Mix all ingredients in a small bowl and pour over the cake allowing
to drip over the edges.
