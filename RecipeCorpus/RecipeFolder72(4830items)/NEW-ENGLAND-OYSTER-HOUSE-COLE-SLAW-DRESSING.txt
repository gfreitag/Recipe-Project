Title: NEW ENGLAND OYSTER HOUSE COLE SLAW DRESSING
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb White vinegar
2 tb Prepared horseradish
1/2 ts Celery seed
Blend ingredients thoroughly and chill. Makes about 2 1/2 cups. Use 1
cup of dressing for each 8 ounces of shredded cabbage, about 1/2 a
small head or 2 cups. This may be refrigerated for up to a month.
