Title: NO-BAKE FRESH FRUIT TART
Categories: Pie/cobbler, Fruits, Formatted
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
1 tb Orange rind; grated
2 tb Orange juice
1/2 c  Sugar, confectioners
1    Crust, crumb 8"
6    Crumb tart shells
2 c  Fresh fruit; such as
-Fresh whole berries
-Seedless green grapes
-Pitted sweet cherries
-Sliced peaches
-Nectarines
-Plums; etc.
Beat cheese, rind, juice, and sugar together until fluffy. Fill pie
crust o tart shells with cheese mixture. Chill thoroughly. Top with
fruit. Add 1 tablesppon lemon juice to peaches or nectarines to
prevent discoloration. S more sugar generously over fruit.
