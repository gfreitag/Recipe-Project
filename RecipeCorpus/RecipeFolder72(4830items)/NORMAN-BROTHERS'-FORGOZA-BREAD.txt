Title: NORMAN BROTHERS' FORGOZA BREAD
Categories: Miamiherald, Breads
Yield: 2 Loaves
MMMMM-----------------FORMATTED BY LISA CRAWFORD----------------------
2 lb White bread dough
1 c  Chopped green onion
1/2 c  Chopped parsley
3 oz Parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Dried oregano
1 tb Granulated garlic
2 tb Crushed fresh garlic
1/3 c  Olive oil
stretch bread dough to about a 12-inch square. Sprinkle all
ingredients onto the dough.  Beginning at the top of the square, roll
up dough. Chop the rolled dough into small pieces and pile pices into
2 ungreased 9-inch pie pans.  Let dough rise in warm place until
doubled. Bake at 350 degrres F for 20 to 30 minutes, until nicely
browned.

Nutritional infor per serving: (10 slices per loaf) 164 cal; 6g pro,
21g carb, 6g fat(35%), .2g fiber, 3mg chol, 287mg sodium Exchanges:
.1 veg, 1.2 bread, .3 meat, 1.1 fat

Source: Norman Brothers' Market, Kendall, Florida Miami Herald,
10/5/95
