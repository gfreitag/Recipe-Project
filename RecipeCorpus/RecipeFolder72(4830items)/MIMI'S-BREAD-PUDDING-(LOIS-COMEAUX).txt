Title: MIMI'S BREAD PUDDING (LOIS COMEAUX)
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2    Long loaf french bread
12 oz Evaporated Milk
12 oz Water
3    Beaten eggs
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 oz Vanilla
1/2 c  Flour-dusted raisins
2    Bananas
MMMMM-----------------------WHISKEY SAUCE----------------------------
1/2    Stick butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Jigger of whiskey or rum
Soak the bread in the evaporated milk and water for about a half
hour. Add the eggs, sugar, vanilla, raisins, ans bananas. bake at 350
deg.F for 45 min to an Hour. Serve hot or cold with Whiskey Sauce.
