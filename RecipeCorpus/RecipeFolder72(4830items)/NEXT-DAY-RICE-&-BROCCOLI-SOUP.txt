Title: NEXT DAY RICE & BROCCOLI SOUP
Categories: New, Text, Import
2 TB butter
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  chopped onion or shallots
1/2    apple, -- peeled and finely
diced
:
2 c  broth or water
1 c  or more cooked broccoli, --
finely chopped
:
1/4    to
1/2 c  cooked rice
salt and pepper
:

Heat butter in a small saucepan. Add onion or shallots, and apple,
cover and cook over low heat for 5 minutes or until tender. Add
cooked broccoli, rice and broth, cover and simmer 5 minutes to
reheat. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve as is, or better
yet, puree in blender. If thick, thin with water; adjust seasoning.
