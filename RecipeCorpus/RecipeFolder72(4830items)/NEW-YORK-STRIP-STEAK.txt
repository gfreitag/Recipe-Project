Title: NEW YORK STRIP STEAK
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
3    New York strip steaks
-- each about 1 inch thick
1/4 c  Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce
-- (Lite, Kikkoman)
1    Garlic clove; minced
1/8 ts Black pepper
Trim fat from steaks; place steaks in large plastic bag with mixture
of lite teriyaki sauce, garlic and pepper. Press air out of bag;
close top securely. Marinate 45 minutes; turn bag over once.
Reserving marinade, broil or grill steaks about 5 minutes on each
side (for rare), or to desired doneness, brushing occasionally with
reserved marinade.

Makes 6 3-oz servings.
