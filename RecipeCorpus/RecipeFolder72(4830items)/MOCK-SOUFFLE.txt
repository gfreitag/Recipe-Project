Title: MOCK SOUFFLE
Categories: Eggs, Cheese
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Cheese Sandwiches No Butter
4 lg Well Beaten Eggs
2 1/2 c  Milk
10 1/2 oz Cream Of Mushroom Soup
1/4 c  Crabmeat, Or Shrimp Or
Chicken
