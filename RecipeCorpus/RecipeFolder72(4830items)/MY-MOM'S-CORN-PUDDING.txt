Title: MY MOM'S CORN PUDDING
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn Cream style corn
1 cn Kernel corn, drained
2    Eggs
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Flour
1 ts Salt
1 c  Milk
2 tb Butter, melted
Mix all ingredients and turn into a greased 1 1/2 qt casserole. Bake
at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until firm and set in the middle.
