Title: MEATBALLS IN CHILE SAUCE
Categories: Appetizers, Meats, Mexican
Yield: 8 Servings
3 ea Corn Tortillas; *
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 lb Ground Beef
1/2 lb Ground Porked
1/2 lb Ground Ham; Smoked, Cooked
1/4 c  Onion; Chopped, 1 Small
1 ea Clove Garlic; Finely Chopped
1 ts Cumin; Ground
1 ts Oregano Leaves; Dried
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Basic Red Sauce; **
1 c  Beef Broth
*    Corn Tortillas should be 6 inches in diameter and should be cut
into
small pieces. ** For Red Sauce Recipe see Sowest2.zip, or use
your favorite mexican
red sauce recipe.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Place tortilla pieces and milk in large bowl; let
stand 15 minutes. Mix in remaining ingredients except basic red sauce
and broth. Shape into 1-inch balls.  Heat basic red sauce and broth
to boiling in 10-inch skillet; reduce heat.  Add meatballs. Cover and
simmer until done, 15 to 20 minutes.
