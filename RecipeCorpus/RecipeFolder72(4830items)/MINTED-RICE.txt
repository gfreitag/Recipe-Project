Title: MINTED RICE
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 2 Servings
-Sue Woodward
1/2 c  Long grain white rice
1/2 md Cucumber; diced
1/4 c  Fresh mint; chopped
1/2 c  Nonfat plain yogurt
Salt
Black pepper
Bring 3 to 4 quarts water to boil.  Add rice; boil 10 mins. Mix
cucumber, mint, and yogurt in a serving bowl. Drain rice; add to
bowl. Add salt and pepper, to taste. Toss well.

Source:  TPA Trib, 2/16/95
