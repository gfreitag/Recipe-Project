Title: MUSTARD SAUCE 3
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Prepared mustard
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Sugar
Blend together, mayonnaise, mustard and lemon juice. Mix dry mustard
and sugar with a little water until dissolved. Stir into first
mixture. Allow to stand in refrigerator 1 hour before using. If a hot
sauce is desired, more dry mustard may be added.
