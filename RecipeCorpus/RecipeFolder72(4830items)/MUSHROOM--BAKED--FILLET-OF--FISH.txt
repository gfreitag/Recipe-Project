Title: MUSHROOM  BAKED  FILLET OF  FISH
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Med. onions
1 c  Sliced mushrooms or buttons
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 1/2 lb Fish fillets (sole,
-flounder, cod, haddock)
1 ds Salt & pepper
1/4 c  Dry white wine
Preparation : Cut onions into rings, saute in 3 tablespoons butter
with mushrooms until soft.  Place fish in greased baking dish.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Spread sauteed mixture over fish, pour
on wine. Dot with 1 tablespoon butter.  Bake uncovered for 20 minutes
or until fish flakes with fork.  Serve with rest of white wine.
