Title: MCDOUGALL CURRIED BEAN SANDWICH SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Spreads, Dips, Snacks, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 Servings
3/4 c  Water
1 ea Onion, finely chopped
1 c  Celery, diced
1 ea Green bell pepper, diced
1/2 c  Carrot, diced
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
2 1/2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1 tb Soy sauce
3 c  Cooked white beans
Place the water in a saucepan, add all the vegetables & the garlic.
Cook stirring occasionally, for 15 minutes. Stir in the curry powder,
cumin & soy sauce, mix well. Remove from the heat. Add the beans; mix
well. Place the mixture in a food processor or blender & process
briefly until chopped but not pureed.  Chill.
