Title: MESS
Categories: Beef
Yield: 4 Servings    1 1/2 lb ground beef
1 cn French style green beans --
(16 oz)
:
1 cn tomato soup
1 sm onion chopped
1 cn mushrooms

In dutch oven or large pot, brown ground beef and onion until onion is
clear. Drain and add other ingredients. Heat through and salt to
taste. Serve plain or on top of noodles or spaghetti.
