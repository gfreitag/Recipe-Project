Title: MOM'S WALDORF SALAD
Categories: Salads, Mom
Yield: 1 Batch
2 lg Apples, chopped
Celery, diced
2 c  Nuts (or less), chopped
Raisins (and/or grapes)
Mayonnaise
Honey
ds Nutmeg
ds Salt
Combine apples with a sprinkle of nutmeg and salt, grapes &/or
raisins, celery, pecans/walnuts, mayonnaise and a spoonful of honey.

Notes:  Brian likes to add 3-4 bananas (sliced), but he doesn't like
the celery.  Mom says the bananas means it isn't Waldorf salad
anymore! :-0
