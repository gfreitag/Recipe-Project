Title: MIXED DISH SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 2 Servings
1    Crisp lettuce
-- trimmed and washed
1    Celery heart
1    Orange; peeled and sliced
50 g  Hard cheese, cubed
2 tb Crotons
1 tb Parmesan, grated
3 tb Garlic mayonnaise
Place the lettuce leaves in the bottom, and around the edge, of two
wooden bowls. Slice the celery lengthwise and place in a radiating
pattern on top. Cut the orange slices into quarters and put them in
the centre. Mix the cubed cheese with the crotons and sprinkle over,
then sprinkle with the grated Parmesan. Dribble garlic mayonnaise
over the top in a lattice pattern and the salad is ready to serve.
