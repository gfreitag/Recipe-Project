Title: MOM'S MANDARIN ORANGE MOLD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lg Pkg.orange jello
1 pt Orange sherbet
1 lg Can Mandarin orange sections
In juice
Dissolve gelatin in hot water as directed on package. Add sherbet and
melt. Add oranges and juice and mix well. Pour into mold, chill
several hours until firm. Garnish with lettuce and fresh orange
slices..

A colorful and tasty party salad.
