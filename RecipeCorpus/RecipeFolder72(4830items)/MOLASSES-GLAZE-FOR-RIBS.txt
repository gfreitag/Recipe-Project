Title: MOLASSES GLAZE FOR RIBS
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  CATSUP
2 tb LIGHT MOLASSES
1 tb LEMON JUICE
1 tb SOY SAUCE
4 ea DASHES OF HOT SAUCE or more
STIR TOGETHER ALL INGREDIENTS IN A SMALL BOWL. USE A BRUSH TO PAINT
SAUCE ON RIBS DURING THE LAST 15 MINUTES OF GRILLING OR BROILING.
