Title: MEXICAN-STYLE MEAT LOAF
Categories: Low-fat, Meats, Mexican, Meats
Yield: 7 Slices
No-stick corn oil cooking
Spray
1 lb Extra-lean ground turkey
3/4 lb Ground beef sirloin
2    Green onions, chopped
3/4 ts Salt
3/4 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Dried marjoram or oregano
1/4 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1 c  Fresh bread crumbs
7 oz Jar sweet roasted red pepper
Drained and chopped
1    Egg white
1/2 c  Canned black beans, drained
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly coat an 8x5x3 inch loaf baking pan
with no-stick corn oil cooking spray; set aside. In a large bowl,
combine the extra-lean ground turkey, ground sirloin, green onions,
salt, cumin, marjoram or oregano, black pepper, bread crumbs, red
pepper, egg white and black beans.  Mix with your hands or a sturdy
spatula to thoroughly combine all ingredients.  Pack into prepared
pan and bake 45 minutes. Remove from heat and allow to rest 5 minutes
before slicing. Cut into 1 inch slices.
