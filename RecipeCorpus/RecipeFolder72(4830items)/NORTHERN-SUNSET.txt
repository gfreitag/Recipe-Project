Title: NORTHERN SUNSET
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Chocolate powder
5 ts Instant coffee granules
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
1 tb Orange liqueur
1 tb Coffee liqueur
Whipped cream
In 2 cup glass measure, combine chocolate powder, coffee and water;
stir to dissolve. Add liqueurs; split mixture between two coffe cups.
Top each with some whipped cream. Makes two 3/4 cup Servings. Source:
The Oakville Journal. Oct.29/96ch
