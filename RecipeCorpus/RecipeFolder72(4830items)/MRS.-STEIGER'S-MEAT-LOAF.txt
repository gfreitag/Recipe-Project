Title: MRS. STEIGER'S MEAT LOAF
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles, Low-fat
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Ground chicken
1 c  Oatmeal
1 tb Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
1 c  Applesauce
Corn meal or bread crumbs may be substituted for oatmeal. Preheat
oven to 350.  Mix together well and bake 1 hour. Vary with chopped
tomato, green pepper, parsley, celery.
