Title: MOCK HOLLANDAISE SAUCE(LOW CHOLESTEROL)
Categories: Sauces, Fish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Hellmanns real mayonnaise
2    Egg whites
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Salt(optional)
In small saucepan with wire whisk beat all ingredients until smooth.
Stirring constantly , cook over medium-low heat until thick (do not
boil). Serve over vegetables or fish. Makes 1 1/2 cups
5 mg cholesterol per Tb.spoon.
