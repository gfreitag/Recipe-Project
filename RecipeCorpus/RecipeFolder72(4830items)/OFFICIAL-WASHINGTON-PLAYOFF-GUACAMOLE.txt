Title: OFFICIAL WASHINGTON PLAYOFF GUACAMOLE
Categories: Mexican, Condiment, Ceideburg
Yield: 3 Servings
Stephen Ceideburg
5    Avocados, peeled and seeded
1 tb Fresh dill, chopped
1/2 tb Garlic salt
1 c  Plain yogurt
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
2 tb Mild, medium or hot salsa
9    Tortilla chips
Mash avocados with fork, leaving some chunks. Add dill, garlic salt
and yogurt, and mix until well-blended. Add lemon juice and desired
salsa to taste. Serve with tortilla chips: To store guacamole, cover
with plastic wrap or aluminum foil and refrigerate.

From the Oregonian's FOODday, 1/26/93.
