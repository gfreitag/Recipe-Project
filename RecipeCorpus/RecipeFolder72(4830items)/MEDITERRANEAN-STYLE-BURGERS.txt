Title: MEDITERRANEAN-STYLE BURGERS
Categories: Beef, Sandwiches
Yield: 8 Servings
-Pam Coombes
2 lb Lean ground beef
1 pk 10oz, frozen chopped spinach
Thawed, and drained well
2 cl Garlic, finely chopped
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 c  Prepared spaghetti sauce
1/2 c  Packaged Italian-seasoned
Bread crumbs
Preheat the broiler or prepare the grill so the coals are hot.
Combien the beef, spinach, Parmesan cheese, spaghetti sauce and bread
crumbs in a large bowl until well mixed. Divide the mixture into 8
equal portions and shape into 8 burgers patties. Broil or grill the
burgers 4 inches from heat for 6 minutes on each side or until cooked
through. Family Circle 6/27/95
