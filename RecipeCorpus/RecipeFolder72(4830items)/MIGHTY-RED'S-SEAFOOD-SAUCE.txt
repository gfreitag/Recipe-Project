Title: MIGHTY RED'S SEAFOOD SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 32 Servings
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/2 c  Ketchup
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Prepared horseradish
8 dr Tabasco sauce
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 ds Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Fresh ground black pepper
Combine all ingredients. Chill before serving. Makes about 2 cups, or
32 one-tablespoon servings.
