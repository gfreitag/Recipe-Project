Title: MR. FOOD'S GRADUATION PUNCH
Categories: Drinks, Punch
Yield: 15 Servings
12 oz Frozen fruit punch drink
- concentrate, thawed
6 oz Frozen orange juice
- concentrate, thawed
6 oz Frozen lemonade
- concentrate, thawed
2    Litres ginger ale
1 qt Orange sherbet
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a large punch bowl, combine the fruit
punch, orange juice, and lemonade concentrates. Stir until well
mixed. Add the ginger ale and sherbet and refrigerate until ready to
serve. NOTE: For a really festive look, slice up a few oranges,
lemons and/or limes to float on top of the punch.
