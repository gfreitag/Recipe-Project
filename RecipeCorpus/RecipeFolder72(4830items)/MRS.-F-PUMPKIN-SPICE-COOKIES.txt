Title: MRS. F PUMPKIN SPICE COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 36 Servings
MMMMM------------------MRS. FIELDS' COOKIE BOOK-----------------------
2 1/2 c  All purp flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
2 ts Pumpkin pie spice
1 c  Dark brown sugar
1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Salted butter, softened
1 lg Egg
1 c  Pumpkin
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Chpd walnuts
Preheat oven 300 F. In med bowl combine flour, soda, salt, and
pumpkin pie spice. Mix well with wire whisk. Set aside. In lg bowl
blend sugars with mixer set at med speed. Add butter and beat to form
a grainy paste. Scrape sides of bowl, then add egg, pumpkin, and
vanilla. Beat at med speed until light and fluffy. Add flour mixture,
raisins, and walnuts. Blend at low speed just until combined. Do not
overmix. Drop by rounded Ts onto ungreased cookie sheets, 1 1/2"
apart. Bake 22-24 mins until cookies are slightly browned along
edges. Immediately transfer cookies to a cool surface.
