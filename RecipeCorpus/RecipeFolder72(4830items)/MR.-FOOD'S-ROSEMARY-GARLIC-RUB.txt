Title: MR. FOOD'S ROSEMARY-GARLIC RUB
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 99 Servings
Season 2 pounds of meat
1 1/2 ts Grated lemon peel
1 ts Dried rosemary leaves,
- crushed
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dried thyme leaves
1/2 ts Coarsely ground black pepper
2    Garlic cloves, minced
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients.
Rub over 2 pounds of the meat of your choice and grill to desired
doneness.
