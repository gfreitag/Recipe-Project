Title: MR. FOOD'S EASY CHICKEN BAKE
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Condensed golden corn soup
1/4 c  Celery -- finely chopped
1/4 c  Onion -- finely chopped
1/2 ts Paprika
2 c  Herb-seasoned stuffing
Cubes
4    Skinless boneless chicken
Breast halves
1 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Brown mustard -- spicy
Preheat oven to 400F.  In a medium-sized bowl, combine soup,
celery,onion, paprika, and stuffing mix.  Spoon the mixture evenly
into a greased 9" x 13" baking dish. Arrange the chicken over the
mixture, pressing it lightly into the mixture. In a samll bowl or
cup, combine the brown sugar and mustard. Spread evenly over the
chicken, then bake for 30 minutes or until the chicken is done and no
pink remains.

Yield:  4 serving
