Title: MRS. FIELDS ICING *
Categories: Frostings, Chocolate
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
8    Unsweetend chocolate
- squares
2 tb Butter
2 lb Powdered sugar
1 c  Whipped cream
4 ts Vanilla
6 oz Milk chocolate chips
1 pk Hershey kisses
Combine chocolate and butter in small saucepan. Place over low heat
until melted. Remove from heat. Combine sugar, cream, and vanilla in
medium size bowl. Stir till smooth. Add chocolate and butter. Set
bowl in a pan of ice and beat with wooden spoon till frosting is
thickened. After frosting bottom layer,add 1 cup of chips. Spread on
top of of cake. Add second layer. Frost second layer. Apply kisses as
desired over top of cake. Add remaining chips to the sides.
