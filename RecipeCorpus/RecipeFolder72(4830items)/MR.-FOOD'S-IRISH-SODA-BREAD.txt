Title: MR. FOOD'S IRISH SODA BREAD
Categories: Grains, Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  (1 stick) butter, melted,
1 tb Butter, melted for brushing
- top of bread
2/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Raisins
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
2    Eggs beaten
2 c  Milk
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 350 F. In a large bowl,
combine all the ingredients except the 1 tablespoon butter; mix Just
until moistened. Place in a greased 9-inch round baking pan and bake
for 1 hour or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
