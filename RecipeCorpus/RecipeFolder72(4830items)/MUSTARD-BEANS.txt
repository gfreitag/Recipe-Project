Title: MUSTARD BEANS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
4 lb Green beans
3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Mustard
1 tb Celery Seed
1 tb Tumeric
3 c  Wine vinegar
Wash and cut beans in pieces and boil in salted water until tender.
Make a sauce of other ingredients by boiling until thick. Pour this
over the beans, mix well and bottle
