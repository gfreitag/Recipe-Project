Title: NO BAKE CHOC. COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Famfavorite
Yield: 24 Servings
1/2 c  MARGARINE
1/2 c  MILK
2 c  SUGAR
4 tb COCOA
1/2 c  PEANUT BUTTER
1 ts VANILLA
3 c  INSTANT QUAKER OATS
MIX FIRST 4 INGREDIENTS. BRING TO A ROLLING BOIL IN LARGE SAUCE POT.
REMOVE FROM HEAT. STIR IN VANILLA AND PEANUT BUTTER UNTIL MELTED. ADD
OATS AND STIR WELL. DROP BY TABLESPOON ON WAX PAPER TO COOL.
