Title: MR. FOOD'S PASTA GREEN SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Cooked rotini pasta
-, cooled
16 oz Shredded Swiss cheese
10 oz Frozen cut green beans,
- thawed and drained
20 oz Frozen green peas,
- thawed and drained
1 c  Green bell pepper strips,
- cut in half
1 c  Chopped cucumber
1/2 c  Sliced scallions
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/3 c  Plain yogurt
3 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 ts Dried dillweed
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
