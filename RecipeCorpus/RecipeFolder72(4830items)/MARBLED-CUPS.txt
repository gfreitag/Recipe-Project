Title: MARBLED CUPS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
4 tb Orange Juice
3 oz Plain Chocolate
1    Sachet of Chocolate Dessert
-eg. birds
7 fl Milk
4 fl Whipping Cream
8    Chocolate Cups
Place juice and chocolates, broken into small pieces, in a bowl.
Stand bowl over a pan of hot water and let chocolate dissolve,
stirring occasionally.

Make up the chocolate dessert with the milk, as directed. Whisk in the
melted chocolate mixture. Lightly whip the cream until it holds its
shape. Carefully swirl the cream through the chocolate mixture.

Immeadiately spoon mixture into chocolate cups and chill for 30
minutes.

Source: Woman's Realm
