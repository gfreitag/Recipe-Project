Title: MEAT & POTATOES MEATLOAF
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 10 Servings
1    Onion, peeled
2    Potatoes
1    Carrot
2 lb Extra-lean ground beef
2    Eggs -OR-
4    Egg whites -OR-
1/2 c  Thawed egg substitute
1/2 c  Non-fat milk
1 tb Worcestershire sauce (opt)
1 ts Dried thyme or other herb
Salt or seasoned salt -AND-
Pepper to taste
Put onion, potatoes and carrot through a shredder, or the fine
shredding disk of a food processor (it's not necessary to pare potato
and carrot). Combine with remaining ingredients; mix well. Shape into
a loaf on a non-stick baking sheet.
Bake, uncovered, in preheated 350-degree oven 1 hour. Makes 10
servings, 170 calories each.
