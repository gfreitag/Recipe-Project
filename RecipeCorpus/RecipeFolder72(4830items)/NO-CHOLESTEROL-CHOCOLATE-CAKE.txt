Title: NO CHOLESTEROL CHOCOLATE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Chocolate
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
8 tb Cocoa
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Lite or cholesterol-
1 x  Free mayonnaise
1 c  Water
1 ts Vanilla
Mix together dry ingredients in a bowl. Add remaining ingredients and
mix on high speed with electric mixer for 3 minutes. Pour into pan
(one 9x13 or two 8" layers) sprayed with nonstick coating and bake at
350 degrees for 30 minutes. NOTE: from the teacher that submitted
this. "We also bake it in ice cream cones, 30 seconds each in the
microwave; top with ice cream."
