Title: MILLY'S MARBLE POUND CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
Lisa Crawley  tspn00b
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter
3 1/2 c  Cake flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
2 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
4    Eggs
1/4 c  Cocoa
Grease a 10" tube pan.  In a lg. bowl with mixer at low speed, beat
sugar and butter until light.  Add flour and remaining ingred. except
the cocoa. Beat 4 minutes. Preheat oven to 350. Remove 2 1/2 c.
batter and beat cocoa into the batter.  Alternately spoon vanilla and
chocolate batter into the tube pan. Cut and twist with knife through
the batter. Bake for one hr. Cool.  Remove from pan.  Sprinkle with
confectioner's sugar if desired. Lisa Crawley  TSPN00B
