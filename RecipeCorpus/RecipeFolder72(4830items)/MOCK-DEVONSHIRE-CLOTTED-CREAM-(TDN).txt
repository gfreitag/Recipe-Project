Title: MOCK DEVONSHIRE CLOTTED CREAM (TDN)
Categories: Desserts, Sauces
Yield: 999 Servings
1 pk (3 oz) cream cheese
1 c  Heavy or whipping cream
1 ts Powdered sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
Blend all ingredients in a blender or food processor, until thick
(food processor works best).  Serve with preserves on warm scones.
Keep refrigerated.
