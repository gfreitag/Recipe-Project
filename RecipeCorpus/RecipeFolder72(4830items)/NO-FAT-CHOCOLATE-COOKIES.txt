~=>Basic Baking Mix Use this premeasured mix to prepare chocolate
cookies, brownies or a cake. Makes 7 cups of mix.

Preheat oven to 350~.  Coat cookie sheets with nonstick vegetable-oil
cooking spray.

Stir together egg whites, corn syrup, water and vanilla in
medium-sized bowl. Stir in baking mix until well blended. Roll dough
into 1-inch balls. Spread sugar on small flat plate. Place 2 inches
apart on cookie sheets. Press balls flat with bottom of glass.

Bake in preheated 350~ oven for 6-8 minutes.  Cool cookies on baking
sheet on wire rack for 5 minutes. Remove cookies from sheet to rack
to cool completely.

Per cookie: 61 calories, 1 g protein, .18 g fat, 14 g carbohydrate,
72 mg sodium, 0 mg cholesterol.

Exhanges: 1/2 starch/bread, 1/2 fruit.
