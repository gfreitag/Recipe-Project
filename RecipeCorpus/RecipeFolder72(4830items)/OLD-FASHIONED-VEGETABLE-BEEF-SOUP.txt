Title: OLD FASHIONED VEGETABLE BEEF SOUP
Categories: Main dish, Soups
Yield: 10 -12
MMMMM---------------------------STOCK--------------------------------
3    Beef shanks; cross cut
8 c  Water
4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Oregano; dried
1/4 ts Marjoram; dried
5 ea Whole black peppercorns
2 ea Bay leaves
~--------------------------------Vegetables------------
1 10    oz.  corn; frozen
3 ea tomatoes; peeled and cut up
2 ea potatoes; medium, cubed
1 c  green beans, fresh; or
-frozen
:
2 ea carrots; cubed
2    celery; chopped
1 ea onion; medium, sliced

Combine all stock ingredients and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat
to low, and simmer for 2 hours. Remove beef; when cool enough to
handle, cut off meat and coarsley chop. Discard bones, strain broth,
and skim excess fat.  Return broth to kettle and add all remaining
ingredients plus chopped meat.  Bring back to a boil, cover, reduce
heat to low, and simmer for an hour longer.
