Title: MEAT LOAF FOR 120
Categories: Meats
Yield: 120 Servings
40 lb Ground beef
16    Eggs, lightly beaten
8 c  Old-fashioned oats
5 c  Tomato juice
3 lg Onions, chopped
1/3 c  Salt
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
3 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Ketchup
6 tb Vinegar
2 tb Prepared mustard
2 tb Brown sugar
Combine beef, eggs, oats, tomato juice, onions, salt and pepper. Form
into 16 loaves. Place in 9"x5"x3" loaf pans. Combine sauce
ingredients; pour 3 tbs. over each loaf. Bake at 350 for 1-1/2 to 2
hours or until no pink remains, basting once with remaining sauce.
