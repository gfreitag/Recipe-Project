Title: MR. FOOD'S NOT-ROLLED "ROLLED" CABBAGE
Categories: Appetizers, Hamburger
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/4 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 md Head of cabbage, shredded
- (12 to 14 cups)
16 oz Jellied or whole berry
- cranberry sauce
28 oz Spaghetti sauce
1 tb Lemon juice
5    Gingersnap cookies, crumbled
- (about 1/4 cup crumbs)
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a medium-sized bowl, combine ground beef,
bread crumbs, egg, salt, and pepper. Form the mixture into 1-inch
meatballs (about 1 tbsp. each). Place half the shredded cabbage in a
large pot, then add the meatballs. Spread the cranberry sauce over
the meat
