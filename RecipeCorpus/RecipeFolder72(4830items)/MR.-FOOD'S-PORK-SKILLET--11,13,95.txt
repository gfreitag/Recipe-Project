Title: MR. FOOD'S PORK SKILLET- 11/13/95
Categories: Pork
Yield: 4 Servings
**Heart4Hom@aol com**
2 tb Butter
4    Pork chops -- 3/4-inch
Thick
1    Medium-sized  onion --
Chopped
1    Garlic clove -- minced
1 cn Italian tomatoes -- (14 1/2
Ounces)
1 ts Italian seasoning
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat and brown
the pork chops for 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the chops from the
skillet and cover to keep warm. Add the onion, bell pepper, and
garlic to the skillet and saute for 3-5 minutes, until tender and
lightly browned. Stir in the tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and
black pepper. Return the pork chops to the skillet; reduce the heat
to low, cover, and simmer for about 30 minutes or until the chops are
cooked through.
