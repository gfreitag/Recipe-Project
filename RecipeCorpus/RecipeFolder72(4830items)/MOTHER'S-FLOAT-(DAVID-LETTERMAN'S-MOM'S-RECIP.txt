Title: MOTHER'S FLOAT (DAVID LETTERMAN'S MOM'S RECIP
Categories: Desserts, Dairy, Dkuhnen msn
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
Pinch of salt
1 tb Butter
1    Egg, separated
2 c  Milk
1 ts Lemon extract
1 tb Granulated sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
In a small saucepan, combine flour, 1/2 cup sugar, salt, butter, egg
yolk and milk.  Over medium heat, bring to a boil. Add lemon extract.
Set aside to cool slightly. In a small bowl, beat the egg white until
stiff. Fold half the egg white into the pudding mixture. To the
remaining beaten egg white, add 1 tbsp. sugar and vanilla. Pour
pudding into small casserole dish.  With a teaspoon, drop lumps of
beaten egg white on pudding, and brown in 350f oven for 5 to 10
minutes. Chill and serve. Source: TV Guide, May 11, 1996.
