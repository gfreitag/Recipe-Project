Title: MR. FOOD'S PINEAPPLE CREAM CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
18 1/2 oz Yellow cake mix
2    Eggs
11 oz Mandarin oranges, drained
15 oz Crushed pineapple,
- juice reserved
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
12 oz Frozen whipped topping,
- thawed
2    Packages (4-serving size)
- instant vanilla pudding
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. In a large
bowl, combine the cake mix, eggs, mandarin oranges, reserved
pineapple juice, and mayonnaise. With an electric beater on medium
speed, beat for about 5 minutes or until blended. Pour into a 9" X
13" baking dish
