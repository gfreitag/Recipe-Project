Title: MARMALADE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
1 c  Flour
3 ts Baking-powder
1 c  Ground suet
1/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Orange marmalade
3    Eggs, well beaten
3/4 ts Salt
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking-powder and salt. Combine
crumbs, sugar, eggs, suet, marmalade, and milk. Add dry ingredients.
Mix thoroughly.  Fill well-oiled pudding molds 2/3 full. Cover. Steam
3 hours. Serve hot with any desired sauce. 6 servings.

Grace Viall Gray, Glen Ellyn, IL.
