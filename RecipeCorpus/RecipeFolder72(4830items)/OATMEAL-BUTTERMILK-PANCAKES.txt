Title: OATMEAL BUTTERMILK PANCAKES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 16 Pancakes
2 c  Quick Quaker Oats; uncooked
1/2 ts Soda
2 1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 c  Sifted all-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
2 tb Sugar
1/3 c  Melted or liquid shortening
2    Eggs; beaten
Add oats and soda to buttermilk.  Let stand 5 minutes. Sift together
flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Add sifted dry ingredients,
shortening and eggs to oats mixture. Stir until combined.

For each pancake, pour about 1/4 cup batter onto hot, lightly greased
griddle.  Bake to a golden brown, turning only once. Serve hot with
butter and syrup.

Makes 14 to 16 pancakes.
