Title: MARIE'S BAKED BEANS
Categories: Beans
6 lb Small White Beans
Yield: 30 Servings
2 lb Brown Sugar
1 1/2 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Catsup
3 lb Bacon
Onions
Salt And Pepper -- To Taste
:
:

Cover beans and let stand overnight. Do Not Drain.

Add teaspoon Salt. Slice Bacon 1/2" X  1" thick. Combine in Large
Roaster and bake at 300 degrees for 5 to 7 hours.

Stir occassionally. Add Liquid as needed ( use a combination of Water
and Catsup, Water and Brown Sugar, or Water and Molasses).
