Title: MEDITERRANEAN TOMATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Ethnic, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
4 ea Tomatoes, thinly sliced
1 lg Cucumber
1/4 c  Black olives, sliced
1/4 c  Scallions, thinly sliced
1 c  Celery, sliced
1/4 c  Mint, chopped
3 tb Parsley, chopped
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Lemon juice
3/4 ts Salt
Black pepper, to taste
Place tomatoes in a large serving dish.  Peel the cucumber. Halve
lengthwise, seed & slice thinly.  Add to the tomatoes. Add the
remaining salad ingredients, toss & set aside. DRESSING: Whisk
together all the dressing ingredients in a small bowl. Pour this
dressing over the salad, toss gently & refrigerate until ready to use.
