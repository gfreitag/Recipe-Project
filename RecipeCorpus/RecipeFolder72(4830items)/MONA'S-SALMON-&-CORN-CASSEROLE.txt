Title: MONA'S SALMON & CORN CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Half-and-half
1 t  Onion -- instant minced
1/8 t  Pepper
1 cn Corn -- whole kernel(12oz)
1 cn Salmon -- drained(7.75oz)
3 oz Cheddar cheese -- shredded
1. Beat eggs in a medium-size bowl; add remaining ingredients except
cheese. 2. Mix well. 3. Pour into a well-greased 1 1/2-quart
casserole; sprinkle with cheese. 4. Bake in preheated 350'F. oven 45
to 50 minutes, or until firm.
