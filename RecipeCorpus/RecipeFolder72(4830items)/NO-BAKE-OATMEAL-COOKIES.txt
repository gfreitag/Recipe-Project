Title: NO BAKE OATMEAL COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Stick of margarine
2 c  Sugar
4 tb Cocoa
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Peanut butter
2 1/2 c  Minute oats
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
Boil margarine, sugar, cooca and milk for 1 1/2 minutes. Add peanut
butter, oats and chopped nuts. Mix well. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto
waxed paper. Let set until cooled. Randy Rigg
