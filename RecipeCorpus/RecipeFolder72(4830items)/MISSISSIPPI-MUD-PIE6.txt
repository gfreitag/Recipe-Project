Title: MISSISSIPPI MUD PIE6
Categories: Pies
Yield: 4 Servings
Crust:
2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine--softene
Filling:
12 oz Cool Whip
3/4 c  Sugar
8 oz Cream cheese--softened
1 sm Package instant
Pudding--chocolate
1 sm Package instant
Pudding--butterscotch
3 c  Milk
Thoroughly blend crust ingredients.<br> Press firmly in bottom of pan.
Blend together half<br> of the cool whip, sugar, and cream cheese.
Spread<br> mixture on top of crust. Whip together the puddings<br>
and mllk and spread on top of cream cheese mixture.<br> Top with
remaining cool whip
