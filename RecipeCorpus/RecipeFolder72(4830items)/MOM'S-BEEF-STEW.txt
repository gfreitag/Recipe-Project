Title: MOM'S BEEF STEW
Categories: Beef, Mexican, Vegetables, Soups/stews
Yield: 6 Servings
4 tb Oil
3 tb Wine Vinegar
1 1/2 lb Beef Stew Meat
1 c  Red Wine
1 md Onion Minced
1    Bay Leaf
1    Clove Garlic, Minced
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 c  Tomato Sauce
4 md Potatoes Cubed
2    Carrots, Sliced
Salt And Pepper To Taste
Saute onion and garlic in oil. Add every thing except potatoes and
carrots to large pot. Cover tightly and simmer 1 1/2 hours until meat
is thoroughly tender. Half an hour before meat is ready add carrots
and potatoes.
