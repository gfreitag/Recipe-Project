Title: MARINATED VEG - (STFM57B)
Categories: Vegetables, Marinade
Yield: 2 Servings
1 ea CAROL MILTON
4 c  Vegetables ;assorted fresh
1/4 c  Lemon juice ;from concentrat
1/4 c  Oil
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Oregano ;or thyme leaves
1/4 ts Pepper
Place vegetables in shallow dish, in small bowl or jar, mix remaining
ingredients; pour over vegetables. Cover. Refrigerate 6 hours.
Stirring occasionaly. SUGGESTED VEGETABLES: Cauliflower, carrots,
Mushroom, Cherry Tomatoes, Brussel sprout, Broccoli flowerette,
Zucchine, Onions,Cucumbers. TIPS: Recipe can be double. Hope this
helps one who ask for this recipe.
