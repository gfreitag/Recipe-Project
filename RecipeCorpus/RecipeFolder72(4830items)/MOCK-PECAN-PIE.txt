Title: MOCK PECAN PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Pie
2    Eggs
1 c  Corn syrup
1 c  Sugar, granulated
1/4 c  Butter; melted or margarine
1 c  Rolled oats, quick cooking
1    Pie shell; 9", unbaked
Beat eggs in small mixing bowl. Beat in next 4 ingredients. Pour into
pie shell. Bake on bottom shelf in 350F oven about 60 minutes until
set.
