Title: NORTH CAROLINA BBQ BASTING SAUCE
Categories: Barbecue, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Cider vinegar
1 tb Tabasco sauce
2 tb Paprika
3 tb Salt
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Chili powder
3 tb Ground pepper
1 c  Water
Recipe by: NC Pork Producers Association Combine all ingredients and
let sit for an hour or more at room temperature
