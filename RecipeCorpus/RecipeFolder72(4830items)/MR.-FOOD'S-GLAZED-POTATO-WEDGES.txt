Title: MR. FOOD'S GLAZED POTATO WEDGES
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Butter
1 ts Butter
1 tb Unsweetened orange juice
1 ts Unsweetened orange juice
1 1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
2    To 3 medium-sized sweet
- potatoes (Peeled, boiled
- and each cut lengthwise
- into 8 wedges)
Orange Slices (optional)
=======================================================
============== ==== == Place the butter in an 8-inch square
microwaveable baking dish and microwave on high power for 30 seconds
or until melted. Stir in the orange juice and cinnamon. Add the
potatoes, tossing to coat. Cover with waxed paper. Microwave on high
power for 6-8 mi
