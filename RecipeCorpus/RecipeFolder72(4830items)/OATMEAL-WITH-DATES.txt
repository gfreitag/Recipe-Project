Title: OATMEAL WITH DATES
Categories: Breakfast, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Oats
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Water
1 c  Dates (pitted and halved)
Bring water to a boil.  Add salt.  Add rolled oats slowly. Cook over
low flame in a double boiler.  Cook 45 minutes. When done, add the
dates. Mix thoroughly. Serve with cream and sugar.
