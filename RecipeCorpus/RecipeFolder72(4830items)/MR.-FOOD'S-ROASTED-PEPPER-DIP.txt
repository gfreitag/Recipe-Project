Title: MR. FOOD'S ROASTED PEPPER DIP
Categories: Sauces, Dressings
Yield: 99 Servings
About 2 cups
7 oz Roasted peppers, drained,
- patted dry, and finely
- chopped (about 1/2 cup)
16 oz Low-fat sour cream
1 ts Minced garlic
1 ts Salt
=======================================================
============== ==== == Place all the ingredients in a medium-sized
bowl; beat with an electric beater on medium speed until thoroughly
blended. Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator in an
airtight container until ready to use. NOTE: Great as a tossed salad
dressing or veggie dip, and also good dolloped on cooked chicken or
meat.
