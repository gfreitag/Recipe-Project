Title: MIKES S/S GREEN BEANS *** (KBSG46A)
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Casserole
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Green beans; drained
1 c  Wax beans
1 c  Kidney beans; washed and dr
1 x  Sauce:
2 ea Garlic
1 c  Wine vinegar
1/4 c  Tarragon vinegar
1 c  Wesson oil
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2 ts Italian seasoning; dry
1 ts Celery seed
Layer in a glass bowl: in between layers place thinly sliced Bermuda
or white onion slices. Blend on medium until thick, cover beans with
sauce and refrigerate for best results before serving.
Enjoy..........ML FROM: MICHAEL LEVINE   (KBSG46A)
