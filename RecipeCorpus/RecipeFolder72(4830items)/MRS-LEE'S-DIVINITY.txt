Title: MRS LEE'S DIVINITY
Categories: Candies, Snacks, Holiday
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Water
3/4 c  White karo
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Nuts (chopped)
3    Egg whites
Boil sugar, water and karo to hard ball stage.  While syrup cooks,
beat eggs till stiff peaks form.  Pour syrup over egg whites and heat
til dry looking but still glossy. Add vanilla and nuts. Pour onto
waxed paper and cut with knife or drop by teaspoonsful onto waxed
paper.

Mrs. Minnie Lee, LPN OB - KDH
