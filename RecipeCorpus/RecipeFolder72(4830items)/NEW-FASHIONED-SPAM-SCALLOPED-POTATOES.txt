Title: NEW-FASHIONED SPAM SCALLOPED POTATOES
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
Vegetable cooking spray
1 cn 99% fat-free condensed cream
-of mushroom soup(10 3/4 oz)
1/2 c  Skim milk
1 cn Diced pimiento, drained(2oz)
1/4 ts Pepper
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(12 oz)
1 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Frozen peas
4 1/2 c  Thinly sliced, peeled
-potatoes
2 tb Dry bread crumbs
1 tb Chopped parsley
Heat oven to 350'F. Spray a 2-quart casserole with vegetable cooking
spray. In medium bowl, combine soup, milk, pimiento, and pepper. In
casserole, layer half of each of SPAM, onion, peas, potatoes, and
sauce. Repeat layers. Cover. Bake 60 minutes or until potatoes are
nearly tender. Combine bread crumbs and parsley; sprinkle over
casserole. Bake, uncovered, 15 minutes longer or until potatoes are
tender. Let stand
10    minutes before serving.
