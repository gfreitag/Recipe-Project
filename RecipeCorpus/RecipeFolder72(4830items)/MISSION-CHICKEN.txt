Title: MISSION CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 8 Servings
2 1/2 lb Broiler-fryer chicken --
Quartered
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Salt
1 ts Seasoned salt
6 oz Can frozen orange juice --
Thawed
1/2 c  Water
2 dr Hot pepper sauce
1 c  Seedless grapes -- halved
1/2 c  Slivered almonds -- toasted
In large skillet or slow-cookin gpot with browning unit, brown
chicken in butter.  Combine cinnamon, cloves,, salt, seasoned salt,
orange juice concentrate, water, and hot pepper sauce.  Pour this
mixture over chicken in slow-cooking pot.  Cover and cook on low for
4 to 5 hours or until chicken is tender. Stir in grapes. Place
chicken in serving dish; sprinkle with almonds.  If preferred, pass
sauce in separate bowl or thicken with flour dissolved in small
amount of water.
