Title: MELITZANOKEFTETHES KROKETES  (EGGPLANT CROQUE
Categories: Vegetables, Main dish, Greek
Yield: 1 Servings
Eggplants
Cracker meal
Oil for frying
MMMMM------------------------THE MIXTURE-----------------------------
1 1/2 c  Bread crumbs
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Kefalotiri cheese, grated OR
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese, grated
1 tb Baking powder
2 tb Parsley, chopped
1 ts Onion, chopped
Salt & pepper to taste
Wash the eggplants and pierce in several places with a fork. Boil
in lightly salted water until tender; drain well. Remove skin and mas
the pulp; measure it. Mix 2 c. of eggplant pulp with the amount of
ingredients listed above and blend well.  Shape into croquettes or
patties. Roll in cracker meal, and fry until golden brown.
