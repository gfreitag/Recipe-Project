Title: OATMEAL & DRIED FRUIT COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 24 Servings
2 c  Whole wheat flour
2 c  Rolled oats
1/2 c  Lightly packed brown sugar
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Baking soda
1 c  Chopped, mixed dried fruit
2/3 c  Corn oil
1/2 c  Buttermilk
In large bowl, combine all of the dry ingredients, including the dried
fruit.  Add the oil and buttermilk; mix well. Roll out the mixture
into 1/8" thickness. Use a cookie cutter to cut 3 1/2" circles.
Arrange on oiled cookie sheets with a spatula.  Bake in 350F oven for
15-20 minutes. Allow to cool on wire rack.
