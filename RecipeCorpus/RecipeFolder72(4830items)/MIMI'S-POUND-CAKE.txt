Title: MIMI'S POUND CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Butter (NO subst)
5    Eggs
2 c  Cake flour
1 2/3 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla
Cream butter and sugar. Drop in eggs one at a time, stirring after
each. Fold in flour and mix well. Add vanilla. Bake in 350 degree
over for about 50 minutes. Mrs. Charles E. Phillips
