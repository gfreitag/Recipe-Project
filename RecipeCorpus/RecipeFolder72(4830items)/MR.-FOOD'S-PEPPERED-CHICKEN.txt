Title: MR. FOOD'S PEPPERED CHICKEN
Categories: Meats, Chicken
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ea Chicken, about 2 1/2 pounds,
- cut into 8 pieces
3/4 ts Salt
1 tb Coarse ground black pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Butter
1/2 c  Chicken broth
2 tb Dry white wine or
- apple juice
=======================================================
============== ==== == Pierce all sides of the chicken with a fork.
In a small bowl, combine the salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Rub the
chicken with the pepper mixture, cover, and chill for 30 minutes. In
a large skillet, heat the oil and butter over medium heat. Add the
chic NOTE: Serve over hot cooked rice or noodles, if desired.
