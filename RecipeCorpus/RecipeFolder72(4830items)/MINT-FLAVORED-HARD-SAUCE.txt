Title: MINT FLAVORED HARD SAUCE
Categories: Desserts, Seasonings, Sauces
Yield: 1 Batch
MMMMM-------------------------HARD SAUCE------------------------------
1    Stick unsalted butter
-- softened
2 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1/8 ts French Cognac Vanilla
1 tb Mint flavored syrup
MMMMM--------------------MINT FLAVORED SYRUP-------------------------
1/4 c  ;Water
1/4 c  Spearmint leaves; packed
1/2 c  Sugar
To make the hard sauce, cream butter well.  Add sugar gradually. Beat
until very creamy.  Add vanilla and mint syrup; blend well.

To make the mint flavored syrup, combine water and sugar and bring to
a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes. Add mint leaves to syrup.
Bruise leaves well by mashing with a spoon. Cover and let steep for 4
hours. Remove leaves and strain syrup.

From Bessie Hulett in _Bessie's Blue Ribbon Recipes_ by Bessie Hulett.
Shively, KY: 1992.  Pg. 110.  Typed for you by Cathy Harned.
