Title: MEAT LOAF VARIANTS
Categories: Beef, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Ground beef
4 ea Bacon strips
1 c  Bread crumbs
2 ea Eggs, beaten
1 ea Onion, small chopped
1 tb Parsley
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
1/2 c  Milk
Combine all ingredients except bacon. Place in loaf pan with bacon on
top. Bake 45 minutes at 350 deg: 1. Replace half beef with covered
veal or pork. Add
1/2    cup* parmesan
cheese + chopped olives. 2. Replace milk with tomato sauce, tomato
soup or 1/2 cup* catsup and
water. 3. Replace some beef with chopped ham, lamb or chicken. 4.
Add cooked peas, carrots or other vegetables. *quantity for standard
serving size.
