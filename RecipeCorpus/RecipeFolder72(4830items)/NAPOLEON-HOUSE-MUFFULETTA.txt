Title: NAPOLEON HOUSE MUFFULETTA
Categories: Sandwiches, Cajun, Ethnic
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------SOUTHERN LIVING 1994 ANNUAL---------------------
2 sl Ham (about 2 oz)
3 sl Genoa salami (about 2-1/2oz)
2 sl Pastrami (about 2 oz)
1/4 c  Italian Olive Salad
1 sl Provolone cheese
1 sl Swiss cheese
5    Inch sandwich bun with
Sesame seeds
Layer first 6 ingredients on bottom half of bun; top with remaining
bun half, and wrap in aluminum foil. Bake at 350~ for 20 minutes or
until sandwich is thoroughly heated. MM Norma Wrenn npxr56b
