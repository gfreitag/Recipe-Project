Title: MUFFALLATAS & OLIVE DRESSING FROM: MY LOVIN
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-------------------------MUFFALLATA------------------------------
1    Round bread
American Cheese
Provolone Cheese
Sliced Ham
Sliced Genoa Salami
MMMMM-----------------------OLIVE DRESSING----------------------------
1 qt Salad Olives
16 oz Holland Onions
11 oz Pickled Cauliflower
9    Ribs Celery
1    Head Garlic
3 oz Italian Seasoning
Olive Oil
MUFFALLATA: Slice bread in half like a hamburger bun. Alternate
layers of meat and cheese. Bake in 350 deg. oven for 15 min. or until
cheese melts. Cut in quarters and serve with olive dressing. OLIVE
DRESSING: Mash up olives and onions. Mix in everything but the olive
oil. Put mixture back into jars and cover with olive oil. Let stand
over night. Also good on other sandwiches.
