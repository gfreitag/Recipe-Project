Title: MOM'S BLUEBERRY MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Cups flour
2    Eggs, unbeaten
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
3 ts Baking powder
1 c  Milk
1 ts Salt
2 c  Blueberries
** Mix dry ingredients together.  Add eggs, oil, and milk, mix well.
Stir in blueberries.(Best to use fresh berries as the batter can turn
purple when frozen ones are used.)
