Title: MY MUSHROOM BROCCOLI SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 4 Servings
6 tb Oil
4 tb Butter
2    Onions *
6    Mushrooms**
3 tb Flour
1 ts Nutmeg
10 c  Chicken stock
3/4 c  35% cream
Salt and pepper
1    Bunch of broccoli***
peeled and chopped ** washed and chopped *** chopped
*

In a medium pan, heat oil and butter and saute onions and mushrooms.
5 min Add flour, stir well and add rest of ingredients except cheese.
Let simmer over medium heat for 8-10 minutes. Add cheese, mix well
and serve.
