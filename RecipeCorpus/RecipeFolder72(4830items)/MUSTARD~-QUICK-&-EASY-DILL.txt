Title: MUSTARD~ QUICK & EASY DILL
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Dijon Mustard
1 tb Fresh chopd. Dill
3 tb Olive oil
Combine one 8 - oz. jar Dijon mustard with 1 tablespoon fresh chopped
dill or 1 teaspoon dried dill weed and 3 tablespoons of olive oil.

Serve with pate or fish; or use as a sandwich spread. Also good added
to a salad dressing.

NOTE: Homemade mustards must be stored in the refrigerator. SOURCE:
Gifts from The Kitchen SHARED BY:Jim Bodle 8/92
