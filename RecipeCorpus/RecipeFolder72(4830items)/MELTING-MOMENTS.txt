Title: MELTING MOMENTS
Categories: Desserts, Toppings
Yield: 30 Servings
10 oz Butter
2 oz Confectioner's sugar
8 oz Sifted flour
2 oz Cornstarch
Melting Moments

lemon curd or thick honey

Cream the butter and sugar until very light.  Add flour and cornstarch
gradually, mixing well.  Put small spoonfuls onto greased cookie
sheets and bake for about 15 minutes at 350 F. Cool on a rack, and
when cool, sandwich together with a little lemon curd or thick honey
in between. Makes about 30.
