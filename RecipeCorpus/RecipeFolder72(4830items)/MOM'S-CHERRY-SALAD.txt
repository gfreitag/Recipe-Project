Title: MOM'S CHERRY SALAD
Categories: Desserts, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 qt Cherries, pitted & drained
1 c  Walnuts
1 tb Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Minature marshmellows
1    Egg, beaten
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
Mix egg, flour, sugar and salt.  Add milk and cook until thick, about
10-15 minutes on low heat.  Pour over cherries, marshmellows and
walnuts. Refrigerate. Will keep 4-5 days.

Bananas and pineapple can be added if salad is to be eaten right away.
