Title: MINUTE A LA KING
Categories: Fish, Main dish, Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c  Milk
1 cn Tuna; drained and flaked
1 cn Peas (8 oz); drained
Toast triangles
Heat soup and milk.  Add tuna and peas.  Bring to a boil and simmer a
minute.  Serve hot over toast points.

From:  The Tuna Cookbook
