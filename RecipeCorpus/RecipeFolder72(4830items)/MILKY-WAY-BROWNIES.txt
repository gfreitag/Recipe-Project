Title: MILKY WAY BROWNIES
Categories: Brownies, Candy bars
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
4    Milky Way candy bars,
-(2.23 oz. each)
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
3/4 c  Flour
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
Stir butter and sliced Milky Way bars in medium saucepan over low heat
until smooth; using a wire wisk if necessary. Remove from heat and
stir in sugar and vanilla. Add eggs one at a time beating well after
each. Stir in flour blended with baking powder and salt. Spread into
greased square dish, bake at 350~F for 25 minutes. Cool and cut into
squares, dust with powdered sugar if desired.
