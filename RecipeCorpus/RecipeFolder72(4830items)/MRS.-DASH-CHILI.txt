Title: MRS. DASH CHILI
Categories: Chili
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Chili tomatoes (15 oz)
1 cn Red kidney beans, undrained
-(15 1/2 oz)
2 tb Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herb
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Cumin
Grated cheese (opt)
Chopped onion (opt)
In medium saucepan, combine all ingredients. Simmer over medium heat
for 5-10 minutes. Garnish with grated cheese and onions if desired.
