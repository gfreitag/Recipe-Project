Title: MULLED HOLIDAY CIDER
Categories: Beverages, Seasonings
Yield: 5 Cups
32 oz Apple juice
10    Pineapple sage leaves
15    Lemon verbena leaves
1/8 ts Aniseed, ground
1/4 ts Ground cloves
Cinnamon sticks
-- 1 for the recipe,
-- and one for each guest
1/2 c  Pineapple
-- canned (unsweetened)
-- OR fresh
1/2 c  Pears (peeled, diced)
In a noncorrodible pot, bring apple juice to a boil, then reduce heat
to medium and add remaining ingredients. Simmer for 30 minutes, or
longer for stronger flavor.  Remove the cinnamon stick, fruit, and
herb leaves. Serve with a cinnamon stick in each mug.

Makes 1-1/4 quarts
