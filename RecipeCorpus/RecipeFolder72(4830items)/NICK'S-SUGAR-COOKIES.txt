Title: NICK'S SUGAR COOKIES
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 5 Dozen +/-
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Shortening
2    Eggs
2 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Cream of tartar
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Mix all ingredients together. Form into balls. Flatten with a glass
bottom dipped in sugar. Bake at 350F for 8-10 minutes on an ungreased
cookie sheet.
