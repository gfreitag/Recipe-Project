Title: NEW YORK STYLE CHEESECAKE
Categories: Cheesecake, Cheesecakes
Yield: 16 Servings
MMMMM-------------------------SEE BELOW------------------------------
Use Basic Cheesecake recipe

This variation, best with a graham cracker crust, if flavored with
sugar-laced sour cream and lemon zest. Add 1 tbs grated lemon zest to
batter.  Bake as directed. Remove from oven and water bath. In bowl
combine 1 cup sour cream, 2 tbs sugar and 1 tsp lemon zest. Spread
mixture over top of cheesecake.  Return to oeven until topping is
set, 6-8 minutes. Garnish with lemon zest curls. Source: Women's
World Nov.5, 1996 Typos by Sarah Gruenwald sitm@ekx.infi.net
