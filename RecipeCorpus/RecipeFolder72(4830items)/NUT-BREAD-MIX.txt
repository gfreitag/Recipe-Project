Title: NUT BREAD MIX
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
9 c  All-purpose flour*
1/4 c  Baking powder
1 tb Salt
1 c  Shortening
Mix flour, baking powder and salt. Cut in shortening completely until
particles are size of coarse cornmeal. Refrigerate in airtight
container up to 1 month. 10 cups mix. *If using self-rising flour,
omit baking powder and salt.
