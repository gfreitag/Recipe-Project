Title: NEW BAKED BEANS
Categories: Beans, Four star
Yield: 1 Servings
3 cn Beans *
1/2    Onion -- diced
1    Bell Pepper -- diced
1 1/2 c  Salsa
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
1 lb Ground Turkey -- cooked
1 ts Mustard Powder
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
Preheat oven to 350 Crumble and cook ground turkey. Combine all
ingredients in baking dish and cook 1 hour or until done. * use any
kind of beans, ie. pork and beans, kidney beans.
