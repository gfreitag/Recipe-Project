Title: MELTING MOMENTS COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
6 tb Confectioner's sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 tb Cornstarch
3/4 c  Flour -- sifted
pn Salt
Cream butter and sugar; stir in sifted dry ingredients. Roll into
small balls about 3/4 inch in diameter. Place on baking sheet and
flatten. Bake 15 minutes at 300F.

: Bell's Best #2 (Mississippi)
