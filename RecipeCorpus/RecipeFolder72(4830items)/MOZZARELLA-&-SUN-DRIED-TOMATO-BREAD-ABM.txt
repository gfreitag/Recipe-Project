Title: MOZZARELLA & SUN-DRIED TOMATO BREAD ABM
Categories: Breads
Yield: 24 Servings
MMMMM-----------------BEST BREAD MACHINE CB EVER----------------------
2 1/2 ts Yeast
1 ts Garlic powder
2 3/4 c  Flour, bread
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1/2 tb Salt
2 tb Olive oil
7/8 c  Water; warm
1/2 c  Mozzarella, shredded
1/2 c  Sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
Drain tomatoes well, reserving oil to use in bread. Bring all
ingredients to room temperature.  Pour all ingredients into bakery,
in order. Set "baking control" at eleven o'clock. Select "white
bread" and push Start.

Tested in DAK 1-1/2 lb R2D2 bread machine.  Sylvia's comment:

Nutritional information per slice: 67 cal, 1 gm fat, 1 mg
cholesterol, 147 mg sodium, 19% calories from fat.
