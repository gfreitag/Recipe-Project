Title: MUSHROOM FAT-FREE DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Low-fat
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 lb Fresh mushrooms
3/4 c  Fat-Free Dressing
1/2 c  Red wine
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1 tb Sugar
2 ea Garlic cloves
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Water
Combine mushrooms and dressing in a skillet and cook until mushrooms
are just tender, about 5 minutes.

Dressing Combine vinegar and salt; stir until salt is completely
dissolved. Add pepper, sugar, garlic, Worcestershire, Dijon and lemon
juice; mix well. Add water and mix well again. Refrigerate to store.
