Title: MOM'S APPLE CRISP
Categories: Pampered, Chef
Yield: 8 Servings
6 lg Granny Smith apple
8    Graham cracker squares
3/4 c  Brown sugar; packed
1/2 c  Rolled oats
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Butter or margarine; melted
Preheat oven to 350.  Peel, core and slice apples using Apple
Peeler/Corer/Slicer.  Prepare enough apples to fill Deep Dish Baker
3/4 full.  Finely chop graham crackers with Food Chopper.  Combine
crumbs, sugar, oats, flour and spices.  Add butter; mix well.  Spoon
mixture over apple slices. Bake 30-35 minutes. Cool slightly. Serve
with whipped cream or ice cream. - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
Contributor: The Pampered Chef
