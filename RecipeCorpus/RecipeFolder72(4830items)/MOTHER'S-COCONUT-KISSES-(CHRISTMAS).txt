Title: MOTHER'S COCONUT KISSES (CHRISTMAS)
Categories: Cookies, Holidays
Yield: 1 Batch
1/2 ts Salt
4 ea Egg whites
1 1/4 c  Granulated sugar; superfine
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Almond extract
2 c  Shredded coconut
Red & Green candied cherry>>
-halves.
Oven 350~.

Add salt to egg whites and beat until stiff, but not dry. Add
sugar, 1 tb. at a time, beating until granules are dissolved. Add
vanilla and coconut, mixing lightly. Drop by teaspoonsful onto
ungreased brown paper on cookie sheets.  Put a cherry half on each
cookie. Bake in moderate oven at 350~ for about 20 min. Slip paper
onto wet tabletop or board. Let stand for 2-3 min.  Loosen cookies
with spatual and remove to wire racks to cool. Store in tin box
between layers of waxed paper.
