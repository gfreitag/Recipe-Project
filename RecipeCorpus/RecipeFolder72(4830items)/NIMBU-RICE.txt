Title: NIMBU RICE
Categories: Vegetables, Vegetarian, Indian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Rice
2 tb Oil
1 ts Mustard seeds
1 ts Urad dal
1 ts Chana dal
1    Cashews; handful
1    Raisins; handful
1 pn Turmeric
1 c  Mixed vegetables
1    Lemon
Cook rice. Heat oil and add all other ingredients except for the
lemon. Fry as appropriate. Mix stuff in pan with rice. Squeeze the
lemon and add the juice to the rice.
