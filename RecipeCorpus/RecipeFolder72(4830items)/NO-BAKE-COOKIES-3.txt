Title: NO-BAKE COOKIES 3
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Milk
1/3 c  Cocoa
ds Salt
3 c  Oatmeal
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Peanut Butter
Combine first 5 ingredients in saucepan.  Cook and stir over med. heat
until mixture boils. Remove from heat and add remaining ingredients.
Stir until thick. Working quickly, drop by tablespoons onto waxed
paper or foil. Refrigerate.
