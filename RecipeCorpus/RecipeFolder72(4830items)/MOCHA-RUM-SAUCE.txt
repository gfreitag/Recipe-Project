Title: MOCHA RUM SAUCE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 3 Cups
3/4 c  Unsalted butter
1 1/3 c  Sugar
1 1/3 c  Whipping cream
1/2 c  Cocoa
3 tb Dark rum, divided
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb + 1 t instant coffee
1 ts Vanilla extract
Melt butter in a heavy saucepan over medium heat. Stir in sugar,
whipping cream, cocoa, 2 tablespoons rum and salt. Bring to a boil;
reduce heat, and cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from
heat, and stir in coffee granules, vanilla and remaining 1 tablespoon
rum. Cover and refrigerate. Let stand at room temperature 30 minutes
before serving.
