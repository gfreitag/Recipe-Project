Title: MARINADE FOR BBQ CHICKEN & PORK RIBS
Categories: Sauce/gravy, Barbecue, Phillipines
Yield: 1 Servings
1 sm Onion
2 ea Garlic cloves (minced)
1 c  White vinegar
1/2 c  Soy sauce
Salt and Pepper (to taste)
Marinade chicken pieces (fryer) or pork ribs overnight, then
barbeque. I usually make two batches of marinade for pork ribs and
chicken together. marinade for BBQ chicken and pork ribs!
