Title: NZKHTUN (EGGPLANT CAVIAR)
Categories: Appetizer, Persian
Yield: 5 Servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2 ea Medium eggplants
1/2 c  Chopped parsley
2 ea Clove garlic
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Dry mint
MMMMM------------------------PREPARATION-----------------------------
Wash and bake the eggplants in 350ø oven for 1 hour or until done.
When done, remove the skin and chop the eggplants. Add chopped
parsley and mix well.  Squeeze garlic into the eggplant mixture, add
the seasoning and vinegar, and mix well.  Let stay for a few hours
before serving.

Source:  In a Persian Kitchen typed by Leonard Smith
