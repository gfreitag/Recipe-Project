Title: MR. FOOD'S SWEET ONION TART
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Butter
1 lb To 1 1/4 sweet onions,
- thinly sliced
3/4 c  Sour cream
2    Eggs, beaten
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1 1/2 c  6 ounces shredded 3 cheese
- blend, divided
1    9 inch unbaked pie shell
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 400 F. In a large skillet,
melt the butter and cook the sliced onions over medium-high heat for
15 minutes,stirring frequently until tender and just slightly golden.
Remove from heat and drain any excess liquid. Place in a medium-sized
NOTE: If desired, add 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg to the sour cream
mixture.
