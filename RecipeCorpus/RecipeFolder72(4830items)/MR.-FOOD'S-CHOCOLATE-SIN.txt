Title: MR. FOOD'S CHOCOLATE SIN
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 10 Servings
1 ea 1-pound chocolate pound
- cake, cut crosswise into
- 1/4-inch slices
2 pk (4-serving size) instant
- chocolate pudding and
- pie filling
3 c  Milk
12 oz Frozen whipped topping,
- thawed
1 c  Walnuts
1 1/4 c  Coarsely chopped Chocolate
- sandwich cookies
=======================================================
============== ==== == Layer half of the pound cake slices in a 9" x
13" glass baking dish. In a large bowl combine the chocolate pudding
mix and milk and blend with a wire whisk until thickened. Fold in
half of the whipped topping. Place half of the mixture over the pound
cake
