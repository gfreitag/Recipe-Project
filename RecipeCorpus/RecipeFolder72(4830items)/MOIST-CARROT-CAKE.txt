Title: MOIST CARROT CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
2 c  Brown sugar
3 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Chopped walnuts
4    Eggs
1 ts Salt
1 c  Oil
3 c  Grated carrot
MMMMM---------------------------ICING--------------------------------
100 g  Cream cheese
50 g  Butter
1 ts Vanilla essence
2 c  Icing sugar
Mix all dry ingredients together in bowl.  Add 1 cup oil and beaten
eggs then grated carrot and walnuts. Mix well. Pour into baking tin.
Bake in moderate oven for 1 hour. To make icing mix together the
following:

100gm cream cheese 50 gm butter 1 tsp vanilla essence
2    cups icing sugar

Enjoy!

Karlene Robinson.
