Title: MOM'S SOFT RAISIN COOKIES
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Water
2 c  Raisins
1 c  Shortening
1 3/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs, lightly beaten
1 ts Vanilla extract
3 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
Combine raisins and water in a small saucepan; bring to a boil. Cook
for 3 minutes; remove from the heat and let cool (do not drain). In a
mixing bowl, cream shortening; gradually add sugar. Add eggs and
vanilla. Combine dry ingredients; gradually add to creamed mixture
and blend thoroughly. Stir in nuts and raisins. Drop by teaspoonfuls
2 in. apart on greased baking sheets. Bake at 350! for 12-14 minutes.
Yield: about 6 dozen.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
