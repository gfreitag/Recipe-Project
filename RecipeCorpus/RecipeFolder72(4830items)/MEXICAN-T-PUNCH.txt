Title: MEXICAN T PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Mexican
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Tequila
2 c  Tea; strong, cold
1 c  Pinapple juice
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  ;Water
1/4 c  Lime juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon; ground
1 1/2 ts Aromatic bitters
Mix all ingredients; refrigerate until chilled.  Stir before serving.
Serve over ice.
