Title: MOCK CHEESECAKE
Categories: Miamiherald, Desserts, Vegetarian, Cheesecakes
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Tofu
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Baked 9-inch graham cracker
-crust
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Put tofu, lemon juice, salt, honey,
vanilla, and sugar in food processor or blender and process until
smooth and creamy. Pour mixture into a baked pie crust. Bake 40
minutes, Cool, then chill.

Per serving: 376 cal; 10g pro, 41g carb, 21g fat (48%)

Source: Cook's Corner, Miami Herald, 9/5/96 formatted by Lisa Crawford
