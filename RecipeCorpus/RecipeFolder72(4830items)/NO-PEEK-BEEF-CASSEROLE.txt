Title: NO PEEK BEEF CASSEROLE
Categories: Try it, Crockpot, Beef
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM-----------------RIVAL CROCK-POT COOK BOOK----------------------
2 lb Stew beef,
Cut into 1 -inch pieces
1 3/8 oz Dry onion soup mix
1/2 c  Red wine
10 1/2 oz Cream of mushroom soup
4 oz Whole mushrooms
Combine all ingredients in CROCK-POT. Stir together well Cover and
cook on Low 8 to 12 hours. Serve over noodles or rice. (High: 3 to 4
hours). This recipe may be doubled.
