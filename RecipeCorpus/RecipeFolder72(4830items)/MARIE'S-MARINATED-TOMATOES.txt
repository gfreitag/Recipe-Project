Title: MARIE'S MARINATED TOMATOES
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Canola oil
3 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 ts Vegetarian Worcestershire
-- sauce
3/4 ts Salt
1 pn Black pepper
1 tb Sweetener
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
2 ts Basil, chopped
1 pn Thyme
4 ea Tomatoes, thinly sliced
Whisk together all the ingredients, except the sliced tomatoes, in a
small bowl.  Place the tomatoes in a container. Pour the marinade
over the tomatoes. Cover & refrigerate for at least 1 hour before
serving.
