Title: MOCK TURKEY
Categories: Vegetarian, Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Rolled oats
2 c  Hot water
2    Green onions, minced fine
4    Eggs
2 tb Cooking oil
2 c  Ground nut meats
(walnut prefered)
1 c  Ground sunflower seeds
2 tb Peanut butter
1 ts Poultry seasoning
2 ts Sea kelp
Servings: 6

Pour the hot water over the rolled oats.  Add the rest of the
ingredients. Oil a baking pan and shape the mixture in the form of
turkey, using stalks of celery for the legs and shaping the mixture
around them. Use small oblongs of the mixture for wings.  Pat oil
over the outside and bake in a moderate oven until done in the
middle, probably 45 minutes. Loosen with a spatula and remove to a
meat platter. Garnish and serve hot. Serves 4 to 6
