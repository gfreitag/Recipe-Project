Title: MARINATED CAULIFLOWER
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Head cauliflower
1 md Onion -- chopped fine
1/2 c  Black olives -- chopped
Fine
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Oil
1 cn Anchovies -- chopped fine
1/2 ts Pepper
Break cauliflower into pieces then steam until just tender. Combine
the remaining ingredients and toss with the warm cauliflower. Let
stand for 24 hours.
