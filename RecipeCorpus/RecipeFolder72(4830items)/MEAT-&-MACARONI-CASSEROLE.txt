Title: MEAT & MACARONI CASSEROLE
Categories: Meats, Cheese/eggs, Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings

3    Medium green peppers
3    Medium onions
1/2 lb Mushrooms
3 tb Salad oil
1 1/2 lb Ground bee, veal or pork
28 oz Can tomatoes

and garlic, until all panjuices evaporate, and meat is browned.
