Title: MARINATED FLANK STEAK *** (NXDM05B)
Categories: Bar-b-q, Marinades, Beef
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Consomme; or
1 c  Beef bouillon
1 c  Red wine
2/3 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Green onions; sliced
1 c  Garlic; minced
2 tb Lime juice
2 tb Brown sugar
Score steak on both sides in 1-1/2" squares. Marinate in other stuff
8 hrs. in refrigerator. Grill to desired degree of doneness. Slice
across grain into thin slices. --> Katie in Colorado FROM: KATIE
KINGSTON (NXDM05B)
