Title: MARSHMALLOW TREATS (FAT FREE)
Categories: Snacks, Desserts, Cookies
Yield: 24 Treats
10 oz Marshmallows
6 c  Rice krispies
-
3 tb Molly McButter, butter
- sprinkles
Melt marshmallows over low heat or in microwave. Add butter sprinkles
and stir well. Add cereal and stir well quickly. Press into 9" x 13"
pan coated with cooking spray (Baker's Joy). Enjoy!
