Title: OAT BRAN BREAD SLO-RISE
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
3 tb Honey or Brown Sugar
2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Active Dry Yeast
3 1/2 c  To 4 1/2 cups of
Bread Flour
1 1/2 c  Plus 2 Tbl. Oat Bran
-Oat Bran Cereal or plain
1 lg Egg
Notes: In a large bowl, stir together 2 cups of lukewarm water and
Brown Sugar or Honey. Sprinkle with Yeast and stir until it has
dissolved. Stir in 1 1/2 cups of Oat Bran. Let the mixture stand for
10 minutes to let the Oat Bran absorb liquid. Do all in a KitchenAid
mixer if available. Stir in Salt. 1 cup at a time, mix in flour until
the dough becomes too difficult to stir. Turn out on to a well
floured work surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8
minutes. 4 or 5 minutes on the KitchenAid. Place the dough in a large
well oiled bowl, turn to coat and cover with plastic wrap. Lightly
oil a 5 by 9 inch loaf pan. Gently punch down the dough and transfer
to a well floured work surface. Cover and let stand for 5 to 10
minutes. Press the dough with your hands into a 10 by 8 inch oval.
Beginning at one narrow end, tightly roll up the dough to form a
loaf. Pinch seam edge into dough to seal, then place in the prepared
bread pan, seam side down. Meanwhile, place oven rack in the lower
third of the oven; preheat to 375 degrees F. In a small bowl, whisk
egg white with one tb of water. Brush a little of the egg white
mixture over the top of the loaf and sprinkle with 2 tb Oat Bran, or
sesame seeds just before placing in the oven Makes one loaf, about 18
1/2 inch slices.
