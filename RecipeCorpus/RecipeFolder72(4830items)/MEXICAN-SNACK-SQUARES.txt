Title: MEXICAN SNACK SQUARES
Categories: Crescents, Appetizers
Yield: 30 Servings
2 cn Crescent rolls (8 oz each)
16 oz Can refried beans
1 c  Dairy sour cream
2 tb Taco seasoning mix*
1 1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Chopped green onions
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1 c  Chopped, seeded tomatoes
1/2 c  Sliced ripe olives
Salsa, if desired
*  from 1 1/4 oz. pkg.
~------------------------------------------------------
~--------------- Heat oven to 375F.  Unroll dough into 4 long
rectangles. Place crosswise in ungreased 15x10x1" baking pan; press
over bottom and 1" up sides to form crust.  Firmly press perforations
to seal. Bake at 375F. for 14 to 19 minutes or until golden brown.
Cool completely.

Spread beans over crust to within 1/2" of edges.  In small bowl,
combine sour cream and taco seasoning mix; mix well. Spread sour
cream mixture over beans. Sprinkle cheese, onions, green pepper,
tomatoes and olives evenly over sour cream.  Cover; refrigerate 1
hour. Cut into squares. Serve with salsa..
