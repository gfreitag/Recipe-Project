Title: MEATLESS MISSION CHILI
Categories: Soups, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Safflower oil
1 x  Clove garlic, minced
1 x  Green Bell Pepper, chopped
1 x  Stalk Celery, chopped
1 x  Sm Onion, chopped (1/4 cup)
1 x  Carrot, shredded
1 x  Med Zucchini, shredded
18 oz Can Tomatoes with juice
15 oz Can Kidney Beans,drained
8 oz Can Tomato sauce (1 cup)
1/4 c  Water
1 1/2 ts Chili powder, or to taste
1/4 ts Hot pepper Sauce, to taste
1 ts Basil
1 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Black Pepper
GARNISH: corn, chopped scallions, shredded Cheddar or Monterey jack
cheese, or a combination, optional. In a Dutch oven or 4-5 qt
saucepan, heat oil. Saute garlic, green pepper, celery, onion,
carrot, and zucchini until crisp/tender, about 3 minutes. As mixture
cooks, stir in remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil over high heat,
then reduce heat to medium. Cover and cook until heated through,
about 5 minutes. Top each serving with a garnish, if desired. If you
wish, set under broiler to melt cheese. VARIATIONS: - add 1/2 cup
whole raw cashews - add 1/2 lb firm tofu, cut into 1/2" cubes ~ add 1
cup cooked corn If reheating, add addt'l liquid, such as water,
tomato juice, or veg stock
