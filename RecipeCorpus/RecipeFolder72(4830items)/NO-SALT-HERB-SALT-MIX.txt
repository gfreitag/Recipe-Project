Title: NO-SALT HERB SALT MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
1/4 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Onion Powder
1/4 ts Paprika
1/8 ts Celery Seed
1/4 ts White Pepper
1/4 ts Dry Mustard
1/4 ts Dried Lemon Peel
1/4 ts Black Pepper
Mix all together in a small glass mixing bowl.  Store in a covered
saltshaker or a plastic bag.   Use 1 tsp. for 4 chicken breast pieces.
