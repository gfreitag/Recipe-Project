Title: OLD SALTY'S INSTANT PIZZA
Categories: Main dish, Fish, Kids
Yield: 4 Servings
213 g  Canned Alaska salmon
-(pink or red)
2    Soft rolls, cut in half
8 ts Tomato relish
75 g  Cheddar cheese, grated
MMMMM-------------------------TO GARNISH------------------------------
Olives
Sliced gherkins
Sweetcorn kernels
Drain the can of salmon. Flake the fish.

Spread each half of the rolls with 2 teaspoons of tomato relish.
Arrange the salmon on top. Sprinkle with cheese and garnish. Toast
under a moderate grill until the cheese has melted and the filling
has heated through. Serve immediately.

Serves 4. Approx. 210 kcals per serving
