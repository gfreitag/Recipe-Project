Title: MR. FOOD'S QUICK BLACK BEAN SOUP 12/5/95
Categories: Soups
2 cn black beans -- drained
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn chicken broth -- condensed
1/2 c  water
1 sm onion -- quartered
2    garlic cloves -- minced
1 1/2 ts ground cumin
1/2 ts dried oregano
2 TB green bell pepper --
chopped;optional
:
4 ts sour cream -- (optional)

Place the beans, chicken broth, water, onion, garlic, cumin, and
oregano in a blender or a food processor. Process the ingredients for
1 minute; turn off the machine and wait until the blades stop, then
scrape down the sides of the container. Process the mixture for
another minute, until smooth. Transfer the mixture to a medium-sized
saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer,
uncovered, for 15 minutes, stirring frequently. Place in serving
bowls and garnish with the chopped bell pepper and sour cream, if
desired.

Yield: about 5 cups
