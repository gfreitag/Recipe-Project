Title: MARIE CALLENDAR'S CAKE LIKE CORN BREAD
Categories: Breads, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
Van Geffen VGHC42A
1    Box corn muffin mix -- (9
Ounces)
1    Box yellow cake mix -- (9
Ounces)
Prepare corn muffin mix just as box directs. Set aside. In another
bowl prepare yellow cake mix per box directions. Pour prepared cake
mix into prepared corn muffin mix and stirwell. Turn batter into
greased 9x12x2 pan. Bake at 350~ for 30 to 35 minutes or till
toothpick comes out clean. Serve warm with Honey Butter. Source:
Gloria Pitzer's Secret Fast Food Recipes
