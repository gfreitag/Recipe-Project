Title: MOCK KISHKE
Categories: Side dishes, Jewish, Passover
Yield: 3 Servings
3 c  Matzoh Meal
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 lg Carrots
3 ea Celery stalks
1 ea Spanish Onion
1/3 c  To 1/2 c Margarine; melted
MOCK KISHKE

Makes 3 Rolls

INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Preheat oven to 350F 2. Combine matzoh meal, salt and pepper. 3.
In a food processor, grate carrots, celery, and onion and add to
matzoh meal mixture. Add melted fat and mix thoroughly. 4. Divide
mixture into thirds. Place each on a strip of foil, shiny side up.
Shape into a roll and wrap with foil. Secures edges of foil. 5. Place
on cookie sheet. Bake 1 hour. Slice into 1 1/2 inch pieces.

From: Passover Foods: Recipes Suitable For Newlyweds. Pub: Canadian
Jewish News - April 6, 1995
