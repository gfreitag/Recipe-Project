Title: MOTHER'S SWEDISH SPRITZ COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
4 1/2 c  Flour
Cream butter and sugar together thoroughly. Add the egg, flavoring, or
flavorings, and flour. Use star disk with cookie press and shape
dough into small wreaths. Bake at 400 for 7 to 10 minutes. Cookies
should be set, but not brown.

Source: Festival Cookie Book
per Donna Ransdell
