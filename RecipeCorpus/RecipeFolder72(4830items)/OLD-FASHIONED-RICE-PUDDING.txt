Title: OLD FASHIONED RICE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Rice
1 ea Egg
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Nutmeg
Cook the milk and rice in top of double boiler until rice is
tender.Beat egg and sugar and add a small amount of rice mixture then
stir back into main rice mixture and add vanilla and nutmeg. Cook a
few minutes longer.
