Title: OBST PLATZ
Categories: Fruits, Mennonite, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------------------BASE---------------------------------
2 c  All purpose flour
1 tb Baking powder
4 tb Granulated sugar
2 tb Shortening
1 c  Cream
1    Egg
3 c  Any fresh or frozen fruits,
-such as cherries, halved
-plums, apple slices,
-apricots or rhubarb
MMMMM-----------------------CRUMB TOPPING----------------------------
3/4 c  Granulated sugar
3/4 c  All purpose flour
1/2 t  Baking powder
2 tb Butter
Cream
Sift together flour, baking powder and sugar; add shortening, mixing
well with fingers until fine crumbs form.  Add the cream and the egg,
mixing well. Pat dough out on a greased cookie sheet, pushing up
sides. Top with a layer of desired fruit. To prepare crumb topping,
mix together sugar, flour and baking powder. Rub in butter and enough
cream to make coarse crumbs. Note:  If using rhubarb, gooseberries,
or other similarly tart fruit, add extra sugar as desired. Speinkle
crumbs over fruit and bake in 375 F8F oven for 25 - 30 minutes. Cut
in squares and serve.
