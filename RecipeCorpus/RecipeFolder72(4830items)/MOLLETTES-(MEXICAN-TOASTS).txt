Title: MOLLETTES (MEXICAN TOASTS)
Categories: Pizza, Appetizers
Yield: 20 Appetizers
IMPERIAL Margarine
10    French rolls, cut lengthwise
-into halves or quarters
LAWRY'S Garlic Powder with
-Parlsey
1 cn Refried beans (16 oz)
Salsa
2 c  Grated Cheddar or Monterey
-Jack cheese
Spread margarine on rolls; lightly sprinkle on Garlic Powder with
Parlsey. Toast under broiler until just golden. Spread each with
refried beans and salsa. Top with cheese. Place under broiler to melt
cheese.

PRESENTATION: Serve with guacamole.
