Title: MISO TAHINI SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Spreads
Yield: 1 Batch
1 tb EDEN Mugi Miso
3 tb Sesame tahini
2 tb Water (or more)
3    Green onions; chopped
Mix well and serve on toast or crackers.

Prep Time: 5 minutes Yield: 1/3 cup
