Title: MUSHROOM  WILD RICE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
4 oz Package wilk rice
2 c  Water
1 tb Beef-flavored bouillon
1/2 lb Fresh muchrooms
1/4 c  Butter
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Minced onion
1 tb Minced fresh parsley
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/2 c  Coarsley chopped pecans
Wash wild rice in three changes of water; drain.
Combine rice, 2 cups water, and bouillon granules ina medium
saucepan; bring to boil.  Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer 30-45
minutes or until rice is tender and water absorbed. Saute mushrooms
in butter and lemon juice until tender. Stir mushrooms, onion,
parsley, garlic powder, and pecans into rice. Yeild: 4-6 servings.
