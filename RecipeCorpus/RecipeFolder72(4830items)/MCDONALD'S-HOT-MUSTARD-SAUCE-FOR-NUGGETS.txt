Title: MCDONALD'S HOT MUSTARD SAUCE FOR NUGGETS
Categories: Sauces, Vegetables, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
1 tb Dijon mustard
2 tb French's prepared mustard
2 tb Heinz 57 sauce
1/4 c  Mayo
1/4 c  Sour cream
Mix all, cover and refrigerate to use within 30 days. Source: Glorie
