Title: MARILYN BURGERS
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Burgers
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Ground almonds
1/4 c  Tahini
1/4 c  Tamari
1/2    Onion, diced fine
1    Carrot, diced fine
Juice of one lemon
Add everything to the almonds. Make patties and fry in a little oil
or broil. Handle carefully as not to break.

Submitted by Marilyn Roth, Marina Del Ray, CA, for the "Build a
Better Burger Contest" sponsored by the North American Vegetarian
Society.

From _Vegetarian Voice_
