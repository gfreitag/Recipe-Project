Title: MINCEMEAT COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Holiday
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Shortening;margarine or but
3/4 c  Sugar; brown
1/2 ts Vanilla
1    Egg,well beaten
2 3/4 c  Flour, sifted
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Milk or cream, sour
1/2 c  Mincemeat
Cream together shortening, sugar and vanilla. Beat until fluffy. Add
the well beaten egg. Add sifted dry ingredients alternately with the
sour milk/cream. Chill batter and divide. Cut on lightly floured
board with a 2 inch cutter.  Put together in pairs, moisten the
undercrust with egg and use 1 1/2 tsp. of mincemeat as filling on
each cookie. Pinch edges together and put on a greased cookie sheet
and bake in hot oven 425 degrees for10-12 minutes.
