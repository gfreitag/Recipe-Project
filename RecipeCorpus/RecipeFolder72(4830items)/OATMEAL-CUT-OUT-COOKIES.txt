Title: OATMEAL CUT OUT COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 3 Dozen
1 1/2 c  Pow. suger
1 c  Butter
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
2 1/4 c  Flour
1 c  Finely chopped oats
1 ts Bak. soda
cream  pow. suger and butter add egg and vanilla add remaining
ingredients roll out and cut into shapes bake 375 ` about 8-10 minutes
