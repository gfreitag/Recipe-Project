Title: MILE HIGH STRAWBERRY PIE
Categories: Desserts, Pies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lg Egg Whites
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Lemon Juice
1 1/4 c  Crushed Strawberries
1/2 pt Whipped Cream
1    10" Pie Shell
Beat the egg whites, sugar, lemon juice and berries at high speed
with a mixer for 15 minutes.  Fold in whipped cream. Pile into pie
shell. Freeze until ready to serve. From: Syd's Cookbook.
