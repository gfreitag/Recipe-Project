Title: MARYANN'S MACARONI & PEA SALAD
Categories: Pasta, Salads
Yield: 999 Servings
1 pk Shell Macaroni
Hellman's mayo
2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Onion powder
1    Dill pickles
2    Bell pepper
1 cn Pimento
1 pk Petite Peas (green) frozen
Salt & pepper to taste
Cook & drain macaroni.  Chop pepper, pickle, and pimento. Stir in
with mayo and seasonings. Stir in peas. Chill several hours.
