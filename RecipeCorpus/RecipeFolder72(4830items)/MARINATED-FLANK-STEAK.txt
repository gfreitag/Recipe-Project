Title: MARINATED FLANK STEAK
Categories: Beef
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Oil
2 tb -water
2    Cloves garlic; minced
2 ts Brown sugar
1 ts Ginger root; chopped
1/4 ts Black pepper
3 lb Flank steak
In a small bowl, combine all ingredients except the flank steak, and
mix well. Score the flank steak on both sides. Place in a glass
baking dish, pour the marinade over the flank steak. Refrigerate for
2-4 hours, turning the steak once. Barbacue or broil the meat 6
inches from the heat, about 5 minutes on each side or until it
reaches the desired doneness, basting occasionally with the marinade.
To serve, slice thinly across the grain of the meat. Makes 4-6
servings. This same marinade recipes can be used for chicken, pork or
fish. ---
