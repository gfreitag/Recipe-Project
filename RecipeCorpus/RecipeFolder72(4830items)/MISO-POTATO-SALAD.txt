Title: MISO POTATO SALAD
Categories: Main dish, Digest
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Sweet white miso
1 1/2 ts Red miso
1/2 ts Honey
1 tb Sake
1 ts Minced ginger
1/4 c  Water
3/4    To 1 lb baby potatoes,
-quartered
2 tb Water
Mix first 6 ingredients together well.  Set aside. Heat pot and add
quartered potatoes and 2 T water. Sautee for 5 minutes or until
potatoes begin to cook partway. Add miso mixture and simmer, covered,
for 10 minutes or until potatoes are fully cooked.  Serve hot or
cold. Adapted from Shurtleff and Aoyagi's _The Book of Miso_
