Title: MARIE AUGUSTUS' PRALINES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 36 Servings
4 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 lb Butter
2 c  Evaporated milk (pet)
1/2 lb Butter
5 c  Pecans, chopped
1 pn Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Place milk, 1/2 lb butter, both sugars and slat in a large heavy pan.
Bring to boil, cook to "softball stage" <this is very hard, you MUST
use a candy thermometer> Remove from heat and add 1/2 lb butter. Beat
constantly. When you feel it begin to thicken, add pecans and
vanilla. When thick QUICKLY drop by teaspoon on greased surface or
foil. Leave 2-3" between each as they will flatten out. REMEMBER THIS
CANDY IS STIRRED CONSTANTLY WHILE BEING COOKED AND BEATEN CONSTANTLY
WHEN REMOVED FROM THE FIRE.
