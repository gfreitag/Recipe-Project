Title: MUSHROOM APPETIZER
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Fresh mushrooms
1/4 lb Butter
1 pk Garlic or Italian "Good
-Seasons" mix
Saute cleaned mushrooms in butter for 5 minutes. Sprinke with the
package of "Good Seasons" dressing mix. Simmer covered, do not boil,
for 1 1/2 hrs. Serve with toothpicks. They go fast.
