Title: NEW ENGLAND CLAM BAKE
Categories: Seafood
4    lobsters
Yield: 1 Servings
8    ears corn
8    potatoes -- cut in half
24    Top Neck Clams
8    hot dogs
24    jumbo shrimp
1 ga ocean water
15 lb fresh seaweed
1 bn tarragon
1 bn chervil
butter -- melted
:

Build a fire and cover with sandstone rocks (from the beach), or lava
rocks. Cover with seaweed. Cover with chicken wire. Cover with more
seaweed. Cover with lobsters, potatoes and herbs. Cover with clams,
corn and shrimp. Cover with seaweed. If seaweed dries out wet it with
ocean water. Serve on a large picnic table covered with news paper.
Have large bowl of melted butter and lemon wedges on the table and a
lot of napkins!
