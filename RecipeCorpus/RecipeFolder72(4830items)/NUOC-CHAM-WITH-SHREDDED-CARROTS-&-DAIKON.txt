Title: NUOC CHAM WITH SHREDDED CARROTS & DAIKON
Categories: Thai, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
2 sm Garlic cloves, crushed
1 sm Fresh red chile pepper,
-seeded and minced
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Fresh lime or lemon juice
1/4 c  Rice vinegar
1/4 c  Nuoc mam (Vietnamese fish
-sauce)
1 sm Carrot, shredded
1 sm Daikon or turnip, peeled and
-shredded
1 ts Sugar
MMMMM---------------------VEGETABLE PLATTER--------------------------
1 lg Head of Boston lettuce,
-separated into individual
-leaves
1 bn Scallions, cut 2" lengths
1 c  Coriander leaves
1 c  Mint leaves
1 c  Fresh Asian or regular
-basil leaves
1    Cucumber, peeled in
-alternating strips, halved
-lengthwise and sliced
-crosswise
4 oz Fresh bean sprouts
Combine the garlic, chile and sugar in a mortar and pound with a
pestle to a fine paste.  Add the lime juice, vinegar, fish sauce and
1/4 cup water. Stir to blend. (Alternately, combine all ingredients
in a food processor or blender and process for 30 seconds, until the
sugar dissolves. Toss the carrot and daikon shreds with the sugar in
a small bowl. Let stand 15 minutes to soften the vegetables. Add the
Nuoc Cham to the softened vegetables and stir.

On a large platter, decoratively arrange the vegetable ingredients in
separate groups.
