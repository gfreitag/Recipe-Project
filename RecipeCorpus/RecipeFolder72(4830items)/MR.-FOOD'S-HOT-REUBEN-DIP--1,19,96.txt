Title: MR. FOOD'S HOT REUBEN DIP- 1/19/96
Categories: Appetizers, Dips
1    can(16oz) sauerkraut --
drained/squeezed dry
Yield: 1 Servings
:
1 sm onion -- finely chopped
1/4 lb corned beef brisket --
(cooked)
:
1 c  mayonnaise
1 c  sour cream
2 TB prepared horseradish
1 c  swiss cheese -- (4 oz.);
shredded
:

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees (F).  Place the sauerkraut, onion, and
corned beef in a blender or in a food processor that has been fitted
with its cutting blade. Process until finely chopped. Combine the
mixture with the remaining ingredients in a 1-quart casserole dish
that has been coated with nonstick vegetable spray. Bake for 30 to 40
minutes, until heated through.
