Title: NEPALI SPICED TEA GIFT PACKAGE
Categories: Beverages, Gifts
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 ts Cardamon seeds
4    Cloves
4    Whole black pepper corns
1/4 ts Fresh ginger root, minced
5 tb Nepali black tea (heaping)
Crush all ingredients, except tea, with a mortar and pestle. Add to
the tea.  Place in a tea ball strainer or small cheesecloth pouch and
tie with a thread. Submitted By ARLENES@HOLLY.COLOSTATE.EDU On
