Title: MEADOWS CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry etc
Yield: 4 Servings
4 ea Chicken breasts,skinned
1 ts Poultry seasoning
2 pk Mushroom soup mix
1 ts Soya sauce
Parmesan cheese
Place chicken breasts in baking dish, sprinkle with soya sauce and
poultry seasoning.  Mix 2 pkg. cream of mushroom soup mix with water,
pour over chicken and sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Bake at 325F for
about 1 hour. If sauce gets too thick, dilute with hot tea.
