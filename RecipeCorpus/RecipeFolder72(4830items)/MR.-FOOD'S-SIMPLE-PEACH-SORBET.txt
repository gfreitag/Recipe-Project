Title: MR. FOOD'S SIMPLE PEACH SORBET
Categories: Desserts, Sherbert
Yield: 3 Servings
20 oz Peaches in heavy syrup
1 tb Orange juice
=======================================================
============== ==== == Place the unopened can of peaches in the
freezer until frozen solid, about 24 hours. Submerge the unopened can
in very hot tap water for 1 minute. Open the can and pour any thawed
syrup into the bowl of a food processor that has been fitted with its
steel
