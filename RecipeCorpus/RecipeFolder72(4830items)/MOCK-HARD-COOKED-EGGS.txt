Title: MOCK HARD-COOKED EGGS
Categories: Eggs, Salads, Vegetarian, Fatfree
8 Oz Egg Beaters. 99% Egg
Substitute -- or substitute
Yield: 2 Servings
:

In an 8" skillet, pour Egg Beaters, or substitute. Cover tightly;
cook over low heat 10 min or until just set. Remove from heat; let
stand, covered for 10 min. Remove from skillet and cool completely.
Chop and use in your favorite egg salad recipe or sprinkle over
salads or casseroles.
