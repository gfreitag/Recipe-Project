Title: MIEL AUX TREFLES ( CLOVERLEAF HONEY)
Categories: Misc
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  White sugar
1 c  -Water
2 c  Clover blossoms
5    Wild roses
Boil the sugar until the sugar has dissolved. Add the clover and
roses and let the mixture stand for 30 minutes. Bring to a boil for 3
minutes and strain the syrup through a cheesecloth or fine meshed
strainer. Bring the syrup to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes. Pour
the honey into jars, and store in a cool, dark place.
