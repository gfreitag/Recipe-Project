Title: MEXICAN DEVILED EGGS-TOFH
Categories: Appetizer, Egg
Yield: 16 Servings
MMMMM-----------------WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A----------------------
8    Eggs, hard-cook; halve
1/2 c  Cheddar, shred
1/4 c  Mayo
1/4 c  Salsa
2 tb Green onions, slice
1 tb Sour cream
Salt
Mash the yolks with cheese, mayo, salsa, onions, sour cream and salt.
Evenly fill the whites. Serve immediately or chill until ready to
serve. Source: Taste of Home. By Patrick & Sarah Gruenwald
<sitm@ekx.infi.net> on Nov 02, 1996
