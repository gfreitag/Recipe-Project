Title: MASTER MIX BEER ROLLS
Categories: Healthy
Yield: 15 Servings
4 c  Master mix
1/4 c  Sugar
12 oz Beer
Recipe by: The Living Heart Diet by M. E. DeBakey ISBN 0-671-61998-5
1.    Preheat oven to 400 F.

2. Combine all ingredients, stirring until flour is just moistened.

3. Fill oiled muffin tins 2/3 full.

4.    Bake for 15 mins.

YIELD: 15 muffins

1 roll = 166 Cal; 3 Prot; 6.3 Fat; 25 Carb; 0.8 Sat; 1.5 Mono; 3.7
Poly; 0 Chol; 322 Na
