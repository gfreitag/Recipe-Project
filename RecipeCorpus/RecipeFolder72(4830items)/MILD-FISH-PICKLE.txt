Title: MILD FISH PICKLE
Categories: Relishes, Spices
Yield: 1 Servings
3 oz Tuna, salmon or anchovy
2 ts White wine
1 tb Vinegar
1/2 ts Mustard seed
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Celery seed (or lovage)
1 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Honey
1 pn Basil
1/4 ts Thyme
1    Mint leaf, finely chopped
In a mixing bowl, thoroughly combine all ingredients. This fish
pickle may be stored in the refrigertator in a glass jar for up tp 2
weeks, and should then be replaced.

Source - The Roman Cookery of Apicius
