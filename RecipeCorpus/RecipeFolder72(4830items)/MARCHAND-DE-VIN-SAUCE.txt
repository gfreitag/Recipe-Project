Title: MARCHAND DE VIN SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 2 Cups
3/4 c  Butter
1/3 c  Fresh mushrooms (finely
Chopped)
1/2 c  Ham, minced
1/3 c  Shallots, finely chopped
1/2 c  Onions, finely chopped
2 tb Garlic, minced
2 tb Flour, all-purpose
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Black pepper
Dash cayenne pepper
3/4 c  Beef stock
1/2 c  Red wine
Melt butter in a 9-inch skillet; add mushrooms, ham, shallots,
onion, and garlic.  Saute until lightly browned. Add flour, salt,
pepper, and cayenne; cook until browned. Blend in beef stock and wine
and simmer over low heat for 35 to 45 minutes.

Southern Living ... August, 1971
