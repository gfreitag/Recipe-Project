Title: MUSHROOM ORZO
Categories: Side dish, Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
2 qt Water
1 1/4 c  Orzo pasta, uncooked
1 ea Garlic clove, pressed
1 c  Mushrooms, chopped
2 ea Scallions, chopped
1 tb Olive oil
1/4 ts Black pepper
Bring water to a boil in a pan.  Stir in the orzo & cook until al
dente. This should take about 7 minutes. Drain & set aside.

Saute the garlic, mushrooms & scallions in the oil in a skillet until
soft, about 3 minutes.  Stir in the orzo, cook for a further 2
minutes. Sprinkle with pepper & serve.

"Vegetarian Gourmet" Winter, 1995
