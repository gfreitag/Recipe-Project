Title: MEATBALLS (CHEF DU JOUR)
Categories: New, Text, Import
1 lb lean ground beef
Yield: 4 Servings
1    egg
1/3 c  Italian bread crumbs
1/2 lg Vidalia onion
1/2    head garlic, -- chopped

In large bowl combine ingredients. Roll into balls. Cook in sauce
during last two hours of simmering. Serve.
