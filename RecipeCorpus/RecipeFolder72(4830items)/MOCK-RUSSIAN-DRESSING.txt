Title: MOCK RUSSIAN DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Mayonnaise Salad Dressing
8 oz Plain Yogurt; 1 Container
8 oz Tomato Sauce; 1 Can
1/4 c  Onion; Finely Chopped
1/4 c  Celery; Finely Chopped
1/4 c  Green Pepper; Finely Chopped
Combine all of the ingredients in a medium bowl, blending well. Cover
and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.

Makes 3 Cups
