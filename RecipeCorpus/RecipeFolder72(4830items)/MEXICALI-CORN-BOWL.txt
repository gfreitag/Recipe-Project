Title: MEXICALI CORN BOWL
Categories: Snacks, Popcorn, Mexican
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Butter
1 tb Dry taco seasoning mix
1 tb Dry chopped chives
4 qt Popped popcorn
In small saucepan, melt butter over low heat. Add taco seasoning and
chives. Blend. Pour over popcorn in large serving bowl and toss
lightly. Serve at once. Makes 4 qts.
