Title: MY CHILI SAUCE
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 6 Pints
6 qt Tomatoes
3 md Onions
4 tb Salt
4 c  White sugar
1/2 ts Cayenne
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Vinegar(any kind)
Skin tomatoes.  Put everything in a large kettle and boil until thick,
stirring often so it doesn't stick. Put in sterilized jars and seal.
Boiling takes a couple of hours until it is good and thick. Taste
when it's pretty well done and add sugar or vinegar to taste. Makes
about 6 pints.
