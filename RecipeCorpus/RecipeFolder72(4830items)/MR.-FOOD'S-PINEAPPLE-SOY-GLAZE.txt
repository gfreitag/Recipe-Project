Title: MR. FOOD'S PINEAPPLE SOY GLAZE
Categories: Sauces, Glazes
Yield: 99 Servings
About 1/2 cup
1/4 c  Pineapple juice
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Light brown sugar
1 ts Cornstarch
=======================================================
============== ==== == Directions: In a small microwaveable bowl,
combine all the ingredients and mix well. Microwave on high for 2 1/2
to 3 minutes, or until thickened. Grill your favorite meat and brush
this glaze on both sides of the meat during the last 5 minutes of
grillin
