Title: MR. FOOD'S FUN ON A BUN
Categories: Sandwiches, Hamburger
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
2 1/4 c  Spaghetti sauce
2 ts Light brown sugar
1 c  Crushed tortilla chips
6    To 8 hamburger buns
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over
medium-high heat for about 8 minutes, stirring occasionally; drain
off the excess liquid. Stir in the remaining ingredients except the
buns. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 8 to 10 minutes, until
heated
