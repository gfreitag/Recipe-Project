Title: NEW YORK RYE - FLORIDA STYLE
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Active dry yeast (1 1/2
-tsp.)
1 c  Rye flour
2 tb Gluten
3 c  Bread flour
2 tb Buttermilk powder
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 tb Honey
1 tb Butter
1 1/2 tb Caraway seeds
1 1/2 c  Water
Add all ingredients and "start" (white bread mode).
