Title: MEXICAN PEPPER CHICKEN
Categories: Mexican, Chicken
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Medium Onion, chopped
1    Clove Garlic, crushed
2 tb Vegetable Oil
1 tb Chili Powder
1 tb Crushed Red Peppers
1 lb Boneless & Skinless Chicken,
Cut into strips
1    Each Green & Red Pepper,
Cut into strips
1 c  Chili Sauce
2 tb Red Wine Vinegar
Cooked Rice *
Cook and stir onion and garlic in hot oil until onion is softened. Add
chili powder and crushed red peppers and cook gently 5 min longer,
stirring occasionally. Add chicken and cook over medium heat for 5
min, stirring frequently.  Add peppers, chili sauce and wine vinegar,
bring to a boil and simmer for 15 min, stirring occasionally. Serve
over cooked rice.

Chili rice mix can be substituted for plain rice.
