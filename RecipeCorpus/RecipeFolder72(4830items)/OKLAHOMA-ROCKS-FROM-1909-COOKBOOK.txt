Title: OKLAHOMA ROCKS FROM 1909 COOKBOOK
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Serving
2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter
3    Eggs
1/2 ts Salt
1 lb Chopped nuts
1 lb Raisins
1 ts Soda dissolve in 1/3 cup
Boiling water
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cloves
Flour to make stiff
Mix the ingredients as given top to bottom, and drop in spoonfuls on
greased baking pan

There are no instructions as to temp 325-350 F should do it. Again no
instructions as to timing begin checking at 8 min.
