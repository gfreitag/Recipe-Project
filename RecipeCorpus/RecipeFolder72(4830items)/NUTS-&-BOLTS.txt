Title: NUTS & BOLTS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 10 Servings
1 pk Pretzel sticks
1 pk Cheerios
1 pk Rice chex
1 pk Wheat chex
1 lb Mixed nuts
1 lb Margarine
3 T  Worcestershire sauce
3 T  Tabasco sauce
1 T  Garlic
Melt oleo or butter; add Worcestershire sauce, Tabasco sauce and
garlic salt. Pour over rest of ingredients; mix well. Put in covered
roaster, set oven at 225 F. and place roaster in oven for over 2
hours. Stir every 15 minutes. (220 F. in electric roaster and stir
every 30 minutes.)
