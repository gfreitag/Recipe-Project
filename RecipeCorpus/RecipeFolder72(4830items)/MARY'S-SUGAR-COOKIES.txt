Title: MARY'S SUGAR COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Confectioner's sugar
(sifted)
3/4 c  Butter
1    Egg
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Almond flavoring
1 ts Soda
1 ts Cream of tartar
Cream butter & sugar.  Mix in egg & flavorings.  Mix dry ingredients &
blend into above mixture, dough will be stiff. Can refrigerate a few
min. or can make into balls & place on a cookie sheet about 1 1/2 to
2 inches apart.  Press down with a fork.  Place pecan on each cookie
(optional) & bake at 375 degrees until brown.
