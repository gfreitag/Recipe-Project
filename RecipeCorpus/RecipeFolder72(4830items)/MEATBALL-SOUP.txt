Title: MEATBALL SOUP
Categories: Soups, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ea Slice bread
1/2 c  Milk
1 lb Ground turkey
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 tb Vegetable oil
3 c  Chicken broth
16 oz Tomato sauce wth tomato bits
1 ea Medium onion
2 ea Carrots, sliced thin
2 ea Celery stalks, sliced
1/4 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Place bread and milk in bowl; soak bread.  [Substitute about 6 ritz
crackers for a different flavor.]

Add meat, salt and pepper and mix well.  Form into 24 balls. Brown
meat balls in oil and drain.

Add remaining ingredients and simmer uncovered for 1 hour. Makes 4-6
servings.
