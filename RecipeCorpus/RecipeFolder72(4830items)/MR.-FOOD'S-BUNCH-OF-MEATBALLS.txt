Title: MR. FOOD'S BUNCH OF MEATBALLS
Categories: Meats, Hamburger, Meatballs
Yield: 12 Servings
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
1    Egg
2 tb Finely chopped onion
1    Garlic clove, minced
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
======================================================= ======

ingredients; mix well. Form the mixture into 1 1/2-inch meatballs and
