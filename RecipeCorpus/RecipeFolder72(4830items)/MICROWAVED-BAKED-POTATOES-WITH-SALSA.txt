Title: MICROWAVED BAKED POTATOES WITH SALSA
Categories: Breakfast, Ethnic, Mexican
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lg Potato; scrubbed
Topping options:
1/2 c  Salsa; * see note
Preparation Time: 0:09 If you don't have a microwave oven, cook the
potatoes in a conventional oven at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours the
night before, and serve them cold for breakfast. Puncture the
potatoes several times with a fork and place in the microwave. Cook
on high for 7 to 8 minutes, depending on the size of the potato and
the number of potatoes cooked, or until tender. Eat plain or with
your choice of toppings.
