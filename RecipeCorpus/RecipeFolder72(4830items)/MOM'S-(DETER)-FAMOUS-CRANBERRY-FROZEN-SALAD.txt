Title: MOM'S (DETER) FAMOUS CRANBERRY FROZEN SALAD
Categories: Holidays, Salads, Fruits, Favorites
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Cranberries -- ground/fresh
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Miniature marshmallows
8 3/4 oz Crushed pineapple --
Drained
1 c  Heavy whipping cream --
Whipped
Grind cranberries; then measure.  Add sugar and let stand 12 hours.
Fold in remaining ingredients.  Pout into 9x5x3 inch loaf pan. Freeze
until firm. Slice before serving.
