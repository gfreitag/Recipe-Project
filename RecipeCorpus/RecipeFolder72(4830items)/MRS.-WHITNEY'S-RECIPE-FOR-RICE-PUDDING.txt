Title: MRS. WHITNEY'S RECIPE FOR RICE PUDDING
Categories: Puddings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Rice
3 qt Milk
8    Eggs
4 oz Butter
6 oz Sugar
Few bitter almonds
Some saffron
Ome pound of rice, 3 quarts milk, 8 eggs, 4 oz. butter, 6 oz. sugar,
a few bitter almonds and some saffron. Boil the rice in the milk
'till a little soft, then add the butter & sugar and stir 'till well
mixed. When cold add the other ingredients and bake it.
