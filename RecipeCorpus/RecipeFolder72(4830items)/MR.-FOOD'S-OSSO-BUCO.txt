Title: MR. FOOD'S OSSO BUCO
Categories: Meats, Veal
Yield: 5 Servings
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Black pepper
3 tb Vegetable oil
3 lb Veal shanks, cut crosswise
- about 2 inches thick
10 3/4 oz Condensed onion soup
1 md Tomato, chopped
1 c  Dry white wine
1/2 c  Water, divided
2 tb Water, divided
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1/2 ts Garlic powder
3 tb Cornstarch
29 oz Whole potatoes, drained
14 1/2 oz Diced carrots, drained
======================================================= ====== In a
shallow dish, combine the flour and pepper. Coat the shanks with the
flour mixture. In a soup pot, heat the oil over medium-high heat and
brown the shanks, about 5 minutes per side. Add the soup, tomato,
wine, 1/2 cup water, lemon juice, parsely, and Note: Osso Buco is
normally served over cooked rice or pasta, but because this version
has potatoes and carrots, you've got an easy one-pot meal.
