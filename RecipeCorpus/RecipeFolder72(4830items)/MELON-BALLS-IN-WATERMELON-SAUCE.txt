Title: MELON BALLS IN WATERMELON SAUCE
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 8 Servings
1/4 sm Watermelon
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Light rum
1 c  Cantaloupe balls
4 c  Honeydew melon balls
Cut watermelon into cubes; press cubes through a strainer or food
mill to make 2 cups juice.

Combine watermelon juice, sugar, and rum in a large bowl; stir until
sugar dissolves.  Add melon balls; tossing well. Cover and chill 2
hours or overnight.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, August, 1979. Typed for you by Nancy
Coleman.
