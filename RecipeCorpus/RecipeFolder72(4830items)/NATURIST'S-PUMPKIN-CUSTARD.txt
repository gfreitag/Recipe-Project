Title: NATURIST'S PUMPKIN CUSTARD
Categories: Desserts, Eggs
Yield: 1 Batch
1 qt Milk
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
6    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Cooked, mashed pumpkin
Beat the eggs and milk together.  Beat in rest of ingredients. Butter
casserole dish and add mixture. Bake in a pan of hot water at 350F.
for about 1 1/2 hours (until knife inserted in middle comes out
clean).
