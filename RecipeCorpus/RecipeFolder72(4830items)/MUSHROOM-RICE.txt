Title: MUSHROOM RICE
Categories: Main dish, Grain
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Cooked Brown Rice
1 c  Chopped mushrooms
1    Bunch green onions
1/4 c  Margarine
Melt margarine. Over medium heat saute mushrooms until soft. Add green
onions and saute briefly Stir in cooked rice and salt and pepper to
taste and heat through
