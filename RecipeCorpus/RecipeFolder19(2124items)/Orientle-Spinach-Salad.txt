Title: Orientle Spinach Salad
Categories: Salads
Servings:  4
1 ea Bunch of spinach
1 cn Water crestnuts(sliced)
1 cn Bean sprouts(drained)
2 ea Hard boiled eggs
1/4 lb Bacon (fried and crumbled)
-------------------------DRESSING-------------------------
1/2 c  Oil
1/8 c  White vinegar
1/2 c  Med. onion
1/4 c  Sugar
1 t  Salt
3 t  Ketchup
Blend dressing one day in advance.
