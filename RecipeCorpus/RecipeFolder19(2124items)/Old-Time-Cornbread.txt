Title: Old Time Cornbread
Categories: Breads Londontowne
Servings:  1
2 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 t  Salt
3/4 t  Soda
1 1/2 c  Cornmeal
3 T  Lard, melted
1 c  Flour
Beat eggs, milk, and lard together. Add remaining
ingredients and mix well. Pour into greased 11" X 7" X
2" and bake in hot (400) oven about 30 minutes.
