Title: Pecan Pie 1
Categories: Dessert
Servings:  8
1/2 ea Butter; stick
3 ea Egg
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Karo; white
3 T  Corn meal
1 t  Vanilla
1 ea Salt; pinch
1 c  Pecans; chopped
1 ea Pie shell
per serving: 250
Approx. Cook
Calories
Fat grams    per serving: 30
Time: 1:30 Cholesterol  per serving: 20
Add all ingredients together in bowl, and mix well.
Pour into pie shell, and bake 1 hour at 300 degrees.
Allow to cool and serve.
