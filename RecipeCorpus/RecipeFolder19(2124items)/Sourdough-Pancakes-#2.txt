Title: Sourdough Pancakes #2
Categories: Breads
Servings:  4
1 c  Active Starter
1 ea Large Egg
2 T  Cooking Oil
1/4 c  Instant Or Evaporate Milk
1 t  Salt
1 t  Baking Soda
2 T  Sugar
Mix ingredients together and let the mixture bubble
and foam a minute or two, then drop on hot griddle in
large spoonfuls.
