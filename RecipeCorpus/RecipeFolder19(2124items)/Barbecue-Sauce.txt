Title: Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Servings:  4
3/4 c  Chopped Onion
3/4 c  Tomato Catsup
1/3 c  Lemon Juice
3 T  Worcestershire Sauce
2 t  Salt
1/2 c  Salad Oil
3/4 c  Water
3 T  Sugar
2 T  Prepared Mustard
1/2 t  Pepper
Cook onion until soft in oil.  Add remaining
ingredients.  Simmer 15 minutes.  Good for steak,
chicken or other barbecue favorites.
