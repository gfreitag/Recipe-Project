Title: Steak Marinade
Categories: Sauces
Servings:  1
2 T  Lemon juice
3 t  Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c  Oil
2 ea Garlic, minced
1. Pierce steak so marinade can penetrate. 2. Place
steak in a non metal dish just large enough to hold it
or in a    sealable plastic bag.  Refrigerate for 12
to 24 hours.
