Title: Mornaise Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Servings:  4
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Flour
2 c  Milk
1/2 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
2 ea Egg yolks
1 T  Whipping cream
1/4 c  Swiss cheese
In microwave melt butter, add flour, stir until
smooth, 1 minute. Gradually add milk.  Beat egg yolks,
add whipping cream.  Gradually add hot mixture.  Add
cheese at the end.
