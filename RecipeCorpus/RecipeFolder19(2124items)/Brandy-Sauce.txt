Title: Brandy Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Servings:  4
1 c  Water
2 T  Corn Starch
2 T  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1 t  Nutmeg
1/4 c  Brandy
1 t  Real Vanilla
Mix dry ingredients and then stir them into a cup of
boiling water.  Boil for 5 minutes and then add
butter, brandy, and vanilla.  Serve hot over mince pie
or gingerbread.
