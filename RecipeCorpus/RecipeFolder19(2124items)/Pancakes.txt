Title: Pancakes
Categories: Breads Londontowne
Servings:  4
3 c  Flour
1 T  Sugar
4 t  Baking powder
1 t  Salt
1 ea Egg
4 T  Shortening
1 1/4 c  Milk
Sift dry ingredients together twice. Mix with wooden
spoon. Add egg, shortening, and milk to make a smooth
batter. Makes light, thick pancakes. Mrs. J. Howard
Beard
