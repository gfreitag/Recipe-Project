Title: HERBED VINEGAR
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Bottle
1 1/2 c  White wine vinegar
1/2 c  Fresh basil leaves
Pour vinegar into non-aluminum 2-quart saucepan. Heat until very hot,
stirring occasionally. Do NOT boil. (If vinegar boils, it will become
cloudy.)

Pour into glass bowl; add basil. Cover with plastic wrap. Let stand
in cool place about 1 week until desired amount of flavour develops.
Strain before using. Store up to 6 months in jar or bottle with
tight- fitting lid.

Variations: Substitute 1 tablespoon of either fresh oregano, thyme,
chervil or tarragon for the basil. Or, substitute cider vinegar for
the wine vinegar.
