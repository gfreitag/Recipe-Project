Title: HERBED PORK ROAST
Categories: Pork
Yield: 8 Servings
1 ea Fresh ham or pork leg - 4 to
7    Pounds
2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1 ts Thyme -- crumbled
1 ts Ground sage
1 ts Lemon peel -- grated
1 tb Lemon juice
1    Clove garlic
Remove skin and excess fat from pork.  Place in large bowl. Pour water
over; add remaining ingredients. cover and refrigerate several hours
or overnight. Turn meat two or three times if possible. Transfer meat
and marinade to slow-cooking pot.  Cover and cook on low for 10 to 12
hours or until meat is done.  If possible, turn meat in pot once
during cooking. Drain and slice.
