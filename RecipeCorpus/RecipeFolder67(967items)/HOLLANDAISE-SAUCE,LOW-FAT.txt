Title: HOLLANDAISE SAUCE/LOW-FAT
Categories: Sauces, Low-fat, Dairy, Eggs
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM---------------------LIZ JONES VXRF36B--------------------------
2 tb Liquid Butter Buds
1 c  Boiling water
12 ts Lite salt; opt
1 tb Flour
2 ea Egg Beaters; beaten
1 x  Juice of 1/2 lemon
Combine Butter Buds and flour in a sauce pan.  Add boiling water,
stirring constantly.  Cook over low heat. Beat Egg Beaters, salt and
lemon juice together. Add some of the first mixture to this before
slowly adding to the Butter Buds, flour, and water.  Cook until the
spoon is well coated. This is delicious, easy to make, and will keep
well. It is just as good warmed over as it is fresh off the stove.
