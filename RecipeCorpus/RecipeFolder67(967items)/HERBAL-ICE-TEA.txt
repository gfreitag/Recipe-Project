Title: HERBAL ICE TEA
Categories: Diabetic, Beverages
Yield: 4 Nice folks
1 ga Filtered water;
3    Flavored herbal tea bags;
-peprermint, strawberry,
-lemon, etc.
Sugar to taste;
Place tea bags in water.  Let steep overnight.  Add ice. Sweeten to
taste.

Source: The San Diego Union Food Section, May 26, 1994 Brought to you
and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master
