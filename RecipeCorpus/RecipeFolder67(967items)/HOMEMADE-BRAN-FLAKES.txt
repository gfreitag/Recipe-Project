Title: HOMEMADE BRAN FLAKES
Categories: Mormon, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Bran
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 c  Nonfat dry milk powder
3 tb Brewer's yeast (optional)
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Oil
1 tb Molasses
1 c  Water
Combine dry ingredients.  Make a well in the center and add
oil,molasses and water. Mix well. Divide into three parts and roll
out as thin as possible on greased cookie sheets. bake in 350 F oven
15-20 minutes, or until lightly browned and crisp. If dough is not
completely dry, turn oven off and let it remain longer. Break into
small pieces. Store in airtight container. Makes 1 lb of cereal.

Origin: Homemaking booklet, Mormon Church, 1978. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens
