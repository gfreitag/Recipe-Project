Title: HOARHOUND CANDY
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/3 ts Pressed hoarhound
1 1/4 c  Boiling water
1/3 c  White corn sirup
2 c  Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
Pour water over hoarhound.  Allow to stand 10 minutes. Strain. Combine
sugar, salt, sirup, and water from hoarhound. Boil to soft crack
stage (275 - 280 F). Pour in thin sheet onto well-buttered baking
sheet. Mark into squares before candy hardens. The Household
Searchlight
