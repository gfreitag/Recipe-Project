Title: HOME-MADE PASTA
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
12 oz Italian Soft wheat flour or
-Plain white flour
2    Egg Yolks
ds Olive Oil
pn Salt
3    Beaten Eggs
On a cool worktop, mix all ingredients except beaten eggs together
with your hands until you have a tacky dough. add beaten eggs and
continue kneading until you have a smooth and pliable dough. Cover
and leave to rest for a good half hour. Roll out on a floured surface
and cut to the size required ir you can use a pasta machine if you
have one.

Source: Floyd on Italy
