Title: HEAVENLY ORANGE CHEESECAKE
Categories: Cheesecakes
Yield: 1 Servings
Crust-----
1 1/2 c  Chocolate Wafer Crumbs
4 tb Unsalted Butter -- melted
Filling-----
1    Envelope Unflavored Gelatin
1/2 c  Orange Juice
24 oz Cream Cheese -- softened
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Whipping Cream -- whipped
2 ts Grated Orange Peel
Combine crumbs and butter.  Press onto bottom of 9-inch springform
pan and bake at 350 for 10 minutes. Cool

Soften gelatin in juice; stir over low heat until dissolved. Combine
cream cheese and sugar.  Gradually add gelatin mixture. Fold in
whipped cream and peel. Chill until firm
