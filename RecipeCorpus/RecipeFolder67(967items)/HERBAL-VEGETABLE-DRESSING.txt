Title: HERBAL VEGETABLE DRESSING
Categories: Herbs, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 ts Fresh parsley
1/2 ts Fresh taarragon
1/2 ts Fresh chives
1/2 ts Fresh chervil
3 tb Wine vingar
9 tb Olive oil
1 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
1.  Mince the fresh herbs, reserving a few leaves to use as
garnishes. 2. Place all ingredients in a small mixing bowl.. Beat
vigorously with a wire whisk until well blended. 3. Garnish with
fresh leaves and serve immediately.
