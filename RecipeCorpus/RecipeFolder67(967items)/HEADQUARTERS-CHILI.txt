Title: HEADQUARTERS CHILI
Categories: Tex-mex, Chili
Yield: 10 Servings
3 lb Lean coarsely ground beef
2 ea Small green peppers, chopped
2 ea Medium onions, thinly sliced
2 ea Cl Garlic, crushed
1/4 c  Cooking oil
3 ea One-pound cans tomatoes
3 tb Chili powder
2 ts Crushed cumin seds or ground
1/4 ts Tabasco sauce
1 c  Water
3 ea 15-ounce cans pinto beans or
Approx. Cook Time: 2 hrs Cook
Fat grams    per serving:
beef, green peppers, onions and garlic in oil in large heavy kettle
until beef is lightly browned. Add all ingredients except beans.
Cover and simmer 45 minutes. Stir in undrained beans; cover and
simmer 25 minutes. Makes about 4 quarts When the outfit got back
after months out on a cattle drive, the headquarters cook took over.
He had a lot more fixin's to work with, and after a lot of mixin' and
tastin' he'd serve up a smooth, thick chili the cowboys could really
dig into. From "Famous Chili Recipes from Marlboro Country".
