Title: HOLY SMOKERS~ TOO SPRINKLE SPICE
Categories: Bar-b-q, Spices, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Granulated garlic
1 tb Onion powder
1 tb Chili powder
1 tb Paprika
1/2 tb Lemon peel
1/2 tb Orange peel
1/2 tb MSG (optional)
1/2 tb Cayenne pepper
Combine all ingredients and mix well. sprinkle on meat (ribs,
shoulders, pork chops, chicken, steaks, brisket, fish, etc.) and let
set for at least 1/2 hour before cooking.
