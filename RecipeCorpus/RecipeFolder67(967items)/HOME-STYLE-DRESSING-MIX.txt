Title: HOME STYLE DRESSING MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ts Instant Minced Onion
1/8 ts Garlic Powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Parsley Flakes
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and blend well. Put mixture
in a foil packet or a 1-pint glass jar and label as Home Style
Dressing Mix. Store in a cool dry place and use within 6 months.
Makes enough mix for 2 cups Home Style Dressing. (2 T) Home Style
Dressing: Combine mix, 1 cup mayonnaise, and 1 cup buttermilk in a
glass jar and blend well. Chill before serving. Makes about 2 cups of
dressing. VARIATION: Substitute 1 cup sour cream for buttermilk and
use as a dip for fresh vegetables.
