Title: HAZELNUT-ORANGE CHEESE BALL
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 Recipe
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
-- softened
1 tb Grated onion
1 1/2 c  Chopped Oregon hazelnuts
-- (roasted)
3/4 ts Grated orange peel
In a bowl, mix cream cheese and margarine together until smooth. Stir
in onion, 1 cup hazelnuts and orange peel. Shape into a ball. Roll in
remaining hazelnuts, wrap in plastic wrap, and chill until firm. Best
if allowed to season overnight. Serve with crackers.
