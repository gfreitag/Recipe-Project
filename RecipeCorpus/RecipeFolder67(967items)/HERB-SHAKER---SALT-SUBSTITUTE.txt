Title: HERB SHAKER - SALT SUBSTITUTE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
Stephen Ceideburg
1 tb Ground cayenne pepper
1 tb Garlic powder
1 ts Dried basil
1 ts Dried thyme
1 ts Dried parsley flakes
1 ts Dried savory
1 ts Ground mace
1 ts Onion powder
1 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1 ts Dried sage
1 ts Dried marjoram
Reach for this instead of the salt shaker.

Combine all ingredients in a jar. The mix can be stored, tightly
covered, in a cool, dark place for up to 6 months. Makes 1/4 cup.
