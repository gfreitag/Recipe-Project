Title: HOLIDAY BRUNCH EGG CASSEROLE
Categories: Holiday, Casseroles, Main dish
Yield: 24 Servings
10    Eggs
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
4 tb Butter
1 pt Cottage cheese, small curd
1 ts Baking powder
16 oz Monterey Jack, shredded
2 c  Chilies, green, chopped
1 lb Ham, sliced
Preheat oven to 350.

Melt butter.  Chop ham.  Beat eggs until light-lemon-colored.
Add flour and remaining ingredients.  Pour into well-greased
casserole dish.  Bake 40 minutes, until knife inserted in middle
comes out clean.
