Title: HELEN'S BAKED BEANS IN TOMATO SAUCE
Categories: Beans, Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Pea beans
1/2 lb Bacon
3/4 c  Onions, diced
1    Green pepper
3/4 c  Celery, diced
28 oz Tomato sauce
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 tb Salt
1/4 c  Light molasses
1/2 c  Brown sugar
Soak beans overnite.  Cook soaked beans til tender. Chop vegetables.
Simmer bacon, onion, pepper, and celery but do _not_ brown. Place all
ingredients in a casserole or bean crock and bake at 300 for 2-7
hours til done.

Helen Peagram
