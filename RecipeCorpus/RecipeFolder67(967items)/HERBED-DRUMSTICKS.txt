Title: HERBED DRUMSTICKS
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 ts Dried tarragon leaves
2 ts Chopped chives
2 tb Chopped parsley
Salt and pepper
8    Chicken drumsticks
1. In a shallow, heat-resistant, non-metallic baking dish, melt
butter in Microwave Oven 1 minute. 2. Add herbs and salt and pepper
to taste. Stir to combine. 3. Place drumsticks in baking dish and
toss lightly to coat drum- sticks well with butter mixture. 4. Heat,
uncovered, in Microwave Oven 14 minutes or until chicken is done.
Turn drumsticks occasionally. Variation: If desired, drumsticks can
be partially cooked over charcoal, then transferred to Microwave Oven
to finish cooking. The Microwave cooking time would have to be
adjusted because of the conventional cooking time.
