Title: HAZELNUT CHOCOLATE PASTRY SHELL
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Crust
1 c  All -purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Cocoa
1/4 c  Shortening
1 c  Oregon hazelnuts,
-- roasted & ground
1/4 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Cold water
Sift flour with salt, sugar and cocoa. Cut in shortening until coarse
meal texture. Blend in hazelnuts and vanilla. Sprinkle water over and
mix lightly. Shape into a ball. Pat mixture over bottom and sides of
a 9" pie plate. Prick crust. Bake at 400 for 8 to 10 minutes. Cool
thoroughly before filling.
