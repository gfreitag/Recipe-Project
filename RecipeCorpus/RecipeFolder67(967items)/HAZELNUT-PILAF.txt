Title: HAZELNUT PILAF
Categories: Side dish, Grains
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Uncooked rice
1/2 c  Coarsely chopped hazelnuts
-(roasted Oregon hazelnuts)
3/4 c  Grated cheddar cheese
1/2    Onion; diced
1    Can sliced mushrooms
-(6 to 8 oz.), drained
--(save liquid)
1 c  Chicken broth
1/4 c  Soy sauce
Yield: 6 to 8 servings.

Combine rice, hazelnuts, cheese, onion and mushrooms; place in a
greased 1-quart casserole. Mix mushroom liquid, chicken broth and soy
sauce, heat to boiling and pour over rice mixture. Cover and bake for
30 to 40 minutes at 350. Fluff with fork before serving.
