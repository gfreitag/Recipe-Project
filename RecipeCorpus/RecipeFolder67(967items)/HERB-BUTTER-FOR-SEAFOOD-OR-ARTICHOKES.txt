Title: HERB BUTTER FOR SEAFOOD OR ARTICHOKES
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1 ts Paprika
1 tb Green scallions
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Marjoram
1/4 ts Basil
1    Clove garlic, crushed
3 tb Fresh parsley, chopped
1/2 ts Soy sauce
Melt butter over medium heat and stir in remaining ingredients.
Simmer 5 minutes.  Serve warm.  May be refrigerated and reheated when
needed.
