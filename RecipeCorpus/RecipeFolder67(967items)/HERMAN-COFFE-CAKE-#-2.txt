Title: HERMAN COFFE CAKE # 2
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Herman
2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Oil
1 c  Raisins or dates
1/2 c  Nuts
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Melted butter
~-----------------TOPPING------------------------------ ~--- Bake at
350 for 30-40 minutes. Mix all ingredients and pour into a 13 x 9
pan. Top with the topping mixture.
