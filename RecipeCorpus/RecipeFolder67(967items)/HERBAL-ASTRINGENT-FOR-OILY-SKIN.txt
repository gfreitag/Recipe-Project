Title: HERBAL ASTRINGENT FOR OILY SKIN
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Chamomile
3 tb Witch hazel
1/2 ts Cider vinegar
1 oz Peppermint extract
8 oz Water
Few drops of peppermint --
Extract
1/4 ts Boric acid -- optional
Boil chamomile, witch hazel, vinegar, peppermint and water. Cool and
strain. Add remaining ingredients in a spray bottle and shake. Keep
refrigerated.
