Title: HELOISES  FLAVORED  COFFEE  MIXES
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 Cups  dry  ~----ORANGE:===============================
1/2 c  Instant coffee
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Powdered milk
1/2 t  Dried orange peel
-----MOCHA:================================
1/2 c  Instant coffee
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Powdered coffee creamer
2 T  Unsweetened cocoa
-----CINNAMON:=============================
1/2 c  Instant coffee
2/3 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Powdered milk
1/2 t  Cinnamon

You can substitute the appropriate amount of artificial sweetener for
sugar in any of these mixes. The label on the sweetener usually will
tell you the equivalent of the amount of sugar. Powdered creamer also
can be substituted for the powdered milk. To make 1 cup of coffee:
Place 2 rounded teaspoons homemade coffee mix into a cup; fill with
hot water. Store in an air tight container.
