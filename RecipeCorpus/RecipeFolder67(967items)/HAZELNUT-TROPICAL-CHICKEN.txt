Title: HAZELNUT TROPICAL CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Raisins
3/4 c  Orange juice
2 lb Cut-up chicken
-- (any pieces you enjoy)
1/2 c  Flour
Seasoning salt to taste
3 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Mandarin oranges
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1 c  Oregon hazelnuts, cracked
1/2 c  Pineapple chunks, drained
2 tb Brown sugar
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sherry
Soak raisins in orange juice for at least 1 hour. Dust chicken pieces
in seasoned flour and sautÂ‚ in olive oil in heavy frying pan until
brown. Place chicken in a baking pan with remaining ingredients. Bake
at 350 for 45 minutes, basting frequently with juices.
