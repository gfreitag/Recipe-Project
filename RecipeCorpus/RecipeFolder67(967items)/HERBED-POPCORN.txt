Title: HERBED POPCORN
Categories: Popcorn, Snacks, Diabetic
Yield: 3 Cups
3 c  Popped popcorn
1/2 ts Dried oregano leaves
1/2 ts Ground coriander
1/4 ts Ground cumin
SOURCE:  Special Celebrations And Parties Cookbook by Betty Wedman,
M.S., R.D., copyright 1989, ISBN #0-13-004219-6. MM format by Ursula
R. Taylor.
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.  Toss to mix.
Yield:  3 cups - 1 serving.
Food Exchange:  1 starch/bread.
Nutritional information per 3 cups:  85 Calories, 3 g Protein, 17 g
Carbohydrate, 1 g Fat, 0 Sodium, 73 mg Potassium, 0 Cholesterol.
