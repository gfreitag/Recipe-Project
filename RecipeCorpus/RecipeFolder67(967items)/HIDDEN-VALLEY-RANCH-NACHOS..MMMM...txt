Title: HIDDEN VALLEY RANCH NACHOS..MMMM..
Categories: Mexican
Yield: 2 Servings
10 oz Tortilla chips
3/4 c  Hidden Valley Ranch Milk
Recipe
Original ranch salad
Dressing -- prepared
6 oz Sharp cheddar cheese --
Shredded
1/4 c  Green onions -- sliced
Arrange chips in shallow baking dish. Pour salad dressing over chips.
Sprinkle cheese and green onions evenly over chips and dressing. Bake
at 350 degrees for 8 minutes or until cheese is melted slightly. Note:
Additional topping suggestions: sliced ripe olives, jalapeno peppers,
diced bell peppers. If you like nachos, you'll LOVE these!
