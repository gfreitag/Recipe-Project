Title: HOMEMADE CRACKER JACK (CARMELCORN)
Categories: Misc
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
6 c  Popped corn
1 c  Shelled peanuts
Heat honey and shortening in a saucepan until blended. Cool. Pour over
popcorn which has been mixed with peanuts, stirring as you pour. When
well-coated, spread on a pan in a single layer. Bake at 350 5-10 min
until crisp, sitrring several times.  The difference between crisp
(not brown) and burnt can be a matter of minutes. Package in plastic
bags and twist-tie.

From the cookbook "The Taming of the Candy Monster" by Vicki Lansky
