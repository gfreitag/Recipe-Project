Title: HERBED POTATO WEDGES
Categories: Vegetables, Cols.l&e
Yield: 2 Servings
1 lg Baking potato, sliced into
Wedges.
Olive oil
1/2 ts Lemon pepper blend seasoning
Scrub potato and slice into wedges (leaving skin on). Brush with
olive oil and sprinkle with seasoning to coat each side. Bake on a
cookie sheet in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes or until done.
