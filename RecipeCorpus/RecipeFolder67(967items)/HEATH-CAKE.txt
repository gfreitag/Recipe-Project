Title: HEATH CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Snacks, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
1    German chocolate cake mix
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
1    Jar - carmel topping
8 oz Cool whip
6 oz Heath candy bars; crushed
Make cake as directed on box. Bake as directed. When done and out off
the oven, punch holes in top of cake. Let cool about 5 minutes. Pour
1 can of sweetened condensed milk over the top. Follow with 1 jar of
carmel topping. Let cool completely. Top with cool whip and sprinkle
crushed candy over the top. Refrigerate for about an hour. Serve
cooled.
