Title: HERBED OVEN "FRIED" CHICKEN
Categories: W-watchers, Low-fat, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
3/4 c  Seasoned dried bread crumbs
1 ts Oregano leaves
Dash each salt, pepper and
Garlic powder
1 1/4 lb Skinned & boned chicken
Breasts, cut into 1-inch
Wide strips
In a small bowl, combine crums and seasonings; dip chicken strips in
water, then into crumb mixture, pressing crumbs to make sure they
adhere and chicken is thoroughly coated.

Spray a nonstick baking sheet with non-stick cooking spray; arrange
chicken on sheet and sprinkle evenly with remaining crumb mixture.
Bake at 350F until tender, about 20 minutes

Per serving:  239 Calories, 3g Fat, 4 Proteins, 1 Bread
