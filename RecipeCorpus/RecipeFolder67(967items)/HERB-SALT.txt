Title: HERB SALT
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Kosher salt -- coarse
5    Garlic cloves
2 ts Paprika
2 ts Chili powder
1 ts Turmeric
1 ts Poultry seasoning
1 ts White pepper -- freshly
Ground
1/2 ts Ginger -- ground
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Celery seed
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Dillweed -- dried
(Makes about 3/4 cup.) Place salt in processor. With machine running,
add garlic cloves and mix until minced, about 20 seconds. Transfer to
bowl. Stir in remaining ingredients. Store in airtight jar at room
temperature. Use Herb Salt to season Elephant Ear Potatoes.
