Title: HERB INFUSED OLIVE OIL
Categories: Italian, Dips, Val's
Yield: 1 Batch
1/2 c  OLIVE OIL
1 ea STRIP LEMON PEEL
2 ea CLOVES GARLIC, SLICED
2 ts ROSEMARY FRESH CHOPPED
1/2 ts RED PEPPER FLAKES
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS AND MICROWAVE FOR 1 MINUTE 15 SECOND ON HIGH,
COOL. REFRIGERATE OVER NIGHT. BRING TO ROOM TEMPURATURE BEFORE
SERVING.
