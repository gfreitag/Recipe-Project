Title: HAZELNUT-CARROT MUFFINS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Batch
2/3 c  Cooking oil
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 c  White flour
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Raw carrots, grated
1/2 c  Oregon hazelnuts
1/4 c  Raisins
Put eggs, sugar and oil in mixing bowl and beat well. Add milk. Sift
flour with cinnamon, salt, soda and baking powder. Add to first
mixture with carrots, nuts and raisins. Pour into muffin tins. Bake
at 350 for
20    minutes.
