Title: HE MAN BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/4 c  Ketchup
2 1/4 c  Water
2 ts Instant beef broth or cubes
1 1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 tb Chile powder
1 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Cayenne powder
1/2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 ds Liquid hot pepper
3 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Liquid smoke
Bring all ingredients to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer,
stirring occasionally, for 15 minutes. If not using immediately, cool
and refrigerate. Will keep several weeks. May be frozen in an
ice-cube tray and stored as cubes. Makes 1 quart.
