Title: HOMEMADE GALIANO 2
Categories: Misc., Liquers
Yield: 6 Serves
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
1/4 ts Anise extract
1 ts Vanilla extract or Vanilla
Bean Extract
3 dr Yellow food coloring
1    Fifth vodka
Prep time: 0 mins   Cook time: 0 mins   Difficulty: **

Combine sugar and water in a pan and bring to a boil. Boil for 1
minute and immediately reduce heat. Simmer for 1 hour or until
thickened. Remove from heat and cool. Pour sugar - water syrup into a
sterilized quart - size bottle. Add anise extract, vanilla and food
coloring. Stir gently and add the vodka. Cover and let the mixture
sit for 10 days to 2 weeks before serving.
