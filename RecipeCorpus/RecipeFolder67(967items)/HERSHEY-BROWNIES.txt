Title: HERSHEY BROWNIES
Categories: Cookies, Chocolate, Kathy's
Yield: 1 Unknown
1 c  Sugar
1    Stick oleo
4    Eggs
1 cn Hershey syrup
1 c  Flour
Nuts if desired
cream together sugar and oleo. add eggs and beat well. add syrup and
flour, mix well. add nuts if desired. spread on a well greased and
floured cookie sheet. bake at 350 degrees for 1/2 hour. top with
frosting or powder sugar. recipe from my sister Carol Flynn.
