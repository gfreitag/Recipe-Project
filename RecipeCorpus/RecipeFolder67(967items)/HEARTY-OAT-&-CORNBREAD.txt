Title: HEARTY OAT & CORNBREAD
Categories: Breads, Main dish, Side dish
1 c  Oats -- quick or old
fashion
Yield: 9 Servings
:
1 c  Cornmeal
1/2 c  Flour, All-Purpose
1 TB Baking Powder
1/4 ts Salt -- optional
1 c  Nonfat Milk
1/2 c  Corn Kernels
2 TB Applesauce
2    Egg Beaters. 99% Egg
Substitute -- or substitute
:
2 TB Onion -- finely chopped

Heat oven to 425 deg F. Spray an 8 or 9" square baking pan with
nonstick spray.

In a med. bowl, combine dry ingredients.

Add remaining ingredients that have been combined in a small bowl; mix
well.

Spread evenly into prepared pan.

Bake 20 - 25 min or until edges are lightly browned. Serve warm.
