Title: HELEN'S SCONES
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM---------------------------
2 1/4 c  Flour
1/4 c  Shortening
1/4 c  Sugar (+)
1/2 c  Milk or more
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1    Egg
Preheat pan to 375.  Sift flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Cut in
shortening.  Add egg and milk. Knead lightly and roll out 1/2" thick.
Cut in triangle. Bake on griddle or electric fry pan about 5 minutes
each side.
