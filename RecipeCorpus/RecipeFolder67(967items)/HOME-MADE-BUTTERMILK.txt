Title: HOME-MADE BUTTERMILK
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Skim or whole milk
2 tb Commercial buttermilk
Combine the skim milk and the commercial buttermilk, mixing well. Let
stand, covered, with plastic wrap in a warm place (similar to where
you would place bread to rise) for 16 to 24 hrs. or until thickened.
Refrigerate in a covered container. Shake well or mix in blender
before using. Store in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks. Makes 2 cups.
To replenish: Use 2 tbl. of your Buttermilk for commercial
buttermilk. Bette Leland
