Title: HERO PIZZAS
Categories: Pizza, Beef, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 lb Ground Beef
1 lb Loaf French Bread
6 oz Seasoned Tomato Paste
1 ts Oregano
2 c  Mushrooms, sliced
1/4 c  Italian Dressing
1/2 c  Mozzarella Cheese, shredded
Cook meat in a skillet over medium heat until browned, stirring to
crumble. Drain in a colander; pat dry well with paper towels, and set
aside.

Slice bread in half lengthwise; place cut sides up on a baking sheet.
Spread evenly with tomato paste; sprinklewith 1/2 ts oregano. Top
with meat and mushrooms. Spoon dressing over mushrooms; top with
cheese, and sprinkle with remaining oregano. Broil 6" from heat 2
minutes or until cheese melts.

Source: Safeway Nutritional Awareness Programme
