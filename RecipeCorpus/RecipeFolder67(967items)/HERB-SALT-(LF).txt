Title: HERB SALT (LF)
Categories: Low-fat, Seasonings
Yield: 1 Servings
-formatted MM:de
-T-Factor Diet bk
1/2 ts Basil;
1/4 ts Thyme;
1/4 ts Dill weed;
1/4 ts Celery seed;
1/4 ts Salt;
1/4 ts Dried parsley;
Combine all ingredients and grind with a mortar and pestle. Store in a
small herb jar.  Makes 1 3/4 teaspoons.

If substituting fresh herbs for the dry in this mixture; triple the
amount called for.  As fresh is less potent than dried.

Per teaspoon:  285mg sodium  Contains only trace amounts of other
nutrients too small to evaluate.
