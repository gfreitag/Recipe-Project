Title: HERSHEY BAR CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Chocolate, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Eggs
16 oz Can hershey syrup
6    Regular hershey bars, melt
1 tb Vanilla
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter,soft
3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Buttermilk OR
1 c  Milk+1 Tb vinegar
Mix syrup, candy bars and vanilla. Cream sugar and butter and add to
chococlate. add eggs to milk. Soift flour and baking soda and add 1/3
at a time. Add buttermilk and mix wel. Pour into greased angel food
pan and bake at 350 F for 1-1/2 hours
