Title: HEARTLAND RAISIN NUT BREAD
Categories:
Yield: 1 Loaf
2 1/4 ts Yeast
2 1/2 c  Bread flour
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
3 tb Wheat germ; toasted
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Brown sugar, dark
4 tb Powdered milk
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 c  Water, plus 2 tb
1 lg Egg
1 ts Lemon juice
3 tb Raisins dark
3 tb Raisins golden
1/3 c  Walnuts; chopped, toasted
Place all ingredients in machine and push start!  Use raisin bread
cycle if possible, adding fruit and nuts at beep.

From THE ULTIMATE BREAD MACHINE COOKBOOK by Lacalamita
