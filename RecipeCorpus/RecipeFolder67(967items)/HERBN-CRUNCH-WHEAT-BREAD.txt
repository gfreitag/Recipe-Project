Title: HERBÂ’N CRUNCH WHEAT BREAD
Categories: Savoury
Yield: 2 -lb loaf
1 1/2 c  Water
2 c  Bread flour
2 c  Wheat flour
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Dry milk
2 tb Margarine or butter, softene
2 ts Salt
2 ts Dried basil leaves
1 ts Dried thyme leaves
2 ts Yeast
2/3 c  Dry-roasted sunflower nuts
Measure carefully, placing all ingredients except sunflower nuts in
bread machine pan in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Add
sunflower nuts at the raisin/nut signal or 5 to 10 minutes before
last kneading cycle ends.

Select Basic/White cycle. Use medium or light crust color. Remove
baked bread from pan and cool on a wire rack.
