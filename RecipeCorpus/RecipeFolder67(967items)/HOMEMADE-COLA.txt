Title: HOMEMADE COLA
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 tb Simple syrup (see direction)
1/2 c  Soda water
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Pure lemon juice
1/8 ts Ground cinnamon;opt.
Instant coffee crystal;opt
SIMPLE SYRUP
1 c  Sugar
Water to make 1-1/2 cups liq
SIMPLE SYRUP:  Heat water.  Place sugar in measuring cup and add
enough hot water to make 1-1/2 cups liquid. COLA: Mix ingredients as
stated. Pour over ice and serve.
