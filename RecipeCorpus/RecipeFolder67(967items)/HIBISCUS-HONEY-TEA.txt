Title: HIBISCUS-HONEY TEA
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Dried hibiscus flowers or
1    Hibiscus tea bag
2    Cloves
3/4 c  Water
1 ts Honey
Lemon peel for garnish
Place the hibiscus flowers or tea bag and cloves at the bottom of a
teapot. Heat the water to boiling and pour the water into the teapot.
Let the tea steep for 3-5 min; then strain or pour it into a cup.
Stir the honey into the tea and garnish with a small slice of lemon
peel.
