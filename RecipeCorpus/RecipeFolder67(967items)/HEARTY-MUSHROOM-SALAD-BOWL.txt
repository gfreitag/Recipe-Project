Title: HEARTY MUSHROOM SALAD BOWL
Categories: Salad
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 lb Fresh mushrooms or
2 cn (6-8 oz ea. sliced
Muchrooms)
1 lb ( 2cups) diced boiled ham
3 c  Cliced cooked potatoes
(chilled)
1 pk (10 oz) frozen peas
(cooked & chilled)
3/4 c  Olive oil or salad oil
1/2 c  Sliced scallions
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 ts Basil leaves, crumbled
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
1/4 ts Ground cumin seed
Rinse, pat dry and slice fresh muchrooms or drain canned mushrooms.
In a large salad bowl arrange clusters of mushrooms, ham, potatoes,
peas and scallions. Mix remaining ingredients. Pour over salad, toss
gently. Serve on lettuce lined platter if desired.
