Title: HERBED POTATO CASSEROLE
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
8 oz Herbed cream cheese, cubed
2 c  Milk
5 lg Potatoes peeled
1/2 ts Each salt and pepper
2 tb Freshly grated Parmesan,
-cheese
4 ts Butter, in bits
In saucepan, stir together cream cheese and milk over medium heat for
about 5 minutes or just until smooth. Remove from heat.

Slice potatoes thinly; arrange in overlapping layers in greased 11x7
inch baking dish, sprinkling with salt and pepper. Pour milk mixture
over top. Sprinkle with Parmesan; dot with butter.
