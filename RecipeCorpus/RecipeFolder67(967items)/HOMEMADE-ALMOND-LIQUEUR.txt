Title: HOMEMADE ALMOND LIQUEUR
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Batch
1 c  ;Water
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Almond extract
1 1/2 c  Vodka; OR brandy, to taste
Mix sugar and water and boil till sugar is dissolved into a syrup.
Cool completely.  Mix 1/2 cup syrup and mix with vodka & extract. Use
the other half for another bottle. Store a few days before using.
