Title: HOME-MADE STRAWBERRY ICE CREAM
Categories: Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 2 Servings
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
1 qt Fresh strawberries, mashed
-or pureed
4    Eggs, beaten
1 ts Vanilla
In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well. Pour into
ice cream freezer and prepare as normal. Makes 2 quarts.
