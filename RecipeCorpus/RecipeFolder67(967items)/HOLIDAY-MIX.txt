Title: HOLIDAY MIX
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 20 Cups
3 c  Corn chex
3 c  Rice chex
3 c  Cheerios
3 c  Pretzels
3 c  Dry roasted peanuts
1 lb Melted white chocolate
M & M's
Mix all ingredients except for M & M's together in a brown paper bag.
Shake to coat evenly. Spread out on cookie sheets to dry. Break apart
and add M & M's when cool. Store air tight.
