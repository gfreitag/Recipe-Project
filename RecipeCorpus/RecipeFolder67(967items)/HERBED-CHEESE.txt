Title: HERBED CHEESE
Categories: Cheese, Spreads, Seasonings, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Recipe
1/2 ts Minced garlic
4    Green onions; chopped
1 tb Dill weed
1/2 ts Salt
16 oz Cream cheese
1 tb Dried parsley
1 tb Milk
1/2 ts Pepper
"Wonderful spread on crackers or melba toast."

Blend all ingredients in a food processor until thoroughly mixed.

* Source: Linda Dudley, Blue Springs, Missouri * The Herb Companion,
August/September 1993 * Typed for you by Karen Mintzias
