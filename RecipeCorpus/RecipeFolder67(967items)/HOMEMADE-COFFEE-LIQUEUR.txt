Title: HOMEMADE COFFEE LIQUEUR
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Batch
4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Instant coffee
3 c  ;Water
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Vodka, high-proof
3 tb Vanilla
Combine sugar and water; boil till sugar dissolves. Reduce heat to
simmer & simmer 1 hour. LET COOL. Stir in vodka & vanilla. Pour up.
