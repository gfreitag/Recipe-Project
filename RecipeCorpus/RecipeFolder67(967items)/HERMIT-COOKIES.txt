Title: HERMIT COOKIES
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sugar
3 c  Flour
1/2 c  Butter
1 ts Cloves
1/2 c  Molasses
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Lukewarm coffee
1 c  Raisins
1    Egg, beaten
Preheat oven to 350.  Cream sugar and shortening.  Add molasses then
soda which has been dissolved in the coffee. Add the sifted dry
ingredients. Add raisin then the beaten egg. Make in long strips on a
greased cookie sheet. Bake for 10 minutes.

This recipe is _really_ easy and tastes great.
