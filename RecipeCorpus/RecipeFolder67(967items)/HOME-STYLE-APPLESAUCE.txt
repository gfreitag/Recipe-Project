Title: HOME-STYLE APPLESAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 4 Servings
10 md Cooking apples -- peeled and
Diced
1/2 c  Water
3/4 c  Sugar
Cinnamon
In slow-cooking pot, conbine apples and water.  Cover and cook on low
for 4 to 6 hours or until apples are very soft. Add sugar and cook on
low another 30 minutes. Sprinkle with cinnamon at serving time.
