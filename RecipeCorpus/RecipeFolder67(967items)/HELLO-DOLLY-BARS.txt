Title: HELLO DOLLY BARS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Recipe
1/2 c  Margarine
2 c  Crushed vanilla wafers
1 c  Chocolate chips
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 c  Coconut
1 cn Eagle Brand or other
-sweetend condensed milk
Melt butter. Layer in a 6 x 9 pan with crumbs, chips, nuts and
coconut. Drizzle canned milk over the top. Bake at 350 for 30
minutes. Cool before cutting. Note: You can add peanut butter chips
to this for a change of pace.
