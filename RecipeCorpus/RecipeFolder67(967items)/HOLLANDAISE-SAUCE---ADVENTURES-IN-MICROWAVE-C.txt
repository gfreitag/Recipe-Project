Title: HOLLANDAISE SAUCE - ADVENTURES IN MICROWAVE C
Categories: Sauces, Microwave, Dairy, Eggs
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter or
Margarine
1/4 c  Half-and-half
2    Egg yolks, beaten
1 tb Lemon juice or vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
Dash ground red pepper
1. Melt butter in a 1-quart glass measuring pitcher in microwave oven
(about 1 minute at High).  Add half-and-half, egg yolks, lemon juice,
salt and red pepper; mix thoroughly. Cook uncovered 2-1/2 minutes at
Medium, or until sauce is thickened; stir.

2. Remove from oven and beat until light.

Yield: 3/4 cup

SOURCE: Adventures in Microwave Cooking
