Title: HOLIDAY COOKIES
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 1/4 c  Soft-a-Silk cake flour
3/4 c  Walnut meal
Mix butter, sugar, and vanilla; then add flour, then walnut meal.
Roll into small balls and bake 6-8 minutes at 400F. While still warm,
roll in powdered sugar.
