Title: HERBED PORK LOIN
Categories: Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Chopped fresh thyme or -- 2
ts Dried
2 tb Chopped fresh sage or -- 2
ts Dried
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cracked black pepper
1    Boneless pork loin roast --
About 2 lbs
1 tb Olive oil
Preheat  oven  to 450.   Combine herbs,  salt and pepper. Brush meat
with oil and roll in herb mixture.
Roast pork 15  min.   Reduce temperature to 375 and continue cooking
until thermometer inserted into center of meat registers 155 degrees,
about 45 min.
Let  sit  10  min.  before slicing.
