Title: HOLIDAY HIGHLAND DRESSING
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 1 Batch
2 c  Quaker Oats, uncooked
-- (quick or old-fashioned)
4 c  Dry bread cubes
1/2 c  Butter or margarine; melted
1 md Onion; grated
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Poultry seasoning (optional)
1/4 ts Sage (optional)
2 tb Water
Place oats and bread cubes in large bowl.  Combine remaining
ingredients. Add to oats mixture; toss gently to mix.

Stuff lightly into body cavity and neck region of turkey. Roast
according to standard roasting directions. For one 10 to 12-lb.
turkey.
