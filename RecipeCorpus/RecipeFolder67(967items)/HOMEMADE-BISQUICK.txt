Title: HOMEMADE BISQUICK
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 4 Servings
CHUCK OZBURN HBWK07A-----
12 c  Sifted flour
6 tb Baking powder
6 ts Salt
3 c  Shortening
Sift dry ingredients together 3 times; cut in the shorten- ing; stir
and mix until  you have a granular texture, like cornmeal; put
mixture in glass jars and cover lightly; makes about 4 quarts. Chuck
in Pok 6/7/93
