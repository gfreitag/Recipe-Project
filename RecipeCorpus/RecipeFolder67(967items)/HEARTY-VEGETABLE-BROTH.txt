Title: HEARTY VEGETABLE BROTH
Categories: Low-fat, Soups, Mcdougall
Yield: 1 Servings
5 qt Water
2 lg Onions; peeled & quartered
4    Cl Garlic; peeled
4    Celery; thickly sliced
4 lg Carrots, scrubbed; thickly s
2 lg Potatoes, scrubbed; coarsely
2    Leeks, white parts only, was
Several large sprigs fresh p
2    Bay leaves
10    Whole peppercorns
4 tb Soy sauce
Recipe by: The New McDougall Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:15 Place
all of the ingredients in a large soup pot. Bring to a boil, reduce
the heat, cover, and simmer over low heat for 3 to 4 hours. Strain
the broth and discard the vegetables.
Freeze in 1- to 2-cup containers for use in recipes calling for
vegetable stock or broth. From the collection of Sue Smith, S.Smith34,
