Title: HEALTH GRAIN BREAD
Categories: Electric, Nuts, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Bread Flour
1/4 c  Whole-Wheat Flour
2 ts Nonfat Dried Milk
1 ts Salt
2 ts Honey
1 tb Applesauce
1/4 c  Grated or chopped carrots
1/4 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Sunflower seeds
1/4 c  Bran flakes
1 tb Wheat germ
2 ts Yeast
This recipe can be used in the regular, rapid or delayed time bake
cycles. ~FOR 1-LB. LOAF
