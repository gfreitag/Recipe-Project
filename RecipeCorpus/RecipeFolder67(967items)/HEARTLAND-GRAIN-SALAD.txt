Title: HEARTLAND GRAIN SALAD
Categories: Wasnone
Yield: 32 Servings
2 c  Wheatberries
2 c  Wild rice
2 c  Barley
2 c  Basmati rice
2 c  Toasted seeds and nuts --
Assortment
2 c  Dried fruits -- assortment
1 bn Parsley -- finely chopped
Kosher salt and pepper
1 c  Virgin olive oil
1/2 c  Balsamic vinegar -- or
Raspberry vinegar
Cook and chilll the grains. Toast the seeds and nuts: some assortment
of pumpkins seeds, sesame seeds, sunflower seeds, chopped peanuts,
hazelnuts or cashews. Chop dried fuits: some assortment of apricots,
dates, figs, raisins, or prunes. Combine all the ingredients and let
set overnight. Adjust the seasonings and dressing before serving; you
may need more of both.

: Lou Jane Temple, Death by Rhubarb Categories
