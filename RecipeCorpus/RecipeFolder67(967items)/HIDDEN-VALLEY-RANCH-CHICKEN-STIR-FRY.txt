Title: HIDDEN VALLEY RANCH CHICKEN STIR-FRY
Categories: Chicken
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 lb Chicken breast strips
-boneless, skinless
1 pk Ranch Salad Dressing Mix
-Hidden Valley Original
16 oz Vegetable medley, thawed
2 tb Water
Heat vegetable oil in large skillet.  Add chicken breast strips. Stir
in one package Hidden Valley Original Ranch Salad Dressing Mix to coat
chicken. Add thawed vegetable medley and water. Stir-fry about 2
minutes.
