Title: HOLIDAY CHEESE BALL
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese
1 c  Grated Sharp Cheddar Cheese
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Grated Mild Colby Cheese
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan Cheese
8 oz Cream Cheese
1 TB Milk
1 sm Clove Garlic -- crushed
Or 1/2 Tsp Garlic Powder
ds Salt
ds Worcestershire Sauce
:
:
:
1/2 c  Chopped Nuts
(Walnuts/Pecans/Sesame
Seeds)
:
:

Have all ingredients at 60 degrees for easier mixing. Combine all
ingredients except nuts. Form into ball or log and roll in nuts. (You
may need to chill before forming into ball or log.) Refrigerate until
used. Serve with crackers, ray veggies or fresh fruit. These also
make lovely holidy gifts. You can color the coating by adding mild
paprika or chopped parsley with the nuts.
