Title: Grandma Clark's Cranberry Jelly
Categories: Toppings, Preserve
Yield: 12 servings
------------------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM------------------------------
2 c  Sugar
4 c  Cranberries, fresh or frozen
1 c  Water
Cook berries in water til mushy and put through seive.  Add sugar and boil
15 minutes til it jells.  Bottle.

Super! HP H. Peagram
