[For a 12-Pound Turkey]
Title: Herb Stuffing
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 1/4 c  Water
12 c  White bread, cubed-24 slices
3/4 c  Parsley
1 lg Onion, chopped
1 c  Butter or margarine-2 sticks
1 c  Celery, finely chopped
2 ts Granulated Chicken Bouillon
1 ts Poultry seasoning
1.  SautÂ‚ onion in butter or margarine until soft in a medium-size frying
pan; stir in celery, chicken broth, poultry seasoning, salt, pepper and
water; heat to boiling.

2.  Pour over bread and parsley in a large bowl; toss lightly until evenly
moist.

Makes approximately 10 Cups or enough to stuff a 12-pound bird.
