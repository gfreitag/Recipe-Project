Title: CRANBERRY HORSERADISH SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Recipe
2 c  Cranberries, finely chopped
1/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Prepared Horseradish
In a small saucepan over medium heat, cook cranberries and sugar,
stirring constantly until mixture is fully blended, about 2 mins.
Mixture does not need to cook.
