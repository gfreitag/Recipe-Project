Title: COTTAGE CHEESE & RAISIN FILLING
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Well drained cottage cheese
2    Crushed saltines or
Crackers
1 tb Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Seedless raisins, chopped
Very fine.
To the cup of well drained and sieved cottage cheeese, andd the
crackers mayonnaise, and raisins. Mix throughly. Jeannie Allen
