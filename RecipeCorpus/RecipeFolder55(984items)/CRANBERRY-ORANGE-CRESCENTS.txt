Title: CRANBERRY ORANGE CRESCENTS
Categories: Berries, Appetizers
Yield: 8 Servings
1 cn (8oz) Pillsbury Crescent
Dinner Rolls
1/3 c  Finely chopped cranberries
2 tb Cracker crumbs
1 ts Grated orange peel
1 ts Sugar
Unroll dough; separate into 8 triangles. Combine remaining
ingredients; spread evenly over each triangle. Roll, place and bake
Crescents as directed on package label.
