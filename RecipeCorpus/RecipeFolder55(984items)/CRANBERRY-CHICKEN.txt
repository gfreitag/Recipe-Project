Title: CRANBERRY CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lb Broiler or fryer chicken cut
1/2 c  Seasoned flour
1/4 c  Corn oil
1 ea Chopped onion
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 c  Whole cranberry sauce
Shake chicken pieces in a bag with seasoned flour. Heat oil in
skillet, saute chicken turning once to browwn. Combine remaining
ingredients and pour over chicken. Cover and cook slowly 40 to 45
minutes or until chicken is tender. From "Make It Better with
CRANBERRIES" Cape Cod Cranberry Growers' Association, Founded 1888
