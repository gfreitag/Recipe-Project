Title: COUSCOUS WITH EGGPLANT
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Olive Oil
1 tb Shallots or Scallions, chop
2 tb Onions, finely chopped
1/4 ts Turmeric
1/4 ts Coriander, ground
1 c  Eggplant, diced
1 x  Salt
1 x  Black Pepper, freshly groun
1 c  Water
1 c  Couscous, precooked
1 tb Butter
1 tb Lemon Juice, fresh
Heat the oil in a saucepan, ad the shallots, onions, turmeric,
coriander, eggplant, salt and pepper. Cook over medium-high heat,
stirring, until wilted, but do not brown. Add the water, bring to a
boil, add the couscous and blend well. Cover tightly, remove from the
heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Add the butter and lemon juice,
stir and blend with a fork to separate the grains. Keep warm. From
The Gazette, 91/10/23. Posted by James Lor.
