Title: CRANBERRY PUDDING
Categories: Puddings, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Sifted flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
2/3 c  Finely chopped suet
1 c  Chopped cranberries
1    Egg
1/3 c  Milk
Mix ingredients in the order given.  Turn into greased mold, cover
with waxed paper and steam 2 hours.
