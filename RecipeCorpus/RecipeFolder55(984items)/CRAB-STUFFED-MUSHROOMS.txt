Title: CRAB STUFFED MUSHROOMS
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 10 Servings
20    Large Mushrooms
Italian dressing
8 oz Crabmeat, well picked
3/4 c  Fresh breadcrumbs
2    Eggs, beaten
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Onion, minced
1 ts Lemon juice
Marinate mushrooms in Italian dressing; drain. Combine crabmeat, a
half-cup of breadcrumbs, eggs, mayonnaise, onion and lemon juice.
Fill mushrooms. Top with remaining breadcrumbs; bake at 375 for 15
minutes. Yields 20 appetizers or 4 main course servings.
