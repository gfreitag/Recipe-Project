Title: CREAM CHEESE POUND CAKE  FBFJ97A
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 lb Butter
8 oz Cream cheese
3 c  Sugar
6    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Almond extract
3 c  Flour
Cream butter and cheese; blend in sugar.  Add eggs one at a time
and continue to blend.  Add extracts and flour and beat until smooth
and light. Pour batter into greased and floured tube pan.  Bake in
325 degree oven 1 1/2 hours.
