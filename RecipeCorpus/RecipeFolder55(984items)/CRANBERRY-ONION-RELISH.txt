Title: CRANBERRY-ONION RELISH
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Chopped Onion
1 sm Clove Garlic Minced
1 c  Cranberries
3 tb Sugar
1 tb Water
1 ts Cider Vinegar
Coat A Small Saucepan With Cooking Spray; Place Over Medium-High Heat
Until Hot.  Add Onion & Garlic. Saute Until Tender. Add
Cranberries,Sugar & 1 T. Water. Bring To A Boil; Cook 3 To 5 Min. OR
Until Mixture Is Thickened. Stir in Vinegar.  Store in An Airtight
Container & Refrigerate. Serve At Room Temperature. (Fat O.1. Chol.
0.)
