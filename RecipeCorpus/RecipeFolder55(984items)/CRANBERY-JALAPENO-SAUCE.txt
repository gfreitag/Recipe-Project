Title: CRANBERY-JALAPENO SAUCE
Categories: Condiment, Fruit
Yield: 1 -3/4 cups
12 oz Cranberries (1 bag)
3/4 c  Lt brown sugar; firmly packd
1 tb ORTEGA diced Jalapenos -OR-
-1 (4-oz.) can ORTEGA diced
-green chiles
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Chopped cilantro or parlsey
In heavy saucepan, over medium heat, heat cranberries, sugar,
jalapenos or chiles and vinegar to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer for 20
to 25 minutes or until thick, stirring occasionally. Stir in cilantro
or parsley. Cool. Cover.  Store in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
