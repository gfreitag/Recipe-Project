Title: CRANBERRY & APPLE CRISP
Categories: Dessert, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Cooking apples
1/4 c  Raw sugar
1 1/2 c  Fresh cranberries
Crumble
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds
1/4 c  Raw cane or demerara sugar
1 1/4 c  Whole-wheat flour
1 c  Oatmeal
* Serve hot with natural yogurt or serve cold with ice cream

1.  Peel, core and dice the apples.  Measure about 4
3/4    cups.

2.  Cook in a saucepan with the sugar and about 2 tbs water until
slightly softened.

3.  Add the cranberies and cook for a further minute. Remove from
heat.

4.  Melt butter or margarine and add the sunflower seeds. Fry gently
for a few minutes.

5.  Meanwhile, mix together the other ingredients in a large bowl,
rubbing in the sugar if lumpy.

6.  Pour in the butter and sunflower seeds and combine to form a loose
mixture.

7.  Place the fruit in a large, shallow ovenproof dish and sprinkle
the crisp topping over.

8.  Cook at 350Ã¸F for about 40 minutes or until the top is golden and
crisp.

Time:  Preparation takes about 20 minutes; cooking takes about 50
minutes.
