Title: CRANBERRY MULLED CIDER
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 12 Servings
24    Whole cloves
1    Orange
8 c  Apple cider
4 c  Cranberry juice
1/2 c  Sugar
2    Cinnamon sticks
1 ts Angostura bitters
24    Whole allspice
1 c  Dark rum
Press cloves into orange and place it in a heavy saucepan. Add water,
cranberry juice, sugar, cinnamon, bitters and allspice. Bring to a
simmer, stirring until sugar dissolves.  Reduce heat to very low,
cover and simmer 1 1/2 hours.  Strain into punch bowl or crockpot.

Two tablespoons rum to each cup is optional.  Walt MM

From Bon Appetite, December 1994
