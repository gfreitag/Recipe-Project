Title: CRANBERRY RELISH (WEDMAN)
Categories: Sauces, Low-fat
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  Fresh or frozen cranberries
1 c  Orange juice
8 pk Equal sweetener 16 tsp 1/3 c
Cook the cranberries with the orange juice until thick. Cool. Add the
sweetener.  Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

1/2 cup serving - 49 cal, 1 fruit exchange 12 grams carbohydrate, 0
pro, 0 fat, 1 mg sodium, 124 potassium, 0 cholesterol

Source:  Am. Diabetes Assoc. Holiday Cookbook by Betty Wedman 1986
Shared but not tested by Elizabeth Rodier Nov 93
