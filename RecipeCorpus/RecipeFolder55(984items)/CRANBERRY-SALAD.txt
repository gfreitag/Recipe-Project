Title: CRANBERRY SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
8 oz Cream cheese
2 tb Miracle whip
2 tb Sugar
1 cn Chunk pineapple (drained)
1 cn Whole cranberry sauce
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1 pk Dream whip
Mix dream whip in a seperate bowl.  Whip cream cheese, miracle whip,
sugar, cranberries and walnuts.  Fold in dream whip. Cut pineapple
into smaller chunks and add. Chill and serve.
