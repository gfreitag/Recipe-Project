Title: CRANBERRY TEA
Categories: Beverages, American
Yield: 4 Quarts
2    Family-sized tea bags
1 ts Whole cloves
2    Cinnamon sticks (2.5 inch)
1 qt Boiling water
2 c  Sugar
2 qt Water
2 c  Cranberry juice cocktail
1 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Combine first three ingredients in a 1-qt container; add boiling
water, cover and steep 5 mins. Pour mixture through wire-mesh
strainer, discarding tea bags and spices. Stir in sugar and remaining
ingredients. Serve over ice.
