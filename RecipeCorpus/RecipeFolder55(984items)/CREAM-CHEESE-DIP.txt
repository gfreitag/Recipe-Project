Title: CREAM CHEESE DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
2 pk (8-oz) cream cheese
1/4 c  Chopped onions
1 cn Drained crushed pineapple
Lawry's salt to taste
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Chopped walnuts
Mix all ingredients together and place in bowl. Optional to sprinkle
with 1/4 cup of chopped walnuts.
