Title: CRANBERRY BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Soda
2 tb Butter
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1    Juice of orange & rind
Boiling water to make 3/4 c
1 c  Nuts
1 c  Raw cranberries or can whole
Sift dry ingredients. Cream butter and sugar and add beaten egg.Add
juice and flour alternately, add nuts and raw cranberries or the
whole cranberry sauce 1 cup.
