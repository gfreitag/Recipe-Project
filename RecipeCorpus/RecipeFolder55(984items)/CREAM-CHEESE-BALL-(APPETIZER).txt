Title: CREAM CHEESE BALL (APPETIZER)
Categories: Appetizers, Beef, Cheese
Yield: 1 Servings
16 oz Cream cheese
2 pk Chipped beef; minced
Chopped scallions; (amount t
Garlic powder or garlic salt
Mix softened cream cheese and other ingrediants. Shape. Let chill
overnight.  Serve with crackers.

I got the recipe from my sister-in-law from Springhill, Florida. I
personally do like cream cheese or chipped beef; yet this combo with
crackers is very addictive.
