Title: COTTAGE CHEESE PIE (M_C-TX)
Categories: Pies, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Cottage cheese
1/2 c  Sugar
3    Well beaten eggs
1 tb Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
Sieve cottage cheese.  Mix ingredients and put in pie shell. Sprinkle
cinnamon on top.  Bake for 20 minutes at 350 or 375 until done. Mrs.
Erma E. Derr
