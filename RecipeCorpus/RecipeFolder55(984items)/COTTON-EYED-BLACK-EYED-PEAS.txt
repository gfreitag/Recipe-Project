Title: COTTON-EYED BLACK-EYED PEAS
Categories: Vegetables, Beans
Yield: 12 Servings
Mrs. l. f. hooker
2 c  Celery; chopped
2 c  Onions; chopped
(14 1/2-oz cans)
(15-oz cans)
1 ts Sugar; optional
1/2 lb Bacon
2 c  Bell pepper; chopped
2 cn Peeled tomatoes; undrained
3 cn Black-eyed peas; undrained
Salt and pepper
Fry bacon, reserving 3 tablespoons of drippings. Saute the celery,
bell pepper and onions in bacon grease. Add tomatoes, black-eyed
peas, salt, pepper, sugar and crumbled bacon; simmer 30 minutes.
