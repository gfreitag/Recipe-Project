Title: CRAB FONDUE (ABSOLUTELY MARVELOUS)
Categories: Appetizers, Marie, Great
Yield: 12 Servings
3 cn Crabmeat
24 oz Cream cheese
2 ts Prepared mustard
2/3 c  White wine
1 sm Onion, diced
1 ts Powdered sugar
1 ds Seasoned salt
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1    Loaf French bread, cut
Into 1" cubes
Combine all ingredients except the French bread in a pan. Stir, over
low heat, until the cheese melts and all ingredients are well mixed.

Serve over a low flame.  Use the bread cubes as dippers.
