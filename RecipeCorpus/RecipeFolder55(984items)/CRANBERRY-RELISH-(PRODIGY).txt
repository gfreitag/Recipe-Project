Title: CRANBERRY RELISH (PRODIGY)
Categories: Fruits, Sauces, Relishes
Yield: 1 Servings
12 oz Fresh cranberries
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Sugar, if necessary
IN SKILLET, COMBINE CRANBERRIES and 3/4 cup sugar. Cook over moderate
heat, stirring often to avoid scorching, until sugar has melted and
cranberries are cooked, about 6 minutes. Taste for sweetness and add
remaining 1/4 cup sugar if too tart, stirring in sugar until
completely dissolved. Remove from heat and cool.

Makes 2 Cups
