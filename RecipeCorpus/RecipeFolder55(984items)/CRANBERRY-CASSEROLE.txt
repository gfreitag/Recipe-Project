Title: CRANBERRY CASSEROLE
Categories: Side dish, Holidays
Yield: 1 Recipe
3 c  Granny Smith Apples,
-peeled and chopped
2 c  Raw cranberries
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  White sugar
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 1/3 c  Quick oats
1 c  Chopped walnuts or pecans
1 1/3 c  Brown sugar
1    Stick butter, melted
Preheat oven to 325

In a shallow 2 qt. casserole, mix cranberries and chopped apples.

In a small bowl mix lemon juice and white sugar. Sprinkle this
mixture over the cranberries and apples and mix together until just
moistened.

In another bowl mix together all ingredients for topping and sprikle
over the top of the cranberry mixture.

Bake at 325 for 1 hr. and 15 min.

*Be sure to use quick oats.  If regular oats are used the recipe will
be runny. Submitted By RHOMMEL <RHOMMEL@IX.NETCOM.COM> On FRI, 1 DEC
1995 143310 ~0500
