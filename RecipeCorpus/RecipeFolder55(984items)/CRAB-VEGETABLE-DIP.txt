Title: CRAB VEGETABLE DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Easy, Spreads
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Garlic
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Tobasco sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1    Boiled egg, hard, chopped
1 c  Crab meat, drained
Mix all ingredients together and let sit overnight.
