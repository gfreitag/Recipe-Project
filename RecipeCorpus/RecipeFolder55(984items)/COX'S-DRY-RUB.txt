Title: COX'S DRY RUB
Categories: Bar-b-q, Spices, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
5 tb Paprika
4 tb MSG
2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts White pepper
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cloves
4 tb Seasoned pepper
4 ts Onion powder
3 tb Chili powder
1 tb Salt
Combine all ingredients and store in a tightly sealed glass jar. Rub
liberally into meats before cooking.
