Title: CREAM CHEESE SALSA SPREAD
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Regular or light cream
-cheese (8 oz)
PACE Picante Sauce
Chopped fresh cilantro (opt)
Place cream cheese on rimmed plate; let stand at room temperature
about 30 minutes to soften. Pour PACE Picante Sauce over and around
cream cheese. Garnish with cilantro, if desired. Serve with vegetable
dippers, crackers or chips.
