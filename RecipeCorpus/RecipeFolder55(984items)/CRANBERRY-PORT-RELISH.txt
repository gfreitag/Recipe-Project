Title: CRANBERRY PORT RELISH
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 1 Recipe
3 c  (12-oz pkg) fresh or
-frozen cranberries
1 md Onion minced
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
1 c  Golden raisins
1 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Port
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Ground cinnamon
Combine all ingredients except cranberries in heavy saucepan. Bring
to boil then simmer for 15 minutes. Add cranberries and simmer
another 20-30 minutes. Towards the end the relish gets very thick.
Make sure you stir it often, since it will stick to the pan.
