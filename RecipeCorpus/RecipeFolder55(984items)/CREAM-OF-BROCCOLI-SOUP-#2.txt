Title: CREAM OF BROCCOLI SOUP #2
Categories: Soups
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Fresh broccoli OR
2 pk (10-oz) frozen broccoli
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Chicken flavor instant
Bouillon OR
6    Chicken bouillion cubes
1/2 ts Thyme
1/4 c  Margarine/butter
1/8 ts Pepper
3 c  Water
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Flour
2 c  Light cream/milk
Thaw broccoli, if time allows. In large saucepan combine broccoli,
onion, water, bouillon, thyme and garlic powder. Bring to boil;
reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes or until broccoli is tender. Stir
to break up broccoli. In blender, blend 1/3 mixture at a time, blend
until smooth. Return to saucepan. Set aside. In medium pan melt
butter; stir in flour and pepper until smooth. Stir in cream 0- cook
and stir until thickened. Add to broccoli mixture. Heat thoroughly.
Do not boil.
