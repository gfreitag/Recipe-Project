Title: CRAN-APPLE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
16 oz Can whole-berry cranberry
-sauce
1 md Unpeeled tart apple; diced
1    Celery rib; thinly sliced
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
In a bowl, combine the cranberry sauce, apple and celery. Cover and
refrigerate. Stir in walnuts just before serving. Yield: 4-6 servings.

Source: Quick Cooking May/June 1998
