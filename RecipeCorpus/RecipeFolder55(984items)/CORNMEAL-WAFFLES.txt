Title: CORNMEAL WAFFLES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Yellow corn meal
2    Eggs (separated)
3 ts Baking powder
1 1/2    To 1 3/4 cups milk
2 tb Sugar
6 tb Liquid shortening (oil)
1 ts Salt
Mix and sift dry ingredients.  Add egg YOLKS, milk and shortening.
Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Bake in waffle iron until done.
