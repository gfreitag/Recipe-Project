Title: COTTAGE CHEESE CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Cottage cheese, cream style
8 oz Noodles, med. width; cooked
1 c  Sour cream
12 oz Cheese, cheddar; shredded
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Chives; snipped
1 tb Margarine
Combine all ingredients except margarine.  Spoon into a buttered 1-1/2
quart casserole dish; dot with margarine. Cover and bake at 350
degrees for 30 minutes.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, May, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy
Coleman.
