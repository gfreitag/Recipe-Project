Title: CORNSTARCH FINGER PAINT
Categories: Children, Kooknet, Cyberealm
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Cornstarch
4 c  Water -- boiling
Food coloring
Dissolve cornstarch in a small amount of cold water. In pot gradually
add boiling water stirring constantly, until mixture thickens. Add
coloring.
