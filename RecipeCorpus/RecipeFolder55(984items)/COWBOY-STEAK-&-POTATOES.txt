Title: COWBOY STEAK & POTATOES
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
2 tb Blue cheese
1 lg Garlic clove; chopped fine
1 ts Black pepper
1 ts Salt
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Worcesterchire
1 tb Powdered instant coffee
-- heaping measure
1/4 c  Cooking oil
2 lb Top sirloin
-- cut 2-inches thick
Vegetable cooking spray
8 md Red potatoes
-- cut into 1-1/2" wedges
1 pk Dried onion soup mix
1. Mix marinade ingredients in food processor or blender.

2. Pour over steak and rub into the surface. Marinate steak several
hours or overnight in the refrigerator.

3. To prepare potatoes, brush with oil and coat with onion soup mix.

4. Spray rack of broiler pan with vegetable cooking spray. Place
steak and potatoes on the rack and broil 6 inches from the heat. Cook
for 30 minutes until steaks are medium-rare (150 F) and potatoes are
tender, turning once. 5.Carve steak across grain into thin slices.
Serve with red potatoes.
