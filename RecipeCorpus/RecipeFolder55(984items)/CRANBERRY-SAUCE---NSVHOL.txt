Title: CRANBERRY SAUCE - NSVHOL
Categories: Side dish, Fruits
Yield: 2 Cups
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
1 pk Cranberries (12 ounce)
In 2 quart saucepan over medium heat, heat sugar and water to
boiling. Add cranberries and return to the boil. Reduce heat to low;
cover and simmer 7 minutes or until berries pop. Refrigerate
overnight.
