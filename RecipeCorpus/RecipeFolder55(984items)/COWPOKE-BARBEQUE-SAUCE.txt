Title: COWPOKE BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Sweet, Sauces
Yield: 1 Serving
1/4 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
1/4 c  Salad oil
2 ts Salt
1 ts Garlic powder
1 c  Vinegar
3/4 c  Lemon juice
2 c  Water
1 ea Worcestershire sauce - 5 oz
1 ea Tabasco sauce - to taste
Combine all ingredients in saucepan.  Bring quickly to boiling point.
Reduce heat and simmer ten minutes. Cool and pour into one-quart
container. (will keep refrigerated several weeks.)
