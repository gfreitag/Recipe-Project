Title: COTTAGE CHEESE RELISH
Categories: Misc
Yield: 4 Servings
3/4 c  Sour cream
12 oz Cottage cheese, large curd;
-well drained
1/4 c  Green pepper; diced, chilled
1/4 c  Green onion; sliced, chilled
2 tb Pimiento; chipped
1/4 ts Salt
ds Pepper
Lettuce
Blend sour cream with cottage cheese; stir in pepper, onion, pimiento,
salt, and pepper.  Serve on lettuce.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine June, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy
Coleman.
