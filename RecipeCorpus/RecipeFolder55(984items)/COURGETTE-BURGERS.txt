Title: COURGETTE BURGERS
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Lean minced meat
1 sm Onion grated.
3 sm Courgettes grated.
1 ts Soy sauce
1 ts Grated fresh root ginger.
1    Egg yolk
Seasoning.
Combine all the ingredients well in a bowl. Shape into four burgers
with wet hands. Rill barbecue or dry fry using medium heat until
cooked through.
