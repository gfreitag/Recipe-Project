Title: CRANACHAN
Categories: Scotland, Grains/rice
Yield: 1 Servings
4 tb Medium oatmeal, lightly
-toasted
2 c  Whipping cream
-a little (heather) honey
-to taste
2 tb (or more!<G>) malt whisky
-(Drambuie may be
-substituted)
2 c  Raspberries
Whip the cream until frothy, adding honey and whisky. Fold in the
toasted, cooled oatmeal.  Place some fruit in the bottom of tall
glasses, layer in the cream, repeating and finishing with the cream
on top. Decorate with a single piece of fruit.
