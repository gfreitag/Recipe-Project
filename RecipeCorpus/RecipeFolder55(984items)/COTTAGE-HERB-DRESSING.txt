Title: COTTAGE-HERB DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads, Vegetables, Cheese
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Milk
12 oz Cottage Cheese; Cream Style,
-1 Carton
1 ts Lemon Juice
1 sm Onion Slice; Thin
3    Radishes; Halved
1 ts Mixed Salad Herbs; Crushed
1    Parsley Sprig
1/4 ts Salt
Put the milk, cottage cheese and lemon juice in a blender container
and blend until smooth.  Add the remaining ingredients to the cottage
cheese mixture and blend until all of the vegetables are chopped.
Chill before serving.

Makes 1 1/2 cups.
