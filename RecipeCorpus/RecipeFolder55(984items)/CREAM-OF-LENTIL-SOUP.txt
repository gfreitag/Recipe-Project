Title: CREAM OF LENTIL SOUP
Categories: Try it, Crockpot, Soup/stew, Legumes
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM------------------RIVAL CROCK-POT COOKING-----------------------
1 lb Dried lentils
1 md Onion, chopped
1    Clove garlic, minced
1 md Carrot, pared and chopped
1 lg Stalk celery, chopped
3 c  Water
1 c  Heavy cream
Salt
Pepper
Soak beans in 6 cups of water overnight; drain. Put beans, onion,
garlic, carrot, celery, and water to Crockpot. Cover and cook on LOW
setting for 8 to 10 hours. Stir in cream; add salt and pepper to
taste.
