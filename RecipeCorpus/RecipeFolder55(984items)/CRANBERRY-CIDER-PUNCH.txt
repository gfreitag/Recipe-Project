Title: CRANBERRY-CIDER PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 32 Servings
2 l  Cranberry ginger ale,
Chilled
2 l  Apple cider, chilled
3    Limes
1 cn (341ml) Frozen Raspberry
Juice Concentrate, optional
Pour ginger ale and cider into punch bowl. Squeeze juice from 2 of the
limes and thinly slice the third. Stir juice into punch. Taste and add
raspberry concentrate, if you wish. If concentrate is still frozen,
you may not need to add ice. Refrigerate until serving time. Float
lime slices on surface. Makes 16 cups, enough for 32 punch-glass
servings.
