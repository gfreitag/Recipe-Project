Title: COUNTRY MEAT LOAF
Categories: Crs, Totest, Post, Chopmeat
Yield: 10 Servings
MMMMM----------------------------LOAF---------------------------------
1/2 c  Onion cooked in
1 tb Butter until transparent
2    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Milk
3/4 c  Oats
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Savory
1/4 ts Pepper
2 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
1/2 c  Ketchup
2 tb Brown sugar
1/2 ts Gravy Master
1/4 ts Dry mustard
Soften onions in butter until clear. Combine with remaining meatloaaf
ingredients. Form into loaf and rerigerate 2 hours. Unmold into larger
baking pan and bake at 350F. a half hour. Remove from oven and drain.
Brush with glaze; return to oven, bake another hour or until no pink
remains, brushing now and then with glaze.
