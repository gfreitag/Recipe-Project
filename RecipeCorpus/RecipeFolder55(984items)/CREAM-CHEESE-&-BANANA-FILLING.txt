Title: CREAM CHEESE & BANANA FILLING
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings
Yield: 1 Servings
3 oz Cream cheese
1 lg Banana
1 ts Lemon juice
Salt
:    Sieve the banana with the lemon juice tp prevent darkening. Also
sieve the cream cheese, and then sieve the banana and the cream
cheese together. Add salt to taste. Serve on date and nut bread or
Boston brown bread. This does not keep long. Makes 1 c. Jeannie Allen
