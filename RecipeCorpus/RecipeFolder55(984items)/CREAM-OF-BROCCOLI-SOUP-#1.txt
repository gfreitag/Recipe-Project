Title: CREAM OF BROCCOLI SOUP #1
Categories: Soups
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Bunch broccoli
1/2 c  Butter
3/4 lb Grated swiss cheese
1 lb Grated cheddar cheese
1 c  Ground cashews
1    Vegetable bouillon cube
1/8 c  Soy sauce
16 oz Sour cream
6 c  Water
Saute broccoli in butter for 10 minutes. Using blender combine all of
the ingredients together. As blender fills, transfer liquid to soup
pot and simmer for 20-30 minutes. Good with muffins or corn bread.
