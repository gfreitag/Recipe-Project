Title: CREAM CHEESE & ALMOND FILLING
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Cream cheese
1/4 c  Pickle relish
1/2 c  Blanched almonds
1/4 c  Celery leaves, ground
Salt and pepper
Paprika
Worcestershire sauce
:    Combine all of the above ingredients until smooth. You may put it
through a food blender to ensure smoothness. This spread refrigerates
well. Jeannie Allen
