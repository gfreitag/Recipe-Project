Title: Peach Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 servings
5 ea Kiwi fruit peeled sliced
1 ea Juice of 1 lemon
4 ea Fresh mint leaves
6 ea Peaches sliced 1/3 segments
3 ea Juice of 3 oranges
1 1/2 tb Sugar
Place peach and kiwi slices in a medium bowl.  Add citrus juices and sugar
to taste.  Stir very gently to blend.  Place mint leaves on top of fruit
and chill several hours.
