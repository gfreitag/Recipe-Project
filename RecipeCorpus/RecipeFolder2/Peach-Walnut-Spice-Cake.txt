Title: Peach Walnut Spice Cake
Categories: Cakes, Fruits
Yield: 12 servings
2 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Baking Soda
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Chopped Walnuts
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown Sugar, packed
4 ea Eggs
1/3 c  Molasses
2 ea Fresh Peaches
