Title: Custard Pie
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Milk
1/2 c  Flour
4 c  Rhubarb
2 ea Eggs,slightly beaten
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Combine ingredients;pour into 9" pastry-lined pan or pie plate. Bake @ 400
degrees 10 minutes.Then bake @ 300 degrees 50 to 60 minutes.Cool.Top with
whipped cream.
