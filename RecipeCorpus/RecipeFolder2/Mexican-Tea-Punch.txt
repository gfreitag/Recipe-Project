Title: Mexican Tea Punch
Categories: Beverages, Mexican
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Tea; strong, cold
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Lime juice
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon; ground
2 c  Tequila
1 c  Pinapple juice
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 1/2 ts Aromatic bitters
Mix all ingredients; refrigerate until chilled.  Stir before serving. Serve
over ice.
