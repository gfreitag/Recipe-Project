Title: Deep Dish Peach Pie
Categories: Pies, Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
3 tb Flour
4 tb Butter
1 c  Heavy Cream, Whipped
2 tb Lemon Juice
1 1/2 lb Fresh Peaches (6 c sliced)
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
