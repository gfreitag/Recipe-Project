Title: BC'S LIME-HONEY MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Lime juice
1/4 c  Dry white wine
Or apple juice
1 tb Finely chopped gingerroot
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Honey
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 ts Grated lime peel
1    Clove garlic, minced
Mix all ingredients in shallow nonmetal dish or resealable plastic
bag. Add 1 pound boneless beef, pork, chicken or turkey, turning to
coat with marinade. Cover and refrigerate at least 8 hours but no
more than 24 hours. Remove meat from marinade; reserve marinade.
Grill meat as desired, brushing occasionally with marinade. Heat
remaining marinade to boiling; boil and stir 1 minute.
