Title: BEEF VEGETABLE & BARLEY SOUP STARTER MIX
Categories: Soup, Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Barley
1/2 ts Dried whole basil
1/2 c  Dried split peas
1/4 ts Dried whole oregano
3    Beef bouillon cubes; crushed
1    Bay leaf
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
Combine the ingredients in a small bowl, and store in an airtight
container. Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #
