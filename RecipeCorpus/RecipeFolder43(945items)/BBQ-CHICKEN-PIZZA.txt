Title: BBQ CHICKEN PIZZA
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz BBQ sauce
1 oz Pizza sauce
3 oz Chicken breast -- roasted
2 oz Gouda cheese -- smoked
2 oz Mozzarella cheese
1 oz Red onion
1/2 oz Green onion
Preheat oven to 500F with pizza stone. Blend BBQ and pizza sauce.
Spread sauce over pizza dough. Cover with shredded chicken. Cover
with cheese and onions. Place on pizza stone in oven and bake until
golden brown.
