Title: BEAN SOUP~ (THE WORLDS BEST)
Categories: Main dish, Soups, Vegetables, Low-cal
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk SMALL PK. SMALL WHITE BEANS
1 ea TOMATO
1 ea CLOVE GARLIC
1 1/2 qt CHICKEN BROTH
1 ea SLICE HAM DICED
1 c  CHOPPED CELERY
1 ea ONION
2 tb LIQUID SMOKE
2 ea MEDIUM POTATOES
2 ea CHICKEN BOUILLON
IN A LARGE POT, COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS-->EXCEPT<--THE BEANS BRING TO
A BOIL, AND THEN TURN TO SIMER IN A SECOND POT WITH COLD WATER, ADD
BEANS. BRING BEANS TO A BOIL AND LET THEM COOL . NOW POUR OFF WATER,
ADD COOL WATER,BRING TO BOIL,LOWER HEAT TO MEDIUM ALLOW TO COOK ON
MED FOR 2 1/2 HOURS OR UNTIL DONE. DO NOT SEASON BEANS WHILE COOKING!
WHEN BEANS ARE DONE, POUR OFF WATER,RINSE BEANS,ADD BEANS TO OTHER
POT SIMMER FOR 1/2 HOUR YOU MAY SUBSTITUTE PORK CHOPS FOR HAM, AND
ADD 1 TSP OF SALT
