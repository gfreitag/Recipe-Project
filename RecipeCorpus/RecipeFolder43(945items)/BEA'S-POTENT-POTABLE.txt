Title: BEA'S POTENT POTABLE
Categories: Drinks, Alcohol
Yield: 8 Servings
25 oz Vodka or run
1 cn Pineapple juice (large can)
1 cn Frozen pink or white
-lemonade(pink looks better)
2 c  Black tea
Mix the above ingredients and freeze until slush/ice. Put one scoop of
mixture into a cup and top up with 7-up or Wildberry Cooler.
