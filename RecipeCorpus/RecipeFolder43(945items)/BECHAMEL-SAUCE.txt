Title: BECHAMEL SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Butter (3/4 stick)
2 tb Corn Starch
2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Parmesan Cheese
1 x  Egg, whipped
In a warm saucepan melt butter. Remove from heat and wisk in Corn
starch until well blended. Return to heat and wisk in 2 cups of Milk
(one at a time), continue until sauce thickens.

Remove from heat and wisk in Parmesan Cheese, then fold in one
whipped egg.

* Keep heat fairly low.

Date: 10-07-93
From: John Soanes
