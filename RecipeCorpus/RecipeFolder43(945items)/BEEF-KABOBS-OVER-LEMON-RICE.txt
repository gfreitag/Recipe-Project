Title: BEEF KABOBS OVER LEMON RICE
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 lb Boneless beef sirloin steak
-- cut in 1-inch cubes
1 sm Zucchini; cut in chunks
1 sm Yellow squash; cut in chunks
1 sm Red pepper; cut in squares
1 sm Onion; cut in chunks
1/4 c  Italian salad dressing
1 c  Hot cooked rice
2 ts Fresh lemon juice
1 tb Snipped fresh parsley
1/4 ts Seasoned salt
Combine beef and vegetables in plastic bag with zippered closing. Add
salad dressing and marinate 4 to 6 hours in refrigerator. Alternate
beef and vegetables on 4 skewers.  Grill or broil, turning and
basting with remaining marinade, 5 to 7 minutes, or to desired
doneness. To prepare Lemon Rice, combine rice and remaining
ingredients. Serve kabobs over Lemon Rice.

Each serving provides: * 412 calories * 28.5 g. protein * 16.3 g. fat
* 36.8 g. carbohydrate * 477 mg. sodium * 74 mg. cholesterol
