Title: BEANS & EGGS
Categories: Beans, Breakfast, Eggs
Yield: 6 Servings
4 oz Sausage links -- cut in 1/2"
Cubes
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 2/3 c  Beans
1/4 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Water
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Basil -- crushed
1/8 ts Pepper
6    Eggs
1    Avocado -- sliced, opt
1    Orange -- peeled,sliced,opt
Brown sausage; remove from pan.  Saute onion in oil. Add beans,
sausage, catsup, water, and seasonings. Simmer, covered, 20 minutes.
Cook eggs as desired. Arrange bean mixtureon platter with eggs,
avocado and orange. Makes 6 servings.

: Idaho Bean Commission
