Title: BEAVER CASTOR'S ALL-PURPOSE RUB
Categories: Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/8 c  Garlic salt
1/8 c  Onion salt
1/8 c  Celery salt
1/8 c  Seasoned salt
1/4 c  Pepper
1/4 c  Paprika
1/4 c  Chile seasoning
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Oregano or Cumin
1/4 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Cloves
Combine ingredients in a bowl or jar with a screw-on lid. Shake until
thoroughly blended.

From: The Great Barbecue Companion, Mops, Sops, Sauces, and
Rubs by Bruce Bjorkman
