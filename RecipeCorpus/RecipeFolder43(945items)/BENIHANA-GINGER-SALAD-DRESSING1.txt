Title: BENIHANA GINGER SALAD DRESSING1
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Onion,. chopped
1/4 c  Peanut oil
2 tb Rice wine vinegar
2 tb Water
1 tb Fresh ginger root, chopped
1 tb Celery, chopped
1 tb Soy sauce
1 1/2 ts Tomato paste
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ds Each salt and pepper
Combine all ingredients in blender of food processor. Process until
almost smooth.
