Title: BEET LEMONADE
Categories: New, Text, Import
2 c  water
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  sugar
1 TB chopped lemon zest
4    whole cloves
1/2 ts salt
3/4 c  fresh lemon juice
4 c  fresh beet juice
8 lg mint leaves
Garnish: Mint sprigs and
thinly sliced lemon
:
:

Over moderate heat add water, sugar, zest, cloves and salt together
in a small saucepan and bring to a simmer. Cook for 23 minutes or
until sugar is completely dissolved. Cool and add lemon and beet
juice along with the mint leaves.

Refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Strain and serve in tall glasses
with ice cubes and garnished with a mint sprig and a lemon slice in
each glass.

Yield:1 1/2 quart
