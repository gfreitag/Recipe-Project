Title: BC'S TEQUILA MARINADE
Categories: Marinades, Alcoholic
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Lime juice
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Tequila or lime juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Ground red pepper (cayenne)
2    Cloves garlic, minced
Mix all ingredients. Makes about 1/2 cup.
