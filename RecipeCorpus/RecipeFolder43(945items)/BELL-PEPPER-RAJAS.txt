Title: BELL PEPPER RAJAS
Categories: Appetizers, Mexican, Cheese, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 ea Green Bell Pepper; *
1/2 ea Red Bell Pepper; *
1/2 ea Yellow Bell Pepper; *
3/4 c  Montery Jack Cheese;Shredded
2 tb Chopped Ripe Olives
1/4 ts Red Pepper; Crushed
*  Peppers should be seeded and cut into 6 strips each.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Cut bell pepper strips crosswise into halves.
Arrange in ungreased broilerproof pie pan, 9 X 1 1/4-inches or round
pan 9 X 2-inches. Sprinkle with cheese, olives and red pepper.  Set
oven control to broil. Broil peppers with tops 3 to 4 inches from
heat until cheese is melted, about 3 minutes.
