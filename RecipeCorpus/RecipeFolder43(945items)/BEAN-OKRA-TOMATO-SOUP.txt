Title: BEAN OKRA TOMATO SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Beans, dried, navy, rinsed
6 c  Water
1    Ham bone
2 lg Ripe tomatoes, peel and chop
1 lg Onion, chopped
3/4 lb Okra
Salt & pepper to taste
In a kettle, dutch oven, etc. bring the beans and water to a boil;
boil for 2 minutes.  Remove from heat, cover and let stand for 1
hour. Add ham bone, tomatoes, and onion.  Bring to a boil, cover and
simmer 1 to 1 1/2 hours or until beans are tender. Add okra and
simmer 15 minutes longer. Remove bone, cut off any meat and add meat
to soup to reheat. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Randy Rigg
