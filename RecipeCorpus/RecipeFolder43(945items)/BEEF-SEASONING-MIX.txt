Title: BEEF SEASONING MIX
Categories: Spices, Cajun
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Salt
1/4 c  Paprika
1 1/2 tb Garlic powder
1 1/2 tb Onion powder
1 1/4 tb Red pepper
1 tb White pepper
1 tb Finely ground black pepper
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Sweet basil
1/2 ts File powder
1/4 ts Ground bay leaves
1/8 ts Thyme
Mix all ingredients together well. Store tightly covered in a glass
jar for use as needed. This is an excellent seasoning for beef
dishes. Use as you would any seasoning mix, and do not add extra salt
to the dish.
Lagniappe: This seasoning mix lends itself to beef, but you may use
it as you like. (Be sure to label the jar so you won't forget what it
is!) About 4 calories per teaspoon.
