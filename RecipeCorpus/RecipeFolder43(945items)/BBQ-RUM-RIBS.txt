Title: BBQ RUM RIBS
Categories: Bbq, Ribs
Yield: 1 Servings
4 lb Spareribs
1/4 c  Ketchup
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 c  Dark rum
2    Cloves garlic -- crushed
1 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
ds Pepper
Wrap ribs in double thickness of foil and bak for 1
1/2    hours at 350 . Unwrap and drain drippings. Combine all
ingredients and pour over ribs. Marinate at room temperature for 1
hour. Bake at 350  for 30 min., basting with sauce, or grill 30 min.,
turning and basting.
