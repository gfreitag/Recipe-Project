Title: BEEF & RICE DINNER
Categories: Chinese, Beef, Rice
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Hamburger Helper *
1 cn (16oz) Mixed Chinese Veg ***
1 cn Condensed Beef Broth **
Small Green Pepper ****
* Rice Oriental Dinner ** 10-1/2 oz *** Drained **** Thinly sliced

Prepare Oriental Rice Dinner as directed on package except omit
water; add enough water to beef broth to measure 3 and 1/3 cups
liquid. Five mins before end of cooking, stir in vegetables and green
peppers.
