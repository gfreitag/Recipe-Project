Title: BBQ SAUCE - TEXAS STYLE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ea Large onion, diced
2 ea Cloves garlic, pressed or
Diced fine
1 ea Juice of one lemon
1 ea Grated peel of one lemon
1/2 c  Catsup
1/2 ts Bottled hot pepper sauce
1 tb Salt
1/2 ts Chili powder
1 ds Sage
Combine ingredients; stir and simmer for 15 minutes. Serve with
broiled chicken or ribs.

Source:  BH & G Barbecue Book, 1965
