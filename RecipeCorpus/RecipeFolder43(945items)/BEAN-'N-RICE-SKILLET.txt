Title: BEAN 'N RICE SKILLET
Categories: Beans, Beef, Fast/health, One-dish me, Pace picant
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Pace Thick 'n Chunky Sauce
2 cn Tomato sauce (8 oz.)
1 1/2 c  Uncooked instant white rice
1 c  Whole kernel corn, frozen
1 c  Red bell pepper, chopped
1 cn Black beans (15 oz.) --
Rinsed and drained
1 lb Ground beef (optional) --
Browned and drained
Combine sauce ingredients in large skillet; mix well. Bring to a
boil. Stir in all remaining ingredients. Reduce heat to low; cover
and simmer about 10 minutes or until rice is cooked and vegetables
are tender.
