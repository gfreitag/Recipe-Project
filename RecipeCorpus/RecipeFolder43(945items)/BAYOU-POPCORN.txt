Title: BAYOU POPCORN
Categories: Popcorn
Yield: 12 Cups
-Sue Woodward
3 tb Butter; or margarine
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Salt
12 c  Popped corn
In a heavy saucepan, melt butter over med. heat.  Stir in remaining
ingredients except popcorn.  Cook for 1 min. Pour over popcorn,
tossing to coat evenly. Serve at once.

Source:  Theatre Popcorn Popper leaflet
