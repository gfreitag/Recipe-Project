Title: BEEF LOAF
Categories: Main dish, Red wine
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Milk
1    Egg,beaten
1/4 c  Celery,finely chopped
1 tb Worcesershire sauce
1/4 ts Ground pepper
3    Slices white bread
1/2 c  Sweet red wine
1/4 c  Onion,finely chopped
1/4 c  Catsup
2 ts Salt
Heat oven to 350'. Mix all ingredients. Spread in ungreased loaf pan,
9x5x3 inches. Bake 1 1/2 hours.
