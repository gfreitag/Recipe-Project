Title: BENIHANA GINGER SALAD DRESSING2
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Soybean oil
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
2 tb White vinegar
1 tb Tomato paste
1 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients in blender of food processor. Process until
almost smooth.
