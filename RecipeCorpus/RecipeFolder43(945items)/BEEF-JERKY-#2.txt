Title: BEEF JERKY #2
Categories: Beef, Snacks, Meats
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Round steak, chuck steak or
-roast
1 ts Onion salt
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/2 ts Lemon pepper
1/2 ts Sausage seasoning
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/2 ts Basil
Approx. Cook Time: 8 hrs
Fat grams    per serving:
Combine the spices in a dish. Cut the meat into strips less than 1/4
of an inch thick. Remove ALL fat. Sprinkle one side with the combined
seasonings and beat with a meat hammer. Turn and repeat the seasoning
and beating. Place the strips on a cookie sheet or other flat pan.
Place in a 120 F oven for 4 hrs. Turn and put back for another 4 hrs.
Keep the oven door propped open for the entire time to allow the
moisture to escape. With a gas oven, the pilot light may be enough to
provide the heat. Store in a plastic bag. Also may be stored in a
pillowcase. Do not store in a paper bag.
