Title: BELGIAN LOAF
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Milk
1/4 lb Shortening
1    Egg
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Soda
in saucepan put first 4 ingredients and bring to a boil, cool. Then
stir in 1 egg, then the dry ingredients, mix in 2nd. cup of flour.
Bake at 350 for
45    min.
