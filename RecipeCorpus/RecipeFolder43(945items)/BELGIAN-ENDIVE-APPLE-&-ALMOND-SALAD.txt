Title: BELGIAN ENDIVE APPLE & ALMOND SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
2 x  Tart green apples, peeled,
Cut into 1/2 inch cubes
3 x  Belgian endives, thinly
Sliced crosswise
2 tb Almonds, peeled and shelled
Dressing:  4 teaspoons oil, 1 T lemon juice, 1 t minced garlic, 1/4 t
salt. (Sorry)  Mix apple, endives and almonds together in a bowl. Mix
the dressing and pour on salad. Toss thoroughly and serve. Makes 6
servings.
