Title: BC'S ROASTED GARLIC MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1 md Bulb garlic
1/3 c  Olive or vegetable oil
2/3 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Spicy honey mustard
3 tb Honey
3/4 ts Dried thyme leaves, crushed
Heat oven to 375 degrees F. Cut one-third off top of unpeeled garlic
bulb, exposing cloves. Place garlic in small baking dish; drizzle
with oil. Cover tightly and bake 45 minutes; cool. Squeeze garlic
pulp from papery skin. Place garlic and remaining ingredients in
blender. Cover and blend on high speed until smooth. Makes about 1
1/2    cups.
