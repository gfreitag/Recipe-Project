Title: BEANS WITH TOMATOES (JUDIAS VERDES CON TOMATO
Categories: Beans, Spain
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Whole green beans
Boiling water
2 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Oil
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Or less to taste
1/3 c  Tomato sauce
1/8 ts Black pepper
2 ts Paprika
Pimiento strips
Cook Beans in water to cover with 2 teaspoons salt until not quite
tender. Drain. Saute in oil with garlic powder for 3 minutes. Add
remaining salt and all other ingredients except pimiento. Reheat.
Garnish with pimiento strips. Walt MM

Serves 8-10
