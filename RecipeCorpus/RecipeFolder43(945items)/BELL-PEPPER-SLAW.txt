Title: BELL PEPPER SLAW
Categories: Salads, Side dish, Vegetables, Low-fat
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  Thinly sliced green cabbage
2 c  Red bell pepper, julienned
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Celery seeds
1/4 ts Pepper
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and toss well. Let stand 15
minutes before serving. Makes 6 1 cup servings.
