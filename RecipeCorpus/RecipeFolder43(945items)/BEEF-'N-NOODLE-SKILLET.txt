Title: BEEF 'N NOODLE SKILLET
Categories: Beef, Casseroles, Fast/health, Mexican, Pasta
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Extra lean ground beef
2 c  Medium egg noodles --
Uncooked
1 c  Whole kernel corn, frozen
1 c  Greeb onions -- sliced
***Sauce***
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Salsa (thick and chunky)
2 cn (8 oz.) no-salt added
Tomatoe sauce
Brown ground beef in large skillet; drain.  Stir in all remaining
ingredients.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer
10 to 12 minutes or until noodles are of desired doneness, stirring
occasionally.

: Fast and Healthy magazine, Sept/Oct 1994
