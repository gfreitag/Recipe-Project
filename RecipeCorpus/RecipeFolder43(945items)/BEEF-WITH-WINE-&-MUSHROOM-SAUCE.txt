Title: BEEF WITH WINE & MUSHROOM SAUCE
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 10 Servings
3 1/2 lb Beef chuck cut in pieces
1 c  Red wine
1 cn Golden mushroom soup
Salt & pepper to taste
Salt and pepper meat. Pour soup and wine over beef. Cover and bake 3
hours at 325 degrees. Serve with noodles or rice.

Mrs. Harold T. Cook
