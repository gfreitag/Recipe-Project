Title: BEETS IN SOUR CREAM
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
16 oz Can beets, drained and diced
1 tb Cider vinegar
1/4 ts Each garlic salt and pepper
1/4 c  Sour cream
1 ts Sugar
Combine all ingredients in 1 qt glass casserole.  Stir gently to mix.
Microwave, covered, 3-5 minutes on High, or until heated through. Stir
every 2 minutes. Let stand, covered, 2-3 minutes before serving.
