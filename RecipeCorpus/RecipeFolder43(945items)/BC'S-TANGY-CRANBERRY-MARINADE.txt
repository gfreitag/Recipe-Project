Title: BC'S TANGY CRANBERRY MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Dried cranberries
1    1-inch piece peeled
Gingerroot
3    Cloves garlic
1 sm Serrano chili -or-
1 T chopped green chilies
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Lime juice
3 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt
Place all ingredients in blender or food processor. Cover and blend
on high speed 1 minute. Makes about 1 1/4 cups.
