Title: APPLE OR GRAPE JELLY
Categories: Preserves
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Bottled juice
3 1/2 c  Sugar
1 pk Liquid Certo (1 pouch)
Combine juice and sugar into a large pan.  Place over moderate heat
and bring to a boil.  Stir in Certo and bring to a full rolling boil
and continue at a full rolling boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat,
skim and bottle at once.

This is from way back in the late 60's.
