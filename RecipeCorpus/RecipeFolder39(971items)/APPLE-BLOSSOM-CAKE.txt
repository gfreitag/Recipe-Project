Title: APPLE BLOSSOM CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Ethnic, Cajun
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  All-purpose flour
1 tb Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Vegetable cooking oil
1 ts Vanilla extract
3    Eggs
3 c  Apples -- diced and unpared
1 c  Pecans -- chopped
Sift dry ingredients into large bowl. Pour oil, vanilla and eggs into
mixture. Beat with spoon until mixed. Add apples and pecans. Place in
greased and floured tube pan. Bake at 325 for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
Cool cake on wire rack.
