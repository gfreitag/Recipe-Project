Title: ANN'S MEAT LOAF
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Beef
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb GROUND BEEF
2 x  EGGS
1 1/2 c  BREAD CRUMBS
3/4 c  TOMATO SAUCE
1 ts ACCENT
1/2 c  WATER
1 pk SOUP MIX
MIX THOROUGHLY AND SHAPE INTO LOAF OR PLACE IN A 9 X 5 BAKING PAN.
BAKE AT 350 DEG F FOR 1 HOUR OR UNTIL MEAT IS COOKED THROUGH.
