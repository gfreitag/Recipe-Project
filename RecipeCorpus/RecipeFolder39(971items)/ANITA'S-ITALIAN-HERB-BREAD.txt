Title: ANITA'S ITALIAN HERB BREAD
Categories: Pam vkbb14a, Breads, Bmm, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 pound loaf
1 1/8 c  Buttermilk
3 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Applesauce (oil)
1 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1/4 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Dried oregano
1 1/2 ts Yeast
Crust light. Bake.
