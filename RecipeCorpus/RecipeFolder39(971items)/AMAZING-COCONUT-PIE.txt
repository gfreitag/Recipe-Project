Title: AMAZING COCONUT PIE
Categories: Dessert, Easy, Mershon, Imposbl pie, Cheese/egg
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Bisquick
4    Eggs
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Coconut
Combine everything but coconut in blender on low for 3 minutes. Pour
into pie plate, let sit for 5 min.  Top with coconut. Bake at 350 for
40 minutes.
