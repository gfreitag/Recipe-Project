Title: APPLE NUT QUICK BREAD
Categories: Breads, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
4 c  Flour
2 ts Baking Soda
1 ts Salt
2 ts Cinnamon
2 c  Sugar
4 x  Eggs, Beaten
1 c  Vegetable Oil
1/4 c  Sour Cream
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Chopped Apples
1 c  Chopped Nuts
Preheat oven to 350 deg F.  Sift together flour, baking soda,
cinnamon and salt.  Set aside.  Combine sugar, eggs, oil, sour cream
and vanilla; beat well. Blend into flour mixture.  Fold in apples and
nuts.  Pour into two greased and floured loaf pans.  Bake 1 hour or
until loaves test done.
