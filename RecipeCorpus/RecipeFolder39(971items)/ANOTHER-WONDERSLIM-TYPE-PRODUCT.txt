Title: ANOTHER WONDERSLIM-TYPE PRODUCT
Categories: Digest, Oct., Text, Reference
Yield: 1 Servings
None
For those who like Wonderslim, there's another fruit puree product on
the market billing itself as a butter substitute for baking:

"Just Like Shortenin'" is made from prunes, apples, and water (no
lecithin).  Product of The Plumlife Company, 15 Orchard Park,
Madison, CT 06443.

Label says "Just Like Shortenin' is good enough to eat with a spoon.
Try that with your current shortening!"
