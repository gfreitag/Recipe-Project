Title: APPLE NUT CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Ethnic, Cajun
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Cooking oil
2 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla extract
3 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
3 c  Apples -- diced and peeled
1 c  Pecans -- chopped
Mix oil, sugar, eggs and vanilla until creamy. Beat in flour, salt and
baking soda. Fold in apples and pecans. Place batter in 13 X 9 inch
pan. Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
