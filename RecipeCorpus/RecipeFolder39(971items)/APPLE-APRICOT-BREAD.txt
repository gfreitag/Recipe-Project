Title: APPLE-APRICOT BREAD
Categories: Breads, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  WATER, BOILING
1 c  APRICOT, DICED
2 c  FLOUR, ALL-PURPOSE
2 1/2 ts BAKING POWDER
1/4 ts BAKING SODA
1/2 ts SALT
1/2 ts ALLSPICE
MILK
1/2 c  SHORTENING
3/4 c  SUGAR
EGG, BEATEN
1 c  APPLE, CHOPPED
3/4 c  WALNUTS, CHOPPED
Pour boiling water over dried apricots and let stand. Sift flour,
baking powder, soda, salt and allspice. Add milk to apricot juice to
make 2/3 c. Beat the shortening adding sugar gradually. Beat in the
egg. Add dry ingredients alternately with the liquid.  Fold in the
fruit and walnuts. Spoon into a greased and floured pan, 9x5. Bake in
350F oven for 1 hour. Cool on rack.
