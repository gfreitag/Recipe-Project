Title: AN AUTHENTIC PESTO
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
12    Walnuts, shelled
2 tb Pine nuts
1 ts Coarse salt
4    (or 5) black peppercorns
3    Garlic clove(s)
4 tb Butter
3 c  Basil leaves
4 oz Grated Parmesan cheese
4 oz Grated Romano cheese
-or added Parmesan cheese
1 1/2 c  Olive oil
Place all ingredients except 1 cup black peppercorns in food
processor and grind very fine. Add remaining olive oil and blend a
few seconds until very smooth.
