Title: ANISEED COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 40 Servings
4 ea Eggs; separated
1 3/4 c  Powered sugar; sifted
1 x  Pinch salt
2 c  Flour, all purpose
1/4 ts Baking powder
2 ts Aniseed; ground
Grease and flour baking sheets.
Beat egg yolks, powdered sugar and salt in a large bowl until pale and
creamy. Beat egg whites until very stiff; fold into egg yolk mixture.
Siftflour, baking powder and aniseed onto egg mixture; fold in
quickly but thoroughly. Fill a pastry bag fitted with a plain nozzle
with cookie mixture. Pipe in small rounds onto prepared baking
sheets. Let stand overnight to dry out.
Preheat oven to 325 deg. Bake cookies 20 minutes. Cool on rack.
