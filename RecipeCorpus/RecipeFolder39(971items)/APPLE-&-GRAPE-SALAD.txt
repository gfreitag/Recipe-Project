Title: APPLE & GRAPE SALAD
Categories: Fruits, Salads, Apples
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Apples; cubed
1 c  Grapes; halved
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1/4 c  Walnuts; chopped
1 ts Lemon juice
1/3 c  Sour cream
1/3 c  Plain yogurt
In large bowl, combine apples, grapes, celery, walnuts and lemon
juice. Mix yogurt and sour cream. Mix lightly into fruit mix.
Refrigerate. If desired, serve on lettuce lined plates.
