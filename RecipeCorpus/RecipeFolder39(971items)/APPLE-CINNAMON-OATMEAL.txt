Title: APPLE CINNAMON OATMEAL
Categories: Cereal
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Apple juice
1    Tart apple, diced
2/3 c  Rolled oats
1    Dash cinnamon
1 c  Skim milk
Place water, juice and apples ina medium saucepan and bring to a
boil. Sitr in the rolled oats and cinnamon. Return to boil, reduce
heat and simmer until the porridge is thick. Serve hot with milk.
