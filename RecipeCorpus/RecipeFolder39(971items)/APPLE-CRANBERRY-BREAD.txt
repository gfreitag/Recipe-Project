Title: APPLE-CRANBERRY BREAD
Categories: Breads, Abm, Fat-free, Fruits
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 1/2 ts Yeast
1/2 ts Salt
2 1/2 c  Bread flour
1/2 c  Multi-grain oatmeal
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
3 tb Molasses
2 tb Apple butter
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Diced apple
1/2 c  Cranberry
