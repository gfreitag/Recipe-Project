Title: APPLE SAUCE (COOK'S CHOICE)
Categories: New, Text, Import
3 TB butter
Yield: 4 Servings
6    Macintosh apples, cored, --
peeled and chopped
:
1/4 c  sugar
1 ts cinnamon
1/2 ts nutmeg
4 TB water

Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and cook over medium heat until
apples become very tender, about 30 minutes. Applesauce may be left
lumpy or pureed with a handblender to become smoother.

Yield: 4 servings
