Title: APPLE-CHEDDAR SPREAD
Categories: Breads, Spreads
Yield: 10 Servings
1 pk (8oz) cream cheese,
Softened
5 ts Milk or enough for spreading
Consistency
1 c  (4oz) shredded mild cheddar
Cheese
1/4 c  Finely chopped apple
1/4 c  Finely chopped walnuts
1/2 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Combine all ingredients until well blended. Makes 10 to 14 servings.

The Best Bagels are made at home by Dona Z. Meilach
