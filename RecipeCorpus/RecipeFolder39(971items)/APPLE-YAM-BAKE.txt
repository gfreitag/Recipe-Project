Title: APPLE YAM BAKE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 Recipe
2    Apples, cored and peeled
1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/3 c  Chopped pecans
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground ginger
2 md Sweet potatoes or
-Southern yams * or
2 cn 16 oz. yams/sweet potatoes
1/4 c  Butter
* cooked and cut into 1" slices. (Bake or peel and steam. Cool first
before slicing.

Preheat oven to 350 F

Toss apples, brown sugar, nuts and spices.  Alternate layers of
apples and yams in 9" square baking dish/pan. (Use what you have.)
Dot with butter.

Bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
