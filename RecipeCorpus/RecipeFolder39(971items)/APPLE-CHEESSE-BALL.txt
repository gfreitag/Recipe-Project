Title: APPLE CHEESSE BALL
Categories: Appetizers, Dips
Yield: 1 Cups
1    8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
Softened
4 oz 1 cup shredded cheddar
Cheese
1/4 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Finely chopped dried apples
1/3 c  Finely chopped nuts
1    Bay leaf
1    Cinnamon stick, halved
Directions;

In larage bowl, beat cream cheese, cheddar cheese and cinnamon until
well blended.  Stir in dried apples. Shape mixture into ball; roll in
nuts. Inssert bay leaf and cinnamon stick on top to resemble stem and
leaf of apple.  Refrigerate till firm. Can be stored in refrigerator
up to 2 weeks.
