Title: AMBROSIA FRUIT
Categories: Breakfast, Fruits, Taste of ho
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn Pineapple chunks in light
-syrup
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 ts Orange peel; grated
2 md Oranges
2 md Apples, unpeeled; diced
1 tb Coconut
Recipe by: Sue Klapper Drain pineapple, reserving 1/4 cup juice in a
saucepan; set pineapple aside. Add brown sugar and orange peel to the
juice; heat until sugar dissolves. Peel and section oranges into a
large bowl, reserving any juice; add the apples and pineapple. Add
pineapple juice mixture and stir gently. Chill. Just before serving,
sprinkle with coconut.  Source: Taste of Home Magazine Oct./Nov. 1994
