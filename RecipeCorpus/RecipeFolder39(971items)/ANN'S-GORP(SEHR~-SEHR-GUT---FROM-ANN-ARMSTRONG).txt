Title: ANN'S GORP(SEHR~ SEHR GUT - FROM ANN ARMSTRONG)
Categories: Marie, Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 12 Servings
Dry roasted peanuts
Dates
Raisins
Gold fish
M & M's
Sesame nut mix
Sunflower seeds
Use your own judgement on how much of each ingredient to use.  I like
dates more than the peanuts, so I use more of those.  Combine all
together and enjoy.
