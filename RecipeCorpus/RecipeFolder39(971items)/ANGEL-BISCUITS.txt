Title: ANGEL BISCUITS
Categories: Breakfast, Breads
Yield: 24 Servings
2 tb Sugar
1 cn Yeast
1/2 c  Water, luke warm
5 c  Flour, sifted
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Shortening, melted
Approx. Cook Time: 15min
Fat grams    per serving:
Put sugar, yeast and water in bowl and let sit. Put melted
shortening and milk together making sure that it is not to hot (115
degrees). Sift together flour, baking powder and salt. Add to yeast
mixture alternatly milk mixture and dry ingredients. Roll out on a
floured cutting board and cut with a biscuit cutter. Place on a
baking sheet and bake for 12 to 15 minutes at 400 degrees. Source:
Shelton
