Title: Halloween Make Up
Categories: Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Solid shortening
2 tb Cornstarch
Liquid food coloring
for red, use 2 drops red for brown, use 2 drops green and 1 drop red
for purple, use 1 drop blue and 2 drops red.

Store in small jars.
