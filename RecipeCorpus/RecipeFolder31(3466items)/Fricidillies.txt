Title: Fricidillies
Categories: Meats, Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 lb Ground chuck
1/2 lb Lean pork
5 sl Fresh bread
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2    Eggs
2 c  Milk
3 tb Margarine
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
Grind beef and pork together twice.  Remove crusts from bread and
tear in small pieces.  Mix all ingredients except margarine together
thoroughly and drop from a teaspoon into hot margarine.  Brown meat
bits and place in a casserole.  Heat soup with 1 can of water and
pour over meatballs and bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees. Randy Rigg
