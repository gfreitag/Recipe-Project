Title: Macadamia Nut Cheese Ball
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
2    8 oz pkgs cream cheese, soft
2    Jars old English cheese
1 c  Grated cheddar
Juice & scrapping of one
Onion
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sweet pickles, chopped
1 c  Chopped macadamia nuts
Blend cheeses, add remaining ingredients less 1/2 the nuts.  Shape
into a ball, add remaining nuts and parsley around the ball.
Refrigerate several hours before serving. Randy Rigg
