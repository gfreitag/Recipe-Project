Title: Dorothy Flatman's Chicken Salad Stuffed Tomatoes
Categories: Salads, Poultry, Vegetables, Tomatoes
Yield: 3 servings
3 ea Large ripe tomatoes
3 c  Chicken, cooked, cubed
1 1/2 c  Celery, sliced
3 ea Eggs, hard-boiled
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
Slice top off ripe tomatoes and scoop out centers.  Chop top and
centers and add chicken, celery and mayonnaise. Mix well.  Fill
tomato shells with chicken mixture, top with quartered hard-boiled
eggs. Created by Dorothy Flatman
