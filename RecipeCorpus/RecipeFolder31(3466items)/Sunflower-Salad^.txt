Title: Sunflower Salad^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2    Or 3 oranges
Lettuce leaves
1/2    Banana
Whipped cream
Nut meats, chopped
Mayonnaise
Divide oranges into sections, removing as much skin and tissue as
possible. Place on lettuce leaf in the shape of a sunflower. Use a
thick slice of banana for center, spoon on whipped cream and nuts.
serve with sweetened mayonnaise.
