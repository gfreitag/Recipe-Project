Title: Molasses Wheat Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------------1 1/2 LB LOAF----------------------------
2 c  +1 Tb bread flour
1 c  Whole wheat flour
3 ts Gluten
1 1/8 c  Water
5 tb Buttermilk powder
2 1/2 ts Red Star Active Yeast
3 tb Molasses
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
1/4 c  Grapenuts
Have ingredients at room temperature.  Add in order described by
machine manufacturer. Use light setting.

For the Hitachi 101 I warm liquid in microwave for 1 minute before I
add to bread pan.

This made tasty tall loaf.

Other makes of machines may have to adjust ingredients to their
specifications.
