Title: 498837 Tirami Su'(Pick  Me  Up)
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
3    Doz  lady fingers
1/4 c  Hershey's cocoa
1    (6 oz.) bag semi-sweet
-Hershey's mini chips
1 pt Heavy cream
1    Pot expresso or strong
-coffee
Dip lady fingers into expresso quickly so that they do not break
apart. Layer in a baking dish.  Whip heavy cream and place some on
first layer of lady fingers.  Sprinkle cocoa and chocolate chips on
cream. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with
cream and chocolate. Keep refrigerated.
