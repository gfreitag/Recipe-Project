Title: Dog Biscuits #1
Categories: Pets
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Salt
2 ea Eggs
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Unsulfered molasses
1/2 c  Soy flour
1/4 c  Corn meal
2 tb Butter or veg. oil
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix dry ingredients.  Add melted
butter/oil, molasses, and egg mixed with milk.  Save 1tb of egg
mixture. Knead together (add milk if too dry) and let sit for 1/2
hour. Roll to 1/2 inch and cut into desired shapes with cookie
cutter. Put on cookie sheet, paint on remaining egg mixture and bake
30 minutes. Let cool and store in sealed container.
