Title: Chicken Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Kix cereal (about 1 cup) --
-crushed
1/2 c  Celery -- thinly sliced
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
1/2 c  Swiss cheese -- shredded
1 c  Mayonnaise
3/4 c  Milk
2 tb Green onion -- chopped
1 ts Salt
1 ts Mustard
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Dried basil
2 cn Tuna (6.5 oz) -- drained
10 oz Frozen green beans -- thawed
-and drained
7 oz Elbow macaroni -- cooked &
-drained
Topping:
1 c  Kix cereal -- slightly
-crushed
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
1/4 c  Swiss cheese -- shredded
Heat oven to 375.  Mix all ingredients except topping in ungreased 3
quart casserole.  Cover and bake 30 minutes.  Sprinkle with topping.
Bake uncovered until hot and cheese is melted, about 10 minutes
longer.
