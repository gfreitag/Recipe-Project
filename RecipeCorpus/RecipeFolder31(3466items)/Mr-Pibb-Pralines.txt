Title: Mr Pibb Pralines
Categories: Candies, Nuts/grains, Oldies
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Sugar; Granulated
1 c  Brown Sugar Dark, Packed
1 c  Mr. Pibb
1 c  Marshmallows Miniature
Pecan Halves *

*  Use 2 to 3 cups of pecan halves.
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
+++

Cook sugars and Mr. Pibb in a saucepan over low heat, stirring
constantly, then gradually bring to the soft ball stage (240 degrees
F on candy thermometer).  Remove mixture from heat; stir in
marshmallows and pecans. Mix vigorously until marshmallows dissolve.
Quickly drop by tablespoon on wax paper to cool.

Makes 2 dozen pralines.

NOTE:

If Mr Pibb is not available in your area, use Dr. Pepper.
