Title: Squid with celery and olives
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Seafood, Vegetables
Yield: 10 servings
1 1/2 kg Boiled squid, cut in rings
400 g  Celery, chopped
400 g  Green and black olives,
-sliced
150 ml Olive oil
50 ml Lemon juice
50 g  Chopped parsley
Salt and pepper
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Mix all together and let marinate. Source: Given to share with the
express permission of Chef de Cuisine
