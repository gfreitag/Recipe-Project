Title: Sweet and Sour Ribs
Categories: Meats, Chinese
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Flour
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 lb Beef short ribs, cut into
2" lengths
1 c  Onion, sliced
1/3 c  Catsup
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Vinegar
1/2 c  Water
1.  Combine flour, sugar, salt and pepper on waxed paper.  Roll ribs
in the mixture, coating them on all sides and shaking off excess.

2.  Arrange ribs in a baking dish or pan in a single layer.  Layer
onions over ribs.  Combine catsup, soysauce, Worcestershire sauce,
vinegar and water in a small bowl; pour over ribs.

3.  Braise, covered in a 325 oven for 1-1/2 hours, basting
occasionally with pan sauce.  Uncover; continue cooking for 1-1/2
hours longer or until ribs are glazed and very tender.
