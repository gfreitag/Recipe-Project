Title: Cream of Mushroom Rice
Categories: Side dish, Rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Milk
2 c  Minute Rice; uncooked
Bring soup and milk to boil in medium saucepan.  Stir in rice; cover.
Remove from heat.  Let stand 5 minutes. Serve immediately. Source:
