Title: Whole Wheat Bagels
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 batch
1 1/8 c  White Bread Flour
1 1/8 c  Whole Wheat Flour
1 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
2 tb Molasses
2/3 c  Water
Options: You can substitute all White Bread Flour.

If you do, be careful in the boiling stage.

You can add - 1/4 Cup well packed raisins & 1 Teaspoon Cinnamon.

You can sprinkle on top - 3/4 Tablespoon Poppy Seeds or 3/4 Tablespoon
Sesame Seeds or 1/4 Cup Onions or your own preferences

For Egg Bagels: Substitute for Water = 1 Egg plus water to make 2/3
Cup + 1 Tablespoon Water and before baking, brush with egg white. You
can also sprinkle with seed.
