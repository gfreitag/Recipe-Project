Title: Marla Trump's Lusty Oysters w/ Ginger and Lime
Categories: Seafood, Herbs/spice, Appetizers, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 cl Garlic; unpeeled
6 tb Butter; room temp
1/4 bn Cilantro; washed & chopped
2 ts Ginger root; minced
2 tb Lime juice
20    Oysters; shucked & drained
1 tb Olive oil
Place the garlic in a small pan and roast at 350Ã¸F for 20 minutes.
Peel the cloves.

Place the garlic, butter, cilantro, ginger and lime juice in a
blender or food processor and puree until smooth.

Place the oysters under the broiler or on the grill over a medium-hot
fire and cook until firm to the touch, about 2 to 3 minutes per side.
While the oysters are still warm, top each with a tsp of the flavored
butter. Serve immediately.
