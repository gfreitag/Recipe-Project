Title: Tandoori Salmon
Categories: Fish, Indian, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Olive oil
1    Garlic clove(s)
-finely chopped
6    8oz salmon steaks
-about 1 1/4" thick
1 tb Garam masala
In a bowl, combine the olive oil and garlic. Rub the mixture all over
the salmon. Sprinkle with garam masala and season lightly with salt.
Cover and refrigerate for up to 2 hours.

Light a grill or preheat the broiler. Grill or broil salmon steaks,
turning once, for about 4 minutes per side, or until nicely charred
and just cooked through.

Transfer each salmon steak to a warmed plate and remove the central
bone with a fork. Serve with Peach Chutney and Minted Yogurt Sauce
(see separate recipes).
