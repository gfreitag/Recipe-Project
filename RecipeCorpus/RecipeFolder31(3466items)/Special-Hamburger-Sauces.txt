Title: Special Hamburger Sauces
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Miracle whip
1/3 c  Creamy French dressing
1/4 c  Sweet pickle relish
1 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Minced dry onions
Stir all together. Makes 2 cups. Store in refg.
