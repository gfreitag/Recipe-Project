Title: Chocolate Pizza (Mw)
Categories: Desserts, Pizza
Yield: 10 servings
1    12 oz pkg semi-sweet real
-chocolate chips
1 lb White almond bark --
Divided
2 c  Mini marshmallows
1 c  Rice krispsies
1 c  Peanuts
1    6 oz jar red maraschino
-cherries, drained/halved
1/4 c  Green maraschino cherries,
Drained/quartered
1/3 c  Angel flake coconut
1 ts Oil
Microwave chips and 14 oz of the almond bark in 2 quart micro-safe
bowl on HIGH 2 min; stir. Continue microwaving 1-2 mins or until
smooth when stirred, stirring every 30 secs. Stir in marshmallows,
cereal and peanuts. Pour onto greased 12" pizza pan. Top with
cherries; sprinkle with coconut. Mirco remaining 2 oz almond bark and
oil in 1 cup glass measuring cup 1 min; stir. Continue micro 30 sec
to 1 min or until smooth when stirred, stirring every 15 sec. Drizzle
over coconut. Refrigerate until firm. Store at room temperature.
Makes 10 to 12 servings.
