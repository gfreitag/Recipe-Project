Title: Louisiana Hot Pepper Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cajun/creol
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  White distilled vinegar
2 ts Salt
2 lb Cayenne or jalapeno peppers
-seed and chop
Simmer vinegar, salt and peppers for at least 5 minutes. Process in
processor. Store in a glass bottle. Put in a dark cabinet and let age
for at least 3 months. When ready to use, strain.
