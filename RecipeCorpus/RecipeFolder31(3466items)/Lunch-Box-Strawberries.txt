Title: Lunch Box Strawberries
Categories: Can/preserv, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 ga Strawberries
1 c  Granulated sugar
2 tb Powdered pectin
Wash berries, remove hulls and slice strawberries. Mix sugar and
pectin together and stir gently into berries; let stand 10 minutes
for sugar to dissolve and juices for form. Place in freezer
containers, leaving 1/2-inch head space; freeze.

Makes about 6 pints.

Source: Oregonian FoodDay June 6, 1995
