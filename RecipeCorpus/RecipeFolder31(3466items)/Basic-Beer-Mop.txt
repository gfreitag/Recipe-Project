Title: Basic Beer Mop
Categories: Bbq/grill, Herbs/spice
Yield: 3 servings
12 oz Beer
1/2 c  Cider Vinegar
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Canola Oil
1/2 md Onion -- chopped
2    Cloves Garlic Clove --
Minced
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1 tb Dry Rub
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan. Heat the mop and use it warm.
