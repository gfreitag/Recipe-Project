Title: Zuppa Di Broccoli - Broccoli Soup
Categories: Italian, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Olive Oil
2 md Onions -- minced
1    Clove Garlic -- minced
1/2 c  Prosciutto -- diced
Black Pepper -- to taste
6 c  Water
1 bn Broccoli
1 tb Butter
4 c  Chicken Broth
1/2 c  Spaghettini In 1 1/2-In
Pieces
1/4 c  Romano Cheese -- grated
In a large pot heat oil and saute onions, garlic, and prosciutto until
soft. Add pepper and 4 cups of the water. Simmer, covered, for 20
mins. Use just the flowerettes of the broccoli; wash them and cook in
2 cups water until crisp. Add the drained broccoli to onions, garlic,
and prosciutto mixture. Add the chicken broth and spaghettini. Cook
for 6-8 mins. Serve in soup bowls with Romano cheese.
