Title: Ancho Chili Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chilies
Yield: 1 servings
This gently spicy sauce can
Be used to flavor main
Dishes or as a base
For other preparations or
Even as a dipping sauce for
Raw vegetables.
Ingredients
1    Dried Ancho chili,
-re-hydrated
1    Yellow onion
1/2 c  Peeled garlic clove
2    Red bell peppers, roasted
-and cleaned
2 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Cumin
1/8 ts Salt
1/4 c  Honey
8 oz Can tomato sauce
1/4 c  Ground oregano
Method: In a deep skillet, heat olive oil and caramelize onion and
garlic cloves. Add all other ingredients, let simmer for 1/2 hour.
Cool. Puree in blender.
