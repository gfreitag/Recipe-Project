Title: Chocolate Gooey Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 48 bars
1/2 c  Margarine
1    German chocolate cake mix
1 1/2 c  Miniature marshmallows
6 oz Chocolate chips
2 c  Cheerios
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
Heat oven to 350.  Heat margarine in jelly roll pan (15 x 10 x 1), in
oven until melted.  Sprinkle and pat dry cake mix over margarine.
Sprinkle with marshmallows, chocolate chips and cereal.  Drizzle
condensed milk evenly over top.  Bake 20 to 25 minutes. While bars
are still warm, run knife around edges of pan to loosen. Source:
