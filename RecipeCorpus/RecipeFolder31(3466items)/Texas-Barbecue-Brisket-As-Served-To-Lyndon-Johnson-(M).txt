Title: Texas Barbecue Brisket As Served To Lyndon Johnson (M)
Categories: Jewish, Meats, Bbq/grill, Tex/mexican
Yield: 10 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
5 lb Brisket of beef
1 ts Salt and freshly ground
-pepper to taste
1 lg Onion -- chopped
2    Stalks celery
1 c  Chili sauce
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
10 oz Bottle beer
Remove most of the fat and wash and dry. Sprinkle with salt and
pepper. Place the meat, fat side up, in a casserole and cover with
the onion, celery, chili sauce, water and parsley. Bake uncovered in
a preheated 325 degree oven for 1 hour. Pour the beer over thebeef
and bake covered, 3 more hours or until tender. Cool to room temp.
Refrigerate, skim off the fat, slice and reheat in the oven.
