Title: Spaghettini In Brodo
Categories: Italian, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Spaghettini In 1 1/2-In
Pieces
1 qt Water -- salted
8 c  Chicken Broth
2    Eggs -- well-beaten
1/3 c  Romano Cheese -- grated
Cook spaghettini aldente in salted water and drain. Bring chicken
broth to boil; add spaghettini. Remove from heat and add beaten eggs,
stirring rapidly until eggs float in strings. Serve sprinkled with
Romano cheese.
