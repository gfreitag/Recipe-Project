Title: Un-Tomato Ketchup
Categories: Vegetables, Fatfree, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------BLEND UNTIL SMOOTH--------------------------
1 c  Cooked carrots
1/4 c  Cooked beets
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Sweetener of your choice
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Oregano
