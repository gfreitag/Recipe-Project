Title: Italian Rice
Categories: Rice, Side dish, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 md Onion -- halved & sliced
1 c  Rice
1/2 c  Water
1 cn Low sodium beef broth
*SEE NOTE
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup,
Condensed
1/2    Stick stick margarine
Brown onion in margarine.  Pour into 1 1/2 quart baking dish.  Add all
remaining ingredients.  Cover with foil and bake in 350 degree oven
for 1 1/2 hours.

NOTE:  For a side dish to complement chicken, substitute low sodium
chicken broth for the beef broth.

NUTRITIONAL INFO:  241 cal; 11.7 g fat (2.4 saturated); 1 mg chol; 30
mg carbo; 700 mg sodium; 131 mg potassium.
