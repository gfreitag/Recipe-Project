Title: Chocolate-Coconut Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Candies, Desserts
Yield: 1 9" pie
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
(14 oz)
1 pk Semi-sweet chocolate chips
(12 oz)
1 1/2 c  Flake coconut
1    Graham cracker pie crust
1 c  Walnuts or pecans, chopped
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Put a layer of chocolate chips in the
bottom of the pie crust.  Cover with a layer of nuts, then a layer of
coconut. Repeat the layers.  Pour the sweetened condensed milk over
the top and place the pie on a baking sheet.  Bake until the top
turns golden, and begins to dry--approximately 40 mins.  Allow to
cool before cutting. Submitted By PAT STOCKETT   On   06-12-95
