Title: Dill Sauce For Chicken Picardy
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Masterchefs
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Dry white wine
2    Shallots -- finely chopped
1/2 c  Chicken stock
2    Fluid ounces  heavy cream
1 ts Clarified butter
2 tb Fresh dill -- chopped
NOTE: Use with Chicken Picardy with Dill Sauce.

TO PREPARE DILL SAUCE:

Add clarified butter to a medium saucepan. Over medium heat, add
shallots and saute. Quickly add chicken stock, white wine, and heavy
cream. Reduce by half and add chopped fresh dill.

Source:  Chef Marcel Kerval of Cafe de France, St. Louis, MO
