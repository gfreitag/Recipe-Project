Title: Kristi Yamaguchi's Crowd-Pleasing Classic Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese*
1 pk Italian salad dressing
1 pk Plain yogurt
1 tb Milk
* - softened

1. Beat all ingredients in small mixing bowl at medium speed with
electric mixer until well blended. Refrigerate.

2. Serve with vegetable dippers or as a baked potato topper.

Makes 2 cups.
