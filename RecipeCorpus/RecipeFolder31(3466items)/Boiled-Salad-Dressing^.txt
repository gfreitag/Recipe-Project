Title: Boiled Salad Dressing^
Categories: Salads, Depress/era, Tasteofhome, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Butter
2    Eggs
2 ts Sugar
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Vinegar
In the top of a double boiler put the butter, eggs, sugar, mustard,
salt and pepper. Cook over very hot water until they begin to
thicken. Stir in the vinegar and continue cooking 3 minutes. Remove
from heat; beat occasionally while cooling. Keep in a cool dark
place. This dressing will remain good for several weeks.
