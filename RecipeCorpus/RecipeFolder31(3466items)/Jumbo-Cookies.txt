Title: Jumbo Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 15 cookies
21 1/2    Brownie mix
1/4 c  Flour
1/4 c  Oil
3 tb Water
2    Eggs
1 c  Peanut butter chips; or
-chocolate chips or M & M's
-or butterscotch chips
Heat oven to 375.  Lightly grease cookie sheet.  Mix brownie mix,
flour, oil, water, and eggs.  Stir in peanut butter chips. Drop by
scant 1/4 cupfuls, 6 cookies at a time onto cookie sheet. Bake just
until set, 12 to 15 minutes.  Let stand 2 to 3 minutes before
removing from cookie sheet. Cool completely. Source: Betty Crocker box
