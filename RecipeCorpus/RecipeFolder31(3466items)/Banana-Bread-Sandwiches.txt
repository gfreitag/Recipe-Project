Title: Banana Bread Sandwiches
Categories: Appetizers, Fruit, Cheese/eggs, Sandwich
Yield: 3 servings
Banana bread
Cream cheese
Crushed pineapple
Slice bread thin.  Mix package of cream cheese with crushed
pineapple; not too thin.  Spread between slices of bread and cut into
4 pieces. Randy Rigg Submitted By MICHELLE BRUCE   On   05-23-95
