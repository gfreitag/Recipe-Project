Title: Barbecued Spareribs #3
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Vinegar
2 tb Granulated sugar
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Catsup
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Paprika
1/8 ts Pepper
1    Peeled minced clove garlic
2 lb Spareribs
In a saucepan, combine all ingredients except spareribs. Cover,
simmer 15 minutes. Meanwhile, place spareribs rounded side up in
baking pan. Bake in 500 degree oven 10-15 minutes. Reduce heat to 325
degrees and bake, covered or uncovered, 1 to 1-1/2 hours, or until
spareribs are very tender brushing frequently on both sides with
sauce.

Before arranging spareribs on serving dish, cut into individual
servings of 2-3 ribs each. Add rest of sauce.
