Title: Old Time Vanilla Ice Cream
Categories: Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 8 servings
6    Eggs, separated
1 c  Sugar
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
13 oz Evaporated milk
2 cn 5.33 oz. evaporated milk
Water for each can of milk
1 tb Vanilla
Beat egg yolks and sugar together.  Add milk and water.  Add vanilla.
Beat egg whites until fluffy and fold into milk mixture.  Freeze in
gallon ice cream freezer, following manufacturer's instructions.

Yield:  1 gallon.
