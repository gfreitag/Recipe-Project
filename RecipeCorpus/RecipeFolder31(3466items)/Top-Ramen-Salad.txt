Title: Top Ramen Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1    Head white cabbage shred
2 bn Green onion sliced
3 tb Butter
2 pk Top Ramen noodles & season
1    Jar sesame seeds
1/2 c  Slivered almonds
1 c  Salad oil
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Soy sauce
3 tb Sugar
Toss cabbage and onions together and refrigerate. Melt butter in pan.
Crush noodles.  Brown noodles, seasoning, sesame seeds, & almonds.
Cool. Mix oil, vinegar, soysauce and sugar. Toss all ingredient
together just before serving. Makes one very large bowlful. (Also
good with cooked chicken or shrimp added) From:
