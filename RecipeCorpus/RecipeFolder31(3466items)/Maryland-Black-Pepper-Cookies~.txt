Title: Maryland Black Pepper Cookies~
Categories: Cake/cookie, Healthy
Yield: 60 servings
2    Eggs -- separated
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Baking powder
1 c  Raisins & nuts
Beat eggs whites and yellows seperately. Then beat together. Add
sugar & mix well. Sift dry ingredients & add to eggs. Stir in raisins
and nuts. Add more flour if needed. The dough should be stiff. Drop
by tbsp onto greased sheet. Bake at 375 for about 7 mins.

Makes about 5 doz.

Per cookie:  Calories 30; fat 0.9; cholesterol 7mg; carbohydrate 5.2g;
fiber 0.2g; protein 0.7g; sodium 19mg.
