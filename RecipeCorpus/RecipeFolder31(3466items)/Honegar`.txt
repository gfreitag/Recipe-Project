Title: Honegar`
Categories: Condiments, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Cider Vinegar
Combine the honey and vinegar. Use as a concentrate: 1 Tbs to a glass
of water. Note: You can also mix honegar with sour cream, cucumber,
yogurt, and fresh dill for a summer salad. Makes about 1 cup.
