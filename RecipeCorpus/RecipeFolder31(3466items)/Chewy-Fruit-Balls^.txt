Title: Chewy Fruit Balls^
Categories: Snacks, Low cal/fat, Healthy
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 c  Dried peaches
1/2 c  Pitted whole dates
1/3 c  Dried apriocots
1/3 c  Golden Raisins
2 tb Graham cracker crumbs
Fast & Healthy 9/92
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
In food processor bowl with metal blade, combine all ingredients
except graham cracker crumbs; process until finely chopped. shape
mixture into 1" balls; roll in graham cracker crumbs. Store in
airtight container inrefrigerator. 24 fruit balls.

Per ball: 30 calories, 8 g carbohydrate, 1 g fiber, 95 mg potassium,
1/2 fruit exchange.
