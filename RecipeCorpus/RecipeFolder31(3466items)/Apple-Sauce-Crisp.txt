Title: Apple Sauce Crisp
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Uncooked rolled oats
16 oz Apple sauce
1 ts Cinnamon
Place butter, sugar and oats into a mixing bowl and mix with a fork
until it looks like coarse crumbs.  Pour the Apple sauce into a
shallow baking dish, or pie pan, and sprinkle evenly with cinnamon.
Sprinkle the butter mixture evenly over the apple sauce.  Bake at 350
degrees for 30 minutes or until top is crisp.  Serve hot or cold.
