Title: Dill Popcorn Seasoning
Categories: Snacks, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Dill weed
1 ts Dried parsley
1 ts Dried chives
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 c  Corn, popped
Butter to taste
Combine the salt, dill, parsley, chives and garlic powder. Mix well.
Use one or two teaspoons per 1/2 cup corn, popped with the butter.
