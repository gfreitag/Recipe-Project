Title: Green Bean Pasta Bake
Categories: Pasta, Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
6 oz Macaroni
1 c  Onion; chopped
1 c  Mushroom; sliced
1/2 ts Basil; crushed
1 tb Oil
14 1/2 oz Stewed tomatoes; italian
8 oz Tomato sauce
16 oz Green beans; drained
1 c  Monterey jack; shredded
Cook pasta as directed on package; drain.  In 10" skillet cook onion,
mushrooms, and basil in oil until onion is tender. Add tomatoes and
tomato sauce; cook, uncovered, over medium high heat, 5 minutes.
Remove from heat. Stir in pasta, green beans and 1/2 cup cheese.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Pour into 8 x 12 baking dish.
Top with remaining cheese. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes.  Source: Del
