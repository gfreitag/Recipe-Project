Title: Lemon Honey`
Categories: Condiments, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Rind From 1 Lemon
1 c  Honey
Grind up the lemon rind in a food processor. Then blend with the
honey. Serve lemon honey over fresh fruit for dessert. Makes 1 cup.
