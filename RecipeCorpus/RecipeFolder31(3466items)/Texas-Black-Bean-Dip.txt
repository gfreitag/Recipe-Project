Title: Texas Black Bean Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetarian, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Black beans; drained (15oz)
1 tb Onion; finely chopped
1 tb Cilantro; finely chopped
2 ts Pickled jalapeno; minced
1 1/2 tb Pickled jalapeno juice
1/2 ts Vege-Sal seasoning  *or*
.  your favorite seasoning
.  blend
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Ground cumin
Combine all the ingredients in a blender or food processor.  Process
until smooth.  Serve cold or warm with your favorite dip chips or
crackers.
