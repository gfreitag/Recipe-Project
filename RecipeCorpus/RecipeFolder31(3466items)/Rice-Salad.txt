Title: Rice Salad
Categories: Italian, Masterchefs, Salads, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
400 g  Rice
200 g  Cooked shrimp
200 g  Fontina cheese, strips
200 g  Canned tuna, strips
100 g  Peas
100 g  Salami, strips
100 g  Ham, strips
100 g  Olives
100 g  Capers
200 ml Olive oil
50 g  Lemon juice
70 g  Parsley
Salt and pepper
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Cook the rice. Mix together.
