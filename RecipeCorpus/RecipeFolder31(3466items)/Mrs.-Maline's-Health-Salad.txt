Title: Mrs. Maline's Health Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 5 servings
1 x  Med head cabbage, shredded
3 x  Carrots, sliced thin
1 x  Green pepper, sliced thin
2 x  Cucumbers, peeled & diced
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Oil
1 x  Lg Onion, sliced thin
3/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
Mix liquids. Add vegetables and let stand in fridge overnight.
