Title: Healthy Heart Macaroni and Cheese
Categories: Casseroles, Pasta, Cheese/eggs, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 tb Margarine
1/4 c  Flour
3/4 ts Dry mustard
1/8 ts Ground red pepper
3 c  Skim milk
1 1/4 c  Shredded low-fat sharp
Cheddar, divided
1/4 c  Shredded low-fat Swiss
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
5 c  Cooked macaroni
In saucepan, melt margarine. Combine flour, dry mustard, and red
pepper. Slowly add to margarine and stir to make a roux. Then add the
milk, stirring after each addition to make a sauce. Add the cheeses
slowly, stirring to melt. Add the salt, pepper and macaroni, stirring
to combine. Put into a casserole dish, cover and bake at 350 for
25-30 minutes or until bubbly and light brown. 1 serving = 223 cal
6.8 g fat Submitted By CAROL VERGE   On   06-06-95
