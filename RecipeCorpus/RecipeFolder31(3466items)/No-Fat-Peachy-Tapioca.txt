Title: No-Fat Peachy Tapioca
Categories: Desserts, Puddings, Fruit, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Tapioca; quick cooking
1 cn Peach slices (16oz)
.  (includes the juice)
1/4 ts Almond extract
Place the water, sugar and tapioca in a 4-cup glass measure or
casserole. Drain the peach juice from the can into the mixture.
Reserve 4 peach slices for garnish.

PLace the remaining peaches in a food processor, pulse on and off a
few times to coarsely mash (or mash the peaches using a potato
masher). Add the mashed peaches to the tapioca mixture; let stand for
5 minutes.

Stirring with a spoon after each 2 minutes, microwave on high 4 to 5
minutes, or until the mixture begins to boil.  Stir in the almond
extract and pour into 4 dessert dishes.  Cool 30 minutes, garnish
with the reserved peach slices and then refrigerate.

Nutritional information: per serving: 106 calories, 0g fat, 10mg
sodium, 26g carbohydrates, 0mg cholesterol
