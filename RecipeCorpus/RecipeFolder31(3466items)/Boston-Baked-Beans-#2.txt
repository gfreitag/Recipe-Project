Title: Boston Baked Beans #2
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Navy (pea) beans
6    Whole cloves
1 md Onion
6    Slices bacon cut into 1/2
-inch pieces
1/2 c  Unsulphured molasses
1/4 c  Firm packed dark brown
-sugar
1 tb Dry mustard
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Salt
Remove and discard any stones from beans; rinse and drain. In 2 1/ 2
quart casserole, combine beans and 6 cup water. In microwave, cook,
covered, on high 16-22 minutes until boiling. Cook on high 2 minutes.
Remove and let stand, covered, 1 hour. Cook, covered, at medium ,50%
power, 30-40 minutes until beans are tender, but not soft. Press
cloves into onion; add with remaining ingredients to casserole. Cook,
covered, on high 30-40 minutes, stirring 4 times, until beans are
soft. If desired, remove and discard onion before serving.
