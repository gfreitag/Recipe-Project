Title: Corned Beef Salad
Categories: Salads, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
3 oz Package lemon gelatin
1 c  Boiling water
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
8 oz Carton of sour cream
3 tb Horseradish
1 sm Onion, minced
1 sm Green pepper, minced
3    Hard boiled eggs
1 cn Corned beef, crumbled
Dissolve gelatin in water, then cool.  Add all ingredients to cooled
Jello. Pour into a 9 x 14 inch pan and chill overnight.  I mix
ingredients in my blender, mixing briefly after each addition, then
mix into the Jello. Randy Rigg
