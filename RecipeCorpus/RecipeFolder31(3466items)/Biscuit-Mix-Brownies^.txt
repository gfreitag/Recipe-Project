Title: Biscuit-Mix Brownies^
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 dozen
Southern Living 2/94
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
1 pk (12oz) chocolate chips
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 cn (14oz) sweetened condensed
Milk
1 lg Egg, lightly beaten
2 c  Biscuit mix
1 1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1 cn (16oz) ready to spread
Chocolate Fudge Frosting,
Optional
Combine chocolate morsels and butter in a microwave sage bowl;
microwave at high 2 minutes, stirring once.

Stir in condensed milk, egg, biscuit mix and pecans. Spoon into a
greased 13x9x2" pan.

Bake at 350F. for 25-30 minutes. Cool slightly on a wire rack. Spread
chocolate frosting on warm brownies, if desired. Cut into squares.
