Title: Sweet Sour Cole Slaw
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 lb Shredded green cabbage
1 ts Salt
1/3 c  Vinegar
1 c  Whipping cream
2/3 c  Sugar
Place shredded cabbage in refrigerator dish for several hours; mix
ingredients in this order: cabbage, salt, sugar, vinegar, whipping
cream. Mix 30 minutes before serving.  Chill and serve. Randy Rigg
