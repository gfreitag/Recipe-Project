Title: Corn Oysters^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Green corn
3 tb Flour, sifted
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg
1/2 c  Milk
Butter for frying
Scrape or grate corn from cob. Mix flour and salt with egg and milk
to make a batter. Stir in the corn. Drop by spoonfuls into hot butter
and fry until brown; turn over and brown other side. Serve hot.
