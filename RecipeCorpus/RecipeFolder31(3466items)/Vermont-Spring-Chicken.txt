Title: Vermont Spring Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1    Chicken, 2 1/2 to 3 lbs.,
-cut up
1/2 c  Butter, melted
1/2 c  Maple syrup
1/2 ts Grated lemon rind
1 ts Salt
1 ds Of pepper
1/4 c  Almonds, chopped
2 ts Lemon juice
Place chicken pieces in shallow buttered baking dish. Mix remaining
ingredients and pour evenly over chicken. Bake uncovered 50-60
minutes at 400 F. Baste occasionally. Delicious on a bed of rice

From: Steve Herrick Source: [Dakin Farm - Ferrisburg, VT]
