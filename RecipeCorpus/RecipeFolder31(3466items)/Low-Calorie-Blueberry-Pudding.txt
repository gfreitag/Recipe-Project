Title: Low-Calorie Blueberry Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Puddings
Yield: 1 servings
1    Envelope unflavored gelatin
1/4 c  Water
2    Eggs, separated
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Skim milk
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Grated lemon rind
1 c  Fresh blueberries
2 tb Sugar
Stir gelatin into water and set aside.  Combine egg yolks, 1/3 cup
sugar, salt and milk in saucepan.  Cook and stir over low heat for 3
minutes. Add gelatin, vanilla and lemon rind.  Cool until pudding
begins to thicken. Fold in blueberries.  Beat egg whites until stiff
peaks form. Slowly beat in 2 tablespoons sugar.  Fold into pudding.
Chill.

Source: King's Orchard, Plantersville, Texas
