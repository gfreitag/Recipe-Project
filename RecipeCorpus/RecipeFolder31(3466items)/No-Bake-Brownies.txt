Title: No-Bake Brownies
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 16 servings
1 c  Evaporated milk
2 c  Miniature marshmallows
1 c  Semiseet chocolate chips
1/3 c  Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
1/2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/2 c  Nuts, chopped
Mix together milk, marshmallows, chocolate chips, sugar, and salt in
heavy saucepan.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Boil for 5
minutes. Remove from heat.  Add butter, vanilla, cracker crumbs and
nuts. Pack into a 9-inch square pan.  Refrigerate to harden.  Cut
into 2-inch squares.

Per serving:  197.6 Calories; 7.4g total Fat (3.0g saturated); 3.7g
Protein; 12.3g Carbohydrates; 2.6mg Cholesterol; 151.3mg Sodium
