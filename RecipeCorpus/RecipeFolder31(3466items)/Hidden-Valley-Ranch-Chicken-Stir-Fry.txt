Title: Hidden Valley Ranch Chicken Stir Fry
Categories: Poultry, Wok
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Vegetable Oil
1/2 lb Chicken breasts; boneless
-and skinless
1 pk Hidden Valley Original
-Ranch Salad Dressing (dry)
16 oz Vegetable Medley; thawed
2 tb Water
Heat oil in a large skillet or WOK.  Add chicken which has been cut
into thin strips.  Stir in dry Hidden Valley Ranch dressing.  Mix to
coat chicken.  Add thawed vegetables and 2 TB water.  Stir fry about
2 minutes.

Sunset Magazine advertisement, May 1994
