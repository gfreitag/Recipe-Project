Title: Guido's Mustard Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
10    Garlic cloves -- minced
1 tb Pepper
1/2 tb Oregano
1 ts Rosemary
1 tb Worcestershire
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
4 tb Teryaki sauce
4 tb Brown sauce or A-1 sauce
1 tb Red wine
4 tb Dijon mustard
Saute garlic over low heat in a small, dry skillet for 2-3 minutes,
stirring constantly.  Add pepper, oregano and rosemary and continue
sauteeing and stirring.  Add Worcestershire and balsamic vinegar and
continue stirring. Add teryaki and brown sauces and continue
stirring. Add wine and Dijon mustard and cook, stirring constantly,
for 2-3 minutes.
