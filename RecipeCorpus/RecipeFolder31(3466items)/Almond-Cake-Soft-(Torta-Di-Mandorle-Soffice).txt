Title: Almond Cake Soft (Torta Di Mandorle Soffice)
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
8    Eggs
300 g  Sugar
300 g  Almond flour
150 g  Flour
10 g  Yeast
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Beat eggs and sugar. Add almonds, flour and yeast. Pour in a mould,
cook in oven 180 C for 30 mins.
