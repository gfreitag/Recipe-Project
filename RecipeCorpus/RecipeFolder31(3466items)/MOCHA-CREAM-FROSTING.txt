Title: MOCHA CREAM FROSTING
Categories: Diabetic, Cake/cookie, Dress/stuff
Yield: 2 cups
2/3 c  Whipping cream
1/4 c  Cocoa powder
1 ts Instant coffee granules
2/3 c  Sugar substitute; Splenda
1/2 c  Plain yogurt; low fat
Beat cream with cocoa and instant coffee until stiff peaks form. Fold
in sugar substitute and yogurt.  Spread over cake.  Store in
refrigerator.

Nutritional information per Tablespoon: Calories: 23 Protein: 0.4 g
Fat: 2.0 g  Carbohydrates:  1.3 g

Origin:  Splenda Low-Calorie Sweetener
