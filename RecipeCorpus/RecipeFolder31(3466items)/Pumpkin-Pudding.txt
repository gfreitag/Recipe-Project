Title: Pumpkin Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Holiday, Puddings
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Cooked pumpkin
1    Egg
1 c  Milk
1 ts Cloves
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1 ds Salt
Place mixture in a saucepan and cook 15-20 mins.  Serve.
