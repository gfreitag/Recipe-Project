Title: Marinated Anchovy
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Fish
Yield: 10 servings
200 g  Boneless anchovy
300 ml Lemon juice
150 ml Olive oil
50 g  Chopped parsley
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.
Mix the lemon juice, olive oil and parsley and pour over anchovies.
Marinate for 3-4 hours.
