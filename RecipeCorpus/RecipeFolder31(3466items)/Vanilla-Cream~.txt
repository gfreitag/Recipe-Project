Title: Vanilla Cream~
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 2 servings
1 1/2 c  Lowfat vanilla yogurt
1/2 c  Light whipping cream
1 tb Confectioner's sugar
1 tb Orange liqueur, such
-as Grand Marnier
Line a sieve with cheesecloth and set it over a bowl.  (Alternately,
use a coffee filter lined with filter paper.) Spoon in yogurt and let
it drain in the refrigerator until reduced to 1 cup, about 1 hour.

In a chilled mixing bowl with chilled beaters, whip cream to soft
peaks. Add the drained yogurt, sugar, and liqueur; fold gently to
mix. Serve immediately or refrigerate, covered, for up to 8 hours.

Per two tablespoons: Calories: 50; fat: 3g; sodium: 16mg; cholesterol:
10mg.
