Title: French Dip Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
10 oz Can beef broth
10 oz Can Franco Am Beef Gravy
10 oz Can C of Mush soup
Combine ingredients & warm in MW 6 min, covered. To serve, place in
cup in center of dinner plate. Surround with French Fries (use in
place of ketchup) Pg 84 Famous Favorites. Formatted for MM by PNewton
