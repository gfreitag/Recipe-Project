Title: TEA PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
3 c  Tea; strong
1 c  Lemon juice
4 c  Orange juice
2 c  Raspberry syrup; or grenadin
1 c  Pineapple, crushed
Sugar; to taste
2 qt Club soda; chilled
Combine all ingredients except club soda in a punch bowl with a large
block of clear ice; allow to chill thoroughly.  (If fresh fruit
juices are used, they should be strained.)  Add club soda just before
serving.
