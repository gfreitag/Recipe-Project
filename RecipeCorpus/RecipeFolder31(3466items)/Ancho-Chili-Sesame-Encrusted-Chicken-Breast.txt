Title: Ancho Chili Sesame Encrusted Chicken Breast
Categories: Poultry, Chilies, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 servings
Serves 8
8 ea Boneless, skinless chicken
-breasts
1/4 c  Ancho Chili Sauce
MMMMM------------------------SESAME CRUST-----------------------------
1 c  Sesame seeds
1/2 c  Poppy seeds
1 tb Onion powder
1 tb Mustard powder
1/4 c  Honey
1/2 c  Tomato sauce
Method: Mix all ingredients for sesame crust with Ancho Chili Sauce.
