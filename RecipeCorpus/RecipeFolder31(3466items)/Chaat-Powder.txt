Title: Chaat Powder
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 tablespoon
2 ts Mango (amchoor) powder
1/4 ts Cumin seeds
-toasted and ground
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Black salt *
1/2 ts Asafoetida powder
Black pepper
1 ds Cayenne
* this is an essential ingredient and there is no substitute

Combine all ingredients. Grind to a fine powder. Store in an airtight
container.
