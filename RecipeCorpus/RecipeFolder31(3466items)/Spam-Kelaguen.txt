Title: Spam Kelaguen
Categories: Spam
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Spam, mashed
2 tb Lemon juice, fresh
1/2 md Onion, diced
2 ea Green onions, diced
1 1/2 c  Coconut, grated
Salt
Pepper
Red Pepper
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and chill thoroughly. Serve
with tortillas or crackers.
