Title: Sugarless Pumpkin Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Pumpkin
1/4 c  Granulated sugar substitute
1 ts Ginger
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Salt
2    Eggs
1 c  Undiluted condensed milk
Mix all ingredients and pour into unbaked pie shell. Bake for 45
minutes at 350*F.
