Title: Spam Eskabechi
Categories: Spam
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Spam
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 lg Onion, sliced lengthwise
1/4 c  Fresh ginger, chopped
2 c  Green Papaya, chopped
1 ea Bell pepper
1/3 c  Vinegar
1 c  Water
2 tb Soy Sauce
2 ea Tomatoes, sliced
3 ea Green Onions, chopped
1 tb Cornstarch
Fry garlic and onions in a small amount of oil. Add papaya and
peppers and cook another 10 minutes on medium heat. Add vinegar,
water and soy sauce. Bring to boil. Add corn startch that has been
added to cold water. Stir in and allow sauce to thicken. Spoon over
warmed spam. Remove to platter and place in warm oven for about 5
minutes to allow flavers to combine. Serve over steamed rice.
