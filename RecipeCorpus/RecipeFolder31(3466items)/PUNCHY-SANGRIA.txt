Title: PUNCHY SANGRIA
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Tasteofhome
Yield: 2 quarts
12 oz Lemonade concentrate, pink;
-thawed and undiluted
4 1/2 c  Rose; chilled
1    Lime; juice of
2 c  Club soda; chilled
1    Lemon; thinly sliced
1    Orange; thinly sliced
Combine lemonade, rose, and lime juice; stir until blended.  Slowly
stir in club soda.  Garnish with lemon and orange slices.  Serve over
ice.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, July, 1980. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
