Title: Jiffy Chili-Hominy Bake
Categories: Main dish, Chili, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 c  Chili with BEANS
1 tb Chili powder
1 cn Condensed cream of chicken
Soup
20 oz Yellow hominy, drained
2 tb Sliced ripe olives
Cheese
Cook ground beef and onion until meat is browned.  Stir in remaining
ingredients except the cheese.  Spoon into a 2 quart casserole.
Cover; bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.  Sprinkle cheese overtop
and continue baking, uncovered for 5 minutes. Randy Rigg
