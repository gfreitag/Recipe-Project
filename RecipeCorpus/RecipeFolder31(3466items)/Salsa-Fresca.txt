Title: Salsa Fresca
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Tomatoes(diced)
1/3 c  Onion(minced)
4    Serrano or jalapeno chiles
1/4 c  Cilantro(chopped)
1    Garlic clove(minced)
1/4 c  Lime juice
1 ts Oregano -- dried
Salt and pepper to taste
Stem, seed and mince peppers.  Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
Taste for seasoning and adjust if necessary.  Let stand 30 minutes
before serving. Store in refrigerator up to 2 days
