Title: English Pierogi
Categories: Appetizers, Polish, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Flour
1    Yeast cake
1 c  Sweet cream
4    Egg yolks
1/4 lb Beef marrow or butter
sm Glass of wine
1 tb Sugar
Break yeast into cream. Add remaining ingredients and knead into a
firm dough. Roll thin, cut into rounds and fill with marmalade,
conserve, apples, gooseberries, cherries, or plums. Fold over and
seal edges. Fry in butter. Note: No servings amount was given. The 1
stands for 1 batch of pierogies.

Recipe from " Treasured Polish Recipes For Americans " Published by
Polanie Publishing Company Minneapolis, Minnesota
