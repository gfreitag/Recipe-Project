Title: Oyster Bay Bake
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
18    Ritz crackrs, crumbled
17 oz Cream style corn
16 oz Oysters, drained
1 cn Mushroom soup
1/4 c  Milk
1 c  Mild Cheddar cheese cubes
1/2 inch size
Put crumbled crackers in a greased 8 inch square pan.  Spread corn
over crackers, arrange oysters over corn.  Combine soup, milk and
cheese. Spoon over oysters.  Sprinkle topping over all and bake at
450 degrees for 10 minutes then 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Topping 1/2 c butter 1 c flour 18 Ritz crackers 1 dash pepper

Cut the butter into the flour.  Crumble the crackers and stir in.
Add the pepper. Randy Rigg
