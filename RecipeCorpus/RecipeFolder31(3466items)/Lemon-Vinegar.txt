Title: Lemon Vinegar
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 2 cups vineg
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: BETTY CROCKER'S COOK---------------------
2 c  White wine or white vinegar
Peel from 2 lemons
Shake vinegar and lemon peel in tightly covered glass jar or bottle.
Store in cool, dry place 10 days. Strain vinegar. Place one strip of
lemon peel in jar to identify, if desired.
