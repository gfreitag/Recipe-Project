Title: Oven Barbecued Spareribs
Categories: Meats, Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
4    To 5 lbs Spareribs
1/4 c  Brown sugar
3 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Vinegar
13 1/2 oz Can Crushed pineapple
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Chopped fresh ginger
Combine all ingredients (except ribs) and cook until thickened. Pour
over browned, drained ribs. Cover; bake 1 hour or until tender.
Serves 6.

(I usually par boil the ribs and then bake them with the sauce in a
slow oven for several hours.)
