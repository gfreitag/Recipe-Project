Title: Dog Biscuits #2
Categories: Pets
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Dry milk powder
1 ea Egg
1 ts Grated carrot
1/2 c  Wheat germ
1 ts Brown sugar
1 c  All-purpose flour
6 tb Marg. or shortening
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Grated cheese
Preheat oven to 325.  Combine flours, milk, marg/shortening, wheat
germ - mix until it resembles corn meal.  Beat brown sugar into egg,
stir mixture into flour mix.  Add water slowly to make stiff dough.
Knead and roll out 1/2 inch thick, cut into shapes with cookie
cutter. Bake for 30 minutes, let cool and store in sealed container.
