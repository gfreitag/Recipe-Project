Title: Mama's Rhubarb Jam
Categories: Can/preserv
Yield: 36 ounces
2 1/2 lb Rhubarb
1 1/2 lb Sugar
1/2 c  Water
2    Oranges (rind & Juice)
Wash rhubarb and cut into small pieces, add sugar and water. Grate
rind of oranges and add to rhubarb. Add orange juice and cook 30
minutes, stirring occasionally. Fill sterile glasses and seal. Makes
about 6 (6oz) glasses. Submitted By KATHRYN CONE   On   06-01-95
