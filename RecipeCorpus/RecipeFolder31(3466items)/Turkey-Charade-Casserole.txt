Title: Turkey Charade Casserole
Categories: Poultry, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Mayonaise
1 c  Chicken broth(h2o or gravy)
1/4 c  Green pepper, chopped
1 cn Chow mein noodles
2 ts Onion, minced
1 c  Milk
2 c  Diced turkey, cooked
2 x  Eggs, beaten
1/4 c  Slivered almonds
Brown onions in butter. Add flour, broth, milk and cook over medium
flame until bubbly. Mix Mayo and beaten egg in a separate bowl. Add
part of white sauce to egg mixture & stir, then return to rest of
mixture. Cook one more minute till thickened. Fold in the rest of the
ingredients. Place in a 2 quart greased casserole and bake at 350 for
35 minutes. Submitted By MARK MIKLAS   On   05-24-95
