Title: 8 Quart relish
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 3 servings
1 qt Chopped mangoes
1 qt Chopped cabbage
1 qt Chopped green tomatoes
1 qt Chopped onions
1 qt Chopped sweet pickles
1 qt Chopped celery
1 qt Vinegar
1 qt Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Tumeric
Salt to taste
Chop all vegetables and measure.  Add sugar, salt and vinegar, cook
for ten minutes.  Mix flour and tumeric with a little water, add and
cook for 5 minutes longer. Randy Rigg
