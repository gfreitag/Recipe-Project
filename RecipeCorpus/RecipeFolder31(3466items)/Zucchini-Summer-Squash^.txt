Title: Zucchini Summer Squash^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Or 2 zucchini
1 sm Onion, chopped
2 tb Or 3 butter
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Tomatoes, chopped
1 c  Cheese, grated
Wash squash and slice in half lengthwise, then slice about 1/4"
thick. Melt butter in large skillet; add zucchini and onion and fry
until moisture is reduced. Add salt, pepper and tomato. Simmer about
10 minutes. Add cheese about 5 minutes before serving. Cover pan to
melt cheese.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
