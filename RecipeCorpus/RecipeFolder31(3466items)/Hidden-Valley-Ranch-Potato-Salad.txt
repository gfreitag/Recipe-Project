Title: Hidden Valley Ranch Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Potatoes, red new; cooked
1/2 c  Green onion; sliced
1 c  Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing
Paprika; or black pepper
Crumbled bacon; optional
Cut slightly cooled potatoes into 1" cubes.  In large bowl, combine
potatoes, onions and ranch dressing; toss gently. Dust with paprika.
Garnish with chives. Serve warm. Source: Hidden Valley ad
