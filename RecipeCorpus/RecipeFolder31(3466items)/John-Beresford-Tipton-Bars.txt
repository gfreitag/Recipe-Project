Title: John Beresford Tipton Bars
Categories: Candies, Snacks, Oldies
Yield: 10 servings
1 lb Golden Almond Bars; (5
Bars)
1 c  Froot Loops Cereal
1 c  Rice Krispies
1 c  Miniature Marshmallows
NOTE:  One pound of almond bark can be substituted for the candy bars
if they are not available.
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
+++

Melt candy bars in a double boiler.  Remove from heat, add cereals,
mix until coated, then add marshmallows.  Mix well.  Pour into a
buttered pan about 7 by 10 inches.  Let cool until set.  Cut into 20
to 30 small squares.
