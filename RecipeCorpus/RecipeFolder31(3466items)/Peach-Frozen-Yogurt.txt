Title: Peach Frozen Yogurt
Categories: Low cal/fat, Desserts
Yield: 7 .5 cups
1 pk Unflavored gelatin
1 c  Skim milk
1/2 c  Sugar
ds Salt
2 1/2 c  Vanilla low-fat yogurt
2 ts Vanilla extract
3 c  Frozen sliced peaches,
-pureed
Sprinkle gelatin over milk in a saucepan; let stand 1 minute.  Cook
over low heat, stirring constantly, until gelatin dissolves.  Remove
from heat, and add sugar and salt, stirring until sugar dissolves.
Stir in yogurt, vanilla, and pureed peaches.  Cover and chill.

Pour yogurt mixture into freezer can of a 4-quart hand-turned or
electric freezer; freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.
Scoop yogurt into individual dishes.  Serve immediately.  (Serving
size: 1/2 cup)

Calories 82 / Protein 3.0 / Fat 0.5 / Carbohydrate 16.6 / Cholesterol
2 / Iron 0.1 / Sodium 43 / Calcium 85
