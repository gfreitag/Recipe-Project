Title: Scallop Cauliflower
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
2    Eggs
1 cn Cream of celery soup
2 pk Frozen cauliflower, thawed
1/2 c  Grated American cheese
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1/4 c  Parsley, cut fine
2 tb Diced onion
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Heat oven to 375 degrees.  Butter a 1 1/2 quart baking dish.  Beat
eggs and stir in remaining ingredients.  Turn into baking dish and
set in pan of hot water.  Bake about 50 minutes or until firm.  Serve
hot. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   06-05-95
