Title: Crispix Mix Sweet Minglers
Categories: Snacks, Kids
Yield: 8 cups
1 c  Chocolate chips
1/4 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Peanuts; dry roasted
1 c  Powdered sugar
In large microwavable bowl, melt chocolate on HIGH for 1 minute. Stir
and heat an additional 30 seconds at HIGH or until melted. Stir in
peanut butter and peanuts. Gently stir Crispix cereal into peanut
butter mixture, until well coated.  Place powdered sugar in 2 gallon
storage bag. Add coated cereal to sugar and close bag. Gently toss
until well coated. Store in airtight container in fridge.  Source:
