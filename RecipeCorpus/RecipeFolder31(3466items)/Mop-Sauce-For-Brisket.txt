Title: Mop Sauce For Brisket
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
CROUCH NNBB09A
1/4 c  Water -- boiling
1 tb Instant coffee
1 c  Tomato sauce
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1/2 c  Worchestershire sauce
6 tb Sugar
2 ts Oregano -- leaf (crushed)
1/4 ts Pepper
6 tb Butter
ADD COFFEE TO BOILING WATER AND THEN ADD THE REST OF THE INGREDIENTS
ONE AT A TIME. COOK ON LOW HEAT 1/2 HOUR. POUR OVER SLICED BRISKET.
