Title: Sweet Onion Relish 2
Categories: Vegetables, Relishes
Yield: 1 servings
3 md Sweet onions
2 tb Butter
3/4 c  Ketchup
Garlic powder to taste
Worcestershire to taste
Tabasco sauce to taste
Saute onions in butter.  Add ketchup, garlic powder, worcestershire
and tabasco. Serve with hamburgers, or as a side dish.

Source: Rock Springs Sweet Onion Festival Cook Book 1992
