Title: Hunter's Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 lb Mushrooms -- slice
1 tb Shallots -- chop fine
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/3 c  Dry white wine
1/2 c  Fresh or canned tomatoes
-peeled and chopped
1/2 c  Beef broth
1/2 ts Fresh tarragon -- chop or
1/4 ts Dried tarragon
1 ts Cornstarch or arrowroot
BLEND WITH
2 ts Water
Salt and pepper
HUNTER'S SAUCE In saucepan cook mushrooms, shallots, salt and pepper
in butter about 10 minutes.

Add wine; cook briefly over high heat. Add tomatoes, broth and
tarragon. Cook, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes. Stir cornstarch
into sauce. Cook and stir until thickened.

Serving ideas:  Serve over flank steak Recipe By
