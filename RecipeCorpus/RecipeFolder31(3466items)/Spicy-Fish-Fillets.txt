Title: Spicy Fish Fillets
Categories: Fish, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
6 oz Fish fillets such as
-flounder, sole or cod
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  (2-oz.) grated Parmesan
-cheese
1/2 c  Cornflake crumbs
1/4 ts Ground red pepper
Preheat oven to 450 F. Dip fish in egg; coat with combined cheese,
crumbs and red pepper. Place in greased shallow baking dish. Bake 5
to 10 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.

Serves 6.

Variation: Substitute boneless, skinless chicken breasts for fish,
decrease temperature to 400 F., bake 15 to 20 minutes or until tender.
