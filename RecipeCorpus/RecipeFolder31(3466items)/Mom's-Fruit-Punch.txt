Title: Mom's Fruit Punch
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 lg Can of apricot nectar
1 lg Can of pineapple juice
1 cn Frozen lemonade concentrate
(as it comes from the can)
3 sm Cans of frozen orange juice
(with water added)
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Water
2 lg Bottles of Ginger Ale
Boil sugar and water together until dissolved.  Mix all ingredients
and serve. Randy Rigg
