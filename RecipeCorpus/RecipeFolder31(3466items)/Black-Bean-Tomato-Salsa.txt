Title: Black Bean-Tomato Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Tex/mexican, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Black beans; canned
1/2 c  Cilantro; chopped
1/4 c  Red onion; chopped
1/4 c  Green onion; chopped
3 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 ts Cumin
1/2 c  Tomatoes; chopped
Salt and pepper
This savory salsa would be good on chicken, seafood, or pork.

Mix all ingredients except tomatoes. When mixed, stir in tomatoes and
season with salt and pepper.

Source: Bon Appetit Barbecue special edition 1993

Personal Note:  I cut the cilantro to 1/4 c. because it tends to
overpower everything else if left in the present measure. I also
added one small jalapeno pepper: chopped, and one small clove garlic;
crushed, to add a little "snap".  Great dip with nacho cheese
