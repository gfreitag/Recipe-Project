Title: Low Density Biskettes
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 biscuits
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Milk or buttermilk
Boot oven to 400F. Combine the first 3 ingredients in a large bowl.
Mix thoroughly. Cut the butter into the flour mixture with a fork.
Stir in the milk and mix well. Knead dough for a few minutes. Roll
out onto a floured surface to about 3/4-inch thickness. Cut out
biscuits with a cutter and place on a greased baking sheet. Bake
until lightly browned on top. *From "Quick Bytes: Computer Lover's
Cookbook" by Diane Pfeifer* -Typed for you by Michelle Bruce
