Title: Pineapple Salsa 2
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
15 1/4 oz Crushed Pineapple -- drain
2 tb Cream of coconut
1 tb Lime juice
1 tb Cilantro -- snip
1 ts Grated lime peel
Combine all ingredients in 2-c glass measure. Microwave (HIGH) until
heated through (about 1-1/2 minutes). Serve over fish steaks and
fillets. (Tested in 650-700 watt microwave oven.)
