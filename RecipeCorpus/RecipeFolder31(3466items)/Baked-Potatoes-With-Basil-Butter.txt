Title: Baked Potatoes With Basil Butter
Categories: Side dish, Potatoes, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
8 lg Russet potatoes (about 5
-pounds)
1 tb Oil
1/4 c  Basil leaves -- chopped
1    Clove garlic -- minced
3 tb Olive oil
4 tb Butter -- softened
Salt and pepper
Preheat the oven to 400.  Scrub the potatoes, pat dry, and rub with a
light film of vegetable oil.  Bake the potatoes for 1 hour, or until
tender. Cook the basil leaves and garlic in the olive oil until
wilted, about 30 seconds.  Mix into the butter and season liberally
with salt and pepper. Serve 4 potatoes with 8 tbsp basil butter.
Reserve 4 potatoes for Crocked Blue Cheese Potatoes and Ham.
