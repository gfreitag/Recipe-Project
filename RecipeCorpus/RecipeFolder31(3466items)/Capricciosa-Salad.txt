Title: Capricciosa Salad
Categories: Salads, Italian, Masterchefs
Yield: 6 servings
300 g  Carrots julienned
300 g  Celery julienned
300 g  Mayonnaise
100 g  Mustard
300 g  Ham julienned
300 g  White radish julienned
Salt and pepper
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Mix all together.
