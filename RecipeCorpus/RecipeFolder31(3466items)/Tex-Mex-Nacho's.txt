Title: Tex Mex Nacho's
Categories: Snacks, Tex/mexican, Vegetables, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
200 g  Blue nacho chips
1 c  Grated Monterey Jack cheese
1 c  Sliced chicken strips
-(cooked)
1 c  Seeded, diced tomato
2 tb Pickled jalapeno peppers,
-chopped fine
Prep time: 15 minutes.  Bake time: 10 minutes.

guacamole, salsa and sour cream as dips

Place about 1/2 the bag of nacho chips onto baking dish. Place half of
remaining ingredients evenly over chips.  Spread remaining nacho's
over and top with remaining ingredients.  Bake in a 400 F oven for
about 8-10 minutes of until cheese melts and is bubbly.  Top with
chop green onions if desired.  Serve with guacamole, salsa and sour
cream as dippers.

Origin: Adapted from Krafts 'What's Cooking' magazine, 12 th issue.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, May/95.
