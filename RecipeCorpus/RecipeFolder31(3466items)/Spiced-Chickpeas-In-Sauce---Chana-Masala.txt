Title: Spiced Chickpeas In Sauce - Chana Masala
Categories: Vegetables, Fatfree, Indian
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Dried chickpeas (kabuli
Chana)
1 ds Oil or Pam
1 ts Cumin seeds
1 ts Turmeric
1 pn Asafetida
1/2    To 1 teaspoon red pepper
1 ea Salt to taste
1 tb +_2_teaspoons shredded
Ginger
1    Green chili, minced
1 sm Tomato, chopped
3 tb Lemon or lime juice
2 tb Cilantro
Wash the chickpeas, and soak overnight.  Drain. [Spray pam in a heated
non-stick saucepan.]  Add cumin, turmeric, asafetida, red pepper and
salt. Stir until cumin seeds crackle.  Add chickpeas and cover with
water by two inches.  Add 1 T. ginger, the chili, and the tomato.
Bring to a boil. Cover and simmer for 1-1/2 - 2 hours or until the
chickpeas are tender. Remove from the heat and sprinkle with lemon
juice, remaining ginger and cilantro.
