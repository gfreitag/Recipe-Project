Title: Mother's Everyday Bread^
Categories: Breads, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2    Cakes yeast
1 ts Sugar
5 c  Warm water
4 ts Salt
3/4 c  Sugar
10 tb Fat
16 c  Flour, about
Dissolve yeast and sugar in 1/2 cup water; set aside for 10 minutes.
Mix together with the yeast the rest of the water, the salt, sugar
and about 6 cups flour. Beat well, then keep adding flour until a
stiff dough is formed. Knead well about 10 minutes. cover, let rise.
Punch down, cover, let rise again. Put into greased pans, let rise
1/2 hour, bake at 350 degrees for about 50 minutes. Makes 5-6 loaves
~ enough for a week if you're lucky! Mmmmmm!
