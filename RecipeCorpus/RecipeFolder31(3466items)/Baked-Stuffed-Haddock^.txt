Title: Baked Stuffed Haddock^
Categories: Fish, Tasteofhome, Depress/era
Yield: 1 servings
2    Or 3 haddock fillets
1 c  Bread crumbs
1/4 c  Butter, melted
1 tb Onion, chopped
2 tb Hot water
Salt and pepper
8 sl Bacon
Mix crumbs, butter, onion and water; season with salt and pepper to
taste. Cut fish in halves, put a tablespoon or so of stuffing on each
piece, top with a slice of bacon. Put in a baking pan, add a little
water and the other bacon. Bake in a 350F. oven, basting occasionally.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
