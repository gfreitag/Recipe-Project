Title: Amerigo's Pesto
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A
3 lg Bn fresh basil
3 tb Parsley
1/4 c  Pine nuts
3 tb Parmesan
1 tb Pecorino
1/4 c  Sun-dried tomatoes -- opt
10 tb Olive oil
In a food processor place basil, parsley, pine nuts, parmigiano,
pecorino and tomatoes (if using) and blend. Add about olive oil to
make into a creamy emulsion. This MAY be sauteed to heat through, or
used directly on hot pasta and mixed in. Source: Tony Cortese,
Amerigo's Restaurant, NYC.
