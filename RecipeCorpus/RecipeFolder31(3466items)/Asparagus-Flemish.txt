Title: Asparagus Flemish
Categories: Stove top, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Asparagus spears; blanched
2 ea Eggs; hard-boiled
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
1/2 c  Butter; melted
Salt & pepper to taste
Peel and press cooled hard-boiled eggs through a sieve. Stir eggs and
lemon juice into melted butter. Season with a little salt and pepper
to taste. Drizzle it over either warm or cold blanced asparagus
spears.
