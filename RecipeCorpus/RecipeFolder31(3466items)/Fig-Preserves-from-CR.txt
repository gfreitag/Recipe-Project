Title: Fig Preserves from CR
Categories: Fruit, Can/preserv
Yield: 3 pints
2 qt Firm figs
4 lb Sugar
1 1/2 c  Water
2 ea Lemons; sliced
1 oz Stick cinnamon
24 ea Cloves
Wash fruit carefully in colander. Let sugar and water come to a
boil. Add lemons and figs; and spices tied in a bag. Boil slowly
until figs are tender and clear; about one hour. Lift fruit carefully
into sterilized jars and cover with juice. Seal while hot.

Mrs. John Welch (Julie Pringle) From _Charleston Receipts_ orig 1950;
1993 edition  ISBN 0-9607854-2-6 Typos by Jeff Pruett
