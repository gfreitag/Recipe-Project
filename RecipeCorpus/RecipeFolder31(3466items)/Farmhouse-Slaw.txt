Title: Farmhouse Slaw
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
4 c  Shredded cabbage
3/4 c  Seedless raisins
1    Apple, chopped
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Salad oil
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Combine cabbage, raisins, apple, celery and onion.  Blend together
remaining ingredients, add to cabbage mixture and toss.  Chill for at
least 30 minutes. Randy Rigg
