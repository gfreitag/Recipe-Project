Title: Marinated Tuna Fish or Swordfish
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 kg Fresh tuna sliced
100 ml Lemon juice
50    Vinegar
70 g  Fresh oregano
100 ml Olive oil
Salt and pepper
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Spread in a plate, slices of tuna. Marinate with lemon, vinegar salt
and pepper for 1 hour. Take out the tuna and put on plate. Put
oregano and olive oil on the top. Serve. Source: Given to share with
the express permission of Chef de Cuisine
