Title: Cranberry Raspberry Mold
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 pk Raspberry Jello
1 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Cold water
1 cn Cranberry-Raspberry sauce
Dissolve Jello in boiling water, add cold water and mix well.  Chill
until slightly thickened.  Add the sauce.  Spoon into mold and chill
until firm. Randy Rigg
