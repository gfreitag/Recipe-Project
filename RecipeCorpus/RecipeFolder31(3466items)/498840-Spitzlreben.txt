Title: 498840 Spitzlreben
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 60 cookies
1 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Plus 3 tbsp. soft
-shortening
2 c  Ground almonds
1 ts Vanilla extract
Mix in 3 1/4 cups flour.  Knead dough very well.  Roll to 1/8 to 1/4
inch thickness.  Cut with cutters.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until light brown. Spread half
of cookies with raspberry jam, top with matching cookies. Dip in
granulated sugar. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   06-17-95
