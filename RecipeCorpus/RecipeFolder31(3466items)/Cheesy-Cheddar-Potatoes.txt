Title: Cheesy Cheddar Potatoes
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
24 oz Frozen hash browns with
-peppers and onions
8 oz Cheddar cheese; shredded
3/4 c  Miracle Whip
1/2 c  Parmesan; grated
Heat oven to 375.  Mix all ingredients, breaking up potatoes with
spoon if necessary.  Spoon into 2 quart casserole. Bake 45 minutes or
until golden brown. Variation: Omit parmesan cheese. Stir in 2 cups
chopped cooked ham. Source: Kraft
