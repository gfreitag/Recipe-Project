Title: Candlestick Salad^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
4    Pineapple slices
Lettuce leaves
2    Bananas
1/4 c  To 1/2 whipped cream
2    Cherries
Mayonnaise
Place pineapple slices on lettuce leaves. In the hollow center, place
1/2 banana so that it stands upright. Put a spoonful of whipped cream
on top and on that a cherry - ripe or preserved. Serve with sweetened
mayonnaise.
