Title: Fancy Style Peas^
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome, Depress/era
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Butter
1 sl Bacon
2 c  Peas, fresh shelled
8    Tiny onions
1/4 c  Cream
Slat & pepper
Cook butter and bacon for 5 minutes. Remove bacon, add peas and some
water; boil about 15 minutes. Also boil onions in a little salted
water. Crumble bacon. Drain peas and onions.Combine peas, onions,
bacon, cream and seasonings. Heat through and serve.
