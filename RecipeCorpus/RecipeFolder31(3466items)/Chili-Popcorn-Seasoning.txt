Title: Chili Popcorn Seasoning
Categories: Snacks, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Ground cumin
1 tb Dried onion flakes
Cayenne pepper to taste
1/2 c  Corn, popped
Butter to taste
Combine the salt, chili powder, garlic powder, cumin, onion flakes and
cayenne and mix well.  Use one or two teaspoons per 1/2 cup corn,
popped with butter.
