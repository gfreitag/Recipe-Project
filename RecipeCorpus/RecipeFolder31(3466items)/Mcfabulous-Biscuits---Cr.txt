Title: Mcfabulous Biscuits   Cr
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  7-Up or Ginger Ale
1 c  Milk
1 tb Oil
1    Egg
4 c  Self-rising flour
Mix these together into a smooth dough, kneed in bowl 5 mins. Pat out
1.5" thick between 2 sheets greased waxed paper, cut with 2.5" glass,
place close together in greased baking pan. bake 425 18 mins. or till
browned.

Makes about 20
