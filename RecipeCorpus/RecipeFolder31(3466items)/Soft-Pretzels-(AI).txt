Title: Soft Pretzels (AI)
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 servings
2 pk Dry yeast
1 1/2 c  Warm water
1/8 ts Salt
4 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Cold Water
1/4 c  Baking Soda
Mix first four ingredients - let rise for 15 minutes. Then shape into
pretzels, lay on a greased cookie sheet, let rise. Mix cold water and
baking soda, brush mixture over pretzels. Sprinkle with coarse salt,
bake at 450 degrees 15-20 minutes. SOURCE:*A. Fairfield, Iroquois
