Title: "Jammy Muffins"
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 12 muffins
2 tb AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil
1/4 c  Raw honey
2 c  Whole Grain Bran Muffin Mix
-- (Arrowhead Mills)
1    Egg beaten or egg replacer
1 c  Water
Prepare Whole Grain Bran Muffin Mix according to package directions.
Before baking, spoon 1 teaspoon apple butter (or favorite flavor)
into center of the unbaked muffin.  Bake at 350 degrees F. for 25-30
minutes.
