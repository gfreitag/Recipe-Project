Title: Salad Bouquets^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Green pepper
Boiling water
Ice water
Romaine, center leaves
1    Head blanched endive
1 sm Head Boston lettuce
1/2 bn Watercress
French dressing
1 tb Chives, chopped
Slice off stem of pepper; remove seeds. Pour boiling water over
pepper; let stand 3 minutes; plunge into ice water. Cut into 1/4"
rings with sharp knife. Put an endive leaf on top of 2 romaine
leaves; next put on a curly leaf of lettuce, then a spray of
watercress. Hold firmly and slip a ring of pepper over stems, making
a bouquet. Put each portion on a salad plate; pour over dressing;
sprinkle with chives. Pretty fancy!

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
