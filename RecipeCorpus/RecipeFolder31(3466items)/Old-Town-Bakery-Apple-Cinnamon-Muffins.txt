Title: Old Town Bakery Apple-Cinnamon Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Fruit, Breads
Yield: 12 servings
3/4 c  Unsalted butter -- softened
1 2/3 c  Sugar
2 lg Eggs
2 ts Vanilla extract
2 1/4 c  Flour
1 tb Ground cinnamon
1 3/4 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Granny Smith apples; peeled
-chopped, packed
MMMMM-----------------------CINNAMON-SUGAR----------------------------
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Ground cinnamon
In mixing bowl cream butter with sugar until fluffy. Add eggs, 1 at a
time, beating until well combined. Add vanilla. Toss together flour,
cinnamon, baking powder and salt in another bowl. Then add,
alternately over low speed, with milk to butter-egg mixture, stirring
only to blend.

Fold in apples carefully.

Fill 12 paper-cup lined muffin cups at least 2/3 full with batter.

Sprinkle each muffin with 1 teaspoon Cinnamon-Sugar. Bake at 350
degrees 25 to 30 minutes or until tops spring back when lightly
touched. Serve warm. Makes 12 large muffins.

CINNAMON-SUGAR - In bowl combine cinnamon and sugar. Toss to
incorporate well. Makes 1/2 cup.

Each muffin contains about: 354 calories; 286 mg sodium; 69 mg
cholesterol; 13 grams fat; 56 grams carbohydrates; 4 grams protein;
0.12 gram fiber.

Source: Old Town Bakery, Pasadena, CA.
