Title: Amerigo's Alfredo Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A
1/4 lb Salted butter
1 qt Heavy cream
1/4 c  Parmigiano -- grate
Fresh ground pepper
In a saucepan melt butter, add cream, and parmigiano. Stir
continuously till smooth and mixture comes almost to the boil. Serve
with pepper and more parmigiani. Source: Tony Cortese, Amerigo's
Restaurant, NYC.
