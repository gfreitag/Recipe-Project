Title: Lowfat Marinara Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 7 servings
1 tb Olive oil
3/4 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Dried rosemary -- crushed
4    Cloves garlic -- crushed
1/2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
14 1/2 oz Tomatoes, canned
-undrained, chopped
1/4 c  Fresh basil -- finely
-chopped
Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat.  Add onion, rosemary,
and garlic; saut√© 3 minutes.  Add sugar and next 3 ingredients; cook,
uncovered, over medium heat 10 minutes, stirring frequently.  Remove
from heat; stir in basil.  Serve with breadsticks.
