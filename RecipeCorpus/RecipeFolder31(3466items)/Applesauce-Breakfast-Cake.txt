Title: Applesauce Breakfast Cake
Categories: Breakfast, Breads, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 recipe
1 c  Unsweetened applesauce
-OR- crushed pineapple
-- (unsweetened)
1/4 c  Molasses
1/4 c  Honey
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Almond extract
1 1/2 c  AM Griddle Lite Pancake Mix
2 ts AM Canola Oil
Mix liquids thoroughly.  Add the dry mix.  Stir gently.  Pour into a
well-oiled square cake pan.  Bake at 350 F. for 20 minutes or until
done. Serve warm with butter and syrup or with a dollop of flavored
yogurt.
