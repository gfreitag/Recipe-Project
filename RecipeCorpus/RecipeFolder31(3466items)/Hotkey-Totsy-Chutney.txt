Title: Hotkey Totsy Chutney
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 cup
1 tb Ginger root, grated
1 ts Hot green chiles, minced
1 ts Heaping sugar
3 tb Cashews
Juice of 2-3 lemons
1 ts Powdered caraway seeds
Salt to taste
2    Bunches cilantro leaves
Place all ingredients (smallest amount suggested) except cilantro in
food processor or blender. Blend until smooth. Slowly add handfuls of
cilantro and continue blending until chutney is smooth. Add small
amounts of any ingredient until desired flavor is achieved. Let sit 2
hours or overnight for flavor enhancement. *From "Quick Bytes:
Computer Lover's Cookbook" by Diane Pfeifer* ~Typed for you by
