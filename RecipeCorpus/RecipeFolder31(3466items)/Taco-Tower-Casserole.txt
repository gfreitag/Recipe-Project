Title: Taco Tower Casserole
Categories: Tex/mexican, Low cal/fat, Meats, Casseroles
Yield: 6 to 8 svgs
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 md Onion, minced
1 pk (1.25-oz.) Taco seasoning
-mix
1 c  Rosarita Traditional Salsa
-mild
1/2 c  Water
1 cn (16-oz.) Rosarita No Fat
-Traditional Refried Beans
2 c  Crushed, unsalted tortilla
-chips
2 c  Grated Cheddar and Monterey
-Jack cheese, mixed (or low
-fat)
x  Lettuce (optional)
x  Tomatoes (optional)
x  Lite sour cream (optional)
x  Chopped olives (optional)
In a large skillet brown hamburger and onions; drain.  Add taco mix,
salsa and water; mix well.  Simmer for 10 minutes, add refried beans
and set aside.  In an ungreased 12- x 9- x 2-inch baking dish place a
layer of 1/2 the tortilla chips, 1/2 meat and bean mixture, and 1/2
the mixed cheeses; repeat layers with remaining ingredients.  Bake,
covered, at 350 degrees F. for 25 minutes.  If desired, serve on a
bed of lettuce and top with tomatoes, sour cream and chopped olives.
Serve with additional salsa.

Source:  Rosarita No Fat Traditional Refried Beans can label.
