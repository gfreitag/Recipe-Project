Title: Arby's Horsey Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Mayo
3 tb Bottled horseraddish cream
-sauce
1 tb Sugar -- or
2 pk Equal
Mix all well. Keep refrigerated, tightly covered to use in 2 weeks.
Do not freeze. Source: Gloria Pitzer.
