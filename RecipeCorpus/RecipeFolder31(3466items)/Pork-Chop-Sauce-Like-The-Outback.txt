Title: Pork Chop Sauce Like The Outback
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Sweet orange marmalade
1/2 ts Dry mustard
Mix all ingredients together. Heat and serve with pork chops or other
meats. Source: Gloria Pitzer.
