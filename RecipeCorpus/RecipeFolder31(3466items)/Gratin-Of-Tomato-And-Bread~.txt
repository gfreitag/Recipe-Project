Title: Gratin Of Tomato And Bread~
Categories: Tomatoes, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/4 lb Cherry tomatoes -- 3+ 1/2
Cups
3 oz Day old bread *
6    Cloves garlic -- peeled,
Sliced
1/2 c  Parsley -- coarsely chop
1/2 ts Black pepper -- freshly
Ground
2 tb Virgin olive oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
* Preferably from a French baguette.

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.  Wash the tomatoes and remove and
discard any stems.  Place the tomatoes in a bowl and mix in the
remainder of the ingredients.  Transfer the mixture to a 6-cup gratin
dish.  Bake at 375 degress for 40 minutes.  Serve immediately.
Nutritional analysis per serving: calories 185; protein 6 gm;
carbohydrates 21 gm; fat 9.4 gm; saturated fat 2.1 gm; cholesterol 5
mg; sodium 506 mg.
