Title: LAMB SHISH KEBAB
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill, Usenet
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
1 ts Garlic, finely
-chopped
2 ts Ginger (fresh),
-finely chopped
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Peanut oil or
-vegetable oil
1/2 ts Turmeric, ground
1/2 ts Coriander, ground
1/2 ts Cumin, ground
1/8 ts Cayenne (or
-to taste)
1 tb Onion, grated
MMMMM---------------------------KEBABS--------------------------------
24    Lamb (lean), cubed
-(approx 2 lbs)
2 md Onions, cut into
-large chunks
2    Green peppers, cut
-into large chunks
2    Tomatoes, cut into
-large chunks
Mix all marinade ingredients in a large bowl.  Add lamb cubes and
marinate for 1 hour. Preheat broiler or light the barbecue.

Divide the meat and vegetables evenly into 4 parts, and skewer the
pieces on 4 skewers. Place 4 to 5 inches from flame. Grill, turning
to cook evenly, for approximately 10 minutes. Serve with rice and
cucumber-yogurt sauce.

NOTES:

*  This is a fairly spicy lamb shish kebab -- Serve it with cucumber
yogurt sauce.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  5 minutes preparation, 1 hour marinating, 10 minutes cooking.
: Precision:  approximate measurement OK.
