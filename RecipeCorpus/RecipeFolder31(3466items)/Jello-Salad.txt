Title: Jello Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
2 pk Orange Jello
Fruit (your choice)
1 c  Whipping cream
1 c  Sour cream
Salted peanuts, crushed
Prepare Jello according to package directions and let it begin to
congeal, add the fruit.  Place in the refrigerator and let set.  Whip
the cream and mix in sour cream.  Spread on top of the Jello.
Sprinkle the peanuts on top.  (I like to use strawberries, kiwi,
bananas etc.) Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   05-17-95
