Title: Pizza Dough
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Pizza
Yield: 15 servings
1 13/16 kg Flour
30 g  Fresh yeast
25 g  Salt
60 g  Sugar
120 g  Parmesan cheese
600 ml Water
30 ml Olive oil
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Dissolve yeast in a little of the warm water. Combine all ingredients
in a mixer bowl. Mix and knead. Proof for 1-2 hours. Source: Given to
share with the express permission of Chef de Cuisine
