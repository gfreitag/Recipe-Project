Title: Danish Yellow Pea Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Split yellow peas
2 qt Chicken stock
1    Meaty smoked ham bone
1 md Onion, chopped
2    Carrots, chopped
2    Ribs
1 md Potato, peeled & finely
-cubed
1    Bay leaf
-dashes
1    Clove garlic, pressed or
-minced
-celery, chopped
-thyme, marjoram, savory
handful of frozen green peas 1 peeled & chopped tomato
Optional:

Rinse and sort peas.  To stock pot, add peas, stock and ham bone;
simmer 1 hour.  Add the veggies and seasonings and cook for another 1
to 1-1/2 hours.  If using optional veggies, add during last 1/2 hour
of cooking. When done, remove bay leaf and serve.  Makes a nice main
course with a green salad & crusty loaf or dill bread.
