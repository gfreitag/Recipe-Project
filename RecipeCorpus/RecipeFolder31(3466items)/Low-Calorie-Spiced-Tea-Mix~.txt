Title: Low-Calorie Spiced Tea Mix~
Categories: Diabetic, Beverages
Yield: 72 servings
1/4 c  + 2 T orange flavored
-drink mix, sugar-free
1/4 c  Lemondade flavored drink
Mix; sugar-free
3/4 c  Iced tea mix; sugar free
1 ts Ground cloves
2 tb Ground cinnamon
1. Combine all ingredients, stirring until blended. Store mixture in
an airtight container. 2. For each serving, place 1 teaspoon mix in a
cup. Add 1 cup hot water, stirring well. Serve hot. Yield: 72 sweet
ones

Food Exchange Per Serving: FREE; CAL: 1; CAR: TR; PRO: TR; FAT: TR;
SOD: 1 MG;
