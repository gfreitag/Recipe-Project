Title: Mama's Tomato and Apple Jam
Categories: Can/preserv
Yield: 2 pints
1    Lemon, chopped
1 pt Tomatoes, fresh or canned
2 c  Diced apples
3 c  Sugar
Cook lemon until tender in a small amount of water. Add remaining
ingredients and boil mixture until thick and clear. Cool slightly,
pour into sterile jars and seal. Makes about 2 pints.
