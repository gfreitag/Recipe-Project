Title: Green Beans in Garlic Butter
Categories: Vegetables, Indian, Side dish, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Unsalted butter
1/2 ts Cumin seeds
3 cl To 4 cloves garlic; peeled
-and crushed or finely
-chopped
1/4 ts To 1/2 tsp chili powder
3 c  Whole green beans
1/2 ts Salt or to taste
Prep- 5 to 10 minutes.  Cooking- 15 minutes.

Melt the butter over low heat and fry the cumin seeds for 30 seconds.

Add the garlic and fry for 1 minute.

Add the chili powder and immediately follow with the beans.  Stir and
fry for 1 to 2 minutes.

Add the salt and mix thoroughly. Cover the pan and simmer the beans in
their own juice until they are tender (10 to 12 minutes), stirring
occasionally.

Remove from the heat.

Serving suggestions: serve with Chicken or Fish.
