Title: Oatmeal Carrot Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 48 cookies
3/4 c  Butter; or margerine
3/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Cloves
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
1 3/4 c  All purpose flour
2 c  Rolled oats
1 c  Finely shredded carrots
1/2 c  Raisins; optional
1 c  Sifted powdered sugar
1/4 ts Vanilla
1 tb Milk; to 2T
Beat butter or margerine in a mixing bowl with an electric mixer on
med to high speed 30 seconds.  Add brown sugar, granulated sugar,
baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, and cloves; beat till combined.
Beat in egg and 1t vanilla till combined.  Beat in as much flour as
you can with the mixer. Stir in any remaining flour and the oats and
the carrots.  Stir in raisins, if desired.  Drop dough by rounded
teaspoons 2 inches apart onto an ungreased cookie sheet.

Bake in 375 oven for 10-12 mins or until edges are golden.  Remove
cookies and cool completely on a wire rack.

For icing, combine powdered sugar, 1/4t vanilla and enough milk to
make of drizzling consistency.  Drizzle icing over cookies.
