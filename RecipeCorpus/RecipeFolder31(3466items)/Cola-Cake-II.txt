Title: Cola Cake II
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 cake
MMMMM----------------------------CAKE---------------------------------
1/2 lb Butter, melted
2 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Buttermilk
2    Eggs
1 c  Classic Coke
1 1/2 c  Miniature marshmallows
MMMMM---------------------------ICING--------------------------------
1/2 c  Margarine
3 tb Cocoa
1/2 c  Cola
4 c  Powdered sugar, sifted
1 c  Chopped pecans (optional)
Cake Directions.

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Butter and flour a 9 x 13 x 2 inch pan.

Combine flour and sugar mixture.  Combine melted butter, cocoa and
Coke. Add to flour and sugar mixture.  Stir well.  Add butter, eggs,
baking soda and vanilla.  Fold in miniature marshmallows.

Pour into prepared pan.

Bake 30 to 35 minutes.  Frost while still warm.

Frosting Directions

Combine softened margarine and cocoa using mixer.  Add cola and
powdered sugar alternately.  Spread on cake, sprinkle with pecans
