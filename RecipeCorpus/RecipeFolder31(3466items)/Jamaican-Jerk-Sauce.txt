Title: Jamaican Jerk Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
4    Habanero peppers -- whole
2 bn Green onions -- chopped
1/2 c  Allspice
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Thyme
1 ts Cinnamon -- ground
1/2 ts Nutmeg
2 tb Soy sauce
8    Garlic cloves
Combine all ingredients in a food processor and blend until smooth.
Store in a clean, tightly covered jar in the refrigerator.  Will last
basically forever.
