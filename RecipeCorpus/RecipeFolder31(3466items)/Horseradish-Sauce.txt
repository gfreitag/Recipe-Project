Title: Horseradish Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/2 tb Butter
3 tb Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk -- heated to boiling
1/4 c  Horseradish
2 tb Dijon mustard
1/2 ts White wine vinegar
2 lg Egg yolks
1/3 c  Heavy cream
Salt
Ground pepper
Melt butter in saucepan; whisk in flour and cook, stirring, 1 minute;
pour in milk, bring to a boil and cook, stirring, 2 minutes; add
horseradish, mustard and vinegar; whisk in egg yolks and cook over
medium-low heat until thickened; DO NOT BOIL; pour in cream; season
to taste. Yield: 2 cups. FROM:    CHUCK OZBURN (HBWK07A)
