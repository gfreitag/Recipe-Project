Title: Upside-Down Orange Biscuits
Categories: Breads, Breakfast
Yield: 6 servings
1    AM Multigrain Biscuit Mix
-- (one recipe)
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Maple syrup
3 tb Orange juice
1 ts Grated rind
Combine last four ingredients; mix well.  Pour about 1 1/2 tablespoons
orange mixture into each well oiled cup of a 6-cup muffin pan.  Make
one recipe biscuits according to instructions on bag.  Place one
biscuit in each muffin cup.  Bake at 400 degrees F. about 10 minutes.
Invert pan on plate.  Serve warm.
