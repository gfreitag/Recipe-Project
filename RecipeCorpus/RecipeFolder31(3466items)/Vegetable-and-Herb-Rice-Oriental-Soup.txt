Title: Vegetable and Herb Rice Oriental Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 recipe
1    Box Quick Brown Rice
-- (Vegetable and Herb)
5 c  Water
4 tb Tamari soy sauce
1 tb Olive oil
1 ts Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  Mung bean sprouts
Cook contents of box according to package directions.  Add the soy
sauce, oil, and lemon juice.  Stir and let simmer 5 minutes more.
Add the sprouts and just heat through.  More tamari soy sauce may be
added to taste.
