Title: Black Forest Mousse
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 10 servings
1 oz Unsweetened chocolate; melt
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
1 c  Cold water
1 pk Chocolate instant pudding;
-4 serving size
3/4 ts Almond extract
2 c  Heavy cream; whipped
21 oz Cherry pie filling; chilled
In large bowl, beat chocolate with sweetened condensed milk. Beat in
water then pudding mix and 1/2 tsp extract. Freeze 5 minutes. Fold in
whipped cream. Spoon equal portions into 10 dessert dishes. Stir
remaining 1/4 tsp extract into cherry pie filling; spoon over
desserts. Source: Comstock ad Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   06-02-95
