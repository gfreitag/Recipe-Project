Title: New Cut Plantation String Beans
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Stringless beans
6 ea Green onions; chopped
Salt and pepper
4 sl Bacon; minced
Fry bacon crisp in heavy pan, then remove bacon.  Cook onions in
fat. When onions are slightly wilted, add beans, stir well, cook one
minute. Add one tablespoon water, cover, cook three minutes, then
uncover. Beans should be bright green. Stir over burner until done to
taste- about two to three minutes. Season to taste with salt and
pepper, and add crisp bacon just before serving.
