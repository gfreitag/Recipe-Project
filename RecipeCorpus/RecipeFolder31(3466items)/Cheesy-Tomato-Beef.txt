Title: Cheesy Tomato Beef
Categories: Pasta, Main dish, Meats, Cheese/eggs, Tomatoes
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Ground beef
14 1/2 oz Stewed tomatoes
1    Onion; chopped
1/2 ts Thyme
16 oz Potatoes;(canned), drained
-and sliced
1 c  Swiss cheese; grated
In skillet, brown meat; drain.  Drain tomatoes; reserving juice. Add
tomato liquid to skillet.  Add onion and thyme. Cook over high heat 5
to 8 minutes or until slightly thickened, stirring often.  Stir in
tomatoes and potatoes; heat through.  Top with cheese; heat until
melted. Source: Del Monte can
