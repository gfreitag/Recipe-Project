Title: Hawaiian Luau Beans
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Pork and beans
3/4 c  Diced ham
1/2 c  Pineapple chunks
1 tb Pineapple syrup
2 tb Brown sugar
ds Ground cloves
Mix all ingredients in a 1 quart casserole.  Bake at 375 degrees for
30 minutes or until all ingredients are hot. Randy Rigg
