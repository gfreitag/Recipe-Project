Title: Cilantro-Lime Salsa
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Fresh corn -- removed from
Cob
1 tb Olive oil
1/2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1    Red bell pepper -- diced
1    Poblano chile -- diced
2    Tomatoes -- diced
Juice of 6 limes
3    Garlic cloves -- minced
1/2 c  Parsley -- chopped
1 c  Cilantro -- chopped
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Cayenne
In a bowl, stir together all ingredients.
