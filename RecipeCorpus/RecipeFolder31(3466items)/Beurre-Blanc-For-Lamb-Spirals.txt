Title: Beurre Blanc For Lamb Spirals
Categories: Salsa/sauce, French
Yield: 4 servings
2    Shallots -- chopped
1 tb Cream
1/4 lb Butter
1 c  White wine
1 pn Salt -- to taste
1 pn White pepper -- to taste
1 pn Cayenne pepper -- to taste
Sweat shallots in butter, add white wine, a pinch each of salt, white
pepper, and cayenne pepper. Reduce. Add cream. Reduce. Whisk in
butter over medium heat. Strain and keep warm. Source: Bill Hufferd
of DC3 Restaurant, Santa Monica, CA
