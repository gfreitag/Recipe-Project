Title: Dishwater Punch
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 52 servings
1 cn Pineapple juice
1/2 qt Apple juice
1 ea Pk Grape Koolaid
2 ea Pk Berry punch Koolaid
1 ea Pk Tropical Punch Koolaid
1 1/2 ea Pk Strawberry Koolaid
11 qt Water
1 ea Red food color
Make sure all koolaid is pre-sweetened (or add appropriate sugar).
Mix all dry together. Add water and juices. Stir well. Add food color
until it looks drinkable. Without food color, it should look like
it's name.
