Title: Island Swizzle
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1    Tub Crystal Light Tropical
Passions Kiwi, passionfruit
Pineapple or strawberry
Orange banana flavor low
Calorie soft drink mix
1 1/2 c  Cold water
1/2 c  Chilled orange juice
1 tb Lime juice
4 c  Crushed ice
Place drink mix, water, orange juice, and lime juice in blender
container, cover.  Blend on high speed until drink mix is dissolved.
Add ice; blend until smooth.

15 Calories per 1 cup serving
