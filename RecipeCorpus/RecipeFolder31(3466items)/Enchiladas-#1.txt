Title: Enchiladas #1
Categories: Main dish, Tex/mexican
Yield: 8 servings
1 lb Ground Beef
8 oz Tomato Sauce
1 pk Enchilada Sauce Mix
1 1/2 c  Water
8 oz Cheddar Cheese, grated
8    Flour Tortillas
Lettuce, shredded
4 oz Sour Cream
In a 2-quart saucepan, combine enchilada sauce mix with tomato sauce
and water.  Bring to a goil, reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes.  Set
aside.

Brown ground beef in a large skillet.  Drain fat.  Add * cup of the
above sauce to meat and mix well.  Add 4 ounces of cheese to meat
mixture.

Coat each tortilla with sauce.  Spread * cup of sauce in the bottom
of 12 x 7* x 2" baking dish.  Spoon 3 tbs. meat mixture down center
of each tortilla.  Fold over tortilla sides to encase meat and place
seam-side down in baking dish.

Spoon remaining sauce over enchiladas.  Top with * cup Cheddar
Cheese. Bake in preheated 350* oven for 15 minutes. Serve hot.

Top with more enchilada sauce, lettuce, and sour cream if desired.
