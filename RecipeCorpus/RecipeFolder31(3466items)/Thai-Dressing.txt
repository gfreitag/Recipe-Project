Title: Thai Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads, Condiments, Thai
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Red chilli shreds
1/2 c  Yellow chilli shreds
1/4 c  Crushed garlic
2 tb Pickled garlic, crushed with
-juice
1 tb Chopped coriander root
1/4 c  Fish sauce
1/2 c  Lime juice
2 tb Palm sugar
Combine all ingredients, stirring well.  Can be stored indefinitely in
a refigerator if kept in a sealed jar.  Can be used as a dipping
sauce and in a variety of Thai dishes.
