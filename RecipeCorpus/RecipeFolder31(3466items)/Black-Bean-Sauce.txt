Title: Black Bean Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chinese
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Chicken broth
3 tb Fermented black beans
1 ts Dried chili flakes
1 ts Sugar
2 tb Dry sherry
1 ts Ginger -- finely grated
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
Water and cornstarch
-mixture
Rinse black beans in cold water to remove salt. Crush coarsely with
back of knife. Combine beans with all other ingredients in a small
saucepan and heat to boiling. Thicken with mixture of cornstarch and
water until correct consistency. Sauce should be thick enough to
easily coat back of spoon.
