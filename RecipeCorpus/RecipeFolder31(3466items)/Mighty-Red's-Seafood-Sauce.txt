Title: Mighty Red's Seafood Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 32 servings
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/2 c  Ketchup
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Prepared horseradish
8 dr Tabasco sauce
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 ds Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Fresh ground black pepper
Blend the chili sauce, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, horseradish,
Tabasco sauce, and dry ingredients. Chill before serving. Makes about
2 cups, or 32 one-tablespoon servings.
