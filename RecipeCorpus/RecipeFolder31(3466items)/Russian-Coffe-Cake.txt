Title: Russian Coffe Cake
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Jewish, Russian
Yield: 1 cake
1/2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs, well beaten
3/4 c  Honey
2 tb Oil
1/2 c  Hot dark tea
2 tb Whiskey
2 c  Sifted flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
3/8 c  Chopped nuts
Grated rind of one lemon
Mix all ingredients together and beat well.  Pour into greased loaf
pan which has been lined with waxed paper on the bottom.  Bake at 325
degrees for 45 minutes to an hour.
