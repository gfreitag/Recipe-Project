Title: Biba's Uncooked Tomato Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 lb Tomatoes; peel, seed --
-chop
12    Basil leaves
2    Anchovies -- chop
2 tb Capers
10    Black olives -- slice
Salt and pepper
1/4 c  Ex-virgin olive oil
3 oz Mozzarella -- shred or
-grate
1/4 c  Pecorino romano -- grate
1 lb Pasta -- cook
Mix all sauce ingredients. Add cheese to hot pasta and serve with
sauce that is at room temperature. Source: Biba's Trattoria Cookbook.
