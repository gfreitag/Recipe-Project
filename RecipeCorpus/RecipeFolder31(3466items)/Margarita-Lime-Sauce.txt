Title: Margarita Lime Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Tequila
1/2 c  Sweet and sour bar mix
2 tb Triple sec
1 1/2 ts Cornstarch
1 tb Cilantro -- finely chopped
Combine tequila, sweet and sour mix, triple sec and cornstarch in
saucepan, stirring until corstarch is wll blended.  Heat until
slightly syrupy and translucent, stirring constantly.  Just before
serving add cilantro.
