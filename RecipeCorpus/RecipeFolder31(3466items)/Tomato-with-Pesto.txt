Title: Tomato with Pesto
Categories: Vegetables, Salads, Tomatoes
Yield: 4 servings
1    Garlic clove
2 tb Pine nuts
2/3 c  Basil leaves, packed
3 tb Parmesan cheese
Salt & pepper to taste
3 lg Tomatoes sliced 1/2"
-thick
1/3 c  Olive oil
In a blender or food processor, finely chop garlic, pine nuts, basil,
olive oil, cheese, salt and pepper. Drizzle tomato slices with olive
oil and dollop each slice with 2 tsp of pesto, and serve remaining
pesto on side.

Tomato Pesto Pasta Salad

Prepare pesto as above. Toss with 2 diced tomatoes, 4 thinly sliced
scallions, 20 sliced pitted black olives and 12 oz cooked macaroni.
