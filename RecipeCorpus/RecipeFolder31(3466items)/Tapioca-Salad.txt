Title: Tapioca Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 pk Orange Jello
1 pk Vanilla tapioca pudding
1 pk Vanilla pudding
3 c  Hot water
1 sm Carton of Cool Whip
1 cn Mandarin oranges, drained
1 cn Pineapple tidbits, drained
Combine Jello and puddings in a saucepan, add hot water and cook until
thickened.  Remove from heat, cool slightly and add Cool Whip.  Fold
in oranges, pineapple.  Chill and serve. Randy Rigg
