Title: Sunny Lemon Cheesecake Squares
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 16 servings
1 pk Lemon cake mix
1/2 c  Margarine; softened
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
11 oz Mandarin oranges; well drain
2    Eggs
2/3 c  Coconut
Heat oven to 325.  Lightly grease 9" square baking pan. Reserve 1 cup
of dry cake mix.  In large bowl, combine remaining cake mix and
margarine at low speed until crumbly. Reserve 1 cup of crumb mixture.
Press remaining crumb mixture in bottom of greased pan.  In same
bowl, combine reserved 1 cup cake mix, cream cheese, orange segments
and eggs at medium speed until well blended.  Stir in coconut.
Spread over base. Sprinkle with reserved 1 cup crumb mixture.  Bake
at 325 for 30 to 40 minutes. Cool completely. Refrigerate until
serving time.  Source:  Pillsbury box Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On
06-08-95
