Title: Smoked Salmon Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Fish, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  (4 oz) Smoked Salmon,
-shredded
1/3 c  Heavy cream
1/8 ts Freshly grated pepper
1/2 ts Capers
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend on high speed until
smooth. Grind extra pepper over top of dip.  Serve with raw
vegetables and crackers.
