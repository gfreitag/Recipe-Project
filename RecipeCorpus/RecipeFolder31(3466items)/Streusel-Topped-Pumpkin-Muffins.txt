Title: Streusel Topped Pumpkin Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Vegetables
Yield: 12 muffins
2 c  Bisquick
1/3 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Ginger
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 c  Pumpkin
1    Egg
MMMMM----------------------STREUSEL TOPPING---------------------------
1/3 c  Bisquick
3 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Nuts; chopped
1 ts Cinnamon
1 tb Margarine
Heat oven to 400.  Grease bottoms of 12 muffin cups. Mix bisquick,
raisins, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and ginger in medium bowl. Beat
milk, oil, pumpkin and egg until smooth. Stir into raisin mixture;
beat vigorously 30 strokes. Fill muffin cups about 3/4 full. Sprinkle
topping over batter in each cup. Bake about 15 to 20 minutes.
Topping: combine dry ingredients and then cut in margarine until
crumbly. Source: Bisquick ad Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   06-08-95
