Title: Strawberry, Peach Cantaloupe Cooler
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 1 beverage
1 c  Strawberries; sliced
1 c  Peaches; chopped
1 c  Cantaloupe balls; pre-frozen
2 tb Sugar;
1 qt Apple juice;
1 qt Sparkling water; chilled
Mint sprigs
Ice
Combine strawberries and peaches in small bowl.  Sprinkle with sugar;
stir gently.   Let stand at room temperature for 30 minutes.  Put
fruit (except cantaloupe) into punch bowl.  Gradually stir in apple
juice and sparking water.  Top with cantaloupe balls, ice and mint
sprigs. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Food Section, May 25,
