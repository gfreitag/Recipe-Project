Title: Cornflakes Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Margarine -- softened
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  White sugar
1    Egg
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Cream of tartar
1 ts Baking soda -- dissolved in
1 tb Buttermilk
1 c  Cooking oil
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Crushed cornflakes
1 c  Oatmeal
Mix every thing in order given. Chill dough and drop by teaspoon on
greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350~ until slightly tan, about 15
minutes.
