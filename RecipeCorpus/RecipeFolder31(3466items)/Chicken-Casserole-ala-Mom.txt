Title: Chicken Casserole ala Mom
Categories: Poultry, Casseroles, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 cn Cream Of Mushroom Soup
3 ea Beaten Eggs
2 1/2 c  Pasta Of Choice, Cooked
2 c  Cooked & Cubed Chicken
1 c  Seasoned Bread Crumbs
1 c  Shreaded Sharp Cheddar
Cheese
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 pk 16.oz Mixed Vegetables
Pre-Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Blend the milk with the soup in a large mixing bowl and stil in the
beated eggs. Add the remaining ingredients, except the Parmesan
cheese and mix well. Pour mixture into a deep 2 quart baking dish.
Sprinkle the top with the Parmesan cheese. Bake uncovered for 35-40
minutes or until a knife inserted into the top comes out clean.

Serve with garlic bread and white wine.

Cooks Notes: Instead of chicken, you can use pork, ham or just plain
hamburger.

Adapted by Papa Rock from a recipe by Eunice Newman...his
