Title: Poetry Biscuits^
Categories: Breads, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1    Text only
Two cups Indian, one cup wheat, One cup sour milk, one cup sweet, One
cup good eggs that you can eat. One half cup molasses, too, One half
cup sugar and thereto, Salt and soda, each a spoon, Mix it up quickly
and bake it soon.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
