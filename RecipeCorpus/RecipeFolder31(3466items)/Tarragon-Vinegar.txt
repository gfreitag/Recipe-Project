Title: Tarragon Vinegar
Categories: Herbs/spice, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  White vinegar
3    Inch fresh tarragon sprig
1    Clove garlic, halved
(optional)
A sweet fragrant infusion, use this in place of regular vinegar if you
like.

Place ingredients in a small saucepan and heat, but do not boil.
Remove garlic clove if using. Place sprig of tarragon and vinegar in
a sterile jar or the bottle in which the vinegar came in. Store on
pantry shelf.

Yields about 2 cups

From the book "Tofu, Tempeh & Other Soy Delights" by Camille Cusumano
