Title: Benihana Magic Mustard Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Toasted sesame seeds --
Crushed
3 tb Dry mustard
3/4 c  Soy sauce
3 tb Heavy cream -- whipped
2 tb Hot water
1/4    Clove garlic -- crushed
Monosodium glutamate (MSG)
Place sesame seeds in a flat pan in oven to toast. Mix mustard and
water in small bowl until a paste is formed. Put paste, sesame seeds,
soy sauce, garlic and a pinch of monosodium glutamate. Blend at high
speed for about 2 mins. Remove and stir in whipped cream. Generally
served with chicken, but good with steak too. Source: Benihana
