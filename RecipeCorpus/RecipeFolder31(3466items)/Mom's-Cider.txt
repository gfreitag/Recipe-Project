Title: Mom's Cider
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
2 qt Apple cider
1 ts Whole allspice
7    Cloves
1    Stick of cinnamon
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  White sugar
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/3 c  Lemon juice
Simmer cider with spices and sugar for about 30 minutes.  Add juices
and strain.  Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator and
reheat when ready to serve. Randy Rigg
