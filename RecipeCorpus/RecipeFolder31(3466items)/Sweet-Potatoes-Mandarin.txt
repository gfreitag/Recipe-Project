Title: Sweet Potatoes Mandarin
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
2 cn (15 oz) sweet potatoes with
Pineapple
11 oz Mandarin oranges, drain
1/2 ts Ginger
1/3 c  Pecan halves
In medium saucepan, combine sweet potatoes with mandarin oranges and
ginger.  Heat through.  Garnish with pecans. Randy Rigg
