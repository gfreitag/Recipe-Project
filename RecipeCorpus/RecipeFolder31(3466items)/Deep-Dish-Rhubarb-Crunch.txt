Title: Deep Dish Rhubarb Crunch
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1/3 c  All-Purpose Flour -- sifted
3/4 c  Oatmeal
1 c  Brown Sugar
1/2 c  Melted Butter
1 ts Ground Cinnamon
4 c  Fresh Rhubarb -- diced
3/4 c  Granulated Sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1 c  Water
1 tb Strawberry Jell-O
Preheat oven to 350 F. Mix flour, oatmeal, brown sugar, butter, and
cinnamon. The press half into the bottom of a buttered 9-inch deep
pie dish or casserole. Cover with the rhubarb. Combine granulated
sugar, cornstarch, and water and cook over low heat, stirring often
until smooth, clear, and thick. Add Jell-O and pour over rhubarb.
Cover with remaining crumb crust and bake for 40 mins or until
mixture is bubbly and golden. Yield: 8 servings.
