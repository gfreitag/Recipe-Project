Title: Zuppa Di Capelvenere E Funghi
Categories: Italian, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
8 c  Chicken Broth
1    Carrot -- diced
1    Stalk Celery -- diced
2 tb Scallions -- chopped
1/2 ts Salt
10    Mushrooms Sliced
1/4 lb Vermicelli
Bring the broth to boil, add carrots, celery, scallions and salt.
Simmer 12 mins. Add mushrooms and vermicelli. Stir well, Cook for
about 15 mins and serve in hot bowls.
