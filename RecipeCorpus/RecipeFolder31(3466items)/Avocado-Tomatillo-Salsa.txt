Title: Avocado-Tomatillo Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2    Avocados -- diced
1 ts Sweet red pepper -- diced
1 ts Sweet green pepper -- diced
1 tb Green onion -- diced
4    Tomatillos -- diced
1    Garlic clove -- minced
2 tb Cilantro -- chopped
2    Serrano chiles -- diced
2 ts Lime juice
3 tb Olive oil
Salt
Combine avocados, sweet red pepper, green pepper, green onion and
half of tomatillos in large mixing bowl.  Place garlic, cilantro,
serrano chiles, lime juice and remaining tomatillos in blender and
puree until smooth. Slowly drizzle in oil. Pour puree into avocado
mixture. Combine thoroughly. Season to taste with salt. Let stand 30
minutes. Makes about 1-1/2 cups.
