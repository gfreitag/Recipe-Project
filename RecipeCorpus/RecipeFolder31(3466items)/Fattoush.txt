Title: Fattoush
Categories: Salads, Middle east, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Romaine lettuce
1 ea Red pepper, seeded & diced
4 ea Tomatoes, sliced
2 ts Capers
16 ea Olives, black
4 sl Whole wheat bread
2 ea Garlic cloves
1 tb Olive oil
MMMMM----------------------FOR THE DRESSING---------------------------
1 tb Olive oil
2 ts White wine vinegar
1 tb Parsley, fresh chopped
1 ts Tarragon, dried
Salt & black pepper
Prepare all the salad ingredients and place in a bowl. Toast the whole
wheat bread and rub with cut cloves of garlic. Lightly sprinkle with
oil. Break into small pieces. Put the dressing ingredients together
in a screw-top jar and mix by shaking well. Toss the garlic toast and
dressing into the salad.
