Title: Baked Salmon with Pesto and Lemon
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Herbs/spice, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1    To 1 1/3 lbs salmon fillet
1/8 ts Salt
Freshly ground black pepper,
-to taste
1/4 c  Prepared pesto
6 sl Lemon
Lemon wedges

Place salmon in lightly oiled baking pan.  Sprinkle lightly with salt
and pepper.  Spread pesto over salmon.  Top with lemon slices.  Bake
in pre-heated 450-degree oven 12 minutes per inch of thickness.  Test
for doneness.  Serve salmon with lemon wedges.
