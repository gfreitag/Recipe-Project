Title: Jerk Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Allspice
1 tb Thyme
1 1/2 ts Cayenne
1 1/2 ts Fresh-ground black pepper
1 1/2 ts Rubbed sage
3/4 ts Nutmeg
3/4 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Salt
2 tb Garlic powder
1 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Oil
1/4 c  Soy sauce
3/4 c  White vinegar
1    Orange -- juice of
Juice of one lime
1    Hot pepper -- seeded
& chopped
1 c  Green onions -- chopped
Toss all the ingredients in the blender and blend-away.
