Title: Waldine's Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
4    Tomatoes; seed -- chop
1 lg Purple onion -- chop
2    Cloves garlic -- minced or
-if you like, chopped
1    Green pepper; seed -- chop
4    Jalapeno peppers; seed --
-chopped
1 cn Rotel Chile & Tomatoes
1/8 c  Olive oil
1/8 c  Balsamic vinegar
Cilantro -- chopped (opt'l)
Mix all and refrigerate  in a glass jar.  Keeps a long time. I used on
grilled fish, chops, scrambled eggs, etc. MM Format Norma Wrenn
