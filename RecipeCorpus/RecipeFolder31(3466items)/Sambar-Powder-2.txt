Title: Sambar Powder 2
Categories: Herbs/spice, Indian
Yield: 3 cups
2 c  Coriander seeds
2 c  Dried red chilies
1/4 c  Black peppercorns
1/4 c  Cumin seeds
2 ts Fenugreek seeds
2 ts Mustard seeds
1/2 c  Yellow split peas
2 ts Poppy seeds
1    Coconut
1 lg Cinnamon stick
-broken into pieces
1 lg Bunch curry leaves
2 ts Turmeric
Dry roast all ingredients except turmeric in a heavy saucepan until
lightly colored. Grind all the ingredients to a fine powder. Store in
an airtight container.
