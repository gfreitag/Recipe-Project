Title: B.L.T. Pizza
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Pizza
Yield: 15 servings
2 1/2 kg Pizza dough
1 1/2    Litres tomato sauce
1 1/2 kg Mozzarella
1 1/2 kg Fresh tomato sliced
750 g  Lettuce leaves
500 ml Mayonnaise
700 g  Bacon
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Panfry bacon cut into 1 cm squares.  Dry after frying. Spread dough,
top with tomato sauce, mozzarella, fresh sliced tomato and the bacon.
Cook. Top with lettuce coated with mayonnaise. Serve. Source: Given
to share with the express permission of Chef de Cuisine
