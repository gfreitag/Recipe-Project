Title: Broccoli and Bits-O-Barley Bake
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 1 recipe
1/3 c  AM Bits-O-Barley
3/4 c  Water
1/4 ts Sea salt (optional)
1/2 c  Shredded Monterey Jack
1 cn Cream of broccoli soup
Combine all ingredients except soup and cheese in a small sauce pan.
Bring to a boil.  Remove from heat, cover and let stand 2-3 minutes.
Add soup (undiluted) and cheese, bake for 20-30 minutes at 350 F. or
until mixture is heated through.

SUGGESTION: Add some sauteed onions, bell pepper, celery or spices of
your choice.
