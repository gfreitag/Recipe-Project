Title: Blackberry Coffee Cake
Categories: Breads, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Pecans or walnuts, chopped
2    Eggs
1 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  Brown sugar
3 1/2 c  Blackberries
1/4 c  Flour
2 tb Butter
Sift together first five ingredients and put in a large bowl; cut in
margarine until mixture looks like crumbs.  In another bowl, mix
together eggs, milk, and vanilla.  Pour over flour mixture and stir
until just moistened.  Spread in a greased 8" x 12" pan; distribute
berries on top. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, flour, and
butter. Mix with a fork until crumbly.  Add nuts and sprinkle over
top of cake. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.  Makes 6 to 8
servings.

Source:  King's Orchards, Plantersville, Texas
