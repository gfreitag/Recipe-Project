Title: Pork Chop Bake
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
6    1" thick pork chops,
-fat removed
4 md Potatoes (uncooked),
-peeled/sliced thin
1    4 oz can mushroom stems
-and pieces, drained
1    Envelope Lipton onion soup
-mix
1 c  Hot water
Brown chops; drain on paper toweling. In buttered 2-quart baking dish,
arrange potatoes, mushrooms and the chops. In a small bowl, combine
water and soup mix; pour over meat. Cover and bake at 350~ for 1 1/4
hours or until potatoes are tender. Serves 6.
