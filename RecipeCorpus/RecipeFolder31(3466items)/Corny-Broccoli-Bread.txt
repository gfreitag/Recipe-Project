Title: Corny Broccoli Bread
Categories: Breads, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
10 oz Broccoli; chopped, thawed
-and drained
8 1/2 oz Corn muffin mix
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Cottage cheese
4    Eggs; beaten
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Heat oven to 375.  Mix all ingredients.  Spoon into lightly greased
loaf 9" loaf pan.  Bake 40 minutes. Source: Kraft ad
