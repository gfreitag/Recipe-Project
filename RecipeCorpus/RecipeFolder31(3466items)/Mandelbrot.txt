Title: Mandelbrot
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Jewish
Yield: 2 loaves
2    Eggs, well beaten
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Oil
1/4 ts Lemon extract
1/2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Sifted flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Chopped almonds
Add eggs to sugar and oil.  Add flavorings.  Sift 1/2 cup flour over
chopped almonds and add to above.  Add balance of dry ingredients.
Makes a soft, moist dough.  Pat with floured hands into two long
loaves on cookie sheet (2 inches wide by 3/4 inch high).  Bake in
medium oven (325) for 25 to 30 minutes.  When slightly cook, cut into
slices of desired size. Submitted By MICHELLE HOWE   On   06-26-95
