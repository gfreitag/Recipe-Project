Title: Pizza Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn 10oz tomato soup
1 tb Dry oregano leaves
1/2 ts Garlic salt
8 oz Mozzarella -- shredded
8 oz Muenster -- shreaded
1/3 c  Parmesan -- grated
8 oz Thin slice pepperoni
1 cn 8oz mushrooms -- drained
Place ingredients on pizza dough in order listed. Allow to rise 20
min in warm place. Bake 450F for 20-25 min. pg36
