Title: Codfish Fritters^
Categories: Fish, Tasteofhome, Depress/era
Yield: 1 servings
Strips of salt codfish
2 tb Flour
1    Egg, separated
1/3 c  Hot milk
Hot lard
Cut fish into finger-sized strips and soak overnight in the ice box.
In the morning, drain and dry. Mix flour, egg yolk, and milk, beating
well to remove all lumps. Add frothy-beaten egg white. Dip fish in
batter, one piece at a time, and fry at once in hot fat to cover.
Cook till golden brown; drain well before serving.
