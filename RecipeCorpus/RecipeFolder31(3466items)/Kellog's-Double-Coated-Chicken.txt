Title: Kellog's Double Coated Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
7 c  Kellog's corn flakes;
.  crushed to about 2 cups
1    Egg
1 c  Skim milk
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 lb Chicken pieces; with or
.  without skin
3 tb Margarine; melted
Place cereal into a shallow dish or pan.  Set aside.

In a small mixing bowl, beat the egg and milk slightly.  Add the
flour, salt and pepper.  Mix until smooth.  Dip the chicken in the
batter. Coat with cereal.  Place in a single layer, skin side up, in
a foil-lined shallow baking pan.  Drizzle with margarine.

Bake in a preheated oven at 350Ã¸F about 1 hour or until the chicken is
tender, no longer pink and the juices run clear.  Don't cover the pan
or turn the chicken while baking.

Nutritional information: per serving with skin: 560 calories, 30g
fat, 37g carbohydrates, 180mg cholesterol, 610mg sodium

Nutritional information: per serving without skin: 400 calories, 10g
fat, 36g carbohydrates, 145mg cholesterol, 620mg sodium
