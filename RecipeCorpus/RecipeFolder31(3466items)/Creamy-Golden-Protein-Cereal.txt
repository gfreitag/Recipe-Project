Title: Creamy Golden Protein Cereal
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 recipe
1/4 c  AM Amaranth Flour
1/4 c  AM Millet Flour
1/4 c  Yellow Corn Flour
1 pn Salt
1/4 c  Sesame Tahini
-OR- ground seeds
3 c  Water
Mix flours, salt, and tahini together.  Add water and stir.  Simmer
over low heat until thick and creamy, stirring constantly with a wire
whisk to prevent lumping or burning.  Once thick, serve hot with milk
or soymilk and favorite sweetener.
