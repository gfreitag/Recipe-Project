Title: Banana Lemonade
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Banana
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
2 tb Water
4 ts Honey
6    Ice cubes
2 sm Fresh mint leaves
Lemon slice
In the container of an electric blender, combine the banana, lemon
juice, water, honey, ice and mint leaves.  Blend until smooth.  Pour
over ice cubes in a tall glass and garnish with lemon.
