Title: Raspberry Vinaigrette
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/4    Olive oil
3/4 c  Raspberry vinegar
2    Lime -- juice only
1    Lime -- zest only
1 ts Ground ginger
Salt
1/2 ts White pepper
Put all the ingredients in a bowl and whisk well.  Taste for
seasoning.
