Title: Slow and Easy Doves
Categories: Game, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
8    To 10 doves
Salt and pepper
4 tb Cooking oil
1 c  Of flour
Directions: Put flour in brown paper bag. Shake doves two at a time
in bag to coat with flour. Sear birds quickly in large skillet until
brown on all sides. Put enough water in skillet to half cover birds.
Cover and simmer for 1 1/2 hours or until doves are tender. Thicken
gravy with flour from paper bag.
