Title: Country Special Beets
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Minced green onion
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
4 c  Cooked sliced beets
Stir sour cream, vinegar, onion, sugar, salt and pepper together.
Pour over beets in saucepan.  Heat (slowly because of sour cream)
stirring occasionally.  Do not boil. Randy Rigg
