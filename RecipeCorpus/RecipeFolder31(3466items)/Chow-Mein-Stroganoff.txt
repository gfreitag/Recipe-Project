Title: Chow Mein Stroganoff
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1 lb Ground chuck
1/4 c  Minced onion
1 cn Chow Mein vegetables
1 ts Soy sauce
1 cn Mushroom soup
1 cn Chinese noodles
1 cn Celery soup
Brown meat and onions then add drained vegetables, soy sauce, and
undiluted soups.  Heat through.  Pour over Chinese Noodles and
sprinkle a few over the top. Randy Rigg
