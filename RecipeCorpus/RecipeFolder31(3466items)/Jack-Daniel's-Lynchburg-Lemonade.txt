Title: Jack Daniel's Lynchburg Lemonade
Categories: Beverages, Fruit, Alcohol
Yield: 1 recipe
1    Part Jack Daniel's
1    Part Sweet & Sour Mix
1    Part Triple Sec
4    Parts Sprite
Ice
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Lemon slices
Cherries
Mix ingredients.  Add ice and stir.  Garnish.  Serve in tall glasses.

Recipe from Jack Daniels, Lynchburg, Tennesee.
