Title: Lime Shrimp in Tortillas
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 6 tortillas
1/2 c  Fresh lime juice
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 ts California Style Garlic
-pepper
1/2 ts Parsley flakes
1 lb Fresh med. shrimp, peeled
-and deveined
1 sm Onion, sliced
1/2    Green bell pepper, s
2 tb Vegetable oil
6    Warm tortillas
Salas
Sour cream
Shredded chedder cheese
Combine lime juice, oil, garlic pepper and parsley flakes in self-clos
plastic bag.  Add shrimp and marinate 15 minutes.  Saute onion and gre
pepper in 2 tb oil 3-4 minutes; add shrimp and cook 4-5 minutes.
Serv with tortillas and desired topping. Recipe from Albertson's Food
Store Submitted By TANANA REYNOLDS   On   05-22-95
