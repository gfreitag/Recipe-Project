Title: Tuna, Beans and Onion Salad
Categories: Vegetables, Salads, Italian, Masterchefs
Yield: 6 servings
750 g  White beans, boiled
600 g  Preserved tuna
200 g  Onion sliced
150 ml Olive oil
70 g  Parsley chopped
Salt and pepper
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Mix all together.
