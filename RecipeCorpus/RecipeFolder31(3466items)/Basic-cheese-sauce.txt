Title: Basic cheese sauce
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
2 tb Margarine or butter
2 tb Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Milk
1 c  Sharp cheese, grated
pn Cayenne pepper
In a medium microwavable bowl, place the margarine, flour and salt;
microwave on high for 2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute.  Gradually
stir in the milk. Microwave on high for 4-5 minutes, stirring every
minute until sauce is thick and bubbly. Add grated cheese and stir to
blend well. Add pinch of cayenne if desired. For thicker sauce, use 3
Tbsp of flour. Submitted By ROBERT WHITE   On   06-13-95
