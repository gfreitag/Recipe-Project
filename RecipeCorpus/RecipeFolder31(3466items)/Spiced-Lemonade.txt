Title: Spiced Lemonade
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Lemon
2 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
4 ea Whole allspice
4 ea Whole cloves
1 ea Cinnamon stick
1 tb Chopped crystallized ginger
1 pn Grated nutmeg
1 c  Fresh lemon juice;(8lemons)
1 c  Club soda; chilled
Remove rind in pieces from lemon with swivel bladed veggie peeler;
avoid any bitter white pith.

Combine water, sugar, lemon rind, allspice, cloves, cinnamon stick,
crystallized ginger and nutmeg in small saucepan. Bring to simmer;
simmer covered 20 mins. Strain into pitcher and chill; discard solids.

To serve, stir in lemon juice and club soda.  Serve over crushed ice.
Makes 4 servings.
