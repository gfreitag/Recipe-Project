Title: Jewish French Toast (D)
Categories: Jewish, Pcake/waffl, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
1    Egg -- slightly beaten
1/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Vanila
2    1/2 Inch Thick Slices
-Challah
2 tb Farmers Cheese, Heaping
1 tb Raisins
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 tb Walnuts -- coarsely ground
Oil Or Butter For Frying
1 tb Honey, Scant
Sliced Fresh Fruit --
-optional
Beat the egg, milk and vanilla and add challah and soak.

Mash the cheese with a fork and 1 tb of the egg batter. Add the
raisins, cinnamon, and nuts.

Spread the cheese mixture on 1 slice of the challah. Cover with
another slice.

Heat the oil or butter in a med-hot pan. Fry the challah sandwich,
turning carefully after one side has browned to brown the other side.
Dribble honey on top before serving. You can also top with sliced
fruit.

: "Jewish Cooking in America" by Joan Nathan
