Title: Asparagus Polonaise
Categories: Stove top, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Asparagus; blanched
2 ea Eggs; hard-boiled
2 tb Fresh parsley; chopped
1/4 c  Bread crumbs
6 tb Butter; melted
Combine chopped hard cooked eggs with chopped parsley and sprinkle it
over cooked, warm asparagus. Saute bread crumbs in butter until
crumbs are golden brown; pour the sauce over the asparagus.
