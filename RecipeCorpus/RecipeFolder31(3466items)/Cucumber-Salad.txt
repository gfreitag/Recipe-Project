Title: Cucumber Salad
Categories: Main dish, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg English cucumber -- sliced
-1/4" thick
1 sm Onion -- sliced 1/4" thick
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb White vinegar
1    Lemon -- juice only
1 tb Dried dill weed
Salt
1 ts Pepper
Place the cucumber and onion in a bowl and toss.  Pour the olive oil
over the top and toss well.  Add the remaining ingredients and toss
well. Taste for seasoning.
