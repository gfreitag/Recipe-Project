Title: Chile Salsa`
Categories: Cowboy, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
5    Plum Tomatoes, Ripe --
Seeded and chopped
1/4 c  White Onion -- chopped
1    Jalapeno -- seeded and
Minced
Or
1 cn (4 Oz) Chiles, Chopped
Mild -- drained
1    Clove Garlic -- minced
1 tb Red Wine Vinegar
1 tb Lime Juice
1 tb Olive Oil
1 ts Dried Oregano
1/4 ts Salt
1. In a medium, combine all the ingredients. Cover and refrigerate
until ready to serve, no longer than 1 day.

Makes about 1 1/2 cups from Charles C. Day of San Cayetano Ranch of
Tumacacori, AZ.
