Title: Beef or Liver One Sun (Neua Reu Tab Daed Dio)
Categories: Meats, Thai, Hot/spicy, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
Thin slices of rump steak
-with a little fat attached
OR
Thin slices of liver
1/2 c  Fish sauce
Oil for frying
Marinate the meat or liver in the fish sauce for about 1 hour.
Drain. Heat some oil over med to high heat and fry the meat being
careful not to overcook it.  Remove and drain on paper towels. Eat as
a snack with cold beer.
