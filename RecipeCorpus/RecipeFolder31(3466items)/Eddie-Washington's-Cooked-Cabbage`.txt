Title: Eddie Washington's Cooked Cabbage`
Categories: Tasteofhome, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4    Cabbage -- shredded
1 tb Sugar
3 tb Bacon Fat Or Vegetable Oil
Salt And Freshly Ground
Pepper -- to taste
In a heavy cast-iron skillet, slowly cook the cabbage with the sugar
in the bacon fat or vegetable oil, covered, about 30 mins, or until
the cabbage gets a reddish tinge. Salt and pepper to taste. Serve
immediately.
