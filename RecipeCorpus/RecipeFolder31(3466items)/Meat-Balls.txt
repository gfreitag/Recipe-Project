Title: Meat Balls
Categories: Appetizers, Meats, Poultry
Yield: 3 servings
1 lb Ground turkey
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Catsup
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Brown sugar
Just form into balls and cook in your crock pot for 8 hours while
you're gone to work.  Great on a Monday evening after an otherwise
crappy day. Randy Rigg
