Title: Tomato Sauce`
Categories: Main dish, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Brown Sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1 cn (6 Oz) Tomato Juice
1 cn (8 Oz) Tomato Sauce
2 ts Vinegar
1/2 ts Instant Onion -- minced
1/2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
In a saucepan, combine sugar and cornstarch. Gradually stir in tomato
juice. Add remaining ingredients. Cook over medium heat, stirring
constantly until thickened, about 5 mins. Makes 1 1/2 cups.
