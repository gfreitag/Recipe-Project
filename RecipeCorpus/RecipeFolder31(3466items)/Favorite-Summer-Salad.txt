Title: Favorite Summer Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1    Pineapple, fresh
6    Apricots; halved
1    Banana; sliced
1 c  Cantaloupe balls
1 c  Watermelon balls
1 c  Grapes, white seedless
1 c  Coconut, flaked
16 oz Sour cream
1/4 c  Pecans; chopped
Chill pineapple overnight.  Peel and core pineapple; cut into
bite-size chunks.  Combine fruit; add flaked coconut, and toss gently.

Combine sour cream and pecans; spoon over fruit.  Chill at least 2
hours before serving.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, July, 1980. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
