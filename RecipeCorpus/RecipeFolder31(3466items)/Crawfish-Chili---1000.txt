Title: Crawfish Chili - 1000
Categories: Cajun/creol, Main dish, Meats, Fish
Yield: 16 servings
2 lb Lean ground beef
1 ts Garlic, chopped fine
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Dried mint
3 tb Chili powder
1 c  Dry white wine
1 ts Lemon or lime juice
1 x  Bacon drippings
2 lb Crawfish tails
2 ts Salt
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1 tb Dried parsley
1 cn (8 oz) tomato sauce
1 x  Water
1 c  Chopped onions
Brown meat in bacon drippings.  Combine all other ingredients with
meat and bring to a boil.  Simmer for a few hours. From Justin
Wilson's "Gourmet and Gourmand Cookbook"
