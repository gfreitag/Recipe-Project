Title: Quick Cheese Grits
Categories: Breakfast, Nuts/grains, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 12 servings
4 qt Water
1 c  Nonfat milk
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Quick grits
3 tb Butter or margarine
4 oz Pasteurized Processed cheese
.  spread
6 oz Sharp cheddar cheese,
.  shredded
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Red pepper
Combine the water, milk and salt in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
Stir in the grits.  Reduce the heat and cook about 5 minutes, stirring
occasionally.

Add the butter, cheeses, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder and red
pepper.  Stir until the butter and cheeses have melted.

NOTE:  If not serving immediately, add milk to make the grits creamy
if needed and reheat in a microwave.
