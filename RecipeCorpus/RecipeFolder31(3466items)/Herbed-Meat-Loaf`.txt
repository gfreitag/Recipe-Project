Title: Herbed Meat Loaf`
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Ground Beef, Extra Lean
2    Eggs
1 c  Bread Crumbs -- fine dry
1 tb Parsley -- chopped
1/4 c  Chives -- chopped
2 tb Fresh Basil Or 1 Tbsp Dried
1    Clove Garlic -- crushed
1/4 c  Green Pepper -- chopped
1/4 c  Celery -- chopped
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black Pepper
3    Strips Bacon
Combine all the ingredients except the bacon and place in a meat-loaf
pan. Cover with the bacon strips and bake 1 1/2 hrs in a 350 F oven.
Serve 4-6.
