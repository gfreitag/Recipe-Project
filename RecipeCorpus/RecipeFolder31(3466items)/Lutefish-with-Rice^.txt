Title: Lutefish with Rice^
Categories: Fish, Tasteofhome, Depress/era
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Rice
2 1/2 lb Lutefish
Water
1 c  Cream
1 tb Butter, melted
Cook rice. boil lutefish until done. Flake, remove bone, mix with
rice, season to taste. Put in a baking dish and cover with cream and
butter. Bake until nice and brown. That's it.
