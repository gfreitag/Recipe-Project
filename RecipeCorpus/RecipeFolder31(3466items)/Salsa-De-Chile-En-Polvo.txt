Title: Salsa De Chile En Polvo
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
Chili Powder Sauce
3 tb Oil
2 tb Flour
1/4 c  Mild chili powder
2 c  Beef broth
2 cn (10 1/2) oz. tomato puree
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Heat oil in a large saucepan.  Stir in flour and cook 1 min. Stir in
chili powder.  Add beef broth, tomato puree, oregano, cumin and
garlic powder. Taste and add salt if needed.  Simmer 15 min. Make
about 4 2/3 cups sauce, enough for 12 enchiladas.  Norma Kelley
