Title: Spiced Vinegar
Categories: Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
1 pt Red wine vinegar
1    Bay leaf
1 ts Allspice
1 ts Coriander seeds
1 ts Cardamon seeds, shelled
1    Cinnamon stick
6    Cloves, whole
2 tb Sugar
Combine all ingredients in the top of a double boiler. Over boiling
heat, heat mixture until simmering. Turn off heat, cover and simmer 2
hours. Leave the spices in the vinegar and pour into clean glass
bottles with stoppers. Store in a cool place until ready to use.
