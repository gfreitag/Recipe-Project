Title: Nut-Flavored Oil
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 2 cups oil;
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: BETTY CROCKER'S COOK---------------------
1 c  Walnuts, almonds or
-hazelnuts
2 c  Vegetable oil
Place nuts and 1/2 cup of the oil in blender or food processor. Cover
and blend, or process, until nuts are finely chopped. Place nut
mixture and remaining oil in glass jar or bottle. Cover tightly and
let stand in a cool, dry place 10 days. Strain oil. Refrigerate up to
3 months.
