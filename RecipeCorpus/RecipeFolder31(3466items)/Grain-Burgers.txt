Title: Grain Burgers
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 recipe
2 c  Cooked AM Medium Brown Rice
1/3 c  Chopped green pepper
1/3 c  Chopped onion
1/3 c  Chopped celery
2    Egg whites; beaten
1/3 c  AM Cornmeal
1/3 c  AM Oatmeal
1 c  AM Wheat Germ
1 tb Soy sauce
Seasonings to taste
MMMMM------------------------FOR COOKING-----------------------------
2 tb Whole grain flour
2 tb AM Wheat Germ
2 tb Vegetable oil
Combine first nine ingredients and season to taste.  Mold into
patties. Dust with flour and wheat germ mixture.  In hot skillet
brown in oil until brown on both sides.
