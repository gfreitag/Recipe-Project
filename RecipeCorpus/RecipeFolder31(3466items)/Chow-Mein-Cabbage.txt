Title: Chow Mein Cabbage
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  Shredded cabbage
1 c  Cut celery
2/3 c  Chopped green peppers
1 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Butter
1/2 ts Salt
Combine vegetables.  Melt butter in skillet; add vegetables and salt.
Cook 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Cover and cook until vegetables
are tender-crisp, 3 to 5 minutes (do not overcook).  Serve
immediately, passing the soy sauce. Randy Rigg
