Title: Cold Slaw^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Cabbage
1    Egg
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Mustard
Chop cabbage fine. Mix egg, vinegar, salt and mustard; cook until
thickened. Cool thoroughly; pour over cabbage.
