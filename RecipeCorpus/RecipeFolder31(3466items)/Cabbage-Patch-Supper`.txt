Title: Cabbage Patch Supper`
Categories: Breakfast, Main dish
Yield: 5 servings
2 tb Butter
1 md Onion -- sliced
1/2 c  Celery -- sliced
1/4 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Chili Powder
1 cn (1 Lb) Tomatoes
1 cn (8 Oz) Kidney Beans
-- drained
1/2 sm Head Cabbage -- sliced
1 lb Weiners
Melt butter in skillet; add onion and celery; cook until tender. Mix
flour with salt and chili powder; stir into onion-celery mixture. Add
tomatoes, kidney beans and cabbage. Cover and cook slowly, stirring
occasionally, for 15 mins. Add weiners and heat 10 mins longer. Makes
5 servings.
