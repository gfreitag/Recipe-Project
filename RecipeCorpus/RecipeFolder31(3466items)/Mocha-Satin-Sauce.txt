Title: Mocha Satin Sauce
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate
Yield: 2 cups
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Whipping cream
1/3 c  Cocoa
1 ts Powdered instant coffee
1 ts Vanilla
In small saucepan over low heat, melt butter.  Stir in whipping cream
and cocoa, and sugar.  Cook over medium, stirring often until mixture
begins to boil.   Reduce heat; simmer five minutes.  Remove from
heat; stir in instant coffee and vanilla.  Serve warm or cool.

Cover; refrigerate leftover sauce.

To reheat; place sauce in small saucepan.  Stir constantly over low
heat until warm.
