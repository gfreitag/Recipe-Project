Title: Baked Marinated Onions
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
2 x  Sweet spanish onions(10oz)
1/4 ts Sugar
1 1/2 ts Italian herbs
1/8 ts Black pepper
1/3 c  White wine vinegar
3/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 ts Salt
1 x  Chopped parsley
Peel onoins and cut crosswise into 1/2" thick slices. Arrange
slices in a

single layer in a shallow baking dish. Combine remaining ingredients
except parsley and blend well.  Pour over onions and let stand at
room temperature for 2-3 hours or overnight. Pour off marinade and
reserve. Cover onions and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, then
uncover and bake another 10 -15 minutes or until tender. Sprinkle
with parsley. Serve hot over hamburgers. SUGGESTION: Use the marinade
to make salad dressing. Submitted By MARK MIKLAS   On   05-24-95
