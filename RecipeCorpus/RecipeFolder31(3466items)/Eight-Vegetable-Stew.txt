Title: Eight Vegetable Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Fatfree, Passover, Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2    Onions
2    Carrots (or 1/2_lb. peeled
Baby carrots)
2    Red peppers
2    Tomatoes
2    Ribs celery
1 c  Mushrooms
1    Zucchini
1/2 lb Fresh green beans
1 c  Tomato paste
1 tb Sugar
1 ds Salt
1 ea Salsa to taste - about
1/4_cup (we added because
It needed spicing up)
Slice onions, carrots, red pepper, tomatoes, celery, mushrooms, and
zucchini, setting tomatoes aside.

Saute all plus green beans in your favorite sautee liquid for 10
mins. Add tomato paste, sugar, salt, tomatoes and salsa with 1 cup of
water. Cover and cook 20 mins.

Serves 4-6.

Source: It's from a lovely illustrated cookbook called The Passover
Gourmet.
