Title: Maple Rolls^
Categories: Pastry, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
3/4 c  Milk, about
Scraped maple sugar
Sift dry ingredients; rub in butter. Add enough milk to make a soft
dough. Roll our about 1/3" thick; spread thickly with maple sugar.
Roll up, jelly roll style, and slice with a very sharp knife. Lay
slices on a greased baking pan; bake at 350 degrees for about 15
minutes.
