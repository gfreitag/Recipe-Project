Title: Chocolate Spritz Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Butter or margarine
Softened
2/3 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
2 1/4 c  Unsifted all purp.
Flour
1/3 c  Unsweetened cocoa
1/2 ts Salt
Cream butter or margarine, sugar, egg and vanilla in large mixer bowl
until light and fluffy.  Combine flour, cocoa and salt; gradually add
to creamed mixture.

Fill cookie press with dough.  Press dough onto cool ungreased cookie
sheet.  Bake at 350 for 5 to 7 minutes or until set.  Remove from
cookie sheet; cool completely on wire rack.  Makes about 4-1/2 dozen.
