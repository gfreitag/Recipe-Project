Title: Weiner Cheesaroni`
Categories: Breakfast, Kids
Yield: 5 servings
1 lb Weiners
7 oz Macaroni -- cooked
1 cn Condensed Cheese Soup
(10 3/4 oz)
2/3 c  Milk
1/2 c  Chopped Green Onions
1 ts Dry Mustard
1/2 ts Caraway Seed
Preheat oven to 350 F. Cut half the weiners into bite-sized pieces;
combine with remaining ingredients in a 2-qt casserole. Top with
remaining whole weiners. Cover and bake 30 mins, or until heated
through. Makes 5 servings.
