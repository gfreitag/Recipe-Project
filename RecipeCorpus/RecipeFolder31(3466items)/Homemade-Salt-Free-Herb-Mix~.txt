Title: Homemade Salt-Free Herb Mix~
Categories: Herbs/spice, Mixes, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
3 ts Onion powder
3 ts Garlic powder
3 ts Dried parsley flakes
1 ts Dried basil leaves
1 ts Dried thyme leaves
1 ts Dried marjaram leaves
1 ts Pepper
In storage container with tight-fitting lid, combine all ingredients;
mix well.  Seal tightly.  Store in cool, dry place for up to 6
months.  Stir or shake well before each use. Makes 13 teaspoons
(about 14 cup)

Nutrition information:  One teaspoon mix contains 1 mg. sodium.
