Title: Herb Popcorn Seasoning
Categories: Snacks, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Thyme
1 ts Basil
1 ts Marjoram
1 tb Dried onion flakes
1/2 c  Corn, popped
Butter to taste
Combine the salt, thyme, basil, marjoram and dried onion and mix
well. Use one or two teaspoons per 1/2 cup corn, popped with butter.
