Title: Cinnamon-Honey Butter
Categories: Herbs/spice, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Butter
1/3 c  Honey
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Beat butter in small mixing bowl on high speed until light and fluffy.
Gradually beat in honey and cinnamon until well blended.  Store,
covered in refrigerator up to 3 weeks.
