Title: Louisiana Crawfish Dressing
Categories: Fish, Side dish, Cajun/creol, Dress/stuff
Yield: 8 servings
6 c  Sliced squash (about 2 lbs)
2 c  Water
1 lg Onion; chopped
1 ea Green pepper; chopped
1 c  Chopped celery
1 cl Garlic; minced
1/2 c  Butter or margarine; melted
1 ea Egg; slightly beaten
1 c  Italian-seasoned breadcrumbs
1/3 c  Beau Monde seasoning
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts To 1/2 ts pepper
1/4 ts Dried whole thyme
1/8 ts Red pepper
1/8 ts Hot sauce
2 c  Peeled crawfish tails
Combine squash and water in a Dutch oven; cook until tender. Drain
well. Saute onion and next 3 ingredients in butter until tender.
Add vegetables and egg to squash; stir in breadcrumbs and remaining
ingredients. Spoon mixture into a greased 12" x 8" x 2" baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Betty Starnes of Baton Rouge, Louisiana in April, 1990 "Southern
