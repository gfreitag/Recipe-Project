Title: Curried Chickpeas and Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Main dish, Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Canola oil
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1 md Onion, chopped
6 ea (to 8) Green onions, cut in
. 1-inch pieces, including
. 3 inches of green
1 1/2 tb Curry powder
1 1/4 c  Tomato juice
3/4 c  Vegetable or chicken broth
3 md Potatoes, cut in 1-inch
. chunks
1 cn (19-oz.) Chickpeas, drained
. and rinsed
1/2 ts Minced jalapeno peppers
. (optional)
MMMMM------------------------PER SERVING-----------------------------
273 x  Calories
9 1/3 g  Protein (9.3g)
47 1/2 g  Carbohydrates (47.5g)
8 g  Fiber
6 1/3 g  Fat (6.3g [0.4g saturated])
625 mg Sodium
In a medium saucepan, heat oil.  Add garlic and both types of onions;
saute until vegetables are soft.  Stir in curry powder and saute for
30 seconds. Add remaining ingredients, cover and simmer for about 40
minutes, or until potatoes are tender.

From USA WEEKEND, May 5-7, 1995.
