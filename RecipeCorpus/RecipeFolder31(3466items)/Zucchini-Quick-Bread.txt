Title: Zucchini Quick Bread
Categories: Vegetables, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
3    Eggs
2 ts Soda
1 c  Salad oil
1 ts Salt
2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 c  Shredded zucchini
3/4 ts Nutmeg
3 c  Flour
1 c  Chopped nuts
8 oz Crushed pinapple
Beat eggs, add oil sugar and vanilla. Continue beating until foamy.
Mix in remaining ingredients. Stir till well blended. Pour into 2
5x9 loaf pans. Bake at 350 for 1hr.
