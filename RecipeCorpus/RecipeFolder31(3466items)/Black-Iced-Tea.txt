Title: Black Iced Tea
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM----------------------JUST ADD BACARDI---------------------------
Crushed ice
3/4 oz Orange juice
3/4 oz Triple sec
3/4 oz Brandy
3/4 oz Black rum
1/2    Lime, juiced
6 oz Coca-Cola classic
Mix and pour over crushed ice in a tall drink glass, stir well. Add
lime and top with Coca-Cola classic.
