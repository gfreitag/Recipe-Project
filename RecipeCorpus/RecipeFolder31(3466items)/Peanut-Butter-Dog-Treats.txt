Title: Peanut Butter Dog Treats
Categories: Pets
Yield: 2 dozen
1 1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Canola oil
2 md Eggs
3 tb Natural crunch peanut butter
2 tb Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Whole Wheat Flour
1/2 c  Unbleached flour
1/2 c  Corn meal
1/2 c  Rolled oats
Preheat oven to 400 degrees In large bowl combine dry ingredients.
Beat the liquid ingredients and peanut butter together in a separate
bowl. NOTE: Before adding oil drain off any extra oil. Add liquid
mixture to dry ingredients and blend with electric beater until
well-mixed. Form the dough into ball and let the dough rest for 10
minutes. On floured board, roll out dough to 1/4" thick. Cut dough
with cookie cutter. Bake cookies for 20 minutes at 400 then turn off
the oven and leave the biscuits inside to cool.
