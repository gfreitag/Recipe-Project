Title: Herman Quick Biscuit
Categories: Breads, Herman, Sourdough
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 c  Oil
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Herman
Mix together on waxed paper.  Form into biscuits. Bake at 350 for 10
minutes.  Yield:  12-15 biscuits.
