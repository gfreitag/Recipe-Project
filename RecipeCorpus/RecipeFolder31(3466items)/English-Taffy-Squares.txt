Title: English Taffy Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Rolled vanilla wafers
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 lb Butter
1 1/2    Sqaures of chocolate, melted
3    Eggs, separated
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
Mix crumbs with nuts.  Cover bottom of 9 x 9 inch pan with crumbs.
Cream butter and sugar and add egg yolks to chocolate, fold in beaten
egg whites and pour on crust.  Add remainder of crumbs. Randy Rigg
