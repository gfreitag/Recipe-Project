Title: Cheese Pizza
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Pizza
Yield: 15 servings
2 1/2 kg Pizza dough
1 1/2    Litres tomato sauce
1 1/2 kg Mozzarella
100 g  Fresh basil leaves
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Spread dough. Top with tomato sauce and then mozzarella cheese. Top
with fresh basil leaves. Cook and serve. Source: Given to share with
the express permission of Chef de Cuisine
