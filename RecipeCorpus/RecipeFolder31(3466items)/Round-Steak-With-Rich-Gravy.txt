Title: Round Steak With Rich Gravy
Categories: Meats, Crockpot
Yield: 3 servings
1 lb Round Steak
1 1/2 oz Pkg onion soup mix
10 1/2 oz Can cream mushroom soup
1/4 c  Water
Cut steak into serving pieces.  Place in crockpot. Add water. Sprinkle
onion soup mix over meat.  Spoon mushroom soup over.  Cover and cook
at low heat 6 to 8 hours or until meat is done.  Serve with rice,
mashed potatoes or noodles.

Source: Crockery Cookery by Mable Hoffman
