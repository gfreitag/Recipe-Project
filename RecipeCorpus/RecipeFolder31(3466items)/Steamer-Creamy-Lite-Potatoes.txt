Title: Steamer Creamy Lite Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
6 ea Whole new potatoes; scrubbed
1/2 c  Plain yogurt
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1 ts Dill weed
Arrange potatoes in steamer basket. Add 1-3/4 cups water to reservoir,
cover and steam 36 to 37 minutes.

Meanwhile, in serving bowl, combine yogurt and seasonings. Fold hot
potatoes into sauce.

Serves 3
