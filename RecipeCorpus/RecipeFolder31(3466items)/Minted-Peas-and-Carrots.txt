Title: Minted Peas and Carrots
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Butter
1 1/2 ts Dried mint
1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 pk Frozen peas and carrots
In a 1 1/2 quart casserole, melt butter in oven.  Add mint, sugar and
salt. Break up vegetables; mix with butter.  Cover; bake at 350
degrees for 1 hour. Randy Rigg
