Title: Pineapple Salsa 1
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  Pineapple -- diced
1 c  Red bell pepper -- diced
1/2 c  Cilantro -- chopped
1/4 c  Purple onions -- diced
2 tb Jalapeno pepper -- minced
1 tb White wine vinegar
1 tb Olive oil
1/4 ts Ground pepper -- fresh
Ground
1    Clove garlic -- crushed
Combine all ingredients in a bowl, and stir well. Cover and chill at
least 30 minutes
