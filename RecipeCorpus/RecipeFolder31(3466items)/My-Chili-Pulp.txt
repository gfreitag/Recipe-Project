Title: My Chili Pulp
Categories: Herbs/spice, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 c  Small dried red chilies
1 c  Water
Put ingredients in a small saucepan.  Bring to a boil, cover, reduce
heat and simmer 5 minutes.  Remove from heat and let cool. Remove
chilies with slotted spoon to a blender.  Add about 1/4 c water from
pan. Puree. Store in refrigerator.
