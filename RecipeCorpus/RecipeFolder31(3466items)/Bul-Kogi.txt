Title: Bul Kogi
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
House, Hawaii".
lbs.    beef short ribs 2 cloves garlic, crushed 1/4 cup
4-5
toasted & crushed sesame seeds 1 cup finely chopped green onions 1
tsp. pepper 1 tsp. sesame oil 2 cups soy sauce 1 cup sugar 1/4 cup oil

Place short ribs in bowl.  Add garlic, sesame seeds, green onions,
pepper, sesame oil, soy sauce, sugar and oil and toss to coat well.
Cover and let stand at room temperature 2 hours.  Barbecue over coals
or run under broiler, turning and basting often with marinade until
meat is tender.
