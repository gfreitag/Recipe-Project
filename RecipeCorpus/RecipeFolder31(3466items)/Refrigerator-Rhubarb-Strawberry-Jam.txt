Title: Refrigerator Rhubarb Strawberry Jam
Categories: Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
6 c  Rhubarb -- diced
3 c  Sugar
1 pk Strawberry gelatin powder
1. Cut the rhubarb into 1/2-inch pieces. Place in a stainless steel
kettle or pan. Combine the sugar with rhubarb and let stand, covered,
overnight. 2. Add 1/4 cup water to mixture. Bring to a boil, turn to
a simmer, then cook for 12 minutes. 3. Remove from heat and stir in
one 3-ounce package of strawberry gelatin. Stir until completely
dissolved. 4. Pour into containers with tight-fitting lids. Store in
refrigerator or divide in small portions and freeze.

Yield: About 1-1/4 quarts.
