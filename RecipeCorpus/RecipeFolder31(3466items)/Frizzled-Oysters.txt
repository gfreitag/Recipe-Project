Title: Frizzled Oysters
Categories: Seafood, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 qt Oysters
3 tb Butter
Salt and pepper
Dry oysters on a napkin. Heat butter in a frying pan and when very
hot add oysters. Season and serve hot. Many of the recipes in this
collection did not contain amounts or oven temperatures. I have typed
them in as they appear in the book, typos and all.
