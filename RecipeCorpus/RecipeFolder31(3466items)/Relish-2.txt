Title: Relish 2
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 servings
2 md Green Peppers, Stems And
-Seeds Removed
2 md Tomatoes, Cored
1 md Onion
1 md Zucchini
1/2 sm Eggplant, Pared
2 tb Salt
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Vinegar
1 c  Water
1 ts Mustard Seed
3/4 ts Celery Seed
1/4 ts Fine Herbs
Using the coarse blade of a food chopper, grind the peppers, tomatoes,
onion, zucchini and eggplant.  Stir the salt into the vegetables and
let stand overnight.  Rinse and drain the vegetables. In a medium
saucepan, combine the sugar, vinegar, water, mustard seed, celery
seed and fine herbs.  Stir in the vegetables.  Bring to boiling then
reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring frequently. Cool,
then cover and chill until ready to use. Makes about 4 cups of relish.
