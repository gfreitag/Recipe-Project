Title: Golden Corn Stuffing Bake
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Golden corn soup
1/4 c  Celery; finely chopped
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
1 1/2 c  Herb seasoning stuffing
4    Chicken breasts; boneless
-& skinless
1 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Margaine; melted
1 ts Mustard; spicy brown
Combine soup, celery, onion and stuffing.  In 9" greased pie plate
spoon stuffing mixture.  Arrange chicken over stuffing mixture; press
lightly into stuffing. Combine sugar, margarine and mustard; spread
evenly over chicken.  Bake at 400 for 25 minutes. Source: Campbells ad
