Title: Jamican Jerk Rub
Categories: Bbq/grill, Herbs/spice
Yield: 3 servings
3/4 c  Onion Powder
3/4 c  Dried Onion Flakes
1/4 c  Ground Allspice
1/4 c  Black Pepper -- fresh
Ground
1/4 c  Cayenne
2 tb Sugar
3 tb Ground Cinnamon
1 tb Ground Nutmeg
1/2 ts Dried Ground Habanero Chile
Optional
Mix the spices throughly in a bowl.  Store in a cool, dark pantry.
