Title: Mom's Whole Wheat Bread^
Categories: Breads, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Warm water
1/4 c  To 1/2 molasses
2 tb Dry yeast (1 cake)
1/4 c  Lard, melted
7 c  To 8 whole wheat flour
Combine water, molasses and yeast in large bowl; let stand 5 minutes.
Add lard and 5 cups flour. Beat about 110 strokes. Add more flour to
make a stiff dough. Knead till smooth and elastic. Let raise till
double. Punch down, cover, let raise again. Form into 3 loaves; place
in well greased pans; bake at 350 degrees for about 50 minutes.
