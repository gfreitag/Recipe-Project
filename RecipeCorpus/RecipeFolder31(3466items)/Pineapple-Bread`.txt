Title: Pineapple Bread`
Categories: Breads, Breakfast, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 lb Butter
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking Powder
pn Salt
1/2 c  (To 3/4)
1 c  Pineapple, Crushed And
Drained
Nuts -- chopped
Cream butter and sugar. Beat eggs in one at a time. Sift flour, baking
powder, and salt together. Alternate adding flour mixture and
pineapple to egg mixture. Add nuts. Grease and flour loaf pans. Bake
at 350 F for 45 mins to 1 hour. Yield: 2 small loaves or 1 large loaf.

As prepared at The Red Lion Inn of Stockbridge, Mass.
