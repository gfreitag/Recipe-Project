Title: BUTTERSCOTCH OAT SQUARES
Categories: Cake/cookie, Usenet
Yield: 3 dozen
2 c  Oats, quick or instant
1 c  Brown sugar (packed)
1/2 c  Butter, melted
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Mix oats and brown sugar, mix in butter and vanilla.  Divide mixture
evenly in 2 ungreased 8-inch square pans, spread evenly. Bake at 375
degrees F. for about 10 minutes or until it looks golden-brown.
Squares will be soft, but will harden when cool.

Allow to cool for 5 minutes, then mark in squares with a sharp knife.
Loosen edges and allow to cool before removing from pans.

NOTES:

*  Easy butterscotch oatmeal pan cookies -- The easiest cookies
(actually cookie-like objects) to make.  My mother makes these, I
don't know where the recipe is from. Yield: makes 30-40.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  5 minutes preparation, 15 minutes cooking and cooling.
: Precision:  approximate measurement OK.
