Title: Mayfest  --  Sauerkraut
Categories: Aust/german, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Sauerkraut
6 sl Bacon
2 tb Flour
1 ts Caraway seeds
In a saucepan, simmer the sauerkraut with the caraway seeds in a small
amount of water until tender.

Cut the bacon into small pieces; fry it until it is transparent.  Add
the flour and stir.  Add the bacon mixture to the sauerkraut mixture;
simmer for 10 minutes and stir.  If the sauerkraut does not thicken,
grate 1 or 2 raw, peeled potatoes into the mixture and simmer another
5 minutes.
