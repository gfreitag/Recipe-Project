Title: Chocolate Mousse Parfait^
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
Southern Living 4/94
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
1 c  (6oz) milk chocolate
Morsels
1/4 c  Whipping cream
2 tb Water
2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Whipping cream, whipped
1 c  Vanilla wafers, crushed
Combine chocolate morsels, 1/4 c whipping cream and water in a heavy
saucepan; cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until chocolate
melts. Cool.

Stir in vanilla, and fold in whipped cream.

Layer mousse and vanilla wafer crumbs into 6 (4 oz) parfait glasses.
Cover.

Freeze at least 1 hour or up to two days. Let stand 10-15 minutes
before serving. Makes 6 servings.

Note: for and easier dessert, prepare first two steps as directed;
chill. Serve with your favorite cookie.
