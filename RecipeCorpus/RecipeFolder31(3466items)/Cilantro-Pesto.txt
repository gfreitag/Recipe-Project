Title: Cilantro Pesto
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Condiments
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Fresh cilantro leaves
3/4 c  Extra virgin olive oil
1/4 c  Pine nuts -- toasted
6    Cloves garlic
1 tb Lime juice (1/2 lime)
1 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 tb Salt
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Source:
Mark Haugen of Tejas, Minneapolis, MN
