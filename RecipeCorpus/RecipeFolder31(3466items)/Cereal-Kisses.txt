Title: Cereal Kisses
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 dozen
2    Egg Whites
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Almond Extract
1 c  Coconut, shredded
2 c  Cereal, flaked
1. Beat egg whites until light, gradually beat in sugar and salt and
continue beating until stiff.

2. Add flavoring and fold in coconut and cereal.

3. Drop from tip of a teaspoon onto greased cookiesheets.

4. Bake in 350 F oven for 15 -20 minutes.
