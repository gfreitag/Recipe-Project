Title: Angel Kisses
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1 1/2 c  Chopped dates
3    Egg whites, beat stiff
1 c  Powdered sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
Mix pecans and dates, slowly add powdered sugar to egg whites.  Add
vanilla and salt, beat well.  Pour in nuts and dates, mix.  Drop by
heaping teaspoonful on a cookie sheet.  Bake about 15 minutes at 350
degrees. Randy Rigg
