Title: Cool Lemon Chicken Salad~
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
2 tb Fresh basil -- chopped OR
2 ts Dried basil
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Grated lemon rind
2 c  Chopped, cooked chicken &/or
Turkey
1 c  Halved red or green grapes
1/2 c  Halved chinese pea pods *
1/2 c  Thin sliced red onion **
Lettuce leaves - chopped or
Torn -- optional
* remove "strings", blanch and cut to bite-sized pieces. ** regular
onion or green onion - including tops can be used.

Mix Miracle Whip, basil, lemon juice and peel.  Add remaining
ingredients, except lettuce.  Mix lightly and refrigerate.  Serve on
lettuce, if desired.

Serves 4 but recipe doubles well.

Per serving:  Calories 334; fat 20.5 g; cholesterol 74 mg;
carbohydrate 16.1 g; fiber 1.6 g; protein 22.1 gl sodium 235 mg;
potassium 358 mg.

Note:  Can add any salad "fixing" (tomatoes, bean sprouts, etc.; black
olives work okay but alter the taste of the whole salad) at serving
time.
