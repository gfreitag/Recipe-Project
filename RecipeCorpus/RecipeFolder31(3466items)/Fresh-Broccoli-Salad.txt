Title: Fresh Broccoli Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Fresh broccoli, cut into
Bite size pieces
12 oz Fresh mushrooms, sliced
2 sm Red onions, thinly sliced
Into rings
2 1/4 oz Can sliced ripe olives
1 1/2 c  Prepared Italian salad
Dressing
1/3 c  Shredded Parmesan cheese
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl; toss to mix well.  Cover and
chill for at least 2 hours.   Yield:  12 servings

Diabetic Exchange:  One serving (prepared with low-fat salad dressing)
equals 2 vegetable, 1/2 fat; also 66 calories, 182 mg sodium, 3 mg
cholesterol, 9 gm carbohydrate, 5 gm protein, 2 gm fat
