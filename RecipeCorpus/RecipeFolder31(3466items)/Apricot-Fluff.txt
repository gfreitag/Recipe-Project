Title: Apricot Fluff
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
MMMMM----------------------BLEND IN BLENDER---------------------------
3 oz Apricot Jello
1 c  Boiling water
8 oz Can of apricots
4 oz Philadelphia cream cheese
Let mixture stand in refrigerator until set.  Add by mixer,
approximately 7 or 8 ounces of frozen whipping topping.  Molds well.
