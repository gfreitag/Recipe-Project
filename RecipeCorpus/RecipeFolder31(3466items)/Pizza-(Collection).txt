Title: Pizza (Collection)
Categories: Italian, Breads, Salsa/sauce, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
Pizza (Joann Pierce)
This is a recipe from a place called Tony's Pizza here in Oregon. A
friend used to work there. It is WONDERFUL!

Crust:

5 Cups flour Mix together the following and let double in size: 1 c
warm water 2 pkgs yeast 1 Tbsp sugar

Mix in separate container while yeast rises: 1 c cold water 2 Tbsp
sugar 1 Tbsp salt

When yeast rises, add to flour & mix. Then add cold mixture and stir
until flour disapears. Knead till smooth. divide into 2 - 3 balls.
Coat with oil and place on a plate. Cover with damp cloth and
refrigerate until ready to use. Roll out gently with hands. Bake at
450 degrees.

Sauce:

6 oz can tomato paste 15 oz. can tomato sauce 2 t. Italian seasoning
2 tsp oregano 2 tsp garlic Salt 1 tsp basil 1/4 tsp Tabasco Mix all
together - spread on unbaked crust - garnish as desired with cheeses
and meats, etc.
