Title: Chicken Waldorf Salad~
Categories: Low cal/fat, Poultry
Yield: 5 servings
2/3 c  Light mayonnaise
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Chicken or turkey --
-cooked/diced
2    Apples -- cored/chopped
2/3 c  Celery -- sliced
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
Combine mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Cover and chill.

Makes 5 cups.

Per 1 cup serving:  Calories 264; fat 13.9 g; cholesterol 65 mg;
carbohydrate 15.6 g; fiber 2.5 g; protein 20.7 g; sodium 439 mg.
