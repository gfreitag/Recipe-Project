Title: GIN PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 quarts
6 c  Water
2 c  Sugar
48 oz Pineapple juice
48 oz Grapefruit juice
8 oz Cherries, maraschino; undrnd
2 c  Gin
33 3/4 oz Ginger ale; chilled
Combine water and sugar in a large saucepan; bring to a boil, and stir
until dissolved.  Cool.

Combine sugar mixture, juice, and cherries; chill.  Add gin and
ginger ale just before serving.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, July, 1980. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
