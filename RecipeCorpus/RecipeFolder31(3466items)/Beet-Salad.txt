Title: Beet Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 pk Lemon Jello
1/2 ts Salt and a dash of pepper
1 c  Boiling water
3/4 c  Pickled beet juice
1/3 c  Sugar
Dissolve Jello in boiling
Water.  Add beet juice,
Sugar, salt and pepper.
When cool, add:
1 ts Ground horseradish
2 ts Grated onion
3/4 c  Diced beets
3/4 c  Diced celery
