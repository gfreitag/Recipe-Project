Title: Beet Perfection Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Unflavored gelatin
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Shoestring beets
2 c  Cold water
1/2 c  Vinegar
2 ts Prepared horseradish
2 c  Finely chopped cabbage
In a saucepan combine gelatin, sugar, and salt.  Drain beets,
reserving liquid (1 cup).  Blend beet liquid into gelatin mixture;
stir over medium heat till gelatin is dissolved.  Remove from heat;
add water, vinegar and horseradish.  Chill till partially set; fold
in drained beets and cabbage. Turn into 5 1/2 cup ring mold; chill
till firm. Unmold onto salad greens or cabbage leaves.  Serve with
mayonnaise, if desired. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On
