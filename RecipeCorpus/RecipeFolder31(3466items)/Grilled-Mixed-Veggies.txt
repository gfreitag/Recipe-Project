Title: Grilled Mixed Veggies
Categories: Vegetables, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 lb Veggies (new potatoes,
-yellow or zucchini squash,
-sweet pepers)
3 tb Olive oil
1 tb Basil; chopped
1 tb Thyme; chopped
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/4 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Mustard; dry
1/8 ts Paprika
1 cl Garlic; minced
If using potatoes, halve and cook, covered, in a small amount of
boiling water for about 10 minutes.  Drain.  Prepare other veggies as
needed. Prepare seasoning mixture and brush on prepared vegetables.
Preheat gas grill.  Place vegetables on a piece of heavy foil or on
grill rack. Cover and grill until tender.  Turn occasionally and
brush with seasoned mixture. 257 calories and 10 grams fat.  Source:
BH & G Gas Grill Cookbook Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   06-22-95
