Title: Lemon Herb Seasoning Salt Substitute
Categories: Low cal/fat, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
4 1/2 tb Dried basil
3 1/4 tb Dried oregano
1 1/2 tb Finely ground black pepper
1 1/2 tb Dried onion flakes
1 1/2 tb Whole celery seed
1 1/4 tb Powdered basil
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Grated lemon rind
Place all ingredients in a medium bowl.  Toss gently with spoon until
well blended just before using.  (For a more lemony flavor, add fresh
lemon juice).  Store in an air-tight container in a cool, dry, dark
place for up to 6 months.  Makes 1 cup.

This seasoning is good on fish, poultry and salads.
