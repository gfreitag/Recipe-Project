Title: Steamer Seasoned Mushrooms
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
12 ea Whole mushrooms
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
Wash mushrooms. Place in rice bowl. Sprinkle with Italian seasoning.
Add 1/3 cup water to reservoir, cover and steam for 8 to 9 minutes.

Serves 2

Source: Rival Automatic Steamer And Rice Cooker Booklet Typos by
