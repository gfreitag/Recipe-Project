Title: Friendship Cake Cream Cheese Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
3 oz Cream cheese -- softened
1 lb Confectioner's sugar
1/2 c  Margarine -- softened
1 ts Vanilla extract
Mix thoroughly at medium speed.  Cake and frosting may be frozen.
