Title: Amerigo's Clam Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A
2 3/4 tb Garlic, chop
3 tb Olive oil
8    Clams -- chop
1 c  Clam juice
Parsley -- chop
Salt and pepper
1 c  Tomatoes; skin, seed, crush
In a saute pan brown garlic in olive oil. Add clams and saute for 1
minute. Add clam juice and simmer. Add parsley, salt and pepper to
taste and tomatoes. Simmer for 5 mins to reduce. Source: Tony
Cortese, Amerigo's Restaurant, NYC.
