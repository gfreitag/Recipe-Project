Title: Pinana Sorbet
Categories: Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 1 quart
2 lg Ripe bananas, peeled and cut
Into 2 inch pieces
1 c  Ripe fresh pineapple
OR
Fresh pack canned, crushed
And drained
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Simple sugar syrup
MMMMM------------------------SIMPLE SYRUP-----------------------------
2 c  Water
2 c  Sugar
To make the simple sugar syrup:  Simmer the water and sugar until
sugar is totally dissolved.  This will make two cups of syrup.  Cool
before using and store in the refrigerator.

Puree bananas, pineapple, lemon juice and syrup in a food processor
until nearly smooth.

Pour the mixture into a chilled ice cream maker and follow
manufacturers directions until mixture is semisolid.  Serve
immediately.
