Title: Rhubarb and Pineapple Jam
Categories: Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
8 c  Rhubarb, cut up
2 c  Chopped pineapple
4 c  Sugar
Mix all together and boil till jam stage, about 30 minutes.  Pour
into hot jars and seal.
