Title: Steamer Smothered Sole
Categories: Seafood, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Sole fillets
1/2 c  Green onion; finely chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper; finely chopped
1 c  Mushrooms; sliced
1 sm Tomato; chopped
1/4 ts Dill weed
Arrange fish fillets in single layer in steamer basket. In order
listed, top with all remaining ingredients. Add 1/2 cup water to
reservoir, cover and steam for 10 to 12 minutes.

Serves 4

Source: Rival Automatic Steamer And Rice Cooker booklet Typos by
Dorothy Flatman 1995
