Title: Beef and Rice Fiesta
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Rice
Yield: 3 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion, sliced
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
7 oz Whole kernal corn
1 ts Salt
1 ts Chili powder
1 1/2 c  Minute rice
1    Green pepper
1 md Tomato
Brown beef in skillet leaving meat in large chunks.  Add onion and
cook over medium heat until tender.  Add 1 1/2 cups of water, soup,
corn, salt, and chili powder.  Bring to a boil.  Stir in minute rice.
Add tomato cut in eights and green pepper cut in strips or rings.
Cover and simmer for 5 minutes.  Fluff with a fork. Randy Rigg
