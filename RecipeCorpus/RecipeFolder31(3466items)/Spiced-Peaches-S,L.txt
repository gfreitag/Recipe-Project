Title: Spiced Peaches S/L
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
2    29 oz cans peach halves
1 ts Cornstarch
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Grated orange rind
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
1/8 ts Ground allspice
Drain peaches, reserving syrup. Combine syrup, cornstarch, and
remaining ingredients in a small Dutch oven; bring to a boil,
stirring constantly. Reduce heat; add peaches, and simmer 1 minute.
Let cool 30 minutes; then chill.

Great for picnics.

Recipe from Lynette Maginnis in April, 1991 "Southern Living". Typis
by Jeff Pruett, including that one.
