Title: Pastry Crackers^
Categories: Snacks, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Pastry trimmings
Grated cheese
OR
Chopped nuts
Roll trimmings into a rectangle. Sprinkle with cheese or nuts, fold
ends to center. Sprinkle again, fold again. Roll out to 1/4" thick.
Cut in strips, twist them and bake in a hot oven.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
