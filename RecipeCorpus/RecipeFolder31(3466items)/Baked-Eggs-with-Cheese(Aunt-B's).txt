Title: Baked Eggs with Cheese(Aunt B's)
Categories: British isl, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
4    Eggs
1 oz Butter
Pepper and salt
4 oz Cheddar cheese
2 tb Cream or top of milk
Grate about 1 oz of the cheeseand with a very sharp knife cut the
remainder into wafer-thin slices.Spread the butter all over the
bottom of a fire-proof dish.Cover with the thin slices of
cheese.Break the eggs carefully on to the bed of cheese being
carefully to not to disturb the yolks.Season with salt and pepper,put
a spoonful of the cream on the top of each egg.Sprinkle over the
grated cheese.Bake for 15 minutes in a hot oven 425 degrees F.(regulo
7)Brown under the grill.
