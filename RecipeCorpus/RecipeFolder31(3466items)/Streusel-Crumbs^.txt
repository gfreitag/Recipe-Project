Title: Streusel Crumbs^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Butter
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Flour
1/4 c  Dry cake crumbs
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Cream butter and sugar; add flour, finely ground cake crumbs (you can
use bread crumbs if you don't have any cake crumbs.) and cinnamon.
Mix till crumbly and sprinkle on top of dough.
