Title: Golden Dijon Barbeque Ribs
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
4 lb Pork Spareribs
1 c  Liquid Honey
3/4 c  French's Dijon Mustard
2    To 3 Tbsp Durkee Hot
Sauce
1  Tbsp   soya sauce 2 tsp onion powder 1/4 tsp garlic powder

To prepare ribs: Cut ribs into serving size pieces. Place in large
saucepan and cover with water. Bring to boil. Reduce heat; cover and
simmer about 1 hour, or until the meat is tender. Drain ribs. Brush
Golden Dijon Barbeque Sauce generously over the ribs. Barbeque or
broil 4-inches from the heat about 5 minutes per side. Baste ribs
again with sauce just before serving. Makes 6 servings.

To make Golden Dijon Barbeque Sauce: In medium saucepan combine honey,
dijon mustard, hot sauce, doy sauce, onion and garlic powders. Bring
mixture to a boil. Reduce heat, simmer, uncovered, 15 to 20 minutes;
stir occasionally. Refrigerate any leftover sauce. ( Great for BBQ
chicken too!).
