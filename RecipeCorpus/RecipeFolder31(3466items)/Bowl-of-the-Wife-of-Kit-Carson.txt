Title: Bowl of the Wife of Kit Carson
Categories: Tex/mexican, Soup/stew, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------YIELDS: 6 SERVES---------------------------
1 qt Chicken stock/broth
2 c  Chicken, precooked &
-chopped coarsely
1 1/2 c  Cooked rice
16 oz Can of garbanzo beans,
-drained
2 x  Chipotles en adobado,
-rinsed, seeded & chopped
1 x  Pinch of oregano
2 c  Monterey Jack cheese, cubed
-1/2"x1/2"
2 x  Avocados, peeled & cubed
Combine stock, chicken, rice, beans, and peppers in a large saucepan
over medium heat. Bring to a simmer, stirring occasionally. Add
oregano. Equally divide cheese and avocado among the bowls and ladle
small amounts of soup into each bowl. There should be more solids
than soup in each. Serve immediately. From: Bon Appetit's Dinner
Party Cookbook, 1983 Knapp Publishing. Posted by Joell Abbott 6/95
