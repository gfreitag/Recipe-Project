Title: Tomato Herb Pasta
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 2 servings
1 1/2 c  All purpose flour
2/3 c  Tomato paste
1 ts Garlic powder
2 tb Minced fresh parsley
2 tb Minced fresh basil
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients until dough forms a ball.
Turn dough out onto a floured surface & knead till smooth & pliable
like bread dough, dry but not cracking.

Place dough in a bowl, cover with plastic wrap & let rest for at
least 30 minutes.  Shape & cook dough as desired.
