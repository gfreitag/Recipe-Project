Title: Vierge Dressing
Categories: British isl, Dress/stuff
Yield: 600 ml
2 1/2 c  Extra virgin olive oil
1/2 oz Coriander seeds
1 bn Tarragon
12    Black peppercorns
4    Shallots
2    Garlic cloves
1 pn Sea Salt
Preparation:

Crush the coriander seeds, black peppercorns and garlic cloves. Chop
the shallots.

1. Warm the olive oil with the coriander seeds in a pan. Place the
tarragon, peppercorns, shallots, garlic and salt in a 600ml screw top
jar, then pour in the oil and coriander. Screw on the lid and leave
to marinate for one week, shaking the bottle daily.
