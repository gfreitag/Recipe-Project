Title: Light And Hot Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Ripe plum tomato,
-seeded/small
1/4 c  Shallots or red
-onions, diced
1 ts Minced fresh red chili,
-seeds & ribs removed,
-depending upon desired
-hotness
1/2 c  Fresh lime juice
1/4 c  Rice vinegar
Salt, to taste
Freshly ground black
-pepper, to taste
2 tb Fresh basil or cilantro
-leaves, chopped
1 ts Fresh chives, snipped
Mix all the ingredients together and use within 1 to 2 hours.  Serve
alongside grilled snapper for generous spooning on top.    Yield:  1
cup.

Per tablespoon:  5 calories, no fat.
