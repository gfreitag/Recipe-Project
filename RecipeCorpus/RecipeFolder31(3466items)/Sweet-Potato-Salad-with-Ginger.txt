Title: Sweet Potato Salad with Ginger
Categories: Salads, Snacks, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 lb Sweet potatoes; cooked, cube
4    Celery stalks; sliced
1/4 c  Parsley; chopped
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Honey
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Ginger; grated
Combine dressing ingredients. Add to sweet potato and celery. Chill
well. To cook sweet potatoes in microwave; pierce and cook for 20 min
on high. Source: Kraft Booklet Typed by: Sharon Verrall
