Title: Cilantro Puree
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 /3 cup
1/4 c  Cilantro leaves
2 md Garlic clove(s)
1/2 md Jalapeo pepper, seeded
2 tb Dried plain breadcrumbs
3 tb Dry white wine
Salt and pepper to taste
Put cilantro, garlic and chile in a mini-chopper and blend until well
mashed. Work in the breadcrumbs until incorporated. Gradually blend
in the wine and season with salt and pepper. Spread on grilled
vegetables or chicken sandwiches.

Food and Wine June 1995
