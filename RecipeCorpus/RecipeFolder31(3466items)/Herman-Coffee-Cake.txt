Title: Herman Coffee Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Breads, Herman, Sourdough
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Herman
2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
2/3 c  Oil
1 tb Mayonnaise
1 c  Chocolate chips
1 c  Nuts
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Melted butter
TOPPIMG Bake in a 350 oven for at least 30 minutes. Mix all
ingredients and put in greased 9 x 13 pan and top with the topping
mixture.
