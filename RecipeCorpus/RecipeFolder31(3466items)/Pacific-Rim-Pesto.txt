Title: Pacific Rim Pesto
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Side dish, Appetizers, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 cup
3 ea Garlic cloves, chopped
3 ea Green chiles, chopped
2 oz Roasted peanuts, coarsely
-- chopped
1 oz Mint, coarsely chopped
2 oz Cilantro, coarsely chopped
1/2 ea Lemon, juiced
Salt
Combine garlic, chiles, peanuts, mint, cilantro, lemon juice & salt
in a blender or food processor.  Keep the pesto in a clean jar in the
refrigerator until needed.  Use within a few days or freeze.

Marlena Spieler, "The Flavor of California"
