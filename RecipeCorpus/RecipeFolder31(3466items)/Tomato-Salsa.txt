Title: Tomato Salsa
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
7 qt Tomatoes(peeled and
Chopped)
4 c  Mild green chiles(chopped)
5 c  Onion(chopped)
1/2 c  Jalapenos(seeded/chopped)
6    Garlic cloves(chopped)
2 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Salt
1 tb Pepper
2 tb Cumin
3 tb Oregano
2 tb Cilantro(chopped)
Combine all ingredients except cumin, oregano and cilantro in large
pot and bring to a boil, stirring frequently.  Reduce heat and simmer
10 minutes. Add spices and simmer another 20 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Ladle not into clean pint jars, leaving 1/2" headspace.
Adjust lids and process in boiling water canner for 20 minutes.
