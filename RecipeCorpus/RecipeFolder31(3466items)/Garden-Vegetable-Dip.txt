Title: Garden Vegetable Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Spring vegetable with herbs
Soup mix -- 0.9oz
1 c  Mayonnaise or salad
Dressing
1 c  Sour cream
Stir together soup mix, mayonnaise and sour cream in medium bowl.
Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours. Yield: about 2 cups.
