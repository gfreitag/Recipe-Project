Title: Easy Does It Meatloaf
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1    Dried onion soup mix
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1    Can chicken and rice soup
Combine meat, dried onion soup and undiluted chicken rice soup.  Mix
well and form into a loaf.  Cook at 350 for 1 1/2 hours.
