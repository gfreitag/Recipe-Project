Title: Spicy Crab and Wine Sauce - Great Chefs
Categories: Basics, Salsa/sauce, Masterchefs, New orleans
Yield: 1 batch
Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)
1/4 ts Pepper, cayenne
1/2 c  Cream
1 ea Bay leaf
4 ea Crabs, cleaned, not cooked
2 oz Butter
2 tb Shallots, minced
2 ea Thyme, sprigs OR
1/2 ts Thyme, dried
Chop the crabs up in rough pieces and cook them in butter with
shallots, thyme and parsley until crabs turn color.

Add wine, salt, pepper, cayenne and cream.  Heat to boiling, add
the bay leaf, and reduce until thick.

Strain into another pan and reduce again until the consistency of
whipping cream.

Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions
