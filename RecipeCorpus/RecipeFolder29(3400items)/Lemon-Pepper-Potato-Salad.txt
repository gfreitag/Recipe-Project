Title: Lemon Pepper Potato Salad
Categories: Low cal/fat, Side dish, Vegetables, Salads, Potato
Yield: 8 servings
Dressing
1/3 c  Light sour cream
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 ts Lemon pepper; or to taste
2 ts Lemon juice
6 md New potatoes; sliced
3    Eggs; hard boiled
1/4 c  Celery; sliced
1/4 c  Green onions; sliced
2 sl Fat free ham or
- sandwich meat
Do not peel potatoes.  In saucepan over med high heat, cook sliced
potatoes in small amount of water until fork tender, about 12
minutes. Drain well.

Slice the eggs and reserve one for garnish.  In large bowl, combine
warm potatoes, two of the sliced eggs and vegetables and ham.

For Dressing:

In small bowl, combine all dressing ingredients, blend well.  Spoon
over salad mixture; toss gently to coat.

Cover, refrigerate until chilled.  Just before serving, garnish with
remaining sliced egg.

NOTES : Makes 8 half cup servings
