Title: Agliata Per Pasta (Garlic & Walnut Sauce for Pasta)
Categories: Main dish, Italian, Pasta, Salsa/sauce, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
3 md Garlic cloves, chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Black pepper
6 tb Olive oil
2 sl Stale bread, crusts removed
2 tb Stock, warmed
1 c  Walnuts, toasted
1/2 c  Parsley, stems trimmed
Soak the bread in the stock & then squeeze out any excess moisture.
Combine the bread with the walnuts, parsley, garlic, salt & pepper in
a food processor.  Process, adding the olive oil in a very slow
stream until you have a thick paste.  Toss with your favourite pasta
& serve hot.

Carol Field, "Italy in Small Bites"
