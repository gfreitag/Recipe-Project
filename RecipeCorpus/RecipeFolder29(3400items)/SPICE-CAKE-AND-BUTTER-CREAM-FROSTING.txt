Title: SPICE CAKE AND BUTTER CREAM FROSTING
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-------------------------SPICE CAKE------------------------------
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1 c  Buttermilk
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter or margarine
-- softened
4    Eggs; beaten
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
MMMMM-------------------BUTTER CREAM FROSTING------------------------
1 ts Vanilla
4 c  Confectioners' sugar
3 tb Milk
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
-- softened
SPICE CAKE:

For the cake, in a mixing bowl, cream sugar and butter. Add eggs; beat
well.  Combine dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture alternately
with buttermilk.

Mix well. Pour into a greased and floured 13x9x2" baking pan. Bake at
350 degrees for 35-40 minutes or until the cake tests done. Cool.

For frosting, cream shortening and butter in a mixing bowl. Add
vanilla. Gradually beat in sugar.  Add milk; beat until light and
fluffy. Frost cake.
