Title: Spare Ribs & Apple Dressing
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Cornbread cubes
1/2 ts Sage
1/4 ts Rosemary,crushed
Salt
Pepper
3 tb Chili sauce
3 lb Pork spare ribs
1/4 c  Water
1 c  Celery,chopped
1    Onion,chopped
1 tb Butter or margarine
1    Apple,small,3/4" pieces
1. Place ribs on a rack in a 13x9x2-inch pan or baking dish; add
water. 2. Cover tightly; bake in preheated 350'F. oven 1 1/2 hours.
3. Meantime, cook celery and onion in butter in a large skillet 2 to
3 minutes. 4. Add apple pieces; continue cooking 2 to 3 minutes
longer. 5. Stir in cornbread cubes, sage and rosemary. 6. Remove ribs
from pan; season with salt and pepper. 7. Pour off and reserve
drippings; add 1/2 cup drippings to bread mixture; tossing lightly to
combine. 8. Shape dressing into 4 equal mounds; place on a rack in
the baking dish. 9. Combine chili sauce with 3 tablespoon drippings;
brush both sides of ribs. 10. Place ribs on top of dressing mounds;
bake, uncovered, 30 minutes, brushing with remaining sauce after 15
minutes. Submitted By BOBBI ZEE   On   04-16-95
