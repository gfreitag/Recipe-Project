Title: Noodle Pudding
Categories: Kids, Rice, Puddings, Pcake/waffl, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 sm serving
1 ts Butter;
1 c  Cottage cheese;
A handful raisins;
8 oz Uncooked flat egg noodles;
1 tb Sugar;
1 ts Cinnamon;
Put a medium-size pot of water on to boil.  Cook the noodles until
tender. While the noodles are cooking, combine sugar and cinnamon in
a small bowl. When noodle are done, drain them well. Transfer them to
a bowl. Add butter and mix well.  Add cottage cheese and raisins and
mix. Transfer to serving bowls, sprinkle with cinnamon-sugar and eat.
Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Food Section, Mar. 23, 1995 +
"Pretend Soup" by Mollie Katzen and Ann Henderson. Brought to you and
