Title: Rum Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Milk
3 tb Flour*
1 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Rum
*Note: Level Tablespoons. Cream butter and sugar until light and very
fluffy and white.  Add the rum and mix. Blend milk and flour and cook
over low heat until thick. Cool, then add to other mixture. Blend
well.
