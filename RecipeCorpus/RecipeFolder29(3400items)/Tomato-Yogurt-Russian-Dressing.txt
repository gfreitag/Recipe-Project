Title: Tomato Yogurt Russian Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
4 tb Tomato puree
1 c  Nonfat plain yogurt
Salt
Pepper
1 ts Dry mustard
2 ts Sugar
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Red wine vinegar
By Joyce Goldstein:  "The ideal dressing for a summer tomato,
cucumber, and red onion salad, it would also be nice on just lettuce,
even a wedge of iceberg.  And it gives you yet another potato salad
option."

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and whisk together well.

Serving size = 2 tablespoons 21 CALORIES, 0.2g FAT, 0.4mg
CHOLESTEROL, 72mg SODIUM (without added salt)

From "Eat More, Weigh Less," by Dr. Dean Ornish, 1993, HarperCollins
books, ISBM 0-06-016838-2 (cloth).
