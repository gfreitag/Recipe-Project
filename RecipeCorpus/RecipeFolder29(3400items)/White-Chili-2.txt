Title: White Chili 2
Categories: Poultry, Chili, Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
2 ts Ground cumin
1 1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
4 c  Diced cooked chicken breasts
3 c  Grated Monterey Jack cheese
1 lb Large white beans
6 c  Chicken broth
2    Cloves garlic, minced
2 md Onions, chopped (divided)
1 tb Oil
8 oz Cans chopped green chilies
Combine beans, chicken broth, garlic and half of the onions in a
large soup pot and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer until
beans are very soft, 3 hours or more.  Add more chicken broth if
necessary.  In a skillet, sautÂ‚ remaining onions in oil until tender.
Add chilies and seasonings and mix thoroughly.  Add to bean mixture.
Add chicken and contine to simmer 1 hour. Serve topped with grated
cheese. Makes 8-10
