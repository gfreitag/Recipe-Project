Title: 279671 Ground Turkey Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1 md Squash, sliced zucchini or
Long neck yellow.
1 cn Durkee onions, Mozzarella
(opt )
1 lb Ground turkey
1 1/2 c  Rice
2 cn Cream of mushroom or celery
Soup
1/2    Bag frozen beans
Spray casserole dish with Pam.  Mix all ingredients raw together,
except for onions (Put on top).  Also cheese if desired.  Bake at 350
degrees for 1 1/2 hours.  Let set 10 minutes before serving. Serves 2
to 4. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-23-95
