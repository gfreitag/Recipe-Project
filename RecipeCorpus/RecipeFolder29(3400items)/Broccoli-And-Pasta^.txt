Title: Broccoli And Pasta^
Categories: Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
Corkscrew pasta, cooked and
Drained
4 c  Broccoli florets, steamed
Until crunchy
2 tb Lowfat yogurt
3 tb Grated Parmesan
1 md Onion, chopped
2 ts Minced galric
2 ts Dried basil
3 tb Chicken stock
1/4 c  White wine
8 oz Shell or tricolored
Saute onion, garlic and basil in stock until soft. Combine pasta,
yogurt, broccoli, onion mixture and cheese. Toss to combine. Eat.

Per serving: Calories: 267 Protein: 11g Carbohydrates: 46g Fat: 4g
Sodium: 119mg Cholesterol: 4mg
