Title: Baked Bean Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 5 servings
1    Bay Leaf
Seasoned Salt, to taste
Pepper -- to taste
1 lg Can Baked Beans
W/o tomato sauce
1 ts Red Wine
Lemon Slices
1 md Onion
1    Clove Garlic
1    Green Pepper
1 tb Bacon Fat
1 c  Fresh Tomatoes, chopped
2 c  Consomme
2    Sprigs Parsley
Saute the onion, garlic, and green pepper in bacon fat until tender.
Then add the tomatoes, consomme, celery, parsley, bay leaf, and
seasonings.

Add the beans and simmer about 30 minutes, then put through a sieve
(or use your blender), reheat, and add the wine just before serving.
Serve in heated bowls with lemon slices on top.

Source:  My Own Cook Book From Stillmeadow and Cape Cod, Glady Taber,
1972. ISBN-0-397-00877-5
