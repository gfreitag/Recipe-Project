Title: Poopcorn Snack^
Categories: Snacks, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
2 c  Air popped popcorn
1/2 c  Oat bran flakes
1 ts Onion powder
Toss while popcorn is still warm.

Per serving: Calories: 154 Protein: 4g Carbohydrates: 30g Fat: 2g
Sodium: 95mg Cholesterol: 4mg
