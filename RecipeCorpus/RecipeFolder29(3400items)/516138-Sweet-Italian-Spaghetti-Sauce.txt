Title: 516138 Sweet Italian Spaghetti Sauce
Categories: Italian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 8 servings
16 oz Tomato sauce
2 lb Spaghetti noodles
1 c  Chopped fresh tomato
1 qt Water
1 tb Salt
1 tb Pepper
1/2 lb Grated Parmesan cheese
2 lb Sweet Italian sausage
1 md Onion, minced
2    Garlic cloves, minced
8 oz Minced black olives
1 c  Minced green pepper
4 oz Olive oil
4 oz Tomato paste
Brown sausage, onions, green peppers, and olives in the oil and
drain. Add tomato paste, tomato sauce, chopped tomato and water mix
well and simmer on low for 2 1/2 hours adding liquid as needed, let
thicken to desired preference. Pour over noodles and sprinkle with
Parmesan cheese as desired. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
03-18-95
