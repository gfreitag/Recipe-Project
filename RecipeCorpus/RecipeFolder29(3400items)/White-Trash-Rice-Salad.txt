Title: White Trash Rice Salad
Categories: Salads, Rice, Seafood, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
-pepper
1 tb Chopped chives
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1 tb Olive oil
1 tb Wine vinegar
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 c  Cooked, chilled rice
1/2 c  Chopped king crab or lobster
1/2 c  Slivered Virginia ham
1/2 c  Finely chopped celery
2 ea Finely chopped hard boiled
-eggs
Salt and freshly ground
Mix all ingredients together, and allow to chill in the fridge for at
least 2 hours. Origin: Adapted from White Trash Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, April/95.
