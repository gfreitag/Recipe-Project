Title: Peanut Butter Surprise Balls
Categories: Kids, Cake/cookie
Yield: 25 balls
Raisins, peanuts, semisweet
-chocolate pieces or
-candy-coated chocolate
-pieces;
1/2 c  Peanut butter;
1 tb Honey;
1/2 c  Nonfat dry milk powder;
2 tb Toasted wheat germ;
1/2 c  Cornflakes;
To make the dough, put peanut butter and honey in a mixing bowl.  Use
a wooden spoon in stir until well mixed.  Add the dry milk powder,
sesame seeds and wheat germ.  Stir till well mixed.  Put the
cornflakes in a pastic bag and use your hands to crush the
cornflakes. Sprinkle the crushed cornflakes on a sheet of wax paper.
Scoop out a small spoonful of the dough and shape it around a raisin,
chocolate piece or candy. Shape dough into a ball, then roll in
cornflake crumbs. Make more balls until all the ingredients are gone.
Store in a tightly covered container in the refrigerator.
