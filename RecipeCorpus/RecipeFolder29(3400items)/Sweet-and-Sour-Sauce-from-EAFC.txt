Title: Sweet and Sour Sauce from EAFC
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chinese
Yield: 3 cups]
3/4 c  Sugar
Red food coloring
1 1/4 tb Cornstarch
1 c  Water
1 tb Shredded Chow Chow pickles
1 tb Shredded ginger
1/2 c  Tomato ketchup
3/4 c  White vinegar
1 tb Salt
Mix ingredients; simmer for 3 to 4 minutes.

From _The Encyclopedia of Asian Food and Cooking_  by Jacki Passmore
