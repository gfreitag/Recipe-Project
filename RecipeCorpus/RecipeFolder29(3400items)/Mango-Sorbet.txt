Title: Mango Sorbet
Categories: Fruit, Desserts, Masterchefs
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Juice, lime, fresh
2 c  Mango, pureed
2 tb Syrup, simple *
* Simple syrups are made from 1 cup of water and 1 cup of sugar
mixed together.

Strain the pureed mangos through a chinois (sieve) and add simple
syrup.  Add the lime juice.  Using an ice cream maker, chill and churn
until frozen.

Source: Great Chefs of San Francisco, Avon Books, 1984
Chef: Mark Miller, The Fourth Street Grill, Berkeley, CA
