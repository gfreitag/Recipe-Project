Title: World's Best Deviled Eggs
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Side dish, Appetizers
Yield: 24 eggs
1 ts Dry mustard;
1/2 ts Paprika;
1/2 ts - 3/4 ts dill; dried
1/4 ts Salt; to taste
1/4 ts Garlic powder;
Milk
12    Eggs; hard-cooked peeled
-sliced in half
1/2 c  Mayonnaise;
2 tb Onion; finely chopped
1 ts Fresh chives; snipped
1 ts Parsley; snipped
Place yolks in a bowl and mash with fork.  Add mayonnaise, onion,
chives, parsley, dry mustard, paprika, dill, salt, pepper and garlic
powder. Stir. If necessary, stir in a tiny bit of milk to help the
consistency, Spoon mixture into egg white halves.  Cover and chill
until serving time. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Easter Food
Section, Apr. 9, 1995 + "Best-Loved Community Recipes". Brought to
you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
