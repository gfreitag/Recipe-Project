Title: 1000122 Microwave Corn Pudding
Categories: Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Flour
2    Eggs, beat well
1 cn Cream style corn
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Sugar
Mix flour, sugar and salt; add eggs, corn and milk; pour into a
buttered baking dish.  Sprinkle top with nutmeg and butter.  Bake in
microwave on High 6 minutes, stir, turn dish then bake 2 more minutes
or until set. Oven directions:  Use 3/4 c. milk and bake at 350
degrees for 1 hour. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-25-95
