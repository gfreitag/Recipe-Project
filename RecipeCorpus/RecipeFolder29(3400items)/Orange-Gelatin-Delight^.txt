Title: Orange Gelatin Delight^
Categories: Desserts, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 c  Dry sherry
Seedless oranges or sliced
Bananas, optional
1    Envelope unflavored gelatin
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Frozen orange juice
Concentrate
Soften gelatin in 1/4 cup water. When softened, heat until dissolved.
Add remaining ingredients, pour into 4 individual dishes and chill at
least 3 hours.

Per serving: Calories: 30 Protein: 4g Carbohydrates: 4g Fat: trace
Sodium: 0mg Cholesterol: 0mg
