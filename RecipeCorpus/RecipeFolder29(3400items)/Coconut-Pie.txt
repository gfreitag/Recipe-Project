Title: Coconut Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Unsweetened desiccated
-coconut
1    Baked 9 inch pie shell
Lime slices
3    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 cn (300ml) sweetened condensed
-milk
Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 30-35 minutes

Be sure to use condensed milk, which is sweeter and thicker than
evaporated milk.

In medium bowl, using an electric mixer, beat eggs and vanilla until
frothy.  Gradually beat in condensed milk until smooth.  Stir in
coconut until well blended.  Pour into pie shell.  Bake in 350 F oven
30-35 minutes, until filling is light golden brown, shielding edges
of pastry with foil if they brown too much.  Serve warm or cold
garnished with lime slices.

Per serving: 550 calories for 6, 412 for 8.  33 g fat for 6, 25 g for
8 good source of calcium for 6.
