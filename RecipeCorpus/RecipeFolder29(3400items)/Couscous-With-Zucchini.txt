Title: Couscous With Zucchini
Categories: Fatfree, Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Your favorite seasonings,
(curry, garlic or Mrs.
Dash)
2 c  Couscous
3 sm Zucchini sliced
1 sm Onion, sliced
3 c  Veggie stock or soy veggie
Chicken flavor
Bring stock, onion & zucchini to a boil, simmer 15 or 20 minutes. Add
couscous. Turn off heat. Let stand 5 or 10 minutes. 2 as main dish or
4 to 6 as side dish

P.s.  this is pretty basic, use your favorite veggies, mushrooms,
carrots, green beans, anything.  You can't go wrong.  Quick and easy
