Title: Cauliflower Scallop
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 lb Cheddar cheese, shredded
And divided
Salt and pepper to taste
Buttered bread crumbs
1 md Cauliflower
1 c  Coarsley chopped corn beef
3 tb Melted butter
3 tb All purpose flour
1 1/2 c  Milk
Wash cauliflower and break into flowerettes.  Cook, covered, in small
amount of water about 10 minutes.  Drain and place in a 2 quart
casserole; sprinkle with corn beef.  Combine butter and flour; cook
over low heat until bubbly.  Gradually add milk.  Cook and stir
constantly until smooth and thickened.  Season with salt and pepper.
Add three fourths of the cheese to sauce, stirring until melted. Pour
over corn beef. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes; sprinkle with
remaining cheese and bread crumbs. Bake an additional 10 minutes.
