Title: 639236 Spam Surprise
Categories: Spam, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
-kernel corn or
-1 cn green beans
1 cn Mushrooms, stems & pieces
-Macaroni, cooked desired
-amt.
1 cn Spam luncheon meat
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c  Diced cheddar cheese
1 c  Milk
1/4 ts Garlic powder
-Optional:  1 can whole
Heat 1 can cream of mushroom soup, 1 cup milk and 1/2 to 1 cup diced
cheddar cheese until it resembles a medium thickness sauce. Add 1 can
diced Spam luncheon meat and 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder. Add
optionals, if desired.  Heat to simmer.  Pour mixture over cooked
macaroni. May be heated in a 325 degree oven for 30 to 45 minutes
until ready to serve. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-01-95
