Title: BLUE CHEESE DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
-Nowakowski
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
4 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Onion powder
1 pk (4 oz.) blue cheese,
-crumbled
"I tasted this tangy
-dressing for the first
-time at a friend's house.
-She
Gave me the recipe, and now
-I make it every week.  I
-always keep some in my
Refrigerator. It taste much
-better than bottled blue
-cheese dressing and is
A snap to make." - Barbara
In a bowl, combine the first 7 ingredients.  Stir in the blue cheese.
Cover and chill at least 2 hours.  Store in the refrigerator. Yields:
2 cups From:  "Taste of Home" Magazine, April/May 1995
