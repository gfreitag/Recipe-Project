Title: Crowd Chicken Casserole
Categories: Poultry, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Slivered almonds
5 c  Cheddar cheese
(20 oz) shredded
-- divided
2 c  Mayonnaise
2 c  (1 lb.) sour cream
5 c  Crushed potato chips
10 c  Diced cooked chicken
10 c  Chopped celery
2 bn Green onions w/ tops
-- sliced
2 cn (4 oz) chopped green chiles
5 cn (3/4 oz) pitted ripe olives
-- drained and sliced
Combine the first six ingredients.  Add 2 cups cheese.

Mix mayonnaise and sour cream; add to chicken mixture and toss. Spoon
into two greased 13x9x1" baking dishes.  Sprinkle with chips. Top
with remaining cheese. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 20-25
minutes or until hot.
