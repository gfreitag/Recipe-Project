Title: Baking Mix, Low Sodium
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Low-sodium baking powder
1 1/4 c  Vegetable oil
9 c  Flour
1/4 c  Sugar
Directions:

Sift together flour, baking powder and sugar twice in a large bowl.
Add oil slowly, using pastry blender, until mixture is texture of
coarse corn meal. Store in a tightly covered container at room
temperature or in the refrigerator. Mix will keep two months at room
temperature, longer in the refrigerator. Spoon mixture into a cup
lightly and level with a knife or spatula.

Yield: 12 Cups 1 Cup: 571 Cal, 10 gm Prot, 23.6 gm fat, 79 gm carb, 0
Chol, 2 mg Sodium To be used in other recipes.
