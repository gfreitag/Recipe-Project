Title: 279658 Crabmeat Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Light cream
-Cayenne pepper to taste
-Extra stuffing mix for top
-of casserole
-Parmesan cheese
1    (8 oz.) can crabmeat
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Pepperidge Farm herb
-stuffing mix
1    Onion, chopped
Mix everything together and top with extra stuffing mix and Parmesan
cheese.  Bake in small casserole dish in a 350 degree oven for 30
minutes. Serve warm with crackers.
