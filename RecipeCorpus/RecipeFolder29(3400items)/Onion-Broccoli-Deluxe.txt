Title: Onion Broccoli Deluxe
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
Dash of pepper
1 c  Milk
3 oz Cream cheese, cut up
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
2    10 oz. boxes frozen cut
Broccoli
2 c  Frozen small onions
4 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1/4 ts Salt
Cook broccoli as directed on the box.  Melt 2 tb. butter; add flour,
salt, pepper; add milk all at once.  Cook until thick and bubbly,
reduce heat, stir in cream cheese, until smooth.  Place in 1 1/2
quart casserole. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.  Add bread
crumbs, brown in remaining butter.  Then bake for an additional 15
minutes. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   04-06-95
