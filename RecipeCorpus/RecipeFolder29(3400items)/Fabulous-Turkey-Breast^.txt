Title: Fabulous Turkey Breast^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 9 servings
1/2 ts Tamari or low sodium soy
Sauce
3 lb Turkey breast
2 ts Cornstarch
3 oz Frozen orange juice
Concentrate
3 cl Garlic, minced
1/4 ts Paprika
1 tb No sugar added marmalade
MMMMM-------------------------MIXED WITH------------------------------
2 tb Cold water
Combine orange juice, garlic, paprika and tamari or soy sauce in a
bowl. Cut two pieces of plastic wrap at least 18" ling and overlap to
create one big piece. Put the turkey in the center and cover well
with orange juice mixture. Wrap turkey VERY VERY tightly with the
wrap, uaing an additional piece of necessary. Repeat wrapping step
with foil. Preheat oven to 350F. Bake turkey, placed on a baking
sheet 3 hours. do not open or peek until time is up. Drain juice from
turkey, mix wtih cornstarch and water mixture in a pan and cook until
thick and bubbly.

Per serving: Calories: 119 Protein: 19g Carbohydrates: 5g Fat: 3g
Sodium: 23mg Cholesterol: 55mg
