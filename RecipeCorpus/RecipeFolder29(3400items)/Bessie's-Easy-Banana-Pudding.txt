Title: Bessie's Easy Banana Pudding
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
16 oz Cool whip
8    Bananas; sliced
12 oz Vanilla wafers
2    4 oz boxes instant vanilla
-pudding
3 c  Cold milk
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
Combine the pudding and milks.  Beat until smooth.  Fold in the cool
whip. Layer the pudding, wafers and bananas in a 9 x 13 pan.  Chill
overnight. Source: Beverly Hillbillies Cookbook
