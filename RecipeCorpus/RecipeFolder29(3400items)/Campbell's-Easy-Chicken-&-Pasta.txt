Title: Campbell's Easy Chicken & Pasta
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Frozen seasoned pasta &
Vegetable combination - 16oz
1 tb Oil
1/2 c  Water
1 lb Skinlees, boneless, chicken
Breasts cut up into chunks
1 cn Campbells Cream of Chicken
Soup (10-3/4 oz can)
In skillet, heat 1 Tbsp oil. Add chicken and cook until browned. Add
soup, 1/2 cup water and vegetable combination. Cover and cook 5
minutes or until vegetables are tender, stirring often.
