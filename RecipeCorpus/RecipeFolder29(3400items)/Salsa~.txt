Title: Salsa~
Categories: Diabetic, Dips/spread, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 c  Fresh finely shredded lemon
Pepper to taste
1 tb Fresh basil -- =OR=
1 tb Dried leaf basil
Hot red pepper flakes --
Optional
1 sm Onion -- chopped
1 sm Green bell pepper --
Chopped
1 sm Red bell pepper -- chopped
1    Tomato -- chopped
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice -- =OR=
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl.  Let stand 20 minutes before
serving.  Add hot pepper flakes if desired. Makes 2 cups. Yield: 16
(2-tb) serving.

Food Exchange per serving:  FREE; CAL: CAL: 9g; CHO: 0mg; PRO: 0g;
SOD: 3mg; FAT: 0g;
