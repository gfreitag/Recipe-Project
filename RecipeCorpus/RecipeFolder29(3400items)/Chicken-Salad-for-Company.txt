Title: Chicken Salad for Company
Categories: Salads, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
12 sl Thick-cut bacon
-cooked crisp and crumbled
3/4 c  Sour cream
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
4 1/2 tb Dijon mustard
4 1/2 tb Raspberry vinegar
2 tb Dill, finely chopped
3    Whole chicken breasts
-poached and
-cut in bite-size pieces
1 lg Sweet red pepper
-cut in julienne strips
2 lg Heads broccoli
-cut in florets
Combine chicken with red pepper in a large bowl.

In a vegetable steamer, steam the broccoli for 2-3 minutes, or until
it just starts to get tender. Add it to the chicken and mix it
gently. Mix in the crumbled bacon.

Whisk together the sour cream, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, raspberry
vinegar and dill. Pour the dressing over the chicken and vegetables
and mix well. Chill salad for at least 2 hours before serving.
