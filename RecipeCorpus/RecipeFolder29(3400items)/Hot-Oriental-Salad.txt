Title: Hot Oriental Salad
Categories: Salads, Main dish, Vegetables, Asian
Yield: 6 servings
-- (1-inch pieces)
1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms, sliced
3/4 c  Spinach
-- washed and torn in pieces
2 tb Kikkoman Soy Sauce
1 tb Vegetable oil
2    Garlic cloves; minced
1/2 ts Minced fresh ginger root
2    Celery stalks
-- cut into julienne strips
8 c  Chinese cabbage chunks
Heat oil in large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.  Add
garlic and ginger; saute until garlic is lightly browned.  Add
celery; stir-fry 2 minutes.  Add cabbage and mushrooms; stir-fry 2
minutes. Add spinach; stir-fry 2 minutes.  Add soy sauce and toss to
coat vegetables. Serve immediately.
