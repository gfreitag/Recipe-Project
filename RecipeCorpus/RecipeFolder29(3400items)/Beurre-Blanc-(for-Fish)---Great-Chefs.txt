Title: Beurre Blanc (for Fish) - Great Chefs
Categories: Basics, Salsa/sauce, Masterchefs, New orleans
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 lb Butter, at room
-- temperature
2 oz Shallots, minced
3/4 c  Wine, white
Reduce white wine and shallots over high heat until the wine is
half its original volume.

Remove from heat and whisk in butter bit by bit until each
addition is emulsified.

Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions
Box 71112, New Orleans, Louisiana - 1983
Chef Daniel Bonnot, Louis XVI Restaurant,
Marie Antoinette Hotel, New Orleans
