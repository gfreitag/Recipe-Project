Title: No-bake Confetti Cereal Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 bars
5 c  Crisp rice cereal
1 1/2 c  Diced mixed dried fruit
10 oz Pkg marshmallows
1/3 c  Butter, cut up
In large saucepan over low heat, cook and stir marshmallows and butter
until marshmallows melt and mixture is smooth.  Cook and stir 3
minutes more; remove from heat.  Stir in cereal and fruit until well
coated. With wet spatula, press into a greased 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan.
Cool; cut in 2" squares.
