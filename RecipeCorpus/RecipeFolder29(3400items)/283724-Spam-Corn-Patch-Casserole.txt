Title: 283724 Spam Corn Patch Casserole
Categories: Spam, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Spam, cubed
-Sharp cheese, grated or
Shaved
Paprika
2 1/2 c  Egg noodles
1 cn Whole kernel corn, drained
1 cn Cream of vegetable soup
3/4 c  Milk
1 ts Salt
Cook noodles in 2 quarts boiling water for 10 minutes.  Drain and
pour into deep 9 inch casserole.  Mix in corn, soup, milk, salt and
Spam. Top with cheese and paprika.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30
minutes. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-01-95
