Title: Hot Dog Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Garlic powder
Salt to taste
1 lb Lean hamburger
2 tb Paprika
2 tb Onion, finely chopped
1/2 ts Mustard powder
Brown meat and onions.  Mash meat into small bits.  Add other
ingredients and simmer for 1 hour, adding water as needed to make a
loose sauce. Use as a topping on hot dogs with mustard and chopped
onions. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   03-28-95
