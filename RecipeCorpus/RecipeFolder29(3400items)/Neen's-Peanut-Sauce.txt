Title: Neen's Peanut Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Nuts/grains
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
1/4 c  Soy sauce
3 tb Lime juice
1 1/2 c  Smooth peanut butter
3/4 c  Unsweetened canned
-coconut milk
2 tb Corn oil
1/2 c  Onion(s), minced
2    Garlic clove(s), minced
1 tb Crushed red pepper flakes
1 ts Ground cumin seeds
Heat the oil in a skillet. Over low heat, saut the onion, red-pepper
flakes and cumin for 10 minutes. Don't let the onion or garlic brown.

Stir in the soy sauce and lime juice. Gradually stir in 1 cup of the
peanut butter and  cup of the coconut milk. Stirring constantly, cook
the mixture over low heat for 5 minutes.

Remove from the heat and spoon into a mixing bowl; let cool. With an
electric mixture, beat in the remaining  cup of peanut butter and the
remaining  cup of coconut milk. Continue to beat until the mixture is
very smooth.
