Title: Glo's Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 40 servings
1/2 c  Raisins
2    Egg whites
1 c  Crushed pineapple,
Unsweetened, drained
1 md Orange, juice and grated
Peel of
2 tb Canola oil
2 c  Rolled oats, regular
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Nutmeg or allspice
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Apple juice concentrate
Combine dry ingredients in one bowl and wet in another. Combine and
let stand to absorb, 2-3 minutes. Drop by teaspoons onto a spryed
cookie sheet. Bake 12-15 minutes at 350F. until browned.

Variation: Add carob chips for the kids.

Per serving: Calories: 53 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 11g Fat: trace
Sodium: 20mg Cholesterol: 0mg
