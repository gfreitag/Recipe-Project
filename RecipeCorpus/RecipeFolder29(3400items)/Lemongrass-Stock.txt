Title: Lemongrass Stock
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 cups
10    Sprigs cilantro
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Coriander seeds
6 c  Cold water
4    Stalks lemongrass
-dried outer leaves removed
Cut into 3" lengths
1    Yellow onion, thinly sliced
8    Thin slices ginger
In a stockpot, combine all the ingredients. Bring to a boil, reduce
heat to a simmer and cook uncovered for about 40 minutes. Pour the
stock through a strainer, pressing as much liquid as possible from
the vegetables before discarding them.
