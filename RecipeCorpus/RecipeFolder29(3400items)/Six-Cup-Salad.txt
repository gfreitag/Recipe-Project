Title: Six Cup Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
1 ct Sour cream
1 c  Pecans
1 c  Chunk pineapple
1 c  Mandarin oranges
1 c  Angel Flake coconut
Drain liquid from pineapple and oranges.  Mix with remaining
ingredients. Prepare salad at least two hours before serving. Randy
