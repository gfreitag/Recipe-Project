Title: 998637 Corn Vegetable Medley
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Sliced carrots
1 c  Cauliflowerets
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
-(optional)
1 cn Campbell's NEW Golden Corn
-Soup
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Broccoli flowerets
In saucepan, heat soup and milk to boiling, stirring often.  Stir in
vegetables. Return to boiling.  Cover; cook over low heat 20 minutes
or until vegetables are tender, stirring often.  Stir in cheese. Heat
through. Makes 6 servings.  If desired substitute with 1 bag (16
ounce) frozen broccoli, carrots and cauliflower for fresh vegetables.
(You would reduce cooking time 15 minutes).
