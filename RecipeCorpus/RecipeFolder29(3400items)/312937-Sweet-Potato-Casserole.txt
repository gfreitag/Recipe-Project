Title: 312937 Sweet Potato Casserole
Categories: Spam, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
-or mixed fruits
1 ts Corn starch
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Margarine
1    (12 oz.) can Spam
1    (18 oz.) can sweet potatoes,
-drained
1    (8 oz.) can fruit cocktail
Arrange sweet potatoes in shallow casserole dish.  Make 5 cups
partially through loaf of Spam and place loaf in dish.  Drain fruit,
saving juice. Combine corn starch and brown sugar.  Stir into juice
and add margarine. Heat to boiling; stir in fruit and simmer 5
minutes. Spoon over slit loaf and sweet  potatoes. Bake at 350
degrees for 25 minutes, basting with juice during baking.
