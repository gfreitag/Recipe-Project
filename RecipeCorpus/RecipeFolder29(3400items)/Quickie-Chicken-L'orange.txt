Title: Quickie Chicken L'orange
Categories: Poultry, Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Sugarless orange marmalade
1 tb Minced garlic
1 tb Cornstarch, optional
2 tb Cold water, optional
1    Chicken, skinned and cut in
Eight pieces
3 oz Orange juice concentrate
1 tb Low sodium soy sauce
Preheat oven to 325F.Combine orange juice, soy sauce, marmalade and
garlic and pour over chicken in a baking dish. Cover and bake until
chicken is tender. If desired, mix cornstarch and water; stir into
drained sauce. Pour over chicken and serve.

Per serving: Calories: 208 Protein: 28g Carbohydrates: 16g Fat: 3g
Sodium: 163mg Cholesterol: 51mg
