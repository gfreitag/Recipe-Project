Title: World Cafe's Basil Pesto
Categories: Poultry, Pasta, Relishes
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Pine nuts, toasted
1    Clove garlic
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Fresh basil leaves, packed
Combine oil, basil, pine nuts & garlic in blender or food processor
and process until creamy.  Used in World Cafe's Spicy Bow-tie Chicken
Pasta Recipe.
