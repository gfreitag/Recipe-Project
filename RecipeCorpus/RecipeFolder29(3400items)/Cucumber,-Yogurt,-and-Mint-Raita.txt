Title: Cucumber, Yogurt, and Mint Raita
Categories: Salads, Indian
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Mint leaves, fresh, minced
1 ts Salt
1    Garlic clove, crushed
2 1/2 c  Cucumber, peeled and thinly
-sliced
2 c  Yogurt
Mix all ingredients together GENTLY, and refrigerate for 3 to 5
hours. The salt will break down the yogurt somewhat, and you will
have a thin, flavorful, and refreshing yogurt salad dish.
