Title: Mushroom Meatballs
Categories: Meats
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Minced onion
1 tb Minced parsley
1    Slightly beaten egg
1/4 ts Salt
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c  Water
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
1 tb Shortening
Blend soup and 1/2 cup of water.  Measure out 1/4 cup of soup mixture
and combine with ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, parsley, egg and
salt. Shape into meatballs about 1 1/2 inches in diameter and brown
in 1 tb. of shortening in a skillet.  Add remaining soup mixture,
cook over low heat for 15 minutes.  Tastes great over noodles. Randy
