Title: AM's Honey Butter
Categories: Condiments, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 cups
1/3 c  Honey
1 c  Butter
Whip butter in a food processor or blender and add honey slowly while
continuing to mix.

Serve on rolls, muffins or bread.
