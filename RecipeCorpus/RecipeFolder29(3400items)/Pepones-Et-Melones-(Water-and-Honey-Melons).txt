Title: Pepones Et Melones (Water and Honey Melons)
Categories: Ancient, Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Pepper, freshly ground
x  A little bit of Liquamen, OR
-a dash of salt
x  Poleiminze, Silphium,
-vinegar if desired
2 ea Honey melon and water melon
-both peeled, diced and
-stoned
x  Passum
x  A little bit of honey
1 tb Parsley, minced
MMMMM------------------------INSTRUCTIONS-----------------------------
Cook diced melons in a pan together with spices and herbs until done.
Sometimes Silphium is added.
