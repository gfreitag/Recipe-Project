Title: Chicken Cordon Blue 1
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 8 servings
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1/2 c  Flour (or more)
Salt, Pepper, Parsley
1 c  Chicken broth
1 c  White wine if desired
8 lg Chicken breasts, pounded
-to 1/4" thickness with
-meat hammer
8 sl Swiss cheese
8 sl Boiled ham
Place cheese and ham on chicken breast (trim to fit so cheese doesn't
render out and burn). Fold in sides of chicken and roll tightly.
Secure with toothpicks or cotton string.  Dip rolls in egg, then in
flour and seasonings.  Brown rolls in 1/4 inch hot oil.  Remove to a
baking dish. Add chicken broth and wine (if desired), cover loosely
with foil.  Bake at 350 for 30 min.
