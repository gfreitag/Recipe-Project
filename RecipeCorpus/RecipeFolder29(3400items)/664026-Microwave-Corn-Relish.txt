Title: 664026 Microwave Corn Relish
Categories: Condiments, Microwave, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
6 tb Sugar
2 ts Salt
2    Minced jalapeno chilies
1/2 ts Cumin
3 c  Whole kernel corn
1 c  Chopped red pepper
1 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
In 3-quart microwave-proof bowl combine:  Cover with plastic, vent.
Microwave on HIGH 8 minutes, stirring once.  Spoon into four 8-ounce
jars. Tighten lids of jars; chill.  Makes 4 cups.  Store in
refrigerator. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-22-95
