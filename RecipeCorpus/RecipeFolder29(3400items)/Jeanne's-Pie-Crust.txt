Title: Jeanne's Pie Crust
Categories: Countyfairs, Pastry
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Shortening, Butter-Flavored
1    Egg
1/2 c  Milk
1 ts White Or Cider Vinegar
3 c  All-Purpose Flour
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Cocoa Powder
1 ts Ground Cinnamon
In a large bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, salt, cocoa and
cinnamon. Cut in the shortening, using a pastry blender or your
fingers, until mixture is crumbly. Stir in the egg. Slowly add the
milk and vinegar, forming a large ball. Wrap in plastic wrap and
chill for at least 1 hour. Roll out on lightly floured surface to
make one 9-inch pie or two 9-inch pie bottoms.

Submitted by Jeanne Kemper to section on the SHELBY COUNTY FAIR AND
HORSE SHOW of Shelbyville, KY. Fair dates: 1st full week after the
4th of July.
