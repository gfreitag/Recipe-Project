Title: Hawiian Ham Salad Pitas
Categories: Side dish, Sandwich, Meats
Yield: 2 servings
3 tb Mayonnaise
2 tb Sliced green onions
3 ea Whole wheat pita bread
Pockets cut in half
Lettuce lettuce
1 cn Crushed pineapple well drain
(8 oz)
1 cn Underwood Deviled Ham spread
(4 1/4 oz)
1/4 c  Pecan pieces
In small bowl, combine all ingredients except pita bread and lettuce.
Refrigerate to chill. Fill pita pockets with ham salad and shredded
lettuce.

Soruce: Underwood Ham ad
