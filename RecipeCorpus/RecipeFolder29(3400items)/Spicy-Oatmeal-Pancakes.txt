Title: Spicy Oatmeal Pancakes
Categories: Mixes, Breakfast
Yield: 18 pancakes
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking Powder
2 c  Spiced Oatmeal Mix
2 c  Milk, Hot
1/2 c  Margarine, melted
Combine mix, hot milk and margarine.  Combine rest of ingredients
beaten eggs,  flour and baking powder.  After everything is mixed
