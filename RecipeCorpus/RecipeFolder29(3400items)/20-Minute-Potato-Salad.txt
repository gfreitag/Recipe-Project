Title: 20-Minute Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
-sliced
1/2 c  Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
1 tb Coarse ground mustard
2 lb Quarted medium red potatoes
1/4 c  Sliced green onions
1 sm Red or green bell pepper;
Recipe by: Kraft ad, 92/04 Preparation Time: 2:00

Add potatoes to boiling water; cook 12 minutes or until tender. Drain.

Toss with remaining ingredients; refrigerate. Makes 6 servings.
