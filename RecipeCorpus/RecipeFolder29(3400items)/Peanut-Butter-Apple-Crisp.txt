Title: Peanut Butter Apple Crisp
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 10 servings
2/3 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Salt
6 tb Margarine; chilled
6 tb Peanut butter; smooth or
-chunky
4 lg Apples; peeled & sliced
1/2 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Oats; rolled quick
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease a 13 x 9 pan.  Mix together apples and
white sugar in large bowl.  Spread mixture evenly in bottom of
prepared pan. In another bowl, combine brown sugar, flour, oats,
cinnamon and salt. Cut in margarine and peanut butter until crumbly
mixture forms. Sprinkle over apples and bake for 40 to 50 minutes,
until apples are tender. Serve warm or cold.  Source: Delicious
