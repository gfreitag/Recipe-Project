Title: Quaker Yuletide Toffee Squares
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 dozen
1 tb Vanilla
1/2 ts Salt (optional)
2 c  Semi-sweet chocolate morsels
2/3    Nuts, chopped (optional)
4 1/2 c  Quaker Old Fashioned Oats,
Uncooked
1 c  Brown Sugar, Firmly Packed
3/4 c  Butter, melted
1/2 c  Corn Syrup
Heat oven to 400 degrees. Grease 15 x 10 jelly roll pan. Comine oats,
brown sugar, butter, corn syrup,, vanilla & salt - mix well. Firmly
press mixture into prepared pan. Bake aout 18 minutes or until
mixture is brown & bubbly. Remove from oven. Immediately sprinkle
morsels evenly over toffee. Let stand 10 minutes. Spread chocolate
evenly over toffee. Sprinkle with nuts. Cool completely - cut into
squares. Store tightly covered in cool dry place.
