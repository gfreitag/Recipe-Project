Title: Creamed Corn Chicken^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
3 lb (to 4) chicken, cut in eight
Pieces
1 ts Poultry seasoning
Paprika
Whole wheat bread crumbs
1 cn (16oz0 no salt added creamed
Corn
1 bn Green onions, chopped
1 c  Skim milk
1    Egg white
2 tb Flour
Preheat oven to 350F. Mix corn, onions, milk, egg white and flour.
Pour into a 13 X 9" baking dish. Lay chicken on top and sprinkle with
poultry seasoning and paprika. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and bake
until chicken is tender, about 1 hour 20 minutes.

Variation: For color, add some pimento strips.

Per serving: Calories: 340 Protein: 38g Carbohydrates: 33g Fat: 5g
Sodium: 125mg Cholesterol: 60mg
