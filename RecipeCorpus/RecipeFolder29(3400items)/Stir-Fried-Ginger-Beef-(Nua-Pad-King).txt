Title: Stir-Fried Ginger Beef (Nua Pad King)
Categories: Asian, Vietnamese, Meats, Main dish, Stir fry
Yield: 2 servings
-or-
1 ts Dried dill
1/2 ts Each salt and pepper
1 tb Sesame seeds
1 c  Onions; sliced
1 c  Carrots; sliced
3 tb Garlic; minced
6    Guavas
1 tb Oil
1 c  Mushrooms; chopped
1 tb Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Green olives; chopped
2 tb Parsley; chopped
4 ts Fresh dill weed; chopped
Heat oil in wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.  Add garlic;
stir-fry until browned, 20 to 30 seconds.  Add beef and stir-fry until
browned.  Add ginger, fish sauce, and soy sauce; stir-fry for 1
minute. Mix in oyster sauce and sugar.  Add bell pepper and onion.
Cover, remove from heat and let stand for 2 minutes.  Serve hot over
freshly-cooked rice.
