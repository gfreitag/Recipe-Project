Title: Do It Yourself Play-Doh
Categories: Kids
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Cream of Tartar
1 ts Salad Oil
Food Coloring
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Salt
1 c  Water
Mix together all ingredients and cook for 3 minutes over low heat.
Stir well.  Add food cloring.  Store in a plastic container.  Kids
love it and it is easier to clean up.
