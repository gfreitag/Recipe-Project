Title: Easter Egg Candy
Categories: Candies, Holiday, Potato, Nuts/grains, Chocolate
Yield: 2 servings
-sugar
1 ea - 8oz package of choclate
-chips
1/8 ea Cake paraffin
1 c  Hot mashed potatoes
2 tb Butter
1 c  Shredded coconut
1 ts Vanilla
3 ea - 1 lb boxes confectioner's
combine potatoes and butter.  Stir in coconut and vanilla.  Add sugar
gradually, mixing well after each addition.  Form into egg shapes,
using 1 tbps mixture for each egg.  Place on waxed paper.  Let harden
for 1-3 days. Melt chocolate over hot water, dip eggs into chocoalte,
using 2 spoons. Place on waxed paper to harden.  Chopped nuts,
candied fruits, or peanut butter may be used instead of coconut.
Origin: Women's Circle, for cooks on the go. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Jan/94
