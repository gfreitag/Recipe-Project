Title: Killer Mashed Potatoes
Categories: Potato, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 1 batch
Water
Salt and pepper
Hot mashed potatoes
1/4 lb Chicken fat
2 md Onion(s), sliced
1 sm Carrot, chopped
Render the chicken fat with the onions, carrots and a little water,
all set over moderately low heat. When the fat has melted completely
and the onions are a very dark brown, mix it all, along with lots of
salt and pepper, into hot mashed potatoes.
