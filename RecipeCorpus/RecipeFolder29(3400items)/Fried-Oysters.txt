Title: Fried Oysters
Categories: Seafood, Appetizers
Yield: 1 serving
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Hot Sauce
Seafood sauce for dipping
6    Oysters
1 lg Egg
Saltine Crackers
2 ts Mayo
Chuck oysters, reserve liquor.  Beat egg and add to reserved liquid,
add mayo and mix well.  Crush several crackers in a bowl; dip oysters
in the liquid and then in the crackers; place on a plate, cover, and
refrigerate for 1 hour. Crush more crackers; dip the oysters again in
the liquid and crackers, and refrigerate again for 30 minutes to set
the coating. Deep fry until golden, drain and serve with seafood
sauce. Submitted By JIM WELLER   On   04-05-95
