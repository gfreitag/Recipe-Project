Title: 999857 Creole Corn
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
-Green pepper
-Onion
1 c  Fresh okra
-Bacon
2 c  -Fresh corn
4 lg Fresh tomatoes
Fry bacon and add corn, tomato, green pepper, onion and okra. Cook 30
to 45 minutes so as not to be soupy.
