Title: Cincinnati Chili 2
Categories: Chili, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Allspice, ground
1/4 ts Cloves, ground
1/16 ts Red pepper, ground
1    Bay leaf
15 oz Tomato sauce
2 tb Cider vinegar
1/2 oz Chocolate, unsweetened
1 qt Beef broth
2 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Onion flakes
4 tb Chili powder
1 ts Cinnamon, ground
1 ts Cumin, ground
3/4 ts Onion, instant minced
1/2 ts Salt
Boil beef broth, add beef slowly, till meat separates into small
pieces. Cover and simmer 30min. Add remaining ingredients, bring to a
boil, and cover and simmer 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
Refrigerate overnight, skim off fat, reheat and serve.

From "The International Chili Society's Official Chili Cookbook"
