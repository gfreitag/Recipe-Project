Title: Light Chocolate Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Gran sugar
1 tb Water
1 oz Bakers chocolate melted
1/3 c  Evap  Milk
2/3 c  Crisco
Blend all ingredients in electric mixer and whip for 10 minutes until
fluffy.  This is an excellent soft fluffy frosting. From: Marcia
Leibowitz Date: 13 Feb 94
