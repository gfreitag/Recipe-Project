Title: Vegetable Horseradish Casserole
Categories: Vegetables, Bbq/grill, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
4 ts Horseradish
1/4 ts Lemon pepper seasoning
1/4 c  Bread crumbs; seasoned dry
1 tb Margarine; melted
2 c  Broccoli; flowerets
1 1/2 c  Cauliflower; flowerets
1 1/2 c  Carrots; thinly sliced
1/2 c  Mayonaise
2 tb Onions; finely chopped
In large mixing bowl combine broccoli, cauliflower and carrots.  For
horseradish mixture, in a small mixing bowl combine mayonaise, onion,
horseradish and lemon pepper seasoning.  Stir horseradish mixture into
vegetables.  Tear off 36 x 18" piece of heavy foil.  Fold in half.
Spray center of foil with Pam.  Place mixture in center of foil.
Combine bread crumbs and margarine; sprinkle over the vegetables.
Bring up two opposite edges fo foil and seal with a double fold. Fold
remaining ends to completely enclose the vegetables, leaving space
for steam to build. Preheat gas grill.  Place foil packet on grill
over medium heat. Cover and grill for 30 minutes or till tender. 204
calories and 17 grams fat. Source: B H & G Gas Grill Cookbook
