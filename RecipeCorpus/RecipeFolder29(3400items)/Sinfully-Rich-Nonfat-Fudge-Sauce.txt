Title: Sinfully Rich Nonfat Fudge Sauce
Categories: Chocolate, Low cal/fat
Yield: 7 servings
Assorted Fresh Fruit,
Cut Up -- optional
Cake (Your Favorite),
Optional
Nonfat Frozen Yogurt,
Optional
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Hershey's Cocoa
Or European Style Cocoa
1 tb Cornstarch -- Plus 1 tsp
1/2 c  Evaporated Skim Milk
2 ts Vanilla Extract
In small saucepan, stir together sugar, cocoa and cornstarch;
gradually stir in evaporated milk. Cook over low heat, stirring
constantly with whisk, until mixture boils; continue cooking and
stirring until thickened and smooth. Remove from heat; stir in
vanilla. Serve warm or cold with assorted fresh fruit, cake or nonfat
frozen yogurt, if desired. Cover; refrigerate leftover sauce. Serves
7.

Nutritional Information Per Serving (2 Tbs sauce) 80 Calories 2 gm
Protein 19 gm Carbohydrate 0 gm Fat 0 mg Cholesterol 25 mg Sodium 55
mg Calcium
