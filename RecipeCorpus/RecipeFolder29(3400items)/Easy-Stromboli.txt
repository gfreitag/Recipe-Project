Title: Easy Stromboli
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
8 oz Cheddar cheese, grated
8 oz Mozzarella, grated
1    Loaf frozen bread dough
1/2 lb Slicing pepperoni
1/2 lb Hot sausage, fried & chopped
Thaw bread dough; roll out on floured surface until quite thin.
Spread with pepperoni, sausage, Cheddar and Mozzarella. Roll up
carefully and place on cookie sheet. Bake at 350 until golden. Serve
hot or cold. Submitted By BOBBI ZEE   On   04-16-95
