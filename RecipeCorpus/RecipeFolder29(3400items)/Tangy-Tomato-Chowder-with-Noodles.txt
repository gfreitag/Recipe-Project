Title: Tangy Tomato Chowder with Noodles
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/3 oz Pkg chicken noodle soup
1/2 ts Tarragon
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan
16 oz Can Italian-style tomatoes
1 1/2 c  Water
1    Chicken bouillon cube
Drain tomatoes, reserving liquid, and chop. Combine liquid with water
and bouillon cube in saucepan and bring to boil. Add soup mix,
tarragon, and chopped tomatoes and bring to boil. Lower heat and
simmer 10 minutes. Serve hot, passing cheese separately.
