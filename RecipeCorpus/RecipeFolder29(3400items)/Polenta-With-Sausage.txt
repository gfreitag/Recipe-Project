Title: Polenta With Sausage
Categories: Italian
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn (16 Oz)
1/2 c  Dry Red Wine
1    Bay Leaf
Polenta (Per Favorite Recipe
For 6)
Tomatoes -- cut up
1 lb Italian Sausage Links, Mild
Cut in 1 1/2" pieces
1/4 lb Pancetta Or Bacon -- cut up
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
1/4 c  Green Pepper -- chopped
1/4 c  Carrot -- chopped
In large skillet cook sausage, pancetta or bacon, onion, green
pepper, and carrot about 15 mins or till meat is browned and
vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally. Drain off fat. Stir in
undrained tomatoes, wine and bay leaf, 1/2 cup water, 1/4 cup water,
1/4 tsp salt, and dash pepper. Boil gently, uncovered, for 35 to 40
mins or until desired consistency, stirring occasionally. Discard bay
leaf. Serve sauce over desired form of Polenta. Makes 6 servings.
