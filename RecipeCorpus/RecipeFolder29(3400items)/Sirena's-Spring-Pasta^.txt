Title: Sirena's Spring Pasta^
Categories: Pasta, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn (6 1/8oz) tuna packed in
Canola oil, drained and
Flaked
Black pepper
1/4 c  Cookin water from pasta
1/2 lb Bowties, cooked
1/2 lb 1% fat cottage cheese
2 ts Nonfat sour cream
1 tb Parmesan cheese
1 ts Dried basil, crumbled
1 c  Thawed frozen peas
Puree cottage cheese, sour cream, Parmesan, basil and cooking water.
Combine with pasta and stir in peas, tuna and pepper.

Per serving:  There was no number of servings listed in the book....
Calories: 300 Protein: 29g Carbohydrates: 33g Fat: 4.7g Sodium: 18mg
Cholesterol: 3g
