Title: Minnie Pearl's Anykind Fruit Pie
Categories: Countyfairs, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
4 tb Butter, melted
2 c  Lightly Sweetened Fresh
Fruit
OR-
Canned Fruit, drained
1 c  All-Purpose Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking Powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Milk
2 ts Vanilla Extract
* Preheat oven to 400 F.

1. Sift together the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Add the
milk and vanilla and mix until smooth.

2. Melt the butter in an 8- or 9-inch pie plate. Pour on the batter
but DO NOT MIX. Top with fruit.

3. Bake in center of oven for 10 mins. Lower temperature to 350 F.
Bake about 50 mins longer, until the pie bubbles and the crust is
lightly browned.

NOTE: The batter will rise up through the fruit and make a crust.
Blueberries make an especially grand pie according to Minnie.

Submitted by Minnie Pearl Fulkerson to section on the APPALACHIAN
FAIR in Gray, TN. Fair date: 3rd full week in August.
