Title: Spiced Oatmeal
Categories: Mixes, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
Milk
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Spiced Oatmeal Mix
Boil water stir in oatmeal mix.  Cook 1-2 min.  Serve with milk.

Note: I found that I need to use only 1/3 cup oatmeal mix.
