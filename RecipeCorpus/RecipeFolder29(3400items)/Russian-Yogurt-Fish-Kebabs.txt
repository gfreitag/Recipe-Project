Title: Russian Yogurt Fish Kebabs
Categories: Fish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
Paprika
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Instant onion
1 ts Salad oil
1 lb Cod or halibut in 1 1/2"
Cubes
3 tb Low-fat yogurt or sour
Dressing
1 ts Salt (or seasoned salt)
Put fish cubes in a small non-reactive bowl and add lemon juice,
yogurt, onion and salt; stir to coat fish evenly. Cover and
refrigerate 30 minutes for flavors to blend. Thread fish on skewers
and brush lightly with oil. Sprinkle liberally with paprika. Broil or
barbecue in preheated grill about 4 inches from heat source, turning
frequently, until fish flakes easily, 8 to 10 minutes.
Date: 04-08-95 (11) Cooking
From: Pat Stockett
