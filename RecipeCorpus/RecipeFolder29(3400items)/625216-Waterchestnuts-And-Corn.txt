Title: 625216 Waterchestnuts And Corn
Categories: Vegetables, Mushroom
Yield: 4 servings
-Salt & pepper to taste
1 cn Water chestnuts, drained &
-chopped (8 oz.)
6    Fresh mushrooms, sliced
6    Green onions, chopped
3 tb Butter
1 cn Whole kernel corn (17 oz.)
Saute mushrooms and onions in 2 tablespoons butter until soft. Stir in
corn, salt and pepper, water chestnuts and 1 tablespoon butter. Heat
through and serve.  What a delicious combination of flavors - you're
sure to make this one over and over.
