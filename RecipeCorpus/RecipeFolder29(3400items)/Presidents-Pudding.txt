Title: Presidents Pudding
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 3 servings
7 oz Can of pimentos, chopped
4    Hard cooked eggs, chopped
1 c  Buttered cracker crumbs
2 c  Medium cream sauce
1/2 lb Cheddar cheese, grated
Layer in a 8 x 8 x 2 inch pan, ending with some sauce on top.  Bake
at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.  May be made the night before and
cooked before serving.  Tastes good with sausage, fresh fruit and
coffee cake. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   04-16-95
