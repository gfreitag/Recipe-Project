Title: Blonde Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Chocolate chips
Nuts
2/3 c  Shortening
1 lb Brown sugar
3 ea Eggs
2 2/3 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
Melt shortening in sauce pan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve.
Cool mixture slightly. Add eggs beating in 1 at a time. Mix in
remaining ingredients. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Cut into
bars.
