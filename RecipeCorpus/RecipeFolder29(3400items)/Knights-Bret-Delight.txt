Title: Knights Bret Delight
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 20 servings
1 cn Cool whip medium container
1 pk French vanilla pudding
1 x  Box of yellow cake mix
1 cn Cherry pie filling
Mix cakes per instructions on box.  Split batter into 3 7x11 pans.
Bake till spongy.  mix pudding per instructions.  Pour pudding into
one of the pans.  Take one cake out and put over the top of pudding.
Pour Cherry pie filling over 2nd layer.  Place last cake over top of
cherry filling. Frost the top with cool whip evenly.  chill and serve.
