Title: Pineapple-Anisette Sauce
Categories: Desserts, Salsa/sauce, Condiments
Yield: 1 cup
2 tb Anisette liqueur
2 c  Fresh pineapple chunks;
-about 1/2 med. pineapple
1.  In a food processor or blender, or with a chef's knife, mince the
pineapple pulp until it is well crushed but not yet a fine puree.

2.  Place the crushed pineapple and Anisette in a small non-reactive
saucepan.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce the heat, and simmer until the
pineapple is well wilted, about 5 minutes.  Chill before serving, or
cover, refrigerate, and use withing 3 days.

Makes 1 1/2 cups.

from The Well-Filled Tortilla by Victoria Wise and Susanna Hoffman
