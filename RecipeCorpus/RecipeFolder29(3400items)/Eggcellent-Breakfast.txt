Title: Eggcellent Breakfast
Categories: Kids, Breakfast, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 friends
4    Eggs;
1 cn Cream mushroom soup;
1/2 c  Green pepper; chopped
1 tb Margarine; melted
(1).  Cook green pepper and margarine in a microwave-safe casserole 1
minute or until green pepper is tender. (2). In a medium bowl,
combine eggs and soup.  Mix well. (3). Add egg mixture to casserole
with green pepper. Cover. (4). Cook 4 to 5 minutes on high or until
eggs are cooked. Stir halfway through cooking.  Serves 4. Source: The
