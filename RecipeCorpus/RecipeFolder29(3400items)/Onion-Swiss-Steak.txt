Title: Onion Swiss Steak
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 servings
2 pk Onion soup mix
2 cn (10 oz) tomatoes
3 lb Round steak, 3/4" thick
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Cut steak into serving pieces, season with salt and pepper and place
into dutch oven.  Sprinkle onion soup mix over top and pour tomatoes
over all. Cover and cook over slow fire for 2 to 3 hours or until
meet is done and tender.
