Title: Caramel Nuts
Categories: Candies, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 cups
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Vanilla
2 c  California walnut halves
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Light cream
2 tb Corn syrup
Combine sugars, cream, and corn syrup; cook to soft-ball stage (236
F). Add butter and vanilla. Cool at room temperature, without
stirring, till lukewarm (110 F). Beat just until mixture begins to
lose gloss; add nuts and stir till well coated. Turn out on waxed
paper; sperate nuts with two forks.

Origin: Mrs. Ted Saladin, Monrovia, California

Source: Better Homes and Gardens, Dec'1955
