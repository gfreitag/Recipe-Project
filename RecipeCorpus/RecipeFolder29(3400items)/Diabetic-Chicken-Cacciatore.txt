Title: Diabetic Chicken Cacciatore
Categories: Diabetic, Microwave, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Pepper
2 lg Chicken Breasts
1 cn Tomato sauce
4 oz Mushrooms (sliced)
1/2 md Onion, chopped
Place chicken in 8x8" baking dish (if a whole chicken is used, use a
dish appropriate for one layer of chicken). Mix all other ingredients
and pour over chicken. Cover with waxed paper and microwave on high
for 9-10 minutes. Re-arrange chicken and recover with wax paper and
cook 6-10 minutes or until chicken is tender. Rest 8-10 minutes
covered to carry over cook.

This is really good served with cooked spaghetti noodles.

1 serving = 3 fat-meat exchanges
1/2 bread exchange
vegetables negligible
200 calories
8 g carbohydrate
22 g protein
9 g fat
