Title: Strawberry and Rhubarb preserves
Categories: Can/preserv, Fruit
Yield: 1 batch
2 qt Strawberries
1 qt Rhubarb,cut in small pieces
8 c  Sugar
Sprinkle rhubarb with sugar and rest 12 hr. Bring it to a boil
quickly. Hull the berries and add to the rhubarb. Simmer 15 min. Jar.
