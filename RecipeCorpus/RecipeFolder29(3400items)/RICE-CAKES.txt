Title: RICE CAKES
Categories: Side dish, Desserts, Pasta
Yield: 5 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Margarine, melted
1 c  Cooked rice (warm)
3 ea Eggs. seperated
1 c  Milk
1 tb Sugar
Beat egg yolks and add to warm rice and beat. Add all other
ingredients and mix together. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Drop
from spoon onto a griddle and brown on both sides. Serve with syrup
(Maple is best). Makes about 10 cakes.

Source: Mrs. Amos Showalter, Paramount, MD.
Memmonite Community Cookbook
by Mary Emma Showalter
