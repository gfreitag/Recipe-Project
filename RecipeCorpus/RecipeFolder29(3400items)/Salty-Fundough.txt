Title: Salty Fundough
Categories: Kids
Yield: 1 batch
2 ts Cream of tartar
2 tb Oil
Saucepan
1 c  Flour
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Salt
Mix all ingredients in the saucepan. Cook until thickened over low
heat. Cool. Then knead and explore dough freely.
This is a nice easy to to work with clay. Perfect for the little ones.
