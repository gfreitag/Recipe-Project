Title: 498807 Banana Kuchen
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Nuts, chopped
1/2 ts Cinnamon
CRUNCHY:--
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Soft oleo
1/4 c  Shortening
3/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 lg Banana, sliced
1/4 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Flour, sifted
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
SPICY  NUT:--
Cream together shortening and sugar.  Add milk, banana, and egg; beat
until smooth.  Add the sifted dry ingredients and blend just to mix.
Spread in 7 x 11 inch pan.  Sprinkle with one of the following
toppings: Spicy Nut - Mix together and sprinkle on kuchen and bake.
Crunchy - Mix together with fork until crumb like.  Sprinkle on
kuchen and bake. Bake at 375 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
