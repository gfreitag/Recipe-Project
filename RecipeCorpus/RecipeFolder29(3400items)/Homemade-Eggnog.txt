Title: Homemade Eggnog
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake
Yield: 1 servings
-with
1 ts Sugar
2 tb Powdered skim milk
1    Egg yolk
1/2 c  Whole milk
1/4 c  Cream
1/2 c  Ice cream
1    Egg white, beaten stiff
Blend all ingredients except for the egg white mixture. Fold in egg
white mixture. Flavor with cinnamon and nutmeg as desired.

538 calories, 17.8 gm. protein.
