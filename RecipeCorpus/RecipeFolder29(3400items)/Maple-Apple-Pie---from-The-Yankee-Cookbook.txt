Title: Maple Apple Pie   from The Yankee Cookbook
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart, Syrups, Fruit
Yield: 1 pie
1 tb Flour
2 tb Butter
Cream
Pastry for a 9" pie
1 cn Spam
1 1/2 qt Apples (tart); peeled and
-sliced thin
1 c  Soft maple sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cinnamon
Have pie pan lined with pastry. Put in sliced tart apples. Spread
over them maple sugar, salt, cinnamon, and flour; dot with butter.
Cover with perforated top crust; brush with cream and bake in a hot
oven (450 deg F) for 10 minutes; then reduce heat to moderate (350
deg F) and bake for 40 to 50 minutes longer. Makes 1 two-crust 9" pie.

From _The Yankee Cookbook_  by Imogene Woolcott pub. by Ives
Washburn, Inc. NY  1939,1963 Typos by Jeff Pruett
