Title: Potato Tikki with Almond-Cilantro Chutney
Categories: Appetizers, Indian, Snacks
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Cilantro, minced
1 1/2 tb Arrowroot
Salt & pepper
Olive oil for frying
1 c  Romaine lettuce, shredded
4 lg Potatoes
1/4 c  Almonds, chopped
1/4 c  Red bell pepper, minced
2 ea Jalapeno peppers, seeded &
-- minced
1/2 tb Lime juice
MMMMM------------------ALMOND-CILANTRO CHUTNEY-----------------------
Salt
Hot paprika
1/4 c  Almonds, chopped
3 tb Cilantro, chopped
3/4 c  Soy yogurt
Boil potatoes until fork tender.  Cool slightly, peel & force through
a coarse sieve to produce smooth mashed potatoes.  Add almonds, bell
pepper, jalapeno pepper, lime juice, cilantro & arrowroot.  Season
with salt & pepper & mix well.  Rub a film of oil on your hand.
divide mixture into 8 portions & flatten into smooth patties, 1/2"
thick.

Brush a seasoned skillet with a film of oil.  Place over low heat &
when hot, slip in the patties.  Fry slowly for 10 to 15 minutes per
side, or until a crisp crust forms.

CHUTNEY:  In a food processor, process nuts until they are powdered.
Add rest of ingredients & process until smooth & green.

Serve patties over shredded lettuce with a bowl of chutney.

Yamuna Devi, "Yamuna's Table"
