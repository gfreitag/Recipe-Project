Title: Mexican Black Bean Salad+
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1/2    Red bell pepper, seeded and
-diced
1    Green bell pepper, seeded
-diced
1/4 bn Celery, chopped
3 c  Black beans, (uncooked)
-cooked till tender
2    Bay leaves, (cooked with the
-beans)
1/2 lg Red onion, chopped in 1/3"
-dice)
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
3/4 tb Crushed garlic
3/4 tb Spike
1/2 ts Cayenne
1/8 c  Cilantro
1/2 tb Salt
1 1/4 tb Chili powder
1/16 c  Lemon juice
1/8 c  Red wine vinegar
1 tb Cumin
1 tb Coriander, ground
1 1/2 tb Paprika
5/8 c  Vegetable oil
Put your beans on to cook.

While beans are cooking, chop vegetables and make dressing.

Let beans cool and when cool, mix everything together. Put in covered
dish and place in refrigerator to let flavors blend for a couple
hours.

I got this wonderful recipe in the deli kitchen of my co-op.
