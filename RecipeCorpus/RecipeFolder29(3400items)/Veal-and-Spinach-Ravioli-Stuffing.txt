Title: Veal and Spinach Ravioli Stuffing
Categories: Pasta, Italian, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Spinach, chopped
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 tb Dry sherry
1/4 c  Parmesan, grated
1 c  Veal, cooked, minced
2 tb Shallots, finely chopped
1 ts Sage
1/4 ts Mace or nutmeg
Place all ingredients in a large mixing bowl and combine thoroughly.
Fill ravioli with mixture.
