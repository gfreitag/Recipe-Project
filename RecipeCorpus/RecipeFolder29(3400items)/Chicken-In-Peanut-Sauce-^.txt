Title: Chicken In Peanut Sauce ^
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Whole wheat flour
1 c  Diced carrots
1/2 ts Turmeric
3/4 c  Hot water
2 tb Chopped parsley
2 1/4    Llb chicken, quartered
1 ts Sea salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 tb Butter
1 c  Coarsley ground peanuts
Rub chicken pieces with salt and pepper. Melt butter in fry pan. Brown
chicken well, then cover pan and cook gently for 40 minutes, turning
once.

Sprinkle peanuts with flour, then add to chicken. Turn chicken pieces
in peanut mixture. Add carrots, turmeric and hot water. Simmer 10
minutes more or until sauce thickens. Adjust seasonings, sprinkle
with parsley nad serve.
