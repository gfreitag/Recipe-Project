Title: Summertime Pasta
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Olive oil
Wine vinegar splash
Salt and peper; to taste
1 lb Linguine
Romano cheese; grated
6    Tomatoes; ripe home-grown,
-peeled and diced
1 cl Garlic; minced
12    Basil leaves; minced
Parsley; spring, minced
Combine all ingredients except linguine and cheese.  Warm mixture
briefly to blend flavors.  Cook linguine according to package
directions until al dente, then top with tomato mixture and sprinkle
with cheese. Source: Delicious Developments
