Title: Shashlyk
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Dill seed
1 ts Sugar
15    Cherry tomatoes
15    Button mushrooms
12 sl Bacon
Hot cooked rice
1 1/2 lb Lamb cut into 1 in cubes
1/4 c  Dry white wine
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Oil
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Onion salt
1 tb Horseradish
Place lamb in shallow baking dish. Combine the next nine ingredients
and pour over lamb. Marinate in fridge 4 hrs or overnight When ready
to cook put lamb on skewer followed by a tomato and a mushroom and
another piece of lamb etc. Broil 4 in from heat or barbeque over hot
coals for 10-15 min turning and basting often with reserved marinade.
Serve over a hot bed of rice.
