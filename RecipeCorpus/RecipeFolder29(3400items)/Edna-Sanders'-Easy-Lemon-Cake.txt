Title: Edna Sanders' Easy Lemon Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Countyfairs
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Baking Powder
1 c  Cream
1 ts Lemon Extract
2 c  Doolsey
4    Easters
2 2/3 c  Dumplin' Dust
pn Salt
NOTE: Betty Sanders' mother-in-law, Edna made a little cookbook over
30 years ago called BABL GORMS IN BOONT, or GOOD FOOD IN BOONVILLE.
Here's a sample of the vocabulary she developed:

egg
milk
potato
sugar
sweet potato
easter
charl
boo
doolsey
cider doolsey boo
beat

* Preheat oven to 350 F.

1. Cream together the doolsey and easters until light and fluffy.

2. Sift thribs (3 times) dumplin' dust, salt and baking powder. To
this mixture add cream and creamed mixture. Add lemon extract and
dreek 3 mins. Pour into 2 greased and dumplin' dusted cake pans. Bake
for 25 to 30 mins. Cool on racks. Frost with your favorite frosting.

From the MENDOCINO COUNTY FAIR AND APPLE SHOW of Boonville, CA. Fair
Dates: 3rd weekend in Sept, Friday through Sunday.
