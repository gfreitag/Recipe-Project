Title: Company Ham Casserole
Categories: Casseroles

1 1/4 c  Chicken broth
3 c  Diced left over cooked ham
1 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
-cheese
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1 pk Thin spaghetti
2 tb Minced onion
6 tb Flour
2 c  Milk
1 c  Half-and-half
-{never use this becuse I
-never have it
Cook spaghetti in boiling salted water as directed on label.drain,
rinse and set aside. saute onion lightly in butter in large sauce
pan. stir in flour. off heat, stir in milk, half-and-half and broth.
put back over heat. cook stirring, until thickened. add ham and
spaghetti. add salt and pepper to taste. turn half the mixture into
shallow 2-quart baking dish. sprinkle with half the cheese. add
remaining spaghetti mixture. sprikle with remaining cheese. bake at
350 degrees for 30 minutes or until golden.
