Title: Marchand de Vin
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Alcohol, Masterchefs, New orleans
Yield: 4 cups
-- chopped
2 oz Butter
3 c  Demi-Glace **
1 1/2 c  Wine, red
1 c  Ham, finely diced
1 c  Mushrooms, fresh, finely
-- diced
1 c  Onions, green, finely
** See recipe for Demi-Glace.

Saute the ham, mushrooms and green onions in butter.  Add
demi-glace and wine, reduce by one-quarter and strain.

Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions
:    Box 71112, New Orleans, Louisiana - 1983
:    Chef Gerhard Brill, Commander's Palace Restaurant, New
Orleans
