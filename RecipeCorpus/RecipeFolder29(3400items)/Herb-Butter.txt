Title: Herb Butter
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Fresh Parsley, finely
Chopped
1 ts Shallots, finely chopped
2 ts Lemon Peel, grated
8 tb Butter, at room temperature
1/8 ts Thyme, crumbled
1/8 ts Garlic Powder
1/8 ts Oregano, crumbled
Mix all ingredients, mashing with a wooden spoon, until thoroughly
combined. Allow to stand in a cool place for several hours, so that
the flavors blend and mellow.

Makes 1/2 cup.

NOTE: see Russian bread recipe for origin. Cathy and Nora offer this
recipe for a spread that is great on any bread.
