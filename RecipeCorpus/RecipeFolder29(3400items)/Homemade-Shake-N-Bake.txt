Title: Homemade Shake N Bake
Categories: Mixes, Shake
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Parsley flakes
-pinch of cayenne
1/2 ts Basil
-pinch poultry seasoning
1 ts Seasoned salt
2 c  Flour
2 c  Crushed tortilla, natcho, or
-barbecue chips
1 ts Black pepper
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Taco seasoning
Pour into a large ZipLoc bag.  Shake until well mixed.  Store in
freezer. Use to coat beef, chicken, pork, and fish.  You can alter
the spices to your own taste.
