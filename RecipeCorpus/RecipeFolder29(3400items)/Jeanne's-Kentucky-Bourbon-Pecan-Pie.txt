Title: Jeanne's Kentucky Bourbon Pecan Pie
Categories: Countyfairs, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
2 tb Maker's Mark Kentucky
-Bourbon (Her Choice)
1    9 Inch Pie Shell, unbaked
3    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Butter, melted
3/4 c  Light Corn Syrup
1/2 c  Chocolate Chips
* Preheat oven to 350 F.

1. In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs and sugar. Gradually beat in the
melted butter and corn syrup. Stir in the chocolate chips, nuts and
bourbon of your choice.

2. Pour the mixture into the pie shell. Bake for 40 to 50 mins until
the filling is somewhat set.

Submitted by Jeanne Kemper to section on the SHELBY COUNTY FAIR AND
HORSE SHOW of Shelbyville, KY. Fair dates: 1st full week after the
4th of July.
