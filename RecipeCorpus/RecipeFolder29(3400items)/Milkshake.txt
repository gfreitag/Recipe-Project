Title: Milkshake
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake
Yield: 2 servings
2 c  Ice cream or sherbet
2 c  Whole milk
One of several tasty ideas to make getting enough calories and protein
easy.  They are a great way to get the nutrition you need even when
you're not feeling well.

Mix together with spoon or in blender.  Approximately 235 calories
and 5.5 gm. protein per cup. Source: Handout from Oncology dietitian
