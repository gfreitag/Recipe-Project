Title: GREAT-GRANDMA'S GINGER CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
2/3 c  Molasses
1 ea Egg, beaten
3/4 c  Boiling water
Whipped topping
2 1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Ground cinnamon
2 ts Salt
ds Ground cloves
Combine the flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, salt, and cloves;
set aside.  In a mixing bowl, cream sugar and shortening.  Add
molasses and egg; mix well. Stir in the dry ingredients alternately
with water. Pour into a greased 9" square baking pan. Bake at 350
degrees for 35-40 minutes. Cool completely. Cut into squares; top
with a dollop of whipped topping. Leftovers will keep several days in
an airtight container. Serves: 9 From: "Taste of Home" Magazine,
April/May 1995
