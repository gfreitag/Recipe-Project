Title: Cheddar Cheese Bread *M*
Categories: Breads, Cheese/eggs, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Nonfat dry milk
1 1/2 c  Grated sharp cheddar; (6 oz
1 tb Soft butter
At room temperature
1 ts Salt
===FOR 11/2 LB. LOAF===
1 pk Yeast
2 tb Sugar
3 c  Bread flour
1 1/4 c  Warm water
Put all ingredients into the pan, select white bread, and push

start.
