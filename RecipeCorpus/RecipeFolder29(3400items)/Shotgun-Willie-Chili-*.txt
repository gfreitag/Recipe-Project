Title: Shotgun Willie Chili *
Categories: Meats, Chili
Yield: 8 cups
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
1 tb Pasilla chili powder
1 tb Ground cumin
1 tb Salt
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
3 lb Ground beef -=OR=- sirloin;
- trimmed, cubed
2 md Onions; finely chopped
1 tb Garlic; minced
9 tb Vegetable oil
15 oz Tomato sauce
1/2 c  Chili powder; YES, that much
14 1/2 oz Beef broth
1 tb New Mexico chili powder
In large bowl, combine beef, onions and garlic. In Dutch oven or
stockpot, brown 1/3 of beef with 3 tbls oil; remove and set aside.
Repeat with remaining beef and oil. Leave last barch in pot and add
first 2 batches. Add all remaining ingredients. Stir until well
blended. Simmer, covered, for 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
