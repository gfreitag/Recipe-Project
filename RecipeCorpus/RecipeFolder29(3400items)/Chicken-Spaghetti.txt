Title: Chicken Spaghetti
Categories: Poultry, Velveeta, Pasta
Yield: 1 unknown
3/4 c  Green pepper
1 ea Onion
4 ea Stalks celery
4 tb Parsley
1 pk Shredded sharp cheese
3 c  Cooked spaghetti - break
-into thirds before cooking
5 ea Boneless chicken breasts
1 cn Chicken broth
1 sm Jar pimentos with juice
2 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 lg Can mushrooms optional
1 lb Velveeta cheese
Salt and pepper to taste
Cut chicken in bite size pieces and cook in large pot with broth until
tender and done. Add pimentos, soup, mushrooms, spaghetti, and chopped
ingredients, and simmer about 10 minutes. Add chopped velveeta cheese
and stir until melted. Pour into cake pan and spread grated cheese on
top. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
