Title: Apricot Nectar Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 cn Orange segments, drained
1 c  Coconut
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
2 c  Apricot nectar
2 sm Boxes of orange Jello
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 c  Crushed pineapple
Use a 13 x 9 x 2 inch glass container.  Heat apricot nectar and mix
with Jello powder.  Chill slightly.  Add 1/2 cup nuts, 1/2 cup
pineapple, 1 can orange segments, coconut and marshmallows.  Let set
until firm. Cream cheese and remaining pineapple and nuts and spread
over Jello. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   03-24-95
