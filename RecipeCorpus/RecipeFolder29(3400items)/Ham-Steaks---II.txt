Title: Ham Steaks - II
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Mustard
Water
2    15 oz. Bone-In Ham Steaks
(pre-cooked)
1/4 c  Brown Sugar
Gash the sides of the ham, then place in a shallow baking dish. Mix
brown sugar, mustard and a bit of water together for spreading
consistency. Spread on surface of ham and bake for 30 minutes at 325
degrees.

SOURCE: S.H.A.R.E. Colorado March/April 1995
