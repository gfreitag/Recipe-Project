Title: 280295 Spam-Mac Casserole
Categories: Spam, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Milk
1 c  Shredded Swiss cheese
1    (12 oz.) can Spam
1 c  Frozen peas & carrots
-Buttered bread crumbs
17 oz Pkg. macaroni (2 c. before
-cooked)
2 tb Oleo
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Flour
1 ts Wet mustard
Cook macaroni as directed; drain.  Melt margarine.  Add onion, cook
until tender.  Stir in flour, mustard and pepper.  Blend in milk,
cook, stirring constantly, until thickened.  Add cheese. Combine with
macaroni, Spam and vegetables.  Pour into casserole. Top with bread
crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
