Title: 663697 Shoe Peg Corn Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
-drained*
1 cn Cream of celery soup
1/2 pt Sour cream
-Salt & pepper to taste
1    Stick oleo
1    Stack Ritz crackers
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Chopped bell pepper
1 c  Grated cheese (mild
-cheddar)
1 cn Whole kernel corn*
1 cn French style string beans,
Mix all ingredients except Ritz crackers and oleo.  Stir and pour in
greased 9"x13" baking dish.  Melt oleo and crunch Ritz crackers and
mix together.  Sprinkle over top of casserole.  Cook on High 10 to 12
minutes. If a bit too soupy, microwave another 2 to 3 minutes. *May
use frozen vegetables, cook separately before mixing other
ingredients. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-22-95
