Title: Danish Apple Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Puddings
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Oats; uncooked
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Strawberry preserves
1/2 c  Margarine; melted
6    Apples; large & tart
1/2    Lemon; juice only
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Cornflakes; crushed
Peel, core and slice apples.  Place in a saucepan with lemon juice
and 1/2 cup water.  Cook until soft.  Add vanilla and set aside.  Mix
cornflakes, oatmeal, brown sugar and cinnamon.  Toss well.  Grease a
9" square pan. Sprinkle with half of the crumb mixture.  Spread with
cooked apples, then with the strawberry preserves.  Top with
remaining crumb mixture. Drizzle with melted margarine.  Bake in a
preheated 350 oven for 30 minutes. Serve warm or cooled.  Source:
