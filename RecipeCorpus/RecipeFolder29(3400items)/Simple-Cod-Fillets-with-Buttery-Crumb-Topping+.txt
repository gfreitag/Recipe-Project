Title: Simple Cod Fillets with Buttery Crumb Topping+
Categories: Seafood, Main dish, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings

1 ts Dried parsley flakes
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Melted margarine or butter
1/3 c  Lowfat milk
1 1/3 lb Thick Cod fillets, 1 to
-1 1/2 inches thick
1/3 c  Low-fat cracker crumbs (you
-can use unsalted saltines
1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F. Pour the milk into a 1 1/2-quart
shallow baking dish (like a gratin.) Add the fish.

2. combine the cracker crumbs, parsley, and salt. Evenly distribute
across the top of the fish.

3. Drizzle the margarine or butter over the crumbs. Place the fish in
the preheated oven and bake for 12 to 17 minutes or until the fish is
done.
