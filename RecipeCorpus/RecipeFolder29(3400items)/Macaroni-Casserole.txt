Title: Macaroni Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Herb seasoning
2 tb Melted margarine
1/2 c  Crumbled saltine cracker
Crumbs or potato chips
2 tb Parmesan cheese
7 oz Package of elbow macaroni
1 cn Campbells cheddar cheese
Soup
1/2 c  Campbells cream of celery
Soup
1/2 c  Milk
Cook macaroni and drain.  Combine soups, milk and seasonings.  Pour
into casserole and sprinkle with crumbs and cheese.  Pour over melted
margarine. Bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes. Randy Rigg
