Title: Shallot Puree - Great Chefs
Categories: Basics, Masterchefs
Yield: 1 cup
1/2 c  Oil, olive
3 ea Shallot, bulbs, peeled,
-- chopped
Puree the shallot with 1/2 cup of olive oil.  Drizzle a little
clear olive oil over the top of the puree and refrigerate overnight
or for up to a week if desired.

You can store this puree in the refrigerator for up to three
weeks without significant loss of flavor.

Source:  Great Chefs of San Francisco, Avon Books, 1984

Chef:   Masataka Kobayashi, Masa's, Vintage Court Hotel,
San Francisco, CA
