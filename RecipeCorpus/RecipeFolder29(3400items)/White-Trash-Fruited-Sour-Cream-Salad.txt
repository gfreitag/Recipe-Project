Title: White Trash Fruited Sour Cream Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit, Whitetrash
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
2 lb Seedlees white grapes
1 pt Sour cream
1 c  Brown sugar
Wash and pat dry grapes.  Mix the remaining ingredients together and
add grapes.  Stir to coat.  Grapes can be cut in half if desired.
Origin: Adapted from White Trash. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, April/95
