Title: 1006290 Cajun Bbq Baked Beans
Categories: Cajun/creol, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Mustard
1 md Onion
1 tb Salt
1 tb Black pepper
1/2 c  Ketchup
1 ga Pork and beans
3 lb Ground beef
8 oz BBQ sauce
1 tb Cayenne pepper
1    Bell pepper
1 c  Brown sugar
In skillet, brown ground beef, add onion and bell pepper, chopped. Add
salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, let simmer together for 20
minutes. Then add both sugars, mustard, beans, BBQ sauce and ketchup,
let simmer 10 more minutes.
