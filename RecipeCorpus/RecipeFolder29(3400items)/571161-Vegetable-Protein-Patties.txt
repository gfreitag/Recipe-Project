Title: 571161 Vegetable Protein Patties
Categories: Vegetarian, Rice
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 ts Dried thyme, crushed
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 c  Grated carrot (1 carrot)
1 c  Rolled oats
1/4 c  Chopped raw almonds
1 c  Cooked brown rice
1 c  Cooked lentils
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Dried oregano, crushed
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Toast almonds in the oven for 8-10
minutes or until a deep golden brown.  Watch carefully, they burn
easily. Combine rice, lentils, soy sauce, oregano, thyme, black
pepper in a food processor until smooth.  Place mixture in a bowl and
add grated carrot, rolled oats and toasted almonds; mix thoroughly.
Form mixture into 8 (1/4 cup) patties. Spray a skillet with nonstick
spray and heat until a drop of water dances on the surface. Place
patties in skillet and cook until lightly browned on both sides. Each
patty contains about 124 calories, 3 gm fat. Submitted By BARRY
