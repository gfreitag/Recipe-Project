Title: BROCCOLI-MUSHROOM MEDLEY
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1 c  Sliced fresh mushrooms
1 md Onion, sliced into rings
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
3 tb Cooking oil
1 1/2 lb Fresh broccoli, cut into
-florets
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Salt, optional
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Cornstarch
Steam broccoli for 1 to 2 minutes or until crisp-tender. Rinse in cold
water and set aside. In a bowl, combine lemon juice, salt, sugar,
cornstarch and nutmeg; set aside. In a large skillet or wok over high
heat, stir-fry mushrooms, onion and garlic in oil for 3 minutes. Add
broccoli and lemon juice mixture; stir-fry 1 to 2 minutes. Serve
immediately. Serves: 6 From:  "Taste of Home" Magazine, April/May 1995
