Title: Escalloped Fresh Corn
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Ground black pepper
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
6 ea Ears corn
2 tb Flour
1 ts Sugar
1 1/4 ts Salt
Remove husks and silks from corn. Cut corn off the cob. (2-1/2 Cups).
Mix corn with next 5 ingredients.  Melt butter or margarine and mix
with bread crumbs. Sprinkle 1/4 cup crumbs over bottom of a 1 quart
casserole. Add corn mixture, cover and bake in a preheated moderate
oven (375) 30 min. Sprinkle with remaining crumbs.  Bake, uncovered,
20 min. or until crumbs are brown.  Serves 6 From: Delicious Orchards
