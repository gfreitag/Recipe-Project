Title: Golden Corn Fritters
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 3 dozen
1 1/2 c  Corn, fresh; cut from cob
-about 3 ears
1/2 c  Milk
2 ts Oil, vegetable
Oil, vegetable; hot
1 c  Flour, self-rising
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2    Eggs; beaten
Combine first 4 ingredients in a large mixing bowl; mix well, and set
aside.

Combine eggs, corn, milk, and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil; mix well.
Stir corn mixture into dry ingredients.

Drop mixture by rounded teaspoonfuls into vegetable oil heated to 375
degrees; cook fritters until golden, turning once.
