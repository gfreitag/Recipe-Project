Title: White Trash Short'nin Bread
Categories: Breads, Whitetrash
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Light brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 lb Margarine
Cream the margarine and the sugar together until light and fluffy.
Add flour and mix until combined thoroughly.  Roll out to about 1/2
inch thick. Cut into rounds and place on a greased and floured cookie
sheet and bake for about 15-20 minutes at 350F Origin: Adapted from
White Trash. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, April/95.
