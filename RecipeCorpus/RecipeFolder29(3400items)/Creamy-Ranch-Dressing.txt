Title: Creamy Ranch Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Garlic clove
1/4 ts Dried basil
1/8 ts Dried oregano
1/8 ts Cayenne
1/2 c  Non-fat cottage cheese
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Horseradish
1 ts Dijon mustard
Put all ingredients in blender and process until smooth Serving Size
~ 2 Tablespoons From Family Circle magazine 1-7-92 Posted by: Sheila
Exner - March 1992
