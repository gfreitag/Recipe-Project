Title: White Trash 3 Hour Sunday Rolls
Categories: Breads, Whitetrash
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  White sugar
1 ts Salt
Flour
2 pk Yeast
2 ea Whole eggs
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 c  Lukewarm water
Mix the yeast, eggs, oil, water, sugar and salt together in a bowl.
Add flour until you get a thick batter.  Allow to rise for one hour.
Make into rolls and let 'em set for another hour to risse.  Grease
tops of rolls with margarine.  Bake in a 500F oven for about 15
minutes or until golden brown. Origin: Adapted from White Trash.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, April/95.
