Title: 962213 Herbed Corn Scallop
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Ready-mix herbed seasoned
-stuffing
2    Eggs
2 cn Cream corn (2 lb.)
1/2 c  Milk
4 tb Melted margarine
2 tb Minced onion
Beat eggs slightly in a medium bowl, stir in corn, milk, butter,
onion, salt and pepper.  Spoon 1/2 of corn mixture into a greased 8
cup baking dish; sprinkle stuffing in on even layer over top; spoon
remaining corn mixture over stuffing.  Let stand 15 minutes.  bake at
350 degrees for 1 hour, or until center is almost set, but still
slightly moist. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-25-95
