Title: Pizza Sandwiches
Categories: Healthy
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1 ts Oregano
1/8 ts Sweet Basil
3/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Rosemary -- optional
2 tb Oil
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
2 1/2 tb Celery -- chopped
2 1/2 tb Green Pepper -- chopped
1 c  Tomatoes, Canned
6 tb Tomato Paste
MMMMM--------------------------SANDWICH-------------------------------
6    English Muffins -- toasted
4 tb Parmesan Cheese -- grated
1 1/2 c  Pizza Sauce
1 1/2 c  Cottage Cheese, Lowfat
SAUCE: Saute onion, celery and pepper in vegetable oil until tender
and translucent. Add tomatoes, tomato paste and seasonings. Cook over
low heat on stove top for approx. 30 mins, stirring occasionally.
Yield: approx. 1 1/2 cups sauce.

SANDWICHES: Mix 2 Tbs cottage cheese with 2 Tbs sauce. Spread mixture
on 1/2 toasted English muffin. Sprinkle 1 tsp parmesan cheese over
top. Broil in oven for approx. 5 mins or until parmesan cheese starts
to turn golden. Yield: 12 pizza sandwiches Approx. 136 cal/serving
