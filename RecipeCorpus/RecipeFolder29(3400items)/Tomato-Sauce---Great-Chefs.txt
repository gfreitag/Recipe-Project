Title: Tomato Sauce - Great Chefs
Categories: Basics, Salsa/sauce, Masterchefs, New orleans
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Bay leaf
2 c  Tomatoes, chopped
Salt (to taste)
Pepper, white (to taste)
Oil
1 md Carrot, minced
1 md Onion, minced
1 ea Celery, stalk, minced
1 ts Garlic, minced
1 ts Thyme
1 ts Oregano
Heat the oil in a pan and add the carrot, onion, celery and
garlic. Cook for 8 minutes over moderately low heat.

Add herbs, tomatoes, salt and pepper.  Simmer for an hour or
more.

Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions
:    Box 71112, New Orleans, Louisiana - 1983
:    Chef Goffredo Fraccaro, La Riviera Restaurant, New Orleans
