Title: Green Bean Bake
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Slivered almonds
2 tb Minced onions
3 1/2 oz French Fried onion rings
2    15 oz. cans of French style
Green beans, drained
10 1/2 oz Can of cream of mushroom
Soup
Empty drained beans into greased casserole dish.  Pour soup over the
beans, mixed with minced onion and almonds.  Arrange onion rings over
the top. Bake, uncovered, in a 300 degree oven for 30 to 40 minutes.
Serve hot. Randy Rigg
