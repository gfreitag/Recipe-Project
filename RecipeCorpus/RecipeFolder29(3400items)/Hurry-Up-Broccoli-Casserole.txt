Title: Hurry-Up Broccoli Casserole
Categories: Countyfairs, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
3    Eggs -- lightly beaten
3 tb Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Dry Mustard
1/4 ts Pepper -- freshly ground
2 pk (10 Oz) Broccoli Frozen
-cut up or equivalent,
-fresh, thawed
8 oz Cottage Cheese
6 oz Cheddar Cheese -- grated
* Preheat oven to 350 F.

1. Mix all ingredients together, just enough to blend. Spoon into a
greased 8 x 8-inch Pyrex baking dish or equivalent casserole.

2. Bake until bubbling and browned, about 30 mins. Serves 4 to 6.
Great company dish, surrounded by pureed winter squash.

Submitted by Caroline Craig to section on the TOPSFIELD FAIR of
Topsfield, Mass. Fair Dates: Early October, 10 days.
