Title: Lemon Sponge Pie - 1000
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
3 tb Flour
1/2 ts Salt
3 ea Eggs, separated
1 ea 9" pie shell
1 ea Lemon, juice & rind
2 tb Butter
1 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Hot milk
Cream butter and sugar. Add egg yolks and beat well. Add flour, salt,
lemon juice and grated rind. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Pour
into unbaked pie shell and bake at 325 degrees for 45 minutes. Mrs.
John P. Elberti Submitted By LAWRENCE KELLIE   On   03-21-95
