Title: Rice Meatballs
Categories: Main dish, Rice, Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1/8 ts Marjoram
Dash of pepper
2 1/2 c  Tomato juice
1/2 ts Sugar
1 c  Minute Rice
1 lb Ground beef
1    Egg, slightly beaten
2 ts Grated onion
2 ts Salt
Combine rice with beef, egg, onion, salt, marjoram, pepper, 1/2 c
tomato juice.  Mix.  Shape into 18 balls and put in a skillet.  Add
sugar to remaining 2 cups of tomato juice.  Pour juice over meatballs
in the skillet.  Brin mix to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer, covered
15 minutes. Baste occasionally. Randy Rigg
