Title: 950722 Vietnamese Corn Crab Soup
Categories: Vietnamese, Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Cornstarch
1 cn Crab meat
1    Egg white
-Salt
1 1/2 qt Chicken stock
1/4 c  Cold water
1/2 pk Frozen corn, cooked
-Pepper
Bring stock to boil.  Dissolve cornstarch in water.  Add mixture to
stock and stir until soup thickens.  Add corn, crabmeat, salt and
pepper to taste.  Simmer until corn and crab are heated.  Just before
serving, bring soup to boil and add egg white.  Break the egg white
with a fork as it congeals.
