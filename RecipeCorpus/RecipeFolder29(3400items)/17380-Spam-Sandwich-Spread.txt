Title: 17380 Spam Sandwich Spread
Categories: Spam
Yield: 1 servings
3    Raw carrots
1 cn Spam
6    Hard cooked eggs, boiled
1 sm Green pepper
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 sm Onion
Grind Spam, eggs, carrots, onion and green pepper.  Use coarse grater
or grinder.  Add remaining ingredients; mix well.  Good on Ritz
crackers or as a sandwich spread.
