Title: 605001 Squaw Corn
Categories: Vegetables, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
-Salt and pepper to taste
2    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Baked ham, chopped
2 tb Fat
2 1/2 c  Cream style corn (or fresh)
Brown ham in fat; add corn, seasoning, and eggs.  Mix and cook over
low heat, stirring constantly, until eggs are set.  Makes 6 servings.
