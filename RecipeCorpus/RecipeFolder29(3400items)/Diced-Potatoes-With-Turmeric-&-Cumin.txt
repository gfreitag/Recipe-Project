Title: Diced Potatoes With Turmeric & Cumin
Categories: Side dish, Indian, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Coriander, ground
1/2 ts Cumin, ground
1/4 ts Cayenne
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 lb New potatoes
5 tb Oil
1/8 ts Asafetida
1/2 ts Black mustard seeds
1/2 ts Cumin seeds, whole
Dice potatoes.  Heat oil in a wok over a medium heat & when hot add,
in quick succession, asafetida, mustard sees, cumin seeds.  Add
potatoes & stir once or twice.  Sprinkle in turmeric.  Continue to
stir every now & then for about 15 minutes.  Sprinkle in the
remaining ingredients, stir & cook 2 more minutes.

Madhur Jaffrey, "A Taste of India"
