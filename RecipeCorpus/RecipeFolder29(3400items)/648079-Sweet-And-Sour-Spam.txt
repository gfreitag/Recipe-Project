Title: 648079 Sweet And Sour Spam
Categories: Spam, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
1    Green pepper, chopped
1 c  Spam, diced
20 oz Can pineapple chunks
-(reserve
-juice)
1/3 c  Brown sugar
3 tb Cornstarch
3 tb Vinegar
2 tb Catsup
2 tb Soy sauce
1 sm Onion, chopped
Mix brown sugar and cornstarch in 10 inch skillet.  Add enough water
to pineapple juice to measure 2 1/4 cups.  Stir juice mixture,
vinegar, catsup and soy sauce into skillet.  Cook over medium heat
until mixture boils and thickens.  Stir in pineapple chunks, onion
and green pepper and diced Spam. Reduce heat and simmer until heated.
Serve over rice. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-01-95
