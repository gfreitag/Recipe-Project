Title: 678894 Irish Stew
Categories: Irish, Potato, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
4 sm Onions
-Nearly a quart of water
2 1/2 lb Chops
8    Potatoes
4    Turnips
Take some chops from loin of mutton, place them in stew pan in
alternate layers of sliced potatoes and chops; add turnips and
onions, cut into pieces.  Pour in nearly a quart of cold water. Cover
stew pan. Let it stew gently until vegetables are ready to mash and
greater part of gravy is absorbed.  Then place in dish. Serve it up
hot.

From White House Cookbook, published 1888.
