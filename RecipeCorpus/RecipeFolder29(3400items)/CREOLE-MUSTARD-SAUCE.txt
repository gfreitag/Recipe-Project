Title: CREOLE MUSTARD SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Poultry, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
1 ts Water
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Green onions, sliced into
1/4-inch pieces
1 tb Crushed garlic packed in oil
1 ts Finely chopped green pepper
1 ts Finely chopped celery
1 ts Finely chopped onion
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Creole-style mustard
OR
1/4 c  Dijon mustard
1 tb Yellow mustard
1 tb Horseradish
1/2 ts Cider vinegar
Dash of Worcestershire
Sauce
1 ts Red wine vinegar
Mix all ingredients together. Serve with Planet Hollywood Chicken.
(From the Miami Herald)
