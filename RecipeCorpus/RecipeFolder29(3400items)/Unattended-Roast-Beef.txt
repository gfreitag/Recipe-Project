Title: Unattended Roast Beef
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
4-7 kg boneless rolled roast
Preheat oven to 500 degrees. Sprinkle liberally with pepper, salt, and
garlic powder. Place on a rack in a roasting pan. Roast for 20
minutes at 500 degrees, then turn down to lowest setting. Leave for 8
hours and - DON'T PEEK! Before serving, turn oven to 350 for 20
minutes. Submitted By CAROL VERGE   On   04-13-95
