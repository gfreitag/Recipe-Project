Title: Strawberry Jam with Pectin
Categories: Can/preserv, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Package powdered pectin
1 tb (15 ml) lemon juice
Red food coloring as
-desired.
1 qt (950 ml) cleaned
-strawberries
3 ea To 4 tsp. (15-20 ml) liquid
-artificial sweetener
Crush strawberries in 1-1/2 quart saucepan.  Stir in artificial
sweetener, food coloring, powdered pectin, and lemon juice.  Bring to
a boil and boil 1 minute.  Remove from heat.  Continue to stir 2
minutes. Pour into freezer containers, cover and freeze.  Thaw before
serving. Store in refrigerator after opening.  Yield:  About 2-2/3
cups (640 ml) 1 Tbsp. (15 mL) = 5 calories. From: Ball Blue Book
