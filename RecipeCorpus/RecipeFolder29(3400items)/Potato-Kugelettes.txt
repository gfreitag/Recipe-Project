Title: Potato Kugelettes
Categories: Jewish, Passover, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
ds Pepper to taste
4 tb Melted margarine, Divided
2 tb Matzoh meal (additional for
Dusting)
1 c  Idaho Potatoes (SEE NOTE)
1/4 c  Grated onion
2    Eggs, well beaten
1 ts Kosher salt
NOTE: To make potatoes easier to handle, parboil them for 10 minutes
and then plunge them into cold water, then immediately drain.  Pat
dry and grate.

Preheat oven to 375.

Combine all ingredients (reserving 2 tb of margarine)

Grease 24 mini muffin cups with the remaining margarine and dust with
the additional matzah meal.

Place one tablespoon of mixture in each muffin cup and bake 25 minutes
until golden.
Adapted from The Jewish Holiday Kitchen Washington Post 4/12/95
