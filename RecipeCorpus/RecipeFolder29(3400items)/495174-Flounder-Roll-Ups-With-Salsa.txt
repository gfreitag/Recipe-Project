Title: 495174 Flounder Roll-Ups With Salsa
Categories: Tex/mexican, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
-leaves
1    Clove garlic, minced
1 ts Ground cumin
-Pinch of fresh or dried
-oregano
-Lemon pepper to taste
1 c  Plain tomato sauce
1 1/2 lb Flounder or sole fillets
1 c  Diced vine-ripe tomatoes or
-oz. can stewed tomatoes
6 tb Shredded Monterey Jack
-cheese
4 tb Minced onion
1    Jalapeno pepper, chopped
2 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
Flatten fish fillets.  Combine remaining ingredients, except tomato
sauce, and put some of mixture on top of each fillet.  Roll fillets,
then arrange seam side down in non-stick baking pan sprayed with
cooking spray. Pour on tomato sauce (and sprinkle with additional
herbs, if desired). Bake uncovered in preheated 350 degree oven for
20 to 25 minutes. Makes 6 servings, 145 calories each.
