Title: Golden Egg Bread *M*
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
4 tb Sugar
3/4 c  Warm water
2    Eggs; room temperature
===FOR 11/2 LB. LOAF===
1 pk Yeast
6 tb Vegetable or corn oil
3 c  Bread flour
Combine all ingredients in the pan in the order listed, select

white bread and push start.
NOTE from DAK: Great for sandwiches. It rises tall and cuts

beautifully.
