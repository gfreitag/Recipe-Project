Title: 439334 Luncheon Goop
Categories: Velveeta, Mushroom, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2    Green pepper, chopped
1/2    Onion, chopped
4    Hard boiled eggs
1 cn Mushrooms
2 c  Thick white sauce
2 cn Chinese noodles
1 pk Velveeta cheese
1 cn Pimientos, chopped
To make thick white sauce, use 3 tablespoons butter and 3 tablespoons
flour to 2 cups of milk.  Melt cheese in white sauce. Add chopped
eggs. Cook pepper and onion in a little butter until soft. Add
pimiento and mushrooms and then add to white sauce. Serve on crisp
Chinese noodles, which have been heated.
