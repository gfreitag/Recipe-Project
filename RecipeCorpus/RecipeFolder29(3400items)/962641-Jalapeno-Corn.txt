Title: 962641 Jalapeno Corn
Categories: Vegetables, Chilies
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2    Sticks margarine
3/4 c  Milk
3    4 jalapenos, chopped
3 cn Shoe peg corn
2    (8 oz.) pkgs. cream cheese
1/4 ts Garlic salt
Melt cream cheese, salt, margarine and milk.  Add corn and jalapenos.
Mix well. Bake at 350 degrees until bubbly and lightly brown.
