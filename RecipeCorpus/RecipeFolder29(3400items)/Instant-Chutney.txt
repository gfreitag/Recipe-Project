Title: Instant Chutney
Categories: Condiments, Relishes
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 ts Pumpkin pie spice
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 c  Plum jam
1/4 c  Raisins
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
Place all ingredients in a small saucepan; bring to a boil over medium
heat, stirring occasionally; stir well to mix. (Or heat in a bowl for
about 3 minutes, stirring twice, in microwave).

Serve warm with meat, rice, curries, etc.
