Title: Italian Bean Cuisine
Categories: Countyfairs, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
8 tb Butter
2 tb Red Wine
8 oz Spaghetti
2 tb Garlic Powder
1 c  Parmesan Cheese, grated
1 1/2 c  Scallions, chopped
2 c  Fresh Mushrooms, sliced
1 cn (15 Oz) Dark Red Kidney
Beans
Drained and rinsed
1. In a large skillet, gently gook the scallions, mushrooms and beans
in the butter and wine for 20 mins, stirring often.

2. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti according to package directions.
Drain and add to skillet. Add the garlic powder and cheese, and mix
thoroughly.

From Tim Howard of the Idaho Bean Growers for the TWIN FALLS COUNTY
FAIR & RODEO of Filer, ID. Fair Dates: 1st week in Sept. for 6 days.
