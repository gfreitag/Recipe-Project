Title: Crunchy Breakfast Fruit
Categories: Diabetic, Breakfast, Fruit
Yield: 1 serving
1 ts Vanilla
1 pk To 2 pk Equal
-depending on taste.
1/2 c  Grape nuts;
3/4 c  Soft ricotta cheese;
-low-fat
3/4 c  Fresh fruit,*
* blueberries, strawberries, raspberries or favorite fruit Mix it all
togeather and enjoy.  Usually I just put it togeather without paying
too much attention to the quanities.  I use frozen fruit when I do
not have fresh fruit. Hope you enjoy this.
