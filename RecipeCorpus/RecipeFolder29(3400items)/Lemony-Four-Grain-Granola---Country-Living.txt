Title: Lemony Four-Grain Granola - Country Living
Categories: Nuts/grains, Breakfast
Yield: 8 cups
-margarine
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Honey
2 ts Finely grated lemon rind
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 c  Dark seedless raisins
2 c  Old-fashioned rolled oats
2 c  Puffed wheat cereal
2 c  Puffed rice cereal
2 c  Corn cereal squares
1 c  Blanched whole almonds
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter or
1. Heat oven to 300'F. Line 15 1/2-by 1O 1/2-inch jelly-roll pan with
aluminum foil; lightly oil the foil or coat with nonstick vegetable
cooking spray.

2. In large bowl, combine oats, wheat and rice cereals, corn squares,
and almonds. In 1-quart saucepan, heat butter until melted. Stir in
oil and honey; heat to boiling, stiffing constantly. Stir in lemon
rind and juice until well mixed; pour over cereal mixture and toss
until well combined.

3. Spread cereal mixture in prepared pan. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or
until golden and dry, stirring every 10 minutes. Remove granola from
oven and stir in raisins. Cool granola and store in airtight
container. For a gift, package granota in a cellophane bag, if
desired.
