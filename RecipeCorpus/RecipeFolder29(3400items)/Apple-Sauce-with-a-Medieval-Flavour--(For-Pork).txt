Title: Apple Sauce with a Medieval Flavour  (For Pork)
Categories: English, Medieval, Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 1 unknown
1 c  Apples; peeled and sliced
1/4 c  Coarsely ground dates
1/2 c  Water
1/8 ts Saffron
Adapted by Maxime de la Falaise from "Two Fifteenth-century Cookery
Books"

"Boil the water with the saffron until it is bright yellow. Add the
remaining ingredients, season and cook until the apples are soft.
Serve with roast pork."

_Seven Centuries of English Cooking_ Compiled and Updated by Maxime
de la Falaise Grove Press, London, 1992 Typos by Jeff Pruett
