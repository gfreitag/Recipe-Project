Title: Skyline Chili 2
Categories: Chili
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Chili powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1    Garlic clove; chopped
1/4 ts Red pepper
1/2 ts Allspice
1/3 tb Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Ground beef
1    Onion, chopped
1 tb Vinegar
15 oz Tomato sauce
1 ts Worcester sauce
1    Bay leaf
12 oz Tomato juice
12 oz ;Water
Brown ground beef and onion.  Drain fat. Add remaining ingredients,
stirring well after each addition. Simmer 2 hours or until liquid is
sufficiently reduced. Serve over spaghetti. Top with chopped onions
and grated Colby or Cheddar cheese; serve oyster crackers on side.
