Title: Pastry Cream - Great Chefs
Categories: Basics, Masterchefs, New orleans
Yield: 4 cups
1/4 c  Hazelnut paste (optional
-- if difficult to find)
1 c  Cream, heavy
1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Sugar
2 lg Eggs
2 ea Egg yolks
1/4 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk, scalded
Beat the sugar and eggs in a bowl until thick and lemon-yellow.

Add flour and beat until smooth.

Pour this mixture into scalded milk and beat smooth while
heating just to the boiling point.

Pour into bowl placed over ice water (to cool mixture) and add
hazelnut paste.

Beat heavy cream with sugar until thick and fold into the cooled
mixture.

Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions
Box 71112, New Orleans, Louisiana - 1983
Chef Daniel Bonnot, Louis XVI Restaurant,
Marie Antoinette Hotel, New Orleans
