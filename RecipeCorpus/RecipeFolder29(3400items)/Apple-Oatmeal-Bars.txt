Title: Apple-Oatmeal Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 batch
1 1/2 c  Butter, melted
4 2/3 c  Finely chopped/sliced apples
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 1/2 c  Oats
1 1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 ts Baking soda
Stir together flour, oats, brown sugar, and soda.  Stir in melted
butter till well combined.  Set 2 cups of oat mixture aside. Pat
remaining oat mixture into an ungreased 15 x 10 x 1 inch baking pan.
Bake in 350 degree oven 12 minutes.
Carefully layer apples atop baked crust.  Sprinkle with cinnamon
and remaining oat mixture.  Slightly pat oat mixture into filling.
Bake in 350 oven 20-25 minutes more or till top is golden. Cool in
pan on wire rack. Cut into bars.
