Title: 954067 Oysters Baked In Cream-Style Corn
Categories: Seafood, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1    (17 oz.) can cream-style
-corn
-Parsley sprigs
1 pt Fresh oysters, well drained
2    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Cracker crumbs
1/2 c  Milk
1/3 c  Melted butter or margarine
Combine all ingredients; pour into a greased 1-quart baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees about 35 minutes or until mixture sets. Oysters
will be tough if they are cooked too long.  Garnish with parsley
sprigs. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-25-95
