Title: Apple Cinnamon Riasin Cookies^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Canola oil
2 tb Raisins
1 sm Apple, peeled and diced
2 tb Grape Nuts
3/4 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Egg Beaters
1/3 c  Apple juice concentrate
2 tb Brown rice syrup
Preheat oven to 375F. Spray a cookie sheet with pam. Mix flour, baking
powder and cinnamon in a bowl. Add Egg Beaters, apple juice
concentrate, brown rice syrup, vanilla and oil. Mix thoroughly. Add
raisins, apples and Grape Nuts. Drop onto sheet by tablespoons. Bake
until golden brown, about 10 minutes. Store extra, (if there are
any), in the refrigerator.

Per serving: Calories: 56 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 12g Fat: trace
Sodium: 48mg Cholesterol: 0mg
