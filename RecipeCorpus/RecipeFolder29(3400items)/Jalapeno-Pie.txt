Title: Jalapeno Pie
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
On the Jalapenos
4    Eggs, beat and pour over the
Cheese.
Jalapenos:  cut in slivers
And cover bottom of pie pan
Or 8 inch square pan.
2 c  Grated sharp cheese, pile it
Bake at 275 degrees for 45 minutes.  Cut and serve warm, please.
