Title: Applesauce-Raisin Muffins
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 12 servings
3/4 c  Applesauce
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Oil, salad
2 c  Flour, self-rising
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon, ground
1 ea Egg; beaten
Combine flour, sugar, and cinnamon.  Combine remaining ingredients;
add to flour mixture, stirring only until flour is moistened.  Fill
greased muffin pans two-thirds full.  Bake at 425 degrees about 20 to
25 minutes or until golden brown.
