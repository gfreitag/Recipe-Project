Title: Zucchini Pickles
Categories: Pickles
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Prepared mustard
2 ts Mustard seed
1 ts Celery seed
1 ts Tumeric
2 lb Zucchini, thinly sliced
1/2 lb Onions, thinly sliced
1/4 c  Salt
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Cider vinegar
Cover zucchini and onions with water and 1/4 cup salt.  Soak for 2
hours and drain.  Bring remaining ingredients to a boil and pour
drained vegetables.  Let stand for 2 hours.  Boil 5 minutes and jar.
