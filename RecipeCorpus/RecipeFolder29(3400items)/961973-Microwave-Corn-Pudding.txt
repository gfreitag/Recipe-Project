Title: 961973 Microwave Corn Pudding
Categories: Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
-corn
3/4 c  Crushed crackers
2 tb Butter, cut in pieces
-Paprika (optional)
1    Egg
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Sugar
1    (16 oz.) can cream style
Place egg in 1 1/2 quart casserole dish and beat well with fork. Stir
in milk, sugar, corn, crackers and butter.  Microwave at medium high
(7) for 7 minutes and stir well.  Sprinkle with paprika if so
desired. Microwave at medium-high (7) for 11 to 14 minutes.  When
done, center will be barely set.
