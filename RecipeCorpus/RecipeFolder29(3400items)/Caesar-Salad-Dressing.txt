Title: Caesar Salad Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
2    Garlic cloves, minced
2    Anchovy fillets, minced(opt)
1/3 c  Olive oil
4 ts Lemon juice
1 tb Mayonnaise
Whisk together all ingredients in small bowl until well combined.
