Title: 889952 Corn Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Green onion, chopped
1/4 c  Cucumber, chopped
1/4 c  Sweet relish
1/2 c  Thousand Island dressing
1    (16 oz.) can whole kernel
-corn
1/2 c  Swiss cheese, cut in small
-cubes
1/4 c  Green pepper, chopped
Combine first six ingredients in glass bowl.  Fold in dressing. Cover
and chill several hours or overnight.
