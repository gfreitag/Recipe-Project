Title: Banana-Chocolate Chip Cookies (Passsover)
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Jewish, Passover
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------FROM: SANDRA TEMES--------------------------
1/2 ea Banana; ripe
2 ea Eggs
1 ea Tb Water
1 ea Tb Orange Marmalade
1/4 ts Salt
3/4 c  Chocolate hips
3/4 c  Pecans or Walnuts
1 c  Matzoh Farfel
1 c  Matzoh Meal
3/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Oil
3 ea Tb Cocoa Powder
In food processor or blender, coarsely chop nuts. Transfer to large
bowl. Add farfel and matzoh meal to processor bowl. Process 3
seconds. Transfer to bowl with nuts. In processor bowl, combine
sugar, oil, cocoa, banana, egs, water, marmalade and salt. Process
just until smooth. Transfer to bowl. Using fork, combine all
ingredients. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop by rounded teasponnfuls on
to greased baking tray. Bake in preheated 350F oven about 20 minutes
or until bottoms are lightly browned. Makes about 3 dozen. From
Article: Jewish Food Too Good To Passover By: Marion Kane (Food
Editor) in Toronto Star 5 April, 1995
