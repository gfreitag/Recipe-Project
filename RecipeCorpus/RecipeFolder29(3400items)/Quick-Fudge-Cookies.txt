Title: Quick Fudge Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings  1 stick butter/marg. 2c. sugar 1/2 c. milk 5-6 Tbsp. Cocoa 2 tsp.
vanilla 2-3 c. oats 1 c. cocoanut

In saucepan boil 2 1/2 min. marg., sugar, milk, cocoa.  Remove add
vanilla oats, and cocoanut.  (Stir Quickly!!!!!!)  drop on wax paper
to harden. (Appx. 1/2 hour)  No Bake cookies-- what more can you ask
for?!!! Submitted By FLORENCE THOMPSON   On   04-21-95
