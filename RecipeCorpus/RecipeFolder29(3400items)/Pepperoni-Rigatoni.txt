Title: Pepperoni Rigatoni
Categories: Pasta, Main dish, Kids
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Basil; chopped, fresh
2    Garlic cloves; minced
26 oz Spaghetti sauce
2 c  Cheese, mozzarella; shredded
8 oz Rigatoni; cooked
1 c  Pepperoni;sliced & quartered
1/2 c  Cheese, parmesan
1/2 c  Onion; diced
1/2 c  Green pepper; diced
Heat oven to 375F. Layer half the ingredients, in order listed, in a
greased 9x13-inch baking pan. Repeat layers. Bake, uncovered, 25-30
minutes until lightly browned.

per serving: 294Cal, 15gFat, 23gCarb, 769mgNa, 48mgChol approximate
cost: $7 submitted by Mimi Price
