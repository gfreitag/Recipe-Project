Title: Pork and shrimp filled egg rolls
Categories: Appetizers, Chinese, Side dish, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn 4 1/2 oz shrimp
1 ea Egg, beaten
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Dry sherry
1/2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
40 ea Wonton or 6 egg roll skins
Cooking oil for deep frying
1/2 lb Ground pork
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
1 tb Cooking oil
2 c  Bok choy, finely chopped
1 c  Fresh mushrooms, chopped
1/2 c  Onion, finely chopped
1/2 c  Celery, finely chopped
1/2 c  Water chestnuts, chopped
1/4 c  Carrots, shredded
In skillet, stir-fry pork and garlic quickly in 1 T hot oil until
meat is browned. Drain off fat. Add vegetables; stir-fry 2-3 minutes
more. Drain shrimp and chop. In bowl, combine shrimp, pork-vegetable
mixture, egg, soy sauce, sherry, sugar and salt. Cool slightly.

Wrap wontons using directions on package or wrap egg rolls. Fry a few
at a time, in deep hot oil (365 degrees) for 2-3 minutes or until
golden brown. Use a slotted spoon to remove. Drain on paper toweling.
Serve warm with one or two sauces.

Makes 40 wontons or 6 egg rolls.
