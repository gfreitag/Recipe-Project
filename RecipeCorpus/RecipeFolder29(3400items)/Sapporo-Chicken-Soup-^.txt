Title: Sapporo Chicken Soup ^
Categories: Poultry, Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Green beans, julienned
4 c  Chicken bouillon
5 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Honey
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Green peppercorns
1/4 lb Cooked cleaned shrimp
1 1/4 lb Boneless chicken breasts,
-cubed
2 tb Coconut oil
1 sm White radish, julienned
1 sm Carrot, juilenned
1 c  Potatoes, juilienned
1    Leek, white part only,
-julienned
Saute chicken in hot oil until tender. Add vegetable strips and saute
a few minutes longer, stirring constantly. Heat chicken bouillon and
pour over vegetables. Cover pan and simmer 15 minutes. Season with
soy sauce, honey, salt and green peppercorns. Add shrimp and reheat
over low heat for 1 minute. Serve.
