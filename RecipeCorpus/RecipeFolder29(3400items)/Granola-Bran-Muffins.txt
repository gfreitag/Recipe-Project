Title: Granola Bran Muffins
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Quick oats
1/2 c  Wheat germ
2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Coconut
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Boiling water
2 1/2 c  Whole bran cereal
2    Beaten eggs
2 c  Buttermilk or sour milk
1/2 c  Oil
1/2 c  Chopped apple
Pour water over cereal and cool. Stir in next 4 ingredients. Combine
dry ingredients and stir into bran mixture. Add last 3 ingredients.
Spoon into 36 greased muffin tins. Bake at 400 degrees for 22 minutes.
