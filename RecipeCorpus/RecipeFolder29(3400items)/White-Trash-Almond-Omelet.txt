Title: White Trash Almond Omelet
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains, Whitetrash
Yield: 3 servings
6 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Slivered almonds
1/2 ea Stick margarine or butter
Brown the almonds with the butter in a hot skillet until toasted.  Add
beaten eggs and stir until firm.  Season with salt and pepper to
taste. Serves 3-4. Origin: Adapted from White Trash Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, April/95.
