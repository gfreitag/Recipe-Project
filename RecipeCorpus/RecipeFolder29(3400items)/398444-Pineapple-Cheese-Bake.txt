Title: 398444 Pineapple Cheese Bake
Categories: Casseroles, Velveeta
Yield: 4 servings
1    Egg, beaten
4 tb Flour
-Mini marshmallows
1    (#303) can pineapple chunks
1/4 lb Velveeta cheese
1/2 c  Sugar
Drain pineapple juice into saucepan.  Add sugar, egg and flour. Cook
until thickened.  Into 1 1/2 quart casserole cut up cheese and mix
with pineapple.  Pour sauce over mixture and top with mini
marshmallows. Bake at 350 degrees until brown.
