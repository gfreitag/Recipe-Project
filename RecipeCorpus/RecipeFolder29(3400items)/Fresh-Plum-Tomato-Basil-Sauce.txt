Title: Fresh Plum Tomato Basil Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
1 cl Garlic; finely chopped
4 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Black olives; dried & cured
-pitted (optional)
1/2 lb Spaghetti or linguine
8    Plum tomatoes; peeled,
-seeded and chopped
1 bn Green onions
10    Basil leaves; torn up
1 tb Oregano; fresh and chopped
Combine all ingredients except pasta in a large skillet; cook for 5
minutes.  Add cooked pasta to skillet; toss and cook for an
additional 2 minutes.  371 calories and 17 grams fat.  Source: Store
