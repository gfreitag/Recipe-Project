Title: 607651 Lentil Patties
Categories: Vegetarian, Rice
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Sauteed onion
1 tb Dry parsley
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Wheat germ
1/2 c  Dry lentils
1/4 c  Brown rice
1    Egg
1/3 c  Shredded cheese
1/4 c  Dry, fine crumbs
Cook lentils and rice in 3 cups water for 45 to 50 minutes. Drain.
Mash slightly.  Let cool 15 minutes.  Add egg, cheese, crumbs, onion,
parsley, salt and pepper.  Shape into 4 patties; coat with wheat
germ. Fry in non-stick pan in oil or oleo until nicely browned. Extra
delicious served with sauteed mushrooms or onions. NOTE: This is an
excellent meat substitute main dish for people on a restricted diet.
