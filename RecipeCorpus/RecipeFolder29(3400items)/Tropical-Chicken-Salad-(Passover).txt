Title: Tropical Chicken Salad (Passover)
Categories: Meats, Poultry, Salads, Jewish, Passover
Yield: 4 servings

1/2-3/4 c Cashews
TROPICAL CHICKEN SALAD
======================
[Serves 4 to 6]
4 c  Chicken; cut up (you can
-use leftover turkey)
2 lg Oranges; peeled; sectioned;
-(don't save juice)
1 1/2 c  Fresh Pineapple; cut into
In a large bowl, combine chicken, fruit and celery. Mix well. Cover
and chill. In a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients except
the nuts. Cover and chill. Just before serving, combine the chicken
mixture with the dressing and nuts. Serve in hollowed out pineapple
shells (easy clean up) or on lettuce. From An Article: Matzoh Free
Zone Is Ideal For Meals By: Eileen Goltz in The Canadian Jewish News
6 April, 1995
