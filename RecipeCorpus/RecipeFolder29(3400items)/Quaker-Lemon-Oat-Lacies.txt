Title: Quaker Lemon Oat Lacies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 dozen
Uncooked
1 tb Lemon Peel, grated
1 ts Vanilla
Powdered Sugar
2 c  Margarine, softened
1 c  Sugar
2 c  All Purpose Flour
3 c  Quaker Old Fashioned Oats,
Beat margarine & sugar until creamy. Add remaining ingredients except
powdered sugar; mix well. Cover & chill 30 minutes. Heat oven to 350
degrees. Shape dough into 1 inh balls; plae on ungreased cookie sheet.
Flatten with bottom of glass dipped in powdered sugar. Bake 12 to 15
minutes or until edges are light golden brown. Cool 1 minute on cookie
sheet remove to wire rack. Cool ccompletely. Sprinkle with powdered
sugar if desired.
