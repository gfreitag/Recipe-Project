Title: Doughboys
Categories: Breads, Meats, Kids
Yield: 1 servings
pn Salt
1/4 c  Water
1    Hot dog
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Shortening
You can also use 3/4 cup of biscuit mix.  Mix ingredient with
shortening and then add water.  Put hot dog on green stick and wrap
dough around it. Cook holding 6 inches from coals so inside will cook
and then brown nearer to the coals.  Ensure the dough is not too
sticky. Submitted By ELEANOR MURRAY   On   03-29-95
