Title: Chipped Ham Sandwiches
Categories: Sandwich, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Mustard
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 ts Relish
3/4 lb Velveeta cheese
1 lb Chipped ham
2 tb Onion flakes
Cube cheese; break up ham; mix all ingredients.  Make in sandwiches
on buns and wrap in foil.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
Chopped luncheon ham can also be used in place of chipped ham. Randy
