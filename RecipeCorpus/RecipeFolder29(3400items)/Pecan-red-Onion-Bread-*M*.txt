Title: Pecan-red Onion Bread *M*
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker, Vegetables, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 c  Chopped fresh red onion
1 1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Chopped pecans or walnuts
===FOR 11/2 LB. LOAF===
1 pk Yeast
1 c  Plus 1 Tbsp. warm milk
3 c  Bread flour
1/4 c  Soft butter
Combine all ingredients in the order given. Select white bread and

push start. Get ready for an unusual treat.
