Title: Creamy Potato Puff
Categories: Casseroles, Potato, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Chopped pimento
1 ts Salt
ds Pepper
8 oz Philadelphia cream cheese
4 c  Hot mashed potatoes
1    Egg, beaten
1/3 c  Finely chopped onion
Combine cream cheese and potatoes; mix well.  Add remaining
ingredients. Bake in a 1 quart casserole at 350 degrees for about 45
minutes. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   04-20-95
