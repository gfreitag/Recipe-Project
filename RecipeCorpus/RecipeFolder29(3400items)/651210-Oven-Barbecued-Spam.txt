Title: 651210 Oven Barbecued Spam
Categories: Spam
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Hunt's tomato sauce
2 tb Brown sugar
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 cn Spam
2 tb Water
1 tb Finely chopped onion
Cut Spam lengthwise not quite through in 8 slices.  Place in greased
baking dish.  Combine Hunt's tomato sauce, water, brown sugar, onion
and Worcestershire sauce.  Pour over meat.  Bake in moderate 375
degree oven for 25 to 30 minutes, basting occasionally with sauce.
