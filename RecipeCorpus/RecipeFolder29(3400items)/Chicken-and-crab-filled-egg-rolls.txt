Title: Chicken and crab filled egg rolls
Categories: Appetizers, Chinese, Side dish, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Egg, beaten
2 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Dry sherry
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
40 ea Wonton skins or 6 egg roll
Cooking oil for deep frying
1 ea Chicken breast, whole small
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
1 tb Oil
1 c  Chinese cabbage, chopped
1/2 c  Pea pods, chopped
1/4 c  Water chestnuts, chopped
2 tb Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Crab meat, cooked or canned
Skin, split, and bone chicken, finely chop. In skillet, stir-fry
chicken and garlic in the 1 T hot cookin oil about 2 minutes. Add
vegetables; stir-fry 2-3 minutes more. Flake crab, chicken-vegetable
mixture, egg, soy sauce, sherry, salt and pepper. Cool slightly.

Wrap wontons or egg rolls. Fry, a few at a time, in deep hot oil (365
degrees) for 2-3 minutes or until brown. Using a slotted spoon or wire
strainer, remove wontons or egg rolls. Drain on paper toweling.

Makes 40 wontons or 6 egg rolls.
