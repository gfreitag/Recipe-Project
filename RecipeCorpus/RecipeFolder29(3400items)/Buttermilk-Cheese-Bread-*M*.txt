Title: Buttermilk Cheese Bread *M*
Categories: Breads, Cheese/eggs, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 c  Warm water
1 ts Salt
1 c  Grated sharp cheddar cheese
===FOR 11/2 LB. LOAF===
1 pk Yeast
1 tb Sugar
3 c  Bread flour
1 c  Buttermilk; room temperatur
Pour in all ingredients in the order listed. Select white bread and

push start.
