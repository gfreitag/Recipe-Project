Title: Pineapple Ice Cream^
Categories: Ice cream, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Nonfat yogurt
1 ts Coconut extract
2 lg Bananas
1 cn (8oz) crushed unsweetened
Pineapple, drained
Peel and wrap banana in plastic wrap. Place pineapple in a plastic
container. Freeze both 24 hours. Remove and let soften a few minutes.
Combine all ingredients in blender nad blend until smooth. Pour into 4
dessert glasses and serve immediately.

Per serving: Calories: 139 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 35g Fat: trace
Sodium: 14mg Cholesterol: trace
