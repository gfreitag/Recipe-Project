Title: Gazpacho Vegetable Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 2/3 cups
1/2 c  Green pepper; chopped
1 tb Lemon juice
8 oz Tub of lite cream cheese
1/4 c  Medium salsa
Combine ingredients.  Chill and serve/  2 Tbsp serving contains 43
calories and 4 grams fat.  Source: Store ad
