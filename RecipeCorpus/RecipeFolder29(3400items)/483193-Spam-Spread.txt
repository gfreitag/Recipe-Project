Title: 483193 Spam Spread
Categories: Spam, Appetizers, Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Tomato paste
-Garlic salt or powder
1 cn Spam, ground
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 lb Velveeta
Grind Spam and Velveeta.  Mix with remaining ingredients.  Spread on
buns and broil.  Serve warm.  Mixture keeps in refrigerator for quite
a while. It also freezes well.
