Title: 2969 Spam Salad
Categories: Spam, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Grated Cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Or more mayonnaise to
-moisten
1 cn Spam (Treet or generic)
1/2 c  Chopped dill pickle
2 tb Onion, chopped (optional)
1/2 c  Grated carrot (optional)
Use a grater and grate your Spam.  Add remaining ingredients. Stir.
This is good served on bread as sandwiches.
