Title: Protein Fortified Milk
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Powdered skim milk
1 c  Whole milk
Mix ingredients in a blender or with a wire whisk until free of
lumps. Keep refrigerated.

186 calories, 11.7 gm protein.

NOTE: For extra protein increase the amount of milk powder. OPTION:
substitute chocolate milk for white milk. 235 calories, 11.1 gm
protein.
