Title: 498798 Italian Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Oil
4    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Chopped nuts
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
Sift together flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.  Make a well in
the center and add oil, eggs, and vanilla.  Mix by hand.  Then add
chopped nuts.  Bake in greased 9x  13 inch pan at 350 degrees for 45
to 50 minutes. When they are warm, put a thin icing of confectioners'
sugar, water and a drop of vanilla, cut into squares.
