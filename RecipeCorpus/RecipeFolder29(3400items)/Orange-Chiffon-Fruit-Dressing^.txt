Title: Orange Chiffon Fruit Dressing^
Categories: Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 16 servings
2 tb (or 3) orange juice
Concentrate
1/2 c  Evaporated skim milk
1/2 c  Lowfat cottage cheese
Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth. Serve with fresh
fruit. Makes 1 cup.

Per tablespoon serving: Calories: 15 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 2g
Fat: trace Sodium: 37mg Cholesterol: trace
