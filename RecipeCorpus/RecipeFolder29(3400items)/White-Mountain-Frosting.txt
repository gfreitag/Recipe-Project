Title: White Mountain Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Egg whites
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Light corn syrup
2 tb Water
Mix sugar, syrup, and water in 1 quart saucepan.  Cover and heat to
boiling point over medium heat.  Then uncover and boil rapidly until
mixture reaches 242 degrees on a candy thermometer or small amount of
mixture dropped into cold water forms a firm ball and holds its
shape. As mixture boils beat egg whites in 1 1/2 quart bowl just
until stiff peaks form. Pour hot syrup very slowly in thin stream
into egg whites and beat constantly on medium speed.  Add vanilla and
beat on high speed until stiff peaks form. Will frost a 13-by-9 inch
cake or two layers.
