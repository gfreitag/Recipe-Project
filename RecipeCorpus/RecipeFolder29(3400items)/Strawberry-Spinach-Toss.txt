Title: Strawberry Spinach Toss
Categories: Salads, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------STRAWBERRY SPINACH TOSS-----------------------
1/3 c  Broken pecans; toasted
1/3 c  Gouda or Edam; cut in
-julienne strips; about 2 oz
5 c  Spinach; torn in bite-size
-pieces
1 c  Sliced strawberries
1 c  Honeydew melon balls
MMMMM-------------------GINGER-HONEY DRESSING------------------------
1/2 ts Grated ginger OR
1/4 ts Ground ginger
2 tb Lime juice
2 tb Honey
1 tb Vegetable oil
Toss all salad ingredients.

Shake all dressing ingredients in tightly covered container.  Yields 5
tablespoons dressing.
