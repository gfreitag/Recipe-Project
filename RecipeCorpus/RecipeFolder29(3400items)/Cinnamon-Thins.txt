Title: Cinnamon Thins
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie
Yield: 40 thins
1    Egg; well beaten
1 1/3 c  Sugar
3 tb Cinnamon
3 c  Quick mix2
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Milk
Mix quick mix and 2 T sugar. In another bowl mix margarine, milk and
egg. Add to dry ingredients.  Knead then form dough into 40 small
balls. Mix 1-1/3 c sugar with cinnamon and sprinkle generously on wax
paper. coat each ball with mixture.  Place balls on another piece of
wax paper and roll with rolling pin till paper thin turning over
several times. Bake 4-5 min at 450 degrees.
