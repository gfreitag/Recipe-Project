Title: Pizza Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Pizza, Desserts
Yield: 2 pizza
1 c  Pineapple chucks;
2    Bananas;
1 pk Strawberries;
2 tb Cornstarch;
1/2 ts Sugar;
1/4 ts Cinnamon;
2 lg Containers Cool Whips
Coconut
1    Yellow cake mix
1/4    Cube margarine;
2    Eggs;
1/4 c  Brown sugar;
1/4 c  Water;
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
1 cn Mandarin oranges;
1 cn Fruit cocktail;
Mix cake mix, butter, eggs, brown sugar and water.  Add nuts.  Grease
and flour a 16" and 12" pizza pan.  Spread on batter and bake at 350
degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.  Drain canned fruit and save 1-1/2 cups
of juice. Bring to a boil the juice and 2 tablespoons cornstarch, 1/2
teaspoon sugar and 1/4 cinnamon.  Cook until thick, but will still
pour. Cover cooled cake with Cool Whip and top with fruit.  Sprinkle
with coconut and drizzle with cooled sauce. Source: Nancy Byrum,
