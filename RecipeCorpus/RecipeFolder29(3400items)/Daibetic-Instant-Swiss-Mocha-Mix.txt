Title: Daibetic Instant Swiss Mocha Mix
Categories: Diabetic, Beverages
Yield: 1 cup
2 tb Instant coffee
1/2 c  Instant skim milk powder
2 tb Cocoa
Combine skim milk powder, cocoa and instant coffee in container of
blender. Blend at high speed until well mixed. Place in dry jar and
store.

To make one serving, add 2 tablespoons of instant swiss mocha mix to
a cup of boiling water.

Makes 1/2 cup mix 1 serving = 2 Tablespoons
1 milk choice
5 g carbohydrate
3 g protein
140 kilojoules
32 calories
