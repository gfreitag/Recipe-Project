Title: High Calorie Hot Chocolate
Categories: Low cal/fat, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Chocolate syrup
2 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
4 oz Half & half cream
4 oz Whole milk
You won't even notice the extra calories hidden in this creamy
version of an old favorite.

Heat milk and cream.  Stir in syrup and milk powder until smooth.
Serve immediately.

Makes 1 serving, 387 calories, 12 g protein.
