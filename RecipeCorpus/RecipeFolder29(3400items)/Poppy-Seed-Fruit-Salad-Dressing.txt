Title: Poppy Seed Fruit Salad Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Fresh orange juice
1 c  Canola oil
1 tb Poppy seeds
Mixed fresh fruit, diced
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Onion salt
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
Mix first 6 ingredients together until sugar is dissolved.  Process
and add oil slowly.  Stir in poppy seeds and pour over fresh diced
fruit. Allow to chill for 2 hours to blend flavours.  Makes 1-1/2
cups of dressing.

Origin: Adapted from a recipe in Appeal, Spring 1995 issue. Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, Mar/95.
