Title: Better Then Tom Selleck
Categories: Puddings, Chocolate
Yield: 16 - 20 folks
1 c  Powdered sugar;
1 c  Cool whip;
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding
1 pk Instant chocolate pudding;
3 c  Milk;
1 1/2    Cubes margarine;
1 c  Flour;
1/2 c  Brown sugar;
3/4 c  Pecans;
1 oz Cream cheese; softened
Mix together the first four ingredients and bake 325 F. for 25
minutes. Stir several times.  Reserve 1/2 cup of baked mixture for
top and spread remainder on the bottom of a 9" x 13" baking dish.
Cool. Mix cream cheese and powdered sugar and fold in Cool Whip.
Spread over the bottom layer in backing dish.  Spread chocolate
pudding made with 1-1/2 cup milk. Spread vanilla pudding made with
1-1/2 cup milk. Sprinkle with reserved mix. Refrigerate until serving
time. Serve 16-20.
