Title: Whiskey Sauce - Great Chefs
Categories: Basics, Salsa/sauce, Masterchefs, New orleans
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-----------------------WHISKEY SAUCE----------------------------
1/4 c  Bourbon
1 c  Cream, heavy
1/2 c  Sugar
Whiskey Sauce: ==============

Beat the cream until it's slightly thickened.

Add sugar and beat until thick.

Whisk in the bourbon and serve with the souffle.

Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions
