Title: Beef Porcupines
Categories: Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Catsup
1 c  Water
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
2 c  Applesauce
1 c  Pre-cooked rice
1/2 c  Cut up onions
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix ground beef, applesauce, rice,
onions, salt and pepper together.  Shape meat mixture into balls.
Place 1 layer deep in a 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan.  Mix catsup and
water together and pour over meatballs.  Bake for 1 hour. Randy Rigg
