Title:  MEATLOAF
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
Tomatoes
1 ts Seasoned salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ts Rubbed sage
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Plus 2 Tbsp. milk
1/4 c  Plus 2 Tbsp. tomato sauce
2    Eggs, beaten
2 lb Ground chuck
1 1/4 c  Finely crumbled biscuits or
-fresh
Breadcrumbs
1 c  Finely chopped onion
1 c  Finely chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Plus 2 Tbsp. uncooked
-regular oats
1/4 c  Plus 2 Tbsp. finely chopped
-seeded
Prehat oven to 375 degrees.  Combine first 9 ingredients in a large
bowl and mix well. Stir in milk, tomato sauce, eggs; mix in beef
until well blended. Shape into a 10x5" loaf and place on a broiler
rack set in a larger pan. Bake for 1 hour to 1 hour 20 minutes, or
until done. Serve with mashed potatoes.
