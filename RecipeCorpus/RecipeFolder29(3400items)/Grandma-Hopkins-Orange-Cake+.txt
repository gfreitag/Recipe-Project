Title: Grandma Hopkins Orange Cake+
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 cake
1 ts Salt
1 c  Sour milk
1 c  Ground up raisins
1    Orange and rind, ground
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
2    Eggs
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Soda
1 ts Baking powder
Mix in order given, save juice of orange for glaze topping. Bake cake
at 350 degrees F. for 35 to 40 minutes.

Glaze: Add 1/2 cup sugar to orange juice-when cake is done, pour over
cake and bake until it forms a sugar crust.
