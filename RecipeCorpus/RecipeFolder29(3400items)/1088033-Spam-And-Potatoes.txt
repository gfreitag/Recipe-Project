Title: 1088033 Spam And Potatoes
Categories: Spam, Potato
Yield: 5 servings
1 cn Cream of mushroom
-or cream of chicken soup
1    (12 oz.) can Spam
2 lg Irish potatoes
Peel potatoes and slice very thin.  Slice Spam in thin slices. In a
casserole dish put a layer of potatoes and a layer of Spam until all
are used.  Pour the can of soup over the potatoes and Spam. Rinse the
can with a small amount of water and pour over casserole to make it
bubble up. Bake covered in 350 degree oven until potatoes are done.
About 1 hour. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-01-95
