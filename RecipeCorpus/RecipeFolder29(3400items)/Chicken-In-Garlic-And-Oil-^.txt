Title: Chicken In Garlic And Oil ^
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
6 md Carrots, julienned
1 md Onion, very thinly sliced
2 cl Garlic, chopped
1    Lemon, juice of
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 c  Olive oil
3 lb Chicken, cut in serving
-pieces
4 md Potatoes, peeled and cut in
1/2" wedges
Preheat oven to 350F. Pour olive oil in bottom of a 14x10x2 1/2" pan
or other large roasting pan. Dip chicken pieces into oil, turning to
coat. Turn chicken skin side up. Arrange potatoes, carrots and onions
around chicken. Sprinkle garlic and lemon juice on top. Season with
salt and pepper. Bake for 1 hour, basting every 15 minutes. Remove
from oven and serve. 4 servings.
