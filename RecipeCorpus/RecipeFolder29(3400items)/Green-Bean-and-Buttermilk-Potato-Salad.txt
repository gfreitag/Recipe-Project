Title: Green Bean and Buttermilk Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Potato, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Dijon mustard
1 ts Prepared horseradish
3    Green onions, finely chopped
1/3 c  Chopped fresh dill, divided
1/2 ts Salt and pepper
2 lb Small new potato nuggets
1/2 lb Green beans, trimmed and cut
-into 1 inch lengths
3/4 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  Sour cream
Cook unpeeled potatoes until fork tender, drain and cool.  Cut into
bite sized pieces if necessary.  Cook green beans until tender crisp,
and a nice bright green.  Rinse under cold water to stop the cooking
process. Whisk together the remaining ingredients except the dill.
Mix together the cooked cooled potatoes and the green beans.  Pour
dressing over and stir gently until coated.  Sprinkle dill over top
and allow to chill for at least 1 hour.  Serves 4-6.

Origin: Adapted from a recipe in Appeal Magazine, Spring 1995. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Mar/95.
