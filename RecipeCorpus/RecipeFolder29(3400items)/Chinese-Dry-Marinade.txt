Title: Chinese Dry Marinade
Categories: Marinades, Asian, Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Ground tumeric
1/4 ts Celery seed
1/4 ts Dry mustard
3 tb Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Paprika
1 ts Salt
3/4 ts Monosodium glutamate
Preparation Time: 0:05 Combine ingredients and use for marinade for
spare ribs and pork.
