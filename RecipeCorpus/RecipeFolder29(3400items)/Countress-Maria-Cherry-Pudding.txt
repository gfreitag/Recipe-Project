Title: Countress Maria Cherry Pudding
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
17 oz Sour cherries; drained and
-reserve juice
2 tb Butter, divided
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Sugar
2 tb Flour
3/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg
2 tb Shortening
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Vanilla
Mix 3/4 cup of sugar, the egg, shortening, 1 cup of flour, soda, salt,
vanilla and cherries well.  Pour the batter into a 9" square pan.
Bake at 350 for 40 minutes..  Combine the brown sugar, 1 T of butter
and 2 T of cherry juice in a saucepan.  Bring the mixture to a boil.
Pour the sauce over the hot cake.  Combine 1/3 cup of sugar, 2 T of
flour, 1 T of butter and 1 cup of cherry juice in a saucepan.  Boil
until clear. Serve warm over pudding.  Source:  Beverly Hillbillies
