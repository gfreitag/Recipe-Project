Title: Marshmallow Delights
Categories: Pastry, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Ground cinnamon
8 lg Marshmallows
1/4 c  Butter or margarine, melted
1 ea (8 oz. tube) refrigerated
-rescent rolls
1/4 c  Sugar
Separate rolls into eight triangles.  Combine sugar and cinnamon.
Dip each marshmallow into butter, roll in cinnamon-sugar and place on
a triangle. Pinch dough around marshmallow, sealing all edges.  Dip
tops of dough into remaining butter and cinnamon-sugar.  Place with
sugar side up in greased muffin cups.  Bake at 375 deg. F for 13-15
minutes. Serve warm.
