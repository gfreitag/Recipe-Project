Title: Broiled Chicken With Mango Butter ^
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
8    Chicken pieces
2 tb Oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Butter, softened
1 tb Mango chutney
1/2    Lime, juice of
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
Combine butter with mango chutney, lime juice and cayenne pepper,
blending well. Shape into a roll, wrap in foil or wax paper and chill
for about 2 hours in freezer. Preheat broiler. Brush chicken pieces
lightly with oil; season with salt and pepper. Broil at least 30
minutes, turning at least twice and brushing again with oil. Divide
mango butter into 8 equal rounds and lay one on each portion of hot
broiled chicken. 4 servings.
