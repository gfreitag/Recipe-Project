Title: Mama's Maple Parfait
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Chopped pecan meats
1 pt Vanilla Ice Cream
Candied pineapple
1/2 c  Maple sirup
1    Egg yolk, well beaten
1/3 c  Heavy cream, whipped
Cook maple sirup with egg yolk in top of double boiler until slightly
thickened. Remove, chill and add to whipped cream. Stir in nut meats.
Fill parfait glasses with alteernate layers of maple mixture and ice
cream. Top with pieces of pineapple.
