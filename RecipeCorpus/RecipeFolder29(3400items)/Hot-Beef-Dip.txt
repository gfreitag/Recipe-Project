Title: Hot Beef Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Meats, Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Chipped dried beef
3 oz Can of mushrooms
1/4 c  Shredded Parmesan cheese
2 tb Chopped parsley
1/4 c  Onion
1 tb Butter
1 c  Milk
8 oz Cream cheese
Heat cheese and milk until blended; add onions that have been cooked
in butter and remaining ingredients.  Serve hot with homemade bread
sticks or crackers. Randy Rigg
