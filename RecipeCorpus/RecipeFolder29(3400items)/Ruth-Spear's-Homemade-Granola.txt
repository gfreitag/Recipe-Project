Title: Ruth Spear's Homemade Granola
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 c  Soy flour
3 tb Sesame seeds
1/4 c  Safflower oil
1/4 c  Honey or malt syrup
1/2 c  Raisins
6 c  Rolled oats
3 tb Sunflower seeds
1 c  Untoasted Wheat Germ
1 tb Vanilla extract
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Grated nutmeg
Preheat oven to 250 F. Combine oats, wheat germ, spices, soy flour,
seeds and vanilla. Combine oil and honey. Stir to blend. Pour honey
mixture over oat mixture, toss thoroughly so there are no large
lumps. Spread out on a large baking pan and bake for 2 hours,
stirring every 20 minutes to redistribute the grains.  Add the
raisins the last 20 minutes. Cool in pan then transfer to a tightly
sealed jar or jars and refrigerate. Will keep for at least a month.

Nutrient: 237 calories, 9 g protein, 34 g carbohydrates, 8 g fat, 0
cholesterol, 3 mg sodium.
