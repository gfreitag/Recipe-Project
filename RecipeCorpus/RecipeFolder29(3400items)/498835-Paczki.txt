Title: 498835 Paczki
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1    Whole egg
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Nutmeg (optional)
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
4 1/2 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk
2    Yeast cakes
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sugar
3    Egg yolks
Scald milk and allow to cool.  Put yeast into warm milk.  Beat sugar
and butter until fluffy.  Add eggs and salt and flavoring. Add flour
and milk gradually, beating well.  Let rise in warm place until dough
doubles in bulk.  Break dough into ball and let rise again then deep
fry until golden brown.
