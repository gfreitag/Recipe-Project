Title: Whole-wheat Bread *M*
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Bread flour
3 tb Nonfat dry milk
1 tb Sugar
1 1/2 c  Warm water
===FOR 11/2 LB. LOAF===
1 pk Yeast
1 ts Salt
2 1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
2 tb Soft butter
Add all ingredients in the order listed, select white bread and

push start.
NOTE: A nutritious real whole wheat bread good for sandwichwes and

"great" for toast.
