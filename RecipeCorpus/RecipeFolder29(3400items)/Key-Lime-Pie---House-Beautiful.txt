Title: Key Lime Pie - House Beautiful
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM------------------FOR GRAHAM CRACKER CRUST-----------------------
1/3 c  Butter, melted
1 1/4 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/4 c  Sugar
MMMMM------------------------FOR FILLING-----------------------------
2 ts Grated lime zest
1/2 ts Cream of tartar
1/3 c  Sugar
4    Eggs, separated
1 cn (14-oz) sweetened condensed
-milk
1/2 c  Key lime juice
Prepare crust: Combine all ingredients for crust. Using the back of a
large spoon, press crumb mixture firmly on bottom of a 9-inch pie
dish. Bake crust for 6 to 8 minutes in a preheated 375'F oven. Remove
and allow to cool.

Prepare filling: In a medium-size bowl, whisk yolks until pale in
color. Stir in the condensed milk. Fold in the Key Lime juice and
lime zest. In a small bowl, stiffly beat 1 egg white and fold it into
the lime mixture. Transfer mixture to prepared piecrust.

In a medium-size bowl, beat the remaining egg whites with cream of
tartar until frothy. Add sugar 1 T at a time, beating until stiff but
not dry. Spread on top of pie. Bake pie until the meringue is golden
in a preheated 350'F oven for about 20 minutes. Cool and chill before
serving. Serves 8.
