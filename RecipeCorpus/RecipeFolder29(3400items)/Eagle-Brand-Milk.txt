Title: Eagle Brand Milk
Categories: Homemade
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Cold water
1 1/3 c  Dry milk
Pour water into 2 cup measure. Stir in powdered milk until smooth.
Let set til well dissolved. Microwave on high 45-60 seconds till it
steams. STir in sugar and vanilla till dissolved. Equals 1 can eagle
brand. Can be frozen.
