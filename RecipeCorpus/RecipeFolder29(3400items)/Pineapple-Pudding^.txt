Title: Pineapple Pudding^
Categories: Puddings, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Cornstarch
2 tb Pineapple juice
4 lg Strawberries
2 c  Crushed pineapple,
Unsweetened, drained, juice
Reserved
Blend all ingredients except strawberries in a blender until smooth.
Pour into top of double boiler and cook until thickened. Cool
slightly and pour into 4 dessert dishes and chill. Top with
strawberry before serving.

Per serving: Calories: 124 Protein: trace Carbohydrates: 31g Fat:
trace Sodium: 1mg Cholesterol: 0mg
