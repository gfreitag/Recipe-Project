Title: Maple Syrup Cream Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Maple syrup
1 ea Pie shell, 9-inch,
-partially baked
1 c  Heavy cream
1 c  Milk
4 ea Eggs
Preheat oven to 325.  Combine the cream and milk in a saucepan and
heat until scaled.  Beat the eggs for a minute until they are foamy.
Stir in 1/2 cup maple syrup.  Slowly add the hot cream mixture to the
egg mixture, stirring constantly.  Pour the custard into the pie
shell and bake for 30 minutes.  Don't over bake; the center should
tremble slightly when the pie is gently shaken.  Remove from oven and
cool. Just before serving drizzle the remaining 1/4 cup of maple
syrup over the top.
