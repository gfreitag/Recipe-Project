Title: 20101 Spam Dip
Categories: Spam, Dips/spread, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1/4    Onion
1/2 ts Mustard
-Mayonnaise
1 lg Can Spam
3    Boiled eggs
1    Dill pickle
Grind or grate Spam, eggs, pickle and onion.  Mix together and add
mustard and mayonnaise to consistency.  Good served with Ritz
crackers. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-01-95
