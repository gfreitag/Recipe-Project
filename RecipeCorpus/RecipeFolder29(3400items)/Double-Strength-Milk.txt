Title: Double Strength Milk
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Nonfat dry milk powder
1 qt Milk
Drink as is for a beverage or use in recipes in place of regular milk.

Pour liquid milk into bowl or blender.  Add dry milk powder and beat
slowly until dry milk dissolves.  Refrigerate.

Makes 4 servings, 259 calories, 19 g. protein.
