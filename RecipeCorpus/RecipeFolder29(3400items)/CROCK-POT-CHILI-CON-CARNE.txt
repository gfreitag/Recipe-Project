Title: CROCK-POT CHILI CON CARNE
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Crockpot, Chili
Yield: 10 servings
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 c  Beef stock
1 cn Tomatoes; 28 ozs.
1 cn Tomato paste; 8 oz.
4 cn Red kidney beans; 1 lb cans
4 lb Ground beef
3 tb Shortening
2 c  Chopped onion
2    Garlic cloves; crushed
4 tb Chili powder
3    Beef bouillon cubes; crushed
1 1/2 ts Paprika
Heat shortening in skillet and brown beef, discard fat, Combine all
ingredients in removable liner, stirring well. Place liner in base.
Cover and cook on low 8-10 hours; high 4-5 hours or auto 6-7 hours
