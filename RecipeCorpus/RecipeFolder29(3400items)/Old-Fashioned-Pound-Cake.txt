Title: Old Fashioned Pound Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
10 ea Eggs
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Cognac, rum or vanilla
4 1/2 c  Flour
2 1/4 c  Sugar
1 lb Butter
Beat butter until smooth, mixer on low speed. Add sugar and beat until
fluffy. Add flavoring then eggs in 4-5 portions. Add salt to sifted
flour. Add 1/2 cup flour at a time beating well after each addition,
on low speed. Line loaf pans with brown paper. Bake 40- 50 minutes at
350 degrees. At holiday time, the addition of fruit makes this a
wonderful fruit cake, or top it with strawberries and cream.
