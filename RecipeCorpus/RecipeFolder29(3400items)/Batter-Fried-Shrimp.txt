Title: Batter-Fried Shrimp
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
Planters Peanut Oil
2 lb Large Shrimp, uncooked
3/4 c  Flour, unsifted
3/4 c  Water
1    Egg, slightly beaten
Peel shrimp leaving tails on; devein.  Split shrimp part way through,
butterfly fashion.  Combine flour, water, egg, sugar and salt to make
a batter.  Dip shrimp into batter and fry in deep, hot (375 degrees
F.) Planters Peanut Oil until golden brown.  Drain on paper towels.

Makes about 6 servings.
