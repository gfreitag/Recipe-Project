Title: Stir Fry Snow Peas And Red Pepper^
Categories: Stir fry, Healthy, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
3    Green onions, diced
3/4 lb Snow peas
SAUCE
1 tb Low sodium soy sauce
2 ts Cornstarch
2 tb Cold water
1 tb Dry sherry
1 tb Apple juice concentrate
1 cl Garlic, minced
3 tb Chicken stock
1 cn (8oz) bamboo shoots,
Drained
1 cn (8oz) sliced water
Chestnuts
1/2 lb Sliced mushrooms
1    Sweet red pepper, julienned
Soften garlic in wok in chicken stock. Add remaining veggies and stir
fry 3-4 minutes. Combine sauce ingredients; add to veggies and stir
until thickened. Eat

Per serving: Calories: 63 Protein: 3g Carbohydrates: 13g Fat: 0
Sodium: 114mg Cholesterol: trace
