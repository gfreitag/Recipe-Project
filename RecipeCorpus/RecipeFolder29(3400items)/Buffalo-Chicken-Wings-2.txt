Title: Buffalo Chicken Wings 2
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 24 wings
MMMMM-----------------------CHICKEN WINGS----------------------------
2 ts (2-5) hot sauce (Frank's
-Louisiana Hot Sauce is the
-local favorite)
1 ts White vinegar
24    Chicken Wings (about 4 lbs)
-Salt (optional)
-Freshly ground black pepper
4 c  Vegetable or corn oil
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
MMMMM-----------------BLUE CHEESE DIPPING SAUCE----------------------
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb White vinegar
1/4 c  Blue cheese, crumbled
Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Onions, finely chopped
1 ts Garlic, finely minced
1/4 c  Parsley, finely chopped
1/2 c  Sour cream
MMMMM-----------------------SIDE MUNCHIES----------------------------
Celery sticks
Carrot sticks
CHICKEN WINGS:

Cut of tips and separate each wing at the joint.  Sprinkle wings with
salt and pepper to taste.  Heat oil in deep-fat fryer or large heavy
pot. When it is quite hot, add half the wings and cook about 10
minutes, stirring occasionally.  When wings are golden brown and
crisp, remove them and drain well.  Add remaining wings and repeat
process. Meanwhile, melt butter in saucepan, add hot sauce to taste
and vinegar. Pour hot mixture over wings and mix well to cover. Serve
with Blue Cheese Dressing and celery and carrot sticks.

BLUE CHEESE DRESSING:

Combine all ingredients and chill for one hour before serving.
