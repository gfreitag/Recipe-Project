Title: 650272 Baked Spam
Categories: Spam
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Pineapple juice
1 tb Whole cloves
2 cn Spam
1 c  Brown sugar
Mix 2 tablespoons of pineapple juice with 1 cup brown sugar together
to make a paste.  Pat mixture into Spam and stud with cloves. Place
in a baking pan and baste with remaining pineapple juice. Bake about
1/2 to 1 hour at 350 degrees.  Continue to baste. Remove from oven.
Let cool, then slice.
