Title: Red Hot Spicy Buffalo Wings
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Melted butter
2 1/2 lb Chicken wings
1/2 c  Red Hot cayenne pepper sauce
MMMMM-------------------------SERVE WITH------------------------------
Blue Cheese Dressing
Celery & Carrot sticks
Split wings and discard tips. Deep fry wings @ 400 degrees for 12
minutes or until cooked; drain. In bowl, combine Red Hot & butter.
Toss wings in sauce to coat. serve with vegetable sticks & dressing.

For equally crispy wings: Broil or grill wings 30 to 45 minutes,
turning often, until cooked. Then toss in Red Hot & butter.
