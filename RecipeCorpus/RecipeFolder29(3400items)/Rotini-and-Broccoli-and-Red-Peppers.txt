Title: Rotini and Broccoli and Red Peppers
Categories: Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Chicken broth
1/2 c  Romano cheese; grated
1/2 lb Rotini pasta
1/4 c  Olive oil
3 cl Garlic; chopped
1 bn Broccoli; chopped
1    Red pepper; sliced
Boil, steam or microwave broccoli until crisp tender.  Heat oil in
large skillet; cook garlic over low heat for 1 minute.  Add peppers
and pre-cooked broccoli; stir fry over high heat for 4 minutes.  Pour
in broth and cook 3 minutes.  Add cooked pasta and cheese; season to
taste and toss. 412 calories and 18 grams fat.  Source: Store ad
