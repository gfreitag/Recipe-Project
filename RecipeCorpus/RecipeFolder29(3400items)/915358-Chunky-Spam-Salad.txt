Title: 915358 Chunky Spam Salad
Categories: Spam, Salads, Dips/spread, Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings
-less-salt, finely chopped
-Dash red pepper
1/2 c  Finely chopped bell pepper
1/2 c  Finely chopped celery
1 ct (8 oz.) cream cheese,
-softened
1 c  Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
1 cn (12 oz.) Spam, reg. or
In small bowl beat cream cheese and salad dressing until smooth. Add
remaining ingredients.  Stir until well blended.  Serve with crisp
crackers, or as a filling for finger sandwiches.  Makes 4 1/2 cups.
