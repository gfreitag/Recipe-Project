Title: 564692 Rocky Mountain Brisket
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Cayenne pepper
6 tb Butter, melted
1 tb Dry mustard
2 tb Liquid smoke
-Salt & pepper
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 ts Celery salt
1/2 c  Water
-Beef brisket
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Pepper
2 tb Chili powder
1 ts Crumbled bay leaf
2 tb Liquid smoke
--BARBECUE SAUCE:
3 tb Brown sugar
14 oz Catsup
Combine salt, pepper, chili powder, bay leaves and liquid smoke. Rub
meat on all sides with mixture.  Place meat fat side up in large
roasting pan. Cover tightly and bake 4 hours at 325 degrees.  After
meat is cooked, cut thin slices across the grain; mix with the
barbecue sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.  Serve on buns.
