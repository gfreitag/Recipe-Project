Title: 654086 Spam And Cheese Souffle
Categories: Spam
Yield: 1 servings
4    Eggs
3 c  Milk
-Salt
1 cn Mushroom soup
1/2 c  Milk
12 sl Bread, crusts off
-Soft butter or margarine
1 cn Spam, grated
-Cheese, grated (about same
-amount as Spam)
Butter 1 side of bread slices and place in 9 x 13 inch pan, butter
side down. Sprinkle Spam over bread.  Sprinkle cheese over meat. Beat
eggs, milk and salt. Dip remaining slices of bread in egg mixture and
place over cheese.  Pour balance over all.  let stand in refrigerator
overnight. Bake about 1 hour at 350 degrees. Take from oven and pour
mushroom soup and milk over top.  Bake in oven another 10-15 minutes.
