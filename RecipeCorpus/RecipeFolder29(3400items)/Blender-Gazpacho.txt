Title: Blender Gazpacho
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
2 lg Cucumbers, chopped
1/3 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1    Lemon, juice of
1 tb Aromatic bitters
Pepper
8 lg Red tomatoes, peeled,
-seeded and chopped
1    Clove garlic, minced
2 sm Onions, chopped
1    Green pepper, seeded and
-chopped
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
1/2 c  Scallions, sliced
Puree the tomatoes in blender. Place in a large bowl. Combine the
remaining ingredients. Ladle the mixture, about 2 cups at a time,
into the blender, and blend until smooth. Combine with the tomato
puree. Chill. Serve very, very cold, sprinkled with scallions.

From: Steve Herrick Source: [Best Recipes - July/August 1991]
