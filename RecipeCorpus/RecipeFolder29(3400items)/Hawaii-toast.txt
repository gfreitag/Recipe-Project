Title: Hawaii toast
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings  1 slice bread butter or mayo 1 slice ham (Danish type) 1 slice
pineapple 1 slice cheese (mozzarella, swiss, american all good)

Spread bread with butter or mayo. Put ham on top. pineapple on top of
ham. Cheese on top of pineapple. Into the oven until cheese is nicely
melted. Submitted By FLORENCE THOMPSON   On   03-19-95
