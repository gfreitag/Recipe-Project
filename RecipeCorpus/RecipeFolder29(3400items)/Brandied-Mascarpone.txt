Title: Brandied Mascarpone
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Brandy
1 ts Vanilla
2    Egg whites
250 g  Mascarpone
1/2 c  Cream
2 ts Sugar
Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and stir well to mix. Beat the
egg-whites until stiff peaks form and fold into the mascarpone
mixture. Spoon into a serving bowl and refrigerate, covered, until
ready to serve.
