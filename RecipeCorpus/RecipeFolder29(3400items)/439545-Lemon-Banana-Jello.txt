Title: 439545 Lemon-Banana Jello
Categories: Velveeta, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 md Size can crushed pineapple
1/2 pt Whipping cream (or more if
-desire a thick topping)
1 lg Pkg. lemon Jello
4    Bananas, sliced
1 pk Miniature marshmallows
1 sm Box Velveeta cheese food
Prepare Jello per instructions on box; let partially set.  Mix in
pineapple and bananas and top with marshmallows.  Chill until firm.
Top with whipped cream (sweetened).  Grate cheese and spread on top.
Keep refrigerated. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-12-95
