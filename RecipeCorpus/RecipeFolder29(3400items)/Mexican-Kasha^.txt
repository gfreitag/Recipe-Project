Title: Mexican Kasha^
Categories: Nuts/grains, Healthy, Low cal/fat, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Cumin
1 ts Chili powder
2 cn (16oz) no salt added
Tomatoes, coarsley chopped,
Save juice
3/4 c  Uncooked kasha
1 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/2    Green pepper, chopped
1/2    Red pepper, chopped
2 cl Garlic, minced
1/4 c  Chicken stock
1 ts Dried oregano
Soften onion, garlic, celery and peppers in stock. Add remaining
ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until liquid
is absorbed and kasha is tender.

Per serving: Calories: 101 Protein: 4g Carbohydrates: 20g Fat: trace
Sodium: 38mg Cholesterol: 0mg
