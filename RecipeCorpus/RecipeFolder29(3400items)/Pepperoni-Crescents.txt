Title: Pepperoni Crescents
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks, Sausage, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 snacks
OR
Mozzarella Cheese
1    Egg white, slightly beaten
1 pk Crescent rolls (8 0z)
24 sl Pepperoni (1 1/2 inch size)
2 sl Provolone Cheese (Quartered)
Preheat oven to 375.  Separate dough into 8 triangles.  Place three
slices of the pepperoni on center of each triangle, top with
quartered cheese slice.  Roll up, start at shortest side of triangle
and roll to opposite point.

Place rolls point side down on ungreased cookie sheet; curve into
crescent shape.

Brush with egg white.   Bake 10 - 14 minutes or until golden brown.
