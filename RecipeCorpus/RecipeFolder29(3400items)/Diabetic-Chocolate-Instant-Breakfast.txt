Title: Diabetic Chocolate Instant Breakfast
Categories: Diabetic, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Choclate sauce
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Milk
1 md Egg
1/2 sm Banana
Combine milk, egg, banana, chocolate sauce and vanilla in a blender.
Blen at high speed 1 minute or until frothy.

Makes 1 serving 1 serving = 1 protein choice
2 milk choices
1 fruits & vegetable choice
23 g carbohydrate
14 g protein
6 g fat
850 kilojoules
202 calories
