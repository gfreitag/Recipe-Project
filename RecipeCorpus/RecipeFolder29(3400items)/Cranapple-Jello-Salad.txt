Title: Cranapple Jello Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Hot water
1 c  Pineapple juice
1 c  Cranberries
1 c  Grated apples
3 oz Strawberry Jello
Put sugar over cranberries and apples.  Combine Jello, water, and
pineapple juice; add cranberries and apples.  Chill until served.
