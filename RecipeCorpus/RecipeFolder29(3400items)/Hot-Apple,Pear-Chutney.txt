Title: Hot Apple/Pear Chutney
Categories: Fruit, Pickles, Condiments
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 c  Oil
2 tb Mustard seeds
15    Peppercorns
2 ts Cumin powder
1 ts Turmeric
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/4 lb Sugar
4    Or more green chiles
2 lb Tart cooking apples or
2 lb Green pears
2 tb Salt
1/2 c  Seedless Raisins
1    Head garlic; peeled
1    Inch of fresh ginger
Seed and chop the chile peppers. Peel, core and slice the apples;
sprinkle them with the salt and set aside. Finely mince half the
garlic and ginger. Finely slice the other half. Heat the oil, put in
all the ginger and garlic and fry until fragrant. Add the other
spices and the chopped chiles. Cook gently for a few min and add the
apples or pears, raisins, vinegar and sugar. Stir and simmer on low
for about 30 minutes. Cool and bottle. Submitted By JIM WELLER   On
03-29-95
