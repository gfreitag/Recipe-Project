Title: 721094 Watergate Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
16 oz Container Cool Whip
2 sm Pkgs. instant pistachio
Pudding
Or any flavor desired
1 c  Flour
1    Cube soft margarine
1 1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
8 oz Softened cream cheese
1 c  Powdered sugar
There are 4 layers to this recipe.  Use an 8 x 12 inch baking dish.
1ST LAYER:  Mix together the following ingredients: 1 cup flour, 1
cube soft margarine and 1 cup walnuts.  Press in bottom of dish to
make crust. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.  Cool completely. 2ND
LAYER: Cream cheese, powdered sugar and 1 cup Cool Whip. Mix together
and spread over the crust. 3RD LAYER:  Mix pistachio instant pudding
mix with 3 cups of milk and spread over 2nd layer. 4TH LAYER: Spread
remaining Cool Whip on top and sprinkle with fine chopped nuts.  12
servings. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-17-95
