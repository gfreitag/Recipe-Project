Title: Refrigerator Grape Jelly
Categories: Can/preserv, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2    Envelopes
1 tb Liquid artificial sweetener
Unflavored powdered gelatin
3 c  Bottled unsweetened grape
Juice
2 tb Lemon juice
In a large heavy bottomed pan, mix grape juice, lemon juice and
gelatin. Bring to a full rolling boil over medium high heat, stirring
often, then boil, stirring, for 1 minutes.  Remove from heat and stir
in artificial sweetener.

Wash 3 half pint glass jars or plastic freezer containers.  Keep
glass jars hot until needed.  Ladle hot jelly into half pint jars or
refrigerator containers, leaving 1/4 inch head space.  Attach lids.
Store in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.  DO NOT FREEZE. DO NOT
PROCESS IN BOILING WATER CANNER.
