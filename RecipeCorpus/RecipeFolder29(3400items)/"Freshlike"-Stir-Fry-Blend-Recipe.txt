Title: "Freshlike" Stir-Fry Blend Recipe
Categories: Stir fry, Poultry, Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 1/2 ts Toasted sesame seeds (opt).
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Cooked chicken cubed
2 c  Cooked rice
16 oz Stir-fry Blend Vegetables
1 ts Instant beef bouillon gran.
5 tb Warm water
2 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Cornstarch
1/2 ts Sugar
Thaw Stir-fry blend veggies in hot water and drain. Dissolve beef
bouillon in warm water.  Add soy sauce, corn starch, sugar and garlic
powder. Stir well. Toast sesame seeds in a dry fry or wok pan using
medium heat until slightly darkened -about 2 minutes. Add oil to fry
pan or wok. Place on high heat. Add veggies and stir-fry for 2-3
minutes. Add soy sauce mixture and cooked meat.  Stir vegetables into
sauce and stir-fry until sauce thickens.  Add toasted sesame seeds.
Serve over rice. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-28-95
