Title: Stud Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 12 muffins
1/4 c  Light brown sugar
1 c  Milk
1/3 c  Maple syrup
3/4    Stick butter, melted
2 c  Flour
1 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 tb Baking powder
3/4 ts Salt
2    Eggs
Mix together flour, cornmeal, baking powder and salt. Beat eggs and
sugar in another bowl until smooth. Add Syrup and milk to egg mixture
and mix well.  Pour over dry ingredients and add 4 Tablespoons of the
melted butter. Fold until moistened. Fill muffin cups and bake 25-30
mins. at 350. Brush tops with remaining butter.
