Title: Crunchy Broccoli Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
-almonds or sunflower seeds,
-whole or chopped
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
8 oz Water chestnuts; sliced and
-drained
2 bn Broccoli
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 lb Bacon; crisp and crumbled
1/2 c  Nuts; walnuts, peanuts,
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2 tb Vinegar
2 tb Lemon juice
1 1/4 c  Mayonaise
2/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Vinegar; plus
Combine dressing ingredients, set aside.  Rinse broccoli well in cold
water and peel stalks.  Chop into bite sized pieces.  Add remaining
ingredients and mix with dressing.  Refrigerate and serve cold.
