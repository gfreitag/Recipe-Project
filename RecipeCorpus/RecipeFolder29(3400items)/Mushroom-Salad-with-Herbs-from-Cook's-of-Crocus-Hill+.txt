Title: Mushroom Salad with Herbs from Cook's of Crocus Hill+
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 recipe
1 tb Herbal vinegar
1/2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Chives, chopped
2 tb Parsley, chopped
3 tb Cilantro, chopped
2 c  Fresh mushrooms
Salt and freshly ground
-black pepper
2 tb Finely chopped onion
1    Clove garlic
1/4 c  Olive oil
Wipe mushrooms with a damp cloth. remove the stems and slice the caps
into thinslices. Put them in a serving bowl and add salt and black
pepper. stir in chopped onion and crushed garlic. Stir in oil and
vinegar, then the lemon juice and chopped herbs. Serve soon after
making.
