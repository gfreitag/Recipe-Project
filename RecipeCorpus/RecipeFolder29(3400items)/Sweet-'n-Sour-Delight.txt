Title: Sweet 'n Sour Delight
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
2 cn Dole pineapple chunks
Undrained
1/4 c  White vinegar
3 tb Brown sugar
3 tb Catsup
3 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Ground ginger
2 c  Hot cooked rice
1 lb Chicken or pork
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 md Onion, chunked
1 ea Clove garlic, pressed
=-or=-
1/4 ts Garlic powder
2 md Carrots, sliced
1 md Green bell pepper
Seeded, chunked
Cut pork or chicken into bite sized pieces. Brown in oil. reduce heat
to low, add onion and garlic. Cover, cook 10 minutes. Add carrots and
green pepper. Combine remaining ingredients, except rice. Stir into
skillet. Cover, simmer 10 minutes longer. Serve with rice.

Source: A Dole promotional booklet.
