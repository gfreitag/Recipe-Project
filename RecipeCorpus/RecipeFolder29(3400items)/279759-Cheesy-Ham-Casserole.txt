Title: 279759 Cheesy Ham Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Corkscrew noodles, cooked,
-drained
1/2 c  Red or green pepper,
-chopped
1/4 c  Milk
3/4 c  Seasoned croutons
-(optional)
1/2 c  Miracle Whip salad dressing
2 c  Fresh or frozen broccoli
-flowerets, thawed
1 1/2 c  (6 oz.) 100% natural Kraft
-shredded sharp Cheddar
-cheese
1 1/2 c  Chopped Oscar Mayer ham
Mix all ingredients except 1/2 cup cheese and croutons.  Spoon into 1
1/2 quart casserole.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese and croutons.
Bake at 350 degrees 30 minutes or microwave on high for 8 to 10
minutes or until heated through.  Makes 4 to 6 servings. Preparation
Time: 10 minutes. Cooking Time:  40 minutes.
