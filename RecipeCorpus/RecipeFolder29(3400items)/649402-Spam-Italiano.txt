Title: 649402 Spam Italiano
Categories: Spam
Yield: 1 servings
-sauce
1/2 ts Oregano
-Dash of garlic powder
1    (12 oz.) can Spam
-Mozzarella cheese
1 c  Ketchup or 8 oz. tomato
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Cut Spam into slices and place in a pie
pan. Cut cheese to fit between Spam slices.  Hold together with
toothpicks. Mix the rest of ingredients together.  Pour over the top.
Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
