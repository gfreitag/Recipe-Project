Title: Monkey Cake or Monkey Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2    Stick butter or margarine
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
4    Cans refrigerator biscuits
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Cinnamon
For the cake:  combine the sugars and the cinnamon in either a lunch
bag or a plastic bag.  Break the biscuits into quarters and add ten
to 12 pieces at a time to the bag and shake to coat.  Place in a
greased tube or bundt pan. For the sauce: Mix last three ingredients
together. Melt butter and sugar in small saucepan. Cook until thick.
Pour over biscuits and bake at 350 Ã¸ for 30 minutes.
