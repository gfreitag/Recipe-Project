Title: Hot Spinach Vinaigrette
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
2 ea 10 oz pkgs spinach
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Vinegar
2 tb Water
Remove coarse stems from spinach; wash leaves well; drain, then shred.
Combine remaining ingredients in a large saucepan; heat to boiling.
Add spinach; toss lightly to mix; cover.  Cook 2 to 3 minutes, or
just until wilted; drain.  Serve hot.  From Family Circle Magazine,
Aug '63. Submitted By TERRI WOLTMON   On   04-22-95
