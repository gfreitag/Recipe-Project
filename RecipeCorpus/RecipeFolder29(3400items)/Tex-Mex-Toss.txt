Title: Tex Mex Toss
Categories: Salads, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Cheddar cheese lite;shredded
12    Tortilla chips; crumbled
Ranch dressing; reduced fat
1/2    Romaine lettuce; torn
1    Tomato; cut into wedges
1/2    Avocado; cubed
1/2 c  Kidney beans; drained
Combine all ingredients and toss.  193 calories and 10 grams fat.
