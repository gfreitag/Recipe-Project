Title: Crescent Butter Cookies
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 cookies
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
Powdered sugar
2 c  Quick Mix
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
Mix all ingredients except powdered sugar. Roll into 2-1/2" lengths.
Shape into crescents.  Cook for 8-10 min at 350 degrees.  While warm
roll in powdered sugar.
