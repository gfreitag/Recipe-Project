Title: 951830 Mommie's Corn And Seafood Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables, Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
2    Stems celery
1 sm Bell pepper
1 md White or yellow onion
-Garlic powder
-Cayenne pepper
-Salt
-ot sauce (Tabasco)
-Parsley
1 lb Shrimp or crawfish (raw,
-peeled)
1 cn Creamed yellow corn
1 cn Whole kernel white corn
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 sm Potato
4 oz Heavy cream
12 oz Whole milk
1    Stick butter or oleo
Chop celery, onion, and bell pepper fine.  Saute seasonings in butter
until soft and onions are clear.  Add peeled, diced potato and raw
seafood. Saute 5 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients and seasonings
to taste. Simmer on low- medium to medium heat, approximately 1/2
hour, stirring occasionally. Serve with Parmesan Bread.
