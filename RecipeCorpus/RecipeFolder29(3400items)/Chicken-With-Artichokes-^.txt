Title: Chicken With Artichokes ^
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk (10 oz) frozen artichoke
-hearts, thawed
1 tb Cornstarch mixed with
1 tb Cold water
2 tb Lemon juice
3    Eggs, beaten
2 tb Chopped parsley
2 tb Butter
4    Chicken breast halves
1 cl Garlic, minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Chicken broth
1/4 c  White wine
Melt butter in heavy skillet. Brown chicken, then add garlic; saute
for 3 minutes. Add salt, pepper, chicken broth and wine. Cover and
simmer for 30 minutes. Add artichoke hearts and simmer, covered, for
10 minutes more. 4 servings.
