Title: Homemade Barbecue Oil
Categories: Can/preserv, Homemade, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Bay leaves
6 ea Black peppercorns
4 ea Mustard seeds
2 ea Onion slivers
1/2 c  Canola oil, or olive oil
Brush this on steaks, fish,
-seafood or any meat before
-grilling.  Try
Also in salads.  Fry
-crouton in this.
2 ea Garlic cloves, peeled
Place first 5 ingredients in any jar that holds 1/2 cup.  Pour canola
oil over top.  Cover.  Let stand in refrigerator for at least 4 days
before using.  Keeps up to 2 weeks ONLY.  MUST be refrigerated.
Makes 1 half pint. Origin: Jean Pare's Preserves Book. Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, Aug/94.
