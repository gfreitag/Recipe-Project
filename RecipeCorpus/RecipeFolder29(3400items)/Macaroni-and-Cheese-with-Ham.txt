Title: Macaroni and Cheese with Ham
Categories: Pasta, Meats, Main dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
-or mixed shredded cheeses
12 oz Can low-fat evaporated milk;
7 oz Macaroni; cooked drained
-(she use small shells)
1/2 lb Cheddar Cheese; shredded
Grease a 9-by-13" glass baking dish.  (She used a new fat-free cooking
spray, which work well.)  In a large bowl, mix cooked macaroni, ham
and cheese until well blended.   Spoon into baking dish.  Evenly pour
the evaporated milk over ingredients in the baking dish.  Bake in a
preheated 350 F. degree oven for 45 minutes, or until top of dish is
lightly browned. Let stand for 5 minutes.  Cut into squares to serve.
Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Food Section, Mar. 30, 1995.
