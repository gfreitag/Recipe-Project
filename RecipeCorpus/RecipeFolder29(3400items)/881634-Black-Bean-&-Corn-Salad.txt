Title: 881634 Black Bean & Corn Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1 sm Red pepper, diced
3/4 c  Picante sauce
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Cumin
1    Clove garlic, minced
1    (16 oz.) can black beans,
-rinsed & drained
4 oz Jack cheese, cubed 1/4"
1    (8 oz.) can whole kernel
-corn, drained
3/4 c  Sliced green onions
3/4 c  Thinly sliced celery
Combine beans, cheese, corn, onions, celery and red pepper in large
bowl. Combine picante sauce, oil, lemon juice, cumin and garlic. Toss
with bean mixture; chill.  Serve with additional picante sauce, if
desired. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-24-95
