Title: Braised Swiss Steak
Categories: Meats, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Stewed tomatoes
2 md Onions, thinly sliced
1 cl Garlic, crushed
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 lb Round steak, cut 1" thick
2 tb Packaged dry bread crumbs
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Butter or margarine, melted
Wipe steak with damp paper towels.  Combine bread crumbs, salt and
pepper.

Sprinkle steak with half the crumb mixture and pound into the steak
with a mallot.   Repeat on other side.

Cut steak into 6 portions.

Melt butter in large Dutch oven.  Add steak; brown on both sides, over
medium heat.   Add tomatoes, onion, garlic and Worcestershire sauce.

Note:  add 1/4 c of chopped celery, if desired...bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 2 hrs., or until meat is tender.

250 calories per serving
Makes 6 servings
