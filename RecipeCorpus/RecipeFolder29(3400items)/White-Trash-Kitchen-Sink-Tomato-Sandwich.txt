Title: White Trash Kitchen Sink Tomato Sandwich
Categories: Sandwich, Vegetables, Whitetrash
Yield: 6 servings
Mayonnaise
Salt and pepper to taste
1 lg To 2 lg tomatoes, chilled
2 sl Fresh bread
Slice tomatoes to 1/4 inch thick.  Spread mayonnaise very thickly on
each slice of bread.  Spread tomatoes on one side, salt and pepper
it, then place another layer of tomatoes and salt and pepper that
also. Cover with remaining slice of bread.  Roll up your sleeves,
lean over kitchen sink and proceed to eat while the juice runs down
your elbows.

Origin: Adapted from White Trash. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, April/95.
