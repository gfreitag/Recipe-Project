Title: Peanut Butter Burger
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
1 3/4 oz Brown Breadcrumps
2    Spring Onions
1 oz Crunchy peanut butter
1 oz Soft cheese with garlic/herb
1 ts Veg. Worcestershire Sauce
Typed for you by Kaz Dunkley

Preparation: Chop the spring onions

1. Preheat the grill to medium. 2. In a medium size bowl beat
together the peanut butter, cheese and
vegetarian worcestershire sauce. Stir in the breadcrumbs and
spring onions and shape into a burget.

3. Grill for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden. Serve in a bun
with
salad and mustard.

Per Serving:

312 Calories 4g Fibre (h) 26g Carbohydrate (m) 17g Fat (h) 2g
Saturated Fat (m) 15g Protein (m) 1.94g Salt (h) no added sugar
