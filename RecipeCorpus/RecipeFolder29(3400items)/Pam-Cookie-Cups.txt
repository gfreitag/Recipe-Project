Title: Pam Cookie Cups
Categories: Cake/cookie, Kids
Yield: 1 batch
Pam Cooking Spray
1 pk Refrigerated Cookie Dough
(20 oz)
MMMMM---------------------IDEAS FOR FILLING--------------------------
Decorate with Favorite
Topping
Ice  Cream
Pudding
Fruit
Freeze one package of cookie dough for at least 1 hour or until firm.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Turn a muffin tin upside down & spray the
outside of each cup with PAM. Evenly cut dough into 1/2 inch thick
slices & place 1 on top of each cup. Bake 11 to 13 minutes or until
golden brown. Dough will bake around cup. Cool 5 minutes then gently
remove. Fill cookie cup with ice cream, oudding or cut up fruit and
decorate with your favorite topping.
