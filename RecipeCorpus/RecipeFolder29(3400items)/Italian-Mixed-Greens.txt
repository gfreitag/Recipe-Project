Title: Italian Mixed Greens
Categories: Salads, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Blue cheese; crumbled
2 tb Walnuts; finely chopped
2 c  Leaf lettuce; torn
2 c  Escarole or endive; torn
8    Radicchio leaves; torn
MMMMM------------------GRAPEFRUIT HERB DRESSING-----------------------
1/4 c  Oil
1 tb Honey
Fresh ground pepper
1 tb Chives; finely chopped
1 ts Oregano; fresh and chopped
2 tb Grapefruit juice
2 tb Lemon juice
Place dressing ingredients in bowl or jar and blend.  Pour over
salad. 252 calories and 22 grams fat.  Source: Store ad
