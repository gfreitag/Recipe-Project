Title: Pineapple Steak Kabobs^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Lean flank steak, cut in
2    "cubes
1/2    Red bell pepper, cut in 1"
Chunks
1 cn (8oz) pineapple chunks,
Unsweetened, juice reserved
8 md Mushrooms
1 tb Tamari or low sodium soy
Sauce
2 tb Water
1 tb Sherry
1 cl Garlic, crushed
1 tb Apple juice concentrate
KABOBS:
Marinate steak in combined marinade ingredients 1-2 hours or more,
turning occasionally. Thread steak, peppers, pineapple and mushrooms
on skewers. Grill on preheated grill about 10 minutes or until
desired doneness, basting with marinade. Serve on rice.

Variation: Substitute hot salsa for the marinade.

Carolyn's note: The original recipe never said what to do with the
pineapple juice.

Per serving without rice: Calories: 201 Protein: 26g Carbohydrates:
13g Fat: 5g Sodium: 211mg Cholesterol: 70mg
