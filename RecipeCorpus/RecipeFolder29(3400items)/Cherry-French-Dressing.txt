Title: Cherry French Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Lemon Juice
2 tb Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
4 oz Maraschino Cherries --
Undrained
1/4 c  Salad Oil
Put all of the ingredients in a blender container and blend until the
cherries are finely chopped.  Chill before serving.  Shake the
dressing to blend, before using.

Makes 3/4 cup.
