Title: Spinach Salad Dressing
Categories: Salads, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 dressing
1/3 c  Catsup
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1 c  Oil
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
1/4 c  Vinegar
Mix all ingredients, shake well, pour over Spinach Salad With Diced
Chicken from previous recipe, toss and serve.

SOURCE:  S.H.A.R.E. Colorado March/April 1995
