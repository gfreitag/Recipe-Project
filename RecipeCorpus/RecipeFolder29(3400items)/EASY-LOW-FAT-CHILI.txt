Title: EASY LOW-FAT CHILI
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Chili
Yield: 7 servings
1 cn Salt-free tomato sauce
-(6 oz)
1 cn Low-salt diced tomatoes;
-undrained (14.5 oz)
2 ts Chili powder, or to taste
1 ts Salt (optional)
1/2 ts Pepper
1 md Onion; chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper; chopped
4 c  ;water; divided
1 cn Great Northern beans; rinsed
-and drained (15 oz)
1 cn Navy beans; rinsed and
-drained (15 oz)
In a large saucepan, cook the onion and green pepper in 1/2 cup water
until tender. Add beans, tomato paste and tomatoes. Stir in chili
powder, salt if desired, pepper and remaining water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
