Title: Coriander Pork Chops
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Brown sugar
2 ea Cloves of garlic
1 tb Crushed coriander seeds
8 ea Black peppercorns, crushed
Posted by Stu Hopper
----------------------------
4 ea Iowa Chops (1"thick)
3 tb Soy sauce
Combine all ingredients, except chops and mix lightly.  Cover chops
and set aside for 30 minutes, brushing occasionally with marinade
mixture. Place chops on grill (bone side down) and cook over moderate
heat 20 to 24 minutes, brushing occasionally with marinade mixture.
