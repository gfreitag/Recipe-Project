Title: 604294 Corn In Sour Cream
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Sour cream
3    Strips bacon
2 cn Whole kernel corn
Fry bacon in skillet until crisp.  Remove bacon; drain.  Pour bacon
drippings from skillet, reserving 2 tablespoons drippings. Return
reserved drippings to skillet; stir in corn.  Add salt and sour
cream; mix well. Crumble bacon over top; simmer until heated through.
Serve immediately. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-22-95
