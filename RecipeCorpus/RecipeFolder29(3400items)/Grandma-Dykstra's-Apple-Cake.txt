Title: Grandma Dykstra's Apple Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Countyfairs
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Peeled Apples, (Washington,
Course), sliced
3/4 c  Chopped Walnuts
2    Eggs
2 c  Granulated Sugar
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
2 ts Vanilla Extract
2 c  Flour
* Preheat oven to 350 F.

1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs. Add the sugar, oil and
vanilla. Mix well. Add 1 cup of flour, baking soda and salt and beat
for 1 min.

2. Beat in remaining flour, then fold in the apples and walnuts.
Spread batter in greased 9 x 11 x 2-inch baking pan. Bake for 35
mins, or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool
on a rack.

From Denise Dykstra for the YAKIMA VALLEY JUNIOR FAIR of Grandview,
WA. Fair dates: mid-August, for 4 days.
