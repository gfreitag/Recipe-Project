Title: Cheesy Broccoli Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Milk
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Cheddar cheese; extra sharp
Black pepper
2 tb Oil
3/4 lb Broccoli; coarsely chopped
1    Onion; chopped
3 tb Flour
2 c  Chicken broth
Cook onion and broccoli in oil, covered, over low heat for 10 minutes,
until broccoli is tender.  Mix in flour, then chicken broth, stirring
constantly; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and add milk and nutmeg;
simmer on low, covered for 4 minutes.  Turn off heat.  Puree soup in
blender or food processor; return to pot.  Stir in cheese over very
low heat, until melted.  Season to taste.  276 calories and 18 grams
fat. Source: Store ad Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   03-21-95
