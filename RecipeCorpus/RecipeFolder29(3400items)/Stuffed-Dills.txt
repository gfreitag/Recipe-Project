Title: Stuffed Dills
Categories: Appetizers, Pickles, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Mayonnaise
Large dill pickles
5 oz Can deviled ham
1 tb Minced celery
Combine ham, celery, and mayonnaise. Cut off end of pickles, and
hollow out centers. Pack solid with ham mixture. Place in the
refrigerator for several hours. Cut into thin slices and serve on
buttered crackers. Submitted By CAROL VERGE   On   04-13-95
