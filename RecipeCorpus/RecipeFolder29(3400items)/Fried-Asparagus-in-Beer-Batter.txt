Title: Fried Asparagus in Beer Batter
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
Garlic powder
Seasoned salt
Italian seasoning, to taste
Olive oil
1 ea To 2 pounds asparagus
1 c  Flour
1 cn Beer
Salt and pepper
Mix flower and seasonings together. Add beer to fry ingredients mixing
slowly until thick enough to cling to asparagus. Cut asparagus into
two inch pieces or leave whole. Deep fry in two inches olive oil
until golden brown, turning once. Posted by: Steve Herrick Source:
[Victoria Island]
