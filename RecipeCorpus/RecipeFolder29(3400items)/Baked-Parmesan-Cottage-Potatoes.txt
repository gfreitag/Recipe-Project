Title: Baked Parmesan Cottage Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Potato
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
1/8 ts Pepper
3 md Potatoes; thinly sliced
1 sm Onion; thinly sliced and
-separated into rings
In a greased 15x10x1-inch baking pan arrange potatoes and onion in a
thin layer.  Melt margarine*; drizzle over potatoes.  Combine garlic
powder, Parmesan cheese, and pepper.  Sprinkle over potatoes.  Bake
in a 450F oven about 25 minutes or until brown.

*3 tablespoons margarine or butter
