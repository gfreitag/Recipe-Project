Title: Banana Nut Pop^
Categories: Snacks, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
Grape Nuts
1/2    Banana
1 tb Apple juice concentrate
Insert a stick in banana. Dip in juice and roll in cereal. Wrap in
plastic wrap and freeze. Thaw a few minutes and eat!

Per serving: Calories: 89 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 22g Fat: 0g
Sodium: 10mg Cholesterol: 0mg
