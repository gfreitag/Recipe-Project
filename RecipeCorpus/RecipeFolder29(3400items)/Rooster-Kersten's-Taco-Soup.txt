Title: Rooster Kersten's Taco Soup
Categories: Countyfairs, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
Tortilla Chips
Cheddar Or Longhorn
Cheese, grated
Fresh Tomatoes, chopped
Black Olives, chopped
Onion, chopped
Sour Cream
1 lb Hamburger Meat
4 c  Tomatoes, Canned, crushed
1 cn (16 Oz) Pinto Or Other
Beans
1 c  Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Picante Or Taco Seasoning
Or to taste
Garnishes
1. In a large heavy pot, cook the hamburger and the onion together
over moderate heat until the meat is brown, stirring often. Spoon off
excess grease, if desired. Add the tomatoes, beans and seasoning.
Simmer, partially covered, for 45 mins.

2. Spoon into 4 large bowls and let each person top with granishes.

From the TWIN FALLS COUNTY FAIR & RODEO of Filer, ID. Fair Dates: 1st
week in Sept. for 6 days.
