Title: Applesauce Snack Cake from Mix
Categories: Cake/cookie, Mixes, Kids
Yield: 8 servings
3 1/2 tb Shortening
1/2 c  Applesauce
1/3 c  Sliced almonds
1 ts Sugar
2 c  Snack-cake mix
3/4 ts Cloves, divided
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1    Egg, slightly beaten
In 8-inch layer-cake or square pan, stir cake mix, 1/2 t cloves &
cinnamon with rubber spatula until well blended.  Stir in egg,
shortening & applesauce until well blended.  Scrape down sides of
pan. Sprinkle evenly with almonds, sugar and remaining 1/4 t cloves.
Bake in preheated 375 degree oven 30 minutes. Cool in pan on rack.
