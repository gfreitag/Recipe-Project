Title: 999138 Herbed Corn On The Cob
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4    Ears corn, unhusked
1/4 c  Butter, softened to room
-temp.
1/2 ts Basil
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Cut four large squares of foil. Peel
back but do not remove the corn husks.  Remove the corn silk. In a
small bowl, blend together the butter, basil, oregano, salt and
pepper. Spread about 1 tablespoon of herbed butter on each ear of
corn, then close the husk around the corn.  Wrap each ear of corn
tightly in foil and bake for 30 minutes or until tender.
