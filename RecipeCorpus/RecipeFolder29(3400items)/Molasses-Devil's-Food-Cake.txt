Title: Molasses Devil's Food Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
3    Sqs  unsweetened chocolate,
-melted
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Flour
3/4 c  Sour milk
2/3 c  Shortening
2/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Soda
1 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
2/3 c  Molasses
Cream shortening.  Add sugar gradually.  Add soda, salt, and vanilla.
Mix in molasses and melted chocolate.  Beat in eggs. Blend in flour
alternately with sour milk.  Pour into 2 well greased and floured 8
inch layer cake pans.

Bake at 375 degrees for 24 minutes. Cool. Ice, if desired.
