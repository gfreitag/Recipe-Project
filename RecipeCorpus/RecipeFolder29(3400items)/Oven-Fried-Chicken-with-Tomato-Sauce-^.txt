Title: Oven Fried Chicken with Tomato Sauce ^
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Flolur
2 tb Tomato paste
8 oz Can tomatoes
1 c  Chicken broth
1 ts Fines herbes
6    Peppercorns
1    Strip lemon peel
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Sugar, optional
Salt and pepper
3 lb Chicken pieces
3 tb Flour
Salt and pepper
1/8 ts Paprika
2    Eggs, beaten
8 tb Cornflake crumbs
4 tb Melted butter
TOMATO SAUCE:
2 tb Butter
1    Onion, finely chopped
1 cl Garlic, crushed
Preheat oven to 400. Roll chicken pieces in flour seasoned with salt,
pepper and paprika. Brush thoroughly with beaten egg and roll in
cornflake crumbs. Place in well buttered ovenproof dish. Pour melted
butter over chicken. Bake 30-35 minutes or until crisp and browned.
While chicken is baking: Melt butter in saucepan. Add onion and
garlic and saute until golden. Whisk in flour, tomato paste,
tomatoes, and chicken broth. Bring to a boil. Add herbs, peppercorns,
lemon peel and cayenne; simmer 20 minutes. Strain sauce and add sugar
if desired. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot chicken with sauce
and rice or mashed potatoes.
