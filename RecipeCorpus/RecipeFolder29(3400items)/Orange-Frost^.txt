Title: Orange Frost^
Categories: Desserts, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 2 servings
La ds cinnamon
2    Ice cubes
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/2    Banana, frozen
1 tb Nonfat milk powder
Blend all in blender until creamy.

Per serving: Calories: 68 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 16g Fat: trace
Sodium: 12mg Cholesterol: 12mg
