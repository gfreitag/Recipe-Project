Title: Pineapple Carrot Raisin Salad
Categories: Side dish, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Dole raisins
1 ct Lowfat vanilla yogurt
(8 oz)
1 cn Dole crushed pineapple in
Juice, drained
1 lb Dole carrots, shredded
Combine all ingredients. Chill.

Source: A Dole promotional booklet.
