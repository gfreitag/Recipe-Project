Title: Uncle Dirty Dave's Cincinatti Hot Dog Sauce
Categories: Chili, Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 batch
1 tb Oregano
1 tb Cumin
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Mustard, ground
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Allspice
3    Cloves, ground
1 ts Cinnamon
3 lb Coarse ground chilli beef
2 tb Bacon drippings
1 pt Beef broth (unsalted)
1 c  Tomato puree (10 1/2 oz)
1 ts Tabasco
1    Chili pepper pod, or more to
-taste
2    Onions, coarsely chopped
2 cl Garlic, finely chopped
3 tb Chilli powder (heaping)
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Brown meat; add bacon drippings and cook 5 minutes.  Put onion and
garlic into blender with enough of the beef broth to let the blender
chop the stuff finely.  Pour into pan with the meat and leftover
broth, tomato puree, Tabasco, pepper, onion and garlic; simmer 30
minutes.

Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer slowly for 1 to 2 hours.
Serve on hot dogs or Polish sausage.  Top with raw onion, grated
sharp Cheddar cheese and plenty of cold beer (root or real).

Another tested and approved formula from Uncle Dirty Dave's Kitchen.
