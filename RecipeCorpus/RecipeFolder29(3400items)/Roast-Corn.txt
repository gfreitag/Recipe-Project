Title: Roast Corn
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
Butter
String
2    Ears or corn
Salt and pepper
Peel ears leaving husks on at bottom and remove corn silk.  Replace
husks covering ears and tie around top.  Dip corn in salt water.
Place corn on screen over hot coals and turn often until all side are
done. When cooked remove husks and add salt and pepper and margerine.
