Title: Mock Hollandaise
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Butter, melted
2    Eggs, beaten
1/4 c  Cream
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cayenne
In a double boiler, mix and cook the first 6 ingreds, stirring
constantly until thickened. Then slowly stir in the butter. Stir
until thick and serve.
