Title: 279680 Rice Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
1    (10 1/2 oz.) can water
1    Stick margarine or butter
1    (8 oz.) can mushrooms
1 c  Uncooked rice
1    (10 1/2 oz.) can French
-onion soup
Mix all ingredients together, cut margarine in pieces and place in 1
1/2 quart covered casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees for approximately
40 minutes or until almost all the liquid has been absorbed.
