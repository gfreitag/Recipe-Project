Title: Baked Corn Pudding
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Puddings
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Bread crumbs
1/2 c  Sour cream; or 1/3c milk
17 oz Cream style corn
17 oz Whole kernal corn; drained
1    Egg
Beat egg, add other ingredients.  Blend well, season with salt and
pepper to taste.  Put in greased casserole and bake, uncovered, in a
350 oven 45 mins or until set.
