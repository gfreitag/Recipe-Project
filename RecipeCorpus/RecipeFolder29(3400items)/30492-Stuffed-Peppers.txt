Title: 30492 Stuffed Peppers
Categories: Spam, Chilies, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Rice, cooked
1 c  Corn, cooked
-Salt and pepper to taste
-Chopped onions may be
-added
6    Peppers, cut in
-half
-STUFFING:
1 cn Corn beef hash
1 cn Spam, ground
Remove seeds.  Place in cold water with a little salt.  Bring to boil;
drain and cool in cold water.  Add 6 homemade biscuits.  Add enough
canned tomatoes to thin as desired.  Stuff peppers.  Place a teaspoon
of butter or margarine on top of each pepper. Bake at 400 degrees
until brown. May be cooked at a lower temperature for longer time.
