Title: 80056 Tex-Mex Corn Bread
Categories: Tex/mexican, Breads
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Seeded and chopped jalapeno
-peppers
2 tb Seeded and chopped sweet
-red peppers
2 tb Corn oil
1 1/2 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Baking soda
2    Egg whites
1 1/2 c  Unsalted cream corn
2 tb Chopped onion
Combine cornmeal, flour and soda in a medium-sized mixing bowl. In
another bowl, mix together egg whites, corn, prepared vegetables and
oil. Stir well.  Add all at once to cornmeal mixture. Stir quickly
just until smooth. Pour batter into a lightly oiled 9 x 13 inch
baking pan. Bake in preheated 400 degree oven for 30 minutes. Remove
from oven when golden brown. Cut into 3 inch squares.  Serve warm.
