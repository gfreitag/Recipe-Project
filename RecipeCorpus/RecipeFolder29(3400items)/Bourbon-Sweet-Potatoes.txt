Title: Bourbon Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Holiday
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Bourbon
Extra butter for pan
6 md Sweet potatoes
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Butter, chopped
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

Bake sweet potatoes until fork tender.  Let cool, peel and cut into
slices about 1/2 inch thick.

Butter a 9 x 13 inch baking pan and place the sweet potatoes in the
pan. Cover with the chopped butter and sugar, pour the bourbon over
the potatoes.  Bake for 30 to 45 minutes until browned and bubbly.

Let sit 15 minutes before serving.
