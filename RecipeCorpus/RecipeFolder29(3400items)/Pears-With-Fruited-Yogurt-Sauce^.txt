Title: Pears With Fruited Yogurt Sauce^
Categories: Desserts, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  No sugar added fruit spread
1 ts Almond extract
4    Sprigs mint
4    Pears, peeled
1 c  White wine
1 c  Water
1 c  Lowfat yogurt
Poach pears in wine and water until soft, about 7 minutes. Combine
yogurt with 1 tablespoon fruit spread and 1/2 teaspoon almond
extract. In another bowl, combine remaining spreadand extract. Take
yogurt and pour into circles on dessert plates. Make 2 thin circles
with fruit spread and swirl with knife. Place pear in center and
garnish with mint.

Variation: Use apple juice for wine and water.

Per serving: Calories: 131 Protein: 3g Carbohydrates: 30g Fat: trace
Sodium: 45mg Cholesterol: 1mg
