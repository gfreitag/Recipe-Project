Title: Spicy Slow Cook Barbeque
Categories: Meats, Crockpot
Yield: 12 sandwiches
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Chili powder
2 ts Salt
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Mustard; dry
1 1/2 lb Stew beef
1 1/2 lb Pork; cubed
2 c  Onions; chopped
3    Green peppers; chopped
6 oz Tomato paste
1/2 c  Brown sugar
Combine all ingredients in crockpot.  Cover and cook on high for 8
hours, stirring occasionally.  Before serving, stir mixture with wire
whisk until meat is shredded.  Serve on sandwich rolls.  Source:
