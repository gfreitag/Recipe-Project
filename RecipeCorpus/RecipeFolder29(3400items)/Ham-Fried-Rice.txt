Title: Ham Fried Rice
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Diced ham
3 tb Soy sauce
4 c  Cooked rice
3 tb Peanut oil
2    Eggs, beaten
2 tb Finely diced onion
1 c  Bean sprouts
Heat peanut oil in skillet; add eggs and scramble.  Transfer eggs to a
dish.  Saute onion, bean sprouts and ham over moderate heat.  Mix soy
sauce with rice; add to ham mixture and cook, stirring constantly,
until rice is evenly browned.  Add egg and season to taste. Randy Rigg
