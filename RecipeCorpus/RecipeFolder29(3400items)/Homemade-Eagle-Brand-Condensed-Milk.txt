Title: Homemade Eagle Brand Condensed Milk
Categories: Homemade
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Margarine or butter
1 c  Hot water
4 c  Powdered milk
Blend in blender until very well mixed.  Store in refrigerator or
freezer.

Origin: Homemaking Booklet, Mormon Church, 1978 Shared by: Sharon
Stevens
