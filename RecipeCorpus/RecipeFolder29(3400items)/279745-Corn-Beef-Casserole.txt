Title: 279745 Corn Beef Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
6 sl American cheese
1 c  French fried onions
1/3    Soup can of milk
1 cn Corn beef
8 oz Egg noodles, cooked &
-drained
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
Cook and drain noodles.  Mix together corn beef (cut into edible
pieces), cheese (tear into pieces), soup, milk and 1/2 the onions.
Combine noodles and meat mixture.  Top with remaining onions.  Bake
at 350 degrees for 30 minutes in 9x9 inch casserole dish.
