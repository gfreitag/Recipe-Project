Title: Congo Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie, Snacks, Desserts
Yield: 24 servings
3    Eggs
1 c  Nutmeats
1 pk Chocolate chips (12oz)
2 1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 3/4 c  Flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Oil or Shortening (melted)
Sift dry ingredients together. Add melted shortening to brown sugar;
add eggs, one at a time, beating after each. Add dry ingredients,
nuts and chips. Spread on sheet 10X15. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to
30 minutes.
