Title: 498826 Pastelitos De Boda
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 servings
1/8 ts Salt
1 c  Finely chopped pecans or
-walnuts
-Powdered sugar
3/4 c  Butter
4 tb Powdered sugar
2 ts Vanilla
1 ts Cold water
2 c  Sifted all-purpose flour
Cream butter until fluffy, then add sugar, vanilla and cold water. Mix
thoroughly and stir in flour, salt and nuts.  Shape into a roll about
1 1/2 inches in diameter.  Wrap in waxed paper and chill thoroughly.
Cut in 1/4 inch slices and place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake in a
400 degree oven for about 6 to 8 minutes or until lightly browned.
Remove from baking sheet and roll in powdered sugar, if desired.
Makes 3 1/2 to 4 dozen cookies. (These may be made into small balls
also and baked for about 8 to 10 minutes.  They should be rolled in
sugar the same as the others.) Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
04-06-95
