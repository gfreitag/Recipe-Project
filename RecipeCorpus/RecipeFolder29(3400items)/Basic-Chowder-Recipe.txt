Title: Basic Chowder Recipe
Categories: Soup/stew, Fish, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 cn Corn
1/4 lb Fish
1 c  Water or milk
1 sl Bacon
1/8 sm Onion
1/2 md Potatoe
Cut bacon and onion small.  Fry in pot until brown.  Add corn, fish
or meat with a little water.   Cook slowly until cooked.  Add
potatoes about 1/2 hr before serving and cook until done.  Season and
if using milk add just before serving.  Heat but do not boil.
