Title: Avocado Orange Salad with Basil
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Basil; shredded
1/2    Red onion; thinly sliced
2    Oranges; peeled & sectioned
2    Avocados; sliced lengthwise
20    Black olives; chopped
Prepare all the ingredients but don't peel the avocado until right
before assembling the salad.  Arrange the orange sections, avocado
slices, onion and olives on a large serving platter or individual
plates. Sprinkle with the basil.  Drizzle with a bottled oil and
vinegar dressing. One serving (including dressing) is 210 calories
and 18 grams fat. Source: Store ad Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On
03-20-95
