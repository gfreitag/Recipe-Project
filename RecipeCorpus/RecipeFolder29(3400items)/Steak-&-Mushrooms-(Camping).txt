Title: Steak & Mushrooms (Camping)
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ea Round steak
1 lb Mushrooms sliced
2 c  Onions, diced
1/4 lb Butter
8 oz Can tomato sauce
-flour
Cut meat into strips and coat with flour.  Saute in melted butter for
5 min.  Add onion and mushrooms, cook another 5 min or until onion
turn clear.  Add remaining ingredients and stir well. Simmer 1 to
1-1/2 hours. Serve over rice.
