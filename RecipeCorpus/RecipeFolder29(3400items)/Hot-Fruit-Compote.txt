Title: Hot Fruit Compote
Categories: Crockpot, Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn (16 oz.) pitted, dark sweet
. cherries, undrained
1/4 c  Dry white wine
2 c  Water
1 lb Dried prunes
3 2/3 c  Dried apricots
1 cn (13 1/2 oz.) or 1-2/3 cups
. pineapple chunks,
. undrained
Put all ingredients in CROCK-POT. Cover and cook on Low overnight (7
to 8 hours). High: 3 to 4 hours. Serve warm.

From Rival Crock-Pot cookbook, date unknown.
