Title: Romaine Salad - Great Chefs
Categories: Salads, Masterchefs
Yield: 6 servings
2 ea Avocados, Haas, sliced
2 tb Juice, lime, fresh
1/2 c  Oil, olive
1 bn Coriander
2 ea Hearts, lettuce, Romaine
4 ea Tomatillos, sliced (or use
-- fresh green tomatoes)
2 md Tomatoes, sliced
Place the lettuce, tomatilloes, tomatoes and avocado in a large
bowl. Sprinkle with lime juice and olive oil, but do not toss.
Garnish with coriander leaves to taste.

Source: Great Chefs of San Francisco, Avon Books, 1984
Chef:   Mark Miller, The Fourth Street Grill, Berkeley, CA
