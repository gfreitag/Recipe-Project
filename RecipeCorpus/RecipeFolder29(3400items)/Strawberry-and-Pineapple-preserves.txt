Title: Strawberry and Pineapple preserves
Categories: Can/preserv, Fruit
Yield: 1 batch
1 c  Canned pineapple
Rind and juice of 1/2 lemon
1 qt Hulled strawberries
4 c  Sugar
Simmer the ingredients 20 min, stirring frequently. When thick, place
in sterile jars and seal.
