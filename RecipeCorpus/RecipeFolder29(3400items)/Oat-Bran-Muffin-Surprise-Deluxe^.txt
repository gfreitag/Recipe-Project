Title: Oat Bran Muffin Surprise Deluxe^
Categories: Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Brown rice syrup
2 tb Crushed pineapple,
Unsweetened
1/3 c  Grated carrots
1 1/2 tb Canola oil
Jam or jelly, no sugar
Added
1 c  Whole wheat flour
2/3 c  Wheat bran
3 tb Oat bran
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Lowfat buttermilk
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
Preheat oven to 375F. Line 12 muffin cups with paper liners and set
aside. Combine dry ingredients in one bowl and wet, except jam in
another. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Spoon half of batter into
muffin cups. Spoon 1/4 teaspoon jam into each and top with remaining
batter. Bake until golden brown and a toothpick inserted in center
comes out clean, about 25 minutes.

Variation: For non-diabetics, add golden raisins, carob chips or diced
green apple.

Per serving: Calories: 147 Protein: 5g Carbohydrates: 28g Fat: 2g
Sodium: 85mg Cholesterol: trace
