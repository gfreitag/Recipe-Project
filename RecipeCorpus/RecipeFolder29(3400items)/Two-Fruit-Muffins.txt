Title: Two-Fruit Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Fruit
Yield: 12 muffins
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Nutmeg OR cinnamon
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Shortening
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Crushed, drained pineapple
1 lg Egg; beaten
1 c  Mashed bananas
Cream shortening and sugar. Add egg. Beat well. Fold in sifted dry
ingredients, alternately with the fruit. Add vanilla. Blend, but do
not over mix. (This makes tunnels in the muffins.) Spoon into greased
muffin pans and bake in a 375 F oven for 20 - 25 minutes, or until
done.

Source: Vancouver Sun circa 1960's
