Title: Peach Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/4 c  Fresh/drained canned
-peaches coarsely chopped
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Almond extract
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Salad oil
Combine flour, salt, sugar and baking soda. Add eggs and oil. Stir
only until dry ingredients are moistened. Stir in vanilla, almond
extract, peaches and nuts. Bake muffins at 350 degrees for 20-25
minutes.
