Title: 998840 Southern Corn Pudding
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 tb Melted butter
2 c  Scalded milk
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper (optional)
1 tb Cornstarch, dissolved in
-sm. amount of milk
2 c  Cream style corn
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
1 ts Sugar
Mix all ingredients together and put in a buttered baking dish. Bake
at 325 degrees until firm.  It will take an hour or maybe longer to
bake. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-22-95
