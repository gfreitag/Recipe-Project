Title: Granola Crunch
Categories: Snacks, Breads, Nuts/grains
Yield: 2 quarts
1 ts Salt
1 cn Sweetened, condensed milk
(NOT evaporated milk)
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 c  Dried banana chips
2 c  Raisins
2 c  Oats
1 1/2 c  Walnuts, coarsely chopped
1 c  Flaked coconut
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds (seedless)
1/2 c  Wheat germ
2 tb Sesame seeds
1 ts Cinnamon
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.  Combine oats, nuts, coconut, sunflower
seeds, wheat germ, sesame seeds, cinnamon, salt, milk and oil in a
large bowl. Mix well. Spread evenly in an aluminum-foil-lined pan.
Bake 55 to 60 min., stirring every 15 mins.  Remove pan from oven and
stir in banana chips and raisins. Cool before serving.
