Title: Orange Fruit Dip
Categories: Desserts, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 cups
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1 1/3 c  Vanilla low-fat yogurt
1/4 c  Low-sugar orange marmalade
Combine all ingredients; cover and chill.  Serve dip with fresh fruit.

Per tablespoon:  FAT 0g(?); SODIUM 11mg; CHOLESTEROL 1mg; CALORIES 14.

From "Choices for Living Happier and Healthier," July-Sept. 1994.

totally fat-free and who, therefore, recommends the use of non-fat
