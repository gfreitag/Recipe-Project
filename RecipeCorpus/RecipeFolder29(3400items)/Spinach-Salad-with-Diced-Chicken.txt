Title: Spinach Salad with Diced Chicken
Categories: Vegetables, Poultry, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1    Carrot, peeled & sliced
1    Tomato, diced
1    Cucumber, peeled & sliced
4    Radishes, sliced
1 bn Fresh Spinach, torn up
2    Green top Onions, sliced
1 c  Mushrooms, sliced
8 oz Chicken, cooked & diced
Prepare vegetables and toss with chicken in a large salad bowl.  Use
Spinach Salad Dressing (in next recipe).  Mix dressing, shake well,
pour over salad, toss, and serve.
