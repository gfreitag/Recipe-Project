Title: 22179 Tomato Flan Tex-Mex Style
Categories: Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Half & Half
-Salt and pepper, to taste
1 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/4 c  Scallions, chopped
2 lg Tomatoes, sliced 1/2" thick
1/4 c  Onion, minced
1    (4 oz.) can green chilies,
-chopped
3    Eggs
Layer tomatoes in buttered 9" pie plate.  Sprinkle with onion and
chilies. Combine eggs, Half & Half, salt and pepper and pour over
tomatoes. Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes, lower oven temperature
to 350 and bake an additional 30 minutes.  Top with sour cream,
cheddar cheese and scallions. Makes 6 servings.
