Title: Austin Chili
Categories: Chili, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Chili powder
12 oz Beer
2 tb Oregano
2 tb Cumin
1 tb Cayenne
2 ts Salt
4 lb Grd round
28 oz Stewed tomatoes
2    Large onions
4    Garlic cloves
1/2 c  Flour
2 cn Ranch beans
Saute onions and garlic in oil.  Brown meat and add ingredients,
except for beans.  Simmer for two hours and then add beans before
serving.
