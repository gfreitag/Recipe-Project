Title: Mushroom-Topped Pork Chops
Categories: Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Finely chopped red onions
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
ds Black pepper
2 tb Grated cheddar cheese
6    Lean pork chops,boneless
1 tb Butter
Topping
1 c  Finely chopped mushrooms
1 tb Butter
1/2 c  Finely chopped parsley
DIRECTIONS Place pork chops in a microsafe dish. Prepare topping; In a
microsafe dish, place chopped mushrooms. Dot with butter. Microwave
at HIGH POWER for 3 minutes until mushrooms don't look raw. Drain.
Let cool. Mix together mushrooms, egg and salt and pepper. Place
topping on pork chops. Microwave at MEDIUM HIGH POWER for 6 minutes,
uncovered. Let rest for 5 minutes. Uncover. Microwave at MEDIUM HIGH
POWER for another 4 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese. Microwave at HIGH
POWER for 3 - 4 more minutes until cheese is melted and chops are
