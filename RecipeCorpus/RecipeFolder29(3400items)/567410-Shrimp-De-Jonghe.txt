Title: 567410 Shrimp De Jonghe
Categories: Cajun/creol, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
2    Cloves minced garlic
1/3 c  Sherry wine
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
2 lb Raw cleaned shrimp
1/2 c  Melted butter
1 ts Salt
2/3 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
2 tb Parsley
Combine butter, salt, garlic, parsley, cayenne and bread crumbs.
Arrange shrimp in shallow 3 to 4 quart casserole and top with crumb
mixture. Sprinkle all over with sherry.  Bake at 375 degree oven for
15 minutes. Do not over cook.  When topping has melted and slightly
browned, serve. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-13-95
