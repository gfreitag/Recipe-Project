Title: Orange-Kissed Beets
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1 cn (8-1/4 oz.) slices beets,
; drained
2 tb Golden raisins
Strips of orange peel
1/3 c  Orange juice
2 tb Light brown sugar
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Cornstarch
1/8 ts Ground ginger
1/8 ts Salt
In a saucepan over medium heat, cook and stir orange juice, brown
sugar, butter, cornstarch, ginger, salt and pepper until thick.  Add
the beets and raisins; heat through.  Garnish with orange peel.
