Title: 962129 Corn Stuffed Yellow Squashes
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1    (17 oz.) can whole kernel
-corn, drained
1 ts Seasoned salt
1 ts Seasoned pepper
3/4 c  Finely shredded sharp
-cheddar cheese
3 md Yellow summer squashes,
-about 6" long
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 tb Chopped onion
2 tb Each cut up red & green
-pepper
Cook squashes in small amount of boiling water 15 minutes or until
just tender. Drain, cut in half and scoop out seed.  Melt butter in
skillet, add onion and cook a few minutes.  Add remaining ingredients
and stuff squashes with mixture. Arrange in shallow baking dish and
bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes.
