Title: Meat Loaf (Camping)
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Bell pepper
2 pk Onion soup mix
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Marjoram
3 lb Ground beef
1 1/2 c  Quick oats
2 ea Eggs
1/2 ts Dry mustard
Mix all ingredients and put in casserole pan.  Place in dutch oven.
Bake 1 hour, covered.
