Title: Plenty of Everything Fudgies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 30 bars
-melted and cooled
2 c  Granulated sugar
4    Extra-large eggs
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
3/4 c  Chopped walnuts
3/4 c  Chopped pecans
1/2 c  Chopped Brazil nuts
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Sweetened shredded coconut
3/4 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
1/4 c  Unsifted cake flour
2 tb Cornstarch
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 lb Unsalted butter; melted and
-cooled (2 sticks)
5 oz Unsweetened chocolate;
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.  Butter and flour a 13x9x2-inch
baking pan.

Sift together the all-purpose flour, cake flour, cornstarch, baking
powder and salt.  Toss the chocolate chips with 1 teaspoon of the
sifted mixture.

Whisk the butter and chocolate in a large mixing bowl.  Blend in the
sugar. Beat in the eggs and vanilla extract.  Add the flour mixture
and mix until the particles of flour are absorbed.  Stir in the
chocolate chips, walnuts, pecans, Brazil nuts, raisins and coconut.

Pour and scrape the batter into the prepared pan.  BAke for 35 to 38
minutes or until just set.

Place the pan on a cooling rack.  Cool completely.  Cut into thirty 2
1/4 by 1 3/4-inch rectangles.  Remove the bars from the pan using a
metal spatula.

Store in an airtight container.
