Title: Basic Vinaigrette^
Categories: Salads, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 16 servings
1 ts Tamari or low sodium soy
Sauce
1/2 ts Dijon mustard, no salt
Added
1 ds Red pepper, optional
1/4 c  Red wine or rice vinegar
1/2 c  Water
4 ts Apple juice concentrate
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Onion powder
1 ts Lemon juice
Combine and shake to mix. Store in refrigerator.

Per tablespoon serving: Calories: 5 Protein: trace Carbohydrates: 1g
Fat: trace Sodium: 8mg Cholesterol: 0mg
