Title: 971098 Spam Soup
Categories: Spam, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Onion, diced
-Spiced, as desired
1 cn Kidney beans
1 cn Whole kernel corn
1 qt Water
1 cn Spam, diced
1 lg Can tomatoes
-Salt & pepper to taste
1    (8 oz.) pkg. uncooked
-macaroni
Put juice from all can vegetables in pot with them.  Put in the onion,
salt, pepper and spices if desired.  Add Spam, then water. If water
does not cover these ingredients add 1/2 to 1 quart more. Bring all
this to a boil and cook for about 15 minutes.  Then add macaroni and
cook for about 7 minutes more. Serve while hot.
