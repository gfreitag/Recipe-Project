Title: Fruit Medley Gelatin Mold^
Categories: Desserts, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
Unsweetened
1/2 c  Seedless grapes, halved
1/2 c  Unpeeled apples, pureed
1    Envelope unflavored gelatin
2 c  Apple-cranberry juice
1/2 c  Crushed pineapple,
Soften gelatin in juice. Heat until dissolved and cool slightly.
Spray a 4 cup mold with pam. Combine fruit with gelatin and pur into
mold. Cover and chill until firm.

Variation: Substutute 1/2 to 1 cup cranberries, crushed in processor
and 2 to 3 tablespoons apple juice concentrate for one of the fruits
listed in the recipe.

Per serving: Calories: 62 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 15g Fat: trace
Sodium: 4mg Cholesterol: 0mg
