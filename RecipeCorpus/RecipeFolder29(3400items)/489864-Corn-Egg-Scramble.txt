Title: 489864 Corn Egg Scramble
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese/eggs, Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
-drained
4    Eggs
1/2 lb Bacon, cut in pieces
16 oz Can whole Kernel corn,
Fry bacon in 10 inch skillet until crisp, drain off fat.  Beat eggs,
stir in corn.  Pour over bacon pieces in skillet.  Cook and stir
until eggs are thickened throughout but still moist.  Season with
salt and pepper. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-17-95
