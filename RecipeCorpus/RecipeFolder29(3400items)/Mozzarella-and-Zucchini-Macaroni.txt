Title: Mozzarella and Zucchini Macaroni
Categories: Vegetarian, Italian, Pasta
Yield: 2 servings
125 g  Grated mozzarella
1    Eggs
Fresh ground black pepper
250 g  Spiral pasta
1/2 tb Olive oil
1    Garlic cloves
250 g  Zucchini
Preparation: thinly slice the garlic cloves. Slice the zucchinis into
rounds.
:

1. Cook the pasta in a large quantity of boiling water until just
tender.

2. Heat the oil in a large pan. Add the garlic and cook for 2-3
minutes.

3. Add the zucchini and cook over a medium heat until tender.

4. Drain the pasta and place in a large bowl.

5. Add the mozzarella, eggs and black pepper.

6. Stir to combine. Add the Zucchini and mix gently.

Serve immediately.
