Title: No-bake Banana-Peanut Nuggets
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 each
1 ts Lemon juice
Powdered sugar
2 1/2 c  Vanilla-wafer crumbs
1/2 c  Mashed ripe banana
1/2 c  Chunky peanut butter
Mix crumbs, banana, peanut butter & lemon juice well.  Shape in 1"
balls; flatten slightly to form nugget shapes.  Roll edges in
powdered sugar. Submitted By TERRI WOLTMON   On   04-22-95
