Title: Hawaiian Meatballs
Categories: Meats, Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Vinegar
1 tb Soy sauce
1/3 c  Chopped green pepper
13 1/2 oz Can of crushed pineapple
2 lb Ground beef
2/3 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/3 c  Minced onion
1/4 ts Ginger
1 ts Salt
1    Egg
1/4 c  Milk
Mix ground beef, cracker crumbs, onion, ginger, salt, egg, and milk
and make into 1 inch balls.  Brown and place in a baking dish.  Mix
cornstarch, brown sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, and green pepper.  Cook
over medium heat until thickened.  Add crushed pineapple plus juice.
Heat and pour over meatballs.  Heat thoroughly and serve. Randy Rigg
