Title: Spiced Oatmeal Mix
Categories: Breakfast, Mixes
Yield: 10 cups
2 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 ts Nutmeg
1 1/2 c  Raisins; or dried apple
8 c  Rolled oats; quick
1/2 c  Brown sugar; packed
2 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients in large bowl.  Stir with a wire whisk. Store
in a cool dry place in a container with a tight-fitting lid for up to
6 months.

This mix makes:  Spiced Oatmeal, Spicy Oatmeal Pancakes, Swiss
Porridge
