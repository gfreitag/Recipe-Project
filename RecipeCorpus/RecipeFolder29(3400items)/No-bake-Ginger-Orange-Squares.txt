Title: No-bake Ginger-Orange Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 each
2 tb Light corn syrup
Granulated sugar
2 c  Gingersnap crumbs
1/4 c  Orange juice
Mix crumbs, orange juice and corn syrup until crumbs are moist.  Turn
out on waxed paper; shape in 6" log.  Roll in granulated sugar; wrap
tight; place in freezer 10 minutes.  With sharp knife, cut in 1/4"
slices. Submitted By TERRI WOLTMON   On   04-22-95
