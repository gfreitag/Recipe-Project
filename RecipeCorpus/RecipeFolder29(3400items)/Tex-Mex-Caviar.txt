Title: Tex-Mex Caviar
Categories: Salads, Tex/mexican, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Fresh jalapeno pepper
Chopped
2 tb Chopped parsley
1/4 c  LF or LC Italian dressing*
10 oz Frozen black eyed peas
1/4 c  Sweet red pepper, chopped
1/4 c  Sweet green pepper, chopped
1/4 c  Red onion, chopped
*Try to use low fat or low calorie dressing.

Rinse and drain peas.  Mix with all other ingredients.  Refrigerate.
