Title: 891346 Corn & Tomato Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
-tsp. dill weed
2 c  Fresh or frozen whole kernel
-corn
10    Cherry tomatoes, halved
1 sm Green pepper, chopped
1/4 c  Plain no fat yogurt
2 tb Ketchup
1 ts Prepared mustard
2    Green onions, including
-tops, chopped
2 tb Snipped fresh dill or 1/2
1.  Combine yogurt, ketchup and mustard in bowl, stir in green onions
and dill.  Set aside.  2.  Bring 2 cups water to boil, add corn and
cook 1 minute or just until tender.  Drain in colander. Rinse with
cold water to stop cooking and drain.  3.  Add to dressing along with
tomatoes and peppers.  Mix well.  Serve on lettuce leaves.
