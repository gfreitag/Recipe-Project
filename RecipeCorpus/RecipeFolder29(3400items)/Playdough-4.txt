Title: Playdough 4
Categories: Kids, Question?
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Cream of tartar
-Food coloring, lots!
-Extract (optional)
Lemon smells great!
1 c  Flour
1 c  Water
1 ts Oil
1/2 c  Salt
Cook over medium heat until mixture pulls away from sides of pan and
becomes dough-like.  Knead until cool.

Keeps 2-1/2 to 3 months unrefrigerated.

Makes enough dough for 3 to 4 children.
