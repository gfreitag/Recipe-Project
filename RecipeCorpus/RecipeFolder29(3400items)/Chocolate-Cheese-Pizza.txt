Title: Chocolate Cheese Pizza
Categories: Chocolate, Healthy
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
2 tb Margarine, diet; melted
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 c  Chocolate graham
-- cracker crumbs
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 tb Fructose
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 c  Egg substitute, real
2    Egg whites; beaten
3/4 c  Bread crumbs; fine
1 c  Strawberries, wash &
-- sliced or raspberries
1 c  Golden raisins
3 tb Dark rum
3 c  Ricotta cheese, non-fat
1/4 c  Unsweetened dutch cocoa
-- powder
1 tb Orange peel; grated
3 tb Orange juice
-- freshly squeezed
Recipe by: Skinny Chocolate - ISBN 0-940625-80-6 CRUST:

Use food processor fitted with steel blade. Combine all crust
ingredients, or toss crust ingredients in bowl, using mixing spoon.
Pat crumbs onto 12-inch pizza pan. Preheat oven to 375 F.

FILLING: Soak raisins in rum 10 mins. Spoon cheese into deep bowl. Add
cocoa, raisins and rum, orange peel, orange juice, fructose, and
cinnamon. Blend in egg substitute, egg whites, and crumbs. Spoon
filling over crust. Bake pizza 30 mins. Cool, slice, and serve
sprinkled with berries.
