Title: Kids Kandy^
Categories: Candies, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Nonfat milk powder
Cocoa
1/4 c  Peanut butter
1 tb Honey
Combine peanut butter, honey and milk powder together. Form into 6
balls and roll in cocoa. Chill.

Per serving: Calories: 230 Protein: 8g Carbohydrates: 18g Fat: 14g
Sodium: 60mg Cholesterol: trace
