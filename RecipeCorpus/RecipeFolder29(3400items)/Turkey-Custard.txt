Title: Turkey Custard
Categories: Poultry, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; grated
3    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Milk
2/3 c  Corn flake crumbs
1 tb Butter
2 c  Turkey; cooked and chopped
1 1/2 c  Bread crumbs; soft or 1 cup
-leftover stuffing
1/2 ts Salt
Ground pepper
Grease a 2 qt casserole with margarine.  Place half of the turkey in
the bottom of the casserole.  Top with half of the bread crumbs or
stuffing. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Add half of the cheese.
Beat eggs and milk. Pour half over the turkey layer in the casserole.
Repeat this process. Sprinkle corn flad crumbs on top and dot with
butter. Bake in preheated 350 oven for 30 minutes, or until a knife
inserted in middle comes out clean. Source: Coupon Saver's Cookbook
by Beryl Marton Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   04-01-95
