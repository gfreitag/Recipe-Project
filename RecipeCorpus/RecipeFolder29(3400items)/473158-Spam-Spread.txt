Title: 473158 Spam Spread
Categories: Spam, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
-Cheese
-Onions
1 cn Spam
6    Boiled eggs
-Pickles
Grind 1 can Spam with 6 boiled eggs, pickles, cheese and onion. Add
mayonnaise and season to taste.  Spread on bread and you are ready to
eat. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-01-95
