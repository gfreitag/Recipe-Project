Title: Cheese Topped Potatoes
Categories: Microwave, Vegetables, Side dish, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------PREP TIME: 5 MINUTES-------------------------
MMMMM-------------------COOK TIME: 10 MINUTES------------------------
2 1/2 tb Cooked and diced broccoli
1 tb Shredded cheddar cheese
1/8 ts Pepper
2 ea Baking potatoes, scrubbed
-and dried
1/3 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
Using fork, pierce each potatoes several times. Place double
thickness of microwave safe paper toweling on bottom of microwave
oven. Place potatoes 1 inch apart on paper toweling. microwave on
high 7-10 minutes turning over halfway through cooking, until just
tender when pierced with knife. Wrap each potatoe carefully in paper
toweling and let stand 5-10 mintues until tender. While potatoes are
standing, in bowl combine yogurt, broccoli, cheese and pepper until
blended. Cut a large x on top of each potatoe and open. Spoon yogurt
mixture over potatoes. Yield : 2 servings
