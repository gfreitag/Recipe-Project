Title: Black Bean Sauce
Categories: Chinese, Vegetables, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Hot chile paste
2 ts Cornstarch
3 tb Fermented black beans
2 ts Sesame oil
1 tb Vegetable oil
2 ts Minced fresh ginger
2 ea Cloves garlic
1/2 c  Chicken stock
1 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Dry sherry
In a small sauce pan heat oil and fry ginger and garlic. Add stock,
soya, sherry, sugar and chile paste. Stir and bring to a boil. Make a
slurry of the starch and a little water and stir in. As the sauce
thickens add the black beans. Take off the heat and just before
serving stir in the sesame oil.
