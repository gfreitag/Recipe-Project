Title: WATERGATE CAKE #2
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 10 servings
1 lg Cool Whip;frozen
1 pk Pistachio Pudding';instant
1 1/4 c  Milk
Optional
Cherries;
Coconut
Nuts,chopped
1 pk Pistachio Pudding';instant
1 pk White cake mix
1 c  Oil
1 c  Ginger ale
3    Eggs
1/2 c  Walnuts;chopped
Topping
Mix together the cake mix, pudding',eggs,ginger ale and oil an chopped
nuts. Bake in 350 degree oven for 40 minutes in a 13x9 or 2 layer
pans. Icing. Mix together until smooth. Spread on cake and top off
with chopped nuts, and cherries or coconut or all three. Looks Good
and tastes great. Good Luck and Enjoy. Joni in S.Jersey
