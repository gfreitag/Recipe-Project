Title: 962178 Corn Oooh-La-La
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sour cream
2 tb Butter or oleo
1 ts Salt
2 cn Whole kernel corn
2 tb Flour
2 tb Onions, chopped
Cook onion in butter or oleo until soft, blend in the flour and salt,
add cream, stirring until smooth, let boil, add drained corn and heat
through. Garnish with crisp bacon and parsley.
