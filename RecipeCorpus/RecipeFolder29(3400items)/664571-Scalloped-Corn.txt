Title: 664571 Scalloped Corn
Categories: Casseroles, Microwave, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Onion salt
1/4 c  Corn flake crumbs
1/4 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1    (17 oz.) can cream style
-corn
1/2 c  Milk
1    Egg
Combine corn, milk, egg and onion salt in 1 quart casserole. Combine
cornflake crumbs and cheese; spread over the corn mixture. Cook in
microwave on cookmatic level 5 (slo cook) for 15-18 minutes or until
knife inserted near center comes out clean.  You may wish to turn
dish halfway through cooking time.
