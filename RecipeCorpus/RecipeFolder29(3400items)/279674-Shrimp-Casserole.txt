Title: 279674 Shrimp Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Seafood
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 c  Fine noodles, uncooked
1 cn French fried onion rings
-(optional)
-Paprika
1    (8 oz.) pkg. small, clean,
-frozen shrimp
1    (10 3/4oz.) can cream of
-shrimp soup
1/2 c  Sharp cheddar cheese,
-grated
In large bowl, mix first five ingredients together.  Put into greased
casserole and bake, covered, for 35 minutes at 350 degrees. Remove
from oven, sprinkle with paprika and stir.  If desired, arrange
French fried onion rings on top.  Return to oven and continue baking,
uncovered, for 5 minutes or until bubbly.
