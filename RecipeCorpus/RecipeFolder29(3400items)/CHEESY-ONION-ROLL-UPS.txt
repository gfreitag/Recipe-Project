Title: CHEESY ONION ROLL-UPS
Categories: Appetizers, Tex/mexican, Snacks
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Lime juice
1 tb Minced seeded jalapeno
-- pepper
10 oz Pkg. flour tortillas
(6"; size)
1 c  (8 oz.) sour cream
8 oz Pkg. cream cheese
-- softened
1/2 c  Finely shredded cheddar
-- cheese
3/4 c  Sliced green onions
"These roll-ups are very fast to fix and you can make them ahead and
keep them wrapped in the refrigerator until you're ready to serve." -
Barbara Keith

Picante sauce

Combine the first six ingredients in a bowl; mix well. Spread on one
side of tortillas and roll up tightly.

Wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.  Slice into 1" pieces.
Serve with picante sauce.
