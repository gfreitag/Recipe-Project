Title: Crispy Shallots
Categories: Vegetables, Question?
Yield: 1 /4 cup
Salt
6 lg Shallots, thinly sliced
2 tb Vegetable oil
Preheat oven to 400F. Toss the shallot slices on a baking sheet to
separate them, then spread in an even layer. Drizzle on the vegetable
oil and stir to coat. Bake in the center of the oven for about 15
minutes, stirring occasionally, until the shallots are evenly browned
and crisp. Season lightly with salt and let cool completely.

Can be refrigerated, covered, for up to 10 days. Rewarm in a 350F.
oven.
