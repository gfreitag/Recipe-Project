Title: Walt's Favorite Chili & Beans
Categories: Chili
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Dry mustard
1 lg Can solid pack tomatoes
Salt to taste
2 lb Dry pink beans
2 lb Coarse ground beef
2    Onions -- sliced
2    Cloves Garlic -- minced
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1 ts Chili powder, or to taste
For a spicier chili, add a pinch of the following:  coriander,
tumeric, chili seeds, fennel, cloves, cinnamon, dry ginger, small
yellow Mexican chili pepper.

Soak beans overnight in cold water. Drain. Add water to cover 2" over
beans and simmer with onions until tender (about 4 hours). Meanwhile,
prepare sauce by browning meat and minced garlic in oil. Add
remaining ingredients and simmer 1 hour. When beans are tender, add
sauce and simmer 1/2 hour more. Serves 6-8.

Source:  Disney World Magazine, March 95. Originally printed 15 years
