Title: Matzo Meal Pie Shell
Categories: Jewish, Pie/tart, Passover
Yield: 1 shell
1 c  Matzo meal; -=OR=-
1 c  Cake meal
2 tb Water; approximately
1/4 c  Margarine -=OR=-
1/4 c  Oil;
1/8    Salt;
1/4 ts Cinnamon (optional)
In a small mixing bowl, cream the margarine or oil with the sugar,
salt, and cinnamon.  Gradually add the matzo or cake meal.  Add water
if necessary, just enough to bind the mixture together.  With the
back of a tablespoon, press the mixture into a 9 or 10 pie plate.
Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F. oven until lightly browned, 10-15
minutes. Cool and fill as desired. Source: The Complete Passover
Cookbook by Frances R. AvRutick. Brought to you and yours via Nancy
O'Brion and her Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On
