Title: Greek Garlic Dressing^
Categories: Salads, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 64 servings
2 cl Garlic, minced
3 tb Fresh dill, finely chopped
1 qt Lowfat yogurt
2 md Cucumbers, peeled, seeded
And diced
Combine. Use a a dip or salad dressing.

Per tablespoon serving: Calories: 9 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 1g Fat:
trace Sodium: 10mg Cholesterol: 1mg
