Title: Fast Break From Eggs Breakfast Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry, Vegetables, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Jicama, peeled and
-julienned
1 c  Bulgar wheat
1/3 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
1/4 c  Scallions, sliced very thin
-(tops included?)
12 oz Turkey sausage (hot or
-mild, your choice)
2 c  Apples, peeled and cut in
-chunks
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Shredded carrots
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Combime the uncooked sausage, apple chunks (small cubes), raisins,
carrots, scallions, nutmeg, cinnamon, jicama, and bulgar wheat with
1/3 cup nonfat plain yogurt.  Bake it in a loaf pan for 40 minutes.
