Title: 500531 Tex-Mex Lasagna
Categories: Tex/mexican, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Garlic salt
12    Taco shells
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1 lb Beef
1    (16 oz.) can corn, drained
1    (16 oz.) can tomato sauce
1 c  Hot salsa sauce
1/2 tb Chili powder
1    (16 oz.) cottage cheese
Brown meat and drain.  Add corn, tomato sauce, hot sauce and chili
powder. Simmer for 5 minutes.  Combine cottage and Parmesan cheeses,
eggs, oregano and garlic salt.  Mix well.  Arrange 6 shells on bottom
of greased 9 x 13 inch pan.  Top with 1/2 of the meat mixture. Spoon
cheese mixture onto meat, add 6 more shells; add remaining meat
mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 30 minutes. Sprinkle
cheddar cheese and put back in oven to melt. Serve with corn muffins.
