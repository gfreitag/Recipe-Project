Title: Buttermilk Pecan Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 3 servings
3 tb Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Pecans
1/2 c  Butter
2 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla
3    Eggs
Use a deep dish pie shell.  Place pecans on bottom of crust.  Melt
butter. Add sugar and vanilla; beat together.  Add eggs, flour and
salt; mix thoroughly.  Stir in buttermilk.  Pour over pecans.  Bake
at 300 degrees for 1 hour. Randy Rigg
