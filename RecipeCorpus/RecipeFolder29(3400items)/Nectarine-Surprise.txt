Title: Nectarine Surprise
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Pineapple juice
1 ts Honey
4    Ice cubes
2 md Nectarines; peeled
-- and pitted
1    Banana; peeled
4    To 5 strawberries
-- washed & hulled
Recipe by: Produce Pete's "Farmacopeia" - ISBN 0-688-12847-5

Cut the fruit into chunks and place in blender. Add the remaining
ingredients. Blend at low speed for about 30 secs, then at high speed
for about 15 secs.
