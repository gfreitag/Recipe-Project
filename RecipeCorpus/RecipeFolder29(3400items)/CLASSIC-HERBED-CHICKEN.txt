Title: CLASSIC HERBED CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Water
4    Boneless, skinless chicken
-breasts
1 pk Lipton Recipe Secrets Savory
-Herb with Garlic Soup Mix
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
Preheat oven to 425 F. In small bowl, blend savory herb with garlic
soup mix, mayonnaise and water; set aside.  Rinse chicken pat dry. In
lightly greased 13 x 9-inch baking or roasting pan, arrange chicken;
brush with soup mixture.  Bake 10 minutes.  Turn chicken and brush
with additional soup mixture.  Bake and additional 15 minutes or
until chicken is tender and no longer pink.
