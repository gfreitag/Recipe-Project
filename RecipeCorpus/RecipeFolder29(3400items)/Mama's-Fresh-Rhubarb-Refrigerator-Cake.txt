Title: Mama's Fresh Rhubarb Refrigerator Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Unsweetened stewed fresh
Rhubarb
24    Vanilla wafers
1 1/3 c  (1 can) Sweetened condensed
Milk
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Blend milk and lemon juice together. Add Rhubarb. Line narrow pan with
waxed paper and fill with alternate layers of fruit mixture and
wafers, finishing with layer of wafers. Chill 6 hours or langer. To
serve, turn out onto small platter, remove waxed paper and cut into
slices. Submitted By KATHRYN CONE   On   03-31-95
