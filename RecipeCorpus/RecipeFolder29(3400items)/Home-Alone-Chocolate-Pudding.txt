Title: Home-Alone Chocolate Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Low cal/fat, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  2% milk
1/4 ts Vanilla extract
3 tb Sugar
2 tb Dutch cocoa
1 tb Cornstarch
Combine the sugar, cocoa, and cornstarch in a 1-quart glass
measure, and stir well.  Gradually add the milk, stirring with a
whisk.
Microwave the milk mixture for 1-1/2 minutes on high.  Stir well.
Microwave on medium-high, (70% power), for 1-1/2 minutes till mixture
is thickened.  Whisk in the vanilla.
