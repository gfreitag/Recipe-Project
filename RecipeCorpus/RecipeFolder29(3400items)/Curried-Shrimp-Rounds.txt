Title: Curried Shrimp Rounds
Categories: Mixes, Seafood, Appetizers
Yield: 40 appetizers
2 tb Green onions; finely chopped
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Curry powder
1/2 c  Water chestnuts;thinly slice
Parsley flakes; for garnish
3 c  Quick mix
2/3 c  Milk
2 cn Shrimp; drained
1 c  Cheese, swiss; grated
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Mix quick mix and milk let dough stand for 5 min.  Knead dough.  Roll
out 1/8" thickness. Cut with cookie cutter.  Mix everything else
except water chestnuts and parsley.  Spoon on dough top with water
chestnut and parsley. Bake at 400 for 10-12 minutes.
