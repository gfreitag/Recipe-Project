Title: Barbecue Taco Salads
Categories: Salads, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
6    Lettuce leaves; large
3 c  Lettuce; shredded
Tomato; chopped
Corn chips
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
8 oz Cheez whiz
1/2 c  Barbecue sauce
1 tb Chili powder
Brown meat; drain.  Add onion; cook until tender.  Add chhez whiz,
barbecue sauce and chili powder; stir until melted.  Arrange lettuce
on plate. Top each leaf with shredded lettuce, meat mixture, tomato
and chips. Source: Kraft ad
