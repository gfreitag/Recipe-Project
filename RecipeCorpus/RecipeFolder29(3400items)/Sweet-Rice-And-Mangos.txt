Title: Sweet Rice And Mangos
Categories: Fatfree, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Honey
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Guar gum to thicken
1 c  'sweet' rice
1    Ripe mango
1/2 c  Mashed low fat tofu
2/3 c  Water
1/4 ts Salt
Rinse the rice and let soak in cold water for 5 to 10 hours. Peel and
slice the mango Mix the last 7 ingredients and blend to a smooth
cream.

Drain the rice well and lay in a thin layer on a steamer insert
(cheesecloth on top of the insert may be needed if the holes are too
large) and cook over steam for about 40 minutes or until soft.

Spoon hot rice into small bowls, top with the chilled mango slices and
spoon over the lightly warmed tofu cream.

Roasted cashews or a few shreds of coconut may be added as a garnish
only if you wish.

Source: from a fatfree Oriental Delights cooking class.
