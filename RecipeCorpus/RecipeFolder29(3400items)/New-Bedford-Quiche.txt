Title: New Bedford Quiche
Categories: Pastry, Vegetables, Sausage, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 quiche
1/2 c  Shredded Swiss cheese
1/2 c  Shredded Gruyere cheese
1 1/4 c  Milk
3 lg Eggs
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
1    Unbaked 9-inch piecrust
1/4 lb Breakfast sausages *
1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
*   Crumbled or thinly sliced

Heat oven to 425oF. Bake piecrust to 9 minutes or until slightly
golden; set aside and reduce oven temperature to 350oF. Meanwhile, in
skillet, cook sausages over medium-low heat until done. Remove and
drain on paper towels, reserving drippings in skillet. Add mushrooms,
onion, and green pepper to skillet; saute until vegetables are
softened. Reserve 2 tsp sausage for garnish; add remaining sausage to
vegetables and stir to mix. Spread sausage and vegetable mixture
evenly over piecrust; top with Swiss and Gruyere cheeses. In 2-cup
measuring cup or small bowl, beat together milk and eggs; pour over
vegetables, sausage, and cheese in piecrust. Bake 30 minutes; top
with reserved crumbled sausage and bake 10 to 15 minutes longer or
until center of quiche is set and firm. Let quiche stand 10 minutes
on wire rack before cutting. Submitted By FRED NUNNAMAKER   On
