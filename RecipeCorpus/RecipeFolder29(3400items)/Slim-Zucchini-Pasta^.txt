Title: Slim Zucchini Pasta^
Categories: Pasta, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/2 ts Dried oregano
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1 lg Shredded zucchini
1/2 c  Chopped green onions
2 c  Cooked Angel Hair
1 tb Fresh parsley
1/2 c  Chicken stock
Cook zucchini and green onion until crisp tender. Combine all
ingredients except cheese and reheat until hot. Toss with cheese and
serve.

Per serving: Calories: 146 Protein: 6g Carbohydrates: 28g Fat: 0.8g
Sodium: 67mg Cholesterol: 2mg
