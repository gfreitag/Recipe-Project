Title: Roasted Red Pepper Couscous with Capocollo
Categories: Pasta, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Red pepper flakes
2 c  Chicken broth
1 c  Couscous
1/4 lb Capocollo; finely chopped
Salt and pepper; to taste
Olive oil
1    Onion; finely chopped
4 cl Garlic; finely chopped
1 c  Roasted red peppers; chopped
1 c  Parsley; chopped
Heat olive oil in large pan and saute onions until tender.  Add
garlic, red peppers, parsley and red pepper flakes, mixing together.
Stir in chicken broth and couscous.  Cover pan, remove from heat and
let stand for 5 minutes.  When couscous is done, stir in capocollo,
salt and pepper. Serve immediately.  Source: Delicious Developments
