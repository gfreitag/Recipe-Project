Title: Quick & Easy Green Beans
Categories: Side dish, Cheese/eggs, Vegetables
Yield: 1 side dish
1/2 ts Garlic salt;
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese;
1 3/4 lb Green beans;
1 pk (8-oz) cream cheese;
1/2 ts Salt;
Chop green beans into 1" pieces.  Steam or micrwave until medium firm.
Combine milk, cream cheese, salt, garlic salt and parmesan cheese
sauce pan until cheese thoroughly melts.  Cover green beans with
sauce and toss to coat evenly.  Reheat entire mixture in microwave an
additional 3 minutes. If desired, garnish will chilled sliced cherry
tomatoes or grated carrots. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Food
Section, Mar. 28, 1995 + Boney's Marketplace Advertisement. Brought
