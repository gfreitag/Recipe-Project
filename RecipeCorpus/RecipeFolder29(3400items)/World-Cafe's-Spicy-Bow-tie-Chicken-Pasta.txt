Title: World Cafe's Spicy Bow-tie Chicken Pasta
Categories: Poultry, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
6 tb (To 8 T) Basil Pesto,
-(See World Cafe's Basil
-Pesto recipe)
1/2 c  Chopped cilantro
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
Extra-virgin olive oil
1 ts (To 2 t) Sambal oelek
-(chili paste)
1 lb Skinless, boneless chicken
Breast, cubed
12 oz Bow-tie pasta
Heat olive oil and chili paste in large skillet.  Add chicken and cook
until golden brown.  Cook pasta in boiling, salted water according to
package directions until al dente.  Drain.  Place chicken mixture in
bowl. Add basil pesto, cilantro and cheese.  Add cooked pasta and
toss lightly but thoroughly.  Serve with additional Parmesan cheese
if desired.

Per serving: 426 calories; 24 g protein; 44 g carbohydrate; 17 g fat;
39 mg cholesterol; 228 mg sodium.
