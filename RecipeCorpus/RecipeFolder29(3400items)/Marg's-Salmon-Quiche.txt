Title: Marg's Salmon Quiche
Categories: Pastry, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Chopped onion
1/2 ts Basil *
1    9" pie shell
7 3/4 oz Can salmon
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese
* Tarragon or oregino may be sustituted

Bake pie shell for 10 minutes at 375oF (It will not be brown).
Sprinkle onion, cheese and flaked salmon on bottom of the pie shell.
Mix milk, eggs and seasoning; pour over the ingredients in the pie
shell. Bake at 375oF for 50 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes before
serving. Submitted By FRED NUNNAMAKER   On   03-22-95
