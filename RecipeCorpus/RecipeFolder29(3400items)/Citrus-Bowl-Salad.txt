Title: Citrus Bowl Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 pt Strawberries; cut in half
2 tb Mint; chopped
2 tb Sugar
2    Grapefruits
2    Oranges
2    Kiwi; peeled and sliced
Grate the zest from the orange into a small bowl; mix with sugar.
Section and cut up grapefruit and oranges, reserving 1/4 cup juices.
Mix juices into sugared zest.  Combine grapefruit, oranges, kiwi,
strawberries and mint.  Pour zest mixture over fruit, toss and chill
for 1/2 hour before serving.  93 calories and 0 grams fat.  Source:
