Title: 1991 ICS WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP BOWL OF RED
Categories: Chili
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------PART 1--------------------------------
2 cn Chicken stock
1    8oz can hunt's tomato sauce
1/2 tb Cumin
1    4oz can mild green chilies
1    Jalapeo pepper; seeded and m
1 ts Black pepper
3 tb Gebhardt chili powder
3 lb Beef; cubed or coarse grou
8 oz Ground pork
1 tb Flour
1 tb Wesson oil
1/3 c  Minced onion
1/2 tb Granulated garlic
1 cn Beef stock
MMMMM---------------------------PART 2--------------------------------
1 ts Granulated garlic
1 ts Tabasco pepper sauce
1/2 ts Brown sugar
4 oz Hunt's tomato sauce
4 tb Gebhardt chili powder
2 tb Mild new mexico chili powder
2 tb Cumin
Recipe by: HBWK07A    Chuck Ozburn

Saute meat in oil, drain and add to a 4-quart  cooking pot; add all
other ingredients in Part 1 and simmer, covered, for 1  hour and 30
minutes; uncover, add all ingredients in Part 2 and simmer for 45
minutes. Randy Robinson Columbus, Ohio (representing the state of
Kentucky)
