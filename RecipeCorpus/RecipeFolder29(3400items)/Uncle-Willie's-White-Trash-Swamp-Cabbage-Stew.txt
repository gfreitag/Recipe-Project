Title: Uncle Willie's White Trash Swamp Cabbage Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables, Meats, Whitetrash
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts White sugar
1    Pod of hot gree pepper,
Chopped up
pn Thyme
1 md Swamp cabbage, chopped
3    Pieces of fatback
2 cn Tomatoes
2 lg Chopped onions
Saute the fatback, onions, and swamp cabbage until slightly browned.
Add the remaining ingredients and simmer until it starts to thicken
and tomatoes start to cook down.  About 10 minutes before serving,
add another pinch of thyme and serve over hot cooked rice.  If you
don't live along the Carolina, Georgia, North Florida coast, Hearts
of Palm in a can will work just as well, but don't overcook them.

Origin: Adapted from White Trash Cooking Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
April/95.
