Title: Hawaiian Muffins^
Categories: Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
2    Egg whites, lightly beaten
1/2 c  Oat bran flakes
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 c  Mashed banana
1/4 c  Drained crushed pineapple,
Unsweetened
3 1/2 tb Orange juice concentrate
1/2 c  Lowfat yogurt
Preheat oven to 375F. Spray 6 muffin cups with pam and set aside.
Combine banana, pineapple, yogurt, egg whites and bran flakes. Set
aside for flakes to soften. Mix with combined flour and baking soda.
Spoon into muffin cups and bake until golden brown and a toothpick
inserted in center comes out clean, about 25 minutes.

Per serving: Calories: 92 Protein: 3g Carbohydrates: 19g Fat: trace
Sodium: 101mg Cholesterol: trace
