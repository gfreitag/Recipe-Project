Title: Swedish Coffee-Snack Cake from Mix
Categories: Cake/cookie, Mixes, Kids
Yield: 8 servings
3 1/2 tb Shortening
3 tb Chopped nuts mixed -with-
1 tb Sugar -and-
1 ts Cinnamon
2 c  Snack-cake mix
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Cloves
1    Egg, slightly beaten
2/3 c  Milk
In 8-inch layer-cake or square pan, stir cake mix, cinnamon &
cloves with rubber spatula until well blended.  Stir in egg, milk &
short until well blended.  Scrape down sides of pan. Sprinkle with
nut-sugar mixture. Bake in preheated 375 degree oven 30 to 35
minutes. Cool in pan on rack. Submitted By TERRI WOLTMON   On
04-05-95
