Title: Potato Soup Special
Categories: Potato, Soup/stew
Yield: 10 servings
1 pt Milk
1 pt Cream
Salt And Pepper, to taste
Fresh Parsley, to taste
1/4 lb Bacon
1/4 lb Leeks, finely diced
1 md Onion, diced
2 qt Chicken Stock
1/2 lb White Potatoes, diced
Saute the bacon, leeks and onion, adding some butter to the
drippings. Add to the chicken stock, then add potatoes.  Cook about
25 minutes.  Add milk, cream, salt and pepper, and let the soup heat
but not boil.

Sprinkle parsley on top and serve in warm bowls.

Source:  My Own Cook Book From Stillmeadow and Cape Cod, Glady Taber,
1972. ISBN-0-397-00877-5
