Title: Island Fruit Parfaits
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1    Banana
1/2 c  OJ
1/2 c  Coconut
1/2    Pineapple; cubed
1    Papaya; cubed
2    Kiwi; sliced
1    Mango; chopped
In large bowl, combine pineapple, papaya, kiwi and mango.  In food
processor combine banana and OJ; process until smooth.  Pour banana
mixture over fruit; mix gently.  Cover.  Chill several hours to blend
flavors. Serve in parfait glasses.  Source: Store ad
