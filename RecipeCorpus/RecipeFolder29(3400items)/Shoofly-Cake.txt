Title: Shoofly Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Margarine
1 c  Hot water
1/2 c  Molasses
2 1/2 c  All purpose flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
In mixing bowl, stir together flour, sugar, baking soda and salt. Cut
in margarine until mixture is like crumbs.  Set aside.  Stir together
water and molasses.  Remove 1/3 cup of the crumbs mixture and set
aside. Into large amount of crumb mixture, add water and molasses
mixture. Stir until blended.  Put in greased 8-inch square baking
pan. Sprinkle reserved crumbs over top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25
to 30 minutes or until cake tester inserted in center comes out clean.
