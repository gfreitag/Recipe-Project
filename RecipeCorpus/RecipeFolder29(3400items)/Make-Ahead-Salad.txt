Title: Make Ahead Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1    Head lettuce, torn in bite
Size pieces
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Sugar
2 cn Water chestnuts, sliced
1 md Onion, diced
1 1/2 c  Celery, diced
1 sm Package of frozen peas
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
Mix mayonnaise and sugar together with everything else but lettuce.
Put lettuce over top, cover, refrigerate overnight.  Mix before
serving. Can mix lettuce with others also, but it tends to wilt at
times. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   04-11-95
