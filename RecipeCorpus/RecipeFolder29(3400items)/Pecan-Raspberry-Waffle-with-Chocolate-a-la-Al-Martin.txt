Title: Pecan Raspberry Waffle with Chocolate a la Al Martin
Categories: Pcake/waffl, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------FROM: AL MARTIN---------------------------
MMMMM------------------NOTE:  AMOUNTS MAY VARY-----------------------
White Creme de Cacao
Chopped pecans
Any good quality pancake
-and waffle mix
German chocolate, grated
1 pt Fresh raspberries, washed
-and drained
1/2 pt Whipping cream
Vanilla
Sugar
Put raspberries in a bowl and add Creme do Cacao to taste.  Allow to
stand a bit. Pre-heat a Belgian waffle iron. Mix up a batch of waffle
batter according to package directions. All chopped pecans to the
batter when mixed. Whip cream to stiff consistancy. Add vanilla and
sugar to taste. Add raspberry mixture to whipped cream and fold in.
Cook waffles and serve topped with a healthy portion of the whipped
cream and raspberries. Garnish with grated chocolate. It just doesn't
get any better than this.
