Title: Turkey & Barley Salad ^
Categories: Poultry, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Pitted prunes, quartered
1    Leek, white part only, cut
-into very thin rings
2 tb Safflower oil
2 tb Red wine vinegar
2 tb Sour cream
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/4 c  Barley
4 c  Water
1 3/4 lb Turkey drumstick
1/8 ts Pepper
1    Carrot, chopped
1    Celery stalk, chopped
1    Vegetable bouillon cube
1 c  Boiling water
Cover barley with water and soak 12 hours. After soaking, transfer to
large saucepan. Rub drumstick well with pepper and place on barley.
Cover and simmer gently 50 minutes, turning drumstick after 30
minutes. Add carrot and celery to turkey for final 15 minutes of
cooking time, adding more water if necessary. Drain barley, Remove
turkey from bone and cut into 1 1/4" pieces. Dissolve bouillon in
boiling water. combine barley with prunes, leek, turkey, bouilon,
oil, vinegar, sour cream and salt. Before serving, add a bit more
vinegar and salt if desired.
