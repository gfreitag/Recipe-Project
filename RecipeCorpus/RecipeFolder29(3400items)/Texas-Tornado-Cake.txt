Title: Texas Tornado Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chopped nuts
ICING:
1/2 c  Butter
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
1 c  Flaked coconut
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
2 c  Fruit cocktail
2 ts Baking soda
2 c  Flour
1/4 c  Brown sugar
Sift baking powder and flour.  Add sugar, eggs, and fruit cocktail.
Blend well.  Pour batter into well greased, lightly floured 9 x 13
inch pan. Mix the brown sugar with nuts.  Sprinkle over batter. Bake
at 325 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes.

ICING Boil the butter, sugar, and milk together for 2 minutes. Stir in
coconut. Mix well.  Spoon over hot cake.
