Title: Mama's Rhubarb and Strawberry Sherbet
Categories: Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Heavy cream
1 pt Strawberries
2 c  Cooked rhubarb
2 tb Lemon juice
Wash berries, hull and mash. Press rhubarb through seive, add
remianing ingredients, pour into freezing tray of refrigerator and
freeze without stirring until firm. When ready to serve, scrape up
thin layers of the mixture with an inverted spoon, beat back and
forth in the tray until smooth and serve immediately.
