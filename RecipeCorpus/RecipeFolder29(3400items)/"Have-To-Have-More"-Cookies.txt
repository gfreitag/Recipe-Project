Title: "Have To Have More" Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 approx.
3/4 c  Cocoa
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 pk White chocolate chips
1 1/4 c  Margarine
2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  +1 tbsp flour
Preheat oven to 325F.

Cream together marg. and sugar, add eggs and vanilla and beat.

Add remaining ingredients and mix well.

Put (rounded teaspoon) on ungreased cookie sheet.

Bake for 8 minutes, taking care not to overcook.

Enjoy!
