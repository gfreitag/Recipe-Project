Title: Grilled Salmon Danish Style
Categories: Seafood, Main dish
Yield: 6 people
MMMMM---------------------------SALMON--------------------------------
1/4 ts Pepper;
2 tb Margarine;
6    (6-oz) salmon filets;
1/4 c  Flour;
1/4 ts Paprika;
MMMMM-------------------------DILL SAUCE------------------------------
1 tb Dry sherry;
2 tb Dill weed;
1/2 c  Heavy cream;
1 c  Or 1/4 lb baby shrimp;
-(or salad) cooked
Salt & Pepper to taste
Sprig of fresh dill;
3 tb Margarine;
1 c  Mushrooms; chopped
1/4 c  Onions; chopped
2 tb Brandy;
2 tb Flour;
1 1/2 c  Fish stock -=OR=-
1 1/2 c  Bottled clam juice;
FISH: Combine flour, paprika, salt and pepper.  Dip filets into flour.
Saute salmon in butter just until flaky.

DILL SAUCE: Saute mushrooms and onions in butter until tender.  Add 2
tablespoons brandy, ignite. Reduce heat and stir in flour.  Cook 2 to
3 minutes. Stir in fish in cream, cook 5 minutes.  Stir in shrimp,
cook through. Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Serve one filet
and 1/3 cup of sauce to each person. For 6 people.
