Title: 498511 Blackened Catfish (Cajun  Style)
Categories: Cajun/creol, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Oregano
2 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Red pepper (cayenne)
6    Catfish Fillets
1/2 lb Butter, unsalted, melted
-SEASONING MIX:
3/4 ts White pepper
1/2 ts Thyme
1 tb Paprika
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Onion powder
Cook outdoors: Preheat cast iron skillet on outdoor-cooker at least 10
minutes, until a white ash forms in skillet bottom. Thoroughly combine
seasoning mix in a small bowl. Dip fillets in melted butter so that
both sides are well coated.  Then sprinkle seasoning mix generously
and evenly on both sides of fillets, putting it in by hand.  Place in
hot skillet and add 1 tablespoon of butter over the top of fillets.
(Caution - butter will flame.)  Cook 2 to 3 minutes on each side
until charred. Serve with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.
