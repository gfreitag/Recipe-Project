Title: Quick Guacamole
Categories: Dips/spread, Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
4 dr Hot sauce (such as Tabasco)
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 md Ripe avocado, mashed
1 ts Lemon or lime juice
1 lg Garlic clove, crushed
Mix all the ingredients.  Serve with tortilla chips.

Per serving:  166 CALORIES, 2.1g PROTEIN, 8.4g CARBOHYDRATES, 4.2g
FIBER, 15.4g FAT (2.5g saturated), 277mg SODIUM

From USA WEEKEND, March 31-April 2, 1995.
