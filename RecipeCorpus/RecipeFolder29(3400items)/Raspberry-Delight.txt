Title: Raspberry Delight
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake
Yield: 1 servings
3 oz Cranberry juice
8 oz Whole milk raspberry yogurt
4 oz Half & half cream
Something new to try, other berry yogurts are delicious too.

Mix in blender or stir well by hand. Makes 1 serving, 463 calories,
12.5 g protein.
