Title: 567280 Arroz-Con-Pollo
Categories: Tex/mexican, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Rice
1 tb Salt
2 ts Cayenne pepper
-Pinch saffron
1 tb Parsley
-Chopped pimiento
4 tb Olive oil
2    Onions, sliced
1    Clove garlic, chopped
-Cut up chicken
1 c  Tomato pulp
1/2 c  Peas
2 c  Boiling water
Saute in oil the onion and garlic.  Add chicken and cook until 3/4
done. Add tomato pulp and peas, cooking slowly.  Add water, rice and
salt, pepper, saffron and parsley.  Cook until rice is tender and
dry. (Rice will be a tan color.)  Garnish with pimiento; serve.
