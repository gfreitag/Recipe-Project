Title: Cottage Cheese Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
4 ts Lemon Juice
2 tb Milk
1 c  Cottage Cheese -- Cream
Style
2 tb Sugar
Put the cottage cheese, sugar, and lemon juice in a blender container,
adjust the lid, and blend until the mixture is creamy.  Add the milk,
a tablespoon at a time, and blend until the desired consistency is
reached.

Use on crisp green salads or molded and fresh fruit salads.
