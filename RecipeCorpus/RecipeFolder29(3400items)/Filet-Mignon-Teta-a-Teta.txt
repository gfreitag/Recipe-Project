Title: Filet Mignon Teta a Teta
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------A VALENTINE'S DINNER FOR 2----------------------
2 ea Filet mignons (6oz each)
3 ea Ounces brandy
1 tb Cashew nuts, coarsely
-chopped
4 tb Butter
2 tb Shallots, minced
1 ea Clove garlic, whole
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Soy sauce
Heat 2 tbsp butter in skillet and add shallots and garlic.  Saute over
medium heat for 5 minutes.  Remove and discard garlic.  Increase heat
to high, add worcestershire sauce and soy sauce.  Place filets in
pan.  Let cook 3 minutes on each side, turning once for slightly
rare.  Transfer filets to warm dish.  Deglaze pan with brandy and
then ignite with a match. Remove pan from heat, swirly in remaining
butter to thicken sauce. Pour over filets and top with cashew nuts.
Serve immediately. Origin: Women's Circle, for cooks on the go.
