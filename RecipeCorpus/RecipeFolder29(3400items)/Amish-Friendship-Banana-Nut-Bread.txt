Title: Amish Friendship Banana Nut Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Amish Friendship starter
2 ea Bananas; mashed
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  Flour
1/3 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs
3/4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
Mix all ingredients together.  Place in greased and sugared bread
pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 50 minutes.
