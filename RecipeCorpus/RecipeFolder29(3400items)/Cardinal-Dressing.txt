Title: Cardinal Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Condiments
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Catsup
1 cl Garlic
Pepper
3/4 c  Mazola oil
6 tb Vinegar
1 ts Minced onion
Measure above into a jar.  Cover tightly and shake well.  Shake
thoroughly before serving. Randy Rigg
