Title: Apple/wine pork chops
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Brown sugar
1/4 c  Red wine
4 ea Pork chops
1 c  Apple juice
1 tb Raisins
Brown pork chops in frying pan. Drain off fat. Add apple juice to
pork chops. evenly distribute brown sugar & raisins on top of each
pork chop. Bring to boil & then simmer covered for 25 to 30 minutes (
do not turn pork chops ). Add red wine & simmer covered 10 to 15
minutes or until all liquid is dissolved ( gone from pan ).
