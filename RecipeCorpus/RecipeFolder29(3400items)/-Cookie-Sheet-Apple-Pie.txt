Title:  Cookie Sheet Apple Pie

3 3/4   cups    flour 1 1/2 tsp. salt 3/4 cup shortening 3 eggs,
lightly beaten 1/3 cup milk 8 cups sliced peeling baking apples 1 1/2
cups sugar 1 tsp.    ground cinnamon 1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg 1 cup
crushed cornflakes 1 egg white, beaten

In a bowl, combine flour and salt.  Cut in shortening until mixture
resembles coarse crumbs.  Add eggs and milk; mix to form dough.
Chill for 20 minutes.  Divide dough in half; roll one half to fit the
bottom and sides of a greased 15x10x1" baking pan. Arrange apples
over crust. Combine sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and cornflakes; sprinkle
over apples.  Roll remaining dough to fit top of pan and place over
apples.  Seal edges; cut slits in top.  Brush with egg white.  Bake
at 400 degrees for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake
for 25-30 minutes or until golden.
