Title: White Trash Soda Cracker Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Crackers, Whitetrash
Yield: 1 servings
3 ea Egg whites
1 c  Nut meats, chopped (your
-choice)
12 ea Soda crackers, crumbled
1 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
12 ea Dates, chopped
Beat the egg whites until foamy.  Gradually add the sugar and continue
beating until stiff peaks form when beaters are raised out of
mixture. Mix the crushed soda crackers with the dates and nuts, and
gently fold into the beaten egg white mixture.  Pour into a greased
pie pan and bake at 350F for about 30 minutes or until brown.  Can be
served with ice cream or whipped topping as garnish. Origin: Adapted
from White Trash. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, April/95.
