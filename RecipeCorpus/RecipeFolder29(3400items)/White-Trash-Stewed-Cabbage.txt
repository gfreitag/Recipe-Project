Title: White Trash Stewed Cabbage
Categories: Vegetables, Meats, Whitetrash
Yield: 1 servings
OR
1 c  Virginia smoked ham chunks
1    Head of cabbage, cut into
Quarters
3    4 slices of fatback
Break up cabbage chunks with your hands.  Fry down the meat.  Add
cabbage and continue to fry for about 10 minutes, stirring often.
Add 1 cup water and salt and pepper to taste.  Bring to a boil, cover
and steam for about 20 minutes on medium heat.  35 minutes if you
prefer it grayer.

Aunt Bertie Mack says you can't cook a good stewed cabbage unless you
know how to sing: Boil that cabbage down boy, boil that cabbage down
Bat you eyes til the crick done rise, But boil that cabbage down!
