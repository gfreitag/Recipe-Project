Title: 918924 Spam And Shrimp Salad
Categories: Spam, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Chopped celery
-Pimento
-Green pepper
1    (8 oz.) pkg. shell macaroni
1    (10 oz.) pkg. frozen peas
1 cn Spam
1 cn Shrimp, left whole
Cook macaroni; cool and drain.  Cook peas; drain and cool.  Mix all
ingredients together.  Moisten with salad dressing to which you may
add a small amount of mustard, vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper.
Chill. Garnish with parsley and tomato slices.
