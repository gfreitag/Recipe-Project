Title: Tapioca Preparation+
Categories: Information
Yield: 1 info
1 c  Milk
1 c  Pearl Tapioca
MMMMM--------------------------COOKING-------------------------------
3 c  Milk
To make tapioca, soak the tapioca in one cup of milk overnight in the
refrigerator..

Mix the mixture with 3 cups of milk in the top of a double boiler (be
sure the water does not touch the bottom of the upper pan. Cook from
1 1/2 to 3 hours depending on the size of the pearls.

Find a recipe for tapioca or invent your own. There are some recipes
in "The Joy of Cooking".
