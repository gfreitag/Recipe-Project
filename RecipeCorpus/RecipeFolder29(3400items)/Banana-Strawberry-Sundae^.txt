Title: Banana Strawberry Sundae^
Categories: Desserts, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
Preserves
1/4 c  Grape Nuts
4    Very ripe bananas
12    Frozen strawberries
1 tb No sugar added jam or
Peel banana, wrap in plastic and freeze 24 hours. Remove from freezer
and break into thirds. Let banana pieces and strawberries thaw 5-10
minutes to soften slightly. Blend in blender until creamy, about 5
minutes. Top with jam, sprinkle with Grape Nuts an

Per serving: Calories: 83 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 21g Fat: trace
Sodium: 23mg Cholesterol: 0mg
