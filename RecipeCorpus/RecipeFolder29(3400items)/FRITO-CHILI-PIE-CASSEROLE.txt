Title: FRITO CHILI PIE CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Cheese/eggs, Meats, Chili
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Chili (19 oz.)
1 c  American cheese; grated
3 c  Fritos corn chips
1 lg Onion; minced
Place 2 cups of corn chips in a baking dish. Arrange chopped onion
and half of the cheese on the corn chips. Pour chili over onion and
cheese, then top with remaining corn chips and grated cheese. Bake at
350~ for 55-60 minutes. Makes 2-4 servings.
