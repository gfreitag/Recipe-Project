Title: Cheesecake Shake
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake
Yield: 1 servings
2 oz Whole milk
3/16    Piece plain cheesecake
1/4 c  Frozen strawberries, thawed
A favorite dessert, shake-style.

Mix well in blender. Makes 1 serving, 356 calories, 7 g. protein.
