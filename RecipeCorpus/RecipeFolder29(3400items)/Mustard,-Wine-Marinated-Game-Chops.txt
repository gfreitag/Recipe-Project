Title: Mustard, Wine Marinated Game Chops
Categories: Game, Hot/spicy, Marinades
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Stone ground mustard
1 c  Red wine
4    Caribou or Deer chops
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Salt
Rub chops with mustard.   Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cover with
wine and marinate overnight in refrigerator. Broil or charcoal grill
to medium rare basting with the marinade.
