Title: Broccoli
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Evaporated milk
1    Can french fried onions
2 pk Frozen broccoli
1 c  Grated sharp cheese
1    Can mushroom soup
Cook broccoli, but not as long.  Put in greased 2 qt baking dish.
Sprinkle cheese over it.  Mix soup and milk and pour over broccoli
mixture.

Bake at 350 for 25 mins.  Sprinkle onions over all and bake 8-10 mins
more. From Westminster Church Cookbook Posted by Lisa Greenwood
