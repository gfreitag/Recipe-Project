Title: Spinach Souffle^
Categories: Healthy, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Low sodium soy sauce
1 pk (10oz) thawed, drained
Chopped spinach
3 md Potatoes, boiled
2    Egg whites
2 tb Skim milk
Process all ingredients except spinach until smooth. Add spinach and
blend until smooth. Bake in a sprayed baking dish at 375F. 15-20
minutes until puffed and golden brown. Makes 4-5 servings.

Per serving: Calories: 71 Protein: 5g Carbohydrates: 13g Fat: 0g
Sodium: 320mg Cholesterol: trace
