Title: Chocolate Taffy Candy
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter
Cook sugar and butter over med heat.  Boil 7 mins. Pour in 9 inch
buttered pan and place chips on top to melt for frosting. Break in
pieces when cool. These are like Heath bars.
