Title: COW PIES
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts, Snacks
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Chopped slivered almonds
2 c  (12 oz.) milk chocolate
-- chips
1 tb Shortening
"The kids loved these once they got a taste of these yummy treats
packed with raisins and almonds and covered in rich milk chocolate."
~ Karen Kenney

In a double boiler over simmering water, melt the chocolate chips and
shortening, stirring until smooth.

Remove from the heat; stir in raisins and almonds.

Drop by tablespoonfuls onto waxed paper. Chill until ready to serve.
