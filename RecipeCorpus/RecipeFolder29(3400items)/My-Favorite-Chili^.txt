Title: My Favorite Chili^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Fresh mushrooms, quartered
2 c  No salt added tomato juice
2 tb Salsa
1 tb Chili powder
1 ts Ground cumin
1 ts Dried leaf basil
1 tb Minced fresh parsley
2 cn (16oz) kidney beans, rinsed
& drained
1 lb Lean flank steak, ground or
Verl lean ground beef
1 lg Stalk celery, cut in 4-5
Pieces
1 lg Onion, quartered
2 lg Carrots, in poeces
2 cl Garlic
1 c  Chicken or veggie stock
1 cn (16oz) no salt added
Tomatoes
1 cn (6oz) no salt added tomato
Paste
1    Diced red bell pepper
1 cn (4oz) chopped green chilies
Coarsley chop vegetables and cook in broth until soft; add tomatoes,
tomato paste and bell pepper. Cook 5 minutes. Add chiles, mushrooms,
salsa and tomato juice and simmer 5 minutes. Add remaining
ingredients and cook another hour. Serve hot. Makes 6 servings.

Variation: Add 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes for a spicier dish.

Per serving: Calories: 257 Protein: 17g Carbohydrates: 42g Fat: 3g
Sodium: 116mg Cholesterol: 38mg
