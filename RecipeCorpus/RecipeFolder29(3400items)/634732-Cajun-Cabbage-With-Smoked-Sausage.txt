Title: 634732 Cajun Cabbage With Smoked Sausage
Categories: Cajun/creol, Main dish, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
12 tb Unsalted butter
2    Onions, julienned
1/2    Head cabbage, shredded
1/2 c  Chicken broth (or bouillon)
1    Bay leaf, halved
1    Apple, julienned
1 1/2 ts Dark brown sugar
1/2 lb Good smoked sausage, cut up
1 ts Salt
3/4 ts Sweet paprika
1/2 ts White pepper
1/4 ts Onion powder
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Cayenne
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Dried thyme
1/8 ts Dried basi
Mix above together.  Melt butter; saute onions over high heat. Add
cabbage, 1/4 cup chicken stock, bay leaf and seasoning.  Cook 5
minutes. Add apple and cook 15 minutes.  Add sugar and remaining
broth. Add sausage; cook until hot.
