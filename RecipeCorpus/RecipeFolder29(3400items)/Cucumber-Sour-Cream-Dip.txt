Title: Cucumber Sour Cream Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 3 cups
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Dill; chopped
1    Cucumber; peeled, seeded and
-finely grated
2 c  Lite sour cream
Squeeze out excess liquid from grated cucumber.  Combine with
remaining ingredients.  Chill and serve.  Each 2 Tbsp serving
contains 32 calories and 2 grams fat.  Source: Store ad
