Title: 539239 Herbed Corn Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
-mix
1 sm Onion, minced
1 ts Parsley flakes
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
2 tb Oleo
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1    Egg, well beaten
1 cn Whole kernel corn, drained
1 c  Herb seasoned bread stuffing
Combine milk and mayonnaise, mix well.  Add egg, corn, stuffing,
onion and parsley.  Pour into greased and floured 8 inch round baking
pan. Toss bread crumbs with melted oleo.  Sprinkle over corn mix.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
