Title: Master Mix Drop Biscuits
Categories: Healthy
Yield: 12 servings
3 c  Master Mix
1 c  Skim Milk
1. Preheat oven to 425 F.

2. Add milk all at once to the Master MIx, stirring until flour is
moistened.

3. Drop from a tablespoon onto an oiled baking sheet.

4. Bake for 10-12 mins.

YIELD: 12 biscuits

1 biscuit = 118 Cal; 3 Prot; 4.8 Fat; 16 Carb; 0.6 Sat; 1.1 Mono; 2.8
Poly; 0 Chol; 250 Na
