Title: White Trash Fig Surprise
Categories: Snacks, Breads, Whitetrash, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Cayenne
3 tb Water
2 c  Flour
1 c  Grated sharp cheddar cheese
1 ea Stick butter
Blend the flour, cheese and butter together until it's orange in
color and smooth in texture. Add the cayenne and the water until it's
a nice smooth consistency.  Roll out and cut into triangles.  In each
triangle, place a fig.  Fold the three points into the center.
Dampen with water or milk and pinch together to seal.  Bake in a
350-375F oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown. Origin:
Adapted from White Trash. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, April/95.
