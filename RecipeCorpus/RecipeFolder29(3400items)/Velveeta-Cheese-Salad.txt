Title: Velveeta Cheese Salad
Categories: Salads, Velveeta
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Flour
1    Egg
2 c  Velveeta cheese, cubed
2 c  Pineapple, save the juice
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
Mix together the cheese cubes, pineapple chunks, chopped nuts and
marshmallows.  In a saucepan, melt the butter; add sugar, flour and 1
cup pineapple juice, adding enough lemon juice to equal 1 cup.  Cook
sauce slightly to a boil.  Gradually add the egg which has been well
beaten. The sauce should thicken.  Let sauce cool completely before
pouring over cheese mixture. Randy Rigg
