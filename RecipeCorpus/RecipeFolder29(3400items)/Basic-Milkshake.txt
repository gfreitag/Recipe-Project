Title: Basic Milkshake
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake
Yield: 1 servings
4 tb Powdered skim milk
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Whole milk
1 c  Vanilla ice cream
Combine all ingredients and mix together. Calories 424, 16.3 gm.
protein.

For variety try:

1. chocolate ice cream , chocolate syrup.

2. Strawberry ice cream, strawberry "Quick".

3. Chocolate chip mint ice cream, mint extract.

4. Coffee ice cream, vanilla extract or chocolate syrup. 5. Peanut
butter.
