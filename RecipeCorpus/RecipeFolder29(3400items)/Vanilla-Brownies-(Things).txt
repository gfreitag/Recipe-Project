Title: Vanilla Brownies (Things)
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/4 c  Brown sugar (packed)
1 ts Vanilla
3    Eggs
12 oz Chocolate chips if desired
2 1/4 c  Unsifted flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Butter - softened
1 1/4 c  Sugar
Preheat oven to 350.  In small bowl combine flour, baking powder and
salt. Set aside.  In large bowl beat butter, sugar, brown sugar and
vanilla until creamy.

Add eggs, one at a time.  Gradually beat in flour mixture.

Add chips (if desired)

Spread evenly into well greased 15 x 10 x 1 baking pan (approximate
size of pan)

Bake 350 35 - 40 min.  Cool in pan on rack.   When completely cool
cut into pieces.
