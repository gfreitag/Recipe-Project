Title: 152666 Spam And Egg Brunch
Categories: Spam
Yield: 1 servings
8    Eggs, lightly beaten
2 2/3 c  Milk
1 ts Dry mustard
8 sl Bread, cubed (after crusts
-are removed)
1 cn Spam or 1 lb. cubed ham
1 c  Shredded Velveeta cheese
Butter a 9 x 13 inch glass baking dish.  Place bread cubes on bottom.
Cover with meat.  Sprinkle cheese over all.  Combine eggs, milk, and
mustard. Pour evenly over cheese.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Bake, uncovered, in 350 degree oven for 45 minutes.  Let sit 10
minutes before cutting. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-01-95
