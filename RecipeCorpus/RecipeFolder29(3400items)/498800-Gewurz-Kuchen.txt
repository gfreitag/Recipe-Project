Title: 498800 Gewurz Kuchen
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1    Stick (1/4 lb.) oleo or
-butter, room temp.
4    Eggs
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1    Jigger rum
3 c  Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
4 ts Baking powder
-Tip of the knife nutmeg
6 tb Cocoa
Sift all together and set aside.  Cream together, then add flour
mixture alternately with 1 cup milk.  Lightly grease a springform or
bundt cake pan and sprinkle with bread crumbs.  Pour in batter and
bake 45 to 60 minutes or toothpick comes out clean.  Sprinkle with
powdered sugar. Ye older the cake the better.
