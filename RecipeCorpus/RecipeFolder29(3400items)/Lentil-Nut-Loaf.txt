Title: Lentil Nut Loaf
Categories: Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Sage or thyme
2 tb Torula yeast
2 tb Soy flour
2    Eggs, beaten
1/2 c  Vegetable stock or water
1 tb Vinegar
Sesame seeds
1 sm Onion, chopped fine
3 tb Oil
1/2 c  Wheat germ
2 c  Cooked, drained lentils
1/2 c  Whole wheat bread crumbs
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds or walnut
. pieces
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Saute onion in oil until soft.

Mix ingredients and place in large, ungreased loaf pan.  Sprinkle
generously with sesame seeds.  Bake for 30 minutes, covered with
aluminum foil or a cookie sheet, and then for 10 minutes uncovered.

From "Laurel's Kitchen:  A Handbook for Vegetarian Cookery &
Nutrition" by Laurel Robertson, Carol Flinders, and Bronwen Godfrey,
Bantam Books 1978, ISBN 0-553-12278-9 (paperback)
