Title: Meal-In-A-Dish
Categories: Poultry, Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Cheddar -OR- Swiss cheese;
- shredded
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Tabasco
1/2 ts Salt
Paprika
4 sl White or whole wheat bread
2 c  Cooked vegetables;
- chopped and drained
- carrots, mushrooms,
- broccoli, or a combo
1/4 c  Green onion; thinly sliced
1 1/2 c  Chicken; cooked, diced
- OR- ham -OR- turkey
Trim crusts from bread, if desired, and spread lightly with butter,
then cut each slice into 4 triangles. Arrange 8 of the triangles,
butter side up, in a Well-buttered 8" square baking dish. Evenly top
with your choice of vegetables, the onion, meat and cheese.
Decoratively arrange remaining bread triangles, butter side down, on
top. Stir together the eggs, milk, Worcestershire, Tabasco and salt.
Pour over bread; press lightly with a fork to be sure egg mixture is
absorbed. Sprinkle with paprika. Cover and chill 4 hours or
overnight. Bake, uncovered, in a 375~ oven for 35-40 minutes or until
set when dish is gently jiggled. Let stand 10-15 minutes before
serving. Makes 4-6 servings. Submitted By BOBBI ZEE   On   04-16-95
