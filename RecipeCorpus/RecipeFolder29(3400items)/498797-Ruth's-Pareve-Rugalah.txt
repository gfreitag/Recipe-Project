Title: 498797 Ruth's Pareve Rugalah
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Baking powder
3    Egg yolks
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 c  Shortening
1/3 c  Sugar
3 c  Flour
Mix shortening, sugar, baking powder and flour in bowl.  Add egg
yolks and orange juice.  Knead in bowl.  (Add a bit more flour, if
needed.) Divide into 4 balls and roll 1/4 inch out into a rectangle.
Sprinkle cinnamon, sugar and chopped nuts or spread with jelly and
nuts. Roll up and cut into 3/4 inch pieces.  Bake on cookie sheet
20-25 minutes at 350 degrees. (Watch Carefully!)  You may roll up 3-4
times over, cut and roll rest up, thus making two rolls or more from
each ball. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-16-95
