Title: Marinated Mirliton
Categories: Appetizers, Relishes, Vegetables, Cajun/creol, Marinades
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 ts Oregano, powdered
1/2 ts Red pepper flakes
Garlic cloves (whole)
-as desired.
Garlic powder to taste
12 ea Mirlitons
10 tb Vinegar (cider)
1/2 ts Thyme, powdered
2 tb Salt
1 c  Corn oil
Peel and remove seed from mirlitons and cut into bite size pieces.
In lge. pot of boiling water, place mirlitons and cook for no more
than 3 min. Drain and cool. Make up marinade and pour over mirlitons.

Floyd Shumann- Hammond, LA
