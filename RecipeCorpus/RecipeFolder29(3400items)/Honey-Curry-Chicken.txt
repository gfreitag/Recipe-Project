Title: Honey Curry Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2    Chicken breasts without
Skin
3 ts Curry Powder
1/4 c  PREPARED MUSTARD
1/3 c  MARGARINE
1/2 c  Honey
Melt margarine in cooking dish you will cook the chicken in, in the
microwave.  Add honey, mustard and curry powder. Stir until well
blended.

Add chicken.  Coat in sauce and turn pieces over. Cook for 20 minutes
uncovered at 375 F.  Turn pieces and baste. Cover with foil and cook
for 25 minutes.

Double sauce if you want to pour over rice.
