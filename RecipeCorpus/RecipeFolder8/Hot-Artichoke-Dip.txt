Title: Hot Artichoke Dip
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/8 ts Tabasco Sauce
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
1/2 c  Sour Cream
14 oz Artichoke Hearts *
* Artichoke hearts should be drained and chopped.
~-------------------------------------------------------------------------
