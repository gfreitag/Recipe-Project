Title: Sausage Balls #1
Categories: Appetizers, Pork/ham, Cheese/eggs, Sausages
Yield: 10 servings
3 c  Bisquick
1/3 c  Water
1 lb Bulk Sausage (Hot Or Mild)
2 c  Sharp Chedder Cheese Grated
Combine all ingredients and shape into balls the size of walnuts. Bake on
cookie sheet at 350øF for 15 minutes. Makes about 5 dozen. NOTE: defrost
and reheat at 350øF.
