Title: Mexican Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 12 servings
--------------------------------FIRST LAYER--------------------------------
2 cn Jalapeno Bean Dip
--------------------------------SECOND LAYER--------------------------------
8 oz Taco Sauce
2    Large Avocados (Mashed)
--------------------------------THIRD LAYER--------------------------------
1 pk Taco Mix
1 pt Sour Cream
------------------------------------TOP------------------------------------
Black Olives
Green Onions
1 c  Chedder Cheese (Shred)
Chopped Tomatoes
Green Chiles
Layer ingredients as listed and serve with corn chips.
