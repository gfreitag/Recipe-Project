Title: West Indies Salad
Categories: Appetizers, Fish/sea
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Olive Oil
4 oz Vinegar
4 oz Ice Water
1 lb Crabmeat
3 tb Chopped Onion
Salt And Pepper
Put onions in bowl, add meat, salt, and pepper.  Then oil, vinegar, and ice
water in that order, let set at least 1 hour before serving.
