Title: Crab Dip #2
Categories: Appetizers, Fish/sea
Yield: 8 servings
2 ts Prepared Mustard
2 ts Powdered Sugar
2/3 c  White Wine
1 lb Crabmeat
1/2 c  Mayonnaise Or Salad Dressing
Garlic Salt, To Taste
2 tb Onion, Grated
Mix together all ingredients except crabmeat.  Heat slowly.  Add Crabmeat.
Serve warm with crackers.
