Title: Brown Sugar Pudding Sauce
Categories: Penndutch, Desserts, Sauces
Yield: 1 servings
-
-
-
1 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1 ts Vanilla
DIRECTIONS  ------------------------------------------------------------
Beat all together until creamy.
Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary
Arts Press, 1936.
