Title: Butter Sauces #4
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Chopped fresh chives
1 tb Or more gold tequila
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 lb Unsalted butter (2 sticks),
Softened.
4 cl Garlic, minced
1/2 c  Chopped fresh oregano
2 tb Chopped parsley
1/2 ts Crushed dried red chile
Pepper
