Title: Fruit Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 servings
2    Cut up peaches
1/2 c  Sugar optional
Non dairy whipped topping
Thawed
Sliced bananas
2    Cut up apples
3    Cut up pears
1    Cut up orange
Seedless grapes cut in half
1 cn Crushed/tidbit pineapple
Mix apples, pears, orange, grapes, pineapple, peaches and sugar together.
Top fruit salad with non-dairy whipped topping then with sliced bananas.
