Title: Jerk Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Thyme
1 1/2 ts Fresh-grnd black pepper
3/4 ts Nutmeg
2 tb Salt
1 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Soy sauce
Juice of one orange
1    Hot pepper, seeded &
Chopped
1 tb Allspice
1 1/2 ts Cayenne
1 1/2 ts Rubbed sage
3/4 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Garlic powder
1/4 c  Oil
3/4 c  White vinegar
Juice of one lime
1 c  Green onions, chopped
Toss all the ingredients in the blender and blend-away.

Enjoy, Terri St.Louis-Woltmon
