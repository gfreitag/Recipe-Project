Title: IMPOSSIBLE CHEESEBURGER PIE
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Ground Beef
1 1/2 c  Onion, chopped
Salt
Pepper
1 1/2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Bisquick
3    Eggs
Sliced Tomato
1 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1.  Heat oven to 400F.  Grease pie plate. 2. Brown beef and onion;
drain. Stir in salt and pepper. Spread in
plate. 3. Beat milk, baking mix and eggs until smooth, 15 sec. in
blender on
high or 1 min. with hand beater.  Pour into place. 4. Bake 25
min. Top with tomatoes; sprinkle with cheese. 5. Bake until knife
inserted in center comes out clean 5-8 minutes.
Cool 5 minutes prior to serving.
