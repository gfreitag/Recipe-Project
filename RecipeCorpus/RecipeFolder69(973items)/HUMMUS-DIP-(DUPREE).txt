and rinsed
Yield: 4 Servings

2 TB fresh lemon juice
4    garlic cloves -- chopped

1 TB dark sesame oil

1/2 ts cayenne
Salt
freshly ground black pepper
Pita bread -- chips for
serving

:
4    chopped green onions -- for
garnish
:
1 TB chopped fresh cilantro --
for garnish
:
1 TB extra virgin olive oil --
for garnish
:

In bowl of a food processor begin to process chickpeas, gradually
adding 1/4 cup hot water to obtain a light, spreadable consistency.
Add lemon juice, garlic, peanut butter, sesame oil, cumin and
cayenne, and season to taste with salt and pepper. Process until well
blended and smooth. Mound spread in a serving bowl or on a platter
and surround with pita chips. Sprinkle with green onion, cilantro,
and olive oil. Makes 2 cups
