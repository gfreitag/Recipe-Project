Title: INDIANA CHILI
Categories: Main dish, Chili
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Hamburger
1    (32 oz.) can tomato juice
2    (30 oz.) cans or 4 (15 1/2
-oz.) cans chili beans
-Chili powder to taste
-Diced onion to taste
Preparation : Brown hamburger in skillet with onions. Drain grease
and add to pot of tomato juice, beans and chili powder. Simmer
covered for about 30 to 45 minutes. Serve topped with chunks of colby
or colby-Jack cheese to melt in, for extra flavor. From: Tracy Schell
