Title: INDONESIAN CHICKEN & RICE
Categories: Poultry, Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Chicken Broth -- (hot)
1 ts Dark Corn Syrup -- or
Molasses
2 tb Soy Sauce
3 tb Vegetable Oil
4    Chicken Breast Halves --
Diced
1 sm Onion -- chopped
1 sm Red Pepper -- chopped
1 ts Ginger -- ground
Crushed Red Pepper
2 ts Curry Powder
3 c  Cooked Rice -- cold
1/2 c  Peanuts -- chopped
1/4 c  Scallions -- minced
1/4 c  Parsley -- minced
Mix broth, corn syrup, and soy sauce.  Stir fry chicken in hot oil.
Add onion, red pepper, ginger, and crushed red pepper and cook 30
seconds. Stir in curry powder and rice and cook for 1 minutes. Add
liquids. Stir in peanuts, scallions, and parsley and heat.
