Title: IMPOSSIBLE NO-CRUST SWEET POTATO PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts, Holiday, S_living
Yield: 1 Pie
4    Sweet potatoes; *
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Butter or margarine;softened
1    Egg
1/2 ts To 1 tsp almond extract
Whipped cream
* Medium size\peeled\cooked\mashed Combine all the ingredients except
whipped cream in a large mixing bowl; beat at medium speed of electric
mixer until well blended. Spoon mixture into a 9-inch pie plate. Bake
at 450^ for 25 minutes or until lightly browned. Serve warm or cool
with a dollop of whipped cream.
YIELD: One 9-inch pie.
