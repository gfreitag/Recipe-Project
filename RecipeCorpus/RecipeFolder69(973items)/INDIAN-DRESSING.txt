Title: INDIAN DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Digest
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 ts Ground coriander
1/4 ts Chopped cilantro
1/2 ts Prepared sweet-hot mustard
Put all ingredients in a small bowl, and stir.  Let sit about 10
minutes.
