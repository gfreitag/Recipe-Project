Title: HUMMUS #2
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Safflower Oil
2 tb Chopped Onion
Clove garlic, minced
1/4 c  Minced fresh parsley
1 ts Basil
1/4 ts Ground Coriander
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Black pepper
15 oz Can drained Chick Peas
3 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Toasted Sesame seeds
1 ds Cumin
GARNISH: lemon wedges, cherry tomatoes, or fresh parsley sprigs (opt)
In a small skillet, heat oil; saute onion and garlic until onion is
softened. Add seasonings. Stir just long enough to soften parsley.
In food processor, combine rinsed and drained chick peas and lemon
juice; process until smooth. Stir in onion and herb mixture; stir in
sesame seeds. Spoon into serving bowl. Top with garnish if desired.
Makes 1 1/2 cups VARIATIONS: - substitute 15-oz can Great Northern
beans for chick peas.
