Title: HOUSE VINAIGRETTE DRESSING
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1/3 c  RED WINE VINEGAR
1 x  SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE
1 pn TARRAGON
1 tb DIJON MUSTARD
1/2 c  OLIVE OIL
1/2 c  VEGETABLE OIL
Whisk vinegar into salt, pepper, tarragon and mustard. Add oils,
continuously whisking until dressing is light and fluffy. This
light, tasty salad is a perfect accompaniment to a fish dinner.
