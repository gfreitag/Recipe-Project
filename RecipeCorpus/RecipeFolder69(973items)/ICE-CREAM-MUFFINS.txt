Title: ICE CREAM MUFFINS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 c  Pillsbury self-rising flour
.or regular flour and add 1
.Tbsp baking powder and 1
.tsp salt
2 c  Very soft vanilla ice cream
1    Egg
2 tb Oil
Measure flour and ice cream into mixing bowl.  Mix only until dry
ingredients are moistened.  Fill well greased muffin cups
three-fourths full.  Bake at 450 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes until
golden brown. Note: You may vary the flavor of ice cream and create
different flavored muffins. For variation, add 1/2 cup chocolate
chips and 1/2 cup chopped nuts in batter. Sprinkle with cinnamon and
sugar.
