Title: INDIVIDUAL SUMMER PUDDINGS
Categories: Fruits, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
7 c  Fresh berries (raspberries,
-blackberries,blueberries)
1/3 c  Granulated sugar
1 tb Lemon juice
12    Soft ladyfingers
Reserve 1 cup berries for garnish.  In nonaluminum saucepan, combine
remaining berries, sugar and lemon juice; cover and cook over
medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, for 7 to 10 minutes or until
softened and juicy but not mushy.

Cut each ladyfinger into thirds; place 2 pieces in each of six 3/4 cup
stemmed glasses.  Spoon scant 1/4 cup fruit mixture over top. Repeat
layers twice, pressing with back of spoon.

Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or up to 1 day. Let stand
at room temperature for 1 hour before serving. Garnish with reserved
berries. Makes 6 servings. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com
Source: Canadian Living's Best Easy Cooking
