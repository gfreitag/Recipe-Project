Title: IRISH CREAM  (BTVC62A)
Categories: Beverages, Snacks
Yield: 8 Servings
6    Eggs
1 c  Sweetened condensed milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 tb Instant coffee (folgers)
1 1/2 c  Irish whiskey; must be the
Tastes better than Bailey's! Whip eggs in blender; add milk and
vanilla. Liquify coffee with 1/2 of whiskey, then blender all
ingredients. Serve chilled. Salute! Rita in Scottsdale FROM: RITA
TAULE (BTVC62A)
