Title: IMPOSSIBLE BUTTERMILK PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Bisquick
1/3 c  Melted butter
1 ts Vanilla
3 x  Eggs
Heat oven to 350. Grease pie plate. Beat all ingredients until
smooth, 30 seconds in the blender on high, or 1 minute with hand
beater. Pour into pie plate. Bake until knife inserted in center
comes out clean, about 30 minutes. Cool 5 minutes. Serve with fresh
fruit, if desired. ~--
