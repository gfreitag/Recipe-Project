Title: ICEBOX ANGEL CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Angel food cake
24    Marshmallows
1/2 c  Milk
1 cn Crushed pineapple (lg),
Well drained
1    Jar maraschino cherries,
Chopped
1 lg Carton of Cool Whip
Angel flake coconut
Crumble angel food cake into a 9x13 inch glass pan. Melt marshmallows
with milk.  Spoon over cake.  Spoon pineapple over top then cherries.
Spread Cool Whip over all. Sprinkle with coconut and refrigerte.
Randy Rigg
