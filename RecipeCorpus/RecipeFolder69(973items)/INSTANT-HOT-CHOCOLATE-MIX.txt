Title: INSTANT HOT CHOCOLATE MIX
Categories: Beverages, Mixes
Yield: 1 Gallon mix
22 oz Non-dairy creamer powder
32 oz Nonfat dry milk powder
1 1/2 lb Dry chocolate mix
1/4 c  Powdered sugar (opt)
Mix all ingredients thoroughly.  Store in tightly covered container.
To serve:  Fill a cup about 1/2 full of mix. Add hot water. OR add
cold water and microwave on HIGH for 2 minutes.
