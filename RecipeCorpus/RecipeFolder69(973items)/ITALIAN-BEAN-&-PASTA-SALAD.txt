Title: ITALIAN BEAN & PASTA SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Recipe
2 c  Cooked AM Anasazi Beans
2 c  Cooked spinach noodles
-OR- vegetable rotelli
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 md Chopped tomato
1/4 c  Chopped bell pepper
1 tb AM Sesame Seed
1/8 c  AM Unrefined Olive Oil
1/8 c  Lemon juice
1 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Basil
1/4 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Sea salt (optional)
Gently mix all ingredients together in a large mixing bowl.
Refrigerate till cold and serve.  Allowing the mixture to set for an
hour before serving will enhance the flavor.
