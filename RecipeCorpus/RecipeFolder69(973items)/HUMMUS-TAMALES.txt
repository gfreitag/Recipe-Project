Title: HUMMUS TAMALES
Categories: Vegetarian, Tex-mex, Mid-east, Main dish
Yield: 9 Servings
1    Basic Masa Dough recipe
18    Corn husks
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
3 c  Chickpeas; cooked
1/2 c  Sesame tahini
6 tb Vinegar, brown rice
3    Garlic cloves; minced
3 tb Parsley, fresh; chopped
2 tb Tamari
To make filling:

Blend filling ingredients in a food processor to make a thick paste.
Add a bit of water, if necessary, to achieve desired consistency.

Fill and cook tamales according to "Tamales: Basic Procedure".

Per serving (2 tamales): 468 cal; 7 g prot; 401 mg sod; 83 g carb;
10 g fat; 0 mg chol; 95 mg calcium

Vegetarian Gourmet, Summer 1993/MM by DEEANNE
