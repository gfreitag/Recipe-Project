Title: HUMMUS III
Categories: Appetizers, Middle east, Snacks, Dips
Yield: 1 Recipe
3 c  Chick peas, cooked
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Lemon juice
4 ea Garlic cloves, chopped
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Tahini
2 tb Fresh parsley, chopped
3 tb Olive oil
Place all ingredients in a blender of food processor, and puree to a
fine consistency, adding a little more liquid as necessary to make a
smooth, light paste. Serve immediately with salad and pita bread.
