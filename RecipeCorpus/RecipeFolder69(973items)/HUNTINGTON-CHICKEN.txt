Title: HUNTINGTON CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 3 Servings
1    4 pound hen
2 c  Shell macaroni
1/2 lb Velveeta cheese, diced
4 c  Broth
1/2 c  Cream
2 tb Butter
8 tb Flour
4 c  Bread crumbs
Stew chicken & season to taste.  Cut in cubes.  Cook macaroni in salt
water & drain.  Thicken broth with flour. Mix chicken, macaroni,
cheese and broth.  Put into 8x12 inch pan. Brown bread crumbs in
butter. Add cream & mix.  Spread on top of the chicken mixture and
bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.
