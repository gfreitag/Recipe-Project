Title: HUMMINGBIRD CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
3 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts B soda
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 c  Oil
3    Eggs, beaten
1 1/2 c  Pineapple (crushed & undrain
1 1/2 c  Bananas
1 c  Nuts
1/2 c  Coconut
Mix all dry ingredients. Mix eggs & oil then stir into dry
ingredients. Add vanilla, pineapple, bananas & nuts. Makes a 3-layer
cake in 9" pans. Bake 350 deg for 30 min. Cool & frost with cream
cheese icing.
