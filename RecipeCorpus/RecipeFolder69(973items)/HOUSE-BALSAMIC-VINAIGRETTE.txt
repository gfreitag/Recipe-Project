Title: HOUSE BALSAMIC VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Salad dres, Chef
Yield: 1 /2 c
MMMMM-------------------OCCASIONAL VEGETARIAN------------------------
1 ts Salt
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 1/2 ts Dijon
1/2 ts Pepper
6 tb Olive oil
1 cl Garlic, sm, minced
1    Scallion, cut 1/8" rounds
2 tb Parsley, chpd
Stir salt into wine and vinegar. Add mustard and stir. Add pepper and
oil; whisk until blended. Add garlic, scallion and parsley.
