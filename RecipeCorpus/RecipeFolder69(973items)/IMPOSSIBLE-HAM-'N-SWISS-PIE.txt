Title: IMPOSSIBLE HAM 'N SWISS PIE
Categories: Casseroles, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
DEBBIE HOLLAND HXRH11B
2 c  Fully cooked ham, cut up
1 c  Swiss cheese, shredded (4oz)
1/3 c  Green onions, chopped
(or regular onions, chopped)
4    Eggs
2 c  Milk
1 c  Bisquick
1/4 ts Salt, if desired
1/8 ts Pepper
Heat oven to 400*.  Grease pie plate, 10x1-1/2 inches. Sprinkle
ham, cheese and onions in plate. Beat remaining ingredients until
smooth, 15 seconds in blender on high or 1 minute with hand beater.
Pour into plate.
Bake until golden brown and knife inserted in center comes out clean,
35-40 minutes.  Cool 5 minutes.
