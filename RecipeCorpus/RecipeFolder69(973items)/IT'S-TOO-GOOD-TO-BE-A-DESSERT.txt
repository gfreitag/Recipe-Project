Title: IT'S TOO GOOD TO BE A DESSERT
Categories: Desserts, Weight watc
Yield: 4 Servings
20 oz Crushed pineapple -- canned
1 1/3 c  Cottage cheese, lowfat
1 c  Whipped topping
1/8 oz Sugar free Jello -- your
Favorite flavor
Mix ingredients together and chill.
