Title: INSTANT PUMPKIN PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
2    3 3/4 oz. packages vanilla
Instant pudding
1 1/3 c  Milk
1 c  16 0z. pumpkin
1 1/2 ts Pumpkin pie spice
4 c  Whipped topping
1    Baked pie shell
Add pudding mix to milk and beat on low speed until blended about 30
sec. Blend in pumpkin, spice and 2 cups of whipped topping. Pour into
pie shell and chill until set at least 4 hours. Garnish with rest of
topping.
