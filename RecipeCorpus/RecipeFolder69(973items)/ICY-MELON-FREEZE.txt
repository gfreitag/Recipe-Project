Title: ICY MELON FREEZE
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Melon, pureed
1/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Orange juice
Combine puree and sugar until sugar dissolves. Add juice and stir.
Place in a freezer container and freeze until partially frozen. Place
in a medium bowl and beat with mixer until fluffy. Replace in
container, cover, freeze until firm. Scoop out and serve on melon
wedge.
