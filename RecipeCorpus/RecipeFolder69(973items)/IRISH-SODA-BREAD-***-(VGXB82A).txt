Title: IRISH SODA BREAD *** (VGXB82A)
Categories: Breadmaker, Ethnic, Fruits, Brunch, Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 tb Butter (I use canola oil)
1/2 c  Raisins (I add a few more)
2 tb Caraway seeds
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Buttermilk
1 1/2 ts Dry yeast
