Title: HSIN-MIN SHRIMP
Categories: Seafood, Fish, Oriental
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Shrimp, raw
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Sherry
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ea Garlic clove, crushed
1/4 c  Peanut oil
Peel and devein shrimp. Mix ingredients well, pour over shrimp and
refrigerate 4 to 6 hours. Remove shrimp and broil about 2 minutes each
side.
