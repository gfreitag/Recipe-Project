Title: HOW TO SET BUCKWHEAT
Categories: Breads, Osg, Info
Yield: 1 Servings  In the evening take 1 qt. lukewarm water; put in crock; add 1 c.
sweet milk and a little salt. Add 1 c. Witch yeast or 1 cake yeast
which has been soaked. Thicken with buckwheat flour, including 1/2 c.
wheat flour. In the morning stir in a little soda and 1/2 tin cup
buttermilk. That evening add sufficient water to make desired amount;
thicken and let stand over night. Next morning thin as before. These
are very good cakes.

Source: Mrs. Pearl Temple, Jefferson Grange, KNox County, OH
