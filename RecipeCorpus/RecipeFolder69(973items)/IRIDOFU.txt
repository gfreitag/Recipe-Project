Title: IRIDOFU
Categories: Vegetarian, Ethnic
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Oil
1 sm Onion, diced
1 sm Carrot, diced
24 oz Tofu, crumbled
2 tb Roasted sesame seeds
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Shoyu
1 ds Pepper
Heat a wok & coat with oil.  Add onion & carrot & saute for 3 minutes
till onion is slightly browned. Add crumbled tofu & the remaining
ingredients. Stirring constantly, saute over medium low heat for a
bout 5 minutes, or until the tofu is light & dry.

Variations:  Add 1/4 c of any of the following: parboiled green peas;
shiitake mushrooms; diced bamboo shoots. Also try adding 1 ts grated
ginger.

Shurtleff & Aoyagi, "The Book of Tofu"
