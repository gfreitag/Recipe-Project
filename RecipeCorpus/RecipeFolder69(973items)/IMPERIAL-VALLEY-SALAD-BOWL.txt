Title: IMPERIAL VALLEY SALAD BOWL
Categories: Salads
1    head Romaine lettuce
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  cheddar cheese, shredded
2    tomatoes
3    green onions
1/2 c  black olives -- chopped
1/2 c  jicama
1/2 c  alfalfa sprouts
1/2 c  beets -- sliced

1. Wash, dry and tear romaine into bite-size pieces. Put into a salad
bowl. Sprinkle half the shredded cheese evenly over greens. 2. Prepare
vegetables: chop tomatoes into bite-size pieces; slice green onions,
using part of the green tops; cut jicama into thin strips; drain
beets very thoroughly and cut into thin strips. 3. Layer all the
vegetables and then black olives and alfalfa sprouts. Sprinkle
remaining cheese over top.

Optional: 1/2 cup drained red kidney beans, chopped hard-boiled egg
and some crisp croutons or broken corn chips placed on top of salad.

: Jo Anne Merrill
