Title: IMPOSSIBLE CHICKEN TACO PIE
Categories: Poultry, Chicken, Bisquick
Yield: 7 Servings
2 c  Diced cooked chicken
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Taco seasoning mix
1 c  Bisquick baking mix
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
Heat oven to 400~.  In a medium bowl mix chicken, onion and seasoning
mix. Arrange in a greased 9" pie plate. in a medium bowl whisk
together

baking mix, milk and eggs until blended; pour into pie plate. Bake
30-35 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake 1-2 minutes longer or until melted. Serve
with lettuce, tomatoes and sour cream if desired.
