Title: IMPOSSIBLE LEMON PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Milk
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Lemon rind, freshly grated
1/2 c  Bisquick
4    Eggs
1/4 c  Butter
Process all ingredients together for 2-3 min. Pour the mixture into a
buttered pie plate.

Bake at 350 deg F for 40-45 min.

Sprinkle 1 Tablespoon of sugar over the baked pie and broil until
browned.

Cool and serve with whipped cream.
