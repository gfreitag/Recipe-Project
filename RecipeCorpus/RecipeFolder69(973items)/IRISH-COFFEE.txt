Title: IRISH COFFEE
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 2 Servings
1 1/2 c  Warm Water
1 tb Instant Coffee Crystals
1/4 c  Irish Whiskey
1 x  Brown Sugar To Taste
1 x  Dessert Topping *
*    Dessert topping should be in a pressurized can.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- In a 2-cup measure combine water and instant
coffee crystals. Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power about 4 minutes
or just till steaming hot. Stir in Irish whiskey and brown sugar.
Serve in mugs. Top each mug of coffee mixture with some pressurized
dessert topping.
