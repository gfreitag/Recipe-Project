Title: ITALIAN BREAD - DG
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
MMMMM-------------------------SMALL LOAF------------------------------
1/2 c  Water
2/3 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 ts Yeast
MMMMM------------------------MEDIUM LOAF-----------------------------
2/3 c  Water
3/4 ts Salt
2/3 ts Sugar
2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
MMMMM-------------------------LARGE LOAF------------------------------
1 c  Water
1 1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
3 c  Bread flour
2 1/2 ts Yeast
Put all ingredients in bread machine. Start white or french bread
cycle.

From Donna German Cookbook 1
