Title: INN CHICKEN
Categories: Casseroles, Chicken, Restaurants
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Chicken breasts, skinless,
-boned
2 cl Garlic; minced
1 c  Sour cream
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Paprika
1 ts Tabasco sauce
1 c  Breadcrumbs
1/2 c  Butter
Butter a baking dish. Combine the garlic, sour cream, lemon juice,
paprika, and tabasco. Marinate the chicken in the mixture OVERNIGHT
in the refrigerator. Roll the chicken in the bread crumbs and place
in the prepared baking dish. Drizzle with the melted butter. Bake in
350 F, 1 hr. Source: Trails End
