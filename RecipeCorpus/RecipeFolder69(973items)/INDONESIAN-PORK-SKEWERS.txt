Title: INDONESIAN PORK SKEWERS
Categories: Pork, Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Boneless Pork, in pieces
16 oz Spiced Crab Apples, drained
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Prepared Mustard
1/4 c  Light Molasses
2 tb Ginger Preserves
1/4 ts Ground Ginger
Combine sauce ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Alternately thread
pork and apples on 6-8 skewers. Brush with sauce.

Place kabobs on unheated rack of broiler pan. Broil 5" from heat 12-15
minutes or until pork is no longer pink, turning kabobs and brushing
with sauce after 6 minutes.
