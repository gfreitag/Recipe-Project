Title: INSTANT HOT CHOCOLATE
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 11 Servings
8 c  Powdered milk
2 1/2 c  Chocolate drink mix -- * see
Note
3/4 c  Powdered non-dairy creamer
1 c  Sifted powdered sugar
*use the powdered instant chocolate drink mix. Mix all ingredients in
a large container.  To serve, add 1/4 cup of mixture to 1 cup boiling
water; top with marshmallows.
