Title: HUMMUS RECIPE
Categories: Digest, August
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Drained rinsed can garbanzo
Beans
3    5 cloves of garlic depending
On how you feel
1/4    To 1/2 cup onion or green or
Red pepper
x  Juice from a whole lemon or
Lime
1    Dribble of sesame oil (leave
Out/in at your own risk!!)
x  Fresh cilantro to taste
x  Another twist = fresh ground
Cumin
place the above in a food processor and blend until nearly smooth

spread in pita pockets heated in micronuker better = place slices of
fresh tomatoes in pitas and fresh ground pepper along with spread of
hummus
