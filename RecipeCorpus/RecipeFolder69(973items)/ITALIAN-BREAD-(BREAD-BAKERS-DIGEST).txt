Title: ITALIAN BREAD (BREAD-BAKERS DIGEST)
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker, Yeast
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 1/3 c  Water
2 tb Oil (olive or other)
3 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Salt
2 ts Yeast
(Keep salt and yeast separate; they can interact if together before
mixing.)  Follow directions for your ABM.
