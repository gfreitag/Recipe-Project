Title: IMPOSSIBLE CUSTARD PIE
Categories: Pies, Pastries
Yield: 1 Servings
In Blender, Combine-----
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Baking Powder
1 pn Salt
3 tb Unsalted Butter -- softened
4 lg Eggs
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Garnish-----
Coconut Flakes
Preheat oven to 400.

Blend for about a minute.  Place in buttered 9 inch pie plate.
Sprinkle with flaked or shredded coconut. Bake until knife inserted 1
inch from edge comes out clean (about 30 minutes)
