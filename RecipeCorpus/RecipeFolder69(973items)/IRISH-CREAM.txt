Title: IRISH CREAM
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 pt Half & Half -or- whipping
-cream
2 tb Hersheys Chocolate Syrup
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Whiskey
1/4 c  Rum
1/4 c  Cream De Cocoa
Mix in blender or with wire whip. Refrigerate or freeze. Serve over
crushed ice.
