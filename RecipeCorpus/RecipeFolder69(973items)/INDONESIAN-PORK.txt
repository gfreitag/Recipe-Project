Title: INDONESIAN PORK
Categories: Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Oil
1 1/2 lb Pork; cut in 1/2 in cubes
3/4 c  Onion; minced
2    Garlic cloves; minced
1/4 ts Dried chili peppers; (ground
1/4 c  Soy sauce; or to taste
2 ts Brown sugar
1 ts Lemon juice
Heat oil in skillet; saute pork, onions, garlic and peppers 10
minutes, stirring frequently.  Add soy sauce, sugar, and lemon juice.
Cook over low heat 10 minutes.
