Title: IRISH CREAM FROSTING
Categories: Frostings, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Butter
2 1/4 c  Confectioners' sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Irish Cream liqueur
In electric mixer owl, cream butter until smooth; beat in
confectioers' sugar gradually, then beat in the salt and the Irish
Cream. Beat the frosting until light and fluffy.
