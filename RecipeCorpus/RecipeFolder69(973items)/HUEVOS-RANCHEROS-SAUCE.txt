Title: HUEVOS RANCHEROS SAUCE
Categories: Mexican
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Onions; chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper; chopped
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 pn Basil
1 pn Garlic powder
Salt and pepper
1 ts Cumin
28 oz Crushed tomatoes
Saute onions and peppers in oil until soft. Add spices and tomatoes.
Continue cooking over low heat until flavors meld. Yield: about 3 cups
Serving suggestions: Can be served over eggs or grits. Pour 1 Tbsp.
sauce over thin pork chops and bake. Pour remaining sauce over chops
when= served.

From: Pepe's Cafe - Key West, FL.
