Title: IDA CULVER'S STRAWBERRY SHORTCAKE
Categories: Desserts, Breads, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM-------------------------SHORTCAKE------------------------------
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg
Milk
MMMMM---------------------------FLUFF--------------------------------
1    Egg white
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Berries, sliced
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Shortcake: sift flour, sugar, baking
powder, and salt. Cut in butter into small pieces. Put egg in a
measuring cup and fill with milk to measure one cup. Beat egg and
milk together and add to flour mixture. Bake in a greased round cake
pan for 20 minutes. Slice fresh strawberry fluff. Fluff:  Put all
ingredients into a bowl Beat at high speed for about 15 minutes until
topping is stiff and fluffy.
