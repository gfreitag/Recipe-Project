Title: INDIAN WELLS LODGE SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Salads
1/2 lb blue cheese -- crumbled
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 ts salt
1 qt Wesson oil
1 TB paprika -- heaping
1 1/2 ts vinegar
2 TB whole oregano

Stir well and serve at room temperature. Makes one quart. From Indian
Wells, California
