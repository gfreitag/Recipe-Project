Title: ITALIAN CAESAR PASTA SALAD
Categories: Appetizers, Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz (3 cups) uncooked pasta
-nuggets (radiatore)
2 c  Shredded romaine lettuce
1/2 c  Sliced marinated sun-dried
-tomatoes
1/3 c  Shredded fresh Parmesan
-cheese
1    (2 1/4 oz.) can sliced ripe
-olives, drained
1    (8 oz.) bottle (3/4 cup)
-Caesar salad dressing
Cooking pasta to desired doneness as directed on package. Drain;
rinse with cold water to cool.

In large bowl, combine cooked pasta and all remaining ingredients; mix
well.  Serve immediately.  6 ( 1 cup ) servings. Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Pillsbury Pasta
