Title: Pineapple Chicken
Categories: Crockpot, Chicken, Low-cal/fat, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM---------------------LIZ JONES (VXRF36B--------------------------
2 tb Mustard; Dijon-style
Soy sauce
1    Garlic clove; minced
3    Chicken breasts *
Pepper
Paprika
20 oz Pineapple  **
* Chicken breasts should be split, skinned and boned. ** Pineapple
should be drained, unsweetened tidbits.
Arrange chicken in crockpot. Sprinkle with pepper and paprika. Mix
soy sauce, pineapple and mustard together; pour over chicken.  Add
minced garlic. Cover and cook on LOW 7 to 9 hours or on HIGH 3 to 4
hours. Makes 6 servings Per serving: 210 calories;  4 g fat;  73 mg
cholesterol; 153 mg sodium. Rival Crock-Pot Slow Cooker Dining Lite.
