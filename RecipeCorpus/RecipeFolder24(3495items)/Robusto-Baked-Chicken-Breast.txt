Title: Robusto Baked Chicken Breast
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea Bell pepper, sliced
1 lg Onion, sliced
-Tony's Chacheres Creole
-seasoning
5 ea Boneless chicken breast
8 oz Robusto italian dressing
-(wishbone)
5 ea Buds of garlic, sliced
Place breast in 13x9 baking dish with a cover. Sprinkle tony's
seasoning on each piece. Slice garlic & place 1 bud on each chicken
breast, Slice onion & bell pepper in thin strips & put over chicken
breast. Pour wishbone dressing over the chicken, cover & place in
refrigerator overnight. Bake with cover on for 45 minutes at 350
degrees.
Note: Any creole / cajun seasoning may be used in place of Tony's.
