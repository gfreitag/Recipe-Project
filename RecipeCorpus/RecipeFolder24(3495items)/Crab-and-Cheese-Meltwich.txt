Title: Crab and Cheese Meltwich
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Cheese/eggs, Sandwich, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Lowfat mayonnaise
1/4 ts Sherry extract
1/2 ea English muffin, toasted
2 oz Crab meat
1 oz Lowfat cheddar cheese
-shredded
Combine crab, cheese, mayonnaise, and sherry extract in topof double
boiler over boiling water. Cook, stirring, until cheese melts and
mixture is hot. Spoon over muffin and serve

19g protein, 10g fat, 20g carb., 503mg sodium, 70mg chol., 238
