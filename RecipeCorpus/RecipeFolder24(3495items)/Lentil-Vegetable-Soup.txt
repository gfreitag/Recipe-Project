Title: Lentil Vegetable Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
1 ea Onion; chopped
3 ea Cl Garlic; minced
3 cn Tomato sauce; (8 oz. cans)
1 c  Brown rice
3 ea Carrots; sliced
2 ea Potatoes; red, diced
1 t  Thyme;dried, crumbled
3 T  Olive oil
1 ea Green bell pepper; chopped
8 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Lentils
1 c  Dry white wine
3 ea Celery; sliced
2 ea Zucchini; sliced
1 t  Basil; dried, crumbled
Heat oil in heavy large saucepan over medium-high heat.  Add
onions, green bepper and garlic and saute until tender, about 5
minutes. Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and simmer until ltentils
and rice are tender and soup is thick, about 1 hour, 15 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper. Recipe from "Too Busy To Cook", Bon
Appetit, January, 1992.
