Title: Southern Chicken Breasts, with Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Chicken, Main dish, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------PREP: 20 MINUTES---------------------------
MMMMM----------------------COOK: 50 MINUTES---------------------------
MMMMM-------------------------SERVES: 4------------------------------
Buttered lima beans or broccoli & homemade biscuits are all you'll
need to complete this down-home dinner. 2 tbsp. vegetable oil 4
chicken breast 1/2's w/ bone 1 cup evaporated milk 2 tbsp. honey 1
tsp. salt 1/4 tsp. nutmeg 1/4 tsp. ground cloves 1 can (16 oz.) sweet
potatoes in syrup, drained, reserving 1/4 cup syrup 1/4 cup raisins
1. In a large frying pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add chicken
breasts & cook until lightly browned, about 5 minutes a side. Drain
on paper towels. 2. Preheat oven to 350o. Arrange chicken in a
13"x9"x2" baking dish. In a small bowl, combine evaporated milk,
honey, salt, nutmeg & cloves. Pour over chicken. 3. Bake 10 minutes.
Remove from oven & arrange sweet potatoes & raisins around chicken.
Pour reserved 1/4 cup syrup over chicken. Return to oven & bake 40
minutes until chicken is tender. Shared by: Liz Norsworthy
