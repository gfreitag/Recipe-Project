Title: Beef Stuffed Peppers
Categories: Ground beef, Main dish, Vegetable, Beef, Rice
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/2 t  Salt
Boiling water
Creole sauce (see recipe)
6 lg Green peppers
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
2 c  Cooked rice
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
Cut tops from peppers; remove membranes and seeds. Cover peppers with
boiling water. Cook 10 minutes, drain. Brown ground beef, onion,
celery and green pepper in skillet. Add salt and rice. Spoon mixture
into peppers. Place in baking dish and pour creole sauce over
peppers. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes, uncover and
bake for 15 minutes longer.
