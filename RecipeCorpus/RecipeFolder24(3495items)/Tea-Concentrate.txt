Title: Tea Concentrate
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 12 servings
5 c  Water
1/4 c  Tea, loose -=OR=-
12    Tea bags
Combine tea and water in a 3- to 4- quart pan.  Bring to a boil
over high heat and boil, uncovered, until liquid is reduced to 4
cups. (About 6 minutes.)
Pour liquid concentrate through a fine strainer into a container and
discard residue.  Cover and chill until cold, at least 3 hours.
Will keep for up to 2 weeks.
To use, dilute by the pitcher or glass.  The optimum dilution ratio
for pouring over ice is about 1 part tea concnetrate to 2 parts water
and/or juice.
Source: Sunset Magazine, August 1994
Date: 09-14-94
