Title: Spicy Jalapeno Spread
Categories: Appetizer, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Green onion; chopped
1 ts Garlic salt
4 c  Cheddar cheese; grated
1    Medium onion; finely chopped
2    Cloves garlic; minced
6    Jalapeno peppers, chopped
In a large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients. Stir until well
blended. Refrigerate overnight to allow flavors to blend before
serving.
