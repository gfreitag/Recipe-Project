Title: Turkey Pancakes
Categories: Turkey, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Onion
1/2 ts Mace
2 c  Cubed, cooked turkey
1/4 ts Nutmeg
ds Cayenne
1 c  Sifted flour
3 tb Butter
2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
Sift flour, baking powder, salt, mace, nutmeg, thyme, and cayenne
together in bowl. Stir in melted butter and milk. Add grated onion
and turkey. Beat until smooth. Heat a greased skillet. Drop by
spoonfuls and brown on both sides. Makes 8 to 10 pancakes. Good
topped with cranberry sauce.
