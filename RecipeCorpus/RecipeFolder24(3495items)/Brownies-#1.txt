Title: Brownies #1
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 16 brownies
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/3 c  Butter
2 oz Unsweetened baking chocolate
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs, well-beaten
2/3 c  Sifted flour
Preheat oven to 350oF. Melt butter and chocolate together in top of
double boiler over hot water. In large mixing bowl, gradually add
sugar to eggs; beat thoroughly. Add melted chocolate mixture; blend
well.

Sift flour, baking powder and salt together into separate bowl; stir
into chocolate mixture. Add nuts and vanilla; mix well. Spread
mixture in greased 8" square baking pan. Bake for 25-30 minutes. Cool
in pan, then cut into squares.

Source: Marian Hoffman, ed., Creative Cooking - Desserts. Typed by
Linda Howard.
