Title: Marble Brownies
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 16 brownies
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
2 oz Unsweetened baking chocolate
-melted & cooled
1/4 c  Butter, softened
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
2/3 c  Cake flour
1/4 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 350oF. Cream butter in bowl. Add sugar; beat until
light and fluffy. Add eggs; beat until mixture is smooth. Sift flour
and salt together in separate bowl; add to egg mixture. Stir in nuts
and vanilla.

Pour half the batter into greased 8" square baking pan. Mix chocolate
with remaining batter; pour over plain batter. Swirl through with
spoon. Bake for 30 minutes. Cool in pan, then cut into squares.

Source: Marian Hoffman, ed., Creative Cooking - Desserts. Typed by
Linda Howard.
