Title: Coconut Parfaits
Categories: Dessert, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Skim milk
1/2 c  Pistachios chopped
1/2 c  Coconut, shredded
2 md Bananas sliced
1 pk Vanilla pudding instant
In small sauce pan, toast coconut, tossing often. cool. Slice bananas,
prepare pudding as to directions, but use low fat milk. into 4 parfait
glasses, spoon half pudding, top with bananas, half coconut, remaining
pudding, top with pistachios and coconut.  1 serving. 275 calories, 8
g fat, 5 mg cholesterol, 460 mg sodium.
