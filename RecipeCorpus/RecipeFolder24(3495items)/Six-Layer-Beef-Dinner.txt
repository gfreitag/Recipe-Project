Title: Six Layer Beef Dinner
Categories: Beef, Casserole, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
3/4 c  Uncooked rice
1 lb Ground beef
1    Tomato sauce can of water
3    Peeled, sliced, raw potatoes
1/2 c  Chopped onion
3    Sliced raw carrots
8 oz Can tomato sauce
Take a large rectangular, butter baking dish, then layer all the
ingredients in the order listed. Cover and bake at 1-1/2 hours at 350
F. degrees.
Date: 09-26-94
From: Earl Shelsby
