Title: Chicken Peanut Curry
Categories: Chicken, Curries, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 T  Sugar
1/2 c  Crunchy peanut butter
1 ea Place chicken in baking
-dish,Brush with a
-tablespoon of oil,and bake
-in
Serves four.
8 ea Chicken thigh cutlets
1/4 c  Oil
1/3 c  Red curry paste
400 cn Coconut milk
1/4 c  Fish sauce
moderately hot oven for about 45 minutes or until chicken is tender,
drain on absorbent paper. 2. Heat remaining oil in medium
saucepan,add curry paste,stir over medium heat for 1 minute. 3. Stir
in coconut milk,sauce,sugar and peanut butter; stir over medium heat
until mixture boils and thickens. 4. Add chicken to coconut mixture,
stir over heat for about 5 minutes until mixture is hot.
