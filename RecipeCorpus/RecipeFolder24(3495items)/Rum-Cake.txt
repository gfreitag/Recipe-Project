Title: Rum Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Lemon Juice
1/4 ts Lemon Rind, grated
Raspberry Preserves
Rum
Whipped Cream
3/4 c  Butter, softened
2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs, separated
2 1/3 c  Cake Flour, sifted
2 ts Baking Powder
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Milk
Cream butter and add sugar gradually until fluffy. Beat yolks until
lemony. Blend into butter mixture. Sift dry ingredients together.
Alternately add dry ingredients and milk to butter mixture a little
at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in vanilla, lemon
juice and rind. Beat whites until stiff but not dry. Fold into
batter. Pour into a greased 8x12 or 9x13 pan. Bake in a preheated
350oF oven for 40 minutes. Cool, cut in half crosswise, spread thin
layer of preserves on one half, top with the other half, moisten with
rum, let stand awhile, cut into squares and tope with whipped cream.
Happy Baking, Marie
