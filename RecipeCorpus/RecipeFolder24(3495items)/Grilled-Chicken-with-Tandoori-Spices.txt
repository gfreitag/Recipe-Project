Title: Grilled Chicken with Tandoori Spices
Categories: Poultry, Down under
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt or to taste
1/2 ts Ground Cumin
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground Coriander
1/4 ts Tumeric
1/4 ts Cardamon
ds Ground Nutmeg
1    Broiler-fryer Chicken, cut
Into 8 pieces
3/4 c  Plain Yogurt
1 tb Lime Juice
1 tb Vegetable Oil
1 lg Garlic Clove, minced
1 1/2 ts Ginger, minced
Wash and dry the chicken pieces and place them in a glass or ceramic
bowl. Mix the remaining ingredients and pour the marinade over the
chicken. Let marinate for 6 hours. Remove the chicken from the
marinade and grill the pieces about 4 to 6 inches from the coals for
about 30 to 45 minutes or until all pieces have cooked through. Turn
chicken occasionally during the grilling process.
Date: 09-18-94
From: Joell Abbott
