Title: Neeps
Categories: Vegetable, Holiday, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
Butter
Salt and pepper
1 lg Turnip, peeled, and diced
Water
Peel and dice turnip.  Boil until tender.  Drain off water.  Mash
like you would for mashed potatoes.  When mashed, add a bit of
butter, and salt and pepper to taste.  For a more colorful dish, use
the yellow turnips. Origin: Sharon Stevens Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
Oct/94.
