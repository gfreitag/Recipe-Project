Title: Amazingly Quick Caramel Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Chocolate, Dessert, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 servings
No Ingredients Found
Microwave 1 bag (14oz) caramels and 1/4 cup milk in glass bowl on
high for 2 minutes, stir until smooth.  Pour onto bottom of prepared
9 inch graham wafer pie crust; sprinkle with 1 cup toasted, chopped
pecans. Refrigerate for 5 minutes.  Microwave 8 quares semi sweet
chocolate and 1/3 cup milk in glass bowl on medium for 2 minutes.
Stir until smooth.

Pour over caramel nut filling; spreading chocolate mixture to edge of
pie. Garnish with additional nuts.  Great make ahead recipe.  Makes 8
servings. Origin: Kraft What's Cooking magazine, 9th issue. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
