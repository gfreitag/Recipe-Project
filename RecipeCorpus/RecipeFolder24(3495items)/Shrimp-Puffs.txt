Title: Shrimp Puffs
Categories: Appetizer, Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 24 puffs
2 t  Dijon mustard
1 T  Green onion; sliced
24    Dill sprigs, fresh
1 pk Miniature patty shells (24)
- frozen
1 pk Shrimp, tiny, cooked, frozen
2/3 c  Sour cream
Bake patty shells - cool completely.

Thaw shrimp and reserve 24 - set aside.

Chop remaining shrimp.  In small mixing bowl, stir together the
chopped shrimp, sour cream, mustard and green onion.

Spoon 1 scant teaspoon shrimp mixture into each patty.  Garnish with
remaining shrimp and small sprigs of fresh dill.

Serve immediately.
