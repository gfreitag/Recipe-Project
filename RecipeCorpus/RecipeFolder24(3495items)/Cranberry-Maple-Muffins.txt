Title: Cranberry Maple Muffins
Categories: Bread, Fruit, Muffin
Yield: 18 servings
1/3 c  Sugar
1 T  Baking powder
1/4 ts Allspice, ground
1/2 c  Maple syrup
1/2 c  Milk
per serving: 127 Fat grams per serving: 4 Approx. Cook
1 c  Cranberries; chopped
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
6 T  Margarine; softened
1    Egg
Calories
Time: 0:20

Bring milk to room temperature.  Preheat oven to 400 F.  In a bowl,
combine cranberries and sugar.  Set aside four one hour at room
temper- ature, stirring occasionally.  Sift together the dry
ingredients and the spices. In another bowl, beat together the
butter, syrup, egg, and milk until smooth.  Add the cranberry
mixture. Quickly fold in the dry ingre- dients, until just moistened.
Spoon into greased 2" muffin pans.  Bake 15 to 20 minutes.  Remove
from pans and serve warm. Submitted By SAM WARING   On   10-07-94
