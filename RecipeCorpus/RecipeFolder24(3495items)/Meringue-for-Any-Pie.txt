Title: Meringue for Any Pie
Categories: 1941, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
Sugar
Few drops of vanilla
2    Egg whites
Few grains salt
4 tb Finely granulated or fruit
Directions Beat egg whites, but not dry with salt. Gradually beat in
sugar or fruit sugar, beat after each addition of sugar until the
mixture stands up in peaks. Flavour with vanilla. Pile meringue
lightly onto pie, swirling to give deep crests and hollows. Brown in
a very slow oven, ( 275 to 300 degreesF.) Use smaller amount of sugar
for a pie with very sweet filling, and the larger amount when the
