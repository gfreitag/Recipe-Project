Title: Garlic Broiled Fillets
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 cl Garlic, sliced
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 lb Scrod or orange roughy
-fillets
2 tb Plus (see next line)
2 ts Olive oil
Preheat broiler. Spray broiler pan with nonstick cooking spray. In
blender, combine remaining ingredients, blend until smooth. Spread
half of mixture evenly over fish. Broil 5 inches from heat for 8-10
minutes. Turn fish carefully, spread with remaining garlic mixture
and broil another 8-10 minutes or until done.

Per serving: 30g protein, 10g fat, 1g carb., 229mg sodium, 73mg
chol., 223 calories.
