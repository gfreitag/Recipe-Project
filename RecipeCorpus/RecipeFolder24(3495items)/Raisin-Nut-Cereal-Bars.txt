Title: Raisin Nut Cereal Bars
Categories: Bread, Dessert, Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 24 bars
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
5 1/2 c  Raisin Nut Bran cereal
1/4 c  Margarine or butter
3 c  Miniature marshmallows
Butter square pan, 9x9x2 inches.  Heat margarine, marshmallows and
cinnamon in 3-quart saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly,
until smooth; remove from heat.  Stir in cereal until thoroughly
coated. Pat into pan; cool.  Cut into 2-1/4 x 1-1/2-inch bars.  Makes
24 bars.

From the back of a box of Raisin Nut Bran cereal. APB
Date: 09-17-94
From: Alan Burgstahler
