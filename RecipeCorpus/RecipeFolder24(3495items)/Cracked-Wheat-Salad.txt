Title: Cracked Wheat Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
2 lg Peppers
2 lg Onions
1 lg Bunch of parsley
2 c  Of Cracked Wheat
2 lg Cucumbers
4 lg Tomatoes
Soak the wheat berries in about 2 cups of water or more and let soak
overnight. Dice vegetables as small as possible. Squeeze water from
the wheat and mix with the vegetables. Season with Salt, pepper, oil
and lemon juice to taste.
