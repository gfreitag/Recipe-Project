Title: Golden Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Vegetarian, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
3    Eggs
3    Tomatoes,sliced
1/2 t  Parsley flakes
2 c  Cheddar cheese,shredded
Paprika
1 c  Milk
1 t  Salt
1/2 c  Rice cereal
1/4 t  Mustard,powdered
2 T  Butter or margarine
1. Combine milk and salt in a large saucepan; bring to a rapid boil.

2. Slowly stir in cereal, stirring constantly until mixture thickens.

3. Stir in mustard and butter.

4. Add eggs, one at a time, beating until well blended.

5. Spread cereal mixture in a greased 9-inch-square baking pan;
arrange tomato slices on top.

6. Sprinkle tomatoes with parsley, cheese and paprika.

7. Bake in preheated 350'F. oven 20 minutes, or until cheese melts.
