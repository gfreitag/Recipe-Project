Title: Quick Curry Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Curries
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-------------------------SPICE MIX------------------------------
4 ts Paprika
1 ts Garam masala
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Chili powder
4 ts Ground coriander
2 ts Ground cumin
2 ts Turmeric
MMMMM---------------------OTHER INGREDIENTS--------------------------
1 ts Sugar
Salt to taste
Pre-cooked meat, etc
6 tb Vegetable oil
Milk
1 cn (14 oz) tomatoes; strained
1. Heat oil on low heat.

2. Mix spices with a little milk, into a paste.

3. Add this paste to the oil and fry gently for a few minutes,
stirring once or twice. Remove from heat and cool a bit.

4. Liquidize the tomatoes in a blender. Stir this blend into the fried
paste. Return to heat. Add sugar and enough milk for required
consistency. Stir continuously.

5. If the sauce is a little too thick, add a little more milk.

6. Add pre-cooked meats, chicken, vegetables or frozen seafood and
simmer until you are ready. Always add more milk if it gets too thick.
