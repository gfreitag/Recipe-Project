Title: Passover Honey Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Jewish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Ginger
1 3/4 c  Matzo meal
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Coffee; instant
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Cloves
1 c  Honey
1/4 c  Orange juice
Grated rind of 1 orange
3    Eggs, separated
1 ts Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Sugar
Heat honey to boil; add orange juice and rind. Cool. Beat egg yolks.
Add oil and sugar, sift together matzo meal and dry ingredients. Add
dry ingredients and honey to egg yolks. Fold in stiffly beaten egg
whites. Bake in one oblong or 2 loaf pans. Line pan with wax paper.
Bake @ 300 degrees for 50 minutes.
