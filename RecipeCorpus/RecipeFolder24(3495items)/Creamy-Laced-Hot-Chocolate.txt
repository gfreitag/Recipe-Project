Title: Creamy Laced Hot Chocolate
Categories: Beverage, Chocolate
Yield: 3 cups
3/4 c  Marshmallow cream
1/2 c  Amaretto
3 tb Sweet ground choc. and cocoa
3 tb Cocoa, unsweetened
2 c  Milk
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
Marshmallows
Whipped cream
Chocolate shavings
Combine chocolate and cocoa and unsweetened cocoa in a small
saucepan; stir well.  Stir in milk; add marshmallow cream. Bring to a
boil over medium-high heat, stirring constantly with a wire whisk
until smooth. Remove from heat; stir in Amaretto.  Pour into mugs;
serve with desired toppings.
