Title: English Fruitcake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, British
Yield: 1 servings
3 1/4 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Sliced candied orange peel
1/4 c  Sliced candied lemon peel
1/4 c  Sliced citron
1/4 c  Sliced candied cherries
1 c  Chopped walnut meats
1 c  Fruit juice
5 1/2 c  Sifted cake flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Mace
1 c  Shortening
1 7/8 c  Brown sugar
3    Eggs, well beaten
1 ts Lemon extract
Sift flour, baking powder and mace together 3 times.  Cream
shortening and sugar together until fluffy.  Add eggs and beat well.
Add lemon extract, fruit and nuts and mix well.  Add dry ingredients
and fruit juice alternately in small amounts, beating thoroughly
after each addition. Pour into tube pan lined with greased brown
paper. Bake in a 300 degree oven for between 2 and 2 1/2 hours. Test
with a toothpick or your cake tester before removing from the oven.
