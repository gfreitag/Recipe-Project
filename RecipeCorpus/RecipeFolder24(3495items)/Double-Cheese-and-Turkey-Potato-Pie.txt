Title: Double Cheese and Turkey Potato Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Turkey, Cheese/eggs, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------PREP TIME: 15 MINUTES------------------------
MMMMM-------------------COOK TIME: 20 MINUTES------------------------
-thawed, drained
1/2 t  Black pepper
1 1/2 c  Grated cheddar cheese,
-divided
3 c  Hot mashed potatoes
4 ea Single sliced cheese, cut
-into strips
1 c  Fresh mushroom, sliced
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 T  Butter or margarine
10 ea Ounce can cream of mushroom
-soup
2 c  Chopped cooked turkey
1 c  Frozen mixed vegetables,
Combine mushrooms, onion and butter in 2 quart microwaveable casserole
dish. Microwave on high for 2 minutes.  Add soup, turkey, mixed
vegetables, pepper and grated cheese to casserole.  Microwave on high
5-6 minutes until mixture in hot. Spoon potatoes over turkey mixture.
Bake 20 minutes at 350F.  Place cheese strips on top of potatoes (in
criss cross pattern) and bake an additional 10 minutes. Origin: Kraft
Cheese Celebration: Recipe Ideas for Thanksgiving. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Sept/94.
