Title: Braised Leeks
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Chicken broth
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Canola oil
4 lb Leeks, trimmed, washed, and
-quartered lengthwise
In a medium skillet with a lid, heat theoil over medium heat. Add the
leeks and chicken broth and cover. Braise for 10-15 minutes, or until
the leeks are tender, stirring occasionally. Remove the lid, cand
ciik for 10-12 minutes, until the broth has almost evaporated.
Increase the heat to high and sprinkle the sugar over the leeks. Cook
10 minutes more, stirring frequently, until the leeks are lightly
carmelized. Serve immediately.

Per serving: 100 calories, 20g carb., 2g protein, 0mg chol., 1.7g/15%
fat
