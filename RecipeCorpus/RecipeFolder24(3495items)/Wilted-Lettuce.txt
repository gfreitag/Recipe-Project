Title: Wilted Lettuce
Categories: Salad
Yield: 3 servings
12 oz Milnot
Onion
1 lg Head of lettuce
2    Eggs
Vinegar
Tear lettuce up into a bowl, add onion.  Beat 2 eggs well; add a can
of Milnot and 1 1/2 tb of vinegar.  Put im a small pan on the stove;
add 2 tb of bacon drippings.  When hot, add the egg mixture.  Cook
until egg mixture thickens.  Pour over lettuce.  Yum! Randy Rigg
