Title: Lightning Mop
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------GOOD ON TURKEY BREAST------------------------
1/4 c  Minced pickled jalepenos
1/4 c  Jalepeno jelly
Makes about 4 cups.
3 c  Chicken or turkey stock
1/2 c  Oil, preferably canola or
-corn
Combine the ingredients in a saucepan.  Heat the mop and use it warm.
