Title: Incredible Shredibles
Categories: Sandwich, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
MMMMM----------------1/2 CUP EACH: SHREDDED FRESH---------------------
4 sl Process cheese, cut in half
1/4 c  Shredded radishes
2 ea Pita breads, halved
Mix salad dressing and vegetables.  Fill pita halves with vegetable
mixture and cheese slices.  Makes 4 halved pitas. 229 cal, 7.25 pro,
22.7 carbo, 12.2 fat, excellent source of Vit A. Origin: Kraft What's
Cooking magazine, 9th issue Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
