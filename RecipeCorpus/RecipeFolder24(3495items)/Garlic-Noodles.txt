Title: Garlic Noodles
Categories: Vegetable, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
3 lg Cloves garlic, minced
3 c  Cooked noodles
2 tb Olive oil
Cook noodles just before making. Keep warm. Saute garlic in oil to
release oils. Toss with noodles.

Per serving: 3g protein, 6g fat, 19g carb., 2mg sodium, 25mg chol.,
142 calories.
