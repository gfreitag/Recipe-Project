Title: Beefy Lasagna
Categories: Main dish, Burgers, Pasta, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 8 servings
3 tb Tomato paste
1/2 c  Wine, dryred
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Cheese, Ricotta
6    Lasagna noodles; cooked and
-drained
16 oz Cheese, Mozzarella; shredded
1 lb Ground beef
4 sl Bacon; finely diced
3/4 c  Onion; chopped
2 lg Garlic cloves; minced
2    Celery stalks; chopped
1    Carrot; peeled and chopped
2 tb Parsley, fresh; minced
8 oz Tomato sauce
1 c  Tomato puree
Combine beef, bacon, onion, and garlic in a 10" skillet; brown meat
well over medium heat.  Drain.

Combine beef mixture, celry,carrot, parsley, tomato sauce, tomato
puree, tomato paste, wine, salt, and pepper in skillet; heat to
boiling. Cover; reduceheat and simmer 40 to 50 minutes, sitrring
occasionally.

Layer half ofmeat sauce, ricotta cheese, noodles, and mozzarella in a
lightly greased 12x8x2" glass baking dish.  Repeat layers.  Bake at
375 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
