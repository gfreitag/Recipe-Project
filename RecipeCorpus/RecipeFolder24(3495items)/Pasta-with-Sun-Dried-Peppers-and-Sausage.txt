Title: Pasta with Sun Dried Peppers and Sausage
Categories: Pasta, Sausage
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Italian sausage, casing
-removed
1 lb Bow tie pasta or penne
Freshly grated Parmesan
-Cheese
1 ea 6 ounce container marinated
-sun dried peppers
2 ea Cloves fresh garlic, chopped
1 lg Onion, chopped
1 ea 28 ounce can crushed
-tomatoes
Crumble sausage into a 6 quart saucepan.  Stir on medium heat until
brown (about 12 minutes).  Drain fat.  Remove marinated sun-dried
peppers from container and reserve the marinade.  Chop peppers into
small chunks or strips and add to sausage, along with chopped garlic
and onion. Cook on medium-high heat stirring continuously, about 8
minutes. Add reserved marinade from the peppers and the can of
crushed tomatoes. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat.  Simmer for 10
minutes, stirring occasionally. Cook pasta al dente in 4 quarts of
boiling water.  Drain cooked pasta. Place on a serving platter.  Pour
sauce over pasta.  Add Parmesan cheese to taste. Serves 4 to 6. From:
Giuseppe Pomodori Sun Dried Tomatoes and Sun Dried Peppers Asbury
