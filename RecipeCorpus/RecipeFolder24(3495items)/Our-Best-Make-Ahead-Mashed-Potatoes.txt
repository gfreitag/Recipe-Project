Title: Our Best Make Ahead Mashed Potatoes
Categories: Potato, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
1/2 t  Black pepper
6 md Sized potatoes, peeled and
-quartered
1 c  Cheez whiz processed cheese
-spread
Place potatoes in saucepan and cover with water.  bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium and 25-30 minutes until tender. Drain.  Mash
potatoes with hand masher or electric mixer.  stir in remaining
ingredients. Refrigerate until just before serving time.  Ro reheat,
microwave at high for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Makes 6
servings. Origin: Kraft Cheese Celebration: Recipe Ideas for
Thanksgiving. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
