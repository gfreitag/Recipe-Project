Title: Steamed Vegetables with Parmesan Cheese - Country Living
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
-strips
1 md Zucchini, cut lengthwise
-into shoestring strips
1 t  Olive oil
1/4 t  Salt
2 T  Coarsely shredded Parmesan
-cheese
2 md Carrots, peeled & cut
-lengthwise into eighths
1/4 lb Green beans, stem ends &
-strings removed, if present
1 md Sweet red pepper, sliced
-lengthwise
1 md Yellow squash, cut
-lengthwise into shoestring
1. In steamer or steamer basket placed in 5-quart kettle, steam
carrots and green beans, covered, over boiling water 5 minutes. Add
pepper, yellow squash, and zucchini. Cover and steam 3 to 5 minutes
longer or until crisp-tender.

2. Remove vegetables to serving dish; toss with olive oil and salt.
Top with cheese and serve.
