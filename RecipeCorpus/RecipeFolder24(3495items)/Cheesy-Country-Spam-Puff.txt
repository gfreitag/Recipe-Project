Title: Cheesy Country Spam Puff
Categories: Main dish, Spam, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(12 oz)
2 c  Shredded sharp Cheddar
-cheese, divided
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Shredded Monterey Jack
-cheese
6 sl White bread, torn into small
-pieces
1 1/4 c  Milk
3    Eggs
1 tb Spicy mustard
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1/2 t  Paprika
Heat oven to 375'F. In large mixing bowl, combine bread, milk, eggs,
mustard, garlic powder, and paprika. With electric mixer, beat at
medium speed 1 minute or until smooth. Stir in SPAM, 1 cup shredded
Cheddar cheese, and onion. Pour into greased 12x8" baking dish. Bake
25 minutes. Top with remaining Cheddar cheese and Monterey Jack
cheese. Bake 5 minutes longer or until cheese is melted. Let stand 10
minutes before serving.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 434; Protein 28 grams;
Carbohydrate 19 grams; Fat 28 grams; Cholesterol 203 milligrams;
Sodium 1077 milligrams.
