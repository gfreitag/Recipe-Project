Title: Lemon Ginger Tea Sparkler
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Ginger, crystallized; minced
2 2/3 c  Ginger ale
1 1/3 c  Tea concentrate (qv)
1    Lemon; thinly sliced
Combiine tea concentrates, ginger and half the lemon slices in a
pan and bring to boiling.  Cover and chill until cold, at least 1
hour. Pour through a fine strainer into a pitcher and add ginger ale.
Discard used lemon slices and ginger.
Pour into glasses over ice, garnish with remaining lemon slices.
(And/or put some in the pitcher.)
Source: Sunset Magazine, August 1994
Date: 09-14-94
From: Karl Lembke
