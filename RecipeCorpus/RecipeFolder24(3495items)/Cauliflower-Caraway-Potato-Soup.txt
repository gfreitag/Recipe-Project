Title: Cauliflower Caraway Potato Soup
Categories: Vegetable, Soup/stew, Potato
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
-greens  Salt and pepper to
-taste
g  Parmesan to taste
1/2 lb Boiling potatoes
1/4 c  Chopped scallion
3 1/2 c  Water
1/3 c  Thinly sliced scallion
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
2 c  1-inch cauliflower florets
1/2 c  Chopped cauliflower leaves
1/4 t  Caraway seeds
In a saucepan combine the potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch
pieces, the cauliflower leaves, the white part of scallion, the
caraway seeds, and 3 1/2 cups water, simmer the mixture for 15
minutes, or until the potatoes are very tender, and in a blender or
food process puree the mixture coarse. In the pan, combine the puree
and the florets, simmer the soup for 5 minutes, or until the florets
are tender, and stir in the scallion greens and salt and pepper to
taste. Serve the soup sprinkled with the Parmesan. Serves 2.
