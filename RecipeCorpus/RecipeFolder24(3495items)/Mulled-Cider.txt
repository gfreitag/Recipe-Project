Title: Mulled Cider
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Lemon juice
8 x  Cloves
2 qt Apple cider
1 c  Light brown sugar, packed
8 x  Cinnamon sticks
In a large saucepan combine all ingredients.  Bring to a boil; reduce
heat, simmer incovered 10 minutes.  Remove spices.  Serve hot or
cold. Makes two quarts. Note: New Englanders have been known to add
Rum on cold winter days.
