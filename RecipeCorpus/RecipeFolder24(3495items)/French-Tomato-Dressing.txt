Title: French Tomato Dressing
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dressing, Salad
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/4 ts Dried basil
1 ts Sugar
1/2 c  Tomato juice
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Lowfat mayonnaise
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Combine all ingredients, mix well, chill.

Per serving: 0g protein, 6g fat, 2g carb., 230mg sodium, 2mg chol., 62
calories.

From: Carolyn Shaw
