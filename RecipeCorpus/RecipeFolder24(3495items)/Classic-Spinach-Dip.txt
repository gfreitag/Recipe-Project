Title: Classic Spinach Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Dip, Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------PREP TIME: 5 MINUTES PLUS CH---------------------
-mix
2 ea Tbps chopped green onion
2/3 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1/2 c  Chopped water chestnuts
3/4 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
1 pk Frozen spinach, thawed,
-well drained and chopped
1 ea 9g pkg dry vegetable soup
Mix together ingredients until well blended.  Refrigerate.  Serve with
assorted crackers, vegetables or toast cut outs.  Makes 4 cups.
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%% %%%%% TOAST CUT OUTS %%%%%
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%% Toast bread by baking slices on a cookie
sheet in a 350F oven for 10 minutes. Press a cookie cutter onto bread
slice for cut out.  (Can be made ahead and frozen in plastic bags.)
