Title: Diced Winter Melon Soup B1
Categories: Chinese, Fruit, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
4    WATER CHESTNUTS, DICED
SALT
MSG
5 c  SOUP STOCK OR CHICKEN
-BROTH
1/2 lb WINTER MELON, DICED
1/4 c  CHICKEN, DICED
Place stock or broth in a pot. Cover & bring to a boil. Add winter
melon, chicken & water chestnuts. Simmer 10 min. Season to taste w/
salt and MSG. Temperature(s): HOT Effort: EASY Time: 00:15 Source:
KAN'S Comments: GRAND ST., SAN FRANCISCO Comments: WINE: WAN FU WHITE
