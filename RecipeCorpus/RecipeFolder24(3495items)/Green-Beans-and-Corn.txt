Title: Green Beans and Corn
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Cumin
1/2 ts Pepper
4 c  Green beans, snipped
4 c  Frozen corn,
2/3 c  Milk, skim
cut green beans into 1 inch lengths.  Combine beans with remaining
ingredients, and bring to a boil. Cook, uncovered, over high heat 5-7
minutes until milk is almost evaporated.
