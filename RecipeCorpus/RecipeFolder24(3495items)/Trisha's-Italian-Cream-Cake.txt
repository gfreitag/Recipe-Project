Title: Trisha's Italian Cream Cake
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Italian
Yield: 1 cake
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Vanilla
7 oz Coconut
1 c  Pecans, chopped
1/2 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Shortening
2 c  Sugar
5    Eggs, separated
2 c  Flour
1 ts Soda
MMMMM-------------------CREAM CHEESE FROSTING------------------------
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Pecans
1 pk Cream cheese (8-oz) softened
1/2 c  Margarine
1 lb Powdered sugar
CAKE:

Cream margarine and shortening with sugar.  Add egg yolks.  combine
flour, soda and salt and add alternately with buttermilk.  Add
vanilla, coconut and pecans.  Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites.
Grease and flour 3 8: cak pans.  Bake at 350 for 25 minutes.

FROSTING:

Cream cheese and margarine.  Add remaining ingredients.  Double
ingredients if you like a lot of frosting.
