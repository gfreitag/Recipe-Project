Title: Tuna Noodle Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Seafood, Pasta
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Cooked peas, drained
1/4 lb Grated cheese
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn French fried onions
Dash of pepper
1 c  Tuna, drained
1/2 pk Noodles, cooked, rinsed, and
Drained
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Blend soup with milk.  Combine all other
ingredients, using 1/2 of the cheese and 1/2 of the onions.  Pour into
greased 1/2 quart casserole dish.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on top.
Bake for 15 minutes.  Add other 1/2 can of fried onions and bake for
5 minutes. Randy Rigg
