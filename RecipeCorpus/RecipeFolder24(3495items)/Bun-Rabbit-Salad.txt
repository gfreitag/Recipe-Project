Title: Bun Rabbit Salad
Categories: Fruit, Kids, Salad
Yield: 4 servings
Raisens
Coconut; flaked
Carrot
4    Pear halves
4 oz Cream cheese; softened
1 tb Milk
Mix milk and cream cheese together.  Put pear halves cut side down on
lettuce covered plate.  "Frost" with cream cheese.  Use raisens for
eyes, thin slices of carrot for ears, and coconut for fur.
Date: 09-28-94
From: Joan Mershon
