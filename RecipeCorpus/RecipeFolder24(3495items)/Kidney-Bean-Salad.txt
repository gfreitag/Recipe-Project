Title: Kidney Bean Salad
Categories: Diabetic, Salad, Vegetable, Beans
Yield: 4 sweet ones
1 ts Dill pickle; grated
2 ts Onion; grated
1    Egg white; hard-cooked
-chopped finely
Romaine Lettuce leaves;
1/4 c  Anchovy Yogurt Dressing;
16 oz (1 can) kidney beans;
-rinsed well
-=OR=-
16 oz (1 can) pinto beans; rinsed
-well
1    Celery stalk; diced
Mix first 5 ingredients together lightly.  Arrange on lettuce leaves
and top with a spoonful of dressing Food Exchanges per serving: 1
STARCH/BREAD EXCHANGE + 1/2 FAT EXCHANGE CAL: 99; CHO: 2mg; CAR: 15g;
PRO: 5g; SOD: 200mg; FAT: 3g;
