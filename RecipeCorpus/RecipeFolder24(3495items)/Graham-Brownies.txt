Title: Graham Brownies
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 16 brownies
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
2 1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
14 oz Can condensed milk
1 ts Cinnamon
Preheat oven to 375oF. Melt chocolate in top of double boiler over hot
water. Remove from heat and cool slightly. In mixing bowl, combine
melted chocolate, graham cracker crumbs, condensed milk, cinnamon,
salt, vanilla and nuts.

Line an 8" square pan with waxed paper; spread batter in pan. Bake
for 25 minutes. Cool in pan, then cut into squares.

Source: Marian Hoffman, ed., Creative Cooking - Desserts. Typed by
Linda Howard.
