Title: Caramel Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 t  Salt
2/3 c  Evaporated milk
2 t  Caramel flavor
2/3 c  White corn syrup
1 1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Butter
Combine first four ingredients in a heavy saucepan.  Cook over medium
heat, stirrin occasionally, until mixture comes to a boil and all of
the sugar is dissolved.  REmove from heat and cool slightly just
until mixture begins to thicken.  Slowly whisk in evaporated milk
until blended. Stir in Caramel flavor; mix well.  Pour into desired
containers. Keep refrigerated. Makes 2 cups, 1 tbsp per serving.
Serve with ice cream, cake, or apple and pear wedges.  Can be served
hot or cold. To serve hot, reheat in top of double boiler or in
microwave. Refrigerate any leftovers. Origin: Watkins Food Gifts
Cookbook Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
