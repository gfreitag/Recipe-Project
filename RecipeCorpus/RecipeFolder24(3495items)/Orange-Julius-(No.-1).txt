Title: Orange Julius (No. 1)
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 4 servings
3 T  Milk
1/4 c  Crushed ice or orange
-sherbet
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Soda water
1    Egg, well-beaten
Put orange juice, soda water, egg, milk and crushed ice or sherbet in
a blender. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy. Serve at once.
