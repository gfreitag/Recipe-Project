Title: Sweet and Spicy Turkey Loaf
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Turkey, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Parsley flakes
1 tb Sweet pickle relish
3 tb Ketchup
1 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 c  Finely chopped onions
1 lb Ground turkey
Salt to taste
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1/16 ts Ground cloves
1/8 ts Garlic powder
Preheat oven to 350. Combine ingredients, shape into loaf and place
on a rack in a shallow baking pan. Bake, uncovered, 1 hour.

Per serving: 21g protein, 12g fat, 6g carb., 285mg sodium, 76mg
chol., 212 calories.
