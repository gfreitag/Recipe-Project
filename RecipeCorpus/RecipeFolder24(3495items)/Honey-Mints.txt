Title: Honey Mints
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 servings
4 dr Oil of peppermint
Green food coloring
1 c  Warm honey
2 3/4 c  Powdered milk (non-instant)
Mix ingredients and knead until all milk is absorbed.  Pull like
taffy, stretch, cut. Origin: Homemaking Booklet, Mormon Church, 1978
Shared by: Sharon Stevens
