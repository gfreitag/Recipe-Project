Title: Pear-Nut Souffle Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Salt
16 oz Pears; drained and diced
3 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/4 c  Walnuts or pecans; chopped
3 oz Gelatin, lemon- or lime-flav
1 c  Water; boiling
1/2 c  Water; cold
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water; stir until dissolved.  Add cold
water, mayonnaise, and salt; beat until smooth.  Pour into a 9x5"
loafpan. Chill in freezer for 20 to 25 minutes or until firm 1 inch
from edge of pan but still soft in center.  Combine pears, cream
cheese, and nuts. Whip chilled gelatin until thick and slightly
fluffy. Fold in pear mixture. Pour into 1-quart mold or individual
molds. Chill until firm.
