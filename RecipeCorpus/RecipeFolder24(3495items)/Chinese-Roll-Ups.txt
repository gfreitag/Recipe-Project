Title: Chinese Roll Ups
Categories: Appetizer, Chinese
Yield: 1 recipe
1/2 c  Diced Water Chestnuts
2 ts Chopped Onions
2 pk Crescent Rolls
1/2 lb Ground beef
1    Env. Liptons Beef Mushroom
Soup
1 c  Bean Sprouts, drained well
Brown beef with onions.

Add soup mix, bean sprouts, water chestnuts and mix together.

Cut Crescent rolls lengthwise and lay out sealing edges with
fingertips. Spoon meat mixture in center of each strip and roll up.
Cut into about 1 1/2" pieces with a sharp knife or a pair of sharp
scissors and place on cookie sheet.  Bake at 375~ 15 min or until
golden brown.

Submitted by Carolyn Stewart.  This recipe has been tried and proven
successful many, many times.
