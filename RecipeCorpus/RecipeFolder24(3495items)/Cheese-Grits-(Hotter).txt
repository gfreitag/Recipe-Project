Title: Cheese Grits (Hotter)
Categories: Vegetable, Dressing, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
Salt; to taste
Pepper; to taste
Parmesan
Paprika
Jalapeno pepper; (opt)
1 c  Grits
4 c  ;water
2    Garlic clove; pressed
1/4 lb Butter
1/2 lb Cheddar; grated
3    Egg
per serving: 406 Fat grams per serving: 30 Approx. Cook
Calories
Time: 1:00

Cook grits in water seasoned with garlic and salt according to
package. Remove from heat.  Add butter and stir to dissolve.  Add
Cheddar chese, holding back a good handful to garnish.  Now add eggs
and mix com- pletely. Salt and pepper to taste.  Place in a buttered
2-quart baking pan. Sprinkle remaining Cheddar on top.  Sprinkle top
with a generous amount of Parmesan, dust with paprika, garnish with
jalapenos and bake, uncovered, in a 300 degree oven for 45 minutes.
