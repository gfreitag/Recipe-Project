Title: Sea-Fare Lorraine
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Seafood
Yield: 2 servings
3 T  Mayonnaise
Black pepper to taste
4 oz Sliced LORRAINE cheese
1    Tomato, sliced
4 oz Fresh or imitation crab
1/4 c  Finely chopped scallion
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
1/4 c  Finely chopped olives
Combine crab, scallion, celery, olives, mayonnaise and pepper. Place
Lorraine on bottom half of roll. Broil until melted. Top with tomato
slices and crab mixture.
Date: 09-26-94
From: Michael Orchekowski
