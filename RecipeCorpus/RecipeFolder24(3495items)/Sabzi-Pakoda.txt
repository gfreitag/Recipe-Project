Title: Sabzi Pakoda
Categories: Indian, Vegetarian
Yield: 12 fritters
1/2 ts Salt
300 ml Water
2 x  Green chillies, fine chop
3 tb Corn oil
1 x  Sm aubergine, chopped
1 x  Onion, sliced
175 g  Sifted besan flour
1/2 ts Cream of tartar
1/2 ts Bicarbonate of soda
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Ground coriander
1/2 ts Mustard seeds
1/2 ts Garam masala
Mix together the flour, raising agents, spices and salt, then slowly
beat in the water and add the chillies. Heat the oil in a pan and fry
the aubergine and onion until soft. When cool, add these to the
batter. Beat again thoroughly and fry spoonfuls of the mixture in hot
oil on both side until brown and crisp. From: The Adventureous
Vegetarian: Rare and Exotic Recipes from Around the World by Colin
