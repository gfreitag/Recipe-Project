Title: Sauteed Snow Peas B1
Categories: Vegetable, Chinese, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  CHICKEN BROTH
1 ts (LEVEL) SALT
1 tb CORNSTARCH
3 tb WATER
1 ts SESAME OIL
1 lb SNOW PEAS
1 tb CORN OIL
2    GREEN ONIONS, SPLIT AND CUT
-IN 2 IN. STRIPS
1 ts WHITE WINE
String snow peas.  Blanch in salted, boiling water for 20 seconds.
Drain. Preheat wok. Put in corn oil. Heat. Add green onions, and
stir-fry. Add wine, broth, snow peas and salt. Bring to boil. Mix
cornstarch with water. Thicken broth to mixture.  Add sesame oil.
Serve at once. Temperature(s): HOT Effort: AVERAGE Time: 00:15
Source: YEN CHING Comments: SOUTH BRENTWOOD, ST. LOUIS Comments:
WINE: WARM SAKI
