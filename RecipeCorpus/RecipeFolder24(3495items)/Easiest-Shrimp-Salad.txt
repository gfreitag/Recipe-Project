Title: Easiest Shrimp Salad
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Fish, Salad
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Minced onion flakes
-reconstituted
1 ds Garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
12 oz Cooked peeled chopped shrimp
1/4 c  Lowfat mayonnaise
2 tb Ketchup
1 ts Lemon juice
Combine all ingredients and mix well. Chill several hours to blend
flavors.

Per serving: 18g protein, 5g fat, 4g carb., 393mg sodium, 171mg
chol., 136 calories.
Date: 09-23-94
From: Carolyn Shaw
