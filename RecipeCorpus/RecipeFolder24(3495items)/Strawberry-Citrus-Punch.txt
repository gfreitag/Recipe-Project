Title: Strawberry Citrus Punch
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 3 quarts
20 oz Frozen strawberries in syrup
-partially thawed
4 c  Orange juice; chilled
1 c  Lemon juice from concentrate
36 oz Lemon-lime soda
In punch bowl, combine juices.  Just before serving, add carbonated
beverage and strawberries
