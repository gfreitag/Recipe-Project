Title: Peanut Butter Fudge
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Candy, Kids, Snack
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Honey
1 tb Water
2 tb Peanut butter
1/3 c  Nonfat dry milk powder
Combine all ingredients, mix well. Place on a sheet of wax paper,
shape into a rectangle about 1/2 inch high. Press firmly in place.
Chill 30 minutes, cut into squares and serve..

REMEMBER...THIS IS TWO SERVINGS.....:)

Per serving: 9g protein, 8g fat, 12g carb., 163mg sodium, 2mg chol.,
147 calories.
