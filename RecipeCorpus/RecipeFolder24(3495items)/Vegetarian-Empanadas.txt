Title: Vegetarian Empanadas
Categories: Main dish, Rice, Vegetable, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Raisins
1/2 ts Cinnamon, ground
1/2 ts Cumin, ground
3 cn Crescent roll dough, refrig.
15 oz Beans, pinto (Mexican style)
3 c  Rice, brown; cooked
1 c  Cheese, Cheddar; grated
1/2 c  Salsa
Combine beans, rice, cheese, salsa, raisins, cinnamon and cumin.
Separate crescent roll dough along perforated lines into rectangles.
Press and seal triangle perforations.  Spoon 1/3 cup mixture on top
half of dough rectangles.  Fold dough over mixture, roll edges; crimp
with fork to seal. Bake at 400 degrees on ungreased baking sheet 12
to 15 minutes or until golden.  Serve warm.

SOURCE: Everything's Better with Rice, USA Rice Council pamphlet
