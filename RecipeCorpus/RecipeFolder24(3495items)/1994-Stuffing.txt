Title: 1994 Stuffing
Categories: Poultry, Holiday, Fruit
Yield: 1 batch
1/4 c  Onion, dehydrated
-to 1/2 cup
2 tb Basil
1/2 ts Granulated garlic
1 tb Poultry seasoning
4    Eggs
2 cn Chicken broth
1    Loaf bread
-sourdough/multigrain
1 c  Wild/Brown rice mixture
2 c  Water
1 lb Sausage, brown & drain
2    Apples, chopped
1 bn Parsely, chopped
4    Celery stalks, diced
Cut bread into cubes, several days early.  Allow to dry hard.  Put dry
seasonings (and parsely) in and mix well.  Cook rice with water.
While rice is cooking, cook sausage, drain well and crumble into
bread cubes. Dice apples and celery and add to mixture.  Add rice
when done. Stir in 1 can broth.  Add eggs and mix well.  Add second
can broth until texture you like.
