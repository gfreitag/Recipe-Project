Title: Fry Bread
Categories: Bread, Native amer
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 t  Baking powder
1 t  Salt
Approximately 1 cup milk
2 c  Flour
2 T  Sugar
1 ea Egg
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Add enough milk to make a
soft dough. Make patties the size of hamburgers. Deep fry until
golden brown on both sides. For hotdogs roll 1/4 inch thick.
