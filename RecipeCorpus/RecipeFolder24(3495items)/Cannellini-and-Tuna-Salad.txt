Title: Cannellini and Tuna Salad
Categories: Italian, Salad, Beans, Seafood, Appetizer
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Red onion, minced
1 tb Basil, fresh minced
3 tb Olive oil
1 cn White Kidney Beans, 15 oz
- Cannellini Beans
1 cn Tuna, drained 6 1/2 oz
Rinse and drain beans.  In medium mixing bowl combine all
ingredients. Toss to combine.
