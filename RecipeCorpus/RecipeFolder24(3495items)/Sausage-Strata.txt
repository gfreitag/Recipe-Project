Title: Sausage Strata
Categories: Main dish, Sausage
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Rosemary
1/4 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Sage
pn Nutmeg
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Mustard, prepared
Parsley; chopped
1 lb Sausage, bulk highly seasond
6 sl Bread, white; crusts removed
6 sl Cheese, Swiss
1 c  Milk
1 c  Half-and-half
4    Eggs; beaten
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Crumble sausage in a skillet, and cook until done.  Drain on paper
towels. Place bread slices in a buttered 13x9x2" baking dish, and
cover with cheese.  Top with sausage.

Combine remaining ingredients except parsley in a mixing bowl; pour
over sausage.  Sprinkle with parsley.  Bake at 350 degrees for 50
minutes.
