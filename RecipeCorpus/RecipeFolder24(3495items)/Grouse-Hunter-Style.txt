Title: Grouse Hunter Style
Categories: Stove top, Game
Yield: 1 servings
-salt and pepper
4 sl Cooked bacon,
2 T  Butter
4    Carrots, sliced
1/4 t  Crushed tarragon
4    Grouse
4 c  Shredded cabbage
-crumbled
16 lg Cabbage leaves
1 c  Chicken broth
1/4 t  Crushed thyme
Sprinkle grouse inside and out with salt and pepper. Combine shredded
cabbage and bacon and put a fourth inside each bird. Wrap each bird
with 4 cabbage leaves and fasten with string. Melt butter in skillet
and place birds in skillet with chicken broth and remaining
ingredients. Reduce heat. Cover and simmer 25 to 30 minutes. Remove
string and cabbage leaves. Serve with sauce in pan over buttered
Date: 09-17-94
noodles. From: Earl Shelsby
