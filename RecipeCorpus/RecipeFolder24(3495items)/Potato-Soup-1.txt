Title: Potato Soup 1
Categories: Soup/stew, Potato
Yield: 4 servings  Instructions:

2 Tbs oil 1 medium onion, chopped coarsely 3 stalks celery, leaves
are fine 3 medium potatoes, diced, peeled 3 cups stock salt & pepper
to taste 1 small bay leaf

Heat oil in heavy pot.  Saute onion & celery till onion is
translucent. Add potatoes, stock, salt & pepper & bay leaf.  Cook for
30-40 minutes. Let cool briefly.

Blend well & re-heat.  Check seasonings & garnish with parsley.
