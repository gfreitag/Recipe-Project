Title: Grilled Eggplant with Pesto
Categories: Appetizer, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
Salt
1 sm Eggplant
1/4 c  Pesto
Slice eggplant in 1/2 inch slices.  Lightly salt and place in a
colander to drain.  Squeeze out excess juices.  Broil or grill until
lightly charred and tender.  Spread with a light coat of pesto and
serve at room tempertature.
