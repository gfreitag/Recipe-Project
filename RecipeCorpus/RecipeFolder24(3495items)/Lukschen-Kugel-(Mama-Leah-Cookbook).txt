Title: Lukschen Kugel (Mama Leah Cookbook)
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
6 ea Eggs
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Raisins (optional)
1/2 c  Walnuts or pecans
1 lb Medium egg noodles
1 c  Cottage cheese
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Cinnamon
Preheat oven to 350 F.  Cook noodles until tender, drain.  Beat eggs
in large bowl.  Add milk, cheese, sour cream, white sugar and
raisins.  Beat together and then add noodles.  Mix well.  Pour into
10 x 14 inch pan. Mix together brown sugar, nuts and cinnamon.
Sprinkle evenly over noodles and bake for 1 hour or until center
seems firm.
