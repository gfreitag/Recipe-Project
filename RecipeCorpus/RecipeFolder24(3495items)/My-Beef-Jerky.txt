Title: My Beef Jerky
Categories: Beef, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Red pepper flakes
1/2 t  Salt
4-5 dashes hot pepper
-sauce (I used 'Cajun
-Sunshine' pepper sauce
-which
I picked up in Safeway a
-while back)
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
%%%%% MARINADE  %%%%%
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
1/2 c  Soya sauce
1/4 c  Worchestershire sauce
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 t  Black pepper
lean piece of beef (I used a steak I had in the freezer) While meat is
still partially frozen, slice in thin slices. Mix all ingredients of
marinade together and marinate meat for at least one hour. Drain and
rinse meat off.  Lay strips on dehydrator and dry according to
manufacturers directions. Origin: Sharon Stevens Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Sept/94.
