Title: Millionaires
Categories: Dessert, Candy
Yield: 1 unknown
7 sm Hershey bars
1/3    Paraffin wax bar
2 pk Caramel kisses
2 c  Pecans; chopped
Melt caramels in double boiler.  Add 3 tablespoons water.  Add chopped
pecans.  Cool and drop by spoonsful on buttered cookie sheet.  Let set
until firm.  Melt Hershey bars and paraffin in double boiler and dip
caramel bits until coated and place on platter.

SOURCE: The Popular Pecan, pamphlet by the Mississippi Pecan Growers
Assn. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
Date: 09-28-94
From: Nancy Coleman
