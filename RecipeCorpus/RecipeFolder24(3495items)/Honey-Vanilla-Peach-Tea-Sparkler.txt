Title: Honey Vanilla Peach Tea Sparkler
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/3 c  Peach nectar or juice
1 1/3 c  Water, sparkling
1 1/3 c  Tea concentrate (qv)
1    Vanilla bean, 3" piece
2 tb Honey
In a 1 to 1 1/2 quart pan, combine vanilla, honey and juice and
heat till boiling.  Cover and chill until cold, at least 1 hour.
Remove vanilla bean and reserve (in a tightly sealed container with
sugar) for other uses.
Combine juice mixture with concentrate and sparkling water and pour
over ice.
Source: Sunset Magazine, August 1994
Date: 09-14-94
From: Karl Lembke
