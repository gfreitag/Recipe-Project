Title: Maude's Green Salad
Categories: Diabetic, Salad, Vegetable, Side dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 10 servings
1/2    Head Romaine lettuce;
5 tb Lo-cal Italtan dressing;
1 tb Parmesan cheese
1/2    Head Iceberg lettuce;
1/2    Head boston lettuce;
1/2    Head chicory;
1/2 lb Spinach;
Rinse and crisp the salad greens.  Break iceberg lettuce into
bite-size pieces.  Carefully pat iceberg dry on towel.  Place in
large plastic bag. Add 1 tb Italian dressing. Shake lightly until all
leaves are covered. Place in strip on medium platter or plate. Repeat
with each green. Sprinkle lightly with Parmesan cheese. Food Exchange
per serving: NEGLIGIBLE CAL: NEGLIGIBLE

Source: The Complete Diabetic Cookbook by Mary Jane Finsand Brought
to you and yours by Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master
