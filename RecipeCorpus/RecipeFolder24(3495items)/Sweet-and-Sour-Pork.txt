Title: Sweet and Sour Pork
Categories: Main dish, Pork, Meat
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Vinegar
2 tb Catsup
2 tb Soy sauce
1    Minced clove of garlic
2 tb Corn starch
2 tb Corn oil
1/2 c  Dark corn syrup
1 lb Pork, cut into 1 inch pieces
15 oz Can of chunk pineapple
1/2 c  Green pepper
Brown pork in corn oil.  Add pineapple, corn syrup, vinegar, catsup,
soy sauce, and garlic.  Bring to a boil.  Simmer 10 minutes.  Mix
corn starch and 2 tablespoons of water; add to pork with green
pepper. Boil 2 minutes, stirring constantly.  Serve over rice. Randy
Rigg
