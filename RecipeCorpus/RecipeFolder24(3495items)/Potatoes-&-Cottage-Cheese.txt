Title: Potatoes & Cottage Cheese
Categories: Casserole, Vegetarian, Potato
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  Worcestershire sauce
3/4 c  Milk
2 T  Parsley,chopped
1/4 c  American cheese,shredded
6    Potatoes,cooked,diced
1/2 lb Cottage cheese
1    Onion,medium,grated
2 t  Salt
1 ds Cayenne pepper
1. Put diced potatoes in a greased shallow 2-quart baking dish.

2. Mix remaining ingredients except American cheese; pour over
potatoes.

3. Top mixture with shredded American cheese; bake in preheated
400'F. oven 15 minutes.
