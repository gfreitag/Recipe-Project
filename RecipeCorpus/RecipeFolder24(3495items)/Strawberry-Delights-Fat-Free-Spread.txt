Title: Strawberry Delights Fat-Free Spread
Categories: Spread, Fruit
Yield: 16 ounces
4 pk Equal Sweetener
-OR
8 t  Sugar
1 cn Promise Ultra Nonfat
-Margarine
8 lg Ripe strawberries, pureed
-(about 3/4 cup)
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients. Whisk until smooth. Keep
refrigerated. Use as a spread on toast, bagels, muffins, or crackers.

From: Michael Orchekowski
