Title: Fagiolini
Categories: Appetizer, Italian, Beans
Yield: 4 servings
Salt and pepper
2 tb Olive oil - more if desired
1 lb Green Beans - Italian style
1/2 ts Garlic, minced fresh
Snip ends from beans and cut into 2 inch pieces.  Steam to desired
doneness.  Add garlic, olive oil and fresh ground pepper and salt to
taste. Toss well.

Maranate for at least 1 hour.  Serve at room temperature.  If Italian
green beans are not available, other styles of fresh green beans can
be substituted.
