Title: Spam Hot Vegetable Salad Sandwiches
Categories: Sandwich, Spam, Vegetable, Salad
Yield: 6 servings
1    Tomato, chopped
1/2 c  Finely chopped broccoli
1/2 c  Thinly sliced carrot
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Peppercorn ranch-style
-salad dressing
6    Unsliced whole wheat buns
-or Kaiser rolls
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(7 oz)
1 c  Shredded Monterey Jack
-cheese
Heat oven to 350'F. Cut thin slice from top of each bun; reserve.
Remove center from each bun, leaving a 1/2" wall. Combine remaining
ingredients. Spoon into buns, pressing filling into buns. Top with
reserved tops of buns. Wrap each sandwich tightly in aluminum foil.
Bake 20 minutes or until thoroughly heated and cheese is melted.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 235; Protein 13 grams;
Carbohydrate 18 grams; Fat 13 grams; Cholesterol 45 milligrams;
Sodium 615 milligrams.
