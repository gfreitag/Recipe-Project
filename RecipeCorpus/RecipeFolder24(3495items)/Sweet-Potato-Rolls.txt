Title: Sweet Potato Rolls
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Bread
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Dried chives
1 ea Egg
6 oz Finely shredded,peeled
-sweet potato
1/2 c  Plain lowfat yogurt
1 tb Plus
1 ts Margarine, melted
1 c  Plus
2 tb -flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Grated lemon peel
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Dried thyme
Preheat oven to 425. In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking
powder, salt, lemon peel, baking soda, basil, thyme, and chives. In
another bowl, combine egg, sweet potato, yogurt, and margarine. Add
to dry igredients; stir until moistened. Divide dough evenly into 8
nonstick muffin cups. Bake 20 minutes until browned. Remove to rack
to cool, or for best flavor, serve hot.

Per serving: 4g protein, 3g fat, 21g carb., 194mg sodium, 35mg chol.,
127 calories.
