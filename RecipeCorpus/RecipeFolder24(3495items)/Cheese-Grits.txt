Title: Cheese Grits
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Bread
Yield: 12 servings
1 t  Salt
1 ds Tabasco or Pickapeppa
Paprika
1 1/2 c  Grits
1/4 c  Margarine
3    Egg; beaten
1 lb Velveeta; grated
per serving: 255 Fat grams per serving: 15 Approx. Cook
Calories
Time: 1:30

Cook grits according to package directions.  Add margarine, eggs,
cheese and salt; cook slowly until cheese melts.  Remove from heat
and add Tabasco or Pickapeppa sauce.  Pour into a casserole or
souffle dish and sprinkle with paprika.  Bake one hour at 250 degrees.
