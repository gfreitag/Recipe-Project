Title: Tuna Cheese Pie
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Cheese/eggs, Fish, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1 tb Minced onion flakes
1 pk Instant chicken broth
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
8 oz Can tuna, drained and flaked
1 oz Parmesan cheese, divided
1 1/3 c  Lowfat cottage cheese
3 ea Eggs
2 tb Flour
Preheat oven to 350. Arrange tuna in a 9-inch pieplate that has been
sprayed with a nonstick cooking spray. In a blender container,
combine half of the parmesan cheese with remaining ingredients. Blend
until smooth. Pour over tuna, top with remaining cheese. Bake,
uncovered, 30 minutes, until set and lightly browned. Let stand five
minutes before serving.

Per serving: 23g protein, 5g fat, 5g carb., 727mg sodium, 159mg
chol., 162 calories.
