Title: Wagon Wheel Sausage Pie
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Hash browns, loose, frozen
2 T  Milk
1/4 t  Pepper
3/4 c  Bisquick
2 ea Egg
12 oz Sausage, little sizzlers
1/2 c  Green onions, chopped
1/2 t  Salt
8 oz Cream cheese
1/3 c  Milk
Cook sausages and drain. Grease 10 inch pie plate. Spread potatoes in
pie plate. Mix together next 5 ingredients and spread over potatoes.
Arrange sausages in spoke fashion on top. Mix together bisquick, milk
and eggs and pour around sausages. Bake uncovered at 400 degrees for
25 to 30 minutes.
