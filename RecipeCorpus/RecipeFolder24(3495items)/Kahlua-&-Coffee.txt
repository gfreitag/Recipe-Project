Title: Kahlua & Coffee
Categories: Alcohol, Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
Nutriwhip, whipped
1 1/2 oz Kahlua
Hot coffee
Add Kahlua to coffee.  Top with whipped Nutriwhip.  Stir with cinnamon
stick, if desired. Origin: Alcohol Recipe Booklet. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Oct/94.
