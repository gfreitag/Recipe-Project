Title: Wish Bone Oriental Flank Steak
Categories: Beef, Asian, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea To 1-1/2 lb beef flank
-steak, cut about 3/4 inch
-thick
1/2 c  Italian Dressing
2 ea Tbps packed brown sugar
2 T  Soy sauce
1/2 t  Ground ginger
For marinade, in a shallow baking dish combine Italian dressing, brown
sugar, soy sauce and ginger.  Set aside.  Score on both sides of meat
at 1 inch intervals diagonally across steak in a diamond pattern.
Place steak in dish, turning to coat.  Marinate in refrigerator 3-24
hours, turning occasionally.  Remove steak.  Reserve marinade.  Grill
steak uncover, over medium coals (or broil) for 12-14 minutes for
medium doneness, turning once.  Meanwhile, bring reserved marinade to
a boil. Pour over steak. If desired, serve with assorted vegetables.
Serves 6. Origin: Wish Bone Salad Dressing Ad in Canadian Living,
Oct/94. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
