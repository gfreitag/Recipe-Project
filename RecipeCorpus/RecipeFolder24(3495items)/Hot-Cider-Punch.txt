Title: Hot Cider Punch
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1/8 ts Ground cloves
4 c  Water
4 c  Apple cider
2/3 c  Orange flavor Tang
Combine ingredients in a saucepan; mix well.  Heat just to boiling.
Serve hot in punch cups or mugs.  This makes about 2 quarts. Randy
