Title: Broccoli in Oyster Sauce
Categories: Side dish, Stir fry, Thai, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Broccoli, cut into florets
6 tb Vegetarian oyster sauce
4 tb Vegetable oil
3 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
Heat the oil in a wok & stir-fry the garlic until it is golden brown.
Add the broccoli & stir-fry for 3 minutes over low heat.  Add the
oyster sauce & stir for 1 minute.  Transfer to a serving dish & serve
hot.

Eng Tie Ang, "Delightful Thai Cooking"
