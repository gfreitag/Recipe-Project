Title: 1-2-3-4 Cake Filling and Topping By James Beard
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 cake
MMMMM-------------------FOR FILLING AND ICING------------------------
3/4 c  Sugar; granulated
1 tb Orange rind; finely grated
3/4 c  Orange juice; strained
2 tb Lemon juice
James Beard's 1-2-3-4 Cake Icing.

Makes: Icing and filling for one cake.

SEE: Cake, 1-2-3-4 Cake by James Beard for the cake recipe.

* Instructions *

Mix the orange juice, lemon juice, sugar and orange rind
:
together and drizzle the mixture over the three still warm cake
layers. Be careful not to let it all soak into one spot.

Then pile the layers on top of each other. The juice mixture
:
will give the cake a lovely, fresh, fruity flavor and it is not rich
like a chocolate or other candy flavored icing. Leave the cake to
cool.

This recipe and the 1-2-3-4 cake recipe by James Beard.
