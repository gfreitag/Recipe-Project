Title: Pistachio Bundt Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Flour
5    Eggs
1/2 c  Oil
1/2 c  Water plus 2 tablespoons
1    Box white cake or sour cream
White cake
2 pk Instant pistachio pudding
1/2 c  Milk
Mix and add eggs, one at a time.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
Let cool for 20 minutes and remove from Bundt pan.  Cut in 3 layers.

Frosting: Use 1 pint of whipping cream.  Beat until barely thick.  Add
package pistachio pudding and 1 (8 ounce) Cool Whip.  Spread between
layers and on all sides of the cake. Randy Rigg
