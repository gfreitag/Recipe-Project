Title: Apple Fudge Squares
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 16 squares
1 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Applesauce
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
1 c  All-purpose flour, sifted
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
2 oz Unsweetened baking chocolate
Preheat oven to 350oF. Grease an 8" square pan on bottom. Sift flour,
baking powder, soda and salt together; set aside. In double boiler,
melt butter and chocolate together over medium heat. Remove from heat
and add sugar and applesauce. Beat in eggs one at a time. Blend in
vanilla and dry ingredients; stir in pecans.

Pour into prepared pan. Spread evenly. Bake 35-40 mintues, until done
as desired. Let cool. Cut into squares or bars.

Source: Marian Hoffman, Whitman's Chocolate Cookbook. Typed by Linda
Howard.
