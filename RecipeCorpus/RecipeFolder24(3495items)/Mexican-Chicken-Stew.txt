Title: Mexican Chicken Stew
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Soup/stew, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Oregano leaves
1 ts Chili powder
15 oz Beans, red kidney; undrained
15 oz Beans, black; drained/rinsed
16 oz Corn, whole-kernel; drained
1/2 c  Green onion; sliced
1 tb Oil, olive
1 lb Chicken breast, boneless;
-cut in cubes
1 c  Chicken broth
4 oz Green chiles; chopped
1 1/2 ts Garlic powder, calif. style
1 1/2 ts Cumin
Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat.  Add chicken and cook 5
minutes, stirring often.  Remove chicken and set aside.

Add remaining ingredients except onion to skillet; mix well.  Bring
to a boil, reduce heat to medium and cook 10 minutes.

Stir in cooked chicken and onion; simmer 5 to 10 minutes.
