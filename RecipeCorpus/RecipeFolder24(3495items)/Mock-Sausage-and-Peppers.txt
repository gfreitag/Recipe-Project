Title: Mock Sausage and Peppers
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Dried marjoram
1/8 ts Ground savory
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Fennel seeds, crushed
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Plus
1 ts -vegetable oil
1/2 c  Finely minced green pepper
1/2 c  Finely minced onion
1 1/4 lb Ground turkey
1/4 ts Ground sage
Heat oil in skillet, add peppers and onions and cook until tender,
about 10 minutes. Cool. Combine peppers and onions with turkey and
remaining ingredients, mixing well. Chill an hour or so to blend
flavors. Form into 8 thin patties. Cook in nonstick skillet until
lightly browned on both sides.

Per serving: 26g protein, 19g fat, 3g carb., 271mg sodium, 95mg
chol., 284 calories.
