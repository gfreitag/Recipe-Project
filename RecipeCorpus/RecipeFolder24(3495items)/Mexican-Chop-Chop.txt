Title: Mexican Chop Chop
Categories: Tex/mexican, Vegetable, Beans, Cheese/eggs, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Kernel corn, drained and
-rinsed
1 md Tomato, seeded and chopped
1/2 c  Kidney beans, drained and
-rinsed
MMMMM----------------1/2 EACH: GREEN PEPPER AND R---------------------
2 lg Pita breads, halved
1/2 c  Chopped monterey jack cheese
1/4 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
Mix all ingredients together.  Fill pitas with mixture. Makes 4 halved
pitas. Per serving: 172 Cal, 10.1 pro, 11.6 carbo, 9.9 fat. Good
source of Vitamin C and calcium. Origin: Kraft What's Cooking
Magazine, 9th issue Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
