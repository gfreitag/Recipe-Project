Title: Fluffy Gold Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, British
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/3 c  Sugar
1 t  Salt
1 c  Milk
4 ea Egg yolks
2 c  Cake flour, sifted
3 t  Baking powder
1/3 c  Shortening
1 t  Vanilla
Sift together dry ingredients and put in mixing bowl. Add shortening
and half the milk. Mix. Add remaining milk, vanilla, and egg yolks.
Blend well. Pour into 2 greased and floured round layer pans and bake
at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
