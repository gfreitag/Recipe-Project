Title: Jellied Biscuits
Categories: Bread, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Sugar
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Cream of tartar
1/2 c  Shortening
3/4 c  Milk
1/3 c  Jelly
"Jellied Biscuits are a
-pleasure to serve because
-they look
So lovely with the jelly on
-them." - Marsha Ransom
2 c  Flour
4 t  Baking powder
In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, sugar, salt and cream of
tartar. Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. ADd
milk; stir quickly with a fork just until mixed. Drop by rounded
tablespoonfuls onto a greased baking sheet. Make a deep thumbprint in
tops; fill each with 1 tsp. of jelly.  Bake at 450 degrees for 10-12
minutes or until biscuits are browned. Makes: 1 dozen From: "Taste of
Home" Magazine, October/November 1994 Posted by: Debbie Carlson -
Cooking
