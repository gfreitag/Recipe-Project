Title: Skillet Okra
Categories: Diabetic, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 8 each 1/2 c
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper
-chopped
16 oz (1 cn) whole tomatoes;
-undrained chopped
1 1/2 c  Fresh okra; sliced
16 oz (1 cn)whole-kernel corn;
-undrained
Combine all ingredients in a large skillet, stirring well. Cover and
bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, and simmer 15-20 mins.
