Title: The Bee Cheese
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Sherry
1 tb Horseradish
1/2 ts Garlic salt
Jim Vorheis
10 oz Sharp cheese, grated
10 oz Butter, softened
Blend all ingredients well and pack in a crock.  Serve with crackers.
This is better if it is made several days before serving.

Colorado Cache Cookbook (1978) From the collection of Jim Vorheis
