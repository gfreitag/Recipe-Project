Title: Savory Pumpkin Spread
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Dried parsley
1/4 t  Mustard powder
1/4 t  Garlic salt (or salt), or to
Taste
1 c  Pumpkin puree
1/4 c  Water (or more)
1 T  Nutritional yeast
1/2 t  Marjoram
Mix all ingredients and serve.  More water can be added for a thinner,
"dipping" sauce.
