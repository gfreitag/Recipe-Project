Title: Blueberry Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
2 c  Blueberries
1    Box of yellow cake mix
3    Eggs
1/3 c  Oil
Mix first 5 ingredients with mixer.  Rinse blueberries.  Fold
blueberries in batter.  Pour into greased pan.  Bake at 350 degrees
for an hour. Usually done in 50 minutes.  Cool for 20 minutes.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar. Randy Rigg
