Title: Easy Spiced Apple Butter
Categories: Spread, Fruit, Can/preserv, Apples
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 t  Nutmeg
1/2 t  Ground cloves
2 1/2 lb Rome beatury (or similar)
-apples, cored and
-quartered, do not
Peel.
(Cooks in the oven so it
-won't scorch)
2 c  Sweet apple cider
1/4 c  Light brown sugar
1/4 c  Butterscotch dessert mix
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Ginger
Cook first 7 ingredients in heavy overproof Dutch oven or other large
kettle over low heat, stirring occasionally until sugar dissolves.
Increase heat and bring to a boil.  Add apples and return to boil.
Cover and bake at 350F until apples are very soft and liquid has
evaporated, stirring occasionally, aobut 2 hours.  Cool slightly.
Puree in food processor or blender until smooth.  Pack into clean
glass containers with tight fitting lids.  Store in refrigerator.
Makes 3 cups, 1 tbps per serving. Keep refrigerated.  use as you
would jam, jelly, or preserves. Origin: Watkins Food Gifts Cookbook
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
