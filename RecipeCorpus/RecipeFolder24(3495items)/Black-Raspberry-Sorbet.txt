Title: Black Raspberry Sorbet
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/4 c  Creme de cassis
1 1/2 qt Black raspberries, washed
-and dried
1 c  Sugar
In blender or food processor, combine all ingredients and blend until
smooth. Strain the puree to remove the seeds, and chill until very
cold. Place the mixture in an ice cream machine and freeze according
to manufacturers directions.

Per serving: 195 calories, 45g carb., 2g protein, 0mg chol., .7g/2%
fat.
