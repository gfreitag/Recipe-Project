Title: Diced Pepper & Tomato Salad
Categories: Side dish, Moroccan, Salad, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea Garlic cloves, chopped
Salt
3 lg Green bell peppers, grilled
-- & skinned
6 ea Tomatoes, skinned & seeded
Rinse & drain the peppers.  Remove the core & seeds & cut the flesh
into small dice.  Dice the tomatoes to the same size.

Combine tomatoes & peppers & add the chopped garlic.  Season with
salt to taste & chill.  Drain off excess liquids before serving.

Robert Carrier, "Taste of Morocco"
