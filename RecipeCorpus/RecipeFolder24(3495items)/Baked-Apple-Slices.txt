Title: Baked Apple Slices
Categories: Fruit, Dessert, Homemade, Apples
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
"Nothing beats these tender apple slices over ice cream for satisfying
harvest flavor.  This old-fashioned treat gives

a new twist to traditional baked apples.  They are also excellent
served over waffles or ham.  I make sure to save room for dessert!" -
large   baking apples, peeled and sliced 3/4 cup
Linda Gaido 3
sugar 1 Tbsp. ground cinnamon 1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg 1/4 tsp. ground
ginger 1/4 cup apple cider 1/2 cup butter or margarine 1/2 cup
walnuts or raisins Vanilla ice cream Place apples in a greased
1-quart baking dish. Combine sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and
apple cider; pour over apples. Dot with butter.  Sprinkle with nuts
or raisins. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 45-60 minutes or
until apples are tender. Serve warm over ice cream. Serves:  4 From:
"Taste of Home" Magazine, October/November 1994 Posted by: Debbie
Carlson - Cooking
