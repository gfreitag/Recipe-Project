Title: Eggplant Croquettes
Categories: Vegetable, British
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sharp cheese, grated
2 ea Eggs
2 T  Onion
1 c  Corn oil
1/2 t  Pepper
2 ea Eggplants, peeled & cubed
1 c  Bread crumbs, seasoned
2 T  Parsley, fresh, chopped
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
1 t  Salt
In covered saucepan, cook eggplant in a small amount of boiling water
until tender, about 4-5 mins. Drain & mash. Stir in remaining
ingredients except oil. Shape into patties. Fry in hot oil until
golden brown, about 5 mins on each side.

Mrs. Robert F. Lewis
