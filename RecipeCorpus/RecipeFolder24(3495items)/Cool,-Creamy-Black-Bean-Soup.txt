Title: Cool, Creamy Black Bean Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Beans
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Balsamic vinegar or lemon
-juice
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
1 tb Snipped chives or sliced
-green onions
4 c  Black beans, rinsed and
-drained (3-15 oz cans)
1 1/2 c  Plain non-fat yogurt
1 cn Mild peeled green chilies
-(4 1/2 oz)
2 t  Cumin
Puree all ingredients but chives or onions in blender or food
processor. Serve cold, topped with a dollop of yogurt and sprinkled
with chives or onions.

Per serving: 292 calories, 21 grams protein, 51 grams carbohydrates,
16 grams fiber, 1 gram fat, 337 milligrams sodium.
