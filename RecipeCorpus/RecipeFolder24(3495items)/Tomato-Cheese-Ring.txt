Title: Tomato Cheese Ring
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
3    Eggs, hard-cooked; chopped
1/2 c  Olives, stuffed; sliced
Lettuce leaves
6 oz Gelatin, lemon-flavored
3 c  Tomato juice; hot
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
6 oz Cream cheese; softened
Dissolve gelatin in tomato juice.  Cool. Beat mayonnaise and cream
cheese with a rotary beater until smooth and well blended.  Add
tomato mixture; beat until well blended.  Chill until thick, but not
set. Fold in eggs and olives. Pour into a 1-1/2 qt. ring mold. Chill
until set. Unmold on lettuce leaves.
