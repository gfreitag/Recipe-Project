Title: Don's Tiger Wing Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Poultry, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
3 T  Heinz 57 sauce
1 t  Dry mustard
1 oz Jack Daniels whiskey
1 ea Bottle Tiger Sauce
1 t  Garlic minced
1 T  Lemon juice (fresh)
1 T  Balsamic vinegar
Place all ingredients into a sauce pan and slowly bring to a slow
boil. Stir continously and boil for 5 minutes. Turn off heat, pour
misture into a bowl, and use as a dip for Buffalo Wings. NOTE: This
is good a BBQ sauce a marinade for pork beef, venison, chicken, and
gator. Keeps in refrigerator for 3 days. ORIGIN: Don Houston's
kitchen circa July 1994 Submitted By DON HOUSTON   On   10-01-94
