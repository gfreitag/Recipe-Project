Title: Carol's Egg Nog
Categories: Beverage, Holiday, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Sugar (or to taste)
Nutmeg
12 ea Eggs, separated
1 pt Heavy whipping cream
3 qt Half & Half
Beat egg whites until stiff. Add sugar to the egg yolks, beat until
creamy yellow. Add vanilla, half & half and whipping cream.Mix well.
Fold in whipped egg whites. Serve cold and sprinkled with nutmeg. If
desired, a little brandy (or a lot) may be added.

Source: Carol Shell
