Title: Deviled Cornish Hens
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Main dish, Poultry, Game
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Dijon mustard
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 ts Pepper
3 tb Dry bread crumbs
2    Cornish hens, skin removed
2 tb Water
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Red wine
1 tb Sherry extract
Split hens in half down the back. Place in a shallow pan. Combine
remaining ingredients, except bread crumbs and pour over hens.
Marinate several hours, turning occasionally. Preheat oven to 350.
Sprinkle half of the bread crumbs over hens. Bake 30 minutes. Turn
hens, sprinkle with remaining crumbs. Bake 30 minutes more.

Per serving: 26g protein, 14g fat, 7g carb., 1546mg sodium, 76mg
chol., 275 calories.
