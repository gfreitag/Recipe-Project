Title: Cracker Jack
Categories: Candy
Yield: 3 servings
1    Stick of butter
1/2 ts Soda
5 qt Popcorn, popped of course
1/4 c  Syrup, light
2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 ts Cream of tartar
Mix all together but soda.  Boil 5 minutes; take off heat, then add
soda. Mix into 5 quarts of popped popcorn.  Heat oven to 200 degrees.
Put mixed popcorn on cookie sheet.  Bake for 1 hour. Randy Rigg
