Title: Monterey Penne
Categories: Pasta, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
4 c  Hot cooked penne pasta,
-drained
1 c  Monterey jack cheese,
-shredded
1 ea Red or green pepper, cut in
-julienne strips
1 c  Quartered, fresh mushrooms
1 ea Onion, coarsely chopped
Place veggies and 2 tbsp water in a covered microwaveable dish.  Cook
on high for 4-5 minutes.  Drain veggies and toss with pasta and
cheese. Makes 2 servings. Origin: Kraft What's Cooking magazine, 9th
issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
