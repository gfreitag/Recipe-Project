Title: 30-Second Smoothie
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 2 servings
4    Ice cubes
1 c  Plain non-fat yogurt
1 c  Orange juice
Whip in blender until frothy.

Variations: For the orange juice, substitute 1 banana or 1 cup
blueberries, strawberries, raspberries, pineapple or sliced peaches.
Or, for the ice, substitute frozen fruit.

Per serving: 237 calories, 15 grams protein, 43 grams carbohydrates,
1 gram fiber, 1 gram fat (0.3 gram saturated), 175 milligrams sodium.
Date: 09-24-94
From: Michael Orchekowski
