Title: Dill Dressing
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dip, Dressing
Yield: 2 cups
1 ts White vinegar
1/4 c  Chopped fresh dill
1 tb Fresh horseradish
1 pt Nonfat plain yogurt
1 pn White pepper
2 tb Chopped scallions
Combine all ingredients; cover and chill.

Per Tb.: 8 calories, 1g carb., .7g protein, 0mg chol., .1g/12% fat.
