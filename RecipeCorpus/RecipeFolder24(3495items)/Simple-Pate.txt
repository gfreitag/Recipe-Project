Title: Simple Pate
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Brandy
2 tb Dry sherry
2 tb Chopped truffles (optional)
2 c  Chicken livers, cleaned
1/4 ts Marjoram
3/4 c  Sweet butter, room
-temperature
Simple Pate xxxxxxxxxxx

salt and pepper to taste

Cover chicken livers with water, salt lightly and birng to boil. Draim
immediately and rise livers in cold water until chilled thoroughly.
Drain. Add marjoram.  Puree livers with suitable machine or pass
through a fine wire sieve. Add remaining ingredients and blend well.
Taste and adjust salt. Serve well-chilled.
