Title: Fruit Sweet and Sugar Free - Egg Wash for Bread
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs, Information
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 c  Water
1    Egg
pn Salt
In a small bowl or cup, use a fork to beat the egg and salt together.
Stir in the water. Use a soft brush to apply the egg wash to rolls or
loaves just before baking them.

Egg wash can be stored in the refrigerator for 2-3 days if well
covered. Submitted By JANE KNOX   On   10-01-94
