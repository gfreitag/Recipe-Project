Title: Peach Ice Tea
Categories: Diabetic, Beverage
Yield: 4 nice folks
-Torani
Sugar to taste
(sugar sub)
4    Regular tea bags;
3 ts Peach syrup; such as Monin
-=OR=-
3 ts Mango syrup; DaVinci or
Brew tea.  Let cool.  Add flavoring.  Add ice.  Sweeten to taste.
