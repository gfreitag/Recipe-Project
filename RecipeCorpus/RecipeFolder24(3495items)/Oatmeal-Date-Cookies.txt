Title: Oatmeal Date Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 dozen
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
3 c  Oatmeal, quickcooking
1 c  Dates, pitted
Pecan halves
1 c  Salad oil
1 tb Vanilla
2 lg Eggs
2 c  Sugar, separated
2 c  Flour
preheat oven to 375. in bowl, beat oil, vanilla, eggs, and 1 1/4 c
sugar until smooth. with mixer at low, beat in flour baking soda,
salt until blended well. with spoon stir in oats and chopped dates.
Place 1/4 c sugar in small bowl, roll heaping tbsp. of dough into
balls, roll balls in sugar and place about 2 inches apart, on 2
ungreased large cookie sheets. with hand press each ball to make a 3
inch round.  if you like garnish top of each cookie with a pecan
half. bake 12 to 15 minutes until golden. with pancake turner, remove
cookies to cool store cookies in tightly covered container. 155
calories per cookie, 7 g fat, 121 mg. cholesterol, 100 mg. sodium
