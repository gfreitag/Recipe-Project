Title: Philadelphian Soft Bread Pretzels
Categories: Bread, Snack, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Sugar
4 1/2 c  All purpose flour
1 1/4 c  Warm water (85F)
1 T  Dry yeast
Combine water, yeast and sugar and let stand untiil yeast has
dissolved and is foamy.  Mix in flour and knead for 7-8 minutes.  Let
dough rise covered, in a greased bowl in a warm place, until double
in bulk.  Form into sticks or pretzels and place on greased cookie
sheets.  Apply a thin egg wash (1 egg yolk diluted with 1-2 tbsp
water or milk)  Sprinkle with coarse salt. Allow pretzels to rise
until almost double in bulk. Bake in preheated 475F oven for about 10
minutes. Cool and serve.
