Title: Cucumber Salad 2
Categories: Salad, Thai, Appetizer, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 sm Red onion, sliced
1 lg Carrot, sliced
1/2 ea Sweet bell pepper, julienned
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper
1 c  White vinegar
1/4 c  Sugar
1 lg Cucumber, halved lengthwise,
-- peeled, seeded & sliced
In a small pot, heat the vinegar & sugar for 5 minutes, or until the
sugar has dissolved completely.  Let the mixture cool completely. Put
the remaining ingredients into a salad bowl & add the vinegar
mixture. The salad is best if marinated for at least 2 hours.  It
will keep in the refrigerator for a week.
