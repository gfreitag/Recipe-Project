Title: Homemade Microwave Caramels
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 servings
-(NOT evaporated)
1 c  Light corn syrup
1 T  Vanilla extract
1 c  Butter or margarine, melted
2 1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
14 1/2 oz Sweetened condensed milk
Butter a 13x9 inch baking dish.  SEt aside.  Combine first 4
ingredients in 3 quart microwave safe bowl.  Microwave (HIGH) 17
minuutes, stirring every 5 minutes.  Remove from microwave.  Stir in
vanilla extract.  Pour into prepared baking dish.  Cool.  When set,
cut into squares and wrap individually in waxed paper.  Store in
refrigerator.  Makes 117 one inch pieces.
