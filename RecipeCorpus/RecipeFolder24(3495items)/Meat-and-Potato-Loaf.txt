Title: Meat and Potato Loaf
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Beef, Main dish, Potato, Meatloaf
Yield: 4 servings
3 oz Grated unpeeled potatoes
2 tb Ketchup
1/4 ts Dried thyme
Salt and pepper to taste
1 lb Very lean ground beef
2 ea Egg whites
1/4 c  Finely chopped onions
1/4 c  Grated carrots
Preheat oven to 350. Combine all ingredients, shape into a loaf and
place on a rack in a shallow baking pan. Bake, uncovered, 1 hour.

Per serving: 22f protein, 15g fat, 7g carb., 181mg sodium, 70mg
chol., 259 calories.
