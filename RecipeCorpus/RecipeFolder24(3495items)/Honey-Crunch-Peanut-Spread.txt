Title: Honey Crunch Peanut Spread
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Kids
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Honey
3/4 oz Toasted oats*
2 tb Peanut butter
Combine all ingredients, mix well.

* To toast oats; place uncooked quick cooking oats in a single layer
on a ungreased cookie sheet. Bake in a preheated 350 oven for 15-20
minutes, until lightly browned.

Per serving: 6g protein, 9g fat, 19g carb., 99mg sodium, 0mg chol.,
167 calories.
