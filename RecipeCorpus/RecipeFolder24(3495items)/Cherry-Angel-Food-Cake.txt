Title: Cherry Angel Food Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Almond flavoring
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Chopped maraschino cherries
Whipped cream
Whole maraschino cherries
1 c  Sifted cake flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
12    Egg whites
1 1/2 ts Cream of tartar
1/4 ts Salt
Sift flour and 1/4 cup of the sugar together 3 times.  Beat egg
whites with cream of tartar and salt until soft peaks form.  Beat in
remaining 1/4 cup of sugar, 2 tablespoons at a time, beating
thoroughly after each addition. Continue beating until stiff peaks
form. Fold in almond flavoring and vanilla.  Sift dry ingredients, 2
tablespoons at a time, over beaten egg whites.  Fold in gently with a
wire whip. Fold in cherries. Carefully push batter into an ungreased
10 inch tube pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes, or until done.
Invert pan; let cool for 1 hour. Just before serving, frost with
whipped cream and decorate with whole cherries. Randy Rigg
