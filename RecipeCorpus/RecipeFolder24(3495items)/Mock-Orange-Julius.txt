Title: Mock Orange Julius
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 3 servings
1    Egg
1/2 c  Coconut milk
8 ea Ice cubes
6 oz Orange Juice Frz CONCENTRATE
1 c  Milk
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Sugar
Put in blender and blend until ice cubes are gone, about 2 minutes.
Serve right away while frothy.  Serving size: 12-13 oz
Date: 09-28-94
From: Sharon Dibble
