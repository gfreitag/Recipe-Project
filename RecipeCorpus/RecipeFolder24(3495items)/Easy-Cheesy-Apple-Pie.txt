Title: Easy Cheesy Apple Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit, Cheese/eggs, Apples
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Cold milk (6)
6 c  Peeled & sliced apples
1 pk Vanilla pudding-not instant
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 c  All purpose flour (1)
1/4 c  Quick oats (2)
1 1/2 ts Sugar (3)
1/2 ts Salt (4)
1/3 c  Cooking oil (5)
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/4 c  Oats
3 tb Butter or marg. softened
1/3 c  Flour
1/3 c  Sugar
Preheat oven to 375~

Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup, level off.  In 9" large glass
pie pan combine first 6 Ingredients. Mix Well. Press into bottom and
sides of pan.  Do not press onto rim.

In large bowl, combine dry pudding and apples.  Spoon into unbaked
crust. Sprinkle with cheese.

Combine topping ingredients. Blend until crumbly. Sprinkle over
cheese.

Bake 45 to 50 min until light golden brown and apples are tender.
Serve warm or cold.  It is best served warm with ice cream. Reheat in
Micro for a few seconds.

Note:  Apples may be cut ahead. Put in plastic bag and sprinkle with
lemon juice.
