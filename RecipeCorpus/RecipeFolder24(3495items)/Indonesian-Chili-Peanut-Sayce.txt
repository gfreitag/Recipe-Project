Title: Indonesian Chili Peanut Sayce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Asian
Yield: 12 servings
2 T  Karo, light or dark
1 T  Black soy sauce
2 T  Sesame oil
Red pepper; to taste
2 T  Oil
1 ts Garlic; minced
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Chicken stock or broth
2 T  Cider vinegar
Heat oil in a pan; add garlic and sautÂ‚ 30 seconds.  Add peanut
butter, and stir until blended.  Add the rest of ingredients.  Heat,
stirring constantly with a whisk, until the sauce is smooth and just
beginning to boil.  Serve.

Variation:  Add 1/2 a very ripe, thoroughly mashed banana just before
serving.
