Title: Shrimp, Pea and Mushroom Stir-Fry
Categories: Main dish, Vegetable, Stir fry, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Peanut oil
3/4 lb Fresh mushrooms, sliced
10 oz Pkg. frozen peas, thawed
1/4 c  Minced green onion
1 1/2 lb Fresh shrimp, peeled and
Deveined
Steamed rice
1 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Fresh grated ginger
3/4 c  Chicken broth
2 tb Soy sauce
1 lg Clove of garlic, crushed
Blend cornstarch with salt, sugar, ginger, chicken broth, soy sauce,
and garlic.  Set aside.  Preheat wok or large skillet for 3 minutes
over high heat.  Add oil and mushrooms.  Stir-fry over high heat for
1 minute. Add peas and onions.  Stir fry 2 minutes.  Add shrimp. Stir
fry 2 to 3 minutes. Don't overcook.  Add chicken broth mixture; stir
until thick. Serve immediately with rice. Randy Rigg
