Title: Shrimp in Cheddar Sauce
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Skim milk
2 ts Sherry extract
1 tb Lemon juice
4 oz Lowfat cheddar cheese,shredd
8 oz Cooked, cleaned shrimp
2 tb Plus (see next line)
2 ts -reduced calorie margarine
1/4 c  Finely chopped onions
1/2 c  Finely chopped celery
3 tb Flour
Melt margarine in a saucepan. Add onions and celery and cook until
tender, about 5 minutes, adding water if necessary, to prevent
drying. Reduce heat to low, stir in flour. Gradually stir in milk.
Add sherry extract and lemon juice. Stir until blended. Add cheese
and shrimp. Cover and cook 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Per serving: 22g protein, 9g fat, 18g carb., 373mg sodium, 121mg
chol., 239 calories.
