Title: Szechuan Beef and Snow Peas
Categories: Asian, Chinese, Beef, Stir fry, Vegetable
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 lb Beef, boneless
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
1 tb Sherry
1 cl Garlic, minced
1 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Soya sauce
MMMMM-----------------------STIR FRY SAUCE----------------------------
2 tb Oil
6 oz Snow peas
1 ea Onion, small, chopped coarse
1 ea Tomato, chunked
2 tb Soya sauce
1 tb Cornstarch
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes
3/4 c  Water
Slice beef across the grain, into thin strips. Combine 1 tb each
cornstarch, soy sauce, sherry, and garlic, stir in beef, let stand 15
minutes. Mix 2 tbsp soya sauce, 1 tbsp cornstarch, pepper and water.
heat 1 tbsp oil in hot wok, add beef stir fry 1 minute. remove, heat
1 tbsp oil in same pan. add snow pas and 1onion. add a pinch of salt.
stir fry 3 minutes. add beef soya sauce mixture and 1 tomato, cook
until sauce boils, and thickens, and tomato is heated through. serve
over rice. Submitted By KATHERINE WALDEN   On   10-04-94
