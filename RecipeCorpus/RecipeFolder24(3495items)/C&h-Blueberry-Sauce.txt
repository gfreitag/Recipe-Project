Title: C&h Blueberry Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 2 cups
1    Juice of lemon
1 t  Cornstarch
1/4 c  Cold water
1/2 c  C&H Granulated Cane Sugar
1 pt Fresh blueberries
-OR
2 c  Thawed frozen blueberries
In medium saucepan, combine sugar, blueberries and lemon juice; stir
over medium heat until sugar dissolves. Combine cornstarch and water;
add and cook until thickened. Chill before serving. Top with
additional blueberries if desired. Makes about 1 1/2 to 2 cups.
