Title: Beef and Vegetable Medley
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Red pepper strips
1 c  Mushrooms, fresh; sliced
1 c  Zucchini; sliced
7 oz Corn; drained
1/3 c  Green onions; sliced
3 c  Rice, brown; cooked
1 lb Steak, sirloin boneless; cut
-in strips
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
1    Garlic clove; minced
1 tb Oil, vegetable
Season beef with salt and pepper.  Brown beef with garlic in oil in
large skillet over high heat.  Stir in red pepper, mushrooms,
zucchini, corn and onions; stir-fry 3 to4 minutes or until vegetables
are tender crisp. Stir in rice; heat thoroughly.

SOURCE: Everything's Better with Rice, USA Rice Council pamphlet.
