Title: Chocolate Squares
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 2 dozen
3/4 c  Sifted cake flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Chopped pecans
2 oz Unsweetened baking chocolate
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
2 oz Unsweetened baking chocolate    1 3/4 c  Confectioners' sugar, sifted
5 tb Hot milk
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 oz Sweet chocolate
3 tb Butter
Preheat oven to 325o. Melt 2 oz unsweetened chocolate and shortening
together in top of double boiler over hot water. Remove from heat and
cool slightly. Beat in sugar. Add eggs one at a time; beat well after
each addition. Sift flour and baking powder together into bowl; add
to chocolate mixture. Add nuts and blend well.

Pour batter into greased, 9"x13" baking pan. Bake for 35 mintues, or
until firm. Cool in pan.

For frosting: Melt 2 oz unsweetened chocolate, sweet chocolate, and
butter in top of double boiler over hot water. Remove from heat.
Combine sugar and heated milk; pour into chocolate mixture. Add
vanilla and beat until thick enough to spread. Spread frosting over
baked chocolate mixture; cut into squares.

Source: Marian Hoffman, ed., Creative Cooking - Desserts. Typed by
