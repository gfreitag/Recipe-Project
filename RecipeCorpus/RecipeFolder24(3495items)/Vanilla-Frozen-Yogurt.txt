Title: Vanilla Frozen Yogurt
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dessert
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Heavy cream
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1    Vanilla bean
2 c  Yogurt cheese
3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/4 c  Skim milk
In a mixing bowl, combine the yogurt cheese, sugar, milk, cream and
vanilla extract. Whisk until well combined. Split the vanilla bean in
half lengthwise, and scrape the seeds into the bowl, stir to combine.
Transfer the mixture to an ice cream machine and freeze according to
manufacturers directions.

Per serving: 167 calories, 29g carb., 7g protein, 13mg chol., 3g/15%
fat.
