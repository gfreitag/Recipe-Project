Title: Stuffed Jalapenos Ii
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
Eggs
Flour
Cornmeal
8 lg To 10 lg pickled jalapenos
Cheddar Cheese
Water
Slit the sides of the peppers, and scrape out the seeds.  Stuff with
triangular pieces of cheddar cheese cut to fit the individual peppers.

Dip peppers in an egg wash then into a mixture of half cornmeal and
half flour.  Repeat, and allow to dry for about 15 minutes.

Deep fry in hot fat until the peppers are lightly browned, and the
cheese is just melting.  Drain on absorbent paper, and serve.

You can also use a crabmeat or shrimp salad stuffing for these (good,
but a waste of good seafood, IMBHO.
