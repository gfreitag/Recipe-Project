Title: Sausage and Sauerkraut
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Sausage, Polish
2 cn Sauerkraut; undrained
Cook sauerkraut over low heat 5 minutes;  add sausage.  Cover and
cook over low heat an additional 15 minutes.
