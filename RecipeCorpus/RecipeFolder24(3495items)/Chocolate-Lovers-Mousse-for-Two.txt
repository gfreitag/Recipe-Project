Title: Chocolate Lovers Mousse for Two
Categories: Dessert, Chocolate
Yield: 2 servings
OR
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Cold whipping cream
AND
Additional whipping cream
For garnish is optional
2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Unflavored gelatin
1/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Mini semi sweet chocolate
Chips
1 tb Orange flavored liqueur
In small saucepan, stir together sugar and gelatin; stir in milk.  Let
stand two minutes to soften gelatin.  Cook over medium heat, stirring
constantly, until mixture just begins to boil.   Remove from heat.

Immediately add small chocolate chips; stir until melted.  Stir in
liqueur or vanilla, cool to room temperature.

In small mixer bowl, beat whipping cream until stiff.  Gradually add
chocolate mixture, folding gently just until blended.   Refrigerate.

Garnish with additional whipped cream if desired.
