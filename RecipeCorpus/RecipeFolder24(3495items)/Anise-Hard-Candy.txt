Title: Anise Hard Candy
Categories: Candy, Dessert
Yield: 1 batch
1 1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Aniseed
2 c  ;Water
Simmer the seeds in water for 15 to 20 minutes; strain the liquid into
another saucepan.  Stir in the sugar.  Boil to the hard crack stage
(290-300 F.), then pour into an oiled shallow pan.  Mark into squares;
break apart when hard, and wrap each piece in waxed paper.

From "An Herb to Know: Anise" column in "The Herb Companion."
Oct./Nov. 1994, Vol. 7, No. 1.  Pg. 25.  Posted by Cathy Harned.
