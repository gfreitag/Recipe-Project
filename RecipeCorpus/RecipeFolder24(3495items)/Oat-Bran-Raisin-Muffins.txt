Title: Oat-Bran Raisin Muffins
Categories: Fruit, Muffin
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Walnuts OR raisins OR other
Fruit or nuts
1 x  Egg equivalent
1/4 c  Honey
3/4 c  Warm skim milk or non-dairy
Equivalent
2 1/4 c  Oat bran
1/4 c  Brown sugar
2 t  Baking powder
1 1/2 t  Ground ginger
1/4 t  Black pepper
1/2 c  Raisins
Mix wet and dry ingredients in separate bowls.  Combine and let set 10
minutes.  Pour into lightly greased/pammed muffin tins. Bake at 375
degrees 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
