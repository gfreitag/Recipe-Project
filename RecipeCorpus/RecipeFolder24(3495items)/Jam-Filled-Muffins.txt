Title: Jam-Filled Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Bread, Breakfast, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
2 ea Eggs
2/3 c  Milk
1/3 c  Butter or margarine, melted
1 t  Grated lemon peel
1/2 c  Raspberry or strawberry jam
"Kids and adults love the
-sweet surprise inside each
Muffin. They go great in
-any lunch." - Jessie
-MacLeod
1 3/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. In a
small bowl, lightly beat eggs; add milk, butter and lemon peel. Pour
into dry ingredients and stir just until moistened. Spoon half of the
batter into 12 greased or paper-lined muffin cups. Make a well in the
center of each; add jam.  Spoon remaining batter over jam.  Bake at
400 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until golden. Yields: 1 dozen From:
