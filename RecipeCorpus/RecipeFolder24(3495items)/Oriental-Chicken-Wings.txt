Title: Oriental Chicken Wings
Categories: Appetizer, Chicken, Chinese, Poultry, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Five-spice powder
2    Green onion;thinly sliced;bu
16    Chicken;wings;
1 c  Soy sauce
1/3 c  Sugar
4 ts Vegetable oil
1 1/2 ts Ginger;ground
(Makes 16)
Blend soy sauce, sugar, oil, ginger and five-spice powder in
large bowl until sugar dissolves. Stir in green onions. Add chicken
wings to marinade, turning to coat. Cover chicken and refrigerate
overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Drain chicken, reserving
marinade. Arrange chicken in 9x12-inch baking dish. Bake until golden
brown and tender, basting occasionally with marinade, 45 to 50
minutes. Serve hot or chilled. (Sandi Anderson)
Date: 09-23-94
From: Elizabeth Wood
