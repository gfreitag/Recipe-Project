Title: Jordan Marsh Blueberry Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Fruit, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
...Jordan Marsh Co., Boston
1/4 c  Margarine
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg, beaten
1 3/4 c  Flour
2 1/2 t  Baking powder
1/4 t  Salt
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Blueberries
"I was given this recipe
-while living on the Cape
-11 years
Ago....it was published in
-the paper and the clipping
-was
Given to me." - Dana Smith
Jordan Marsh Blueberry
-Muffins
Cream margarine and sugar; add egg.  Add dry ingredients alternately
with milk.  Fold in blueberries. Fill 12 large muffin cups about 2/3
full and sprinkle with sugar.  Bake at 375 degrees for 25 to 30
minutes. I make these every blueberry season and they are wonderful!!
