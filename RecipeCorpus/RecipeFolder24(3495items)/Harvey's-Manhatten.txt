Title: Harvey's Manhatten
Categories: Alcohol, Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
Ice
Maraschino cherry
1 ea Part Harvey's Bristol Cream
2 ea Parts Ry whisky
1 ds Angostura bitters
In a coctail shaker, stir ice with bristol cream, rye and bitters.
Strain into a martini glass or old fashioned glass and garnish with a
maraschino cherry. Origin: Alcohol Recipe Booklet. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Oct/94.
