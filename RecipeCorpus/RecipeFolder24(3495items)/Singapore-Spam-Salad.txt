Title: Singapore Spam Salad
Categories: Salad, Spam, Asian
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM--------------------WARM SESAME DRESSING-------------------------
2 tb Sesame oil
1/4 t  Garlic salt
1 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Rice vinegar
1/4 c  Olive oil
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
1 c  Chopped green onions
1 c  Chopped celery
1    Green bell pepper, chopped
1 c  Thinly sliced radishes
1 pk Sliced almonds, toasted
-(6 1/2 oz)
1/2    Head Iceberg lettuce, thinly
-sliced
1/2    Head Romaine lettuce, thinly
-sliced
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(12 oz)
3    Carrots, grated
In saucepan, combine all dressing ingredients. Stir constantly until
sugar dissolves. In large bowl, toss together all salad ingredients.
Serve warm dressing with salad.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 432; Protein 13 grams;
Carbohydrate 36 grams; Fat 28 grams; Cholesterol 34 milligrams;
Sodium 453 milligrams.
