Title: Applespice Gingerbread
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie
Yield: 16 servings
2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Allspice
8 oz Applesauce
3 lg Eggs
1/4 c  Icing sugar,
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 c  Margarine, softened
1/2 c  Apple juice
3 ts Ginger ground
2 ts Cinnamon
Heat oven 350F. Grease 9  by 9 pan.  Into large bowl, mix all
ingredients, except icing sugar.  blend well, constantly scraping
bowl with spatula. Beat at high for 2 minutes more. Pour batter into
pan, bake 1 hour, until toothpick comes clean.  cool 10 minutes, dust
with icing sugar. Submitted By KATHERINE WALDEN   On   10-04-94
