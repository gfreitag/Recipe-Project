Title: Ding Dong Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Crisco
5 1/3 oz Evaporated milk
1    Stick of softened butter
2 ts Vanilla
1    Box of devils food cake mix
4    Eggs
1 c  Cold tap water
1    Envelope of Dream Whip
Filling:
Stir together the first 4 ingredients and beat at high speed for 4
minutes. Pour into greased and floured 9x13 inch pan.  Bake at 350
degrees for 40 minutes, or until inserted toothpick comes out clean.
Cool 10 minutes, then remove from the pan.

Filling: Combine all of the filling ingredients and beat for 30
minutes without stopping.  Frost top and sides with any kind of
chocolate frosting. Randy Rigg
