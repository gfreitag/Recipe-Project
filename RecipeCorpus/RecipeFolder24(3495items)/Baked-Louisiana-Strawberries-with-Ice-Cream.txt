Title: Baked Louisiana Strawberries with Ice Cream
Categories: Cajun/creol, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
-eighths
1 T  Sugar
1/3 c  Sweet white wine
-vanilla ice cream
2 c  Strawberries, halved
2/3 c  Blueberries
2    Plums, pitted, cut into
-eighths
1    Peach, pitted, cut into
================DIRECTIONS============================ Preheat oven
to 325 degrees F.  Place fruit in 8-inch square glass baking dish.
Sprinkle with sugar.  Pour wine over fruit. Bake until fruit is
tender about 20 minutes. Cool slightly. Serve warm with vanilla ice
cream.

Serves 6.

NOTE: Other fruit can be substituted and the results are always

wonderful. From: Fred Towner
