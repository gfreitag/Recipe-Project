Title: Creamy Avocado and Zucchini Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Garlic salt
3 md Avocados; peeled and cubed
3 md Zucchini; thinly sliced
Salad greens
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Milk
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
Combine first 6 ingredients, stirring well.  Add avocado and
zucchini; toss gently.  Serve on salad greens.
