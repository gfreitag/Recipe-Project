Title: Amaretto Cheese Bars
Categories: Dessert, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 dozen
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Amaretto
1 1/4 c  Butter
2 1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1 c  Almonds, slivered; chipped
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
3    Eggs
1/2 c  Amaretto
16 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/2 c  Sugar
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In large saucepan, melt butter.  Remove
fromheat; stir in graham cracker crumbs, almonds, sugar, and 1/4 cup
Amaretto.  Reserve 1 cup for topping. Press remaining mixture into
15-1/2x10-1/2x1" baking pan.  Bake 10 to 12 minutes.  Set aside.

In large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until well blended.
Add eggs, one at time.  Stir in 1/2 cup amaretto.  Pour over crust.
Bake additional 20 to 25 minutes until set. Cool completely on wire
rack. Refrigerate before serving.  Cut into bars.

SOURCE: Amaretto di Amore Recipes, given out at Simply Southern, the
1994 Southern Living Cooking School Typos by Nancy Coleman.
