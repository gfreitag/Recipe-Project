Title: Leftover Bean Soup
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Beans, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Dried thyme
12 oz Cooked beans
2 c  Evaporated skim milk
Salt and pepper to taste
In blender, blend all ingredients until smooth. Pour into saucepan,
heat and serve. You can add water if a thinner soup is desired.

Per serving: 17g protein, 1g fat, 37g carb., 1mg sodium, 5mg chol., 22
calories.
Date: 09-13-94
From: Carolyn Shaw
