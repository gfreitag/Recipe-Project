Title: Figs in Sauternes
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Fruit, Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
-minced
Juice of 1 lemon
1 lb Fresh figs
2 tb Finely chopped fresh mint
1 1/2 c  Sauternes
2 tb Sugar
1 ea Vanilla bean, seeds removed
- and pod reserved.
1    Zest of 1 lemon, finely
In a medium saucepan, combine the Sauternes, sugar, vanilla bean pod
and seeds, zest, and juice. Simmer over medium heat for 8-10 minutes,
until tender. If the syrup becomes too thick, stir in 1 tb. of water.
Remove the vanilla pod and stir in the mint. Cool the figs in the
syrup and serve at room temperature.

Per serving: 155 calories, 40g carb., 1g protein, 0mg chol., .4g/1%
fat.
