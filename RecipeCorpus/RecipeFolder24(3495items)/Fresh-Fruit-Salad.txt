Title: Fresh Fruit Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 1 unknown
1 c  Celery; diced
1/3 c  Pecans; chopped
1 c  Raisins
2    Bananas, firm
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
3    Apples, red
Combine mayonnaise, lemon juice, sugar, and salt.  Stir together diced
fruit, celery, and pecans.  Add mayonnaise mixture.  Garnish with
salad greens, and other fresh fruit.

SOURCE: The Popular Pecan, pamphlet by the Mississippi Pecan Growers
Assn. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
