Title: Broiled Grapefruit
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Alcohol
Yield: 2 servings
1 T  Honey
1    Grapefruit
1 T  Sweet white wine
Cut grapefruit in half; remove seeds. Cut around edges and sections to
loosen; remove centers. Set oven control to broil and/or 550'. Mix
wine and honey; pour over grapefruit halves. Place on rack in broiler
pan. Broil grapefruit halves 4 to 6 inches from heat until juice
bubbles and edges of peel are light brown, 5 to 10 minutes.
