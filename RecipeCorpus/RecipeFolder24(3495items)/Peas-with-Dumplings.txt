Title: Peas with Dumplings
Categories: Main dish, Vegetable, Pasta
Yield: 3 servings
Flour to make stiff dough
1 ts Salt
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1/3 c  Milnot
2 cn Peas
Cook peas; set aside.  Heat 2 quart saucepan of water to boiling.
Take a small amount of dough on tip of a knife and dip in boiling
water. The dumpling will slip off.  Repeat with remainder of dough.
Boil for about 3 to 5 minutes, then drain.  Dot with butter.  Add to
cooked peas. Season with salt to taste. Randy Rigg
