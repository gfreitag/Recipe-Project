Title: Su Ts'ai Tang
Categories: Chinese, Vegetable, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
1 x  Red pepper, diced
2 x  Onions, sliced
120 g  Tofu, diced
900 ml Strong vegetable stock
Salt and pepper to taste
85 ml Sesame oil
1 x  Sm. cabbage, fine chop
2 x  Sm. carrots, diced
2 md Potatoes, peeled & diced
1 x  Green pepper, diced
Heat the sesame oil, throw in all the veggies and tofu, saute for a
few mins. Add the vegetable stock and simmer for 20 mins. Taste and
adjust the seasonings. This is a steaming hot vegetable stew eaten in
the winter. Make sure that the sesame oil is of high quality and
really flavours the stew. You can also roast and grind sesame seeds &
add them at the beginning of cooking. From: The Adventurous
Vegetarian by Colin Spencer Typed by: Joell Abbott 10/94
