Title: Old Fashoned Potato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Potato
Yield: 1 pot
1 ts Butter
1/2 c  Flour
1 ea Egg, Beaten
1/4 c  Milk for dumplings
8 ea Potatos, Cubed
1 qt Milk
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
Boil Potatos until soft, drain.  Add quart of milk and season to
taste, mix well.  Mix butter into flour, add the egg and milk, mix
well.  Drop by spoonfulls into the hot milk- potato mixture.  Cover
and cook about 10 minutes or until dumplings are done.
