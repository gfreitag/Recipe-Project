Title: Dilled Carrots
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 ts Dill seed
1 1/3 ts Mustard seed
1 cl Garlic, cut in half
2 tb Sugar
1 lb Carrots, cut into 4-inch
-julienne strips
1 c  Vinegar
1 c  Water
2 ts Salt
Cook, carrots, covered, in one inch of boiling water 3-4 minutes until
tender-crisp. Place in jar. In small saucepan, bring remaining
ingredients to boil. Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes. Pour over
carrots. store in fridge.

Per serving: 2g protein, 1g fat, 22g carb., 1141mg sodium, 0mg chol.,
87 calories.
