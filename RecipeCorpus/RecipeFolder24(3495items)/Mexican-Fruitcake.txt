Title: Mexican Fruitcake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Pecans
Frosting:
2 c  Powdered sugar
8 oz Cream cheese
1    Stick of butter, melted
1 ts Vanilla
Cake:
20 oz Can of crushed pineapple
With juice
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking soda
2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
Mix cake ingredients and pour into a 13x9 inch pan.  Bake at 350
degrees for 45 minutes.  Frost cake while hot! Randy Rigg
