Title: Marinated Tomato Salad
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Side dish, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
2 tb Plus (see next line)
2 ts -vegetable oil
Salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 c  Thinly sliced tomatoes
1/2 c  Thinly sliced red onion
1 1/2 ts Dried basil
3/4 ts Dried tarragon
1/4 ts Dried oregano
Place tomatoes and onions in a shallow bowl, overlapping slightly.
Combine remaining ingredients and pour over vegetables. chill several
hours to blend flavors.

Per serving: 1g protein, 1 g fat, 5g carb., 6mg sodium, 0mg chol., 105
calories.
