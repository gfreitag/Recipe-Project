Title: Bacha's Moroccan Salad Dressing
Categories: Appetizer, Moroccan, Salad, Dressing
Yield: 1 recipe
1/8 ts Hot red pepper***
Salt & black pepper
4 tb Italian parsley, chopped
4 tb Cilantro, chopped
2 tb Lemon juice*
8 tb Olive oil
4 sm Garlic cloves, chopped
1/2 ts Sweet red pepper**
Mix together all the ingredients & let sit for a while to allow the
flavours to marry.  The use.

* Or replace with wine vinegar ** I use Hungarian paprika, it's
sweeter than Spanish *** I use cayenne. You need the hot to contrast
with the sweet.

Robert Carrier, "Taste of Morocco"
