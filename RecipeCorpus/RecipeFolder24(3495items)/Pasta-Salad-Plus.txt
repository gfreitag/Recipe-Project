Title: Pasta Salad Plus
Categories: Salad, Seafood, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk Louis Kemp Crab Delights
Flakes or chunks (8 oz)
1 pk Pasta salad mix (9-11 oz)
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 t  Dried basil leaves, crushed
Prepare pasta salad mix according to package directions. Combine
mayonnaise and basil; stir in pasta mixture and Crab Delights. Chil
at least 1 hour before serving.

285 calories per serving.
