Title: Lemon Vinaigrette
Categories: Dressing, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
3/4 t  Freshly-ground black pepper
1 c  Olive oil
1 md Lemon
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar OR
-red-wine vinegar
1 t  Dijon mustard
PREPARATION:  Squeeze 1/4 cup lemon juice into a small mixing bowl.
Whisk in vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper.  Slowly whisk in the
olive oil. (Can cover and refrigerate for up to 1 week.) Makes 1 1/2
cups [COOKS; Jul/Aug 1988] Posted by Fred Peters.
