Title: Baked Orange Squash
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Grated orange peel
1 tb Plus
1 ts Reduced calorie margarine
4 ts Firmly packed brown sugar
2 c  Cooked, mashed, butternut
-or acorn squash
1/4 c  Frozen orange juice
-concentrate, thawed
1 ds Ground ginger
Preheat oven to 375. In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients, mixing
well. Place mixture in a 1-quart baking dish that has been sprayed
with a nonstick cooking spray. Bake, uncovered, 20 minutes.

Per serving: 2g protein, 2g fat, 19g carb., 48mg sodium, 0mg chol.,
110 calories.
