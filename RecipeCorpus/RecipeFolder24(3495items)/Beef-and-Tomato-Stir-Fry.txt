Title: Beef and Tomato Stir Fry
Categories: Asian, Beef
Yield: 1 servings
2    Tomatoes, cut into wedges
1    Green pepper, sliced
1/2 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1 c  Beef broth
1 c  Uncooked rice
1 lb Beef, cut into strips
3 tb Cornstarch
3 tb Soy sauce
1 lg Onion, sliced and chopped
1    Clv garlic, chopped
Mix beef strips with cornstarch and soy sauce.  Let stand 15 minuts.
In the meantime, start rice.  Slice vegetables.  Heat vegetable oil
in a wok or large skillet.  Add meat and stir fry until browned.
Remove meat. Add chopped onions, garlic and peppers to the skillet.
Stir fry for about 2 minutes.  Add tomatoes and mushrooms and stir
fry for another minute or 2. Return meat to pan, add beef broth.
Cook gently for 5 minutes. To serve, top fluffy rice with the beef
mixture.

Serves 4
