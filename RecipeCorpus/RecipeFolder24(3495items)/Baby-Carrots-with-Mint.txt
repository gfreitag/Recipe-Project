Title: Baby Carrots with Mint
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Minced fresh ginger
4 c  Baby carrots
1/4 c  Minced fresh mint
Steam the carrots over boiling water until tender, 3-5 minutes.
Remove from the heat, drain and save 2 tb. of the cooking water.

In a bowl, while the carrots are still hot, toss with the cooking
broth, mint, and ginger. Serve immediately.

Per Serving: 12g protein, .2g/4% fat, 12g carb., 0mg chol., 50
calories. Submitted By CAROLYN SHAW   On   09-29-94
