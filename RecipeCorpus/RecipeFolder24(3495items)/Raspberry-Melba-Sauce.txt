Title: Raspberry Melba Sauce
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Cornstarch
6 ts Sugar
1 c  Raspberries, fresh or frozen
2 tb Water
In a small saucepan, combine raspberries and 1 tb of the water. Bring
to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until the berries start to fall
apart. Dissolve the cornstarch in the rest of the water. add to
berries and cook until mixture thickens, about 1 minute. Remove from
heat. Strain mixture and stir in sugar. Serve over any fruit or
frozen dietary dessert.

Per serving: 2g protein, 0g fat, 8g carb., 0mg sodium, omg chol., 88
calories.
