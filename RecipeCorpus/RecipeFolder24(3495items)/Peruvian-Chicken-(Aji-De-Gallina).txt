Title: Peruvian Chicken (Aji De Gallina)
Categories: Main dish, So. america, Poultry, Rice
Yield: 1 batch
4 sl Bread
1 lg Can evaporated milk
4 oz Grated Parmesan
1 lg Onion; finely chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
4 lb Chicken
1/2 c  Oil
1/2 lb Nuts; chopped
-- such as walnuts
2 ts Ground garlic
6    Chiles; liquidized
MMMMM-------------------------GARNISHES------------------------------
Boiled rice
-- to serve 8
6    Potatoes
Olives
Hard boiled eggs
Boil chicken in salted water.  Bone and break into bite size pieces.

In a saucepan, heat oil and fry onion, garlic and chiles. Salt and
pepper to taste.  Fry until golden.  Add bread, soaked in chicken
broth, crusts removed.  Cook slowly for 10 minutes; then add chopped
nuts, grated cheese and chopped chicken.  Two or 3 minutes before
serving, add evaporated milk.

Decorate with halved potatoes, eggs quartered lengthwise, and olives.
Serve with boiled rice.

Yield: 8 servings.
