The number of vegetables will depend on the number of guests.  Peel
cucumber and cut in fairly fine rings.  Sprinkle with about 1
teaspoon of salt per cucumber.  Place in refrigerator 1 hour.  Slice
the onion finely in rings.  Marinate in vinegar about 1 hour.  Slice
tomatoes and green peppers (take out seeds) in rings. To make
dressing: dissolve and mix ingredients, Take cucumber and squeeze out
water (in hands). Drain the onion and break up into separate rings.
Put all vegetables in a bowl and mix with dressing, being careful not
to break tomatoes. Refrigerate for an hour before serving. Dust with
paprika if desired. Fedor Mediansky. Bon-Appetit, Exec.Chef. Magnus
