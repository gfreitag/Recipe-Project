Title: Orange Sherbet Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
8 oz Pineapple, crushed; drained
1 c  Marshmallows, miniature
1/2 pt Cream, whipping; whipped
6 oz Gelatin, orange-flavored
1 c  Water; boiling
1 pt Sherbet, orange; softened
11 oz Oranges, mandarin; drained
Dissolve gelatinin boiling water.  Add sherbet and stir until melted;
mixture will be thick.  Add remaining ingredients, stirring until well
blended.  Pour into a 2-quart mold or individual molds.  Refrigerate
8 to 10 hours before serving.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June 1973. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
