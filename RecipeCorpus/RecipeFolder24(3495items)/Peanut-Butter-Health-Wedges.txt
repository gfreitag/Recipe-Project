Title: Peanut Butter Health Wedges
Categories: Sandwich, Snack
Yield: 1 servings
Few chopped walnuts
2    Slices buttered bread
1 tb Peanut butter
1 tb Chopped celery
2 tb Grated cheese
Spread the unbuttered side of a bread with peanut butter.   Place the
bread (buttered side down) onto snack maker.  Sprinkle celery,
walnuts and cheese over peanut butter.  Top with a slice of bread
(buttered side up), latch lid and toast for 2 minutes.  Serve hot or
cold. Submitted By GAIL SHIPP   On   10-12-94
