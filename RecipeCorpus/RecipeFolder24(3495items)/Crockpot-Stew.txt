Title: Crockpot Stew
Categories: Crockpot
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Bouillon
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Cream of celery soup
(do not add water to soup
1 lb Beef stew meat
2 lg Potatoes
1 lg Onion, quartered
2 ea Carrots, sliced
2    Celery, sliced
Layer all ingredients as listed in crock pot.  Cook all day on low.
May be baked in oven for 5 hours at 225 deg. Source: SHARON BARNARD
Date: 09-17-94
