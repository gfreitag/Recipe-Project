Title: Sweet Onion Shrimp Balls
Categories: Appetizer, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
Salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 lb Shrimp
1 md Sweet onion
1    Egg
1 md Potato
oil for frying

Grind onions, potatoes and shrimp.  Stir in eggs, salt, and pepper.
Batter must be thick.  Drop by teaspoons into hot oil and fry until
brown.

Source: Rock Springs Sweet Onion Festival Cook Book 1992
