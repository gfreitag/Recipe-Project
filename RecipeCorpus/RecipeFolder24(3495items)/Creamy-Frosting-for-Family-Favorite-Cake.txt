Title: Creamy Frosting for Family Favorite Cake
Categories: Diabetic, Dessert, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 tb Diet margarine, melted
1/2 c  Equal-Sugar twin
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Non-fat cottage cheese,
-sieve
1/8 ts Salt
Mix all ingredients.  Beat until smooth.  Spread on Cake.  Frosts one
cake.

Date: 11-18-93
From: Jeffrey Dean
