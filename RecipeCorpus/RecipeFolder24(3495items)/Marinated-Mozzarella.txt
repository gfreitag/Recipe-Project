Title: Marinated Mozzarella
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Cheese/eggs, Salad, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Water
2 ts Dried oregano
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Dried thyme
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Sugar
8 oz Part skim mozzarella
- cut into 1/2-inch cubes
2 tb Plus (see next line)
2 ts -vegetable oil
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
3 tb Red wine vinegar
Place cheese in 1 quart jar. Combine remaining ingredients in a
saucepan, bring to a boil and boil 10 minutes. Cool and pour over
cheese. Store in fridge up to two weeks.

Per serving: 7g protein, 9g fat, 2g carb., 132mg sodium, 16mg chol.,
117 calories.
