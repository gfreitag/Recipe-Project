Title: Dynamite Chili with Beans
Categories: Chili
Yield: 4 servings
16 oz Tomatoes, whole drained
2 T  Chili powder
1 x  Green chile, jalapeno or
1/2 x  Green pepper,seeded chopped
1 x  Garlic clove, minced
2/3 lb Boneless pork, 1/2" cubes
1/3 lb Beef stew meat, 1/2" cubes
2 c  Water
1/2 c  Pinto beans, soaked
1 T  Oil or bacon drippings
1 x  Onion, sliced
1 t  Oregano, dried, crumbled
2 t  Cumin
2 T  Tortilla flour
1/3 c  Dry red wine
* Mixed with Water to form a paste (optional) Combine Water and Beans
in medium saucepan and bring to bOil over medium high heat. Reduce
heat and simmer until tender, about 1 hour. Heat Oil in large skillet
over med-high heat.  Add Onion, Green Pepper, and Garlic. Saute until
tender. Transfer to Dutch Oven and set aside. Add Pork and Beef to
same skillet. Brown well. Stir into vegetables in Dutch Oven. Add
Beans and their liquid along with Tomatoes and seasonings. Mix well,
cover and simmer 1 hour. Add Wine and cook, uncovered, 30 minutes.
Season with Salt and Pepper. If mixture is too liquid, stir in some
of the tortilla flour paste to thicken. Fred Drexel, 1981 World
Champion Chili
