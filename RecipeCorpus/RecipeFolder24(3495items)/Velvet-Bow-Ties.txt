Title: Velvet Bow Ties
Categories: Pasta, Cheese/eggs, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Milk
2 c  Velveeta process cheese
-product, cubed
MMMMM----------------1 CUP EACH: JULIENNED CARROT---------------------
2 T  Fresh basil, chopped
3 c  Hot cooked bow tie shaped
-pasta, drained
heat or microwave cheese and milk together until melted.  Blend
together until smooth.  Loghtly toss sauce with vegetables, pasta and
basil. Makes 4 servings. Origin: Kraft What's Cooking magazine, 9th
issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
