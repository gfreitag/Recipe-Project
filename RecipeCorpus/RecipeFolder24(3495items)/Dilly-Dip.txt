Title: Dilly Dip
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dip, Snack
Yield: 8 servings
1/8 ts Celery seed
1 ts Dried chives
1 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
1 ts Dill weed
1 tb Minced onion flakes
Combine all ingredients and chill to blend flavors.

Per serving: 2g protein, 0g fat, 2g carb., 20mg sodium, 2mg chol., 20
calories.
