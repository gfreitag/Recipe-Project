Title: Zucchini Parmesan
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt
ds Pepper
2 tb Cheese, Parmesan; grated
4 sm Squash, zucchini; thinly
-sliced
2 tb Butter (or marg.)
Put zucchini, butter, and seasonings in skillet.  Cover and cook
slowly for 5 minutes.  Uncover and cook, turning slices, until barely
tender (about 5 minutes more).  Sprinkle with cheese; toss.
