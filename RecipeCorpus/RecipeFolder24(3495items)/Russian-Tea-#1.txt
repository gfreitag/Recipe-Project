Title: Russian Tea #1
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 1 batch
1 c  Instant tea
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground cloves
Dash salt
1 3/4 c  Tang (orange drink mix)
1 pk (1/2 oz) lemonade,
-unsweetened
1 c  Sugar (optional)
Mix ingredients.

Use 2 teaspoons per serving.
