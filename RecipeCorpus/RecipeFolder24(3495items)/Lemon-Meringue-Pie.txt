Title: Lemon Meringue Pie
Categories: Diabetic, Dessert, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Lemon juice
4    Egg whites (meringue)
4 pk Equal (meringue)
1 1/2 ts Cream of tartar (meringue)
1 1/2 c  Water
4 pk Equal (or 1T sweetener)
4 1/2 ts Cornstarch
2    Eggs
Cook all ingredients in double boiler until thickened.
Pour into pie shell. Top with meringue which has been beaten stiff.
Bake 450F for 15 minutes or until golden brown.
