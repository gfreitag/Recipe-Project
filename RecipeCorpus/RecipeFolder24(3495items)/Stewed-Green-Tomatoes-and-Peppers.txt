Title: Stewed Green Tomatoes and Peppers
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
6 lg Green tomatoes
pn Salt to taste
pn Pepper to taste
1 lg Onion, sliced
3 tb Butter
2 md Green or Red Sweet Peppers
Cook onion in butter in skillet for e=three minutes; do not brown.

Wash core peppers and tomatoes, cut into eighths lengthwise.  Add to
onion. Simmer covered, stirring often, for 20 minutes or until
peppers are just tender.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
