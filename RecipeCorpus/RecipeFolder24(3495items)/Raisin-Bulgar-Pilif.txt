Title: Raisin Bulgar Pilif
Categories: Diabetic, Nuts/grains, Side dish, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
-might try 1/2 n 1/2
1/4 ts Salt;
1/4 ts Ground cumin;
1 1/3 c  Bulgur;
1 1/3 c  Boiling water
4 tb Golden or dark raisins;
Place bulgur in a medium-size bowl and cover with boiling water.  Add
remaining ingredients and stir to combine.  Let stand 30 minutes until
water is absorbed. Fluff and serve warm. This was a serving for 4, but
change it becauce I wanted to frezze some, but instead the
grandchildren came and stayed for supper. Food Exchange per serving:
1 STARCH/BREAD EXCHANGE  CAL: 89; CHO: 0mg; CAR: 20g; PRO: 3g; SOD:
57mg; FAT: 0g;
