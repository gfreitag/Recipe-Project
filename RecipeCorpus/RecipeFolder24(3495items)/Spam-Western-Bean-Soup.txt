Title: Spam Western Bean Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Spam, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
2 t  Worcestershire sauce
2 t  Prepared mustard
2 cn Pinto beans, rinsed and
-drained (15 1/2 oz)
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(12 oz)
2 tb Chopped parsley
1 c  Chopped onion
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Sliced carrots
3 cn Condensed chicken broth
-(14 1/2 oz)
1/3 c  Chili sauce
3 tb Firmly packed brown sugar
3 tb Cider vinegar
In 5-quart saucepan, saute onion in oil until golden. Stir in carrots,
chicken broth, tomatoes, chili sauce, brown sugar, vinegar,
Worcestershire sauce, and mustard. Mash half of beans with fork; add
mashed beans and whole beans to soup. Blend well. Bring to a boil.
Cover. Reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes or until carrots are tender.
Stir in SPAM and parsley. Simmer 2 minutes.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 331; Protein 22 grams;
Carbohydrate 34 grams; Fat 13 grams; Cholesterol 46 milligrams;
Sodium 2263 milligrams.
