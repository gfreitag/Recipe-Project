Title: Vegetable Juice Cocktail
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 4 servings
1    Canned beet; diced
1/2 c  Beet liquid
1 1/2 c  -Ice water
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
3    Carrots; peeled & chopped
3    Radishes
3    Scallions
1/4 c  Celery leaves
1/4 c  Watercress
Puree all ingredients in a blender.  Cover and refrigerate for one
hour, strain, mix in salt & pepper and serve.
