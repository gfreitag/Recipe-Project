Title: Cranberry Cherry Tea Sparkler
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/3 c  Cherry juice or cider
1 1/3 c  Water, sparkling
1 1/3 c  Tea concentrate (qv)
1/3 c  Cranberries, dried
Combine tea concentrate and cranberries in a pan and bring to boil.
Cover and chill until cold, at least 1 hour.  Pour through fine
strainer into a large pitcher, discard cranberries, add juice and
sparkling water and mix well.  Pour over ice to serve.
Source:  Sunset Magazine, August 1994
Date: 09-14-94
From: Karl Lembke
