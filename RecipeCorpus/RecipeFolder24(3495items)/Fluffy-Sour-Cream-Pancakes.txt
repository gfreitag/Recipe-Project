Title: Fluffy Sour Cream Pancakes
Categories: Breakfast, Pancakes
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Sugar
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Butter (or marg.); softened
3    Eggs; separated
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
1 ts Soda
1 1/4 c  Flour, all-purpose
Beat egg yolks well.  Combine sour cream and soda; stir into egg
yolks. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt; stir into sour
cream mixture.  Add butter, and beat on medium speed of electric
mixer 30 seconds.  Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form; fold
whites into batter.

For each pancake, pour about 1/4 cup batter onto a hot, lightly
greased griddle.  Turn pancakes when tops are covered with bubbles
and edges look cooked.
