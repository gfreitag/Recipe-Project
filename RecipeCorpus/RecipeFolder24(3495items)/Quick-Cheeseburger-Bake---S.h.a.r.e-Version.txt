Title: Quick Cheeseburger Bake - S.h.a.r.e Version
Categories: Casserole, Meat, Burgers
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Carrots, miniatures
1/4 c  Milk
2 c  Bisquick
3/4 c  Water
1 c  Cheddar Cheese, shredded
2    Beef Patties, broken up in
-small bits
3/4 c  Onion, chopped
1 cn Broccoli Cheese Soup
1 c  Broccoli
1 c  Cauliflower
Heat oven to 400Ã¸F. Generously grease a 9 x 13 x 2" baking dish.
Cook beef and onion in a skillet until beef is brown.  Drain. Stir in
soup, vegetables, and milk.  Stir baking mix and water in the baking
dish until moistened.  Spread in pan evenly.  Spread beef mixture
over batter, sprinkle with cheese.  Bake 30 minutes.  Makes 8 to 10
servings.

This recipes uses S.H.A.R.E. items received in the August 1994 food
package.  The broccoli cheese soup and the fresh veggies make the
difference in taste.
