Title: Chicken Honey Nut Stir Fry
Categories: Poultry, Chicken, Vegetable, Asian, Stir fry
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Oil, vegetable
2 lg Carrots, diagonally cut
2 ea Celery stalks
1/2 c  Cashews or peanuts
Rice
1 lb Chicken breasts, boned, skin
3/4 c  Orange juice
1/3 c  Honey, liquid
3 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Cornstarch
1/2 ts Ginger, ground
Cut chicken into thin strips, set aside.  In a small bowl, combine
juice, honey, soy sauce cornstarch and ginger,mix well. Heat 1 tbsp
oil in a wok, add carrots and celery, stir fry about 3 minutes,
remove,set aside. add remaining oil, add meat stir fry  3 minutes,
add vegetables, add sauce and nuts, cook and stir until sauce is
thickened. servie over rice Submitted By KATHERINE WALDEN   On
