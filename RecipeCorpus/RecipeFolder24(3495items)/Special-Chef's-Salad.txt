Title: Special Chef's Salad
Categories: Salad, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable, Dressing
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 lb Cooked Ham; Cut Into 1/2"
Strips, 1 cup
3 oz Cream Cheese; Cubed, 1 Pk
2 lg Eggs; Hard Cooked, Chopped
1 sm Onion; Sliced
2 c  Lettuce; Bite Sized Pieces
2 c  Spinach; Bite Sized Pieces
1/2 c  Green Pepper; Chopped
1/2 c  Carrots; Peeled & Shredded
1/2 c  Cucumber; Halved & Sliced
MMMMM------------------OIL AND VINEGAR DRESSING-----------------------
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 tb Ketchup
3/4 ts Sugar
Combine the lettuce, spinach, green pepper, carrots, cucumber, ham,
cream cheese, eggs and onion in a large bowl; cover and refrigerate.
Prepare the Oil And Vinegar Dressing; cover and refrigerate.

OIL AND VINEGAR DRESSING:

Combine all of the ingredients in a glass jar, cover and shake well.

Makes 3/4 Cup.

TO SERVE:

Pour the dressing over the vegetables, toss gently to coat and serve.
