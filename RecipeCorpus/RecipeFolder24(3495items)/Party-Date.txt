Title: Party Date
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, British
Yield: 25 servings
1 1/2 c  Shortening
6 ea Eggs
3 T  Cocoa
4 1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Chocolate bits
3 c  Dates, cut up
3 c  Boiling water
3 t  Baking soda
4 c  Sugar
1 c  Chopped nuts
Mix dates and water and allow to cool. Combine 3c sugar, shortening,
eggs, cocoa and flour. Add baking soda to date mixture and combine.
Pour into 2 greased loaf pans and sprinkle with topping of 1c sugar,
nuts and chocolate bits. Bake at 350 degrees for 45-50 minutes. Slice
and serve hot. Garnish with whipped cream.

Mrs. Henry D. Chaplin
