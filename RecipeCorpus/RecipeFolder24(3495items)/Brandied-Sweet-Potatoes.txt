Title: Brandied Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish, Sweetpotato
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Seedless raisins (optional)
1/4 c  Cognac or other brandy
1/2 c  Chopped apple (optional)
4    Medium sweet potatoes
2/3 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Butter
1. Wash potatoes; don't peel. Boil in water (enough to cover potatoes)
until barely soft (15 min).  Drain, cool, peel. Slice into a greased
casserole. 2. Preheat oven to moderate (350 degrees F). 3. Bring to a
boil brown sugar, water, butter, and raisins. Add cognac and pour
mixture over potatoes. 4. Bake uncovered, 30 min, basting several
times with syrup in casserole.
