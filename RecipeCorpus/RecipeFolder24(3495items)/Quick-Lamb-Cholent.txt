Title: Quick Lamb Cholent
Categories: Lamb, Jewish, Beans, Meat
Yield: 8 servings
2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1/2 t  Ginger
3 c  Water
2 c  Dried lima beans
2    Lamb shanks
2    Onions, sliced
3 T  Fat
2    Cloves garlic, minced
Soak the beans overnight in water to cover. Bring to a boil and cook
30 minutes.  Drain.

Brown the lamb and onions in the fat.  Add the garlic, salt, pepper,
ginger and water.  Cover and cook over low heat 2-1/2 hours or until
lamb and beans are tender.  Add a little water if necessary.  Serves
6 - 8

From: The Art of Jewish Cooking by Jennie Grossinger Shared By: Pat
