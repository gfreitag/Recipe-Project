Title: Strawberry Yogurt Cooler
Categories: Diabetic, Fruit, Dessert, Snack
Yield: 2 sweet ones
1 c  Sparkling mineral water;
Or sletzer or club soda
1 c  Strawberries; sliced
1/2 c  Plain nonfat yogurt;
Combine ingredients in a blender or food processor, process until
smooth. Pour into chilled glasses and serve. Food Exchange per
serving: 1/2 NONFAT MILK EXCHANGE; CAL: 54; PRO: 4g; CAR: 10g; FAT:
TRACE; CHO: 1mg; SOD: 70;

Souce: Joslin Diabetes Gourmet Cookbook by Bonnie Sander Polin, Ph.D.
and Frances Towner Giedt. Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion
and her Meal-Master.
