Title: Happy Accident Shrimp Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Casserole, Seafood, Rice, Clam/oyster
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 pk Green onion dip mix
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Herb-flavored dressing
3 tb Butter (or marg.); melted
2 c  Rice; cooked
2 c  Shrimp; cooked
3 tb Onion; minced
8 1/2 oz Green peas, with liquid
ds Black pepper
Combine all ingredients except dressing and butter.  Spoon mixutre
into 1-1/2 quart casserole dish.  Stir dressing in melted butter and
sprinkle over casserole.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 30
minutes. Remove from oven and cover casserole dish for a few minutes
before serving.
