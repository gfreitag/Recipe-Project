Title: Crown Jewel Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Water; boiling
1 1/2 c  Ginger ale
1 c  Grapes, seedless; sliced
1 c  Banana slices
1/2 c  Cherries, maraschino; sliced
1 pk Gelatin, unflavored
3/4 c  Water; cold
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
3 oz Cream cheese; softened
6 oz Gelatin, lemon-flavored
Soften gelatin in cold water; stir over low heat until dissolved.
Combine salad dressing and cream cheese, mixing until well blended.
Stir in softened gelatin and blend well.  Pour into a 2-quart mold;
chill until firm.

Dissolve lemon-flavored gelatin in boiling water; add ginger ale.
Chill until slighlty thickened.  Fold in grapes, banana slices, and
cherries. Pour over molded gelatin layer.  Chill until firm.
