Title: Mocha Spice Cake
Categories: 1941, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 cake
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/3 ts Salt
1 ts Soluable coffee or coffee
Extract
3/4 c  Sour milk
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 c  Butter or shortening
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  White sugar
2    Eggs
1 3/4 c  Pastry flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
Directions Cream until very soft. Mix then blend in both sugars.Cream
until light and fluffy. Beat well and add eggs. Sift, then measure
flour, then re-sift with baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon,
cloves, nutmeg and salt. Dissolve coffee in sour milk and add lemon
juice. Add the dry ingredients to the sugar mixtures alternately with
wet ingredients, combining thoroughly after each addition. Turn into
a well greased and floured 8" square pan. Bake in a moderate oven 350
degrees F for 45 to 50 minutes. From: Georgina Major
Date: 09-08-94
