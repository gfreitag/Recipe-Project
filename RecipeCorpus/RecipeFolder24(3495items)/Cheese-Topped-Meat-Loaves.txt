Title: Cheese Topped Meat Loaves
Categories: Meat, Meatloaf, Ground beef, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Milnot
2    Eggs
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 lb Ground beef
2 c  Soft bread crumbs
3/4 c  Chopped onion
Mix all of the above in one large bowl.  Shape into 8 loaves.  Place
in ungreased cake pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.  Top each
loaf with American cheese and bake until cheese is melted. Randy Rigg
