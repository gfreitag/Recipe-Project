Title: Pineapple Brownies
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Fruit, Chocolate
Yield: 16 brownies
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Sifted flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Margarine
2 oz Unsweetened baking chocolate
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs, well-beaten
8 oz Crushed pineapple, well
-drained
Preheat oven to 350oF. Melt shortening and chocolate together in top
of double boiler over hot water. Blend in sugar. Remove from heat;
blend in eggs, pineapple and vanilla. Sift flour, baking powder,
baking soda and salt together into a separate bowl. Add to pineapple
mixture; blend well. Stir in chopped nuts.

Pour batter into well-greased, 8" square pan. Bake for 35-40 minutes.
Cool in pan for 5 minutes; cut into squares, remove brownies from
pan, and cool on wire rack.

Source: Marian Hoffman, ed., Creative Cooking - Desserts. Typed by
Linda Howard.
