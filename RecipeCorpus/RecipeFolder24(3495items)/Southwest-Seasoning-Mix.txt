Title: Southwest Seasoning Mix
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Ground cumin
1 t  Cayenne pepper
1 t  Crushed red pepper flakes
1 t  Black pepper
1 t  Dried oregano
2 T  Chili powder
2 T  Paprika
1 T  Ground coriander
1 T  Garlic powder
1 T  Salt
Combine and store in an airtight jar or container.
