Title: Dijon Pork Steaks
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Pork, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Dijon mustard
1/8 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Vegetable oil
1 lb Boneless pork chops
3 tb Soy sauce
1/4 c  Dry white wine
2 ts Sherry extract
Combine marinade ingredients and pour over chops. Marinate several
hours or overnight. Broil or grill, turning occasionally and basting
until done to taste.

Per serving: 28g protein., 11g fat, 2g carb., 950mg sodium, 83mg
chol., 237 calories.
Date: 09-24-94
From: Carolyn Shaw
