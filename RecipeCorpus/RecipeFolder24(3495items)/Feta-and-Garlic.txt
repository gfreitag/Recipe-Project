Title: Feta and Garlic
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/3 lb Crumbled feta cheese
-salt/pepper
3    Roasted red peppers; peeled
-chopped
1/2 c  Almonds; toasted
8 lg Garlic cloves; minced
1 ts Red pepper flakes
Good served as vegetable dip, over baked potatoes, or a sauce for
grilled meats.
