Title: Meat Loaf
Categories: Diabetic, Main dish, Meat, Crockpot
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Green pepper; chopped finely
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dry Mustard;
1 tb Prepared horseradish; if
-desired
1/4 c  (1 md) egg;
2 c  Ground round;
3 sl Bread; cubed fine;
1/4 c  Catup;
1/3 c  Onion; chopped finely
Preheat oven to 400F. Mix all ingredients well.  Form into a loaf.
Place in foil-lined baking pan and bake until down. Food Exchange per
serving: 1/2 STARCH/BREAD EXCHANGE + 3 MEAT EXCHANGES +

Source Recipes for Diabetics by Billie Little and Penny L. Thorup
