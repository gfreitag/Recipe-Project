Title: Salmon Hash
Categories: Tex/mexican, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Coriander
4 tb Oil
4 tb Cilantro, diced
4 tb Green or red salsa
8    Tortillas, flour
8 tb Yogurt, non fat
30 oz Salmon, canned
1    Chili,serrano, or hot
1/2 c  Onion, red, diced
4 tb Bread crumbs
1 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Cloves
rinse salmon and remove skin.  Cut chili in half and remove seeds and
membrane.  Turn on food processor, feed chunks of onion and chili,
until coarsely minced. add salmon, bread crumbs, spices and process
until paste is made.  if necessary, add more bread crumbs to make a
patty tht will hold its shape.  Shape into 8 patties. fry in oil
until browned on each side. serve patties on warm tortillas with
salsa, garnished with yogurt and cilantro leaves.
