Title: Vegan Pumpkin Pie
Categories: Dessert, Pie/tart, Vegetarian, Pumpkin
Yield: 1 pie
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Ginger
1    Unbaked crust of your choice
10 oz Firm silken tofu
15 oz Canned pumpkin
1/2 c  Fruit source* or
-- granulated date sugar
1/3 c  Oil*
2 tb Molasses
*Note that this is even better (but more expensive) if you use 2/3 cup
maple syrup instead of the fruit source and reduce the oil to a scant
1/4 cup.

Blend all ingredients, pour into pie shell and bake for 1 hour at 350
F. or until done.
