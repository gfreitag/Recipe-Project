Title: Lobio
Categories: Ussr, Beans, Vegetarian, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Paprika
Pinch cayenne pepper
Salt and fresh grd. pepper
-to taste
225 g  Red kidney beans, soaked o/n
150 ml Olive oil
4 x  Cloves of garlic, chopped
120 g  Walnuts,roughly crushed
Cook the beans in plenty of boiling water for 45 mins. or until
tender. Drain. Heat half the olive oil, throw in half the garlic,
cook for a moment then add the beans and half the walnuts. Heat
together gently and season well with the paprika, cayenne, salt &
pepper.

Meanwhile, put the remaining oil, garlic and walnuts in a food
processor. Blend to a thick paste and when the beans are hot, stir
this in. Can be served hot or chilled for a bean salad. From: The
Adventurous Vegetarian by Colin Spencer Typed by: Joell Abbott 10/94
