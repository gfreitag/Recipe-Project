Title: Easy Teriyaki Ribs
Categories: Meat, Pork, Asian, Japanese
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Teriyaki bottled sauce
1/4 c  Orange marmalade
3 lb Spareribs
Salt and pepper to taste
Preheat oven to 325F.   Lightly sprinkle salt and pepper on pork
sideribs, cut into 2 rib portions. Place on rack in foil lined
broiler. Bake for 45 minutes.  Mis teriyaki sauce and marmalade,
brush both sides or ribs with mixture.  bake 30 minutes longer,
turning often and brush often with sauce. Submitted By KATHERINE
WALDEN   On   10-04-94
