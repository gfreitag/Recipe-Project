Title: Spicy Jalapeno Olives - Country Living
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 7 appetizer
1 sm Green jalapeno pepper,
-sliced
2    Cloves garlic, crushed
Fresh red and green jalapeno
-peppers (opt.)
1 c  (about 28) brine-cured
-fresh green olives or
-Spanish-style olives,
-drained
1 T  Olive oil
1 sm Red jalapeno pepper, sliced
1. In medium-size bowl, soak olives in cold water to cover 1 hour to
reduce their saltiness, changing water twice.

2. Meanwhile, in 1-quart saucepan, beat oil over medium heat. Add
sliced red and green jalapenos and garlic; saute 1 minute. Remove
from heat and let stand until cool while olives are soaking.

3. Drain olives and add to jalapeno mixture. Toss until well mixed.
Transfer olive mixture to small serving bowl. Cover and refrigerate
overnight to allow flavors to blend. Return olives to room temperature
before serving. Garnish with fresh jalapenos, if desired. These
olives are best consumed within a week.
