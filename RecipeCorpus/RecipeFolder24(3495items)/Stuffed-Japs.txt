Title: Stuffed Japs
Categories: Tex/mexican, Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
1 sl Bacon
6 lg Pickled Jalapenos
6 T  Monteray Jack Cheese
Slice pepers and remove seeds. Stuff with cheese, sprinkle with fried
bacon bits. Place under broiler, in oven, or on your grill. Anything
to heat through and melt cheese. Spicy HOT !! Very good. From, Lee
