Title: The Original Baked Spam
Categories: Main dish, Spam
Yield: 6 servings
1 t  Water
1 t  Prepared mustard
1/2 t  Vinegar
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat (12 oz)
Whole cloves
1/3 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
Heat oven to 375'F. Place SPAM on rack in shallow baking pan. Score
surface; stud with cloves. combine brown sugar, water, mustard, and
vinegar, stirring until smooth. Brush glaze over SPAM. Bake 20
minutes, basting often. Slice to serve.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 156; Protein 9 grams;
Carbohydrate 12 grams; Fat 8 grams; Cholesterol 45 milligrams; Sodium
575 milligrams.
