Title: Mock Filet Mignon
Categories: Main dish, Burgers, Sthrn/livng, Beef
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Soysauce
1/4 ts Pepper
6 sl Bacon
1 1/2 lb Ground chuck
2 oz Mushroom stems/pieces;
-drained and finely chopped
1 tb Onion; minced
Combine first 5 ingredients; mix lightly.  Shape mixture into 6
patties 1-1/2" thick.

Wrap 1 slice bacon around each patty; secure with wooden pick.  Place
on a broiler rack; broil 3 to 6 minutes on each side, depending on
desired degree of doneness.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in the 1970s. Typos by
