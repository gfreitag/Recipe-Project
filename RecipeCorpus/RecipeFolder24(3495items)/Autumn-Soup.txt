Title: Autumn Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable, Ground beef
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Salt
1 ts Kitchen bouquet
1    Bay leaf
1/8 ts Basil
1    Can stewed tomatoes w/juice
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Onion, chopped
1 c  Carrots, chopped
1 c  Celery, chopped w/leaves
1 c  Potato, chopped
3 c  Water
Brown beef and onion, drain. Place in large Dutch oven. Add the
remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and
simmer for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
