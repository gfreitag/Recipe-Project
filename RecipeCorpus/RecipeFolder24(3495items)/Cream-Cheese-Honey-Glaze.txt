Title: Cream Cheese Honey Glaze
Categories: Dessert, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
2-3 tbsp honey
1 t  Grated orange peel
4 ea Ounces cream cheese
1 1/2 t  Orange juice
In small bowl of mixer beat the cream cheese with the orange juice.
Gradually beat in 2-3 tbsp honey (or to taste).  Blend in the orange
peel. Origin:  Hearth and Home Companion Shared by: Sharon Stevens
