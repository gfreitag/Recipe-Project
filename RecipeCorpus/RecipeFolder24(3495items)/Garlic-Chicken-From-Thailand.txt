Title: Garlic Chicken From Thailand
Categories: Chicken, Thai, Main dish, Bbq/grill, Down under
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: AUSTRALIAN VOGUE WIN---------------------
1 T  Black peppercorns
4 ea Whole plants fresh coriander
4 T  Lemon juice
1 kg Chicken breasts (other
-pieces can be used)
4 ea Cloves garlic
2 t  Salt
Cut the chicken into serving pieces, or cut breasts in half.  Crush
garlic and coarsely crush peppercorns with a mortar and pestle or in
a blender. Finely chop the roots, stems and leaves of well-washed
coriander. Mix all the seasonings together and rub well into the
chicken pieces. Place pieces on a grill over glowing coals and cook
carefully, turning frequently until chicken is tender and the skin is
crisp. Serve with saffron rice. Fedor Mediansky Bon-Appetit,
Exec.Chef. Magnus Johansson
