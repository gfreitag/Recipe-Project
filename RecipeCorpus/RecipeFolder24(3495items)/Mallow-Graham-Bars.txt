Title: Mallow-Graham Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 18 servings
-coarsely chopped
1/2 c  Seedless raisins
1/2 c  Chopped dry roasted peanuts
2 T  BLUE BONNET Margarine
3 c  Marshmallows
1 pk HONEY MAID Honey Grahams,
In large saucepan over low heat, melt margarine. Add marshmallows;
stir until melted. Remove from heat; stir in graham crackers, raisins
and peanuts. Spread mixture into greased 9x9" baking pan. Cool 1 hour
or until firm. Cut into 18 bars. Store in airtight container.

Makes 18 bars.
