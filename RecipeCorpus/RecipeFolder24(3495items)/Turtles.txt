Title: Turtles
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 servings
Pecan Halves
Nestles Caramel as req
Dipping Chocolate
Spread waxed paper on countertop, assemble this on the paper. Place a
small piece of the caramel on a couple of the Pecan Halves, pour
dipping Chocolate over the nuts, allow a small part of the nuts to be
exposed. Submitted By PAUL HOWARD   On   10-16-94
