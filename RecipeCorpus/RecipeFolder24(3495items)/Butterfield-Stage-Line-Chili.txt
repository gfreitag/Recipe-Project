Title: Butterfield Stage Line Chili
Categories: Chili, Main dish, Game, Soup/stew, Tex/mexican
Yield: 25 servings
-BUTTERFIELD STAGELINE CHILI
1981 ea WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 T  Cumin
1 t  Oregano
1 oz Tequila
2 ea Jar (3oz) chili powder
4 md Onions, minced
1/4 c  Oil
2 lb Ground pork
1 cn (15oz) tomato sauce
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 t  Salt
1 T  Dry mustard
1 cn Beer
2 ea Beef bouillon cubes
10 lb Beef brisket, finely chopped
1 1/2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
7 cn Whole green chiles, minced
1 lb Whole tomatoes, finely
-chopped
Brown onions, and beef in oil. Stir in garlic, pork, chiles, cumin,
oregano, salt, dry mustard, tomato sauce, tomatoes, tequila, beer,
chili powder and bouillon cubes. Bring to boil, then reduce heat and
simmer 2-3 hours. Stir occasionally. Do not stir the last 30 min
before serving. Serves 25
