Title: Smoked Fish Spread
Categories: Seafood, Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Prepared mustard
1 ds Of Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1 1/2 lb Smoked fish
2 ts Minced onion
2 ts Finely chopped celery
1    Clove garlic, crushed
2 tb Finely chopped sweet pickle
Remove skins and bones from fish.  Flake fish well.  Combine with
remaining ingredients and chill.  Serve with assorted crackers or
party breads. Yield: 3-1/2 cups.

From: Southern Living, The Quick & Easy Cookbook Shared By: Pat
Stockett
