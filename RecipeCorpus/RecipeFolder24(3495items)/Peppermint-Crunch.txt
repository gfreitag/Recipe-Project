Title: Peppermint Crunch
Categories: Candy
Yield: 3 servings
1 lb Peppermint chips
2 lb White chocolate
Melt chocolate and stir in the peppermint chips.  Spread on waxed
paper and let stand until firm.  Break into pieces and store in
airtight container. Randy Rigg
