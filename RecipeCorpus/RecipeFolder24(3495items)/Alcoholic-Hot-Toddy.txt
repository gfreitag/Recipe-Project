Title: Alcoholic Hot Toddy
Categories: Beverage, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Stick cinnamon
Very hot water or tea
1/2 ea Lemon slice
1 1/2 oz Rum
1 t  Sugar
1 T  Lemon juice
Combine sugar, lemon juice and rum in a mug.  Fill mug with water or
tea. Add cinnamon stick and garnish with lemon. Origin: Alcohol
Recipe Booklet. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
