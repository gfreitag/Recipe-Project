Title: Barbecued Chicken and Prawns
Categories: Chicken, Bbq/grill, Down under, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: AUSTRALIAN VOGUE WIN---------------------
Lemons
Olive oil
Black pepper
Whole chicken breasts
Green peeled prawns
White wine
Marinate chicken and prawns in a mixture of white wine and lemon
juice for 1 hour before cooking.  Barbecue on hotplate moistened with
olive oil. Squeeze lemon over chicken and prawns while cooking and
season with freshly ground black pepper.  Prawns only take a few
minutes to cook. June Satterley. Bon-Appetit, Exec.Chef. Magnus
Johansson
