Title: Saucy Skillet Pasta
Categories: Main dish, Burgers, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Italian seasoning
8 oz Ziti; uncooked (or rigati or
-other tube pasta)
4 oz Mushrooms, sliced; drained
4 oz Cheese, mozzarella; shredded
1 lb Beef, ground; OR ground trky
15 oz Tomato sauce
15 oz Tomatoes, whole; undrained
-and broked up
1 pk Spaghetti sauce mix
Brown ground beef in large skillet over medium-high heat.  Drain fat.

Add tomato sauce, tomatoes, spaghetti sauce mix and Italian seasoning.
Bring to a boil.  Stir in pasta and mushrooms.  Cover and simmer 30
minutes or until pasta is tender, stirring occasionally.

Sprinkle cheese over top.  Cover and cook 2-3 minutes or until cheese
is melted.
