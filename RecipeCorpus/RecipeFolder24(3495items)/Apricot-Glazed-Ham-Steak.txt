Title: Apricot Glazed Ham Steak
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Pork, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb White wine
1 ts Dijon mustard
12 oz Boneless ham steak
2 tb Reduced calorie apricot jam
Score one side of ham in a crisscross pattern and place in a shallow
baking pan. Combine remaining ingredients and spread evenly over ham.
Broil 5 inches from heat until hot and bubbly, about 3 minutes.

Per serving: 18g protein, 5g fat, 4g carb., 1061mg sodium, 45mg
chol., 137 calories.
Date: 09-24-94
