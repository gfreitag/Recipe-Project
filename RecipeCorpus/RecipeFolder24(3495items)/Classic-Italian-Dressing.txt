Title: Classic Italian Dressing
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dressing, Salad
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/2 ts Dried mustard
2 ts Parmesan cheese
1 ts Sugar
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 ts Minced onion flakes
1 ts Dried oregano
Combine all ingredients in a jar and shake well. Chill to blend
flavors.

Per serving: 0g protein, 9g fat, 1g carb., 5mg sodium, 0mg chol., 85
calories.

From: Carolyn Shaw
