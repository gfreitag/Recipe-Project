Title: Baked Herbed Fish
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Finely chopped onions
1 cl Garlic, minced
1 1/2 lb Fish steaks
1 ea 8oz can tomato sauce
1/4 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Dried basil
Preheat oven to 350. Place fish in a shallow baking pan that has been
sprayed with a nonstick cooking spray. Combine remaining ingredients,
spread evenly over fish. Bake, uncovered, 40 minutes.

Per serving: 33g protein, 1g fat, 6g carb., 594mg sodium, 97mg chol.,
174 calories.
