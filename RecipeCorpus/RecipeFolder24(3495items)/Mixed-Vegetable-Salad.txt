Title: Mixed Vegetable Salad
Categories: Salad, Thai, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Cilantro, chopped
4 tb Peanuts, roasted & crushed
2 ea Red chilies, julienned
1/4 c  Lime juice
1/8 c  Soy sauce
2 ts Sugar
1 sm Head letuce, diced
1 ea Cucumber, peeled & sliced
1 sm Green papaya, seeded &
-- julienned
2 ea Tomatoes, cut into wedges
4 tb Green onions, chopped
In a large salad bowl, arrange in layers the lettuce, cucumber,
papaya, tomatoes, onion & cilantro.  In a small bow, mix together the
peanuts, chilies, lime juice & soy sauce.  Pour over the salad, toss
& serve.

Eng Tie Ang, "Delightful Thai Cooking"
Date: 09-21-94
From: Mark Satterly
