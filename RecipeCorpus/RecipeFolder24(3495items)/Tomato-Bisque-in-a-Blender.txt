Title: Tomato Bisque in a Blender
Categories: Main dish, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
2 lg Garlic cloves, crushed
1 1/2 t  Prepared horseradish
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
1    Avocado, cubed (opt)
1 cn Peeled Italian tomatoes
-(28 oz)
-OR
4 lg Fresh tomatoes
1 c  Plain non-fat yogurt
1/4 t  Hot sauce, such as Tabasco
Puree all ingredients but avocado in blender or food processor. Serve
chilled or at room temperature, ladled over avocado cubes, with
low-fat tortilla chips.

Per serving: 156 calories, 6 grams protein, 18 grams carbohydrates, 7
grams fiber, 8 grams fat (1 gram saturated), 640 milligrams sodium.
