Title: Pace Cream Cheese Dip
Categories: Dip
Yield: 1 servings
4 tb Pace Picante Sauce
8 oz Cream Cheese
Soften cream cheese in a bowl.  Stir in Pace Picante Sauce.  Add lemon
juice, salt and pepper to taste.  Blend well.  Serve with corn chips
or potato chips.  Add more Pace Picante Sauce for a hotter taste.

From: Pace booklet.
