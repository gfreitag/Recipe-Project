Title: Out of This World Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
3/4 c  Sugar
6    Bananas, sliced
1 c  Chopped pecans
2    Baked pie shells
1 cn Cherry pie filling
20 oz Can of pineapple
1 tb Corn starch
1 pk Strawberry jello
In a saucepan, combine pie filling, pineapple, corn starch, and
sugar. Cook until thick; add jello and cool.  Add bananas and nuts.
Pour into shells. Chill and serve with cream topping. Randy Rigg
