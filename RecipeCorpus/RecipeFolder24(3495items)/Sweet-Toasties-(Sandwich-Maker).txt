Title: Sweet Toasties (Sandwich Maker)
Categories: Snack, Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Flour
1 ts Cream of tartar
-non-stick cooking spray
-raspberry jam
2 tb Sugar
1    Egg
1/2 ts Baking powder
-pinch salt
-whipped cream
Place sugar, milk and egg in a bowl and beat 3-4 mins. Sift flour and
other dry ingredients.  Add to egg mixture and continue to beat until
the mixture slides off the back of spoon. Heat sandwich maker and
spray top and base. Spoon about 2 Tbs of the mixture into each
section of the sandwich maker. Lower the lid and cook 2-3 mins. or
until golden brown. Serve hot or cold with raspberry jam and whipped
cream.
