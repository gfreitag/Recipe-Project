Title: Cheese Onion Bread
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 c  Bisquick
1 c  Grated sharp cheese
1 tb Poppy seeds
1/2 c  Chopped onion
3 tb Melted butter
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Saute onion in 1 tablespoon of butter
until tender, but not clear.  Combine egg and milk; add to Bisquick
and stir only until dry ingredients are moist.  Stir in onion and 1/2
cup of cheese. Place in a greased 9x9 inch baking dish; top with
remaining cheese, poppy seeds, and remaining melted butter.  Bake for
30 minutes; cut into squares. I love this bread with chili,  with
BEANS! hehe Randy Rigg
