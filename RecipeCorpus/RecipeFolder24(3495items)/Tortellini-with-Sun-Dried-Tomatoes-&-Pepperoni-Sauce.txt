Title: Tortellini with Sun Dried Tomatoes & Pepperoni Sauce
Categories: Salad, Pasta
Yield: 8 servings
2/3 c  Olive oil
3 T  Onion; minced
1/2 lb Spinach tortellini
1/2 lb Plain tortellini
6 lg Basil leaves; fresh
1/4 lb Pepperoni; thinly sliced
1 c  Sun dried tomatoes; drained
1 T  Dijon mustard
2 cl Garlic
2 t  Lemon juice
1/2 t  Red pepper flakes
Cook tortellini together, rinse under cold water and drain.

Reserve a few pieces of pepperoni for garnish.  In food processor,
grind remainder of pepperoni, tomatoes, garlic, lemon juice and red
pepper flakes.  With motor running, add oil and blend well.  In
mixing bowl, toss onions, tortellini and sauce until well coated.

Garnish with basil leaves and additional pepperoni.  Serve at room
temperature.
