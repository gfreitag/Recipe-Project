Title: Raspberry Tapioca
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dessert, Fruit, Pudding
Yield: 4 servings
-tapioca
2 tb Plus (see next ingredient)
2 ts Sugar
2 c  Fresh or frozen unsweetened
-raspberries
3 tb Uncooked quick cooking
Palce berries in a blender container. Blend until smooth. Pour thru a
strainer. In a saucepan, combine berries with enough water to equal
two cups. Stir in tapioca and sugar. Let stand 5 minutes. Bring to a
boil, remove from heat. Cool in pan 20 minutes. Then stir and divide
among 4 custard cups. Chill

Per serving: 1g protein, 0g fat, 22g carb., 0mg sodium, 0mg chol., 87
calories.
