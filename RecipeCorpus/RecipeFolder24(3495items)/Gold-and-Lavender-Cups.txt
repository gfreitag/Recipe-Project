Title: Gold and Lavender Cups
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Crushed lavender blossoms
; (scant ts)
18    Dozen nasturtium blossoms
; rinsed and debugged
2 tb Nasturtium flowers, minced
1 tb Chive blossoms
; pulled from stems
1 ts Poppy seeds
3/4 c  Low-fat cottage cheese
Combine all ingredients, except nasturtium blossoms.  Carefully stuff
blossoms with cheese mixture.

Arrange on a platter covered with nasturtium leaves; garnish with
whole chive blossoms.

From Sage Cottage Herb Garden Cookbook by Dorry Baird Norris.
Chester, CT: The Globe Pequot Press, 1991.  Pp. 149-150.  ISBN
0-87106-239-9. Posted by Cathy Harned.
