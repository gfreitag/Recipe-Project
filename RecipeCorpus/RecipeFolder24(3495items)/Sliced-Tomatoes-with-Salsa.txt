Title: Sliced Tomatoes with Salsa
Categories: Dressing, Salsa/sauce, Salad
Yield: 2 servings
(or fresh parsley)
1/8 ts Ground cumin
1/8 ts Red pepper; crushed
1    Garlic clove; crushed
1 ts Cider vinegar
1 c  Alfalfa sprouts
2    Tomato; sliced
1/2 c  Green bell pepper; minced
1/8 c  Green onions; minced
1/2 tb Fresh cilantro; minced
Preparation Time: 6:10 Combine the salsa ingredients, stir well, and
chill for 3 to 6 hours, if possible. To serve, place some sprouts on
individual salad plates, arrange tomato slices over the sprouts, and
top with salsa.
