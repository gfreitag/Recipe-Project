Title: Marinated Cucumbers
Categories: Diabetic, Vegetable, Salad, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 t  Seasoned salt;
2 tb Olive Oil;
1/8 tb Pepper; coarsly ground
6 lg Cucumbers, sliced
1 lg Onion; chopped
3/4 c  Rice vinegar;
Combine all ingredients together in large bowl. Mix well. Cover.
Refrigerate four to six hours.  Serve with salad, or by itself.
