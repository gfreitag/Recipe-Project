Title: Yeast Free Chapati
Categories: Bread, Middle east
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
Water
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Cornmeal
Mix dry ingredients together.  Mix water into the flour mixture until
the dough rolls away from the sides of the bowl, but does not become
sticky. Flatten dough with your hands, and place chapati in an oiled
skillet. Cook 10 minutes over medium heat.  Flip, and cook an
additional 10 minutes. Origin: Yeast Free Living. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Oct/94.
