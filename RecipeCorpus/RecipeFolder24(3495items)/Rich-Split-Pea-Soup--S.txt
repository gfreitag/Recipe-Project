Title: Rich Split Pea Soup -S
Categories: Main dish, Vegetable, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------BRING TO BOIL IN 4 QUART POT---------------------
1 sl Kombu; 3", optional
6 c  ;water
1 c  Vegetable broth
MMMMM----------------ADD & SIMMER 40 MINUTES MORE---------------------
1 md Onion; diced
2 c  Peas; dried split
2 md Carrots; diced
MMMMM-----------------ADD & SIMMER 5-10 MINUTES----------------------
Tamari; to taste
8 oz Seitan; cubed
If adding kombu and it didn't dissolve in the cooking process, remove
from pot, cut in small squares and add to the soup. Garnish soup with
fresh parsley and serve. Per serving: Cal 189, Protein: 23 gm,
Carbo.: 28 gm, Fat 1 gm Note: You can vary this soup by using other
vegetables, such as turnips, potatoes, and celery. A touch of fresh
or dried basil is a nice change.
