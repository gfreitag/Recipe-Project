Title: Teriyaki Steak
Categories: Beef
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Honey
1 1/4 lb Boneless beef steak
-fat removed
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 ts Sherry extract
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Ground ginger
In a shallow pan combine marinade ingredients. Add steak and marinade
for several hours. Preheat broiler or grill, broil or grill meat,
basting occasionally until done to taste.

Per serving: 38g protein, 9g fat, 5g carb., 1464mg sodium, 103mg
cholesterol, 270 calories.
Date: 09-12-94
From: Carolyn Shaw
