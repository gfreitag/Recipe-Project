Title: Fresh Chanterelles and Fettuccine - Country Living
Categories: Pasta, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  Fresh or 1/4 t dried thyme
-leaves
1/8 t  Ground nutmeg
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 lb Fresh fettuccine
Fresh thyme sprigs
1 1/4 t  Salt
1 T  Butter
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/2 lb Chanterelles or other wild
-or cultivated exotic
-mushrooms, sliced
1 c  (1/2 pint) heavy cream
1. In 6-quart saucepot, heat about 3 quarts water and 1 t salt to
boiling.

2. Meanwhile, in large skillet, melt butter over medium heat; add
onion and saute 3 to 5 minutes until translucent. Add mushrooms,
cream, thyme leaves, remaining 1/4 t salt, and the nutmeg. Cook,
stirring, 10 to 12 minutes or until sauce thickens. Stir in cheese.

3. When sauce is almost finished, add fettuccine to boiling water and
cook 2 to 4 minutes until al dente.

4. Drain fettucine very well, then place in serving bowl and toss with
sauce. Serve family style or divide among 4 individual bowls. Garnish
with thyme sprigs.
