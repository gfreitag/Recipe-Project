Title: Planters Punch
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 2 quarts
1/3 c  Grenadine syrup
1 c  Dark rum; optional to 1/2 c
Ice
46 oz Pineapple juice; chilled
1 c  Water
6 oz Frozen orange juice concent.
1/2 c  Lemon juice from concentrate
In small punch bowl, combine all ingredients except ice.  Server over
ice; garnish as desired
