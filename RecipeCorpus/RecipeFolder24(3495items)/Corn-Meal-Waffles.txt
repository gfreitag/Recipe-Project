Title: Corn Meal Waffles
Categories: Breakfast, Pancakes, Waffles
Yield: 8 waffles
1 c  Yellow corn meal
2    Eggs, beaten
2 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  Salad oil
1 c  All-purpose flour, sifted
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
Sift together dry ingredients, except corn meal.  Stir in corn meal.
Combine eggs, buttermilk and oil.  Stir into dry ingredients.  Mix
only enough to moisten all flour.  Bake in preheated waffle iron.

Makes 8 to 10 waffles.

NOTE:  Waffle thickness may vary with liquid content.  For thicker
waffles, reduce amount of liquid slightly.
