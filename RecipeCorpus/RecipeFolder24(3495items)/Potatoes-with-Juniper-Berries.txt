Title: Potatoes with Juniper Berries
Categories: Vegetable, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
1    Red bell pepper, seeded and
-diced
6    Crushed juniper berries
1 1/2 lb Potatoes, peeled and coarsly
-grated
1/4 t  Freshly ground black pepper
Place potatoes in a colander and rinse well under cold running water.
Do not drain, but place them in a skillet with other ingredients.
Cook gently, turning occasionally, for 1/2 hour.  Add water
sparingly, as needed.
