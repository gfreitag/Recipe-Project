Title: Chili Rellano Casserole
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs, Tex/mexican, Casserole, Chilies
Yield: 1 servings
-salt & Pepper
1 cn (13 oz) evaporated milk
4    Eggs, separated
2 lg Cans whole green chilies  1
-lb cheddar cheese
1 lb Montrey Jack Cheese
3 T  Flour
Place 1/2 of chilies in bottom of greased casserole dish, cover with
all of cheddar cheese.  Top with rest of chilies.  Cover with all of
jack cheese. Beat egg whites until stiff.  Beat yolks with flour,
milk, salt and pepper in large bowl.  Fold egg whites into yolk
mixture.  Pour over casserole. Bake in 325 dutch oven for 45 minutes
or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
Date: 09-25-94
From: Earl Shelsby
