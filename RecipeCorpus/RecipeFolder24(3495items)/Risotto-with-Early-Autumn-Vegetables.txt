Title: Risotto with Early Autumn Vegetables
Categories: Vegetable, Italian, Rice, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Cooked corn kernels
1 t  Chopped fresh rosemary
1/4 t  Pepper
pn Salt
pn Hot pepper flakes
1 T  Grated lemon rind
1 1/2 c  Arborio rice
4 1/2 c  Vegetable or chicken stock
3/4 c  Freshly grated parmesan
-cheese
1 T  Lemon juice
Creamy and delectable,
-risotto is as comforting
-as it comes.  Serve with
A tomato and lettuc salad.
2 T  Olive oil
2 T  Butter
1 ea Onion, chopped
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1 ea Zucchini, cut into large
-dice
1 ea Sweet red pepper, diced
In large heavy saucepan, heat half each of the oil and butter over
medium heat; cook onion, garlic and mushrooms, stirring, for 5
minutes or until softened.  Add zucchini, red pepper, corn, rosemary,
pepper, salt and hot pepper flakes.  Cook, stirring, for 3-5 minutes
or until liquid has evaporated.  Remove from pan and set aside; keep
warm.  Heat remaining oil and butter in same pan over medium high
heat.  Add lemon rind and rice; cook, stirring for 1 minutes.  Stir
in 1/2 cup stock. Cook, stirring until liquid is absorbed.  Add
stock, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring until absorbed before next
addition, until rice is tender, 15-18 minutes in total. Stir in 1/2
cup cheese, lemon juice and vegetables. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Makes 4 servings. Per Serving: about
560 calories, 16 g protein, 20 gram fat, 80 gram carbo, high source
fibre, excellent source calcium. Origin: Canadian Living, October,
1994. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
