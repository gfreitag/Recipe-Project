Title: Chicken in Red Wine
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Chicken, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Instant chicken broth
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Dried thyme
2 cl Garlic, minced
1 sm Bay leaf
1 tb Parsley flakes
3 lb Skinned chicken parts
12 sm White pearl onions, peeled
12 sm Whole mushrooms
1 tb Plus (see next ingredient)
1 ts -vegetable oil
1 c  Dry red wine
1/2 c  Water
Preheat oven to 350. Place chicken in casserole, top with onions and
mushrooms. Combine remaining ingredients, pour over chicken. cover
and bake 1 hour. Remove and discard bay leaf before serving.

Per serving: 36g protein, 10g fat, 5g carb., 412mg sodium, 115mg
chol., 261 calories.
