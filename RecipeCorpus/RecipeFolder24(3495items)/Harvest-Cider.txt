Title: Harvest Cider
Categories: Beverage, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 3 quarts
1 qt Ginger ale; chilled
Ice
2 qt Apple cider or juice
1 c  Lemon juice from concentrate
1/2 c  Sugar
In punch bowl, combine cider, lemon juice, and sugar; stir to dissolve
sugar.  Chill.  Just before serving, add ginger ale.  Serve over ice.
Garnish with apple slices sprinkled with cinnamon if desired.
