Title: Raspberries with Champagne Sabayon
Categories: Dessert, Down under, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
-Wine and Cookbook 1985
-Source:  Australian Vogue
3 punnets raspberries icing sugar kirsch champagne sabayon: 3 to 4 egg
yolks 3 to 4 tablespoons sugar 500 ml brut champagne

Arrange raspberries in a pyramid.  Dust with icing sugar and sprinkle
with kirsch.  Chill in refrigerator for 1 hour.

To make sabayon: combine yolks, sugar and champagne and set bowl over
a saucepan of boiling water.  Whisk vigorously until mixture is thick
and fluffy.  Pour sauce around pyramid and serve with sugared
shortbread biscuits. Howqua Dale. Bon-Appetit, Exec.Chef. Magnus
Johansson
