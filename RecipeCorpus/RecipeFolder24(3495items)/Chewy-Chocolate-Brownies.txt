Title: Chewy Chocolate Brownies
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 40 brownies
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour, sifted
1 c  Pecans, chopped
4 oz Unsweetened baking chocolate
3/4 c  Butter or margarine
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Sugar
Preheat oven to 375oF. Butter a 12"x8" pan. Melt chocolate with
butter over low heat, stirring constantly; or melt in microwave bowl,
HIGH for 1-1 1/2 minute. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla. Set
aside. In large bowl, cream sugar and eggs abuot 5-6 minutes until
sugar is dissolved. Fold chocolate, flour and nuts into sugar
mixture, just until well belnded.

Pour into prepared pan. Bake for 25-35 minutes. Cool. Frost with your
favorite frosting. Refrigerte about 1 hour; cut into squares. Garnish
with pecans if desired. Store in refrigerator.

Source: Marian Hoffman, Whitman's Chocolate Cookbook. Typed by Linda
Howard.
