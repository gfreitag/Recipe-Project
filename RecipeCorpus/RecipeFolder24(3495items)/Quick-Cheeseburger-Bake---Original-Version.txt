Title: Quick Cheeseburger Bake - Original Version
Categories: Casserole, Burgers
Yield: 8 servings
-desired
1/4 c  Milk
2 c  Bisquick
3/4 c  Water
1 c  Cheddar Cheese, shredded
1 lb Ground Beef
3/4 c  Onion, chopped
1 cn Condensed Cheddar Cheese
-Soup
1 c  Frozen Mixed Vegetables, if
Heat oven to 400Ã¸F. Generously grease a 9 x 13 x 2" baking dish.
Cook beef and onion in a skillet until beef is brown.  Drain. Stir in
soup, vegetables and milk.  Stir baking mix and water in the baking
dish until moistened.  Spread in pan evenly.  Spread beef mixture
over batter, sprinkle with cheese.  Bake 30 minutes.  8 to 10
servings.
