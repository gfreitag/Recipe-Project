Title: Apple-Raisin Bread Pudding
Categories: Pudding, Fruit, Bread, Apples
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 t  Nutmeg
1/4 t  Salt
1 md Apple, pared, cored and
-chopped
6 sl Cinnamon-raisin bread, cubed
1 t  Rum extract (opt)
Baker's Joy No Stick Spray
2    Whole eggs
2    Egg whites
1 cn Evaporated milk (12 oz)
1/2 c  Honey
1 t  Vanilla extract
3/4 t  Cinnamon
Preheat oven to 350. Spray a 9" square baking dish with BAKER'S JOY.

In large mixing bowl, whisk whole eggs and egg whites until foamy.
Whisk in evaporated milk, honey, vanilla, salt, nutmeg and rum
extract (optional).

Add chopped apple and bread cubes. Mix until bread is evenly
moistened. Turn mixture into prepared baking dish.

Bake at 350'F. for 35-40 minutes until knife inserted in center comes
out clean.

Serve warm, topped with light cream or non-dairy whipped topping.
Sprinkle lightly with additional topping, if desired.
