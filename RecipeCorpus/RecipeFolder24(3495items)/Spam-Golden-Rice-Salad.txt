Title: Spam Golden Rice Salad
Categories: Salad, Spam, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Coarsely chopped pecans,
-toasted
1    Yellow bell pepper, chopped
1/2 c  Golden raisins
Lettuce cups, if desired
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cut in
-strips (12 oz)
2 c  Cooked wild rice, brown
-rice or white rice
1/2 c  Chopped carrot
MMMMM------------------SHERRY CHIVE VINAIGRETTE-----------------------
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1    Garlic clove
1/4 t  Freshly ground pepper
1/3 c  Olive oil
2 tb Sherry wine vinegar
1/4 c  Snipped fresh chives
Combine all ingredients except lettuce cups. Cover and chill 4 hours
or overnight. Serve in lettuce cups, if desired.

SHERRY CHIVE VINAIGRETTE: In container or electric blender, combine
olive oil, sherry wine vinegar, snipped fresh chives, chopped fresh
parsley, garlic, and freshly ground pepper. Cover and process until
chives are minced.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 394; Protein 13 grams;
Carbohydrate 30 grams; Fat 26 grams; Cholesterol 45 milligrams;
Sodium 577 milligrams.
