Title: Snacking Cornbread (Nathalie Dupree)
Categories: Bread, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
7 oz Can creamed corn
1 c  Self-rising cornmeal mix
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Peanut oil
1 c  Sour cream
Grease and 8-by-8-inch pan.  Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.  Beat
together the eggs, oil, sour cream and corn.  Add the corn meal mix.
Pour into the prepared pan and bake 30-40 minutes.

Source: New Southern Cooking.  Nathalie Dupree.
Date: 09-27-94
From: Rich Harper
