Title: Vegetable Relish Italiano
Categories: Can/preserv, Relish, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
-olives, or combination of
-two
3/4 c  White wine vinegar
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Water
2 T  White sugar
1 1/2 t  Onion salt
1 t  Oregano
1/2 t  Italian pepper
1/4 t  Garlic powder
1/2    A msall head of cauliflower,
-cut into florets and
-sliced
2    Carrots, cut into 2 inch
-strips
3    Stalks celery, cut into 1
-inch pieces
1    Green pepper, cut into 2
-inch strips
4 oz Sliced pimento
1/2 c  Pitted green or black
Place all ingredients in a large saucepan.  Bring to a boil, stirring
occasionally. Reduce heat and simmer, covered for 5 minutes.  Pour
into appropriate containers.  Cool.  Refrigerate at least 24 hours.
Drain well before serving.  Makes 6 cups, 1/2 cup per serving.

Keep refrigerated.  Makes a great accompaniment with any pasta meal.
