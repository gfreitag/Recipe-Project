Title: Matzo-Apple Pudding (Matza Kugel Mit Eppel)
Categories: Jewish, Cake/cookie, Pudding
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Nutmeg; grated
2/3 c  Sugar
pn Salt
1/2 c  Almonds; coarsely cut
12 ea Matzoth
2 ea Apples; peeled;cored & diced
2 tb Shortening
4 ea Eggs; separated
1 ts Cinnamon
PASSOVER MATZO-APPLE KUGEL

1. Preheat oven to slow (300F). 2. Crumble the matzoth and pour over
water to cover. Soak for a few
minutes, then squeeze out as much moisture as possible. Transfer to
deep mixing bowl. Add apples and shortening. Stir well. 3. Beat
the egg yolks well and add cinnamon, nutmeg, sugar and salt.
Add to matzo mixture. Stir in chopped almonds. 4. Beat egg whites
until stiff. Gently fold into matzo mixture. 5. Pour mixture into a
larged greased casserole dish. Bake 1 hour, or
until crust is evenly browned.

From: So Eat, My Darling: A Guide To the Yiddish Kitchen
AU: Naf Avnon and Uri Sella
