Title: Maui Spam Muffins
Categories: Sandwich, Spam, Muffin
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Pineapple slices, drained
-(15 1/4 oz)
1 sm Green pepper, cut into 8
-rings
1/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
2 t  Water
4    English muffins, split and
-toasted
Butter or margarine
Prepared mustard
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, thinly
-sliced (7 oz)
Heat oven to 375'F. Spread muffins with butter and mustard. Overlap
SPAM slices on each muffin half. Place a pineapple slice and a green
pepper ring on each muffin. Combine brown sugar and water; spoon over
sandwiches. Place muffins on baking sheet. Bake 10 minutes. Serve hot.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 179; Protein 7 grams;
Carbohydrate 29 grams; Fat 4 grams; Cholesterol 20 milligrams; Sodium
437 milligrams.
