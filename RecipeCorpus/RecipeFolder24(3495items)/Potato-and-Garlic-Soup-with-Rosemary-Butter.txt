Title: Potato and Garlic Soup with Rosemary Butter
Categories: Potato, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Unsalted butter - room
-temperature (1/4 stick)
INGREDIENTS
-+++++++++++++++++++++++++++
-+++++++++++++++++++++++++++    1 1/2 t  Rosemary - fresh, finely
-chopped OR 1/2 teaspoon
Dried, crumbled
DIRECTIONS
-+++++++++++++++++++++++++++
-+++++++++++++++++++++++++++
-++++++
-++++++
3 c  Chicken stock - OR canned
-low-salt broth
1 lb Russet potatoes - peeled,
-diced
20 ea Clove garlic - medium
1 c  Milk
Bring chicken stock, diced potatoes and garlic cloves to boil in heavy
large saucepan.  Reduce heat to medium and cook until potatoes and
garlic are tender, stirring occasionally, about 15 minutes.  Puree
mixture in batches in blender or processor.  Return to saucepan.
Stir in milk. Season soup to taste with salt and pepper.  (Can be
made 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Mix butter and rosemary in
small bowl to blend. Bring soup to simmer.  Ladle soup into heated
bowls.  Top each with small spoonful of rosemary butter. Serves 4.
