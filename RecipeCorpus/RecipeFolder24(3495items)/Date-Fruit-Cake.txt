Title: Date Fruit Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, British
Yield: 2 servings
1 lb Walnut meats, not cut up
1 c  Flour
3 t  Baking powder
4 ea Eggs, separated
4 c  Dates, stoned but not cut up
1 lb Candied cherries, not cut up
1/2 t  Salt
1 c  Sugar
1 t  Vanilla
Mix dates, walnuts and cherries. Sift together flour, salt and baking
powder, and mix with the fruit mixture. Add sugar and mix. Beat 4 egg
yolks until thick and mix thoroughly through cake mix. Add vanilla.
Beat egg whites until stiff and dry and fold into mixture. Line 2
loaf pans with wax paper, pour in mix and bake 1 hr at 350 degrees.
