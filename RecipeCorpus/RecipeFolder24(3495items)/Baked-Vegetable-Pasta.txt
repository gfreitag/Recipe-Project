Title: Baked Vegetable Pasta
Categories: Pasta, Vegetable, Casserole
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Broccoli, cut in small
-pieces
1 1/2 t  Oregano
1 t  Basil
32 oz Tomato sauce, commercial or
-homemade
1 lb Mostaccioli or ziti
1 T  Olive oil
2 ea Cloves fresh garlic,
-minced, or 1 tsp bottled
1 md Onion, finely minced
4 ea Carrots
Preheat oven to 350F.  Prepare pasta as directed on package.  Drain
well. Heat oil in wok or large skillet, add garlic and onion, stir
fry 1 minutes, and add carrots, broccoli, oregano and basil.  Spread
1/2 cup tomato sauce in bottom of 10x13 baking dish.  Layer pasta,
vegetable mixture and tomato sauce.  Top off with any remaining
tomato sauce. Place in oven, and bake for 20 minutes, or until heated
through.  Serve hot. Origin: Yeast Free Living. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Oct/94.
