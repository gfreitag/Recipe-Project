Title: Meat and Rice Filling
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Lamb, Rice, Beef
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon or 1/4
-teaspoon allspice or 1/2
ts Dried basil/marjoram
1-2 tablespoon
-raisins/pine nuts optional
1/2 lb Lean ground beef/lamb
1 3/16 c  Rice washed and drained
1 ea Tomato skinned and chopped
2 tb Finely chopped parsley
Salt and black pepper
Put the lamb, rice, tomato, parsley, salt, pepper and cinnamon and
raisins and/or pine nuts in a bowl and knead very well by hand until
thoroughly blended. Fill vegetables no more than three-quarters full,
to allow for the expansion of the rice. Use cooked rice if vegetables
are to be baked.
