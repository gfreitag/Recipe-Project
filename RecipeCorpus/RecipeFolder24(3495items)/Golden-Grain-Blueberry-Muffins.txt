Title: Golden-Grain Blueberry Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin, Fruit
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 c  Yellow cornmeal
2 1/2 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Baking soda
3/4 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Salt
1 c  Blueberries - fresh or
-frozen, thawed
Baker's Joy No Stick Spray
3/4 c  Quick cooking oats
3/4 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1    Egg, slightly beaten
3/4 c  All-purpose flour
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 T  Firmly packed brown sugar
3 T  Quick cooking oats
Preheat oven to 375'F. Spray 16 muffin cups with BAKER'S JOY.

In large bowl combine quick cooking oats, buttermilk, honey,
vegetable oil and egg. Let sit 5 minutes.

In small bowl combine remaining ingredients.

Stir dry ingredients into oat mixture. Blend only until dry
ingredients are moistened. Carefully fold in blueberries.

Spoon batter equally into prepared muffin cups. Sprinkle with topping.

Bake at 375'F. 20 minutes, until toothpick inserted in center comes
out clean.

Coll on wire rack for 10 minutes. Turn muffins out onto serving plate.
Serve warm if desired. *** TOPPING: ***

Blend oats and brown sugar together.
