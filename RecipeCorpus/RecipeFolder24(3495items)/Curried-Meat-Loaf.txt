Title: Curried Meat Loaf
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Beef, Main dish, Meatloaf
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
2 ea Egg whites
3/4 oz Wheat germ
3/4 c  Tomato sauce
1 tb Plus (see next ingredient)
1 ts -reduced calorie margarine
2 sm Sweet apples,peeled,chopped
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1 lb Very lean ground beef
2 ts Curry powder
Preheat oven to 350. Cook onion and apple in margarine until onion is
lightly browned, about 15 minutes. Combine with remaining ingredients
EXCEPT 1/2 cup of the tomato sauce, shape into a loaf and place on a
rack in a shallow baking pan. Top with remaining tomato sauce. Bake,
uncovered, 1 hour.

Per serving: 24g protein, 18g fat, 16g carb., 405mg sodium, 70mg
chol., 317 calories.
