Title: Hershey's Best Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 36 brownies
-European-style cocoa
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 t  Baking powder
1/4 t  Salt
1 c  Chopped nuts (opt)
1 c  Butter or margarine
-(2 sticks)
2 c  Sugar
2 t  Vanilla extract
4    Eggs
3/4 c  HERSHEY'S cocoa or HERSHEY'S
Heat oven to 350'F. Grease 13x9x2" baking pan. In large microwave-safe
bowl, place butter. Microwave at HIGH (100%) 2 to 2 1/2 minutes or
until melted. Stir in sugar and vanilla. Add eggs, one at a time,
beating well with spoon after each addition. Add cocoa; beat well
until blended. Add flour, baking powder and salt; beat well. Stir in
nuts, if desired. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake 30-35 minutes
or until brownies begin to pull away from sides of pan. Cut into
bars. Makes about 36 brownies. From: Michael Orchekowski
