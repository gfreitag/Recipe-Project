Title: Baked Ham Slice
Categories: Pork
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
Cut slashes in edge of a one-inch thick center-cut slice of ham. Mix
1 tsp. dry mustard with 1/4 cup brown sugar and sprinkle on ham
slice. Place in heavy skillet or baking pan.  Pour on milk at side of
ham slice until it barely reaches top.  Bake, uncovered in 350 degree
oven for 1 hour. For ready to eat ham, cut time in half. (Most milk
should be absorbed by ham. From: Lancaster Farming Shared By: Pat
