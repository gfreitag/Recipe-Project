Title: Stir-Fried Bean Sprouts with Tofu
Categories: Side dish, Thai, Stir fry, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
-- inch lengths
1 pn Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
2 tb Soy sauce
1 lb Tofu
4 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Garlic, finely chopped
1 lb Bean sprouts
5 ea Green onions, cut into 1 1/2
Drain the tofu & cube.  Heat oil in a wok over medium heat & stir-fry
the garlic for a few minutes, until it turns golden.  Set aside.  Add
the tofu, stir-fry for 5 minutes.  Add the bean sprouts, onions,
salt, pepper & soy sauce.  Add the reserved garlic.  Stir-fry for no
more than 1 minute. Sprinkle with another dash of pepper & serve hot.

Adapted from Puangkram C. Schmitz & Michael J. Worman, "Practical Thai
Cooking"
