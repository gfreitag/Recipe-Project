Title: Pineapple Limeade
Categories: Beverage, Tex/mexican
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Lime Juice
1 qt Sparkling Water; Chilled
1/2 c  Sugar
3 c  Pineapple Juice
Mix all ingredients except sparkling water; refrigerate until
chilled. Just before serving, stir in sparkling water.  Serve over
ice. Garnish with lime slices, if desired.

From: MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI
