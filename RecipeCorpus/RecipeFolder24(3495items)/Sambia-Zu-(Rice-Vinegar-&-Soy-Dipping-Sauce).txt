Title: Sambia-Zu (Rice Vinegar & Soy Dipping Sauce)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Dip, Japanese
Yield: 1 cup
2 ts Shoyu
1/8 ts Salt
MSG
2 1/2 T  Rice vinegar
2 1/2 T  Niban dashi
4 ts Sugar
Actual yield is 1/2 cup.

TO PREPARE:

Combine the rice vinegar, niban dashi, sugar, soy sauce and salt in a
1 quart enameled or stainless steel saucepan and sprinkle lightly
with MSG. Stirring constantly, bring the sauce to a boil, uncovered,
over high heat.

Then immediately remove the pan from the heat and set the sauce aside
to cool to room temperature.

TO SERVE:

Save the sambai-zu in tiny individual cups or dishes, as a dipping
sauce for Shime saba, Kani kyuri ikomi, or kani sunomono.
