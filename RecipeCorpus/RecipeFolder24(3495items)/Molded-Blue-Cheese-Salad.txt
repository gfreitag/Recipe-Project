Title: Molded Blue Cheese Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1 pt Strawberries, fresh; sliced
Pineapple slices
Sour cream
2 pk Gelatin, unflavored
1 qt Milk; divided
16 oz Cream cheese; softened
6 oz Cheese, blue; crumbled
Soften gelatin in 1/2 cup cold milk for 5 minutes.  Heat remaining
milk; add gelatin and stir until dissolved.  Cool until slightly
thickened. Blend cream cheese and blue cheese; gradually add gelatin
mixture, beating until smooth and fluffy.  Pour into mold. Chill
until firm. Serve with strawberries, pineapple slices, and sour cream.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June 1973. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
