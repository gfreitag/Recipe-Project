Title: Orange Julius's
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------IN A BLENDER MIX---------------------------
1/4 c  Granulated sugar.
1 t  Vanilla extract.
2 ea Egg WHITES.
1 ea Heaping cup ice
1 c  Orange juice (the more tart
-the better. You can also
Substitute your favorite
-juice.
1 c  Water.
Mix on high speed till frothy. Makes about 4 servings.
