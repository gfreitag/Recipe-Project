Title: Broccoli with Mustard Dressing
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Vinegar, balsamic
1 tb Dijon mustard
6 c  Broccoli, cut into flowerets
1 tb Oil, olive
1 tb Lemon juice
cut broccoli into flowerettes, peel stem, cut into diagonal pieces.
Steam for 6-7 minutes until just tender crisp. meanwhile, in serving
dish, whisk remaining ingredients for dressing.  When broccoli is
cooked, drain and toss into dressing.
