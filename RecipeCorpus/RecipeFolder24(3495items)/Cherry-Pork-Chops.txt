Title: Cherry Pork Chops
Categories: Pork, Fruit, Crockpot
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Lemon juice
1/2 t  Instant chick bouillon
-granules
1/8 t  Ground mace
6    Pork chops, cut 3/4 inch
-thick
1/4 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1/2 cn Cherry pie filling (1 cup)
Trim excess fat from pork chops.  Brown pork chops in hot skillet with
butter or oil.  Sprinkle each chop with salt and pepper.  Combine
cherry pie filling, lemon juice, chicken bouillon granules, and
ground mace in crock pot.  Stir well.  Place pork chops in crockpot.
Cover.  Cook on low for 4-5 hours.  Place chops on platter.  Pour
cherry sauce over meat.
