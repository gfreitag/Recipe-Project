Title: Raita Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 1 servings
Salt and freshly ground
-pepper
1/4 c  Fresh coriander leaves, or
-parsley, finely chopped
2 c  Low fat yogurt
1/2 c  Onions, finely chopped
2-3 medium tomatoes,
-finely chopped
1 t  Ground cumin
Combine ingredients and refrigerate for an hour or longer. Origin:
Yeast Free Feast Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
