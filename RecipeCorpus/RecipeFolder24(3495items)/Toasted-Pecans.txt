Title: Toasted Pecans
Categories: Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 depends ?
Salt
Pecan halves
3 tb Butter
Lay pecan halves on foil-lined pan.  Cover with 3 tablespoons butter.
Sprinkle salt to taste.  Put in 300 degree oven for 15 minutes.  Turn
off heat.  Leave in oven 1-1/2 to 2 hours (doors closed).

SOURCE: The Popular Pecan, pamphlet by the Mississippi Pecan Growers
Assn. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
Date: 09-28-94
From: Nancy Coleman
