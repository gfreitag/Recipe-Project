Title: Penne Puttanesca - Country Living
Categories: Pasta, Salsa/sauce, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Chopped and pitted green
-and oil-cured ripe olives
3 T  Capers
1 T  Chopped fresh marjoram
-leaves
1 pk (12-oz) dried penne or other
-tubular pasta
1 c  Boiling water
1/4 c  Dried tomatoes
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 t  Anchovy paste
8    Cloves garlic, finely
-chopped
1. In small bowl, combine water and tomatoes; set aside 5 minutes for
tomatoes to soften.

2. In large, heavy skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; add onion
and anchovy paste and saute until onion is golden brown. Add garlic,
dried tomatoes and their soaking liquid, the olives, capers, and
marjoram; cook sauce, stirring occasionally, 9 to 12 minutes or until
half of liquid has evaporated.

3. Meanwhile, cook penne following package directions. Drain penne
very well, then place in serving bowl and toss with vegetable
mixture. Serve family style or divide among 4 individual bowls.
