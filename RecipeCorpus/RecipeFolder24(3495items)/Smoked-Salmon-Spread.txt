Title: Smoked Salmon Spread
Categories: Spread, Dip, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
Finely Chopped Green Onions
Liquid Smoke To Taste
Pepper To Taste
12 oz Philly Free
8 oz Philly Lite
1 lg Can Salmon In Water, Drained
Combine all ingredients and mix well.  Refrigerate until ready to
serve. Typed by Syd Bigger.
