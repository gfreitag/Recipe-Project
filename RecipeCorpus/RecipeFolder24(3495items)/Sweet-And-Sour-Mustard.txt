Title: Sweet-And-Sour-Mustard
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Condiment, Herbs/spice, Salsa/sauce, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
2 T  Water
2    Eggs, lightly beaten
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Cider OR raspberry vinegar
1/3 c  Dry mustard
In a saucepan, whisk together all ingredients. Cook over low heat,
stirring constantly, until thickened. Pour into small jars and
refrigerate. Yield: 1 1/2 cups.
