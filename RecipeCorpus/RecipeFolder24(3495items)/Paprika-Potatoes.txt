Title: Paprika Potatoes
Categories: Potato, Side dish, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
"These tasty potatoes are golden and crusty on the outside and tender
on the inside.  When a meal needs a comforting, homey touch, I whip
up a batch." - Ruth Andrewson 4 large potatoes, peeled, cooked and
quartered 3 Tbsp.   butter or margarine 1/2 tsp. paprika

In a large skillet, slowly saute potatoes in butter until golden
brown, about 10-15 minutes.  Sprinkle with paprika. Serves: 4-6 From:
"Taste of Home" Magazine, October/November 1994 Posted by: Debbie
Carlson - Cooking
