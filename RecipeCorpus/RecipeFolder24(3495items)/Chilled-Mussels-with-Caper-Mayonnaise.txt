Title: Chilled Mussels with Caper Mayonnaise
Categories: Seafood, Clam/oyster
Yield: 2 pounds
3 tb Capers
1 t  Tabasco
1 tb Lemon juice
2 lb Steamed mussels chilled
-and on half shells
1 c  Mayonnaise
From Daniel's Restaurant.

Combine mayonnaise, capers, Tabasco and lemon juice. Dollop mussels
with caper mayonnaise and serve with parsley garnish if desired.
