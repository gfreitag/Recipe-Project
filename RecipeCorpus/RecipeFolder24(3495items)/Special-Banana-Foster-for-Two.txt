Title: Special Banana Foster for Two
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
ds Ground cinnamon
3 tb Light rum
Vanilla or coffee ice cream
2    Firm, small bananas, peeled
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
2 tb Butter
Cut bananas in half lengthwise, then crosswise into quarters  Drizzle
with lemon juice.  Heat brown sugar and butter together in a 10 inch
skillet until sugar is melted and caramelized.  Add bananas and cook
slowly one to two minutes until heated through and glazed.  Sprinkle
lightly with cinnamon.  Add rum, carefully ignite.  Spoon liquid over
bananas until flames die out, about one minute.

Serve warm over firm ice cream.
