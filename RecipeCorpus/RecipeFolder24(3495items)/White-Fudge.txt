Title: White Fudge
Categories: Candy, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Butter flavor
1/2 t  Black walnut flavor
1/4 c  Chopped black walnuts
2 c  White sugar
1 c  Whole milk
1/2 t  Salt
Place sugar, milk, and salt in heavy saucepan.  Place over medium
heat and cook, stirring constantly, until all sugar is dissolved.
Cover and cook to boiling.  Remove cover and continue to boil to 240F
(soft ball stage). Remove from heat and allow to cool until bottom of
pan is warm to touch. Add butter and black walnut flavors and beat
until creamy. Add chopped nuts.  When stiff, pour into greased 8 inch
square pan. Makes 48 pieces.
