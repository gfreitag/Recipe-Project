Title: Grape-Nuts Raisin Pudding
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dessert, Pudding
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 c  Raisins
1 ts Vanilla extract
6 ts Firmly packed brown sugar
Ground cinnamon
1 c  Water
1 1/2 oz Grape-nuts cereal
1 tb Plus
1 ts Quick cooking tapioca
1/4 ts Maple extract
Combine all ingredients except cinnamon in the top of a double boiler
over boiling water. Cook, stirring, until mixture thickens, about 5
minutes. Sprinkle with cinnamon, serve warm

Per serving: 3g protein, 0g fat, 38mg carb., 150mg sodium, 0mg chol.,
213 calories.
