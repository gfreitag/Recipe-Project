Title: Crostini with Tomatoes and Pesto
Categories: Italian, Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Pesto
3 md Ripe tomatoes
Dice and seed 2 to 3 medium or 1 to 2 large tomatoes - no need to peel
them. Add 1 to 2 tablespoons of fresh pesto.  Mix well and spread over
crostini toasts.
