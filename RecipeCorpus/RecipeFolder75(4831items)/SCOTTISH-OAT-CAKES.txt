Title: SCOTTISH OAT CAKES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2 c  Rolled oats
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 1/4 c  Shortening
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Boiling water
2 c  Bran flakes
Add soda to boiling water and let stand until cool. Mix together
flour, baking powder, salt, bran flakes, rolled oats and sugar. Cut
in shortening; add water and soda.  Roll outthin on a floured board.
Bake in hot oven until golden brown.

: Bell's Best #2 (Mississippi chapter)
