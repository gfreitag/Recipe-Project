Title: SPANISH BAKES RICE
Categories: Mexican, Rice, Side dish, Jaw
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Long grain rice
1/4 c  Vegetable shortening
1/4 c  Diced onion
1 tb Cumin seeds (comino)
1/2 c  Diced tomato
1 tb Granulated chicken base
1/2 ts Salt (more to taste)
3 c  Water
Preheat oven to 275 degrees. Heat shortening in a heavy saucepan over
medium heat. Add rice and cook, stirring frequently, 3-4 minutes or
until rice begins to brown. Add onion and saute 2 minutes or until
onion softens. Add cumin, tomato, chicken base, salt and water. Stir
well to desolve chicken base. Bring to a boil, pour into a 2-quart
casserole dish, cover tightly and bake 1 hour.
