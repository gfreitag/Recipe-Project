Title: SAVANNAH RED RICE
Categories: Appetizers, Meat, Miscellaneo, Rice
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 lb Bacon
1/2 c  Chopped Onion
2 c  Raw Rice
2 c  Red Tomatoes
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Tabasco
Fry bacon until crips.  Remove from pan & cook onions until tender in
bacon fat.  Add rice, tomatoes, seasonings and bacon, crumbled. Cook
over low heat about 10 minutes. Pour into two-qt. casserole and cover
tightly (can use foil).  Cook in 350-degree oven for one hour,
stirring with fork a couple of times. Serves 6-8 people.
