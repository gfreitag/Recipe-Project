Title: SHRIMP SALAD SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
1 lb Shrimp, cooked, shelled
Finely chopped
2    Stalks celery, finely minced
3    Green onions, finely diced
2 oz Canned pimientos, finely
Diced
2 tb Chopped parsley
2 tb Mayonnaise
Directions: Mix all ingredients with a spoon, blending well. Chill for
several hours to allow the flavors to blend. Serve on cucumber slices,
zucchini slices, party rye or crackers of choice submitted by marina
