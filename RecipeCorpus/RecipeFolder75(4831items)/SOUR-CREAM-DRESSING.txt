Title: SOUR CREAM DRESSING
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Eggs, hard boiled
1/2 pt Sour cream
2    Spring onions
3 tb Vinegar (4?... to taste)
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
Red pepper or paprika
Mash egg yolk, add salt and mustard, sugar and paprika or red pepper.
Add sour cream and enough vinegar to make it thick and soupy. Chop
and add egg whites and chopped spring onions. Especially good over
Spring leaf lettuce. Mrs. Brice McAdoo Clagett
