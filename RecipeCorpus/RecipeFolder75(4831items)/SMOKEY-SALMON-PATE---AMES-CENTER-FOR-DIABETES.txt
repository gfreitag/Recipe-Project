Title: SMOKEY SALMON PATE - AMES CENTER FOR DIABETES
Categories: Diabetic, Holidays, Seafood, Pate
Yield: 40 Appetizers
1 c  Salmon (15oz)
2/3 c  Cottage cheese, low fat
1/4 ts Liquid smoke seasoning
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Onion; minced
2 tb Olives, ripe; finely chopped
1 ts Worchestershire sauce
1/2 ts Paprika
Drain salmon and chop fine, or mash with a fork.
Press cottage cheese through a strainer, or blend smooth; combine
with salmon and remaining ingredients.
Mix well.  Cover and chill for at least one hour.
Serve with melba toast or party rye.

1    serving is 3 appetizers.
1 lean meat 20 cal; fat 1gm, pro 2gm, sodium 75mg, chol 6mg
