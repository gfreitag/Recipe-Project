Title: SHIRRED EGGS WITH BASIL OR TARRAGON
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Breakfast
Yield: 6 Servings
3 tb Approx. chopped fresh basil
-or tarragon
6    Eggs
2 tb Whipping cream
Salt and pepper
Lightly butter six 1/2 cup ramekins or custard cups. Evenly sprinkle
bottoms with half of the herbs.  Break 1 egg into each ramekin;
sprinkle with remaining herbs. Spoon 1 tsp. whipping cream over each.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Place ramekins in shallow baking dish; pour in enough boiling water
to come halfway up sides of ramekins. Bake in 350Ã¸F oven for about 15
minutes or until eggs are cooked but yolk is still slightly runny.
Serve at once in ramekins.  Makes 6 servings.  Typed in MMFormat by
