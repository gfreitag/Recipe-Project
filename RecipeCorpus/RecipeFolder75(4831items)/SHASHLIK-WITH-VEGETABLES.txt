Title: SHASHLIK WITH VEGETABLES
Categories: Harned 1994, Main dish, Sephardic, Veal, Yemenite
Yield: 1 Batch
2 lb Lamb; cut in 36 pieces
Salt, pepper and cumin
3    Onions; peeled
3    Tomatoes; unpeeled
Hot cooked rice
Season the lamb with salt, pepper and cumin.  Cut the onions and
tomatoes into quarters.  Alternate the lamb with the vegetables on
skewers; grill or broil for 5 minutes on each side. Serve with rice.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings.

From _The Yemenite Cookbook_ by Zion Levi and Hani Agabria. New York:
Seaver Books, 1988.  Pg. 153. ISBN 0-8050-0394-0. Electronic format by
Cathy Harned.
