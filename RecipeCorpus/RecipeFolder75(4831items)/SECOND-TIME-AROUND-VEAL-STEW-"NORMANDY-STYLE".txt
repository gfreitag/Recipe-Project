Title: SECOND TIME AROUND VEAL STEW "NORMANDY STYLE"
Categories: New, Text, Import
2 TB butter
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Granny Smith apple, --
peeled, cored & cut into
1/2" dices
:
:
1/2 c  apple cider
1/2    veal stew from above
4    kirby cucumbers, -- seeded &
diced into 3/4" cross=
piece
:
:
1/4 c  cream, -- optional

Saute apple in butter until almost tender. Add the apple cider and
reduce until almost entirely absorbed by the apples. Add the veal
stew and cream and bring to a simmer. Set the cucumbers on the top,
cover and steam for a minute or so just to heat through. Adjust the
seasoning.
