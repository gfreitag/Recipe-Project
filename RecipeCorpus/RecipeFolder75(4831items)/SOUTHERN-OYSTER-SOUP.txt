Title: SOUTHERN OYSTER SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Oyster liquor
2    Dozen oysters
6 c  Milk
4 tb Butter
1/2 ts Mace
1/8 ts Salt
1 ds Of pepper
From: Gone With The Wind Cook Book; Valerie Whittle

Paprika

Heat oyster liquor to boiling point.  Add oysters and simmer until
edges curl.  Scald milk; add butter, oysters, liquor, and seasonings.
Serve at once in hot soup bowls with a dash of paprika over soup.
Serves 6
