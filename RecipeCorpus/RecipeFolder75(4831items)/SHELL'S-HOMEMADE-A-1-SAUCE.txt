Title: SHELL'S HOMEMADE A-1 SAUCE
Categories: Condiments, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Bottled Steak Sauce
1/8 c  Liquid (i.e. water, broth..)
2    Cloves garlic, minced  *OR*
1 tb Garlic Powder
1/8 c  Barbeque Sauce
1/8 c  Ketchup
1/8 c  Worcestershire Sauce
1/8 c  Soy Sauce
Mix all ingredients in small saucepan and simmer 5 to
10    minutes. MMM-MMM GOOD!
