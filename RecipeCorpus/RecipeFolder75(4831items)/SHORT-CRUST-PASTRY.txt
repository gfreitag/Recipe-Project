Title: SHORT CRUST PASTRY
Categories: Pastry
Yield: 8 Oz
8 oz SR Flour
1/2 ts Salt
2 oz Lard
2 oz Margarine
7 ts Approx Cold Water
1 oz Caster Sugar (For sweet Pastry)

1. Mix flour and salt in a basin, rub in lard and margarine. 2. Using
a knife to cut and stir, mix with cold water to form a stiff paste.
3. turn dough on to a floured board or worktop and roll out
