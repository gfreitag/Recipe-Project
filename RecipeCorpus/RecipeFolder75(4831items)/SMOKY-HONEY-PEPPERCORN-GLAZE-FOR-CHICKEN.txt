Title: SMOKY HONEY-PEPPERCORN GLAZE FOR CHICKEN
Categories: Newspaper, Sauces, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Honey
1 tb Ketchup
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Liquid smoke
1/8 ts Crushed red pepper flakes
1    Whole chicken; rinsed &
.  dried
In small bowl, combine all ingredients except chicken until
well-blended. Place chicken in roasting pan; brush with glaze. Roast
at 350 degrees, brushing with additional glaze every 15 minutes,
until chicken is deep-golden brown and chicken juices run clear, 1
hour to 1 hour and 15 minutes. ** Fort Worth Star Telegram -- Food
section -- 27 Mar 96 **
