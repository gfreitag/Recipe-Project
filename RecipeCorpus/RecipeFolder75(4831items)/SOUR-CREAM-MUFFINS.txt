Title: SOUR CREAM MUFFINS
Categories: Diabetic, Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
1/4 c  Diet margarine
3/4 c  Imitation sour cream
-or sour half & half
1 1/3 c  All purpose flour
Non nutritive sweetener to
-equal 1/2 cup sugar
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
ds Nutmeg
1    Egg beaten
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.  Cream margarine; add sour cream;
mix well.  Sift dry ingredients.  Add alternately with egg to first
mixture. Spoon into greased muffin tins; bake for 15 minutes.

PER SERVING: 1 muffin = 1 Bread, 2 Fat  Calories:151
