Title: SOUTHWESTERN CHICKEN SALAD
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Perdue Fit 'n Easy Chicken
Tenders
2 tb Olive oil -- divided
Salt -- to taste
Pepper -- to taste
1/2 c  Fresh lime juice
1 sm Red onion
1/4 c  Fresh cilantro
1/2 ea Jalapeno pepper -- seeded
2 c  Cantaloupe -- dried, ripe
1 lg Avocado -- peeled/seeded/dic
1 c  Apple -- peeled and diced
1    Head Romaine lettuce
Brush chicken with 1 tablespoon oil and  sprinkle lightly with salt
and pepper.  Grill or broil 6-8 inches from heat source for 2-3
minutes perside until cooked through. Dice and reserve. In a food
processor combine lime juice, onion, cilantro, and jalapeno. Process
until finely chopped. Gradually add oil with machine running. Season
to taste with salt and pepper. In a salad bowl toss chicken, melon,
avocado and jicama with dressing.  Serve on a bed of Romaine lettuce
leaves.
