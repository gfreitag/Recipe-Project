Title: SPAM CORN CHOWDER
Categories: Soups
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Chopped onion
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 1/2 c  Diced, peeled potatoes
1/2 c  Chopped green bell pepper
2 cn Cream-style corn (17 oz)
2 c  Milk
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(12 oz)
in 3-quart saucepan, saute onion in butter over medium heat 5-10
minutes or until golden. Add potatoes and green pepper. Cook and stir
2 minutes. Add corn and milk. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer
15 minutes or until potatoes are tender, stirring occasionally. Stir
in SPAM. Simmer 2 minutes.
