Title: SIMPLE SUMMER VEGETABLE SOUP
Categories: Main dish, Soups, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
2 tb Butter
1 ea Onion; Md, Chopped
2 ea Carrots; Md, Scrape & Sliced
2 ea Stalks Celery; Sliced
8 c  Chicken Broth
2 ea Tomatoes; Md, Peel & Chop
2 ea Zucchini; Md,Quarter & Slice
1/2 c  String Beans Or Peas
1 c  Spinach; Fresh, *
1 x  Salt & Pepper; To Taste
*    Wash and cut the fresh spinach into strips.
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++

Melt the butter in a soup pot; add the onion, carrots and celery.
Cook over medium heat, stirring often, for 5 minutes. Add the broth
and tomatoes and simmer another 10 minutes. Add the spinach and cook
for an additional 1 minute.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
