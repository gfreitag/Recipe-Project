Title: SAUSAGE BALLS #1
Categories: Appetizers, Pork, Cheese/eggs, Meats
Yield: 10 Servings
1 lb Bulk Sausage (Hot Or Mild)
2 c  Sharp Chedder Cheese Grated
3 c  Bisquick
1/3 c  Water
Combine all ingredients and shape into balls the size of walnuts.
Bake on cookie sheet at 350øF for 15 minutes. Makes about 5 dozen.
NOTE: defrost and reheat at 350øF.
