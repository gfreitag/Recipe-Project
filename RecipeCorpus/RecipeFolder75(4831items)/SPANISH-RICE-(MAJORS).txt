Title: SPANISH RICE (MAJORS)
Categories: Diabetic, Grains, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Rice cooked;
2 ts Green pepper; diced
1/2    Onion; chopped
2 ts Pimento bits;
1 ts Salt; (if you must)
1/2 ts Pepper;
1 c  Tomato juice;
1 ts Worchestershire sauce;
Mix all ingredients together and bake at 350F for 30 minutes or until
heated through and bubbly.  Fluff with fork before serving. Food
Exchange per serving: 1 STARCH/BREAD EXCHANGE + 1/2 VEGETABLE EXCHANGE

Source: Sugar Free....That's Me by Judith S. Majors Brought to you and
yours by Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master
