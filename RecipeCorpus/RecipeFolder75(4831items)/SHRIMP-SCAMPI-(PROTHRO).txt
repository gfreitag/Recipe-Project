Title: SHRIMP SCAMPI (PROTHRO)
Categories: Main dish, Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
3    Clove of garlic crushed
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Parsley
1 ts Salt
2 ts Lemon peel
2 lb Jumbo shrimp
Put in sauce pan and melt together-let sit until ready to cook. Peel
shrimp and add to above sauce and saute until done. (about 2-4 min.)
Sprinkle parsley as you cook. Serve with parsley and lemon wedges to
garnish. Spoon excess sauce from pan over shrimp.
