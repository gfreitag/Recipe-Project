Title: SOUTHERN-STYLE HONEY RICE
Categories: Rice, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  Cooked rice
1/2 c  Roasted peanuts, chopped
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Honey
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 ts Ginger
Chopped Parsley
Combine rice and peanuts.  Set aside and keep warm. Combine butter and
honey in a saucepan, cook over low heat just until butter melts. Stir
in cinnamon and ginger. Pour over rice, stirring well. Garnish with
parsley.
