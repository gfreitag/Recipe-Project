Title: SLOE COMFORTABLE SCREW AGAINST THE WALL
Categories: Beverages, Drinks, Val's, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 oz Vodka
1/2 oz Sloe gin
1/2 oz Southern Comfort
4 oz Orange juice
1 ds Galliano
In a tall glass add lots of ice, pour in vodka, gin and southern
comfort. Top with Orange juice and splash with galiano. that's it.
