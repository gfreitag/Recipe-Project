Title: SPICE CAKE IN A PAN
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 16 Servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Nutmeg
6 tb Oil
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Vanilla
7/8 c  Water
Preheat oven to 375'F. Grease and flour an 8x8-inch square cake pan.
In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, sugar, baking soda and spices.
Mix well. Add oil, water vinegar and vanilla. Blend until smooth.
Pour into prepared pan and bake for 35 minutes or until tests done.
