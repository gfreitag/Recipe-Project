Title: SPECIAL PASSOVER APPLE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts, Passover
Yield: 8 Servings
3    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Oil
2/3 c  Matzoth Cake Meal, Sifted
1/2 c  Potato Starch
5    Apples, Sliced Thin
Sugar And Cinnamon
Beat eggs well. Add sugar and oil. Add sifted cake meal and potato
starch. Lightly oil 8" square baking pan. Place apples which have
been sprinkled with sugar and cinnamon in the bottom of the pan. Pour
the batter over the apples. Bake at 325F for about an hour.

This recipe has been enjoyed by my family for years at Pesach- Carole
Walberg

Recipe by: Molly Sherman- The Passover Feast II
