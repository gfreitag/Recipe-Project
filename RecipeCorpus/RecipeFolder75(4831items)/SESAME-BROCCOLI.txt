Title: SESAME BROCCOLI
Categories: Side dishes
Yield: 6 Servings
1 bn Broccoli; broken into floret
1/2 c  Toasted sesame seeds
1/4 c  Sake
1 1/2 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Sesame oil
2 ts Honey
Recipe by: Joel Ehrlich <Joel.Ehrlich@SALATA.COM> Cook the broccoli in
boiling salted water until tender crisp. Drain thoroughly. Let stand
and cool to room temperature. Combine the remaining ingredients in a
large bowl. Add the broccoli and toss thoroughly just before serving.

Yields 6 Servings
