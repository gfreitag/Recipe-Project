Title: SILVER CLOUDS
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
1 qt Vanilla ice cream
1/3 c  Amaretto Di Sarino
1/3 c  White Cream De Cacao (Arrow)
Whipped cream
Kalua (small amount)
Preparation : Mix ice cream in blender until creamy, slowly add
liquor when softened.  Pour into glasses and top with whipped cream.
Pour a little Kalua on top. Serves 4.
