Title: SESAME HALVAH
Categories: Desserts, Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
6 c  Sesame seeds
1 1/2 c  Honey
1 ts Ginger
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Allspice
1.  Toast 6 cups of sesame seeds in a skillet or oven. 2. Blend the
seeds in a blender one cup at a time. 3. Add honey, ginger, cinnamon
and ginger and mix in well. 4. Pat into a 13" X 9" pan and
refrigerate. Cut into squares.
