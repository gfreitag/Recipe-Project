Title: SESAME MUSTARD VINAIGRETTE
Categories: New imports, Dressings
2 TB whole grain mustard
Yield: 4 Servings
2 ts rice wine vinegar
1/4 ts sugar
1/4 ts salt
1/4 ts pepper
2 TB vegetable oil
1 ts sesame oil
2 TB green onions -- chopped

In a small bowl, whisk together mustard, vinegar, sugar, salt, and
pepper. Slowly whisk in the oils. Mix in the green onio
