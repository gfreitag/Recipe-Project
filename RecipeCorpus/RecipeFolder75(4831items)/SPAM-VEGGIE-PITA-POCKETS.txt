Title: SPAM VEGGIE PITA POCKETS
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 8 Servings
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(7 oz)
1 c  Chopped broccoli
1 c  Chopped cauliflower
1    Tomato, chopped
1    Carrot, peeled and chopped
1/3 c  Chopped cucumber
1/3 c  Finely chopped onion
1/2 c  Italian salad dressing
4    Pita pocket breads, halved
STATE FAIR RECIPE Carol Green, Austin, MN. In bowl, combine SPAM,
broccoli, cauliflower, tomato, carrot, cucumber, and onion. Toss with
dressing. Cover and chill several hours. Spoon salad mixture into
pocket bread.
