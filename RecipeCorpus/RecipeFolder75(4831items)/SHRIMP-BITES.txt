Title: SHRIMP BITES
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 32 Servings
-----Blend:-----
1    Stick butter
1 1/2 tb Mayonnaise
1    Jar Kraft Old English Sharp
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
Cheese (5 oz.)
7 oz Canned shrimp or crab meat
6    English muffins
Split muffins and spread with shrimp mixture. Freeze on cookie sheet,
then store in plastic bag in freezer. When ready to serve, cut into 4
or 6 pieces, and bake at 400Â° for 6 minutes.
