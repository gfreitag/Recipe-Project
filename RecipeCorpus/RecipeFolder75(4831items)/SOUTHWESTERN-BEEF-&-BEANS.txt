Title: SOUTHWESTERN BEEF & BEANS
Categories: Beef
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Dried pinto beans
6 c  Cold water
1/2 lb Salt pork -- cut up
1 lb Lean chuck steak -- cut into
1    " cubes
1 ea Red chili peppers or....
1/2 ts Crushed red pepper
1 ea Medium onions -- chopped
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
6 oz Tomato paste
1 1/2 tb Chili powder
1 ts Salt
1 ts Cumin seed
1/2 ts Marjoram
Soak beans in water overnight.  Brown salt pork in skillet or
slow-cooking pot with browning unit.  In slow-cooking pot, combine
soaked beans (with water), browned pork, and remaining ingredients.
Cover and cook on low for 9 to 10 hours.
