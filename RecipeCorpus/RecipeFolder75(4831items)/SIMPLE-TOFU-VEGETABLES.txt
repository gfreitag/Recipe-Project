Title: SIMPLE TOFU VEGETABLES
Categories: Main dish, Side dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
2 md Onions, cubed
1 md Green bell pepper, cubed
1/2 lb Mushrooms, halved
1 lb Cubed tofu
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds
1 c  Water
1 ts Basil
1 ts Sage
In hot oil, saute onions till limp.  Add pepper, mushrooms, tofu,
seeds water & herbs.  Simmer till vegetables are limp. Serve over
cooked rice.
