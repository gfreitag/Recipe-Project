Title: SHEILA'S CAULIFLOWER
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
Cauliflower -- fresh or
Frozn
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Mustard
3/4 c  Milk
1 c  Cheddar cheese
Salt and pepper
1/4 c  Butter
Cook cauliflower until tender; drain; salt and pepper. Pour butter
over cauliflower.  Mix milk, mayonnaise and mustard together; pour
over cauliflower and sprinkle cheese on top.Cook 5-10 minutes at 350F.
