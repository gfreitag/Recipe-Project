Title: SPECIAL SALAD (M_C-TX)
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
1    Layer shredded lettuce
1    Layer chopped onion
1    Layer chopped green pepper
1    Layer chopped celery
Walnuts, chopped
1    Layer cooked and cooled
-frozen peas
Water chestnuts
Real bacon bits
Frost entire top with Miracle Whip. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons sugar over
the Mayonnaise or Miracle Whip. Cover with saran wrap and refrigerate
over night. Before serving sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese and layer
of crisp bacon pieces.
