Title: SCOTLAND OAT BREAD
Categories: Half-time, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Honey
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Water
3 tb Applesauce*
2 ts Salt
3 tb Gluten
3/4 c  Oat bran
1 1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 c  Oat flour
3 ts Yeast
Place in pan according to custom. *May use vegetable oil. Adapted from
Bread in Half the Time pg. 264
