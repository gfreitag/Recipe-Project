Title: SELYODKA POD UKROPNYM SOUSOM
Categories: Russian, Ethnic, Seafood, Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ea Herring boned & skinned
- cut into 1" diagonal pcs.
1/3 c  Red wine vinegar
1/2 c  Olive oil
2 ts Sugar
2 ts Dried dill chopped
- or -
2 tb Dill fresh & chopped fine
Mix everything except the fish together. Place fish in deep dish and
pour the mixture oevr it. Refrigerate for 4-6 hours.
