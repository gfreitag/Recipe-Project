Title: SOUR CREAM COLESLAW
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Dairy sour cream
2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Vinegar
1 tb Mayonnaise or salad dressing
6 c  Finely shredded cabbage
Blend together sour cream, sugar, salt, pepper, vinegar and
mayonnaise. Stir into cabbage. Source: Farm Journal's Cooking &
Entertaining in America
