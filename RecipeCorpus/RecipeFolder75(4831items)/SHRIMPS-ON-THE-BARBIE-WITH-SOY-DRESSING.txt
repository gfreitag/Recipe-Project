Title: SHRIMPS ON THE BARBIE WITH SOY DRESSING
Categories: Seafood, Main dish
Yield: 2 Servings
1 lb Jumbo Shrimp
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
3 tb Chinese Rice Wine
1 ts Ginger, minced
1    Galic Clove, minced
2 tb Honey
2 ts Seasame Oil
Shell & devein the shrimp, but leave the tails on. Combine the
remaining ingredients. Pour the dressing over the shrimp. Toss shrimp
to coat all surfaces. Let marinate for 1 hour. Grill shrimps about 4
to 6 inches from the coals for about 2 minutes per side, or until the
flesh is opaque.
