Title: SCRAMBLED EGGS - CHRISTIAN
Categories: Breakfast, Eggs
Yield: 6 Servings
12    Eggs -- beaten
1/3 c  Buttermilk
Salt and pepper -- to taste
2 tb Butter
Add buttermilk, salt, and pepper; mix well.  Melt butter in a large
skillet. add eggs and cook to desired doneness while stirring
constantly.
