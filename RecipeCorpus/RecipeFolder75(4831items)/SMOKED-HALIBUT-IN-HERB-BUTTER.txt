Title: SMOKED HALIBUT IN HERB BUTTER
Categories: Fish
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Smoked halibut
1 c  Butter
1    Garlic clove
1 pn Onion salt
1/4 ts Marjoram
1/8 ts Thyme
1 pn Ground oregano
Melt butter in saucepan over low heat. Add whole garlic, seasonings
and herbs. Remove from heat and let steep for an hour. Place halibut
in baking dish. Discard garlic and pour herb butter over fish. Warm
up in microwave or oven at 350 degrees F. for 15 minutes.

Andrea, Cordova, Alaska
