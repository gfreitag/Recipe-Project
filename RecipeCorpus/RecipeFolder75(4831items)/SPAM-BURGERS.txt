Title: SPAM BURGERS
Categories: Misc
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn Spam
3 tb Mayonaisse or salad dsg
6    Lettuce leaves
2    Sliced tomatoes
6 sl Cheese of your choice
6    Hamburger buns, split
Slice Spam into 6 slices. In skillet over medium heat, cook meat until
lightly browned. Spread mayonnaise on cut sides of buns. Layer
lettuce, tomato, meat and cheese on bun bottom. Add anything that
suits your fancy.

Source:  Geo. A. Hormel Co, 1992
