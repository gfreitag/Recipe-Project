Title: SOUR CREAM DILL SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
1 pn Freshly ground black pepper
1 pn Sugar
4 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Grated onion
2 tb Finely cut dill
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
Beat the egg until fluffy and lemon colored. Add the remaining
ingredients, blending in the sour cream last. Stir until blended and
chill.
