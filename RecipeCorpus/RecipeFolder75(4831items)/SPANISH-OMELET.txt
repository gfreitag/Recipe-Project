Title: SPANISH OMELET
Categories: Cajun, Main dish, Eggs, Cheese
Yield: 2 Servings
1    Onion,chopped
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 cn Tomatoes(8oz)
1 ts Salt
3    Eggs,well beaten
2 tb Hot pepper sauce
Crackers
Grated cheese
1. In medium skillet, brown onion in butter; stir in tomatoes, salt,
hot sauce and eggs.

2. When mixture is cooked, pour over crackers and sprinkle with grated
cheese.
