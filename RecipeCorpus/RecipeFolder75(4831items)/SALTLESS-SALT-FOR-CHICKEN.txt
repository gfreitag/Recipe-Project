Title: SALTLESS SALT FOR CHICKEN
Categories: Seasonings, Herb/spice
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Rosemary
1 tb Thyme
1 tb Marjoram
1 tb Savory
1 tb Basil
Grind to a fine powder and rub on chicken for roasting, grilling of
frying.
