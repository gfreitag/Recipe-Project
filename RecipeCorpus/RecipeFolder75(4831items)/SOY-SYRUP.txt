Title: SOY SYRUP
Categories:
2 c  thin soy sauce
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  brown sugar
1    lime juiced

Combine all and reduce at a simmer until a syrup is achieved, 70-80
percent reduction.

Slice tempura on the bias and some straight cuts. Zigzag wasabi oil
and soy syrup on the plate. Place small mound of leeks and surround
with slices. Garnish with sesame seeds and ginger.
