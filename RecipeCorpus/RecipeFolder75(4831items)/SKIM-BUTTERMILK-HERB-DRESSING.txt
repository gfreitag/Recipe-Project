Title: SKIM BUTTERMILK-HERB DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Buttermilk; (skim)
1/4 c  Fresh chives; snipped
2 tb Red onion; finely chopped
1 tb Mixed herbs; fresh, chopped
1/2 ts Garlic; minced
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Recipe by: McCall's In small bowl, combine buttermilk, chives, onion,
herbs, garlic, salt and pepper; whisk until dressing is blended and
smooth.
