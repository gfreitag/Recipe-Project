Title: SEASONED SALT
Categories: Misc.
Yield: 1 Servings
6 tb Salt
1/2 ts Thyme leaves
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/2 ts Garlic salt
2 1/4 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Curry powder
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Onion powder
1/8 ts Dillseeds
1/2 ts Celery salt
Combine all ingredients in a small jar; cover and shake until well
blended. Yield: about 1/2 cup.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in the 1970s. Typos by
Nancy Coleman. Via: RFIX_S 09-28-1994
