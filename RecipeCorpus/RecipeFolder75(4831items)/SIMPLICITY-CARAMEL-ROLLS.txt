Title: SIMPLICITY CARAMEL ROLLS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Loaves frozen bread dough
1 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Whipping cream
1 c  Nuts, chopped
2 tb Margarine, softened
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Cinnamon
Thaw bread dough.  Mix brown sugar and whipping cream. Pour mixture
into two 9 inch round baking pans. Sprinkle 1/2 cup of nuts over
brown mixture. Roll dough into 10x18 inch rectangle. Spread with
butter, sugar and cinnamon.  Roll in jelly roll fashion.  Cut 1 inch
slices and place in pans.  Cover and let rise until double in size.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to
25    minutes. Randy Rigg
