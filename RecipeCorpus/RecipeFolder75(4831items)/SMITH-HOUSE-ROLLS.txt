Title: SMITH HOUSE ROLLS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
-Pat Dwigans fwds07a-
1 pk Yeast
1 1/2 c  Lukewarm water
2    Eggs; unbeaten
4 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
3/4 c  Crisco oil
4 1/2 c  Plain flour
Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water.  Add other ingredients, adding
flour last.  Make a soft dough; knead lightly; set aside in a warm
place until double. Roll out on a floured surface, cut with round
cutter. Toll in melted butter and fold over.  Let rise again 30-35
minutes. Bake at 400~ for 10 minutes or until lightly browned.

Smith Boarding House was in Dahlonega, GA; not sure if it is still
there or not.
