Title: SAUTEED ORANGE ROUGHY ** SMCD91F
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 2 Servings
2 ts Margarine
1/2 c  White wine
1/2 ts Dill weed
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/4 c  Mushrooms, chopped
1/2 lb Orange roughy
Chopped parsley
Lemon wedges
Heat margarine, wine, dill week, garlic and mushrooms in skillet
until hot. Add fish; saute until fish flakes when tested with a fork.
Remove to a warm platter. Sprinkle with parsley. Garnish with lemon.
