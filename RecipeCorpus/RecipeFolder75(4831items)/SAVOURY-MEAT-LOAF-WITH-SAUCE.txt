Title: SAVOURY MEAT LOAF WITH SAUCE
Categories: Meats
Yield: 8 Servings
1    Can Cream of Mushroom and
Onion Soup
2 lb Lean ground round
1/3 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/4 c  Ketchup
2    Eggs, beaten
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Water
In large bowl, combine 1/2 cup soup, beef, bread crumbs, ketchup,
eggs, worcestershire sauce, mustard and pepper; mix throughly with
hands. Press mixture firmly in 9x5 inch loaf pan. Bake at 350F for 60
60 70 minutes or unitl done.  Drain liquid.  Let stand 5 to 10
minutes. In small saucepan over medium heat, combine remaining soup
and water. Heat through, stirring occasionally.  Serve with meat
loaf.  Serves 6 to 8 Source: Longo's Supermarket. Oakville, Ont.
