Title: RUSSIAN TEA #1
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 30 Servings
52 1/2 c  Tang (orange drink mix)
30 pk (1/2 oz) lemonade,
-unsweetened
30 c  Sugar (optional)
30 c  Instant tea
30 ts Cinnamon
15 ts Ground cloves
Dash salt
Mix ingredients.

Use 2 teaspoons per serving.

From: VIP cookbook, 1977, American Cancer Society. Typed by Dale &
Gail Shipp, Columbia Md.
