Title: SOUTHERN STYLE BLACKEYE PEAS
Categories: Vegetables, Pork, Grains, Southern
Yield: 1 Pot
2 c  Blackeye peas
1 c  Uncooked rice
1/2 ts Pepper
1 sm Ham hock
8 c  Cold water
Salt to taste
Place ham hock in water; bring to boil; reduce heat and simmer for 30
minutes.  Add peas to ham, continue simmering 1 1/2 hours, add rice
and simmer 45 minutes or until rice and peas are done and liquid is
almost absorbed.  Remove meat from bone/  Serve peas and rice on hot
platter, place ham on top.

Recipe on back of 2 lb. package
of Flavorite Blackeye Peas
