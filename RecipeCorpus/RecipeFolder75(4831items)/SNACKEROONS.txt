Title: SNACKEROONS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Batch
1 c  Grated chedder cheese
3/4 c  Flour
3/4 c  Rice crispies (slightly
Crushed)
1/2 c  Crushed walnuts
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/2 c  Butter (soft)
6 sl Crisp crushed bacon
2 tb Bacon drippings or oil
4 ts Ice water (to 5 tsp)
Combine all of the ingredients, form into balls and bake for 10 to 12
minutes at 375F.
