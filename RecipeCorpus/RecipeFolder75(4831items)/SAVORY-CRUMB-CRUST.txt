Title: SAVORY CRUMB CRUST
Categories: Vegetarian, Crust
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Bread crumbs, whole wheat;
--fine
1/4 c  Flour, whole wheat
1/2 c  Wheat germ, raw
1/2 c  Oats, rolled, raw
1/4 ts Salt
Basil and/or marjoram to
--taste
1/2 c  Margarine, melted
Mix together all the dry ingredients, combining thoroughly.

Drizzle in the melted margarine, and toss with a fork, until the
mixture is uniformly moistened.

Press into an oiled 9" pie pan and prebake at 350 for
10    minutes.

Adapted from The Enchanted Broccoli Forest by Mollie Katzen, MM by
Dianne Smith/DEEANNE
