Title: SPANISH SPINACH
Categories: Spain, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Fresh spinach, washed and
-trimmed
1 ts Olive oil
1/4 c  Pine nuts
1/4 c  Golden raisins
-Salt to taste
Saute spinach in olive over medium heat until wilted. Remove from
heat; add remaining ingredients and mix well. Serves 4.
