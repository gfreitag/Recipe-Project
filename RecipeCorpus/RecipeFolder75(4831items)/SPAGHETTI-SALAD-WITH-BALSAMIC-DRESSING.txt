Title: SPAGHETTI SALAD WITH BALSAMIC DRESSING
Categories: Pasta, Salads, Appetizers, Easy
Yield: 8 Servings
SALAD:
8 oz Uncooked spaghetti, broken
-into thirds
6    Green onions, cut into 1/4
-inch pieces
4    Italian plum tomatoes, cut
-in half lengthwise, sliced
4 oz Mozzarella cheese, diced
(1 cup )
DRESSING:
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Dried basil leaves
1/4 ts Salt
Cook spaghetti to desired doneness as directed on package. Drain;
rinse in cold water to cool.

In medium bowl, combine cooked spaghetti and all remaining salad
ingredients.

In small bowl, combine all dressing ingredients; blend well. Add to
salad; toss to coat.  Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate
until serving time.  Yield: 8 (3/4-cup) servings. Typed in MMFormat
by cjhartlin@msn.com
