Title: SAVANNAH SOYS
Categories: Beans
Yield: 10 Servings
6 c  Soybeans -- cooked
2 tb Molasses
OR 2 drops maple flavoring
2 tb Brown sugar
Juice of 1 lemon
Salt -- to taste
1 cn Tomato soup
1 md Onion -- whole
2 tb Oil
Mix all ingredients except the onion.  Place in bean pot or baking
dish. Put the whole onion in the center. Bake in moderate oven,
adding a little bean broth or tomato juice as needed to keep moist.
Bake 1 hour or longer.
