Title: SOUTHERN SWEET POTATO PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Grated sweet potatoes
1 cn (6 oz) evaporated milk
1/4 ts Mace
1/4 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1 pn Of salt
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Butter, melted
2 tb Molasses
1 c  Sweet milk
2    Eggs
3    To 4 tbsp bourbon whiskey
1 ts Lemon juice
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Beat eggs lightly, add sugar, stir in
potatoes and seasoning. Add milk and butter. Bake in a 2 quart
casserole 30 minutes, then stir away from the sides, mix and bake
another 30 minutes. Serve warm from the baking dish with Red Currant
Jelly Sauce. Serves 6 to 8.

From: Family magazine Nov 91 Posted by: Bill McGimpsey
