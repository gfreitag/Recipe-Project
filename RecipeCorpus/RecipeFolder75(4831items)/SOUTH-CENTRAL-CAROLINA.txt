Title: SOUTH CENTRAL CAROLINA: BASTE/BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Peanut oil
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
1 ea Onion, minced
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/3 c  Cider Vinegar
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Honey
1 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Dry mustard
1 ts Ginger
-Salt to taste
Mix thoroughly and allow flavors to blend.
