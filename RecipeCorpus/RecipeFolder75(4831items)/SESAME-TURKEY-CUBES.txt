Title: SESAME TURKEY CUBES
Categories: Appetizers, Tvg
Yield: 1 Servings
T. Grant, HWWK11B
3 lb Cooked Turkey Breast
-cut into 1" cubes
1 c  Mayonnaise
3/8 c  Soy Sauce
1/8 c  Sesame Oil
1 cl Garlic, finely minced
1 1/2 ts Dijon mustard
1 1/2 ts Szechuan hot oil (to taste)
1 c  Toasted sesame seed
Mix together all ingredients except cubed turkey. Place a long
toothpick with fancy end into each turkey cube. Dip first into
mayonnaise mixture, then into sesame seeds. Chill until serving time.
Theresa V. Grant, HWWK11B.
