Title: SNACK: PERKY PARTY MIX
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  M&M's Plain or Peanut
Chocolate Candies
3 c  Thin pretzel sticks, broken
In half
3 c  Bite-size Cheddar cheese
Crackers
1 1/2 c  Raisins
Combine all ingredients.  Serve as a snack.  Makes about 8 cups mix.
