Title: SHIRLEY TEMPLE
Categories: Non-alcohol, Gin, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ds Grenadine
6 oz Ginger ale -- or to fill
Fill a collins or highball glass with ice. Add grenadine and fill with
ginger ale. Garnish with a maraschino cherry and an orange slice.
