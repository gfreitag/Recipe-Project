Title: SOURSOP SHERBERT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Sugar
2 c  Water
2 c  Soursop puree
1 tb Lemon juice
1    Egg white
Boil sugar and water for five minutes, cool to lukewarm. Add remaining
ingredients. Freeze in freezer until a bit firm. Remove and beat until
fluffy. Do this two times. Yield 1 quart.
