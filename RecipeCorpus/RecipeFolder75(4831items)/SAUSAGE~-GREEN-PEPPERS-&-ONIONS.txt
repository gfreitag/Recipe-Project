Title: SAUSAGE~ GREEN PEPPERS & ONIONS
Categories: Pork, Italian, Meats
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Rope sausage *
2    Green peppers, sliced
1/4 ts Salt
2 md Onions, sliced
1/4 ts Pepper
4 tb Butter or margarine
* Cut sausage into 5-inch sections.

1.  In a large pot, boil sausage for approximately 30 minutes.

2.  In a skillet, melt butter and on medium low heat, add green
peppers and onions.  Cover.  Stir occasionally until peppers and
onions are tender.

3.  Remove vegetables from skillet and add sausage links . Fry on
medium heat until brown.   Add green peppers and onions. Turn off
heat. 4. Serve on hot dog buns with mustard.

Serves 8
