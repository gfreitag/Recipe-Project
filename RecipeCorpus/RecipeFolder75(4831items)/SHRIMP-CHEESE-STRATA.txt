Title: SHRIMP-CHEESE STRATA
Categories: Appetizers, Crocker
Yield: 3 Cups
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
1/4 c  Dairy sour cream
1 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Curry powder
1/4 ts Salt
4 1/4 oz Tiny shrimp, rinsed and
-drained
1    Hard-cooked egg, chopped
3 tb Finely chopped green onions
-(with tops)
Flatbread
Mix cream cheese, sour cream, lemon juice, curry powder and salt.
Spread about 1/4 inch thick on 8-inch plate. Top with shrimp and egg;
sprinkle with green onions. Serve with flatbread. 3 cups spread; 25
calories per tb.

Source: Betty Crocker's Cookbook, 6th Edition
