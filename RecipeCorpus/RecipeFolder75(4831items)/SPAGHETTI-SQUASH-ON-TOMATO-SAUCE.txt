Title: SPAGHETTI SQUASH ON TOMATO SAUCE
Categories: Low, Fat
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2    Spaghetti Squash
1 cn Diced Italian Tomatoes
Bake squash in tray and about 1/2 inch of water at about 350F until
soft and stringy. Scoop squash contents into plastic lunch box. Dump
one can of Italian Tomatoes on top. DO NOT MIX!

At work: Mix and nuke for 3 minutes on HIGH.
