Title: SOFT MOLASSES APPLE COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Apples
Yield: 60 Servings
MMMMM--------------------LORELI AGUDA WSKD49A-------------------------
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 c  Molasses
1 tb Lemon juice
3 1/2 c  All purpose flour, sifted
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Ginger
2 ts Baking soda
1/8 ts Salt
1/3 c  Boiling water
1 c  Tart apples, pared, cored,
-and finely chopped
Cream shortening and sugar. Beat in egg. Stir in molasses and lemon
juice; blend well. Sift together dry indredients; add to creamed
mixture. Stir in boiling water; mix well. Add chopped apples; mix.
Chill thoroughly. Drop by teaspoons on greased baking sheets. Bake in
350~ oven for 8 to 10 minutes.
