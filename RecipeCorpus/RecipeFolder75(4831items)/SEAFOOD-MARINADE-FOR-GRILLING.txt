Title: SEAFOOD MARINADE FOR GRILLING
Categories: Seafood, Seasonings
Yield: 1 Servings
Fish, can be salmon,
Swordfish, shark or
Kabobs made of shrimp
Or scallops or
Mixture with above
Juice 3 lemons
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Dijon mustard
1/4 c  Fresh snipped dill
1/4 ts Salt
Fresh ground pepper (to
Taste)
1    Clove garlic, minced
(optional)
Mix all ingredients well.  Marinade fish 2 hours in refrigerator.
Place fish on hot grill 8 to 14 minutes per side depending on
thickness. Baste with marinade.
