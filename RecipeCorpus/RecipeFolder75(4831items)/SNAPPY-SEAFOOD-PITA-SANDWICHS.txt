Title: SNAPPY SEAFOOD PITA SANDWICHS
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
2 pk Crab or lobster delights*
1 md Tomato, chopped
1/2 c  Red onion, finely chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper, chopped
1/2 c  Yogurt, plain low-fat
2 oz Feta cheese, fine crumbled
1/4 c  Cilantro, chopped
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 cl Garlic, minced
1/4 ts Black pepper, fresh ground
4    Pita breads, cut in half
*Louis Kemp Fat Free Crab or Lobster Delights.  May use up to 3 oz
feta cheese and up to 6 pita breads.

In a large bowl, combine all ingreditents ::except:: bread; mix well.
Chill.  Serve seafood mixture in pita bread halves OR on lettuce
leaves, as desired.
