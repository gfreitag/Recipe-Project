Title: SOUTHERN GRAVY
Categories: Sauces, Beef
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Beef instant bouillon
1 1/2 c  Unsifted flour
1/2 ts Black pepper
3 tb Drippings or margarine
1 3/4 c  Milk or water
Make ready mix by combining first 3 ingredients in medium bowl.Store
at room temperature in pint jar with tight fitting lid.Shake before
using.

For Gravy: Stir 1/4 cup ready mix into drippings.Cook and stir until
brown.(This is to cook the flour;do not burn it.) Add water or milk;
stir until thick and bouillon is dissolved.
