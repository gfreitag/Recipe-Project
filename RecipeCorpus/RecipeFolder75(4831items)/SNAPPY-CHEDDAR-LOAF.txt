Title: SNAPPY CHEDDAR LOAF
Categories: Breads, Cheese
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Butter Or Regular Margarine;
Softened
1/2 c  Cold Packed Cheddar Cheese
Food
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/8 ts Tabasco Sauce
1    French Bread; 20-Inch Loaf
Combine the butter, cheese food, Worcestershire sauce and Tabasco
Sauce in a medium mixing bowl.  Beat, with an electric mixer set at
medium speed, until well blended, then set aside. Cut the French
bread into 1-inch slices.  Spread the slices with the butter mixture
and reassemble the slices to form a loaf on a 26-inch length of
heavy-duty foil. Securely wrap the loaf into a loose packet and
refrigerate. When ready to grill, cook the bread 4-inches from gray
coals (medium heat). Grill about 15 minutes turning once, or until
the butter mixture is melted and the bread is hot.

Makes 1 loaf.
