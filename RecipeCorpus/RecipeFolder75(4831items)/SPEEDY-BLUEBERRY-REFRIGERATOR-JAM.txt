Title: SPEEDY BLUEBERRY REFRIGERATOR JAM
Categories: Canning
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Fresh or frozen blueberries
2 c  Sugar
3 oz Lemon flavored gelatin
In large saucepan, combine all three ingredients. Bring to aboil.
Cook and stir for 2 minutes. Pour into jars; refrigerate.
Yield: 3 cups
