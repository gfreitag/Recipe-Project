Title: SEASONING SALT
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Salt
1 tb Celery salt
1 tb Pepper
2 tb Dry mustard
4 tb Paprika
2 tb Garlic powder
1 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Oregano
Mix all ingredients together and store in an airtight container.

Orginated: Jackie Lelleman, Vernon, B.C. Source: St. James School &
Parish Cookbook, 1986

From the collection of K. Deck
