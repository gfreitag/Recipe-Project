Title: SCOTTISH OATEN BREAD - WITH COKE
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Rolled oats, old-fashioned
1/2 c  Sugar
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 lg Egg
3 tb Oil, or shortening, melted
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  COCA-COLA
1 c  Prunes, very well drained
- and coarsely chopped, OR
- 1/2 c dried prunes
1/2 c  Walnuts, chopped
Prune halves, optional
Lightly spoon the flour into a measuring cup, level off. . In a large
bowl, stir together the flour, rolled oats, sugar, baking powder,
baking soda, and salt. In another bowl, beat with a fork, the egg,
oil, and vanilla extract until well blended. Add to the flour
mixture. Add the Coca-Cola, prunes, and nuts, blend thoroughly with a
spoon. Turn into a well-greased, lightly floured, 9 x 5 x 3-inch loaf
pan. Garnish with prune halves, if desired.  Bake in a preheated, 350
F oven for about 1 hour or until a wooden toothpick inserted in the
centre comes out cleans. Cool on a rack for 20 minutes before
removing the bread from the pan. Store in foil overnight before
slicing.
