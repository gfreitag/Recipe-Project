Title: SESAME~ MUSTARD RUB
Categories: Marinades, Bar-b-q
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------BEEF,POULTRY,PORK,LAMB,FISH---------------------
1    Garlic clove; crushed
1 ts Mustard seeds
1/2 ts Finely slivered lime peel
2 ts Lime juice
2 tb Sesame oil
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mash until fairly smooth. Makes
about 3-1/2 Tbsp.
