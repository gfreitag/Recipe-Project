Title: SERRANO & GINGER CUSTARD ("PEPPERS A COOKBO
Categories: Desserts, Dairy
Yield: 8 Servings
4 lg Eggs
1 c  Sugar, granulated
2 tb Liqueur, Orange flavor
1 tb Ginger, fresh
- peeled and minced
3    Peppers, Serrano
Red or Green
- seeded and diced
1 qt Cream, heavy
Whisk eggs and sugar in nonreactive metal bowl until smooth. Add
orange liqueur, ginger, peppers and cream; set bowl over simmering
water; whisk until mixture thickens (about 30 minutes) Divide into 8
small serving bowls, refrigerate until firm (about 2 hours).
