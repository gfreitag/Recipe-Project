Title: SPAGHETTI SAUCE 04/17/93
Categories: Sauces, Pasta
Yield: 10 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground chuck
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1    12 oz can tomato paste
2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Thyme
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1 cn Cooked tomatoes (28 oz)
2 ts Garlic salt
1/2 ts Basil
1/4 ts Pepper
Microwave chuck, garlic & onion in Ultra 21 2 Qt. Casserole with
Colander for 4 minutes.  Pour off fat and transfer mixture to 2 Qt.
casserole. Stir in remaining ingredients. and cook on half power
18-21 minutes, stirring several times.  Cover & let stand 5 minutes.
This makes enough sauce to adequately cover 1 lb of Spaghetti.
