Title: SHRIMP-CHEESE BAKE
Categories: Main dish, White wine
Yield: 8 Servings
6    Eggs
1/2 c  Dry white wine
3/4 ts Dry mustard
10    Slices white bread,1/4"
2 cn Small shrimp(4 1/2oz)
2 1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Snipped parsley
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Shredded American cheese
Heat oven to 325'. Beat eggs, milk, wine, parsley, mustard and salt
with rotary beater. Stir in remaining ingredients. Pour into
ungreased baking dish, 11 3/4x7 1/2x1 3/4 inches. Bake uncovered
until center is set, about
1    hour.
