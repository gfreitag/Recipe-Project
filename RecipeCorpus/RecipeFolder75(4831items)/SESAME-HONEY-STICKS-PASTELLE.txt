Title: SESAME HONEY STICKS PASTELLE
Categories: Candies, Greek
Yield: 1 Servings
Karen Mintzias
1 c  Sesame seeds
1 c  Honey
Spread the sesame seeds on a pan and toast in preheated oven at
350 degrees F for 5 minutes.  Bring honey to a boil. Add seeds and
continue cooking on medium heat to 280 F (light crack reading). Watch
it carefully, it burns quickly.
Pour into buttered pan, and flatten with spatula to 1/2"
thickness. Score into 2" pieces while hot. Allow to cool. Store in
plastic bag.

From: "The Complete Greek Cookbook" by Theresa Karas Yianilos. Avenel
Books, New York.
