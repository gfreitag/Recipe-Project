Title: SOY WHIPPED CREAM
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Soy milk
1/2 c  Oil
1 tb Maple Syrup
1/2 ts Vanilla
Place soy milk 1/4 cup oil in blender. Blend at highest speed and
slowly drizzle in remaining 1/4 cup oil. Blend in syrup and vanilla.
Add a little more oil if necessary to thicken. Chill and serve.

NOTE: This recipe does not work well on damp, rainy days. 95% of its
calories are from FAT 1050 calories in entire recipe Andrew Barbara
