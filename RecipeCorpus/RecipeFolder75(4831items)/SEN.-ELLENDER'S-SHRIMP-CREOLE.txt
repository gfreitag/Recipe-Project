Title: SEN. ELLENDER'S SHRIMP CREOLE
Categories: Wildgame, Seafood
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Basic Sauce recipe
3 lb Shrimp, peeled
8 oz Tomato paste
Cook tomato paste with basic sauce thoroughly. Add shrimp and continue
cooking for 15 to 20 minutes, stirring as necessary. Serve with rice.
Source:  DIXIE magazine, New Orleans LA Oct 03 1971 Recipe date:
10/03/71
