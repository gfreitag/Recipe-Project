Title: SIAM CHILI DIPPING SAUCE
Categories: Appetizers, Dips
Yield: 2 Servings
1/3 c  Water
1/3 c  White Vinegar
1/3 c  Granulated Sugar
1/2 ts Chili Paste With Garlic
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic; Minced
In a heavy sauce (preferably enamel), bring the water to a boil. Add
the remaining ingredients and return to a boil. Cook for 2 to 3
minutes or until the sugar is dissolved. Serve hot. Makes about 1 cup
of dipping sauce. SUGGESTED DIPPERS:  Fried Wonton, Egg Rolls, Noodle
Chips, Seafood
