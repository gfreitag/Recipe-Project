Title: SNAPPY CHICKEN BALLS
Categories: Appetizers, Holiday
Yield: 80 To 90
2 c  Chicken; cooked, finely
-chopped
1 1/2 c  Almonds; finely chopped
1 tb Green onion; finely chopped
4 tb Major Grey's chutney;
-chopped
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 ts Curry powder
Salt to taste
1 c  Grated coconut
Candied orange peel
-optional
Combine chicken, almonds, green onion and chutney.  In a separate
bowl, blend cream cheese, mayonnaise and curry powder.  Combine
chicken mixture and cream cheese mixture.  Chill.  Shape into
bite-size balls. Roll each ball in grated coconut.  Refrigerate,
covered, until ready to serve. Garnish with tiny yellow and white
flowers or candied orange peel. Makes 80 to 90 balls and will keep 3
days. From The Best of the Best from Mississippi
