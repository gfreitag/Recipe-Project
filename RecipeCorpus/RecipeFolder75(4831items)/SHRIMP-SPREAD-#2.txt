Title: SHRIMP SPREAD #2
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood, Spreads
Yield: 2 Servings
16 oz (2 Pk) Softened Cream Cheese
1/2 lb Cooked Shrimp Chopped
1 tb Prepared Horseradish
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Lemon Juice
Finely Chopped Green Onions*
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1/8 ts Garlic Powder
*  Green onions should be to taste.  1 to 2 Tablespoons.
~------------------------------------------------------
~------------------ In small mixer bowl, beat cheese until fluffy;
gradually beat in lemon juice.  Stir in remaining ingredients. Chill
to blend flavors. Garnish as desired.  Serve with crackers or fresh
vegetables. Refrigerate left- overs.
