Title: SLOPPY TOMS
Categories: Vegetarian, Sandwiches
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Oil
1    Onion; chopped
1/2    Green bell pepper
1/2 c  Water
1    Stalk celery
1 c  Soy beans; chopped
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/8 c  Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Saute onions, pepper and celery in oil until tender. Add remaining
ingredients. Heat through. Serve on buns.
