Title: SKILLET CHICKEN & WILD RICE
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Cut-up cooked chicken
2 1/4 c  Water
1/3 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1 sm Onion, chopped (about  1/4
-cup)
1 cn (10 3/4 ounces) condensed
-cream of mushroom soup
1 pk (l6 ounces) seasoned long
-grain and wild rice
An easy "toss-together" dish for even your busiest nights.

Mix all ingredients in 10-inch skillet. Heat to boiling; reduce heat.
Cover and simmer 25 to 30 minutes or until rice is tender and liquid
is absorbed.

6    servings.
