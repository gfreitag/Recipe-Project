Title: SAUSAGE STROGANOFF
Categories: Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Pork sausages
1 c  Onion, chopped
1 c  Mushrooms
1 cn Tomato paste
3/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Wine
1    Salt, pepper, garlic & dill
1 c  Sour cream
1 tb Flour
Cook sausage, onion and mushrooms. Stir in tomato paste, water, wine
and spices. Bring to boil. Reduce heat. Cover, simmer 10 minutes.
Combine sour cream and flour. Stir into meat mixture. Stir till
thickened. Serve over noodles.
