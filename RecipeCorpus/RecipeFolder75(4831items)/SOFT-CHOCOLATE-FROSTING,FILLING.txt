Title: SOFT CHOCOLATE FROSTING/FILLING
Categories: Chocolate, Fillings, Frostings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sugar
3 tb Potato starch -- (or corn
Starch)
1/8 ts Salt
1 c  Boiling water
2 tb Margarine
1 c  Chocolate chips -- (6 oz)
1 ts Vanilla -- or pkg vanilla
Sugar
Mix sugar, starch, and salt, add boiling water.  Cook, stirring
constantly, till it comes to a boil, boil one min. and remove from he
at. Stir in chips till smooth.  Stir in margarine and vanilla. Spread
while warm.

If using vanilla sugar, add with the regular sugar.
