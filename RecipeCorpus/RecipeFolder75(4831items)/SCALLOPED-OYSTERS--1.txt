Title: SCALLOPED OYSTERS -1
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pt Oysters; (select or large)
1/2    Light cream
3 c  Ritz crackers; crumbled
1/2 c  Margarine; melted
1 ts Salt
2 ts Celery seed
1/4 ts Pepper
Recipe by: "Life goes on." <JEANL@UCS.INDIANA.EDU> Preheat oven to 375
degrees. Grease 11 1/2 x 7 1/2 x 1 1/2 baking dish. Arrange oyster
(with liquor) in baking dish. Pour about half the cream over oysters.

Combine crackers, margrine and seasonings, sprinkle over oysters. Pour
remaining cream over oysters and seasonings mixture.

Bake uncovered 30-40 minutes.
