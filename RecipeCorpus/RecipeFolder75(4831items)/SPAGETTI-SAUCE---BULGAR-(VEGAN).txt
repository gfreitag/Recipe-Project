Title: SPAGETTI SAUCE - BULGAR (VEGAN)
Categories: Digest, Dec.
Yield: 1 Servings
x  Water for saute (or your
Favorite, but compatible
Sub )
1 c  Chopped onion
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1 cn Chopped peeled tomatoes,
With liquid (14oz)
1 cn Tomatoe sauce (8oz)
1 tb Parsley
1    Bay leaf
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 c  Cooked bulgur
In 3qt pan, saute onion and garlic. Stir in tomatoes, sauce, and
spices. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer, uncovered, 20
minutes. Discard bay leaf, stir in bulgur and serve over your
favorite pasta, or spagetti squash.
