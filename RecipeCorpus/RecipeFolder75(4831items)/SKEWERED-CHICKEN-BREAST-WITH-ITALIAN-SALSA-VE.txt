Title: SKEWERED CHICKEN BREAST WITH ITALIAN SALSA VE
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
Boneless skinless chicken
Breast halves
Bamboo skewers
Vinaigrette----
Red wine vinegar
Anchovy paste
Capers
Garlic
Chopped parsley
Cut boned chicken breast halves into 8 to 10 cubes (about 1/2 inch)
and thread on a bamboo skewer. Broil 3 inches from heat for 2
minutes; turn and cook for 1 minute more. Serve with a vinaigrette
flavored with red wine vinegar, anchovy paste, capers, garlic, and
chopped parsley for dipping.
