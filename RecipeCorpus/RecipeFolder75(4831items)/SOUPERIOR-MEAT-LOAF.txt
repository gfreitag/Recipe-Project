Title: SOUPERIOR MEAT LOAF
Categories: Beef, Harned 1994, Main dish, Soups
Yield: 8 Servings
1    Envelope Lipton Recipe
-Secrets onion soup mix
2 lb Ground beef
1 1/2 c  Fresh bread crumbs
2    Eggs
3/4 c  ;Water
1/3 c  Ketchup
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients.  In a large baking pan,
shape into a loaf.  Bake at 350 F. for 1 hour or until done. Also
good (according to the package) with their beefy onion,
onion-mushroom or beefy mushroom soup mixes.)

Recipe on Lipton Onion soup mix package.  Electronic format by Cathy
Harned.
