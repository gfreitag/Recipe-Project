Title: SHRIMP & PISTACHIO SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood, Spreads
Yield: 2 Cups
1 c  Creamed cottage cheese
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Onion powder
1 c  Chopped cooked shrimp OR
1 cn (4 1/2oz) tiny shrimp,
Rinsed and drained
1/2 c  Chopped pistachio nuts
Place cottage cheese, lemon juice and onion powder in blender or food
processor. Cover and blend on high speed about 2 minutes, stopping
blender occasionally to scrape sides, or process, 30 seconds or until
smooth. Pour into small bowl. Stir in shrimp and nuts. Cover and
refrigerate at least 1 hour. Serve with assorted crackers or ruffled
potato chips if desired.
