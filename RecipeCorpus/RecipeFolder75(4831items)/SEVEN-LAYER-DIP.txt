Title: SEVEN LAYER DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Mexican
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn Refried beans (for bottom)
2    Ea tomatoes, diced*
1    Bunch sliced green onions
1    Sm. can chopped black olives
1 c  Guacamole
1 pk Taco seasoning
1 c  Sour cream
2 c  Shredded cheddar (top)
*   squeeze seeds and juice from tomato
~------------------------------------------------------
~---------------- Mix taco seasoning with sour cream. Starting with
beans and ending with cheese layer ingredients in 8x8" square pan.
