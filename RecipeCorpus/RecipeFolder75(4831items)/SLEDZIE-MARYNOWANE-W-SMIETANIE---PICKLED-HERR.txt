Title: SLEDZIE MARYNOWANE W SMIETANIE - PICKLED HERR
Categories: Fish
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Pickled herring, drained
1 lg Onion, chopped
1    Garlic clove, crushed
6    Eggs, hardboiled, peeled and
Chopped
1    Apple, cored, chopped
1 ts Lemon juice
1 c  Sour cream
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Dill, fresh, chopped
- or use parsley
1. Cut herring into small cubes.  Mix herring with onion, eggs,
apple, and lemon juice. 2. Combine sour cream, garlic, salt and
pepper; add to herring mixture and mix well. Sprinkle with dill or
parsley. 3. Serve with rye bread.
