Title: SHOJI'S HOUSE DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Japanese, Dressings
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Oil
5 tb Onion -- mince
3 tb Rice vinegar
3 1/2 ts Light soy sauce
3 1/2 ts Ketchup
3 1/2 ts Water
1 1/2 ts Celery -- mince
1 ts Ginger -- grate
1 ts Lemon peel
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground pepper
Blend until smooth; store covered. Drain salad well and toss with
dressing immediately before serving, or serve dressing on the table.
