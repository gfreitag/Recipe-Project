Title: SOUSA PORTUGESE BEANS
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Pinto beans
16 oz Tomato sauce
1 lg Onion chopped
1    Ham hock
1    Clove garlic
1/2 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Chili powder
3 ts Sugar
Salt and pepper
Put beans into large pot and cover with 3 inches of water. Add all
ingredients except the sugar and cook until done. Add the sugar. I
like to throw in some Linguica sausage when I have it.
