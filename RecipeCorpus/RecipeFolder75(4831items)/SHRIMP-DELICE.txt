Title: SHRIMP DELICE
Categories: Seafood, Appetizers, France
Yield: 8 Servings
24    Small shrimp, deveined and c
-ooked
1 tb Chopped chives
8    Artichoke bottoms sprinkled
-with pepper
2 oz Brie, cut into 8 pieces
Place shrimp and chives on artichoke bottoms.  Top with Brie. Place
under broiler until cheese is melted. Serve hot; sprinkle with
additional chopped chives if desired.
