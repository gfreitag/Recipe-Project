Title: SOPA DE AJO
Categories: Soups/stews, Cheese/eggs, Mexican, Garlic
Yield: 4 Servings
4 sl Firm white bread
4 c  Stock
1 tb Butter
1    Bay leaf
1 tb Olive oil
1 ts Lemon juice
4    Cloves garlic, minced
4    Eggs
Serves 4

Trim crusts from bread, dice.  Cook bread cubes and garlic in butter
and oil over medium-high heat until cubes are golden. Remove and keep
warm. Combine stock, bay leaf and lemon juice in same pan. Bring to a
simmer over medium heat.  Break eggs, one at a time into a saucer and
slip into hot broth.  Poach until whites are set but yolks are still
liquid (4 minutes). With a slotted spoon, carefully transfer each egg
to a warm soup bowl. Ladle broth over eggs and sprinkle with
croutons. Makes 6 cups.
