Title: SAVORY CHICKEN BY DOROTHY GREINKE
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Chicken Breast halves skined
4 tb Olive Oil, divided
2    Red bell pepper, seeded *
1 tb Parsley, finely chopped
1/3 c  Flour
1    Onion, large, sliced
1 c  Mushroons, freshed, sliced**
*  Cut into 1" strips ** Salt and pepper to taste.

1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees 2. In a shallow plate dredge chicken
in flour; shake off excess.
3. In a large skillet heat 2 tablespoons of the oil. Quickly saute
the
chicken over medium-high heat, turning over once, untill lightly
browned. Remove to a 2-quart, covered baking dish.
4. Heat remaining oil in skillet. Over medium heat saute onions,
pepper
strips and mushrooms, tossing occasionally, untill onions are
wilted
and peppers are crisp-tender.  Season with salt and pepper. Cover
chicken with pepper mixture.
5. Bake, covered for 30 minutes 6. Uncover; bake 10 minutes more.
7. Sprinkle parsley over all. Serve.
