Title: SCHNITZEL BEANS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 qt Green beans -- cut
2 tb Bacon -- chopped
1 tb Shortening
3 md Onions -- sliced
4 md Tomatoes -- sliced
1 ts Salt
1 c  Hot water
ds Red pepper
Fry bacon crisp; add remaining ingredients.  Simmer for 1 hour. Add
water as needed.
