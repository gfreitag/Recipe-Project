Title: SESAME CHICKEN THIGHS
Categories: Main dish, Oriental
Yield: 5 Servings
10 ea Chicken thighs
3 tb Light soy sauce
2 tb Dry Sherry
3/16 ts Grated fresh ginger
3 tb Cornstarch
4 tb Raw sesame seeds
1 x  Peanut oil for deep frying
Cut the chicken thighs in half across the bone.
Marinate them in the soy sauce, sherry, ginger & enough cornstarch
to make a thick sauce for 1 Hr.  Add the sesame seeds. Deep fry
peices in peanut oil at 375 until lightly browned. You can try
substituting beef for the chicken.
