Title: SCALLOPED RHUBARB
Categories: Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
-EILEEN LAMPARELLI  CGDR39A
3 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/2 c  Butter
3 c  Rhubarb; raw; diced
1 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Water
Preheat your oven to 325.  Toss the bread crumbs with the melted
butter in a bowl. Add the rhubarb and sugar and mix gently. Place the
mixture in a 7x11" casserole and pour 1/3C. of the water over it.
Bake uncovered for 45 minutes.
