Title: SOUTH OF THE BORDER FLIP (TVP TACOS)
Categories: Digest, Mar95
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Tvp, dry
1 3/4 c  Boiling water
1 cn Tomatoes, canned -- 28 oz
1 ts Basil
1 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Cumin
1/2 ts Thyme
1 ts Salt
1 ea Pepper -- to taste
1 tb Chili powder
1 ea Onion -- diced
Reconstitute TVP in boiling water. Simmer TVP, and all the
ingredients to blend.

Source: Adapted from something in the Food section of our paper last
week (The Columbus Dispatch).
