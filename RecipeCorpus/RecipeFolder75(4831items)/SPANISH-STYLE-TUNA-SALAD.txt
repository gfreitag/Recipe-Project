Title: SPANISH STYLE TUNA SALAD
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
8 c  Bite-size pieces salad
-greens
3 tb Olive or vegetable oil
4 ts Red wine vinegar
1/8 ts Salt
Freshly ground pepper
1/2 sm Red onion, thinly sliced
12    Pitted green olives
3    Roma (plum) tomatoes,sliced
1 cn (6 oz.) tuna in water,
-drained and flaked
Toss salad greens, oil, vinegar, salt and pepper in large bowl.

Add remaining ingredients; toss.  Makes 4 servings. Typed in MMFormat
