Title: SHEENA'S CABBAGE BEEF SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 12 Servings
1 md Head of cabbage
3 cn Kidney beans
1 lg Jar of Worchestire sauce
1 cn Tomatoes (optional)
2 md Green mangos
3 cn Water
Salt to taste
Chop up cabbage, and green mangos.  Combine in large pot with beans
and water.  Cook for 5 minutes and add Worchestire sauce. Continue to
cook until cabbage is just slightly limp but still crispy.
