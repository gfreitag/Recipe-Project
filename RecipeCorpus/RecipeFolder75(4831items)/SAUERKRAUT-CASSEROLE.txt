Title: SAUERKRAUT CASSEROLE
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Italian sausage, mild
-cut into 1-inch slices
1    Onion, lg. -chopped
2    Apples; peeled and quartered
1 cn Sauerkraut, 27oz; undrained
1 c  -water
1/2 c  Brown sugar; packed
2 ts Caraway seed
In a skillet, cook sausage and onion until sausage is brown and
onion is tender; drain. Stir in apples, sauerkraut, water, brown
sugar and caraway seed. Transfer to a 2-1/2 quart baking dish.
Cover and bake at 350F for 1 hour. Garnish with snipped parsley if
desired.
