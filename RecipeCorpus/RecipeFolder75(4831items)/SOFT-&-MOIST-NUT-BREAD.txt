Title: SOFT & MOIST NUT BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
1/4 c  Water
3/4 c  Cottage cheese
3/4 c  Sour cream
3 tb Sugar
1 1/2 tb Butter
2 1/2 c  White bread flour
2 ts Yeast
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Broken walnuts
1/3 c  Quick cooking oats
1/2 c  Raisins
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Soda
2 ts Cinnamon
Heat first 5 ingredients till lukewarm.  Put remaining ingredients in
pan, add warmed ingredients, and press start. This is a wonderful
bread.
