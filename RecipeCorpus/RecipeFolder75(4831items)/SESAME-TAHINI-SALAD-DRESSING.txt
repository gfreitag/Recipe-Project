Title: SESAME TAHINI SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Vegan
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Tahini
1 t  Dijon mustard
1    Garlic cloves, or to taste
2 T  Tamari or soy sauce
1/2 t  Pepper, or to taste
1/2 c  Water
Combine all ingredients in a blender and puree. You may prefer to add
a little water for a thinner dressing. Use on salads, steamed
vegetables, or baked potatoes in place of butter or margarine.

This special, zesty dressing was developed by Joseph Forest, a
professional vegetarian chef.
