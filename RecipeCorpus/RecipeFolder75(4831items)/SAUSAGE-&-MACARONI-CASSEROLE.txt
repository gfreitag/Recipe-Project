Title: SAUSAGE & MACARONI CASSEROLE
Categories: Meats, Casseroles
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Sausage meat
1    Onion; chopped
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Garlic clove; chopped
3 c  Macaroni; cooked
1 c  Mushrooms; drained
1 c  Cottage cheese
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 c  Spaghetti sauce
1/4 ts Basil
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Dry mustard
Breadcrumbs (opt)
Grated cheese
Preheat oven to 350F. Saute  sausage meat till light brown. Drain fat,
separate  meat with  fork,  set aside. Saute onion, garlic &
mushrooms. Add sausage meat, spices. Stir in sauce. Add macaroni &
stir. Arrange alternate layers  of macaroni-meat mixture and cottage
cheese; ending with meat. Top with bread crumbs & cheese if desired.
Bake at 350F for 25-30 min. SERVES:4
