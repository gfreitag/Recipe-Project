Title: SHOO FLY CAKE+++FGGT98B
Categories: Sweet cakes, Eastern, Jr league
Yield: 12 Servings
4 c  Flour
1 lb Brown sugar
1 c  Butter
2 c  Boiling water
1 c  Molasses
2 ts Baking soda
1 pn Salt
Combine flour, sugar, and butter and blend to fine crumbs.
Reserve 1 1/2 cups of the crumbs for the topping.
Mix together water, molasses, and soda.  Add to remaining
crumbs. Mix well.  Batter will be rather thin. Pour into a
well-greased and floured 9 x 13-inch pan.
Sprinkle the 1 1/2 cups crumb mixture on top.
Bake at 350^ for 45 minutes. YIELD: 12 - 16 Servings. From: The
