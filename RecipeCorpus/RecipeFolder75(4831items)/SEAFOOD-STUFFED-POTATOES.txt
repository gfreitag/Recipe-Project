Title: SEAFOOD STUFFED POTATOES
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 5 Servings
6 md Baking potatoes
6 sl Bacon; cut in small pieces
1/3 c  Minced onion
1/3 c  Milk
1    Egg; beaten
1/4 ts Sweet paprika
2 tb Butter
1 c  Chopped clams
-(one 6 1/2 OZ. can)
Buttered bread crumbs
Bake potatoes, remove from oven and split lengthwise into halves.
Scoop out potatoes and mash with milk, egg, paprika, and butter. Fry
bacon until soft, add onion, cook until bacon is crisp. Add clams and
saute one minute. Fold into mashed potatoes. Refill shells. Top with
buttered bread crumbs. At this point, potatoes can be refrigerated
for up to 3 days, tightly wrapped, or frozen for 1 month. Bake
unfrozen at 350 for 25 minutes or frozen at 350 for 45 minutes.
Serves 6.

Andrea, Cordova, Alaska
