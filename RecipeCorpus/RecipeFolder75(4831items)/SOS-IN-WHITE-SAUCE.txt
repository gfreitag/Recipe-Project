Title: SOS IN WHITE SAUCE
Categories: Sos
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Ground chuck
6 c  Milk
4    Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Flour
4    Salt
1    Pepper
1. In a skillet, brown ground chuck. Drain. 2. Add butter, salt,
pepper, and milk. Stir. 3. In a separate container, mix water with
the flour until a thick paste is made. 4. When the milk in the
skillet gets warm, almost to a boil, slowly add flour paste until the
mixture thickens to the desired constancy. 5. Serve over toast,
bread, waffles or muffins.
