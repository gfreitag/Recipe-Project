Title: SHAKE & BAKE - BASIC
Categories: Spice mix
Yield: 1 Batch
1 ea Lipton Tomato Cup-O-Soup pkg
4 tb Brown Sugar
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
1 ts Onion
1 c  Finely ground Corn Flakes
1 tb Black Pepper
1 tb Seasoned Salt
1 tb Paprika
2 tb Corn Starch
2 1/2 c  Krust-eze
Combine all ingredients.  Use as regular shake and bake on chicken.

Variation for Pork:  Add 1 tsp Sage for each two cups of mix.
