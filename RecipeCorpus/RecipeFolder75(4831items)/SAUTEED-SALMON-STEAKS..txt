Title: SAUTEED SALMON STEAKS.
Categories: Wildgame, Fish
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Apple cider
2 tb Prepared mustard
4    Fish steaks
Melt butter in a skillet. Spread surface of fish steaks with mustard.
Add cider to butter and sautee steaks ten minutes on a side in this
mixture. Also for: Any large fish steak will work. Suggestions: SUBST
beer for Apple Cider. Recipe date: 11/28/87
