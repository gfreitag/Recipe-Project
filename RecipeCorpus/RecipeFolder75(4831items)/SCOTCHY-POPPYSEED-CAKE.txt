Title: SCOTCHY POPPYSEED CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 pk Butterscotch pudding *
3    Eggs
1 c  Oil
1 c  Water; cold
1/3 c  Poppy seeds
* use regular pudding, not instant pudding.
******************************************* Soak the poppy seeds in
the cold water. Grease a 9x13" pan. Mix all ingredients well and bake
at 350~F for 35 minutes.
