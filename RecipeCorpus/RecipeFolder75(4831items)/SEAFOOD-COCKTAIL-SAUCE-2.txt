Title: SEAFOOD COCKTAIL SAUCE 2
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Chili sauce
2 tb Lemon juice (or up to 4T)
1 tb Horseradish (or up to 2T)
2 ts Worchestershire Sauce
1/2 ts Grated onion
Dash of bottled pepper sauce
Combine all ingredients; mix well. Add salt to taste. Chill.

Makes 1 1/4 cup sauce
