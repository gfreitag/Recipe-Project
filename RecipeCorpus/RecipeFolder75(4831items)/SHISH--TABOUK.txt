Title: SHISH  TABOUK
Categories: Chicken
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Breasts of chicken, skinless
And
Boneless
8    Cloves garlic
2    Fresh lemons
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
Salt and pepper
Cut chicken into bite sized pieces and marinate with vegetable oil,
mashed garlic and lemon juice overnight. Bake in oven for 30 minutes
at 350 degrees.
