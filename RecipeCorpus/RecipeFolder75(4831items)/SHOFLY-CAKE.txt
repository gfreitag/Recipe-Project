Title: SHOFLY CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Baking molasses
1 tb Baking soda
2 c  Water -- boiling
4 c  Flour
2 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Shortening
1/2 ts Salt
Mix the molasses, baking soda and water and let cool. Cut the
remianing ingredients to form a crumb, reserving one cup of the
crumbs. Mix the remaining crumbs with the molasses mixture, it will
be lumpy. Pour into a ungreased 9 x 13 pan and sprinkle reserved
crumbs on top. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes.
