Title: SAUSAGE ROUNDS
Categories: Meats, Cheese, Appetizers, Sandwiches
Yield: 52 Servings
2    Loaves small round
- party rye bread
1 lb Ground beef
1 lb Hot sausage
1 lb Velveeta cheese
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Garlic powder to taste
3 ds Tabasco sauce
Brown meat and sausage and drain. Turn meat low and add cheese slices
on top of meat; cover until it melts. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder,
tabasco, and oregano. Spread on party rye and freeze. Broil 10
minutes at 400~. Makes about 52 sandwiches.
