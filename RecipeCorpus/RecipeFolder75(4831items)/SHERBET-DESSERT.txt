Title: SHERBET DESSERT
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 ga Pineapple sherbet
10 oz Frozen strawberries, thawed
- (1 package)
Chopped pecans, as desired
- (optional)
4    Bananas, chopped
Soften sherbet.  Add strawberries and pecans; mix well. Add bananas.
Serve. Preparation time: 15 minutes
