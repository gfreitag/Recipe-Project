Title: SHREDDED ZUCCHINI & CARROT SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Low-fat
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Medium zucchini
1    Medium carrot
1/4    Small onion
1/3 c  Raisins
1/3 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
1/4 c  Unsalted, dry-roasted
-peanuts
1/2 ts Dried dillweed
1/8 ts Pepper
Using a food processor, coarsely shred zucchini, carrot and onion.
Drain any excess liquid. Combine shredded vegetables, the raisins,
yogurt, peanuts, dill and pepper. Mix well. Serve on lettuce leaves.

Nutrition: 132 calories; 5 grams fat (1 gram saturated fat); 0
cholesterol; 29 mg sodium; 20 grams carbohydrates
