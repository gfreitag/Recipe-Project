Title: SOUR MILK COTTAGE CHEESE
Categories:
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM-------------------HOUSEHOLD SEARCHLIGHT------------------------
2 qt Sour milk
Cream
Salt and Pepper
Pour milk into double boiler.  Heat over warm water until soft curd
has formed.  Pour into thin cloth bag. Drain. Proceed as for sweet
milk cottage cheese.
