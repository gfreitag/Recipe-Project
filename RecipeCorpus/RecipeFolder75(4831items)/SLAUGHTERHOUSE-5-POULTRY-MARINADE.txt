Title: SLAUGHTERHOUSE 5 POULTRY MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Italian salad dressing,
Wishbone
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1 ts Dijon mustard
Combine all ingredients and marinate 1/2 chickens for 6-8 hours.
Boneless chicken breasts 2-3 hours. Remove from marinade and pat dry.
Sprinkle with Poultry Rub
