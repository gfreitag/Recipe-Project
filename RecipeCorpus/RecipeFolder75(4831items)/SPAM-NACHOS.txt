Title: SPAM NACHOS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 10 Servings
1 pk CHI-CHI's Tortilla Chips
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(12 oz)
1 cn CHI-CHI's Refried Beans
-(15 oz)
1 cn CHI-CHI's Salsa (16 oz)
1 pk Shredded Mexican pasteurized
-processed cheese (8 oz)
Heat oven to 425'F. Place chips on baking sheet. Sprinkle SPAM over
chips. In bowl, combine refried beans and salsa; pour over chips.
Sprinkle with cheese. Bake 6-7 minutes or until cheese is melted.
Serve immediately.
