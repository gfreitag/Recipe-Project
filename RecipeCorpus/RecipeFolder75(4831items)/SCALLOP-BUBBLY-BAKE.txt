Title: SCALLOP BUBBLY BAKE
Categories: Seafood, Casseroles, Main dish
Yield: 2 Servings
1 lb Scallops
1    Can lobster meat, 8oz
1 md Onion
3    Celery stalks
1 cn Mushrooms, sliced, 8oz
1 md Green pepper, finely diced
2 tb Butter
1 cn Cream of Mushroom soup, 10oz
1 c  Buttered bread crumbs
Wipe scallops dry and lightly salt. Saute vegetables. Place in a
greased casserole. Cover with soup, top with buttered bread crumbs.
Bake at 350F for 30-35 minutes.
