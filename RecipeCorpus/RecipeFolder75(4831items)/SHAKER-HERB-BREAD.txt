Title: SHAKER HERB BREAD
Categories: Abm, Breads, Misc
Yield: 1 Loaf
2 1/4 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
3/4 ts Celery seed
3/4 ts Caraway seed
3/4 ts Ground sage
3/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
4 tb Powdered milk
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 c  Water
1 lg Egg
1 ts Lemon juice
Place all ingredients in machine and push start! Do not use timer.

From THE ULTIMATE BREAD MACHINE COOKBOOK by Lacalamita
