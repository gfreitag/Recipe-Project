Title: SPELT BREAD
Categories: Breads, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Spelt flour
1 pk Quick-rising dry yeast
2 tb Sugar or other granular
-sweetener
-Pinch of salt
2/3 c  Very warm soy milk
2 tb Melted margarine
In large bowl, combine flour, yeast, sugar, and salt. Stir in soy
milk and 1 Tablespoon melted margarine until dough forms. Turn dough
out onto floured board and knead about 3 minutes or until smooth.
Grease mixing bowl and place dough in bowl, turning to coat lightly.
Cover and place in warm spot away from drafts. Let rise about 30
minutes. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Place dough on greased baking
sheet, or, if desired, bake in greased 8- x 4-inch loaf pan. Brush
with remaining tablespoon of melted margarine and bake about 25
minutes or until golden and loaf sounds hollow when tapped on top.
