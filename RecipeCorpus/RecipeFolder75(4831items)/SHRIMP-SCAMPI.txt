Title: SHRIMP SCAMPI
Categories: Seafood, Appetizers
Yield: 5 Servings
1 lb Shrimp, raw
1 ea Egg, beaten
1/4 c  White wine
3 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
3 tb Butter
1 x  Salt
1 x  Pepper
In skillet heat butter and garlic until sizzling. Dip shrimp in egg
and quickly drop in skillet. Saute' quickly over high heat 2-4
minutes. Add white wine, salt and pepper. Serve over a bed of rice.
