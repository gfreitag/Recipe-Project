Title: SESAME CHICKEN (LIZARDO)
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
10    Chicken thighs
3 tb Light soy sauce
2 tb Dry Sherry
1/2 ts Fresh ginger; grated
3 tb Cornstarch
4 tb Raw sesame seeds
Peanut oil; deep frying
Cut the chicken thighs in half across the bone. Marinate them in the
soy sauce, sherry, ginger & enough cornstarch to make a thick sauce
for 1 Hr. Add the sesame seeds. Deep fry pieces in peanut oil at 375
until lightly browned. You can try substituting beef for the chicken.
