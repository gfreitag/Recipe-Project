Title: SNAPPER VERACRUZ
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
6    8 ounce snapper fillets
Melted butter
Salt & pepper
2 c  Veracruz sauce
MMMMM-----------------------VERACRUZ SAUCE----------------------------
1 tb Chopped garlic
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1/2 c  Diced tomato
2 tb Tomato paste
1/4 c  Sliced green olives
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Chablis wine
1 ts Oregano
1/2 c  Water
Prepare Sauce, Saute onion and garlic in olive oil until soft Add all
remaining ingredients, mix well. Bring to boil and simmer 10 minutes

Prepare Fish. Place fish fillets on a baking pan, skin side down.
Baste fish with melted butter. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Pour
veracruz sauce over snapper filets Bake at 350&deg; for 15 - 20
minutes, until fish flakes easily. Serve. Servings: 6 Posted to
