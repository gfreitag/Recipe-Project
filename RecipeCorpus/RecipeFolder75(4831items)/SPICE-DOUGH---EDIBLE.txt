Title: SPICE DOUGH - EDIBLE
Categories: Kids
Yield: 1 Batch
-Robbie Shelton
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking Powder
1/3 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/3 c  Water
4 tb Salad Oil
Mix dry ingredients.  Add water and oil.  Knead until dough sticks and
forms a ball.

NOTE:  This recipe can be fried in oil.

This recipe is from ACC - Child Development Class.
