Title: SAUSAGE & CHICKEN JAMBALAYA
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Cajun
Yield: 4 Servings
4 ea Chicken breasts
1 ea Bell pepper, sliced
2 cn Beef broth
2 tb Butter or oleo
1 c  Rice
1 ea Onion, sliced
1 lb Hot sausage
Saute sliced pepper, onion and hot sausage in butter for 5 min. In
9 x 13-inch pan, mix consomme and rice. Add sausage mixture. Place
chicken breasts on top and cover with foil. Bake at 350 F. for 1 hr.,
uncover and bake add'tl. 30 min.

Yield:  4 servings
