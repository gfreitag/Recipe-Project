Title: SNOW PEAS WITH SESAME SEEDS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Water
3 tb Sesame seeds
1 lb Snow peas
1 tb Shallots, chopped
1 tb Butter
Black pepper, ground
1. Pour the water into a saucepan. Bring to a boil and add the snow
peas t the pan. Blanch them for 1 minute and drain. 2. Melt the
butter in a nonstick frying pan. Add the sesame seeds and brow them
lightly. Add the snow peas, shallots, and pepper. Saute for 1 minute
and serve.
