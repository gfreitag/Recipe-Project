Title: SPAGHETTI WITH CHPPD BROCCCOLI & PIGNOLI IN B
Categories: Main dish, Italian, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Thin slivers of garlic
12 oz Spaghetti
2 tb Pignoli (toasted in hot skil
2 c  Fresh or frozen Broccoli
1    Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/3 c  OLIVE OIL
Heat oil in a meduim skillet over low heat; stir in garlic. Saute,
stirring, just until edges begin to turn golden, about 3 minutes. Add
the broccoli; cover and cook over low heat until tender, about 5
minutes. Cook spaghetti til al dente.

Toss with broccoli and garlic oil; sprinkle with pignoli and grated
cheese before serving.
