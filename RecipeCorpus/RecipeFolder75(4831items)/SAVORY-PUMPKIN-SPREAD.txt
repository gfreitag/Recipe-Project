Title: SAVORY PUMPKIN SPREAD
Categories: Digest, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Pumpkin puree
1/4 c  Water (or more)
1 tb Nutritional yeast
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/2 ts Dried parsley
1/4 ts Mustard powder
1/4 ts Garlic salt (or salt), or to
Taste
Mix all ingredients and serve.  More water can be added for a thinner,
"dipping" sauce.
