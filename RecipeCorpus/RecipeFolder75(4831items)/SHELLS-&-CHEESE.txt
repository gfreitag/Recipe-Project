Title: SHELLS & CHEESE
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 6 Servings
3    BOXES Of Shells And Cheese
1 1/2    2/3 Pot Of Water
1 x  Anything You Want
PUT WATER IN POT. BRING WATER TO A BOIL. POUR IN SHELLS. PUT CHEESE
SAUCE ON TOP. KEEP BOILING LIGHTLY FOR 10MIN. REMOVE CHEESE SAUCE AND
DRAIN. EMPTY CHEESE SAUCE ON PASTA. YOU CAN NOW ADD CHICKEN, SALAMI,
CARROTS, PEAS, OR ANY THING ELSE.
