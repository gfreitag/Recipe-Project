Title: SHRIMP DIP
Categories: Dips, Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Milk
1 c  Mayonnaise
3 ea Tabasco sauce (drops)
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1 ea Onion (chopped)
8 oz Cheddar cheese (cubed)
5 oz Shrimp (drained)
Place all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth. Served
chilled.
