Title: SHAVED FENNEL WITH BLOOD ORANGES~ POMEGRANATE
Categories: New, Text, Import
2 lg round bulbs of fennel,
trimmed, -- rinsed and
dried
Juice of 1 lemon
Yield: 4 Servings
:
:
:
4 TB extra virgin olive oil
4 lg blood oranges, -- zest
removed and set aside
:
1    pomegranate, rind removed
and -- seeds extracted
:
8 oz hard pecorino -- (Sardo or
Tuscan)
Salt and pepper
:
:

Shave fennel on mandolin or slicer 1/32" thick into large metal mixing
bowl. Toss with lemon juice and extra virgin olive oil and set aside
in refrigerator.

Remove pith and skin from orange, cut into segments and remove seeds,
saving all juices. Toss segments into bowl with fennel, add
pomegranates and toss gently to mix.

Arrange 4 tall piles in 4 separate plates. Shave pecorino in long
shards over top of each plate and serve.

Yield: 4 servings
