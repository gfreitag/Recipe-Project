Title: SKYLINE CHILI NO. 2
Categories: Chili, Beef, Chilibeef
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts L&P sauce
12 oz Tomato juice
1 tb Chili powder
1    Garlic - chopped
1/2 ts Allspice
3/16 ts Pepper
1    Onion - chopped
1 cn Tomato sauce
1    Bay leaves
12 oz Water
1/2 ts Cinnamon
3/16 ts Red pepper
1/3 tb Salt
Brown ground beef and onion.  Drain fat.  Add remaining ingredients,
stirring well after each addition. Simmer 2 hrs or till liquid is
sufficiently reduced. Serve over spaghetti. Top with chopped onions
and grated Colby or Cheddar cheese. Oyster crackers on side. From:
Pat Stockett Date: Tue, 10-0
