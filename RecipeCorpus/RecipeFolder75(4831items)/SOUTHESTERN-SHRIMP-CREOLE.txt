Title: SOUTHESTERN SHRIMP CREOLE
Categories: Main dish, Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Margarine or butter
1 md Onion, chopped
1 lg Green pepper, chopped
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 pk Diced pimientos (4 oz)
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 cn RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and
-Green Chilies (10 oz)
1 lb Shrimp, shelled and deveined
Cooked rice
In a large skillet, melt margarine; saute onion, pepper and garlic
until tender. Stir in pimientos, salt, pepper and RO*TEL; simmer 10
minutes. Add shrimp and cook until done, about 5 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Serve with cooked rice.
