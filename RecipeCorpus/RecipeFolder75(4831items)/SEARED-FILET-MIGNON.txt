Title: SEARED FILET MIGNON
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 6 Servings
6 sl Filet Mignon,, each about
-1/2 pound
Salt and pepper
1/3 c  (6 tablespoons) butter
1 1/2 tb Olive oil
Season the Filet Mignons with salt and pepper. Combine the butter and
oil in a large saute pan over high heat. When the pan is hot and foam
has subsided, add the beef. Sear for three minutes per side for rare.
Transfer to a warm platter Yield: 6 servings Posted to MC-Recipe
