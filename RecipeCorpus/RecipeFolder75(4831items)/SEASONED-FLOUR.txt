Title: SEASONED FLOUR
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
2 tb Salt
1 tb Celery salt
2 tb Dry mustard
1 tb Pepper
4 tb Paprika
2 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Oregano
Mix together. Store in an airtight container. Great for coating pork
chops, chicken, fish....or add a little to your favorite recipe.

Orginated: Margaret Moore, Vernon, B.C. Source: St. James School &
Parish Cookbook, 1986

From the collection of K. Deck
