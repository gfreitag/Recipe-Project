Title: SLOPPY JOES
Categories: Sandwiches, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lg Onion, diced
2 md Green peppers, diced
3 tb Oil
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
2 1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1 tb To 2 tbsp. chili powder
1 pn Pepper
1 ts Salt
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Mustard
1 tb Sugar
1 1/2 c  Dry Texturized Vegetable
Protein (TVP)
Saute onion and green peppers in oil.  Add remaining ingredients, and
simmer together for 20 minutes and serve hot on soft sandwich buns.

Source: Vegetarian cooking class at East Senior High School, provided
from The New Farm Vegetarian Cookbook
