Title: SHARKY'S MUSTARD SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Paprika
1 tb Ground black pepper
1 tb Salt
1 ts Garlic powder
1 c  Prepared yellow mustard
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  White vinegar
Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl; Stir until creamy.
Chill for about 15 minutes before use.

Source:  Kansas City Barbeque by Bill Venable, Rick Welch, Bruce
Daniel
