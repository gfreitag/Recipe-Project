Title: SHRIMP AU GRATIN~ II
Categories: Main dish, Shellfish, Cajun
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Shrimp
1 c  Thin white sauce
1/2 c  Buttered bread crumbs
2/3 c  American cheese, grated
1 tb Parsley, finely chopped
Alternate layers of cleaned, cooked shrimp, white sauce and 1/2
cup of the cheese in greased baking dish. Sprinkle with parsley and
remaining cheese. Sprinkle buttered crumbs over top. Bake at 400 F.
for 20 minutes or until crumbs are brown. Courtesy Telephone Pioneers
