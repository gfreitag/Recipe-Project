Title: SHRIMP KABOBS
Categories: Bar-b-q
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Dijon Mustard
16 oz Bottle K.C. Masterpiece
-BBQ sauce
1/2 c  Honey
Large shrimp
Whole mushroom caps
Mix mustard, BBQ sauce & honey.  Place shrimp & mushrooms on skewers
and marinate overnight.  Grill over direct heat 7-8 minutes till
shrimp are done.
