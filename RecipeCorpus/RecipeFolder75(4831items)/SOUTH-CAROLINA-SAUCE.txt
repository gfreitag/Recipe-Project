Title: SOUTH CAROLINA SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Prepared mustard
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Light brown sugar
1 c  Cider vinegar
2 tb Chili powder
1 ts Pepper
1 ts White pepper
1/4 ts Cayenne
5 dr Tabasco sauce -- to taste
1/2 ts Soy sauce
2 tb Butter
Combine all ingredsients except soy and butter in saucepan and simmer
10 min. Remove from heat. Stir in soy sauce and butter. Use as a
basting sauce for BBQ meats or as a condiment when serving grilled
pork, beef or chicken.
