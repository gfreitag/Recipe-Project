Title: SESAME GINGER CHICKEN
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Honey
1 tb Sesame seeds -- toasted
1/2 ts Ground ginger
4    Boneless skinless chicken
Breast halves
2    Green onions with tops --
Cut in thin strips
In a small bowl, combine the first four ingredients; set aside. Pound
the chicken breasts to 1/4-inch thickness. Grill over medium-hot
coals, turning and basting frequently with soy sauce mixture, for 8
minutes or until juice run clear.  Garnish with onions.

: Taste Of Home June/July '96
