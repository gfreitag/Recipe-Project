Title: SHERRY 'N SPICE MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Sherry
1/4 c  Salad oil
1 ea Medium onion, grated
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Dry mustard
3/4 ts Dried herbs, such as thyme,
-marjoram, rosemary, oregano
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper, fresh ground
1/4 ts Allspice
Blend all ingredients well.  Use as a marinade for beef, veal, lamb,
or chicken.  Use remaining marinade to baste during barbequing.
