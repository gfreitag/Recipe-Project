Title: Strawberry Tart Filling
Categories: Italian
Yield: 8 meringues
1 qt Ripe strawberries
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Almond flavoring
Strawberry frozen yogurt
Italian Meringue Shells
Stem strawberries and slice thickly. Combine in a bowl with sugar and
almond flavoring. Fill meringue shells with strawberry frozen yogurt
and top with the strawberries.
