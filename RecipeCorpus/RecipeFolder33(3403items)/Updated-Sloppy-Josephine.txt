Title: Updated Sloppy Josephine
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 1 serving
1/2 c  Lean ground beef (4 oz)
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped mushrooms
1/2 c  Salsa, mild or spicy
1 tb Ketchup
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Ground cumin
1/4 c  Shredded Jarlsberg Lite or
-low-fat Swiss cheese (2 oz)
1    French-style roll, toasted
Crumble beef into a 4-cup glass measure. Sprinkle onions on top.
Cover with vented plastic wrap and microwave on high for 2 minutes.
Stir to break up beef; add mushrooms and re-cover. Microwave on high
1-2 minutes, or until beef is no longer pink.

Drain liquid and add salsa, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce and cumin.
Re-cover and microwave on high 2-3 minutes, or until bubbly. Serve on
roll topped with cheese.
