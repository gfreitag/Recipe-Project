Title: Tesque Garden Guacamole Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 cup
1    Ripe avocado
2 oz Cream cheese, softened
1/4 c  Fresh tarragon, chopped
-OR
1/4 c  Fresh dill, chopped
1/4 c  Fresh oregano, chopped
Juice of 1/2 fresh lime
1/2 ts Balsamic vinegar
1/2 ts Olive oil
1/2 ts Chimayo red chile, finely
-ground, to taste
Mash the avocado with the cream cheese. Add the other ingredients. Mix
well. Serve immediately with blue corn chips.
