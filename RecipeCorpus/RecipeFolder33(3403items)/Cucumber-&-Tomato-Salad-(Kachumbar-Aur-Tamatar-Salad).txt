Title: Cucumber & Tomato Salad (Kachumbar Aur Tamatar Salad)
Categories: Indian, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 md Cucumber -- peel, seed &
Chop
1 md Onion -- minced
1 md Tomato -- diced
2 ts Extra virgin olive oil
1 tb Lemon juice
1/8 ts Salt
Pepper
In a bowl combine the cucumber, onion, tomato, olive oil and lemon
juice. Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Cover and chill in the
fridge until ready to serve.  49 calories and 2 grams fat.
