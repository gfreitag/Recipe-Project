Title: Champagne Fruit Parfait
Categories: Alcohol, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Watermelon balls
2 c  Honeydew melon balls
2 c  Cantaloupe balls
2 c  Pineapple chunks in juice --
Drained
1 c  Strawberries
1 c  Grapes
1 c  Peaches -- sliced
Lemon juice
Champagne -- *see note
Mint sprigs
* For a non-alcoholic version, substitute ginger ale for Champagne.

Mix fruits VERY gently. Arrange in attractive pattern in parfait
glasses. Sprinkle each with few drops of lemon juice. Be sure to have
a whole strawberry in each glass. Fill each glass with Champagne.
Garnish with a mint sprig.

: Jo Anne Merrill
