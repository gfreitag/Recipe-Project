Title: Chocolate Cherry Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
3/4 c  Water
1 1/2 c  White bread flour
1/2 c  Wheat bread flour
1 tb Dry milk
2 tb Molasses
1 ts Salt
1/3 c  Chocolate chips
1/3 c  Cherries, dried
2 ts Triple Sec liquer
1/4 ts Orange peel
1 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

Kids love it, but for adults, Chocolate Cherry turns a coffee break
into a pleasant interlude.  With a sweet topping, it becomes a new
dessert bread. [Outstanding!]

Success Hints
-------------

Make sure you use real chocolate chips

You can use either dried bing or dried tart red cherries.

Use regular, rapid bake or delayed time bake cycle.
