Title: Rosemary-garlic Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 2 servings
2 lg Garlic cloves, crushed
1 1/2 tb Butter
2    Pieces French bread, sliced
3/4-inch thick
1    Rosemary sprig
1. Place crushed garlic in small skillet along with butter.  Heat
until butter begins to bubble.  Brush bread generously with garlic
butter, using the rosemary sprig as a brush.

2. Place bread on baking sheet with rosemary sprig arranged across
slices. Broil 4 inches under broiler until bread is browned, about 30
seconds. Discard rosemary.  Serve bread immediately.
