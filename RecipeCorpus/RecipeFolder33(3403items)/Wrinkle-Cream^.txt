Title: Wrinkle Cream^
Categories: Information, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz White wax
2 oz Strained honey
2 oz Lily bulb juice
Melt ingredients together, cool. Apply to the face every night and it
is said your wrinkles will disappear. Many of the recipes in this
collection did not contain amounts or oven temperatures. I have typed
them in as they appear in the book, typos and all.
