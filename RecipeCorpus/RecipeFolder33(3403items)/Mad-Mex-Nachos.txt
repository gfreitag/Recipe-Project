Title: Mad Mex Nachos
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 10 servings
8 oz Italian sausage, casings
-removed
1 pk Yellow or white corn
-tortilla chips (10 oz)
1 cn Rosarita Refried Beans
-(16 oz)
1 c  Rosarita Picante Sauce Mild
1 c  Diced tomatoes
1 1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
Rosarita Nacho Sliced
-Jalapeno Peppers
MMMMM---------------------OPTIONAL GARNISHES--------------------------
Guacamole
Sour cream
Cook sausage until brown and crumbly; drain and keep warm. Place
tortilla chips on a baking sheet. Combine refried beans and picante
sauce in saucepan, heat until warm, stirring occasionally. Spoon bean
mixture over chips. Top with sausage, tomatoes, cheese and jalapeno
slices. Bake at 400'F. for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted. Top
with guacamole and sour cream, if desired. Makes 8-10 servings.
