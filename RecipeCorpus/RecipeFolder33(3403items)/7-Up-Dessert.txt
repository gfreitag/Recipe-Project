Title: 7-Up Dessert
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------FILLING---------------------------------
2 pk Lemon jello -- (3 oz)
2 c  Water -- boiling*
2 c  7-Up
1 lg Can crushed pineapple --
- drain/save juice
2 lg Bananas -- diced
Juice of 1 lemon
MMMMM------------------------TOPPING---------------------------------
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
2 tb Unbleached flour
1    Egg white -- whipped
2 tb Margarine -- melted
1 c  Pineapple juice*
1 c  Dream Whip powder
1 cn Coconut
To prepare filling, make jello following package directions.  Then add
7-Up.  When mixture is about to congeal, add pineapple, and bananas
which have been soaked in lemon juice.  Pour mixture into a 9 x 13"
pan and let chill until firm.  To prepare topping, combine sugar,
flour, egg white, margarine, and pineapple juice in a saucepan.  Cook
over low heat until thickened, stirring as it cooks, until mixture is
thick.  Let cool.  Fold in whipped cream and coconut.  Spread this
over firm chilled filling.
