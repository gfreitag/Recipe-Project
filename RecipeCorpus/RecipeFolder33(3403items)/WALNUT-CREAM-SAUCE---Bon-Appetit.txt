Title: WALNUT CREAM SAUCE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 1/2 cup
2 c  Whipping cream
3/4 c  Finely chopped walnuts,
-toasted
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
Combine cream, walnuts and nutmeg in heavy large skillet. Boil until
thickened to sauce consistency, about 6 minutes. Season with salt and
pepper.
