Title: Holiday Classic Green Bean Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Holiday, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 pk Frozen cut green beans,
-thawed and drained (9 oz)
-OR
2 cn Cut green beans, drained
-(16 oz ea)
3/4 c  Milk
1 cn Condensed cream of mushroom
-soup (10 3/4 oz)
1/8 ts Ground black pepper
1 cn Durkee French Fried
-Onions (2.8 oz)
In a medium bowl, combine beans, milk, soup, pepper and 1/2 CAN French
Fried Onions; pour into 1 1 1/2 quart baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at
350'F. for 30 minutes or until heated through. Top with remaining
onions; bake, uncovered, 5 minutes or until onions are golden brown.
