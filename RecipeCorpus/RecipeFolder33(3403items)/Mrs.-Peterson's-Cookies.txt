Title: Mrs. Peterson's Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Drippings or lard
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Molasses
1 tb Baking soda
1 ts Ginger
1    Egg, beaten
pn Salt
Flour
Cream drippings and sugar. Add molasses and egg and beat well. Add
baking soda, ginger, salt and enough flour to make a soft dough. drop
by spoonfuls on greased baking tin. Bake in a moderate oven for 15-20
minutes.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
