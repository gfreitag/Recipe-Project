Title: Khao Cua (Ground Toasted Rice)
Categories: Thai, Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Rice, short grain --
Uncooked
Place rice, 1/2 cup at a time, in dry wok and heat over moderate heat,
stirring frequently to keep it from burning, and to allow it to
develop a uniform, deep golden color - 2 to 3 minutes. Then remove it
from heat and cool to room temperature. Grind to a fine power in
blender, spice or coffee grinder.

GEnie R.WEISSGERBE "Delightful Thai Cooking" p. 18
