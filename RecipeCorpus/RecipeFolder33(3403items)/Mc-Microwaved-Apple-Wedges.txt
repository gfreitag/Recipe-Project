Title: Mc Microwaved Apple Wedges
Categories: Breakfast, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Ginger
1/8 ts Cloves
1    Large unpeeled apple; cored
-and cut into 8 wedges
Combine the sugar, cinnamon, ginger and cloves in a small bowl and mix
well.  Press the cut sides of the apple into the spice mixture.
Place four wedges on a paper plate, cover with waxed paper, and
microwave on high for about 45 seconds.  Serve at once.

Variation:  To make this in a conventional oven, place the wedges in a
small casserole dish and bake, covered, at 250 for about 15 mins.
