Title: Tangy Cranberry Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1 1/4 c  Water
3 c  White bread flour
2 tb Dry milk
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
1/3 c  Cranberries, dried
3 tb Orange marmalade
2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
3 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

Wanna bet there's nothing new to do with that leftover turkey?  This
moist, light bread promises to redefine your idea of a turkey
sandwich. But don't stop there... try a slice of sharp cheddar with a
little English mustard. Experiment - and enjoy!

Success Hints
-------------

Place dried cherries away from water if using the delayed bake cycle.

Use the regular, rapid bake or delayed bake cycle.
