Title: Southwest Onion Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 2 cups
1 cn Borden or Meadow Gold Sour
-Cream (16 oz)
1 pk Mrs. Grass Recipe, Soup &
-Dip Mix
1/4 c  Prepared salsa
2    Jalapeno peppers, finely
-chopped
Tortilla chips or
Assorted fresh vegetables
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients except chips and vegetables;
mix well

Chill to blend flavors; stir before serving.

Serve with chips or vegetables. Refrigerate leftovers.
