Title: Tomato Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tomatoes
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Sugar, or less, to tastse
1/2 c  Oil
1/3 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 ts Paprika
Salt and pepper to taste
Mix together and store in fridge.  Can be used on macaroni salads.
Origin: Homestyle Canadian Classics, Ladies of the Royal Purple.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, July/95.
