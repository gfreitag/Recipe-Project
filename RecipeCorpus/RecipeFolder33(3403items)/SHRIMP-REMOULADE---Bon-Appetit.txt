Title: SHRIMP REMOULADE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/4 c  Creole mustard or Dijon
-mustard
2 tb Prepared white horseradish
2 ts Paprika
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
3/4 c  Olive oil
2/3 c  Finely chopped celery
2/3 c  Finely chopped onion
2 tb Thinly sliced green onions
2 tb Finely chopped fresh
-parsley
1 lb Peeled deveined cooked
-medium shrimp
Butter lettuce
Whisk first 6 ingredients together in medium bowl to combine.
Gradually add oil, whisking constantly until mixture thickens. Stir
in celery, onion, green onions and parsley. Season with salt and
pepper. Place shrimp in large bowl. Mix in enough dressing to coat.
Cover shrimp and remaining dressing separately and refrigerate until
chilled, about 2 hours. (Can be made 6 hours ahead. Keep
refrigerated.)

Arrange lettuce on 4 plates. Spoon shrimp atop lettuce. Serve, passing
remaining dressing separately.
