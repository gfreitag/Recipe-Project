Title: CHICKEN TORTELLINI SOUP WITH SPINACH - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Soup/stew, Poultry, Pasta/rice
Yield: 2 servings;
2 tb Olive oil
4 oz Mushrooms, thinly sliced
2 lg Garlic cloves, chopped
3    14 1/2-oz cans low-salt
-chicken broth
1 pk (9-oz) fresh chicken &
-vegetable tortellini
3 c  Thinly sliced ready-to-use
-spinach leaves (about 5 oz)
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
Additional grated Parmesan
-cheese
Heat oil in heavy large saucepan over medium heat. Add mushrooms and
garlic; saute until mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes. Add broth
and bring to boil. Add tortellini, cover pan partially and boil until
tortellini are tender but still firm to bite, about 5 minutes. Add
spinach and simmer until wilted, about 2 minutes. Mix in 2 T cheese.
Season soup with salt and pepper.

Ladle soup into bowls. Serve, passing additional cheese separately.

Bon Appetit/October/94  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
