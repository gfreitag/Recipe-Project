Title: Powter Spicy Red Beans and Rice
Categories: Vegetables, Pasta/rice, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Vegetable oil
2 lg Onions; diced
1 lg Clove garlic; minced
2 lg Green peppers; diced
3    Celery stalks; sliced
2    Bay leaves
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Cumin
1 ts Paprika
2 c  Canned crushed tomatoes
1 tb Cider vinegar
1 c  Vegetable broth; or more
1 1/2    15oz can kidney beans,
-rinsed and drained
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce; or to
-taste
4 c  Cooked rice
Heat oil in a large saucepan.  Saute onion, garlic, green peppers, and
celery for 5 mins.  Add all the spices, tomatoes, vinegar, and veg
broth. Simmer for 10 mins.

Add beans, more veg broth if necessary, hot sauce, and salt.  Heat
thru and cook 5 mins, until all flavors are blended.  Cook rice
according to package directions.  Remove bay leaves before serving.

Serve over white rice.
