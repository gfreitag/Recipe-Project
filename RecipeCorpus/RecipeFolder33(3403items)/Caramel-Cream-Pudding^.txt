Title: Caramel Cream Pudding^
Categories: Puddings, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Shortening
2 tb Sugar
1    Egg
1 c  Flour
3/4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Milk
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
3/4 c  Sour milk
2 1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Flour
Beat shortening and sugar together till creamy. Add egg and beat hard
for about 2 minutes. Sift together flour, baking powder and salt. Add
to creamed mixture alternately with the milk. Pat dough in bottom of
a baking tin. For the sauce: Using an egg beater, mix together the
sour cream, sour milk, sugar and flour. Pour the sauce over the dough
and bake in a 350 degree oven for about 45 minutes. As this bakes,
the sauce sinks to the bottom and the dough comes to the top! Serve
hot.
