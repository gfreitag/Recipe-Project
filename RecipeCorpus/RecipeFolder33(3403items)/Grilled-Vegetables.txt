Title: Grilled Vegetables
Categories: Vegetables, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 serving
Eggplant
Squash
Bell pepper
Zucchini
Portobello mushrooms
Olive oil
Salt
Pepper
Cut vegetables into 1 1/2" slices. Place on microwaveable dish and
microwave 3 minutes. Remove and brush vegetables on both sides with
olive oil; season with salt and pepper. Place on hot, clean grill and
cook until tender, turning once.
