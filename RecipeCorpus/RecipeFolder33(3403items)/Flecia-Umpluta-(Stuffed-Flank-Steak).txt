Title: Flecia Umpluta (Stuffed Flank Steak)
Categories: Meats, Romanian
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Toasted bread cubes
1/4 c  Hot water
3/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Melted butter or margarine
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 x  Eggs, slightly beaten
2 x  Flank steaks
1 x  Salt and pepper
1 x  Flour
1 x  Fat
1/2 c  Water
Combine the first eight ingredients and toss lightly with a fork.
Place steaks together and sew edges with cord, leaving an opening.
Fill with stuffing, sew edges together. Sprinkle with salt and
pepper. Coat with flour. Brown on both sides in a small amount of
fat. Add 1/2 cup of water, cover and bake 2 hours or until tender.
Recipe may be halved and just put stuffing over one steak and bake,
or roll and tie. Submitted By MARK MIKLAS   On   07-27-95
