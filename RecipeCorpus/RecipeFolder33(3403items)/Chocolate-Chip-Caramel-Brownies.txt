Title: Chocolate Chip Caramel Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 brownies
2 c  NESTLE TOLL HOUSE Semi-Sweet
-Chocolate Morsels, DIVIDED
-(12 oz pkg)
1 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  UNDILUTED CARNATION
-Evaporated Skimmed Milk*
1/4 c  Unsweetened applesauce
2 tb Margarine
4 oz Liquid egg substitute
1 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
2 ts Vanilla extract
12    Caramels
1 tb UNDILUTED CARNATION
-Evaporated Skimmed Milk*
HEAT 1 cup morsels, sugar, 1/2 cup evaporated milk, applesauce and
margarine in large, heavy saucepan over low heat, stirring until
morsels are melted. Remove from heat. Stir in egg substitute. Stir in
flour, baking soda, salt, vanilla and remaining morsels. Spread into
13x9" baking pan sprayed with Mozola No Stick Cooking Spray.

BAKE in preheated 350'F. oven for 16-20 minutes or just until set.
Melt caramels with 1 tablespoon evaporated milk in small,
microwave-safe bowl on HIGH (100%) power for 1-3 minutes, stirring
every 15 seconds until smooth. Drizzle over warm brownies. Serve warm
or cool.

* NOTE: CARNATION Evaporated Lowfat Milk may be substituted.
