Title: Orange Sorbets
Categories: Desserts, Holiday
Yield: 5 cups
1 1/2 c  Fresh orange juice
2 tb Finely grated orange zest
3 c  Sugar Syrup
Combine all ingredients in a medium-sized bowl, then transfer to an
ice-cream maker; freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.
(Sorbet keeps for up to two weeks in the freezer.)

Per 1/4-cup serving: 101 calories, no fat, no cholesterol.
