Title: Horseradish Slaw
Categories: Tomatoes, Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 1 cup
4    Ripe plum tomatoes, seeded
2 c  Romaine lettuce, finely
-shredded and packed tightly
3 tb Finely diced red onion
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sour cream
2 tb Prepared horseradish
1/2 ts Salt
This slaw is best served on top of grilled steak or chicken
sandwiches, or on a burger, for that matter.

Dice the tomatoes and put them in a medium-sized bowl with the
lettuce and onion.

In a separate small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream,
horseradish and salt. Add enough mayonnaise mixture as needed to
moisten the lettuce, tomatoes and onions in the other bowl. Stir well.
