Title: Mashed Potatoes For A Crowd
Categories: Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
30 lb Potatoes
1 qt Milk
1 tb Salt
1/2 c  Butter
Peel potatoes, cook until tender.  Drain well.  Mash until smooth.
Add butter and salt.  Slowly add the milk while mixing until you get
a smooth consistency.  Beat until light and fluffly.
