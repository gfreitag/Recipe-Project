Title: Salgadinho com Queijo, Cebola, e Malaguetas (onion/Chile/
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables, So. america, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 12 appetizers
2 ea Red onions; thinly sliced
2 ea Fresh Malagueta chiles;
-seeds and stems removed,
-minced   -OR-
2 ea Pequin or serrano chiles
3/4 c  Sour cream
1/2 ts Lime juice
12 ea Puff pastry shells, prepared
6 tb Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese
Salt and pepper to taste
Combine the onions with the chiles, sour cream, and lime juice, then
cover and refrigerate.  Bake the puff pastry shells according to the
instructions on the box until 3/4 done, then remove them from the
oven. Let the pastry cool on the baking sheet, then evenly spread the
onion mixture in each shell.  Top the mixture in each pastry with the
Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.  Place the pastry lid on each
shell and finish baking the shells until light brown.  Serve
immediately.

Authors' heat scale: Medium
