Title: Pumpkin Ginger Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 20 servings
1 1/2 c  Pumpkin
5 tb Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
2    Egg whites -- whipped
1/2 c  Apple juice -- at room
Temperature
2 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1 1/2 c  Unbleached flour
2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
4 1/2 ts Ginger
1 ts Nutmeg
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare muffin tins with cooking spray and
flour. In a mixing bowl, combine pumpkin, applesauce, egg whites, and
juice.  In another mixing bowl, combine sugar, flour, baking soda,
baking powder, salt, ginger, and nutmeg.  Mix wet ingredients with
dry ingredients just until moistened.  Use an ice cream scoop to fill
muffin tins two thirds full.  Bake 25 minutes.
