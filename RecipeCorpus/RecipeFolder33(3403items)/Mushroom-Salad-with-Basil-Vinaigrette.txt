Title: Mushroom Salad with Basil Vinaigrette
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------VINAIGRETTE-----------------------------
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Olive or vegetable oil
2 tb Chopped fresh basil
-OR
1 ts Dried basil leaves
2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Hot pepper sauce
2    Garlic cloves, minced
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
2 cn Green Giant Whole Mushrooms,
-drained (4.5 oz)
1/2    Red onion, thinly sliced
6 c  Torn salad greens (romaine
-and bibb lettuce)
1/2 c  Grated carrot
In medium bowl, combine all vinaigrette ingredients; blend well. Add
mushrooms and onion. Cover; refrigerate at least 2 hours. Place salad
greens and carrots on salad plates. Top with mushrooms and onion.
Spoon vinaigrette of salads.

TIP One 4.5-oz jar sliced mushrooms can be substituted for the whole
mushrooms.
