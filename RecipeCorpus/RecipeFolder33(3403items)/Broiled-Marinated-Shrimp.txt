Title: Broiled Marinated Shrimp
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Uncooked large shrimp
1 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Onion; chopped
2 tb Parsley; chopped
1 ts Tarragon; crushed
1/2 ts Chervil
1/4 ts Basil
Wash shrimp. Remove vein. Mix ingredients and use to marinate shrimp
in refrigerator at least 2 hours. Broil over hot coals till done.
