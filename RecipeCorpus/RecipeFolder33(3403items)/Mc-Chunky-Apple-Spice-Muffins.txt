Title: Mc Chunky Apple Spice Muffins
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 12 muffins
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Oat bran; or another cup
-or whole wheat flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Allspice
2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Ginger; powdered
1 ts Arrowroot
1/4 c  Honey
1/2 c  Unsweetened applesauce
2    Tart apples; peeled, diced
1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Preheat the oven 400.

Mix the dry ingredients and wet ingredients separately. Combine them
and mix thoroughly.  Spoon into nonstick muffin tins and bake for
about 25 mins, until golden brown on top.
