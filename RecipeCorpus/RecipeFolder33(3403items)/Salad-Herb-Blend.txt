Title: Salad Herb Blend
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 c  Dried Parsley
1/4 c  Dried Majoram
2 1/2 tb Dried Basil
1 1/2 tb Sesame Seeds
1 1/2 tb Chili Pepper Flakes
1 1/2 tb Powdered Rosemary
1 1/4 tb Powdered Celery Seed
2 1/2 ts Dried Savory
2 1/2 ts Powdered Sage
2 1/4 ts Dried Thyme
2 ts Granulated Onion
2 ts Dried Dill Weed
1 1/4 ts Finely Ground Black Pepper
3/4 ts Garlic Powder
Place all of the ingredients in a medium bowl.  Toss gently, with a
spoon, until well blended.  Store, in an airtight container, in a
cool, dry, dark place for up to 6 months.
