Title: Sate Baba Pvmp45b
Categories: Thai
Yield: 8 servings
1    Head garlic -- crushed
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Black pepper(ground)
1 tb Rock salt
1 c  Native vinegar
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 lb Lean pork -- cut into 1"
Cubes
Prepare marinade by combining first 6 ingredients. Marinate pork
overnight in the fridge.  Skewer pork pieces in bamboo sticks.
Charcoal broil. Serve with Sate Sauce and Achara. Posted by Agnes
Hanno. MM:MK VMXV03A.
