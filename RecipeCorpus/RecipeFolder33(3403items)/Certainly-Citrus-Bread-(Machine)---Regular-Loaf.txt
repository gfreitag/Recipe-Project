Title: Certainly Citrus Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
2/3 c  Water
2 c  White bread flour
1 tb + 1t Dry milk
2 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 tb + 1t Butter
2 tb Orange marmalade
2 ts Lemon juice
2 ts Lime juice
Pinch lemon peel
1 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

This tangy bread has a fine, light texture.  The subtle yet
distinctive flavor makes it a perfect accompaniment for fish or
chicken. Try it with swordfish baked with lime butter or your
favorite lemon chicken recipe.

Success Hints
-------------

If using fresh lemon peel, grate very fine and avoid using the white
pulp.

Use regular or rapid bake cycle.
