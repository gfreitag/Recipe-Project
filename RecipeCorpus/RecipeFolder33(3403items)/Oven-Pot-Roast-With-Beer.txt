Title: Oven Pot Roast With Beer
Categories: Meats, Alcohol
Yield: 8 servings
4 lb Chuck roast -- * see note
1    Yellow onion -- sliced
2    Celery stalks -- chopped
1/4 c  Green peppers -- chopped
1/3 c  Water
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Salt
12 oz Beer
* Use a chuck roast or similar cut.

Place roast in large oven-proof pan with lid. Mix the remaining
ingredients except beer and pour evenly over roast. Roast uncovered
in 350-degree oven for 1 hour.

Pour room temperature beer over, COVER, and cook for 2-3 hours longer
depending on the thickness of the roast. Thicken liquid with flour if
you wish.

: Jo Anne Merrill
