Title: Sugarless Lactose Free Hot Cocoa Mix
Categories: Mixes, Beverages
Yield: 15 servings
3/4 c  Sugar substitute
1/2 c  Hershey's Cocoa
2 1/2 c  Powdered Nondairy Creamer
1    Dash Salt
In large mixing bowl combine all ingredients.  Blend well.  Store in
tightly covered container.

Makes 3 3/4 Cups of mix.

TO USE:

Combine 1/4 cup mix and 3/4 cup boiling water.  Stir.
