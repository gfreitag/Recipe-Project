Title: Jimmy Dean Sausage Balls
Categories: Breakfast, Sausage
Yield: 32 balls
1 pk JIMMY DEAN SAUSAGE, uncooked
10    Fat-free saltine crackers,
-crushed
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
4 oz Egg substitute
1 tb Chicken broth
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
Zesty sauce
1/2 c  Chili sauce
2 tb Cider vinegar
2 tb Brown sugar
Combine all ingredients together and roll into bite size balls. Place
sausage balls 1" apart on lightly greased baking sheet. Bake sausage
balls at 375'F. for 20 minutes.

ZESTY SAUCE: Combine chili sauce, cider vinegar and brown sugar in a
saucepan. Cook over low heat 10 minutes. Serve as a dip for the
sausage balls.

OTHER DELICIOUS SERVING IDEAS: Serve Jimmy Deas Sausage Balls along
with your favorite spaghetti sauce and pasta. Or use in hot meatball
sandwiches. Jimmy Dean Light Sausage can be used as a delicious
alternative to ground meat or regular pork sausage in any of your
favorite family recipes. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On
08-12-95
