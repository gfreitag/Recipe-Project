Title: Floating Island #1
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------------FLOATING ISLANDS---------------------------
2    Egg whites *
2 tb Sugar
MMMMM------------------------CUSTARD BASE-----------------------------
1 cn 14 oz Eagle Brand Sweetened
Condensed Milk
2    Egg yolk
1/2 c  Lime juice from concentrate
2 dr Green food color (optional)
2 tb Flaked coconut, toasted
Meringues: In small mixer bowl, beat 1 egg whites* until soft peaks
form. Gradually beat in 2 tablespoons of sugar, beating until stiff
but not dry. Drop one fourth of the mixture onto simmering water in
large skillet; repeat to make 4 islands. Simmer uncovered 5 minutes
or until meringues are set. Remove with slotted spoon; drain on paper
towels.

*Use only grade a clean un cracked eggs.

Custard:

Prepare floating islands (meringues)

Meanwhile in medium bowl, beat sweetened condensed milk and egg
yolks; stir in real lime and food coloring. Spoon into four 6 oz
dessert dishes. Top each with a "Floating Island"  Chill 2 hours or
until set. Garnish with coconut.  Refrigerate leftovers.
