Title: Tzatziki (Yoghurt Garlic Sauce)
Categories: Greek, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
Tzatziki (Yoghurt Garlic
-Sauce)
1 c  Yogurt (thick)
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Thin sliced cucumber
-slices, drained
1 ea Clove garlic
3 tb Oil
1 ts Vinegar
Add minced garlic to yogurt and work in salt, vinegar and oil,
working well until well blended.  Add sliced cucumbers and mix well.
Serve over fried zucchini squash.  A very cool and refreshing treat.
More or less garlic, salt  and vinegar can be added to suit your
taste.  Can also be used as a tossed salad dressing.
