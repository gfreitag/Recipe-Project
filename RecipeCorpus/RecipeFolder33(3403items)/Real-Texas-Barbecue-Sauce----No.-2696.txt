Title: Real Texas Barbecue Sauce    No. 2696
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 cups
1/4 c  Worcestershire Sauce
3 cl Garlic, Chopped Fine
1/2 c  Onions, Chopped
2 ts Chili Powder
1 c  Butter, Melted
1 c  Vegetable Oil
1/4 c  Brown Sugar
3    Lemons, Juice Only
1 c  Tomato Catsup
Tabasco Sauce
1 ts Dry Mustard
2 ts Salt
2 ts Red Pepper
1 tb Black Pepper, Ground
Combine all the ingredients in a saucepan. Bring to a boil. Reduce
heat. Simmer for 15 minutes. From: Joel Ehrlich
