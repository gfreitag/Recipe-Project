Title: Cranberry Apple Crisp
Categories: Breads, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Cranberries -- chopped
2 c  Apples -- shredded
1 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Margarine
1/4 ts Salt
3/4 c  Unbleached flour
2 ts Cinnamon
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a baking dish with cooking spray and
flour. In a mixing bowl, combine cranberries, apples, sugar,
margarine, salt, flour, and cinnamon until mixture is crumbly.  Bake
for 45 minutes.
