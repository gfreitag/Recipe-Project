Title: Mott's Baked Dessert Pizza
Categories: Desserts, Pizza
Yield: 8 servings
1    Refrigerated ready-made
-pie crust dough
1 c  MOTT'S Apple Sauce
Red food coloring
MMMMM------------------ASSORTED TREATS SUCH AS-----------------------
Gummie fish
Raisins
Flaked coconut
Fruit
HEAT the oven to 450'F.

PLACE the pie dough flat on a baking sheet.

BAKE for 9-11 minutes or until lightly golden and crisp.

MIX Apple Sauce with food coloring, in a small bowl.

SPREAD the Apple Sauce over the baked dough leaving a 1" border.

DECORATE as desired. Use your imagination and favorite treats to
create your own special MOTT'S Dessert Pizza.
