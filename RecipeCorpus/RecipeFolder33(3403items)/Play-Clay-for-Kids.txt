Title: Play Clay for Kids
Categories: Kids, Information
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Baking soda
1 c  Cornstarch
1 1/4 c  Water
Mix corn starch and baking soda, add water, mix. Bring to a boil,
stirring constantly. Thicken to consistency of mashed potatoes.

WARNING:  This is NOT edible.

Form clay into desired shaps, let dry for 36 hours, color with paint
or felt-tip marker. Coat with SPRAY shellac or clear nail polish.
