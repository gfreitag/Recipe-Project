Title: Garam Masala (Bengali Style)
Categories: Indian, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Cloves
1    Inch cinnamon stick
2 tb Black cardamom seeds
Dry roast the spices separately in a skillet over moderate heat until
aromatic.  Grind to a fine powder in a spice grinder.  Store in an
airtight container for 1 to 2 months.  Makes about 6 tbsp.
