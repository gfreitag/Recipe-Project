Title: WHITE CHOCOLATE SAUCE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salsa/sauce, Desserts
Yield: 2 cups
6 oz Imported white chocolate
-(such as Lindt), finely
-chopped
6 oz Cream cheese, room temp.
1/2 c  (1 stick) unsalted butter,
-room temperature
2 tb Cognac or brandy
Stir chocolate in top of double bouer over simmering water until
melted and smooth. Let cool to barely lukewarm.

Using electric mixer, beat mm cheese and butter in medium bowl until
fluffy. Gradually beat in chocolate. Mix in Cognac. Transfer to bowl.
(Can be made 4 hours ahead. Chill. Let soften slightly at room
temperature before serving.)
