Title: BLACK BEAN SALAD W/ ARTICHOKES RED BELL PEPPER & GOAT CHE
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings;
1 cn (15 to 16-oz) black beans,
-rinsed, drained
2/3 c  Chopped red bell pepper
1/2 c  Chopped sweet yellow onion
-(such as Maui or Vidalia)
1    6-oz jar marinated artichoke
-hearts
1    Bunch fresh arugula
2 oz Soft mild goat cheese (such
-as Montrachet), crumbled
Combine beans, bell pepper and onion in medium bowl. Stir in
artichokes with marinade. Season with salt and pepper.

Arrange arugula on 2 plates. Spoon bean salad atop arugula. Sprinkle
goat cheese over and serve.
