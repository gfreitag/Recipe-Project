Title: Green Korhonen
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Cocktails
Yield: 1 serving
1 oz Vodka (preferably Finlandia)
1/2 oz Blue Curacao
1/2 oz Triple Sec
Orange Juice
Sprite
Fill tall glass 1/3 with ice. Insert spirits. Stir. Fill up 50-50 with
Orange Juice and Sprite. If you got it right, the color is green.
NEVER use more than 1/2 oz Triple Sec.
