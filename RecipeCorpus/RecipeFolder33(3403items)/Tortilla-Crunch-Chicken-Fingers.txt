Title: Tortilla Crunch Chicken Fingers
Categories: Snacks, Poultry
Yield: 24 strips
1 pk Lipton Recipe Secrets Fiesta
-Herb with Red Pepper Soup
-Mix
1 c  Finely crushed plain
-tortilla chips (about 3 oz)
1 1/2 lb Boneless skinless chicken
-breast halves, cut into
-strips
1    Egg
2 tb Water
2 tb Melted margarine or butter
Preheat oven to 400'F.

In medium bowl, combine fiesta herb with red pepper soup mix and
tortilla chips. In large plastic bag or bowl, combine chicken and egg
beaten with water, coating well. Dip chicken in tortilla mixture,
coating well. In 15 1/2x10 1/2x1" jelly roll pan sprayed with no
stick cooking spray, arrange chicken; drizzle with margarine. Bake
uncovered 12 minutes or until chicken is done. Makes about 24 chicken
strips.

TIP: Serve with your favorite fresh or prepared salsa.
