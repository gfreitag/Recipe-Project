Title: PETA Vanilla Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Sugar
3 tb Cornstarch
1/8 ts Salt
2 c  Soy milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
Mix sugar, cornstarch, and salt in a large saucepan.  Gradually blend
in soy milk stirring constantly to avoid lumps.

Cook the mixture over low heat, stirring constantly, until thickened.
Cook for an additional 2-3 mins, stirring now and then.

Remove pudding from heat and add vanilla.  Let cool for 10 mins,
stirring occasionally.  Pour pudding into large serving dish or
individual dishes, and chill until firm, about 2-3 hours.
