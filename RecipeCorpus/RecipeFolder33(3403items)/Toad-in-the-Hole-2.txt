Title: Toad in the Hole 2
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
4 sl Bread
3 ts Butter or margarine
4 ea Eggs
Salt
Pepper
Remove centers from each slice of bread with a biscut cutter. Brown in
butter on one side then turn.  Slip egg into hole and cook to desired
firmness, seasoning to taste.
