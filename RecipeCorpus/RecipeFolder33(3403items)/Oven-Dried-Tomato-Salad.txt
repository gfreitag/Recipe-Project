Title: Oven-Dried Tomato Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 lb Assorted field greens
10    Dried tomatoes, chopped
1    Yellow sweet pepper, sliced
-and chopped
3 oz Large, ripe olives, whole
1/2    Cucumber, sliced
1 oz Pine nuts
4 oz Feta cheese, crumbled
Mix all of the ingredients together in a large bowl in the order
given.

Nutritional information per serving: 266 calories; 8 grams fat (3
grams saturated fat); 13 grams cholesterol; 44 grams carbohydrates;
14 grams protein; 1680 milligrams sodium; 218 milligrams calcium; 8
milligrams iron. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-07-95
