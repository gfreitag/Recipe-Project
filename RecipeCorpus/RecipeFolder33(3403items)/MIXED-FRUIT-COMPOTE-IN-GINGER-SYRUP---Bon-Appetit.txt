Title: MIXED-FRUIT COMPOTE IN GINGER SYRUP - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar
12    Quarter-size slices peeled
-fresh ginger
8    Thin slices peeled seeded
-cantaloupe
4    Strawberries, huued, halved
2    Apricots or plums, pitted,
-halved
1    Star fruit (carambola),
-sliced OR
1 c  Sliced peeled quartered
-pineapple
1    Kiwifruit, peeled, sliced
1 tb Fresh lime juice
Combine water, sugar and ginger in heavy medium saucepan. Bring to
boil, stirring until sugar dissolves. Boil until liquid is reduced to
1 C, about 15 minutes. Cool to room temperature.

Arrange all fruit in shallow bowl. Sprinkle with lime juice. Strain
syrup over fruit. Remove 3 ginger slices from strainer; cut into
matchstick-size strips. Sprinkle strips over fruit. Cover and chili
at least 3 hours or up to 8 hours.

Arrange compote on plates; spoon some syrup over and serve cold.

Bon Appetit/August/94 Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
