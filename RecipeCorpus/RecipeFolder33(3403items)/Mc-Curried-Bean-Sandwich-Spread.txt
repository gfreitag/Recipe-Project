Title: Mc Curried Bean Sandwich Spread
Categories: Vegetables, Sandwich, Dips/spread
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Water
1    Onion; finely chopped
1 c  Diced celery
1    Green bell pepper; diced
1/2 c  Diced carrot
2    Cl Garlic; minced
2 1/2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1 tb Soy sauce
3 c  Cooked white beans
Recipe by: The New McDougall Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:20 Place the
water in a saucepan and add all of the vegetables and the garlic.
Cook, stirring occasionally, for 15 minutes. Stir in the curry
powder, cumin, and soy sauce, and mix well. Remove from the heat. Add
the beans; mix well. Place the mixture in a food processor or blender
and process briefly until chopped but not pureed.  Chill.
