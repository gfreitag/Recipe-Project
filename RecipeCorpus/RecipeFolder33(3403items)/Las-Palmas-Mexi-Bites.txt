Title: Las Palmas Mexi-Bites
Categories: Appetizers, Tex/mexican
Yield: 10 servings
1 cn Refrigerated pizza crust
-dough (10 oz)
6 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 cn LAS PALMAS Salsa Mexicana,
-well drained, divided usage
1 c  Shredded lettuce
1 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
Sliced green onion tops
Sliced ripe olives
Pickled jalapeno slices
1. Preheat oven to 425'F. On lightly greased baking sheet, press pizza
dough into a 13 1/2x11 1/2" rectangle. Bake 8-10 minutes until
browned; cool.

2. Meanwhile, in medium bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Beat in
1/2 cup drained salsa. Spread over cooled crust.

3. Top with shredded lettuce, cheese and remaining drained salsa.
Garnish with green onions, olives and jalapenos. Cut into 2" squares
to serve. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-09-95
