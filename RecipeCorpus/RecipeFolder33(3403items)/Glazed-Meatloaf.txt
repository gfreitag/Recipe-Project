Title: Glazed Meatloaf
Categories: Poultry, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Turkey; cooked
1/2 c  Cracker crumbs or bread crum
1 c  Rice; cooked
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/4 c  Carrot; grated
1/4 c  Celery; chopped fine
1 1/2 ts Mustard; prepared or dijon
1 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts White pepper
1/4 c  Tomato sauce
2    Egg whites
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Honey
Recipe by: Cooking without Fat Preparation Time: 0:20 Preheat oven at
350. Prepare a loaf pan with cooking.  Combine all ingredients except
ketchup and honey and mix well.  Turn mixture into a prepared pan and
bake for 40 minutes.  Combine ketchup and honey.  Spread evenly over
meat and bake 5 minutes longer.
