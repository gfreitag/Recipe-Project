Title: Molly McButter Lite Fettuccine Alfredo
Categories: Main dish, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
2 sm Garlic cloves, minced
1 1/3 c  Skim milk, divided
2 ts All-purpose flour
2 tb Fat free cream cheese
1 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
5 ts Molly McButter Natural
-Butter Flavor Sprinkles
4 c  Hot cooked fettuccine
2 ts Chopped fresh parsley
Freshly ground pepper
In medium saucepan, combine minced garlic and 1 cup skim milk. Bring
to a boil. Reduce heat to simmer. Stir in flour and remaining 1/3 cup
milk. Continue stirring for about 2 minutes, until thickened. Add fat
free cream cheese. Whisk vigorously until smooth. Cook for 2 minutes.
Add grated Parmesan cheese; whisk until smooth. Remove from heat.
Stir in Molly McButter. Pour the sauce over hot cooked fettuccine.
Garnish with fresh parsley and black pepper. Makes 4 1-cup servings.
