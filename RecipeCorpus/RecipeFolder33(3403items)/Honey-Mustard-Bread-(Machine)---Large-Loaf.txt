Title: Honey Mustard Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
3/4 c  Water
2 c  White bread flour
1 c  Wheat bread flour
1 tb Dry milk
1/4 c  Honey
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chicken broth, low salt can
2 1/2 tb Gourmet mustard
2 ts Chives, dried
2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
3 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

The mustard adds a subtle, secondary flavor.  This bread makes an
unforgettable ham sandwich, or serve it warm with baked ham or roast
duck.
