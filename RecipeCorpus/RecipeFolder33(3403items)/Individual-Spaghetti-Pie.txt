Title: Individual Spaghetti Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 serving
1 c  Cooked spaghetti
1/2 cn Tuna packed in water,
-drained (6 oz)
1/2 c  Frozen peas, thawed
1/2 c  Canned condensed cream of
-asparagus soup
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/3 c  Shredded Jarlsberg Lite or
-low-fat Swiss cheese
Place spaghetti in a small microwaveable pie plate or ramekin. In a
bowl, mix tuna, peas, soup and curry powder. Stir in cheese and spoon
over spaghetti. Cover with wax paper and microwave on high 3-4
minutes, or until steaming hot.
