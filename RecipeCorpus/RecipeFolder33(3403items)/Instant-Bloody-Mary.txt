Title: Instant Bloody Mary
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 oz Vodka
3 1/2 oz Spicy V8 Cocktail
1 ds Lemon juice -- (optional)
Fill an old fashioned glass with ice. Add vodka, V8, and (if desired)
a dash of fresh lemon juice. Stir and garnish with a slice of lime
and a celery stalk.
