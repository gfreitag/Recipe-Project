Title: Tropical Pineapple Banana Shake
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Pineapple; crushed
1 lg Banana; quartered
1 c  Buttermilk*; at room tempera
1 tb Honey
2 ts Ginger
1 dr Coconut extract
Recipe by: Reader's Digest Live Longer Cookbook Preparation Time:
0:05 In a blender, combine pineapple, banana, buttermilk, honey,
ginger, and coconut extract.  Whirl for 1 minute or until smooth.
Pour into glasses.
