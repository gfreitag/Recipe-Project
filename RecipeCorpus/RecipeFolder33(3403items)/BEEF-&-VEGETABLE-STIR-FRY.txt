Title: BEEF & VEGETABLE STIR FRY
Categories: Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
2 lb Beef sirloin or inside round
--steak; boneless
4 tb Vegetable oil
4 c  Fresh vegetables; cut up
(broccoli, carrot, celery)
4    Garlic cloves; minced
2 cn Tomato soup
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Vinegar
Slice beef across the grain into thin strips,

In large skillet or wok, heat oil.  Stir-fry beef until browned.
remove from skillet; set aside.

In same skillet, in remaining oil, stir-fry vegetables with garlic
until tender-crisp.

Stir in soup, soy sauce and vinegar.  Bring to boil.  Return beef to
skillet.  Heat through, stirring occasionally.  Serve with rice, if
desired.

Origin:  Campbell's Tomato Soup
