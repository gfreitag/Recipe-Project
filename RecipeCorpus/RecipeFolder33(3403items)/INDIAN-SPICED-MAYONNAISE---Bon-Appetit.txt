Title: INDIAN SPICED MAYONNAISE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Condiments
Yield: 1 cup
1 c  Packed fresh mint leaves
1 c  Packed fresh cilantro leaves
1    Jalapeno chili, seeded,
-minced
3 tb Chopped onion
2 ts Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Combine 1 C mint leaves, 1 C cilantro leaves, minced jalapeno chili
and chopped onion in work bowl of processor, Process until very
finely chopped. Mix in cider vinegar. Add mayonnaise and process just
until combined. Season mayonnaise to taste with salt and pepper.
(Mayonnaise can be prepared 3 days ahead. Cover tightly and
refrigerate.)
