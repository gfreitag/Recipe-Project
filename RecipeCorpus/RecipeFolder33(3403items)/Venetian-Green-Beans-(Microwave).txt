Title: Venetian Green Beans (Microwave)
Categories: Side dish, Microwave, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Finely sliced onion
1 cl Garlic; crushed
2 tb Butter
1 lb Green beans
14 oz Can tomatoes; drained and
-chopped
1/4 ts Dried marjoram
Salt and pepper
Put the onion, garlic, and butter in a medium casserole dish and
cook on high for 3 minutes.
Add the beans, tomatoes, and marjoram.  Cover and heat on high for 8
minutes.  Leave to stand for 5 minutes.
Season lightly with salt and pepper, and serve hot.
