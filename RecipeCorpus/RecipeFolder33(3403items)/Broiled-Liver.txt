Title: Broiled Liver
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 serving
1 sl Calf's Liver
Butter
3 sl Bacon
1    Bermuda onion; sliced
Sauce Marchand de Vin
Preheat the broiler. Brush the liver with butter. Broil 1 minute per
side. Saute the bacon slices to your liking. Saute the onion slices
in the bacon drippings until soft. Place the sautee bacon slices on
top of the liver. Place the sauteed onion slices on top of the bacon
slices. Serve with Sauce Marchand de Vin (Mushroom Wine Sauce). From:
