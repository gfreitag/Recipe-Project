Title: LINGONBERRY SAUCE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
1    10-oz jar Swedish
-lingonberries in sugar OR
1 c  Canned whole berry
-cranberry sauce
1/2 c  Ruby Port
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Dijon mustard
Simmer lingonberries, Port and sugar in heavy small saucepan until
thick and reduced to 1 C, stirring frequently, about 15 minutes.
Whisk in mustard. Let cool to room temperature.

NOTE: They offer this sauce with a venison terrine at the Hotel
Residence in Trondheim. Try it at home with grilled pork or chicken.

Bon Appetit/August/94 Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
