Title: Easy Banana Tiramisu
Categories: Newspaper, Fruit, Puddings, Desserts, Chocolate
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2 c  Milk; low-fat
2 tb Instant coffee granules
1 pk Cream cheese; 8oz
1 pk Vanilla pudding; 4-serving
.  size
2 c  Frozen nondairy whipped
.  topping; thawed
3 md Ripe bananas; sliced
6 oz Ladyfingers; split and cut
. in half
1 1/2 oz Semisweet chocolate; grated
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
1    Banana; sliced (opt)
Chocolate shavings (opt)
Stir together the milk and coffee until the coffee is almost
dissolved.

Beat together the cream cheese and sugar in a large bowl until smooth
and blended.  Add the pudding mix; gradually beat in the coffee
mixture until smooth and blended.  Gently stir in the whipped topping
and the three sliced bananas until just blended.

Layer 1/3 of the ladyfingers on the bottom and sides of a 3-quart
casserole dish; evenly spoon in 1/3 of the cream mixture and sprinkle
with 1/2 the grated chocolate.  Repeat layers, ending with cream
mixture. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.

Garnish with additional sliced bananas and chocolate shavings, if
desired.

Nutritional Information per serving:  235 calories, 11g fat, 32g
carbohydrates, 57mg cholesterol, 208mg sodium
