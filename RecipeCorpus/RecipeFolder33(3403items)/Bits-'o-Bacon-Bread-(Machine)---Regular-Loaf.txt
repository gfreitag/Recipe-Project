Title: Bits 'o Bacon Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
2/3 c  Water
1 2/3 c  White bread flour
1/3 c  Wheat bread flour
4 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
4 ts Butter
1/2 ts Green pepper corns *
1/2 ts Sweet basil, dried
Pinch Garlic powder
1/4 c  Bacon, with fat
1 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

This is one of those loaves the whole family eats up, right from the
oven. If it lasts long enough, this tasty loaf enlivens any soup or
salad.

Success Hints
-------------

* Green pepper corns should be dried & crushed

Cook bacon till almost crisp.  Set bacon fat aside while you crumble
cooked bacon, then remix bacon & fat before measuring.

After loaf is partially cooled, brush with butter and sprinkle a
little coarsly-ground salt on top.

Use regular or rapid bake cycle.
