Title: Salsa Peruana Aji de Miguel (Miguel's Peruvian Aji Sauce)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetables, So. america
Yield: 1 cup
1/2 c  Olive oil
4 ea Or more fresh aji chiles;
-seeds and stems removed,
-minced, or substitute
-yellow wax hot chiles or
-jalapenos
2 cl Garlic; minced
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1 tb Fresh lime juice
Heat the oil in a small skillet, and when it's hot, add the chiles
and the garlic, lower the heat, and stir constantly to avoid burning
the garlic.  Add the remaining ingredients and stir.  Simmer for ten
minutes and then serve over rice or pasta.
