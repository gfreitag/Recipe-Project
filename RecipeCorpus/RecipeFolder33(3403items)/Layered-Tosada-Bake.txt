Title: Layered Tosada Bake
Categories: Poultry, Tex/mexican, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Turkey; ground/cooked
1 md Onion; chopped
1 pk Taco seasoning mix
1 cn Tomato sauce; (8 oz)
1 cn Fat-free refried beans; (16
1 c  Baking mix
1/2 c  Cornmeal
1/4 c  Skim milk; at room temperatu
1    Egg white
2 tb Olive oil
1 c  Fat-free sour cream
1    Egg whites
2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
Recipe by: Betty Crocker's Mexican Made Easy Preparation Time: 0:15
Preheat oven at 375.  Prepare a 12 x 7 x 2" baking dish, with lightly
cooking spray.  Cook and stir turkey and onion in a skillet until
meat is brown. Stir in seasoning mix, tomato sauce and beans.  In a
mixing bowl, combine baking mix, cornmeal, milk, egg white, and oil
until moistened. Spread in dish.  Spoon meat mixture over dough.  Mix
remaining ingredients; spoon over meat mixture.  Bake uncovered 30
minutes.  Let stand 10 minutes before cutting.
