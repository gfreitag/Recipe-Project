Title: Cold Crappie Salad
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Cooked, flaked crappie (or
-bluegill)
1    Hard cooked egg, quartered
6 sl Cucumber pickles
1    Tomato, cut in quarters
8 sl Pickled beets
Lettuce, shredded
Dressing:
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Ketchup
2 ts Relish
1 ts Horseradish
1/4 ts Mustard
Put shredded lettuce all over the plates. Place fish in center.
Arrange egg, pickles, tomato and beets around edge of plate. Top with
dressing (or use Thousand Island dressing).
