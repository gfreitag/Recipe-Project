Title: Chocolate Peanut Butter Mickey Mouse Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 30 cookies
2 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Butter or margarine,
-softened
1 c  Peanut butter
1 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
2    Eggs
2 c  NESTLE TOLL HOUSE Semi-Sweet
-Chocolate Morsels, divided
1 c  Quick oats
COMBINE flour and baking soda in small bowl. Beat butter, peanut
butter, sugars and vanilla in large mixer bowl until creamy. Beat in
eggs; beat in flour mixture. Stir in 1 cup morsels and oats; freeze
until slightly firm but now hard.

ROLL dough into 1 1/2" ball; press down slightly on ungreased baking
sheet. Roll a 1 1/2" ball; cut in half. Attach to first ball as ears.
Repeat with remaining dough; place 2" apart.

BAKE in preheated 350'F. oven for 10-15 minutes or until light brown
and centers are soft. Let stand for 3 minutes; remove to wire racks
to cool.

MICROWAVE remaining morsels in heavy-duty plastic bag on HIGH (100%)
power for 45 seconds; knead. Microwave at addtional 10-20 second
intervals, kneading until smooth. Cut tiny corner from bag; squeeze
to pipe chocolate to create Mickey Mouse faces. Allow chocolate to
set. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-12-95
