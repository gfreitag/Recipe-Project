Title: Ant Poison^
Categories: Information, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1    Text only
Dissolve one pound of sugar in one quart of water. Add 125 grains of
arsenate of soda (10 cents worth.) Boil, strain and cool. Add a few
drops honey. Saturate small sponges in this syrup and plant where the
ants can have ready access to them.
