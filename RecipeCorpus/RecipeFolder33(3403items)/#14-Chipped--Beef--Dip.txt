Title: #14 Chipped  Beef  Dip
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Pkg. cream cheese
1 tb Milk
1    (2 1/2 oz.) jar chipped
-beef
2 tb Minced onion
3 tb Chopped green pepper
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Chopped almonds or walnuts
Preparation : Mix all together. Place in casserole. Heat at 350
degrees for 15 minutes. From: Glenn Deery Date: 01-30-95
Submitted By DALE SHIPP   On   07-30-95
