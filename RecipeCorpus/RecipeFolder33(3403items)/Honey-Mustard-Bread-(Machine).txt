Title: Honey Mustard Bread (Machine)
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1/2 c  Water
1 1/2 c  White bread flour
1/2 c  Wheat bread flour
2 ts Dry milk
2 1/2 tb Honey
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Chicken broth, low salt/can
1 2/3 tb Gourmet mustard
1 ts Chives, dried
1 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

The mustard adds a subtle, secondary flavor.  This bread makes an
unforgettable ham sandwich, or serve it warm with baked ham or roast
duck.
