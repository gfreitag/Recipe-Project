Title: RIVER BISTRO CAESAR SALAD-DETROIT - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads
Yield: 2 generous s
4    Anchovy fillets, minced (or
-1 1/2 t anchovy paste)
1 lg Garlic clove, pressed
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 ts Red wine vinegar
1/4 ts Hot pepper sauce (such as
-Tabasco)
1/4 c  Olive oil
1    Head romaine lettuce, torn
-into bite-size pieces
-(about 8 C)
1 c  Purchased croutons
1/2 c  Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese (about 1 1/2 oz)
Whisk minced anchovies, garlic, mustard, vinegar and hot pepper sauce
in large bowl. Gradually add 1/4 C olive oil and whisk until thick.
Season dressing to taste with salt and pepper. Add lettuce and toss.
Add croutons and cheese and toss again. Divide salad between plates
and serve.
