Title: Vegetables with Toasted Walnut Butter
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 5 servings
1 pk Green Giant American
-Mixtures California Style
-Frozen Vegetables (1 lb)
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped walnuts
1/4 ts Salt
Cook vegetables as directed on package; drain. Melt butter in small
saucepan over medium heat; add walnuts and salt. Cook for 2-4 minutes
until lightly browned, stirring constantly. Stir into prepared
vegetables. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-14-95
