Title: Pumpernickel
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 loaf
3 1/2 ts Yeast
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Rye Flour
1 tb Unsweetened Cocoa
1/2 c  Whole-Bran cereal
3 tb Molasses
1 tb Safflower Oil
1 3/4 c  Bread Flour
1 1/4 c  Warm Water
2 ts Caraway Seed
All ingredients should be at room temperature before starting. Add the
ingredients to the pan in the order listed. Select "White Bread".
Press "Start". Joel.
