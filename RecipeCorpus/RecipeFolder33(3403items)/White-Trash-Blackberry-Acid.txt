Title: White Trash Blackberry Acid
Categories: Beverages, Oldies, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 ga Blackberries, unwashed
1 oz Tartaric acid
Sugar
Put berries in stone crock, cover with water and add acid.  let stand
for 24 hours, then strain.  To each pint of liquid, add a pound of
sugar. Boil for 20 minutes.  Bottle hot, and keep in a dry, cool
place. Serve ice cold, diluted with water, or else over crushed ice.
If you're lucky, it'll ferment... Origin: White Trash Cooking. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, July/95.
