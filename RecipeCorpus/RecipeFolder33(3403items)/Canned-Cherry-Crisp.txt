Title: Canned Cherry Crisp
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1    21 oz. can cherry pie
-filling
1 ts Almond extract
1/4 c  Rolled oats, uncooked
1/4 c  Toasted coconuts
2 tb All-purpose flour
2 tb Brown sugar
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
2 tb Butter or margarine,
-softened
Pour cherry pie filling into an 8-inch, round microwave-safe dish.
Sprinkle with almond extract. Spread mixture evenly in dish.

In a small bowl, combine oats, coconut, flour, brown sugar and
cinnamon; mix well.

Cut in butter with a fork until mixture is crumbly. Sprinkle oat
mixture over cherry filling.

Microwave, uncovered, on HIGH (100% power) 4 to 5 minutes (rotating
dish twice), or until filling is hot and bubbly.
