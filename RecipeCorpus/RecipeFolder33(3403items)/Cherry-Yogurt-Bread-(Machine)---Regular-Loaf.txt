Title: Cherry Yogurt Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1/2 c  Water
2 c  White bread flour
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Cherries, dried
1/3 c  Cherry yogurt, low fat
2 tb Applesauce, unsweetened
2 ts Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

This sweet bread is surprisingly versatile.  You can use it in lieu of
raisin bread for French toast, or serve it warm with cream cheese and
honey for a dessert bread.  The applesauce and yogurt contribute a
refleshing moistness to this light loaf.

Success Hints
-------------

Add the dried cherries whole, at "add-in" time.

This recipe can be used with the regular and rapid bake cycles.
