Title: SHRIMP SMORREBROD - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Sandwich, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
6 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Salt
1 ts Black peppercorns
3/4 lb Uncooked small shrimp
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Chopped fixsh dill
2 tb Drained horseradish
1 ts Grated lemon peel
Butter, room temperature
4    Slices white bread, crusts
-trimmed
4 sm Butter lettuce leaves
Bring first 4 ingredients to boil in large saucepan, stirring until
sugar dissolves. Add shrimp; remove from heat. Let stand until shrimp
are just cooked through, about 10 minutes. Drain shrimp and chill
until cold. Peel and devein shrimp.

Mix mayonnaise, dill, horseradish and lemon peel in small bowl.
Season with salt and pepper.

Butter 1 side of each bread slice. Spread some mayonnaise mixture
over. Top each slice with lettuce leaf. Arrange shrimp atop lettuce.
Spoon additional mayonnaise mixture over shrimp.
