Title: Whiskey a Go Go Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Dry mustard
2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Burbon; to taste
1/2 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Vinegar
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Tabasco sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Soy sauce
4    Cl Garlic; crushed
1/2 ts Pepper
1 tb Parsley; dried
In a pot mix dry mustard and a little of the ketchup to form a paste.
Slowly add the rest of the ketchup, stirring constanly. Add everything
else. bring to boil over medium heat then simmer 10 min.
