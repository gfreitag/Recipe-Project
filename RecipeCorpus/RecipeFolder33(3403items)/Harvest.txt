Title: Harvest
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Water
1 c  Cranberries -- chopped
1 c  Apple -- grated
1/2 c  Apple juice
1 tb Lemon peel
2    Egg whites -- whipped
2 c  Unbleached flour
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1/2 c  Raisins
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.
In a mixing bowl, combine water, cranberries, apple, apple juice,
lemon peel, and egg whites.  In another mixing bowl, combine flour,
sugars, raisins, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
Mix wet ingredients with dry ingredients just until moistened.  Pour
batter into prepared pan(s). Bake for 20 minutes depending on pan
size.
