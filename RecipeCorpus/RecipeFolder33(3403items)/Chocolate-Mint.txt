Title: Chocolate Mint
Categories: Holiday, Chocolate
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM-------------------------BATTER----------------------------------
2 c  Unbleached flour
1 1/2 c  Granulated sugar
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
3/4 c  Water -- cold
1/2 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
1/2 ts Peppermint extract
7    Egg whites -- whipped
Mint candies
MMMMM------------------------FROSTING---------------------------------
3 c  Whipping cream -- chilled
1 1/2 c  Powdered sugar -- sifted
3/4 c  Cocoa powder -- sifted
1 1/2 ts Peppermint extract
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking powder
and salt. In another mixing bowl, combine water, applesauce,
peppermint extract, egg whites.  Divide batter between prepared
pan(s).  Bake until top springs back when touched lightly, about 50
to 55 minutes depending on pan size.
