Title: Sauteed Chicken in Pumpkin Chili Puree
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1    Whole Chicken Breast,
Skinned, Boned & Cut
Into 1/2" Wide Strips
1 1/2 tb Pumpkin Seeds, Toasted
1    Chile pepper
1 tb Almonds, toasted
1 tb Peanut oil
Combine the peanut oil, toasted pumpkin seeds, toasted almonds and the
chili pepper in a blender. Puree thoroughly to form a very fine
grained mixture. Saute the chicken strips in the pumpkin seed puree
until cooked to your liking. Serve.
