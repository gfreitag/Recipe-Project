Title: SLICED TOMATOS SALAD WITH TARRAGON - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
8 lg Tomatoes, sliced
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Balsamic vinegar
1    Bunch fresh tarragon,
-stemmed
Arrange tomatoes on platter. Drizzle oil, then vinegar over tomatoes.
Season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with tarragon.
