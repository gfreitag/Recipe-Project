Title: Best-Ever Stewed Apples
Categories: Side dish, Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
8 ea Granny Smith Apples
1 c  Apple juice
4 ea Cloves
Sugar to taste
Peel, core and thickly slice 8 Granny Smith apples.  Place in a large
pan with + cup apple juice and 4 cloves.  Bring to the boil and
simmer, covered, for 15 mins.  Stir occasionally so the apples cook
evenly.  Add + cup sugar and simmer, covered, for further 5 mins,
stirring occasionally.

Makes 4 cups.
