Title: Strawberry Colada
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Pineapple juice
1/4 c  Nonalcoholic pina colada mix
1 c  Strawberries
2 c  Strawberry ice milk or ice
-cream
4    Whole strawberries
In a blender or food processor, combine pineapple juice, pina colada
mix, 1 cut strawberries and ice milk. Blend until smooth. Pour into 4
glasses. Garnish with whole strawberries.
