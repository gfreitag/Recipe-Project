Title: Sweet and Spicy Spareribs
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2    Cl Garlic; minced
1 ts Jalapeno pepper; finely chop
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Rosemary
3 tb Honey
1 c  Tomato sauce
2 md Orange juice only
Salt and pepper; to taste
Preheat bbq grill on low.

Place the first 7 ingredients in a small saucepan and cook over very
low heat. Meanwhile, blanch ribs in boiling water for 15 min. Drain
ribs and baste with the warm mixture. Place ribs on hot grill and
partly cover. cook 30 min., depending on size. Turn ribs frequently,
baste occasionally and season well.
