Title: Pumpkin Seeds
Categories: Holiday, Kids
Yield: 6 servings
Seeds from 1 pumpkin
2 tb Margarine
ds Worcestershire sauce
Salt
While carving pumpkin save seeds and rinse them.  Melt margarine in
saucepan and add worcestershire sauce.  Mix with seeds.  Spread seeds
on a baking sheet and sprinkle with a little salt.  Bake seeds at
350, stirring occasionally, for about 15 to 20 minutes, until seeds
are crispy.
