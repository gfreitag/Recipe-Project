Title: Sugar Free No Bake Cookies
Categories: Diabetic, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 24 servings
6 tb Nestle Sugar Free Quik
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Milk (use 1%)
1 c  Flaked coconut
3 c  Quick oatmeal
*NOTE* On margarine you can use `I Can't believe it's not butter'

In food prosessor or mixer bowl add Sugar Free Quik, vanilla,
margarine and milk and blend until smooth. Add coconut and oatmeal
and blend until mixed together well.

On a cookie sheet place a piece of wax paper and take spoonful of the
mixture and rroll into a ball place close together. Ready to eat in 1
hour. Keep in the refrigerator.
