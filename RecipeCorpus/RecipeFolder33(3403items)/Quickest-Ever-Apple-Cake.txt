Title: Quickest-Ever Apple Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Barbara
Yield: 1 cake
1    Or 2 tart apples, peeled,
-cored, thickly sliced, and
-sprinkled with lemon juice
3/4 c  Plus 2 Tb. sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
12 ts Butter, melted (1 1/2
-sticks)
1 c  Flour, sifted
1    Egg, slightly beaten
Arrange apples in buttered 9" pie plate.  Sprinkle with 1 tablespoon
sugar and cinnamon.

Pour melted butter in medium bowl.  Add 3/4 cup sugar, flour, and egg.
Stir until smooth.  Spread batter evenly over apples.  Sprinkle with 1
tablespoon sugar.

Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes, until pale golden brown.
