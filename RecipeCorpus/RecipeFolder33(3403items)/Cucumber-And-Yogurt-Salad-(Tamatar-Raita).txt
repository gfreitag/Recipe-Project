Title: Cucumber And Yogurt Salad (Tamatar Raita)
Categories: Indian, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 sm Cucumber -- peeled & seeded
1 sm Onion -- minced
2 1/2 c  Plain yogurt
1 tb Mint leaves -- chopped
1    Chili pepper -- seeded &
Sliced
Finely shred the cucumber into 1/2" lengths.  Place in a bowl with the
onion and yogurt.  Stir to mix.  Cover and chill in fridge until
ready to serve,  Garnish with mint and pepper.  90 calories and 0
grams fat.
