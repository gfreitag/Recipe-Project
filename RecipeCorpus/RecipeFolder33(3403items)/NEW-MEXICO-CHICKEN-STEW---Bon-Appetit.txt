Title: NEW MEXICO CHICKEN STEW - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Poultry, Soup/stew
Yield: 2 servings;
1 tb Olive oil
2    Boneless chicken breast
-halves
1 ts Ground cumin
2 lg Garlic cloves, chopped
1 cn (14 1/2-oz) chili-style
-chunky tomatoes
3/4 c  Drained canned golden hominy
2 tb Canned diced green chilies
4 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
Heat oil in heavy medium skillet over medium-high heat. Season
chicken with cumin, salt and pepper. Add chicken to skillet; saute
until brown, about 3 minutes per side. Reduce heat to medium low. Add
garlic; saute 30 seconds. Add tomatoes, hominy, green chilies and 2 T
cilantro. Simmer uncovered until juices thicken slightly and chicken
is just cooked through, turning chicken once, about 7 minutes.
Transfer chicken to platter; spoon sauce over. Garnish with 2 T
cilantro.

Bon Appetit/January/1994  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
