Title: Chocolate Doubles Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins, Chocolate
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2 c  Unbleached flour
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/4 c  Cocoa powder -- sifted
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt -- at room temperature
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Applesauce -- whipped
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare 12 muffin tins with cooking spray and
flour. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda and
salt.  In another mixing bowl, combine water, applesauce, vinegar,
vanilla, and chocolate chips.  Stir just until moistened.  Use an ice
cream scoop to fill muffin tins two thirds full.  Bake for 20 to 25
minutes or until brown.
