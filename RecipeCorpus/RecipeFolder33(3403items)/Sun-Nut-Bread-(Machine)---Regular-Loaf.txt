Title: Sun Nut Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
3/4 c  Water
1 3/4 c  White bread flour
1/2 c  Wheat bread flour
1 tb Dry milk
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
2 tb Honey
1/4 c  Sunflower seeds
1 1/4 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

This is a bread to make sunflower-lovers sing.  The sunflower seeds
impart a lovely, subtle taste and a lovely, chewy texture.  Toasted,
this bread does wonderful things with fruit and jelly, and it will
breath new life into that predictable cheese sandwich.

Success Hints
-------------

Sunflower seed salt content varies by brand.  If using low-salt or
unsalted seeds, adjust salt accordingly.  If usint unsalted sunflower
seeds, increase the salt by a pinch.
Use regular, rapid bake or time delayed bake cycle.
