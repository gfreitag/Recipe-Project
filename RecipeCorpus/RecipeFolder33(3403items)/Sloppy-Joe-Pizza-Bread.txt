Title: Sloppy Joe Pizza Bread
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Lean ground beef
3/4 c  Frozen corn
3/4 c  Barbecue sauce
1/2 c  Green onion
1/2 ts Salt
1    Pizza crust
1 1/2 c  Cheddar cheese
1.  Heat oven to 425 degrees F.  In large nonstick skillet, brown
ground beef over medium heat 8 to 10 minutes or until no longer pink,
stirring occasionally.  Pour off drippings. 2. Stir in corn, barbecue
sauce, green onions and salt, if desired; heat through. 3. Place
bread shell on large baking sheet.  Top evenly with beef mixture;
sprinkle with cheese. Bake 12 to 15 minutes or until cheese melts;
cut into 6 wedges. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   08-14-95
