Title: 847345 Snow Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Lemon flavored gelatin
2    Egg whites, stiffly beaten
1 c  Boiling water
MMMMM------------------------SOFT CUSTARD-----------------------------
2 c  Milk
3    Slightly beaten eggs
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water; cool.  When congealing has
started, fold in egg whites.  Serve with Soft Custard.  Put 1 3/4
cups milk in saucepan; cover and bring to boiling point; reduce heat.
Combine eggs, sugar, salt and remaining milk.  Add to scalded milk
stirring constantly. Cook, stirring constantly, until a coating forms
on a spoon. Remove from heat and cool.  Add vanilla. Serve in tall
glasses with whipped cream and coconut, or use a pudding sauce.
Yield: 2 1/4 cups. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   07-29-95
