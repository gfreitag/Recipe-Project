Title: PIZZA WITH MOZZARELLA CLAMS AND TOMATOES - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings;
2 tb Olive oil
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1/4 ts Dried crushed red pepper
1 c  Packed grated mozzarella
-cheese (about 4 oz)
3 tb Chopped fresh  parsley
2 tb Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese
1 tb Chopped fresh thyme
-or 1 t dried
1    10-oz tube refrigerated All
-Ready Pizza Crust
Nonstick vegetable oil spray
2 lg Plum tomatoes, thinly
-sliced
1 cn (6 1/2-oz) chopped clams,
-drained
Prepare barbecue (medium heat). Mix oil, garlic and crushed red
pepper in small bowl. Let stand 10 minutes. Combine mozzarella,
parsley, Parmesan cheese and thyme in medium bowl. Unroll pizza dough
on heavy large baking sheet. Stretch dough into 12-inch square. Cut
dough into 4 squares.

Spray grill rack with vegetable oil spray. Place dough squares on
grill rack and grill until tops are dry and set and bottoms are light
brown, about 3 minutes. Using spatula, transfer dough, grilled side
up, to baking sheet. Brush top of dough with oil mixture. Cover with
tomato slices and sprinkle with clams and cheese mixture. Return
pizzas to grill, tent with aluminum foil and cook until cheeses melts
and bottoms of pizza squares are crisp, about 3 minutes. Transfer to
plates and serve.

Bon Appetit/August/94 Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
