Title: Fruit Salad for a Crowd
Categories: Fruit, Salads
Yield: 100 servings
MMMMM-------------------------SERVES 100------------------------------
48 ea Oranges, sectioned and then
-halve the sections
4 ea 15oz cans pineapple chunks
2 lb Grapes
1/2 lb Candied cherries, halved
2 c  Walnut pieces
12 ea Bananas, cubed
2 qt Mayonnaise or salad dressing
12 ea Heads lettuce
In large bowl, combine all the fruit and then toss with the dressing.
Chill thoroughly.  If serving individually, arrange lettuce leaves in
bowls, then spoon salad on top of lettuce. Origin: Homestyle,
Canadian Classics, Ladies of the Royal Purple. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, July/95.
