Title: Main Dish Fruit
Categories: Vegetarian, Kids
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Brown rice, short grain
-(about 4 cups cooked rice)
4 c  Water
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cinnamon, ground
1/4 c  Pine nuts, toasted
1/4 c  Raisins
8 oz Fresh apricots; cut into
-eighths
Place rice, water, and salt in a large saucepan over medium-high
heat. Cover, bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium.  Cook until
tender, about 30 minutes.
Transfer rice to a large bowl and sprinkle with cinnamon.  Add
remaining ingredients to rice, toss, and season to taste.  Serve
immediately.
Per serving:  210 cal, 4 g fat, 18% cal from fat, 6 g protein, 39 g
carbohydrates, 146 mg sodium, 0 mg cholesterol. Source: Sesame Street
