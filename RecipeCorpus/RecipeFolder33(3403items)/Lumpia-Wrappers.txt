Title: Lumpia Wrappers
Categories: Appetizers, Filipino
Yield: 10 servings
2 lg Eggs
2 tb Oil or softened lard
1 c  Cornstarch
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Salt
2 c  Water
Beat eggs with oil or lard. Add cornstarch, flour, salt and water;
beat until smooth. Cook in an omelette or nonstick pan into thin
crepes.
