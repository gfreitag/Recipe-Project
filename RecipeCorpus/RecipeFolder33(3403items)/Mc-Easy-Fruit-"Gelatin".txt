Title: Mc Easy Fruit "Gelatin"
Categories: Fruit, Desserts, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
6 oz Frozen apple juice conc.
3/4 c  Unsweetened applesauce
1/3 c  Cornstarch
1 ts Vanilla extract
Mix the juice, applesauce, and cornstarch in a small pan.  Cook over
medium heat until thickened and clear, stirring constantly.  Add the
vanilla. Stir and pour into an 8 inch square nonstick pan.  Chill.
When cool, cut into squares.
