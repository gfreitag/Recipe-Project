Title: Strawberry Preserve Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
Makes about 2 cups
MMMMM--------------------PREP TIME: 5 MINUTES-------------------------
MMMMM-------------------COOK TIME: 10 MINUTES------------------------
4 c  Fresh hulled and quartered
-or frozen unsweetened
-strawberries
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1 tb Cornstarch
In saucepan, combine strawberries, sugar and cornstarch.  Cook,
stirring constantly over medium heat until juices run.  Continue to
cook, stirring often, for about 5 minutes or until nice and thick.
Let cool if not serving immediately.  Store in tightly covered jar in
fridge for up to 2 weeks. Origin: Homemaker's May/95. Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, July/95.
