Title: Easy Egg Braid
Categories: Breakfast, Breadmaker
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
4 ts Butter
2 tb Honey
1    Egg
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Water
Honey Glaze
To complete Easy Egg Braid in bread machine:  Select "Normal" program.
Press "Start".

To bake Easy Egg Braid in conventional oven: Place all ingredients
into pan. Select "Dough" program. Press "Start." Bread machine will
beep at end of program. Press "Stop/Reset;" remove dough. Divide into
3 parts. Roll each third into 12-inch ropes.  Pinch top ends
together.  On greased cookie sheet, loosely braid. Tuck ends under to
seal. Cover; let rise in warm place until almost doubled, about 30
minutes. Bake at 400 F 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown. Remove
from cookie sheet. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   08-14-95
