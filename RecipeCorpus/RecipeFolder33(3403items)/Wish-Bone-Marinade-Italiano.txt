Title: Wish-Bone Marinade Italiano
Categories: Poultry, Salsa/sauce, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Wish Bone Italian Dressing
2 1/2    To 3 lbs. chicken pieces
From : Sallie Krebs, Sat 06 Aug 95 04:00, Area: COOKING

In shallow dish, pour dressing over chicken. Cover and marinate in
refrigerator, turning occasionally 3 to 24 hrs. Remove chicken
reserving marinade. Grill or broil turning and basting frequently
with reserved marinade until done Do not brush with marinade last 5
minutes of cooking.

4 servings

Source: Midwest Living, August 1995
