Title: White Cookies^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter or lard
1 c  Sour cream
1 ts Baking soda dissolved in
Water
2    Eggs, beaten with nutmeg or
Lemon
3 1/2 c  To 4 flour
Cream together the sugar and lard or butter. Blend in well the sour
cream and soda in water. Beat in eggs; add flour to make a soft
dough. Roll out quite thin and bake at 350 degrees for about 8
minutes. This is one of Hana's favorite cookie recipes.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
