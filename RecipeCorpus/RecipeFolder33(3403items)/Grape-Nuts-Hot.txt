Title: Grape-Nuts Hot
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 serving
1/2 c  GRAPE-NUTS Cereal
1/3 c  Skim milk
Place GRAPE-NUTS cereal in microwavable bowl. Add skim milk.
Microwave on High to taste: 30 seconds for a BIG crunch OR 60 seconds
for a little crunch. Add fresh fruit or honey, if desired.
