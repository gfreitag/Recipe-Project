Title: MOM'S FRENCH DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Sugar
2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard (Colemans
-good)
1/2 ts Paprika
1 cn Tomato soup (10 1/2 oz),
-undiluted
1 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Tarragon vinegar
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 sm Onion, grated
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
Pour all ingredients into a quart-size plastic container; shake well
to blend.  Refrigerate overnight before serving to allow flavors to
blend. Serve over a fresh green salad. Will keep for weeks in the
refrigerator. Yields:  Approximately 1 quart From: Mom (Dulcinea
Ertz) Posted by: Debbie Carlson
