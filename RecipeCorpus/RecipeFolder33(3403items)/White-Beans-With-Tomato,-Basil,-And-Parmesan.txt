Title: White Beans With Tomato, Basil, And Parmesan
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Olive oil
3    Cl Garlic
3 c  Great northern beans
1 1/2 c  Tomato; chopped
1/4 c  Basil
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1 tb Lemon juice
Recipe by: Reader's Digest Live Longer Cookbook Preparation Time:
0:15 In a skillet, heat oil over low heat.  Add garlic and cook,
stirring occasionally, for 4 minutes or until lightly colored and
fragrant.  Stir in the beans, tomato, and basil and cook for 7
minutes or until heated through and the tomato has released its
juices.  Stir in cheese and lemon juice.
