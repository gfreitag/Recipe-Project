Title: Nectarine Smoothie
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
2    Soft, ripe nectarines
1 c  Nonfat plain yogurt
1 c  Low-fat milk
Cut the nectarines into chunks and discard the pits. Put in blender
with yogurt and milk and puree until smooth.
