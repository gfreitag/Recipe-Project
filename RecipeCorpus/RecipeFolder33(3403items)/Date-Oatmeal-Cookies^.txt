Title: Date Oatmeal Cookies^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/2 c  Oatmeal
2 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 c  Butter, or more
1 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Baking soda
Water
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 lb Dates
1 c  Sugar
Chopped nuts
1 c  Water
Beat together the butter and sugar. Add oatmeal, flour, baking soda
and enough water so as to roll nicely. Mix with hands; divide dough
in half. Roll out first the bottom crust. Add filling and cover with
top crust. Bake in moderate oven. Cook dates, sugar, nuts and water
in a saucepan. Cool before pouring onto dough. From Mollie.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
