Title: Creamy Lemon Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Sour cream
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
3 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Sugar
Combine in food processor until smooth and creamy.  Chill.

Can be used as a dressing for Spinach salad.
