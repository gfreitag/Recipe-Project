Title: Singapore Sling
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Gin
2 oz Sour mix, bottled
1/2 oz Grenadine
1 ds Cherry brandy
4 oz Club soda
Cracked ice
Ice cubes
Fill a mixing glass with ice. Add gin, sour mix, and grenadine. Shake
and strain into a Collins glass filled with ice cubes. Fill with club
soda and top with cherry brandy.
