Title: Margarita For The Masses
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Cocktails
Yield: 1 serving
1 cn Frozen limeade
Tequila
Triple Sec
Empty still frozen can of limeade into blender. Use empty can as
measure and pour 1 can of Tequila and 1 can of Triple Sec. Fill
blender with ice and blend until slush-like.
