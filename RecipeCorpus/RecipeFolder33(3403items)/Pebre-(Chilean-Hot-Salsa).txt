Title: Pebre (Chilean Hot Salsa)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetables, So. america
Yield: 1 -1/2 cups
2 tb Olive oil
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1/3 c  Water
4 ea Fresh Aji chiles; seeds and
-stems removed, minced
OR
1 ea Habanero; as above
2 cl Garlic; minced
1/2 c  Onion; minced
1/2 c  Cilantro; minced
1 ts Fresh oregano; minced
Salt to taste
Combine the olive oil, vinegar, and water in a bowl and beat with a
whisk.
Add the remaining ingredients. Mix well, and let stand for 2 hours
to blend the flavors.

The Authors' heat scale: HOT
