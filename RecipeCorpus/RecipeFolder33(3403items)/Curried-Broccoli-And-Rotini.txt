Title: Curried Broccoli And Rotini
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/3 c  Rotini or gemelli pasta
1 tb Olive oil
1 ts Curry powder
3 c  Broccoli flowerets
1/2 c  Bell pepper; chopped
1/2 c  Fat-free plain yogurt
1/3 c  Chicken broth
4 ts Unbleached flour
1 ts Mustard; prepared
Recipe by: Quick, Healthy, And Delicious Cooking Preparation Time:
0:15 Cook pasta according to package directions.  Meanwhile, in a
skillet heat oil.  Add curry powder and stir fry over medium high
heat for 1 minute. Add broccoli and peppers; stir fry for 3 to 4
minutes or until crisp-tender. For sauce in a saucepan, stir together
yogurt, chicken broth, flour, mustard, and salt.  Stir into broccoli
mixture.  Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly.  Cook and stir
for 1 minute more.  Add pasta to broccoli mixture; toss to mix. Heat
through.
