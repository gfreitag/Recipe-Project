Title: Tangerine Tapioca^
Categories: Desserts, Low cal/fat, Healthy
Yield: 4 servings
Cooking Light 10/94
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
2 1/2 ts Grated orange rind
2 1/2 c  (2%) lowfat milk
1/3 c  Sugar
3 tb Quick cooking tapioca,
Uncooked
1    Egg, lightly beaten
1/2 c  Tangerine juice
6    Fresh orange segments,
Optional
combine orange rind and milk in a 2-qt glass measure. Microwave at
high 5 minutes or until milk is 180 degrees and tiny bubbles form
around edge (do not boil.) Add sugar, tapioca and egg to milk
mixture; stir well. Microwave at high 5 minutes or until slightly
thickened. Let stand 5 minutes; stir in juice.

Place plastic wrap on surface and chill. Garnish with orange
segments, if desired. 4 (3/4 cup) servings.

Per serving: 197 calories, 6.8 g protein, 4.2 g fat (19%), 33.5 g
carbohydrate, 0 g fiber, 65 mg cholesterol, .4 mg iron, 201 mg
calcium. Submitted By CAROLYN SHAW   On   08-13-95
