Title: Choco-Sandwiches
Categories: Kids, Snacks
Yield: 15 servings
1    Recipe
1 c  Chocolate frosting
Rice Krispies* treats
Prepare original rice krispies treats recipe.  Press mixture into 15
x 10 jellyroll pan coated with Pam.  Cool completely, about 30
minutes. Cut down the center into 2 halves.  Spread 1 cup chocolate
frosting on one half and place second half on top to form two layers.
Chill about 30 minutes. Cut into 2 x 2" squares.
