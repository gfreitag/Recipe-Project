Title: Creamy Caesar
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1    Garlic clove
1 lg Egg (or 2 smaller)
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Anchovie paste (optional)
1 ds Of pepper
1 c  Salad oil
Blend in a blender until
-smooth.  Chill about 2
-hours.
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
1 lg Head romaine lettuce
2 c  Croutons
8    Bacon slices, cooked and
-crumbled
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
DRESSING:

Tear lettuce in bite sized pieces in salad bowl.  Add croutons.  Add
dressing.  Toss to coat.

CROUTONS: 2 tbsp butter or margarine 1 garlic clove, sliced 4 5 white
bread slices, cubed

Saute garlic in butter for 2-3 minutes.  Remove garlic and discard.
Add bread cubes.  Fry until golden, stirring often.  Remove.  Cool.

Origin: Jean Pare's Salads Shared by: Sharon Stevens
