Title: Chicken Tetrazzini Dinner
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 2 servings
1 1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Margarine or butter
1 pk Lipton Noodles & Sauce -
-Chicken Flavor Tetrazzini
2 c  Cut-up cooked chicken or
-turkey
1/2 c  Thawed frozen or drained
-canned peas
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese (opt)
In medium saucepan, bring water, milk and margarine to the boiling
point. Stir in Noodles & Sauce - Chicken Flavor Tetrazzini and
continue boiling over medium heat, stirring occasionally, 6 minutes.
Stir in chicken, peas and cheese. Continue boiling 2 minutes or until
noodles are tender and chicken is heated through.
