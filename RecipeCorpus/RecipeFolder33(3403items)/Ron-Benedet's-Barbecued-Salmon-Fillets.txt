Title: Ron Benedet's Barbecued Salmon Fillets
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1    Salmon fillet, about 1 1/2
-lbs
2 tb Butter, melted
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Tomato ketchup
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
2 tb Onion, minced
1 tb Brown sugar
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Salt
Description:

Perfect for barbecue lovers with a barbecue.  The secret's in the
sauce. Great served with parslied potatoes and a fresh green salad.

Cooking Time: 10 min.
Preparation Time: 10 min.

Instructions:

Cut the salmon fillet into four serving pieces and place on a
greased, foil lined baking sheet.  Mix together the rest of the
ingredients, heat gently in a saucepan and pour over the salmon.
Place under a broiler, or on a well oiled barbecue, and cook on one
side only, allowing about 10 minutes per inch of thickness of fish.
