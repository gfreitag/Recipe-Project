Title: Baby Porcupine Appetizers
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Rice
1 lb Ground beef
1 tb Onions -- minced
2 tb Green bell peppers -- finely
Chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Celery salt
1    Clove garlic
2 c  Tomato juice
4    Cloves
1/2 ts Oregano
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
Combine rice, ground meat, onion, green pepper, salt, celery salt and
garlic; form into balls about 1-1/2 inches in diameter.

Heat tomato juice, cloves, oregano and Worcestershire sauce in
skillet. Add meatballs. Cover tightly and simmer for 50 minutes.
Remove cloves before serving.

: Jo Anne Merrill
