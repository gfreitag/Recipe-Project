Title: Bunny Salad
Categories: Salads, Kids
Yield: 1 servings
1    Chilled pear half
2    Raisins
1    Red cinnamon candy
2    Blanched almonds
-cottage cheese ball
Place crisp lettuce leaf on plate.  On top of it, place pear upside
down. Make bunny, using narrow end for face.  Two raisins for the
eyes, 1 red cinnamon candy for the nose and 2 blanched almonds for
the ears. The cottage cheese ball makes the tail.

From: Betty Crocker's Cook Book for Boys and Girls Shared By: Pat
