Title: Blender Pesto
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Fresh basil, torn
1/2 c  Olive oil
2 tb Pine nuts, lightly toasted
2    Garlic cloves, crushed
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese, freshly
-grated
2 tb Romano cheese, freshly
-grated
3 tb Butter, softened
Put basil, olive oil, pine nuts, garlic and salt in blender and mix
at high speed. Stop from time to time and scrape sides of blender cup
with rubber spatula. Pour mixture into a bowl and beat in cheeses.
Beat in softened butter. Before spooning pesto over pasta, add to it
a tablespoon or two of the hot water in which the pasta has boiled.
