Title: Super Spinach Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread, Tex/mexican
Yield: 3 cups
-JUDI M. PHELPS
10 oz Pkg. frozen chopped spinach;
-thawed and squeezed almost
-dry
8 oz Pkg. cream cheese; cubed
1/3 c  Pace picante sauce
1/4 c  Green onions with tops;
-chopped
1/4 ts Ground cumin
1 md Tomato; seeded and chopped
4    Crisply cooked bacon slices;
-crumbled
1/2 c  Monterey jack cheese OR
Cheddar cheese; shredded
-(about 2 oz)
Combine spinach, cream cheese, picante sauce, green onions and cumin
in saucepan; cook over low heat until cheese is melted and mixture is
hot. Stir in tomato and bacon; heat through.  Transfer to heated
shallow serving dish, chafing dish or fondue pot.  Sprinkle with
cheese. Serve with crackers or chips.  Makes about 3 cups. Source:
Favorite Recipes, Pace Picante Sauce, A Treasury of Tex-Mex Cooking.
