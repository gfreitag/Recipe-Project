Title: Bistro Steak Subs
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 lb Boneless beef sirloin steak
1    French bread
Lettuce leaves
1    Roasted red peppers
3/4 ts Garlic salt
1/2 c  Jalepeno pepper Monterey
-Jack cheese
Mild giardiniera, if desired
Place beef steak on grid over ash-covered medium coals; grill 16 to 21
minutes for medium-rare to medium doneness, turning once. Line bottom
of bread with lettuce; top evenly with pepper strips. Carve steak
into thin slices; season with garlic salt.  Arrange over peppers; top
with cheese. Close sandwich; cut into 4 to 6 portions.  Serve with
giardiniera, if desired.
