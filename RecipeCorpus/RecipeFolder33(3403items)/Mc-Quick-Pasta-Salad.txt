Title: Mc Quick Pasta Salad
Categories: Vegetarian, Salads
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Uncooked whole wheat or
-spinach elbow macaroni
-shells, or spirals
1 1/2 c  Chopped veggies; scallions
-gr or red peppers, broccoli
-celery, carrots, pimientos
-alone or in any combo
1/4 c  To 1/2c low sod oil free
-salad dressing
Cook the macaroni in boiling water just til tender, 8-10 mins. Mix
with the veggies and dressing, and toss to coat.
