Title: Baked Red Fish with Tomato Sauce
Categories: Cajun/creol, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
1    Red fish (4-5 lb.)
2    Onions, chopped
1 cn Tomato paste
1 lg Can tomatoes
1 bn Parsley
1/2 c  Bell pepper, chopped
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1/2 c  Cooking oil
1    Clove garlic, minced
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Salt and pepper fish and place in baking dish.

Sauce:

Brown flour in oil, add onions, celery, garlic, and bell peppers. Cook
until soft. Add tomatoes and paste, cook about 20 minutes. Add a
little water, if necessary, to prevent scorching.

Pour sauce over fish in dish. Bake until fish flakes, basting often.
Sprinkle with chopped parsley just prior to serving.
