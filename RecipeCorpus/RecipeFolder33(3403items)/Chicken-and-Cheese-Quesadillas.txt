Title: Chicken and Cheese Quesadillas
Categories: Tex/mexican, Poultry, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk Shredded Monterey Jack
-cheese (8 oz)
1 pk Shredded mozzarella cheese
-(8 oz)
6 lg Flour tortillas
1/4 lb Goat cheese
3/4 c  Chopped grilled chicken
-breast
1/2 c  Shredded fresh basil
Black Bean and Corn Salsa
Place Jack and mozzarella cheeses in plastic food bag or bowl with
top and shake to combine. Place 3 tortillas on microwave-proof
platter. Sprinkle cheese mixture on tortillas. Dot with goat cheese
and chicken pieces, sprinkle with basil on top with remaining
tortillas. Microwave 1-2 minutes on HIGH, turning once. Serve
immediately with Black Bean and Corn Salsa. Submitted By MICHAEL
