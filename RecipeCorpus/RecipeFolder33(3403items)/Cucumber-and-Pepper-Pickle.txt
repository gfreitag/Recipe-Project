Title: Cucumber and Pepper Pickle
Categories: Can/preserv, Pickles, Vegetables, Penndutch
Yield: 3 quarts +
3 qt Cucumbers; sliced
3 ea Green peppers; sliced
6 ea Medium onions; sliced
2 tb Salt
2 1/2 c  Sugar
1 qt Vinegar
1 tb Mustard seed
1/4 ts Alum
Pare cucumbers and slice in thin rings. Slice onions and peppers and
mix vegetables together. Add salt and let stand 3 hours. Drain liquid
off vegetables and add sugar, alum, vinegar, and mustard seed. Bring
to a boil and pack in jars. Seal.
