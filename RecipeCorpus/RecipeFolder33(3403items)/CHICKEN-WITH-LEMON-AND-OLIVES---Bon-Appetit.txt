Title: CHICKEN WITH LEMON AND OLIVES - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
2 tb Balsamic vinegar or red wine
-vinegar
1 tb Chopped fresh
1    Rosemary or 1 t dried
1 tb Chopped fresh thyme OR
1 ts Dried
6 lg Chicken breast halves
3 tb Olive oil
1    Onion, chopped
4    Garlic cloves, chopped
1/2 ts Turmeric
3 c  Chicken stock or canned
-low-salt broth
1 lg Lemon, thinly sliced
1/2 c  Drained capers
12    Greek green olives,* pitted
12    Kalamata olives,* pitted
Mixed Vegetable Couscous
(separate recipe)
Whisk first 5 ingredients in bowl. Place chicken in baking dish.
Season with salt and pepper. Pour marinade over; turn to coat. Cover
and refrigerate overnight.

Preheat oven to 350'F. Remove chicken from marinade; pat dry. Heat 3
T oil in heavy large Dutch oven over high heat. Working in batches,
add chicken, skin side down, and brown well, about 3 minutes. Using
tongs, transfer chicken to bowl. Add onion and garlic to Dutch oven.
Reduce heat to medium; cook until onion is tender, stirring often,
about 5 minutes. Add tunneric; stir 1 minute. Add stock, lemon,
capers and all olives. Bring to boil, scraping up any browned bits.
Add chicken; cover. Transfer to oven; bake until chicken is cooked
through, about 30 minutes.

Using tongs, place chicken in bowl. Cover to keep warm. Boil cooking
liquid in Dutch oven until reduced to 2 C, about 15 minutes. Mound
couscous on platter. Top with chicken. Spoon some of cooking liquid
over. Serve, passing remaining cooking liquid separately.

*Available at Greek and Italian markets and some supermarkets.
