Title: Nam Prik Gang Masaman (Masaman Curry Paste)
Categories: Thai
Yield: 1 servings
20    Chilies, red -- dried
And chopped
4 ts Coriander -- ground
2 ts Cumin -- ground
2 ts Nutmeg -- ground
1 ts Cardamom -- powdered
2 ts Cinnamon -- ground
1 ts Cloves -- ground
12    Garlic cloves -- crushed
2 lg Onion, red -- chopped
2 ts Pepper, black -- ground
2 ts Shrimp paste (Kapi)
4 ts Salt
8 tb Lemon grass -- chopped fine
2    Galangal (Khal) 1"
8    Bay leaves
Combine all ingredients except bay leaves in blender, grind into a
paste. Add the bay leaves directly when making curried chicken or
beef.

GEnie - R.WEISSGERBE "Delightful Thai Cooking" p. 19
