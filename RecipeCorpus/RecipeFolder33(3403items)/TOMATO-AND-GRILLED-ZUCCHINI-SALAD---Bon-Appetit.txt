Title: TOMATO AND GRILLED ZUCCHINI SALAD - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
2 lb Medium-large zucchini,
-trimmed, halved lengthwise
2 tb Plus 1 C olive oil
2 c  Fresh basil leaves
3    Garlic cloves, coarsely
-chopped
3 tb Balsamic  vinegar
1 tb Dijon mustard
2 1/2 lb Large tomatoes, sliced
Prepare barbecue (medium-high heat). Position grill rack about 6
inches above heat source. Brush zucchini on all sides with 2 T oil.
Grill until tender, turning occasionally, about 8 minutes. Transfer
to plate and cool.

Puree basil, garlic, vinegar and mustard in processor. With machine
running, add 1 C oil; process until dressing is thick. Season with
salt and pepper.

Place tomatoes on platter. Cut zucchini into 1 1/2-inch pieces. Mix
zucchini and 3/4 C dressing. Spoon onto platter. Drizzle remauung
dressing over tomatoes.
