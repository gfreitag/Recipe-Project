Title: Pickapeppa Hot Sauce (Guyana)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetables, So. america, Caribbean
Yield: 1 -1/2 cups
12 ea Dried "Bird Peppers"
-(chiltipins); seeds and
-stems removed, crushed,
OR substitute piquins or
-Thai chiles
1/2 ts Dried mustard
3 tb Soy sauce
3 tb Lime juice
1/4 c  Ketchup
1 ts Salt
2 ts Brown sugar
1 c  Dry sherry
Note: Not the commercial stuff from Jamaica.

Combine all ingredients in a bottle and refrigerate for at least a
week to blend all the flavors.  Shake at least once a day.

Authors' heat scale: Medium

_Hot and Spicy Latin Dishes_  from Chili Pepper Magazine Dave DeWitt,
Mary Jane Wilson, Melissa T. Stock Prima Publishing, 1995 ISBN
