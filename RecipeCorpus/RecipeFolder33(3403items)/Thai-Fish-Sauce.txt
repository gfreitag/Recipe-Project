Title: Thai Fish Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Thai, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 1 cup
1 cn Anchovies, drained (2 oz)
2    Garlic cloves, lightly
-crushed
1/2 ts Dark-brown sugar, packed
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/4 c  Water
Place anchovies, garlic, brown sugar, salt and water in small
saucepan. Simmer over medium-low heat 10 minutes without stirring.

Remove from heat, cool slightly, then strain through double layer of
cheesecloth. Store covered in refrigerator up to 2 weeks. Makes about
3/4 cup.
