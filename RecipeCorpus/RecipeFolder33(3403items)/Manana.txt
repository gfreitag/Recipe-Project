Title: Manana
Categories: Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
3    Whole tangerines --
-squeezed
2 oz Tequila
3/4 oz Triple Sec
1    Scoop crushed ice
3/4 oz Lime juice
1    Splash soda water
Squeeze the juice form the tangerines into a blender. then tequila,
triple sec and crushed ice. Blend until smooth. Strain into a tall
glass and pour in lime juice and soda or tonic til full. Stir slowly.
Toss in a cherry for garnish.
