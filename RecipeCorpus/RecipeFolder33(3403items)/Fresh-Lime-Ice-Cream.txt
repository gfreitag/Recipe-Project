Title: Fresh Lime Ice Cream
Categories: Ice cream, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb + 1 tsp grated lime zest
1/2 c  + 1 tbsp fresh lime juice
2 c  Sugar, preferable superfine
pn Salt
2 c  Heavy cream
2 c  Milk
Combine the zest, juice, sugar and salt.  Stir to mix well.
Gradually add the cream and then the milk.  Stir gently until sugar
dissolves. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably
overnight to allow the flavours to blend.  Stir, and freeze according
to ice cream Origin: Great Food Without Fuss. Shared by: Sharon
