Title: N.C. BBQ sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Vinegar
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Ketchup
2 1/2 tb Chili powder
1 tb Pepper
1 1/2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
Bring vinegar and water just to simmer. Add other ingredients and stir
well. Bottle and refrigerate. Shake well before using.
