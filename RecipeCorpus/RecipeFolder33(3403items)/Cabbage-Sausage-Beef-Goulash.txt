Title: Cabbage-Sausage-Beef Goulash
Categories: Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 10 servings
1 lb Pork sausage
1 lb Ground beef
1    Onion -- chopped
28 oz Chopped tomatoes
6 oz Tomato paste
2 tb Vinegar
1 tb Chili powder
1 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes
10 c  Shredded cabbage
* The spiciness of the pork depends upon your taste. Experiment with a
mixture of mild and hot.

In a large kettle, brown sausage, beef and onion; drain well. Add
remaining ingredients except cabbage, mix well. Stir in cabbage and
simmer 20-25 minutes or until cabbage is tender.

Serving Ideas:  Serve with French bread and a pot of pinto beans.

: Jo Anne Merrill
