Title: Peanut Butter Cups
Categories: Candies, Chocolate
Yield: 20 servings
1 c  Semisweet chocolate disks --
Melted
1    7 oz Hershey Bar -- melted
1/3 c  Peanut butter -- at room
Temperature
2 1/2 tb Powdered sugar -- sifted
Melt chocolate disks and Hershey bar in a double boiler.  In a small
mixing bowl, combine peanut butter and powder sugar until smooth.
Shape into balls and set aside .  With small paint brush, brush
melted chocolate onto the bottom and up sides of paper candy cups
till about 1/8 inch thick. Put a peanut butter ball in each chocolate
coated candy cup.  To seal peanut butter balls, spoon another layer
of melted chocolate over each ball. Chill or freeze until hardened.
