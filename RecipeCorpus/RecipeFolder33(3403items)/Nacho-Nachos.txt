Title: Nacho Nachos
Categories: Snacks, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn (16-oz.) OLD EL PASO refried
-beans
3/4 c  OLD EL PASO Thick 'n Chunky
-salsa or picante
8 oz (2 cups) Shredded Colby-
-Monterey Jack blend cheese,
-divided
x  OLD EL PASO pickled jalapeno
-slices, if desired
x  Tortilla chips
1.  Combine refried beans and salsa; mix well.

2.  Arrange tortilla chips in single layer on large, microwave-safe
platter.  Spread half of bean mixture on chips.   Top with 1 cup
cheese and desired amount of jalapeno slices.

3.  Microwave on HIGH for 1-1/2 to 3-1/2 minutes or until cheese is
melted, rotating plate a quarter turn halfway through cooking.
Repeat with remaining ingredients.
