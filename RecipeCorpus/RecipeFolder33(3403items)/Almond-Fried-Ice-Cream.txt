Title: Almond Fried Ice Cream
Categories: Chinese, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
8    Scoops vanilla ice cream
Oil for deep frying
1 c  Tangerine sauce
2 c  Sliced almonds
Scoop the ice cream in advance; return to freezer until extremely
hard.

Heat the cooking oil in a deep-fryer. Roll each ice-cream ball in the
tangerine sauce then in the sliced almonds, coating thoroughly.
Deep-fry for a few seconds only. Serve immediately.

Temperature(s): HOT Effort: EASY Time: 00:10 Source: PLUM BLOSSOM
Comments: STEMMONS FREEWAY NORTH, DALLAS Comments: WINE: WANG FU
