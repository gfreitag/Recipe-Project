Title: Thai Sardines Salad
Categories: Salads, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
Shimizu BWHT68A
1/4 c  Onions -- chopped
1 tb Lime juice -- leaves;
Chopped
1 cn Sardines packed in oil
1 tb Sliced chilies (or to
Taste)
1 tb Cilantro or peppermint
Salt to taste
Mix all the ingredients together well. Be careful not to break up the
sardines too finely. Let stand 10 mins before serving. Serve with
iceberg lettuce leaves or cooked rice.
