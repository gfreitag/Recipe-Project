Title: Lemon Spice Tea
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
2 c  Warm Water
1 tb Honey
2 ea Slices Lemon
2 ea Inches Stick Cinnamon
2 ea Tea Bags
Lemon Slices (Opt.)
Mix ingredients except tea bags. Bring to a boil and simmer a couple
of minutes.   Add tea bags and steep.
