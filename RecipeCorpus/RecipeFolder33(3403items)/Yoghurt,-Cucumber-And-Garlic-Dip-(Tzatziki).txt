Title: Yoghurt, Cucumber And Garlic Dip (Tzatziki)
Categories: Vegetables, Herbs/spice, Dips/spread, Greek
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Cucumber peeled lengthwise
-leaving every second strip
-unpeeled
2    (or 3) Garlic cloves, peeled
-and crushed with salt with
-the blade of a knife
1 lb Thick yoghurt
2 tb Oil
1 tb Vinegar or lemon juice
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Sprigs of mint or dill
Cut the cucumber into very small dice and combine with the garlic and
salt. Beat together the yoghurt, oil and vinegar and fold into the
cucumber. Spoon the dip into a bowl and garnish with mint or dill.
This can be served with fried eggplant and crusty bread.
