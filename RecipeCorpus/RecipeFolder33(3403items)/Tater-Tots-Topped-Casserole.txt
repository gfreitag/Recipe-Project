Title: Tater Tots Topped Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Potatoes
Yield: 5 servings
1/2 c  Frozen Ore-Ida chopped
-onions
1 lb Ground beef
Salt
Pepper
1 cn Cream of celery (or cream of
-mushroom) soup
4 c  Frozen Ore-Ida Tater Tots
Preheat oven to 400'F. Grease a shallow 2-quart baking dish with
non-stick spray.

In 10" skillet over medium-high heat, cook onions and ground beef
until meat loses red color. Spoon off excess fat. Season with salt
and pepper.

Spread meat mixture in baking dish; cover with undiluted soup and top
with singer layer of frozen Tater Tots.

Bake 45 minutes, or until hot and bubbly in center.
