Title: PENNE WITH BASIL-SEAFOOD SAUCE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Fish/seafoo, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 lb Bay scallops
10 oz Uncooked shrimp, peeled,
-deveined
4    Garlic cloves, minced
1 1/3 lb Tomatoes, seeded, chopped
1 c  Chopped fresh basil OR
1 tb Dried
3/4 c  Bottled clam juice
1/2 c  Whipping cream
1/2 lb Penne, freshly cooked
Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add scallops and
shrimp and saute until just cooked through, about 3 minutes. Using
slotted spoon, transfer seafood to bowl. Add garlic to same skillet;
saute 1 minute. Add tomatoes and half of basil; simmer until sauce
thickens, about 10 minutes. Increase heat to medium-high. Add clam
juice and cream and simmer until thickened to sauce consistency,
about 6 minutes. Add penne, seafood, remaining basil and toss. Season
with salt and pepper.
