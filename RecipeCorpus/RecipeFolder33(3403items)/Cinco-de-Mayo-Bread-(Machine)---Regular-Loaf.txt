Title: Cinco de Mayo Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
2/3 c  Water
2 c  White bread flour
2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Olive oil
2/3 c  Corn meal
1/3 c  Creamed corn
2 tb Green chilies, canned/diced
1 ts Jalapeno peppers,    "
1 ts Cilantro, dried
1 1/2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
2 ts Yeast, acfive dry
Source:  Electric Bread

Served with chili, this lightly textured loaf will convince you never
to back to plain corn bread again!

Success Hints
-------------

Drain canned chilies and jalapeno peppers well.  Be careful! Jalapeno
and chili juice can sting.

This recipe can be used with regular and rapid bake cycles.
