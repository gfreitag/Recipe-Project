Title: Lemon Coffee
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 2 glasses
2 c  Cold, strong black coffee
1 c  Lemon sherbet, slightly
-softened
1 tb Grated lemon rind
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Sugar
Maraschino cherries for
-garnish
Place all ingredients, except cherries, in a blender and blend at
medium speed for 2 minutes. Serve in frosted glass; garnish with a
cherry. Makes 2 tall glasses full.
