Title: Tarragon Salad Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads
Yield: 2 cups
1 c  Tomato sauce
1/2 c  Tarragon vinegar
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Paprika
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Salad oil
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Prepared mustard
1 sm Clove garlic; cut up
1 sm Onion; quartered
3 tb Horseradish
Blend all together in blender.  Keep covered in covered jar in
refrigerator.

Makes 2 1/2 cups.

from The Farmer's Daughter Cookbook by Kandy Norton Henely
