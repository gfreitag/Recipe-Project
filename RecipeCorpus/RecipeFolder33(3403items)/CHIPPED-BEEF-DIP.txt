Title: CHIPPED BEEF DIP
Categories: Meats, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 batch
1 1/2 pt Dairy sour cream
3 tb Parsley flakes
2 ts Beau Monde seasoning (your
-guess is as good as mine)
1 1/2 pt Mayonnaise
2 ts Dill weed
2 tb Minced onion
9 oz Shredded corned beef
Combine together all ingredients.  Cut off top of small rye bread
loaf, break inside into small pieces, fill with dip mixture.  Serve
top and pieces on side for dipping.  Will fill a small round twice or
a large one once.
