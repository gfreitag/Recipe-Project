Title: Akiko-san
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Cocktails
Yield: 1 serving
2 oz Sake
12 oz Bottle of Kirin Dry Beer
Drink two ounces of the beer. Refill with Sake. Close the bottle with
your thumb and turn it upside down for a second. Enjoy (and don't
forget the arigato).
