Title: Cranberry Apple Cobbler
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 c  Cranberries -- sliced
1 1/2 c  Apples -- grated
1 tb Orange peel -- grated
1/2 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Cloves
1 tb Unbleached flour
1/2 c  Unbleached flour
1 tb Margarine -- in bits
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Margarine
1/3 c  Fat-free plain yogurt
1/2 ts Granulated sugar
Preheat oven at 400.  Prepare a baking pan with cooking spray and
flour. In a mixing bowl, combine cranberries, apples, orange peel,
brown sugar, cinnamon, cloves, and 1 tablespoon flour.  Put mixture
in prepared baking pan.  Dot with 1 tablespoon margarine.  In another
bowl, combine remaining flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the
remaining margarine, combine until resembles crumbs, and stir in
yogurt (dough will be sticky).  Drop dough by spoonfuls onto
cranberry mixture, sprinkle with sugar. Bake for 30 minutes.
