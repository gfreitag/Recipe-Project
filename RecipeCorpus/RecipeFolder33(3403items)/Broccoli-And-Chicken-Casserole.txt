Title: Broccoli And Chicken Casserole
Categories: Vegetables, Poultry, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
4    Noodles
2 1/2 c  Chicken; chopped/cooked
1/2 c  Onions; chopped
1    10 oz pkg broccoli
1    10 oz can cream of mushroom
1/2 c  Skim milk; at room temperatu
1/2 c  Swiss cheese; shredded
1 ts Basil
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
Recipe by: Better Homes & Gardens New Dieter's Cookbook Preparation
Time: 0:15 Preheat oven at 350. Cook noodles according to package
directions. Drain. Prepare the bottom of a 2 1/2-quart casserole dish
lightly with cooking spray. In a mixing bowl, combine noodles,
chicken, onions, and broccoli. In another mixing bowl, combine soup,
milk, cheese, basil, salt, and pepper.Stir in noodle mixture. Pour
entire mixture into prepared dish. Bake,covered for 40 minutes.
