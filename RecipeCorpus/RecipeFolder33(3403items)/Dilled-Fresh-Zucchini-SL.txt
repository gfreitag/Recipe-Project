Title: Dilled Fresh Zucchini SL
Categories: Can/preserv, Pickles, Vegetables
Yield: 10 pints
6 lb Medium zucchini; thinly
-sliced
2 c  Thinly sliced celery
2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Dillseeds
2 c  Vinegar (5% acidity)
6 cl Garlic; halved
Combine vegetables in a large bowl; cover with ice cubes.  Cover
and let stand about 3 hours.  Drain well.
Combine sugar, dillseeds, and vinegar in a large Dutch oven; bring
to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.  Add vegetables, and
bring to a boil.
Pack into hot, sterilized jars, leaving 1/4-inch headspace.  Add 1
to 2 pieces of garlic per jar.  Cover at once with metal lids, and
screw bands tightly.  Process in boiling water bath for 15 minutes.
