Title: Sputnik (an old Russian favorite)
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Cocktails
Yield: 1 serving
Vodka (a bottle should be
-enough)
Sprite
1. Fill glass 50-50 with Vodka and soda.

2. Drink half of glass contents.

3. Refill with Vodka only.

4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until inside your head you hear BEEP BEEP
BEEP! Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-05-95
