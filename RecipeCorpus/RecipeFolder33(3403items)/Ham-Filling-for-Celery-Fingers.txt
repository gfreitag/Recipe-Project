Title: Ham Filling for Celery Fingers
Categories: Appetizers, Meats, Vegetables, Snacks
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 c  Minced ham
4 tb Sour cream
1 tb Chopped sweet pickle relish
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 ts Prepared mustard
ds Black pepper to taste
Several stalks freshly
-washed celery, cut into
-3" lengths
Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly.  Use to fill celery
fingers. Yields 2/3 cup filling.

NOTE:  This recipe only takes a couple of minutes to prepare and does
not heat up the kitchen.
