Title: Aunt Annie's Icebox Cookies^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Lard
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 1/4 c  Flour
1    Egg
1/4 ts Salt
Beat lard and sugars together till creamy. Add egg and beat well. Add
flour and salt, mix thoroughly. Chill in icebox several hours until
firm. Slice with a sharp knife and put on baking sheet and bake in a
moderate oven until light brown.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
