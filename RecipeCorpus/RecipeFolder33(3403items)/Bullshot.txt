Title: Bullshot
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Cocktails
Yield: 1 serving
1 oz Vodka
1 oz Clear BULJONG
dr Angostura Bitters
dr Squeezed lemon
dr Worcestershire sauce
Salt
Pepper
Shake well. Cool the drink well before serving. If there is no way of
cooling the drink, serve with ice. The best way is to make the drink
at least an hour in advance and serve it without ice.
