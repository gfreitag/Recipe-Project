Title: Multi-Grain and More Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1 1/4 c  Water
2 1/4 c  White bread flour
1/2 c  Wheat bread flour
2 tb Dry milk
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  7 Grain rolled cereal
3 tb Wheat germ
2 tb Oat bran
2 tb Cracked wheat
2 1/2 tb Honey
2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
3 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

Packed with good-for-you stuff!  All those grains give it a full, rich
flavor - and enough body to stick to your ribs.  If you want the
benefits of cereal and the timeliness of toast, this is the bread for
you.

Success Hints
-------------

Any unsweetened multi-grain cereal should work in this bread.

For accent & crunch, sprinkle a few oats on top after the final rise,
just before baking.

This recipe can be made with the regular, rapid or delayed time bake
cycles.
