Title: Shrimp Salad For A Crowd
Categories: Salads, Fish/seafoo, Desserts
Yield: 25 servings
2    3oz pkgs cherry jello
4 c  Tomato juice
1 tb Vinegar
2    7 5oz cans chopped or broken
Shrimp
1 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Grated carrots
Onion salt
Dissolve the jello with hot tomato juice.  Stir until no crystals
remain. Add veinger.  Cool.  When half set, add the shrimp, celery,
carrots and a little onion salt.  Refrigerate until completely set,
overnight.

Origin: Homestyle, Canadian Classics, Ladies of the Royal Purple.
