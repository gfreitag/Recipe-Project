Title: Tire Swing
Categories: Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
3 oz Amaretto
3 oz Orange juice, California
1 oz Rum -- Bacardi 151
1    Scoop Crushed ice
throw it all in a blender and set to blur. pour it in a brandy
snifter or mason jar and garnish with a cherry. find an old tire
swing by a lake or river kick back , relax and enjoy the view. That's
why god created blenders and scenery.
