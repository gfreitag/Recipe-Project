Title: Stuffed Apples Baked in Beer
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
6 md Cooking apples
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Great Western Beer
1. Core apples.  Remove 1-inch strip of peel around top.

2. Mix raisins, brown sugar and cinnamon.  Fill apple centres.

3. Place apples in a baking dish.  Pour Great Western Beer over.

4. Bake at 350 degrees F for 40 to 45 min, or until tender, basting
occasionally.

5. Cool to room temperature, basting while cooling.  Serve with it's
own sauce. If desired, add cream Makes 6 servings.
