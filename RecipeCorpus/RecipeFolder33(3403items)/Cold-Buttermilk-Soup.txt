Title: Cold Buttermilk Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Fish/seafoo, Vegetables, Herbs/spice
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 lb Shrimp, cooked and chopped
1 md Cucumber, finely diced
1 tb Minced fresh dill
1 tb Prepared mustard
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
1 qt Buttermilk
Mix together the shrimp, cucumber, seasonings and stir in the
buttermilk. Chill thoroughly.

Origin: Great Food without Fuss. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, July/95.
