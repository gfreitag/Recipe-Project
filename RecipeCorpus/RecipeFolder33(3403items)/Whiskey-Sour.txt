Title: Whiskey Sour
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1    Lemon -- Juiced
1/2 ts Powdered sugar
2 oz Blended whiskey
Cracked ice
Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add lemon juice, whiskey, and
sugar. Shake and strain into a sour glass filled with ice cubes.
Garnish with a slice of orange and a cherry.
