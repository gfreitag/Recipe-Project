Title: Grilled Turkey w/Ancho-Cashew Mole
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
3    Turkey tenderloins (1/2 lb)
1/4 c  Juice, lime
1/8 ts Cumin, ground
1/8 ts ;pepper
1/4 ts ;salt
Vegetable cooking spray
ANCHO_CASHEW MOLE SAUCE
-(recipe needed)
combine the first 4 ingredients in a shallow dish; cover and marinate
in refrigerator 1 hour, turning occasionally.  Remove turkey from
marinade; discard marinade.  Sprinkle salt over turkey; set aside.

Prepare grill.  Place turkey on grill rack coated with cooking spray,
and grill 10 minutes on each side or until turkey is done.  Cut
turkey into 1/4" thick slices.  Serve with Ancho-Cashew Mole Sauce
and shour cream. Garnish with cilantro sprigs, if desired.

Serving size:  3 ounces turkey, 1/4 cup sauce, and 2 teaspoons sour
cream.

Calories 222 (28% from fat); Protein 28.7g; Fat 6.8g (sat 1.6g, mono
2g, poly 2.1g); Carb 10.7g; Fiber 2g; Chol 59mg; Iron 2.5mg; Sodium
202mg; Calc 56mg
