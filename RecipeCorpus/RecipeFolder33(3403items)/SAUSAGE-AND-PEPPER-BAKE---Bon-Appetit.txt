Title: SAUSAGE AND PEPPER BAKE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2    Red bell peppers, thinly
-sliced
2    Green bell peppers, thinly
-sliced
2 md Onions, thinly sliced
4 lg Garlic cloves, chopped
2 ts Dried oregano
1/4 ts Dried crushed red pepper
2 tb Olive oil
2 lb Sweet Italian sausages
Preheat oven to 350'F. Combine first 6 ingredients in 13x9x2-inch
glass baking dish. Pour oil over and stir to coat. Season with salt
and pepper. Brown sausages in heavy large nonstick skillet over high
heat, turning often, about 8 minutes. Arrange on pepper mixture.

Bake until sausages are cooked through and peppers are tender,
stirring occasionally, about 45 minutes.
