Title: Home Made Chicken Gravy Base And Recipe
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 3 servings
MMMMM---------------------CHICKEN GRAVY MIX--------------------------
1 1/3 c  Dry milk powder
3/4 c  All purpose flour
3 tb Chicken bouillon granules
1/4 ts Ground sage
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/8 ts Yellow food color
MMMMM---------------------------GRAVY--------------------------------
1 c  Cold water
1/2 c  Chicken gravy mix
MIX Combine milk powder, flour, chicken bouillon and sage, cut in
margarine and food coloring until pieces resemble cornmeal. Store in
tightly covered container in the refrigerator up to 2 months. Makes
about 3 cups mix.

GRAVY In saucepan blend cold water into gravy base mix. Cook and stir
until mixture is thickened and bubbly. Cook 1 minute more. Makes 1
cup gravy.
