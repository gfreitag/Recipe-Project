Title: APPLE SOY MARINADE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/2 c  Apple juice
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Oil
3 ea Bay leaf
2 ea Garlic clove, minced
1 ea Onion, large, chopped
1/2 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Pepper
Combine, add meat and chill 12 to 24 hours before cooking. Makes
enough for 4-5 pound roast or brisket. Source:  GOURMET.ZIP file -
recipes from Donna Endreson
