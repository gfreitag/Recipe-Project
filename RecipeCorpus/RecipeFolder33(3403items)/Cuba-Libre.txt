Title: Cuba Libre
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 oz Light rum
1 ts Lime juice
6 oz Cola -- or to fill
1/4    Lime
Pour lime juice and rum in highball glass filled with ice cubes. Fill
glass with cola and stir. Garnish with lime wedge.
