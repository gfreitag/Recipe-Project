Title: Classic Cocktail Popcorn
Categories: Side dish, Snacks
Yield: 1 servings
Classic Cocktail Popcorn
========================
[Cooking time: 10 minutes]
-[Serves: 6]
2 packages Classic Butter Kentucky Poppers Microwave Popcorn, popped
according to directions on the package

1/4 cup butter 1/4 cup corn oil 2 large cloves of garlic, finely
chopped 1 cup green onions, finely chopped

In a small, heavy skillet melt butter and oil over medium-high heat.
Add garlic and onion and saute until onion is soft. Toss with popped
corn and add salt and pepper to taste.
