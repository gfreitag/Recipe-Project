Title: Salty Dog
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 oz Vodka
5 oz Grapefruit juice -- or to
Fill
Margarita salt
Ice cubes
Rim a highball glass with margarita salt or other coarse-ground salt.
Fill the glass with ice cubes. Add vodka and grapefruit juice and
stir.
