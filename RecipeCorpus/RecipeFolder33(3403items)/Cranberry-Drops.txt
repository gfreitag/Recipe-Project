Title: Cranberry Drops
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 66 servings
1 c  Granulated sugar
3/4 c  Brown sugar -- packed
3 c  Unbleached flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Margarine -- softened
1/4 c  Skim milk -- at room
Temperature
2 tb Orange juice
1    Egg white -- whipped
2 1/2 c  Cranberries -- chopped
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a baking sheet with cooking spray.  In a
mixing bowl, combine sugars, flour, salt, and baking soda.  In another
mixing bowl, combine margarine, milk, orange juice, egg white, and
cranberries.  Mix dry ingredients with wet ingredients just until
moistened.  Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls about 2" apart on a prepared
baking sheet.  Push down slightly with a spoon.  Bake for 10 to 15
minutes.
