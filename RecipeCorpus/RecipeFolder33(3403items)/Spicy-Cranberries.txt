Title: Spicy Cranberries
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ea One Inch Sticks Of Cinnamon
1 c  Fresh Cranberries
Combine 1/4 cup of water and the sugar in a small saucepan.  Add the
cinnamon.  Bring to a boil, stirring, and boil until the sugar
dissolves. Add the cranberries and continue to boil until the
cranberries pop, about 2 minutes.  Cool and remove the cinnamon
sticks. Serve in hollowed out lemon cups made by scooping out the
lemon segments in a lemon half. Submitted By RICH HARPER   On
08-06-95
