Title: SAUTEED KALE WITH SESAME SEEDS - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 c  Water
3    Bunches kale (about 2 lb),
-stems trimmed, leaves
-chopped
2 tb Vegetable oil
3    Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 ts Dried crushed red pepper
1 1/2 tb Oriental sesame oil
2 tb Sesame seeds, toasted
Bring 1 1/2 C water to boil in heavy large Dutch oven over high heat.
Add kale, cover and boil until almost tender, stirring occasionally,
about 4 minutes. Uncover; cook until liquid evaporutes, about 3
minutes. (Can be made 4 hours ahead. Cover, Let stand at room
temperature.)

Heat vegetable oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add
garlic and dried red pepper; saute 1 minute. Add kale; saute until
heated through, about 3 minutes. Transfer to bowl. Drizzle with
sesame oil and toss to coat, Season vath salt and pepper. Top with
sesame seeds.

Bon Appetit/October/94  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
