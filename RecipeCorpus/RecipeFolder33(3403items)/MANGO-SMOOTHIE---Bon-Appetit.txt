Title: MANGO SMOOTHIE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
1    Ripe mango, peeled, pitted,
-chopped (about 1 1/4 C)
3/4 c  Chilled nonfat milk
1/4 c  Vanilla nonfat yogurt
3/4 ts Vanilla extract
3    Ice cubes
Pinch of salt
Fresh mint sprigs
Combine all ingredients except mint in blender. Blend until mixture is
smooth and creamy. Pour into glasses. Garnish with mint sprigs and
serve.
