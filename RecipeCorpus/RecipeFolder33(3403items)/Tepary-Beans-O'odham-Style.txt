Title: Tepary Beans O'odham Style
Categories: Side dish, Native amer
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  White or brown teparies
8 c  Water
1 c  Fresh green peeled chilies
-OR
2 c  Enchilada sauce
1/2 ts Chiltepin pepper to red
-chili powder
1/4 ts Salt
Sort and wash beans well, then soak overnight. Bring to a boil in a
large pot, then drain off the water. Add fresh water to cover beans,
then add 2" more of water to the pot. Cover and simmer for 4 hours
(or cook in electric slow cooker crock-pot on low for 6-8 hours). In
the final half hour of cooking, add salt, chiles or sauce.
