Title: AVOCADO "BUTTER" - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Dips/spread, Vegetables
Yield: 1 /2 cup
1    Avocado, peeled, pitted,
-chopped
3 1/2 ts Fresh time juice
Puree avocado and juice in processor until smooth. Season to taste
with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
