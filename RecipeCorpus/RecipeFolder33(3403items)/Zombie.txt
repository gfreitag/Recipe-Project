Title: Zombie
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Light rum
1 oz Dark rum
1/2 oz Grenadine
1/2 oz Triple sec
1 oz Apricot brandy
2 oz Orange juice
2 oz Sour mix, bottled
1    Splash Rose's Lime Juice
1 ds 151-proof rum
1    Lemon slice
Cracked ice
Ice Cubes
Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add light and dark rums,
grenadine, triple sec, apricot brandy, orange juice, and sour mix.
Shake well and strain into a Collins or highball glass filled with
ice cubes. Top with a dash of 151-proof rum and garnish with a lemon
slice.
