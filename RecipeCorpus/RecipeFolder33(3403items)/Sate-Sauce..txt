Title: Sate Sauce.
Categories: Thai, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
47    MARY WITT
4 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Chili powder
1/2 c  Onion -- (minced)
2 ts Coriander
2 ts Garlic -- (minced)
1/2 ts Cumin
1 c  Peanut butter
2 c  Chicken stock
3/4 c  Honey
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
Cayenne -- (pinch)
1 ts Chili oil
saute onion, garlic, chili powder, coriander & cumin in oil. Stir in
balance of ingredients and bring to a boil. Simmer until sauce is
creamy. Cool and Hold. Enjoy! Mary in K.C.
