Title: Crockpot Spiced Rum Cider
Categories: Beverages, Crockpot
Yield: 10 servings
4    Cinnamon sticks -- broken
1 ts Allspice -- whole
1 ts Whole cloves
7 c  Apple juice
1/3 c  Packed brown sugar
Margarine -- or butter
1 c  Rum
* Rum is optional; use more or less as desired.

Tie cinnamon, allspice, and cloves in spice bag or cheesecloth. In a
crockpot, combine spice bag, apple juice, rum and brown sugar. Cover;
cook on low-heat setting for 3 to 4 hours. Discard spice bag. Ladle
hot punch into cups; float about 1/2 teaspoon of butter on top of the
cider in each cup.

Yield: 10 servings of 6 ounces each.

: Jo Anne Merrill
