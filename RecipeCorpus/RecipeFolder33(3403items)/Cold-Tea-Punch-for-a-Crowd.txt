Title: Cold Tea Punch for a Crowd
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 20 servings
Serves 20
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Cold water
6 ts Bags
2 c  Boiling water
12 1/2 oz Can orange juice
12 oz Can unsweetened pineapple
-juice
6 1/2 oz Can lemon juice (or 12-1/2
-oz frozen)
2 qt Ginger ale
In small saucepan, combine the sugar and water.  Boil to a thin syrup.
Steap bags in boiling water.  Cool.  Add orange, pineapple and lemon
juices.  Pour into a 4 quart punch bowl to serve.  Just prior to
serving add ginger ale. Origin: Homestyle, Canadian Classics. Ladies
of the Royal Purple. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, July/95.
