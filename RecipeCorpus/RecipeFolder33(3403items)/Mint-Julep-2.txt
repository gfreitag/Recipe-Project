Title: Mint Julep 2
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
4 tb Golden Syrup -- --Or --
Sugar syrup
1 oz Bourbon
4    Fresh Mint Leaves
In a chilled glass (traditionally a sliver tumbler), place syrup and
mint. Muddle the mint with a mortar until it becomes a paste, then
add bourbon. Fill glass with crushed ice. Decorate with a fresh sprig
of mint. One to a customer will do the trick.
