Title: Nam Chim Priew Waan (Sweet And Sour Sauce)
Categories: Thai, Pasta/rice
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar -- granulated
1/4 c  Vinegar -- rice
1/2 ts Pepper, chili, dried --
Crushd
1 ts Salt
2 tb Ketchup
2 tb Cornstarch -- mix w/4T
Water
Boil water, then lower heat. Add sugar, vinegar, crushed chili, salt,
ketchup. Gradually add cornstarch paste, stirring until loosely
thickened.

GEnie - R.WEISSGERBE "Delightful Thai Cooking" p. 17
