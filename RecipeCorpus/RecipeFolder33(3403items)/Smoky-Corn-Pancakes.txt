Title: Smoky Corn Pancakes
Categories: Newspaper, Breads, Breakfast
Yield: 12 pancakes
1/2 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Cornmeal
1/2 c  Flour
1/3 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter; melted
2 c  Corn; cooked
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Salsa
1/4 ts Liquid smoke
In a large bowl, pour boiling water on the cornmeal and let stand for
10 minutes.

Add all the remaining ingredients and gently stir until well blended.
Make pancakes using 1/4 cup of batter for each.  Can be fried in oil
or cooked in a nonstick pan.
