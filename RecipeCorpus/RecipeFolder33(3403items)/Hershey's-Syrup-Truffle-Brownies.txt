Title: Hershey's Syrup Truffle Brownies
Categories: Snacks, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 36 brownies
1 c  Butter or margarine,
-softened (2 sticks)
1 3/4 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 1/2 c  HERSHEY'S Syrup (16 oz cn)
1 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  HERSHEY'S Cocoa
1/4 ts Baking soda
1 c  Coarsely chopped nuts (opt)
1/2 c  HERSHEY'S CandyBar Sprinkles
Heat oven to 350'F. Grease 13x9x2" baking pan. In large mixer bowl,
beat butter and sugar until well blended. Add eggs and vanilla; beat
well. Add syrup; beat well. Stir together flour, cocoa and baking
soda; gradually add to butter mixture, mixing well. Stir in nuts, if
desired. Spread batter into prepared pan. Bake 35 minutes; top with
HERSHEY'S Candy Bar Sprinkles. Bake additional 10-15 minutes or until
wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely in
pan on wire rack. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Cut into bars.
