Title: Spiced Rum Cider
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 10 servings
4    Cinnamon sticks; broken
1 ts Allspice; whole
1 ts Whole cloves
7 c  Apple juice
1/3 c  Packed brown sugar
Margarine; or butter
1 c  Rum (more or less); optional
Recipe by: Jo Anne Merrill Preparation Time: 4:00 Tie cinnamon,
allspice, and cloves in spice bag or cheesecloth.  In a crockpot,
combine spice bag, apple juice, rum and brown sugar.  Cover; cook on
low-heat setting for 3 to 4 hours.  Discard spice bag. Ladle hot
punch into cups; float about 1/2 teaspoon of butter on top of the
cider in each cup.
