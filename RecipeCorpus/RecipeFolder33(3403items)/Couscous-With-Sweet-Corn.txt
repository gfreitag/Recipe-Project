Title: Couscous With Sweet Corn
Categories: Vegetables, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Couscous
1 1/2 c  Corn
1 c  Tomatoes; stewed
2    Cl Garlic; minced
1 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Chives
1 tb Lemon juice
Recipe by: The Healing Foods Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:15

In a saucepan, bring broth to boil.  Add couscous.  Cover the pan,
remove from heat, and let stand for 5 minutes, or until liquid has
been absorbed.

Fluff with a fork. In a skillet over medium heat, combine corn,
tomatoes, garlic, chili powder, and cinnamon.  Cook until the liquid
from the tomatoes evaporates, about 5 minutes.  Stir in the chives
and lemon juice.

Add couscous and stir to combine.
