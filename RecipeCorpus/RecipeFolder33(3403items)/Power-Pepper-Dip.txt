Title: Power Pepper Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 serving
1 pt Sour cream
2 tb TRAPPEY'S sliced jalapeno
-peppers, minced
4 ts RED DEVIL Cayenne Pepper
-Sauce
4 tb Taco seasoning mix
Mix all ingredients and chill in sealed container two hours before
serving. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-12-95
