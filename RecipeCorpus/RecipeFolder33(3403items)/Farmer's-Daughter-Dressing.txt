Title: Farmer's Daughter Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads
Yield: 2 quarts
1/2 c  Finely chopped garlic cloves
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
2 tb Salt
1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Prepared mustard
1 ts White pepper
1 qt Salad oil
3 c  White vinegar
Beat ingredients except oil and vinegar to a thick paste,
approximately 10 minutes.  Add 1 cup oil until mixture emulsifies.
Add oil and vinegar alternately, beating vigorously.

Makes 2 quarts.

from The Farmer's Daughter Cookbook by Kandy Norton Henely
