Title: Banana French Toast
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
2    Egg whites; whipped
1/2 c  Skim milk; at room temp.
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Cloves
1/2 c  Bananas; mashed
4 sl Bread
Recipe by: Quick & Easy Diabetic Menus Preparation Time: 0:15

Prepare skillet with cooking spray and heat on medium until hot.  In a
shallow bowl, combine egg whites, milk, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and
bananas. Dip bread slices into the mixture and place on heated
skillet and cook until browned on one side, about 3 to 5 minutes.
Turn over and cook about 2 to 3 minutes more on other side.  Repeat
with remaining mixture.
