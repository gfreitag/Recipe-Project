Title: Mediterranean Pepper Salad
Categories: Salads, Herbs/spice, Fish/seafoo, Cheese/eggs, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
2    Green peppers, cut into
Strips
1/2 ts Crushed oregano
2    Red peppers, cut into
Strips
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Olive oil
ds Pepper
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1/2 lb Cooked shrimp
1    Garlic clove, minced
1/2 lb Feta cheese, cubed
Black olives
Combine together and chill in fridge until ready to serve.
