Title: Garrett's Smoked Tomato Dressing
Categories: Condiments, Dress/stuff, Tomatoes, Salads
Yield: 3 cups
1 lg Fresh tomato (8 oz.); peeled
2 1/2 tb Olive oil
3/4 ts Liquid Smoke
2 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Fresh garlic; finely diced
1 ts Capers; finely diced
-- drained
1 2/3 c  Mayonnaise
2    To 3 tb. fresh parsley
-- finely diced
Cut tomato in half horizontally.  Squeeze out all juice and discard.
Chop tomato into bite-size pieces.

In a bowl, combine olive oil, Liquid Smoke, sugar, salt and garlic.
Add chopped tomato.  Marinate 45 minutes.

After marinating, place tomato mixture in container of blender and
blend 1 to 2 minutes.  Add capers, mayonnaise and parsley.  Blend an
additional 3 to 5 minutes.  Refrigerate.

Colombo wrote: "I have had several requests for this recipe and, after
testing, can understand why.  Those who prefer a tangy dressing may
want to cut back on the sugar."

From Garrett's Restaurant, as requested by Janice Joslin/La Grange,
KY, who described this as being a "sweet version of Thousand Island
dressing," in Alice Colombo's 02/08/95 "Cook's Corner" column in "The
