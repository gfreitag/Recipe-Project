Title: Marguerites
Categories: Holiday, Muffins
Yield: 36 servings
1 c  Margarine -- softened
1    Egg white -- whipped
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Unbleached flour
3/4 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/8 ts Baking soda
1 c  Rolled oats
1 c  Nuts -- chopped
Preheat oven at 375.  Prepare baking sheet with cooking spray.  In a
mixing bowl, combine margarine, egg white, and vanilla.  In another
mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, baking soda,
oats, and nuts. Pour mixture into mini-muffin tins.  Bake for 15 to
20 minutes. While hot, tap muffin tin to get muffins out of pan.
Decorations that can be used are cinnamon candies and green icing.
