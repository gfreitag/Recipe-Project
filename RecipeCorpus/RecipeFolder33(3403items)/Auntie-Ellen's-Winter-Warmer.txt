Title: Auntie Ellen's Winter Warmer
Categories: Scot/irish, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 quart
1/2    Bottle good Scotch whiskey
1/2 lb Caster sugar
1/2 lb Honey
1/2 pt Sweet cider
1 sm Bottle rosehip syrup
Mix the sugar, cider, honey and rosehip syrup together in a large
bowl. Add the whisky and stir thoroughly for a few minutes. Bottle
and store in a cool place.  If rosehip syrup is unavailable then any
fruit syrup will suffice. The quantities can be reduced in proportion
if required. Scottish Heather Honey is preferable. A delicious sweet
and potent drink.
