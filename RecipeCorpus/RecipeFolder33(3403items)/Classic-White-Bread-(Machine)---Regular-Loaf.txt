Title: Classic White Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
3 3/8 c  Water
2 c  White bread flour
1 tb Dry milk
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
1 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
1 1/2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

This is a classic - plain white bread, but a world away from
store-bought!

Success Hints

with the regular, rapid or delayed time bake cycles.
