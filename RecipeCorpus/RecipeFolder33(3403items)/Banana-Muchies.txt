Title: Banana Muchies
Categories: Kids, Snacks
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Bananas -- mashed
1/2 c  Peanut butter
ds Cinnamon
12    Graham cracker squares
In a small bowl, mix bananas with peanut butter and a dash of
cinnamon. Chill the mixture.  Spoon 2 tbsp of the mixture onto a
graham cracker square.  Cover with another graham cracker square.
Wrap and freeze.
