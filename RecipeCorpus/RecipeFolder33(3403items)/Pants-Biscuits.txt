Title: Pants Biscuits
Categories: Breakfast, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 100 servings
8 oz Honey
10 1/2 oz Margarine or butter
3 3/4 lb Bread flour
1 1/4 oz Baking powder
1/2 oz Salt
1 1/4 lb Milk
1 lb Sugar
1 oz Cinnamon
Cream honey and margarine.  Combine bread flour, baking powder and
salt. Gradually add dry ingredients to creamed mixture alternately
with milk. Roll out dough to 1/4" thickness. Cut into "pants" shape.
Combine cinnamon and sugar.  Sprinkle over biscuits.  Bakes at 350
degrees F for 15 to 20 minutes.
