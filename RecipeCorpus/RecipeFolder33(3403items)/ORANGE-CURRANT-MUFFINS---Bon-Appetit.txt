Title: ORANGE-CURRANT MUFFINS - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Breads, Muffins
Yield: 12 muffins
2 c  All purpose flour
1/2 c  Sugar
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
Pinch of salt
1 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  (1 stick) unsalted butter,
-melted, cooled
2 lg Eggs, beaten to blend
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
1/4 ts Lemon extract
3/4 c  Dried currants
2 ts Grated orange peel
Position rack in center of oven and preheat to 400'F. Line twelve
1/3-C muffin cups with paper liners. Combine first 5 ingredients in
large bowl. Whisk orange juice, melted butter, eggs, lemon juice and
lemon extract in medium bowl to blend. Add to dry ingredients and mix
just until combined. Fold in currants and orange peel. Divide batter
equally among prepared muffin cups. Bake until golden and tester
inserted into center of muffins comes out clean, about 25 minutes.
Transfer muffin tin to rack and cool.
