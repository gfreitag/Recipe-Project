Title: Georgia Peach Cooler
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1    Peach
1 tb Honey
1 c  Milk -- chilled
1/2    Banana
Use a food processor or blender for this recipe. Fit steel blade in
processor work bowl. Combine all ingredients and process only 8-10
seconds. For blender preparation, cut peach into 3-4 pieces, add
other ingredients and whirl until smooth.

Serve immediately in tall glass.
