Title: APRICOT-GINGER BUTTER - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Dips/spread
Yield: 2 cups
2 c  Coarsely chopped dried
-apricots (about 1/2 lb)
1 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Water
1 1/2 tb Chopped peeled fresh ginger
2 tb Honey
2 tb (or more) sugar
Combine chopped dried apricots, orange juice, 1/2 C water and chopped
fresh ginger in heavy medium saucepan. Cover tightly and simmer until
liquid is reduced by half and apricots are very tender, stirring
frequently, about 20 minutes. Puree apricot mixture in processor
until smooth. Mix in 2 T honey and 2 T sugar. Season butter to taste
with more sugar, if desired. Transfer butter to bowl and cool
completely. (Butter can be made 1 week ahead. Cover tightly and
refrigerate.)
