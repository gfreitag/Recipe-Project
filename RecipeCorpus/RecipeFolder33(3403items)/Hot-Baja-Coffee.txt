Title: Hot Baja Coffee
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 10 servings
8 c  Hot Water
3 tb Instant Coffee Granules
1/2 c  Coffee Liqueur
1/4 c  Creme De Cacao
3/4 c  Whipped Cream
2 tb Semisweet Chocolate --
Grated
In slow-cooker, combine hot water, coffee, and liqueurs.  Cover and
heat on LOW 2-4 hours.  Ladle into mugs or heat-proof glasses.  Top
with whipped cream and grated chocolate.

Per serving: 98 calories, 3g fat.

Crockery Favorites, Mable Hoffman
