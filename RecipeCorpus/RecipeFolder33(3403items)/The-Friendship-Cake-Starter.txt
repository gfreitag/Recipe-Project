Title: The Friendship Cake Starter
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Milk
2/3 c  Flour
Mix in plastic or glass container with tight lid.  Cover, store at
room temperature for 17 days, stirring once a day.

After that, leave undisturbed for 1 day.  Stir again daily for 3
days. Add 1 cup each of sugar,milk,and flour, then stir the next 4
days. Add another cup of each sugar, milk and flour.  Mix well.

Give 1 cup each to 2 friends, use 1 cup to make a cake or use as base
for another batch of starter.
