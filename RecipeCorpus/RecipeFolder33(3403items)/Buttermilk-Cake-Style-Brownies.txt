Title: Buttermilk Cake-Style Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 18 servings
2 c  Flour
6 tb Buttermilk Powder
3/4 ts Baking Soda
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Margarine
3 tb Cocoa
1 1/2 c  Water
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
Sift flour, buttermilk powder, baking soda and salt together into a
mixing bowl.  Mix in sugar and set aside.

Combine margarine, cocoa and water in a saucepan and melt over low
heat. Stir together well.  Add cocoa mixture to dry ingredients and
mix just until moistened. DO NOT OVERBEAT. Add eggs, one at a time,
and mix until smooth.  Add vanilla.  Batter will be very runny. Pour
batter into greased 10x15-inch pan with sides.

Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.  Frost with Chocolate
Buttermilk Icing.
