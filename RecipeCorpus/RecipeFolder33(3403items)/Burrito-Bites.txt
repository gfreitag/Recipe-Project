Title: Burrito Bites
Categories: Tex/mexican, Appetizers
Yield: 40 appetizers
PAM No Stick Cooking Spray
1 cn RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and
-Green Chilies
1 c  Instant rice
1/3 c  Water
1    Green pepper, diced
2    Green onions, sliced
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese,
-divided (8 oz)
1 cn Ranch Style Refried Beans
-(16 oz)
10    Flour tortillas (6-7")
1 c  Salsa
Preheat oven to 350'F. Spray a 9x12" baking dish with PAM; set aside.
In a medium saucepan, combine RO*TEL, rice and water; heat to a boil.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer 1 minute. Remove from heat and let sit
5 minutes or until all liquid is absorbed. Stir in pepper, onions and
1 cup cheese. Spread about 3 tablespoons beans over each tortilla to
within 1/8" from edge. Layer rice mixture over beans; roll up. Place
seam side down in prepared baking dish; cover with foil. Bake in
preheated oven 25 minutes or until hot. Cut tortillas into 4 pieces
and place on platter. Top with salsa and cheese.

To serve as a delicious entree, prepare as directed but do not cut.
Top with salsa and cheese. Return to oven and bake 5 minutes or until
cheese melts.
