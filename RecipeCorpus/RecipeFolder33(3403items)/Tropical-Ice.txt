Title: Tropical Ice
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Mashed Bananas
2 cn Crushed Pineapple  --
-undrained (20 oz.)
10 oz Can Maraschino Cherries
-drained and chopped
4 c  Orange Juice
2 tb Lemon Juice
1/2 c  Sugar
Ginger Ale
White Wine
Combine first 6 ingredients in blender and freeze until firm.  To
serve punch, partially thaw fruit mixture.  Place in punch bowl and
break into chunks.  Add ginger ale and white wine to taste.
