Title: Pesto Sauce Deluxe
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Fresh basil
3    Garlic cloves -- minced
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1 c  Black walnuts -- chopped
1 c  Olive oil
Black pepper
* The cheese is optional but, if using, use a hard cheese and grate.
Use a food processor for easiest preparation.

Chop basil leaves in food processor or blender until finely chopped.
Mix the basil with chopped walnuts, minced garlic and cheese in the
processor or blender. While machine is running, slowly pour in a thin
stream of oil. Continue blending until desired consistency. Add black
pepper.
