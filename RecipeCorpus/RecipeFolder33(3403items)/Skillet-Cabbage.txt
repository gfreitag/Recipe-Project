Title: Skillet Cabbage
Categories: Vegetables, Native amer
Yield: 1 servings
6 sl Bacon, cut in julienne
Strips
1 md Cabbage, cut into 8 wedges
3/4 c  Water
6    Scallions, washed & sliced,
Including tops
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1. Brown the bacon in a large heavy skillet.  Add scallions and saute
until golden brown. 2. Place cabbage wedges in the skillet; add
water, salt and pepper; cover, and simmer for 35 minutes, turning
occasionally with a fork. Makes 6 servings.
