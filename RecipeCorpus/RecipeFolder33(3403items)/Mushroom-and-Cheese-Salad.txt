Title: Mushroom and Cheese Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Very crisp, fresh white
-mushrooms
Juice of 1/2 lemon
2/3 c  Swiss cheese, cut into
-strips
3 tb Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Remove stems and save for another recipe. Wipe caps clean and slice.
Add the lemon juice to mushrooms and toss to coat to prevent
blackening. Add strips of the swiss cheese and toss with olive oil,
salt and pepper. Origin: Great Food Without Fuss. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, July/95.
