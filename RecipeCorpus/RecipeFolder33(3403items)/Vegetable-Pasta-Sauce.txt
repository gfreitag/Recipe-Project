Title: Vegetable Pasta Sauce
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Olive oil
1 md Onion; chopped
1    Cl Garlic; chopped
1 c  Bell peppers; chopped
1 lb Turkey; ground
1 c  Celery; chopped
2 c  Zucchini; grated
1 c  Carrot; grated
2 cn Tomato sauce; (8 oz)
1 cn Tomato paste; (6 oz)
2 c  Water
1 ts Basil
1 ts Oregano
Recipe by: Quick & Easy Diabetic Menus Preparation Time: 0:40 In a
skillet, heat oil over medium heat.  Add onion, garlic and peppers
until tender. Add meat until no pink remains.  Then add celery,
zucchini, carrot, tomato sauce, tomato paste, water, basil and
oregano.  Simmer over medium heat for at least 1 hour to cook
vegetables.  Serve over cooked pasta.
