Title: Dijon Ham Glaze
Categories: Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
1 cn Crushed pineapple, drained
-(8 oz)
1/2 c  Firmly packed light brown
-sugar
1/4 c  GREY POUPON Dijon Mustard
5 lb Boneless ham
In small saucepan, heat pineapple, brown sugar and mustard to a boil.
Reduce heat; simmer 5 minutes.

Spoon 1/2 cup glaze over ham during last 30 minutes of baking. Heat
remaining glaze mixture; serve with sliced ham.
