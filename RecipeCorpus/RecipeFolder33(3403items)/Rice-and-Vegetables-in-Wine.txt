Title: Rice and Vegetables in Wine
Categories: Vegetarian, Vegetables, Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 unknown
2 tb Vegetable oil
1    Onion; chopped
1 md Zucchini; chopped
1 md Carrot; chopped
1 ea Celery stalk; chopped
1 c  Long grain rice
1 1/4 c  Vegetable stock or water
1 c  White wine
Heat the oil in a saucepan and saute the onion.  Add the rest of the
vegetables and stir them over a medium heat until browned.
Add the rice, vegetable stock and white wine, cover and cook for
about 15-20 minutes until all the liquid has been absorbed.
30 minutes to make.  Good source of vitamin A.
Source:  Linda McCartney's Home Cooking.
