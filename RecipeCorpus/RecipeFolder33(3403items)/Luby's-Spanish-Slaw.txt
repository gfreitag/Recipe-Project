Title: Luby's Spanish Slaw
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 10 servings
2 lb Cleaned shredded cabbage
1/4 c  Fresh parsley sprigs
1/3 c  Shredded carrots
1/2 c  Green bell pepper, sliced
1/4-inch strips
1/4 c  Scallions, sliced 1/8" thick
1 tb Diced red bell peppers or
Pimentos, drained
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Water
1/3 c  Dill pickle juice
1/4 c  White vinegar
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 ts Salt
1 ts Black pepper
Whisk together all dressing ingredients and pour over prepared
vegetables and mix well.  Refrigerate.  Serves 10.
