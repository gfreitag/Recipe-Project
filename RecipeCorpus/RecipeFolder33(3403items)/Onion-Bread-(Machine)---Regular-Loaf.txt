Title: Onion Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
3/4 c  Water
2 c  White bread flour
1 1/2 tb Dry milk
1 1/2 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
1/4 c  Onions, dried
1/2 tb Onion paste
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Poppy seeds
1 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

The aroma has a wonderful affect, and so does the taste.  Serve it
warm with split pea soup, and your senses will think they've died and
gone to heaven!
Success Hints
-------------

Use dried onion flakes, not fresh.

If you can't find onion paste in a tube, you may substitute 1/4 t
onion powder.  Don't use onion salt.

Use regular or rapid cycle.
