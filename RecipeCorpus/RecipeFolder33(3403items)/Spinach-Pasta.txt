Title: Spinach Pasta
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 20 servings
MMMMM----------------YIELD: 3/4 POUND FRESH PASTA---------------------
1 1/2 c  Unbleached all-purpose or
-semolina flour
1/2 lb Fresh spinach, wilted and
-squeezed dry, or 5 oz
-frozen
Spinach, defrosted and
-squeezed dry
1 lg Egg
1 tb Vegetable oil
Mix the flour with the spinach before adding the other ingredients to
the bowl of a food processor or electric mixer. Mix and knead the
dough, let it rest, roll it out, and cut the pasta to size desired.
