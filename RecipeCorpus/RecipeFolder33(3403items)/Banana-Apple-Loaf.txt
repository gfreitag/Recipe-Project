Title: Banana-Apple Loaf
Categories: Breads, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Margarine -- softened
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
2    Eggs
3 tb Sour cream
1/2 c  Mashed bananas -- (2
Bananas)
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
2    Apples -- chopped
1/2 c  Black walnuts -- chopped
* Use stick margarine, not the soft kind, or use butter.

1. Cream together margarine and sugars, beat in eggs thoroughly.
Blend in sour cream, banana and vanilla. 2. Combine flour, baking
powder, soda and cinnamon. Gradually add to butter mixture. 3. Gently
fold in chopped apples and nuts. Pour into greased and floured loaf
pan. 4. Bake about 1 hour at 375 degrees. Cool before slicing.

: Jo Anne Merrill
