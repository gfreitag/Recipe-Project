Title: Burk's Cafe Spicy Molasses Tomato Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
2 tb Minced garlic
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1    32-oz can tomato sauce
1/4 c  Beer
2 tb Molasses
2 tb Red wine vinegar
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Dijon mustard
1 1/2 ts Ground cumin
1 1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Ground allspice
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Ground coriander
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts White pepper
1. Puree the garlic and onions in a food processor.

2. Place in a heavy bottom saucepan with the tomato sauce, beer,
molasses, vinegar, lemon juice, mustard, cumin, ginger, allspice,
cayenne, coriander, nutmeg, paprika, black pepper and white pepper.

3. Simmer over low heat about 2 hours, stirring often to avoid
scorching. The sauce will cook down and darken in colour.

Data per 2-tablespoon serving: Calories.......28 Carbohydrates......6g
Monounsaturated fat.....0g Protein........0g Sodium..........338mg
Polyunsaturated fat.....0g Fat............0g Saturated fat......0g
Cholesterol............0mg

Burk's Cafe is a popular restaurant in the Ballard district of
Seattle. This thick, spicy sauce is served over several meat and
poultry dishes at Burk's, and also makes a great barbecue sauce.
