Title: Sweet Thrill Bars
Categories: Snacks, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 63 servings
20 lb Cake crumbs
5 lb Sugar
8 oz Milk powder
2 lb Shortening
1 1/2 oz Baking soda
5 lb Honey
1 lb Sugar
3 oz Cinnamon
1 oz Cloves
1 oz Nutmeg
2 lb Water
1 oz Baking powder
2 1/2 lb Cake flour
6 lb Raisins
1 lb Nuts
1 lb Chocolate
Blend first ten ingredients. Add water, scrape and mix smooth. Add
baking and flour and mix until smooth. Add remaining ingredients; if
mix is too heavy, add water. Pour into 7 16"x24" sheet pans. Bake at
360 degrees F for approximately 20 minutes. Cut into 2"x3" squares.
