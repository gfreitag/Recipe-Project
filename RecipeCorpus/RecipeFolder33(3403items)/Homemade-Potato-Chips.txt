Title: Homemade Potato Chips
Categories: Snacks, Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
2 md Potatoes; sliced 1/16" thick
1 tb Parmesan cheese; grated
1 ts Paprika
Recipe by: Reader's Digest Live Longer Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:15
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a baking sheet with cooking spray.
Arrange potato slices in 1 layer on baking sheet and bake, turning
occasionally, for 45 minutes or until golden brown.  In a small bowl,
combine cheese and paprika and sprinkle mixture over the potatoes.
Bake 5 minutes more, let cool, and transfer to a serving dish.
