Title: Hidden Valley Ranch Baked Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
3 lb Chicken, Cut Up
1 pk Dry Hidden Valley
Ranch Salad Dressing
Mix  (Original Style)
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees. Dredge the chicken pieces in the dry
salad dressing mix. Bake, skin side up, until brown and crispy (about
45 minutes). Serve hot. Alan Burghstaler From: Joel Ehrlich
