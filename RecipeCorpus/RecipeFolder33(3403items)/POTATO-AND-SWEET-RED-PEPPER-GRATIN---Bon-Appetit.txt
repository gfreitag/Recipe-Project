Title: POTATO AND SWEET RED PEPPER GRATIN - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
Nonstick vegetable oil spray
2 lb Russet potatoes, peeled,
-cut into 1/8-inch-thick
-slices
2 md Red bell peppers, seeded,
-cut into matchstick-size
-strips
2 ts Dried thyme
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
3 tb Olive oil
1/3 c  Dry white wine
1/3 c  Canned low-salt chicken
-broth
Position rack in center of oven and preheat to 375' F. Spray 8x8-inch
glass baking dish with vegetable oil spray. Arrange 1/3 of potatoes in
overlapping rows in prepared dish. Top with half of red bell peppers.
Combine dried thyme, salt and pepper in small bowl. Sprinkle 1/3 of
mix- ture over peppers and potatoes. Sprinkle 1/3 of cheese over
peppers and potatoes. Drizzle 1 T oil over. Repeat layering with 1/3
of potatoes, remaining red bell peppers, 1/3 of thyme mixture and 1/3
of cheese. Drizzle 1 T oil over. Top with remaining potatoes. Combine
wine and broth in bowl. Pour over all.

Top with remaining thyme mixture, cheese and 1 T oil. Bake until
potatoes are tender when pierced with fork and top is golden brown,
about 1 1/2 hours. Transfer to rack and cool 5 minutes. (Potato
gratin can be prepared 2 hours ahead. Cover tightly with foil and let
stand at room temperature. Rewarm in 350'F oven until heated through,
about 20 minutes.)
