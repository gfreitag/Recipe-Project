Title: Burnt Grapes
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
4 c  To 5 c seedless red or green
-grapes
2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Brown sugar
Wash and dry grapes.  Mix sour cream, sguar and vanilla together.
Pour cream mixture over grapes and stir gently.  Pour into a 9x13 pan
or serving dishes. In a saucepan, bring butter and brown sugar to a
boil. Immediately pour over grapes, Don't stir.  Chill for 3-4 hours.
Serves 6.

Can substitute fresh raspberries, blackberries, sliced peaches, for
the grapes.

Origin: Homestyle Canadian Classics, Ladies of the Royal Purple.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, July/95.
