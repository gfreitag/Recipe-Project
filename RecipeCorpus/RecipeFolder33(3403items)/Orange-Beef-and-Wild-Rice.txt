Title: Orange Beef and Wild Rice
Categories: Meats, Low cal/fat, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 lb Topround steak; 1/2" cubes
1    Onion; chopped
2    Carrots; sliced
1/2 lb Mushrooms; sliced
2    Cloves garlic; finely chop'd
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1 ts Grated orange zest
1 c  Orange juice
1 cn (13 3/4 oz) reduced-sodium
-- beefbroth
1 pk (6 oz) wild and white rice
-- pilaf
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
Prep: 10 minutes, Cook: 40 - 45 minutes Cost: $ 1.66 each

1) Brown beef in nonstick dutch oven.  Add onion, carrot, mushrooms;
cook 8 minutes.  Add garlic; cook 1 minute.  Add thyme, zest, juice,
broth, rice with seasonings.

2) Simmer, covered 30 to 35 minutes.  Stir in parsley.

Nutrient Value per serving: 346 calories, 29 g protein, 5 g fat, 46 g
carbohydrate, 559 mg sodium, 53 mg cholesterol.

Exchanges: 1 1/2 starch/bread, 2 meat, 1/2 fruit, 1 3/4 vegetable,
1/4 fat.
