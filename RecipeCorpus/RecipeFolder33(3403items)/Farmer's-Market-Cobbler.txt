Title: Farmer's Market Cobbler
Categories: Newspaper, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Butter
3/4 c  Flour; all-purpose
1 ts Baking powder (heaping)
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Milk
1 qt Fruit (blueberries, peaches,
. apricots, blackberries,
. pears or apples); fresh
. or frozen
Melt the butter in a 2-quart baking dish.  Combine the dry
ingredients, reserving 1/2 cup sugar.  Stir into melted butter in the
baking dish. Add the milk and stir again.

Bring the fruit and 1/2 cup of sugar to a boil.  As soon as it boils,
remove from the heat and pour over the batter.  Don't stir.

Bake at 400Ã¸F for 30 minutes.

Nutritional Information per serving:  241 calories, 10g fat, 38g
carbohydrates, 2mg cholesterol, 320mg sodium
