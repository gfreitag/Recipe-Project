Title: Nam Pla Prig (Sweet And Sour Fish Sauce)
Categories: Thai
Yield: 2 servings
4    Garlic cloves -- chopped
Fine
1 tb Pepper, chili, red --
Crushed
6 tb Lime juice -- fresh
1/4 c  Fish sauce (Nam Pla)
1/2 c  Sugar -- granulated
1 c  Water -- warm
Combine ingredients, stir well, serve at room temperature.

GEnie - R.WEISSGERBE "Delightful Thai Cooking" p. 17
