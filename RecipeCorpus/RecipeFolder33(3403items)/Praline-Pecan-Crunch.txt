Title: Praline Pecan Crunch
Categories: Snacks, Cake/cookie
Yield: 10 cups
1 pk Quaker Oats Squares cereal
-(16 oz; 8 cups)
2 c  Pecan pieces
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Margarine (1/2 stick)
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Baking soda
Prep Time: 10 minutes. Bake Time: 1 hour.

Heat oven to 250'F. Combine cereal and pecans in 13x9" pan; set aside.
Combine corn syrup, brown sugar and margarine in 2-cup microwaveable
bowl. Microwave at HIGH 1 1/2 minutes; stir. Microwave at HIGH 1/2 to
1 1/2 minutes more or until boiling. Stir in vanilla and baking soda
and pour over cereal mixture; stir to coat evenly. Bake 1 hour,
stirring every 20 minutes. Spread on baking sheet to cool; break into
pieces. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-05-95
