Title: Pepperoni Pizza Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Sausage, Cheese/eggs, Tomatoes
Yield: 1 servings
26 oz Can tomato soup
1 ea Soup can water
2/3 c  Sliced pepperoni
1 ts Dried Italian seasoning,
-crushed
Shredded mozzarella cheese
Croutons
In 2 quart saucepan, combine soup, water pepperoni and Italian
seasoning. Heat through.  Serve topped with cheese and croutons.
Makes 6 cups. VARIATION: 1/2 pound cooked, drained ground beef for
pepperoni. Origin: Campbell's 75th Anniversary Cookbook. Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, July/95.
