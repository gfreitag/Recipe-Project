Title: Purple People Punch for a Crowd
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 10 servings
1 qt Unsweetened grapefruit juice
12 1/2 oz Can frozen grape juice
-concentrate
1 qt Carbonated water
Lemon juice
Combine the grapefruit and grape juices together.  Chill.  Just prior
to serving, add the carbonated water and lemon juice.  Garnish with
lemon slices and a block of ice.  If a sweeter drink is desired, use
7-up or Sprite in place of carbonated water. To "SPIKE" the punch, at
13 oz of vodka or gin.  Makes 10 servings. Origin: Homestyle,
Canadian Classics. Ladies of the Royal Purple. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, July/95.
