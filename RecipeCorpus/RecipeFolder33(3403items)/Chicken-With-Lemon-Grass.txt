3 c  Boneless chicken -- cut up
6 c  -Water -- or more
1    Stalk fresh lemon grass --
Cut into 2 inch piec
3 tb Thai chili in oil -- "Nam
Prig Pow"
1 sm Tomato -- cut up
1 sm White onkion -- cut up
1 c  Chinese straw mushrooms --
Canned
1/2 c  Celery -- sliced
2    Or 3 kaffir leaves
2    Green onion -- cut into
2    Inch pieces
4    Or 5 fresh coriander --
Leaves; cut up
1/8 ts Msg (opt.)
2    Fresh hot chilis -- sliced
Fish sauce to taste
GEANG GAI SAI THA-KRAI-----
THAI CHICKEN WITH LEMON
GRAS-----
In a large sauce pan, bring water to a boil, add chicken, white onion,
lemon grass, celery, MSG and fish sauce. let it cook 8 to 10 minutes.
Add the remaining ingredients. Simmer on low heat 10 minutes longer.
Serve hot over warm cooked rice.
