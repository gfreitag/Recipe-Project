Title: Palouse Soup Mix
Categories: Holiday
Yield: 10 servings
2 1/2 c  Green split peas -- (16 oz
Pkg)
2 1/2 c  Lentils -- (16 oz pkg)
2 1/2 c  Pearl barley -- (16 oz pkg)
2 c  Macaroni OR -- (8 oz pkg )
2 c  Brown rice
1/2 c  Celery flakes -- (3/8 oz)
1/2 c  Parsley flakes -- (1/4 oz)
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts White pepper
Mix all ingredients together.  Store in a jar with a tight fitting
lid. Stir before using.
To give as a gift.  Present these instructions on a nice card:
Combine 1 cup of soup mix with 4 cups of water or seasoned stock in a
large pan. Add 1 cup of cooked chopped meat, if desired.  Bring to a
boil.  Reduce heat to low and cover pan.  Simmer gently for 45 to 50
minutes, or until peas are tender.  Add 1/2 teaspoon of salt if
desired.
