Title: Irresistible Low Fat Chocolate Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 18 servings
Vegetable cooking spray
1 cn Eagle Brand Low Fat
-Sweetened Condensed Milk
-(14 oz; NOT evap. milk)
1/3 c  Unsweetened cocoa
3/4 c  Semi-sweet chocolate chips
4    Egg whites
1/4 c  Unsifted flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla extract
Preheat oven to 350'F. Spray 13x9" baking pan with cooking spray. In
2-quart glass measure with handle, combine Eagle Brand Low Fat, cocoa
and chips. Cook in microwave oven on 100% power (high) 2 minutes or
until chips melt, stirring after each minute. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Turn into prepared pan. Bake 20 minutes or until center
is set. Cool. Store tightly covered at room temperature.

NOTE: Microwave ovens vary in wattage and power output; cooking times
may need to be adjusted.

Nutrition Facts (1 brownie): 120 calories; 3 grams total fat; <5
milligrams totals cholesterol; 60 milligrams sodium; 20 grams
carbohydrate; 3 grams protein (values are rounded).

% of Calories from: fat 23%; carbohydrate 67%; protein 10%.
