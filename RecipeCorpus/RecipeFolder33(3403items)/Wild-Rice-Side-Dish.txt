Title: Wild Rice Side Dish
Categories: Side dish, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Wild rice -- raw
1/2 lb Mushrooms -- sliced
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Sliced almonds or water
Chestnuts
3 c  Chicken broth
Put rice, mushrooms, onion and almonds in a 2 quart casserole.  Add
chicken broth and dot with butter.  Cover and bake at 325 degrees for
1 1/2 hours.
