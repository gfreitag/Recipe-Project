Title: Black Bean and Corn Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Black beans (16 oz)
1 c  Frozen corn, thawed
1/2    Basket cherry tomatoes,
-seeded and coarsely chopped
1 cn Chopped green chiles (3 oz)
3    Green onions, sliced
2 tb Cilantro, chopped
2 tb Red wine vinegar
3 tb Oil
1 ts Ground cumin
Salt
Drain liquid from beans and rinse in colander. Combine beans, corn,
tomatoes, chiles, green onions and cilantro in small bowl. In separate
bowl, combine red wine vinegar, oil, cumin and salt to taste. Pour
vinegar-oil mixture over bean mixture. Toss before serving.
