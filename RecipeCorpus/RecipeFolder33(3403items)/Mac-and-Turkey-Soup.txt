Title: Mac and Turkey Soup
Categories: Poultry, Pasta/rice, Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 lb Turkey; ground/cooked
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Bell pepper; chopped
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Oregano
3 cn Tomato soup
3 c  Water
2 c  Elbow macaroni; cooked
2 ts Lemon juice; at room tempera
Recipe by: Campbell's Preparation Time: 0:15 Spray saucepan with
cooking spray.  Heat over medium-high heat 1 minute. Add beef, onion,
pepper, basil, and oregano; cook until turkey is browned and
vegetables are tender, stirring to separate meat.  Stir in soup,
water, macaroni, and lemon juice. Heat through, stirring occasionally.
