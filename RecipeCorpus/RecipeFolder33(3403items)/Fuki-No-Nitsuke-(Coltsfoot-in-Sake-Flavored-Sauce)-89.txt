Title: Fuki No Nitsuke (Coltsfoot in Sake-Flavored Sauce) 89
Categories: Japanese, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
8 oz Canned fuki (coltsfoot); cut
In half
1 c  Niban dashi
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
2 tb Sake (rice wine)
Combine the fuki, dashi, sugar, salt and sake in a 1 quart saucepan,
stir together thoroughly and bring to a boil over high heat.  Cook
briskly for 5 minutes, then drain the fuki, and serve at room
temperature as part of a Japanese meal.
