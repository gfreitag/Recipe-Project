Title: MIGNONETTE SAUCE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
1 c  Raspberry vinegar*
3 tb Finely chopped shallots
Combine vinegar and shallots in small Season to taste with salt and
pep- per. Refrigerate until cold. (Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Keep
refrigerated.)

Used with oysters.

*Available at specialty foods stores and some supermarkets.

Bon Appetit/October/94  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
