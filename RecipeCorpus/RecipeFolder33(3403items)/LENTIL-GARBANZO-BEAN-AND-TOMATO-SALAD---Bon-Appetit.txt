Title: LENTIL GARBANZO BEAN AND TOMATO SALAD - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Lentils
2 lg Carrots, peeled, diced
1    Bay leaf
1 cn (15 to 16-oz) garbanzo
-beans (chick-peas), rinsed,
-drained
1/2    Basket cherry tomatoes,
-halved
1 c  Chopped fresh parsley
5    Green onions, chopped
4 1/2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Plus 1 t fresh lemon juice
Combine 2 1/2 C water, lentils, diced carrots and bay leaf in heavy
medium saucepan. Bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer until
lentils are just tender, about 25 minutes. Drain; transfer to large
bowl. Cool.

Mix all remaining ingredients into lentils. Season to taste with salt
and pepper. (Can be prepared 4 hours ahead. Cover and refrigerate.)

Bon Appetit/January/1994  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
