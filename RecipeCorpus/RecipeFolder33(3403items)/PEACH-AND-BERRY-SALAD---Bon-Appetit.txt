Title: PEACH AND BERRY SALAD - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads, Fruit, Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 servings
3    Peaches
2    1/2 pint baskets
-blackberries
1    1-pint basket strawberries,
-hulled, sliced
1/4 c  Honey
1/2 ts Ground cardamom
Bring medium pot of water to boil. Add peaches and blanch 30 seconds.
Drain. Transfer to medium bowl, cover with cold water and cool.
Drain. Peel peaches and slice. Place in medium bowl. Add all
remaining ingredients and mix to blend. (Can be prepared 1 hour
ahead. Cover and let stand at room temperature.)

Bon Appetit/July/94  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
