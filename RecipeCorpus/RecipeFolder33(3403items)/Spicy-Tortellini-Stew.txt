Title: Spicy Tortellini Stew
Categories: Vegetables, Soup/stew, Tomatoes, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Mushrooms; sliced
1 c  Onions; chopped
1 tb Olive oil
1    Cl Garlic; minced
2 c  Stewed tomatoes; chopped
1    14 1/2 oz water
1 cn Tomato sauce; (8 oz)
1/2 c  Salsa
2 tb Basil
1 pk Cheese tortellini; (9 oz) un
Recipe by: Fresh Food Favorites Preparation Time: 0:15 In a skillet,
combine mushrooms, onions, oil, and garlic.  Cover and simmer until
mushrooms are tender, stirring once.  Add remaining ingredients except
tortellini.  Mix well.  Re-cover, for 10 to 15 minutes or until
mixture is hot and flavors are blended, stirring once.  Add
tortellini. Re-cover and heat until tortellini are tender, stirring
once. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
