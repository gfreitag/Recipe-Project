Title: Fiesta Rice & Sausage
Categories: Side dish, Pasta/rice, Sausage
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  COMET LONG GRAIN RICE,
-uncooked
1 pk FARMER JOHN SAUSAGE, Links
-or Patties
1 cn NEWMAN'S OWN BANDITO SALSA,
-Mild
Shredded cheese
Cook COMET RICE in 2-quart saucepan according to package directions.
In frypan, cook FARMER JOHN SAUSAGE until browned. Cut Sausage into
pieces.

When rice is tender and liquid is abcorbed, ctir in cut-up sausage and
salsa. Garnish with shredded cheese. Makes 4 6-oz servings.

Serve with fruit salad.
