Title: Root Beer (Alcoholic)
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Galliano
1 oz Kahlua
Beer or club soda to fill
In a frosted beer mug, add Galliano and Kahlua. Fill with beer or club
soda. (Beer works best to produce a foamy, root beer-like "head.")
