Title: Besan Ladoo (Gram Flour Balls)
Categories: Desserts, Indian
Yield: 4 servings
175 g  Chick pea (gram) flour
6 tb Ghee
100 g  Sugar
Sieve the chick pea (gram) flour.

Heat the ghee in a heavy-based sauce pan over a medium heat and fry
the flour until golden.

Remove from the heat and cool. Add sugar and mix well.

When cold, make into small balls about the size of a walnut.
