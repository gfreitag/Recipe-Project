Title: Chocolate Mini Cakes
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 c  Unbleached flour
1 c  Granulated sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
3/4 c  Skim milk -- at room
Temperature
1 ts Vanilla
4 ts Egg whites -- whipped
1    Bars unsweetened chocolate
(2 oz) melted
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare 8 small loaf pans with cooking spray and
flour.  In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and
salt. In another mixing bowl, combine milk, vanilla, egg whites, and
chocolate. Pour into pans, filling each 1/2 full.  Bake for 20 to 25
minutes.  Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans.
