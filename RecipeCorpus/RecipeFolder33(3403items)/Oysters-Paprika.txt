Title: Oysters Paprika
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Paprika
1/2 tb Chili Powder
3/4 c  Bread crumbs, fresh
24 ea Oysters on the half shell
Combine dry ingredients.  Sprinkle bread crumb mixture over oysters
and broil until edge of oyster curls.
