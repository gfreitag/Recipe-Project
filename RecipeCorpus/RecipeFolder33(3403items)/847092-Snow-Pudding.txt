Title: 847092 Snow Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 10 servings
1    (3 oz.) pkg. Jello (lime or
Lemon)
1 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Cold water
2    Egg whites
pn Salt
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1 1/3 c  Milk
1/3 c  Sugar
2    Egg yolks
1    Or 2 tbsp. flour
1 ts Vanilla
Dissolve Jello and sugar in boiling water.  Add cool water and let
cool until syrupy.  Whip.  Add salt and 2 beaten egg whites. Cool to
set. May put in mold.  For Sauce:  Blend heated milk with sugar and
egg yolks. Add flour to thicken. Stir over heat until thick (when
mixture begins to boil). Add vanilla.  Cool. Serve custard sauce over
Jello mix. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   07-29-95
