Title: Rocotos Rellenos Numero Dos (Stuffed Chiles No. 2)
Categories: Side dish, Appetizers, Vegetables, So. america
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 ea Onion; diced
1 cl Garlic; crushed
1/3 c  Raisins
2 ea Hard-boiled eggs; chopped
8 ea Black olives; sliced
4 sl Bread; soaked in milk then
-squeezed and drained
1/2 c  Grated Gouda cheese
12 ea To 16 rocoto chiles or
-large jalapenos; stems
-removed and seeds removed
-through a hole at the top
In a medium skillet, brown the ground beef with the onion and garlic.
Remove from the heat and mix in the raisins, hard-boiled eggs, olives,
bread, and cheese.
Stuff the chiles with mixture, stupping the opening with a plug of
cheese. Place them in a covered casserole dish and bake in a 350
degree F oven for 20 minutes.

Authors' heat scale: Hot
