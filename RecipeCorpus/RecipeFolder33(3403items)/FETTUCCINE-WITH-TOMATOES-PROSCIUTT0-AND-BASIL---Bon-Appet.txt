Title: FETTUCCINE WITH TOMATOES PROSCIUTT0 AND BASIL - Bon Appet
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Pasta/rice
Yield: 2 servings;
1 lb Tomatoes (about 4 medium),
-seeded, coarsely chopped
2 oz Thinly sliced prosciutto,
-cut crosswise into 1/2-inch
-wide strips
1/2 c  Thinly sliced fresh basil
3 tb Olive oil (preferably
-extra-virgin)
1 tb Balsamic  vinegar
2 lg Garlic cloves, minced
8 oz Fettuccine
Mix tomatoes, prosciutto, basil, olive oil, vinegar and garlic in
large bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Cook pasta in large pot of boiling salted water until tender but
still firm to bite. Drain. Add pasta to tomato mixture and toss.
Serve immediately.
