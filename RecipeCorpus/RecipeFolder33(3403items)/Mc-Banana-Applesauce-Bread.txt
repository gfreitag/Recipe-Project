Title: Mc Banana Applesauce Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 loaf
1/2 c  Mashed banana; about 2 med
3/4 c  Unsweetened applesauce
1/4 c  Honey
1/2 c  Water
1 tb Lemon juice
2 c  Whole wheat pastry flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Dried grated lemon peel
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Raisins
Preheat the oven 350.

Stir and blend together the wet ingredients.  In a large bowl combine
the dry ingredients and raisins.  Pour the wet ingredients into the
dry and mix thoroughly.  Pour into a nonstick 8x4x3 loaf pan and bake
for 1 hour, or until a cake tester tests dry.
