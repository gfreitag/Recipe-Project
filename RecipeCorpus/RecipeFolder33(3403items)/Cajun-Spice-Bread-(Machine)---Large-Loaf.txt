Title: Cajun Spice Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1 1/8 c  Water
3 c  White bread flour
2 tb Dry milk
1 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
1 1/2 tb Cajun spice blend
1 tb Tomato paste
1 ts Onion paste
1/2 ts Parsley flakes
2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
3 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

This is not a shy little bread to quench the fire of jambalaya or your
blackened redfish.  This is a bread with legs - bread that will get
up and dance to Zydeco music all by itself!  The Cajun spice mix
promises to send a wake-up call to your taste buds when you serve it
with a salad or red beans and rice.

Success Hints
-------------

1/4 t onion powder may be substituted for onion paste.

Use regular or rapid bake cycle.
