Title: German Chocolate Cake Icing (Coconut Pecan) I
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Sugar
2 ea Egg yolks
1/2 ts Vanilla
2/3 c  Chopped pecans
2/3 c  Evaporated milk
1/3 c  Crisco shortening
1 1/3 c  Flaked coconut (3 1/2 oz.)
Combine sugar, milk, egg yolks, Crisco and vanilla.  Cook and stir
over medium heat about 12 minutes or until mixture comes to a boil.
Remove from heat, add coconut and pecans.  Beat until frosting is
thick.  Cool 15 minutes.

Makes only enough to spread on top of layers.
