Title: Cajun Potatoes
Categories: Potatoes, Cajun/creol
Yield: 8 servings
6    (can use 8) potatoes, peeled
*and cut into chunks
1/4 c  (or 1/3) canola oil *
Cajun seasoning (found in
*the spice section of the
*supermarket)
(1)  Coat the potatoes with oil, spread out on a cookie sheet,
sprinkle
with Cajun seasoning, and bake in a preheated 350 degree F. oven
for
1 hour.

*The more oil, the crispier the potato.

From THE ABSOLUTE BEGINNER'S COOKBOOK 2 or "Which Way Do I Fold Egg
Whites?"  by Jackie Eddy and Eleanor Clark
