Title: Beet Pasta
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------YIELD: 3/4 POUND FRESH PASTA---------------------
1 1/2 c  Unbleached all-purpose or
-semolina flour
2 ea To 3 Tbsp grated cooked or
-canned beets
2 lg Eggs
1 tb Vegetable oil
Add the flour, beets, eggs, and oil to the bowl of a food processor or
electric mixer.  The more beets you use, the redder the pasta will
be. Mix and knead the dough, let it rest, roll it out, and cut the
pasta to size desired. Pasta Salads! From the collection of Jim
Vorheis
