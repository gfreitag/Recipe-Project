Title: My Pancakes
Categories: Pcake/waffl, Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM---------------------------
2 c  Flour
1    Egg
3/4 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
3 tb Sugar
3 tb Oil
1 1/4 c  Milk
Mix all ingredients in processor.  Add milk to consistency desired.
