Title: Hurricane
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Dark rum
1 oz Light rum
1/2 oz Galliano
2 oz Passion fruit syrup
1/2 oz Orange juice
1    Splash Rose's lime juice
1    Splash Pineapple juice
Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add rum, galliano, passion fruit
syrup, and orange juice. Shake and strain into highball or collins
glass filled with ice cubes. Top with a splash of Rose's lime juice
and a splash of pineapple juice.
