Title: Sangrita
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
6    Red ripe tomatoes -- peeled
& seeded
1 c  Orange juice
4    Green chiles -- jalapeno or
Serrano
1/4 md Onion -- or to taste
1/3 c  Lime juice
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 oz Tequila
4 ds Tabasco sauce -- or to
Taste
Dice tomatoes, onions, and chiles and place in a pitcher. Add orange
juice, lime juice, sugar, and salt. Refrigerate at least six hours,
preferably overnight.

To serve, pour 1-1/2 ounce tequila and 2 dashes tabasco sauce in a
beer goblet. Add 1/2 cup of sangrita mixture and stir. A great
hangover remedy!

: Joe Robertson
