Title: Sangria Punch
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 8 servings
2    Oranges -- Sliced
2    Lemons -- Sliced
2    Limes -- Sliced
2    Fifths rose wine
-- or Burgundy
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Brandy
28 oz Club soda -- chilled
1/2 oz Triple sec
2 ds Rose's Lime Juice
(optional)
In a large pitcher, combine sliced fruit, wine, brandy, triple sec,
Rose's lime juice, and sugar. Refrigerate overnight. Before serving,
add chilled club soda and stir gently.
