Title: Spiced Potato Chat
Categories: Indian, Side dish, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
4 md Potatoes -- boil in skin &
Cool
1    Chili pepper -- seeded &
Diced
1/2 ts Red pepper powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Lemon juice
2 tb Coriander leaves -- chopped
Peel the potatoes and cut into 1/2" cubes.  Place in a bowl and add
the chili, red pepper, salt, lemon juice and coriander.  Toss well to
mix. Serve at room temperature with Tamarind Chutney.  81 calories
and 0 grams fat.
