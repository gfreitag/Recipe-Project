Title: SMOKED SAUSAGE CHOWDER
Categories: Sausage, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Fully-cooked smoked
-sausage, cut into 3/4-inch
-slices
4 sl Bacon, cut into 1/2-inch
-pieces
2 md Onions, thinly sliced
4 md Potatoes, peeled and cut
-into 1/2-inch pieces
1 1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Salt
ds Fresh ground pepper
2 c  Milk
Cook bacon in large saucepan until crisp; remove to absorbent paper.
Add onions and cook until tender, stirring occasionally. Pour off
drippings. Add potatoes, water, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil,
reduce heat, cover tightly and simmer 20 minutes or until potatoes
are tender. Add milk, sausage and bacon, stirring to combine, and
heat slowly 10 to 12 minutes. Makes  4 servings.
