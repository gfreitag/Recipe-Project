Title: Cheese 'n Weiner Crescents
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 8 servings
1 pk Pillsbury Crescent Dinner
-Rolls
4 sl American cheese, sliced
-3 1/2x1/2"
8    Weiners
1. Heat oven to 375'F. Slit weiners to within 1/2" of ends; insert 3
strips of cheese in each slit.

2. Separate dough into triangles; wrap dough over weiner, keeping
cheese up. Place on ungreased cookie sheet, cheese sides up.

3. Bake at 375'F. for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
