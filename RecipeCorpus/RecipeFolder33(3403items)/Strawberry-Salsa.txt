Title: Strawberry Salsa
Categories: Breakfast, Fruit, Appetizers, Condiments
Yield: 24 servings
1 pt Fresh strawberry
3    Sweet red pepper
2    Green bell pepper
2    Tomato
1    Anaheim pepper
1/4 c  Cilantro
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Triple Sec
2 tb Tequila
1 ts Dried red chili  pepper
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
Combine all ingredients; mix well.  Refrigerate overnight to allow
flavors to blend.

Serving tip:  Service 2 ounces Strawberry Salsa on grilled swordfish,
poached halibut, blackened fish or grilled chicken.
