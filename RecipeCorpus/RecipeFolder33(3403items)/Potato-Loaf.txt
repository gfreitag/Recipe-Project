Title: Potato Loaf
Categories: Potatoes, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Fat-free cottage cheese
2 1/2 c  Mushrooms; coarsely chopped
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
3/4 c  Onions; sliced thin
3    Egg whites; at room temperat
1/3 c  Bread crumbs
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
6 md Potatoes; peeled/ shredded
Recipe by: Reader's Digest Live Longer Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:15
Preheat oven at 400.  Prepare a loaf pan cooking spray.  In a blender,
puree cottage cheese for 1 minute or until smooth.  In a mixing bowl,
combine pureed cottage cheese, mushrooms, Parmesan cheese, onions, egg
whites, bread crumbs, marjoram, salt, and pepper.  Fold in potatoes.
Transfer mixture to prepared pan(s).  Bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes
or until firm.
