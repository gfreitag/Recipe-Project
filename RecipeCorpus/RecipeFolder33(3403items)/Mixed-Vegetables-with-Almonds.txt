Title: Mixed Vegetables with Almonds
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Birds Eye Farm Fresh
-Mixtures Broccoli,
-Cauliflower and Carrots
-(16 oz)
2 tb Butter
2 tb Slivered Almonds
1/4 ts Lemon & Herb Seasoning
Prepare Birds Eye FARM FRESH MIXTURES Broccoli, Cauliflower and
Carrots as directed on the package. While vegetables are cooking,
saute slivered almonds in butter, over medium heat for 3-5 minutes.
Pour buttered almond mixture over prepared vegetables. Top with Lemon
and Herb Seasoning. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-05-95
