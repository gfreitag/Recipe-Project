Title: BBQ sauce with Beer
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats, Poultry, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Viegar
1/2 c  Honey
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Brown sugar
1/2 c  Beer (may be flat)
ds Tabasco sauce; to taste
ds Ground lemon pepper; to tast
Mix all ingredients. Can be used as a marinade, use some of sauce to
marinade over night. Then grill and baste with left over sauce.
