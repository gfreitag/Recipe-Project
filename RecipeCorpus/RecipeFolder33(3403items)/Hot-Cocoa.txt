Title: Hot Cocoa
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Water
1/3 c  Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
1/4 c  Sugar
3 c  Milk
Optional-----
1 ds Nutmeg
1 ds Cinnamon
1 tb Vanilla
Splash Of Liqueur
Blend cocoa and sugar in a saucepan.  Add water, mix thoroughly (a
wire whisk is ideal).  Add optional spices. Bring to a boil over
medium heat and boil about a minute.  Remove from heat, add vanilla
or liqueur, and serve in mugs.
