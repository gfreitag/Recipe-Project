Title: Spaghetti-Cheese Omelet
Categories: Breakfast, Pasta/rice
Yield: 2 servings
4    Eggs
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
2 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
2 c  Cooked spaghetti
Black pepper
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Margarine
Tomato sauce -- spicy
Beat eggs lightly and add the cheese (use Romano cheese if you
prefer), parsley, salt and pepper to taste. Add this mixture to 2
cups of spaghetti and mix well. Heat oil and margarine in a heavy
skillet. Spread spaghetti mixture over bottom of pan and cook over
medium heat for 3-4 minutes, until bottom has a golden crust. Invert
onto plate and then slide back into skillet. Cook until crusty. Serve
with a spicy tomato sauce.

: Jo Anne Merrill
