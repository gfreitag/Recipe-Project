Title: Banana on a Stick
Categories: Kids, Snacks, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
-Bananas
-Graham Crackers
-Peanut Butter
-Popsicle Sticks
Crush graham crackers into crumbs. Cut peeled banana in half
crosswise. Insert stick in flat end. Spread with peanut butter and
roll in graham cracker crumbs. From: Donna Ransdell Date: 08-22-93
Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   07-30-95
