Title: Brandy Alexander
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 oz White creme de cacao
1/2 oz Brandy
2 oz Heavy cream
1 ds Nutmeg
Cracked ice
Fill a mixing glass with ice. Add creme de cacao, brandy, and cream.
Shake well and strain into a cocktail glass. Sprinkle nutmeg on top.
