Title: Spam Shake
Categories: Meats, Spam
Yield: 1 servings
SPAM SHAKE
==========
1 cn Of Spam
1 ea Tin of Anchovies
2 ea 12oz cans of beer
4 oz Tomato juice
1 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 c  Chopped up parsley
1/4 c  Chopped scallions
x  Dash of Tabasco
x  Salt (if you'd need it)
x  Pepper to taste
Put it in blender and blend until smooth. Serve chilled with Celery
stick.
