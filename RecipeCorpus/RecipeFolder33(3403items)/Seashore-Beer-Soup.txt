Title: Seashore Beer Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Condensed tomato soup
1 c  Condensed green pea soup
12 oz Great Western Beer
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1 c  Tiny shrimp
1 c  Half and half or cream
1. Place condensed soups in saucepan; stir in beer.  Add garlic salt.

2. Heat to simmering, stirring until smooth.  simmer 3 to 4 minutes.

3. Just before serving, add undrained shrimp and half and half.  Heat
to serving temperature; do not boil. Makes 6 servings
