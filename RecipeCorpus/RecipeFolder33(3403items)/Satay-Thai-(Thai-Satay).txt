Title: Satay Thai (Thai Satay)
Categories: Thai
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Pork tenderloin -- sliced
Thin or 1" cu
6    Garlic cloves -- crushed
1 tb Coriander -- ground
1 tb Cumin -- ground
1 tb Turmeric -- ground
1 ts Pepper, white -- ground
1 tb Sugar -- brown
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Coconut milk
Mix pork, garlic, coriander, cumin, turmeric, pepper, sugar, salt and
coconut milk in a bowl ... thoroughly. Marinate for at least 1 hour
... preferably overnight in the refridgerator.

Put 4 pieces of pork on a bamboo skewer and browl over hot charcoal
for 3 minutes on each side. Serve with Nam Chim Taeng Kwa or Nam Chim
Satay.

[Specifically for Jennie who wanted recipes to use the sauces and
likes Satay Thai!] BTW, the same recipe can be used with beef or
chicken.
