Title: Hot Chocolate Float
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
2 c  Warm Water
2 ea Env. Instant Cocoa Mix
Ice Cream
Ice cream should be one of the following:  Chocolate-chip Mint or
Peppermint.

Mix warm water and cocoa mix til well blended. Add a scoop of ice
cream and serve.
