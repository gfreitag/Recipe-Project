Title: Beer BarBQ Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Beer
1 c  Ketchup
1/3 c  White vinegar
1/3 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
3 tb Worchester sauce
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Salt
Combine all ing. in saucepan.  Bring to a boil over medium high heat
stirring occasionally.  Reduce heat to medium, cook 5 minutes stirring
occasionally.
