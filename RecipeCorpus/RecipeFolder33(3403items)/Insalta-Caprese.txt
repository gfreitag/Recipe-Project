Title: Insalta Caprese
Categories: Side dish, Cheese/eggs, Salads, Italian
Yield: 6 servings
12 oz Mozarella, Boccancini or
-Fior Di Latte, thinly
-sliced
12    Basil leaves
1 lb Tomatoes, ripe, cored,
-thinly sliced
1/3 c  Extra Virgin Olive Oil
x  Salt
x  Pepper, freshly ground
This super-simple Italian classic is a treat with garden-fresh
tomatoes. For the best results, use the best quality ingredients.

DIRECTIONS:

1. On platter or individual serving plates, alternate cheese slices,
basil leaves and tomato slices. Season with salt and pepper to taste
and drizzle oil on top. Cover and let stand at least 30 minutes
before serving. Serves 4 to 6.
