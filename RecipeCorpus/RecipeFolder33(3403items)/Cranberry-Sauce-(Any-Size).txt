Title: Cranberry Sauce (Any Size)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 16 servings
3 c  Unbleached flour
1 tb Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Egg whites -- whipped
1 c  Granulated sugar
2 c  Cranberry sauce -- canned
1/2 c  Skim milk
1/2 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and
salt.  In another mixing bowl, combine egg whites, sugar, cranberry
sauce, milk, and applesauce.  Mix dry ingredients with wet
ingredients just until moistened. Pour batter into prepared pan(s).
Bake for 55 minutes, depending on pan size.
