Title: Cuba Libre Supreme!
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Cocktails
Yield: 1 serving
1 1/2 oz Southern Comfort
1/2    Lime
Coca Cola
Fill 1/4 of a tall glass with ice. Squeeze lime into glass. Add lime
shell. Pour Southern Comfort. Stir. Fill up with Coca Cola.
