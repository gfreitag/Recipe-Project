Title: Saskatchewan Red-Eye Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
2 1/2 c  Tomato juice
12 oz Beer
1/3 c  Onion - chopped
1/3 c  Celery leaves - chopped
-(optional)
2 1/2 ts Sugar
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
1 ea Bay leaf
2 ea Env unflavoured gelatin
1/4 c  Water
1. Combine tomato juice (reserve 1/4 cup), beer, onion, celery leaves,
sugar, lemon juice, slat, and bay leaf in a sauce pan. Simmer,
uncovered, 10 minutes.

2. Meanwhile, sprinkle gelatin over cold water and reserved tomato
juice in a large bowl. Let stand to soften.

3. Strain hot tomato juice mixture into bowl; stir until gelatin is
completely disolved.

4. Pour into a lightly oiled 1 quart mold.  Chill until firm.  Unmold
onto crisp salad greens. Makes 8 servings.
