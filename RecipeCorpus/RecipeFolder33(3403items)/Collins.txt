Title: Collins
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Cocktails
Yield: 1 serving
1 oz Gin
The juice of one lemon
1 ts Sugar
dr Angostura Bitters
Sodawater
Pour all ingredients except soda into a grogg-glass. Stir until all
sugar has dissolved. Fill up with soda. Stir and decoarate with a
slice of lemon. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-05-95
