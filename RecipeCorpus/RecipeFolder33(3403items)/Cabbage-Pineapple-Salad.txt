Title: Cabbage Pineapple Salad
Categories: Vegetables, Salads, Oldies, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
3 c  Shredded cabbage
1 c  Diced marshmallows
1 c  Diced pineapple
Few grains salt
Boiled or mayonnaise
-dressing
Combine cabbage, pineapple, marshmallows, and salt.  Moisten with
boiled or mayonnaise dressing.  Mix lightly with 2 forks.  Serve on
beds of shredded lettuce.  8 servings.

Leta Hostetler, Elk City, OK.
