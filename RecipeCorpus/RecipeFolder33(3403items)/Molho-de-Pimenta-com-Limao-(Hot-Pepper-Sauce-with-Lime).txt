Title: Molho de Pimenta com Limao (Hot Pepper Sauce with Lime)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetables, So. america
Yield: 3 /4 cup
6 ea Fresh Malagueta chiles  -OR-
-substitute pequins; seeds
-and stems removed, minced
1 cl Garlic; minced
1 md Onion; minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Lime or lemon juice
Note: the Malagueta pepper is a close relative of the Tabasco pepper.

Combine all ingredients and allow to sit at room temperature for 2
hours to blend all the flavors

Authors' Heat scale: Hot

_Hot and Spicy Latin Dishes_  from Chili Pepper Magazine Dave DeWitt,
