Title: Summer Squash with Parmesan Cheese (Microwave)
Categories: Side dish, Microwave, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea Chopped shallots
1 3/4 lb Summer squash; skinned,
-seeded, and cubed
3 tb Butter
Salt & pepper
3 tb Parmesan cheese; grated
Put the shallots in a casserole dish.  Cover and microwave on high
for 3 minutes.
Add the squash and 1/4 cup water.  Cover and microwave on high for 10
minutes.
Put the butter in a bowl and melt for 1 minute on high.  Sprinkle
with salt and pepper and pour over the squash.  Add the Parmesan and
stir well. Transfer to a serving dish and serve. _The Ultimate
Microwave Cookbook_ Elisa Vergne, 1988 Gallery Books ISBN
