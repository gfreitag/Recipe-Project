Title: Tuscan White Beans with Sage
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil, divided
2 oz Prosciutto or pancetta,
-chopped fine
1 sm Onion, sliced
3    Garlic cloves, crushed
8    Fresh sage leaves, chopped
2 cn Cannellini beans, rinsed and
-drained
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Fresh sage for garnish
In a small skillet heat 1 tablespoon olive oil and saute meat, onion
and garlic 8-10 minutes, or until onion is tender. Stir in sage and
cook 1 minute. Add beans and remaining olive oil, salt and pepper to
taste and toss gently, taking care not to break the beans. Transfer
to a serving dish and garnish with sage.
