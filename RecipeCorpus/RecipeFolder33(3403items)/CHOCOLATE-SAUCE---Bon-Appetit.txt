Title: CHOCOLATE SAUCE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salsa/sauce, Chocolate, Desserts
Yield: 1 1/3 cups
8 oz Semisweet chocolate,
-chopped
5 1/2 tb Unsalted butter, cut into
-small pieces
2 tb Instant coffee powder
-dissolved in 1/2 C hot
-water
Combine all ingredients in heavy small saucepan. Whisk over medium
heat until mixture is smooth, about 5 minutes. (Can be made 5 days
ahead. Chill. Rewarm over low heat, stirring constantly.)
