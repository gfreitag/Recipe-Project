Title: Italian Wheat Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1 c  Water
2 c  White bread flour
1 c  Wheat bread flour
1 1/2 tb Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Pesto sauce
2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
3 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

This is a light wheat bread with a succulent pesto flavor.  Serve it
plain or use it for garlic bread with spaghetti or lasagna.

Success Hints
-------------

Use freshly prepared pesto sauce or make your own.  Stir the sauce
well before measuring to blend oil.

Use regular or rapid bake cycle
