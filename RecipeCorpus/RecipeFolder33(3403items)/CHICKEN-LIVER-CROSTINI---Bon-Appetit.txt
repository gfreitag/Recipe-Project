Title: CHICKEN LIVER CROSTINI - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Appetizers
Yield: 16 crostini
2 tb (or more) olive oil
1/4 c  Chopped shallots
6 oz Chicken livers, trimmed,
-cut into bite-size pieces
2 tb Sweet white dessert wine
-(such as late harvest
-Riesling)
1 ts Capers, rinsed, drained
1    6 1/2-inch-thick baguette
-slices
Heat 2 T oil in heavy medium skillet over medium heat. Add shallots
and livers; saute until livers are cooked through and shallots are
brown, about 12 minutes. Add wine; simmer 30 seconds. Transfer
mixture to processor. Add capers; process to coarse paste, adding
more oil by teaspoonfuls if mixture is dry. Season with salt and
pepper. Spread on baguette slices and serve.

Bon Appetit/July/94  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
