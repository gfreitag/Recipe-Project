Title: Classic White Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/8 c  Water
3 c  White bread flour
1 1/2 tb Dry milk
2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
3 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

This is a classic - plain white bread, but a world away from
store-bought!
