Title: RASPBERRY VINAIGRETTE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Dress/stuff
Yield: 3 /4 cup
1/3 c  Raspberry preserves
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
4 ts Rice vinegar
4 ts Cider vinegar
1 tb Dry white wine
1 ts Coarse-grained mustard
Place preserves in medium bowl. Gradually whisk in oil. Stir in rice
vinegar, cider vinegar, wine and mustard. Season to taste with salt
and pepper.
