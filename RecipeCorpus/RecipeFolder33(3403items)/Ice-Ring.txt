Title: Ice Ring
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Frozen lemonade concentrate,
-thawed (6 oz)
3 c  Water
1 c  Fresh strawberries with tops
-OR
1 c  Frozen strawberries without
-sugar (other fruits,
-grapes, sliced oranges,
-limes, peaches or
-nectarines can be used,
-depending on punch)
In a 2-quart non-metal container, combine lemonade and water; mix
well. Pour mixture into a 6-cup mold; freeze until slushy, about 2-3
hours. Place strawberries in slush, allowing tops of strawberries to
show. Freeze until firm or overnight. Remove ice ring from mold; wrap
in plastic wrap and store in freezer until needed.
