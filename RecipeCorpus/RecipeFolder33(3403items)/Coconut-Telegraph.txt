Title: Coconut Telegraph
Categories: Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Bacardi Light
1 oz Chivas Regal
1 oz Coco Lopez
2 oz Sweetened condensed milk
1 ds Shredded coconut meat
Blend everything except the shredded coconut.  Pour it over rocks
glass or Flinstones jelly glass filled with cubed ice and garnish
with the coconut.
