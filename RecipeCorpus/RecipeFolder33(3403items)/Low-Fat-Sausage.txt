Title: Low Fat Sausage
Categories: Poultry, Breakfast
Yield: 8 servings
1 lb Turkey; ground/cooked
2 1/2 ts Sage
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/8 ts Thyme; flakes
1/4 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Mustard
1/8 ts Cayenne
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 c  Water cold
Recipe by: Rosemary Matejka Preparation Time: 0:15 Mix spices in water
first.  Then add turkey. Mix batter with hands until thoroughly
blended. Cook and freeze in smaller portions.
