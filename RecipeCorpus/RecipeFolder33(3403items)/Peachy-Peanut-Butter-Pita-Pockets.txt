Title: Peachy Peanut Butter Pita Pockets
Categories: Kids, Snacks
Yield: 4 servings
4    Pita bread
3/4 c  Crunchy peanut butter
2    Fresh California peach
Cut about 3 inches off of one edge of pita bread to get inside.
Carefully open pocket and spread a thin layer of peanut butter on
both inside walls. Fill with peach slices.

Note:  Warm pita bread slightly to make it more pliable.
