Title: Powter Apple Fritters
Categories: Breakfast, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
3 md Apples; peeled, cored
-and cut into 1/4 inch
-rounds
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1 lg Egg
1 c  Apple juice
1 tb Vegetable oil
Dry apples with paper towel and set aside.  Apples need to be dry or
batter will not stick.

Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon in a bowl.

In a separate bowl, beat egg, juice, and 1 1/4t of veg oil.  Mix wet
and dry ingredients together until there are no lumps.

In a nonstick pan, heat 1/2t oil.  Dip apples in batter and let
excess drip off.  Saute slices a few at a time.  Cook until golden,
turn and brown the other side.  Continue until all the apples are
done.  Drain on paper towels.
