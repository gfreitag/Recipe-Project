Title: Fruit Smoothie
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Fruit cocktail, can  --
-chilled
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Nonfat dry milk powder
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Ice cubes
Cinnamon  -- ground
(dashes)
PER SERVING:  87 cal., 5g Pro., 16g Carbo., 0g fat, 3mg Chol., 81
Sodium 298mg Potassium
In a blender container combineundrained fruit cocktail and remaining
ingredients.  Cover; blend till combined.  Add ice cubes; cover and
blend till smooth.  Sprinkle with addition

Serve immediately. BETTER HOMES AND GARDENS
