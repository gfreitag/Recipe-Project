Title: LINGUINE WITH PESTO-CLAM SAUCE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Appetizers, Pasta/rice
Yield: 2 servings;
1 cn (10-oz) baby clams,
-drained, juices reserved
1/2 c  Dry white wine
1/2 c  Purchased pesto sauce
8 oz Linguine
1/3 c  Chopped Italian parsley
Boil reserved clam juices and wine in heavy medium saucepan over high
heat until reduced to 1/2 C, about 6 minutes. Mix in clams and pesto
sauce.

Meanwhile, cook pasta in large pot of boiling salted water until
tender but still firm to bite. Drain well. Add pasta and parsley to
sauce and toss to coat. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Bon Appetit/January/1994  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
