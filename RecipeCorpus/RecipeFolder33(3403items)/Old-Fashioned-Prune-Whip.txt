Title: Old-Fashioned Prune Whip
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Dried prunes -- cooked,
Mashed
2 ts Lemon juice
1 ds Salt
1 ts Lemon zest
4 tb Powdered sugar
4    Egg whites
Pit prunes and mash. Blend in lemon peel and lemon juice, 2
tablespoons sugar and salt. To stiffly beaten egg whites, add
remaining sugar, beating until mixed well. Fold in prune mixture
gently. Pile lightly into 1-1/2 quart baking dish. Bake at 350
degrees for 20-30 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out
clean. Serve warm with custard sauce if desired.

: Jo Merrill
