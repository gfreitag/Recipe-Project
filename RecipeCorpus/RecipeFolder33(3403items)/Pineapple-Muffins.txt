Title: Pineapple Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Unbleached flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Rolled oats
1/3 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1    Egg white -- whipped
1/2 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
1 c  Pineapple -- crushed/undrain
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare muffin tins with cooking spray and
flour. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt,
cinnamon, oats, and sugar.  In another mixing bowl, combine egg
white, applesauce, and pineapple.  Add dry ingredients with wet
ingredients just until moistened. Use an ice cream scoop to fill
muffin tins two thirds full.  Bake 20 to 25 minutes.
