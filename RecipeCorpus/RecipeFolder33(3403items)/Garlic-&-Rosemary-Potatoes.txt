Title: Garlic & Rosemary Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 5 servings
2 lb Red potatoes; cut in half
1 tb Rosemary
1 ts Salt
2    Cl Garlic; chopped
Recipe by: Anita A. Matejka Preparation Time: 0:15 Preheat oven at
400. Spray a baking pan with cooking spray.  Sprinkle potatoes with
rosemary, salt, and garlic.  Mix well.  Bake for 20 to 30 minutes.
