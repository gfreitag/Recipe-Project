Title: Mock Pink Champagne
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 20 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
1 cn Frozen orange juice
-concentrate (6 oz)
1 cn Grapefruit juice
-concentrate (6 oz)
28 oz Ginger ale
1/3 c  Grenadine syrup
Mix sugar and water in saucepan; boil for 5 minutes. Cool. Add frozen
concentrates; refrigerate. Chill for several hours. At serving time,
add ginger ale and grenadine syrup.
