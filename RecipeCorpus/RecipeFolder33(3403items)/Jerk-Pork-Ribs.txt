Title: Jerk Pork Ribs
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1    Rack pork ribs, cut into
-2-3 rib portions (3 lb)
2 c  Jamaican Jerk Sauce
Coat ribs with Jamaican Jerk Sauce, rubbing sauce in well. Cover and
refrigerate overnight. Bake at 350'F. 45 minutes, occasionally
turning and basting. Remove from oven and grill over medium hot coals
until browned and cooked through, about 25-30 minutes, turning ribs 4
or 5 times and basting with Jamaican Jerk Sauce.

Each serving contains about:

622 calories; 1,720 milligrams sodium; 123 milligrams cholesterol; 51
grams fat; 11 grams carbohydrates; 30 grams protein; 0.35 gram fiber.
