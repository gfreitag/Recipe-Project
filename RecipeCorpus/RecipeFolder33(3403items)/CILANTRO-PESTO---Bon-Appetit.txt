Title: CILANTRO PESTO - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Herbs/spice, Pasta/rice
Yield: 2 cups
3    Bunches fresh cilantro,
-stems removed, leaves
-coarsely chopped
-(about 2 1/2 C)
1/2 c  Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese (about 1 1/2 oz)
1/2 c  Pine nuts or walnuts
1/4 c  Chopped garlic
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
1 tb Plus 1 t chili powder
1 tb Ground cumin
1 ts Dried crushed red pepper
1 1/2 ts (or more) salt
3/4 c  Olive oil
Combine first 8 ingredients and 1-1/2 t salt in processor. Using
on/off turns and periodically scraping down sides of work bowl,
process until finely chopped. With machine running, gradually add oil
through feed tube and blend until incorporated. Season with pepper
and more salt, if desired. (Can be prepared ahead. Press plastic wrap
onto surface of pesto and refrigerate up to 1 week or freeze up to 1
month.)

*Toss with fettuccine.
