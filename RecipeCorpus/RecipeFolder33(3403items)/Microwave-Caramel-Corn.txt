Title: Microwave Caramel Corn
Categories: Microwave, Snacks
Yield: 8 servings
3 qt Popcorn -- * see note
1 c  Brown sugar
Margarine -- not diet
1/4 c  Corn syrup
1/2 ts Baking soda
* Use about 3 quarts or 2 large bowls of popped corn.

1. Boil brown sugar, margarine and syrup in microwave for 2 minutes.
Add the baking soda; mix well. 2. Pour mixture over popped corn and
place in microwave for 2 minutes. 3. Remove from microwave and stir
very well. Return mixture to microwave for one more minute. Cool a
bit before serving.
