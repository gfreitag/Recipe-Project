Title: Tortilla Chip Soup
Categories: Tex/mexican, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Chicken; cooked
2 c  Stewed tomatoes
1/2 c  Bell peppers; chopped
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
2 c  Chicken broth
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Black pepper
2 c  Tortilla chips; crushed
1 c  Monterey jack cheese; shredd
Recipe by: Anita A. Matejka Preparation Time: 0:15 In a large pan
combine chicken, tomatoes, peppers, onion, broth, lemon juice, and
black pepper, simmer for 20 minutes  Place chips in individual bowls,
sprinkle with 1/2 cup cheese, and pour soup mixture over it.  Top
with remaining cheese.
