Title: Zucchini with Gruyere Cheese  (Microwave)
Categories: Side dish, Microwave, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 3/4 lb Zucchini; cut into strips
1 tb Butter
Salt and pepper
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Cornstarch
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1 ea Egg
1 c  Gruyere cheese
Put the zucchini in a casserole dish with the butter and 2 to 3
tablespoons water.  Season with salt and pepper, add the garlic
powder and stir well.  Cover and microwave on HIGH for 14 minutes.
Put the cornstarch in a small bowl and mix to a smooth paste with
the lemon juice.  Add the egg and whisk until foamy.
Drain the zucchini, reserving the cooking liquid.  Pour the liquid
into the bowl containing the cornstarch and egg mixture.  Microwave,
uncovered, on HIGH for 1 minute. Whisk.  Taste and adjust seasoning.
Put the zucchini in a warmed serving dish.  Add the Gruyere,
stirring carefully.  Pour the sauce over, stir again and serve.
