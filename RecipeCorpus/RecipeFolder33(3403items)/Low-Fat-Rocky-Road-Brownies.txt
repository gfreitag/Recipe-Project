Title: Low Fat Rocky Road Brownies
Categories: Chocolate, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
4    Egg whites -- whipped
1 c  Marshmallow creme
1 tb Vanilla
1/2 c  Unbleached flour
1 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa powder -- sifted
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
Preheat oven at 325.  Prepare a 9 x 13" pan with cooking spray and
flour. In a mixing bowl, combine egg whites, marshmallow creme, and
vanilla.  In another mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder,
baking soda, baking powder, and salt.  Mix dry ingredients with wet
ingredients just until moistened.  Bake for 18 minutes.
