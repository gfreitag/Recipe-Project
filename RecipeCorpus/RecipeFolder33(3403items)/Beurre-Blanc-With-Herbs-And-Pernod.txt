Title: Beurre Blanc With Herbs And Pernod
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Shallot -- minced
3/4 c  White wine dry
1/4 c  Heavy cream
8 tb Butter
Salt
White pepper
2 tb Fresh parsley -- minced
2 tb Tarragon  (2 teaspoons
Dried) -- minced
2 tb Chervil fresh -- minced
2 tb Chives -- minced
1 tb Pernod or ricard
Combine shallots and wine in a heavy saucepan . Cook over medium
~high heat until wine has nearly evaporated.

Add cream and reduce by half. Add butter quickly one tbls at a time
whisking constantly. Remove saucepan from heat and stir in salt,
pepper herbs and pernod.
