Title: Light and Easy Fruited Chocolate Mousse
Categories: Healthy, Puddings, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1 pk Frozen strawberries in syrup
1 pk Gelatin
3/4 c  Boiling water
1/4 c  HERSHEY'S Cocoa
1 c  Ice cubes
1/2 c  Frozen light dessert topping
1.  In blender container, place gelatin; carefully pour in boiling
water. Cover; blend on low speed until gelatin is completely
dissolved. 2. Add cocoa; continue blending until smooth.  Immediately
add ice cubes, blending until melted.  Add reserved strawberries and
light whipped topping; continue blending, just until combined and
smooth. 3. Immediately pour into dessert dishes; refrigerate until
set, about 4 hours. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   08-14-95
