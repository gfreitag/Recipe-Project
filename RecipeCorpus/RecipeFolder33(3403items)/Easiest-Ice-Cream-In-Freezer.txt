Title: Easiest Ice Cream In Freezer
Categories: Ice cream
Yield: 8 servings
1 qt Milk
1 qt Half and half
1    Instant pudding mix -- any
Flavor
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk --
NOT evaporated
2 c  Mixed fruit -- * see note
* Use the fruit of your choice or mix fruits such as bananas,
strawberries, peaches, etc.

Mix all the ingredients by blender or by hand and pour into freezer
container. Freeze according to freezer directions.
