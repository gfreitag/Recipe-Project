Title: Spicy Popcorn
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 10 servings
1 tb Water
4 ts Margarine
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts White pepper
1/8 ts Cayenne
10 c  Popcorn; popped
Recipe by: Betty Crocker's New Choices Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:15
Preheat oven at 400. Prepare baking sheet with cooking spray.  Place
popped popcorn on  prepared baking sheet.  Toss combination of
seasonings for the flavor.  Bake for 10 minutes, tossing once.
