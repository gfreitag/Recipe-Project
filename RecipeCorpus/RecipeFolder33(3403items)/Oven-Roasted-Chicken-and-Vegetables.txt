Title: Oven Roasted Chicken and Vegetables
Categories: Poultry, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Savory herb with garlic soup
-mix
1 pk Green Giant American
-Mixtures Heartland Style
-Frozen Vegetables
12 oz Frozen potato wedges
2    Chicken breast halves, cut
-in half crosswise
1. Preheat oven to 450'F. Reserve 2 tablespoons soup mix.

2. In plastic bag or bowl, toss vegetables and potatoes with
remaining soup mix until coated; spread on a large foil-covered
baking sheet. Toss chicken with reserved 2 tablespoons soup mix;
arrange on same baking sheet.

3. Bake at 450'F. for 15-18 minutes until chicken is cooked through
and vegetables are lightly browned.
