Title: Quick Tartar Sauce For Chicken Or Fish
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
3    Sweet pickles -- finely
Chopped
1 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
1    Hard-boiled eggs -- chopped
1 tb White wine vinegar
* Use only real mayonnaise.

In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients. Cover tightly and chill.
