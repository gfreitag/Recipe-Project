Title: ROASTED LOBSTER WITH BASIL OIL - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Fresh basil leaves
1 1/2 c  Olive oil
8    Live lobsters (1 1/2 lb
-each)
Bring medium saucepan of water to boil. Add basil and blanch 20
seconds. Drain. Transfer leaves to processor and blend well. With
machine running, add 1 C oil through feed tube and blend until
smooth. Season with salt and pepper. (Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover
and chill. Bring to room temperature before using.)

Preheat oven to 400'F. Bring large pot of water to boil. Add 2
lobsters head first; boil 2 minutes. Transfer to cutting board.
Repeat with remaining lobsters, boiling 2 at a time. Turn lobsters so
that undersides face up. Using heavy large knife, cut lobsters
lengthwise in half. Remove green tomalley (liver), if desired. Place
lobsters cut side up on baking sheets. Brush with 1/2 C olive oil.
Season with salt and pepper. Roast lobsters until cooked through,
about 20 minutes. Brush some basil oil over. Serve; pass remaining
basil oil separately.
