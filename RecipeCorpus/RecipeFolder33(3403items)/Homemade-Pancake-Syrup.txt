Title: Homemade Pancake Syrup
Categories: Can/preserv, Tasteofhome, Syrups, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Water
3 c  Granulated sugar
3 c  Grown sugar
1 c  Corn syrup
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Vanilla
1 tb Maple flavoring
Combine first 4 ingredients in large saucepan.  Stir on medium high
until it boils.  Add lemon juice, vanilla and maple flavoring.  Stir.
Fill hot sterilized bottles to within 1/4 inch of top.  Seal.  Makes
3-1/2 pints. Origin: Jean Pare's Preserves Book. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Aug/94.
