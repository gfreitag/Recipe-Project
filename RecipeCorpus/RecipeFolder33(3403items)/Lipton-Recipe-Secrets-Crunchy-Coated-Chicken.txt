Title: Lipton Recipe Secrets Crunchy Coated Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Margarine or butter, melted
1/4 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 lb Chicken thighs and breasts
1/2 c  Cornflake crumbs
1    Env. Lipton Recipe Secrets
Savory Herb w/ Garlic Soup
Mix
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In small bowl, combine hot pepper sauce
with melted butter. In a second bowl combine Lipton Savory Herb Soup
Mix w/ cornflake crumbs, set aside. Dip chicken in butter mixture,
then in cornflake soup mix, until evenly coated. In 13 x 9-inch
baking or roasting pan, arrange chicken. Bake uncovered 30 minutes or
until chicken is done.
