Title: SHRIMP PASTA SALAD - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads, Pasta/rice, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
8 oz Small cooked shrimp
4 oz Small pasta shells, freshly
-cooked
1/2 c  Chopped seeded peeled
-cucumber
1/2 c  Chopped seeded tomato
1/4 c  Chopped black olives
1/4 c  Chopped red onion
1/4 c  Chopped fresh dill or 1
-T dried dillweed
2/3 c  Regular or reduced-calorie
-mayonnaise
1 tb White wine vinegar
Combine shrimp, pasta, cucumber, tomato, olives, onion and dill in
medium bowl. Whisk mayonnaise and vinegar to blend in small bowl. Add
dressing to salad and toss to coat. Season to taste with salt and
pepper. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour and up to 2 hours.

Bon Appetit/January/1994  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
