Title: Glazed Baked Ham
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Small canned Ham
4 tb Prepared mustard
1 ea Small Can Jellied Cranberry
Combine sauce and mustard, bring to simmer, blending well. Spread
prepared glaze on ham and heat through in 350 F oven or in microwave.
