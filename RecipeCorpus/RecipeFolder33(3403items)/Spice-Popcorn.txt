Title: Spice Popcorn
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Granulated sugar
1/8 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Margarine; melted
2 qt Popcorn; popped
Recipe by: I Love Popcorn Preparation Time: 0:15 Preheat oven at 400.
Prepare baking sheet with cooking spray.  Place popped popcorn on a
prepared baking sheet.  Toss combination of seasonings for the
flavor. Bake for 10 minutes, tossing once.
