Title: Beer Basted Steaks
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
6    Sirloin steaks
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 tb Grainy mustard
1 tb White vinegar
1    Onion, finely chopped
1    Bay leaf
1 c  Beer
Combine all ingredients in a pan. Bring to boil, reduce heat, simmer
for 10 minutes, cool.

Pour mixture over steaks. Let stand 1 to 2 hours, turning steaks
occasionally.

Barbecue until golden brown on both sides.

Source: NSLC Consumer brochure - Summer 95
