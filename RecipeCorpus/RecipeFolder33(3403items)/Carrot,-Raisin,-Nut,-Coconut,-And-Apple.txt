Title: Carrot, Raisin, Nut, Coconut, And Apple
Categories: Breads
Yield: 16 servings
2 c  Unbleached flour
1 1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Carrots -- shredded
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Nuts -- chopped
1/2 c  Coconut -- shredded
1 c  Apples -- shredded
3    Egg whites -- whipped
1 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
2 ts Vanilla
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda,
cinnamon, and  salt.  In another mixing bowl, combine carrots,
raisins, nuts, coconut, apples, egg whites, applesauce, and vanilla.
Mix dry ingredients with wet ingredients just until moistened.  Pour
batter into prepared pan(s).  Bake for 1 hour depending on pan size.
