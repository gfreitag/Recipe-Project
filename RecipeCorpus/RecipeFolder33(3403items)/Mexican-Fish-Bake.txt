Title: Mexican Fish Bake
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Tortilla chips; crushed
2 c  Rice; cooked
1 c  Kidney beans; drained
1 1/2 lb Fillets; cut into 1" strips
1 c  Salsa
1/2 c  Monterey jack cheese; shredd
1/2 c  Onions; chopped
3 oz Tortilla chips; for serving
Recipe by: The Health Lover's Guide To Super Seafood Preparation
Time: 0:15 Preheat oven at 400.  Prepare a 2 quart casserole dish
with cooking spray.
Using half the ingredients at a time, make alternate layers of
crushed tortilla chips, rice, beans, fish strips, salsa, cheese, and
onion.  Bake for 25 to 30 minutes. Serve with tortilla chips.
