Title: Honey Scones
Categories: Breads
Yield: 24 servings
6 oz Honey
8 oz Egg
3 oz Vegetable oil
3 oz Half and half
2 lb Biscuit mix
2 tb Grated orange peel
Egg wash
Method: Combine and mix honey, eggs, oil and half and half. Stir into
biscuit mix and grated orange peel. Divide dough into 2 parts, place
on floured board and roll each part into circle 1/2-inch thick. Cut
each round into 12 wedges. Place on greased or paper-lined pans.
Brush with egg wash. Bake in 400 F oven 10 minutes or until lightly
browned. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   08-14-95
