Title: The Shack BBQ Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
6 tb Chili powder
6 tb Pepper
6 tb Salt
4 c  Ketchup
4 c  Vinegar
4 c  Water
1 lg Onion; minced
Combine chili powder, pepper and salt. In a large saucepan, combine
Ketchup, vinegar, water and onion and stir in dry mixture. Bring to
rolling boil, reduce heat and cook uncovered 1 1/2 hours, stirring
every 10 min.The mixture will reduce  almost to half. Pour into
sterilized jars and let rest for a few days.
