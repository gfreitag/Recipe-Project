Title: Cherry Cashew Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts, Holiday
Yield: 54 servings
1 c  Butter or margarine
3/4 c  Granulated sugar
3/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
2    Egg
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 pk Vanilla milk chips
1 1/2 c  Dried tart cherries
1 c  Lightly salted cashews
1.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. 2. In a large mixing bowl, combine
butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla. Mix with
electric mixer on medium speed until thoroughly mixed. 3. Combine
flour and baking soda; gradually add flour mixture to butter mixture.
4. Stir in vanilla milk chips, dried cherries and cashews. 5. Drop by
rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets. 6. Bake in a
preheated 375 degree F. oven 12 to 15 minutes, or until golden brown.
Let cool on wire racks; store in a tightly covered container.
