Title: DATE CRUMB BARS - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 lb Whole pitted dates
2/3 c  Packed golden brown sugar
2/3 c  Orange juice
1 tb Vanilla extract
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Old-fashioned oats
1 1/2 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Packed golden brown sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Baking soda
Pinch of salt
1 c  (2 sticks) chilled unsalted
-butter, cut into pieces
3/4 c  Coarsely chopped walnuts
Vanilla ice cream (opt.)
FOR FILUNG: Stir first 3 ingredients in medium saucepan over medium
heat until sugar dissolves. Simmer until dates are tender and syrup
is thick, about 3 minutes. Cool; mix in vanilla. Puree filling in
processor until smooth.

FOR CRUST: Preheat oven to 350F. Butter 13x9x2-inch baking pan. Mix
first 6 ingredients in large bowl. Add butter and rub in with
fingertips until mixture resembles coarse meal and forms moist clumps.

Firmly press half of crumb mixture onto bottom of prepared pan. Spread
filling over crust. Add walnuts to remaining crumb nuxture and
sprinkle over filling. Bake until topping is golden brown, about 40
minutes. Cool in pan. (Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover and let
stand at room temperature.)

Cut into squares. Serve with vanilla ice cream, if desired.
