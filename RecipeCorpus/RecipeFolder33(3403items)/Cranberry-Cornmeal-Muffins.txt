Title: Cranberry Cornmeal Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Unbleached flour
3/4 c  Cornmeal
1/3 c  Granulated sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1    Egg white -- whipped
3/4 c  Buttermilk* -- at room
Temperature
1/4 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
1/2 ts Lemon peel -- grated
1 c  Cranberries -- chopped
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare 12 muffin tins with cooking spray and
flour. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, 1/3 cup sugar,
baking powder, and salt.  In another mixing bowl, combine egg white,
buttermilk, applesauce, and lemon peel, and cranberries.  Use an ice
cream scoop to fill muffin tins 2/3 full.  Bake for 20 minutes or
until golden.
