Title: Mc Lentil Potato Burgers
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 10 burgers
1/2 c  Chopped yellow onion
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Water
2 c  Cooked and drained lentils
-mashed
2 c  Mashed potatoes
1 c  Whole wheat bread crumbs
1 ts Parsley
1 1/2 ts Sage
1 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/4 ts Rosemary
1/8 ts Black pepper
Preheat the oven 350.

Saute the onion and celery in water about 10 mins. Combine in a bowl
with the remaining ingredients and mix well.  Form into patties and
place on a nonstick baking sheet.

Bake 20 mins, then turn and bake an additional 20 mins.

Serve hot on toasted whole wheat burger buns.
