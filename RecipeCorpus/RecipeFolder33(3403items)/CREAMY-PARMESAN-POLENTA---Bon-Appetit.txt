Title: CREAMY PARMESAN POLENTA - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Yellow cornmeal
2 1/2 c  Canned low-salt chicken
-broth
1 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Whipping cream
2 tb (1/4 stick) butter
Combine water and cornmeal in small bowl. Bring broth to boil in heavy
large saucepan. Gradually add cornmeal mixture, stirring until well
blended. Reduce heat to low and simmer until polenta is very thick,
stirring occasionally, about 15 minutes. Mix in 1/2 C cheese, cream
and butter. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Transfer polenta to
serving bowl. Sprinkle remaining 1/2 C cheese over.
