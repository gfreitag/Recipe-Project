Title: CHIPOTLE-ANCHO CHILI PESTO - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Herbs/spice, Condiments
Yield: 1 1/3 cups
2    Dried ancho or pasilla
-chilies*
1 lg Red bell pepper
1    To 2 canned chipotle chilies
-in adobo sauce,* rinsed,
-seeded
1/3 c  Pine nuts (about 2 oz)
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
2    Garlic cloves
1/4 c  (or more) olive oil
Remove stems and seeds from ancho chilies. Place chilies in large
bowl. Cover with boiling water. Let stand until soft, about 30
minutes. Drain, reserving 1 T soaking liquid.

Roast bell pepper over gas flame or in broiler until blackened on all
sides. Seal in paper bag and let stand 15 minutes. Peel, stem and seed
pepper.

In processor, blend anchos, 1 tablespoon soaking liquid, bell pepper,
chipotles, pine nuts, fresh lemon juice and garlic until almost
smooth. With machine running, gradually add 1/4 C olive oil; process
until smooth. If pesto is dry, mix in more oil by spoonfuls. Season
with salt. (Can be made ahead. See note at end of Sun-dried Tomato
and Olive Pesto recipe.)

*Dried ancho, pasilla and canned chipotle chilies are available at
Latin American markets, specialty foods stores and some supermarkets.
