Title: Lemonade Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Desserts, Fruit, Barbara
Yield: 1 pie
1 cn (14 oz) sweetened condensed
-milk
1 cn (6 oz) frozen lemonade,
-partially thawed
2    (or 3) drops yellow food
-coloring (optional)
1    8 oz carton whipped topping
1    8" graham cracker crust
In a large bowl, combine milk, lemonade and food coloring (if
desired). Fold in whipped topping.  Spoon into crust and chill.

Recipe from the kitchen of my wife, Barbara Cloninger.  Typed in
Meal-Master format by Arthur Cloninger, Huntington, WV.
