Title: CHEWY COCONUT SQUARES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Sugar;divided
1/2 c  Flour
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla or almond extract
1    Egg
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
1/2 c  Coconut
Preheat in 350F oven. Cream butter and 1/4 cup sugar till light and
fluffy. Stir in 1/2 cup flour. Put in greased 8 X 8"pan and bake at
350F for 10 minutes. (You may have to press it out to a thin layer.)
Meanwhile, mix remaining 1/2 cup sugar, 2 tbsp flour, salt, vanilla,
egg, nuts and coconut. Spread evenly over baked dough. Bake 20-25
minutes or till golden. While warm, cut into squares. Source: Joanne
Menard
