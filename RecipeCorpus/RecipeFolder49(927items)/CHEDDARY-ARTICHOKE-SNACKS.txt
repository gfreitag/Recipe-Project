Title: CHEDDARY ARTICHOKE SNACKS
Categories: New import
Yield: 4 Servings
20 sl French bread, toasted
1    Jar (6 oz.) marinated
Artichoke hearts, drained &
Chopped
1/4 c  Bottled roasted red peppers
2 tb Sliced green onion
1 pk (10 oz.) CRACKER BARREL
Extra Sharp Natural cheddar
Cheese, thinly sliced
TOP toast slices with artichokes, peppers, onion and cheese. Place on
cookie sheet.

BROIL 2 to 3 minutes or until cheese begins to melt. Garnish with
additional red pepper and green onion. Makes 20.
