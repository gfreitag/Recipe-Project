Title: CHEESY BAGEL BITES
Categories: Appetizers, Crocker
Yield: 36 Appetizers
3    Bagels, split and toasted
1 tb Prepared mustard
1/3 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 ts Garlic powder
2    Green onions (with tops),
-chopped
Spread bagels with mustard. Mix remaining ingredients; spread to
edges of bagels.

Set oven control to broil and/or 550F. Broil bagels with tops about 5
inches from heat until topping is bubbly, about 1 minute. Cut each
bagel into 6 pieces. 36 appetizers; 30 calories per appetizer.

Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
