Title: CHEESY SCALLOPED POTATES
Categories: Side dishes, Vegetables
4    Potatoes,Boiled,Peeled
Before Boiling
Yield: 6 Servings
:
15 oz Evaporated Milk,Skim
1 cn Cream Of Chicken Soup
14 c  Onions,Raw,Chopped
1 c  Cheddar Cheese,Shredded

Boil peeled and cubed potates until tender.  Drain. Add evaporated
milk, soup and onion and cook until onions are tender. Add cheese and
cook over low heat until melted.
