Title: CHERRY COBBLER-MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Bake it all mix
1/4 c  Sugar
4 ts Cornstarch
1 lb Can tart red cherries --
Pitted
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Milk
Put juice from cherries and 1/4 cup sugar and cornstarch in a
saucepan. Cook until thickened.  Add cherries and butter. Pour
mixture into shallow 1 quart casserole.  Blend Mix and 1 Tablespoon
sugar. Add milk to form a soft dough. Drop by spoonfuls onto the
heated fruit mixture. Bake 15 to 20 minutes in a 425 F. oven. Serve
warm with topping or whipped cream. Yield: 4 servings
