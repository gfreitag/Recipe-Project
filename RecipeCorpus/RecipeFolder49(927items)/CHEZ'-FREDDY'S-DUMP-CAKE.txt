Title: CHEZ' FREDDY'S DUMP CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Yellow cake mix
1 cn Cherry pie filling
1 cn Crushed pineapple
1    Stick oleo (or more)
1 c  Pecans; whole  (or more)
1. Preheat oven to 350Ã¸; grease an oblong cake pan.

2. Using #2 cans of pineapple and cherry pie filling, spread the
crushed pineapple (include the juice) on the bottom of the pan.  Now
spread the cherry pie filling over or around the pineapple.

3. Sprinkle the *dry* cake mix over the top.

4. Place whole (or chopped) pecans over the dry cake mix.

5. On top of the pecans and dry cake mix, place thin slices of butter
over the top...about 1/2" apart.

5. Bake for 1 hour or until golden brown.
