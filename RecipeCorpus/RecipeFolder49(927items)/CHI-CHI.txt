Title: CHI CHI
Categories: Mixed drink, Vodka, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 oz Vodka
1 1/2 oz Coconut cream
4 tb Crushed pineapple
2 c  Crushed ice
Place all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth. Strain into
highball glass or beer goblet and garnish with pineappple slice,
orange, and cherry.

NOTE: Crushed pineapple juice canned in its own juice is preferable
for a less-sweet drink; use pineapple canned in syrup for a sweeter
concoction.
