Title: CHEESY GARLIC BREAD 2
Categories: Breads, Italian, Garlic, Cheese/eggs, Appetizers
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine, soft
4    Cloves garlic, minced
1/4 ts Dried oregano
1    Loaf French Bread, halved
Lengthwise (1 lb)
3 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
In a small bowl, combine butter, garlic, and oregano; spread on cut
sides of bread.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Place on an ungreased
baking sheet.  Broil for 3 minutes or until golden brown. Slice and
serve hot.
