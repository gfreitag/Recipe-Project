Title: CHEWY GRANOLA BARS # 2
Categories: Bar cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Bisquick baking mix
1 1/2 c  Quick-cooking oats
1 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/2 c  Margarine or butter,softened
1    Egg
1/3 c  Semisweet choc. chips,
Or raisins, or mixed nuts
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Mix all ingredients (except chocolate
chips) with fork until moistened (mixture will be crumbly). Stir in
chocolate chips. Press mixture evenly in ungreased rectangular pan
13x9x2". Bake until center is set, 15-17 minutes. Cut into bars,
about 3 x 1-1/2 ", while warm. Makes
27    bars
