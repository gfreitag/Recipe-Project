Title: CHEESE SNACKS
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cayenne Pepper
1/4 lb Butter
1 c  Grated Cheese
