Title: CHEESECAKE(EASY @ DELICIOUS)
Categories: None, Cheesecakes
Yield: 1 Servings
9    Inch graham cracker crust
8 oz Cream cheese, soften
14 oz Can sweeten condensed milk
1/3 c  Lemon juice from
-concentrate
1 ts Vanilla extract
MMMMM--------------------------OPTIONAL-------------------------------
21 oz Can cherry or blueberry pie
-filling
In large bowl, beat cheese until fluffy.  Gradually beat in sweeten
condensed milk until smooth.  Stir in lemon juice and vanilla. Pour
into prepared crust. Chill about 3 hours or until set. Top with
desired amount of pie filling before serving.  Keep chilled!
