Title: CHEF'S BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Gallon
1 c  Oil
2 1/2 c  Onions, finely chopped
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
5 tb Prepared mustard
3 ts Salt
5 tb Worcestershire
5 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Vinegar
3 pt Water
Saute the onions in the oil without browning.  Add all other
ingredients. Simmer for 30+ minutes to thicken.
