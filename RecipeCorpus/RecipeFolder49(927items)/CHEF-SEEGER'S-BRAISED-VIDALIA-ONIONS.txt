Title: CHEF SEEGER'S BRAISED VIDALIA ONIONS
Categories: Vegetables, Low-fat
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lg Onions, sweet
1 tb Brown sugar
1/4 c  Stock, beef; defatted
1/2 tb Parsley; optional
Use Vidalias, Walla-Wallas, Imperial Sweets, Texas 1015's, Kulas, or
another sweet onion. Preheat oven to 350. Peel onions and place in
Pam-sprayed casserole. Salt and pepper to taste. Sprinkle with brown
sugar. Pour beef stock over onions, cover, and bake 1-1/2 hours. To
serve, spoon pan juices over each onion and garnish with julienned
parsley.
