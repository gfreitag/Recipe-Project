Title: CHERRY WALDORF GELATIN
Categories: Salads, Fruits
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Boiling Water
6 oz (1 pk) Cerry Flavor Gelatin
1 c  Cold Water
1/4 c  Lemon Juice
1 1/2 c  Chopped Cored Apples
1 c  Chopped Celery
1 c  Chopped Walnuts Or Pecans
1 x  Lettuce Leaves
1 x  Garnishes*
*   Garnishes to include Apple slices and/or celery leaves.
~------------------------------------------------------
~------------------ In medium bowl, pour boiling water over gelatin;
stir until dissolved. Add cold water and lemon juice; chill until
partially set. Fold in apples, celery and nuts.  Pour into lightly
oiled 6-cup mold or 9-inch square baking pan.  Chill until set, 4 to
6 hours or overnight. Unmold on lettuce leaves and garnish as desired.
