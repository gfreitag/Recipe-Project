Title: CHICKEN & ORANGE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Poultry, Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Scallions Or Green Onions; *
2 tb Lime Juice
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Cooked Chicken; Cut Up
1 c  Green Peas; Cooked
1 c  Mayonnaise Or Salad Dressing
1/4 c  Carrot; Finely Chopped
1/4 c  Celery; Finely Chopped
1/4 c  Fresh Cilantro;FinelySnipped
3 tb Orange Juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon; Ground
1/4 ts Pepper; Freshly Ground
Lettuce Leaves
3    Oranges; **
2    Avocados; ***
*   Finely chop the scallions or green onions including a few green
tops. **  Oranges should be pared and sectioned, or can be unpared,
cut into
wedges. *** Avocados should be pared and cut into wedges.
~------------------------------------------------------
~------------- ~-- Sprinkle the scallions with the lime juice and 1/4
t salt; cover and refrigerate.  Mix the remaining ingredients except
the lettuce, oranges and avocados and refrigerate at least 1 hour.
Spoon chicken mixture onto lettuce.  Garnish with oranges and
avocados; sprinkle with scallions.
