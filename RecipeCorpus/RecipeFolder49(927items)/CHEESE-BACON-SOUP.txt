Title: CHEESE-BACON SOUP
Categories: Soups, Stews
Yield: 6 Servings
6 sl Bacon -- cooked and
Crumbled
1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
1/2 c  Carrot -- chopped
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1/3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Half and half
1 c  Milk
1 cn Clear chicken broth-14.5oz
2 c  Sharp cheddar cheese --
Shredded
Remove bacon from drippings.  Add vegetables to bacon drippings and
cook until tender.  Blend in flour, salt and pepper; stir well.
Gradually stir in half-and-half, milk and chicken broth. Cover and
cook until thickened and bubbly, stirring occasionally. Add cheese,
stirring antil melted. Top each serving with bacon.
