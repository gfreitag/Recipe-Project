Title: CHERRY JUBILEES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
Fast & Fancy Cookie Recipe
Stir 1/2 cup drained, chopped maraschino cherries (from one 10-oz
jar) into dough. Roll out 1/8" thick; cut out using 2 1/2" scalloped
round cutter. Bake 10 minutes. In small bowl stir 1/2 cup
confectioners' sugar & 1 Tbls. maraschino cherry juice until smooth;
spoon by 1/4 tsp. over centers of cookies. Top each with maraschino
cherry half. Submitted By THESERVS@GATE.NET (THOMAS E. HAUG)  On
