Title: CHEESECAKE ALA LINDA-BAH-LINDA
Categories: Cheese, Cheesecake, Dairy, Deserts, Favorite
FILLING:
Yield: 8 Servings  :
4 ea 8 oz Phila Brand cream
cheese -- room temp
:
2 ea 16 oz sour cream -- room
temp
:
6 lg eggs -- room temp
2 c  sugar
1 TB vanilla
1/2 TB cinnamon (my addition)
CRUST:
:
1 1/3 c  vanilla wafers -- crushed
1/4 c  sugar
1/2 c  butter -- melted

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Combine ingredients for crust and
press into botton of greased 10" springform pan. Mix the filling
ingredients and beat until well mixed, do not overbeat! Pour filling
mixture in pan. Bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes.
