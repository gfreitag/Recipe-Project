Title: CHICKEN & GARDEN VEGETABLE TWIST
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Olive oil
1 c  Broccoli, chopped
1 c  Carrots, thinly sliced
1 cn Cream of celery soup
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 c  Skim milk
2 tb Parmesan cheese, grated
2 c  Corkscrew macaroni, cooked
2 c  Chicken, cooked, cubed
1 ts Basil leaves, dried, crushed
In skillet, in hot oil, cook broccoli and carrots with basil and
garlic powder until tender, stirring often.

Stir in soup, milk and cheese; add macaroni and chicken. Heat
through, stirring often.

Nutritional values per serving:  Calories 350, Protein 29g,
Carbohydrate 34g, Fat 1g, Cholesterol 65mg, Sodium 470mg
