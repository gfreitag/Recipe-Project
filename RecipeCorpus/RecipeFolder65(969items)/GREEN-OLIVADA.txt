Title: GREEN OLIVADA
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Pitted green olives
1 1/2 tb Capers, rinsed and drained
1    Garlic clove
Pepper to taste
3 tb Extravirgin olive oil
1 ts Grated lemon zest
2 lb Small Yellow Finn or Yukon
-Gold potatoes,
Place olives and capers in a food processor. Pass the garlic clove
through a garlic press onto the olives and capers. Add the pepper.
With the motor running, slowly pour the oil through the feed tube and
process until smooth. Transfer to a bowl, stir in the lemon zest and
adjust seasoning. To serve: Slice potatoes into 1/8- inch slices and
spread each slice with some of the olivada. Arrange on a platter.
Yield: 16 potato slices. ______ Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #
