Title: GREEN SALAD
Categories: Cajun, Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Iceberg lettuce, torn into
- 1-inch pieces
1 md Tomato, chopped
1 c  Broccoli flowerets
1 c  Mushrooms, sliced
1 c  Carrots, grated
1/4 c  Scallions
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1 c  Spicy Vinaigrette
Combine lettuce, tomato, broccoli, mushrooms, carrots, scallion and
walnuts in a large salad bowl.  Toss to distribute evenly. Pour Spicy
Vinaigrette Dressing over.

Prep. time: 30 minutes
