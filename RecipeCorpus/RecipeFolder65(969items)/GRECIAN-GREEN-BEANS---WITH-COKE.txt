Title: GRECIAN GREEN BEANS - WITH COKE
Categories: Vegetables, Beans
Yield: 1 Quart
32 oz Whole green beans, small,
- drained (2-16 oz cans)
2 md Shallots, or small onions,
- peeled, thinly sliced,
- and separated into
- individual rings
2 md Cloves garlic, minced
1/4 c  Fresh parsley, chopped
2 tb Sugar
2 ts Oregano leaves
2 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  COCA-COLA
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Vinegar
In a large bowl, combine the garlic, parsley, sugar, oregano, mustard,
salt, Coca-Cola, olive oil, and vinegar, stirring until the sugar is
dissolved. Add the beans and shallots/onions, toss lightly with a
fork. Pack into 1 quart jar. Cover and refrigerate several hours or
overnight for the flavours to blend. Serve chilled or as a hot
vegetable with steak, hamburger, or meat loaf.
