Title: GRILLED LIME TORTILLAS **
Categories: Breads, Grill, Mexican, Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
4 tb Butter or margarine
1 ts Grated lime peel
4 ts Lime juice
4    10" flour tortillas
Heat margarine until melted. Stir in lime peel and lime juice. Brush
on bith sides of tortillas. Grill tortillas 5-6" from medium coals,
turning once, until golden brown. Sprinkle with salt if desired. Cool
slightly; break into pieces. NOTE: If there isn't room on your grill
for 2 large tortillas, cut them into 6 wedges before grilling.
