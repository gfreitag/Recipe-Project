Title: GRAPENUTS
Categories: Breakfast, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
3 1/2 c  Graham flour
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
2 c  Buttermilk
2 ts Vanilla
Mix all ingredients, spread on a jellyroll pan and bake at 350
degrees for 1 hour. Let cool overnight then break into pieces and
grind in a food processor until grapenut consistency(you know, the
cereal). Toast in a 250 degree oven for 35 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Good on ice cream, as a cereal or just to munch on.
