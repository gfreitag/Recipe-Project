Title: GRAVLAX SAUCE (COLD MUSTARD SAUCE)
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Mustard
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Vinegar
6 tb Oil
6 tb Fermented cream
Dill
Mix mustard, sugar and vinegar in a bowl. Add oil a little at the time
until well blended. The sauce will thicken rapidly and must be stirred
viorously. Add fermented cream and a lot of finely chopped dill.
