Title: GREAT CHOCOLATE SAUCE
Categories: Desserts, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/2 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
In saucepan, mix cocoa & sugar. Blend in milk. Over med heat, heat to
boiling, stirring constantly, 5 minutes, until thickened. Cool; stir
in vanilla. Serve at room temp.
