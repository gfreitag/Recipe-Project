Title: GRASSHOPPER PARFAITS
Categories: American, Chocolate, Alcohol
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1 tb Granulated sugar
1 pk Cream cheese -- softened
1 1/4 c  Sweetened condensed milk --
14 oz Can
3 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Green creme de menthe
Liqueur
2 tb White creme de cacao
Liqueur
12 ea Cream filled chocolate
Sandwich cookies -- coarsely
Chopped
In small mixer bowl, whip cream until soft. Add sugar; beat until
stiff. In large mixer bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy; gradually
beat in sweetened condensed milk. STIR in lemon juice, and liqueurs.
Fold in whipped cream; chill 20 minutes.

Do not stir. In parfait glasses, layer 1 tablespoon cookies and 1/4
cup filling. Repeat layers. Top with remaining cookies. Chill 4 hours.

Variations: Coffee: substitute coffee flavored liqueur for creme de
menthe. Brandy Alexander: substitute 1/3 brandy for creme de menthe.
Orange: Omit creme de cacao and substitute 1/4 cup orange liqueur for
creme de menthe.
