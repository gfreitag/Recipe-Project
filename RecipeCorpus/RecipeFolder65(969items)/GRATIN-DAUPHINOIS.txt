Title: GRATIN DAUPHINOIS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
3 md Potatoes; up to 4
-peeled and thinly sliced
2 c  18% cream; or milk
2 ea Eggs
2 oz Emmenthal cheese; up to 3 oz
-grated
The accompanying dish to Roast Herbed Lamb from Lisette Dionne, the
mother-in-law of Jean Charest (A conservative leadership candidate
who may be Canada's next prime minister).

Make two layers of potato slices in a greased casserole, sprinkling
cheese, salt, pepper and freshly grated nutmeg on each layer. Whisk
cream and eggs together and pour over potatoes. Bake in preheated
350F oven 1 hour or til potatoes are tender.

Source: The Toronto Sun posted by Anne MacLellan
