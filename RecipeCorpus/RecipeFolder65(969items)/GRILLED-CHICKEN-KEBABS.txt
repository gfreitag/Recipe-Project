Title: GRILLED CHICKEN KEBABS
Categories: Poultry, Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Jar Chicken Tonight Cooking
-Sauce for Chicken
-Sweet and Sour
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
1 lb Boneless chicken breasts,
-cut into 1" cubes
12 oz Bacon strips, cut in half
Hot, cooked rice
In a medium saucepan, thoroughly combine sauce, Worcestershire sauce
and lemon juice. Remove 1 cup sauce mixture and place in a sealable
plastic bag with chicken cubes. Marinate chicken in the refrigerator
for 1 hour or longer. Preheat grill or broiler. Remove chicken from
marinade; wrap with bacon and place on skewers. Discard remaining
marinade. Grill or broil 10 to 15 minutes or until chicken is
thoroughly cooked. Heat remaining sauce in a saucepan and serve with
chicken. Serve kebabs over rice.
