Title: GREEN BEANS IN TOMATO VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Beans, Dressings
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Green beans, trimmed
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 lg Garlic clove(s), minced
1/4 c  Corn oil
2 tb Basil, minced
2 tb Flat-leaf parsley, minced
Salt and pepper to taste
1 lb Plum tomatoes
-seeded and chopped
1/4 c  Red onion(s), minced
Steam the green beans for 5 min, until they're crisp-tender. Plunge
them into cold water to stop the cooking; drain them well.

Put the vinegar, mustard, and garlic into a large bowl and whisk them
together. Gradually whisk in the oil. Add the basil, parsley, and
salt and pepper and stir well. Add the green beans, tomatoes, and
onion and toss everything together. Serve at room temperature.
