Title: GREEN ZUCCHINI SAUCE
Categories: Digest, Nov.
Yield: 1 Servings
1 md Zucchini
10 fl Oz ff plain yoghurt
1    Clove garlic (or more)
1 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Dried basil
x  Salt and fresh ground black
Pepper to taste
Just liquidize the whole lot and serve. It's also great on baked
potatoes.
