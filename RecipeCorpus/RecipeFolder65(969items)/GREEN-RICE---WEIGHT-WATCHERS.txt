Title: GREEN RICE - WEIGHT WATCHERS
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese, Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Uncooked Rice
2 cn Cream Of Mushroom Soup
1 bn Green Onions -- sliced
3/4 c  Celery -- sliced
1 lb Bag Broccoli -- chopped
4 tb Diet Margarine -- (melted)
8 oz Shredded Cheese -- (Velveeta
Works grea
Combine rice and melted margarine.  Add onions, cheese, celery,
broccoli (frozen) and soup.  Stir to mix. Place in 2 qt casserole and
bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until rice is cooked (Cover while
baking).

Makes 8 servings:
