Title: GRIDDLE CAKE(WELSH)
Categories: Cakes, Brakefast, Welsh
Yield: 6 Cakes
8 oz Flour
3 oz Butter
2 oz Castor sugar
1    Egg
2 oz Currants
1/2 ts Bicarbonate of soda
2 tb Milk
TEISEN GRI Sift the flour, bicarbonate of soda and salt together. Rub
the butter into the flour and add the sugar and fruit. Beat the egg
and add with enough milk to make a soft dough. Turn on to a floured
board and roll out to a thickness of 1/2 inch. Cook on a hot greased
griddle.
