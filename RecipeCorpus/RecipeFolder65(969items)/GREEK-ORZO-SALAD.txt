Title: GREEK ORZO SALAD
Categories: Cheese, Pasta, Salads, Sauces and, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
-----Dressing-----
2 ts Dill
2    Cloves Garlic -- minced
1/4 c  Olive Oil
3 tb Lemon Juice
2 tb Red Wine Vinegar
Pepper -- to taste
-----Salad-----
1 c  Orzo
4    Roma Tomatoes -- seeded and
Diced
1    Cucumber -- peel/seed/dice
3    Green Onions -- sliced
8 oz Feta Cheese -- crumbled
Whisk all ingredients together, then chill.

Boil orzo in water until barely done.
