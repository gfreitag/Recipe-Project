Title: GREEK DEVILED EGGS WITH OUZO
Categories: Appetizers, Ethnic
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Hardboiled eggs
1 tb Ouzo ( Greek liquer )
1 tb Chopped parsley
1/4 ts Fresh chopped mint
3 tb Mayonnaisse
6    Black olives chopped
Mash egg yolks with ouzo into a smooth paste. Combine parsley , mint
and mayo.Blend both mixtures till smooth. Fill egg whites with
mixture and garnish with chopped black olives.The anise flavored
liquer make thses eggs fragrant and delicious.
