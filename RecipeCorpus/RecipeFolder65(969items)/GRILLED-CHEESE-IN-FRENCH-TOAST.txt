Title: GRILLED CHEESE IN FRENCH TOAST
Categories: Sandwiches
2    eggs -- beaten
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  milk
1/4 c  dry sherry
1/4 ts Worchestershire sauce
8 sl white bread
or whole wheat bread
:
4 sl Cheddar cheese -- -thickly
sliced
or Monterey jack cheese
:
:

In a shallow bowl, combine eggs, milk, sherry and Worcestershire.
Assemble 4 cheese sandwiches, then dip each in the egg mixture and
grill slowly in butter, turning once to get both sides golden brown.
