Title: GRILLED SEAFOOD~ MEAT~ POULTY SEASONING
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Salt
1 tb Granulated garlic
Or powdered garlic
1 tb Fresh ground black pepper
1 tb Paprika
3/4 ts Granulated onion
Or powdered onion
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1/4 ts Dried thyme -- crumbled
1/4 ts Dried oregano -- crumbled
Use this on roasted or grilled seafood, meat and poultry. Makes about
3/4 cup. Mix all ingredients. Store in glass jar.
