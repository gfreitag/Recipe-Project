Title: GRAPEFRUIT & POPPY SEED BREAD ABM
Categories: Breads
Yield: 24 Ounces
MMMMM-----------------BEST BREAD MACHINE CB EVER----------------------
2 3/4 ts Yeast
2 1/2 c  Bread flour
3 tb Wheat germ
3/4 c  Wheat bran
3 tb Powdered milk
1/4 c  Poppy seeds
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1/2 tb Salt
3 tb Grapefruit peel, grated
1 1/2 tb Oil
1/4 c  Grapefruit juice
1 c  Water; warm
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order. Set "baking control" at 10 o'clock. Select "white bread" and
push Start. In hot & humid weather, use 1/8 c less water.

Could you make a more nutritious, refreshing commuter breakfast than
thick slices of this bread, either plain or spread with butter, cream
cheese, or marmalade?  It also makes an unusual but delicious turkey
or chicken sandwich.
