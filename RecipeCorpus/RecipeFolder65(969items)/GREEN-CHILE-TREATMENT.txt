Title: GREEN CHILE TREATMENT
Categories: Marinades, Bar-b-q
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-------------------BEEF,POULTRY,PORK,LAMB------------------------
4 oz Mild or hot green chiles
2 ts Mild ground chiles
-=OR=- chili powder
2 tb Vegetable oil
Combine all ingredients in a food processor. Process until smooth.
Makes about 1/3 cup.
