Title: GRANDMA'S MEAT LOAF
Categories: Crs, Totest, Post, Chopmeat
Yield: 8 Servings
1    Egg, beaten
1/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
3 sl Bacon, cut in 1/4" pieces
1 sm Chopped onion
2 oz Cubed cheddar
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 1/2    Lean ground beef
1/2 lb Bulk pork sausage
Preheat oven to 450F.

Combine. Form into loaf and bake until no pink remains.
