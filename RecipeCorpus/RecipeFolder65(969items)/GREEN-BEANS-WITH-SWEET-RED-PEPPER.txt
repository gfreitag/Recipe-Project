Title: GREEN BEANS WITH SWEET RED PEPPER
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 lb Green beans, trimmed
1/4 c  Diced sweet red pepper
2 tb Toasted sesame seeds
1/2 ts Each sesame oil and lemon
-juice
Salt and pepper
In saucepan of boiling water, cook green beans for 4 minutes or just
until tender-crisp; drain well.

Toss beans with red pepper, sesame seeds, sesame oil, lemon juice,
and salt and pepper to taste. Makes 2 servings. Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source: The Canadian Living 20th Anniversary
Cookbook.
