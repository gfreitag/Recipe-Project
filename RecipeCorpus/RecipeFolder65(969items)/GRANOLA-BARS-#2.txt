Title: GRANOLA BARS #2
Categories: Bars, Deserts
Yield: 6 Servings
5 1/2 c  Butter, softened
2 3/4 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg
1/4 c  Molasses
1/2    Orange (rind
And all) pureed
1 3/4 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 c  Rolled oats
1 c  Coconut
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Nuts (your choice)
Cream together sugar, butter, egg, molasses and pureed orange.
Combine all dry ingredients and mix thoroughly with butter mixture.

Press into large flat pan (I just used a cookie sheet) and bake 10
minutes at 350 degrees or until browned.

Cut while warm, but cool before removing from pan. Walt MM
