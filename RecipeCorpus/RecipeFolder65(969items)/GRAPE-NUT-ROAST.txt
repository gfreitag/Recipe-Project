Title: GRAPE NUT ROAST
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Grape nuts
2 ea Beaten eggs
1 1/2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Chopped nuts
1 ts Grated onion
1 c  Chopped celery
1 ts Salt
2 tb Margarine
Combine all ingredients.  Let stand for 20 minutes. Pour into a
greased loaf pan & bake at 350F for 35 minutes.

From Sandee Eveland
