Title: GREEN GAGE PLUM SALAD
Categories: Salad
Yield: 10 Servings
2 cn (16-oz) green gage or purple
-plums
1/2 c  Syrup from plums
2 c  Grape juice
2 pk (3-oz) lemon gelatin
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Lemon juice
2 pk (3-oz) cream cheese
Heat grape juice and add to gelatin, with syrup, mashed plums, salt
and lemon juice. Cool. Pour 1/2 gelatin mixture into 1-1/2 quart mold
and chill until firm. When remaining gelatin thickens like raw egg
white, beat in cream cheese until frothy. Pour over firm gelatin
mixture and chill until firm.
