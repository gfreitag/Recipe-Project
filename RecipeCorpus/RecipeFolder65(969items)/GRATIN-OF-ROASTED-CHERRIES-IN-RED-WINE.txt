Title: GRATIN OF ROASTED CHERRIES IN RED WINE
Categories: Fruit, Sauce, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Dry red wine
3/4 c  Granulated sugar
1    3-inch cinnamon stick
2    Whole cloves
1    3-inch strip lemon zest
(yellow part of peel)
2 lb Bing cherries, stemmed and
Pitted
1. Combine wine, sugar, cinnamon stick, cloves and lemon zest in a
saucepan.  Bring to a boil, reduce the heat and simmer 5 minutes. 2.
Prepare the cherries and place in a shallow baking dish. Pour the
syrup over the cherries and bake in a preheated 350-degree oven 30
minutes. 3. Let the cherries cool a bit in the syrup. Serve warm,
room temperature or chilled with ice cream or sweetened creme fraiche.
