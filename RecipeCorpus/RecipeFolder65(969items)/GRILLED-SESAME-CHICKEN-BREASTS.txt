Title: GRILLED SESAME CHICKEN BREASTS
Categories: Chicken
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Unsweetened white grape
-juice
1/4 c  Reduced-sodium soy sauce
1/4 c  Dry white wine
1 tb Sesame seeds
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1 lb Boneless chicken breast
-halves, skinned
Combine first 7 ingredients in a shallow dish and mix well. Add the
chicken, turning to coat; marinate, covered, in refrigerator for at
least 2 hours. Prepare the grill. Remove chicken from marinade;
reserve marinade. Grill 4 to 5 inches from medium-hot coals, turning
and basting frequently with marinade, about 15 minutes total.
