Title: GRANOLA POPCORN
Categories: Snacks, Appetizers, Desserts, Popcorn, Diabetic
Yield: 1 Batch
MMMMM-----------------------DEBBIE CARLSON----------------------------
1/4 c  Butter
3 tb Honey **
3 tb Brown sugar **
1/2 c  Corn, popped
1 c  Toasted nuts
1 c  Rolled oats
1 c  Toasted coconut
1 c  Raisins

In a heavy saucepan, put the butter, honey and brown sugar. cook
over a moderate heat until melted.  Pour over the popped corn, nuts,
oats, coconut and raisins. Bake at 300 F for 30 Minutes.
