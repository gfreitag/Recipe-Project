Title: GRANDMOTHER'S BROWN SUGAR COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Shortening
4 ea Eggs
5 c  Flour
3/4 ts Baking soda
3/4 ts Cream tartar
1 ts Vanilla
Flour to make a soft dough. Roll, cut and bake in quick oven.

Note: Quick oven is probably around 425 F.

Source: Mrs. E. J. Peterson, Ganges Grange, Richland County, OH
