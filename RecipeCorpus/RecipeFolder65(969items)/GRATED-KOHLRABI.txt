Title: GRATED KOHLRABI
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 1 Servings
3    Kohlrabi; trimmed & peeled
Salt
4 tb Butter
Sweet marjoram
Grate kohlrabi, toss lightly with salt and allow to sit in a colander
for 30 minutes.  Squeeze water out. Melt butter in a saute pan and
stir in the kohlrabi. Cover and cook over medium heat for 10 minutes,
stirring occasionally.  Uncover and cook another 2 minutes until
lightly browned.
