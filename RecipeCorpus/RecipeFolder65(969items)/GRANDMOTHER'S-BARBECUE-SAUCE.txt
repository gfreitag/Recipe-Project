Title: GRANDMOTHER'S BARBECUE SAUCE
Categories: Meat, Miscellaneo, Sauces and
Yield: 1 Servings
1 qt Vinegar
64 oz Bottle Ketchup
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Lemon Juice
2 tb Ground Red Pepper
2 tb Ground Black Pepper
1 ts Salt
Place all the ingredients in a saucepan.  Simmer on low heat until the
sauce is as thick as you want it. 2 Tbsp. liquid smoke can also be
added during the last
30    minutes or so if desired.
