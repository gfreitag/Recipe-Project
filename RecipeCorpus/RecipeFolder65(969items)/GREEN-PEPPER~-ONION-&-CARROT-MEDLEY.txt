Title: GREEN PEPPER~ ONION & CARROT MEDLEY
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
8    Green peppers,medium
4    Onions,medium
1 pk Carrots(16oz)
3 tb Salad oil
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Basil
1/2 ts Black pepper,coarsely ground
1. Cut green peppers into slices. Thinly slice onions. Cut carrots
into matchstick-thin strips.
2. In 8-quart Dutch oven over medium-high heat, in hot salad oil, cook
green peppers, onions, carrots, salt, basil, and black pepper,
stirring, until vegetables are tender-crisp.
