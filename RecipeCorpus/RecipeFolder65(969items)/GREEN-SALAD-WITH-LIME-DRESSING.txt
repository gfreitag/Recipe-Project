Title: GREEN SALAD WITH LIME DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Fruits, Vegetables, Low-fat
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Lime
1/2 c  Miracle Whip Light Dressing
2 tb Milk
1 tb Honey
2 c  Packed fresh spinach leaves
2 c  Packed Boston lettuce
2 c  Halved strawberries
1 cn (284mL) Mandarin oranges
Grate rind and sqeeze juice from lime. Combine rind, lime juice,
dressing, milk and honey. Cover and chill. Toss remaining ingredients
in a large bowl; drizzle with dressing mixture.

Source: Miracle Whip Light Dressing bottle.
