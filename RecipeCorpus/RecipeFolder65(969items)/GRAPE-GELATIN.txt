Title: GRAPE GELATIN
Categories: Desserts, June 1996 i
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Concord grape juice
3 1/2 c  White grape juice
2 pk Unflavored gelatin
1/4 c  Sugar
Combine grape juices. Pour 1 cup juice into another bowl; sprinkle
gelatin over. Let stand to soften, 5 minutes. Bring sugar and 1 cup
juice to a boil over medium high heat, stirring to dissolve. Add to
gelatin mixture; whisk to dissolve gelatin. Stir in remaining 2 cups
juice into gelatin mixture. Pour into a 9x13 inch Pyrex pan, cover,
and refrigerate to set, about 2 hours. When ready to serve, cut
gelatin into 1-inch cubes and serve in tall glasses.
