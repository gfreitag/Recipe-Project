Title: GREEN RICE
Categories: Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  Chicken stock
1 c  Wild rice; rinsed
1 c  Long-grain brown rice
1 tb Butter
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 sm Sweet green pepper; diced
1/2 c  Parsley; fresh, chopped
6    Green onions, chopped
1 cl Garlic; chopped
In 12-cup casserole, cover and microwave stock and wild rice at High
for 10 minutes. Stir in brown rice; cover and microwave at Medium
(50%) for 45 minutes or until tender. Let stand for 15 minutes. In
microwaveable dish melt butter with oil. Add green pepper, parsley,
onions and garlic; microwave at High for 2 minutes or until slightly
softened. Stir into rice.
