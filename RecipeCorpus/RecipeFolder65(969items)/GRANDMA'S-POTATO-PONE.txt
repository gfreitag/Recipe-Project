Title: GRANDMA'S POTATO PONE
Categories: Creole, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  White sugar
1 c  Cane molasses
1/2 c  Butter or margarine,melted
1 c  Heavy cream
4    Eggs,well beaten
1/2 c  Flour,all-purpose
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Nutmeg
3 c  Potatoes,grated raw
3 c  Yams
1. Combine all ingredients, adding potatoes or yams last.
2. Turn mixture into greased ovenproof skillet; bake in preheated
350'F. oven 30 minutes, or until done.
3. When cool, slice as you do cake.
