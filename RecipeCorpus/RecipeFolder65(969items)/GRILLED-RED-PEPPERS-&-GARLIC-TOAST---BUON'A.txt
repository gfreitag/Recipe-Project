Title: GRILLED RED PEPPERS & GARLIC TOAST - BUON'A
Categories: Appetizers, Italian
Yield: 6 Servings
Red peppers
MMMMM------------------------GARLIC TOAST-----------------------------
1/4 c  Olive oil
2    Garlic cloves, sliced
Grilled Red Peppers: Select sufficient number of red peppers to serve
6 as appetizers. Cut peppers into quarters and broil until skin
blackens. Place peppers in a plastic bag and let cool for 15 minutes.
Skin will peel right off. Slice and preserve in oil for up to 3
weeks. Garlic Toast: In a small pot, combine olive oil and sliced
garlic. Simmer for 5 minutes. Brush 12 slices of bread with garlic
oil and bake at 400F(200C) for 10 minutes or until pale gold.
