Title: GREEN RICE 2
Categories: Grains, Side dish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Onion; Chopped
1 c  Celery; Chopped
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Cheese Whiz
1 cn Chilies
1 c  Rice; Uncooked
Saute onion and celery in butter until clear. Combine: all of the
ingredients and pour into a casserole (9x13) and bake for 45 minutes
at 350~. DELICIOUS !
