Title: GREEN ONION DRESSING
Categories: Creole, Condiment
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Egg yolk
1 c  Chopped green onions
3 tb White vinegar
1 tb Creole mustard
1/4 ts Chopped garlic clove
1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts White pepper
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Vegetable oil
Combine all ingredients in screw-top jar; shake well.
