Title: GRAPE JELLY
Categories: Jams, Breakfast, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Grape juice [white or purple
3 c  Sugar
4 oz Certo
Put juice and sugar in large sauce pan.Bring to a boil on high heat
stirring constantly. Add certo & bring to boil again and boil 1/2 min,
stirring constantly. Remove from heat and skim. Put in bottles.
