Title: GREEN CURRY PASTE/NAM PRIK KAENG KHIEW
Categories: Misc
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Cumin seeds
1 ts Coriander seeds
6    Fresh green chilies,
Chopped
1 tb Chopped lemon grass
1 ts Chopped coriander root
1 tb Chopped shallots
1 tb Chopped garlic
1 ts Chopped galangal
7    Peppercorns
1 ts Salt
1 ts Shrimp paste
Place the cumin and coriander seeds in a pan, without adding any oil.
Dry-fry them, stirring, for 1-2 minutes until they are aromatic and
slightly browned. Pound them with the remaining ingredients to
produce a fine paste.
