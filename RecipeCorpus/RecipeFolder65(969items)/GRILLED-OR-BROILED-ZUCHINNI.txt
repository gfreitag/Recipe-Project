Title: GRILLED OR BROILED ZUCHINNI
Categories: Vegetables, Mimi
Yield: 4 Servings
EVA ESTES BXGT29B
4 sm Zuchinni
4 tb Butter
1/2 ts Lemon-pepper seasoning
ds Garlic salt
2 tb Parmesan cheese
Wash, dry and halve lengthwise the zucchini and arrange in a
single layer on a greased broiler pan or hinged grill. Score zucchini
diagonally. Melt butter and add lemon-pepper seasoning and garlic
salt. Brush on cut side of zuchinni. Sprinkle with cheese.  Broil 8
minutes or grill until tender and cheese browns. No need to turn.
