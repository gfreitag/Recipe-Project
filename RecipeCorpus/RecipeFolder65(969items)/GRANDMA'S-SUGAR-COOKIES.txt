Title: GRANDMA'S SUGAR COOKIES
Categories: Desserts, Cookies
Yield: 66 Cookies
1 c  Shortening (part butter)
2 c  Sugar
2 lg Eggs
5 c  Flour
2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
2 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Milk
Lighter and fluffier than most sugar cookies. Preheat oven to 375
degrees. Beat shortening, suugar and eggs together until light and
lemon-colored. Add the rest of the ingredients alternately with milk.
Drop cookies onto ungreased cookie sheet with a teaspoon or #40
dipper. Bake until just light brown. Cool on racks and frost with
favorite frosting. Sprinkle with colored for the holidays.
