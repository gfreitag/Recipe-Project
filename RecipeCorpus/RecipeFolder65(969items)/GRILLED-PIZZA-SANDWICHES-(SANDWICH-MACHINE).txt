Title: GRILLED PIZZA SANDWICHES (SANDWICH MACHINE)
Categories: Sandwiches, Pizza
Yield: 6 Servings
2/3 c  Pizza sauce (canned)
8    Slices bread
4    Large slices salami
4    Slices cheese
1    Garlic powder
1    Butter or margarine
Spread pizza sauce on one side of each bread slice. Top one slice with
salami slice then cheese slice. Sprinkle with garlic powder. Top with
second slice of bread, sauce side down. Butter both sides.

Grill

From Better Homes & Gardens, April 1959, by Mrs. J. C. Baker, Solon,
Ohio Posted by: Sheila Exner - October 1991
