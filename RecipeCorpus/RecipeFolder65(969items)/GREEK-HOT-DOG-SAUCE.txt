Title: GREEK HOT DOG SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 24 Servings
3 lb Ground beef
3 tb Cooking oil
3    Chopped onions
2    Cloves chopped garlic
2 tb Chili powder
2 tb Cumin powder
2 tb Paprika
1 tb Hot pepper flakes
1 1/2 ts Salt
3 c  Cold water
Fry ground beef, onions and garlic in oil until beef is done. Then add
remaining ingredients, simmer uncovered till thick. (2 - 4 hours)
Note: I use 1 teaspoon of hot pepper flakes.
