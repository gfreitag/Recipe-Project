Title: GRANDMAS OHIO FARM CHILI
Categories: Soups, Vegetables, Chili, Beef
Yield: 1 Servings
3 cn Tomato soup
4 cn Water
1 lb Hamburger
1 lb Hot sausage
1    Large onion (minced)
3 cn Kidney or chili beans
2 tb Cumin
3    Bay leaves
3 ts Tabasco
1 ts Salt
Brown hamburger & sausage, drain off grease, then dump it all in a
big pot, bring it to a boil, then simmer for 45 minutes.

Serve over a scoop of mashed potatoes in a large bowl.
