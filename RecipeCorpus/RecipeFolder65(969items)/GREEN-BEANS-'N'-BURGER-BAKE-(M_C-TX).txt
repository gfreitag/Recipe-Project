Title: GREEN BEANS 'N' BURGER BAKE (M_C-TX)
Categories: Meats, Beef, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 md Onion, chopped
1 1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 c  Cooked rice
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1 lb Cut green beans, drained
3 tb Chopped pimento
10 3/4 oz Cream of celery soup
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Lemon juice
Crumble beef into heated skillet.  Add onion.  Cook over moderate
heat, stirring occasionally, until meat loses red color. Drain off
grease. Add garlic salt, pepper, rice and one-half cup of the cheese.
Turn mixture into greased, shallow 2-quart casserole. Sprinkle beans
and pimento on top. Combine soup, mayonnaise, milk and lemon juice.
Mix until smooth. Pour soup mixture over beans.  Sprinkle remaining
one- half cup cheese on top. Bake, uncovered, in oven preheated to
350 for 30 minutes. Makes Six servings.
