Title: GREEK SUNDAY BREAD
Categories: Abm, Posted-mm
Yield: 8 Servings
2 ts Yeast
3 1/2 c  Bread flour
1/8 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Orange peel -- grated
1/4 ts Anise seed
1/4 ts Cinnamon
3 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 1/2    Egg
4 tb Butter
1 1/4 c  Milk
Put all ingredients in bread machine and turn on.

: "The Bread Machine Cookbook," Donna German
