Title: Pasta & Red Bean Salad
Categories: Salads, Pasta, Vegetarian, Appetizers, Beans
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Tofu mayonnaise
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Coriander
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Sage
Salt & pepper
2 c  Rotini, or ziti
2 c  Red kidney beans, cooked
1 c  Diced zucchini
1 sm Green bell pepper, diced
1 md Tomato, chopped
1/3 c  Chopped green olives
Cook pasta till al dente.  Rinse & drain.  Put in large bowl & add rest of
the ingredients.  Mix thoroughly & serve at room temperature.

Nava Atlas, "Vegetariana"
