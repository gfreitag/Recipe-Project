Title: Grape Nut Roast
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Grated onion
1 c  Chopped celery
1 ts Salt
2 tb Margarine
1 c  Grape nuts
2 ea Beaten eggs
1 1/2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Chopped nuts
Combine all ingredients.  Let stand for 20 minutes.  Pour into a greased
loaf pan & bake at 350F for 35 minutes.

From Sandee Eveland
