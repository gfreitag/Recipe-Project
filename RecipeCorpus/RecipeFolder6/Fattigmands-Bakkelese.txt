Title: Fattigmands Bakkelese
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Cardamom
Citron or orange peel; crus
-hed
1 tb Lemon juice
Flour; to roll
10 ea Egg yolks; beaten with
10 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Cream; thick
4 ea Egg whites; stiffly beaten
1/2 c  Butter; melted - strained
Roll and cut. Bake in deep fat.

Note: Calls for 1 to 2 t. cardamom and a little crushed citron or orange
peel. Fat at 375 F. ?

Source: Mrs. Dan Beardsley, Braconville Grange, Trumbull County, OH
