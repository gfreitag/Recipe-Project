Title: Pineapple Nut Cake
Categories: Cakes, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 c  White sugar
14 oz Can crushed pineapple
2 1/2 c  All purpose flour
1/2 ts Salt
Mix above ingredients together & Bake at 325F for 30 to 40 minutes.  Ice
with nut icing.
