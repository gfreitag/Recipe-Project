Title: Baked Macaroni & Tofu
Categories: Vegetarian, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Cheesy gravy
4 c  Cooked elbow macaroni
1 c  Mashed tofu
Preheat oven to 350F.  Combine tofu & macaroni.  Place in a baking dish.
Pour gravy over the mixture & bake 20 minutes.
