Title: Tofu Ricotta Cheese
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
4 tb Oil
2 ts Basil
1 ts Garlic
2 lb Firm tofu
1/3 c  Lemon juice
4 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
Grind tofu through a food mill or mash with fork. Mix in other ingredients.
Use in recipes that call for a Ricotta style filling.
