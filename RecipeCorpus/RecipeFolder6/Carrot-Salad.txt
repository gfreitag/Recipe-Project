Title: Carrot Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ea Onion, cut in rings
1 ea Green pepper, large
3/4 c  Sugar, white
5 c  Carrots, sliced
1 c  Tomato soup
3/4 c  Vinegar, white
1/2 c  Oil
1 ts Mustard, dry
Parboil carrots for about 12 - 15 minutes.  Add rest of ingredients and
marinate in refrigerator at least 24 hours.  Serve chilled
