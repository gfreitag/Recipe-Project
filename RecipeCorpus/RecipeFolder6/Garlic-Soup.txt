Title: Garlic Soup
Categories: Soups/stews, Appetizers, Vegetarian, Garlic
Yield: 6 servings
2 lg Bulbs garlic, peeled
1/2 ts Thyme
1 ds Cayenne
Salt to taste
1 1/2 qt Water
4 ea Potatoes, diced
1 ea Carrot, diced
2 ea Celery stalks, diced
1 ea Onion, diced
Bring water to a boil.  Add vegetables, garlic & spices.  Cook over medium
heat for 20 to 30 minutes.  Puree & serve.
