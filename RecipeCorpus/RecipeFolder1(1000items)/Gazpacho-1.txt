Title: Gazpacho 1
Categories: Soups/stews, Vegetables, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
3 x  Garlic, cloves
1/2 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Chili powder
4 tb Olive oil
12 oz V8 or tomato juice
1 x  Zucchini
1 x  Green pepper, small
1 x  Onion, small
1 x  Cucumber
1 x  Tomato, large
Finely chop vegetables and garlic ( or run through food processor or
blender for 10 seconds ).  Add vegetables and spices to juice and
refrigerate.
