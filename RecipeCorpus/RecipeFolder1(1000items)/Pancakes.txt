Title: Pancakes
Categories: Breads, Londontowne, Breakfast, Pancakes
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Egg
4 tb Shortening
1 1/4 c  Milk
3 c  Flour
1 tb Sugar
4 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
Sift dry ingredients together twice. Mix with wooden spoon. Add egg,
shortening, and milk to make a smooth batter. Makes light, thick pancakes.
Mrs. J. Howard Beard
