Title: FREEZER MIX SKILLET GOULASH
Categories: Main dish, Hamburger, Beef
Yield: 5 Servings
3 1/2 c  Browned/Seasoned Freezer Mix
1 cn Tomatoes;(16oz),canned
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1/2 c  Water
1 1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Basil leaves
1/8 ts Marjoram leaves
3 oz Noodle; uncooked, fine
Cook freezer mix large skillet until meat is brown. Drain off fat.
Stir in tomatoes (with liquid) and remaining ingredients; break up
tomatoes. Heat to boiling. Reduce heat; cover and simmer, stirring
occasionally, until noodles are tender, about 20 minutes. (A small
amount of water can be added if necessary.)
