Title: FIZZ CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 5 Servings
1    Chicken
16 oz Coca-Cola
1 c  Catsup
Skin chicken pieces; salt and pepper to taste.  Place in electric fry
pan with Coca-Cola.  Simmer for 30 minutes. Add catsup and stir
gently. Cook for 30 minutes turning occasionally. Source: Georgia H.
Smathers, contributor, Carolina Coastline Cuisine
