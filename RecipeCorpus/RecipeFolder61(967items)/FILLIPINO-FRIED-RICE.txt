Title: FILLIPINO FRIED RICE
Categories: Grains/rice, Phillipines, Asian, Beef/veal, Ham/pork
Yield: 2 Servings
-FDGN81A
2 c  Rice; steamed
1/2 lb Bacon; fried crisp
1 sm Green bell pepper; diced
1    Medium onion; diced
1    Small cabbage; shredded
2    Cloves garlic; chopped
3    Carrots; slivered
Leftover pork or beef roast;
-diced in bite size chunks
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1 c  Frozen green peas; thawed
In large skillet, fry bacon until crisp. Remove, leaving 1 to 2 T. of
bacon grease. Stir fry all vegetables until crisp or desired texture.
Add meat and crumbled bacon. Mix well. Add steamed rice.
Thoroughly mix; add soy sauce and peas and mix.
