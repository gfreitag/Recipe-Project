Title: FIRE DRILL SALSA
Categories: Chile heads, Ethnic, Salsas
Yield: 2 Cups
4 lg Tomatoes, diced
1 lg Onion, diced
1 md Bell pepper, diced
1 c  Habanero chilies, chopped
1/4 c  Chopped fresh cilantro
2 cl Garlic, minced
Mix and serve with torilla or potato chips. Nutritional analysis per
1/2 cup:  Calories 34.6, protein 1.3 g, carbohydrate 7.5 g, fat .4 g,
cholesterol 0, dietary fiber 1.9 g, sodium 7.4 mg. From Snack Cliff
