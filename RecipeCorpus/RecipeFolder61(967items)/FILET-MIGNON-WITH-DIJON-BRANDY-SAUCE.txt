Title: FILET MIGNON WITH DIJON BRANDY SAUCE
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Beef
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Filet mignon
Salt and black pepper
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Brandy
2 tb Finely chopped onion
1 c  Heavy cream
1/4 c  Dijon mustard
Sprinkle filets with salt and black pepper to taste.In a large
skillet,melt butter.Cook filets as desired.Pour brandy over
meat.Ignite carefully.When flame dies,remove meat to a heated
platter.Cook onion in drippings.Stir in heavy cream.Simmer until
sauce slightly thickens.Mix in mustard.Serve sauce over
filets,immediately.
