Title: FREEZER MIX SLOPPY JOES
Categories: Hamburger, Beef
Yield: 8 Servings
1 pk Beef-Tomato Freezer Mix
1/4 c  Water
1 c  Celery; sliced
1 tb Catsup
1 ts Brown sugar
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
8 ea Hamburger bun; split/toasted
Dip container of frozen mix into hot water just to loosen. In 2-quart
saucepan, heat frozen mix, water, celery, catsup, brown sugar and
Worcestershire sauce to boiling. Reduce heat; cover and simmer,
stirring frequently, until mix is partially thawed, about 15 minutes.
Uncover and simmer, stirring occasionally, until of desired
consistency, about 10 minutes. Spoon onto bottom halves of buns; top
with remaining halves.
