Title: FREEZER CUCUMBER PICKLES
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 10 Pints
4 lb Pickling cucumbers, sliced
8 c  Thinly sliced onions
1/4 c  Salt
3/4 c  Water
4 c  Sugar
2 c  Cider vinegar
Combine cucumbers, onions, salt and water in two large bowls. Let
stand at room temperature for 2 hours. Add sugar and vinegar; stir
until sugar dissolves. Pack into 1-pint freezer containers, leaving
1-inch headspace. Cover and freeze for up to 6 weeks. Thaw at room
temperature for 4 hours before serving.
