Title: FIRST PLACE FAVORITES CHEWY CHOCOLATE BROWNIE
Categories: Desserts, Low-cal
Yield: 16 Servings
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Diet margarine, melted
3 tb Cocoa
Sugar subst. to taste
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Canned evap. skim milk
5    Pecan halves, chopped
preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a bowl mix flour, baking powder and
salt.
Beat eggs.  Melt margarine.  Add all ingredients to flour mixture.
Bake for 20 minutes.

exchanges:1/2 bread, 1/2 fat Source: First Place Favorites
