Title: FIESTA TACO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Beef, Vegetables, Ethnic
Yield: 6 Servings
-MARY WILSON   BWVB02B
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 c  Taco sauce
6 c  Torn lettuce
1 md Green bell pepper; cut into
- strips
2 md Tomatoes; cut into wedges
1/2 c  Pitted ripe olives; drained
1 c  Corn chips
1 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
- (4 oz)
1/2 c  Thousand Island dressing; if
- desired
Cook ground beef over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until brown;
drain.  Stir in taco sauce; heat through. Toss lettuce, bell pepper,
tomatoes, olives and corn chips in large bowl. Spoon heat beef
mixture over lettuce mixture; toss. Sprinkle with cheese. Serve
immediately with dressing.
