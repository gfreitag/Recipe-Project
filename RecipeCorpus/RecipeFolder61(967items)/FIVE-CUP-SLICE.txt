Title: FIVE CUP SLICE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 1/4 c  Raisins
1 1/4 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1 1/4 c  Peanuts
1 1/4 c  Coconut
10 oz Sweetened condensed milk
Combine ingredients together in bowl. Stir well to mix. Spread into
greased 9 X 9-inch pan. Bake in 350F oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Cut
into squares.
