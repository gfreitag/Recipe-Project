Title: FLUFFY MASHED SWEET POTATOES
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Cooked, mashed sweet potatos
3 ea Bananas, mashed
1 1/2 c  Soy milk
1/2 c  Prune juice
3 tb Honey
1 ts Allspice
2 ts Chopped candied ginger
Preheat oven to 375F.  Combine all ingredients except ginger & beat
till fluffy.  Pour into greased casserole & bake 50 to 60 minutes.
Sprinkle with candied ginger. Serve as a side dish or sweet enough to
serve as a dessert.
