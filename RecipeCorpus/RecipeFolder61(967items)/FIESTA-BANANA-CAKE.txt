Title: FIESTA BANANA CAKE
Categories: Nopsi, Desserts, Fruits, Cakes
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 pk White cake mix
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Mashed bananas
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Add sour cream to cake mix and beat for 1 minute.  Add baking soda to
bananas.  Add to batter and continue beating 1-1/2 minutes. Fold in
nuts. Pour into 2 greased and waxed paper lined 8-inch cake pans.
Bake in 375 degree oven for 20 to 25 minutes.
