Title: FIBRE-RICH: WINTER GARDEN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
4 c  Cabbage, shredded
4 c  Carrots, shredded
2 c  Broccoli, chopped
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1 c  Low-fat plain yogurt
1/4 c  Chutney
1 ts Curry powder
Salt
Pepper
In bowl, combine cabbage, carrots, broccoli, raisins and sunflower
seeds.

Dressing: In small bowl, stir together yogurt, chutney and curry
powder. Pour over cabbage mixture; toss well to coat evenly. Season
with salt and pepper to taste.

Per serving: about 190 calories, 6 g Protein, 5 g fat,
34 g  carbohydrate

Source: Canadian Living magazine Feb 95 Presented in article by
Barbara McQuade "Health & Well-Fare: Top 10 Foods to Help Fight Colds
and Flu"
