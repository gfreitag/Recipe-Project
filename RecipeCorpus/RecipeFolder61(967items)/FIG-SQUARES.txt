Title: FIG SQUARES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Figs
1/3 c  Crystallized ginger
1 c  Powdered sugar
2 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Toasted coconut
1/4 ts Salt
3/4 c  Nuts
Combine figs, nuts, salt, and ginger.  Put through food chopper using
medium knife.  Add lemon juice. Knead into a paste on a board dredged
with powdered sugar. Roll thin. Cut in 1-inch squares. Put together
in layers of three, sprinkling the coconut between the layers. Mrs.
R.F. Higginbotham, Covington, OK.
