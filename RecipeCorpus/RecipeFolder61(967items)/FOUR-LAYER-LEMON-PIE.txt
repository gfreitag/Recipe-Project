Title: FOUR-LAYER LEMON PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM---------------------------LAYER--------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Sugar, heaping
5 tb Milk
MMMMM---------------------------LAYER--------------------------------
8 oz Cream cheese,softened
4 oz Whipped topping
1 c  Powdered sugar
MMMMM---------------------------LAYER--------------------------------
6 oz Instant lemon pudding mix
3 c  Milk
MMMMM---------------------------LAYER--------------------------------
4 oz Whipped topping
LAYER 1: Mix all ingredients together and pat into a pie pan.Bake in
a preheated 400 degree oven until browned,8 to 10 minutes.
LAYER 2: Beat together and pour into pie shell.Chill.
LAYER 3: Mix until thickened and pour on cream cheese layer.Chill.
LAYER 4: Cover pie with 4 oz. whipped topping.Chill until serving
time.Serves 8
