Title: FINGER PAINT (IOWA CHURCH COOKBOOK)
Categories: Kids, Inedibles
Yield: 1 Recipe
MMMMM---------------------LISA CRAWLEY/TSPN--------------------------
1/3 c  Cornstarch
3 tb Sugar
2 c  Cold Water
Food Coloring
1. Mix Cornstarch, sugar and water in a 1 qt. saucepan. 2. Cook and
stir over medium heat about 5 minutes or until thickened; remove from
heat. 3. Divide the mixture into separate cups or containers. Tint
mixture in each container with a different color. Stir several times
until cool. Store in airtight container. Works best if you use the
paint the same day you make it. Martelle Christian Church
Cookbook/Iowa
