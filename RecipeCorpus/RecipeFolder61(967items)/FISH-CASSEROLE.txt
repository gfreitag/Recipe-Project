Title: FISH CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Seafood, Fish, Wrv
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-----------------WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VHGC42A----------------------
2 lb Speckled trout, redfish or
-red snapper fillets
1/2 c  Creamy Italian salad
-dressing
1/2 c  Butter; melted
1 1/2 c  Potato chips; crumbled
8 sl Velveeta cheese
NOTE-Do not use ridged potato chips and only use "creamy" dressing.
Place fish in a 9x12" baking dish. Pour salad dressing over fish and
then melted butter. Sprinkle potato chips over fish and add cheese
slices. Bake for 15 minutes in a preheated 350~ oven. Source: The
Times-Picayune
