Title: FRAGRANT GINGER-HOT PEPPER SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 8 Servings
1 tb Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Gingerroot, peeled & minced
2 ts Crushed red pepper
1/2 ts Salt
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1/4 c  Chopped fresh cilantro
1/4 c  Minced green onions
2 1/2 tb Water
Heat vegetable oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Add minced
gingerroot, red pepper, salt and garlic; saute 2 minutes. Remove from
heat; spoon into a small bowl and stir in remeaining ingredients.

Reprinted from Cooking Light magazine, March 1996 issue p.106
