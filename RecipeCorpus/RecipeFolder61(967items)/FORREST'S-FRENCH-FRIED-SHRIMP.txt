Title: FORREST'S FRENCH-FRIED SHRIMP
Categories: Shrimp
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Unpeeled medium-size fresh p
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Water
3 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 lg Egg, lightly beaten
Vegetable oil
Peel shrimp, leaving tails intact; devein, if desired.

combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl. Add water
and next 3 ingredients; stir until smooth.

Pour oil to depth of 2 inches into a Dutch oven; heat to 375 degrees.
Dip shrimp into batter, and fry, a few at a time, until golden. Drain
on paper towels.
