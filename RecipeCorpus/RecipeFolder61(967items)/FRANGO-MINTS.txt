Title: FRANGO MINTS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
12 oz Chocolate
1    Peppermint extract
1/2 c  Margarine
1 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
2    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
Melt and cool chocolate.  Cream together margarine, sugar, eggs and
vanilla.  Add cooled chocolate and a few drops of mint to creamed
mixture. Pack into 8 inch square pan. Cool and set and cut in squares.
