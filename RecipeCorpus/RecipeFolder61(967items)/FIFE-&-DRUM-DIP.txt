Title: FIFE & DRUM DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Peanut Butter
1 tb Toasted Coconut
8 oz Vanilla Yogurt
Add the peanut butter and coconut to the yogurt and use as a dipper
for apple or pear slices.  Or eat plain. Yield: 1 serving.
