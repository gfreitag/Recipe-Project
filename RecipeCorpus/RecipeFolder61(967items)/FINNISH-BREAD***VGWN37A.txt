Title: FINNISH BREAD***VGWN37A
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
1 1/8    Butter
1/2    Sugar
3 1/2    Flour
Method:mix well and let stand in cold place for 1
hour. Roll into strips about 2/3 inch in diameter and
cut into small pieces. brush with egg and sprinkle
with sugar and chopped nuts. Bake in a moderate oven
till golden brown(350)
