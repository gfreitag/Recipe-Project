Title: FLUFFY PINK GRAPEFRUIT FROSTING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Egg whites
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Light corn syrup
1/3 c  Pink grapefruit cocktail
2 ts Vanilla
2    To 3 drop red food coloring
Makes enough to fill and frost one two layer cake.

In the top of a double boiler, combine all the ingredients except
vanilla and food coloring. Place over boiling water and beat with an
electric mixer about seven minutes or until frosting will stand up in
soft peaks. Remove from water and beat in vanilla and food colouring.
Use immediately.
