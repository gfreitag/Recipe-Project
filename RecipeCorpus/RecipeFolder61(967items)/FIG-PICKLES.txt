Title: FIG PICKLES
Categories: Fruits, Canned
Yield: 1 Batch
1 c  Water
Pickling spice to include
Cloves and cinnamon (whole)
6 c  Sugar
1 c  Vinegar
COVER 3 quarts figs with 2 quarts boiling water and allow to stand 5
minutes.  Make syrup out of above ingredents (spices should be in a
bag made of cheesecloth. Drain figs. Boil in syrup 3 consecutive days
10 minutes each day, then pack into jars to within 1/4 inch of top.
Put on cap, screw band firmly tight. Process in boiling water bath 10
minutes.
