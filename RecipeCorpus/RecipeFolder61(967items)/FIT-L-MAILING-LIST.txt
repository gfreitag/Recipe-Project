There is a fitness mailing list called FIT-L (Exercise/diet/wellness
talk list)

To subscribe, send mail to LISTSERV@ETSUADMN.ETSU.EDU with

SUBSCRIBE FIT-L <your name>

in the body of the message.

After you have subscribed, you can get the digest version by sending
mail to the same address above with SET FIT-L DIGEST in the body of
the message.
