Title: FRANKFURTERS WITH A FLAIR
Categories: Easy, Meats, Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Frankfurters
1/4 lb Sharp cheddar cheese
1/3 ts Powdered or bottled
-horseradish
1/2 ts Dijon mustard
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Steak sauce
1/4 ts Onion powder
2    8 ounce cans tomato sauce
1/4 ts Brown sugar
1 ts Chili powder
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Make a slit lengthwise in each
frankfurter. Combine next 6 ingredients; stuff franks. Place franks
in shallow baking pan; mix tomato sauce, brown sugar and chili
powder. Pour over franks. Bake for 30 minutes.

Serve on a split bun or as an entree with rice, pasta or noodles.
