Title: FREEZER MIX ROMANOFF
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Beef-Mushroom Freezer Mix
1/3 c  Water
1 pk Noodles Romanoff
Dip container of frozen mix into hot water just to loosen. In 2-quart
saucepan, heat frozen mix and water to boiling. Reduce heat; cover and
simmer, stirring frequently, until mix is thawed, about 20 minutes.
Prepare noodles Romanoff as directed on package except ~ omit butter
and milk; stir sour cream-cheese sauce mix and cooked noodles into
meat mixture and heat to boiling. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 5
minutes.
