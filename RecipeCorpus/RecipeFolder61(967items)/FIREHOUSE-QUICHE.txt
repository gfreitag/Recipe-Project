Title: FIREHOUSE QUICHE
Categories: Cheese, Eggs, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Zucchini grated
1 ea Onion small chopped
1 c  Bisquick
4 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Corn oil
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese grated
1/2 ts Parsley dried
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper freshly ground
Mix all ingredients. Pour into a large, greased pie pan. Bake at 350
degrees F for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
