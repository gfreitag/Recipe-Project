Title: FRAN~S POTATOE SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 1 6 servings
1 pk Smokey-links cut-up
1 tb Oil
2 c  Celery-diced
1 c  Onion diced
2 cn Cream of celery soup
16 oz Cheese whiz
22 oz Milk
5 c  Potatoes diced
saute smokey-links,onion,celery in oil..till done.Add rest of
ingredients and simmer for at least 45 min. The longer the better.
