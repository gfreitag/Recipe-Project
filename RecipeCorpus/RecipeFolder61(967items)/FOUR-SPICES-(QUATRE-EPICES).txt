Title: FOUR SPICES (QUATRE EPICES)
Categories: Spices, French
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 tb Black peppercorns, heaped
2 ts Whole cloves
2 ts Nutmeg, freshly grated
1 ts Ground ginger
Mix, then grind in a spice grinder or coffee mill, then re-mix. Store
in an airtight container, keep in a cool place away from light.
Muchly used in French charcuterie and with slowly cooked meat and
poultry dishes.
