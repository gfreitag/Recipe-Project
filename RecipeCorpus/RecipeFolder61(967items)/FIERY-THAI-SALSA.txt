Title: FIERY THAI SALSA
Categories: Salsa, Thai
3 c  cucumbers -- chopped seeded
peele
Yield: 4 Servings
:
1 c  green onion -- chopped
3/4 c  radishes -- chopped
1/4 c  fresh mint -- chopped
3 TB fresh ginger -- minced &
peeled
:
3 TB fresh lime juice
2 TB sugar
1 TB minced garlic
1 ts minced garlic
1 1/2 ts sesame chili oil

Combine all ingredients in large bowl.  Season with salt. Cover and
refrigerate until juicy, about 1 hour. Stir before serving cold.
