Title: FINE MOLASSES COOKIES
Categories: Desserts, Cookies
Yield: 1 Batch    1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt 1 c Shortening 4 ts Baking soda 2 Eggs, beaten 2 ts
1 ts Ginger 4 c
Cinnamon 1 c  Cane or sorghum molasses
A.p. or unbleached flour

Cream sugar and shortening; add eggs and molasses. Mix flour, salt,
soda and spices.  Add to creamed mixture. Roll into walnut-sized
balls. If desired, roll balls in granulated sugar. Place the balls on
greased cookie sheets.  Bake at 375 F for close to 10 minutes. Makes
about three dozen.
