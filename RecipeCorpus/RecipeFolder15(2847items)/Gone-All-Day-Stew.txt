Title: Gone All Day Stew
Categories: Stew, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Celery chunks
2    Beef bouilion cubes
1 tb Italian herb seasoning  or -
1 ts each thyme, oregano,  -
Rosemary
1    Bay leaf
-
10 3/4 oz Tomato soup
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Flour
2 lb Beef chuck, cut 1" cubes
3 md Carrots, cut 1" diag
6 md Yellow onions, quartered
4 md Potatoes, peeled, cut
1 1/2 " Cubes
Mix together soup, water, and flour until smooth. Combine with remaining
ingredients in a covered roasting pan. Bake for 4 to 5 hours at 275 deg f.
When ready to serve adjust seasonings to taste. Serve or noodles or with
french bread.
