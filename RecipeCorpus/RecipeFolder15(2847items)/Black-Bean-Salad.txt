Title: Black Bean Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  oil
1/2 c  honey
2 ea cloves garlic, pressed
1 ea lemon, juiced
1 c  white wine
1 ts oregano
1 tb parsley
2 c  French-cut green beans
3 c  black beans, cooked
1 c  garbanzo beans, cooked
1 c  adzuki beans (or red beans),
2 ea onions, sliced
2 ea green peppers, sliced
2 ea stalks celery, sliced
2 ea green onions, thinly sliced
Combine all ingredients in large bowl, mixing thoroughly.  Chill
overnight.  Serves 12
