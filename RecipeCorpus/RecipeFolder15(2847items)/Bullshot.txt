Title: Bullshot
Categories: Cocktails
Yield: 1 servings
4 fl Beef bullion
2 ds Tabasco sauce
1 ds Pepper
2 fl Vodka
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Salt
Mix and serve in old fashoned glass over ice. May also be served hot. Heat
beef bullion to near boiling. Mix in other ingredients and serve in a mug.
