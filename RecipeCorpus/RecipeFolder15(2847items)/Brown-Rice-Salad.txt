Title: Brown Rice Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  chopped onion
1 ea Dressing:
3 tb olive oil
1 ts lemon juice
2 tb mayonnaise
1 ds cayenne
1 ea salad:
4 c  brown rice, cooked
1/2 c  swiss cheese, grated
1 cn (6.5 oz) tuna (optional)
2 tb fresh chopped parsley
2 ea tomatoes, cubed
Combine and toss salad ingredients.  Add dressing.  Chill at least 1 hour
to allow flavors to blend.  Serves 6.
