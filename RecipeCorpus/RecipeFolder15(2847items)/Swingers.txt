Title: Swingers
Categories: Beef, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts salt
2 tb wheat germ
1 ea clove garlic, minced
1/2 c  sliced mushrooms
1 c  sliced green olives
2 lb ground round
1 1/2 c  onion, coarsely chopped
1 1/2 c  green pepper, coarsely chopp
1 1/2 c  tomatoes, coarsely chopped
2 c  cheddar cheese, cubed
Mix all ingredients.  Shape into patties.  Refrigerate 2 to 3 hours.
Press patties together to prevent crumbling.  Cook 10 minutes on each
side.  Serves 6.
