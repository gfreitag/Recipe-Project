Title: Granola Bars
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 16 servings
2 ea eggs, beaten
2 c  granola
Combine granola and eggs, mixing thoroughly.  Spread mixture into an oiled
6 x 8-inch pan.  Press firmly into pan.  Cut into 1 1/2 x 2-inch
rectangles before baking.  Bake 20 minutes at 300 degrees.  Cool and
recut.  Makes 16 bars.
