Title: Brown Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1 cn Beef stock
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1/2 ts Salt
In skillet melt butter.  Add flour, salt, pepper and beef stock.
Cook 15 minutes over very low heat or double boiler.  Consumme' can
substitute for beef stock.
Serve with beef dishes.
