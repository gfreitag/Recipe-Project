Title: Turkey Salad (Hot)
Categories: Holidays, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
4 ea HARD BOILED EGGS, CHOPPED
3/4 c  CREAM OF CHICKEN SOUP, 1 cn
1/4 ts ONION POWDER
2/3 c  SLIVERED ALMONDS
1 c  SHREDDED CHEDDAR CHEESE
1 1/2 c  CRUSHED POTATO CHIPS
4 c  TURKEY, CUT UP
1/2 ts ACCENT, OPTIONAL
2 tb LEMON JUICE
3/4 c  MAYONNAISE
1 ts SALT
1 ts WHITE PEPPER
1 c  CHOPPED CELERY
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS EXCEPT FOR THE CHEESE AND POTATO CHIPS IN A
CASSEROLE DISH. TOP WITH CHEESE AND POTATO CHIPS. BAKE AT 400 DEG F. FOR
20 TO 25 MINUTES.
