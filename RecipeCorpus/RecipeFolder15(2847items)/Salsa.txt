Title: Salsa
Categories: Salsa
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Lemon juice
Hot pepper sauce    or
Jalapenos to taste
28 oz Can tomatoes, chopped and -
Peeled
7 oz Can chopped green chiles
2 ea Bunches, green onions   --
Chopped fine
Combine tomatoes, chiles, sugar, salt, pepper and lemon juice. Add hot
pepper sauce or jalapenos to taste. makes about 1 1/2 quarts.
