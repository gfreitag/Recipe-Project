Title: Zucchini Cookies
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 24 servings
2 ts BAKING POWDER
2 ts SODA
1/2 ts SALT
1 c  CHOCOLATE CHIPS
1 c  CHOPPED NUTS
1 1/2 c  GRATED ZUCCHINI
1 1/2 c  SUGAR
1/2 c  BUTTER
1 ts VANILLA
3 1/2 c  FLOUR
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS IN A LARGE BOWL AND DROP BY SPOONSFUL ON A BAKING
SHEET. BAKE AT 350 DEG F. FOR 12 TO 13 MINUTES.
