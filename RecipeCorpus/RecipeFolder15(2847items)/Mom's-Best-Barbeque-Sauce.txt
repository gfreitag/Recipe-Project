Title: Mom's Best Barbeque Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 12 servings
1 tb DIJON MUSTARD
1 ts GRATED LEMON RIND
1/2 c  SUGAR
1 tb HORSERADISH
2 ts CUMMIN POWDER
3 tb CHILI POWDER
3 tb HOT SAUCE
1 x  SALT TO TASTE
2 tb BUTTER
2 ea MEDIUM ONIONS, SLICED
3 ea CLOVES GARLIC, MINCED
1/4 c  SUNFLOWER OIL
2 c  KETCHUP
1/3 c  CIDER VINEGAR
1/3 c  LEMON JUICE
1/4 c  WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE
1/3 c  DARK CORN SYRUP
IN A LARGE SAUCE PAN, MELT BUTTER AND SAUTE ONION AND GARLIC FOR ABOUT 5
MINUTES. ADD ALL REMAINING INGREDIENTS AND MIX WELL. BRING TO A BOIL.
REDUCE HEAT AND SIMMER FOR 30 MINUTES OR UNTIL SAUCE THICKENS.
