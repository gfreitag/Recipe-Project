Title: Pecan Cookies
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 15 servings
----------------------------------COOKIES----------------------------------
2 c  FLOUR
3/4 c  CHOPPED PECANS
1 c  BUTTER
1/2 c  POWDERED SUGAR
1 ts VANILLA
----------------------------------TOPPING----------------------------------
1 ea POWDERED SUGAR
CREAM BUTTER, SUGAR, AND VANILLA. ADD FLOUR AND PECANS. MIX WELL. ROLL
DOUGH INTO BALLS. BAKE AT 350 DEG F. FOR 15 MINUTES. ROLL IN POWDERED
SUGAR.
