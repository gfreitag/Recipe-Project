Title: FESTIVE LAMB CHOPS
Categories: Lamb, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
4 ea Lamb chops, thick
4 ea Swiss cheese slices
1 ea Onion, sliced
Use twice the amount of rib or loin chops in place of thick ones.
Broil chops until brown, turn and broil on other side. Trim cheese to
chop size. Place onion and cheese slices on each chop. Broil until
cheese begins to melt.
