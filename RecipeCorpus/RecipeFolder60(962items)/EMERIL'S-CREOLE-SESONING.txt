Title: EMERIL'S CREOLE SESONING
Categories: Spices
Yield: 1 Cup
2 1/2 tb Paprika
2 tb Salt
2 tb Garlic powder
1 tb Black pepper
1 tb Onion powder
1 tb Cayenne
1 tb Dried oregano
1 tb Dried thyme
In a bowl stir together the paprika, the salt, the garlic powder, the
black pepper, the onion powder, the cayenne, the oregano, and the
thyme. The Creole seasoning keeps in airtight container for 6 months.
