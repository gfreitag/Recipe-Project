Title: ELDERFLOWER WATER - ANTI-FRECKLE LOTION (TLC)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
110 g  Fresh Elderflowers
300 ml Boiling Spring Water
2 tb Eau de cologne
1. Put elderflowers in a bowl and bruise them with a wooden spoon 2.
Pour over boiling water and stir for a few seconds 3. Leave to infuse
and cool 4. Strain and add Eau de Cologne. Transfer to a jug and use
a funnel to pour it into a clean jar or bottle.
