Title: ENGLISH TOASTED BREAD - SOURDOUGH ABM
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Loaf
MMMMM------------------------1 POUND LOAF-----------------------------
2 ts Active dry yeast
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
2 tb Dry milk
1 ts Sea salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
5/8 c  Warm water
1/2 c  Sourdough starter
1 tb Canola oil
1 ts Honey
MMMMM----------------------1-1/2 POUND LOAF---------------------------
1 pk Active dry yeast
3 c  Bread flour
3 tb Dry milk
1 1/2 ts Sea salt
3/4 ts Baking soda
7/8 c  Warm water
3/4 c  Sourdough starter
1 1/2 tb Canola oil
2 ts Honey
In a small cup combine warm water and baking soda and set aside for 5
minutes. Add dry ingredients to machine and then wet ingredients,
including baking soda-water mixture. Start machine using whole wheat
mode.

Source: The Bread Machine Gourmet
