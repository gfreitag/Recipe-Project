Title: EVERGREEN MINT PUNCH
Categories: Holiday, Christmas
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Water
10 oz Mint jelly
3 c  Pineapple juice; chilled
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  Vodka
64 oz Lemon-lime carbonated drink;
- chilled
1 qt Lime sherbet
In small saucepan, combine water and jelly; cook and stir until jelly
melts. Cool. In large punch bowl, combine jelly mix, juices and
vodka. Just before serving, add carbonated beverage and lime sherbet.
