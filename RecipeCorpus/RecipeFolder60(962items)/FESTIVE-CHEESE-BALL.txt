Title: FESTIVE CHEESE BALL
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk (3-oz) softened cream
Cheese
1/2 lb Shredded process sharp
American cheese (2 cups)
2 tb Toasted sesame seeds
3 tb Worcestershire
Sauce
Coarsely chopped pecans/
Slivered almonds lightly
Toasted
Celery stalks
Beat cheeses with electric mixer until creamy; blend in sesame seeds
and worcestershire sauce. Shape into a ball; chill several hours.
Roll ball in nuts just before serving. Serve with celery or crackers.
