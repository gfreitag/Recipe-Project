Title: ENCHILADA CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion, chopped
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Ro-Tel tomatoes & chilies
12    Corn tortillas
12 oz Cheddar cheese, grated
Salt and pepper to taste
Brown ground beef and onion; drain off grease.  Set aside. Mix
mushroom soup and tomatoes in blender. Add to ground beef. In a
greased 9x13 inch casserole dish, layer 5 to 6 tortillas, torn into
bite size pieces, half the ground beef mixture and 1/2 the grated
cheese. Repeat layers; ending with cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for 30
minutes. Randy Rigg
