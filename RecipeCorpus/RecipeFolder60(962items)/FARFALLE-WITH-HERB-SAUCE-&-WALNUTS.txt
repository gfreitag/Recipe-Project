Title: FARFALLE WITH HERB SAUCE & WALNUTS
Categories: Tvfn
1 lb farfalle -- cooked according
to package directions
Yield: 1 Servings
:
3 c  basil leaves -- picked
clean
:
2 c  fresh mint sprigs --
cleaned
:
2    cloves garlic -- minced
3/4    extra virgin olive oil
1/2 c  vegetable stock
1 1/2 ts salt
1/2 ts fresh pepper
1 TB lemon juice
1/2 c  walnuts -- toasted,chopped
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated

In a blender, or food processor, add the herbs and garlic, and while
the machine is running, drizzle in 1/2 olive oil, the vegetable
stock, and then the rest of the oil. Add salt, pepper and lemon,
blend and taste and adjust seasoning. Toss with cooked pasta while
still warm, fold in nuts and cheese. Garnish with fresh herb sprigs.
