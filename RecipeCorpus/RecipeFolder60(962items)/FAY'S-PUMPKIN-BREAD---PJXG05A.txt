Title: FAY'S PUMPKIN BREAD - PJXG05A
Categories: Holiday, Quickbreads, Gifts, Fruits, Favorite
Yield: 8 Servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
3 c  Sugar
3 1/2 c  Flour; sifted
2/3 c  Vegetable oil
2 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Pumpkin
2/3 c  Water
1 ts Ground nutmeg
2/3 c  Pecans; coarsely chopped
1    Egg
Mix all ingredients together.  Pour into well-greased loaf pans. Bake
1 hour at 350 degrees.  Remove from pan and cool completely on wire
rack before wrapping. This may be frozen. From my friend Fay Carrico
