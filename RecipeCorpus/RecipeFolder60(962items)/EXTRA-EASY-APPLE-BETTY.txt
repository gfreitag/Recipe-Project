Title: EXTRA-EASY APPLE BETTY
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
10 c  Sliced apples, peeled
1/4 c  Apple juice
2 c  Fine, oatmeal cookie crumbs
1/4 c  Butter, melted
1/2 ts Cinnamon
In large bowl, toss apples with apple juice.  In separate bowl, toss
together cookie crumbs, butter and cinnamon. Spread half of the
apples in 8 inch square baking dish; sprinkle with half of the crumb
mixture. Repeat layers.  Bake in 375F oven for 50 minutes or until
apples are tender. Makes 6 servings. Canadian Living Magazine.
October 96 issue. Typed in MMFormat by Cindy Hartlin. Aug. 27, 1996.
