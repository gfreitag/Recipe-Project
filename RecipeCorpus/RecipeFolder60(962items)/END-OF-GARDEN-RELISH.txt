Title: END-OF-GARDEN RELISH
Categories: Pickle/reli, Can/preserv
Yield: 20 Servings
1 c  Chopped cucumbers
1 c  Chopped green peppers
1 c  Chopped cabbage
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Green tomatoes, chopped
1 c  Chopped carrots
1 c  Chopped celery
2 c  Vinegar
2 c  Sugar
2 ts Turmeric
2 ts Mustard seed
1 ts Celery seed
Soak cucumbers and tomatoes in salt water while grinding the rest.
Drain. Mix everything and boil for 20 minutes. Fill clean, sterile
bottles and seal.

From: Ron Curtis
