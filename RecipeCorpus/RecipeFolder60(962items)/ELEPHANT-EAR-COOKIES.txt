Title: ELEPHANT EAR COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 36 Cookies
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Molasses
1/2 c  Shortening; melted
1    Egg
1/2 c  Sour milk
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Soda; dissolved in milk
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Raisins (chopped)
Mix all ingredients in order given; drop by teaspoonfuls on greased
cookie sheet. Bake 10 minutes in 375-degree oven.
