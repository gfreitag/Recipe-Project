Title: FESTIVE FROZEN MINT PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Pie
1    (6oz) Ready-made Nabisco
Oreo Pie Crust
1 cn (14oz) Sweetened Condensed
Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
1 ts (to 1 1/2t) Peppermint
Extract
10    (to 12) drops Green Food
Coloring, optional
2 c  (1pt) whipping cream, or
Whipped topping
In large mixer bowl, combine sweetened condensed milk, peppermint
extract and, if desired, food coloring. Fold in whipped cream. Turn
into crust; cover. Freeze 6 hours or until firm. Garnish as desired.
Freeze leftovers.

Preparation Time: 8 minutes
