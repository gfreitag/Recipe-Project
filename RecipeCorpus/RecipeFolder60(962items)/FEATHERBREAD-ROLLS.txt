Title: FEATHERBREAD ROLLS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Milk, scalded
2 tb Butter
1    Egg, well beaten
2 c  Flour
1 pk Yeast, dissolved in...
1/4 c  Warm water
1 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
Add butter to warm milk.  Then add sugar, salt, yeast dissolved in
warm water, egg, and flour; beat well. Let rise 2 hours in warm
place. Beat again, and pour into muffin tins. Let rise again and bake
in hot oven (425) degrees.  This recipe makes 1 dozen rolls.
