Title: FAT FREE CREAM CHEESE FROSTING
Categories: Fat-free ba, Fillings &, Icings
Yield: 16 Servings
1 c  Nonfat cream cheese
1 c  Ricotta cheese, part skim
Milk
1/2 c  Sugar, brown
1 ts Vanilla extract
Place all ingredients in a food processor and blend until smooth
