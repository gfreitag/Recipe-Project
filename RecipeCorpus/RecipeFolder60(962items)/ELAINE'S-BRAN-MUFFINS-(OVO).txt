Title: ELAINE'S BRAN MUFFINS (OVO)
Categories: Breads, Muffins, Prodigy, Dec.
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/4 c  Whole-grain wheat pastry flo
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Egg beaters
1 1/2 c  Bran
5 oz Water; boiling
1 c  Soy milk
1/4 c  Applesauce
1/4 c  Honey
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Raisins
Recipe by: Elaine French Preparation Time: 0:40 Combine flour, soda,
and egg replacer. In a large bowl, pour boiling water over bran. Mix
well. Add nut milk, applesauce, honey, vanilla and raisins. Blend
well. Stir dry ingredients quickly into wet ingredients. Spoon batter
into non stick muffin tin. Bake at 425 degrees for 20 minutes.
