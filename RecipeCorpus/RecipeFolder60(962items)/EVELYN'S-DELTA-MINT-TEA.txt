Title: EVELYN'S DELTA MINT TEA
Categories: Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 Servings
7 sm Tea bags
12    Sprigs mint
8 c  Boiling water
7    Lemons -- juiced
3    Lemon rinds
2 c  Sugar
8 c  Water
Steep tea, mint and lemon rind in boiling water for 12 minutes.
Remove from water. Add juice and sugar. Strain, then add rest of
water. Makes 1= gallon.
