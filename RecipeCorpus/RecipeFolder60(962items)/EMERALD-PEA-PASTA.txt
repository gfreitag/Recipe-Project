Title: EMERALD PEA PASTA
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  Frozen green peas, defrosted
2 tb Chopped fresh mint
1 1/2 c  All purpose flour
1 pn Salt
1 pn Pepper
Place peas & mint in a food processor & puree.  Add remaining
ingredients & pulse until a ball of dough forms. Turn out onto a
floured surface & knead until smooth & pliable like a firm bread
dough. Place dough in a bowl, cover with plastic wrap & let rest for
at least 30 minutes. Shape & cook dough as desired.
