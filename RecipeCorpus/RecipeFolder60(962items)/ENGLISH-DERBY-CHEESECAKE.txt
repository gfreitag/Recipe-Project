Title: ENGLISH DERBY CHEESECAKE
Categories: Cheesecake, Ethnic, Cheesecakes
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Cream cheese
1 c  Sugar
5    Eggs, separated
2 c  Sour cream
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Vanilla
Crumb lined pan
Beat cheese and sugar smooth. Beat in yolks to blend. Blend in sour
cream, vanilla and lemon. Beat whites stiff not dry. Fold into
mixture. Pour into crumb lined springform pan.Bake 300 for 1
hour.Keep in oven 1 hour with door closed. Open oven door, and keep
cake in 1/2 hour longer. Remove and chill overnight.A light fluffy
cheesecake
