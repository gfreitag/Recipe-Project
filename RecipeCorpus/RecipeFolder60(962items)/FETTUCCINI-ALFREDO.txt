Title: FETTUCCINI ALFREDO
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 lb Fettuccini
3/4 c  Butter
1 c  Grated parmesan cheese
2 c  Half and half cream
Melt butter in saucepan, stir in cream and cheese; simmer, stirring
constantly until cheese is melted and smooth. Cook fettuccini, drain,
do not rinse. Pour piping hot sauce over drained fettuccini and toss
gently.
