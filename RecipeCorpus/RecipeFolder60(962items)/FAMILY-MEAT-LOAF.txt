Title: FAMILY MEAT LOAF
Categories: Hamburger, Main dishes, Microwave
Yield: 6 Servings    1 1/2 lb Ground Beef, Extra Lean
2/3 c  Quick-Cooking Oats
1 c  Milk, 1% Lowfat
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Poultry Seasoning
2    Eggs
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1    Onions -- Chopped
1/4 c  Brown Sugar, Packed
2 ts Mustard
1/8 ts Ground Nutmeg
1/2 c  Catsup

Combine first 9 ingredients.  Put in round microwave-safe tube pan.
Combine remaining ingredients and pour over top of meat mixture.
Microwave at 70% power for about 15 minutes or until temperature
probe says 160 degrees. Let stand 5 minutes.  Cut in half and
transfer to serving plate if desired.
