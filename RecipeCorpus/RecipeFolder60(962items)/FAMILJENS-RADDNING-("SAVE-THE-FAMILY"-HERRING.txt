Title: FAMILJENS RADDNING ("SAVE THE FAMILY" HERRING
Categories: Appetizers, Side dish, Fish, Sweden, Submitted
Yield: 2 Servings
2    Herring fillet, pickled;
-drained
1/2 c  Sour cream
2    Eggs; hard cooked, yolk and
-white seperated, chopped
-fine
1 sm Cucumber; peeled, halved,
-seeded, chopped fine
1/2 c  Beets, pickled; chopped fine
1/4 c  Parsley; chopped fine
Arrange fillets side by side on long chilled platter. Make diagonal
cuts 1/2 inch apart through both fillets. Spread sour cream in a
circle around fillets. On border of platter, alternate mounds of
chopped egg white, egg yolk, cucumbers, beets, and parsley.
