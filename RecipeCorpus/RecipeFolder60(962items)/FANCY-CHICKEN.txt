Title: FANCY CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb VEGETABLE OIL
3 lb FRYER CHICKEN, CUTUP
10 1/4 oz CREAM OF CHICKEN SOUP
1/8 ts DRIED THYME
1/8 ts PEPPER
IN A SKILLET, OVER MEDIUM HEAT, IN HOT OIL, COOK CHICKEN 10 MINUTES OR
UNTIL BROWNED ON ALL SIDES. SPOON OFF FAT. COMBINNE SOUP AND 2 TBSP.
WATER. STIR THYME, PEPPER AND SOUP IN WITH CHICKEN. REDUCE HEAT,
COVER AND COOK FOR 30 TO 35 MINUTES OR UNTIL TENDER. STIR
OCCASIONALLY.
