Title: FALAFEL SAUCE
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
16 oz Yogurt
1 md Onion diced finely
1 md Cucumber finely grated
Minced garlic to taste
Put yogurt into several layers of cheesecloth in a colander and let
drain overnight to thicken.

Mix yogurt, onion, cucumber and if desired, garlic. Let sit several
hours and serve with falafels or as a bread or cracker dip.
