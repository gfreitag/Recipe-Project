Title: ENSEMADA
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Phillipines
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Flour
10 ea Egg yolks
1 lb Butter
1/2 c  Warm water
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Yeast
Mix all ingredients and let rise..  When mixture rises, punch down the
dough and let rise again. Cut the dough into small portions. Roll each
portion, spreading some more butter. Coil the dough and place in
greased pan. Let rise. Bake at 350 degrees for around 4 minutes.
While warm, brush with butter and sprinkle sugar and parmesan cheese.
