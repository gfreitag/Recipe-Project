Title: FANTASTIC FOODS COUCOUS SALAD
Categories: Grains, Salads, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Cooked coucous
6    Green onions, chopped
2 md Tomatoes, seeded & diced
1 c  Cucumber, seeded & chopped
1/2 c  Garbanzo beans, cooked
1 c  Parsley, chopped
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
3/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice
2    Cloves minced garlic
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 ts Ground coriander
1 ts Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
Mix all salad ingredients together and toss with the salad dressing.

Source: Fantastic Foods Coucous 12 oz package.
