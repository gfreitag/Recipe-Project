Title: EVA'S ZUCCHINI BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
3    Eggs
1 c  Vegetable oil
2 c  Granulated sugar
2 c  Shredded zucchini
2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Chopped walnuts
1 c  Raisins (optional)
Beat the eggs. Add the oil and sugar, and mix well. Preheat the oven
to 325 F. Grease and flour 2 bread pans. Mix all dry ingredients
together, and add sugar/egg/oil mixture to it. Fold in the zucchini
and nuts (also raisins, if used) Bake 1 1/2 hours.
