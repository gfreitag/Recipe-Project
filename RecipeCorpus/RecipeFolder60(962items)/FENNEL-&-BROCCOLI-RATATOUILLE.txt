Title: FENNEL & BROCCOLI RATATOUILLE
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Olive oil
1    Sweet red onion
1    Fennel bulb
2    Fresh, ripe tomatoes
1    Sweet red pepper
1 c  Broccoli florets
1/2 c  Hot pepper salsa
Heat oil and saute onions until limp.  Slice fennel and saute in pan
along with pepper and then tomatoes. Add broccoli and continue to
cook another 5 minutes.

(Serve hot or cold as a side dish or heaped on crusty French Bread.)

From: The Cook's Garden catalog - Spring/Summer 1993 (page 89)
