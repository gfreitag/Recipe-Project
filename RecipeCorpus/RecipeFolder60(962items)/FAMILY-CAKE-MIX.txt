Title: FAMILY CAKE-MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 9 Servings
2 c  Bake it all mix
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
1    Egg
Combine mix and sugar. Add milk and vanilla and beat until well
mixed, by hand or about 2 minutes on medium speed of electric mixer.
Add egg and continue beating until well mixed, by hand or about 1
Pour batter into an 8 by 8 by 2-inch pan,
minute on medium speed.
bottom rubbed with shortening.  Bake in a 375 F. oven 25 to 30
minutes. Frost cooled cake with Snowy Orange Icing (see index), if
desired. Yield: One 8-inch square cake
