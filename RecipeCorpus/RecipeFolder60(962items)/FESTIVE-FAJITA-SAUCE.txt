Title: FESTIVE FAJITA SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  LIME JUICE
1/4 c  OIL
1 ea ONION, SLICED
1 tb MINCED GARLIC
1/4 c  CILANTRO
1 ea SLICED JALAPENO PEPPER
1 ea DICED RED PEPPER
1 ea SALT & PEPPER TO TASTE
USE ON SEAFOOD, CHICKEN, TOFU, TEMPEH, FLANK STEAK. MIX ALL
INGREDIENTS WELL. SPREAD ON MEAT AND BROIL OR GRILL.
