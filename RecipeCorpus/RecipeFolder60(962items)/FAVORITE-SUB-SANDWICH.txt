Title: FAVORITE SUB SANDWICH
Categories: Sandwiches, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Shredded lettuce
1/2 c  Kraft Ranch Dressing divided
1    Loaf Italian bread, cut in
-1/2 lengthwise
1 pk (10 oz) Oscar Mayer All
-American Sub Sandwich
-Variety-Pak
4    American Singles Process
-Cheese Food or
-Kraft Deluxe Process
-American Cheese Slices
1    Medium Green Pepper, thinly
-sliced.
1 cn (2 3/4 oz) sliced pitted
-ripe olives (optional)
Toss lettuce with 1/4 cup of the dressing; set aside. Brush cut
surfaces of bread with remaining 1/4 cup dressing. Fill loaf with
meat, cheese slices, vegetables, olives, and lettuce mixture. Makes 4
to 6 servings.

VARIATIONS:  Substitute Kraft Caesar Ranch Dressing or Kraft Free
Ranch nonfat dressing for Kraft Ranch Dressing. Substitue 4 to 6
Kaiser rolls for Italian bread loaf.
