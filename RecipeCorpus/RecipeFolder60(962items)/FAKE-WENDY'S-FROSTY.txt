Title: FAKE WENDY'S FROSTY
Categories: Beverages, Ice cream, Fake, Copycat
Yield: 2 Servings
3/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Hershey or Nestle chocolate
--drink powder
4 c  Vanilla ice cream
Combine all of the ingredients in a blender.  Blend on medium speed
until

creamy.  Stir if necessary.  If too thin, freeze the mixture in the
blender or in cups until thicker. Source: Top Secret Recipes by Todd
Wilbur
