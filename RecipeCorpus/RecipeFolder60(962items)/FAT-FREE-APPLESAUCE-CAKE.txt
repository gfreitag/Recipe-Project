Title: FAT FREE APPLESAUCE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Fat free, Fruits
Yield: 10 Servings
No Stick cooking spray
1 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Skim milk
2    Egg white
1/3 c  Light or dark corn syrup
2    Apples, peeled cut into
Wedges
2 tb Cinnamon-sugar
Directions Preheat oven 350 F. cook 50 minutes or till toothpick
comes out clean when tested Spray a 9 in round baking pan with spray.
In large bowl, flour, baking powder, salt. In medium bowl, using a
wire whisk thoroughly mix sugar and milk.  Whisk in egg whies and
corn syrup. Gradually stir into dry ingredients until smooth. Pour
into prepared pan. Arrange apples overtop, overlapping to cover.
Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Bake 50 minutes or until done, cool in
pan or on wire rack.
