Title: FAVORITE MEAT LOAF
Categories: Meats, Dinner
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 lb Ground pork
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Sage and poultry seasoning
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2    Eggs
1 c  Tomato juice
4    Slices of bread
Combine meat with onion and seasonings. Beat eggs and add to tomato
juice, cube bread and add to liquid mixture. Beat well and add to
meat mixture. Pack into loaf pan and bake at 350 F for 1 1/4 hours.
Let stand for 10 minutes, drain off liquid and turn out onto plate.
Spread with ketchup.
