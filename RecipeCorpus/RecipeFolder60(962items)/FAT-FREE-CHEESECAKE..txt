Title: FAT FREE CHEESECAKE.
Categories: L, O, W, F, Cheesecakes
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Bars of ff cream cheese
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
2    Egg whites or egg sub
Graham crackers for crust
Soften cream cheese and add rest of ingredients. Blend until smooth
and pour into crust. Could add lemon juice and zest to make a lemony
cheesecake. Bake for
30    minutes at 350 degrees.
