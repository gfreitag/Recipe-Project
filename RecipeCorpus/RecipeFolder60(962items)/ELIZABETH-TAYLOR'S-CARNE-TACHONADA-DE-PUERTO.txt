Title: ELIZABETH TAYLOR'S CARNE TACHONADA DE PUERTO
Categories: Beef, Pork, Celebrity, Tex-mex
Yield: 6 Servings
PATTI - VDRJ67A-----
3/4 lb Smoked raw ham -- 1x1/2"
Strip
30 ml Garlic -- mash to paste
5 lb Beef pot roast
1/2    Bell pepper -- mince
4 tb Oil
1    Onion -- mince
2 c  Tomato puree
Salt & pepper

indentations which have been made with a sharp knife in the pot
roast. Rub roast well with salt and pepper. Brown on all sides in hot
