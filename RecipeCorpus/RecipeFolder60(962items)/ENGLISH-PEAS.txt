Title: ENGLISH PEAS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 Pan
2 pk (10-oz ea) frozen peas
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 c  Diced water chestnuts
1 c  Diced celery
1 sm Jar pimentos
1/2 c  Melted margarine
1 c  Bread crumbs
Cook peas and drain.  Combine peas, onions, soup, celery, water
chestnuts, and pimentos.  Put in a 2-quart casserole. Melt margarine
and combine with bread crumbs (toasted).  Sprinkle on top.  Bake for
30-35 minutes at 350 degrees.
