Title: ELEGANT WHITE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts, Make-a-mix, Tried & tr
Yield: 10 Servings
3 1/2 c  Basic Cake Mix
3/4 c  Milk
3    Egg whites
1 ts Vanilla
Preheat oven to 375F.  Grease and flour two 8" round pans. Combine
Basic Cake Mix and milk in a large bowl. Beat at medium speed 2
minutes. Scrape bowl and beaters. Add egg whites and vanilla. Beat 2
more minutes. Pour into prepared pans.  Bake 20 to 25 minutes, till a
toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pans 10
minutes, then cool on wire racks. Frost as desired.

~ - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

NOTES : Makes one 2-layer cake or 24 cupcakes.
