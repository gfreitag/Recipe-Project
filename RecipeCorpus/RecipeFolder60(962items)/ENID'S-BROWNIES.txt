Title: ENID'S BROWNIES
Categories: Bar cookies, Nuts
Yield: 35 Servings
6 oz Unsweetened Chocolate
2    Sticks Unsalted Butter
6    Eggs
2 1/2 c  Granulated Sugar
2 ts Vanilla Extract
1 3/4 c  All-Purpose Flour -- sifted
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Chopped Walnuts -- optional
Preheat oven to 325. Butter a 10x15-inch baking sheet (jellyroll
pan). Dust with flour and shake out excess. Melt the chocolate and
butter in a small saucepan over VERY low heat, or in the top of a
double boiler. Set aside to cool slightly. Beat the eggs, sugar and
vanilla in a bowl with an electric mixer until thick and fluffy. Stir
chocolate into the eggs. Add 1 3/4 cups flour and the salt; mix until
just combined; do not overmix. Stir in the chopped walnuts, if
desired. Bake in center of oven 25-30 minutes or until center of
brownies is just set. Allow to cool completely before cutting into
squares about 2x2 inches.

Per brownie (without nuts) about 212 calories, 13g fat.
