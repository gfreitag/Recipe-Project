Title: FETTUCCINI WITH FRESH BASIL & TOMATOES
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Ripe tomatoes,peel,chop
2    Cloves garlic,chopped
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 lb Cooked fettuccini
1    White onion,chopped
3/4 c  Fresh basil,chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Grated Cheddar cheese
Mix tomatoes,onion,garlic,basil,olive oil,salt and black pepper;let
stand at room temperature for 1 hour.Cook fettuccini;drain.Toss with
sauce.Sprinkle with cheese.Serve at once.
