Title: EVERYTHING BUT THE KITCHEN SINK COOKIES
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/3 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Butter
2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
2    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 c  Vanilla chips
1/2 c  Butterscotch chips
1 c  Pecans
2 c  Oats
Anything else you have
-laying around.
Mix butter, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla.  Beat until smooth.

Stir in everything else.

Drop onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake for 10 minutes at 375
degrees. Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet before removing to paper
towels.
