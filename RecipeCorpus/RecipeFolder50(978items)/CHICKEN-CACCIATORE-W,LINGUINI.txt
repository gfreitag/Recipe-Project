Title: CHICKEN CACCIATORE W/LINGUINI
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
4 lb Chicken pieces -- skinned &
Defatted
2 ts Olive Oil
6    Green Onions
40 ml Garlic -- sliced thin
SAUCE:
6 oz Tomato Paste
1/2 c  Dry White Wine -=or=-
1/4 c  Dry White Cooking Wine
1/4 ts Pepper
3/4 c  Chicken Stock
1    Bay Leaf
1/8 ts Sweet Marjoram
1 c  Mushrooms -- sliced
Saute chicken in olive oil, green onions and garlic. Mix together the
remaining ingredients for the sauce. Simmer chicken in sauce 1 hour
covered. Serve with cooked linguini, rice or potatoes or other pasta
