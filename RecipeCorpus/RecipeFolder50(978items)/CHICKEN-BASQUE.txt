Title: CHICKEN BASQUE
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
3 lb Chicken parts
Salt
1 cn Tomato soup,condensed(10.75)
4 c  Eggplant strips,3/4" thick
1/2 c  Green pepper,diced,chopped
1/8 ts Pepper
2 tb Shortening
Pepper
1/4 c  White wine,dry
1/2 lb Mushrooms,quartered
2    Garlic cloves,medium,minced
1    Bay leaf
1. Brown chicken in shortening in a large skillet.
2. Transfer chicken to a greased 3-quart casserole; sprinkle with
salt and pepper.
3. Pour off fat in skillet; stir in soup and wine.
4. Mix in remaining ingredients; pour over chicken.
5. Cover; bake in preheated 350'F. oven 1 hour.
6. Remove bay leaf before serving.
