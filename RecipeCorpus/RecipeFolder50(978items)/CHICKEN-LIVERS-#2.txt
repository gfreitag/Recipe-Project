Title: CHICKEN LIVERS #2
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Chicken livers
2 tb Honey
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb White wine
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1    Or 2 cloves minced garlic
Wash chicken livers and pat dry. Combine other ingredients, adding oil
last. Marinate livers in the honey mixture. Bake at 375 degrees for
15 to 20 minutes. I usally turn the livers about half way.
