Title: CHICKEN ALA CAN-CAN
Categories: Chicken, Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
Ingredients:
1 c  Diced or chopped chicken,
-cooked
1 cn Cream celery soup
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 cn Chicken broth
1 1/3 c  Minute rice
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Of tabasco
1/3 c  Sherry
Salt and pepper
1/2 cn French onion rings
Directions: Mix all ingredients except french onion rings in
casserole and place in oven heated to 325 degrees for 7 minutes.
Sprinkle french onion rings on top and bake for 7 more minutes.
