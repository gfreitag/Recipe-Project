Title: CHICKEN SALAD WITH DRIED CHERRRIES
Categories: Cherries, Chicken, Grapes, Nuts, Yogurt
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Chicken breasts -- poached,
In cubes
1/4 c  Low fat mayonnaise
1/4 c  Plain yogurt
1/4 c  Red or green seedless grapes
Halved
1/4 c  Walnut or macadamia nuts --
Chopped
1/4 c  Dried cherries
Mix mayonnaise, yogurt and chicken. Add fruits and nuts. Season
lightly with salt and pepper and serve on a bed of lettuce Posted by
bobbi744@sojourn.com.

: Lansing State Journal, Suzy Cook-Starke, Lansing
