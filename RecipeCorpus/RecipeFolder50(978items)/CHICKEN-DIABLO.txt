Title: CHICKEN DIABLO
Categories: Main, Dish
6    whole chicken breasts
without skin
Yield: 6 Servings
:
4 TB margarine
1/2 c  honey
1/4 c  mustard
1 ts curry powder

melt margarine in small sauce pan. add all spices and mix well. coat
chicken well and bake @ 375ยก for 1 hour
