Title: CHICKEN SPECTACULAR
Categories: Poultry, Casseroles
Yield: 12 Servings
3 c  Cooked,diced chicken
1 pk Uncle Ben's wild and white
Rice, cooked
1 cn Cream of celery soup
1    Med. size jar sliced pimento
1    Med. onion, chopped
1 cn French-style green beans,
Drained
1 c  Hellman's mayonnaise
1 cn Water chestnuts, diced
Salt & pepper to taste
Grated cheddar cheese
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix all ingredients together, pour into
greased 3 qt casserole and spread grated cheese over top. Bake about
25 to 30 minutes.
