Title: CHICKEN ARTICHOKE & BROCCILI SALAD
Categories: Artichokes, Broccoli, Chicken, Healthwise, Salads - ma
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Cubed cooked chicken
14 oz Can artichoke hearts-drained
& quartered
2 c  Broccoli florets -- cooked,
Tender crisp
4    Green onions -- chopped
Salt and fresh ground pepper
To taste
Yogurt Dressing:
3/4 c  Low Fat plain yogurt
1 ts Fresh dill -- finely chopd
1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Fresh ground pepper
1 tb Safflower oil
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Tarragon or other
Herb-flavored vinegar
1/2 c  Cucumbers, seeded -- finely
Chopd
2    Green onions -- finely
Chopd
In large bowl, combine chicken, artichokes, broccoli, onions, salt and
pepper to taste. Cover and refrigerate. Yogurt Dressing: In bowl,
combine yogurt, dill, sugar, salt, pepper, oil, lemon juice, vinegar,
cucumber and onions. Blend. Cover and refrigerate. Mix Salad with
dressing just before serving.
