Title: CHICKEN STEW
Categories: Crockpot, Chicken, Potatoes, Low-fat, Wrv
Yield: 10 Servings
MMMMM-----------------WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A----------------------
2 lb Chicken breasts; skin, bone,
-1" cubes
2 cn Fat-free chicken broth
3 c  Potatoes; peel, cube
1 c  Onion; chop
1 c  Celery; slice
1 c  Carrots; slice thin
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Rubbed sage
1/2 ts Dried thyme
6 oz Can no-salt-added tomato
-paste
1/4 c  Cold water
3 tb Cornstarch
In a slow cooker, combine the first 11 ingredients; cover and cook on
HIGH for 4 hours. Mix water and cornstarch until smooth; stir into
stew. Cook, covered, 30 minutes more or until the vegetables are
tender. 193 cal; 3 gr fat; 14% fat. Source: Country Woman 3/97. MM
Waldine Van Geffen vghc42a.
