Title: CHICKEN FRYING BATTER
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Corn meal, yellow
1 1/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Baking powder
2 tb Salt
2 tb Pepper
1/2 ts Parsley flakes
Mix all ingredients together.  Cut up chicken and wash. Dip chicken in
batter and deep fry in hot grease. Randy Rigg
