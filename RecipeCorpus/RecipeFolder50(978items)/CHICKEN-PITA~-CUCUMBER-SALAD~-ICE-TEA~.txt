Title: CHICKEN PITA~ CUCUMBER SALAD~ ICE TEA~
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
2 oz Cooked chicken breast meat
1 ea Small pita
Lettuce
2 ea Tomato slices
1 tb Honey
1 tb Dijon mustard
1/2 c  Sliced cucumber
2 tb Low-cal vinaigrette
Combine sliced chicken, lettuce, tomato, honey and mustard. Pack into
pita bread.  Serve with cucumber salad and ice tea. Posted by: Sheila
