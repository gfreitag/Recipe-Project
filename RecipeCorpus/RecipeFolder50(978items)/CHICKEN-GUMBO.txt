Title: CHICKEN GUMBO
Categories: Main dish, Cajun, Chicken
Yield: 1 Gallon +
1 c  Flour
1 c  Oil
1    Chicken, about 4 lbs, cut up
2 lg Onions, chopped
1 c  Celery, chopped
2 ts Parsley, chopped
2 ts Green onion tops, chopped
Salt
Black pepper
Brown the chicken in the oil.  Take the chicken out. Add the flour to
the oil and make a medium brown roux. Cook onions and celery in the
roux. Put chicken back in and add 1 gallon of water slowly . Cook
slowly until chicken is almost ready to fall off the bones. Add water
as needed. Salt and pepper to taste.  Add parsley and onion tops.
Serve over rice.
