Title: CHICKEN JAMBALAYA 1
Categories: Creole, Poultry
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Onion,chopped
1 c  Celery,diced
1/4 c  Butter,margarine,chicken fat
1 qt Chicken broth
1 c  Rice,uncooked
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Black pepper
4 c  Chicken,cooked,diced
1 1/2 c  Carrots,diced
2 tb Parsley,minced
1. In 2-quart saucepan, cook onion and celery in butter until golden
brown.
2. Add chicken broth, rice, salt and pepper; cover and cook over low
heat
20    minutes.
3. Add chicken and carrots; cook 15 minutes longer, or until carrots
are tender.
4. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
