Title: CHICKEN SALAD RBTN28A
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 10 Servings
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Sweet pickles
Baked chicken
1/2 lb Grapes (seedless)
3/4 c  Mayonaise
1/4 c  Pimentos
Remove the chicken from the bone and chop into small pieces. Mix
remaining ingredients with chicken and chill.
