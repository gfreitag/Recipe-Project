Title: CHICKEN MACARONI CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Pasta, Poultry, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------KAREN GODFREY----------------------------
1 1/2 c  Macaroni, uncooked
1 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 1/2 c  Chicken or turkey, cooked
4 oz Mushrooms, drained
1 c  Cream of mushroom soup
1 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Curry powder
1 c  Peas, frozen
Heat oven to 350F. Stir together all ingredients; pour into an
ungreased 1 1/2 qt. casserole dish. Cover; bake 1 hour.
