Title: CHICKEN CACCIATORE
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Pieces  chicken
1 c  Sliced  onion
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Peanut oil
1 ea Medium clove   garlic
1 cn 1-lb can plum tomatos
1 tb Chopped parsley
1/2 ts Crushed oregano
1/4 ts Thyme
3/4 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1/4 lb Sliced  mushroom
Heat oil in large skillet.  Add chicken pieces, brown and set aside.
Add onion,green pepper and garlic; Cook until onion is tender. Force
Italian plum tomatoes through a strainer. Add tomato liquid,parsley,
oregano, thyme, salt and pepper to skillet. Cook over low heat for 15
minutes; stir occasionally. Add chicken; cover and cook over low heat
45 minutes. Stir occasionally. Add mushrooms.  Cook uncovered 15
minutes or until sauce is desired consistency.
