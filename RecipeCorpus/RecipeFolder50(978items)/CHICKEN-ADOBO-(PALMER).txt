Title: CHICKEN ADOBO (PALMER)
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1 sm Onion (chopped)
4    Cloves garlic
3/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Vinegar
Combine sauce in gredients.  Pour over 2 - 5 lbs of chicken.

This may be cooked either in a crockpot or on the oven.

In a crockpot, cook for 6 - 8 hours.  Serve hot

In the oven, bake in a 350 degree oven for 1 hour and simmer on the
stove till done.

Jackie Palmer
