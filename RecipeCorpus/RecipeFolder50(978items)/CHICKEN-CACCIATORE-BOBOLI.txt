Title: CHICKEN CACCIATORE BOBOLI
Categories: Pizza, Chicken
Yield: 1 Servings
-Waldine Van Geffen VGHC42A
1 lg 12" or
4 sm 6" Boboli Italian bread
-shells
1/4 oz Pk Boboli Pizza Sauce Pouch
-for each small shell or
5 oz Pk for large shell
5 oz Chicken; cook, dice
2 oz Green bell peppers; diced
1 oz Fresh mushrooms; chopped
1/2 oz Onions; minced
4 oz Mozzarella; shredded
Combine all ingredients except cheese and shell in a bowl. Spread
mixture on shell. Sprinkle top with cheese. Bake at 450~ for 12-15
minutes (5-8 minutes for 6") Source: Boboli Co (wrv)
