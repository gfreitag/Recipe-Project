Title: CHICKEN IN LIME
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Onion; minced
1    Cl Garlic; minced
1/2 ts Powdered cumin
1/2 ts Coriander; crushed
1/4 ts Turmeric
Chicken parts
1/4 c  Oil
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Lime juice
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper.  Brown in oil. Push to one
side. Saute onion and garlic.  Add cumin, coriander, turmeric, and
soy sauce. Cook, covered, stirring frequently, about 20 minutes. Add
water as needed. Sprinkle with lime juice just before serving.
