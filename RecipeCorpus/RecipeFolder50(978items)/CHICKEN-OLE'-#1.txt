Title: CHICKEN OLE' #1
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Hot cooked rice
4    Boneless skinless chicken
Halves cooked
2 c  Cups velveeta cheese salsa
Dip
Arrange rice on serving platter; top with chicken and velveeta cheese
spread salsa dip.  Garnish with bell and jalepeno peppers. Makes 4
servings
