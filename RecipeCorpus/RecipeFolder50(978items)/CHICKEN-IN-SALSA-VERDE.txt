Title: CHICKEN IN SALSA VERDE
Categories: Poultry, Mexican, Condiments, Salsa
Yield: 6 Servings
2 ts Canola oil
1 lb Chicken pieces, skinned,
-boned
1/2    Onion, chopped
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 lb Fresh or 12 ounces canned
-tomatillos
3    Whole roasted green chilies,
-chopped
1/2 c  Apple juice
1 tb Fresh chopped cilantro
Spray a non-stick skillet with vegetable cooking spray. Heat oil and
saute chicken.  Add onion and garlic, stirring until limp. Add
remaining ingredients.  Reduce heat, cover, and simmer about 15
minutes.
