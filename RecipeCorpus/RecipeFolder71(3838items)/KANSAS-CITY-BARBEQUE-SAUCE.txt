Title: KANSAS CITY BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Bar-b-q
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Salt
3 c  Canned beef broth
1/4 c  White vinegar
1/2 c  Worchestershire sauce
1 c  Tomato paste
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Honey
1 tb Chili powder
3 tb Paprika
1 tb Sage
1 1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Tumeric
3 cl Garlic, crushed
Blend all together in a large saucepan and simmer for
1 1/2    hours.
