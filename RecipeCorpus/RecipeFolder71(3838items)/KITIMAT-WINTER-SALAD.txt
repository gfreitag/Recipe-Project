Title: KITIMAT WINTER SALAD
Categories: Salads, Side dish
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Carrots, cleaned and grated
1 ts White wine or rice vinegar
1 tb Brown sugar
1/4 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Pinch of salt
Put carrots in a bowl and sprinkle with vinegar and
brown sugar. Mix together well. Rub raisins between
your hands to seperate them. Seed, if necessary. Add
raisins, mayonnaise and salt to carrots and gently mix
together. Chill thoroughly in refrigerator before
serving. Source: British Columbia Heritage Cookbook
by Mary Evans-Atkinson   pub 1984
ISBN 0-920620-60-4
