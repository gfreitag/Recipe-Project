Title: LINGUINI WITH CLAM SAUCE
Categories: None
Yield: 2 Servings
1 1/2 tb Olive oil
3    Cloves garlic
1/4 c  Chopped fresh parsley
1    Can chopped clams
1 1/2 tb White wine, to taste
Cooked linguini
Saute the garlic gently in the olive for about five minutes. Add rest
of ingredients and bring just to a simmer. Serve over linguini with
grated parmesean cheese. Serves two.
