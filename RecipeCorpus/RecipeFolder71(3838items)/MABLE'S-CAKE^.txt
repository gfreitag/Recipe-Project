Title: MABLE'S CAKE^
Categories: Crs, Der, Homestyle
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Lard
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Nutmeg
In a medium sized saucepan, mix together sugar, raisins, water and
lard. Bring to a boil and stir in baking soda. Cool and add flour,
salt and spices. Mix well. Bake in moderate oven.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
