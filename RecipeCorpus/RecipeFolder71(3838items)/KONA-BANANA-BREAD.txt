Title: KONA BANANA BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Butter; softened
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3 ea Eggs
1 1/4 c  Banana; mashed
1 3/4 c  Flour
1 1/4 ts Baking soda
3/4 ts Salt
Mix in order and pour into a greased 13x9 pan.  Bake at 350 degrees
for 35-40 minutes.  (In a glass pan, 325 for 30 minutes) Makes good
muffins too.  (25 minutes in muffin tins.)  Chocolate chips are a
good addition to the muffins.

Contributor:  Carol Mitchell
