Title: LAMB MARINADE
Categories: Condiments, Meats
Yield: 1 /2 cup
1/4 c  Salad or olive oil
2 tb Wine vinegar
2 ts Mustard, prepared
1 ts Rosemary leaves,
-crushed
1 1/2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Onion salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Ginger, ground
1/4 ts Garlic, minced
-(instant)
Mix together.  Refrigerate.  Marinate lamb overnight.

NOTES:

*  A marinade for any cut of lamb.

: Difficulty:  trivial.
: Time:  5 minutes preparation.
: Precision:  no need to measure.
