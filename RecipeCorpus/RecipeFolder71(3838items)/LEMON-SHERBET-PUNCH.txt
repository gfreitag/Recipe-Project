Title: LEMON SHERBET PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Frozen, Restaurants
Yield: 1 Qt
MMMMM-------------------MICKEY'S GOURMET CKBK------------------------
4 c  Lemon sherbet
1 c  Froz lemonade concentrate,
-thawed
3 c  Warm water
Ice cubes
Lemon slices
Liberty Tree Tavern, Magic Kingdom Place sherbet in mixing bowl. Using
mixer on low, add lemonade concentrate and water. Mix well, 5 mins.
Refrigerate. Serve in tall glasses with ice and garnish with lemon
slices.
