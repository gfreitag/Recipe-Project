Title: MAPLE BAKED BEANS #2
Categories: Appetizers, Pulses and
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Navy Beans -- (dry)
2 c  Chili Sauce
1 ts Dry Mustard
5    Strips Bacon -- chopped
1 c  Maple Syrup
1/2 ts Salt
1 md Onion -- chopped
Soak beans overnight and drain. Add remaining ingredients and enough
water to cover beans. Bake at 325 F for 2 hours or until beans mash
easily. Remove 1 cup cooked beans and mash them. Add to remaining to
beans to thicken.

8    Servings.
