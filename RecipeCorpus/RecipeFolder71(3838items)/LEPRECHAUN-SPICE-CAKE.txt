Title: LEPRECHAUN SPICE CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Water, hot
1 c  Guiness,warm(or more water)
2 tb Margarine (soy)
15 oz Raisins
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cloves
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
3 c  Whole wheat pastry flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
Bring sugar, water, Guiness, margarine, raisins, salt and spices to a
boil in a heavy saucepan, simmer for 5 minutes. Allow to cool. Stir
dry ingredients together. Mix into cooled mixture. Bake in a lined
(with 2 layers of wax paper) large loaf pan at 350F for 1 1/2 hours.
Cut in 24 slices. Total calories per slice-185 Fat-1 gram From the
Vegetarian Journal March/April 1993
