Title: KATHY HIRDLER'S FIRE CAMP CHILI
Categories: Chili
Yield: 1200 Servings
100 lb Pinto beans
48 ea Large onions, chopped
4 c  Jalapeno chilies with juice
40 lb Meat (ham, sausage, pork,
1 x  Bacon ends, ground beef, etc
4 c  Chili powder
1 x  Salt to taste
Soak beans overnight, then raise to a boil on high
heat. Add all ingredients and simmer until tender
(about 6 hours). Add water as necessary.  Stir
occasionally.  Makes 60 gallons. Serves 1200
approximately.
