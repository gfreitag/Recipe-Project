Title: MAPLE OATMEAL DROPS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1 ea Egg
1/2 c  Maple syrup (dark)
1/3 c  Dairy sour cream
1 3/4 c  All purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/4 c  Rolled oats (quick)
In a small bowl or mixer, cream butter and sugar until light and
fluffy. Add egg, maple syrup and sour cream. Beat well. Mix flour,
baking soda, cinnamon, ginger and salt. Add to creamed mixture. Stir
in oats. Drop by spoonful on greased pan. Bake at 375Ã¸ 8 to 10
minutes. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar. Makes 4 Dozen Cookies. By
Florence Phelps From Maple Dessert Recipes by Massachusetts Maple
