Title: LIMA BEANS W/CHILE &SEED
Categories: Snacks, Low-cal
Yield: 1 Servings
Jalapeno;Chopped;
2/3 c  Lima Beans
1/2 c  Sunflower Seeds
1    Tomatoe;Chopped;medium
2 tb Onion;Chopped
1 ts Cilantro;Chopped fresh
Heat Sunflower seeds, Onion, Tomatoe & Jalapeno till onion is
clear. Add Lima Beans and cook for a couple minutes. Add cilantro &
serve.
