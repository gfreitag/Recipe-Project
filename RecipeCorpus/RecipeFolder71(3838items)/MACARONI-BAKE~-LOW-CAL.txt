Title: MACARONI BAKE~ LOW CAL
Categories: Main dish, Low-cal
Yield: 5 Servings
2 c  Macaroni, cooked
1 ea Onion, chopped
2 tb Margarine
1/4 c  Flour
2 c  Skimmed milk
2 ts Dill weed
2 ts Parsley
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Pepper
2 c  Low fat cottage cheese
1/3 c  Bread crumbs
1 pn Paprika
Preheat oven 350F.  Saute onions in margarine, add flour. Stir in
milk, little at a time until thick. Add spices. Add cheese. Add
macaroni. Pour into shallow pan. Top with crumbs and paprika. Bake 45
minutes.
