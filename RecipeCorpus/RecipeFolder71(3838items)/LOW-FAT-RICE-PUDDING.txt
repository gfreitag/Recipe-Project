Title: LOW-FAT RICE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
2 ea Egg whites
1 ea Egg
2 c  Cooked rice
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Raisins
2 c  Skim milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
Ground cinnamon
Heat oven to 325*.  Beat egg whites and egg in ungreased 1 1/2 qt
casserole . Stir in remaining ingredients. Spinkle with cinnamon to
taste. Bake uncov ered 50-60 minutes, stirring after 30 minutes,
until knife inserted halfway between center and edge comes out clean.
Serve warm or cold. Immediately re frigerate any remaining pudding.
