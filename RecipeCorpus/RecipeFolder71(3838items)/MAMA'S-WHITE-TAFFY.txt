Title: MAMA'S WHITE TAFFY
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1/2 tb Vinegar
1/4 ts Cream of Tartar
1 ts Lemon extract
Place the sugar, water, vinegar and cream of tartar in a sauce
pan. Cook to 290 degrees, or to the soft-crack stage. Add the extract
and pour out on a butterred platter. When cool enough to handle, pull
until white and glossy. Cut into sticks or cushions.

Culinary Arts Institute Encyclopedia Cookbook, 1948
