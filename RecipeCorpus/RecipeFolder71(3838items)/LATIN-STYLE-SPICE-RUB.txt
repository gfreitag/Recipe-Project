Title: LATIN-STYLE SPICE RUB
Categories: Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Cumin
1/4 c  Chili powder
2 tb Coriander seeds, crushed
1 tb Cinnamon
1 tb Brown sugar, packed
2 tb Salt
1 tb Red pepper flakes
2 tb Black pepper, fresh ground
Combine all the ingredients and grind to a powder in a spice mill,
coffee grinder, or with a mortar and pestle. Cover in a cool, dark
place, it will keep for about 6 weeks.

Source:  The Great Barbecue Companion, Mops, Sops, Sauces, and Rubs by
Bruce Bjorkman
