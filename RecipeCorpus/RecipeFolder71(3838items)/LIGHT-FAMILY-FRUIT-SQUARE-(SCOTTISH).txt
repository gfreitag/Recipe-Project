Title: LIGHT FAMILY FRUIT SQUARE (SCOTTISH)
Categories: Cakes, Scottish
Yield: 1 Cake
8 oz SR Flour
6 oz Butter or Margarine
6 oz Caster Sugar
3    Eggs
Pinch of Salt
2 tb Milk
3 oz Dried Fruit
Grated Zest of 1 Orange
Set oven to 150F/Gas 4. Line a 8 inch square cake tin with
:
buttered greaseproof paper. Cream together the butter or margarine
and sugar into a bowl. Add the eggs one at a time, beating each well
into the mixture. Sift the flour and salt and fould in gradully with
the milk. Add the fruit and the orange zest, ensuring it is evenly
mixed in. Spoon the mixture into the tin and bake for 40-45 minutes
until a skewer inserted into the cake comes out clean. Turn on to a
wire rack to cool and then cut into squares.
