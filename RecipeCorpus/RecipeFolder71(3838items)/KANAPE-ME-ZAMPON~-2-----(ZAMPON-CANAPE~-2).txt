(ZAMPON CANAPE~ 2)
Title: KANAPE ME ZAMPON~ 2
Categories: Appetizers, Greek
Yield: 1 Servings
Zampon
Crackers
Cooked beet slices
Pitted black olives
Cut zampon to fit shape of the crackers you are
using.  With a sharp knife, cut out small hearts and
diamonds from the beet rounds; cut out small clubs and
spades from the olives.  Decorate the zampon canapes
with these so they resemble playing cards.
