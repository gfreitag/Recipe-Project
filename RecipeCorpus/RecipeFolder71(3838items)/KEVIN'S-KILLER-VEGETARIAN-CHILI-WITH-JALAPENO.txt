Title: KEVIN'S KILLER VEGETARIAN CHILI WITH JALAPENO
Categories: Soups, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Dark red kidney beans,drain
1 cn Black beans, drained
1 cn Garbonzo beans, drained
1 cn Stewed tomatoes, drained
Cut in smaller pieces
2 cn (15 oz) tomato sauce
(You may want to add)
1    Green pepper, chopped
1 md White onion, chopped
1 cn Beer (optional)
1    Handful jalapenos, sliced
Chili powder
1-1/2
1 1/2 tb Cumin
3/4 c  Prepared TVP(about 3/8c dry)
Place all ingredients, except beer, in a large kettle
and mix well. Add beer, little by little, as chili
comes to a boil. Reduce hear and continue cooking for
20-25 minutes. (Be sure to use rest of beer during
this part of cooking and stir chili occasionally to
mix it well.)
