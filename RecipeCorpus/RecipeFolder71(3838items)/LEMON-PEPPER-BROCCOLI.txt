Title: LEMON PEPPER BROCCOLI
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Small broccoli florets
1 tb Olive oil
2 ts Freshly grated lemon peel
1/8 ts Crushed red pepper -- to
Taste
1/8 ts Salt
Bring 1-inch of water to boil in a medium saucepan. Place broccoli in
a vegetable steamer, set into saucepan, cover and cook 2 minutes or
until broccoli is crisp-tender. Remove insert and let broccoli drain.

Heat oil in a medium skillet over medium heat.  Add lemon peel and
crushed red pepper and stir until peel begins to brown, about 30
seconds. Add broccoli and salt and stir until hot, about 1 minute.
