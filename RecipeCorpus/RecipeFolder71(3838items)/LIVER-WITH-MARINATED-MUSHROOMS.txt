Title: LIVER WITH MARINATED MUSHROOMS
Categories: Offal
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Fresh mushrooms
3/4 lb Calves liver,
1/4 c  French salad dressing
-Minced parsley
For 30 minutes marinate mushrooms in dressing.  Drain. Reserve
marinade. Brush liver on both sides with marinade. Place on broiler
pan. Broil 3 minutes; turn, top with mushrooms and broil 3-4 minutes
longer. Garnish with parsley.
