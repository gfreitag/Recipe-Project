Title: LEMON MARINADE.
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
Suitable for pork, chicken
And fish.
15 ml 1 tbsp sunflower oil.
1    Clove garlic, crushed.
Juice of half a lemon.
Ground black pepper.
30 ml 2 tbsp chopped fresh herbs,
-or 2,5 ml half tsp
-dried mixed herbs.
Mix all ingredients together. Leave to marinade for at least half an
hour. Cook over a barbecue, under the grill or in the oven at 200 c,
400 f, gas 6. VARIATION Try spices instead of the herbs.
