Title: LISA'S CORNY BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 10 Servings
3 c  Flour
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Yellow Cornmeal
1 1/2 ts Dry Yeast
7/8 c  Milk; warmed
1/4 c  Water; plus enough for doug
1 1/2 tb Butter
Add all ingred. into Bread machine in order per your manufacturer.
NOTE: Very nice texture and flavor.  A definite DO AGAIN!
