Title: KIDNEY BEAN SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks, Spreads
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 1/2 c  Kidney beans, cooked
1 ea Garlic clove
1/2 ts Cumin seeds
1/2 ts Tabasco sauce
1/2 tb Cider vinegar
2 tb Water
2 tb Fresh cilantro or parsley
Place all the ingredients in a food processor or
blender and process until smooth. "The McDougall Plan"
by John McDougall Typed by Lisa Greenwood
