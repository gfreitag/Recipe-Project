Title: LIGHT & ZESTY PASTA SALAD
Categories: Low fat, Pasta, Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Fat-free Zesty Italian salad
Dressing
1/2 c  Fat-free Miracle Whip salad
Dressing
1 c  Broccoli florets
2 c  Corkscrew noodles, cooked
And drained
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1/2 c  Tomato -- chopped
1/4 c  Scallions -- sliced
Combine the dressings in a small bowl and mix well. Steam the
broccoli just until crisp-tender.  Drain and let cool.

In a large bowl, mix the broccoli florets with the pasta, green
pepper, tomato, scallions, and dressing. Mix well. Chill 2 hours.

Less that 1 gram of fat per serving.
