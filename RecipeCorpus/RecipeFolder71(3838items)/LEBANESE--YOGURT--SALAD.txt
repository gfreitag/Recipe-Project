Title: LEBANESE  YOGURT  SALAD
Categories: L, E, B, A, N
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lg Cucumber, peeled, seeded &
Diced
2    Cloves garlic, crushed
2 c  Yogurt, beaten with a fork
To
Loosen it
3 tb Fresh mint or 1 tbsp. dried
Mint
Preparation : Simply combine ingredients and mix together with a fork.
Serve as a salad or side dish. Serves 4-6.
