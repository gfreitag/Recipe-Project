Title: MAMA'S ITALIAN MEATLOAF
Categories: Meats, Hamburger, Italian, Beef
Yield: 12 Servings
2 lb Lean ground beef
1 lb Italian sausage
1 1/2 c  Cracker crumbs
2    Eggs, beaten
1 md Onion, chopped
1    Bell pepper, chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Oregano
1 cl Garlic,minced
1 c  Pizza sauce
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/2 lb Boiled ham,sliced thin
8 oz Grated Mozzarella cheese
1 cn (8 oz) mushrooms,drained
Mix first eleven ingredients together.Reserve 1/4 cup pizza sauce. On
a large sheet of wax paper,pat meat into a 10 x 13" rectangle.
Arrange ham on top of meat mixture,leaving a small margin.Spread
mushrooms over ham.Sprinkle cheese over all.Reserve small amount of
cheese.Start at 10" size and roll meat like a jelly roll.Use wax
paper to lift.When rolled tightly,seal edges.Place seam side down in
a 9 x 13" pan.Brush with reserved pizza sauce.Bake 1 1/4 hours @ 375
degrees,10 minutes.Before done,sprinkle reserved cheese over and
finish baking.Serves 10 to 12.
