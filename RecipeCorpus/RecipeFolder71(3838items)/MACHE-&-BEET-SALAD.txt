Title: MACHE & BEET SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Salad
2 c  Mache
4    White or golden beets
-- cooked, peeled & chopped
1 ts Chopped lovage or parsley
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
5 tb Olive oil
2    Eggs, hard-boiled
1/2 c  Home made croutons
In a salad bowl combine lovage or parsley, vinegar and oil. Add
beets, toss to coat and marinate for 1 hour. Add mache, toss, garnish
with eggs and croutons.

From: The Cook's Garden catalog - Spring/Summer 1989 - page 4)
