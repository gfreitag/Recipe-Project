Title: LEMON-ORANGE FILLING
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 10 Servings
1 c  Sugar
1/3 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Orange rind; grated
1 tb Lemon rind; grated
1 1/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
4    Egg yolks; well beaten
Recipe by: Southern Living Preparation Time: 0:10 Combine sugar,
flour, salt, and water in a heavy saucepan; stir well. Stir in fruit
rind and juices. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until
mixture thickens and boils. Gradually stir about one-fourth of hot
mixture into egg yolks; add to remaining hot mixture, stirring
constantly. Return to a boil; cook 1 to 2 minutes, stirring
constantly. Remove from heat, and let cool completely.  (Mixture will
be thick.) Yield: 2-1/4 cups.
