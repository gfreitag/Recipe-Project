Title: LACTO-OVO: APPLE CAKE
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Eggs
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Oil
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour(unbleached white)
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
5 md Apples, diced
1 c  Walnuts, chopped
Beat eggs until light & fluffy.  Gradually add in sugar, oil &
vanilla. Sift together flour, salt, soda, cinnamon & nutmeg (I don't
sift, just mix);  add to first mixture. Stir in apples & walnuts.
Place in greased 9x13" pan.  Bake in 350 deg. oven for 45 min. or
until center springs back when touched.  Allow to cool in pan on a
rack for 15 min; spread with Cream Cheese Frosting (recipe follows).

CREAM CHEESE FROSTING

1 pkg (3 oz) cream cheese 3 T butter, softened 1/2 t vanilla 1 1/2 c
powdered sugar

Beat cream cheese and butter together.  Add vanilla and beat.
Gradually beat in powdered sugar until smooth.

Note:  Both cake and frosting freeze well. -- Gina Pasquale Email:
pasquale@lanl.gov
