Title: LEMON CREAM DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces, Vegetarian, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Tahini
8 oz Firm (or firm silken) tofu
1    Lemon; zested and juiced
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1    Garlic clove; minced
1/4 c  Brown rice syrup; -OR-
1/3 c  -Honey
2 tb White miso
1/2 c  Water
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until the mixture is
smooth. Cool and use as desired.

Source: Friendly Foods - by Brother Ron Pickarski, O.F.M.
