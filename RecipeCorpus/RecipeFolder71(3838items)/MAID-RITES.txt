Title: MAID-RITES
Categories: Beef, Sandwiches
Yield: 1 Servings
-LISA CRAWLEY/TSPN
2 lb Ground Beef
1 c  Water
1 md Onion; grated
3/4 c  Catsup
2 ts Dry Mustard
1    Or 2 tbsp. Chili Powder
Salt and Pepper
Brown the ground beef and pour off the fat.  Add the water and onion
and cook over med. heat for 5-10 min. Add the remaining ingred. and
cook for 15-20 min.
