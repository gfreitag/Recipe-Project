Title: LENTIL FATTA SALAD
Categories: Salads, Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Lentils
4 c  Water
1 c  Onion, finely chopped
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 ts Salt and pepper
1/2 c  Pita, cubed
1/2 c  Green onion, chopped
Fresh lemon juice
Olive oil
Boil together the lentils, water, onion, oil, salt and pepper until
very soft (about 50 to 60 minutes). Drain and cool. Add cubed pita
bread and green onion; combine well. Serve with fresh lemon juice and
additional olive oil to taste.
