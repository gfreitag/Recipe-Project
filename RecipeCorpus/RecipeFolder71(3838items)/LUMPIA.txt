Title: LUMPIA
Categories: Appetizers, Filipino
Yield: 30 Lumpia
1 lb Ground meat or poultry
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Garlic cloves; minced
1 tb Patis or soy sauce
1 lg Onion; chopped
2 tb Flour
1 cn (8 oz) water chestnuts
-- chopped
1/4 c  Water
2 lb Bean sprouts
30    Lumpia wrappers
1 1/2 ts Salt
Salad oil for frying
In a saucepan, brown meat with garlic and onion.  Add water
chestnuts, bean sprouts, salt, pepper, and patis or soy sauce; cook
for 2 minutes. Drain and cool thoroughly. Combine flour and water to
make a paste. To make lumpia, place 2 tablespoons filling on a
wrapper. Fold nearest edge of wrapper over filling; fold left and
right sides toward center. Roll tightly toward open edge.  Seal with
paste.  Heat oil to 375 F. Fry lumpia until golden brown; drain.
Serve with Lumpia Dipping Sauce. Makes 30 lumpia.
