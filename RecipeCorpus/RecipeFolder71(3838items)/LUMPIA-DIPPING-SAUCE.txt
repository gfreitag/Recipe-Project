Title: LUMPIA DIPPING SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Condiments, Filipino
Yield: 1 Recipe
3    Garlic cloves; minced
1/8 ts Salt
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/8 ts Pepper
Combine all ingredients; serve with hot lumpia.  Makes about 1/4 cup.
