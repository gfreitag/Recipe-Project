Title: MA FOSTER'S SLOPPY JOES
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Beef, ground
1    Onion, chopped
1 c  Catsup
2 tb Vinegar
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Mustard
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Celery seed
Salt and pepper,
-to taste
In a frying pan, brown ground beef and onion.  Add remaining
ingredients and cook slowly for 15 minutes or more. Serve hot by
scooping the mixture onto toasted buns.

NOTES:

*  A spicy and simple sloppy joe -- This recipe comes from my wife's
mom, who got it from her mother-in-law in western New York state.
It's very easy to make and tastes a lot better than any mix.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  5 minutes preparation, 15 minutes cooking.
: Precision:  approximate measurement OK.
