Title: MANHATTAN
Categories: Mixed, Drinks
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 oz Blended whiskey
1/2 oz Sweet vermouth
STRAIGHT UP: Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add whiskey and
sweet vermouth. Stir and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
Garnish with a cherry.

ON THE ROCKS: Fill a rocks glass with ice cubes. Add whiskey and sweet
vermouth and stir. Garnish with a cherry.
