Title: MAPLE OATMEAL
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 9 Servings
-CHRISTINE ERICKSON MMVH58B
1 pk Yeast
1 c  Oats; quick cooking
3 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
1/3 c  Maple syrup
1 tb Oil
1 1/4 c  Plus 1 T warm water
Put all the ingredients into the pan in the order listed; select white
bread and push start. -----
