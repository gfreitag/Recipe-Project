Title: LITTLE CAESAR'S CRAZY SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Dressings, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Tomato paste (15-ounce)
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Dried marjoram
1/4 ts Dried oregano
1/4 ts Ground thyme
1.  Combine all the ingredients in an uncovered saucepan over medium
heat. 2. When the sauce begins to bubble, reduce the heat and simmer
for 30 minutes, stirring often. 3. Remove the sauce from the heat and
let it cool. Store in a tightly sealed container in the refrigerator;
it will keep for 3 to 4 weeks. Makes
1 1/2    cups.
