Title: KOOL-AID PLAYDOUGH
Categories: Kids, Misc, Tlc
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Salt
2 pk Unsweetened Kool-aid
2 c  Boiling water
3 tb Vegetable oil
Mix dry ingredients.  Add water and oil, stir.  Mix or
knead with hands when cool.  Store in Ziploc bag or
container with lid.  Lasts for several months.
