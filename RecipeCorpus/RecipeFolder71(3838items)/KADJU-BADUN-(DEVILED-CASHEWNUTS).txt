Title: KADJU BADUN (DEVILED CASHEWNUTS)
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
225 g  Cashewnuts
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Water
250 ml Oil
1 ts Chilli powder
Mix  the  cashewnuts,  paprika,  turmeric,  salt  and
water together in a bowl and allow to stand for 1
hour. Heat the oil and deep fry the cashewnuts until
evenly browned. Remove from the oil, drain well and
place on serving dish. Sprinkle with chilli powder and
mix well.
