Title: MANDARIN ORANGE-ALMOND SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2    Head lettuce
1/2    Head romaine lettuce
2 cn 8oz. mandarin oranges(drain)
2 c  Sliced celery
2 tb Minced parsley
4    Green onions sliced
1 c  Sliced almonds
4 tb Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
Dash of tabasco
Dash of lemon pepper
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Canola oil
Wash and tear lettuce into bite size pieces. Combine oranges, celery,
parsley and green onion. Chill well. Put sugar into heavy skillet, add
almonds and stir constantly over low heat until carmelized. Turn out
onto foil - cool and break into small pieces. Dressing: Combine all
ingredients and toss with salad just before serving.
