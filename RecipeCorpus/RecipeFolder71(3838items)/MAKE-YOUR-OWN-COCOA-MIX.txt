Title: MAKE YOUR OWN COCOA MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
MARY WILSON  BWVB02B
5 c  Instant nonfat dry milk
3/4 c  Cocoa
3/4 c  Sugar
Blend all ingredients and store in plastic bag in a dry place. Use as
needed. For 4 cups chocolate milk, place 1 1/2 cup cocoa mix in a
pot. Add 3 cups water. Heat quickly to boiling and lower heat and
cook for 3 minutes, stirring constantly. Chill and serve. For 1 cup
chocolate milk, use 6 tbsp, cocoa mix and 3/4 cup water.
