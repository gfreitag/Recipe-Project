Title: LEMON MIST
Categories: Desserts, Osg
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Sugar
Juice of 1 lemon and small
-amount of fine cut peel
1 c  Milk
1 c  Cream; whipped
Mix sugar and lemon together, let stand at least 2 hours. Mix all
thoroughly, place in freezing trays. Freeze as quickly as possible,
Stir once or twice during freezing. Makes 6 average servings.

Source: Ellen Dush, Wyoming Grange, Licking County, OH
