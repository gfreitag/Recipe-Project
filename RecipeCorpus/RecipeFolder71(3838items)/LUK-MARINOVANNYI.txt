Title: LUK MARINOVANNYI
Categories: Russian, Ethnic, Vegetables, Sidedish
Yield: 1 Quart
2 tb Freh dill minced
1 ea 1 lb jar pearl onions,
- drained
1 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Red pepper flakes
1 ts Caraway seeds
12 ea Black peppercorns
Put the drained onions into a jar. Combine the other ingredinets
together in saucepan. Bring to a boil, pour over the onions. Cap the
jar tightly and cool to room temp. Refrigerate for 2 weeks before
opening.
