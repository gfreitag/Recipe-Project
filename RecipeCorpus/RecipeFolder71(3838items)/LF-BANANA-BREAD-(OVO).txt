Title: LF BANANA BREAD (OVO)
Categories: Digest, Nov.
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Applesauce (or lemon
Yogurt)
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Liquid egg substitute (I
Used Second Nature)
3    Overripe bananas, mashed
2 c  Flour (less if you use whole
Wheat)
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Black walnuts (or English,
If you must)
1/2 ts Salt
Combine applesauce/yogurt and sugar. Add egg and bananas. Beat in
flour, soda and salt (sift these together first). Add nuts. Bake in
moderate oven (350 degrees) until a toothpick (or knife) inserted in
the center of the loaf comes out clean. Let cool at least 10 minutes
and remove from loaf pan. Makes 1 good size loaf.
