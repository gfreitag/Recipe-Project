Title: LUMPIA SAUCE (PAALAT)
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
12    Sugar
1    Soy sauce
2    Port or chicken broth
1    Salt
2    Cornstarch
14    Water
6    Garlic; minced
Recipe by: Nila Sweeney 1. Blend sugar, soy, broth and salt and bring
to boil.

2. Disperse cornstarch in water, then use to thicken broth mixture.

3. Sprinkle with minced garlic and serve.
