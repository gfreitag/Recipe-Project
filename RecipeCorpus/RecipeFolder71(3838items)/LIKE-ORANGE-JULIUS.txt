Title: LIKE ORANGE JULIUS
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 3 Servings
6 oz Orange Juice Frz CONCENTRATE
1 c  Milk
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg
8    Ice cubes
Put in blender and blend until ice cubes are gone, about 2 minutes.
Serve right away while frothy. Serving size: 12-13 oz
