Title: LOUISIANNA SHRIMP SALAD
Categories: Meats, Seafood, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Shrimp, cooked, chopped
2    Celery stalks, chopped
1    Egg, boiled, finely chopped
1 ts Pickle relish
2 tb Mayonnaise
Salt & pepper to taste
Mix ingredients together thoroughly and add seasoning to taste. Serve
on lettuce or stuffed Avocado.
