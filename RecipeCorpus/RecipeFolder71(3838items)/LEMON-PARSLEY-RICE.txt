Title: LEMON PARSLEY RICE
Categories: Rice
1 c  rice
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  finely chopped parsley
Juice of half a lemon
:
2 TB butter
Salt & pepper
:

Heat 2 cups of water to a boil and stir in rice. Return to a boil,
cover and reduce heat to low. Cook for 20 minutes. Remove lid and
fluff rice with a fork. Toss in parsley, lemon and butter. Season and
serve.
