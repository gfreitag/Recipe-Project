Title: KOLACHES - CHEESE FILLING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 36 Servings
1 1/2 c  Creamed cottage cheese,
Small curd
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/8 ts Salt
3 ts Melted butter
Drain cottage cheese, press in cheese cloth to
remove as much liquid as you can.

In mixing bowl, combine cheese and remaining
ingredients until blended and creamy.

Yield:   1 1/2 to 2 cups

Enjoy! - Jeff Duke
