Title: MANGO KEY LIME MARGARITAS
Categories: Beverages, Mexican
Yield: 2 Servings
2    Tequila
1    Cointreau
1/4    Fresh mango; peeled and
-chopped
1/2 c  Fresh orange juice
1/2    Key or mexican lime; juice
-only
1 c  Ice; crushed
2 sl Key or mexican lime
-(garnish)
Combine tequila, Cointreau, mango, orange juice, lime juice and ice
in a blender and blend until slushy. Serve in large martini glasses.
Garnish with lime slices. Contributor: Nuevo Tex-Mex - David Garrido
& Robb Walsh
