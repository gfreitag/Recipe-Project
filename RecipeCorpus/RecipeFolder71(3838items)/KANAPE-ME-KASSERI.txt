Title: KANAPE ME KASSERI
Categories: Appetizers, Greek
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Kasseri cheese
Sliced white bread
6 sl Mortadella or other salami>>
Chopped.
Chopped olives or pickles>>>
Or relish.
Trim crust from bread and cut into desired shapes
with canape cutter or sharp knife.  Top each pc.
w/thin slice of cheese cut to fit the sape of the
bread.  Granist edges w/mortadella or other salami,
and the chopped olives, pickles, or relish.
