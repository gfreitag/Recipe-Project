Title: LOMI LOMI SALMON  FBFJ97A
Categories: Fish, Hawaii, Asian
Yield: 2 Servings
4 oz Smoked salmon;finely chopped
1/2 c  Green onion; sliced
1/2 c  Tomato; peeled,chopped
2 tb Green pepper; chopped
Mix all ingredients together with hands.  Chill well before
serving. Formatted by: Dorie Villarreal
