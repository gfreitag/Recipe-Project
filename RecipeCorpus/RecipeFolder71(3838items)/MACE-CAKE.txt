Title: MACE CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Butter (or 1/2 c. butter &
-1/2 c. Crisco)
2 c  Sugar
5    Eggs
2 c  Sifted flour
1/2 c  Milk or buttermilk
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Mace
2 ts Vanilla
Mix all ingredients and bake in a greased and floured bundt pan for
one hour at 350 degree.

Top with icing or serve plain.
