Title: LITTLE CHOCOLATE POTS
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 7 Servings
1 1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Chocolate Chips
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Sugar
1 pn Salt
Pour milk into saucepan and heat to boiling. Meanwhile, combine
remaining ingredients in blender. When milk is boiling, add other
ingredients and stir until chocolate is dissolved and mixture begins
to thicken. Pour mixture into blender and blend at low speed for 1
minute. Pour into 7 custard cups.  Chill at least 2 hours before
serving.
