Title: LOVE APPLE PIE
Categories: Pies, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
1/3 c  Tomato catsup
2 ts Lemon juice
5 c  Thinly sliced peeled apples,
1 x  (I like the tartness of Gran
3/4 c  Granulated sugar
3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground allspice
1/3 c  Butter or margarine, softene
1 ea Unbaked 9-inch pie shell
Blend catsup and lemon juice; combine with apples. Combine sugar,
flour, cinnamon and allspice; cut in butter until thoroughly mixed.
