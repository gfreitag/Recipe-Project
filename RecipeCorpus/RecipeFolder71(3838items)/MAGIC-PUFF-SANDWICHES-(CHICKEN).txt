Title: MAGIC PUFF SANDWICHES (CHICKEN)
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/4 c  Bisquick baking mix
1 cn Mushroom stems & pieces
3/4 c  Mayonnaise or Salad
Dressing
(4 oz. drained)
1    Egg
1/4 c  Onions -- sliced green
1 c  Cut-up cooked chicken
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Cheddar chese -- shredded
Pepper -- dash of
(about 8 oz.)
6 sl Bread
Heat oven to 450F.  Mix baking mix, mayonnaise and egg. Stir in other
ingredients, except bread.  Spread ingredients evenly on bread. Bake
on ungreased cookie sheets until puffy and golden brown, about 10
minutes. Serve immediately.  6-open-faced sandwiches.

High altitude directions: (3500 to 6500); heat oven to 475F. Stir 2
tbs all-purpose flour into baking mix. Bake. 10 to 12 minutes.
