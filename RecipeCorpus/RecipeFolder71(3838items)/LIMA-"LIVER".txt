Title: LIMA "LIVER"
Categories: Appetizers, Pulses and, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
10 oz Lima Beans -- frozen
3    To 4 Hard-Boiled Eggs
1/4    Bag Ground Nuts
2    Onions -- diced
Salt -- to taste
Cook beans.  Saute onion.  Combine all ingredients in blender and mix
well until spreading consistency is attained.
