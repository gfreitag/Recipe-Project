Title: KUKN BARBEQUE SAUCE *** DTNR09C
Categories: Bar-b-q, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
12 oz Chili sauce
2 c  Brown sugar
1 x  Lemon juice; half
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ds Tobasco
Combine ingredients in sauce pan.   Bring to boil, and
reduce to simmer for at least 1/2 hour.    Tastes
great on chicken, pork, or sausages. KUKN is a new
radio station in our city, so that's where the name
comes from. Hope you like it! FROM:    LINNETT VOGEL
(DTNR09C)
