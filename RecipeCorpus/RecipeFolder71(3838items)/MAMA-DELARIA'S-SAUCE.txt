Title: MAMA DELARIA'S SAUCE
Categories: New, Text, Import
3 TB olive oil
Yield: 4 Servings
1    head garlic, -- chopped
1/2 lg Vidalia onion, -- chopped
1/4 lb lean ground beef
Salt
Pepper
Fresh ground red pepper
:
:
:
4 lg 28 ounce cans tomato sauce
1 lg 28 ounce can crushed
tomatoes
:
1/4 c  grated Romano cheese
1/4 c  grated Parmesan cheese

In large saute pan heat olive oil. Add onions and saute until onions
become transparent. Add garlic. Add beef and season with salt,
pepper, and red pepper. Cook until beef begins to brown. Once brown,
add sauce and crushed tomatoes. Stir to combine. Bring to a simmer,
reduce heat and let simmer for about 4 - 6 hours. Before serving add
Romano and Parmesan cheese.
