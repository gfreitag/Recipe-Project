Title: KASHA BREAD
Categories: Dg, Whole wheat, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/8 c  Water
3 tb Raisins,Seedless
1 1/2    Egg Beaters
1 1/2 tb Apple Juice,concentrate
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Gluten
1/3 c  Buckwheat Flour,Whole-Groat
3 c  Whole-Wheat Flour
2 ts Yeast
NOTES : 63.2 cal\oz. Process the raisins and water in
food processor before inserting into machine. Rises
nicely. D. German IV Page 81 Medium
