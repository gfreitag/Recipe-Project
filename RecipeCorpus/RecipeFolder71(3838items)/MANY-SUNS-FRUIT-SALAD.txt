Title: MANY SUNS FRUIT SALAD
Categories: Miamiherald, Fruits, Salads
Yield: 10 Servings
2 c  Diced papaya
2 c  Diced mango
1/2 c  Passion fruit pulp or juice
-concentrate
1 ts Grated lemon zest
1 tb Grated ornage zest
Chopped minced leaves for
-garnish
Gently combine all ingredients in a glass bowl and chill. Garnish
with mint leaves.

Nutrtional info per serving: 26 cal; .4g pro, 7g carb, tr fat (3%)

Source: Kitchen Tropicale, Miami Herald 8/17/95 format by Lisa
Crawford, 7/27/96
