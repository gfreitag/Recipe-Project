Title: MANGO BLOSSOMS
Categories: Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Large mangoes,firm-ripe
1    Large orange,cut into wedges
Slide a sharp knife parallel to broad side of mango and against pit;
cut off both sides. Place halves cut side up; cut flesh to the skin,
but not through, to make 1/2" squares. Gently push up the skin to
flare out mango sections; serve with orange wedges.
