Title: LAURA'S ALLERGIC STRAWBERRY SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Strawberry jello
3/4 c  Boiling water
1 pk Frozen strawberries
-(partially thawed)
1    Mashed banana
8 oz Can crushed pineapple and
-juice (undrained)
8 oz Can sour cream
Dissolve jello in hot water. Add strawberries, banana, pineapple and
juice and sour cream. Pour half of above in pyrex mold and let set up
in refrigerator. Then put layer of sour cream on top. Cover with rest
of jello mixture and chill till firm or overnight.
