Title: LASAGNE
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Hamburger
1 c  Onion
2    Large garlic cloves
2 ts Oregano
2 cn Tomato soup
1/2 c  Water
2 ts Vinegar
1/2 lb Wide noodles
1 pt Cottage cheese
1/2 lb Mozerella cheese grated
brown meat and onion and garlic, add soup, water,oregano & vinegar.
Cook noodles and assemble alternately with the sauce and cheeses in
pan. Bake in moderate oven for 30 min. Let stand 15 min. to set
before serving.
