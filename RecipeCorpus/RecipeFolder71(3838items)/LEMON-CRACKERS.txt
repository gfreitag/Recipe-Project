Title: LEMON CRACKERS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Shortening
2 tb Bakers ammonia
1 ts Oil of lemon
2 ea Eggs
2 tb Milk; new
1 pt Milk; new
Flour
Soak ammonia over night in pint of milk. Beat eggs seperately and ad
2 T. milk to yolks. Mix dough stiff and roll thin. Prick well with
fork and bake.

Note: No temperature given. 425 F. ?

Source: Mrs. Anna Balthaser, Spencerville Grange, Allen County, OH
