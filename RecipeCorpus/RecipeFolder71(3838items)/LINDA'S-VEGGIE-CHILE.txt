Title: LINDA'S VEGGIE CHILE
Categories: Main meal, Vegetables, Vegetarian, Low fat
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Stewed Tomatoes
1 3/4 c  Tomato Sauce
1 3/4 c  Kidney Beans -- Canned
1 lg Carrot -- diced
1 lg Onion -- diced
1/2 c  Green Pepper -- diced
1 c  Turnip -- diced
1 c  Celery -- diced
1 tb Chili Powder -- to taste
1/2 ts Cayenne Pepper -- to taste
Mix all in pot.  Bring to boil, reduce heat  and let simmer until
vegetables a softened.

NOTES : Per Serving - Calories 368.9, Total Fat 1.6g, 3.5%CFF
