Title: LIME-CILANTRO DRESSING
Categories: Salads
1/2 c  olive oil
juice of 1 lime
Yield: 1 Servings
:
4    garlic cloves
peeled, ends removed
:
1/2 c  cilantro -- wash, dry
fresh ground black pepper
:
1/4 ts sugar -- to taste
1/8 ts salt -- to taste

In a food processor fitted with a metal blade, combine the olive oil,
lime juice, garlic, cilantro leaves, and black pepper. Process until
smooth. Add sugar and salt to taste. Makes 1/2 cup. Chef Pete
