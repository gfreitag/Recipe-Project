Title: KEBAB DIGI (PAN KEBABS)
Categories: Ethnic, Ground beef, On the gril
1 lb ground beef
Yield: 4 Servings
1 md onion, grated -- (1/2 cup)
1/2 ts salt
1/4 ts pepper
1/2 ts ground cumin
1/4 ts baking soda

Mix everything together and let sit for several hours,
overnight, or for just a few minutes. Oil a shallow
metal baking dish, put the kebab mixture into the pan
and flatten out to about 3/4 inch thick.  Then
separate the meat with a spatula into strips 1 inch
wide and 5 inches long.  Leave 1/2 inch space between
the strips.  (to simulate meat on skewers)  Broil for
about 6 minutes, turning over once.Serve warm with
rice.
