Title: LEMON & ROSEMARY T-BONE STEAKS
Categories: Beef, Barbacue
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/3 c  Fresh rosemary -- chopped
6    T-bone steaks -- (12-14 oz.
Each)
Combine oil, lemon juice, roesmary and generouse amount of pepper in
large glass baking dish. Add steaks and turn to coat. cover and chill
4 to 6 hours. Prepare barbecue (medium-high heat). Remove steaks from
marinade. Sprinkle with salt. Grill to desired doneness, about 4
minutes per side for rare. Transfer to plates and serve.

: Bon Appetit - August 1995
