Title: LISA'S KRAUT RYE BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 10 Servings
3 c  White flour
1 c  Plus 2 tb. Rye Flour
1 tb Yeast
4 tb Gluten Flour
4 tb Sugar
2 1/2 tb Dry milk
2 tb Veg. oil
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Sauerkraut;put in strainer
-pushing with back of spoon
-to drain; chop fine;loosely
-packed
1 1/4 c  Water; warmed
Put all in Bread machine according to manufacturers' instructions.
Select White Bread Cycle.   1 tb of caraway may be added at the beep!
Note: George loved this. Delicious served with Roast Pork or Ruebens!
