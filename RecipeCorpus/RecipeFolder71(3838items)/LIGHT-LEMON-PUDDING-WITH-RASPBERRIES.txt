Title: LIGHT LEMON PUDDING WITH RASPBERRIES
Categories: New, Text, Import
1 c  skim milk
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  lowfat lemon yogurt
2 TB honey
1 ts vanilla
3 1/2 oz package instant lemon
pudding & pie filling
Fresh raspberries
:
:

Mix milk and yogurt in bowl with electric mixer. Add pie filling,
honey and vanilla. Beat on low until thickened. Chill, covered, 1
hour to thicken. Serve with fresh raspberries.
