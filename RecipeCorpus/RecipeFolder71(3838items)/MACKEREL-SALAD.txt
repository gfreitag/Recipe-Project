Title: MACKEREL SALAD
Categories: Fish, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
12 oz Ready-to-eat smoked-Mackerel
-fillets, fresh or frozen,
-skinned and cubed.
10 oz Small New Potatoes, Scrubbed
-quartered, cooked and
-cooled
2    Celery Stalks, trimmed and
-thinly sliced
1/4 pt Reduced-Calorie Thousand
-Island Dressing
Chopped Fresh Chives to
-Garnish
In a large bowl, combine smoked-mackerel fillets, cookeed potatoes,
celery and dressing. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with
chopped chives.

Source: CHAT Magazine
