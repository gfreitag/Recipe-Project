Title: MAKARONIA
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 lb Sweet butter
1/4 lb Mizithra (or Feta); grated
1 1/2 lb Spaghetti; cooked al dente
16    Jumbo Calamata olives *
*Note: Olive meat should be shaved from the pits. In a large saucepan
place the butter and heat it on medium until it has melted. Add the
cheese and stir it in. Add the pasta and toss it in so that it is
well coated with the butter and cheese.  Add the shaved olives and
toss them in well.
