Title: LIVER DUMPLINGS (MAJGOMBOC)
Categories: Soups/stews, Ethnic
Yield: 6 Servings
1 oz Of any kind of liver
1/4    Of an egg, or tsp. of dried
-egg
A small nut of fat
1 ts Each of breadcrumbs and
-flour
a pinch each of salt, pepper and finely chopped parsley

Scrape the liver with the blunt side of the knife and mix well with
the fat and other ingredients.  Drop a teaspoonful at a time into the
boiling soup and keep boiling for three to seven minutes, or till
they rise to the surface of the soup.

From: Hungarian Cooking Shared By: Pat Stockett
