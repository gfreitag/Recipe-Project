Title: LEWIS' CONEY ISLAND HOT DOGS
Categories: American, Ground beef, Hot dogs, Sandwiches, Sauces
Yield: 12 Servings
1/4 lb Ground beef -- lean
6 oz Tomato paste
1 1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Pickle relish
1 tb Instant minced onion
1 tb Mustard
3 ts Chili powder
1 ts Sugar
12    Hot dogs -- heated
12    Hot dog buns -- toasted
In a medium saucepan.  Cook meat, crumbling with a fork, until it
looses its red color. Add remaining ingredients, except franks and
buns, and simmer for about 30 minutes.

: Charlotte Lewis Baltimore Sun 5/95
