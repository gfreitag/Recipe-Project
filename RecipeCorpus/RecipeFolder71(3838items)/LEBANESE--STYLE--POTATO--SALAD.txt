Title: LEBANESE  STYLE  POTATO  SALAD
Categories: L, E, B, A, N
Yield: 1 Servings
6 md Potatoes, boiled in jackets
Until barely fork tender,
Peeled and cubed
1/2 bn Parsley
1/2 bn Scallions
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/3 c  Oil
1/4 c  Simaq
1/2    Clove garlic
1 1/2 ts Salt
Preparation : Finely chop scallions and parsley. Add to cubed
potatoes in large bowl.  Mash garlic and salt. Add to lemon and oil.
Pour over potatoes and mix well. Add simaq if desired and adjust
seasoning.
