Title: LYNDON JOHNSON'S CHILI CON CARNE
Categories: Chili, Beans, Celebrity
Yield: 8 To 10
-Mary Wilson  BWVB02B
2 lb Lean ground chuck or round
- steak
1 lg Onion; diced
2    Garlic cloves; crushed
Tabasco sauce
1 ts Ground oregano
1 ts Cumin seeds
2 tb Chili powder
2 cn Whole tomatoes, 14 1/2 oz ea
2 cn Kidney beans, (14 1/2 oz ea)
2 c  Water
In a large heavy skillet, sear meat with onion and garlic until
lightly browned.  Add 5 or more shakes of Tabasco (to taste) and rest
of ingredients.  Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Cover. Simmer about 1
1/2 hours. Stir occasionally. Skim off fat as it cooks out.

TIPS:  Chili taste best after refrigerating overnight then
reheating. Serves 8 to 10.
