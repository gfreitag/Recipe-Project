Title: KUGELA
Categories: Poland, Potatoes, Ham/pork
Yield: 8 Servings
5 lb Potatoes; grated
1/2 lb Bacon; 1" pieces
4    Egg
2 lg Onion; grated
2 tb Salt
4 oz Milk, whole
Saute bacon with grated onions lightly. Combine
potatoes with bacon,onions,milk eggs, and salt.. Place
in oiled glass pan (13 x 9 x 2)
Baked at 400 degree for 1/2 hour then lower it to
350 degree until done, till knife inseted in center
comes out clean.
