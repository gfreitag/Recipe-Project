Title: KEY LIME PIE *** (VMFX91A)
Categories: Pies, Desserts, American
Yield: 8 Servings
4 ea Eggs; separated
14 oz Can sweetened condensed milk
3 oz KEY WEST lime juice
1 ea Graham cracker crust
combine yolks and cond.milk mix well add lime juice a
little at a time stir until filling is smooth and
creamy------ pour into crust and bake at 350 for 10
mins. Chill two hours serve with whipped cream. debi
FROM: DEBI MATTHEW   (VMFX91A)
