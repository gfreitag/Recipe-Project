Title: LUCIOUS LIME FROST
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 5 Cups
1 1/2 c  Lime sherbet
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Frozen limeade concentrate,
-thawed
Scoops of lime sherbet
Mint leaves
Spoon sherbet into blender container.  Add milk and limeade
concentrate. Cover and blend at high speed until smooth. Top each
serving with a scoop of sherbet; garnish with mint leaves. Makes
about 5 cups Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Ontario
Milk Marketing Board Cookbook,
