Title: KOHL'S PEANUT BRITTLE
Categories: Improv, Kohl, Desserts
Yield: 1 Serving
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Karo (red label) syrup
1/2 c  Hot water
1    Lump of butter (about 1 tb)
1/2 lb Or more raw peanuts
- rolled with a rolling pin
1 ts Baking soda
Boil sugar, syrup, water, and butter until golden
brown stirring often. Add baking soda to the raw
peanuts. Grease a cookie sheet. Pour peanuts into the
syrup. Remove from the fire AT ONCE. Pour on on the
greased cookie sheet, smoothing over into a thin layer.

When cooled, crack up into serving pieces.
Taken From:  IT NEVER TURNS OUT THE SAME WAY COOKBOOK
A Collection of Recipes from the Kitchen of Joyce &
Clem Kohl
