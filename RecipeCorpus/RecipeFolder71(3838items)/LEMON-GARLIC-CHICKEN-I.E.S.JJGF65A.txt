Title: LEMON GARLIC CHICKEN I.E.S.JJGF65A
Categories: Chicken, Main dish, Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PHILLY.INQUIRER---------------------------
3 lb BROILER-FRYER CUT UP
2 ts GARLIC POWDER
2 tb BUTTER
1 c  PAPRIKA
3 tb FRESH SQUEEZED LEMON
LEMON WEDGES,PARSLEY,GARNIS
SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE
RUB CHICKEN PARTS WITH BUTTER,AND PLACE IN ROASTING PAN.SPRINKLE
WITH LEMON JUICE,SALT PEPPER AND GARLIC.TOP WITH A SPRINKLING OF
PAPRIKA.PLACE PAN IN PREHEATED 350 DEGREE OVEN,AND BAKE FOR ABOUT 45
MINUTES,TURNING OVE TO 400 DEGREES FOR THE LAST 8 MINUTES.GARNISH
WITH LEMON WEDGES AND PARSLE AND SERVE.MAKES 3 OR 4 SERVINGS.
