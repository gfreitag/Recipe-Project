Title: LIME-LITE CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Jaw, Low-cal, Low-fat
Yield: 4 Servings
3 tb Lite soy sauce
1/4 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Grated fresh lime peel
2 ts Lime juice
4    Boneless, skinless chicken
Breast halves
Combine soy sauce, sugar, lime peel and juice; pour over chicken in
large plastic food storage bag 30 minutes, turning bag over
occasionally. Broil chicken 7 minutes on each side,l or until no
longer pink in center.
