Title: KUTIA WIGILIJNA - CHRISTMAS EVE KUTIA
Categories:
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Cracked wheat or bulgur
2 c  Water, hot
1/2 c  Poppy seeds
1 c  Honey
2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
5 ml Festive spirits
1. Soak wheat in 2 cups of hot water for 30 minutes.
Bring to a boil and cook covered until tender.
2. Mix in poppy seed.
3. Cook honey with remaining 2 cups of water for 20
minutes. Cool and serve with wheat and poppy seed
mixture. 4. For added enjoyment a liberal amount of
festive spirits may be added. In Poland the festive
spirit is usually called "Spiritus" which is grain
alcohol.
