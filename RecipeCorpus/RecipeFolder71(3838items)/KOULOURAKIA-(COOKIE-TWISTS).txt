Title: KOULOURAKIA (COOKIE TWISTS)
Categories: Desserts, Cookies, Greek, Holiday
Yield: 10 Dozen
1 c  Butter; softened
2 1/4 c  Sugar
10    Eggs
4 ts Vanilla extract
4 ts Baking powder
9 c  All-purpose flour (approx.)
1    Egg; slighlty beaten
Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.  Add 10
eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition;
add vanilla.

Combine baking powder and flour; add enough flour to
creamed mixture to make a pliable dough.  shape into
twists.  Brush tops of cookies with beaten egg.  Bake
on ungreased cookie sheets at 375 degrees for 20 to 25
minutes.

SOURCE: Souther Living Magazine, sometime in the early
1970s. Typed for you by Nancy Coleman.
