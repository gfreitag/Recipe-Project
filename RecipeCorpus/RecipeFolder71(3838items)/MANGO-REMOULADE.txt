Title: MANGO REMOULADE
Categories: Fish, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Very ripe mango, peeled and
-pureed
2    Limes, seeded
1 1/4 c  Olive oil
2    Egg yolks, room
-temperature
1/2 bn Cilantro
Blend all ingredients in a food processor until smooth. From the
files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.
Feb 1994
