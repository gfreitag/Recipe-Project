Title: LEMON ICE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pt Lemon juice
1 qt Sugar
1 qt Water
2    Egg whites
To lemon juice, add sugar and water, in which the thin rind of three
lemons has been allowed to stand until highly flavored; when partly
frozen add egg whites, beaten to a stiff froth.
