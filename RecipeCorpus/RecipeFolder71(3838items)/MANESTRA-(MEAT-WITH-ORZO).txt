Title: MANESTRA (MEAT WITH ORZO)
Categories: Greek, Pasta, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
Karen Mintzias
1 tb Oil
1 lb Beef or lamb stew meat
2    Onions; chopped
1 c  Tomato sauce;  -=OR=-
1 tb -Tomato paste instead
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Spearmint flakes
4 c  Boiling water
1 c  Manestra (orzo)
1 c  Grated cheese (Mizithra!)
Heat oil in large saucepan and brown meat and onions for 10 minutes
over medium heat.  Add tomato, seasonings, and 2 cups water. Cover
and simmer for 1
1/2    hours until meat is tender.

Add remaining water and bring to a boil.  Add manestra. Stir once or
twice. Simmer 20 minutes more. Serve hot with grated cheese.

From "The Complete Greek Cookbook" by Theresa Karas Yianilios, Avenel
Books, New York.
