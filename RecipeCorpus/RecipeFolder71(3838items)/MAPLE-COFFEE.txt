Title: MAPLE COFFEE
Categories: Beverage, Posted-mm
Yield: 2 Cups
1 c  Half-and-half
1/4 c  Maple syrup
1 c  Hot brewed coffee
Sweetened whipped cream
Source: Southern Living Magazine 1/97

Cook half-and-half and maple syrup in a saucepan over medium heat,
stiffing constantly, until thoroughly heated (do not boil). Stir in
coffee, and serve with sweetened whipped cream.
