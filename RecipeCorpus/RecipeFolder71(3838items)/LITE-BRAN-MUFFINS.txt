Title: LITE BRAN MUFFINS
Categories: Digest, July, Muffins
Yield: 12 Servings
1 1/3 c  Quaker Oats (quick cooking)
1 1/2 c  Fiber One or All Bran
Cereal
1 1/4 c  Skim milk
MMMMM----------------------------ADD---------------------------------
1 tb Baking powder
1 x  Pinch of salt
1 tb Sweetener
2    Egg whites or egg replacer
To equal one egg
1 c  Raisins
Revised from a recipe on the Fiber One box. Mix together and add to
cereal mixture.  Spoon into veg sprayed (or teflon coated) cupcake
tins. Bake 25 mins. at 400 degrees. Makes 12.

My best estimate: 140 calories each These aren't light, fluffy
muffins, but they're satisfying and make a great snack!
