Title: MAINE TANGY LOBSTER
Categories: Seafood, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Lobster meat
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Dry mustard
1/3 c  Butter
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Salt;pepper to taste
Place all ingredients,except lobster,in top part of double boiler.
Melt over hot water;stir to blend well.Cut lobster in small pieces;
add to mixture and cook 6 minutes.Serve on toast with lemon wedges.
Serves 4.
