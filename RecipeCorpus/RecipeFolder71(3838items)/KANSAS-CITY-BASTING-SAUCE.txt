Title: KANSAS CITY BASTING SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Cider vinegar
1 c  Cranberry juice concentrate
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Catsup
1/2    Lemon, quartered
4    Bay leaves
4    Whole cloves
2 md Cloves of garlic, chopped
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 c  Corn syrup
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts White pepper
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Rosemary
1 ts MSG
1/2    Onion, chopped, cooked until
-clear in color
Cook onion and garlic until clear. Combine all
ingredients mixing well.
