Title: LEMON MERINGUE RICE
Categories: Desserts, Rice
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Milk
1/2 c  Rice
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
1/2    Lemon rind -- grated
3/4 ts Salt -- to taste
2 lg Eggs
2 tb Powdered sugar
1/2 ts Lemon juice
1. Put in top of double boiler the 3 cups milk and 1/2 cup raw rice.
Cook over hot water until rice is soft, about 30-35 minutes
