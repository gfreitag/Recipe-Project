Title: KENTUCKY REBEL PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
1 ea Stick melted butter
1 c  Sugar
1 c  White corn syrup
4 ea Eggs
2 tb Bourbon
1 ea Unbaked pie shell
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1 c  Pecans or walnuts
Combine first 5 ingredients.Pour into pie
shell.Sprinkle chocolate chips on top;pat down with
spatula.Top with pecans or walnuts.Bake 45 minutes @
350    degrees.
