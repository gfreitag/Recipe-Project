Title: KFC BUTTERMILK BISCUITS
Categories: Breads, Copycat
Yield: 18 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
2 1/2 tb Granulated sugar
1    Egg, beaten
3/4 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  Club soda
1 ts Salt
5 c  Bisquick biscuit mix
1) Preheat oven to 450 F. 2) Combine all ingreds.
Knead by hand until smooth. 3) Flour your hands. Pat
the dough flat to 3/4" thickness on wax paper and
punch out biscuits with floured biscuit cutter. 4)
Bake on greased baking sheet until brown.

Yield: 18 biscuits
