Title: LENTIL - POTATO BURGERS
Categories: Vegetables, August
Yield: 5 Servings
1/2 c  Yellow onion; chopped
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1/4 c  Water
2 c  Lentils, cooked; drained & m
2 c  Potato; mashed
1 c  Whole-wheat bread crumbs; in
1 ts Dried parsley
1 1/2 ts Dried sage
1 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Dried marjoram
1/4 ts Dried rosemary
1/8 ts Black pepper; ground
Preparation Time: 0:50 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Saute the
onion and celery in the water about 10 minutes. Combine in a bowl
with the remaining ingredients and mix well. Form into patties and
place on a nonstick baking sheet. Bake for 20 minutes, then turn and
bake for an additional 20 minutes Serve hot on toasted whole wheat
burger buns.
