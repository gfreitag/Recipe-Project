Title: LOMBO DE PORCO DE MONCAO (SPICY LOIN OF PORK)
Categories: Pork, Portuguese
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Loin of pork
1/2 c  Wine, white, dry
4    Cloves
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1    Bay leaf
Salt
Pepper, black
16    Chestnuts, roasted; (opt)
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Put the meat in a roasting dish with the
wine, cloves, nutmeg. bay leaf, salt and pepper.  Place the dish in
the preheated oven and cook for 1 1/2 hours, basting the meat fre-
quently. Deglaze the cooking juices, season and serve with the sliced
meat which can be garnished with chestnuts.
