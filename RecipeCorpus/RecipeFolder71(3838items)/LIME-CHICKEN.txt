Title: LIME CHICKEN
Categories: Chicken, Indonesian, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
6 ea Chicken thigh
2 tb Oil
1 ea Onion, med; chopped
2 ea Garlic clove; crushed
1/4 ts Thai chile, dried; crushed
1 ts Cumin, ground
1 ts Coriander, ground
1/2 ts Turmeric
2 tb Soy sauce
1 x  Lime wedges
per serving: 357 Fat grams per serving: 6 Approx. Cook
Calories
Time: 0:30 Brown chicken in oil.  Add the remaining ingredients in
the order listed.  Reduce the heat and simmer, covered, 15 to 20
minutes or until the chicken is tender. Serve with lime wedges.
