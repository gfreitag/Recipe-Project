Title: LENTILS & RICE PILAF
Categories: Grain/pasta, Beans
Yield: 2 Servings
-----MIAMI HERALD-----
2 c  Low-salt vegetable stock
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Long grain white rice
1/2 c  Lentils
1 md Onion -- sliced
2 md Tomatoes -- diced
1 ts Dried thyme
Salt and pepper to taste
Combine the stock and water.  Brings 2 cups liquid to a boil. Rinse
lentils and slowly ass to boiling stock so that liquid continues to
boil. Cover and cook 5 minutes. Rinse riceand add to lentils with
onion, tomatoes, thyme amd remaining 1 cup liquid. Cover and simmer
20 minutes. Salt and pepper to taste.
