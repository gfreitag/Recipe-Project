Title: MANCATTAN CHOPPED CHICKEN LIVER
Categories: Petfood
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Chicken livers
1    Hard-boiled egg
1/4 c  Chopped apple
Remove connective tissues from chicken livers and rinse. Place livers
in a single layer on a microwave-safe dish, and cover tightly with a
microwave-safe plastic wrap.  Microwave on high for 2 to 3 minutes.
Uncover and stir; replace plastic wrap and microwave on high for 3
minutes. Put livers in food processor, add egg and apple, and chop
coarsely. Serve. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Cat
Treats.
