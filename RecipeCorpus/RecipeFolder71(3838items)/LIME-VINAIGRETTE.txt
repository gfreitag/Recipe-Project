Title: LIME VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Recipe
1/3 c  Fresh lime juice
-- (from 2 limes)
1/3 c  EDEN Olive Oil
1/3 c  EDEN Brown Rice Vinegar
3 tb EDEN Ume Plum Vinegar
2    Garlic cloves
-- peeled and pressed
1/4 ts Lima Sea Salt
3 tb Warm water
1 tb Fresh basil, minced
1 tb Fresh dill weed, minced
Place all ingredients in a jar, cover and shake well. Chill and store
in refrigerator.

Prep. Time: 15 minutes Yield: 1-1/4 cups
