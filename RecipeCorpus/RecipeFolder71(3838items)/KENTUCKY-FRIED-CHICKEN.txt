Title: KENTUCKY FRIED CHICKEN
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Salt
2 c  Flour
2 tb Pepper
4 tb Paprika
1 ts Garlic salt
1 tb Ground mustard
1 tb Ground french thyme
1 tb Sweet basic leaves
1 ts Ground oregano
1 tb Ground Jamaica ginger
Mix all spices and flour together.  Take pieces of
cut-up chicken and dip in raw egg (slightly beaten).
Roll in spices. Fry chicken in oil. Bake in 350 F oven
for 45 minutes in foil.

Good luck.
