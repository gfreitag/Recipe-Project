Title: LUSCIOUS STRAWBERRY FROSTING
Categories: Frostings
Yield: 1 Recipe
-JUDI M. PHELPS
2 c  Heavy cream
1/4 c  Confectioners' sugar
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Strawberries; cut up
Whip together heavy cream, confectioners sugar, and salt. Fold in
vanilla and cut up strawberries.  Frost cooled cake and refrigerate
until serving time. Source: Cookbook USA
