Title: LIGHT RANCH DRESSING
Categories: Digest, August, Dressings, Lacto
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Finely chopped parsley OR
1 ts Dried flakes
2    Cloves garlic, minced
3/4 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Dried basil, crushed
1/4 ts Celery seed
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts White wine vinegar
1/8 ts Salt
Combine ingredients and refrigerate.
