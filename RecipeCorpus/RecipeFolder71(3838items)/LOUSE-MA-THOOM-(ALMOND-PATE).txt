Title: LOUSE MA THOOM (ALMOND PATE)
Categories: Appetizers, Lebanese, Nuts, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Almonds, blanched & toasted
3 ea Garlic cloves
1 md Onion, coarsely chopped
2 tb Olive oil
12 oz Mushrooms, halved
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Thyme
Chop the toasted alomnds in a food processor until they resemble very
fine breadcrumbs.

In a large skillet, saute the onion & garlic in the olive oil until
the onions are tender.  Add all the remaining ingredients. Saute for
approximately 5 minutes. The mushrooms need to be limp.

Transfer this mixture to the food processor & process with the
almonds. Chop well until you have a thick paste.

Place the pate in a shallow dish & surround with crackers, melba
toast, pita chips or French bread slices.

Mary Salloum, "A Taste of the Mediterranean: Vegetarian Style" From:
