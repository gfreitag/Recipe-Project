Title: KOHL'S CARROT CAKE #1
Categories: Improv, Kohl, Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 1 Cake
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Wesson oil
2 c  Grated carrots
1 sm Can crushed pineapple,
-drained
Sift together: Flour, baking powder, baking soda,
cinnamon & salt. Mix together: Oil, sugar and eggs.
Add dry ingredients gradually. Add carrots, pineapple
and nuts. Pour into a greased oblong cake pan. Bake in
a 350 degree preheated oven 30-35 minutes.

Frost cooled cake with cream cheese frosting.

Taken From: IT NEVER TURNS OUT THE SAME WAY COOKBOOK
A Collection of Recipes from the Kitchen of Joyce &
Clem Kohl
