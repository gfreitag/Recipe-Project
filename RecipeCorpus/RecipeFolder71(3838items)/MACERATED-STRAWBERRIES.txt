Title: MACERATED STRAWBERRIES
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 2 Cups fruit
2 pt Strawberries
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Grand Marnier or other
-orange liqueur
Hull Strawberries, cut in half, and place in small bowl. smash fruit
until soft and juicy, stirring occasionally with fork, about 4 hr.
Spoon over pound cake or angel food.
