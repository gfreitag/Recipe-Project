Title: MAPLE NUT SUNDAE
Categories: Desserts, Text
Yield: 6 Servings
Text Only
Pour thick maple sirup or maple-flavored sirup over ice-cream. If
desired, sprinkle with chopped nuts.  If the sirup is too thin it may
be combined with a small quantity of corn-starch. Cook over hot water
until of desired consistency. Elizabeth Shaffer, Albuquerque. N.M.
