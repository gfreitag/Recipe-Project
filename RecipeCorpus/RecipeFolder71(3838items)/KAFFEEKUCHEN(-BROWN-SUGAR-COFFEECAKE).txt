Title: KAFFEEKUCHEN( BROWN SUGAR COFFEECAKE)
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Butter
4 c  Flour
4 ts Baking powder
2    Eggs
2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
Topping:
4 tb Butter
4 tb Sugar
8 tb Flour
2 ts Cinnamon
Mix together all the cake ingredients ( the first 7
ingredients ). and place
in a greased long oblong pan or two smaller ones.Brush
with either melted butter or milk before addingthe
crumb topping. For topping mix all ingredients until
crumblike and sprinkle over batter. Bake in a 350 oven
about 30 minutes or tested done. A very old German
family recipe.
