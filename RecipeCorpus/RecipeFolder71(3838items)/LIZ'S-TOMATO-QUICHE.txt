Title: LIZ'S TOMATO QUICHE
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 lb Gruyere cheese; shredded
1    9" unbaked pastry shell
3    Tomatoes; peeled, chopped
- and drained
3 tb Instant minced onion
3 tb Hot water
Salt, pepper
1 ts Dried basil; crushed
2    Eggs
3/4 c  Milk
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
Scatter Gruyere cheese over pastry shell. Spread layer of tomatoes
over cheese. Add onion, which has been soaked in hot water until
softened. Season to taste with salt and pepper and add basil. Beat
eggs and milk together and pour over tomatoes. Sprinkle with Parmesan
cheese. Bake at 350F 45 minutes, or until done.
