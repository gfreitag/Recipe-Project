Title: MAMA'S EASY SOURDOUGH BISCUITS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
4 1/2 c  Self-rising flour
2 c  Buttermilk
2/3 c  Cooking oil
2 pk Yeast
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Soda
Mix all ingredients.  Keep tightly covered in refrigerator. Take
out as needed and knead with a little extra flour and bake. Courtesy
