Title: LUBY'S SHREDDED CARROTS
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Whole (or 1.6 lbs peeled,
-shredded) carrots
1 c  Crushed pineapple (drained)
1 c  Mayonaise
1 c  Plumped raisins (see NOTE)
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
NOTE* Soak raisins in water or pineapple juice to plump, then drain.

Combine carrots, pineapple, mayonaise, raisins and sugar. Chill
until ready to serve.
