Title: KNACH KUKEN
Categories: Breads, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
1 c  Hickory nuts
Sponge; enough for 1 loaf
Butter; piece size of a wal
-nut
Enough sponge for a loaf of bread. Work into this
a piece of butter size of a walnut. Mix well. Place in
greased bread loaf pan, let rise until double in bulk
and bake.

Note: Assume moderate oven 350 - 400 F. Time ?
probably 40 - 50 minutes.

Source: Mrs. H. S. Lewis, Benton Grange, Ottawa
County, OH
