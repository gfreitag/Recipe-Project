Title: KANSAS SPICY EGGS
Categories: Appetizers, Spice mix
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Apple cider
1 c  White vinegar
2 ts Salt
1 ts Mixed pickling spice
1    Cl Garlic; peeled
1/2    Sliced
1/2 ts Mustard seed
Recipe by: christi@meaddata.com (Christi Wilson) Heat
mixture to near boiling and simmer for five minutes.
Pour solution over hard-cooked peeled eggs in a quart
jar or other suitable container that can be closed or
sealed tightly. Seal and immediately store in
refrigerator to season.
