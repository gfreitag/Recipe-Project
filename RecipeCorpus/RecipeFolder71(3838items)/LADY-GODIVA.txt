Title: LADY GODIVA
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
2 oz Creme de cocoa
3 oz Kahlua
6    Scoops vanilla ice cream
1 c  Strawberries
1 c  Half and half or cream
Powdered sugar (optional)
Preparation : Mix in blender until smooth. Serve in brandy glasses.
Top with whipped cream and a whole strawberry.
