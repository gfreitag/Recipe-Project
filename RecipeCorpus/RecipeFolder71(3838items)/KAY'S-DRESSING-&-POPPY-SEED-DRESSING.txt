Title: KAY'S DRESSING & POPPY SEED DRESSING
Categories: Diabetic, Dressings
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------KAY'S DRESSING----------------------------
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  White vinegar;
1/2 ts Salt;
1/2 ts Dry mustard;
1/8 ts Ground pepper; fresh
1/16 ts Paprika
Sugar substitute equivalent
To 4 teaspoons sugar
MMMMM--------------------POPPY SEED DRESSING-------------------------
1/2 c  Cider vinegar;
1 tb Poppy seed;
Omit paprika
Combine all ingredients in a pint jar and cover
tightly. Shake vigorously and store in refrigerator.
Shake before using. Food Exchange per serving: THIS IS
A "FREE" FOOD   CAL: 4 CHO: 1g; PRO: 0g; FAT: 0g;
Low-sodium diets:  Omit salt

Variation Poppy Seed Dressing.  Follow recipe for Kay's
dressing(above), but use cider vinegar in place of
white vinegar, omit paprika, poppy seeds. Delicious
over fruit salad. Food Exchange per serving: THIS IS A
"FREE" FOOD    CAL: 9 CHO: 2g; PRO: 0g; FAT: 0g;
Low-sodium diets: Omit salt.

Source: The Art of Cooking for the Diabetic by Mary
