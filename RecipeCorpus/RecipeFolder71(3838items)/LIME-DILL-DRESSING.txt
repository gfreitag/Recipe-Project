Title: LIME DILL DRESSING
Categories: Fruits, Seasonings, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Fresh lime juice
1 tb Vinegar
1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Dry mustard
2 ts Dill seed, coarsely ground
1 tb Mayonnaise
1/3 c  Olive oil
Salt & freshly ground pepper
Blend together all ingredients except olive oil; add oil in a steady
stream, whisking or blending until thoroughly combined. Add salt and
pepper to taste.

Yield: 1/2 cup.

From 1994 Shepherd's Garden Seeds Catalog, pg. 23. Posted by Cathy
Harned.
