Title: MAPLE WALNUT WHEAT BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
1/4 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Butter/margarine
1 1/2 tb 100% pure maple syrup
1/4 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Plus 1T water
1/3 c  Chopped walnuts
Automatic Bread Program:

Press Select for Normal. Press START. When machine BEEPS 10 times, 5
minutes before the end of the second kneading, add 1/3 c chopped
walnuts. Bread will be ready in 3 hrs. After baking, press
STOP/RESET. Remove bread from machine. Cool.
