Title: MAMA'S CABBAGE
Categories: Vegetables
1/2 c  water -- * see note
Yield: 1 Servings
1 sm cabbage -- cut up *see note
4 TB corn oil
salt -- to taste
pepper -- to taste
:
:

Bring water to boil, using 1/2 cup water if cabbage is small or up to
one cup if cabbage is larger. Add the cabbage and oil, cover and cook
for about 15 minutes. The cabbage should be soft. Season with salt
and pepper and serve hot with cornbread.

NOTES : May need up to 1 cup of water. May use a medium cabbage.

Yield: 2 servings
