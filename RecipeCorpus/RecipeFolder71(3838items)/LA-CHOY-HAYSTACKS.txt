Title: LA CHOY HAYSTACKS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 48 Servings
6 oz Chow mein noodles
12 oz Chocolate chips, melted
1/3 c  Peanuts
Mix all ingredients together.  Drop by spoonsful onto lightly greased
baking sheet and chill.
