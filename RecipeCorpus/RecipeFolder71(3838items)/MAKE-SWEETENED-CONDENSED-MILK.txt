Title: MAKE SWEETENED CONDENSED MILK
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Water
3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/3 c  Instant skim milk
4 tb Butter
Heat water and sugar in medium saucepan until sugar dissolves,
approximately 5 minutes.  Add butter. heat mixture until butter melts.
Remove from heat.  Place instant milk in food processer or blender.
Process until well
Pour water\sugar mixture in blender\processor.
blended. Makes 1 cup sweetened condensed milk for fudge recipe. No
leftovers like when you use the canned sweetened condensed milk.
