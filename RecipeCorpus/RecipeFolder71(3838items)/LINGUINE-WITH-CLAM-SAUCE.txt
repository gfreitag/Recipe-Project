Title: LINGUINE WITH CLAM SAUCE
Categories: Pasta, Italian, Sauces, Seafood
Yield: 2 Servings
3/4 c  Heavy Cream
3/4 ts Onion Salt
1/2 ts Thyme Leaves
13 oz Chopped Clams *
8 oz Cooked, Drained Linguine
* Include the juice from one can of chopped clams.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream, onion salt,
thyme leaves and chopped clams. Cook 8 ounces of linguine as package
directs, drain. Toss with sauce and serve.
