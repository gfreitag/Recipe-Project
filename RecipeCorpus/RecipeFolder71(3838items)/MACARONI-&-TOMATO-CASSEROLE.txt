Title: MACARONI & TOMATO CASSEROLE
Categories: Crockpot, Pasta, Meats
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Ground beef
1 c  Onion; chopped
1 1/2 c  Water
1 cl Garlic; minced
1/4 c  Green pepper; chopped
2 cn Tomatoes; chopped, 1lb ea
1 c  Tomato juice
Salt and pepper
1/2 ts Oregano
2 c  Macaroni; uncooked
Brown the meat, onion, green pepper, garlic and drain. Combine
tomatoes, juice and the drained meat mixture. Stir in the seasonings.
Mix well and place in crockpot and cook low for 6 to 8 hours. Stir in
uncooked macaroni and cook 2 to 3 hours more.
