Title: LEEK & POTATO SOUP
Categories: Sauces and, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Leeks (sliced white parts on
1/2 oz Butter
6 1/8 oz Potatoes (1 large or 2 mediu
Recipe by: Norma D. Hosken
stock
3/4    pints
salt & pepper

1.  Fry leeks until golden.

2.  Put potatoes, leeks, stock and seasoning in a pot, and simmer for
1/2 hour.

3.  When cooked sieve or mash, and then season to taste using plenty
of pepper.

Difficulty    : easy. Precision
: measure ingredients.
