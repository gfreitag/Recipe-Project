Title: LEMON-STRAWBERRY PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 20 Servings
1 cn (16-oz.) Frozen orange juice
-concentrate; thawed
1 pk Frozen sliced strawberries;
-half thawed with juice
1 cn (6-oz.) Frozen lemonade
-concentrate
1 qt Carbonated water
1 qt Ginger ale
Thinly sliced oranges and
-lemons, for garnish
Combine orange juice, strawberries, and frozen lemonade in a
punchbowl with ice.  Just before serving, add carbonated water and
ginger ale. Garnish with orange and lemon slices.  Makes 20 servings.
