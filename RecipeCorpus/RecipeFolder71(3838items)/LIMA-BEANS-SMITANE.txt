Title: LIMA BEANS SMITANE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
10 oz Lima beans, frozen
1    Pimento
1/2 c  Sour cream
3 tb Chives
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Cook limas as package directs. Dice pimento and chop chives. Drain
limas. Lightly toss all ingredients together. Source: Good
Housekeeping Illustrated
