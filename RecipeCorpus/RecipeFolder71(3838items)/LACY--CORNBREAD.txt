Title: LACY  CORNBREAD
Categories: New, Text, Import
1/4 c  plain cornmeal
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ts salt

Add water until mixture will pour out easily.  Cook on electric fry
pan at 350 degrees.  Stir and pour patties into fry pan one
tablespoon at a time. Turn frequently until done. Be sure to grease
fry pan.
