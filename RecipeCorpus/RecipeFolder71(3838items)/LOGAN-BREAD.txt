Title: LOGAN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Eggs
(any mixture of whole wheat
And rye)
3/4 c  Wheat germ
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Powdered milk
1 c  Oil
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Molasses
1/4 c  Sorghum syrup -- or maple
Syrup
(any combination of these
Four sweeteners
Totalling 1 cup works fine)
1/2 c  Shelled walnuts or pecans
1 c  Dried fruit (raisins, dates,
Apricots -- peaches, etc)
Beat all the ingredients together in a large bowl. Pat down into two
greased 5 x 9 loaf pans.  Bake at 275 for two hours, or until a tester
comes out clean. The bread will be very heavy and dense. Each loaf
weighs
24    ounces.
