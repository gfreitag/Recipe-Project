Title: KETCHUP MEAT LOAF
Categories: Beef, Sandwiches
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 kg Lean minced beef
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
1    Egg
1/3 c  Tomato ketchup
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1/2 ts Oregano
Combine ingredients, mix well, bake at 350 degrees F
for 1 hour. Makes great sadwiches!  Just slice when
the loaf is cold. Recipe by my future mother in-law,
Joan MacNab.
