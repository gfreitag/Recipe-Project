Title: KAHLUA FUDGE BROWNIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Flour -- sifted
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Butter
3    Squares unsweetened baking
Chocolate
3 lg Eggs
2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  + 1 Tb Kahlua
3/4 c  Walnuts -- chopped
Grease a 9" square pan.  Preheat oven to 350F.  Sift
flour, baking powder, and salt together.  Melt butter
and chocolate together over low heat.  Beat eggs and
sugar until light. Add flour mixture, chocolate
mixture and 1/4 cup
Kahlua.  Blend well.  Stir in nuts.  Bake 30 minutes
or until done.  Cool in
pan.  Brush with remaining Kahlua. From Becky Witter
