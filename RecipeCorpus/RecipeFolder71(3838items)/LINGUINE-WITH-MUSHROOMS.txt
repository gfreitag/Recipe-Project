Title: LINGUINE WITH MUSHROOMS
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Shiitake mushrooms, cleaned
& sliced
6 tb Olive oil
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
1/2 ts Hot red pepper flakes
Salt & pepper, to taste
2 tb Parsley, chopped
3/4 lb Linguine
Cook mushrooms in olive oil over low heat for 10 minutes. Add garlic,
red pepper flakes, salt & pepper. Cook, stirring for 5 minutes over
low heat. Cover and cook for 15 minutes longer. Add parsley and mix.
Meanwhile, bring water to boil in large kettle. Boil linguine until
aldente. Drain linguine, add sauce and mix. Sprinkle with extra
parsley.

Recipe from Anne Marie Chiappetta's Kitchen.
