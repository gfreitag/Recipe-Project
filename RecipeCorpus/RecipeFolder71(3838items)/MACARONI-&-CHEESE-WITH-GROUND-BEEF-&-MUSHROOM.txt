Title: MACARONI & CHEESE WITH GROUND BEEF & MUSHROOM
Categories: Meats, Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground Beef, Lean
3 pk Kraft Macaroni And Cheese
1/2 lb Mushrooms, Fresh
Salt & Pepper To Taste
Brown ground beef in a large frying pan. Add sliced mushrooms as soon
as the ground beef starts to brown. Prepare the Macaroni and Cheese
per the directions on the package. As soon as the Macaroni and cheese
is ready add the ground beef and mushrooms, mix and serve. From:
Syd's Cookbook.
