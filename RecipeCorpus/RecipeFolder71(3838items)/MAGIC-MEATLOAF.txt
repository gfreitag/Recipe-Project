Title: MAGIC MEATLOAF
Categories: Crockpot, Main dish, Hamburger
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground chuck
1    Egg; beaten
1/4 c  Milk
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 sl Bread; crumbed
1/2 sm Onion; chopped
2 tb Green pepper; chopped
2 tb Celery; chopped
6    Potatoes; cut-up
Ketchup
Mix egg, milk, salt & breadcrumbs. Let stand to soften. Thoroughly
combine with meat & chopped vegs. Shape into loaf & put in crockpot.
Top with ketchup. Place potatoes around the sides (if you peeled
them, coat with butter to keep from darkening). Cover & cook on high
for 1 hr., then on low for 8-9 hrs.
