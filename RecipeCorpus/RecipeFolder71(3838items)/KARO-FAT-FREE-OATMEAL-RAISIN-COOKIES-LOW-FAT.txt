Title: KARO FAT-FREE OATMEAL RAISIN COOKIES LOW-FAT
Categories: Cookies, Low-fat
Yield: 1 Servings
-No stick cooking spray
1 c  Flour  (I used half whole
-wheat and half reg. flour.
-Very good.
1 c  Quick oats
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2    Egg whites
1/3 c  Light or dark corn syrup
1 ts Vanilla 1/2 c. raisins
If you are not watching the calorie count you can add
nuts too.

Preheat oven to 375  degrees.  Spray cookie sheets
with cooking spray. (Do this even when they are teflon
coated.) In large bowl combine dry ingredients.  Stir
in egg whites, corn syrup and vanilla until well
mixed. Add raisins.  Batter will be stiff.  Drop by
rounded teaspoonfuls onto prepared cookie sheets, Bake
10 mins. or until firm. Do not overbake. Remove; cool
on wire rack.  Makes 2 1/2 dozen cookies.
