Title: MAI TAI
Categories: Mixed drink, Rum
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Rum
1 oz Dark rum
1 oz Creme de noyaux or orgeat
Syrup
1/2 oz Triple sec
1 1/2 oz Sour mix, bottled
1 ds Rose's Lime Juice
1 ds Pineapple juice
Cracked ice
Ice cubes
Fill a mixing glass with crushed ice. Add rum, creme de noyaux,
triple sec, and sour mix. Shake well and strain into a collins glass
filled with ice cubes. Top with a dash of Rose's lime juice and a
dash of pineapple juice. Garnish with an orange slice and a cherry.
