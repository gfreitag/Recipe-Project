Title: LONG ISLAND ICED TEA 3
Categories: Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 oz Vodka
1/2 oz Gin
1/2 oz Light rum
1/4 oz Tequila
1/2 oz Triple sec
1 tb Fresh lemon juice; or to tas
6 oz Cola; or to fill
Cracked ice
Ice cubes
Recipe by: Joe Robertson Preparation Time: 0:05 Fill a mixing glass
with ice. Add liquors and lemon juice (but NOT the cola). Shake and
strain into a collins glass filled with ice cubes. Fill with cola.
Garnish with a slice of lemon and a sprig of mint and serve with a
straw and an iced tea spoon.
