Title: MACARONI & CHEESE SOUP
Categories: Soups/stews, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 Servings
7 oz Macaroni & cheese
1/2    Stick butter
3 tb Flour
28 oz Chicken broth
-or 3 1/4 cup of your own
1 c  Beer
10 oz Frozen mixed vegetables,
-thawed
1 c  Diced ham
In large saucepan cook the macaroni from the mix in boiling water
until tender; drain & set aside. In the same pan, melt the butter.
Blend in the flour & cheese sauce packet from the dinner mix.
Gradually stir in the chicken broth & beer. Cook & stir until
thickened & bubbly. Add the vegetables & ham. Cook & stir until
heated thru. Stir in the cooked macaroni. From 9/78 Better Homes &
Gardens.
