Title: KOHL'S FUDGE FROSTING
Categories: Improv, Kohl, Desserts, Frosting
Yield: 1 Recipe
1/4 lb Butter or oleo
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Milk
6 tb Cocoa
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Put everything into a saucepan except the vanilla.
Bring to a boil, stirring as needed to mix.  Boil 2
minutes without stirring.  Pour into a bowl, and beat
about 5 minutes or to a gravy consistency.  Add
vanilla. Pour over cool cake.  Sprinkle nuts on top if
desired.

Taken from: IT NEVER TURNS OUT THE SAME WAY COOKBOOK
A Collection of Recipes from the Kitchen of Joyce &
Clem Kohl
