Title: KING ARTHUR MOLASSES CRUMB CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  White whole wheat flour
1 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Butter or margarine (two
Sticks)
1 c  Molasses
2    Eggs
1 c  Hot water
1 ts Baking soda
In a large bowl, mix together flour and sugar. Cut in
butter till mixture is crumby. Remove 1 cup of mixture
and set aside. To the remaining crumb mixture, add
molasses, eggs, water and baking soda. Beat till
smooth. Pour batter into a lightly greased 9"x13" pan.
Sprinkle reserved crumbs evenly over top. Bake in a
preheated 350 oven for 35 to 45 minutes, or until cake
tests done.
