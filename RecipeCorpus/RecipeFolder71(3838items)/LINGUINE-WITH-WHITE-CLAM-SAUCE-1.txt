Title: LINGUINE WITH WHITE CLAM SAUCE 1
Categories: Pasta, Main dish, Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
2/3 c  Margarine
1/3 c  Olive oil
3    Cloves garlic
3    (8 oz) cans minced clams
-drained
Black pepper to taste
1/3 c  Finely chopped parsley
1/4 ts Basil
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Salt
1 lb Linguine
Melt margarine in saucepot;  add oil;  heat.   Saute garlic and clams
in hot  oil mixture over medium heat for 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in
parsley, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer 5-10 minutes. Cook
linguine according to package directions;  drain well. Return to pot;
add sauce; toss lightly.  Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
