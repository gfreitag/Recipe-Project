Title: MACRONI SQUARES
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 8 Servings
1 pk Macaroni & Cheese Mix
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
3 ea Eggs beaten
3 tb Green pepper chopped
2 tb Green onion chopped
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/8 ts Dry mustard
2 tb Chopped parsley
MMMMM------------------------TOMATO SAUCE-----------------------------
2 cn Stewed tomatoes 14 oz.
1 ea Basil leaf
1/2 ts Oragano
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
Prepare macaroni mix & stir in rest of ingredients except tomato
sauce. Pour mixture into greased 8 in. square pan and bake in 350 F
oven 30-40 minutes or until firm. Meanwhile prepare tomato sauce.
Remove squares from oven & let set for 5 min. Cut in squares and
serve with tomato sauce.
