Title: LOBSTER STEW
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/4 lb Lobster, boiled
5 c  Milk
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1 pn White pepper
4 tb Butter
1 ts Salt
Remove meat from lobster body and claws. Cube. Scald milk with lobster
shells in it. Discard shells. Slightly saute' lobster in butter; add
the milk and bring back to boil. Remove from heat, add cream, season
to taste and serve.
