Title: KURBISBRAI MIT KASE
Categories: Cheese/eggs, German
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Pumpkin
1/2 oz Ground ginger
3 oz Butter
4 oz Sliced apples
4 oz Grated cheese
2 c  Salt water
Salt & pepper
Cut pumpkin in segments and remove peeling and
seeds.  Boil in salted water with ginger and apples
for 20 minutes until soft.  Pass pulp through a sieve
and blend with butter.  Season to taste.  Place in a
shallow dish, sprinkle grated cheese and brown under
grill.
