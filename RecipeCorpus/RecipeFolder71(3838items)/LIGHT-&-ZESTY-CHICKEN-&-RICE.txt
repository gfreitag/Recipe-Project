Title: LIGHT & ZESTY CHICKEN & RICE
Categories: Poultry, Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Chicken Breasts
1/3 c  Bottled Italian Dressing
1    Bag (16 oz) Frozen Broccoli
-"Carrots & Caulifour"
Durkee Onions(2.8 oz)
1 3/4 c  Chicken Boullion
1/2 ts Italian Seasoning
Place chicken breasts in 8 x 12 inch baking dish, pour salad dressing
over chicken.  Bake uncovered at 400 degrees for 20 minutes. Place
rice, vegetables and 1/2 can dried onions around and under chicken.
Combine boullion and Italian seasoning - pour over chicken and
vegetables. Bake uncovered 25 minutes. Top with remaining onions,
bake 2-3 minutes longer. Let stand 5 minutes before serving. Serves 4.
