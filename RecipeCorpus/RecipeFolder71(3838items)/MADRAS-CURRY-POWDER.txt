Title: MADRAS CURRY POWDER
Categories: Spices
Yield: 1 Cup
30 ts Red New Mexican chile
2 ts Fenugreek seed
4 ts Cayenne
2 ts Black pepper
24 ts Coriander seed
6 ts Cardamom
24 ts Cumin seed
2 ts Cloves
1 ts Ginger
All dried and ground separately and then ground together. If each
part is 1 teaspoon, the yield is 1 cup.
