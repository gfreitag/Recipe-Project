Title: Popcorn Muffins
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Sugar
2 tb Melted shortening
1 c  Milk
1    Egg, well beaten
1 1/2 c  Flour
3/4 c  Ground popped corn
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder, salt, and sugar. Add
milk, popped corn, egg, and shortening.  Fill well-oiled muffin tins 2/3
full.  Bake in hot oven (435Ã¸ F) 25 minutes. 6 servings. Grace Viall Gray,
Glen Ellyn, IL.
