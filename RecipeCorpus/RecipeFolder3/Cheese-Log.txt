Title: Cheese Log
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 10 servings
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Red pepper
2 tb Mayonnaise
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 lb New York Sharp Cheese
1/2 lb Pimento cheese
1/2 lb Cream cheese
2    Garlic cloves
Grate cheese.  Add all ingredients, chill a little and divide into thirds.
Roll in paprika.  Can be frozen.
