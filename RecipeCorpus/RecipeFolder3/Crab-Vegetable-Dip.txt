Title: Crab Vegetable Dip
Categories: Dips, Fish/sea
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Tobasco sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1 ea Boiled egg, hard, chopped
1 c  Crab meat, drained
1/2 c  Chilli sauce
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Garlic
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Mix all ingredients together and let sit overnight.
from Gail
