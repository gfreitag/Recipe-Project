Title: Microwave Polenta
Categories: Italian
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Olive oil
3 1/2    Q water
1 c  Cornmeal
1/2 ts Salt
Mix all ingredients in a 3-quart microwave-safe dish.  Cover and cook on
high power for 5 minutes.  Stir and cook on high power until thickened,
about 5 minutes longer.

Makes 4 servings.
