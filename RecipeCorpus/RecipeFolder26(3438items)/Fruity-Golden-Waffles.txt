Title: Fruity Golden Waffles
Categories: Breakfast, Fruit, Waffles
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Honey
1/2 ts Salt
5 c  Water
4 c  Rolled oats
1/3 c  Whole-wheat flour
1/4 c  Dates; chopped or
Mix the oats, flour, dates, raisins, or honey, and salt in a large
bowl. Add the water to make a batter.  Pour the batter into a blender
in batches until consistency is smooth.  (or process all at once in a
food processor.) Cook for 8 to 10 minutes in a waffle iron set on
high. Notes: 1. This freezes well 2. You may omit the sweetener and
use these as a base for bean or other savory sauces.
