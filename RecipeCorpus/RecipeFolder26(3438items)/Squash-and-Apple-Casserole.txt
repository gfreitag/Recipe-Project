Title: Squash and Apple Casserole
Categories: Side dish, Casserole, Vegetable, Fruit, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Sugar, brown; firmly packed
6 tb Butter (or marg.)
Cinnamon, ground
3 c  Squash, butternut; cubed,
-cooked
1 1/2 c  Apples; stewed
Salt
Place 1 cup squash in a buttered casserole; top with 1/2 cup apples.
Sprinkle lightly with salt and 1/4 cup sugar; dot with 2 tablespoons
butter. Repeat layers twice; sprinkle with cinnamon. Bake at 350
degrees for 45 minutes or until lightly browned.
