Title: Lazy Day Lima Bean Casserole
Categories: Beans, Cheese/eggs, Tomato
Yield: 2 servings
2 c  Cooked, large lima beans
2 md Tomatoes
Salt and pepper to taste
2 c  Grated American cheese
2/3 c  Undiluted evaporated milk
1/2 t  Prepared mustard
combine cheese, milk and mustard.  Cook and stir over hot water until
cheese melts and sauce is smooth.  Put lima beans into ovenproof
casserole. Cover with 3/4 of sauce.  Top casserole with tomato
slices, salt and pepper and the remaining sauce.  Bake in a 375F oven
for 25 minutes or until lightly browned and bubbly on top. Origin:
Women's Circle, for cooks on the go. Shared by: Sharon Stevens
