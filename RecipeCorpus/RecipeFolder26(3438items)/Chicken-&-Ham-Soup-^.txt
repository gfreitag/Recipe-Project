Title: Chicken & Ham Soup ^
Categories: Chicken, Soup/stew, Pork
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Chopped fresh tarragon
-OR-
1/2 ts Dried tarragon
1 tb Chopped parsley
5 c  Clear chicken stock
1/2 c  White wine
2 sl Mild cooked ham, fat removed
-and shredded
1/2 c  Lightly cooked fresh peas
Heat chicken stock in large saucepan. When stock is hot, add wine,
ham, peas and tarragon. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
