Title: Old World Rye
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------1 1/2 LB-------------------------------
1 1/2 tb Soy flour
3/4 c  Whole-wheat flour
1/3 c  Rye flour
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
3 tb Buttermilk powder
1 1/2 tb Vital gluten; optional
1 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
2 1/2 ts Lemon juice
1 c  Water
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Honey
3/4 ts Salt
1/3 ts Baking soda
2 1/2 ts Onion flakes; dried
1 tb Caraway seed
1 1/2 tb Wheat germ
MMMMM--------------------------1 3/4 LB-------------------------------
2 tb Soy flour
1 c  Whole-wheat flour
1/2 c  Rye flour
2 c  Bread flour
1/4 c  Buttermilk powder
2 tb Vital gluten; optional
2 ts Active dry yeast
1 tb Lemon juice
1 1/4 c  Water
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Honey
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 tb Onion flakes; dried
1 tb Caraway seed
2 tb Wheat germ
Recipe by: The Bread Machine Cookbook V - ISBN 1-55867-093-9 Add lemon
juice 10 mins after starting machine. CYCLE: wheat, white sweet; timer
SETTING: light to medium

NOTES : Add the lemon juice 10 mins after starting the machine --- in
all
machines. If fresh buttermilk is on hand, substitute it for the
water (cup for cup) and omit the dried buttermilk powder.

From:  Dan Klepach
