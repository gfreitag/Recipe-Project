Title: Chocolate Caramels
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 10 servings
3/4 c  Light Karo
1/2 c  Coarsely chopped nuts
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Butter
3 oz Unsweetened chocolate
2 1/4 c  Sugar
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
Melt butter and chocolate in 3-quart heavy saucepan over low heat.
Stir in sugar, condensed milk and Karo until well blended. Cook over
medium heat stirring freqently, to firm ball stage, 248 degrees,
15-18 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir in nuts and vanilla. Pour into
well-buttered 9-inch square pan. When candy is firm, cut into 1-inch
squares. Wrap in plastic wrap. Store in cool place. Makes 2 1/2
pounds.
