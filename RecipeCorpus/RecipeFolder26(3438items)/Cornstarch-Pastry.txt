Title: Cornstarch Pastry
Categories: Pastry
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------FROM THE KITCHEN OF: GARY &---------------------
1 T  Sugar
1 ea Egg Yolk
1 1/2 t  Salt
Water
3/4 c  Cornstarch
1 t  Baking Powder
1 1/4 c  Flour
6 T  Shortening
Mix and sift all the dry ingredients. Rub in shortening with a fork or
finger tips, then add beaten egg yolk. Add enough water to hold paste
together and roll out.
