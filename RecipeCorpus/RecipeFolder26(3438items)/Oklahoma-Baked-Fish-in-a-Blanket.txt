Title: Oklahoma Baked Fish in a Blanket
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
No Ingredients Found
Clean a large whole fish weighing 4-5 pounds.  Season fish inside and
out with salt and pepper.  Sprinkle with lemon juice and dot with
butter. Make a thick paste of flour and water.  Cover entire fish
with paste. Place on greased baking sheet.  Bake in preheated very
hot oven (450F) for 25-30 minutes.  Remove from oven.  Crack crust
with mallet and remove fish carefully.  Discard crust.  Serve fish
with lemon. Makes 6 servings.

Origin: Woman's Day Encyclopedia of Cookery, Volume 1, Oklahoma
section. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, April/94
