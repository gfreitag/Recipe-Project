Title: Louisa May Alcott's Apple Slump
Categories: Dessert, Apples, Bread
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour;
Sifted
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Milk
6 c  Apples, pared, cored
And sliced
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Water
Recipe by: The American Heritage Cookbook - ISBN 0-8281-04-03-4
Combine apple slices, sugar, cinnamon, and water in a saucepan with a
tight-fitting lid. Heat to the boiling point. Sift together flour,
salt, baking powder. Stir in enough milk to make a soft dough. Drop
dough from a tablespoon onto apple mixture. Cover tightly and cook
over low heat for 30 mins. Serve warm with New England Nutmeg Sauce
or rich cream. This dessert can be made with various fresh or frozen
berries. Serves 6.

From:  Dan Klepach
