Title: Molho De Pimenta E Limao (Pepper and Lemon Sauce) (Brazil
Categories: Salsa/sauce, So. america, Fruit, Chilies
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Finely chopped garlic
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice
4 ea Bottled tabasco peppers,
-drained and finely chopped
1/2 c  Finely chopped onions
In a small bowl, combine the peppers, onion, garlic and lemon juice,
and stir until they are well mixed.  Marinate, uncovered, at room
temperature for an hour before serving, or refrigerate, covered, for
as long as 4 hours. Makes about 1 cup. From: FOODS OF THE WORLD:
LATIN AMERICAN COOKING, Time-Life, New York. 1968 From the files of
Karin Brewer
