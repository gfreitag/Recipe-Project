Title: Quick Ginger Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Chinese, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2    Red or Green Peppers, cut
-into strips
6    Green Onions, cut into 2"
-pieces
1 lg Garlic Clove, minced
2    Whole Chicken Breasts,
-skinned, boned, cut into
-1" chucks
1/3 c  Water
3 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Cooking Sherry (optional)
1 tb Corn starch
1 ts Sugar
1 1/2 ts Minced fresh Ginger, or
-1/2 ts dried ginger
3 tb Corn oil, divided
Stir water, soy sauce, sherry, corn starch, sugar and ginger until
smooth; set aside.  In large skillet heat 1 tbsp. corn oil over
medium-high heat. Add peppers, green onions, and garlic; saute 4
minutes. Remove. Add remaining 2 tbsp. corn oil; heat, Add chicken;
saute 5 minutes or until lightly browned.  Stir corn starch mixture.
Add to skillet with vegetables. Stirring constantly, bring to boil
over medium heat; boil 1 minute. If desired, serve with rice.
