Title: Blue Cheese and Walnut Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains, Fish
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 ts Thyme; minced OR
1/4 ts Dried thyme
Salt and pepper
1/4 c  Yogurt
1/4 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Blue cheese; crumbled
1/3 c  Walnuts; finely chopped
Stir all the ingredients together in a small bowl. Taste for
seasoning and spoon on top of grilled or broiled swordfish.
