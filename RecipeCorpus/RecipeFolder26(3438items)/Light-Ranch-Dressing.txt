Title: Light Ranch Dressing
Categories: Fatfree, Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Celery seed
1/4 t  Pepper
1/4 t  White wine vinegar
1/8 t  Salt
1 T  Finely chopped parsley OR
1 t  Dried flakes
2    Cloves garlic, minced
3/4 t  Onion powder
1/2 t  Dried basil, crushed
Combine ingredients and refrigerate.
