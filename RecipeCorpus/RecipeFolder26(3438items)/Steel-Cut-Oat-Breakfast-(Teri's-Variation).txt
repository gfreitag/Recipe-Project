Title: Steel-Cut Oat Breakfast (Teri's Variation)
Categories: Fatfree, Breakfast, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 c  Crushed pineapple
1 t  Cinammon
1 t  Nutmeg
2 t  Brown sugar
1 1/3 c  Water
1 c  Steel Cup Oats
1 t  Blackstrap Mollasses
1/2 c  Dried currants
Boil water, oats, mollasses, currants, sugar.  Turn down flame. Add
remaining ingredients.  Stir occasionally. Remove when all liquid has
been absorbed - around 15-20 minutes.

Serve hot.

For a nice creamy texture, mix in some solid-pack pumpkin after
removing from the heat.
