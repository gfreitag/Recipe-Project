Title: Madame Pale's Lava Flow
Categories: Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 2 helpers
6 oz Pineapple juice;
6 oz Coconut syrup;
1 oz Half and half;
Crushed ice;
4    To 5 fresh strawberries OR
4    To 5 fresh raspberries;
1 oz Guave nectar;
Splash of grenadine;
Blend berries, nectar and grenadine until smooth.  Set aside.  Blend
pineapple juice, coconut syrup and half and half with crushed ice.
Pour into berry mixture so it that it swirls and suggests a lava
flow. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune Food Section, Dec 22, 1994
and The Kona Village Resort. Brought to you and yours via Nancy
O'Brion and her Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On
12-27-94
