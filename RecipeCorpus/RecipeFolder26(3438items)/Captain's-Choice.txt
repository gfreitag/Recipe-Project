Title: Captain's Choice
Categories: Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 1 human
2    Sprigs mint leaves;
Whipped cream to taste;
4 oz Hot cranberry juice;
4 oz Apple juice;
2    Cinnamon sticks;
Warm cranberry juice and apple juice over low heat.  Add cinnamon
stick. Pour into glass Irish coffee mugs.   Garnish with whipped
cream a sprig of mint. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune Food
Section, Dec. 22, 1994 and Loew's Bay Resort. Brought to you and
yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
