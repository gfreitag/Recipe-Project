Title: Tuna Fish Pate
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Seafood, Fish, Spread
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Dijon mustard
2 tb Grated onion
2 tb Brandy
Salt and pepper to taste
2    (6 1/2oz) cans tuna fish
-drained
1 c  Butter, softened
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Lemon juice
Mix all ingredients together well to form smooth mixture. Pack
tightly into serving dish. Chill well before serving.
