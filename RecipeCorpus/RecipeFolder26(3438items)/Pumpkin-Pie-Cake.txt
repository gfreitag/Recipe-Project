Title: Pumpkin Pie Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Pumpkin
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  White sugar
2/3 c  Milk
1 t  Vanilla
1 1/2 t  Cinnamon
1 ea Box Yellow Cake mix Reserve
-1 cup for topping
1/2 c  Butter, melted
1 ea Egg
29 oz Pumpkin
3 ea Eggs
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/4 c  Butter, cold
1 c  Nuts, chopped
1 c  Cake mix (reserved)
1/2 c  White sugar
Stirring by hand, mix together the cakes, melted butter and 1 egg (do
not use cake mix directions).  Spread mixture in bottom of a
9x13-inch pan. Mix all of remaining ingredients together and pour
over first mixture.

TOPPING:  Mix the 1 cup of cake mix, sugar, butter and nuts together
and sprinkle over top of cake.  Bake cake at 350 degrees for 55
minutes.
