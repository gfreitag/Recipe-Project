Title: Crazy Banana Cake - Jean Ramsay
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 t  Baking
-Soda
2/3 c  Mashed
-very ripe Bananas
2 lg Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 t  Baking Powder
1/8 t  Salt
2/3 c  Chopped Walnuts
1/3 c  Oil
1/3 c  Buttermilk
1 t  Banana Flavoring
Into a square cake pan, measure flour, sugar, baking powder, baking
soda, salt and nuts. Stir with fork to mix well. Add remaining
ingredients; stir until blended and smooth. With a rubber spatula,
scrape any ingredients clinging to the sides, corners, and bottom of
the pan into the batter. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes
until a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
Cool in pan on a rack. Cut into 9 squares.
