Title: Swiss Sausage Casserole
Categories: Breakfast, Casserole, Sausage, Sthrn/livng, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 9 servings
1 1/4 c  Milk
3/4 c  Half-and-half
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
ds Pepper
ds Nutmeg, ground
2 lb Sausage, mild bulk
1 ts Mustard, prepared
12 sl Bread, thin-sliced sandwich;
-crusts removed
6 sl Cheese, Swiss
3    Eggs
Cook sausage until browned, stirring to crumble; drain well.  Combine
sausage and mustard, stirring until blended.  Cut bread slices in half
diagonally.  Space half of bread evenly in a greased 12x8x2" baking
dish. Sprinkle sausage over bread; top with cheese slices.  Space
remaining bread halves evenly on top of cheese slices.

Combine remaining ingredients; beat until blended.  Pour egg mixture
over bread in baking dish.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.

Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes or until egg
mixture is set.
