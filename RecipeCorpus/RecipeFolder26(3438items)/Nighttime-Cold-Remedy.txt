Title: Nighttime Cold Remedy
Categories: Information
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Hot water
2 tb Brandy
1    Lemon
1/4 c  Maple syrup
Squeeze all the juice from the lemon and stir in the maple syrup. Add
the hot water and brandy. Use entire recipe. Sip slow and go to sleep
for 8 hours. Repeat every 24 hours as needed.
