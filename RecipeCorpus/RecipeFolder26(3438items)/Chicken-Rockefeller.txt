Title: Chicken Rockefeller
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Sthrn/livng, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
-and skinned
Salt; to taste
Pepper; to taste
3 tb Butter (or marg.); melted
10 oz Spinach, chopped
1    Egg; beaten
1/4 c  Cheese, Parmesan; divided
1 c  Breadcrumbs, Italian-style
12    Chicken breast halves; boned
Cook spinach according to package directions; drain well, and allow to
cool.  Combine spinach, egg, and 1 tablespoon Parmesan; set aside.
Combine breadcrumbs and remaining Parmesan in a shallow pan; set
aside.

Sprinkle chicken breasts with salt and pepper; roll each in breadcrumb
mixture.  Place in a greased 13x9x2" baking dish.

Place 1 heaping tablespoon of spinach mixture on each chicken breast,
spreading to form a small mound.  Sprinkle with remaining breadcrumb
mixture, and drizzle with butter.  Bake chicken at 350 degrees for 40
minutes.
