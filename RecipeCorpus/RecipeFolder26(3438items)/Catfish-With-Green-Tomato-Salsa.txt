Title: Catfish With Green Tomato Salsa
Categories: Seafood, Fish, Salsa/sauce, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  Cilantro, chopped
2 t  Lime juice
1/4 t  Ground coriander
1 lb Catfish, cut into 4 serving
-pieces
1/2 c  Prepared Chunky Salsa
2 ea Green tomatoes, chopped
Pat fish dry with paper towels.  Arrange in an 8-inch square baking
dish so that pieces are towards the sides of dish.  Combine salsa,
tomatoes, cilantro, lime juice and coriander. Spoon mixture over
fish. Cover with vented plastic wrap.  Rotating dish midway through
cooking, cook on high un microwave, 3 to 4 minutes, or just until
fish flakes. Serve on a heated tortilla garnished with cilantro
sprigs. Source: National Fisheries Institute
