Title: Waffles - Country Cooking
Categories: Breakfast, Waffles
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
Confectioners' sugar
Berries (opt.)
Mystery Syrup, opt.
3 lg Eggs
1 1/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1 3/4 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
2 T  Granulated sugar
1. Separate eggs, placing yolks in a medium-size bowl and whites in a
small bowl. With electric mixer on high speed, beat yolks until light
yellow and thick. Reduce mixer speed to medium. Beat in milk and oil
until combined.

2. Add flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt to milk mixture and beat
until smooth batter forms.

3. Wash and dry beaters. With mixer on high speed, beat egg whites
until soft peaks form; gently fold into batter.

4. Spoon batter onto preheated waffle iron which has been coated with
non-stick vegetable cooking spray. Bake until waffle is golden brown
on both sides. Remove to individual plate and dust with
confectioners' sugar. Serve with berries and syrup, if desired.

Makes 12 5 by 3 1/2-inch waffles
