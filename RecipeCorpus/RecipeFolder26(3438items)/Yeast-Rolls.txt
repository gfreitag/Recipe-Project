Title: Yeast Rolls
Categories: Bread
Yield: 50 servings
2 c  Water
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Lard
6 1/2 c  Flour, all purpose
1 1/2 t  Salt
3/4 c  Sugar
5 ea Pks yeast
Water has to be warm and milk also.  Put yeast into 1/2 cup warm
water to dissolve.  Mix into flour, salt, sugar and lard. Pinch out
into rolls. Let rise and bake at 350 for about 15 minutes or until
brown.
