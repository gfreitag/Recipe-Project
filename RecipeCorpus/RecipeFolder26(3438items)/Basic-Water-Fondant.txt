Title: Basic Water Fondant
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
Few grains of salt
4 c  White sugar
1/4 t  Cream of tartar
Mix all ingredients together. Put over high heat and stir until well
blended.  Cook to soft ballstage (230F).  Remove and cool. Always let
it cool in the pan that it cooks in.  When almost cool, pour onto
platter and beat till set.  Work with hands and add flavoring.  Place
in tightly covered bowl and store in cool place. Origin: My recipe
files Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
