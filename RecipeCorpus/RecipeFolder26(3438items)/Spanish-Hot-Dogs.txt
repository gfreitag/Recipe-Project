Title: Spanish Hot Dogs
Categories: Tomato, Rice, Hot dogs, Main dish, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Green pepper, chopped
1 ea Onion, chopped
Rice
1 lb Hotdogs (or as many as you
-have)
1 cn Stewed tomatoes or plain
-canned tomatoes
Start the pepper and onion sauteeing in a little butter.  slice the
hotdogs into pennies, add to half cooked pepper & onion mix.  Add
canned tomatoes, taste for seasoning, lower heat and simmer while
rice cooks. Cook as much rice as you need and serve hotdog mix over
rice
