Title: Crunchy Sauerkraut Salad
Categories: Fatfree, Apples, Vegetable, Salad
Yield: 10 servings
MMMMM--------------------MDFD30E HELEN JOLLY-------------------------
3 T  Lemon juice
2 T  Sugar
1/2 t  Dried parsley flakes
1/2 t  Dried basil
1/2 t  Dill weed
1/4 t  Pepper
1 cn (27 ozs.) sauerkraut, rinsed
And drained
1    Unpeeled apple, chopped
1 sm Onion, chopped
3/4 c  Finely chopped dill pickle
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
In a bowl, combine all ingredients. Cover and chill for at least 1
hour. Yield: 10-12 servings.
