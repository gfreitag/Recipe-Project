Title: Cheese Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Chicken, Cheese/eggs, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
ea Salt and Pepper for Taste
1 pk Shredded Cheddar Cheese
4 lg Chicken Breasts
1 cn Cheddar Chees or Nacho Soup
1/2 cn Milk
Place chicken in single layer baking dish and add salt and Pepper to
chicken.

Mix cheese soup, milk, and salt and pepper well in bowl until blended.

Pour mixture over chicken and bake in the oven at 350 degrees for 1
1/2 hours or until tender.

When alomost done, sprinkle with cheddar cheese and place in oven
until melted.
