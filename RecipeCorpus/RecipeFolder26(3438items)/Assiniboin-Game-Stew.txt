Title: Assiniboin Game Stew
Categories: Native amer, Game, Soup/stew, Vegetable, Potato
Yield: 8 servings
4    Green onions, sliced
4    White turnips, peeled and
-diced
4 md Potatoes, peeled and diced
1    Leek, chopped
2 lb Cubed moose, elk, deer or
-lean beef
1/3 c  Maple syrup
4 c  Water
Salt to taste
Place meat on skewers and sear over an open fire or brown in a large
skillet.  Place browned meat and remaining ingredients in a large pot.
Simmer over an open fire or on the stove over medium-low heat for
about 1 hour, until meat is tender.

From "Spirit of The Harvest:  North American Indian Cooking," by
Beverly Cox and Martin Jacobs.
