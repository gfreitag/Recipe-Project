Title: Mounds Candy Bar
Categories: Candy, Chocolate
Yield: 20 servings
1 ts Almond extract
1 lb Powdered sugar
3/4 c  Mashed potatoes
1 lb Coconut (4 cups)
MMMMM--------------------------COATING-------------------------------
4 oz Semi-sweet chocolate
6 oz Chocolate bits
Mix ingredients, roll into balls. Put in fridge for 1 hour. COATING:
Melt over hot water and dip balls. Place on wax paper. Put in fridge.
Store in tightly sealed container. NOTE: Any chocolate will work,
aslong as it will harden. Rorna Baker *P RDXS99B 12/27/92 11:17am
