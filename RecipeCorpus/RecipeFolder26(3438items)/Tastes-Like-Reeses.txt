Title: Tastes Like Reeses
Categories: Candy, Nuts/grains, Dessert, Kids, Chocolate
Yield: 1 pan
2 c  Chocolate chips,
- sweetened
1 c  Peanut butter
1/3 lb Graham cracker crumbs 1/2 bx
1/2 lb Soft butter 2 sticks
1 lb Confectioners sugar 1 box
Combine all but Chocolate Chips until smooth, press into a buttered 9
X 9 inch pan.  Melt Chocolate Chips very slowely or use microwave.
spread the melted chocolate over the mixture in the pan, allow to
cool and harden. Cut into squares.
