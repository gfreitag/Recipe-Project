Title: Jalapeno/Salsa Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Tomato, Chilies
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Salt
1/2 c  Distilled white vinegar
1 t  Garlic salt
1 lb Fresh firm jalapeno peppers
1 lb Onions peeled and chopped
1 lb Tomatoes peeled (about 3)
Cut stems and remove seeds from peppers, wash and place in pot with
onions and tomatoes. Add salt, vinegar and garlic salt and cook about
1 hour, or until onions are soft. Puree in a blender and store in the
refrigerator. Makes about 3 pints. NOTE: Wear gloves when removing
seeds from peppers.
