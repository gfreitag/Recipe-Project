Title: Cheese Brioche, ABM
Categories: Breadmaker, Bread, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 lb. loaf
6 tb Butter, unsalted
2 lg Eggs
1/2 c  Water
1/3 c  Swiss cheese, finely diced
1 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
1 3/4 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
Add all ingredients except the Swiss chese in the order suggested by
your abm manual and process on the basic bread cycle according to the
manufacturer's directions.

At the beeper (or a few minutes before the end of the kneading cycle)
add the Swiss cheese.
