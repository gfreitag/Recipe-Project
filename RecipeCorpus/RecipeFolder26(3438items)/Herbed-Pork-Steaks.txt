Title: Herbed Pork Steaks
Categories: Main dish, Pork, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 4 servings
-finely crushed
4    Pork steaks;1/2 - 3/4" thick
3 tb Shortening
1 oz Gravy mix, mushroom; opt.
1    Egg
3 tb Milk
1/3 c  Cheese, Parmesan; grated
1 1/4 c  Stuffing mix, herb-flavored;
Combine egg and milk in a shallow dish, beating with a fork until well
blended.  Combine cheese and stuffing mix in a shallow dish.  Dip
steaks into egg mixture; then dredge in stuffing mixture, coating
well.

Heat shortening to 350 degrees in an electric skillet; cook steaks
until browned on both sides.  Cover and reduce heat to 250 degrees;
cook 30 minutes or until steaks are done.

If desired, prepare mushroom gravy according to package directions.
Serve with steaks.
