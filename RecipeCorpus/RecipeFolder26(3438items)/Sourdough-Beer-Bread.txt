Title: Sourdough Beer Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Sourdough
Yield: 1 loaf
3 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Yeast
1 1/3 c  Sourdough starter
1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Flat beer
2 tb Vegetable oil
Place all the ingredients in the machine and program for basic white
bread. Set the temperature control to Dark and press Start. Suggest
is to flatten beer, pour it into a small saucepan and heat it to a
simmer. By the time the beer cools down, it will be flat.

Recipe from Bread Machine Baking Perfect Every Time, originally
posted by Marianne Wright, Illinois p.reynolds. Genie
