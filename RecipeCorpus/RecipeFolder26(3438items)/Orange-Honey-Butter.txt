Title: Orange-Honey Butter
Categories: Condiment, Fruit, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Frozen orange juice
-concentrate
1 c  Butter; softened
1/3 c  Honey
1 tb Grated orange peel
Blend together and store in refrigerator.
