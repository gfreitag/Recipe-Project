Title: Sweet and Salty Snack Mix
Categories: Diabetic, Snack
Yield: 9 servings
1 1/2 qt Air-popped popcorn;
1 c  Honey-flavored bear-shaped
Graham crackers
1/2 c  Raisins;
Cinnamon
1 c  Pretzel sticks;
1/4 c  Peanuts;
1 tb Margarine; melted -=OR=-
1 tb Butter melted
Sugar substitute equivalent
To 1 tb sugar
In a large bowl, combine pretzel sticks and peanuts.  Pour melted
margarine over mixture; sprinkle with sugar substitute.  Toss gently
to coat. Add popcorn crackers and raisins; sprinkle lightly with
cinnamon. Toss mixture gently.  Store in airtight container.  9
(1-cup) serving.

Food Exchange per serving: 1 STARCH EXCHANGE + 1 FAT  EXCHANGE; CAL:
130; PRO: 3g; CAR: 22g; FAT: 5g; CHO: 9mg; SOD: 210mg; POT: 125mg;
