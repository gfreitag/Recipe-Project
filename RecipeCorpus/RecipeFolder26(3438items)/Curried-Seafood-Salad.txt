Title: Curried Seafood Salad
Categories: Salad, Fish, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
10 oz Frozen peas, cooked
4 c  Rice, cooked
1/4 c  Italian salad dressing
Lettuce leaves
3    Hard cooked eggs, wedge cut
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Curry powder
1 cn Tuna, drained (7 oz size)
2 c  Shrimp,cooked/shelled/veined
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
Early in day to allow flavors to blend:
In large bowl stir mayonnaise, lemon juice & curry powder with fork
until well blended.  Add tuna, shrimp, celery and peas and toss well.
Cover and refrigerate.  In medium bowl toss rice with Italian
dressing, cover and refrigerate.
Before serving, arrange ring of rice mixture in lettuce lined salad
bowl, spoon tuna mix into center. Garnish with egg wedges.
