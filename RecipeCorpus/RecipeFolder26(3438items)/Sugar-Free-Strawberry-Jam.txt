Title: Sugar Free Strawberry Jam
Categories: Diabetic, Can/preserv, Fruit
Yield: 2 3/4 cups
6    10 packets sugar
-substitute
1 pk (3 oz) strawberry-flavored
3 qt Whole strawberries;
-fresh or frozen
-sugar-free gelatin
Crush berries until a small amount of juice forms.  Place in a medium
saucepan; bring to a boil.  Boil 2 minutes.  Stir in gelatin until
dissolved. Remove from heat add the sweetner and mix well. Adjust
sweetner to taste. Pour into jars or plastic containers. Store in
refrigerator up to 3 weeks. Yield: 2-3/4 cups Diabetic Exchange: one
1-tablespoon serving equals a free food; also 10 calories, 6 mg.
sodium, 0cholestrol, 3 gm. carbohydrates, trace protein, 00 fat.
