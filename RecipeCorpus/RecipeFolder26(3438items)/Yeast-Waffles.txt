Title: Yeast Waffles
Categories: Breakfast, Waffles
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Sugar
2 c  Flour
2    Eggs
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Warm water
1 pk Dry yeast
2 c  Warm milk
1/2 c  Melted butter
1 ts Salt
Use a large mixing bowl, batter will raise to twice it's volume.  put
water in mixing bowl, sprinkle with yeast.  Let stand to dissolve for
5 minutes. Add the milk, butter, salt, sugar, and flour to the yeast
mixture and beat until smooth and blended.  Cover with platic wrap
and let stand overnight at room temperature.  Just before cooking,
beat in the eggs, add the baking soda, and stir until well mixed.
Batter will be very thin. Bake until golden and crisp.  Left over
batter will keep will for several days covered in the refrigerator.
