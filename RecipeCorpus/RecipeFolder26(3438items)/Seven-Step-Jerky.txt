Title: Seven Step Jerky
Categories: Can/preserv, Game, Marinade
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Onion powder
2/3 t  Black pepper
3 lb Venison (or other steak,
-lean)
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 t  Accent
2 t  Seasoned salt
2/3 t  Garlic powder
Slice your choice of meat into strips about 3/8" thick to ensure total
sauturation of the eat while it is marinating. In container, combine
all ingredients. Stir this mixture well with a spoon to dissolve all
of the soluble ingredients. Now the marinade is ready to be used.

Place the strips of meat in the marinade and be sure they are
completely submerged. Marinate overnight, turning occasionally.

Lay marinated meat strips on oven rack. Cook for 6 to 8 hours at 150
degrees. They get crisper the longer they bake.

Store finished jerky in ziplock bags or other airtight containers to
seal in freshness. Jerky will keep up to two years.
