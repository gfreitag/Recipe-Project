Title: Apple Fantasy Dessert
Categories: Fatfree, Dessert, Apples
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar
3 t  Vanilla
3 c  Apples, cut fairly small,
Skins on is fine.
2/3 c  Flour
3 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
4    Egg whites
1/2 c  Sugar
Beat eggs, add sugar and vanilla and beat well.  Add dry ingredients
and mix.  Dump in apples and stir until evenly distributed.  Bake in
a deep baking dish,  45 minutes at 350.  Serve with custard.
