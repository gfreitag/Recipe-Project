Title: Mashed Potatoes And Mashed Potato Patties
Categories: Side dish, Potato
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------------MASHED POTATOES---------------------------
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 md Potatoes (about 1 3/4 lbs.)
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Butter
MMMMM-------------------MASHED POTATO PATTIES------------------------
1    Egg; beaten
Bacon drippings or vegetable
-shortening
1    Recipe mashed potatoes
1/2 c  Self-rising flour
1/4 c  Onion; finely minced
Recipe by: Fannie Flagg's Original Whistle Stop Cafe Cookbook MASHED
POTAOES:  Cook potatoes in boiling water covered for 15 mins, or until
tender. Pour off water, leaving potatoes in saucepan; return to heat
and shake over heat briefly to evaporate water. Remove from pan and
mash with a potato masher. Add next 4 ingredients and mash well.
Serve hot. YIELD: 4 servings

MASHED POTATO PATTIES:  Allow mashed potatoes to cool. Stir in flour,
onion, and egg; mixing well. Drop 1/4 cup of potato mixture at a time
into 1/8 inch of hot bacon drippings in a heavy skillet; press into
3/4-inch thick rounds with the back of a spatula or fork. Fry until
golden brown, turning once. Drain well. YIELD: 1 dozen

NOTES : Combination of 2 recipes - since they both went together.
This is
a definite "try me" for breakfast.
