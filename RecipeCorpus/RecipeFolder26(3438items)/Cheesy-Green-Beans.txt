Title: Cheesy Green Beans
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Beans, Side dish, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Frozen cut green beans
1 tb Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 ts Margarine or butter
1 tb Finely chopped onion
1 cl Garlic, minced
Melt margarine in small saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and
garlic; cook, stirring frequently, until onion begins to brown. Set
aside. In same saucepan, cook green beans as directed on package;
drain. Add onion and garlic; cook until thoroughly heated.

Per serving: 60cal., 3g pro., 8g carb., 3g fiber, 2g fat, 4mg chol.,
35mg sod., 180mg pot., 1 vegetable and 1/2 fat exchanges.
