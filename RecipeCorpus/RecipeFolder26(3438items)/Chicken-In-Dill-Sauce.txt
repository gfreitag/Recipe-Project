Title: Chicken In Dill Sauce
Categories: Polish, Chicken, European, Salsa/sauce, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Flour
1 c  Light cream
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Dill, fresh; chopped
1    Frying chicken, about 3 lbs,
-cut in pieces
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
Salt and pepper; to taste
3    Parsley
Recipe by: The Eastern European Cookbook-ISBN 0-486-23562-9

Saute chicken pieces in butter in a heavy saucepan or kettle. Season
with salt and pepper. Add parsley and enough water to cover chicken.
Cook slowly, covered, about 30 mins., or until chicken it tender.
Remove chicken pieces to a warm plate and keep warm. Combine flour,
cream, lemon juice and dill and add to drippings. Mix well and cook
over low heat, stirring, until thickened. Correct seasoning. Spoon
over chicken and serve with potatoes or rice. Serves 4.
