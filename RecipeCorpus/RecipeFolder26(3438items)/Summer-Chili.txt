Title: Summer Chili
Categories: Fatfree, Chili, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Red or green pepper chopped
1/4 lb Mushrooms sliced
1/2 c  Tomatoes chopped
2 t  Chili powder
1 t  Cumin
1/4 t  Black pepper
1/8 t  Cayenne
1 c  Tomato sauce
1 t  Honey
1 sm Onion chopped
1 1/2    Cloves garlic minced
1 T  Olive oil
1 c  Water
1 c  Kidney beans
1/4 c  Celery chopped
1/4 c  Carrots sliced fine
1 c  Squash chopped
1 c  Zucchini chopped
In a large saucepan, cook onion and garlic, in oil until tender.  Add
water, ki dney beans, celery, carrots, squash, zucchini, pepper,
mushrooms, tomatoes, tomato sauce, and honey  until soft. Mix in
chili powder, cumin, black pepper, and cayenne until blended.  Simmer
al l ingredients about 1 to 2 hours or until bubbly.
