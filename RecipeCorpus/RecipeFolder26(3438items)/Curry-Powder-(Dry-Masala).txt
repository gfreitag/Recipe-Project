Title: Curry Powder (Dry Masala)
Categories: Curries, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 cup
2 ts Cumin seeds
2 ts Black peppercorns
2 ts Mustard seeds
2    Dried hot red peppers
-or to taste
1 ts Saffron threads OR
1 ts Ground turmeric
1/2 c  Coriander seeds
2 ts Anise seeds
2 ts Cloves
Grind all the ingredients together. Store in an airtight container at
room temperature for up to 6 months.

Add 1 large onion, finely chopped, and 4 garlic cloves, chopped, to
make curry paste.
