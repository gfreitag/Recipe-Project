Title: New York-Florida Rye
Categories: Bread
Yield: 14 servings
1 ts Salt
1 tb Honey
1 tb Butter
1 1/2 tb Caraway seeds
1 1/2 c  Water
1 pk Yeast
1 c  Rye flour
2 tb Gluten
3 c  Bread flour
2 tb Buttermilk powder
1 tb Sugar
This makes a 1 1/2 lb. loaf.  Recipe by Marilyn Frieman in St.
Petersburg Submitted By PAT REYNOLDS   On   01-05-95
