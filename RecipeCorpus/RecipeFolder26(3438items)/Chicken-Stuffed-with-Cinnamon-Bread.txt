Title: Chicken Stuffed with Cinnamon Bread
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Poultry, Chicken, Apples
Yield: 4 servings
-chopped (about 1 cup)
2 tb Margarine or butter; melted
1/2 c  Apple juice
1/4 ts Cornstarch
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
4 x  Chicken breasts; boneless,
-skinless, (about 1 lb)
2 sl Cinnamon bread; cut into
-1/4-inch cubes
1 md Apple; tart cooking, finely
Place chicken breast halves between 2 pieces of waxed paper. Pound
chicken to 1/4-inch thickness.  Mix bread cubes and 1/4 cup of the
chopped apple.  Place 1/4 cup bread mixture on each chicken breast
half. Roll chicken around bread mixture; secure with toothpicks.
Cover and grill the chicken 4 to 6 inches from medium coals 20 to
25 minutes, turning after 10 minutes, and brushing with margarine,
until done. Remove toothpicks.  Mix apple juice, cornstarch and
cinnamon in 1-quart saucepan.  Cook over medium heat until slightly
thickened. Spoon over chicken. Mistyped for you by Elizabeth Wood.
