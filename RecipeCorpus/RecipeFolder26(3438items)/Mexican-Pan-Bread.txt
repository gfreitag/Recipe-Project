Title: Mexican Pan Bread
Categories: Fatfree, Bread, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Cornmeal
1 t  Chile powder
1 t  Cumin
1 cn Mexe-beans
1    Chopped onion
2    Cloves garlic, chopped
1    Egg replacer
Preheat oven to 350F Mix everything, pour in a Pam'ed iron skillet,
bake 10 minutes.

Salud!
