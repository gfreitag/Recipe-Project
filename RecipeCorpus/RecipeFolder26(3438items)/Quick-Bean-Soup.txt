Title: Quick Bean Soup
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Vegetable, Soup/stew, Beans
Yield: 4 servings
-drained and rinsed
15 oz Can chick peas, drained and
-rinsed
14 1/2 oz Can vegetable broth
2 ts Chili powder
2 ts Oil
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 md Green or yellow bell
-pepper, chopped
1 cl Garlic, minced
15 1/2 oz Can great northern beans,
Heat oil in large saucepan or Dutch oven over medium heat until hot.
Add onion, bell pepper and garlic; cook and stir 5 minutes or until
onion is tender. Add remaining ingredients; mix well. Bring to a
boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 15 minutes or until thoroughly
heated.

Per serving: 230 cal., 10g pro., 34g carb., 10g fiber., 5g fat, 0mg
chol., 780mg sod., 270mg pot., 2 starch, 1 lean meat and 1/2 fat
exchanges.
