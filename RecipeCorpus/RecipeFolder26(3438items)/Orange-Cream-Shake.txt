Title: Orange Cream Shake
Categories: Diabetic, Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 ts Orange extract;
1 c  Ice; crushed
1/4 c  Frozen whipped topping;
-thawed
3/4 c  Pineapple-orange juice;
2 tb Instant non-fat dry milk;
Sugar substitute equivalent
-1 tb sugar
In blender container, combine all ingredients.  Cover; blend until all
slush consistency.  Serve immediately.  2 (1 cup) servings. Food
Exchange per serving: 1 FRUIT EXCHANGE; CAL: 80; PRO: 2g; CAR: 16g;
FAT: 1g; CHO: 1mg; SOD: 30mg; POT: 230mg; Source: Fast and Healthy
Magazine, Jan/Feb 1995. Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion
and her Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On   01-06-95
