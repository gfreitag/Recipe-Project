Title: Hamburger or Hot Dog Relish
Categories: Can/preserv, Relish, Vegetable, Hot dogs, Burgers
Yield: 1 servings
6 c  Sugar
1 T  Celery seed
2 T  Mustard seed
1 1/2 t  Tumeric
4 c  Cider vinegar
2 c  Water
4 c  Chopped onions
1 md Cabbage chopped
4 c  Green tomatoes, chopped
12 ea Green peppers, chopped
6 ea Red peppers, chopped
1/2 c  Salt
Vegetables can be chopped by hand, or in food processor. Sprinkle
salt over the chopped vegetables, and let stand overnight.  In
morning, drain and rinse until water runs clear.  Bring remaining
ingredients to a boil, and pour over vegetables.  Bring back to boil
again, and simmer for 3-5 minutes. Pack into clean, sterilized jars
and seal.  Allow to 'cure' for about a week before using. Origin:
Sharon Stevens. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
