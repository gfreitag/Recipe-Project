Title: Potatoes In Sour Cream
Categories: Polish, Potato, European, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
-temperature
1 tb Dill, fresh; chopped -
(to 2 tablespoons)
Salt and pepper; to taste
2 lb Potatoes, (about 6 med.);
-peeled and sliced
2 md Onions; peeled and chopped
3 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Sour cream; at room
Recipe by: The Eastern European Cookbook-ISBN 0-486-23562-9

Parboil potatoes for 5 mins. Drain and slice. In a medium skillet,
saute onions in butter until tender. Add sliced potatoes, sour cream,
dill, salt and pepper and cook slowly, covered, until tender, about
20 mins. Serves 4 to 6.

NOTES : The Poles consume great quantities of potatoes, which are
prepared
in interesting dishes. This is a typical one.
