Title: Sandwich Garlic Bread
Categories: Bread, Herbs/spice, Sandwich
Yield: 2 servings
Summer savory
Butter or Margerine
Bread
Garlic powder
Onion salt
Sweet Basil
Parsley
Melt butter in microwave on low heat or in a pan. Mix in seasonings to
taste and spread on the bread. Place in a preheated Sandwich maker
for 2 to 3 min or till done to desired brownness.

Great with spaghetti and lasagne.
