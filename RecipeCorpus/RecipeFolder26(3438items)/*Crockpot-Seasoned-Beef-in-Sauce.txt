Title: *Crockpot Seasoned Beef in Sauce
Categories: Crockpot, Beef, Salsa/sauce, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Curry powder
1 ts Molasses
1/2 ts Ginger
1 c  Water
1 c  Sour cream
2 T  Horseradish sauce
2 lb Round steak
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ea Medium onions, sliced
1 T  Worchestershire sauce
Cut round steak into cubes.  Coat with mixture of flour, salt and
pepper. Brown cubes in small amount of vegetable or peanut oil. Put
into a crockpot.  Add onions, Worch. Sauce, curry, molasses, ginger
and water. Slow cook 6 hours. Just before serving add sour cream
mixed with horseradish.

I usually serve this over boiled brown rice, with peas and a cottage
cheese pear salad.
