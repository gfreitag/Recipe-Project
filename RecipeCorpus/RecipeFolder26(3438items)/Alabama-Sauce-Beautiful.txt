Title: Alabama Sauce Beautiful
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Paprika
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Brown sugar, packed firmly -
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
2 tb Cider vinegar
Recipe by: Fannie Flagg's Original Whistle Stop Cafe Cookbook Combine
all ingredients in a saucepan; bring to boil, stirring constantly
until sugar melts. Boil, uncovered, until reduced to 1 1/3 cups.

NOTES : FOR CHICKEN AND PORK

From:  Dan Klepach
