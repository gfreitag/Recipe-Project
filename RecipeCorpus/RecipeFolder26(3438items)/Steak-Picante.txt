Title: Steak Picante
Categories: Beef, Main dish, Chili, Mushroom
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Tomato sauce
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sliced ripe olives
1 1/2 lb Thick round steak
1 1/2 c  Fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 ts Chili powder
Saute mushrooms, onions, and green pepper in oil for 5 minutes.  Add
chili powder, tomato sauce, salt and olives.  Place steak in shallow
baking dish and pour sauce over steak.  Cover and bake at 350 F for 1
1/2 hours or until tender.

From Loren Martin, Big Cabin Okla.
