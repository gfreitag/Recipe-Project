Title: Hamburger Cheese Dip
Categories: Dip, Ground beef, Cheese/eggs, Sausage, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 lb Velveeta cheese
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 lb Ground beef
1 lb Jimmy Dean Sausage
Brown sausage and beef, drain.  Add cheese and soup.  Heat on low
until cheese is melted. Source:Saadia
