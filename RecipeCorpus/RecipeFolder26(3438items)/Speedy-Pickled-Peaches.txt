Title: Speedy Pickled Peaches
Categories: Mom's, Fruit, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
-cup granulated sugar
From peaches
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 ea 3" stick cinnamon
1 ea No. 2 1/2 can peach
Halves, drained
Whole cloves
1 c  Peach syrup drained   1/2
Week or so ahead: Stud each peach half with 3 or 4 cloves. Simmer
with rest of ingredients 3 or 4 min. Cool; then store in
refrigerator. Serve with poultry, meat, or fish. Nice too to serve as
dessert, with vanilla ice cream.
