Title: Gingerbread Waffles 2
Categories: Breakfast, Waffles, Herbs/spice
Yield: 6 waffles
1 1/2 ts Baking powder;
1 ts Cinnamon;
1 ts Ground ginger;
1/4 ts Allspice;
2    Egg white;
1/4 c  Margarine; at room temp.
1/4 c  Honey;
1/2 c  Molasses;
1/4 c  Fat-free egg substitute;
2 c  Sifted unbleached flour;
Preheat a waffle iron.  In a large bowl, beat the margarine, honey and
molasses until well-blended.  Beat in the egg substitute and milk. In
a medium bowl, whish the flour, baking powder, cinnamon, ginger and
allspice. Stir the dry ingredients into the mixture just enough to
moisten all ingredients.  In a clean, dry medium bowl, beat the egg
whites until stiff. Gently fold the whites into the batter.  Bake in
a waffle iron until lightly browned, about 5 to 7 minutes.  Makes: 6
waffles. Per Waffle. 343 cal; 7.8 g fat; 3 mg cho; 200 mg sodium;
