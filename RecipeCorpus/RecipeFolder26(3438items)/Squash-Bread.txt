Title: Squash Bread
Categories: Bread, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 1 loaf
1/2 ts Nutmeg, ground
1 c  Squash, butternut or acorn;
-cooked, mashed
2    Eggs; beaten
1/4 c  Margarine; melted
1 1/2 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Soda
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon, ground
Combine first 7 ingredients; stir lightly, and set aside.

Combine squash, eggs, and margarine; mix well.  Add to flour mixture,
stirring only until blended.  Pour into a greased and floured 9x5x3"
loafpan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 50 to 60 minutes or until bread
tests done.
