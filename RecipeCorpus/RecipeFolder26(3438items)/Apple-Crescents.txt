Title: Apple Crescents
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 pk Crescent rolls
2 c  Apples; peeled, chopped
3/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Flour
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
2 tb Hot water
1/2 c  Confectioner's sugar
Recipe by: Sue Klapper Preparation Time: 0:25 In bowl combine apples,
sugar, flour and cinnamon.  Separate crescent rolls into 16 triangles.
Spoon mixture into center of each triangl well blended. Drizzle over
warm crescents.  Source: Favorite Apple Recipes
