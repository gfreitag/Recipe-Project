Title: Quick Sourdough Bread *M*
Categories: Bread, Sourdough, Breadmaker
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 tb Water
1/4 ts Ginger
1 ts Poppy seed
1/2 c  Water
2 ts Yeast
2 1/4 c  Bread Flour
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
1 tb Wheat Germ
1 tb Vinegar
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1/2    Egg white
Load as directed for your bread machine.
