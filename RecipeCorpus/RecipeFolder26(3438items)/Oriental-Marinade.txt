Title: Oriental Marinade
Categories: Marinade, Herbs/spice, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Sugar
1/4 t  Ground ginger
Garlic powder to taste
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Water
2 T  Vinegar
1 T  Vegetable oil
Combine and marinate for 3-4 hours, preferrably in fridge. Origin:
Tupperware recipe insert. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
