Title: Strawberry Bread 1
Categories: Bread, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 16 servings
1 3/4 c  Sifted flour
2 t  Baking powder
3 ea Egg whites
1/3 c  Oil
2/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Mashed strawberries
Beat oil and 1/3 cup sugar.  Add strawberries and mix well.  Sift
flour and baking powder and add to batter and beat.  In a separate
bowl, beat egg whites to soft peaks.  Fold egg whites into batter.
Pour into lightly oiled loaf pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 55
minutes. Remove from oven and allow to stand 10 minutes, then place
on wire rack to cool.
