Title: Corn Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable, Potato
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Milk
2 c  Corn, fresh, canned whole
Kernal or frozen
1 ts Salt
Dash paprika
3 sl Salt pork; cubed
1 lg Onion; sliced
4 lg Potatoes; sliced
2 c  Water
6 lg Soda crackers
Recipe by: The American Heritage Cookbook - ISBN 0-8281-04-03-4 Fry
salt pork in a saucepan until crisp and lightly browned. Stir in
onion and cook until golden, then add potatoes and water. Continue
cooking until potatoes are tender. Crumble soda crackers into a bowl,
pour in milk, and soak. Add to the cooked potatoes, then add corn,
salt, and paprika. Simmer over a low heat for 8 to 10 mins. Serves 4.

From:  Dan Klepach
