Title: Crockpot Veal Scallopini
Categories: Crockpot, Veal, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
-16-ounce can tomatoes
2 sm Onions, thinly sliced
1 1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Leaf oregano
1 sm Clove garlic, minced
2 lb Veal cubes
3/4 c  Flour mix*
1 t  Sugar
1/4 c  Oil
4 cn Sliced mushrooms**
*Add 1 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper to flour **drain mushrooms
Directions: Roll veal cubes in seasoned flour and fry in hot oil
until well browned. Put into CROCK-POT. Add all remaining
ingredients. Stir together well. Cover and cook on Low 6 to 8 hours
Serve over rice or fettucini. From Rival Crock-pot cook book, date
unknown
