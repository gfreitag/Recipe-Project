Title: Smothered Chicken No. 2
Categories: Chicken, Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
Chicken bouillon (enough to
-cover)  paprika
Sage
2 ea Whole chickens cut into
-pieces
3 c  Diced celery
2 md Diced green peppers
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
2 lb Diced mushrooms
lb Seasoning
3 c  Diced carrots
1 ea Very large diced onion
Remove the skin and fat from chickens (except from the wings) and
place in a large roasting pan.  Sprinkle the seasoning on each piece
of chicken and cover with diced vegetables (except the mushrooms).
Pour chicken bouillon over the mixture.  Liquid should come just to
the top of the vegetables. Bake at 375 F. for 2 hours, then stir
mushrooms into the mixture and bake another half hour.  Serve over
rice.
