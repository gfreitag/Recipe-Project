Title: Fried Cheese
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Egg, beaten
2/3 c  Bread crumbs
1 c  Shortening
4 sl Swiss cheese, cut 3/4 inch
-thick
Salt to taste
1/2 c  Flour
Sprinkle cheese with salt.  Dip slices first in flour, then in beaten
egg, fry quickly in hot shortening until golden brown. Origin:
Women's Circle, Savory Cooking Shared by: Sharon Stevens, May/94
