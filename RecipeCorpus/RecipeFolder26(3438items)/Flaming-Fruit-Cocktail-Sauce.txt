Title: Flaming Fruit-Cocktail Sauce
Categories: Mom's, Ice cream
Yield: 8 servings
2 T  Butter
Or margarine
1 T  Cornstarch
1/4 c  Brandy or light rum
1 ea No. 2 1/2 can fruit
Cocktail, drained
1/2 c  Granulated sugar   1/2
-teasp. grated lemon rind
In double boiler or chafing dish, heat first 5 ingredients, stirring,
until thickened. Add brandy; don't stir. Light brandy; spoon, over
and over, into flame. When flame expires (alcohol has burned off),
spoon over vanilla ice cream. Makes 8 servings.
