Title: Lima Bean Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetarian, Beans
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 x  Lemon, juice only
1/2 lb Lima beans
1 x  Garlic clove, minced
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Place beans in a saucepan with water to cover. Cook until tender,
about 1 1/2 hr. Drain. Add garlic and lemon juice and mix well. Serve
hot or cold.
