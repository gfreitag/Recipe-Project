Title: Breadless Salami Sandwich
Categories: Sausage, Appetizer, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
Carrots, julienned
Celery, julienned
1/2 lb Thinly sliced hard salami
Dijon mustard
Chopped parsley
Spread each slice of salami with a thin coating of mustard, sprinkle
with parsley. Place several strips of carrot and celery on, roll up,
arrange on platter and serve.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
