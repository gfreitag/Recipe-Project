Title: Carrot Loaf
Categories: Bread, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
1 t  Baking powder
1/4 t  Salt (optional)
1 t  Cinnamon
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Oil or margarine
1 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Shredded carrots
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 t  Baking soda
Mix together and bake at 350F for 55-60 minutes. Origin: My recipe
