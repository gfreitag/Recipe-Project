Title: Chess Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1    Box of powdered sugar
3    Eggs
8 oz Cream cheese
1    Egg
1    Box of yellow cake mix
1    Stick of butter
Mix together 1 egg, cake mix and butter.  Press into a 9 x 13 inch
buttered pan.  Mix together the powdered sugar, 3 eggs and cream
cheese; pour over the crust.  Bake at 350 degrees for about 35
minutes or until golden brown. Randy Rigg
