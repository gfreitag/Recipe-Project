Title: Melba Sauce
Categories: Mom's, Ice cream, Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 t  Cornstarch
1 T  Cold water
1 pk Thawed frozen
Raspberries
1/2 c  Currant jelly
In saucepan, mash raspberries; add jelly. Bring to boil. Add
cornstarch mixed with water; cook, stirring, until clear. Strain;
cool. Makes 6 servings. Traditionally it's served over vanilla ice
cream, which has been topped with canned peach half.
