Title: Chive Roquefort Spread
Categories: Condiment, Cheese/eggs, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/2 tb Chopped chives
1 tb Lemon juice
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 c  Cream cheese
1/2 c  Roquefort cheese; crumbled
1/2 c  Butter
Blend together and store in refrigerator.
