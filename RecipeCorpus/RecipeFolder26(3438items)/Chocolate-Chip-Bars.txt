Title: Chocolate Chip Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Snack, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 tb Flour
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
2    Square cake pans
1 c  Choc. chips (6 oz)
1/2 c  Margarine (1 stick)
1/2 c  Light brown sugar
1/2 c  White sugar
1    Egg
1 c  Flour
350 for 15 minutes

By hand - cream butter and gradually add brown sugar and white sugar.
Beat in egg and vanilla. Sift and stir in flour, salt and sugar
mixture. Spoon into 2 greased cake pans. Bake at 350 for 15 mins.
Cool in pans and then cut into bars.
