Title: Fat Free Orange Dressing For Salad
Categories: Fatfree, Fruit, Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Curry powder
1    Clove garlic
1    Ripe mango, peeled with
Fruit taken off the stone
1/2 c  Orange juice
Blend it all together and enjoy.
