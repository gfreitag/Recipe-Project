Title: Italian-Flavored Croutons
Categories: Bread, Salad, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Basil
2 tb Olive oil
1 c  Cubed bread
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Garlic powder
toss all and spread in shallow baking pan bake 250 for 20 minutes
freeze for 1 year store in pantry several weeks
