Title: Oriental Dipping Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Dip
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  White vinegar
1/4 t  Minced fresh ginger
2 T  Soy sauce
2 T  Water
Combine all ingredients and mix well.  Makes 1/4 cup.  Serves 4.
