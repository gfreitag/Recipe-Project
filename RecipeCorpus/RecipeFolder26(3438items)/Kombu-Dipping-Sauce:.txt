Title: Kombu Dipping Sauce:
Categories: Fatfree, Dip, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Sugar
1 t  Salt
1 c  Seaweed stock
1/4 c  Dark soy sauce
1/4 c  Mirin (seasoned rice wine)
Make a seaweed stock by immersing several pieces of kombu (thick kelp
leaves) in cool water, let it sit overnight. To make dipping sauce,
simmer the ingredients. Add some green onions or sesame seeds if you
like.
