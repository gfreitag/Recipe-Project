Title: Marinated Chuck Roast
Categories: Main dish, Sthrn/livng, Beef, Marinade
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Bourbon; opt.
1/2 c  Sugar, brown; firmly packed
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Meat tenderizer
3 lb Roast, boneless chuck
5 oz Soy sauce
1 1/2 c  Water
Sprinkle tenderizer on all sides of roast; pierce with a fork.

Place roast in a 13x9x2" baking pan.  Combine remaining ingredients
in a small bowl, stirring well; pour over roast.  cover and chill 12
hours or overnight.

Bake, covered, at 325 degrees for 2-1/2 hours or until meat is tender.
Serve with marinade.
