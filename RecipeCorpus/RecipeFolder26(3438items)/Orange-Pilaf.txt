Title: Orange Pilaf
Categories: Rice, Fruit, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Orange juice
1 t  Salt
1 ea Orange, peeled and cut into
-small pieces
1/4 c  Slivered almonds
6 ea Servings
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
1 c  Long-grain raw rice
In a large saucepan over medium-high heat, cook celery and green
onions in butter until tender, about 4 to 5 minutes.  Add the rice
and brown lightly, stirring frequently, for another 4 to 5 minutes.
Add orange juice, salt and 1 cup water, heat to boiling. Cover and
reduce heat, simmering the rice for 25 minutes until it is tender and
the liquid is absorbed. Gently stir in the orange pieces and almonds.
