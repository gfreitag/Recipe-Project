Title: Virginia's Devils Food Cake
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Mom's, Chocolate
Yield: 1 cake
2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Soda
Salt
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2    Or 3 eggs
1 c  Cold coffee
1/2 c  Cocoa
Cream sugar and butter.  Break eggs in and add dry ingredients
alternately with coffee.  Add vanilla and salt.  Add vinegar last.
Cook in two layers and ice with chocolate icing. (no temp setting
given)
