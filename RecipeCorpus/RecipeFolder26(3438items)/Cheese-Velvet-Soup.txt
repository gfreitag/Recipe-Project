Title: Cheese Velvet Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Sthrn/livng, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
4 c  Milk
1 c  Cheese, American; shredded
1/2 c  Peas, green; frozen, cooked
1/4 c  Pimiento; diced
1/4 c  Butter (or marg.)
1/4 c  Onion; minced
1/4 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Salt
Melt butter in a 3-quart Dutch oven over low heat.  Saute onion until
tender.  Blend in flour and salt; cook 1 minute, stirring constantly.
Gradually stir in milk; cook over medium heat, stirring constantly,
until thickened and bubbly.

Reduce heat; add cheese, stirring until melted.  Stir in peas and
pimiento; heat soup thoroughly (do not boil).
