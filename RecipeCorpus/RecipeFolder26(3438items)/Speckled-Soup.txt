Categories: Soup/stew, Beans, Kids, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
Morning.
2 ea Saute garlic, onion,
-celery, and carrots in oil
-for about 10 min.
3 ea Add soaked beans and the 4
-c water or stock.
4 ea Simmer until beans are

-another 30 min
Or so, until tender.
6 ea Before serving, stir in
-lemon juice, salt and
-pepper.
