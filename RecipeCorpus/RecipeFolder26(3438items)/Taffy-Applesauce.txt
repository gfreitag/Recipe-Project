Title: Taffy Applesauce
Categories: Mom's, Ice cream, Apples
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Corn syrup
1/2 c  Water
1 T  Butter or
1/8 t  Nutmeg
1 1/2 c  Sliced, pared
Apples margarine
1 c  Brown sugar, packed   1/2
-teasp. cinnamon
Cook first 4 ingredients 20 min., or until thickened. Add butter,
cinnamon, and nutmeg. Makes 4 servings. Our vote: Serve warm over
vanilla ice cream.
