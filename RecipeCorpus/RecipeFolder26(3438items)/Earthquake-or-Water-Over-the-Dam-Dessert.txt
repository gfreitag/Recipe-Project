Title: Earthquake or Water Over the Dam Dessert
Categories: Dessert, Chocolate, Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1    Stick softened margarine
1 lb Powered sugar
8 oz Cream cheese
Coconut
1 c  Chopped nuts
1    German chocolate cake mix
(2 layer type)
Cover the bottom of a 9" x 13" x 3" baking dish with coconut. Cover
the coconut with nuts. Prepare cake mix per package directions. Pour
over nuts. Combine margarine, powered sugar and cream cheese and beat
until smooth. Spread over cake batter. DO NOT STIR! Bake 1 hour at
350 degrees F. Turn out onto tray (coconut should be on top). From:
