Title: Oatmeal Bread Abm
Categories: Breadmaker, Nuts/grains, Bread
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 tb Salt
2 tb Oil
1    Eggs
1 1/4 c  Water; warm
1/2 tb Yeast
1 c  Oats
3 c  Flour, bread
1 tb Sugar
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order. Set "baking control" at 11 o'clock. Select "white bread" and
push Start.

In hot, humid weather, use 1/8 c less water.

Tested in DAK 1-1/2 lb R2D2 bread machine.  Sylvia's comment: perfect!
