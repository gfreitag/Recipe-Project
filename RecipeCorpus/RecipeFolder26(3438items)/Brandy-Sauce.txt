Title: Brandy Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Sugar
ds Salt
3 tb Brandy
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Heavy cream
2    Eggs; separated
Recipe by: The American Heritage Cookbook - ISBN 0-8281-04-03-4 Scald
milk and cream together in the top of a double boiler. Beat egg yolks
until light in color, then beat in sugar, a little at a time, and
salt. Add milk-cream mixture gradually, beating constantly. Pour back
into double boiler and cook over boiling water until sauce thickens
ehough to coat a wooden spoon. Remove from heat, stir in brandy, then
pour over stiffly beaten egg whites, beating vigorously. Cool before
serving.

From:  Dan Klepach
