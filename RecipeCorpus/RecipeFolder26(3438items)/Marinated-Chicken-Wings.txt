Title: Marinated Chicken Wings
Categories: Poultry, Chicken, Marinade, Main dish, Appetizer
Yield: 3 servings
Bread or cracker crumbs
24    Chicken wing pieces
1 ts Bell's Poultry Seasoning
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
1/2 ts Fennel, ground
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Parsley, dried
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 ts Sugar
2 ts Dry mustard
MAKE MARINADE: Blend oil and vinegar. Add sugar, mustard, and fennel.
Add lemon juice, parsley, salt and pepper to taste. [This keeps very
well in refrig, so you may want to make extra and keep it] Marinate
wing pieces for about 1/2 hour; drain. Roll in crumbs and place on
oiled baking sheet. Sprinkle with seasoning, salt and pepper. Bake in
375 degree oven for 35-45 minutes until wings are light brown and
tender. Serve warm or cold. Submitted By NICK LA ROCCA   On   12-19-94
