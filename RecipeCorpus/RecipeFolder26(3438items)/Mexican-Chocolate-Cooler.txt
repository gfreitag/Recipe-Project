Title: Mexican Chocolate Cooler
Categories: Chocolate, Beverage, Kids, Tex/mexican
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM-------------------FROM: ARIZONA COOKBOOK------------------------
1 t  Cinnamon
2 ea Qts chocolate milk
1 1/4 c  Water
1 t  Sugar
Combine 1/2 cup water, sugar, and cinnamon. Boil 1 minute. Add
remaining 3/4 cup water. Pour into 12-cup muffin pan and freeze. To
servie: Place cinnamon ice cubes in cups filled with chocolate milk.
