Title: Irish's Cream Liqueur 2
Categories: Beverage, Alcohol, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 ts Lemon ext
1/4 ts Instant coffee
1 3/4 c  Irish whiskey
2    Eggs
1 1/3 c  Evaporated milk
1/2 ts Chocolate syrup
1 ts Vanilla
Place all ingredients in a blender; blend well.  Bottle and let
mellow in frig at least one week before serving.  We found this best
after 1 to 2 weeks.  Store in frig.  liqueur may be served at room
temp by removing from frig an hour or two before serving.

Use within 6 months.

From The Art of Making and Cooking with Liqueur
