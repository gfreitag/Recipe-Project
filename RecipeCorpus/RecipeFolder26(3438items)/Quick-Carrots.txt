Title: Quick Carrots
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  Water
1/4 t  Salt
Chopped fresh parsley
2 c  Fresh or frozen sliced
-carrots
1 T  Butter or margarine
2 T  Sliced green onions
In a saucepan, combine first 5 ingredients. Cover and simmer for 8-10
minutes or until carrots are crisp tender. Sprinkle with parsley.
Serves 4 SOURCE:*Taste of Home Magazine, December/January 1995.
