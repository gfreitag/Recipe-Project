Title: Louisiana Seafood Gumbo
Categories: Main dish, Cajun/creol, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 md Clove garlic minced
46 cn Mixed vegetable juice
1/2 lb Medium shrimp (15-20)
1/4 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Diced green pepper
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1/2 t  Crushed thyme leaves
2 c  Okra in 1 1/2" pieces
1/4 c  Butter/margarine
1 c  Chopped onion
1 md Bay leaf
Shell and devein shrimp, leaving last segment and tail in place. Set
aside. In 6 quart saucepan over medium heat, in hit butter, cook
flour until lightly browned, about 3 minutes, stirring constantly.
Stir in onion, green pepper, bay leaf, garlic and thyme; cook until
onion and green pepper are tender, stirring often. Gradually stir in
vegetable juice, a little at a time, stirring until smooth after each
addition. Add okra. Heat to boiling; reduce heat to low. Cover;
simmer 30 minutes. Add fish, shrimp and hot pepper sauce. Simmer 10
minutes until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork and shrimp
are pink, stirring gently now and then. Discard bay leaf. Serve in
soup bowls; top with a scoop of rice.
