Title: Dehydrator Corn Apple Chews
Categories: Can/preserv, Vegetable, Apples
Yield: 1 servings
-another to believe it!
So great you have to have
MMMMM-----------------------SOAK OVERNIGHT----------------------------
1 lg Apple, cored and chopped
-(with skin)
1/2 c  Raisins
1 c  Dried sweet corn in 1 cup
-water
Put soaked corn and water
-into blender with
Blend, adding water s required to keep bladesmoving, until quite
smooth. Spread, 1/4 inch thick and dry as for fruit leather.  Do not
overdry as this is a chewy wafer. Origin: Dry It - You'll Like It!
circa 1973. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
