Title: Onion Pie
Categories: Vegetable, Pie/tart, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 c  Minced fresh parsley
6-8 Medium Onions. thinly
-sliced
2 T  Cooking oil
6 ea Eggs
In a large skillet, saute onions in oil until soft, but not browned;
drain well. In a mixing bowl, beat eggs. Add bread crumbs, Parmesan
cheese, parsley and onions; mix well. Placed in a greased 10 inch pie
pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes or until a knife inserted
near the center comes out clean. Serves 6-8. SOURCE:* Reminisce
Magazine Nov./Dec. 1994 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 12/94
