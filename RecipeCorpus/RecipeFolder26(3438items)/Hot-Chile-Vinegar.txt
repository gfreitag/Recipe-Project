Title: Hot Chile Vinegar
Categories: Condiment, Tex/mexican, Chilies
Yield: 1 servings
1 qt Distilled white vinegar
1 lb Hot chiles, any variety
-fresh green or red
Combine ingredients in a pan, bring to a boil, lower heat, and simmer
for 5 minutes.  Pack in clean, sterilized jars and let stand in a
cool, dark place for a week.  Taste for heat and remove the chiles or
leave them in for a jar of liquid fire. The Whole Chili Pepper From
the collection of Jim Vorheis
