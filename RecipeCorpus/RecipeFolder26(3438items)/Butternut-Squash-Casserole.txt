Title: Butternut Squash Casserole
Categories: Side dish, Casserole, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Butter (or marg.); melted
2 tb Coconut, flaked
1/2 ts Ginger, ground
1/2 ts Coconut flavoring
2 c  Squash, butternut; cooked,
-and mashed
3    Eggs; beaten
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Milk
Combine all ingredients, mixing well; pour into a lightly greased
1-quart casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until set.
