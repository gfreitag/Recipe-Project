Title: Sharon's Chow Mein
Categories: Asian, Main dish, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
-drained
1 t  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
Sesame oil
Leftover chicken or beef,
-slice in thin strips
2 ea Green onions, chopped
Soya sauce, to taste
1/2 ea Head cabbage, sliced very
-thin
1 lg Onion, sliced in thin strips
4 ea Stalks celery, cut thinly
-on diagonal
Mushrooms, sliced
1 pk Noodles (dry steamed)
Bean sprouts, rinsed and
In large Dutch oven, saute the onion, celery and cabbage with the tsp
sugar until just shiny.  Add mushrooms.  Add the bean sprouts, dry
noodles and 1/2 cup water.  Cover pot, and lower heat to lowest
setting. Allow to steam until noodles are soft, and sprouts are
tender crisp. Sprinkle sliced meat and chopped green onions on top,
and serve. Origin: Sharon Stevens. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
