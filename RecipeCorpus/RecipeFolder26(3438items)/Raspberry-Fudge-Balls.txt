Title: Raspberry Fudge Balls
Categories: Fruit, Chocolate, Cheese/eggs, Candy
Yield: 30 servings
1/4 c  Raspberry preserves;seedless
1/3 c  Almonds; finely chopped
8 oz Cream Cheese; softened
6 oz Chocolate chips; melted
3/4 c  Vanilla wafer crumbs
Combine cream cheese and chocolate.  Mix until well blended. Stir in
crumbs and preserves.  Shape into 1 inch balls and roll in chopped
almonds. Chill several hours. Makes 30 balls. Thank you Laurie Thelen.
