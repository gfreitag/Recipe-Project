Title: Chocolate Chips Peanut Butter Gems
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 60 servings
2/3 c  All-purpose flour, sifted
1/2 t  Baking soda
1/2 t  Baking powder
3 oz Small semi-sweet chocolate
-chips
3 T  Butter, softened
4 T  Creamy peanut butter
3 T  Brown sugar
2 ea Eggs, beaten
1 t  Vanilla
Beat butter, peanut butter and brown sugar together.  Add eggs and
vanilla and beat until fluffy.  Sift flour, baking soda, and baking
powder together and add to peanut butter mixture.  Mix well.  Fold in
chocolate chips. Drop by the level teaspoonful on a non stick cookie
sheet. Bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for eight minutes
