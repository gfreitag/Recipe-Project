Title: Fireside Cran-Apple Tea
Categories: Fatfree, Beverage, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 4 servings
Apple
4    Cinnamon Sticks
1/3 c  Brown Sugar
1 1/2 c  Cranberry Juice
1 1/2 c  Water
4    Herbal Tea -- bags, cinnamon
In med saucepan, bring cranberry juice and water to boil.  Add tea
bags and cinnamon sticks.  Remove from heat; cover and steep for 5
min.  Place cinnamon sticks in preheated mugs.  Add brown sugar to
tea mixture.  Stir until dissolved.  Pour hot tea into mugs.

: Home Cooking, Nov 1994
