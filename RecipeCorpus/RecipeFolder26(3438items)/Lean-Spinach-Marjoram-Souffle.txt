Title: Lean Spinach-Marjoram Souffle
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 c  Chopped spinach
2 lg Egg yolks
1/4 t  Cream of tartar
1 c  Nonfat milk
3 T  Chopped fresh marjoram
1/2 t  Pepper
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
-cornstarch. Add
1 1/2 T  Cornstarch
1 T  Instant minced onion
1/8 t  Ground nutmeg
1/4 c  Shredded parmesan cheese
6 lg Egg whites
1 ea Lightly coat interior of a
-1 3/4-quart souffle or
-other straight-sided
Baking dish with oil.
2 ea In a 2- to 3-quart pan,
-stir milk smoothly into
marjoram, onion, pepper, and nutmeg. Stir over high heat until mixture
comes to a boil. Smoothly puree in a blender with spinach, 2
tablespoons Parmesan, and egg yolks. 3. In a large bowl, beat egg
whites and cream of tartar on high speed until whites hold soft
peaks. Fold spinach mixture into whites. Scrape into oiled dish.
Sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese. 4. With a knife tip, draw a
circle on souffle top 1" from edge. Bake in a 375'F. oven until
richly browned and center jiggles only slightly when gently shaken,
about 25 minutes.
