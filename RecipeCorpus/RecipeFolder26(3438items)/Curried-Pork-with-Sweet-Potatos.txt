Title: Curried Pork with Sweet Potatos
Categories: Pork, Curries, Sweetpotato, Stir fry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 tb Salt; optional
ds Pepper
1 c  Chicken stock
2 tb Curry powder
Oil for stir-fry
2 lg Sweet potato
1 lb Pork
1    Onion
2 sl Ginger root
2 tb Sesame oil
1 tb Soy sauce
Recipe by: Jen Kuiper

Peel sweet potatos and cut into 1/2 inch slices. Cut pork in 1/2 inch
cubes. Slice onion thinly. Mince ginger root

Heat oil and deep-fry sweet potatos until golden.  Drain on paper
towels.

Heat remaining oil.  Add ginger root and onion and stir-fry a few
times. Add pork and brown lightly on all sides.

Stir in soy sauce, salt and pepper.  Then add stock and curry powder
and bring to a boil, stirring.

Add deep fried potatos and simmer, covered, until pork is done, about
30-40 minutes.
