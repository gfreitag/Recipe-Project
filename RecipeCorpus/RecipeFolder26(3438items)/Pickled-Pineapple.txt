Title: Pickled Pineapple
Categories: Mom's, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
pn Salt
6 ea To 8 whole cloves
1 ea 4" stick cinnamon
1 ea No. 2 1/2 can pineapple
Chunks or slices
3/4 c  Vinegar
1 1/4 c  Granulated sugar
Week or so ahead: Into saucepan, drain syrup from pineapple; add
vinegar, sugar, salt, cloves, and cinnamon. Simmer, uncovered, 10
min.; add pineapple chunks. Bring to boil. Cool; refrigerate. Nice
with poultry, meat, or fish, or as hors d'oeuvres.
