Title: Creme de Cacao-Cola
Categories: Diabetic, Beverage, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
Ice;
1 tb Creme de cacao;
3/4 c  Diet cola soda;
Combine all ingredients in glass, stir to blend. FOOD EXCHANGE PER
SERVING: 1/5 BREAD EXCHANGE: CAL: 15 SOURCE: The Diabetic Chocolate
