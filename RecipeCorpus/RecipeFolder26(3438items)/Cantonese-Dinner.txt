Title: Cantonese Dinner
Categories: Crockpot, Pork, Mushroom, Vegetable, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
-
1 ea 4-ounce can mushrooms,
Drained
1 ea 8-ounce can tomato sauce
1 1/2 T  Vinegar
1 ea /2 teaspoons salt
2 t  Worcestershire
1 1/2 lb Pork steak,
1/2 ea Thick, cut into strips 3
-tablespoons brown sugar
2 T  Oil
1 lg Onion, sliced
1 sm Green pepper,
Cut into strips
Brown pork strips in oil in skillet to remove excess fat. Drain on
double paper towel. Place pork strips and all remaining ingredients
into CROCK-POT. Cover and cook on Low 6 to 8 hours. (High: 3 hours).
Serve over hot fluffy rice. Serves 4. From Rival Crock-pot cook book,
date unknown
