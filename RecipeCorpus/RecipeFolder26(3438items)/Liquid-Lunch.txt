Title: Liquid Lunch
Categories: Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
8    Strawberries; hulled
2 ts Lecithin
2 ts Bee pollen
1 ts Honey
3/4 c  Plain yogurt
3/4 c  Raspberry juice
1/2 c  Crushed ice
1    Banana; peeled
- cut into pieces
Combine yogurt, raspberry juice, ice, banana, strawberries, lecithin,
bee pollen and honey in blender. Blend at high speed until thick.
Serve in chilled glasses.
