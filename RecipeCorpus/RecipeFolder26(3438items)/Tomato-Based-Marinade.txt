Title: Tomato Based Marinade
Categories: Marinade, Tomato, Fruit, Salsa/sauce, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Basil or oregano
1/4 t  Onion salt
1/4 t  Garlic powder
Black pepper, to taste
8 oz Can tomato sauce
2 T  Lemon juice
1 T  Vegetable oil
2 t  Worchestershire sauce
Combine and use with beef, chicken, or lamb.  Marinate for 3-4 hours,
preferrable in fridge. Origin: Tupperware recipe insert. Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
