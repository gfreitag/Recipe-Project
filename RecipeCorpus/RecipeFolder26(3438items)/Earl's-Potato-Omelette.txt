Title: Earl's Potato Omelette
Categories: Potato, Cheese/eggs, Main dish, European
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
Salt and pepper
2 md Onions, peeled, sliced
2 T  Lard or oleo
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
3 ea Eggs, beaten
6 md Potatoes, peeled, boiled
In a skillet, melt shortening. Saute onions until opague. Slice
potatoes while onions are cooking. Add potatoes to skillet. Salt and
pepper. Add beaten eggs and turn several times with pancake turner to
mix seasoning and eggs into onions and potatoes. Cook under medium
heat until firm.
