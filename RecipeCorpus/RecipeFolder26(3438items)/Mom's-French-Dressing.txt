Title: Mom's French Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing, French
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  White sugar
1 t  Salt (optional)
1 c  Oil
1 c  Vinegar
1 t  Dry mustard
1 sm Onion, chopped fine
Mix into a glass quart bottle with a tight fitting lid.  Store in
fridge. Shake well before using.  Will keep in fridge for a month. To
Make Russian Dressing:  Add 5-6 tsp mayonnaise and 4-5 shakes of
worchestershire sauce. Mix mayo and wooster first, adding a little
dressing at a time until smooth consistency, then add remaining
dressing until fully blended. Origin: My Mom, Shirley Whittaker.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
