Title: Popcorn Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Popcorn
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Ground popped corn
1 ts Salt
2 tb Shortening; melted
1 ea Egg, well beaten
1 1/2 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
1 tb Sugar
1 c  Milk
Mix flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Add milk, popped corn, egg,
and shortening.

Fill well oiled muffin tins 2/3 full.

Bake at 425 about 25 mins.
