Title: Old Bay Turkey Cakes
Categories: Turkey, Cake/cookie, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Mayonnaise
2 ts Old Bay seasoning
2    Scallions; finely chopped
3 T  Butter
1    Egg; lightly beaten
2 ts Pepper, black
1/2 c  Bread crumbs, italian
1 lb Turkey; cooked, minced
In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients except turkey and butter.
After ingredients are thoroughly combined, gently stir in turkey.
Form into 4 or 5 turkey cakes. Saute in butter until lightly browned
on both sides.
