Title: Serbian Cinnamon Cookies
Categories: Yugoslavian, Cake/cookie, Herbs/spice, Dessert, European
Yield: 72 servings
1/3 c  Blanched almonds slivers
1 tb Lemon juice; fresh
2 ts Lemon peel; grated
2    Eggs; beaten
1 c  Sugar
1 2/3 c  All-purpose flour; sifted
1 tb Cinnamon; ground
Recipe by: The Eastern European Cookbook-ISBN 0-486-23562-9

Combine eggs and sugar in a bowl; mix well. Add flour and cinnamon;
mix again. Stir in almonds, lemon juice and lemon peel. Mix until
ingredients are thoroughly combined. Chill in refrigerator for 1
hour. Roll out on a floured board to a thickness of 1/4 inch. Cut
into 3 x 1/2 inch strips. Arrange on a greased and floured baking
sheet. Bake in a preheated moderate (350Â°F.) oven about 15 mins, or
until cooked. Makes about 6 dozen.

From:  Dan Klepach
