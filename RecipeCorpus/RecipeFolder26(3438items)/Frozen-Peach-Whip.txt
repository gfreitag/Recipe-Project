Title: Frozen Peach Whip
Categories: Mom's, Ice cream, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Strained peaches
(baby pack)
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 c  Heavy cream, whipped
EXTRA SPECIAL USE FOR
-BABY-PACK FRUITS
1 ea Egg white
1/3 c  Granulated sugar  1
-tablesp. Iemon juice
Turn temperature control of refrigerator to coldest setting. Beat egg
white until stiff but not dry; beat in sugar and peaches. Fold orange
and lemon juice into cream, fold into peach mixture. Pour into
freezing tray; freeze until firm, stirring once. Then return
temperature control to normal setting. Makes 6 servings.
