Title: Blueberry Lemon Bread
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker, Fruit
Yield: 1 lb loaf
1/3 c  Dried Blueberries
2 ts Sugar
1 1/2 ts Lemon Peel; finely shredded
1 1/2 ts Bread Machine Yeast
1/2 c  Milk
1    Egg
1 tb Butter or Margarine
3/4 ts Salt
2 c  Bread Flour
Add ingredients to bread machine pan in order suggested by
manufacturer, addin dried blueberries with flour. Process on basic
bread cycle, light color setting, if available.
