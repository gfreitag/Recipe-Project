Title: Chicken in White Wine Sauce ^
Categories: Chicken, Main dish, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
1    Bay leaf
2 c  White wine
1/2 c  Chicken broth
4 tb Butter
3 tb Flour
1 ts Tarragon
1/4 c  Whipping cream
3 lb Chicken pieces
3 sl Onion
4    Sprigs parsley
-OR-
1 ts Dried parsley
1/8 ts Nutmeg
6    Peppercorns
Preheat oven to 350. Place chicken pieces in casserole dish with
onion, parlsey, nutmeg, peppercorns and bay leaf. Add wine and broth.
Bake 35-45 minutes, or until chicken is tender. Transfer chicken
pieces to serving dish and keep warm. Boil liquid left in casserole
for a few minutes until slightly reduced. Strain and measure out 2
cups of liquid. Melt butter lin saucepan. Whisk in flour, then add
the 2 cups strained liquid. Season to taste, then add tarragon and
cream. Spoon sauce over chicken. Serve with rice or mashed potatoes.
