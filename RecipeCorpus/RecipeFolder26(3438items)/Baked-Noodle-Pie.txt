Title: Baked Noodle Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pasta, Sausage, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1/2 c  Cottage cheese, drained
-1/2 cup shredded cheddar
-cheese
Snipped parsley
8 oz Bulk pork sausage
5 oz Noodles
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
-1/4 cup fine dry bread
-crumbs
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 ea Beaten egg
1 md Cooking apple, chopped
2 ea Eggs
In a skillet cook the pork sausage and the chopped apple till sausage
is brown; drain off fat.  Set aside.  Cook the noodles in boiling
salted water till al dente.  Immediately drain in a colander. Rinse
with cold water; drain.  Combine the 2 eggs, the 1/2 cup shredded
cheddar cheese, dry bread crumbs, and chopped onion.  Pour the egg
mixture over the noodles; toss to coat. Turn mixture into a greased 9
inch quiche dish or pie plate. Combine the 1 beaten egg, cream style
cottage cheese, and 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese; stir in the
sausage and apple mixture. Spoon mixture over the noodles in the
quiche dish or pie plate.  Bake in a 350F oven for 20-25 minutes or
till heated through.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving. Sprinkle
with parsley, and cut into wedges to serve. Makes 6 main dish
servings.
