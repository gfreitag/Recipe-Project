Title: Mary Lena's Eagle Brand Fruit Cake
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Mom's, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 cake
1/2 c  Flour
1 cn Eagle Brand Milk
1    Jar cherries
2 c  Pecans
2 c  Dates
1 c  Coconut
Mix and cook in loaf or angel food pan lined with waxed paper.  Cook
30 min. at 300 degrees.

From:  Becci McClain's maternal grandmother's favorite recipe
collection. 1/95.
Submitted By BECCI MCCLAIN   On   01-06-95
