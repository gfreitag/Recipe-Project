Title: Pan-Fried Ropa Vieja
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Tex/mexican, Vegetable
Yield: 2 ceppa kin
1/2 md Green pepper; sliced
1 cn (4.5 oz) whole tomatoes;
1 ts Dried oregano;
1/2 c  Sliced pimentos;
Salt & pepper to taste;
1/2 lb Flank steak;
Vegetable oil spray;
1/2 md Onion; sliced
2    Carrots; thinly sliced (1 c)
2 cl Garlic; crushed
Cut meat into paper-thin slices.  Spray a medium-size nonstick
skillet with vegetable oil spray.  Saute meat 2 minutes.  Remove to a
plate. In same pan, saute onions and carrots 5 minutes.  Add garlic
and green pepper and saute 5 more minutes.  Add tomatoes and oregano.
Break up tomatoes with a spoon as they cook.  Simmer 5 minutes.
Return meat to pan. Mix well. Add pimentos, salt and pepper.  Cook 1
minute. Serve over rice. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Food
Section, Jan. 5, 1995 Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and
her Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On   01-05-95
