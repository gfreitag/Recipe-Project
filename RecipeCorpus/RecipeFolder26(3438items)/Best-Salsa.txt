Title: Best Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Appetizer, Chilies, Tomato
Yield: 1 big batch
2    Green peppers chopped
1/2 c  Apple cider vinegar
1    Small can green chopped
Chilies
2    Cans tomatoes
Large store bought
4    Cloves garlic minced
2    Medium onions chopped
Combine all and boil on high until excess liquid is gone. Makes a
large batch refrigerate extra.

My family loves this recipe, we make one batch with the mild chilies
and a second batch with hot chilies for the spicier bunch. This is
great for parties.
