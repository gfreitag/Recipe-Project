Title: Jim Bowie's Hush Puppies
Categories: Bread
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Bacon dripping
2 tb Baking powder
Milk
Peanut oil
JAY MORTON   (KJWT81A)
2 c  Cornmeal
1 c  All Purpose flour
2 tb Onions; minced
1    Eggs
Mix all ingredients with just enough milk to make a thick mixture. Wet
hands and roll mixture into 1 1/2 inch balls. Drop into deep fat and
remove when brown. Drain on absorbent paper.
