Title: Zucchini Sour Cream Casserole
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Casserole
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Saltines (or fine dry
-breadcrumbs
6 md Zucchini, about 2 lbs.; cut
1 cn Sour cream; 8 oz
2 tb Margarine
1 c  Sharp cheddar cheese;
-shredded
Recipe by: Fannie Flagg's Original Whistle Stop Cafe Cookbook Cook
zucchini in boiling salted water covered for 10 to 15 mins or until
tender. Drain well. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Combine sour cream and
margarine in a small saucepan and cook over medium-low heat until
margarine melts, stirring constantly. Remove from heat; stir in
cheese, seasoned salt, and pepper. Layer half of zucchini, sour cream
mixture, and cracker crumbs in a greased shallow 1 1/2-qt casserole;
repeat layers. Bake for 20 to 25 mins, or until hot. YIELD: 6 servings
