Title: Nero Wolfe's Green-Corn Pudding
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs, Pudding, British
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Pimiento, chopped
3 lg Eggs
4 tb Butter, melted
1 c  Milk
6    Ears very young corn
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Salt
1>. Preheat oven to 350 deg.F. Combine all ingredients in the order
given except for the eggs. 2>. Separate the eggs, beating the yolks
and adding them to the mixture. 3>. Beat the egg whites until stiff
and fold them in last. 4>. Put the mixture into a greased 1 1/2-quart
casserole and bake for 35 minutes. Variation: For chicken and corn
pudding, add 1 cup of cooked (preferably poached) chicken meat and
omit the sugar.
(Too Many Cooks) Source: The Nero Wolfe
Cookbook by Rex Stout
