Title: Rolls From Sourdough Starter
Categories: Bread, Sourdough
Yield: 15 rolls
MMMMM-------------------NORMA WRENN NPXR56B&A------------------------
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Sourdough starter
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
Combine dry ingredients, mixing well.  Stir in sourdough starter and
oil. Turn dough out on a floured surface. Knead until smooth and
elastic, about 8-10 minutes. Shape dough into 2-inch balls. Place in
greased pan. Let rise, covered, for 30-40 minuts. Bake at 425 degrees
for 15-17 minutes.

Source: Fannie Johnson, Brownwood, Abilene Reporter-News 9/17/94
