Title: "Home Made" Potato Soup
Categories: Crockpot, Potato, Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Onions, chopped
1 ea Carrot, pared and sliced
1 ea Stalk celery, sliced
Chopped chives
6 ea Potatoes, in bite size
-pieces   1 tbs parsley
-flakes
5 c  Water
1 T  Salt
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 ea 13-ounce can evaporated milk
4 ea Chicken bouillon cubes
2 ea Leeks, in bite size pieces
Pepper
1/3 c  Butter
Put all ingredients except evaporated milk and chives in CROCK-POT.
Cover and cook on Low 10 to 12 hours ( High: 3 to 4 hours). Stir in
evaporated milk during last hour. If desired, mash potatoes with
masher before serving. Serve topped with chopped chives.
