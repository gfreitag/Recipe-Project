Title: Spaghetti Quick Lunch
Categories: Fatfree, Vegetable, Pasta
Yield: 1 servings
x  Salt (spinach generally
Requires you to use a bit
More salt. I used
Quite a bit.)
6 oz Corn spaghetti cooked
2    4 Tbs. minced Parsley
2    3 Tbs  minced fresh Chives
1 pk Frozen leaf spinach
Pile into plastic lunch container. Nuke for 5 minutes. Mix. Nuke
again for a minute or two, if spinach is not cooked enough.
