Title: Norwegian Waffles
Categories: Breakfast, Waffles, European
Yield: 20 servings
1 ts Baking soda
1 tb Cinnamon
4    Eggs
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Milk
Beat eggs and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the other ingredients
gradually. Bake in hot waffle iron. Norwegian wZ  than the usual
American waf than being a breakfast food, they are served cold(like
cake) with coffee in the afternoon. This tastes great with butter,
whipped cream, jam, or fresh fruit on top and eat. Source: AOL From
