Title: Florida Cheese Puffs
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1 t  Paprika
48    Stuffed Green Olives
2 c  Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1/2 c  Softened Butter
1 c  White Flour
Blend cheese and butter, stir in flour, salt and paprika.  Mix well
Wrap 1 teaspoon of the mixture around each olive, covering it
completely. Arrange on a baking sheet and freeze. Then place in
plastic bags and return to freezer. To bake, preheat oven to 400
degrees. Place puffs on a baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes. Serve
hot from the oven. Submitted By MARLENE MANN   On   01-20-95
