Title: Swiss-Broccoli Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Sthrn/livng, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Milk
1 c  Half-and-half
1 c  Cheese, Swiss; shredded
ds Pepper
1 lb Ham hocks; or meaty ham bone
10 oz Broccoli, chopped
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Flour, all-purpose
Cook ham hocks in water for 1-1/2 hours or until meat falls off bone.
Drain, reserving 1-1/2 cups broth.  Let ham cool; dice and set aside.

Combine broccoli and reserved ham broth in a 3-quart Dutch oven; heat
to boiling.  Cover and reduce heat; simmer 5 minutes or until the
broccoli is tender.

Combine 1/4 cup water and flour, stirring well.  Gradually stir flour
mixture, milk, half-and-half, and cheese into broccoli.  Cook over
medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened.  Add diced ham and
pepper; heat thoroughly (do not boil).
