Title: Classic Fudge
Categories: Candy, Chocolate
Yield: 1 pound
Of salt
2 tb Margarine
1 ts Vanilla
6 tb Cocoa
2 tb Oil
3/4 c  Milk
2 c  Sugar
Place cocoa, oil and milk in heavy saucepan. Sir constantly over very
low heat until smooth and slightly thickened, about 5 minutes. Add
sugar and salt; stir over medium heat until sugar is dissolved and
mixture boils. Continue boiling, without stirring, till mixture
reaches 234'. Remove from heat; add butter and vanilla. DO NOT STIR.
Cool to lukewarm (110'). Beat until mixture begins to lose its gloss
and holds its shape. Pour at once into buttered 8x4-inch loaf pan.
Cool until set; cut into squares. Let stand in pan until firm. Makes
1 pound. Submitted By BARB HARKINS   On   11-10-94
