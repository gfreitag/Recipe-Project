Title: Applesauce Loaf
Categories: Bread, Apples, Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 t  Cloves
1/3 t  Allspice
1/3 c  Water
1/3 c  Walnuts
2/3 c  Raisins
1 c  Applesauce
1/3 c  Margarine
1 1/3 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
1 2/3 c  Flour
1 t  Baking soda
1 t  Salt (optional)
1/2 t  Cinnamon
Mix together in order given.  Pour into loaf pan and bake for one
hour at 350F. Origin: My recipe file Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
Jan/95.
