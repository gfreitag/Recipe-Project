Title: Hawaiian Beef
Categories: Ground beef, Fruit, Beans, Meatballs
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Onion, chopped
1 t  Salt
1 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Brown sugar
2 T  Vinegar
2 t  Dry mustard
14 cn Kidney beans, rinsed and
-drained
14 oz Can baked beans, drained
20 oz Can pineapple chunks,
-drained
I remember my Mom and I
-making this dish for an
-open house we had one
Year.... I must have rolled
-200 little meat balls...
-8-}  They were so
Good, that there wasn't
-more than a small
-container left after the
-party
Was over...
1 ea Ground beef
Combine ground beef with chopped onion and salt.  Form into balls the
size of latrge walnuts.  Brown on all sides in hot skillet.  Stir in
ketchup, brown sugar, vinegar and mustard.  Bring to a boil.  In
greased 2 quart casserole, combine beans and pineapple chunks.
Arrange meat balls on top. Pour sauce over all.  Bake in 350F oven
for 30-40 minutes. Makes 6-8 servings. Origin: My Mom, Shirley
Whittaker. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
