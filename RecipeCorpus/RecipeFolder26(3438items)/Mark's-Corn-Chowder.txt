Title: Mark's Corn Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable, Potato
Yield: 5 servings
1 1/2 c  Corn; frozen or fresh
1 1/2 c  Water
;salt and pepper
1 tb Corn oil
1 md Onion; diced
2 md Potatoes; diced
Heat oil in large saucepan. When hot, saute the diced onions 5 mins,
stirring occasionally. Add potatoes and half the corn and saute a few
more mins. Combine the remaining corn with the water, cook for 5
mins, then blend til smooth. Add the pureed corn to the sauce pan.
Season to taste. Bring to a boil, and simmer until the potatoes are
cooked. Recipe by Mark Satterly
