Title: Sweet Success Bbq Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Sthrn/livng, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Yellow mustard
3/4 c  Honey
1 t  Salt
2 oz Sour mash whiskey
1 c  Heinz Ketchup
3 T  Garlic minced
4 T  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1 T  Black pepper
Mix all ingredients together in a sauce pan and heat slowly. Stir
constantly. When mixture starts to boil remove from heat and store in
refrigerator or use immediately. * NOTE: 1/4 c of fresh squeezed
grapefruit juice or lime juice may be added if desired. ORIGIN: Don
Houston's kitchen. circa 1988
