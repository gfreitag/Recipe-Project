Title: Easy Rancho Applesauce Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tex/mexican, Apples
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 T  Cocoa
1/2 t  Nutmeg
2 t  Baking Soda
1/2 t  Salt
1 c  Raisins
1 t  Vanilla
2 c  Unbleached Flour
1 T  Corn Starch
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Cloves
1 t  Baking Powder
1 c  Nuts
1/2 c  Oil
3 c  HOT Applesauce
Sift together all the dry ingredients, add nuts and raisins.  Stir in
the oil, vanilla and hot applesauce.  Pour into a 9 x 13-inch pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F. for 30 to 40 minutes or until done.
