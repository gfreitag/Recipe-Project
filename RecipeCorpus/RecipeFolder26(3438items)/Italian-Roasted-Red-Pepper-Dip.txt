Title: Italian Roasted Red Pepper Dip
Categories: Dip, Low-cal/fat, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea (7 ounce) jar roasted red
-peppers, drained
1/4 c  Minced scallions
1/2 t  Dried basil leaves
63 ea Reduced fat Wheat Thins
-Snack Crackers
Makes 1-1/3 cups
1/2 c  Nonfat sour cream
-alternative
1 T  Parmesan cheese
1 ea Clove garlic
1 t  Sugar
1 t  Coarse ground black pepper
In electric blender, blend sour cream alternative, parmesan cheese,
garlic, sugar, mustard, and black pepper until smooth.  Add red
peppers, blend using on-off motion until peppers are coarsely
chopped.  Remove from blender container; stir in scallions and basil.
Chill at least 1 hour. SErve as a dip with crackers. Per serving (1
Tbsp. dip, 3 crackers) 31 calories, 1 g total fat, 0 g saturated fat,
0 mg cholesterol, 55 mg. sodium. From: Nutrition Update Shared By:
