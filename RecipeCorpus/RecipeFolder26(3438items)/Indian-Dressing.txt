Title: Indian Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Chopped cilantro
1/2 t  Prepared sweet-hot mustard
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 t  Ground coriander
Put all ingredients in a small bowl, and stir.  Let sit about 10
minutes.
