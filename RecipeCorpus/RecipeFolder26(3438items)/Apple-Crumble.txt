Title: Apple Crumble
Categories: Diabetic, Fruit, Dessert, Apples
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-----------------------APPLE CRUMBLE----------------------------
3 tb Apple juice;
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice;
3 c  Apples*; diced peeled
1/2 c  Cinnamon;
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
-sugar
3 tb Margarine; cut up
3 tb Butter cut up
1/4 c  All-purpose flour;
1/3 c  Rolled oats; quick-cooking
Brown sugar substitute
-Equivalent to 2 tb brown
Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Place apples in ungreased 9" pan.
Sprinkle with cinnamon, apple juice and lemon juice; toss gently to
coat.  In medium bowl combine flour, oats and brown sugar substitute;
mix well. With pastry blender or fork, cut in margarine until mixture
is crumbly. Sprinkle evenly over apples. Bake at 400 degrees for 15
to 20 minutes or until topping is golden brown.   If desired, serve
warm with light with whipped topping. 6 servings. Food Exchanges per
serving: 1/2 STARCH EXCHANGE; + 1 FRUIT EXCHANGE; + 1 FAT EXCHANGE;
CAL: 130; PRO: 1g; CAR: 18g; FAT: 6g; CHO: 0mg; SOD: 70mg; POT:
105mg; Source: Fast and Healthy Magazine, Jan/Feb, 1995. Brought to
