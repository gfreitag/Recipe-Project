Title: Dehydrator Granola
Categories: Can/preserv, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
-recipe, so use what you
-have on hand, and have
Fun.
This is one of our favorite
-versions.  There's
-probably nothing as
Adaptable as a granola
MMMMM------------------------MIX TOGETHER-----------------------------
1/2 c  Oil
2 t  Vanilla
1/2 c  Honey (or more, to taste)
1/2 c  Hot water
MMMMM------------------------POUR IT OVER-----------------------------
1 c  Flax seeds
1 c  Freshly grated coconut (if
-you have it)
3/4 c  Chopped walnuts
3/4 c  Chopped dates, raisins, or
-currants
4-5 cups oatmeal, in a
-large bowl, and allow to
-soak, while adding
(right on top)
1 c  Raw sunflower seeds
1 c  Sesame seeds
Mix it alltogether and spread on parchment to dry.  To speed drying,
spread thinly. Store in plastic bags or jars in refrigerator.
VARIATIONS: Change ingredients and/or amounts as you please.  Try
adding or substituting chia seeds, fresh wheat germ, almonds, cashews
or other nuts, a little brewer's yeast, chopped dried fruit,
whatever. Origin: Dry It - You'll Like It! circa 1973. Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
