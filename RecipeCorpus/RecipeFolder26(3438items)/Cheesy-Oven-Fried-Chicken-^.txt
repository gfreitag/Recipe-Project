Title: Cheesy Oven Fried Chicken ^
Categories: Chicken, Poultry, Main dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1    Egg, beaten
1/4 c  Soft white bread crumbs
1/4 c  Grated cheddar cheese
4 tb Butter
4 lg Chicken quarters
1 tb Flour
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 ts Dry mustard
Preheat oven to 400. Coat chicken in flour seasoned with salt pepper
and dry mustard. Brush chicken with beaten egg and roll in a mixture
of bread crumbs and cheese. Place in casserole dish and dot with
small pieces of butter. Bake 40 minutes or until golden brown and
tender.
