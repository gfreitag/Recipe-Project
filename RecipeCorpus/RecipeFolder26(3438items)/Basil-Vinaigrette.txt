Title: Basil Vinaigrette
Categories: Dressing, Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Dried oregano (or 1/2 tsp
-fresh)
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
1/4 t  Dried basil (or 1/2 tsp
-fresh)
Put all ingredients in a small bowl, and stir.  Let sit about 10
minutes. Tanya Heikkinen theikkinen@marvin.ag.uidaho.edu
From Fatfree Digest April-May 1994, Formatting by Sue Smith (using
MMCONV)
