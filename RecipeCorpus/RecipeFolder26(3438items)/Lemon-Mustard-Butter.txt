Title: Lemon-Mustard Butter
Categories: Condiment, Fruit, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 tb Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 c  Butter
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Grated lemon rind
Blend together and store in refrigerator.
