Title: Apple Pound Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 16 servings
2 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Apples; finely chopped
1/2 c  Raisins
1 c  Pecans; chopped
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Oil
Recipe by: Sue Klapper Preparation Time: 1:15 In mixing bowl, combine
flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg.  Wit for 1 hour and 15
minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out
