Title: Cream Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
But it is also very good
-over mashed potatoes.
This is the gravy that
-should be served with
-Chicken Fried Steak.
MMMMM----------------YIELD: ABOUT 1 CUP OF GRAVY---------------------
1/2 t  Instant Chicken Bouillon
1/2 t  Freshly Ground Black Pepper
2 T  Butter Or Regular Margarine
2 T  Unbleached Flour
1 c  Milk
Melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium-low heat and stir in
the flour until well blended and smooth.  Gradually add the milk,
bouillon, and pepper and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Reduce
the heat to low and cook 3 to 5 minutes, stirring frequently, until
the mixture is smooth and slightly thickened.

From The Redbook Magazine January 1986
