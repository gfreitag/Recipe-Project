Title: Orange Milk Sherbet
Categories: Mom's, Ice cream
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Grated orange
rind
1/8 t  Salt,
2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Fresh, frozen, or
cn Lemon juice
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
Combine first 4 ingredients; add very slowly to milk, stirring till
sugar dissolves. Add salt. Freeze in 2-qt. crank freezer until
difficult to turn, using 8 parts crushed ice to 1 part ice-cream
salt. Makes 1 1/2 qt. Note: This sherbet may curdle in the making,
but when done it's nice and creamy.
