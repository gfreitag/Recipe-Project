Title: Caraway Blue Cheese Spread
Categories: Condiment, Cheese/eggs, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Caraway seeds
White pepper to taste
1/3 c  Blue cheese, crumbled
3 oz Cream cheese
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
Blend together and store in refrigerator.
