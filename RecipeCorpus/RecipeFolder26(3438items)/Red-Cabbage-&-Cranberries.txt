Title: Red Cabbage & Cranberries
Categories: Fatfree, Fruit, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
2 T  Brown Sugar
1 T  Lemon Juice
1 t  Ground Cloves
1 md Red Cabbage
12 oz Cranberries -- fresh
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Red Wine Vinegar
Wash & finely shred cabbage.  In lg skillet, toss cabbage with
cranberries. Combine remaining ingredients in a bowl.  Pour over
cabbage. Simmer until cabbage and berries are tender.

Serves 8 Recipe By Home Cooking, Nov. 1994
