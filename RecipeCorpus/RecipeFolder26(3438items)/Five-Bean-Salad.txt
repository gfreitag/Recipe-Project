Title: Five Bean Salad
Categories: Salad, Beans
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Chick peas
1 cn Baby lima beans
1    Purple onion
1 cn Cut string beans
1 cn Wax beans
1 cn Red kidney beans
MMMMM--------------------------COMBINE-------------------------------
2/3 c  Salad oil
1 c  Vinegar
1 c  Sugar
Drain all beans and put in large bowl. Pour liquid over and mix
lightly. Cover and let stand at least 12 hours. Drain and cover with
onion rings.
