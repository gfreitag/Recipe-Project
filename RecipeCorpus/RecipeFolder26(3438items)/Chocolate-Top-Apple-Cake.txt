Title: Chocolate Top Apple Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Chocolate, Apples
Yield: 16 servings
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Milk
2 c  Apples; peeled, chopped
***topping***
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Nuts
1/2 c  Miniature chocolate chips
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2    Eggs
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
2 1/2 c  Flour
Recipe by: Sue Klapper Preparation Time: 0:50 For cake, cream together
sugars and butter.  Add eggs, beating well.  Sift over the cake. Bake
at 350 for 45 to 50 minutes. Source:  Favorite Apple R
