Title: Dehydrating Green Beans
Categories: Can/preserv, Beans, Question?
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
Choose tender, young beans and cut as desired.  Strips dry the
fastest, or into slices.  Dried bean powder nicely in a seed or
coffee mill and then can be added to broth or soups. Sprinkle over
casseroles when powdered, or use whole.

Origin: Dry It - You'll Like It!  circa 1973. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Jan/95.
