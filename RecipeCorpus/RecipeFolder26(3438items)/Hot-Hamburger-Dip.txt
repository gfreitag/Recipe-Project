Title: Hot Hamburger Dip
Categories: Dip, Beef, Tomato, Cheese/eggs, Crockpot
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Oregano
1 T  Parsley
1 T  Sugar
1 ea (4oz) can mushrooms, chopped
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 ea (8oz) can tomato sauce
1/4 c  Ketchup
1 ea (8oz) package cream cheese
1 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1 ea Clove garlic, mashed
Saute beef until brown.  Add onion and garlic, cook until tender.
Add all other ingredients and stir over low heat until cream cheese
melts. Pour into crockpot and keep warm.  Serve with corn chips or
taco chips. Origin: Women's Circle, For Cooks on the Go Shared by:
Sharon Stevens
