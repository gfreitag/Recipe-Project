Title: Barnegat Light Tilefish
Categories: Fish, Bbq/grill, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
1 lb Tilefish steaks; about 1-in
-thick
1/3 c  Lo-cal Italian dressing
Place fish in shallow baking dish.  Combine remaining ingredients and
pour over fish.  Cover and refrigerate 30 minutes, turning once.
Remove fish, reserve marinade for basting.  Place fish on a lightly
oiled grill, 4-6 inches from moderately hot coals.  Cook for 4-5
minutes. Baste, turn and cook 4-5 minutes longer or just until fish
flakes easily (Or microwave on high about 3 minutes.  Allow to stand
for 5 minutes before serving)
