Title: Jelly Jewels
Categories: Candy, Homemade, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
2 1/4 c  Flavored juice (raspberry,
-apple, etc)
5 ea Envelopes gelatin
3 c  Sugar
Bring to boil.  Boil gently for 20 minutes.  Skim.  Pour into lightly
greased pan and let set overnight in fridge.  Cut into small squares
and roll in berry sugar. This is a cheap imitation of aplets and
cotlets... 8-} Origin: My recipe file. Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
Jan/95.
