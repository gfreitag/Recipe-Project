Title: Chinese Five-Spice Powder Ii
Categories: Condiment, Chinese, Herbs/spice, Mixes
Yield: 1 /3 cup
2 T  Fennel seed
12 ea Whole star anise
2 T  Whole, black peppercorns
36 ea Whole cloves
12 ea Inches of stick cinnamon
Put spices together into a blender and pulverize into a powder. Store
in a cool place in an air-tight container. Colorado Cache Cookbook
(1978) From the collection of Jim Vorheis
