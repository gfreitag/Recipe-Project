Title: Quick Fluke
Categories: Fish, Main dish, Tomato, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
1/4 c  Melted butter
1 c  Grated Swiss cheese
2 lb Skinned fluke fillets
2 lg Tomatoes, cut in sm.
-pieces
2 T  Grated onion
Place fillets on greased baking platter and sprinkle with onion, salt
and pepper.   Cover with tomatoes.  Pour melted butter over tomatoes.
Broil about 4 inches from the heat for 10 minutes or until fish
slakes easily when tested with a fork.  Remove from the the heat and
sprinkle with cheese.  Place under heat again until cheese melts.
