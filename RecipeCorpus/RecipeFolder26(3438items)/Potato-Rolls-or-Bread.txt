Title: Potato Rolls or Bread
Categories: Bread, Potato
Yield: 48 servings
2    Eggs
1/4    To 1/2 c soft butter or oil
1 c  Lukewarm mashed potatoes
7 c  To 7 1/2 c whole wheat flour
1 tb Yeast
1 1/2 c  Lukewarm water or potato
Water
1/4 c  To 1/2 c sugar or honey
1 1/2 ts Salt
Dissolve yeast in water.  Add sugar, salt, eggs, butter, potatoes.
Stir in flour to make a pliable dough. Knead 5 to 10 minutes. Let
rise until doubled in a warm place, or cover and let rise in
refrigerator for at least 2 hours.

Form dough into about 48 balls and place in greased baking pans or
muffin tins; or make into 2 loaves. Let rise until doubled. Bake
rolls at 400F for 15 minutes; loaves at 350F for 30 to 40 minutes.
