Title: Hot Peppermint Patty
Categories: Beverage, Chocolate
Yield: 1 human
Whipped cream to taste;
1 oz Peppermint syrup;
6 oz Chocolate;
Mix syrup into warm chocolate and garnish with whipped cream. Source:
Phoenician, Phoenix. and The San Diego Union-Tribune Food Section,
