Title: Meat and Bean Surprise
Categories: Beef, Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains, Beans
Yield: 1 servings
1 md Onion, diced
1 c  Oatmeal
1/2 c  Wheat germ
1/4 c  Soya sauce
Salt and pepper to taste
2-3 lbs hamburger
2 ea Eggs
1/4 c  Sesame seeds
1 1/2 c  Fresh bean sprouts, rinsed
-and drained
Mix together and form into a meatloaf, or meat patties.  If made into
patties, cook and then can be covered with mushroom soup and served
with soup as a gravy. Origin: Sharon Stevens. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Jan/95.
