Title: Squash Stir-Fry
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
3 md Squash, yellow; thinly slicd
3    Tomatoes; cut in wedges
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Cheese; shredded
2 tb Oil, vegetable
1    Garlic clove; minced
1 lg Onion; sliced and separated
-into rings
3 md Squash, zucchini;sliced thin
Pour oil around top of hot wok, coating sides until bottom is covered
with oil.

Add garlic; stir-fry briefly.  Add onion; stir-fry 1 minute.  Stir in
zucchini and yellow squash; cook 2 to 3 minutes or until
crisp-tender. Add tomatoes, salt, and pepper; stir well.  Sprinkle
cheese over vegetables, stirring just until cheese melts.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, May, 1980. Typed for you by Nancy
