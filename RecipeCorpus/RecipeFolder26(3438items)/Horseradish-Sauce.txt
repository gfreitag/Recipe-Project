Title: Horseradish Sauce
Categories: Polish, Salsa/sauce, European
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Dill or parsley, fresh;
-minced
Salt and pepper; to taste
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
2 tb Flour
2 c  Vegetable or meat broth
1/2 c  Horseradish, prepared or
-freshly grated, drained
Recipe by: The Eastern European Cookbook-ISBN 0-486-23562-9

Heat butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour. Slowly add broth and cook,
stirring, until thickened.  Add horseradish, lemon juice, dill, salt
and pepper. cook slowly, stirring often, about 10 mins. Serve with
roast meat or fish. Makes abour 2 cups.
