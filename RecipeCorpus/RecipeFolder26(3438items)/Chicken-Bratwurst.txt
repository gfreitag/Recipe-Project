Title: Chicken Bratwurst
Categories: Sausage, Chicken, Homemade
Yield: 3 poundsgs
3/4 ts Caraway seeds, crushed
3/4 ts Dried marjoram
1 ts Fine grind white pepper
1 ts Salt, to taste
3    Feet small hog or sheep
-casings
3 lb Chicken meat, 15% fat
1/2 ts Allspice
Grind meat through the fine disk; mix with remaining ingredients and
chill in refrigerator 30 minutes. Run thru fine disk again. Prepare
casings and stuff. Refrigerate up to two days or freeze.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
