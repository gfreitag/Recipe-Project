Title: Yogurt Rye Bread (Abm) (Ovo)
Categories: Fatfree, Breadmaker, Bread
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1/4 c  Wheat gluten
1/3 c  Rye flour, dark
2 t  Yeast -- rapid-rise
2/3 c  Water -- @85-105F
2 T  Brown sugar
1 t  Salt
2/3 c  Yogurt, skim milk
Run on whole wheat cycle.

NOTES : This is a somewhat dense bread.  It approximates sourdough
rye.
