Title: Duxelle of Mushrooms
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Vegetarian, Mushroom
Yield: 2 cups
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Marsala
Salt & pepper
1 pn Garlic powder
3 c  Fresh mushrooms, diced
1 ts Margarine
1 ts Olive oil
1/2 c  Shallots, diced
1/2 c  Onions, diced
Saute mushrooms in margarine & oil over low heat for 5 minutes.  Add
remaining ingredients & cook, stirring often, until the mushrooms are
tender, about 15 to 20 minutes.

Use to fill pasta dough of your choice & cook as desired.
