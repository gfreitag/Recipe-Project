Title: Date Loaf
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Sugar
4    Eggs, separated
1 ts Vanilla
1 lb Dates
1 lb Pecans
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
Sift flour, salt and baking powder 3 times.  Dredge dates and pecans
in flour.  Mix all together, adding egg whites last.  Bake at 200
degrees for 1 hour until golden brown. Randy Rigg
