Title: Taratoor (Sesame Sauce)
Categories: Middle east, Arab states, Salsa/sauce, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 1 1/2 cups
1 c  Cold water
1/2 c  Lemon juice, fresh
1 ts Salt
3 md Garlic cloves; peeled and
;finely chopped
1 c  Tahina paste; (ground,
;hulled sesame seeds)
In a deep bowl, mash the garlic to a paste with a pestle or the
back of a large spoon.  Stir in the tahina.  then, with a whisk of
spoon, beat in 1/2 cup of the cold water, the lemon juice, and salt.
Still beating, add up to 1/2 cup more of water, 1 tablespoon at a
time, until the sauce has the consistency of thick mayonnaise and
holds its shape almost solidly in a spoon.  Taste for seasoning.
Taratoor can be served as a sauce with baked fish or fried
cauliflower, and is used as an ingredient in hummus and baba ghannooj.
