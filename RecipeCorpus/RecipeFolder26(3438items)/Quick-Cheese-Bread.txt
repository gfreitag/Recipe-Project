Title: Quick Cheese Bread
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
4 t  Baking powder
1 c  Grated sharp cheddar cheese
1 c  Milk
2 c  Flour
1 1/2 t  Salt (optional)
2 ea Eggs, beaten
1/4 c  Melted shortening
Sift together the dry ingredients.  Stir in grated cheese.  Beat the
eggs, and mix with the milk and melted shortening.  Add liquid
mixture to the dry and stir until just blended.  Do not overmix.
Pour into greased loaf pan and bake in 350F oven for 45-60 minutes or
until loaf tests clean. Origin: Sharon Stevens. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Jan/95.
