Title: Poppycock 2
Categories: Snack, Popcorn, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Corn syrup
2/3 c  White sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 qt Popped corn (1/2-3/4 c
Kernels)
2/3 c  Pecans
1/3 c  Almonds
Bring corn syrup, sugar, butter and vanilla to a boil over medium
heat, stirring constantly until boiling. Boil for 10 to 15 min,
stirring occasionally, until it turns a caramel color. Add vanilla.
Pour over popcorn and nuts. Mix well. Spread on buttered cookie sheet
to cool and then break into pieces. Source: Calgary Herald
