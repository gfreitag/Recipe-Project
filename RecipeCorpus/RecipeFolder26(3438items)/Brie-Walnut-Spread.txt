Title: Brie Walnut Spread
Categories: Condiment, Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Kirsch liqueur
1/2 c  Walnut pieces
3/4 c  Brie
1/2 c  Cream cheese
Blend cheese and Kirsch together in food processor. Stir in nuts.
Store in refrigerator.
