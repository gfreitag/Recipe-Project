Title: Honey Buns 2
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie
Yield: 10 servings
10 oz Hungry Jack Refrigerated
2 tb Margarine melted
-Big Flaky Biscuits
1/4 c  Chopped nuts
1/3 c  Honey
1/3 c  Margarine, melted
2 tb Cinnamon
Heat oven to 350 degrees. In a small bowl, combine nuts, honey and
1/3 cup melted margarine; pour into ungreased 8 x 4 or 9 x 5 loaf
pan. Separate dough into 10 biscuits. In small bowl, combine brown
sugar and cinnamon. Pat about 1 teaspoonful of mixture on one side of
each biscuit. Stand biscuits on edge slightly overlapping, in two
rows of five biscuits each in prepared pan. Drizzle with 2
tablespoons melted margarine. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30
minutes or until golden brown. Cool 3 minutes; turn onto serving
plate. Serve warm or cool. Makes 10.
