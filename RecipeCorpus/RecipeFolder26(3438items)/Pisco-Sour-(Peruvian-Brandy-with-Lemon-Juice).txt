Title: Pisco Sour (Peruvian Brandy with Lemon Juice)
Categories: So. america, Fruit, Beverage, Alcohol
Yield: 1 cocktail
4    Ice cubes
1 4oz. cocktail glass or
Tumbler, chilled
1 tb Egg white
1 1/2 ts Sugar, powdered
3 oz Pisco (Peruvian brandy)
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice; fresh
Combine the egg white and sugar in a mixing glass, and stir with a
bar spoon to dissolve the sugar.  Add the pisco, lemon juice and ice
cubes. Place a bar shaker on top of the glass and, grasping the glass
and shaker firmly together with both hands, shake them vigorously 9
or 10 times. Remove the shaker, place a strainer on top of the mixing
glass and pour the Pisco sour into a chilled cocktail glass or
tumbler.
