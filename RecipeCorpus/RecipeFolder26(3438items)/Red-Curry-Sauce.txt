Title: Red Curry Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Curries, Fish
Yield: 1 cup
1 c  Coconut milk
2 ts Fish sauce
1/2 ts Sugar
1 tb Cilantro leaves
-finely minced
1/2 ts Grated lime zest OR
1    Wild lime leaf; minced
1 tb Vegetable oil
1    Shallot; minced
1    Garlic clove;
-finely minced
1    Quarter-sized piece ginger
-peeled and finely minced
2 ts Red Curry Paste
In a wok, heat the oil over high heat. SautÂ‚ the shallot, garlic and
ginger until soft, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in the curry paste and cook,
stirring, for 1 minute. Add the coconut milk and simmer over low heat
for 5 minutes. Stir in the remaining ingredients and taste for
seasoning. Serve over grilled, poached or broiled fish. Also
delicious spooned over rice.

If prepared in advance, refrigerate the sauce until needed and reheat
just before serving.
