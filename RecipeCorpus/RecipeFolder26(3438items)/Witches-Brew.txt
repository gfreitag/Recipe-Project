Title: Witches Brew
Categories: Beverage, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 8 servings
2 T  Lemon juice
3 ea Cinnamon sticks
1 qt Apple juice
1 1/2 c  Pineapple juice, unsweetened
2 T  Honey
Mix all ingredients together in a saucepan.  Heat over low heat until
ready to serve.  Remove cinnamon sticks before serving. Source:
Children's Party Book
