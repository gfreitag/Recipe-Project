Title: Bronco Bread
Categories: Bread, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Flour
1 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Baking soda
1 c  Fresh blueberries, or
-frozen or canned,
Well-drained
1 ea Loaf
2 T  Softened butter
1/4 c  Hot water
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 1/2 T  Freshly grated orange rind
1 ea Egg
1 c  Sugar
Combine butter, water, orange juice and rind in a bowl.  Add egg and
mix well.  Add dry ingredients and fold in blueberries by hand. Bake
at 325 F in greased 9x5-inch baking pan for 1 hour and 10 minutes.
Cool on rack. Colorado Cache Cookbook (1978) From the collection of
Jim Vorheis
