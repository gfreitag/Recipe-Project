Title: Tarragon-Salmon Spread
Categories: Condiment, Fish, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Dried tarragon
1/8 c  Vodka
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 c  Canned salmon
1/4 c  Smoked salmon
1/2 c  Butter
Blend together and store in refrigerator.
