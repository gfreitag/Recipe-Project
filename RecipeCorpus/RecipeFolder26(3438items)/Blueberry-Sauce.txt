Title: Blueberry Sauce
Categories: Mom's, Ice cream, Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Thawed frozen   Pinch salt
Blueberries 2 tablesp. water
1 T  Lemon juice
1 t  Grated lemon rind
1 T  Butter or   2 tablesp.
-granulated sugar
Margarine
1 1/2 t  Flour
In saucepan, melt butter. Blend in flour, then remaining ingredients.
Cook, stirring, until thickened. Makes 4 servings. Royal served warm
over vanilla ice cream.
