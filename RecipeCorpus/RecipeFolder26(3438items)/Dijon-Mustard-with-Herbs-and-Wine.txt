Title: Dijon Mustard with Herbs and Wine
Categories: Condiment, Salsa/sauce, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Tarragon
1/4 c  Red wine
1 c  Dijon mustard
1/2 ts Basil
Mix all ingredients.  Refrigerate overnight to blend flavours before
using. Store in refrigerator.
