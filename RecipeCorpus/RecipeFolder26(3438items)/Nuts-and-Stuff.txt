Title: Nuts and Stuff
Categories: Snack, Nuts/grains
Yield: 14 servings
1 c  Bite-size wheat squares
-cereal
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
3 c  Mixed nuts or peanuts
2 c  Cheese-flavored fish-shaped
-crackers
1 c  Bite-size corn squares
-cereal
In an ungreased 15 1/2x10 1/2x2-inch baking pan combine nuts,
crackers, and cereal.  Set aside.

In a small saucepan melt butter or margarine.  Add Worcestershire
sauce. Drizzle over cereal mixture and toss gently to coat cereal
evenly.

Bake in a 300F oven about 40 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes.

Cool cereal mixture in the pan, stirring occasionally.  Store mixture
in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.  Makes 14 (1/2-cup)
servings.
