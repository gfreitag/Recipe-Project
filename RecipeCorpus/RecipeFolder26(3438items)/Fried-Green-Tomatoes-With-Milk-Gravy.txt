Title: Fried Green Tomatoes With Milk Gravy
Categories: Side dish, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
All-purpose flour
Milk
Salt
Pepper
1/4 c  Bacon drippings
4    Green tomatoes; sliced 1/2"
2    Eggs; beaten
Fine dry breadcrumbs
Recipe by: Fannie Flagg's Original Whistle Stop Cafe Cookbook Heat
bacon drippings in a large heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Dip
tomatoes in eggs, then coat with breadcrumbs. Fry in bacon drippings
until golden brown on both sides, adjusting heat to fry slowly.
Transfer tomatoes to a serving platter, using a slotted spatula.
Measure drippings remaining in skillet; return to skillet. For each
Tbs of drippings, stir in 1 Tbs flour until smooth. Cook over low
heat for 1 min, stirring constantly. For each Tbs flour you have
used, stir in 1 cup milk, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper until
smooth. Cook until thickened, stirring constantly; pour over
tomatoes. YIELD: 4 to 6 servings

From:  Dan Klepach
