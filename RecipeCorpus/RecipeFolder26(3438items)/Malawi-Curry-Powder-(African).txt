Title: Malawi Curry Powder (African)
Categories: Herbs/spice, Curries, African
Yield: 3 /4 cup
2 ts Black mustard seeds
1 tb Cumin seeds
1 tb Turmeric
10    Cloves
2 ts Ground cinnamon
10 sm Dried hot red chilies
-piquins or santakas
-seeds and stems removed
3 tb Coriander seeds
1 tb Black peppercorns
3 tb Poppy seeds
Combine all ingredients in a spice mill and grind to a fine powder.
Store in an airtight container. The hottest curry powder found in
Africa.
