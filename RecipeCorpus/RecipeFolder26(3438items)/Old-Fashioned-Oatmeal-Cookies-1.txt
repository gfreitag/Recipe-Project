Title: Old Fashioned Oatmeal Cookies 1
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 48 servings
2 c  Raisins
1 c  Chopped nuts
1/4 c  Dark brown sugar
1 ea Pk Spice cake mix
2 c  Quick oatmeal
2 ea Eggs
3/4 c  Cooking oil
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine all the ingredients and mix
until well blended.  Drop from teaspoon onto an ungreased cookie
sheet. Bake about 12 minutes.  Cool about 1 minute, then remove to a
rack. Makes about 4 dozens 2-1/2 inch cookies. Note: These soften as
they age.
