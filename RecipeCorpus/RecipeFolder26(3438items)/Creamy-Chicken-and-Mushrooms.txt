Title: Creamy Chicken and Mushrooms
Categories: Chicken, Mushroom, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 c  Of milk
1/8 t  Pepper
2 T  Margarine
1 1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms
1/8 t  Garlic powder
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 cn Cream of Mushroom soup
2 T  Dry sherry  (optional)
4 ea Chicken breast halves
1 sm Onion, sliced
In skillet, in 1 tbs  hot margarine, cook chicken 10 min. or until
browned on both sides.  Remove.  In remaining 1 tbs  hot margarine,
cook mushrooms and onion with garlic powder until tender and liquid
is evaporated, stirring often.   Add remaining ingred.   Heat to
boiling. Return chicken to skillet.  Cover.  Cook over low heat 5
min. or until chicken is no longer pink, stirring occasionally.
Serves 4 Prep time: 10 min. Cooking time:  25 min.
