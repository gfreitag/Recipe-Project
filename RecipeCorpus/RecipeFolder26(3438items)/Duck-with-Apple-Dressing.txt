Title: Duck with Apple Dressing
Categories: Game, Apples, Dressing, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Raisens
1/2 c  Cashews [chopped]
1 sl Bacon [cut into halves]
1    (3«-4 lb) duck
4 sl Toasted bread crumbs
4 tb Butter [melted]
1/4 c  Water
3 lg Apples [peeled, cored]
[& diced fine]
Rinse the duck and pat dry inside and out...
1)

Combine the remainder of the ingredients EXCEPT the bacon,
2)
mixing well. Spoon the dressing into all cavities of the duck then
place in a baking dish... 3) Place the bacon strips over the top and
bake in a 350ø oven `til the duck is tender... 4) Serve with wild
rice and favorite veggie...
