Title: Spicy Chicken Wings
Categories: Poultry, Bbq/grill, Chicken, Appetizer
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes, crushed
12    Chicken wings, tips removed
-(2 pounds)
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Chili powder
1 ts Instant minced onion flakes
1 ts Garlic powder
Preheat oven to 375 F.

Combine oil and chili powder on a large jelly-roll pan.  Set in
preheated oven for 5 minutes.

In a large bowl, combine onion flakes, garlic powder, salt and red
pepper flakes.  Add chicken wings and toss to coat with the spices.

Remove pan from the oven.  Place chicken wings in single layer; turn
to coat with chili oil using tongs or a wide spatula.  Bake for 1
hour, or until chicken wings are tender, turning once or twice.

Makes 4 servings.
