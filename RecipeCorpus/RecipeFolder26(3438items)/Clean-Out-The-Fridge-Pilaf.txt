Title: Clean-Out-The-Fridge Pilaf
Categories: Main dish, Fatfree
Yield: 4 servings
1/2    A red pepper, diced
1    Onion, chopped
2    Plum tomatos, chopped
-salt to taste
2 c  Brown basmati rice
4 c  Water
1/3 lb Mushrooms, chopped
1/2    A green pepper, diced
Throw everything in a pot, bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 45
minutes until the rice is cooked.

Happy eating, Kirstin Kirstin Reade Wilcox <krw3@columbia.edu>
