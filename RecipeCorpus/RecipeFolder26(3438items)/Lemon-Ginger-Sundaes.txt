Title: Lemon Ginger Sundaes
Categories: Dessert, Low-cal/fat, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM------------------------LEMON SAUCE-----------------------------
1 ts Grated lemon peel
1/2 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Margarine or butter
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Cornstarch
1/4 c  Water
MMMMM--------------------------SUNDAES-------------------------------
3 tb Finely crushed gingersnap
-cookies
1 c  Frozen fat free vanilla
-yogurt
In small saucepan, combine sugar and cornstarch; mix well. Stir in
water until well blended. Bring to a boil. Cook, stirring constantly,
until thickened. Remove from heat, stir in lemon peel, lemon juice,
and margarine. Cool slightly. Scoop yogurt into 2 individual dessert
dishes. Top each with lemon sauce; sprinkle with cookie crumbs. Serve
immediately.

Per serving: 200 cal., 3g pro., 42g carb., 0g fiber, 2g fat, 0mg
chol., 140mg sod., 40mg pot., 1 starch, 1 1/2 fruit and 1/2 fat
exchanges.
