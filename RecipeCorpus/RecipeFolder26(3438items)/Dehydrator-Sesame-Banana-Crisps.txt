Title: Dehydrator Sesame Banana Crisps
Categories: Can/preserv, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
Blend very ripe banana with a small wedge of lemon.  Mix together
equal parts of pureed banana and sesame seeds.  Spread quite thin and
dry. When dry, turn and finish drying until crisp.

VARIATIONS:  Add a touch of coriander. Use orange peel instead of
lemon. Add pumpkin or sunflower seeds for taste and texture
variation. Origin: Dry It - You'll Like It!  circa 1973. Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
