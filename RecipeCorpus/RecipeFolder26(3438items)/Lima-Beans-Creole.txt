Title: Lima Beans Creole
Categories: Beans, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
- 1/2 tsp Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
3 1/2 c  Lima beans, fresh
1/4 c  Onion; finley chopped
16 oz Tomatoes, whole; undrained
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
2 T  Green pepper; chopped
6 ea Sl Bacon
Cook beans in boiling salted water in a Dutch oven until tender (20
to 30 minutes); drain.  Return beans to Dutch oven, and set aside.
Cook bacon until crisp.  Remove from skillet, reserving2 tablespoons
drippings; crumble bacon, and set aside.  Saute onion and green
pepper in reserved drippings until tender. Stir onion mixture, bacon
and remaining ingredients into beans; cover and simmer 15 minutes.
