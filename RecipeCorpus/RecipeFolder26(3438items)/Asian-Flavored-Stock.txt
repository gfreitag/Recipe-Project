Title: Asian-Flavored Stock
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetarian, Vegetable, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------VEGGIE LIFE; JAN 1995------------------------
1    Serrano pepper; chopped
7 ts Soy sauce
1 tb Tamarind paste
4 lg Mushrooms; sliced
8 c  Water
1/2 c  Fresh lemon grass; chopped
1 sm Garlic head; peeled
5 sl Fresh ginger root
2 c  Scallions; chopped
1 c  Carrots; chopped
1 c  Celery; chopped
1. In a large pot, combine all ingredients. Bring just to a boil,
lower the heat and simmer for 45 minutes.

2.  Strain the liquid, pressing on the solids to extract as much of
the liq as possible. Discard the solids and refrigerate overnight.

Makes 6 to 7 cups.
