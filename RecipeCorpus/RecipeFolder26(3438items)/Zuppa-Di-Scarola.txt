Title: Zuppa Di Scarola
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable, Pasta, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Parsley; chopped
1 qt Chicken Broth (recipe)
1 c  Small macaroni; such as
- acini di pepe or pastina
2 c  Endive; shredded with a
-knife
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese; beaten with
1    Egg
2 md Carrots
2    Celery stalks
1 sm Onion
2 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Oregano
1    Garlic clove; minced
1/2 ts Salt
Basil leaves; chopped
Dice carrots, celery and onions.  Add oil to soup pot and saute
vegetables until soft (5-8 minutes).  Stir in garlic.  Sprinkle with
seasonings and chopped parsley.  Add broth and simmer 20 minutes. Add
macaroni and cook 10 minutes. Add endive to simmering soup, cook 5
minutes. Stir in cheese and egg mixture.  Serve hot with additional
grated cheese, if desired. Submitted By NICK LA ROCCA   On   12-24-94
