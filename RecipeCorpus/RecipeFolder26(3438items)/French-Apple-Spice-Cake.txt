Title: French Apple Spice Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 ts Allspice
1    Egg
1/2 c  Oil
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
1 1/2 c  Apples; peeled, chopped
1 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
Recipe by: Sue Klapper Preparation Time: 0:50 In large mixing bowl,
stir together apples and sugar; let stand.  Sift toge x 13 baking
pan. Bake at 350 for 40 to 50 minutes.  Source: Favorite Apple
Recipes.
