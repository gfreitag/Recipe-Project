Title: Lentil Roast (Ovo Lacto)
Categories: Beans, Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Corn flakes
1/3 ts Sage
Salt
Pepper
2/3 c  Onions; chop
1 1/3 c  Lentils
2/3 c  Nuts; grind
2 c  Egg whites
1 1/2 c  Milk; FF
2/3 c  Applesauce
Mix all ingredients together. Bake at 350 deg. After 45 minutes coat
top with undiluted tomato soup and continue baking for 15 minutes
more. :
