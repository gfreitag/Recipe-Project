Title: Sherry's Not Beef Stew
Categories: Fatfree, Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
5 tb Your favorite beef style
Broth or miso broth
Seasoning
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 tb Gravy Master
1 tb Garlic Powder or 2 cloves
Chopped
2 md Onions, peeled and cut
3 lg Carrots-sliced
2    Stalks celery,sliced
5 md Potatoes- peeled- random
Cut
1/2 lb TVP beef style chunks
1    10 oz. pkg.frozen peas
Combine all except peas in large pot or dutch oven. Add water enough
to cover 1 inch below ingredients (about 4 cups).

Cover & cook over very low heat for approx 1 hr, stirring
occasionally. Add peas and cook an additional 15 min. (Times may vary
~ many times I forget to turn off and the flavor seems to improve as
it sits and barely steams.
