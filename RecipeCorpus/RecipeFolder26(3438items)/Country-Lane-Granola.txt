Title: Country Lane Granola
Categories: Breakfast, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Water
1 1/3 c  Oil
1 tb Vanilla
1 tb Maple flavor
2 ts Salt
10 c  Oatmeal
1 c  Coconut
3/4 c  Sunflower seeds, roasted,
-unsalted
1 c  Nuts-pecans, almonds,
-peanuts,raw
Mix together all ingredients, toast in the oven at 325* stirring
every 10 min. 4 or 5 times.
