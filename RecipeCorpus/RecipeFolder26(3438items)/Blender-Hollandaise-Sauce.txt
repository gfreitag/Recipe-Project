Title: Blender Hollandaise Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Paprika
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1/4 lb Butter, melted
3 ea Egg yolks
1 T  Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
Heat blender container with hot water. Bring butter almost to boiling
in pyrex container in microwave. Put egg yolks in warm blender
container and blend at low speed for a few seconds. Add lemon juice
and seasonings. Then pour butter slowly into running blender - about
10 seconds pour time. Finish for a few seconds at high speed.  Pour
finished sauce into hot pyrex measure cup.
