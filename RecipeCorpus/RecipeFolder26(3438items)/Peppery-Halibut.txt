Title: Peppery Halibut
Categories: Main dish, Low-cal/fat, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 2 servings
2 ts Olive oil
2 tb Lemon juice
1/8 ts White pepper
1/8 ts Hot pepper sauce
2 tb Flour
1/2 ts Lemon pepper seasoning
1/2 ts Dried parsley flakes
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/2 lb Halibut steak, 3/4" thick
In pie pan or shallow dish, combine flour, lemon pepper seasoning,
parsley and garlic powder; mix well. Rinse halibut and pat dry. Cut
steak in half. Coat both sides of esch steak half with flour mixture,
shaking off excess. Heat oil in small nonstick skillet over medium
high heat until hot. Brown steak halves 1-2 minutes on each side.
Reduce heat to medium; cook 5-7 minutes, turning once, or until
halibut is firm and flakes easily with fork. Meanwhile, in small bowl
combine lemon juice, pepper and hot pepper sauce; mix well. to serve,
drizzle lemon juice mixture over halibut.

Per serving: 210 cal., 27g pro., 7g carb., 0g fiber, 8g fat, 40mg
chol., 230mg sod., 590mg pot., 1/2 starch and 3 lean meat exchanges
