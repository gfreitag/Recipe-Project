Title: How To Make Fresh Garbanzos
Categories: Fatfree, Information, Beans
Yield: 1 servings
None
Use Dried Garbanzos Beans soak them overnight. Use a big bowl or jar
and 1/3 the amount of garbanzos you need and 2/3 water. Rinse the
Garbanzos Beans a few times until the water is clear.The garbanzos
expand a great deal so leave room for that and some air space in the
jar or bowl. I often leave the jar in the sink in case it overflows.

Drain the garbanzo beans. Use fresh water and pour the garbanzo beans
into a pot covering the beans with about 1/2 inch to an inch of
water. Bring to a boil. Skim the foam from the water. Simmer the
garbanzos for about an hour. The garbanzos are ready when soft.
