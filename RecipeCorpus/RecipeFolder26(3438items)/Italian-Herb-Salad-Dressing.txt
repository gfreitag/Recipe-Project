Title: Italian Herb Salad Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Salad, Italian, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Sugar
1/2 t  Dried oregano
pn Pepper
3/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1 T  Grated Parmesan or Romano
-cheese
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine all ingredients; shake
well. Refrigerate.  Shake well again before serving over greens.
Yields: 1 1/4 cups From: "Taste of Home" Magazine, October/November
1994 Posted by: Debbie Carlson - Cooking
