Title: Cocoa Coconut Candy
Categories: Candy, Nuts/grains, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
pn Of salt
1/2 t  Vanilla
1/2 c  Shredded coconut
4 T  Confectioners' sugar
2 T  Boiling water
2 T  Cocoa
Blend the sugar, cocoa and salt, add boiling water and stir until
smooth. Add vanilla.  Dust hands lightly with powdered sugar; take
1/2 tsp. of mixture, roll into ball, roll in coconut. Shared By: Pat
Stockett
