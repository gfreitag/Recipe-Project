Title: Oven Baked French Fries
Categories: Potato, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 c  Onion, minced
Salt & Pepper to taste
6 tb Margarine
2 lb Baking Potatoes
Preheat oven to 425Ã¸.  Place margarine in 9x13" baking pan and melt in
oven.

Wash potatoes well.  Cut into lengthwise wedges about 1/2" across. Add
potatoes to margarine and stir to coat.  Sprinkle with onion, salt and
pepper.

Bake 30 minutes or until tender and browned, turning occasionally.
