Title: Zucchini with Beef Stuffing
Categories: Vegetable, Ground beef
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Bacon grease
1 ea Egg, beaten
2 sl Bread, soft,crumbed
1/3 c  Parmesan cheese
2 lb Zucchini,large
1 lb Beef, ground
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
1 tb Parsley, minced
1 ea Garlic clove
Scoop out centers of zucchini and chop.  Shape beef into large patty,
fry 5 min per side in bacon grease.  Break into small chunks.
Combine beef, onion, seasonings, chopped zucchini, egg and bread.
Spoon mixture into zucchini shells, top with parmesan cheese.  Bake
300 degrees 1 hour or until zucchini is tender.
