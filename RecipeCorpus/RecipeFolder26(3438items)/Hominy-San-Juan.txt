Title: Hominy San Juan
Categories: Vegetable, Cheese/eggs, Side dish, Casserole
Yield: 1 servings
-chilies
1 c  Grated Cheddar cheese
Salt to taste
6 ea Servings
1 ea 16-oz can white hominy
1 c  Sour cream
1 ea 4-oz can chopped green
Drain hominy and place in a saucepan.  Add sour cream, chilies and
cheese and mix over low heat.  Add salt.  Place in an ungreased 1
quart casserole and bake uncovered at 375 F for 20 minutes. Colorado
Cache Cookbook (1978) From the collection of Jim Vorheis
