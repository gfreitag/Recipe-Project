Title: Cream Chicken Primavera
Categories: Pasta, Poultry, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
Pasta and vegetables
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Light sour cream
2 ts Butter or margarine
3/4 lb Boneless skinless chicken br
1    16 oz. pkg green giant prima
Recipe by: from Green Giant ad Preparation Time: 0:20
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown chicken in
butter.
Stir in sour cream; heat thoroughly.
