Title: Ginger Dressing
Categories: Fatfree, Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Soy sauce
1 1/2 tb Ketchup
1/2    Lemon - juiced
x  S & p to taste
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Rice wine vinegar
2 tb Water
1 tb Chopped ginger root
Mix well in cuisinart. That's all there is to it. Enjoy!!!! T-Bone
