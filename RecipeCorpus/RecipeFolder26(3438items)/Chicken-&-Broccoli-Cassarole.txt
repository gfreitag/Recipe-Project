Title: Chicken & Broccoli Cassarole
Categories: Casserole, Poultry, Chicken, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1    Topping:
1 pk Canned biscuits
1/2 c  Sour cream
1    Egg
2    Chicken breasts
1 1/2 c  Chopped broccoli
1    Cream of chicken soup
1/2 c  Onion, diced
1/2 c  Sour cream
Mix first four ingredients in 2 1/2 qt cassarole.  Bake for 20-25
minutes at 375.  Seperate biscuits and cut in half rounds.  Arrange
on top of cassarole.  Beat egg well add sour cream.  Pour over top of
biscuits.
Bake at 375 for 25-30 minutes until golden brown. Recipe courtesy
