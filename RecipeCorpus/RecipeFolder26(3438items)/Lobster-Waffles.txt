Title: Lobster Waffles
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Sthrn/livng, Waffles
Yield: 6 waffles
3 c  Waffle mix
1/2 lb Lobster meat; cooked
MMMMM------------------------CHEESE SAUCE-----------------------------
1/3 c  Milk
11 oz Soup, Cheddar cheese
Chop lobster meat.  Prepare waffle mix as directed on package.  Add
lobster meat.  Bake in hot waffle iron until brown.  Serve with
Cheese Sauce.

Cheese Sauce: Combine cheese soup and milk. Heat. Yield: aobut 1-3/4
cups.
