Title: Mushroom Burrito Filling
Categories: Tex/mexican, Chilies, Mushroom
Yield: 4 servings
4 ea Green chiles, seeded and
-chopped
Juice of one lemon
1 T  Fresh thyme
1 c  Sour cream, or to taste
Salt and pepper
4 T  Vegetable oil
2 lb Fresh mushrooms, cultivated
-whites
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
4 ea Green onions, white and
-green parts, sliced
In a heavy skillet, heat oil and saute mushrooms, garlic, onions and
chiles until mushrooms are tender but not overly mushy.  Squeeze in
lemon juice and cook a minute more.  Season with thyme. Refrigerate
until serving time. Just before serving, reheat and, over low heat,
add sour cream. Heat until warm.  Season to taste with salt and
pepper and serve with warm flour tortillas.
