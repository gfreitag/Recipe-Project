Title: Jerk Chicken 2
Categories: Chicken, Herbs/spice, Caribbean, Beef, Pork
Yield: 3 /4 c  onio
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Salt
1    Chicken, whole
3/4 lb Ribs, beef or pork
4    Cloves of garlic
3    Jalapeno peppers
2 tb Ground Allspice
1 tb Minced ginger
1 tb Salad oil
Description:

This is the main stay of Jamaica cooks

Instructions:

Place all the condiments (8) into a blender and puree until it forms a
paste.

Rub the paste on to the meat and cover with plastic wrap.

Refrigerate for 24 hrs. Remove the plastic wrap.

Place the meat on the grill in a covered BBQ next to a pan filled with
water and roast over low coals for 4 to 5 hours.

Remove meat from grill and let stand for 15 minutes before carving and
serving
