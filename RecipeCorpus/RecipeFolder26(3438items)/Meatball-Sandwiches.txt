Title: Meatball Sandwiches
Categories: Main dish, Sandwich, Ground beef, Meatballs
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/8 ts Paprika
2 tb (plus 1 cup) bottled
-marinara sauce
3 tb Chopped parsley
1    Loaf Italian bread
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 c  Seasoned bread crumbs
2 tb Chopped onion
1 cl Garlic, finely chopped
2 tb Grated Parmesan
1/2 ts Dried leaf oregano
1.  Preheat oven to 350F.  Combine beef, crumbs, onion, garlic,
Parmesan, oregano, pepper, paprika and 2 tablespoons sauce in bowl.
Shape meat mixture into 12 equal meatballs.  Place on a jelly-roll
pan.

2.  Bake in preheated 350F oven 15 to 20 minutes until cooked through.

3.  Heat 1 cup sauce in a saucepan.  Stir in parsley. Divide bread
crosswise into fourths; split.  Spoon meatballs and sauce into bread.

Per sandwich:  618 calories, 33 g pro, 26 g fat, 61 g carb, 1,377 mg
sod, 3 mg chol.
