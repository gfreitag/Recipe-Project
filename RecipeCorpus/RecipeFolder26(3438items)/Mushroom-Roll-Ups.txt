Title: Mushroom Roll-Ups
Categories: Appetizer, Mushroom, Vegetable, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Chives
1 t  Lemon juice
1 ea Loaf very thinly sliced
-white bread
1/2 lb Mushrooms, minced
1/4 c  Butter
1 c  Light cream
3 T  Flour
3/4 t  Salt
Saute' mushrooms in butter.  Stir in 1 cup light cream and cook until
thick.  Add remaining ingredients except bread.  Cut crusts off
bread. Roll bread with a rolling pin until thin.  Spread mixture on
bread and roll up. Slice in thirds. (Do not cut if you plan to put in
freezer.) Bake 10 minutes at 400 F.  Recipe can be doubled by adding
8 ounces of lobster meat.  Yield: 3-1/2 dozen.  Can be frozen. From:
An Olde Concord Christmas Shared By: Pat Stockett
