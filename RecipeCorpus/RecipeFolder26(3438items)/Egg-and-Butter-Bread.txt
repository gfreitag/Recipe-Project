Title: Egg and Butter Bread
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 loaves
1 tb Salt
3    Eggs; slightly beaten
7 1/2 c  Sifted flour
1    Egg yolk
Sesame seeds
2 pk Dry yeast
1/2 c  Warm water
1 1/2 c  Scalded milk
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
Dissolve yeast in warm water.  Combine all remaining ingredients
except flour, egg yolk and sesame seeds.  Mix wel, add yeast.  Stir
in 3 cups flour.  Add remaining flour a little at a time; knead until
smooth. Place in a greased bowl and let rise until double in bulk.
Punch down and let rest 30 minutes.  Divide into 9 strips; braid into
3 loaves. Let rise 1 hour.  Beat egg yolk and 2 tablespoons water
together; brush carefully over bread.  Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes at 425 degrees.
