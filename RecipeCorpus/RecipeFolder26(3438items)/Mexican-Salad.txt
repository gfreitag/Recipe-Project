Title: Mexican Salad
Categories: Salad, Tex/mexican, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------FROM: ARIZONA COOKBOOK------------------------
4 sl Bacon, fried crisp and
-crumbled
4 ea Hard-cooked eggs, sliced
1/2 t  Salt
1 t  Chili powder
1/2 c  Vinegar
2 lg Green peppers, cut into
-1-inch chunks
1 md Onion, cut into chunks
4 md Tomatoes, peeled and cut
-into chunks
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
Chop vegetables. Fry bacon until crisp; remove. Chop and add to
vegetables. To bacon fat in skillet, add vinegar, salt and chili]
powder. Mix well and pour over vegetables. Serve on shredded lettuce.
