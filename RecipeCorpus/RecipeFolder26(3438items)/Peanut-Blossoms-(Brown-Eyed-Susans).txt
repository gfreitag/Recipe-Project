Title: Peanut Blossoms (Brown-Eyed Susans)
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Milk
1 t  Vanilla
1 ea Egg
48 ea Milk chocolate candy
-kisses
Sugar, for rolling
1 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1 t  Baking soda
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Peanut butter
Heat oven to 375F.  Combine the flour, sugar, brown sugar, baking
soda, salt, shortening, peanut butter, milk, vanilla and egg in a
large bowl. Blend with an electric mixer at low speed until a stiff
dough forms. Shape into 1-inch balls and roll in additional sugar.
Place 2-inches apart on ungreased cookie sheets. Bake for 10 to 12
minutes or until golden brown. Immediately top each cookie with a
candy kiss, pressing down firmly so the cookie cracks around the
edge. Remove from the cookie sheets and cool completely. Makes 4
dozen. Per serving (cookie): 100 calories; 2 g protein, 12 g
carbohydrate; 5 g fat; 65 mg sodium. [The Complete Book of Baking;
Pillsbury Publications; 1993] [ISBN 0-670-77147-3] Posted by Fred
Peters.
