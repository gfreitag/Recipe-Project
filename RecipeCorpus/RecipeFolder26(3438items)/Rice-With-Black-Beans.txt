Title: Rice With Black Beans
Categories: Fatfree, Beans, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
And drained
2/3 c  Water
1/2 t  Oregano
1 md Onion, chopped
2    Cloves garlic
1 cn (14.5 oz.) stewed tomatoes
1 cn (15 oz.) black beans, rinsed    1 1/2 c  Minute Brown Rice
Saute onions and garlic in a small amount of liquid (the original
recipe called for oil, but I just used a little water) until tender.
Stir in tomatoes, beans, water, and oregano.  Bring to a boil.  Stir
in rice. Return to boil.  Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 5
minutes.  Remove from heat.  Let stand 5 mintes.

That's it!  ENJOY!!  :)
