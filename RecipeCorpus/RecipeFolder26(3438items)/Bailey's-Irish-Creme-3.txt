Title: Bailey's Irish Creme 3
Categories: Beverage, Alcohol, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------FROM: TOM MORSTAD--------------------------
1/2 T  Hershey's Chocolate Syrup
1/2 t  Coconut Extract
1/2 t  Almond Extract
2 cn Evaportated milk
1 c  Irish Whiskey
1/2 c  Vodka
Put in blender.  Walk around table three times to age to perfection.
Blend. *** KEEP REFRIDGERATED ***
