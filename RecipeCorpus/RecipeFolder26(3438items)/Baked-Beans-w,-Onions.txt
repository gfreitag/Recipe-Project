Title: Baked Beans w/ Onions
Categories: Side dish, Beans
Yield: 100 servings
4 lb Bacon
2 c  Brown sugar
4 c  Maple syrup
24 lb Baked Beans, canned
6 lb Onions, sliced
4 lb Olives, stuffed
Place beans in greased casseroles. Make a sauce of the maple syrup and
brown sugar and pour over beans. Mix well. Cover with onions. Wrap
slices of bacon around olives and lay one in center of each
casserole. Place in 350 degF oven until bacon is browned and beans
are heated; about 40 minutes.
