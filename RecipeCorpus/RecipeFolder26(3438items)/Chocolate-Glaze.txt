Title: Chocolate Glaze
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Light corn syrup
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
2 tb Butter
Combine chocolate chips, butter, and corn syrup in saucepan. Stir
over low heat until chocolate is melted. Remove from heat and add
vanilla.

Frost top and sides of DD Brownie Torte. Chill until set.
