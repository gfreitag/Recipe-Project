Title: Layered Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Head lettuce (shredded)
1 c  Marie's creamy Italian
-Garlic dressing
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 pk Frozen peas (thawed in cold
-water)
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Chopped green pepper
Layer first five ingredients in clear glass bowl in order given. Mix
dressing and mayonnaise together then spread over layered ingredients.
Sprinkle to cover with grated sharp cheddar cheese. Sprinkle with
chopped or sliced hard boiled egg.
