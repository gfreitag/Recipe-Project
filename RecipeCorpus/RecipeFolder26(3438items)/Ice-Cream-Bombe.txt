Title: Ice Cream Bombe
Categories: Mom's, Ice cream
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
Fill 1-qt. mold with 1 qt. ice cream, leaving center hollow. With
back of spoon, smooth ice cream so it is equally distributed over
bottom and sides of mold. Spoon 1 pt. sherbet into hollowed center;
press. Freezer-wrap, freeze. To serve, dip mold into cool water 5 to
10 sec.- unmold. Try: Chocolate or coffee ice cream, orange sherbet
Vanilla ice cream, orange sherbet Pistachio ice cream, lemon sherbet
Strawberry ice cream, raspberry sherbet
