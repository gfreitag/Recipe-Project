Title: Super Potato Cheese Casserole
Categories: Vegetable, Potato, Cheese/eggs, Casserole
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Minced parsley
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dill weed
-Dash pepper
-Paprika to taste
2 lb Russet potatoes, cooked,
-peeled and shredded
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
1 1/2 c  Swiss cheese, shredded
1 md Carrot, shredded
1/4 c  Minced onion
Combine all ingredients except 1/2 cup Swiss cheese and paprika. Turn
into buttered 1 1/2 quart casserole; sprinkle with remaining cheese
and paprika. Bake in a preheated 350 oven30-40 minutes.

Source: Fabulous Vegetarian Recipes by Johna Blinn
