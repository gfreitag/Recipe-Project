Title: Salsa Pronto
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Dip
Yield: 2 servings
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
2 t  Lemon juice
1/8 t  Hot pepper sauce
1 c  Mexican style stew tomatoes
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
2 T  Chopped fresh cilantro
Place tomatoes in blender container.  Cover and run on lowest speed 2
seconds to chop tomatoes.  Combine with onion, cilantro, lemon juice,
garlic and pepper sauce.  Add additional pepper sauce, if desired.
Serve with tortilla chips.  Makes 2 cups.
