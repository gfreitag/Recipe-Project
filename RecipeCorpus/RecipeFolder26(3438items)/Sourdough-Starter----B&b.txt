Title: Sourdough Starter -  B&b
Categories: Bread, Sourdough
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Warm water
2 ts Sugar
1 1/4 ts Salt
2 1/3 c  Flour
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Warm water
2 ts Salad oil
1 pk Yeast
Recipe by: Stacked arms B & B in Wiscasset,  Maine combine the milk,
1/4 C water and oil, bring to a boil. Cool to lukewarm. Sprinkle the
yeast on 1/2 C warm water; stir until dissolved; add with sugar and
salt to the cooled milk mixture. Stir the liquid into the flour just
enough to blend thoroughly. Cover; let stand in a warm place for
12-18 hours to sour.
