Title: Linda's Nut Candies
Categories: Candy, Dessert, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
10 oz Slivered almonds
12 oz Semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 c  Walnuts; chopped
2 c  Butter
2 c  Sugar
6 tb Water
In electric skillet, melt butter.  Add sugar, almonds and water. Place
candy thermometer into mix. Temperature of skillet should be about 400
degrees. Cook, stirring constantly to hard/crack stage on
thermometer. Pour mix quickly onto a large jelly roll sheet (large
cookie sheet with sides). Spread very quickly. Cool in refrigerator.
Melt chocolate chips over double boiler.  Spread over cooled candy
and sprinkle walnuts while chocolate is still soft.  Press nuts into
chocolate to secure. Cool again. Break into pieces and store in an
airtight container.
