Title: Orange-Glazed Chicken
Categories: Diabetic, Chicken, Fruit, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Vegetable oil
1/2 t  Orange marmalade
ds Nutmeg
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 T  All-purpose flour
1/2 t  Salt (optional)
1/4 t  Pepper
1 ea Boneless skinless chicken
-breast half
Combine flour, salt if desired, and pepper; coat chicken breast. In a
skillet, heat oil on medium; brown chicken. Spread marmalade on top of
chicken; sprinkle with nutmeg. Add orange juice and simmer for 10-15
minutes or until chicken juices run clear. Serves 1. Diabetic
Exchanges: One serving (without added salt) equals 4 Lean Meat, 1
Fat, 1 Fruit, 1/2 Starch; also, 370 Calories, 71mg Sodium, 83mg
Cholesterol, 23g Carbohydrate, 31g Protein, 17g Fat. SOURCE:*Taste of
Home Magazine, December/January 1995. POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 1/95
