Title: Dutch Boys
Categories: Main dish, Sandwich, Sthrn/livng, Hot dogs, Vegetable
Yield: 10 sandwiches
1 c  Sauerkraut; drained
10    Swiss cheese strips
10    Wieners
10    Wiener buns
Mayonnaise (or butter)
Heat wieners and place in buns that have been spread with mayonnaise.
Add drained sauerkraut and strips of cheese.  Broil, open faced,
until cheese is melted and buns are toasted.  Or wrap tightly in foil
and grill about 15 minutes.
