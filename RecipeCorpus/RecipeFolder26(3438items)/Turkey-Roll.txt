Title: Turkey Roll
Categories: Crockpot, Turkey
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
(It's never been easier. . . or better) Place 2 pound frozen turkey
roll in CROCK-POT. Add 1 cup chicken broth or barbecue sauce. Cover
and cook on low 8 to 10 hours. (Gravy packet may be added to the
turkey in CROCK-POT or mixed with dressing to be packed lightly
around turkey roll). Submitted By EARL SHELSBY   On   01-08-95
