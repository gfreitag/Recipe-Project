Title: Iced Summer Green Pea Soup
Categories: Appetizer, Soup/stew, Vegetable, British
Yield: 4 servings
1    Lettuce
250 g  Thin cream
750 ml Chicken stock
1/2    Lemon; juice
750 g  Peas; shelled
Salt
Pepper, ground
1 md Onion; sliced
1 md Potato; sliced
Slice the potatoes and onions thinly and put them in a saucepan with
the shelled peas (weighed shelled), the lettuce and half the chicken
stock. Bring gently to the boil, cover and simmer quarter of an hour.

Pour into a blender or liquidiser and blend to a puree. Return to the
rinsed out pan, reheat and simmer 5 minutes. Now add the cream and the
lemon juice. Season well with salt and pepper, cool and chill. Serve
well chilled.
