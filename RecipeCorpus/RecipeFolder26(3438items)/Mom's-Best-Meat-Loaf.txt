Title: Mom's Best Meat Loaf
Categories: Ground beef, Herbs/spice, Vegetable, Meatloaf
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 t  Salt
1 t  Prepared horseradish
1 t  Sugar
1 t  Ground allspice
1 t  Dill weed
Additional ketchup
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1 c  Milk
1 ea Egg, lightly beaten
3/4 c  Soft bread crumbs
1 md Onion, chopped
1 T  Chopped green pepper
1 T  Ketchup
In a large bowl, combine the first 12 ingredients; mix well. Press
into an ungreased 8 1/2" x 4 1/2" x 2 1/2" loaf pan. Bake at 350
degrees for 1 hour. Drizzle top of loaf with ketchup; bake 15 minutes
more or until no pink remains. Serves 6-8. SOURCE:*Taste of Home
Magazine, December/January 1995. POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 1/95
