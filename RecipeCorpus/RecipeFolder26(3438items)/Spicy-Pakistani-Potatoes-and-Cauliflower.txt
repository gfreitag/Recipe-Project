Title: Spicy Pakistani Potatoes and Cauliflower
Categories: Indian, Side dish, Vegetarian, Potato
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM------------------VEGETARIAN TIMES; JAN 95-----------------------
1/8 ts Ground cloves
8 oz Potato; cooked, diced
1 sm Cauliflower, cut into floret
3 tb Cooking water
Salt to taste
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 md Onion; finely chopped
2 ts Garlic; minced
1 ts Ginger root; grated
Black pepper; to taste
1/2 ts Ground cardamom
Preparation Time: 0:30 Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Add onion; saute until translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in garlic,
