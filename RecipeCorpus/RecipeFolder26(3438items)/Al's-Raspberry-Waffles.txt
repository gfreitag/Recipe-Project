Title: Al's Raspberry Waffles
Categories: Waffles, Fruit, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------NOTE:  AMOUNTS MAY VARY ACCO---------------------
White Creme de Cacao
Chopped pecans
Any good quality pancake
-and waffle mix
g  German chocolate
1 pt Fresh raspberries, washed
-and drained
1/2 pt Whipping cream
Vanilla
Sugar
Put raspberries in a bowl and add Creme do Cacao to taste. Allow to
stand a bit.  Pre-heat a Belgian waffle iron. Mix up a batch of
waffle batter according to package directions. All chopped pecans to
the batter when mixed. Whip cream to stiff consistancy. Add vanilla
and sugar to taste. Add raspberry mixture to whipped cream and fold
in. Cook waffles and serve topped with a healthy portion of the
whipped cream and raspberries. Garnish with grated chocolate.
