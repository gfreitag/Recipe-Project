Title: Black Bean and Rice Salad (Vegan)
Categories: Salad, Beans, Rice, Fatfree
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Fresh parsley; snipped
1/4 c  Low calorie italian salad dr
1 tb Fresh lime juice
Lettuce leaves
2 c  Cooked rice; cooled
1 c  Cooked black beans
1 c  Chopped tomatoes
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded; op
Leftover black beans and rice combine to make a great lowfat, low
cholesterol salad.  Combine rice, beans, tomato, cheese (if desired),
and parsley in large bowl. Pour dressing and lime juice over rice
mixture; toss. Serve on lettuce leaves. Nutrition (per serving): 217
calories, Total Fat 2 g   (9% of calories) Source: USA Rice Council
