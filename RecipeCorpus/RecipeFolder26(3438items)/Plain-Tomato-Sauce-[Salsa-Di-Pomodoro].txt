Title: Plain Tomato Sauce [Salsa Di Pomodoro]
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Tomato, Italian
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
Salt
1 qt Tomato puree *
3    Garlic cloves, sliced
2    Sweet basil sprigs
~* May be canned or home-canned ; amount is approximate.

Heat saucepan. Add oil, basil and garlic and cook only until garlic is
softened. Add puree and a little salt. Cook for 20 minutes or until
creamy. May be made ahead of time and frozen in recipe-size batches.
