Title: Half & Half Mustard Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 tb Cornstarch
3/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Tarragon
1 pt Half and half
4 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Dried mustard
2    Egg yolks
Mix together cream, sugar, mustard, egg yolks and cornstarch.  Cook
in a double boiler until thickened.  Add vinegar, basil, and
tarragon. Cook for an additional 5 to 7 minutes.  Store in
refrigerator. Submitted By HILDE MOTT   On   01-07-95
