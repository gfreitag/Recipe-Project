Title: Yapple Pudding ?? (Vegan)
Categories: Fatfree, Sweetpotato, Apples, Pudding
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Applesauce
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 md To small yam, baked, &
Completly cooled
Remove yam skin, mash up in a bowl. Add applesauce and cinnamon, stir
up well, enjoy.
