Title: Gourmet Cranberry Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Berry sugar
1/4 c  Grand Marnier
3 c  Fresh or frozen cranberries
1 ea Orange cut in quarters
Chop cranberries and orange in a blender or food processors.  Add
sugar and grand marnier.  Store in sterilized jars.  Makes excellent
gifts for Christmas. Origin: My recipe file Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Jan/95
