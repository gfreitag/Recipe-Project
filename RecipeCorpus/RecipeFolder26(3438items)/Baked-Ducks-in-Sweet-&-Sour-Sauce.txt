Title: Baked Ducks in Sweet & Sour Sauce
Categories: Game, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Catsup
2 tb Lemon Juice
1/2 c  Onion [grated]
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 c  White vinegar
2    Wild ducks
1    Orange [sliced]
1    Apple [sliced]
2 tb Oil
2 tb Brown sugar
1/4 c  Worchestershire sauce
Rince the ducks and pat dry inside and out, then stuff the
