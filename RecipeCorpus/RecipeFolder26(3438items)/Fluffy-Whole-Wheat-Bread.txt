Title: Fluffy Whole Wheat Bread
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 24 ounces
2 ts Yeast
2 1/2 c  Flour, whole wheat
2 c  Rice, cooked
1/2 c  Water; warm
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 tb Oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 tb Syrup, corn
Scald milk; add oil, salt, and corn syrup.  When lukewarm, add ALL
ingredients to breadmachine.  Set "baking control" at 11 o'clock.
Select "white bread" and push Start.

Sylvia's comment:  Rose great.  Only change I want to make is
eliminate the two-steps, just dump in dry milk and water.
