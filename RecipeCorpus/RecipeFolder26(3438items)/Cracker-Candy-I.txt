Title: Cracker Candy I
Categories: Candy, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Pecans, chopped
Saltine crackers
1 c  Margarine or butter
1 c  Brown sugar
12 oz Chocolate chips
Melt butter and brown sugar. Bring to a boil for 3 minutes. Lina a
cookie sheet with saltines. Pour butter-sugar mix over all and bake
at 350~F for 7 minutes. Sprinkle choco chips on top and smooth all
over. Sprinkle with chopped nuts. Cool, then refrigerate to harden.
Do not cut, break into pieces. This freezes well.
