Title: CHILI-CHEESE BREAD
Categories: Breads, Cheese
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Monterey jack cheese; grated
4 oz Chiles; chopped, *
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 ea French bread; loaf, unsliced
*  You can use one can of sweet green chiles or jalapenos that have
been chopped. +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++ ++++ Mix the cheese, peppers, and mayonnaise, blending
well. Spread on the cut surface of the French bread, which has been
sliced in half horizontally. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 to 30
minutes and cut into thick slices and serve hot.
