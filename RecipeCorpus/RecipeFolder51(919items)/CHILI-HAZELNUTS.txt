Title: CHILI HAZELNUTS
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks, Fruits/nuts
Yield: 2 Cups
2 c  Oregon hazelnuts
3/16 c  Butter or margarine
3/4 ts Chili powder
1 ds Salt
Melt butter in sauce pan; stir in chili powder, salt and hazelnuts.
Turn into shallow baking pan. Bake in a
275    oven for 20 minutes.
