Title: CHILI DOGS 2
Categories: Snacks, Sandwiches
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Ea. FRANKS
1/2 lb Hamburger
2 tb Oil
1    Med. Onion, chopped
1 lb Kidney Beans, can
8 oz Tomato Sauce, can
1 1/2 ts Chile Powder
Brown hamburger in heavy skillet. Add Onion and Garlic; cook til
Onion is tender. Add remaining ingredients and simmer 15 min. Preheat
FRANKS and buns. Place FRANKS in buns; top with sauce. Sprinkle
cheese and chopped Onions over top.

Serves 6
