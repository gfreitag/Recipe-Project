Title: CHILI KIDNEY BEAN BREAD
Categories: Breads, Abm
Yield: 1 Lg. loaf
MMMMM-------------------LISA CRAWLEY/TEASPOON------------------------
3 c  Whole Wheat Flour
1/3 c  Popcorn Flour
1 1/2 ts Bouquet Garni
1 tb Chili Powder
2 ts Date Sugar
1 1/2 ts Sea Salt
1 c  Mashed Kidney Beans
1 1/4 c  Water
2 tb Oil
4 ts Active Dry Yeast
Place ingred. in breadmachine according to your mfg. instructions.
Use the WHOLE WHEAT CYCLE... I use the WHITE BREAD CYCLE on WELBILT
ABM100.
