Title: CHILI PAT'S RECIPE
Categories: Chili
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Hamburger
1 ea Onion chopped
1 tb Fat
1 cn Kidney Beans
1 cn Tomato soup
2/3 c  Water
1 ts Salt
2 ts Vinegar
1/2 ts Chilles or 1 tsp. your taste
Heat fat in Frypan, add onion and hamburger and brown. Add rest of
ingredients and simmer till thick. Very Good.
