Title: CHINESE BROWN GRAVY
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 2 Servings
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Sesame oil
1/2 c  White wine
Blend all ingredients and cook until slightly thickened.
