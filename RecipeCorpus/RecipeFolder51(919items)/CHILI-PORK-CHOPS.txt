Title: CHILI PORK CHOPS
Categories: Pork
Yield: 4 Servings
NORMA WRENN NPXR56B-----
4    Lean pork chops
20 oz Can creamed corn
1/2 ts -salt
2 ts Chili powder
1/8 ts -pepper
1 ts Instant minced onion
1/4 c  Sweet pepper flakes
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
2 tb Melted butter
Trim excess fat from pork chops.  Rub each side with a little salt and
pepper. Brown chops in melted pork fat. Place in casserole. Combine
corn, salt, chili powder, pepper, onion and pepper flakes; spoon over
chops. Cover casserole and bake 35 minutes in 350 degree oven. Blend
bread crumbs with melted butter; sprinkle over casserole and bake,
uncovered 15 minutes longer.
