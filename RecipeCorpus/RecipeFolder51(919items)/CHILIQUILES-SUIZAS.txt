Title: CHILIQUILES SUIZAS
Categories: Main dish, Mexican
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef or stringy beef
1 pt Sour cream
2    Onions
2    Or 3 cloves garlic
1 pt Whole, peeled tomatoes
1 lb Jack cheese, sliced or
Shredded
Several slices American or
Other mild
Yellow cheeses
pn Oregano and cumin
1/2 ts Ground coriander
Corn tortillas
Wheat tortillas- use one
Dozen
Salt and pepper
Brown meat in skillet with onions, garlic and spices, then layer in a
deep round dish, alternating with both kinds of tortillas and the
other ingredients, reserving the yellow cheese. Bake for 30 to 40
minutes at 350 F. and top with the yellow cheese for the last
10    minutes.
