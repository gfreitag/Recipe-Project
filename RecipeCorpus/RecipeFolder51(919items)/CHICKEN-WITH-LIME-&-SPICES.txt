Title: CHICKEN WITH LIME & SPICES
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Chicken breast fillets
2 tb Olive oil
3 tb Lime juice (fresh)
1 ts Coriander; ground
1 ts Cumin; ground
1/2 ts Turmeric
1 tb Fresh mint; chopped
Cut the chicken fillets into 1.5 cm strips.

Make the marinade by combining the remaining ingredients except the
olive oil in a small bowl.  Add the chicken and marinade for at least
7 hours but preferably overnight turning occasionally.

Drain the chicken and reserve the marinade.

Heat the olive oil in a medium pan; add chicken and cook over a high
heat for 5-10 minutes or until lightly browned and tender; stir in
the reserved marinade.

Serve the chicken strips with hummus rolled in pita bread.
