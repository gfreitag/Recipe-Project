Title: CHICKPEA SALAD
Categories: Digest, Nov.
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Minced parsley
1/4 c  Minced onion
1 lg Clove garlic, minced or
Pressed
6 tb Lemon juice
1/8 ts Cayenne
1/2 ts Salt or to taste
1 1/2 c  Drained chickpeas
1 1/2 c  Sliced zucchini or yellow
Crookneck squash
6 tb Bean liquid from the can
Whisk together everything but the chickpeas and squash. Stir in the
chickpeas and squash.  Let marinate in the fridge for at least a few
hours for flavors to blend.
