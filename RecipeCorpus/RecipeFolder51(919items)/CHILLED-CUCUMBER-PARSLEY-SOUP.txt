Title: CHILLED CUCUMBER PARSLEY SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Natural yogurt
2 c  Light cream OR 1 cup milk
& 1 cup heavy cream
2    Medium seedless cucumbers,
Peeled and chopped
1 c  Finely chopped chives OR
Green onions
2 ts Salt
Stir yogurt and cream together until smooth.  Stir in remaining
ingredients and refrigerate until serving time. Garnish with thin
unpeeled cucumber slices and a dash of paprika. Serves 6.
