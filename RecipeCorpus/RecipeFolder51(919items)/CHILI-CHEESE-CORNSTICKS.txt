Title: CHILI CHEESE CORNSTICKS
Categories: Appetizers, Chili
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/4 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 lg Egg
1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Milk
2 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar
1 tb Baking powder
2 tb Chopped green chilies
Beat egg, milk and 1/4 c. vegetable oil.  Add dry ingredi- ents,
stirring only to mix.  Toss in cheese and chilies. Bake in greased
pans at 425 degrees for
15    minutes.
