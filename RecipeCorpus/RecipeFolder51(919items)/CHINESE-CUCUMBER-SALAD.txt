Title: CHINESE CUCUMBER SALAD
Categories: Chinese, Salads, Veggies
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM------------------HOW TO BOIL WATER, TVFN-----------------------
1 tb Soy sauce
1/4 c  Rice wine vinegar
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Veg oil
1 ds Sesame oil
1 ts Red pepper flakes
2    Cucumbers, seeded, thinly
-sliced
1 sm Red bell pepper, thin sliced
Mix soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, oil, and pepper flakes. Mix in
cucumbers and bell pepper. Chill. Typed by Sherilyn Schamber
