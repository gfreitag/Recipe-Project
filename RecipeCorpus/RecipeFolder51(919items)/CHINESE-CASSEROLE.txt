Title: CHINESE CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Ethnic, Chinese
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef, browned
1 lg Onion, diced
2 c  Celery, diced
1 cn Tomato soup
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Bean sprouts (drained)
Mix together and place in greased 2 quart casserole. Cover top with
chow mein noodles.  Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
