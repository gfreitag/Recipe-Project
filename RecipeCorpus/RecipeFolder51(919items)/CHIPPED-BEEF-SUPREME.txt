Title: CHIPPED BEEF SUPREME
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Recipe
8 oz Package cream cheese
2 tb Milk
2 tb Chopped green pepper
2 tb Minced onion
3 oz Chipped beef, shredded
1/2 c  Sour cream
Combine cream cheese and milk over low heat.  Add remaining
ingredients and mix well.  Make at least 12 hours in advance for best
flavor. Great with cracker assortment. May be served cold or warm.
