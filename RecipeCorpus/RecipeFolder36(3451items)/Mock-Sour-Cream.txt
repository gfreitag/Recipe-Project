Title: Mock Sour Cream
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Condiments
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
1/2 c  Low-fat buttermilk
2 c  Low-fat cottage cheese
Combine ingredients in a blender and process until smooth, scraping
down sides of container often. Refrigerate and use as needed.
