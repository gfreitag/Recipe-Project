Title: Pumpkin Cheesecake
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Allspice -- ground
1 ts Ground ginger -- or
-allspice
1 c  Whipping cream
1 tb Vanilla extract
1 ea Pumpkin, canned -- 1 pound
2 1/2 lb Cream cheese -- softened
1 c  Sugar -- granulated
4 lg Egg -- lightly beaten
3 ea Egg yolk -- lightly beaten
3 tb Flour, all-purpose
2 ts Cinnamon -- ground
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1/4 c  Sugar -- granulated
1 1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
6 tb Butter -- melted
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix crumbs, butter & sugar.  Press into springform pan and smooth out
and bake for 10 minutes.  Cool before filling.

Preheat oven to 424 degrees.
In large mixing bowl, beat together cream cheese, sugar, eggs, &
yolks. Add flour, cinnamon, cloves, & ginger.  Beat in whipping cream
and vanilla then add the mashed pumkin and beat at medium speed till
just mixed thoroughly.  Pour into prepare crust and bake for 15
minutes.  Reduce temperature to 275 and bake for an hour.  Turn off
heat & leave cake in oven overnight to cool.
