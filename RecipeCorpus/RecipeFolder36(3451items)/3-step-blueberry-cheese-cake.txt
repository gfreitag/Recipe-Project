Title: 3 step blueberry cheese cake
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
2 ea Eggs
1 ea 9oz graham cracker crust
1 cn Blueberry pie filling
2 pk 8oz cream cheese
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Vanilla
Mix cream cheese, sugar, vanilla until smooth and creamy. Add eggs
and mix well. Pour into pie crust. Spoon 1/4 to 1/3 of pie filling on
to top. Gently swirl with toothpick. Bake at 350 degrees for 40
minutes or until center is set. Cool to room temperature and then
refrigerate. Serve topped with remaining pie filling.
