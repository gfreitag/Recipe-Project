Title: Low-Salt Fish Stock
Categories: Soup/stew, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 16 cups
8    Sprigs cilantro
1 md Onion(s), chopped
3    Garlic clove(s), mashed
2    Bay leaves
18 c  Water
2 lb Fish bones
1    Leek, chopped
1 c  Celery, chopped
In a Dutch oven or stew pot, bring the water to a boil. Add the fish
and boil until foam appears on surface. Skim off foam and add other
ingredients. cover and simmer for 30 min. Let cool, then strain and
discard solids.

Will keep, refrigerated in a tightly sealed jar, for 1 week.
