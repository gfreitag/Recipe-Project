Title: Sprouting Dill Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Dill seeds
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1/4 cup in a quart jar, or tray sprout which will yield about 2
cups of sprouts. Soak for 8 hours.

Rinse 3 times daily for 3 to 5 days. Move into sunlight to green.

Harvest when sprouts are 1 to 1-1/2 inches long. Use in salads,
sandwiches, and juices.
