Title: Absurdly Easy Chocolate Cake (Vegan)
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
6 tb Oil
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Cold water
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
3 tb Cocoa
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 tb Salt
Mix the dry ingredients.  Add the wet ingredients.  Stir until smooth.
Bake at 350 for 40 minutes.
