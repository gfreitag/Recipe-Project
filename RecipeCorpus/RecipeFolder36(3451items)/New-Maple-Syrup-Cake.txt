Title: New Maple Syrup Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Syrups
Yield: 2 servings
No Ingredients Found
Follow either recipe for Large Cake above, except substitute 1 cup
maple syrup for 1 cup of the the sugar (use only 1/2 cup sugar.)
Decrease milk to 1/2 cup.  Add the syrup with it.  Omit vanilla.
Blend in 1.2 cup cut up butternuts (or other nuts).  Bake.  Cool.
Frost with Maple Syrup Icing or with a cooked white frosting with
maple flavoring added. From:  Betty Crocker Picture Cookbook
