Title: Layered Dinner
Categories: Main dish, Soup/stew, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 servings
1 ea Zucchini, sliced
1 c  Corn, frozen or fresh
1 c  Peas, frozen or fresh
6 ea Potatoes, sliced
1 lg Onion, sliced
2 ea Carrots, sliced
1 ea Green bell pepper, sliced
MMMMM--------------------OPTIONAL VEGETABLES-------------------------
Green beans
Mushrooms
Broccoli
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Sage
2 tb Parsley flakes
2 1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1/4 c  Tamari
1 ts Thyme
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Basil
Layer vegetables in large casserole in order given.

Mix together ingredients for sauce and pour over vegetables.

Cook six hours at high or 12 at low.
