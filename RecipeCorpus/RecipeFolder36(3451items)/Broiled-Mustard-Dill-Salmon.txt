Title: Broiled Mustard-Dill Salmon
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Chopped fresh dill or 3/4
-tsp. dried
1 lb Salmon fillet
1    Lemon
2 tb Stone ground mustard
Cooking Time: 15 min
Preparation Time:

Instructions:

Preheat broiler.  Set oven rack 4-5 inches from the element.  Rinse
fish, pat dry and lay skin side down on a baking sheet.  With a zester
or grater remove zest (yellow part of peel only) from lemon; squeeze 1
tbs juice from lemon.  Mix lemon zest, lemon juice, mustard and dill.
Brush over salmon.  Broil salmon until opaque in center, about 5-6
minutes for 1/2-inch thick fillet; 10-12 minutes for 1-inch thick
fillet.
