Title: APPLE BEET BORSCHT
Categories: Vegetarian, Soup/stew, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Apple cider
1/4 c  Tamari
1/4 c  Sherry
2 tb Molasses or honey
1 tb Dried dill
2 ts Dried basil
1 tb Caraway seeds
1 tb Canola oil
3 oz Tomato paste
4 c  Water or light vegetable
-stock
1/4 x  Med. reg or green cabbage
-thinly sliced
2 ea Carrots, julienned
1 x  Clove Garlic, minced
3 x  Beets, julienned
1 md Onion, thinly sliced
Saute caraway seeds in canola oil. Then, combine all ingredients in a
soup pot. Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer 2-3 hours,
stirring occasionally.
Puree one-third of the soup in food proceessor; return puree to the
pot and mix thoroughly. Serve hot or cold with a dollop of Tofu Sour
Cream.
