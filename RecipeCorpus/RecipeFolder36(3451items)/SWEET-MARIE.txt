Title: SWEET MARIE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Peanut butter
2 c  Rice krispies
1/2 c  Corn syrup
1/2 c  Brown sugar
mix corn syrup, brown sugar and peanut butter. cook on stove on low
heat until mixture bubbles, add the rice krispies and mix well. put
in greased 8x8 pan and press down,when cooled icen with chocolate
icing.
