Title: Quinoa and Nut Dressing
Categories: Side dish, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
Finely chopped
2    Mint leaves, minced
1/3 c  Extra-virgin olive oil
3 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Black pepper
1 1/2 c  Cooked quinoa
2 tb Walnuts or pecans
Finely chopped
2 tb Hazelnuts
Finely chopped
2 tb Pistachio nuts
Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl and let stand until
ready to use as a stuffing or side dish.
