Title: Pineapple Chicken
Categories: Crockpot, Poultry, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM----------------RIVAL CROCK-POT SLOW COOKER---------------------
2 tb Mustard; Dijon-style
1 x  Soy sauce
1 ea Garlic clove; minced
6    Chicken breasts;skinned,bond
1 x  Pepper
1 x  Paprika
20 oz Pineapple tidbits; drained
Arrange chicken in crockpot. Sprinkle with pepper and paprika. Mix
soy sauce, pineapple and mustard together; pour over chicken.  Add
minced garlic. Cover and cook on LOW 7 to 9 hours or on HIGH 3 to 4
hours.

Per serving: 210 calories;  4 g fat;  73 mg cholesterol;  153 mg
sodium, 17% of calories from fat.
