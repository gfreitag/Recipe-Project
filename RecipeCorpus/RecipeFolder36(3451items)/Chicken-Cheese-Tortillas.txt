Title: Chicken-Cheese Tortillas
Categories: Main dish, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
Minced
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Salt
10    8" flour tortillas
2 c  Monterey jack cheese --
Grated
3 tb Olive oil
1 md Onion -- diced
1    Clove garlic -- minced
4    Skinless boneless chicken
Breast halves
4 oz Green chiles -- chopped
1    Jalapeno pepper -- seeded &
Heat oil over medium high heat in a large skillet.  Add onion and
garlic and cook, stirring occasionally for 7 to 10  minutes, until
onion begins to color.  Meanwhile, cut chicken into small cubes.  Add
chicken, green chilies and pepper to the skillet.  Sprinkle with
cumin; increase the heat to high.  Cook, stirring constantly, until
chicken is opaque, about 1 to 2 minutes.  Taste and add salt if
needed.  Remove from heat.  Preheat the oven to 300.  Warm tortillas.
Oil a 13 x 9 baking dish.  Divide chicken equally among tortillas,
spooning filling down the center of each tortilla; top with cheese.
Roll up to enclose filling; place in the oiled baking dish, seam side
down.  Cover baking dish with foil and bake until tortillas are
heated through and cheese is melted, about 10 minutes.
