Title: HIGH-PROTEIN DRINK
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
3    Pecans; broken
2 tb Raw wheat germ
2 tb Protein powder
1 c  Vanilla honey ice milk
1 c  Raw milk
1    Banana; peeled
- and cut in chunks
Combine ice milk, raw milk, banana, pecans, wheat germ, and protein
powder in blender container. Blend until smooth but still thick. Pour
into chilled glass.
