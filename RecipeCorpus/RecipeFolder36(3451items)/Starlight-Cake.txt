Title: Starlight Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Soft shortening
1 c  Milk
1 1/2 ts Flavoring
2 ea Eggs
Prepare the pan and use
-method as given in recipe
-above (Light Golden),
Except add all the milk at
-once.  Makes thin 9"
-layers.
2 1/8 c  Sifted Gold Medal Flour
Pour into prepared pans.  Bake.  Cool.  Finish with any desired
frosting.
