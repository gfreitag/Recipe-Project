Title: Apple Crisp
Categories: Desserts, Guestchefs
Yield: 4 servings
1    Egg, Beaten
3 ts Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 c  Water
6    Sliced Apples
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking Powder
1/4 ts Salt
Slice apples into greased baking dish.  To make topping:  Combine
ingredients, add to well beaten egg and mix until crumbly.  Spread
over apples.  Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar mixture.  Dot with
butter and pour 1/4 cup water over top.  Bake at 350Ã¸F for about 50
minutes or until top is browned and apples are done.  Serve with
cream, or vanilla ice cream.  From: Syd's Cookbook.
