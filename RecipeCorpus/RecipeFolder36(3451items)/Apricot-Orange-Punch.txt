Title: Apricot Orange Punch
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 18 servings
3 c  Chilled dry white wine
3 c  Chilled champagne or
-sparkling white wine
300 g  Pkg frozen raspberries,
-thawed
1 cn (355ml) frozen orange juice
-concentrate, thawed
4 c  Apricot nectar
1/2 c  Sugar, or to taste
1/2 c  Orange liqueur
4 c  Chilled soda water
Orange and lime slices

In large bowl, coming all ingredients.  Add raspberries and slices of
orange and lime last.  Makes about 18 cups.

Origin: Adapted from Appeal, Winter/95.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/95.
