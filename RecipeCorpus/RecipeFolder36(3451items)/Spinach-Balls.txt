Title: Spinach Balls
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Onion, minced
1 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Butter, melted
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1 pk Chopped spinach, cooked and
-drained
1 c  Dried bread crumbs
1 ea Egg, beaten
Combine all ingredients. Chill approximately 1/2 hour. Roll into
small ball and place on a lightly greased baking sheet. Bake at 325
degrees for 20-25 minutes.
