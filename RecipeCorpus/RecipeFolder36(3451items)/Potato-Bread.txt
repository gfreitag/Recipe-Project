Title: Potato Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
3 ts Chives -- chopped
3/4 c  Plain mashed potatoes
3/4 c  Reserved potato water --
Plus 1 tbsp
1 ts Lemon juice
2 1/4 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
3 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
2 tb Unsalted butter
Use basic cycle and do not use on timer because the water will be
absorbed.
