Title: Bananas Foster - TDN_94/02/20
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Banana liqueur
1/4 c  Cognac
Vanilla ice cream
2 tb Unsalted butter
4 tb Dark brown sugar
6    Bananas
1 pn Cinnamon
Combine butter and brown sugar in saucepan. Cook over medium heat
until carmelized. Cut bananas in quarters, split. Place bananas in
saucepan; cook until tender. Add cinnamon and liqueur. Blend well.
Heat cognac and pour over bananas. Do not stir. Ignite. Have ice
cream in serving dishes. Spoon flaming bananas over top.
