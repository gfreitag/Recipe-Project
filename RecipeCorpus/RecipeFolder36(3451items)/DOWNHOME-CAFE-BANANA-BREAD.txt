Title: DOWNHOME CAFE BANANA BREAD
Categories: Desserts, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs, beaten
4    Bananas, finely crushed
1 ts Lemon juice
Cream together butter and  sugar.   Mash bananas.  Add lemon juice to
mashed bananas.  Beat eggs.  Add eggs and crushed  bananas,  to the
creamed butter and sugar.  Combine well.  Sift together flour, baking
powder, and salt.  Add to creamed mixture.  Add vanilla. Pour into
greased loaf pan. Bake  at 350 degrees for 60 minutes. Keeps well,
refrigerated. Yield 1 Loaf.
