Title: Egg Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Water -- less 1 1/2 tbsp
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1 lg Egg
1    Egg yolk
1 ts Lemon juice
2 1/4 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
1 tb Dark brown sugar
4 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
2 tb Unsalted butter
Use basic cycle with crust on light setting.
