Title: Barley-Orzo Pilaf
Categories: Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Quick-cooking barley
1 tb Thyme, chopped or
1 ts Dried thyme
2 tb Scallion greens, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
1 ts Canola or vegetable oil
1    Onion(s), finely chopped
1    Garlic clove(s), minced
1/2 c  Orzo
3 c  Chicken stock
In a medium saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Add onions and saute
until softened, about 5 min. Add garlic and orzo and saute until orzo
is golden, 3-5 min. Add chicken stock and bring to a boil. Add barley
and thyme. Stir once, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 10
min. Turn off heat and let stand for 5 min to absorb any remaining
liquid. Stir in scallions. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
