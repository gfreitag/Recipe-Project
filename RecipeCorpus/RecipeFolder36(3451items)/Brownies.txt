Title: Brownies
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
1/3 c  Half and half
1 pk 20 1/2 oz brownie mix
1    Egg
1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Butter melted
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2/3 c  Chopped pecans
1 pk 3 1/2 oz instant vanilla
Pudding
Put butter in 8 inch baking dish.  Sprinkle with sugar and pecans and
2 tb of dry pudding mix.  Pour half and half over all.  I seperate
bowl combine remaining ingredients and spoon into baking dish.
Microwave on high 10 to 12 minutes rotating dish 3 times during
cooking From 365 microwave recipes calendar 1990
