Title: Bon Secour Lobster Bisque DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Bay leaf
1/3 c  Butter, melted
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper, freshly ground
1 ds Tabasco
1 1/2 c  Lobster, cooked
2 c  Milk
2 c  Half & Half
1 ea Onion slice
2 ea Celery ribs
1 ts Parsley
Scald milk and half & half with onion, celery, parsley
and bay leaf. Strain.  In a large saucepan, stir
together melted butter, flour, and seasonings.  Slowly
stir in milk.  Place over low heat and stir until
slightly thickened.  Add lobster.  Top with crisp
croutons, if desired. From "The Beach Cookbook"
formatted by Mary Bowles.
