Title: Cretons
Categories: Appetizers, Meats
Yield: 3 quarts
2 tb Ground cinnamon
1 ts Pepper
2 ts Ground cloves
2 ts Ground allspice
1 c  Unseasoned dry bread crumbs
3 lb Leaf lard, cut in small
-pieces
6 lb Lean pork, cut in pieces
6 lg Onions, cut in pieces
2 tb Salt
Method: In heavy pan, cook lard till fat separates, leaving crisp
cracklings; drain and discard fat.

Grind cracklings with pork and onions. Mix in seasonings and cook,
stirring occasionally, till meat is well done. Stir in crumbs. Pack
into small molds that have been rinsed in cold water. Refrigerate 12
hours. Slice and serve cold between slices of French bread.
