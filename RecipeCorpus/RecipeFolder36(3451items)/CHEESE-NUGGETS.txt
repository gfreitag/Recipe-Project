Title: CHEESE NUGGETS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 36 servings
2 c  Grated sharp cheddar cheese
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  All-purpose flour
Mix butter and flour together well. Add cheese and work in well. Shape
into small marble-sized balls. Place on ungreased baking sheet. Bake
in 375F (190C) oven for about 10 minutes. Makes 36.
