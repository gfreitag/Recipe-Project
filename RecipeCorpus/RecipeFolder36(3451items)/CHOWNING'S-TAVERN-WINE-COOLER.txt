Title: CHOWNING'S TAVERN WINE COOLER
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 6 servings
Sprig of mint
Maraschino cherry
1    Serving
3/4 c  Lemonade
1/4 c  Dry red wine
This makes a colorful as well as refreshing drink if the liquids are
not mixed together.  Pour the lemonade over crushed ice, then add the
red wine. Garnish with a sprig of mint and a cherry. Good for hot
days.
