Title: Finadene Sauce I
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Hot/spicy
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Chopped onions
10 ea To 12 medium hot peppers
-(boonie peppers)
1/3 c  Soy sauce
Mash hot peppers to obtain the hot taste. Depending on the 'hotness'
desired mash a few or all of them.
This will keep in the refrigerator. Some local ladies prefer to add
the onions fresh, just before serving. Goes well with most everything.
Yield 1 scant cup.
