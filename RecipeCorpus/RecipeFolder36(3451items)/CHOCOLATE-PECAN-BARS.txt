Title: CHOCOLATE PECAN BARS
Categories: Desserts, Holiday, Chocolate
Yield: 1 1ervings
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1 c  Softened butter or marg.
3 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
4    Eggs slightly beaten
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
2 1/4 c  Chopped pecans
6    Sq. chopped semi-sweet
Chocolate
1 1/2 c  Corn syrup
1 1/2 c  Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------DRIZZLE-------------------------------
2    Sq. semi-sweet chocolate
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In large bowl mix at medium speed flour, sugar, and margarine
:
until mixture resembles crust crumbs.  Press firmly into a greased
foil-lined 15 by 10 inch cookie sheet with sides.  Bake for 20
minutes. Filling:
Put 6 squares of chocolate with the corn syrup in a saucepan
:
and melt over low heat, stirring until chocolate melts.  Remove from
heat and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla until blended.  Stir in
pecans.  Pour filling over hot crust, and spread eveningly.  Bake for
30 minutes or until filling is firm around edges.  It should be
slightly soft in the middle.  Cool in pan on a rack.
melt chocolate and drizzle over filling.  Chill and then cut
:
into bars.
