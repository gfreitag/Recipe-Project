Title: Drop Sugar Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 dozen cook
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1 3/4 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Sugar
2    Egg
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 ts Almond extract
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1] Cream butter, sugar, and eggs until well blended. Add almond &
vanilla extract.

2] Mix together flur, baking powder, baking soda, nutmeg, cinnomon, &
salt. Stir into first mixture.

3] Drop by heaping teaspoonfulls about 2 inches apart onto lightly
greased baking sheets. [Cookies will spread some.]

4] Bake at 375#F for 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown. Remove at
once to wire racks to cool. Store in airtight container.

5] When cool, decorate with favorite frosting, sprinkles, or whatever
you may wish.
