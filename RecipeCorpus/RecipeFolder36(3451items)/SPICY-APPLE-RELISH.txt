Title: SPICY APPLE RELISH
Categories: Relishes, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Honey
1 c  Chili sauce
4 md Apples
2 tb Butter
Cut unpeeled apples in wedges, removing cores.

Melt butter in skillet, add honey and chili sauce, mix thoroughly and
heat.  Add apples, coating with chili sauce mixture and cook till
slightly soft, (10-12 minutes) turning occasionally to baste fruit.

Serve hot or cold as meat relish and garnish.
