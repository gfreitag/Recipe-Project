Title: Moravian Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday, Herbs/spice
Yield: 72 servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Ground ginger
4 1/2 c  Sifted all purpose flour
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
MMMMM----------------------------ADD---------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Softened butter or margarine
1/2 c  Lard, softened
1 c  Firmly packed light brown
-sugar
Mix well, and then cut in
MMMMM-----------------------GRADUALLY ADD----------------------------
1/2 ts Cider vinegar
1 1/2 c  Light molasses
Thin, crisp rolled cookies with a good ginger and molasses flavour.

Sift together into bowl and reserve:

Mix thoroughly and chill dough for several hours or until firm enough
to roll.  Using a small amount of dough at a time, roll onto floured
board to 1/8 inch thickness.  Cut with floured fancy cookie cutter
and place on greased cookies sheets.  Bake in a preheated 350 F oven
for about 10 minutes, or until browned.  Cool on wire racks and
decorate with frosting.  Makes about 72 cookies.  Store in airtight
container. Cookies may be frozen.

Origin: Adapted from the Woman's Day Encyclopedia of Cooker, Volume 3.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/95.
