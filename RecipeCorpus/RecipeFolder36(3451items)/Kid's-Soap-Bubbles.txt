Title: Kid's Soap Bubbles
Categories: Kids
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Glycerin
1 1/2 ts White sugar
1/2 c  Liquid dishwashing soap
2 1/2 c  Water
Place all ingredients into a container.  Stir.  Using a twist tie,
shape to form a bubble maker.  Makes 3 cups.
Origin: Adapted from Jean Pare's Kid's cooking.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/95.
