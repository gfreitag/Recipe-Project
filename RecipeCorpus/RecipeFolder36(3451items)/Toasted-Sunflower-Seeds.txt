Title: Toasted Sunflower Seeds
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
Soy or tamari sauce
Sunflower seeds
Sesame seeds  (optional)
Place seeds in baking pan.  Sprinkle with soy sauce.  Bake in slow
oven, stirring occasionally.  These are done when they are toasty
brown and they are delicious.  A healthy snack.
