Title: Orange Ham Glaze
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Orange juice
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1 c  Karo pancake syrup
1 ts Grated orange rind
In 1-quart saucepan stir together all ingredients. Bring to boil over
medium
heat and boil 5 minutes. Score ham, place in foil lined pan. Brush
glaze on ham during last hour of baking.
