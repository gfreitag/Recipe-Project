Title: Jeanne's Beans
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Ketchup
1 ts Mustard
1 ts Horseradish
2 cn Pork And Beans
1/3 c  Brown Sugar
2 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1 tb Barbecue Sauce
Mix all ingredients together and put in a greased casserole. Bake
covered at 400F for 60 minutes. Uncover and cook at 350F for 30
minutes.
