Title: Nfld. Egg Nog
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
6    Eggs
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Bottle of Newfie Screech
1 qt Milk
1 cn Evaporated milk
Seperate eggs and beat yolks with 1/4 c of sugar, and whites with
1/4 c of sugar.  In large container, pour 1 bottle of Newfoundland
Screech, 1 qt of milk, 1 tin of evaporated milk, and egg yolk
mixture. Beat for about two minutes then top with egg whites.
Sprinkle with nut-meg.
