Title: Low-Salt Curried Baked Fish - SL 1/87
Categories: Low cal/fat, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Chablis or other dry white
-wine
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Dried whole dillweed
1/2 ts Curry powder
Vegetable cooking spray
8 ea 4-oz orange roughy fillets
-(or other lean white fish)
1/2 c  Low-salt reduced calorie
-mayonnaise
Cooking time: 30 minutes
Coat a broiling rack with cooking spray. Place rack in a shallow
baking pan; arrange fillets on rack.
Combine remaining ingredients; spread over fillets. Bake at 350
degrees for 25 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a
fork. Yield: 8 servings.

147 calories, 22.6g protein, 5.1g fat, 1.3g carbohydrate, 67mg
cholesterol, 53.5mg sodium, 245mg calcium per *3-oz* serving.

Recipe from Alice McNamara in January, 1987 "Southern Living".
