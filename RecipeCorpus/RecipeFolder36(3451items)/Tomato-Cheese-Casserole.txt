Title: Tomato Cheese Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Cheese/eggs, Tomatoes
Yield: 3 servings
3/4 c  Sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1/4 ts Oregano
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 tb Sugar
3/4 c  Pepperide Farm stuffing
1 cn Tomatoes, No. 2 1/2 size
1/2 tb Margarine
1/2 ts Garlic salt
Partially drain can of tomatoes.  Mix all ingredients and top with
cheddar cheese.  Bake for about 40 minutes at 350 degrees.
