Title: Guam Barbecued Chicken Marinade
Categories: Guam, Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
2 cl Garlic; minced (2 ts)
1/8 ts Black pepper
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1 sm Onion; minced
Mix together lemon juice, soy sauce, onion, garlic and pepper. Pour
marinade over chicken in a dish and marinate, covered and
refrigerated, at least one hour.

Source: Peter Cruz Benavente, Oregonian Food Day, Nov 1994
