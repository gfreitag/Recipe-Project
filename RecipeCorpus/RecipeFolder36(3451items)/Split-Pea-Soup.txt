Title: Split Pea Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Marjoram
1 c  Celery, diced
1 c  Carrots, sliced
1 ts Parsley flakes
1 lb Split peas
2 qt Water
1    Meaty ham bone
1 1/2 c  Onions, sliced
1/2 ts Pepper
Salt to taste
Cover peas with water and soak overnight, or boil gently 2 minutes and
soak for 1 hour.  Drain.  Add 2 quarts of water, ham bone, onion and
seasonings.  Bring to a boil; cover and reduce heat to simmer for 2
hours, stirring occasionally.  Remove bone, but off meat.  Add
remaining ingredients.  Cook slowly for 45 minutes.  Salt to taste.
