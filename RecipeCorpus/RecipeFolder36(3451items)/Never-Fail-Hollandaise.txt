Title: Never Fail Hollandaise
Categories: Low cal/fat, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 recipe
1    Salt
1    Cayenne
2 tb Unsalted butter
1 tb Cornstarch
2 tb Lemon juice
-freshly squeezed
In the top of a double boiler, slowly whisk 3/4 cup water into the
cornstarch. Stir in the lemon juice, salt and cayenne to taste. Place
the top over simmering water and cook, stirring constantly, until
slightly thickened, 2 to 3 minutes.

Remove the top from the heat. Whisk in the egg yolks, one at a time,
and one tablespoon of the butter. Replace over the water and cook
until slightly thickened, about three minutes, stirring constantly.

When ready to serve, whisk in the remaining butter; taste and correct
the seasonings.

From: The Great Food Cookbook
