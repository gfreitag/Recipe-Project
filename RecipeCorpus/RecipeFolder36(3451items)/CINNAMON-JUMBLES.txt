Title: CINNAMON JUMBLES
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 batch
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sugar/cinnamon mixture
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg
3/4 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Vanilla
Mix first three ingredients then stir in buttermilk and vanilla.
Blend in flour, soda, and salt.  Chill dough.  Heat oven to 400 F.
drop dough onto cookie sheet and sprinkle with sugar/cinnamon.  Bake
till light brown.
