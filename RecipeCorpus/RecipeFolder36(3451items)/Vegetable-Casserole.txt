Title: Vegetable Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Mushroom soup
1 cn Tomato soup
1 c  Celery, diced
Salt and pepper
1 1/2 lb Ground beef, browned
1/2 pk Noodles
Onion, to taste
3/4 pk Vegetables, frozen
Put into a greased casserole.  Sprinkle with cracker crumbs.  Dot with
butter plus bread croutons.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
