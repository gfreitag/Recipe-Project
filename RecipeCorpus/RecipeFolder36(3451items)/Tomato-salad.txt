Title: Tomato salad
Categories: Salads, Middle east
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Lemon
1/4 c  Olive oil
6 ea Tomatoes, finely diced
3 ea Cucumbers, peeled, diced
2 bn Green onions, chopped
Finely dice tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions.  Squeeze juice from
lemon, add to salad.  Add olive oil.  Allow to sit at room
temperature for 30 minutes before serving for best flavor.  Salt and
pepper may be added to taste.

Barghouti family recipe.
