Title: CHICKEN AND BLACK BEAN SALAD
Categories: Poultry, Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1    Sweet yellow pepper; chop
2    Tomatoes, coarse chop
1/4 c  Coriander; chop, fresh
30 oz Black beans; drain,rinse
3 c  Chicken; cooked, cubed
6    Green onions; sliced
1    Sweet red pepper; chop
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1    Jalapeno pepper; minced
1 ts Grated lime rind
1/4 c  Lime juice
1 cl Garlic; minced
Use canned black beans (15oz (425mL) per can)
In large bowl, gently stir together black beans, chicken, onions, red
and yellow peppers and tomatoes.
Dressing: in small bowl, whisk together jalapeno pepper, lime rind and
juice, garlic, salt and pepper; gradually whisk in oil. Pour over
salad; add coriander and toss gently.
