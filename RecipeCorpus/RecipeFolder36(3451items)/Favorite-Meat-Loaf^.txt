Title: Favorite Meat Loaf^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
3/4 ts Poultry seasoning or sage
1/8 ts Pepper
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
2    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Milk
2/3 c  Finely crushed butter
Flavored crackers
1/2 c  Chopped onion
MMMMM----------------------OPTIONAL GARNISH---------------------------
Onion rings
Green pepper rings
Preheat oven to 350F.

Combine all ingredients. Bake until no pink remains.
