Title: Kay Baker's Fast Clam Chowder
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 batch
4 c  Water
2 ts Salt
2 tb Cornstarch
1    13-oz can evaporated milk
2 tb Butter
1    20-oz can minced clams
Pepper to taste
5    Diced potatoes
1 c  Onion; chopped
Melt the butter in a saucepan.  Add the onions and saute lightly.
Drain the liquid from the canned clams and add to the saucepan, but
reserve clam meat.  Add the water, salt, pepper, and cornstarch and
stir well.  Add the potatoes.  Cover the pot and cook over low heat
10 minutes.  Uncover, and add evaporated milk and clam meat.  Cook
and stir until mixture simmers. Serve hot.
