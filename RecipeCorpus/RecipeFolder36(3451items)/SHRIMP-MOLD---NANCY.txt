Title: SHRIMP MOLD - NANCY : PHOENIX BRIDGE DIRECTOR
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
2 cn Shrimp
1 c  Mayo
ds Tabasco, garlic, pepper
ds Worchestershire sauce
1 cn Tomato soup
9 oz Cream cheese
1 1/2    Envelopes Knox gelatin
1/3 c  Water
1/4 c  Onion, celery, grn. pepper
Melt soup and cream cheese together.
Dissolve gelatin in 1/3 c water and add to above.
Add vegetables (diced), and shrimp, mayo and spices and flavorings.
Mold and chill.  Tupperware cereal bowl does nicely.
Serve with crackers.
