Title: German Tongue Salad #1 - Zungensalat
Categories: Aust/german, Appetizers, Meats, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Knob celery; cooked, diced
Salt and pepper
1 pn Powdered mustard
1 c  Mayonnaise
Oil and vinegar
2 c  Smoked tongue; cooked,
-julienned
1 c  Macaroni; cooked
1 c  Carrot; cooked, diced
1/2 c  Green peas; cooked
Toss ingredients with seasonings and mix with mayo plus just a little
oil and vinegar to sharpen the flavor of the dressing. Chill 1 hour
and serve with Westphalian rye bread.

Variations: When smoked salami sausage is substituted for tongue, it
is called Danischer salad- Danish Salad.

From: The German Cookbook by Mimi Sheraton, 1965.
