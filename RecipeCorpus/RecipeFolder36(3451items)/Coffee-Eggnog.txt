Title: Coffee Eggnog
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Beverages, Alcohol, Chocolate
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Grated nutmeg
1/4 c  Kahlua (optional)
4 c  Eggnog
4 c  Cold strong coffee
1/2 c  Sugar, or to taste
GARNISH: 1/2 cup shaved bittersweet and white chocolate, nutmeg

Mix together the eggnog, coffee, sugar and nutmeg.  Blend in blender
and serve with additional nutmeg on top, or garnish.  Makes about 8
cups.

Origin: Adapted from Appeal, Winter/95.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/95.
