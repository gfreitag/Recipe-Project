Title: Easy Pasta Salad (Vegan)
Categories: Salads, Pasta/rice, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Red pepper, chopped
1    Or 2 green onions, thinly
-sliced
1    Bottle Fatfree Ital Dressing
1 lb Tri-colored pasta, corkscrew
1    Bunch broccoli
2    Carrots, peeled and sliced
1    Cucumber, peeled, halved,
-and sliced
Cook pasta, drain and rinse with cold water.  Cut broccoli into
florets and cook in microwave for a minute or so (or blanch in some
hot water). Rinse with cold water.

Put pasta, broccoli and the rest of veggies in a big bowl.  Chill
well. Just before serving, pour dressing over pasta and vegetable and
stir well. You can add whatever vegetable you'd like, but don't skimp
on the red pepper.  It adds a wonderful sweetness.
