Title: CABBAGE SALAD WITH CREAM DRESSING
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Heavy cream
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 md Cabbage; thinly sliced
3 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
Pepper
Combine sugar, salt, pepper and cream. Stir until sugar and salt
dissolve. Add vinegar. Arrange cabbage slices in salad bowl, pour
dressing over cabbage.
