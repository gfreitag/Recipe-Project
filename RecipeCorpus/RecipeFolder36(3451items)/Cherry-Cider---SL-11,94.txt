Title: Cherry Cider - SL 11/94
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 2 quarts
3 oz Package cherry-flavored
-gelatin
2 qt Apple cider
1 ea 3-inch cinnamon stick
Combine apple cider and cinnamon stick in a saucepan; bring to a
boil. Reduce heat, and simmer 15 minutes.  Add gelatine, stirring 2
minutes or until gelatine dissolves.   Serve beverage hot.   Yield: 2
quarts.
