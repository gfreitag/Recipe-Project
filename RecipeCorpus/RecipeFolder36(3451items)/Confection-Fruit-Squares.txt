Title: Confection Fruit Squares
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 16 pieces
8 oz S&W Glace Cake Mix (1 con-
-tainer)
3 1/2 oz S&W Natural Glace Pineapple
-Wedges (1 container)
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  S&W Shelled Walnuts; chopped
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.  In medium bowl, beat eggs, sugar and
vanilla until light and fluffy.  Stir in flour and HALF each of
walnuts, glace cake mix and pineapple wedges (batter will be stiff).
Spoon batter into greased 9-inch square baking dish, spreading dough
evenly. Combine remaining walnuts and glace fruit; sprinkle mixture
over top and press lightly. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until top has a
firm, dull crust. Cool in pan; cut into squares.  Makes 16 pieces.

Nutritional Information Per Serving (1 square):  170 calories; 2g
protein; 35g carbo; 1g fiber; 3g fat; 25mg chol; 50mg sodium.

Source:  S&W advertisement in Sunday coupon circular.
