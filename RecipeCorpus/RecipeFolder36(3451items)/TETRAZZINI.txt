Title: TETRAZZINI
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Non fat dry milk
1 3/4 c  Chicken broth
1 c  Grated Cheddar cheesed
2 c  Diced cooked chicken
4 oz. noodles
1/4 c  Butter
1/3 c  Chopped green pepper
1/3 c  Chopped onion
4 oz. can of mushrooms
4 tb Cornstarch
1/4 ts Pepper
Prepare noodles according to package directions.  Saute peppers,
onions and mushrooms in butter.  Add cornstarch, salt and pepper; add
chicken broth all at once; add dry milk; cook until thick and bubbly.
Combine meat and cheese; add to sauce.  Turn into 1 1/2 quart
casserole; bake until cheese metls (about 15 minutes).  Serve over
noodles.  Bake in oven at 350 degrees.
