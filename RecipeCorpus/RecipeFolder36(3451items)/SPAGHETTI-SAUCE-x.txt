Fry onions in oil and butter (not too much), put in ripe tomatoes,
garlic and 2 mashed bananas.  Tomato paste a little mixed herbs, bay
leaf, preferrably very thin strips of meat instead of mince.  When
nearly cooked red wine.  Leave for at least 1 day.
