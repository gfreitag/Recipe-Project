Title: Rum Cream Liqueur
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 recipe
4 ts Instant coffee powder
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/4 ts Coconut extract
14 oz Can sweetened condensed
-milk
1 c  Dark rum
1 c  Heavy cream
1/4 c  Chocolate-flavored syrup
Combine all ingredients in blender or food processor.
Whirl at high speed until well blended and smooth.
Serve immediately over ice. Or cover tightly and store
in refrigerator up to 2 weeks. Stir before serving.
