Title: Coconut Fudge
Categories: Candies
Yield: 64 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 c  Flaked coconut
1/2 ts Coconut extract
Sliced almonds (optional)
6 oz Cream cheese, softened
5 1/4 c  Powdered sugar
1 c  Chopped blanched almonds
Butter and 8 inch square pan; set aside.  Beat cream cheese and enough
powdered sugar in a medium bowl with electric mixer to make a stiff,
but not dry mixture.  Stir in chopped almonds, coconut and coconut
extract. Press mixture into prepared pan.  Arrange sliced almonds on
top; gently press into fudge.  Score fudge into squares with knife.
Refrigerate until firm.  Cut into squares.  Store in refrigerator.
