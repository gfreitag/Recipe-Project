Title: Orange Pasta
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
1    6 oz can frozen orange juice
-concentrate, thawed
1 ts Olive oil or cooking oil
3 1/3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Finely shredded orange peel
2    Beaten eggs
Stir together 3 cups of the flour and orange peel.  Combine eggs,
concentrate, and olive oil; add to flour.  Mix well.  Sprinkle
kneading surface with the remaining 1/3 cup flour.  Knead for 10
minutes, cover and let rest for 10 minutes.  Roll out and cut into
desired shapes.  Makes 1-1/2
pounds.

Origin:  Better Homes and Gardens  Pasta Book
Shared by:  Sharon Stevens  Oct/91
