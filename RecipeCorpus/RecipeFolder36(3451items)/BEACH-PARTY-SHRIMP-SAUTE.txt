Title: BEACH PARTY SHRIMP SAUTE
Categories: Appetizers, Main dish, Tex/mexican, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Onion; thinly sliced
1 tb Garlic; mashed
2    Jalapeno peppers; sliced (se
Juice of 2 lemons or 4 limes
1 c  Oil
2 lb Medium shrimp; about 60, in
1 tb Salt
1 ts Pepper; coarsely ground
1 tb Oregano; crushed
Recipe by: Mexican Family Cooking
In large pan or wok over a camp fire (if at the beach) heat oil.  Add
washe unshelled shrimps and stir-fry for 3 minutes.  Add seasonings
and remainder ingredients.  Just before serving add the freshly
squeezed lemon or lime juice.
