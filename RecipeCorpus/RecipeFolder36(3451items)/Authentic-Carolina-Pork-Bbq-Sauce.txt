Title: Authentic Carolina Pork Bbq Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 gallon  1ga Vinegar
6oz Texas Pete
10 oz Worcestershire Sauce
11/4 oz Red pepper flakes, crushed
Mustard, optional

Combine all ingredients. Mustard is optional if you like it a bit
thicker.

This can be used with a pork roast. Slow roast pork and then chop it.
Add sauce.
