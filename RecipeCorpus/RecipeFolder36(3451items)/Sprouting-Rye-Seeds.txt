Title: Sprouting Rye Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Rye seed
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1 cup in quart jar, or tray sprout which will yield about 2 to 3
cups of sprouts. Soak for 12 hours.

Rinse twice daily for 2 to 3 days.

Sprouts will be the length of the seeds. Use in granola, salads, baked
goods.
