Title: Fruit Smoothie
Categories: Beverages, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Vanilla frozen yogurt
1 1/2 c  Lowfat milk
-reduced cholesterol
1 c  Papaya
1/2 c  Guava
1/2 c  Kiwi
Remove the pulp from the papayas, guavas and kiwi.  Place the fruit
in a freezer-safe bowl, cover and freeze.  Once frozen, place the
fruit in a blender and process until smooth.  Add the frozen yogurt
and milk and process just until smooth.  Serve in dessert glasses.
