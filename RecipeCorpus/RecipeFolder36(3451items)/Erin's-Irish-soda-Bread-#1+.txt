Title: Erin's Irish soda Bread #1+
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 loaf
2 tb Brown sugar or honey
1 tb Wheat germ
2 c  Buttermilk, divided
4 c  Flour OR 2 c flour and 2 c
-oatmeal or bran
3/4 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
Mix dry ingredients (with exception of soda) Stir soda into 1 of the
cups of buttermilk. Add to dry ingredients along with lightly beaten
egg and the other cup of buttermilk. Form into bun or loaf. Place in
butter greased baking dish. slit top. Bake in 350 degree oven for 1
1/4 hours.

Given to me by my very Irish friend Erin Moir
