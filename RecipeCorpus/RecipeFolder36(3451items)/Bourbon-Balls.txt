Title: Bourbon Balls
Categories: Candies, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 tb White Karo syrup
2 oz Bourbon
1 c  Vanilla wafer crumbs
1 c  Finely chopped pecans
1 c  Powdered sugar
Mix syrup with bourbon well.  Blend into dry ingredients.  Form into
balls and shape like pecans.  Roll in powdered sugar.  Keep in closed
container. Randy Rigg
