Title: Fillets Stuffed with Cheese
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
-fillets cut in half
1 lg Ripe tomato, sliced
12 sl Montary jack Cheese
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/3 c  Butter
1 1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 1/2 ts Basil leaves
2 tb Chopped green onions
6    Fresh sole or orange roughy
Heat oven to 350F.  In 13x9 baking pan melt butter.
Stir in garlic and basil and grn onions. Dip both
sides of fillet halves into seasoned butter. Set half
of fillets aside & other half place in pan...layer
with 2-3 slice cheese and 1 slice tomatoe. Cover with
remaining fillet half & sprinkle w/parm cheese. Bake
for 25-35 min. or until fish flakes.
