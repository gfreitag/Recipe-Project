Title: MY FAVORITE BANANA BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 3 servings
2    Egg
3/8 c  Oil
1 1/2 c  Bananas
3/4 c  Milk
1 1/8 c  Sugar
2 1/2 c  Flour
3/4 ts Baking soda
2 1/4 ts Baking powder
1 1/8 ts Salt
3 ts Orange rind, grated
Sift first 5 ingredients, combine remaining ingredients, stir into
dry mix, bake at 350 for 50 minutes in a greased lined loaf pan.
