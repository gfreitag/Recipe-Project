Title: Southern Crab Royal DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
1 ds Nutmeg
1/4 ts Dry mustard
2 ea Egg yolks, beaten
1 lb Crabmeat
8 ea Patty shells, baked
1 x  Mushrooms & parsley/ garnish
1 1/2 tb Onion, minced
2 tb Green pepper, finely chopped
3 tb Margarine
2 tb Flour
1 ts Salt
2 c  Half & Half
Saute onion and green pepper in margarine until
tender.  Add flour and salt and cook 2 minutes.
Gradually stir in Half & Half and stir until smooth.
Add nutmeg and mustard.  Gradually add egg yolks,
stirring constantly.  Add drained, flaked crabmeat.
Cook 5 minutes.  Serve in individual baked patty
shells and garnish with fresh mushrooms and parsley.
From "The Southern Heritage Company's Coming!
Cookbook" formatted by Mary Dishongh Bowles.
