Title: Diablo Chicken Chili
Categories: Chili, Vegetables, Meats, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1    To 2 Chopped jalapeno
-peppers
1/2    Stick Butter
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
4 ts To 5 ts Chili powder
4 ts Cumin
2    Cloves Garlic, minced
2 tb Brown sugar
1 cn Kidney beans
1 cn Black beans
1 lb To 2 lb Boneless chicken
-breasts
2 md Onions, chopped
2    Green peppers, chopped
2    Chopped carrots
1 pk Fresh mushrooms (8 oz.)
1 cn Tomatoes (28 oz.)
1 cn Tomato puree (28 oz.)
Brown chicken breasts in hot oil, in large pot with 1 clove garlic, 1
teaspoon chili powder and 1 teaspoon cumin. Set aside. Cube when
cool. In the same pot, saute, green peppers, onions remaining garlic,
mushrooms and carrots in 1/2 stick of butter with 2 teaspoons of
chili powder and 2 teaspoons cumin. Add remaining ingredients and
rest of spices. Simmer for 3 hours.
