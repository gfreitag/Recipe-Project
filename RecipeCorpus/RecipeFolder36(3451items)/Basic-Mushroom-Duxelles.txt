Title: Basic Mushroom Duxelles
Categories: Information, Vegetables
Yield: 1 cup
(by hand)
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Soy sauce
3 tb Butter
2/3 c  Onion(s), chopped
2 c  Button mushrooms
Finely chopped
Melt the butter in a medium saute pan over medium heat. Add the
onion, and saute until it is translucent, about 3 min. Add the
mushrooms, salt, sugar, and soy sauce and continue to stir slowly.
The mushrooms will begin to give off liquid. Continue to stir until
all the liquid has evaporated and the mixture resembles a paste, 8-12
min. Remove from the heat, let cool, and pack in a jar. Refrigerate
or freeze.
