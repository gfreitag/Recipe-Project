Title: BEST ANTIPASTO
Categories: Appetizers, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Pickling onions or 3 16oz. j
3 lb Mushrooms or 5 10oz.tins mus
2 lb Green peppers
5    16oz. tins ripe olives
5    11oz. bottles of ketchup
2    13oz. large tins anchovies
3/4 c  Vinegar
2 lb Cucumbers or 2 24oz.dill pic
3 lb Cawliflower
2 lb Green beans or 4 15oz. tins
2 lb Red peppers
5    8oz. tins tuna fish
5    16oz. jars stuffed olives
3/4 c  Olive oil
Boil cawliflower 3 minutes. Drain and set aside. Peel pickling
onions, cut cucumbers in slices and boil green beans until tender.
Set all these vegetables aside.
In large canning kettle, fry sliced mushrooms in the oil, add
ketchup, cut green beans and red peppers. Simmer 10-15 minutes.
Add all the vegetables, olives and remaining ingredients (separate
anchovies and break up tuna), simmer for another 12 minutes.
