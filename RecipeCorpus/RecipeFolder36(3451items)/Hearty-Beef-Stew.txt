Title: Hearty Beef Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Meats, Crockpot
Yield: 6 servings
2 cn Beef broth (undiluted)
3/4 c  Quick cooking tapioca
2    Bay leaves
-salt and pepper to taste
5 md Potatoes
2 lb Stew beef, cut in 1 inch
-cubes
5 md Carrots cut in 1 inch
-pieces
1 lg Onion cut in chunks
3    Stalks of celery, sliced
TO BE ADDED LAST 30 MINS RIGHT BEFORE SERVING
5 medium. Potatoes peeled and cut in 1 inch cubes
Cooked until just done and add to stew

Trim all fat from meat.  Put all ingredients (except potatoes) in
CROCKPOT.  Mix thoroughly. Cover and cook on LOW for 12 hours. or HIGH
5-6 hours.

Add cooked potatoes, stir with cooked stew, and let sit for
about 30 minutes for flavor to merge.

Serves 6 - 8

Depending on how much juice is made by meat and vegetables, more
water might be needed if gravy becomes too thick.
