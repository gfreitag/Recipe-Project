Title: QUICK AND EASY PEANUT BUTTER FUDGE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Crunchly peanut butter
1 ts Vanilla
4 c  Sugar
1 c  Evaporated milk
1 tb Butter
Bring sugar, milk and butter to a rolling boil in a heavy saucepan.
Boil 1 minute.  Remove from heat and quickly add peanut butter and
vanilla.  Mix thoroughly.  Spread/press into 10 x 13 x 1" pan.  Let
cool slightly, but cut into pieces while still warm, before it's
entirely set.

Optional:  Add 6 oz chocolate chips along with peanut butter.
