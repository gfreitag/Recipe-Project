Title: Crab & Cheese Salad^
Categories: Salads, Appetizers, Holiday, Fish/seafoo, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Finely chopped green bell
Pepper
2    Hard boiled eggs
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  French dressing or cocktail
Sauce
Salt to taste
1 pk (6oz) frozen crabmeat
-=OR=-
1 cn (6oz) crab, drained
1/2 c  (2oz) Swiss cheese,
Shredded
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
If using frozen crab, drain about half the liquid off.

Combine all ingredients; chill and serve.
