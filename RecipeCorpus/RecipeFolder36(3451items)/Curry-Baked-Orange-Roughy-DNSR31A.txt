Title: Curry Baked Orange Roughy DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Curry powder
4 ea Orange roughy fillets
30 ea Round buttery crackers/crush
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
3/4 ts Prepared mustard
3/4 ts Lemon juice
3/4 ts Hot sauce
3/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
Combine all ingredients except fillets and crackers.
Brush over top of fillets.  Dredge both sides of
fillets in cracker crumbs.  Place spiced side up in
greased baking dish.  Bake at 400 degrees for 20
minutes or until fish flakes easily.  Serve at once.
