Title: Sprouting Triticale Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar
1 c  Triticale seeds
Use 1 cup in a quart jar which will yield about 2 to 3 cups of
sprouts. Soak for 12 hours.

Rinse twice daily for 2 to 3 days.

The sprouts will be the length of the seed. Use in granola, salads,
soups and baked goods.
