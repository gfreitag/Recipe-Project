Title: Cinnamon Ornaments
Categories: Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
Applesauce
3/4    -1 Cup
4 1/8 oz Bottle Of Ground Cinnamon
Mix 3/4-1 cup of applesauce with 4 1/8 oz bottle ground cinnamon to
form stiff dough.  Roll to 1/4".  Cut with cookie cutters.  Make hole
for ribbon.  Let dry several days, turning  occasionally.
