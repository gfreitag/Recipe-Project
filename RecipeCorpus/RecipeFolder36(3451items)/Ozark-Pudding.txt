Title: Ozark Pudding
Categories: Puddings
Yield: 3 servings
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Chopped apples
1    Egg
3/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Flour
1 1/4 ts Baking powder
Beat egg; gradually add sugar and beat until smooth.  Stir in flour,
sifted with baking powder; add vanilla, nuts and apples and mix well.
Bake in a shallow pie pan in a 350 degree oven for 30 to 35 minutes.
Cut as pie and serve with whipped cream.
