Title: Pineapple Pasta
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz To 6 oz can frozen pineapple
-juice concentrate, thawed
1 ts Olive oil or cooking oil
3 1/3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
2    Beaten eggs
In a mixing bowl, stir together 3 cups of the flour and salt.  Combine
eggs,
pineapple juice concentrate, and oil; add to flour.  Mix well.
Sprinkle kneading surface with the remaining 1/3 cup flour.  Knead
for 10 minutes, cover and let rest for 10 minutes.  Roll out and cut
into desired shapes. Makes 1-1/2 pounds.

Origin:  Better Homes and Gardens Pasta Book
Shared by:  Sharon Stevens  Oct/91
