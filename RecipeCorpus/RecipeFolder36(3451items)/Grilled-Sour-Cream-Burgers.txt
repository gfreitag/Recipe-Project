Title: Grilled Sour Cream Burgers
Categories: Sandwich, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Ground beef
4 ea Hamburger buns; buttered and
-toasted
Commercial sour cream/French
-onion dip (optional)
1/2 c  Commercial sour cream/French
-onion dip
3 tb Fine dry breadcrumbs
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
Combine first 4 ingredients in a bowl; mix well.  Add ground beef;
stir until blended.  Shape into 4 patties about 1/2-inch thick. Grill
over medium coals 6 to 8 minutes on each side.  Serve on buns with
additional sour cream dip, if desired.  Yield: 4 servings.
