Title: Crab with Curried Rice DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM----------------------------CRAB---------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Sharp cheese, coarsely grate
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Sherry
1 x  Salt, pepper, paprika/ taste
2 lb Crab meat
4 c  Thick white sauce
1/2 c  Green pepper, finely diced
1/2 c  Pimientos, chopped
1 c  Celery, finely diced
MMMMM------------------------CURRIED RICE-----------------------------
1 tb Curry powder
1 x  Salt to taste
2 c  Rice
2 c  Chicken stock
Season white sauce with salt, pepper, Worcestershire
and sherry.  Combine all ingredients or arrange in
layers in casserole dish, keeping back 1/2 cup cheese
for top of dish.  Sprinkle paprika over top and bake
for one hour in 350 degree oven.  Serve with curried
rice: combine all ingredients and steam for one hour.
