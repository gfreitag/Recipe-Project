Title: Plato's Platter
Categories: Potatoes, Cheese/eggs, Vegetables, Greek, Tomatoes
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Sliced black olives
1/2 c  Chopped, seeded tomato
1 pk (750 gram) McCain Gold n
-Crisp Cross Trax Potatoes
1/2 c  Crumbled feta cheese
Bake the potatoes in a 450 F oven for about 10 minutes.  Turn potatoes
over and bake an additional 5 minutes or until nice and crispy.  Place
on platter.  Sprinkle the feta cheese, olives and tomato over top and
serve immediately.

Origin: 1996 Recipes Plus Calendar by Eagle Brand and Realemon.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/95.
