Title: SPINACH CREPES
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1    Egg, beaten slightly
1 tb Flour
1 tb Butter
2 tb Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1 pn Nutmeg or mace
2 pk Frozen spinach
3 tb Minced onion
Make a roux with onion, butter & flour.  Stir in milk & egg.  Add
spinach, salt and nutmeg.  Heat through.  Fill crepes, and bake 10-15
min. at 350.
