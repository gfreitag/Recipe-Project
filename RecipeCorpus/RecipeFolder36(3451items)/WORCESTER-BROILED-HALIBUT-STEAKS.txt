Title: WORCESTER BROILED HALIBUT STEAKS
Categories: Main dish, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
4 ts Worcesterhire Sauce
1 ts Salt
2 tb Butter (or marg.)
2 lb Halibut steaks
2 tb Orange juice
1 tb Orange peel; traged
Arrange fish on rack in broiler pan; set aside.  Combine orange juice
and peel, Worcestershire sauce and salt.  Brush lighlty over fish.
Dot with butter.  Place under pre-heated hot broiler 10 to 12 minutes
or until fish flakes when tested with a fork, basting with remaining
Worcestershire mixture.

SOURCE: Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce Advertisement in the
February, 1973 issue of Southern Living Magazine. Typed for you by
Nancy Coleman.
