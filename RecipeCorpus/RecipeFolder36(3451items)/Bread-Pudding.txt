Title: Bread Pudding
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Raisins
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Day old bread
2 tb Butter
2    Eggs
1 c  Milk
1/3 c  Sugar
Put butter in glass bowl and microwave on high 30 seconds.  Add
remaining ingredients and microwave on high 5 to 6 minutes stirring
after 3 minutes of cooking.
