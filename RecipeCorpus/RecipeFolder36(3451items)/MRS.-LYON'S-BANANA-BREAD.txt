Title: MRS. LYON'S BANANA BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
3    Mashed bananas or 1 cup
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Margarine
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour
Cream margarine and sugar, add the beaten eggs then the flour, soda
and salt mixture, lastly add the mashed bananas and mix well. Pour
into greased and floured loaf pan and bake 1 hour in 350 oven.
