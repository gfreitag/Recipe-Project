Title: FRUIT AND NUT BRIOCHE
Categories: Breads, Side dish, Holiday, Masterchefs, Medieval
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Raisins or sultanas
1/3 c  Currants
1/3 c  Dried apricots, sliced
A few glace cherries
170 g  Pure New Zealand Unsalted
-Creamery Butter, softened
-but not melted
1 tb Fresh yeast
150 ml Lukewarm milk
250 g  Flour
4    Eggs beaten
1 pn Of salt
4 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Almonds
1/2 c  Hazelnuts
Preheat oven to 170C. Dissolve yeast in milk.  Add flour, eggs,
salt, sugar, nuts and fruit.  Beat well.  cover and let rise in a
warm place until doubled in bulk.  Punch down, add butter and beat
well making sure there are no lumps of butter.  Pour in to well
buttered loaf tin (the mixture should half fill the tin).  Allow to
rise again until the tin is 3/4 full. Bake at 170 C until a skewer
comes out clean-about 20-25 minutes.  Cool before slicing Serves 6.
