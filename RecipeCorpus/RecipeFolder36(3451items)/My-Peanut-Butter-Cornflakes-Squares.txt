Title: My Peanut Butter Cornflakes Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 60 servings
7 c  Cornflakes
1 c  Wheatgerm
1 1/2 c  Peanuts
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Corn syrup
1 c  Peanut butter
Combine sugar, corn syrup and peanut butter in a pan. Bring to a boil,
stirring til smoothly blended.  For chewy squares boil for 1 minute.
For crisp squares boil for 2-3 minutes. Combine remaining ingredients
in a large bowl. Pour syrup over cereal mix and blend well. Turn into
a greased 11X15X2 pan .  Press down firmly. Cut into squares when
cool.
