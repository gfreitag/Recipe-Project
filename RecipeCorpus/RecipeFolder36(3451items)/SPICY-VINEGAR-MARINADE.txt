Title: SPICY VINEGAR MARINADE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------FOR BEEF,POULTRY,PORK,LAMB----------------------
2    Celery ribs w/leaves;chopped
2    Juniper berries; crushed
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Dried rosemary
1/2 ts Dried basil
1 tb Brown sugar
1 c  Red wine vinegar
1 c  Water
1    Onion; sliced
2    Garlic cloves; minced
8    Cloves
6    Parsley sprigs
2    Bay leaves
Combine all ingedients in a bowl.  Makes about 3-1/2 cups.
