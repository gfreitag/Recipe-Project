Title: Roasted Shallot and Turkey Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Poultry, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Sliced fresh mushrooms
1 tb Fresh tarragon, chopped
Coarse black pepper
1 c  Grated mozzarella cheese
1 c  Monterey jack, grated
6 lg Shallots
12    Inch thin crust pizza shell
-(homemade or commercial)
2/3 c  Or antipasto
1/3 lb Shredded cooked turkey
1/2 c  Sliced black olives
Preheat oven for 450F.  Pinch off root ends of shallots and squeeze
out of the skin.  Spread the antipasto over pizza shell.  Sprinkle the
shallots, olives, turkey, mushrooms and tarragon on top of antipasto.
Grated pepper over top, and then cover with cheeses.  Bake for about
15 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and a nice golden brown color.

Origin: Adapted from Appeal, Winter/95.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/95.
