Title: Cherry Oatmeal Chewies^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Chopped candied red
Cherries
1 c  Chopped candied green
Cherries
3/4 c  Butter flavored shortening
1 1/2 c  Firmly packed light brown
Sugar
1    Egg
1/3 c  Milk
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Quick oats, uncooked
1 c  Flour
Preheat oven to 375F.

Grease cookie sheets.

Place foil on cooling surface.

Combine dry ingredients.

Cream remaining ingredients except cherries until well blended. Add
dry mixture, stir in cherries.

Drop rounded tablespoons 2" apart on cookie sheets and bake 10-12
minutes or until lightly browned. Don't overbake. Cool a couple of
minutes on sheets, then remove to foil. Makes about 2 1/2 dozen.
