Title: LITTLE FRUIT CAKES
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts, Fruit, Holiday
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Sugar
2 c  Dates; chopped
1/2 c  Candied cherries; cut-up
2/3 c  Flour
Powdered Sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
2    Eggs
1 c  Pecans
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Baking powder
Blend shortening and sugar and beat in eggs.  Stir in remaining
ingredients.  Spread mixture in a 9" pan and bake at 350Ã¸ for 40-45
minutes.  Cut into squares while warm and roll in confectionary sugar.
