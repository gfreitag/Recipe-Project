Title: Marinated Turnips
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
3 tb Cider vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 md White turnips, slice thin
1/3 c  Celery tops, chopped fine
1/3 c  Oil
In a bowl combine all ingredients; toss and then chill until serving
time.
