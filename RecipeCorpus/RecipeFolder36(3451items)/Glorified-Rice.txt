Title: Glorified Rice
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Rice, cooked
3/4 c  Small marshmallows
2 c  Cool Whip
1 pk Lemon Jello
1 pt Water
1/4 c  Nuts
3/4 c  Pineapple, crushed
Dissolve Jello in boiling water.  Cool until it starts to thicken.
Add remaining ingredients, folding in Cool Whip last.
