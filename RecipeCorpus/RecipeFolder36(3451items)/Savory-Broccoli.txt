Title: Savory Broccoli
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Dried oregano
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cumin
2 tb Water -- or more if needed
1 lg Broccoli, (about 1 1/2 lb)
2 tb Olive oil
1    Onion -- chopped
4    Cloves garlic -- minced
Cut broccoli florets into bite-size pieces.  Heat oil over medium-high
heat, add onion and saute until transparent, 5 to 10 minutes.  Add
garlic to the skillet and stir over medium heat until softened, about
2 minutes. Add oregano, salt, cumin and broccoli pieces and stir
constantly for about 2 minutes.  Add water, cover and simmer for 10
minutes, or until broccoli is bright green and tender.
