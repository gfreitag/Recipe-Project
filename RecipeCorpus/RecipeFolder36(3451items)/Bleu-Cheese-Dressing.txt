Title: Bleu Cheese Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Fresh lemon juice
1 sm Clove
-
Salt to taste
Garlic, pressed or finely
Minced
12 oz Bleu cheese
1 1/4 c  Buttermilk
3 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 ts Onion powder
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Pepper
Put cheese and buttermilk in food processor, process till fairly
smooth but with little lumps of cheese.  Remove to a bowl and whisk
in rest of ingredients - no blender or processor here.  If too thick
add more buttermilk.
