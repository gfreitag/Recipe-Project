Title: Sprouting Lettuce Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Lettuce seeds
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 3 tablespoons in a quart jar, or tray sprout which will yield
about 2 cups of sprouts. Soak for 8 hours.

Rinse 2 to 3 times daily for 4 to 5 days. Move into sunlight to green.

Harvest when sprouts are 1 to 1-1/2 inches long. Use as a garnish.
Flavor is strong.
