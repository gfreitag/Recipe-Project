Title: FALAFAL RECEIPE
Categories: Main dish, Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Chic peas
INGREDIENTS:
For falafel, drain and rinse 1 cup chick peas that have
been soaked overnight in fresh water. Grind them in a
food processor. Soak 1 slice white, crust-less bread
in water and squeeze dry. Chop with 2 cloves minced
garlic and 2 tablespoons parsley. Add to the chick
peas. Add 1/4 cup bulgar (rinsed and drained), 1/2
teaspoon each of coriander, cumin and pepper and 1
teaspoon salt. Mix well and refrigerate at least 30
minutes. Heat oil in a large pot. Form chick pea
mixture into small balls and deep fry 2 to 3 minutes.
Serves 8.
