Title: ICEBOX ANGEL CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1    Jar maraschino cherries,
Chopped
1 lg Carton of Cool Whip
Angel flake coconut
1    Angel food cake
24    Marshmallows
1/2 c  Milk
1 cn Crushed pineapple (lg),
Well drained
Crumble angel food cake into a 9x13 inch glass pan.  Melt
marshmallows with milk.  Spoon over cake.  Spoon pineapple over top
then cherries. Spread Cool Whip over all.  Sprinkle with coconut and
refrigerte. Randy Rigg
