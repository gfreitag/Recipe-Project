Title: Fireside Punch
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Peach schnapps
1 c  Rum
6 c  Apple cider
12 oz Can frozen lemonade
-concentrate
In a Dutch oven, combine first 3 ingredients, bring to
a boil.  Remove from heat; stir in schnapps and rum.
Serve hot.

Yield 12 - 6 oz. servings. Here is something that will
warm you from head to toe after a chilly day of
sledding, snow shoveling, ice skating, snow
mobiling...etc.
