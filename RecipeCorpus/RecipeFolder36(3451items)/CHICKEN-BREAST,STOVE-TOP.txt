Title: CHICKEN BREAST,STOVE TOP
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea Cut up pears
1 pk Stove top stuffing mix
2 ea Chicken breasts.
2 c  Apple juice
brown chicken breasts in oil, add the apple juice and cut up pears.
add the seasoning packet from the stuffing mix. cover and cook 5-10
minutes over medium heat. remove from stove and add the pk. stuffing
mix and stir.cover and set aside for 5 minutes.
