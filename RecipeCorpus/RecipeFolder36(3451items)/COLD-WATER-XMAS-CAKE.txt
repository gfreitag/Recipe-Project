Title: COLD WATER XMAS CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
-------ADD-----------------
3 c  Flour
1 ts Soda
1 x  Pinch salt
1 ts Vanilla
1 x  Cherries and nuts
1 x  Mixed dried peel (optional)
2 c  White sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Nutmeg
2 tb Shortening
1 lb Seeded raisins
2 c  Cold water
Boil and stir for 5 minutes the sugar, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg,
shortening,  raisins and water. Let cool and then add the remaining
ingredients. Next pour into large well greased and floured tube pan.
Bake at 375* for 1 1/2 hours.
