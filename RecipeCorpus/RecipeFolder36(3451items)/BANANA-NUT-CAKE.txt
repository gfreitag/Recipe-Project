Title: BANANA NUT CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Walnuts (optional)
3    Mashed bananas
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Oil
1    Egg
Mix together in order given.  Pour into lightly greased and floured
oblong or small loaf pans.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes.
