In a large saucepan, melt the butter and sautee onion 2 minutes. Add
ptoatoes and cook 3 minutes longer, stirring frequenntly. Pour in

cook 10 minutes longer, or until potatoes art tender. Season with
salt and pepper. Sprinkle with chives before serving. Makes 6 to 8
servings.
