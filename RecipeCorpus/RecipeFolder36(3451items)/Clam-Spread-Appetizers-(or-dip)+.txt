Title: Clam Spread Appetizers (or dip)+
Categories: Appetizers, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 1 recipe
8 oz Cream cheese
1 ea Loaf thinly sliced cocktail
-rye bread
1 cn Diced clams
2 tb Diced onions
ds Tabasco
ds Worcestershire
Combine ingredients and spread, rather thickly, on bread. Broil till
brown and bubbly.

Alternative: Thin out with milk and use as a dip.
