Title: Vegetable Marinade
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Accent
1 ts Sugar
1 1/3 c  Oil
1/2 c  White vinegar
1 tb Dill weed
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Salt and pepper
Pour over vegetables.  Marinate overnight.  Vegetables to try:
broccoli, cauliflower, mushrooms, tomatoes and others.
