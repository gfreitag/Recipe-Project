Title: Low-Salt Tangy Broiled Scamp - SL 1/87
Categories: Low cal/fat, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Scamp fillets or other lean
-white fish fillets
1 c  Soft low-sodium breadcrumbs
Vegetable cooking spray
1 tb Reduced-calorie margarine;
-melted
15 minutes cooking time
3 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Dried whole tarragon
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Paprika
1 ts Dried whole oregano
Cooking time: about 15 minutes.

Combine first 6 ingredients in a shallow dish. Dip fish fillets into
lemon juice mixture; lightly coat with breadcrumbs. Place on a
broiler pan coated with cooking spray. Drizzle reduced-calorie
margarine over fish.
Broil 7" from heat about 7 minutes or until fish flakes easily when
tested with a fork. Yield: 4 servings.

224 calories, 25.9g protein, 3.8g fat, 20.1g carbohydrate, 64mg
cholesterol, 86mg sodium, and 294mg calcium per 3-oz serving.

Recipe from M.E. Carr in 1/87 "Southern Living". Typos by Jeff Pruett.
