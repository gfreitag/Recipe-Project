Title: EDIE ACSELL'S MARINADE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------BEEF,POULTRY,PORK,FISH,VEG'S---------------------
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
3    Garlic cloves; minced
1/2 c  Safflower oil
1/2 c  Tomato juice
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Makes about 2 cups.
