Title: SOUTH TEXAS CHUCK-WAGON RUB
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------FOR BEEF-------------------------------
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Seasoned salt
1    Garlic clove; crushed
1 ts Seasoned pepper
1 ts Ground dried mild chiles
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mash until smooth.
Makes about 2 Tbsp.
