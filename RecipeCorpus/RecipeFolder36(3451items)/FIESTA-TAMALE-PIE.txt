Title: FIESTA TAMALE PIE
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
18    Pitted ripe olives
2    Eggs, well beaten
1 c  Corn meal
1 c  Milk
1 c  Grated Cheddar cheese
1 tb Vegetable oil
1    Clove garlic,
-peeled & crushed
1 sm Onion, minced
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/4 lb Bulk pork sausage
16 oz Stewed tomatoes, undrained
16 oz Whole kernel corn, drained
1. Combine vegetable oil, onion and garlic in a large, heat-
resistant, non-metallic mixing bowl; heat, uncovered, in Micro- wave
Oven 2 minutes or until onion is tender. 2. Add ground beef and
sausage to onion and garlic. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 5
minutes or until meat is browned. Stir occasionally. 3. Drain excess
fat. 4. Add tomatoes, corn, salt and chili powder to meat mixture.
Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 4 minutes or until mixture comes
to a boil. 5. While meat mixture is cooking, combine eggs, corn meal
and milk in a medium-sized bowl. Blend thoroughly. Set aside 6. Pour
meat mixture into an 8-inch square, heat-resistant, non- metallic
baking dish. 7. Press olives into meat mixture. 8. Spoon corn meal
mixture over meat mixture. 9. Sprinkle grated cheese on top. Heat,
uncovered, 10 minutes or until cheese melts.
