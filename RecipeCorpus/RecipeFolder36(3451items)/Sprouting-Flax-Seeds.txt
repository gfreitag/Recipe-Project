Title: Sprouting Flax Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Flax seed
1 ea Prepared clay saucer -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1/4 cup in a clay saucer, or tray sprout, which will yield about
1 cup of sprouts.

Without soaking or rinsing the seeds, mist with water 3 times daily
for 3 to 5 days. Move into sunlight to green.

Harvest when sprouts are 1 to 1-1/2 inches long. Use in salads or
juices.
