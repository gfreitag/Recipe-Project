Title: Cilantro-Cashew Pesto
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
3/4 c  Canola oil
1 tb Lime juice
1/8 ts White pepper
2    Garlic clove(s), minced
1/2 c  Cilantro, chopped
1/4 c  Cashews, chopped
2 tb Grated Romano cheese
In a blender, add all ingredients and blend until thoroughly mixed.
Refrigerate, covered with a thin layer of olive oil. Use within 3
weeks.

This recipe is wonderful with any pasta, grilled chicken breasts, or
after thinning with 2 tbs of red wine vinegar, as a salad dressing.
