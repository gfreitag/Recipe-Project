Title: Sea Food Ragout DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
1 x  Salt to taste
2 dr Onion juice
1/4 ts Pepper
3/4 c  Chicken, cooked and diced
2 tb Sherry
1 ts Parsley, finely chopped
1 pt Oysters
2 lb Shrimp, cooked and peeled
1/4 c  Butter
3/4 lb Mushrooms
3/4 c  Cream
1/4 c  Flour
1 ds Cayenne
Parboil oysters.  Make sauce of cream, flour and
butter.  Add seasoning, then shrimp, mushrooms,
chicken, oysters, sherry and parsley.  Heat through.
Serve hot.  "Charleston Receipts"  formatted by Mary
Bowles.
