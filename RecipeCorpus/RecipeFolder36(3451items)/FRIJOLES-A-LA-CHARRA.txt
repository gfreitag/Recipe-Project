Title: FRIJOLES A LA CHARRA
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1    Large tomato, chopped
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Fresh ground black pepper
2 tb Chopped cilantro
4    Slices bacon
3    Jalapeno chiles, stems and
-seeds removed, diced
1    Small onion, diced
1 qt Cooked pinto beans,and juice
Saute the bacon, jalapenos and the onion ntil well browned. Add the
beans, tomato and spices; bring to a boil, add cilantro, reduce heat
and simmer 15 minutes.
