Title: Beef N Cheddar Biscuits^
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome, Cheese/eggs, Breads
Yield: 12 servings
Biscuits
1 c  (4 oz) shredded cheddar
Cheese
1 lb Ground beef, cooked and
Drained
1/4 c  Bbq sauce
2 pk (4 1/2oz) refrigerated
Preheat oven to 350F.

Press biscuits into bottom and up sides of 12 greased muffin cups. Top
with combined meat and bbq sauce. Sprinkle with cheese.

Bake 15-18 minutes or until biscuits are golden and cheese is melted.
