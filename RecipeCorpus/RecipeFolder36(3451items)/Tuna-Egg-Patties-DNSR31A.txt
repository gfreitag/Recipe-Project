Title: Tuna-Egg Patties DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Paprika
1 c  Milk
2 ea Eggs
1 cn Tuna
2 tb Water
3 tb Pimiento, chopped
2 c  Corn Flakes, crushed
1/4 c  Butter
1 tb Onion, chopped
1/4 c  Flour
1 tb Parsley, minced
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Grease 8x8x2 inch baking dish.  Melt butter in pan and
blend in flour, salt and pepper.  Cook 2 minutes.
Stir in milk and cook until thick. Stir in tuna,
pimiento, onion, parsley, salt, paprika.  Spread in
dish. Refrigerate 1 hour.  Cut in 9 squares.  Coat
with flour.  Shape into patties.  Beat eggs and water.
Dip patties into egg.  Coat with crushed flakes.  Fry
in hot oil for 5 minutes on each side over medium
heat. Serve with hot chili sauce.  From Gourmet,
formatted by Mary Dishongh Bowles.
