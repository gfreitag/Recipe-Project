Title: ARGENTINIAN CHIMICHURRI-STYLE MARINADE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------FOR BEEF AND POULTRY-------------------------
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Fresh oregano leaves; chpd.
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Malt vinegar
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Fresh parsley; chopped
3    Large garlic cloves; minced
Combine all ingredients in a glass jar with tight fitting lid. Let
stand at room temperature for 24 hours. Makes about 1-1/3 cups
