Title: EAGLE BRAND Irish Cream Liqueur
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
4    Eggs
2 tb Chocolate flavored syrup
2 ts Instant coffee
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Almond extract
1 1/4 c  Irish Whiskey; or brandy,
-rum, rye whiskey, bourbon,
-scotch
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
1 c  Heavy cream
In blender container, combine all ingredients, blend until smooth.
Store tightly covered in refrigerator up to one month.  Stir before
serving
