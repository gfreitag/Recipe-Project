Title: Scalloped Corn
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
Crumbs
1/2 sm Onion
3 tb Chopped green pepper or
Pimento
Salt and pepper
2 tb Butter or margarine
1    No  2 can whole-kernel or
Cream-style corn
1/2 c  Milk if using cream-style
Corn or 3/4 cup if using
Whole-kernel corn
1 c  Dried bread or cracker
Combine corn and milk.  Add bread or cracker crumbs, onion, green
pepper and seasonings. Pour in to greased 1 quart casserole. Dot with
butter. Bake in moderate oven (350 dg) 30 minutes.
