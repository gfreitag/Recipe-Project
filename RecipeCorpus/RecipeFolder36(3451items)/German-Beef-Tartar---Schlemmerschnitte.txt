Title: German Beef Tartar - Schlemmerschnitte
Categories: Aust/german, Appetizers, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 pn Each: salt, pepper, paprika
-and Worcestershire sauce
1 sl Toast; buttered, crusts
-removed
1/2 oz Fresh caviar
1 tb Onion; minced
1    Gherkin pickle; sliced
4 oz Beef filet; or other lean
-steak, scraped not ground
1    Egg yolk
1 ts Olive oil
1 ts Ketchup
1    Anchovy; chopped
5    Capers;  chopped
Mix beef, egg yolk, oil and seasonings. Spread on buttered toast and
top with caviar. Serve with minced onion on the side along with a
sliced gherkin and an ice-cold shot of vodka or Schnaps.
From: The German Cookbook by Mimi Sheraton, 1965.
