Title: Sweet Potato Fries
Categories: Side dish, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 recipe
1/8 ts Ground red pepper (cayanne)
1 lb Sweet potatoes, cut into
-1/2" sticks
2 tb Thawed orange juice
-concentrate
1 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 450F

Line a cookie sheet with foil and spray with Pam

In a medium bowl combine orange juice concentrate,
onion powder, salt and cayanne.  Add potato sticks and
turn to coat.

Arrange coated potato sticks on the oiled foil in a
single layer. Bake 10 min; turn carefully, bake 10
more minutes or until tender The total batch has 416
calories, 1 gram of fat, and tastes pretty good
