Title: Linguine in Clam Sauce DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
8 oz Linguine, cooked, drained
1 x  Parmesan cheese, grated
3 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 c  Butter, melted
16 oz Sliced mushrooms, drained
13 oz Clams, minced, drained
1/2 c  Parsley, fresh chopped
Saute garlic in butter in large skillet over low heat
for 1 minute.  Add mushrooms and cook 5 minutes.  Stir
in clams, parsley, salt and pepper. Heat thoroughly.
Pour over linguine and toss well.  Heat through and
top with Parmesan cheese.  Formatted by Mary Dishongh
Bowles.
