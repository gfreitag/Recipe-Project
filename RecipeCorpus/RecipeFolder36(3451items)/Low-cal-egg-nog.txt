Title: Low cal egg nog
Categories: Beverages, Low cal/fat, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
3    Packets equal sweetener
1/2 ts Brandy or rum flavouring
Ground nutmeg
2    Eggs; separated
4 c  Skim milk
1 ts Vanilla
Combine the egg yolks and milk in a saucepan.  Cook over medium heat
until the mixture coats a metal spoon. cool. Beat the egg whites
until soft peaks form.  add to the egg custard mixture with the
vanilla, sweetener, an flavoring.  Mix lightly. Cover and chill.
Pour into serving cups and sprinkle with nutmeg. calories per 1/2 c
serving: 70
