Title: Fudge Oatmeal (no bake) Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 lb Butter
Dash of salt
2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/2 c  Milk
Combine ingredients.  Bring to a boil and boil hard for one minute.
Remove from fire and add 1 tablespoon of vanilla.  Pour into a bowl
containing 3 cups of raw oatmeal (quick) and 1/2 cup of peanut butter.
Mix well.  Drop quickly by tablespoons onto waxed paper.
