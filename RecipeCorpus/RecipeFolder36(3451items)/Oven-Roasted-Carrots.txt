Title: Oven-Roasted Carrots
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
5    Cloves garlic
1 tb Vinegar
Salt and pepper -- to taste
3 tb Olive oil
1 lb Carrots -- peel, cut
-in sticks
Preheat the oven to 425.  Spread 2 tbsp oil on a cookie sheet.
Scatter carrots across the pan.  Cut each garlic clove into 2 or 3
pieces and scatter among carrots.  Drizzle with remaining 1 tsp oil
and vinegar. Bake for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally; carrots
should be brown but not burn--they will not be brown all over.
Remove from the oven and season with salt and pepper.  Serve hot,
cold or at room temperature.
