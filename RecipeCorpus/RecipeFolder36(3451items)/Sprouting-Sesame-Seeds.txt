Title: Sprouting Sesame Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sesame seeds
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1 cup in a quart jar, or tray sprout which will yield about 2
cups of sprouts. Soak for 8 to 10 hours.

Rinse 3 or 4 times daily for 3 days.

The sprouts will be the length of the seed. Use in granola or baked
goods.
