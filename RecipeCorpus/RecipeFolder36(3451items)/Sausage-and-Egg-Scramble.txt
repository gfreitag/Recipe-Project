Title: Sausage and Egg Scramble
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Salt
18    Eggs
1    Box of onion garlic croutons
2 lb Sausage
2 c  Milk
3 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
Brown sausage & drain.  Place in the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan.  Mix
eggs, milk, cheese and salt.  Pour over sausage.  Sprinkle croutons
over the top of the egg mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes
then serve.
