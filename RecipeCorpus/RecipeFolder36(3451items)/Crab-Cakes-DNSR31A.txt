Title: Crab Cakes DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Dry Mustard
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Bread crumbs, soft
1 lb Crabmeat, drained
1 x  Vegetable oil for frying
2 ea Egg whites, beaten
2 tb Mayonnaise
2 ts Parsley, fresh chopped
1 1/4 ts Old Bay seasoning
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Combine all except vegetable oil.  Shape into 8
patties.  Place on baking sheet lined with wax paper.
Chill 30 minutes.  Cook in hot oil 6 minutes each
side  or until browned.  Formatted by Mary Dishongh
Bowles.
