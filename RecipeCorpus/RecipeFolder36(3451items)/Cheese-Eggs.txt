Title: Cheese Eggs
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Margarine; melted
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Cheese, American; shredded
6    Eggs
1/2 ts Salt
Place equal amounts of cheese in 6 greased custard cups.  Break an
egg into each cup.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Pour a small amount of margarine and milk over each egg.  Place in a
pan of hot water, and bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes or
until desired doneness.
