Title: VENSION CASSEROLE
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Cream of celery soup
Frozen french fries
Pepper
1 lb Ground venison or beef
1 sm Onion
1 cn Cheddar cheese soup
1. Dice onion and mix with ground meat and pepper to taste (use
*lots*). 2. Press into the bottom of a 9x9" pan.
3. Mix together the two cans of soup - do *not* dilute with water.
Spread
mixture over the meat.
4. Cover entire surface generously with frozen french fries.
5. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes or until done - fries should be golden
and crispy.
