Title: CHEDDAR BAKED POTATO SCLICES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
4 md Baking potatoes, cut into
-1/4" slices (about 4 cups)
1 c  Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 cn Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Pepper
1. In small bowl, combine soup, paprika and pepper.
2. In greased 2-qt. oblong baking dish, arrange potatoes in
overlapping
rows. Sprinkle with cheese, spoon soup mixture over the cheese.
3. Cover with foil; bake at 400Ã¸F. 45 minutes; bake 10 minutes or
until
potatoes are fork-tender.
