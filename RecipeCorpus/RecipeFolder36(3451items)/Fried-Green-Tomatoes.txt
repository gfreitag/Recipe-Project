Title: Fried Green Tomatoes
Categories: Tomatoes
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Milk
1 c  Seasoned Dry Bread Crumbs
3 tb Butter or Margarine
1 tb Cooking Oil
1 tb Brown Sugar
1 c  All-purpose Flour
4    To 6 Green Tomatoes, medium,
-sliced 1/2' thick
1    Egg, beaten
Combine sugar and flour; place on a shallow plate. Dip both sides of
each tomato slice into the mixture. Combine the egg and the milk. Dip
each tomato slice, then dip into bread crumbs. In a skillet, heat
butter and oil over medium-high. Fry tomatoes until brown, but firm
enough to hold their shapes.

Yield: about 6 servings.

SOURCE: Helen Bridges, Washington, VA, Reminisce Magazine Sept/Oct 92
SHARED BY: Jim Bodle 9/92
