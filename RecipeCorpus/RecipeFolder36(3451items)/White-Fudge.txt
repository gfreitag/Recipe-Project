Title: White Fudge
Categories: Candies, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Light corn syrup
1/4 ts Salt
2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Coarsely chopped walnuts
1/3 c  Candied cherries
*INGREDIENTS*
2 1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
1/4 c  Milk
2 tb Butter
In a saucepan, combine sugar, sour cream, milk, butter, corn syrup,
and salt. Cook over medium heat to 238 degrees (240 if weather is
damp) this is softball stage, about 9-10 minutes.

Remove from heat and let stand 1 hour or until lukewarm - 110 degrees.

Add vanilla and beat until it begins to lose it's gloss and holds it's
shape. This requires very little beating. quicky stir in nuts and
candied cherries. Turn into buttered 9X9 pan. Yields 1 1/2 pounds
fudge.
