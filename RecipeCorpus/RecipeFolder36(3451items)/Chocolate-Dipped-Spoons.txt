Title: Chocolate Dipped Spoons
Categories: Chocolate, Holiday
Yield: 8 servings
4 oz Semi sweet chocolate
OR white chocolate
8 ea Assorted spoons (plastic or
-silver)
Stir your coffee with a
-specialty spoon.  Make
-some up ahead to have on
Hand for company.
Partially melt the chocolate.  Dip spoons into melted chocolate and
set aside to harden. Redip to coat again.  You can use white
chocolate to drizzle onto dark, or vice versa.  Decorate with ribbon.
Store in airtight container.  Makes 8 spoons.
Origin: Baker's Festive Recipe pamphlet.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/95.
