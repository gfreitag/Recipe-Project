Title: Chicken rice casserole
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Mushrooms no salt
1 cn Franco American gravy mush.
1 md Onion, sliced
1 c  Water
6 ea Garlic cloves
4 ea Drumsticks
2 ea Breast, boneless, skinless
1 md Green pepper, sliced
1 c  Long grain rice,
1 cn Whole tomatoes no salt
1/2 ea Bag frozen oriental veggues
Place the chicken in a oven dish. Add mushrooms, onions, tomatoes with
juice, garlic cloves and bake in 350 degree oven for 1 hour. Then take
out the chicken and add the rice, frozen vegetables, mushroom gravy
and water. Place the chicken back in the pan and bake another hour or
until all the liquid is gone.
