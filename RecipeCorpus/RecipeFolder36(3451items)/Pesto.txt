Title: Pesto
Categories: Pasta/rice, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Pine nuts
2    Cloves Garlic
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
2 c  Fresh basil leaves -- firmly
-packed
1/4 c  Oil
Combine all ingredients in a processor or blender, process until
smooth.

Note:  Pesto freezes well.  Freeze in ice cube trays, then put the
cubes in plastic freezer bags.
