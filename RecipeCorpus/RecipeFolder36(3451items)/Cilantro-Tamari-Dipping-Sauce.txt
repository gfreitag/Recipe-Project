Title: Cilantro-Tamari Dipping Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 /3 cup
Finely chopped
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper flakes
1 ts Cilantro leaves
Finely chopped
1/4 c  Champagne vinegar
Or apple cider vinegar
2 tb Tamari
1/2    Garlic clove(s), peeled
Combine all of the ingredients and serve as a dip.
