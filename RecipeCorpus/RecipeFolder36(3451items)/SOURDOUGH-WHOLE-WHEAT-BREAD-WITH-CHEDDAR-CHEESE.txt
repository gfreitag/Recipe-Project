Title: SOURDOUGH WHOLE WHEAT BREAD WITH CHEDDAR CHEESE
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------DIRECTIONS FOR 1 LB. LOAF----------------------
1 tb (plus 1 tsp) brown sugar
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Sourdough starter
1/3 c  Water
1/2 c  Grated cheddar cheese
1 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
1 1/3 c  Bread flour
2/3 c  Whole wheat flour
1/3 c  Wheat bran
1 ts Salt
MMMMM----------------INSTRUCTIONS FOR 1.5 LB LOAF---------------------
2 tb Brown sugar
1 1/2 tb Vegetable oil
1 1/2 c  Sourdough starter
1/2 c  Water
3/4 c  Grated Cheddar cheese
2 ts Active dry yeast
2 c  Bread flour
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 c  Wheat bran
1 1/2 ts Salt
1.  Add all ingredients except Cheddar cheese in the order suggested
by
your bread machine manual and process on the basic bread cycle
according to the manufacturer's instructions.
2.  At the beeper (or at the end of the first kneading in the
Panasonic
or National), add the cheese.
