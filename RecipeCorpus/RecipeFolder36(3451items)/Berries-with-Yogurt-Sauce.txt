Title: Berries with Yogurt Sauce
Categories: Desserts, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Confectioners sugar
1/8 ts Grated orange peel
2 c  Strawberries cut in quarters
1/2 c  Plain yogurt low fat
2 tb Reduce calorie raspberry
Spread melted
In blender combine all ingredients except strawberries and process
until smooth.  Into each dessert dish put 1/4 of the strawberries and
top each portion with 1/4 of the sauce.  Serve immediately
Each serving is 1/2 fruit 1/4 milk 15 optional calories
