Title: Hot and Sour Soup (La Suan Tang)
Categories: Chinese, Soup/stew, Hot/spicy
Yield: 4 servings    2    Eggs
1 t  Salt
2 T  Soy sauce
1/2 t  5-Spice powder
1 T  Cornstarch in 1 cup water
2 pt Stock
3 T  Vinegar
White pepper

Beat the eggs.  Mix together the salt, soy sauce, 5-spice and
cornstarch mixture.  Put the stock on to boil and stir in the salt
and cornstarch mixture. As the soup comes to a boil pour in the eggs.
Add the vinegar and white pepper.
