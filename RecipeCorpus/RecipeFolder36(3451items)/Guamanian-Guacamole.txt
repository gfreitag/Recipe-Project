Title: Guamanian Guacamole
Categories: Vegetables, Dips/spread, Guam
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Onion, chopped fine
ds Or two of tabasco sauce
2 ea Avocados, mashed and pureed
1 ea Lemon or lime squeezed,
-juice only
1 ea Garlic clove, chopped fine
Combine all ingredients, mix well and allow to chill and blend in
refrigerator.
From: Real Guamanian Recipes By Way Of Dorothy's Kitchen
By: Dorothy Horn
