Title: PAPRIKA SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 2 cups
1 ts Onion juice
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Water
1/4 ts Salt
1    Lemon; juice of
ds Hot sauce
1/2 c  Salad oil
1 c  Mayonnaise
3    Garlic cloves
2 tb Chili sauce
2 tb Catsup
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Pepper
1 ts Mustard; dry or prepared
Combine all ingredients except salad oil in container of electric
blender; blend 5 seconds.  Add salad oil slowly, continuing blending;
blend 5 seconds after adding oil.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in 1977. Typed for you by
Nancy Coleman.
