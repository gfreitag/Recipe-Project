Title: Sprouting Corn
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Corn kernels
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1 cup kernels in a quart jar, or tray sprout, which will yield
about 3 cups sprouts. Soak for 20 hours.

Rinse 3 times daily for 2 to 4 days.

Harvest when the sprouts are 1/2 inch long. Use in casseroles, soups,
and tortillas, or bake, steam, or stir fry.
