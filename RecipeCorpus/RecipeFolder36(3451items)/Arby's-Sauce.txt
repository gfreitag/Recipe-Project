Title: Arby's Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Tabasco sauce
1 c  Catsup
2 ts Water
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Onion powder
Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan and cook over medium heat,
stirring constantly, until the sauce begins to boil, 5 to 10 minutes.
Remove the sauce from the heat.  Cover and allow to cool.
Pour into a covered container for storage in your refrigerator.
Keeps for a month or two.  Makes 1 cup.
