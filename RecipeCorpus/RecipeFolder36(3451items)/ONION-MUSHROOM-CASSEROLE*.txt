Title: ONION MUSHROOM CASSEROLE*
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 5 servings
3 ts Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Cheddar cheese; grated
1/4 c  Cracker crumbs
5 c  Vidalia onions, peeled
1/4 c  Butter; melted
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Fresh mushrooms; sliced
3 oz Cream cheese
Combine onions with butter, mushrooms and salt; simmer for 5 minutes
in a medium size sauce pan.  Add cream cheese.  When melted, add
flour and milk. Reheat. Turn into a 2 qt. greased casserole dish.
Sprinkle with misture of grated cheese and crumbs.  Bake in a hot 400
degree oven for 30 minutes.
