Title: Molasses Oat Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
1 tb Unsalted butter
1 c  Water -- plus 2 tbsp
2 tb Molasses
1 ts Lemon juice
2 1/4 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1/2 c  Rolled oats
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Dark brown sugar
Use basic cycle.  Do not use timer.
