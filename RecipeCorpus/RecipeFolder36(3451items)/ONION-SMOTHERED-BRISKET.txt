Title: ONION SMOTHERED BRISKET
Categories: Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1    Brisket; 5 to 6 pounds
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 lb Onions; sliced
2 tb Garlic powder
2 tb Paprika
1 ts Pepper
Mix garlic powder,paprika and pepper and rub into brisket.Heat oil in
Dutch oven and sear brisket on all sidesRemove and set aside.Saute
onions in Dutch oven.Replace brisket and cover totally with
onions.Cover tightly and place in 275 deg.oven for 3 1/2 to 4 hours.
