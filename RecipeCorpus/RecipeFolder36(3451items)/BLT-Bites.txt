Title: BLT Bites
Categories: Snacks, Appetizers, Tomatoes, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Chopped green onions
3 tb Grated parmesan cheese
2 tb Snipped fresh parsley
16    To 20 cherry tomatoes
1 lb Bacon, cooked and crumbled
1/2 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
Cut a thin slice off of each tomato top. Scoop out and discard pulp.
Invert the tomatoes on a paper towel to drain. In a small bowl,
combining all remaining ingredients; mix well. Spoon into tomatoes.
Refrigerate for several hours.
