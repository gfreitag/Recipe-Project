Title: Sprouting Soybeans
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Soybean seeds
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar - OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 3/4 cup in a quart jar, or tray sprout which will yield about 1
quart of sprouts. Soak for 12 to 24 hours, changing the soaking water
once.

Rinse 3 to 4 times daily for 3 to 4 days.

Harvest when sprouts are 1/2 to 2 inches long. Use in oriental dishes,
salads, casseroles, baked goods or steam.
