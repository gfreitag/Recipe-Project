Title: Swedish Glgg
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 24 servings
1 c  Prunes; large,pitted
1    Orange;cut into fourths
2    Bottles red wine;dry
1 3/4 c  Brandy
1 3/4 c  Vodka
1/3 c  Sugar
10    Whole cloves
7    Cardamom pods;crushed
2    Cinnamon sticks
1    Gingerroot; « inch
2 c  Water
10    Almonds;blanched into halves
1 3/4 c  Raisins
Tie cloves, cardamom pods and seeds, cinnamon and gingerroot in
cheesecloth bag. Heat spice bag, water, almonds, raisins, prunes, and
orange to boiling in dutch oven; reduce heat.
Cover and simmer 45 minutes, remove spicebag, prunes and orange.
Stir in remaining ingredients. Cover and heat until mixture begins to
bubble. Ladle almonds and a few raisins in each cup before filling
with hot glgg.
