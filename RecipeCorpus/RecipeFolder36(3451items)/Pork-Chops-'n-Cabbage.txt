Title: Pork Chops 'n Cabbage
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1    Head Cabbage
Milk
6    Pork chops
1 lg Onion -- sliced
Salt and pepper
Brown pork chops.  Turn chops to brown second side; adding onion
slices. Arrange browned chops in a greased 9x13 casserole with onion
slices around them.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Slice cabbage
thin; spread over chops.  Pour milk over all until just barely
covered.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Bake at 350 for 45 to 60 minutes or until chops are tender.  (Cabbage
will cook down considerably.)

Renee Cole  21 Oct 1994
