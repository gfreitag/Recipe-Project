Title: Carolina Rice Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Unsalted butter
3/4 c  Cooked rice
3/4 c  Water
1 lg Egg
1 ts Lemon juice
2 1/4 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
2 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
Use basic cycle.  Do not use timer.
