Title: APPLE HONEY DRESSING
Categories: Condiments, Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Vinegar
1/4 ts Grated lemon peel
1 ts Celery seeds
1/2 c  Salad oil
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Mustard, dry
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Applesauce, smooth
1/4 c  Honey, strained
Combine all ingredients except oil in mixing bowl.  Mix well.  Add oil
slowly, beating thoroughly. Chill.  Shake or beat well before serving.

1 1/2 cups

Ladle over salad.
