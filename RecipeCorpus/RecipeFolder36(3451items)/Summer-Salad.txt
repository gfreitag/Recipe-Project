Title: Summer Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
3 md Tomatoes -- sliced
2 tb Cilantro -- minced
2 tb Green onions -- minced
3 tb Olive oil
1    Lime -- juice only
Salt and pepper -- to taste
2    Avocados -- sliced
Combine olive oil and lime juice with salt and pepper in a small bowl.
Slice avocados and coat with lime juice mixture.  Arrange on plates
with sliced tomatoes.  Sprinkle with cilantro and green onions.
