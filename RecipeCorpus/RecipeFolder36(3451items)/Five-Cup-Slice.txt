Title: Five Cup Slice
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 recipe
1 1/4 c  Coconut
10 oz Sweetened condensed milk
1 1/4 c  Raisins
1 1/4 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1 1/4 c  Peanuts
Combine ingredients together in bowl. Stir well to
mix. Spread into greased 9 X 9-inch pan. Bake in 350F
oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Cut into squares.

From Charmaine, Toronto, Ontario
