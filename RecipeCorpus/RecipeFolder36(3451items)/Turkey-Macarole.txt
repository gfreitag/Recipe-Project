Title: Turkey Macarole
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Milk
3 c  Turkey, cooked & diced
1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
1/4 c  Pimento, sliced
Colby longhorn or Cheddar
Cheese for topping
1 pk 8 oz macaroni
2 c  Sliced celery
2 cn Cream of chicken soup
1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
1/3 c  Almonds, slivered
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Onion, diced
Cook macaroni. Drain set aside. heat oven to 350 degrees. Melt butter
in large saucepan over medium heat. Add celery and onion. Cook 2
minutes. Stir in undiluted soup, milk, and seasonings. Pour into
greased 3 quart casserole. Add turkey, pimento and macaroni. Stir
gently until just mixed. Cheese can be diced or sliced. (I use my
hand grater on a very coarse grate.) Scatter cheese over top of
mixture. Cover. Bake 25 minutes or until hot.
