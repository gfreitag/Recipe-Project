Title: Peanut Butter Snowballs
Categories: Desserts, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Graham crackers
8    Sq. semi-sweet chocolate
1 c  Sifted icing sugar
1/2 c  Craft peanut butter
3 tb Softened margarine
Beat sugar, butter, and peanut butter.  Stir in crumbs, and
:
shape into 1 inch balls.
Place on wax lined cookie sheet and chill for 30 minutes.
Melt chocolate in microwave for 2 minutes. (I would try 1
:
:
minute first).
Dip balls into chocolate and return to cookie sheet.  Chill
:
and store in fridge.
