Title: Sprouting Lentils
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Lentil seeds
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 3/4 cup in a quart jar, or tray sprout which will yield about 6
cups of sprouts. Soak for 8 to 10 hours.

Rinse 2 to 3 times daily for 2 to 3 days.

Harvest when the sprouts are 1/4 to 1/2 inch long. Use in salads,
sauces, dips, juices, soups or casseroles.
