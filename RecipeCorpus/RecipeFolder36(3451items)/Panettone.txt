Title: Panettone
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker, Italian
Yield: 12 slices
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Dry milk
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Fleischmann's Bread Machine
-Yeast
1/2 c  Chopped mixed dried fruit
3/4 c  Plus 2 tb water
1/4 c  Margarine or butter, soft
1    Egg
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
3 1/4 c  Gold medal better for Bread
-Flour
1. Make 1 1/2-pound recipe with bread machines that use 3 cups flour.

2. Measure carefully, placing all ingredients except fruit in bread
machine pan in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Add fruit
at the raisin/nut signal or 5 to 10 minutes before last kneading
cycle ends.

3. Select Sweet or Basic/White cycle. Use Medium or Light crust color.
Do not use delay cycles. Remove baked bread from pan and cool on wire
rack.

Per Slice: Calories 205; Protein 5g; Carbohydrate 35g; Fat 5g;
Cholesterol 20 mg; Sodium 320mg.
