Title: Chicken and Ham Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
2    Onions chopped
3/4 pt Chicken stock made with
-liquid concentrate.
2 ts Light soy sauce
1 ts Corn flour.
1    Chicken breast fillet
-skinned and sliced thinly.
2 md Courgettes
1 1/2 oz Mushrooms
1 1/2 oz Ham chopped
Place chicken courgettes, mushrooms ham onions and stock in a pan.
Bring to the boil. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 5 minutes.
Blend soy sauce with the corn flour and add to the pan stirring
constantly until the soup thickens.

Serve immediately.
