Title: Baked Apple Doughnuts
Categories: Desserts, Breads, Breakfast
Yield: 60 servings
1 c  Shortening
2    Eggs -- beaten
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Apples -- grated
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
3 c  Flour
3 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Nutmeg
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Butter -- melted
1/2 c  Sugar
Mix flour, baking powder, sugar, salt, and nutmeg.  Cut
in shortening until mixture looks like crumbs.  Add eggs,
milk, apples, nuts and mix thoroughly.

Fill well greased tiny muffin pans 2/3 full.  Bake in
preheated 350-degree oven for 20-25 minutes.

In a plastic bag mix sugar and cinnamon.  Dip doughnut
tops in butter and shake in bake to coat.
