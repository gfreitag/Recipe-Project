Title: TROUT BAKED IRISH STYLE
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Scot/irish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Dried basil leaves
4    Whole trout (@ about 8 oz.);
-drawn
-salt
-JUDY GARNETT PJXG05A
4    Green onions; sliced
1    Green pepper; chopped
1/4 c  Margarine or butter
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/4 c  Fresh parsley; snipped
Cook and stir onions and pepper in margarine until onions are
tender; remove from heat. Stir in bread crumbs, parsley, the lemon
juice, 1 tsp. salt and the basil.
Rub cavities of fish with salt; stuff each with about 1/4 c.
stuffing. Place fish in greased oblong baking dish, 13 1/2x9x2
inches. Cook uncovered in 350 deg. oven until fish flakes easily with
fork, 30 to 35 minutes. Garnish fish with cherry tomatoes and parsley
if desired.
Source: Betty Crocker's International Cookbook
