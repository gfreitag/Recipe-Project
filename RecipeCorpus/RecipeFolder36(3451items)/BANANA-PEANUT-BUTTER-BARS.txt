Title: BANANA PEANUT BUTTER BARS
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts, Nuts/grains
Yield: 48 servings
1 c  Bananas, mashed
4 c  Uncooked rolled oats
1/2 c  Peanut butter, creamy
3/4 c  Margarine, softened
1 c  Dark brown sugar, packed
1    Egg
1/2 ts Salt
In a large bowl, beat butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
Beat in egg, salt, bananas and peanut butter. Stir in the rolled
oats. Mix well. Spread in a greased 13 X 9 X 2-inch pan. Bake in a
350 F. oven for 1 hour or until a wooden pick inserted in the center
comes out clean. Cut into 2 x 1 inch bars. Makes about 4 dozen bars.
