Title: HELEN'S FAVORITE BANANA BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1    Egg
1/4 c  Oil
1 c  Bananas
1/2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Sugar
1 3/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
3/4 ts Salt
2 ts Orange rind, grated
Sift first 5 ingredients, combine remaining ingredients, stir into
dry mix, bake at 350 for 50 minutes in a greased lined loaf pan.
