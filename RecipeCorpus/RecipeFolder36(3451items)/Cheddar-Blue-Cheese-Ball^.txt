Title: Cheddar Blue Cheese Ball^
Categories: Appetizers, Holiday
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  (1oz) blue cheese, crumbled
ds Celery salt
ds Onion salt
1/2 c  (4oz) chopped walnuts
1 pk (8oz) cream cheese,
Softened
1 pk (10oz) sharp Cheddar cold
Pack cheese spread
Beat cream cheese, add in Cheddar until well mixed. Stir in blue
cheese, celery and onion salts to taste. Roll into a ball then roll
in nuts. Chill several hours until firm.
