Title: Grandma's Gravy
Categories: Condiments, Holiday, Snacks
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Chicken broth
Black pepper to taste
1 cn Creamy chicken-mushroom
Soup
Stir together and cook until thickened.  Serve over Grandma's
Dressing, with cranberry sauce.
