Title: Cranberry Casserole
Categories: Side dish, Holiday
Yield: 1 recipe
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  White sugar
3 c  Granny Smith Apples,
-peeled and chopped
2 c  Raw cranberries
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 1/3 c  Brown sugar
1    Stick butter, melted
1 1/3 c  Quick oats
1 c  Chopped walnuts or pecans
Preheat oven to 325

In a shallow 2 qt. casserole, mix cranberries and
chopped apples.

In a small bowl mix lemon juice and white sugar.
Sprinkle this mixture over the cranberries and apples
and mix together until just moistened.

In another bowl mix together all ingredients for
topping and sprikle over the top of the cranberry
mixture.

Bake at 325 for 1 hr. and 15 min.

*Be sure to use quick oats.  If regular oats are used
the recipe will be runny.
