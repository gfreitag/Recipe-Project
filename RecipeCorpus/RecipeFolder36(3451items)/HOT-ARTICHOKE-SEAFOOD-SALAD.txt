Title: HOT ARTICHOKE SEAFOOD SALAD
Categories: Blank
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Fresh crab meat, drained
-and picked through
1/2 ts Tabasco sauce
1/2 c  Toasted almonds
Assorted crackers
14 oz Can artichoke hearts,
-drained and finely
-chopped
2 c  Mayonnaise
2 c  Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Liberally grease a 1 1/2 quart
glass baking dish.  Combine artichoke hearts, mayonnaise, cheese,
crab meat, and Tabasco sauce.  Mix well and spoon into the baking
dish.  Top with almonds.  Bake for about 15 to 20 minutes or until
hot.  Serve on crackers.
