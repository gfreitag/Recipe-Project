Title: Sopa Castilla la Vieja (Almond Soup)
Categories: Soup/stew, Spanish
Yield: 6 servings
3 sl Bread; toasted
-- cut into 1-inch strips
1/3 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 c  Toasted sliced almonds
3 cn Condensed consomme'
(10-1/2 ounces each)
3    Soup cans water
1/2 c  Ground blanched almonds
1 tb Olive oil
Heat consomme' and water.  Mix almonds and oil and add to soup.  Beat
until well blended.  Spoon soup into individual heat-proof bowls.
Top bowls with toast strips.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Broil until
cheese is golden. Sprinkle with sliced almonds.

Adaption from recipe by Chef Mariano Bocos, The Spanish Pavilion
Campbell's Great Restaurants Cookbook, U.S.A.
