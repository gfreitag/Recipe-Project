Title: RED DEVIL RUB
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------BEEF POULTRY,PORK,FISH------------------------
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1/4 c  Dijon mustard with seeds
2 ts Olive oil
1/4 c  Finely slivered fresh basil
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mix thoroughly.
Makes about 1/2 cup.
