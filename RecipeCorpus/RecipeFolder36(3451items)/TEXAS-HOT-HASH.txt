Title: TEXAS HOT HASH
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/2 ts Chili powder
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
8 oz Can tomato sauce
1/4 c  Sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
-(optional)
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 lg Onion, thinly sliced
1 lg Green pepper, finely chopped
1    Clove garlic,
-peeled and crushed
16 oz Can stewed tomatoes
3/4 c  Instant rice
1. In a deep, 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole crumble
the ground beef. Add the onion, green pepper and crushed garlic.
2. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 6 minutes or until meat is
browned. Stir occasionally to break up the meat.
3. Add the remaining ingredients except the Cheddar cheese.. Mix
throughly to combine.
4. Heat, covered, in the Microwave Oven 7 minutes.
5. lf desired, sprinkle Cheddar cheese over the top of the meat
mixture. 6. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 1 1/2 minutes or until
cheese melts.
