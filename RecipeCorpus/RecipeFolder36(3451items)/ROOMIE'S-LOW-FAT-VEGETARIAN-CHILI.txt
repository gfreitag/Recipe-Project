Title: ROOMIE'S LOW FAT VEGETARIAN CHILI
Categories: Chili, Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Lime or 2 T lemon juice
1 tb Chili powder
1 1/2 ts Oregano
1 ts Olive oil
2    Medium onions
2    Cloves of garlic, minced
56 oz Canned tomatoes or 3lb fresh    1 1/2 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Cinnamon
12 oz Light beer
Dry roasted peanuts
Cheddar cheese
15 1/2 oz Red kidney beans
15 oz White kidney beans
15 oz Chickpeas
1 1/2 c  Chopped celery
1    Green pepper, chopped
1. In a large pot, heat oil and saute onions and garlic until soft.
2. Add all ingredients except beer, peanuts and cheddar cheese.  Cover
and simmer 1 1/2 hours.
3. Add beer and simmer uncovered for 30 minutes.
4. Add handful of peanuts and sprinkle cheese on each serving.
