Title: Chocolate Bread Pudding
Categories: Puddings
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Cocoa
2/3 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
Pinch of salt
1 ts Vanilla
Mix and let soak for about
1 half hour:
2 c  Bread in 1 inch cubes
4 c  Milk
Mix and add to the above:
Cook for 1 hour at 300 degrees or until a knife comes out clean.  If
you have one, use a large iron skillet to bake the pudding in.
