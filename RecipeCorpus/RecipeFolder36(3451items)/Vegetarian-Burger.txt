Title: Vegetarian Burger
Categories: Main dish, Bbq/grill, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 burgers
1/4 ts Celery seeds
1 ts Sesame oil
1 ts Lite soymilk
1 c  Basmatic rice, cooked
1 tb Sesame seeds
1 tb Cracked wheat germ
1/2 c  Broccoli, chopped
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Carrot, chopped
2 tb Parsley, fresh, minced
2 tb Basil, fresh, minced
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
In a food processor, blend together the broccoli, onion, carrot,
parsley, basil, garlic, celery seeds, oil & soymilk.  Add the rice &
blend until the mixture resembles a coarse paste.  Transfer to a
large mixing bowl.

Mix in the sesame seeds & wheatgerm.  Form into 6 patties & cook
either on a foil covered grill or in a skillet until golden brown on
both sides.

Brian Stone, printed in "Vegetarian Gourmet" Summer, 1995
