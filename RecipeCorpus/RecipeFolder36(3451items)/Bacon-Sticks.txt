Title: Bacon Sticks
Categories: Appetizers, Meats, Breads, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
5 sl Of bacon, cut lengthwise
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
10    Thin bread sticks, any
-flavor
Dredge one side of bacon strip in cheese.  Whith cheese side out, roll
bacon around bread stick diagonally.  Place sticks on microwave dish
or paper plate lined with paper towels.  Microwave on high 4-1/2 to 6
minutes.  When done, roll in cheese again.

Origin: Women's Circle, for cooks on the go
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/94
