Title: SWEET FRIED BANNANAS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 24 servings
2 tb Flour
1 tb Olive oil
Egg white (beaten stiff)
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Olive oil (for frying)
4    Bananas Large
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Lemon Juice
1/2 ts Grated orange peel
3 oz Rum
Cut bananas into halves lengthwise. sprinkle with sugar, lemon juice,
orange peel, and Rum.  Let stand for 2 hours.
Make a batter of flour, water, and 1 tablespoon oil, mixing well, and
blend in stiff egg white.   Drain bananas, dip into batter and fry in
hot olive oil until just golden in color.
Sprinkle with sugar and serve hot.
