Title: HASTY PUDDING
Categories: Main dish, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Yellow cornmeal
4 c  Water
1 ts Salt
Bring water and salt to boil over direct heat in top part of double
boiler; sprinkle in cornmeal, stirring constantly. Cook over boiling
water for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Sweeten to taste with
maple syrup, honey, molasses, or sugar; serve in deep bowls with milk
or cream.
