Title: Apricot Meat Loaf
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Grated orange rind
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Fresh bread crumbs
2 tb Diced dried apricots
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 tb Slivered almonds
1 ts Vegetable oil
1 sm Onion, chopped
3/4 ts Ground cumin AND coriander
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Salt and pepper (each)
1 ea Egg
Saute the onions until softened.  Set aside. Mix together the egg,
orange rind and juice.  Add the cooled onions and spices.  Stir in the
meat, bread crumbs and apricots.  Mix together until blended and shape
into a loaf form in shallow pan. (or use loaf pans) Sprinkle almonds
on top and bake in a 350F oven for about 60-75 minutes or until no
longer pink inside.
Origin: Canadian Living, October, 1995
