Title: SESAME, MUSTARD RUB
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------BEEF,POULTRY,PORK,LAMB,FISH---------------------
2 ts Lime juice
2 tb Sesame oil
1    Garlic clove; crushed
1 ts Mustard seeds
1/2 ts Finely slivered lime peel
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mash until fairly
smooth. Makes about 3-1/2 Tbsp.
