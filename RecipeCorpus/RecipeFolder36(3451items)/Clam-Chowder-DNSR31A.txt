Title: Clam Chowder DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
16 oz Clams, minced, undrained
2 c  Milk
2 tb Butter
2 ea Bacon slice
4 tb Onion, diced
2 ea Potato, small, diced
1 c  Water
1 ts Salt or to taste
Cook bacon in skilled until crisp.  Drain.  Saute
onion in bacon grease until tender.  Cook potato in
water, salt and pepper until tender.  Stir in clams,
milk, butter, and onion.  Simmer for 3 minutes.
Sprinkle bacon over each serving.  Formatted by Mary
Dishongh Bowles.
