Title: Confetti Bean Chili
Categories: Chili
Yield: 12 servings
2 cn (14 1/2 oz each) diced
-tomatoes, undrained OR
3 c  Diced fresh tomatoes
15 oz Can black beans *
15 oz Can kidney beans *
15 oz Can garbanzo beans *
2 1/2 c  Water
1 lg Onion, chopped
2 cn Chicken broth
2    Cloves garlic, minced
3 tb Chili powder
1 1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1 lb Carrots, sliced
1 lb Red potatoes, cubed
* Rinsed and drained

In a Dutch oven, simmer onion in broth for 5 minutes.
Add next six ingredients: bring to a boil.  Reduce
heat. Cover, simmer 10 minutes.  Add remaining
ingredients.  Cover simmer 20 minutes.
