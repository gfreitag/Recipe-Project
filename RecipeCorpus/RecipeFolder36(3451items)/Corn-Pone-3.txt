Title: Corn Pone 3
Categories: Oldies, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Corn meal
Milk
1/2 c  Shortening
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
Cream shortening and sugar, beat in eggs. Stir in baking powder, corn
meal and enough milk to make a light cake batter. Bake in a slow oven
(300 degrees) until it tests done, 45 minutes to an hour.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
