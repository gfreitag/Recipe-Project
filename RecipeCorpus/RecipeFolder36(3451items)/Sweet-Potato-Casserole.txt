Title: Sweet Potato Casserole
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles, Holiday, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2    Stick margarine or butter,
Melted.
3 c  Mashed sweet potatoes
2/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Mix ingredients together and pack in 2 qt casserole.

Cut 1/2 c sugar, 1/2 c flour and 1/2 stick butter together. Sprinkle
on top of potatoes.

Sprinkle with pecans.

Bake at 350 for 30 min.
