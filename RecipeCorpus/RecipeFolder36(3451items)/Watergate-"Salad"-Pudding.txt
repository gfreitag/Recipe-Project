Title: Watergate "Salad" Pudding
Categories: Puddings, Desserts, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1 sm Bag pecan pieces
1 sm Can crushed pineapple
12 oz Cool Whip
1 sm Box pistachio pudding
1/2    Bag little marshmallows
Mix together crushed pineapple and pistachio pudding; add Cool Whip
mixing thoroughly; add marshmallow and pecan pieces. Chill in
refrigerator for 30 minutes or until ready to eat.
