Title: ELEGANT CHICKEN SALAD
Categories: Poultry, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Dijon mustard
1/4 c  Chives
2 tb Tarragon; chopped
Salt and pepper
3 c  Rice (wild or white)
1/4 c  Olive oil
2    Chicken breasts
3 c  Chicken broth or water
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
2 tb Orange juice
Poach chicken in broth or water for about 20 minutes. In salad bowl
combine vinegar, juice, and mustard. Gradually whisk in oil. Stir in
chives and tarragon and season with salt and pepper. Cut chicken into
bite sized pieces. Add chicken and rice to dressing. Toss well.
