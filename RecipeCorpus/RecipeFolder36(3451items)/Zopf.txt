Title: Zopf
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
4 tb Unsalted butter
3/4 c  Water
1 lg Egg
1    Egg yolk
2 1/4 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar
MMMMM-------------------------EGG WASH:------------------------------
1 lg Egg -- beaten w/2 pin salt
Add all ingredients except egg wash.  Set machine for knead and first
rise.  Remove dough and punch down.  Let rest for 5 minutes before
hand shaping.  Lightly sprinkle work surface with flour.  Divide
dough into 3 equal pieces.  Dampen hands and roll each piece into a
16" long rope. Sprinkle dough with flour if too sticky.  Lay ropes
next to each other and pinch top ends together .  Braid the ropes and
pinch the remaining ends together.  Place on a lightly greased 13 x 9
baking pan.  Tuck ends under. Cover with a clean kitchen cloth and
let rise until doubled in size, about 1 to 1 1/2 hours, and set
aside.  Preheat oven to 375.  Prepare egg wash.  Brush braid with egg
wash.  Wait 5 minutes.  Brush again.  Wait 15 minutes to let egg wash
dry.  Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from oven and
cool on wire rack.
