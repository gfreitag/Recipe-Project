Title: Crab Casserole
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Guestchefs
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Diced Green Pepper
4 lg Beaten Eggs
3 c  Milk
10 1/2 oz Cream Of Mushroom Soup
Grated Cheddar Cheese
8    Slices Bread
2 c  Crabmeat
1 c  Diced Onion
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
1 c  Chopped Celery
Paprika To Taste
Cook the celery for 10 minutes in a small amount of water.  Drain the
celery.  Dice 4 slices of bread into the casserole.  Mix the crabmeat,
onion, Miracle Whip, green peppers and celery.  Place over bread in
casserole.  Dice remaining 4 slices of bread and place on top of
crabmeat mixture.  Mix the eggs and milk; pour over top.  Bake for 15
minutes in a 325øF oven.  Spoon soup over top.  Add grated cheese and
paprika. Continue to bake at 325øF for one hour.  Serve hot.  From:
Syd's Cookbook.
