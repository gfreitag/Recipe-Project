Title: GREEN BEANS WITH MUSHROOMS AND CHERRY TOMATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Olive oil*
1 c  Fresh sliced mushrooms
1 c  Cherry tomatoes, halved
1 1/2 lb Fresh green beans, washed
-and trimmed
1 md Onion, finely chopped
2 ts Fresh garlic, chopped
Steam green beans until tender.  Drain and set aside. Saute onion and
garlic in a medium skillet coated with olive oil, until tender. About
2-3 minutes. Add mushrooms and saute until tender.  Stir in green
beans and tomotoes until heated.
