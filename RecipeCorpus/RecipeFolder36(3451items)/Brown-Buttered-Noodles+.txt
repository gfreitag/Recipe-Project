Title: Brown Buttered Noodles+
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Onions, diced
Salt and pepper
1 pk Egg noodles, medium width
1/2 c  Butter
Cook noodles according to package directions, about 5 minutes. Drain.
Meanwhile, in large saucepan, melt butter. Saute onions til tender.
Add salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Origin: Helen Vujovitz - Pioneer Press morning newspaper.
