Title: Busy Day Meatloaf^
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Cream of mushroom
1 pk Onion soup mix
2 lb Lean ground beef
1 cn Tomato soup
Form meat into loaf and freeze. When ready to bake, place in  a large
piece of foil, pour soups over top seal and bake ata 300F. for 4
hours.
