Title: Kidri
Categories: Main dish, Middle east, Pasta/rice
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Garlic powder
2 ea Potatoes, peeled & diced
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 lb Beef, good steak or roast
4 c  Rice, uncooked
2 ts Beef bouillion
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 ea Can garbanzo beans
Dice beef in small cubes.  In large stock pot, sautee beef and onions
until beef is half cooked.  Stir in potatoes, spices, and drained
garbanzo beans.  Add boullion, and cook for a few minutes.  Add dry
rice and 8 cups water.  Stir well. Allow to come to a boil.  Cover
and reduce heat.  Cook until rice is tender.  (30-45 minutes).

Barghouti family recipe
