Title: Pork Cutlet With Mint And Cranberry
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Herbs/spice, Fruit
Yield: 10 servings
300 ml Cranberry Sauce
Salt & Pepper
Mint to Taste
10 x  230g Pork Cutlets, trimmed
5 g  Cayenne Pepper
40 ml Oil
Method
1. Season and oil pork
2. Cook on chargrill with a low heat.
Serving suggestion
Serve with French Fries and Carrot. Garnish with Cranberry
Sauce and mint.
