Title: Tom Morstad's Baileys Irish Creme
Categories: Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
-Chocolate Syrup
1/2 ts Coconut Extract
1/2 ts Almond Extract
2 cn Evaporated milk
1 c  Irish Whiskey
1/2 c  Vodka
1/2 tb Hershey's
Put in blender.  Walk around table three times to age to perfection.
Blend. *** KEEP REFRIGERATED ***
