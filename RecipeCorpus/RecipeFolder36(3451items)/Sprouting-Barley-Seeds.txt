Title: Sprouting Barley Seeds
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Quart jar
1 c  To 1-1/2 cups barley seeds
Water to 3 times seeds
Use 1 to 1-1/2 cups seeds in a one quart jar, which will yield about 1
quart of sprouts. Soak for 12 hours.

Rinse 2 to 3 times daily for 2 to 3 days.

The sprouts will be the length of the seed. Use in salads,
casseroles, and breads.
