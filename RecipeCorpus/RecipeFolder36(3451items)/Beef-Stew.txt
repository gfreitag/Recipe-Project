Title: Beef Stew
Categories: Meats, Crockpot, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------TYPED BY HELEN PEAGRAM------------------------
1    Bay leaf
Salt to taste
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ts Paprika
3    Onions, quartered
1    Celery stalk, cut up
3    Carrots, cut up
3    Potatoes, cut up
2 lb Beef chuck or stew meat in
-1 1/2 inch cubes
1 c  Water or beef stock
1 ts Worcestershire
1    Garlic clove
Put all ingredients in crock pot in order listed.  Stir just enough
to mix spices.  Cover and set to low for 10-12 hours. (High 5-6 hours)
