Title: FILET DE TOFU WITH APRICOT DIJON SAUCE
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables, Fruit, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Orange juice
1 c  Apricot puree or jam
2 lb Extra firm tofu
-- thinly sliced
1 sm Onion, chopped
2 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
Vegetable oil
1 tb Tamari
1 ts Dijon mustard
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
2 pn Dill
Scallions, chopped
Pan-fry onion and garlic in oil.  Gradually add tamari, mustard,
orange juice, and apricot puree.  Stir until you have a smooth sauce.

In separate pan, fry tofu in oil, and add to sauce.  Cook mixture
over low heat until sauce reduces by half.

Place tofu in serving dish, and garnish with chopped shallots and
dill. Serve with stir-fried vegetables, rice, or a salad.

John Paino & Lisa Messinger, "The Tofu Book"  Posted by Karen Mintzias
