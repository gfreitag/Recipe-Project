Title: Fifteen Bean Soup (Vegan)
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
3    Cans DelMonte Italian style
-stewed tomatoes
1 ts Chili powder
Salt and pepper to taste
1    1 lb 4 oz 15 bean soup
-package.  Follow dirs
-on pkg for soaking
1    Onions, chopped
1    Clove garlic, chopped
After soaking beans overnight, drain the beans and add remaining
ingredients (do not drain tomatoes).  Cook over low heat for several
hours until beans are tender.
