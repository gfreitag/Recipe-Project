Title: Seafood Tagine
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
-cut into sticks
6 md Tomatoes -- sliced
2 sm Lemons -- thinly sliced
1 sm Onion -- thinly sliced
2 lb Monkfish -- 4 thick slices
1 c  Fresh parsley -- chopped
1/2 c  Cilantro -- chopped
5    Cloves garlic -- minced
3/4 c  Olive oil
Juice of 1 lemon
2 ts Tumeric
2 ts Ground cumin
2 ts Paprika
Salt
Freshly ground pepper
6 md Carrots -- peeled and
Combine oil, lemon juice, tumeric, cumin, paprika and salt
and pepper to taste in a bowl.  Set aside.  Place half the
carrots sticks on bottom of 2-quart baking pan or Dutch
oven.  Cover with half the tomatoes. lemon and onion slices.
Arrange fish slices on top.  Sprinkle with some of the parsley,
cilantro and garlic.

Cover with remaining tomaotes, lemon and onion slices.
Sprinkle remaining parsley, cilantro and garlic.  Top with
remaining carrots.  Pour oil-lemon sauce over all.  cover
tightly and bake in a preheated 350-degree oven until
vegetables are tender, 50 minutes to 1 hour.
