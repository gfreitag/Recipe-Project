Title: South of the Border Casserole
Categories: Tex/mexican, Casseroles, Pasta/rice, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1    (4-oz) can diced green
-chilies drained
2 ts Beef flavor instant bouillon
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1/4 ts Ground cumin
1 ds Cayenne pepper
1 1/2 c  (6-oz) cubed muenster cheese
1 1/2 c  (6-oz) cubed cheddar cheese
1    (7-oz) pkg elbow macaroni -
-2 cup uncooked
1 lb Ground turkey
1 md Onion chopped
1 c  Sliced celery
1/2 c  Sliced pitted ripe olives
1    (16-oz) can whole tomatoes
-cut up and undrained
Prepare elbow macaroni according to package directions; drain. In
medium skillet, combine ground turkey, onion, celery and garlic. Cook
until turkey is browned. Stir in next seven ingredients. SImmer 10
minutes. Combine macaroni, meat mixture and cheeses. Pour into 3
quart casserole. Cover. Bake
in 350 degree oven until hot and cheeses melt, about 20 minutes.
Garnish as desired. Refrigerate leftovers.
