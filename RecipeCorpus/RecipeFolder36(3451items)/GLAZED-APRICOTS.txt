Title: GLAZED APRICOTS
Categories: Relishes, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Brown sugar
1    Cinnamon stick
1 lb Apricot halves, canned
1 tb Butter
Drain Syrup from apricots.  Add butter, sugar, and cinnamon to syrup
and boil 3 minutes.  Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes.  Serve hot
with poultry or meat.
