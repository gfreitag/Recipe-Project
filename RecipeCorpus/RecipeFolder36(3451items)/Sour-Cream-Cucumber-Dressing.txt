Title: Sour Cream Cucumber Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Dill; fresh, chopped OR
1/2 ts Dried dill
1 ts [or more] grated onion
1 ds Hot pepper sauce
1/2 md Cucumber
1 c  Dairy sour cream
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Sugar [opt'l]
1/2 ts Salt  [opt'l]
Halve the cucumber lengthwise, removing the seeds and then cut the
cucumber in pieces.  Add to a blender containing all of the rest
of the ingredients and blend in short bursts until the cucumber is
finely chopped. (When necessary, stop the blender and use a rubber
spatula to scrape down the sides.) Refrigerate at least one hour
to chill and allow flavors to blend before using.

Makes 1 3/4 cups.
