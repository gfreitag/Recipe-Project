Title: Hot Beef Spread #2
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 recipe
1/2    Green pepper, chopped
1/4 ts Pepper
4 oz Can mushrooms
16 oz Cream cheese
1 c  Sour cream
4 oz Chipped beef
1    Onion, chopped
Combine all ingredients. Put into a baking dish. Bake
at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Serve hot with your
favorite crackers.
