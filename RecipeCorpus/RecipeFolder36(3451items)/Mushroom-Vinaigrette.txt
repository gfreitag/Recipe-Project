Title: Mushroom Vinaigrette
Categories: Condiments, Vegetables, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
Peeled and minced
2 tb Champagne vinegar
Or apple cider vinegar
1 tb Basil leaves, minced
1 tb Parsley leaves, minced
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 c  Cremini mushrooms,
Washed, dried,
Thinly sliced
1/4 lg Yellow onion(s)
Peeled and finely diced
1    Garlic clove(s)
Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the
mushrooms, onion, and garlic, and saute until soft, stirring
constantly, about 7 min. Remove the pan from the heat, place the
mushroom mixture in the bowl of a food processor, and blend on high
speed until creamy, about 1 min. Add the vinegar, basil, and parsley,
and blend for 15 seconds. Season with salt and pepper and serve warm
or cold.
