Title: Green Tomatoes
Categories: Vegetarian, Tomatoes, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
Pepper
Garlic
Oil
2 md Green tomatoes, thinly
-sliced
Flour
Salt
From Mark Drake:

Combine flour, salt, pepper and garlic.  Dip tomatoes in seasoned
flour, then fry in hot oil.  Serve hot.

****************************************************************

From Brian Murrey:

Slice 'em up, roll in a mix of corn meal, salt, and pepper.  then
fry them in light oil.

They're pretty good batter fried as well.
