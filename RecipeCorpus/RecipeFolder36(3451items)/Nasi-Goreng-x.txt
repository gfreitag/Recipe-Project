Title: Nasi Goreng x
Categories: Pasta/rice, Main dish, Indonesian
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Dried shrimp, ground
1/2 tb Blachan
1 c  Bean shoots
1    Spring onion, chopped
A little shredded lettuce
1 tb Fried shallots
2 tb Vegetable oil
1    Egg
2 c  Cooked rice
1/2 c  Cooked potato, cubed
1/2 c  Fried beancurd, cubed
1/2    Onion, sliced
Heat oil and lightly scramble egg, then add rice, potato,
beancurd and onion.

Stir fry over high heat for a minute or two, then add shrimp and
blachan.  Keep stirring.

Toss in bean shoots and spring onion, and stir fry for a few
minutes.  Keep frying over high heat until rice feels dry but
clingy.

Serve sprinkled with lettuce and shallots.
