Title: Calzones
Categories: Breadmaker, Italian
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
3 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
2 tb Olive oil
1 c  Water -- plus 2 tbsp
1/2 ts Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
8 oz Italian sausage
1 tb Oil
15 oz Ricotta cheese
1/2 c  Mozzarella cheese -- grated
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
In heavy skillet cook sausages in 1 tbsp oil.  Cool to room
temperature and slice into 1/4" pieces.  All all dough ingredients
and program for knead and first rise.  When done remove and press
down.  Let rest for 5 minutes.  Prepare fillings and set aside.
Preheat oven to 400.  On lightly floured work surface, roll dough
into 6" long cylinder.  Cut dough into 6 equal pieces.  Lightly
sprinkle balls of dough with flour.  Roll out each ball of dough into
a 1/4" thick circle.  Place 1/6 of the cheese filling in the center
of each circle.  Place 1/6 of the sausage slices on top of the
cheese.  With your fingertip, wet the inside circle with water. Fold
the dough over to make a half-moon and seal.  Fold the edge up and
crimp so that the calzone is well sealed.  Place on lightly oiled
baking sheets.  Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown.  Serve hot.
