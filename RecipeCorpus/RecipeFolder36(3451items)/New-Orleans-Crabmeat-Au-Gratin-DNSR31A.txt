Title: New Orleans Crabmeat Au Gratin DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Parsley, fresh minced
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Red pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
1/8 ts White pepper
1 1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Flour
1 1/2 ts Butter
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 lb Crabmeat, fresh lump
1/3 c  Green onions, chopped
3 tb Parmesan cheese, grated
Combine milk and flour in blender and process smooth.
Melt butter in heavy pan over low heat.  Add flour
mixture and cook over medium heat, stirring
constantly, until thick.  Add cheese and stir until
melted.  Stir in remaining ingredients and mix well.
Heat through.  Formatted by Mary Dishongh Bowles.
