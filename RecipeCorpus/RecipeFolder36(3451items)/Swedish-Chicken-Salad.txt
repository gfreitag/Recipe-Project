Title: Swedish Chicken Salad
Categories: Poultry, Salads, West europe
Yield: 1 servings
-washed and dried
2    Hard-cooked eggs, shelled
-and cut into wedges
6    Stuffed olives, sliced
2 tb Capers, drained
3 tb Finely chopped dill pickles
3 c  Diced cold, cooked chicken
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/3 c  Sour cream
2    To 3 tsps. curry powder
-Salt, pepper to taste
-Crisp lettuce leaves,
This curry-flavored chicken salad, called honssallad, is a Swedish
smorgasbord specialty, but it may also be served as a luncheon
entree.

Combine chicken with mayonnaise, sour cream, and curry powder. Season
with salt
and pepper and mix well. Refrigerate 1 hour or longer to blend
flavors. When
ready to serve, arrange lettuce leaves on a platter and spoon salad
over them. Decorate with remaining ingredients.
SERVES 4 TO 6.
