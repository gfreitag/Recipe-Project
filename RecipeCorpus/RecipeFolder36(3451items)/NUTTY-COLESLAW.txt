Title: NUTTY COLESLAW
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 10 servings
2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Seasoned salt
2 tb Red wine vinegar
2/3 c  Bottled Italian dressing
6 c  Shredded cabbage
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
1/3 c  Chopped celery
1/3 c  Unsalted, roasted peanuts
Combine cabbage, green onions, celery and peanuts in large bowl. Blend
sugar, salt, vinegar and dressing. Pour over cabbage mixture and toss
well.
