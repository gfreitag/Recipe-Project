Title: Roasted Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Side dish, Potatoes
Yield: 1 recipe
Thyme
4    Peeled, cubed sweet potatoes
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Preheat oven to 375 deg F

Lightly oil roasting pan (spraying is good) Place
cubed sweet potatoes in pan and drizzle/spray a little
oil on them.

Sprinkle thyme all over. Toss and Sprinkle again.

Roast in oven for 30 minutes, tossing halfway through
the cooking time.

They come out a little like roast potatoes/baked fries.
