Title: Spiced Nuts
Categories: Snacks, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 cups
1/4 ts Cayenne
2 tb Plus 2 tsp
Brown sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 lb Pecan halves
(abt 2 1/2 cups)
1/2 lb Blanched almonds
(abt 1 1/2 cups)
3 tb Butter
1 tb Orange zest,
Finely chopped
2 ts Lemon zest
Finely chopped
2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 1/2 ts Ground coriander seeds
1 1/4 ts Ground mace
Heat the oven to 300 F. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over
medium-low heat. Add the zests, cinnamon, coriander, mace, cayenne,
brown sugar and salt. Combine until the mixture is bubbly and well
blended. Add the nuts and stir to coat them evenly. Transfer to a
baking sheet, spread the mixture evenly, and bake for 25 min,
stirring every 5-7 min. For crisper nuts, increase the cooking time
to 35-40 min.
