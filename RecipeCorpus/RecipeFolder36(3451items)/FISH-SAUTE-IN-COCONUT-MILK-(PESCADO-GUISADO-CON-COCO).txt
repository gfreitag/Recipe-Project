Title: FISH SAUTE IN COCONUT MILK (PESCADO GUISADO CON COCO)
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1/4 c  Sweet green pepper, sliced t
1/4 c  Tomato, sliced
1/4 c  Celery, chopped
1 lb Whole sea bass,
1 x  Red snapper, mackerel or sim
1 c  Rich Coconut Milk (see below
1/2 ts Salt, or to taste
Score the whole fish 3 times diagonally on each side.
Bring the coconut milk to a boil in a skillet over moderate heat. Add
the fish and baste for 3 minutes.  Then add the salt, black pepper,
sweet green pepper, tomato and celery.  Continue to baste for 5
minutes.
Simmer the fish in an uncovered skillet over low heat, turning the
fish over once, for 10 minutes.
Serve warm. Source: False Tongues and Sunday Bread by Copeland Marks
