Title: Cherry Cheesecake
Categories: Desserts, Guestchefs
Yield: 6 servings
1    9" Graham Cracker Crust
1 cn Cherry Pie Filling
8 oz Softened Cream Cheese
1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  Thawed Cool Whip
Beat cream cheese and sugar until creamy.  Blend in Cool Whip.  Pour
into unbaked pie shell.  Top with cherry filling.  Chill for at least
3 hours before serving.  From: Syd's Cookbook.
