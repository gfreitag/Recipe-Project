Title: LAMB CUTLETS IN AROMATIC COFFEE SAUCE
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 ts Mixed spice
1/4 tb Lemon juice
30 g  Ghee
6    Lamb cutlets
Boiled rice
Prunes, pitted
Fresh rosemary for garnish
60 g  Butter
1/3 c  Strong coffee
1/3 c  Honey
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Mace
1/4 ts Cardamom
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Melt butter on a low heat, mix in coffee, then honey, Worcestershire
sauce, spices and lemon juice.   Cover and remove from heat.

Melt ghee in a large frying pan, add cutlets and fry until browned on
both sides.   Return sauce to heat, bring to boil and pour over
cutlets.

Serve with boiled rice and pitted prunes, heated through in the
sauce, if desired.  Garnish with rosemary.
