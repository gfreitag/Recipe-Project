Title: FOUR HOUR STEW
Categories: Meats, Misc
Yield: 6 servings
6    Carrots sliced
3    Stalks of celery sliced
1 cn Mushroom soup
1 cn Water
1 1/2 lb Stew beef
1    Onion sliced
1 pk Onion soup mix
3 ts Minute tapioca
Grease casserole and put in diced beef, add rest of ingredients in
order given. Cover and bake in 250 F oven for 4 hours. Very good.
Recipe from N.B. Women's institute Cook Book.
