Title: Low-Salt Vegetable-Stuffed Flounder Rolls - SL 1/87
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
-steak sauce
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Low-sodium chicken-flavored
-bouillon granules
2 tb Lemon juice, divided
8 ea 4-oz flounder fillets
Paprika
Parsley sprigs (optional)
Lemon slices (optional)
Vegetable cooking spray
10 oz Package frozen chopped
-broccoli; thawed and well-
-drained
1/2 c  Carrots; shredded
1/4 c  Green onions; chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper; chopped
1/4 c  Celery; chopped
1 cl Garlic; minced
1 1/2 ts Low-sodium herb-and-spice
Cooking time: about 45 minutes.

Place a skillet over medium heat until hot; coat with cooking spray.
Saute broccoli and next 5 ingredients until crisp-tender; stir in
steak sauce, ginger, pepper, bouillon granules, and 1 tablespoon
lemon juice. Spoon 1/8 of vegetable mixture onto each fillet;
carefully roll up and place seam-side down in a 12"x8"x2" baking dish
coated with cooking spray. Brush with remaining 1 tablespoon lemon
juice and sprinkle with paprika. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 20
to 25 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.
Garnish with parsley and lemon slices if desired. Yield: 8 servings.

86 calories, 15.5g protein, .8g fat, 3.9g carbohydrate, 42.5mg
cholesterol, 82.3mg sodium, 36.5mg calcium per *3-oz* serving.
