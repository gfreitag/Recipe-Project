Title: Yogurt & Onion Salad
Categories: Salads, Middle east
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Yogurt, plain
1 lg Onion, purple
2 tb Black pepper
Finely dice purple onion.  Stir in yogurt and black pepper.  Salt may
be added to taste.  Allow to sit for at least 30 minutes or
refrigerate overnight for flavors to mix.  Serve with breads, meats,
or over rice.

Barghouti family recipe
