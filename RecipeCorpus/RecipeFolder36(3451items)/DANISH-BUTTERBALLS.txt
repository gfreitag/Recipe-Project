Title: DANISH BUTTERBALLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1 c  Pecans, fine chopped
Cream together butter and sugar. Add other ingredients.  Work with
hands. make into 1 inch balls.  Put on greased cookie sheet.  bake
for 30 minutes at 300 F. Cool and roll in powdered sugar or colored
sprinkles. ( red and green for Christmas)
