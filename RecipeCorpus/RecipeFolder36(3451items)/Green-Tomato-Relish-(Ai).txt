Title: Green Tomato Relish (Ai)
Categories: Amerindian, Tomatoes, Relishes, Can/preserv
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Salt
1 pk Green tomatoes, sliced
6 lg Onions
Set overnight; drain and rinse. Add water to get salt out and boil a
few minutes, drain again. Add vinegar to cover tomatoes and onions.
Add 4 pounds brown sugar, 1 stick cinnamon. Boil 2 hours until thick.
Can. 35 min.

SOURCE: Doris Kenyon, Allegany Seneca, Iroquois Cookbook
SHARED BY: Jim Bodle 10/92
