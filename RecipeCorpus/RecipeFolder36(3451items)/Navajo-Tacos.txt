Title: Navajo Tacos
Categories: Tex/mexican, Main dish, Kooknet
Yield: 1 servings
Chili powder
Salt and pepper
Grated cheese
Lettuce
or
Hamburger
Minced onion
Mexe-beans   or
Chili beans
Tomato sauce
Tomato juice
Fry hamburger and onion.  Add beans and season to taste. Add tomato
sauce, simmer. Serve with grated cheese and lettuce.
