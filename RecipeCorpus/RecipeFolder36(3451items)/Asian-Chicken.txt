Title: Asian Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Brown sugar
1    Clove garlic -- finely
Chopped
1/4 ts Pepper
4    Skinless boneless chicken
-breast halves
3 tb Soy sauce
1 1/2 tb Red wine vinegar
Preheat the oven to 350.  Lightly oil a glass baking dish large
enough to hold chicken breasts in a single layer.  Place chicken
breasts in the dish.  Combine remaining ingredients and pour over
chicken.  Bake for 20 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
Serve hot or cold.
