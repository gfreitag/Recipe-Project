Title: CHEESE DELIGHTS
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 30 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Rice Krispies
4 oz Sharp cheddar cheese, grated
3 tb Butter
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
Preheat oven to 350øF.  Set out a baking sheet.

Mix cheese and butter till well blended.  Stir in flour and salt and
mix well.  Add Rice Krispies and work in by hand.

Divide dough into half, then cut each half into 15 pieces. Roll each
piece into a ball, placing on baking sheet, and flatten with a fork.

Bake in preheated 350øF oven for about 10 minutes. Makes at least 30
snacks.  Store in a sealed container (like empty coffee can).

From Ethel Meyers
