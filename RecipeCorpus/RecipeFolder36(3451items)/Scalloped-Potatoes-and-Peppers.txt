Title: Scalloped Potatoes and Peppers
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
Salt and pepper to taste^
1 cn Cream of Mushroom soup
1 cn Milk
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese grated
1/2 c  Mozzarella cheese grated
4 lg Potatoes, sliced 1/8 in
1/3 c  Sweet red Pepper, sliced
1/3 c  Sweet green pepper, sliced
1/3 c  Sweet yellow pepper, sliced
1/2 c  Onion, minced
Spray 13 by 9 baking dish with Vegetable Oil Spray.  Mix milk and soup
together. layer as follows, potatoes, peppers, onion, salt and pepper,
cheese for 2 layers, then for last layer, top with soup mixture, then
top with cheese.

Bake 350 for 1 hour.   covered, uncover and bake 10 minutes longer

let sit for about 10 minutes before serving.
