Title: Cranberry Butter^
Categories: Dips/spread, Fruit, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
Thawed
3/4 c  Finely chopped cranberries
1 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 c  Softened butter
2 tb Orange juice concentrate,
Blend sugar, butter and orange juice well; gently stir in cranberries.
Chill until firm.
