Title: Nantucket Sleigh Ride
Categories: Holiday, Beverages
Yield: 8 servings
1 ea Cinnamon Stick, (About 2
-Inches)
1/2 c  Fresh Cranberries
1 1/2 c  Grand Marnier Or Other
Orange Liqueur
4 ea Orange Spice Tea Bags
6 c  Boiling Water
2 c  Orange Juice
1 qt Cranberry Juice
2 tb Honey
1. Place the tea bags in the bottom of a large pot or kettle. Cover
with the boiling water and steep for 5 mins. Remove and discard the
tea bags. 2. Add the orange juice, cranberry juice, honey, cinnamon
stick, and whole cranberries to the pot of tea. Bring just to a boil,
then simmer 15 mins. Stir in the Grand Marnier and serve at once
ladled into mugs. Makes 8 servings.
