Title: Crab-Stuffed Potatoes DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Bread crumbs
1/4 ts Old Bay seasoning
1/2 ts Old Bay seasoning
1/4 ts Paprika
6 oz Crabmeat, lump, drained
6 ea Potatoes, large, baked
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/4 c  Green onion, finely sliced
Cut a 1 inch lengthwise strip from top of each potato.
Carefully scoop out pulp, leaving 1/4 inch shell.
Mash pulp.  Combine pulp, butter, sour cream, cheese,
salt, 1/2 t Old Bay seasoning, pepper, crab and onion.
Stuff shells with this mixture.  Mix remaining
ingredients and sprinkle over.  Bake at 425 degrees
for 15 minutes.  Formatted by Mary Dishongh Bowles.
