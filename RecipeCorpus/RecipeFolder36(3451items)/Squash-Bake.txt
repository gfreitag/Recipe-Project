Title: Squash Bake
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
2    Eggs, beaten
2 tb Margarine
1/2 ts Onion salt
2 c  Squash, yellow summer squash
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
2/3 c  Mild cheddar cheese, grated
2 tb Sugar
Cook squash in just a little bit of water until tender.  Drain and
mash. Measure 2 cups and mix with remaining ingredients except
cheese.  Pour into buttered 1 quart casserole and top with grated
cheese.  Dot with butter.  Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
