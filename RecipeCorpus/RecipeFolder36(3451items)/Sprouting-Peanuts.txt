Title: Sprouting Peanuts
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Peanut seeds
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1-1/2 cups in a quart jar, or tray sprout which will yield about 1
quart of sprouts. Soak for 14 hours.

Rinse 2 or 3 times daily for 3 to 4 days.

Harvest when sprouts are 1/4 to 1 inch long. Use in soups, steam, or
stir fry.
