Title: Chile Dill Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chilies
Yield: 4 cups
1/4 c  Grated cucumber
1 tb Dill seed
1/4 ts White pepper
1 pn Cayenne
Diced pimiento and chile
To garnish
1 c  Low-fat cottage cheese
3 tb 1% milk
1/4 c  Low-fat mayonnaise
2 tb Lime juice
1 c  Diced pimentos
1 c  Green chiles, diced
In a deep bowl, combine cottage cheese, milk, mayonnaise, and lime
juice, mix thoroughly. Blend in pimentos, chiles, cucumber, dill
seed, white pepper, and cayenne. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours or
overnight to enhance flavors.

Serve as a sauce over fish, veal, or chicken, or mix with prepared
cold pasta shells and serve as a salad or side dish.

Makes 4-5 cups.
