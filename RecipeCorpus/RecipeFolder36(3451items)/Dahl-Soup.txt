Title: Dahl Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Indian, Appetizers, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Kombu strip
12 c  Water
Salt, to taste
2 tb Cilantro
Lemon slices, to garnish
1 sm Onion, minced
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cumin, ground
1 tb Oil
3 c  Red lentils
Saute onion, cinnamon & cumin in oil in a stockpot until the onion is
translucent, about 2 minutes.

Stir in the lentils, kombu & water.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat,
cover & simmer for 1 hour.  Season to taste & garnish with cilantro &
lemon slices.
