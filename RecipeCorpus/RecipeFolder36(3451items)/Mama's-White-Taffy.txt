Title: Mama's White Taffy
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Cream of Tartar
1 ts Lemon extract
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1/2 tb Vinegar
Place the sugar, water, vinegar and cream of tartar in a sauce pan.
Cook to 290 degrees, or to the soft-crack stage. Add the extract and
pour out on a butterred platter. When cool enough to handle, pull
until white and glossy. Cut into sticks or cushions.
