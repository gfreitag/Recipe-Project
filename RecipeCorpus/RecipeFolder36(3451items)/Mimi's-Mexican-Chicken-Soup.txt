Title: Mimi's Mexican Chicken Soup
Categories: Herbs/spice, Poultry, Pasta/rice, Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1    Chicken breast, boneless
;cooked, shredded
1 c  Rice, cooked
1 c  Tomatoes, chopped
1/2 c  Fresh cilantro, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tb Vegetable or olive oil
1    Onion, chopped
2    Garlic cloves
;finely chopped
1 lg Red bell pepper, diced
1 qt Chicken broth, homemade
1/3 c  Fresh lime juice (authentic)
-or-
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Fresh cilantro, chopped
In large saucepan, heat oil and saute onion, garlic and bell pepper
until softened and fragrant - several minutes.  Add the chicken
broth; bring to a boil.  Add the fresh lime or lemon juice, chicken
meat and rice and bring back to a boil.  Add tomatoes and cilantro,
then turn off heat immediately. Taste and add salt and pepper if
desired.

Serve immediately with cilantro sprinkled over top as a garnish to
each bowl.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings.

From 1991 "Shepherd's Garden Seeds Catalog," pg. 3.  Posted by Cathy
