Title: Apple Wheat Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
4 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
1 tb Unsalted butter
1/2 c  Water -- plus 3 tbsp
3/4 c  Tart apples -- coarsely
Chopped
1 ts Lemon juice
2 1/4 ts Yeast
2 2/3 c  Bread flour
1/3 c  Whole-wheat flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Dark brown sugar
Use basic cycle.
