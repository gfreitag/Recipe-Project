Title: ROTEL DIP
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Velveeta cheese
1 cn Ro-Tel tomatoes
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 lb Hamburger meat
1 lb Sausage
1 lg Onion, finely chopped
1 lb Jalapeno cheese
Brown hamburger meat and sausage together; set aside.  Saute onions,
set aside.  Melt the jalapeno cheese and Velveeta cheese; add RoTel
tomatoes and soup.  Add hamburger meat, sausage and sauteed onion;
heat until bubbly.  Serve with chips. Randy Rigg The Pinnacle Club
