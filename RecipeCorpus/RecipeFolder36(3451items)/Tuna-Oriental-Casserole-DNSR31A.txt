Title: Tuna Oriental Casserole DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Casseroles
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Soy Sauce
2 tb Pimiento
2/3 c  Milk
3 oz Chow Mein Noodles
2 c  Macaroni, cooked
14 oz Tuna
1 c  Celery, chopped
1 cn Cream of celery soup
1/2 c  Water Chestnuts, sliced
Combine all ingredients except Chow Mein Noodles in a
2 qt casserole dish.  Top with the noodles.  Bake at
350 degrees for 25 minutes.  From Laura Dishongh
Beckham, formatted by Mary Dishongh Bowles.
