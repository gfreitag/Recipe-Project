Title: Roast Pork Loin with Rosemary
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
Salt & pepper to taste
18    Small new potatoes
1 c  Water
3 lb Boneless pork loin
3    Cloves garlic
4    Sprigs rosemary
Preheat oven to 350F. Put the pork lion in a roasting pan and score
the meatt att 2-inch intervals.  Slice garlic cloves thinly and press
the garlic slivers and the rosemary sprigs into the slits.  Salt &
pepper the pork to taste.

Wash the new potatoes and arrange them around the pork loin.  Add
about 1 cup of water or just enough water to keep the roast moist.
Bake uncovered att 350F for 1 1/2 hours or until the pork is no
longer pink in the center.  Half-way through the baking, turn the
potatoes once and add more water if necessary.
