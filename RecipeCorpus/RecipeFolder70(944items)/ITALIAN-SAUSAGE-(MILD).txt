Title: ITALIAN SAUSAGE (MILD)
Categories: Italian, Pork
Yield: 12 Servings
5 lb Pork butt; ground
5 ts Salt
5 ts Fennel seed
1 1/2 ts Crushed hot pepper
1 1/2 ts Pepper; black
1 c  ; water
Combine all ingredients, mix well and stuff into hog casing or make
patties.  To cook, fry or bake.

The Sausage Making Cookbook, by Jerry Predika Collection of Clarence
