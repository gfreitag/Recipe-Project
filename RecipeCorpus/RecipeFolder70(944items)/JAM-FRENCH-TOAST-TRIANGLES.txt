Title: JAM FRENCH TOAST TRIANGLES
Categories: Breakfast
1/4 c  preserves, any flavor
Yield: 6 Servings
6 sl whole wheat bread --
divided
:
6 TB Egg Beaters=AE 99% egg
substitute
:
1/4 c  skim milk
2 TB margarine
1 TB sugar
1/4 ts ground cinnamon

Evenly divide and spread preserves on 3 bread slices; top with
remaining bread slices to make 3 sandwiches, pressing to seal. Cut
each sandwich diagonally in half. In shallow bowl, combine egg
beaters and milk. Dip each sandwich in egg mixture to coat. In large
skillet or griddle, over medium-high heat, brown sandwiches in
margarine until golden brown on both sides.  Combine sugar and
cinnamon; sprinkle over sandwiches...
