Title: JACK'S TURKEY BURGER
Categories: New, Text, Import
2 lb minced raw turkey thigh meat
no skin
Yield: 4 Servings
:
3 TB minced parsley
2 TB dry bread crumbs
1 TB fresh chopped thyme
1 TB Dry Rub #1
1 TB minced onions

Prepare a wood or charcoal fire and let it burn down to embers.

In a large mixing bowl combine turkey meat with first 6 ingredients.
Mix thoroughly and chill in refrigerator for at least 2 hours. When
ready to grill, form into large patties and grill for 5 minuets on
each side or until done. Make sure turkey mixture is cold before
grilling. Serve with your choice of condiments.

Yield: 6 servings
