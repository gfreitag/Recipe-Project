Title: JOY'S ENCHILADAS
Categories: Casseroles, Mexican, Ground beef
Yield: 1 Servings
2    3 lbs.    hamburger
1    Dozen
2 sm Onions, chopped
19 oz Can enchilada sauce
1 lb Cheddar cheese, shredded
Corn tortillas
Brown beef in skillet.  Add chopped onion and saute until tender.
Drain. Layer in casserole dish or pan; tortillas, meat sauce and
shredded cheese, ending with cheese on top. Bake at 350 degrees for
25    minutes.
