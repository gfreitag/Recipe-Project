Title: JAJA ZAWIJANE W SZYNCE (HAM & EGG WRAPS)
Categories: Appetizers, Poland, Upload
Yield: 12 Appetizers
3 ea Eggs; hard-cooked
12 sl Ham; 5" x 1"
sm Onions, pickled; or
-red sweet pepper slices,
-chives or anchovies
Peel and quarter eggs. Wrap a ham slice around each egg quarter,
leaving egg ends showing. Secure ham with a wood pick. Garnish with
onions, pepper slices, chives or anchovies.
