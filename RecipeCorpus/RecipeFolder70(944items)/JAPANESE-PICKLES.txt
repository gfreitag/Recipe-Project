Title: JAPANESE PICKLES
Categories: Condiments, Misc
Yield: 6 Servings
4    Japanese cucumbers
-- thinly sliced, OR...
3    -standard cucumbers,
-- peeled and sliced
1    Red onion; thinly sliced
1/2 c  Rice vinegar
2 ts Rice syrup
1/4 ts Salt
3    Fresh red chili peppers
-- seeded
2 tb Fresh cilantro
Combine all ingredients and refrigerate. Serve as a compliment to
grilled or spicy foods, or with chicken or fish.

Nutritional Info Per Serving: Protein: 1 gr. (11%); Carbohydrates: 10
gr. (84%); Fat: .3 gr. (5%); Calories: 42; Sodium: 94 mg.;
Cholesterol: 0 mg.

Exchanges: 2 vegetable
