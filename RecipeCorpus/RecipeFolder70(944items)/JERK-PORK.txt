Title: JERK PORK
Categories: Pork
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Jalapenos, chopped
3    Green onions, chopped
4 1/2 tb Allspice, ground
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg, ground
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2    Garlic cloves, chopped
1 ts Ginger, grated
4    Bay leaves, crumbled
2 tb Oil
2 lb Pork chops
Place peppers, green onions, herbs and spices in a food processor.
Finely chop to form a paste.  Add oil and puree until smooth. Rub on
both sides of pork and let stand 30 minutes. Grill on barbecue.
