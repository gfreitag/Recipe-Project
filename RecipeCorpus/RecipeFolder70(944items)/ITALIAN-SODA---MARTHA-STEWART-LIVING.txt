Title: ITALIAN SODA - MARTHA STEWART LIVING
Categories: Beverages, Soup/stews, Italian
Yield: 1 Soda
1    Part flavored syrup
4    Parts seltzer or mineral
-water
Fresh mint or fruit for
-garnish
Fill a tall glass with ice, add syrup, and fill with seltzer. Stir
well. Add more syrup to taste. Garnish with fresh mint or fruit.

Fruit, nut and herb-flavored syrups are available at specialty-food
stores.

Martha Stewart Living/August/94  Scanned & fixed by Di Pahl
