Title: ITALIAN SAUSAGE & PEPPER LOAVES
Categories: Sandwiches, Sausage
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Italian sausage links
1    Onion; chopped
3    - 4 green bell pepper; diced
1 cn Or 2 cups fresh mushrooms;
- optional
2    Jar Italian-style cooking
- sauce (16 oz)
3    - 4 loaves Italian style
- bread
Mozzarella cheese; shredded
Paprika
Cut sausage into 1-inch pieces and halve each piece lengthwise.
Brown susage well; remove to heavy large saucepan.
In same skillet, saute bell peppers, onion and mushrooms. With
:
slotted spoon, transfer into pot with meat, allowing excess fat to
drain away. Stir in sauce and simmer all about 45 minutes, on low
heat, stirring occasionally.
Spoon onto split Italian bread.  Sprinkle with cheese and
:
paprika. Place under broiler until cheese is melted. Makes 6
sandwiches. Formatted by Mary Wilson, BWVB02B.
Date: 07/02/96
