Title: ITALIAN POPCORN
Categories: Popcorn
Yield: 8 Cups
-Sue Woodward
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 pk 0.8-oz. Italian salad dres-
-sing mix
1 tb Lemon juice
8 c  Popped corn
In a small saucepan, bring oil to a boil.  Stir in lemon juice and
salad dressing mix.  Pour over popcorn; toss lightly to coat evenly.
Serve at once.

Source:  Theatre Popcorn Popper leaflet

From: Suewoodward
