Title: ITALIAN VEGETABLE DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Mayonnaise/salad dressing
1 pk (6-oz) Italian salad
Dressing mix
1/4 c  Fine chopped red pepper
1/4 c  Fine chopped green pepper
Mix together sour cream, salad dressing and Italian salad dressing.
Mix until smooth. Stir in peppers. Chill.
