Title: ITALIAN SPAGHETTI
Categories: Italian, Hamburger, Pasta, Beef
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Ground beef
1    Medium onion,chopped
2 cn Tomato sauce(15 oz)
2 cn Tomato paste(15 oz)
1 cn Pitted ripe olives(7 1/2 oz)
2 pk Spaghetti sauce mix w/mushro
1 c  Chopped green pepper
3 c  Water
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Oregano leaves
2    Cloves garlic,minced
1    Bay leaf
16 oz Uncooked long spaghetti
Grated Parmesan cheese
Cook and stir meat and onion in large skillet or Dutch oven until
meat is brown. Drain off fat. Stir in remaining ingredients except
spaghetti and cheese; heat to boiling. Reduce heat; cover and simmer
1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally. Remove bay leaf. Cook spaghetti
as directed on package; drain. Serve sauce over spaghetti and
sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
