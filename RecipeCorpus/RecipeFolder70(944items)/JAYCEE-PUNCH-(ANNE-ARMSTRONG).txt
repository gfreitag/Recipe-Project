Title: JAYCEE PUNCH (ANNE ARMSTRONG)
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Marie, Rec.food
Yield: 20 Servings
1 x  Fifth Southern Comfort
1 cn Large pineapple juice
24 oz Frozen lemonade(undiluted)
1 qt Cranberry juice
2 x  Liters ginger ale
1 x  Gingerale float **
**  Gingerale Float.

You will need extra ginger ale for this.  Pour the gingerale into a
pretty mold.  Place in the freezer overnight. Place float into a
punch bowl. Add the rest of the ingredients and stir until blended.
