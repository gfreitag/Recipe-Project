Title: JAMAICAS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Butter
2 1/2 c  Brown sugar
3    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Coarsely chopped nuts
1 1/4 c  Carob chips
Sift together flour, baking powder and salt; set aside. Melt butter.
Add sugar and beat until well mixed. Let cool slightly. Add eggs one
at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in dry ingredients,
followed by vanilla. STir in nuts and carob chips. Turn into lightly
oiled 9 x 13-inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Let cool.
Cut into bars.
