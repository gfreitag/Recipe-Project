Title: JAYNE'S SESAME CUCUMBER SALAD
Categories: Cucumbers, Salads
1 TB toasted sesame seeds --
350F 5 minutes
Yield: 1 Servings
:
1 TB sugar
1/4 ts salt
1 ts cornstarch
2 TB water
1/2 c  rice wine vinegar
2 md cucumbers -- sliced thin or
diced
:
1/2 c  finely chopped celery

Blend liquids and cook until mixture comes to a boil, cook one minute
more. Add sesame seeds and cool. Sprinkle dressing over cucumbers and
celery, chill well.
