Title: JANET'S CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES (CAMPBELL)
Categories: Desserts, Biscuits
Yield: 1 Batch
1/2 c  Margarine/butter
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  White sugar
2    Eggs
1/2 tb Vanilla
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Chocolate chips
Mix all in order.  350F.  Remove slightly underdone. From Marie
Campbell
