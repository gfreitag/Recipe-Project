Title: JENELL MCKINNEY'S SQUASH RELISH
Categories: Condiments, Relishes, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Recipe
2 ts Salt
8 c  Squash, chopped
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped green pepper
1 c  Chopped green tomato
2 c  Vinegar
2 1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Mustard seed
1 ts Celery seed
Sprinkle salt over vegetables.  Let set for one hour; drain. Boil
rest of ingredients and add drained vegetables Cook 8 to 10 minutes.
Place in sterilized jars and seal.
