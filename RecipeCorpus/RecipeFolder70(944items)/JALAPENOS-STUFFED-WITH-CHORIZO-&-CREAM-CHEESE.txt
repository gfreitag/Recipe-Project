Title: JALAPENOS STUFFED WITH CHORIZO & CREAM CHEESE
Categories: Appetizers, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Finely minced onion
1    Clove garlic, finely minced
3 oz Chorizo (Mexican sausage)
2 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 tb Sour cream
Salt to taste
12    Jalapenos, seeded, halved
-and deribbed
Heat oil in a skillet and saute onion and garlic over medium heat 2-3
minutes, until transparent. Add chorizo and cook 5 minutes, breaking
up meat with a fork as it cooks.
When fully cooked, remove skillet from heat and cool slightly, then
stir in cheese and spor cream, season with salt and spoon intp
jalapenos; serve.
