Title: JOHN'S POTATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Batch
2 lb Red skinned potatoes
1 tb Celery salt
1/2 c  Green onion stems; cut up
1 tb Ground black pepper
2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Crumbled blue cheese
1 pn Dried celery leaves
Cook potatoes, then cut into small pieces, leaving skins on. Combine
all ingredients.  Chill before serving.

Made by John Harned, Summer 1992.  Posted by Cathy Harned.
