Title: JOHN'S CURRY POWDER
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2    TSP GINGER
1/2    TSP CINAMON
1/2    TSP CARDAMON
1    TSP CORRIANDER SEEDS
1    TSP TUMERIC
1    TSP CUMIN POWDER
2    TSP CHILLI POWDER
all the spices are to be ground with a pestle & mortar or an electric
blender into a fine powder. there is enough here for one meal.
