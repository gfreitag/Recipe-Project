Title: JACK'S PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  LEMON JUICE
3 c  ORANGE JUICE
40 oz PINEAPPLE JUICE
4 ea LARGE BANANAS, MASHED
2 c  SUGAR
1 x  LEMON LIME SODA, AS NEEDED
MIX ALL INGREDIENTS EXCEPT FOR LEMON-LIME SODA. FREEZE MIXTURE. TO
SERVE PARTLY THAW AND MIX WITH EQUAL PART SODA. STIR LIGHTLY.
