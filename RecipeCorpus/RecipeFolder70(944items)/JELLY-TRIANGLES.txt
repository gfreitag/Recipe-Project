Title: JELLY TRIANGLES
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Mix
1/2 c  Water
Roll 1/4 inch thick. cut into 3 inch squares. Place a tsp of jelly,
jam or marmalade on each square. Fold diagonally to form a triangle.
Crimp edges together firmly with a fork. Insert fork through top
crust of triangle to allow escape of steam while baking. Bake
425    for 12-14 minutes
