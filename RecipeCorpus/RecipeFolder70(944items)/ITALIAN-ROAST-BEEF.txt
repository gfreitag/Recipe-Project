Title: ITALIAN ROAST BEEF
Categories: Beef
Yield: 1 Servings
4 lb Beef roast
2    Cl Garlic
2 tb Basil, dried or 1/2 cup fres
1 ts Dried oregano
Salt
Pepper
1 lg Can italian tomatoes with ba
1    Onion, chopped
750 ml Italian red wine
Recipe by: Carla Drebing <drebingc@essex.hsc.colorado.edu> Cut slits
in the roast and insert pieces of garlic and onions, brown roast over
med-high heat. Add all ingredients and enough water to cover, cook
3-4 hours until tender, covered over low heat. Reduce sauce to two
cups.
