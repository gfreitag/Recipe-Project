Title: ITALIAN PASTA SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Hamburger
1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms and liquid
1 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Oregano
1 lg Crushed garlic clove
1/2 ts Basil
3 1/2 c  Whole peeled tomatoes
1 ts Salt
2/3 c  Tomato paste
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Water
Brown ground chuck or hamburger with onion and garlic in large
saucepan. Drain off fat. Add remaining ingredients. Stir to break up
tomatoes. Boil gently, uncovered 1 hour. Stir occasionally. Serve
over hot cooked pasta.
