Title: ITALIAN HAMBURGER DEEP DISH
Categories: Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1    Clove garlic, finely chopped
Instant mashed potato buds,
-enough for 6 or 7 servings
2 tb Instant minced onion
1 ts Dried oregano leaves
3 md Tomatoes, sliced
4 oz Mozzarella cheese, sliced
-or shredded
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a rectangular baking dish, 11x7x1
1/2". Cook beef, salt, pepper and garlic in a 12" skillet over medium
heat, stirring occasionally, until beef is brown; drain.

Prepare potatoes as directed on the package for 6 or 7
servings--except stir in onion and oregano.  Spread half of the
potato mixture on bottom of baking dish. Layer with beef and half of
the tomatoes. Top with the remaining potatoes and tomatoes; sprinkle
with cheese.

Bake uncovered for about 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
