Title: JUNKET
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Junket
2 c  Milk
Warm milk slowly to lukewarm.  Remove from fire. Crush junket. Pour
into lukewarm milk.  Stir 1 minute, or until dissolved. Pour into
molds. Let stand undisturbed until firm, or about 20 minutes. Chill.
4 servings.
