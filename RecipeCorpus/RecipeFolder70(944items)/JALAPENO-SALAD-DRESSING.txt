Title: JALAPENO SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Yogurt, plain
2 tb Skim milk
1 tb Jalapenos, minced
1/4 ts Garlic, minced
1/4 ts Cumin, ground
1/8 ts Salt
Stir all ingredients together.  Cover and chill until serving time.
This dressing is recommended for shredded Jicama, a taco salad, or
fajita salads.  It may be altered into one great dip....
