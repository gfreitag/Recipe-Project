Title: JALAPENO LIME SAUCE
Categories: New import
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Ranch dressing
1/4 c  Roasted green chilies
1/4 c  Bottled sliced jalapenos
1 1/2 tb Lime juice
3 tb Fresh cilantro leaves,
Chopped
1/2    Roma tomato, diced
Pulse all ingredients in a blender until smooth (You may have to do
this in batches) Chill and use as needed. Makes about 4 cups.

Source: Chuy's Restaurant, Dallas, TX Dallas Morning News 9/18/96
