Title: ITALIAN SAUSAGE 1
Categories: Italian, Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
5 lb Coarse ground pork butt
6    Cloves pressed garlic
1 tb Crushed red pepper
1 tb Fennel seeds
1 ts Thyme
2    Bay leaves, crushed
1 tb Salt
2 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Coriander
1 tb Paprika
1 c  Cold water
Combine all ingredients, mix well and stuff into hog casing. To cook,
fry or bake.
