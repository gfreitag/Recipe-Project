Title: ITALIAN-SEASONED BREADED VEGETABLES
Categories: Appetizers, Dips, Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Italian seasoned dry bread
Crumbs
1/3 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
2/3 c  Hellmann's Real Mayonnaise
6 c  Assorted vegetables, such as
Broccoli florets,carrot
Sticks, mushrooms,
Green or red pepper strips,
Yellow squash
And/or zucchini strips
Preheat oven to 425F.  Place bread crumbs and cheese in plastic food
bag, shake to combine.  Place mayonnaise in another bag; add
vegetables and shake to coat. Add mayonnaise-coated vegetables, half
at a time, to crumb mixture; shake to coat well. Place on ungreased
cookie sheet separating so that pieces do not touch. Bake 10 minutes
or until golden. Makes 6 to
8    servings.
