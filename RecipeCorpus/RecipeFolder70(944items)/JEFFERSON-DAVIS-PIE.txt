Title: JEFFERSON DAVIS PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Pie shell, 9 inch baked
1/2 c  Butter
2 c  Sugar, light brown
4    Egg yolks
2 tb Flour, all purpose
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Allspice
1 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Cream or evaporated milk
1/2 c  Dates; chopped
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Pecan meats, broken
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Cream together butter and brown sugar.
Beat in 4 egg yolks. Mix together flour and spices and then add. Stir
in cream, dates, raisins and nuts. Fill the shell;bake the pie until
set, about 30 minutes.  When cool take 2 or 3 egg whites and make a
meringue as a topping. The dates are not necessary but a nice
extra;without them and the nuts it is called a Kentucky or Arkansas
pie. This is a great favorite in southern cafeteria and small town
restaurants. Source: mom
