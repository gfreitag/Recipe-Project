Title: JUICE ALPOKAT (INDONESIAN AVOCADO DRINK)
Categories: Beverages, Indonesian
Yield: 2 Servings
5 tb Sugar
5 tb ;Water
2    Avocado, med
1/2 c  Milk, cold
1 c  Chocolate milk
Ice; crushed
Make simple syrup by combine sugar and water in small saucepan over
medium- high heat.  Stir until clear. Remove from heat and let cool.
Spoon out avocado pulp and place in blender. Add syrup and blend to
mix, then add cold milk and blend.  Divide between 2 tall glasses.
Top each serving with 1/2 cup chocolate milk (to form separate layer)
and crushed ice.
per Shelley Rodgers
