Title: JUDY'S SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces/dips
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Salt
1 ts White pepper
1    Clove garlic; crushed
1 c  Salad oil
1 c  Vinegar
1.   Put sugar, paprika, salt, pepper and garlic in jar.

2.  Stir in oil, then vinegar.  Mix thoroughly and serve.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~ For variation, add 1/4 cup crumbled Roquefort cheese.
