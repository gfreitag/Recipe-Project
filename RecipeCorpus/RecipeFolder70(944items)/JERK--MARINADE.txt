Title: JERK  MARINADE
Categories: Sauces, Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Onion, finely chopped
1/2 c  Scallion, finely chopped
2 ts Ground thyme
1 ts Salt
2 ts Sugar
1 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1    Hot pepper, finely ground
1 ts Black pepper
3 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Cooking oil
1 tb Vinegar
Mix together all the ingredients.  A food processor fitted with a
steel blade is ideal for chopping and grinding. This will provide
an
excellent marinade for chicken, beef, or pork. Store leftover
marinade in the refrigerator in a tightly closed jar for about 1
month.
