Title: JALAPENO VINAIGRETTE (SMCD91A)
Categories: Salads, Mexican, Tex-mex
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Lime juice -- freshly
Squeezed
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 1/2 tb Sherry vinegar
1/2    Fresh jalapeno pepper --
Seede
1/2 ts Dijon mustard
1/3 c  Olive oil
1 ts Salt
In a blender or food processor, combine all ingredients except the
olive oil and whirl 1 to 2 minutes. With machine running, add olive
oil, 1 tbs at a time. Chill. Makes 1 cup
