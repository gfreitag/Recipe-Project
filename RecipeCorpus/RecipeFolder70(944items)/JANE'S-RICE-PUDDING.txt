Title: JANE'S RICE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  Milk
7 tb Uncooked long-grain white
-rice
1    Strip fresh lemon peel
=(1"x3")
1/3 c  Granulated sugar
2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1    Strip fresh orange peel
=(1" x 3")
Pinch of cinnamon
Mix milk, rice, and peels in large saucepan. Bring to a boil. Reduce
heat and simmer 25 minutes, stirring often, until mixture starts to
thicken and starts to stick to bottom of pan. Reduce heat to very low
and cook 5 to 10 minutes more. Add the sugar and stir 2 to 3 minutes
until dissolved. Cool slightly before stirring in vanilla. Cover and
refrigerate 2 hours. Stir in heavy cream. Top with cinnamon.
