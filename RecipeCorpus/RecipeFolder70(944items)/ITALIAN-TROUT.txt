Title: ITALIAN TROUT
Categories: Main dish, Fish, Italian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ea Fresh Trout
1 ea Large onion, chopped fine
8 oz Can tomato sauce
2 ea Stalks celery, chopped fine
3 tb Oregano
1/4 c  Olive oil
In a large frying pan, mix oil, celery, onion, oregano, salt and
pepper.  Saute for 5 minutes.  Add sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes or
until thick.  Add trout. Turn over after 4 minutes. Cook 12 more
minutes or until done.
