Title: JOHNNY CAKE MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Flour
1 c  Cornmeal
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sugar
4 ts Baking powder
1 ea Egg
1 c  Milk plus 2 tblsp
4 tb Cooking oil
Put all dry ingredients in bowl, beat egg lightly and add the milk to
it. Add to dry ingredients and mix well. Add the oil and mix well .
Put in oiled pan and bake in hot oven 400 for 20-25 min.
