Title: SPICY SESAME SAUCE - MASTER CHEFS
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Cup
1/3 c  Tahini, (sesame seed
-- paste) + 1 tablespoon
1/3 c  Vinegar, Chinese, rice
1/3 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 tb Oil, sesame
1 tb Garlic, finely chopped
1 tb Oil, chili, hot
1/2 ts Peppercorns, Szechwan,
-- ground
Use a processor or blender to combine sauce ingredients. Set
aside.

Source:  New York's Master Chefs, Bon Appetit Magazine
:  Written by Richard Sax, Photographs by Nancy McFarland
:  The Knapp Press, Los Angeles, 1985

Chef:   Simon Teng, Auntie Yuan Restaurant, New York
