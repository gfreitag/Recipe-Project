Title: SPICY GERMAN MUSTARD
Categories: Condiments, Dressings, German
Yield: 1 Cup
1/3 c  Yellow mustard seeds
1 tb Brown mustard seeds
1/4 c  Mustard powder
1/2 c  Cold water
1 c  Apple cider vinegar
2 tb Brown sugar
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Dill seed
1/4 ts Tarragon leaves, crushed
Soak mustard seeds & the powder in the cold water for
3    hours.

Combine the remaining ingredients in a glass or porcelain pan & simmer
until the mixtue is reduced by half, about 10 minutes. Pour through a
strainer onto the mustard mixture.

Transfer to a double boiler.  Cook & stir until thickened, about 10
minutes.  Spoon into a container & cover. Refrigerate for 3 days
before using.  Makes a good dressing for German Potato Salads.

"Vegetarian Gourmet" Winter, 1995
