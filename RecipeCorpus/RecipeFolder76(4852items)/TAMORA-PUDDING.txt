Title: TAMORA PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
1 md Papaya, slightly unripe,
-- peel & seeds removed
1 c  Pineapple chunks, fresh
2 md Oranges, juiced -=OR=-
1/2 c  Orange juice
Cut the papaya into small pieces.  Put in a blender with the remaining
ingredients.  Blend at medium speed until smooth. Pour into pudding
glasses.  Refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours or until the puddings are
firm. Then serve immediately. If keeping rhe puddings longer, cover
tightly & store in the refrigerator for 3 to 5 days.
