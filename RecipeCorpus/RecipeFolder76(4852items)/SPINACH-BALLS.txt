Title: SPINACH BALLS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2 pk 10 oz. chopped frozen spinac
2 c  Herb stuffing
2 ea Onions, finely chopped
6 ea Eggs, beaten
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Thyme
6 tb Melted butter
1/2 ts Pepper
1 tb Garlic salt
Cook and drain spinach.  Mix together and make into balls. Bake at
350 degrees for 15 minutes.  Very good.
