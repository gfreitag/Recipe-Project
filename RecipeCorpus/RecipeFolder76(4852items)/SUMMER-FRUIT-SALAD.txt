Title: SUMMER FRUIT SALAD
Categories: Salads, Fruits, Low-fat
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Cooked rice
- cooled to room temperature
1/2 c  Quartered strawberries
1/2 c  Grape halves
1/2 c  Quartered kiwifruit slices
1/2 c  Pineapple tidbits
1/2 c  Banana slices
1/4 c  Pineapple juice
2 tb Plain yogurt
1 tb Honey
Lettuce leaves
Combine rice and fruits in large bowl.  Blend pineapple juice,
yogurt, and honey in small bowl; pour over rice mixture. Toss
lightly. Serve on lettuce leaves.

Each serving provides: * 161 calories * 2.6 g. protein * 0.5 g. fat *
37.6 g. carbohydrate * 8 mg. sodium * 1 mg. cholesterol
