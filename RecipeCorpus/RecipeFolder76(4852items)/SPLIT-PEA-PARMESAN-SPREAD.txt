Title: SPLIT PEA PARMESAN SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese, Pulses and, Sauces and
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Split Peas -- cooked
2 tb Cottage Cheese
2 tb Parmesan Cheese
2 tb Olive Oil -- or less
1/4 ts Dried Basil
Pepper
2    Cloves Garlic -- crushed
After cooked split peas have cooled (in order to better judge
consistency), mash them up.  Then mix in other ingredients and
refrigerate.
