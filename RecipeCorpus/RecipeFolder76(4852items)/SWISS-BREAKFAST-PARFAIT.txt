Title: SWISS BREAKFAST PARFAIT
Categories: Breakfast, Low-fat
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Quaker Oats, uncooked
-- (quick or old-fashioned)
16 oz Vanilla yogurt
-- nonfat or lowfat
8 oz Crushed pineapple in juice
-- undrained
2 tb Sliced almonds (optional)
2 c  Sliced strawberries
-- fresh or frozen
In medium bowl, combine oats, yogurt, pineapple and almonds; mix well.
Cover, refrigerate overnight or up to 4 days. To serve, layer oat
mixture and strawberries in 4 parfait glasses. Garnish with additional
strawberries, if desired.  Serve chilled.

Nutrition Information (1 parfait): * Calories 240 * Fat 2g * Sodium
80mg * Dietary Fiber 4g
