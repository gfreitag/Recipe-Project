Title: SPICY TURKEY LOAF
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
3/4 c  Onions, chopped
1 tb Butter or Margarine
1 1/4 lb Ground turkey
1 ea Egg, lightly beaten
2 tb Dried bread crumbs
1/4 c  Basil, fresh, chopped
1/4 c  Parsley, fresh, chopped
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 ts Dried thyme leaves
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Dried red pepper flakes,
1 x  Crushed
1 x  Salt & Pepper to taste
Saute onions in melted butter or margarine. Stir in ground turkey,
egg and bread crumbs. Combine seasonings and add to turkey mix. Shape
into a loaf and put in a pan. refrigerate 1 hour, then cover with tin
foil and bake at 350 F (180 C) for 1 hour; remove the foil for the
last 15 minutes. Serve hot or cold. Source: TV Guide Dec 28/91-Jan3/92
