Title: TEXAS ORANGE CAKE
Categories: Box cakes, Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Yellow cake mix
4    Eggs
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
11 oz Mandarin oranges with juice
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 lg Vanilla instant pudding
15 oz Crushed pineapple & juice
1/2 c  Half & half
12 oz Cool Whip
1 c  Coconut
Beat eggs, oil and oranges together. Add cake mix. Beat until thick.
Bake in greased and floured 9x13" pan for 35-45 minutes at 350~F.
Cool.
Topping: Mix pudding and pineapple; beat until thick. Add half and
half; beat again. Place on cooled cake. Cover with Cool Whip and
sprinkle with coconut. Refrigerate.
