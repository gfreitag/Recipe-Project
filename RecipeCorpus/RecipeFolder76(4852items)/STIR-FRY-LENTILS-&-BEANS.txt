Title: STIR FRY LENTILS & BEANS
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 Recipe
1/4 c  Sesame oil
1/2 c  Diced onion
1/2 c  Diced celery
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
2 c  Cooked AM Red Lentils
1 c  Cooked AM Adzuki beans
1/2 c  Fresh mung bean sprouts
1/2 c  Sliced water chestnuts
2 ts Low sodium tamari sauce
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Onion powder
Heat the oil in a large skillet or wok and saute the onion, celery and
mushrooms until brown.  Add the remaining ingredients and cook,
stirring until heated through.
