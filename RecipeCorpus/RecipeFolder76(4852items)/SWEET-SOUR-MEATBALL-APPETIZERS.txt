Title: SWEET SOUR MEATBALL APPETIZERS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
1 ea Egg -- slightly beaten
2 tb Minced onions
2 tb Milk
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Salt
ds Pepper
1 tb Vegetable oil
2/3 c  Chili sauce
2/3 c  Red currant or grape jelly
Combine first 8 ingredients.  Form into 40 meatballs. Brown lightly
in oil. Cover; cook over low heat for 5 minutes. Drain excess fat.
Combine chili sauce and jelly; pour over meatballs. Heat, stirring
until jelly is melted. Simmer 10 to 12 minutes, until sauce thickens,
basting occasionally. Makes 40 appetizer meatballs.
