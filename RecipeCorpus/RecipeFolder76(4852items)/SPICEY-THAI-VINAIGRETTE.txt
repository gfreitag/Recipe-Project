Title: SPICEY THAI VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Oriental, Sauces and, Thai
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Whole Garlic,Clove,Raw --
Finely chopped
1/4 ts Ginger,Fresh Slices --
Finely chopped
3 tb Vinegar, Rice
1 ts Brown Sugar
1 ts Soy Sauce
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 ts Oil,Sesame
1/4 ts Pepper,Red,Crushed
Onions,Red (optional) --
Chopped
Orange, Mandarin (optional)
Jicama,Raw,Sliced
(optional)
Combine all ingredients and mix well. Pour over mixed lettuces, toss
and serve.
