Title: THE ORIGINAL "HOT BROWN" SANDWICH
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 6 Servings
-Sue Woodward
4 oz Butter
6 tb Flour
3 c  To 3.1/2-cups milk
6 tb Parmesan cheese; grated
1    Egg; beaten
1 oz Heavy cream; whipped
Salt; to taste
Pepper; to taste
Roast turkey breast; sliced
12 sl Toast
Parmesan cheese; grated
12 sl Bacon; fried crisp
To make sauce - in a skillet, melt butter, add flour and stir until
absorbed.  Stir in milk and 6 tbsp cheese; add eggs. Do not allow to
boil. Remove from heat; fold in cream with seasonings.

For each Hot Brown - place two slices toast in ovenproof dish. Cover
toast with sliced turkey and sauce. Sprinkle with additional cheese;
broil until speckled brown and bubbling.  Remove from broiler; add 2
crisp strips of bacon on top.  Serve at once.

Source:  Old Brown Hotel in Louisville, KY (Bradenton Herald, 6/8/95)
