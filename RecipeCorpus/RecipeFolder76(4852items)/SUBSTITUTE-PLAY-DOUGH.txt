Title: SUBSTITUTE PLAY DOUGH
Categories: Kids, Play dough
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Salt
1/4 c  Oil
Food coloring
1/4 c  Water
Heat and stir until it forms a ball. It will still feel slightly
sticky. Cool and knead. Store in covered container.
