Title: SWEDISH JULBROD (CHRISTMAS BREAD)
Categories: Breads, Machine, Holidays, Swedish
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 c  Milk
4 tb Butter
1    Egg
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Cardamom
3 c  Bread flour
2 1/2 ts Yeast
----------------------------
2/3 c  Raisins
2/3 c  Citron; sliced
1 tb Almonds; chopped
Recipe by: Donna Rathnell German - The Bread Machine Cookbook II Use
Raisin Bread Cycle.  At beep add the raisins, citron, and chopped
almonds.
