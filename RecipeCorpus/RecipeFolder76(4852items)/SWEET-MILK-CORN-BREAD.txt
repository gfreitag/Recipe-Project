Title: SWEET MILK CORN BREAD
Categories: Breads, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Corn meal
1 1/2 c  Milk
4 tb Shortening; melted
1 ts Salt
1 c  Flour; white
2 ea Eggs; well beaten
2 tb Sugar
3 tb Baking powder
Stir all together. Pour in baking pan. Brush top with melted butter
and bake in moderate oven 25 minutes.

Note: Moderate oven is 350 - 400 F.

Source: Mrs. Mabel Smith, Center Grange, Morgan County, OH
