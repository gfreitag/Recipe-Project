Title: STEWED EGGPLANT
Categories: Ethnic, Vegetables, Soups/stews
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 md Onion, chopped
4 ea Bay leaves crumbled
1 lg Eggplant
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 3/4 c  Canned tomatoes
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1/2 ts Caraway seeds, bruised
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Heat oil in a skillet, add onion & bay leaves & cook 4 to 5 minutes,
stirring occasionally.  Meanwhile, peel the eggplant, slice it then
dice. Add pieces to skillet. Add rest of the ingredients. Raise heat
till mixture begins to simmer then cover, reduce heat & simmer 20
minutes til the eggplant is very tender.

Ismail Merchant, "Indian Cuisine"
