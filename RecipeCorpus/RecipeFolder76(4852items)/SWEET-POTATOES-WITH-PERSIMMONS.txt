Title: SWEET POTATOES WITH PERSIMMONS
Categories: Digest, Nov., Thanksgivin
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Water
1 lb Fresh sweet potatoes, peeled
And cut
1/2 c  Sugar
2    Persimmons, slied
Juice of 1/2 lemon and lime
Put all ingredients into saucepan and cook, stirring occasionally,
until sweet potatoes are done and medium syrup is formed.
