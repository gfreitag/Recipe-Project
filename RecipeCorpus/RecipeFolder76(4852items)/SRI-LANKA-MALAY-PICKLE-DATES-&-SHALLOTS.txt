Title: SRI LANKA MALAY PICKLE DATES & SHALLOTS
Categories: Sauces, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
---------> source <---------
"A taste of Sri Lanka"
By Indra Jayasekera
MMMMM------------------------MALAY PICKLE-----------------------------
150 g  Shallots
1    Chile, fresh
75 g  Dates
1    Pickled lime
3 sl Ginger
2    Garlic cloves
2 ts Mustard seeds
75 ml Vinegar
1 ts Paprika
25 g  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
===========================> Directions <========================

Clean   the  shallots,   halve   them,   cut  the chile lengthways
and chop the  dates  and pickled lime. Grind the ginger, garlic,
chile and mustard seeds together and add sufficient vinegar to make a
thick paste. Add the paprika. Boil the remaining vinegar with the
sugar and add the dates. Remove from the heat and add the shallots,
ground mixture and salt and mix well.
