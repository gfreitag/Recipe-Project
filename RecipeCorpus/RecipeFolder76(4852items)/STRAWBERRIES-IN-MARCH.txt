Title: STRAWBERRIES IN MARCH
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 80 Servings
3/4 lb Macaroon (cookie) coconut
1 cn Condensed milk
20    Drops red coloring
3 sm Pkgs. strawberry jello
1 ts Almond flavoring
Knead all ingredients together except for 1 small pkg. jello and
refrigerate for 3 hours or overnight.  Mold into shape of strawberry.
Roll in third package of jello. Make 2 leaves on top using icing in a
can with tips or make buttercream icing and use #67 leaf tip.
Refrigerate. Makes 80 ~ 90.

NOTE:  I buy a 14. oz. bag of flaked coconut and grind finely in a
food processor.
