Title: SURIMI~SPINACH & CANNELLINI BEAN CASSEROLE
Categories: Seafood, Beans, Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Large onion,finely chopped
3    Cloves garlic,minced
4 ts Ground cumin
2 ts Olive oil
1 lb Crab surimi chunk seafood
2    15 oz can cannellini beans
10 oz Pkg. chopped spinach,thawed
1    Red bell pepper,cut in strip
In a 2 1/2 quart casserole,combine onion,garlic,cumin and olive
oil;mix well.Do not cover.Microwave for 4 minutes.Add surimi,drained
beans,spinach and bell pepper;toss gently to combine.Cover with lid
or vented plastic wrap.Stirring midway through cooking,microwave on
high 5 to 6 minutes or until thoroughly heated.Let stand 3 minutes.
