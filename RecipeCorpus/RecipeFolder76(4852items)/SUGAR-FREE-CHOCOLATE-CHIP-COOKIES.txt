Title: SUGAR FREE CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts, Diabetic
Yield: 48 Servings
1/2 c  Softened margarine
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Non-fat dry milk
1/2 c  Sugar Twin
1/4 c  Water
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
2 oz Diatetic choc candy, diced
Cream together margarine and Sugar Twin.  Add egg, vanilla, non-fat
dry milk and water.  Beat 1 minute at medium speed. Fold in chocolate
candy. Drop from teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350F
for 20-25 minutes. 31 calories per cookie.
