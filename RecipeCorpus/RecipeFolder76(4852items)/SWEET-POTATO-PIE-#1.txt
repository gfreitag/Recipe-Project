Title: SWEET POTATO PIE #1
Categories: Vegetables, Pies
Yield: 12 Servings
6 c  Sweet potatoes
2 c  Sugar
4 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
1 c  Evaporated milk
8 oz Butter
2    Eggs, beaten
2 ts Vanilla
2    9-inch pie crusts
Boil sweet potatoes 30-40 minutes.  Peel.  Beat potatoes, butter and
salt with mixer; add other ingredients, beating after each addition.
Pour into crusts and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until
firm. Serve with whipped cream or Cool-Whip.
