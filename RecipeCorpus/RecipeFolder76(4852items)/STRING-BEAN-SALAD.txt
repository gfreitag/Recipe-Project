Title: STRING BEAN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Thai, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb String beans, blanched &
-- sliced
2 ea Green chilies, julienned
2 ea Red chilies, julienned
1/2 lb Tofu, pressed & cubed
1/2 c  Coconut milk
2 tb Soy sauce
4 tb Lime juice
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Chili sauce, see recipe
Place the beans on a large serving platter & arrange the green & red
chilies & tof cubes over the top.  In a small bowl, mix together the
coconut milk, soy sauce, lime juice, sugar & chili sauce. Just before
serving, pour the dressing over the salad.
