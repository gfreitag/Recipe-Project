Title: TAMARIND-APRICOT SAUCE
Categories: Miamiherald, Sauces
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
MMMMM-----------------FORMATTED BY LISA CRAWFORD----------------------
1 cn Sweetened tamarind nectar
-(8oz)
4 oz Apricot jam
2 tb Honey
1 ts Dijon style mustard (opt)
Bring the nectar and jam to a boil and cook until thickened. Stir in
honey and mustard.

Nutritional info per tablespoon: 24 cal; tr pro, 6g carb, tr fat, 6mg
sodium

Source: Miami Herald, 10/5/95
