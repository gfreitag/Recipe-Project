Title: STAGE COACH CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Casseroles
Yield: 4 Servings
2/3 c  Hidden valley;  original sa
1    Egg;  slightly beaten
2 1/2 lb Chicken; fryer, cut up
1/3 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Cornflake crumbs
1/4 c  Butter; melted
In a bowl, combine Salad Dressing and egg; set aside. Rinse chicken
with water and drain well. Dredge chicken pieces in flour mixed with
salt and pepper; dip in Salad Dressing-egg mixture; roll in cornflake
crumbs. Arrange chicken pieces in a broiler pan, lined with foil if
desired. Drizzle with butter. Bake at 350^F. for 50 minutes to 1 hour
or until fork tender. Makes 4 to 6 servings. I hope this is what you
were looking for.
