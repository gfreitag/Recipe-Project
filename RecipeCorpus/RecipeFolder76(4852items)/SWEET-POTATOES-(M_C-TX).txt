Title: SWEET POTATOES (M_C-TX)
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lg Cans sweet potatoes or yams
1/2    - 3/4 stick margarine
Cinnamon to taste
Nutmeg to taste
1/4 c  Brown sugar
Marshmallows
Heat potatoes in juice.  Drain.  Mix in rest of ingredients, except
marshmallows. Mash all together. Place marshmallows on top. Bake in
350 oven until heated, or place in microwave on medium until heated
(about 5-6 minutes).
