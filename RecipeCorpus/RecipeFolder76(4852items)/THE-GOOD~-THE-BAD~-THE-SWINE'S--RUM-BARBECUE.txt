Title: THE GOOD~ THE BAD~ THE SWINE'S  RUM BARBECUE
Categories: Sauces, Barbecue
Yield: 1 Qt
1 c  Dark rum
2 tb Liquid smoke
2 tb Barbecue sauce,
Bottled, smoky
3 tb Onion flakes, dry
1 tb Mustard, dry
2 c  Ketchup
1 pn Habanero chile pepper
Combine the rum, liquid smoke, barbecue sauce, onion flakes and dry
mustard in the top of a double boiler. Heat water in the bottom of
the double boiler and cook for 15 minutes over medium heat. Stir in
the ketchup and cook the sauce for 3o minutes, or until well
flavored. Stir in the chile pepper to taste. Walt
