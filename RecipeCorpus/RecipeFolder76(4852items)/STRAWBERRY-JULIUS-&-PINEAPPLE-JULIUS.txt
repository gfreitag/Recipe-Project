Title: STRAWBERRY JULIUS & PINEAPPLE JULIUS
Categories: Beverages, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Frozen sliced strawberries
Thawed
Or
1 cn Crushed pineapple in juice
(8-oz)
1 c  Water
2    Egg whites
3/4 ts Vanilla extract
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Crushed ice -- heaping
1.  Combine all the ingredients in a blender set on high speed for
exactly
1    minute. Makes 2 drinks.
