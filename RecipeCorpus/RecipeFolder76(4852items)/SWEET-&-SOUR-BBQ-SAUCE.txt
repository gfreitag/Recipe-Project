Title: SWEET & SOUR BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
12 oz Onion -- chopped
4    Cloves garlic -- minced
2    Inch fresh ginger root
1 pt Red wine
Olive oil
Flour to thicken
JUICE OF:
2    Orange
2    Lime
1    Lemon
2 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Capers
1 tb Malt
2 tb Molasses
2 ts Mustard
1 ts Chili sauce or tabasco
Spices:
pn Nutmeg
2    Pods
4    Cloves -- ground
1/2 ts Allspice
Black pepper
Cardamon -- ground
Chop the onions, garlic and ginger and slowlycook in the oil until
soft. Add flour to thicken ( thin if you want to marinade). Add
everything else and cook for half an hour.
