Title: STUFFED MUSHROOMS PARMA
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 30 Servings
1 lb Mushrooms, medium
1/2 c  Parmesan; grated
1/4 c  Margarine
1 tb Scallions; chopped
Remove stems from mushrooms and chop.  Combine stems with Parmesan
Cheese and melted margarine and onions. Fill mushroom caps with
cheese mixture. Broil 2 to 3 minutes or until golden brown.
