Title: SPICY CARROTS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
5 lg Carrots chopped
1 1/2 c  Seasoned Rice Vinegar
1/2 c  Olive Oil
2 cl Garlic - Sliced
1/4 c  Chopped Cilantro
1/2 c  Onion sliced into 1/2 rings
1/4 c  Jalapenos w/
Juice sliced lengthwise
Bring all ingredients to a boil except carrots.  Pour mixture over
carrots and let sit at least 2 hrs. Enjoy! These keep for a very long
time, at least 2-3 weeks, put in airtight container. Refrigerate
before eating.
