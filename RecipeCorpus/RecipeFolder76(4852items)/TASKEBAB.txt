Title: TASKEBAB
Categories: Beef
Yield: 5 Servings
1 lb Diced beef
Olive oil
1    Eggplant, peeled and diced
3 c  Light tomato sauce
1/2 sm Onion,diced
1/4 ts Basil
1/4 ts Oregano
Salt and pepper to taste
Fry beef and eggplant in oil in skillet (or eggplant may be deep-
fried).Prepare light tomato sauce.Combine with remaining ingredients.
bake,uncovered,on low heat about 45 minutes,as for stew,to allow
sauce to blend through meat and eggplant.Serves 4 to 5.
