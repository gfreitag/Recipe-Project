Title: STARLIGHT MINT SUPRISE
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Flour
1 ts Soda
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Crisco
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Firmly packed Brown Sugar
2    Unbeaten eggs
2 tb Water
1 ts Vanilla
1. Grease baking pans or cookie sheets. 2. SIFT together pre-measured
flour, soda, and salt. 3. CREAM butter and shortening together. (Hint
you can use all butter if you choose.) Gradually ADD the two sugars.
BLEND in 2 unbeaten eggs. ADD the water and the vanilla. BEAT WELL.
4. ADD the sifted, dry ingredients. MIX thoroughly. Cover with wax
paper*. Refrigerate at least two hours. 5. Preheat oven to 375øF.
Open package of Starlight Solid Milk Chocolate Wafers. Enclose wafer
in 1 tablespoon of chilled dough. Place on cookie sheet 2" apart. Top
each cookie with a walnut half. 6. Bake at 375ø for 10-12 minutes.
