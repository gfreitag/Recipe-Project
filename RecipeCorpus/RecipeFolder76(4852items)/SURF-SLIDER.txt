Title: SURF SLIDER
Categories: Boat, Drinks, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
2 oz Rum
1 oz Blue curacao
2 oz Pineapple juice
1/2 oz Simple syrup
1    Scoop Ice
You guessed it.  throw it all in a blender and let'er rip until
smooth. Serve it up in any kind of glass you like.

(For more than one serving. 1 part simple syrup--4 parts pineapple
juice--2 parts blue Curacao-- 4 parts rum. 1 scoop of ice per part of
simple sugar. Mix the same way.)
