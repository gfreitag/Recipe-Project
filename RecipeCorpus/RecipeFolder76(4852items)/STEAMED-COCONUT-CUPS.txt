Title: STEAMED COCONUT CUPS
Categories: Desserts, Thai, Fruits, Vegetarian
Yield: 15 Cups
MMMMM------------------------DARK MIXTURE-----------------------------
2 c  Coconut milk
1 c  Rice flour
3/4 c  Raw sugar -=OR=- brown sugar
1 pn Salt
MMMMM-----------------------LIGHT MIXTURE----------------------------
1 c  Coconut cream
2 tb Raw sugar -=OR=- white sugar
2 tb Rice flour
1 pn Salt
Blend all the ingredients for the DARK MIXTURE well & fill 15 large
foil cups half full.  Steam over medium heat for 20 minutes or until
the mixture is set. Blend the ingredients for the LIGHT MIXTURE & put
2 tb in each foil cup. Steam another 25 minutes or until it appears
dry on top. Cool & run a knife around the foil cup to facilitate
removal. Serve cool or at room temperature with the light side up.
