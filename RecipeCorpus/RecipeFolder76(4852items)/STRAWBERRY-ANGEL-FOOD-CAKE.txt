Title: STRAWBERRY ANGEL FOOD CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
1    Angle Food Cake
1 lg Pkg Frozen stawberries,
-thawed and drained
1 sm Can crushed pineapple,
-drained
16    Marshmallows, cut up
1/2 ts Vanilla
4 c  Whipped topping
Cut cake in 3 layers.  Mix remaining ingredients together, folding
gently. Ice with strawberries and topping mixture. Keep in
refrigerator after iced. Yields: 12 - 15 servings.
