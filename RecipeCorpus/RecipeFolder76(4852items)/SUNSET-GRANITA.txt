Title: SUNSET GRANITA
Categories: New import
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  Chopped ripe mango or papaya
2 tb Orange liqueur
In a saucepan, stir together water and sugar. Simmer over medium heat
for about 5 minutes until the sugar has completely dissolved. Let
syrup cool and place in blender. Add lemon juice, fruit and liqueur
and blend mixture until it is smooth. Place in a metal dish, cover
and freeze for at least 3 hours, stirring the outer frozen mixture
into the center every half hour, until firm but not solidly frozen.
Just before serving, scrape with a fork to lighten the texture Makes
4    (1/2 cup) servings.
