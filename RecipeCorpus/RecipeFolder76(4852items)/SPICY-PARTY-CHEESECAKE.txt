Title: SPICY PARTY CHEESECAKE
Categories: Appetizers, Southwester, Cheesecakes
Yield: 1 Servings
16 oz Cream cheese; softened
2 c  Monterey jack cheese;
-shredded
8 oz Sour cream
3    Eggs
1 c  Salsa; hot or mild, choose
4    Anaheim chili pepper;
-roast, seed, peel
Or 4-oz can canned green
-chile
8 oz Sour cream
6 oz Avocado dip; homemade is bes
1 md Tomato; seed,  chop
Tortilla chips
Chives, cilantro, parsley
Combine cream cheese and Monterey Jack.  Beat with electric mixer
'til ligh and fluffy.  Beat in first 8 oz of sour cream. Add eggs and
mix just to combine. Stir in salsa and chile. Pour into 9-inch
springform pan. Place on baking sheet and bake at 350 for 40-45
minutes 'til center is almost set

Immediately spre4ad with remaining sour cream.  Cool on rack. Chill 3
to 4 hours.

To serve, remove from pan.  Garnish with the  other goodies. Serve
with tortilla chips.
