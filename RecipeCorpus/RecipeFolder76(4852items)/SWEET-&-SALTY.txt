Title: SWEET & SALTY
Categories: Candies, Famfavorite
Yield: 24 Servings
1 1/4 c  CHOPPED PEANUTS
2 1/2 c  BROKEN PRETZEL STICKS
1 lb ALMOUND BARK
MELT ALMOUND BARK AND MIX IN PEANUTS AND PRETZEL STICKS. PLACE ON WAX
PAPER LINED BAKING SHEET AND SPREAD TO A THICKNESS OF ABOUT 1/2 INCH.
WHEN COOL BREAK OR CUT INTO BITE SIZED PIECES.
