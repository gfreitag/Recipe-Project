Title: SWEET POTATO PATTIES
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Sweet potatoes, cut into
-pieces and boiled
2 tb Virgin or extra-virgin olive
-oil
1/2 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper and nutmeg
1/4 c  Hot skimmed milk
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
1/2 c  Pecans, ground
Butter
Drain sweet potatoes and mash with a fork, ricer or electric mixer.
Add oil, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and milk, beating until light and
fluffy. Stir in chopped pecans and shape into 8 flat round patties.
Dip both sides into ground pecans to coat.  Place on a lightly oiled
baking sheet and dot with butter.  Bake in a preheated 450F. oven for
20 minutes. Serve with a mushroom gravy.
