Title: ST LOUIS' RUM BALLS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 30 Servings
1 3/4 c  Fine vanilla wafer crumbs
1 c  Finely ground nuts
1 c  Powdered sugar
1/4 c  Cocoa
3 tb Light corn syrup
1/4 c  Light rum
1/3 c  Powdered sugar for dredging
1/8 c  Kahlua
Place all but dredging sugar in a bowl and mix well. Roll into 1"
balls and dredge in sugar.  Keep in sealed container.
