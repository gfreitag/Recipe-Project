Title: STEAK BENIHANA
Categories: Beef, Rice, Oriental
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Uncooked rice
1    6 oz sirloin steak, boneless
1 tb Peanut oil
2 lg Mushrooms, sliced
Salt
Pepper
Cook rice according to package directions. Set aside. Heat griddle to
350 drgrees and pour on oil to coat surface. Sear steak on both
sides. Cut steak into strips. Return to griddle along with mushroom
slices. Turn meat pieces until done to preference. Season with salt
and pepper to taste. Serve with boiled rice and magic mustard sauce.
