Title: STUFFED PEPPERS (PA DUTCH STYLE)
Categories: Casserole, Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 x  Green pepper ; lengthwise,
1 x  Grind together:
1 lb Cheese ;  colby long horn c
1 ea Med Onion
15 oz Corn ; can of (not creamed
1 ds Accent
mix  well  spray baking pan with Pam or Mazola spray to prevent
sticking. Place peppers in pan cut side up. Fill withthe filling.
Bake at 350 until cheese is bubbly and melted. NOTE: This mixture
makes a good casserole by itself or with cut up peppers.
