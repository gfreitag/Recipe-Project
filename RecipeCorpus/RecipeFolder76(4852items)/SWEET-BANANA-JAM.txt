Title: SWEET BANANA JAM
Categories: Spreads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Mashed banana
2 tb Lemon juice
Blend ingredients together until smooth.  Heat to a boil in a
saucepan; turn to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, until
mixture thickens. Yields 1/3 cup. SOURCE: unknown, from a file on
Canada Remotes Systems posted by Anne MacLellan
