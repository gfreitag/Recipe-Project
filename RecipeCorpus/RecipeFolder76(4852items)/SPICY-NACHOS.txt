Title: SPICY NACHOS
Categories: Cajun, Appetizers
Yield: 24 Nachos
24    Taco chips
16 oz Can refried beans
1 c  Monterey Jack cheese
2 tb Jalapeno peppers,chopped
2 tb Scallions
Preheat oven to 350.  Place taco chips on an ungreased baking sheet.
Spread a layer of refried beans over each chip. Sprinkle with cheese,
pepers and scallions.  Bake for 10 minutes or until cheese in melted.

Prep. time: 30 minutes
