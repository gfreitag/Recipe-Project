Title: TAOS HONEY LEMON DRESSING
Categories: Salad
1    lemon
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  honey
1/2 c  unseasoned rice vinegar
1/4 c  peanut oil
1 ts pure red chile powder

Zest the lemon, being careful not to include any of the bitter white
part. Finely mince the zest. Squeeze the lemon juice into a bowl, and
add the zest and remaining ingredients. Combine well and chill. Typos
by Brenda Adams <adamsfmle@sprintmail.com>
