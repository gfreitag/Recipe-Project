Title: TANTALIZING TOFU
Categories: Vegetarian, Easy, Main dish
Yield: 2 Servings
3 oz Tofu; cut into bite-size
-pieces
3 oz Broccoli; cut into bite-size
-pieces
3 oz Turnip greens; coarsely
-chopped
1 1/2 oz Walnuts; chopped
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Soy oil
3 oz Romaine lettuce
Saute all ingredients except for lettuce in soy oil for 3 to 4
minutes. Arrange on a bed of lettuce.
