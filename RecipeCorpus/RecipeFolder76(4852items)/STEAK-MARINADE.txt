Title: STEAK MARINADE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Lemon juice
3 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c  Oil
2 ea Garlic, minced
1. Pierce steak so marinade can penetrate. 2. Place steak in a non
metal dish just large enough to hold it or in a sealable plastic bag.
Refrigerate for 12 to 24 hours.
