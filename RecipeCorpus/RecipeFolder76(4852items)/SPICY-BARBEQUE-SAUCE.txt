Title: SPICY BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 12 Servings
12 oz BEER
1 1/2 c  KETCHUP
1/2 c  BROWN SUGAR
1/4 c  WINE VINEGAR
1 ts DRY MUSTARD
1 ts RED PEPPER FLAKES
1 ts BASIL
2 ts DIJON MUSTARD
8 ea DASHES WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE
1/2 ts GROUND CUMMIN
1 ea CLOVE GARLIC
IN A LARGE SAUCE PAN, COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS AND BRING TO A BOIL.
REDUCE HEAT AND SIMMER FOR ABOUT 30 MINUTES, COVERED.
