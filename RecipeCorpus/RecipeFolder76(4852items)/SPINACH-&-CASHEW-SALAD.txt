Title: SPINACH & CASHEW SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Chopped raw spinach leaves
1/2 c  Watercress
4    Green onions; sliced
1/3 c  Cashews
1/2 c  Croutons
Vegan salad dressing
-- (your favorite one)
Combine the spinach, watercress, green onions, and cashews in a
serving bowl.  Spoon the dressing over the salad and toss. Eat
immediately while crisp.

Source: The Compassionate Cook - by Ingrid Newkirk and PETA Typed for
you by Karen Mintzias
