Title: SPICY APPLESAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 4 Servings
10 md Apples (eating kind)
1/2 c  Water
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Ground cloves
Cut apples into medium-size pieces; do not core or peel. Place in
slow-cooking pot with all other ingredients. Cook on low 7 to 8 hours
until apples are very soft. Strain through sieve. Serve hot or cold.
