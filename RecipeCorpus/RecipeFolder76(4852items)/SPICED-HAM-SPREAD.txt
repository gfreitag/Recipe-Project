Title: SPICED HAM SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Pork
Yield: 32 Servings
4 c  Minced Baked Ham
2 tb Dijon mustard
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Cinnamon
1    Garlic clove, crushed
2 tb Unsalted butter, softened
Crushed water-pack green
-peppercorns to taste
Combine ham, mustard, mayonnaise, cinnamon, garlic and butter in
bowl; mix well. Add peppercorns gradually to desired taste. PAck
mixture into oiled 4 cup mold. Chill until firm. Serve with crackers
or party bread or use as stuffing for fresh vegetables.

Source: Great Recipes from Great Gardeners
