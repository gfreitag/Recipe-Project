Title: SPICY VINAIGRETTE DRESSING
Categories: Cajun, Salads
Yield: 1 Cup
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
2 tb Olive oil
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Dried basil
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts File' powder (optional)
Combine vinegar, oil, cayenne pepper, basil, mustard, black pepper and
file' powder in a small bowl or cruet. Mix well. Use as a dressing
for you favorite salad.

Prep. time: 10 minutes
