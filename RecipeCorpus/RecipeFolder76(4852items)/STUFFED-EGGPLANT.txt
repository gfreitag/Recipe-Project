Title: STUFFED EGGPLANT
Categories: Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
3 md Eggplants
2 tb Olive oil
1 md Onion, chopped
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1    Green pepper, chopped
1 lb Ground round
1/2 c  Grated romano cheese
1/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
2 tb Tomato paste
1 1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Freshly ground pepper
Basic tomato sauce
1.  Boil Eggplants in large kettle of water, covered, for 15 minutes.
Drain and cut lengthwise.  Carefully remove the pulp leaving a 1/2
inch thick shell.

2.  Preheat oven to 350F.

3.  In a skillet heat the olive oil, add the onions, garlic and green
peppers and saute until just tender. Remove to a bowl. Saute the beef
until it loses its pink color. drain and add to the bowl.

4.  Chop the egg plant pulp and combine with the sauteed mixture. Add
remaining ingredients except tomato sauce and mix well. Fill the
shells with the mixture and place in a greased baking dish. Brush the
tops with additional oil and bake about 45 minutes. Serve with tomato
sauce.
