Title: SPICY RICE
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
225    G, 8 oz long grain brown
Rice
5 ml 1 tsp ground turmeric.
1    Stick cinnamon.
1    Bay leaf.
4    Green cardamum pods.
6    Black pepper corns.
Put all the ingredients in a saucepan with enough water and bring to
the boil. Cook for 35 minutes or until the rice is tender.
