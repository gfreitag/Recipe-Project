Title: STRAWBERRY SORBET - WHNP81A
Categories: Ice cream, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Water
3/4 c  Sugar
2 c  Fresh strawberries
1/2 c  Orange juice
In a small saucepan, combine water and sugar.  Stir over low heat
until sugar dissolves; bring to a boil.
Boil gently 5 minutes without stirring; set aside to cool. Wash
strawberries; remove and discard caps.
Puree strawberries in blender or food processor fitted with metal
blade. In a medium bowl, combine pureed strawberries, cooled syrup
and orange juice. Pour into ice-cream canister. Freeze in ice-cream
maker according to manufacturer's directions.  Makes about 1 quart.
Per Serving: 90 calories - 0 prot. - 23 g. carb. - 0 fat - 0 chol.
~ 7 mg. calcium.
