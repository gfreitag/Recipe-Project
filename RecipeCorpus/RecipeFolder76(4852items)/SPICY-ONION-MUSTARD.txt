Title: SPICY ONION MUSTARD
Categories: Spreads, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Cold water
1 c  Dry mustard
1 c  White wine vinegar
1 c  Dry white wine
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
2 cl Garlic, crushed
1    Bay leaf, crumbled
1 tb Honey
ds Tabasco sauce
1 ts Dry tarragon
6    Allspice, whole
Combine water and mustard.  Mix into a paste.  Set aside. Combine the
vinegar, wine, onion, garlic, bay leaf, honey, hot sauce, tarragon,
and allspice in a large, non corrosive pan. Boil rapidly until
reduced by half. Stir repeatedly.  Strain the mixture into a double
boiler. Stir in the mustard paste.  Cook over simmering water until
thick, about 10 to 15 minutes. Cool. Cover and store in the fridge.
Stir again before using.
