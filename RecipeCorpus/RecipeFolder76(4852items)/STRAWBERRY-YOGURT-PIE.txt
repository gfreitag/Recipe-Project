Title: STRAWBERRY YOGURT PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
1 ea Envelope unflavored gelatin
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Milk
2 ea Large egg yolks
8 oz Cream cheese
1 tb Honey
1 ts Grated orange rind
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Plain yogurt
1 ea 8" baked graham cracker shel
1 x  Halved or sliced strawberrie
In a 1 1/2 quart saucepan,sprinkle gelatin over water to soften a
matter of minutes.In a 1 cup measure beat together milk and yolks
until blended;add to gelatin mixture.Over low heat stir constantly
with a wide rubber spatula,especially around side of pan,until
gelatin is dissolved;cool at room temperature.In a medium bowl,beat
together cheese,honey,orange rind and vanilla gradually and gently
beat in yogurt until smooth.Chill until very slightly thickened.Turn
into graham cracker shell;chill to set.At serving time,cover surface
with strawberries.Serves 6.
