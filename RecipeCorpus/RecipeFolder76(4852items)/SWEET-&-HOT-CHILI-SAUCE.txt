Title: SWEET & HOT CHILI SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 /4 cup
Stephen Ceideburg
2 sm Fresh Chilies, seeded and
-finely sliced
3 tb Soy Sauce
Juice of 2 Limes
2 tb Rice Vinegar
4 tb To 5 tb Sugar
3 tb Nam Pla (Fish Sauce)
Put all ingredients into a saucepan. Mix well, then bring to a boil
over high heat. Cook until mixture is reduced by half. Remove from
heat. Cover and store in the refrigerator until ready to use.

SOURCE: Flower and Garden Magazine Feb/Mar 92
