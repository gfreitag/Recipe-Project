Title: SPIT-WAD SANDWICHES
Categories: Crs, Kids, Hlween, Gross
Yield: 1 Servings
2 sl Bread
Creamy peanut butter
2 lg Marshmallows
MMMMM---------------------------TOOLS--------------------------------
Butter knife
Plate
Spread each slice of bread with peanut butter.

Using clean fingers, pull apart marshmallows into many tiny
spit-wad-sized pieces.

Press the marshmallow wads onto the peanut butter.

Put together, wad sides facing, and enjoy. Serves 1 who loves spit
wads.
