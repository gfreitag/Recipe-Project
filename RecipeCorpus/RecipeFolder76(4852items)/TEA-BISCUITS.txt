Title: TEA BISCUITS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sifted  Flour
4 ts Baking Powder
1/4 ts Salt
-Cut in 3-5 tablespoons
-Crisco
2/3 c  Milk
-Pre-Heat oven to 450F.
Combine flour, bankin powder and salt in bowl.  Cut in Crisco until
mixture resembles small peas.  Add Milk and stir together until
mixture comes away from sides of bowl. Put on a floured surface and
knead for a few seconds. Bake until golden brown (12 minutes)
