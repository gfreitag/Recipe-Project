Title: SPICY CHICKEN TIDBITS
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry, Jaw, Holiday
Yield: 10 Servings
2 lb Boneless, skinless chicken
Breast, cut into 2 to 3
Inch pieces
Wooden or bamboo skewers
MMMMM--------------------MARINADE NO. 1 (RED-------------------------
1 c  Plain yogurt
1 tb Ginger root, minced
1 tb Garlic, minced
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Salt
8    Drops red food coloring
MMMMM-------------------MARINADE NO. 2 (GREEN------------------------
1 lg Onion
1/2 c  Cilantro
1/2    Inch ginger root
3    Cloves garlic
2    Serrano peppers
1 ts Salt
4    Drops green food coloring
To prepare Red Marinade: Beat ingredients well with fork.

To prepare Green Marinade: Blend ingredients in food processor until
smooth (use 1/4 cup of water if needed).

In separate airtight containers, marinate 1 pound chicken in each of
the 2 marinades overnight. On wood skewers, which have been soaked
overnight in water, alternate "red" and "green" chicken, about 2
each, and grill for 8 to 10 minutes.
