Title: TAILGATE PARTY MIX
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 8 Servings
3 tb Butter
1/4 c  Hot pepper sauce
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
2 cn (2.8 oz. ea) french fried
-onions
2 cn (4 oz. ea) shoe string
-potatoes
2 c  Corn chex cereal
1 c  Mini pretzels
Melt butter in a 10 x 15 pan. Stir in hot pepper sauce, worcestershire
sauce and cheese. Add other ingredinets, one at a time, stirring well
after each addition. Bake at 250 deg f for 45 minutes, stirring each
15 minutes. Cool completely. Store in airtight container.
