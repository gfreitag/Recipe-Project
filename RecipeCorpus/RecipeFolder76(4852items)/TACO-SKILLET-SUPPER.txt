Title: TACO SKILLET SUPPER
Categories: Casseroles, Mexican
Yield: 8 Servings
4 c  Thinly sliced potatoes
1    Small onion, chopped
1 ts Chicken bullion
1 c  Hot water
1 lb Ground turkey
1    Envelope taco seasoning mix
1/4 c  Skim milk
1 1/4 c  Salsa, divided
1/2 c  Quick cooking oats
1    Egg
Coat a 2 qt baking dish with non stick spray. Add potatoes, and 1 T
onion. Dissolve bullion in water, pour 1/2 c over potatoes. Combine
turkey, taco seasoning mix, milk, 1/4 c salsa, oats, egg, and
remaining onion, mix well. Spread over potatoes. Combine remaining
salsa and chicken broth, pour over turkey mix. Bake uncovered at 350
for 1 hour, or until potatoes are tender.
