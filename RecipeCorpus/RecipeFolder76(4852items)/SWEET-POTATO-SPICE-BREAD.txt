Title: SWEET POTATO SPICE BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
1/3 c  Molasses
2/3 c  Honey
2/3 c  Mashed sweet potatoes
1/3 c  Oil
2    Eggs; beaten
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Cloves
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Mace
1 ts Freshly grated ginger
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Unbleached all-purpose flour
1 tb Baking powder
1 c  Chopped walnuts or pecans
Preheat oven to 325 F.  Oil a 9" x 5" x 3" loaf pan. In a large bowl,
blend molasses, honey, sweet potatoes, oil and eggs. In a small bowl,
combine remaining ingredients.  Add to potato mixture and stir untill
well mixed. Spoon batter into loaf pan and bake until a toothpick
inserted in middle comes out clean, about one hour. Turn out onto a
wire rack to cool before slicing. Hint: Serve it with nonfat cream
cheese.
