Title: THICK OVEN-FRIED POTATOES
Categories: Side dishes, Vegetables
6 md Potato, White, Raw, Peeled
PAM
Salt
Pepper
Yield: 8 Servings
:
:
:

Cut potatoes lengthwise into 1/2-inch thick slices; cut slices into
1/2-inch thick sticks the length of the potatoes. Arrange potatoes in
a single layer on 2 ungreased 12x15-inchrimmed baking pans. Spray
with PAM. Bake, uncovered, in a 400 degree oven, turning over with a
spatula as needed, until evenly browned and crisp, about 1 hour.
Sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
