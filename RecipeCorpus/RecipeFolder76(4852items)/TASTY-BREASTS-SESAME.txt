Title: TASTY BREASTS SESAME
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
1 sm Bottle Italian dressing
1    Clove garlic; minced
4 md Chicken breasts; skinless,
-boneless
1 ds Sesame seeds
1 ds Pepper
1    Lime
1.  Pour dressing into 10" frying pan to 1/4" depth.  Add garlic and
heat at med-low setting.

2.  Rinse breasts in water.  Arrange in single layer in pan, sprinkle
with sesame seeds and pepper.  Turn chicken when white on bottom (1-2
mins) and sprinkle more seeds.

3.  Cut half lime into 4 slices and place on each piece of chicken.
Squeeze juice from other half over chicken.  Cover and cook for 4
mins, or until coooked.

4.  Serve on bed of pasta with sauce spooned over top.
