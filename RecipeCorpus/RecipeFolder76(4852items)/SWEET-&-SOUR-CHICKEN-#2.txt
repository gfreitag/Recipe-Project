Title: SWEET & SOUR CHICKEN #2
Categories: Chinese, Chicken
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Chicken, boned and cubed
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Salt
10 oz Pineapple chunks
1/2 c  Sliced onion
2 tb Butter or cooking oil
1/3 c  Vinegar
2 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Soy sauce
3/4 c  Sliced green bell pepper
Brown chicken in butter in a large skillet. Combine onions and bell
peppers after chicken is near done. Combine other ingredients Cook
until clear and slightly thick, about 2 minutes. Add to chicken and
cook about 1 hour or until chicken is done. Serve over cooked rice.
