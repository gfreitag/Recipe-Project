Title: TAPENADE-MOLTO MARIO
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Anchovy fillets, soaked in
-milk overnight, patted dry
2 tb Anchovy paste
1 c  Pitted olives (Gaeta,
-Calamata or black)
1/4 c  Capers, roughly chopped
3 tb Dijon mustard
3 tb Red wine vinegar
1/2 c  Extra virgin olive oil
1/2 sm Red onion, roughly chopped
In a food processor, blend all ingredients until a smooth, homogenous
paste is formed (about 2 minutes). Tapenade may be refrigerated
several weeks, well wrapped.
