Title: SPLIT PEA & HAM SOUP
Categories: Ham, Soups, Legumes, Restaurants
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM-------------------MICKEY'S GOURMET CKBK------------------------
1 lb Dried green split peas
5 c  Chicken broth
5 c  Water
1    Meaty ham bone OR 2 smoked
-ham hocks
2    Ribs celery, diced
3 tb Parsley, chpd
1/2 ts Tarragon leaves
1 c  Carrots, diced
1 c  Onion, diced
1    Leek, white only, sliced
2 tb Dry sherry
1/2 ts Pepper
Garden Gallery, The Disney Inn Soak and rinse peas 20 mins. In 6 qt
pot, combine water, broth and peas; bring to boil. Add ham bone,
carrots, celery, leek, tarragon and 1T parsley. Reduce heat to med
low; simmer, partially covered, stirring occasionally, 1 hr. Remove
from heat. Remove meat from bone; shred. Remove excess fat and return
meat to pot. Add sherry, pepper and remaining 2T parsley. Heat.
