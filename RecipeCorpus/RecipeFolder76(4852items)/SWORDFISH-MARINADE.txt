Title: SWORDFISH MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Orange juice
1 tb Olive oil
1 tb Tomato paste
1 tb Parsley leaves; minced
1    Garlic clove; minced
1/2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 tb Oregano
1/4 tb Pepper
Recipe by: Dale Coggins <dcoggins@COUNSEL.COM> This is the best
swordfish marinade I have ever made. Whisk everything together,
marinate the swordfish in it for an hour, and grill.
