Title: TART APPLES BAKED WITH ORANGE JUICE & HONEY
Categories: New, Text, Import
4    tart granny smith apples
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Ground cinnamon
Yield: 4 Servings
:
:
2 ts tupelo honey
2 TB golden raisins
1/4 c  orange juice

Spray glass baking pan with light cooking spray. Cut cone out of top
of each apple and spoon some lemon juice into each. Sprinkle with
cinnamon. Add some honey and juice to each and stuff each with
raisins. Microwave, lightly covered, until skins have split and
apples are softened. Serve hot.
