Title: TANAKA TRADITIONAL NOODLES
Categories: Pasta, Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 5 Servings
1 pk EDEN Udon
-OR- EDEN Lotus Root,
-- Jinenjo, Buckwheat or
-- Mugwort Soba
2 tb EDEN Toasted Sesame Oil
-OR- Hot Pepper Sesame Oil
1 pk EDEN Shiitake Mushrooms
-(soaked in...
1 c  -hot water for 20 minutes)
1 md Onion; sliced
1 c  Carrot
-cut into matchstick pieces
2 c  Chinese cabbage or bok choy
-- or mustard greens,
-- finely chopped
3 tb EDEN Organic Shoyu
2 tb Grated fresh ginger
1/3 c  EDEN Tamari Almonds, sliced
-- (optional)
Prepare pasta. Rinse, drain and mix with 1 tablespoon toasted sesame
oil. Set aside. Reserve shiitake soaking liquid, discard stems and
slice shiitake mushrooms caps. Heat oil, saute onion, add carrots,
shiitake and soaking liquid. Simmer 5 minutes. Mix in greens, add
pasta, shoyu, ginger and almonds. Serve.

Cooking: 10 minutes Yield: 5 Servings
