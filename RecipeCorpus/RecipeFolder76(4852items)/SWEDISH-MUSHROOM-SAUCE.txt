Title: SWEDISH MUSHROOM SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Mushrooms, Sliced
4 tb Butter
4 tb All-Purpose Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts White Pepper
1 1/2 c  Half and Half
1 1/2 tb Lemon Juice
1 1/2 tb Sherry,Dry
Offer as a sauce or a side dish. Saute mushrooms in butter until
limp. Stir in flour, salt, and pepper. Add cream; cook, stirring
constantly, until thick and smooth. Stir in lemon juice and Sherry;
salt to taste. Makes about 2 1/2 cups sauce or 3 to 4 servings.
