Title: TEXAS STYLE COLE SLAW
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
6 c  Green cabbage; grated
1 lg Green bell pepper; chopped
1 lg Red bell pepper; chopped
1/2 c  Fresh coriander; chopped
1 lg Garlic clove; mashed
1    Jalapeno pepper; chopped
1    Onion; chopped
1 c  Olive oil; or corn oil
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1 ts Sugar
Salt and pepper; to taste
Recipe by: "Jessica A. Walton" <ryanja@WKUVX1.WKU.EDU> Grate cabbage.
Chop bell peppers and coriander.  Mix together. Make adressing by
combining the remaining ingredients. Toss with cabbage mixture and
allow to stand for one hour before serving.

Serves:  8

Comments:  A twist on cole slaw that is perfect with barbeque.
