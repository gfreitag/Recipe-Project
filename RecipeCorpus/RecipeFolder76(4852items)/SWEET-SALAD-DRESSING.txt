Title: SWEET SALAD DRESSING
Categories: New, Text, Import
1/2 c  strawberry jelly
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  salad oil or nut oil
3 TB lime juice
1/2    clove garlic, -- squeezed
through press
:

In large bowl combine all ingredients. Whisk together until smooth.
Serve.
