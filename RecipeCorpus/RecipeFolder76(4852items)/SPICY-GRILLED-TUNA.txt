Title: SPICY GRILLED TUNA
Categories: Fish, Cuba, Update
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1 ea Shallot, minced
1/2 ts Cumin powder
1/4 c  Chopped fresh cilantro
2 lb Tuna fillet
In a small mixing bowl, combine olive oil, salt, cayenne, lemon juice,
shallot, cumin and cilantro. Place tuna in glass or ceramic dish. Pour
marinade over tuna and marinate 1 hour in refrigerator, turning once.
Brush grill with vegetable oil and heat to medium. Grill fish 4 to 5
minutes on each side. Garnish with cilantro sprigs and lemon wedges.
Serves 4. SPICY GRILLED TUNA
