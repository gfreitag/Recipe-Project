Title: SUN-DRIED TOMATO SALSA
Categories: Salsa, Tomatoes
Yield: 1 Cup
1/2 c  Sun-dried tomatoes; finely
Chopped
1/2 c  Black olives
2    Cloves garlic
1 tb Fresh basil
2 tb Olive oil
1 ts Red wine vinegar
Salt & pepper to taste
Combine all ingredients until well blended. Season with salt &
papper to taste. This is a good salsa to accompany seafood.
