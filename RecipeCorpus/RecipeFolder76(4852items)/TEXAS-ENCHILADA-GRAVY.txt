Title: TEXAS ENCHILADA GRAVY
Categories: Tex-mex, Sauces
Yield: 1 Batch
2 tb Lard (or shortening)
2 tb Flour
2 tb Chili powder
3 c  ;Water, warm
Salt; to taste
Melt lard in a skillet, stir in the flour and make a light roux. Add
the chili powder, water and salt; cook until thick.

-- The Only Texas Cookbook
