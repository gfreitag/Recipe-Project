Title: TEACHER RICE MIX
Categories: Rice
1 c  uncooked white rice
Yield: 1 Servings
2 TB beef boullion granules
1 ts dried onion flakes
1/2 ts rosemary
1/2 ts marjoram
1/2 ts thyme

Mix everything  together, and store in an airtight jar. This recipie
doubled fits nicely into a pint jelly canning jar.

To prepare, cook according to the directions on the rice package.
