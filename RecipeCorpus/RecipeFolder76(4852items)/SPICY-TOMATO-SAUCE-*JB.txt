Title: SPICY TOMATO SAUCE *JB
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Cups
3    Garlic cloves, minced
3 tb Olive oil
1 lg Onion, chopped fine
1/2 ts Dried hot red pepper flakes
2 cn (28-to-32 ounce) tomatoes
-including juice, pureed
-coarse in a blender in 2
-batches
1/2 ts Dried thyme, crumbled
In a heavy 5-quart saucepan cook garlic in oil over moderate heat,
stirring, until golden and add onion and red pepper flakes. Cook
mixture, stirring, until onion is softened and add tomato pur√©e,
thyme, and salt and pepper to taste. Simmer sauce, stirring
occasionally, 35 minutes, or until thickened slightly. Sauce may be
made 3 days ahead and chilled, covered.

Makes about 6 cups.
