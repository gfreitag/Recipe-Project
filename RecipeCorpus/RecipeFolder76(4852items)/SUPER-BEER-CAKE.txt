Title: SUPER BEER CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Beer
Yield: 10 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3    Egg; separated
2 1/2 c  Flour, sifted
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Beer
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/8 ts Cream of tartar
Preheat oven to 375. Cream soft butter and sugar until fluffy. Add egg
yolks, one at a time, beating well after each. Blend in sifted dry
ingredients alternately with beer, beginning and ending with dry
ingredients. Add vanilla. Sprinkle in baking powder. Beat egg whites
and cream of tartar until stiff. Fold gently but thoroughly into
batter. Grease and flour two 9-inch layer pans. Pour batter into
pans. Bake at 375 for 25 min. or until cake tests done.
