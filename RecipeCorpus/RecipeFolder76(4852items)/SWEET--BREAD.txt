Title: SWEET  BREAD
Categories: Bread, Abm
Yield: 1 Loaf
2 1/2 ts Dry yeast
3 c  Whole wheat flour
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Dry milk powder
1 1/2 tb Oil
5 tb Honey
1 lg Egg
9 1/2 oz Water about 130 degrees
Preparation :
The "Welbilt" bread machine on sweet bread cycle. Again add
ingredients in order listed.  The critical ingredient again is the
amount of water after a couple of tries you'll find the right amount.
*You may have to cut back on yeast to about 1 teaspoon depending on
weather and time of year.
