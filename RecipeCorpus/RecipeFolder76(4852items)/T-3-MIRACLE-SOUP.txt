Title: T-3 MIRACLE SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 8 Servings
6 lg Onions
2    Bell peppers
3 cn Tomatoes
1    Cabbage head
1    Celery stalk
1 pk Soup mix, onion
Cut up all vegetables in a large pot, cover with water, add onion
soup mix, and boil for 10 minutes. Cover and lower heat and simmer
until vegetables are soft.
