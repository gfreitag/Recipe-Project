Title: TABBOULEH SALAD
Categories: New, Text, Import
1/3 c  fine bulgur wheat
Yield: 4 Servings
1/3 c  rich vegetable stock, --
heated to boiling
:
2 TB olive oil
2 c  minced fresh parsley
3 TB minced fresh mint
1 TB minced scallion
2 ts minced garlic
2 TB fresh lemon juice
1 ts grated lemon zest
Salt and freshly ground
black pepper
:
:

In a bowl, combine the bulgur, the boiling stock, and the olive oil
and cover. Allow to stand for at least 30 minutes, then uncover and
fluff with a fork. Stir in the parsley, mint, scallion, garlic, lemon
juice and lemon zest. Correct the seasoning with salt and pepper.
