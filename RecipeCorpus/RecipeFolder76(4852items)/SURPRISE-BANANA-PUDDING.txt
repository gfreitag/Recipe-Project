Title: SURPRISE BANANA PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
2/3 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
4 c  Milk
5    Egg yolks
1 tb Peanut butter
1 pk Vanilla wafers (12-oz)
6    Bananas, medium-size, ripe,
-sliced
1    Carton frozen whipped
-topping (8-oz), optional
Serves 8

*DIRECTIONS*

Combine sugar, flour, and salt in top of a double boiler; stir in
milk. Bring water to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cook, stirring
constantly, 10 to 12 minutes or until slightly thickened.

Beat egg yolks until thick and lemon colored. Gradually stir about
one-fourth of hot mixture into yolks; add to remaining hot mixture,
stirring constantly. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture
thickens. Remove from heat, and gently stir in peanut butter and
vanilla.

Layer one-fourth of vanilla wafers in a 3-quart casserole; top with
one-third of bananas. Pour one-third of filling over bananas. Repeat
layers twice, ending with remaining vanilla wafers. Spread top with
whipped topping, if desired. Chill. Yields 8 to 10 servings.

Source: "Southern Living: 1986 Annual Recipes" Oxmoor House, 1986.
