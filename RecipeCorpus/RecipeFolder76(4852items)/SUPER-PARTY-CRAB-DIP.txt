Title: SUPER PARTY CRAB DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Dips, Shrimp
Yield: 12 Servings
16 oz Philly cream cheese;
-softened
1 pk Imitation crab meat; 16
-ounces
1/2 c  Salad dressing; not mayo!!!
1    Shrimp sauce; 8 ounce jar
Mix first three ingredients.  Spread on platter, then "frost" with
shrimp sauce...chill...serve.  It will be the biggest hit of any
get-together.
