Title: STIR-FRY CHERRY TOMATOES & HERBS
Categories: Seasonings, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pt Cherry tomatoes, stemmed
;washed, patted dry
;(abt. 36)
1 tb Butter
1 tb Olive oil
1 lg Garlic clove, minced
1    Shallot, minced
-or-
2    Scallions, minced
1/3 c  Fresh basil, chopped
1 tb Fresh oregano, chopped
Salt
Freshly ground pepper
In a large skillet, heat butter and oil.  Add garlic and shallots (or
scallions); saute for 2 to 3 minute until fragrant. Add tomatoes and
herbs, shaking pan continuously for 2 to 3 minutes, until heated
through. Do not over-cook. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve immediately.

From 1994 "Shepherd's Garden Seeds Catalog," pg. 49. Posted by Cathy
Harned.
