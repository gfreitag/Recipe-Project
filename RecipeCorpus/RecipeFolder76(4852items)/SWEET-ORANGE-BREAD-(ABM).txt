Title: SWEET ORANGE BREAD (ABM)
Categories: Digest, August, Abm, Ovo, Lacto
Yield: 12 Servings
2    Water
1    Egg white
2    Orange juice
3/4    Salt
1    Applesauce or margarine
2    Bread flour
1 1/2    Instant dry milk powder
3    Sugar
1/4    Orange peel
1 1/2    Rapid rise yeast
Combine all ingredients according to manufacturer's directions.
Process in regular, delayed, or rapid bake cycle.
