Title: TARATOUR
Categories: Appetizers, Oriental, Sauces, Snacks
Yield: 2 Cups
1 c  Tahini
2 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
1/3 c  Lemon juice
Salt & pepper
1/4 c  Water
Fresh parsley, chopped
Combine tahini, garlic, lemon juice, salt & pepper in a blender.
Blend till smooth.  Gradually add the water until the sauce reaches
the desired consistency: it should be thicker than a dressing or
sauce. Add up to
1/2    TOMATO & ONION SALAD water. Adjust seasonings. Garnish with
parsley.
