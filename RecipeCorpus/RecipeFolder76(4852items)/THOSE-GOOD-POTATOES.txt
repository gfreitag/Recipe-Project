Title: THOSE GOOD POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Mc
6 md potatoes, cooked til tender
& sliced thin
Yield: 6 Servings
:
1 lg onion, sliced thin
1    green pepper, sliced thin
1 sm jar Pimentoes
1 c  sour cream
1 cn cream of chicken soup
1 ts salt and pepper
4    eggs, hard-boiled
1 c  bread crumbs
1 c  sharp cheddar cheese,
shredded
:

Mix sour cream and soup in 2 qt. casserole or 13 x 9 x 2 baking dish.
Mix in everything else, saving 1 egg and crumbs for topping.

Bake 350 oven for 30 min.  If made ahead of time and kept
refrigerated, then bake for 50 min.
