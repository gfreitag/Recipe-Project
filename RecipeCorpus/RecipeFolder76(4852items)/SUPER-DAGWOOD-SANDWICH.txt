Title: SUPER DAGWOOD SANDWICH
Categories: Main dish, Snacks
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Loaf French bread
2 tb Butter; softened (optional)
8 oz Ham; fully cooked, thinly
-sliced
6 oz Provolone cheese; sliced
3 tb Thousand Island dressing
4 oz Turkey; cooked, thinly
-sliced
4 oz Salami; thinly sliced
2 tb Butter; melted
1 tb Toasted sesame seeds
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Using an electric or serrated knife,
slice the bread horizontally into 3 equal pieces.
Spread the first piece with half of the softened butter, (if
using). Top with the ham, Provolone cheese and half of the Thousand
Island dressing.
Place the second piece of the bread on top.  Spread with the
remaining softened butter, if using.
Top with the turkey, salami, remaining Thousand Island dressing and
the top slice of bread.  Place loaf on a baking sheet.
Stir together the 2 Tbls melted butter and the sesame seed. Brush
the mixture over the top and sides of the loaf. Cover loosely with
foil.
Bake in the 400 degree oven for 10 minutes.  Remove the foil and
bake for 5 minutes more.  Slice to serve. If you prefer a cold
sandwich, you can also add lettuce, sprouts and/or slices of tomato,
onion and/or green sweet pepper. From Better Homes & Gardens
America's Best-Loved Community Recipes.
