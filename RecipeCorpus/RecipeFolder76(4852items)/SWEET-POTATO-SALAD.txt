Title: SWEET POTATO SALAD
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Sweet potatoes; baked
1/2 c  Green peas; cold cooked
1/2 c  Crushed unsweetened pineappl
3 tb Parsley; chopped fresh
1/3 c  Favorite dressing
8    Lettuce leaves; (optional)
Peel and slice sweet potatoes and place in a small bowl. Add remaining
ingredients; toss lightly. If desired, serve on lettuce leaves.
