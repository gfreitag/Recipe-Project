Title: SWEET & SOUR MEATBALLS ... STUART
Categories: Appetizers, Party, Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Onions; chopped
1 ea Egg
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
10 oz Grape jelly
12 oz Chili sauce
DIRECTIONS -   MIX TOGETHER HAMBURG, ONIONS, EGG,BREAD CRUMBS, SAUCE,
SALT AND   PEPPER. MAKE INTO BALLS, BAKE AT 350 FOR 20 MIN SAUCE;
HEAT JELLY AND CHILI SAUCE TOGETHER, POUR OVER MEATBALLS.
