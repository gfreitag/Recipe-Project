Title: SWEET CORN SNACK
Categories: Snacks, Vegetables
8 lg ears of corn
Yield: 16 Servings
3 TB sugar
2 ts salt
1/4 c  milk

Cut the kernels from 8 large ears of corn.  Combine with 3
tablespoons of sugar, 2 teaspoons of salt and 1/4 cup milk in a
heavy-bottomed saucepan. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat to
simmer and cook for 15 minutes, stirring frequently.  Pour into a
shallow, greased baking pan and dry in oven for 1-1/2 hours at 250^F,
stirring occasionally. Corn should be light brown.

Store in a covered container for snacking.  Yield 7-8 cups.

: Detroit News 9/3/96
