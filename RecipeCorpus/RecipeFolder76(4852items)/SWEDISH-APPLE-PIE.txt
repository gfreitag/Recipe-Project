Title: SWEDISH APPLE PIE
Categories: Apples, Ethnic, Low-fat/low, Pies & past
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
4    Egg whites
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
(optional)
2 ts Baking powder
2 c  Apple -- finely chopped
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  In a large bowl, mix first
ingredients; the mixture will be thick.  Stir in chopped apple. Spray
a pie pan with no stick spray. Put mixture in the pan and bake for
about 35 minutes.
