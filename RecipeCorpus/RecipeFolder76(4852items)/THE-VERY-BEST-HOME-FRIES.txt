Title: THE VERY BEST HOME FRIES
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
4 lg Potatoes
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Butter
1    Green onion, sliced
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/4 ts Salt
Pinch each pepper and
-paprika
Scrub potatoes.  In pot of boiling salted water, cook potatoes for 15
minutes or until tender but firm; drain well.

Refrigerate until cold.  Cut into 1/2 inch cubes.

In nonstick skillet, heat oil and butter over high heat; cook
potatoes, turning to coat all, for 3 to 4 minutes.

Cook for about 8 minutes longer, turning often, or until crisp and
golden. Remove from heat; stir in onion, garlic, salt, pepper and
paprika. Makes 4 servings.   Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com
Source: Canadian Living's Best Vegetables
