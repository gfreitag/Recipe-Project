Title: STEAMED BABY CARROTS
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Valentine's
Yield: 2 Servings
16 ea Baby carrots, scraped
1 ts Soy margarine
1/8 ts Nutmeg
Steam baby carrots in a saucepan with 1/4" of water until
tender-crisp. Should take about 4 minutes. Drain & toss with the
margarine & nutmeg. Allow 8 carrots with each serving, arrange them
on plates in a fan shape with the narrow tips pointing down.
