Title: THE LARK'S IMPOSSIBLE GREEN BEAN/MUSHROOM SUP
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 8 Servings
-This Divine Creation
-CREATED by The.LaRK
16 oz Green beans; drained
10 3/4 oz Cream of Mushroom soup
10 3/4 oz Water; Use Soup can
2 c  Mushrooms; fresh, sliced
1 ts Onion powder
3    Eggs
6 oz Cheese; grated
2 c  Bisquick
Mix all ingredients together.  Pour into large baking dish. Bake at
375F for 45-60 minutes.

Enjoy!!
