Title: THE BEAST
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
3    shots white rum -- baccardi
Yield: 2 Servings
1    shot Triple Sec
1 c  apple juice -- fresh
1 cn blueberries
1 c  ice
2    mint leaves -- for garnish

Place all ingrediants into blender and blend until smooth. Serve in
tall cocktail flutes. Garnish with mint leaves
