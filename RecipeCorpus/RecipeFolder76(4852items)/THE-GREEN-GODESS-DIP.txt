Title: THE GREEN GODESS DIP
Categories: Cheese, Sauces and
Yield: 3 Servings
1 c  Green Onion -- chopped fine
1/3 kg Soft White Cheese (9% 5% Or
1/2    % Fat)
1/3 kg Sour Cream
1/3 c  Dill -- chopped fine
2 tb Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Parsley -- chopped fine
2 tb Lemon Juice -- fresh if
Possible
1 tb Vinegar
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
Combine all ingredients in a small bawl. Stir well. Serve chill.
