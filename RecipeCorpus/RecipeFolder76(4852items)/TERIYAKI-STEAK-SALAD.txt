Title: TERIYAKI STEAK SALAD
Categories: Main dish, Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Top round steak
1    12 oz bottle teriyaki marine
1 c  Shredded mozarrela
8    Green onions sliced w tops
24    Cherry tomatoes halved or
6 radished sliced
1    Med green bell pepper chop
1/4 c  Sweet & sour salad dressing
Marinade steak in teriyaki sauce in refrigerator for several hours,
turning occasionally. Remove steak and broil 3 to 5 inches from heat
source for 5 to 8 minutes or medium rare. baste with marinade after
turning.Cool and slice. Combine remaining ingredients in bowl and
toss with dressing. arrange among 6 plates.Slice meat across grain
crosswise and arrange atop greens.
