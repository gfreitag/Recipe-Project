Title: STUFFED POTATO SKINS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 16 Quarters
1/2 lb Beef, ground
3/4 c  Jack cheese, shredded
1/2 c  Ranch dressing
1/4 c  Olives, ripe and sliced
1 tb Green chiles, chopped
16    Potato skin quarters, cooked
In a medium bowl stir together the beef, 1/2 cup of the cheese, ranch
dressing, olives and green chiles until well blended. Using a spoon,
put approximately 2 T filling on each potato skin. Sprinkle potato
skins with the remaining 1/4 cup cheese.  Broil 6" from heat for 3 to
5 minutes or until hot and cheese is melted. Makes 16
