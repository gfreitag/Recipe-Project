Title: SWEET-N-SOUR SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce, Dips
Yield: 16 Servings
8 oz Tomato sauce
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Garlic, minced, dried
2 ts Salt
1 ts Basil, leaf
1 ts Mustard, dry
1/4 ts Tobasco sauce
Combine all ingredients and allow flavors to blend at least 2 hours.
Baste ribs, chops, or chicken breasts.

If desired, this sauce may be thickened with cornstarch or arrowroot.
