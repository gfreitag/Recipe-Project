Title: TARATOOR II (SESAME SAUCE)
Categories: Sauces, Middle east, Dips
Yield: 1 1 1/2 cups
3 md Garlic cloves, chopped
1 c  Tahini paste
1 c  Cold water
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 ts Salt
In a deep bowl, mash the garlic to a paste with a pestle or the back
of a large spoon.  Stir in the tahina. then, with a whisk of spoon,
beat in 1/2 cup of the cold water, the lemon juice, and salt. Still
beating, add up to 1/2 cup more of water, 1 tablespoon at a time,
until the sauce has the consistency of thick mayonnaise and holds its
shape almost solidly in a spoon.  Taste for seasoning. Taratoor can
be served as a sauce with baked fish or fried cauliflower, and is
used as an ingredient in hummus and baba ghannooj.
