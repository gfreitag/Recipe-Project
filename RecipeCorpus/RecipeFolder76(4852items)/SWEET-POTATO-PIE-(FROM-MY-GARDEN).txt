Title: SWEET POTATO PIE (FROM MY GARDEN)
Categories: Wasnone
3 lg sweet potatoes -- peeled,
sliced,
boiled
Yield: 1 Servings
:
:
2    sticks butter
1 1/2 c  sugar
4    eggs -- beaten
1/2 c  buttermilk
1 ts vanilla
2    homemade or pre-made pie
crusts
:

Preheat oven to 300 degrees. In large bowl mash potatoes while still
hot . Add butter and mix well. Stir in sugar and eggs. Add milk and
vanilla. Stir to combine. Pour into pie crusts. Bake for 1 hour.
Serve hot or room temperature.
