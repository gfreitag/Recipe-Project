Title: SPICY TEXAS STYLE BBQ CHICKEN
Categories: Barbecue, Chicken, Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/2 ts Mustard, dry
1 c  Bull's eyes bbq sauce
1 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Pepper, red
3 lb Chicken
Combine seasonings and rub onto chicken pieces. Bake skin side down
at 375 for 30 minutes. Brush with sauce and bake 30 more minutes,
brushing every 10 minutes. On grill, cook 45 minutes, brushing
frequently during last 30 minutes.
