Title: SYRIAN CHICKEN & RICE
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
PATTI - VDRJ67A-----
1 1/2 lb Ground beef -- sirloin or
Roud
1 c  Rice
2 c  Chicken broth
1/2 c  Burgandy wine
1 c  Chicken; cooked -- diced
1 1/2 ts Allspice
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 c  Pine nuts -- toasted -OR-
Walnuts -- chopped
Brown beef and drain all fat. Add rice, broth, wine, allspice, nutmeg,
cinnamon, salt and pepper. Simmer 20 minutes or until rice is done.
Add nuts and cooked chicken. Stir and simmer 5 minutes or until
chicken is heated. Can be frozen.
