Title: TAMMY'S DUMP CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Cake mixes, Fruits, Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 12 Servings
32 oz Crushed pineapple
2 cn Cherry pie filling
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 c  Chopped nuts
4 oz Butter -- melted
Drain juice from 1 can pineapple and most of the second. SAVE Layer
1st pineapple & pie filling sprinkle cake mix on top, add nuts, and
pour on melted butter. Pour on saved pineapple juice, so top is wet.

bake 1 hour @ 350
