Title: STRAWBERRY LOAF CAKE BSWN00A
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
2 c  Sugar
3 x  Eggs, beaten
1 c  Oil
10 oz Frozen strawberries, thawed
2 lb Confectioners' sugar
10:12 PM
04/23/92
Combine flour, soda, salt, cinnamon and sugar; mix together well.
In another bowl, combine eggs, oil and (sliced) strawberries. Add to
the dry ingredients and mix well. Pour into 2 greased 9x5" loaf pans.
Bake in pre-heated 350 degree oven for about 1 hour.
Leave in pan to cool. When almost cool, turn onto wire rack and
dust top with confectioners' sugar.
These freeze well and are very moist when thawed. If you freeze
them, don't dust with confectioners' sugar until thawed. Source:
newsletter7-86, Geneva Hawkins
