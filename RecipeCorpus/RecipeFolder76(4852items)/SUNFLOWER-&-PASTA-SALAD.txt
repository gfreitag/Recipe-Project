Title: SUNFLOWER & PASTA SALAD
Categories: Garlic, Salads, Sauces, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
4 oz Bow-tie pasta, cooked
1 c  Cherry tomatoes, halved
1/4 c  Sliced black olives
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Sunflower oil
Garlic powder
Black pepper
1/2    Cucumber, sliced
4    Sliced radishes
2 tb Roasted sunflower seeds
Combine salad ingredients.  Mix together dress in jar and shake until
blended.  Pour over pasta mixture and toss lightly. Chill several
hours or overnight, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle with additional
sunflower seeds.
