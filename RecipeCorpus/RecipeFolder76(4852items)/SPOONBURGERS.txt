Title: SPOONBURGERS
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Beef
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Ground Beef
2/3 c  Chopped Onion
1 tb Mustard
1 cn Condensed Chicken Gumbo Soup
1 tb Catsup
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Water
Brown meat and onion. Add other ingredents, Stir over low heat in a
skillet for 30 minutes. Serve on buns... May also add garlic to
taste. From: Beth Benjamin/ Marcia Wilder / Bettye Maynne.
