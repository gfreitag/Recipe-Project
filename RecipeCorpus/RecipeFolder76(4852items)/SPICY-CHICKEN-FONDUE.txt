Title: SPICY CHICKEN FONDUE
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
6    Boneless skinless chicken
Breasts
4 tb Oil
2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Chili powder
Curry Sauce-----
1 tb Oil
1    Onion -- finely chopped
2 ts Mild curry power
3 ts All-purpose flour
1 1/4 c  Milk
6 ts Mango chutney
Salt and pepper
Cut chiken into 3/4 in pieces and mix with oil paprika and chilli
powder. Place chicken on a serving plate. To make curry sauce heat
oil in a saucepan. Add onion and cook until soft. Stir in curry
powder and cook for 2 minutes then stir inflour. Gradually stir in
milk and bring slowly to the boil stirring all the time. COntinue to
cook until sauce thickens. Simmer for 5 minutes then add chutney and
season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with the chicken cooked in the
hot oil.
