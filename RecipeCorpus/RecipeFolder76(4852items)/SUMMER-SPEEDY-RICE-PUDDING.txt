Title: SUMMER-SPEEDY RICE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
-Cathy Svitek bcxv75a
MMMMM------------------------POST TRIBUNE-----------------------------
1    Rice,boil in bag (family siz
1    Lowfat Yogurt (Vanilla);8oz
2 tb Honey
1/2 ts Cinnamon;optional
1 1/2 c  Strawberries
Bluesberries
Raspberries; chopped
Peaches;or combination
Cook rice according to pkg directions; drain Cut bag open and
empty into medium bowl; cool to room temp. Stir in yogurt, honey and
cinnamon; cover and chill. Just before serving, layer with or stir in
fruit. Makes 4 servings, about 3 1/2 cups pudding.
