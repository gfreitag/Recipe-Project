Title: SPICY SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Catsup
1 c  Chili sauce
1/2 c  Prepared horseradish
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 sm Onions or green scallions
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic salt or 1 garlic pod
2 tb Vinegar
4    Dashes tobasco sauce
2 tb Sugar
1 c  Green pepper
Mix in a bowl, catsup, chili sauce, horseradish, chopped onion,
chopped green pepper.  add all seasonings, let stand in refrigerator
several hours before serving.
