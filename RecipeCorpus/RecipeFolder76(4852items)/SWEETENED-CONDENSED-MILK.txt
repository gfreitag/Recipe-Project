Title: SWEETENED CONDENSED MILK
Categories: Misc
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Cold water
1 1/2 c  Nonfat dry milk powder
3/4 c  Sugar
Measure water into 2-cups glass measure.  Stir in milk powder until
smooth. Microwave almost 1 minute on high or until milk is steaming
Stir in sugar until dissolved. Cool. Yield: 14 oz. milk.
