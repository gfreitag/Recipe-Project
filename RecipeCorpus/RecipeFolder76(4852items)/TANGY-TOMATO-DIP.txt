Title: TANGY TOMATO DIP
Categories: Low fat, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Ff yogurt
1 c  Cottage cheese
1/4 c  Tomato juice
1 tb Onion flakes
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Lemon juice
2 tb Catsup
1 ts Soy sauce
Sieve cottage cheese and fold in yogurt. Blend in mustard, soy sauce,
catsup, tomato juice and onion flakes. Makes 2 cups.
