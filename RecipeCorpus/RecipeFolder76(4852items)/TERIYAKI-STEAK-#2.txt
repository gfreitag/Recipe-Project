Title: TERIYAKI STEAK #2
Categories: Beef
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM----------------FAVORITE RESTAURANTS, BON A---------------------
4 sm 3/4" thick NY strip steaks
-OR 2-3 lb flank steak,
-trimmed and scored
1 1/4 c  Light brown sugar
1 c  Soy sauce
1 1/2" piece ginger, peel,
-grate
1/2 cl Garlic, crushed
1/8 ts Sesame oil
Beamreach Restaurant, Kauai, Hawaii Arrange steak in shallow dish.
Combine remaining ingreds and blend well. Pour over steak. Cover and
marinate in refrigerator at least 3 hrs or overnight, turning
occasionally. Broil or bbq to desired doneness. If using flank steak,
slice diagonally across grain before serving. S: Fav Rest Rs, Bon A
