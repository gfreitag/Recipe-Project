Title: SPINACH STUFFING
Categories: Side dish, Poultry
Yield: 1 Recipe
8 pk Frozen chopped spinach
-cooked, retain liquid
1    Onion sauteed in about
-1-2 tbls. oil
Garlic salt to taste
4 c  Grated swiss cheese
8    Eggs
3/4 c  Oil
3 c  Bread crumbs
This recipe will stuff approximately a 20 lb. turkey

Cook Spinach and reserve liquid.  Let cool.  In large bowl mix
spinach with the rest of ingredients and refrigerate. I advise taking
this out and letting it come down to room temp since it tends to be
hard to handle.
