Title: SPICY OKRA/BASMATI RICE/BLACK BEANS
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Onions; chopped
2 c  Tomatoes, fresh; chopped
1 ts Sesame oil; hot
1 c  Okra; sliced
1 ts Garlic; chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2    Bouillon cube, vegetable
2 c  Basmati rice
1 c  Beans, black, canned
In a medium-sized saucepan, saute the onions and tomatoes in the oil
for 5 minutes. Add the okra, garlic, salt, pepper and bouillon cube.
Cook for 15 to 20 minutes. Serve hot over the rice and beans.
