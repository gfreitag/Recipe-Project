Title: SWEET & SAVOURY GRILLING SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Grill
Yield: 2 Cups
1 1/2 c  Thick tomato sauce
3 tb Sweetener
1 tb Molasses
1 tb Olive oil
2 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Mustard
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Oregano
Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl & mix well. Cover & let
stand for at least 1 hour.

Use to as a marinade for grilled seitan or tempeh.
