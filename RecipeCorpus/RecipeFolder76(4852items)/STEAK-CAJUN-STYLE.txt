Title: STEAK CAJUN-STYLE
Categories: Cajun, Beef
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Margarine, melted
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts White pepper
4    Ribeye steaks
Heat a skillet to 350 degrees and pour melted margarine in. Combine
seasoning in a small bowl. Dip steaks in melted margarine and
sprinkle with season mixture. Place steaks in skillet and cook each
side for about 3 1/2 minutes. Serves 4.

Source: More Cajun Cooking, Modern Publishing, New York, NY Copyright
