Title: STUFFED TOMATOES (FROM MY GARDEN)
Categories: Wasnone
4    tomatoes
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  couscous -- cooked
3    carrots -- diced
1 c  small peas
2    zucchini -- diced
1/2 c  orange juice
1/4 c  mint -- chopped
Salt
Pepper
:
:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Carefully remove top quarter of tomatoes
and scoop out inside pulp. Be careful not to break the walls. In
small bowl combine remaining ingredients. Fill the shells with
mixture. Bake for 30 minutes.
