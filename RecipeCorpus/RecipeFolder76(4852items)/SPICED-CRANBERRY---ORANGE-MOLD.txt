Title: SPICED CRANBERRY - ORANGE MOLD
Categories: Jello, Dessert
Yield: 10 Servings
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
2 pk (4-serving size)JELL-O Brand
-Cherry Flavor Gelatin
-Dessert
1 cn (16 oz)whole berry cranberry
-sauce
1 c  Cold water
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 t  Ground cinnamon
1/8 t  Ground cloves
1    Orange sectioned diced
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
STIR boiling water into gelatin in large bowl 2 minutes or until
completely dissolved. Stir in cranberry sauce, cold water, lemon
juice, cinnamon and cloves. Refrigerate about 1 1/2 hours or until
thickened (spoon drawn through leaves a definite impression). STIR in
orange and walnuts. Spoon into 5-cup mold.

REFRIGERATE 4 hours or until firm. Unmold. Garnish as desired. Makes
10 (1/2-cup) servings.
