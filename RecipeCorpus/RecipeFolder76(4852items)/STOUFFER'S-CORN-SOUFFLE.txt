Title: STOUFFER'S CORN SOUFFLE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
2    Eggs
2 tb Cornstarch
2 tb Sugar
Salt and pepper
ds Nutmeg
1 lb Can cream style corn
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Milk
With  electric mixer beat eggs until foamy. Beat in remaining
ingredients as listed. Pour into Pam-sprayed 8" square baking dish.
Bake on center rack of prehgeated 400~ oven 35 minutes or until knife
inserted comes out clean. Source: Gloria Pitzer's Make Alike Recipes.
