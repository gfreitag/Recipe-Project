Title: SWEET MONKEY BREAD
Categories: Breads, Breakfast
Yield: 12 Servings
3 cn Biscuits (10 ct. each)
1 c  Sugar
3 tb Cinnamon
1/2 c  Margarine (1 stick)
1 c  Brown sugar
Open biscuits; cut into quarters and set aside.  Mix sugar and
cinnamon. Roll biscuits in sugar mixture until well coated. Put in
buttered 13 x 9 x 2 inch cake pan. Melt margarine and brown sugar;
pour over biscuits. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.

NOTE: May be sprinkled with nuts.
