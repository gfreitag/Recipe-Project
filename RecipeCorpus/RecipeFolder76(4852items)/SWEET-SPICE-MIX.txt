Title: SWEET SPICE MIX
Categories: Spices
Yield: 2 Servings
2 tb Cinnamon
1 tb Nutmeg
1 1/2 ts Ground cloves
1 1/2 ts Ground allspice
1 tb Ground ginger
1 tb Dried orange/lemon peel
Orange peel should be grated.  Sprinkle on plain toast, French toast,
waffles, pancakes, oatmeal, vanilla pudding, cut-up apples or oranges.
Sprinkle over muffins before baking and apple pie before warming.

Buy spices in bulk from a natural foods store.
