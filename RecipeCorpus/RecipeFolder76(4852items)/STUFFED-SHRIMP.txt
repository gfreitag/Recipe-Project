Title: STUFFED SHRIMP
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 2 Servings
8 ea Shrimp, jumbo
2 tb Parsley, chopped
1 c  Bread crumbs
6 tb Butter
1/4 c  Crab, flaked
1/4 c  Sherry
1/4 c  Almond, chopped
Remove shell up to tail. Cut shrimp down to the back leaving in
butter- fly fashion. Remove large intestine. Place in baking dish.
Melt butter. Combine remaining ingredients. Place stuffing in each
shrimp cavity. Bake in preheated 350 deg oven for 20 minutes or till
tails curl up and shrimp meat is bright white. Serve with drawn
butter and lemon wedge.
