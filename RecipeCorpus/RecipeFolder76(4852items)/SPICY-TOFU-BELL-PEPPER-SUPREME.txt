Title: SPICY TOFU-BELL PEPPER SUPREME
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 2 Servings
2 tb Olive oil, extra virgin OR
2 tb Vegetable stock
1 1/2 c  Bell peppers, mixed green
-and red; diced
3/4 c  Onions, yellow; cubed
1 1/4 c  Salsa, tomato mild
1/2 c  Olives, black; pitted
1 1/2 c  Garbanzo beans, canned;
-drained
1 c  Tofu; baked, cubed
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Combine the oil, peppers, and onion in a saucepan and cook over high
heat for 5 to 8 minuts. Reduce the heat and add the remaining
ingredients. Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes. Serve with Fresh
Corn Salad or brown rice.
