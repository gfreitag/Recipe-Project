Title: SWEET NOODLE KUGEL
Categories: Kugel, Noodle, Sour, Crea, Pineapple
Yield: 12 Servings
1 lb Broad noodles, cooked and
Drained
1 1/2 pt Sour cream
1 1/2 c  Butter, melted
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
5    Eggs, beaten
1 lb Crushed pineapple, well
Drained
4 oz Dried apricots or peaches,
Diced
Cinnamon sugar
Grease 9 by 13 inch-pan. Combine all ingredients except cinnamon
sugar. Pour into prepared pan. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Bake at
350F for 45 minutes.
