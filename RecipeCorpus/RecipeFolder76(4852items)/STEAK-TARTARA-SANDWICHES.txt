Title: STEAK TARTARA SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches, Beef
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Ground beef for the best
Quality
2    Egg yolks
Salt and Pepper
1 md Onion chopped very thiny
8    Anchovies
1    Lemon
Butter
Mustard
Chiles serranos chopped
8    Bread slices for to make
Sandwiches
Mix the meat with the egg yolks, salt and pepper, add olive oil as
needed for to be a soft dough. In the bread slices put the things in
this order: butter, mustard, meat, onion finely chopped, 3 or 4 lemon
drops, and one anchovie. Add chiles serranos chopped if desire.
Patricia Wriedt
