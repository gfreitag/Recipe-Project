Title: SPINACH & RED ONION SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
6 c  Torn fresh spinach
1/4    Small red onion, thinly -
Sliced
1    Apple, chopped
1/4 c  2% plain yogurt
2 tb Apple juice
1 1/2 ts Cider vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
Pinch of pepper
In salad bowl, toss together spinach, onion and apple. Whisk together
yogurt, apple juice, vinegar, salt and pepper; toss with salad to
caot. Makes 4 servings. Source: Canadian Living Magazine Typed in
MMFormat by Cindy Hartlin. Aug. 27, 1996.
