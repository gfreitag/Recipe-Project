Title: TEA SYRUP
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 4 Servings
6 c  Water
4 c  Sugar
1 c  Lipton tea leaves
Bring water to a hard boil; add tea leaves.  Let set for 15 minutes.
Drain hot tea into sugar and stir until dissolved. Add 1 cup of this
syrup to 1 pitcher of cold  water.  (This syrup will keep as long as
2 weeks in the refrigerator.) Posted by Joey Mills Fido Cooking
