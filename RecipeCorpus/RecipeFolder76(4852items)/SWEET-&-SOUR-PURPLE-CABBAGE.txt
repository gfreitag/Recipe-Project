Title: SWEET & SOUR PURPLE CABBAGE
Categories: Vegetables, Low-fat
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ts Margarine
1/2 c  Chopped onion
3 c  Chopped purple cabbage
2 c  Apples - sliced and halved
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Vinegar
1/2 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Saute onion in cooking spray and margarine for 1 minute. Add
cabbage and apples and saute for 3 minutes.
Add the remaining ingredients and stir well. Cover. Cook over low
heat 20-25 minutes.
Sprinkle with carraway seed if desired.
Yield: 6 side-dish servings
