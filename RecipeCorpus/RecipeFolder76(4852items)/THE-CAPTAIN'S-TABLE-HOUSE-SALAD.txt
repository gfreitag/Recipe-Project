Title: THE CAPTAIN'S TABLE HOUSE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ea HEAD CHICKORY, CLEANED AND
1 x  BROKEN INTO BITE-SIZE PIECES
1 ea HEAD ROMAINE, CLEANED AND
1 x  BROKEN INTO BITE-SIZE PIECES
1 ea CARROT, SHREDDED
1 ea TOMATO, SLICED INTO EIGHTHS
1 ea CUCUMBER, SLICED THINLY
1 x  HOUSE VINAIGRETTE DRESSING
Toss ingredients together and sprinkle with dressing.
