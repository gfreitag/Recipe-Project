Title: SPICY PEANUT YOGURT DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Dips, Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
1/4 c  Peanut Butter
1 c  Plain Yogurt
1 ts Coriander; Ground
1 1/4 ts Cayenne Pepper
1/8 ts Pepper
Blend the peanut butter and yogurt together, mixing until smooth. Add
all of the other ingredients, blending well. Cover and chill. Makes
about 1 1/4 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS:  Seafood, Kiwi, Plums,
Saltines
