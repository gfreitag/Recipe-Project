Title: SPLENDIFEROUS GUACAMOLE DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Dips, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
4 ea Ripe Avocados; Md.Lg.
1/4 c  Lime Juice
2 c  Tomatoes; Fresh, Ripe, Diced
1 c  Yellow Onion; Diced
1 tb Jalapeno Pepper; Diced
1/4 ts Hot Sauce
1/4 c  Fresh Cilantro; Chopped, OR
4 ts Dried Cilantro; Crushed
MMMMM-------------------------GARNISHES------------------------------
1 x  Cilantro
1 x  Sour Cream
Peel and pit the avocados, then coarsely mash them in a bowl with a
fork. Mix in the lime juice, blending well. Add the other ingredients
one at a time, blending well after each addition, in the order given.
Garnish with sour cream and sprinkle a little cilantro on top. May be
served at room temperature or chilled. Makes about 5 1/4 cups of dip.
SUGGESTED DIPPERS: Warm Tortilla Chips, Baby Corn, Black Olives,
Jicama, Mushrooms
