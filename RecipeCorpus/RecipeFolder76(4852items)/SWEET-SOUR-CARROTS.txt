Title: SWEET-SOUR CARROTS
Categories: Vegetables, Relishes
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Frozen sliced carrots
1    Green pepper, lg, diced
1    Onion, lg, diced
1 cn Condensed tomato soup
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Oil
1 ts Pepper
1 ts Salt
1 ts Dry mustard
Cook 1 bag of frozen sliced carrots in salted water until just tender.
Combine with green peppers and onions. Simmer remaining ingredients
together until flavors blend. Pour over carrot mixture. Serve hot, or
may be chilled and served as a relish. Will keep, covered, in
refrigerator several days. Mrs. Harold T. Cook
