Title: TAVERNS (LOOSE-MEAT SANDWICH FILLING)
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 Batch
1 lb Hamburger
1/3 c  Catsup
1 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Vinegar
1 tb Mustard
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chopped onion (optional)
Brown hamburger with onions (if desired).  Add catsup, brown sugar,
vinegar, mustard, worcestershire sauce, and salt. Mix and heat..
Serve in hamburger buns.
