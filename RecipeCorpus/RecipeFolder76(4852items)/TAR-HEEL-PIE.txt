Title: TAR HEEL PIE
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Chocolate chips
4 oz Butter, melted
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Plain flour
1/2 c  White sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2    Eggs, beaten
1    Unbaked pie shell
Pour warm butter over chocolate chips and stir. Blend all remaining
ingredients (other than pie shell) and stir into chocolate chip
mixture. Pour into pie shell and bake in 350 F oven for 30-40
minutes. Tar Heel Pie (from the kitchen of Mary Baker)
