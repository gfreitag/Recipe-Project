Title: TERRY'S TACO DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 12 Servings
1 cn Refried Beans
Chili Powder -- to taste
Tabasco Sauce -- to taste
8 oz Cream Cheese
1 pt Sour Cream
1 pk Taco Seasoning Mix
Lettuce -- shredded
Cheddar Cheese -- shredded
Tomatoes -- diced
Black Olives -- sliced
Layer the following in a glass casserole dish, in the order given:

1.  Heat refried beans in microwave for 2 minutes. Blend in chili
powder and tobasco.  Blended until smooth.

2.  Blended together:  cream cheese, sour cream, and taco seasoning.

3.    Shredded lettuce.

4.  Shredded cheddar cheese, diced tomatoes, and black olives.
