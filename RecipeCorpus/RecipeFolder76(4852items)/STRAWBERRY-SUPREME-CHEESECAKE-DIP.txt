Title: STRAWBERRY SUPREME CHEESECAKE DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Dips, Fruit, Mc, Cheesecakes
16 oz cream cheese -- softened/or
low-fat
Yield: 1 Servings
:
1/2 c  strawber
1/4 c  sugar
1/4 c  light sour cream
1 TB orange liqueur
1 ts vanilla extract

Process cream cheese, strawberries, sugar, sour cream, liqueur, and
vanilla in a blender or food processor until very smooth. Pour into
bowl, cover, and chill at least 2 hours. Serve with your choice of
dippers.
