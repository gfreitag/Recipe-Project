Title: SPICY RED LENTIL DISH
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 3 Servings
2 tb Oil
1 c  Red lentils
1    Onion,chopped
1 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
2 1/2 c  Water
Stir fry lentils,onion, and spices in oil for 2 minutes. Add water
and cook over medium heat for 15 minutes in covered pot. Stir
occasionally. Serve hot with rice.
