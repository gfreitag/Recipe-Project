Title: SWEET POTATO PIE (AINBINDER)
Categories: Desserts, Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 8 SERVINGS--------------------------
1 1/2 c  Sweet potato; boiled
2    Eggs; separated
3/4 c  Brown sugar
2 c  Milk
2 tb Butter; melted
1 ts Nutmeg or cinnamon
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 pn Salt
Mash the boiled sweet potatoes.  Beat the egg yolks with the sugar;
add to the potatoes with the milk, melted butter and spice. Beat the
egg whites, baking powder and salt. Fold the potato mixture in the
well-beaten egg whites.  Pour into a pastry-lined pie pan. Bake at
350F until a silver knife inserted in the center comes out clean,
about 45 minutes. [ The Legends of Louisiana Cookbook; Sheila
Ainbinder; ISBN 0-671-70817-1 ]
