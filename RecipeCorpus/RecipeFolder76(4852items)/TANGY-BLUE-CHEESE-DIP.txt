Title: TANGY BLUE CHEESE DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese, Dips, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Blue Cheese; Crumbled
1 1/2 c  Sour Cream
1 ts Garlic; Minced
2 tb Fresh Chives; Finely Chopped
2 ts Dried Chives; Crumbled, *
1/8 ts Hot Sauce
*    Use the dried chives if the fresh is not available, otherwise
omit. +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++ Blend the blue cheese and sour cream thoroughly.
Add all the other ingredients, blending well. Cover and chill. Makes
about 2 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS:  Carrots, Cherry Tomatoes,
Pineapple, Apple, Pearapple, Cocktail Black Bread, Italian Or French
Bread Chunks
