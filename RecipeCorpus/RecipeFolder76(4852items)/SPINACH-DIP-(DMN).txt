Title: SPINACH DIP (DMN)
Categories: Dips, Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
2    (10-ounce) packages chopped
Frozen spinach, thawed and
Drained well
1 c  Chopped parsley
1/3 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
4 ts Chopped onion
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Dried thyme
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
Crackers
Mix all ingredients, except crackers, thoroughly and chill at least 4
hours. Serve with crackers. Makes 12 to 16 servings.

Per serving: Calories 134 Fat 13g Cholesterol 11mg Sodium 309 mg
Percent calories from fat 85%
