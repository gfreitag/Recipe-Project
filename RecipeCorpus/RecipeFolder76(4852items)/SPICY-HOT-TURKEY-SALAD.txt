Title: SPICY HOT TURKEY SALAD
Categories: Cajun, Salads
Yield: 3 Cups
3 c  Green Salad
2 c  Cooked turkey
1 c  Hot Pepper Dressing
Place Green Salad in a large bowl.  Add turkey, and mix well. Pour
dressing over salad.  Toss lightly to distribute.

Prep. time: 30 minutes
