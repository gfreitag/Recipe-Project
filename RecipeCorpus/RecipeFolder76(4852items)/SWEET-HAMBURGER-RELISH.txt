Title: SWEET HAMBURGER RELISH
Categories: Pickles, Relishes
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Chili Sauce, Bottled
1/2 c  Sweet Pickle Relish
1 pn Ground Black Pepper
1 pn Cayenne Pepper
1 pn Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
Mix all ingredients together.  Put in covered jar in refrigerator.
Will keep for a number of weeks.
