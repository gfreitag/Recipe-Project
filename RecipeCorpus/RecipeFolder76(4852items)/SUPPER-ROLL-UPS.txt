Title: SUPPER ROLL-UPS
Categories: Main dish, White wine
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Frozen asparagus(16oz)
1 cn Cream/mushroom soup(10oz)
2 tb Chopped almonds
2 pk Sliced turkey meat(4oz)
1/3 c  Dry white wine
Heat oven to 350'. Cook asparagus spears as directed on package;
drain. Wrap each turkey slice around 2 or 3 asparagus spears. Place
in ungreased baking dish, 10x6x2 inches. Mix soup and wine; pour over
turkey. Garnish with almonds. Bake uncovered until bubbly, about 25
minutes.
