Title: SUGAR & SPICE CASHEWS
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Cashews
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Cloves
1/4 c  Light corn syrup
Melt butter.  Add sugar and corn syrup.  Microwave on high 2 minutes.
Stir and cook on high 3 more minutes. Stir once to mix in vanilla and
spices. Pour immediately over nuts. Toss and cook on high for 3
minutes, stirring occasionally.  Spread on waxed paper to cool. Makes
1 quart.
