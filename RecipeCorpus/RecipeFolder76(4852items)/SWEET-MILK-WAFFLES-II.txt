Title: SWEET MILK WAFFLES II
Categories: Breads, Breakfast
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/3 c  Ready-mixed flour
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Melted shortening
1 c  Sweet milk
1 ts Sugar
Sift flour, measure, and sift.  Beat egg yolks until light and foamy.
Add sugar and shortening.  Mix well. Add milk alternately with flour
to egg mixture.  Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Bake in hot
waffle iron. 6 servings. The Household Searchlight - 1941
