Title: SWEET & SPICY GERMAN MUSTARD
Categories: Other sauce, German
Yield: 2 Quarts
2/3 c  Mustard seed
1/2 c  Dry mustard
1 c  Cold water
2 c  Cider vinegar
4 ea Garlic cloves
1/4 c  Brown sugar
2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Tarragon
1/4 ts Tumeric
4 ts Honey
Combine seeds and mustard with cold water.  Allow to sit refrigerated
overnight.

Combine all remaining ingredients except honey. Simmer, uncovered
10-15 minutes, reduce volume by one half. Pour this mixture through a
strainer, into mustard and seed mixture. Blenderize thoroughly. Cook
in double boiler until consistancy of gravy.  Add Honey. Will keep
refrigerated several years.
