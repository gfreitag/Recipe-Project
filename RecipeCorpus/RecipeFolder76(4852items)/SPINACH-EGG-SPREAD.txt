Title: SPINACH-EGG SPREAD
Categories: Eggs, Spreads, Sandwiches, Picnic
Yield: 1 Recipe
2 pk Frozen chopped spinach;
- thawed, well-drained
1/2 c  Onion
1/2 c  Celery; diced
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 ts Horseradish
4    Hard-cooked eggs; chopped
1/8 ts Salt
Black pepper; optional
Mix drained, thawed spinach with remaining ingredients.
Refrigerate to chill thoroughly.
Pack in container and place in ice chest, being sure it stays
:
cold. Make into sandwiches at the beach. Goes well with dark bread.
