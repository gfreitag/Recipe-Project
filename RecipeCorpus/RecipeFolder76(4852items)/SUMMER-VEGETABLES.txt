Title: SUMMER VEGETABLES
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
Vegetable cooking spray
4 ea Cloves garlic, minced
2 c  Zucchini, sliced
2 c  Yellow squash, sliced
1 c  Tomato, chopped
1/2 c  Green bell pepper, sliced
1/2 c  Vegetable stock
1 tb Chopped fresh basil
1 ts Dried whole basil
Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray; place over
medium-high heat until hot. Add garlic, and cook 1 minute. Add
zucchini and next 4 ingredients. Cook 3 minutes or until vegetables
are crisp-tender, stirring constantly. Stir in basil. Yield: 4
servings.

Frances Wiltshire in "Southern Living" June, 1991
