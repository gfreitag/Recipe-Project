Title: SPICY SOUR CUCUMBER SALAD
Categories: Pickles, Salad dress, Sauces, Thai, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
8 tb Spicy sour source --
Chilled
1    Chili
1/2 sm Cucumber -- peeled and
Sliced
1. Place thinly sliced cucumber into shallow serving disk and pour
over spicy sour sauce (see recipie).

2. Chill for 2-3 hours prior to serving.
