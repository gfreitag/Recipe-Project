Title: THEAD FAMILY SLAW
Categories: Sides
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Head cabbage
1 lg Carrot
Mayonnaise
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Cider vinegar
2 tb Heavy cream
1 ts Celery seed
1 ts Seasoned salt
1 ts Black pepper
Chop the cabbage.  Shred the carrot.  Mix with all ingredients except
mayonnaise in large bowl.  Add mayonnaise to desired consistency.
Usually in the neighborhood of 3/4 to 1 cup. Refrigerate for at least
1 hour. It'll collapse significantly.
