Title: STIR FRIED VEGGIES
Categories: Vegetarian, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
-LISA HLAVATY  FDGN81A
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1    Red pepper; cut in strips
1 c  Celery hearts; sliced
1 bn Asparagus; diagonally sliced
1    Zucchini; thinly sliced
1/2 lb Mushrooms; sliced
3/4 ts Seasoned salt
1/4 ts Dried basil and thyme leaves
1/4 ts Coarsely ground black pepper
In a large skillet or wok, melt butter over medium heat. Add red
pepper, celery and asparagus.  Stir fry about 3 minutes. Add
zucchini, mushrooms and seasonings. Use more butter, if necessary.
Stir fry 2 minutes longer. Serve hot. Many be halved or doubled.
