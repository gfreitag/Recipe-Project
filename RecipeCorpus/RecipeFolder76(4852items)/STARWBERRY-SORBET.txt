Title: STARWBERRY SORBET
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
1 qt Strawberries, hulled
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
In heavy saucepan boil water and sugar for 5 minutes: do not stir. Add
strawberries; cook 5 minutes more. Stir in Lemon juice; cool. Puree.
Pour into 13 x 9 pan. Freeze 2 hours; beat until smooth. Repeat
freezing and beating.  Freeze 8 hours.
