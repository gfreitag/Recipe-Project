Title: SWEET POTATO PIE (FROM VEG TIMES)
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Pies, Vegan
Yield: 8 Servings
1    9" pie crust
4 md Sweet potatoes
2/3 c  Water or soy milk
1 1/2 tb White miso
1 tb Cornstarch
1 pn Cardamom
1 pn Cinnamon
1 pn Nutmeg
1 pn Ginger
1/4 c  Walnut halves
Prepare pie crust & set aside.

Preheat oven to 375F degrees.

Wash & scrub potatoes. Place in a baking pan, brush skins with some
olive oil & bake for 90 mins until very soft. Cool & then peel. Mash
& add the rest of the ingredients. Blend until the mixture has a
creamy consistency.

Reduce oven to 350F degrees.

Spoon mixture into pie crust. Garnish with walnuts. Bake for 60 mins.

Chill or cool to room temperature before serving.

Variation: Add 1 to 2 cups cooked chick peas to the sweet potato
before cooking the pie.  Ensure they are well mashed into the mixture.

Source: "Vegetarian Times" October, 1991
