Title: STUFFED SPUD MARCO POLO
Categories: Stuft spuds, Ham, Poultry
Yield: 2 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------STUFFED SPUDS----------------------------
2    Baked potatoes
3/4 c  White sauce
1/3 c  Cooked turkey, diced
1/3 c  Ham, diced
1/3 c  Cooked broccoli
Cut a thin slice from top of each potato. Keep shells warm. Remove
pulp; mash with 1/2c white sauce, reserving the remaining 1/4c to put
on top. Add turkey, ham, and broccoli; mix gently. Heap in to shells.
Spoon 2T white sauce over each potato and put under the broiler until
lightly browned.
