Title: STEAK & ALE MARINADE
Categories: Marinade, Meats
Yield: 12 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
FOR STEAKS
1/2 c  Ketchup
2/3 c  Tomato juice
1/4 c  Red wine -- or purple grape
--FOR CHICKEN OR PORK--
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Pineapple juice
1/4 c  White wine-or white grape
Mix ingredients for each marinade and marinate steaks or chicken up
to 24 hours in the fridge. Add a dash of worcestershire sauce to
steak marinade. Source: Gloria Pitzer's Secret Recipe Newsletter
