Title: SUNNY OATMEAL COOKIES
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
2/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Butter or margarine,softened
1    Egg
1/4 c  Sweet molasses
3/4 c  Rolled oats
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds
Cream butter, egg and molasses.  Sift together flour, sugar, soda,
salt, and spices.  Add flour mixture into egg mixture. Beat until
smooth. Add rolled oats and sunflower seeds and stir well. Drop from
tablespoon onto greased cookie sheet about 3 inches apart.  Bake at
350 F for 8-10 minutes.
