Title: SWEET & SOUR TOFU
Categories: Main dish, Thai, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
4 oz Dried shiitake, soaked
2 lb Tofu
2 ea Japanese cucumbers, peeled
1 ea Green bell pepper
3 md Tomatoes, cut into wedges
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
MMMMM-------------------------SEASONINGS------------------------------
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Tomato paste
1 1/2 tb Cornstarch dissolved in
3/4 c  Water
1/2 ts Pepper
2 ts Chili sauce, see recipe
1 ds Lemon juice
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
2 ea Green onions, chopped
Trim the shiitakes & set aside.  Press the tofu for 30 minutes & cube.
Quarter the cucumbers lengthwise & cut into 1 1/2" lengths. Chop the
bell pepper. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Add the tofu &
carefully stir- fry until the colour changes. Remove the tofu & add
the garlic. Stir-fry until the colour changes. Add all the vegetables
to the wok & stir-fry for 2 minutes.  Add all the seasonings &
continue to stir-fry for 3 to 5 minutes.  Serve garnished with green
onions.
