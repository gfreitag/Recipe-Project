Title: TANGY GREEN BEAN CASSEROLE
Categories:
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Fresh green beans -- cut in
1 1/2    In pcs.
2 tb Onion -- finely chopped
2 tb Olive or vegetable oil
1 tb Vinegar
1    Garlic clove -- minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Dry bread crumbs
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
In a saucepan, cover beans with water.  Cook until crisp-tender;
drain. Add onion, oil, vinegar, garlic, salt and pepper; toss to
coat. Transfer to an ungreased 2-qt. baking dish.  Toss crumbs,
cheese and butter; sprinkle over bean mixture.  Bake, uncovered, at
350 for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.

: Taste Of Home June/July '96
