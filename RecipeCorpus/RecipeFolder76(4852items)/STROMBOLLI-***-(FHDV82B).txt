Title: STROMBOLLI *** (FHDV82B)
Categories: Main dish, Appetizers, Italian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ea Bread dough; frozen
1 x  Mozzarella cheese
1 x  Italian salad dressing
1 ea Jars Olive; italian
1 x  Pepperoni
1 x  Ham
1 x  Provolone cheese
Let dough thaw and rise a little.  Roll out dough into a thin layer
on a floured surface.  Brush dough with a thin layer of Italian salad
dressing. Layer ham, provolone cheese, pepperoni, and mozzarella
cheese.
Spread olive mix on top.  Roll up and seal edges.
Bake at 350 degrees until brown. FROM:    STEPHANIE TAVERA (FHDV82B)
