Title: SUGAR &  SPICE POUND CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Butter Or Margarine,
-Softened
1 1/2 c  Sugar
5    Eggs
1 c  Plum Jam Or Preserves
1/2 c  Whipping Cream
2 1/2 c  All-Purpose Flour
2 ts Ground Ginger
1 ts Baking Powder
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground Nutmeg
Preheat oven to 325F. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar until
fluffy using an electric mixer. Add eggs, ont at a time, beating well
after each addition. Stir in jam and cream. In a medium bowl, sift
together remaining ingredients. Add dry ingredients to creamed
mixture; beat until smooth. Pour batter into a greased and floured
10" tube pan. Bake 1 hour to 1 hour 5 minutes. Test for doneness with
a toothpick. Cool in pan 10 minutes. Remove from pan. Cool completely
on a wire rack. Store in an airtight container. Posted to MC-Recipe
