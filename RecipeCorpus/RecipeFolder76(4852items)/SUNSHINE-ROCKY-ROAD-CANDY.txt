Title: SUNSHINE ROCKY ROAD CANDY
Categories: Candies, Chocolate, Kids
Yield: 2 Servings
1 ga Zip-Loc bag
1 lb Chocolate
1/4 lb Walnuts
1/2 lb Mini marshmallows
1/4 lb Raisens
Put chocolate in Zip-Loc bag and allow to melt in the sun. Add all
remaining ingredients. Close bag and mix well. Chill until hardened.
