Title: THE WEDGE OF LIGHT
Categories: Potatoes, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
4 lg Potatoes, unpeeled, scrubbed
1 tb Olive oil
1 1/2 ts "Cajun Spice" seasoning
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

Cut each potato lengthwise into 4 slices.  Then cut each slice into 2
wedges.

Place potatoes in a large bowl.  Add olive oil and Cajun spice. Toss
well to coat potatoes evely with oil and seasoning.

Transfer potatoes to a large baking sheet that has been sprayed with
on-stick spray.  Spread evenly ini a single layer. Bake for 20
minutes. Turn wedges over bake another 20 to 25 minutes, until golden
brown on the outside and tender on the inside.

Source:  Looneyspoons Cookbook

Per serving:  Calories: 280, Fat: 3.8g, Calories from fat: 11.8%
