Title: SPICY CHICKEN SALAD
Categories: Cajun, Salads
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
2 c  Cooked chicken, chunked
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Green onion, chopped
1/4 c  Water chesnuts, sliced
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Garlic powder
4    Lettuce leaves
Combine chicken, mayonnaise, green onions, water chestnuts, cayenne
pepper, dry mustard and garlic powder in a medium-sized bowl. Mix
well. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.  Serve on top of lettuce
leaves.

Prep. time: 1 hour
