Title: THIT NUONG UOP NGU VI HUONG (PORK TENDERLOIN)
Categories: Vietnam, Ham/pork
Yield: 6 Servings
3 lb Pork tenderloin cut lengthwi
-se into 5 or 6 strips
1 c  Soy sauce
1 tb "five spices"
1 ts Chopped fresh ginger
1 tb Sugar, granulated
2 ts Cooking oil
Marinate the pork for 1 hour at room temperature. Charcoal broil until
golden. The marinade can be refrigerated and reused, each time adding
1/4 of the above spices Source: Vietnamese Dishes by Duong Thi Thanh
Lien
