Title: SWEDISH RYE BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------ONE POUND LOAF----------------------------
1 ts Yeast
1 c  Bread flour
2/3 c  Rye flour
1/3 ts Grated orange peel
1 1/3 ts Caraway seeds
1 ts Salt
2/3 tb Butter or margarine
2 1/2 tb Honey
1/2 c  (plus 1 Tb.) Water
1.  Put ingredients in breadmaker mix/bake container and bake on
whole-
grain setting. ~--
