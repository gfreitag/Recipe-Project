Title: THREE TASTE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Recipe
1/4 c  EDEN Brown Rice Vinegar
1/4 c  EDEN Organic Shoyu
1/4 c  EDEN Mirin
Mix ingredients and enjoy as a sauce, dressing or marinade.

VARIATION: Ponzu Sauce, a delicious flavor combination, is made by
adding the juice of a lemon or lime to the above recipe.
