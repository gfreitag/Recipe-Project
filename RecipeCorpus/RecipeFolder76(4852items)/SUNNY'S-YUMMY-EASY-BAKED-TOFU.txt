Title: SUNNY'S YUMMY EASY BAKED TOFU
Categories: Miamiherald, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
1    16 oz block firm tofu
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Ginger
1 ts Basil
Rinse tofu and slice into 8 equal slices.  Put slices in a baking
dish. Pour soy sauce to cover slices, top and bottom. Do not drench.
mix flour, chili powder, ginger and basil in a bowl. Dredge wach
pieces of tofu in the flour mixture and place ona generously oiled
cookie sheet. Bake at 375 degrees F for 15 minute on each side. Serve
with noodle sor rice.

Nutritional info per serving: 246 cal; 22g pro, 21g carb, 10g fat
(36%)

Source: Dr Sunny Matthews, Miami Dade Community College(Wolfson),
Vegetarian-Nutrition Center Miami Herald 2/15/96 format by Lisa
Crawford, 7/7/96
