Title: SWEET POTATO PECAN BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Loaf
2 1/4 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
4 tb Rolled oats
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 pn Nutmeg
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Brown sugar, dark
3 tb Powdered milk
3 tb Butter or margarine
3/4 c  Sweet potatoes, cooked,
-mashed
3/4 c  Water
3 tb Raisins, dark
1/3 c  Pecans, chopped
Place all ingredients in machine and push start. Use raisin bread
cycle, adding fruit and nuts at beep.

From THE ULTIMATE BREAD MACHINE COOKBOOK by Lacalamita
