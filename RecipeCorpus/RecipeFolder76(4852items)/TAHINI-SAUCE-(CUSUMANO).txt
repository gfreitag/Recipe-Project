Title: TAHINI SAUCE (CUSUMANO)
Categories: Sauces, Spreads, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Tahini (sesame butter)
2 tb Mashed tofu
2 tb Yogurt
3 tb Water
1/2 ts Ginger powder
Pepper to taste.
Mix. Serve with fresh tofu
-or anything else.
>From the cookbook "Tofu, Tempeh & Other Soy Delights" by Camille
Cusumano AR/94
