Title: SPICY TEXAS BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Rubs
Yield: 10 Servings
1 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Brown sugar -- firmly
Packed
1/4 c  Lime juice
2 tb Ground red chiles
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 c  Chopped onion
2    Jalapenos -- finely chopped
2    Cloves garlic -- finely
Chopped
12 oz Tomato paste
12 oz Beer, any brand
*  Jalapeno chiles should be seeded and finely chopped. Heat all
ingredients to boiling in a 2 quart saucepan; reduce heat to low.
Cover= and simmer 1 hour; stirring occasionally.

Makes about 5 cups of sauce.

: Norm Corley
