Title: SUMMER DESSERT
Categories: Dessert, Norwegian
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Cream, whipped
4 c  Kefir milk, yogurt or butter
Milk
Juice of half a small lemon
3 tb Honey, mild
1 ts Pure vanill extract
Almonds
Blueberries
Lemon slices
Crisp cookies
Whipe cream. Whisk kefir into whipped cream a little at a time. Add
lemon juice, honey and flavoring. Pour into colorful bowl. Keep in
freezer about one halfhour before serving time, serve ice cold.
Decorate with a few thin lemon slices, and sprinkle chopped almonds
on top.
