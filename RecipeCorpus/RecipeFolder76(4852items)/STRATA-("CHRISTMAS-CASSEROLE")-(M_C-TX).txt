Title: STRATA ("CHRISTMAS CASSEROLE") (M_C-TX)
Categories: Casseroles, Cakes, Snacks, Breakfast
Yield: 12 Servings
6    Eggs, lightly beaten
1 c  Shredded colby or mild
-cheddar cheese
2 c  Milk
1 ts Dry mustard
6    Slices of bread, crusts
-removed and cubed
1 lb Sausage, fried & scrambled
1 ts Salt
Mix all ingredients.  Pour into 9" X 13" pan. Refrigerate overnight.
Bake at 350  for 35-40 minutes. Most requested recipe from the 1992
Mother-Daughter Style Show/Luncheon. Perfect for Christmas
morning--you assemble it the night before so you don't miss any of
the excitement on Christmas morning.
