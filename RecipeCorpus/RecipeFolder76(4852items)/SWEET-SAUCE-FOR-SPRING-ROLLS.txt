Title: SWEET SAUCE FOR SPRING ROLLS
Categories: Digest, August
Yield: 36 Servings
4 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1 c  Broth or water
2 tb Corn starch
1/4 c  Cold water
1    Clove garlic, crushed with
1/4 ts Salt
Combine sugar, soy sauce, and broth.  Bring to a boil. Add corn starch
mixed smoothly with the cold water, and stir until the mixture
thickens some.  Simmer, stirring for 1 minute. Stir in garlic. Serve
any temperature.
