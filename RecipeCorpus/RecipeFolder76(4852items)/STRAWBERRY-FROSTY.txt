Title: STRAWBERRY FROSTY
Categories: Beverages, Fruits, Holiday
Yield: 6 Servings
3 oz Strawberry Flavored Gelatin;
-1 Package
1 c  ;Boiling Water
1 qt Cold Milk
1 qt Strawberry Ice Cream
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Fresh Strawberries;
-Optional
Put the strawberry flavored gelatine and the boiling water into a
blender container and blend at low speed until the gelatin is
dissolved. Pour 1/2 c of the gelatin mixture into a glass measuring c
and set aside. Add half the milk to the remaining mixture in the
blender and blend on low speed until mixed.  Add half the strawberry
ice cream and blend until smooth. Pour into tall glasses. Repeat with
the reserved gelatin mixture, milk, and ice cream.  Garnish each
glass with a fresh strawberry, if desired
