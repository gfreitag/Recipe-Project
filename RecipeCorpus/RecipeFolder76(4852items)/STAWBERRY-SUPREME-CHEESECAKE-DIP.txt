Title: STAWBERRY SUPREME CHEESECAKE DIP
Categories: Dips, Cheesecakes
Yield: 1 Servings
16 oz Cream Cheese -- softened
1/2 c  Fresh Or Frozen
Strawberries
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Sour Cream
1 tb Orange Liqueur
1 ts Vanilla Extract
Process cream cheese, strawberries, sugar, sour cream, liqueur, and
vanilla in a blender or food processor until very smooth. Pour into
bowl,cover, and chill at least 2 hours. Serve with your choice of
dippers.
