Title: STRAWBERRY CHEESE SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Fruits
Yield: 1 Cup
1 pk Cream cheese - softened
1 tb Granulated sugar
Pinch salt
1/2 c  Crushed strawberries
1 ts Lemon juice
Whip cheese until light.  Combine sugar, salt and strawberries. Let
stand for 10 minutes; drain well. Blend strawberries and lemon juice
with cheese. Makes about 1 cup * Great snack with crackers or plain
bread. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Canadian Living
