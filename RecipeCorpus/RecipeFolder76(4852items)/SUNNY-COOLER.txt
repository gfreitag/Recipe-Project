Title: SUNNY COOLER
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Plain yogurt
1/4 c  Frozen orange juice concentr
1 tb Sugar
Put yogurt, orange juice concentrate, and sugar in a blender. Cover
and blend on high speed about two minutes until smooth and frothy.
Pour into a tall aglass. Serve immediately. Makes about 1 serving.
