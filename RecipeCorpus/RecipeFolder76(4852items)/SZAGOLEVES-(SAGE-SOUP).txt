Title: SZAGOLEVES (SAGE SOUP)
Categories: Hungary, Soups/stews, New, Formatted, Hungarian
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Sage; minced
1 ts Lemon rind; grated
2 tb Sugar, granulated
Lemon juice
2 c  Wine, red, dry
1    Cinnamon stick
Boil the sage and lemon rind in 3 cups of water for about 30
minutes. Strain. Add sugar, lemon juice to taste, and wine. Bring to
a boil.
Put the cinnamon stick in a soup tureen and pour soup over. Serve in
cups.

Note: This is a spiced drink as much as a soup, from Gyula Vasvary,
master chef of the 1820's.
