Title: SWEET POTATOES WITH MAPLE GINGER CREAM
Categories: Veggies, Posted-mm, Suzy
Yield: 4 Servings
4 md Sweet potatoes
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 tb Pure maple syrup
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground giner
1/4 c  Chopped pecans, toasted
Source: Southern Living Magazine 1/97

PIERCE sweet potatoes several times with a fork, and place on a baking
sheet.

BAKE at 375 degrees F. for 1 hour.

STIR together sour cream and next 3 ingredients. Spoon over split
baked potatoes, and sprinkle with pecans.
