Title: STEAMED SAMBAL PEASANT STYLE (SAMBAL LADA UAP
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
Stephen Ceideburg
5    Fresh red chillies
1    Onion, quartered
2    Tomatoes
Salt, to taste
1/4 ts Shrimp paste, grilled
-lightly, or 1 anchovy
-fillet
Place chilli, onion and tomatoes in a bowl and steam until tender.
Put in a blender with salt to taste and shrimp paste to make into a
very fine paste. Or pound in a mortar.

Yields 1 small bowl.

From "Dishes From Indonesia", Yohanni Johns, Thomas Nelson Ltd.,
Sydney, 1971. SBN 17 001929 2 [It says "SBN", not "ISBN" in the book.
S.C.]
