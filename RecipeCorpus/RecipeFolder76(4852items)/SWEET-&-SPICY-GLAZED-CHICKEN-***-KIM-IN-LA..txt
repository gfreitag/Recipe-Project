Title: SWEET & SPICY GLAZED CHICKEN *** KIM IN LA.
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Casserole
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Chicken;  boneless, skinles
8 oz French dressing
1/2 c  Apricot preserves
2 tb Onion soup mix.
Preheat oven to 375 Arrange chicken in baking dish Blend remaining
ingredients & pour over chicken Bake for 50-60 minutes, basting once
or twice I served this over Uncle Ben's long grain & wild rice. MMMM
MMMM good! Let me know if you like it! FROM:    KIM FRANCIS (DRSH57B)
