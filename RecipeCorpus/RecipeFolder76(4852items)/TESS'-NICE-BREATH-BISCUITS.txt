Title: TESS' NICE BREATH BISCUITS
Categories: Dog
Yield: 1 Serving
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 c  Corn meal
1/3 c  Chopped fresh mint
-(or 1 T dried)
1/2 c  Chopped parsley
6 tb Safflower oil
3/4 c  Water
Preheat oven to 350.  Combine flour, corn meal, mint and parsley in
large bowl.  Add oil and water and mix thoroughly. Roll out to 1/4"
thickness on a floured surface and cut into desired shapes with
cookie cutters. Bake 40 min., or until lightly browned.  Turn heat
off and let biscuits dry out in oven several hours. Store in an
airtight container in fridge.
