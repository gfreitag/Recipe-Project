Title: SUPER SWINE SIZZLERS DRY RUB
Categories: Bar-b-q, Spices, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
2 T  Paprika
1 T  Onion salt
1 T  Garlic salt
1 T  Ground basil
1 1/2 T  Lemon pepper
1 T  Red pepper
1 1/2 T  Black pepper
1 T  Mustard flour
(available at specialty
Grocers)
Combine all ingredients and rub into meat before cooking.
