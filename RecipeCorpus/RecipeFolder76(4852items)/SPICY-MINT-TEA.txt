Title: SPICY MINT TEA
Categories: Beverages, Formatted
Yield: 4 Servings
6 c  Water
2    Cinnamon sticks
4    Clove, whole
4    Allspice, whole
2 c  Mint leaves
Bring the water, cinnamon, cloves and allspice to a boil. Boil for 1
minute. Stir in mint leaves. Remove from heat and steep for five
minutes. strain into cups.
