Title: SWEET POTATO CRUNCH
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 8 Servings
2 1/2 lb Sweet potatoes, baked
-and peeled
8 3/4 oz Pineapple tidbits, drained
1/4 c  Bourbon
1/8 ts Nutmeg
2 tb Light brown sugar
1/4 c  Butter, melted
MMMMM-----------------------CRUNCY TOPPING----------------------------
1/2 c  Light brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter, melted
1/2 c  Cornflakes, crushed
Preheat overn to 350.  Mash potatoes until smooth and add to rest of
the main ingredients. Can be mixed in a food processor. Turn into
souffle dish or 2 qt shallow baking pan. Top with crunchy topping.

TOPPING: mix brown sugar, melted butter and crushed cornflakes. Toss
until well coated.  Sprinkle over sweet potato mixture. Bake
uncovered for 30 minutes or until topping is brown and crisp. Serves
8 -10.
