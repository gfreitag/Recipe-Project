Title: SPRINGTIME GARDEN PILAF
Categories: Rice
Yield: 8 Servings    2 3/4 c  water
1/4 c  margarine
2 TB instant chicken bouillon
1/4 ts salt
1/8 ts pepper
1/4 ts minced garlic
1 1/4 c  uncooked long grain rice
1/2 c  carrots -- finely chopped
1/2 c  slivered almonds -- toasted
1/4 c  fresh parsley -- chopped
2 TB green onion -- sliced

In a saucepan bring water, margarine, boillon, salt, pepper and
garlic to a full boil.

Add rice and carrots; return to a full boil. Reduce heat to low;
simmer till rice is tender (25 - 30 minutes).

Stir in remaining ingredients.

Garnish with green onions.

: Janet Baker
