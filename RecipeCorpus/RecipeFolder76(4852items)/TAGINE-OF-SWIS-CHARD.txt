Title: TAGINE OF SWIS CHARD
Categories: Salads, Moroccan, Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
12 c  Swiss chard, finely chopped
1 c  Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Cilantro, chopped
1/2 c  Salad oil
1 ts Sweet paprika
Salt & pepper
1/4 c  Raw rice
Place chard in the casserole with the onion, cilantro, oil, paprika,
salt, pepper & 1/4 c water.  Cook, covered, 30 minutes. Add the rice
& continue cooking until all the liquid has been evaporated, about 20
minutes. Serve warm.
