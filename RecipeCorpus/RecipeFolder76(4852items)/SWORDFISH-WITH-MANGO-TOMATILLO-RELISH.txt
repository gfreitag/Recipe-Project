Title: SWORDFISH WITH MANGO-TOMATILLO RELISH
Categories: Fish, Seafood
Yield: 2 Servings
1    Mango -- peeled and diced
1 md Tomatillo -- husked, washed
-
1/2    Red bell pepper -- seeded
And minced
2 tb Fresh cilantro -- minced
2 tb Lime juice
2/3 lb Swordfish
Ground black pepper -- to
Taste
And diced
Combine the mango, tomatillo, bell pepper, cilantro and lime juice.
Set aside. Place the fish on a rack in a baking pan or use a broiler
pan and sprinkle fish with pepper. Bake in a preheated 450-dregree
oven for 12 minutes per inch of thickness. Serve with the relish on
the side.

: Rozanne Gold
