Title: STIR-FRIED BEAN SPROUTS WITH TOFU
Categories: Side dish, Thai, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Tofu
4 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Garlic, finely chopped
1 lb Bean sprouts
5 ea Green onions, cut into 1 1/2
-- inch lengths
1 pn Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
2 tb Soy sauce
Drain the tofu & cube.  Heat oil in a wok over medium heat & stir-fry
the garlic for a few minutes, until it turns golden. Set aside. Add
the tofu, stir-fry for 5 minutes. Add the bean sprouts, onions, salt,
pepper & soy sauce.  Add the reserved garlic.  Stir-fry for no more
than 1 minute. Sprinkle with another dash of pepper & serve hot.
