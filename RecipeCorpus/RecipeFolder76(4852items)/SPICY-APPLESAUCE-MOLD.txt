Title: SPICY APPLESAUCE MOLD
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 1 Batch
1/2 c  Apple juice
1 tb Unflavored gelatin
-(Equiv = 1 envelope)
3 tb Red cinnamon candy
2 c  Applesauce
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Lemon juice
Sprinkle gelatin over apple juice in small saucepan; let stand a few
minutes.  Heat, stirring, until gelatin is completely dissolved.
Remove from heat and stir in candies, stirring until dissolved. Stir
in remaining ingredients.  Turn into small mold and chill until firm.
