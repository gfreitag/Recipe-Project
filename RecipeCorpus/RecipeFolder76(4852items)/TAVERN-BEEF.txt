Title: TAVERN BEEF
Categories: Crockpot, Beef, Sandwiches
Yield: 8 Servings
-CRANE ALDEN
2 1/2 lb Ground beef;lean
1 lg Onion;peeled and quartered
1 c  Ketchup
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Beef broth;or water
8    Hamburger buns;toasted
In a skillet,brown and crumble ground beef; drain well. Place beef and
remaining ingredients except hamburger rolls in a crockpot. Cover and
cook on LOW 6 to 12 hours. Taste for seasoning before serving and
correct if necessary.  Spoon over toasted hamburger buns.
