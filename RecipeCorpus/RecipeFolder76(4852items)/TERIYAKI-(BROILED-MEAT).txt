Title: TERIYAKI (BROILED MEAT)
Categories: Sauce/gravy, Beef/veal, Japan
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Sugar
7 tb Shoyu (soy sauce)
1/4 ts MSG; opt'l
2 ts Ginger root; grated
1/4 ts Garlic
1 lb Steak; cut thin
-(4" width - 4" - 6" length)
:

Make the shoyu sauce with grated ginger root, garlic, sugar and
shoyu. Add meat and let stand for 2 hours or more before broiling.
Note: May add more seasoning to suit your taste.
