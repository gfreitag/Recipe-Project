Title: TANGY GINGER DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dressings
Yield: 1 Cup
1/4 c  Chopped peeled fresh ginger
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Water
3 tb White vinegar
3 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Lemon peel, finely chopped
2 tb Tomato paste
1/2 ts Sugar
Combine all ingredients in blender. Blend on high until smooth, about
2 minutes. Season dressing to taste with salt and pepper.
