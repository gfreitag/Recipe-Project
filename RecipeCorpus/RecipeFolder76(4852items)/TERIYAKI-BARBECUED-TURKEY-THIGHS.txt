Title: TERIYAKI BARBECUED TURKEY THIGHS
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Turkey thighs
-- (each about 3/4 pound)
3/4 c  Kikkoman Teriyaki Sauce
1/4 c  Unsweetened pineapple juice
Bone turkey thighs, leaving skins intact; place in large plastic bag.
Combine remaining ingredients; pour over turkey. Press air out of
bag, tie top securely. Refrigerate overnight; turn bag over
occasionally. Place turkey on grill about 6 inches from moderate-hot
coals. Cook 45 minutes, or until meat thermometer inserted into
thickest part registers 180 F. Turn pieces over frequently.

Makes 4 to 6 servings.
