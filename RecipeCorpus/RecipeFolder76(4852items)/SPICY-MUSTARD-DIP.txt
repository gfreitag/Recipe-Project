Title: SPICY MUSTARD DIP
Categories: Cajun, Dips
Yield: 2 Cups
1 pt Sour cream
2 tb Dijon style mustard
1 ts Salt
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Onion salt
3/4 ts Hot sauce
1 ts Dried parsley
Combine sour cream, mustard, salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, onion
powder, hot sauce and parsley in a medium-sized mixing bowl. Mix
well. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours.  Serve with vegetable
crudite,

Prep. time: 2 1/2 hours
