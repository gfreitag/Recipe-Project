Title: TASSO HOLLANDAISE SAUCE
Categories: New, Text, Import 	ess, Of, Emeril
2    egg yolks
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ts fresh lemon juice
1/4 ts salt
1/8 ts Tabasco sauce
2 ts water
1    stick (1/4 pound) butter, --
melted warm
About 1/4 c  finely chopped
-tasso or spiced ham
:
:
:

In a stainless steel bowl set over a pot of simmering water over
medium heat, whisk the egg yolks with the lemon juice, salt, Tabasco,
and water until pale yellow and slightly thick. Be careful not to let
the bowl touch the water.

Remove the bowl from the pot whisking vigorously, add the butter, 1
teaspoon at a time, until all is incorporated. Add the tasso and
continue whisking for
30    seconds. Serve immediately.

Yield: 3/4 cu
