Title: SUCCOTASH (FROM MY GARDEN)
Categories: Wasnone
1 TB butter
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  heavy cream
3 c  corn kernels
Salt and pepper
:
2 c  lima beans -- blanched
2    red peppers -- diced

In large pan heat butter. Add corn, lima beans and red peppers. Cook
for 3 minutes, or until peppers are soft. Add heavy cream, salt and
pepper. Serve immediately
