Title: TAO HOO YOD SAI (STUFFED TOFU WITH GROUND POR
Categories: Pork
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Tofu (14.2oz)
1/4 lb Pork, ground
3    Garlic cloves; crushed
2 tb Scallions; chopped fine
2 tb Coriander; chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper, white, ground
1 tb Cornstarch
1    Egg; beaten
2 c  Oil, vegetable; for frying
Cut tofu into 1" cubes, scoop center of each cube out, set aside to
outer shells. Mix scooped out tofu, pork, garlic, scallion,
coriander, salt, pepper, cornstarch and egg. Stuff back into tofu
shells. Heat oil in wok over medium heat, deep fry the stuffed tofu
until golden brown. Drain on paper towels. Serve with Nam Chim Priew
Waan (recipe).

GEnie - R.WEISSGERBE "Delightful Thai Cooking" p. 26
