Title: SWISS VEGETABLE MEDLEY (BERNIE ROBERTS)
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese
Yield: 6 Servings
16 oz Frozen veg combo
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/4 ts Pepper
4 oz Diced pimento
1 c  Shredded swiss cheese
1 cn Durkee fried onions
1/3 c  Sour cream
Thaw and drain frozen vegetables. In lg. bowl combine vegetables,
soup, 1/2 C cheese, sour cream, pepper, pimento and 1/2 can fried
onions. Pour into shallow 1 qt. casserole and bake covered 30 min. at
350Deg. Sprinkle remaining cheese and onions in diagonal rows across
top; bake uncovered 5 min. or until golden.
