Title: SPICED MOCHA MIX
Categories: Beverages, Gifts
Yield: 3 Cups
1 1/2 c  Non-dairy creamer
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Instant coffee granules
Or crystals
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
Direction;

In a large bowl or container, combine all ingredients; mix well store
mix in tightly covered container. To serve, spoon 3 Tablespoons mix
into mug. Add 1 cup boiling water, stir to blend. If desired garnish
with dollop of sour cream.

Create a gift, by filling 1/2 pint jar.Add an attractive bow, and
attach label with directions.
