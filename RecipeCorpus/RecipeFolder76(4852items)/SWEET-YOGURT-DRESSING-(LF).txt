Title: SWEET YOGURT DRESSING (LF)
Categories: Low fat, Dressings, Salads
1 c  Plain, nonfat yogurt
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Raisins -- (golden are
nice)
:
1/4 c  Chopped nuts; (walnuts --
pecans, or other)
:
1 1/2 TB Honey

Mix all ingredients, and chill overnight.  Serve cold. Makes about 1.5
cups. Author's Note: Fresh chopped fruit becomes a special salad or a
light dessert with this dressing.
