Title: STRAWBERRY LOAF
Categories: Breads, Cakes
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Margarine
1 1/2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
3 c  Flour
3/4 ts Cream of tartar
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Strawberry jam
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 ts Vanilla & 1 ts lemon juice
1 ts Grated orange peel
Cream margarine and sugar, add the eggs one at a time beating well.
add jam and sour cream alternately with the dry ingredients, add
vanilla and lemon juice and orange peel. Pour into two greased loaf
pans and bake at 350 for about 70 min.
