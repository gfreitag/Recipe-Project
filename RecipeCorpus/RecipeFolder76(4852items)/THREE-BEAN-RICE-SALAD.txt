Title: THREE BEAN RICE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
16 oz Cut wax beans, drained
16 oz French cut green beans,
-drained
8 3/4 oz Red kidney beans, drained
1/2 c  Prepared Italian salad
-dressing
1/4 c  Thinly sliced onion rings
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 1/2 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Cooked rice
Mix beans, salad dressing, onion, salt and pepper in a large bowl. Set
aside to allow flavors to blend. Fold rice into bean mixture. Cover
and chill thoroughly. Serve on a bed of lettuce.
