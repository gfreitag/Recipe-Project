Title: SWORDFISH BROCHETTES
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 tb Olive oil
2    Garlic cloves,finely chopped
1 tb Rosemary,chopped fresh
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1    Summer yellow squash,1/2" th
1    Sweet green pepper,1" pieces
8    Cherry tomatoes
1 lb Swordfish,cut into 1" cubes
1. Combine lemon juice, oil, garlic, rosemary, salt, pepper in
medium-size bowl. Add squash, pepper, tomatoes, fish. Refrigerate,
covered, 30 minutes.
2. Preheat broiler. Thread vegetables and fish, alternating pieces,
on four 10" metal skewers.
3. Broil 6" from heat, turning once, 8 minutes until cooked through.
