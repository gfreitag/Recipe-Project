Title: SWEET SALAD DRESSING MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Instant Minced Onion
1 ts Dry Mustard
1 ts Celery Seed
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and blend well. Put mixture
in a foil packet or 1-pint glass jar and label as Sweet Salad
Dressing Mix. Store in a cool, dry place and use within 6 months.
Makes about 1/2 cup of mix. Sweet Salad Dressing: Combine mix, 3/4
cup vegetable oil, and 1/4 cup vinegar in a glass jar. Stir until
well blended; chill. Makes about 1 1/4 cups of dressing.
