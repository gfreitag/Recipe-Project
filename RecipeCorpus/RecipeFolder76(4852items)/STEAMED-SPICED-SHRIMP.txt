Title: STEAMED SPICED SHRIMP
Categories: Shrimp, Appetizers
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 tb Or more of seafood seasoning
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Water
1 lb Shrimp
1) Place vinegar; seafood seasoning to taste, salt, and water in
saucepan; bring to a boil.

2) Add shrimp to saucepan; toss gently. Cover tightly and steam over
high heat until shrimp are tender., about 2-5 minutes depending on
the size of the shrimp.

3) Drain shrimp. Remove shells and devein. Serve hot or cold.
