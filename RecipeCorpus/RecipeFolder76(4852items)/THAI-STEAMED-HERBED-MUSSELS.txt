Title: THAI STEAMED HERBED MUSSELS
Categories: Seafood, Thai
Yield: 1 Servings
2 kg Mussels
1 c  Basil leaves
4    Green onions
-----dipping sauce-----
1/2 c  Lemon juice
Lemon grass; optional
2 tb Fish sauce
1 ts Sugar
2    Coriander leaves; chopped
3    Cl Garlic
1 ts Red pepper flakes
Recipe by: "Michael E. Kinneer" <mkinner@LEX.ULTRANET.COM> Open the
mussels by steaming them along with the basil and green onions.

Blend the ingredients for the sauce with enough warm water to make a
dippin sauce and serve with the mussels.
