Title: SWEET MILK CAKES
Categories: Breads, Cakes
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Pancake or ready-mixed flour
1 tb Sugar
1 c  Milk
2 tb Melted shortening
1    Egg, well beaten
Sift prepared flour, measure, and sift with sugar. Combine egg, milk,
and shortening.  Add dry ingredients and beat until smooth. Bake on
hot griddle.  16 servings. The Household Searchlight - 1941
