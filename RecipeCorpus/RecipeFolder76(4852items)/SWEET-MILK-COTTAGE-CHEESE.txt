Title: SWEET MILK COTTAGE CHEESE
Categories:
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM-------------------HOUSEHOLD SEARCHLIGHT------------------------
2 qt Milk
2    Rennet tablets
2 tb Cold water
Cream
Salt
Pepper, black
Paprika
Heat milk to lukewarm.  Dissolve rennet tablets in cold water. Add to
milk. Let stand in warm place until set. Pour into thin cloth bag.
Drain. Remove curd from bag. Break into fine pieces. Moisten with
cream. Season to taste. The Household Searchlight
