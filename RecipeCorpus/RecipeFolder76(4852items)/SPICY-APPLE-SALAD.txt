Title: SPICY APPLE SALAD
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 8 Servings
2 pk Lemon jello (small)
1 cn Applesauce
1/2 c  Cinnamon hearts
1 pk Cream cheese(small),softened
3 tb Canned milk
2 tb Lemon juice
Place candies in 2 cups boiling water, boil until candies melt.
Dissolve jello in boiling water.  Add applesauce and lemon juice and
1 cup cold water. Chill until set. Mix cream cheese and milk. Spread
on top of jello. Cut into squares.  Serves 8-10.

C.C.--Lake Charles, La.
