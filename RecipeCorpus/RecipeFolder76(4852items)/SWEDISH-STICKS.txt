Title: SWEDISH STICKS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg
2 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Jam or jelly
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Coconut
1 tb Confectioner's sugar
Cream the shortening and brown sugar.  Add the egg flour and vanilla.
Pat evenly into cookie sheet (moisten fingertips to prevent
sticking). Bake 12-15 minutes at 350F. Immediately upon removing from
the oven, spread with the jam.  Sprinkle with the nuts and coconut
then dust with 1 tablepoon confectioner's sugar.  Cut into 1"x2 1/2"
strips. From Kris Bumford

: Concord Hospital Admitting Cookbook, Concord, NH
