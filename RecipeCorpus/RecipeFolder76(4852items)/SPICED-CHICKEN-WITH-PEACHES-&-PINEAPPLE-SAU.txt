Title: SPICED CHICKEN WITH PEACHES & PINEAPPLE SAU
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Chicken breast halves; with
8 oz Crushed pineapple
1 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Almonds; sliced
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cloves
16 oz Peaches; drained and pureed
Salt and pepper
In a large frying pan, combine chicken, pineapple, orange juice,
raisins, almonds, cinnamon, and cloves. Simmer, partly covered, 45
minutes, turning chicken occasionally. Add peach puree to pan and
stir until well blended. Simmer 15 minutes longer, until chicken is
tender and sauce is slightly thickened.  Season with salt and pepper
to taste.
