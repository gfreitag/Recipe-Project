Title: STRAWBERRY PUNCH 2
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 32 Servings
3 cn Orange juice concentrate;
- 6 oz. each, thawed
3 cn Pink lemonade concentrate;
- 6 oz. each, thawed
1 qt Ginger ale; chilled
2 pk Strawberries; frozen w/syrup
- 10 oz. each
Add cold water to fruit juice concentrate as directed on cans; stir in
ginger ale and frozen strawberries. Add ice cubes.
