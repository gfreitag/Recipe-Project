Title: GLEN'S FISH SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Catsup
1 tb A-1  Steak Sauce
3 tb Chili sauce
3 ds Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Lemon juice
Mix well and use for barbecuing fish.
