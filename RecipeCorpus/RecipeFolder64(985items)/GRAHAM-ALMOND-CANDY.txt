Title: GRAHAM-ALMOND CANDY
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
12    Double graham crackers
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1    2 1/2 oz pk sliced almonds
Line jelly roll pan with foil.  Place single layer of graham crackers
on foil.  Boil butter and sugar together for 2 minutes, stirring
constantly. Pour over graham crackers. Sprinkle with almonds. Bake at
350 degrees for 10 minutes.  Lift foil from pan, cool, and break into
pieces.
