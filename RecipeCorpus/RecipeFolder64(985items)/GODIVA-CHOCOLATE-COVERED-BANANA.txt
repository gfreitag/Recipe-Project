Title: GODIVA CHOCOLATE COVERED BANANA
Categories: Godiva, Alcohol, Beverages, New (to be
Yield: 1 Servings
2 oz Godiva Liqueur
1/2 oz Dark Rum -- (Myers's)
1/2    Banana -- Sliced
1/2 c  Vanilla Ice Cream
Pour Godiva into blender. Add rum, banana and ice cream. Blend until
smooth. Pour into serving glass. Garnish with banana slice.

Makes 2 drinks.
