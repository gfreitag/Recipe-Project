Title: GOOSEBERRY JELLY
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
3 lb Green gooseberries
Sugar
Wash gooseberries.  Remove stems and blossom ends. Cover with water.
Cook slowly until soft.  Drain through jelly bag. Combine sugar and
juice in equal proportions. Boil rapidly until jelly sheets from
spoon. The Household Searchlight
