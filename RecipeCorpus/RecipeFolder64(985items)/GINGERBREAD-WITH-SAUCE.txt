Title: GINGERBREAD WITH SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 14 Servings
1/3 c  Butter Buds. -- liquid, or
1/2    C
Promise UFF margarin
1/2 c  Molasses
1 ts Ginger
2 ts Allspice
2 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Nutmeg
2 ts Baking Soda -- dissolved in
1    C
Boiling water
1/2 c  Sugar -- or 1/4 C sugar +
3    Pkts Sweet 'N Low
2 1/2 c  Flour
2    Egg Beaters. 99% Egg
Substitute -- 1/2 C
Optional Vanilla Sauce Or Caramel Sauce Recipes To Follow.

Cream Butter Buds and sugar.  Add remaining ingred.; add Egg Beaters
last. Bake in a bundt or tube pan that has been sprayed with a
non-fat cooking spray.  Bake 35 - 40 min. at 350 deg. F. Serve warm
with the sauce of your choice.

This cake is very good!
