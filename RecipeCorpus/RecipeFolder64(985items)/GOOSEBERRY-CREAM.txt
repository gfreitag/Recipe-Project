Title: GOOSEBERRY CREAM
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 pt Gooseberries, stemmed
1 tb Butter
1/4 c  Sugar
4    Egg yolks
Boil the gooseberries very quickly in just enough water to cover
them. Stir in the butter when the gooseberries become soft, then
press them through a sieve. While the pulp is hot, sweeten it with
sugar to taste and then beat in the egg yolks. Serve in cups or
glasses.

Source:  Time-Life "Good Cook" series: Fruits
