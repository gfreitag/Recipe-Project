Title: GINGER BEER (WAGNER)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
6 oz Bruised ginger
3 qt Water
5 lb Loaf sugar
1/4 lb Honey
Gill of lemon juice
17 qt Water
2    Drachms essence of lemon
(about 2 ts)
1    Whole egg
Put ginger and 3 quarts water into a very large kettle and boil for 30
minutes. Add sugar, honey, lemon juice and 17 quarts more water.
Strain through a cloth and when it is cold, add essence of lemon and
egg. Let stand for 3-4 days before bottling.
