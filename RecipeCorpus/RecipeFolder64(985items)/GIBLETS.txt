Title: GIBLETS
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
Giblets
1/2 ts Salt
2    Peppercorns
2    Cloves
1 sm Bay leaf
Onion - small amount
Cover gizzard, heart and neck with water; sprinkle with other
ingredients. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer uncovered until
gizzard is fork-tender. Liver is very tender and can be fried,
broiled or simmered in water 5 to 10 minutes.

Giblet broth can be used in stuffing, gravy and recipes where the
chicken broth is specified. Cooked giblets can be cut up and added to
gravy or stuffing.

From: Betty Crocker's Cookbook
