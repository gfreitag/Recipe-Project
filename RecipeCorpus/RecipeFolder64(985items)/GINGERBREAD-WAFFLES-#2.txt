Title: GINGERBREAD WAFFLES #2
Categories: Breakfast, Bread
Yield: 4 Servings
2/3 c  Molasses
1/3 c  Butter; melted
2    Egg yolks
1 c  Buttermilk
2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Crystalized ginger; chopped
2    Egg whites; beaten stiff
Combine the molasses, butter and egg yolks and whisk with fork until
smooth.  Blend in the buttermilk.

Sift together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, ginger,
nutmeg and salt into a mixing bowl. Stir in the crystalized ginger.
Make a well in the center of the dry mixture and add the molasses
mixture, beating quickly until smooth.  Fold in the egg whites. If
the batter seems too thick, add a little more buttermilk. Bake in
your waffle iron until a dark golden brown.  Makes 4 big, thick
waffles.
