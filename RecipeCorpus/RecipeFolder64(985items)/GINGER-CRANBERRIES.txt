Title: GINGER CRANBERRIES
Categories: Relishes, Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Cranberries
1    Orange; quartered
1 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Honey
3/4 c  Sugar, white
1 1/2 ts Ginger
Grind oranges and cranberries with the coarsest blade of a sausage
grinder. Mix with all other ingredients. Chill at least 2 hours
before serving.
