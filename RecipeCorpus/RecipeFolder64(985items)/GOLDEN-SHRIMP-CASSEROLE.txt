Title: GOLDEN SHRIMP CASSEROLE
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
5 sl Bread; cut in 1/2" cubes
1/4 c  Butter (or marg.); melted
1/2 c  Mushrooms; sliced
2 c  Shrimp; peeld,cookd,deveined
2 c  American cheese; shredded
3    Eggs; beaten
2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
ds Black pepper
ds Paprika
Brown bread cubes in butter; remove from skillet and set aside. Cook
mushrooms in butter for about 10 minutes. In a buttered 1-1/2quart
casserole dish, alternate layers of bread cubes, mushrooms, shrimp,
and cheese.

Combine eggs, milk, and seasonings; pour over layers. Place casserole
dish in a pan of hot water, and bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour
and 15 minutes.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy
Coleman.
