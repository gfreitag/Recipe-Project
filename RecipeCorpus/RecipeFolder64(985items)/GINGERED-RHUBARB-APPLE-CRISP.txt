Title: GINGERED RHUBARB APPLE CRISP
Categories: Desert
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Diced rhubarb
3 lg Apples,peeled, cored and
Sliced
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Minced crystallized ginger
1 tb Flour
Topping-----
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Brown sugar
4 tb Butter, cold, cut into
Chunks
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 c  Oatmeal
Place rhubarb, apples, 1 cup sugar, crystallized ginger, and 2
tablespoons flour in a large bowl and toss well. Turn into a greased
9" pie pan. Combine topping ingredients in a bowl and mix with a
pastery blender until crumbly.  Spread crumb mixture over rhubarb and
apples and bake at 350F for 45-50 minutes, until fruit is tender and
topping is browned. Serve warm...
