Title: GINGERBREAD & APPLESAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Whole-wheat flour
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
2 ts Double-acting baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Cinnamon
1/3 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1/2 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Hot water
2 c  Unsweetened applesauce
2 tb Brown sugar
3 tb Candied ginger root --
Finely chopped
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Sift together flour, soda, baking powder, salt, ginger, cloves, and
cinnamon. Cream butter and white sugar. Beat in egg and molasses.
Stir in dry ingredients, alternating with water. Place applesauce in
a buttered Dutch oven or other large, heavy casserole dish. Mix brown
sugar, crystallized ginger, and 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon. Sprinkle over
applesauce. Pour gingerbread batter over all. Bake at 350 degrees for
35 minutes, or until gingerbread is done. Serve at once.
