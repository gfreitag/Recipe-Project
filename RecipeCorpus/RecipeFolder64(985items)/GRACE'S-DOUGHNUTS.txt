Title: GRACE'S DOUGHNUTS
Categories: Doughnuts
2    eggs--beaten
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  sugar
2 1/2 ts baking powder
1/4 ts salt
1 ts HOT melted butter
1/2 ts nutmeg
1/2 ts ginger
1/2 c  milk
1 1/2 c  all-purpose flour

Beat eggs until thick and lemon colored.  Add rest of ingredients. On
a bread board dusted with flour and a little ginger, roll out 1/2
inch thick and cut with doughnut cutter. Let stand for 1 hour covered
with a lines towel. Deep fry in fat at 400 degrees.. Remove from fat,
shake off excess fat and shake in a paper bag full of cinnamon and
granulated sugar or confectioners sugar.
