Title: GINGERED BEET SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
6 md Beets; scrubbed
1 ts Olive oil
1 1/2 ts Fresh ginger, minced
2 tb Mellow miso
1 tb Sesame seeds
1 tb Lemon juice
Boil beets in a large, heavy saucepan until tender. Remove cooked
beets to a bowl of cold water.  When cool enough to handle, slip off
skins and discard. Slice or chop beets. In a small skillet or
saucepan over medium heat, cook ginger and sesame seeds in oil,
stirring constantly, for 1 minute.  Remove pan from heat and stir in
miso and 1/4 cup beet cooking liquid. Cool dressing mixture slightly
before adding lemon juice. Toss dressing with beets until well coated.
