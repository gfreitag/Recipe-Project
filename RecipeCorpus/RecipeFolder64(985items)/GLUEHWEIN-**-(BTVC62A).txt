Title: GLUEHWEIN ** (BTVC62A)
Categories: Beverages, German, Liqueur
Yield: 8 Servings
32 oz Dry red wine
1/4 c  Orange juice
2 tb Lemon juice
1 x  Spiral orange peel
1 x  Stick cinnamon
4 ea Cloves
8 tb Sugar
1/3 c  Rum
Combine wine, juice, peel, cinnamon, cloves and 4 Tbl sugar in
saucepan. Simmer 8-10 minutes; strain into punch bowl. Heat rum
slightly. Place remaining sugar in large metal spoon or ladle. Light
rum with a match; pour over sugar. Pour burning sugar over punch. Be
careful and ENJOY!!! FROM: RITA TAULE   (BTVC62A)
