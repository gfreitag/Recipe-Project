Title: GOLDEN GATE PUNCH
Categories: Sucaryl
Yield: 14 Per 125ml
250 ml Lemon juice
250 ml Orange juice
250 ml Grape juice
60 ml Liquid sucaryl
1 l  Water
Combine all ingredients and chill well.Serve over ice cubes,crushed
ice,or a ring mold of ice.Garnish with lemon or orange slices,as
desired. Makes 14 servings(approx.125ml each).

per 125ml serving: 24 calories, trace protein, 0 fat, 6.5 g
carbohydrate. note: (with sugar approx. 86 calories
