Title: GINGER STUFFED EGGPLANT
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
6 md Eggplants
2 ea Onions, chopped
Oil
Tamari
1/4 c  Grated ginger
9 ea Celery stalks, sliced
3 ea Red peppers, sliced
6 tb Peanut butter
1 ts Curry powder
1 ds Cayenne
1 ds Nutmeg
Cut eggplants in half lengthways.  Hollow out shells & set aside. Cube
eggplant pulp.  Saute onions & eggplant cubes in oil till tender. Add
a tb or so of tamari to taste.

In a large bowl, mix eggplant mixture, ginger, celery, peppers, peanut
butter, cayenne & nutmeg.  Place in eggplant shells & bake at 350F
for 45 minutes.

Joel Rapp, "Mother Earth's Vegetarian Feasts"
