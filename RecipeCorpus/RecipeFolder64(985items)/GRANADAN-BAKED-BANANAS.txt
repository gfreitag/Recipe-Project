Title: GRANADAN BAKED BANANAS
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Fresh orange juice
1/2 c  Dark rum
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Vanilla extract
4    Ripe bananas, peeled &
-sliced lengthwise
Preheat oven to 350 degrees (F).  Grease well a 9 inch ovenproof pan.
Put the brown sugar in a heavy saucepan. Heat carefully until it
liquifies. Add the orange juice, rum, cinnamon, nutmeg and vanilla.
Boil for a few seconds.  Then, pour over the bananas. Place in the
oven and bake for about 30 minutes. ---
