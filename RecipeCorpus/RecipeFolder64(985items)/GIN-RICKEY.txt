Title: GIN RICKEY
Categories: Mixed drink, Gin, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 oz Gin
1 tb Fresh lime juice -- (juice
1/2 lime)
6 oz Club soda -- (or to fill)
Fill a highball glass with ice. Add gin and lime juice. Fill with
club soda and stir. Garnish with a wedge of lime.
