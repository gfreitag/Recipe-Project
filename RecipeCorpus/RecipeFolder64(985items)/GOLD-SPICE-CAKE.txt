Title: GOLD SPICE CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Allspice
2/3 c  Chopped raisins
2/3 c  Chopped nuts
To the recipe for gold cake add:

Sift spices with flour, baking powder, and salt. Dredge raisins in 2
tablespoons of the flour.  Add raisins and nuts. Pour into well-oiled
loaf pan. Bake in moderate oven (375 F) about 40 minutes. The
