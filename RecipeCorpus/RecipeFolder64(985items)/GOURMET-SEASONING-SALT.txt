Title: GOURMET SEASONING SALT
Categories: Condiments, Harned 1994, Herb/spice, Seasonings
Yield: 114 Servings
1 c  Salt
2 ts Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Dried whole oregano
1 ts Ground marjoram
1 ts Dried whole thyme
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Celery seeds
1/4 ts Dried whole dillweed
-- (opt'l., I included)
Combine all ingredients; mix well.  Store in an airtight container.

From Inez Thornburg of Long Beach, MS in "Southern Living 1982 Annual
Recipes," December 1982, pg. 297. Tried 10/22/89. Posted by Cathy
Harned.
