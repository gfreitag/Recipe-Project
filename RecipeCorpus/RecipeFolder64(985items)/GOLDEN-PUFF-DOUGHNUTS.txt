Title: GOLDEN PUFF DOUGHNUTS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Recipe
2 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 c  Granulated white sugar
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 ts Nutmeg or mace
1/4 c  Wesson or Crisco liquid
-oil
3/4 c  Milk
1    Egg
Sugar & Cinnamon Mixture Sift together dry ingredients. Then stir
all ingredients together with a fork until thoroughly mixed. Drop by
teaspoonsful (small size) into deep hot oil or fat fryer at (375
degrees). Fry until golden brown (about 3 minutes). Drain on paper
towels and cooling rack. Roll warm puffs in cinnamon-sugar or glaze
puffs by dipping into a thin confectioner's sugar icing. DO NOT cook
too many at one time, as fat will cool down and puffs will absorb
grease and remain doughy.
