Title: GOLDEN MUSHROOM CHICKEN THIGHS
Categories: None
Yield: 3 Servings
6    Chicken thighs
1 cn Golden mushroom soup
Pull the skin off the thighs. (Not as hard as it sounds, it usually
just peels off.) Rinse in cold water. Put in crockpot. Pour in one
can of golden mushroom soup and 1 can water. Cook on high at least 4
hours or until chicken falls off bones. Remove bones.
