Title: GOLD LAYER CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts -Salt
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
3    Egg yolks
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 375F. Have  all  ingredients  at room temp. Sift
flour & resift  with baking  powder & salt. Add butter & cream till
soft. Add sugar gradually & cream till light.  Beat in egg yolks. Add
vanilla or lemon flavouring.  Add  flour mixture to butter migxture
in 3 parts, alternating with  milk. Stir batter smooth after each
addition. Bake in greased layer pans about 25 min. Spread LEMON
FILLING between layers when cool. Frost MAKES: 2 8" ROUND CAKES
Source: _Joy of Cooking_
