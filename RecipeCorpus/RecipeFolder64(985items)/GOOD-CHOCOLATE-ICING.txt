Title: GOOD CHOCOLATE ICING
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Stick margarine
4 tb Cocoa
1/3 c  Buttermilk
1 lb Confectioner's sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Vanilla
Mix margarine, cocoa, and milk in a sauce pan and bring to a boil.
Remove, stir in sugar, salt and vanilla. (1 cup chopped nuts may be
added) From Pat Crafts
