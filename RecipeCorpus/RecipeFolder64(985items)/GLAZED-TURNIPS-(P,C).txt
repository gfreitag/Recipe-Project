Title: GLAZED TURNIPS (P/C)
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Pressure co
Yield: 4 Servings
-MARY WILSON  BWVB02B
1 md Yellow turnips; cubed
1    Dozen dried apricots
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Butter or margarine
Presoak apricots 1 hour.  Place turnips cubes in cooker. Pressure
cook at 15 lbs. pressure for 3 to 5 minutes. Reduce pressure with
cool water. Add sugar and butter and heat over low flames until
glazed. Serves 4.
