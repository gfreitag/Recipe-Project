Title: GIANT'S HOLIDAY BAKE
Categories: Vegetables, Brand name, Christmas
Yield: 5 Servings
1 lb Green Giant American Mixture
-frozen broccoli, carrots &
-cauliflower
1 cn Cream of broccoli soup
1/2 c  Milk
1 ts Sauce sauce
2 13/16 oz French's French fried onions
In a 2 qt microwave safe casserole, combine vegetables, soup, milk,
soy sauce, and 1/2 can of onions. Microwave on HIGH for 10 to 12
minutes (or bake in 375 oven for 30 to 35 minutes) until vegetables
are hot, stirring occasionally. Top with remaining onions. 5 servings.
