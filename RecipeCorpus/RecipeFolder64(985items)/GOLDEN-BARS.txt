Title: GOLDEN BARS
Categories: Desserts, Snacks, Cookies
Yield: 16 Servings
2/3 c  Oil
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Egg beaters
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Nutmeats
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
ds Salt
Mix oil and dry ingredients and spread in an 8" square pan, sprayed
with non-stick cooking spray. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes at 350 deg f
or until surface will spring back when pressed lightly. Cool and then
cut in bars, 2" square. Makes 16 squares.
