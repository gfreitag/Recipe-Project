Title: GORGONZOLA CREAM SAUCE
Categories: Cheese, Pasta, Sauces & ma, Mc
2 c  milk
Yield: 6 Servings
1 TB flour
1 TB butter -- melted
3 TB gorgonzola cheese -- cru

Melt butter in saucepan.  Add flour and mix 'til smooth. Add milk,
stirring until thickened. Then add cheese, stirring until melted and
sauce is smooth.

~ - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

NOTES : Serve over pasta, Gorgonzola & Walnut Ravioli
