Title: GOLDEN FRUITCAKE
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Chopped dried apricots
2 c  Seeded raisins, chopped
2/3 c  Chopped candied cherries
2/3 c  Chopped candied orange peel
2/3 c  Chopped citron
1/2 c  Blanched almonds, shaved
1 ts Grated lemon rind
4 c  Sifted cake flour
1 1/2 c  Shortening
1 1/2 c  Sugar
8    Eggs
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
Combine fruits, nuts and rind with half of flour. Cream shortening and
sugar until fluffy.  Beat in eggs, one at a time. Combine remaining
flour, salt and baking powder and sift together 3 times. Add to
creamed mixture. Add fruit and nut mixture and blend well. Pour into
greased loaf pans lined with greased brown paper. Bake in a 300
degree over for 1 1/2 hours. Makes 4 loaves (7x3x2 1/2)
