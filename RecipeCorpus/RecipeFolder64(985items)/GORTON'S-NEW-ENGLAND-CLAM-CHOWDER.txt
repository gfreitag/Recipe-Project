Title: GORTON'S NEW ENGLAND CLAM CHOWDER
Categories: Soups, American
Yield: 4 Servings
2 cn Gorton's clams
1 c  Pared diced potatoes
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 c  Milk
-Salt and pepper to taste
-Cooking Sherry (optional)
Drain clams.  Cook onion in butter.  Add potatoes and clam juice and
cook until tender.  Add clams and milk. Heat and season. Do not boil.

Yield: 4 servings.
