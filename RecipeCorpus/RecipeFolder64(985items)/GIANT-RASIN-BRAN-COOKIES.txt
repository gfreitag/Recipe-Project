Title: GIANT RASIN BRAN COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Kathy's
Yield: 18 Cookies
2 c  Raisin bran cereal, crushed
To 1 1/2 cups
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  All purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
3/4 c  Butter, softened
2/3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
2    Eggs
stir together rasin bran cereal, flours and soda; set aside. in large
mixing bowl, beat butter and sugars until light and fluffy. add eggs;
beat well. stir in cereal mixture, thoroughly combining. drop by
scant 1/4 cup measure onto ungreased cookie sheet. bake in 350
degrees oven about 14 min. or until lightly browned, recipe from
"Kellogg Company"
