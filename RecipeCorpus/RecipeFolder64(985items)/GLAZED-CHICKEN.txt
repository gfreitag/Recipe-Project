Title: GLAZED CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
4 tb Soy sauce; divided
2 tb Lemon juice; divided
1/4 ts Garlic powder
3/4 ts Ground ginger
4    Chicken breast halves withou
2 tb Honey
1 tb Water
Vegetable cooking spray
Combine 2 tablespons soy sauce, 1 tablespoon lemon juice and ginger;
pour over chicken, stirring to coat. Marinate for 30 minutes. Combine
remainin soy sauce, lemon juice, honey and water. In wok or large
skillet, brown chicken; add honey mixture.  Over medium heat, cook 5
minutes. Over high heat, cook uncovered an additional 5 minutes or
until chicken is glazed; stir occasionally.
