Title: GOLDEN GLAZED CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Onions, sliced
4    Bone-in chicken breasts
1 tb Curry powder
570 ml (1 jar) Uncle Ben's Oriental
Stir-Fry Sauce
Spread onions in bottom of a lightly greased 13x9-inch (33x23 cm)
glass baking dish. Arrange chicken on top. Stir curry powder into the
jar of Oriental Sauce, and then pour sauce over chicken breasts.
Bake, uncovered at 375F (190C) for 40-45 minutes, or until chicken is
no longer pink inside. Baste 2 or 3 times during cooking.

Source: Uncle Ben's ad, Modern Woman magazine, July 1994, page 17
