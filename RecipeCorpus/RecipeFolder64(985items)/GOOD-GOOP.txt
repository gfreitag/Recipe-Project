Title: GOOD GOOP
Categories: Beef, Pasta
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Ground Beef -- * see note
1 cn Tomato Soup
1 cn Cream Of Chicken Soup
1 cn Bean Sprouts -- drained
1 cn Chow Mein Noodles -- ** see
Note
* (I now use ground turkey) browned with 1 chopped up onion ** (save
a few for the top)

Stir all this together and bake in a casserole or cake pan at 350 for
about an hour, or until bubbly.  Add a salad and some muffins and you
have an easy, fairy cheap meal
