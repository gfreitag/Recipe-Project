Title: GRAHAM CRACKER APPLESAUCE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Applesauce
24    Graham crackers
1/4 lb Butter, melted
1 c  Sugar
2 ts Cinnamon
2    Egg whites
Chopped nuts
A Great Favorite with Children:

Roll crackers into crumbs and combine with butter, cinnamon, sugar.
Put 1/2 of mixture into square cake pan. Spread with applesauce, and
then rest of crumb mixture. Spread with stiffly beaten egg whites and
sprinkle with chopped nuts. Bake 1 hour in slow oven. Serve with
plain cream or whipped cream sweetened and flavored. Serves 4.
