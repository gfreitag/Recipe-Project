Title: GRANDMA CLARK'S COOKIE PRESS SHORTBREAD
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H PEAGRAM---------------------------
1 c  Brown sugar, sifted
2 c  Butter
4 c  Flour
1 ts Salt (omit ?)
1/2 c  Cornstarch
1/2 c  Icing sugar
Directions: Mix well, put in cookie press and put cookies on ungreased
cookie sheet. Bake at 325 approx 20 minutes. Cookies should not brown
too much.
