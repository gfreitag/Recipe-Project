Title: GLORIO COOKIES
Categories: Cakes, Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
COOKIES-----
18 oz Devils food cake mix
2 tb Water
2 tb Cooking oil
1/4 c  Bitter cocoa powder
FILLING-----
1    Envelope unflavored gelatin
1/4 c  Cold water
1 c  Crisco
1 ts Vanilla
1 lb Powdered sugar +1 cup
mix up cookies take small balls roll them in your hands place in
cookie sheet bake 350 for 10 to 15 minutes let cool and put in
filling. this was on phil's show recipes by Gloria the copy cat lady
