Title: GOOBER BEANIE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
-Sue Woodward
MMMMM----------------AUNT BEE'S MAYBERRY COOKBOOK---------------------
1 cn 16-oz. red kidney beans
1 c  16-oz. small green peas
1 c  16-oz. yellow wax beans
1 cn 16-oz. green beans
1 lg Red onion; chopped
2 c  Red cabbage; shredded
7 oz Jar pimento
1/2 c  Oil
1 ts Salt
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Vinegar
Drain all of the beans and combine in a lg. bowl.  Add the remaining
ingredients.  Mix well and cover. Refrigerate for about 12 hours. Stir
occasionally. Drain before serving.

Authors - Ken Beck and Jim Clark
