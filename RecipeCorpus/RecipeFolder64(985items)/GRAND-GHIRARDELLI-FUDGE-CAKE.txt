Title: GRAND GHIRARDELLI FUDGE CAKE
Categories: Ghirardelli, Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
INGREDIENTS:
2 c  Unsifted cake flour
1 3/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Ghirardelli Cocoa
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Butter or margarine, very
-soft
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Water
2    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
DIRECTIONS: Heat oven to 350-F. Measure the 6 dry ingredients into
large mixer bowl. Mix on low 1 minute to combine. Add remaining
ingredients, all at once. Mix on medium 1 minute, scraping down bowl,
to blend mixture; beat on high for 3 minutes. Spread into 2 parchment
lined or greased and lightly floured 9 by 1-1/2" round cake pans.
Bake at 350-F for 30 to 35 minutes; cool 5 minutes. Remove layers and
cool on racks.

When completely cool, frost with Chocolate Butter Cream Frosting*

* separate recipe

Source: Recipes from Ghirardelli Chocolate Company of San Francisco

From: Sallie Austin
