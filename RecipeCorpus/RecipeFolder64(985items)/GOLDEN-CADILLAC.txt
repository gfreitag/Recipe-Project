Title: GOLDEN CADILLAC
Categories: Mixed, Drinks, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Galliano
1 oz White creme de cacao
2 oz Heavy cream
Cracked ice
Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add Galliano, creme de cacao,
and cream. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
