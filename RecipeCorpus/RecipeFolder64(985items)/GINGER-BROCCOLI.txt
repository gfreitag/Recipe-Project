Title: GINGER BROCCOLI
Categories: Vegetables, Vegetarian, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Broccoli
-- florets and stems sliced
-- into diagonal pieces
2 ts Roasted sesame oil
3 tb Tamari
2 ts Ground ginger
2 ts Brown sugar
4 tb Water
In a medium frying pan over medium heat, saute the broccoli in the
sesame oil for about 8 minutes. Combine the tamari, ginger, and
sugar, and add to the broccoli. Add water, cover, and simmer for 2
minutes.
