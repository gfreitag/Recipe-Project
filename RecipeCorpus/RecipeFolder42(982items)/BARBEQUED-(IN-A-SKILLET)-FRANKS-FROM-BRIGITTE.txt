Title: BARBEQUED (IN A SKILLET) FRANKS FROM BRIGITTE
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Ketsup
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Sugar
1 c  Water
1 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt
Dash of hot sauce
8    -10 franks
Mix all, except the franks, well and put in a skillet and bring to a
boil. Add franks and simmer 15-20 minutes. Tastes great and is fast.
