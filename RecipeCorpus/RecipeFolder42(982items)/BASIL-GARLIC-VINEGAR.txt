Title: BASIL GARLIC VINEGAR
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM--------------------LORI NORMAN RFHH52E-------------------------
1/2 c  Fresh basil leaves; coarsely
-chopped
2    Cloves garlic; peeled; split
Vinegar
Pint jar; sterilized
Place 1/2 cup coarsely chopped fresh basil leaves and 2 cloves split
peeled garlic in a sterilized pint jar. Heat wine or distilled WHITE
vinegar to just below the boiling point. Fill jar with vinegar, and
cap tightly. Allow to stand 3 to 4 weeks. Strain vinegar, discarding
basil and garlic. Pour vinegar into a clean sterilized jar, adding a
new sprig of fresh basil, if desired. Seal tightly.  Use in dressings
for rice, pasta, antipasto salads or in flavored mayonnaise. Source:
