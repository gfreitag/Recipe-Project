Title: BASIC WAFFLES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Milk; sweet
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Flour
1 tb Butter; melted - or substit
-ute
Beat yolks of eggs, add milk, salt, flour and baking powder mixed
together and shortening. Add stiffly beaten whites of eggs and bake
on waffle iron unto crisp and brown.

Source: Cora T. Riffle, Jerusalem Grange, Adams County, OH
