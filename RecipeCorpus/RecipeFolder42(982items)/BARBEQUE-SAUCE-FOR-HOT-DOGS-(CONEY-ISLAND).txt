Title: BARBEQUE SAUCE FOR HOT DOGS (CONEY ISLAND)
Categories: Bbq sauces, Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
4 ea Strips of bacon, finely
-chopped
1 c  Ground beef
1 ea Onion, finely chopped
1 ea Clove garlic, finely chopped
1 ts Barbeque spice
1 c  Tomato juice
1/2 c  Salad oil
1 ea Sprig of parsley, finely
-chopped
1/4 c  Findely diced celery
1 ts Chili powder
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 pn Oregano, pepper
Saute the bacon, onion, celery and garlic in the oil until golden
brown. Add other ingredients and simmer for about 1 hour.

Source:  Hibachi Barbecue by George E. Engler
