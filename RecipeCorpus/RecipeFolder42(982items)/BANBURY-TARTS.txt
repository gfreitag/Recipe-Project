Title: BANBURY TARTS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Shortening
1/2 c  Currants
1 1/2 tb Minced candied orange peel
1 1/2 tb Minced candied lemon peel
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1/8 ts Cinnamon
1/2    Egg, slightly beaten
2 tb Cookie or biscuit crumbs
1 1/2 tb Minced candied citron
Cream shortening and sugar.  Add candied peel, citron, currants,
crumbs, nutmeg, cinnamon, and egg.  Mix thoroughly. Cut plain pastry
in rounds 6 inches in diameter. Place 1 tablespoon of fruit mixture
on 1/2 of each round.  Moisten edges.  Fold like a turnover. Press
edges together. Crimp. Brush with slightly beaten egg, or water.
Sprinkle with sugar. Bake in hot oven (450 F) 10 minutes, or until
brown.

Florence Taft Eaton, Concord, MA.
