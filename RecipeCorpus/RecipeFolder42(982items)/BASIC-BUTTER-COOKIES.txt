Title: BASIC BUTTER COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Cornstarch
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
3/4 c  Butter; room temperature
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
Recipe by: Cooking With Bon Appetit - Cookies Preheat oven to 300F.
Sift the first 3 ingredients into a large bowl. Add butter and mix
well. Stir in walnuts.  Drop by teaspoons onto a baking sheet.  Bake
until cookies are lightly golden, 20 to 25 minutes. Cool on racks.

Makes about 30 cookies.
