Title: BASICS: RICE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
1 1/3 c  Water
pn Salt
2/3 c  Rice or brown rice
Basics for new cooks:

in saucepan, bring water and salt to boil; stir in rice. cover and
reduce heat to low; simmer white rice for 20 minutes, brown rice for
40 minutes, or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed. Makes 2
cups, or 2 servings.

Source: Canadian Living magazine, Apr 95 Presented in article by
Elizabeth Baird: "Only The Best" Recipe by Canadian Living Test
