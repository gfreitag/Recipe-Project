Title: BANANA CUPCAKES-MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Bake it all mix
1    Egg
1/3 c  Granulated sugar
1/3 c  Milk
1    Banana -- peel/slice
2 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
2 tb Brown sugar -- packed
Break egg into bowl, beat and stir in sugar and milk. Add Mix
gradually. Stir to combine thoroughly.  Rub muffin cups well with
shortening or use paper liners in muffin pan. Drop about half the
batter by spoonfuls into muffin cups. Place 2 or 3 slices of banana
on top of the batter and pour melted butter over the fruit. Sprinkle
brown sugar over the banana slices in each cup. Drop remaining batter
by spoonfuls into muffin cups. Bake in a 400 F. oven about 15
mintutes. Yield: 12 large individual coffee cakes
