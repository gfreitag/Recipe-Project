Title: BANANA ORANGE OATMEAL
Categories: Breakfast, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Orange juice
1    Banana, chopped
1 ts Grated orange rind (opt.)
Pinch salt
1 1/3 c  Quick-cooking rolled oats
Pinch grated nutmeg
In saucepan, bring orange juice, banana, orange rind (if using) and
salt to boil; gradually stir in rolled oats. Cook, stirring, for 1
minute; remove from heat.

Stir in nutmeg; cover and let stand for 5 minutes. Makes 4 servings.
Variation: Cinnamon Apple Oatmeal: Substitute 3 cups apple juice for
orange juice, rind and banana. Substitute 1/4 tsp. cinnamon for
nutmeg. Serve sprinkled with raisins, if desired.  Typed in MMFormat
