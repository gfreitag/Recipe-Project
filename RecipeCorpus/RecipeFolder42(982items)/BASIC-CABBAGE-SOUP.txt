Title: BASIC CABBAGE SOUP
Categories: Digest, Mar95
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ea Cabbage, chopped
1 ea Carrots, chunked
1 ea Onions, chunked
1 ea Potatoes, chunked
1 ea With salt, lots of pepper,
Maybe some dill and garlic.
Simmer or nuke til it is done to your liking.  I would question the
"magical" claims, though.
