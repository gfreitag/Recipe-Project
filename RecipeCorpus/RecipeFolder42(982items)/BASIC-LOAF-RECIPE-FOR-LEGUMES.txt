Title: BASIC LOAF RECIPE FOR LEGUMES
Categories: Vegetarian, Easy, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Legumes; cooked
1 c  Bread crumbs, wheat germ or
-ground nuts
1 c  Liquid--soymilk, tomato
-juice, etc.
Seasonings to taste--garlic,
-onions, etc
Mix all ingredients thoroughly. Bake in loaf pan in 350 degree oven
until firm, about 30 minutes.
