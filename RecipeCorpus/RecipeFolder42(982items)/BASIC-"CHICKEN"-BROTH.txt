Title: BASIC "CHICKEN" BROTH
Categories: Soups, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Water, boiling
3 tb Corn oil
2 tb Nutritional yeast (or more)
1 ts Poultry seasoning  (or more)
1/2 c  Mushrooms; chopped
1/2 c  Onions; finely chopped
1 tb Vegetable broth powder or
--bouillon
Onion salt to taste
Stir ingredients together for an instant broth. Use in recipes
calling for chicken stock, and as a basis for chickenless noodle or
rice soup.

For a basic "chicken" gravy, use the above recipe, stirring in flour
at medium to low heat until desired thickness is reached. Delicious
over mashed potatoes, stuffing and rice.

Source:  _Instead of Chicken, Instead of Turkey_,
