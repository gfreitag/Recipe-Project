Title: BARLEY WITH VEGETABLES (COOKED IN A RICE COOKER)
Categories: Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Barley
2 c  Broth of your choice
1 lg Onion, chopped
2    Ripe tomatoes, diced
8 oz Can mushrooms, drained
1 tb Vegan worstershire sauce
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Dried parsley
x  Pepper
Put all in rice cooker and stir.  Put on cover and turn to COOK. Will
be ready in about 45 minutes.  Can probably do this on the stove as
well. Just bring to a boil, turn down to low and simmer for 45
minutes.
