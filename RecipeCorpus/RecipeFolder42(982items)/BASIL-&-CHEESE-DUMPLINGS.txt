Title: BASIL & CHEESE DUMPLINGS
Categories: Soups
Yield: 12 Servings
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Fresh chives; snipped -OR-
1 tb Green onion; sliced
1/2 ts Dried basil leaf
1/2 c  Creamed cottage cheese (4oz)
1 c  All-purpose flour; sifted
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
Melt butter or margarine in a small skillet.  Add chives or green
onion and basil.  Cook, Stirring constantly, until herbs soften.
Remove skillet from heat.  Stir in cottrage cheese; set aside.  Sift
together flour, salt and baking powder into a medium bowl. Add
cottage-cheese mixture. Stir until combined. Drop by rounded
teaspoonfuls into simmering soup. Cover soup and simmer until
dumpling are firms, about
15    minutes. Makes about 12.
