Title: BASIL JICAMA FRUIT SALAD
Categories: Vegetarian, Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Grapes, red, seedless
1 c  Grapes, green, seedless
1 c  Cantaloupe, honeydew or
-mango; cubed
1 c  Pineapple chunks, fresh
1    Orange; peeled, sliced and
-quartered
1    Nectarine; cubed
1/2 c  Strawberries; halved
1/2 c  Jicama; peeled, cut in
-matchstick pieces
1/4 c  Orange juice
1 tb Basil, fresh; chopped OR
1 ts Basil, dried; crushed
Basil springs, optional
In medium-sized bowl, combine all ingredients except basil sprigs;
mix gently. To serve, garnish with a sprig of basil.
