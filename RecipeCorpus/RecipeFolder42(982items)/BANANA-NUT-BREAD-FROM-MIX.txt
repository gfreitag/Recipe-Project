Title: BANANA-NUT BREAD FROM MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
3 3/4 c  Sweet Quick Bread Mix
2    Eggs -- beaten
1 tb Lemon juice
2 md Bananas -- mashed
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Preheat oven 325F. Grease one 9x5 or two 7x3 loaf pans; set aside. In
med bowl, combine all ingredients, stirring to blend. Turn into
prepared pan or pans. Bake 50 - 60 min in preheated oven until a
wooden pick inserted in center comes  out clean. Cool on rack 5 min.
Turn out of pan. Cool right-side up on rack. Makes 1 or 2 loaves.
