Title: BASIC BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Barbecue, Beef, Chicken, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1 ts Cayenne
2 tb Liquid barbecue smokeae
1/4 c  Brown sugar
2 ts Dry mustard
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Butter
Salt; to taste
Combine in saucepan and simmer slowly for 30 min. to blend flavors.
Brush on any meat orchicken as it grills and serve with cooked meat
as well. Makes two cups.
