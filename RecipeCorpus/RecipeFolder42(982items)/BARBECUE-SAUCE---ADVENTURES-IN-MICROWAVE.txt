Title: BARBECUE SAUCE - ADVENTURES IN MICROWAVE
Categories: Sauces, Microwave, Relishes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  White vinegar
1/4 c  Water
1 1/2 ts Celery seed
1/4 ts Salt
Dash garlic powder
Dash pepper
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 ts Prepared horseradish
1 md Onion, chopped
1. Combine all ingredients in a 2-quart glass casserole. Cover with
waxed paper.

2. Cook in microwave oven 3 minutes at High, or until hot; stir once.

Yield: about 1-1/2 cups.
