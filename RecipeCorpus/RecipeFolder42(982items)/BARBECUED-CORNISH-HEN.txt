Title: BARBECUED CORNISH HEN
Categories: Poultry, Bar-b-q
Yield: 6 Servings
4    Cornish game hens
Butter, melted
BARBECUE SAUCE:
1    Clove garlic, minced
2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Oil
1 1/4 c  Worcestershire
2 tb Chopped onion
1 ts Paprika
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Ketchup
Source: MAINPOUL.ZIP

Brush each Cornish hen with butter and place on vertical roaster. Mash
garlic and salt together in a bowl. Combine with remaining
ingredients. Set Cornish upright on grill over coals and cover
barbecue grill. Brush with sauce every 10 minutes for about 45
minutes or until tender.
