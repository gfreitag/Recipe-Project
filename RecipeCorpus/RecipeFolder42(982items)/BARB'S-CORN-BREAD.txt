Title: BARB'S CORN BREAD
Categories: Breads, Muffins, Side dishes
1    box Jiffy corn muffin mix
Yield: 6 Servings
1    egg
1 cn creamed corn -- 15 ounces
3 TB butter -- melted
1/2 c  sugar

Mix all ingredients.  Bake in greased 9 X 13 pan or 18 muffin cups
for 20 to
25    minutes at 350.
