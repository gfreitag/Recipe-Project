Title: BARBECUED LAMB ON SKEWERS
Categories: Chinese, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Stewing lamb, cut in
1 1/4" cubes
4 tb Fresh lemon juice
3    Cloves garlic, minced
1 tb Hot chili oil (or
1/2 ts Cayenne and
1 tb Salad oil)
1/4 ts Salt
1 pn Sugar
Combine all ingredients and marinate lamb cubes for 2 hours in covered
bowl. Drain lamb, reserving marinade for basting during barbecuing.
Skewer lamb; you should have enough for two skewers per serving.
Barbecue until browned, but still juicy. Overcooking will dull
flavors. Serve with nang (Moslem bread) or shao bing (baked sesame
rolls).
