Title: BARBECUED CHICKEN WINGS - PEAK GAI YANG *
Categories: Thai, Chicken, Bar-b-q
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Chicken Wings
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Chopped Lemon Grass
8    Cloves Garlic, Chopped
1/2 ts White Pepper
1/4 c  Minced Cilantro Root Or:
-Coriander Root
1 ts Tumeric
This is a favorite barbecue recipe.  The enticing aroma of the
sizzling meat on the grill makes it very popular with the street
vendors' clientele.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Combine all the marinade ingredients and marinate
the chicken wings overnight. Barbecue the wings over medium coals for
5 to 7 minutes on each side until they are cooked through and golden
brown.

Serve with steamed sticky rice.

From: Thailand The Beautiful Cookbook.  Typed by Syd Bigger.
