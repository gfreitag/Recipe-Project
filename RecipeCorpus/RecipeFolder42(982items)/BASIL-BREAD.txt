Title: BASIL BREAD
Categories: New, Text, Import
1    store bought pizza dough
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  pesto

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. On a greased baking sheet, roll pizza
into a 9 x 13 rectangle. Brush pesto over entire surface. Roll the
dough lengthwise to form a log. Brush surface with olive oil and bake
for 30 minutes. Slice on the diagonal to show spiral pattern. Serve.
