Title: BASIL-SESAME DRESSING
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Dressings, Harned 1996, Herb/spice, Salads
Yield: 1 Batch
1    Garlic clove; minced
3 tb Rice vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Grated Parmesan or Asiago
-cheese
1/4 c  Chopped fresh basil
2 tb Chopped Italian parsley
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/2 ts Sesame oil
Pepper; to taste
Whisk together all ingredients.  Add pepper.

Yield: 3/4 cup.

Shepherd writes: "A delicious flavor combination for all your garden
fresh greens."

Recipe developed by Renee Shepherd and Fran Raboff. From 1996
"Shepherd's Garden Seeds" catalog.  Pg. 24. Electronic format by
Cathy Harned.
