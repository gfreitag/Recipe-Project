Title: BARBECUED CATFISH
Categories: Fish/shellf, Barbe/grill, S living
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Catfish; 1 lb. each
1/8 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Oil, vegetable
Clean, skin, and fillet fish.  Combine remaining ingredients. Baste
fish with sauce.  Place fish in a well-greased, hinged fish basket.
Place on grill about 3 to 4 inches from coals. Cook 7 to 8 minutes on
each side or until fish flakes easily, brushing frequently with the
sauce.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, July, 1980. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
