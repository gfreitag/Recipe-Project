Title: BARBECUED DOVE
Categories: Poultry, Barbecue, Hot
Yield: 4 Servings
16    Dove breasts
1 md Yellow onion
1 1/2 c  Catsup
2/3 c  Brown sugar
2    Garlic cloves, crushed
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Vinegar or lemon juice
8 dr Tabasco sauce, or more!
Combine in a mixing bowl and stir. Brush sauce on breasts repeatedly
as they cook on the grill. Grill 3 to 5 minutes, then turn and grill
another minute. Wild rice goes great with these babies.

From "Guns" magazine. November, 1996 by Clair Rees.
