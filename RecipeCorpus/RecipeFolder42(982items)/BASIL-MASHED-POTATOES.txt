Title: BASIL MASHED POTATOES
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 4 Servings    2 1/2 lb baking potatoes, -- peeled
and cubed
:
3/4 c  heavy cream
1/2    stick unsalted butter
2 TB lemon juice
1/2 c  chopped basil leaves
1 TB grated ginger
1 TB minced garlic
Salt
:

In a large saucepan of salted water bring potatoes to a boil. Simmer
about 20 minutes or until tender. Drain. Pass potatoes through a
ricer into a mixing bowl. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan combine
cream and butter. Bring to a boil and keep hot until potatoes are
cooked. With a mixer or electric beater whip potatoes on slow speed,
slowly drizzling in cream mixture. Do not overmix. Fold in lemon
juice, basil, ginger and garlic. Season with sal
