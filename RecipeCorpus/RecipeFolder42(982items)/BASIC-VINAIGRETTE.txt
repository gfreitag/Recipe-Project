Title: BASIC VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Low-fat, Salads
Yield: 1 Cup
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
-or rice wine
1/2 c  Water
1 1/2 tb Apple juice concentrate
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Onion powder
1 ts Fresh lemon juice
1 ts Low-sodium soy sauce
1/2 ts Dijon mustard no-salt added
1 ds Red (cayenne) pepper
Place all ingredients in jar with a tight-fitting lid and shake well.
When mixture is well blended, place in refrigerator until served.
