Title: BANANA CREAM CHEESE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Cake
1    Yellow cake mix, prepared in
-13x9 pan.
8 oz Cream cheese, room
-temperature
1 pk (3-oz) instant vanilla
-pudding
2 c  Milk
3    Or 4 bananas
1 lg Container Cool Whip
1 c  Chopped nuts
Beat cream cheese until creamy. Add milk, gradually; add pudding,
beating until well mixed. Pour over cooled cake. Slice the bananas
over cake. Cover with Cool Whip and top with nuts.
