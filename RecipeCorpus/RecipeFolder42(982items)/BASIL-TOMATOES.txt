Title: BASIL TOMATOES
Categories: Tomatoes, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
3 ea Tomato, medium sliced
2 tb Olive oil
1 ts Basil
1 ts Vinegar
1 pn Salt
1 pn Pepper
1 pn Garlic powder
Combine oil, vinegar, basil, salt and pepper. Add tomatoes and evenly
coat. Add pinch garlic powder. Chill at least 2 hours.
