Title: BASIL VINEGAR
Categories: Basil
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Vinegar
1    Handful? basil, fresh
-shredded
Heat vinegar until just before boiling.  Combine basil and vingegar
in a glass bottle, and let cool. Seal tightly, and infuse for two
weeks. Strain the vinegar into a fresh bottle, and add a sprig of
basil.
