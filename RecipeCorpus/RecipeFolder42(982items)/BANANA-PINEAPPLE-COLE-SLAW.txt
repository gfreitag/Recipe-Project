Title: BANANA PINEAPPLE COLE SLAW
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Cabbage, Shredded
1 c  Carrots, Shredded
8 oz Pineapple, Tidbits Drained *
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
1/2 c  Sour Cream
1 tb Honey
1/4 c  Pecans, Chopped
1    Banana, Sliced
* Reserve drained liquid.
~------------------------------------------------------
~------------- ~-- In a medium bowl, layer cabbage, carrots and
pineapple. In a small bowl, combine Miracle Whip, sour cream, honey
and 1 T of the reserved pineapple juice. Blend well.  Spoon and
spread the dressing over the salad. Sprinkle with pecans. Refrigerate
until serving time. Just prior to serving time add sliced banana,
toss and serve. From: Syd's Cookbook.
