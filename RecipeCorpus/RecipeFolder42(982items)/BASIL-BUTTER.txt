Title: BASIL BUTTER
Categories: Basil
Yield: 1 Servings
4 oz Butter -- softened
1    Clove garlic -- chopped
Fine
1    Handful? basil, fresh
-- shredded
Salt and pepper -- to taste
Blend ingredients together well.  Shape into a roll, wrap in foil and
refrigerate.  This is fantastic on breads, veggies, pasta, or basting
poultry.
