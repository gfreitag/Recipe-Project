Title: BASIL BUTTERED BEANS
Categories: Digest, Oct., Lacto
Yield: 2 Servings
2 c  Green Beans -- fresh, cut
Into 2" pieces
2 tb Chopped Onion
2 tb Chopped Celery
1/4 c  Water
1 ts Butter Buds. -- or butter,
Melted
1 1/2 ts Fresh Basil -- minced, or
1/2 ts Dry
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Pieces
REVISED: the original recipe called for 2 T butter or margarine melted

In a saucepan, combine beans, onion, celery and water. Cover and cook
for 5 min or until beans are tender. Drain. Add the butter, basil,
salt and pepper; stir to coat. Serve immediately.

: Taste of Home, June/July 1994, pg16
