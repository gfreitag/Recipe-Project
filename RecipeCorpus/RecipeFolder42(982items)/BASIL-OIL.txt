Title: BASIL OIL
Categories: Basil
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Handful? basil, fresh
-bruised
1 c  Olive oil
Combine basil and oil in a tightly-sealed bottle. Leave to infuse for
two weeks; the leaves can be used normally, and the oil kept to
flavor salads, soups, or marinades.
