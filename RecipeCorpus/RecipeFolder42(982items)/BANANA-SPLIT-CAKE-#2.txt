Title: BANANA SPLIT CAKE #2
Categories: Cakes, Fruits
Yield: 1 Cake
-PHYLLIS SMITH <Prodigy>
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1    Stick margarine; melted
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding;
-prepared
1 cn Crushed pineapple; drained
-well, #2 size can
2    To 3 bananas; sliced
8 oz Cool Whip; thawed
1 c  Coconut
Nuts; chopped
1    Jar Maraschino cherries
CRUST: Mix crumbs and margarine together for crust and pack down in
bottom of 2-qt. oblong dish.  Layer on top of crust the next listed
ingredients in order given. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate
for two hours or more before serving.  Source: My Famous Little Black
Box
