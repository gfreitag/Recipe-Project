Title: BARBECUED BEANS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Dried kidney beans, washed
-and soaked
2 lg Yellow onions, chopped fine
2    Cloves garlic, crushed
2 tb Bacon drippings
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 ts Powdered mustard
1/2 ts Chili powder
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
5 c  Water
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Drain beans, place in an ungreased 3
quart bean pot or casserole, and mix in all remaining ingredients.
Cover tightly (use a double thickness of foil if casserole has no
lid) and bake 5 to 6 hours, stirring occasionally, until beans are
tender and sauce thick. If mixture gets too dry before beans are
done, stir in a little warm water.
