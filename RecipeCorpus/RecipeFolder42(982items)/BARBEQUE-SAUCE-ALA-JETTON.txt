Title: BARBEQUE SAUCE ALA JETTON
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Tomato ketchup
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Chili powder
1/8 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Water
3 ea Stalks celery, chopped
3 ea Bay leaves
1 ea Clove garlic
2 tb Onion, chopped
4 tb Butter
4 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Paprika
1 ds Black pepper
Combine all ingredients and bring to a boil.  Simmer about 15 minutes.
Remove from heat and strain.  This is a table sauce to be served with
beef, chicken or pork. Do not cook things in it. (Makes about 2 1/2
cups sauce)

Source:  Walter Jetton's LBJ Barbeque Cook Book, Simon &
Schuster
