Title: BARBECUE SAUCE #2
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
3/4 c  Chopped Onion
3/4 c  Tomato Catsup
1/3 c  Lemon Juice
3 tb Worcestershire Sauce
2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Salad Oil
3/4 c  Water
3 tb Sugar
2 tb Prepared Mustard
1/2 ts Pepper
Cook onion until soft in oil.  Add remaining ingredients. Simmer 15
minutes.  Good for steak, chicken or other barbecue favorites.
