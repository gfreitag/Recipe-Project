Title: BANANAS FOSTER - DE CARLE
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Dark rum
1 tb Creme de Banana
3 lg Bananas, split and sliced
Vanilla ice cream
ds Cinnamon
In a wide skillet, melt butter and sugar over medium heat, stirring
constantly until mixture has blended to caramel.

Add liquors and ignite.  (Use caution!)  Heat bananas in mixture for 2
minutes and serve over ice cream. Sprinkle w a dash of cinnamon.

Russell deCarle's recipe, from an article in Modern Woman, Nov 1994.
