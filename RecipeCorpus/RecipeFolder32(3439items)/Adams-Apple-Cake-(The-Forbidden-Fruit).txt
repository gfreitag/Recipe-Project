Title: Adams Apple Cake (The Forbidden Fruit)
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts, Jerusalem
Yield: 8 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
Cake
5 lg Eggs
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Vegetable oil
2 c  Flur
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
6    Or 7 tart cooking apples
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
Topping
1 c  Heavy cream
1 ts Rum
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Or
1/4 c  Melted butter
1/4 c  Walnuts
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Sugar
Cake: Beat the eggs together until they are light and frothy; blend
them well with the sugar. beat in the oil. Sift together the flour,
baking powder, and salt and add this to theegg mixture. Add the
vanilla; beat well. peel the apples and slice them thin

Preheat oven to 350.

Grease a 9 inch by 13 inch pan. Pour in enough batter to cover the
bottom well, a little less than half the batter. Arrange the apples
evenly over the batter and cover them with the remaining batter. Bake
for 1 hour.

Toppigs: To serve this as a dessert, spread the cake with whipped
cream sweetened with the 1/4 cup of sugar and flagored with the
cinnamon and rum extract.

To serve it as a coffee cake, brush the top with the melted butter.
Sprinkle a mixture of walnuts, cinnamon and sugar on top. Then pace
the cake under the broiler for about 2 minutes. Serve it warm.

: "The Flavors of Jerusalem" by Nathan and Goldman
