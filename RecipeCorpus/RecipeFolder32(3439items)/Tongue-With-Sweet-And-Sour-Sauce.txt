Title: Tongue With Sweet-And-Sour Sauce
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Fat
1    Onion -- diced
2 tb Flour
2 c  Tongue stock -- * see note
1/3 c  Vinegar
1/3 c  Honey
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Powdered ginger
1/4 c  Seedless raisins
1/4 c  Blanched almonds -- sliced
1    Lemon -- thinly sliced
1    Cooked tongue
* Note:  See the recipe for "Boiled Pickled Tongue" which is included
in this database.    Melt the fat in a sauce pan and lightly brown
the onion. Sprinkle the flour on the browned onion; gradually add the
stock, stirring constantly until mixture boils. vinegar, honey, salt,
ginger and raisins. Cook over low heat 5 minutes. Add the almonds and
lemon. Cook 2 minutes. Slice the tongue and serve with the sauce.
Serves 6.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie
