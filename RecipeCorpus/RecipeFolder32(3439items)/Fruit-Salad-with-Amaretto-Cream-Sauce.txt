Title: Fruit Salad with Amaretto Cream Sauce
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 pt Raspberries
1/4 pt Blueberries
1/4 pt Strawberries
-washed and halved
1/4 c  Orange segments, diced
1/4 c  Grapefruit segments, diced
1/4 c  Granny Smith apple, diced
1/4 c  Green grapes, pref seedless
1/4 c  Peach, diced
1/4 c  Apricot, diced
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Plus 1 tbs sugar
1 ts Chopped mint
1/4 c  Heavy cream
2    Egg yolks
1/4 c  Amaretto liqueur
Mix together all the fruit, the lemon juice,  cup of the sugar, and
the mint. Cover and refrigerate the salad overnight.

The next day, bring the cream to a boil and set the pan aside to cool
slightly. Whisk the egg yolks and the remaining 2 tbs sugar together.
When the cream has cooled, whisk in the egg and sugar mixture. Strain
the sauce and stir in the liqueur. Serve in a small pitcher to pour
over individual salad servings.
