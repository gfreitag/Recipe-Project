Title: Apple Schmarren
Categories: Jewish
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Flour -- sifted
1/8 ts Salt
2    Eggs
1 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Sliced apples
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Cinnamon
Sift the flour and salt into a bowl.  Beat the eggs and milk together
and add to flour, beating until smooth.  Stir in the apples.    Melt
the butter in a 9-inch skillet and pour the mixture into it.    Bake
in a 350 degree oven 15 minutes or until set.
