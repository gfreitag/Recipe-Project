Title: Tomato and Chicken Salad
Categories: Can/preserv, Poultry, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
-Waldine Van Geffen VGHc42A
4 c  Chicken breast; cook, dice
3 c  Celery; dice
2 c  Green or red pepper; diced
1 c  Walnuts; chopped
1/2 c  Fresh chives; chopped or
3 tb Dried
1 c  Mayo
1/4 c  Lime juice
Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Oil-pk tomaotes; chopped
In a large mixing bowl, combine the chicken, celery, pepper, walnuts
and chives. Add the mayo and lime juice and mix well. Season with
salt and pepper (be generous with the pepper). Lightly mix in the
tomatoes. To allow the flavors to blend, let the salad stand for 15
to 30 minutes before serving. Source: Sun-Dried Tomatoes! (wrv)
