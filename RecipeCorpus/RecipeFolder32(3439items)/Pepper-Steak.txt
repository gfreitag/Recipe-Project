Title: Pepper Steak
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Steak
4 tb Oil
1/2 c  Scallions or onions
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
5    Green peppers -- thinly
Sliced
1 c  Sliced celery
1 1/2 c  Beef broth
2 tb Cornstarch
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Soy sauce
Boiled white rice
