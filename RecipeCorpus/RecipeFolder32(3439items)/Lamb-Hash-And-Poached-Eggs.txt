Title: Lamb Hash And Poached Eggs
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Guestchefs
Yield: 6 servings

Diced
1 md Onion -- finely diced
2 tb Flour

2 md Potatoes
1/2 ts Salt
Ground black pepper
