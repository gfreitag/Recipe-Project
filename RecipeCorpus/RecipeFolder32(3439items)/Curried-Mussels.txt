Title: Curried Mussels
Categories: Seafood, Guestchefs
Yield: 8 servings
96    Mussels -- scrubbed clean
And beards removed
1/4 c  Dry white wine
1 tb Curry powder
1 tb Finely minced garlic
1 c  Chopped tomatoes -- (Peeled
And seeded)
1/2 tb Chopped fresh dill --
=OR=-
1/4 ts -Dried dill
1/2 c  Whipping cream
Salt
COMBINE MUSSELS, wine, curry powder, garlic and tomatoes in 3-quart
pan. Cover and bring to boil over high heat. (If using dried dill,
add it now.) Uncover, add cream and continueto boil until mussels
open, about 5 minutes or more, depending on size of mu Discard any
unopened mussels. Season to taste with salt (and fresh dill, if
using). Arrange mussels in bowls and pour in broth.
