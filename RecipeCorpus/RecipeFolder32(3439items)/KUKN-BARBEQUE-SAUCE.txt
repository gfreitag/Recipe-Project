Title: KUKN BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
12 oz Chili sauce
2 c  Brown sugar
Lemon juice; half
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ds Tobasco
Combine ingredients in sauce pan.   Bring to boil, and reduce to
simmer for at least 1/2 hour.    Tastes great on chicken, pork, or
sausages.
