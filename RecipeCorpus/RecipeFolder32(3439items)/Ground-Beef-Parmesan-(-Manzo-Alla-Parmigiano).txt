Title: Ground Beef Parmesan ( Manzo Alla Parmigiano)
Categories: Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 ts Monosodium glutamate
1    Egg -- well beaten
2 ds Pepper
1 md Onion -- chopped
6 sl Mozzarella cheese
1 ts Salt
MMMMM-------------------TOMATO-CHEESE MIXTURE------------------------
6 oz Tomato paste
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
Mix thoroughly ground beef, egg, onion, salt monosodium glutamate and
pepper. Shape meat mixture into a large square about 3/4" thick. Cut
into 6 equal portions. Arrange portions on broiler rack. Set
temperature control of range at broil. Place in broiler
