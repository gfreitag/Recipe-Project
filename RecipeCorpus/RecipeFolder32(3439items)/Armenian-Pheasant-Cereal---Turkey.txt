Title: Armenian Pheasant Cereal - Turkey
Categories: Nuts/grains, Game
Yield: 6 servings
1 lg Pheasant
3 c  Pearl barley bulgur
1 ga Water
1    Cube butter
Salt & pepper
From : Sallie Krebs, Thu 06 Jul 95 04:00, Area: COOKING

Soak barley overnight. Boil pheasant in the water and add seasonings,
cook until well done. Add barley and butter to the broth, then add
the remaining pheasant. Beat with a wooden spoon for along time until
it looks like oatmeal mush. Serve in bowls with a spoonful of brown
sugar sprinkled on top.
