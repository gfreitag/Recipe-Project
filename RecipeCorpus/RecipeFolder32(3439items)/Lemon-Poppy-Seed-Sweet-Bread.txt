Title: Lemon Poppy Seed Sweet Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Desserts, Breads
Yield: 8 servings
8 oz Lemon yogurt, lowfat
2 tb Oil
2    Eggs
2 tb Lemon rind -- finely minced
1 ts Salt
3 tb Sugar
4 tb Buttermilk, dried
3 1/3 c  Bread flour
3 tb Poppy seeds
1 tb Yeast
Place all ingredients in the machine.  Program for sweet bread or
basic white bread and push start.
