Title: Braciole Balsamico
Categories: Meats, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------FOR THE BEEF ROLLS--------------------------
1 1/2 lb Top round -- VERY thin
Sliced
1/2 c  Fresh basil -- minced
2    Roasted red peppers --
Diced
4 tb Parmesan -- fresh grated
2 tb Parsley -- minced
Salt and pepper
6 sl Proscuitto -- very thin
MMMMM----------------------FOR THE MARINADE---------------------------
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Parsley -- minced
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
2 tb Pepper -- black fresh
Ground
2    Cloves garlic -- finely
Minced
1/4 c  Olive oil
ALYCE MANTIA (KRBS41A-----
Place beef slices on counter or flat surface. (Get your butcher to
slice them; he can do it so much neater than we can!) Sprinkle with
the red peppers and parsley. Cut proscuitto to fit each beef slice
