Title: PETA Hot Chocolate
Categories: Beverages, Breakfast
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ds Salt
1/2 c  Water
6 c  Vanilla soy milk
;tofu whipped cream; opt
;cinnamon sticks; opt
In a 2 quart saucepan, stir together the cooca, sugar, and salt until
well blended.  Add the water and stir until smooth.  Cook the mixture
over med heat until boiling, stirring constantly with a spoon or wire
whisk. Lower the heat and cook 2 mins more, stirring constantly.

Stir in the soy milk and heat until tiny bubbles form around the edge,
stirring constantly.  Remove the pan from the heat.  Beat with a wire
whisk or electric mixer until smooth and foamy, then pour into 8
ounce mugs.

Top with whipped tofu and garnish with cinnamon sticks.
