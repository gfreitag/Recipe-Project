Title: PETA Goblin Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Margerine; or water
1 md Onion; chopped\
16 oz Can pumpkin puree
1 1/3 c  Water
3 c  Soy milk
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Sugar
;pepper to taste
;croutons
In large saucepan over medium heat, heat the margerine or water and
cook the onion until tender, stirring often, about 5 mins.  Stir in
the pumpkin, water, soy milk, nutmeg, sugar and pepper.  Cook over
med heat until just boiling, stirring constantly.  Garnish with
croutons. From The Compassionate Cook by PETA and Ingrid Newkirk
