Title: Caramel Pudding^
Categories: Puddings, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Brown sugar
3 c  Water
3 ts (rounded) cornstarch
1 c  Walnuts, chopped
1 ts Vanilla
Put sugar and water in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Dissolve
cornstarch in a little cold water, add to syrup and boil about 5
minutes. Stir occasionally. Take from fire and add nuts and vanilla.
Pour into small bowls, cool. Serve with whipped cream.
