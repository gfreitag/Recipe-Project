Title: Sloppy Joes
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 lg Onion -- chopped
1 sm Green bell pepper --
-chopped
14 1/2 oz Stewed tomatoes (mexican
-style) - undrained
1/4 c  Chili sauce
1 ts Dried thyme
2 ts Mustard
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Celery seed
1 tb Cold water
1 ts Cornstarch
6    Hamburger buns
Cook ground beef, onion and bell pepper in 10" skillet over medium
heat about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until beef is brown;
drain.  Stir in tomatoes, chili sauce, thyme, mustard, worcestershire
sauce and celery seed.  Heat to boiling; reduce heat.  Cover and
simmer 5 minutes.  Mix water and cornstarch; stir into beef mixture.
Boil and stir 1 minute.

TO STORE:  Refrigerator-Refrigerate tightly covered no longer than 48
hours. Freezer-Cool mixture 30 minutes.  Place in labeled airtight 1
1/2 quart freezer container.  Freeze no longer than 2 months.

TO REHEAT FROM REFRIGERATOR: About 15 minutes before serving, cover
and heat beef mixture in 3 quart saucepan over medium heat, stirring
frequently, until hot and bubbly. Microwave-About 10 minutes before
serving, place beef mixture in 1 1/2 quart casserole.  Cover and
microwave on high 8 to 10 minutes, stirring every 3 minutes, until
hot and bubbly.

TO REHEAT FROM FREEZER: Stove-About 20 minutes before serving, dip
container into hot water to loosen; remove container.  Cover and heat
in 3 quart saucepan over medium heat, turning and stirring
occasionally, until hot and bubbly. Microwave-About 20 minutes before
serving, remove lid from freezer container; place container upside
down in 2 quart casserole. Microwave on high 5 minutes; remove
container. Cover and microwave 10 to 15 minutes longer, breaking up
and stirring every 5 minutes, until hot and bubbly. 375 calories and
18 grams fat.
