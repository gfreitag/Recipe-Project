Title: Almond-Butter Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Almond butter
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 ts Almond extract
1/2 c  Flour
1/4 c  Cocoa
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Grated coconut
Preheat the oven to 350.  Combine the almond butter and sugar.
Add the eggs and almond extract: mix well.  In another bowl, combine
the flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt.  Add the dry ingredients
to the wet, and mix well.  Stir in the coconut.  Pour into a greased
9-inch square baking pan.  Bake for 35 minutes.
