Title: Egg Quiche
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
16 sl Bread
1 lb Ham
8    Thick slices of Old English
Cheese
Arrange 8 slices of bread in a buttered pan then add ham, then
cheese, then 8 slices of bread.  Beat 7 eggs along with 1 1/2
teaspoons of dry mustard, 1 teaspoon salt, scant 3 cups of milk and
pour over all. Refrigerate overnight.

Topping: 3 cups Corn flakes, crushed 1 stick of margarine

Drizzle over all and bake at 300 degrees for 1 hour. Randy Rigg
