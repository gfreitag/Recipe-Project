Title: Wild Rice and Corn Salad
Categories: Salads, Rice, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
2 tb Sherry vinegar
Salt and pepper to taste
2 c  Uncooked wild rice
2 c  Corn kernels
-(about 4 ears)
1/2 c  Tomato, peeled
-seeded and diced
1/4 c  Basil, chopped
Combine the olive oil and vinegar, add salt and pepper to taste, and
refrigerate the dressing for 2 hours before serving.

Boil the rice for about 45 minutes and let it cool.

Cut the kernels off the cobs and blanch them in boiling water for
about 5 minutes. Allow the corn to cool.

Combine the rice and corn, and add the tomato and basil. When you are
ready to serve the salad, add the dressing and toss gently.
