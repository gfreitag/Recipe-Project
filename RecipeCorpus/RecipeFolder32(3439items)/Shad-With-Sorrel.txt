Title: Shad With Sorrel
Categories: Seafood, Guestchefs
Yield: 2 servings
1 lb Shad fillets, boned --
Skinned
Salt -- to taste
White pepper -- to taste
1/4 c  Dry white wine
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Chopped sorrel leaves
1 tb Unsalted butter
PLACE SHAD IN A MEDIUM saute pan and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
