Title: Potato Pancake No. 2
Categories: Aust/german, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Potatoes
Directions:
2 tb Flour
1 sm Onion -- grated
4    Strips Bacon, cooked
2    Eggs -- beaten
Crumbled
1 pn Black pepper
1 ts Salt
Butter or margarine
2 ts Chopped parsley
More(Y/n/=)?
Peel potatoes, wash with cold water and drain. Grate at once and
drain off water that collects. Mix potatoes, onion, flour, bacon
bits, eggs and seasonings, blending well. Heat butter in skillet.
Just before butter browns, drop in 1/3 cup batter and flatt Should
make approx. 12 pancakes. Best served with sour cream.
