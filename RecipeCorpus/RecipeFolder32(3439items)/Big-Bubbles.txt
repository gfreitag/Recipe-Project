Title: Big Bubbles
Categories: Kids
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Joy or Dawn dishwashing
-liquid
6 c  Water
3/4 c  Light corn syrup
Mix ingredients in bucket.  Let settle 3-4 hours.  Makes super big
bubbles.
