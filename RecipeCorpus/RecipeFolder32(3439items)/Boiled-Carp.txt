Title: Boiled Carp
Categories: Jewish, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
4    Onions -- sliced
2    Beets -- peeled and sliced
3 c  Water
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
6 sl Carp
Combine the onions, beets, water, salt and pepper in a deep skillet.
Place the fish in it.  Cover, bring to a boil and cook over low heat
45 minutes, removing the cover after 30 minutes. Serve hot or
chilled. Serves 6. Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING by Jennie
