Title: Good Cake Without Frosting^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Shortening or lard
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
2 c  Brown sugar
MMMMM------------------------EGG MIXTURE-----------------------------
1 c  Sour milk
1 ts Baking powder
2    Eggs, beaten
Mix together flour, cloves, salt, shortening, cinnamon, nutmeg and
brown sugar. Take out 2/3 cup of mixture for topping. To rest of
mixture, add milk, baking soda and eggs. Beat well. Pour batter into
greased pan; put egg mixture on top of cake and bake in a slow oven
till done.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
