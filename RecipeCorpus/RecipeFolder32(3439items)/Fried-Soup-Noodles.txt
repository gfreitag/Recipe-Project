Title: Fried Soup Noodles
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Pasta
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1    Egg
Fat -- for frying
Make a well in the center of the flour and drop the salt and egg into
it. Work in the flour and knead until smooth and elastic.  Cover with
a bowl for 20 minutes, then roll out as thin as possible on a lightly
