Title: Oatmeal Wafers
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 60 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
2/3 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Oatmeal
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Vanilla
4 tb Heavy cream
4 tb Corn syrup
Preheat oven to 350. In a large saucepan, melt the butter. then stir
in all the other ingredients in the order given. Blend well. Drop the
batter by rounded tsp on a heavily greased cookie sheet. Leave space
between teaspoonfuls, as the wafers will spread
