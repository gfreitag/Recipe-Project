Title: ORANGE CREAM CHEESE POUND CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Butter (1 stick; softened)
8 oz Cream cheese (softened)
3 c  Cake flour
1 tb Orange extract (or almond)
1 c  Margarine (2 stcks,softened)
3 c  Sugar
6    Eggs
Cream butter, margarine, cream cheese, and sugar until smooth and
creamy. Add other ingredients and beat well.  Bake in 10 inch Bundt
cake pan at 325 degrees for 1 hour and 20 minutes.
