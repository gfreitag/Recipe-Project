Title: Chunky Clam Rarebit
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 9 servings
3 tb Butter
1/2 c  Chopped green bell pepper
1/2 c  Chopped mushrooms
3 tb Flour
1 cn (28 oz) Italian peeled --
Tomatoes; crushed, w
1 c  Milk
2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese --
(about 8 ozs.)
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 cn (6-1/2 oz.) minced clams
1. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in green
pepper and mushrooms and cook until softened but not browned, about 5
minutes. 2. Stir in flour. Cook, stirring, 1 minute. Gradually add
tomatoes and their liquid, stirring constantly. Add milk, cheese,
mustard, Worcestershire, and clams with liquid and cook, stirring,
until heated through and cheese has melted, about 5 minutes.
