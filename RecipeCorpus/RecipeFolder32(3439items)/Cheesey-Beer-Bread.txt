Title: Cheesey Beer Bread
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Breads, Alcohol
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Flour
4 ts Baking powder
2 ts Salt
1 1/2    Grated cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Freshly grated parmesan
-cheese
1 tb Dried parsley
1 c  Beer
1 tb Liquid honey
Combine flour, baking powder, salt, cheeses and parsley. Stir in beer
and honey until well mixed. Spread into a greased loaf pan. Bake at
350 degrees F for 45 minutes until browned on the outside, and a
toothpick inserted comes out clean. Turn out on a rack to cool.
