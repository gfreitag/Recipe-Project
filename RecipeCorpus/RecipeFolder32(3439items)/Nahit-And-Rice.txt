Title: Nahit And Rice
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Chickpeas
1/2 c  Raw rice
1 ts Salt
2 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Honey
Wash the chickpeas and soak overnight in water to cover. Drain.
Cover with fresh water; bring to a boil and cook over low heat 2
hours.  Drain.
