Title: Hamentaschen -  Poppy Seed Filling
Categories: Jewish, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Poppy seeds
1 c  Milk
3/4 c  Honey
1 ts Grated lemon rind
1/2 c  Seedless raisins
Have the poppy seeds ground or put through your food chopper.
Combine with the milk and honey.  Cook over low heat, stirring
frequently, until thick. Stir in the lemon rind and raisins.  Cool
and fill the dough. Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKI
