Title: Kreplach -  Chicken Liver Filling
Categories: Jewish, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Chicken livers
1/2 c  Minced onions
2 tb Chicken fat
2    Hard-boiled egg yolks
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Cook the livers and onions in the fat for 10 minutes, mixing
frequently. Grind or chop the livers, onions, eggs, salt and pepper.
Cool before placing in squares of dough.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF
JEWISH COOKING by Jennie Grossinger Published by Ba
