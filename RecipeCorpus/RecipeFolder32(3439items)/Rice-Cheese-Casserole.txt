Title: Rice-Cheese Casserole
Categories: Jewish, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Grated carrots
2 c  Cooked rice
2    Eggs -- beaten
1/2 c  Light cream
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 tb Grated onion
2 c  Grated American cheese
Combine the carrots, rice, eggs, cream, salt, pepper, onion and 1 1/2
cups cheese.  Pour into a greased 1 1/2-quart casserole.  Sprinkle
with the remaining cheese.    Bake in a350 degree oven 50 minutes.
Serves 6. Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOK
