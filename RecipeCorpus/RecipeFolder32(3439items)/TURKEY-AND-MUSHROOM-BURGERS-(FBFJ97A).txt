Title: TURKEY AND MUSHROOM BURGERS (FBFJ97A)
Categories: Low cal/fat, Poultry, Vegetables, Vegetarian, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Egg white; (about 2 tbsp)
1/4 c  White wine; dry
1/3 c  Soft french bread crumbs
1/4 ts Salt
1 lb Ground turkey (breast best)
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Shallots; finely chopped
2 oz Mushrooms; finely chopped
1/2 x  Cooking spray
In medium size bowl, beat egg white and wine until blended. Stir
in crumbs, salt, pepperm and shallots; then lightly mix in turkey and
mushrooms.  Shape turkey mixture into 6 patties, each about 1/2 inch
thick. Cook just like hamburgers either on the stovetop using
non-stick spray on pan or on the grill (best!).
Recipe originally posted by Jenee Tapert (xpkr42a) in the Healthy
eating topic.
