Title: Orange Sherbet Dessert
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
2 pk Gelatin, lemon flavor
1 c  Water, hot
1 cn Mandarin Oranges, drained
1 cn Pineapple, canned crushed,
- drained
1 pt Orange Sherbet
2    Bananas, sliced
Dissolve gelatin in hot water.  Add sherbet, mixture will thicken
almost immediately.  Add mandarin oranges and pineapple. Fold in
bananas.

Pour into 8x10-inch dish and refrigerate.
