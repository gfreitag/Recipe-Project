Title: Dairy-Style Cabbage
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
6 c  Shredded cabbage
4 tb Butter
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Sugar
1    Egg
1 c  Sour cream
Cook the cabbage in the butter over low heat for 45 minutes, stirring
frequently.  Stir in the salt, lemon juice and sugar. Cook 5 minutes
longer.    Beat the egg and sour cream together and add to the
cabbage, mixing steadily until it begins to thicken.  Serve 4 to 6.
Recipe Source: THEART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger
Published by Bantam Books, Inc.
