Title: Cheesy Mushroom Pizza/Sun-dried Toms
Categories: Can/preserv, Boboli, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
-Waldine Van Geffen VGHC42A
2 c  Mushrooms; sliced
2 tb Oil
1/2 c  Pizza or tomato sauce
1    12" or 4 6" Boboli shells
1 c  Cheddar; shredded
4    Sun-dried tomatoes; cut in
-strips, dry-pk
Saute mushrooms in oil 2 minutes. Spread sauce on Boboli; sprinkle
cheese over. Top with mushrooms and tomatoes. Bake at 450~ for 10
minutes. (wrv)
