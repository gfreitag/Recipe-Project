Title: Barbecued Beef Buns
Categories: Meats
Yield: 8 servings
4 c  Cooked Beef Strips -- 2 X
1/4    "
2 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1 c  Ketchup
4 ts Prepared Mustard
1/2 c  Cider Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter Or Regular Margarine
1/4 ts Chili Powder
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Black Pepper
2 md Onions -- Thinly Sliced
1/2 c  Water
2 sl Lemon
8    Hamburger Buns
Combine all of the ingredients, except the hamburger buns, in a
4-quart Dutch oven.  Cook over hig heat until the mixture comes to a
boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 25minutes or until the
mixture thickens. Remove the lemon slices.  Pour into a container,
cover and refrigerate.

TO SERVE:

Warm over medium heat and serve in the hamburger buns.
