Title: Boboli Topping 7
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Ethnic
Yield: 1 servings
Cheesy Mushroom Pizza
1 lg Boboli shell or 4 small
1 c  Mushrooms -- sliced
1 c  Cheese -- shredded
2 tb Oil
4    Sun-dried tomatoes -- cut
Into
1/2 c  Pizza or tomato sauce
Strips
ANN NIGGEMANN MBHP65B-----
Saute' mushrooms in oil for 2 minutes.  Spread sauce on shell;
sprinkle cheese over.  Top with mushrooms and tomatoes. Bake at 450
degrees for 10 minutes. Formatted for MM by Norma Wrenn npxr56b (wrv)
