Title: Ground Beef Chop Suey
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped celery
Cook the 3 ingredients together until celery is tender.  Add 1 can of
cream of mushroom soup, 1 can cream of chicken soup, 1 1/2 cups of
hot water, 1/2 cup of raw rice and 1/4 cup soy sauce.  Bake at 350
degrees, covered, for 1/2 hour, then uncover and bake another 1/2
hour. Put in a can of chow mein noodles on top and bake 15 to 20
minutes longer. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   07-12-95
