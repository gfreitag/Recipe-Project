Title: Potato Salad
Categories: Jewish, Potatoes, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Potatoes
1/4 c  Minced onions
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Vinegar
Buy small potatoes and cook them in boiling water about 20 minutes or
until tender but firm.  Peel and cube the potatoes.  Add the onions,
salt and pepper.  Mix together the mayonnaise and vinegar; pour over
the salad. Toss until well blended. Serve hot or cold. Serves 4 to 6.
