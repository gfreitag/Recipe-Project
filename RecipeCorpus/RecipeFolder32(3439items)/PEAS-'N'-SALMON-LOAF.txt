Title: PEAS 'N' SALMON LOAF
Categories: Casseroles, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
2 cn Salmon,drained(15.5oz ea)
2    Eggs,beaten
1 tb Onion,chopped
2 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Cracker crumbs,fine
1 cn Peas,drained(8.5oz)
1/4 c  Tomato catsup
1. Combine in a mixing bowl salmon, eggs, onion, lemon juice, salt and
cracker crumbs.
2. Carefully fold in peas.
3. Pack into a greased 1 1/2-quart loaf pan; bake in a preheated
350'F. oven 30 minutes.
4. Spread loaf with catsup; bake 5 minutes longer.
