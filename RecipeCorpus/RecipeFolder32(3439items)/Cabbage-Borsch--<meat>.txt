Title: Cabbage Borsch  <meat>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Brisket
Beef bones
2 qt Water
2    Onions -- diced
3 c  Canned tomatoes
3 lb Cabbage -- coarsely
Shredded
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Sugar
3 tb Seedless raisins
Combine the brisket, bones and water in a deep saucepan. Bring to a
boil and skim.  Add the onions and tomatoes. Cover and cook over low
heat 1 hour.  Add the cabbage, salt andpepper.  Cook 1 hour.  Stir in
the lemon juice, sugar and raisins.  Cook 20 min
