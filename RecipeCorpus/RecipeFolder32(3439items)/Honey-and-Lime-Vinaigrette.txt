Title: Honey and Lime Vinaigrette
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 2 /3 cup
2 tb Honey
Juice of 2 limes
-(about 3 tbs)
4 tb Walnut or olive oil
1 tb Sherry vinegar
Combine the honey, lime juice. and vinegar, mixing well with a whisk.
