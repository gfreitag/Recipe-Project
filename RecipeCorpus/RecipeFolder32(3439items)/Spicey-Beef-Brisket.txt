Title: Spicey Beef Brisket
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
4 lb Beef brisket, flat half
24 oz Salsa sauce
12 oz Beer
6    Pita breads, cut in half
1    Lettuce head - shredded
3    Tomatoes - chopped
Trim fat from brisket.  Place in ungreased baking dish, 13 1/2 by 9
by 2 inches.  Mix salsa sauce and beer; pour over meat.  Cover
tightly. Bake in 325 degree oven 3 1/2 hours or until tender. Remove
beef; slice across the grain.  Stuff pita bread with lettuce and beef
slices; top with tomatoes. Serve with additional salsa sauce if
desired.

Source: Pittsburgh, Pa Post-Gazette (23 Oct 1994).
