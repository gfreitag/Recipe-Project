Title: Puerto Rican Rice
Categories: Ethnic, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  White rice
1 lg Can of tomato sauce
1/2 c  Diced ham
1 ts Paprika
2 tb Sofrito
1 c  Peas
1 ts Oregano
3 tb Capers
1 1/2 c  Water
EILEEN LAMPARELLI
CGDR39A-----
In a skillet fry dry rice with ham; add sofrito, oregano, tomato
sauce, paprika and water.  Cover and let simmer until rice is almost
tender. Add peas and capers. Heat another 5 minutes and serve.
