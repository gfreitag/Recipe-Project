Title: Praline Frosting^
Categories: Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Flour
1/3 c  Nuts, chopped
3 tb Butter, melted
1 tb Water
Mix together well and spread over warm cake. Place in a moderate oven
(350 degrees) about 5 minutes. Will frost a small cake. for a large
cake, double the recipe.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
