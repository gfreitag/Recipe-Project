Title: Wild Rice Casserole
Categories: Rice, Game, Casseroles, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Washed wild rice
2 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Long grain rice
1 tb Butter
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
1/2 c  Finely chopped celery
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1/3 c  Sliced almonds
1 c  Chopped water chestnuts
1/4 c  Soy sauce
In a 2 quart saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.  Stir wild
rice into boiling water, cooking on low for 20 minutes.  Return to
boiling state, stir in long grain rice.  Simmer for 20 minutes.  In a
frying pan, melt butter and saute onion, celery, mushrooms, almonds
and water chestnuts, until slightly browned, about 5 minutes.  In a 2
quart greased casserole, combine the vegetables, rice and soy sauce.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 F for 20 minutes.  Serves 6.

Origin:  Homestyles, Canadian Classics Shared by: Sharon Stevens
