Title: Strawberry Banana Drink
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Fresh berries OR
2    8 oz frozen berries
2    Bananas
1 c  Yogurt
1 tb Milk powder
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Honey (not with frozen)
1/4 ts Vanilla
Blend.
