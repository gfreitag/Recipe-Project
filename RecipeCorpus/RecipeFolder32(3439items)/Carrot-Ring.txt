Title: Carrot Ring
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Mashed cooked carrots
1 c  Bread crumbs
3    Egg yolks, beaten
2 tb Chopped onion
2 tb Celery, chopped
2 tb Chopped parsley
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Paprika
1 c  Milk
2 tb Butter, melted
3    Beaten egg whites
Mix all ingredients, then add beaten egg whites.  Put in a greased
ring mold and place in a pan of hot water and bake for 1 hour at 350
degrees. Peas may be served in the center of the ring. Randy Rigg
