Title: Kasha
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Buckwheat groats
1    Egg -- beaten
2 1/2 c  Boiling water
1 1/2 ts Salt
You can buy fine, medium or coarse groats; use whichever type you
prefer. Mix the groats and egg in a saucepan over low heat until the
grains separate.  Add the water and salt. Cover and cook over low
heat 15 minutes. All the water should be absorbed; if not, drain
thoroughly. Serve with melted fat or butter or use in other recipes.
Serves 6. Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING by Jennie
Grossinger Published by Ban
