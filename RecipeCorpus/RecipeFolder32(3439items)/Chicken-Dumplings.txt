Title: Chicken Dumplings
Categories: Breads, Oldies
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
3    Eggs, beaten
1/4 c  Milk
Recipe from 1904.
Mix all ingredients well.  POur out onto well floured board.  With
rolling pin, roll out 1/2 batter at a time.  Cut into 1 inch squares.
Drop one at a time into 2 quarts of boiling chicken broth in a 4
quart Dutch oven. Cook 20 minutes.

Origin: Women's Circle, for cooks on the go. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Jan/94
