Title: Flanken Soup  <meat>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
3 lb Plate flank
Beef bones
3 1/2 qt Water
1    Onion
1 tb Salt
Soup greens
1    Bay leaf
1/4 ts Peppercorns
Combine the beef, bones and water in a deep saucepan. Bring to a boil
and skim.  Add the onion, salt, soup greens, bay leaf and
peppercorns. Cover loosely and cook over low heat 2 hours, or until
meat is tender. Remove beef and strain the soup.    Makes about 2 1/2
quarts soup. Serve the beef with horseradish.  Serves 8 to 10. Recipe
Source: THE ART OF JEWISH CO
