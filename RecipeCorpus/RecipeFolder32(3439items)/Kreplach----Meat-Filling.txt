Title: Kreplach -  Meat Filling
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Fat
1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Minced onions
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Heat the fat in a skillet and cook the meat and onions in it for 10
minutes, stirring frequently.  Add the salt and pepper.  Cool before
placing in squares of dough.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH
COOKING by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc.
