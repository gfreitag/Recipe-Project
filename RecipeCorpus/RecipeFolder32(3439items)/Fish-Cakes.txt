Title: Fish Cakes
Categories: Jewish, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Cooked and flaked codfish
2 c  Mashed potatoes
3 tb Melted butter
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2    Eggs -- beaten
1/2 c  Milk
Butter or fat -- for frying
Mix all the ingredients together and taste to correct seasoning.
Shape into 8 cakes and chill for 2 hours.    Fry in the butter or fat
until browned on both sides. Serve with hot or cold tomato sauce.
Serves 4. Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKIN
