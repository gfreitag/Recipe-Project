Title: Horseradish Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Oldies
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Heavy cream
1 tb Grated or evaporated
Horseradish
2 tb Lemon juice
Salt & paprika
Beat cream till quite thick; add horseradish. (If evaporated
horseradish is used, mix it with a tablespoon cold water and allow it
to be absorbed before adding it to the cream) Pour in lemon juice
slowly, stirring constantly. Season, serve very cold. this is
especially good with tomatoes.
