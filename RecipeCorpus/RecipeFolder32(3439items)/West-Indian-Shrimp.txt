Title: West Indian Shrimp
Categories: Ethnic, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 lb Shrimp, cooked -- drained
And peeled
2 tb Olive oil
1 tb Angostura bitters
1 ts Dried chervil
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Cumin seed
1/4 ts Saffron
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
In an ovenproof skillet, heat olive oil until smoking. Quickly saute
shrimp. Sprinkle with bitters and herbs. Top with Parmesan cheese and
broil until lightly browned.
