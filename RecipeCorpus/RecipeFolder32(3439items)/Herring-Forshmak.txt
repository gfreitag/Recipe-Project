Title: Herring Forshmak
Categories: Jewish, Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
4    Salt herring fillets
4 tb Unsalted butter
2    Onions -- diced
4 sl White bread
1    Apple -- peeled and diced
1 c  Sour cream
2 tb Bread crumbs
Soak the herring in water to cover overnight.  Change the water twice.
Drain.    Melt the butter in a skillet.  Brown the onions in it.
Coarsely chop the herring, bread, apple, sour cream and browned
onions. Turn into a buttered bakingdish and sprinkle with the crumbs.
Bake in a 425 degree oven 25 minutes. 6. Recipe Source: THE ART OF
JEWISH COOKING by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc.
