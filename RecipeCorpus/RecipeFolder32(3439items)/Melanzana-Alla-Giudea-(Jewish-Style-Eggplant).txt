Title: Melanzana Alla Giudea (Jewish Style Eggplant)
Categories: Jewish, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings

4 lg Eggplants
2 ts Salt
40    Milliliters
1 tb Cooking oil
1 tb Butter
Garlic -- minced

lengthwise and scrape out the pulp, leaving a 1/2 inch of shell.

squares. Place the squares in a strainer or colander and mix with
salt. Place a heavy saucepan on top to crush and set aside for one
