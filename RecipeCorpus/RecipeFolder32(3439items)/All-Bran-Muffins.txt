Title: All-Bran Muffins
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 8 muffins
2 tb Shortening
1/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 c  Kellogg's All-Bran
3/4 c  Milk
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
Cream shortening and sugar thoroughly; add egg and beat until
creamy.Add All-Bran and milk; let soak until the moisture is taken
up. Sift flour with salt and baking powder; add to first mixture and
stir konly until flour disappears. Fill greased muffin pans 2/3 full
and bake in a moderate oven 400 degrees for about 30 minutes or until
done.

Note; When sour milk or buttermil is used instead of sweet milk, use
1/2 tsp soda and only 1 tsp baking powder. Makes 8 large or 12 small
muffins.

From Kellogg's Phamphlet
