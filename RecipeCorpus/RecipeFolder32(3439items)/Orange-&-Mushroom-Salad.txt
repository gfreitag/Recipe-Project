Title: Orange & Mushroom Salad
Categories: Salads, Appetizers, Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 sm Endive, to serve
3 ea Oranges, segmented
1 bn Watercress sprigs
4 oz Button mushrooms, sliced
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
4 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1 tb Orange juice
Salt & pepper
1 ea Garlic clove, crushed
1 tb Chives, snipped
1 tb Black olives, chopped
Mix together all the dressing ingredients except the olives.

Line a dish with the endive leaves.  Arrange a ring of orange
segments then a ring of watercress sprigs.  Just before serving,
drizzle on about 1/3 of the the dressing.

Stir the olives into the remaining dressing & toss in the mushrooms.
Pile into the centre of the dish & serve.

Pamela Westland, "Fruit"
