Title: Grilled Greek Chicken (Miracle Whip)
Categories: Poultry, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
2 ts Parsley flakes
2 tb Dry white wine OR water
2 tb Fresh Lemon juice
1 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Dried Oregano leaves,crushed
1 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1 ea Chicken, cut up, skinned
- about 3 1/2 pounds
Mix all ingredients except chicken.  Pour mixture over chicken;
cover. Refrigerate at least 20 minutes to marinate.
Remove chicken from marinade; place on grill over medium coals.
Grill, covered, 15 to 20 minutes on each side or until cooked through.
Prep Time: 15 minutes plus refrigeration
Grilling Time: 40 minutes
