Title: Chiliquiles
Categories: Ethnic, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
From the Kitchen of

Lawrence & Cindy Kellie
1 cn Tomato sauce -- large can
24    Corn tortillas
Garlic salt
2 lg Onion -- diced
Onion salt
1 cn Olives
Cayenne pepper
Saute onions.  Add tortillas broken in small pieces. Add all other
ingredients.  Mix well.  Place in baking dish (what size??? whatever
it fits in).  Warm to serve. Add Longhorn Cheddar cheese (or any
