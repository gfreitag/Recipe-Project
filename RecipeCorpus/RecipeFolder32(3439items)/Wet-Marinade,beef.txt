Title: Wet Marinade/beef
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Bourbon
1 c  Brown sugar
2/3 c  Soy sauce
1    Cilantro ; chopped
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Worshestershire sauce
2 c  Water
3    Thyme ; chopped
Mix  all the ingredients together and marinade meat in refrigerator 8
to 12 hours, rotating meat occasionally.
