Title: Matzo Fry
Categories: Jewish, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 2 servings
4    Matzos -- whole
3    Eggs
1/2 ts Salt
4 tb Butter or fat
Hold the matzos under cold running water until softened. Place in a
shallow dish.  Beat the eggs and salt together and pour over the
matzos. Let soak 10 minutes, then fry in the butter or fat until
browned on both sides. Serves 2 to 4.    Recipe Source:
