Title: BARBECUE SHRIMP
Categories: Seafood, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Medium whole shrimp
1 1/2    Sticks butter
1/2 c  Olive oil
2    Lemons
6    Cloves garlic, pressed
3 tb Worcestershire
1/2 ts Cayenne
1 tb Salt (yup!)
1/2 ts Pepper
1 tb Italian seasoning
French bread

Melt butter in a large, shallow baking pan.  Add 1 1/2 squeezed
lemons & everything except shrimp.  Let simmer 5 minutes.  Wash
shrimp (leave shells on) & place in pan, & baste.  Bake at 350
degrees until shrimp are pink on top, 5-8 minutes.  Turn shrimp, salt
& pepper lightly, & bake 10 minutes. Squeeze 1/2 lemon & a few dashes
Worcestershire on top & serve with French bread for dipping that
great sauce. 4 servings. This is from the New Orleans Times-Picayune
Cooking Contest, 1980. Gail B.
