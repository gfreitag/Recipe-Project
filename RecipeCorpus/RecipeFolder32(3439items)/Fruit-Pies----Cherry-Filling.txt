Title: Fruit Pies -  Cherry Filling
Categories: Jewish, Desserts, Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 cn (#2)  Sour red cherries --
Drained
1 c  Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
2 tb Flour
1/4 ts Almond extract
Mix together all the ingredients.  Fill the pie.  Cover with top
crust. Bake in a 400 degree oven 45 minutes or until browned.
