Title: Pate de Picate (Chicken Liver Spread)
Categories: Romanian, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Chicken livers
1 tb Chicken fat or oil
1 x  Onion, medium
3 x  Eggs, hard boiled
1 x  Mayonnaise
1 x  Salt, garlic salt or
-garlic juice

Fry livers until well done. Put livers, eggs and onion through meat
grinder. Add fat or oil, salt, garlic; mix well. Add mayo to desired
consistency.
