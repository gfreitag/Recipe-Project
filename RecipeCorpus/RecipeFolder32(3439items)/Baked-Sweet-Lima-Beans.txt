Title: Baked Sweet Lima Beans
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Dried lima beans
2 ts Salt
3 tb Butter or fat
1/2 c  Honey
Soak the beans overnight in water to cover.  Drain.  Add fresh water
to cover.    Cover and bring to a boil; cook over low heat 1 1/2
hours, adding salt after 1 hour of cooking.  Drain.    Combine the
beans, butter or fat and honey in a baking dish.  Bake
