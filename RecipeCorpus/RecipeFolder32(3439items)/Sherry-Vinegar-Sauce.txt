Title: Sherry Vinegar Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sherry vinegar
1 tb Chicken stock
-or chicken glaze
4 tb Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Boil the vinegar in a saucepan over high heat until it has reduced by
half. Add the chicken stock or glaze and olive oil. Add salt and
pepper to taste. Set the sauce aside over low heat to keep warm.
Serve over grilled chicken.
