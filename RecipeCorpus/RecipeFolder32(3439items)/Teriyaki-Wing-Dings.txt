Title: Teriyaki Wing Dings
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry, Asian
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Brown sugar
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Pepper
3 lb Chicken wings**
**Drumettes or chicken wings, cut at joints and wing tips removed.
Combine all ingredients; mix well; add chicken. Cover and refrigerate
at least six hours or overnight, turning occasionally. Preheat oven
to 375 degrees. Arrange chicken on rack in aluminum-fool-lined
shallow baking pan. Bake 40 to 45 minutes, basting occasionally with
marinade. Refrigerate leftovers. Rita Taule's own.
