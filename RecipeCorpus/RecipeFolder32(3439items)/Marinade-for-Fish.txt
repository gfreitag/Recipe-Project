Title: Marinade for Fish
Categories: Fish, Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 /3 c  brow
1/4 c  Pickling spice
2 c  Light soy sauce
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Water
Onion powder
Garlic, Minced
Tabasco sauce
Pepper
1 1/2 c  Dry White Wine
Instructions:

Fillet fish.  Mix all ingredients and marinade fish with skin side
up. Cook on grill skin side down.
