Title: Cajun seasoning
Categories: Herbs/spice, Cajun/creol
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  (250ml) salt
1/4 c  (63ml) cayenne pepper
1/4 c  (63ml) black pepper
1/4 c  (63 ml granulated garlic
1/4 c  (63ml) onion powder
1 1/2 tb (23ml) paprika
1 1/2 tb (23ml) celery seed
[ note:  use only dried seasonings ]

In a food processor add salt and cayenne pepper. process for 30
seconds. Add black pepper and granulated garlic and process another
15 seconds. Add remaining ingredients and process another minute.
Store in air- tight container. Use for gumbo,jambalaya,etouffe, or
for your regular cooking. Can also be used like salt at the dinner
table. makes about 2-1/4 cups (563ml)
