Title: Noodle-Cheese Pudding
Categories: Jewish, Pasta, Cheese/eggs, Puddings
Yield: 6 servings
4    Eggs
3/4 c  Sour cream
1 ts Salt
2 tb Sugar
2 c  Cottage cheese
5 c  Cooked fine noodles
4 tb Dry bread crumbs
3 tb Melted butter
Beat the eggs, sour cream, salt and sugar together.  Stir in the
cheese and noodles.  Turn into a buttered 2-quart baking dish or
casserole. Sprinkle with the bread crumbs and butter.    Bake in a
375 degree oven 40 minutes. Serves 6. Recipe Source: THE ART OF
JEWISH COOKING by Jennie Grossinger Published by
