Title: Blitzkuchen (Lightning Cake)
Categories: Cake/cookie, Aust/german
Yield: 1 cake
1 c  Butter -OR-
3/4 c  Margarine
1 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour sifted WITH
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
Grated rind AND
Juice of 1 lemon
Sugar AND cinnamon
Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs one at a time.  Beat well, add
flour, lemon juice and rind and spread 1-inch thick on flat pan.
Sprinkle with a mixture of sugar and cinnamon ang bake 20 minutes at
