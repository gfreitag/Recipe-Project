Title: Seafood Stuffing for Salmon
Categories: Seafood, Dress/stuff, Fish
Yield: 12 servings
6 tb Butter
2 md Onions, minced
1 1/2 c  Mushrooms, finely chopped
3 ea Clove garlic, crushed
2 md Bunches parsley, chopped
1/2 c  Fine bread crumbs
4 oz Crabmeat, cut in small
-pieces
4 oz Shrimp, shelled and
-deveined, cut in smalll
-pieces
4 oz Scallops, cut in small
-pieces
2 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Cayenne
Salt and pepper to taste
Melt butter in med. saucepan, then add onions and mushrooms, saute
until soft.  Then add garlic, and saute until tender, but not
browned. Add parsley and remove from heat.

Stir in bread crumbs, and add assorted seafood, lemon juice, cayenne
and salt and pepper.

Fill salmon with mixture and bake as directed. Source: Official Expo
86 Cookbook
