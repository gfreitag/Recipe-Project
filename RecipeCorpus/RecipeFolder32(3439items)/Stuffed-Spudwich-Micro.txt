Title: Stuffed Spudwich Micro
Categories: Microwave, Potatoes, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
4 ea Potatoes (8 oz each)
1/2 c  Light mayonnaise
1/4 c  Green onions; sliced
1/4 c  Celery; sliced
1/4 c  Pickle relish
2 ts Prepared yellow mustard
1/4 ts Pepper
Salt to taste
4 ea Cooked ham; thin slices
Pierce potatoes with tines of fork; microwave on HIGH 18 to 20 minutes
until potatoes are fork tender; cool 10 minutes.

Halve potatoes and carefully scook pulp into bowl leaving 1/4-inch
thick shells; reserve shells. Mash potato pulp; mix in remaining
ingredients except potato shells and ham. Fill shells with potato
mixture. Sandwich 2 halves together with 1 folded ham slice in
middle. Wrap each "sandwich" in a paper napkin to eat out of hand.

Makes 4 servings

Nutritional Information Per Serving Calories: 280 Fat: 9 g
Cholesterol: 15 mg Sodium: 390 mg Carbohydrate: 46 g Fiber: 4 g
Protein: 7 g

Menu Suggestions Fresh Fruit Salad Pudding Cups

NOTE: Microwave cooking times are based on a 700 watt microwave.
Adjust cooking times to your own oven.
