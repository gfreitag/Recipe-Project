Title: Fluffy Knaidlach  <passover>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Passover
Yield: 1 servings
3    Egg yolks
1 ts Salt
3/4 c  Matzo meal
3    Egg whites
Beat the egg yolks and salt together.  Stir in the matzo meal and
chill 1 hour.  Beat the egg whites until stiff but not dry and fold
into the matzo-meal mixture.  Form into 18balls.  Cook in boiling
soup or salted water for 20 minutes. Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH
COOKING by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc. (c) 1958
