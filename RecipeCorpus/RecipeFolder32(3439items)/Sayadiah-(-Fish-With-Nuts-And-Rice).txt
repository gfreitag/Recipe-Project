Title: Sayadiah ( Fish With Nuts And Rice)
Categories: Fish
Yield: 4 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
1 c  Rice
1 tb Salt
1 lb Fish fillets frozen or
Fresh
Cooking oil
Salt and pepper to taste
2    Onions chopped small
Or more if you want
1/2 c  Pine nuts (Pignolias) or
Chopped almonds
6 lg Carrots
1 ts Cumin
1 c  Boiling water
Rinse the rice, place it in a bowl, cover it with hot water and salt,
and let stand for 1/2 hour. This ensures non sticky rice. If they are
frozen, thaw the fish fillets just enough to break them apart. brown
the fish slightly in oil and season them with
