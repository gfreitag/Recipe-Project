Title: Spicy Spareribs
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Paprika (Hungarian)
1 tb Season salt
1 ts Season salt
1 tb Pepper
1 ts Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Celery seed
3 lb Spareribs
1/2 c  White wine vinegar
Combine first 9 ingredients in a small bowl, and set aside. cut ribs
into serving size pieces. Pour Vinegar into a spray bottle, and mist
the ribs. Coat ribs with seasoning mixture. Grill about 5 inches
from medium coals for 1 hour, misting with vinegar
