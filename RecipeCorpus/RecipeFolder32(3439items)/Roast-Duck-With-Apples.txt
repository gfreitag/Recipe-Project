Title: Roast Duck With Apples
Categories: Jewish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
6 lb Duck
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
3 c  Diced apples
12    Pitted prunes
1    Egg
4 tb Dry bread crumbs
2 tb Brown sugar
Singe the duck to remove pin feathers; wash and dry. Make a paste of
the salt, pepper and minced garlic.  Rub into the duck.  Mix together
the apples, prunes, egg, bread crumbsand sugar.  Stuff the duck and
close the opening. Place on a rack in a roasting
