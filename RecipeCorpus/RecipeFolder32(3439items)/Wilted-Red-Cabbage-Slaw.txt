Title: Wilted Red-Cabbage Slaw
Categories: Jewish, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Red cabbage
3 c  Boiling water
1 tb Salt
4 tb Salad or olive oil
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ts Sugar
4 tb Minced onion
1/2 c  Grated apple
Shred the cabbage and combine with the boiling water and salt.  Let
stand 10 minutes.  Drain.    Mix the oil, lemon juice, pepper and
sugar together. Add to the cabbage with the onion and apple. Toss
until well mixed. Chill for 1/2 hour before serving.  Serves 6 to 8.
Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger
