Title: Pizella-Small Eggplant Pizza
Categories: Vegetables, Ethnic
Yield: 4 servings
1    Eggplant
Dried oregeno
Mozzarella cheese
1 c  Thick tomato pasta
Sliced thin
Sauce with spices
Salt
Grated parmesan chese
Dried Basil
Cut the eggplant crosswise into 3/8" slices. Then cut each slice into
quarters. Salt lightly on each side and set on paper towels for 20
minutes.Then pat dry pieces and arrange them on a PAMed cookie sheet.
Brush lightly with olive oil and sprinkle pieces Serve immediately.
