Title: Meat Borsch  <meat>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
3 qt Water
2 lb Brisket
Beef bones
8    Beets -- grated
2    Onions -- diced
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
1 tb Salt
3 tb Brown sugar
1/3 c  Lemon juice
2    Eggs -- beaten
Combine the water, meat and bones in a deep saucepan. Bring to a boil
and skim.  Add the beets, onions, garlic and salt. Cover and cook
over medium heat 2 hours.  Add the brown sugar and lemon juice. Cook
30 minutes. Taste to correct seasoning if necessary.
Beat eggs in a bowl.  Gradually add a little hot soup, beat steadily
to prevent curdling.  Return to sauce
