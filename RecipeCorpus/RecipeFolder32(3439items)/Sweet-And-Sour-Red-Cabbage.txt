Title: Sweet-And-Sour Red Cabbage
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
6 c  Shredded red cabbage
1/2 c  Grated apple
3/4 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1 tb Flour
4 tb Vinegar
1 1/2 tb Sugar
2 tb Butter or fat
Cook the cabbage, apple, water and salt over low heat 15 minutes.
Mix the flour and vinegar until smooth and add to the cabbage with
the sugar and butter or fat.  Cook 15 minutes longer.  Serves 6.
