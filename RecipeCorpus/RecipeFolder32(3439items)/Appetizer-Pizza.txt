Title: Appetizer Pizza
Categories: Kids, Appetizers
Yield: 14 servings
1 cn 7.5 oz buttermilk biscuits
1 md Onion; cut in rings
1 c  Zucchini; chopped
4 oz Mushrooms, can; drained
2 tb Margarine
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/4 c  Milk
1    Egg; beaten
1/2 ts Salt
Separate each biscuit into two layers.  Place on bottom and sides of
greased 12-inch pizza pan, pressing together to form crust.  Saute
onions, zucchini and mushrooms in margarine.  Combine cream cheese,
milk, egg and salt, mixing until well blended.  Stir in vegetables;
pour over crust. Bake at 400 F., 20 to 25 minutes or until crust is
golden brown. Serve warm. Source:  Philadelphia Brand Cream Cheese
