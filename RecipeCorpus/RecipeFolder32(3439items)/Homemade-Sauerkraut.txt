Title: Homemade Sauerkraut
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
12 lb Cabbage
4 tb Salt
3    Green apples
A wooden keg is best for sauerkraut, but if you don't have one, use a
crock or sterile glass jar.    Shred the cabbage finely (reserving
some large leaves) and mix with the salt.  Peel and shred the apples.
Arrange layers of the cabbage and apples, press
