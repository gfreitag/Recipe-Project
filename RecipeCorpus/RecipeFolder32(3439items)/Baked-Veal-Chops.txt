Title: Baked Veal Chops
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Matzo or cracker meal
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Eggs
4    Veal chops
4 tb Fat
2    Onions -- thinly sliced
Mix the meal, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt and the pepper together.  Beat the
eggs and remaining salt.  Dip the chops in the meal, the eggs and
then the meal again.    Heat the fat in a baking pan.  Arrange the
chops in it and surround with the sliced onions.  Ba
