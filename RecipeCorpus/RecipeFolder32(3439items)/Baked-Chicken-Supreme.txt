Title: Baked Chicken Supreme
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
4 c  Kellogg's Corn Flakes,crushe
To equal 2 cups
1 ts Paprika
1    Egg
3/4 c  Milk
3/4 c  All purpose flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Poultry seasoning
6    Chicken pieces,breasts,thigh
Or legs
3 tb Butter or margarine,melted
Mix together crushed corn flakes and paprika.Place in a shallow dish
and set aside.In another shallow dish beat together egg and milk.Add
flour,salt,pepper and poultry seasoning; mix until smooth.Dip chicken
in batter,then coat with crumb mixture.Place in single layer,skin
side up, in well-greased or foil-lined shallow baking pan. Drizzle
with melted butter. Bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour or until
chicken is tender.Do not cover pan or turn chicken while baking.

Suggestions; Different herbs and spices may be added to batter kor
crumb mixtkure.

From box of Kellogg's Corn Flakes
