Title: Marinara Sauce    (Fggt98b)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Low cal/fat
Yield: 7 servings
1    (46-ounce) can tomato juice
Or 2 teaspoons fresh
Low sodium
1/2    Teasp dried whole oregano
1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms -- sliced
Or 1 teaspoon fresh
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1 cl Garlic -- chopped
1/4 c  Chopped red pepper
1    Bay leaf
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Dried whole basil
SERVE OVER SPAGHETTI, RICE, OR NOODLES, OR AS A POTATO TOPPER Combine
all ingredients in a large Dutch oven, simmer, uncovered for 45
minutes. Remove bay leaf. YIELD: 3-1/2 cups (1/2 cup per serving)
