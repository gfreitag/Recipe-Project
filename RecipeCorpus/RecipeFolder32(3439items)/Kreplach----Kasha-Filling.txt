Title: Kreplach -  Kasha Filling
Categories: Jewish, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Minced onions
3 tb Chicken fat or butter -- *
See note
1 1/2 c  Cooked kasha -- ** see note
1/4 ts Pepper

dish.   ** Note:  The recipe for "Kasha" is included in this database.
