Title: Fruited Ice Cream Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Kids
Yield: 12 servings
1 qt Vanilla ice cream
1 1/3 c  Fruit, diced & drained
1 lg Package Jello; any flavor
Boiling water
Dissolve Jello in 2 cups boiling water.  Add 1 quart vanilla ice
cream by spoonfuls, stirring until ice cream is melted. Add fruit.
Chill until set, about 30 minutes. Source: Jello
