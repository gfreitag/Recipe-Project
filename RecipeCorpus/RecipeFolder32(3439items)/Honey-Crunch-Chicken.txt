Title: Honey Crunch Chicken
Categories: Low cal/fat, Poultry, Nuts/grains, Syrups
Yield: 3 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 4 SERVINGS--------------------------
1 1/4 lb Boneless skinless chicken
2 ea Tb Plus 2 ts lowfat
-mayonnaise
1 1/2 oz Grape Nuts cereal, crushed
1 ea Tb Plus 1 ts honey
Wash chicken and pat dry. Place in a small shallow baking pan. Using a
pastry brush, spread mayonnaise over both sides of chicken. Sprinkle
crushed cereal evenly over top of chicken. Drizzle evenly with honey.
Preheat oven to 375. Let stand at room temperature for 10 minutes.
Bake uncovered for 45 minutes. Per serving: 32g protein, 7g fat, 15g
carb., 258mg sodium, 103mg chol., 255 calories.
