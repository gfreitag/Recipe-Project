Title: Oriental Grilled Vegetables (Miracle Whip)
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Peanut butter
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Garlic powder
4 dr Hot pepper sauce, to taste
8 md Carrots, cut in half,
- lengthwise
2 ea Summer squash, cut in 1/2"
- diagonal pieces
2 ea Zucchini, cut in 1/2"
- diagonal pieces
1 ea Red onion, cut in thick
- slices
Mix all ingredients except vegetables.  Heat grill. Place
vegetables on grill over medium-hot coals.  Grill 10 to 15 minutes or
until vegetables are tender, turning occasionally and brushing with
dressing mixture.

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Grilling Time: 15 minutes
