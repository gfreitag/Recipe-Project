Title: Sun-dried Tomato Dressing
Categories: Tomatoes, Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 4 servings
-Waldine Van Geffen VGHC42A
8    Dried tomato halves
1/4 oz Flat anchovy fillets
2 cc 2
2 tb Lemon juice
4 ts Olive oil
1/2 ts Low-sodium soy sauce
1/4 ts Fresh ground pepper
Soak tomatoes in boiling water to cover, 2 minutes. Drain and reserve
water. In a mini processor, combine tomatoes, 2 T reserved soaking
liquid, anchovies and garlic; process until finely chopped. Add lemon
juice, oil, soy sauce and pepper; process until blended. Stir ini 2
more T soaking liquid. Cover and refrigerate until ready to use. (wrv)
