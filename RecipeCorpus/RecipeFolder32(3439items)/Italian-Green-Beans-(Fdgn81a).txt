Title: Italian Green Beans (Fdgn81a)
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Frozen french sliced
3 tb Italian style bread crumbs
Green beans (9oz)
1/8 ts Garlic powder
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
Cook green beans according to package. Drain. Toss with butter.
Sprinkle with crumbs and garlic powder and toss again. Place in
shallow casserole, sprinkle with parmesan cheese and bake, covered,
at 350 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes, or until cheese melts
