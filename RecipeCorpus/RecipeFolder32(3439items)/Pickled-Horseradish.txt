Title: Pickled Horseradish
Categories: Condiments, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
1 c  Finely ground horseradish
Root (use finest blade)
1 1/2 ts Brown sugar
3 tb Vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
Combine ingredients, and let stand overnight before using. It will be
hot, very hot, for several days so go easy. Makes 3/4 cup.
