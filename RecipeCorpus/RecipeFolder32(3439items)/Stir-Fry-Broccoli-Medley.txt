Title: Stir-Fry Broccoli Medley
Categories: Wok, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Pineapple juice
4 c  Broccoli florets
4    Sweet peppers, thin sliced
1    Onion, thin sliced
2 tb Oyster sauce
1 tb Low-sodium soy sauce
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1/4 c  Crushed pineapple
6 c  Hot cooked rice
Get the pineapple juice boiling in the wok. Add broccoli, peppers, and
onions. Toss to coat and stir-fry for 5 min.

Now, add oyster sauce, soy sauce, and garlic and stir-fry for 2 min.
Add the pineapple and heat though.
