Title: Dairy Tzimmes
Categories: Jewish, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Prunes
4 c  Boiling water
1 c  Farfel
1 ts Salt
2 tb Lemon juice
1/3 c  Honey
4 tb Butter
Wash the prunes and soak in the water for 1 hour.  Bring to a boil
and add the farfel, salt, lemon juice, honey and butter.    Place
mixture in baking dish.  Cover and bake in a 375 degree oven 45
minutes, removing the cover for the last 15 minutes.  Serv
