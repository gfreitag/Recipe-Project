Title: Hamburger Cheese Bake
Categories: Casseroles, Pasta, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
16 oz Tomato sauce
1 ts Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 c  Medium noodles; uncooked
1 c  Cream style cottage cheese
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/4 c  Sour cream
1/3 c  Green onion; sliced
1/4 c  Green pepper; chopped
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
In large skillet, cook meat and onion till meat is lightly browned and
onion is tender., Stir in tomato sauce, sugar, salt, garlic salt and
pepper. Remove from heat. Meanwhile, cook noodles according to package
directions, drain. Combine cottage cheese, cream cheese, sour cream,
green onion and green pepper. Spread half the noodles in a 11x7"
baking pan, top with a little of the meat mixture. Cover with cheese
mixture. Add remaining noodles and meat sauce. Sprinkle with parmesan
cheese. Bake at 350~ for 30 minutes. Makes 8-10 servings.
