Title: Veal Burgers
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Minced onions
6 tb Fat
4 sl White bread
1 1/2 lb Ground veal
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3    Eggs -- beaten
3/4 c  Matzo or cracker meal
Brown the onions in half the fat.  Wet the bread and mash until
smooth. Mix the browned onions, bread, veal, salt, pepper and 2 eggs.
Shape into hamburgers.  Dip in the remaining beaten egg and then in
the meal. Heat the remaining fat in a skillet.  Cook burgers over low
heat until browned on both sides.  S
