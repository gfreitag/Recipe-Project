Title: Cake - Marion Partridge^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Butter
3/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs, beaten
1/2 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
Beat butter and sugar together. Add eggs and beat well. Put vanilla
into milk and add to mixture. Gradually beat in flour and baking
powder, mix till smooth. pour into greased and floured pan and bake
in a moderate oven.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
