Title: Springerle (Anise Cookies)
Categories: Cake/cookie, Aust/german
Yield: 1 recipe
4 c  Flour
4 lg Eggs
2 c  Sugar
1 tb Butter
Anise seed OR
4    Drops anise oil
1/2 ts Baking powder
Beat eggs thoroughly; add sifted sugar and butter.  Beat for 15
minutes. Add enough sifted flour to make a stiff dough.  Roll out
about 1/2-inch thick.  Sprinkle with flour and press the patterns on
the dough.

Separate the cakes with a dough cutter and place on a board covered
with anise seed or use 4 drops of anise oil stirred into the dough.

Keep in a cool dry place overnight.  In the morning bake, in a
moderate oven, 350Ã¸, a light brown about 12 minutes.
