Title: Chicken Quiche
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 3 servings
1    9 inch unbaked pie shell
1/2 c  Diced chicken
3 tb Sliced almonds
1 1/2 c  Shredded Swiss cheese
2    Eggs, beaten
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Mace
1/8 ts Pepper
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
Put chicken, almonds, Swiss cheese in pie shell in that order.
Combine rest of the ingredients except Parmesan cheese.  Pour over
chicken, etc. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.  Bake in a 375 degree
oven for 30 minutes. Randy Rigg
