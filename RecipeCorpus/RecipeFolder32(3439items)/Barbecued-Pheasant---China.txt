Title: Barbecued Pheasant - China
Categories: Bbq/grill, Chinese, Game
Yield: 1 servings
2    Pheasants-whole
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2    Garlic cloves-minced
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Sugar
1 1/2 ts Five spice
2 tb Oil
Wash and dry pheasants. Mix together the soy sauce, garlic, salt,
pepper, sugar, five spices and oil. Rub the pheasant inside and out.
Let stand for one hour. Place on a rack in a shallow roasting pan.
Roast in a 425 oven for two hours or until tender and brown. Baste
and turn frequently. To serve, carve into joints or chop up into inch
wide pieces Chinese fashion.
