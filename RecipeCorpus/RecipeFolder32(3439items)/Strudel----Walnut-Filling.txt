Title: Strudel -  Walnut Filling
Categories: Jewish, Pastry, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Chopped walnuts
2 c  Chopped apples
1 c  Sugar
2 ts Cinnamon
3 tb Grated lemon rind
Spread the nuts on half the oiled dough (two recipes for strudel
dough are included in this database) and cover with the apples.
Sprinkle with the sugar mixed the cinnamon andlemon rind.  Roll up.
