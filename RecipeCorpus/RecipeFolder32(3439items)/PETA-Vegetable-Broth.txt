Title: PETA Vegetable Broth
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 2 quarts
1/2 ts Parsley
1/4 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Black pepper
1    Bay leaf
2 lg Carrots; slices
2    Stalks celery; sliced
2 md White onions; sliced
2 lg White or red potatoes;
-quartered
1 lg Clove garlic
1/2 ts Salt
Put the prepared vegetables and herbs in a large pot and add water to
cover.  Bring the vegetables to a boil, uncovered.  Lower the heat,
cover and simmer 1 1/2 hours.

Strain thru a colander for a thin broth or stock.  For a richer broth,
puree the mixture in a blender.
