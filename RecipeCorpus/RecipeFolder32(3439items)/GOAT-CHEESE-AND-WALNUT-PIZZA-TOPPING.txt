Title: GOAT CHEESE-AND-WALNUT PIZZA TOPPING
Categories: Main dish, Appetizers, Vegetarian
Yield: 2 servings
6 oz Fresh goat cheese
1/2 c  Walnuts
2 tb Walnut or safflower oil
CRUMBLE THE GOAT CHEESE and sprinkle it all over the pizza. In a
small bowl toss the walnuts with the walnut or safflower oil to coat.
Place the walnuts all over the pizza. Bake according to directions.

Makes Enough for 2 9-Inch Pizzas
