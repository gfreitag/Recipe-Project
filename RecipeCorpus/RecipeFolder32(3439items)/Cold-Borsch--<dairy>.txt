Title: Cold Borsch  <dairy>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
10 lg Beets -- peeled and grated
2 1/2 qt Water
1    Onion -- minced
2 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2    Eggs
1 c  Sour cream
Combine the beets, water, onion and salt in a saucepan. Bring to a
boil and cook over low heat 1 hour.  Add sugar and lemon juice.  Cook
10 minutes and taste to correct seasoning.    Beat the eggs in a
bowl. Gradually add the soup, stirring steadily to p boiled potatoes.
Garnish with sour cream. Makes about 2 quarts. Recipe Source: THE ART
OF JEWISH COOKING by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books,
