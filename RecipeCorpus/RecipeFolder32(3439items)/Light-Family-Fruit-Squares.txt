Title: Light Family Fruit Squares
Categories: Scot/irish, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 cake
8 oz Flour; self-rising
6 oz Butter or margarine
6 oz Caster sugar
3    Eggs
pn Salt
2 tb Milk
3 oz Dried fruit; mixed
Zest of orange; grated
Set oven to 350Ã¸F or Mark 4.  Line an 8 inch square cake tin with
buttered greaseproof paper. Cream together the butter or margarine
and sugar in a bowl.  Add the eggs one at a time, beating each well
into the mixture. Sift the flour and salt and fold in gradually with
the milk. Add the fruit and the orange zest, ensuring it is evenly
mixed in. Spoon the mixture into the tin and bake for 40 to 45
minutes until a skewer inserted into the cake comes out clean. Turn
on to a wire rack to cool and then cut into squares.
