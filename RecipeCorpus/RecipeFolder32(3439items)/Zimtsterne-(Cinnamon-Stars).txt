Title: Zimtsterne (Cinnamon Stars)
Categories: Cake/cookie, Aust/german
Yield: 1 recipe
1 lb Sifted sugar
6    Egg whites
1 lb Almonds
3/4 ts Cinnamon
Grated rind of 1 lemon
Grind almonds fine with brown skin left on.  Stir the sugar and
lemon rind into the beaten egg whites, and continue beating for 15
minutes, then add the cinnamon and mix.  Reserve a small part of this
mixture for top of cookies, then to the large portion, add the
almonds. Roll out about 1/4-inch in thickness, using only as much
flour as necessary. Cut with star-shaped cutter, brush each cookie
with the reserved egg white mixture. Place on well-greased baking
sheet and bake at 300Ã¸ until light brown.
