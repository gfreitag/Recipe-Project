Title: Spinach With Bechamel
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Guestchefs
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM--------------------------SPINACH-------------------------------
2 lb Spinach
1 c  White Sauce
MMMMM--------------------WHITE SAUCE BECHAMEL-------------------------
1 tb Butter
1 1/2 tb Flour
1 c  Milk -- warmed
SPINACH: Cook spinach briefly by either boiling or steaming. Drain and
squeeze out excess moisture. Mix with 1 cup white sauce and warm over
low heat, 3-to-5 minutes.

WHITE SAUCE (BECHAMEL): Melt the butter in a saucepan over low heat.
Add the flour and cook, stirring with a wooden spoon, 2-to-3 minutes.
