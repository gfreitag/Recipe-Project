Title: Orlando's Sicilian Baked Sausage (Gscf27a)
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
1    Red bell pepper --
Julienned
Diced with juice
1    Green bell pepper --
Julienned
1 1/2 lb Italian sausage
2    Onions -- sliced
Cut into 2" pieces
2 md Potatoes -- diced in
2 tb Dried basil
lg Chunks
Salt and pepper to taste.
28 oz Canned tomatoes (3 cups)
Combine all ingredients in large covered casserole and bake in a 350F
oven for 1 1/2 hours. Do not add any water. I use the hot sausage.
