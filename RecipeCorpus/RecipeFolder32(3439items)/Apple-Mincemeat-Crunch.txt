Title: Apple Mincemeat Crunch
Categories: Pastry, Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Moist cooked mincemeat
3    Apples, sliced thin
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Quick cooking oats
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Margarine
Butter an 8 inch round baking pan.  Pour in the mincemeat.  Arrange
apples on top of this.  Combine oatmeal, sugar, flour.  Cut in
margarine. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes.  Serve warm or
cold with whipped topping or ice cream.  Sprinkle the topping over
the apple mincemeat mixture before baking. Randy Rigg
