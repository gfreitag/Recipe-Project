Title: Talciu (Potato Loaf)
Categories: Potatoes, Romanian
Yield: 6 servings
6 c  Grated potatoes
1 x  Egg
1/2 c  Shortening
1 x  Salt & pepper, to taste
Combine and blend well. Pour into well greased 13x9x2 inch pan.
Spread shortening over the top. Bake at 325 for 1-1/2 hours.
