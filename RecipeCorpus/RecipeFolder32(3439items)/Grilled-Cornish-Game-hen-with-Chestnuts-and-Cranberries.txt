Title: Grilled Cornish Game hen with Chestnuts and Cranberries
Categories: Poultry, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
4    Cornish game hens (18oz ea)
6 tb Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
12    Canned, peeled
-whole chestnuts
1/3 c  Cranberries
-fresh or frozen
Brush the hens with 4 tbs of the olive oil and season with salt and
pepper. Grill over a hot grill for approximately 7 minutes per side.
If a grill is unavailable, bake the hens in the oven at 450F for 15
minutes. Remove and set aside.

In a skillet over medium-high heat, saut the chestnuts in the
remaining 2 tbs olive oil for 2-3 minutes. Add the cranberries and
continue to saut for another 2 minutes, until the chestnuts are
golden brown. Serve hot.
