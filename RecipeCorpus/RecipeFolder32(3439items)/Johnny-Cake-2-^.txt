Title: Johnny Cake 2 ^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Melted cracklings or fat
2    Eggs
1 ts Salt
2 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Corn meal
Beat eggs well and add salt, buttermilk and soda. Mix only to
moisten; do no over beat. Pour into greased baking tin and bake at
400 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
