Title: Mock Seafood Salad
Categories: Jewish, Seafood, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream
3 tb Cider vinegar
2 tb Anchovy paste
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Pepper
4 tb Minced scallions
2 tb Minced parsley
1    Garlic clove -- minced
1    Lettuce head -- shredded
4    Endive -- sliced
1 cn Tuna fish -- drained and
Flaked
Sliced tomatoes
Mix together the mayonnaise, sour cream, vinegar, anchovy paste, salt,
Worcestershire sauce, pepper, scallions, parsley and garlic. Add the
lettuce, endive and tuna fish. Toss and serve cold with sliced
tomatoes. Serves 4.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by
Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam B Recipe By
