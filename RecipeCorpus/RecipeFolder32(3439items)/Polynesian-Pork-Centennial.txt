Title: Polynesian Pork Centennial
Categories: Ethnic, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
6    Pork chops
1 cn Pineapple chunks in juice
1    Onion -- chopped
1/4 c  Celery leaves -- minced
1    Garlic clove -- minced
12    Prunes -- pitted
2 ts Soy sauce
1/2 ts Marjoram
1 c  Celery -- sliced diagonally
1 c  Rice
1 ts Ginger
1. Trim excess fat from chops; lightly grease heavy skillet wtih fat
from 1 chop. Brown chops on both sides; drain off excess fat. Season
lightly with salt and pepper. 2. Drain pineapple (medium can, use
sweetened or in its own juice as you desire). Combine the juice,
onion, celery leaves, garlic, prunes, soy sauce and marjoram. Pour
over chops. Simmer, covered, for 20-30 minute (depending on thickness
of chops). 3 Prepare rice according to package directions; add
ginger, stirring to blend. Serve pork over rice.

: Jo Anne Merrill
