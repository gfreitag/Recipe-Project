Title: Kartoffelsuppe Mit Milch (Potato Soup with Milk)
Categories: Soup/stew, Aust/german
Yield: 1 recipe
3    Potatoes
1 pt Milk
2 ts Chopped onion
1 tb Butter
1 ts Salt
1 tb Flout
1/8 ts White pepper
1 pn Celery salt
2 ts Chopped parsley
Boil potatoes until soft; beat potatoes with wire potato masher.
Scald milk and onion in double boiler.  Add hot liquid to potatoes.
Strain and return to double boiler.  Make white sauce of remaining 5
ingredients; add to potato mixture in double poiler and cook five
minutes more. Add chopped parsley just before serving.  (1 cup of
tomato juice may be added.)
