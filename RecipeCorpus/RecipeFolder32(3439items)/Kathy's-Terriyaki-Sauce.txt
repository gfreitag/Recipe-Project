Title: Kathy's Terriyaki Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 48 ounces
48 oz Soy sauce
3    Glugs white wine (apx 1/2 c)
1 1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
2    Swishes garlic powder (apx
2 tsp)
3 tb Fresh grated ginger
Mix together. Boil for 5 minutes. At this point you can cool and
bottle.

For great chicken wings, Add wings, boil for 5 - 10 minutes, until
cooked remove them to a baking pan or serving dish, sprinkle with
sesame seeds. Can serve immediately or make wings ahead and rewarm.

This sauce keeps well in the frig for several weeks. Will freeze and
can be reused or stored for later. Source: Kathy Hansen
