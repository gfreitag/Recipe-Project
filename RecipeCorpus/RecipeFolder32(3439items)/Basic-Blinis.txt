Title: Basic Blinis
Categories: Pcake/waffl
Yield: 42 blini
2 c  Milk
1/2 pk Yeast
2 ts Sugar
3 c  Flour
3    Eggs; separated
5 tb Butter; melted
1/2 ts Salt
Scald the milk and let cool til lukewarm. Add the yeast and stir. Add
the sugar and 1/2 the flour, mix, cover and let stand in a warm place
to rise until doubled in size. Beat the egg yolks with melted butter
and salt. Add to the batter. Add the rest of the flour and beat until
smooth. Let rise again. Beat the egg whites until stiff and _gently_
fold into the batter. Let batter stand 10 minutes before spooning
onto a hot griddle and frying. 1 Tbls portions will yield about 42
blinis or 6-7 generous portions. Submitted By JIM WELLER   On
07-08-95
