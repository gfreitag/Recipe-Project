Title: Peanut Butter & Honey Crunchies
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 10 servings
5 c  Multi-grain Cheerios
1 c  Spanish peanuts
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Banana chips
1 pk Vanilla pudding mix, not
Instant (4 serv)
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Peanut butter
Lightly butter jelly roll pan, 15 x 10.  Mix cereal, peanuts, raisins
and banana chips in large bowl.  Mix pudding mix and honey in 1 quart
saucepan. Heat to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly;
reduce heat. Boil and stir 1 minute; remove from heat.  Stir in
peanut butter. Pour over cereal mixture; toss until evenly coated.
Spread in pan. Refrigerate 30 minutes; break into bite-size pieces.
Store snack in covered container at room temperature or in
refrigerator.
