Title: Supa de Tarcan (Tarragon Soup)
Categories: Soup/stew, Romanian
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Veal or lamb
1 x  Onion, chopped
2 x  Celery stalks, chopped
1/4 c  Tarragon, fresh, chopped
1 pt Sour cream
1 tb Flour
1 qt Water
1 x  Salt & pepper, to taste
Place meat in a pot to boil, removing foam as it boils. Add onion,
tarragon and celery to soup when meat is boiled. Make a paste of sour
cream and flour, adding a little soup to thin, then return to soup.
Add salt & pepper.
