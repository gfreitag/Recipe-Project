Title: Green Pea Guacamole+
Categories: Dips/spread, Appetizers
Yield: 1 recipe
1    Pk. frozen green peas, (10
-oz.)
1 c  Avocado, peeled and cubed
1/2 c  Sour cream, nonfat
-alternative
1/4 c  Chopped scallions
2 tb Fresh cilantro
1 1/2 tb Fresh lime juice
1 tb Canned green chiles, drained
-and chopped
1/8 ts Pepper
1 sm Garlic clove, minced
Cook peas according to package directions; drain. Place peas and
remaining ingredients in food processor and process till smooth.
Spoon into bowl; cover and chill. serve with no-oil tortilla chips.
