Title: PETA Lentil Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Veg oil
1 md Onion; sliced
1 md Carrot; sliced
4 c  Hot vegetable broth
1 c  Lentils
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Thyme
2    Bay leaves
;salt to taste
1 tb Lemon juice
Heat the oil in a large pot over med heat, then saute the onion and
carrot for about 5 mins, or until tender.  Add the broth, lentils,
pepper, thyme, bay leaves and salt.  Cover and simmer until lentils
are tender, about 40-50 mins.  Stir in the lemon juice and extra salt
to taste. From the Compassionate Cook by PETA and Ingrid Newkirk
