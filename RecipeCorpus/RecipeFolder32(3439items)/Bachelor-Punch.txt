Title: Bachelor Punch
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 20 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
2 c  Sweetened condensed milk
2 c  Cognac
1 c  Vodka
2 c  Creme de cacao
Raisins
With a electric beater, beat the condensed milk with the cognac and
vodka. Beat in the cream de cacao, Serve the punch, well chilled, in
small punch cups with a sprinle of raisinson top. It can be stored
covered.
