Title: PETA Onion Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 3 cups
1 lb Soft tofu
2 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Soy sauce; or tamari
1 pk Vegan dry onion soup mix
In a blender or food processor, combine tofu, lemon juice, sugar and
soy sauce or tamari and blend until very smooth.  Pour the mixture
into a serving bowl and stir in the soup mix.  Stir well and
refrigerate for 4 hours.

Serve with chips or raw veggies.
