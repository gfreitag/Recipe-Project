Title: PLANTATION STEW
Categories: Meats, Soup/stew
Yield: 7 servings
3 1/2 lb Lamb-stew meat
1/2 c  Olive oil
6    Potatoes-cubed
2    Red bell peppers,diced
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Thyme
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Orange juice
1 tb Lea & perrins
1 tb Parsly-chopped
1/4 c  Sherry
2 qt Chicken stock
Heat oil and saute lamb till brown on all sides add the stock and
simmer for 2 hours add the remaining ingredients,expect for the shery
simmer for 1/2 hour more,add the sherry just before serving.
