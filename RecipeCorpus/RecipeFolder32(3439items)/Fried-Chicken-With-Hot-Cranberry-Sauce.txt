Title: Fried Chicken With Hot Cranberry Sauce
Categories: Jewish, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
2    Broiler chickens (1 1/2 lb
Each)
2 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 1/2 c  Flour -- sifted
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1    Egg
1/2 c  Water
Fat -- for deep frying
1 c  Canned whole cranberries
1/2 c  Applesauce
Have the broilers cut up.  Season with 2 teaspoons salt, the pepper,
and sprinkle with 3 tablespoons flour.    Sift the remaining flour,
salt and the baking powder into a bowl. Beat in the egg and water.
Dip the chicken pieces in this batter, coating them well. Heat the
fat to 370 degrees and fry a few pieces at a time in it. Drain and
place in a baking pan. Cover and bake in a 350 degree oven for 30
minutes, remo for the last 10 minutes. Combine the cranberries and
applesauce and heat. Serve with the fried chicken.  Serves 6 to 8.
Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING by JennieGrossinger
