Title: Frito Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1    Head of lettuce, chopped
1 sm Onion, chopped
14 1/2 oz Fritos, crushed
1 lb Cheddar cheese, grated
1 lg Can of Ranch Style brown
-beans, rinsed and drained
Mix with 1 small bottle of Catalina salad dressing. Randy Rigg
