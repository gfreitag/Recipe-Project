Title: Perfumed Rice (Com Gao Thom)
Categories: Rice, Vietnamese
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Thai perfumed rice
2 1/2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1.  Wash the rice several times and drain; when the washing is clear,
the rice is as clean as it can possible be.  In large families rice
needs washsing with both hands but cooking for a nuclear family does
not require more than the rubbing of rice betwee

2.  Put the rice and water in a saucepan, add the salt and bring
quickly to a boil.  Reduce the heat immediately and cover.  Allow to
simmer for as long as necessary - 30 minutes is about right.  The
rice should be taken off the heat and allowed to rest f
