Title: Honeyed Herring
Categories: Jerusalem, Fish
Yield: 10 servings
7    Pat dwigans -- nathan and
Goldman
6    Salt herrings -- cleaned
Soaked overnight and
Filleted -- with each
Fillet halved
1 1/2    To 2 cups water
2 lg Onions
1/2 c  Sultana raisins
2 tb Liquid honey
ds Cinnamon
ds Ground ginger
1    Lemon -- peeled and sliced
Thin
Soak herrings again while preparing sauce. Sauce: bring water to
boil, add onions and raisins, and simmer slowly for 2 hours, addig
more water if necessary. When the onions are tender, add honey,
spices and lemon slices. cook for10 minutes. remove lemon slices.
Drain fillets, roll up and fasten with toot
