Title: Honeyed Carrots
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Butter or fat
4 c  Sliced carrots
3 tb Orange juice
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ginger
4 tb Honey
Combine all the ingredients in a saucepan.  Cover and cook over low
heat 25 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serves 6.    Recipe Source:
THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam
Books, Inc.
