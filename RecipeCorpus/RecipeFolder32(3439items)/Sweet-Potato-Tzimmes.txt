Title: Sweet-Potato Tzimmes
Categories: Jewish, Meats, Potatoes, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Fat
3 lb Brisket
4 c  Boiling water
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Nutmeg
8    Carrots -- sliced
4    Sweet potatoes -- peeled,
Quartered
1/3 c  Brown sugar
Melt the fat in a Dutch oven or casserole and brown the beef.  Add the
water, salt, pepper and nutmeg.  Cover and cook over low heat 1 1/2
hours. Add the carrots, sweet potatoes and brown sugar.  Cover and
bake in a 350 degree oven 2 hours, removing the
