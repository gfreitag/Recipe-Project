Title: German Potato Salad 3
Categories: Aust/german, Salads, Potatoes, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 8 SERVINGS--------------------------
5 ea Bacon strips
3/4 c  Onion, chopped
2 tb All-purpose flour
2/3 c  Cider vinegar
1 1/3 c  Water
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
6 c  Potatoes, sliced, cooked
-and peeled
In a large skillet, fry bacon until crisp; remove and set aside.
Drain all but 2-3 tablespoons of drippings; cook onion until tender.
Stir in flour; blend well.  Add vinegar and water; cook and stir
until bubbly and slightly thick.  Add sugar and stir until is
dissolves. Crumble bacon; gently stir in bacon and potatoes. Heat
through, stirring lightly to coat potato slices.  Serve warm.
