Title: Velveeta Hamburger & Noodle Casserole
Categories: Meats, Casseroles, Pasta, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Noodles; dry
1 lb Ground beef
1 sm Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Milk
Salt & pepper as desired
1/2 c  Velveeta cheese; cut up
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
Cook noodles al dente. Brown ground beef and onion. Drain grease. Mix
together with noodles. Add the rest of the ingredients. Cheese may be
mixed in or sprinkled on top. Bake 1 hour at 350~.
