Title: Chicken Riganato
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
MARY SUE DAY
2 1/2 lb Fryer
1 ts Oregano
Salt & pepper to taste
1 c  Water
Juice of 2 lemons
1    Garlic clove -- - minced
1/2    Block butter
2 tb Olive oil
(RBCK34E)
Rub chicken w/juice of 1 lemon, salt & pepper. Mix remaining lemon
juice w/oregano, garlic, & additional salt & pepper, & rub cavity of
chicken. Place in pan w/butter & water, andbrush w/oil  Bake in oven
450F. until golden brown; lower heat to 350F. Bake Variation: Omit
butter. Season chicken as directed above. Place in roasting pan w/1c.
water. Mix together 1/2c. olive oil & 2T. tomato paste; pour over
chicken and bake.
