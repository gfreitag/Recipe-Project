Title: Knishes -  Meat Filling
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Minced onions
2 tb Chicken fat
1 1/2 c  Ground cooked meat
1/2 c  Cooked rice
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Lightly brown the onions in the fat.  Add the meat, rice, egg, salt
and pepper, mixing until smooth.  Place on dough.    Recipe Source:
THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam
