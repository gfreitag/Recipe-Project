Title: Italian Potatoes
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 lb Butter -- (melted)
1 tb Heaping parsley
1/4 c  Oil
1 ts Crushed red peppers
2    Cloves -- minced garlic
1/4 c  Parmeson cheese
1 md Onion -- chopped
ea potatoes; (cut in quarters) In casserole add butter, oil,
3-4
garlic, onions and potatoes. Stir ingredients Sprinkle parsley,
peppers, cheese and salt Bake 1 her at 350, might take longer.
Potatoes should be crispy.  Good luck! FROM: JEANNIE BAUER
