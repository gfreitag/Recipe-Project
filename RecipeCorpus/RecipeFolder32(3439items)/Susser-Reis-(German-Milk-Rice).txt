Title: Susser Reis (German Milk Rice)
Categories: Aust/german, Desserts
Yield: 1 recipe
1 c  Washed rice
4 c  Water
1 pt Rich milk
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Butter
2    Egg Yolk
Boil rice until tender.  Drain off water.  Add milk, sugar and
butter. When done, remove from fire, and carefully beat in yolks of
eggs. Turn into dish and sprinkle liberally with sugar and cinnamon.
(Egg yolks may be omitted.)

~ Mrs. H. F. Ransleben
