Title: Yeast Crumb Cake
Categories: Jewish, Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk (or cake) Yeast
1/3 c  Sugar
2 tb Lukewarm water
1/3 c  Lukewarm milk
2 c  Flour -- sifted
4 tb Butter
2    Eggs
1/4 ts Salt
3 tb Melted butter
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
4 tb Melted butter
4 tb Sugar
2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Flour -- sifted
Combine the yeast, 1 tablespoon sugar and the water in a small bowl.
Set aside for 5 minutes.  Stir in the milk and 1/2 cup flour.  Cover
and set aside to rise in a warm placeuntil double in bulk.    Cream
the butter, gradually adding the remaining sugar
