Title: Preffernuesse (Cookies)
Categories: Cake/cookie, Aust/german
Yield: 80 lg.soft
2 tb Butter
2 c  Sugar
4 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Cloves
2 ts Nutmeg
pn Black pepper
4    Eggs
3 c  Flour WITH
2 ts Baking powder added
1 c  Chopped pecans
Cream butter and sugar.  Mix cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and pepper and
add to creamed mixture.  Add eggs, flour and baking powder, and pecans
tomixture.

Drop cookies on ungreased cookie sheet and bake at 350Ã¸ for 12 - 15
minutes.

This makes 80 large soft cookies or about 156 small cookies of harder
texture.
