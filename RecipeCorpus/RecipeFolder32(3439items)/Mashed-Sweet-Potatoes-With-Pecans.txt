Title: Mashed Sweet Potatoes With Pecans
Categories: Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
6 md Sweet potatoes -- approx.
-2 lbs
2    Eggs
2 tb Barley malt syrup -- or
3 tb Sugar
2 tb Butter -- melted
pn Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Orange juice
2 tb Honey
1/2 c  Pecan halves
Preheat oven to 425F. Bake sweet potatoes until tender - about 45-60
minutes (or bake in microwave for approx 20 min or until tender).
Remove skin and discard. Reduce oven to 375. Mash pootatoes and beat
in eggs, syrup, one tablesppon margarine, salt and cinnamon. Add
enough orange juice to moistend and make fluffy. Spoon into a 1 1/2
quart baking dish and drizzle with honey and remaining one tablespoon
margarine. sprinkle with pecans and bake for 20 min. Serve warm.
