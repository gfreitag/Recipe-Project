Title: Mavim's Knaidlach  <passover>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Passover
Yield: 1 servings
4    Egg yolks
1 ts Salt
1 ds Cayenne pepper
2 ts Grated onion
2 tb Chicken fat -- melted
4    Egg whites -- stiffly
Beaten
3/4 c  Matzo meal
Beat together the egg yolks, salt, cayenne pepper, onion and fat until
creamy; fold into the egg whites.  Gradually fold the matzo meal into
the egg mixture.  Chill 1 hour. Moisten hands and shape the mixture
into 1/2-inch balls. Cook in a covered saucepa
