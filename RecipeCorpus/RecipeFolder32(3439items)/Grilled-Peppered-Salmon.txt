Title: Grilled Peppered Salmon
Categories: Seafood, Bbq/grill
Yield: 2 servings
2 ea (6-oz.) Salmon steaks
1 ts Cracked black pepper*
x  Olive oil or canola spray
x  Salt, to taste
*  Do not substitute regular pepper.

Cover salmon and microwave on high for 1 minute.  Pat steaks dry to
remove juices.  Press 1/4 tsp. pepper into each side of steaks and
spray with olive oil.  Grill 2 minutes on each side.  Squeeze on lime
juice and serve.

Per steak:  254 calories, 33g protein, 7g carbohydrates, 0.3g fiber,
12g fat (1.8g saturated), 80mg sodium.
