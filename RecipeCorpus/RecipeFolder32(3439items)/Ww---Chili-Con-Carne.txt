Title: Ww - Chili Con Carne
Categories: Main dish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1    Beef Broth Mix - WW
Seeded and diced
12 oz Beans, drained, canned
1/2 c  Tomato puree
Pinto or red kidney
1/4 c  Onion -- diced
8 oz Pork, cooked -- dice
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
1 c  Tomatoes, canned -- crushed
1 ts Oregano leaves
1    Pepper, med. green bell
1/8 ts Cumin -- ground
In a small bowl combine broth mix with reserved liquid from canned
beans and enough water to make 1 cup.  In a large saucepan combine
broth mixture and remaining ingredients; simmer, stirring
occasionally, about 4 minutes or until desired consistency.

Each serving provides:  3 1/2 protein,  1 1/2 vegetable and 5
optional cal.
