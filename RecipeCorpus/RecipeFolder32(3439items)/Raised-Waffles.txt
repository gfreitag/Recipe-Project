Title: Raised Waffles
Categories: Breads, Breakfast, Guestchefs
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Warm water
1 pk Dry yeast
2 c  Milk -- warmed
1/2 c  Butter -- melted
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
2 c  All-purpose flour
2    Eggs
1/4 ts Baking soda
USE A RATHER LARGE mixing bowl--the batter will rise to double its
original volume. Put the water in the mixing bowl and sprinkle in the
yeast. Let stand to dissolve for 5 minutes. Add the milk, butter,
salt, sugar and flour to the yeast mixture and beat
