Title: Broccoli-Rice Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Jo Ferry
1 c  Minute Rice; uncooked
8 oz Cheese Whiz***
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1 pk Chopped broccoli; thawed
1/2 c  Milk
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
***You can substitute 1 can cheddar cheese soup. Mix everything
together in a casserole dish and bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 45
minutes.
