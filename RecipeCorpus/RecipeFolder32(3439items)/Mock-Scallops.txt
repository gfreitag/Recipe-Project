Title: Mock Scallops
Categories: Jewish, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Halibut, 1/2" thick
3/4 c  Flour -- sifted
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
1 c  Milk
6 tb Butter
Cut the halibut in 1/2-inch cubes to resemble bay scallops.    Mix
together the flour, salt, pepper and paprika.  Dip the cubes in the
milk and then in the seasoned flour.    Cook the scallops in the
butter over low heat, until browned on all sides.  Serv
