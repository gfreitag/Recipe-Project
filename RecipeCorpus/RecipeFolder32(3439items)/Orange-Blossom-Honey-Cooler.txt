Title: Orange-Blossom Honey Cooler
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Orange-blossom honey
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Orange juice
1 c  White rum
1 ts Grated nutmeg
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
2 sl Orange, halved
2 sl Lemon, halved
Combine the honey, lemon juice, orange juice, rum, and nutmeg with
ice and shake well until the honey is blended in. Serve  cup of the
mixture poured over 1 cup ice to each person as a aperitif. Garnish
each glass with half slices of orange and lemon.
