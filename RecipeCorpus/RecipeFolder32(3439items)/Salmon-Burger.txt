Description:

Serve salmon burgers as you would your favorite hamburger-with all the
trimmings.

Instructions:

Drain salmon, reserve 2 tablespoons liquid; flake.  Combine salmon,
egg, onion, green pepper, bread crumbs, reserved liquid, lemon juice,
lemon peel and seasonings; mix well.  Form into 4 or 5 patties.
Pan-fry in lightly oiled skillet about 5 minutes or until lightly
browned; turn halfway through cooking time.  Top with choice of
toppings.

Source/Author: Alaska Seafood Marketing Institute / typo Bob Shiells

Notes:

Substitute egg white for whole egg for lower total fat and cholesterol
content.
