Title: Matzo-Farfel Charlotte  <passover>
Categories: Jewish, Desserts, Passover
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Matzo farfel
3    Egg yolks
2/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Sweet red wine
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Grated orange rind
2 tb Salad oil
3    Egg whites -- stiffly
Beaten
Soak the farfel in cold water for a few minutes, then drain and crush
to a paste.  Beat the egg yolks and sugar together until thick.  Stir
in the wine, salt, orange rind, oil and farfel.  Fold in the egg
whites.  Turn into a greased 1 1/2-quart baking di
