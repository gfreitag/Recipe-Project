Title: Pineapple Sweet Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Desserts, Breads
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Warm water
1/4    Stick unsalted butter -- cut
Into pieces
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Sour cream
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
2 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 tb Yeast
4 oz Dried pineapple -- chopped
Place all the ingredients except dried pineapple in the machine,
program for white bread and push start.  Add the dried pineapple when
the timer on Hitachi reads 3:41.  The finished bread is featherly
light with a solid texture.
