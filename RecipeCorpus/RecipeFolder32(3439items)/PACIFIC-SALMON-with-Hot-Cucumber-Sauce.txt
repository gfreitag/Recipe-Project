Title: PACIFIC SALMON with Hot Cucumber Sauce
Categories: Seafood, Microwave
Yield: 4 serving
1 1/2 lb Pacific Salmon steak
1/2 c  Sour Cream
1/2 c  Cucumber, chopped
2 ts Green onion, chopped
1/2 ts Dill weed
1/2 ts Salt
pn Pepper (white preferred)
Description:

Moist and Flavourful Salmon Steaks with a creamy, nicely textured
sauce in 10 minutes flat.

Instructions:

In a 12X8X2" microwave baking dish arrange slamon steaks with thickest
parts in the corners.  Dampen 4 squares of paper towel.  Layering one
over the other, cover the dish.  Microwave at HIGH for 6-7 min.  Let
stand. Test for doneness.  Juices should run clear and flesh should
be opaque when fork-tested.  Meanwhile combine remaining ingredients
in a small microwave-safe bowl.  Micowave at 50% power for 2 1/2 min.
or until evenly heated, stirring twice during cooking period.  Pour
hot sauve over slamon and serve immediately.
