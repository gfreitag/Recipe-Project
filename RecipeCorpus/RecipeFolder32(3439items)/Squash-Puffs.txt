Title: Squash Puffs
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 22 servings
1 c  Squash -- cooked
1    Egg -- beaten
1/3 c  Flour
1/3 c  Cornmeal
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt -- or more
ds Pepper
1 md Onion -- grated
1 1/2 c  Cooking oil
Combine squash and egg.  Add all other ingredients except oil and mix
until well blended.  Heat oil for deep frying.  Drop batter by level
tablespoonful into hot oil.  Deep fry until golden brown.  Drain on
paper towels.

Source: " Favorite Recipes from Quilters" by Louise Stoltzfus
