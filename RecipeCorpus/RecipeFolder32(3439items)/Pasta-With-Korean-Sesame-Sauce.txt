Title: Pasta With Korean Sesame Sauce
Categories: Side dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
10 oz Angel hair pasta
1 tb Dark sesame oil
3 tb White vinegar
2 tb Hot bean paste
2 tb Soy sauce
3 tb Green onions -- sliced
2    Cloves garlic -- finely
Chopped
2 ts Ginger root -- grated
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Pepper
1 md Cucumber -- thinly sliced
Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente.  Drain.
Combine sesame oil, vinegar, bean paste, soy, onion, garlic, ginger,
sugar and pepper in medium-size bowl.  Heat large skillet over medium
heat.  Add sesame seeds; cook, shaking skillet occasionally, 2 to 3
minutes or until toasted and golden.  Add pasta along with vinegar
mixture to skillet, tossing to coat.  Garnish with cucumbers and
serve.  172 calories and 8 grams fat.
