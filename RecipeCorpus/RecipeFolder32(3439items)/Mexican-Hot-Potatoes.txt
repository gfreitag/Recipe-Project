Title: Mexican Hot Potatoes
Categories: Tex/mexican, Meats, Poultry, Side dish, Potatoes
Yield: 2 servings
1 lb Lean Ground Beef or Turkey
Small Onion; chopped
Garlic Clove; minced
1 c  Sour Cream OR Sour Half/Half
1/3 c  Chunky Hot Salsa
1 ts Oregano
1/8 ts Chili Powder
1/2 ts Salt
4 ea Baked Potatoes
In large skillet, brown beef with onion and garlic. Drain. Stir in
sour cream, salsa, oregano, chili powder and salt. Serve over hot
baked potatoes. Makes about 2-1/2 cups
