Title: Blintzes -  Meat Filling
Categories: Jewish
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Ground cooked meat
2 tb Grated onion
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Minced parsley
Mix all of the ingredients together.  Fill pancakes.    Recipe
