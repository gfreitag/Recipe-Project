Title: Crispy Frog Legs
Categories: Game, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
5 lb Small [?] frog legs
3/4 c  Lemon juice or vinegar
Crushed ice
1 c  Milk
6 ea Eggs, separated
2 tb Olive or vegetable oil
1/4 ts Salt
Salt & pepper
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
Vegetable oil
Wash frog legs thoroughly. Place in a large Dutch oven; sprinkle
with lemon juice, and cover with crushed ice. Refrigerate for 1 to 3
hours.
Combine milk, egg yolks, olive oil, and 1/4 teaspoon salt; mix
well. Beat egg whites until stiff; fold into batter.
Sprinkle frog legs with salt and pepper; dip each in batter, and
dredge in flour.  Fry until golden brown in deep oil heated to 375
degrees F. Drain on paper towels. Recipe from Orange Lake, Florida in
"Southern Living", May 1980 Typos by JP
