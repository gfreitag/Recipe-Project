Title: MEMPHIS IN MAY BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Herbs/spice, Bbq/grill, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
6 tb Salt
6 tb Black pepper
6 tb Chili powder
4 c  Ketchup
4 c  White vinegar
4 c  Water
1 lg Yellow onion; diced
1/2 c  Sorghum molasses
BBQ SAUCE DIRECTIONS:

Combine all ingredients in a large saucepan.  Bring to a rolling boil,
reduce heat and simmer for 1-1/2 hours, stirring every 10 minutes or
so. Pour into steralized jars, seal and let stand for 2 to 6 weeks
before using.
Makes 62 oz. sauce.

1991 Memphis in May contest.
