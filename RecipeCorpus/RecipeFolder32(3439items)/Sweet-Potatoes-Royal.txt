Title: Sweet Potatoes Royal
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Dried apricots
1 c  Brown sugar
2 lb Sweet potatoes -- cooked and
Peeled
1/4 c  Melted butter or fat
1/2 c  Blanched almonds -- sliced
Wash the apricots and soak in 2 cups of water for 2 hours.  Bring to
a boil and cook over low heat 20 minutes or until tender. Stir in the
sugar. Slice the potatoes 1/2-inch thick. Arrange layers of the
potatoes and undrained apricots in a casserole.  Pour the butter or
fat over the top. Bake in a 375 degree oven 35 minutes, basting
twice. Sprinkle with the almonds and bake 10minutes longer. Serves 8.
