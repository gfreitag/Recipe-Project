Title: Bean Panache
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1    Onion -- chopped
2 tb Butter
1/2 lb Green beans -- cooked and
Drained
1/2 lb Wax beans -- cooked and
Drained
1/2 lb Lima beans -- cooked and
Drained
Salt and pepper -- to taste
1 tb Chopped parsley
Cook the onion in the butter for 10 minutes.  Toss with the green,
wax and lima beans.  Add salt and pepper to taste and sprinkle with
the parsley. Serves 4 to 6.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH
COOKING by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc.
