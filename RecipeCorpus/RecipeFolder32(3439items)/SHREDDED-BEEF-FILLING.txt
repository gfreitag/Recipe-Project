Title: SHREDDED BEEF FILLING
Categories: Meats, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
1 md Onion -- chopped
1 1/2 tb Vegetable oil
2 c  Finely chopped or shredded
-cooked
-lean beef (leftover pot
-roast are good)
1 cn 4 oz chopped green chilies
1 ts Ground cumin
1 c  Canned or homemade enchilada
-sauce
Saute onion in hot oil in a skillet.  Blend in beef, chilies, cumin
and enchilada sauce.  Simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Use
for Chimichangas, tacos, enchiladas or tamales
