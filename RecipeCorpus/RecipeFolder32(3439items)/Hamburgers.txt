Title: Hamburgers
Categories: Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 1 recipe
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1 md Onion, finely chopped
1    Clove garlic, minced
4 tb HEINZ's 57 Sauce
3    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Graham crackers, finely
-crushed
In a large bowl, mix all of the ingredients and form into patties.

Cook outside on a BBQ grill.
