Title: Sweet Potatoes Hawaiian
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Butter or fat
6    Sweet potatoes -- peeled
And cut in half lengthwise
1 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Crushed pineapple --
Undrained
Melt the butter or fat in a skillet; brown the potatoes in it.  Add
the brown sugar and pineapple.  Cook over low heat 45 minutes or
until tender.
Turn the potatoes frequently.  Serves 6 to 8.    Recipe Source: THE
ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Gross
