Title: French Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Oldies
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Paprika
OR
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Prepared mustard
4 tb Olive oil
1 1/2 tb Vinegar
In a shallow dish, mix salt and pepper. Add the mustard and the oil;
stir well to mix. Add the vinegar, a little at a time, beating the
mixture continuously with a fork. Serve as soon as it is mixed well.
