Title: Spiced German Pot Roast  Grdg72b
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Shortening
1 ts Salt
4 lb Beef chuck roast
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Catsup
1 c  Water
2 tb Vinegar
2    Garlic cloves -- crushed
H-------------------Gravy Melt shortening in a large skillet, or
large pan you can bake in. Brown meat over medium heat about 15
minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Reduce heat;add water to
skillet or baking pan, and cover tightly . Place in 325 degree Place
meat on warm platter; keep warm while making gravy. Serve gravy with
meat. 6-8 serv. Spicy Gravy Pour drippings into bowl, leaving brown
particles in pan. Let fat rise to top of drippings; skim off fat,
reserving 1/4 cup. Place reserved fat in pan.
