Title: Baked Tomatoes Stuffed with Leeks
Categories: Side dish, Tomatoes, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
6 md Tomatoes
1 lg Or 2 small leeks
4 tb Butter
Salt & freshly ground black
-pepper
Tabasco sauce
3/4 c  Grated Swiss cheese
6 tb Dry bread crumbs
3 ts Butter
3 tb Chopped parsley
Preheat oven to 400 F.
Grease a shallow baking dish or pan large enough to hold the 6
tomatoes.
Wash tomatoes and cut off stem end (not too close). Using a
teaspoon, scoop out seeds and pulp and discard. Turn tomatoes upside
down on paper towels to drain and place in baking dish.
Wash leeks very thoroughly and pat dry.  Cut away the tough green
tips. Lay the leek pieces flat on a cutting board and slice crosswise
very fine.
Melt the 4 tablespoons butter in a saute' pan, add leeks and cook
until soft (do not overcook). Season with salt, pepper, and Tabasco.
Spoon 1/6 of the cooked leeks into each tomato, then a tablespoon
of cheese and a tablespoon of breadcrumbs over the cheese.  Place a
half-teaspoon of butter on top of each tomato.  Lower oven
temperature to 350 F and bake for 12 minutes.
Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve.
