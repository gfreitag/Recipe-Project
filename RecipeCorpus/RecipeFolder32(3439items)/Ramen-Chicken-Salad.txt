Title: Ramen Chicken Salad
Categories: Poultry, Salads, Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
3 lb Shredded cabbage
1 bn Green onions chopped
1 lb Seasoned diced chicken
2 oz Sliced almonds
2 ea Packages chicken ramen
-(save seasoning packets
-for dressing)
1 ea Ounce toasted sesame seeds
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/3 c  Oil
1 c  Red wine vinegar
2 ea Packets seasoning from ramen
1/4 c  Soy sauce
Salt, pepper, sugar to taste
Mix cabbage, green onions, chicken, almonds, ramen,  and sesame seeds.
together, set aside. In a small bowl mix oil, vinegar, seasoning
packets from ramen, soy sauce, salt, pepper and sugar to taste. Pour
over salad mixture and toss well.
