Title: Carrot And Apple Tzimmes
Categories: Jewish
Yield: 6 servings
4 c  Grated carrots
1 tb Fine barley
3/4 c  Grated apples
8 tb Butter or fat
1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Combine all the ingredients in a sauce pan.  Cover and cook over low
heat 2 hours, or until the barley is soft. Watch carefully while
cooking and add more water if necessary.  Serves 6.    Recipe Source:
THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Pu
