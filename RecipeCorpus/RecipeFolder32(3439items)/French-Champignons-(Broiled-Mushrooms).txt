Title: French Champignons (Broiled Mushrooms)
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
1 1/4 lb Mushrooms -- cleaned and
Halved lengthwise
2    Onions sliced thin
Corn oil
1    Beef or chicken bouillon
Cube -- crumbled
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tb Flour -- optional
1/2 c  Light cream -- optional
Water -- optional
saute the onion slices in hot corn oil until they are slightly brown.
Add the mushrooms and cover. Cook over low heat for 5-7  minutes.
Stir in the crumbled bouillon cube and add salt and pepper to taste.
If a thiccker mixture is desired, as for an omele
