Title: Prime Minister Eggplant A La Golda
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
1 md Large eggplant
Salt
3 lg Eggs
2 ts Water
3/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
Oil for frying
1 c  Tomato puree
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ts Sugar
2 tb Lemon juice
Peel the eggplant and slice it thinly crosswise. Sprinkle, each slice
with salt and set it aside so that the bitter juice can drain off.
beat the eggs with waer, salt and cheese. Dip each eggplant slice in
the egg-cheese batter and fry it on both sides in very hot oil Remove
and set it aside on a paper
