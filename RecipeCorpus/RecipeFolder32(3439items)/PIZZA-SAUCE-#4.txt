Title: PIZZA SAUCE #4
Categories: Pizza, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Tomato paste
1/4 pt Oregano
3 c  Water
1/4 pt Basil
3 pt Cooking oil
1/2 pt Parsley
1    Clove garlic (chopped)
1 pt Parmesan cheese,grated
1/4 c  Raw onion (chopped)
1 pt Black pepper
1/2 pt Brown sugar
Combine ingredients in a 1 qt sauce pan and cook for 30-40 minutes.
Makes enough sauce for 4 12 " Pizzas.
