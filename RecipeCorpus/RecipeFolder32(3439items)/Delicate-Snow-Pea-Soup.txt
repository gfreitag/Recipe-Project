Title: Delicate Snow Pea Soup
Categories: Healthy, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
4 c  Chicken stock
1 c  Finely chopped bok choy
1/4 c  Thinly sliced mushrooms
2 oz Tofu, cut in small cubes
1 cl Garlic, minced
3/4 ts Grated ginger
1 ts Low sodium soy sauce
1 c  Fresh snow peas
1/4 c  Diced green onions with
-tops
1    Egg white, lightly beaten,
-optional
Bring all ingredients except snow peas, green onions and egg whites.
Simmer 20 minutes. Add snow peas and green onion. cook 5 minutes;
slowly stir in egg   white, if desired, to make a ribon effect. Makes
6 servigs.

Variation: For a creamy soup, add 1 heaping teaspoon cornstarch mixed
incold   water plus 1/2 teaspoon light soy sauce. Add to soup, heat
until thick and bubbly.

Calories: 37 Protein: 3g Carbohydrates: 4g Fat: 0.5g, 12% Sodium: 45mg
Cholesterol: 0mg
