Title: Lemon-Poppy Muffins
Categories: Breads, Guestchefs
Yield: 12 servings
1    Egg
3/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Oil
1 c  Milk
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
2    Lemons -- grated zest only
1/3 c  Poppy seeds
This recipe was developed by Richard Simpson, pastry instructor at
the New York Cooking School.

PREHEAT OVEN TO 400F. Beat egg, salt and sugar until light and
fluffy. Add oil in a stream and continue beating. Beat in milk. Sift
flour and baking powder together several timesand add to batter. Add
lemon zest and poppy seeds. Beat until thoroughly mixe Bake in
greased muffin tins for 20-25 minutes.
