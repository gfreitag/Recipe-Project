Title: Aceitunas Con Limon Y Tomillo (Lemon Thyme Green Olives)
Categories: Vegetables, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
14 oz Unpitted green olives
1    Lemon -- slice thin
2 tb Fresh thyme
1/2 c  Olive oil
Crush or cut slits in olives. Place in a jar with sliced lemon. Place
thyme over olives and pour over olive oil. Let stand at room
temperature for 24 hours.
