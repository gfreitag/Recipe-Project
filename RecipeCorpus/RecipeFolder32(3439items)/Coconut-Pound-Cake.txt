Title: Coconut Pound Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
3    Sticks of margarine
3 c  Flour
1/4 ts Baking powder
5    Eggs
3 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
1 c  Coconut
1 ts Vanilla
Cream margarine and sugar until fluffy.  Add 1 egg at a time.  Add
vanilla, flour and milk alternately, beginning and ending with flour.
Bake in a 350 degree oven in a tube pan for 1 hour and 20 minutes.
