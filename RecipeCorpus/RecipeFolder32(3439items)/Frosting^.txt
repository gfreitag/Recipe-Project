Title: Frosting^
Categories: Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Butter
1    Egg yolk
1 tb Cocoa
1 tb Warm, black coffee
2 c  Powdered sugar
Mix butter and egg yolk well. Beat in coffee and cocoa. gradually add
sugar. spread on cooled cake. Easy and tasty.
