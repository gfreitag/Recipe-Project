If using filo leaves, take from refrigerator 2 hours before
beginning. Wash fresh spinach several times and chop coarsely. Thaw
frozen spinach. Preheat oven to 350. Saute onions and scallions in
olive oil until golden and then add spinach. Cook until just

golden and puffy. Serve hot or cold.
