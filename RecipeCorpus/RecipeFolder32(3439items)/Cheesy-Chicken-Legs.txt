Title: Cheesy Chicken Legs
Categories: Poultry, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Parmesan cheese, grated
1 tb Dried minced parsley
1 ts Italian seasoning
Salt & Pepper to taste
4    Chicken legs
Melted Butter or Margarine
Combine parmesan cheese, parsley, Italian seasoning, salt and pepper.
Dip chicken in melted butter, then in cheese mixture.  Place in
shallow baking dish.  Drizzle margarine or butter over chicken.  Bake
at 375 degrees for about 25 minutes or until chicken is done.

Source:  S.H.A.R.E. Colorado May/June 1995
