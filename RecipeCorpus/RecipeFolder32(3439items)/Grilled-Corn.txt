Title: Grilled Corn
Categories: Vegetables, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
4    Ears corn with husks
4 tb Butter
4 ts Pure Chimay chile powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Open the husks but do not peel them off. Remove the silk from the
corn and wash the ears. Brush each ear with 1 tbs of the butter and
sprinkle with 1 tsp of the chile powder. Add salt and pepper if
desired. Wrap the corn back in the husks and tie the ends with a
strip torn off the husk. Grill over hot mesquite or charcoal for
about 45 minutes, turning the ears every 5-10 minutes. Serve hot or
cold.
