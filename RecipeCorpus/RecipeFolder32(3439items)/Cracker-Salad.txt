Title: Cracker Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1    Stack of saltine crackers
1 c  Chopped bermuda onion
1 c  Sweet pickles, chopped
1 md Green pepper, chopped
1 sm Jar of pimentos, drained
5    Hard boiled eggs, chopped
1 pt Mayonnaise
Crush crackers and put in a large mixing bowl.  Add all ingredients
and mix well.  This salad tastes best if put in a covered plastic
container and chilled for a few hours prior to serving. Randy Rigg
