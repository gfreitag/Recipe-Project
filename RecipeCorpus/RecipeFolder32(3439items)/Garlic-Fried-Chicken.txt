Title: Garlic Fried Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  All-purpose flour
2 tb Lemon juice
2 cl Garlic; crushed
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Celery salt
1 ea 2-1/2 to 3 pound broiler-
-fryer, cut up and skinned
1 c  All-purpose flour
Vegetable oil
Combine first 6 ingredients; mix well. Place chicken in a shallow
pan, and pour buttermilk mixture over top.  Cover and refrigerate at
least 3 hours or overnight.
Remove chicken from liquid. Dredge chicken in flour.
Heat 1 inch of oil in a large skillet to 325 degrees F, add
chicken and fry 30 to 35 minutes, turning once. Drain chicken well on
paper towels.
