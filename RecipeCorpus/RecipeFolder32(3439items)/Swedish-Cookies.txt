Title: Swedish Cookies
Categories: West europe, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 recipe
2 c  Sugar
1 lb OR 2 cups butter or marg.
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
5 c  Flour
Cream sugar and butter or margarine well.  Add egg and beat well.
Add vanilla.  Mix well.  Add flour gradually until well blended.
Shape with cookie press (sic)or ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350Ã¸
for 5 to 10 minutes depending on thickness.
