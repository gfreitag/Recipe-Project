Title: Oua Umplute (Stuffed Eggs)
Categories: Romanian, Appetizers
Yield: 24 servings
12 x  Eggs, hard boiled
1/2 c  Chicken breast, ground fine
1 tb Butter
1 tb Sour cream
1 ts Prepared mustard
1 pn Salt
1 x  Black pepper, to taste
1 x  Mayonnaise
Remove shells from eggs. Slice in half and remove yolks into a bowl.

Smooth the yolks with a fork so they will not be too lumpy. Grind
chicken very finely in processor or grinder. Add to yolks, chicken,
butter, sour cream, mustard, salt & pepper. Fill eggs and serve with
mayo. Submitted By MARK MIKLAS   On   07-10-95
