Title: Layer Cake
Categories: Jewish, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
6    Egg yolks
1/3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Cake flour -- sifted
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
6    Egg whites -- stiffly
Beaten
Beat the egg yolks; gradually add the sugar, beating until thick and
light in color.  Sift flour, salt and baking powder into egg-yolk
mixture and fold in.  Stir in the vanilla and fold in the egg whites.
Turn into two 8-inch layer-cake pans that have been greased and
dusted with flour. Bake in a 350 degree oven 20 minutes, or until
browned and free from sides of
