Title: Apple Taffy Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 1/3 c  Water
1/2 c  Margarine -- softened
4    Eggs
3/4 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 1/2 c  Apples (about 3) -- peel &
-in chunks
3/4 c  Brown sugar
Heat oven to 350.  Grease and flour 13 x 9 pan.  Mix cake mix, water,
margarine, eggs, graham cracker crumbs and cinnamon in large bowl on
low speed, scraping bowl frequently, 30 seconds.  Beat on medium
speed, scraping bowl occasionally, 2 minutes.  Fold in apples.  Pour
into pan; sprinkle evenly with brown sugar.  Bake about 38 to 43
minutes; cool completely.
