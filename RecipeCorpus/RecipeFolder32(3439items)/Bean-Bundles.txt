Title: Bean Bundles
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings  Drain 1 can of whole green beans.  Make 4 bundles using 1 strip of
half cooked bacon per bundle.  Secure the wrapped bundles with
toothpicks. Put bundles in a baking pan and put a small cube of
Velveeta cheese on top. Spoon 1 tablespoon of Durkee's famous sauce
over the cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes. Randy Rigg
