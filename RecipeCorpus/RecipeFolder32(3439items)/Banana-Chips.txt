Title: Banana Chips
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
Dwigans fwds07a -- nathan
And goldman
Cooking oil
2 lb Very green bananas
Salt
heat the oil to 370. Slice bananas 1/8 inch thick and fry one cupful
at a time. It takes only a few minutes. remove when golden brown and
crisp like potato chips. Drain on paper towl and sprinkle with salt.
