Title: Cheese Filling (The Art of Fine Baking)
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Yellow raisins
1 tb Cognac
1 c  Cream cheese (or cottage)
1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Flour
1    Egg yolk
1 ts Melted butter
1 tb Sour cream
1/2 ts Grated lemon rind
1/2 ts Vanilla
Servings: 1

Mix raisins with cognac.  Cream together cheese, sugar, and flour.
Stir in egg yolk, then melted butter, sour cream, lemon rind, and
vanilla. Add cognac-soaked raisins.

From "The Art of Fine Baking" by Paula Peck, copyright 1961
