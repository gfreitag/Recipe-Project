Title: Tzatziki III
Categories: Greek, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cup(s)
2 c  Plain yogurt *
1 lg Cucumber
1 ts Salt
2 cl Garlic, minced
2 tb Fresh dill, finely chopped
1 tb White wine vinegar
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Olive Oil
Cooking Time: 2 hour standing
Preparation Time: 15 min

Instructions:

Line plastic or nylon sieve with cheese-cloth set over bowl.  Spoon
yogurt into sieve.  Cover and let drain in refrigerator 2 hours.

Meanwhile, peel, seed, and coarsely grate cucumber.  Place in
colander, Sprinkle with salt and cover with plate small enough to fit
inside colander; let drain 1 hour.  Rinse cucumber; pat dry on paper
towels.

In a medium bowl combine yogurt, cucumber, garlic, dill, vinegar and
pepper.  Stir in olive oil, a little at a time until blended.
