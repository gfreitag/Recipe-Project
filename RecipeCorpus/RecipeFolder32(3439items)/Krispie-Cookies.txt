Title: Krispie Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 45 servings
1 1/4 c  All purpose flour
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Soft butter
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Kellogg's Rice Krispies
1 c  Semi-sweet chocolate chips
Sift together flour and cinnamon.Set aside.Cream together
butter,sugar and vanilla until fluffy and light.Add dry ingredients
with rice krispies and chocolate chips.Mix well. Drop by level
tablespoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets and bake  at 350 degrees
for about 15 minutes or until cookies are a pale golden brown.

From box of Kellogg's Rice Krispies
