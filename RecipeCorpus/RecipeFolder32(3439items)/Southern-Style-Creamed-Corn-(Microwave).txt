Title: Southern-Style Creamed Corn (Microwave)
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
6 md Ears fresh corn
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Butter
1 ts Sugar
1/2 c  Half-and-half
2 ts Cornstarch
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Coarsely ground black pepper
Cut corn from cob, scraping cobs well to remove all milk.
Place butter in a 2-quart casserole, and microwave at HIGH for 1
minute or until melted; stir in corn, water, and sugar. Cover with
heavy-duty plastic wrap.  Microwave at high for 3 minutes; stir well,
and give dish a half-turn. Cover and microwave at HIGH for 5 to 7
minutes or until corn is done.
Combine remaining ingredients, and beat with a wire whisk until
cornstarch is dissolved; add to corn, mixing well.  Cover and
microwave at HIGH for 2 to 2-1/2 minutes or until thickened and
bubbly, stirring at 1-minute intervals. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
