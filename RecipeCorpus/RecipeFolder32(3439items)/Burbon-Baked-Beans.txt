Title: Burbon Baked Beans
Categories: Side dish, Main dish, Vegetables, Alcohol
Yield: 12 servings
4    16oz cans Boston Baked Beans
1 cn 16oz crushed pineapple
- drained
1    12oz jar chili sauce
1/2 c  Strong brewed coffee
1/2 c  Bourbon
1/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1 tb Mollasses
3/4 ts Dry mustard
Combine beans and remaining ingredients in a Crock-Pot or other slow
cooker.  Cover and cook on high 2 hours; uncover and cook to desired
consistency.  Serve with a slotted spoon.

NOTE:  To make strong coffee, dissolve 1 teaspoon instant coffee
granules in 1/2 cup hot water.
