Title: Marinated Flank Steak
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Consomme -- or
1/2 c  Green onions -- sliced
1 c  Beef bouillon
1 c  Garlic -- minced
1 c  Red wine
2 tb Lime juice
2/3 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Brown sugar
Score steak on both sides in 1-1/2" squares. Marinate in other stuff
8 hrs. in refrigerator. Grill to desired degree of doneness. Slice
