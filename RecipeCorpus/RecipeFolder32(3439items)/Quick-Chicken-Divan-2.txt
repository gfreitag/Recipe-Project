Title: Quick Chicken Divan 2
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Broccoli spears(10oz)
4    Chicken servings,cooked
1 cn Cream/chicken soup(10.75oz)
1/3 c  Skim milk
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese,shredded
1. Layer broccoli in a shallow greased 10x6x2-inch baking dish; top
with sliced chicken.

2. Blend together soup and milk; pour over chicken.

3. Sprinkle dish with cheese.

4. Broil 4 to 5 inches from heat until hot and lightly browned on
top, or bake in preheated 450'F. oven 15 minutes.

NOTE: Makes an outstanding company dish!
