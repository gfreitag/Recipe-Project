Title: Spicy Sausage Vegetable Strata
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 lb Bulk sausage (spicy)
1 md Onion -- chopped
1    French bread loaf -- sliced
-3/4 inch thick
1/2 c  Roasted red peppers -- cut
-in 1/4 inch strips
2 md Zucchini (about 2 cups) --
-thinly sliced
6    Egg
3/4 c  Ricotta cheese
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
Cook sausage and onion in 10" skillet over medium heat, stirring
occasionally, until sausage is no longer pink; drain well.  Arrange
bread slices in 11 x 7 baking dish.  Spread sausage mixture over
bread.  Top with peppers and zucchini.  Beat eggs and ricotta cheese
in medium bowl until blended.  Stir in milk.  Pour over mixture in
baking dish.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.

TO STORE: Cover tightly and refrigerate at least 2 hours but no longer
than 24 hours.

TO COOK: About 1 hour before serving, heat oven to 350.  Uncover pan
an bake 45 to 55 minutes or until top is puffed and center is set.
Serve immediately.
