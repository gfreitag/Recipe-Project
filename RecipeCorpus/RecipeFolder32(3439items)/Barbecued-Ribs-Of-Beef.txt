Title: Barbecued Ribs Of Beef
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
3 lb Short ribs
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Dry mustard
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Catchup
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Minced onions
1    Garlic clove -- minced
You can use the top of the roast beef bones.  Have them cut in 2-inch
pieces.    Brown the ribs in a heated casserole or Dutch oven; pour
off the fat.  Combine all the ingredients and add to ribs. Cover and
bake in a 350 degree oven 2 hours.  Remove cover
