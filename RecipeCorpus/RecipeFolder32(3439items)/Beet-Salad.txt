Title: Beet Salad
Categories: Jewish, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Grated cooked beets
4 tb Grated horseradish
2 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
2 ts Vinegar
2 tb Salad or olive oil
Mix all the ingredients together and chill for 2 hours. Serves 6 to 8.
