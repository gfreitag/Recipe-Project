Title: Allergy Lactose-Free Cheese Spread
Categories: Allergy, Cheese/eggs, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Lecithin
3/4 lb Cheddar cheese
Mix in processor until desired consistency.  Scrape into jar and
refrigerate.
