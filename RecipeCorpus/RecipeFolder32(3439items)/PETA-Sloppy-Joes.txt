Title: PETA Sloppy Joes
Categories: Vegetarian, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Margerine; or water
1/2 c  Onion; minced
1/2 c  Green pepper; minced
1 lb Firm tofu; patted dry and
-mashed
6 tb Ketchup
6 tb Chili sauce
1/2 ts Salt
;pepper to taste
4    Burger buns; lightly toasted
Heat the margerine or water in a large skillet over med heat.  Add the
onion and green pepper and saute or broil until the veggies are well
cooked, about 5 mins.  Add the mashed tofu and saute for another 15
mins, until the tofu is completely cooked.

Add the ketchup, chili sauce, salt, and pepper and continue to cook
over low heat until the mixture is heated thru.  Add a little water
if the mixture is too dry.

Spoon into burg buns.
