Title: Flavourings for Soy Bean Milk
Categories: Allergy
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Lime juice
1 tb Molasses
1 tb Honey
3/4 c  Frozen raspberries
2 tb Fruit syrup
2 tb Maply syrup
2    Tbps malt syrup
1 pn Salt
2 ts Vanilla
1    Banana (blended)
To improve the flavor of soy milk, one of more of the follolwing may
be added to one cup;

chocolate syrup to taste

Origin: The Allergy Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, July/95.
