Title: Broiled Salmon with Whole-Grain Mustard and Sage Crust
Categories: Fish
Yield: 4 servings
4    Boned salmon fillets
-(6oz ea)
1 tb Olive oil
4 tb Whole-grain mustard
4 tb Sage, chopped or
1 tb Dried sage
Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet and brush them with olive
oil on both sides. Mix the mustard and sage together. Coat the top of
each fillet with the mustard mixture. Broil the salmon for about
12-15 minutes or until the mustard coating is lightly browned. Serve
hot.
