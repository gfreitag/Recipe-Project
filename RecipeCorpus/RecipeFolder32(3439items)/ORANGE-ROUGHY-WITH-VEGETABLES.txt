Title: ORANGE ROUGHY WITH VEGETABLES
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 2 servings
2 lb Orange Roughy Fillets
2 tb Lemon Juice, Divided
16 oz Can Stewed Tomatoes, cut
1/2 ts Mixed Italian Herbs
1 c  Celery; minced
1 c  Onion; chopped
1 c  Bell Pepper
1 c  Mushrooms; sliced
1/4 ts Thyme
pn Garlic powder
pn Hot Pepper flakes
Spray a 13x9 Pan with non stick cooking spray and sprinkle with 1
tbls. of Lemon Juice.  Arrange fish in a single layer and sprinkle
with 1 tbls. Lemon Juice. Combine remaining ingredients and spoon
over fish. Bake uncovered in a 350 Degrees oven for 25-30 Minutes,
until Fish flakes and sauce is thick.

Serves 4:  Each serving Counts 4 proteins, 2 vegetables.
