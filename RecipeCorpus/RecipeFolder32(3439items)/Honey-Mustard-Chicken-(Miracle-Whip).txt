Title: Honey Mustard Chicken (Miracle Whip)
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
2 tb Dijon Mustard
- OR Stone Ground Mustard
1 tb Honey
2 lb Chicken breast halves,
- skinned
Heat grill.  Mix salad dressing, mustard and honey.  Brush top of
chicken with 1/2 of the dressing mixture.
Place chicken, bone side up, on grill over medium coals.  Grill,
covered, 15 to 20 minutes.  Brush with remaining dressing mixture;
turn. Continue grilling 15 to 20 minutes or until chicken is cooked
through.
VARIATION:  Substitute boneless skinless chicken breast halves
(about 1 1/4 lbs.) for the bone-in chicken breast halves.  Decrease
cooking time to 8 to 10 minutes on each side.
OVEN DIRECTIONS: Heat broiler.  Place chicken, bone side up, on
rack of broiler pan.  Brush with 1/2 the mixture.  Broil 15 to 20
minutes. Turn; brush with remaining mixture.  Continue broiling 15 to
20 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Grilling Time: 40 minutes
