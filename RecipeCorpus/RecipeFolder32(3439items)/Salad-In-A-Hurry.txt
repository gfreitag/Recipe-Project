Title: Salad In A Hurry
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Carton of Cool Whip
1 cn Cherry pie filling
1 c  Pineapple chunks, drained
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
3    Bananas, sliced
2    Apples, peeled, cored and
-diced
Mix all ingredients together in a dish.  Chill and serve. Randy Rigg
