Title: Cornish Hen With Wild Rice
Categories: Jewish, Poultry
Yield: 3 servings

1/4 c  Minced onion
1/4 c  Chopped mushrooms
Ground giblets of hen
4 tb Chicken fat
3 ts Salt
3/4 ts Pepper

Small)
1/2 c  Boiling water
