Title: Baked Salmon
Categories: Jewish, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Butter
4 sl Salmon
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Onion -- sliced
1    Bay leaf
1 c  Light cream
Melt the butter in a baking dish.  Arrange the salmon in it and
sprinkle with the salt and pepper.  Surround with the onion slices
and bay leaf. Bake in a 375 degree oven 15 minutes.  Pour the cream
over the fish and bake for additional 25 minutes, basting frequently.
Serves 4 to 6. RecipeSource: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING by Jennie
Grossinger Published by Bantam
