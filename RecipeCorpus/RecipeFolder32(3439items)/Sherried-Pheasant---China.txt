Title: Sherried Pheasant - China
Categories: Chinese, Game, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1    Pheasant - disjointed
1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Dry sherry
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Ginger-chopped
Combine flour and salt, coat the pheasant pieces with the mixture.
Melt a half cup butter, brown pheasant on all sides and place
pheasant pieces in a covered dish. In a saucepan combine the
remaining butter, sherry, soy sauce, lemon juice and ginger. Bring to
a boil stirring constantly and pour over pheasant. Bake in a
preheated 350 oven for about one hour or until tender. Turn pheasant
once during baking.
