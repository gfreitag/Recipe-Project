Title: Potato Knaidlach  <passover>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Passover, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
2    Eggs
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Grated onion
1/3 c  Potato flour
3 tb Matzo meal
4 c  Grated drained potatoes
Beat the eggs, salt and onion together.  Stir in the potato flour,
matzo meal and potatoes.  Shape into 1 1/2-inch balls.  Cook in
salted water 20 minutes or until they rise tothe top.  Drain.  May be
served with meat dishes, too.  Makes about 18.    Reci
