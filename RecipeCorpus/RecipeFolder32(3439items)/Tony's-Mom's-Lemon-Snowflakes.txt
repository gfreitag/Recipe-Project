Title: Tony's Mom's Lemon Snowflakes
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 60 servings
1 pk Lemon cake mix w/pudding
2 1/4 c  Thawed cool whip
1    Egg
Powdered sugar
1.  In a mixing bowl, combine cake mix, whipped topping, and egg.
Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until blended. Batter
will be very thick and sticky.

2.  Drop by teaspoonsful into powdered sugar; roll lightly to coat.
Place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake at 350F for 10-12 minutes or
until lightly browned.

Yields:  5-6 dozen

Source:  Eilene Kiger - Blodgett Oregon (Tony Hammerquist's mom!)
