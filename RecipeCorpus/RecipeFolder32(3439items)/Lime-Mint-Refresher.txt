Title: Lime-Mint Refresher
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 10 servings
1 3/4 oz Package whipped topping mix
1/2 c  Cold milk
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 qt Vanilla ice cream; softened
1 pt Lime sherbet; softened
1/4 c  Creme de menthe
Combine whipped topping mix, milk, and vanilla; beat until smooth
and stiff.  Add remaining ingredients; stir well. Freeze.  Yield: 10
to 12 servings.
