Title: PETA Tony and Elaine La Russa's Tofu Onion Burgers
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Firm tofu; mashed well
1 md Onion; chopped very fine
2 tb Wheat germ
2 tb Flour
2 ts Garlic powder
2 tb Soy sauce; or tamari
;pepper to taste
;oil for frying
Mix all the ingredients except oil in a bowl and form patties. Heat a
little oil in a skillet and fry patties until brown and very crisp,
about 10 mins.  Serve these burgers on buns or as patties with a
sauce. From The Compassionate Cok by PETA and Ingrid Newkirk Typed by
Lisa Greenwood
