Title: Salami And Eggs
Categories: Jewish, Cheese/eggs, Sausage
Yield: 1 servings
4 sl Salami
2    Eggs
1/8 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
Fat -- for frying
Cut the salami in strips.  Lightly beat the eggs, salt and pepper.
Fry the salami until lightly browned in a skillet.  Pour the eggs
over it and cook over low heat until theeggs are set on the bottom.
Turn over, pancake fashion, and cook until set.  S
