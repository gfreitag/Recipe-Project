Title: Mama's Cherry and Raspberry Jam
Categories: Can/preserv, Dips/spread
Yield: 30 ounces
2 c  Pitted cherries
2 c  Raspberries
4 c  Sugar
Crush cherries slightly and cook until the skins are tender. Add
raspberries and cook until the mixture begins to thicken. Add sugar
and cook rapidly until thick. Cool slightly, pour into sterile jars
and seal with parafin. Makes 5 (6 oz) glasses.
