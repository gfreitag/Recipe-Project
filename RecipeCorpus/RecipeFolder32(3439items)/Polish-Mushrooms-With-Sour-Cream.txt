Title: Polish Mushrooms With Sour Cream
Categories: Polish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
6 tb Butter or margarine
1 lg Onion -- chopped
2 tb Flour -- minced
2 tb Milk
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
1 1/4 lb Mushrooms -- sliced
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
In melted butter, in large skillet, saute onion till golden.
Sprinkle with flour; add milk and 3/4 cup sour cream; while stirring,
bring just to a simmer.  Then add sliced mushrooms, salt, pepper, and
paprika.  Simmer, covered, 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Stir in
remaining sour cream ; heat thoroughly, while stirring constantly.
Serve at once.

Source:  Good Housekeeping Cookbook; edited by Dorothy B. Marsh;
