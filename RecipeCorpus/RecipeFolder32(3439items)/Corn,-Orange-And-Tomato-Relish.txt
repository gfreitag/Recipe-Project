Title: Corn, Orange And Tomato Relish
Categories: Relishes, Vegetables, Fruit, Tomatoes
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Corn Kernels (1 large ear),
-fresh
1 lg Orange
3/4 c  Tomatoes; diced
1/4 c  Red Bell Pepper; diced
1 tb Cilantro, fresh; chopped
1 sm Jalapeno Pepper; finely
-minced
1 tb Seasoned Rice Vinegar
1 tb Lime Juice, fresh
X  Freshly ground black
-pepper
X  Salt
CORN, ORANGE AND TOMATO RELISH

[Jean-Marc Fullsack] [Makes 2 cups]

Bring 6 quarts of water to boil. Add the corn. Allow the water to
return to a boil and blanch the corn for 1 minute, or just until
tender. Drain and refresh under cold running water. Drain well and
set aside.

Peel the orange including the white pith and the membrane between
segments. Cut each segment into quarters.

In a large bowl, combine the corn, orange pieces, tomatoes, red bell
pepper, cilantro and jalapeno. Pour the vinegar and lime juice over
the vegetables and toss well. Season to taste with pepper and salt.
Serve at room temperature.

SERVING SIZE: (1/4 cup)

sodium without added salt.
