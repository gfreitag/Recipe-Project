Title: Pasta a la Grecque
Categories: Pasta, Vegetables, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
4 ea Tomatoes
3/4 lb Med shrimp; cooked, shelled
-and deveined
1/2 lb Feta cheese; crumbled
6 ea Scallions; chopped
4 ts Fresh oregano; minced
x  Salt and freshly ground
-black pepper
1 lb Spinach linguine
PREP TIME:  15 minutes, plus 1 hour to marinate

COOKING TIME:  10 minutes

1.  Cut tomatoes in half cross-wise.  Squeeze to extract seeds.
Coarsely chop tomatoes.  Combine tomatoes, shrimp, feta cheese,
scallions, oregano, salt and pepper in a large bowl.  Toss well and
let marinate in refrigerator for 1 hour.

2.  Cook linguine according to package directions.  Rinse and drain
well. Toss warm pasta with shrimp and feta cheese mixture and serve
immediately.

PER SERVING:  290 calories; 10g fat; 31% calories from fat; 147mg
cholesterol; 1.5g fiber.

Source:  Kathryn Arnold in "Delicious!  Your Magazine of Natural
Living" (natural foods store give-away), July 1995.
