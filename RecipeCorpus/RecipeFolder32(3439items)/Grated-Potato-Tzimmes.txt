Title: Grated-Potato Tzimmes
Categories: Jewish, Potatoes, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Unsweetened prunes
3 c  Water
8    Potatoes -- peeled
1/3 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Potato flour
2 tb Melted fat
1/8 ts Pepper
Wash the prunes and soak in the water 1 hour.  Bring to a boil. Slice
5 potatoes into a 2-quart casserole and pour the undrained prunes
over them. Add the brown sugar, lemon juice and 1 teaspoon salt.
