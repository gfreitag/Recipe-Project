Title: Mousht (Fish With Tahnia)
Categories: Fish
Yield: 4 servings
7    Pat dwigans
1/4 c  Cooking oil
4 sm St peters fish or trout
2 md Size onions -- sliced
1 sm Can tahina
1 tb Lemon juice
10    Milliliters
Salt and pepper to taste
Garlic -- crushed
Preheat oven to 325. heat a small amount of cooking oil and saute the
onion slices for a few minutes. Add the fish and saute them for a min
or so on each side just to flavor them.In a bowl blend the tahina
with the lemn juice and garlic. Sprinkle the fish
