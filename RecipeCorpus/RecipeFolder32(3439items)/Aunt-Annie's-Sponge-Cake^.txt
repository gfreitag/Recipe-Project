Title: Aunt Annie's Sponge Cake^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
4    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
4 tb Cold water
1 c  Flour
1 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
Separate eggs. beat egg yolks with sugar. Add water and mix well.
Stir in flour, cornstarch, baking powder and vanilla and stir until
well blended. Beat egg whites until stiff. gently fold in to batter.
Bake in a moderate oven. Very good!
