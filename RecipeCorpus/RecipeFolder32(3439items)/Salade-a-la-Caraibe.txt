Title: Salade a la Caraibe
Categories: Side dish, Caribbean, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  White cabbage, shredded
1 c  Cucumber, grated
1 ea Red bell pepper, sliced in
-- circles
1 ea Mango, peeled, stoned, slice
5 ea Scallions, chopped
1 ea Avocado, peeled & sliced
1 ea Garlic clove, crushed
Juice of 2 lemons
5 tb Olive oil
1 ea Handful watercress
Salt & pepper
First, arrange the cabbage, cucumber, bell pepper, mango & scallions
in a salad bowl.  Now put in half the avocado slices.  Then blend the
garlic with the lime or lemon juice, olive oil & salt & pepper.  Pour
half the dressing over the vegetables & mango & toss.

Now arrabge the watercress & remaining avocado slices on top &
drizzle the rest of the dressing over before serving.

Troth Wells, "The World in Your Kitchen"
