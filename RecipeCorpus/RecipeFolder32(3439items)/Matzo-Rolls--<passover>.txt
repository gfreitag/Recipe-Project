Title: Matzo Rolls  <passover>
Categories: Jewish, Muffins, Passover
Yield: 12 servings
3    Eggs
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Chicken broth or milk
1 c  Matzo meal
1/3 c  Cake meal
4 tb Melted chicken fat or
Butter
Beat together the eggs and salt.  Stir in the broth (or milk)
alternately with the matzo meal and cake meal.  Add the melted fat or
butter.  Fill 12 greased muffin cups 2/3 full.    Bake in a 350
degree oven 30 minutes or until browned.    Recipe Source:
