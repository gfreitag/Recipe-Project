Title: Cajun Spice Mix
Categories: Herbs/spice, Hot/spicy, Cajun/creol
Yield: 2 /3
7 1/2 tb Paprika
6 tb Garlic Powder
6 tb Ground Black Pepper
3 tb Ground Red Pepper
3 tb Dried Thyme
3 tb Dried Oregano
3 tb Onoin Powder
Blend all ingredients.  Transfer to small jar, seal tightly. Store in
cool, dry place.  Makes about 2/3 cup.
