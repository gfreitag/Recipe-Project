Title: Fruit Pies -  Rhubarb Filling
Categories: Jewish, Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Rhubarb -- sliced 1" long
1 c  Sugar
3 tb Flour
1/8 ts Salt
2 tb Butter -- (optional)
Mix the rhubarb, sugar, flour and salt together and fill the pie
plate. Dot with butter if you want to use it. Cover with top crust.
Bake in a 400 degree oven 45 minutes oruntil browned.    Recipe
Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossing
