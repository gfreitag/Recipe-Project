Title: Pheasant Del Monico - United States
Categories: Game
Yield: 6 servings
1    Pheasant-disjointed
1/2 c  Butter-melted
Salt & pepper to taste
1 c  Biscuit mix
3 sl Processed cheese
From : Sallie Krebs, Thu 06 Jul 95 04:00, Area: COOKING

Dry pheasant and brush with seasoned melted butter. Dredge pheasant
pieces with the biscuit mix. Remove pheasant pieces and place in a
roasting pan. Pour butter over bird, cover and bake in a 350 oven for
one and a half hours. Remove from oven and place cheese on pheasant
pieces, return to oven and cook until cheese has melted and is
slightly brown.
