Title: A Simple Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Oldies
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Oil
1/2    Lemon -- juice of
1 sm Onion -- minced
1/3 c  Ketchup
2/3 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Paprika
1 ts (scant) salt
Beat all ingredients together with a rotary beater or wire beater. Or
you can put it in a jar, cover and shake well. this is a fine
dressing to use on lettuce and fresh tomatoes.
