Title: Impossible Burrito Bake
Categories: Casseroles, Ethnic
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Bisquick
Salt & pepper to taste
1/3 c  Water
1 c  Salsa
1 cn Refried beans -- any
Variety
1 c  Or more -- shredded cheddar
Ch
1 lb Ground beef
JUDY WALTER  NWTP92A-----
Mix Bisquick, water and refried beans together and put it in the
bottom of a greased pie plate. Brown and drain seasoned ground beef;
put on top of mixture in pie plate. Add salsaand then cheese. Bake,
uncovered, for 30 minutes at 350 or till golden brown
