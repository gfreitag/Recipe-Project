Title: Hard Tack (Knackebrod)
Categories: West europe, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Potato water
1 pk Yeast
Flour (enough to make soft
-sponge)
1 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sweet cream
1    Egg yolk
1 tb Salt
Cornmeal
Combine potato water, yeast and flour. Let rise overnight in warm
place.

Next morning, add the rest of the ingredients, except cornmeal. Knead
dough down once; let rise. Make into buns, let rise 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
Roll each bun as thin as possible in cornmeal, using a corrugated
rolling pin. Bake in a hot oven. Turn frequently. Source:
Scandinavian Recipes, compiled and published by Julia Peterson
Tufford (1973) From: Sallie Krebs
