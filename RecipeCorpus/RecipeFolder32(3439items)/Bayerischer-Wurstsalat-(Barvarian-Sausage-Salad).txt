Title: Bayerischer Wurstsalat (Barvarian Sausage Salad)
Categories: Aust/german, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Knockwurst -- Cooked /
Cooled
1/2 ts Salt
2    Pickles -- Small
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Onion -- Medium
1/4 ts Paprika
3 tb Vinegar
1/4 ts Sugar
1 tb Mustard -- Prepared *
1 tb Capers
2 tb Vegetable Oil
1 tb Parsley -- Chopped
*  Mustard must be the strong Djon or Gulden Type.
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++
Cut the knockwurst into small cubes.  Mince the pickles and onion.
Mix together the vinegar, mustard and oil. Add salt, pepper, paprika
and sugar. Adjust seasonings if desired. Add the capers; mix well.
Stir in the chopped knockwurst, pickles, and onions. Just before
serving, garnish with chopped parsley.
