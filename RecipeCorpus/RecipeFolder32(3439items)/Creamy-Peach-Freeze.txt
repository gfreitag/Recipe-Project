Title: Creamy Peach Freeze
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1 3/4 oz Package whipped topping mix
16 oz Can sliced peaches; drained
Prepare whipped topping mix according to package directions.
Combine whipped topping and peaches in container of electric blender;
process until smooth.  Spoon into 4 serving dishes, and freeze 2 to 3
hours or until firm.  Remove from freezer 5 minutes before serving.
