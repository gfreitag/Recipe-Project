Title: Salade De Carottes Crues ( Raw Carrot Salad)
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
1 lb Carrots
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Vinegar or lemon juice
3 tb Minced parsley
2    Garlic cloves minced
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 ts Sugar optional
Peel the carrots and grate them fine. Mix the oil and lemon juice
together, add the carrots, parsley and garlic. Season with salt and
pepper.

: "The Flavors of Jerusalem" by Nathan and Goldman
