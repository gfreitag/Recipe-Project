Title: Sweet Potato Spice Bread
Categories: Muffins, Breads, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Molasses
2/3 c  Honey
2/3 c  Sweet potatoes, mashed
1/3 c  Oil
2    Eggs -- beaten
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Cloves
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Mace
1 ts Freshly grated ginger
1 c  Flour, whole-grain wheat
1 c  Flour, all-purpose --
Unbleached
1 tb Baking powder
1 c  Walnuts -- or pecans,
Chopped
Preheat oven to  325F.  Oil a 9X5X3 loaf pan.

In a large bowl, blend molasses, honey , sweet potatoes, oil and eggs.

In small bowl, combine remaining ingredients.  add to potato mixture
and stir until well mixed.

Spoon batter into  loaf pan and bake until a toothpick inserted in
middle comes out clean - about 1 hour.  Turn onto wire rack to cool
before slicing.

Per slice:  223 calories;  5 g protein;  128 mg sodium;  31 g carbo;
10 g fat;  27 mg cholesterol;  85 mg calcium
