Title: Super Smoking Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-----------------------*INGREDIENTS*----------------------------
1 lb Margarine - corn oil type
2/3 c  Sherry - or red wine (I'd
-use chicken broth as a
Substitute if you're
-avoiding alcohol, with
Perhaps a dash of balsamic
-or red wine vinegar)
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Soy sauce
2    Clove garlic - finely
-chopped
1/2 c  Parsley - chopped
2 ts Salt
1 c  Water
Combine all ingredients in heavy saucepan and bring to a boil. Lower
heat and simmer for 30 minutes.  Paint on meat surfaces before, once
during and at the end of smoking.  Keeps indefinitely refrigerated.
