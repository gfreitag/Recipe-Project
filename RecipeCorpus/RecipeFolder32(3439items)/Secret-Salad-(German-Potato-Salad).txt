Title: Secret Salad (German Potato Salad)
Categories: Side dish, Dress/stuff
Yield: 12 servings
5 lb Potatoes
1/4 lb Bacon -- finely chopped
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
1 tb Flour
2 ts Salt
1 1/4 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
2/3 c  Cider vinegar
1/3 c  Water
1/2 ts Celery seed
3 tb Parsley -- chopped
Cook potatoes in their jackets until tender.  Cool, peel and slice
thinly. Fry bacon until crisp, add chopped onion and cook 1 minute.
Mix the flour, sugar, salt, and pepper and blend in.  Stir in the
vinegar and water.  Cook 10 minutes, stirring well.  Pour dressing
over sliced potatoes and add the celery seed and chopped parsley.
Toss and serve warm.
