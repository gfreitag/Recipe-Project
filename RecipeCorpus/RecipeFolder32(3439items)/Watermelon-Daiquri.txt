Title: Watermelon Daiquri
Categories: Fruit, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Watermelon --
-peeled/seeded/cubed
1/2 c  Rum
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
1/4 c  Orange flavored liqueur
Ice cubes
Freese watermelon in a shallow pan for at least 6 hrs.  Combine frozen
watermelon, rum, lime juice, & orange flavored liqueur in cantainer
of an electric blender; process until smooth stopping once to scrape
down sides. Add enough ice to bring mix to 5 level cups; process
until smooth. Repeat procecedure if necessary until mix measures 5
cups.

From Southern Living Magazine, June 1995 issue.
