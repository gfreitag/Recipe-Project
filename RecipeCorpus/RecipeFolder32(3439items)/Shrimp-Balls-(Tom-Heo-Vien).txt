Title: Shrimp Balls (Tom Heo Vien)
Categories: Vietnamese, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Shrimp -- ground
1 sm Hard-boiled egg -- finely
Ground
1 sm Onion -- finely ground
1    Egg -- beaten
Salt and pepper
1.  Mix all the ingredients using your hand, then shape into small
balls. Drop into the simmering broth to cook.

Recipe Source: VIETNAMESE COOKING - EXOTIC DELIGHTS FROM INDO-CHINA by
Paulette Do Van.  Published by Chartwell Books, Inc. Copyright (c)
