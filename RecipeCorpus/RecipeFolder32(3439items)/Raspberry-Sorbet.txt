Title: Raspberry Sorbet
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Water
1/3 c  Sugar
1 pt Ripe raspberries
1/2 ts Raspberry vinegar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Framboise, optional
Make a syrup by combining the water & sugar in a non-corrosive pot &
bring to a boil.  Simmer for 5 minutes.  Remove from heat & let cool.
Puree the raspberries in a food prcoessor & strain through a fine
sieve to remove the seeds.  Combine the syrup, raspberries, vinegar,
lemon juice & framboise. Freeze following the instructions for making
Lemon Sorbet.

VARIATION: Blackberry Sorbet.  Substitute blackberries for the
raspberries, 1/2 ts balsamic vinegar for the raspberry vinegar & 1 tb
blackberry brandy for the framboise.

You can use frozen raspberries as well.  Double the amount of
framboise & decrease the sugar to 1/4 c.
