Title: Cucumber Raita
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Nonfat Plain Yogurt
2 sm Cucumbers; Peeled, Seeded
-And Finely Diced (About 2
-Cups)
2 md Green Onions W/Tops; Finely
-Chopped, About 4 T.
1/8 ts Cayenne Pepper
In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth.  Add the remaining
ingredients. Blend well, cover, and chill until ready to serve.  Can
be made up to 2 hours in advance.
