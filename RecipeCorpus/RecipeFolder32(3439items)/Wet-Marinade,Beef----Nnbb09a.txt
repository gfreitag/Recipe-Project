Title: Wet Marinade/Beef    Nnbb09a
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Bourbon
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Worshestershire sauce
2/3 c  Soy sauce
2 c  Water
1 ea Cilantro -- chopped
3 ea Thyme -- chopped
Mix  all the ingredients together and marinade meat in refrigerator 8
to 12 hours, rotating meat occasionally.FROM:    MIKE CROUCH (NNBB09A)
