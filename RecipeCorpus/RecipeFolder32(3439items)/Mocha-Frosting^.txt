Title: Mocha Frosting^
Categories: Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Powdered sugar
1 ts Cocoa
2 tb Butter
1/4 ts Vanilla
2 tb Cold, strong coffee
Cream butter. Add sugar and cocoa gradually. Add vanilla, then coffee,
gradually until mixture is smooth, creamy and thick enough to spread.
