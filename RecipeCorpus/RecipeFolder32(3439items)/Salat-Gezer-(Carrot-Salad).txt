Title: Salat Gezer (Carrot Salad)
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
Dwigans fwds07a -- nathan
And goldman
4    Carrots
1    Handful raisins
1/4 c  Fresh orange juice
1/2 ts Sugar
Peel and grate the carrots. Mix in a bowl with the raisins, orange
juice and sugar. Add more or less sugar according to taste.

: "The Flavor of Jerusalem" by Nathan and Goldman
