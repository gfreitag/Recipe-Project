Title: Chinese Chicken Salad Dressing III
Categories: Chinese, Dress/stuff, Poultry, Salads
Yield: 1 batch
3 tb Dry sherry
1/2 ts Fresh ginger, grated
3 tb Soy sauce
1/2 c  ;Water
2 tb Minced green onion
3 tb Rice wine vinegar
2 tb Hoisin sauce
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Sesame oil
Combine and blend all ingredients.

Martinez writes: "I also use this as a regular salad dressing.  It
has a really good flavor."
