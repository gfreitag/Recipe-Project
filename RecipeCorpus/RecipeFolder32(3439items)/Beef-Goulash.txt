Title: Beef Goulash
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
3 lb Chuck
2 tb Fat
5    Onions -- diced
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Green peppers -- sliced
1 c  Canned tomato sauce
Cut the beef in 2-inch cubes.  Heat the fat in a heavy saucepan or
Dutch oven and brown the meat in it.  Stir in the onions and continue
to brown. Sprinkle with the salt, paprika and pepper.  Cover and cook
