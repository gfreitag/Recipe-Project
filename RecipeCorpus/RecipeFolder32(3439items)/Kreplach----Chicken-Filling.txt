Title: Kreplach -  Chicken Filling
Categories: Jewish, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Ground cooked chicken
4 tb Browned minced onion
1    Egg yolk
3/4 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 tb Minced parsley
Blend all ingredients together.  Place in squares of dough.    Recipe
