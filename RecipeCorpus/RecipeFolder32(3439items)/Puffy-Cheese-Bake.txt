Title: Puffy Cheese Bake
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
16 sl White bread, cut into cubes
1 lb Sharp cheese, grated
1/2 lb Butter, melted
8    Eggs
4 c  Milk (1 qt.)
Arrange bread in buttered rectangular casserole, sprinkle with grated
cheese and pour melted butter over cheese.  Beat eggs well; add milk
and pour over bread mixture.  Refrigerate overnight and bake about 1
hour at 350 degrees. Randy Rigg
