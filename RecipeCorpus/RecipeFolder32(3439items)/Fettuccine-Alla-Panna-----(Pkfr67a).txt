Title: Fettuccine Alla Panna
Categories: Pasta, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Unsalted butter
1/2 c  FRESH grated parmesan
Cheese
1/2 c  Cream
Put butter and cream in wide shallow serving bowl in warm (200 degree)
oven.  When butter has melted stir in 2/3 of the cheese. When pasta
is al dente, toss with sauce, sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Delicious. FROM: MONICA DEBIASE   (PKFR67A)
