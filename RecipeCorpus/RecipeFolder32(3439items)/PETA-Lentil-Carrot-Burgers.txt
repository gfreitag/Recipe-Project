Title: PETA Lentil Carrot Burgers
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 10 servings
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
1/4 c  Grated carrots
1/4 c  Water
3 c  Cooked lentils, mashed
2 tb Fresh parsley; chopped
3 tb Tomato paste
3/4 c  Bread crumbs
;salt to taste
;oil for frying
Cook the onion and carrot in the water until tender, about 10 mins.
Drain of excess water, then combine the onion and carrot with the
remaining ingredients except oil.  Form into patties and fry in a
lightly oiled frying pan until browned on both sides and heated
through, about 10 mins. From The Compassionate Cook by PETA and
Ingrid Newkirk Typed by Lisa Greenwood
