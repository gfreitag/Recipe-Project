Title: German Bundt Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Aust/german
Yield: 1 cake
1 1/4 c  Butter
2 1/4 c  Sugar
7    Eggs
2 1/2 c  Sifted flour
2 1/2 ts Vanilla
Cream butter and sugar well.  Add whole eggs, one at a time, beat
after each addition.  Add flour, all at once, and beat well.  Add
flavoring.

Bake  in greased and floured bundt pan at 325Ã¸ F. for one hour.

Cool ten minutes before removing from pan.
