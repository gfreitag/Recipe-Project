Title: Cheese Pancakes
Categories: Jewish, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 4 servings
2    Egg yolks
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Sugar
2 c  Cottage cheese -- drained
1 c  Flour -- sifted
2    Egg whites -- stiffly
Beaten
Butter -- for frying
Beat the egg yolks and sugar together.  Stir in the cottage cheese and
flour and fold in the egg whites.    Heat enough butter to cover the
bottom of a skillet about 1/4-inch. Drop the batter into it by the
tablespoon. Fry until browned on both sides. Serve hot, garnished
with sour cream and jelly.  Serves 4.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF
JEWISH COOKING by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc.
