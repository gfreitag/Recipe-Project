Title: Hamburger Mac-Casserole
Categories: Pasta, Main dish, Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 1 casserole
1 c  Leonardo elbow macaroni
2 tb Flour
1/2 ts Salt (optional)
1 lb Hamburger
2 tb Butter
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Shredded cheese
1/2 c  Buttered bread crumbs
Cook Leonardo elbows according to directions.  Drain. Place in
buttered casserole.  Melt butter in double boiler.  Add flour, salt
and pepper and blend.  Add milk slowly until sauce thickens.  Add
shredded cheese. Fry hamburger in fry pan, drain off excess fat, and
combine with elbows. Mix thoroughly, add sauce and top with bread
crumbs. Bake in moderate oven (350 degrees F) for 30 minutes.

Recipe from: Leonardo Elbow Macaroni box.
