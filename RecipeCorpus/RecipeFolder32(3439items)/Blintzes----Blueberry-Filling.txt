Title: Blintzes -  Blueberry Filling
Categories: Jewish
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Blueberries
3 tb Sugar
1 tb Cornstarch
1/8 ts Nutmeg
Toss all the ingredients together.  Fill pancakes.    Recipe Source:
