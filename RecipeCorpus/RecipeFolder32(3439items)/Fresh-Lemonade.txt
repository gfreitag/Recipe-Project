Title: Fresh Lemonade
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
1 c  Fresh lemon juice
1 1/2 c  Sugar
Mix together until sugar dissolves. Store concentrate in covered
glass jars or bottes, for one glass of lemonade mix 1/4 cup
concentrate with 3/4 cup iced water or to taste.

: "The Flavors of Jerusalem" by Nathan and Goldman
