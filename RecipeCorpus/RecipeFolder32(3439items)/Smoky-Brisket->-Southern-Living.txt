Title: Smoky Brisket > Southern Living
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Onion salt
1 ts Celery salt
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 tb Liquid smoke
2 1/2 lb Beef brisket
Combine seasonings, and pour over brisket; marinate overnight.  Place
brisket in a roaster.  Cover with aluminum foil; then place lid on
roaster. Bake at 250 degrees for 5 hours.  Do not remove lid during
baking. Let stand 15 minutes before slicing.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, December, 1976. Typed for you by
Nancy Coleman.
