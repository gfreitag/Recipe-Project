Title: Summer Vegetables And Cheese
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 sm Onion -- thinly sliced
2 tb Cooking oil
2 md Zucchini -- sliced
2 md Yellow squash -- sliced
3 md Tomatoes -- diced
8 oz Mozzarella cheese -- grated
Saute onion with oil in large skillet or wok.  Cook until clear.  Add
zucchini and yellow squash and cook until just tender.  Add tomatoes
and cheese and cook until cheese has just melted.  Serve immediately.

Source: " Favorite Recipes from Quilters" by Louise Stoltzfus
