Title: Quick Cheese-Olive Snacks
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Appetizers, Breads
Yield: 10 servings
1 c  American cheese, grated
1 cn Olives, ripe, drained 4.25oz
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
6    English muffins, split
Combine the cheese, olives and onion. spread over muffin halves.
Broil 4 in. from the heat for 5 minutes or until the cheese is
melted. Serve immediately.
