Title: Rhode Island Clam Cakes
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Flour
8 ts Baking Powder
3 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
4 ea Eggs
2 c  Milk
1 pt Clams, canned with liquid
2 c  Lard, or more -- For deep
Frying
Mix all of above (except lard).  Deep fry at 375 by dropping from
oiled spoon.  Salt and pepper after cooking.
