Title: Piroshki -  Prune Filling
Categories: Jewish, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Honey
3/4 c  Orange juice
2 ts Lemon juice
1 lb Unsweetened pitted prunes
1 tb Grated orange rind
Cook the honey, orange juice and lemon juice for 5 minutes.  Add
prunes and cook over low heat 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Drain and chop the prunes.  Add the orange rind.  Cool before filling
squares.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING
