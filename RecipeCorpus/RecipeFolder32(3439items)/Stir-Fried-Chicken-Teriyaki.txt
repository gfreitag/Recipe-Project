Title: Stir-Fried Chicken Teriyaki
Categories: Poultry, Vegetables, Wok
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Vegetable Oil
1/3 c  Soy Sauce
2 tb Light Molasses
2 ea Garlic cloves
2 ts Ground Ginger
2 ts Dry Mustard
5 ea Chicken Breasts
-(boneless/skinless, cut
-into strips)
1/2 c  Bamboo shoots
-(canned/sliced)
1 1/2 c  Thawed snow peas
4 ea Green Onions (sliced)
1 ea Fresh cooked Rice - to serve
Combine first 6 ingredients in bowl. Add chicken to marinade and stir.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour.  Drain Chicken, saving marinade. Heat
half of marinade in Wok over medium-high heat. Add chicken and
stir-fry 4 minutes. Add snow peas and bamboo shoots and stir-fry 1
minute. Add green onion, stir fry until chicken is cooked through,
about 1 minute. Serve with rice.
