Title: Veal Goulash
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Veal
2    Onions -- diced
3 tb Fat
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ts Paprika
2    Green peppers -- sliced
3/4 c  Canned tomato sauce
Cut the veal in 1 1/2-inch cubes.  Brown the veal and onions in the
fat. Add the salt, pepper, paprika, green peppers and tomato sauce.
Cover and cook over low heat 1 hour oruntil veal is tender.  Serve
with noodles or dumplings.  Serves 4 to 6.    Reci
