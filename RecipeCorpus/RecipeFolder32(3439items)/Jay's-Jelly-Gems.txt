Title: Jay's Jelly Gems
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 recipe
1 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
2    Egg yolks
2 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Almond flavoring
Jelly, flavor optional
Cream butter and sugar.  Add egg yolks, flour and flavoring.  Mix
well. Form into small balls.  Place on greased cookie sheet.  Press
thumb into each cookie.  Fill indentation with jelly.

Bake in oven @ 400Ã¸ until brown.
