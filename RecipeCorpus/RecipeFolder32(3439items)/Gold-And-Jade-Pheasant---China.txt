Title: Gold And Jade Pheasant - China
Categories: Vegetables, Chinese, Game, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
2    Pheasants-roasted and
Sliced
1    Onion-diced
6 c  Water
2 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Salt
1 ts Accent
12    Thin slices of ham
1 bn Broccoli
In a saucepan add the pheasants, water, salt and onion; bring to a
boil. Cover and cook over low heat for one and a half hours. Slice
the pheasant. Strain two cups of the broth.

Mix the Accent, cornstarch and a little water, stir into the broth.
Cook over low heat for five minutes. Taste for seasoning. On a large
platter rotate ham and pheasant, arrange the broccoli on the sides of
the meat. Pour sauce over all.
