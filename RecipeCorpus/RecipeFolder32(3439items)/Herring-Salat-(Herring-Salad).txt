Title: Herring Salat (Herring Salad)
Categories: Aust/german
Yield: 1 servings
6    Salt herring -- cleaned and
4 c  Apples -- peeled and
Washed
Quartered
2 c  Boiled beef
1 lg Onion
1 c  Dill pickles
Pepper to taste
1 c  Pickled beets
Put through food chopped, add pepper and mix. A little cream and
sugar may be added. Source: Amana Colony Recipes - Family-size
recipes of the foods prepared and served in the Amana Villages for
over a century. Compiled by The Ladies Auxiliary of the Home Recipe By
