Title: Red Wine and Thyme Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
2 c  Red wine
1/4 c  Thyme leaves
1/4 c  Shallots, minced
1/2 c  Veal stock
1 c  Butter (2 sticks)
-cut in cubes
Combine the red wine, thyme, and shallots and reduce over medium heat
until the liquid is gone. Add the stock and reduce over very low heat
until cup liquid remains. Slowly add the butter, keeping the heat
low, until the butter has melted. Strain the sauce and keep it warm
until you are ready to serve it. Serve with meats.
