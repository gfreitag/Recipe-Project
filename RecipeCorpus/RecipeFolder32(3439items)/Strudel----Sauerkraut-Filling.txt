Title: Strudel -  Sauerkraut Filling
Categories: Jewish, Pastry, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Sauerkraut
1/4 c  Minced onion
4 tb Butter or chicken fat
2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
Rinse the sauerkraut in cold water and drain.  Cook the sauerkraut and
onions in the butter or fat for 15 minutes. Stir in the sugar and
pepper. Cool.  Spread on strudel dough(two recipes for strudel dough
are included in this database) and roll up.  Ser
