Title: Dry Rub Masion Bbq Spice
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Paprika
1 tb Chili powder
1 ts Cumin
1 ts Coriander
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Cayenne
mix all ingredients together and use as desired to rub on meats before
grilling.
