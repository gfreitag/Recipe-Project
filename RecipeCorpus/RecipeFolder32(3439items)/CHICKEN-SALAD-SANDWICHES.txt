Title: CHICKEN SALAD SANDWICHES
Categories: Poultry, Salads, Sandwich
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Cooked diced chicken
1/2 c  Diced celery
1/2 c  Diced roasted sweet red
-peppers
1/2 c  Diced green pepper
1/2 c  Diced mushrooms
Source: MAINPOUL.ZIP

Mayonnaise and lemon juice

Mix together chicken, celery, red and green peppers and mushrooms. Add
enough mayonnaise and lemon juice to moisten and give good flavor.
Use as a sandwich filling.
