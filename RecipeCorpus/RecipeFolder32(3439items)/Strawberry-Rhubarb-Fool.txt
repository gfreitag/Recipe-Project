Title: Strawberry-Rhubarb Fool
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
1 ea White Cake Mix (2 layer
-size)
1 ea Coconut Cream or Vanilla
-instant pudding (4 serv)
1 ea Eggs
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Wesson Oil
1 c  Flaked coconut
1/3 c  Bacardi Dark Rum
Frosting
1 ea Coconut Cream or Vanilla
-Instant pudding (4 serv)
1/3 c  Bacardi Dark Rum
1 ea 8oz can crushed pineapple
-(in juice)
1 ea 9 o Container frozen
-whipped topping, thawed
6 c  Chopped rhubarb in 1/2 inch
-peices
1 1/2 c  Strawberries
1 ts Grated lemon rind
3 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Water
1 c  Sugar
2 c  Whipping cream
In a heavy bottomed saucepan, combine rhubarb, strawberries, lemon
rind and juice, water and sugar.  Cover and cook over low heat,
stirring and occasionally, until fruit is tender, about 20 minutes.

Cool, puree and taste, adding more (or less) sugar if desired.
(Recipe may be prepared in advance to this point and refrigerated for
4 or 5 days). Whip cream and fold into fruit.  Looks lovely served in
stemmed glasses with any crisp buttery cookie.

Makes 10-12 servings
