Title: SOUTHWEST PIZZA
Categories: Pizza, Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Cornmeal
1    - 10 oz can Pillsbury all
-ready pizza crust
1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 c  EACH:diced green and red
-bell pepper
1    4 oz can green
-chilies,drained
1    8 oz pkg. shredded Colby and
-Monterey Jack cheese
1/2 c  Chopped cilantro
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.Sprinkle cornmeal onto bottom of a
greased 10 x 15" jelly roll pan.Spread dough into pan.Bake  5
minutes.Remove from oven.Spread with combined tomato sauce and chili
powder;top with bell peppers,chilies and cheese tossed with
cilantro.Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until crust is golden brown. Makes
6 to 8 servings.
