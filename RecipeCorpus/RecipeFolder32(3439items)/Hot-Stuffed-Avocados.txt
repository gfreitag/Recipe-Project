Title: Hot Stuffed Avocados
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Butter -- melted
2 tb Flour
1 c  Light cream (half & half)
1/2 ts Worcestershire sacue
2 tb Onion -- finely minced
2 tb Celery -- finely minced
2 c  Chicken -- cooked and cubed
3    Ripe avocados -- peeled and
In
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese -- grated
Water
Half
Place the melted butter and flour in a saucepan.  Cook over a high
heat for 1 minute to lightly brown, stirring constantly. Stir in the
cream. Continue cooking, stirring constantly, until thick. Turn the
heat down and add the worcestershire sauce, onion, celery, and
chicken. Heat thoroughly but, do not boil. Place the avocado halves
in a baking dish. Fill with chicken mixture. Sprinkle cheese on top.
Pour 1/4 inch of water in bottom of baking dish. Bake in a preheated
350 degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until the avocados are hot
and the cheese is melte Makes 6 servings.
