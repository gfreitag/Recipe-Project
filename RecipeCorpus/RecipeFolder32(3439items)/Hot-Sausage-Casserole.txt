Title: Hot Sausage Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 lb Hot Italian sausage, cut in
1/2 inch slices
1 c  Chopped onions
2 ts Oregano
1 cn Cheddar cheese soup
1 cn Tomato soup
1 c  Water
4 c  Cooked wide noodles
4 oz Cheddar cheese, cut up
In a skillet, brown beef, sausage and onion with oregano, until done.
Stir to seperate meat, use shortening if necessary.  Pour off fat.
Add soups and water.  Chill 1 hour.  In separate container, chill
noodles. Combine meat and noodles.  Pour in 12 x 8 inch pan.  Cover
and place in a cold oven. Bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes.  Top
with Cheddar cheese until hot and melted. Randy Rigg
