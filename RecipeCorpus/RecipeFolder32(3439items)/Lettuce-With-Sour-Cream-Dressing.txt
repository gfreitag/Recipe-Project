Title: Lettuce With Sour-Cream Dressing
Categories: Jewish, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
6    Scallions -- sliced
1    Lettuce head
Mix together the sour cream, vinegar, salt, pepper and scallions.
Tear (don't cut) the lettuce into small pieces and pour the
dressing over it. Toss.  Serves 4.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF
JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Boo
