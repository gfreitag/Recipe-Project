Title: Macaroni and Cheese with Spaghetti Sauce
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1 lb Macaroni
1 lb Lean ground beef
15 oz Can of Chef-Boy-Ar-Dee
Spaghetti sauce with
Mushrooms
Garlic salt, pepper, cumin,
Oregano to taste
1/2 lb Sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
1 md Onion, chopped
Brown ground beef, chopped onion together in nongreased frying pan.
Season with garlic salt, pepper, cumin, oregano.  Add
Chef-Boy-Ar-Dee's sauce. Simmer for 40 minutes.  Add more sauce or
water if necessary. Boil macaroni; drain.  Alternate macaroni, sauce,
cheese. Repeat. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   07-12-95
