Title: Western Style Bbq  Trgs42a
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Ketchup,
1/2 c  Lemon juice,
1/4 c  Onion ; minced
1 ts Hot pepper
1 ts Worcestershire sauce.
Place all ingredients in saucepan.  Bring to a boil and simmer 30
minutes or until slightly thickened.
