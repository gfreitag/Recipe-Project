Title: Pasta Nicoise
Categories: Seafood, Pasta, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 lb Green beans; fresh, sliced
-diagonally into 1-inch
-pieces & lightly steamed
1/4 c  (pitted) Kalamata olives
1/4 c  Red onion; chopped
1/2 c  Flat leaf parsley; minced
1 tb Capers (optional)
16 ea Cherry tomatoes; halved
1 ea Clove garlic; minced
2 tb Olive oil
3 tb Red wine vinegar
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 tb Fresh oregano; minced
2 cn (6.5-oz.) cans water-packed
-tuna; drained and flaked
1 lb Rotini (corkscrew pasta)
PREP TIME:  15 minutes, plus 1 hour to marinate

COOKING TIME:  10 minutes
1.  Combine green beans, olives, onion, parsley, capers and tomatoes
in a large bowl.  Combine garlic, olive oil, vinegar, mustard and
oregano in a small jar and shake well.  Pour dressing over vegetables
and toss well. Let marinate in refrigerator 1 hour.

2.  Cook rotini according to package directions.  Rinse and drain
well. Toss warm pasta with tuna and marinated vegetables.  Serve
immediately.

PER SERVING:  384 calories; 8g fat; 19% fat calories; 22mg
cholesterol; 4g fiber.

Source:  Kathryn Arnold in "Delicious! Your Magazine of Natural
Living," (natural foods store give-away) July 1995.
