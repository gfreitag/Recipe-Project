Title: Whipped Cream Dessert^
Categories: Desserts, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 pt Whipping cream, beat stiff
1/2 c  Pineapple, cubed
Few marshmallows, chopped
Fine
Few black walnuts, chopped
Mix all ingredients together and serve in dessert or parfait glasses.
Serve with nut wafers. This delicious dessert is very fine and does
not take long to prepare. Enough for 6 people - it's very rich.
