Title: Pepper Salad
Categories: Jewish, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
8    Green or red peppers
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1    Bay leaf
1 c  Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Water
Cut the peppers in half, discarding the seeds and fibers. Pierce each
pepper half with a fork and hold it over a flame or heat until the
skin browns and blisters.  Rub the skin off, then slice the peppers.
