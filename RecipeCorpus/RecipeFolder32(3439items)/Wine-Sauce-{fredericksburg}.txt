Title: Wine Sauce {fredericksburg}
Categories: Aust/german, Salsa/sauce, Desserts
Yield: 1 recipe
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1    Well-beaten egg
3/4 c  Wine
1 pn Nutmeg
1/2 ts Lemon extract
Mix and before serving set in a bowl of hot water until warm.  Do
not cook.  Stir until well blended.  Especially good served over
bread pudding or rice puddings.

~ Mrs. Victor Nixon, Fredericksburg, Tx typed & posted by teri
