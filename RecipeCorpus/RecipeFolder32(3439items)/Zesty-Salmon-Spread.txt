Title: Zesty Salmon Spread
Categories: Salads, Fish, Appetizers, Sandwich
Yield: 4 appetizers
1 lb Salmon; cooked and flaked
2    Celery stalks; with
-leaves, finely diced
1/2 sm Onion; finely diced
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Dill weed
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts French Mustard OR
1 ts Horseradish
1 ds Tabasco sauce OR
1 ds Louisiana Hot sauce
Mix Salmon, onions and celery in a bowl. Add the other ingredients
and mix. Chill mixture thoroughly. Serve in scoops on a lettuce leaf
or on canape crackers. Also makes an excellent filling for salmon
sandwiches or croissant.
