Title: Baked Herring And Potatoes
Categories: Jewish, Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
6    Salt herring fillets
3 tb Butter
4    Boiled potatoes -- peeled
And sliced
2    Onions -- sliced
2 tb Dry bread crumbs
Soak the herring overnight in cold water to cover.  Drain and cut into
1-inch pieces.    Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a baking dish. Arrange
layers of the potatoes, herring and onions in it, starting and ending
with the potatoes.  Sprinkle with the bread crumbs and dot with
remaining butter. Bake in a 400 deg
