Title: Barbeque Sauce With Beer Robert
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Meats
Yield: 12 servings
2 ea 14-oz bottles catsup
2 tb Black pepper
1 ea 12-oz bottle chili suace
1 ea 5-oz bottle steak sauce
1/2 c  Prepared mustard
1/2 c  Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Dry mustard
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Salt
1 ea 12-oz bottle beer
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar -- firmly
Packed
2 ts Mince garlic
Combine all ingredients, except garlic, in saucepan and simmer 30
minutes over medium heat.  Add minced garlic before using.  Baste
meat during last 15 minutes of grilling time.
