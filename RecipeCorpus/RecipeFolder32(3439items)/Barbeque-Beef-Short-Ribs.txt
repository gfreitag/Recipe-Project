Heat oven at 350~F.  Heat the 3 tablespoons fat in a heavy skillet.
Add the minced garlic and short ribs and brown well on both sides.
Transfer meat and dripping to a large casserole or Dutch oven.  Slice

Cover very tightly and bake in oven 1-1/2 hours or until meat is very
tender when pierced with a fork. To serve, place short ribs on a large
platter and pour sauce over the meat. Serves 4.
