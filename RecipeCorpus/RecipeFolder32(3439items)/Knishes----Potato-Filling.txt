Title: Knishes -  Potato Filling
Categories: Jewish, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chopped onions
6 tb Chicken fat or butter
2 c  Mashed potatoes
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Brown the onions in the fat or butter.  Beat in the potatoes, egg,
salt and pepper until fluffy.  Place on dough.    Recipe Source: THE
ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam
Books, Inc.
