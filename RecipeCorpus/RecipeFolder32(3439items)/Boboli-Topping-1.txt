Title: Boboli Topping 1
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Ethnic
Yield: 1 servings
Classic Pizza Toppings
1/4 c  Cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Tomato (or Spaghetti sauce)
1/2 c  Pepperoni -- sliced
Yours or commercial
1/2 c  Mushrooms; steamed --
Drained
1/2 c  Mozzarellah preserves
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese -- freshly
3/4 c  Ground beef -- browned and
Grated
Drained*
Lightly coat BOBOLI with sauce. Top with your choice of the above
toppings. *Brown ground beef (or turkey if you prefer) with onion,
crushed garlic, and any herbs you like. Salt and pepper to taste.
Bake in oven 10-15 minutes at 400 degrees until cheese is melted.
