Title: Chicken Soup  <meat>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1    Soup chicken
Chicken feet -- * see note
3 1/2 qt Water
2    Onions
1 tb Salt
2    Carrots
3    Celery stalks
1    Parsley root
2    Sprigs Dill
3    Sprigs Parsley
* Note:  The feet add strength to the soup, so use as many as you can
get. Clean the chicken and feet thoroughly.  Combine in a deep
saucepan with the water and onions.  Bring to a boil and cook over
medium heat 1 1/2 hours. Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and cook
over low heat 1 hour longer, or until chicken is tender.  Remove
chicken and strain soup.    Makes about 2 to 2 1/2 quarts soup.  Use
the chicken in other dishes or serve with the soup.    Recipe Source:
THE ARTOF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam
Books, Inc.
