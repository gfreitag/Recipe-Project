Title: Dairy Liver
Categories: Jewish, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Salad oil
1 1/2 lb Mushrooms -- sliced
1/2 c  Diced onion
1    Hard-boiled egg
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Heat the oil in a skillet.  Cook the mushrooms and onion over medium
heat for 10 minutes.    Chop the mushrooms, onion, egg, salt and
pepper together until smooth.  Chill.  Serves 6 as an appetizer or 12
as a spread. Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH CO
