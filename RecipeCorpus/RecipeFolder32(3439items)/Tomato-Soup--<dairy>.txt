Title: Tomato Soup  <dairy>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Tomatoes
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Canned tomatoes
1 c  Water
4 tb Grated onion
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Sugar
1 c  Scalded milk
2/3 c  Cooked rice
Over low heat, cook tomatoes, water, onion, salt and sugar for 30
minutes.
Force through a food mill.  Stir in the milk and rice.  Serve hot.
Serves 6.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie
Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc.
