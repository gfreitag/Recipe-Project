Title: Mocha Torte
Categories: Jewish, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Flour -- sifted
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
4    Egg yolks
1 c  Powdered sugar
4 tb Ice water
1 tb Coffee essence
1 ts Vanilla
4    Egg whites -- stiffly
Beaten
