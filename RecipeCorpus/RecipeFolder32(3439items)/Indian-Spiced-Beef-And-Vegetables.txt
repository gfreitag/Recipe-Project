Title: Indian Spiced Beef And Vegetables
Categories: Ethnic, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 ts Cumin seeds
1 lg Onion -- finely chopped
1/2 ts Ground coriander
1 1/2 c  Tomato juice
1/2 ts Garam masala*
1 c  Peas -- thawed
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 1/2 ts Salt
Freshly cooked rice
1/2 ts Ground cumin
EILEEN LAMPARELLI
CGDR39A-----
Cook beef and onion in heavy large skillet over medium heat until met
is no longer pink, crumbling with fork, about 10 minutes. Add all
remaining ingredients, except rice. Cover and cook 15 minutes. Serve
with rice. GARAM MASALA - An Indian spice mixture To substitute, mix
3/4 ts. ground cumin with 3/4 ts. ground coriander, 1/2 ts. pepper,
1/2 ts. ground cardamon, 1/4 ts. ground cloves, and 1/4 ts. ground
cinnamon. Use 1/2 ts. of spice mixture for this recipe. SOURCE:
Shere-Punjab Restaurant, Toronto, Canada
