Title: Seafood Newburg
Categories: Jewish, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
4 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
2 c  Light cream
1 1/2 c  Cubed cooked salmon
1 1/2 c  Cubed cooked halibut
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Paprika
3 tb Sherry
3    Egg yolks
Melt the butter in the top of a double boiler; stir in the flour, and
gradually add 1 cup of the cream, stirring steadily until mixture
reaches the boiling point.  Add the salmon, halibut, salt, paprika
and sherry. Place over hot water and cook 5 minutes
