Title: Baked Apples - House Beautiful
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
Kanten coulis, recipe
-follows
6 lg Rome apples, washed, cored
-3/4 of the way through and
-1 to 1 1/2 inches of skin
-around the top of each
-apple removed
In a baking dish just large enough to hold the apples, spoon about
1/2-inch of the Kanten coulis, place apples on top and fill each hole
with Kanten coulis. Bake 30 to 45 minutes in a preheated 350' oven
until the apples are soft but retain their shape. Serve warm or at
room temperature. Serves 6.
