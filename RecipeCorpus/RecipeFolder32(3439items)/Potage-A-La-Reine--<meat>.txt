Title: Potage A La Reine  <meat>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Chicken fat
3 tb Minced onion
1/4 c  Flour
4 c  Chicken broth
1/2 c  Shredded chicken
Heat the chicken fat and cook the onion in it 10 minutes. Add the
flour and then gradually the broth, stirring constantly until mixture
reaches the boiling point.  Add the chicken and cook over low heat 10
minutes. Serves 4 to 5.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING
by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc. (c) 1958 Recipe
