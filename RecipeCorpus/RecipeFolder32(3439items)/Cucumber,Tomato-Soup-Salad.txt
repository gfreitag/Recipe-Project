Title: Cucumber/Tomato Soup Salad
Categories: Vegetables, Soup/stew, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
10 3/4 oz Cream of Tomato Soup
2 pk Gelatin
1 c  Cottage cheese, drained
1/2 c  Celery, diced
1/2 c  Cucumbers, diced
Mayonnaise (optional)
Sprinkle gelatin over soup and let stand 5 minutes.  Add other
ingredients and pour into molds.  Chill until set.  Serve with
dollops of mayonnaise, if desired.
