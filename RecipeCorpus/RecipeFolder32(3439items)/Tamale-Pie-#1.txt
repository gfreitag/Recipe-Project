Title: Tamale Pie #1
Categories: Tex/mexican, Meats, Pie/tart, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
2 lb Ground Beef
12 oz Tomatoes
1    Med. Onion, Chopped
1    Clove Garlic, Chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Oregano
3 1/2 oz (1/2 7oz can) Green Chilies
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 c  Grated Mild Cheese
1/2 c  Cornmeal
1/2 c  Unbleached Flour
1 ts Baking powder
Dash Salt
2 tb Oil
2 ts Sugar (Optional)
1/2 c  Milk
Other Half Green Chilies
Brown meat in skillet.  Add remainging ingredients and simmer 20
minutes. Pour into a 4-qt casserole.  Top with cheese. Mix rest of
ingredients together and pour over the top of meat and cheese.  Bake
at 375 degrees F. for 30 minutes (or until topping is done).
