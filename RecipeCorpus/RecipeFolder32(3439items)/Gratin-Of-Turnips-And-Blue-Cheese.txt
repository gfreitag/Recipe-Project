Title: Gratin Of Turnips And Blue Cheese
Categories: Vegetables, Guestchefs
Yield: 4 servings
4 md Turnips -- peeled
And sliced paper thin
1 md Yellow onion -- peeled and
Finely sl
4 tb Melted butter
1/2 c  Whipping cream
4    Anchovy fillets -- finely
Chopped
1/4 ts Nutmeg -- preferably freshly
1/4 ts Freshly ground pepper
1/2 c  Crumbled Roquefort -- OR
Stilton
Or
Other blue cheese
PREHEAT OVEN TO 375F. Toss together the turnips and onion with the
melted butter and place in a 9-inch square or round baking dish.
Cover tightly and place in preheated oven for 30 minutes. In a small
pot over medium heat, combine cream, anchovies, nutmeg
