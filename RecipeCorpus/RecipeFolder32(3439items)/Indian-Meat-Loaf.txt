Title: Indian Meat Loaf
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 9 servings
4 c  Kellogg's Corn Flakes
2    Eggs,slightly beaten
1 c  Cream style corn
1 c  Cooked tomatoes
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1 tb Chopped onions
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Sage
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 lb Ground pork
Crush corn flakes slightly; combine with remaining ingredients; mix
thoroughly.Spread in greased 9x9 pan.Bake in moderate oven 350
degrees for about 1 hour.Cut into squares to serve.
