Title: Crock Pot Cheese Potatoes
Categories: Crockpot
Yield: 3 servings
5 lb Potatoes
Salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2    Sticks of margarine
3 c  Shredded mild Cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Milk
Boil potatoes in skins until partially cooked; cool, peel, and grate.
Divide potatoes in 3 equal portions.  In a large round crockpot, put
in a layer of grated potatoes, salt and pepper, 1/2 stick of butter,
cut into pats, 1 cup shredded cheese.  Repeat this procedure two more
times, then pour milk over this. Cover and cook on MEDIUM heat for 2
1/2 hours, the last 1/2 hour put on HIGH. Randy Rigg
