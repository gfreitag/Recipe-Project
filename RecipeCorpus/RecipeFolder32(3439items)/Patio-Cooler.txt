Title: Patio Cooler
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 3 quarts
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
2 c  Tea, strongly brewed
4 c  Orange juice
2 c  Pineapple juice
28 oz Ginger ale, chilled
1    Orange, sliced
Combine sugar and water in pan over low heat until dissolved.  Cool.
Combine with tea and fruit juices.  Chill thoroughly.  Just before
serving, stir in ginger ale.  Garnish with orange slices.

Recipe from Denise Rounds.  Converted to Meal-Master format by Arthur
Cloninger.
