Title: Citrus and Cilantro Vinaigrette
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
1/2    Grapefruit
1    Orange
1    Lemon
1    Lime
1 ts Honey
1 tb Sherry vinegar
3 tb Olive oil
1 tb Cilantro, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
Juice the grapefruit, orange, lemon, and lime and combine the juice
in a mixing bowl. Add the honey, vinegar, olive oil, and cilantro and
mix well. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper. Refrigerate
until ready to serve.
