Title: Kielbasa and Meatballs in Beer
Categories: Appetizers, Meats, Sausage
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1 md Onion, chopped fine
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Pinch of garlic powder
2 lb Kielbasa, cut in 1/4 inch
Slices
16 oz Beer
1 lg Bottle of ketchup
3    Peppers, diced
3    Onions, diced
1/2 c  Water
Mix beer, ketchup, 3 peppers, 3 onions and water in a large pan; add
sliced Kielbasa and simmer for 1 hour.  Mix ground beef, bread
crumbs, onion, salt, pepper, garlic powder.  Shape into balls.  Drop
into Kielbasa mixture and simmer another hour.  Skim off grease and
serve or let cool and reheat slowly before serving.
