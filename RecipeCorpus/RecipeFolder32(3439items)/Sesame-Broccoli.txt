Title: Sesame Broccoli
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Salad oil
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Sesame seeds, toasted
1 tb Soy sauce
4 ts Sugar
Cook 1 pound of fresh broccoli in a small amount of boiling, salted
water until tender, about 15 minutes; drain.  Set aside. In a
saucepan, combine the aforementioned ingredients.  Heat to boiling,
pour over broccoli. Randy Rigg
