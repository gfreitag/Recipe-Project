Title:  BEEF ENCHILADA CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Tex/mexican, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion, chopped
1 lb Can refried beans
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Garlic powder
Oil
1/3 c  Taco sauce
19 oz Can enchilada sauce
1    Dozen corn tortillas
10 oz Cheddar cheese, grated
Saute beef and onions; drain.  Stir in beans, salt, garlic powder,
and taco sauce.  Heat until bubbly.  Add enchilada sauce.  In a
separate fry pan, pour in enough oil to about 1/4" and heat.  Soften
tortillas, one at a time, by quickly dipping in hot oil, turning, and
draining on paper towels.

In a 3 quart casserole, make layers of tortillas and meat sauce.  Top
with grated cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 45 minutes
or until heated through.
