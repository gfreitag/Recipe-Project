Title: Cucumbers With Dill And Sour Cream
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
Dwigans fwds07a -- nathan
And goldman
3 lg Cucumbers
2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Vinegar -- cider or white
2 tb Cold water
1 ts Sugar
3 tb Chopped fresh dill
Freshly ground black pepper
To taste
Sour cream
Peel the cucumbers and slice very thin, Place in a colander, sprinkle
with salt and set aside for 1/2 hour.  Mix the vinegar, water, sugar,
and dill, combine with the cucumber slices and mix well. Sprinkle the
mxture with pepper and chill until icy cold. Serve with a large
spoonful of sour cream on each portion.
