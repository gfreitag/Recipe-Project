Title: Fusilli W/Broccoli & Garlic
Categories: Italian, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Broccoli flowerttes
6 c  Garlic -- thinly sliced
1/3 c  Olive oil
Salt & pepper -- to taste
Saute garlic in olive oil until JUST beginning to brown. DO NOT allow
garlic to brown. At the FIRST sight of browning the garlic is
finished. Any more cooking will make the garlicbitter. Add broccoli &
toss to coat, then add this mixture to the hot, drain Season w/salt &
pepper to taste & enjoy! SUSAN FROM: SUSAN AARONSON (PSTT79C)
