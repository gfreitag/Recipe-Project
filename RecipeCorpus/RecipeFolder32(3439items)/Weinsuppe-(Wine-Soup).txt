Title: Weinsuppe (Wine Soup)
Categories: Soup/stew, Aust/german
Yield: 1 recipe
1 qt Water
1/4 c  Whole tapioca
1 c  Wine
1/4 c  Raisins
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  Sugar
4    Slices lemon
1 sm Piece of stick cinnamon
1 1/2 c  Toasted bread cubes
Bring water to boil.  Add the whole tapioca, cinnamon, lemon slices,
and raisins.  Cook until clear and raisins are done.  Take from heat
and add sugar, wine and vanilla.  Just before serving add bread cubes
toasted in a tablespoon butter
