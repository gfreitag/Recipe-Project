Title: Pheasant Kabab - India
Categories: Game, Herbs/spice, Indian
Yield: 1 servings
3    Pheasants-whole
2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Coriander
1 ts Ginger
2 1/2 c  Onions-chopped
2 ts Turmeric
4 tb Butter-melted
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1/2 c  Yogurt
Wash and dry the pheasant; prick the skin with a fork. Mix together
the salt, pepper, coriander and ginger. Rub the pheasants with the
spice mixture. Roast in a moderate 350 oven for twenty five minutes.
