Title: Beef And Lima Bean Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Homemade
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn (12 Oz)
2 c  Baby Lima Beans, Fresh Raw
OR
1 pk Baby Lima Beans, Frozen
Beer
4 tb Vegetable Oil
2 1/2 lb Lean Beef Cubes
1    Clove Garlic -- minced
1 lg Onion -- diced
1/4 ts Dried Thyme
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
Place oil in a Dutch oven. Add beef cubes and brown. Add garlic,
onion, and seasonings. Saute for 5 mins more. Add beer, cover, and
simmer for 2 hours. or until meat is just about tender. Add water if
necessary, but only small amounts at a time, since a fairly
concentrated essence is desired. After the 2 hrs, add lima beans and
continue cooking another half hour. Serve over steaming hot rice.
Yield: 4 servings
