Title: Light Banana Freeze
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
-concentrate, thawed OR
-fresh orange juice
1 tb Honey; or to taste
4    Ripe bananas; cut into large
-chunks
3 tb Frozen orange juice
This is best eaten right away or it will lose its smooth consistency.

1.  Place the bananas in a freezer container.  Immediately sprinkle
with orange juice, seal the container, and freeze for 4 to 6 hours or
overnight.

2.  Just before serving, add the honey to a food processor bowl, drop
the banana slices, a few at a time, into the bowl, and puree until the
consistency of soft ice cream.

3.  Serve immediately, garnished with thin slices of orange.

163 calories per serving
