Title: Cauliflower Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 lb Bacon; fried and crumbled
1/3 c  Parmesan cheese
1/2 c  White sugar
1/2 c  Cashews; chopped
1    Head lettuce; chopped
1    Raw head cauliflower; chop
2 c  Mayonnaise
1 md Onion; chopped
Layer in order given in large bowl; lettuce, cauliflower, mayonnaise,
onion, bacon, cheese, sugar, and cashews. Should be make at least 24
hours before serving. Toss together before serving.

In an almost identiacal recipe, it was suggested to mix the cheese,
sugar and mayo separately, add just before serving, and toss.
Recipe from Roger's Department Store Cookbook
