Title: Boboli Pizza Marietta
Categories: Pizza, Italian, Sausage
Yield: 1 servings
4    Italian sausages; cooked;
-sliced
8 sl Red onion, thin, separated
1/2 c  Pizza OR tomato sauce
1 lg Boboli or 4 small
1 c  Green pepper; thin strips
1 c  Mozzarella cheese, shredded
Spread sauce on Boboli.  Sprinkle with cheese. Arrange remaining
ingredients on top.  Bake at 450 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.
