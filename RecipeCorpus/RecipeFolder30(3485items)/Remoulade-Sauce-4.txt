Title: Remoulade Sauce 4
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Wine vinegar
1 tb Capers
1/2 c  Chopped parsley
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Onion juice
1 c  Mayonnaise (homemade)
1 ts Chopped anchovies
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 tb Tarragon vinegar
1 tb Dry sherry
Mix all ingredients together.  Make one day before using.

Recipe from Mrs. John B. Goodhue, Beaumont, TX.

Source: Talk About Good! Cookbook
