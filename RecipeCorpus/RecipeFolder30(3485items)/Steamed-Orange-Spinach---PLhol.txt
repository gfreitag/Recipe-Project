Title: Steamed Orange-Spinach - PLhol
Categories: Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Pignoli (pine nuts) or
-chopped walnuts
1 lb Fresh spinach
3 tb Orange rind
Juice of 1/2 orange
Stem and wash spinach, shaking off excess water.  Place spinach,
orange rind, and orange juice in a large skillet or wok over high
heat.  Toss spinach until just tender.  Toss with pignoli.  Serve.
