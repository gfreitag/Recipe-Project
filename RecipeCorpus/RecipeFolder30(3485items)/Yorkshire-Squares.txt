Title: Yorkshire Squares
Categories: Meats, Casseroles
Yield: 5 servings
1 1/2 tb Onion -- grated
1 ts Chopped Parsley
1 ts Chopped Chives
1/2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
2 ts Salt
Pepper
1 c  All-Purpose Flour
1/3 ts Salt
1 c  Milk -- divided
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Shortening Or Drippings
1 1/2 lb Ground Beef
Combine flour and 1/3 tsp salt in a bowl and make well in the center;
pour in 1/2 cup of milk and beat thoroughly. Add whole eggs, one at a
time, beating after each addition. Add remainder of milk, and beat.
Heat drippings in a baking pan until hot, but not smoking; add half
of the batter; spread with mixture of meat and seasonings; add
remainder of batter and bake at 400 F for 20 mins. Lower temperature
to 350 F and continue baking for 20 to 25 mins longer. Yield: 5 to 6
servings.
