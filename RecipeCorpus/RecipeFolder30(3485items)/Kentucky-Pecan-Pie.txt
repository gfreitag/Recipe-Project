Title: Kentucky Pecan Pie
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Vanilla
2 lg Lightly Beaten Eggs
1 1/4 c  Shelled Whole Pecans
1 c  White Corn Syrup
1 c  Packed Dark Brown Sugar
1/3 ts Salt
1/3 c  Melted Butter
Combine the syrup, sugar, salt, butter and vanilla.  Mix well.  Add
the slightly beaten eggs.  Pour into a 9" unbaked pie shell.
Sprinkle pecans over all.  Bake in a 350Ã¸F oven for 45 minutes.  When
cool, top with whipped cream or ice cream.  From: Syd's  Cookbook.
