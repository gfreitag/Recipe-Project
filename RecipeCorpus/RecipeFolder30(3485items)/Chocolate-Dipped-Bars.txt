Title: Chocolate Dipped Bars
Categories: Snacks, Kids
Yield: 40 bars
5 c  Triples cereal
1 1/2 c  Chocolate chips
2 tb Shortening
3 tb Margarine
25 lg Marshmallows; or 3 1/2 cups
-miniature marshmallows
Butter 13  x 9 pan.  Place margarine and marshmallows in large
microwave bowl.  Microwave uncovered on high 1 1/2 to 3 minutes,
stirring after 1 minute, until mixture is melted and smooth when
stirred. Stir in cereal until well coated.  Press mixture in pan,
using buttered back of spoon; cool completely.  Cut into 2 1/2 x 1"
bars. Mix chocolate chips and shortening in 4 cup microwave measure.
Microwave uncovered on high 1 to 2 minutes or until chips are melted;
stir.  Dip about one half of each bar into chocolate mixture to coat;
place on waxed paper to cool. Source: Triples box
