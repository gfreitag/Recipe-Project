Title: Chicken Mac Bake
Categories: Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Swanson chunk white chicken
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Buttered bread crumbs
1 1/2 c  Shredded, sharp cheddar
Cheese
1 c  Cream of mushroom soup
3/4 c  Milk
4 c  Cooked macaroni
Combine all but 1/2 cup of cheese and the bread crumbs.  Pour into 1
1/2 quart baking dish.  Cover and bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes
or until hot.  Stir and top with bread crumbs and cheese; return to
oven until melted. Randy Rigg
