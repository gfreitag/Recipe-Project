Title: Cashew and Mustard Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce, ', Side dish, Appetizers
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Honey
3 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Oil
1 ts Fine black pepper
2 tb Tarragon vinegar
1/2 ts Chilli powder
1 c  Water
2 c  Roasted salted cashews
1 ts French mustard
This creamy puree of nuts is spiked with mustard, chilli and pepper
for a great sauce to accompany grilled meats and seafood.

Puree all together until smooth and creamy. Makes about 2 1/2 cups.
Mix will keep for up to 1 week in fridge.
