Title: Holiday Unbeatables
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 25 servings
1/2 c  Egg whites (3 to 4 whites)
2 c  Chopped walnuts
1 c  Chopped Candied Cherries
2 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking Powder
In mixing bowl combine sugar, flour, baking powder and egg whites.
Stir until thoroughly blended. Add walnuts and cherries, mix well.
Drop by teaspoonfuls on greased and floured cookie sheets. Bake at
325 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes, until light brown. Let stand 3
minutes, then remove.

Source: Teresa Sellis.  Came from a recipe her son made in school.
