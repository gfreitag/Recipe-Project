Title: Cream Cheese-Orange Spread+
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Almonds or hazlenuts,
-toasted and chopped
1 pk Cream cheese (3 oz.)
Frozen orange juice
Combine the cream cheese with the frozen orange juice to taste. The
mixture should be of a smooth spreading consistancy. stir in the
chopped nuts and serve with nut or banana bread.

recipe found in "Cooking with Love and Paprika" by Joe Pasternak
