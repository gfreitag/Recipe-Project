Title: Peach Or Apricot Muffins
Categories: Tasteofhome, Muffins
Yield: 10 servings
1/3 c  Butter -- melted
1    Egg -- beaten
1 ts Vanilla Extract
1 c  Milk, Or 1 Cup Sour Cream
2/3 c  Fresh Peaches Or Apricots --
Peeled and chopped
2 c  All-Purpose Flour -- sifted
1/3 c  Brown Sugar -- firmly
Packed
3 ts Baking Powder
1/4 ts Baking Soda
1/4 ts Ground Allspice
Preheat oven to 400 F. Combine sifted flour with dry ingredients and
sift twice more. Stir melted butter, beaten egg, and vanilla into
milk or sour cream. Add to flour mixture, stirring just enough to
moisten. Add chopped peaches or apricots. Stir only until mixed.
Batter should look lumpy. Fill greased muffin cups 2/3 full. Bake for
25 mins or until golden brown. Yield: 10 to 12 muffins.
