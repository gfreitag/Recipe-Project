Title: Guacamole, RO*TEL Style
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 quart
ds Hot pepper sauce
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
3    Ripe avacados, peeled and
-mashed
1 cn RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and
-Green Chilies, drained
-(10 oz)
1 md Onion, chopped
Combine all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours.
