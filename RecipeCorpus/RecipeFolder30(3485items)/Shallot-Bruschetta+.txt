Title: Shallot Bruschetta+
Categories: Italian, Appetizers
Yield: 2 servings
4    Slices french bread (3x5")
Freshly grated Romano cheese
Freshly ground black pepper
3 tb Olive oil
5 lg Shallots or green onions
-thinly sliced
Preheat broiler. Heat oil in a heavy small skillet over medium heat.

Add shallots and saute till tender, stirring frequently, about 5
minutes.

Broil one side of the bread until toasted. Spread shallot mixture on
second side of bread. Sprinkle generousely with pepper and cheese.
Broil until beginning to brown. Serve immediately.
