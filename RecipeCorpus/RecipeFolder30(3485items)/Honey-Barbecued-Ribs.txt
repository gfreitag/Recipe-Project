Title: Honey Barbecued Ribs
Categories: Chinese, Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Pale Dry Sherry
1 tb Lemon Juice
1/2 ts Ground Ginger
1/4 ts Garlic Powder
3 lb Spareribs
1/3 c  Soy Sauce
3 tb Honey
2 tb Plum Jam
Cut rack of spareribs into quarters; place in a shallow roasting pan.
Combine all remaining ingredients. Pour over spareribs. Cover and
marinate 2 to 3 hrs. Bake at 325 F for 1 hr, turning ribs frequently.
Makes 4 servings as dinner, or 6 to 8 servings as part of a Chinese
meal.
