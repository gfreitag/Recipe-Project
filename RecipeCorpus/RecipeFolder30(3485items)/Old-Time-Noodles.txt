Title: Old Time Noodles
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Flour
1    Egg
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Milk
Combine the egg, salt and milk, beating slightly.  Add enough flour
to make a very stiff dough -- almost rubbery.  Roll out to thickness
of a dime on a floured surface.  Let surface of noodles dry for 20 to
30 minutes. Cut and cook in chicken broth. Randy Rigg
