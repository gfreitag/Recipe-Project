Title: Chocolate Chip Cheesecake 2
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Chocolate chips, mini;
-divided
1    Graham cracker crust
2 pk Cream cheese; 8 oz, softened
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
Mix cream cheese, sugar and vanilla at medium speed with mixer until
well blended.  Add eggs; mix until blended. Stir in 1/2 cup of the
chips. Pour into crust. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup chips. Bake
at 350 for 40 minutes or until center is almost set. Cool.
Refrigerate 3 hours or overnight.  Garnish with additional chips,
fresh fruit and cool whip, if desired.  Source: Keebler ad
