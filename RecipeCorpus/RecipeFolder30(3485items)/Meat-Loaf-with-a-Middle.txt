Title: Meat Loaf with a Middle
Categories: Low cal/fat, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Mustard
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
1 sm Tomato; sliced thinly
1 lb Chopped meat
1 ts Seasoned salt
1/4 ts Poultry seasoning
Place meat in bowl.  Add salt and poultry seasoning and mix well.

Divide meat into 2 parts.  Shape each into an oval and place on waxed
paper.  Spread mustard on each oval.  Place cheese and tomato on one
oval of meat.  Place rest of meat on top and press edges together to
seal. Broil for 15 minutes on 1 side and 10 minutes on the other
until done.

4 grams carbohydrate per serving.
