Title: Maple Glazed Ham Steak
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Butter; melted
8    California dried figs
-- halved
2    Bananas; peeled & quartered
1    Fully cooked ham steak
-- (1 to 1-1/2 inches thick)
Powdered cloves
1/3 c  Maple-flavored syrup
1 tb Lemon juice
Slash edges of ham.  Sprinkle with cloves.  Place in shallow baking
pan. Bake at 350 F. for 20 minutes.  Arrange figs and bananas in pan
around ham. Brush with combination of maple syrup, lemon juice and
bananas. Heat an additional 15 minutes; brush again with sauce.
Makes 4 to 5 servings.
