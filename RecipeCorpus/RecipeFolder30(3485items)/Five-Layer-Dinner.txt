Title: Five-Layer Dinner
Categories: Meats, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Onions -- sliced
1 c  Tomatoes -- cooked
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
1 1/2 c  Raw Potatoes -- sliced
1 c  Celery -- cut into 1"
Pieces
1 c  Ground Beef
Arrange ingredients in layers in a greased casserole dish in the order
given. Bake at 350 F for about 1 1/4 hrs. Yield: 4 servings.
