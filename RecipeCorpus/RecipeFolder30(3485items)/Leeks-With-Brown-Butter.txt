Title: Leeks With Brown Butter
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
2 1/2 ts Lemon juice
Salt
16    Leeks
6 oz Butter
4 tb Bread crumbs -- toasted
Trim and clean leeks, leaving them whole.  Drop in boiling water;
simmer about 10 minutes till done.  Drain; keep warm.

In skillet, heat butter till sizzling and golden brown.  Stir in
crumbs, lemon juice and salt; pour over leeks and serve.
