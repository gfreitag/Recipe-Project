Title: Chicken with Green Grapes
Categories: Poultry, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Dried leaf basil
1/4 ts Onion powder
2 ts Canola oil
1 lb Boneless, skinless chicken
-pieces
1 c  Fresh green seedless grapes
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Cider vinegar
1 tb Catsup
2 ts Canola oil
1/4 c  White grape juice
-concentrate
1/4 ts Sweet paprika
Marinade:

Combine marinade ingredients in a bowl.  Add chicken, cover and
refrigerate at least 2 hours.  Spray a non-stick skillet with
vegetable cooking spray. Heat;  add oil and chicken pieces, lightly
brown.  Add marinade, cover and simmer about 15 minutes.  Add grapes,
cook until heated. Serve at once.
