Title: Gipsy Veal Saute
Categories: Meats
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Dry vermouth; OR
2 tb Chicken broth
1/3 c  Heavy cream
1 1/2 tb Sun-dried tomato paste
1/2 lb Veal scallops
2 tb Butter
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1/2 c  Sliced scallions; incl tops
Saute veal in butter until lightly browned. Remove from pan. Add
scallions & mushrooms. Saute lightly. Add vermouth or chicken broth &
bring to a boil. Add cream & tomato paste. Cook 2 mins. Return veal
to pan to heat through.  From Amore Sun-dried Tomato Paste pkg.  MM
by Deidre Ganopole in Anchorage, Alaska.
