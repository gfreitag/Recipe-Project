Title: Pretzel Jello Salad Or Dessert
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
12 oz Cool Whip
2 pk Strawberry jello (or 6 oz)
2 pk 10 oz frozen strawberries,
- partially thawed
2 c  Pretzels -- crushed
3 tb Plus 1/4 cup sugar
3/4 c  Butter or oleo -- melted
8 oz Cream cheese
1st Layer:

Combine 2 cups crushed pretzels, 3 tbsp. sugar and 3/4 cup melted
butter. Pat into bottom of 9x13-inch baking dish. Bake at 350~ for 7
minutes. Cool.

2nd Layer:

Mix 8 ounces cream cheese, 1/4 cup sugar and 12 ounce carton Cool
Whip. Spread over cooled crust.

3rd Layer:

Combine 6 ounces strawberry jello and 2 cups boiling water. While
jello is still hot, mix in two 10-ounce packages partially thawed
frozen strawberries. Cool until slightly thick. Spread over cream
cheese layer. Refrigerate at least 4 hours. May also be frozen. This
recipe is liked by men, children and ladies.
