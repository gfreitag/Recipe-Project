Title: Pineapple-Berry Freeze
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------ALCOHOL FREE ENT---------------------------
2 oz Pineapple juice
4 oz Lemon lime soda
1 c  Strawberries, cut in pieces
1/2 oz SIMPLE SYRUP
2    Scoops pineapple sherbet
Cool and sweet. Combine strawberries and syrup in blender. Blend 15
sec. Add sherbet, juice and soda. Blend 10 sec. Serve with straw.
