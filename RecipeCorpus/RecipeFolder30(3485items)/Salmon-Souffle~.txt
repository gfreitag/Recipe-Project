Title: Salmon Souffle~
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Onion, finely chopped
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Paprika
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Margarine or butter, melted
4 sl Bread -- torn into pieces
1 cn 15 1/2oz Salmon,
Drained/flaked
2    Eggs, separated
Preheat oven to 350~. Mix milk, margarine and bread; stir in salmon,
egg yolks, lemon juice, onion, salt and pepper. Beat egg whites until
stiff; fold into salmon mixture. Pour into greased 1 1/2-quart
casserole. Sprinkle with paprika. Bake, uncovered, until puffed and
golden, about 1 hour. Serves 6.

Per serving using margarine:  Calories 134.6; fat 7.2g; cholesterol
81mg; carbohydrates 10.3g; dietary fiber 0.5g; protein 7.1g; sodium
534mg.
