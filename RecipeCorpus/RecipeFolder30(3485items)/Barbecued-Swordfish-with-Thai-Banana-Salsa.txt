Title: Barbecued Swordfish with Thai Banana Salsa
Categories: Bbq/grill, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 lb Swordfish
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
2 tb Oyster sauce
1 tb Honey
3 tb Soy sauce; light
3 tb Sherry; dry
2 tb Oil
MMMMM------------------------BANANA SALSA-----------------------------
2 tb Lime juice
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Fish sauce
2 tb Thai chili sauce
2    Bananas; peeled and chopped
1    Red pepper; chopped
1/4 c  Cilantro; chopped
2 tb Ginger; minced
2 tb Orange juice
Marinate swordfish in barbecue sauce 15 min to 2 hours. Combin
ingredients for banana salsa in a bowl. Taste and adjust seasonings.
Barbecue swordfish over medium heat for about 8 min. It is done when
it just feels firm to the touch. Serve with salsa. Source: The
Calgary Herald May 7, 1995 from a cooking class by Hugh Carpenter
(Fusion Food Cookbook) at a cooking class for Benkris School of
