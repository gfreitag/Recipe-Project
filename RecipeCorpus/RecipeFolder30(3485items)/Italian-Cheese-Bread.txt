Title: Italian Cheese Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 loaf
MMMMM----------------------1 1/2 POUND LOAF---------------------------

3/4 ts Salt
3/4 ts Dried italian seasoning,
Crushed

2 c  Whole wheat flour
