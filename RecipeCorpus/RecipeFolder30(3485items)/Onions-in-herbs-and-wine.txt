Title: Onions in herbs and wine
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Basil
1/4 ts Thyme
1/2 c  Dry white wine
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tb Olive oil
2    Garlic cloves, crushed
4    Yellow onions, peeled &
Sliced
1/4 ts Oregano
Heat a large frying pan and add thr oil and garlic, alonf with the
onions.
Saute until they just begin to brown. Add the herbs and wine and
simmer until the onions are very tender, about 10 minutes.
