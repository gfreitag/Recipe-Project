Title: Nippy Deviled Eggs
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Paprika
8    Eggs; hard-cooked
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Mustard, prepared
1/2 ts Horseradish, prepared
Cut eggs in half lengthwise; remove yolks.  Mash yolks; stir in
mayonnaise, mustard, horseradish, salt, and pepper.

Fill egg halves with mixture; chill.  Sprinkle with paprika before
serving.
