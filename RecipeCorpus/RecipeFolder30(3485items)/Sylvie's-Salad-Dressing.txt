Title: Sylvie's Salad Dressing
Categories: French, Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 24 servings
1 tb Dijon mustard
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Various herbs
12 oz Plain yogurt
6 oz Light olive oil
1    Juice of lemon
1 tb Brown mustard
Mix all ingredients together. It should be the consistency of ranch
dressing. If too thick, thin with more oil.

Makes approximately 1-1/2 cups, or about 24 servings.

Nutritional analysis per serving: 72.6 calories; 7.4 grams fat; 0.9
milligrams cholesterol; 26.2 milligrams sodium; 90% of calories from
fat.
