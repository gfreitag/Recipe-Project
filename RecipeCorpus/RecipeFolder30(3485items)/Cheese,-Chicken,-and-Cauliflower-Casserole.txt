Title: Cheese, Chicken, and Cauliflower Casserole
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Poultry, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Chopped parsley
1 1/2 c  Cooked chicken
Salt and pepper to taste
1 ds Of paprika
1 sm Cauliflower, separated in
-flowerets and cooked
1/2 lb Swiss cheese, sliced
1    Onion, sliced thin
1/2    Green pepper, coarsely
-chopped
1/3 c  Diced celery
3 tb Butter
3 tb All-purpose flour
2 c  Milk
2    Carrots, sliced thin
Saute onion, pepper, and celery in butter until tender. Stir in flour
and then gradually add milk and cook over low heat until smooth and
slightly thickened. Add carrots,parsley, chicken, and seasonings and
simmer for 15 minutes. Stir this mixture occasionally. Add
cauliflower. Place in a shallow casserole. Top with cheese slices.
Bake in preheated moderate oven (350 F.) until cheese is melted,
about 15 minutes.

Makes 6 servings.
