Title: North Carolina Grated Sweet Potato Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Ground Allspice
1/4 ts Ground Cloves
1/2 c  Seedless Raisins
1/2 c  Coconut, Shredded -- fresh
Or prepared*
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
1 ts Orange Rind -- grated
5 c  Sweet Potatoes, Raw --
Coarsely grated
3/4 c  Brown Sugar, Packed *
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Butter -- melted
3    Eggs -- well beaten
1/4 ts Nutmeg -- grated
1/2 ts Ground Cinnamon
* Use up to 1 cup of packed brown sugar if using fresh coconut.
Preheat oven to 400 F. Mix all the ingredients together and pour into
a buttered, medium-size casserole or skillet. Bake for 50 to 60 mins.
As crust forms around edges during baking, remove from oven and stir
pudding well to mix the crust throughout. Do this several times until
cooking is finished. Serve warm or cold, plain or topped with cream
or ice cream. Yield: 8 servings
