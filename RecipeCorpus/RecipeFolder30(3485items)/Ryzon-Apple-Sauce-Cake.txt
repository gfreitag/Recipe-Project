Title: Ryzon Apple Sauce Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 cake
1 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Allspice
1 c  Raisins
3/4 c  Honey
1 c  Applesauce
1 c  Corn flour
3/4 c  Buckwheat flour *
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Soda
1 ts Cinnamon
Mix and sift dry ingredients, add raisins, honey, and apple sauce,
beating thoroughly. Bake in loaf tin 45 minutes, in moderate [350 to
375 degrees F] oven.

From _The Ryzon Baking Book_ by Marion Harris Neil. General Chemical
