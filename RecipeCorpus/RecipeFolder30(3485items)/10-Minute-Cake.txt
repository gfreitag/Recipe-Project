Title: 10 Minute Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Salt
3    Eggs
2 c  Flour
1 c  White sugar
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Butter, softened
Place all ingredients in large bowl. Using electric mixer, beat on low
speed for about a minute, or until no lumps remain.  Increase speed
to high and continue to beat for about 3-4 minutes until batter is
smooth and silky looking.  Pour into greased 9 inch cake pan.  Bake
in a 350F oven for about 45-50 minutes or until it tests clean.
Allow to cool in pan. Can be iced with your favorite icing, or
sprinkled with a dusting of icing sugar.

Origin: Adapted from Canadian Living, May 1995. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, April/95.
