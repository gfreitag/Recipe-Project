Title: Mushroom Dill Chicken Rolls
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
-skinless and pounded 1/4"
-thick
2 tb Margarine; melted
Pepper
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Dill weed
2 tb Margarine; in pieces
1 c  Hot water
2 c  Stove Top Stuffing, in
-canister
1 sm Red bell pepper; chopped
1    Egg; beaten
4    Chicken breasts; boneless,
Heat oven to 400.  Stir cut margarine into hot water until melted. Add
stuffing crumbs, red pepper and egg; stir until moistened. Spoon
stuffing evenly on chicken; roll tightly. Secure with toothpicks.
Place in greased 9" square baking pan.  Brush with melted margarine;
sprinkle with pepper. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until cooked through.
Heat soup, water and dill in saucepan.  Serve over chicken.  Source:
