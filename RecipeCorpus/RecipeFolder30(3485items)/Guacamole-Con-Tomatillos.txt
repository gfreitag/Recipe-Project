Title: Guacamole Con Tomatillos
Categories: Tex/mexican, Dips/spread
Yield: 2 cups
IF hotter sauce is wanted
2 lg Ripe avocados
1 tb Minced onion
ds Salt to taste
ds Pepper to taste
4    TO 5 fresh or canned
Tomatillos
3    Canned green chilies, seeded
*OR*
1    Serrano or jalapeno pepper
MMMMM----------------------OPTIONAL GARNISH---------------------------
1/4 c  Cilantro leaves, minced
If using fresh tomatillos, discard husks and wash.  Boil in a small
amount of water until just tender, about 5 minutes, drain.

If using one of the hotter fresh chilies, discard stem and seeds.

Combine tomatillos and chilies in a blender and blend until smooth.

Halve the avocado and remove seed.  Scoop out pulp and mash with a
fork. Stir in tomatillo mixture with onions.  Add salt and pepper to
taste. Garnish with cilantro if desired.
