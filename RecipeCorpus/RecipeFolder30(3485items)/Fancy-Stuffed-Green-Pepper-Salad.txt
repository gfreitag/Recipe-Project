Title: Fancy Stuffed Green Pepper Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 2 servings
3 tb Mayonnaise
1 tb Sour cream
1 lg Green, red or yellow bell
-pepper
2 tb Finely chopped walnuts or
-pecans
1    4 oz jar chopped pimentoes
2 pk 3 oz cream cheese
Combine all ingredients, except bell pepper. Remove stem and seeds
from bell pepper; fill with cream cheese mixture; chill thoroughly.
Slice thin crosswise slices. Serve on chilled salad plates over cold,
crisp lettuce leaves. Salad dressing of your choice may be served
with the salad. Serves 2 - 4.
