Title: Party Punch
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
1    Orange, sliced
Ice
1 pt Cranberry juice
1 pt Lemonade
1 l  Ginger ale or club soda
From "Simply Vegan," Debra Wasserman. Vegetarian Resource Group,
1991, $12.

Mix all the ingredients in a large punchbowl and serve.

Per serving: 222 calories, 57 grams carbohydrates.
