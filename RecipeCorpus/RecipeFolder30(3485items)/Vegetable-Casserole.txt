Title: Vegetable Casserole
Categories: Healthy, Vegetables
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Red Bell Pepper -- chopped
1/2 c  Green Peas, Frozen
1/2 c  Chicken Bouillon
1/3 c  Olive Oil
2    Clove Garlic
1    Bay Leaf
2 ts Salt
1 ts Fresh Dill
1/2 ts Ground Savory
1 c  Carrots -- thinly sliced
1 c  Green Bean Halves -- fresh
1 c  White Potatoes -- peeled,
Diced
1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
2 md Tomatoes -- quartered
1 sm Yellow Squash -- sliced
1/2 sm Cauliflower -- cut in
Flowerets
1/2    Bermuda Onion -- chopped
Preheat oven to 350 F. Place vegetables in a large ungreased
casserole and toss well. Heat bouillon in a saucepan with remaining
ingredients, Bring to a boil and pour over vegetables. Bake for 1
hour, stirring occasionally.

Yield 12 servings.
