Title: 279773 Corn Salad Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
Drained
1    Jar chopped pimiento
1 c  Chopped green onion
2 cn Whole kernel corn, drained
1 pk Cream cheese
1 sm Carton sour cream
1 ts Lemon juice
2    Garlic cloves
1 cn Chopped black olives,
Cream together the cream cheese, sour cream, lemon juice and garlic.
Add the rest of the ingredients and stir.  Serve hot for a casserole
or cold for a salad dish.
