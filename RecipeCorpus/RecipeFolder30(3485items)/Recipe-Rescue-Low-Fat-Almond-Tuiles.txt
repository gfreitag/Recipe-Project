Title: Recipe Rescue Low Fat Almond Tuiles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Low cal/fat
Yield: 48 cookies
1 tb Vanilla
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sliced blanched almonds
2 lg Egg whites
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  All-purpose white flour
1/4 c  Salted butter; melted
Line baking sheets with parchment paper. Whisk together all the
ingredients except the almonds; stir in almonds with a rubber
spatula. Cover bowl with plastic and refrigerate 1 hr.

Preheat oven to 325. Drop scant teaspoonfuls of batter unto baking
sheets and with a spatula or knife smooth batter into thin circles.
Bake about 10 to 12 min. While cookies are still hot, remove them
from the sheets and curl around a rolling pin. Cool.
