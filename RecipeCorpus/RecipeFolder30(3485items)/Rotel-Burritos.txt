Title: Rotel Burritos
Categories: Tex/mexican, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Burrito seasoning mix
8 oz Velveeta
8 oz Sour cream
1 cn Sliced black olives
10    Flour tortilla
1 cn Rotel tomatoes & chilies
1 lb Ground beef
1    Onion, chopped
Brown ground beef and drain all fat.  Add onion and seasoning mix.
In a separate pan melt Velveeta, add sour cream and Rotel and stir.
Spread tortillas with seasoned beef mixture, sprinkle the sliced
black olives over, and pour a little sauce over each one.  Roll up,
place in a greased pan, pour the rest of the sauce over all.  Top
with the rest of the sliced olives.  Cover and bake at 350* for 20-30
minutes. Submitted By SALLY HILFIGER   On   04-29-95
