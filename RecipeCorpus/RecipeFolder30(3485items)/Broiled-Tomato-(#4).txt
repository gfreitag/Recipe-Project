Title: Broiled Tomato (#4)
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 ts Light brown sugar
x  Salt and Freshly ground
. black pepper
1 lg Ripe but firm tomato, about
. 8 to 10 ounces
x  Olive oil
1.  Halve the tomato crosswise.

2.  Brush the cut side of each half lightly with oil.

3.  Sprinkle each half with sugar, salt to taste, and freshly ground
black pepper.

4.  Place on a small baking sheet.  Broil 5 to 7 minutes, until soft
to the touch and golden on top.  Serve immediately.

From "Woman's Day:  The Only 25 Recipes You'll Ever Need," by Sidney
Burstein, ISBN 0-385-41179-0, 1990.
