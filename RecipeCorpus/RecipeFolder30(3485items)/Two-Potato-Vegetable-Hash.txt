Title: Two Potato Vegetable Hash
Categories: Potato, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
-coarsely grated (1 cup)
-(peeled)
1 1/4 c  Water
1 tb Chicken soup boullion
1 tb Onion flakes
2 ts Parsley
1/2 ts Tarragon
1/8 ts Pepper, or to taste
2 lg Sweet potatoes or yam,
-peeled and grated coarsely
-(2 cups)
2 lg Baking potatoes, grated
-coarsely (2 cups)
2 sm Zucchini, coarsely grated (
-cup)
2 sm Turnips or rutabaga's
Combine the vegetables, water and spices in a large skillet.  Cook,
uncovered, on high heat until vegetables are crisp tender and most of
the water has evaporated, about 10-15 minutes.  Serve immediately.

Origin: Adapted from Watkins Jiffy Dinner Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, May/95.
