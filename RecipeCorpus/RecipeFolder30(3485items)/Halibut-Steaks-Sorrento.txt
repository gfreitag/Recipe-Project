Title: Halibut Steaks Sorrento
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
4 ts Minced Fresh Parsley
Salt
Fresh Ground Pepper
4 ts Olive Oil
4    Lemon Wedges
4    Halibut Steaks
8    Thick Slices Ripe Tomato
1/4 c  Slivered Black Olives
4 ts Capers, Rinsed, Drained
1 lg Clove Garlic, Minced
Preheat oven to 400Ã¸F.  Place four 12 inch squares of aluminum foil
on work surface.  Place 1 halibut steak on center of each piece of
foil.  Top with tomato slices,olives, capers, garlic and parsley, in
that order. Sprinkle with salt and pepper; drizzle with oil.  Double
fold foil to seal packets and place on baking sheet.  Bake for 15
minutes.  Check halibut in one packet; fish should be opaque
throughout and firm to the touch.  Serve at once with lemon wedges.
337 calories per serving.  From: Syd's Cookbook.
