Title: Dill & Spice Oyster Crackers
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Ranch dressing mix
1 c  Oil
2 pk (12 oz ea) bags oyster crack
1/4 c  Dill, fresh, minced
Mix together, package dry ranch dressing mix, fresh chopped dill and
oil. Pour over two 12 oz bags of small size oyster crackers and mix
and coat well. Spread on baking sheets and toast lightly in a 350
degree oven. Allow to cool before serving.
