Title: Chicken Cilantro
Categories: Poultry, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
2    Green onions, chopped
1/4 c  Chopped cilantro or parsley
1    Tomato, chopped
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 lb Chicken pieces, skinned,
-boned
1/2 ts Paprika
2 ts Canola oil
Sprinkle chicken pieces with paprika, patting it in.  Heat oil in a
skillet and brown the chicken.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cover,
reduce heat, cook about 15 minutes.  Turn chicken at least twice
during cooking.
