Title: Pesto, Tomato & Cheese Sandwiches
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 x  Tomato, sliced
1 x  Hamburger bun or rolls
1 x  Pesto
1 x  Slices cheese
Spread pesto on roll, place a slice of tomato on top and cover with
any kind of cheese(mozzarella, feta, sharp cheddar or jack). Place
under broiler and cook until cheese is bubbly and browned. Simply
Delightful!!! Submitted By MARK MIKLAS   On   05-17-95
