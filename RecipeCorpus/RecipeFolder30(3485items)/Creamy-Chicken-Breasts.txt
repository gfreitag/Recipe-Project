Title: Creamy Chicken Breasts
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Crushed Pepperidge Farm Herb
-Stuffing
1/2 c  Melted butter
4    Boned chicken breasts
4 sl Swiss cheese
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1/4 c  White wine
Arrange chicken in shallow baking dish and put a slice of cheese on
top of each piece. Stir together soup and wine. Pour over chicken.
Sprinkle with stuffing and drizzle melted butter over all. Bake,
uncovered, at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours. Turn oven down to 300
degrees for 1/2 hour.
