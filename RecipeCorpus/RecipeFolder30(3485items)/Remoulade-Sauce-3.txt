Title: Remoulade Sauce 3
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Clove garlic - grated or
-put through press
Dash Tabasco sauce
1/2 c  Finely chopped celery
-(optional)
1/2 c  Finely chopped bell pepper
-(optional)
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
-(optional)
1 ea Pt mayonnaise (homemade is
-best)
1/2 c  Creole mustard
2 tb Salad mustard [presume she
-means yellow mustard]
Salt to taste
1 tb Horseradish
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Juice of 1 lemon
Mix all ingredients together, blending well.  Keep in well covered
jar in the refrigerator.  This is especially good served over boiled
shrimp and shredded lettuce.

Recipe by Deborah Crawford. Source: Talk About Good! Cookbook
