Title: Savory Seafood Trio Casserole
Categories: Seafood, Rice, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Chopped Onion
Parsley
Green Pepper
Slivered Almonds
1 c  Chopped Celery
3 tb Dry Sherry
1 c  Dry Bread Crumbs
3 tb Butter
6 1/2 oz Drained Crabmeat
6 1/2 oz Drained Shrimp
6 1/2 oz Drained Tuna
2 lg Hard cooked Eggs
1 c  Uncooked Minute Rice
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 1/4 c  Miracle Whip
10 1/2 oz Cream Of Mushroom Soup
In a large bowl, mix all ingredients except eggs, crumbs and butter.
Add the eggs.  Stir in carefully.  Combine bread crumbs and butter.
Sprinkle on top and bake in a 350Ã¸F oven for 1 hour.  From: Syd's
Cookbook.
