Title: Dump Cake - Low Fat Version
Categories: Cake/cookie, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
1    Box Betty Crocker Light Cake
-Mix (white or yellow)
1 c  Liquid Butter Buds
21 oz Can cherry pie filling
-(regular)
16 oz Can crushed pineapple,
-undrained
Place pie filling in a glass casserole dish (9x11). Add pineapple.
Sprinkle dry cake mix over top of fruit. Pour liquid Butter Buds on
top. Bake at 350 until golden brown (35-40 minutes).  Check for
doneness at 30 minutes. Top with Dream Whip or non-fat frozen yogurt.
Best served warm. Serves 24. (Yuh, right !!)
