Title: Mama Condon's Punch
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 40 servings
3    Tea bags in 1 c hot water
1 1/2 qt Ginger ale
3 lg Pkg froz strawberries
2 lg Cn frozen orange juice
2 lg Cns froz lemonade, not pink
2 lg Cn pineapple juice
Pour all ingredients in punch bowl that has an ice ring in it. You
can also freeze some strawberries in a jello mold filled with water
to use as an ice ring.
