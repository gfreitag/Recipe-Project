Title: Mint Cordial^
Categories: Homestyle
Yield: 1 servings
1 pt Water
1    Orange, juice of
1 c  Pineapple juice
1 lg Bunch mint
2    Lemons, juice of
1 lb Sugar
Pick off mint leaves, crush thoroughly, add lemon juice and stand
aside for one hour. Boil the water and sugar to a syrup, pour over
mint and lemon. Cool and strain. Add orange and pineapple juices and
serve with a sprig of fresh mint in each glass. Serve ice cold.
