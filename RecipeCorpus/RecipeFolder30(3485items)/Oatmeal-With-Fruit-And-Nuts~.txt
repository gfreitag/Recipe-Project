Title: Oatmeal With Fruit And Nuts~
Categories: Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Pitted whole Dates --
Snipped
2 tb Sliced Almonds
1 tb Brown Sugar
2 2/3 c  Skim Milk
3 c  Water
1/2 ts Ground Cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/3 c  Regular Rolled oats
1 sm Apple -- chopped
In a medium saucepan combine water, cinnamon, and salt. Bring to
boiling; stir in oats. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Let
stand, covered, till of desired consistency. Divide oatmeal among 4
serving bowls. Top each bowl with chopped apple, snipped dates,
sliced almonds, and brown sugar. Divide milk among serving bowls.

Per serving: 204 calories, 7 g protein, 38 g carbohydrates, 4 g fat,
1 mg cholesterol, 146 mg sodium, 315 mg potassium.
