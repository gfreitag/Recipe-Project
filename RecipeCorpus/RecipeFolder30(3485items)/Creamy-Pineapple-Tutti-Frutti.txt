Title: Creamy Pineapple Tutti-Frutti
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Mandarin oranges
1/2 c  Whipping cream
Grated chocolate (garnish)
1 cn 8 oz crushed pineapple
4 oz Gingersnaps
1 tb Dry sherry
2 tb Golden raisins
Drain pineapple; crush gingersnaps. Mix together with sherry, raisins
and oranges. Lightly whip cream; gently fold in pineapple-gingersnap
mixture; chill. Spoon into dessert or parfait glasses; sprinkle with
grated chocolate; serve cold. Serves 4.
