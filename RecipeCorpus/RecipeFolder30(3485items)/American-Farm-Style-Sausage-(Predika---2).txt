Title: American Farm Style Sausage (Predika - 2)
Categories: Breakfast, Homemade, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
5 ts Salt
5 ts Black pepper
1 c  Cold water
5 lb Medium ground pork butt
1 ts Cayenne
1 tb Thyme
FROM :    Clarence Fontish

Combine all ingredients, mix well and stuff into sheep casing.

Source: The Sausage Making Cookbook, by Jerry Predika
