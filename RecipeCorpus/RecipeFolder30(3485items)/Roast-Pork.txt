Title: Roast Pork
Categories: Homemade, Meats
Yield: 5 servings
4    Bay Leaves
1/2 c  White Vinegar
1/2 ts Dried Thyme
4 lb Pork Roast, Loin Or Center
-Cut
2    Cloves Garlic -- minced
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
Preheat oven to 325 F. Pierce the roast in several places with a
two-pronged fork. Force some minced garlic into each hole, again with
the aid of the fork. Sprinkle liberally with salt and pepper. Place
the bay leaves in the bottom of the roasting pan. Place the roast on
top, fat side up. Combine vinegar and thyme, and pour evenly over the
roast. Place in oven, allowing 35 mins baking time per pound. Baste
occasionally.

Yield: 5 to 6 servings
