Title: Blackberry Cake with Icing
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
2 tb Cocoa
3 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Mashed blackberries
1 c  Blackberry juice
2    Eggs
Mix butter, sugar and eggs.  Add dry ingredients alternately with
berries and juice.  Pour in a 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan and bake at 325
degrees for 45 minutes.

Icing: 1 cup powdered sugar 2 tb. cocoa 2 tb. butter enough hot
coffee to make a paste for desired consistency Spread on the cake.
