Title: Kahlua Creamy Fudge
Categories: Cake/cookie, Candies
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Semi-sweet chocolate pieces
1 c  Milk chocolate pieces
2/3 c  Chopped nuts (optional)
1 ts Vanilla
Marshmallow creme
1 1/3 c  Sugar
1    7 oz jar
2/3 c  Evaporated milk
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Kahlua
1/4 ts Salt
Line 8" square baking pan with foil.  In large saucepan, combine
sugar, marshmallow creme, milk, butter, Kahlua and salt.  Bring to
rapid boil stirring constantly for 5 minutes.  Remove from heat; add
all chocolate and stir until melted.  Add nuts and vanilla and turn
mixture into pan. Refrigerate until firm.
