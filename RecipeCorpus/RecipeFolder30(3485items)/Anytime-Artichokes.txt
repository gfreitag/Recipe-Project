Title: Anytime Artichokes
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1    Bay Leaf
Salt
Pepper
2    Fresh Artichokes *
Miracle Whip
1/2    Lemon
* Artichokes should have stems, top 1" of cone, tough bottom leaves
and leaf tips trimmed.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Place artichokes in a large pot and add enough water to cover
artichokes halfway.  Add lemon, bay leaf and salt.  Heat to boiling
over medium high heat.  Reduce heat and cook covered until bottoms
are tender when pierced with a knife, about 20 minutes.  Drain Well.
Serve with a small dish of Miracle Whip for dipping leaves and heart.
From: Syd's Cookbook.
