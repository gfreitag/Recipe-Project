Title: Marinated Veal Steaks in Onion Sauce
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 cn Cream of chicken soup
3/4 c  Milk
1 tb Vinegar
1/2 c  Ripe olives, sliced
1 1/2 lb Veal steaks
1 c  French dressing
1/2 c  Flour
2 tb Oil
1 ea Onions
Slice onion.  Marinate steaks in French dressing at least three
hours. Preheat oven to 350.  Drain. Dip steaks in seasoned flour.
Melt oil in large skillet.  Brown steaks on both sides. Add onion,
soup, and milk. Cover and cook slowly on surface heat or in oven 45
minutes., Add vinegar and olives.
Cover and cook 10 minutes more.
