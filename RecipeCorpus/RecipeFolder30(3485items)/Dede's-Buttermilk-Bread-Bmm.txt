Title: Dede's Buttermilk Bread Bmm
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
MMMMM----------------------1 1/2 POUND LOAF---------------------------
1 tb Butter; or margerine
3 tb Honey
1/4 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 ts Red Star yeast
1 1/8 c  Buttermilk; or 4T dry
-buttermilk powder and
-1 1/8c water
3 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
Place all ingredients into bread pan, select Light Crust setting, and
press start.

After baking cycle ends, remove bread from pan, place on cake rack,
and allow to cool 1 hour before slicing.
From Bread Machine Magic by Linda Rehberg and Lois Conway
