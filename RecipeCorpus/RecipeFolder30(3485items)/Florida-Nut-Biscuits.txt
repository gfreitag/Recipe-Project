Title: Florida Nut Biscuits
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Chopped pecans or walnuts
3 tb Brown sugar
1 pk 8 oz ready-to- bake biscuits
1/4 c  Fresh orange juice
1 tb Grated orange rind
Place biscuits in an ungreased 9-inch round baking pan. Bake
according to package directions. In a small bowl, combine orange
jucie, grated rind, nuts, and brown sugar. Spoon over biscuits. Lower
heat to 300 degrees, and bake 4-5 minutes longer, or until glazed.
Serve at once. Makes 8 biscuits.
