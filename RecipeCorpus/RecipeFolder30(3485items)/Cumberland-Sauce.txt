Title: Cumberland Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Claret Wine
1/4 ts Ground Ginger
4 ts Cornstarch
1 tb Lemon Juice
1    Orange Peel -- 1-Inch
Square
3/4 c  Orange Juice
1/2 c  Currant Jelly
Put all of the ingredients in a blender container and blend until the
ingredients are combined and the orange peel is finely chopped.  Pour
into a saucepan and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture is
thickened and bubbly.  Cook 1 to 2 minutes longer.  Serve the sauce,
hot or cold, with meat or poultry.

Makes 1 1/2 cups.
