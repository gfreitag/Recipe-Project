Title: Fat Free Oatmeal Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2    Egg whites
1/3 c  White Karo syrup
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Raisins
1 c  Flour
1 c  Quick oats
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
Preheat oven 325-275 (try lower 1st). Spray cookie sheets with spray.
In large bowl combine dry ingredients. Stir in all but raisins and
mix well. Add raisins and drop by tsp onto cookie sheets. Bake 10
minutes
