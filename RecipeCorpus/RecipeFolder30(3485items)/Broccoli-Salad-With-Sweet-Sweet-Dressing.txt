Title: Broccoli Salad With Sweet Sweet Dressing
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sunflower kernals
1/2 c  Currants
2 c  To 3 cups Broccoli
Flowerets
1/2 c  Celery -- sliced
MMMMM--------------------SWEET SWEET DRESSING-------------------------
2 ts Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sugar
SWEET SWEET DRESSING: Mix ingredients until smooth and sugar is
dissolved.

Mix broccoli, celery, sunflower kernals, and currants. Toss with
enough dressing to coat. Chill.
