Title: Reames Cheesy Broccoli Tuna Bake
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Tuna, drained (12 1/2 oz)
1 cn Mushroom pieces, drained
-(opt; 4 oz)
1 pk Chopped broccoli, thawed
1 c  Shredded mild cheddar cheese
1 pk REAMES Home Style Frozen
-Egg Noodles (12 oz)
1 cn Cheddar cheese soup (11 oz)
1 cn Evaporated milk (5 oz)
1 ts Instant minced onion
Preheat oven to 350'F. Cook noodles in boiling water 20 minutes.
Blend soup and milk into smooth sauce. Add onions and mushrooms.
Drain noodles. Layer 1 1/2 cups noodles, 1/3 cup tuna and 1 cup
broccoli in a 2-quart casserole. Pour in 1 cup of sauce. Repeat
layers. Bake 20 minutes. Then add cheese and bake 15 more minutes.
Makes 6 1-cup servings.
