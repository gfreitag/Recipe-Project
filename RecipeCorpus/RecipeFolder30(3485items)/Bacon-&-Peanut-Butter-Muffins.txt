Title: Bacon & Peanut Butter Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Meats, Nuts/grains
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Milk
2 tb Bacon fat or butter; melted
3 sl Bacon; cooked, finely
-chopped
4 tb Peanut butter; approx.
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg
Yield: 12 muffins

Mix thoroughly flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.  In a
medium-size bowl beat the egg; add milk, melted fat, and chopped
bacon.  Stir in the flour mixture, but do not beat; mixture should be
just moistened.  Pour a little batter into each well-greased muffin
tin, then drop in about 1/3 teaspoon of peanut butter before filling
tins 3/4 full.  Bake in a preheated 400 F oven for 20 to 25 minutes.
