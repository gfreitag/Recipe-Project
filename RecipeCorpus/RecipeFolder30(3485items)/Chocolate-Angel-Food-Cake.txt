Title: Chocolate Angel Food Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Cocoa
2 c  Cold water
2 c  Miracle Whip
4 tb Vanilla
4 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Baking soda
3 ts Baking powder
MMMMM---------------------------ICING--------------------------------
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/2 c  Butter
Chopped nuts, optional
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Vanilla
Sift flour, sugar, salt, baking soda, baking powder and cocoa.  Add
water, Miracle Whip and vanilla.  Grease and flour 2  9 x13 inch pans
and bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.  Ice while hot.

Icing: Combine ingredients. Boil exactly 2 minutes. Then beat until
slightly cool. Pour over hot cake. Sprinkle with nuts, if desired.
Randy Rigg
