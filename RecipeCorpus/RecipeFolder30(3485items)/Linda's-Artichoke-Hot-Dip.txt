Title: Linda's Artichoke Hot Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1/4 ts Dill
1/4 ts White pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
8 1/2 oz Chopped artichoke hearts
-(not pickled etc.)
1 c  Mayonnaise
3 oz Cream cheese
To serve, combine all in an ungreased casserole, uncovered, and bake
for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.  Serve hot with Triscuits or Wheat
Thins.
