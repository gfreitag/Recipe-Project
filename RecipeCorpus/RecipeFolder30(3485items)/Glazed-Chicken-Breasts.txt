Title: Glazed Chicken Breasts
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
6    Chicken breast halves
-- (boneless)
1/3 c  Teriyaki Baste & Glaze
-- (Kikkoman)
3/4 ts Grated lemon peel
Combine teriyaki baste & glaze, lemon peel and juice; brush both
sides of chicken breasts with mixture.  Place, side by side, in
shallow baking pan, skin side down.  Bake in 350 F. oven 20 minutes.
Turn chicken over; brush with remaining baste & glaze mixture.
Return to oven and bake 20 minutes longer, or until chicken is tender.

Makes 4 to 6 servings.
