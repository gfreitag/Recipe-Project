Title: Golden Eggplant
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Butter -- melted
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 c  Light Cream -- OR
1 c  Evaporated Milk
2 1/2 c  Eggplant -- cubed, peeled
18    Saltine Crackers --
Crumbled
1/2 c  Sharp Cheese -- grated
1/4 c  Chopped Celery
2 tb Chopped Pimiento
Cook eggplant in boiling water for 10 mins. Drain. Combine with
remaining ingredients and turn into casserole. Bake at 350 F for 45
mins. Serves 6. Note: May be prepared ahead of time and refrigerated.

from IF YOU CAN'T STAND TO COOK by Lorraine Gifford; easy-to-fix
recipes for the Handicapped Homemaker. Published 1973 by Zondervan
Publishing House.
