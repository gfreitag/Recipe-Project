Title: Sweet and Sour Chicken <mom's>
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Oil
Salt
Pepper
2    Chicken breasts, split
And boned
Flour
MMMMM---------------------------JUICE--------------------------------
1 tb Soy sauce
1/4 ts Ginger
1    Chicken bouillon cube
Green pepper
1 sm Can pineapple chunks
Water
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Corn starch
Wash chicken breasts and pat dry.  Coat with flour. Heat oil in
skillet, add chicken and brown on all sides.  Place in shallow
roasting pan and sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Preheat oven to 350.

Drain can of pineapple chunks, saving juice.  Pour juice into 2c
measure and add enough water to make 1 1/4 cups.  Pour into medium
saucepan and add sugar, corn starch, soy sauce, ginger and bouillon
cube.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly until smooth.  Boil for 2
minutes.  Pour over chicken.

Bake uncovered 30 mins.  Add pineapple chunks and 1 large green
pepper cut into strips.  Bake 30 mins more.
