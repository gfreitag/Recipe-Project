Title: Kentucky Biscuits
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Sugar
Pam cooking spray
1 c  Bisquick
1 tb (rounded) Sour cream
1/4 c  Club soda
Mix together in a bowl all ingredients.  Dip hand into just enough
Bisquick that you can knead dough right in the bowl until smooth and
elastic. Shape into 6 patties equal in size, 1-1/2" thick.  Put close
together in Pam-sprayed 8" round baking pan.  Spray tops with Pam.
Bake 450 degrees F. (HOT) for 18-20 minutes, or until golden.

From "Secret Recipes from Gloria Pitzer" (as seen in "Freebies"
magazine, May/June/July 1995).
