Title: Chicken and Cortland Apple Couscous
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
-cored and coarsely chopped
1/4 c  Unsalted butter, but into
-small pieces
1/2 ts Ground coriander seeds
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/8 ts Ground cloves
2 c  Coarsely chopped cooked
-chicken
2 c  Dry couscous
2 1/2 c  Boiling chicken stock
1/2 c  Golden raisins
1/2 c  Toasted slivered almonds
2    Cortland apples, peeled,
Preheat oven to 400F. Combine all ingredients in a 9x13x2 inch glass
baking pan. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.

Serves 4.

From the cookbook "Apples" by Robert Berkley
