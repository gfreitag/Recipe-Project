Title: Spicy Cheese Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs, Dips/spread
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
1 ts Cumin
1/2 ts Lime juice
1/2 ts Ground red pepper
16 oz Cheez whiz
1 c  Spaghetti sauce; plain only
2 tb Chili powder
2 ts Brown sugar
Microwave all ingredients in 1 1/2 qt bowl on high for 4 to 5 minutes,
stirring every 2 minutes.  Serve with tortilla chips or vegetable
dippers. Source: Kraft Ad
