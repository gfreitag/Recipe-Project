Title: Florentine Crescents
Categories: Microwave, Sandwich, Velveeta
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Velveeta (cubed)
1/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
2    (8 oz.) cans Pillsbury
-refrigerated Quick Crescent
-Dinner Rolls
3    Crisply cooked bacon slices
-(crumbled)
1    (10 oz.) pkg. frozen,
-chopped spinach
-(thawed and well drained)
Combine spinach, Velveeta, bread crumbs, and bacon in a 1 1/2-quart
microwave safe bowl.  Microwave on HIGH 2 1/2 to 4 1/2 minutes or
until cheese spread is melted, stirring every 1 1/2 minutes. Unroll
dough and separate into 16 triangles.  Cut each in half lengthwise,
forming 32 triangles.  Spread each triangle with rounded teaspoonful
of spinach mixture.  Roll up starting at wide end. Place on greased
cookie sheet. Brush dough with beaten egg if desired. Bake at 375
degrees for 11-13 minutes or until golden brown.
