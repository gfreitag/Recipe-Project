Title: Chicken Ranchero
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Cumin
1    Clove garlic, minced
2    Zucchini, sliced
Salt and black pepper
Rice, if desired
2 1/2 lb Chicken breasts,thighs and
-legs,skinned
14 1/2 oz Can Mexican style stewed
-tomatoes
8 oz Can tomato sauce
7 oz Can diced green chilies
Combine all ingredients except zucchini, salt and black pepper in a 4
quart pot. Cover and simmer 45 minutes. Add zucchini and salt and
black pepper to taste. Cover and simmer 15 minutes more. Serve with
rice, if desired.

Makes 4 to 6 servings.
