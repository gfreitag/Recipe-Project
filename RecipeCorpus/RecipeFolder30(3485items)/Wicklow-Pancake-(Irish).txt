Title: Wicklow Pancake (Irish)
Categories: Irish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1 pn Chopped thyme
2 tb Chopped chives or scallions
1 x  Salt and pepper
2 tb Butter
4 ea Lg. eggs*
600 ml Milk
4 oz Fresh breadcrumbs
1 tb Parsley, chopped
* Or six small ones. -- Beat the eggs lightly, then add the milk,
breadcrumbs, herbs and seasonings, and mix well.  Heat 1 tablespoon
of the butter in a pan until foaming, then pour in the mixture and
cook over a low flame until it is brown underneath and just set on
top.  Put under the grill to finish.  Serve cut into wedges with a
knob of butter on each portion.
