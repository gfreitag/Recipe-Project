Title: French Salad Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads
Yield: 1 cups about
1 tb Grated onion
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Salt
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1/3 c  Ketchup
1    Garlic clove, minced
Combine oil, vinegar, sugar, ketchup, garlic, onion, paprika, salt and
lemon juice in covered container; shake to blend.  Store in
refrigerator. Audrey Anderson   St. Louis.
