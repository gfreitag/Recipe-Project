Title: Basic Ground Beef Pasties
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Snacks
Yield: 9 pasties
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
5/8 c  ;cold water
1 1/2 c  Lard; or shortening
4 c  Flour
2 ts ;salt
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Carrots; chopped or grated
;salt & pepper
1 lb Ground beef
1 1/2 c  Potatoes; diced (to 2 cups)
1/2 c  Onions; chopped
FILLING: Combine and mix well - can also use a can of cream or
chicken or cream of mushroom shop, eliminating salt and pepper.

CRUST: Mix flour and salt together. Cut in half of shortening until
mixture resembles meal, then cut in remaining shortening.  Add water,
work with fork or hands and shape dough until firm and oblong.  Then
divide into 9 portions.  Roll out into 6" circle.  Place 1/2 cup of
filling on circle, fold over and seal.  Prick with a fork several
times. Bake at 400F for 10 minutes and then lower to 350F and bake
35-40 minutes longer. (Might need more baking time if using soup in
filling).
