Title: Buckaroo Beans
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Sweet Peppers, Red Or Green
Chopped
2 ts Chili Powder
2 tb Brown Sugar
1/2 ts Dry Mustard
1/4 ts Crushed Oregano Leaves --
OR
Ground Cumin
Salt -- to taste
1 lb Dried Pink, Red, Or Pinto
Beans
6 c  Water
1/2 lb Smoked Ham
1 lg Onion, Thickly Sliced Or --
Coarsely chopped
2    Cloves Garlic -- minced
1 sm Bay Leaf
2 c  Tomatoes, Fresh Or Canned
Soak beans for 2 hrs in 6 cups water. Cut smoked ham in 1/2- inch
cubes; add to beans and soaking water along with onion, garlic and
bay leaf. Bring to boiling point; cover tightly. Cook at low heat for
1 1/2 hrs or until beans are almost tender. Add remaining ingredients
except salt. Allow to simmer, covered, for 2 hrs, stirring once or
twice. Taste and add salt if necessary. There should be enough liquid
left on the beans to resemble a medium-thick gravy. The covered pot
may be placed in a 325 F oven after the first step, to finish
cooking. Yield: 8 to 10 servings.
