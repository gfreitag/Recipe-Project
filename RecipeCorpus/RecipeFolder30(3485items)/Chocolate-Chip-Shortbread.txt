Title: Chocolate Chip Shortbread
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 5 dozen
2 c  Flour
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Mix butter, sugar, vanilla, and flour into bowl until it forms a
ball. Add chocolate chips and work into dough.  Roll into 1 inch
balls. Place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Press with fork.  Bake at
325 oven for 10 to 15 minutes.  Makes 5 dozen
