Title: Marinated Italian Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Italian salad dressing
4    Boneless skinless chicken
-breasts
Place chicken breasts in a shallow pan; pour dressing over.  Marinate
for 15 minutes.  Place chicken on rack of a broiler pan.  Broil for
5-7 minutes on each side or until juices run clear. Yield: 4 servings.

SOURCE:*Jean Brenneman, Cedar Rapids, IA, Country Woman Magazine
Mar/Apr 93 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 6/93
