Title: Courgette, Honey and Yoghurt Loaf
Categories: Breads, Syrups
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Sugar
1 c  Bran
3 c  White Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Each ground ginger and
-nutmeg
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 c  Orange juice
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Light salad oil
1/2 c  Mild honey, eg blue borage,
-rata
2    Eggs
2 md Courgette, grated
1/2 c  Yoghurt or buttermilk
Finely grated rind of 1
-orange
Finely grated rind of 1
-lemon
Beat oil and honey until well mixed. Add eggs one after the other and
beat after each addition. Stir in grated courgette and yoghurt and
rinds. Combine dry ingredients (except soda) and separately combine
orange juice and soda. Fold into creamed mix quickly and lightly with
nuts. Pour mix into a greased log pan and bake at 180Ã¸C for 50
minutes or until a skewer comes out clean when inserted into the
centre. Stand for 5 minutes before turning out.

Source: Cuisine March '95
