Title: American Penn-Dutch Sausage
Categories: Homemade, Meats, Penndutch
Yield: 10 servings
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: SAUSAGE-MAKING COOKB---------------------
MMMMM------------------FROM :  CLARENCE FONTISH-----------------------
1/3 c  Sage
3 tb Coriander
1 tb Black pepper
5 lb Pork butt, coarse ground
2 tb Cloves, ground
2 tb Salt
1 c  Cold Water
Combine all ingredients, mix well and stuff into sheep casing.  To
cook, fry or bake.
