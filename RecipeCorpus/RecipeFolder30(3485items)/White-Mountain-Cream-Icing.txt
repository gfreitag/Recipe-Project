Title: White Mountain Cream Icing
Categories: Homestyle, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1    Egg White
1/2 ts Vanilla Extract
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Cream Of Tartar
1/4 c  Water
Boil the sugar, cream of tartar, and water together. Beat egg white
until stiff. Remove sugar syrup from fire when little threads spin
off a spoon, and pour over beaten egg white. Beat vigorously with
sweeping strokes until cool. Add vanilla extract and spread on cooled
cake.

Yield: 1 cup.
