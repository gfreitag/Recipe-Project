Title: Refreshing Tropical Fruit (with no oil dressing)
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Lime juice
1 ts Minced candied ginger (opt)
1 ts Poppy seeds (opt)
1 ts Toasted sesame seeds (opt)
1 cn DOLE Tropical Fruit Salad,
-drained (15 1/4 oz; reserve
-1/2 cup syrup)
1/4 ts Grated lime peel
Mix syrup, grated lime peel and lime juice. If desired add minced
candied ginger, poppy seeds or toasted sesame seeds. Pour over fruit.
