Title: Maryland Chicken
Categories: Poultry, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Water
1/2 c  Minced salt pork
Salt and pepper
1    Three-pound chicken
1 c  Bread crumbs
2 tb Butter or butter substitute
2    Eggs, well beaten
Cut chicken in pieces.  Dip in eggs which have been seasoned and
diluted with the water.  Roll in bread crumbs.  Fry pork in heavy
roaster until crisp and brown.  Add chicken.  Brown slowly. Season
with salt and pepper. Dot with butter.  Cover.  Bake in slow oven
(325 F) about 3 hours, or until chicken is tender. Remove chicken.
Make a medium thick sauce of drippings left in roaster. Serve with
chicken. 6 servings.

Florence Taft Eaton, Concord, MA.
