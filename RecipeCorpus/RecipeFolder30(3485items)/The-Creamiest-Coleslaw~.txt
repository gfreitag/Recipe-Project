Title: The Creamiest Coleslaw~
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 ts Sea salt
1/2 ts Fresh ground pepper
8 c  Shredded red cabbage
8 c  Shredded green cabbage
4    Carrots -- grated
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sour cream
2 tb Grated onions
2 tb Chopped parsley
2 ts Dijon mustard
1 1/2 ts Celery seed
In a large bowl, whisk mayonnaise, sour cream, onion, parsley,
mustard, celery seed, salt and pepper. Add red and green cabbage, as
well as carrots. Toss until well combined. Serves 24.

Per serving:  calories 72.9; fat 6.2g; cholesterol 5mg; carbohydrates
4.4g; dietary fiber 1.4g; protein 1.0g; sodium 99mg.
