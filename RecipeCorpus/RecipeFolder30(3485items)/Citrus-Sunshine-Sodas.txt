Title: Citrus Sunshine Sodas
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Citrus Ice Milk (see below)
4 c  Lemon-lime carbonated
. beverage, chilled and
. divided
1/4 c  Thawed orange juice
. concentrate
1 cn (8 oz.) Unsweetened crushed
. pineapple, undrained
Combine orange juice and pineapple in a blender, and process until
smooth. Spoon 3 tablespoons orange juice mixture in each of 6 tall
glasses. Spoon 1/2 cup Citrus Ice Milk into each glass.  Add 2/3 cup
carbonated beverage to each glass.  Serve immediately.

CALORIES 212 (2% from fat); PROTEIN 37.1g; FAT 0.4g (sat 0.2g, mono
0.1g, poly 0.1g); CARB 39.7g; FIBER 0.5g; CHOL 2mg; IRON 0.6mg;
SODIUM 72 g; CALC 113 mg
