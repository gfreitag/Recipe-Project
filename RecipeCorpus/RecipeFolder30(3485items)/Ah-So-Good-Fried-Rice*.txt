Title: Ah So Good Fried Rice*
Categories: Side dish, Rice, Asian
Yield: 6 servings
Drained  (14 oz)
1 cn LA CHOY sliced water
Chestnuts, drained
(8 oz)
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
2 tb WESSON oil
3/4 c  Chopped bacon or cook ham
1 ts Minced fresh garlic
1/2 ts Minced gingerroot
3 ea Eggs, well beaten
3 c  Cold cooked rice
1 cn LA CHOY chop suey vegetables
In large nonstick skillet, heat oil. Add bacon, garlic and ginger;
cook and stir 30 seconds. Add eggs; cook until softly scrambled,
stirring occasionally. Stir in all remaining ingredilents except
green onions; heat thoroughly, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle with
green onions.

Source:"Favorite Brand Name Chinese Collection", Publication
Internationals Ltd., 1993
