Title: Basic White Bread for Welbilt ABM
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 loaf
1 1/2 tb Nonfat dry milk
1 1/2 tb Butter/margarine
1 c  Plus 3 tablespoons, plus 1
-teaspoon water
1 3/4 ts Active dry yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Granulated sugar
Press SELECT for Yeast Bread.

Press BAKING CONTROL for Medium.

Press START.  Bread will be ready in 4 hours and 4 minutes.

from Easy Baking by Claudia Burns and Tom Lacalamita (came with the
Welbilt) typed by Tiffany Hall-Graham
