Title: Fluffy Strawberry Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Heavy cream -- whipped
1    9-inch graham cracker crust
-cooled
Fresh strawberries
(optional; for garnish)
1 pk 3 oz cream cheese, softened
1 cn 14 oz can sweetened
-condensed milk
2 1/2 c  Fresh or thawed unsweetened
Frozen -- strawberries
3 tb Lemon juice
In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Beat in sweetened
condensed milk. Puree or mash berries. Stir strawberries and lemon
juice into cream cheese mixture. Fold in whipped cream. Pour into
crust. Mixture should mound slightly. Freeze 4 hours, or until firm.
Before serving, garnish with fresh strawberries, if desired. Keep
leftover pie in freezer. Serves 6-8.
