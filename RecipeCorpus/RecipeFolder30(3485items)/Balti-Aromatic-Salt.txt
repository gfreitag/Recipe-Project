Title: Balti Aromatic Salt
Categories: Herbs/spice, ', Indian, Balti
Yield: 1 jar
1 ts Ground allspice
3 1/2 oz Coarse sea salt
1 ts Ground Cinnamon
MMMMM-----------------FOR SPICIER AROMATIC SALT----------------------
1 tb Ground almond
1/2 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Ground fenugreek
1 ts Dried mint
Finely grind the mix, and store in a screw top jar.

Recipe from Balti Restaurant Cookbook - Pat Chapman ISBN 0 7499 1342 8
