Title: Mother Murphy's Chickenhearted Rice
Categories: Poultry, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
Soy sauce
Butter
3 c  Cooked rice
1 lb Chicken hearts
1 lg Sweet onion
1/2 ts Sambal Oelek *
3 ts Sugar
* chili pepper, garlic Chop onion very coarsely. Saute in butter until
onion begins to turn glassy. Add chicken hearts, cook until done. Add
soy sauce to taste, Sambal Oelek, and sugar. Serve over rice.
