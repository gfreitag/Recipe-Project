Title: Seafood Cocktail Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 ts Tobasco Pepper Sauce
1 tb Lemon Juice
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Horseradish -- Prepared
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
3 tb Tomato Catsup
Mix all of the ingredients together and chill until serving time.
