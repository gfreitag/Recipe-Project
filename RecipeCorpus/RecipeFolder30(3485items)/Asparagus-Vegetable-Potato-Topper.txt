Title: Asparagus Vegetable Potato Topper
Categories: Side dish, Vegetarian, Potato
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Mushrooms; sliced
1 cn Cream of asparagus soup
2 tb Milk
6    Potatoes; hot baked split
1 tb Oil
3/4 lb Asparagus; trimmed and cut
-into 1" pieces
2 md Carrots; thinly sliced
1/4 ts Basil; crushed
In 3 qt saucepan over medium heat, in hot oil, cook asparagus and
carrots with basil until tender-crisp; stirring often. Add mushrooms;
cook until tender, stirring often. Add soup and milk. Heat through,
stirring occasionally.  Spoon over potatoes.  Source: Campbells ad
