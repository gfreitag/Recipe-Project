Title: Candy Bar Pie
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
8 oz Cool whip, chocolate; thawed
1    Oreo pie crust
1 pt Vanilla ice cream; softened
1/2 c  Caramel sauce
1/2    Peanuts; chopped
Spread ice cream evenly onto bottom of crust.  Place in freezer until
ice cream is firm.  Spread caramel sauce over ice cream. Sprinkle
with peanuts; cover with cool whip. Freeze 4 hours or until firm. Let
stand at room temperature 20 minutes before serving. Source: Cool
