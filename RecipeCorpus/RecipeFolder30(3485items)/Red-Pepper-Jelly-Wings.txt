Title: Red Pepper Jelly Wings
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1 1/2 ts Tabasco sauce
1 1/2 ts Soya sauce
2    Ginger cloves - chopped
1 ts Salt
2 1/2 c  Chicken wings - separated
1/2 c  Red pepper jelly
1/4 c  Orange juice
2 tb Orange rind - grated
1/4 c  Olive oil
In a large bowl combine all ingedients.  Add chicken wings to mixture
and marinate for 2 hours in refrigerator.  After this time remove
wings from marinade and place on broiling pan.  Keep the marinade for
use later. Set oven to "broil" and place rack 6" from the element.
Broil for 7 minutes, turn wings, baste with marinade and broil a
further 8 minutes. Serve hot.

You can also cook these wings over the BBQ.  Cook wings the same
amount of time over medium heat.  Be sure to preheat the BBQ and
brush the grill with oil to prevent sticking

Serves 2 to 4.
