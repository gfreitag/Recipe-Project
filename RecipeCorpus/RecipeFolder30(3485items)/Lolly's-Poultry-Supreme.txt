Title: Lolly's Poultry Supreme
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
# Green Pepper - sliced
1    Clove Garlic - sliced
8    Mushrooms - sliced
6 oz Chicken or Turkey - boneless
-and skinless all fat
-removed
# Onion - sliced
Salt to taste

Put Poultry piece in the bottom of a small microwave covered dish.
Salt to taste. Add other ingredients.

Cover and Microwave for 14 minutes on high. Rotate dish at half time.
Makes 1 serving.

Variations:  Add # can stewed tomatoes (14-16 oz can). Also can add #
C rice and # C water to above and increase microwave time to 22
minutes rotating dish after 10 mins.
