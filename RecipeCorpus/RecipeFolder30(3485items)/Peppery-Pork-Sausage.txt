Title: Peppery Pork Sausage
Categories: Homemade, Sausage
Yield: 2 servings
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1 ts Ground thyme
2    Bay leaves crumbled
1 lg Sweet red pepper, minced
1 ts Salt
3/4 ts Cracked black pepper
1 ts Crushed red chile
2 lb Pork, coarsely ground
1    10 oz pkg. chopped spinach,
-thawed
1 tb Olive oil
1 sm Onion, minced
Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat.  Saute onion and garlic
until tender.  In a large bowl, add the onion and garlic mixture and
the remaining ingredients.  Knead until well combined.

Form into patties or slip into beef casings, forming 6-inch links.

Poach the sausages, then saute or grill them until brown.
