Title: Roast Chicken with Fresh Sage
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
Juice Of One Lemon
Salt And Pepper To Taste
2 oz Fresh Sage, Chopped
10    Cloves Garlic, Chopped
3 lb Roasting Chicken
Mix fresh sage and garlic.  Remove giblets and stuff sage mixture
under the skin of the chicken.  Place chicken in a shallow baking
dish.  Squeeze lemon juice in body cavity and over chicken.  Season
with salt and pepper to taste.  Bake at 375Ã¸F for 1 hour and 15
minutes until golden brown.
