Title: Pasta Pagoda*
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Asian
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Dry sherry
1 ea Chicken bouillon cube
1 ts Ground ginger
1/8 ts Crush red pepper (optional)
1 pk Frozen oriental vegetables
(16 oz)
8 oz MUELLER'S ridged mostaccioli
Cooked and drained
1 tb MAZOLA corn oil
1/2 c  Sliced green onions
1 lg Clove garlic, minced
1 lb Boneless chicken breasts
Cut into 1/2 strips
1 1/4 c  Water
1 ts ARGO corn starach
3 tb Low sodium soy sauce
In large skillet heat corn oil over medium-high heat. Add green
onions and garlic; saute 30 seconds. Add chicken; saute 3-4 minutes.
In small bowl stir water, cornstarach, soy sauce, sherry, bouillon
cube, ginger and crushed red pepper until smooth. Add to skillet with
vegetables. Stirring constantly, bring to boil over medium heat; boil
1 minute. Reduce heat; simmer 3 minutes or until chicken is cooked
through. Toss with mostaccioli.

Source: Back of Mueller's Mostaccioli box.
