Title: Peach Dessert
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Sliced canned peaches, drain
1/2    Stick of margarine
1 c  Sugar
Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.  Melt margarine in a baking dish.
Mix sugar, flour, baking powder and milk together.  Pour in baking
dish (13 x 9 x 2 inches) on top of melted margarine.  Drain fruit and
place on top of batter.  Sprinkle top with cinnamon and nutmeg.  Bake
at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until done.  Serve warm or cold.
