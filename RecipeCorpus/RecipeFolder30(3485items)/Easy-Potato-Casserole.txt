Title: Easy Potato Casserole
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Sour cream
1 md Onion, minced, if desired
2 lb Thawed hash brown potatoes
1 cn Cream of potato soup
1 cn Cheddar cheese soup
Put all ingredients in a greased 2 1/2 qt. casserole and bake covered
1 1/3 hours at 325 degrees.  Garnish with paprika.  You can
substitute other cream soups, if desired.  I use cream of onion and
cheddar cheese. Submitted By DOROTHY THOMPSON   On   04-26-95
