Title: Quick & Easy Grilling Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 cup
2 tb Orange juice
2 tb Ketchup
2 tb Packed brown sugar
2 tb Teriyaki sauce
Before igniting grill, spray rack with non-stick cooking spray.  For
sauce, combine all ingredients.  Brush sauce on meat, poultry, fish
or vegetables. Grill, truning occasionally, until desired doneness.
Mak about 1/2 cup.

Per Serving size of 2 tbs:  Cal 45,  Fat 0g,  Chol 0g, Sodium 430mg,
C 11g, Sugars 11g.
