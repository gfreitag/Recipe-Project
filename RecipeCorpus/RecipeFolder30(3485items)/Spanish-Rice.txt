Title: Spanish Rice
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Vegetable juice cocktail
1 c  Chicken broth
1 cn RO*TEL Whole Tomatoes and
-Green Chilies, cut up(10oz)
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Uncooked rice
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
2    Garlic cloves, minced
In a medium saucepan, saute rice, onion, green pepper and garlic in
hot oil until the rice is golden. Stir in remaining ingredients; heat
to a boil. Reduce heat and cover, simmer for 30 minutes.
