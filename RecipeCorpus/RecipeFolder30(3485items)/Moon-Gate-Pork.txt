Title: Moon Gate Pork
Categories: Chinese, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Apple Butter *
1 ts Chili Powder *
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground Ginger
2    Pork Tenderloins, (Approx 1
1/2 lb Ea.)
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Plum Jam
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
* The apple butter and chili powder approximate Hoisin Sauce. If
Hoisin Sauce is available, use 2 Tbs instead of the apple butter and
chili powder.

Wipe pork tenderloin; place in a shallow glass baking dish. Combine
all remaining ingredients. Pour over pork, turning each piece until
well coated. Cover and refrigerate overnight to marinate. Uncover and
roast in a 325 F oven for 50 to 60 mins, turning frequently. Serve
hot or cold. Serve thinly sliced as hors d'oeuvres, or part of a
Chinese dinner. Makes 6 to 8 servings.
