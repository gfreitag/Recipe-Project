Title: Angie's Chicken 'n Dumplings
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 8 servings
6 cn Buttermilk Biscuits
-dairy/refrig type
1    Chicken;whole
2 qt Water;more as needed
Boil the chicken whole. When done, cool then skin and debone. Return
meat to broth. Bring to a boil. Cut each buscuit in 4 pieces.
(Usually 10 buiscuits per can). Drop cut buscuits rapidly, one at a
time into the broth. Cook on medium heat for about 20 minutes, then
turn to low and cover pan. When buscuits drop to the bottom - it's
done! Serve and eat.

Recipe from Angie Gregory, Brooksville Florida
