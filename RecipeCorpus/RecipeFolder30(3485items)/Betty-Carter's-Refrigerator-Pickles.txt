Title: Betty Carter's Refrigerator Pickles
Categories: Can/preserv, Vegetables
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 c  Salt
1 1/3 tb Celery seed
1 1/3 tb Mustard seed
1 1/3 ts Turmeric
12 lb Cucumbers,small -- very
Small
1    Onion -- sliced
4 c  Sugar
4 c  Vinegar
Slice cucumbers and onion and place in gallon jars. Mix remaining
ingredients well. Pour mixture over cucumbers. Screw lids on tightly.
Place in refrigerator. Let stand 5 days before tasting. Will keep for
a year.
