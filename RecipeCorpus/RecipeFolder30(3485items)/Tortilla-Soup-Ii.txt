Title: Tortilla Soup Ii
Categories: Soup/stew, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 pk Doritos
Sour Cream
Avocado
Salt
6    Tomatoes
1 lg Onion
2    Clove Garlic
1    Pepper, Jalapeno
32 oz Chicken Broth
Notes:  Recipe doesn't specify what size bag of Doritos or number of
servings recipe makes. All veggies should be fresh.

Saute tomatoes, onion, garlic, and jalapeno.  Stir in chicken broth.
Crumble Doritos into separate bowls.  Pour mixture over crumbled
doritos and garnish with 1 tablespoon sour cream and a couple of
slices of avocado. Season with salt.

From:  The Texas Celebrity Cookbook, p. 230.  Recipe from Mr. and
Mrs. Coke Smith (Tracy)
