Title: Blackberry Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Pears (1 lb 10 oz)
2 c  Cool Whip
1 pk Frozen blackberries (Also
-tough to find sometimes.)
1 pk (6 oz) blackberry jello
-(This is pretty hard to
-find.)
2 c  Boiling water
1 c  Cold water
Dissolve jello in boiling water.  Add cold water.  Chill until
slightly thickened.  Mash pears (I suppose you could put them in the
food processor).  Fold into thickened jello.  Swirl in Cool Whip.
Fold in thawed blackberries.  Chill until set.
