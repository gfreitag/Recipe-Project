Title: Reunion Pea Casserole
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Sliced yellow zucchini
1 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Butter or margarine, melted
4 ea Eggs, well beaten
2 c  (8oz) shredded Mozzarella
-cheese
2 c  (8oz)shredded Cheddar cheese
2 ea (8oz) packages refrigerated
-crescent dinner rolls
1 lb Bulk pork sausage
2 ea 16 oz cans black-eyed peas,
-drained
1 ea 4oz can chopped green chiles
-drained
1 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Dried whole oregano
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Salt
Cooking time: a little over 1 hour.

Cook sausage in skillet over medium heat until browned, stirring to
crumble. Drain well. Combine with next 7 ingredients; set aside.

Saute squash, zucchini, and onion in butter until tender, and drain
well. Cool 5 minutes. Combine eggs and cheeses; fold into squash
mixture, set aside.

Separate crescent roll dough into 2 long rectangles; pinch dough
together. Roll out to a 17" x 13" rectancle. Place dough in a lightly
greased 13" x 9" x 2" baking dish; press on bottom & up sides to form
crust. Layer sausage and pea mixture on dough, and top with squash
mixture.

Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 to 35 minutes. Let stand 15 minutes
before serving.

Recipe from Joyce Carrol, printed in "Southern Living" January, 1987
