Title: Beef Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Homemade
Yield: 6 servings
Peeled and halved
6    Carrots -- halved
10 sm White Boiling Onions (To
12    )
3    Stalk Celery -- cut in
Thirds
2 md Tomatoes, Red Ripe -- cut in
Eighths
1 c  Dry Red Wine
2 tb Flour
1/4 c  Cold Water
4 tb Butter
3 lb Lean Beef Cubes
2 md Onions -- chopped
1    Clove Garlic -- chopped
Fine
3 c  Water
2    Bay Leaves
pn Dried Thyme
1 1/2 tb Salt
2 ds Pepper -- or to taste
5    White Potatoes (Good-Sized)
Melt butter in a large Dutch oven. Add beef cubes and brown fairly
rapidly. Remove beef cubes, lower heat, and add onions and garlic.
Saute until onions are limp. Return beef cubes and add 3 cups water,
bay leaves, thyme, salt, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for 1 1/2
hours. Then add potatoes, vegetables, and wine. Cook for 1 hr more.
Mix flour with the cold water in a lidded jar and shake vigorously.
Add to stew to thicken gravy to desired consistency. Yield: 6 servings
