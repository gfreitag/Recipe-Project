Title: Chocolate Cream Nectar^
Categories: Homestyle
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Whipped cream
2    Squares chocolate
1/2 c  Brewed coffee
3 c  Water
Melt chocolate in saucepan over low heat. Add coffee and cook 2
minutes, stirring constantly. Add sugar and water and cook 5 minutes.
Chill, add vanilla. Put about a tablespoon whipped cream into
glasses, pour in chocolate. Be sure beverage is thoroughly chilled
before serving.
