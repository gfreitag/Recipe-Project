Title: 279804 Midwest Wild Rice Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Rice
Yield: 8 servings
2 ts Chicken flavor instant
Bouillon
1/4 ts Pepper
4 c  Water
2 tb Soy sauce
6 sl Bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 c  Uncooked wild rice
1/2 c  Uncooked brown rice
1 c  Sliced carrots
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1 md Onion, chopped
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  In 3-quart casserole, combine all
ingredients and cover.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 to 2 hours or
until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.
