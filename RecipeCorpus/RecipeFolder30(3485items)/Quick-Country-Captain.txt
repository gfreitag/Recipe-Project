Title: Quick Country Captain
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Cooked chicken chunks
1/4 c  Raisins
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Thyme leaves,crushed
1/2 c  Water
2 c  Diced tomatoes
2 tb Salad oil
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Green pepper,cut in 1/2"
-squares
1 ts Curry powder
In a large skillet heat oil until hot. Add onion and green pepper;
saute until tender, about 5 minutes. Add tomatoes, chicken, raisins,
salt, thyme and water. Simmer, covered, until hot, about 5 minutes.
Serve over steamed rice, if desired.

Serves 4.
