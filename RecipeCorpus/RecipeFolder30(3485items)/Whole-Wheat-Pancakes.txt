Title: Whole Wheat Pancakes
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 3 servings
2    Eggs, beaten
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Milk
1    Banana, chopped
3/4 c  Whole wheat flour
1/4 c  All purpose flour
1/2 c  Wheat germ
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
In a mixing bowl, combine flours, wheat germ, baking powder and salt.
Add eggs, milk and vanilla.  Fold in the banana.  Bake on a lightly
greased griddle until bubbles break on the surface, then turn.  Bake
until done. Serve with your favorite butter and syrup. Randy Rigg
