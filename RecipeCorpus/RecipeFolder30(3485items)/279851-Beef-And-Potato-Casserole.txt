Title: 279851 Beef And Potato Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
2 md Onions
1 lb Hamburger meat
1/2 ts Salt
3 md Potatoes
1 cn Mushroom soup
2 tb Fat
Season meat with salt and brown in hot fat.  Slice potatoes and onions
thinly.  Make alternate layers of potatoes, onions and meat. Pour
mushroom soup over top and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
