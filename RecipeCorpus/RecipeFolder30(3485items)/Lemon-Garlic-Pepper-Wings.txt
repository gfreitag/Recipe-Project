Title: Lemon Garlic Pepper Wings
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Crushed Black Peppercorns
2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Olive Oil
2 1/2 lb Chicken Wings *
1/2 c  Lemon Juice
5    Chopped Garlic Cloves
* Chicken Wings should have tips removed and be cut in half at the
joint.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Place wings in a plastic bag and add the lemon juice, let stand
for 2-3 minutes. Add all other ingredients, sqeeze to mix, then let
stand for 2 hours. Knead periodically. Remove wings and save
marinade. Cook wings on a grill turning and basting with marinade
until golden and tender. Serve immediately or refrigerate until
needed.
