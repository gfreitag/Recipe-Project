Title: Chicken with Peaches
Categories: Poultry, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Cornstarch
3/4 lb Chicken pieces, skinned,
-boned
2    Fresh peaches, sliced,
-peeled
1/2 c  Peach preserves
1/4 c  Boiling water
1 tb Brandy or brandy flavoring
1 ts Catsup
2 ts Lemon or lime juice
Spray baking dish with vegetable cooking spray.  Set aside.  Preheat
oven to 375 F (190 C).  In a small bowl, combine peach preserves,
boiling water, brandy, catsup, lemon or lime juice, and cornstarch.
Place chicken breast in baking dish.  Spoon sauce over.  Bake
uncovered, basting several times, about 30 minutes.  Add peach
slices, spoon some of the sauce over, continue baking 10 minutes.
