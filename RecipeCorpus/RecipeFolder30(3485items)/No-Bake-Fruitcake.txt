Title: No-Bake Fruitcake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Miniature Marshmallows --
Packed
1 tb Lemon Juice
3 tb Orange Juice
2/3 c  Evaporated Milk
2 tb Honey
Whipped Cream -- for
Topping
1 c  Candied Fruits, Assorted
Your Choice
1 c  Golden Raisins
1/2 c  Dates -- chopped
3/4 c  Walnuts -- chopped
3 1/2 c  Graham Cracker Crumbs
1/2 ts Ground Cinnamon
1/4 ts Grated Nutmeg
1/8 ts Ground Cloves
In a large bowl mix candied fruits, raisins, dates, nuts, cracker
crumbs, and spices. In a separate bowl mix marshmallows, citrus
juices, milk, and honey, and stir well. Blend everything together
until crumbs are moist, then press into an 8-inch loaf pan. Chill for
several days before slicing, and top each slice with a dollop of
whipped cream.

Note: Rum can be substituted in place of citrus juices or sprinkle on
top if you wish.
