Title: Butter Bean and Corn Chowder
Categories: Vegetarian, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------VEGETARIAN JOUR; NOV-DEC 94---------------------
1/4 c  Celery; finely chopped
1/2 ts Oregano
Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Vegetable broth
2 c  Butter beans; cooked
15 oz Canned creamed corn
1 sm Carrot; coarsely chopped
In 2-quart saucepan, combine all ingredients. Heat to boiling over
high heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, until carrots and
celery are tender, about 15 minutes.

Per serving: 193 cal; 1 g fat
