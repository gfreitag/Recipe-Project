Title: Pet Party Mix
Categories: Pets
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 c  Melted butter
1 c  Soft dog treat,
(Pupperoni, or similar)
1/2 c  Grated American cheese
2 c  Cheerios
2 ts Dry gravy mix
2 c  Spoon-size Shredded Wheat
2 c  Chex or Crispix
1/2 c  Imitation bacon bits
Preheat oven to 250 degrees.  Pour melted butter into 9x13 pan.  Stir
in cheese, bacon bits, and gravy mix.  Add cereal and stir until all
pieces are coated.  Heat until crisp, about 45 minutes.  Let cool and
add dog treats.  Store in tightly sealed container.
