Title: Golden Popcorn Patties
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Water; hot
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Syrup; light
Boil to soft ball stage. Add 1 t. vanilla, 1 t. lemon juice. Pour
over 5 qts of popcorn, heated with 1 c. peanuts or 1 c. Walnut meats.
Cover wiht hot syrup. Mix and spread. The cut into squares.

Source: Mrs. C. W. Harding, Powler Grange, Trumbull County, OH
