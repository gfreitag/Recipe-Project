Title: Baked Potato Topping
Categories: Potato
Yield: 2 servings
Oregano To Taste
Basil To Taste
Rosemary To Taste
1 c  Mayonnaise
Tarragon To Taste
Garlic Powder To Taste
Thyme To Taste
Blend together the mayonnaise, tarragon, garlic powder, thyme,
oregano, basil and rosemary.  Top baked potato and blend in well with
a fork. From: Syd's Cookbook.
