Title: Irish Curd Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs, Desserts, Irish
Yield: 10 servings
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1 x  Shortbread; See Recipe # 22
MMMMM-------------------------CHEESECAKE------------------------------
1/2 ts Lemon Rind; Grated
1/2 ts Lemon Juice
3 ea Eggs; Large, *
2 c  Cottage Cheese
3 tb Sugar; Granulated
1 tb Butter; Softened
MMMMM------------------------SUGAR GLAZE-----------------------------
2 tb Unbleached Flour
2 tb Confectioners' Sugar
2 tb Butter; Melted
*    September 2 of the eggs and leave 1 whole.
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.  Press the cottage cheese through a
sieve.  In a large mixing bowl, beat together the cheese, sugar,
butter, lemon rind, lemon juice and 2 large egg yolks.  Beat the 2
egg whites until they form stiff peaks and then blend them into the
cheese mixture. Beat the remaining egg, then use it to paint the
crust to prevent to prevent sogginess.  Mix any remaining beaten egg
with the sugar, butter and flour to make the glaze.  Pour the cheese
mixture into the prepared crust then pour the glaze over the top.
Bake the cake for about 35 minutes, or until the top is golden.  Cool
to room temperature and serve.
