Title: Big Meal Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1 md Onion -- chopped
1    Green pepper -- shredded
1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
2 1/2 c  Tomatoes -- (1 No. 2 can)
Opt
1    Ham bone
1 1/2 qt Water
1 lb Dried split peas,
(2 1/4 cups)
2 md Carrots -- chopped
Cover ham bone with water. Cover tightly. Simmer, but do not boil,
until almost tender, about 1 1/2 hours. Add peas, carrots and onion.
Simmer 1 hour. Sieve half of vegetable mixture; return to soup
kettle. Add green pepper, celery and tomatoes, if desired. Simmer 10
- 15 minutes. Serves 6.
