Title: Parslied Rice
Categories: Rice, Herbs/spice
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/8 ts Black pepper
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
1 1/2 c  Instant rice
2 ts Powdered chicken boullion
2 ts Parsley
Bring water to a boil, add remaining ingredients. Stir.  Cover pot
and let stand about 10 minutes.  Fluff with fork prior to serving.

Origin: Adapted from Watkins Jiffy Dinner Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, May/95.
