Title: Green Salad with Apple and Bacon
Categories: Salads, Meats, Fruit, Cheese/eggs, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
%%%%%  VINAIGRETTE  %%%%%
1/2 c  Olive oil
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Dried oregano
1 pn Of granulated sugar
Salt and pepper to taste
1    Head lettuce (or combo of
-various different kinds)
2    Carrots, diced
1    Red apple, diced (with skin)
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
4    Sliced cooked bacon,
-crumbled
Combine together, and allow to chill for at least 1 hour.

In chilled bowl, break up lettuce into bite sized pieces.  Add the
diced carrots and apple.  Toss. Drizzle over top of salad, and toss
gently to coat.  Sprinkle the cheese and bacon over top just prior to
serving. Makes 4-6 servings.

Origin: Adapted from Canadian Living, May 1995. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, April/95.
