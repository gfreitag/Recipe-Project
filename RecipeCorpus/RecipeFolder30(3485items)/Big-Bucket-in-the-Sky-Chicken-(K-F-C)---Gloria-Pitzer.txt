Title: Big Bucket in the Sky Chicken (K F C) - Gloria Pitzer
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
2 pk Good Season Italian Dressing
-(dry)
1 ts Salt 1/4 pound butter or
-marg
3 c  Self rising flour
2 pk Lipton's Tomato Cup of
-Soup**
1 tb Paprika
** If can't find Liptons, use Knorr's Tomato Basil Soup Mix

Mix all together then place some in a plastic bag and shake chicken
pieces in it.  Melt butter and place in pan. Bake for 1 hour 350
degrees. Make sure you coat the chicken twice. Can also be done
skinless.
