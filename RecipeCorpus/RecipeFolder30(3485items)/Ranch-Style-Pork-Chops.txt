Title: Ranch Style Pork Chops
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1    Tart apple; cored & sliced
2 tb Brown sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 tb Apple mint jelly
6    Lean pork steaks or chops
Salt and pepper
6    California dried figs
-- sliced
Place meat in large shallow baking dish.  Season with salt and
pepper. Top with dried figs and apples.  Sprinkle with brown sugar
and cinnamon. Dot each chop with a dab of jelly.  Bake, uncovered,
for 40 minutes at 350 degrees F.  Uncover and bake an additional 20
minutes.
