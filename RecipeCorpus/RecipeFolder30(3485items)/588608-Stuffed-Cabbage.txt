Title: 588608 Stuffed Cabbage
Categories: Vegetables, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Onion
ds Garlic salt
1 tb Sugar
Sour salt
2    Ginger snaps
1 tb Strawberry jam
1    Head cabbage
1 lb Ground beef
Tomato sauce
1/4 c  Uncooked rice
1 1/4 c  Hot catsup
1/2 c  Water
Boil cabbage leaves (approximately 12) until soft, drain.  Mix ground
beef with 1/2 teaspoon salt, dash of garlic salt, if desired, 1/4 cup
uncooked rice and 1/4 cup hot catsup.  Divide evenly and roll in
cooked cabbage leaves.  Mix 1 small can tomato sauce, 1 cup hot
catsup, 1/2 cup water and 1 small sliced onion.  Place in frying pan
and cook over medium low heat until starts to bubble.  Place cabbage
rolls in pan. Simmer slowly about 1/2 hour.  Add 1 tablespoon sugar
and a little sour salt. Simmer 1 hour. Add 1 or 2 crumbled ginger
snaps. Stir evenly. Taste sauce for either more sour salt or add 1
tablespoon strawberry jam. Enjoy! Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
05-05-95
