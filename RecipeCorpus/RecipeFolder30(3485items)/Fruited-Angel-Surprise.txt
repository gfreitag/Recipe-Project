Title: Fruited Angel Surprise
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  Miniature marshmallows
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1    10-inch Angel food cake
1 c  (to 1 1/2 cups) Whipping
-cream, whipped and
-sweetened
1 c  Whipping cream -- whipped
3 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Crushed pineapple, drained
1/4 c  Quartered maraschino
-cherries
Combine whipping cream, sugar and vanilla. Fold in fruits,
marshmallows and walnuts. Chill. With serrated knife, cut 1-inch
slice from top of angel food cake. Cut out a ring 2 inches wide and 2
inches deep. Spoon in chilled filling. Replace top slice. Frost top
and sides with sweetened whipped cream. Chill. Garnish cake as
desired. Serves 12.
