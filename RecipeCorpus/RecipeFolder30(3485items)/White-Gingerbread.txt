Title: White Gingerbread
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Cinnamon, ground
1 ts Nutmeg, ground
2    Eggs; beaten
1 c  Buttermilk
2 tb Vinegar
1 c  Butter (or marg.); softened
2 c  Sugar
4 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Soda
1 ts Ginger, ground
Combine butter and sugar; add flour.  Mixture will be crumbly.  Put
half of crumb mixture into a well-greased 13x9x2" pan; reserve other
half.

Combine soda, spices, eggs, buttermilk, and vinegar.  Pour over
mixture in pan; top with reserved crumb mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees
for about 25 to 30 minutes.
