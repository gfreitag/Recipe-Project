Title: Brock's Spinach Dip
Categories: Vegetarian, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 quart
-vegetable soup mixes
-(cup of soup)
1    Medium sized Onion
6 oz Fresh Spinach (rinsed)
16 oz Sour Cream
1 pt Mayonnaise
4    Individual packages dried
Mix sour cream, mayonaise and vegetable soup mix together in a bowl.
Add finely chopped, uncooked spinach and finely diced onion to other
ingredients. Mix thoroughly and chill for at least 1 hour.

Makes 1 quart.

Recipe:  "J.C. Brock Corp. Buffalo, N.Y. 14210
