Title: McFabulous Special Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 pint
1/4 c  Sweet pickle relish
1 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Dry minced onion
1 c  Miracle Whip salad dressing
1/3 c  Bottled creamy French
. dressing
Use fork to stir well.  Keep refrigerated, tightly covered to use
within 2 to 3 weeks.

From "Secret Recipes from Gloria Pitzer" (as seen in "Freebies"
magazine May/June/July 1995).
