Title: Guacamole - Standard
Categories: Tex/mexican, Dips/spread
Yield: 2 cups
1 sm Tomato diced
1    Canned jalapeno, seeded and
Finely chopped (optional)
1/4 c  Onion, finely minced
2 lg Ripe avocados
2 ts Fresh lime OR lemon juice
1/4 c  Sour cream (optional)
1 sm Clove garlic, crushed
Halve the avocados, remove pit, scoop out pulp and mash with a fork.
Add lime OR lemon juice sour cream (if using) and the rest of the
ingredients. Mix well.

Note: To prevent discoloration of dip, place pits on top of dip during
storage.
