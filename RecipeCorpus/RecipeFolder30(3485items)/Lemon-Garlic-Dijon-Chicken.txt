Title: Lemon-Garlic-Dijon Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
3    Cloves garlic
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 tb Dijon mustard (or more, to
-taste)
3/4 ts Lemon pepper
Chicken Salt and pepper Juice and pulp of 1 lemon (or more)

Place chicken in the broiler until it starts to brown.  Place all of
the remaining ingredients in a saucepan and heat for a few minutes.
Brush the chicken with the sauce, turning to coat all sides.  Place
the chicken in the oven, covered with a little sauce, and bake for 30
minutes at 350F. Serve over rice.
