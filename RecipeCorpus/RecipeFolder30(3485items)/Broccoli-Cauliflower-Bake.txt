Title: Broccoli Cauliflower Bake
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
-and drained
2 c  Cauliflower; flowerets,
-cooked and drained
2 13/16 oz French fried onions
1 cn Cream of broccoli soup
1/2 c  Milk
1 ts Soy sauce
ds Pepper
2 c  Broccoli; flowerets, cooked
in 1 1/2 qt casserole, combine soup, milk, soy and pepper. Stir in
broccoli, cauliflower and 1/2 of the onions. Bake at 350 for 25
minutes. Top with remaining onions. Bake 5 minutes. Source: Campbells
