Title: Hidden Valley Ranch Pasta Primavera
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
-Ranch Salad Dressing Mix
16 oz Thawed vegetable medley
6 oz Rotelle pasta
3 tb Olive oil
1 pk Hidden Valley Original
Cook rotelle pasta; drain. Stir in olive oil, Hidden Valley Ranch
Salad Dressing Mix and thawed vegetable medley. Heat and serve.
