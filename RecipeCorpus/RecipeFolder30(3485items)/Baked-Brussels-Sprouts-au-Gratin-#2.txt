Title: Baked Brussels Sprouts au Gratin #2
Categories: Vegetables, Restaurants
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-----------------RESTAURANTS OF NEW ORLEANS----------------------
1 1/2 ts Parsley, chpd
1 c  Water, reserved from cooking
1/2 ts Salt
1/8    Pepper
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese, grated
2 1/2 tb Bread crumbs
1 qt Brussels sprouts
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Green onions, chpd
1 tb Flour
1/2 sl Bacon
1 1/2 ts Garlic, chpd
Preheat oven 325 F. Wash and "x" bottom of brussels sprouts. Place in
boiling water, enough to cover. Boil and simmer 10 mins or until
tender. Drain and reserve 1 c hot liquid. Melt butter in sm skillet.
Add green onions and saute 3 mins. Stir in flour and cook until flour
is dissolved, 3 mins. Add bacon, garlic and parsley. Cook 3 mins
more. Pour in reserved water. Add salt and pepper. Bring to a boil,
stirring constantly until thickened to consistency of heavy cream
Remove bacon from sauce. Place sprouts base down in a buttered
casserole. Pour sauce over. Top wih grated cheese then breadcrumbs.
Bake 30 mins or until top is browned. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On
