Title: Onion Dipping Sauce Like Chili's
Categories: Down under, Dips/spread
Yield: 2 servings
-(like Heinz bottled
-Sauceworks)
1 lb Carton sour cream
1    Envelope Taco Seasoning Mix
2 tb Horseradish cream sauce
Whip ingredients together briskly. Cover tightly and refrigerate or
freeze to use within a few months. Makes about 2 cups.
