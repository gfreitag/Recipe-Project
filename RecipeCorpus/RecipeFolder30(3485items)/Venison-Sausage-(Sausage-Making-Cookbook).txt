Title: Venison Sausage (Sausage Making Cookbook)
Categories: Game, Homemade, Sausage
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Coriander
1 1/2 ts Mustard Seed
6    Cloves pressed garlic
2 tb Black pepper
1 c  Cold water
4 lb Coarse Ground Venison
1 lb Fine ground bacon
1 tb Salt
1 tb Sage
1 ts Allspice
2 tb Sugar
From :    Clarence Fontish The Sausage Making Cookbook, by Jerry
Predika

Combine all ingredients, mix thoroughly and stuff into hog casings.

To cook, boil, bake or fry.
