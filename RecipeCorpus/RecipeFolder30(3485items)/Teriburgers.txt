Title: Teriburgers
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
Lettuce leaves
Tomato slices
Onion slices
1 lb Ground chuck
2 tb Prepared mustard
2 tb Kikkoman Teriyaki Sauce
4    Hamburger buns
Combine first 3 ingredients; mix well and shape into 4 patties.
Broil or grill to desired doneness, brushing with additional teriyaki
sauce, if desired.  Serve on hamburger buns with lettuce leaf and
tomato and onion slices.
