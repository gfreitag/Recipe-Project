Title: Top Of Stove Short Ribs
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Brown Sugar
2 tb Vinegar
1/4 ts Ground Cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground Cloves
1/4 ts Ground Allspice
3 lb Short Ribs Of Beef
1/4 c  All-Purpose Flour
2 tb Shortening
1 cn Condensed Beef Broth (10 1/2
Oz)
1 c  Dried Apricots
Dust ribs with flour; brown in shortening in large heavy pan. Pour off
excess fat. Combine remaining ingredients; pour over ribs. Cover.
Cook over low heat about 2 1/2 hrs or until ribs are tender; turn
ribs and baste with sauce occasionally. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
