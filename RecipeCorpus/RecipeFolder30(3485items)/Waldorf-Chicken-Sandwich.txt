Title: Waldorf Chicken Sandwich
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Raisins
1    Celery stalk, finely chopped
6 sl Whole grain bread
1 cn Underwood Chunky Chicken
-Spread (4 1/2 oz)
1 pk Cream cheese, softened (3oz)
1/2 md Apple, finely chopped
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well. Cover and
refrigerate 1 hour. Serve on whole grain bread.
