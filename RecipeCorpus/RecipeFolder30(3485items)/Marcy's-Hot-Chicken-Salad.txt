Title: Marcy's Hot Chicken Salad
Categories: Poultry, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Crushed potato chips
2 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 c  Diced chicken
1/2 c  Toasted almonds
2 ts Grated onion
2 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Grated Cheddar Cheese
From: Marcy Moreno Courtesy: Donna Ransdell

Combine chicken, almonds, onion, celery, lemon juice, salt and mayo.
Put in baking dish. Sprinkle with grated cheese and potato chips.
Bake at 400 for 20 minutes or til hot and bubbly.

Marcy says, "My kids don't care for this dish much, but Mike and I
both think it is wonderful!"
