Title: Sweet Oatmeal Bread W/whole Wheat
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Whole wheat
1 ts Salt
2 tb Molasses
1 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Yeast
-Gaye Levy DTXT63A
1/2 c  Oatmeal (Quaker Oats)
3/4 c  Water
1 ts Water (in addition)
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
Place all ingredients in bread pan, select Light Crust setting.

This was really good but next time I will add some powdered milk to
make it softer.

Adapted from Sweet Oatmeal Bread in Bread Machine Magic. From: Gaye
