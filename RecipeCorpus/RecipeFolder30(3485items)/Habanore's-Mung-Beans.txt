Title: Habanore's Mung Beans
Categories: Vegetables, Indian, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
3 md Potatoes, peeled, quartered
1 ts Salt
1 tb Olive Oil
1 ts Cumin seeds
2 ts Lemon juice
2 tb Chopped coriander leaves
1 c  Yellow split mung beans
2/3 c  Finely chopped onions
1 tb Grated ginger
2 ts Minced garlic
1/2 ts Turmeric
4 ea Habanero's Peppers
3 c  Vegetarian broth
Wash mung beans.  Put in a deep pot with onions, ginger, garlic,
turmeric, Habaneros's, cumin & 3 cups water.  Bring to a boil &
simmer for 45 minutes.

Add potatoes, salt & another 2 cups water.  Cook for 15 minutes.

Add lemon juice & coriander leaves.  Mix well. Serve with plain cooked
rice.
