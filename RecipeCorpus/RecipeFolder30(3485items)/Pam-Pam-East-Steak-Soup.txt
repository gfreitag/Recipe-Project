Title: Pam Pam East Steak Soup
Categories: Meats, Soup/stew, Restaurants
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM-----------------------CUISINE OF CA----------------------------
1 c  Flour
32 oz Cn whole tomatoes, minced
2 qt Beef stock 8c
Salt and pepper
2 c  Half and half
2 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Butter
1 md Onion, minced
1 lg Carrot, minced
4    Ribs celery, minced
Pam Pam East, SF Brown meat. Add butter, onion, carrot, and celery.
Stir in flour and cook 10 mins. Add tomatoes, stock, salt and pepper.
Bring to boil, simmer 1 hr. Add half and half 10 mins before removing
from stove. Can be frozen. If too thick, add more stock.
