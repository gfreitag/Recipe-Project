Title: Shinina
Categories: Beverages, Middle east
Yield: 8 servings
Salt to taste
32 oz Plain yogurt
In a large pitcher, combine yogurt with 23 oz water and salt to
taste. Thin with extra water, if needed. Serve over ice.

Per serving: 69 calories, 4 grams protein, 5 grams carbohydrates, 4
grams fat, 2 grams saturated sat, 14 milligrams cholesterol, 52
milligrams sodium.
