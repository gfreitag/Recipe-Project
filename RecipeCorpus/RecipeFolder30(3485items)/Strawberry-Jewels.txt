Title: Strawberry Jewels
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 36 servings
MMMMM-----------------------------CC----------------------------------
1 c  Whole wh pastry flour
1 c  Rolled oats
Jar strawberry preserves/jam
1 c  Butter
6 oz Cream cheese, softened
2 ts Vanilla
Cream together butter and cream cheese.  Blend in vanilla.  Add flour
and stir in oats.  Chill sough overnight.  Roll out on lightly
floured boatd to 1/4" thickness.  Cut with 2" round cutter.  Place on
ungreased cookie sheets.  Make an indentation on each cookie in it's
center with your thumb. Fill with 1t preserves.  Bake 350 deg, 20
mins or until golden. Source: CC Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On
05-14-95
