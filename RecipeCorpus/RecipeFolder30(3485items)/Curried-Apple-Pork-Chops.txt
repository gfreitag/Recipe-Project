Title: Curried Apple Pork Chops
Categories: Meats
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Ginger root, minced
1    Clove garlic, minced
2 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Curry powder
2    Pork chops, 1 inch thick
1    Tart apples, peeled, cored,
Cubed
1 md Onion, sliced thin
Place pork chops in a baking dish.  Combine remaining ingredients and
cover pork chops completely.  Cover and bake at 325 degrees for 40-45
minutes. Remove apple mixture from chops and serve on the side.
