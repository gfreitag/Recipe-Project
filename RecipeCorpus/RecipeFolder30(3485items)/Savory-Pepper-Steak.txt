Title: Savory Pepper Steak
Categories: Crockpot, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 2 quarts
2 lg Green peppers; seeded and
-cut into 1/2 inch strips
1 tb Beef flavor base (paste or
-granules)
1 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
Fluffy rice
2 lb Beef round steak; about 1/2
-inch thick
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 md Onion; chopped
1 cl Garlic; small, minced
1 cn Tomatoes
Cut steak into strips.  Combine 1/4 cup flour, the salt and pepper;
toss with steak strips to coat thoroughly.  Add to crock-pot with
onion, garlic and half of green pepper strips; stir.

Combine tomatoes with beef base, soy sauce and worcestershire sauce.
Pour into crock-pot, moistening meat well.  Cover and cook on low
setting for 8 to 12 hours.

One hour before serving, turn to high setting and stir in remaining
green pepper strips.  If thickened gravy is desired, make a smooth
paste of 3 tablespoons flour and 3 tablespoons water; stir into
crock-pot. Cover and cook until thickened.  Serve gravy with Pepper
Steak over hot fluffy rice.

4 servings (about 2 quarts).

Source: Rival Crock-Pot Cooking by Marilyn Neill, 1975.  Golden
Press, New York.
