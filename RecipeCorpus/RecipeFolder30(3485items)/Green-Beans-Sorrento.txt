Title: Green Beans Sorrento
Categories: Side dish, Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
-sliced, 5 oz. each
Salt; to taste
Pepper; to taste
1/2 c  Olives, ripe; pitted, sliced
3 cn Green beans; partially
-drained, 16 oz. each
2 cn Soup, cream of celery;
-10 3/4 oz. each
2 cn Water chestnuts; drained and
Combine all ingredients except olives; spoon into a greased 2-quart
baking dish.  Cover; bake at 300 degrees for 1-1/2 hours.  Uncover
and sprinkle olives on top;  bake an additional 10 minutes.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in the 1970s. Typos by
