Title: Purple Cabbage
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1    Bay leaf
1/2 c  Bouillon
Sugar, salt and pepper to
Taste
1    Purple or red cabbage, shred
2    Sour apples, shred
3 tb Bacon grease
1 lg Onion
4    Cloves
Stick cloves into onion.  Add to the shredded cabbage, apples,
grease, bay leaf, onion, bouillon and seasonings.  Simmer till
tender. Add liquid from time to time if needed. Randy Rigg
