Title: Lumpia
Categories: Meats, Seafood, Stove top, Filipino
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Shrimp
1/2 lb Fresh bean sprouts
1 sm Dry onion
1 1/2 ts Msg
1 tb Shortening
1 lb Ground pork
1/2 bn Green onions
1 sm Can water chestnuts
1    Egg
-salt and pepper
50    Won ton wrappers chilled
Mince peeled shrimp, bean sprouts, water chestnuts as fine as
possible. Heat shortening, saute dry onion, add pork and seasoning.
Cover and cook until cooked thru. Drain pork mixture. Add shrimp,
water chestnuts, green onions. Add egg. Mix. To wrap. Put 1 Tbls meat
mixture near corner of wrapper, fold corner over mixture, fold side
corner toward center. fold last corner over to make envelope (I
dampen edges with egg white) Fry in 1 inch of shortening.
