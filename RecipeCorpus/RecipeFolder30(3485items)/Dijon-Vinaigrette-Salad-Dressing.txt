Title: Dijon Vinaigrette Salad Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Restaurants
Yield: 1 1/3 c
MMMMM--------------------SANTA BARBARA COOKS-------------------------
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Dijon
1/4 c  Rice wine vinegar
1 c  Olive oil
1 cl Garlic
1 ts Dill
1 ts Tarragon
1 ts Parsley
1/4 ts Salt
Pierre Lafond Restaurant.

Drop garlic into food processor, using the chopping blade. Add all
remaining ingreds and process until smooth.
