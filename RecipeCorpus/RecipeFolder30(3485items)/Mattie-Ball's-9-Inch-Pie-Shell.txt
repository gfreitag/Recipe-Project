Title: Mattie Ball's 9-Inch Pie Shell
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vinegar
1/3 c  Ice Water
1 c  All-Purpose Flour
1/3 c  Lard Or Crisco
pn Salt
Combine the flour, lard, and salt, breaking up with a fork until a
crumbly consistency is reached. Add the vinegar and enough ice water
to make a soft dough. Roll out on a floured board into a 9 inch
round, and then press into a 9" pie pan.

NOTES:  The addition of the vinegar adds flakiness to this crust.
