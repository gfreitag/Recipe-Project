Title: California Chess Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Walnuts or pecans
1 c  Chopped seedless raisins
1 ts Vanilla
1    9 inch unbaked pie shell
1/2 c  Butter
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
Cream butter and sugar with salt.  Add eggs one at a time, beating
well after each addition.  Blend in nuts, raisins and vanilla.  Turn
into crust. Bake at 425 degrees for 10 minutes.  Then reduce heat to
325 degrees; bake an additional 30 to 35 minutes. Randy Rigg
