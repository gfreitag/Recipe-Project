Title: Boboli Pizza Presto
Categories: Italian, Pizza, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1 lg Green pepper, strips
Pepper
1/4 lb Pepperoni, sliced
1 oz Cheddar cheese, grated
1 oz Mozzarella cheese, strips
1 lg OR 4 sm Bobolis
1/4 lb Ground beef, cooked
1 tb Butter
2 oz Black olives, sliced
1 md Yellow onion, sliced
1 1/2 ts Oregano
Preheat oven to 450~.  Saute onion until tender.  Add green pepper and
pepperoni, stir frequently for several minutes. Remove from heat and
add sliced olives and cooked hamburger. Mix combination together with
oregano and black pepper and spread Boboli. Sprinkle grated cheese
and mozzarella over top. Heat in oven for 6 to 8 minutes.
