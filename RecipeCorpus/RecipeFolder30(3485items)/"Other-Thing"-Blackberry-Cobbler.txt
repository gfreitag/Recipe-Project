Title: "Other Thing" Blackberry Cobbler
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Grated lemon rind
1 1/2 c  Heavy cream
1 pt Fresh blackberries
1 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
Preheat oven to 350 degrees

Mix flour and baking powder into a bowl, and add sugar, eggs, milk,
vanilla, and lemon rind, and stir

Pour this over the washed berries and bake for one hour

Beat the heavy cream until it foams, and add 2 T sugar; beat again

Serve the cobbler with the whipped cream.
