Title: Fresh Herb Butter
Categories: Healthy, Dips/spread
Yield: 8 servings
-Parsley & Dill, combined
1/4 ts Salt
1 pt Heavy Cream
1 ts Chopped Fresh Chives,
Place cream in a mixing bowl and beat with electric beater until water
separates from a solid mass. Pour off the liquid, season the butter
with herbs and salt, and spoon into a decorative dish. Excellent
served with fresh vegetables, potatoes, or rice.

Yield: approx. 8 oz.
