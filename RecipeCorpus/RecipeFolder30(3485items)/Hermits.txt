Title: Hermits
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 72 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
2 1/2 c  Raisins
1 1/4 c  Nuts
1 c  Shortening
2 c  Packed brown sugar
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Coffee, cold
3 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
Mix together shortening, sugar, and eggs.  Stir in coffee.  Sift dry
ingredients together and add to creamed mixture.  Stir in nuts and
raisins. Chill for 1 hour.

Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

Drop rounded teaspoonsful about 2 inches apart on lightly greased
baking sheet.  Bake about 8-10 minutes, just until set.

: Betty Crocker, 1956
