Title: Veggiewiches
Categories: Sandwich, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Celery; sliced
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Pepper
8 sl Rye OR Pumpernickle bread
Lettuce
8 oz Container soft-style Cream
-cheese
3/4 c  Carrot; shredded
1/4 c  Green pepper; chopped
1/4 c  Cucumber; chopped
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
In a mixing bowl combine soft-style cream cheese, carrot, green
pepper, cucumber, onion, celery, lemon juice, and pepper.  Chill, if
desired.
Spread vegetable mixture on half of the rye or pumpernickle bread
slices. Top with lettuce and remaining bread slies.  Makes 4
sandwiches. Source: BH&G Summertime Cooking. Typos by Elizabeth Wood.
