Title: Fish and Okra Stew
Categories: Fish, Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 9 servings
1/2 c  Water
2 md Sweet potatoes; peeled and
- diced
6 c  Okra; fresh, sliced
1 c  Tomato; peeled, diced
1 lb Cod fillets; cut into 1 inch
- pieces **
Vegetable cooking spray
1 c  Onion; diced
2/3 c  Carrot; diced
1/2 c  Bell pepper; green, diced
1/2 c  Celery; diced
3 cn Vegetable juice cocktail;
- spicy hot (V8), 6 0z cans
** suggested alternates: Lingcod, Sea Bass, Snapper Coat a Dutch oven
with cooking spray; place over medium-high heat until hot. Add onion
and next 3 ingredients; saute until tender. Add vegetable juice
cocktail, water, sweet potato, okra and tomato. Bring to a boil;
cover; reduce heat and simmer 35 minutes or until potato is tender.
Add fish; stir gently. Cover and cook 10 minutes or until fish flakes
easily when tested with a fork.

Goes great with coleslaw and crusty French rolls.

Nutrients per serving: calories 149; protein 12.9g; carbohydrates
21.6g; fat 1.1g; cholesterol 19mg; sodium 268mg
