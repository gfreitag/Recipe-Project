Title: Pineapple Pudding Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Puddings
Yield: 3 servings
3 tb Soft butter
1 cn Crushed pineapple plus juice
1 c  Nuts
2    Eggs
2 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Soda
1 ts Baking powder
Sift together flour, sugar, salt, soda and baking powder; blend in
butter, pineapple, nuts, eggs, and coconut.  Beat well and bake in a
greased pan for 45 minutes at 350 degrees. Randy Rigg
