Title: Uncooked Blackberry Jam
Categories: Can/preserv, Fruit
Yield: 7 half-pints
1 c  Water
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 qt Blackberries
5 c  Sugar
1 pk Powdered pectin (1 3/4oz)
Crush enough blackberries in a large bowl with a potato masher to
measure 3 cups.  Stir in the sugar, and let the berry mixture stand
20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Combine the pectin and water in a saucepan; bring to a boil, stirring
constantly.  Boil 1 minute.

Add the pectin mixture, cinnamon and cloves to the blackberry mixture,
stirring 2 to 3 minutes or until all the sugar dissolves.

Spoon the jam into the jars or freezer containers, filling to within a
1/4-inch from the top; seal.  Let stand at room temperature 24 hours
or until set.  Store in the refrigerator or freezer.
