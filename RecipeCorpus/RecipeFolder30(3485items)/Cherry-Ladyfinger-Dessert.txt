Title: Cherry-Ladyfinger Dessert
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1 pk Ladyfingers; 3 oz. pkg.
Cream, whipped; optional
Cherries, maraschino; opt.
16 oz Cherries, red sour; pitted
1/2 c  Sugar
3 oz Gelatin, cherry flavored
1/2 pt Cream, whipping
Drain cherries, reserving juice, set cherries aside.  Measure cherry
juice and add enough hot water to make 1 cup; combine with sugar in a
saucepan and bring to a boil.  Dissolve gelatin in hot liquid; chill
until mixture is slightly thick.

Whip cream and fold into thickened, cooked gelatin mixture.  Fold in
cherries.  Line bottom of 8-inch square pan with halved ladyfingers.
Spread cherry mixture evenly over ladyfingers.  Refrigerate
overnight. To serve, garnish with whipped cream and maraschino
cherries, if desired.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in the 1970s. Typos by
