Title: Seven Layer Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 48 servings
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
1 c  Chopped nuts
Semi-sweet chocolate pieces
Butterscotch pieces
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter
1 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1 c  Shredded coconut
1    6 oz pkg
1    6 oz pkg
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Melt butter in 13 X 9 X 2" pan. Sprinkle
crumbs evenly over the melted butter and press down. Sprinkle on the
coconut, then the chocolate, then the butterscotch pieces. Pour milk
evenly over all.  Sprinkle on the nuts and press lightly. Bake for 30
minutes. Cool in pan on wire rack.  Cut into 1 X 2" bars. Makes 48
bars.
