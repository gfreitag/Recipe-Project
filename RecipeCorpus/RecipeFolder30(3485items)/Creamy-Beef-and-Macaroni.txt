Title: Creamy Beef and Macaroni
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk Elbow macaroni, cooked,
-drained (7 oz)
1 c  KRAFT Shredded Cheddar
-Cheese (4 oz)
1 lb Ground beef
1 cn Spaghetti sauce (30 oz)
1/2 c  MIRACLE WHIP or MIRACLE WHIP
-Light Dressing
Brown ground beef; drain. Add spaghetti sauce, MIRACLE WHIP and
macaroni. Heat stirring occasionally. Top with cheese.
