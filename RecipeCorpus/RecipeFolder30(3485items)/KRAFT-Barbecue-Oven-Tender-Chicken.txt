Title: KRAFT Barbecue Oven Tender Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn KRAFT Original Barbecue
-Sauce (18 oz)
1    Broiler-fryer chicken, cut
-up (3 to 3 1/2 pounds)
Prep time: 5 minutes. Cooking time: 1 hour.

Heat oven to 350'F. Place Chicken in 13x9" baking dish. Pour barbecue
sauce over chicken. Bake, uncovered, 1 hour or until chicken is
cooked through. Makes 3-4 servings.
