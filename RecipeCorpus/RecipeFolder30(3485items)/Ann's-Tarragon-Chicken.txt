Title: Ann's Tarragon Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 ds Garlic powder
1 ds Onion powder
3/4 lb Chicken pieces, skinned,
-boned
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 tb Dried leaf tarragon
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts White pepper
In a small bowl, combine Parmesan cheese, tarragon leaves, paprika,
white pepper, and a dash of garlic powder and onion powder.  Preheat
oven to 350 F (175 C).  Spray a baking dish with vegetable cooking
spray. Spray chicken with vegetable cooking spray and then roll in
Parmesan cheese mixture. Place coated pieces in baking dish.  Cover
with foil and bake about 20 minutes.  Uncover and continue baking 10
minutes until lightly browned.
