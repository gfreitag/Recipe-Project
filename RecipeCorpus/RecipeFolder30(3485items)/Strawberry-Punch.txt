Title: Strawberry Punch
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 20 servings
1    Bottle champagne
1 pt Fresh strawberries
2 lg Cn froz lemonade concent
1 lg Cn pineapple juice
1    Bottle white wine
Combine frozen lemonade and pineapple juice in punch bowl; add bottle
of white wine. Mix well. Add ice before srving and pour in chilled
champagne. Garnish with strawberries. Servings estimated.
