Title: CALIFORNIA TURKEY TOSTADAS
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 tostadas  1 lb ground turkey 1 TB oil 1/2 c chopped onion 1/2 ts salt 1/8 ts
pepper 1/8 ts garlic powder 4 oz can diced green chiles 1 1/2 c
shredded cheddar cheese (6 oz) 4 tostada shells (or fry corn
tortillas in 1/4 cup oil til
crisp) 4-5 c shredded lettuce 1/2 c chopped tomato 1/4 c sour cream
1/4 c sliced olives

Brown turkey in oil till crumbly.  Add onion and saute lightly. Stir
in salt, pepper, garlic powder, green chilies and 1 cup cheese. Place
each tostada shell on plate and top with lettuce, turkey mix and
remaining cheese.  Garnish with chopped tomato, sour cream and sliced
olives. Makes 4 servings.
