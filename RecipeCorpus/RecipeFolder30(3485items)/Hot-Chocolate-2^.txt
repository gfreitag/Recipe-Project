Title: Hot Chocolate 2^
Categories: Homestyle
Yield: 1 servings
1    Text recipe
Heat to the boiling point 1 cup of milk. Mix together 1 teaspoon
cocoa and 1 teaspoon sugar and add to the milk. Stir until well
dissolved. This makes one cup. Half water may be used. Many of the
recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
