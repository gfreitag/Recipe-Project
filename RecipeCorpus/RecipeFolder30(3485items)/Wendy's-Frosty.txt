Title: Wendy's Frosty
Categories: Beverages, Ice cream
Yield: 2 servings
4 c  Vanilla ice cream
3/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Chocolate drink powder
Combine all ingredients in a blender.  Blend on medium speed until
creamy.
Stir if necessary.  Source: Top Secret Recipes
