Title: Maltaise Asparagus Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Lemon Juice
1 ts Minced Orange Zest
1/4 ts Salt
2 ds Tabasco Sauce
1/2 c  Unsalted Butter
3 lg Egg Yolks
1 1/2 ts Hot Water
2 ts Orange Juice
Melt butter.  Heat egg yolks and 1-1/2 ts water in top of double
boiler over simmering water, whisking constantly, until yolks triple
in volume. Remove pan from water; whisk butter very gradually into
egg yolks.  Whisk in remaining ingredients.  Let stand at room
temperature until ready to serve.  Serve with cooked fresh asparagus.
From: Syd's Cookbook.
