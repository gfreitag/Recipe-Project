Title: Big Boy Strawberry Pie
Categories: Fruit, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Water
1 sm Pkg. strawberry jello
4 c  Fresh strawberries
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
Combine the sugar and the 1 cup of water; bring to a boil. Stir in
paste of cornstarch and the 1/2 cup of water. Boil gently until clear
and thick, about 5 minutes. Stir in jello. Cool a little. Fold in
fresh berries and pour into baked shell.
