Title: Quick Peach Cobbler
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
3/4 c  Milk
1 lg Can of peaches
1    Stick of butter
1 c  Self rising flour
1 c  Sugar
Melt butter.  Mix flour, sugar, and milk, then pour over butter.  Pour
peaches and juice over the mixture.  Use a 9 x 13 inch pan.  Bake 15
minutes at 350 degrees, then 400 degrees until brown. Randy Rigg
