Title: Lime Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Whipping cream
12 oz Cottage cheese
1 tb Lemon juice
60    Minature marshmallows
1 pk Lime Jello
1 sm Can of crushed pineapple
1 c  Boiling water
Pour boiling water over marshmallows and Jello.  Add lemon and
pineapple juice and enough water to make one cup.  Add Jello, mix and
let stand until begins to congeal.  Add pineapple and cottage cheese
and fold in whipping cream.  Let set in refrigerator for 6 to 10
hours. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   05-16-95
