Title: Dixie Chickagetti
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Rice, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Celery; sliced
1 c  Black olives; sliced
2 c  Chicken, cooked; chopped
2 c  Cheddar cheese; grated
2 c  Chicken broth
Oil or margarine
1 sm Jar of pimentos (4 oz.)
1/3 c  Onion; chopped
1 tb Salt
1/3 c  Pepper, green; diced
1 lb Spaghetti
Heat small amt. of oil/margarine in large saucepan. Saute vegetables
until a little less crisp.  Stir in chicken, broth and pimento, and
heat to boiling.  Add salt. Break spaghetti into small pcs. and add
to broth. Boil pasta until done, abt. 10 min.  Add more water or
broth if necessary. Pour mixture into casserole dish and sprinkle
olives over. Sprinkle cheddar cheese over all.  Cover with foil.
bake at 350 F. for 30 min.

Source--Granny's Beverly Hillbillies Cookbook
