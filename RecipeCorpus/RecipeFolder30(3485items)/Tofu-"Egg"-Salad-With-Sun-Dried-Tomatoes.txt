Title: Tofu "Egg" Salad With Sun-Dried Tomatoes
Categories: Side dish, Snacks, Salads
Yield: 2 sandwiches
1 pn Parsley, fresh is best
5 ea Sun-dried tomatoes, diced
4 sl Whole-wheat bread
Lettuce leaves, as needed
6 oz Tofu
2 ts Dijon mustard
2 ea Scallions, minced
Salt & black pepper
Roughly mash the tofu in a mixing bowl.  Mix in the mustard, minced
scallions, salt & pepper.  Add the parsley & stir in the diced
tomatoes.

Generously heap the tofu spread on the bread.  Layer with lettuce
leaves & any other vegetables, such as watercress or bean sprouts,
that you might desire.  Top with another slice of bread to make into
a sandwich. Serve immediately.  Otherwise the bread will become soggy.
