Title: Baked Fruit Compote
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
2    Pears
1 ts Grated orange peel
1 ts Grated lemon peel
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
4    Dried apricots
4    Dried prunes
1/4 c  Apple juice
1/4 c  Apricot nectar
1/4 c  Water
2    Apples
Simmer apricots, prunes and liquid in saucepan 6 minutes. Peel, core
and cut pears and apples into 1 inch pieces. Add pear, apple and
remaining ingredients to saucepan. Cook 12 minutes until tender.
Serve warm or chilled. From: Family Circle magazine - 1-7-92 Posted
by: Sheila Exner - March 1992
