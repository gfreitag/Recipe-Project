Title: Oven Barbecued Chicken Quarters
Categories: Poultry, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
4    Whole broiler-fryer legs
-(thigh and drumstick)
Skin removed (about 2
-pounds)
1 c  Chili sauce
2 tb Firmly packed brown sugar
2 tb Spicy brown mustard
1 ts Liquid red pepper seasoning
In a large bowl, combine chili sauce, brown sugar, mustard, and red
pepper seasoning.  Add chicken; turn to coat well, cover.  Chill 1
hour, turning occasionally. Preheat oven to 350 F. Line a jelly-roll
pan with foil. Place chicken in pan; reserve sauce. Bake 1 hour,
basting several times with sauce, or until chicken is tender.

Makes 4 servings.
