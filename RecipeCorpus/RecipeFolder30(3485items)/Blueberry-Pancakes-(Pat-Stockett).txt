Title: Blueberry Pancakes (Pat Stockett)
Categories: Fruit, Pcake/waffl, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Blueberries folded in
-(frozen or fresh)
