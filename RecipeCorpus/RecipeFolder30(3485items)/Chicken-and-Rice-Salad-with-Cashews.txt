Title: Chicken and Rice Salad with Cashews
Categories: Poultry, Rice, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
1/2 c  Chopped salted cashew nuts
3/4 c  Salad dressing or mayonnaise
Salt and pepper
Salad greens
3 c  Cooked rice
2 c  Coarsely diced cooked
-chicken
1/2 c  Diced celery
2    Green onions, chopped
Combine rice, chicken, celery, onion, lemon juice and nuts. Add salad
dressing and mix lightly. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Chill, and serve on salad greens.

Makes 4 servings.

Vol. 2]
