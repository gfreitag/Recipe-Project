Title: Easy Peanut Brittle
Categories: Candies, Homemade
Yield: 24 servings
2 c  Peanuts, Regular Or Spanish
3 c  Sugar
Stir sugar slowly in a large frying pan over low heat. When melted to
the color of clear caramel, add nuts. Pour onto a generously buttered
cookie sheet. When cold, break up into small, bite-size pieces.
Yield: 24 small pieces
