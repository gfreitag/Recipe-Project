Title: Quick Chicken Caccitorri
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Basil
1 ts Oregano
3 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 cn Tomato soup
3    Cloves garlic
1 cn Tomato sauce
1    Stewing or frying chicken
1 cn Italian style stewed
-tomatoes
1/2 pk Linguini
1 cn Olives, chopped
1 c  Dry red wine
2 c  Water
Brown the chicken in the olive oil.  Put all of the ingredients
except the wine and linguini into a large pot and bring to a boil.
Turn stove to simmer and cook for 1 hour. Remove chicken, cool and
debone. Return chicken to pot, add wine and linguini. Bring back to a
boil and cook until linguini is tender. Serves 1 for four days or 4
for one day.
