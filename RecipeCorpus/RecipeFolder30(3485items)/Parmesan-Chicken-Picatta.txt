Title: Parmesan Chicken Picatta
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Olive or vegetable oil
1/3 c  Dry white wine or chicken
-broth
6    Lemon wedges
6    Boneless, skinless chicken
-breast (about 1 3/4 lbs.)
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
2 tb Dry bread crumbs
Rinse chicken pieces with water; do not dry. Coat chicken with
combined Parmesan cheese and bread crumbs.

Heat oil in 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat. Brown chicken 5
minutes on each side or until lightly browned and tender. Remove
chicken to platter; keep warm.

Reduce heat to medium. Add wine to skillet; stir to loosen drippings.
Remove from heat. Serve over chicken with lemon wedges.

Prep: 8 Minutes Cooking Time: 12 minutes Makes 6 servings.
