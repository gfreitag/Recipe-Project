Title: Jalapeach Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Peach chutney
2 ts Brown sugar
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground cumin
Makes about 2 cups
16 ea Ounce can peaches in heavy
-syrup, undrained
1/4 c  Minced onion
3 tb Minced picked jalapenos
2 ts Pickling liquid from jar or
-can of pickled jalapenos
Mix the ingredients in a saucepan and bring the liquid to a simmer.
Reduce the heat to low and cook the mixture until the onions are
tender and the sauce thickens, approximately 25 to 30 minutes. Use
the sauce warm or chilled.  It keeps, refrigerated for a couple of
weeks. Serve bacon glazed with Jalapeach Barbecue Sauce.  Bake
thick-sliced strips of bacon for about 10 minutes at 350 F.  Then
brush both sides with the sauce and continue cooking for another 5 to
7 minutes per side. From: Smoke & Spice Shared by: Pat Stockett
