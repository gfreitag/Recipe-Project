Title: Eggless, Milkless, Butterless Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cloves
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Water
1 c  Brown sugar -- packed
2 c  Raisins
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/3 c  Shortening
1/4 ts Salt
Put water, brown sugar, raisins, shortening, salt and spices in
saucepan and mix.  Bring to a boil.  Cook for 3 minutes; cool.  Sift
together flour, soda, and baking powder.  Stir into cooled mixture
until mixed thoroughly. Pour into a greased loaf tin.  Bake in a 350
degree oven for one hour.
