Title: Barbara's Skillet Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 12 servings
Season salt, pepper to taste
Bisquick
4    Lg. boneless chicken breasts
12 sl Cheddar cheese
12 sl Deli ham (very thin sliced)
Servings: 12

ING **
QUAN    MEAS

*DIRECTIONS*

Cut the chicken breasts into three portions each. Salt and pepper
them and roll them in Bisquick. Fry in hot oil, turning frequently,
until done. Drain the oil and place a slice of ham, then a slice of
cheese on top of each slice. Cover and cook until cheese melts.

Source: Barbara Pierce
