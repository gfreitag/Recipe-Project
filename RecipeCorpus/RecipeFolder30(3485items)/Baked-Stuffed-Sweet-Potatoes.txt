Title: Baked Stuffed Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Tasteofhome, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
Mashed Banana -- OR
Peaches, Canned -- OR
Applesauce
Butter
1 ts Orange Or Pineapple Juice
1    Pat
ds Brown Sugar, Cinnamon,
Ginger, Or Nutmeg -- ground
Bit Crushed Pineapple -- OR
To stuff, remove insides of cooked potato. To each mashed sweet
potato add your choice of the above ingredients. Restuff the potato
shell or, for a more glamorous presentation, place in a half-orange
shell and bake for 15 mins more in a 425 F oven.
