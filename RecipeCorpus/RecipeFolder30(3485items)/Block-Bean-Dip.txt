Title: Block Bean Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 24 servings
16 oz Sour cream
8 oz Grated sharp cheddar cheese
Chopped cilantro for garnish
Taco chips or small flour
Tortillas
1    15 oz. can black beans,
Drained
1/2 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Chopped onion
In blender or food processor, blend black beans, cumin and salt until
smooth. In glass bowl or baking dish, layer bean mixture, chopped
onion, sour cream and grated cheese. Top with cilantro, if desired.
Serve with taco chips or small flour tortilla halves. F.A.T. Reducer:
Use lowfat sour cream and lowfat cheddar cheese. *From "Quick Bytes:
Computer Lover's Cookbook" by Diane Pfeifer* ~Typed for you by
