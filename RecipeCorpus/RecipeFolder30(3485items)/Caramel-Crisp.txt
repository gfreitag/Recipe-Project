Title: Caramel Crisp
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
3 ts Vinegar
3 tb Water
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Baking molasses
1 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Butter
Cook until it forms hard ball in water. Add soda and pour over about 2
gallons of popcorn.

Source: Mrs. Albert Seckel, United Grange, Marion County, OH
