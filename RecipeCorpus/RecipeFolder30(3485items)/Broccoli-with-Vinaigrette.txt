Title: Broccoli with Vinaigrette
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
-mustard
2    Garlic cloves, pressed or
-minced
1 bn Broccoli
1/2 c  Seasoned rice vinegar
2 ts Stone-ground or Dijon-Style
Break the broccoli into bite-size florets. Peel the stems and slice
them into 1/4" thick rounds. Steam just until tender, about 3 minutes.

While the broccoli is steaming, whisk the dressing ingredients in a
serving bowl. Add the steamed broccoli and toss to mix. Serve
immediately.

Keeps well in the refrigerator. Dressing is tasty on other
vegetables, too. Serve 4-6.
