Title: AMARETTO COFFEE - GGhol
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Instant coffee crystals
Dessert topping
1 1/2 c  Warm water
1/3 c  Amaretto
Dessert topping should be in a pressurized can. In a 2-cup measure
stir together water and instant coffee crystals. Micro-cook,
uncovered, on 100% power for about 4 minutes or just till steaming
hot. Stir in Amaretto. Serve in mugs.  Top each mug of coffee mixture
with some dessert topping.
