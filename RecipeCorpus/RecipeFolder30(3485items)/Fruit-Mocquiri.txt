Title: Fruit Mocquiri
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM----------------------ALCOHOL FREE ENT---------------------------
1 ts Confectioners' sugar
4 oz Lemon-lime soda
Garnish with fruit
3    Ice cubes
4 oz Strawberries, peaches,
-bananas OR other fruit
1/2 ts Lime juice
Any delicious flavor but heavenly with strawberries. 4 oz cocktail
glass. Blend 30 sec. Garnish with appropriate fruit.
