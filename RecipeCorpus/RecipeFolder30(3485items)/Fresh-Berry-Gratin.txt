Title: Fresh Berry Gratin
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
-softened
3 tb Skim milk
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
1/3 c  Packed light brown sugar
3 c  Fresh berries (raspberries,
-blueberries and/or
-blackberries)
4 oz Low-fat cream cheese,
Place berries in an even layer in a 9-inch pie plate or divide among
four individual gratin dishes.  In a small bowl, whisk together cream
cheese, milk and lemon juice.  Spread over the berries, leaving the
outer edge uncovered.  Set aside in the refrigerator for up to one
hour.

Preheat the broiler.  Press sugar through a coarse sieve over the
berries, covering them evenly.  Broil until the sugar has melted,
about four minutes.  Serve immediately.

178 calories per serving: 4 g protein, 6 g fat, 31 g carbohydrate;
171 mg sodium; 10 mg cholesterol.

From Eating Well Magazine, Apr '93.

Makes 4 servings
