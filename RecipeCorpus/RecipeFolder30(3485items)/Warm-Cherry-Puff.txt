Title: Warm Cherry Puff
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Almond extract
2 tb Margarine; softened
21 oz Cherry pie filling
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Bisquick
1/4 c  Sugar
Heat oven to 400.  Blend milk, eggs, bisquick, sugar, margarine and
extract in blender 15 seconds, or beat on high speed of mixer 1
minute. Pour into greased 10" pie pan. Spoon cherry filling over the
top. Bake 25 to 30 minutes.  (Also works well with apple or peach
filling. Source: Comstock label
