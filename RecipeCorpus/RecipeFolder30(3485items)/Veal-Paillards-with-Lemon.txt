Title: Veal Paillards with Lemon
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
Non-stick vegetable cooking
-spray
1 tb Fresh parsley, minced
1    Lemon, thinly sliced
2 ts Unsalted margarine, melted
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 ts Freshly-ground pepper
1 lb Veal cutlets
Combine margarine, lemon juice, mustard, and pepper in a small bowl;
set aside.  Trim any excess fat from veal; flatten to about 1/8-inch
thickness. Brush veal with lemon mixture.  Coat skillet with
vegetable spray; place over medium-high heat until hot.  Saute veal
for 1 minute on each side, or until done.  Transfer to a warmed
platter. Sprinkle with parsley and garnish with lemon slices.

Makes 4 servings.
