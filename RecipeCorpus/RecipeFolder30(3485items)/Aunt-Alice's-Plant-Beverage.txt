Title: Aunt Alice's Plant Beverage
Categories: Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Clear Ammonia
1/2 ga Warm Water
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Epsom Salts
1/2 ts Saltpeter
Mix all ingredients and shake well. Feed a teaspoonful to plants once
a month. Beverage will keep indefinitely.

DO NOT INGEST. This is for your plants only!
