Title: Toll House Ting-A-Lings
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 serving
-cut small
1/4 c  Uncooked, dried apricots,
-cut small
1/4 c  Raisins or dates, cut fine
1/4 c  Candied ginger, cut fine
2 pk Nestle's Semi-Sweet
-Chocolate Morsels.
1/2 ts Salt
4 c  Cornflakes
1/4 c  Chopped walnuts
1/4 c  Uncooked prunes, stoned and
Melt Chocolate in top of double boiler.

Mix well and drop by teaspoonfuls on wax paper. Allow to harden.
Serve as cookies with a dessert or as candy. Makes 2 dozen cookies.
