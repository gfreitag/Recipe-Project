Title: Curried Sauce For Turkey Or Chicken
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Curry Powder
1 c  Chicken Broth
1/4 c  Flour -- Unbleached
1 c  Light Cream -- 1/2 Pt.
Salt & Pepper -- To Taste
1/4 c  Butter Or Margarine
1 ea Clove Garlic -- Chopped
1 ea Onion; Chopped -- To Taste
1 ea Apple; Peeled --
Cored&Chopped
In a saucepan, melt the butter and saute the garlic, onion and apple
for about 5 minutes.  Stir in the curry powder and saute for 1 minute
more. Stir in the flour and gradually add the chicken broth and
cream.  Season to taste with the salt and pepper.  Serve with cooked
sliced turkey or chicken.

Makes 2 cups of sauce.
