Title: Remoulade Sauce 5
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
Dash paprika
Dash Tabasco sauce
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Water
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Prepared mustard
2 ea Cloves grated garlic
1 ea Small grated onion
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Salad oil
1 ts Black pepper
Blend all ingredients together in blender.

Recipe from Mrs. Herman R. Brown. Source: Talk About Good! Cookbook
