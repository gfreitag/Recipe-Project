Title: Twinkie Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
6    Heath candy bars, crushed
1 ct Cool Whip, 9 oz.
1    Box Twinkie snack cakes
1    Box instant chocolate
Pudding mix
Prepare the pudding according to package directions.  Break up
Twinkies into bite size pieces into an oblong dish.  Spread with
pudding, sprinkle with 3 crushed Heath bars.  Cover with topping and
remaining 3 crushed Heath bars.  Chill and serve.  Delicious! Randy
