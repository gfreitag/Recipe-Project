Title: Arizona Orange Pancakes
Categories: Breakfast, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Finely grated orange rind
1 c  Orange juice
1    Egg -- separated
2 tb Cooking oil
1 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
2 ts Sugar
In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, sugar, and
orange rind. Stir in orange juice, egg yolk and oil only until flour
is absorbed. In a small bowl, beat egg white until it stands in soft
peaks; fold into batter.

Bake on hot, lightly oiled griddle, turning to brown both sides.
Serve hot with butter or margarine, and honey or maple syrup. Makes
12 (3-inch) pancakes.
