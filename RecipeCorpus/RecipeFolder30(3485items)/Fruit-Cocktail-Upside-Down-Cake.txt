Title: Fruit Cocktail Upside Down Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 21 servings
1 pk Lemon cake mix
Whipped cream
1/2 c  Margarine
1 c  Brown sugar
28 oz Fruit cocktail; drained
Heat oven to 350.  Melt margarine in 13 x 9 baking pan in the oven;
sprinkle brown sugar evenly over the margarine. Spread drained fruit
cocktail over the sugar. Prepare cake mix as directed on the package
and spread the batter over tge fryut.  Bake for about 45 minutes to
50 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes for the topping to begin to set. Turn
upside down onto a large platter or cookie sheet. Serve topped with
whipped cream. Source: Times Union newspaper
