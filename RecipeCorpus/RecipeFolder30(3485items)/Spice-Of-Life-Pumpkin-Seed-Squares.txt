Title: Spice-Of-Life Pumpkin-Seed Squares
Categories: Healthy, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 25 servings
1 1/2 c  Grapenuts cereal
1/3 c  Pumpkin seeds
3    Dried apricots -- snipped
Into pieces
1 tb Shredded coconut
1/4 c  Tahini
1/4 c  Honey
1 tb Butter
2 tb Dry milk
1/2 c  Raisins
The zinc in these pumpkiny confections can really help put some pow
in your love life.

In a saucepan over low heat, blend tahini, honey, and butter.  Add
raisins. Remove from heat.  Add dry milk and Grapenuts.  Press the
snipped apricots over the surface.  Garnish with the coconut.  Cool
for about an hour, then cut into 1 1/2-inch squares.  Keep
refrigerated. Bring to room temperature before serving.

Approximately 75 calories each.

from Smart Cookies by Jane Kinderlehrer "Recipes for a Romantic
Evening"
