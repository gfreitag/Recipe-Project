Title: White Potato And Cheese Casserole
Categories: Homemade, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Swiss Or Cheddar Cheese,
Grated
Salt And Pepper, to taste
8 md Potatoes, peeled
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 pt Heavy Cream
2    Eggs, lightly beaten
Preheat oven to 450 F. Boil potatoes in salted water until tender.
Drain and mash with a potato masher until lump-free, adding milk as
you do. Stir in cream, eggs, and cheese, reserving some of the cheese
to sprinkle on top. Season with salt and pepper. Place in an
ungreased casserole dish and bake for 15 mins or until golden brown.

Yield: 6 servings
