Title: Fruit Platter
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
2    Kiwifruit, peeled
1/2    Fresh pineapple, peeled
1/2 sm Cantaloupe, peeled & seeded
Fresh mint sprigs as garnish
2    Strips of orange rind
. (2x3/4-inch)
1 ts Sugar
1 pt Strawberries, hulled & halvd
3    Seedless oranges, peeled
Position the knife blade in the food processor bowl; add the orange
rind and sugar.  Process 1 minute or until finely chopped.  Sprinkle
over strawberries in a bowl; set aside.

Position the slicing disc in the food processor bowl.  Drop oranges,
one at a time, through the food chute.  Apply light pressure with the
food pusher, and process until sliced.  Repeat the procedure with the
kiwifruit, pineapple and cantaloupe.

Arrange fruit, including strawberries, on a serving platter.  Garnish
with fresh mint, if desired.

NOTE:  The fruit may be sliced with a sharp knife.  (The OLD way ...
