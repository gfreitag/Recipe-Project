Title: Strawberry Crunch Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
1    Egg
21 oz Cn Strawberry pie filling
1/2 c  Pecans
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 pk Pillsbury Plus Cake Mix,
- White or Yellow
1/2 c  Butter, softened
1 1/4 c  Oatmeal
Heat oven to 350~F. Grease 13x9 pan. Combine cake mix, 6 T margarine,
1 cup oats and mix until crumbly. Reserve 1 cup crumbs for topping.
To remaining crumbs add egg and mix well. Press into prepared pan.
Pour strawberry filling over crust and spread evenly on top. To
reserved crumbs add remaining oats, margarine, pecans and brown
sugar. Beat until thoroughly mixed. Sprinkle over strawberry mixture.
Dot with a little margarine. Bake 30 to 40 minutes at 350~F. Cool.
Cut into squares. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   05-14-95
