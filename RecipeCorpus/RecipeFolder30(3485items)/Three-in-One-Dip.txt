Title: Three in One Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 2 cups
1/2 c  Salsa; chunky
8 oz Cheez Whiz
8 oz Cream cheese, tub; softened
Beat all ingredients with electric mixer on medium speed or with wire
whisk until well blended. Refrigerate. Serve hot or cold with
tortilla chips or vegetable dippers.  Source: Kraft ad
