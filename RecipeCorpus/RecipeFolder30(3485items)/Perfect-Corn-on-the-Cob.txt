Title: Perfect Corn on the Cob
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
Salt
Pepper
4    Ears Fresh Sweet Corn
Butter
Husk and remove silk from ears of corn.  Place in a large pot and add
just enough water to cover.  Heat covered over high heat to boiling,
then boil 2-3 minutes.  Drain.  Serve hot with butter, salt and
pepper.  77 calories per ear of corn.  From: Syd's Cookbook.
