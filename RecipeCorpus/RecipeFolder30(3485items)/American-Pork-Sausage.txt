Title: American Pork Sausage
Categories: Breakfast, Homemade, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Ground Mace
2 ts Coriander
1    Whole Nutmeg, grated
1 c  Water
5 lb Medium Ground Pork
1 tb Salt
2 tb Sage
2 ts Freshly Ground Pepper
1 ts Ground Cloves
FROM :    Clarence Fontish

Combine all ingredients, mix well and stuff into sheep casing or make
into patties. Source: The Sausage Making Cookbook, by Jerry Predika
