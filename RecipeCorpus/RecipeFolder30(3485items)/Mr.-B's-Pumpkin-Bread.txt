Title: Mr. B's Pumpkin Bread
Categories: Cake/cookie, Restaurants
Yield: 6 individual
MMMMM-----------------------CUISINE OF CA----------------------------
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Chpd walnuts
1/2 c  Raisins
Flour for dusting
2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Water
1 c  + 2T corn oil
6    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
16 oz Cn pumpkin
4 3/4 c  All purp flour
Mr B's, Palmdale.

Place sugar, water, oil, eggs, vanilla, and pumpkin in lg mixing
bowl. Beat on low to combine. Sift flour, nutmeg, cinnamon, salt,
soda, and powder together and add gradually to egg mixture to
combine. Lightly dust walnuts and raisins with flour and add to
mixture. Pour into 6 well greased 4 1/4x2 3/4" loaf pans, filling a
little more than half full. Bake 350 F, 1 hr or until done. Cool on
rack. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   04-28-95
