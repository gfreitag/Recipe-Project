Title: Mint-Lime Ice
Categories: Homestyle, Ice cream
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Honey
2    Egg Whites -- stiffly
Beaten
1 1/2 c  Boiling Water
8 oz Jar Mint Jelly
1 pt Ginger Ale
3    Limes, Juice Of
Pour boiling water over jelly in a bowl. Stir until dissolved. Let
cool. Add ginger ale, lime juice, and honey. Pour into 2 ice trays,
cover with wax paper or foil, and put in freezer. Chill to semisolid
stage. Remove from freezer and mix in beaten egg whites. Replace in
freezer until solid.

Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
