Title: No Bake Chocolate Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Peanut butter
3 c  Oats (quick oats)
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Cocoa
1/8 ts Salt
1    Stick of margarine
Combine in a saucepan the sugar, cocoa, salt, margarine, and milk.
Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Add peanut butter, oats
(quick) and vanilla. Drop by teaspoonful on waxed paper. Randy Rigg
