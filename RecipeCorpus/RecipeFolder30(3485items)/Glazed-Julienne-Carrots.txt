Title: Glazed Julienne Carrots
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
Salt
Fresh Ground Pepper
2 md Carrots, Julienned
1 tb Unsalted Butter
1/2 ts Sugar
Pour 1 inch of water into a large heavy skillet.  Heat to boiling
over high heat.  Add carrots and simmer uncovered until crisp tender,
4-8 minutes. Drain carrots, return to skillet, and heat to evaporate
moisture. Add butter and sugar; heat, tossing constantly until
carrots are glazed. Season with salt and pepper.  Serve hot.  From:
Syd's Cookbook.
