Title: Homemade Sausage
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats
Yield: 16 servings
1 ts Black Pepper
1 sm Red Chili Pepper Or 1/4 Tsp
Cayenne
2 lb Pork Butt -- fresh ground
1 ts Ground Sage
1 1/2 ts Salt
Mix and blend all ingredients using your fingers. Shape into 2-inch
patties. Fry until crisp and brown on both sides. Yield: 16 to 18
patties.
