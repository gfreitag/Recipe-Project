Title: Jalapeno Black-Eyed Peas
Categories: Side dish, Casseroles, Vegetables, Chilies, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
-shelled (about 6 cups)
1 sm Jalapeno pepper; chopped
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Minced cooked ham
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
9 sl Bacon; cut in 1/2" pieces
1 sm Onion; chopped
4    Cloves garlic; minced
4 c  Water
2 lb Fresh black-eyed peas,
Cook bacon in a Dutch oven until crisp; remove bacon with a slotted
spoon, reserving 2 tablespoons drippings in Dutch oven. Drain bacon,
and set aside.
Saute' onion and garlic in reserved drippings until tender. Add
water, peas, jalapeno, and pepper. Bring to a boil. Cover, reduce
heat, and simmer 1 hour. Add bacon, ham, and green onions, and cook
an additional 15 minutes. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.

Recipe from Keith deVille of Texas  in _Southern Living_ July, 1988
