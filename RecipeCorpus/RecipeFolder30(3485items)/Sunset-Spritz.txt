Title: Sunset Spritz
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 2 qts
MMMMM----------------COOKING WITH HAWAIIAN MAGIC---------------------
MMMMM------------------FRUIT, CHOOSE 1 OR COMBO-----------------------
Strawberries
Pineapple chunks
2 l  Bottle lemon lime soda 7 Up
Lemon
Lime
Cherries, fresh or bottled
"This is a wonderfully refreshing and pretty beverage you may want to
prepare ahead by keeping fruit cubes frozen and stored in freezer."

Slice fruit bite sized. Place one piece of fruit into each cubicle of
ice cube tray. Freeze. Fill glasses with soda and use fruit ice
cubes. Garnish with pineapple or lemon slices and umbrellies.
