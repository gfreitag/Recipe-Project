Title: Old Fashioned Stuffed Cabbage
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Honey
2    Pieces sour salt (citric
Acid)
1/2 c  Raisins
1 lg Can tomato sauce
1    Head cabbage
1 lb Ground beef
Matzoh meal
1    Egg
1/2    Grated med. onion
Precook cabbage for about 5 minutes until it is pliable.  Combine
tomato sauce, sour salt, honey and raisins.  Simmer for 10 minutes.
Adjust the honey to taste.  Combine meat, matzoh meal, onion and some
sauce from above.  Wrap meatballs in cabbage envelope style and place
in a roasting pan. Cover with sauce and some additional honey. Cover
pan and bake at 350 degrees for 2 hours.  This dish freezes well.
