Title: Finucci Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
4    Hard boiled eggs
1 c  Mayonnaise
1    Onion, diced
4 lg Potatoes, cubed
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Butter
1 c  Pickled beets, drained
Cook potatoes in boiling, salted water until tender.  Drain and add
salt, butter and beets.  Mash until tender and no lumps remain.  Add
3 of the eggs, onion and mayonnaise.  Mix well with potatoes and
beets. Use the remaining egg to decorate the top of your salad. Randy
