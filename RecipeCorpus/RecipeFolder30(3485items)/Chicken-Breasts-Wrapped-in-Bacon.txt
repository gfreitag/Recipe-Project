Title: Chicken Breasts Wrapped in Bacon
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 lb Dried beef or 1 can
1 pt Sour cream
4    Chicken breasts,boned and
-skinned
4    Thick bacon slices
Wrap chicken with the bacon. Grease casserole and line with dried
beef. Brush with sour cream. Bake @ 300 degrees for 3 hours.
