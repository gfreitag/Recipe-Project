Title: SPICY CRANBERRY SAUCE - TKMhol
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Holiday
Yield: 1 batch
1/8 ts SALT
2 c  CRANBERRY'S
1 c  SUGAR
1 c  WATER
1 ea 3" CINNAMON STICK
Place ingriedents in sauce pan (except Cranberries.) bring to boil and
allow to boil for 5 min.  Add previously omitted Cranberries,
continue to boil for 5 min without stirring.  Place in bowl cool and
remove the Cinnamon stick.

Serve with Poultry.
