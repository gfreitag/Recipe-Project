Start heating oven to 450 F. In hot fat in skillet, saute onions and
green pepper until tender. Add soup, milk, chicken, vegies. Pout into
8 inch pie plate. Roll dough into 9 inch circle; arrange on chicken
mixture; flute edge; prick. Bake 15 minutes or until biscuit dough is
done. Makes 4 servings.
