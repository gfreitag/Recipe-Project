Title: 279778 Cheese Broccoli Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 tb Minced onion
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/3 c  Crushed butter flavored
Crackers
2 tb Butter or margarine
1    (10 oz.) pkg. frozen
Chopped broccoli, partially
Thawed
1    Egg, beaten
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 cn Cream celery soup
Combine broccoli, egg, mayonnaise, soup, cheese, onion and
Worcestershire sauce; blend well.  Place into ungreased casserole
dish. Garnish with cracker crumbs and dot with butter.    Bake,
uncovered in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.  Makes 4 to 6 servings.
