Title: Sea Bass with Almond Tarator
Categories: Fish, Hot/spicy, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
4    Sea bass fillets (6 oz ea)
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes
MMMMM--------------------ALMOND TARATOR SAUCE-------------------------
3 tb Lemon juice
2    Garlic cloves
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 c  Blanched slivered almonds
1 c  White breadcrumbs, packed
3/4 c  Water
Stir flour, paprika and red pepper in a shallow dish. Season fish
fillets with salt. Coat with flour mix, shaking off excess. Heat oil
in a large skillet over med-high heat; add fish and cook until golden
brown on both sides, about 4 minutes per side.

For Sauce: Finely grind the almonds in processor. Add bradcrumbs, 1/4
cup water, lemon juice and garlic; process until thick and smooth,
about 2 minutes. With machine running, gradually add olive oil
through feed tube. Mix in enough water to make a thick creamy sauce.
Season to taste with salt.

Spoon sauce over fish fillets to serve.
