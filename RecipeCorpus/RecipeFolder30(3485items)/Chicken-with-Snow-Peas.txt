Title: Chicken with Snow Peas
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
-bite-size pieces
8 oz Fresh mushrooms,cleaned and
-sliced
1/2 lb Fresh snow peas
3 c  Cooked rice
1/3 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Lime juice
2 tb Water
1 1/2 tb Cornstarch
1 lb Boneless chicken breast or
-thigh meat,cut into
Combine soy sauce, lime juice, water and cornstarch in a 2 qt. round
casserole. Stir chicken and mushrooms into soy sauce mixture. Cover
and stirring midway through cooking, microwave on high 7 to 8
minutes. Add snow peas, cover and microwave on high 2 minutes or
until snow peas are tender crisp. Serve over a bed of fluffy rice,
garnishing with lime wedges or slices of lime.

Makes 4 servings.
