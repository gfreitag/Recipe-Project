Title: Hodge Podge Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1    Onion; chopped
2 lb Hamburger; cooked and drain
MMMMM----------------------------ADD---------------------------------
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Garlic salt
2    Cans minestrone soup
3/4    Of a 46oz can of v8 juice
16 oz Can pork and beans
Simmer for at least 2 hours over low heat.
