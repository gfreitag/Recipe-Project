Title: Cranberry Kiss
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
Club soda
Orange twist for garnish
6 oz Ocean Spray Cranberry Juice
Cocktail
1 oz Orange juice
Pour cranberry juice cocktail and orange juice into a glass with ice.
Top with club soda. Garnish with orange twist. Makes one serving.
