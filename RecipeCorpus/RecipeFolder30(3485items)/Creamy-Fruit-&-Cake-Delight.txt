Title: Creamy Fruit & Cake Delight
Categories: Desserts, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk Jello, sugarfree; 4 serving
-size (any red flavor)
1/2 c  Cold water
Ice cubes
1 c  Cool Whip; lite
1    Entenmanns Fat Free Loaf
-Cake
1/2 c  Bananas; diced
1/2 c  Strawberries; sliced
3/4    Boiling water
Divide cake cubes and fruit evenly among 6 dessert glasses. Stir
boiling water into geltain in large bowl 2 minutes or until
completely dissolved. Mix cold water and ice cubes to make 1 1/4
cups. Add to gelatin, stirring until slightly thickened.  Remove any
remaining ice. Stir whipped topping into gelatin with wire whisk
until well blended. Let stand 5 minutes or until thickened.  Spoon
into dessert glasses. Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm.  70 calories
and 1.5 grams fat. Source: Jello ad Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On
05-15-95
