Title: Ciorba
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
4 oz Egg vermicelli
2 md Chopped tomatoes
2    Egg yolks
1/4 lb Cut green beans
2/3 c  Sour cream
2 c  Chicken stock
1/4    Lemon, squeezed
1 ts Salt
4 oz Shredded cheddar cheese
3 1/2    To 4 lb. chicken
1/2 ts White pepper
2 md Grated carrots
3 c  Water
1    Green pepper cut in strips
1/2 c  Fresh chopped dill
1    Red pepper, diced
1/2 c  Chopped parsley
8    Chopped scallions
Combine first 11 ingredients, bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer
about one hour.  Add next three ingredients and cook about 5 minutes
until vermicelli is tender.  Remove from heat and set aside for 5
minutes. Beat yolks and cream and whisk in 1/4 cup of cooking liquid.
Stir into ciorba. Add remaining ingredients and sprinkle individual
servings with cheese.
