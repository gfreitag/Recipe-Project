Title: Orange Chicken Casserole
Categories: Poultry, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
-boned
1/2 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
1/2 ts Dried leaf tarragon
Paprika
11 oz Mandarin orange slices,
-drained
1 c  Uncooked long grain white
-rice
1/2 c  Chicken broth
2 c  Orange juice
2 tb Dried currants
2 tb Chopped pistachios
3/4 lb Chicken breasts, skinned,
Preheat oven to 350 F (175 C).  Spray a shallow 2-quart baking dish
with vegetable cooking spray.  Spread rice evenly over bottom of
dish. Combine chicken broth, orange juice, currants, and pistachios.
Pour over rice. Place chicken breasts on top.  Brush chicken with
yogurt. Sprinkle with tarragon and paprika.  Cover and bake about 30
minutes; uncover, add oranges and continue baking about 15 minutes
until chicken is done.
