Title: Grilled Burgers with Basil
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Olive oil
2 ts Red wine vinegar
1/2 ts Sugar
4    Hamburger rolls
1 lb Ground beef; 93% lean
4 sl Tomato
4 sl Onion; red, thin
1 tb Basil, heaping slices
Place tomato slices in a shallow dish; top each with 1 onion slice.
Combine basil, olive oil, vinegar and sugar. Pour over tomatoes.
Cover and refrigerate up to 1 hour. Form hamburger into 4 1/2" thick
patties. Grill hamburger patties over medium heat, about 7-9 minutes,
turning once. Salt and pepper to taste.  358 calories and 14 grams
fat. Source: Store ad Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   04-26-95
