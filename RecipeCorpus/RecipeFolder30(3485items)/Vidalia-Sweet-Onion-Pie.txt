Title: Vidalia Sweet Onion Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
5 tb Butter, melted
2 tb Oil
3/4 c  Milk
1/4  c. cheddar cheese, grated
1 c  Saltine cracker crumbs
2 1/2 c  Onions,vidalia, sliced
2 ea Eggs
salt and pepper to taste
Combine crumbs and butter and press into an 8 inch pan. Bake eight
minutes at 350 degrees. Saute onions in oil until tender and put into
shell. Mix remaining ingredients except cheese and pour over onions.
Top with extra cheese and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes Shared
by: Bill Jernigan, June/94.
