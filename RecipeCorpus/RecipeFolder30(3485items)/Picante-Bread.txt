Title: Picante Bread
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Cornmeal
1 1/2 ts Yeast
-Sharon Sanford-VDBM45A
1 1/4 c  Pace picante sauce
2 c  Bread flour
