Title: Gingered Chicken Stir Fry
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Cooking oil
1/4 lb Snow peas or 1 - 16 oz. pkg.
-frozen snow peas
1/4 c  Oriental Stir Fry Sauce
4    Servings hot cooked rice
1 pk Country Pride Boneless
-Chicken Tenders
1    11 oz can mandarin oranges
1 ts Grated fresh ginger or 1 1/2
-tsp. ground ginger
Cut Country Pride Chicken Tenders in half. Drain oranges;reserve 1/2
cup syrup. Cook ginger in hot oil 30 seconds. Add chicken and stir
fry about 3 minutes. Add snow peas,reserved syrup and stir fry sauce.
Heat to boiling, stirring.Reduce heat, cover and cook 2 minutes. Top
with orange sections. Serve over rice.

Makes 4 servings.
