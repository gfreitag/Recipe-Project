Title: Dump Cake 2
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Fruit, Mom's
Yield: 8 servings
2 pk Butter, melted (sticks)
1 c  Walnuts, chopped
1 cn Crushed Pineapple
1 cn Fruit Pie Filling (any)
1 pk Cake mix (any flavor)
Preheat oven to 325F.  Spread over bottom of 9X13 Cake pan in layers
in this order: Crushed pineapple, Pie filling, Cake mix, Walnuts and
then drizzle butter over the entire thing.  Bake at 325F. for 50
