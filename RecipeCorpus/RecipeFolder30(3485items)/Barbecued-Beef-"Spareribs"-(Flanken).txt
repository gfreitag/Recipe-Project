Title: Barbecued Beef "Spareribs" (Flanken)
Categories: Meats, Jewish
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Ketchup
1/3 c  Water
1 md Onion; finely chopped
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
X  Salt and Pepper to taste
X  Parsley, for garnish
3 lb Beef Spareribs; cut into 2
-1/2 inch pieces
2 tb Vinegar
4 tb Brown Sugar
2    Garlic Cloves; minced
1 tb Prepared Mustard
BARBECUED BEEF "SPARERIBS" (FLANKEN)

......................................................................
Bake with sauce, or pre-cook beef spareribs in water to remove some
of the fat and make them more palatable.
......................................................................

Preheat oven to 350F. Place beef spareribs in a roasting pan. Combine
remaining ingredients for sauce and pour over spareribs. Cover and
bake for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally. Remove cover and bake
for an additional 30 minutes. Skim off fat. Serve on plattter,
garnished with parsley.

VARIATIONS:

BARBECUED LAMB RIBLETS:

Use 3 pounds of lamb riblets, cut into 2 1/2 inch pieces. Proceed as
above. Cover and bake for 1 hour. Remove cover and bake for an
additional 20 minutes.

Ribs can be simmered in water for 30 minutes, then bake with sauce
ingredients for 1 hour.
