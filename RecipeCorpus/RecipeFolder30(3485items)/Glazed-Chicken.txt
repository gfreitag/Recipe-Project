Title: Glazed Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 8 servings
-Dressing with honey
2    Chickens, cut into quarters
-or eighths
1 sm Jar apricot marmalade
1 pk Onion soup mix
8 oz Bottle Kraft's Russian
Mix first 3 ingredients. Marinate chicken pieces in mixture for a few
hours or overnight. Bake chicken in an oven pan, using all the
marinade, @ 350 degrees for 1 hour or until nicely browned. Baste
while baking.

Serves 8.
