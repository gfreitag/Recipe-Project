Title: Polly-o Quick Lasagna
Categories: Italian, Main dish, Pasta, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
30 oz Spaghetti sauce with meat
9 ea Lasaga noodles, uncooked
15 oz Polly-o whole milk ricotta
3 c  12oz Poll-o Mozzarella(Whole
1/2 c  4oz grated parmesan chesse
1. Mix ricotta cheese, 1/2 mozzarella cheese, 1/4 C Parmesan together.

2. Layer 1/4 of the sauce, 3 noodles and 1/3 of ricotta in 13" x 9"
baking dish. Repeat layers twice, ending with sauce.
Top with remaining mozzarella and 1/4 C Parmesan.

3. Bake at 350 for 55 min. or until thoroughly heated. let stand 10
min. before serving.
