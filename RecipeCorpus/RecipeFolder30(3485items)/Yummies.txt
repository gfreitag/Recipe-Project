Title: Yummies
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate
Yield: 25 pieces
1/3 c  Evaporated milk
1 c  Chocolate chips
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1    German Chocolate Cake mix
1/2 c  Margarine (1 stick)
1/3 c  Evaporated milk
50    Kraft caramels (1 bag)
Melt butter and mix with cake mix and 1/3 cup evaporated milk.  Press
1/2 of the mixture in bottom of cake pan (9 X 13").  Bake 8 minute at
350 degrees.  While it baking, melt caramels with 1/3 cup evaporated
milk. Sprinkle chocolate chips & nuts over baked cake and pour melted
caramels over all.  Drop the remaining half of the cake mix by
spoonfuls over the caramel mixture.  Bake for 15-20 minutes more at
350 degrees. (Use a 5.33 oz can of evaporated milk - it's plenty!)
