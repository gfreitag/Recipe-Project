Title: Chicken Wild Rice Salad
Categories: Poultry, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Water chestnuts, drained
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 lb Seedless green grapes,halved
1 c  Salted cashews
1    Grape clusters
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/3 c  Milk
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Tarragon, crumbled
3 c  Cooked cubed chicken
3 c  Cooked wild rice
1/3 c  Green onion, finely sliced
Servings:  8

Blend mayonnaise, milk, lemon juice and tarragon, set aside. In large
bowl,combine chicken, wild rice, green onion, water chestnuts, salt
and pepper. Stir in mayonnaise mixture until blendid. Refrigerate
coverd, 2-3 hrs. Just before serving, fold in grapes and cashews.
Garnis with clusters of grapes. 8 1 cup servings.

Tip: One cup uncooked wild rice equals 3-4 cups cooked.
