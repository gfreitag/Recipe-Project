Title: Orange Souffle Omelet
Categories: Desserts, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Orange-flavored brandy
1 1/2 ts Flour
1 ts Grated orange peel
2 tb Confectioner's sugar
Sliced oranges, raspberries,
-strawberries, or grapes
2    Eggs, separated/room temp
1/8 ts Salt
1 pn Cream of tartar
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Frozen orange juice
-concentrate, thawed
Preheat oven to 400~. Butter an 8-inch overproof skillet or crepe pan.

Beat egg whites with salt and cream of tartar until soft peaks form.
Add 1/4 cup sugar, 1 tablespoon at a time, and beat until stiff but
not dry.

Beat yolks in another bowl until thick and pale yellow. Mix in orange
juice, brandy, flour and peel. Gently fold in whites. Spoon mixture
into prepared skillet. Sift confectioners' sugar over top. Bake until
golden brown, 10 to 12 minutes. Garnish with oranges or other fruit.
Serves 2.
