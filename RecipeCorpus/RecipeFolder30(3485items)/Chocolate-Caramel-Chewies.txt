Title: Chocolate-Caramel Chewies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 48 servings
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
1 c  Milk chocolate chips
22    Caramel candy -- quartered
3/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Flour
Preheat oven to 350.  In a medium bowl, cream butter and brown sugar
until fluffy.  Add eggs and vanilla; stir until smooth.  In a small
bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.  Add dry ingredients to
creamed mixture; stir until a soft dough forms.  Stir in pecans,
chocolate chips, and caramel pieces.  Drop tablespoonfuls of dough 2
inches apart onto a greased baking sheet.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes or
until edges are lightly browned.  Allow cookies to cool slightly on
pan; trasnfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Store in an airtight container.

Yield: about 4 dozen cookies.

Source: Leisure Arts The Spirit of Christmas Book 8.
