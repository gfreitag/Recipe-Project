Title: Ratatouille - Topped Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
14 oz Chopped Canned Tomatoes
1    Yellow Squash, Sliced
1    Zucchini, Sliced
2 tb Fresh Oregano
Pepper To Taste
1/2 lb Favorite Pasta
4    Chicken Breasts *
1 tb Olive Oil
1 md Onion, Chopped
2    Cloves Garlic, Minced
1/2    Red Bell Pepper, Chopped
1/2    Green Bell Pepper, Chopped
1 sm Eggplant, Cubed
* Chicken breasts should be boned and skinned.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Coat a nonstick skillet with olive oil.  Add onions, garlic,
peppers and eggplant.  Saut 10 minutes until soft.  Add remaining
ingredients.  Cover and simmer for 15 minutes.  Preheat oven to
350øF.  While sauce is simmering, brown chicken in skillet sprayed
with olive oil cooking spray for about 3 minutes on each side.  Place
in baking dish, set aside and keep warm.  Cover chicken with sauce,
cover with foil and bake for 15 minutes. Serve over your favorite
pasta.  For vegetarian dish omit chicken and serve over rice or pasta.
