Title: Chicken with Mustard Relish
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
-skinned and halved
1/4 c  Zucchini, finely chopped
1/4 c  Summer squash, finely
-chopped
1/4 c  Red onion, finely chopped
1 tb Dijon-style mustard
1 tb Plain low-fat yogurt
2 ts Olive oil
1    Clove garlic , minced
1/4 ts Shallots or green onion,
-chopped
3/4 ts Fresh thyme, snipped (or 1/4
-tsp crushed dried)
2    Whole chicken breasts,
Combine olive oil, garlic, shallots and thyme.  Rub on pieces of
chicken. Grill chicken on uncovered grill, over medium coals for 20
minutes. Turn and grill for 10 to 20 minutes more or until chicken is
tender. For relish, combine zucchini, squash, onion, mustard, and
yogurt. Place one piece of grilled chicken on each warmed serving
plate.  Spoon some relish on each plate.

Makes 4 servings.

[LOW CALORIE RECIPES; Summer 1988] Posted by Fred Peters.
