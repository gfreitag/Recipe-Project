Title: Fig Stuffed Baked Apples
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Calif. dried figs, chopped
1/2 c  Orange juice
4    Cooking apples
1 ts Cinnamon
1    Lemon; grated peel only
Core apples and peel 1/3 way down.  Mix cinnamon, lemon peel and
chopped figs with 2 tablespoons of orange juice.  Using a small
spoon, stuff center and cover tops of apples with fig mixture.
Arrange in shallow baking dish and pour remaining orange juice over
apples.  Bake at 350 degrees 45 to 60 minutes, basting occasionally
with orange juice, until apples are tender.
