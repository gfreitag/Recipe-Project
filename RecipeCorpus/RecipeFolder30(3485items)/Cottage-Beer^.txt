Title: Cottage Beer^
Categories: Homestyle
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Yeast
10 ga Water
1    Peck good wheat bran
3    Handsful hops
2 qt Molasses
Put bran and hops into water, and boil until bran and hops sink to the
bottom. Strain through a thin cloth into a cooler. When it is about
lukewarm, add molasses. As soon as molasses is dissolved, pour all
into a 10-gallon cask and add yeast. When fermentation is over with,
cork up the cask and it will be ready in 4-5 days.
