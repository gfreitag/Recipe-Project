Title: Frozen Cherry Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Cherry pie filling
9 oz Box of Cool Whip
1 cn Eagle Brand milk
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 sm Can of crushed pineapple
Mix lemon juice with Eagle Brand milk.  Add the undrained pineapple
and cherries.  Mix well.  Fold in Cool Whip.  Place in an 8 x 8 dish
and freeze overnight.  For variation you may add chopped nuts and
coconut. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   05-15-95
