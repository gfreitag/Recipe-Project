Title: Butterscotch Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 16 servings
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Nuts -- chopped
2/3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Butter
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Sift flour with baking powder and salt
and set aside. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat, add sugar,
and stir until dissolved; cool 5 minutes. Beat in egg and vanilla,
mix in dry ingredients and nuts. Spon into a greased 8x8 pan and bake
30-35 minutes until brownies just begin to pull from sides of pan.
Cool in pan on a rack before cutting.
