Title: Double Bran Citrus Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 10 servings
1 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1    Egg; lightly beaten
Grated rind of 1/2 an orange
Grated rind of 1/2 a lemon
1/2 c  Dates or prunes; chopped
1 tb Sesame seeds
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour with wheat
-bran (Five Roses)
1/2 c  Oat bran
1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
Yield: 10 muffins

In a large bowl, combine flour, oat bran, sugar, baking powder,
baking soda and salt.  In a separate bowl, beat buttermilk, oil, egg
and citrus rinds. Add to dry ingredients, mixing until just dampened
and still lumpy. Lightly stir in dates and spoon into lightly greased
muffin cups. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.  Bake at 375 F for 20
minutes.
