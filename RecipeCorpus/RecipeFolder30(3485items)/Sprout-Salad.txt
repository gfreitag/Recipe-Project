Title: Sprout Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Olive oil
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Sugar or honey (to taste)
1/2 ts Garlic flakes
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/4 ts Ginger
2 c  Fresh mung bean sprouts
-(chinese bean sprouts)
1 md Tomato, diced
4    Green onions, sliced
%%%%%  DRESSING  %%%%%
2 tb Wine vinegar
black pepper to taste lettuce leaves

Rinse and drain the sprouts.  Shake excess water off.  Combine with
the tomatoes and the green onions.  Combine the dressing ingredients
and toss with salad. Chill until ready to serve, and then serve on a
bed of lettuce.

Origin: Adapted from Watkins Jiffy Dinner Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, May/95.
