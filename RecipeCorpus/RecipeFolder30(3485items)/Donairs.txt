Title: Donairs
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Sandwich
Yield: 3 loaves
3 ts Paprika
2 ts Onion powder
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Garlic powder
5 lb Hamburger
1 ts Pepper
1 ts Cayenne
1 ts Oregano
Combine in a large bowl.  Knead and shape into 3 meat loaves and bake
at 300 degrees for 2-1/2 hours.

Sauce:  1 cup of carnation milk and place in freezer 20 minutes and
then whip.  Add 1/2 cup of sugar, 2 tsp white vinegar, 1/2 tsp garlic
salt. Whip until thick.
