Title: Chicken-Pecan Cheese Spread
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Chopped chutney
1 tb Curry powder
2 tb Coarsely chopped parsely
8 oz Neufchatel cheese, softened
1 c  Chopped cooked chicken
3/4 c  Chopped pecans, toasted
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
Chopped pecans, for garnish

1. In a food processor, combine cheese, chicken, toasted pecans,
mayonnaise, chutney, curry & parsely. Process just until combined.

2. Place in a serving dish & garnish w/ chopped pecans. Refrigerate 2
hours or until chilled. Serve w/ crackers & sliced apples.
