Title: 279852 Green Bean Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Sharp Cheddar cheese,
Grated
1 cn French fried onions
2 c  Cracker crumbs
2 c  Mushroom soup
1 cn French green beans
1    Stick butter
Butter casserole dish.  Mix cheese and cracker crumbs.  Alternate
layers of green beans, soup and crumb mixture.  Keep repeating.  Top
with French fried onions and pats of butter.  Bake at 350 degrees for
40 minutes. Keep covered first 30 minutes.
