Title: Ham & Vegetable Skillet
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables, Meats, Stove top
Yield: 4 servings
1    Zucchini; chopped
1 lg Tomato; cut in eighths
2 cn Hormel Chunk Ham; 5 oz size,
-drained and flaked
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; grated
2 md Potatoes; unpeeled and cut
-in 1/2" cubes
1 lg Onion; chopped
1 tb Oil
1    Green pepper; chopped
In 10" skillet over med-high heat cook potatoes and onions 5 minutes
or until brown and almost tender. Add green pepper and zucchini. Cook
5 minutes or until crisp and tender. Add tomato and ham. Sprinkle with
cheese.  Cover 3 to 5 minutes or until cheese melts. Source: Hormel Ad
