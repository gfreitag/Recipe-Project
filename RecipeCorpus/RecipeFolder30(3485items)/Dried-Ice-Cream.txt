Title: Dried Ice Cream
Categories: Desserts, Snacks
Yield: 1 servings
12 ea Fruit roll up sheets
1/2 ga Ice cream; any flavor
Let ice cream melt in a bowl. Spread about 1c of ice cream evenly
onto each fruit roll up sheet <just as you would a fruit roll up>.
Dry 12 hours at 155. Store in airtight bags.
