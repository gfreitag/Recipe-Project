Title: Schwartze Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese/eggs, Potato
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Butter, melted
2 c  Cheddar cheese, grated
Grated onion & salt to taste
2 lb Hash browns, frozen
2 c  Sour cream
2 cn Mushroom soup
Thaw the hash browns slightly. Mix all ingredients and put into a
9"x13" pan. Bake at 350 F. for 1 - 1 1/2 hours, until hot and bubbly
throughout.
