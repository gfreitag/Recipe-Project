Title: FREEZER JAM
Categories: Can/preserv, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
3 1/2 c  Mashed fresh strawberries
5 c  Sugar
1 pk Powdered pectin
1 c  Water
"I just found this recipe in Country Living magazine. Although it
calls for strawberries, it'll probably work with your blackberries as
well." - Judy Krygier

Makes About 4 cups of jam:

1.  In a large saucepan combine pectin and water and bring to a boil.
Boil 1 minute.

2.  Stir in strawberries and sugar and boil 1 minute longer.

3.  Refrigerate overnight before packaging for the freezer.
