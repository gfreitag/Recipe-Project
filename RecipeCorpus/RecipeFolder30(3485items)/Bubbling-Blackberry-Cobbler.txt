Title: Bubbling Blackberry Cobbler
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 9 servings
MMMMM---------------------BLACKBERRY FILLING--------------------------
3 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 tb Butter -=OR=-
1 1/2 tb -margarine, melted
5 1/4 c  Blackberries
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Tapioca
MMMMM----------------BROWN-SUGAR SHORTCAKE CRUST---------------------
1/4 c  -margarine, melted
1/2 c  Whipping cream -=OR=-
1/2 c  -lowfat milk
-Vanilla ice cream
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Butter -=OR=-
Preheat oven to 425^F. Grease an 8-inch square baking pan or dish.

Wash fresh blackberries thoroughly. In a large mixing bowl, carefully
spoon together blackberries, sugar, tapioca and lemon juice. Pour
blackberry filling into greased baking pan or dish; drizzle melted
butter or margarine on top and set aside. In another large bowl,
combine crust ingredients. Stir vigorously with a wooden spoon about
30 seconds until a stiff dough forms. Spoon dough on top of
blackberries to create a cobblestone effect. Bake 20-25 minutes or
until crust is golden brown. Cool 30 minutes; then serve with vanilla
ice cream, whipped cream or a jug of cream.

Makes 5 or 6 servings.
