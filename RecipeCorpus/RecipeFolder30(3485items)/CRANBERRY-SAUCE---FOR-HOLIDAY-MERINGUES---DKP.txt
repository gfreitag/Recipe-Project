Title: CRANBERRY SAUCE - FOR HOLIDAY MERINGUES - DKP
Categories: Holiday, Salsa/sauce, Desserts
Yield: 2 cups
2 c  Cranberry juice cocktail
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1/8 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients in a heavy saucepan; stir well.  Place over
medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a boil. Boil
1 minute; remove from heat.  Cover and chill thoroughly.
