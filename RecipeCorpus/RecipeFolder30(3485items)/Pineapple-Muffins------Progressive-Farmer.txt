Title: Pineapple Muffins  --  Progressive Farmer
Categories: Breakfast, Breads, Fruit
Yield: 18 muffins
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2    Eggs, lightly beaten
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 cn Pineapple (15oz), crushed,
.  undrained
2 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
Drain the pineapple, reserving the juice.  Set pineapple and juice
aside.

Combine the flour, baking powder and salt in a large bowl.  Make a
well in the center of this mixture.  Set aside.

Combine the sugar and oil, stirring until blended.  Stir in the eggs,
vanilla, pineapple and 1/4 cup of the reserved pineapple juice; add
to the dry ingredients, stirring just until moistened.  Spoon into
greased muffin pans, filling two-thirds full.

Bake at 350Ã¸F for 25 minutes or until golden.  Remove from the pans
immediately.
