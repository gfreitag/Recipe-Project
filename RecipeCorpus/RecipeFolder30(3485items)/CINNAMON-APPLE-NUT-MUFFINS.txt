Title: CINNAMON APPLE-NUT MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins, Breakfast, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Milk
2 tb Butter, melted
2 ea Egg whites, lightly beaten
1/4 c  Chopped walnuts
Sugar for topping
3/4 c  Peeled, finely chopped apple
1/2 c  Sugar, divided
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 c  Flour
3/4 c  Whole wheat flour
2 ts Baking powder
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  In small bowl, toss apple with 1/4 cup
of the sugar and the cinnamon.  In large bowl, combine remaining 1/4
cup sugar, the flours, baking powder and salt. Mix together milk,
butter, and egg whites; stir into dry ingredients just until
moistened. Add apple and nuts. Fill lightly greased muffin cups 3/4
full; sprinkle each lightly with sugar. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or
until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Makes: 1 dozen
muffins
