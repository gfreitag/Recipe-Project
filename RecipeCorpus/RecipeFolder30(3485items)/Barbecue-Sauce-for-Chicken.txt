Title: Barbecue Sauce for Chicken
Categories: Bbq/grill
Yield: 2 cups
Pepper; to taste
Onion salt; optional
Garlic salt; optional
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Oil, salad
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 ts Hot sauce
1 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients; cook over medium heat until sauce boils. Keep
sauce warm; brush on chicken occasionally while grilling.
