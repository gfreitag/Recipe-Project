Title: Molasses Crinkles
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
2 1/4 c  Flour
2 ts Soda
1/2 ts Cloves
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Soft shortening
1 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg
1/4 c  Molasses
Mix thoroughly shortening, sugar, egg and molasses.  Sift together
flour, soda, cloves and cinnamon.  Then mix together.  Chill dough;
roll into balls.  Dip tops in sugar; place sugar side up 3 inches
apart on cookie sheet.  Bake 10 to 12 minutes at 375 degrees. Randy
