Title: Wine Marinade for Steaks
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
4 ts Worcestershire sauce
3 ts Garlic powder
ds Pepper; freshly ground
1 c  Oil, salad
1 c  Sherry, cooking; or red wine
1/2 c  Soy sauce
Combine all ingredients.  Marinate steak 4 hours or overnight.
