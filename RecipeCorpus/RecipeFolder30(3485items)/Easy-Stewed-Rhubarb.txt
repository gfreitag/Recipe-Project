Title: Easy Stewed Rhubarb
Categories: Desserts, Vegetables
Yield: 3 cups
2 tb Water
6 c  Chopped rhubarb
1 c  Granulated sugar
In large saucepan combine rhubarb, sugar, and water and cook over
medium heat stirring until sugar has dissolved.  Reduce heat to
medium low simmer uncovered and stirring occasionally for about 15
minutes or until slightly thickened and rhubarb is in threads.  Let
cool. From Canadian Living Magazine May 1993
