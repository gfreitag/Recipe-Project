Title: Freezer Cucumber Salad
Categories: Salads, Can/preserv
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Celery seed
2 c  Sugar
1 c  White vinegar
7 c  Cucumbers
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Red pepper
1 ts Salt
Slice and peel cucumbers.  Mix ingredients altogether and store in a
cool place.  Stir twice daily for 4 to 5 days.  Fill containers and
store in the freezer. Randy Rigg
