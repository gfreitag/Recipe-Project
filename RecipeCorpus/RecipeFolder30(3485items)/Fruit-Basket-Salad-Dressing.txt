Title: Fruit Basket Salad Dressing
Categories: Salads, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
-- drained
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Finely chopped CA dried figs
1 c  Orange or peach yogurt
8 1/4 oz Canned crushed pineapple
Combine figs with yogurt, pineapple and nutmeg; chill until serving
time. Spoon over fruit salad.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
