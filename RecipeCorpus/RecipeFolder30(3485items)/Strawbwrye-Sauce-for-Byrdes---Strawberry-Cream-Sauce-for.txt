Title: Strawbwrye Sauce for Byrdes - Strawberry Cream Sauce for
Categories: Medieval, West europe, Salsa/sauce, Poultry
Yield: 5 cups
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/8 ts Salt
2 tb Honey
2 c  Fresh strawberries
2 c  Heavy cream
1 c  White wine
Remove all stems and blemishes from strawberries. Mash 1 cup of
strawberries with a fork; or crush them with a mortar and pestle; or
briefly blend in a blender. Cut the other cupful of strawberries into
quarters or eighths.

Slowly heat the cream in a pot, carefully avoiding boiling.

Add the wine, cinnamon, salt, honey, and strawberry pulp; stir while
heating slowly for 4 minutes.

Add the cut strawberries immediately before serving.
