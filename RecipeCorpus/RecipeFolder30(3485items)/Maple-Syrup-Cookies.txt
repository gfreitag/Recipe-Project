Title: Maple Syrup Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Canadian
Yield: 50 servings
1 1/8 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Seedless raisins or chopped
Nuts
1 1/8 c  Maple syrup
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
2    Eggs
1 1/8 ts Vanilla extract
3 1/8 c  All-purpose flour
Cream together syrup and butter. Add eggs and vanilla; beat well.
Heat oven to 400 F. Combine flour, baking powder and salt. Add milk
and flour mixture alternately to syrup mixture. Fold in raisins or
nuts. Drop from a 5ml measure about 5 cm apart onto greased baking
sheet. Bake in 400 F oven for 10 minutes.
