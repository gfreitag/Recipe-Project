Title: Black Bean and Corn Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Salsa/sauce, Snacks, Tex/mexican
Yield: 2 cups
ds Lime juice
16 oz Can black beans
9 1/2 oz Jar corn relish
Mix together beans, corn relish and lime juice.  Chill.

Serve with pita chips, corn chips, or crackers.
