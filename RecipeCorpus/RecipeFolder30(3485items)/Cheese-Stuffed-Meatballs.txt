Title: Cheese-Stuffed Meatballs
Categories: Low cal/fat, Main dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 ts Dried sage
4 oz Cheddar or Swiss cheese;
-cut into 8 cubes
1 tb Olive oil
2 tb Diced onion
8 oz Lean ground beef or turkey
1 tb Soysauce
1.  Preheat the oven to 325F.

2.  Oil a shallow baking pan with a little olive oil or pan spray.

3.  Heat the oil in a frying pan over moderate heat until hot but not
smoking.  Add the onion and saute until golden brown, about 10
minutes.

4.  Combine the onion, beef, soy sauce, and sage.  Divide the mixture
into eight portions.  Take a chunk of cheese and cover with one
portion of the mixture to form a meatball shape.  Repeat to form a
total of eight meatballs.

5.  Place the meatballs in the oiled pan and bake for 30 minutes.

Note:  The meatballs can also be fried in a frying pan, using 2 tb of
oil.
