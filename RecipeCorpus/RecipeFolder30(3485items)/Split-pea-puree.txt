Title: Split pea puree
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 4 cups
1 3/4 c  Split peas (about 12 ozs.)
Capers
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 c  Onion, chopped
6 1/2 c  Water
Heat oil in a large Dutch oven over medium heat, add chopped onion.
Saute 5 minutes, add water and split peas. Bring to boil and then
reduce heat. Cover and simmer until peas are very soft and falling
apart, about 1 hour. Uncover and simmer until reduced to abpout 4
cups, stirring often, about 1 hour. Season with salt & pepper. Serve
warm or room temperature. Top with capers or crumbled feta cheese.
