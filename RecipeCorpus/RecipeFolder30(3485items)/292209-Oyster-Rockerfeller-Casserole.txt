Title: 292209 Oyster Rockerfeller Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
With no salt & drained
1    Stick oleo or butter,
Melted
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Tabasco
Parmesan cheese
2    Doz  oysters
4    Green onion tops
1 lg Celery stalk and tops
1 ts Tarragon vinegar
4    To 5 sprigs parsley
4    Rolled anchovies
1 pk Chopped spinach, cooked
Mix melted butter and seasonings.  Add to spinach, mix well and add
crumbs. Place oysters in casserole, add spinach mixture and top with
cheese. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.
