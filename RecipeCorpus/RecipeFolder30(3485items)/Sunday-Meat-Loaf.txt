Title: Sunday Meat Loaf
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Ground Sage
1 c  Milk
2/3 c  Tomatoes, Canned
1/4 c  Minced Onions
1    Egg -- well beaten
1/3 c  Apple Jelly
2 ts Hot Water
Orange Sections -- optional
1 lb Veal Neck Or Breast, Ground
OR
Chuck Beef -- OR
Lamb Shoulder
1 1/4 c  Regular Oats
2 1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Celery Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Combine all but last three ingredients; pack into a greased 9 x 5 x
3-inch loaf pan. Bake at 350 F for 1 hr. Turn out into baking dish,
glaze as follows. Cook the apple jelly and hot water until smooth,
then spoon some of this glaze over the loaf. Return to oven for 10
mins longer. Remove and garnish with orange sections if desired.
Spoon more glaze over orange sections and loaf. Yield: 6 to 8
servings.
