Title: Fresh Spinach Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Oil
1/3 c  Vinegar
1    Onion -- chopped in blender
2/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Salt and pepper
Spinach -- chopped
1 cn Bean sprouts
4    To 5 pieces bacon,
- fried crisp, crumbled
1 cn Water chestnuts -- sliced
1 cn Chow mein noodles
Dressing:
Combine salad ingredients.

Mix dressing ingredients and pour over salad; toss to coat.
