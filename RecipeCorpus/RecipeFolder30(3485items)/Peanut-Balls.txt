Title: Peanut Balls
Categories: Candies
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla
2 lb Sifted powdered sugar
1/2 lb Philadelphia cream cheese,
Softened
1/4 lb Butterm softened
Cream butter and cream cheese.  Add sifted sugar gradually, then knead
until it holds together and is no longer sticky.  Form into balls and
store in refrigerator overnight.  Coat with unsweetened chocolate and
paraffin. Roll in finely chopped nuts. Randy Rigg
