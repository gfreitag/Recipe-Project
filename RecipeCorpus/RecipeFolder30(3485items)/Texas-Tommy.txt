Title: Texas Tommy
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 lgrvings
Roll
Garnishes
1 lg Hot dog
1    Square white American cheese
2 sl Bacon
(From the Cup, Pottstown, Pa.)

Split the hot dog down the center, and lay cheese inside. Wrap the
hot dog and cheese in bacon and secure with a toothpick. Drop the
whole thing into a deep fryer. It will sink in the grease, and when
it floats it's done. Serve with roll and favorite garnishes.
