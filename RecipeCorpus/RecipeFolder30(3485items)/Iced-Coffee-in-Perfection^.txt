Title: Iced Coffee in Perfection^
Categories: Beverages, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
Whipped cream
Cracked ice
1 pt Cold good coffee
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1 pt Milk
Have both coffee and milk well chilled; mix well, add cracked ice.
Just before serving, put the whipped cream on top of the glass and
dust over with cinnamon. This is very good on hot summer days.
