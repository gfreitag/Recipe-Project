Title: Malinnik
Categories: Russian, Soup/stew, Fruit, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Sugar
6 tb Sour cream
1 lb Raspberries fresh
2 1/4 c  Claret
2 tb Sparkling water<Club soda>
Run 12 oz of the berries through a blender to puree. Stir in the
sugar and claret. Chill well. Just before serving add the sparkling
water/club soda and the reserved berries. Serve topped with
tablespoon dollop of sour cream. Origin: Chef Mikhail Junovskoda,
Kirov-CIS, circa, 1993 Submitted By DON HOUSTON   On   05-03-95
