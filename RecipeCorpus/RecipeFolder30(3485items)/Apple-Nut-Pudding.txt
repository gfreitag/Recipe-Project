Title: Apple Nut Pudding
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Vanilla
5 tb Butter
1 1/2 c  Chopped apples
1 c  Chopped nuts
1    Egg
1 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Soda
Beat egg; add sugar, flour, soda, salt and vanilla.  Then add apples
and nuts.  This is a bit stiff, but the apples make the liquid.  Bake
for 45 minutes in a 350 degree oven. Randy Rigg
