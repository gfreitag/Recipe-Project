Title: Sweet And Sour Spicy Rye Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Sandwich, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Anise Seed
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Vegetable Oil
1/3 c  Honey
3/4 c  Sourdough Starter
3/4 c  Water
2 1/4 ts Active Dry Yeast
2 1/4 c  Bread Flour
3/4 c  Rye Flour
1 1/2 tb Orange Zest -- grated
3/4 ts Ginger -- ground
3/4 ts Cloves, Ground
Add all ingredients in the order suggested by your ABM manual and
process on the basic bread cycle according to the manufacturer's
directions.
