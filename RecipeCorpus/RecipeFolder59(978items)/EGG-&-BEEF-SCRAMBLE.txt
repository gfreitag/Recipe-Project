Title: EGG & BEEF SCRAMBLE
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Beef
Yield: 2 Servings
2 tb Butter
3 1/2 oz Pkg. dried beef
6    Eggs, slightly beaten
1/8 ts Pepper
3 tb Milk
3 oz Pkg cream cheese, softened
In skillet, melt butter on low heat. Tear beef into bite-sized pieces.
Saute in butter 3-4 minutes or until lightly browned. Combine eggs
with pepper, milk, & cream cheese. Pour into skillet & slowly cook
until cheese is melted & eggs are thoroughly cooked. 2-3 servings.
