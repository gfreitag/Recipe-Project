Title: EGGNOG WAFFLES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Sifted flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
2    Eggs, well beaten
2 c  Eggnog
6 tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Chopped nutmeats, pecans or
English walnuts
Sift flour with baking powder,soda and salt.Combine eggs,eggnog and
shortening.Add mixture to sifted dry ingredients.Mix until
smooth.Fold in nuts.Bake waffles in a preheated waffle iron until
steam no longer escapes from the grid.
