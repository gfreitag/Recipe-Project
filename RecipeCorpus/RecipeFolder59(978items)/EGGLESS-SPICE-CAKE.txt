Title: EGGLESS SPICE CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Cake
1 3/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Nutmeg
2 1/4 c  Flour
3/4 c  Sour milk
1/2 c  Pecans or walnuts
1) Cream butter and sugar well.

2) Combine dry ingredients and add alternately with milk to
butter/sugar mixture.

3) Add the nuts and put in a well greased 8 x 8 inch pan.

4) Bake at 350 for 35 to 40 minutes. Submitted By RHOMMEL
