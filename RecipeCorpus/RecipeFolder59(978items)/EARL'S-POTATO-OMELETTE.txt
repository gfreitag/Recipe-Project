Title: EARL'S POTATO OMELETTE
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 Omelette  2 med onions, peeled, sliced 6 med potatoes, peeled, boiled 2 tbs
lard or oleo 3 eggs, beaten salt and pepper

Directions: In a skillet, melt shortening.  Saute onions until opaque.
Slice potatoes while onions are cooking. Add potatoes to skillet.
Salt and pepper. Add beaten eggs and turn several times with pancake
turner to mix seasoning and eggs into onions and potatoes. Cook under
medium heat until firm.
