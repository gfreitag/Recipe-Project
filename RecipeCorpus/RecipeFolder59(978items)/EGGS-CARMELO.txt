Title: EGGS CARMELO
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Lightly salted butter
1 tb Dried rosemary
6    Very ripe plum tomatoes,
-sliced
12    Eggs
Salt & pepper to taste
Melt butter; add rosemary and bring to a quick bubble over high heat.
Add tomato slices, reduce heat to medium and cook 7 to 10 minutes.

Preheat broiler.

Beat eggs with salt and pepper. Add to tomatoes and mix gently with
spatula. Broil 3 to 5 minutes, until eggs are set to desired doneness.
