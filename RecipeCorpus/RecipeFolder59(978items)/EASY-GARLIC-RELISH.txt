Title: EASY GARLIC RELISH
Categories: Relishes, Garlic, Low-cal
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Cucumber (medium)
2    Garlic cloves, peeled
Salt & pepper to taste
Use an unwaxed garden cucumber; wash, but don't peel.
Slice it into food processor bowl.  Add garlic.
Process with pulse motion just until coarsely chopped.
Empty mixture into filter-lined coffee cone to drain, for one hour or
more.  Season to taste.  Makes approximately 1 cup, under 5 calories
per tablespoon.
