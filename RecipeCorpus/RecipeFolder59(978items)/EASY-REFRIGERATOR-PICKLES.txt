Title: EASY REFRIGERATOR PICKLES
Categories: Pickles
Yield: 6 Servings
7 c  Sliced cucumbers
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Sliced sweet onions
1 c  White vinegar
1    Bell pepper, finely sliced
1 tb Salt
1 ts Celery seed
Combine cucumbers, onion, and bell pepper in a large bowl. Add the
sugar, vinegar, salt and celery seed. Mix well. Cover and refrigerate
at least 24 hours before serving. For a different taste, add garlic,
dill, oregano, or hot pepper sauce.

Source: Rock Springs Sweet Onion Festival Cook Book 1992
