Title: EGGS FLORENTINE
Categories: Cheese, Eggs
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 lb Spinach, cooked
4 ea Egg
3 tb Butter
2 tb Onion, chopped
1 ea Morney sauce recipe
Heat butter in skillet.  Add onions and cook until tender. Add
spinach and toss until mixed. Place in a baking dish. Slip eggs into
hollows made in mixture. Cover with mornay sauce and bake at 350 deg
for 10 minutes or till eggs are set.
