Title: EGG SAILBOATS
Categories: Egg, Sailboats, With, Cheese, Mast
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Hard boiled eggs -- peeled
6 sl American cheese
12    Toothpicks
Cut the eggs in half lengthwise.  Cut the cheese slices in half
diagonally for form triangular sails and skewer each on a toothpick
mast. Stick the toothpick into the egg to form a boat.
