Title: DUMP CAKE #2
Categories: Haven't tri, Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
1 cn Cherry pie filling
1 cn Crushed pineapple
1    Yellow cake mix
1/2 c  Nuts -- chopped
1    Cube butter -- sliced
Dump cherry pie filling, crushed pineapple in a 9 x 13-inch pan. Do
not stir. Sprinkle cake mix evenly over fruit. Dot with butter. Top
with nuts. Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour. Serve warm with vanilla
ice cream, whipped cream or plain cream poured over top.
