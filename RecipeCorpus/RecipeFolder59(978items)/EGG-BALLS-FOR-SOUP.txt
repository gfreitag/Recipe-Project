Title: EGG BALLS FOR SOUP
Categories: Eggs, Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
8 ea Egg yokes
1/2 tb Wheat flour
1 ts Salt
Take the yokes of 6 hard-boiled eggs and half a tablespoonfull of
wheat flour, rub them smooth with the yokes of two raw eggs and aa
teaspoon of salt; mix all well together; make it into balls, and drop
them into the boiling soup a few minutes before taking it up.
