Title: DYNAMITE CHEESE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Cake
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1 c  Graham cracker crumbs (or
-vanilla cookies)
1/2 c  Ground nuts
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Margarine
MMMMM----------------------------CAKE---------------------------------
3 pk (8oz) cream cheese; softened
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1    Lemon , juice of
6 lg Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Almond extract
1 pt Sour cream
Crust: Mix together with hands and press into an 8" springform pan,
covering bottom and 1-inch up the sides, set aside.

Cake: Cream sugar and cheese till smooth, get out all lumps. Add eggs
1 at a time, beating only until incorporated. Add remaining
ingredients and blend well. Pour into pan and bake at 325 for 1 hour.
Turn off heat and leave in oven with door closed for 1 additional
hour, DO NOT OPEN DOOR! Cool completely and refrigerate. (Can be
frozen)

Cake is moist enough not to require toppings. It's good with fresh
sliced fruit or berries very impressive with glazed whole
strawberries on top
