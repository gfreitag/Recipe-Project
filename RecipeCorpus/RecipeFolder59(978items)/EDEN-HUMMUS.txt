Title: EDEN HUMMUS
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
24 oz EDEN Great Northern Beans
-OR- EDEN Garbanzo Beans
- (jar-OR-
2 cn EDEN Navy Beans (15 oz ea)
-OR- EDEN Garbanzo Beans
1/4    CUP tahini
2 tablespoons EDEN EXtra Virgin Olive Oil 2 tablespoons EDEN Umeboshi
Paste 3-4 cloves garlic, chopped % lemon, juiced

Blend all ingredients in a food processor or

mash with a potato masher, adjusting consistency

with a little water if desired. Now you can make

a quick, homemade version of this Middle Eastern favorite. We use
umeboshi paste for added flavor. Serve as a dip with chiPs or with
warm pita bread.

Prep Time: 10 minutes Yield: 6 servings

Per serving: 237 Calories; 11g Fat (38% calories from fat); 10g
Protein; 28g Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 102mg Sodium
