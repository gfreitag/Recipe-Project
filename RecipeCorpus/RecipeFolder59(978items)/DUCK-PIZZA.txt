Title: DUCK PIZZA
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Hoisin sauce
1 oz Pizza sauce
3 oz Duck meat without skin --
Roasted
1/2 oz Green onion
1/4 ts Sesame seeds
1/4 ts Peppercorns -- Szechwan
3 oz Mozzarella cheese -- smoked
Preheat oven to 500F with pizza stone. Blend hoisin and pizza sauces.
Spread sauce on dough and cover with shredded duck. Cover with cheese
and onions. Sprinkle sesame seeds and peppers evenly across pizza.
Place on pizza stone in oven and bake until golden brown.
