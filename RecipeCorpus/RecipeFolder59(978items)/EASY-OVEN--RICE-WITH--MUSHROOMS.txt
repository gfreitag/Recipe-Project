Title: EASY-OVEN  RICE WITH  MUSHROOMS
Categories: Kaz
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Uncle Ben's rice
2 c  Chicken consomme (canned -
Do not dilute)
1 c  White wine
1 ts Dried minced onion
1    Stick margarine (cut in
Pieces)
1 cn (4 oz.) mushrooms -- drained
And cut in p
1/4 ts Black pepper
Mix all the above ingredients in an oven-proof Pyrex dish. Cover with
aluminum foil.  Cook in a 350 degree oven for one hour. Serves 6
people.
