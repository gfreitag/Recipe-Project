Title: DUTCH SLAW
Categories: Penndutch, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1 x  Cabbage
1 ea Egg
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
Shred cabbage rather fine. Put in sauce pan and sprinkle with salt.
Cover pan and place over a low flame and steam until very tender.
Beat the egg, add the sugar, salt, pepper and vinegar and pour over
the steamed cabbage. Heat for 5 minutes. Serve at once. Source:
Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press,
1936.
