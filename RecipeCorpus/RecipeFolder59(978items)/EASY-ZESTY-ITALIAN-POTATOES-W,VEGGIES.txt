Title: EASY ZESTY ITALIAN POTATOES W/VEGGIES
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 10 Servings
8 lg Potatoes
3    Carrots
3    Celery stalks
1    Onion
1/2 c  Kraft Zesty Italian Dressing
1 tb Butter
1 1/2 c  Tomato sauce*
Salt
Pepper
Cut potatoes into 1 inch chunks. Cut celery, carrots and onions into
chunks. Place in a baking dish.  Pour dressing over top add salt and
pepper and butter. Make sure there is enough dressing to cover the
bottom of the dish.  Stir.  Bake in 350Ã¸F oven for about 1 1/2 hours.
Stir around about every 10 - 20 minutes. After cooking for approx. 50
minutes add tomato sauce. Stir and continue cooking for another 20
minutes. *You can add whatever vegetables you want and can also leave
out the tomato sauce if desired.  Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Cook for A Cure Cookbook
