Title: EASTERN NORTH CAROLINA-STYLE BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  White vinegar
1 c  Cider vinegar
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Crushed red pepper flakes
1 tb Tabasco sauce
Salt - to taste
Freshly cracked black pepper
To taste
Mix all ingredients together. Store in a covered jar in the
refrigerator. Will keep for 2 months. Source: The Thrill of the Grill
by Chris Schlesinger
