Title: EASY BLENDER POTATO KUGEL
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
5    Med Potato; cubed
1    Lg Onion; diced
3    Egg
1 1/2 ts Salt ;(optional)
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/4 c  Margarine; melted
1/3 c  Flour
Blend all ingredients until potatoes are finely ground, or approx. 2
min. Pour into pre-heated, lightely-oiled 9" round or loaf pan and
bake at 350 or until golden brown, about 1-1/2-2 hrs. When doubling
recipe use 9"x13" pan.
