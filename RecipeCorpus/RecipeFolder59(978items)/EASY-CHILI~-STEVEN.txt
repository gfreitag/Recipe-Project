Title: EASY CHILI~ STEVEN
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 2 Servings
1 cn Kidney beans
1/2 lb Hamburger
1 cn Tomato soup
1 tb Chili powder
1 sm Onion, chopped
Dump all into a pot.  Bring to a boil.  Turn down and simmer about 30
minutes. Son Steven brought this home from a boy scout campout many
years ago. We have been using it ever since then. We prefer the dark
red kidney beans. You can double or triple amounts.
