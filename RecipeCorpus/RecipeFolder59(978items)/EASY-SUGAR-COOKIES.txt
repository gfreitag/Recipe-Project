Title: EASY SUGAR COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 36 Cookies
1 c  Butter
1 c  Oil
1 c  Sugar, confectioners
1 c  Sugar, granulated
2    Eggs
4 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Lemon extract
1 ts Salt
1 ts Soda
1 ts Cream of tartar
Cream butter, oil, sugars, and eggs. Add sifted dry ingredients. Mix
until well blended. Drop the dough by teaspoonfuls into a dish of
granulated sugar and roll into balls. Place on an ungreased cookie
sheet. Bake at 375 F. for eight to ten minutes.
