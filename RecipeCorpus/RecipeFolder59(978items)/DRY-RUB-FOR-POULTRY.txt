Title: DRY RUB FOR POULTRY
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Basic Dry Rub (see recipe)
1 ts Dry mustard
1    Bay leaf -- crumble
=OR=-
1 ts Leaf sage or rosemary
Leaves
1    Garlic clove -- minced
1 ts Grated lemon peel
Mix fresh just before using. Rub on chicken or turkey before
barbecuing. Makes enough for 6 to 8 pounds chicken or 2 large
turkeys. From: Barbecued Ribs, Smoked Butts and Other Great Feeds by
Jeanne Voltz
