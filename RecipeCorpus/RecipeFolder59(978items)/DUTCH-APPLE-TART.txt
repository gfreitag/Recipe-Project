Title: DUTCH APPLE TART
Categories: Penndutch, Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 x  *pastry
5 ea Apple
1 tb Flour
1 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Nutmeg
2 ts Butter
Line a deep dish with pastry. Sprinkle the flour and cup of the sugar
on bottom of the crust. Peel and quarter the apples and place cut
side down. Cover with the balance of the sugar, dot with the butter
and sprinkle with the nutmeg. Bake at 350-F about 35 minutes or until
apples are baked and rich syrup has formed. Source: Pennsylvania
Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
