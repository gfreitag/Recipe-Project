Title: EASY BEEF BRISKET
Categories: Main dish, Crockpot, Meat
Yield: 8 Servings
3 lb Beef brisket
1 cn Onion soup
1/2 c  Catsup
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ea Jar carrot baby food
Place brisket in crockpot, mix all other ingredients and pour over
brisket. Cook on low 8 - 10 hours. Tastes best the next day when the
liquid has been absorbed by the meat.
