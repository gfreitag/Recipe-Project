Title: EASY HAZELNUT CANDY SQUARES
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 Batch
1 lb Coating chocolate
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Coconut
3/4 c  Roasted & chopped hazelnuts
-(Oregon hazelnuts)
3/4 c  Toasted rice cereal
Melt chocolate on the top of a double boiler over hot tap water. (Not
on the stove!) Add remaining ingredients and stir to mix well. Pour
into a 9x13-inch metal pan that has no coating in the pan. Chill
until partially set and then score into small squares. Return to
refrigerator until hard. Take out of the pan and break into scored
pieces.
