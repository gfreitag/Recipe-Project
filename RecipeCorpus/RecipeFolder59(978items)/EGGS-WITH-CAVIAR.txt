Title: EGGS WITH CAVIAR
Categories: Side dish, Appetizers, Christmas
Yield: 12 Servings
12    Hard-boiled eggs
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
Salt, pepper
1    2 oz. jar caviar
Cut eggs in half, scoop our yolks of each into bowl. Mash together egg
yolks and mayonnaise, season with salt and pepper. Place a heaped
teaspoonful of egg yolk mixture back into egg halves. Put a small
teaspoonful of caviar over egg mixture.  If desired, red and black
caviar can be used for colour contrast. Source: Dinner Party Cookbook
ch.
