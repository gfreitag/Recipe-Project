Title: EGGPLANT-STUFFED CREPES
Categories: Vegetarian, Crepes, Main dish
Yield: 8 Servings
4 tb Onion; chopped
4 c  Eggplant; diced, cooked
4 c  Tomatoes, fresh; chopped
1 c  Vegetable broth
4 tb Curry powder
1 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Salt
8    Garlic cloves; chopped
24    Crepes
Saute all the ingredients, except the crepes, in a large skillet over
medium heat for 10 minutes. Divide the mixture evenly between the
crepes. Roll up and serve hot. Top with Greek Tomato Sauce.

Makes 24 crepes
