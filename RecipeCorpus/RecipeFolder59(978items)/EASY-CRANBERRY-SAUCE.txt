Title: EASY CRANBERRY SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 24 Servings  14 oz MARIE'S Glaze for Strawberries
12 oz fresh or frozen cranberries
1/2 c  orange juice
1 tb sugar  (optional)

In 2-quart saucepan, combine glaze, berries and juice. Over
medium-high heat, heat to boiling. Reduce heat to low. Cook 10
minutes or until berries are soft, stirring often.

Remove from heat. Pour into bowl. Cover; refrigerate at least 2 hours
before serving. (For sweeter sauce, stir in sugar.) Serve with cooked
pork, ham or poultry. Refrigerate any remaining sauce. Makes about
3    cups sauce.
