Title: EGGLESS CEASAR SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Sauces, Dressings, Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Cloves of garlic
1 tb Oil
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Anchovies
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Dyjon mustard
1/4 c  Yogart
Parmasan cheese
Olive oil
Mix all ingredients to-gether except oil, blend in oil very slowly.
Refrigerate until ready to use.
