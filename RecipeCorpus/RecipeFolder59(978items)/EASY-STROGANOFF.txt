Title: EASY STROGANOFF
Categories: German, S_living, Beef, Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion -- chopped
2 tb All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 oz Can sliced mushrooms
10 3/4 oz Can Cream of Mushroom soup
Undiluted
8 oz Sour cream
Hot cooked noodles
Cook ground beef and onion in a large skillet until meat is browned,
stirring to crumble meat; drain. Stir in flour and next 3
ingredients; cook and stir 1 minute. Stir in soup. Simmer 10 minutes,
stirring occasionally. Add sour cream, and heat thoroughly. Serve
over noodles. Yield: 4 servings.
