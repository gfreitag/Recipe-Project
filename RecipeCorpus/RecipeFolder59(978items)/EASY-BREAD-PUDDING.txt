Title: EASY BREAD PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 6 Servings
8 sl Bread -- cubed
4    Eggs -- beaten
1 c  Sugar
4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Raisins
Place bread cubes in greased 2-quart baking dish. Beat eggs, sugar,
milk, and vanilla in small bowl. Stir in raisins. Pour over bread
cubes. Sprinkle with sugar and nutmeg. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to
40 minutes or until golden brown.
