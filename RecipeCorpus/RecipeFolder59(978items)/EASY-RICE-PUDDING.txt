Title: EASY RICE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 c  Milk
1 pk Vanilla pud. mix
1/2 c  Quick cooking rice
1/3 c  Raisins (optional)
In a 4 c. glass measuring cup combine all ingredients, microwave on
high 6 min. stirring occasionally. Cover with plastic wrap and let
stand until mixture thickens. Serve cold or reheat at serving time.
