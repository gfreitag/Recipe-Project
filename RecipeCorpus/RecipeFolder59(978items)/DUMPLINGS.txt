Title: DUMPLINGS
Categories: Stews
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
3 ts Baking powder
2 tb Shortening
1 c  Milk
Sift to-gether flour, salt and baking powder.Cut in shortening .
Gradually stir in milk and mix till smooth. Drop by tblsp. on hot
stew, cover tightly and cook 10-12 min. without lifting cover.
