Title: EGG SUBSTITUTE
Categories: Misc
Yield: 1 Recipe
1    Egg white
2 1/4 ts Nonfat milk powder
2 ts Safflower oil
Combine ingredients in a blender,; whirl.  Yield is the equivalent of
1 egg.

Note: May be frozen.  Will keep in the refrigerator for 1 week.

Recipe may be multiplied.  Can be used in recipes calling for regular
whole eggs. Submitted By WA6AWD@AOL.COM On THU, 9 NOV 1995
