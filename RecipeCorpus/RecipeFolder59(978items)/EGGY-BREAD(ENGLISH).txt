Title: EGGY BREAD(ENGLISH)
Categories: English
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Thick slices white bread
3    Eggs
2 tb Milk (optional)
Tomato or brown
Sauce, to taste
Salt and pepper
Butter for frying
Whisk together the eggs, milk (if required), sauce, salt and pepper.
Cut the slices of bread into quarters. Soak each piece of bread in
the egg mixture and coat well. Heat a little butter in a large frying
pan, and fry the soaked bread until golden brown, turning over once.
Any spare egg mixture should be poured over the bread as it is fried.
Serve with crisply fried bacon for breakfast, or as a snack.
