Title: EGG FOO YONG
Categories: Oriental
Yield: 4 Servings
3 ea Egg
1 c  Bean sprout
1/2 c  Pork, cooked
2 tb Peanut oil
2 tb Onions, chopped
1 tb Soy sauce
Beat eggs until thick; stir in sprouts, pork, onion, and soy sauce.
Heat oil in skillet and pour in some of mixture at a time. Using
spatula push eggs as they cool over meat to form pattie. When pattie
is firm, turn over to brown other side. Serve with sauce.
