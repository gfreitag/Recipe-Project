Title: EDIE'S STEAK SOUP (M_C-TX)
Categories: Beef, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Ground chuck
1    Stick oleo or margarine
1/2 c  Flour
2    Carrots, diced
1 md To large onion, diced
2    Celery stalks, diced
2 md Potatoes, diced
1 pk Frozen mixed vegetables
1 cn Tomatoes (15-20 oz.)
4    Beef bouillon cubes
Salt, pepper and garlic
-salt or garlic to taste
In dutch oven size pan melt margarine. Add flour and stir until
smooth. Add 2 cups water, stir until smooth. Add 3 cups more water,
carrots, celery,potatoes, mixed vegetables, tomatoes, bouillon cubes,
salt, pepper and garlic. In separate pan brown meat and cook onion.
Drain. Add to dutch oven. Simmer
1 1/2    hours.  Freezes well.
