Title: EGGNOG ICE CREAM
Categories: Christmas, Desserts, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 10 Servings
2 c  Milk
2 c  Cream
2 ts Vanilla essence
7    Large egg yolks
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Freshly grated nutmeg
2 tb Rum
2 tb Brandy
Extra nutmeg
1 c  Combined glacÂ‚ fruits,
Almonds and chocolate,
Finely chopped
In a large saucepan combine the milk, cream and vanilla essence and
bring to the boil.  Whisk together the egg yolks and sugar. Add the
cream mixture in a stream while whisking, then transfer the custard
to a saucepan. Cook the custard over moderate heat, stirring
constantly with a wooden spoon until it coats the back of the spoon.
Strain through a fine sieve. Allow the custard to cool and stir in
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg, brandy and rum, then chill the custard, covered,
overnight. Stir in the additional nutmeg and freeze the custard in an
ice-cream maker, according to the manufacturer's instructions. Add
combined fruit, almonds and chocolate, then freeze until firm.
