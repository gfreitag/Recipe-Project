Title: EGGLESS FRENCH TOAST #2
Categories: Frenchtoast, Breakfast, Brunch, Diabetic
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Skim milk
1 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Vanilla or other extract
-of your choice
2 sl Whole wheat bread/1 oz.slice
2 ts Reduced-calorie margarine
SOURCE:  Lean and Lucsious and Meatless, volume 3 in the series, by
Bobbie Hinman and Millie Snyder, copyright 1992, ISBN #1-55958-110-7.
MM format by Ursula R. Taylor.
Combine milk, cornstarch and vanilla in a small bowl and stir until
conrstarch is dissolved.  In a shallow pan - place bread slices and
pour milk mixtures over the bread.
In a preheated nonstick skillet or griddle (on medium heat) - melt
1 tsp. of the nargarine.  Then place bread in skillet or griddle and
brown lightly. Turn bread over, adding remaining margarine and brown
on the other side.  Serve hot.
Sprinkle with cinnamon or maple syrup of topping of your choice.
NUTRITIONAL INFO per serving:  130 calories, 5 g protein, 3 g fat,
20 g carbohydrate, 258 mg sodium, 2 mg cholesterol. EXCHANGES: 1/2
fat serving, 1 bread serving, 1/4 milk serving, + 15 additional
calories.
