Title: EASY-MADE BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Catsup
1 c  Water
1/2 ea Onion, finely chopped or
-grated
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
2 tb Light brown sugar
1 ts Paprika
3/4 ts Liquid smoke, or to taste
1/8 ts Celery seed
8 dr Tabasco sauce or hot pepper
Sauce
Combine ingredients in saucepan.  Bring to a simmering stage. Simmer
about 15 minutes to blend flavors and thicken slightly.

Source:  National Pork Producers Council
