Title: EGGLESS SWEET MATZO BRIE
Categories: Passover, Low-fat
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Not Milk (see beverages)
2    Ripe Bannanas ; peeled
1/4 ts Cinnamon
8    Matzo Pieces
2 ts Oil
Place Not milk, bannanas and cinnamon in a blender cup and blend for
one minute. Break matzo into 3" x 3" pieces and soak matzo in liquid
mixture for three minutes. Fry soaked matzo in oiled frying pan over
medium heat until brown on each side. Serve warm.
