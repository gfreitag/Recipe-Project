Title: DURGIN-PARK BLUEBERRY CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 6 Servings
-CYGNUS, HCPM52C
2 c  Flour
4 ts Baking powder
3/4 ts Salt
2    Eggs; beaten
3/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Butter, melted
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 1/4 c  Blueberries
Extra flour
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Shift flour, baking powder, and salt
together, blend eggs with sugar, then combine with butter and dry
ingredients.  Stir in milk to make thick batter. Dust berries with
extra flour and add to the batter, mixing well. Spoon into a greaserd
9x14x3 baking dish.  Bake for about 30 minutes

Source:  The Colorado Daily, January 20, 1993 issue
