Title: EGG FOO YOUNG
Categories: China, Seafood, Cheese/eggs, Formatted
Yield: 3 Servings
6    Eggs; beaten
1 tb Soy sauce
1 c  Bean sprouts
1/8 ts Pepper, white
1 tb Oil, vegetable
1 ts Salt
2    Scallions; thinly sliced
Oil, vegetable; for frying
1 md Onions; minced
1/2 lb Shrimp; finely diced
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Sherry, dry
1 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Cornstarch
Rinse bean sprouts in cold water. Heat oil in pan and add scallions,
onion & sprouts. Stir fry 1". Add cornstarch, soy sauce, shrimp, salt
& pepper. Mix well. Remove to dish & allow to cool. Combine with eggs.
Heat 2 T oil in pan & ladle in 1/6 of egg mix. Fry til golden on
both sides. Keep pancakes warm in low oven, while making sauce.
Combine sauce ingredients in small pan, bring to boil & simmer gently
til thickened. Makes 6 pancakes.
