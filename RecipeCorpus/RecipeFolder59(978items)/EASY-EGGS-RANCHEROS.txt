Title: EASY EGGS RANCHEROS
Categories: Breakfast, Brunch, Eggs
Yield: 4 Servings
-Sue Woodward
1 cn 8-oz. tomato sauce
1 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Ground cumin
8    Eggs
1/4 ts Pepper; freshly ground
1/4 ts Paprika
1 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
-(4-oz)
4    Tostada shells
Sour cream
Jalapeno pepper slices; opt
Combine tomato sauce, chili powder, and cumin in a lightly greased 9"
quiche dish.  Break eggs over sauce, arranging evenly in dish.
Sprinkle with pepper and paprika. Cover and bake at 350~ for 15 to 20
mins. Sprinkle with cheese and bake an additional 5 mins. or to
desired degree of doneness.  Spoon eggs onto tostada shells; top
w/dollop of sour cream. Garnish w/jalapeno pepper slices, if desired.

Source:  Mrs. John R. Allen of Dallas, TX (Southern Living, 4/88)
