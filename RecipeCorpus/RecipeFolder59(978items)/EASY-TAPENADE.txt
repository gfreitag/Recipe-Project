Title: EASY TAPENADE
Categories: Misc
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Chopped parsley
1/2 c  Pitted nicoise olives
-=OR=- Calamata olives
8    Anchovies
1 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Olive oil
This is a fast, simple version of this classic olive relish. COMBINE
ALL INGREDIENTS in a food processor and process until a paste forms.
Makes 1/2 Cup
