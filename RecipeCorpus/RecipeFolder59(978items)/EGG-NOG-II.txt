Title: EGG NOG II
Categories: Beverages, Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
3    Eggs - beat until light
-and fluffy
MMMMM-----------------------BEAT IN SLOWLY----------------------------
1/4 c  Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
MMMMM--------------------------BEAT IN-------------------------------
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
MMMMM---------------------STIR IN MIXTURE OF--------------------------
2 c  Evaporated milk
1 c  Water
-(or 3 cups of milk)
1/2 ts Nutmeg
Chill well
