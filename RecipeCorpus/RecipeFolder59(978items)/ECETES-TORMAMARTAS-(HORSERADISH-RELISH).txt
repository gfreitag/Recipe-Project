Title: ECETES TORMAMARTAS (HORSERADISH RELISH)
Categories: Sauce/gravy, Relishes, Hungary, Upload
Yield: 1 Cup
1 ea Horseradish root; peeled,
-grated
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar, granulated
1 ts Vinegar, cider
2 tb Sour cream
Mix grated horseradish with salt, sugar, vinegar and sour cream.
