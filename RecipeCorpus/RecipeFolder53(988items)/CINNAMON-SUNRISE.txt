Title: CINNAMON SUNRISE
Categories: Abm, Bread, Posted-mm
Yield: 16 Servings
MMMMM------------------INGREDIENTS - SMALL LOAF-----------------------
3/4 c  Water
2 1/4 c  Better for Bread flour
1 tb Dry milk Powder
2 1/2 tb Brown sugar
3/4 ts Salt
2 tb Butter or Margarine, small
Pieces
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/3 c  Pecans, chopped
1 ts Yeast, fast rise
--- OR ---
1 1/4 ts Active dry yeast
MMMMM------------------INGREDIENTS - LARGE LOAF-----------------------
1 1/8 c  Water
3 1/3 c  Better for Bread flour
1 1/2 tb Dry milk powder
3 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Butter or margarine, small
Pieces
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
1 1/2 ts Yeast, fast rise
=OR=
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Place in pan in order recommended by breadmachine manufacturer. Use
RapidBake cycle or standard.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION: Calories: 217 Protein 11%, Cholesterol 8 mg
Carbohydrate 59%, Sodium 240 mg, Fat 30% (per 2 oz serving -
equivalent 2 Breads Diabetic Diet)
