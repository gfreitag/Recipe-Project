Title: CINNAMON ORNAMENTS #1
Categories: Christmas, Nonfood
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4    -1 C applesauce
4 1/8 oz Bottle ground cinnamon
Mix 3/4-1 C applesauce with 4.12-oz bottle ground cinnamon to form
stiff dough. Roll to 1/4". Cut with cookie cutters. Make hole for
ribbon. Let dry several days, turning occasionally.
