Title: CIRCLE J BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1 sm Onion -- minced
3/4 ts Prepared mustard
1 tb Grated fresh horseradish
1 tb Mixed thyme, marjoram,
Parsley
2 tb Vinegar
3 c  Water
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2/3 c  Butter
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 ts Juice from bottled tabasco
Peppers
2 ts Sugar
3/4 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Black pepper
3/4 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients and cook slowly for 45 minutes. Use to baste
meat or fish while cooking, or dip slices or chunks of hot cooked
meet into the heated sauce before serving.
