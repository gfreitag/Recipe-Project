Title: CINDY'S CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIE
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
1    Stick of butter
6 tb Brown sugar
6 tb White sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
1    Egg
3/4 c  Flour
1 c  Chocolate chips
1 c  Oats
1/2 ts Salt
Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes. Can substitute 3 bags of instant
oatmeal instead of oats and salt.
