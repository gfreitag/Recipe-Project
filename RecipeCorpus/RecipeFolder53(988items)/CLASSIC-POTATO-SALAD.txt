Title: CLASSIC POTATO SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  BEST FOODS Real Mayonnaise
2 tb Vinegar
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
4 c  Potatoes (5 to 6 medium)
-cooked, cubed, peeled
1 c  Sliced celery
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2    Hard-cooked eggs, chopped
Combine mayonnaise, vinegar, salt, sugar and pepper. Stir in remaining
ingredients.  Cover; chill.  Makes 5 cups.

On back of Best Foods Mayonnaise jar.
