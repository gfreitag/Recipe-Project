Title: CINNAMON ROLLS+++FGGT98B
Categories: Master mix, Rolls, Jewel
Yield: 12 Servings
1    Recipe beaten batter rolls
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
When Beaten Batter is light, stir in raisins. Drop by spoonfuls into
muffin cups, rubbed with shortening. Combine sugar and cinnamon.
Sprinkle over rolls.  Let rise in a warm place until double in size,
about 30 to 40 minutes.  Bake in a 375 F. oven 15 to 18 minutes.
Remove rolls from pan while hot.  Serve warm. YIELD: 12 large
