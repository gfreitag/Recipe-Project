Title: CHUNKY APPLE SPICE MUFFINS
Categories: Bakery, Muffins, Vegatarian
Yield: 12 Muffins
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Oat bran
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Allspice
2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Ginger
1 ts Arrowroot
1/4 c  Liquid sweetener
1/2 c  Unsweetened applesauce
2 ea Tart apples, peeled & diced
1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Preheat the oven 400F.

Mix the dry ingredients and wet ingredients separately. Combine them
and mix thoroughly.  Spoon into nonstick muffin tins and bake for
about 25 mins, until golden brown on top. "The McDougall Plan" by
