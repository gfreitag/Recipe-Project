Title: CHOKECHERRY SYRUP #1
Categories: Syrup, Chokecherry
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Chokecherry juice
1 c  Sugar
Bring to a rolling boil for about 15 minutes.  Put in hot, sterilized
jars. Seal with two-piece lids and process 8 minutes in water bath.
