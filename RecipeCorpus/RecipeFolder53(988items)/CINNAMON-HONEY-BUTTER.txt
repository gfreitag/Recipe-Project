Title: CINNAMON HONEY BUTTER
Categories: Spreads
Yield: 10 Servings
2    Sticks (quarter lb) butter
PLUS
3 tb Butter (at room temperature)
3/4 c  Margerine
1/3 c  Honey
2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Soft-type Brown Sugar
Mix all ingredients until blended, using a mixer. Makes 3 half-pound
units of spread which can be frozen if necessary. Use as spread on
coffee cakes or on just plain toast!
