Title: CLASSIC HERBED CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Quick, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk LIPTON Recipe Secrets Savory
-Herb with Garlic Soup Mix
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb ;Water
4 ea Boneless, skinless chicken
-breast halves (about 1 lb.)
x  Nonstick cooking spray
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.

In small bowl, blend savory herb with garlic soup mix, mayonnaise and
water.  In 13- x 9-inch baking or roasting pan sprayed with nonstick
cooking spray, arrange chicken; brush with 1/2 of the soup mixture.
Bake uncovered, turning once and brushing with remaining soup
mixture, 25 minutes or until chicken is done. Makes about 4 servings.

(Also terrific with Lipton Recipe Secrets Golden Herb with Lemon or
Fiesta Herb with Red Pepper Soup Mix. Taste tested from The Lipton
Kitchens.)

Source:  Lipton advertisement in Sunday coupon circular, Oct. 1995.
