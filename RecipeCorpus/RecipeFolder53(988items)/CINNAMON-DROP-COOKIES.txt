Title: CINNAMON DROP COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Raisins
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Shortening; scant
3 ea Eggs
1 c  Walnuts; english
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Cinnamon
3 tb Raisin juice
3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
Cook and cool raisins. Mix and drop by spoonful on baking sheet. Bake
in moderately hot oven for 15 minutes.

Source: Mrs. Flora Hall, Knox County, OH
