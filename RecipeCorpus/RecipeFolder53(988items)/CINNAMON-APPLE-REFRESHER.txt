Title: CINNAMON APPLE REFRESHER
Categories: Beverages, Tea
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Boiling water
5    Cinnamon-apple herbal tea
-bags
1 1/3 c  Cranberry juice
2 tb Sugar
Apple slices (opt.)
In a teapot, pour boiling water over tea bags; cover and steep for 5
minutes.  Remove bags.  Stir in cranberry juice and sugar. Pour into
glasses. Garnish with apple slices if desired. Source: Taste of Home
mag, June/July 1996
