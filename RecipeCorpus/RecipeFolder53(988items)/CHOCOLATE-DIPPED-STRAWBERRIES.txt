Title: CHOCOLATE-DIPPED STRAWBERRIES
Categories: Fruits/nuts, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
12 oz Semisweet chocolate pieces
6 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Vanilla
Strawberries with stems
Melt chocolate pieces in top of double boiler. Blend in butter and
vanilla. Dip strawberries in chocolate, swirling to coat evenly.
Place on waxed paper and set in cool dry place.
