Title: CILANTRO RICE
Categories: Rice, Salad
Yield: 8 Servings
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 c  Cooked long-grain white rice
Room temp
3/4 c  Each green/red bell peppers
Chopped
1/3 c  Green onions -- thinly
Sliced
1/4 c  Fresh cilantro -- minced
1. Whisk the oil, lime juice, salt and pepper in a bowl until well
blended. 2. Add remaining ingredients. Toss to coat with dressing.

Makes 5 cups or 8 servings.
