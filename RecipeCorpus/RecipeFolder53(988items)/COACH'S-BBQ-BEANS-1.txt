Title: COACH'S BBQ BEANS 1
Categories: Meat, Miscellaneo, Pulses and, Vegetables
Yield: 30 Servings
1 ga Baked beans (Allen's)
1 ea Green pepper -- diced
1 ea Onion -- diced
1    Bottle liquid Barbecue Smoke
1 c  Mustard
1 c  Cookie's BBQ Sauce
5 tb BBQ Spice
1/2 c  Maple syrup (Pure)
Mix all ingrediets well and cook slowly for 4 to 5 hours over a slow
heat
