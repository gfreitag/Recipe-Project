Title: CHOCOLATE-BUTTERSCOTCH HAYSTACKS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts, Jvn
Yield: 60 Servings
2 c  Butterscotch chips
2 c  Chocolate chips
12 oz Chow mein noodles
Melt chips.  Add in noodles.  Drop onto waxed paper and chill until
firm.
