Title: CHOKECHERRY SYRUP WITH PECTIN
Categories: Syrup, Chokecherry
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Chokecherry juice
1 pk Commercial pectin
1 c  White corn syrup
3 c  Sugar
Dissolve the pectin in choke cherry juice.  Add corn syrup and bring
to a boil.  Add sugar and boil 2 minutes. Put into hot sterilized
jars, seal and process 8 minutes in water bath.
