Title: CINNAMON CANDY
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Butter or butter substitute
1/2 c  Corn sirup
1 tb Cinnamon
1/2 c  Water
Combine ingredients.  Boil to soft crack stage (275 - 280 F). Pour
into well-buttered, shallow pan.  When cool, cut in squares. Jennie
M. Cable, Columbia, MO.
