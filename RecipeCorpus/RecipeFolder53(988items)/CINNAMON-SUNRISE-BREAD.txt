Title: CINNAMON SUNRISE BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM------------------------REGULAR LOAF-----------------------------
3/4 c  Water
2 1/4 c  White bread flour
1 tb Dry milk
2 1/2 tb Brown sugar
3/4 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/3 c  Pecans, chopped
1 ts Yeast, fast rise **OR**
1 1/4 ts Yeast, active dry
MMMMM-------------------------LARGE LOAF------------------------------
1 1/8 c  Water
3 1/3 c  White bread flour
1 1/2 tb Dry milk
3 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Butter
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
1 1/2 ts Yeast, fast rise **OR**
2 ts Yeast, active dry
+++ servings are based on 8 for regular loaf, 12 for large loaf. This
recipe can be baked with the regular, rapid or delayed time bake
cycles. Be sure to follow the instructions for your particular brand
of bread baking machine. CALORIES per 2 oz serving : 217 PROTEIN: 11%
