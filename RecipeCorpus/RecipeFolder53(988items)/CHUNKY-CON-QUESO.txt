Title: CHUNKY CON QUESO
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 lb Hamburger
1 md Onion, chopped
1 tb Garlic powder
1 cn Rotel Tomatoes, drained
1 cn Tomatoes, chopped, drained
1 sm Mexican Velveta Cheese (box)
1 sm Velveeta Cheese, regular box
Corn Chips
In a skillet, brown hamburger, onion and garlic powder. Add tomatoes
and cheese.  Pour all into a crock pot. Serve with corn chips.
