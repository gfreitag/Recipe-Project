Title: COCA COLA CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
3 lb Cut Up Chicken
3/4 c  Coca Cola
1 c  Catsup
Place cut up chicked in an UNgreased skillet. Mix 3/4 cup of COKE and
1 cup of Heinz catsup together and pour over the chicken. Bring to a
boil. Turn the burner down, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes. Uncover
and cook 1 hour or until tender.

(This tastes just like barq chicken) DO NOT SALT.
