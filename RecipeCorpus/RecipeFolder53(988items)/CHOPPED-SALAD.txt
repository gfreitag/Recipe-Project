Title: CHOPPED SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Onion -- chopped
1 c  Tomato -- chopped
1 c  Cucumber -- chopped
1 c  Red and green bell peppers
Chopped
1 c  Tart apple -- chopped
3 1/2 tb Lemon juice
1/3 c  Olive oil
Salt and pepper
Mix chopped vegetables and apple together in mixing bowl. Combine
remaining ingredients: pour over vegetables and apple. Toss.
Refrigerate to allow to blend.
