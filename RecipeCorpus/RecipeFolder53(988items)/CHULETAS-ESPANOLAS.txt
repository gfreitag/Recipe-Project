Title: CHULETAS ESPANOLAS
Categories: Chiles, Mexican, Meats, Pork, New mex
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Pork chops
Salt and pepper
To taste
Red chile sauce
1 ts Onion salt
1 ts Cumin
1 ts Oregano
2 cl Garlic, sliced
Season chops with salt and pepper.  Brown chops in small amount of
fat. Add seasoning to red chile sauce.  Simmer meat in sauce for 1 -
2 hours or until very tender. Serve with rice. Makes 6 servings.
