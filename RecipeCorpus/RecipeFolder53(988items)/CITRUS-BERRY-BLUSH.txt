Title: CITRUS BERRY BLUSH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 8 Servings
2 pt STRAWBERRIES
4 c  VANILLA ICE CREAM
2 ea LEMON PEELS, GRATED
1 c  FRESH SQUEEZED LEMON JUICE
1 c  FRESH SQUEEZED ORANGE JUICE
2/3 c  SUGAR
2 c  CRUSHED ICE
1 ea LEMON, SLICED INTO RINGS
PUREE THE BERRIES IN A BLENDER TO YIELD ABOUT 2 CUPS. IN THE BLENDER,
COMBINE STRAWBERRIES PUREE AND REMAINING INGREDIENTS EXCEPT FOR THE
LEMON RINGS. BLEND UNTIL SMOOTH. POUR INTO GLASSES AND GARNISH WITH
LEMON RINGS. MAKES 8 - FOUR ONCE SERVINGS.
