Title: CHUTNEY BARBECUE GLAZE
Categories: Bbq sauces, Chutneys, Other sauce
Yield: 3 Cups
1 ea Crushed pineapple, 20 oz can
1/2 c  Chutney, cut-up
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Butter
1 ts Salt
1 ts Ginger, ground
In a saucepan combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil; reduce heat
and simmer 15 minutes. Use to baste lamb, pork or ham the last 15
minutes of grilling. Pass remainder.
