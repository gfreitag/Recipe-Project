Title: Pineapple Mustard Horseradish Sauce
Categories: Sauces, Fruits
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 oz Dry mustard
1 ts Black pepper
10 oz Pineapple preserves
10 oz Apple jelly
6 oz Horseradish
Mix all ingredients and let sit in refrigerator for 1 hour. Hope you enjoy.
