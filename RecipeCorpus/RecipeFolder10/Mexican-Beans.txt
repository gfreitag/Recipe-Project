Title: Mexican Beans
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1    Clove Garlic
1 tb Red Chili Powder
1/4 ts Ground Cumin
1/2 ts Oregano
Meats should include only one of the following: Saltpork, Ham Hock,
2 c  Dry Pinto Beans
Water To More Than Cover
1/2 lb Meat *
1    Large Onion Chopped
*
