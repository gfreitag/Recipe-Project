Title: BRONWYN'S OATMEAL SLICE
Categories: Cookies, Usenet
Yield: 1 batch
1    Egg
1/2 lb Butter
1 T  Golden syrup
1 C  Flour, self-raising
1 C  Rolled oats
1 C  Coconut, desiccated
1 C  Sugar, raw
Combine flour, oats, coconut, sugar and egg.  Melt butter and add golden
syrup, then pour over combined ingredients and mix.  Spread over flat
baking dish (about 1/2-inch thick), and cook for 25-30 mins at 350 degrees
F. Slice when warm.

NOTES:

*  Baked oatmeal biscuits (cookies) -- This recipe comes from my wife's
sister-in-law's grand-mother (seriously).  Real good stuff.  Yield:  1
batch.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  10 minutes preparation, 30 minutes baking.
: Precision:  Measure the ingredients.
