Title: DILL DIP
Categories: Dips, Usenet
Yield: 1 batch
1 t  Onion, grated
1/4 t  Dill, dried
1 ds Salt
2 T  Light cream (or less)
3 oz Cream cheese
1 T  Green olive,
-pimento-stuffed
-(finely chopped)
Combine first 5 ingredients.  Stir in enough cream to make the mixture have
dipping consistency.  Chill.  Serve in a hollowed-out whole loaf of bread.
Use the discarded bread for dipping.

NOTES:

*  A cold dip -- I first discovered this dip at a reunion of old college
roommates.  It comes from the wilds of Farmington, Connecticut.  Yield:
Makes 2/3 cup of dip.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  3 minutes preparation, several hours chilling.
: Precision:  no need to measure.
