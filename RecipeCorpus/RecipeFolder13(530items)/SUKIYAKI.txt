Title: SUKIYAKI
Categories: Meats, Usenet
Yield: 4 servings
2    Tomatoes, peeled,
-sliced thinly
1/2 C  Soy sauce
4 C  Spinach leaves
-(fresh), whole
1 lb Sirloin steak,
-sliced very thinly
4 T  Oil
4 C  Celery or water
-chestnuts, sliced thinly
2    Onions (mild),
-sliced thinly
3 C  Mushrooms, sliced thinly
1 C  Bamboo shoots, diced
1 C  Beef broth
Heat oil in skillet.  Add all vegetables except spinach.  Cook a few
minutes, stirring occasionally. Pour broth and soy over vegetables. Stir.
Add spinach and beef. Simmer 5 minutes. Serve hot over rice.

NOTES:

*  An oriental-style cook-at-the-table dinner.

: Difficulty:  easy, but preparation tedious.
: Time:  45 minutes preparation, 10 minutes cooking.
: Precision:  no need to measure.
