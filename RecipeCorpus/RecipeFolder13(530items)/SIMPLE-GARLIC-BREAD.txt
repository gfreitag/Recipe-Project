Title: SIMPLE GARLIC BREAD
Categories: Quick bread, Usenet
Yield: 4 servings
1    Loaf Italian or
-French bread
Butter
Garlic salt
Cheese, grated
Preheat broiler.  Slice loaf of bread lengthwise and butter both halves.
Sprinkle garlic salt and grated cheese on both halves.  Place under broiler
until brown (about 3 minutes).

NOTES:

*  This is a quick and painless way to make garlic bread in a hurry.  It
might not be a gourmet recipe, but it is easy and tasty.

*  This also works well with almost any other kind of bread.

: Difficulty:  trivial.
: Time:  3 minutes preparation, 3 minutes cooking.
: Precision:  no need to measure.
