Title: CUCUMBER YOGURT SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Middle east, Usenet
Yield: 4 servings
2 t  Green chilis (hot),
-seeded and chopped
-(optional)
1/2 t  Garlic (finely chopped)
Salt, to taste (optional)
3/4 lb Cucumber (coarsely
-grated)
3/4 t  Cumin seeds, whole
1 C  Yogurt, plain
1 t  Vegetable oil
Toast cumin seeds in small, heavy skillet until browned.  Do not burn. When
they start to crackle, remove from heat, shaking skillet.  Combine all
ingredients.  Blend well and chill.

NOTES:

*  A Mideast-style sauce with cucumbers and yogurt -- This recipe came
originally from the local paper, years ago.  They liked it with lamb.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  5 minutes preparation, 1 hour chilling.
: Precision:  approximate measurement OK.
