Title: CAULIFLOWER AND CHEESE
Categories: Entrees, Usenet
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Butter
1/2 C  Bread crumbs
3/4 C  Parmesan cheese, grated
1    Cauliflower, cut
-into bite-sized pieces
2 md Onions, chopped
1 lb Mushrooms, fresh
Cut cauliflower into bite-sized pieces, place in a steamer and steam to
desired tenderness, about 10-15 minutes.

In a frying pan, saute onions and mushrooms in butter until brown (about 5
minutes).  Remove from heat and stir in the parmesan and bread crumbs.  Mix
well.

Put the steamed cauliflower in a baking dish and toss with the mixture in
the frying pan. Bake for 4-5 minutes at 350 degrees F.

NOTES:

*  Steamed cauliflower with mushrooms and parmesan.  Yield:  Serves 4-6.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  30 minutes.
: Precision:  no need to measure.
