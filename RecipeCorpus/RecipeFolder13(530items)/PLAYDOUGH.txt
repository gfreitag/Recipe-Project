Title: PLAYDOUGH
Categories: Fun, Kids, Usenet
Yield: 1 batch
2 C  Water
1 T  Mineral oil
Food coloring
2 C  Flour
1 C  Salt
2 T  Cream of tartar
Mix all ingredients in a saucepan.  Cook over medium heat, stirring until
stiff.  Allow to cool; knead.

NOTES:

*  Homemade playdough -- I got this from a friend of mine.  It even smells
like the real thing.  Yield:  3 Cups

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  10 minutes.
: Precision:  approximate measurement OK.
