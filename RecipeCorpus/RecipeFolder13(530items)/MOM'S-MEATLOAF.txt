Title: MOM'S MEATLOAF
Categories: Meats, Usenet
Yield: 6 servings
2    Eggs (slightly beaten)
2 T  Horseradish
1 pn Salt
1/4 C  Milk or water
1 T  Mustard, dry
3/4 C  Catsup
1 C  Bread crumbs
3/4 C  Onion, minced
1/4 t  Garlic powder
2 lb Meat, ground
-(I often use
-a combination of
-beef and lamb)
Combine all ingredients except for about 1/2 cup of the catsup.  Place into
a loaf pan, or shape into a loaf on a baking sheet. Spread the reserved
catsup on top of the loaf. Bake at 400 degrees F. for 40 minutes.

NOTES:

*  A nice, simple meatloaf -- This is the meatloaf my mother makes.  Where
she got the recipe, who knows? Probably from her mother.... I think it's
the best meatloaf in the world (and I'm sure you think your meatloaf is the
best!).  Yield:  Serves 6.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  10 minutes preparation, 45 minutes cooking and cooling.
: Precision:  approximate measurement OK.
