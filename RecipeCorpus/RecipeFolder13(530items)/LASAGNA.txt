Title: LASAGNA
Categories: Pasta, Freezer, Usenet
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 t  Salt
2 t  Oregano
1/4 t  Pepper
1 lb Mozzarella cheese,
-shredded
1    Garlic clove
3 C  Cottage cheese
2    Eggs, beaten
1/2 C  Parmesan cheese
2 T  Parsley
1 lb Italian sausage
1 lb Beef, ground
2    Celery stalks,
-chopped
2    Carrots, chopped
1 lg Onion, chopped
2 lb Italian tomatoes,
-canned
12 oz Tomato paste
10    Lasagna noodles,
-cooked
Combine meat, celery, carrots, onion, garlic; cook till soft.    Add
tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, oregano and pepper. Simmer.

Mix together the cottage cheese, eggs, parmesan cheese and parsley.

Layer meat sauce, cheese mixture, mozzarella cheese and cooked lasagna
noodles in a baking dish. Bake at 375 degrees F. for 30 minutes. 10 minutes
before it is done, top with additional cheese.

NOTES:

*  Simple cheese-and-tomato lasagna -- This is Lasagna the way my mother
makes it. Yield: Serves 6-8.

*  Freezes well.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  30 minutes preparation, 30 minutes baking.
: Precision:  approximate measurement OK.
