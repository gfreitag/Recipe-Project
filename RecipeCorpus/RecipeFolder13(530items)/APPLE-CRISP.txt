Title: APPLE CRISP
Categories: Dessert, Usenet
Yield: 6 servings
1    Egg
1/3 C  Solid shortening
-(e.g. Crisco)
1/2 t  Cinnamon
6 C  Apple slices (or less)
3/4 C  Sugar
1 C  Flour
1 t  Baking powder
3/4 t  Salt
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.  Fill a greased baking dish
(approximately 6x10 inches) with the apple slices.

Mix together with fork until crumbly the sugar, flour, baking powder, salt
and egg. Sprinkle over apples.

Melt the shortening, cool slightly and pour over apple-flour mixture.
Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Bake 30-40 minutes at 350 degrees F.  Serve with
vanilla ice cream.

NOTES:

*  Easy baked apple dessert -- I got this recipe from Marsha Foregger. It's
very easy and makes a yummy dessert.  Yield:  serves 6 to 8.

*  I usually sprinkle some raisins and walnut halves in among the apple
slices.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  10 minutes preparation, 40 minutes baking.
: Precision:  measure the ingredients.
