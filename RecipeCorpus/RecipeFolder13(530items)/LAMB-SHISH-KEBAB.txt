Title: LAMB SHISH KEBAB
Categories: Meats, Barbecue, Usenet
Yield: 4 servings
----------------------------------MARINADE----------------------------------
1/2 t  Turmeric, ground
1/2 t  Coriander, ground
1/2 t  Cumin, ground
1/8 t  Cayenne (or
-to taste)
1 T  Onion, grated
1 t  Garlic, finely
-chopped
2 t  Ginger (fresh),
-finely chopped
2 T  Lemon juice
1/4 C  Peanut oil or
-vegetable oil
-----------------------------------KEBABS-----------------------------------
2    Green peppers, cut
-into large chunks
2    Tomatoes, cut into
-large chunks
24    Lamb (lean), cubed
-(approx 2 lbs)
2 md Onions, cut into
-large chunks
Mix all marinade ingredients in a large bowl.  Add lamb cubes and marinate
for 1 hour. Preheat broiler or light the barbecue.

Divide the meat and vegetables evenly into 4 parts, and skewer the pieces
on 4 skewers. Place 4 to 5 inches from flame. Grill, turning to cook
evenly, for approximately 10 minutes. Serve with rice and cucumber-yogurt
sauce.

NOTES:

*  This is a fairly spicy lamb shish kebab -- Serve it with cucumber yogurt
sauce.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  5 minutes preparation, 1 hour marinating, 10 minutes cooking.
: Precision:  approximate measurement OK.
