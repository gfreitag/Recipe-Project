Title: Classlc Macaronl Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1    Jar pimientos -- 2 oz
Chopped/drained
Lettuce leaves
Paprika, optional
1    Recipe Old-Fashioned Egg
Salad
1    Box elbow macaroni -- 7 oz
Cooked/drained
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
In a bowl, combine egg salad, macaroni, green pepper, mayonnaise and
pimientos. Cover and chill. For each serving. spoon about 3/4 cup
onto a lettuce leaf. Sprinkle with paprika if desired. Yield: 8
servings
