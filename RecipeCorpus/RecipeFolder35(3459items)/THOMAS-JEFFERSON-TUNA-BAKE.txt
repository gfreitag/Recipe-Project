Title: THOMAS JEFFERSON TUNA BAKE
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD:  4 SERVINGS--------------------------
1 lb Frozen french fries
1 cn (@ 7 oz.) tuna
1 c  Sliced black olives
8 oz Plain yogurt
1 ts Seasoned pepper
1 ts Ground celery seed
1 ea Onion, chopped
1 ea Green pepper, chopped
2 tb Butter, melted
2 c  Milk
2 pk (@ 1 1/4 oz. ea.) cheese
-sauce mix
Preheat oven to 400F.  In a medium saucepan, saute onion and green
pepper in butter.  Combine milk with cheese sauce mix and add to
saucepan. Stir over medium heat until thick and smooth.  Remove from
heat and stir in all remaining ingredients except french fries.
Spread half the fries in an 8-inch square baking pan and top with all
of the cheese sauce mixture. Layer the top with remaining fries. Bake
30 minutes or until fries are golden brown. Adapted from a 70's
newspaper clipping.
