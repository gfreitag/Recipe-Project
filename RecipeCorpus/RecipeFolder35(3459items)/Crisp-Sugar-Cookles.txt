Title: Crisp Sugar Cookles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
5 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Milk
1 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
2 c  Sugar
1 ea Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla.
Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; add to creamed
mixture alternately with the milk;Â· Cover and refrigerate for 15-30
minutes; On a floured surface, roll out dough to 1/8-in. thickness.
Cut out cookies into desired shapes and place 2 in. apart on a
greased baking sheet. Bake at 350 deg. for 10 minutes or until edges
are lightly browned. Yield: 8 dozen (2-1/2-inch cookies)
