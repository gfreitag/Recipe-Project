Title: Meat & Potato Loaf
Categories: Main dish, Crockpot, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Beef broth
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 lb Italian sausage
1    Onion, small, chopped
1 c  Instant Potato Flakes*
1    Egg, slightly beaten
*Not reconstituted. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients. Shape
into a 7" meatball. Place a trivet in pot. Place meat ball on a
double thickness of cheesecloth (about 24" square). Holding the ends
of cheesecloth, gently lower meat into pot. Loosely fold cloth over
top of meat. Cover; cook on LOW 6-7 hours. Holding ends of cloth,
lift meat from pot. Cut into wedges. Makes 6 or 7 wedges. Cal 287;
Pro 22g. Carb. 7g. Fat 19g. Submitted By CAROL KATZ   On   10-01-95
