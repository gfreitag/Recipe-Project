Title: Apple Crisp Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
4 md Baking apples
Peeled and cut into 1/2 "
Slices
Pastry for a single-crust
Pie
2/3 c  Sugar
3 tb All-purpose flour
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPLNG:-------------------------------
Softened
1/2 c  Caramel ice cream topping --
Or caramel apple dip
Vanilla ice cream, optional
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/3 c  Packed brown sugar
1/3 c  Milled oats
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Roll pastry to fit a 12-in. pizza pan; fold under or flute the edges.
Combine sugar, flour and cinnamon in a bowl. Add apples and toss.
Arrange the apples in a single layer in a circular pattern to
completely cover pastry. Combine the first five topping ingredients;
sprinkle over apples. Bake at 350 deg. for 35-40 minutes or until
apples are tender. Remove from the oven and immediately drizzle with
caramel topping or dip. Serve warm with ice cream if desired. Yield:
12 servings
