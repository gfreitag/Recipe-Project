Title: Sautd Zucchini Slices
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
1 ds Pepper
2 tb Salad oil
1 ts Salt
1 lb Zucchini
1 ts Salt
1    Egg; beaten
1/2 c  Flour
1/8 ts Garlic powder
Wash and slice unpeeled zucchini into 1/2 inch slices.  Dip into egg
and then flour mixed with poultry seasoning, salt, garlic and pepper.
Heat oil in skillet and saut zucchini 2 minutes on each side or
until tender.
