Title: Creamy Dllled Carrots
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
pn White pepper
1 tb All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Light cream
2 ts Dried dill
Or 2 tablespoons fresh dill
4 c  Carrots -- thinly sliced
3/4 c  Water
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Sugar
In a saucepan, combine carrots, water. butter, salt, sugar and pepper.
Cover and simmer until carrots are crisp-tender, about 10 minutes.
Drain liquid into a small saucepan; set the carrots aside and keep
warm. Bring liquid to a boil. In a small bowl, combine flour and
cream until smooth; slowly add to liquid, stirring constantly. Simmer
for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Pour over the carrots: stir in
dill. Cover and let stand for 15 minutes before serving. Yield: 6-8
servings
