Title: APPLE CIDER PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 32 servings
1 ts ALLSPICE
2 ea CINNAMON STICKS 3-INCH PIECE
2 ea ORANGES,STUDDED WITH CLOVES
1 ga APPLE CIDER
2/3 c  SUGAR
1 ts WHOLE CLOVES
HEAT CIDER, SUGAR, CLOVES, ALLSPICE AND CINNAMON TO BOILING; REDUCE
HEAT. COVER AND SIMMER 20 MINUTES.  STRAIN PUNCH AND POUR INTO PUNCH
BOWL. FLOAT ORANGES IN BOWL.
