Title: Asparagus with Curry Sauce - SL 1/90
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea 10-oz packages frozen
-asparagus spears; thawed
2 tb Capers
1/2 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
2 ts To 3 tspn curry powder
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
1 lb Fresh asparagus spears -OR-
Combine first 3 ingredients; cover and chill.
Snap off tough ends of asparagus; remove scales with a knife or
vegetable peeler, if desired.  Cook asparagus, covered, in a small
amount of boiling water 6 to 8 minutes or until crisp-tender; drain.
Arrange asparagus spears on a serving platter; top with curry
sauce, and sprinkle with capers.  Yield: 4 servings.

January, 1990 "Southern Living" "A New Year's Eve Dinner for Four".
