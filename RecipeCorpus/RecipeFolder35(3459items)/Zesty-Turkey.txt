Title: Zesty Turkey
Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 10 servings
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Ground allspice
4 ea Bay leaves -- crumbled
4 lb Turkey breast
2 c  Chicken broth -- low-sodium
2 tb Sage -- rubbed
1 tb Pepper
2 ts Curry powder
2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Dried parsley -- flakes
2 ts Celery seed
In small bowl, combine spices and mix well. Place turkey on a rack in
a roasting pan; rub with spice mixture. Add broth to pan. Bake at 350
deg. for 2 to 3 hrs. or until done, basting every 30 min. Yield: 6
servings.
