Title: Golden Fish Cakes
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Dry parsley flakes
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Italian seasoned bread
-crumbs
2 tb Cooking oil
Tartar sauce (optional)
Lemon wedges (optional)
1 lb Flaked cooked whitefish,
-cod or haddock
1 1/2 c  Soft bread bread crumbs
3 ea Eggs, beaten
2-4 T Water
1 md Onion, chopped
2 tb Mayonnaise
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
In a bowl; combine the first nine ingredients; mix well. Shape into 12
patties, adding additional water if needed; coat with the Italian
bread crumbs. In a large skillet, cook the patties in oil for 4-5
minutes on each side or until lightly browned. Serve immediately with
tartar sauce and lemon wedges if desired.
Serves 4-6.
SOURCE:* Country Magazine, August/September 1995
