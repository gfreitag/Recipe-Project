Title: BEEFEATER'S KIDNEY BEANS
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 md Onion, chopped
1 tb Chili powder
1/4 lb Process cheese, 1/2" cubes
1/2 lb Ground beef
2 c  Home tomato sauce
1/2 c  Water
2 lb Kidney beans, canned
Brown beef in large saucepan.  Add tomato sauce, water, liquid from
beans, onion and chili powder.

Bring to boil, cover and simmer 10 to 15 minutes.  Serve topped with
cheese.
