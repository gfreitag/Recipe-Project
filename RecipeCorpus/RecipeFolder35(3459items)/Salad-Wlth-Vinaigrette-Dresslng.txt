Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tasteofhome

3 dr Hot pepper sauce
Salad greens
Bell peppers, mushrooms,
Tomatoes
And/or other vegetable of

3/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  White wine vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Garllc powder
In a jar:with a tight-fitting lid, combine the first seven
