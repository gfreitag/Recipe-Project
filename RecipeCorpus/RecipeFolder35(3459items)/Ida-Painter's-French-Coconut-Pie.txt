Title: Ida Painter's French Coconut Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Route 66
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Coconut
1    9 Inch Pie Shell -- unbaked
3    Eggs -- beaten
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Margarine -- melted
1 tb Vinegar
Combine ingredients in order given and pour into pie shell. Bake in
preheated 350 F oven for 40 to 45 mins or until set. Makes 4 to 6
servings.
