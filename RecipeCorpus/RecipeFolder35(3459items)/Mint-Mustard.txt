Title: Mint Mustard
Categories: Meats, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1    Clove Garlic -- minced
1 ts Fresh lemon juice
6 tb Chopped fresh mint
3 tb Mayonnaise
3/4 c  Dijon mustard
In a small bowl, stir together all ingredients. Store in jar or
container with tight-fitting lid. Refrigerate until ready to use.
