Title: Clnnamon Mlnl Mufflns
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Skim milk
1/3 c  Margarine -- melted
TOPPING:
2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 c  Margarine -- melted
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1/2 ts Ground allspice
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, nutmeg and
allspice. Combine the egg, milk and margarine; mix well. Stir into dry
ingredients just until moistened. Spoon into greased or paper-lined
mini muffin cups. Bake at 400 deg. for 12-14 minutes or until muffins
test done. For topping, combine sugar and cinnamon. Brush the tops of
warm muffins with margarine; sprinkle with cinnamon-sugar. Yield: 2
dozen. Nutritional Information: 96 calories, 128 mg sodium, 9 mg
cholesterol, 12 gm carbohydrate, 1 gm protein, 5 gm fat
