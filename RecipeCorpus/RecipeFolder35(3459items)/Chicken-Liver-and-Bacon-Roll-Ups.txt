Title: Chicken Liver and Bacon Roll-Ups
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 8 servings
12 sl Bacon; cut in half
1/2 c  Cracker crumbs
1 lb Livers, chicken
3 tb Mustard, Dijon
Dip chicken livers lightly in mustard.  Wrap a half slice of bacon
around each, and secure with a toothpick; coat livers with crumbs.
Place in a 8" square baking dish; bake at 425 degrees for 25 minutes.
