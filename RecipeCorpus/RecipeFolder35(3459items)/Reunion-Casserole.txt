Title: Reunion Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
Undiluted
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 8 oz
1/3 c  Olives -- stuffed &slice 8
Oz
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Salt
8 oz Noodles -- wide cooked
And drained
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1/2 lb Sausage meat -- hot
1 c  Chopped onion
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese --
Divided
1 lg Green pepper -- chopped
1 cn Corn, whole kernel, canned
12 oz Drained
1 cn Tomato soup -- 10 3/4 oz
In a large Dutch oven, brown the beef, sausage, and onion. Drain off
fat. Stir in 1 cup cheese, green pepper, corn, soup, tomato sauce,
olives, garlic, salt and noodles. Place in a 13x9x3 inch baking pan.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Cover and bake at 350 deg. for 35
min. Uncover and bake 10 minutes longer. Yield: 8 to 10 servings.
