Title: Apricot Baked Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Apricot preserves
1 pk Dry onion soup mix
2 lb Chicken parts
1/2 c  Russian dressing
2 tb Mayonaise
Put chicken in glass baking dish arranging thickest pieces around
edge. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over chicken.  Cover with
waxed paper and microwave on high 18-20 minutes, turning chicken over
and rotating dish once during cooking From 365 Microwave Calendar 1990
