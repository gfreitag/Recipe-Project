Title: Master Baking Mix
Categories: Breads
Yield: 10 servings
1 tb Salt
2 c  Shortening
10 c  Flour
1/3 c  Baking Powder
Sift dry ingredients together in large bowl. Cut in shortening until
mixture is texture of coarse cornmeal.  Keep stored in labeled,
tightly closed container 1 to 6 months in pantry in dry weather.  In
hot or humid weather, keep in the refrigerator.  Use as required in
place of "Bisquick" or other "Baking Mixes" in recipes.
