Title: Munger Moss Cherry Cream Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Route 66
Yield: 8 servings
pn Salt
1 dr Red Food Coloring
2 c  Dream Whip
1    Ten-Inch Pie Crust -- baked
1 pk (3 Oz) Cream Cheese
1/2 c  Powdered Sugar
1 c  Dream Whip
1 cn (16 Oz) Sour Cherries
2 tb Cornstarch
Soften cream cheese and blend with powdered sugar until smooth. Fold
in 1 cup of Dream Whip and blend well. Spoon mixture into pie shell
and place in freezer for 20 mins. Drain cherries. Heat cherry juice
and thicken with cornstarch. Add salt and a drop of red food
coloring. Add cherries to juice and allow mixture to cool. Spread
over cream cheese mixture. Cover with Dream Whip. Store in
refrigerator until served. Makes 8 servings.
