Title: Dllled Carrots
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Butter or margarine
2 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground ginger
3 c  Carrots -- sliced
1/4 c  Green pepper -- diced
2 tb Fresh dill -- minced
1 tb Honey
In a saucepan, cook carrots in a small amount of water until
crisp-tender; drain. Add remaining ingredients. Cook over low heat
for 1 to 2 minutes or until hot. Yield: 3-4 servings
