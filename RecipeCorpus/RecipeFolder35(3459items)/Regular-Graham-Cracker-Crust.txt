Title: Regular Graham Cracker Crust
Categories: Pastry, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
15 ea Graham crackers
Cinnamon
2 1/2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Butter
Burn about 2 1/2 Tbsp. sugar in small non-stick skillet until golden
brown and liquid.

Remove from heat and add  1/2 Stick butter.  Stir until butter melts
and pour over 15 crushed graham crackers.  Add a couple sprinkles of
cinnamon and mix then press into bottom of pan.  Now it's ready for
the filling of your choice.
