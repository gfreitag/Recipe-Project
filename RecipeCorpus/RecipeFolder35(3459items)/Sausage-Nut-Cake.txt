Title: Sausage Nut Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Hot coffee
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 ts Soda, heaping
1 ts Allspice
1 lb Pork sausage, unseasoned
1 c  Raisins
3 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Cloves
Mix sausage and sugar in hot coffee, then add raisins and nuts.  Add
other ingredients, beat well.  Place in a greased pan and bake in a
350 degree oven for 30 to 35 minutes.  Can be baked in loaf of layer
pans.
