Title: Pumpkin Butter (Vegan, with honey)
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Cloves
16 oz Can solid pack pumpkin
2/3 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Honey
In medium saucepan combine pumpkin, brown sugar, honey, lemon juice,
cinnamon and cloves.
Bring to a boil over med high heat, stirring frequently.  Reduce heat;
simmer 20 mins, or until thickened, stirring occasionally.

Ladle mixture into clean jars or freezer containers.  Cover with lids.
Stir in refrigerator several weeks or freeze several months.
