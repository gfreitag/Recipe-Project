Title: Mom's Hot Buttered Drink Mix
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 3 cups mix
2 tb Ground cinnamon
2 1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
2 1/4 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
1 c  Butter; unsalted, softened
3 tb Maple syrup
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients with an electric mixer
until well blended.  Store in an airtight container in a
refrigerator.  Give with serving instructions.

Makes about 3 cups of drink mix.

To Serve:  Stir 1 1/2 teaspoons drink mix into 6oz of hot apple cider
or other juices.  For hot buttered rum, add 1oz rum to cider mixture.

Source:  A pamphlet Mom received in the mail.
