Title: Dan's Peanut Butter Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Route 66
Yield: 12 servings
1 lg Container Cool Whip
Graham Cracker Crust
1 c  Chunky Peanut Butter
8 oz Cream Cheese
1/2 c  Powdered Sugar
Cream peanut butter with cream cheese and sugar. Fold in Cool Whip
and pour into a 13 x 9 inch baking dish lined with graham cracker
crust. Sprinkle top with graham cracker crumbs. Refrigerate until
served. Makes 10 to 12 servings.
