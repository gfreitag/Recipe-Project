Title: Savory Roast Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Fresh savory -- minced
Or 1 teaspoon dried savory
4 ea Boneless skinless chicken
Breast halves
2 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
Place chicken, breast side up on a rack in a shallow roasting pan.
combine butter, lemon juice and savory; bush over chicken. Bake
uncovered at 375 deg. for 1 1/2 hrs. or until juices run clear,
basting occasionally with the pan drippings. Yield 4 servings.
