Title: Route 66 Chess Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Route 66
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Butter -- melted
1/4 c  Milk
4 tb Lemon Juice
1    9 Inch Pie Shell -- unbaked
2 c  Sugar
1 tb Flour
1 tb Yellow Cornmeal
4    Eggs -- unbeaten
1/2 ts Vanilla
Combine dry ingredients and stir in eggs, vanilla, butter, milk and
lemon juice. Pour into an unbaked 9-inch pie shell. Bake for 35 to 40
mins in preheated 350 F oven. Makes 6 to 8 servings.
