Title: Freezer Peanut Butter Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
Chocolate sauce
Chopped walnuts
1 qt Vanilla ice cream --
Softened divided
1 ea Graham cracker crust -- 8 or
9    In
Spread half of the ice cream into crust. Combine peanut butter and
corn syrup; spread over ice cream. Spread evenly with remaining ice
cream. Drizzle with chocolate sauce; top with nuts. Cover and freeze
for 3-4 hours. Remove from freezer 15 minutes before serving. Yield:
6-8 servings. Editor's Note: If desired, recipe can be prepared
several weeks before serving. Cover tightly with foil and freeze
