Title: Creamy Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ds Salt
Minature marshmallows
5 lb Sweet potatoes -- peeled &
Cooked
4 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
In a large bowl, mash sweet potatoes. Add eggs, orange juice, butter,
sugar, vanilla, nutmeg and salt; mix well. Transfer to a greased 3-qt.
baking dish. Bake at 350 deg. for 35-40 minutes or until set. Top with
marshmallows; return to oven until they just begin to puff and melt,
about 5-10 minutes. Yield: 10-12 servings
