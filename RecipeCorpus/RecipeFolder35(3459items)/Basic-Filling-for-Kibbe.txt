Title: Basic Filling for Kibbe
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 1 recipe
1/4 c  Parsley, minced
1 1/2 ts Syrian Mixed Spices or
1 ts Allspice and
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Dried currants
Salt and pepper to taste
3 tb Butter
1/4 c  Pine nuts
1 lg Onion(s), finely chopped
1 c  Lean ground lamb
-reserved from recipe for
-basic raw lamb and bulgur
Melt the butter in a medium skillet, spoon off the surface foam and
discard. Add the pine nuts and toast them, stirring frequently, over
low heat until the nuts are tan-colored. Remove with a slotted spoon
and set aside. In the same skillet, add the onions and sautÂ‚ them
over medium heat, stirring frequently, for 5-8 minutes, or until they
begin to brown. do not brown them. Add the lamb and stir until it
loses its pink color. Stir in the parsley, spices, currants, reserved
pine nuts and the salt and pepper. Let the mixture cool slightly
before using.
