Title: Peanut Butter Sundaes
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
Vanill ice cream
Salted peanuts -- optional
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Creamy peanut butter
In a saucepan, combine sugar and water. Bring to a boil; boil 1 min.
or until sugar is dissolved. Remove from the heat; stir in peanut
butter. Place in a blender; blend on high until smooth. Cool
slightly; pour over ice cream. Sprinkle with peanuts if desired.
Refrigerate and leftovers. Yield: 1 1/2 cups.
