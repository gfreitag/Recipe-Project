Title: Crispy Winter Relish
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 2 cups
4 ts Liquid sucaryl
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Dry mustard
ds Cayenne
1 c  Finely chopped apples
1 c  Finely shredded cabbage
1/2 c  Finely diced celery
1 tb Chopped green pepper
1 tb Chopped pimiento
3 tb Vinegar
Combine apples and vegetables.Combine vinegar,sucaryl,salt,and spices
blending well.Pour over apples and vegetables,tossing
lightly.Refrigerate at least 1 hour.

Per 1/4 cup serving: 17 cal protein 0.5g fat trace carbohydrates 5g

(with sugar)   53 cal

1 serving = 1/2 fruit exchange
