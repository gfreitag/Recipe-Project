Title: MARTIN'S OCTOPUS SALAD
Categories: Salads, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Fennel loosley packed
Lemon juice to taste
1 tb Ginger
2 tb Garlic minced
2 tb Shallots
3 lb Octupus cooked cut small
1 c  Rice wine vinegar
2 c  Olive oil
1 1/2 c  Red peppers sliced
1 1/2 c  Green peppers sliced
combine all ingredients in a ss bowl and refrigerate for 12 hours.
