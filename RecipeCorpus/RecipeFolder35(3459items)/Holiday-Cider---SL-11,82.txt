Title: Holiday Cider - SL 11/82
Categories: Beverages, Fruit, Hot/spicy
Yield: 1 gallon
3/4 c  Red cinnamon candies
2 ea (3-inch) cinnamon sticks
1 md Orange
1 1/2 ts Whole cloves
1 ga Apple cider
Slice orange into 6 wedges; stud wedges with cloves; set aside.
Combine cider and candies in a Dutch oven over medium heat; cook
until candies are dissolved and mixture is thoroughly heated.  Pour
cider mixture into a heat-proof punch bowl.  Add clove-studded orange
wedges and cinnamon sticks.  Serve hot.   Yield: 1 gallon.

From Terri Burke of West Virginia, in November, 1982 "Southern
