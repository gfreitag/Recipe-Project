Title: Jiggs Smoke House Barbecue Beans
Categories: Route 66, Vegetables
Yield: 20 servings
2 tb Sorghum
2 tb Brown Sugar
1 ga Pork And Beans
1 1/2 c  Jiggs Barbecue Sauce
Combine ingredients with pork and beans in a large crock pot and heat
slowly until ready to serve. Makes 20 servings.
