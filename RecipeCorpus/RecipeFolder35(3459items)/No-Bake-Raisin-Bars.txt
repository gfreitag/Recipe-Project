Title: No-Bake Raisin Bars
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
3 c  Miniature marshmallows
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
5 c  Multi-grain puffed rice
Cereal
In a large saucepan or microwave-safe bowl, heat marshmallows and
butter over low until melted; mix well. Stir the cereal, raisins,
walnuts and cinnamon. Pat into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in.
baking pan. Cool. Cut into bars. Yield: 2 dozen
