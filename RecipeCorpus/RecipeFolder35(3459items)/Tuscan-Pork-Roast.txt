Title: Tuscan Pork Roast
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 ea Boneless pork top loin -- 3
To 4 lb
8    Cloves garlic -- peeled
1 tb Dried rosemary
1 tb Olive oil
In a blender or food processor, combine garlic, rosemary, olive oil
and salt; blend until mixture turns to paste. Rub over the roast;
cover and let stand for 30 min. Place on a greased baking rack, fat
side up, in a roasting pan. Bake, uncovered, at 325 deg 2 to 2 1/2
hrs. Let stand for 15 min. before slicing. Yield: 10-12 servings.
