Title: Chocolate Milk Shake
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
1 1/2 c  Skim milk
1 c  Low-calorie chocolate ice
Milk
1/4 c  Cocoa
1/3 c  Hot water
3 ts Liquid sucaryl
In blender,combine cocoa,hot water,and sucaryl;buzz to blend.Add milk
and ice milk; buzz a few minutes until foamy.

Per serving 214 calories, 11g protein, 6.5g fat, 33g carbohydrates.(
with sugar = 358 calories)

1 serving -   1 bread exchange, 1 fat exchange,  1 1/2 c skim milk
