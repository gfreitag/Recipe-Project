Title: Mock Pasta Alfredo
Categories: Pasta/rice, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Salt -- optional
8 oz Corkscrew noodles --
Cooked/drained
2 ea Tomatoes -- seeded/chopped
1 1/2 c  1% Cottage cheese
1/2 c  Skim milk
2 ea Garlic cloves -- minced
2 tb All-purpose flour
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Dried basil
In a blender or food processor, process cottage cheese, milk and
garlic until smooth. Add flour, lemon juice, basil, mustard, pepper
and salt if desired; process until well blended. Pour into a
saucepan. Cook over medium heat until thickened. Do not boil. Serve
over noodles; sprinkle with chopped tomatoes. Yield: 4 servings.
Diabetic exchanges: One serving (prepared without added salt) equals
2-1/2 starch, 2 lean meat, 1 vegetable. Nutritional Information: 316
calories, 368 mg sodium, 8 mg cholesterol, 53 gm carbohydrate, 20 gm
protein, 2 gm fat
