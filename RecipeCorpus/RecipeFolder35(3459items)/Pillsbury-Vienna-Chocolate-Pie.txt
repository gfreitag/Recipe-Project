Title: Pillsbury Vienna Chocolate Pie
Categories: Chocolate, Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
Crusts (15 oz)
1 pk Pillsbury Refrigerated Pie
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 1/2 ts Vanilla Extract
1/2 c  Margarine or Butter,
Softened
2 oz Unsweetened Chocolate,
Melted
1/4 c  Slivered Almonds
1 1/2 c  Granulated Sugar
3 tb Flour
3/4 ts Instant Coffee Granules or
Crystals
1/4 ts Ground Cinnamon
ds Salt
4 lg Eggs
Prepare pie crust according to package directions for one-crust
filled pie using 9-inch pie pan.
Heat oven to 400 degrees. In medium bowl, combine sugar, flour,
instant coffee, cinnamon and salt; mix well. In large bowl, beat eggs
at high speed until light in color. Beat in dry ingredients. Add
buttermilk, vanilla, margarine and chocolate; mix well. (Filling may
look curdled.) Pour into crust lined pan. Sprinkle with slivered
almonds. Bake at 400 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes or until center is
set. Cover edge of crusts with strips of foil after 15 minutes of
baking to prevent excessive browning.

Nutrition information per serving: Calories-490, Calories from
Fat-240, Total Fat-27g, Saturated Fat-8g, Cholesterol-115mg,
Sodium-340mg, Total Carbohydrate-56g, Dietary Fiber-2g, Sugars-39g,
Protein-6g. Dietary Exchanges: 2 Starch, 1 1/2 Fruit and 5 1/2 Fat
