Title: Sweet Pineapple Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 20 servings
2 cn Crushed pineapple -- 8 oz
ea Undrained
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking soda and baking powder.
In another bowl, mix pineapple, eggs and oil; stir into the dry
ingredients just until moistened. Fill greased or paper-lined muffin
cups two-thirds full. Bake at 350 deg. for 20 to 25 min. or until
muffins test done. dool in pan for 10 min before removing. Yield:
about 20 muffins.
