Title: Duchesse Potatoes
Categories: Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Nutmeg
2    Eggs
2    Egg yolks
4    Whole Potatoes
1/2 c  Hot milk
2 tb Butter -- melted
1 ts Salt
Boil potatoes until tender. Mash with 1/4 cup milk, butter, salt,
pepper, and nutmeg. Beat eggs and egg yolks together, then beat in
remaining milk. Beat egg mixture into potatoes until fluffy. Serve at
once.
