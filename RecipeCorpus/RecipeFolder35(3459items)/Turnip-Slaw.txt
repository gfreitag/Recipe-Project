Title: Turnip Slaw
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Vinegar
2 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 c  Turnips -- shredded peeled
1/4 c  Sweet red peppers --
Chopped
1/4 c  Green onions -- thinly
Sliced
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
In a bowl, combine all ingredients except turnips. Pour over turnips
and toss well to coat. Refrigerate several hours for flavors to
blend. Yield: 4 servings.
