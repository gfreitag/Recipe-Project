Title: Salted Nut Squares
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Ounces
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk --
14    Ounces
2 c  Miniature marshmallows
3 c  Salted Peanuts
Without skins, divided
3 tb Butter or margarine
2 c  Peanut butter chips -- 12
Place half of the peanuts in an ungreased 11-in. x 7-in. x 2-in.
baking pan; set aside. In a saucepan, melt butter and peanut butter
chips over low heat. Add milk and marshmallows; cook and stir until
melted. Pour over peanuts. Sprinkle the remaining peanuts on top.
Cover and refrigerate. Cut into bars. Yield: 5-6 dozen
