Title: Baked Peach Pancake
Categories: Breakfast, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter or margarine
Ground nutmeg
Sour cream, optional
2 c  Peaches
Fresh or frozen sliced
Peeled
4 ts Sugar
1 ts Lemon juice
3 ea Eggs
In a bowl, combine peaches with sugar and lemon juice; set aside. In a
mixing bowl, beat eggs until fluffy. Add flour, milk and salt; beat
until smooth. Place butter in a 10-in, skillet; bake at 400 deg. for
3-5 minutes or until melted. immediately pour butter into hot
skillet. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until pancake has risen and is
puffed all over. Fill with peach slices and sprinkle with nutmeg.
Serve immediately with sour cream if desired. Yield: 4-6 servings
