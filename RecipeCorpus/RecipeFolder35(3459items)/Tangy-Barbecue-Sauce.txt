Title: Tangy Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 ea Bay leaf
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
1/2 c  Water
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Ketchup
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
2 ts Prepared mustard
1 ts Salt
Combine all of the ingredients in a small saucepan; bring to a boil,
stirring occasionaly. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
Discard bay leaf. Use as a basting sauce when grilling chicken, pork
or beef. Yield: 1-1/2 cups
