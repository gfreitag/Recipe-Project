Title: Crockpot Italian Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Crockpot, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Mushrooms; 4 oz, or 1 cup
1 x  Fresh mushrooms
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 lb Chicken; cut up
1 cn Soup; cream of mushroom
1 pk Salad dressing mix
1 x  Seasoned salt and pepper
Combine all ingredients except muushrooms and sour cream in CROCKPOT.
Cook on LOW for 6-8 hours.  Add sour cream and mushrooms the last 1/2
hour of cooking.
