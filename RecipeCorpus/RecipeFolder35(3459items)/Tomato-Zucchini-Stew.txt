Title: Tomato Zucchini Stew
Categories: Tomatoes, Soup/stew, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Garlic salt -- or power
1/2 ts Pepper
3 c  Corn -- fresh or frozen
2 md Green pepper -- 1 in. piece
1/4 c  Cornstarch
1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/4 lb Italian sausage -- bulk
1 1/2 c  Celery -- 3/4 pieces sliced
8 md Tomatoes -- peeled & cut
Sixth
1 1/2 c  Tomato juice without salt
4 sm Zucchini -- 1/4 in. pieces
2 1/2 ts Italian seasoning
1 ts Salt
In a 4 qt. dutch oven brown and crumble sausage. Add tomatoes, tomato
juice, zucchini and seasonings; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover
and simmer for 20 min. Add corn and peppers; cover and simmer for 15
min. Combine cornstarch and water; stir into stew. Bring to a boil;
cook and stir until mixture thickens. Sprinkle with cheese. Yield: 6
to 8 servings. Editor"s Note: Three 28 oz  cans of tomatoes with
liquid (cut up) may be substituted for the fresh tomatoes and tomato
juice.
