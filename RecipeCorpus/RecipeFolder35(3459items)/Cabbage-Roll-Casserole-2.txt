Title: Cabbage Roll Casserole 2
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Cooked rice
1 cn Tomato soup (10 oz)
3 c  Cabbage shredded
1 lb Ground beef
1    Onion, chopped
Salt and pepper
Directions: Brown meat, add everything else but cabbage. Mix well. Put
cabbage in a greased casserole. Ad the meat mixture and bake covered
at 350 for 1 1/2 hours.
