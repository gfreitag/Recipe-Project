Title: Vegetables and Spam
Categories: Spam, Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 1 jim bodle
Paprika;
1 cn Spam; diced
1 tb Catup;
Salt & pepper TO TASTE
2    Onions
2    Red/yellow/Green peppers;
-- sliced
2    Tomatoes; chopped
Fry onions in oil, add peppers, tomatoes, spam, catsup, seasonings,
cook all in a skillet on stove.
