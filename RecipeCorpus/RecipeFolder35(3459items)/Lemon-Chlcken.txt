Title: Lemon Chlcken
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Ounces
3 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 ts Lemon peel -- grated
1/2 ts Salt -- optional
1 1/2 c  Instant rice -- uncooked
1 c  Frozen broccoli -- chopped
Thawed
1/4 c  Fresh parsley -- minced
1 lb Boneless skinless chicken
Breasts -- cut into strips
1 md Onion -- chopped
1 lg Carrot -- thinly sliced
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Cornstarch
1 cn Chicken broth -- 14-1/2
In a skillet, cook chicken, onion, carrot and garlic in butter until
chicken is lightly browned, about 5 minutes. In a bowl, combine the
cornstarch and broth; stir in lemon juice, peel, salt and rice. Add to
skillet and bring to a boil. Reduce heat; add broccoli and parsley.
Cover and simmer 5-10 minutes or until rice is tender. Yield: 4
servings. Diabetic Exchanges: One serving (without added salt) equals
3 lean meat, 2 starch, 1 vegetable; also, 367 calories, 132 mg
sodium, 73 mg cholesterol, 39 gm carbohydrate, 31 gm protein, 9 gm fat
