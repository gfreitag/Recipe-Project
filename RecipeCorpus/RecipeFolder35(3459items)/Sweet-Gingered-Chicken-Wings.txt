Title: Sweet Gingered Chicken Wings
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Frozen orange juice
Concentrate -- thawed
1/2 ts Ground ginger
Snipped fresh parsley,
Optional
1 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Salt
2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Pepper
24 ea Chicken wings
SAUCE:
In a bowl, combine flour, salt, paprika and pepper. Coat chicken
wings in flour mixture; shake off excess. Place wings on a large
greased baking sheet. Bake at 350 deg. for 30 minutes. Remove from
the oven and drain. Combine honey, orange juice concentrate and
ginger; brush generously over chicken wings. Reduce heat to 325 deg..
Bake for 30-40 minutes or until chicken tests done, basting
occasionally with more sauce. Sprinkle with parsley before serving if
desired. Yield: 2 dozen
