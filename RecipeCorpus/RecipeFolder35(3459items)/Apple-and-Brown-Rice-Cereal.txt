Title: Apple and Brown Rice Cereal
Categories: Low cal/fat, Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Cinnamon
Equal sweetner to taste *2
1/2 c  Walnuts, chopped
3 1/2 c  Brown rice, cooked
6 oz Apple juice concentrate *1
6 oz Water
1 1/2 c  Apple, chopped
*1 use apple juice with no sugar added. *2 can be substituted with any
comparable sweetner.

Place first 4 ingredients in sauce pan and bring to a boil.  reduce
heat to medium high.  Cook 5-8 minutes or until liquid cooks down and
apples are tender.  Stir constantly.  Pour into 4 bowls, sprinkle on
sweetner and top with walnuts.

Carbohydrates..........57.3 g.
Fat.................... 5.8 g.
Cholesterol..............O  mg.
Calories........299
Protein.........5.8 8g.
Sodium..........5 mg.
