Title: Grandma's Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
6 lb Red salad potatoes
Cooked, peeled and sliced
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1/4 c  Green onions -- sliced
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Leaf lettuce, optional
3 ea Hard-cooked eggs -- sliced
Paprika
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Vinegar
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Sugar
4 1/2 ts Cornstarch
3/4 c  Salad dressing -- or
Mayonnaise
3/4 c  Heavy cream -- whipped
In the top of a double boiler over boiling water, heat water, butter
and vinegar in a bowl, beat eggs: add sugar and corn starch. Add to
butter mixture; cook and stir constantly until thick, about 5-7
minutes. Remove from the heat and allow to cool. Stir in the salad
dressing; fold into the whipped cream. In a large bowl, toss
potatoes, onion, green onions, salt and pepper. Pour the dressing
over and mix gently. Chill. Serve in a lettuce-lined bowl if desired.
Garnish with the hard-cooked eggs and sprinkle with paprika. Yield:
18 servings
