Title: Budget Macaroni And Cheese
Categories: Pasta/rice, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
1 ds Pepper
1 c  Milk
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese -- 4
oz Chopped
1    Box macaroni -- elbow 7 oz
3 tb Butter or margarine
3 tb All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
Cook the macaroni according to package directions. Drain; set aside
and keep warm. In a saucepan over medium-low heat, melt butter. Add
flour, salt and pepper; stir to make a smooth paste. Gradually add
milk, stirring constantly. Heat and stir until thickened. Remove from
the heat; stir in cheese until melted. Pour over macaroni and mix
well. Yield: 4 servings.
