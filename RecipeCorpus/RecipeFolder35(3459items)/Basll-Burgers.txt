Title: Basll Burgers
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Garlic salt
8 ea Hamburger buns
Monterey Jack cheese slices,
Optional
2 lb Lean ground beef
1/4 c  Red wine -- or beef broth
1/4 c  Ltalian bread crumbs
1/4 c  Red onion -- minced
1/2 c  Basil leaves -- minced
In a large bowl, combine first six ingredients; mix well. Shape into
eight patties. Grill over medium-hot coals until burgers reach
desired doneness. Serve on hamburger buns; top with cheese slices if
desired. Yield: 8 servings
