Title: Mom's White Clam Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Italian, Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Black pepper; fresh ground
1/2 c  Parsley; chopped
3 cn Minced clams; 6 1/2oz each,
.  including 1/2 cup juice
4 tb Olive oil
4 tb Butter
2 cl Garlic; minced
2 tb Shallots; minced
Heat the oil and butter in a large skillet.  Add the garlic and
shallots, and saute until golden.  Add the remaining ingredients,
heat thoroughly and serve over pasta.
