Title: Seven Layer Bars Micro
Categories: Microwave, Cake/cookie
Yield: 56 servings
1 c  Shredded coconut
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
6 oz Butterscotch chips
1 c  Walnuts; chopped
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Graham cracker crumbs
-14 cracker squares
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
Heat margarine in microwave safe 13 x 9 inch pan on high (100% power)
about 45 seconds to 1 minute or until melted.

Blend in graham cracker crumbs; press evenly in bottom of dish. Pour
sweetened condensed milk evely over crumbs. Sprinkle on coconut,
chocolate chips and butterscotch cips. Top with nuts; press lightly
into pan.

Microwave on high 8 to 10 minutes, turning every 2 minutes or until
bubbling. Cool; cut into 56 bars.
