Title: Llme Cream Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Lime peel -- grated
2 dr Green food coloring
2/3 c  Whipped cream
1 ea Egg -- beaten
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Lime juice
In the top of a double boiler, combine the egg and sugar. Cut the
butter into tablespoons; add to double boiler. Add lime juice and
peel; cook and stir over boiling water for 8-10 minutes or until
thick. Remove from the heat; add food coloring. Cool. Fold in whipped
cream. Chill for at least 1 hour. Yield: 1 serving
