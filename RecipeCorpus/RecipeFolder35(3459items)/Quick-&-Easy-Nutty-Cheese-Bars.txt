Title: Quick & Easy Nutty Cheese Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 24 servings
Divided
1 c  Brown sugar
16 oz Cream cheese -- softened
1 pk Yellow cake mix
3/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped &
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease and flour 13 x 9 pan.  Stir together in
medium bowl cake mix, 3/4 cup margarine, 3/4 cup nuts and press
evenly into bottom of prepared pan.  Stir together in medium bowl
with spoon brown sugar and cream cheese until well mixed.  Spread
topping evenly over the base in the pan.   Sprinkle with 3/4 cup
nuts.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes. Cool completely before cutting into
bars.  Store in refrigerator in airtight container.
