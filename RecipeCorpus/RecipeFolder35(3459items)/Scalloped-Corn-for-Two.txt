Title: Scalloped Corn for Two
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Round buttery cracker crumbs
-(Ritz crackers)
1 tb Butter or margarine; melted
1/2 c  Fresh corn, cut from the cob
1/4 ts Salt
Dash of pepper
1 ea Egg; beaten
1/2 c  Milk
Combine first 5 ingredients, mixing well. Pour into a lightly
greased 2-cup baking dish; dot with 1 tablespoon butter.  Combine
cracker crumbs and melted butter, stirring well; sprinkle over corn
mixture. Bake at 375 degrees F for 40 minutes. Yield: 2 servings.

From Mrs. J.T. Ballard of Texas in September, 1982 "Southern Living"
