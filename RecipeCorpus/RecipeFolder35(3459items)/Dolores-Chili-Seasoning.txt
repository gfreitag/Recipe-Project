Title: Dolores Chili Seasoning
Categories: Chili, Route 66, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Ground Oregano
1/2 c  Cayenne Pepper
2 ts Black Pepper
1/2 lb Salt
1 c  Ground Instant Coffee
5 lb Chili Powder
3 lb Paprika
1 lb Garlic Salt
1 1/2 lb Sugar
1 c  Ground Comino (Cumin)
Combine and store to season your own chili. When ready to use, add to
taste to chunks of your own cooked chili meat, tomato sauce, and
beans.
