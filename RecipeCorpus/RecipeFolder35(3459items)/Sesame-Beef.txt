Title: Sesame Beef
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 ea Green onions -- thinly
Sliced
2 ea Garlic cloves -- minced
1 tb Sesame seeds
Rice or chow mein noodles
1 lb Sirloin steak
Cut into 1/8-inch strips
2 tb Sugar
3 tb Cooking oil -- divided
2 tb Soy sauce
1/4 ts Pepper
Place beef in a glass bowl. Combine sugar, 2 tablespoons oil, soy
sauce, pepper, onions, garlic and sesame seeds. Pour over beef and
toss to coat. Let stand 15 minutes. In skillet or wok, heat remaining
oii over high heat; add beef and marinade. Stir-fry until beef is
brown and has reached desired doneness. Serve immediately over rice
or noodles. Yield: 4 servings
