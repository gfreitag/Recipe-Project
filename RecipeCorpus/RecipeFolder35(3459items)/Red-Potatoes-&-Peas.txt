Title: Red Potatoes & Peas
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Margarine
1/2 ts Fresh ground pepper
2 tb Fresh mint -- chopped
3 md Red potatoes -- scubbed
1 bn Green onion -- chopped
10 oz Frozen peas
Cut potatoes into 3/4" pieces.  Place potatoes and onions in a
saucepan, barely cover with water and bring to boil.  Simmer until
just tender.  add peas, cover and cook for 1 minute.  Drain, add
margarine, pepper and mint. Toss gently and heat for 1 minute more.
125 calories and 1 gram fat.
