Title: Ice Water Fudge Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
Chocolate
1 1/2 ts Soda
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Ice water
3 c  Flour
3/4 c  Shortening
2 1/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
3    Eggs
3    1 oz. squares unsweetened
Cream shortening, eggs, sugar and vanilla.  Melt chocolate and add to
above.  Sift dry ingredients and ice water alternately to other
ingredients.  Bake in greased pan, 7 x 13 inch, at 350 degrees for 30
to 35 minutes.
