Title: Steak-House Burgers SL
Categories: Sandwich, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
Romaine lettuce
6 ea Kaiser rolls or hamburger
-buns; sliced in half
1 lg Tomato; cut in 6 slices
Mushroom sauce
2 tb Chopped chives
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Grated onion
2 tb Commercial steak sauce
1 tb Minced fresh parsley
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Grated lemon rind
MMMMM-----------------------MUSHROOM SAUCE----------------------------
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms; sliced
3 tb Butter or margarine; melted
Combine beef, onion, steak sauce, parsley, salt, pepper, and lemon
rind; mix well.  Shape into 6 patties abot 3/4-inch thick.  Grill
patties over medium-hot coals 8 to 10 minutes on each side or to
desired degree of doneness, turning once.  Remove patties from grill.
Place lettuce on bottom half of each roll; top with patty, tomato
slice, Mushroom Sauce, and chives.  Cover with top, and serve
immediately.

Mushroom Sauce: Saute mushrooms in butter in a skillet until tender
and liquid is evaporated. Add Dijon mustard, stirring well. Yield:
3/4 cup.

Mrs. Iris Brenner of Florida in September, 1987 "Southern Living"
