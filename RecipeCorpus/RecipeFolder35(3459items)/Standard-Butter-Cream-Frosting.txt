Title: Standard Butter Cream Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 2 cups
1 ts Vanilla
Vegetable food coloring
.  as desired
1/4 c  Butter
2 c  Confectioner's sugar; sifted
3 tb Sweet or sour cream
Cream the butter and gradually work in 1/2 cup of the sugar, beating
well, then add the remaining sugar alternately with the cream, still
beating constantly.  Finally add the vanilla and a very little
coloring, if desired.

Fruit juices may be used in place of the cream, in which instance
omit the vanilla.  If orange juice is used add a slight grating of
orange rind, or lemon rind with lemon juice.
