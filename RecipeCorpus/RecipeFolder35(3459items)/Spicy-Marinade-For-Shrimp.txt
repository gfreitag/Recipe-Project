Title: Spicy Marinade For Shrimp
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Lime juice
2 tb Cilantro, chopped
1 ts Oil, sesame
1 ts Chili paste, red
1 c  Vinegar, rice
1/2 c  Olive oil
2 ea Garlic clove, minced
1 x  Salt
24 ea Shrimp, large, cleaned
Combine all ingredients and marinate shrimp for 30 minutes. Thread 3
shrimp onto a water-soaked bamboo skewer and grill 2 to 3 minutes per
side.
