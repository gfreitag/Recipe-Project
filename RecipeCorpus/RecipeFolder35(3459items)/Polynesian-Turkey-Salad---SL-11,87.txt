Title: Polynesian Turkey Salad - SL 11/87
Categories: Salads, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Curry powder
11 oz Can mandarin oranges;
-drained
Lettuce leaves
4 c  Cooked turkey or chicken;
-diced
1 c  Red Delicious apple;
-unpeeled and diced
1 c  Pecans; chopped
15 1/4 oz Can pineapple tidbits;
-drained
Combine first 4 ingredients in a large bowl; mix well.  Combine
mayonnaise, lemon juice, and curry powder; stir into turkey mixture.
Add mandarin oranges, and gently toss.  Cover and chill.  Serve on
lettuce leaves.  Yield: 6 to 8 servings.

Mrs. Robert A. Bailey of Tennessee in November, 1987 "Southern Living"
