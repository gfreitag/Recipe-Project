Title: Lamb Shoulder Cushion Roast With Stuffing
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Dress/stuff
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Mint flakes    *1
2 c  Apples, chopped unpeeled
1 c  Bread crumbs, 1/4 "
1/4 c  Milk
4 lb Shoulder of lamb, square cut
2 tb Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
2 tb Butter
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ginger, ground
*1 2 tbs. fresh chopped mint

Debone roast to form a pocket.  Saute onion and celery in butter.  Add
remaining ingredients and toss lightly.

Fill cavity with stuffing, skewer or sew edges together.

Place, fat side up, on rack in shallow pan.  Roast uncovered in 325
F. oven about 2 1/2 hours or 30 to 35 minutes per pound.
