Title: Chinese Style Country Ribs
Categories: Crockpot, Asian, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1    Garlic clove; crushed
4 lb Spareribs, country style
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Orange marmelade
1 tb Catsup
Combine soy sauce, marmalade, catsup and garlic. Brush on both sides
of the ribs. Place in a crockpot and pour remaining sauce over all.
Cover and cook on low for 8 to 10 hours.
