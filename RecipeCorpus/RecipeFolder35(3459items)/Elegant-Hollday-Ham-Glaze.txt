Title: Elegant Hollday Ham Glaze
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 cn Whole-berry crenberry --
Sauce l6 ounces
Drippings from a baked ham
Optional
2 tb Cornstarch -- optional
2 tb Cold water -- optional
Juice and grated peel of 1
Orange
Juice and grated peel of 1
Lemon
1 c  Sweet red wine -- or
Cranberry juice
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
In a saucepan, combine orange and lemon juices and peel, wine or
cranberry juice, corn syrup and mustard. Simmer for 30 minutes. Add
the cranberry sauce and ham drippings if desired; heat through. If a
thicker glaze is desired, bring just to a boil. Combine cornstarch
and water; add to glaze. Cook and stir until slightly thickened.
Serve warm over sliced ham. Yield: 3-1/2 cups
