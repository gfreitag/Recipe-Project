Title: Apricot Oatmeal Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 15 servings
1/4 c  Broken walnuts
3 tb Liquid sucaryl
1    Egg,beaten
1 1/4 c  Skim milk
2 c  Biscuit mix
1 c  Quick-cooking oatmeal
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1/3 c  Dried apricots,chopped
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.Combine biscuit mix,oatmeal,salt,baking
powder,apricots,and walnuts.Combine sucaryl,egg,and milk,blending
well.Add to dry ingredients,stirring only to mix.Turn into a freased
loaf pan. Bake 60 minutes or until done.

per serving; 120 calories, 3.5g protein, 4g fat, 17.5g carbohydrates.

1 serving - 1 bread exchange,  1 fat exchange
