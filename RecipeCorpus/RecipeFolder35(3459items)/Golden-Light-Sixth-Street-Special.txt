Title: Golden Light Sixth Street Special
Categories: Main dish, Route 66, Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Sour Cream
ds Hot Sauce
1 lg Flour Tortilla
1    1/4 Lb Hamburger Patty
1 lg Slice Swiss Cheese
1/2    Tomato -- chopped
Cook and season a long, narrow hamburger patty on grill. Top meat with
cheese during last minute of cooking. Place meat, cheese, chopped
tomato, sour cream and hot sauce on large flour tortilla that has
been heated. Wrap and serve while hot.
