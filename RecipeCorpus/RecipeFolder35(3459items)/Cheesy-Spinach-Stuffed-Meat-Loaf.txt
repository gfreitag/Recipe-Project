Title: Cheesy Spinach-Stuffed Meat Loaf
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 1/2 lb Ground round
3/4 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/4 c  Egg -- substitute
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING:-------------------------------
3 tb Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 ts Italian seasoning
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 pk Frozen spinach
(l0 ounces)chopped, thawed
And well draine
1/2 c  Low-fat mozzarella cheese
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING:-------------------------------
Shredded
3 tb Ketchup
1/4 c  Low-fat mozzarella cheese --
In a large bowl, combine meat loaf ingredients, mixing lightly but
thoroughly. On waxed paper, pat into a 14-in. x 10-in. rectangle. In a
medium bowl, combine filling ingredients; mix well. Spread over
rectangle, leaving 3/4 in. around edges. Starting at short end, roll
up jelly-roll fashion. Press meat mixture over filling at both ends
to seal. Place loaf, seam side down, on a rack in a roasting pan.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 deg. for 1 hour. Spread ketchup on top;
return to the oven for 15 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese: let stand a
few minutes. Cut into 1-in. thick slices. Yield: 6 servings. Diabetic
Exchanges: One serving equals 3 lean meat, 7 starch, 1 vegetable;
also, 259 calories, 776 mg sodium, 39 mg cholesterol, 14 gm
carbohydrate, 30 gm protein, 9 gm fat
