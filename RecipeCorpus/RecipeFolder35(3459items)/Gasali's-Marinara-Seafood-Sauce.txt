Title: Gasali's Marinara Seafood Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Herbs/spice, Italian, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 10 servings
400 g  Cuttlefish
400 g  Shrimp
500 g  Tomato sauce
75 g  Chopped parsley
Salt and pepper
100 ml Olive oil
50 g  Garlic
75 g  Onion
1    Glass white wine
500 g  Clams and mussel meat
400 g  Squid
Fry garlic and onion in olive oil. Add clams, mussels, cuttlefish and
shrimp. Saute for a few minutes. Add white wine, then tomato sauce.
Salt and pepper to season. Just before serving, add in chopped
parsley (do not cook).

Source:  Given to share with the express permission of Chef de Cuisine
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.
