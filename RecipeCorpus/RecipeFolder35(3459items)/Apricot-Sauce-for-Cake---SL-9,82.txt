Title: Apricot Sauce for Cake - SL 9/82
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Salsa/sauce, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 -1/2 cups
1 pn Salt
1/8 ts Almond extract
1 c  Dried apricots
2 c  ;Warm water
1/4 c  Sugar
For pound cake, angel food cake, or ice cream.

Soak apricots in warm water 1 hour; cover and cook over low heat
for 15 to 20 minutes or until tender. Remove from heat.
Pour apricots and liquid into container of an electric blender;
process until smooth.  Stir in remaining ingredients, and mix well.
Serve over cake.  Yield: 1-1/2 cups.
