Title: Ranch Mac 'n' Cheese
Categories: Pasta/rice, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
Cubed
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz
1/2 c  Saltine crackers -- crushed
1 lb Macaroni -- elbow
Cooked and drained
Grated Parmesan cheese
Ranch salad dressing -- mix
1 oz Each
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
2    Envelopes
-
1 ts Lemon pepper
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Garlic salt
1 c  Colby cheese -- cubed
1 c  Monterey jack cheese --
In a dutch oven combine the first eight ingredients. Cook and stir
over medium heat until cheese has melted and mixture begins to
thicken. Fold in the sour cream. Add crackers and macaroni. Cook
until heated through, stirring frequently. Spoon into a serving dish;
sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
