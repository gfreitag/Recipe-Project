Title: Belgian Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Microwave
Yield: 1 dozen
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Butter melted
1/4 c  Cinnamon
1/3 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
Put first six ingredients in bowl.  Stir in milk.  Fill paper lined
plastic muffin cups 1/2 full.  Microwave on high 2-3 minutes,
rotating pan once during cooking.  Roll muffins in melted butter,
then cinnamon From 1990 365 microwave calendar
