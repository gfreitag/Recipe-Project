Title: Betty's Country Inn Salad Dressing Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Route 66
Yield: 20 servings
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Salad Dressing
2 c  Lukewarm Water
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Sugar
4 tb Cocoa
4 c  Flour
4 ts Baking Soda
Sift together the sugar, cocoa, flour, soda and salt. Add salad
dressing, water and vanilla. Stir well. Do not beat!

Grease and flour three 9-inch layer cake pans. Pour batter in pans
and bake in preheated 350 F oven for 30 to 35 mins. Frost with your
favorite frosting.

Makes 3 layers or 20 generous servings.
