Title: Macaroni & Cheese
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
ds Worcestershire sauce
8 oz Macaroni
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Cream
Cook macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and return to
pot. Over low heat, add butter and stir until melted. Add cream and
cheese and heat, stirring constantly, until cheese melts. Season with
a dash or two or Worcestershire Sauce. Serve at once.
