Title: U.S. Senate Bean Soup
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
Or 1/3 cup instant potato
Flakes
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
Parsley or chives for
Garnish
1 lb Great northern beans
2 ea Smoked ham hocks
3 md Onion -- chopped
3    Cloves garlic -- minced
3 ea Celery -- stalks chopped
1/4 c  Fresh parsley -- chopped
1 c  Potatoes, mashed
Place beans and enough water to cover in a saucepan; bring to a boil
and boil for 2 min. Remove from the heat and soak for 1 hr. Drain and
rinse beans. In a large kettle, place beans, ham hocks and 3 quarts
water. Bring to boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 2 hours. Skim
fat if necessary. Add onions, garlic, celery, parsley, potatoes, salt
and pepper; simmer 1 hr. longer. Remove meat and bones from the soup.
Remove meat from the bones; dice and return to kettle. Heat through.
Garnish with parsley or chives. Yield: 8 to 10 servings. (2 1/2
quarts).
