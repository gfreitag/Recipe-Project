Title: Marshmallow Popcorn Balls
Categories: Desserts, Snacks, Kids
Yield: 16 servings
6 qt Popped corn
Vegetable cooking spray
50 lg Marshmallows
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
Combine marshmallows and butter in a Dutch oven; cook over low heat
until marshmallows melt; stirring mixture occasionally.
Place popped corn in a large pan.  Pour hot mixture over popped
corn, tossing to coat.
Coat hands with vegetable cooking spray; shape mixture into balls.
Place on wax paper to cool.  Wrap popcorn balls individually in
colored cellophane or plastic wrap; store in a cool, dry place.
Yield: 6 popcorn balls.
