Title: Easy Chicken & Cheese Melt
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Onion powder
1/8 ts Dry mustard
6    English muffins -- split &
-toasted
10 oz Swanson chunk chicken
8 oz Cheddar cheese -- shredded
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
1/4 c  Green onion -- chopped
Heat oven to 375.  Mix all ingredients except english muffins.  Top
each english muffin half with 1/4 cup of the chicken mixture.  Bake
10 minutes or until heated.
