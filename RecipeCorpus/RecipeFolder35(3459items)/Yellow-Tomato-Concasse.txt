Title: Yellow Tomato Concasse
Categories: Vegetables, Relishes, Tomatoes
Yield: 6 servings
-chop
1/4 c  Fresh chives -- diced fine
4    Whole Summer harvest yellow
-tomatoes -- peel,deseed
Peel and remove the seeds from the tomatoes. Chop the tomatoes
coarsely, until they appear to be "medium dice." Dice the chives to a
fine consistency. Combine the chives and yellow tomatoes.

Serve as part of "Grilled Grouper Savoy Grill" (see recipe of that
name in this cookbook).

: David Leo Banks of Harry's Savoy Grill, Wilmington, DE
