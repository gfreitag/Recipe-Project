Title: Broccoli with Black Olives
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Butter
12    Imported black olives
-pitted and chopped
Freshly ground pepper
Salt
1 md Head of broccoli
-cut into florets
-stems peeled and sliced
-crosswise 1" thick
Bring a large saucepan of water to a boil. Add salt and the broccoli
stems and cook for 1 minute. Add the broccoli florets and cook until
just tender, about 4 minutes. Drain well in a colander and shake dry.
Return the broccoli to the saucepan and toss with the butter. Add the
olives, season with salt and pepper and toss again. Spoon the
broccoli into a dish and serve.
