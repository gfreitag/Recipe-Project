Title: Dan's Peppery London Broll
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Crushed red pepper
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
3/4 lb Beef flank steak
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
With a meat fork, poke holes in both sides of meat. Make a paste with
garlic, seasoned salt and red pepper; rub over both sides of meat.
Place the steak in a gallon-size plastic bag. Add Worcestershire
sauce and close bag. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, turning once.
Remove meat; discard marinade. Broil or grill over hot coals until
meat reaches desired doneness, 4-5 minutes per side. To serve, slice
thinly across the grain. Yield: 2 servings
