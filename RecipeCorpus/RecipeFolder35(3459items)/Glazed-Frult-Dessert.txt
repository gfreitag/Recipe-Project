Title: Glazed Frult Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Mandarin oranges -- 11 oz
Drained
2 ea Bananas -- sliced
Whipped topping
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding mix
3 3/8    Ounces
1 cn Pineapple chunks
20 oz In natural juice, liquid
Drained
In a mixing bowl, combine pudding and pineapple juice. Stir until
thickened. Fold in pineapple, oranges and bananas. Refrigerate until
serving. Garnish with a dollop of whipped topping. Yield: 6 servings.
