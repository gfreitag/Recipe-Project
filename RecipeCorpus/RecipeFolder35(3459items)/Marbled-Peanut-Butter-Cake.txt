Title: Marbled Peanut Butter Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
4    Eggs
1 cn Chocolate frosting
1/4 c  Peanuts -- chopped
1 pk Fudge marble cake mix
1 1/2 c  Peanut butter
1 1/4 c  Water
Heat oven to 350.  Grease and flour 13 x 9 pan.  Beat cake mix, 1 cup
peanut butter, water and eggs in large bowl on low speed 30 seconds.
Beat on medium speed 2 minutes.  Continue as directed on package.
Bake 35 to 40 minutes.  Cool completely.  Mix frosting and 1/2 cup
peanut butter. Spead over top of cake.  Sprinkle with peanuts.
