Title: Honey Mufflns
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/3 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
2 ea Eggs
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and
salt; set aside. In a mixing bowl, beat eggs. Add honey, orange
juice, butter and vanilla; mix well. Stir into dry ingredients just
until moistened. Fill 12 greased muffin cups 2/3 full. Bake at 375
deg. for 15-20 minutes or until golden. Yield: 1 dozen
