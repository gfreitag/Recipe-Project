Title: Norma Alloway's Satellite Cafe Rolls
Categories: Breads, Route 66
Yield: 30 servings
1/4 c  Margarine -- melted
1/2 ts Salt
4 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Milk
1 pk Yeast
3 tb Sugar
3    Eggs -- well-beaten
Warm milk and add yeast and sugar. Let stand a few mins to proof.
Stir in eggs, margarine, salt, and flour. Let rise, shape into buns
and let rise again. Bake at 375 F for 18 to 20 mins. Makes 30 rolls.
