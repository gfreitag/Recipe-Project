Title: Salmon Pasta Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/3 c  Fesh lemon -- or lime juice
1/2 ts Dill weed
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Head lettuce -- torn
1 pk Spiral pasta -- 8 oz
Cooked/drained
1 cn Salmon -- 14 3/4 oz.
1 1/2 c  Cherry tomatoes --
Quartered
1 md Cucumber -- quartered/sliced
1 sm Red onion -- sliced
In a large bowl, toss the pasta, salmon, tomatoes, cucumber and
onion. For dressing, combine the oil, lemon or lime juice, dill,
garlic, salt and pepper; mix well. pour over pasta. Cover and chill.
Serve over lettuce. Yield: 6-8 servings
