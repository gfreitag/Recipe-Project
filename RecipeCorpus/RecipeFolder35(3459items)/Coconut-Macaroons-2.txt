Title: Coconut Macaroons-2
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
ds Salt
1/4 ts Almond Extract
2 c  Coconut
2    Egg Whites
1/3 c  Sugar
2 tb Flour
Heat oven to 325 F. Grease and lightly flour cookie sheet. In medium
bowl, beat egg whites lightly. Add sugar, flour, salt and almond
extract; blend well. Stir in coconut. Drop dough by tablespoonfuls 2
inches apart onto greased and floured cookie sheet. Bake at 325 F for
13 to 17 mins or until set and lightly brown. Immediately remove from
cookie sheet.

Makes 12 cookies.
