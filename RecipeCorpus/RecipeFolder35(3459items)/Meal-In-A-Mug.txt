Title: Meal In A Mug
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
Cut-up
With liquid
1    Envelope sloppy joe mix
1 c  Macaroni -- uncooked elbow
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean
2 c  Water
1 cn Pork and beans -- undrained
1 cn Tomatoes -- 14 1/2 oz
In a large saucepan, brown ground beef; drain. Add water, pork and
beans, tomatoes and sloppy joe mix. Bring to a boil; add macaroni and
reduce heat. Cover and simmer 10 mix or until the macaroni is almost
cooked. Pour into thermoses. Yield: 8 servings.
