Title: Horseradish - Chive Butter - SL 11/86
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 cups
-chopped
2 tb Prepared horseradish
1 c  Butter; softened
1/4 c  Fresh of frozen chives;
Combine all ingredients.  Spoon into butter molds, if desired.
CHill until ready to serve.  Invert molds and press gently to remove
butter. Serve butter at room temperature with smoked turkey.
