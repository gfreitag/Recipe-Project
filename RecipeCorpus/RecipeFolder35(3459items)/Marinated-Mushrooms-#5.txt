Title: Marinated Mushrooms #5
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 3 1/2 cups
1/4 ts Thyme, ground
1    Garlic clove, halved
6    Peppercorns
1    Bay leaf
1 1/2 lb Small mushrooms, fresh
3/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Heinz red wine vinegar
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Basil leaves, dried
Combine all ingredients (except mushrooms) in a large saucepan.  Heat
to boiling.  Cover; simmer 10 minutes.  Add mushrooms; simmer
uncovered for 3 minutes.  Transfer mushrooms and liquid into a bowl.
Cover; chill several hours or overnight, stirring occasionally.
Drain and serve as an appetizer on a relish tray.  Makes about 3 1/2
cups.

Recipe from back label of a bottle of Heinz Gourmet Red Wine Vinegar.
