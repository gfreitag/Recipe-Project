Title: 7 Gables Stuffed Peppers
Categories: Main dish, Route 66, Cajun/creol
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM--------------------STUFFED PEPPERS------------------------------
1/4 c  Bread Crumbs
1/4 ts Seasoned Salt
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 lg Peppers -- cut in half
1 lb Lean Ground Beef
1 1/2 ts Beef Base
2 tb Onions -- diced
1    Egg
3/4 c  Cooked Rice
1/4 ts Garlic Powder
MMMMM---------------------CREOLE SAUCE-------------------------------
1 ts Minced Garlic
1/4 ts Basil
1 tb Sugar
1 cn (20 Oz) Tomatoes
1 cn (10 Oz) Tomato Puree
2 tb Minced Onion
2 tb Minced Green Pepper
Preheat oven to 350 F. Combine meat with all ingredients except
peppers. Stuff peppers with mixture and place in baking dish. Cover
and bake for 15 mins. Uncover peppers and continue cooking until
done, about 30 mins. Meanwhile combine all ingredients for Creole
Sauce and heat in saucepan. Pour sauce over peppers and return to
oven to keep hot until served. Makes 8 servings.
