Title: Onion Dlll Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Cream-style cottage cheese
3/4 c  Sour cream
3 tb Sugar
3 tb Minced dried onion
2 tb Dill seed
1 1/2 tb Butter or margarine
1 pk Active dry yeast -- l/4
Ounce
3 1/2 c  Bread flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 ea Egg -- unbeaten room temp
1/4 c  Water
In bread machine pan, place first four ingredients in order given. In
a saucepan, combine remaining ingredients and heat just until warm
(do not boil). Pour into bread pan. Select "white bread" setting.
Bake according to bread machine directions. Yield: 1 large loaf
