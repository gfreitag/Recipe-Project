Title: Scallops Newport
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
2 oz Smoked salmon
2    Fluid ounces  Beurre blanc
See Recipe
4 oz Sea scallops
4 oz Mushrooms
2 oz Fresh spinach
NOTE: See the recipe for "Beurre Blanc (White Butter Sauce)" in this
cookbook.

Saute scallops. When ready, add mushrooms, spinach, and smoked salmon
and toss.

Add Beurre Blanc and toss well. Serve.
