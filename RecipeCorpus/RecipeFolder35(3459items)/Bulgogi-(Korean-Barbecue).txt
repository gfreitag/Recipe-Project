Title: Bulgogi (Korean Barbecue)
Categories: Meats, Asian, Main dish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Thinly sliced rump beef
MMMMM---------------------SEASONING MIXTURE--------------------------
1 ts Minced ginger
1 ts Sesame seeds
1 tb Toasted and ground sesame
-seeds
1 tb Sake or dry sherry
2 tb Sesame oil
2 tb Sugar
4 tb Soy sauce
4 tb Minced green onions
2 ts Minced garlic
1 ts Minced ginger
ds Pepper
Combine beef and seasoning mixture.  Do this just before cooking
because meat marinated for a long time in soy sauce tends to become
tough. Charcoal broil, pan-broil, or saute.  Serves 4. KYUNG'S KOREAN
