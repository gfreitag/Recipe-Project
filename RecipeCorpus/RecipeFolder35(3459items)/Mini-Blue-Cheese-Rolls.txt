Title: Mini Blue Cheese Rolls
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
-
Refrigerated breadsticks --
Ll ounces
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Blue cheese -- 4 ounces
1    Tube
In a saucepan, melt the butter and blue cheese over low heat. Unroll
dough and cut each breadstick into six pieces; place in a foil-lined
11-in. x 7-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Pour cheese mixture over dough.
Bake at 400 deg. for 20 minutes or until butter is absorbed and rolls
are lightly browned. Carefully lift foil out of pan; place on a
serving dish. Serve hot. Yield: 4-6 servings
