Title: Two Cranberry Relishes
Categories: Holiday, Relishes
Yield: 4 cups
MMMMM----------------------CELERY/CRANBERRY---------------------------
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Lemon juice
4 c  Fresh cranberries
2 c  Coarsely chopped celery
1 md Unpared apple, cut up
MMMMM--------------------GRAPEFRUIT/CRANBERRY-------------------------
1 lg Grapefruit
2 c  Fresh cranberries
3/4 c  Sugar
Celery Cranberry Relish

Using coarse blade of food chopper, grind cranberries, celery and
apple. Stir in sugar and lemon juice.  Cover and refrigerate.

Grapefruit Cranberry Relish

Put cranberries through food chopper.  Add sugar, mix well.

Peel, section and dice grapefruit, stir into cranberries.  Chill.
