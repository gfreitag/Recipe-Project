Title: Four Bean Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
1 cn French-style green beans --
Drained
1 bn Green onion -- sliced
1 md Green pepper -- chopped
1 c  Chili sauce
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1 cn Pork and beans -- 16 oz
Undrained
1 cn Pinto beans -- 15 oz.
Rinsed and drained
1 cn Kidney beans -- 15 oz.
Rinsed and drained
Combine all ingredients in a ungreased 2 qt. casserole. Bake,
uncovered, at 350 deg. for 1 hr. or until heated through and bubby.
Yield: 8 to 10 servings.
