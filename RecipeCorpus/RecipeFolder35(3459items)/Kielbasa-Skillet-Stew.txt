Title: Kielbasa Skillet Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
2 cn Tomato sauce -- 8 oz each
1 cn Green chiles -- 4 oz
Chopped
2 md Carrots -- thinly sliced
1/2 md Green pepper -- chopped
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/8 ts Pepper
5 ea Bacon slices
1 md Chopped onion
1 lb Sausage links -- thinly
Sliced
Smoked cooked kielbasa
2 cn Great northern beans -- 15
1/2 oz Each
Undrained
In a 12 in. skillet, cook bacon until crisp; remove to paper towel to
drain. In drippings, cook onion and sausage until the onion is tender;
drain. Stir in remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat;
cover and simmer for 45 min. or until vegetables are tender, stirring
occasionally. Crumble bacon and sprinkle on top. Yield: 6-8 servings.
