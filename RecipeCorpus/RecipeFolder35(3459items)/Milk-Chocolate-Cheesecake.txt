Title: Milk Chocolate Cheesecake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
Whipped topping
Strawberries -- for garnish
1 pk Fudge marble cake mix --
Divided
16 oz Cream cheese -- softened
8 oz Milk chocolate -- melted
3    Eggs
2/3 c  Heavy cream
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease and flour a 13 x 9 pan.  For filling,
combine cocoa packet, 1/4 cup dry cake mix, cream cheese and melted
chocolate in large bowl.  Beat at high speed with electric mixer for
2 minutes.  Add eggs and cream.  Beat 1 minute at high speed.  For
crust, stir remaining dry cake mix and melted butter in medium bowl.
Mixture will be crumbly. Sprinkle mixture into pan.  Pour filling
over crust mixture.  Bake at 350 for 30 to 35 minutes.  Cool in pan.
Refrigerate until chilled.  Garnish each serving with whipped cream
and strawberries.
