Title: Witmor Farms Macaroni Salad
Categories: Route 66, Salads
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Sweet Pickle Relish
2 tb Chopped Pimento
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Tarragon Vinegar
3/4 ts Sugar
ds White Pepper
1/2 c  American Cheese -- shredded
2 c  Macaroni
Water To Cook Macaroni
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1 tb Salad Oil
1/4 c  Chopped Onion
1 c  Chopped Celery
Cook macaroni in boiling water until done, about 7 mins. Drain and add
salt, Worcestershire and salad oil. Stir in onion, celery, relish and
pimento. In a separate container, combine mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar,
and pepper. Stir into salad and add shredded cheese. Chill before
serving.

Makes 8 to 10 servings.
