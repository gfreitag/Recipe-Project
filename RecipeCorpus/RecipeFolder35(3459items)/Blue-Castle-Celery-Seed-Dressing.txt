Title: Blue Castle Celery Seed Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Route 66, Salads
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Dry Mustard
1 tb Celery Seed
1 c  Oil
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Onion -- grated
1 tb Salt
3/4 c  Vinegar
Combine all ingredients but the oil in bowl of an electric mixer. Set
mixer at low speed and combine. Then add oil very slowly and beat
until all oil is combined. Makes 2 cups.
