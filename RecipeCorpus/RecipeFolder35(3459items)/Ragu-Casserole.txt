Title: Ragu Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Casseroles, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Italian seasoning
16 oz Ragu sauce
1 1/2    Inch chunk Velveeta cheese
Shredded cheddar cheese
8 oz Thin noodles
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1/4 c  Chopped onion
Green pepper to taste
Salt and pepper to taste
Brown beef, onion, green pepper, add salt, pepper and Italian
seasoning, Ragu and Velveeta.  Simmer until cheese is melted.  Cook
noodles. Layer noodles and meat sauce in greased 2 quart casserole
dish then top with cheddar cheese.  Bake in 350 degree oven for 25
minutes. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   10-26-95
