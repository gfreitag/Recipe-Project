Title: Jalapeno Corn Muffins
Categories: Cajun/creol, Breads, Muffins
Yield: 12 muffins
1 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 tb Baking powder
1 ts Salt
3/4 c  Milk
1 tb Veg oil
1 tb Softened butter
4 lg Eggs
2 tb Seeded and minced
Jalapeno peppers
2 c  Corn kernels (at room temp)
1 c  All purpose flour
Preheat oven to 375.  Grease a 12 muffin tin with the softened butter.

In large bowl, whisk the eggs - add the jalapenos and corn. Stir in
the flour, cornmeal, baking powder and the salt.  Add the milk and
oil. Stir just enough to mix well.

Evenly divide mixture into 12 muffin cups.

Bake until gold about 25 mins.  Cool slightly before serving.
