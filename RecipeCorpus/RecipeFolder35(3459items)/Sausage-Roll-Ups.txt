Title: Sausage Roll Ups
Categories: Meats
Yield: 3 servings
Cinnamon
Sugar
Powdered sugar
Pancake mix:
1 cn Apple pie filling
1 pk Sausage links
Make pancakes, heat apple pie filling, add sugar and cinnamon to
taste. Roll sausage links inside pancake, put apple mixture on top
pancake. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top and whip cream if desired.
