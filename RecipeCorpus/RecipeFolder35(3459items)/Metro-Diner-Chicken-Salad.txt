Title: Metro Diner Chicken Salad
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Route 66, Salads
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
3/4 ts Onion Powder
3/4 ts Pepper
1 1/2 c  Salad Dressing
2 1/2 lb Chicken
1 c  Onion -- finely diced
1 c  Cheese -- grated
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
1 c  Celery -- finely diced
Boil the chicken, cool, bone and chop into 1/4 inch pieces. Add onion,
cheese, pecans and celery. Blend well. Add remaining seasonings and
mix. Stir in salad dressing and blend thoroughly. Makes 10 servings.
