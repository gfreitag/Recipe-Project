Title: Continental Zucchini
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
oz Drained
1 ts Salt -- optional
1/4 ts Lemon pepper
1/2 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
1 tb Cooking oil
1 lb Zucchini -- 3 small cubed
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
1    Jar pimiento -- 2 oz chopped
Drained
1 cn Whole kernel corn -- 15-1/2
Heat oil in a large skillet. Saute zucchini and garlic for 3-4
minutes. Add pimiento, corn, salt if desired and lemon pepper; cook
and stir for 2-3 minutes or until zucchini is tender. Sprinkle with
cheese and heat until cheese melts. Yield: 6 servings. Diabetic
Exchanges: One serving (prepared with low-fat mozzarella cheese and
without added salt) equals 1 vegetable, 1 meat, 1/2 starch; also, 131
calories, 107 mg sodium, 10 mg cholesterol, 15 gm carbohydrate, 8 gm
protein, 6 gm fat
