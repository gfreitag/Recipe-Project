Title: Marshmallow Dellghts
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
Refrigerated crescent rolls
8    Ounces
1    Tube
-
1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Ground cinnamon
8 lg Marshmallows
Separate rolls into eight triangles. Combine sugar and cinnamon. Dip
each marshmallow into butter, roll in cinnamon-sugar and place on a
triangle. Pinch dough around marshmallow, sealing all edges. Dip tops
of dough into remaining butter and cinnamon-sugar. Place with sugar
side up in greased muffin cups. Bake at 375 deg. for 13-15 minutes.
Serve warm. Yield: 8 servings
