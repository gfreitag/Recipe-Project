Title: Cherry Crumb Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 16 servings
Blueberry 21 oz
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
Whipped cream or ice cream,
Optional
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Chilled
1 pk Yellow cake mix -- 18 1/2
Oz
1 cn Cherry pie filling -- or
In a mixing bowl, cut butter into cake mix as for pastry dough. Set
aside 1 cup. Pat remaining crumbs onto the bottom and 1/2 in. up the
sides of a greased 13x9x2 in. baking pan. Spread pie filling over
crust. Combine the walnuts with reserved crumbs; sprinkle over top.
Bake at 350 deg. for 30-35 min. Serve warm with whipped cream or ice
cream if desired. Yield: 12 to 16 servings.
