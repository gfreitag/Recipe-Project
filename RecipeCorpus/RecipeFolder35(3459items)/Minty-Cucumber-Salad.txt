Title: Minty Cucumber Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1    Orange -- zest only, grated
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Sugar
3 lg Cucumbers -- peel, halve &
-seed
1/2 c  Mint leaves -- chopped
1/4 c  Parsley -- chopped
Cut cucumber halves crosswise into crescents.  Toss them in a bowl
with the mint, parsley and orange rind.  Whisk oil, vinegar and sugar
in a small bowl and pour over salad.  Cover salad and refrigerate for
at least 4 hours.  Toss again before serving very cold.
