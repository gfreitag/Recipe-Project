Title: Cherry Chocolate Clusters
Categories: Chocolate, Can/preserv, Candies
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------FROM:  PA. RED CHERRY GROWER---------------------
2 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Light corn syrup
1 ea 6 oz. pkg. chocolate chips
2 c  Dried cherries
2 tb Confectioners sugar
Combine chips and syrups in the top of a double boiler.  Stir until
chocolate is melted. Remove from heat and add vanilla, sugar and
cherries. Drop by spoonfuls on a buttered cookie sheet. Chill. Yields
50 cookies. From: Lancaster Farming
