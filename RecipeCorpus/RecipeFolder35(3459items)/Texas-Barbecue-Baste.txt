Title: Texas Barbecue Baste
Categories: Bbq/grill, Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Grated lemon rind
Cayenne to taste
Salt and pepper to taste
2/3 c  Cider vinegar
1/3 c  Water
1 tb Butter or margarine
Combine all ingredients in saucepan.  Heat until butter is melted.
Use with beef, chicken, pork, or doves
From: The Pedernales Country Cookbook
