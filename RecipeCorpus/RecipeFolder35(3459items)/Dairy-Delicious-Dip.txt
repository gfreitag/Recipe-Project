Title: Dairy Delicious Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
2 tb Maple syrup
1 pk Cream cheese -- 8 ounces
Softened
1/2 c  Sour cream
In a small mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, sour cream, sugars and
syrup to taste; beat until smooth. Chill. Serve with fresh fruit.
Yield: 2 cups
