Title: Frittata for 8
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Italian, Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Mozzarella (shredded)
2 c  Vegetable strips
1/4 c  Parmesan
1 tb Olive oil extra virgin
10 ea Eggs medium
1 tb Chives
*** use same method to oven roast the vegetables as discribed in
Roasted Vegetable Lasagna

Heat oil in non stick pan. beat eggs and pour into pan, stirring
constantly for about 15 seconds. top with vegetable strips and
chives. cook 5 minutes, medium heat.  top wwith cheese, broil until
eggs are completely set, and cheese is bubbly
