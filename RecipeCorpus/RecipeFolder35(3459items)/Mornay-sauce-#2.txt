Title: Mornay sauce #2
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
Mustard
Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Basic white sauce [Bechamel]
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; grated
Prepare basic white sauce and stir in cheese until melted. Optionally
season with mustard or worcestershire sauce.
