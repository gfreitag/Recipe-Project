Title: Beef and Cheese Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Ground beef cooked
1 lb Sausage cooked and sliced
12 oz Canned tomatoes with chilies
16 oz Processed cheese cubed
Put tomatoes and cheese in glass bowl dish and microwave on medium 6
to 8 minutes, stirring  twice during cooking.  Add beef and sausage
and microwave on medium 2 minutes.  Let stand 3 minutes.  Serve with
chips Submitted By ELEANOR MURRAY   On   10-07-95
