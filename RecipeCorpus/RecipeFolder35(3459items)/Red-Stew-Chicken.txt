Title: Red-Stew Chicken
Categories: Chinese, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
-removed
3 c  Boiling Water
4 lb (To 6) Stewing Hen, Cleaned,
Towel-Dried And -- loose fat
MMMMM----------------------SEASONINGS--------------------------------
1/3 c  Soy Sauce
2 ts Honey
4 sl Ginger
2 tb Sherry
1. Place the hen in a dutch oven with the boiling water.

2. After the mixture comes to a boil, add all seasonings and return
to a boil.

3. Reduce the heat, cover and simmer for 2 hours, or until it is done.

4. Serve the chicken with boiled white steamed rice, and the cooking
sauce (skim off the fat).
