Title: Gingered Turnips
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Ground ginger
2 ts Soy sauce
2 lb Turnips, peeled and cubed
1/4 c  Beef stock
1/2 ts Sugar
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan, simmer until turnips are
tender (15 minutes).  Drain, mash until fluffy, adding cooking liquid
as needed for desired consistency. Randy Rigg
