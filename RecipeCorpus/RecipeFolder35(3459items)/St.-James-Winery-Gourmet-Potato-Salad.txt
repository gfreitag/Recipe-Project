Title: St. James Winery Gourmet Potato Salad
Categories: Route 66, Salads, Vegetables, Potatoes
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Dried Whole Tarragon
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts White Pepper
1 tb (To 2) Dijon Mustard
1/4 c  Green Onions -- thinly
-sliced
1/4 c  Fresh Parsley -- minced
12 sm (To 14) New Potatoes,
-About 1 1/2 Lbs) --
-unpeeled
3 tb St. James Country White
-Wine
1/4 c  Olive Oil
2 tb Wine Vinegar
Wash potatoes and cook in boiling water until tender. Cut into
1/4-inch slices, add wine and let stand for 10 mins. Combine olive
oil and next five ingredients and stir well. Pour over potato
mixture. Add green onions and parsley and stir gently. Makes 8
servings.
