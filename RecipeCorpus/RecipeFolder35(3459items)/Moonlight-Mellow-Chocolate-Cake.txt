Title: Moonlight Mellow Chocolate Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 16 servings
Softened
1/2 c  Water
3    Eggs
1/2 c  Nuts -- chopped
1 pk German chocolate cake mix
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Mashed potatoes -- cold
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE:--------------------------------
2 tb Milk
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1 tb Butter or margarine
Heat oven to 350.  Grease and flour a bundt pan.  In a large bowl,
combine cake mix, cinnamon, nutmeg, potatoes, 1/2 cup butter, water
and eggs at low speed until moistened; beat 2 minutes at high speed.
Stir in nuts. Pour into greased and floured pan.  Bake at 350 for 40
to 50 minutes. Cool upright in pan 25 minutes; invert onto serving
plate.  Cool completely. In small saucepan, combine chocolate chips,
butter and milk. Cook over low heat until chocolate melts, stirring
constantly.  Remove from heat; stir in powdered sugar until smooth.
Add a few drops milk if needed. Drizzle over cooled cake.
