Title: Peanut Butter Brownie Cups
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 16 servings
Brownie mix is best
16    Miniature peanut butter cups
1 pk Fudge brownie mix 21-1/2 oz
Betty Crocker's Fudge
Mix brownie batter according to package directions.  Fill paper-lined
or foil-lined muffin cups two-thirds full.  Remove wrappers from
peanut butter cups; set one in each muffin cup and press down until
batter meets the top edge of the candy.  Bake at 350F for 20-25
minutes. From Country Woman J/F '94 Typed for you by Dottie Theriault
