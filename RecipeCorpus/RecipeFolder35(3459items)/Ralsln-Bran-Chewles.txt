Title: Ralsln Bran Chewles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
3 c  Raisin Bran cereal
3/4 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
2 tb Honey
2 ts Vanilla extract
In a mixing bowl, cream shortening and sugars. Add eggs, honey and
vanilla; mix well. Combine flour, baking soda and salt; add to
creamed mixture. Stir in cereal. Fold in raisins and walnuts. Drop by
teaspoonfuis onto greased baking sheets. Bake at 350 deg. for 12-14
minutes or until done. Yield: 4 dozen
