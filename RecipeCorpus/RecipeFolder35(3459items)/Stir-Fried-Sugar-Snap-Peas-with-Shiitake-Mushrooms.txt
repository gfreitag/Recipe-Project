Title: Stir-Fried Sugar Snap Peas with Shiitake Mushrooms
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
-thickly sliced
1/2 lb Sugar snap peas
-or snow peas
-(abt 2 1/2 cups)
-strings removed
1/2 ts Oriental sesame oil
2 ts Peanut oil
1/3 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Sugar
1 c  Shiitake mushrooms
-(abt 2oz)
1. Place a wok or large cast-iron skillet over high heat for 1
minute. Add the peanut oil and turn the heat to low. Add the salt,
pepper, and sugar. Stir for a few seconds to dissolve.

2. Add the mushrooms and stir-fry until they soften, about 2 minutes.
Remove from the heat and add the sesame oil. Serve hot or at room
temperature.
