Title: French Dresslng
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Paprika
2 ts Salt
1 sm Onion -- quartered
1 c  Vegetable oil
2/3 c  Ketchup
2/3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Vinegar
Combine all ingredients in a blender container. Cover and blend at
medium-high until well mixed. Chill before serving. Store in a covered
container in the refrigerator. Yield: 3 cups.
