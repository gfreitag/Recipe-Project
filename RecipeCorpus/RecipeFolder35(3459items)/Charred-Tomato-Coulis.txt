Title: Charred Tomato Coulis
Categories: Vegetables, Relishes, Tomatoes
Yield: 6 servings
-mixture (5:1) -- to taste
2    Fluid ounces  Extra virgin
-olive oil
2    Summer harvest tomatoes --
-peeled & deseeded
1 ds Salt and white pepper
TO PREPARE: Peel and remove the seeds from all the tomatoes. Place the
tomatoes, extra virgin olive oil, and salt and white pepper mixture
in a food processor and puree to a fine texture. Reserve until ready
to assemble.

Serve as part of "Grilled Grouper Savoy Grill" (see recipe of that
name in this cookbook).

: David Leo Banks of Harry's Savoy Grill, Wilmington, DE
