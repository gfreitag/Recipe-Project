Title: Wide-A-Wake Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Route 66, Bbq/grill
Yield: 2 servings
2 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 ts Meat Smoke
1 ts Flour
1/4 c  Water
3 tb Onion -- finely chopped
1/2 ts Salt
4 tb Celery -- finely chopped
1/2 tb Cain's Barbecue Sauce
1/4    Green Pepper -- finely
Chopped
Combine all ingredients and cook slowly for 40 mins. Store covered in
refrigerator until needed. Makes 2 1/2 cups.
