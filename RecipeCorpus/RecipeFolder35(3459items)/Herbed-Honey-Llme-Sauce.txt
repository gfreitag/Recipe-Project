Title: Herbed Honey Llme Sauce
Categories: Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Fresh rosemary -- minced
Or 1/4 teaspoon dried
Rosemary, crushed
1/2 ts Salt
ds Pepper
1 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Water
1/2 c  Minced onion
1 tb Olive oil
1 c  Chicken broth -- or dry
White wine
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Lime juice
2 ts Dry mustard
In a saucepan, saute onion in olive oil until tender. Stir in broth or
wine, honey, lime juice, mustard, rosemary, salt and pepper; bring to
a boil. Reduce heat. Combine cornstarch and water; stir into sauce.
Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened. Serve over turkey,
chicken, fish or pork. Cover and refrigerate leftovers. Yield: about
1-1/2 cups
