Title: Caramel Apple Crisp
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
40 ea Caramel candy -- quartered
9 c  Apple -- sliced peeled
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING:-------------------------------
2/3 c  Instant oats, dry
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  All-purpose flour
3 tb Butter or margarine
In a bowl, combime flour, sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg; add caramels
and stir to coat. In another bowl, toss apples with orange juice. Add
caramel mixture and mix. Spread into a greased 13x9x2 inch baking
pan. For topping, combine sugar and flour in a small bowl; cut in
butter until crumbly. Add oats and walnuts; sprinkle over apples.
Bake at 350 deg. for 45 min. or until apples are tender. Yield: 16 to
20 servings.
