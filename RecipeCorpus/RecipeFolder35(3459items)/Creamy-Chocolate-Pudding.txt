Title: Creamy Chocolate Pudding
Categories: Puddings
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Skim milk
3 tb Liquid sucaryl
1 ts Vanilla
5 tb Flour
1/3 c  Cocoa
1/4 ts Salt
Combine flour,cocoa,and salt in saucepan.Slowly add milk and
Sucaryl.Cook over medium heat,stirring comstantly until thick.Remove
from heat; add the vanilla.Pousr into serving dish or individual
dessert dishes; chill.(Pudding will thicken the more it is
chilled.)If desired garnish with Low-calorie whipped topping.

Per servings: Calories 82 Protein 5.5 g Fat 1.5g Carbohydrates 13.5g
(with sugar   190 calories)

1 serving =  1/2 bread exchange
=  1/2 skim milk

Low sodium omit the salt
