Title: Beans With A Difference (Microwave)
Categories: Microwave, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
2    Spring onions (or chives) --
-finely chopped
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Cooked green beans
2 tb Sour cream
2 tb Cream cheese
1/4 ts Curry powder
Mix all ingredients together except beans. Place in a glass jug.  Heat
on HIGH 30-40 seconds.  Pour over beans, mix in if wished.
If cooking conventionally, heat over very low heat.

Doreen Randal  5 Apr 1994
