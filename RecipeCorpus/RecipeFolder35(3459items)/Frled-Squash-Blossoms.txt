Title: Frled Squash Blossoms
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Vegetable oil
Additional oil for frying
12 lg Freshly picked squash
Blossoms
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Ground cumin
1 ea Egg
In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking powder, garlic salt and
cumin. In another bowl, beat egg, milk and oil; add to dry
ingredients and stir until smooth. In a skillet, heat 2 in. of oil to
375 deg.. Dip blossoms into batter and fry in oil a few at a time
until crisp. Drain on paper towels. Keep warm until serving. Yield: 4
servings
