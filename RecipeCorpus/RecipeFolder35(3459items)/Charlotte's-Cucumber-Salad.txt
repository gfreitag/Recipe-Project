Title: Charlotte's Cucumber Salad
Categories: Route 66, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Dill Seed
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2    To 4 Cucumbers
1 c  Boiling Water
3/4 c  Sour Cream
2 tb Vinegar
Peel cucumbers and slice very thin. Pour boiling water over them.
Drain immediately and cover with ice water. Drain again and dry. Mix
together sour cream, vinegar, dill, sugar, salt, and pepper. Toss
with cucmbers. Chill 30 mins and enjoy! Makes 6 to 8 servings.
