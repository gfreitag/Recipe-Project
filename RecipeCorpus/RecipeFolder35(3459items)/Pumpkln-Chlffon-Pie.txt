Title: Pumpkln Chlffon Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 3/8 oz Each
1 cn Pumpkin -- l6 ounces
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Ground cloves
Chopped nuts and/or
Additional
Whipped topping, optional
1 pk Cream cheese -- 3 oz
Softened
1 tb Sugar
1 ct Frozen whipped topping -- 4
oz Thawed
1 ea Graham cracker crust -- 8 or
9    Inches
1 c  Cold milk
2 pk Instant vanilla pudding mix
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add
whipped topping and mix well. Spread into crust. In another bowl,
bear milk and pudding mixes on low speed until combined; beat on high
for 2 minutes. Let stand 3 minutes. Stir in pumpkin and spices; mix
well. Spread over cream cheese layer. Chill. Garnish with nuts and/or
whipped topping if desired. Yield: 6-8 servings
