Title: Turkey - Asparagus Casserole - SL 11/86
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
-;toasted
10 oz Package frozen asparagus;
-thawed, drained, and cut
-into 1-inch pieces
1/4 c  Chopped pimiento; drained
2 1/2 c  Chopped cooked turkey
10 3/4 oz Can cream of mushroom soup;
-undiluted
10 3/4 oz Can cream of celery soup;
-undiluted
1/4 c  Plus 2 tbsp slivered almonds  2 13/16 oz Can French-fried onion rings
Combine first 4 ingredients in a bowl; stir well.  Spoon half of
mixture into a greased 8-inch square baking dish.
Combine asparagus and pimiento; stir gently.  Sprinkle over
mixture in baking dish.  Spoon remaining turkey mixture on top. Cover
and bake at 350F for 35 minutes; uncover.  Sprinkle with onion rings,
and bake, uncovered, an additional 10 minutes.  Yield: 4 servings.

From Helen Goggans of Arkansas, in November, 1986 "Southern Living".
