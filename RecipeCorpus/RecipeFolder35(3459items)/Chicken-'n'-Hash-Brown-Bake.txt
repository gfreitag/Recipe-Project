Title: Chicken 'n' Hash Brown Bake
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Soup -- 10-3/4 ounces
Undilu
2 ts Instant chicken bouillon
Granules
2 tb Onion -- finely chopped
2 tb Sweet red pepper -- finely
Chopped
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
Paprika
1/4 c  Almonds -- sliced
1 pk Frozen Southern-style hash
Brown potatoes -- 32 ounces
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 c  Chicken -- diced cooked
1 cn Sliced mushrooms -- 4 ounces
Drained
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 ounces
2 c  Chicken broth -- or stock
1 cn Condensed cream of chicken
Thaw hash browns overnight in refrigerator. Layer in an ungreased
13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Place chicken and mushrooms over the hash browns. Stir together sour
cream, broth, soup, bouillon, onion, red pepper and garlic; pour over
all. Sprinkle with paprika and almonds. Bake, uncovered, at 350 deg.
for 50-60 minutes or until heated through. Yield: 8-10 servings
