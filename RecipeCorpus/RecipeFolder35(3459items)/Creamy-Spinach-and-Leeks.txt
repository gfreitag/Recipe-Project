Title: Creamy Spinach and Leeks
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
2 lb Spinach; chopped
1 c  Milk
2 tb Parmesan cheese; grated
2    Leeks
3 tb Butter; or marg.
1 cl Garlic; minced
3 tb Flour
1/4 ts Salt
Trim and wash leeks. Slice into rings. Melt butter in a large skillet
and cook leeks and garlic 10 min. In a small bowl combine flour and
seasonings. Sprinkle over leeks and cook, stirring, 2 min. Add
spinach and milk and cook 5 min until spinach tender and sauce
thickens. Stir in Parmesan and serve.

From Women's World, Aug 29, 95. Adapted and posted by Jim Weller.
