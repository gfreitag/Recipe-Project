Title: Sauerkraut with Onion Gravy
Categories: Vegetables, Oldies
Yield: 1 serving
Pepper;
2 tb Flour;
1 c  Boiling water -=OR=-
1 c  Soup stock;
1 qt Plain or canned sauerkraut;
2 tb Butter -=OR=-
2 tb Margarine;
1/2 c  Onion; minced
Cover sauerkraut with cold water; bring to a boil and simmer five
minutes, then drain.  In a the meantime prepare the gravy as fellows:
MELT the butter or margarine in a small frying pan.  Add the onion
and fry until yellowed.   Then stir in a few grains of pepper, and
the flour to make a paste; gradually add the water or stock.  When
boiling rapidly stir into the sauerkraut.

Source: The Serive Cookbook #2 By Mrs. Ida Baily Allen, F. W.
Woolworth Co. I935
