Title: Dllly Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Green pepper -- chopped
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Prepared mustard
1 ts Salt
Paprika
6 ea Green pepper strips -- 1/4
Inch wide
6 lg Red potatoes
Boiled, peeled and diced
4 ea Celery ribs -- diced
3 md Dill pickles -- finely
Chopped
1    Jar pimientos -- 4oz.
Drained/diced
3 ea Hard-cooked eggs -- diced
In a large bowl. combine the potatoes, celery, pickles, pimientos,
eggs and the chopped green pepper. In a small bowl, combine
mayonnaise, mustard and salt; add to the potato mixture and mix well.
Spoon into a serving bowl. Sprinkle with paprika. Arrange green
pepper strips in the center in the shape of wheel spokes. Chill,
Yield: 8 servings
