Title: Route 66 Pig Hip Sauce
Categories: Route 66
Yield: 1 servings
*****  NONE  *****
Chill a stainless steel mixing bowl until very cold. Take 1 large egg
from refrigerator and using an electric mixer, beat egg thoroughly.
While continuing to beat, slowly add 3 cups of good quality vegetable
oil and 7 ounces of Heinz catsup. To this, add 2 ounces of
Worcestershire sauce, 1/2 cup of sugar, and a pinch of salt. Serve
over thinly sliced ham. Makes 1 quart.
