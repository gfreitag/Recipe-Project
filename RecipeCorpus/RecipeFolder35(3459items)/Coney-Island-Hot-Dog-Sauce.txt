Title: Coney Island Hot Dog Sauce
Categories: Main dish, Salsa/sauce, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Chilli powder
1 tb Prepared mustard
1    6 oz. can tomato paste
1    6 oz. can water
13/16    Hot dogs, as you want
1 tb Butter
1 tb Margarine
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
2    Onions, chopped
1    Clove garlic, crushed
-salt & pepper to taste
In a saucepan, combine all ingredients; but the hot dogs.  Simmer
until thick.  DO NOT BROWN GROUND BEEF FIRST!  In a food processor
fitted with a metal blade or a meat grinder, grind the hot dogs & add
to the sauce; stir. Cook 15 minutes longer.

Serve this sauce over hot dogs in buns.
