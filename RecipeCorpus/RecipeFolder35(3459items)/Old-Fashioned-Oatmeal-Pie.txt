Title: Old-Fashioned Oatmeal Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Softened
1/2 c  Currants
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 ea Unbaked pie pastry 9 inches
Whipped cream, optional
2 ea Eggs
3/4 c  Honey
3/4 c  Quick-cooking oats
3/4 c  Flaked coconut
3/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
In a mixing bowl, beat eggs. Add honey, oats, coconut, brown sugar,
butter, currants and walnuts; mix well. Pour into pie shell. Bake at
350 deg. for 40-45 minutes or until browned and a knife inserted near
the center comes out clean. Cool. Serve with a dollop of whipped
cream if desired. Yield: 8 servings
