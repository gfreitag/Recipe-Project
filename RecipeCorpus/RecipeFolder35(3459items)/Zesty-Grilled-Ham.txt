Title: Zesty Grilled Ham
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1    To 1-1/2 pounds and 1 inch
Thick
Cooked ham steak
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1/3 c  Prepared horseradish
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1    Fully
In a small saucepan, bring brown sugar, horseradish and lemon juice
to a boil. Brush over both sides of ham. Grill over medium-hot coals,
turning once, until heated through and well glazed, about 20-25
minutes. Yield: 4 servings
