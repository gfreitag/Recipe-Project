Title: Mozzarella Meat Loaf
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 sl Mozzarella cheese -- halved
Green pepper rings,
Optional
Sliced mushrooms, optional
2 tb Butter or margarine --
Optional
Chopped fresh parsley,
Optional
2 lb Lean ground beef
2 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
1 c  Saltine cracker crumbs
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Dried oregano
1 cn Pizza sauce -- 8 ounces
Mix beef, eggs, crumbs, milk, Parmesan cheese, onion, salt and
oregano. Shape into a loaf and place in a greased 9-in. x 5-in. x
3-in. loaf pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 1-1/4 hours or until no pink
remains; drain. Spoon pizza sauce over loaf and top with mozzarella
cheese slices. Return to the oven for 10 minutes or until the cheese
is melted. Meanwhile, if desired, saute green pepper and mushrooms in
butter; arrange on top of meat loaf. Sprinkle with parsley if
desired. Yield: 8-10 servings
