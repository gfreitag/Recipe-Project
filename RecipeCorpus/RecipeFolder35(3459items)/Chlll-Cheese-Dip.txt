Title: Chlll Cheese Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Chopped
1/2 c  Salsa
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- 4 ounces
Shredded
Tortilla chips
1 pk Cream cheese -- 8 ounces
Softened
1 cn Chili con Carne -- l5
Ounces
1 cn Green chilies -- 4 ounces
Spread cream cheese in the bottom of an 8-in. square baking dish.
Layer with chili, green chilies and salsa. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake
at 350 deg. for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted and dip is
warmed. Serve with tortilla chips. Yield: 8-10 servings
