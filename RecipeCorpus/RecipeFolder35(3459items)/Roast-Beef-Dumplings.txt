Title: Roast Beef Dumplings
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Pasta/rice
Yield: 3 servings
Grated rind of 1/2 lemon
4    Strips bacon, chopped
1/4 c  Bacon fat
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 1/2 c  Cooked rice
2 lb Ground cooked beef
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2    Eggs
(The beef can be raw or cooked) Combine the rice, beef, salt, pepper,
eggs, and lemon rind.  Fry the bacon until crisp; then add and mix
well. Form dumplings the size of an egg.  Flatten these a little with
a spoon. Then brown on both sides in hot bacon fat.  Remove to a warm
platter. Stir the lemon juice and sour cream into the fat to make a
gravy and pour over dumplings. Randy Rigg
