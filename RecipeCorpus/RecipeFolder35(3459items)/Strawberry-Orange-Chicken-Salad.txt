Title: Strawberry-Orange Chicken Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 c  Pancake syrup --
Strawberry-flavored
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Cashews -- or pecans
Optional
2 c  Spinach -- torn
2 c  Leaf lettuce -- torn
3/4 c  Cooked chicken -- cubed
2/3 c  Fresh strawberries --
Sliced
1 ea Orange -- cut into segments
Divide spinach and lettuce among two salad plates. Arrange chicken
strawberries and oranges on lettuce. combine syrup and vinegar;
drizzle over salads. Top with nuts if desired. Yield: 2 servings.
