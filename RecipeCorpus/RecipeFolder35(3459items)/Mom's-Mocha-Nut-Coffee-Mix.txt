Title: Mom's Mocha-Nut Coffee Mix
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 6 cups mix
2 1/2 ts Vanilla-butter nut extract
1 pk Instant hot cocoa mix; 30oz
1 1/4 c  Instant coffee granules
In a medium bowl, combine all the ingredients until well blended.
Store in an airtight container.  Give with serving instructions.

Makes about 6 cups of coffee mix.

To Serve:  Stir 3 rounded teaspoons coffee mix into 6oz of hot water.

Source:  A pamphlet Mom received in the mail.
