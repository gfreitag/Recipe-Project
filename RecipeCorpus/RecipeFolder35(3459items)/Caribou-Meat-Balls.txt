Title: Caribou Meat Balls
Categories: Game, Appetizers
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Prepared mustard
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c  Catsup  OR
1    Egg   AND
1 tb Tomato paste
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 lb Ground caribou
1/4 lb Ground pork
1/4 c  Quick oatmeal, Red River
-cereal, bread or
-cracker crumbs
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
1/2 ts Salt
Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl; shape the mixture into
small balls, using a teaspoon of meat mixture for each. Cook in a
skillet over medium heat in a little hot fat. Turn frequently until
brown on all sides, 5 to 7 minutes. Makes 5 dozen miniature meat
balls. Serve with your favorite barbecue sauce.
