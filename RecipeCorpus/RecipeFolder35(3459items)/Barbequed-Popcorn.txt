Title: Barbequed Popcorn
Categories: Low cal/fat, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Paprika
8 c  Popped plain popcorn
2 tb Plus 2 ts reduced calorie
Margerine
3/4 ts Chili powder
In small saucepan over medium heat combine margerine and spices.  Stir
until margerine is melted. Pour spice mixture over popcorn in a large
bowl mix thoroughly to coat. Each serving provides 1 Bread Exchange,
1 fat exchange From weight watchers
