Title: Sweet-And-Sour Spare Ribs
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Cornstatch
1 c  Ketchup
2/3 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Cold water
6 lb Pork spare ribs
Or pork loin back ribs
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Sugar
Place ribs on a rack in a large shallow roasting pan. Bake,
uncovered, at 350 deg. for 1-1/2 hours. Meanwhile, combine sugars and
cornstarch in a medium saucepan. Stir in ketchup, vinegar and water;
bring to a boil. Cook and stir until thickened and clear. Remove ribs
and rack from pan. Discard fat. Place ribs back in roasting pan; pour
about 1-1/2 cups of the sauce over ribs. Bake 30 minutes longer. Cut
ribs into serving-size pieces; brush with remaining sauce. Yield: 6-8
servings
