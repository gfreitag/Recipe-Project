Title: Tuna Salad For Two
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Provolone cheese --
Julienned
Or mozzarella cheese
DRESSING:
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 tb Lemon juice
1 sm Garlic clove -- minced
1 1/2 ts White wine vinegar
3/4 ts Italian seasoning
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 c  Lettuce -- torn
1 pk Corkscrew macaroni
5 oz Cooked and drained
1 cn Tuna
8 oz Drained and flaked
1 md Tomato -- cut in wedges
1 ea Celery rib -- sliced
1 ea Carrot -- peeled/sliced
1 sm Cucumber -- sliced
1/4 c  Green pepper -- strips
1 c  Broccoli florets
On two salad plates, arrange the first 10 ingredients in order
listed. In a jar with tight-fitting lid, combine dressing
ingredients; shake well. Pour over salads and serve immediately.
Yield: 2 servings
