Title: Heart Association Stuffed Acorn Squash
Categories: Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
4 ts Margarine
Cinnamon
2    Acorn squash, cut in half
1 c  Unsweetened applesauce
4 ts Brown sugar
Place squash halves cut side down in shallow baking pan. Cover
bottom with water. Bake at 400* for 50-60 minutes or until tender.
Turn squash over. fill each cavity with applesauce and brown sugar.
Dot with margarine. Sprinkle with cinnamon. Continue baking until
applesauce is bubbly, about 15-20 minutes.
