Title: Cheeseburger Casserole
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Casseroles
Yield: 5 servings
1 cn (8 Oz) Peas -- drained
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 lb Lean Ground Beef
1/3 c  Onion -- chopped
1 cn Tomato Soup, Condensed (10
-3/4 Oz)
MMMMM-----------------------BISCUITS---------------------------------
1/4 c  Milk -- plus 2 Tbs
2 tb Cooking Oil
8    Cubes American Or Cheddar
Cheese, (1/2-inch cubes)
1 c  Pillsbury's Best All-Purpose
Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking Powder
1/4 ts Salt
Combine ground beef and onion in 1 1/2-qt casserole. Bake at 425 F
for 20 mins. Drain. Add tomato soup, peas, Worcestershire sauce, salt
and pepper. Place biscuits seam-side down on casserole. Bake at 425 F
for 25 to 30 mins.

Biscuits: Combine dry ingredients in mixing bowl. Combine milk and
cooking oil. Add to dry ingredients all at once, stirring until dough
clings together. Knead lightly on floured surface 8 times. Divide
into 8 equal portions. Mold each portion around a cheese cube,
pinching to seal edges.

Tips: Instead of making biscuits, use 1 can Pillsbury Refrigerated
Country style or Buttermilk Biscuits molding each biscuit around a
cheese cube. Place on hot casserole. Bake at 400 F for 25 to 30 mins.

Serves 5 to 6.
