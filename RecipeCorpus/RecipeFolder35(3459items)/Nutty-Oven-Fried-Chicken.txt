Title: Nutty Oven-Fried Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Broiler-fryer chicken
(2 to 3 pounds)  cut up
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
1/3 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 c  Buttermilk biscuit mix
1/3 c  Pecans -- finely chopped
2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
1/2 ts Dried sage
In a shallow dish, combine biscuit mix, pecans and seasonings; mix
well. Dip chicken pieces in milk; coat generously with pecan mixture.
Place in a lightly greased 13-in, x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish.
Drizzle butter over chicken. Bake, uncovered, at 350 deg. for 1 hour
or until juices run clear. Yield: 6-8 servings
