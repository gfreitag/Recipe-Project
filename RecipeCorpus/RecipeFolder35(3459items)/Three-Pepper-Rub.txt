Title: Three-Pepper Rub
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Black pepper
-coarsely ground
1 pn Cayenne
1 ts Salt
3 tb Olive oil
3 tb Grated lemon zest
1 tb Lemon juice
1 sm Hot chile pepper, minced
Combine all the ingredients, then smear the rub on the chicken. Put
everything in a zip-top plastic bag and use it to massage the dry
marinade onto the chicken breasts. Marinate for 2-4 hours.

Makes enough for 2 whole chicken breasts.
