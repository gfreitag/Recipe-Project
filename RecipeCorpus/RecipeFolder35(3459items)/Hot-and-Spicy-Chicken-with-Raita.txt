Title: Hot and Spicy Chicken with Raita
Categories: Poultry, Hot/spicy
Yield: 4 servings
12    Hot and Spicy Chicken Wings
MMMMM---------------------------RAITA--------------------------------
300 ml Natural Yoghurt
Salt
Ground Black Pepper
1/2    Cucumber
1 tb Chopped Mint
1 tb Chopped Coriander
Preparation Time: 10 minutes Cooking Time: 25-40 minutes Per Serving:
168 calories, 6g fat

1. Barbecue the chicken wings for 25-30 minutes turning occasionally.
2. Mix together the cucumber, mint, coriander and yoghurt then chill
until required. Season to taste just before serving. 3. Serve the
chicken with the raita.

Delicious accompanied with basmati or pilau rice and mango chutney.
