Title: Chick-Pea Crunchies
Categories: Snacks, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
1/2    Black pepper
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Ground coriander
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
2 c  Cooked chickpeas
-drained well, blotted dry
1 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
1/2 ts Salt,
1. Preheat the oven to 400Ã¸F.

2. Toss the chickpeas with the oil, salt, black pepper, cumin,
coriander, and cayenne pepper. Spread them on a non-stick baking
sheet and bake for 30-40 minutes, or until chickpeas are golden brown
and crisp. They taste best served the same day.

Note1: The spicing is limited only by your imagination.

Note2: If you use canned chickpeas, reduce or omit the salt.
