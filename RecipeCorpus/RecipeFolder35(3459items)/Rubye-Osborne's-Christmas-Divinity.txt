Title: Rubye Osborne's Christmas Divinity
Categories: Candies, Route 66, Holiday
Yield: 40 servings
3    Egg Whites
1 ts Vanilla
Walnuts Or Pecans -- to
-taste
3 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  White Corn Syrup
1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Salt
Combine sugar, corn syrup, water, and salt. Place over medium heat,
allow to come to boiling point. Continue cooking, stirring
occasionally until a few drops will form a brittle ball when dropped
in cold water (265 F). Meanwhile, beat egg whites until stiff. Pour
syrup into whites in a fine stream while beating until candy loses
its gloss. Stir in nut meats and vanilla and drop quickly on waxed
paper or pour into a buttered pan and cut into squares. Makes about
40 pieces.
