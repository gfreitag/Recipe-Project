Title: Beef Slaw-Wiches
Categories: Diabetic, Meats, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Sliced green onions
8 sl White or whole wheat bread
8 oz Thinly sliced cooked roast
Beef
1/3 c  Reduced-calorie thousand
Island salad dressing
1 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Caraway seed
2 c  Finely shredded cabbage
Stir together salad dressing,mustard,and caraway seed.combine shredded
cabbage and green onions; toss with salad dressing mixture.Toast bread
slices.For sandwiches,spoon about 1/4 cup of the cabbage mixture onto
each of 4 of the bread slices.Arrange thebeef slices atop
sandwiches.Spoon about 1/4 cup of the cabbage mixture atop each
sandwich.Top with the remaining toasted bread slices.

2 1/2 lean meat exchanges
One serving equals
1 starch /bread exchange
1/2 vegetable exchange
1 1/2 fat exchange

Per serving: Calories 238 Protein 21g Carbohydrate 18g Total Fat 9g
Saturated Fat  2g   Cholesterol  57mg   Sodium  384mg   Potassium
347mg
