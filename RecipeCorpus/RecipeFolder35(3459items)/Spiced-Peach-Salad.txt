Title: Spiced Peach Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
4 ea Cinnamon sticks
2 pk JeLL-O 3 oz. pkg --
Peach-flavored
1 cn Peach halves -- 29 oz
1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb Vinegar
2 c  Water
1 tb Whole cloves
In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, vinegar and water. Tie cloves and
cinnamon in a cheesecloth bag; place in the saucepan. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered for 10 min. Remove from the heat and
discard spice bag. Add gelatin; stir until dissolved. Drain peaches,
reserving syrup; set peaches aside. Add water to syrup to equal 2
cups. Add to gelatin mixture stir well. Chill until slightly
thickened. Thinly slice peaches; add to gelatin. Pour into a 2  qt.
glass bowl; chill until firm. Yield: 8-10 servings. Editor's Note: If
desired 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon and 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
may be substituted for the whole spices; combine with the gelatin
before adding to sugar mixture.
