Title: Monte Crisco Sandwiches
Categories: Breads, Appetizers, Snacks, Sandwich
Yield: 2 sandwiches
4 sl Swiss cheese
2 sl Ham
2 sl Turkey
4 sl White bread
1    Egg; beaten
1/4 c  Milk
Preheat griddle or a large cast iron frying pan to 350. Butter
griddle.

Combine and beat eggs and milk in a shallow dish. Dip slices of bread
one at a time into the egg mix. Assemble sandwiches by placing a
slice of bread on the grill, then a slice of cheese, then ham and
turkey, then the second slice of cheese and the second piece of bread.

Grill about 2 to 3 minutes per side until lightly browned and the
cheese is melting. Cut sandwiches in halves and serve.

Variation of an old classic by Jim Weller.
