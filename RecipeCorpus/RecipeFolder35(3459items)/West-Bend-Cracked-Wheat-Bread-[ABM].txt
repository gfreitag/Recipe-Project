Title: West Bend Cracked Wheat Bread [ABM]
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 1/2lb loaf
2 tb Brown sugar; packed
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Butter or marg
2 ts Active dry yeast
1 c  Plus 2 tbsp water [80 deg]
1 3/4 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 c  Cracked wheat cereal
Add water to the pan. Add flours, cereal, sugar and salt to the pan.
Level dry ingredients. Place a piece of butter in each corner. Make a
well and add yeast. Program for "whole wheat".

From the West Bend ABM manual. Posted by Jim Weller.
