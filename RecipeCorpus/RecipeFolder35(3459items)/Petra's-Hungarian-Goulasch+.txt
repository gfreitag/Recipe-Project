Title: Petra's Hungarian Goulasch+
Categories: Hungarian, Soup/stew, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
Black pepper
2 cl Garlic, minced
1/2 lb Mushrooms, quartered
1/2 c  Sour cream
Water to cover
2 lg Onions, sliced thinly
1    Red pepper, cut in 1 1/2"
-chunks
1 lb Veal or beef stew meat
3 tb Paprika
Fry up onions and red pepper. Add stew meat and brown. Add paprika,
pepper and garlic. Cover with water and add sour cream. Cook,
covered, till meat is tender. add mushrooms and cook for 10 more
minutes.

Serve wtih egg noodles or spaetzle.
