Title: Praline Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1    Egg, beaten
Praline topping, Mix:
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Flour
1/3 c  Chopped nuts
3 tb Melted butter
1 tb Water
2 c  Cake flour
2 ts Baking powder
3/4 ts Salt
1 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Shortening
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Milk
Stir shortening to soften.  Sift dry ingredients over shortening.  Add
milk and mix until flour is dampened; then beat 2 minutes.  Add egg
and beat 1 minute longer.  Turn into a 9 x 9 inch greased cake pan.
Bake 25 minutes at 375 degrees.  Spread at once with Praline Topping.
Bake 5 minutes longer.  Cool and cut cake in pan.
