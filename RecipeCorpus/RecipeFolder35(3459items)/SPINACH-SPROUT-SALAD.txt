Title: SPINACH SPROUT SALAD
Categories: Salads, Low cal/fat
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Arugula, chopped (optional)
2 tb Olive oil
2 ts Lemon juice, fresh
2 ts Soy sauce -OR-
1/2 ts Dijon mustard
2 c  Lettuce, chopped
2 c  Spinach, chopped
2 c  Alfalfa sprouts
1 c  Assorted sprouts *1
1 c  Cucumber, peeled, cubed
1 md Tomato, sliced
*1 lentil, mung bean, aduki, radish, pea, red clover or wheat berry.
Combine vegetables and sprouts in large bowl.  Add oil and toss.  Add
lemon juice and soy sauce or mustard and toss again.
