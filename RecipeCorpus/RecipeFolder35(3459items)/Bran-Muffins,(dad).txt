Title: Bran Muffins,(dad)
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 14 small
1 1/2 c  Kellogg's all-bran
1 1/4 c  Skim milk
1    Egg
1/3 c  Unsweetened applesauce
1 1/4 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Splenda
Stir together flour,baking powder,salt and splenda.Set aside.Measure
cereal and milk into large bowl and stir to combine.Let stand 1 to 2
minutes,until cereal is softened.Add egg and applesauce.Beat well.Add
flour mixture,stirring only until combined.Portion batter evenly into
12 muffin pans.Bake in 400 degree oven until done or lightly browned.

Note: the splenda should be measured with the flour and rest of dry
ingredients then added to wet ingredients.
