Title: Pennsylvania Dutch Shoofly Pie
Categories: Penndutch, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 9" pie
1    Pie crust; 9", unbaked
MMMMM---------------------------CRUMBS--------------------------------
1 ts Cinnamon
pn Salt
1 1/2 c  Flour; all-purpose
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Brown sugar; packed
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/2 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Boiling water
**  To Make the Crumbs: Combine all the crumb ingredients together in
a large bowl until the mixture resembles large crumbs. Set aside.

**  To Make the Filling: Preheat the oven to 350Ã¸F.

Combine the molasses and water together in a bowl.  Stir in the
baking soda and ginger.

Mix in 2/3 of the crumb mixture with the filling mixture and pour
into the pie shell.  Sprinkle the remaining crumbs on top.

Bake until golden brown, about 30 minutes.

Nutritional Information: per serving: 332 calories, 5g protein, 10g
fat, 56g carbohydrates, 26mg cholesterol, 338mg sodium
