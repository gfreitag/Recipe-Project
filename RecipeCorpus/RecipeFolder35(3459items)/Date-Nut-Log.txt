Title: Date Nut Log
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 1/4 c  Light cream
1 pk Whipped cream -- optional
40    Square graham crackers --
Crushed divided
24 lg Marshmallows -- snipped
8 oz Dates -- chopped
In a bowl combine 2 cups of graham cracker crumbs, marshmallows,
dates and walnuts. Stir in cream; mix thoroughly. Shape into 14 x 3
inch log. Roll in remaining crumbs. Wrap tightly in plastic wrap or
foil. Refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight. Slice; garnish with
whipped cream if desired. Yield: 10 to 12 servings.
