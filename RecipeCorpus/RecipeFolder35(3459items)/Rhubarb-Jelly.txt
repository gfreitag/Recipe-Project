Title: Rhubarb Jelly
Categories: Can/preserv, Tasteofhome
Yield: 30 servings
Optional
2 pk Liquid fruit pectin -- 3 oz
Each
5 lb Rhubarb -- cut into 1 in.
Piece
7 c  Sugar
1 dr Red food coloring --
Grind the rhubarb in a food processor or grinder. Strain through a
jelly bag, reserving 3 1/2 cup of juice. Pour juice into a large
kettle; add sugar and food coloring if desired. bring to a boil over
high heat, stirring constantly. Add pectin; bring to a full rolling
boil. Boil for 1 min. stirring constantly. Remove from the heat; let
stand a few minutes. skim off foam. Pour hot into hot jars, leaving
1/4 in. headspace. Adjust caps. Process for 10 min. in a
boiling-water bath. Yield: 8 half pints.
