Title: Gwynne's Tuna And Noodles
Categories: Casseroles, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Egg noodles, cooked and
-drained
1/4 c  Bread crumbs
Parmesan cheese
Salt and pepper to taste
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
-F.
2 cn Tuna (6 1/8 oz. ea.)
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup,
-undiluted (10 3/4 oz.)
2/3 cn Water
1 c  Frozen peas, unthawed
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Tarragon
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
In a saucepan, combine soup, water, spices and peas. Bring to a boil;
simmer until peas are tender. Remove from heat. Add noodles and tuna
fish; mix well. Pour into a greased 2 quart casserole dish and
sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Sprinkle with bread crumbs. Bake at
375 degrees F. for 1/2 hour or until top is browned. SOURCE:*Gwynne
