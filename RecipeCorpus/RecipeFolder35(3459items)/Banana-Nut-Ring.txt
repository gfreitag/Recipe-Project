MMMMM----------------------BANANA NUT RING---------------------------
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
3/4 ts Ground cinnamon
2/3 c  Chopped nuts
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Mashed ripe bananas
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Sour cream
MMMMM------------------------BANANA GLAZE-----------------------------
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
Additional pecan halves
-(optional)
1 c  Mashed ripe bananas
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Brandy (optional)
Preheat oven to 350.  Cream 1 cup sugar and butter or margarine until
light and fluffy.  Beat in eggs, bananas and vanilla.  Fold in sour
cream. Sift flour, baking powder, soda and 1/4 tsp. cinnamon
together. Stir in dry ingredients just until blended. Combine nuts,
remaining 1/4 cup sugar and 1/2 tsp. cinnamon.  Sprinkle one third of
nut mixture into a 9-cup fluted tube pan or a 11 x 7 x 1-1/2 in.
baking pan. Spoon one-third of batter into pan and spread evenly.
Repeat layering twice. Bake in a 350 oven for 45-60 minutes until a
skewer inserted in center comes out clean. Cool upright in pan on
wire rack for 10 minutes, then turn out and cool completely. Spoon or
spread Banana Glaze over ring. Garnish with pecan halves, if desired.
Makes 12 servings.  About 400 calories per serving. Banana Glaze:
Combine bananas and sugar in a small saucepan.  Cook and stir over
medium heat for 5 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Add lemon juice,
brandy and cinnamon. Cool slightly before spooning over ring. Shared
