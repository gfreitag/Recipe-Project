Title: Chewy Coconut Macaroons
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 18 servings
2 tb Flour
2    Egg Whites
1/2 ts Almond Extract
1 1/2 c  Coconut -- flaked
1/3 c  Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
Combine coconut, sugar, salt and flour. Stir in egg whites and almond
extract; mix well.

Drop by teaspoonful onto lightly greased cookie sheets. Bake at 325 F
for 20 mins or until golden brown around the edges. Remove from
cookie sheets at once.

Makes approximately 1 1/2 dozen.
