Title: Toasty Garlic Bread
Categories: Breads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Softened
Grated Parmesan cheese
Parsley flakes
Garlic powder
1 ea French bread
(12 to 15 inches), cut into
L-inch slices
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Spread both sides of bread with butter. Place, cut side down, on a
cookie sheet. Sprinkle one side with cheese, parsley and garlic
powder. Broil 4 in. from the heat until light golden brown. Turn
slices and repeat with other side. Serve immediately. Yield: about
12-15 slices
