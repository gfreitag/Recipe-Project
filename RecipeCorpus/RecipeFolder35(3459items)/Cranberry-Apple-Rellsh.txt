Title: Cranberry Apple Rellsh
Categories: Can/preserv, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Walnuts -- chopped optional
2 tb Liquid artificial sweetener
1/2 ts Lemon juice
2 c  Fresh cranberries -- ground
Or frozen
1/2 c  Apple -- diced peeled
1/4 c  Raisins
In a bowl, combine all ingredients. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
Yield: 10 servings. Diabetic Exchanges: One 1/4-cup serving (prepared
without walnuts) equals 1/2 fruit; also, 24 calories, 1 mg sodium, 0
cholesterol, 6 gm carbohydrate, trace protein, trace fat
