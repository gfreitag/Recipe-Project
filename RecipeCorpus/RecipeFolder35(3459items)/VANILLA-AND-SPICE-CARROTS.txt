Title: VANILLA AND SPICE CARROTS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb WATER
1 1/2 ts VANILLA
1 1/2 ts CINNAMON
3 ea CARROTS, SLICED
1 tb BUTTER
2 tb SORGHUM
SAUTEE CARROTS IN BUTTER FOR 2 MINUTES. MIX TOGETHER REMAINING
INGREDIENTS AND POUR OVER CARROTS.  STIR AND SIMMER ON LOW HEAT FOR 5
TO 10 MINUTES UNTIL CARROTS ARE SLIGHTLY TENDER.
