Title: Rice Brittle
Categories: Candies, Oldies
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Puffed rice
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Butter or butter substitute
1/4 ts Cream of tartar
Combine water, sugar, cream of tartar, vinegar, and butter.  Boil to
hard crack stage (285 - 290 F).  Remove from fire.  Add flavoring and
rice. Stir until all the grains are coated.  Pour into well-buttered
pan. Spread in a thin sheet.  Let cool. Break in pieces. Mrs. G.G.
Cornell, Hazel Green, AL. Submitted By ROBERT MILES   On   09-24-95
