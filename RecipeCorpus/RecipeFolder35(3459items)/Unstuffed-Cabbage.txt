Title: Unstuffed Cabbage
Categories: Meats, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Hot rice
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Chopped onion
1 sm Head cabbage -- shredded abt
-1 lb
1 cn Mexican-style tomatoes --
-28 oz, with liquid
In a Dutch oven, brown beef and onion; drain. Stir in cabbage. Cover
and cook for 5 minutes or until cabbage is crisp tender. Stir in
tomatoes, brown sugar, vinegar, salt and pepper. Cook 10 minutes
longer, stirring occasionally. Serve over rice. Yield: 4-6 servings.
