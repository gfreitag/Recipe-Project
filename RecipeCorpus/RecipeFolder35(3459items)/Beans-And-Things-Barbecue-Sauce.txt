Title: Beans And Things Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Route 66, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Liquid Smoke
1 1/4 ts Dry Mustard
1 ts Chili Powder
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
1 ts Black Pepper
1/4 tb Cayenne
2 1/4 c  Catsup
2 1/4 c  Water
2 ts Beef Broth
3 tb Brown Sugar
1/2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
2    Shakes Tabasco Sauce
1 tb Lemon Juice
Combine all ingredients in large saucepan and bring to boil; then
simmer for 15 mins. Cool and store in refrigerator until needed.
Makes 1 quart.
