Title: Spam Jello
Categories: Spam
Yield: 6 servings
1/2    Bell pepper, chopped
4    Hard boiled eggs, chopped
1/4 c  Relish (pickle, corn, etc.)
2    Stalks celery, finely
-chopped
2 dr Tabasco
1 lg Box lemon or lime Jello
1 lg Can Spam
1 md Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Ice cold water
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream
Dissolve Jello in boiling water.  Add cold water, mayonnaise and sour
cream to Jello. Chop Spam; add to Jello with remaining ingredients.
Chill until firm. Makes 6 to 8 servings.  This recipe is excellent on
a hot day. Submitted By ELIZABETH WOOD   On   10-15-95
