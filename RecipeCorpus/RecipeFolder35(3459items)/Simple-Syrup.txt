Title: Simple Syrup
Categories: Syrups, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
This can be prepared well in advance.

Place sugar and water in a heavy saucepan and bring to a boil. Boil
for five minutes and transfer to a container to cool. Can be
refrigerated for up to 6 weeks.
