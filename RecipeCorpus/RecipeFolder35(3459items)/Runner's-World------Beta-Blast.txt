Title: Runner's World  --  Beta Blast
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1/2    Mango; frozen in chunks
1/2 c  Carrot juice
1 tb Honey
3 sm Ice cubes
2    Apricots; pitted & sliced
1/2    Papaya; frozen in chunks
A muscle-rejuvenating, beta-carrotene-filled orange-colored wonder.

Add all the ingredients (except for the honey) to a blender in the
order listed, then blend on high speed for 30 seconds.  Add the honey
and blend a few seconds more.

Serve in a frosted glass.

Options:  Add orange juice for a thinner consistency.

Nutritional Information: per serving: 200 calories, less than 1g fat
