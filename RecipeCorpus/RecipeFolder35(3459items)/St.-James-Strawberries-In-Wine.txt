Title: St. James Strawberries In Wine
Categories: Desserts, Route 66
Yield: 4 servings
*****  NONE  *****
Slice one quart of fresh strawberries and lightly sugar, using about
1/4 cup sugar. Let berries sit to draw the juices. Stir in 3/4 cup
dry white wine before serving.

Makes 4 one-cup servings.
