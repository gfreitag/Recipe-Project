Title: Freezing Lentils
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
-Makes 12 to 16 pints
2 lb To 2-1/2 lb in pods
1. Choose pods of young, tender lentils.

2. Wash, shell, and wash again.

3. Blanch lentils 1 minute. Cool; drain well.

4. Pack into containers. Seal, label and freeze.

5. Cook frozen lentils 6 to 10 minutes
