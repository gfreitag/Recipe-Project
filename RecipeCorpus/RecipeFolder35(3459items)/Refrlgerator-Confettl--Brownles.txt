Title: Refrlgerator Confettl  Brownles
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips --
12    Ounces
1 c  Evaporated milk
1/2 ts Peppermint extract --
Optional
3 c  Graham cracker crumbs
2 c  Colored miniature
Marshmallows
1 c  Walnuts -- chopped or
Pecans
1 c  Confectioners' sugar
In a large bowl, combine the crumbs, marshmallows, nuts and sugar. In
a saucepan, melt chocolate chips and milk over low heat, stirring
often until blended and smooth. Remove from the heat; add extract if
desired. Set aside 1/2 cup. Pour remaining chocolate mixture over
crumb mixture; stir until well blended. Pour batter into a greased
8-in. square baking pan. Press down evenly. Frost with reseved
chocolate mixture. Chill for 1 hour. Yield: 2-1/2 dozen
