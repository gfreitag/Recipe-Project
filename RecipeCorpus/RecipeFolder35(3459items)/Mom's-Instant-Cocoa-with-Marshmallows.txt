Title: Mom's Instant Cocoa with Marshmallows
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 16 cups mix
.  6oz jar
8 1/2 c  Dry milk; nonfat, 25.6oz pkg
3 c  Instant chocolate drink mix;    1 1/2 c  Confectioners sugar
1 1/2 c  Miniature marshmallows
.  for milk,  16oz pkg
1 1/2 c  Non-dairy coffee creamer;
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients.  Store in airtight
container. Give mix with serving instructions.

Makes about 16 cups of cocoa mix, enough for 32 servings.

To Serve:  Stir 1/2 cup cocoa mix into 9oz of hot water.

Source:  A pamphlet Mom received in the mail.
