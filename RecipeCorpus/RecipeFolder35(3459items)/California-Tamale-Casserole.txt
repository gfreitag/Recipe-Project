Title: California Tamale Casserole
Categories: Tex/mexican, Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 2 servings
1 cn (16-oz) tomatoes
1 cn (17-oz) whole kernel corn
-drained
1 cn (7.5-oz) pitted black olive
-drained
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
3/4 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 ea Egg beaten
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 pk Chili seasoning mix
2 ts Seasoned salt
Combine cornmeal, milk and egg in 2 1/2 quart casserole. Brown meat in
skillet, stirring to keep crumbly. Add chili seasoning mix, salt,
tomatoes, corn, and olives and mix well. Stir into cornmeal mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees 1 hour 15 minutes. Sprinkle cheese over top and
bake 5 minutes longer.
