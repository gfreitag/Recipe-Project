Title: Dinner Table Doves - Charcoal Delight
Categories: Poultry, Game, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 batch
Olive oil
Cavender's Greek seasoning
Dove breasts
Bacon slices
Wrap the dove breasts in the bacon slices (pin the bacon with
toothpicks to hold it on the meat).

Coat each breast with olive oil.

Sprinkle with Cavender's Greek Seasoning.

Place the wrapped breasts in a casserole dish, cover and leave
overnight in the refrigerator.

Cook over hot charcoal for about 20 minutes or until the bacon is
done.
