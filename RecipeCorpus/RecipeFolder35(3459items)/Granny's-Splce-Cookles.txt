Title: Granny's Splce Cookles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Ground cloves
Red hot candies, nonpareils
And/orsprinkle
1 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg -- lightly beaten
2 tb Light corn syrup
2 tb Grated orange peel
1 tb Cold water
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add egg, corn syrup, orange
peel and cold water. Combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, ginger and
cloves; add to creamed mixture and mix well. Chill for at least 1
hour, On a lightly floured surface, roll dough, a portion at a time,
to 1/8-in. thickness. Cut into desired shapes. Place on greased
baking sheets. Decorate as desired. Bake at 375 deg. for 6-8 minutes
or until lightly browned. Yield: about 4 dozen (3 inch cookies).
