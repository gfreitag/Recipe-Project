Title: Monster Mash Dip (Halloween)
Categories: Low cal/fat, Dips/spread, Holiday, Kids
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Onions -- finely chopped
1 tb Fresh or frozen chives --
Chopped
1/4 ts Dried dillweed
1/4 ts Pepper
4 1/2 c  1% low fat cottage cheese
1/3 c  Fat free mayo or salad
Dressing
3 tb White vinegar
3 tb Grated parmesan cheese
Put all in food processor and process until smooth cover and frigerate
atleast 1 hr before serving serve with assorted veggies. Yield: 4 cups
