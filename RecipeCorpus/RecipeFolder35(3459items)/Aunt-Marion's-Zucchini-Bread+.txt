Title: Aunt Marion's Zucchini Bread+
Categories: Breads
Yield: 2 loaves
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 tb Cinnamon
1/4 ts Baking powder
1 c  Nutmeats
3    Eggs
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Oil
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Grated zucchini
Mix in order given. Bake in two loaf pans in 350 degree oven about one
hour.

Given me by my Mother's sister and it is very, very good!
