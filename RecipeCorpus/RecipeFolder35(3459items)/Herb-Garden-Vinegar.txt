Title: Herb Garden Vinegar
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
-peppercorns, crushed
1/8 ts Red pepper flakes
2    Stalks fresh tarragon
1    Stalk fresh rosemary
1    Stalk fresh thyme
1    Bottle white wine vinegar
2 tb Rice vinegar
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
Few grains whole coriander
Few grains whole green
Slide whole herb stalks into bottle, stem end down.  Add peppers and
coriander.  Pour liquid ingredients into sterilized bottle with
non-metal cap (cork is best).  Let set for four to six weeks.
Strain, pour back into bottle, replacing herbs with one fresh stalk
of each.
