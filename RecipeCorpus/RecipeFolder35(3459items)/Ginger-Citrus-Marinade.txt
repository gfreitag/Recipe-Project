Title: Ginger-Citrus Marinade
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1    2" piece ginger
-thinly sliced
Grated zest of 1 lime
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/3 c  Canola oil
2    Scallions, thinly sliced
1/4 c  Orange juice
1    Lemon, thinly sliced
2 tb Honey
Combine all the ingredients in a nonreactive dish or in a zip-top
plastic bag. Turn the chicken breasts during marinating to distribute
the flavors evenly. For best results, marinate for 4-6 hours in the
refrigerator.

Makes enough for 2 whole chicken breasts.
