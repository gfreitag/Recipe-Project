Title: Plum Orange Conserve
Categories: Can/preserv, Dips/spread, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
1 1/2 c  White sugar
1/4 c  Seedless raisins
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2    Orange, unpeeled
2 tb Water
2 c  Coarsely chopped (unpeeled)
-plums
Wash orange and cut into thin wedges; thinly slice, discarding any
seeds.  In 8 cup measure, combine orange and water.  Microwave,
uncoverd, on high for 2 minutes, or until rind is tender.  Stir in
plums, sugar, raisins and lemon juice.  Microwave, uncovered and
stirring every 3 minutes, on high, for 15-18 minutes, or until
thickened enough that a spoonful isn't runny after sitting on a plate
for a minute.  Fill, seal and stor jar in refrigerator for up to 1
month. Makes 2 cups.

Origin: Canadian Living, June/95.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/95.
