Title: Romaine Leaves with Figs and Red Peppers
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Olive oil
2 ts Balsamic vinegar
1 sm Garlic clove(s),
8 ts Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Pepper
16    Hearts of romaine
-lettuce leaves
1/2 c  Red bell pepper
-julienne-cut
7    Dried figs, thinly sliced
2 tb Chicken broth
Arrange 4 lettuce leaves on each of 4 salad plate. Top each with bell
peppers and figs.

Combine chicken broth, oil, vinegar and garlic in a bowl; stir well.
Top each salad with 1 tbs dressing and 2 tsp cheese; sprinkle with
pepper.
