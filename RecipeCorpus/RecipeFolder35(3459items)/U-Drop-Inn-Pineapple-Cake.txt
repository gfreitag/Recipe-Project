Title: U Drop Inn Pineapple Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Route 66
Yield: 1 servings
2    Eggs
1 cn (20 Oz) Crushed Pineapple --
-undrained
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking Soda
pn Salt
Grease and flour a 9 x 13 baking pan. Combine flour, sugar, salt,
eggs, pineapple and vanilla. Pour into baking pan and bake in
preheated 350 F oven for 40 mins. Top with your favorite cream cheese
or coconut frosting.
