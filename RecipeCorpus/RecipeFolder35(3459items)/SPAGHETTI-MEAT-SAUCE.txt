Title: SPAGHETTI MEAT SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Meats, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
6 oz Tomato paste
1 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1    Bay leaf
1 lg Onion, chopped
1    Garlic clove, minced
1/4 c  Salad oil
1 lb Ground beef
2 1/2 c  Tomatoes, canned
Saute onion and garlic in hot oil.  Add meat and cook until browned.
Add remaining ingredients; Simmer slowly for 1 hour.
Cool quickly; freeze in refrigerator trays ( with dividers). Transfer
cubes to freezer container.
To serve, heat as many cubes as needed over low heat.  Spoon sauce
over fresh cooked spaghetti, sprinkle with grated cheese
