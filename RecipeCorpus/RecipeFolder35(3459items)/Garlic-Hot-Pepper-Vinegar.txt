Title: Garlic-Hot Pepper Vinegar
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
2    Fresh cayenne, jalapeno or
-other hot peppers
2 c  White vinegar
2 lg Cloves  garlic -- peeled
In a small stainless steel saucepan, heat vinegar to boiling.
Meanwhile thread garlic and hot pepper on wooden skewer.  Place in
clean pint jar. Carefully pour hot vinegar into jar.  Cover; let
stand several days at room temperature to blend flavors.  If desired,
strain into decorative jar and add fresh garlic and peppers.  Store
in cool, dark place.  Makes 1 pint.
