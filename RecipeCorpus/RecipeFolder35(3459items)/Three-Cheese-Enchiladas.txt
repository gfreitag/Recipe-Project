Title: Three Cheese Enchiladas
Categories: Tex/mexican, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Diced
1/2 c  Sliced green onions
1 ts Crushed cumin
8    Flour tortillas -- each 7-8
Inches
Shredded lettuce
Chopped tomato
Sliced black olives
1 1/2 c  Monterey Jack cheese -- 6 oz
Shredded divid
1 1/2 c  Cheddar cheese -- 6 oz.
Shredded, divi
1 pk Cream cheese -- 3 oz.
Softened
1 c  Picante sauce -- divided
1 md Red or green bell pepper --
Combine 1 cup Monterey Jack cheese, 1 cup cheddar cheese, cream
cheese, 1/4 cup picante sauce, the red pepper, onions and cumin; mix
well. Spoon 1/4 cup cheese mixture down the center of each tortilla.
Roll and place, seam side down, in a l3-in. x 9-in. x 2-in, baking
dish. Spoon remaining picante sauce evenly over enchiladas; cover
with remaining cheeses. Bake at 350 deg. for 20 minutes or until hot.
Top with lettuce, tomato and black olives; serve with additional
picante sauce, if desired. Yield: 4 servings
