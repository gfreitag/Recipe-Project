Title: Barbeque Pecans
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Hot sauce
4 c  Pecan halves
2 tb Butter melted
3 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Ketchup
Put all ingredients in glass baking dish.  Microwave on high 4 to 5
minutes, stirring every minute.  Salt to taste.
