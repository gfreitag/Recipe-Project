Title: Creme Fraiche
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Desserts, Masterchefs
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Buttermilk
1 c  Whipping cream
Combine whipping cream and buttermilk in a glass container. Let stand
at room temperature for 8 hours. Then refrigerate for 10 days.
