Title: Turkey-In-The-Round Sandwiches
Categories: Sandwich, Poultry, Snacks
Yield: 4 servings
-total
8 sl (thin) onion; (red or white)
1    Roasted red bell pepper;
-cut into 4 pieces
1/4 c  Cilantro leaves;
2 sm Italian bread shells; such
-as boboli
2 tb Reduced-fat mayonnaise;
2 tb Jalapepo cheese dip;
4 sl (thick) turkey breast; 6-oz
Briefly hold the bread rounds over an open gas flame, turning once,
until they are lightly toasted on both sides, 30 - 40 seconds total.
Use a long, serrated knife to carefully slice each round in half
crosswise. Spread 4 slices with a portion of the mayonnaise and
cheese dip. Layer with meat, vegetables and cilantro.  Close the
sandwiches and cilantro. Close the sandwiches and serve.  Yield 4
servings.
