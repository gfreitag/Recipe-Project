Title: Nockerl of Noques (not unlike Italian Gnocci)+
Categories: Jewish, Aust/german
Yield: 1 recipe
2    Eggs
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/4 c  Flour (approximate)
1/4 c  Regular cream of wheat
1 tb Vegetable oil
3/4 c  Water, boiling hot
1 c  Mashed cooked potato
Saute cream of wheat in oil till golden. Add boiling water, lower
heat and cook, stirring, for a minute until the mixture pulls away
from the sides of the pan.

Remove from heat and mix well with remaining ingredients except
flour. Add flour and work into a firm claylike dough.

On floured board roll dough into long ropes about 1/2 inch thick. Cut
into 1 inch lengths. Then curl slightly by pressing them lightly on
the back of fork tines. Cook in gently boiling salted water for 5
minutes. Drain and serve with butter, sour cream or your favorite
spaghetti sauce.
