Title: Peach Praline Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Walnuts
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 ea Unbaked pastry shell -- 9
Inches
Whipped cream or ice cream
Optional
4 c  Ripe peaches -- sliced
Peeled
1 c  Sugar -- divided
2 tb Quick-cooking tapioca
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Chopped peeans -- or
In a large bowl, combine peaches, 1/4 cup sugar, tapioca and lemon
juice; mix well. In a small bowl, combine flour,nuts and remaining
sugar; cut in butter until crumbly. Sprinkle a third of the crumbs
into pie shell; cover with peaches. Sprinkle remainins crumbs on top
sake af 450 deg for 70 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 deg; bake for 30
minutes or until the peaches are tender and topping is golden brown.
Serve with whipped cream or Ice cream if desired. Yield: 6-8 servings
