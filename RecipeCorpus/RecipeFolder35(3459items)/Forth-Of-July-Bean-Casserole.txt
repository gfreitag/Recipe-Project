Title: Forth Of July Bean Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Barbecue sauce
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Packed brown suqar
2 tb Prepared mustard
2 tb Molasses
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 lb Sliced bacon -- diced
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 c  Onion -- chopped
1 cn Pork and beans -- 28 ounces
1 cn Lima beans -- 17 oz
Rinsed/drained
1 cn Kidney beans -- 16 oz
Rinsed/drained
In a large skillet, cook bacon, beef and onion until meat is browned
and onion is tender; drain. Transfer to a greased 2-1/2-qt. baking
dish; add all of the beans and mix well. In a small bowl, combine the
remaining ingredients; stir into beef and bean mixture. Cover and
bake at 350 deg. for 45 minutes. Uncover; bake 15 minutes longer.
Yield: 12 servings
