Title: Tacoed Eggs
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Ground beef -- cooked
Left-over taco-seasoned
Meat
Flour tortilla -- warmed
Optional
Salsa
8 ea Eggs -- beaten
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
2 tb Chopped onion -- finely
2 tb Green pepper -- finely
Chopped
4 dr Hot pepper sauce
In a bowl combine eggs, cheese, onion, green pepper and hot pepper
sauce. Cook and stir in a nonstick skillet until eggs begin to set.
Add taco-seasoned ground beef; cook until eggs are completely set. If
desired spoon onto a warmed tortilla and roll up; top with salsa.
