Title: Rack of Spam
Categories: Spam, Fruit, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Pineapple chunks
Maraschino cherries
1 cn Spam
Peanut butter
Butterfly the loaf of Spam by slicing it into thin sections without
cutting all the way through. Bend open the Spam slices and stick
pieces of pineapple in between them. For garnish, top with some
peanut butter and a few Maraschino cherries. Place in the microwave
for about 4 minutes.
