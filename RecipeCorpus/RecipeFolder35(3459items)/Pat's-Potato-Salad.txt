Title: Pat's Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 16 servings
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Vinegar
4 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Salt
12 md Red potatoes -- cubed
Boiled and peeled
1 md Red onion -- chopped
1 c  Fresh parsley -- chopped
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
In a large bowl, combine potatoes, onion and parsley. In a small bowl,
combine remaining ingredients. Pour over potatoes and mix well.
Refrigerate at least 1 hr. before serving. Salad can be prepared a
day ahead. Yield: 12 to 16 servings.
