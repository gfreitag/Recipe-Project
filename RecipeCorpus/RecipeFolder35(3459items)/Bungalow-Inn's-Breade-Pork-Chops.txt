Title: Bungalow Inn's Breade Pork Chops
Categories: Main dish, Route 66, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Fine Cracker Crumbs
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
Shortening
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
4    To 6 Pork Chops
Beat eggs and milk until blended well. Dip chops into mixture, then
into cracker crumbs to coat. Salt and pepper to taste and place in
skillet with small amount of hot shortening. Brown on both sides.
Reduce heat and cook until well done. Makes 4 to 6 servings.
