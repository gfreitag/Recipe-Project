Title: Beef Noodles
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Mixed vegetables -- 16 oz
Drained
1 ts Dried oregano
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Noodles -- uncooked
md Egg
1 lb Ground beef, lean
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 cn Italian tomatoes -- 14 1/2
oz Each
Stewed
2 cn Beef broth -- 10 1/2 oz
Each
In a dutch oven or soup kettle, brown beef and onion; drain. Add
tomatoes, broth, vegetables and seasonings. Bring to a boil; add
noodles. Reduce heat to medium-low; cover and cook for 10-15 min. or
until the noodles done. Yield: 6-8 servings. (2 quarts)
