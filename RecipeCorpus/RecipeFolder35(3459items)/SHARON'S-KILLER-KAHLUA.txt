Title: SHARON'S KILLER KAHLUA
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 3 fifths
2 oz Pure vanilla extract
1    Monarch 151 rum  (fifth)
4 c  Water
4 c  Sugar
4 oz Jar instant coffee
Bring to boil water and sugar.  Remove from heat and add instant
coffee. Bring to boil again. Remove from heat and add vanilla extract
(very important you use pure extract NOT imatation). Cool before
adding rum. Put in bottles and cork. Makes about 3 fifths. Source:
SHARON DIBBLE Submitted By SHARON DIBBLE   On   10-18-95
