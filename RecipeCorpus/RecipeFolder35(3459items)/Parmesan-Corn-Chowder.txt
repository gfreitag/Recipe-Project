Title: Parmesan Corn Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Margarine
1/4 c  Flour
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1 c  Cream-style corn
2 c  Boiling water
2 c  Diced potatoes, with skins
-on
1/2 c  Sliced carrots
1/2 c  Sliced celery
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 1/2 ts Salt
To the water; add potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, salt and pepper.
Cover and simmer. Do not drain. Make a white sauce with the
margarine, flour and milk. Add corn and undrained vegetables. Heat,
but do not boil. Add cheese, heat through. Serves 6-8.
