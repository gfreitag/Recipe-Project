Title: Dutch Garden Dip~
Categories: Healthy, Dips/spread
Yield: 5 servings
1 tb Finely chopped celery
2 ts Finely chopped shallot
2 ts Minced fresh tarragon
-- OR --
1/2 ts Dried tarragon
1/3 c  Nonfat plain yogurt
1 tb Nonfat mayonnaise
1 tb Finely chopped sweet red
-pepper
1 tb Finely chopped yellow
-pepper
Prep Time:  8 min.

In a small bowl, stir together all ingredients.  Cover and chill in
the refrigerator until serving time.

Makes 2/3 cup or 5 servings.

Per 2 tablespoons:  13 calories; 0.1 g fat (4% of calories); 0 g
fiber; 0 mg cholesterol; 51 mg sodium.
