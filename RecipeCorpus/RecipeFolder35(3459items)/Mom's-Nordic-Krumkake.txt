Title: Mom's Nordic Krumkake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts, Holiday
Yield: 1 batch
1 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
2    Eggs
Beat the eggs with the sugar until light.  Add the remaining
ingredients and stir until smooth.  Heat a Krumkake Iron over low
heat; brush lightly with melted butter.  Pour about 1 Tbsp of batter
onto the iron.  Cook the wafer slowly until golden on one side, turn
and cook on the other side. Remove each wafer with a fork and roll
over a cylinder form.
