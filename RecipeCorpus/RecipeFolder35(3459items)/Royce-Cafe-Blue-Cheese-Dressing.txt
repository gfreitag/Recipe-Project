Title: Royce Cafe Blue Cheese Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Route 66, Salads
Yield: 5 servings
1 tb Garlic Powder
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Savory Salt
4 oz Blue Cheese -- crumbled
1 qt Miracle Whip Dressing
2 1/2 ts Onion Powder
Blend together and refrigerate before serving over salad. Makes 5
cups.
