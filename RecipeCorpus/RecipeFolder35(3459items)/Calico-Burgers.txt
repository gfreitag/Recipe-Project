Title: Calico Burgers
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Dried parsley -- flaked
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 ds Pepper
1 1/2 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1/2 c  Rice -- cooked
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Green pepper -- chopped
MMMMM-----------------------BARBECUE SAUCE----------------------------
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Dried basil
2/3 c  Water
1/4 c  Ketchup
3 tb Chili sauce
In bowl, combine the first eight ingredients; mix well. Shape into
four to six oval patties. Grill over hot coals until meat reaches
desired doneness, about 15 to 20 min. Combine all sauce ingredients
in a saucepan; simmer for 15 min. Serve with burgers. Yield: 4 to 6
servings.
