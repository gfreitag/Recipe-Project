Title: Turkey Stroganoff
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Cooked turkey -- cubed
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz
1 cn Mushroom pieces -- drained
1/2 ts Dried rosemary
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Frozen broccoli flowerets --
Cut 1/4 in strips
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Chopped onion
3 tb All-purpose flour
1 cn Chicken broth -- 10 3/4 oz
Undiluted
Cook broccoli according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
In a 2 qt. microwave-safe casserole, melt butter. Add onion; cover
and cook on hith until tender. Add flour and blend well. Whisk in
broth; cook on high for 4-6 min. or until thickened and bubbly,
stirring every 2 min. Add remaining ingredients except noodles. Cook
on high for 2-3 min. or until heated through stirring once. Serve
over noodles. Yield: 4 servings.
