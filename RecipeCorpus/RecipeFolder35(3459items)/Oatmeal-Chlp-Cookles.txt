Title: Oatmeal Chlp Cookles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
6 c  Quick-cooking oats
3 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips --
12    Ounces
2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Packed brown sugar
4 ea Eggs
2 ts Vanilla extract
In a mixing bowl, cream butter, sugars. eggs and vanilla. Combine
oats, flour, baking soda and salt; stir into creamed mixture. Add
chocolate chips and mix well. Chill dough for 1 hour or until firm.
Roll dough into 1-1/2-in. balls; place on greased cookie sheets. Bake
at 350 deg. for 11-13 minutes or until lightly browned. Yield: about
7 dozen
