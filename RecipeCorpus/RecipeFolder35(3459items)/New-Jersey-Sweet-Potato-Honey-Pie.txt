Title: New Jersey Sweet Potato Honey Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Evaporated milk
2 c  Pureed cooked sweet potatoes
1 c  Clover honey
MMMMM----------------1/2 TSP. EACH: NUTMEG, GINGE---------------------
3 ea Beaten eggs
1 ts Cinnamon
pn Of ground cloves
Thoroughly mix together, pureed potatoes, honey, milk, salt, spices
and eggs.  Pour into 9" pie shell.  Bake for 40 to 45 minutes at 400
degrees. Best served chilled with whipped cream on top. From: Sweet
Potatoes with That Old Fashioned Flavor Shared By: Pat Stockett
