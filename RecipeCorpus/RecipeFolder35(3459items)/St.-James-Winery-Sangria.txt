Title: St. James Winery Sangria
Categories: Beverages, Route 66
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Bottle Club Soda (28 Oz)
1    Bottle Red Wine (750 Ml)
1    Orange -- thinly sliced
1    Lemon -- thinly sliced
Combine the wine, orange, lemon, and sugar. Let sit one hour at room
temperature. When ready to serve, add the club soda and serve over
crushed ice.

Makes 6 eight-oz. servings.
