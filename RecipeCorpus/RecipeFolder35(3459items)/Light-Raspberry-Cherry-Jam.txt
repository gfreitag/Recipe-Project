Title: Light Raspberry Cherry Jam
Categories: Can/preserv, Dips/spread, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/4 c  Pitted sour cherries
2 tb Light fruit pectin crystals
1 3/4 c  Crushed raspberries
In small bowl, mix pectin crystals with 1/4 cup of the sugar.  In 16
cup microwaveable bowl, combine raspberries, cherries and pectin
mixture. Microwave, uncovered, on High, for 5-8 minutes, or until a
full rolling boil, stirring once.  Stir in remaining sugar and
microwave, uncovered, on High for 4-6 minutes, or until returned to
full rolling boil, stirring twice.  Microwave on High for another
minute.  Fill, seal and store jars in fridge for up to one month.
Makes 3 cups. Origin: Canadian Living, June/95.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/95.
