Title: Campfire Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Minced fresh parsley
1 tb Worcestershire sauee
Salt and pepper to taste
1/3 c  Chicken broth
5 md Potatoes -- peeled/thinly
Sliced
1 md Onion -- sliced
6 tb Butter or margarine
1/3 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
Place the potatoes and onion on a large piece of heavy-duty foil
(about 20in. x 20 in.); dot with butter. Cormbine the cheese, parsley,
Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper; sprinkle over potatoes. Fold
foil up around potatoes and add broth. Seal the edges of foil well.
Grill, covered, over medium coals for 35-40 minutes or until potatoes
are tender. Yield: 4-6 servings
