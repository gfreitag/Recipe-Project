Title: Old-Fashioned Egg Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
6 ea Hard-cooked eggs -- chopped
1/2 c  Celery -- finely chopped
Lettuce leaves or bread
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Dried minced onion
1/4 ts Salt
In a bowl, combine mayonnaise, lemon juice, onion, salt and pepper.
Stir in eggs and celery. Cover and chill. For each serving, spoon
about 1/2 cup onto a lettuce leaf or spread on bread. Yield: 3-4
servings
