Title: Pecan stuffing
Categories: Poultry, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sweet red pepper (minced)
1/2 ts Cayenne Pepper
1 1/2 c  Chicken Stock
Salt & Pepper to taste
4 c  Chopped Pecans
4 c  Bread cubes
4 tb Butter, melted
1/4 c  Fresh Basil (chopped)
Preheat oven to 350 F. Combine all ingredients, season with S & P.
Place in greased 9"x 13" baking dish, cover with foil and bake for 20
minutes. Remove foil and bake for 15 minutes.
