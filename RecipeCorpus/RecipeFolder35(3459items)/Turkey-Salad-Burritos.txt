Title: Turkey Salad Burritos
Categories: Poultry, Country wom, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
Dressing
1/4 c  Picante sauce
1/2 ts Salt
6 ea Tortilla flour -- soft 8 in.
Long
Picante sauce
Black olives for garnish
2 c  Cooked turkey -- chopped
3/4 c  Chopped celery -- finely
Chopped
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Black olives -- sliced
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Mayonnaise -- or salad
In med. mixing bowl, combine turkey, celery, onion, black olives and
cheese. In another bowl, whisk together salad dressing, picante sauce
and salt. Pour sauce over turkey mixture; blend well. Spoon filling
onto tortillas; wrap burrito. Heat in 13x9x3 inch baking pan at 350
deg. for 20 minutes or until thoroughly warmed or mixrowave each
burrito 2 to 2 1/2 min. on high. Serve with additional picante sauce;
garnish with black olives. Yield: 4 servings.
