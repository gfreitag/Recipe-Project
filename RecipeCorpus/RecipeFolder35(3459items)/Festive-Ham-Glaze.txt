Title: Festive Ham Glaze
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 16 servings
1 tb Orange peel -- grated
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Ground cloves
8 lb Cooked ham -- bone-in
1 1/2 c  Orange juice
1 1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
Score surface of ham, making diamond shapes 1/2 in deep. Place in a
large baking dish. In a bowl, mix remaining ingredients; pour over
ham. Cover and refrigerate overnight, turning ham occasionally.
Reserving glaze, remove ham to a rack in a shallow roasting pan,
Insert meat thrrmometer. Bake, uncovered at 325 deg. until
thermometer. Bake uncovered at 325 deg. until thermometer registers
140 deg. about 2-4 hours, brushing occasionally with glaze. Yield: 10
to 16 servings.
