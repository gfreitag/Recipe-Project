Title: Mom's Raisin Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 72 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 c  Water
2 c  Raisins
1 c  Shortening
1 3/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs -- lightly beaten
1 ts Vanilla extract
3 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
Combine raisins and water in a small saucepan; bring to a boil. Gook
for 3 mlnutes; remove from the heat and let cool (do not drain). In a
mixing bowl, cream shortening; gradually add sugar. Add eggs and
vanilla. Combine dry ingredients; gradually add to creamed mixture
and blend thoroughly. Stir in nuts and raisins. Drop by teaspoonfuls
2 in. apart on greased baking sheets. Bake at 350" for 12-14 minutes.
Yield; about 6 dozen.
