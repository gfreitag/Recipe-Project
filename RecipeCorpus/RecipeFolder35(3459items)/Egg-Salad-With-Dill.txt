Title: Egg Salad With Dill
Categories: Sandwich, Cheese/eggs, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper -- to taste
8    Eggs, hard-boiled
1/2 c  Purple onions -- finely
-chopped
1/3 c  Fresh dill -- chopped
Peel the eggs and quarter them.  Place in a mixing bowl with the
onion and dill.  In another bowl whisk together the mayonaise, sour
cream and mustard and pour over the egg mixture.   Toss gently and
season to taste with salt and pepper.  Toss again.  Serve immediately.
