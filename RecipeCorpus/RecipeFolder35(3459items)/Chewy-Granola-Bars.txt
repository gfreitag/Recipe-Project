Title: Chewy Granola Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Quick cooking oats
1 1/4 c  Crispy rice cereal
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Raisins
Or semisweet chocolate chips
Optional
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Honey
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 ea Egg
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Ground cinnamon
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars. Add honey, vanilla and
egg; mix well. Combine dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture. Stir
in oats, cereals and nuts. Add raisins or chocolate chip if desired.
Press into a greased 13x9x2 inch baking pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 25
to 30 min. or until the top top is lightly browned. yield 2 doz.
