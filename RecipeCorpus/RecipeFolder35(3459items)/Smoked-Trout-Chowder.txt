Title: Smoked Trout Chowder
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Bbq/grill, Soup/stew
Yield: 2 servings
Salt and pepper
Parsley
Dill
1/3 lb Hot smoked lake trout
1 c  Cream of potato, celery or
-mushroom soup
1 c  Milk
Heat fish and milk to boiling. Flake fish and add. Season to taste
with salt and pepper. Serve and sprinkle with parsley and dill.

From: Smoking Salmon & Trout by Jack Whelan. Published by: Airie
