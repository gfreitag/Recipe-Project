Title: Spinach Garlic Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Italian seasoning
1/2 ts Fennel seed -- crushed
1 lg Tomato -- seeded & chopped
1 1/2 c  Mozzarella cheese -- 6 oz.
Shredded
1 tb Dried basil
Chopped fresh parsley,
Optional
Bread
1    Loaf
French or Italian bread 1
Pound
1 md Onion -- chopped
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
3 tb Olive oil
1 pk Spinach
Thawed & well drained frozen
& chopped
1 tb Parmesan cheese -- grated
Cut bread in half lengthwise. Place with cut side upon a baking
sheetl; set aside. In a skillet, saute onion and garlic in oil until
tender. Add spinach and stir until heated through,about l
minute.Remove from the heat. Stir in Parmesan cheese,ltalian
seasoning and fenne seed; mix well. Spread evenly over bread halves.
Sprinkle with tomato, mozzarella and basil. Bake at 400 deg. for 8-l0
minutes or until cheese is melted. Garnish with parsley if desired.
Cut into slices and serve immediately. Yield: 8-10 servings
