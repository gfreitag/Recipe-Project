Title: Fluffy Cranberry Cheese Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Cream cheese -- 3 ounces
Softened
1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Whipped topping -- thawed
1 ea Pastry shell -- 9 inches
Baked
CRANBERRY TOPPING:
1 pk Raspberry flavored gelatin
3    Ounces
1/3 c  Sugar
1 1/4 c  Cranberry juice
1 cn Jellied cranberry sauce -- 8
Ounces
FILLING:
In a mixing bowl, combine gelatin and sugar; set aside. In a saucepan,
bring cranberry juice to a boil. Remove from the heat and pour over
gelatin mixture, stirring to dissolve. Stir in the cranberry sauce.
Chill until slightly thickened. Meanwhile, in another mixing bowl,
beat cream cheese, sugar, milk and vanilla until fluffy. Fold in the
whipped topping. Spread evenly into pie shell. Beat cranberry topping
until frothy; pour over filling. Chill overnight. Yield: 6-8 servings
