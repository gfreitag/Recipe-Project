Title: Bob's Barbecue Potato Salad
Categories: Route 66, Salads, Bbq/grill, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
2 qt Salad Dressing
1 c  Mustard
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
5 ga Potatoes
16    Hard-Cooked Eggs -- sliced
2 c  Onion -- finely chopped
1 qt Sweet Relish -- with juice
Boil potatoes until tender, then peel and slice while hot. Add eggs,
onion, relish, salad dressing and mustard. Salt and pepper to taste.
Allow salad to season in the refrigerator. If the salad is to stiff,
boil 2 cups of water and immediately pour over the misture, stirring
in quickly.
