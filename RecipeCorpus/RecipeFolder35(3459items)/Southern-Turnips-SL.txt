Title: Southern Turnips SL
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Bacon drippings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Red pepper
2 tb Minced fresh parsley
2 lb Turnips; peeled and cubed
2 md New potatoes; unpeeled and
-cubed
1 ea Bay leaf
4 c  Water
1 md Onion; chopped
Combine turnips, potatoes, bay leaf, and water in a Dutch oven.
Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes or until
vegetables are tender.  Drain; remove bay leaf.
Saute onion in bacon drippings until tender.  Add turnips,
potatoes, salt, pepper, and red pepper; toss gently.  Spoon into
serving dish; sprinkle with parsley.

From Evelyn Snellings of Virginia in "Southern Living" September, 1987
