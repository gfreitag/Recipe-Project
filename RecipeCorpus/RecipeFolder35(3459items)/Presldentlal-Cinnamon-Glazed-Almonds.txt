Title: Presldentlal Cinnamon-Glazed Almonds
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sugar
4 ts Ground cinnamon
3 c  Whole almonds
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
2 ea Egg whites
pn Salt
Place butter in a 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan. Bake at 325 deg.
until melted, about 5-7 minutes. Meanwhile, in a mixing bowl, beat egg
whites with salt until soft peaks form. Gradually add sugar, beating
until stiff peaks form. Fold in cinnamon and almonds; pour over
butter and toss to coat. Bake ar 325 deg. for 40 minutes, turning
every 10 minutes, or until almonds are crisp. Yield: 3 cups
