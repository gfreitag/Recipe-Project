Title: CARROT HASH BROWNS
Categories: Low cal/fat, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 3 servings
3 md Potatoes, all purpose *1 *2
1/2 sm Whit onion *2
1/2 ts Sea salt
2 tb Butter
1 ts Safflower oil
3 md Carrots *1 *2
*1 peeled *2 finely grated

In large skillet melt butter and oil.  Add carrots, potatoes and
onions. Add seasoning.  Saute until browned on one side.  Flip over
and saute on second side until browned.  Break apart into small
chunks or serve in wedges cut from the round.
