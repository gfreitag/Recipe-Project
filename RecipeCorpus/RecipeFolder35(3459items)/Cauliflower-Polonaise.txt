Title: Cauliflower Polonaise
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
-florets
Salt -- to taste
Parmesan cheese -- to taste
Black pepper -- to taste
4 tb Unsalted butter
5 tb Fresh bread crumbs
1    Eggs, hard-boiled
2 tb Parsley -- minced
1    Head cauliflower -- trimmed,
Melt 2 tbsp of the butter in a small skillet and add the bread
crumbs. Cook over medium heat until lightly browned, 5 minutes.  Set
aside.  Press the hard cooked egg through a sieve or whirl in food
processor until finely minced.  Combine the crumbs, egg and parsley
in a small bowl and mix well. Set it aside.  Bring a large pot of
water to a boil and add the cauliflower.  Simmer until just tender, 6
to 8 minutes.  Drain thoroughly and pat dry.  Melt the remaining 2
tbsp butter in a large skillet and saute the cauliflower until heated
through, 3 to 4 minutes.  Season with salt and sprinkle with the
reserved garnish.  Toss to coat well.  Serve the cauliflower
immediately with a generous sprinkling of cheese and pepper.
