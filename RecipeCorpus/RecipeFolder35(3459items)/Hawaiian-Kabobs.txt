Title: Hawaiian Kabobs
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 tb Brown sugar
2 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Pepper
Cooked rice, optional
Tomatoes
1 cn Unsweetened pineapple chunks
20    Ounces
2 lg Green peppers -- cut in l"
Pcs
1 lg Onion -- quartered optional
16 md Fresh mushrooms
18    Cherry
1/2 c  Soy sauce
Drain pineapple, reserving 1/2 cup juice Place pineapple chunks and
vegtables in a large bowl; set aside. In a saucepan combine reserved
pineapple juice with soy sauce, olive oil, brown sugar and seasonings;
bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered,for 5 minutes.
Pour over vegetable mixture; cover and refrigerate for at least 1
hour, stirring occasionally. Remove pineapple and vegetables from
marinade and reserve marinade. Alternate pineapple, green pepper,
onion if desired, mushrooms and tomatoes on skewers. Grill kabobs for
20 minutes or until sofl, turning and basting with marinade
frequently. Serve over rice if desired. Yield: 8 servings
