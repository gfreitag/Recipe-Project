Title: Herb-Infused Oil
Categories: Condiments, Information, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 cup
-basil, oregano, thyme mix
1 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Herbs, chopped
1. Heat the olive oil gently over medium heat, until it is hot but
not yet at frying temperature, 2-3 minutes.

2. Add the herbs. Remove from the heat. Steep uncovered for 6-8 hours.

3. Strain and discard the herbs. Put the oil in a glass jar.

Use immediately or refrigerate for up to 3 months.

Substitute for olive oil in salad dressing, use on pasta and in pasta
salads.
