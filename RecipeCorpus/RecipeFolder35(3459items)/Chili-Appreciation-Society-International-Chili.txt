Title: Chili Appreciation Society International Chili
Categories: Chili, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
16 oz Beef stock
1 tb Cumin -- ground
2 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Pepper -- ground
4 tb Chili powder
1/4 c  Masa
1 c  Water
1 tb Paprika
2 lb Chuck beef, cubed -- in
Stew-sized pieces
1 md Onion -- chopped
2 tb Chopped suet or vegetable
-il
4    Jalapenos, stemmed, seeded,
-alved
8 oz Tomato sauce
12 oz Beer
Brown beef and onion in fat. Add peppers, tomato, beer, stock, cumin,
garlic, pepper, and half the chili powder. Simmer until meat is done,
about 2 hr.
Make a paste of the masa and water. Stir this quickly into the chili.
Add the paprika and the rest of the chili powder. Cook 15 min.
Remove Jalapenos and serve.
