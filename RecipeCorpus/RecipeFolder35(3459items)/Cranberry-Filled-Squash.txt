Title: Cranberry Filled Squash
Categories: Side dish, Holiday
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Butter, melted
ds Salt
4 sm Acorn squash
1 c  Chopped unpared apple
1 c  Fresh cranberries, chopped
1 sm Orange, peeled and diced
Cut squash in half lengthwise, remove seeds.

Place cut side down in 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking dish.  Bake in
preheated 350 degree oven for 35 minutes.  Turn cut side up, sprinkle
with salt

Combine remaining ingredients, fill squash with fruit mixture.
Continue baking for another 25 minutes or until squash is tender.
