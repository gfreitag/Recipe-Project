Title: Seven-Minute Puddlng
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Egg yolks
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/3 c  Sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
2 c  Milk
In a microwave-safe mixing bowl, combine sugar and cornstarch. With a
hand mixer, beat in milk and egg yolks until smooth. Microwave an
medium for 5 minutes. Beat well with mixer. Microwave on high for 2
minutes; stir. Blend in butter and vanilla. Pour into serving dishes;
cool. Yield: 3-4 servings
