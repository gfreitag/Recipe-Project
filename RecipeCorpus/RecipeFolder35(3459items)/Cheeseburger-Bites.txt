Title: Cheeseburger Bites
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 24 servings
1 ds Pepper
6 sl Bread
24    Cubes cheddar cheese, 1/2"
-cubes
1 ea Egg -- yolk beaten
1/2 lb Lean ground beef
2 tb Grated onions
1/2 ts Salt
In a bowl, combine egg yolk, ground beef, onion, salt and pepper.
Shape mixture by teaspoonfuls into 24 balls. remove crusts from
bread; roll flat and cut into 1 1/2 rounds. Place meatballs on bread
rounds; make a depression in each ball and fill with acube of cheese,
making sure bread is covered with meat mixture. Place on baking
sheet. Broil in preheated oven about 6 inch from heat for 3 to 5
minutes or until no pink remains. Garnish with ketchup, mustard,
slice green onions or sliced dill pickles if desired. Yield 2 dozen.
