Title: Ollie's Restaurant Cinnamon Rolls
Categories: Pastry, Route 66
Yield: 1 batch
35 c  Flour
Butter -- melted
Cinnamon
Sugar
3 qt Milk -- scalded and cooled
1 1/2 c  Yeast
4 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
Add yeast to the milk and let stand to proof. Combine sugar, eggs, and
flour. Add milk and yeast mixture and knead to combine. Let dough
rise in warm place until doubled in bulk. Roll out to 1/4-inch
thickness. Cover with melted butter and a mixture of cinnamon and
sugar. Roll dough and cut into 3/4-inch slices. Place on greased
baking sheets and let rise again for 30 to 40 mins. Bake for 8 to 10
mins in a preheated 375 F oven. Serve warm.
