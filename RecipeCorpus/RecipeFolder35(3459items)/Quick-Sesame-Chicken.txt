Title: Quick Sesame Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Rubbed sage
4    (6oz) chicken breast
-halves, skinned
Vegetable cooking spray
1 cn (8oz) unsweetened pineapple
-slices, undrained
1 tb Sesame seeds, toasted
3 tb Honey
DRain the pineapple slices, reserving 3 tbs juice; set pineapple
slices aside. Combine the reserve pineapple juice, sesame seeds,
honey, and sage in a small bowl; stir well.

Place the chicken, bone side up, on a broiler pan coated with cooking
spray. Brush the chicken with honey mixture. Broil for 25 minutes,
basting frequently with honey mixture. Turn the chicken over; broil
an additional 15 minutes or until tender. Place chicken on a serving
platter; set aside, and keep warm.

Place the pineapple slices on the broiling pan and brush with honey
mixture. Broil 2Â« minutes per side. Cut pineapple slices in half and
arrange over chicken.
