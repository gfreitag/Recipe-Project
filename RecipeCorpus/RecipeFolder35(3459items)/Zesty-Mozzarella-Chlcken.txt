Title: Zesty Mozzarella Chlcken
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Dried aregano
8 ea Boneless skinless chicken
Breast halves
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 8 ounces
1 ts Dried basil
1 c  Mozzarella cheese -- 4 oz
Shredded
1 ea Egg white -- lightly beaten
2 tb Milk
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
2 tb Parmesan cheese -- grated
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Cayenne
1/4 ts Pepper
In a shallow bowl, combine egg white and milk. In another bowl,
combine the bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese and seasonings. Dip each
chicken breast in the egg white mixture, then in the crumb mixture.
Melt butter in a skillet; brown chicken on both sides until no longer
pink and juices run clear. Meanwhile, heat tomato sauce and basil
until warm. When chicken is done, sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
Remove from the heat and cover for 2-3 minutes or until cheese melts.
Serve with tomato-basil sauce. Yield: 4 servings
