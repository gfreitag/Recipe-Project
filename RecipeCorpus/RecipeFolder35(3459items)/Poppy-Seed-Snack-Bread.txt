Title: Poppy Seed Snack Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
4 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
1 c  Hot water
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Poppy seeds
1 pk White cake mix
(l8-1/2 ounces)without
Pudding
1 pk Instant coconut cream
Pudding mix -- 3.4 ounces
In a mixing bowl, combine the cake and pudding mixes, eggs, water and
oil; beat for 2 minutes. Fold in poppy seeds. Pour into two greased
and floured 8-1/2-in. x 4-1/2-in. x 2-1/2-in. loaf pans. Bake at 350
deg. for 35-40 minutes or until golden. Yield: 2 loaves
