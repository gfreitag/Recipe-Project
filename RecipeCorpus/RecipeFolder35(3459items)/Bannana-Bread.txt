Title: Bannana Bread
Categories: Low cal/fat, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Unsweetened apple sauce
3 ea Egg whites
1 ts Vanilla
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Ground cinnamon
2 c  Mashed banana
Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and cinnamon and set
aside. In another bowl combine banana, apple sauce, egg whites and
vanilla. Add dry ingredients to apple sauce mixture all at once and
stir until moistened. Pour batter into loaf pan sprayed with non
stick cooking spray. Bake at 350 for 50 to 60 minutes.  Cool in pan
10 minutes. Remove from pan and cool completely.  Makes one loaf
