Title: Meatless Spaghetti
Categories: Italian, Tasteofhome
Yield: 14 servings
Undrained
2 cn Tomato sauce -- 15 oz each
1 cn Tomato paste -- 6 oz
1 tb Brown sugar -- optional
2 ts Dried basil
2 ts Dried oregano
2 ts Dried parsley -- flakes
1 ts Salt -- optional
1/2 ts Pepper
2 lb Spaghetti -- cooked &
Drained
Beef bouillon
6    Cloves garlic -- minced
1 c  Chopped celery
1 lg Chopped onion
2 tb Cooking oil
6 sm Zucchini -- chopped
1 lg Green pepper -- chopped
1 cn Olives -- 6 oz pitted ripe
4    Cubes
1 c  Hot water
1    Jar sliced mushrooms -- 6 oz
Drained
1 cn Tomatoes -- 28 oz diced
In a large saucepan or a dutch oven, saute garlic, celery and onion
in oil until tender. Add zucchini, green pepper and olives; saute for
2-3 min. Dissolve bouillon in water; add to begetables. Add the next
10 ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for
1 hr. stirring occasionally. Serve over spaghetti. Yield: 14 servings.
