Title: Surprise Stufflng
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Condensed cream of chicken
Soup -- 10-3/4 oz undiluted
3 ea Eggs beaten
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
2 c  Yellow summer squash --
Chopped
1 md Onion -- chopped
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
4 c  Crumbled corn bread
In a large skillet, saute the squash and onion in butter until tender.
Remove from the heat; stir in remaining ingredients. Transfer to a
greased l-qt. casserole. Bake, uncovered, at 350 deg. for 40 minutes.
Yield: 6 servings
