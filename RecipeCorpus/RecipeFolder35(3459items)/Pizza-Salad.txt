Title: Pizza Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 16 servings
2/3 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
2 ts Dried oregano
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Croutons -- optional
1 lb Macaroni -- spiral cooked
3 md Tomatoes -- seeded and
Diced
1 lb Cheddar cheese -- cubed
2 bn Green onion -- sliced
3 oz Pepperoni slices
3/4 c  Vegetable oil
In a large bowl, combine macaroni, tomatoes, cheddar cheese, green
onions and pepperoni. In a small bowl, combine, oil Parmesan cheese,
vinegar and seasonings; pour over macaroni mixture. Cover and
refrigerate for several hours. Top with croutons just abefore serving
if desired. Yield: 16 servings.
