Title: Honeyed Pork Chops
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 sm Onion, finely chopped
1/4 ts Ginger, ground
1/8 ts Pepper
1 tb Toasted sesame seeds
6 ea Pork chops, cut 3/4 " thick
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1 tb Ketchup
1/2 c  Water
3 tb Honey
Toasted Sesame seeds: Spread sesame seeds in shallow pan and place in
350 F. oven.  Remove pan when seeds are a pale brown, about 20
minutes. Watch to prevent overbrowning which will destroy the flavor.

Brown chops in a small amount of fat; place in 13 x 9 x 2" baking
pan. (Do NOT SALT CHOPS! Soy sauce will do the job)

Combine remaining ingredients except sesame seeds. Pour over chops.
Sprinkle on sesame seeds.

Cover baking pan and bake in 325 F. oven for about 1 hour or until
chops are tender.
