Title: Cheesy Garlic Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1    Loaf French bread,
About 20 inches, halved
Lengthwise
1/3 c  Fresh parsley -- minced
Optional
Paprika, optional
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Sharp cheddar cheese -- 4
oz Shredded
1 c  Green onions -- thinly
Sliced w/tops
3 ea Garlic cloves -- minced
Mix mayonnaise, cheese, onions and garlic; spread on bread halves. If
desired, sprinkle with parsley and paprika. Wrap each half in foil.
Refrigerate for 1-2 hours or freeze. Unwrap and place on a baking
sheet. Bake at 400 deg. for 8-10 minutes (20-25 minutes if frozen) or
until puffed but not brown. Cut into slices. Yield: 12-15 servings
