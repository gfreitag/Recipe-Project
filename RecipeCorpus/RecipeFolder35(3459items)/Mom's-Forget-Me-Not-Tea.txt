Title: Mom's Forget-Me-Not Tea
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 4 cups mix
1 pk Cherry-flavored soft drink
.  mix; unsweetened,  0.14oz
2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1    Jar instant orange breakfast
.  drink mix; 15oz
1 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Instant tea mix; unsweetened
1/2 c  Lemonade mix; presweetened
In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients, mix well.  Store in an
airtight container.  Give with serving instructions.

Makes about 4 cups of tea mix.

To Serve:  Stir 2 heaping tablespoons of tea mix into 8oz of hot or
cold water.

Source:  A pamphlet Mom received in the mail.
