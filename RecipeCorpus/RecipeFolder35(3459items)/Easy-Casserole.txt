Title: Easy Casserole
Categories: Meats, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
Sloppy Joe sauce
1 c  Water
1 pk (16 oz) shell macaroni,
Cooked and drained
2 c  Grated mozzarella
1/2 c  Grated parmesan
1 lb Pork sausage
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Italian seasoning
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 cn (29 1/2 oz) Hunts Manwich
In a large skillet, cook pork and beef until it loses redness; drain
fat. Add onion, Italian seasoning and garlic powder; cook until onion
is soft. Add Manwich and water; simmer 5 minutes. In 3-qt casserole,
layer half the meat mixture, macaroni and both cheeses. Repeat layers
using remaining meat, macaroni and cheeses. Bake at 350 for 30 min.

The Sloppy Joe sauce really gives this its flavor.
