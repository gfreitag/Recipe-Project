Title: Mashed Spinach, Indian Style
Categories: Side dish, Indian, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Cayenne
2 md Tomatoes, chopped
Salt
1 ts Garmam masala
3 c  Packed spinach
1 tb Ghee
2 md Onions, diced
2 ea Garlic cloves, sliced
1 ts Grated ginger
Wash & drain spinach.  Bring to a boil in a pan with a little water,
remove from heat & drain very well.  Chop spinach.

Heat ghee in a skillet & saute onion & garlic until they are golden.
Add ginger, cayenne & tomatoes.  Stir over a moderate heat for 3
minutes.  Mix in the chopped spinach.  Add salt & cook, stirring, for
5 minutes. Sprinkle with garam masala & continue to stir-fry for 3 to
5 minutes.
