Title: Llght 'n' Rich Chocolate Puddlng
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Cornstarch
2 c  Skim milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
16 ea Envelopes sugar substitute
Or 6 tablespoons sugar
3 tb Unsweetened cocoa
In a saucepan, combine sugar substitute or sugar, cocoa and
cornstarch. Whisk in the milk. Simmer, stirring constantly, until
thickened, about 5 minutes. Stir in the vanilla. Cool. Yield: 4
servings. Diabetic Exchanges: One serving (prepared with sugar
substitute)  equals 1/2 milk, 3/4 starch; also, 95 calories, 63 mg
sodium, 2 mg cholesterol. 17 gm carbohydrate, 5 gm protein 1 gm fat
