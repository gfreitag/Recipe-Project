Title: Chlcken Wlth Mushroom Sauce
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Breast halves
1/3 c  Chicken broth -- or port
Wine
1 1/2 c  Heavy cream
Minced fresh parsley,
Optional
Paparika, optional
8 oz Fresh mushrooms -- thinly
Sliced
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
Salt and pepper to taste
8    Skinless boneless chicken
In a skillet, saute mushrooms in butter until tender. With a slotted
spoon, remove mushrooms and set aside. Reserve butter in the skillet.
Combine flour, nutmeg, salt and pepper; coat chicken pieces and shake
off excess. Brown chicken in skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms.
Add broth or wine to the remaining flour mixture; stir until smooth.
Fold in cream. Pour over the chicken and mushrooms; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until chicken is
no longer pink. Garnish with parsley and paprika if desired. Yleld: 4
servings
