Title: Bayou Burgers
Categories: Meats, Soup/stew, Cajun/creol
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Tb Prepared mustard
Hamburg buns or toast
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
10 oz Can chicken gumbo soup 284mL
Combine beef and onion in a 10 x 6 x 2" glass dish. Microwave 6-8
minutes, stirring twice, pour off fat.  (Plastic sieve is good for
this.) Blend in soup and mustard, cook 3 minutes or until hot.  Stir.
Serve with buns, toast or potato. Source: Cooking with Campbell's
Today Favorite quick recipe, Elizabeth Rodier Oct 93
