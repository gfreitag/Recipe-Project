Title: Carmel Pecan Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
1    Unbaked pastry shell
1 c  Pecans, coarsley chopped
1 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb All purpose flour
1/2 c  Butter, melted
2    Eggs, beaten
Combine sugar and flour, mixing well.  Add butter, eggs, milk and
vanilla; stir until well blended.  Pour mixture into pastry shell;
sprinkle with pecans.  Bake at 325 degrees for 50 minutes or until
set. Makes one 9 inch pie. Randy Rigg
