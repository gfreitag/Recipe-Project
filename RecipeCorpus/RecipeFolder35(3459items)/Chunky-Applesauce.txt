Title: Chunky Applesauce
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
2 ts Vanilla extract
1 ts Ground cinnamon
8 c  Tart cooking apples
Chopped peeled (about 3-1/2
Pounds)
Place all ingredients in a large saucepan or Dutch oven. Cover and
cook over medium-low heat for 30-40 minutes or until apples are
tender. Remove from the heat; mash apples (a potato masher works
well) until sauce is desired consistency. Serve warm or cold. Yield:
6 servings (about 3-1/2 cups)
