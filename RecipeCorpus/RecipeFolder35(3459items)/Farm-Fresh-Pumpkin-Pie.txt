Title: Farm Fresh Pumpkin Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 pie
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ginger
1/8 ts Cloves
4    Eggs, slightly beaten
1    Pie Shell, baked
1 1/2 c  Pumpkin, cooked
1 1/2 c  Evaporated Milk
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients in a double boiler and cook until thick.

Cool and poor into pie shell.

This pie filling can also be served as a pudding.

Source: Carroll's Pumpkin Farm
