Title: Blt Bities
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
1/3 c  Chopped green onions
3 tb Grated parmesan cheese
2 tb Fresh parsley -- snipped
20 ea Cherry tomatoes
1 lb Bacon -- cooked & crumbled
1/2 c  Mayonnaise -- or salad
Dressing
Cut a thin slice off of each tomato top. Scoop out and discard pulp.
Invert the tomatoes on a paper towel to drain, In a small bowl,
combine all remaining ingredients; mix well. Spoon into tomatoes.
Refrigerate for several hours. Yield: 16 to 20 appetizer servings.
