Title: Apple Pecan Syrup
Categories: Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Chopped apples
1/4 c  Chopped pecans
1/4 c  Butter
1 c  Maple syrup
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Put butter, syrup, and cinnamon in glass bowl and microwave on high 2
minutes.  Add apples and microwave on high 3 to 4 minutes more,
stirring twice during cooking.  Add pecans. From 365 Microwave
Calendar 1990. * CROSSPOSTED Submitted By HOME_COOKI  On   09-25-95
