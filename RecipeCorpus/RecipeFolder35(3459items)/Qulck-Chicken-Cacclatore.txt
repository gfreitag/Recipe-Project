Title: Qulck Chicken Cacclatore
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Green chilies -- 4 ounces
Chopped
1/4 ts Dried basil
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/4 ts Garlic powder
ds Cayenne pepper
Cooked spaghetti or rice,
Optional
1 md Green pepper -- cut in
Strips
1 md Onion -- sliced in rings
8 oz Fresh mushrooms -- sliced
1 tb Olive oil
4 ea Chicken breast halves --
Boned/skinned
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 15 ounces
In a large skillet, saute green pepper, onion and mushrooms in olive
oil for 4-5 minutes or until crisp-tender. Place the chicken breasts
over the vegetables. In a bowl, combine tomato sauce, chilies and
seasonings. Pour over the chicken; cover and simmer for 20 minutes or
until chicken is tender and no longer pink. Serve over spaghetti or
rice if desired. Yield: 4 servings
