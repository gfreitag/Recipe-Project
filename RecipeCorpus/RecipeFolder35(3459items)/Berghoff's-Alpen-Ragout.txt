Title: Berghoff's Alpen Ragout
Categories: Main dish, Route 66, Meats
Yield: 5 servings
1 1/2 c  Brown Gravy
3 oz White Wine
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
3 ds Tabasco Sauce
4 oz Sour Cream
2 1/2 lb Veal -- sliced in 2" strips
1/4 lb Butter
1/4 lb Fresh Mushrooms -- thinly
-sliced
1/2 lb Onions -- thinly sliced
Brown veal in butter. Saute mushrooms and onions in butter and add to
meat. Add the gravy and wine and cook for 20 mins. Season to taste
with salt, pepper, and Tabasco sauce. Add sour cream just before
serving. Makes 5 generous servings.
