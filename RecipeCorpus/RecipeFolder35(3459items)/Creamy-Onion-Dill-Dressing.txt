Title: Creamy Onion Dill Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Low-sodium beef bouillon --
Granules
1 ts Hot water
1 ts Dried onions -- minced
1 c  Plain lowfat yogurt
2 tb Light mayonnaise
1 1/4 ts Fresh dill weed -- snipped
1/2 ts Dried parsley -- flaked
1/4 ts Garlic powder
In a small bowl, mix yogurt and mayonnaise. Add dill, parsley, garlic
powder and pepper. In another bowl, dissolve bouillon in water. Add
onion; add to yogurt mixture and mix well. Refrigerate several hours
before serving. Yield: 1 cup. Low fat.
