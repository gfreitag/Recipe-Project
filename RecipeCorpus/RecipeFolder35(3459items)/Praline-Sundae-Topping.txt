Title: Praline Sundae Topping
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
1 c  Evaporated milk
1/2 c  Pecans -- toasted
1 ts Vanilla extract
Ice Cream
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
16 lg Marshmallows
2 tb Light corn syrup
1 ds Salt
Melt butter in a saucepan. Add  brown sugar, marshmallows, corn syrup
and salt. Cook and stir over low heat until marshmallows are melted
and mixture comes to a boil. Boil for 1 min. Remove from the heat;
cool for 5 min. Stir in evaporated milk, pecans and vanilla; mix
well. Serve warm or cold over ice cream. Store inthe refrigerator.
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
