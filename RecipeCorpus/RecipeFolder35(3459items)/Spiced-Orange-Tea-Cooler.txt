Title: Spiced Orange Tea Cooler
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Liquid sucaryl
1 1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lime juice
1 1/2 c  Tea,chilled
Grated peel from 1 orange
3    Whole cloves
6    Whole allspice
1 c  Boiling water
Simmer grated orange peel and spices in boiling water 20 minutes;
strain. Add sucaryl,orange juice,lime juice,and tea.Pour over ice in
4 tall glasses.Garnish with orange slice,cherry and mint.

per serving; 44 calories, 1g protein, trace of fat, 11.g
carbohydrates(with sugar -152calories.
