Title: Runner's World  --  Mocha Madness
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1    Shot of expresso; decaf
.  or regular
2 ts Cocoa powder
4 sm Ice cubes
1/2 c  Vanilla frozen yogurt;
.  low-fat  (naturally)
A cool, bracing, low-fat treat for you chocolate and coffee lovers.

Add all ingredients to the blender in the order listed, then blend on
high speed for 30 seconds.

Serve in a frosted glass.

Nutritional Information: per serving: 180 calories, 2g fat, 8g protein
