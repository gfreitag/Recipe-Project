Title: 1981 World Championship Butterfield Stageline Chili
Categories: Chili, Soup/stew
Yield: 25 servings
-chopped
1 tb Cumin
1 ts Salt
1 ts Oregano
1 tb Dry mustard
1 oz Tequila
1 cn Beer
1    3 oz jar Chili powder
2 ea Beef bouillon cubes
4 ea Medium onions -- minced
10 lb Lean beef brisket -- finely
-chopped
1/4 c  Oil
1 1/2    Cloves Garlic -- minced
2 lb Ground pork
1    7 oz can whole green chiles
-minced
1    15 oz can Tomato sauce
1 lb Whole tomatoes -- finely
Brown onions, and beef in oil. Stir in garlic, pork, chiles, cumin,
oregano, salt, dry mustard, tomato sauce, tomatoes, tequila, beer,
chili powder and bouillon cubes.
Bring to boil, then reduce heat and simmer 2-3 hours. Stir
occasionally. Do not stir the last 30 min before serving.
Serves 25
