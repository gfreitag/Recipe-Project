Title: Vanilla Oil
Categories: Herbs/spice, Condiments
Yield: 1 pint
1 pt Cooking oil
1    Vanilla bean
Place a long, split bean in a pint of good-quality cooking oil. Steep
for a week and use as needed.

For salads and sauteeing - try it with poultry, delicately seasoned
meats, seafood, and vegetables.

Source:  Old Farmer's Almanac, 1996
Submitted By DAVE SACERDOTE   On   10-16-95
