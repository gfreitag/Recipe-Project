Title: Spring River Inn Chess Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Route 66
Yield: 11 servings
3 3/4 c  Milk
3 lb Butter -- melted
11    9 Inch Pie Shells --
-unbaked
36    Eggs
4 tb Vanilla
3/4 c  Cornmeal
3/4 c  Lemon Juice
1 ga Sugar
Mix the above and pour into 11 unbaked 9-inch pie shells. Sprinkle
pecans or coconut on top if desired. Bake 35 to 40 mins in preheated
350 F oven. Makes 11 pies.
