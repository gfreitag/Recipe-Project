Title: Troplcal Frult Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Bananas -- sliced
1 cn Mandarin oranges -- 11 oz.
Drained
Shredded coconut
1/2 c  Plain yogurt -- or vanilla
1 tb Frozen orange juice
Concentrate
2 ea Kiwifruit -- peeled &
Sliced
In a small bowl, combine yogurt and orange juice concentrate. Place
fruit in individual dessert bowls. Drizzle each serving with yogurt
sauce. Sprinkle with coconut. Yield: 6 servings
