Title: Hearty Shortcake
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Prepared mustard
2 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c  Beef broth
3 c  Buttermilk biscuit mix *1
3 c  Brown beef cubes
10 oz Frozen peas, cooked, drained
3 ea Eggs, hard cooked, diced
1 ea Onion, chopped
*1 all purpose Combine all ingredients except biscuit mix. Heat
thoroughly, about 20 minutes.

Make biscuits by package directions ( may add 3/4 cup shredded cheese)
split hot biscuits in half and top with beef mixture.
