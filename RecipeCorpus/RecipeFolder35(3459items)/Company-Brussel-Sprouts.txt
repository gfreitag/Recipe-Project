Title: Company Brussel Sprouts
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/3 c  Chicken broth -- or dry
White wine
1 ts Olive oil
1/2 ts Pepper
pn Salt
Brussels sprouts -- trimmed
And halved
4 ea Bacon strips -- diced
1    Dozen
-
1 md Onion -- chopped
2 tb Fresh chives -- snipped
1 ea Carrot -- thinly sliced
10 ea Stuffed green olives --
Sliced
In a skillet, fry bacon just until cooked. Drain, reserving 2
tablespoons drippings. Add remaining ingredients; cook and stir over
medium-high heat for 10-15 minutes or until brussels sprouts are
crisp-tender. Yield: 4 servings
