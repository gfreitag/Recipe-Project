Title: Nutty Sweet Potatoe Biscuits
Categories: Breakfast, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Sweet potatoes -- mashed
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
4 ts Baking powder
1 1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
3/4 c  Nuts -- chopped
In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon,
nutmeg and nuts. In another bowl, combine sweet potatoes, sugar,
butter and vanilla; add to flour mixture and mix well. Turn onto a
lightly floured surface; knead slightly. Roll dough to 1/2-in.
thickness. Cut with a 2-1/2-in. biscuit cutter and place on lightly
greased baking sheets. Bake at 450 deg. for 12 minutes or until
golden brown. Yield: 1-1/2 to 2 dozen
