Title: DiJon Bean Salad
Categories: Low cal/fat, Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Italian dressing, (no oil)
3 tb Vinegar, red wine
2 ts Dijon mustard
pn Pepper
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 pk Equal sweetener
20 oz Kidney beans *1
16 oz Corn, canned *1
16 oz Chickpeas, canned *1
1 c  Carrots, fine chopped
2 tb Onion, chopped
3 md Green onions, chopped
3 tb Sweet pickle relish
*1 Drained and rinsed

Combine first 7 ingredients in large bowl. Place remaining
ingredients in separate bowl.  Mix well and pour over bean mixture.
Toss to coat.

210 Protein 10.7 g. Sodium 274 mg.
40.3 g. Fat 1.9 g. Cholesterol 0.0 mg
Calories
Carbohydrate
