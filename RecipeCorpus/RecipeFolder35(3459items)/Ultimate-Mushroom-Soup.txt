Title: Ultimate Mushroom Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
8 c  Hot chicken broth
2 c  Heavy cream
Salt and white pepper
-to taste
6    White mushroom caps;
-sliced
Chives;  chopped
2 lb Fresh mushrooms; wiped
-clean
And chopped
8 tb Unsalted butter
2 cl Garlic; minced
12    Scallions; chopped
8 tb All-purpose flour
In a large saucepan over medium heat, saute mushrooms in butter with
garlic and scallions for 7-10 minutes, stirring. Stir in the flour,
remove from heat and bring to room temperature. Return to heat and
stir in hot broth, a little at a time, beating constantly. Cook,
stirring until bubbly. Stir in the cream and season to taste. Add
sliced mushrooms and ladle into bowls. Sprinkle with chives before
serving.  Keeps well for several days.

For best of the best, don't skimp and don't substitute! Serves 6
