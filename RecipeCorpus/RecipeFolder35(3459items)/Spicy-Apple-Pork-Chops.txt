Title: Spicy Apple Pork Chops
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
Vegetable cooking spray
2 tb Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Apple cider
2 ea Apples; peeled and sliced
1/3 c  Raisins
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Ground allspice
4 ea 1-inch thick porkchops
2 tb Butter
MMMMM------------------CARAWAY BUTTERED NOODLES-----------------------
1 ts Caraway seeds
8 oz Package egg noodles
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
Combine first 5 ingredients in a small bowl.  Dredge pork chops
with 2 tablespoons flour mixture, reserving remaining flour mixture.
Melt butter in a large skillet; brown pork chops on both sides.
Reserve meat drippings. Place pork chops in a 2-quart baking dish
coated with cooking spray.
Add brown sugar and remaining 2 tablespoons flour mixture to meat
drippings; stir until smooth.  Cook 1 minute, stirring constantly.
Gradually add apple cider; cook over medium heat, stirring constantly,
until cider mixture is thickened and bubbly.
Arrange apple slices over chops; sprinkle with cinnamon.  Cover
and bake at 350 degrees F for 1 hour or until chops are tender.
Serve immediately with Caraway Buttered Noodles. Yield: 4 servings.

CARAWAY BUTTERED NOODLES:
Cook noodles according to package directions; drain well.  Melt
butter in a small skillet; add caraway seeds.
Transfer noodles to a warmed serving platter; drizzle with
caraway-butter mixture.  Toss until noodles are coated. Yield: 4
servings.
