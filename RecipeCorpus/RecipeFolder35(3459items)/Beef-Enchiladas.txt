Title: Beef Enchiladas
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 md Green pepper -- chopped
1 tb Cooking oil
1 cn Tomato sauce -- l5 ounces
1 cn Chopped green chilies -- 4
Ounces
2 ts Chili powder
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- 4 ounces
Shredded
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Cottage cheese
1 cn Chopped ripe olives -- 4-1/4
oz Drained
2 tb Minced fresh parsley
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
8    Flour tortillas -- 7 inches
SAUCE:
1 md Onion -- chopped
In a skillet, brown the ground beef. Drain. Combine with cottage
cheese, olives, parsley, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Place about
1/3 cup filling on each tortilla; roll up. Place tortillas, seam side
down, in an ungreased 13-in. x 9-in, x 2-in. baking dish. For sauce,
saute the onion and green pepper in oil until tender. Add tomato
sauce, green chilies, chili powder, sugar and garlic powder. Pour
over tortillas. Cover and bake at 350 deg. for 30 minutes. Sprinkle
with cheese and return to the oven for 5 minutes or until cheese
melts. Yield: 4 servings
