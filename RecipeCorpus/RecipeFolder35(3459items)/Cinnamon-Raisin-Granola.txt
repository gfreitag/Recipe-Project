Title: Cinnamon-Raisin Granola
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 30 servings
1/4 c  Honey
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 cn Raisins
4 c  Oats -- old-fashioned oats
1 c  Coconut
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
In a large bowl, combine oats and coconut; set aside. In a saucepan,
combine brown sugar, oil, honey and cinnamon; bring to a boil. Remove
from the heat and stir in vanilla. Pour over oat mixture; stir to
coat. Spread in a large shallow baking pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 15
to 20 min. stirring occasionally. Cool. Add raisins. Store in an
airtight container. Yield: 6 cups.
