Title: Corn-Noodle Casserole
Categories: Crockpot, Pasta/rice, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 x  Margarine stick
1/4 c  Sugar
1 pk Curly noodles (12 oz)
2 cn Cream style corn
1 lb American cheese; shredded
Cook and drain noodles. Add remaining ingredients. Mix together and
put in crock pot. Cook until cheese is melted.

