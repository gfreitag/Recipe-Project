Title: The Big Texan "Texas Caviar"
Categories: Dips/spread, Route 66
Yield: 1 servings
-chopped
1/4 c  Seasoned Salt
1/2 c  Chili Powder
1/2 c  Ground Cumin
1 ts Ground Red Pepper
1 ga Black-Eyed Peas -- drained
5 md Jalapenos -- minced
1 sm White Onion -- chopped
2 c  Italian Dressing
2    Green Bell Peppers --
Combine black-eyed peas with remaining ingredients. Serve chilled
with corn chips. Texas-sized recipe makes 5 quarts.
