Title: Two-Minute Cookles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Or old-fashioned oatmeal
5 tb Unsweetened cocoa
1/2 c  Raisins
Chopped nuts or coconut
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Sugar
3 c  Dry quick-cooking oatmeal
In a large saucepan, heat butter, milk and sugar. Bring to a boil,
stirring occasionally. Boil for 1 minute. Remove from the heat. Stir
in oatmeal, cocoa and raisins, nuts or coconut. Drop by
tablespoonsful onto waxed paper. Cool. Yield: about 3 dozen cookies
