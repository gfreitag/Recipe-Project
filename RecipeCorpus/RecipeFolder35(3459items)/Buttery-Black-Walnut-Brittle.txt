Title: Buttery Black Walnut Brittle
Categories: Candies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Butter -- no substitutes
1 1/2 c  Black walnuts
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Corn syrup
1/4 c  Water
In a saucepan, cook sugar, corn syrup and water until sugar dissolves
and mixture comes to a boil. Add butter; cook until mixture reaches
280 deg. on a candy thermometer. Stir in walnuts; cook until 300 deg.
(hard crack stage). Remove from the heat and stir in baking soda.
Spread immediately into a greased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan.
When cool, break into pieces. Yield: 1-1/4 pounds
