Title: Veloute Sauce
Categories: French, Poultry, Meats, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 1 cup
Salt and pepper
Herbs to taste
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1 c  Stock; beef, chicken or fish
Melt butter in a saucepan over moderate heat until foaming but not
browned. Add the flour and whisk until well blended. Heat the liquid
separately until almost boiling. Add all at once while stirring
vigorously. Bring it just to a boil to thicken and reduce heat.
Season with salt, pepper and up to 1/2 ts dried or 1 ts chopped fresh
herbs. Simmer gently for 5 min.

Use 1 tb each butter and flour for a thin sauce and 3tb each for a
very thick sauce.
