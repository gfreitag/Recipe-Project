Title: Red-Cooked Cabbage
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Peanut oil
2 lb Napa cabbage
-cut into 1" pieces
2 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ts Cornstarch
2 tb Dry sherry
1 md Onion(s)
-cut into large dice
4 sl Ginger
-1"x1/2"x1/4"
2    Garlic clove(s), crushed
1. Dissolve the cornstarch in 1 tbs of the sherry and set aside.

2. In a wok or large skillet, sautÂ‚ the onion, ginger and garlic in
the oil over medium heat until the garlic begins to color, 3-4
minutes. Add the cabbage and toss.

3. Stir in the soy sauce, sugar, remaining 1 tbs sherry, and the
pepper. Cover and cook until the cabbage is wilted, 10-15 minutes.
Uncover and remove the ginger.

4. Stir the cornstarch mixture and add to cabbage, stirring with a
wooden spoon, until the liquid has thickened and the cabbage is
glazed, about 30 seconds. Serve hot.
