Title: Crockpot Chicken And Rice Casserole
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Crockpot
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c  Celery -- diced
1 c  Minute rice
4 lg Chicken breasts
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 cn Cream of celery soup
Mix in crock-pot the 3 cans of soup and rice. Place the chicken on
top of the mixture, then add the diced celery. Cook for 3 hours on
high or 4 hours on low.

Makes 4 servings.

More rice, about 1/2 cup, and 2 other chicken breasts may be added to
make 6 servings.
