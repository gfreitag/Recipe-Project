Title: Lemon Turkey In Pastry Shells
Categories: Poultry, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Cooked Vegetables; OR
16 oz Frozen Mixed Vegetables;
-Thawed, 1 Pkg
2 c  Cooked Turkey Or Chicken;
-Cubed
10 oz Frozen Puff Pastry Shells;
-1 Pkg
24 oz Seasoned Turkey Gravy; 2
-Jars
2 tb Lemon Juice
1/4 ts Dried Thyme Leaves; Crushed
Bake the pastry shells according to the package directions.
Meanwhile, in a saucepan over medium heat, combine the gravy with
lemon juice, thyme, vegetables, turkey or chicken, heating through.
Spoon the mixture into the baked pastry shells and serve hot.

Each Serving Contains:

447 Calories    23 Grams Fat    38 Milligrams Cholesterol
841 Milligrams Sodium
