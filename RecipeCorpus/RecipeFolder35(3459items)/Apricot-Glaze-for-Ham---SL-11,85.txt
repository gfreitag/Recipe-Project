Title: Apricot Glaze for Ham - SL 11/85
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cake/cookie, Fruit, Meats
Yield: 1 -2/3 cups
3/4 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 ea 17-oz can apricot halves;
-drained
1/2 c  Light or dark corn syrup
Combine all ingredients in container of an electric blender; process
until smooth.
To serve, brush glaze on ham during last hour of baking, basting
every 15 minutes.  Heat remaining glaze; serve with ham. Yield: 1-2/3
cups.

Peggy H. Amos of Virginia, in November, 1985 "Southern Living" Typos
by Jeff Pruett
