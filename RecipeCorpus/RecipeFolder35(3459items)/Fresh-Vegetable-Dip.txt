Title: Fresh Vegetable Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
1 ts Dried parsley -- flakes
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ds Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 c  Sour cream -- 12 oz
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Dried onions -- minced
1 ts Dried dill weed
In a small bowl, combine sour cream, mayonnaise, onion, dill, parsley,
garlic salt and worcestershrire sauce. chill for at least l hr. Serve
with fresh vegetables. Yield: 2 cups.
