Title: Plum And Ginger Chutney
Categories: Relishes, Meats, Dips/spread, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Peeled and grated fresh
-ginger
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
2 lb Plums, pitted -- coarsely
-chopped
3/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
3    Cloves Garlic -- minced
1 sm Yellow onion -- minced
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
In a nonaluminum saucepan over high heat, combine all ingredients.
Stir well and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer,
uncovered, until the mixture thickens, 1-1/2 to 2 hours.
Transfer to jars or other containers with tight-fitting lids, let
cool, cover and refrigerate for up to 3 weeks.

Makes about 2 cups.
