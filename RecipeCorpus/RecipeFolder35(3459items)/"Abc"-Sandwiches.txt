Title: "Abc" Sandwiches
Categories: Snacks, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Walnuts -- finely chopped
12 sl Bread -- toasted & buttered
12 ea Bacon strips -- cooked
6 ea Hard-cooked eggs -- sliced
6 ea Tomato slices -- optional
2 c  Cheddar cheese -- 8 ounces
Shredded
1 md Apple -- finely chopped
3/4 c  Salad dressing -- or
Mayonnaise
In a bowl. combine cheese. apple. salad dressins and walnuts Spread
on 6 slices of bread: place 2 slices of bacon on each. Cover with egg
slices. Top with tomatoes if desired and remaining slices of bread.
Yield: 6 servings
