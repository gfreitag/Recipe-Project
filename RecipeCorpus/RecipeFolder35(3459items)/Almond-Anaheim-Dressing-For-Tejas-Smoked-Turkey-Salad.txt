Title: Almond-Anaheim Dressing For Tejas Smoked Turkey Salad
Categories: Salads, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 16 servings
1 tb White wine vinegar
1 tb Lime juice
1 c  Corn oil
1 1/2    Whole Anaheim chili peppers
1/4 c  Almond slivers -- blanched &
Toasted
STEP ONE: Prepare the Chiles--
Roast chiles over an open flame until the skin blisters. Then place
them in a covered bowl or plastic bag until the skin begins to
loosen. Peel and seed.

STEP TWO:
Place chiles, almonds, vinegar, and lime juice in the bowl of a food
processor fitted with a metal blade. Puree. Then, with the motor
running, slowly add oil until the mixture has thickened.

Serve with "Tejas Smoked Turkey Salad" recipe in this database.
