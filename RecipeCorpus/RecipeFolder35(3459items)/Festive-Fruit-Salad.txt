Title: Festive Fruit Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Pears -- 20 oz
Drained/choppe
3 ea Kiwifruit -- peeled and
Sliced
2 lg Apples -- unpeeled chopped
1 c  Pecan halves
1 cn Pineapple chunks -- 20 oz
1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb All-purpose flour
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
2 cn Mandarin oranges -- drained
11 oz Ea.
Drain pneapple, reserving juice. Set pineapple aside. Pour juice into
a small sauce pan; add sugar and flour. Bring to a boil. quickly stir
in egg; cook until thickened. Remove from the heat; cool.
Refrigerate. in a large bowl, combine pineapple, oranges, pears,
kiwi, apples and pecans. Pour dressing over and blend well. Cover and
chill for l hour. Yield: 12-16 servings
