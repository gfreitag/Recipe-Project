Title: DOUBLY CHOCOLATE COOKIES
Categories: Candies, Cake/cookie
Yield: 10 servings
2/3 c  COCOA POWDER
3/4 ts BAKING SODA
1/4 ts SALT
2 c  SEMI-SWEET CHOCOLATE CHIPS
1/2 c  CHOPPED NUTS (OPTIONAL)
1 c  BUTTER, SOFTENED
1 1/2 c  SUGAR
2 ea EGGS
2 ts VANILLA
2 c  ALL-PURPOSE FLOUR
HEAT OVEN TO 350F.  IN LARGE MIXER BOWL, BEAT BUTTER, SUGAR, EGGS AND
VANILLA UNTIL LIGHT AND FLUFFY.  STIR TOGETHER FLOUR, COCOA, BAKING
SODA AND SALT; ADD TO BUTTER MIXTURE.  STIR IN CHOCOLATE CHIPS AND
NUTS, IF DESIRED.  DROP BY ROUNDED TEASPOONFULS ONTO UNGREASED COOKIE
SHEETS.  BAKE 8 TO 10 MINUTES OR JUST UNTIL SET.  COOL SLIGHTLY;
REMOVE FROM COOKIE SHEET TO WIRE RACK.  COOL COMPLETELY.  MAKES ABOUT
4 1/2 DOZEN COOKIES.
