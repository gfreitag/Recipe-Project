Title: Rocks
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 60 servings
2 1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Soda
4 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
3    Eggs, well beaten
1 c  Walnuts, cut in pieces
1 c  Raisins
Cream butter with sugar, beat in eggs and add flour sifted with salt,
soda and cinnamon.  Add vanilla, nuts and raisins.  Drop from spoon
on a buttered cookie sheet.  Bake in a 350 degree oven until light
brown. Randy Rigg
