Title: Fresh Sweet Pea Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1    Unpeeled carrot, chopped
2 tb Olive oil
3 c  Vegetable stock
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 lb Fresh peas in the pod
2 c  Onion(s), chopped
1 md Leek
-cleaned and chopped
-abt 1 1/4 cups

-celery and carrots
Saut축ed leeks,
1. Wash the pea pods, then shell the peas. Reserve the pods.

2. Saute the onions, leek, and carrot in the olive oil until they
wilt, about 5 minutes. Add the pea pods and the stock. Bring to a
boil, reduce the heat to low, cover,and simmer for 40 minutes.

3. Strain the stock, discard the solids, and return the liquid to the
saucepan. Add the peas and simmer until they're tender, about 5
minutes. Remove the peas and pur축e them in a little of the stock.
Return the pur축e to the stock and simmer for 1 minute. Add the salt
and pepper. Serve hot with a garnish of saut축ed leeks, celery and
carrots.
