Title: Apricot Banana Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  100% bran cereal -- not
Flakes
3/4 c  Dried apricots -- 6 ounces
Chopped
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1/3 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
2/3 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Ripe bananas -- mashed 2/3
Medium
1/4 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs; mix well. Combine
bananas and buttermilk. Combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda
and salt; add to creamed mixture alternately with banana mixture.
Stir in bran, apricots and nuts. Pour into a greased 9-in, x 5-in. x
3-in. loaf pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 55-60 minutes or until bread
tests done. Cool 10 minutes before removing from pan to a wire rack.
Yield: 1 loaf
