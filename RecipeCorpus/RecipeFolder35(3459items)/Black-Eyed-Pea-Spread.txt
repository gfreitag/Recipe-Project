Title: Black-Eyed Pea Spread
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 cup
1 x  Large garlic clove, chopped
1/2 ts Dried tarragon
1/4 ts Pepper
1 x  Salt, to taste
1 x  15 oz can black-eyed peas or
1 1/2 c  Cooked black eyed peas
1/4 c  Fresh parsley, tight pack
2 tb Lemon Juice
2 tb Olive oil
Reserve a few peas for garnish and place the remaining peas in food
processor along with parsley, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic,
tarragon and pepper. Process until smooth. Taste and adjust
seasonings, adding salt if desired. Transfer to a serving bowl and
garnish with reserved peas. Serve with toasted slices of french bread
or crackers. Submitted By MARK MIKLAS   On   10-28-95
