Title: Winter Vegetable Puree
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
Washed Well -- cut across
In 1" pcs
1 tb Kosher Salt -- plus more to
Taste
Pepper, Freshly Ground -- to
Taste
1 lg Celery Root, Trimmed, Peeled
Cut in 1/2" cubes
2 md Baking Potatoes, Peeled --
Cut in 1" cubes
2 md Turnips, Peeled -- cut in
1/2    " cubes
4 md Leeks, White Parts Only,
Combine the celery root, potaotes, turnips, and leeks in a large
saucepan and cover with cold water. Place over medium-high heat and
bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until all of the vegetables
are tender, about 30 mins. Drain well and pass the vegetables through
a ricer. Add salt and pepper to taste. Divide among 4 plates and
serve immediately. Serves 4 as a side dish.
