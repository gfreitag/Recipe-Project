Title: Low-Calorie Whipped Topping
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 cups
2 tb Lemon juice
3 ts Liquid sucaryl
1/2 c  Nonfat dry milk
1/2 c  Ice water
Chill bowl and beater.Mix dry milk and ice water in bowl; beat with
electric mixer until soft peaks form.Add lemon juice and sucaryl; beat
until stiff peaks form.

Per 1/4 cup serving: Calories 16 protein 1.5g no fat carbhydrate 2.5g
(with sugar  32 calories)

1 serving = 1/4 cup skim milk
