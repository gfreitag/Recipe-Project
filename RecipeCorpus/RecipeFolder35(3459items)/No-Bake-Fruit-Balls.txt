Title: No Bake Fruit Balls
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 doz. servi  3 cups miniature marshmallows, or 30 quartered 2 cups graham wafer
crumbs 1/2 cup green cherries, halved 1/2 cup red cherries, halved
1/2 cup chopped walnuts 1 - 15 oz. can sweetened condensed milk

Mix together.  Chill mixture overnight or for several hours. Shape
chilled mixture into balls,  about 1" in diameter, then roll in fine
coconut. Makes about 4 doz.  Freezes well.
