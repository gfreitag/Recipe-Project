Title: Green Beans with Double Sesame Dressing
Categories: Salads, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Green beans, trimmed
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1 tb Ground roasted sesame seeds
-(see page 40)
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Sherry vinegar
2 tb Soy sauce
1 1/2 tb Peanut oil
1/2 tb Oriental sesame oil
1. To prepare dressing, combine the vinegar, soy sauce, peanut oil,
sesame oil, sesame seeds, and pepper. Whisk or shake in a tightly
covered jar until blended.

Can be prepared several hours in advance.

2. Steam the beans for 3-4 minutes, then plunge them in ice water.
DRain well.

3. Put the beans in a serving bowl, then toss with as mush of the
dressing as you need to coat the beans. SErve.

Can be prepared up to 3 hours ahead.
