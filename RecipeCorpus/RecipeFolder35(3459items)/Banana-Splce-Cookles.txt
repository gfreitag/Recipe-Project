Title: Banana Splce Cookles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Packed brown sugar
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Mashed ripe bananas -- 2 to
3    Medium
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
In a mixing bowl, cream shortening and brown sugar. Add eggs and
bananas; mix well. Combine dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture
and mix well. Stir in nuts and raisins. Chill (dough will be very
soft). Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto greased baking sheets. Bake
at 350 deg. for 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned. Yield: 3 dozen
