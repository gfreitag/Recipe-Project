Title: Christmas Spice Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 72 servings
3/4 c  Wheat flour, whole-grain
2 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Ground allspice
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
3/4 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Corn syrup, dark
1/2 c  Dark molasses
4 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
In mixing bowl, cream sugar and butter. Blend in orange juice, corn
syrup and molasses. Combine flours, ginger , baking soda, salt,
cloves, nutmeg and allspice. Add to creamed mixture; mix well. Chill
4 hours. Roll a portion of the dough on a lightly floured surface to
1/4" thick. Cut into desired shapes. Place 2" apart on greased baking
sheets. Repeat with remainig dough. Bake at 350 deg for 12-15
minutes. Cookies will be chewy baked 12 minutes or crunchy baked 15
minutes. Makes 6-7 dozen.
