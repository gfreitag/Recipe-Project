Title: Carolina Slaw
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Cider vinegar
1 ts Celery salt
1 ts Celery seed
2/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 x  Head of cabbage(3lb)
1 x  Green pepper, medium,sliced
1 x  Bermuda onion, sliced
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Dry mustard
Quarter, core and slice cabbage finely. Combine with green pepper
and onion.
Bring remaining ingredients to a boil, pour over cabbage mix and
toss well. Cool, cover and refrigerate. Improves with age.
