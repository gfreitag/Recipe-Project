Title: Fireworks Chili
Categories: Chili, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts MSG
2 ts Oregano
1 tb Beef bullion
3 tb Cumin
2 tb Mole' powder
2 tb Red chili powder
1 tb Hot Mexican chili powder
1 cn Chicken broth
2 tb Corn syrup
2 cn Tomato puree
5 lb Beef (cubed)
1 lb Sausage
6    Cloves garlic
1    Beer
2 cn Beef broth
1/4 c  Olive oil
3    Onions (chopped)
4    Jalapenos (chopped)
1 cn Green chilies
1/2 ts White pepper
In large stock pot, heat olive oil; add onions, jalapenos, and green
chilies.  Cook until tender.  In separate skillet, brown meats in beef
broth and beer using only one can of broth.
Add to stock pot.  Combine all remaining ingredients.  Simmer two
hours. Enjoy!

Second place, 1994 Michigan Championship Chili Cook-off.
Recipe submitted by Ken and Annette Horn of Canton.
