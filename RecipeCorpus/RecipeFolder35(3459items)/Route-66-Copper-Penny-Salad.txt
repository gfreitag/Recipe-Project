Title: Route 66 Copper Penny Salad
Categories: Route 66, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Oil
1 ts Prepared Mustard
1/2 c  Vinegar
2 lb Fresh Carrots -- sliced
1/2 c  Green Onion -- diced
1    Green Pepper -- thinly
-sliced
1 cn Tomato Soup (10 1/2 Oz)
Combine carrots, onion and green peppers. Mix together the tomato
soup, sugar, mustard, oil, vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce. Pour
over carrot mixture. Toss lightly. Refrigerate overnight or longer.
Drain before serving. Makes 8 servings.
