Title: Corn Bread (Vegan)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Plus 2T baby food applesauce
1/2 c  Lowfat soy milk
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
3/4 c  White flour
3/4 c  Polenta; or regular cornmeal
4 tb Sugar
5 ts Baking powder
Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
Mix wet ingredients in another bowl.
Add wet to dry and stir.

Bake at 375 in a glass 9 inch quichy pan or 8x11 pyrex, for about 30
mins until top starts to look golden brown.

Serve under spicy beans, or warm with maply syrup and honey.
