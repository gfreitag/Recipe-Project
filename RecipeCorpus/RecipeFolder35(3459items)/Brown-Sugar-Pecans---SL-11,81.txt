Title: Brown Sugar Pecans - SL 11/81
Categories: Desserts, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 dozen
1 pn Salt
2 c  Pecan halves
2 ea Egg whites
3/4 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
1 tb Vanilla extract
Beat egg whites (at room temperature) until stiff peaks form.
Gradually add sugar, beating until well blended.  Fold in remaining
ingredients.
Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheets.  Bake at
250F for 30 minutes.  Remove from sheet to wire racks to cool. Yield:
6 to 7 dozen.

From Reba Walley of Mississippi, in November, 1981 "Southern Living".
