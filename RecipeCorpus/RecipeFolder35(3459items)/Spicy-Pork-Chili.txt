Title: Spicy Pork Chili
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
Rinsed and drained
1 cn Tomato paste -- 6 oz
3/4 c  Water
2 ts Brown sugar
1 ts Dried oregano
1 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes -- droed
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ds Hot pepper sauce
1 lb Ground pork
2 lg Chopped onion
4    Cloves garlic -- minced
1 md Sweet red peppers --
Chopped
1 md Green pepper -- chopped
1 c  Chopped celery
2 cn Tomatoes -- 14 1/2 oz each
Diced with liquid
1 cn Kidney beans -- 16 oz
In a dutch oven, brown pork and onions until pork is no longer pink;
drain. Stir in the garlic, peppers and celery; cook for 5  min. Add
remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer
for 45 min. Yield: 6 to 8 servings ( 2 1/2 quarts).
