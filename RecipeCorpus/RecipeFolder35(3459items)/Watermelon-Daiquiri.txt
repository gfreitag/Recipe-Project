Title: Watermelon Daiquiri
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 5 servings
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
1/4 c  Orange flavored liqueur
Ice cubes
4 c  Watermelon;
-peeled, seeded, cubed
1/2 c  Rum
Freeze watermelon in a shallow pan for at least 6 hrs.  Combine frozen
watermelon i,rum, lime juice, & orange flavored liqueur in container
of am electric blender; process until smooth stopping once to scrape
down sides. Add enough ice to bring mix to 5 level cups; process
until smooth repeat procedure if necessary until mix measures 5 cups
