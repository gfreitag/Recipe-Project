Title: Philly Cream Cheese Chive Spread
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Garlic salt
8 oz Cream cheese -- cubed
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Chives -- chopped
Stir cream cheese and milk and saucepan over low heat until smooth.
Stir in chives, juice and garlic salt.  Serve over hot baked potatoes
or vegetables.
