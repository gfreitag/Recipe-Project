Title: Crockpot Steak Soup
Categories: Main dish, Soup/stew, Crockpot, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
2    Beef boullion cubes
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 pk Frozen mixed vegetables
1/2 c  Onions; diced
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Flour
1 lb Ground beef steak; browned
-and drained
4 cn Beef consomme (10 oz ea)
1/2 c  Carrots; diced
1/2 c  Celery; diced
1 cn Whole tomatoes; chopped
1 1/2 ts Kitchen bouquet
Place all ingredients except butter and flour into crockpot and cook
at low for 6 to 8 hours.  One hour before serving raise temp. to
high. Prepare a roux with the butter and flour. Add to pot and let
cook for another hour. Submitted By CAROL KATZ   On   10-01-95
