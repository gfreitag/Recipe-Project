Title: Mexicali Meat Pie
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Pie/tart, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
-unbaked
1    8 Or 9-Inch Pastry Shell --
MMMMM------------------------FILLING----------------------------------
1 tb Instant Minced Onion
1/2 ts Oregano
4    Green Olives, Stuffed --
-sliced
6 sl Bacon, Crisp, Crumbled -- if
-desired
1    Egg
1 lb Lean Ground Beef
1 cn (7 Oz) Whole Kernel Corn
W/Sweet Peppers -- drained
1/2 c  Soda Cracker Crumbs
1/2 c  Chili Sauce
2 tb Sweet Pepper Flakes
MMMMM------------------------TOPPING----------------------------------
1/2 ts Dry Mustard
1/2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1 c  Cheddar Cheese -- shredded
1    Egg -- slightly beaten
2 tb Milk
1/2 ts Salt
Beat egg slightly in mixing bowl. Add ground beef, corn, cracker
crumbs, chili sauce, sweet pepper flakes, onion and oregano. Mix
well. Press meat mixture firmly into pastry-lined pie pan. Bake at
425 F for 20 to 25 mins. Spread topping on filling. Top with olives
and bacon. Bake an additional 5 mins or until cheese melts. Let stand
10 mins before serving.

Topping: Combine egg and milk; add salt, dry mustard, Worcestershire
sauce, and cheese.

Makes 6 servings.
