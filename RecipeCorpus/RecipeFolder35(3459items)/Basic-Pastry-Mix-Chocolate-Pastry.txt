Title: Basic Pastry Mix Chocolate Pastry
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Chocolate drink mix
2    To 3 tablespoons cold water
(8-inch to 10-inch pie):
1 1/2 c  Basic Pastry Mix
Combine the pastry mix and drink mix. Gently mix in enough water to
moisten dry ingredients. Proceed as for regular pie pastry
