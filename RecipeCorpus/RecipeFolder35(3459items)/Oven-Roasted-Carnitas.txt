Title: Oven Roasted Carnitas
Categories: Tex/mexican, Appetizers, Meats
Yield: 1 batch  Cut fresh pork shoulder into small, even cubes and sprinkle with salt
and pepper. [Optionally marinade in something fruity/spicy before
cooking -JW]. Place the meat on cookie sheets and cook in a very slow
oven [200 F] for up to 2 hrs- until the cubes are crisp.

By Elena Zelayeta.

From the New York Times Cook Book by Craig Claiborne.
