Title: Route 66 Pileup
Categories: Breakfast, Route 66
Yield: 1 servings
*****  NONE  *****
Spread a cup of freshly prepared cubed cattage fries on the plate.
Top with two slices of crisply fried bacon and fresh chopped green
chili. Add melted cheddar cheese and two eggs, cooked to order. Top
with red or green chili sauce and serve hot. Serves one.
