Title: Tarragon Pasta Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Mayonnaise -- or salad
Dressing
4 ts Fresh tarragon -- or 1 tsp.
Dried
2 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
8 oz Macaroni -- bow tie pasta
Cooked and drained
1 cn Tuna in water -- 9 oz
Drained and flaked
1/3 c  Sweet red peppers --
Chopped
1/4 c  Green onions -- sliced
In a large bowl, combine pasta, tuna, red pepper and onions. In a
small bowl or jar with tight-fitting lid, combine remaining
ingredients; mix or shake well. Pour over salad and toss gently.
chill for 1 hour. Yield: 4 servings.
