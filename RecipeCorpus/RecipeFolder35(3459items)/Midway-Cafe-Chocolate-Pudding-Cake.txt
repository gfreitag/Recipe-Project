Title: Midway Cafe Chocolate Pudding Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Route 66
Yield: 10 servings
Batter Of 1 Devil's Food
-Cake Mix
1 c  Pecans -- chopped
Whipped Cream
1 c  Brown Sugar
1/4 c  Cocoa
2 c  Water
12    Marshmallows -- cut in
-quarters
Combine brown sugar, cocoa, and water. Spread in greased 9- x 13-inch
baking pan. Dot with marshmallows. Spoon devil's food cake batter over
sugar and cocoa mixture. Top with chopped pecans and bake for 45 mins
in preheated 350 F oven.

If baking in Pyrex dish, reduce heat to 325 F. Serve in bowls with
whipped cream on top.

Makes 10 to 12 servings.
