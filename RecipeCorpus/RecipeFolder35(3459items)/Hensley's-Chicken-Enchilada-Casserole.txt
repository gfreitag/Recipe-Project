Title: Hensley's Chicken Enchilada Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Route 66, Casseroles
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Mushroom Soup (10 1/2 Oz)
1 cn Chicken Soup (10 1/2 Oz)
1 cn Tomatoes With Green Chilis
-(10 Oz) -- diced
1 1/2 c  Cheese -- grated
8 oz Tortilla Chips -- lightly
-crushed
1 lg Onion -- diced
1/3    Stick Margarine
3 c  Cooked Chicken -- diced
1/2 c  Chicken Broth
Place half the crushed chips in bottom of a greased 9 x 12 baking
dish. Saute onion in margarine. Combine all remaining ingredients and
stir to combine. Pour mixture over crushed chips. Sprinkle remaining
chips on top. Bake uncovered for 30 mins in a preheated 350 F oven.
Makes 8 to 10 servings.
