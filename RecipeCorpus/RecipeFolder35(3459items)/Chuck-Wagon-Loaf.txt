Title: Chuck Wagon Loaf
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Cooking Oil
2 tb Catsup
Milk
Sesame Seeds
2 c  Pillsbury's Best All-Purpose
Flour
1 tb Baking Powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Curry Powder
MMMMM------------------------FILLING----------------------------------
2 tb Instant Minced Onion
1 ts Seasoned Salt
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Poultry Seasoning -- if
-desired
1 lb Lean Ground Beef
1 cn Mushroom Stems & Pieces --
-drained
1 cn Condensed Cheddar Cheese
Soup -- (10 3/4 oz)
3 tb Flour
MMMMM-------------------------SAUCE-----------------------------------
1 tb Catsup
1/3 c  Milk
Combine flour, baking powder, salt and curry powder in mixing bowl.

Combine water, oil and catsup. Add to dry ingredients all at once,
stirring until dough clings together. Knead lightly on floured
surface 8 times. Roll out to a 12x9-in rectangle. place filling in
row down center of rectangle. Bring each edge over and seal. Place
seam-side down on ungreased cookie sheet. With knife make diagonal
slashes 1 1/2-inches apart through crust just to filling. Brush with
milk; sprinkle with sesame seeds. Bake at 375 F for 40 to 45 mins.
Cut in slices; serve with sauce.

Filling: Combine ground beef, mushrooms, 1/2 cup cheese soup, flour,
onion, seasoned salt, salt and poultry seasoning in mixing bowl.

Sauce: Blend milk and catsup with remainder of cheese soup. Heat to
boiling.

Note: For use with Pillsbury's Best Self-Rising Flour, omit baking
powder and salt in crust.

Makes 6 to 8 servings.
