Title: Molasses Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill, Low cal/fat
Yield: 2 cups
-seeded and minced
2/3 c  Molasses
2/3 c  White vinegar
1/3 c  Dijon mustard
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Olive oil
1 sm Onion(s)
-minced (1/2 cup)
1    Garlic clove(s)
-minced (1 tsp)
1    JalapeÂ¤o chili
Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion,
garlic, and chili, and cook, stirring well, for 2-3 minutes, or until
soft but not brown. Stir in the remaining ingredients and simmer for
5 minutes. Let the sauce cool before serving. Goes particularly well
with pork and duck.
