Title: Grilled Mushrooms
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Melted
1/2 ts Dill weed
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/2 lb Whole fresh mushrooms
md Size work best
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Thread mushrooms on skewers. Combine butter, dill and garlic salt;
brush over mushrooms. Grill over hot coals for 10-15 minutes, basting
and turning every 5 minutes. Yield: 4 servings
