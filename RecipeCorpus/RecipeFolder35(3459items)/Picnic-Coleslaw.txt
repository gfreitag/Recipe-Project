Title: Picnic Coleslaw
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
With tops, thinly sliced
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
6 c  Cabbage -- shredded
2 c  Carrots -- shredded
8 ea Bacon strips -- cooked &
Crumbled
12 ea Green onions
In a bowl, combine cabbage, carrots, bacon and onions. In a jar with
tight-fitting lid, mix vinegar, oil, sugar and salt; shake well. Just
before serving, pour dressing over cabbage mixture and toss. Yield:
12-16 servings
