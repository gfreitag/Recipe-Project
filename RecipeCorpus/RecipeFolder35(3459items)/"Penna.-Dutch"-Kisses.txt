Title: "Penna. Dutch" Kisses
Categories: Cake/cookie, Penndutch
Yield: 1 batch
1 c  Nut meats
Pinch of salt
1 c  Powdered sugar
3    Egg whites
Beat egg whites very stiffly;  fold in sugar, salt, and lastly the nut
meats.  Drop teaspoonfuls of this batter on a greased, floured baking
tin and bake at 350 F for 10 minutes or until golden and rather dried.

From "Cooking with the Pennsylvania Dutch", A. Monroe Aurand, Jr.
(ed.), 1946
