Title: Strawberry Yogurt Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
12 oz Strawberry yogurt
4 oz Cool Whip*
Red food coloring -- if
Desired
1 pk White cake mix
3/4 c  Water
1/3 c  Oil
3    Egg whites
Heat oven to 350.  Grease and flour 13 x 9 pan.  Beat cake mix,
water, oil, egg whites and 6 ounce yogurt in lage bowl on low speed
30 seconds. Beat at medium speed 2 minutes.  Pour into pan.  Bake 35
to 40 minutes; cool completely.  Fold 6 ounce yogurt and 2 drops red
food coloring into whipped topping.  Spread over top of cake.
