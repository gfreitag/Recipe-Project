Title: No Bake Oatmeal Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Peanut butter
2 1/2 c  Minute oats
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
1    Stick of margarine
2 c  Sugar
4 tb Cocoa
1/2 c  Milk
Boil margarine, sugar, cocoa and milk for 1 1/2 minutes.  Add peanut
butter, oats and chopped nuts.  Mix well.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto
waxed paper.  Let set until cooled. Randy Rigg From: Carl Berger
