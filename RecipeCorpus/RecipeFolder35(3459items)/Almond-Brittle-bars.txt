Title: Almond Brittle bars
Categories: Candies
Yield: 40 bars
1 c  Sugar
2 c  Flour, sifted
6 oz Semi sweet chocolate pieces
1/2 c  Almonds, fine chopped
1 c  Butter
2 ts Instant coffee powder
1 ts Salt
3/4 ts Almond extract
Beat together butter, coffee powder, salt, and almond extract.
Gradually beat in sugar. Beat until light and fluffy.

Stir in flour and chocolate pieces.  Press batter into ungreased 15
1/2 x 10 1/2 x 1 jelly roll pan.  Sprinkle almonds over the top.

Bake in 375 F. oven 23 to 25 minutes or until golden brown.  Set pan
on rack; cut into 2 1/2 x 1 1/2 inch bars while warm.  When cool
remove from pan.

Note:  If you want to break the cookies in irregular pieces, cool
baked dough in pan on rack then break into pieces with fingers.
