Title: Hush Puppies-1
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Milk
1 sm Chopped onion -- finely
Chopped
Oil for deep-fat frying
1 c  Yellow cornmeal
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 ea Egg -- beaten
In a medium bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder and salt; mix
well. Add the egg, milk and onion; stir just until mixed. In a
deep-fat fryer or electric skillet, heat oil to 365 deg. Drop batter
by teaspoonfuls onto oil. Fry 2 to 2 1/2 min. or until golden brown.
Drain on paper towelds. Serve warm. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
