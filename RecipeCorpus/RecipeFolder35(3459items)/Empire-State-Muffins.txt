Title: Empire State Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Fruit, Vegetables, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Baking Powder
2 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Ground Cinnamon
2 ea Eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
2 c  Shredded Unpeeled Apples
1 1/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Chopped Cranberries
1 c  Shredded Carrots
1 c  Chopped Walnuts or Pecans
2 1/2 c  All-Purpose Flour
In a large mixing bowl, combine apples and sugar. Gently fold in
cranberries, carrots, and nuts. Combine dry ingredients; add to mixing
bowl. Mix well to moisten dry ingredients. Combine eggs and oil; stir
into apple mixture. Fill 18 greased muffin tins 2/3 full. Bake at 375
degrees for 20-25 minutes. Cool 5 minutes before removing from tins.
Yield: 18 Muffins SOURCE: Beverly Collins, North Syracuse, NY,
Country Magazine Oct/Nov 92 SHARED BY: Jim Bodle 12/92
