Title: Apricot Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Pineapple juice
2 tb Flour
2 tb Butter
1 lg Package of cream cheese
1 ct Cool Whip
1 lg Box apricot Jello
1 cn Crushed pineapple
2    Bananas, sliced
1 c  Small marshmallows
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg, beaten
Mix Jello as usual, add next 3 items.  Pour in a 8 x 13 inch dish,
let set. Mix sugar, egg, juice, flour, butter and cook, then add
cream cheese. Let cool then add Cool Whip, put on top of Jello when
it is set. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   10-18-95
