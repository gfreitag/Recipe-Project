Title: Cajun Beef Burgers
Categories: Cajun/creol, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 md Onion sliced and separated
-into rings
4 lg Tomato slices, 1/4 inch
-thick
Dairy sour cream
1 lb Ground beef
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Dried basil leaves
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Dried thyme leaves
Divide ground beef into 4 equal portions and form into patties 4
inches in diameter. Combine all spices in small bowl. Press seasoning
mixture evenly into both sides of patties. When cooked, garnish with
onion, tomato slices and sour cream.
