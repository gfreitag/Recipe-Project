Title: Spanish Rice And Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1/4 c  Fresh parsley -- minced
1/2 c  Chicken broth
1 c  Tomatoes -- chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
1 ea Broiler fryer chicken
2 1/2    To 3 pounds, cut up
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Celery salt
1 ts Paprika
1 c  Uncooked rice
3/4 c  Onion -- chopped
Place chicken in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Combine
garlic salt, celery salt and paprika; sprinkle over chicken. Bake,
uncovered, at 425 deg. for 20 minutes. Remove chicken from pan.
Combine rice, onion, green pepper and parsley; spoon into the pan. In
a saucepan, bring broth, tomatoes, salt and chili powder to a boil.
Pour over rice mixture; mix well. Place chicken pieces on top. Cover
and bake for 45 minutes or until chicken and rice are tender. Yield:
4-6 servings
