Title: Fresh Vegetable Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
2 md Tomatoes -- peeled &
Chopped
3/4 lb Green beans -- fresh
1    "pieces
1 1/4 c  Sweet corn -- fresh
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground pepper
1 lg Onion -- sliced
3    Cloves garlic -- minced
1 tb Olive oil
1 lb Yellow squash -- cut into
1/2    " cubes
1 lb Squash -- cut into 1/2"
Cubes
Pattypan
In a large skillet, saute onion and garlic in oil until tender. Add
squash, tomatoes and beans. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 15 min. or
until squash is tender. Add corn, salt and pepper. Cook for 3 min. or
until corn is tender. Yield: 12 servings.
