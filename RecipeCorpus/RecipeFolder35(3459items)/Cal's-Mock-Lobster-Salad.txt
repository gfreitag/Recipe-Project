Title: Cal's Mock Lobster Salad
Categories: Route 66, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Celery Seed
3/4 c  Tomato Juice
1 c  Tomatoes, Canned
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
1/2 lb Crackers -- crumbled
1 1/2    Stalks Celery -- chopped
1 sm Onion -- chopped
3    Hard-Cooked Eggs -- chopped
1 c  Mayonnaise
Combine crackers with celery, onions, and eggs. Blend mayonnaise with
celery seed, tomato juice, and canned tomatoes. Stir into first
mixture and season to taste. Makes 8 servings.
