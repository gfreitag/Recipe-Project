Title: Asparagus Ham Quiche
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Chopped onion
6 ea Eggs
2 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Buttermilk baking mix
2 tb Vegetable flaked -- dried
1/4 ts Pepper
2 pk Frozen asparagus spears --
10 oz Each
Cut and thawed
1 lb Cooked ham -- chopped
2 c  Swiss cheese -- shredded 8
Oz
In two greased 9 inch. pie plates, layer the asparagus, ham, cheese
and onion. In a bowl beat eggs. Add remaining ingredients and mix
well. Divide in half and pour over asparagus mixture in each pie
plate. Bake at 375 deg. for 30 min. or until a knife inserted near
the center comes out clean. Yield: 12 servings.
