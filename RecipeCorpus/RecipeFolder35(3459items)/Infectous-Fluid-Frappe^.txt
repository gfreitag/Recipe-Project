Title: Infectous Fluid Frappe^
Categories: Kids, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
3 oz Box lime jello
Nutmeg
8    Maraschino cherries
1 qt Eggnog
MMMMM---------------------------TOOLS--------------------------------
Blender
8 sm Dessert glasses
Knife
Cutting board
With an adult's help, chop the cherries into small bits.

Pour the eggnog and jello powder into a blender. Blend for one minute
or until jello is completely dissolved and the eggnog is a uniform
gangrenous green color.

Pour the green ooze into eight glasses. Drop cherry chunk blood clots
on top of each and sprinkle with powdered plasma (nutmeg). Serves 8
wittle wound wuvers.

From the Book: Gross Grub by Cheryl Porter Random House ISBN
