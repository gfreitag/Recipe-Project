Title: Creamy Russian Dressing
Categories: Condiments, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 cup
1 tb Onion, dried, minced
1 ts Chili powder
1/2 c  Cottage cheese, low-fat
1/2 c  Yogurt, low fat
3 tb Chili sauce
Place all ingredients into food processor, mix until smooth.  Chill.
Serve over fresh green salad or use as a dip.
