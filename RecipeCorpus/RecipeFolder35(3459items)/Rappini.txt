Title: Rappini
Categories: Holiday, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
Onions
Rappini
Bacon
Another one from Publix, Cocoa Beach, Florida

Preparation: wash throughly, tear greens and discard any tough stalks.

Cooking: steam in a small amount of water in a covered pan until just
tender, only a few minutes. Drain, add cooked bacon and onions, toss
and serve immediately.
