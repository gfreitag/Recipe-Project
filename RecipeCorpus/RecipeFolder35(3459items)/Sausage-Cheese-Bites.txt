Title: Sausage Cheese Bites
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 48 servings
Cheese
1 c  Water
1 lb Mild pork sausage
4 c  Buttermilk biscuit mix
2 c  (8 oz.) shredded cheddar --
In a skillet, cook and crumble sausage.  Drain.  In a large bowl,
combine biscuit mix and cheese.  Add the sausage and stir until well
blended. Stir in water just until mixed. Shape into 1 1/2" balls.
Place on greased baking sheets. Bake at 375 degrees for about 15
minutes or until golden. Baked bites may be frozen; reheat at 375
degrees for 6-8 minutes. Yields: 4 dozen From: "Taste of Home"
