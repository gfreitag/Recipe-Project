Title: Bologna Salad Sandwlches
Categories: Snacks, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
30 ea Slices bread
15 ea Slices process American
Cheese
Lettuce leaves, optional
1 1/2 lb Balogna -- or ham ground
1 1/4 c  Mayonnaise
3/4 c  Sweet pickle relish -- well
Drained
3 tb Onion -- chopped
In a bowl. mix bologna, mayonnaise. relish, onion and Worcestershire
sauce. Use about 1/3 cup of the salad for each sandwich, and top with
a slice of cheese and lettuce if desired. Yield: 15 sandwiches.
Editor's Note; All of the sandwiches don't have to be made at once.
Salad will keep for 4-5 days stored in a covered container in the
refrigerator
