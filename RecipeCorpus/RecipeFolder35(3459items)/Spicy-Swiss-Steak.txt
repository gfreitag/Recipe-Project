Title: Spicy Swiss Steak
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
3/4 c  Chili sauce
3/4 c  Water
1 ea Green Pepper (optional)
6 tb Flour
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 lb Round steak 1" thick
4 md Onions
6 tb Shortening
Combine flour, salt, and pepper; rub into both sides of steak or
pound in with meat mallet or edge of heavy plate.  Cut into 6
portions.

Peel and slice onions.  Preheat skillet, add half of shortening then
onions, browning lightly.  Remove from skillet.

Add remaining shortening.  Brown steak on both sides.  Reduce heat.

Add celery, garlic, chili sauce and water.  Cover; simmer for 1 hour.

Cut green peppers in slices.  Add peppers and onions to meat. Continue
cooking 30 minutes or until meat is tender.
