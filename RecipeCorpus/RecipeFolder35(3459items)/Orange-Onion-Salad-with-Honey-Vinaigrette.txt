Title: Orange-Onion Salad with Honey Vinaigrette
Categories: Salads
Yield: 2 servings
2 md Seedless thin-skinned
-oranges, peeled
-and sliced crosswise
1/2 sm Red onion, sliced
-separated into rings
4 c  Torn curly endive
2 tb White wine vinegar
2 tb Orange juice
4 ts Water
2 ts Vegetable oil
2 ts Honey
1/4 ts Cracked black pepper
Combine first 6 ingredients in a jar. Cover tightly and shake
vigorously; set aside. Divide the orange slices and onion rings
between two endive-lined salad plates. Drizzle 3 tbs dressing over
each salad.
