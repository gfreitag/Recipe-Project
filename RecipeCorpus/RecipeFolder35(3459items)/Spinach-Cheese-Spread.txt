Title: Spinach-Cheese Spread
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 2/3 cups
1/4 ts Garlic powder
10 oz Pkg frozen spinach, thawed
-drained, squeezed dry
1/2 c  1% low-fat cottage cheese
8 oz Carton non-fat cream cheese
2 tb Parsley, minced
1 tb Lemon juice
Place cottage cheese and cream cheese in a food processor and process
until smooth. Add parsley and remaining ingredients; process until
smooth. Serve with crackers.
