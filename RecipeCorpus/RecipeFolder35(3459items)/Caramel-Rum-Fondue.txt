Title: Caramel Rum Fondue
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
1/3 c  Whipping cream
2 ts Rum or 1/4 t rum extract
7 oz Caramels
1/4 c  Miniature marshmallows
Combine caramels and cream in crock pot.  Cover and heat until
melted, 30 to 60 minutes.  Stir in marshmallows and rum.  Cover and
continue cooking 30 minutes.  Serve with apple wedges or pund cake.
