Title: Popcorn Pizzazz (Halloween)
Categories: Holiday, Kids, Snacks
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/2 ts Grated parmesan cheese
15 c  Popped corn (popped without
Fat or salt)
Butter flavored cooking
Spray
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Ground thyme
Combine first 7 ingredients.  Coat popped corn lightly with cooking
spray;

sprinkle with about 1/3 of seasoning mix, & toss.  Repeat procedure
twice. Serve immediately.
Yield 15 cups

They aded a note that if there is a party where numerous foods are
served allow 1/2 c popcorn per child.
