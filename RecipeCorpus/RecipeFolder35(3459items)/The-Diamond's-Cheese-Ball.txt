Title: The Diamond's Cheese Ball
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Route 66, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1 ts Salt
Pecans -- chopped
2 c  Cheddar Cheese -- grated
16 oz Cream Cheese -- softened
1 c  Blue Cheese -- crumbled
1/2 c  Onion -- finely chopped
Combine cheeses with onion, Worcestershire, and salt. Mold into a
ball then roll in chopped pecans. Chill.
