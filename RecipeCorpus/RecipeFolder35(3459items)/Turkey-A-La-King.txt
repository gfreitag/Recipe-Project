Title: Turkey A La King
Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Milk
3 c  Chicken -- or turkey cooked
1 cn Mushroom stems and pieces --
Drained
8 ea Toast slices -- or pastry
Shells
1 md Onion -- chopped
3/4 c  Celery -- sliced
1/4 c  Green pepper -- diced
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Sugar
1 1/2 c  Chicken broth
In a skillet, saute the onion, celery and green pepper in butter until
tender. Stir in flour and sugar until a paste forms. Gradually stir in
broth. Bring to a boil; boil 1 min. Reduce heat. Add milk, turkey and
mushrooms; heat through. Serve either in pastry shells or over toast.
Yield: 8 servings.
