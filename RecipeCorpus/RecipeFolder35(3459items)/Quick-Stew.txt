Title: Quick Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
Salt
Pepper
7 oz Mashed potatoes, instant
20 oz Vegetables, frozen, mixed
2 c  Beef broth
5 tb Flour
3 c  Brown beef cubes
Cook veggies as directed on package.  Drain.

Heat 1 1/2 c. broth, add flour and remaining 1/2 cup broth to make
smooth paste.  Heat, stirring frequently, until smooth and thickened.

Add beef, vegetables, salt and pepper to taste, simmer 20 minutes.

While stew simmers prepare instant potatoes per instructions.  Serve
stew piping hot with potatoes around edge.

VARIATIONS:

Omit potatoes and serve stew over quick cooking rice or top hot stew
with packaged refrigerator biscuits and bake in 425 F. oven for 20
minutes.
