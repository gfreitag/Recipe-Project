Title: Pumpkln Chocolate Chlp Cookles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Quick-cooking oats
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 c  Cooked pumpkin -- or canned
1 1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
3/4 c  Packed brown sugar
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
1 ts Vanilla extract
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars. Beat in egg and vanilla.
Combine flour, oats, baking soda and cinnamon; stir into creamed
mixture alternately with pumpkin. Fold in chocolate chips. Drop by
tablespoonfuls onto greased baking sheets. Bake at 350 deg. for 12-13
minutes or until lightly browned. Yield: 4 dozen
