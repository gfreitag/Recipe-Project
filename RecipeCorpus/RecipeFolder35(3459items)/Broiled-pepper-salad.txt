Title: Broiled pepper salad
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Parsley, chopped
1 ts Tarragon, fresh, chopped
3 tb Tarragon vinegar
1 ea Juice of 1/2 lemon
1 x  Salt & pepper to taste
1 ea Yellow pepper, halved
1 ea Red pepper, halved
1 ea Green pepper, halved
4 tb Olive oil
2 ea Garlic cloves, smashed
1 ea Dry shallot, chopped
Preheat oven at broil. Place peppers cut-side-down in baking dish.
Sprinkle each with few drops of olive oil and season well. Place on
middle rack in preheated oven and cook 10 to 15 minutes. Remove and
set aside. Place garlic, shallot, parsley, tarragon, remaining oil,
vinegar and lemon juice in small bowl. Season and mix well, using
whisk. Pour dressing over peppers, season and marinate until cooled.
From Morning Star electronic magazine - July 1994
