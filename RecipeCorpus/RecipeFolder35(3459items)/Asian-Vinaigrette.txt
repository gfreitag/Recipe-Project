Title: Asian Vinaigrette
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 /2 cup
2 tb Rice wine vinegar
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Sesame oil (opt)
2    Garlic clove(s)
-minced (2 tsp)
1 tb Ginger, minced
1/4 c  Chicken stock
Combine all the ingredients in a jar with a tight-fitting lid. Shake
until well blended. Correct the seasoning, adding soy sauce to taste.

Serve with salmon, tofu, or cucumber salads.
