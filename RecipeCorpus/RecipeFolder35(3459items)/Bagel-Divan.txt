Title: Bagel Divan
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Cooked broccoli
2 ea Green onions chopped
1 cn Cream of Mushroom soup
2 ts Worchestershire sauce
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Grated cheddar cheese
3 ea Plain bagels split
2 tb Butter
1 ea Clove garlic chopped
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1 ea Red pepper diced
2 c  Diced cooked chicken
In medium saucepan heat butter, add garlic and mushroom saute.  Add
red pepper and cook 1 minute.  Add chicken, broccoli and green
onions. Heat. Reduce heat and add cream of mushroom soup,
worchestershire sauce and salt. Stir well and heat through 3 to 5
minutes. Toast bagels and butter lightly. Spoon chicken mixture onto
bagel halves and top with grated cheese. Submitted By ELEANOR MURRAY
