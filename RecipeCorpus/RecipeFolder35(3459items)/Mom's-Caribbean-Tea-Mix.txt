Title: Mom's Caribbean Tea Mix
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 3 cups mix
.  gelatin; (3oz each)
1 c  Sugar
3/4 ts Coconut extract
2 c  Instant tea; unsweetened
.  powdered
2 pk Orange-Pineapple flavored
In a food processor, combine all ingredients.  Process until well
blended. Store in an airtight container.  Give with serving
instructions.

Makes about 3 cups of tea mix.

To Serve:  Stir 2 level tablespoons of tea mix into 6oz of hot water.

Source:  A pamphlet Mom received in the mail.
