Title:  SOUTH-OF-THE-BORDER WON TON
Categories: Appetizers, Chinese, Meats
Yield: 30 servings
1/2 c  Grated Monterey Jack cheese
2 tb Chopped ripe olives
35    Won Ton skins or wrappers
Oil or shortening for frying
1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1    Green onion, finely chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Garlic salt
In small skillet, break up beef with a fork.  Cook with onion, salt,
chili powder and garlic salt for several minutes.  Stir in Monterey
Jack cheese and olives.  Cool slightly.  Place about 1 rounded
teaspoon filling in center of each won ton skin.  Moisten edges of
skin.  Fold 2 opposite corners together, forming a triangle.  Seal
edges. Drop into hot oil or shortening in a fryer.  Fry about 1
minute, until crisp and golden.  Drain. Serve hot.  Good plain or
with guacamole sauce, if desired.

Yields:  30-35 appetizers
