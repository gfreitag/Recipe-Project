Title: Nuts 'N' Chunks Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------PREP TIME: 15 MIN--------------------------
MMMMM---------------------BAKE TIME: 10 MIN--------------------------
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 pk (170gram) white chocolate,
-chopped
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
2 oz Semi sweet chocolate
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Milk
1 ea Egg
Melt the semi sweet chocolate, add the peanut butter, sugar and
butter, beating until creamy. Add milk and egg, continuing to beat.
Stir in the flour, soda, white chocolate chunks and the pecans.  Drop
by tsps onto ungreased baking sheets, leaving about an inch between
cookies.  Flatten slightly and bake in a 375F oven for 10-12 minutes.
Do NOT overbake. Cool on baking sheets for about 5 minutes, remove to
cooling racks. Makes about 1-1/2 dozen cookies. Origin: What's
Cooking, 13th issue, booklet by Kraft. shared by: Sharon Stevens,
Oct/95.
