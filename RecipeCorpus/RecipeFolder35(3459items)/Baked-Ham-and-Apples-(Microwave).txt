Title: Baked Ham and Apples (Microwave)
Categories: Main dish, Fruit, Meats, Microwave
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb To 2 tb prepared mustard
1 ts Grated orange rind
1 tb Parsley; chopped, fresh
3 c  Ham; cooked and diced
3 ea Cooking apples; unpeeled and
-cut into wedges
1/2 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
2 tb All-purpose flour
Combine first 7 ingredients; mixing well.  Spoon mixture into a
2-quart casserole, and cover with heavy-duty plastic wrap.  Microwave
at HIGH for 7 to 9 minutes or until apples are tender, stirring
mixture after 4 minutes. Sprinkle parsley over top.
