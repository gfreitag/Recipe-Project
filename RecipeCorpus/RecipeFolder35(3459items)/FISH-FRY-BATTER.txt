Title: FISH FRY BATTER
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 3 servings
With an electric mixer at
-Low
Speed, slowly adding a
-little
Water at a time, until the
-batter
Becomes smooth, and has the
-tex-
2/3 c  Cornstarch
2/3 c  Cornmeal
1/3 c  Flour
2 ts Salt
2 ea Egg whites
4 tb Baking powder   ture of
-rich dairy cream.
Mix together all ingredients
Let set for at least 30 minutes, before using.
Then dredge fish pieces in plain flour, shaking off ALL excess. You
only want a very thin coating on fish to make the batter adhere
better. Immediately dip fish in batter and fry in hot oil.
