Title: Bean, Barley & Sausage Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Crockpot
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Barley, regular
2 x  Garlic cloves; peeled
1 x  Parsely sprigs
1 lb Kidney beans, dry
1 pk Smoked Polish sausage,sliced
1 tb Salt
1 3/4 qt Water
Put rinsed beans in large saucepan. Add water, cover and let stand
overnight. Drain off water the next day. Put beans, barley and
remaining ingredients into a crock pot. Stir. Cover and cook on high
for 4 hours.
