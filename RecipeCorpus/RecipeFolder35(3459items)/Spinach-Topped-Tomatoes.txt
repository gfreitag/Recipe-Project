Title: Spinach-Topped Tomatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 ea Egg -- beaten
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
1/4 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
Shredded Parmesan cheese
Optional
1 pk Spinach -- 10 oz. frozen
Choppe
2 ea Chicken bouillon cubes
Salt
3 lg Tomatoes -- halved
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1/2 c  Chopped onion
In a saucepan, cook spinach according to package directions with
bouillon; drain well. Cool slightly; press out excess liquid. Lightly
salt tomato halves; place with cut side down on a paper towel for 15
minutes to absorb excess moisture. Meanwhile, in a small bowl,
combine spinach with bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, onion, butter,
egg, garlic, pepper and cayenne pepper. Mix well. Place tomato
halves, cut side up, in a shallow baking dish. Divide the spinach
mixture over tomatoes. Sprinkle with shredded Parmesan cheese if
desired. Bake at 350 deg.  for about 15 minutes or until heated
through. Yield: 6 servings
