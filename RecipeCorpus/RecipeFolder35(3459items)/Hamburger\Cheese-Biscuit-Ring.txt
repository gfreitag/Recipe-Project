Title: Hamburger\Cheese Biscuit Ring
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Ketchup
1 ts Chili powder
1/4 c  Pimento stuffed olive, slice
12 ea Cheese biscuits
Pimento olives for garnish
2 lb Ground beef
1/3 c  Chopped onion
1 cn Cream of celery, condensed
1/2 c  Tomato juice
3/4 c  Beef bullion
MMMMM----------------------CHEESE BISCUITS---------------------------
2 c  Buttermilk biscuit mix
2/3 c  Milk
1/4 c  Shortening
1/3 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
Brown ground beef and onion in large skillet, pour off excess fat.
Add all ingredients except biscuits, simmering until slightly
thickened.

To serve:  spoon mixture into center of platter and surround with hot
cheese biscuits, garnish with sliced Pimento stuffed olives.

CHEESE BISCUITS

cut shortening and shredded cheese into 2 cups all p[purpose
buttermilk biscuit mix.  Add milk, stir with fork to make soft dough,
beat 15 strokes. Drop tablespoonfuls of dough onto greased baking
sheet. Bake at 425 F. 12 to 15 minutes.
