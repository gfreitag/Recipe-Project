Title: Dinner Beef Pie
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Brown beef cubes
1 cn Tomato soup, condensed
2 tb Prepared mustard
1/4 c  Ketchup
3/4 ts Salt
Pastry for 2 crust pie
2 tb Shortening
1 1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1 1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1 1/2 c  Green pepper, chopped
Heat shortening in skillet, add celery, onion, and green pepper. Saute
until soft, stir in beef, soup, and seasoning.

Place meat mixture in pastry lined 9 inch pie pan and adjust top
crust. Flute crust and cut vents.  Bake in 425 F. oven 40 to 50
minutes.
