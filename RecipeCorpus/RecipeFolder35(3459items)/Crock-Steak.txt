Title: Crock Steak
Categories: Meats, Crockpot
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Lipton Onion Soup mix (dry)
1    To 1 1/2 lbs round steak
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
Cut the steak into bite sized pieces.  Put all the ingredients in your
crock pot with about 1/2 a cup of water (less at lower altitudes).
Cook 6-8 hours on low.  Mashed potatoes or rice are mandatory with
this recipe. The mushroom/onion gravy is incredible!
