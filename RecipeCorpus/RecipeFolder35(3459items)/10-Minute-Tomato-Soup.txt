Title: 10-Minute Tomato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Milk
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 c  Canned tomatoes -- crushed
1/2 ts Baking soda
In a large heavy saucepan, heat tomatoes until boiling. Remove from
heat: add remaining ingredients. Return to heat and cook on medium
until butter is melted and the soup is heated through. Yield: 3-4
servings (1 qt.)
