Title: Taco Bake
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk Shell pasta -- 8 oz
Cooked and drained
1 cn Green chiles -- chopped
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese --
Divided
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1 sm Onion -- chopped
3/4 c  Water
1 pk Taco seasoning mix -- 1 1/4
Oz
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 15 oz.
In a skillet, brown ground beef and onion over med. heat; drain. Add
the water, taco seasoning and tomato sauce; mix. Bring to a boil;
reduce heat and simmer for 20 min. Stir in macaroni, chilies and 1
1/2 cups of cheese. Pour into a greased 1 1/2 quart dish. Sprinkle
with the remaining cheese. Bake at 350 deg. for 30 min. or until
heated through. Yield: 6 servings.
