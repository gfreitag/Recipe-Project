Title: Italian Sausage Sandwiches
Categories: Sandwich, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
4    Cloves garlic -- minced
2 ts Dried basil
1 ts Dried oregano
1 ts Salt -- optional
20 ea Hot dog buns
Mozzarella cheese --
Optional
20 ea Italian sausage links
4 lg Green pepper -- thinly
Sliced
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 cn Tomato paste -- 12 oz
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 15 oz.
1 c  Water
1 tb Sugar
In a large Dutch oven, brown sausages a few at a time; discard all
but 2 tablespoons drippings. Saute peppers and onion in drippings
until crisp-tender; drain. Return sausages to pan along with tomato
paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, garlic, basil. oregano and salt;
bring to boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 30 min. Serve on
buns. Top with cheese if desired. Yield: 20 servings.
