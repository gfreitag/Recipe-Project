Title: Three-In-One Onion Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Snacks
Yield: 1 servings
-Brand Neufchatel cheese
2 tb Sliced green onions
1 pk (8 oz) Cheez Whiz Light
-Process Cheese Product
1 pk (8 oz) Light Philadelphia
Beat all ingredients at medium speed with electric mixer until well
blended.  Serve immediately or refrigerate several hours.

Serve with vegetable dippers or tortilla chips.  Makes 2 cups.

TO HEAT: Microwave all ingredients in 1 quart microwave-safe bowl on
HIGH 3 minutes or until thoroughly heated, stirring every minute.

TO MAKE AHEAD: Prepare as directed, cover.  Refrigerate overnight.
