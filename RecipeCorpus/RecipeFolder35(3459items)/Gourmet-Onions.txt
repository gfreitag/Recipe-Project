Title: Gourmet Onions
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Sugar
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
3 c  Sliced onions
3 tb Butteer
1/4 c  Dry sherry
In a 10" covered skillet cook onions in butter about 10 minutes or
until tender but not brown, stirring occasionally. Add sherry, salt,
dash of pepper and sugar. Cook, uncovered, 2 to 3 minutes. Turn into
serving dish. Sprinkle with Parmesan.
