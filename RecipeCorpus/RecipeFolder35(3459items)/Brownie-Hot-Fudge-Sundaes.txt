Title: Brownie Hot Fudge Sundaes
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 20 servings
Cream
8 oz Cool Whip -- thawed
20    Whole maraschino cherries
1 pk Duncan Hines brownie mix
Family size
1/2 ga Mint chocolate chip ice
MMMMM------------------------FUDGE SAUCE:-----------------------------
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
2 tb Butter or margarine
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease bottom of 13 x 9 pan.  Prepare, bake and
cool brownie mix according to directions.  Cool completely.  Cut into
20 servings.  For fudge sauce, combine chocolate chips, butter,
condensed milk and vanilla extract in medium saucepan.  Cook over
medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is hot and slightly
thickened.  For each serving, place brownie on plate.  Top with scoop
of ice cream and dollop of cool whip.  Garnish with cherry.
