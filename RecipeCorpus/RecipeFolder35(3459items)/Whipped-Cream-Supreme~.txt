Title: Whipped Cream Supreme~
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Honey
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  Evaporated skim milk
1/3 c  Nonfat powdered milk
Pour milk into a small stainless steel or copper bowl and freeze
until ice crystals just begin to form, about 15 to 20 minutes. Using
a hand-held blender or mixer, beat in the powdered milk. Continue
beating until mixture is foamy. Add honey and vanilla; beat until
stiff peaks form. Use immediately. Makes about 1 3/4 cups.

Per 1/4 cup; 31 calories; 0.1 g fat (2%)
