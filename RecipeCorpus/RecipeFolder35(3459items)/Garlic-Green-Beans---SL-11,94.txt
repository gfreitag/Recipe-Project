Title: Garlic Green Beans - SL 11/94
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Lemon-pepper seasoning
1/4 ts Salt-free herb seasoning
1/3 c  Fresh parsley; chopped
2 lb Fresh green beans
1 c  ;water
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
4 cl Garlic; minced
Was beans, and remove ends.
Bring water to a boil in a Dutch oven.  Add beans; return to a
boil, cover, and simmer 10 minutes or until crisp-tender.  Drain and
keep warm.
Melt butter in Dutch oven over medium heat; add garlic, and cook,
stirring constantly, until tender.  Stir in seasonings.  Add beans;
cook, stirring constantly, 3 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
Spoon into a serving dish; sprinkle with parsley.
Yield: 8 servings.
