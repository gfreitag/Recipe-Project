Title: Sweet-And-Sour Shrimp
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Waterchestnuts -- sliced
Drained
1 lb Shrimp -- peeled cooked
Deveined
Hot cooked rice
1 cn Cherry pie filling -- 21 oz
3 tb Cider vinegar
3 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Ground ginger
1 ea Green pepper -- cut 1/4 in
Strips
In a saucepan, combine pie filling, vinegar, brown sugar and ginger;
mix well. Cook over med. heat until filling is hot  and bubbly. Add
green pepper, water chestnut and shrimp. Cook over med. heat for 4-5
min. or until shrimp are heated through. Do not overcook. Serve over
rice. Yield: 4 servings.
