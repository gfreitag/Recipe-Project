Title: Mixed Vegetables al Forno
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
-cut into 1" squares
3/4 c  Green beans (3oz)
-stem ends removed
-cut into 2" long pieces
6    Plum tomatoes, quartered
1 tb Thyme leaves or
1 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts (to 2 tsp) olive oil
1 md Onion(s), sliced
4    Garlic clove(s), smashed
-coarsely chopped
2 md Yellow summer squash
-sliced 1" thick
1 md Zucchini
-sliced 1" thick
1 md (to large)
-portobello mushroom
1. Preheat the oven to 350øF.

2. Put the onion, garlic, squash, zucchini, mushrooms, beans,
tomatoes, thyme and salt in a large ovenproof pot with a lid. Drizzle
with olive oil, cover, and bake for 1« hours. Serve warm or hot over
orzo or rice.

Can be kept covered in their pot at room temperature for several
hours. Reheat gently.
