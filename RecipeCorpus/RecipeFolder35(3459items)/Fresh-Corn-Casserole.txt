Title: Fresh Corn Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
-seeded and chopped
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Diced Monterey Jack cheese
1/2 c  Cornmeal
1 1/2 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 350F.
-Generously butter a 2
-quart rectangular baking
Dish. In blender, puree
1 c  Corn, fresh or frozen
4 ea Green chillies, drained,
Spread above ingreddients in baking dish.  Bake 50-60 minutes. Serve
with sliced tomatoes.  This is delicious and very light Origin:
