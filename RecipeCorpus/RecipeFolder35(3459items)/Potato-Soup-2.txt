Title: Potato Soup 2
Categories: Potatoes, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
4    Hard-boiled eggs
Salt
Black pepper
6 md Potatoes -- peeled & diced
1 md Onion -- chopped
1/2 lb Canadian bacon -- chopped
2 c  Milk -- scalded
Place potatoes and onions in a large soup kettle. Add water just to
cover and bring to a boil. Simmer until potatoes are just tender. Add
remaining ingredients and season to taste. Heat through and serve at
once.
