Title: Dolores Restaurant Brandy Sauce
Categories: Route 66
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Butter
1 c  Boiling Water
1/4 c  Brandy
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Cornstarch
1/4 ts Salt
Mix sugar, cornstarch and salt. Add butter and boiling water. Mix
well and cook together 6 mins or until clear. Add brandy after you
remove saucepan from heat. Serve warm over cake or pudding. Makes 8
to 10 servings.
