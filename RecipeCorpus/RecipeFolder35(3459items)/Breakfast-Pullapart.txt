Title: Breakfast Pullapart
Categories: Breakfast, Breads, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 9" cake
-dough; thawed
1/4 c  Plus 2 tbsp butter or
-margarine; melted
1 c  Pecans; chopped
2/3 c  Sugar
2 ts To 3 tsp ground cinnamon
1 ea (1-pound) loaf frozen bread
Sprinkle pecans evenly in bottom of a lightly greased 9-inch
pieplate. Set aside.  Combine sugar and cinnamon, and set aside.
Cut bread into quarters, and cut each quarter into 6 pieces.  Dip
each dough piece into butter; coat in sugar mixture.  Arrange dough
pieces over pecans.  Cover, and let rise in a warm place (85F), free
from drafts, for 1 hour.  Bake at 350F for 30 minutes or until done.
Immediately invert on a serving plate.  Yield: one 9" coffee cake.

Evelyn Pollard of Missouri, in November, 1981 "Southern Living".
