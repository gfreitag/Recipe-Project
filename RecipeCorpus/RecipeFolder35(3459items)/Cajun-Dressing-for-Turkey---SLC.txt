Title: Cajun Dressing for Turkey - SLC
Categories: Dress/stuff, Cajun/creol, Poultry, Holiday
Yield: 8 servings
1 ea 1.15 ounce envelope instant
-chicken noodle soup mix
1 c  Uncooked long-grain rice
3 c  ;water
1 lb Hot bulk pork sausage
1 sm Onion; chopped
1 sm Green pepper; chopped
1 1/2 ea Stalks celery; chopped
Brown sausage in a Dutch oven, stirring to crumble; drain.  Add
remaining ingredients, and stir well.  Place mixture in a lightly
greased 12" x 8" x 2" baking dish.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees F
for 1 hour until moisture is absorbed.  Yield: 8 servings.
