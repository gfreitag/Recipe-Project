Title: Mustard Grllled Steaks
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Pepper -- coarsely ground
2 ea Beef top loin steaks -- 1
Inch thick
1 lg Onion
Cut in 4 thick slices
1/3 c  Dijon mustard
1 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
2 tb Honey
1 tb Cider vinegar
1 tb Water
1/4 ts Hot pepper sauce
Combine the first seven ingredients. Brush over both sides of the
steaks and onion slices. Grill over hot coals, turning once, 15-20
minutes or until steaks have reached desired doneness and onion is
tender. Brush occasionaily with sauce during grilling. Serve onion
slices alongside each steak. Yield: 2 servings
