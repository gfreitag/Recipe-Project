Title: Dllly Mashed Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Fresh dill -- minced
2 ts Dill weed
1 tb Dried onion -- minced
3/4 ts Seasoned salt
6 md Potatoes -- peeled and
Cubed
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 ounces
In a saucepan, cover potatoes with water; cook until very tender.
Drain; mash with milk. Stir in remaining ingredients. Yield: 6-8
servings
