Title: Grilled Venison And Vegetables
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
12    Fresh
1/2 md Green or sweet red pepper --
Cut in 1" pcs
2 sm Zucchini -- cut in 1"
Chunks
1 lg Onion -- cut in wedges
12 sm New potatoes -- parboiled
Mushrooms -- optional
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Ketchup
1 ds Pepper
1 ds Garlic powder
1 1/2 lb Boneless venison steak
Cut into l-1/4-inch cubes
2 ea Cherry tomatoes
In a glass bowl or plastic bag, combine vinegar, honey, soy sauce,
ketchup, pepper and garlic powder; set aside 1/4 cup. Add meat to
bowl or bag; stir or shake to coat. Cover (or close bag) and
refrigerate for 4 hours. One hour before grilling, toss vegelables
with 1/4 cup marinade. Drain meat reserving marinade. Thread meat and
vegetabies alternately on skewers. Brush with marinade. Grill over
medium-hot coals, turning and basting often, for 15-20 mnutes or
until meat and vegetables reach desired doneness. Remove from skewers
and serve. Yield: 4-6 servings
