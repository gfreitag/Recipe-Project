Title: Good Morning Breakfast
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Breads, Sausage, Breakfast
Yield: 10 servings
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1 pk Sausage, Breakfast
3 c  Milk
Green Pepper
1/2    Loaf of Bread
1 1/2 c  Corn flakes (Cereal)
1/4 c  Butter
6    Eggs
4 oz Cheese; Marbled
Prepare the night before and refrigerate.

Cover bottom of pan with a layer of bread cut to fit tight. Place
Sausage on top of bread, then lay on slices of the marble cheese.
Cover with another layer of bread, beat eggs and milk, add chopped
pepper and worcestershire sauce and spread on top.

In the morning. Preheat oven to 350F.

Melt butter and pour over top. Cover with corn flakes and bake for one
hour.  Let stand 10 minutes before eating.  Serves 10
