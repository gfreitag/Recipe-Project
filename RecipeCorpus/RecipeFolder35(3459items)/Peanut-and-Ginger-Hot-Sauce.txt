Title: Peanut and Ginger Hot Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
1 tb Honey
1 1/2 tb Rice wine vinegar
2 ts Hot chile oil
1/2 c  Creamy peanut butter
1 tb Canola or peanut oil
1/3 c  Hot water
-plus extra if desired
2    Thin scallions
-finely chopped
2 lg Garlic clove(s)
-finely chopped
1 tb Ginger, finely chopped
1 tb Sesame oil
3 tb Tamari or light soy sauce
Put all ingredient into a blender and blend until smooth. The sauce
will be thick, If you wish to dilute it a bit, add 1-2 tbs more hot
water.
