Title: Sweet tidbits snack mix
Categories: Snacks, Kids
Yield: 4 cups
1/3 c  Flaked coconut, optional
1/3 c  Honey roasted peanuts
1 c  Seedless or golden raisins
2 tb Butter
2 tb Honey
2 1/2 c  Multi bran chex cereal
1/3 c  Banana chips
combine butter and honey in large microwave-safe bowl. microwave on
high 1 to 1 1/2 min. or until melted. mix well. gradually add cereal,
banana chips, coconut, and peanuts stiring until all pieces are
evenly coated. microwave on high 3 to 4 min. stiring every min. stir
in raisins. spread on waxed paper to cool. store in airtight
container.
