Title: Wiener Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Garlic powder
12 c  Milk
4 ea Frankfurters -- 1/2 lb cut
Pieces
5 tb All-purpose flour
5 tb Butter or margarine --
Softened
2 pk Frozen vegetable medley --
16 oz For stew
1/2 c  Celery -- diced
1/2 c  Onion -- diced
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Pepper
In a 3 qt. saucepan, combine the first seven ingredients. Cover with
water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer until the
vegetables are tender, about 10 to 15 min. Add milk and wieners;
return to boil. Meanwhile, blend flour and butter. Add to boiling
stew, stirring constantly. Cook until thickened. Yield: 6 to 8
servings.
