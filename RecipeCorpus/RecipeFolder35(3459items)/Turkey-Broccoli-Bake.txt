Title: Turkey Broccoli Bake
Categories: Poultry, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
-Divided
12 oz Homestyle Or Fat Free Turkey
-Gravy; 1 Jar
1/2 c  Evaporated Milk
1 ds Pepper
16 oz Frozen Broccoli Cuts;
-Thawed And Drained
2 c  Cooked Turkey; Cubed
2 c  Soft Bread Cubes
8 oz Sliced American Cheese;
In a buttered 9-inch baking dish, layer the broccoli, turkey, half the
cheese slices and bread cubes.  Combine the gravy with the milk and
pepper and pour over the bread.  Bake, in a preheated 375 Degree F.
oven, for 30 minutes.  Top with the remaining cheese slices and
continue to bake for another 10 minutes.  Let stand for 10 minutes
before serving.

Each Serving Contains:

301 Calories    15 Grams Fat    60 Milligrams Cholesterol
893 Milligrams Of Sodium
