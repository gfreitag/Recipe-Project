Title: Sauerkraut Hot Dog
Categories: Sandwich, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
1 tb Mustard -- prepared
1/2 ts Caraway seed
12 ea Hot dogs, beef
1 cn Sauerkraut -- 15 oz.
Rinsed and drained
1/4 c  Sweet pickle relish
2 tb Brown sugar
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan; cook on low heat until heated
through. Serve over hot dogs. Yield: 12 servings. Makes 2 cups.
