Title: Grandmother's Orange Salad
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
Oz
1 pt Orange sherbet -- softened
2 ea Banana -- sliced
1 cn Mandarin oranges -- 11 oz
1 cn Crushed pineapple -- 8 oz
Water
1 pk Orange gelatin powder -- 6
Drain oranges and pineapple, reserving juices. Set oranges and
pineapple aside. Add water to juices to measure 2 cups. Place in a
saucepan and bring to a boil; pour over gelatin in a large bowl. Stir
until gelatin is dissloved. Stir in sherbet until smooth. Chill until
partially set (watch carefully.) Fold in oranges, pineapple and
bananas. Pour into an oiled 6 cup mold. Chill until firm. Yield: 8-10
servings.
