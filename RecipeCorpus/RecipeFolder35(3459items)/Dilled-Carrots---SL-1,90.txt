Title: Dilled Carrots - SL 1/90
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Butter or margarine
2 ts Dried whole dillweed
2 ea 10-oz packages frozen whole
-baby carrots
1 ts Sugar
Cook carrots according to package directions, adding sugar to the
water.  Drain well.  Add butter and dillweed, tossing gently until
carrots are evenly coated.  Yield: 4 to 6 servings.

January, 1990 "Southern Living" "A New Year's Eve Dinner for Four".
