Title: Apples Stewed in Calvados
Categories: Fruit, Alcohol, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Apricot preserves
-pref sugar-free
1/4 c  Apple juice
2 tb Calvados (apple brandy)
-omit if unavailable
6    Apples
-(at least 3 varieties)
-peeled, cored
-cut into quarters
1/2 c  Raisins
1 ts Ground cinnamon
Put the apples raisins, cinnamon, preserves, and Calvados into a
heavy pot with a lid. cook over low heat until the apples are soft,
20-30 minutes. Serve warm, at room temperature, or cold.

Refrigerate for up to 3 weeks. Return to room temperature before
serving.
