Title: Potato Sausage Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Potatoes, Sausage, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Sliced peeled raw potatoes
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
-(8oz)
Chopped fresh parsley,
-optional
1 lb Bulk pork sausage
1 cn (10-3/4 oz) condensed cream
-of mushroom soup, undiluted
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Brown sausage; drain and set aside.  Combine soup, milk, onion, salt
and pepper.  In a greased 2 qt casserole, layer half of the potatoes,
half of the soup mixture and half of the sausage.  Repeat, ending
with sausage. Cover and bake at 350F for 1 hour or until potatoes are
tender. Spprinkle with cheese; return to oven, uncovered, for 2-3
minutes or until the cheese melts.  Garnish with parsley if desired.
Makes 6 servings. Origin: Mrs. Fred Osborn, in Reminisce magazine,
March/April 1993 Shared by: Sharon Stevens
