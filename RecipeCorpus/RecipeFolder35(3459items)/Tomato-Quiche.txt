Title: Tomato Quiche
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Dried thyme
2 c  Monterey Jack cheese --
Divided 8oz shredded
1 ea Unbaked pie pastry -- 10
Inches
4 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  Light cream
1 c  Onion -- chopped
2 tb Butter or margarine
4 lg Tomatoes
Peeled seeded  chopped and
Drained
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
In a skillet, saute onion in butter until tender. Add tomatoes, salt,
pepper and thyme. Cook over medium-high heat until liquid is almost
evaporated, about 10-15 minutes. Remove from heat. Sprinkle 1 cup
cheese into bottom of pie shell. Cover with tomato mixture; sprinkle
with remaining cheese. In a mixing bowl, beat eggs until foamy. Stir
in cream; mix well Pour into pie shell. Bake at 425 deg. for 10
minutes. Reduce heat to 325 deg.; bake 40 minutes more or until top
begins to brown and a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
Let stand 10 minutes before cutting. Yield: 6-8 servings
