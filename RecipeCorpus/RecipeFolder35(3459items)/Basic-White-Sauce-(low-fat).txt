Title: Basic White Sauce (low-fat)
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
1 tb Margarine
2 tb All-purpose flour
1/8 ts Whit pepper
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Instant nonfat
-dry milk powder
Combine water and milk powder; stir well. Melt margarine in a heavy
saucepan over medium heat. Stir in the flour and pepper, and cook for
1 minute, stirring constantly with a whisk. Gradually add the milk,
stirring constantly. Cook 10 minutes or until thickened and bubbly.

Variations: ~Sherry-Nutmeg Sauce: Add 1 tbs grated Parmesan cheese, 1
tbs dry sherry, and 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg to Basic White Sauce; stir
well. Serve with vegetables or pasta. ~Cheese Sauce: Add « cup
shredded reduced-fat sharp cheddar cheese, ¬ tsp dry mustard, a dash
of ground red pepper to Basic White Sauce; cook over low heat until
the cheese melts, stirring constantly. Serve with vegetables.
~Mushroom Sauce: Saut « cup of chopped mushrooms in vegetable
cooking spray for about 1 minute. Add the mushrooms, 1 tsp dried
tarragon and 1/8 tsp salt to Basic White Sauce; stir well.
