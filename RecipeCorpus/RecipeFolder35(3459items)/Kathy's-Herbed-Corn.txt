Title: Kathy's Herbed Corn
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
8 ea Ears sweet corn husked
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
2 tb Fresh parsley -- minced
2 tb Fresh chives -- minced
In a small bowl, combine first six ingredients. Spread 1 tablespoon
over each ear of corn. Wrap corn individually in heavy duty foil.
Grill, covered, over medium coals for 10-15 minutes, turning
frequently, or until corn is tender. Yield: 8 servings
