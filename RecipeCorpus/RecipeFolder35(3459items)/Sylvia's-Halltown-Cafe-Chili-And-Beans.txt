Title: Sylvia's Halltown Cafe Chili And Beans
Categories: Main dish, Route 66, Chili
Yield: 4 servings
-ground
2 tb Chili Powder
1 cn (16 Oz) Tomatoes
1 cn (16 Oz) Pinto Beans
3 tb Chopped Onion
2 tb Green Pepper -- chopped
3    Cloves Garlic -- minced
2 tb Vegetable Oil
1 lb Lean Ground Beef -- coarsely
Saute onions, green pepper, and garlic in vegetable oil until tender.
Add ground beef and brown thoroughly. Add chili powder, tomatoes, and
beans. Heat thoroughly, adding water it needed. Makes 4 to 5 servings.
