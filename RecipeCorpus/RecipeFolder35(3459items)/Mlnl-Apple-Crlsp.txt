Title: Mlnl Apple Crlsp
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Quick-cooking oats
1/8 ts Ground cinnamon
Cream, optional
1 md Apple -- peeled & sliced
1 tb All-purpose flour
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Butter or margarine
Place apple slices in a small greased baking dish. In a small bowl,
combine flour and brown sugar; cut in butter until mixture resembles
coarse crumbs. Add oats and cinnamon. Sprinkle over apple slices.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 deg. for 35-40 minutes or until tender. Serve
with cream if desired. Yield: 1 serving
