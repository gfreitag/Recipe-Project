Title: Caribou Empanadas
Categories: Game, Meats, So. america
Yield: 1 batch
2    Jalapeno peppers; chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tb Vinegar
1 sm Can tomato sauce
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1    Recipe pie crust pastry
1 lb Ground caribou
1/4 c  Butter
2 lg Onions; chopped
1/2 c  Chopped olives
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Raisins
In a large skillet, melt butter, and brown meat. Add other
ingredients and simmer about 20 min. Remove from the stove and chill.

Make your favorite pie pastry, cut in circles about the size of a
cup.  Place the meat mixture in center and fold over, pricking with
fork. Place on greased cookie sheet and bake in 350 oven, 20 to 25
minutes.
