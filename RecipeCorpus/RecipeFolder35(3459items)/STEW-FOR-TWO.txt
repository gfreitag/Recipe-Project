Title: STEW FOR TWO
Categories: Low cal/fat, Soup/stew
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Peas, frozen, optional
1/4 ts Celery seed
1/4 ts Sage, dried
1/4 ts Marjoram, dried
1/2 ts Sea salt *2
1    Vegetable bullion cube
2 c  Water
8 ea New potatoes, tiny
3 lg Carrots
3 tb Butter
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 ea Celery stalk, chopped
4 ea Broccoli stalks *1
2 sm Zucchini, sliced
1 c  Lima beans, frozen, optional
*1 Without florets, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch pieces. *2 May
substitute seasoned salt or salt free seasoning.

Place potatoes and carrots, whole, in vegetable steamer, covered, over
boiling water for 15 minutes.  Cut carrots in 1/2 inch slices.  Peel
potatoes and cut into 1 inch cubes.  Set aside.  Melt butter in large
heavy saucepan.  Add potatoes, carrots, onion, celery, broccoli,
salt, bullion, and water.  Bring to boil then simmer covered for 5
minutes. Add Zucchini and peas.  Return to boil, cover, and simmer
for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
