Title: Brandied Peaches SL
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 3 pints
1 ea 4-inch cinnamon stick
1/8 ts Ground mace
1 c  Brandy
2 ea 29-ounce jars spiced peaches
-in heavy syrup
12 ea Whole cloves
Drain peaches, reserving syrup; set aside.  Insert one clove into
each peach; place in a medium saucepan.  Add reserved syrup,
cinnamon, and mace, cook 5 minutes over medium heat.  Remove from
heat.
Carefully remove peaches from syrup mixture; pack into 3 hot
sterilized 1 pint jars, leaving 1/2-inch headspace.  Pour 1/3 cup
brandy in each jar. Bring syrup to a boil; reduce heat, and simmer 15
minutes or until mixture is reduced by half.
Pour hot syrup mixture over peaches, leaving 1/2-inch headspace in
jars. Cover at once with metal lids, and screw bands tight.  Process
in boiling water bath 20 inutes.  Serve over Blancmange or ice cream,
if desired. Yield: 3 pints.
