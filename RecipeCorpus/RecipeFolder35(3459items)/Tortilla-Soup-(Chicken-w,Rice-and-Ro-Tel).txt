Title: Tortilla Soup (Chicken w/Rice and Ro-Tel)
Categories: Soup/stew, Tex/mexican, Chilies, Poultry, Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 quart
-green chiles ( Ro-Tel )
Tortilla chips; broken
Shredded cheddar cheese
2 ea (10-3/4 oz) cans reduced
-sodium chicken with rice
-soup; undiluted
1 ea 10 oz can tomatoes with
Combine soup and tomatoes with green chiles in a saucepan.  Cook
over medium heat until thoroughly heated, stirring occasionally.
Sprinkle each serving with chips and cheese.
