Title: Gingerbread Snackin' Cake+
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 9 servings
1/8 ts Ground cloves
1/4 c  Hot water
1/4 c  Molasses
1/4 c  Margarine, melted
1    Egg, beaten
Baking spray with flour
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/3 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Plus 3 tb. sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground ginger
Combine first 8 ingredients in a large bowl, and stir well; make a
well in center of mixture. Combine water and next 3 ingredients. Stir
just till dry ingredients are moistened.

Spoon batter into an 8" square pan coated with baking spray. Bake at
350 degrees for 30 minutes or until a wooden pick inserted in the
middle comes out clean. cool 10 minutes in pan on wire rack. Yield: 9
servings (about 200 caolories per serving.)
