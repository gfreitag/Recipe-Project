Title: Refrigerator Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Powdered sugar
9 oz Cool Whip
1    Box white cake mix
12 oz Coconut
16 oz Sour cream
Bake cake in 2 layers cut to make 4 layers.  Mix coconut, sour cream
and sugar.  Set aside 1/2 cup of this mixture, spread rest over the 3
layers. Mix reserve with Cool Whip and spread over top and sides.
Refrigerate 3 days before serving.
