Title: Sweet Nothings
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1    Box of Rice Chex cereal
1    Box powdered sugar
1    Stick of butter
1/2 c  Peanut butter
6 oz Butterscotch chips
Melt butter, peanut butter and chips together.  Pour over Rice Chex,
coating well.  Shake coated Rice Chex in a bag with powdered sugar.
Keeps well in a covered glass jar. Randy Rigg
