Title: Cranbery-Jalapeno Sauce
Categories: Condiments, Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 -3/4 cups
-green chiles
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Chopped cilantro or parlsey
12 oz Cranberries (1 bag)
3/4 c  Lt brown sugar; firmly packd
1 tb Ortega diced Jalapenos -OR-
-1 (4-oz.) can Ortega diced
In heavy saucepan, over medium heat, heat cranberries, sugar,
jalapenos or chiles and vinegar to a boil.  Reduce heat; simmer for
20 to 25 minutes or until thick, stirring occasionally.  Stir in
cilantro or parsley. Cool. Cover.  Store in refrigerator for up to 2
weeks.

Nutrition per serving (2 tablespoons):  57 calories; 0g total fat; 0g
saturated fat; 0mg cholesterol; 6mg sodium.
