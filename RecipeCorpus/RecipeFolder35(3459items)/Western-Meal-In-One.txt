Title: Western Meal In One
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Beans -- red 16 oz drained
2 1/2 c  Tomatoes -- with juice
Chopped
3/4 c  Rice -- uncooked
3/4 c  Cheddar cheese -- grated
1/4 c  Black olives -- sliced
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1 tb Vegetable oil
1    Clove garlic
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1 ts Salt
1 ts Chili powder
Brown beef in vegetable oil with garlic. Add the onion and green
pepper; cook until onion is transparent. Drain off fat. In a 2 qt.
casserole, combine the meat mixture, salt, chili powder, beans,
tomatoes and rice. Bake, covered at 350 deg. for 30 min. Uncover and
sprinkle with cheese and olives; bake for 15 min. more. Yield: 6 to 8
servings.
