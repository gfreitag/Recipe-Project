Title: Syrian Mixed Spices
Categories: Herbs/spice, Middle east, Mixes
Yield: 1 1/2 tables
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/8 ts Ground cloves
1/8 ts Ground cardamon
2 ts Ground allspice
1 ts Black pepper, finely ground
1/2 ts Freshly grated nutmeg
Combine all the ingredients well and store in a tightly covered
container until ready to use.
