Title: Mushroom Sauce for Fish
Categories: Game, Fish/seafoo, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Parsley
1 tb Butter
12 ea Oysters
1 x  Salt & pepper to taste
6 ea Mushrooms
1 ts Thyme
3 ea Tomatoes, large (or 1 can)
1 ea Onion, large, minced
Brown onion in butter. Chop tomatoes and seasonings. Slice mushrooms
thinly. Brown all with the onion. Add oyster liquor and oysters.
Combine, add pepper and sauces. Simmer for 20 minutes. Also for: Use
with any baked fish. Source: FISHES AND FISHING IN LOUISIANA, Pub.
1933 Recipe date: 01/17/33
