Title: Butter Pecan Crunch
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 qt Butter pecan ice cream --
Softened slightly
1 ct Frozen whipped topping -- 8
oz Thawed
2 ea Heath candy bars -- 1.4 oz.
ea Crushed
2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
2 pk Instant vanilla pudding mix
3 3/8 oz Each
2 c  Milk
In a bowl, combine crumbs and butter. Pat into the bottom of an
ungreased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. pan. Chill. In a mixing bowl, beat
pudding mixes and milk until well blended, about 1 minute. Fold in
the ice cream and whipped topping. Spoon over crust. Sprinkle with
crushed candy bars. Freeze. Thaw 20 minutes before serving. Yield:
12-16 servings
