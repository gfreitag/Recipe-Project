Title: Speedy Bean Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Kidney beans
(15-1/2 ounces), rinsed and
Drained
1 cn Great northern beans
(15 ounces), rinsed and
Drained
1/2 md Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
3/4 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Vinegar
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 cn Garbanzo beans
(l5 ounces), rinsed and
Drained
In a bowl, combine sugar, vinegar, oil, salt and pepper. Stir in all
remaining ingredients. Let marinate until serving time. Yield: 6
servings
