Title: Marbles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 30 cookies
1/2 c  Lightly Salted Butter, at
Room Temperature
1 lg Egg
1/2 c  Sour Cream
1 ts Vanilla Extract
1 c  Semi-sweet Chocolate Chips
2 c  All Purpose Flour
1/2 ts Baking Powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Light Brown Sugar, firmly
Packed
1/2 c  Granulated Sugar
Heat oven to 300 degrees. Have ungreased cookie sheets ready. In
medium size bowl, thoroughly mix flour, salt and baking powder. Set
aside. In a large bowl, mix sugars with an electric mixer. Add butter
and beat until just blended. With mixer on medium speed, add egg,
sour cream and vanilla and beat until pale and fluffy; about 2
minutes. Scrape down sides of bowl. Add flour mixture, and mix at low
speed just until blended. Do Not overmix. Melt chocolate chips in top
of double boiler, stirring constantly, until smooth. Cool chocolate a
few minutes, then pour over cookie batter. Using a wooden spoon or
rubber spatula gently stir (or fold) chocolate into dough, but Don't
mix in completely. Drop dough by rounded teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart
on ungreased cookie sheets. Bake 23 to 25 minutes. Do Not let brown.
Immediately transfer cookies to a cool flat surface.
