Title: Funk's Grove Route 66 Sugar Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Route 66
Yield: 84 servings
2    Eggs
1 ts Baking Soda
1 ts Cream Of Tartar
1/2 ts Salt
4 c  Flour
1 c  Margarine
1 c  Granulated Sugar
1/2 c  Powdered Sugar
3/4 c  Corn Oil
1/2 c  Maple Syrup
Cream margarine and sugars together, add corn oil, maple syrup and
eggs and cream well. Sift together the soda, cream of tartar, salt
and flour. knead dry ingredients into creamed mixture to form dough.
Chill dough for 1 hour.

Drop by teaspoonsful on cookie sheet and press with glass dipped in
sugar to flatten. Bake in preheated 350 F oven for 12 mins or until
browned.

Makes 7 dozen cookies.
