Title: Red Beet Relish
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 4 pints
1/2 ts White pepper
1/2 c  Liquid sucaryl
2 c  Vinegar
4 c  Chopped raw cabbage
4 c  Chopped cooked beets
1 ts Salt
1 c  Prepared horseradish
Combine all ingredients in large kettle.Cover,bring to the boil.Ladle
into clean hot jars; seal at once.

per 1/4 cup serving: calories 18, 0.5g protein, no fat, 4.5g
carbohydrates.

1 serving = 1/2 "B" vegetable exchange
