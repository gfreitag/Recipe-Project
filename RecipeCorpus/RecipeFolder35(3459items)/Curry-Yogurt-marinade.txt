Title: Curry-Yogurt marinade
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Ground cumin seeds
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Ground allspice
1/4 ts Ground turmeric
1 ts Crushed red pepper flakes
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Plain yogurt
3 tb Lemon juice
1 lg Garlic clove(s), minced
2    Scallions, thinly sliced
1/2 c  Cilantro
-coarsely chopped
1 1/2 ts Ground coriander seeds
Combine all the ingredients in a nonreactive dish or in a zip-top
plastic bag. Turn the chicken breasts during marinating to distribute
the flavors evenly. For best results, marinate for 4-6 hours in the
refrigerator.

Makes enough for 2 whole chicken breasts.
