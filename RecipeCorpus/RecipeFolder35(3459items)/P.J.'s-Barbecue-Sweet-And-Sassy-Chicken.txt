Title: P.J.'s Barbecue Sweet And Sassy Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Route 66, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
1/2 c  Pj's Barbecue Sauce
2 c  Water
ds Salt
1/2 ts Black Pepper
1    Frying Chicken, Skinned --
-cut in pieces
1 1/2 ts Ginger
1 c  Brown Sugar
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
Prepare chicken and place in shallow pan. Combine remaining
ingredients and pour over chicken. Cover container and place in
refrigerator. Allow to marinate for at least 3 hours or preferably
overnight. Turn chicken several times. When ready to cook, remove
chicken from marinade and place in uncovered baking pan in preheated
350 F oven for one hour. If preferred, cook on outdoor grill away
from flame. Makes 4 to 5 servings.
