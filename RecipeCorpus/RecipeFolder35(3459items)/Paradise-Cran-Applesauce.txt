Title: Paradise Cran-Applesauce
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
8 c  Apples, peeled -- sliced
2 c  Sugar
4 c  Cranberries -- fresh or
Frozen
1/4 c  Water
In a covered saucepan, simmer cranberries and water for 20 to 25 min.
or until tender. Press through a sieve or food mill; return to the
saucepan. Add apples; cover and simmer for 35 to 40 min. or until
apples are tender but retain their shape. Add sugar. Simmer for 5
min. stirring occasionally. Yield: 8 to 10 servings.
