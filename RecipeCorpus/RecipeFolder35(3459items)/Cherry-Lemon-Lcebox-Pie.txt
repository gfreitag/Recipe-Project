Title: Cherry-Lemon Lcebox Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1 ea Pastry shell -- 9 in. baked
1 cn Cherry pie filling -- 21
Ounces
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk --
-14 oz
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Almond extract
In a bowl, combine the milk, lemon juice and extracts; stir until
thickened, about 2 minutes. Beat cream until stiff; fold into milk
mixture. Pour into pastry shell. Refrigerate for 10 minutes; top with
pie filling. Chill for at least 2 hours or overnight if desired.
Yield: 6-8 servings
