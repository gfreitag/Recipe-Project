Title: Low-Fat Hollandaise Sauce
Categories: Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 cup
2 1/2 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Margarine
1/8 ts Salt
Paprika (opt)
1 1/2 tb Cornstarch
1/2 ts Dry mustard
2/3 c  1% low-fat milk
2 tb Egg substitute
Combine the cornstarch and mustard in a 2-cup glass measure.
Gradually add milk and egg substitute, stirring with a wire whisk
until blended. Microwave on high 1 minute; stir well. Microwave on
high for an additional 1Â« minutes or until thickened, stirring every
30 seconds. Add the lemon juice, margarine and salt. Sprinkle with
paprika if desired.
