Title: Saturday Skillet Breakfast~
Categories: Low cal/fat, Breakfast, Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Chili powder
5    Eggs, beaten
12 sl Bacon, turkey, cubed 1/2" pi
1 md Potato; cut into small cubes
2    Green onions; thinly sliced
Recipe by: from Louis Rich pamphlet
Cooking Time:  17 min.
Prep Time:  10 min.

Place turkey bacon and potato in nonstick skillet. Cook on medium
heat 12 minutes or until potatoes are fork-tender, stirring
frequently.
Stir in onion and chili powder. Pour egg product evenly over
mixture; cover. Reduce heat to low and cook 5 minutes or until
mixture is set.
Cut into 4 wedges.
Makes 4 servings.

Per serving using egg product:  Cal 265; fat 8 g; protein 14 g;
choleste 40 mg; carbohydrate 9 g; sodium 775 mg.
