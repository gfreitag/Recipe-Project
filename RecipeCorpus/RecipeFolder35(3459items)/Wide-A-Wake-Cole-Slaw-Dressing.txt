Title: Wide-A-Wake Cole Slaw Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Route 66, Salads
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 ts Celery Seed
1/2 ts Sugar
1/3 c  Buttermilk
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Vinegar
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
Combine mayonnaise with vinegar, garlic powder, celeery seed, and
sugar. Stir in buttermilk, adding more or less to make pouring
consistency. Makes 2 cups.
