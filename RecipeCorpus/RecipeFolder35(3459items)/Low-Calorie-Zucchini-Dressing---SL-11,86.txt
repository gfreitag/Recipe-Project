Title: Low Calorie Zucchini Dressing - SL 11/86
Categories: Dress/stuff, Side dish, Low cal/fat, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
-stuffing
1 ts Poultry seasoning
1/2 ts Butter flavoring
1/4 ts Rubbed sage
1/8 ts Pepper
Vegetable cooking spray
4 3/4 c  Zucchini; chopped
1/2 c  Plus 2 tbsp onion; chopped
1/2 c  Plus 2 tbsp celery; chopped
1 c  ;boiling water
1 c  Unsalted chicken broth
2 ea Eggs; slightly beaten
1 ea (8-oz) package herb-seasoned
Cook zucchini, onion, and celery in 1 cup boiling water 15 minutes;
drain well.  Mash.  Add chicken broth, eggs, stuffing mix, poultry
seasoning, flavoring, sage, and pepper; stir well.  Spoon mixture
into a shallow 2-quart baking dish coated with cooking spray.  Bake,
uncovered, at 350F for 25 minutes or until heated.  Yield: 8
servings. (about 149 calories per 1 cup serving).
