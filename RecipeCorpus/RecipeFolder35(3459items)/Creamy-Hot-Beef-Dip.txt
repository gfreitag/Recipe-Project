Title: Creamy Hot Beef Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 tb Onion -- minced
1 ts Dried green pepper flakes --
Optional
1/2 ts Garlic powder
Pepper to taste
Raw Vegetables or crackers
1 pk Cream cheese -- 8 ounces
Softened
1 c  Sour Cream -- 8 ounces
3 oz Dried beef
Rinsed and finely chopped
2 tb Green pepper -- chopped
Combine first eight ingredients in a 1-qt. baking dish. Bake at 375
deg., uncovered, for 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly. Serve with
raw vegetables or crackers. Yield: about 2 cups
