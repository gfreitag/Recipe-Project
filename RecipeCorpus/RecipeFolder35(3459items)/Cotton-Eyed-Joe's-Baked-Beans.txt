Title: Cotton Eyed Joe's Baked Beans
Categories: Route 66, Vegetables
Yield: 10 servings
1/4 c  Barbecue Sauce*
1 ts Powdered Mustard
3 dr Liquid Smoke
1 ts Onion -- powdered
2 cn (16 Oz) Pork And Beans
1/8 ts Salt
3 1/2 tb Brown Sugar
2 tb Worcestershire Sauce
* Cotton Eyed Joe's private brand is suggested.

Combine all ingredients in a large container and bake at 300 F for one
hour. Makes 10 servings.
