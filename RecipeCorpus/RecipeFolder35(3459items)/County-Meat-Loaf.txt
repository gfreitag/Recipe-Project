Title: County Meat Loaf
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
1    Box stuffing -- corn bread
Mix
1 1/2 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1/2 lb Ground veal -- ground
1/4 lb Ground pork
1 ea Egg
1 cn Cream of celery soup -- 10
3/4    Oz
Undiluted
1/2 ts Pepper
In a large bowl, beat egg. Add soup, pepper and stuffing mix. Combine
beef, veal and pork; add to egg mixture and mix well. Press into a
9x5x3 inch loaf pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 1 1/2 hrs. or until no pink
remains. Drain. Yield: 8 to 10 servings.
