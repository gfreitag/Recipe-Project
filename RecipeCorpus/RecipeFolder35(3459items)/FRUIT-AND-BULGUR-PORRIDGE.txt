Title: FRUIT AND BULGUR PORRIDGE
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts SALT
1/3 c  RAISINS/DRIED FRUIT BITS
1 1/2 c  WATER
1/2 c  BULGUR
2 tb ROLLED OATS
1 tb TOASTED WHEAT GERM
1 tb MOLASSES
IN A MEDIUM SAUCEPAN STIR TOGETHER ALL INGREDIENTS.  COVER AND CHILL
OVERNIGHT OR UP TO 3 DAYS.  TO SERVE, COOK THE CHILLED MIXTURE OVER
MEDIUM HEAT ABOUT 8 TO 10 MINUTES OR UNTIL BUBLLY; STIR FREQUENTLY.
SERVE WITH MILK.  MICROWAVE DIRECTIONS: COOK ON 100% POWER FOR 5 TO 7
MINUTES COVERED WITH PLASTIC WRAP AND VENTED ON SMALL EDGE OF DISH.
FOR SINGLE SERVING: PLACE ABOUT 1/2 CUP OF MIXTURE IN A BOWL AND
COVER WITH VENTED PLASTIC WRAP.  COOK FOR ABOUT 2 TO 3 MINUTES OR
UNTIL HEATED THROUGH AND LIQUID IS ABSORBED, STIRRING ONCE.  FOR EACH
1/2 CUP SERRVING: 114 CALORIES, 2 GRAMS PROTIEN, 26 GRAMS
CARBOHYDRATES, 0 GRAMS FAT, 0 MG CHOLESTERAL,281 MG SODIUM.
