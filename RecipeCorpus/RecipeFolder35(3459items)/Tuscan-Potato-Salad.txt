Title: Tuscan Potato Salad
Categories: Vegetarian, Potatoes, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
6 tb Cider vinegar
Salt
Pepper to taste
1/2 c  Fresh parsley
3 lb Cubed potatoes
2/3 c  Parmesan
1 c  Ricotta
4    Cloves garlic, mashed
1/2    Red onion, thinly sliced
Cook potatoes in boiling salted water until just tender.  Drain. While
potatoes are still hot, stir in remaining ingredients.   Chill before
serving.
