Title: Spicy Bean Curd With Minced Beef
Categories: Chinese, Main dish, Meats, Wok
Yield: 4 servings
3    Green onion -- finely
-sliced
1 c  Chicken or beef stock
1 tb Black soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Cornstarch -- blended with
-water
1 ts Powdered sichuan
Peppercorns
1 lb Soft bean curd
3 tb Oil
4 oz Lean beef -- minced
1 tb Salted black beans
1 tb Chili paste
1 tb Salted soy beans
1 tb Ginger root -- very finely
-chopped
1 ts Garlic -- very finely
-chopped
Cut the bean curd in 3/4" dice.  Heat oil and stir fry beef and black
beans for 3 to 4 minutes.  Add chili paste, salted soy beans, ginger,
garlic and green onions.  Stir fry for another 2 minutes, then add
the stock and bean curd.  Simmer for 5 minutes, season with soy sauce
and salt, then thicken with cornstarch.  Sprinkle with sichuan pepper
and serve.
