Title: Chickpea and Celery Salad with Cilantro
Categories: Salads, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Red onion
-or scallion, chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Rice vinegar
2 tb Olive oil
2 c  Cooked chickpeas
-homemade or canned
1 c  Celery, chopped
1/2 c  Celery leaves, chopped
1/3 c  Cilantro, chopped
Combine the chickpeas, celery, celery leaves, cilantro, onion, salt,
pepper, vinegar, and oil. Toss well.

Can be prepared several hours ahead and left at room temperature. Can
be kept for 1 day in the refrigerator. Return to room temperature
before serving.
