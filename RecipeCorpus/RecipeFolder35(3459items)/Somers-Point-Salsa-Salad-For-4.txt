Title: Somers Point Salsa Salad For 4
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Black olives, sliced
1 c  Salsa
4    Sprigs cilantro
1    Onion
1/2 lb Calamari
4 oz Marinated artichoke hearts
1    Head Bibb lettuce
Slice onion and calamari.  Microwave on HIGH, covered, about 3 minutes
until squid turns opaque.  Mix with marinated artichoke hearts and
chill.  At serving time, divide lettuce among 4 bowls, top with
calamari mix, salsa, black olives and cilantro sprigs.  Serve with
piping hot cornbread.
