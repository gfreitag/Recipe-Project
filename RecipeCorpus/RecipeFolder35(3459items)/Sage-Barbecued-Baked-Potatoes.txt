Title: Sage Barbecued Baked Potatoes
Categories: Potatoes, Bbq/grill, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Olive oil
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
1/2 ts Each, salt and pepper
4 ea Baking potatoes
1 ea Sweet red pepper
16 ea Sage leaves
Scrub baking potatoes and slice almost all the way through in 1/4 inch
intervals. (place knife beside potato so the cutting knife hits the
edge of the one beside the potato to ensure cutting all the way
through) Cut red peppers into quarters, slice crosswise into strips.
Insert one strip into each slit of potato.  Insert sage leaf into
every third slit on potato.  Stir together the olive oil, garlic,
salt and pepper.  Brush over the potatoes and wrap in tin foil,
sealing well.  Place potatoes on greased grill over medium heat,
cover and cook, turning occasionally, for about 1 hour, or until
potatoes are tender.  Makes 4 servings. Origin: Canadian Living,
June/95. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/95.
