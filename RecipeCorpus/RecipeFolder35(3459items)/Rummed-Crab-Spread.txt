Title: Rummed Crab Spread
Categories: Appetizers, Fish/seafoo, Dips/spread
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Dark rum
2 tb Prepared mustard
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper, fresh ground
6 1/2 oz Crab meat, can, drain\flaked
1 tb Capers
2    Eggs, hard cooked, quartered
2 tb Lime juice, fresh
Combine all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth.  Start at
slow speed and increase to high.  Serve as a spread with crackers or
thin toast fingers.

1 1/2 cups
