Title: Microwave Meatball Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Mushroom soup, condensed --
Undiluted 10 3/4 oz
1 cn Beef broth -- undiluted 10
1/2    Oz
1/2 ts Dried savory
1/4 ts Dried thyme
2 tb Cornstarch
2 tb Water
1/4 ts Brown sauce -- optional
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 ea Egg -- beaten
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/2 c  Onion -- finely chopped
2 tb Onion soup mix -- dry
1 cn Potatoes, canned -- 16 oz
Drained
1 1/4 c  Frozen carrot slices
1 1/4 c  Frozen peas
Combine beef, egg, crumbs, onion and soup mix; mix well. Shape into 1
1/2 in. balls. Place in a mixrowave-safe baking dish. Cover and
microwave on high for 5 min. Turn meatballs and microwave 5 min.
more; drain. Combine the mix seven ingredients; spoon over meatballs
and stir once. Combine cornstarch, water  and browning sauce; stir
into the stew. Microwave on high for 2 min. or until thickened and
bubbly Yield: 8 servings.
