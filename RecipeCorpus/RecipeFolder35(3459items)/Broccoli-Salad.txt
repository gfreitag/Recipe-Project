Title: Broccoli Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Green onion -- sliced
With tops
2 pk Frozen broccoli -- 10 oz
Cut
Thawed and drained
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sweet pickle relish
1 ts Sugar
1 ds Pepper
1 ds Salt
1 c  Celery -- chopped
In a bowl, combine mayonnaise, relish, sugar, salt and pepper. Add
celery and green onions; mix well. Add broccoli and toss gently to
coat. Chill until serving. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
