Title: Pecan Fudge Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
Softened
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Pecans -- chopped
1 ea Pie crust (9 inch) unbaked
1 pk Whipped cream, light --
OPtional
1 1/4 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Cocoa
1/3 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
3 ea Egg
3 tb Butter or margarine --
In a large mixing bowl, beat the first eight ingredients until
smooth. Stir in nuts; pour into pie shell. Bake at 350 deg. for 55 to
60 minutes or until set. Cool completely. Garnish with whipping cream
if desired. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
