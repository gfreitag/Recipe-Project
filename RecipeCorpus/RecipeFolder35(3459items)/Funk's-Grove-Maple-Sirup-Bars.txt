Title: Funk's Grove Maple Sirup Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Route 66
Yield: 24 servings
1 tb Butter
1    Egg
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  Chopped Pecans
1/2 c  Butter
1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Flour
3/4 c  Brown Sugar
1/3 c  Maple Syrup
Cream butter and sugar in food processor. Add flour and process until
just blended. Dough does not form ball. Pat into bottom of greased
9-inch square pan. Bake at 350 F for 15 mins or until lightly
browned. Beat brown sugar, syrup, and butter to blend. Beat in egg
and vanilla. Pour over shortbread. Sprinkle with nuts. Bake 25 mins
or until set. Cool and cut into bars.

Makes 2 dozen bars.
