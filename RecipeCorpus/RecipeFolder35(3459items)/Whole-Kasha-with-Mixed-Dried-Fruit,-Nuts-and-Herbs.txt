Title: Whole Kasha with Mixed Dried Fruit, Nuts and Herbs
Categories: Side dish, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
Peel of 1 lemon, minced
4 c  Cooked whole kasha groats
-cooked in chicken stock
-for extra flavor
1 c  Diced mixed dried fruit
1/2 c  Toasted walnuts
-coarsely broken
2 tb Canola oil
1 lg Onion(s), finely chopped
3    To 4 stalks celery
-with leaves
-finely chopped
2 tb Sage, minced
2 tb Thyme leaves
Salt and pepper to taste
Heat the oil in a large skillet and sautÂ‚ the onions, stirring
occasionally, until wilted. Add the celery, sage, thyme, salt and
pepper and cook, stirring, for 5 minutes more. Stir in the lemon peel
and combine with the cooked kasha. Steam the dried fruit in a
vegetable steamer to soften and add along with the walnuts. Serve hot
as a side dish or use as a stuffing.
