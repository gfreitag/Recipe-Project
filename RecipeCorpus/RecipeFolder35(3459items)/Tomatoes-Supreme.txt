Title: Tomatoes Supreme
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
8 ea Thin sweet onion slices
1/2 c  Vinegar
8 ea Green pepper rings
Chopped fresh parsley
8 ea Thick tomato slices
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 ts Sugar
Place tomato slices on paper towel to drain excess moisture; place on
serving plate. Combine salt, pepper and sugar; lightly sprinkle half
over tomatoes. Top each tomato with an onion slice. Sprinkle 1
tablespoon of vinegar over each onion. Sprinkle remaining salt
mixture over onions. Cover and refrigerate at least 30 minutes before
serving. Garnish with green pepper rings and parsley. Yield: 8
servings
