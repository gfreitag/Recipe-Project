Title: Pumpkin Whoopie Pies (These Are The Best!)
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 tb Cinnamon
1/2 tb Ginger
1/2 tb Ground cloves
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Veg. oil
1 1/2 c  Cooked, mashed pumpkin
-(not the canned stuff, too
-runny.)
2    Eggs
3 c  Flour
1. Cream sugar and oil. 2. Add pumpkin and eggs. Add flour, salt,
baking powder, soda, vanilla, and spices. Mix well. 3. Drop by
heaping teaspoons onto greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for
10-12 mins.

Variation: Add 1/2 cup ground Black Walnuts for a great additional
flavor.

Book: "From Amish and Mennonite Kitchens", Phyllis Pellman Good and
Rachel Thomas Pellman.
