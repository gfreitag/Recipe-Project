Title: Cucumber Potato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1    Green onion
1 ts Dried dill weed
Or 1 tablespoon chopped
Fresh dill
Additional salt and pepper
To taste
Sliced
4 md Potatoes -- peeled and
Diced
1 ts Salt
2 c  Water
1 md Cucumber -- peeled/seeded/sl
Ced
1/4 ts White pepper
1 c  Heavy cream -- or milk
In a large saucepan, cook potatoes in salted water until very soft.
Place sieve over a large bowl. Pour potatoes and liquid into sieve
and force potatoes through. Return to saucepan. Stir in cucumber,
pepper, cream, milk and onion. Simmer gently for about 5 minutes or
until cucumber is tender. Add dill, salt and pepper. Serve hot or
cold. Yield: 4 servings. Diabetic Exehanges: One serving (prepared
with skim milk) equals 1 starch, 1/2 skim milk, 1 vegetable; also 131
calories, 631 mg sodium, 2 mg cholesterol, 27 gm carbohydrate, 6 gm
protein, trace fat
