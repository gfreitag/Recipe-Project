Title: Hoppen John
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Hot water
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 sl Bacon, diced
1 md Onion, chopped
16 oz Black eyed peas
1 c  Instant rice
Cook bacon in microwave till crisp, add onion and cook.  Mix all
ingredients and cook in microwave on high for 8 to 10 minutes.  Let
stand 5 to 10 minutes. Randy Rigg
