Title: My Crepes
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM---------------------------
1 ts Salt
3/4 c  Milk
Water to right consistency
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
2 lg Eggs
Throw everything except water in processor.  Pulse a couple of times
to mix.  Add water til mixture is fairly runny. Let rest 15 to 30
minutes. Make crepes as usual. This is great with a filling of
cottage cheese and strawberries.
