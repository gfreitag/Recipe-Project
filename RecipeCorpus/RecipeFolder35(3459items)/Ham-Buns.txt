Title: Ham Buns
Categories: Sandwich, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/4 c  Cooked ham -- chopped 8 oz
1 c  Swiss cheese -- shredded 4
Oz
8 ea Hamburger buns -- or 16 mini
Buns
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1 sm Onion -- grated
1 tb Poppy seeds
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 ts Mustard -- prepared
In a bowl, mix butter, onion, poppy seed, worcestershrire sauce and
mustard until well blended. Add ham and cheese; mix well. Divide
evenly among buns. Place in a shallow baking pan and cover with foil.
Bake at 350 deg. for 15 to 20 min. or until hot. Yield: 6 to 8 main
dish or 16 to 20 appetizer servings.
