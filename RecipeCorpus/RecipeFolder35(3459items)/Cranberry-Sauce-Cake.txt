Title: Cranberry Sauce Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 16 servings
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 ts Orange extract
1 c  Walnuts -- chopped
Icing
1 c  Confectioner's sugar
2 tb Orange juice
3 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar -- 1 oz
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 cn Cranberry sauce --
Whole-berry 16 oz
1/3 c  Orange juice
1 tb Orange peel -- grated
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, mayonnaise, cranberry sauce,
orange juice and  peel, baking soda, salt and extract; mix well. Fold
in walnuts. Cut waxed or parchment paper to fit the bottom of a 10
inch tube pan. Spray the pan and paper with nonstick cooking spray.
Pour batter into paper-lined pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 60 to 70 min.
or until the cake tests done. Cool 10 minutes in pan before removing
to a wire rack. Combine icing ingredients; drizzle over the warm
cake. Yield: 12 to 16 servings.
