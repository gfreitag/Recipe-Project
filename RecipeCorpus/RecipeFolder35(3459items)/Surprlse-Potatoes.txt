Title: Surprlse Potatoes
Categories: Potatoes, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Salt and pepper to taste
6 md White potatoes -- peeled
1 c  Whipping cream
Shred the potatoes and rinse in cold water. Drain tharoughly. Place
in a greased 9-in. x 9-in. baking pan: pour cream over all and
sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cover with foil and bake at 325 deg.
for 1-1/2 hours. Uncover and bake 30 minutes longer or until lightly
browned on top. Yield: 6 servings
