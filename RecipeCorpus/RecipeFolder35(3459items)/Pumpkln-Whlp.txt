Title: Pumpkln Whlp
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Pumpkin pie spice
1 1/2 c  Whipped topping
Gingersnaps, optional
1 pk Instant butterscotch pudding
Mix -- 3.4 ounces
1 1/2 c  Milk -- cold
1 c  Canned pumpkin
In a mixing bowl, beat pudding and milk until well blended, about 1-2
minutes. Blend in pumpkin and pie spice. Fold in whipped topping.
Spoon into dessert dishes. Chill. Garnish with gingersnaps if
desired. Yield: 6 servings
