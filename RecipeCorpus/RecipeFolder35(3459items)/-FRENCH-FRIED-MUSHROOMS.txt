Title:  FRENCH-FRIED MUSHROOMS
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 30 servings
1 c  Corn flake crumbs
Oil or shortening for frying
Salt to taste
Grated Parmesan cheese
Lemon wedges, if desired
1    Egg
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
30 md Mushrooms
1/3 c  Flour
In shallow dish, beat egg with milk and salt.  In another dish, roll
mushrooms in flour.  Dip mushrooms in egg mixture and coat with corn
flake crumbs.  Fry in hot oil or shortening in fryer for 1 to 2
minutes until golden. Drain.  Sprinkle with salt to taste and grated
Parmesan cheese. Serve with lemon wedges, if desired.

Yields:  30 appetizers
