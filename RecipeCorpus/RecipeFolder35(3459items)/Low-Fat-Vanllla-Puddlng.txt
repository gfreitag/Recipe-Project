Title: Low-Fat Vanllla Puddlng
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Pudding mix -- .9 ounce
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Maraschino cherries,
Optional
Mint leaves, optional
1 c  Skim milk
1 c  Fat-free plain yogurt
1 c  Fat-free sugar-free vanilla
Yogurt
1 pk Sugar-free instant vanilla
In a mixing bowl, combine milk, yogurt, pudding mix and vanilla. Beat
on high speed for 2 minutes. Spoon into serving dishes. Chill. If
desired, garnish with cherries and mint. Yield; 6 servings. Diabetic
Exchanges: One serving (without garnish) equals 1/2 skim milk, 1/2
starch; also, 88 calories, 261 mg sodium, 3 mg cholesterol, 15 gm
carbohydrate, 5 gm protein, 1 gm fat
