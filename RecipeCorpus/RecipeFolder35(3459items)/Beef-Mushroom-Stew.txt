Title: Beef Mushroom Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1    Jar whole mushrooms -- 8
Ounces
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
2 ea Bay leaves
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
1 tb Dried parsley flakes
Cooked rice or noodles
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
3 lb Beef round steak -- cut into
Cubes
2 tb Cooking oil
1 c  Burgundy -- or beef broth
3/4 c  Water
Place flour, salt and pepper in a plastic bag: add beef cubes and
shake to coat on all sides. Brown beef in oil in a large saucepan.
Stir in burgundy or broth, water, mushrooms, onion, bay leaves,
garlic and parsley; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer
for 1-1/2 hours or until meat is tender. Thicken if desired. Serve
over rice or noodles. Yield: 8-10 servings
