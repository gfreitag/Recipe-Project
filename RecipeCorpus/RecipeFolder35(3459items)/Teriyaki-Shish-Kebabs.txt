Title: Teriyaki Shish Kebabs
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Chunks
3 sm Zucchini -- cut in l"
Chunks
1/2 lb Whole fresh mushrooms
(medium size work best)
1/2 lb Boiling onions -- peeled
1 lg Green pepper
Or sweet red , cut into
L-inch pieces
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Ground ginger
2 lb Boneless beef sirloin steak
(1-1/2 inches thick), cut
Into l-1/2-inch
1/2 ea Fresh pineapple
Trimmed and cut into 1-inch
Combine first five ingredients; toss with beef. Cover and refrigerate
overnight. Drain beef, reserving marinade. Thread meat, pineapple and
vegetables alternately on long skewers. Grill over hot coals for 15-20
minutes, turning often or until meat reaches desired doneness and
vegetables are tender. Simmer the marinade in a small saucepan over
low heat for 15 minutes. Rernove meat and vegetables from skewers;
serve with marinade. Yield: 6-8 servings
