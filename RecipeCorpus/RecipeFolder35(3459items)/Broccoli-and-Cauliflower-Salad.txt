Title: Broccoli and Cauliflower Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 sm Bunch of broccoli
1 sm Head of cauliflower
1 lg Onion
10    Slices of crisp bacon
Dressing
Slice broccoli and cauliflower very thin.  Chop onion.  Mix dressing
and add to vegetables.  Crumble bacon on top is desired.  Cover and
store in refrigerator up to several days.
