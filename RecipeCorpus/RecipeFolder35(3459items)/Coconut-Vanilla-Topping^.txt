Title: Coconut Vanilla Topping^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Tapioca
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Additional juice
3/4 c  Fruit juice
3/4 c  Coconut
1 ts Vanilla
BLEND smooth all but the additional juice. Gradually add this to the
blender. Pour into a saucepan. Let sit 5 minutes. Lightly boil until
tapioca is clear, stirring, about 10 minutes. Spoon over FRUIT NUT
CAKE, LIGHT & EASY CAKE, WAFFLES, cereal, toast or Source: Of These
Ye May Freely Eat: A Vegetarian Cookbook by JoAnn Rachor . ISBN
