Title: Spinach Chicken Salad
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lg Cucumber -- sliced
3 ea Green onions -- with tops
Sliced
lg Spinach leaves, optional
Orange slices, optional
DRESSING:
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb White wine vinegar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dried minced onion
1 ts Lemon juice
2 tb Fresh parsley -- minced
5 c  Chicken
Cubed cooked (about 3 whole
Breasts)
2 c  Green grape halves
1 c  Snow peas
2 c  Spinach -- packed torn
2 1/2 c  Celery -- sliced
7 oz Corkscrew pasta
Or elbow macaroni, cooked
And drained
1    Jar marinated artichoke
Hearts
(6 ounces) , drained and
Quartered
In a large bowl, combine chicken, grapes, peas, spinach, celety,
pasta, artichoke hearts, cucumber and green onions. Cover and
refrigerate. Combine all dressing ingredients in a jar or small bowl;
mix well and refrigerate. Just before serving, pour dressing over
salad and toss. If desired, serve on a spinach leaf and garnish with
oranges. Yield: 8-10 servings.
