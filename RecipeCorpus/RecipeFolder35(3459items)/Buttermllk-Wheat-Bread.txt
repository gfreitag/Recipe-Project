Title: Buttermllk Wheat Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Bread flour
1/3 c  Whole wheat flour
1 pk Active dry yeast -- l/4
Ounce
1 1/2    Buttermilk
1 1/2 tb Butter or margarine
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
In bread machine pan, place all ingredients in order given. Select
"white bread" setting. Bake according to bread machine directions.
Yield: 1 medium loaf
