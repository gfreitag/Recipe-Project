Title: Howard's Sauerbraten
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 lb Beef rump roast
Or eye of round
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
GlNGERSNAP GRAVY:
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Gingersnap crumbs -- abt 12
Cookies
2 1/2 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1 ea Carrot -- finely chopped
1 ea Celery rib -- finely
Chopped
2 md Onions -- sliced
8    Whole cloves
4 ea Bay leaves
1/2 ts Whole peppercorns
In a medium saucepan, combine the first eight ingredients; bring to a
boil. Place roast in a glass bowl. Pour hot marinade over; cover and
refrigerate for 48 hours, turning meat twice each day. Remove roast,
reserving marinade; pat dry with paper towel. In a Dutch oven, brown
the roast on all sides in butter. Strain marinade; discard vegetables
and seasonings. Add marinade to Dutch oven. Cover and simmer until
meat is tender, about 3 hours. For gravy, remove 1-1/2 cups of the
pan juices to a skillet; add water and sugar. Bring to a boil,
gradually dissolving sugar. Add gingersnap crumbs; simmer until the
gravy thickens. Serve with sliced roast. Yield: 8 servings
