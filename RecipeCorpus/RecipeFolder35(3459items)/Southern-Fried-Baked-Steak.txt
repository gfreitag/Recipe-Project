Title: Southern-Fried Baked Steak
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
Cooking oil
1    Onion
1 c  Beer broth
Chopped
2 lb Round steak -- trimmed
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Milk
Pound steak to tenderize; cut into serving-size pieces. Season with
salt and pepper. Dust with flour. Combine milk and egg: dip meat into
egg mixture and back into flour. In a skillet, brown meat on both
sides in oil. Place onion in an ungreased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in.
baking pan. Place meat over onion. Pour broth over all. Cover tightly
and bake at 350 deg. for 1-1/2 hours. Uncover; bake 20 minutes more.
Yield: 6 servings
