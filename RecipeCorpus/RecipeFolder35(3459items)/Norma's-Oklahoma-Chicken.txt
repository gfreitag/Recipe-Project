Title: Norma's Oklahoma Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Route 66
Yield: 8 servings
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
1    Jar Pimientos, Chopped (3
-1/2 Ozs) -- drained
1 c  Sour Cream
Wild Rice
8    Chicken Breasts Without
Skin
1 cn Cream Of Chicken Soup
1 c  White Cooking Wine
1 tb Basil
Place chicken breasts in a large pan so that meat is in a single
layer. Combine soup, wine, basil, salt, and pepper. Pour mixture over
chicken and cover container with foil. Bake for 1 hour at 300 F.
Remove from oven and spread with pimento and sour cream. Return to
oven to keep warm. Serve chicken on a bed of wild rice. Makes 8
servings.
