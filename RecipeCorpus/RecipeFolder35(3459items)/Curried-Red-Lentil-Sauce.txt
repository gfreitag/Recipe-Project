Title: Curried Red Lentil Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 batch
1 ts Ground coriander seeds
1 ts Turmeric
1/4 ts Ground cardamon
1/4 ts Cayenne
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/8 ts Ground cloves
Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Dried, peeled
-split red lentils
-picked over and washed
3 c  Water
2 ts Olive oil
1 lg Garlic clove(s), minced
1 md Onion(s), minced
1 ts Ground cumin seeds
In a 2-qt saucepan, bring lentils and water to a boil and lower the
heat. Simmer for 10 minutes. Do not drain the cooking liquid. Set
aside.

In a small skillet, heat oil and saut the garlic and onions over
medium heat, stirring often for about 5 minutes. Combine spices in a
small bowl and add to the onion mixture. continue to saut, stirring,
for 1 minute. Add to the lentils and cooking liquid. Transfer to a
blender and pure.

Note: if you dilute the sauce with chicken stock and serve it topped
with minted yogurt, it becomes a silky soup.
