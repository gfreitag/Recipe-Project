Title: All-American Barbecue Sandwiches
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Vinegar
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Salt
1 tb Pepper
18 ea Hamburger buns -- split
4 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 1/2 c  Onion chopped
2 1/4 c  Ketchup
3 tb Prepared mustard
3 tb Worcestershire sauce
In a Dutch oven, cook beef and onion until meat is browned and onion
is tender; drain. Combine ketchup, mustard, Worcestershire, vinegar,
sugar, salt and pepper; stir into beef mixture. Heat through. Serve
on buns. Yield: 18 servings
