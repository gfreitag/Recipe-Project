Title: Smoked Mackerel Pate.
Categories: Appetizers, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
1/2    Lemon, squeezed
100 g  3,5 oz smoked mackerel
50 g  2 oz low fat curd cheese.

Remove the skin and bones from the smoked mackerel. Place in
liquidizer with cheese and lemon.

149 kilocalories 621 kilojoules 15,1 G protein 1,1 G carbohydrate of
which sugars 1,1 G 9,3 G fats of which saturates 3,4 G. 0,5 G sodium.
no dietary fibre.
