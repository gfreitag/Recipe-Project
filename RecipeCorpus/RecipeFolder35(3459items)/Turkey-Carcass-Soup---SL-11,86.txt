Title: Turkey Carcass Soup - SL 11/86
Categories: Soup/stew, Poultry
Yield: 5 quarts
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Dried parsley flakes
1 ts Dried whole basil
1 ea Bay leaf
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Poultry seasoning
1 pn Dried whole thyme
1 ea ;turkey carcass
4 qt ;water
6 sm Potatoes; diced
4 lg Carrots; diced
2 ea Stalks celery; diced
1 lg Onion; chopped
1 1/2 c  Cabbage; shredded
7 1/2 oz Can tomatoes; drained and
-chopped
1/2 c  Barley; uncooked
Place turkey carcass and water in a large Dutch oven; bring to a
boil. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 2 hours.  Remove carcass from
broth, and pick all meat from bones.
Return meat to broth, and add remaining ingredients.  Simmer 1
hour or until vegetables are tender.  Remove bay leaf.   Yield: 5
quarts.

Mrs. Carl M. Day, Jr. of South Carolina, in November, 1986 "Southern
Living".   Typos by Jeff Pruett.
