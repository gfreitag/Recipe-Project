Title: Sour-Cream baked Noodles - FF
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Egg; slightly beaten
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Freshly ground pepper
4 tb Butter; melted
1/4 lb Noodles, 1/4-inch wide or
-wider; cooked
1 c  Cottage cheese
1 c  Sour cream
MMMMM-------------------MIX'N'MATCH VARIATIONS------------------------
1 c  Tuna; well-drained and
-flaked
1 1/2 c  Diced cooked poultry
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper; chopped
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1 c  Mushrooms; sliced
Preheat the oven to 375F.  Butter a 1-1/2 quart casserole dish.
[Cook noodles according to package directions.]  Put the cooked
noodles into a bowl and toss with the remaining ingredients.  Put
into the casserole dish and bake 50 to 60 minutes, or until bubbling
and set.

Note: This recipe has infinite variations.  Use the butter to saute
any one or more of... [in the ingredients above, under Mix'n'Match],
then add to the noodles.
