Title: Oreo Stuff
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1 ct Cool Whip 16 oz.
1 cn Condensed milk
1 pk Oreo cookies, large package
2 sm Boxes chocolate pudding mix
1 pk Cream cheese 8 oz.
Crumble all but 8 or 10 cookies in bottom of 9x13 inch pan.  Mix
pudding with directions on box.  Pour pudding over cookie crumbs. Mix
cream cheese, condensed milk and Cool Whip.  Put on top of pudding.
Garnish with remaining cookies.  Refrigerate. Randy Rigg
