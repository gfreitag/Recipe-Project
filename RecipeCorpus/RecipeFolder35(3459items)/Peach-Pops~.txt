Title: Peach Pops~
Categories: Healthy, Snacks, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Pineapple juice
2 ts Grated lemon peel
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Vanilla
3 md Ripe peaches, peeled,
-pitted and sliced (about
-1 1/2 cups)
1 c  Skim milk
Prep Time:  10 min.   Freezing Time:  9 hr.   Standing Time:  5 min.

In blender or food processor, blend or process all ingredients until
smooth. Pour the mixture into 4 (6 to 8 ounce) paper cups. Cover with
foil and freeze until partially frozen. Insert a wooden stick in the
center of each and freeze at least 8 hours or until completely
frozen. To serve, let the pops stand at room temperature for 5
minutes. Then gently peel the paper cup from the frozen pop.

NOTE:  You can prepare these pops with other fruits such as apricots,
strawberries or bananas.

Makes 4 servings.

Per serving:  63 calories; 0.2 g fat (3% of calories); 1 g fiber; 1 mg
cholesterol; 32 mg sodium.
