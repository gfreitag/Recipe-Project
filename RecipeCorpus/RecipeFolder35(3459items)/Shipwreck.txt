Title: Shipwreck
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1 cn Pork and beans -- 32 oz
1/2 lb Bacon -- sliced
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1 lg Onion -- chopped
In a skillet, cook bacon until, crisp. Remov to paper towels to drain;
crumble and set aside. Drain drippings from skillet. Brown the beef;
drain. Add onion and cook until tender, about 5 min. Combine ketchup
and brown sugar; stir into beef mixture. Stir in pork and beans and
all but 2 tablespoons of the bacon. Transfer to an 8 in. square
baking dish. Top with the remaining bacon. Bake, uncovered, at 350
deg. for 1 hours. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
