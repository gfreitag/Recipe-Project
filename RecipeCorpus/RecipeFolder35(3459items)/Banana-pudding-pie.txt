Title: Banana pudding pie
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart, Fruit, Puddings
Yield: 1 pie
2 lg Bananas, sliced (2 cups)
1 pk Nilla pie crust
Prepared whipped topping
1 pk 3 3/8 oz instant vanilla
Pudding & pie filling
2 c  Cold milk
Prepare pudding as package directs for pie filling using milk.
Arrange 1 cup banana slices over bottom of crust. top with prepared
pudding. chill at least 1 hour. to serve, garnish with whipped
topping and remaining banana slices.

recipe from "Nabisco Foods"
