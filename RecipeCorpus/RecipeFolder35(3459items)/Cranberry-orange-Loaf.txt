Title: Cranberry-orange Loaf
Categories: Desserts, Breads, Fruit
Yield: 1 loaf
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg
3/4 c  Orange juice concentrate
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  Cranberries
1 3/4 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
Coarsely chop cranberries.
Mix dry ingredients, stir in cranberries.  Mix liquids together, then
add to dry, beat for about a half minute (batter will be lumpy).
Greased loaf pan.
350 degrees for 60 - 65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the
middle comes out clean.
