Title: Seminole Pumpkin Fry Bread
Categories: Breads, Vegetables, Amerindian
Yield: 4 servings
3 ea Tb Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
1 c  Pumpkin Puree
2 ts Baking Powder
4 lg Eggs
1 c  Milk
1 c  Unbleached All-Purpose Flour
2 ea Tb Butter; Melted
MMMMM-------------------------GARNISHES------------------------------
Sifted Confectioners' Sugar
Syrup
Butter
In a food processor or blender, combine all of the ingredients except
the garnishes and blend until smooth.  Set aside for 30 minutes to 1
hour. Heat a well greased griddle or skillet, adding about 1/2 cup of
the batter when hot.  Fry as you would a pancake, turning after
bubbles appear throughout. Serve with butter and syrup or sifted
confectioners's sugar. YIELD: About 8 fry breads.
