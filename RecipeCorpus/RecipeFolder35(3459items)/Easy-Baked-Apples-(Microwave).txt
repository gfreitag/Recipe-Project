Title: Easy Baked Apples (Microwave)
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Ground nutmeg
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/3 c  ;Water
4 lg Cooking apples
1/4 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
1 ts Ground cinnamon
Core apples, and slice a thin circle of peel from top of each.
Place apples in a 9" pie-plate.
Combine sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg; spoon mixture into cavities of
apples.  Top each with 1/2 tablespoon butter.  Pour water into
pieplate; cover with heavy-duty plastic wrap.
Microwave at HIGH for 6 to 8 minutes or until apples are
crisp-tender, giving dish a half-turn after 3 minutes.  Let stand 5
minutes before serving.   Yield: 4 servings.
