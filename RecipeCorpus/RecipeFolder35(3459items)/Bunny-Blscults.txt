Title: Bunny Blscults
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Or red candied cherries
20 ea Slivered almonds
1    Tube pink decorator icing
1    Tube refrigerated biscuits,
-8 ounces
10 ea Raisins
5 ea Cinnamon candies
Separate biscuits; place five of them on a greased baking sheet about
2 in. apart. Cut remaining biscuits in half. Shape biscuit halves to
form ears and firmly attach to whole biscuits. On each biscuit, press
on two raisin eyes, one candy or cherry nose and four slivered almond
whiskers. Bake at 375 deg. for 8-10 minutes or until biscuits are
browned. Cool slightly; frost ears with icing Yield: 5 biscuits
