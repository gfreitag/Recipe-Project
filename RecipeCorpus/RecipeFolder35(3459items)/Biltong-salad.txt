Title: Biltong salad
Categories: Salads, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Quartered Hard Boiled Eggs
2 tb Chopped Parsley
1/2 c  Bean Sprouts
1 1/16 c  Croutons
bn Spinach leaves
bn Lettuce leaves
bn Red Butter Lettuce leaves
9 oz Thin sliced moist Biltong
Toss leaves together lightly.  Add remaining ingredients except
croutons. Add dressing of choice. sprinkle croutons on top.
