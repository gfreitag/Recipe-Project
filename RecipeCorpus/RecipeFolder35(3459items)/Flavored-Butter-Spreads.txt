Title: Flavored Butter Spreads
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 /2 cup
Add your desired ingredient
.  from the list below
1/2 c  Butter or margarine;
.  softened
Make warm-from-the-oven bread even more special by serving it with a
flavored butter spread.

**  Directions

To prepare, beat the softened butter in a small bowl at high speed
until light and fluffy.  Add one of the following; beat until well
blended and smooth.

*  1/8 to 1/4 tsp garlic powder

*  1 to 2 tsp minced garlic

*  1/8 to 1/4 tsp dried thyme leaves; finely crushed

*  1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

*  2 Tbsp pesto sauce (purchased or home-made)

*  1/4 cup honey

*  1/4 cup orange marmalade

*  2 Tbsp maple-flavored syrup  (or use the real stuff)

*  2 Tbsp orange juice plus 1 Tbsp orange zest

These butter spreads can be prepared ahead and stored in the
refrigerator. For easier spreading, let them soften at room
temperature before serving.
