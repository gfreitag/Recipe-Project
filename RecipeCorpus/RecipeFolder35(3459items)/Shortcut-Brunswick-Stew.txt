Title: Shortcut Brunswick Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
1 3/8 ts Pepper
1 pk Frozen lima beans -- 10 oz
1 pk Frozen corn kernels -- 10
Oz
2 c  Cooked chicken -- cubed
1 tb Cornstarch
2    Strips bacon
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1 cn Tomato soup, condensed --
Undiluted 10 3/4 oz
1 1/4 c  Water -- plus 1 T. divided
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
In large skillet, cook bacon until crisp; remove to paper towel to
drain. In dripping, cook onion until tender. Stir in soup, 1 1/4 cups
water, worcestershire sauce an pepper; bring to a boil. Add beans and
corn; return to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20 min.
Crumble bacon; add to stew with chicken. Combine cornstarch with the
remaining water; stir into stew. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly
until thickened. Yield: 4 Servings.
