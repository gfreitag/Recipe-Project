Title: Chewy Maple Bars
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Rolled oats
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Maple syrup
1 ea Egg
2/3 c  All-purpose flour
In a mixing bowl, cream sugar and shortening. Add syrup and egg and
beat well. Combine flour and baking powder; add to creamed mixture.
Add vanilla and mix well. Stir in oats and walnuts. Pour into a
greased 9-in. square baking pan. Bake at 350 deg for 35 minutes. Cut
into squares while warm. Cool on a wire rack. Yield: about 2 dozen.
