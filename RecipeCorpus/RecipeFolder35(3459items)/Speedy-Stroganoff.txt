Title: Speedy Stroganoff
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Mushroom stem and pieces --
10 oz Drained
1 cn Cream of chicken soup --
10 3/4    Ounces
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 ounces
Cooked wide egg noodles
Chopped fresh parsley
2 tb Cooking oil
2 c  Roast beef
Leftover cubed cooked
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
1/3 c  Onion -- chopped
2 tb All-purpose flour
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Paprika
In a skillet, heat oil over medium high. Saute beef, garlic and
onion. When onion is tender, reduce heat to low; stir in flour, salt
and paprika. Cook 5 minutes. Stir in mushrooms, soup and water.
Simmer 10 minutes. Fold in sour cream; heat through but do not boil.
Serve immediately over noodles. Garnish with parsley. Yield: 4-6
servings
