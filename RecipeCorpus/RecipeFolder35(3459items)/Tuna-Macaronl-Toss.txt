Title: Tuna Macaronl Toss
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Tuna
(3-1/4 ounces) drained and
Flaked
1/2 c  Frozen peas
2 ts Pimientos -- diced
Cooked noodles
Chopped fresh parsley
1/3 c  Onion -- chopped
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb All-purpose flour
3/4 c  Milk
1 ea Chicken bouillon cube
1/4 ts Salt -- optional
1/8 ts Pepper
In a saucepan, saute onion in butter until tender. Stir in the flour,
forming a smooth paste; cook and stir for 1 minute. Gradually stir in
milk. Add bouillon, salt if desired and pepper: cook until thickened.
Stir in tuna, peas and pimientos; heat through. Serve over noodles
and garnish with parsley. Yield: 1 serving
