Title: Banana Oreo Pudding
Categories: Puddings
Yield: 3 servings
Half of them, 20 oz. size
10    Bananas, sliced
Meringue
Dozen egg whites
1 ts Vanille
1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1    Dozen egg yolks
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
4 c  Milk
1 1/2    Bags of Oreo cookies, crush
Preheat oven to 4540 degrees.  Combine sugar, flour, salt, yolks and
milk; cook in double boiler until thick.  Remove from heat and add
vanilla. In a 2 or 3 quart dish, layer crushed Oreo cookies, bananas
and custard, making several layers.  Line sides of dish with whole
cookies, layer with remaining ingredients. Meringue: Beat whites
until peaks form. Add vanilla and sugar. Spread on pudding; bake
until browned. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   10-24-95
