Title: HUEVOS MOTULENOS
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs, Meats, Vegetables, So. america
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Mashed, fried black beans
1 c  Diced, cooked ham
1/2 c  Cooked peas
4 lg Eggs, fried
4 tb Crumbled Cotija or grated
-Parmesan cheese
1 1/2 md Onion, finely chopped
1 ea 3-inch green chili, minced
2 tb Oil
3 lg Tomatoes, peeled and chopped
Salt to taste
8 ea Corn tortillas
Bacon fat or lard
Make a salsa by sauteing onion and chili in oil until well softened.
Add tomatoes and cook over low heat until well blended. Season with
salt to taste.  Set aside. Fry tortillas in fat until they're crisp.
Spread 4 tortillas with hot fried beans and sprinkle with diced ham
and peas. Top each mixture with a fried egg, then cover with
remaining tortilla. Heat salsa until it is very hot and spoon over
each portion. Top with cheese. Makes 4 servings. From: San Antonio
Express News, "A Yuccatecan Feast", by Bonnie Walker.  7/19/95 Posted
by: Karin Brewer
