Title: Party Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Dried chervil -- or 1 T.
Fresh chives
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
1 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
4 c  Mashed potatoes -- 8 to 10
Large
Or 4 cups prepared instant
Potatoes
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz
1 pk Cream cheese -- 8 oz
Softened
In a large bowl, combine potatoes, sour cream, cream cheese, chives
and garlic powder. Turn into a greased 2 qt. casserole. Combine bread
crumbs with butter; sprinkle over potatoes. Bake at 350 deg. for 50
to 60 min. Top with cheese and serve immediately. Dish can be
assembled a day ahead and baked just before serving. Yield: 10 to 12
servings.
