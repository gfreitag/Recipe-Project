Title: Braised Pork Chops with Cream Gravy
Categories: Meats, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Dried Thyme leaves
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Flour
1/3 c  Milk
4    Loin pork chops, 1/2" thick
1/2 c  Water
2 ts Dried parsley flakes
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Onion powder
In large skillet over med-hi heat, brown pork chops on both sides.
Add water, parsley, salt, onion powder, thyme and worcestershire
sauce. Cover; simmer 30 - 30 min or until pork chops are tender.

Remove pork chops from skillet; keep warm.  In small bowl, combine
flour and milk; blend until smooth.  Gradually stir into hot mixture
in skillet. Cook until mixture boils and thickens, stirring
constantly.  Serve hot gravy with pork chops.
