Title: Applesauce Carrot Cake
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------------CAKE---------------------------------
1/2 c  Butter or margarine;
-softened
2 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  Grated carrots
1 c  Chopped walnuts
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Ground allspice
1 c  Applesauce
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
2 tb (to 3) Applesauce
3 oz Cream cheese; softened
2 c  Sifted confectioners' sugar
Combine flour, sugar, baking soda, salt and spices in large mixer
bowl. Add applesauce and butter; beat 2 minutes at medium speed,
scraping bowl and beaters occasionally.  Add eggs; beat 2 minutes.
Stir in carrots and nuts. Pour into greased and floured 9-inch square
pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 to 55 minutes, until wooden pick
inserted near center comes out clean. Cool; frost with CREAMY APPLE
FROSTING: Beat cream cheese. Beat in sugar and applesauce until
smooth.

From:  Seneca Applesauce label
