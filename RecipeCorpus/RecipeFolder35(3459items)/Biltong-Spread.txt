Title: Biltong Spread
Categories: Dips/spread, Cheese/eggs, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/8 oz Grated Biltong
Salt
Pepper
4 1/2 oz Cream cheese
2 tb Mayo
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Chopped parsley
Combine all ingredients and serve between slices of toasted bread.
