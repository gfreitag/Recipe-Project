Title: Bacon Cheese Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 3 servings
8    12 oz. shredded Velveeta
Cheese
1/4 ts Onion salt
4 tb Hot taco sauce
8 oz Cream cheese
1/3 c  Milk
1/4 ts Dry mustard
8 sl Crisp crumbled bacon
Cook cream cheese slowly.  Add everything except bacon.  Cook until
cheese melts.  Add bacon and keep warm in your crock pot.  Serve with
chips. Randy Rigg
