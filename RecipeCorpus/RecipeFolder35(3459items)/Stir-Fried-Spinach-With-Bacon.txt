Title: Stir-Fried Spinach With Bacon
Categories: Chinese, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
4    Fresh Mushrooms -- thinly
-sliced
2 tb Oil
1 1/2 lb Fresh Spinach Leaves -- wash
& remove stems
6 sl Bacon -- cut in 1" pieces
3    Scallions -- chopped
MMMMM-------------------------SAUCE-----------------------------------
1/4 ts Dry Mustard
2 ts Soy
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Sherry
1 ts Sugar
1. Heat the wok. Add the bacon and cook until almost crisp. Pour off
the bacon grease.

2. Add 2 Tbs oil, the scallions, and fresh spinach. Stir-fry quickly
until the volume of the spinach is reduced to one half.

3. Add the remaining ingredient with the sauce and cook 1 min more.

Serve Immediately.
