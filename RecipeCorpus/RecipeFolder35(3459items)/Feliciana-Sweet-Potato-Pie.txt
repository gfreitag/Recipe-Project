Title: Feliciana Sweet Potato Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Vanilla
Pie shell:
3 oz Cream cheese
1    Stick butter
1 c  Flour
3    Sweet potatoes
1/2    Lemon
1    Stick butter
3 tb Flour
1 c  Sugar
Filling:

Mix cream cheese, butter and flour by hand and roll out on floured
board. Place
in pie pan.

Peel and cut up sweet potatoes. Place in pot with 1/4 of lemon with
1" of boiling water; cook until tender. Mash sweet potatoes, measure
2 cups and mix
with other ingredients. Add juice of 1/4 lemon, mix and pour into pie
shell.
Bake in a 350 F oven for 1 hour.

From: NANCY BIRD
