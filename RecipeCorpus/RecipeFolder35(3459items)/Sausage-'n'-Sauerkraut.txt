Title: Sausage 'n' Sauerkraut
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 lg Green pepper -- chopped
3 tb Brown sugar
1    Bay leaf
1 ts Dried oregano
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Mashed potatoes, optional
2 lb Fresh Polish sausage
1 cn Sauerkraut -- 16oz rinsed &
Draine
1 cn Omatoes -- 16oz w/ liquid,
Cut
1 c  Chopped celery
1 lg Onion -- chopped
Cut the sausage into 1-in. slices; saute in a Dutch oven until fully
cooked and browned. Drain. Add sauerkraut, tomatoes with liquid,
celery, onion, green pepper, brown sugar and seasonings; mix well.
Cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
Remove bay leaf. Serve over mashed potatoes if desired. Yield: 8
servings.
