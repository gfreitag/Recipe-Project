Title: Lazy-Day Cranberry Relish
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Orange peel -- grated
4 c  Cranberries fresh or frozen
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Orange juice
In a slow cooker, combine sugar, orange juice and peel; stir until
sugar is dissolved. Add the cranberries. Cover and cook on low for 6
hr. Mash the mixture. Chill several hours or overnight. Yield: 10 to
12 servings. (3 cups)
