Title: Buffet Wild Duck
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Game, Holiday
Yield: 20 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Butter sauce
Orange twists (optional)
Fresh rosemary leaves (opt'l
2 ea To 3 large cooking apples;
-cored and cut into wedges
10 ea 1-1/2 lb to 2-pound wild
-ducks; dressed
2 c  Burgundy wine
1 c  ;water
MMMMM------------------------BUTTER SAUCE-----------------------------
1/4 c  Green onions; sliced
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 ts Prepared mustard
1 c  Butter; melted
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Fresh parsley; chopped
Place apple wedges in cavities of ducks. Arrange ducks in a single
or double layer in an extra-large roaster or two large Dutch ovens;
pour Burgundy and water over ducks.  Cover and bake at 250 degrees F
for 3 hours or until ducks are very tender, basting occasionally.
Remove ducks from roaster, discarding apple wedges and pan
drippings; let ducks chill thoroughly.
Bone ducks, and cut meat into bite-sized pieces; return to roaster.
Sprinkle meat with salt and pepper.  Drizzle Butter Sauce over meat;
toss gently.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for an additional 30
minutes or until thoroughly heated.  Transfer meat to serving
platter, and garnish with orange twists and rosemary, if desired.
Yield: 20 servings.

BUTTER SAUCE:
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan; heat thoroughly.  Yield:
1-2/3 cups.
