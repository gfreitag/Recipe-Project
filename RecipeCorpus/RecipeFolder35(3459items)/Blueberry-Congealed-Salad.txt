Title: Blueberry Congealed Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
12 oz Crushed pineapple
1 cn Blueberry pie filling
3 oz Grape Jello
2 c  Boiling water
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla
8 oz Cream cheese
1 c  Sour cream
Dissolve Jello in boiling water, add pineapple with juice.  Add
blueberry pie filling.  Pour into mold and chill.  Add topping listed
above. Mix and spread over gelatin.  Sprinkle with chopped nuts.
