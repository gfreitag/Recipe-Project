Title: Michigan Bean Bake
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
3 tb Prepared mustard
1 1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 lg Sweet onion -- chopped
Catsup -- 14oz.
1    Jar Northern beans -- 48oz.
Cooked
1 1/2 lb Pork -- cut in l" cubes
1/2 ts Salt
1    Bottle
Combine all ingredients; mix gently but well. Place in beanpot,
casserole or slow cooker. Bake at 300 deg., uncovered, for 5 to 6
hours; cover last hour. Or heat on low in slow cooker overnight.
Yield: 10-12 servings.
