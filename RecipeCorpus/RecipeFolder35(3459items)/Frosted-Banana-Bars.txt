Title: Frosted Banana Bars
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ds Salt
FROSTING:
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1 pk Cream cheese -- 8 ounces
Softened
4 c  Confectioners' sugar
2 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
2 c  Sugar
3 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  Ripe bananas -- mashed 3
Medium
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Beat in eggs, bananas and
vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; add to creamed
mixture and mix well. Pour into a greased 15-in, x 10-in. x1-in.
baking pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 25 minutes or until bars test done.
Cool. For frosting, cream butter and cream cheese in a mixing bowl.
Gradually add confectioners' sugar and vanilla; beat well. Spread
over bars. Yield: 3 dozen
