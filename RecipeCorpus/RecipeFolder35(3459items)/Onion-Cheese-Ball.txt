Title: Onion Cheese Ball
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Dried minced onion
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Fresh parsley -- minced
Crackers
2 c  Cheddar cheese -- 8 oz
Shredded
4 oz Roquefort cheese
4 pk Cream cheese -- 3 oz ea.
Softened
In a mixing bowl, beat cheeses, onion and Worcestershire sauce until
well mixed. Shape into a ball and roll in parsley. Chill. Serve with
crackers. Yield: 1 cheese ball (4 inches)
