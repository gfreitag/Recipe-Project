Title: Cottage-Cauliflower Spread
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Onion -- grated
1 1/2 tb Fresh oregano -- chopped
Freshly ground black pepper
Whole wheat crackers
1 c  Cream-style cottage cheese
2 c  Cauliflowerets -- cooked
1 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
2 ea Garlic cloves -- minced
In a mixing bowl, combine cottage cheese and cauliflower. Mix until
almost smooth. Stir in Parmesan cheese, garlic, onion, oregano and
pepper. Spoon into a greased 1-qt. baking dish. Bake at 400 deg. for
20 minutes. Serve with crackers. Yield: about 3-1/2 cups
