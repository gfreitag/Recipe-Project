Title: Chocolate Velvet Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Chocolate
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Milk
4 oz Semisweet chocolate square
8 oz Whipped topping -- thawed
1    Graham cracker pie crust --
-extra size
2 pk Chocolate pudding mix --
-instant, 4 serving
In medium bowl, combine pudding and milk; beat with electric mixer
until well blended, about 3 minutes.  Gradually blend in chocolate
until smooth. Fold in whippped topping.  Turn into crust.  Freeze
until firm; about 4 hours.  Garnish with additional whipped topping.
