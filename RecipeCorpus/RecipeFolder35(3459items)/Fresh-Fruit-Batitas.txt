Title: Fresh Fruit Batitas
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM------------------------BASIC BATITA-----------------------------
2 ea Scoops crushed ice
2 tb Milk
MMMMM----------------------MANGO VARIATION---------------------------
6 tb Mango nectar
4 ea Slices fresh mango
MMMMM---------------------PINAPPLE VARIATION--------------------------
6 tb Pineapple juice
2 ea Slices fresh pineapple
MMMMM----------------------BANANA VARIATION---------------------------
6 tb Banana nectar
1 ea Banana; peeled, sliced
Place milk, ice and fruit and juice of desired variation in a blender
and puree until smooth.

Makes 2 servings

Per serving: Calories: 55 (11% from fat) Fat: 1 gram Cholesterol: 2 mg
Sodium: 8 mg
