Title: Pizza Style Pasta
Categories: Pasta/rice, Pizza, Sausage, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM---------------------PREP TIME: 20 MIN--------------------------
MMMMM---------------------COOK TIME: 20 MIN--------------------------
1 c  Slice red pepper strips
2 c  Mozzarella cheese, grated
-and divided
3 c  Cooked rotini, drained
2 c  Pizza sauce, homemade or
-commercial
1 c  Slice pepperoni
Mix together all ingredients, saving about 1/2 cup grated mozzarella
for spinkling on top.  Spoon into a greased round shallow casserole
and sprinkle on the 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese.  Bake in a 350F oven
for about 20 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and starting to brown
slightly. Makes 8 servings. Origin: What's Cooking, 13th issue,
booklet put out by Kraft. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/95.
