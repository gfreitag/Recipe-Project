Title: Pheasant And Wild Rice
Categories: Pasta/rice, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
ts Pepper
6 ea Bacon strips -- cut up
1    Oven cooking bag
2 c  Uncooked wild rice
1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms -- sliced
1 lg Pheasant -- halved
Or 2 small pheasants (about
4    Pounds)
1 cn Condensed cream of mushroom
Soup -- undiluted
2    Soup cans water
3/4 c  Onion -- chopped
2 1/2 ts Dried parsley flakes
2 ts Dried oregano
2 ts Garlic powder
1 1/2 ts Paprika
2 ts Salt
In a saucepan, combine first nine ingredients; bring to a boil.
Meanwhile, place bacon in an oven cooking bag. Sprinkle rice and
mushrooms over bacon. Add pheasant. Pour soup mixture into bag. Seal
and slit according to package directions. Bake at 325 deg. for 2 to
2-1/2 hours. Yield: 6-8 servings
