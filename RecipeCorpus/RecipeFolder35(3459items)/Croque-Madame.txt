Title: Croque-Madame
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
4 sl Thin natural Gruyere
1 tb Water
Dash of salt and freshly
Ground pepper
8 sl White bread, crusts removed
1/4 pn Butter, softened
4 sl Broad thin slices cooked
-chicken, breast meat
Spread the bread with some of the softened butter. Make 4 sandwiches.
each with one slice of chicken and one of cheese. Press firmly
together. Proceed as with the Croque-Monsieur.

Comment

This traditional French appetizer is an elegant delicate version of
the grilled cheese sandwich. Each sandwich may be cut in quarters
before dipping to make bite-sized hot hors-d'oeuvres for casual
entertaining as well as formal dinners.

Source; Completely Cheese, The Cheese Lover's Companion, Anita May
Pearl
