Title: Gasali's Seafood Lasagna (Lasagne Al Corallo)
Categories: Pasta/rice, Italian, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 10 servings
600 ml Bechamel
150 g  Butter
600 g  Lasagna (dough)
1 1/2    Litres seafood sauce
Cook pasta until al dente. Mix in seafood sauce, bechamel and butter.

Source:  Given to share with the express permission of Chef de Cuisine
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.
