Title: Apple Cinnamon Chiffon Pie
Categories: Fruit, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Applesauce
2 tb Sugar
1    9 Inch Pie Shell -- baked
3    Eggs -- separated
1/4 c  Water
1    Env Unflavored Gelatin
2 tb Red Cinnamon Candies
In a medium-sized saucepan, beat egg yolks with water. Sprinkle
gelatin into saucepan and let stand 1 min. Add candies and
applesauce. Stir over low heat until gelatin is dissolved, about 5
mins. Pour into large bowl and chill, stirring occasionally, until
mixture mounds slightly when dropped from spoon.

In a large bowl, beat egg whites until soft peaks form; gradually add
sugar and beat until stiff. Fold into gelatin mixture. Turn into
prepared crust and chill until firm.
