Title: Toast Turkey Sandwiches
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Miracle WhipÂ® light
1/3 c  Cranberry sauce -- whole
Berry
12 ea French bread slice --
Buttered 1/2 in
6 ea Cooked turkey -- thin slice
6 ea Cooked ham -- thin slice
2 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
Make six sandwiches, dividing turkey and ham evenly between bread. In
a shallow bowl; beat eggs and milk. Dip sandwiches, turning to coat
both sides in a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Brown
sandwiches on both sides. Combine the salad dressing and cranberry
sauce; mix well. Serve with sandwiches. Yield 6 servings.
