Title: ALL PURPOSE MARINADE - RECIPELU
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 6 Servings
3    Dry white wine
1/2    Cayenne pepper
1    Onion powder
1/2    Soy sauce
1/2    Garlic powder
1.  Mix all ingredients together.  Marinate the meat for 3-6 hours

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~` NOTES
: Good on chicken, beef, pork.
