Title: ALMOND PASTE
Categories: Nuts
Yield: 1 Servings  Ingredients:

6 oz Ground Almonds
3 oz Caster Sugar
3 oz Icing Sugar
1 ts Lemon Juice
1    Egg Yolk
Few drops of Almond Essence

Directions:

To make the almond paste: Sift the ground almonds and sugars
together. Add the remaining ingredients, mix well and knead lightly
on a board.
