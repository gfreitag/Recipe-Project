Title: ALL-AMERICAN TUNA-SALAD SANDWICHES
Categories: Low fat, Fish/shellf
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Cans (6 ounces each)water-
-packed white tuna; drained
1 c  Diced celery
1 c  Shredded carrots
1 c  Diced tomato
1/2 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
1/4 c  Thinly sliced scallions
2 tb Reduced-fat mayonnaise
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
8 sl Whole-wheat bread, toasted
Put tuna in a medium-size bowl and flake with a fork. Add remaining
salad ingredients and mix until blended. Makes 3 1/3 cups tuna salad-
enough for 4 sandwiches. Prep time: 15 minutes. Total cost: $5.60.
