Title: 600366 THE BEST "GREEK CHICKEN" IN THE WORLD
Categories: Greek, Chicken
Yield: 8 Servings
3 md Whole chickens
8    Idaho baking potatoes
1/2 c  Olive oil
3    Lemons, cut into wedges
-Salt and pepper
-Fresh oregano (generous
-amount)
1    Stick butter
Place scrubbed potatoes with skins in oven at 375 degrees. Bake for 45
minutes or until done.  After potatoes are baked, slice them 1 1/2
inches thick. Place sliced potatoes around freshly cleaned chickens
in a large baking pan.  Sprinkle chickens and potatoes with olive
oil, salt and pepper, fresh oregano, and lemon juice. Place chunks of
butter inside chickens and onto potatoes. Also place a lemon wedge
freshly squeezed inside each chicken.  Place inside oven at 325
degrees. Baste chicken and potatoes with juices from the pan for
added moisture. Chickens will be done when skins are crispy brown and
legs lift easily from the joint. Serve the meal with fresh Greek
bread, slightly toasted, to dip into the juices.
