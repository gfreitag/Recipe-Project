Title: ALMOND DEVILED EGGS
Categories: Egg
Yield: 12 Servings
6    Hard-boiled egg
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 ts Garlic salt
3 ts Roasted almonds; finely chop
12    Roasted almonds
Fresh parsley
Recipe by: Country Woman Preparation Time: 0:30 Slice eggs in half
lengthwise; remove yolks and set whites aside. In a small bowl, mash
yolks, mayonnaise, mustard, garlic salt and chopped almonds. Evenly
fill the egg whites, Garnish with whole almonds and parsley. Chill
until read to serve.Yield: 12 servings.
