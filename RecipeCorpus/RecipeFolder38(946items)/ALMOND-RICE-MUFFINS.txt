Title: ALMOND RICE MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Quick, Gluten-free
Yield: 12 Muffins
2 c  AM Brown Rice Flour
1 tb Non-alum baking powder
1 tb Arrowroot powder
1/4 c  Honey
1 ts Almond extract
1 1/4 c  Water or soymilk
2 tb AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil
1/2 c  Ground almond
Mix dry and liquid ingredients separately, then combine the two and
mix thoroughly (this is a thin batter). Fill 12 paper muffin cups 2/3
full and sprinkle tops with almond.  Bake at 350 F. for 25-30 minutes.
