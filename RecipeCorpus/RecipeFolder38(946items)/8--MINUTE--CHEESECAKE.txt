Title: 8  MINUTE  CHEESECAKE
Categories: Kaz, Cheesecakes
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk (8 oz.) cream cheese --
Softened
1/3 c  Sugar
1 c  (1/2 pt.) sour cream
2 ts Vanilla
1    (8 oz.) container Birds Eye
Cool Whip whip
1    Keebler
-
Fresh strawberries for
Garnish
Ready Crust graham cracker
Pie crust
Beat cheese until smooth; gradually beat in sugar. Blend in sour
cream and vanilla.  Fold in whipped topping, blending well. Spoon
into crust. Chill until set, at least 4 hours. Garnish with fresh
strawberries if desired.
