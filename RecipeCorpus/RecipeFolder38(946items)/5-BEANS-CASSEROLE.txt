Title: 5-BEANS CASSEROLE
Categories: Prodigy, Dec.
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Garbanzo beans
1 cn Lentils
1 cn Great northern whites
1 cn Pinto beans
1 cn Kidney beans
1 pk Lipton's French Onion soup
2    Generous Tbsp Kozlowski
Farms South of the Border no
Fat Dressing.
3 tb Hain's honey barbeque sauce
Heat all the ingredients.  Simply wonderful.
