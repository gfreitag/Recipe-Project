Title: 7 LAYER CASSEROLE
Categories: Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Uncooked rice
1 c  Whole kernal corn
1    Small can tomato sauce
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Chopped onions and green
Peppers
1 lb Plus hamburg
1    Small can tomato sauce
1/4 c  Water
In a casserole dish, layer ingredients as listed.  Top with 3 to 5
slices of bacon.  Bake, covered, at 350 degrees for 1 hour, then bake
uncovered for 1/2 hour.
