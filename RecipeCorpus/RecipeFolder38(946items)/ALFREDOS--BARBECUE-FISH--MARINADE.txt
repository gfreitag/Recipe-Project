Title: ALFREDOS  BARBECUE FISH  MARINADE
Categories: Sauces, Seasonings, Seafood
Yield: 30 Servings
1 c  Soy sauce
1 c  Lime juice
1 c  Vinegar
1 c  Oil
2 ts Pepper
2 ts Salt
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Lawrys season salt
1/4 c  Sesame seeds
1    Clove garlic -- minced
Marinate fish for at least one hour before cooking. Serves 30.
