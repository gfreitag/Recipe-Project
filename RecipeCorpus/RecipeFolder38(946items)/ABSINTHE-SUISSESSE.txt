Title: ABSINTHE SUISSESSE
Categories: Essence, Of, Emeril
Yield: 4 Servings    1 1/2 oz Pernod
1/2 oz orgeat -- (almond simple
syrup)
:
1    egg white -- (optional)
1/2 oz heavy cream
4 oz shaved ice

In freezer chill an old-fashioned glass until frosted. In a blender
combine all ingredients; process 5 seconds or until frothy. Pour into
prepared glass and serve
