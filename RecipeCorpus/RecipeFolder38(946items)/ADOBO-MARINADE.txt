Title: ADOBO MARINADE
Categories: Cuba, Sauce
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ts Ground cumin
4    Cloves garlic
3/4 c  Lime juice
Pepper -- to taste
Blend cumin, garlic and lime juice until smooth. Taste and season with
pepper.  Marinade fish (chicken or meat) in the refrigerator for 1
hour.
