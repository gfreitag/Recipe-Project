Title: A GARDEN OF SPRING TULIPS CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Holidays
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Betty Crocker Super Moist
-lemon cake mix
1 1/4 c  Water
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
3    Eggs
1    Tub Betty Crocker Creamy
-Deluxe lemon ready-to-
-spread frosting
20    Gumdrop Tulips
GUMDROP TULIPS:
10    Green leaf-shaped grumdrops
20    Green toothpicks
20 sm Assorted colored gumdrops
Heat oven to 350Ã¸.  Generously grease and flour 2 round pans 8 or 9x
1 1/2 inches.  Beat cake mix (dry), water, oil and eggs on low speed
30 seconds. Beat on medium speed 2 minutes. Pour into pans. Bake 25
to 34 minutes or until cake springs back when touched lightly in
center. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans. Cool completely on wire
rack. Fill layers and frost cake with frosting.  Garnish with Gumdrop
Tulips.

Gumdrop Tulips:  Cut green gumdrops horizontally in half for leaves.
Thread 1 leaf half onto middle of each toothpick. Snip bottom of each
gumdrop 4 times to form tulip petals. Place on end of toothpick.
