Title: 1000 ISLAND DRESSING #2
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 1 Qt
1    Egg, hard boiled
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Milk
2 tb Ketchup
2 tb Green hamburger relish
1 tb Chopped green pepper
1 tb Dehyrated onion flakes
Mash egg with fork, stir in rest of the ingredients. Walt MM
