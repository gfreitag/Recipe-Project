Title: 1000 ISLAND DRESSING (FANNY FARMER COOKBOOK)
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 1 Pt
1/3 c  Salad oil
2 tb Orange juice
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Paprika
2 ts Minced onion
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Dry mustard
2 ts Minced parsley
Salt to taste
Combine in pint jar, put lid on securely & shake until well blended.
