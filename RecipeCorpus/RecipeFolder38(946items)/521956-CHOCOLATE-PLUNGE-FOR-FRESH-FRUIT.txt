Title: 521956 CHOCOLATE PLUNGE FOR FRESH FRUIT
Categories: Sauces, Chocolate
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Karo light or dark corn
-syrup
1/2 c  Heavy or whipping cream
1 pk (8 oz.) Baker's semi sweet
-chocolate or 2 pkgs. (4 oz.
-each) Baker's
-German sweet chocolate
In medium saucepan stir corn syrup and cream.  Bring to boil over
medium heat.  Remove from heat.  Add chocolate, stir until completely
melted. Serve warm as a dip for fruit. Makes 1 1/2 cups.
