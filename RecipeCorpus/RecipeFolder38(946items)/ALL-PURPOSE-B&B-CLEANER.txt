Title: ALL PURPOSE B&B CLEANER
Categories: Non-edible
1/2 c  hosehold ammonia
Yield: 1 Gallon
2 c  70% isopropyl alcohol
1 ts dish detergent
water to make one gallon
:

Use a spray bottle to clean.  Glass, counter tops, enamel, sinks,
toilets all come squeeky clean and disinfected. I store in a used
gallon container. I run a B&B and find its great and saves me a lot
of time. NOTE: Poison, keep out of reach of children.
