Title: ALICE'S  PICKLES
Categories: Kaz
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Celery seeds
1    To 1 1/2 c. white vinegar
1    To 1 1/2 c. sugar
7 md Cucumbers -- slice
2 tb Salt
Fresh vegetables - onion --
Green beans, yellow
Boil for 3 minutes 2 tablespoons celery seeds, 1 to 1 1/2 cups white
vinegar and 1 to 1 1/2 cups sugar and cool. Soak in 2 tablespoons
salt for 1 hour cucumbers, then drain. Mix boiled mixture and all
vegetables in large jar and refrigerate.
