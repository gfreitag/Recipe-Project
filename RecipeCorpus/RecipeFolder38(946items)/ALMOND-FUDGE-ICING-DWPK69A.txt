Title: ALMOND FUDGE ICING DWPK69A
Categories: Icings
Yield: 12 Servings
1 lb Powdered sugar
8 oz Cream cheese
1/2 c  Margarine or butter
1/4 c  Cocoa
Mix ingredients together well with electric mixer, then sprinkle
remaining 1/2 cup almonds to decorate. Hope you enjoy this. I do!
