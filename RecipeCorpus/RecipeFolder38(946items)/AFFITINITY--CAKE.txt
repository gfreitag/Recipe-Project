Title: AFFITINITY  CAKE
Categories: Kaz
Yield: 4 Servings
3/4 c  Butter
2 c  Brown sugar
3    Eggs
1 c  Milk
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Chopped nuts
Do not mix above ingredients separately, put into bowl and beat all
together 10 minutes.  Bake in a slow over (325 degrees) for 50
minutes.
