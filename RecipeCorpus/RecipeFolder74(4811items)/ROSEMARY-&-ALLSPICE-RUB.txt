Title: ROSEMARY & ALLSPICE RUB
Categories: Marinades, Bar-b-q
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-----------------FOR BEEF,POULTRY,PORK,LAMB----------------------
1    Garlic clove; crushed
10    Allspice berries; crushed
1/2 ts Finely chpd.rosemary leaves
2 tb Olive oil
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mix until smooth. Makes about 1/4
cup.
