Title: REESE'S PEANUT BUTTER BARS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 40 Servings
1 1/2 c  Graham Crackers; crushed
1 lb Powdered sugar
1 1/2 c  Extra Chunky peanut butter
1 c  Butter; melted
12 oz Chocolate chips
In bowl, mix 1 1/2 cups graham crackers, crushed, with 1 lb. box
powdered sugar. Add 1 1/2 cups extra chunky peanut butter and add 2
sticks melted butter. Mix well. Pat into a 9 x 13 buttered pan. Melt
12 oz bag chocolate chips -- spread on top. Put in fridge for 10-15
mins, then cut into small squares. Return to fridge until firm.
