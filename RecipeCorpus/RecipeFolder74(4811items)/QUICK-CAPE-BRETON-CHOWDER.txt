Categories: Seafood, Soups
Yield: 8 Servings
- Cal/Serving: 150
- Prep Time: 10 Min
- Cooking Time: 10 min
1 lb Cod fillets; frozen *
1 tb Butter
1/2 c  Onion; finely chopped
10 oz Cream of celery soup
14 oz Clam chowder
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Water
Parsley; chopped
