Title: PORK BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Bar-b-q
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Onion, finely minced
3/4 c  Garlic, finely minced
2 tb Tiger sauce
2 tb Worchestershire sauce
2 1/2 tb Mustard
Blend together all ingredients.  The technique used was to stab large
holes in a pork roast, massage the sauce into the holes produced and
marinate overnight.
