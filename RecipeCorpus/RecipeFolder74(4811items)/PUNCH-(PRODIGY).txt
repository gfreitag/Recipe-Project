Title: PUNCH (PRODIGY)
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
1 c  Strong tea
3 c  Fruit nectar
- such as mango, pear,
- OR apricot
1 qt Soda water
BOIL SUGAR AND WATER together for 10 minutes, then pour into a bowl.
Add the tea and fruit nectar and chill. Add the soda water just
before serving.

Makes 1/2 Gallon
