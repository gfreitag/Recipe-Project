Title: POPPY SEED CAKE *** NFXS18B
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn Solo poppy seed filling
1 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Sugar
4 x  Eggs, separated
1 c  Sour cream
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Baking soda
Cream butter and sugar. Add Solo. Add egg yolks, one at a time,
beating after each. Blend in vanilla and sour cream. Sift together
flour, soda and salt. Add gradually. Fold in stiffly beaten egg
whites. Pour into greased tube or bundt pan. Bake at 350 for 1 hr
15-20 mins. Drizzle with glaze made with powdered sugar and milk
