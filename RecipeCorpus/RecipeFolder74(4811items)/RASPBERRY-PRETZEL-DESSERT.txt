Title: RASPBERRY PRETZEL DESSERT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Pretzel sticks, crushed
3 tb Sugar
3/4 c  Butter, melted
1 ct Cool Whip (12 oz.)
1 pk Cream cheese, softened 8 oz.
1 lg Box of raspberry jello
2 c  Boiling water
2    Boxes of frozen raspberries
10 oz. size
Mis the crushed pretzels, sugar and butter.  Pat into a 9x13 inch pan
and bake at 400 degrees for 7 minutes. Let cool. Carefully fold cream
cheese to Cool Whip and spread on top of pretzel layer. Mix the jello
and water; stir in frozen raspberries.  Pour over second layer.
Refrigerate overnight. Randy Rigg
