Title: QUICK BEEF SAUSAGE - NELLA
Categories: Appetizers, Hamburger, Beef, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
4 lb Lean Ground Beef
1/4 c  Morton Tender Quick Salt
2 ts Pepper
2 ts Liquid Smooke
1 ts Garlic Powder
In a large bowl combine ground beef and quick salt. Mix and leave
overnight in the refrigerator.  Next day add pepper, liquid smoke and
garlic powder. Mix well, divide into 3 rolls. Bake at 225Ã¸F for 4
hours on broiler pan on lower rack in oven.  Wrap in paper towels to
absorb grease. Refrigerate or freeze.
