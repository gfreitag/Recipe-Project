Title: ROUND STEAK SUPREME
Categories: Meats, Crockpot, Beef
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM------------------JANICE NOHRENN  KRVD70B-----------------------
4 lb Round steak; cut in serving
Size pieces
1 c  Fresh mushrooms
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 cn Beefy mushroom soup
1/2 c  Undiluted evaporated milk
Brown meat and place in crock pot. Combine remaining ingredients and
pour over meat. Cook on low heat 8 to 10 hours. Serve with mashed
potatoes or rice.
