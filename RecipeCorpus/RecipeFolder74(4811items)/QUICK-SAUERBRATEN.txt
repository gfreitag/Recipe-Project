Title: QUICK SAUERBRATEN
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Beef gravy (10 3/4 oz.)
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
3 tb Sugar
10    Whole cloves
1 c  Water
1/3 c  Gingersnaps, crushed fine
8 sl Cooked pot roast (thin sl.)
Combine gravy, water, vinegar, gingersnaps, sugar and cloves in
large skillet.  Bring to boil, stirring. Simmer and stir abt. 5 min.
or until thickened and smooth. Add beef slices. Cook until meat is
hot, basting with sauce.  Serve with mashed potatoes.
