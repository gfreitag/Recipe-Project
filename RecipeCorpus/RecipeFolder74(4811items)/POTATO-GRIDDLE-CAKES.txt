Title: POTATO GRIDDLE CAKES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Potatoes; grated
1 ea Egg; beaten
1/2 c  Flour; sifted
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt; level
1/8 ts Pepper
Mix and bake on greased griddle in three-inch cakes. Serve with
butter and syrup.

Source: Mrs. W. D. Mosure, Ewing Grange, Hocking County, OH
