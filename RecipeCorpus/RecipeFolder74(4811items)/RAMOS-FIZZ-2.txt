Title: RAMOS FIZZ 2
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, New (to be
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Gin
1/2 oz Sweet Cream
1 1/2 oz Fresh Lemon Juice
1    Egg White
1 ts Powdered Sugar
2 ds Orange Juice -- * see note
4 oz Club Soda -- or to fill
Ice Cubes -- cracked
* Or orange flower water.

Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add gin, cream, lemon juice, egg
white, powdered sugar and orange juice. Shake well and strain into a
chilled Collins glass. Top with club soda.

Not worth the risk of giving a customer saminilla from the raw egg and
getting sued!
