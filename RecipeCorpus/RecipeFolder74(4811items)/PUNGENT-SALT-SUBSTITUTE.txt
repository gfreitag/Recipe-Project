Title: PUNGENT SALT SUBSTITUTE
Categories: Spices
Yield: 6 Servings
3 ts Basil
2 ts Summer savory
2 ts Celery seed
2 ts Ground cumin
2 ts Sage
1 ts Thyme
2 ts Marjoram
Put ingredients into a blender, food processor or clean coffee
grinder. (Or crush with a mortar and pestle.) Blend well. Store in a
glass container in a cool, dry place. To prevent caking, add a few
grains of rice.
