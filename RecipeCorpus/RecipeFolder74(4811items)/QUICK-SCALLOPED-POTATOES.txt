Title: QUICK SCALLOPED POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
5 md Potatoes
2 md Onions
3 ts Salt
3 tb Flour
3 tb Marg
2 1/2 c  Milk
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Paprika
Preheat oven to 400. Peel and slice onions.  Peel and slice Potatoes.
Cover with hot water and add 2 tsp salt. Bring to boil and cook 5
minutes. Drain and put in a greased casserole. Mix marg and flour in
microwave safe glass measuring cup.  Microwave 1 minute. Add Milk
slowly whisking to keep lumps from forming. Add 1 ts salt, pepper and
paprika. Microwave stirring occasionally til cooked.  Makes fairly
loose sauce. Pour over potato/onion mixture in casserole and bake for
35 minutes.
