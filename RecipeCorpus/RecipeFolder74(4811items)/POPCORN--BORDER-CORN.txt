Title: POPCORN--BORDER CORN
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks, Thanksgivin
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Unpopped corn (8 cups
Popped)
1 c  Shredded Monterey Jack
Cheese
2 ts Chili powder
2 ts Paprika
2 ts Ground cumin
Pop popcorn.  Mix spices into the shredded cheese. Sprinkle mixture
over unseasoned popcorn and toss until well blended.

From:  The Thanksgiving Cookbook Holly Garrison Macmillan Publishing
Company, 1991
