Title: RANOLA BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3/4 c  Whole wheat flour
2 c  Bread flour
1 c  Granola, ground finely in
-blender
3/4 ts Salt
1 3/4 ts Sugar
3/4 c  Warm water
1/2 c  Buttermilk
2 tb Sweet butter
2 tb Honey
1    Egg (Use ingredients at room
-temperature.)
"Use your favorite granola. We used one with dates and nuts. Healthy,
preservative-free bread has never tasted this good."

Add all ingredients into the pan in the order listed, select white
bread and push "Start."

Try adding 1/2 cup raisins or dates!
