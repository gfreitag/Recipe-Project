Title: RO*TEL CORNBREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
3/4 c  White Cornmeal
3/4 c  Yellow Cornmeal
3 tb Flour
1/4 ts Soda
1 1/2 ts Baking Powder
1 ts Salt
2 tb Vegetable Shortening; melted
1 c  Buttermilk
1    Egg; beaten
1    Jalapeno; finely chopped
10 oz RO*TEL Tomatoe w/Green Chili
Mix all ingredients together.  Bake at 425 degrees for 25 to 30
minutes. This recipe comes from a book "Snake, Rattle, and RO*TEL."
