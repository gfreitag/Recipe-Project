Title: QUICK CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Butter
1 tb Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped celery
3/4 c  Uncle ben's rice
1 cn Mushroom soup
1 cn Chicken & rice soup
1 cn Beef consomme
Saute onion & celery, add to other ingredients in casserole & bake at
350 until rice is cooked approximately 1 hour.
