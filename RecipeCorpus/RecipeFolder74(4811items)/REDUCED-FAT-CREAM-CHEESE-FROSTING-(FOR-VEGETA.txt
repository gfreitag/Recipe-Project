Title: REDUCED FAT CREAM CHEESE FROSTING (FOR VEGETA
Categories: Cakes, Low-fat
Yield: 1 Servings
INGREDIENTS
12 oz Lo-Fat cream cheese,
-softened
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1 1/2 ts Pure vanilla extract
In a mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar and vanilla; beat with
an electric mixer until smooth and creamy.
