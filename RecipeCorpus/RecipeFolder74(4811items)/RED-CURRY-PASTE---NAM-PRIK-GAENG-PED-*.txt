Title: RED CURRY PASTE - NAM PRIK GAENG PED *
Categories: Thai, Condiment
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Chopped Onions
8    Cloves Garlic
10    Dried Red Jalapeno Chilies
4    Thin Slices Galangal
2 tb Chopped Lemon Grass
1 tb Chopped Cilantro/Coriander
-Root Or Stems
1/2 ts Cumin
1 ts Shrimp Paste
1 ts Salt
3 tb Oil
Red and green curry pastes are the basis for most Thai curries.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Combine all the ingredients except the oil in a
blender and process until smooth.

Heat a small skillet on medium-high heat and add the oil. Slowly fry
the curry paste for 5 minutes until it is fragrant. Remove and store
in a jar for future use. Makes 2-1/2 cups.

From: Thailand The Beautiful Cookbook.  Typed by Syd Bigger.
