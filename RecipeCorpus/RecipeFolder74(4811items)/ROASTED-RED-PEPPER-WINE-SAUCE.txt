Title: ROASTED RED PEPPER WINE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 999 Servings
2 ea Red bell peppers, roasted,
-peeled and seeded
1 ts Balsamic vinegar
1/3 c  Dry white wine
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1 ts Olive oil
Dash ground white pepper
In food processor or blender, combine bell peppers, vinegar, wine,
garlic, olive oil and white pepper. Process until smooth. Pour into
small saucepan and simmer 10 minutes.
