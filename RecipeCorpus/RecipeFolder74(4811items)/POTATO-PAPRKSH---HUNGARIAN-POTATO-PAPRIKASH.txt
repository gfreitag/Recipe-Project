Title: POTATO-PAPRKSH - HUNGARIAN POTATO PAPRIKASH
Categories: Try, It, Hungarian
Yield: 4 Servings
4 oz Cooking oil
1    Clove of garlic, crushed
1/4 ts Caraway seeds
4 lb Potatoes, peeled and
-sliced thin.
2    Green peppers, seeded and
-cut into strips
2    Onions, finely chopped
1 c  Of water
1 ts Paprika
4    Tomatoes, peeled,seeded
-and chopped
-Salt (you can vary the
-amount to your taste.)
1  In a heavy-bottomed casserole, heat the oil and fry the onions and
garlic. 2 Add the caraway seeds, paprika, and water. 3 Add the
potatoes, peppers, and tomatoes. 4 Simmer for thirty minutes.

Difficulty: easy.  Time: 15 minutes preparation, 30 minutes cooking.
Precision: no need to measure.

A hearty dish, good as either a main course or a side dish.
