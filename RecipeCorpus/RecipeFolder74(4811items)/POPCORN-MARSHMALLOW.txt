Title: POPCORN MARSHMALLOW
Categories: Kids
Yield: 6 Servings
26 c  Popped popcorn
1 c  Salted peanuts
1/2 c  Baking gumdrops
In a saucepan melt: 1/2 cup
-margarine
1/2 c  Cooking oil
500    Gms. white marshmallows
Stir constantly while marshmallows melt and mix with melted margarine
and oil mixture. Do not allow this to boil, just melt over low heat.
Mix into popped corn mixture. Press into greased tube pan.
