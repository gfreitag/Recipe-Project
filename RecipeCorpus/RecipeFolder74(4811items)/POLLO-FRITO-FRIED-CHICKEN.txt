Title: POLLO FRITO FRIED CHICKEN
Categories: Mexican, Poultry
3 lb chicken
fryer, cut into serving
pieces
Yield: 4 Servings
:
:
1/2 c  flour
1 ts salt
1 ts paprika
1/8 ts pepper
2 TB oil
2 TB butter
1 sm onion -- chopped
1    clove garlic -- chopped
2 TB parsley -- chopped
1    green bell pepper --
chopped
:
3 TB flour
1 c  chicken stock
Salt and pepper to taste
:
1 TB lemon juice

Roll the chicken pieces in the flour, which has been well mixed with
the seasonings. Heat the oil and butter in a skillet; place chicken
pieces skin side down in skillet and cook, uncovered, for 15 to 25
minutes on each side, turning only once. Transfer chicken to heated
serving platter and keep warm. In the same skillet, adding more
butter if necessary, saute' the onion, garlic, parsley, and pepper.
Push the vegetables to one side, stir in the 3 tablespoons of flour,
cooking slowly for a few minutes; then add the chicken stock and stir
until thickened. Add salt and pepper to taste and lemon juice; blend
well. Pour this sauce over the chicken and serve. Makes 4 servings.
