Title: PRUNE BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 lb Dried prunes
3/4 c  Boiling water
1 3/4 c  Flour
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 ea Egg
2 tb Salad oil or melted crisco
Soak prunes in cold water 2 hours; drain. Pit prunes; chop. Add
boiling water; let stand 5 minutes. Sift to-gether flour, sugar, salt
and soda. Add prune mixture. Beat egg and add. Add salad oil or
melted crisco. Pour into greased loaf pan and bake in 325 oven 1
hour. Remove from pan and cool on rack.
