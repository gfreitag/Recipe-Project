Title: QUICK CHEESE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Velveeta cheese 1/2 lb
2 tb Butter
1 c  Milk
Salt and pepper
1 tb Cornstarch
Cut up cheese into a sauce pan.  Add butter and milk. Heat slowly
until cheese has melted.  Salt and pepper to taste. Dissolve
cornstarch in 1/2 cup water, stir in a little at a time until sauce
is of the required consistency.
