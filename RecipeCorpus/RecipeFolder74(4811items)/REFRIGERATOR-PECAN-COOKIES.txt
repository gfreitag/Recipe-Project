Title: REFRIGERATOR PECAN COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/4 c  Flour
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 ts Soda
1    Egg
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  Butter
1/2 c  Pecans
1/2 c  Sugar
Cream butter, sugars and egg.  Add remaining ingredients. Can be
dropped by teaspoonful or chilled and cut in 1/4-inch slices. Bake at
375 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes.
