Title: RED CHEESE SPEARS
Categories: None
Yield: 48 Servings
1/2 lb Red Belgian endive,ends
-trimmed
6 oz Chevre fromage blanc
-OR
6 oz Whipped cream cheese
3 tb Chopped salted pistachios
Trim base of endive heads to separate large outer leaves (save extras
for other uses, such as mixed salads).

In cup of each leaf place about 1 teaspoon cheese; sprinkle cheese
with the nuts and arrange leaves on a tray.
