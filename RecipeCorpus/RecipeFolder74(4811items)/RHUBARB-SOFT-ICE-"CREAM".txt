Title: RHUBARB SOFT ICE "CREAM"
Categories: Fruits, Desserts
Yield: 1 Quart
1 lb Rhubarb; cut in rough
-chunks (don't peel)
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  ;Water
2 tb Port, elderberry wine, or
-red currant liqueur
1    Egg white
Boil the rhubarb, sugar, and water together until the rhubarb is soft,
about 10 minutes.  Puree in a food processor or blender, then blend
in the wine and egg white. Allow to cool, then freeze the mixture in
an ice cream freezer according to the manufacturers directions.
