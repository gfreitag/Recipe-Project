Title: QUICK RAISIN BREAD
Categories: Breads, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
3/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Raisins
2 c  Cereal flakes
1 ea Egg
1 1/2 c  Buttermilk
4 tb Shortening; melted
Dredge raisins in small amount of flour, sift dry ingredients
together and add raisins and cereal. Beat eggs slightly, add milk and
melted shortening, blend well with first mixture but do not overmix.
Bake in greased loaf pan 350 F. about 1 hour.

Note: Type of cereal not given, corn or bran could probably be used.

Source: Ruth Hutchinson, Marathon Grange, Clermont County, OH
