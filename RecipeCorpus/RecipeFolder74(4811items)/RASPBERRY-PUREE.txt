Title: RASPBERRY PUREE
Categories: Candies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2    10 oz pkgs
-
2 tb Superfine sugar
1 tb Kirsch or other
Cherry-flavoured brandy
Frozen raspberries --
Defrosted & drained
Rub the raspberries with the back of a large spoon through a fine
sieve set over a mixing bowl.  Stir in the sugar and kirsch, cover
tightly with plastic wrap and refrigerate until ready to serve.
