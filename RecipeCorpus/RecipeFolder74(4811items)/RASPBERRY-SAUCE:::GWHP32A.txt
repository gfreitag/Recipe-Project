Title: RASPBERRY SAUCE:::GWHP32A
Categories: Sauce, Desserts
Yield: 16 Servings
1    Ingredient
1 c  Raspberries;fresh or->
12 oz Frozen (no sugar)
10 oz Raspberries;frozen w/sugar
1/2 c  Raspberry preserves
1 ts Kirsch
2 ts Lemon juice; or to taste
4:40 PM Drain raspberries, reserving syrup.
03/09/92
Place fresh and frozen raspberries and preserves in the bowl of a
food processor. Puree with the metal blade. Pass the puree through a
food mill to eliminate seeds. Stir in kirsch and lemon juice. Source:
Bristol Farms Cooking Demo Shared by Barb Day
