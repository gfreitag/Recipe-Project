Title: PRALINE COFFEE
Categories: Beverage, Liquor, Posted-mm
Yield: 6 Cups
3 c  Hot brewed coffee
3/4 c  Half-and-half
3/4 c  Firmly packed Lt.Brown sugar
2 tb Butter or margarine
3/4 c  Paraline liqueur
Sweetened whipped cream
Source: Southern Living Magazine 1/97

Cook first 4 ingredients in a large saucepan over medium heat,
stirring constantly, until thoroughly heated (do not boil).

Stir in liqueur; serve with sweetened whipped cream.
