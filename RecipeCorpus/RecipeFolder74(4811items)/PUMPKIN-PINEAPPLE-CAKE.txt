Title: PUMPKIN PINEAPPLE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 10" cake
3 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Vegetable oil
15 oz Crushed pineapple, UNdrained
2 ts Vanilla
3 lg Eggs, beaten fluffy
2 c  Pumpkin, raw, grated
1 c  Pecans, chopped
Combine dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl.  Blend in oil, then
pineapple and vanilla.  Add eggs, beating mixture well. Then stir in
pumpkin and nuts.  Grease and flour a 10" tube pan. Pour in batter.
Bake in a 350 degree oven 1 hour and 15 minutes, or unitl cake tester
inserted near center comes out clean.

Source:  "Old-fashioned Pumpkin Recipes"  -a Bear Wallow book
