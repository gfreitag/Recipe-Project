Title: RED DEVIL EGGS
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Eggs, hard cooked
2 tb Mayonaisse
1 ts Grated onion
1 ts Mustard
1/2 ts Worcestshire sauce
1 1/2 oz Deviled ham
Salt and pepper to taste

Cut hard cooked eggs in half - removing yolks to bowl ~ mix remaining
ingredients with yolks and pile lightly into reserved halves. Dust
lightly with paprika before serving.
