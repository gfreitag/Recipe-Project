Title: POPEYE'S BISCUITS #2
Categories: Breads, Biscuits, Cake mix, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
DABBS (RFNR19B)
4 c  Pioneer biscuit mix
8 oz Sour cream
6 oz 7-Up
Cut sour cream in biscuit mix, add 7-up. Roll on floured surface. Cut
or shape with hands. Bake 6 to 8 minutes at 400~. (wrv)
