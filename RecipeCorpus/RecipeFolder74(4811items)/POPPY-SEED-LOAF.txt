Title: POPPY SEED LOAF
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Poppy seeds
1 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  Flour
1/2 lb Butter
2 ts Baking powder
4    Egg whites, beaten until
-foamy
Mix together poppy seeds and milk and leave in dish for 2 hours. Mix
other ingredients in order given. Bake 325 degrees for 1 hour in
large greased loaf pan.
