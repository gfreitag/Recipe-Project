Title: PUMPKIN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 16 Servings
1/2 tb Yeast
1/2 c  Wheat flour
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
1/2 ts Pumpkin pie spice
1/4 ts Lemon peel, grated
1/2 ts Salt
3/8 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Pumpkin, canned
1    Eggs
1 tb Butter
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Water, warm
