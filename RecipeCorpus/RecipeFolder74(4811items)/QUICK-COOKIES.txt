Title: QUICK COOKIES
Categories: Desserts, Cookies
Yield: 30 Cookies
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  White sugar
1/4 c  Butter
1    Egg, unbeaten
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  Chopped nuts (optional)
Cream sugars and butter together. Mix in egg. Add flour sifted with
baking powder. Add vanilla and nuts. Drop from a spoon on a lightly
greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350deg for 7 minutes. Check for cookie
to be just browned on the bottom for chewy cookies.
