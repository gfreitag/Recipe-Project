Title: RAINBOW PUNCH
Categories: Non-alcohol, Cocktail, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Orange juice
1 1/2 c  Pineapple juice
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
1 tb Grenadine syrup
1/2 ts Bitters
1 c  Club soda
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Maraschino cherries
Orange slices
1.  Mix all ingredients except soda in a glass measure or pitcher. 2.
Pour into 4 ice-filled tall glasses. Top each with 1/4 cup club soda.
Garnish with cherries and orange slices.

Source Woman's Day 12/17/97
