Title: ROSE HIP JELLY
Categories: Preserves, Flowers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Rose hips
1 c  Water
Sugar
Wash hips.  Add water and boil until fruit is soft. Put through a fine
sieve to remove seeds.  Measure and heat liquid; when simmering add an
equal amount of sugar and boil quickly to jelly stage. Pour into hot
sterilized glasses and seal when cool.
