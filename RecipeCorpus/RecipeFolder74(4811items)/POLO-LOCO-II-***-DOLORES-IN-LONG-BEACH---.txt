Title: POLO LOCO II *** DOLORES IN LONG BEACH  -
Categories: Ethnic, Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  White vinegar
1 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  White wine ; or vermouth
1 x  Garlic; to taste
1 ts Salt
1 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Tabasco
1 tb Parsley ; chopped or cilant
2 ea Chickens; halved
Combine ingredients, marinate chicken at least one hour. (To me, one
clove = why bother?) If you like spicy, I have one more for you, also
from the Times and tasty! FROM: DOLORES GILLETTE (SRDS42A)
