Title: REUBEN MEATLOAF
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Ground beef, crumbled
1 c  Breadcrumbs; soft, preferab
1/2 c  Thousand island dressing
1    Egg
1/2 ts Caraway seed (optional)
Pepper
Filling:
1 c  Sauerkraut; 8 oz
1 oz Corned beef (from can), chop
1/2 c  Cheese; swiss, shredded
Approx. Cook Time:
Fat grams    per serving:
:20 Combine all meat mixture ingredients in medium bowl. Press 1/2 of
mixture in bottom of loaf pan. Make slight indentation, lengthwise
down centre of meat loaf. Combine all filling mixture and put in
indentation. Top with remaining meat mixture, pressing to enclose
filling and sealing edges. Cook at HIGH (100%) 13-18 minutes or till
firm and temperature is 145F. Let stand covered for 5 minutes.
