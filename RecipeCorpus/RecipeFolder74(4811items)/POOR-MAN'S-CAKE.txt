Title: POOR MAN'S CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Sugar
2 c  Raisins
1 c  Lard *
1/4 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Cloves
1 x  Pinch Of Salt
1 ts Cinnamon (Or To Taste)
*    The original recipe calls for Lard.  I don't know if shortening
will
work or not.
~------------------------------------------------------
~------------------ Boil together all ingredients. Add water just
enough water to cover raisins.  Boil until raisins are tender. Cool.
Add 1 t baking soda and enough Unbleached flour to make a stiff
batter. Pour batter into 9-inch round cake pan.  Bake in moderate
oven until cake springs back when touched lightly (about 1 hour at
350 degrees F). Serve warm or cold with cream, custard sauce or
powdered sugar frosting. Walnuts may be added to batter just before
baking.
