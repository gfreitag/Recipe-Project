Title: RICE PUDDING A LA MARGE CLARK
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Rice
1 qt Milk
1/4 c  Sugar
Cook milk and rice for about 35 minutes, just below boiling. Watch
careful during the last 15 minutes, to be sure that it doesn't boil
over. 5 minutes before completion, add the sugar, stirring constantly.

Pour into a pie pan, and top with cinnamon. Serve warm or cold. Makes
1 pan.

Rice Pudding a la Susan Barrington

GRANDMA'S RICE PUDDING

Use 1 egg per cup of milk and 1 tablespoon sugar or honey per cup of
milk. Add 1/2 cup raisins per egg. Stir in 1/2 tsp vanilla. Add 1/3
cup cooked rice per cup of milk. Mix well and sprinkle with nutmeg.
Set pan in hot water.  Bake at 325 degrees for 45 - 60 minutes.

This is incredibly easy and ALWAYS turns out!
