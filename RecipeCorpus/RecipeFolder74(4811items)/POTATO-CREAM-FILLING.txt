Title: POTATO CREAM FILLING
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Potatoes, mashed
1/4 c  Cream cheese
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Sour cream
1 ts Orange rind, grated
3    Egg yolks
1 ts Lemon rind, grated
1 ts Vanilla
Cream butter until soft.  Beat in mashed potatoes and cream cheese.
Stir in remaining ingredients.
