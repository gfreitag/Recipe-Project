Title: RICE PILAF ( MW )
Categories: Microwave, Rice, Mushrooms
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Sliced Fresh Mushrooms
1    Green Onion, Sliced
1 1/2 ts Butter Or Margarine
2/3 c  Water
3 tb Long Grain Regular Rice
1/8    Med Bell Pepper *
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Dried Sage, Crushed
1 ts Snipped Parsley
*  Bell pepper may be any color and should be cut into 1-inch Julienne
Strips.

In a 1-quart casserole micro-cook mushrooms, onion, and butter or
margarine, uncovered, on 100% power for 1 to 2 minutes or till
vegetables are tender.  Stir in water, rice, bell pepper, salt and
sage. Micro-cook, covered, on 100% power for 2 to 3 minutes or till
boiling. Micro-cook, covered, on 50% power for 12 to 15 minutes or
till rice is tender and liquid is absorbed, stirring once. Sir in
parsley. Let stand, covered, for
5    minutes.  Serve hot.
