Title: PULITZER PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
2 cn Pie filling (my family
-prefers peach or cherry)
1    Double yellow cake mix
-(butter in mix works best)
1    Stick butter or margarine
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1/2 c  Shredded cocoanut
serves 6
Pulitzer Pudding

Place  pie  filling  in  9 x 12  baking  dish,  spread evenly.
Sprinkly cake mix (right out of the box) evenly over filling. Take
pats of butter or margarine and place evenly over cake mix. Sprinkle
with chopped pecans. Bake at 375 for approx.  30 minutes. As mixture
starts to brown on top, sprinkle  cocoanut over it and bake until
cocaonut just starts to brown. Let  cool for 20 min. and ENJOY!!
