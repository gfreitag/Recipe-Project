Title: QUICK CHERRY TOMATOES
Categories: Prevention, Vegetable
2 pt cherry tomatoes
Yield: 8 Servings
2 ts reduced-calorie margarine
2 TB fresh basil

In a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, saute tomatoes in melted
margarine just until heated through, about 3 minutes. Sprinkle with
basil. Cook and stir until basil is wilted, about 1 minute. Serve hot.

Per serving
22    calories, 0.8 g fat (27%)

Variation ^ Use a combination of basil, parsley, oregano and thyme,
or dill and parsley instead of all basil. (c) 1996 Rodale Press
