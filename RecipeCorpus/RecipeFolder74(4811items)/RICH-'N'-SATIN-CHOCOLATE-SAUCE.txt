Title: RICH 'N' SATIN CHOCOLATE SAUCE
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  CHIPITS Semi-Sweet Chocolate
1 x  -chips
1/2 c  Corn Syrup
1/4 c  Milk
2 tb Butter
Combine chocolate chips, corn syrup, and milk in a saucepan. Bring to
a boil over medium heat and boil 1 minute, stirring constantly.
Remove and stir in butter; blend well. Serve with pie or pour into
sterilized jars and seal for gift giving. Stores for up to 1 month in
the refrigerator.
