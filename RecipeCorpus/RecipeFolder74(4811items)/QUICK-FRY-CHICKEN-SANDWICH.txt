Title: QUICK FRY CHICKEN SANDWICH
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 ts EACH dill weed & salt
Dash of pepper & garlic pwdr
4    Chicken breasts*
2/3 c  Sour cream OR mayonnaise
3 oz Cream cheese
1/2 ts Dill weed
1 tb Lemon juice
4    Sandwich rolls
4    Lettuce leaves
8    Slices tomato
This is a speed-scratch recipe *Use boneless, skinless chicken breast
helves, pounded thin.

Melt butter in a skillet; stir in the 1/4 tsp dill and salt along
with the pepper and garlic; add chicken and cook until browned on
both sides.

Meanwhile combine sourcream, cheese, 1/2 ts dill and juice. Spread
cheese mixture over bottom half of rolls; top with lettuce, tomato
and cooked chicken. Top with remaining half of roll.
