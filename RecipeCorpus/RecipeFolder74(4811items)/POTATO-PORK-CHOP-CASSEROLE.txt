Title: POTATO PORK CHOP CASSEROLE
Categories: Casserole
Yield: 3 Servings
1 cn Mushroom soup
1 sm Carton of sour cream
1/4 c  Water
1 tb Parsley
Salt and pepper to taste
6    Pork chops
2 c  Potatoes
Mix above ingredients.  Brown pork chops.  Slice 2 cups of potatoes
thin. Alternate layers of mix and potatoes. Salt and pepper to taste.
Cover with pork chops. Bake 1 hour and 15 minutes at 375 degrees
covered. Randy Rigg
