Title: RED DEVIL RUB
Categories: Marinades, Bar-b-q
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-------------------BEEF POULTRY,PORK,FISH------------------------
1/4 c  Dijon mustard with seeds
2 ts Olive oil
1/4 c  Finely slivered fresh basil
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mix thoroughly. Makes about 1/2
cup.
