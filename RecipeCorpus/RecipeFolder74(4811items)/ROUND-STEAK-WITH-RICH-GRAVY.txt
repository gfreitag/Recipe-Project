Title: ROUND STEAK WITH RICH GRAVY
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Crockpot
Yield: 5 Servings
2 lb Round steak
1    (1.5 oz) pkg onion soup mix
1/4 c  Water
1 cn (10.5 oz)condensed cream of
Mushroom soup
Cut steak into 5 or more serving pieces. Place in slow-cooking pot.
Add dry onion soup mix, water and condensed mushroom soup. Cover and
cook on low for 6 to 8 hours. Excellent when served with mashed
potatoes. Source: Crockery Cookery by Mable Hoffman
page 102
:

**NOTE: I used blade steak instead of round steak, works just as
well!**
