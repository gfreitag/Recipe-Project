Title: QUICK ONION CHEESE BREAD
Categories: Breads, Cheese
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
3 tb Sugar
1 c  Cheddar, stale; grated
4    Scallion; chopped
1 1/2 c  Beer
1    Egg; beaten
1 ts Sesame seeds
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Combine flour, baking powder, sugar,
cheese, and scallions.  Stir beer in gently to form a thick, sticky
batter. Pour batter into greased loaf pan, 8-1/2 x 4-1/2 inches.
Brush top of loaf with beaten egg and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
Bake in oven for 1-1/4 hours or until loaf browns. Remove from oven
and cool on a wire rack.
