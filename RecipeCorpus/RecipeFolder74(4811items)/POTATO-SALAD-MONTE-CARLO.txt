Title: POTATO SALAD MONTE CARLO
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Side dish, Misc
Yield: 8 Servings
1/4 ts Garlic powder
3 lb Potatoes
1/3 c  Green onions; minced fine
4    Eggs; hard boiled, sliced
1/2 ts Onion salt
1 ds White pepper; to taste
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Cook the potatoes with the skins on and after cooling, peel and dice.
Mix all the ingredients well and allow to set for at least two hours.
