Title: PORK BARBECUE SANDWICHES
Categories: Pork, Sandwiches
Yield: 5 Servings
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
1 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1 cn Condensed tomato soup
- (10 1/2 oz)
1/4 c  Catsup
2 tb Vinegar
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 c  Cooked pork; thinly sliced
5    Sandwich buns; split
In large skillet, combine all ingredients except pork and sandwich
buns; blend well.  Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer 10 minutes.
Stir in cooked pork; simmer an additional 10 minutes. Serve on
sandwich buns. Yields 4 sandwiches.
Date: 07/02/96
