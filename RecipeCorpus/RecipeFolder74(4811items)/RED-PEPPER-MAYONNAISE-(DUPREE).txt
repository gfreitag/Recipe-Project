Title: RED PEPPER MAYONNAISE (DUPREE)
Categories: Dupree
2 c  mayonnaise
Yield: 1 Servings
2    red bell peppers -- roasted
6 TB fresh basil -- finely
chopped
:
1 TB lemon juice
Salt and pepper
:

In a food processor or blender, puree the mayonnaise, peppers, basil,
and lemon juice. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Makes 2-1/2
cups

: Nathalie Dupree, Well-Stocked Pantry
