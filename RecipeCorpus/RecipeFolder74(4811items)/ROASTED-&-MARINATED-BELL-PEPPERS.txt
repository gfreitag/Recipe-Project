Title: ROASTED & MARINATED BELL PEPPERS
Categories: Vegetables, June 1996 i
Yield: 8 Servings
6 tb Olive oil -- plus more for
Grill
3    Whole yellow bell peppers
3    Whole red bell peppers
2 lg Garlic cloves -- minced
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1 1/4 ts Fresh thyme -- finely
Chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Freshly ground pepper
Brush grill with oil; heat to medium to hot. Grill peppers until
blackened, 8 to 12 minutes. Let peppers cool in a paper bag. Peel and
seed, saving juices. Cut into 1-inch strips. In a bowl, combine
garlic, vinegar, thyme, salt, pepper and any pepper juices. Gradually
whisk in oil. Toss peppers with vinaigrette and arrange on a platter
to serve.
