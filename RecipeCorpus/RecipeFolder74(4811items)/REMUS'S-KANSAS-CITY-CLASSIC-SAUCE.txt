Title: REMUS'S KANSAS CITY CLASSIC SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Mace
14 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Curry powder, oriental pref
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 c  White vinegar
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 c  Ketchup
1/3 c  Dark molasses
Place all of the dry ingredients into a bowl.  Add vinegar and stir.
Add remaining ingredients and stir until mixture is thoroughly
blended. This sauce may be served room temperature or heated. Yield:
2 cups

Source:  From Remus Powers, PhB., Originator of the Diddy-Wa-Diddy
Sauce Contest.
