Title: QUICK FRIED CABBAGE WITH EGG
Categories: Side dishes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Cabbage
1 md Onion
1 tb Peanut oil
1/2 ts Chile powder
Pepper
Salt; if you must
2    Eggs
Recipe by: Piers Thompson <pjt1@SCIGEN.CO.UK> Finely slice the
cabbage and onion.  Heat the oil in a pan and fry the onio until
soft. Add the cabbage, chile powder and seasoning and fry for 3 minu
Cover, reduce heat to low and fry for a further 10 minutes.  Beat the
eggs pour them over the cabbage. Stir until the eggs have set.
