Title: ROOT AWAKENING!
Categories: Dip, Sauce
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ts Ground cumin
1 c  Nonfat yogurt
3 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
Pepper -- to taste
Make a tangy dip for raw vegetables or a sauce for cooked. Combine all
ingredients and serve.

: Steven Raichlen, "Cumin" in Prevention Mag, Sept 1996
