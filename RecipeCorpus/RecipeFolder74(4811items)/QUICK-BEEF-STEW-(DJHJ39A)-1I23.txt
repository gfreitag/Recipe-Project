Title: QUICK BEEF STEW (DJHJ39A) 1I23
Categories: Beef, Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Beef; cubed leftover cooked
2 tb Butter
1 pk Beef stroganoff sauce mix
1 c  Water
1 c  Mixed vegs.-cooked (frozen m
Brown beef in butter. Blend sauce mix with water & add to meat
stirring. Add vegs, stir & heat.
