Title: RICE BATTER BREAD II
Categories: Breads, Quick, No-gluten
Yield: 1 4x8" loaf
2 c  AM Brown Rice Flour
2 tb Non-alum baking powder
1/2 ts Sea salt (optional)
1/4 c  Potato starch or other flour
2    Eggs; beaten (optional)
1 c  Water or soymilk
2 tb AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil
3 tb Honey
Mix dry ingredients and liquid ingredients separately; then combine
both mixtures.  Pour into oiled and floured 4" x 8" loaf pan and bake
45-50 minutes at 350 F. Let cool completely in pan.
