Title: REGENA'S  BUFFALO  WINGS
Categories: Kaz
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lb Chicken wings
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Water
1 tb Soy sauce
1/3 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Catsup
Cut chicken wings into 3 pieces, discarding the tip portion. Bake at
400 degrees for 20 minutes or until brown and crispy. Combine brown
