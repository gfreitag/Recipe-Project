Title: PUFFED WHEAT BALLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 36 Servings
12 c  Puffed wheat cereal
2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 c  Corn syrup
2 tb Butter
1 c  Evaporated milk
1/3 c  Sugar
Place cereal in a large bowl; set aside.  In a heavy saucepan, bring
brown sugar and corn syrup to a boil. Add butter. Combine evaporated
milk and sugar; add to boiling mixture; add to a boiling mixture and
continue cooking until a soft ball forms when liquid is dropped into
cold water (240Ã¸F on a candy thermometer).  Pour over cereal and stir
to coat. Shape into 2-inch balls.
