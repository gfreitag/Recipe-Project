Title: QUICHE CRUSTS
Categories: Breads, Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-------------------------RICE CRUST------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Cooked Brown Rice
2 tb Butter;softened
2 tb Chopped Onion
1/4 ts Basil
1    Egg;slightly beaten
Combine ingredients.  Press into bottom and sides of greased 9" pie
plate. Use any quiche filling.  Tuna tastes especially good in this
crust. PROTEIN:  12.5 grams; CALORIES: 570

These are all from the _Whole Foods For The Whole Family Cookbook_.
