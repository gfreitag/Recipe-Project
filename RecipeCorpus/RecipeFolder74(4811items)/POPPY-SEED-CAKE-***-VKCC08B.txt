Title: POPPY SEED CAKE *** VKCC08B
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1 pk Yellow cake mix
3 oz French vannilla pudding mix
1/3 c  Sweet sherry
1/3 c  Poppy seed
Stick butter
4 x  Eggs
8 oz Sour cream
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
Stick butter
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Sherry
1/4 c  Water
Mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl, beat for a full 5 mins.
Pour into a greased and floured tube pan. Bake at 350 for 1 hr. Cool
slightly an then pour glaze on cake in pan. GLAZE: Mix glaze
ingredients together and bring to a boil, boil for a full 3 mins.
Prick top of cake and pour hot glaze over cake. Let cake cool in pan.
Freezes great. 12:05 PM
