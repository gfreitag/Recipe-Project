Title: REFRIGERATOR ICE CREAM
Categories: Desserts, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ea Eggs
2 tb Salt
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Whipping cream
1 1/2 c  Milk; whole or condensed
1 ts Vanilla
Beat egg whites until stiff and add 2 T. sugar. Beat yolks and add
rest of sugar. Add milk to beaten yolks, then beaten whites. Whip
cream, added last. Freeze in refrigerator trays.

Note: This recipe uses uncooked eggs and may pose a health hazard due
to salmonella.

Source: Union County, OH
