Title: RICHEST POUND CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter flavored Crisco
7 ea Eggs
3 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
Blend sugar and Crisco.  Add eggs, one at a time. Keep beating while
adding flour.  Mix in vanilla. Pour into greased and floured tube
pan. Cook about one hour at 250-300 degrees.
