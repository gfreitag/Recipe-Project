Title: PRUNE ICE-CREAM
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Prunes
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
3 tb Sweetened condensed milk
9 tb Water
Few grains salt
Wash prunes.  Cover with cold water.  Heat to boiling. Simmer until
soft. Rub through sieve.  There should be 1 cup prune pulp. Combine
prune pulp, milk, water, sugar, lemon juice, and salt. Pour into
freezer. Freeze. 6 servings. I.M., Greenfield, IN.
