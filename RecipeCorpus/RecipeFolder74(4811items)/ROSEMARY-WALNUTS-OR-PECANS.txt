Title: ROSEMARY WALNUTS OR PECANS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 2 Cups
2 T. oil
2 C. walnut or pecan halves
1 T. rosemary (fresh, chopped)  1 1/2 tsp. Kosher salt

Preheat oven to 350° and heat oil on small, flat pan for 2 minutes.
Toss nuts with the salt and rosemary. Pour on pan with heated oil.
Shake pan to coat nuts. Bake 5 minutes. Shake pan and bake another 5
minutes. Cool slightly before serving. Store in airtight container
and reheat in 300° oven until warm.
