Title: POPCORN ITALIANO
Categories: Appetizers, Italian
Yield: 8 Servings
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 sm Clove garlic,minced
1/2 ts Dried oregano leaves,crushed
8 c  Hot popcorn
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
In 1 1/2 quart sauce pan over medium high heat,in hot butter, cook
garlic with oregano.In a large bowl,drizzle butter mixture over
popcorn;toss with cheese.Makes 8 cups.
