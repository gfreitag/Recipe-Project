Title: QUICKIE VEGETABLE SOUP
Categories: Soups, Low-cal
Yield: 6 Servings
12 oz Can v-8 juice
1 c  Water
1/2    Chopped onion
2    Ribs celery
1    Sliced carrot
16 oz Can green beans
Any other vegt. you like.
Combine and simmer for about one hour.
