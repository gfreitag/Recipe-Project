Title: PORK & CABBAGE SANDWICHES
Categories: Pork, Cabbage, Sour cream
Yield: 10 Servings
1 lb Pork sausage, bulk
7 c  Cabbage, shredded
1 md Onion, chopped
1 md Pepper, sweet red, Thin Slic
1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar
1/4 c  Sour cream
Hamburger buns or Pieta
Bread.
In a large skillet, brown sausage, drain. Add cabbage, onion, red
pepper, water, salt and sugar; mix well. Cover and cook for 20
minutes or until the vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally.
Stir in sour cream and heat through. Serve on buns or pita bread.
