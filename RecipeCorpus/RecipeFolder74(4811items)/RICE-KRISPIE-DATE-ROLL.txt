Title: RICE KRISPIE DATE ROLL
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 5 Servings
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 c  Finely cut pitted dates
1 c  Granulated sugar
2 ea Eggs (beaten)
3 c  Rice Krispies
1 x  Powdered Sugar
Melt butter or margarine in skillet over LOW heat. then add dates,
sugar and eggs. Cook over low heat stirring constantly until mixture
forms a ball.  (Goes together in pan.) Cool - then stir in 3 cups
rice krispies. Spread powdered sugar on wax paper and make into
rolls. Refrigerate and slice as needed.
