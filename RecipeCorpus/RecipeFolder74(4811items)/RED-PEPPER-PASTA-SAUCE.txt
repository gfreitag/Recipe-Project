Title: RED PEPPER PASTA SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1    7 oz. jar
Drained
1 c  Ricotta
2 tb Fresh parsley
3    Garlic
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Kosher salt
1/8    To 1/4 t
2 ts Olive oil
Roasted red peppers --
Hot pepper flakes
Proces garlic, oil and lemon juice for 20 sec. Add rest of
ingredients and process till it reaches desired consistency, Serve
over fettucini or spread some on sliced semolina bread and broil.
