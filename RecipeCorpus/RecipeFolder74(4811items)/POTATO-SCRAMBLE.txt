Title: POTATO SCRAMBLE
Categories: Breakfast, Casseroles, Main dish, Mcdougall p
Yield: 2 Servings
1 md Yellow onion; chopped course
1    Carrot; scrubbed & sliced
1/4 c  Water
2    Potato; scrubbed & sliced
1/4 lb Fresh mushrooms; cleaned & s
1/2 tb Soy sauce, low sodium; optio
Preparation Time: 0:40 Cook the onions and carrots in the water for 5
minutes. Add the potatoes and mix well. Cover the pan to steam. Stir
occasionally. Add more water if necessary to prevent sticking. Steam
15 minutes, then add the mushrooms and soy sauce. Cook another 10
minutes. Serve hot.
