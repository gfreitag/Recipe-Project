Title: RED KIDNEY BEAN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Side dish
2 c  kidney beans, canned
Yield: 4 Servings
4 TB red wine vinegar
3 TB olive oil
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Salt
2 ds Pepper
4 TB Celery -- diced
2 TB Onion -- chopped

Drain kidney beans, combine beans with a mixture of wine vinear,
olive oil, oregano, salt and pepper. Blend in celery and onion. Chill
in refrigerator, serve in crisp lettuce cups.
