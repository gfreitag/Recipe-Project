Title: POPCORN - OZARK STYLE
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  OIL
3/4 c  POPCORN
HEAT A DEEP POT, WITH A LID, UNTIL WARM. ADD COOKING OIL TO COVER THE
BOTTOM OF THE POT. HEAT UNTIL SMOKING HOT. ADD POPCORN AND COVER,
SHAKING UNTIL CORN IS POPPED. REMOVE FROM POT AS SOON AS POPPING
STOPS.
