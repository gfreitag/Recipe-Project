Title: RICE~ CORN & CHEESE CASSEROLE
Categories: Cheese, Rice, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Cooked Rice -- Brown or
White
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Chili Powder
2 c  Corn Kernels
1/2 ts Black Pepper -- Ground
1 sm Onion -- Chopped Fine
Paprika
2 c  Sharp Cheddar -- Grated
1 1/2 c  Whole Milk
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Combine all the ingredients except
the paprika in a large mixing bowl. Blend thoroughly. Pour into a
well greased (2 quart) casserole dish. Sprinkle with paprika. Bake 40
to 45 minutes. Serve hot
