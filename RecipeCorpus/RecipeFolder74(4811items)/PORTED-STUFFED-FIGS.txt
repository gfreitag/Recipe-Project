Title: PORTED STUFFED FIGS
Categories: Fruits, Desserts, Appetizers
Yield: 24 Servings
2 c  California dried figs
-- (about 24 figs)
1/2 c  Port (or sherry)
1/4 c  Ricotta cheese
Whole natural almonds
-- toasted
Cover figs with water in a saucepan.  Bring to boil; cover and simmer
for 20 minutes.  Drain; place figs in small bowl along with port.
Cover and chill overnight. Drain. Remove stems; slice figs partially
through in an X pattern.  Fill with small spoonfuls of ricotta
cheese. Top with a whole almond, pressed halfway into cheese.
