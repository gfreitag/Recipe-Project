Title: PUNCH BY CAJUN BOB'S "MARDI GRAS MADNESS"
Categories: New import
Yield: 4 Servings
Block of ice)
1    (40 oz) bottle grape juice
1    (48-oz) can unsweetened
Pineapple juice
1    (2-liter) bottle lemon-lime
Soft drink
1    Fifth of vodka
2    Oranges or lemons
2    Limes
Place ice ring in empty punch bowl. Add liquids in order listed. For
decoration: Slices of oranges and limes can be floated on top of the
punch.

Makes about 65 (1/2-cup) servings.

Adapted and Typos by Bobbie Beers From The Dallas Morning News
