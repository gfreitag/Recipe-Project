Title: ROCKY ROAD PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 c  Cold half and half or light
1/3 c  Bakers real semi-sweet choco
1 ea Jello chocolate instant pudd
1 x  & pie filling (4 serving siz
1/3 c  Miniature marshmallows
1/3 c  Chopped nuts
3 1/2 c  Cool Whip whipped topping
1 ea Graham cracker pie crust
Pour half and half into large bowl.Add pie filling mix.Beat with
wire whisk until well blended,about 1 minute.Let stand 5 minutes.
Fold in whipped topping,chocolate chips,marshmallows and
nuts.Spoon into pie crust.Freeze until firm,about 6 hours or
overnight.
Remove from freezer and let stand 10 minutes to soften before
serving. Store leftover pie in freezer.
