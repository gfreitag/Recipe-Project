Title: QUICK TOMATO SALSA
Categories: Htbw
3    plum tomatoes -- chopped
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2    red onion -- chopped
1    jalapeno -- seeded and
chopped
:
3 TB cilantro -- chopped
Juice of one lime
:
1 TB olive oil
Salt and pepper
:

In a medium bowl combine tomatoes, onion, jalapeno and cilantro. Stir
in lime juice and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.

Yield: 1 1/2 cups
