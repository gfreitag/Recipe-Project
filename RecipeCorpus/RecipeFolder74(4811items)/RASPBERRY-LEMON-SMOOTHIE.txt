Title: RASPBERRY LEMON SMOOTHIE
Categories: Beverages, Tea
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Boiling water
8    Lemon herbal tea bags
2 c  Pineapple juice
1 pt Raspberry sherbet
Lemon slices (opt.)
In a teapot, pour boiling water over tea bags; cover and steep for 5
minutes.  Remove bags.  Chill tea.  In a blender, process the tea,
pineapple juice and sherbet until smooth. Pour into glasses. Garnish
with lemon if desired. Source:  Taste of Home mag, June/July 1996
