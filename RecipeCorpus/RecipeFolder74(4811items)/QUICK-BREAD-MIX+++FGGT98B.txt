Title: QUICK BREAD MIX+++FGGT98B
Categories: Quick, Breads, Basic, Southern, Living
Yield: 1 Servings
10 c  All-purpose flour
1/3 c  Baking powder
1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Salt
1 ts Salt
2 c  Shortening
Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl; mix well. Divide flour
mixture and shortening in half. Cut 1 cup shortening into half of
flour mixture with a pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse
meal. Repeat with remaining shortening and flour mixture. Combine
mixtures. Store in an airtight container at room temperature up to 6
weeks. Refrigerate or freeze for longer storage. Yield:  13 cups
