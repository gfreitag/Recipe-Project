Title: PRUNE COFFEE CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Prunes
2 c  Flour
3/4 c  Sugar
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 ea Egg
1/2 c  Milk
1/3 c  Butter or shortening
1 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Brown sugar
3 tb Butter, melted
Soak prunes in just enough warm water to cover; set aside. Mix and
sift all dry ingredients.  In a separate bowl, beat egg until light
and fluffy. Stir in milk and melted butter.  Gradually add this
liquid to flour mixture and combine thoroughly.  Turn into lightly
oiled 8-inch square baking dish. Cut soaked prunes into pieces and
press down into dough. Combine cinnamon, brown sugar and melted
butter. Spread over prunes. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes.
Serves 6
