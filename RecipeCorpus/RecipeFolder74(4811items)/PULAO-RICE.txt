Title: PULAO RICE
Categories: Indian, Rice
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/3 c  Long Grain Rice
1/4 c  Ghee Or Butter
2 md Onion, Sliced
1    Clove Garlic, Crushed
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Peas
2 1/2 c  Hot Water
Clean, wash and soak rice for a half hour.  Heat the ghee and fry the
onions until golden.  Add the drained rice, garlic, salt and peas and
continue to cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Gradually add
the hot water, blending thoroughly, and bring to a boil. Simmer,
covered, for 20 to 25 minutes or until all the liquid has been
absorbed and the rice is tender.

NOTE:  Any of the following can be added:  Cloves, Cinnamon, Caraway
Seeds, Tumeric, Ginger, Red Pepper, Coriander, Parsley, Garam Masala
or Cumin.

From How To Make Good Curries by Helen Lawson Copyright 1973
