Title: REAL CORNBREAD
Categories: Side dish, Breads
2 c  Yellow cornmeal
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Flour
3 TB Vegetable oil
2 ea Egg
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 TB Sugar
1 TB Baking powder

Measure dry ingredients and stir.  Add milk, mix well. Add eggs, beat
>> thoroughly.  Stir in oil, beat aagain until well blended. Melt
Crisco in heated cast iron skillet (mine measures about 9 inches
across the bottom). Pour the batter into the hot pan. Bake in a 400
degree F. oven about 40 minutes, until top is lightly brown. ANN
PUCKETT (HGPS48A)
