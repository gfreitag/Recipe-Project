Title: RAINBOW TROUT WITH GREEN ONION - GINGER MAYON
Categories: Fish
Yield: 2 Servings
4    Rainbow Trout (4 oz. each)
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
1/4 c  Lime Juice
1 tb Oriental Sesame Oil
Mayonnaise:
3 tb Regular or Light Mayonnaise
5 ts Green Onions, thinly sliced
1 ts Ginger
-- peeled & finely minced
1 ts Fresh Lemon Juice
1/2 ts Soy Sauce
1/8 ts Oriental Sesame Oil
Marinate trout. Combine mayonnaise ingredients; refrigerate. Cook
trout. Serve immediately with mayonnaise. Makes 2 to 4 servings.
