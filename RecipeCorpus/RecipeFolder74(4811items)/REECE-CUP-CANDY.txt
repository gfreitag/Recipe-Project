Title: REECE CUP CANDY
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Margarine; melted
2 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/2    Margarine stick
6 oz Chocolate chips
Mix candy ingredients and press into pan. TOPPING: Melt butter over
low heat. Add chocolate chips. Pour over peanut butter mixture. Chill
and cut into squares.
