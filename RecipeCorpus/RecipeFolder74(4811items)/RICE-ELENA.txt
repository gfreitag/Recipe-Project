Title: RICE ELENA
Categories: Casseroles, Cheese, Side dishes, Vegetables
Yield: 12 Servings
4 c  Rice; Cooked
2 c  Dairy Sour Cream
8 oz Green Chilies; Chopped, 2 Cn
1 lb Monterey Jack; Grated
1 x  Salt; To Taste
1/4 c  Cheddar; Sharp, Grated
Butter a 2-quart casserole.  Mix all of the ingredients, except the
cheddar cheese, together. Pour into the casserole and sprinkle the
cheddar cheese on top of the mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees F. for 30
minutes. Serve hot.
