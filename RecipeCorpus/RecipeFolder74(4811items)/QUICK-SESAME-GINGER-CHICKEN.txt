Title: QUICK SESAME GINGER CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Low-cal, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 tb Sesame seeds, toasted
1 tb Grated fresh gingerroot
3 tb Low sodium soy sauce
3 tb Honey
6    4 oz. skinned, boned chickes
Veg. cooking oil
Combine first 4 ingredients.

Place chicken between 2 sheets of heavy duty plastic wrap and flatten
to 1/4" thickness, using meat mallet or rolling pin. Brush half of
soy sauce mixture over chicken, coating both sides.

Coat grill rack with cooking spray, place on grill over med-hot coals.
Place chicken on rack, grill, covered, 8 to 10 minutes or until
chicken is done, turning and basting frequently with remaining soy
sauce mixture

187 calories, 4.3g fat, 70mg cholestrol
