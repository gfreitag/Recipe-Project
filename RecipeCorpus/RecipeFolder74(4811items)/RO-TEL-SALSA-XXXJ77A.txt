Title: RO-TEL SALSA XXXJ77A
Categories: Sauces, Tex-mex
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Ro-Tel Tomatoes w/Chilies
2 ea Green Onions; chopped fine
1 ea Garlic Clove; minced
1 ea Large Tomato; chopped
1/2 ea Lime; juice only
1/2 ts Salt
Mix and enjoy.  To increase heat, add canned or fresh jalapenos to
taste. This recipe came from the back of a can of Ro-Tel Tomatoes
w/Green Chilies.
