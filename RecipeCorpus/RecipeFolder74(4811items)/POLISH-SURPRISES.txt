Title: POLISH SURPRISES
Categories: Meats, Cheese, Appetizers, Sandwiches
Yield: 60 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 lb Hot sausage
3/4 lb American or velveeta cheese
1/4 lb Mozzarella cheese
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
Party rye slices (2 loaves)
Brown meats together. Drain well, wiping excess grease from skillet.
Add cheese and sauce to meat. Stir over low heat till cheese has
melted. Spread on party rye bread slices. Put on cookie sheet and
bake at 400~ for about 8-10 minutes, till heated through.
