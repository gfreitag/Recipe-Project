Title: RASPBERRY CORDIAL
Categories: Fruits, Alcohol
Yield: 1 1/2 quarts
2 pt Ripe raspberries; picked
-over
2 c  Granulated sugar
1 qt Vodka
Place sugar in a 3 quart glass jar with a lid. Add the raspberries and
vodka; stir and cover. Place in a dark, cool place. Each week for
about 2 months, open the jar and stir the cordial. Strain the
finished cordial through a very fine sieve into a decanter. Its color
is vibrant. Makes 1 1/2 quarts. Posted by Sarah Gruenwald 1/97
