Title: PULLED PORK TACOS WITH TOMATILLO SALSA
Categories: New, Text, Import
4    red bliss potatoes, cooked
and -- slice 1/4 inch thick
Yield: 4 Servings
:
1 lb pulled BBQ pork
1 c  grated Monterey Jack cheese
8    white corn tortillas
Salt and freshly ground
pepper
Olive oil
:
:
:

Prepare a wood or charcoal fire and let it burn down to embers.

For each serving, lay 1 1/2 ounces of pork over half of the tortilla,
top with potato slices, 1/4 cup of cheese and season to taste with
salt and pepper. Fold over the tortilla and brush with olive oil.
Grill for 3 minutes on each side, or until crispy and cheese has
melted. Serve with Tomatillo Salsa.

Yield: 8 servings
