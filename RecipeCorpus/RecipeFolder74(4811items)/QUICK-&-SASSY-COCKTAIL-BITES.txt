Title: QUICK & SASSY COCKTAIL BITES
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 8 Servings
1    1.25 oz pkg chili seasoning
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Catsup
1 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Cider vinegar
1 ts Yellow mustard
1 lb Smoked sausage; sliced and h
Recipe by: McCormick Schilling Spice Co. Combine the first 6 sauce
ingredients in a 2-quart saucepan. Bring to a boil, t urn down heat
and simmer for 15 minutes. Add the cubed smoked sausage and cook on
medium for another 15 minutes, stirring a few times. Serve in a
chafing dish or from a small platter with toothpicks.

NOTE:  If suace does not thicken, add a bit of cornstarch.
