Title: POULET AU MIEL
Categories: Oven, Baked
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Prepared mustard
1/4 c  Melted butter
2 ts Curry powder
4    Chicken breast
Mix honey, mustard, melted butter and curry powder. Remove skin from
chicken breasts. Pour sauce over breasts and cook for 1hour to 11/4
hours. Baste often.
