Title: POODLE TOAST
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 4 Servings
3 tb Margarine
3 tb Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Canned tomatoes, drained
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Scalded milk
3    Weiners cut into 1/4 inch
-pieces
6 sl Toast
Melt margarine, then blend in flour and salt. Add tomatoes, soda, and
milk. Cook 10 minutes, stirring as needed. Add wiener rounds. When
hot, serve over hot toast.

Makes 4 servings.
