Title: POTATOES~ TOMATOES & ONIONS
Categories: Microwave, Low-fat, Potatoes, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lg Onions -- peeled, sliced and
1    Clove garlic -- crushed
3 md Potatoes -- peeled & sliced
(1/4
16 oz Tomatoes, canned -- sliced
Put onion rings and garlic in microwave safe bowl with a little water
microwave on high 7 minutes.  Add potatoes and tomatoes (partially
drained) and microwave for app. 21 additional minutes, or until most
of liquid is absorbed, stir every 7 minutes.
