Title: RAMEN PASTA SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
3 pk Ramen noodles, uncooked
3 c  Finely shredded cabbage
1/2 c  Thinly sliced celery
1/2 c  Finely shredded Cheddar
1/2 c  Diced carrot
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb White vinegar
2 ts Sugar
2 ts Seasoned salt
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts White pepper
Paprika
Prepare Ramen noodles according to package directions but do not add
spice packets; drain. In large bowl combine noodles, cabbage, celery,
cheese, and carrot. In small bowl blend oil, vinegar, sugar, seasoned
salt, dry mustard, and pepper. Add to noodle mixture; toss to coat.
Chill thoroughly. Toss gently and sprinkle with paprika before
serving.
