Title: PRIZE FRUIT CHILI SAUCE
Categories: Pickles, Sauces.
Yield: 1 Serving
30    Large tomatoes
6    Onions
6    Pears
6    Peaches
3    Sweet red peppers
3    Green peppers
6 c  Sugar
1 lg Bunch celery
2 tb Salt
1 qt Vinegar
1 sm Tin tomatoe paste
1/2 c  Pickling spices (tied in
-a cheesecloth bag.)
Chop all vegetables, mix together all remaining ingredients, add and
simmer slowly until thick, about 2 hours. Remove pickling spices and
discard. Store in sterilized jars.
