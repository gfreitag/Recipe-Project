Title: RICH APPLE BUTTER
Categories: Condiments, Jams
Yield: 10 Servings
4 lb Cooking apples
2 c  Cider
3 c  Granulated sugar
2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground cloves
1/8 ts Ground allspice
Servings: 10

Stem and quarter apples; do not peel.  Cook apples and cider,
covered, in crockpot on LOW for 10 hours.  Add sugar and spices and
continue cooking for 1 hour. Pour into hot sterilized jars. Store in
a cool dry place. Or, pour into freezer containers and freeze. Makes
ten
6 oz jars.
