Title: QUICK LOW-FAT BURRITOS
Categories: Low-fat, Quick, Easy, Mexican, Jw
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Tortilla
1/4 c  Beans; canned
1/4 c  Rice; cooked
Cilantro pesto or
Salsa
Lettuce; shredded
Monterey Jack; or other
Cheese- reduced fat variety
Top tortilla with beans and rice; warm in the microwave. Top with
cilantro pesto or salsa, shredded lettuce and a little grated low-fat
cheese. Roll up and serve.
