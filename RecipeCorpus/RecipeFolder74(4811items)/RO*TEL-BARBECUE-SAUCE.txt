Title: RO*TEL BARBECUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 2 Cups
1 cn RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and
-Green Chilies (10 oz)
1/2 c  Ketchup
2 tb Brown sugar
1/4 c  Diced onion
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
In a saucepan combine all ingredients. Heat to a boil; reduce heat.
Stirring occasionally, simmer 30 minutes or to desired consistency.
Brush on ribs or chicken during the last 10-20 minutes of grilling or
roasting. Makes 1 1/3 cups sauce.
