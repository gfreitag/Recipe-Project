Title: RICE FLOUR PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Indian
Yield: 1 Servings
4 1/2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Sugar
2 oz Rice flour
dr Rose Water
1 oz Almonds
1/2 oz Pistachio nuts
1. Blanch (opt) and shred nuts. 2. Mix rice flour into the milk and
mix till smooth. 3. Cook over medium heat till of creamy consistency
(20-30 minute.??) 4. Simmer and add sugar and stir for 2-3 minutes
more. 5. Cool (in refrigerator 30 minute) add the rose water, almonds
and pistachios (maybe before it cools). 6. Pour into individual
dishes and serve.
