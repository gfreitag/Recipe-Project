Title: ROSEMARY SHORTBREAD COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 15 Servings
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Rice Flour
3/4 lb Butter
2/3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Fresh Rosemarry, Chopped
Preheat oven to 350. Combine ingredients. Roll out on a floured board
to about 3/8" thick. Cut out cookies. Bake on greased cookie sheet 20
minutes.
