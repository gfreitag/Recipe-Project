Title: RIGATONI A LA VODKA***
Categories: Italian, Pasta, Mrs. g, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
- G Granaroli XBRG76A
-MM:MK VMXV03A
1 1/2 c  Plain tomato sauce
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 lb Bacon diced
1/3 c  Vodka
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1    Packet bouillion
Salt and pepper to taste
1 lb Rigatoni
Grated cheese(parmagiana)
Lightly sautee bacon. Drain except for 2 tbls drippings. Add butter
and simmer on low heat for 3 min. Add vodka and tomato sauce and
simmer 5 min. Add cream. salt and pepper and keep warm(never cook
with cream on high heat-it will separate) Cook macaroni according to
directions. Drain well, mix with sauce, top with cheese and serve.
