Title: RATATOUILLE BREAD ABM
Categories: Breads
Yield: 24 Ounces
MMMMM-----------------BEST BREAD MACHINE CB EVER----------------------
1/3 c  Summer squash, chopped
1/3 c  Zucchini, chopped
1/3 c  Bell peppers, chopped
3 tb Olive oil
2    Plum tomatoes
2    Garlic cloves, crushed
2 1/4 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1/3 c  Wheat bran
1/2 tb Sugar
1/2 tb Salt
1 1/2 tb Chopped fresh basil
3/4 c  Water; warm
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order. Set "baking control" at 10 o'clock. Select "white bread" and
push Start.

In hot & humid weather, use 1/8 c less water.

This bread makes a wonderful cold steak or grilled lamb sandwich.
