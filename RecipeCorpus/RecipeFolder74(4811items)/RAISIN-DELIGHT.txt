Title: RAISIN DELIGHT
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Serving
1 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Raisins
2 ts Vanilla
1 c  White sugar
2 c  Flour
1 tb Butter
4 c  Boiling water
1/4 c  Butter
4 ts Baking powder
1 c  Milk
Preheat oven 350 F. Combine brown sugar, 1 tablesbpoon butter,
raisins, boiling water and boil together for 5 minutes. Remove from
heat and stir in vanilla; set aside. Cream butter and sugar. Sift
flour and baking powder together and add alternately with milk to
into a greased 9 x 13 in pan.

dessert may be served plain, or with whipped cream or vanilla ice
