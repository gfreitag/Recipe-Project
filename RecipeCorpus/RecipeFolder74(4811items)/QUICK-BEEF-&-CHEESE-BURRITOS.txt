Title: QUICK BEEF & CHEESE BURRITOS
Categories: Meats, Cols.l&e
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 1/4 c  Chunky salsa
8 oz Monterey jack cheese, cubed
8    Flour tortillas, warmed
Sliced lettuce
Chopped tomato
Sliced black olives
Brown the beef in a skillet over medium-high heat until no longer
pink. Pour off drippings.  Stir in salsa and cheese; heat until
cheese melts.

To serve, divide filling among tortillas.  Fold bottom edge of each
tortilla up over filling; fold sides to center, overlapping edges.
Top with lettuce, tomato and olives.
