Title: RIB EYE EXPRESS STEAK SAUCE #7
Categories: Other sauce, Dips
Yield: 16 Servings
4 c  Catsup
2 c  Water
1 c  Brown sugar
6 tb Onion Powder
1 c  Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Vinegar, cider
4 ts Seasoned salt
3 ts Chili powder 'Hot'
1 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Black pepper, ground
2 ts Red Pepper Sauce
1 ts Ground red peppers
Mix all ingredients except brown sugar.  Simmer until desired
thickness (about 30 minutes).  Add sugar, stirr until smooth mixture.
