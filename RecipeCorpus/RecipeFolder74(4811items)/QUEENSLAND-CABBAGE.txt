Title: QUEENSLAND CABBAGE
Categories: Australia, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Butter
2 tb Oil
4 c  Cabbage, shredded
1 md Onion, sliced
1/2 c  Chicken broth
1/2 ts Thyme
1/4 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1 c  Tomato, chopped and seeded
Salt & Pepper, to taste
1 tb Butter
1 tb Parsley, fresh chopped
Melt butter with oil in large pot.  Add cabbage, onion, broth and
thyme. Simmer, covered 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add
mushrooms, tomatoes, salt & pepper. Stir, cover, simmer until just
tender (approx 10-15 min). Add 1 Tbsp. butter and parsley.
