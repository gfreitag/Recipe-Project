Title: RICE PUDDING (WEDMAN)
Categories: Diabetic, Grains, Desserts
Yield: 6 Sweet ones
1/2 c  White rice;
1/4 c  Water;
2 c  Skim milk;
1/4 c  Sugar;
1 ts Vanilla extract;
1/2 c  Raisins;
Cook the rice in 3/4 c water over medium heat until tender, about 45
minutes.  Add the skim milk and sugar. Cook slowly over low heat until
thickened, about 30 minutes. Cook. Add the vanilla and raisins and
spoon into six serving dishes.

Food Exchanges per serving: 1 STARCH/BREAD EXCHANGE + 1 FRUIT
EXCHANGE + 1/2  MILK EXCHANGE; CAL: 189; PRO: 4g; CAR: 28g; FAT: 2g;
SOD: 283mg;

Source: Quick & Easy Diabetic Menus by Betty Wedman, M.S.,R.D.
