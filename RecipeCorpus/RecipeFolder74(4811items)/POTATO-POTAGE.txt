Title: POTATO POTAGE
Categories: Potatoes, Soups
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Celery -- sliced
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
2 tb Margarine
1    8 oz package  cream cheese
Cubed
1 c  Milk
2 c  Potato -- chopped and
Cooked
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Parmesan cheese -- Grated
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Salt
ds Pepper
Saute celery and onion in margarine. Add cream cheese and milk; stir
over low heat until cream cheese is melted. Stir in remaining
ingredients; heat thoroughly.

: Monette Carr, State Fair of Texas Prizewinning Recipes
