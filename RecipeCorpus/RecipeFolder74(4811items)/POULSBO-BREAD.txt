Title: POULSBO BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
From: Gaye Levy ** DTXT63A
1 1/8 c  Water
3/4 c  7 grain mix
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
1/3 c  Flour, whole wheat
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Molasses
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Powdered buttermilk
1 1/2 ts Yeast
1 1/2 tb Margarine or Applesauce
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds, optional
Put all ingredients except sunflower seeds into pan. If using
sunflower seeds, set to "mix" cycle and add add beep.
