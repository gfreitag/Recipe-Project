Title: POTATO PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Pie crust
1 c  Potatoes, cooked
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ginger
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cloves
3    Eggs, slightly beaten
1 c  Milk
Mix all ingredients with mixer.  Pour into unbaked pie shell and bake
until light brown at 325 degrees. Randy Rigg
