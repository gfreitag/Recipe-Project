Title: RICE WITH GARLIC & HERBS
Categories: Appetizers, Rice
Yield: 4 Servings
240 g  Long-Grain Rice -- (3 dl.)
5    Deciliters Water
1 ts Thyme
1 tb Oregano
1/4 ts Salt
1 dl Fresh Chives -- finely
Chopped
2    Cloves Garlic -- crushed or
Minced
Rinse rice and discard water. Bring rice, water, thyme, oregano and
salt to a boil. Let boil covered for 40-25 minutes at low heat. Add
chives and garlic before serving.
