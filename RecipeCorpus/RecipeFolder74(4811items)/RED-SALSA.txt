Title: RED SALSA
Categories: None
Yield: 3 Servings
MMMMM------------------INGREDIENTS/PREPARATION-----------------------
Stir 1 cup bottled salsa, 2 cucumbers, peeled, seeded and chopped
fine, and 1 cup finely chopped cherry tomatoes in small bowl. Makes 3
cups. Source: Magic Chef Cookbook CDROM
