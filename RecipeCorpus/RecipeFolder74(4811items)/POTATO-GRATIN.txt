Title: POTATO GRATIN
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
5 md Potatoes, peeled and sliced
4 md Onions, sliced
2 tb Margarine
1 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 ds Nutmeg
1 ts Dried thyme leaves
1/2 c  Whipping cream
Saute onions in margarine. In shallow baking dish arrange potatoes and
onions in layers. Sprinkle with garlic and spices in between layers.
Bake at 350 for 40 minutes. Add whipping cream all over top. Increase
temp to 400 bake for another 10-20 minutes.
