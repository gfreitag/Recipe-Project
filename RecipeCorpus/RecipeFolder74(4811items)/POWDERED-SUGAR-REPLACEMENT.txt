Title: POWDERED SUGAR REPLACEMENT
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
-==========================
-(Yield: 4 cup (1000 mL))
Ingredients:

2 cups    (500 mL) nonfat dry milk powder 2 cups (500 mL) cornstarch
1 cup (250 mL) granulated sugar replacement Combine all ingredients
in food processor or blender. Whip until well blended and powdered.

1/2 nonfat milk or 1/2 bread
Exchange: 1/4 cup (60 mL): 1 bread
Calories 1/4 cup (60 mL): 81 Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.40
