Title: POTATO LATKES
Categories: Ethnic, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
2    Eggs
3 c  Grated, drained potatoes
4 tb Grated onion
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
5 tb Matzoh meal
Oil for frying
Beat the eggs and add the rest of the ingredients. Blend well. Fry in
hot oil, being careful not to use too much oil.

Serve with applesauce, or sour cream.
