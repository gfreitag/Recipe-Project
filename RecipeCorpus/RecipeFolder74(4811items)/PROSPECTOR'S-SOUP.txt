Title: PROSPECTOR'S SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Bacon fat
3 tb Flour
1 qt Of boiling water
half a can of milk onion, optional

Place 2 tbsp bacon fat and the 3 tbsp flour into a saucepan and stir
over a medium fire until the flour is golden brown. Than add 1 quart
of boiling water and half a can of milk, stirring in slowly until
smooth and season with salt and pepper to taste. An onion may be
added to improve the flavor.

Origin: A Taste of Oregon, (Oregon; End of the Trail, 1951) Shared by:
Sharon Stevens
