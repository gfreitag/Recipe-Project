Title: POTAGE FROMAGE (CHEESE SOUP)
Categories: Soups, Canadian
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Onion,chopped
1/2 c  Celery,chopped
1/2 c  Butter
4 tb Flour
2 c  Chicken stock or bouillon
4 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Cottage cheese
Cook vegtables in butter until they are soft.Add Flour.Stir over low
heat for 3 min..Add the chicken stock and milk.Bring to a boil
stirring all the time.Allow to cool and pass the soup in a blender
while adding cheese gradually.Return to the pot to reheat.DO NOT
BOIL.Serve hot.
