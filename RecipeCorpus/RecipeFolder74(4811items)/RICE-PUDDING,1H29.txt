Title: RICE PUDDING/1H29
Categories: Grains, American, Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 qt Milk
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg-well beaten
2 tb Butter; melted
Place the milk, salt, & rice in a saucepan. Bring to a slow boil &
cook uncovered over low heat for 20 mins.
Season w/ nutmeg, vanilla, cinnamon, & sugar. Add the egg & butter &
mix well. Pour into a baking dish and brown lightly under the
broiler, watching carefully, for one minute. Allow to cool and chill
thoroughly before serving.
