Title: QUICK LEMON RICE SOUP
Categories: Soups/stews, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 Servings
1 sm Onion or 3 scallions;
-chopped
3 c  Fresh kale; chopped
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Black pepper
1/8 ts Tumeric
1/3 c  Vegetable broth
3 c  Pre-cooked rice
8 c  Water or vegetable broth
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 c  Nutritional yeast
1 tb Tamari or soy sauce
In a large pot, saute onion, kale, and seasonings in 1/3 c vegetable
broth over medium heat for 5 minutes. Add remaining ingredients and
simmer for 10 minutes longer. Serve warm.

Total calories: 133   Fat: 1 gram
