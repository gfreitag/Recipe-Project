Title: PORK TACOS
Categories: Pork
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn Stewed tomatoes -- (16 oz)
1 cn Tomato sauce -- (8 oz)
1/2 c  Bell peppers -- chopped
1    Clove garlic -- chopped
1 ts Sugar
2 c  Pork -- chopped/cooked
3/4 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
1/2 c  Onions -- chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Olive oil
12    Taco shells
3/4 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
Preheat oven or toaster oven at 300.  Place taco shells in oven to
heat watch carefully could brown quickly . In a skillet, heat oil.
Add undrained tomatoes, tomato sauce, bell peppers, garlic, onion,
and sugar until softened.  Then, combine pork, 3/4 cup cheese and
salt. Spoon about 1/4 cup mixture into each taco shell.
