Title: QUEEN SCONES
Categories: Bakery, Breakfast, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
2 1/4 c  Wholewheat flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 pn Salt
3 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Medium or soft tofu
1/2 c  Water
Combine flour, baingp owder, salt & sugar in a bowl. Mix well. Stir
in the oil & the raisins. Put the tofu & water in a food processor or
a blender & blend well, until smooth.  Pour into the dry mixture &
stir until well combined. Turn out onto a floured board, roll out the
dough & cut into scones. Place on a lightly oiled baking sheet & bake
at 425F for 15 minutes.
