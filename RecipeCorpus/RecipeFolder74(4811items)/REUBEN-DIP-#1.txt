Title: REUBEN DIP #1
Categories: Appetizers, Dips
Yield: 4 Servings
-JUDI M. PHELPS
9 oz Corn beef; cooked
8 oz Swiss cheese; shredded
8 oz Mozzarella cheese; shredded
16 oz Sauerkraut; drained, use
-scissors to cut in small
-pieces
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Thousand Island dressing
Mix ingredients together and cook 15-20 minutes to blend flavors. Use
bagel chips either plain or flavored (garlic are delicious),
pumpernickel or rye bread pieces are excellent too.
