Title: RANCH BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Catsup
1/3 c  Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Salt
1 ds Tabasco sauce
1 c  Water
Combine all ingredients.  Heat to boiling; simmer 30 minutes. If
sauce gets too thick, add small amount of water. Use to baste ribs,
etc.

Source:  Better Homes and Gardens Barbecue Book
