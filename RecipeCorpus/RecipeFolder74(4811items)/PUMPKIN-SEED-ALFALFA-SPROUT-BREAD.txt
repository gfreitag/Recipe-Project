Title: PUMPKIN SEED ALFALFA SPROUT BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 15 Servings
-Barb Day GWHP32A
1 pk Yeast
2 1/2 c  Better for Bread flour
1 c  Wheat flour
2 tb Gluten
1 1/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Instant nonfat dry milk
1 c  Alfalfa sprouts; 11 oz
1/2 c  Pumpkin seeds;packaged/green
-unsalted
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Honey
1 1/2 c  Very warm water
Add all the ingredients in the order listed,select white bread and
push "Start." A hearty wheat bread with extra fiber, nutrients, and
an exciting differentflavor. Shared by Barb Day
