Title: PUMPKIN SPICE
Categories: Seasonings
Yield: 1 Servings
Use 1 tsp for 3 tb total
Use 1/8 tsp for trial amount
4    Ground cinnamon
2    Powdered ginger
2    Grated nutmeg
1    Ground allspice
1    Ground cloves
Combine all ingredients (or those of your choice) and store in an
airtight jar.  Try a little in plain oatmeal cookies, white or raisin
bread.

Source:  Cut Your Bills in Half - Rodale Press Shared by Elizabeth
Rodier Oct 93, tested without nutmeg and allspice
