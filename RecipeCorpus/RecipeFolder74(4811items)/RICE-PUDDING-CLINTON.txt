Title: RICE PUDDING CLINTON
Categories: Pudding, Rice, Dessert
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Rice -- cooked
5    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Butter; or oleo -- melted
2 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Raisins -- optional
Beat eggs; add sugar, butter, milk, vanilla and nutmeg. Add rice and
raisins if desired and pour into a greased 2-quart casserole. Bake at
350 for 45 minutes. Thirty Years at the Governor's Mansion via Dixie
Daniels, Intelec Cooking
