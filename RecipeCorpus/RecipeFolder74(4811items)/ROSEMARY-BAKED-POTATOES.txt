Title: ROSEMARY BAKED POTATOES
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 1 Recipe
2 lb Red potatoes
-- cut into 1 1/2" pieces
1 1/2 tb Butter
1 1/2 tb Canola oil
1 tb Fresh rosemary; OR...
1 ts -dried rosemary
Salt
Freshly ground pepper
Melt butter and blend with oil. Put potatoes in a shallow baking dish
and pour the butter/oil mixture over them, stirring to coat. Sprinkle
with rosemary, salt and pepper (to taste) and bake at 400 degrees
until browned (about an hour), stirring occasionally. If pressed for
time, potatoes can be par-boiled for 5 to 8 minutes and then baked
for 25 to 30 minutes.

Nutritional information per serving: Protein: 4 grams (6%);
Carbohydrates: 46 grams (68%); Fat: 8 grams (26%); Calories: 264;
Sodium: 15 mg.; Cholesterol: 9 mg. Exchanges: 3 bread, 1 fat
