Title: RICE PUDDING (LF)
Categories: Low fat, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Rice
6 c  Skim milk
3/4 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla extract
-cinnamon
-nutmeg
Heat to almost boiling stirring almost constantly. Reduce heat,
cover, and simmer for 1 hour stirring occasionally. Add vanilla,
cinnamon, and nutmeg.
