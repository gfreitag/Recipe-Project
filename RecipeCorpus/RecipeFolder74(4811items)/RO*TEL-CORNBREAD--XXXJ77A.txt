Title: RO*TEL CORNBREAD  XXXJ77A
Categories: Breads, Tex-mex, Quick
Yield: 8 Servings
3/4 c  White cornmeal
3/4 c  Yellow cornmeal
3 tb Flour
1/4 ts Soda
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
2 tb Vegetable shortening; melted
1 c  Buttermilk
1    Egg; beaten
1    Jalapeno; finely chopped
10 oz Ro*tel tomato w/green chili
All ingredients together.  b
degrees for 25 to 30 minutes. This recipe comes from a book "Snake;
Rattle; and RO*TEL."
