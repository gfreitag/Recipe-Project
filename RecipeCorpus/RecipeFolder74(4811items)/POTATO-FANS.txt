Title: POTATO FANS
Categories: Vegetables
3 TB butter
Yield: 4 Servings
1    clove garlic -- minced
2 TB finely chopped parsley
salt & pepper
:
4 md Idaho potatoes

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. In a small sauce pan melt together the
butter, garlic, and parsley. Peel each potato and cut each potato
crosswise into 1/4" slices without slicing all the way through the
potato. Place potatoes in baking dish and drizzle with butter
mixture. Bake for 1 hour or until golden, crispy and the potatoes
have fanned out. Serve hot.
