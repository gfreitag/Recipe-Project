Title: ROSE WINE GLAZE
Categories: Desserts, Cheesecakes, Liquor
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
3/4 c  Apple jelly
1 tb Rose wine
1 ts Sugar
(See Cheesecake Tart w/ Fruit.) (Makes about 3/4 cup.) Heat all
ingredients in heavy small saucepan over low heat, swirling pan
occasionally, until jelly melts and sugar dissolves. Increase heat
and boil until syrupy, about 6 minutes. Use in Cheesecake Tart recipe
as indicated.
