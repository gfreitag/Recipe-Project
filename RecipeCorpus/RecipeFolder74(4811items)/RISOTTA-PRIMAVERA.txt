Title: RISOTTA PRIMAVERA
Categories: Rice, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Butter
1/2 c  Onion, finely chopped
14 oz Can chicken broth, reduced
-sodium,
(or 1 can vegetable broth)
1 c  Arborio rice
1 md Carrot, peeled & finely
Chopped
1/2 c  Sweet red pepper, finely
Chopped
1/2 c  Fresh or frozen green peas
1/3 c  + 2-4 T. water
1 ts Dried basil leaves
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese, grated
1    Clove garlic, finely
Chopped
1/4 ts Black pepper
In a 1-qt microwave-proof dish, microwave butter on high for 20-30
secs until melted.  Stir in onion, cover and microwave for 1 min.

Stir broth & rice into onion mixture; cover. Microwave on high 6 min.
Rotate halfway through.  Stir in carrot & red pepper; cover and
microwave 4 min on high.

Stir peas, 1/2 c. water and basil into rice mixture. Cover and
microwave 4 min. on high.  Stir, cover, and microwave on high 2 more
min.

Stir in parmesan cheese, garlic and pepper.  Cover and set aside for
6-8 min., stirring occasionally, until rice is cooked but slightly
chewy. Stir in an add'l 2-4 T of water for a creamier texture.

: Country Living, May 1995
