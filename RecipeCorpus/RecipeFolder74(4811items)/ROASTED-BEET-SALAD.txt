Title: ROASTED BEET SALAD
Categories: New, Text, Import
2 bn baby beets, -- roasted
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb mesclun or mixed greens
1/2 c  toasted walnuts
1    shallot, -- chopped
3 TB walnut oil
2 ts raspberry vinegar
1/4 lb goat cheese
Salt
Pepper
:
:

Cut off beet tops. In a large serving bowl, toss beet tops with mixed
greens and walnuts. Add beets to salad. In a skillet, saute shallot in
walnut oil. Deglaze with vinegar. Pour this dressing over salad and
toss. Sprinkle top with goat cheese. Season with salt and pepper and
serve immediately.
