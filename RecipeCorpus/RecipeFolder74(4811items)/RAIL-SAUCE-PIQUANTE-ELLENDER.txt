Title: RAIL SAUCE PIQUANTE ELLENDER
Categories: Wildgame, Poultry, Spices
Yield: 1 Servings
12    Rails, dressed and cut up
1    Basic Sauce recipe
8 oz Tomato sauce
8 oz Tomato paste
Thoroughly cook both tomato products with Sen. Ellender's Basic
Sauce. Add rails and cook until tender. Serve with rice or spaghetti.
Source: DIXIE magazine, New Orleans LA Oct 03 1971 Recipe date:
10/03/71
