Title: POSTAMAZOO
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 lb Polskakielbasa
2 cn Kidney beans 16 oz.
1 pk Shell macaroni 8 oz.
Slice sausage and brown; drain fat.  Pour into kidney beans and
simmer. Boil macaroni to desired consistency and drain. Mix into
sausage and beans and you're ready to eat! burp! Randy Rigg
