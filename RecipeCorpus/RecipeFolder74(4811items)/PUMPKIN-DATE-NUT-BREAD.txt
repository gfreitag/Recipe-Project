Title: PUMPKIN DATE NUT BREAD
Categories: Cheese, French, Breads, Jewish, Low fat
3/4 c  pumpkin pie filling
Yield: 10 Servings
1/3 c  milk -- skim
1 lg egg
1 TB butter
1 ts salt
2 1/3 c  bread flour
2/3 c  flour -- whole-grain wheat
3 TB brown sugar -- packed
2 TB vital wheat gluten
1/2 ts cinnamon
1/4 ts ginger
1/4 ts nutmeg
1/8 ts allspice
2 ts yeast
1/2 c  walnuts* -- coarsely
chopped
:
1/3 c  dates* -- chopped

Add ingredients to the machine in the order listed with the exception
of walnuts and dates.  Bake on fruit and nut cycle. When addition
beep sounds, add walnuts and dates. Makesa 1 1/2 pound loaf.
