Title: ROASTED MUSTARD POTATOES
Categories: Rosie's
Yield: 4 Servings
Vegetable oil cooking spray
4 tb Dijon-style mustard
2 ts Paprika
1 ts Ground Cumin
1 ts Chili Powder
1/8 ts Cayenne Pepper
16    Baby Red Potatoes
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Spray a roasting pan 3 times to coat
with the vegetable oil. Put the mustard, paprika, cumin, chili powder
and cayenne pepper in a large bowl.  Whisk to blend. Prick the
potatoes several times with the tines of a fork and add them to the
bowl. Toss to coat the potatoes evenly. Pour the coated potatoes in
the prepared roasting pan, leaving a little space between them. Bake
for 45 minutes to 1 hour, until the potatoes are fork tender.
