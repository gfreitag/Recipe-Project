Title: QUINOA TABOOLI (AM)
Categories: Salads, Grains
Yield: 1 Recipe
2 c  Cooked AM Quinoa
1 c  Chopped parsley
1/2 c  Chopped green onion
1    Garlic clove; pressed
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  AM Unrefined Olive Oil
1/8 ts Sea salt (optional)
1/8 ts Pepper (optional)
Whole lettuce leaves
Mix first 9 ingredients and place in a salad bowl lined with washed
lettuce leaves.  Refrigerate for at least two hours to let flavor
blend. Fresh mint and black olives make nice garnishes for this dish.
