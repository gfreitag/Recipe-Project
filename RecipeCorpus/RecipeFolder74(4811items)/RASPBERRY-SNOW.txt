Title: RASPBERRY SNOW
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 c  Water
3/4 c  Frozen raspberry juice
-concentrate, thawed
The refreshing flavor of raspberry gives kids a refreshing cool lift
on a summer day.

Combine together and pour into ice cube trays. Freeze. In food
processor, crush ice cubes and spoon into cups or bowls to serve.
Makes 6 8 servings.

Origin:  Canadian Living cooking collection: Great Desserts Shared by:
Sharon Stevens.
