Title: PUMPKIN PIE SPICE
Categories: Spices
Yield: 2 Servings
1/4 c  Cinnamon
1 tb Ground ginger
2 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Ground allspice
1 ts Ground cloves
Makes a scant 1/2 cup.  Add 1 T per pie to pumpkin pie filling. Stir
1 T into 2 cups applesauce.  Stir 1 t into 2 cups softened vanilla
ice cream or frozen yogurt. Serve over warm fruit or gingerbread.

Buy spices in bulk from a natural foods store.
