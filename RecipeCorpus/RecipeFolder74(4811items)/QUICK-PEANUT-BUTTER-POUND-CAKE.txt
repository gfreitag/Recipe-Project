Title: QUICK PEANUT BUTTER POUND CAKE
Categories: Box cakes, Cakes
Yield: 24 Servings
1 pk Yellow cake mix
3    Eggs
2/3 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Creamy peanut butter
1 sm Instant vanilla pudding
Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Blend well. Beat on
medium speed for 5 minutes. Pour mixture into a greased and floured
13x9" pan. Bake at 350~ for 1 hour. Cool in pan 10 minurtes. Remove
from pan and cool completely.
