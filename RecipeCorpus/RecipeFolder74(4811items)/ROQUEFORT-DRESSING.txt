Title: ROQUEFORT DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dressings
Yield: 1 Cups
3 oz Roquefort cheese; crumbled
1 tb Onion; minced
1 sm Garlic clove; minced
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
ds Pepper
Combine cheese, onion, and garlic in a small mixing bowl; mix on low
speed of electric mixer 3 minutes. Add remaining ingredients; beat
mixture 2 mintues or until smooth. Chill.

Yield: 1-1/2 cups

Source: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in 1980. Typed for you by
Nancy Coleman.
