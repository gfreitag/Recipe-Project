Title: ROBIN'S ARMENIAN RICE PILAF
Categories: Rice, Side-dish
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Stick butter; melted
1 1/2 c  Egg noodles; broken
1 c  Long-grain rice
2 c  Chicken broth
2 c  Water
1/2 tb Salt
Brown noodles in butter.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer
covered over medium heat 20-30 minutes or until all liquid is
absorbed.
