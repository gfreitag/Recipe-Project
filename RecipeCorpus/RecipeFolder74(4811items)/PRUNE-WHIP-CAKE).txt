Title: PRUNE WHIP CAKE)
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Soda
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Allspice
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Prune juice
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Milk
2 ea Eggs
Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, soda,salt and spices. Add
shortening, add prune juice and milk, then vanilla. Mix well then add
the eggs and mix well. Pour into greased and floured tube pan or 13
x9 inch pan. Bake at 350 for 50 minutes
