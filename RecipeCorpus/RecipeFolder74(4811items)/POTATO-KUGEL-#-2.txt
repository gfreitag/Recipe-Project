Title: POTATO KUGEL # 2
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
9    Potatoes, grated
4    Slices bacon
1/2    Onion, chopped
1 c  Milk, boiled
1    Egg, beaten
2 tb Sour cream (heaping)
1    Dash Salt
1    Dash pepper
Put potatoes into colander and press out juices.  Cook bacon and keep
the fat. (Vegetarians omit meat.) Combine potatoes, bacon, onion and
hot bacon fat, and pour into a 9 x 13-inch baking dish. Pour milk
over top. Pour on egg and sour cream. Salt and pepper to taste. Bake
at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until light brown on top. Serve
immediately or let flavors blend overnight.
