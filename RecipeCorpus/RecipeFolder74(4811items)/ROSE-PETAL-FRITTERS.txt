Title: ROSE PETAL FRITTERS
Categories: Snacks, Misc
1 ea bunch of rose petals
Yield: 4 Servings

Use the Fanny Farmer beer batter. Toss the petals in and mix gently.
Drop into the hot oil and fry until golden. To fry: Dip chunks of
food into batter. Fry in 3-4 inches of fat at 375 degrees until
golden brown. Drain on paper toweling.  Sprinkle fruit fritters with
confectioners sugar or top with a sweet sauce.
