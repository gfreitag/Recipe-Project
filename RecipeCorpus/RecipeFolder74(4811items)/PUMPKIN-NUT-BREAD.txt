Title: PUMPKIN NUT BREAD
Categories: Breads, Entertain, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
3 1/3 c  Flour
2 ts Soda
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
3 c  Sugar (or 2 cups, works
-fine)
4    Eggs
2 c  Pumpkin
2/3 c  Water
1 c  Chopped nuts
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Sift dry ingredients into a large
bowl. Make a well and add sugar, eggs, pumpkin and water. Beat well
until smooth. Add nuts, and mix into mixture. Pour into three greased
and floured loaf pans. Bake at 350 degrees F for one hour.

These freeze very well.
