Title: QUICK GRAHAM ROLLS^
Categories: Crs, Der, Homestyle
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Graham flour
1/2 ts Salt
4 ts Baking powder
2 tb Fat
3/4 c  Milk, about
Soft butter
Sift together the dry ingredients. Rub in the fat, add milk and mix
to a smooth dough. Turn out onto a floured board; divide dough into
small portions and form into rolls the size and thickness of 2
fingers. Brush with soft butter; bake at 425 degrees until brown.
