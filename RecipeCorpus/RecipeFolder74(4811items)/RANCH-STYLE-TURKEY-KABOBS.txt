Title: RANCH-STYLE TURKEY KABOBS
Categories: Poultry, Low fat
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Turkey tenderloins, cut
-into 1 1/2 inch cubes
2    Zucchini, sliced 1 " thick
1    Red bell pepper, cut into
-1 1/2 inch pieces
1/4 c  Purchased ranch salad
-dressing
Spray broiler pan with nonstick cooking spray.  Thread turkey cubes,
zucchini slices and bell pepper pieces onto four 12 inch metal
skewers. Brush each kabob with 1 tablespoon salad dressing. Place
kabobs on spray-coated pan.

Broil 4 to 6 inches from heat for 10 to 12 minutes or until turkey is
golden brown and no longer pink, turning once halfway through cooking.
