Title: PORK CHOP & CORN STUFFING BAKE
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Campbells' Golden Corn Soup
1/4 c  Ea chopped celery & onion
1/2 ts Paprika
4    Boneless pork chops 3/4"
1 tb Packed brown sugar
1 ts Spicy brown mustard
1 1/2 c  Corn bread stuffing
1.  Combine soup, celery, onion, paprika and stuffing. In 9" greased
pie plate, spoon stuffing mixture. Arrange chops over mixture;
press lightly into the stuffing mixture.

2.  Combine sugar and mustard; spread evenly over chops.

3.  Bake at 400F for 30 minutes, or until chops are fork-tender.
