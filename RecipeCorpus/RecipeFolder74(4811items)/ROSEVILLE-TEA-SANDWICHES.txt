Title: ROSEVILLE TEA SANDWICHES
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Minced green onion
1 tb Each chopped fresh dill and
-capers
1 1/2 ts Prepared horseradish
Pinch pepper
8    Slices whole wheat bread
1 1/2 ts Butter, softened
4    Slices smoke salmon
12    Thin slices cucumber
In small bowl, combine mayonnaise, onion, dill, capers, horseradish
and pepper.  Trim crusts from bread; spread with butter, then
mayonnaise mixture.

Arrange salmon and cucumber over 4 of the bread slices; top with
remaining bread.  Cut each sandwich into 4 triangles or squares.
Makes 4 servings. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: The
Canadian Living Cookbook.
