Title: RED KIDNEY BEAN BURGERS
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian, Burgers
Yield: 1 Servings
16 oz Cooked red kidney beans
1/2 c  Small onion
1/2 ea Green pepper
1 ea Steamed & mashed carrot
1/8 c  Picante salsa sauce
1 c  Bread crumbs
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Chili powder
Mix all ingredients.  Form into equal sized balls & flatten into
patties. Bake at 450 for 15 to 20 minutes. Serve on buns with
tortilla chips.
