Title: PORK CHOPS & RICE (P/C)
Categories: Pork, Rice, Pressure, Cooker
Yield: 4 Servings
-MARY WILSON  BWVB02B
4    Pork Chops
1 tb Fat or drippings
4 tb Uncooked rice
4 sl Onion
4 sl Green pepper
2 c  Canned tomatoes
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Brown chops well on both sides in cooker in melted drippings. Place
rack under chops in cooker.  On each chop put a tablespoon of rice, a
slice of onion and green pepper, and a section of tomato. Add salt
and pepper and cover with liquid from tomatoes. Pressure cook at 15
lbs. pressure for 15 minutes. Reduce pressure immediately. Serve 4.
