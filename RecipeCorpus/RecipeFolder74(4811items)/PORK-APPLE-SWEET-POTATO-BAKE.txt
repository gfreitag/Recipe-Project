Title: PORK-APPLE-SWEET POTATO BAKE
Categories: Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
5    Sweet potatoes,medium,peeled
Salt to taste
1/3 c  Honey
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Butter or margarine
6    Apples
Pepper to taste
1/4 c  Soy sauce
6    Pork chops
1. Cut sweet potatoes into 1/2-inch slices.
2. Core apples but do not peel; cut into 1/2-inch rings.
3. Arrange sweet potato slices over the bottom of a greased
13x9x2-inch casserole or baking pan.
4. Put half the apple rings on top of sweet potatoes, saving the
other half to use on top with pork chops.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Mix together honey, soy sauce and water; pour over sweet potatoes
and apples.
7. Bake in preheated 350'F. oven 30 minutes.
8. Season pork chops with salt and pepper; brown in a skillet in 1/2
tablespoon margarine.
9. Arrange chops and remaining apple rings over precooked sweet
potatoes and apples; baste with pan juices and dot with remaining
margarine.
10. Bake 30 minutes longer, or until done.
