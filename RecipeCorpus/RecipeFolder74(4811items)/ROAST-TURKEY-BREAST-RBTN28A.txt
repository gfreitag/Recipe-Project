Title: ROAST TURKEY BREAST RBTN28A
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 12 Servings
5 lb Turkey breast
2 c  Apple cider
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Corn starch
Put breast in small roast pan & bake 450F 30 minutes,no cover. Mix 1
1/2 Cups Cider with 1/4 C Soy Sauce & pour over Turkey Breast, cover
& bake 1 1/2 hours at 325F. Mix 2 T. Cornstarch with 1/2 C apple
Cider, pour into pan with breast, bake uncovered untill thickened.
Drain sauce into seperate bowl & serve with turkey...
