Title: REGGAE POPCORN
Categories: Popcorn
Yield: 8 Cups
-Sue Woodward
3 tb Butter
1 tb Ground cumin
1 tb Sugar
1/2 tb Dried red pepper flakes; not
-a typo
8 c  Popped corn
In a heavy saucepan, melt butter over med. heat.  Stir in remaining
ingredients except popcorn.  Cook, stirring constantly, until sugar
dissolves.  Pour over popcorn; toss to coat evenly. Serve at once.

Source:  Theatre Popcorn Popper leaflet

From: Suewoodward
