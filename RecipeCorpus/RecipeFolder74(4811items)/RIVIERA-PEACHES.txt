Title: RIVIERA PEACHES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Peach halves
2/3 c  Raspberry jelly
pistachio ice cream

Place 2 peach halves in each of 8 sherbet glasses. Melt 2/3 c
raspberry jelly; pour over peaches. Chill 3 hours. Just before
serving, top each serving with 1 scoop pistachio ice cream.
