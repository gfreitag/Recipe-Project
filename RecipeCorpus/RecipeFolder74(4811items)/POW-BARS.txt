Title: POW BARS
Categories: Bars, Chocolate
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Honey Or Molasses
1/2 c  Peanut Butter
1 c  Dry Milk, Non-Instant
** Options **
Chocolate Chips
Raisins Or Dried Fruit Bits
Coconut
Cashew Butter -- *
Almond Butter -- *
1. Kneed all ingredients together with your hands, adding enough milk
powder to form a stiff but not crumbly dough. Optional ingredients
can be kneaded in as well. 2. Shape into logs about 2-3 inches long
and 1 inch in diameter. 3. Roll in powdered milk, confectioner's
sugar or coconut.

Makes about 6. * May be used in place of peanut butter.
