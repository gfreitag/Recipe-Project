Title: ROQUEFORT LETTUCE & FLAGEOLET BEAN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Olive oil (perferably extra
-virgin
3 tb White wine vinegar
6 c  Oakleaf lettuce or red or
-green leaf lettuce
1 cn (15 oz) flageolet bean or
-small white beans, drained
-rinsed
4 oz Roquefort cheese, crumbled
-(about 1 cup)
6    Fresh mint leaves, finely
-chopped
3/4 ts Honey
Whisk oil and vinegar in small bowl.  Season dressing to taste with
salt and pepper. Combine lettuce, beans, cheese and mint in large
bowl. Combine with enough dressing to coat. Drizzle with honey and
toss well.

Flageolet beans are small kidney beans ranging from pale green to
white.
