Title: ROBERT PPEARSON'S GIANT WONDER
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1 huge loaf
1 1/8 c  Water
3 tb Applesauce (Marg)
1 ts Vanilla
2 tb Brown Sugar
1 1/2 tb Cinnamon
1 1/8 ts Salt
2/3 c  10 or 9 or 7 grain cereal
Crush with rolling pin
2/3 c  Cornmeal
2 2/3 c  Bread flour
5 tb Buttermilk powder
1 tb Caraway seeds
1 tb Yeast
2 tb Gluten
2 ts Lecithin
1/3 c  Raisins or currents
This will produce a 33oz loaf. If you leave out lecithin, add 1/2 T
gluten. He calls it NO NAME BREAD
