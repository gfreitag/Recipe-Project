Title: RECESS CUPS
Categories: Candies, Cake mix
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz Hersery bar
1 1/2 c  Peanut butter
melt chocolate and 1/2 cup of peanut butter together then melt peanut
butter in another pan. place cupcake liners in muffin tins put in 1
tablespoon of chocolate peanut butter mix in each tin let sit 30
minutes add some melted peanut butter on top of each let sit 30
minutes then top with another tablespoon of chocolate mix. This was
on phil's show recipe from the copycat lady Gloria
