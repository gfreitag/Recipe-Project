Title: RHUBARB COMPOTE
Categories: New, Text, Import
2 lb fresh rhubarb stalks, --
sliced to 1-inch lengths
Yield: 4 Servings
:
1 1/2 c  orange juice
1/3 c  clover honey
lowfat vanilla yogurt
cinnamon
:
:

Simmer rhubarb, orange juice, and honey together in nonreactive pan
for 8 minutes until rhubarb is tender when pierced with a fork.
Remove from heat and cool until warm. Serve in bowls topped with
dollops of yogurt.
