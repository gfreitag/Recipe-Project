Title: RED BEANS & RICE I
Categories: Cajun, Rice, Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Red Beans
1 lb Ham Seasoning Meat with Fat
3 qt Water
2 c  Onions; Chopped
1/2 c  Green Onion; Chopped
1/4 c  Parsley; Chopped
6    Toes Garlic; Chopped
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Cayenne Pepper
NOTE: Tony's Seasoning may be added for extra taste.
Place washed beans, ham, onions and water in a 4 quart microwave-safe
casserole. Cover; cook on HIGH in microwave for 2 and 1/2 hours,
stirring occasionally and adding more water. Stir in green onions,
parsley, garlic and seasonings. Cook on MEDIUM for 1 hour. Serve over
Rice.
NOTE: To save cooking time, set the beans in a pot of water
overnight. Then add all ingredients and cook about half as long. Use
your judgement on cooking time.
