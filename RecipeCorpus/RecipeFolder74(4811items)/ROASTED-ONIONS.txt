Title: ROASTED ONIONS
Categories: Side dish, Indian, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lg Spanish Onion
1 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Chopped coriander leaves
Peel onion, cut in half & slice into 1/4" thick slices. Separate the
slices into shreds.

Heat a large skillet till hot, then add the oil.  When smoking hot,
add the onions, do not stir but let them sizzle for 30 seconds. Now
stir, keep roasting, tossing & turning for about 2 minutes. They
should be crisp. Toss with coriander & serve.

Julie Sahni, "Classic Indian Cooking"
