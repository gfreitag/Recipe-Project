Title: PUMPKIN BREAD (M_C-TX)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 30 Servings
3 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg
3 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
1 c  Nuts
2 ts Soda
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Pumpkin (2 cups)
1 c  Oil
2/3 c  Water
Sift dry ingredients in a bowl.  Mix well.  Add other ingredients and
beat. Bake 350  for approximately 1 hour. Makes 3 loaves.
