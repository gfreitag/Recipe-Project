Title: POLYNESIAN TERIYAKI SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Soy Sauce
1/3 c  Apricot-Pineapple preserves
1/2 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Cornstarch
1/4 c  Water
Combine first four ingredients in saucepan and bring to a boil slowly.
While sauce is cooking, combine cornstarch and water and add to
sauce. Cook until sauce thickens.

Source:  Best Barbeque Recipes by Mildred Fischer
