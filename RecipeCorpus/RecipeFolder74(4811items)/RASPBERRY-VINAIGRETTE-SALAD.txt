Title: RASPBERRY VINAIGRETTE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 12 Servings
MMMMM--------------------BETTY PINDER TKHN51B-------------------------
VINAIGRETTE
6 tb Raspberry vinegar
1 1/2 tb Dijon mustard
1 1/2 ts Sugar
3/4 ts Pepper
3/4 c  Olive oil
SALAD
1 lg Head red leaf lettuce
-chopped
1 lg Head romaine lettuce
-chopped
1 bn Arugula, chopped
3/4 lb Mushrooms, chopped
3/4 lb Tomatoes, chopped
4    Green onions, chopped
1/4 c  Minced fresh dill
Mix first 5 vinaigrette ingredients in small bowl. Gradually whisk in
olive oil.  Combine salad ingredients in large bowl. Add enough
dressing to season to taste and toss well.
