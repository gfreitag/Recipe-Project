Title: RICE PUDDING FOR 2
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 2 Servings
2 ts Butter
1/4 c  Long grain rice
1 tb Sugar
1 1/4 c  Milk
In saucepan melt butter and stir in rice to coat. Stir in sugar and
milk and bring to a boil, turn down heat to medium low and simmer for
20-25 minutes stiring often until milk is absorbed and rice is
tender. At this time add raisins if you wish and a pinch of nutmeg.
Very good.
