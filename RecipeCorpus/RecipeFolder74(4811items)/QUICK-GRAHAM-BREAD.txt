Title: QUICK GRAHAM BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Cold water
1/2 c  Melted shortening
3/4 c  Milk
1 c  White flour
1 1/3 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Graham flour
Dissolve sugar in water; add shortening and milk. Sift white flour,
measure, and sift with salt and baking soda. Add to first mixture. Add
graham flour. Mix thoroughly. Bake in well-oiled bread pan, in
moderate oven (375 F) 1 hour.  1 loaf. The Household Searchlight -
1941
