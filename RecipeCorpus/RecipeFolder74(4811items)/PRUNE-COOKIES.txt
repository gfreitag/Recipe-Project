Title: PRUNE COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 5 Servings
1 c  Cooked prunes
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Molasses
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Shortening
2    Eggs, well beaten
1 1/4 c  Cake flour
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Vanilla
Drain prunes.  Remove pits.  Cut prunes into pieces. Cream shortening
and sugar.  Add molasses and eggs. Beat thoroughly. Sift flour,
measure, and sift with baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Add to first
mixture. Beat thoroughly.  Add prunes and flavoring. Mix thoroughly.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto well-oiled baking sheet. Bake in hot oven
(400 F) about 12 minutes. 15 servings. The Household Searchlight
