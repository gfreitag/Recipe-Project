Title: RICE SOUP
Categories: Soups, Appetizers, Vegetarian, Rice
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Ghee
1 c  Mixed vegetables, diced*
2    Garlic cloves
2 c  Cooked rice
3 c  Water
1/2 ts Sage
1 ts Rosemary
1 ts Parsley
1 tb Soy sauce
Salt & pepper
Heat ghee in a large soup pot.  Saute the diced vegetables & garlic
for 15 minutes.  Pour over water, bring to a boil & simmer until the
vegetables are almost tender. While the vegetables are simmering, add
the herbs in order.  About 5 minutes before the end of the cooking
time, add the cooked rice.  Add more water if necessary.

"Tasajara Cooking"
