Title: RED-HOT MOLDED HEARTS
Categories: Salads, Valentine's
Yield: 10 Servings
1/4 c  Red-hot candies
1 c  Boiling water
1 pk (3 oz) strawberry gelatin
2 1/2 c  Applesauce
In a bowl, dissolve candies in water.  Stir in gelatin until
dissolved. Fold in applesauce. Pour into 12 oiled 1/3 cup individual
molds, a 4 cup heart shaped mold or a 1 quart bowl. Chill for at
least 3 hours. Yield: 10 - 12 servings  Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Taste of Home.
