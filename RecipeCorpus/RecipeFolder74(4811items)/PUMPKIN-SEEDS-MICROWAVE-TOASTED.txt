Title: PUMPKIN SEEDS MICROWAVE TOASTED
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Pumpkin seeds
1 ts Butter or oil
-Salt OR:
Parmesan, grated
Clean strings from pumpkin seeds as much as possible. Toss with
butter or oil. Spread seeds in pie plate, in circle with open space
in middle. Microwave at High 5-6 minutes, stirringevery 2 minutes,
till seeds are dry and slightly crisp. Sprinkle with salt or
Parmesan. Let stand 5 minutes. Makes: 1 cup Source: The Toronto Sun
