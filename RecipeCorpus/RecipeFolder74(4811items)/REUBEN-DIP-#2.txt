Title: REUBEN DIP #2
Categories: Appetizers, Dips
Yield: 1 Recipe
1    (3 oz.) pkg. cream cheese
1/4 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Swiss cheese; grated
4 oz Corn beef; finely diced
1/4 c  Chopped sauerkraut; drained
2    To 3 tbsp. milk
Combine and heat all ingredients over low heat.  Serve with party rye
or rye or pumpernickel bread sticks. May be served at room
temperature. I usually double this. Source: Unknown.
