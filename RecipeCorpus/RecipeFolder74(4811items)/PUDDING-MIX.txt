Title: PUDDING MIX
Categories: Mixes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Recipe
2 1/3 c  Instant non fat dry milk
1/2 c  Cornstarch
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1. Mix ingredients thoroughly.

2. Store in tightly covered container in refrigerator. Use within 3
months.

About 2-1/2 cups mix
