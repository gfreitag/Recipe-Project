Title: REUBEN BREAD
Categories: Breads, Abm, Good
Yield: 1 Loaf
2 1/2 ts Active yeast
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
1 c  Rye flour
2 tb Gluten
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/8 ts Baking soda
1 ts Caraway seeds
3 oz Swiss cheese, shredded
1/4 c  Sauerkraut,rinsed & drained
3 tb Thousand island dressing
1 c  Plus 2 tbs warm water
2 1/2 oz Deli sliced lean corn
Beef
All ingredients must be at room temperature except water at 110
degree F. Add all ingredients to ABM in the order listed. Select
white bread and start. Source: Dak 1990 News letters
