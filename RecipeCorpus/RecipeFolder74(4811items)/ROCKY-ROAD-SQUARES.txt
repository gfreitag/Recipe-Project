Title: ROCKY ROAD SQUARES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 32 Squares
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Graham wafer crumbs
1 1/2 c  Angel Flake Coconut
1 1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 pk (300g) Semi-Sweet Chocolate
Chips
1 1/2 c  Miniature marshmallows
1 cn (300mL) Sweetened Condensed
Milk
2    Squares Semi-Sweet Chocolate
,melted
Preheat oven to 350F (180C). In 13x9-inch (3.5L) baking pan, melt
butter in oven. Sprinkle crumbs over butter; mix together and press
onto bottom of pan. Layer coconut, nuts, chocolate chips and
marshmallows over crust. Drizzle sweetened condensed milk evenly over
all. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from oven;
drizzle with melted chocolate squares. Cool completely. Cut into
squares. Store loosely covered at room temperature.

Source: Eagle Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk label
