Title: QUICK BUCKWHEAT CAKES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Flour; buckwheat
3/4 c  Flour; white
3 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 3/4 c  Milk; sweet
3/4 ts Salt
1 ea Egg
3 tb Shortening; melted
Sift dry ingredients. Mix egg, milk and shortening and add to dry
ingredients. Beat until smooth. Source: Orpha Robertson, Weaver
Grange, Knox County, OH
