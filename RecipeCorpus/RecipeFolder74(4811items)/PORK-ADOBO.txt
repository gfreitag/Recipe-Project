Title: PORK ADOBO
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
3    Cloves fresh garlic, minced
3 lb Pork, cut in 2-inch cubes
15    Whole black peppercorns
1    Bay leaf
1/2 c  Vinegar
6 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Sugar
Mix all ingredients together in large pot and simmer 1 hour. Serve
with rice and green vegetables.
