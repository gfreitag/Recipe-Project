Title: PULLED MINTS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Sugar
4 tb Butter
1 c  Water
Powdered sugar
1/2 ts Peppermint flavoring
Boil sugar, water, and butter to hard ball stage (265 ~ 270 F). Add
flavoring.  Turn onto wet platter. Cool. Pull until brittle. Cut in
1-inch pieces. Place in dish of powdered sugar. Leave until mints
become creamy. Margaret Scharfeneker, Buckhannon, W. VA.
