Title: RASPBERRY ZINGER VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 1 Cup
1/2 c  White vinegar
2    Raspberry Zinger tea bags
-(Celestial Seasonings)
3/4 c  Olive oil
1 ts Balsamic vinegar
1 ts Sugar
Steep your Raspberry Zinger tea bags in vinegar for 10 minutes.
Remove and gently squeeze remaining flavor from tea bags before
discarding. In a jar, add the rest of your ingredients and shake.
Serve over any salad you like.

Recipes from "Cooking With Tea" by Jennifer & Mo Siegel (and inside
of tea box)
