Title: Orange-Pecan Dressing
Categories: Rice/grains, Misc., Dressings
Yield: 16 servings
1 tb Honey or sugar
1 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 tb Chopped pecans
Shake all ingredients in tightly covered container. ABOUT 1 CUP DRESSING;
75 CALORIES PER TABLESPOON..
