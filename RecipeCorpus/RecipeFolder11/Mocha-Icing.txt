Title: Mocha Icing
Categories: Toppings
Yield: 6 servings
4 ts Cocoa
1/4 c  Butter
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1 tb Coffee
1/8 ts Salt
1    Egg yolk, well beaten
Sift sugar.  Cream butter.  Add sugar.  Beat until smooth.  Add remaining
ingredients.  Beat until well blended.
