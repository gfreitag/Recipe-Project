Title: Scallion Puree
Categories: Condiments, Foodmagazin
Yield: 1 servings
(about 1 bunch)
Chopped and patted dry
1/2 c  Olive oil
2 c  Scallions greens,
In a saucepan heat  1/4 cup olive oil over moderate heat and cook
scallions just until tender and bright green (do not let brown). In a
blender puree scallion mixture with remaining  1/4 cup olive oil
until smooth. Season with salt and pepper and let cool. Use to
garnish soup.

Makes about 3/4 cup.
