Title: Mushroom Crab Puffs^
Categories: Appetizers, Holiday, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 36 servings
5 oz Jar sharp cheese spread
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 cn (6oz) crab, drained and
Cleaned
3 tb Thinly sliced green onion
36 lg Stuffing mushrooms
1 c  Oil
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  White wine vinegar or white
Vinegar
Marinate mushoom caps only in a ziploc bag with combined oil,
vinegar, and pepper 4-24 hours.

Blend cheese and mayonnaise. Stir in crab and onion.

Drain mushrooms, discarding mayonnnaise. Dry caps; fill with crab
mixture. Broil 4" from heat 5-8 minutes until hot and bubbly. Serve
warm.
