Title: BRISTOL ICE BOX CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
3 c  Vanilla wafer crumbs
1/2 pt Heavy cream, whipped
1/4 c  Butter, melted
2 c  Crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 c  Sugar
Mix first 4 ingredients.   Line a 1 quart dish with a layer of
crumbs, then a layer of mixture.  Repeat the process, ending with
crumbs. Refrigerate 3-4 hours or overnight.

To serve, garnish with whipped cream.  Could substitute CoolWhip if
desired.

Source: Virginia Hospitality Submitted by Dale/Gail Shipp
