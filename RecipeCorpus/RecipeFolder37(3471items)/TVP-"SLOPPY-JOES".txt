Title: TVP "SLOPPY JOES"
Categories: Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings
2    8 oz cans tomato sauce
1 1/2 tb Red wine vinegar
1 tb Molasses
2 tb Chili powder
1 1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Tabasco sauce
Hamburger buns
1 c  TVP
3/4 c  Boiling water
1    Green bell pepper, diced
1    Onion, minced
1    Stalk celery, minced
1    Carrot, minced
1/3 c  Water
Combine TVP and boiling water and set aside.  "Saute" bell pepper,
onion, celery and carrot in 1/3 cup of water in a deep skillet or
dutch oven until soft, about 5 to 7 minutes. Add TVP and remaining
ingredients and cook over medium heat for 5 to 10 minutes until
heated through. Serve on toasted hamburger buns.
