Title: MOUNTAIN HIGH HERB BAKED EGGS
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Fresh chives, chopped
2 ts Fresh parsley, chopped
3/4 c  Shredded sharp Cheddar
- cheese
1/4 lb Ham, flaked
8 lg Eggs, fresh
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 c  Sour cream
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Grease 4 1-cup ramekins. Divide flaked
ham between ramekins.  Whisk together eggs, mustard and sour cream.
Stir in herbs and top with shredded cheese. Bake 25 to 30 minutes
until golden and set.

Makes 4 servings.
