Title: Pour Pizza^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Pizza, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Oregano
2    Eggs
2/3 c  Milk
1 cn (15oz) pizza sauce
2 c  Shredded mozarella
Cornmeal
1 lb Ground beef
1    Chopped onion
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Flour
Lightly sprinkle a greased 13X9" baking dish with cornmeal and set
aside. Preheat oven to 400F.

Brown beef and onion; drain and season with salt and pepper.

Make a smooth batter out of flour, oregano, eggs and milk. Pour into
baking dish and cover with crumbles meat mixture. Bake 15-20 minutes.
Remove from oven and cover with sauce and cheese. Return to oven and
bake another 15 minutes.
