Title: LYCHEE SHERBET
Categories: Beverages, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Milk
1 c  Half and half
1 ts Lemon juice
24    Lychees
1 pk Gelatin, unflavored
1/4 c  Water, cold
1/3 c  Milk
Peel and seed lychees.  Squeeze lychees through 2 thicknesses of
cheesecloth to obtain 1 cup juice.  Sprinkle gelatin over cold
water and let stand 5 minutes.  Scald milk, add soaked gelatin,
and stir until thoroughly dissolved.  Add sugar, mixing well.
Cool.  Add milk and half and half.  Stir in lychee and lemon
juice.  Freeze in ice cream freezer.

Source: _Heart of the Palms: Favorite recipes of the Palm Beaches_
