Title: GOOEY CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1    Box confectioners sugar
1 pk 8 oz. cream cheese
2    Eggs
1    Butter cake mix
1    Margarine stick
2    Eggs
Mix together cake mix, margarine and 2 eggs.  Batter will be stiff.
Spread in a greased 9x13 pan.  Top with mixture of confectioner's
sugar, cream cheese and 2 eggs.  Pour over cake mixture and bake at
300 degrees for 40 to 60 minutes. Randy Rigg
