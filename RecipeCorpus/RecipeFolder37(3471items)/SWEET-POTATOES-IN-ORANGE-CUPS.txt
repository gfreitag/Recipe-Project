Title: SWEET POTATOES IN ORANGE CUPS
Categories: Vegetables, Fruit, Side dish, Holiday
Yield: 16 servings
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground cloves
Salt
Pepper
Paprika
8    Oranges
24 oz Cooked sweet potatoes
1/2 c  Orange juice
3 tb Butter at room  temperature
3 tb Dark brown sugar
Halve and squeeze oranges, save juice and orange cups.  Put orange
cups in large buttered baking dish.  Combine in a bowl, using mixer,
the sweet potatoes, orange juice, butter, brown sugar, nutmeg,
cloves, salt, and pepper.  Mix until smooth.  Fill the orange cups
with sweet potato mixture. Can be frozen at this point or bake in a
350F oven, uncovered for 20 to 30 minutes.  Sprinkle with paprika
before serving.

Yield: 16 servings.

From: An Olde Concorde Christmas
