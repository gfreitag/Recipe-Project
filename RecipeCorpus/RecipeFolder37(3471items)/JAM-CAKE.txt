Title: JAM CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Allspice
4    Eggs
ICING:
6 tb Cream
6 tb Butter
2 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter
4 tb Buttermilk, dissolved with
1 tsp. of soda
1 c  Blackberry jam
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Mix the first 7 ingredients together, then add eggs one at a time.
Mix well and pour batter into 3 well greased and floured cake pans.
Bake at 350 degrees until done.  Icing: Cook until it forms soft ball
in cold water.  Beat until thick. Randy Rigg The Pinnacle Club BBS
