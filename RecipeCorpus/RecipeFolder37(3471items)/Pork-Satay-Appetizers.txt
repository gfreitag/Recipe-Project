Title: Pork Satay Appetizers
Categories: Appetizers, Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 16 servings
1 c  Soy sauce
12 oz Dijon style mustard
1/2 c  Honey
2 lb Boneless pork loin; sliced
-- into thin strips
4    Minced garlic
Preperation time: 20 minutes.

Save half the sauce for dipping, marinate pork in other half for 30
minutes.  Remove strips from marinade, discarding marinade and weave
on bamboo skewers.  Grill or broil 3 - 4 minutes.  Serve with reserved
sauce.

Other sauce ideas: salsa, sweet & sour or honey mustard.

Nutritional info: per serving, 94 calories, Protein 13 gm, Fat 3 gm,
Sodium 354 mg, Cholesterol 33 mg.

From National Pork Producers Council, 1995
