Title: RUBY PUNCH BOWL
Categories: Beverages, Holiday, Alcohol
Yield: 24 servings
Or other red table wine
2 c  Chilled cranberry cocktail
1 l  Chilled apple cider
Ice
Thin lemon or lime slices
1 1/2 c  Water
2 c  Sugar (or less, to taste)
2    3-4 inch sticks cinnamon
2 ts Whole cloves
1/8 ts Salt
2    Bottles (750 ml) burgundy
In saucepan bring water, sugar, spices and salt to boil. Lower heat
and simmer 10 minutes; strain-out and discard spices; cool syrup.
Combine spiced syrup with wine, cranberry juice cocktail and cider.
Pour into punch bowl, add ice and lemon slices.
Source: Calgary Herald  Dec 15/93
