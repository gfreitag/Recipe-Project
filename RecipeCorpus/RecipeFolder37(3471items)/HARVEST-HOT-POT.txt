Title: HARVEST HOT POT
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
6 oz Button mushrooms
2 ts Chopped fresh rosemary
2 ts Chopped fresh thyme
1 ts Yeast extract
1    Salt and pepper
8 oz Pearl barley
2 ts Sunflower oil
8 oz Shallots or onion
2 x  Small leeks *
1    Large potato, diced
6 oz Carrots, sliced
*  trimmed and sliced
1. Cover the barley with plenty of water. Bring to the boil, cover
and simm for 30 - 40 minutes. Drain and reserve the stock.
2. Heat the oil in a large pan and gently fry the shallots, leeks ,
potato, carrots and mushrooms for 8 - 10 minutes. Add the
rosemary,thyme and cooked barley.
3. Make the reserved stock upto 3/4 pint with water. Add to the
barley and vegetables together with the yeast extract. Bring to the
boil, cover and simmer for 30 minutes. Season to taste. Serve hot.
