Title: MEAT SAUCE II
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Meats, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
15 oz Can tomato sauce
1 lb Lean ground beef
1    Clove garlic,
-peeled and crushed
1/2 ts Dried oregano leaves
1/2 ts Dried basil leaves
1. In a medium-sized, heat-resistant, non-metallic bowl crumble
beef.
2. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 5 minutes, stirring
frequently until meat is browned. Drain off excess fat.
3. Add remaining ingredients and stir to blend well.
4 Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 5 minutes, stirring
occasionally.
5. Serve over spaghetti.
