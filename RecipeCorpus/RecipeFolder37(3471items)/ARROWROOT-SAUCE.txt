Mix the arrowroot thoroughly  with the water in the saucepan, using a
wire whisk. Add the remaining ingredients and mix well. Cook over
medium heat, stirring constantly until thickened. Keep warm over low
heat. Serve over hot vegetables, whole grains or stir frys. Keeps up
to 7 days in refrigerator or may be frozen.
