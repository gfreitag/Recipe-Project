Title: ORANGE FRUIT CREMES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Orange juice (see note)
-Ice cubes (see note)
1 c  Milk; lowfat or nonfat
1 pk Orange sugar free gelatin;
- 4 serving size pkg.
3/4 c  -water; boiling
Note: Combine the orange juice and ice cubes to make 1 cup. Mix
gelatin and boiling water until gelatin is dissolved. Add orange
juice and ice cube mixture. Stir until ice cubes are dissolved, add
milk. Chill and garnish with orange slice if desired. Also try this
with 1/2 cup strawberry puree in place of orange juice, changing the
gelatin flavor to strawberry or cherry. ---
