Title: Tomato Juice Cocktail - SL 1/90
Categories: Beverages, Holiday, Tomatoes
Yield: 6 cups
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ea Bay leaf
6 ea Whole cloves
2 ea Whole allspice
3 tb White vinegar
1 ea 46-oz can tomato juice
1 tb Instant minced onion
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Bon appetit seasoning
1 ts Powdered horseradish
Combine all ingredients except vinegar in a large saucepan; bring
to a boil.  Reduce heat, and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat; stir in vinegar, and let cool.  Strain and
discard bay leaf, cloves, and allspice.  Cover and chill 3 to 4
hours. Pour beverage into glasses.  Yield: about 6 cups.

Robbie Ann Colopy of Virginia, in January, 1990 "Southern Living"
