Title: Potato Rolls^
Categories: Potatoes, Breads
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 c  Olive or vegetable oil
2    Eggs, lightly beaten
1 c  Mashed cooked potato
4 c  To 4 1/2 flour
1 pk Yeast
1 tb Brown sugar
3/4 c  Water
2 ts Salt
Proof yeaast in sugar and water. Mix in salt, oil, eggs, and potatoes.
Beat in flour to form a soft dough.

Knead dough until smooth and elastic, about 10 minutes, adding flour
as needed. (Dough should be somewhat sticky.) Put in a greased bowl,
turn to coat top, cover and let rise until doubled.

Punch down; divide in 12 pieces, shape into rolls. Place on a greased
cookie sheet. Cover loosley with a cloth and let rise until doubled.
Bake in a preheated 375F. oven until golden and sound hollow when
tapped.

Per roll:
237cal., 6g pro., 6g fat, 39g carb., 368mg sod., 35mg chol., 2 1/4
starch/bread, 1/5 meat and 1 fat exchanges.
