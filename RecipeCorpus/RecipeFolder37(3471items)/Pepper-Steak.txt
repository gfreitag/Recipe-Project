Title: Pepper Steak
Categories: Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Beef broth
1 cn Tomatoes
2 ts Soy sauce
1 1/2 tb Corn starch
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Shortening
1 lb Round steak
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 tb Onion, chopped
1 cl Garlic
2    Green peppers
Cut steak in 1 inch strips.  Brown in shortening.  Add all other
ingredients mixing corn starch with water thickening.  Cook at medium
heat for 1 hour.  My son enjoys this served over rice.
