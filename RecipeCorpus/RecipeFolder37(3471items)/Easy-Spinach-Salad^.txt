Title: Easy Spinach Salad^
Categories: Salads, Holiday, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
Toasted
Italian or poppy seed
Dressing
1 1/2 lb Spinach, washed and dried
2 cn (11oz) mandarin orange
Sections
1 pk (2.25oz) sliced almonds,
Tear spinach into bite sized pieces; combine with remaining
ingredients.
