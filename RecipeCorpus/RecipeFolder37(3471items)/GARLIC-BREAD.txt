Title: GARLIC BREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/2 ts Parmesan cheese
1    Loaf French or Vienna bread
1/3 c  Melted butter or margarine
1. Slice bread in 1/2-inch slices, but do not cut all the way through
to crust.
2. In a small bowl combine remaining ingredients until smooth.
3. Spread mixture between each slice and on the top of bread.
4. Place in bread basket or wrap in paper toweling.
5. Heat in Microwave Oven 30 to 45 seconds.
