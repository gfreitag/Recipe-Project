Title: UNION GRILL STRAWBERRY SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
Pinch cinnamon
1 ds Tabasco(optional)
2 pt Strawberries (1 pk frozen)
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Grenadine syrup
Throw the whole mess in the food processor/blender and hit the
ani-hiliate button, chill and drown what ever the intended
destination!

We us this recipe with the tabasco for a breakfast blintz we serve
for sunday brunch during the summer, and it worked VERY well
with this cheesecake as well
