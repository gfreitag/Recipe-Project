Title: Marinated Flank Steak^
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Dijon mustard
1 ts Pepper
1 cl Garlic, minced
2    (1lb) flank steaks
1 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/3 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
3 tb Worcestershire
Marinate meat in combined ingredients at least 6 hours or overnight
in a ziploc bag, turning occasionally. Discard marinade, retaining
onion slices.

Encase onions in aluminum foil; grill steaks and onions over medium
high coals

(350-400 degrees) about 15 minutes or till of desired doneness,
turning.

Cut across diasgonally into thin strips.
