Title: RITZ CRACKER TORTE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Sugar
21    Ritz crackers, crushed
3    Egg white
Combine crackers and nuts.  Beat egg whites to consistency of
meringue, fold in one cup sugar.  Combine all ingredients.  Bake in 8
or 9 inch pie pan for 30 mins at 350.  Cool.  Top with ice cream or
whipped cream.
