Title: NORSKE NOOK SOUR CREAM RAISIN PIE
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1    Egg white
1 1/2 c  Raisins
1    Pie shell;unbaked 9 inch
1/4 ts -Salt
2 c  Sour cream
2 1/4 c  Sugar
4 ts Flour
4    Egg;separated
Combine sour cream, 1 3/4 cup of the sugar, flour, egg yolks and
raisins in a heavy saucepan or top of double boiler and cook,
stirring constantly, until thick and glossy. Pour in baked pie shell
and chill. Put egg whites into a metal mixing bowl over a pan of hot
water. Stir in remaining 1/2 cup of sugar and salt. After a few
seconds, when whites are tepid and sugar is dissolved, whip until
they stand in peaks. Spread across pie, touching entire rim of crust,
and place under broiler until just barely browned (less than a
minute.)
