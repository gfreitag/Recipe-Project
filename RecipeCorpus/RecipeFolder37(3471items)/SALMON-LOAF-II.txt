Title: SALMON LOAF II
Categories: Main dish, Fish/seafoo, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Minced onion
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Dried parsley flakes
4 c  Cooked or canned salmon
2 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Milk
3 c  Soft bread crumbs
2    Eggs, beaten
1. In a large bowl sprinkle lemon juice over salmon.
2. In medium-sized bowl combine milk, bread crumbs, eggs, onion, salt,
pepper
and parsley flakes. Pour over salmon and blend well.
3. Pour into a greased, heat-resistant, non-metallic loaf pan.
4. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 12 minutes.
5. Cool 5 minutes. Place on serving platter.
