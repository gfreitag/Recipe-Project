Title: Orzo with Pesto and Peas
Categories: Side dish, Pasta/rice, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Prepared pesto sauce
Salt and pepper
1 c  Orzo
1 c  Frozen peas
1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling, salted water for 6-8
min. Add the peas and cook until the pasta in tender but still firm,
1-2 min longer. Drain well.

2. In a large bowl, toss the pasta and peas with the pesto and season
with salt and pepper to taste.
