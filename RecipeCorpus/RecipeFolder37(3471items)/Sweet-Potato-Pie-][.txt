Title: Sweet Potato Pie ][
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/4 ml Ground nutmeg
1 ea Egg; beaten
1 5/8 dl Milk
1/16 dl Vanilla
1 ea Pie shell, 9-inch, unbaked
5/8 kg Sweet potatoes or yams
13/16 dl Butter or margarine;softened
7/16 dl Granulated sugar
7/16 dl Brown sugar, packed
1 1/4 ml Salt
Cook potatoes in boiling salted water until tender. Drain, peel and
mash. Combine butter, sugars, salt and nutmeg in large mixer bowl.
Beat at medium speed until creamy. Beat in sweet potatoes until well
mixed. Beat in egg. Gradually add milk and vanilla and beat at low
speed. Spoon into unbaked pie shell. Bake at 350 degrees 50 to 60
minutes or until set. Cool to room temperature before serving. Store
in refrigerator until ready to serve. Source: Mary McLeod Bethune,
National Council of Negro Women founder. Recipe published in Los
Angeles Times, 2-27-92.
