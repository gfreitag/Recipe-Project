Title: Parmesan Wonton Chips^
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Snacks, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Grated Parmesan
18    Wonton wrappers
Cook half the wrappers at a time in a large pot of boiling water 1 1/2
minutes. Carefully remove with a skimmer and put in a bowl of cold
water.

Spray a cookie sheet with Pam. Arrange skins, side by side (still
wet) and spray lightly with Pam. Sprinkle with cheese. Repeat with
other half of skins.

Variation: Substitute Herbes de Provence for cheese.

Per serving:

79 calories, 0.6g (7%) fat, 1.3g carb, 2mg chol., 185mg sodium.
