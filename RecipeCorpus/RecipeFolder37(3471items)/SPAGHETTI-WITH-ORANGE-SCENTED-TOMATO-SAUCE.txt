Title: SPAGHETTI WITH ORANGE-SCENTED TOMATO SAUCE
Categories: Main dish, Italian, Pasta/rice, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
Salt
Juice of 1 orange
2 dr Tabasco sauce
1 lb Spaghetti
3 tb Olive oil
3 ea Garlic cloves, chopped
15 ea Fresh basil leaves
2 lb Ripe tomatoes, diced
Heat 2 tb olive oil with the garlic & half the basil leaves until the
garlic colours.  Add tomatoes & salt.  Cook over high heat until the
sauce thickens, 15 to 20 minutes.

Add orange juice, season with tabasco & continue to cook over high
heat for about another 5 minutes.  Check the seasonings.  Toss with
cooked spaghetti.

Marlena Spieler, "200+ Vegetarian Pasta Recipes"
