Title: Sweet Potatoes with Apricot Glaze^
Categories: Potatoes, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Grated lemon rind
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 c  Chopped pecans, toasted
Grarnish: orange-rind rose
And cruls
6 md Sweet potatoes (about 2 2/3
Pounds)
1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Apricot nectar
1/4 c  Butter or margar ine
1 ts Grated orange rind
For 10 mins. cook the sweet potatoes in boiling water or until tender.
Cool to touch; peel and cut into 1/4" slices.

Arrange potatoes in a lightly greased, 2 quart shallow baking dish;
set aside.

In a saucepan combine brown sugar and the next 5 ingredients; bring
to a boil,stirring often. boil for 1 mins. Drizzle mixture over sweet
potatoes.

If desired cover and refrigerate overnight. remove from refrigerate,
and let sit at room temperature for 30 mins.

Bake uncovered, at 350F. for 35 to 40 mins. or until thoroughly
heated. Sprinkle with pecans. If desired granish with orange-rind
rose and curls.
