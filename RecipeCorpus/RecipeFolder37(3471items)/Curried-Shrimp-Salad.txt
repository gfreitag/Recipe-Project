Title: Curried Shrimp Salad
Categories: Salads, Pasta/rice, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Cooked bay shrimp
2 tb Cilantro, chopped
Salt and pepper
1/2 lb Bow tie pasta
2 ts Curry powder
1/2 c  Italian
Or vinaigrette dressing
1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling, salted water until
tender but still firm, 8-9 min. Drain and rinse well in cold water.

2. While the pasta is cooking, combine the curry powder and salad
dressing and stir well.

3. In a medium bowl, stir together the pasta, dressing, shrimp, and
cilantro. Season with salt and pepper and toss well.
