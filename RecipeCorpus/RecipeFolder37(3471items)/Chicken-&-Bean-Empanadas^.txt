Title: Chicken & Bean Empanadas^
Categories: Appetizers, Holiday, Poultry, Vegetables
Yield: 40 servings
MMMMM------------------CHICKEN AND BEAN FILLING-----------------------
Cheese
1/4 c  Salsa verde or green slasa
1/8 ts Ground cumin
ds Or so Tabasco
1/2 c  Finely chopped cooked
Chicken
1/2 cn (15oz) black beans, rinsed,
Drained and mashed
1/2 c  Finely shredded cheddar
MMMMM---------------------------PASTRY--------------------------------
1    Beaten egg
1/2 c  Milk
1    Egg
1 tb Water
Salsa, sour cream, and/or
Guacamole dip (optional)
3 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Shredded monterey jack
Cheese with jalapeno
Peppers
3/4 c  Shortening or lard
Prepare filling. Combine all ingredients; set aside.

For Pastry, stir together flour and salt. Cut in cheese and
shortening till mixture resembles coarse cornmeal. Stir in egg and
milk until well combined.
Knead 10-12 strokes on a lightly floured surface; divide in half.

Roll out to 1/8". cut into 3" rounds with a floured cutter. Reroll to
make 20
rounds per half.

Place 1 teaspoon filling in center of each. Moisten edges with water,
fold in
half and seal edges with a fork. Prick several steam holes in each.
Brush with

combined egg and water; bake in preheated 425F. oven 15-18 minutes
until golden. Serve with sour cream, salsa, and guacamole, if desired.
