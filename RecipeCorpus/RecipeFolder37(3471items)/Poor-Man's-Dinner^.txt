Title: Poor Man's Dinner^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Potatoes, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
1 lg Onion, thinly sliced
2 cn Cream of mushroom or cheddar
Cheese soup
Fresh parsley
1 lb Ground beef
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
5 lg Potatoes, peeled and sliced
Brown beef seasoned with pepper and garlic powder. In casserole,
thinly layer the beef, potatoes and onions. Pour soup over; cover and
bake at 350F. until potatoes are tender, about an hour. Sprinkle with
parsley before serving.
