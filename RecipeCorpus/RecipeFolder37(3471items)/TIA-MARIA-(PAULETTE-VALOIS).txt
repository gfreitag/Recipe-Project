Title: TIA MARIA (PAULETTE VALOIS)
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 72 servings
1 1/4 qt Sugar
2 oz Antiqua coffee(Spice Island)
26 oz Brandy (el cheapo brand)
1    Vanilla bean
1 1/4 qt Water; boiling
Mix coffee and sugar well.  Pour in boiling water, mix well, let
stand until cool.  Add brandy, cut vanilla bean lengthwise and drop
into bottle.  Let stand 30 days.  Make approx 3 1/2 bottles (90 oz)
