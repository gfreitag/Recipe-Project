Title: Meat & Potato Squares^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Potatoes, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------MEAT MIXTURE-----------------------------
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Bread crumbs
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 pk (16oz) frozen shoestring
Potatoes
1 lb Ground beef
1    Egg
1/4 c  Milk
1 ts Salt
1 ts Prepared mustard
Combine meat mixture; set aside.

Press half the potatoes in a greased 8" square pan. Spread evenly with
meat mixture. Press with remaining potatoes. Bake at 400F a half hour
or until meat is cooked and potatoes are lightly browned. Cut into
squares.
