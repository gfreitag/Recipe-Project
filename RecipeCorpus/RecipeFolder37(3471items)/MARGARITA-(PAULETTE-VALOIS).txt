Title: MARGARITA (PAULETTE VALOIS)
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Lime or Lemon juice; fresh
1 1/2 oz Tequila
1/2 oz Triple sec
Moisten cocktail glass rim with fruit rind and spin rim in salt.
Shake ingredients with cracked ice.  Strain into glass.  Sip over
salted rim.

Source: The Rochon Family Cookbook (1990)
From: Paulette Valois
