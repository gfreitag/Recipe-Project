Title: Indian Scrambled Eggs With Ginger
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs, Indian
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Butter
6    Eggs
1/4 c  Milk
Salt and pepper to taste
4    Green chiles; skinned
-seeded, chopped fine
1 ts Fresh ginger; chop fine
4 tb Onions; finely chopped
Saute onion and ginger in butter until onion is soft.
Beat the eggs, milk, salt and pepper together and pour into skillet
with the onions and ginger.
Sprinkle the chiles on top and scramble over low heat until the eggs
are done.
