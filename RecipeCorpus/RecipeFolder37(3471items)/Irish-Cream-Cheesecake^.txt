Title: Irish Cream Cheesecake^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 16 servings
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
6 tb Melted butter
1 3/4 c  Finely crushed chocolate
Wafers or graham crackers
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/3 c  To 1/2 Bailey's*
3/4 c  Mini chocolate chips
1 ts Glour
1 c  Sugar
3 pk Cream cheese, (24oz),
Softened
4    Eggs
MMMMM---------------------GARNISH---OPTIONAL--------------------------
Chocolate curls
Whipped cream
Preheat oven to 375F.

Combine crust ingredients and press onto bottom and up sides of
lightly greased 9" springform pan; set aside.

Beat cream cheese and sugar until very smooth and creamy. Do not
overbeat. Stir in Bailey's by hand. Gently stir in combined teaspoon
flour and 1/2 cup chips.; stir into batter gently. Sprinkle remaining
chips over.

Bake 45-55 minutes or until firm to the touch. Loosen sides of
cheesecake by running a knife around edge of pan. Cover cake; chill
at least 3 hours. Pipe with whipped cream and top with chocolate
curls, if desired, before serving.
