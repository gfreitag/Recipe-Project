Title: CORN WHEELS WITH LIME BUTTER
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-------------------HELEN MEADOWS, MONTANA------------------------
2 tb Fresh lime juice
1/2 ts Pepper (optional)
4 md Ears of corn
2 tb Butter or margarine
Using a cleaver or large knove, cut corn into 1-in lengths. In a
large pot, bring water to boil over high heat. Add corn wheels; cover
and boil 2 minutes. Melt butter, mix with lime juice. Transfer corn
to serving platter and drizzle with lime-butter. Sprinkle with
pepper, if desired.
