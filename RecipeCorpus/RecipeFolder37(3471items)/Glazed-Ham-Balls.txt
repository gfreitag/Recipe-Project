Title: Glazed Ham Balls
Categories: Holiday, Appetizers, Meats
Yield: 48 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 lb Ground fully cooked ham
1/2 lb Lean ground beef
2/3 c  Red currant jelly
2 tb Prepared mustard
Green onion slivers
1    Beaten eggs
3/4 c  Soft bread crumbs (1 slice)
1/3 c  Dried tart red cherries or
Dried cranberries, finely
Snipped
1/4 c  Finely chopped green onion
Combine all ingredients except jelly, mustard and green onion
slivers. Form into 1" balls.

Bake at 350F. on a jelly roll pan 15-18 minutes or until done; drain
if needed.

Heat jelly and mustard together until jelly melts. Stir in ham balls;
keep warm. Garnish with green onion slivers.
