Title: Butternut Squash Puree with Cranberries
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Light brown sugar; packed
Salt and pepper to taste
3 1/2 lb Butternut squash
12 oz Fresh cranberries
1/2 c  Sweet butter
Peel and seed the squash;  cut into 1-inch pieces.  Cook in a pot of
boiling, lightly salted water for 15 minutes.  Add the cranberries and
continue to cook until the squash is very tender and the cranberries
burst, about 10 minutes.  Drain the squash and cranberries well.
Combine the squash, cranberries, butter, and brown sugar in the bowl
of an electric mixer or food processor and process until the mixture
is pureed.

If necessary, this dish can be refrigerated overnight and reheated.
Season with salt and pepper and serve hot.
From Yankee Magazine's Christmas in New England, 1995
