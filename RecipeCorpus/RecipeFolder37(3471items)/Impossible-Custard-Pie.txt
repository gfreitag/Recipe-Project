Title: Impossible Custard Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
4    Eggs
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Flaked coconut
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Baking powder
1 pn Salt
3 tb Soft unsalted butter
Preheat oven to 400.

In blender, combine all ingredients. Blend for about a minute.  Place
in buttered 9 inch pie plate.  Sprinkle with flaked or shredded
coconut.  Bake until knife inserted 1 inch from edge comes out clean
(about 30 minutes).
