Title: SHIRRED EGGS (BAKED)
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Breakfast, British isl
Yield: 3 servings
3    English muffins, toasted
Butter
Salt
Pepper
6    Eggs
6 tb Dry Sherry
6 tb Light Cream
6    Bacon strips
Preheat oven to 350 deg.
Butter the insides of 6 muffin cups in a large muffin tin.
Cook bacon until most of the fat is rendered out but bacon is still
limp. Place 1 slice each into the bottom of the buttered muffin cups
(curl around so that bottom is completely covered.  Crack 1 egg into
each muffin cup.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Dot with butter.
Place 1 tb each of Dry Sherry and Light cream into each muffin cup.
Bake in 350 deg. oven 10 to 15 minutes or until egg is of desired
doneness.
Serve on halves of English muffins.
