Title: Glazed Julienne of Carrots~ Turnips & Rutabagas^
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 lb Rutabaga, julienned
2 c  Water
3/4 lb Carrots, julienned
3/4 lb Turnip, julienned
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
3 tb Butter
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Water
Bring vegetables and water to a boil; reduce heat, cover and simmer
10-12 minutes until crisp-tender; drain.

Combine sauce ingredients; bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Toss
gently with vegetables, serve immediately.
