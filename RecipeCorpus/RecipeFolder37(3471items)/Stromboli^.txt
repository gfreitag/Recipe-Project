Title: Stromboli^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Main dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 16 servings
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1/2 ts Salt
4 c  To 5 flour
1 pk Yeast
1 1/3 c  Warm ater
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/2 lb Thinly sliced ham
1 lb Shredded mozzarella
Vegetable oil
Oregano to taste
3 tb Prepared mustard
12 sl American cheese
12 sl Hard salami
1 1/2 lb Ground beef, browned and
Drained
Preheat oven to 400F.

Dissolve yeast in water. Stir in oil, salt and flour. Turn our and
knead until smooth and elastic, about 10 minutes. Place in greased
bowl; turn to grease top. Cover and let rise until doubled.

Divide dough in half and roll out to 15X12". Layer  half with: half
mustard, cheese, salami, beef, ham and mozzarella. Fold over and seal
ends well. Repeat with other dough and ingredients.

Bake until lightly browned, 25-30 minutes. Cool slightly before
cutting into 1" slices.
