Title: Apple Lasagne
Categories: Pasta/rice, Desserts
Yield: 14 servings
6 tb All-purpose flour
6 tb Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Uncooked quick-cooking oats
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon (optional)
1 ds Ground nutmeg (optional)
3 tb Margarine
1 c  Sour cream
1/3 c  Packed brown sugar
8    Lasagne noodles
2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 c  Ricotta cheese
1    Egg -- lightly beaten
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1 ts Almond extract
2 cn Apple pie filling (20 oz
- each)
1. Cook lasagne noodles according to package directions. Drain in
colander.

2. Preheat oven to 350 deg F. Grease 13 x 9 inch baking pan.

3. Combine Cheddar cheese, ricotta cheese, egg, granulated sugar and
almond extract in medium bowl; blend well.

4. Spread 1 can apple pie filling over bottom of prepared pan. Layer
half the noodles over filling, then spread cheese mixture over
noodles. Top with remaining noodles, then remaining can of apple pie
filling.

5. Combine flour, 6 tablespoons brown sugar, oats, cinnamon and
nutmeg in small bowl. Cut in margarine until crumbly. Sprinkle over
apple pie filling.

6. Bake lasagne 45 minutes. Cool 15 minutes.

7. Meanwhile, prepare garnish by blending sour cream and 1/3 cup brown
sugar in small bowl until smooth. Cover, refrigerate.

8. To serve, cut lasagne into squares and garnish with sour cream
mixture. Makes 12 to 16 servings.

Source: Cooking Class, No. 8, Pasta
