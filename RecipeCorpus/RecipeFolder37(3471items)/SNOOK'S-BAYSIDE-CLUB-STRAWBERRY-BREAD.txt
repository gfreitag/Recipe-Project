Title: SNOOK'S BAYSIDE CLUB STRAWBERRY BREAD
Categories: Breads, Desserts
Yield: 2 loaves
1 1/4 lb Fresh strawberries; sliced
-OR-
-Unsweetened frozen berries,
- thawed, thoroughly drained
- and coarsely chopped
1/2 c  Walnuts, coarsely chopped
2    8-1/2 x 4-1/2-inch loaf pans
3 c  All-purpose flour
2 c  Sugar
3 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 1/4 c  Vegetable oil
4    Eggs
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease and flour the two loaf pans.

Mix the flour, sugar, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt in a large
mixing bowl.  Add the oil, eggs, strawberries, and walnuts.  Beat
until the dry ingredients are just moist.  Pour into the prepared
loaf pans and bake for 1 hour.  The breads are done when they start
to come away from the sides of the pan and an inserted wooden pick
comes out clean.
