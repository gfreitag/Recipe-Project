Title: Russian Tea Mix
Categories: Russian, Beverages, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 c  Tang
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Instant tea
2 qt Presweetened lemonade
Mix all ingredients together and store in a dry cupboard, it will keep
for a long time if kept dry.

To make the tea; mix 2 teaspoons of the dry mix in a cup of hot water
stir until dissolved.
