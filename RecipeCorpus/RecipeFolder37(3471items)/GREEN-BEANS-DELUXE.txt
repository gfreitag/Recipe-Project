Title: GREEN BEANS DELUXE
Categories: Main dish, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 6 servings
8 oz Can French fried onion rings
32 oz Canned cut green beans
10 1/2 oz Can cream of mushroom soup
1. In a deep, 1 1/2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole,
combine cut green beans and soup. Blend well.
2. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Sprinkle top of bean mixture with onion rings and heat, un-
covered, in Microwave Oven an additional 3 minutes or until onions
are heated through.
