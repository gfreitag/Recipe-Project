Title: Marinated Mushroom Dressing
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 10 servings
2 ts Pepper
8    Cloves garlic,finely chopped
2 ts Accent
2 lb Med. white mushrooms, sliced
2 c  Olive oil
1 c  Red wine vinegar
4 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl; cover.  Refrigerate for 3
days. Stir occasionally.  Remove from the refrigerator several hours
before serving.  Stir well.

Source:  The American Country Inn and Bed & Breakfast Cookbook.
