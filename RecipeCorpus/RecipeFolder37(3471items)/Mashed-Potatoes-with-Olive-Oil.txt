Title: Mashed Potatoes with Olive Oil
Categories: Potatoes, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
4    Garlic clove(s), peeled
1/2 c  Extra-virgin olive oil
6 tb Butter
3 lb (to 3 1/2 lb)
Yukon Gold potatoes
Peeled, cut in 1/2" pieces
Cook potatoes and garlic in large saucepan of boiling salted water
until tender, about 15 min. Drain, reserving  1/2 cup cooking liquid.
Place potatoes and garlic in large bowl. Add oil and butter and mash
until smooth, adding some reserved cooking liquid if necessary to
moisten. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
