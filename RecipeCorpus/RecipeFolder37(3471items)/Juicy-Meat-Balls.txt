Title: Juicy Meat Balls
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ds Worcestershire sauce
1 lb Ground chuck
3 c  Corn flakes, crushed
1 cn Campbells onion soup
Mix well, form into balls.  Put in muffin tins and bake in a 350
degree preheated oven for 1/2 hour.

The source of this recipe is unknown, which is
irrelevant, and untested but posted nevertheless
for the enjoyment of others.
