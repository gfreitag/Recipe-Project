Title: EGG BURRITO
Categories: Tex/mexican, Cheese/eggs, Main dish, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
Refried beans
Chili Verde
Mozzarella, Cheddar and
- Monterey Jack cheese,
- shredded, as desired
Flour Tortilla
2    Eggs
1/4 ts Buttermilk
Salt
Pepper
1    Strip of bacon, cooked crisp
Heat tortilla on hot grill.  Beat eggs with buttermilk, salt and
pepper; scramble until set but not hard.  Spread scrambled eggs on
warmed tortilla.  Top with a strip of crisply cooked bacon, refied
beans, Chili Verde.  Sprinkle heavily with cheeses; roll up.  Smother
with Chili Verde, if desired.  Buzz in microwave oven until cheese is
Cooking time: 2
melted. Preparation time: 15 minutes
minutes
