Title: Tortelloni with Tomato-Butter Sauce
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
Very finely minced
4 tb Butter
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Fresh ground pepper
18 oz Fresh cheese tortelloni
1 cn (28 oz) peeled
Plum tomatoes
1 1/2 c  Basic, loosely packed
1 sm Onion
1. Cook the tortelloni in a large pot of boiling, salted water until
tender but still firm, 7-8 min. Drain well.

2. While the pasta is cooking, puree the tomatoes in a blender or food
processor until smooth. Shred enough of the basil to make 1 cup.
Finely mince the onion.

3. In a large frying pan, melt 3 tbs butter over medium-high heat
until bubbly but not brown. Add the onion and cook until translucent
and lightly browned, 2-3 min.

3. Add the tomatoes to the pan and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to
medium-high, cover and cook for 6 min. Season with salt and pepper.
Stir the remaining butter and basil into the sauce. Toss the sauce
with the cooked tortelloni and serve.
