Title: MIMOSA CRYSTALS
Categories: Candies, Holiday, Information
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Corn syrup
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Flowers
1 oz Gum arabic
1 c  Water
Dry the flowers. Dissolve gum arabic in 1/2 cup hot water and cool.
Carefully coat the petals with this mixture.  Put them on wax paper
so they will not touch and mix the corn syrup and sugar in 1/2 cup
water and cook to a soft ball.  Dip petals into syrup and dry.
Sprinkle with finely granulated sugar.

* Source: Flower Cookery (The Art of Cooking with Flowers) * by Mary
MacNicol * Fleet Press Corporation - New York * Library of Congress
