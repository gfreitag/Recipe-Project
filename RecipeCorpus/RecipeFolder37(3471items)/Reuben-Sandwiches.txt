Title: Reuben Sandwiches
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 3 servings
Sauerkraut
Thousand island dressing
Swiss cheese
Corn beef, boiled
Rye bread
Butter
Slice and butter the bread.  Place beef on the bread.  Duh!  Then
place some well drained sauerkraut and thousand island dressing on
it.  Top with a slice or two of Swiss cheese and broil or you can
grill it.
