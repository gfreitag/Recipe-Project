Title: SAVORY TRADITIONAL BREAD STUFFING
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Poultry seasoning
1/4 ts Freshly ground pepper
Salt to taste
1/4 lb Butter
4 tb Finely chopped onion
4 tb Finely chopped celery
4 c  Dry bread crumbs
Melt the butter in a skillet and stir in the onion and celery.  Cook
over low heat until the onion is soft.  Add this mixture to the
crumbs and toss lightly with poultry seasoning and plenty of pepper
and salt. Stuff a 12-15 pound turkey.
