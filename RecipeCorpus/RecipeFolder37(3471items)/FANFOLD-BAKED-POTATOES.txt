Title: FANFOLD BAKED POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Green onions, sliced thin
4 tb Cheddar, grated
2 tb Parmesan
4    Potatoes
2 tb Marg, melted
1 tb Parsley flakes
1 ts Salt
Directions: Peel potatoes, rinse and pat dry. Cut into thin slices
but not all the way through. Spread to make a fan. Place in a
micro-wave dish and sprinkle with melted marg, parsley, salt and
green onions. Microwave on high for 10 minutes, re-arranging after 5
minutes.  Let rest for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with the cheeses.
Microwave another 5 minutes until cheese is melted and potatoes soft.
