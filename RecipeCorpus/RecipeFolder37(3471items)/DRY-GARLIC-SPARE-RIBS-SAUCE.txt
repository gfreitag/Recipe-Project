Title: DRY GARLIC SPARE RIBS SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
3    Cloves garlic
3 tb Soya sauce
1 c  Brown Sugar
1 c  Water
Cut spare ribs into serving size pieces and boil until tender. In
saucepan mix water brown sugar garlic and soya sauce, bring to a boil
and add the spare ribs and boil another 20 minutes.
