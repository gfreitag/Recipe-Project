Title: Smoky Barbecue Ribs
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Crockpot
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Gingerroot, fresh, grated
1/2 ts Liquid smoke
1 c  Chili sauce
1/4 c  Green onions; chopped
4 lb Spareribs, cut in single
-ribs
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Soy sauce
1/4 ts Dried red pepper flakes
Use chili sauce that is similar to ketchup, not the hot spicy variety
with chile peppers.

In medium bowl, combine all ingredients except ribs.  Dip each rib
into sauce.  Place coated ribs in cooker.  Pour remaining sauce over
ribs in pot.  Cover and cook on LOW 5-6 hours or until meat is tender.

Per serving: Cal 542; Prot 36g;  Carb 17g; Fat 36g; Chol 143mg;
Sodium 948mg

Crockery Favorites,  Mable Hoffman
