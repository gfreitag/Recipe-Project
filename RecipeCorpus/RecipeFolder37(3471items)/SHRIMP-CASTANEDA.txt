Title: SHRIMP CASTANEDA
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Butter
Salt to taste
1 1/2 lb Fresh shrimp
1 ts Ginger, minced
1 md Onion, sliced
Sauce: 1 cup soy sauce 1/4 cup sherry 2 squeezes of fresh lemon juice

Saute onions and ginger in butter.  When the onions are slightly
cooked, add the shrimp.  Stir fry for 2 minutes.  Add salt and I use
a couple of dashes of Accent.  Mix soy sauce, lemon juice and sherry
in a bowl.  Pour sauce over cooked shrimp while still in hot pan.
