Title: DRY GARLIC SPARE RIB SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
3    Cloves garlic
3 tb Soya sauce
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Water
Put all in saucepan and bring to boil. Add spareribs that were boiled
until tender and bring back to boil. Very Good and simple. This can
be doubled or tripled of course depending on amt. of ribs to be
served.
