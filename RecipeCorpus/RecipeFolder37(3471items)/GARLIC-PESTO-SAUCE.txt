Title: GARLIC PESTO SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Parmesan cheese, grated
1 tb Olive oil
Salt & pepper to taste
3/4 c  Chicken broth (low fat)
1/2 c  Basil leaves (fresh -
-loosely packed)
3    Garlic cloves peeled
Heat broth to boiling. Combine ingredients in blender or food
processor; process until basil and garlic are minced. (Or by hand,
use a mortar and pestle: Mash basil and garlic together, then mix
with remaining ingredients.)
