Title: MOM'S CORNED BEEF HASH AND CABBAGE
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1 ts Fat
1 cn Corned beef
4 c  Potatoes, diced
3 tb Onion, minced
Salt and pepper  Cabbage

Brown onion in fat; add corned beef and break up. (this works better
if can has been chilled first) Add cooked potatoes, milk and
seasonings. Simmer for 30 min.  Prepare cabbage and cook.  Serve hash
and cabbage together.
