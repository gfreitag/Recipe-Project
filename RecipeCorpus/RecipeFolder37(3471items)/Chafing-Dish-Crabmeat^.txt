Title: Chafing Dish Crabmeat^
Categories: Appetizers, Holiday, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Garlic powder
2 ds Hot sauce
1 lb Fresh crabmeat, drained and
Flaked
Mebla toast or toast points
2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Capers
1 tb Prepared horseadish
1 ts Grated lemon rind
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
In a saucepan combine the first 7 ingredients. Stir in crabmeat
gently. Cover and chill up to 1 day, if desired. Cook over low heat,
stirring gently, until thoroughly heated.( Do not boil.) Pour mixture
into a chafing dish and keep warm. Serve with melba toast.
