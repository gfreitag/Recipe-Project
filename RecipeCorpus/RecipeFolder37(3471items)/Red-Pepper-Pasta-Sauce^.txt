Title: Red Pepper Pasta Sauce^
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetables, Pasta/rice, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Salt
ds Hot pepper sauce
1 tb To 2 chopped fresh basil
3 c  Chopped red bell pepper
1 cl Galric, minced
1 c  Chicken broth
1 tb Tomato paste
Bring all to a boil; reduce heat and simmer until slightly thickened,
about 20 minutes. Process in blender or processor until smooth. Serve
over hot cooked pasta. Garnish with additional basil, if desired.

Microwave directions:
Microwave covered, the pepper, onion and garlic 4-4 1/2 minutes until
vegetables are tender. Add 1/2 cup chicken broth, tomato paste, salt
and hot pepper sauce. Cook, uncovered, on high 2-3 minutes. Continue
as above.

Per half cup serving:
35 cal., 2g pro., 6g carb., 1g fiber, 1g/26% fat, 360mg sodium, 240mg
potassium, 1 vegetable exchange.
