Title: GLAZED BELGIAN CARROTS
Categories: Vegetables, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Honey
1/4 ts To 1/2 t ground ginger
1 lb Frozen Belgian carrots
6 tb Butter or margarine
1. Place frozen carrots in a deep, 1 1/2 quart, heat-resistant,
non-metallic
casserole.
2. Heat, covered, 2 minutes in Microwave Oven. Add remaining
ingredients. 3. Uncover and heat an additional 4 minutes or until
carrots are tender. Let
stand for 1 to 2 minutes before serving.
