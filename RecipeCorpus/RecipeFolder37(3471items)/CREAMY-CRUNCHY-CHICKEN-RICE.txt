Title: CREAMY CRUNCHY CHICKEN RICE
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Cooked, hot rice
1 cn 10 oz. green peas
1 pk 8 oz. cheese spread
2 c  Cooked chicken
1 cn Fried onions
Combine cheese spread and rice. Add chicken, peas and 1/2 onion.
Place in casserole and bake at 350 F for 15 minutes. Top with
remaining onions and bake another 5 minutes.
