Title: BROWN SUGAR KISSES
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 30 servings
- sieve with wooden spoon
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Coarsely chopped pecans
1    Egg white
1 c  Brown sugar, sifted by
- forcing through coarse
Beat egg white until stiff but not dry.  Add brown sugar and continue
beating until thoroughly mixed. Add vanilla and pecans and stir.
Drop by teaspoon on a greased cookie sheet and bake for 5-6 minutes
in 400F oven. Allow to cook for 10 minutes before removing from
cookie sheet.
