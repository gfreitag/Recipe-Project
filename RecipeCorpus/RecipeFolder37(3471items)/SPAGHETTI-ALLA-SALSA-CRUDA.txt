Title: SPAGHETTI ALLA SALSA CRUDA
Categories: Pasta/rice, Main dish, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Fresh basil, rough chopped
Extra virgin olive oil
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 lb Spaghetti
6    Tomatoes, fresh, ripe
Parsley, fresh, chopped
2 cl Garlic, large, chopped
Wash the tomatoes, then cut into small pieces and set in a colander to
drain.
In a large saucepan containing 7 pints(6L) of salted water, cook the
pasta al
dente. Drain the spaghetti in a colander
Put the tomatoes, basil, garlic, parsley and a little oil into a large
serving bowl. Add the hot pasta and mix gently to blend all the
ingredients thoroughly. Add more oil, salt, plenty of pepper and stir
gently again. Serve
at room temperature.
