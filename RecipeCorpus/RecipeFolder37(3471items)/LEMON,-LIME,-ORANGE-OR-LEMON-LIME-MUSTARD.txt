Title: LEMON, LIME, ORANGE OR LEMON-LIME MUSTARD
Categories: Condiments, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Honey
Grated zest of lemon, lime
-or orange peel
1 c  Dijon-style mustard
1 tb Fresh lemon, lime or
-orange juice
Mix all in a medium bowl. Transfer to a jar with a tight-fitting lid.
Store in a cool dark place for 2 weeks, then refrigerate.

Makes 2 tb per "serving"

Recipe by Helene Sawyer in  "Gourmet Mustards" ISBN # 0-914667-07-6
Published by Culinary Arts Ltd.
