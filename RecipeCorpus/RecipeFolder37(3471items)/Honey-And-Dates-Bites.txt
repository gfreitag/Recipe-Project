Title: Honey And Dates Bites
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 30 servings
3 tb Honey
1 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  Dates
2 c  Chopped walnuts
Chop dates and walnuts very fine.  Mix dates with half walnuts
portion, honey, and lemon juice.  Roll into 1 in. "balls" , then roll
into rest of walnuts.  Keep in fridge.
Makes 2 1/2 dozen.

Note: nuts and dates must be real fine: suggested to use the meat
grinder in book).

Eleonore Beaudoin   3 Nov 1994
