Title: Cider Snap ( Mw )
Categories: Microwave, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Thin Apple Slices (Opt.)
1 c  Apple Cider Or Juice
2 ts Red Cinnamon Candies
In a nonmetal mug combine apple cider and cinnamon candies.
Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power for 1 1/2 to 2 minutes or till
candies dissolve and the cider is steamining hot, stirring once.
Garnish with apple slices, if desired.
