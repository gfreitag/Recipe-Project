Title: CREAMED SPINACH III
Categories: Vegetables, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Nutmeg
6 oz Cream cheese, at room temp.
Milk (optional)
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
20 oz Frozen chopped spinach
1/2 ts Salt
1. In a small heat-resistant, non-metallic bowl melt butter in
Microwave Oven 30 seconds.
2. Add onions and heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 4 minutes
or until onion is lightly browned. Stir occasionally.
3. Cook spinach. Drain well.
4. Combine browned onion, spinach, salt, pepper, nutmeg and cream
cheese in a shallow, 1 1/2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic
casserole. If desired, add milk to make a thinner mixture.
5. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 5 to 6 minutes or until heated
through.
