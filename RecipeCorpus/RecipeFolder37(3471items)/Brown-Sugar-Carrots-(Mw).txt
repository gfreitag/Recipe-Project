Title: Brown Sugar Carrots (Mw)
Categories: Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
Brown sugar
Carrots
This is a very simple way to prepare carrots.  Slice them crosswise
into small rounds and place in a microwave dish.  Add a tablespoon or
two of brown sugar and microwave until cooked.  No water, no butter,
no salt. Simple and delicious.
