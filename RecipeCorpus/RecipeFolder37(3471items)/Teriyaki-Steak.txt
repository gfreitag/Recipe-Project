Title: Teriyaki Steak
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 3 servings
1    Sirloin steak
1/4 c  Soy sauce
3    Portions of garlic-in press
2/3 c  Dry Concord wine
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Ginger
Marinate steak in juices; turn over after 12 hours on each side.  This
recipe is meant to be cooked on the grill.  Save some juices to put
on the meat while grilling.
