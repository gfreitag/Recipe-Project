Title: FANTASTIC ARTICHOKE DIP
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1    Onion; finely chopped
2    Cl Garlic; finely chopped
2    Jars Marinated artichoke hea
2    Of pita bread
8 oz Cream cheese
12 oz Shredded mozzarella
1 c  Mayonaise
1 c  Grated parmasean
Drain artichokes well and tear them apart with your fingers.
Cut up pita bread into chip size triangles, seperate and bake on a
cookie sheet until crispy (approx 5 minutes).
Combine all other ingredients and mash.
Bake in a uncovered casserole dish for approx 30 minutes @ 350 or
until bubbly.
Serve hot out of the oven and scoop up dip with pita bread.
