Title: Fried Ginger
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
Oil
Salt
1    3" piece peeled ginger
2 tb Flour
Julienne the ginger into very thin matchstick pieces. Toss with the
flour. Heat 1-inch of oil until hot in a small skillet over medium
heat. Fry ginger until crisp, about 1 min. Drain and salt while warm.

Use as a garnish.
