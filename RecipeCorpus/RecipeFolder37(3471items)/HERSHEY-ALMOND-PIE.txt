Title: HERSHEY ALMOND PIE
Categories: Pie/tart, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 pt Heavy cream, whipped
1    9 inch pie shell, cooled
-or graham cracker shell
6    Hershey almond bars
1/2 c  Milk
15    Marshmallows (about 1/4 lb)
Chop almond bars into small pieces and melt in double boiler with
milk. Add marshmallows and stir until melted.  Chill mixture
thoroughly.  Fold cream into chocolate mixture and pour into cooled
pie shell.  Chill until firm (over night for best results).  Top with
whipped cream and serve.
