Title: Clam Fritters^
Categories: Appetizers, Fish/seafoo, Holiday
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1    Egg, beaten
Oil for deep frying
Lemon slices
Cocktail sauce, optional
1 cn (6 1/2oz) minced clams
Milk
1 c  Flour
1/4 c  Cornmeal
2 ts Baking powder
Drain clams, reserving liquid. Add milk to equal 2/3 cup. Combine dry
ingredients. Combine milk, clams, and egg. Stir into dry ingredients
just until moistened.

Heat oil to 365F. Drop batter by rounded teasoons into fat and fry
until golden brown, about 2 minutes. Garnish with lemon and serve
with cocktail sauce, if desired.
