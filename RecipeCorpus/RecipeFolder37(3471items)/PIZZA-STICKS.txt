Title: PIZZA STICKS
Categories: Breads, Pizza, Main dish, Appetizers
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/2 c  Pizza sauce, heated
1 cn 11 oz Pillsbury Soft
-breadsticks
24    Thin pepperoni slices
-1 1/2" diameter
Separate and unroll breadsticks.  Place 3 pepperoni slices in single
layer over one half of each breadstick. Fold remaining half of
breadstick over top; seal end and twist.  Place on ungreased cookie
sheet. Combine cheese, Italian seasoning and garlic powder. Sprinkle
evenly over each breadstick.

Bake in preheated 350 degree F. oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until
golden brown.  Serve with pizza sauce. Makes 8 pizza sticks.
