Title: Huevos Rancheros
Categories: Tex/mexican, Cheese/eggs, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Freshly ground pepper
1/4 c  White wine
6 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
1/4 c  Sliced ripe olives
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
Saute onion, green pepper
-and garlic in oil.
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped green pepper
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
3 tb Olive oil
1 tb All purpose flour
2 ea (16 oz) cans tomatoes,
-drained and chopped
1/2 ts Dried whole oregano
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Chili powder
Stir in flour and cook 1 minute.  Add next 7 ingredients; cook over
medium heat 5 minutes. Pour sauce into a 12x8x2 inch baking dish. At
this point you can continue or set into refrigerator awaiting the
appointed hour.  Remove from fridge and a hour before serving time.
Make 6 indentations in sauce, and break an egg into each.  Sprinkle
with cheese, black olives and green onions.  Bake at 350~ for 15
minutes or until eggs are set.
Serve immediately.  Serves 6.
