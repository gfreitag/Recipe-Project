Title: LADYFINGERS
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 14 cakes
1/3 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Orange flavoring
1/4 ts Salt
3    Egg white; stiffly beaten
2    Egg yolk; beaten until
-thick
1/3 c  Cake flour
Sift sugar.  Measure.  Fold gradually into egg whites.  Carefully fold
in egg yolks which have been combined with flavorings and salt.  Sift
flour.  Measure and sift carefully.  Fold into mixture.  Cover a
baking sheet with un-oiled paper.  Shape the lady fingers with a
pastry tube having each about 4 inches long, and 1 inch wide.  Bake
in moderate oven (375 F) 10 minutes.  As soon as removed from oven
slip from baking sheet, using a wide spatula.  Place the bottom of
one lady finger on the bottom of another.  Lay on waxed paper.  Cool.
Dust with powdered sugar.
