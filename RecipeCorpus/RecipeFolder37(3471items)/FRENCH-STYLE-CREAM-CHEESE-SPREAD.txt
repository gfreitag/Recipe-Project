Title: FRENCH-STYLE CREAM CHEESE SPREAD
Categories: Tasteofhome, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Worchestershire sauce
1 ts Herbes de Provence, crushed
1/4 ts Seasoning salt
Fresh thyme (optional)
Assorted crackers
1 pk 8 oz soft cream cheese
1/4 c  Margarine or butter, soft
1    Clove garlic, minced
1 tb Snipped parsley
1 tb Water
2 ts White wine vinegar
1.  In a medium bowl, stir together the cream cheese, margarine or
butter, garlic, parsley, water, vinegar, Worchestershire sauce,
Herbes de Provence, and seasoning salt. Cover and refrigerate over-
night.

2.  To serve, garnish with fresh thyme, if desired. Serve with
crackers.

======================================================================
Herbes de Provence
In a small storage container, combine 1 T dried marjoram, 1 T dried
thyme, 1 T dried savory, 1 t dried basil, 1 t dried rosemary, 1/2 t
dried sage, and 1/2 t dried fennel seed. Store in airtight container.
Crush before using.
======================================================================

Makes:  1 1/3 cups
Source:  Good Housekeeping Magazine, Dec. 1992

Calories:  58 per tablespoon; 6 gm fat; 1 gm protein; 72 mg sodium
