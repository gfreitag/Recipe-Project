Title: TONY'S SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Condiments, Salads
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Oregano chopped
1    Garlic clove minced fine
2 tb Fresh basil minced
2 ts Soy sauce
1/3 c  Chablis
1/3 c  Balsamic vinegar
3 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1 ts Salt
3 ts Black pepper fresh ground
1/4 c  Olive oil extra lite
Mix all ingredients into the oil. Stir well. regrifgerate for 2 days.
Shake well and pour liberally over salad. Origin: Tony
D'Olivo.....Tampa, fl. cieca 1990
