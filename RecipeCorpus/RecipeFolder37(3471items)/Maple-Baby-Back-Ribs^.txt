Title: Maple Baby Back Ribs^
Categories: Meats, Appetizers, Holiday
Yield: 20 servings
4 lb Pork baby back ribs
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
2 ts Soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Few dashes Tabasco
3/4 c  Maple syrup
1/4 c  Frozen apple juice
Concentrate, thawed
1/4 c  Green onion, thinly sliced
1 tb Brown sugar
Have the meat man cut the ribs in half across the bone. Cut into
appetizer size pieces. Roast on a rack at 350F. an hour and 15
minutes. Drain.

Combine glaze ingredients. Heat to boiling; reduce heat and simmer 1
minute.

Brush ribs with glaze. Roast 25-30 more minutes, brushing with glaze.
