Title: PECAN COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Flour
2 c  Pecan pieces
1 c  Butter
2 tb Water
5 tb Powdered sugar
Soften butter and mix with electric mixer all ingredients except the
pecans.  Add pecans and spoon onto unbuttered cookie sheet.  Bake at
350 degrees for 20 minutes.  Frosting: Using powdered sugar, milk and
a little vanilla, mix for a drizzling consistency.  Drizzle over
cookies. Randy Rigg
