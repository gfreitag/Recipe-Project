Title: Lady Fingers 4 รก
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 20 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract
3/4 c  Sifted cake flour
Confectioners' sugar
pn Salt
4    Egg whites
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
4    Egg yolks
2 ts Lemon juice
Beat egg whites and dash of salt until soft peaks form.  Gradually
add 1/4 cup of granulated sugar, beating until stiff, but not dry.
In small mixing bowl, beat yolks until light and fluffy.  Gradually
add remaining granulated sugar; beat until thick and lemon-colored.
Beat in lemon juice and vanilla.  Fold yolks into whites.  By hand,
fold in flour.  Line cookie sheet with plain brown paper.  Insert
straight-sided 1/2 inch round tip in plastic bag; fill with batter.
Pipe 4 x 1 inch strips 1 inch apart on brown paper.  Or, spoon into
ungreased lady finger baking pans.  Lightly sprinkle or sift
confectioners' sugar over all.  Bake at 350 degrees for 8-10
minutes.  Makes about 20.
