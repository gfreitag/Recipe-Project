Title: Plum Jam
Categories: Fruit, Can/preserv, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk 1.75 oz powdered pectin
7 c  Sugar
6 c  Plums -- (about 4 pounds)
3/4 c  Water
1 tb Lemon juice
Combine plums, water, lemon juice, and pectin in a large sauce pot.
Bring to a rolling boil over high heat, stirring constantly. Add
sugar; return to a rolling boil. Boil hard 1 minute, stirring
constantly. Pour hot into hot jars, leaving 1/4 inch head space.
Adjust caps. Process 10 minutes in boiling water bath.
Yield: about 8 half pints.

Variations:
Brandied Plum Jam: add 1 cup brandy to fruit mixture.

Plum Orange Spice Jam: add zest from 1 orange, 1/2 tsp cinnamon, and
1/2 tsp nutmeg to fruit mixture.
