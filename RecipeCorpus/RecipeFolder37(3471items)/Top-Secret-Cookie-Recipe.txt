Title: Top-Secret Cookie Recipe
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 112 servings
1 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
2 ts Baking soda
24 oz Chocolate chips
1    8 oz Hershey bar, grated
3 c  Chopped nuts -- (your
-choice)
(Recipe may be halved)
2 c  Butter
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Brown sugar
4    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
4 c  Flour
5 c  Blended oatmeal
Measure oatmeal and blend in a blender to a fine powder.

Cream butter and both sugars. Add eggs and vanilla with flour,
oatmeal, salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Add chocolate chips,
Hershey bar and nuts.

Roll into balls and place apart on a cookie sheet. Bake 10 muinutes at
375~.

Makes 112 cookies.
