Title: PASTA WITH UNCOOKED FRESH TOMATOES VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Pasta/rice, Salsa/sauce, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 tb Fresh basil leaves, shredde
1 1/2 tb Chopped fresh parsley
Freshly ground white pepper
Cooked pasta
1 3/4 lb Firm, vine-ripened tomatoes
- peeled, cored, halved,
-seeded, and cut into 1/2"
-pieces
3 tb Olive oil
In a mixing bowl, stir together the tomatoes, oil, lemon juice,
basil, and parsley. Season to taste with white pepper and let the
mixture sit at room temperature, uncovered, for 20 to 30 minutes.
Toss with cooked pasta the moment the pasta has been drained.
