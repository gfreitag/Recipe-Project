Title: SOURDOUGH OATMEAL BREAD ABM
Categories: Breads
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 tb Salt
2 tb Oil
1 3/4 c  Sourdough starter
2 tb Water
1/2 tb Yeast
3/4 c  Oats
2 1/4 c  Flour, bread
1 tb Sugar
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order. Set "baking control" at 10 o'clock. Select "white bread" and
push Start.

Tested in DAK R2D2.  Sylvia's comments: top cracked, try less flour &
more starter.

Cathy's notes: This is a very moist sourdough bread.

Source: The Bread Machine Cookbook by Donna Rathmell German
