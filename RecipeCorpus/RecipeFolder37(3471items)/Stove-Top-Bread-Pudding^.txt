Title: Stove Top Bread Pudding^
Categories: Breads, Puddings
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Nonfat milk
1 ts Maple syrup
2 sl Raisin bread, cut in 1/2"
Cubes
1    Egg
Blend egg, milk and maple syrup. Toss bread crumbs in to coat.

Stir in a nonstick skillet over medium heat until set.

Per serving:
274cal., 13g pro., 8g (25%) fat, 28g carb., 272mg sod., 277mg chol.,
1g fiber.
