Title: FOAMY ORANGE SAUCE
Categories: Holiday, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Orange juice
1 ts Orange rind
-finely grated
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Icing sugar
1    Egg
-beaten
Cream the butter, add the sugar and beat until light and fluffy.
Beat in the egg. Very gradually add the orange juice, a drop at a
time. Stir in the orange rind. Beat the sauce until light and fluffy.
Serve immediately. Yields about 1 1/2 cups.

From the book "Canadian Christmas Cooking" by Rose Murray
