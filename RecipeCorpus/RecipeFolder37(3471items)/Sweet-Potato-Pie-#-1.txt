Title: Sweet Potato Pie # 1
Categories: Cajun/creol, Desserts, Potatoes, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Butter, melted
1 ts Nutmeg or cinnamon
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 pn Salt
1 1/2 c  Boiled sweet potatoes
2    Eggs, separated
3/4 c  Brown sugar
2 c  Milk
Mash the boiled sweet potatoes.  Beat the egg yolks with the sugar;
add to the potatoes with the milk, melted butter and spice.  Beat the
egg whites, baking powder and salt.  Fold the potato mixture in the
well-beaten egg whites.  Pour into a pastry-lined pie pan.  Bake at
350 F until a silver knife inserted in the center comes out clean,
about 45 minutes. Serves: 8.

SOURCE:[ The Legends of Louisiana Cookbook; Sheila Ainbinder;
ISBN 0-671-70817-1 ]
