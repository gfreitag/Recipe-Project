Title: Chinese Chicken Wings
Categories: Chinese, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Prepared mustard
2    Dozen chicken wings
-garlic powder
1    Bottle of soy sauce
2 ts Ginger, freshly grated
2    Cloves of garlic, minced
1/2 c  Of brown sugar
Mix soy sauce, ginger, garlic, brown sugar, and mustard together;
blend well.  Marinate chicken in mixture for a couple of hours or
longer. After marinating, drain the chicken wings and reserve the
marinade. Place on tray and bake 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees, turning
and basting with reserved marinade frequently.  Sprinkle with garlic
powder.  Place chicken wings under broiler for a minute or two to get
crispy, just before serving.  Makes 8 t0 12 servings.
