Title: Egg Nog
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 25 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Vanilla
Nutmeg
12    Eggs
10 c  Milk
3 1/4 c  Icing sugar
Brandy, rum, scotch or rye
Beat eggs in large bowl until light and lemon colored.
Slowly beat in sugar,salt and vanilla.
Stir in milk.  Add liquor to taste.
Refrigerate for 24 hours before serving.  To serve, run through
blender to foam up.  To store for a few days, keep covered in fridge.
Garnish with nutmeg.
Yield: 25 servings, 5 oz each.
