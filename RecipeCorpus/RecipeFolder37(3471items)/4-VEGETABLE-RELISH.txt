Title: 4 VEGETABLE RELISH
Categories: Relishes, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
3 lg Tomatoes, peeled, wedged
1/2 c  Green pepper, chopped
1/2 c  Cucumber, peeled, grated
1 tb Onion, grated
Combine all ingredients.  Chill 1/2 hour or longer to blend flavors.

Excellent with fried chicken.  A summer substitute for salad.
