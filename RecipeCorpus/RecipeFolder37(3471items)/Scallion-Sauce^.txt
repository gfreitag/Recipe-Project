Title: Scallion Sauce^
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 16 servings
1 ts Minced fresh gingerroot
2 ts Cornstarch DISSOLVED IN
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Rice vinegara
1/2 c  Scallions, minced
2 cl Minced galric
Combine, boil, reduce heat and simmer 4 minutes.

Per tabalespoon:
19cal., trace pro., trace fat, 1g carab., trace sod., 0mg chol., 0g
fiber.
