Title: Peanut Butter Shortbreads^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 16 servings
1/4 c  Creamy peanut butter
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Butter, softened
1/2 c  Sugar
Preheat oven to 350F.

Combine ingredients until it resembles coarse meal.

Press into an 8" round pan. Pierce with a fork to form 16 wedges,
taking care not to cut through to bottom.

Bake about 1 hour until lightly browned. Cut into wedges while still
warm.
