Title: SAUTEED STRING BEANS
Categories: Vegetables, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Dried oregano leaves, crushd
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
4 tb Butter or margarine
34 oz (2 cans) whole string beans,
-drained
1/4 ts Dried savory leaves, crushed
1. In a shallow, 1-quart,heat-resistant, non-metallic baking dish,
melt butter or margarine in Microwave Oven 30 secnnds.
2. Add string beans and heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 5 minutes
or until heated through.
3. Add remaining ingredients and toss lightly to coat all beans with
butter sauce. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 2 minutes.
