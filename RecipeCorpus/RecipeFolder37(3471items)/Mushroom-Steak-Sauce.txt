Title: Mushroom Steak Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1    Clove Garlic, Minced
1/8 ts Celery Salt
3 tb Butter
2 c  Mushrooms, Sliced
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 c  Onion, Chopped
1/8 ts Black Pepper, Ground
Mushroom Steak Sauce

[Yields 4 Servings]

Melt the butter in a skillet over medium low heat.
Add all the other ingredients.
Saute until tender (about 5 minutes).
Spoon over steaks.
