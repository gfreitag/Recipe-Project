Title: GRAHAM CRACKER BOURBON BALLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 30 servings
1 1/2 tb Light corn syrup
1/4 c  Bourbon
Confectioner's sugar
1 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1 c  Chopped walnuts
2 1/2 tb Unsweetened cocoa powder
1 c  Confectioner's sugar
1.  Combine graham cracker crumbs, walnuts, cocoa powder, sugar, corn
syrup, and bourbon.  Mix together well.
2.  Shape mixture into 1/2 inch balls.  Roll in sugar.  Chill in
refrigerator.  Roll in sugar once more.
3.  Store in refrigerator in a tightly covered container.
