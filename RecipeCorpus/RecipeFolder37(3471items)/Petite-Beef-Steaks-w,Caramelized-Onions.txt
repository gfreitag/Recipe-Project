Title: Petite Beef Steaks w/Caramelized Onions
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Grated fresh ginger root
1 ts Dried rosemary crushed
4 ts White wine vinegar
Salt and pepper
2    Beef loin steaks 1 inch
-thick
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 lg Or 2 medium onions cut into
-thin wedges about 3 cups
Heat oil in large frying pan over medium heat. Add onions, sugar,
ginger and rosemary. Reduce heat to medium low; cook and stir
occasionally, 15-20 minutes, or until onions are golden brown.
Increase heat to medium; add vinegar. Cook and stir until vinegar is
evaporated and onions are glazed. Meanwhile, cut each beef top loin
steak crosswise in half. Place steaks on rack in broiler pan so
surface of meat is 3-4 inches from heat. Broil 15-20 minutes to
desired degree of doneness, rare or medium, turning once. Season
steaks with salt and pepper; top with caramelized onions.
