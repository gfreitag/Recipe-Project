Title: Raspberry Milk Shakes^
Categories: Ice cream, Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 5 servings
1 qt Vanilla ice cream
1 pk (10oz) frozen raspberries
1/2 c  Milk
Blend raspberries and milk iin blender; process until smooth. Add ice
cream and blend until smooth.
