Title: Frozen Fruit Swirl
Categories: Desserts, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-------------------SHARED BY IRIS GRAYSON------------------------
1 1/2 c  Slightly thawed frozen
-berries
4 ea Ripe bananas, peeled,
-sliced and frozen solid
Place frozen banana pieces in a food processor.  Pulse with on and off
motions until finely chopped; whirl until pureed.  Spoon puree into a
bowl and place in the freezer.

Add berries to the food processor.  Whirl until pureed.  Remove
bananas from the freezer.  Swirl in berry puree.  Serve at once.
Makes 6 servings.

Approximate nutritional analysis:  83 calories per 1/2-cup serving; 1g
protein; 21g carbohydrate; 4g fat (4% of calories); 2g fiber; 1.5mg
sodium; 37% of the Daily Value for vitamin C; 22% for vitamin B6.

Source:  Pritikin Longevity Center, Head Teacher Susan Massaron, in
AMERICAN HEALTH magazine, November 1995, pg. 63.
