Title: CRUSTLESS SPINACH QUICHE
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
12 oz Muenster, shredded
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Black pepper
1 lg Onions, chopped
1 tb Oil
10 oz Spinach, frozen
5    Eggs
Thaw and press spinach dry.  Preheat oven to 350. Saute onion
in oil in large skilet until tender.  Add spinach and cook until
excess moisture evaporates; cool.  Combine eggs, cheese, salt,
and pepper; stir into spinach mixture.  Pour into greased 9" pie
plate.  Bake 30 minutes, until set.

Source: _Southern Living Annual Recipes, 1984_
