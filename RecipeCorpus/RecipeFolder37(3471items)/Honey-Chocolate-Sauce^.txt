Title: Honey Chocolate Sauce^
Categories: Chocolate, Salsa/sauce, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Butter
1 1/2 c  Honey
1 1/2 c  Cocoa
Mix well; microwave 1 minute. Stir. Microwave another 1 to 1 1/2
minutes. Pour into sterile jars and store in the refrigerator.
