Title: BAKED ZITI
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Casseroles, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
Italian Tomato Sauce(recipe)
3/4 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
8 oz Pk. ziti (Pasta), cooked
-conventionally
1. In a deep, 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole combine
ziti, tomato sauce and 1/3 cup shredded cheese.
2. Sprinkle remainder of cheese on top of casserole and heat-
uncovered, in Microwave Oven 5 to 8 minutes or until cheese is melted
and sauce is bubbly.
