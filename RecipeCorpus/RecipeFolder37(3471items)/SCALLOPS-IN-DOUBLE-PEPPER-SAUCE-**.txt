Title: SCALLOPS IN DOUBLE PEPPER SAUCE **
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Chopped Onion
1/2 ts Tabasco Pepper Sauce
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Drained Capers
1/4 c  Olive Oil
5    Chopped Garlic Cloves
1 lb Bay Or Sea Scallops
3/4 c  Slivered Red Peppers
3/4 c  Slivered Green Peppers
Slivers of red and green bell peppers give this quickly prepared dish
vibrant color, and the garlic, Tabasco sauce and capers provide lots
of flavor.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- In a large skillet, heat the oil and add the garlic.  Cook until
golden, about 1 minute.  Add the scallops, peppers, onion, Tabasco
sauce and salt. Stirring constantly, cook for 5 minutes, or until the
scallops turn white and the vegetables are crisp-tender.  Stir in the
capers and serve immediately.

From: The Tabasco Cookbook.
