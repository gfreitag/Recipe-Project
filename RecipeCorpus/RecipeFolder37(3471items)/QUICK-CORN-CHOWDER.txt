Title: QUICK CORN CHOWDER
Categories: Soup/stew, Main dish, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Finely chopped fresh parsley
3 sl Raw bacon, diced
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
16 oz Can cream-style corn
1/4 c  Hot water
1. Place bacon and onion in a deep, 2-quart, heat-resistant,
non-metallic casserole.
2. Heat, covered with paper toweling, in Microwave Oven 3 minutes.
3. Add remaining ingredients, except parsley. Stir to combine.
4. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 5 minutes or until soup is
heated through. Stir occasionally.
5. Allow soup to stand 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
6. Garnish with parsley.
