Title: Burke's Greek Spaghetti
Categories: Greek, Pasta/rice, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Grated Old English cheese
1 lb Spaghetti -- cooked &
- drained
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cn Milk
1 lg Green pepper -- chopped
1 lg Onion -- chopped
1    Stick butter
1 sm Can Ripe olives
1 md Can Mushrooms
1 lg Can Stewed tomatoes
Saute peppers and onions in melted stick of butter.  Add olives,
mushrooms and stewed tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes.  Layer
spaghetti, sauce and cheese; repeat layers.  Top with cream of
mushroom soup diluted with 1/2 can of milk.  Bake for 35--45 minutes
at 350 degrees until bubbly. '50
