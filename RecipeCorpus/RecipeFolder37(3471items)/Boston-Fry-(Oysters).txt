Title: Boston Fry (Oysters)
Categories: Fish/seafoo, White house
Yield: 1 batch
MMMMM---------------------------RECIPE--------------------------------
Prepare the oysters in egg batter and fine cracker meal;  fry in
butter over a slow fire for about ten minutes;  cover the hollow of a
hot platter with tomato sauce;  place the oysters in it, but not
covering;  garnish with chopped parsley sprinkled over the oysters.

From the White House Cookbook, 1929 ed.  Attributed to "Boston Oyster
House".  MMed by Dave Sacerdote
