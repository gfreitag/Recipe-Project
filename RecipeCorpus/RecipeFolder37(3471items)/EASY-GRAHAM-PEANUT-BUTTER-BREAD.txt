Title: EASY GRAHAM PEANUT BUTTER BREAD
Categories: Breads, Nuts/grains
Yield: 18 servings
1    Egg
1 1/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Peanut butter
3 c  Graham flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 c  Sugar
Mix the dry ingredients.  Add the rest of the ingredients. Mix well.
Pour into a greased loaf pan. Bake at 350 F. for 40 to 45 minutes.
