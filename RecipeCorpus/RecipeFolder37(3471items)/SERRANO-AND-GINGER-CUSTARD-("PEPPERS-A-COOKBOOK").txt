Title: SERRANO AND GINGER CUSTARD ("PEPPERS A COOKBOOK")
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
3    Peppers, Serrano
Red or Green
- seeded and diced
1 qt Cream, heavy
4 lg Eggs
1 c  Sugar, granulated
2 tb Liqueur, Orange flavor
1 tb Ginger, fresh
- peeled and minced
Whisk eggs and sugar in nonreactive metal bowl until smooth.
Add orange liqueur, ginger, peppers and cream; set bowl over simmering
water; whisk until mixture thickens (about 30 minutes)
Divide into 8 small serving bowls, refrigerate until firm (about 2
hours).
