Title: VINAIGRETTE DRESSING II
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables, Salads, Salsa/sauce, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Canned pimiento, finely
-chopped
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Wine vinegar
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Finely chopped parsley
2 tb Finely chopped chives
1. Combine all ingredients in a small bowl. Stir until well blended.
Makes 1 cup
