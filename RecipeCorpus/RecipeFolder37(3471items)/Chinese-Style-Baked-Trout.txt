Title: Chinese Style Baked Trout
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Chinese
Yield: 4 servings
4    Lake trout or salmon steaks
1 tb Vegetable oil; divided
4    Green onions; chopped
8    Snow peas; sliced on
-the diagonal
2 ts Grated ginger root
1 cl Garlic; minced
2 tb Dry sherry
2 tb Soy sauce
3 tb Water
1 ts Brown sugar
Preheat oven to 375F. Mix ginger root, garlic, sherry, soy sauce,
water and sugar in a small bowl for sauce; set aside. Place fish
in a well oiled baking dish. Pour sauce over fish. Cover and bake
20 min. until fish turns opaque. Meanwhile stir fry the snow peas
in a little oil. Remove sauce from the baking dish; set aside.
Broil fish 3-4 minutes until crispy. Pour sauce over fish and
garnish with pea pods and chopped scallions.
