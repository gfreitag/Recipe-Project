Title: MASHED POTATO PANCAKES
Categories: Potatoes, Side dish, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 12 pancakes
2    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Milk
4 tb Light corn syrup
1 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Mashed potatoes
2 c  Flour, sifted
1 ts Salt
3 ts Baking powder
Combine potatoes, sifted flour, salt, and baking powder.

Mix together the eggs and milk and stir lightly into the potato-flour
mixture. Add corn syrup and nutmeg and beat well.

Bake on a greased griddle until cakes are brown on both sides.
