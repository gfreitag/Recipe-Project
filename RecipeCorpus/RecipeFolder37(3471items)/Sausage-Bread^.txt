Title: Sausage Bread^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Sausage, Breads
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Melted butter
1    Loaf frozen bread dough,
Thawed
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/4 ts Sage
2    Eggs, lightly beaten
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper flakes
Combine filling ingredients.

Roll out dough to a 12X10" rectangle. Spread filling to within 1" of
edges. Roll up jelly roll fashion, sealing edges well. Bake on an
ungreased baking sheet at 350F. 30-35 minutes until golden and bread
tests done. Brush with butter before serving.
