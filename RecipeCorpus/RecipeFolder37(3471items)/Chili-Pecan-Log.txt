Title: Chili Pecan Log
Categories: Foodmagazin, Cheese/eggs, Appetizers, Holiday
Yield: 24 servings
12 oz Cheddar cheese; shredded
2 tb Chili powder
1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
8 oz Cream cheese
6 oz Garlic cheese; diced
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 ds Hot pepper sauce
Place unwrapped cream cheese and garlic cheese in a 2-quart glass
measure. Stirring midway through cooking, microwave on medium (50
percent) 2 minutes, or until softened. Blend Worcestershire and
pepper sauce into cheeses. Fold in Cheddar, mix well.

To make two (6-inch) logs, work with half of cheese mixture at a time.
Sprinkle half of desired amount of chili powder on wax paper. Roll
cheese mixture in chili powder to form a log. Sprinkle half of pecans
on same waxed paper; roll log in pecans to coat. Repeat with remaining
ingredients. Wrap each log in aluminum foil and chill until firm.

Serve with crackers.

Nutritional analysis per serving: 130 calories, 11 grams fat, 2 grams
carbohydrates, 30 milligrams cholesterol, 224 milligrams sodium, 72
percent of calories from fat.
