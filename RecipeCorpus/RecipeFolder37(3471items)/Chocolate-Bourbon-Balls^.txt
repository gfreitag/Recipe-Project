Title: Chocolate Bourbon Balls^
Categories: Holiday, Candies, Chocolate
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/2 c  Vanilla wafer crumbs (about
70    Cookies)
1/2 c  Sifted powdered sugar
1 c  Finely chopped pecans
1 c  (6 oz) semisweet chocolate
Morsels, melted
1/2 c  Bourbon
3 tb Light corn syrup
Stir first 3 ingredients until blended.

In a large bowl combine crumbs, powdered sugar, and pecans; stiring
well add chocolate mixture. Cover and let stand at room temperture 30
mins.

Shape mixture into 1" balls; put in granulated sugar. Store in
airtight container in refigertor.
