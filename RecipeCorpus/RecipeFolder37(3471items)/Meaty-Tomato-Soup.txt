Title: Meaty Tomato Soup
Categories: P-cooker, Main dish, Soup/stew, Vegetables, Tomatoes
Yield: 4 - 6 folks
1 ts Lemon juice;
1/2 ts Salt;
1/2 ts Pepper;
1 ts Sugar;
1 c  Sour cream;
2 tb Cornstarch;
Hot cooked rice;
1 lb Short rips
3 c  Water;
1 lg Onion; chopped
2    Carrots; sliced
1 1/2 c  Cabbage; shredded
2 1/4 lb Tomatoes; peeled quartered
1    Bay leaf;
1 cl Garlic;
Combine all ingredients except sour cream, cornstarch and rice in
pressure cooker.  Close cover securely.  Place pressure regulator on
vent pipe. COOK 20 MINUTES.  Let pressure drop of its own accord.
Mix sour cream and cornstarch together until smooth.  Gradually stir
in 1 cup of soup. Pour mixture into remaining soup, stirring
constantly.  Heat soup.  Place rice in soup bowls before filling with
hot soup. Source: Pressure Cooking is Pleasure Cooking
