Title: CAPPUCCINO ROYALE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb White rum
2 tb Dark creme de cacao
Sugar
1/2 c  Half-and-half
1/2 c  Freshly brewed espresso
2 tb Brandy
Whisk half-and-half in heavy small saucepan over high heat until
frothy, about 3 minutes.  Divide espresso coffee between 2 cups.  Add
half of brandy and creme de cacao to each cup.  Rewhisk half-and-half
and pour into cups.  Sweeten to taste with sugar.
