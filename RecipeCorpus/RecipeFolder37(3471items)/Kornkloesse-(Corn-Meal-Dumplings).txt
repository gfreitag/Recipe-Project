Title: Kornkloesse (Corn Meal Dumplings)
Categories: Aust/german, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 recipe
1    Egg
3/4 c  Cooked or canned whole ker-
--nel corn
1 ts Finely chopped onion
2 tb Melted butter or substitute
1 c  Yellow corn meal
2 ts Salt
2 c  Boiling water
1 c  Flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
Few grains pepper
:    Slowly add corn meal and salt to water, cook, stirring
constantly, 2 minutes or until thick.  Remove from heat; cool.  Sift
together 3/4 cup flour, baking powder and pepper.  Add to corn meal
and mix well. Beat egg and add together with corn, chopped onions,
and butter. :    Shape into balls; roll in remaining flour.  Drop on
hot stew, cover tightly and cook 12 minutes without removing cover.
These are fine with boiled chicken or pork.
