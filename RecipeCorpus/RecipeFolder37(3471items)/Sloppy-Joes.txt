Title: Sloppy Joes
Categories: Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Ketchup
1 ts Prepared mustard
Buns
1 lb Ground Beef
1 cn Tomato or chicken gumbo
Soup
Brown the ground beef in a frying pan Add soups, ketchup and mustard.
Heat through Serve on split hamburger buns or French Bread slices.
