Title: BOMBAY CASHEWS
Categories: Appetizers, Indian, Snacks, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 cups
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
4 c  Raw cashew halves
2 tb Coriander seeds
2 tb Cumin seeds
3 tb Avocado or almond oil
1 tb Hot paprika
1/2 tb Freshly ground black pepper
Preheat oven to 375F.  Grind spice seeds in a grinder & place in a
large pan.
Add oil & remaining seasonings & stir to mix.  Pour in nuts & coat
well with
seasonings.
Divide nuts into 2 batches & spread on a greased cookie sheet.  Bake
fro 20 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes, until golden.  Serve warm
or at room temperature.  Cool completely before storing in an
airtight container. Will keep about 2 weeks.
