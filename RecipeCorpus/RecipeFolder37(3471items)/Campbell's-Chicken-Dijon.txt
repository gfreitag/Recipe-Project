Title: Campbell's Chicken Dijon
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Water
1 tb Dijon-style mustard
1/8 ts Pepper
4 c  Hot cooked rice
Vegetable cooking spray
4    Skinless boneless chicken
Breast halves
10 3/4 oz Can reduced fat cream of
-celery soup
Prep time:  5 min.    Cooking time:  20 min.

Spray skillet with cooking spray and heat over medium-high heat 1
minute. Cook chicken 10 minutes or until browned. Set chicken aside.
Add soup, water, mustard and pepper. Heat to a boil. Return chicken to
pan. Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or until chicken is done.
Serve with rice.
Serves 4.
