Title: Baked Stuffed Onions^
Categories: Syrups
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ts Dijon mustard
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Chopped Italian parsley for
Garnish
4 md Yellow onions, peeled
2 tb Honey or other sweetener
1/2 c  Finely chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Wheat germ or bread crumbs
1/2 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 350F. Slice about a half inch off the top of each.
Hollow out
a bowl shaped space to hold about 1/4 cup filling. Slice enough off
the bottom

to let onions stand upright.

Combine remaining ingredients, except parsley. Place about 1/3 cup of
water around onions. Bake 30 minutes, adding water, 1/4 cup at a time
if needed.

Stuff onions. Bake another 30 minutes or until softened. Garnish woth
parsley.

Serve warm.

Per serving:
245cal., 8g pro., 11g fat, 23g carb., 0mg chol., 375mg sod., 6g fiber.
