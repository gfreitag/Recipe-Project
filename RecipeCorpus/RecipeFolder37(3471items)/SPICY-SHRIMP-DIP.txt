Title: SPICY SHRIMP DIP
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 & 2/3 cups
1    Carton sour cream (8-oz)
1    Envelope (0.7-oz) Italian
- salad dressing mix
2 tb Green pepper, finely chopped
2 ts Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  Water
1/2 lb Unpeeled medium-size fresh
- shrimp
1 pk Cream cheese, softened
- (3-oz.)
Bring water to a boil; add shrimp, and cook 3 to 5 minutes or until
shrimp turn pink. Drain well; rinse with cold water. Chill, peel,
devein, and chop shrimp.

Beat cream cheese at medium speed of an electric mixer until creamy.
Stir in sour cream, shrimp, and remaining ingredients. Cover and
chill at least 1 hour. Serve with fresh vegetables.
Yield: 1 2/3 cups
