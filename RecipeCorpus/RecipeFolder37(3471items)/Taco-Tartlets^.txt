Title: Taco Tartlets^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Pie/tart, Meats
Yield: 24 servings
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 c  Coarsley crushed tortilla
Chips
1 c  Sour cream
2 tb Taco sauce
3 tb Chopped ripe olives
MMMMM------------------------MEAT SHELLS-----------------------------
1 c  (4oz) shredded cheddar
Cheese
1 lb Ground beef
2 tb Taco seasoning
2 tb Water
Preheat oven to 425F.

Combine meat shell ingredients and press into sides and bottoms of
mini muffin cups. Combine filling ingredients and put about a
teaspoon into each shell. Bake 7-8 minutes until cooked and bubbly.
Remove from pan and serve.
