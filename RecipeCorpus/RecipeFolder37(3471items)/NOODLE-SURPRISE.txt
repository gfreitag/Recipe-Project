Title: NOODLE SURPRISE
Categories: Casseroles, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts -Salt
3 ts Oil
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Cheese; grated
-Bread crumbs
2 c  Noodles; whole wheat
2    Eggs; beaten
1    Onion; chopped
2/3 c  Yogurt; plain
6    Mushrooms; chopped, large
2/3 c  Oil; sunflower
Preheat  oven to 350F.  Cook noodles  till tender;  drain.  Saute
onion, mushroom,  sunflower seeds in oil till vegs soft &  seeds
crisp. Combine drained noodles &  veggies.  Stir  in cheese, eggs,
yogurt & seasonings. Turn into an oiled casserole, top with bread
crumbs & cheese. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes  or till casserole is
firm. SERVES:4
