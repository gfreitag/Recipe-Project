Title: Potato Meat Loaf^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Potatoes, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 lb Potatoes, peeled, grated,
Rinsed and drained
1    Egg, beaten
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 ts Soy sauce
Preheat oven to 350F.

Combine ingredients and bake in a loaf pan an hour, until no pink
remains.
