Title: Quick And Easy Sauteed Salmon Steaks
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
1/2    Red onion sliced and cut
-into half rings
2 tb Margerine
1/2 ts To 1 ts garlic, minced
4    Salmon Steaks
1 ts Soy Sauce (thin or mushroom)
-or fish sauce
4 sl Lemon
In large and heavy non-stick skillet, melt margerine, add soy or
oriental fish sauce and garlic.  Let fuse and ad onion, allow to
soften fully then and add salmon. Cook 4 to 5 minutes. Turn and
continue another 4 to 5 minutes or until it easily flakes. Garnish
with lemon slices.

SOURCE: Bill Birner
