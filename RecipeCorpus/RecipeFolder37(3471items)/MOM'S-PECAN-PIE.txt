Title: MOM'S PECAN PIE
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1 pn Salt
1 tb Butter
1 c  Pecans
3    Eggs, beaten
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Karo syrup (light)
Mix all ingredients together and pour into unbaked pie shell.  Bake
at 400 degrees for 5 minutes, then reduce to 350 degrees and bake and
additional 45 minutes.
