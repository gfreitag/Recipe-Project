Title: AUNT ELSIE'S HOT DOG BAR B QUE SAUCE
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 10 servings
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
Pepper to taste
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 tb Fat
1 1/4 c  Catsup
2 tb Pickle relish
Saute onion in fat until tender.  Combine rest of ingredients in
skillet with onion.  Cut hotdogs 3 or 4 times but not all the way
through (about a fourth of the way). Place in sauce and simmer until
plump and hot.
