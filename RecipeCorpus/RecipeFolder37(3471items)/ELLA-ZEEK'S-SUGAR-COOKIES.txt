Title: ELLA ZEEK'S SUGAR COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 48 servings
2    Eggs
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  White sugar
1 c  Shortening
1/4 c  Milk
Cream sugars and shortening.  Add milk, vanilla and eggs and mix
well. Stir in remaining ingredients and mix well. Refrigerate for
about 1 hour.
With floured hands roll teaspoonful of dough into a ball. Place on
cookie sheet.  With three fingres together, press ball flat, leaving
ridges down the middle. Bake in 400Ã¸F oven for 9 to 12 minutes or
until lightly gold.

Ella was a neighbor who was ten years older than God when I was a
child. She always had these in her cookie jar, and usually would bake
a dozen every day since the dough keeps so well. It is one of my all
time favorites and I have never tasted them anywhere else.
