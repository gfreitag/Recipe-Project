Title: TOMATO PRESERVES
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 24 ounces
1 ea Lemon, thin sliced
1 ts Ginger, ground
5 lb Tomatoes, firm
5 lb Sugar
Skin and cut up tomatoes.  Add remaining ingredients.
Simmer slowly until thick, about 45 minutes.
pour into hot, sterilized glasses.  Seal immediately.
