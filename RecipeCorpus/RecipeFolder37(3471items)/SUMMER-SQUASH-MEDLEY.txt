Title: SUMMER SQUASH MEDLEY
Categories: Vegetables, Low cal/fat, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 bn Onions, green
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper, black
2 tb Butter
1 lb Squash, yellow
1 lg Bell peppers
1 lg Tomatoes
Melt butter in skillet.  Wash and slice squash; saute.  Cut bell
pepper into chunks and add; wash and slice green onions and add. Cut
tomato into wedges. When vegetables are tender, add tomato, sugar,
salt, and pepper, and cook two minutes.  Serve at once.
