Title: Gremolata Baked Cod
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
-minced
2 ts Olive Oil
Salt And Pepper
1 1/4 lb Cod fillet, cut into 4
-pieces
1    Sliced bread, crust
-removed
1    Lemon
1    Clv  Garlic, Minced
2 tb Parsley, chopped
2 tb Shallot or green onion,
Cooking Time: 15 min.
Preparation Time:
Oven Temp:  450oF

Instructions:

Tear or chop bread into crumbs and put in a bowl.  Preheat oven to
450F.  Peel zest from lemon and mince; sqeeze juice from lemon.  Add 1
tbs, lemon zest, garlic, parsley and shallots to bread crumbs and
stir. Drizzle with oil and stir to thoughly combine; season with salt
and pepper.  Rinse fish and pat dry; arrange pieces in a single layer
on a lightly oiled baking pan.  Pour lemon juice over fish, then top
with gremolata mixture.  Bake fish until it is opaque through the
center, abput 10 minutes for fish 1 inch thick.  Garnish with
remaining lemon zest.
