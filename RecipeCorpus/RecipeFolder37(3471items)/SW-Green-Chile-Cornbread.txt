Title: SW Green Chile Cornbread
Categories: Tex/mexican, Breads, Chilies
Yield: 1 pan bread
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 c  Flour
1 c  Yellow cornmeal
2 tb Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
1/2 c  Green chilies, diced
1 1/2 c  Corn, cream style
1/2 c  Jack cheese, shredded
In a mixer, cream butter and sugar; reduce speed to slow and add
eggs, one at a time.  Add chilies, corn, both cheeses, flour,
cornmeal, baking powder and salt.  Mix well.

Bake for 1 hour at 325 F in a well buttered 9" square pan.

Recipe from Bobbie Kopf, FIDO: Cooking Conference, 12/21/95.
