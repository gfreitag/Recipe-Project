Title: Cream Cheese Pound Cake^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
6 lg Eggs
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Flour
1/8 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Softened butter
1 pk (8oz) cream cheese,
Softened
3 c  Sugar
Beat butter nad cream cheese until creamy. Slowly add sugar, beat 5-7
minutes. Add eggs one at a time, beating just until yellow
disappears. Mix vanilla in well.

Slowly beat in combined flour and salt just until blended. Pour into a
greased and floured 10" tube pan. Place in 300f. oven along with a 2
cup ovenproof glass measuring cup and bake 1 1/2 hours or utnil tests
done. Cool in pan on rack 10-15 minutes; remove from pan and finish
cooling on rack.
