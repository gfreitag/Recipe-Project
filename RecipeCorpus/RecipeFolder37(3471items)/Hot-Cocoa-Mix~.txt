Title: Hot Cocoa Mix~
Categories: Healthy, Beverages, Mixes
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa
Water
2 1/2 c  Nonfat dry milk powder
1 c  Powdered sugar -- sifted
To prepare mix, in a large mixing bowl stir together the dry milk
powder, sifted powdered sugar, and cocoa. Store in a tightly covered
container. Stir before using.

For each serving, spoon 1/4 cup of the cocoa mix into cup. Add 2/3 cup
hot or boiling water. Stir to mix well.

Per serving: Calories: 98; total fat: 1g; saturated fat: 0.5g;
cholesterol: 3mg; sodium: 83mg.
