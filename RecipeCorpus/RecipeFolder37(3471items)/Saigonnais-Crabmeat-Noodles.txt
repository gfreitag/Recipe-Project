Title: Saigonnais Crabmeat Noodles
Categories: Salads, Fish/seafoo, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
Salt and pepper
1 cn (6 oz) crabmeat, drained
1/3 c  Cilantro, chopped
3 oz Thin dried rice noodles
5 lg Garlic clove(s)
2 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
1. Bring a medium saucepan of water to a rolling boil over high heat.
Stir in the noodles and remove from the heat. Use 2 forks to stir and
separate the noodles, then let them soak for 5 min until softened and
bright white. Drain and place on a cutting board in a log-shaped
pile. Cut crosswise into 2-inch lengths; you'll have about 2 1/2 cups.

2. Meanwhile, chop the garlic, In a large frying pan over medium heat,
combine the oil and garlic and cook, stirring often, about 2 min.
Increase the heat to medium-high and add the noodles, salt and
pepper. Cook, stirring often, 4-5 min, until the noodles are tender
but still firm. Add the crabmeat, cilantro and additional pepper and
toss well.
