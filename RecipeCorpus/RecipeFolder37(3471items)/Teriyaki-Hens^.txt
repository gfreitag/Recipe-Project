Title: Teriyaki Hens^
Categories: Poultry, Bbq/grill
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Honey
2 tb Minced, peeled fresh
Gingerroot
2 cl Garlic, minced
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper flakes
2 pk (6lb) fresh cornish hens,
Quartered
1/4 c  Reduced sodium soy sauce
2 tb Rice wine vinegar or white
Vinegar
Combine marinade ingredients. Marinate hens 2 to 4 hours in the
refrigerator.

Preheat broiler. Broil 6-8" from heat 20-25 minutes, turning now and
then.
