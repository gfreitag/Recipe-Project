Title: Eggnog
Categories: Holiday, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
-whiskey
Few grains of salt
1    Egg white
1    Egg yolk
1 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Cream
1/8 c  To 1/4 c rum, brandy or
In a small bowl, beat egg yolk until light. Beat in slowly sugar,
cream, rum, and salt.
In another bowl whip egg white until stiff. Fold white lightly into
other ingredients.  Transfer mixture into punch glass.

Yield: 1 serving
