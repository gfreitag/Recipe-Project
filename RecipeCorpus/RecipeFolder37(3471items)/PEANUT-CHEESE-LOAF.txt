Title: PEANUT CHEESE LOAF
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Chopped salted peanuts
2/3 c  Fine bread crumbs
1/4 lb Cheese, grated
1    Egg
1/3 c  Milk
2/3 c  Cooked oatmeal
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
3 tb Minced onion
1 ts Salt
2 ts Lemon juice
Combine all ingredients.  Put mixture into a greased loaf pan and
bake in a 350 degree oven for about 1 hour.  Serve hot with mushroom
or tomato sauce.
