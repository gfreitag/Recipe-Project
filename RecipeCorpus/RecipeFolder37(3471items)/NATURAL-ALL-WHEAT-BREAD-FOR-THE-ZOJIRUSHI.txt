Title: NATURAL ALL WHEAT BREAD FOR THE ZOJIRUSHI
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 loaf
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1/4 c  Wheat gluten
2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Water; 110ø degrees
3 ts Dry yeast
1/3 c  Honey
Place 3 teaspoons fo dry yeast into Home bakery pan.  Add 1 1/2 cups
of lukewarm water (110ø degrees).  Mix with spatula until dissolved.
Add 1/3 cup of honey and blend together with spatula.  In a separate
bowl, mix 3 cups of wheat flour and 1/4 cup of wheat gluten, also add
2 t. of salt, then place then ingredients into pan.  Select BASIC
WHEAT BREAD setting and press START.
