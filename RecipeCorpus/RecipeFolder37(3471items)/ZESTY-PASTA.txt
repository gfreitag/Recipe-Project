Title: ZESTY PASTA
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 3 servings
1 bn Green onions; chopped
1/4 c  Cilantro leaves (or more)
-- washed and chopped
2 tb Lime juice
1 tb EDEN Organic Shoyu
Or Tamari
1 pk Kudzu & Sweet Potato Pasta
-- (EDEN brand)
1 tb EDEN Toasted Sesame Oil*
-- *Hot Pepper
2 c  Pea pods, chopped
1 md Red pepper; chopped
1 c  Bean sprouts
Cook pasta according to package directions, set aside. Heat oil, add
pea pods and red pepper, saute' 2 minutes. Add bean sprouts, simmer
2-3 minutes. Add remaining ingredients, mix in pasta and serve.

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Yields: 3 servings
