Title: Campbell's Creamy Chicken & Vegetables
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Dried basil leaves --
Crushed
16 oz Bag frozen vegetable
-combination *
Cooking time:  25 min.
4    Skinless boneless chicken
Breast halves
1/8 ts Garlic powder
Vegetable cooking spray
10 3/4 oz Can reduced fat cream of
-mushroom soup
Prep time:  5 min.

* Broccoli, cauliflower, carrots.

Sprinkle chicken with garlic powder. Spray skillet with cooking spray
and heat over medium-high heat 1 minute. Cook chicken 10 minutes or
until browned. Set chicken aside.
Add soup, milk, lemon juice, basil and vegetables. Heat to a boil.
Return chicken to pan. Cover and cook over low heat 10 minutes or
until chicken is done.
Serves 4.
