Title: CRICKETS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 60 servings
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Land O Lakes butter, soften
1 c  Diced toasted almonds
1 c  Chocolate covered raisins
1 1/4 c  All purpose flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
Combine all ingredients except almonds and raisins in large mixer
bowl. Beat 1 minute at medium speed or mixer.  Stir in almonds and
raisins. Drop by teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie
sheets.  Bake at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes. Deborah Zurrow
Lombard, Illinois
