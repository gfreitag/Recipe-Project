Title: Ginger Dressing
Categories: Salads, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Hoisin sauce
2 tb Sesame oil
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
1/4 c  Oil
1 tb Light soy sauce
Servings 4
2 tb Fresh ginger, chopped
1 x  Cayenne pepper to taste
2 tb Dijon mustard
1 tb Sherry
Blend all ingredients in blender.  Makes about 1-1/2 cups.
