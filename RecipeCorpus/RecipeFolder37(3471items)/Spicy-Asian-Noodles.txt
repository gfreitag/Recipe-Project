Title: Spicy Asian Noodles
Categories: Appetizers, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Seasoned rice vinegar
2 tb (to 3 tbs) Chinese
Chile-garlic paste
1 lb Soba (buckwheat) noodles
Or spaghetti
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Asian sesame oil
1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling, salted water until
tender but still firm, 7-9 min. Drain well.

2. In a small bowl, stir together the soy sauce, sesame oil, rice
vinegar, and chile paste until well blended.

3. Toss the drained pasta with the sauce. Serve at room temperature.
