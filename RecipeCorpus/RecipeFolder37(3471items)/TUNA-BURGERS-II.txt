Title: TUNA-BURGERS II
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Finely chopped onion
1 tb Finely chopped celery
1 tb Finely chopped pimiento
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
6    Hamburger buns, split
-and buttered
4 sl American cheese, cut
-into eighths
2    Hard-cooked eggs, chopped
2 md Sweet pickles, diced
6 1/2 oz Can tuna, drained and flaked
1 tb Finely chopped green pepper
1. Combine all ingredients except buns in a large mixing bowl. Blend
thoroughly.
2. Place bottom of hamburger buns in a shallow, 10 inch round
heat-resistant, non-metallic baking dish. Spoon tuna mixture onto bun
halves.
3. Place top half of hamburger bun on tuna mixture.
4. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 4 minutes or until cheese melts.
