Title: SOUTHERN PEACH YAM BAKE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
2 cn Yam, drained (17 oz)
1    16 oz can peach slices,drain
1 1/2 c  Miniature marshmallows
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
3 tb Flour
1/2 ts Nutmeg
2 tb Margarine
Combine sugar, flour and nutmeg; cut in margarine until mixture
resembles coarse crumbs.  Add nuts.  Arrange yams and peaches in 1
1/2 quart casserole; sprinkle with sugar mixture.  Bake at 350
degrees, 35 minutes. Sprinkle with marshmallows.  Broil until lightly
browned.
