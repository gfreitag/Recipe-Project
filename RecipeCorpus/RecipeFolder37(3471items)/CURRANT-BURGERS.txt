Title: CURRANT BURGERS
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Walnuts
1/2 ts Soy sauce
1 ts Vegetable salt
Pepper to taste
2 tb Butter
1 lb Ground turkey
2/3 c  Currants
1/2 c  Seasoned bread crumbs
- (whole grain)
1/4 c  Onions, minced
1/4 c  Celery, minced
Combine turkey and currants.  Add next 7 ingredients and mix until
well-blended.  Melt butter in a large frying pan. Make 3-inch patties
and fry over medium heat.  Turn once, when browned, then serve.
