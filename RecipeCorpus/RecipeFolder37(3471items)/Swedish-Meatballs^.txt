Title: Swedish Meatballs^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 10 servings
MMMMM-------------------------MEATBALLS------------------------------
1/4 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Butter
2 lb Ground beef
1/2 lb Ground pork
2    Eggs, beaten
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 1/2 ts Dried dill
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/2 ts Dried dill
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Fresh dill sprigs, optional
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Flour
2 cn (14 1/2oz) beef broth
1 pt Heavy cream
Combine milk, eggs, bread crumbs and seasonings; set aside.

Cook onion until soft in butter; add to crumb mixture. Add beef and
pork. Cover and refrigerate an hour. Shape into meatballs. Bake at
350F 20-25 minutes. Transfer to a casserole dish.

To make sauce, melt butter; stir in flour until smooth. Stir in broth,
bring to a boil. Reduce heat, stir in cream, dill, salt and pepper.
If you want a thicker sauce, cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring
ocasionally. Pour over meatballs and bake at 350F. 40-45 minutes
until heated and bubbly. Garnish with fresh dill, if desired.
