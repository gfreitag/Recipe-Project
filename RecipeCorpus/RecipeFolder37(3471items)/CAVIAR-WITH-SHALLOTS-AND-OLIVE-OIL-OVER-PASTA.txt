Title: CAVIAR WITH SHALLOTS AND OLIVE OIL OVER PASTA
Categories: Pasta/rice, Salsa/sauce, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 tb Fresh chives, finely chopped
Cooked angel hair pasta
1/4 c  Olive oil
4 md Shallots, chopped fine
1/4 c  American golden caviar
In a medium skillet, heat the oil with the shallots over moderate
heat. As soon as the shallots sizzle, pour them with the oil over
individual servings of pasta. Top each serving with a dollop of
caviar and a sprinkling of chives.
