Title: PARMESAN CHEESE CONES
Categories: Holiday, Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 12 servings
3/4 ts Oregano;crushed
1/4 ts Garlic powder
2 c  Almonds
12 oz Cream cheese;soft
1/2 c  Parmesan;grated
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
Combine ingredients except nuts. Form into a pair of pine cones on a
large serving board or platter, using nuts to form `petals'. Decorate
with evergreen sprigs and Christmas balls and serve with fruit and
crackers.
