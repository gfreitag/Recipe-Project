Title: Indian Egg Curry
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs, Indian
Yield: 4 servings
8    Hard cooked eggs; chopped
4    Toasted english muffins
Fresh cilantro or parsley
-chopped
2 tb Curry powder <below>
1 c  Onion; finely chopped
2 tb Butter
1 tb Flour
1 c  Milk
Saute onion in butter until soft.
Stir in curry and flour and heat an additional 2 mins.
Slowly add the milk, stirring constantly as sauce thickens.
Add chopped eggs and heat thru.
Pour over muffins, garnish with parsley or cilantro and serve.
Curry Powder

5T  hot dried red chile powder
1/2 t   powdered ginger
1/2 t   mustard seeds
1/2 T   cloves
2 inch  stick cinnamon
4   T   coriander seeds
4   T   cumin seeds
3   T   turmeric
1/2 t   fenugreek
1/2 t   cardamom

Mix together and blender in grinder until fine
