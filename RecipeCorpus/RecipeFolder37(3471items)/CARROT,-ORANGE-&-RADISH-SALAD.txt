Title: CARROT, ORANGE & RADISH SALAD
Categories: Salads, African, Moroccan, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Orange juice
1 ds Orange-blossom water
1 ds Cinnamon
Salt & pepper
Pita wedges
1 lb Carrots, peeled & shredded
1 lg Onion, cut into bite-sized
-- chunks
3/4 c  Radishes, thinly sliced
1/2 c  Cilantro, chopped
3 tb Olive oil
Combine carrots, oranges, radishes & cilantro in a salad bowl.  Whisk
together the olive oil, juices, water, cinnamon, salt & pepper & pour
over the salad.  Cover & chill.  Serve garnished with pita wedges.
