Title: Minestrone-Style Turkey Soup
Categories: Poultry, Soup/stew, Holiday, P-cooker
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Great northern beans; drain
- (15 oz.)
1 cn Garbanzo beans; drained
- (15 oz.)
1 lg Onion; chopped
1    Clove garlic; minced
1/4 c  Snipped parsley
1 ts Dried basil; crushed
-MARY WILSON  BWVB02B
1    Meaty turkey carcass; cut
- apart
1 tb Instant chicken bouillon
- granules
1    Bay leaf
2 c  Cabbage; shredded
1 cn Stewed tomatoes; cut up
- (14 1/2 oz.)
Combine turkey, bouillon, bay leaf, and 7 cups water. Bring to
boiling; reduce heat.  Cover; simmer 1 hour. Remove turkey carcass;
cool. Remove meat from bones and chop. Discard bones. Strain broth.
Return broth and turkey to pan.  Stir in cabbage, undrained tomatoes,
beans, onion, garlic, parsley, basil, and 1/8 teaspoon pepper. Bring
to boiling; reduce heat. Cover; simmer 25-30 minutes.  Serves 6.
