Title: Johnny C's Cabbage and Onion Bow Ties
Categories: Side dish, Pasta/rice, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Butter
3 tb Italian parsley, chopped
Salt and pepper
1/2 lb Bow tie pasta
1 sm Cabbage
1 md Onion(s)
1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling, salted water until
tender but still firm, 8-10 min. Drain well.

2. While the pasta is cooking, quarter the cabbage lengthwise and
remove and discard the tough inner core. Cut the cabbage crosswise
into 1-inch strips to make 5 cups; reserve the rest for another use.
Chop the onion.

3. Heat the butter in a large frying pan over medium-high heat, until
melted and bubbly but not brown. Add the onion and cook, stirring
often, until softened, 2-3 min. Add the cabbage and cook, stirring
often, until bright green and softened, 3-4 min.

4. Toss the pasta with the cabbage and onion mixture and the parsley,
adding salt to taste and seasoning generously with fresh ground
pepper.
