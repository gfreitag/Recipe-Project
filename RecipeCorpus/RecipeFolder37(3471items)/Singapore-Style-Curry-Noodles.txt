Title: Singapore-Style Curry Noodles
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Indian-style curry paste
1/2 lb Cooked bay or small shrimp
Salt and pepper
1/2 lb Wire-thin rice noodles
1/2 md Green bell pepper
2    Green onions
1. In a large bowl, soak the rice noodles in hot water to cover until
limp and bright white, about 5 min.

2. Meanwhile, cut the green pepper into thin slices about 2-inches
long. Cut the green onions crosswise into thin slices.

3. Drain the noodles. Place them on a cutting board in a long thin
log. Cut crosswise into 2-inch lengths.

4. In a large frying pan, heat the curry paste for 1 min over medium
heat. Add the green pepper and green onions and cook 1 min, stirring
occasionally. Add the rice noodles, shrimp, salt and pepper and cook,
stirring and tossing often, for 4-6 min, until the noodles are tender
and evenly coated with curry paste.
