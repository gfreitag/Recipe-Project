Title: GRAPEFRUIT CRUNCH
Categories: Fruit, Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Brown Sugar, packed firmly
1/4 c  Butter, melted
2    Grapefruit, halved
1 c  Whole Wheat Flakes Cereal,
-crushed
Remove seeds from grapefruit halves.  Cut around edges and
sections to loosen; remove centers.  Mix cereal, sugar and butter;
spread on grapefruit.
Set oven control at broil.  Broil grapefruit 5" from heat for
about 3 to 4 minutes, or until golden brown.

Source:  SHARE Colorado Cookbook  11/20/93
