Title: FRANKS AND BEANS
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Fruit, Casseroles, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
-unsweetened, undrained
1/4 c  Chili sauce
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1 lb Frankfurters
32 oz Can pork and beans
8 oz Crushed pineapple in juice,
1. Slice frankfurters into 1/4-inch rounds.
2. Place frankfurters, beans, pineapple, chili sauce and onions in a
2-quart,
heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole.
3. Heat, covered, in Microwave Oven 8 minutes stirring after 4
minutes. 4. Allow to stand covered for 2 minutes before serving.
