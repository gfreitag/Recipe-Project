Title: Hamburger Pie
Categories: Meats, Pie/tart, Potatoes, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
Fat or lard or burger grease
Salt and pepper
1 cn Condensed tomato soup
10 1/2 oz. size
1/2 c  Warm milk
1 md Onion, chopped
1 lb Ground beef
1 cn Green beans, No. 2 size
5 md Potatoes, cooked
1    Egg, beaten
Cook onion in hot fat (the stuff) until golden.  Add meat and
seasonings; brown.  Add beans and soup; pour into greased 1 1/2 quart
casserole.  Mash the potatoes; add the milk, egg and season.  Spoon
in mounds over meat. Bake in moderate oven at 350 degrees for 30
minutes.
