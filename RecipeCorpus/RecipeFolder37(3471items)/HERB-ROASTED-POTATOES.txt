Title: HERB-ROASTED POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Prepared GOOD SEASOMS Garlic
& Herb Salad Dressing
2 lb Red potatoes, cut into chunk
1    Onion, green pepper and red
Pepper, cut into chunks
Heat oven to 400-degree F.  Mix all ingredients in shallow baking pan;
arrange in single layer.

Bake 45 minutes or until potatoes are tender and golden brown,
stirring occasionally.

Makes 4 to 6 servings.

*NOTE: the salad dressing should be PREPARED.
