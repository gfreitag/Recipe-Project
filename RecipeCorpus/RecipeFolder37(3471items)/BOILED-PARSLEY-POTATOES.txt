Title: BOILED PARSLEY POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Finely chopped parsley
6 md Potatoes, peeled & quartered
Water
1/2 ts Salt
1. Place potatoes in water to cover in a deep, 2-quart, heat-
resistant, non-metallic casserole.
2. Add salt and heat, covered, in Microwave Oven, 20 minutes or until
fork-tender, Drain.
3. Add butter and parsley.
4. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven, 2 minutes, Toss to combine
thoroughly.
