Title: Granola Raisins Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 tb Oil
1 1/2 tb Honey
1 1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Granola
1/3 c  Raisins
2 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
1 c  Whole-wheat flour
3 tb Powdered milk
1 1/2 ts Salt
Add all ingredients except granola and raisins in the order suggested
by your manual and bake on raisin bread setting.  At the beep add the
granola and raisins.  This bread is good warm or cold.
