Title: Hearty White Bean & Pasta Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 10 servings
19 oz Can White beans
4 c  Chicken broth
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 1/4 c  Cooked pasta
1/4 lb Thick-sliced bacon
3 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
1/4 c  Finely chopped carrots
15 oz Can Tomatoes with juice
Cut bacon into pieces and fry crisp. Drain bacon on paper towels.
Remove grease from pan and add olive oil. Add onion, celery, and
carrots; cover and cook about 10 minutes.
Add tomatoes with juice and cook uncovered about 8 minutes. Add beans,
broth, salt and pepper and cook 5 minutes. Mash some of the beans for
thickening.
Return bacon to pan and add pasta. Cook 5 minutes.
