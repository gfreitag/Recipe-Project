Title: Chicken with Coconut Curry Sauce
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 2 servings
(like Thai Red Curry Base)
1/3 c  Canned chicken broth
1/2 c  Canned unsweetened
Coconut milk
1 tb Lime juice
2    Chicken breast halves
-skinned and boned
2 ts Vegetable oil
1 lg Green onion, chopped
2 ts Curry paste
Place chicken between sheets of waxed paper. Using mallet or rolling
pin, pound chicken to even  1/2-inch thickness. Peel off paper.
Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat oil in large non-stick
skillet over high heat. Add chicken; saute until brown and almost
cooked through, about 2 min per side. Transfer chicken to plate. Add
green onion and curry paste to skillet. Stir 1 min.. Add broth; boil
until reduced to glaze, about 1 min. Add coconut milk; cook until
sauce coats spoon, stirring constantly, about 1 min. Mix in lime
juice. Return chicken and any collected juices to skillet. Reduce
heat to medium-low; simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 2
min. Transfer chicken to plates. Spoon sauce over, dividing equally.
