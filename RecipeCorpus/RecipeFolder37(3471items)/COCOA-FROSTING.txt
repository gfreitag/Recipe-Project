Title: COCOA FROSTING
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts, Microwave
Yield: 8 servings
3 c  Confectioners' sugar, sifted
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 tb Cocoa
2 tb Butter
3 tb Milk
1. Combine cocoa, milk and butter in a medium-sized, heat- resistant,
non-metallic bowl.
2. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 1 1/2 minutes or until butter is
melted.
3. Gradually add confectioners' sugar and beat with electric mixer
till smooth. Stir in vanilla.
Fills and frosts two 8-inch layers.
Note: If consistency is too thick, add 1 to 2 tablespoons more
milk as you are beating in confectioners' sugar.
