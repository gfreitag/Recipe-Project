Title: NEIMAN-MARCUS COOKIE
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 112 servings
24 oz Chocolate chips
2 ts Vanilla
1    8oz. Hershey bar, grated
4 c  Flour
3 c  Chopped nuts
5 c  Blended oatmeal*
2 c  Butter
1 ts Salt
2 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
2 c  Brown sugar
2 ts Soda (Baking soda?)
4    Eggs
Cream butter and both sugars. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix together with
flour, oatmeal, salt, baking powder and soda. Add chocolate chips,
Hershey bar and nuts. Roll into balls and place two inches apart on a
cookie sheet. Bake for 10 minutes at 375. Makes 112 cookies. (Recipe
may be halved).
* Measure oatmeal and blend in a blender to a fine powder.
