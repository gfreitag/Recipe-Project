Title: Red Raspberry Chocolate Bars^
Categories: Holiday, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Fruit
Yield: 36 servings
1    Egg
12 oz Jar raspberry preserves
1 pk (10oz) chocolate chips
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Finely chopped pecans
1 c  Softened butter
Preheat oven to 350F. and grease a 13X9" pan.

Combine flour, sugar, nuts, butter and egg until crumbly. Set aside 1
1/2 cups of the mixutre and press remaining into the bottom of the
baking pan. Spread with preserves and sprinkle with chips, then
remaining crumbs.

Bake 40-45 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool; cut into bars.
