Title: Taco Cresents^
Categories: Meats, Sandwich, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
Olives, drained
2    Eggs  lightly beaten
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
2 cn Refrigerated cresent rolls
3/4 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 pk Taco seasoning
1 cn (4 1/2oz) chopped ripe
Brown beef and onion; drain. Add taco seasoning and olives; set aside.
When cool, add cheese and eggs, mixing well.

Seperate dough onto a baking sheet. Place 2 tablespoons meat on each
triangle, roll up and shape into cresents. Bake at 375F. 10-15 minutes
until lightly browned.
