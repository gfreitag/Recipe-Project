Title: Garlic Thyme Corn Sticks^
Categories: Breads, Holiday
Yield: 18 servings
1 ts Dried thyme
2 lg Eggs, lightly beaten
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 1/4 c  Cornmeal
3/4 c  Flour
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
1 ts Baking powder
Heat greased cast iron cornstick pans 3 minutes at 425F.

Combine dry ingredients.

Combine wet ingredients and add to dry, stirring just until mixed.

Spoon batter into pans, filling 2/3 full. Bake 12 minutes or until
lightly browned.
