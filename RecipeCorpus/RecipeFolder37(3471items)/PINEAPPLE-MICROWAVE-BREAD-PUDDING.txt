Title: PINEAPPLE MICROWAVE BREAD PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Pineapple; 14 oz crushed
4 c  Bread cubes;stale or toasted
Whipped cream or vanilla
Ice cream
1/2 c  Butter
3/4 c  Sugar
3    Eggs; beaten
1 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
In medium size mixing bowl beat together butter and sugar. Add beaten
eggs, milk, vanilla and drained pineapple. Fold in bread cubes. Pour
mixture into 1.5 l (1 1/2 qt) casserole. Cook on Med (50%) 15-20
minutes or till firm.
Top with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.
SERVES: 4-6
