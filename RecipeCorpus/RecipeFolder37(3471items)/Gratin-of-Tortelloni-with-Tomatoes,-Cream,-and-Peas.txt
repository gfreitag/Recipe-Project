Title: Gratin of Tortelloni with Tomatoes, Cream, and Peas
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Frozen green peas, thawed
Salt and pepper
1/3 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
18 oz Tortelloni
1 1/2 c  Heavy cream
3 cn (14 1/2 oz ea)
"pasta-ready" tomatoes
1. Preheat the broiler.

2. Cook the tortelloni in a large pot of boiling, salted water until
tender but still firm, 7-8 min. Drain well.

3. Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan, bring the cream to a boil over
medium-high heat. Boil, stirring occasionally, until the cream in
reduce by half, 4-5 min. Add the tomatoes and peas and season with
salt and pepper to taste. Heat the sauce through toss with the
tortelloni.

4. Spoon the tortelloni into a 15-inch gratin dish. Sprinkle the
Parmesan cheese on top.

5. Broil about 4-inches from the heat until hot and bubbly, about 2
min. Serve immediately.
