Title: Killer Cranberry Sauce
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce, Poultry, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Cranberries
1/2 c  Toasted pecans
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1    Navel orange
1/2 ts Grated ginger
Grate the orange peel and add to a pot with the sugar and ginger. Add
the juice from the orange into the pot and simmer over medium heat
until the sugar is dissolved. Add cranberries and cook until they
pop, about 5 minutes. Add pecans and cool sauce.
