Title: Pecan Tassies
Categories: Foodmagazin, Holiday, Breads
Yield: 42 tassies
2 c  Flour
1 c  Butter (less 2 Tbsp)
2 pk Cream cheese  (3oz)
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 1/3 c  Pecans; chopped
1/4 ts Salt
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Butter
Preheat oven to 3S0 F. Mix butter, cream cheese and flour
together. Form into 1-inch balls. Press into miniature
muffin pans. Spoon filling into muffin pans on top of
crust. Bake 20 to 30 minutes. Makes 42 to 48 tassies.

Filling: Mix together 2 eggs, 1 1/2 cups sugar, 2 tablespoons
butter, 1 1/3 cups chopped pecans and 1/4 teaspoon salt.

Per serving:  Calories 118; Fat 8g; Cholesterol 25mg; Sodium 68mg
Percent calories from fat  60%
