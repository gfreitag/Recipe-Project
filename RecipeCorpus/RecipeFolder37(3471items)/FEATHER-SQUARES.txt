Title: FEATHER SQUARES
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Milk
4 tb Melted shortening
2 tb Brown sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
Mix and sift flour, baking powder and salt and sugar. Stir in beaten
egg, milk and melted shortening.Mix only enough to blend. Pour into a
well greased 8x8 pan and sprinkle on the brown sugar-cinnamon
mixture. Bake in a hot oven 400 F 20 min. Cut in squares.
