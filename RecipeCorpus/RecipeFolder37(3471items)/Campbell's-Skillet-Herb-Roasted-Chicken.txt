Title: Campbell's Skillet Herb Roasted Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
-thighs
Vegetable cooking spray
10 3/4 oz Can reduced fat cream of
-chicken soup
1/2 c  Water
4 c  Hot cooked rice
1/4 ts Ground sage
1/4 ts Dried thyme leaves --
-crushed
4    Skinless boneless chicken
-breast halves
--OR--
8    Skinless boneless chicken
Cooking time:  20 min.
Prep teim:  10 min.

Mix sage and thyme. Sprinkle chicken with seasoning mixture.
Spray skillet with cooking spray and heat over medium heat 1 minute.
Cook chicken 15 minutes or until chicken is done. Remove and keep
warm. Add soup and water. Reduce heat to low. Heat through. Serve
over chicken and rice.
Serves 4.
