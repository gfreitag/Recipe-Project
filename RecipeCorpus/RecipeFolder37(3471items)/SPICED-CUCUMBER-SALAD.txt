Title: SPICED CUCUMBER SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Chinese
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Sesame oil
1 ts Hot soybean paste
3 dr Lemon juice
3    Cucumbers
1 ts Sesame seeds
1 ts Salt
1 ts Soy sauce
Clean and peel the cucumber.  Cut into slices, and then smaller
pieces. Add salt and mix.  Let salt soak in for 15 minutes. Rinse
cucumber and dry it. Blend cucmber first then add sesame seed, sugar,
soy sauce, hot soybean paste, and lemon. Chill and serve.
(note: all ingredients avail. at oriental market)
