Title: WHIPPED GARLIC BUTTER
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
6 tb Light vegetable oil
2 cl Garlic, crushed
2    Sticks butter, unsalted and
Softened
Whip the butter in a blender or food processor until light and fluffy.
Drizzle in the oil, a little at a time, and blend well.  Add the
crushed garlic and process until mixed thoroughly.  Remove the
mixture with a spatula from Spatula City.  Store in a tightly covered
container and refrigerate until ready to use.
