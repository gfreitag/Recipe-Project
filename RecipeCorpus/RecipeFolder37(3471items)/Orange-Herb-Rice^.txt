Title: Orange Herb Rice^
Categories: Holiday, Fruit, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Marjoram
1/8 ts Thyme
1 c  Uncooked long grain rice
2 tb Butter
2 tb Chopped onion
2 c  Water
1/2 ts Grated orange rind
1/2 c  Orange juice
Cook onion in butter until tender. Add water, seasonings and orange
juice. Bring to a boil; stir in rice. Cover and reduce heat and
simmer for 20 mins.
or until water is absorbed and the rice is tender.
