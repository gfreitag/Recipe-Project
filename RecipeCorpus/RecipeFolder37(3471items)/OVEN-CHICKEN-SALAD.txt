Title: OVEN CHICKEN SALAD
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Vegetables, Salads, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Mushroom stems & pieces, can
-drained
1/4 c  Canned pimiento, finely
-chopped
8    Puff pastry shells, baked
3 tb Butter or margarine
5 tb Flour
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 3/4 c  Chicken stock
3 c  Cooked chicken
1. Combine all ingredients except cheese and the second 1 cup of
croutons in
a 1 1/2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole.
2. Sprinkle with cheese and the 1 cup of croutons or crushed potato
chips. 3. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 6 to 8 minutes or until
bubbly.
