Title: Orange Julius (Real Thing)
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Vanilla extract
1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  (heaping) ice
1 c  Orange juice
1 c  Water
2    Eggs
Combine all of the ingredients in a blender set on high speed for
exactly 1 minute.
Makes:  2 cups
