Title: TURKEY AND RICE QUICHE
Categories: Poultry, Pasta/rice
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Dried basil
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1/4 ts Ground red pepper
1/2 c  Skim milk
3    Eggs, beated
Vegetable cooking spray
1/2 c  (2 oz) Shredded Mozzarella
3 c  Cooked rice, cooled to room
Temp
1 1/2 c  Chopped cooked turkey
1    Medium tomato, seed & diced
1/4 c  Sliced green onions
1/4 c  Finely diced green pepper
1 tb Chopped fresh basil or
1.  Combine rice, turkey, tomato, onions, green pepper, basil, salt,
red
pepper, milk, and eggs in 13x9x2" pan coated with cooking spray.
Top
with cheeses.
2.  Bake at 375F for 20 minutes or until knife inserted near center
comes
out clean.  To serve, cut quiche into 8 squares, cut each square
diagonally into 2 trianges.
