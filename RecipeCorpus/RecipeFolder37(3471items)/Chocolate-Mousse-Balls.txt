Title: Chocolate Mousse Balls
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate
Yield: 20 balls
Vanilla wafer crumbs, walnut
Or Graham cracker crumbs
16 oz Hershey chocolate bars
8 oz Cool Whip, thawed
Melt Hershey's candy bars.  Set aside to cool.  Add thawed Cool Whip
and mix together.  Put into the refridgerator until set.  Roll into
balls. Roll into crushed cookie crumbs.  Store in the refrigerator.
