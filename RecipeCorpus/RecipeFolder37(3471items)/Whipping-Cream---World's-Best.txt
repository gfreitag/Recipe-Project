Title: Whipping Cream - World's Best
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
Little sugar and vanilla
1/4 c  Creme fresh
3/4 c  Whipping cream
Put 1/4 cup creme fresh in bowl and beat with sugar to taste and a few
drops of vanilla. Blend together with  3/4 cup whipping cream,
whipped.
