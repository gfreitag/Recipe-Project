Title: QUICK STIR-FRY
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 md Red pepper; chopped
1/4 c  EDEN Mirin
1/4 c  EDEN Brown Rice Vinegar
1/4 c  EDEN Organic Shoyu
-OR- Tamari
1 ts EDEN Kuzu; dissolved in...
1/4 c  -water
1 bn Green onions
1 pk Any EDEN Udon Soba
-OR Specialty Noodles
1 pk EDEN Shiitake Mushrooms
--(soaked in...
1 c  -water for 20 minutes)
1 tb EDEN Hot Pepper Sesame Oil
1 c  Pea pods, chopped
1    Head broccoli; chopped
Cook pasta, set aside. Reserve shiitake soaking liquid, discard stems,
slice tops. Cook shiitakes in reserved liquid for 15 minutes. Heat
oil, saute' broccoli and pea pods for 2 minutes. Add red pepper,
saute 1 minute more. Add shiitakes, reserved liquid, mirin, brown
rice vinegar and shoyu, bring to boil. Add dissolved kuzu until
mixture thickens and becomes clear. Serve over pasta, garnish with
green onions.

Cooking Time: 15-20 minutes
Prep Time: 30 minutes
Yield: 4-5 Servings
