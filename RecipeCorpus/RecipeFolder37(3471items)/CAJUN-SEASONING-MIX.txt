Title: CAJUN SEASONING MIX
Categories: Cajun/creol, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts White pepper
3/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Dried thyme leaves
1/2 ts Dried oregano leaves
1 tb Paprika, sweet
2 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Onion powder
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Ground red pepper (cayenne)
Mix all ingredients well.  This seasoning is fantastic when used on
not only fish, but chicken and steak as well. Dip the meats in melted
butter and then into this mix before cooking. Source: Paul
Prudhomme's Seasoned America.
