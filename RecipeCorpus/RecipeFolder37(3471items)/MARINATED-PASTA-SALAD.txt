Title: MARINATED PASTA SALAD
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/2 ts Basil
1/4 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Thyme
1    Clove garlic, crushed
Dash pepper
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1 ts Oregano leaves
1/2 ts Dry mustard
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
- thinly sliced
2/3 c  Sliced pepperoni
2    Medium tomatoes
4    Hard-cooked eggs, wedged
2 1/2 c  Pasta shells or spirals
- uncooked
1 c  Broccoli flowerets
- cooked tender-crisp
1/2    Medium red onion
Combine all dressing ingredients.  Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Cook pasta, following package directions.  Rinse cooked pasta in cold
water to prevent sticking.  Add broccoli, red onions and pepperoni.
Chill.  Just before serving, scile tomatoes into eights and add to
salad.  Mix in dressing
and toss well.  Garnish with egg wedges. Serve immediately.
Preparation time: 20 minutes
