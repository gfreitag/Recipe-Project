Title: South of the Border Dip^
Categories: Dips/spread, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Chopped onion
]1/2 green pepper, chopped
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 lb Ground beef, browned
1 cn (15 1/2 oz) refried beans
1 cn (8oz) tomato sauce
1 pk Taco seasoning
MMMMM---------------------SOUR CREAM TOPPING--------------------------
1/4 ts Chili powder
1 c  (8oz) sour cream
2 tb Shredded cheddar cheese
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Cheese
Tortilla chips
2 c  Shredded lettuce
1 c  (4oz) shredded cheddar
Bring to a boil the meat, beans, tomato sauce, seasonings, onion and
pepper. Pour into a 9" pie plate.

Combine topping ingredients and spread over meat. Top with lettuce and
cheese. Serve with chips.
