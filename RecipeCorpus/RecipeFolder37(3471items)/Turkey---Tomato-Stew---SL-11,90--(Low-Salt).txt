Title: Turkey - Tomato Stew - SL 11/90  (Low Salt)
Categories: Soup/stew, Poultry, Tasteofhome, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb No-salt-added tomato paste
1 ts Dried whole basil
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 1/2 c  Chopped, cooked turkey
-breast; skinned before
-cooking and cooked without
-salt
1 ea 10 ounce package frozen
-green peas
2 ea 10.5 ounce cans ready-to-
-serve, no-salt-added
-chicken broth
1 ea 14.5 ounce can no-salt-added
-tomatoes; undrained and
-chopped
1 c  Carrots; sliced
1 c  Celery; sliced
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1 cl Garlic; minced
Combine first 10 ingredients in a Dutch oven.  Bring to a boil;
reduce heat, and simmer, uncovered, 30 minutes.  Stir in turkey and
peas; simmer an additional 15 minutes.  Yield: 4 servings (252
calories per 1-1/3 cup serving).

Protein 40g, 1.3g fat, 23.4g carbohydrate, 94mg choloesterol, 489mg
sodium, and 102mg calcium.

From Louise E. Ellis of Tennessee, in November, 1990 Southern Living.
