Title: HAM DELITES
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 md Onion, grated
2 pk Party rolls
1 pk 4 oz. cooked ham
1 pk 4 oz. swiss cheese, sliced
1/4 lb Butter, softened
2 ts Mustard
2 tb Poppy seeds
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
Make the spread and cover rolls.  Add ham and cheese to fit each
roll. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes. Randy Rigg The
Pinnacle Club BBS 812-963-9139
