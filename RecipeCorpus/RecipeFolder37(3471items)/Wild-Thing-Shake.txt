Title: Wild Thing Shake
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1    Ripe banana
Milk
1    12 oz coke classic
1    Snickers candy bar
4 lg Scoops vanilla ice cream
Combine all in a blender and add milk to make it as thick or thin as
you may like.  LOADED WITH CALORIES!!!
