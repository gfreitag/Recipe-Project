Title: Eggs Obstaculos
Categories: Tex/mexican, Cheese/eggs, Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
8    Eggs
1 c  Beer
Toast
2 tb Oil
3/4 c  Tomato sauce
Hot sauce, to taste
Combine oil, tomato sauce, and hot sauce in a shallow pan.  Heat to
boiling.  Break eggs into pan and heat slowly until eggs are cooked.
Pour beer over.  Serve immediately over toast.  (I haven't tried this
but may sometime soon.)

Recipe from Michael Loo, FIDO: Cooking Conference, 12/27/95.

Converted to Meal-Master format by Arthur Cloninger, Huntington, WV.
