Title: Egg Noodles with Garlic and Herbs
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
Loosely packed
2 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
Salt and pepper
1/2 lb Egg noodles
4 lg Garlic clove(s)
1 1/2 c  Mixed herbs *
* any combo works well; pick 3-4 from these: dill, tarragon, basil,
sage, oregano, rosemary, Italian parsley, thyme.

1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling, salted water until
tender but still firm, 7-9 min. Drain well.

2. Meanwhile, chop the garlic, Mince the herbs; you'll have about 1
cup.

3. Combine the olive oil and garlic in a large frying pan. Cook over
medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the garlic is fragrant but
not browned, 2-3 min. Remove from the heat and stir in the minced
herbs.

4. Add the cooked noodles to the frying pan and toss. Season with
salt and pepper to taste and toss well.
