Title: HEALTH WEDGES
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Grated cheese
2    Slices, whole wheat bread
1 tb Crunchy style peanut butter
1 tb Celery, chopped
Place a slice of bread (buttered side down) onto the sandwich maker
and spread bread with peanut butter.  Sprinkle celery and cheese over
peanut butter.  Sprinkle celery and cheese over peanut butter.  Top
with a slice of bread (buttered side up).  Lower lid, latch and toast
for 2-3 minutes or until done.
