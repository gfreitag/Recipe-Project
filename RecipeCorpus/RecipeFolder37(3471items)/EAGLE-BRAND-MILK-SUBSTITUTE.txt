Title: EAGLE BRAND MILK SUBSTITUTE
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  -Boiling water
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Powdered Milk; PLUS:
2 tb Powdered Milk
Beat for 5 minutes and chill until ready to use in any recipe that
calls for condensed milk.  You can not tell the difference and it
cost just pennies compared to the store, and made with low fat milk
it has less fat content.
