Title: CREAMY HOT COCOA MIX
Categories: Beverages, Chocolate
Yield: 20 servings
1 ts Salt
1 c  Miniature marshallows
2 c  Instant nonfat dried milk
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa
Stir all ingredients together and store in a tightly covered jar for
up to 2 months.

TO USE:
Put 2 to 3 heaping tablespoons of the mix into a mug and fill
:
with boiling water or, for an extra rich drink, hot milk.  top with
whipped cram and cinnamon for a special treat.

Yield: 20 oz
