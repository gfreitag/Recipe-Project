Title: QUICK MACARONI AND CHEESE
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Buttered bread crumbs
2    Tomatoes; cut into wedges
2 cn Tuna; drained and flaked
32 oz Macroni/cheese (canned)
Combine tuna and macaroni and cheese and pour into a greased
casserole. Top with buttered bread crumbs and bake at 350F for 30
minutes or unitl bubbling.  During the last 5 minutes of baking time,
arrange the tomatoes in pinwheel fashion around the top and continue
baking.

From:  The Tuna Cookbook
