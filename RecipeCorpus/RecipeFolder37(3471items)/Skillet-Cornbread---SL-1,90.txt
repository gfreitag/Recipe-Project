Title: Skillet Cornbread - SL 1/90
Categories: Side dish, Breads, Holiday
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 ea Egg; beaten
1 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  Vegetable oil; divided
1 1/4 c  Yellow cornmeal
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 tb To 2 tb sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
Combine first 6 ingredients in a large bowl; add egg, buttermilk,
and 3 tablespoons oil, stirring just until dry ingredients are
moistened.
Pour remaining 1 tablespoon oil into an 8-inch cast-iron skillet,
and place in a 425 degree oven for 20 minutes or until lightly
browned. Yield: about 6 servings.
