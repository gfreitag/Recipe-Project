Title: Winter Vegetable & Barley Soup^
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Vegetarian, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Chopped turnips
1 c  Chopped potatoes
1 c  Cauliflower florets
1 c  Brossoli florets
1 c  Chopped asparagus
1 c  Cooked kidney beans
2 ts Dried basil
2 ts Dried oregano
1 ts Dried rosemary
1 c  Uncooked barley
14 c  Water
1/2 c  Low sodium tamari
2 ts Granulated garlic
2 ts Granulated onion
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 c  Chopped onions
1 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Chopped carrots
Boil balrey and 2 cups water in saucepan. Reduce heat and simmer,
covered, until done, about 45 minutes.

Boil remaining water, tamari, garlic, onion, and pepper. Add onion,
celery, carrots, turnips, and potatoes and boil 10 minutes.

Add cauliflower and boil 2 minutes. Add broccoli, asparagus, and
beans; cook one minute. Season to taste.

Per serving:
174 cal., 8g pro., 654mg sod., 34g carb., 1g fat, omg chol., 64mg
calcium.
