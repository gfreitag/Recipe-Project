Title: TUNA ALMANDINE
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Tuna; drained, flaked
2 tb Onion; chopped
1 pk Noodles with almond sauce mx
2 c  Boil water
2 oz Mushrooms; chopped, drained
Put noodles (just as they come from box) into buttered casserole and
sprinkle with sauce mix.  Stir in water, mushrooms, tuna, and onion.
Comver and bake at 375F for 25 minutes.  Sprinkle with almonds that
come in the package over top just before serving.

From:  The Tuna Cookbook
