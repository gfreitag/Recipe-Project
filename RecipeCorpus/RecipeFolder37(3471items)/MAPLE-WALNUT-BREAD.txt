Title: MAPLE WALNUT BREAD
Categories: Syrups, Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Bread Flour
2 tb Dry Milk
1 tb Sugar
5/8 c  Water warm
2 ts Yeast
1 tb Butter
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Maple Syrup, warm
1/4 c  Walnuts (Ground)
Shell 1/4 Cup walnuts (about 6 or 7). Grind up in a blender. Warm
maple syrup in microwave 30 seconds. Put all ingredients in Hitachi
Bread Machine and set bread color for light. Now start the regular
bread cycle and that's it.
For the Welbilt/DAK machine, use 7/8 cup of water for a 1 1/2 pound
loaf and the Zojirushi machine, add 1 tablespoon more water.
For the Welbilt/DAK machine, use 5/8 cup of water for the 1 pound loaf
and the Zojirushi machine, add 1 tablespoon more water.
This adaptation comes from the book, "Bread Machine Magic Book of
Helpful Hints", by Linda Rehberg and Lois Conway, ISBN 0-312-09759
