Title: Campbell's Oriental Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Wok, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
-mushroom soup
3 tb Water
1 tb Soy sauce
4 c  Hot cooked rice
Vegetable cooking spray
1 lb Skinless boneless chicken
-breasts, cut into strips
3 c  Cut-up vegetables -- *
10 3/4 oz Can reduced fat cream of
Prep time:  15 min.    Cooking time:  25 min.

* Use a combination of broccoli flowerets, sliced carrots and red or
green pepper strips.

Spray skillet with cooking spray and heat over medium-high heat 1
minute. Add chicken in 2 batches and stir-fry until browned. Set
chicken aside.
Reduce heat to medium. Remove skillet from heat and spray with cooking
spray. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
Add soup, water and soy sauce. Heat to a boil. Return chicken to pan.
Heat through. Serve over rice.
Serves 4.
