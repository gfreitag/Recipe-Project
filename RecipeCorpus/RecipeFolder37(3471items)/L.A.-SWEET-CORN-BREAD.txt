Title: L.A. SWEET CORN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 32 servings
15 oz Corn bread mix
18 1/2 oz Yellow cake mix
Mix cake and corn bread batters according to package directions. Blend
together and turn into 2 greased 9-inch square baking pans. Bake at
350F 30 to 35 minutes, or until corn bread springs back when lightly
touched.
