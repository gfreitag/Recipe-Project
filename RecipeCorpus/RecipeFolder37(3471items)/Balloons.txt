Title: Balloons
Categories: Puddings, Oldies
Yield: 6 servings
1    Teacup of sugar
1 tb Of butter
1 tb Of lard
6    Eggs
7 tb Of flour
1 qt Of milk
Cream the butter and  lard with the flour.  Beat the eggs and sugar
together.  Mix the milk in gradually, bake quickly and eat with sauce.

From:  Housekeeping in Old Virginia
Shared by: Pat Stockett
