Title: Chicken and Dressing
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 50 servings
1 tb Salt
1 tb Pepper
1 tb Bay leaf, crushed
2 1/2 qt Stock
1 pt Onion, chopped
6 lb Stale bread, cubed
1 pt Parsley, chopped
1 pt Celery, chopped
2 lb Butter, melted
2 qt Chicken
1 tb Sage
Mix all ingredients together.  Bake in large pans, 350 degree oven
for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
