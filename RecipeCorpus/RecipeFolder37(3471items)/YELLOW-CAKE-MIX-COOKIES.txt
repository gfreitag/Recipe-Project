Title: YELLOW CAKE MIX COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Water
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 pk Chocolate chips (12 oz.)
1 c  Crunchy peanut butter
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Oil
Mix all ingredients together then drop by teaspoon on ungreased cookie
sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes. Randy Rigg
