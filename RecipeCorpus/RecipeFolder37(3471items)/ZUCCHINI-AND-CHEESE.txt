Title: ZUCCHINI AND CHEESE
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 ea Tomatoes, sliced
1 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1 1/4 lb Zucchini
1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Ground pepper
Wash and scrub zucchini well and cut into thin round pieces.
Combine flour with half the salt, pepper and oregano in a bowl. Coat
zucchini slices in mixture.
Heat oil in a skillet and saute' zucchini until brown. Place zucchini
in a greased baking dish and top with tomato.
Combine sour cream and the rest of the salt, oregano and pepper and
spread over tomato slices.
Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
