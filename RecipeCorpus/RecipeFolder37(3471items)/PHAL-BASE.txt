Title: PHAL BASE
Categories: Curries, Herbs/spice, Indian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings  INGREDIENTS:

1    Recipe mild curry base
2 ts Ground Black Pepper
4 ts Chilli powder
1 tb Chopped Chilli pickle
Chilli Sauce (any), to taste (Optional)

DIRECTIONS:

1. Proceed with stages 1 and 2 of mild curry base.

2. Add the extra ingredients above and fry them in.

3. Carry on with stages 3 to 5.
