Title: Salmon Appetizers^
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Appetizers, Holiday
Yield: 12 servings
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
2 tb Chopped fresh chives
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
1 pk Frozen puff pastry
4 oz Smoked salmon, cut into thin
Strips
Preheat oven to 375F.

Cut 1 sheet puff pastry into 2" rounds and put into greased muffin
cups. Top with several strips of salmon and top with about 1
tablespoon combined cream cheese, chives and lemon juice.

Bake 15-18 minutes. Serve warm.
