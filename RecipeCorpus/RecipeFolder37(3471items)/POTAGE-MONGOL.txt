Title: POTAGE MONGOL
Categories: Soup/stew, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 6 servings
2    Soup cans water
1/4 c  Dry sherry
10 1/2 oz Can condensed tomato soup
10 1/2 oz Can condensed green pea soup
1. In a deep, 2 1/2-quart heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole
or heat-resistant, non-metallic soup tureen, combine the tomato
soup, pea soup and water; stir until smooth.
2. Heat, covered, in Microwave Oven 10 minutes or until soup
bubbles. Stir several times during heating.
3. Before serving, stir in sherry.
