Title: DUCHESS POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Appetizers, Microwave, Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Butter or margarine,
-softened
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Paprika
6 md Potatoes, peeled & cut into
-large cubes
Hot water
1 ts Salt
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1/4 c  Milk or light cream
1. In a deep, 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole
place potatoes and hot water to cover. Sprinkle with the 1
teaspoon salt. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 12 minutes to
18 minutes or until potatoes are fork-tender. Drain excess water.
2. Partially mash potatoes with a potato masher.
3. Add egg, milk, butter or margarine, the 1 teaspoon salt and
pepper. Beat with electric mixer until smooth.
4. Place in a heat-resistant, non-metallic serving dish and
sprinkle with paprika. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 4
minutes.
