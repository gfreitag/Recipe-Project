Title: Joan's Cranberry Orange Bread (machine)
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Orange marmelade
2 ts Yeast
1/2 c  Dried cranberries, chopped
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Oatmeal (regular)
1    Egg
3 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
Put everything except cranberries in bread machine in the order
listed. . Set on "light" and "mix bread" (for Hitachi) or equivalent.
Add cranberries at sound.

Make appropriate adjustments for other machines, or methods of baking.
This is an excellent holiday bread: very pretty and festive looking.
From the kitchen of: Joan Mershon
