Title: MAPLE-PECAN PRALINES
Categories: Candies, Desserts
Yield: 24 servings
1/4 ts Cream of tartar
2 c  Pecan pieces
2 ts Maple flavoring
3 c  Sugar
1 c  Evaporated Milk
2/3 c  Light corn syrup
2 tb Butter or margarine
Combine first five ingredients in a dutch oven; heat to boiling,
stirring constantly.  Stir in pecan pieces; cook over medium heat,
stirring occasionally, unti mixture reaches soft ball stage (236Ã¸).
Remove from heat; stir in flavoring.  Beat with wooden spoon 5 to 8
minutes or until mixture is creamy and begins to thicken.  Working
quickly, drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto wax paper; let cool.
