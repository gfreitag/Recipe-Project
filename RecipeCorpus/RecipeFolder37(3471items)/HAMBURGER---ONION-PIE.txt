Title: HAMBURGER - ONION PIE
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Flavor extender
2 tb Bisquick
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Cottage cheese
1 c  Bisquick
1/3 c  Light cream
1 lb Hamburger
2 ea Med. onions
1 ts Salt
Heat oven to 375 F. Mix bisquick with cream with hands, knead gently
10 times. Roll dough to fit 9 " pie plate. Saute beef and onion in
frypan, add next four ingredients mix well & put in pie plate. Beat
eggs slightly and blend in cottage cheese. Pour over meat, sprinkle
with paprika and bake 30 minutes
