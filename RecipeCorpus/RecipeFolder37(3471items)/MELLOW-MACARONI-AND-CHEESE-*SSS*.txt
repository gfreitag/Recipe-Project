Title: MELLOW MACARONI AND CHEESE *SSS*
Categories: Casseroles, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
Salt and pepper
8 oz Cheddar, grated (2c)
8 oz Macaroni,cooked, cooled
1 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Seasoned bread crumbs
-Sherilyn Schamber
1/4 c  Butter
3/4 c  Onion, chpd
1/4 c  Flour
1 c  Milk
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Prep: 30 mins  Cook: 30 mins. Melt butter in lg saucepan, add onion.
Saute, 3 mins. Add flour, cook 2 mins, stirring constatnly. Whisk in
milk, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper; cook and stir until
thickened. Stir in cheddar, ook and stir until melted. Mix in
macaroni and sour cream. Pour into greased 2 qt casserole; sprinkle
with bread crumbs. Bake 375, 30 mins. Source: Knudsen
