Title: Date & Orange Chutney^
Categories: Condiments, Relishes, Fruit, Vegetarian, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Brown rice syrup
1/4 c  Raisins
pn Crushed chili pepper
pn Salt
1    Chopped orange
1 c  Chopped dates
1 ts Freshly grated gingerroot
1/3 c  Water
1/3 c  Rice vinegar
Cook over  medium-low heat 20-30 minutes. Cool and serve.

Keeps well in refrigerator several weeks.

Per serving:
120 cal., 1g pro., 47mg sod., 32g carb., 0.2g fat, 0mg chol., 22mg
cal.
