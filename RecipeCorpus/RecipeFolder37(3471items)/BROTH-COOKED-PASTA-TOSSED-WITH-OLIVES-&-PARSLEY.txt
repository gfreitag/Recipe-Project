Title: BROTH COOKED PASTA TOSSED WITH OLIVES & PARSLEY
Categories: Main dish, Pasta/rice, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
25 ea Kalamata olives, pitted &
-- diced -=OR=- olive paste
1/4 c  Parsley, chopped
1 lb Pasta, your choice
1 qt Vegetable stock
3 tb Olive oil
Cook the pasta in the boiling stock until *al dente*.  Drain.
Reserve the stock for future use.
Toss the hot, drained pasta with the remaining ingredients & serve
immediately.
