Title: SUNDOWNER
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 oz Orange liqueur
Sweet-Sour Mix
1 ds Grenadine syrup
Ice cubes
1 1/2 oz Cognac
1/2 oz Galliano
Fill 8-ounce highball glass with ice cubes. Add cognac, Galliano and
orange liqueur. Add enough Sweet-Sour Mix to almost fill glass, then
add grenadine syrup.
