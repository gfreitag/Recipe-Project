Title: CHARLIE BROWN'S MOTHER'S BUTTERED OVEN-POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
Ice Water
Salt And Pepper
4 md Potatoes, Peeled
2 tb Butter, Melted
Slice the potatoes into lengths (like French fries).  Soak for 1 hour
in ice
water.  Remove and dry with paper towels.  Dip each piece of potato
into the
melted butter and arrange in a shallow baking pan. Salt lightl with a
salt shaker.  Cook in a preheated 375 - 400 Degree F. oven until the
potatoes are
brown and tender when poked with a long fork.
