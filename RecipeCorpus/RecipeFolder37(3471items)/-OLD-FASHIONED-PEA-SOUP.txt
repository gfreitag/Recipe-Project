Title:  OLD FASHIONED PEA SOUP
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 12 portions
1 md Onion, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 lb Whole green peas
1/2 lb Split green peas
1    Smoked ham shank, uncooked
Soak whole green peas overnight in a four quart kettle filled with
water. In the morning add split peas and remaining ingredients.  Boil
slowly for about 4 hours.  Remove ham shank and serve soup without
straining.

Source: The New Ford Treasury of FAVORITE RECIPES from Famous
Restaurants Compiled and tested by Nancy Kennedy, Women's Editor,
Ford Times - 1964
