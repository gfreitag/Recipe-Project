Title: CORNMEAL MUFFINS, JOYCE'S
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
1    Egg
1 c  Milk
2 tb Melted butter or margarine
1 c  Flour
1 c  Cornmeal
2 tb Sugar
4 ts Baking powder
Sift to-gether flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder and salt. Add
beaten egg and milk, mix well and add melted butter or margarine. Put
into greased muffin pans and bake in 400 F oven 20 -25 min
