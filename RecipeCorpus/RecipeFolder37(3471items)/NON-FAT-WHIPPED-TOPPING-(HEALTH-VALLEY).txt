Title: NON-FAT WHIPPED TOPPING (HEALTH VALLEY)
Categories: Low cal/fat, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Honey; up to 2 ts to taste
1/4 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  Nonfat milk
1/3 c  Instant nonfat dry milk
-solids
Pour nonfat milk into a small metal mixing bowl. Set bowl in freeazer
until ice crystals begin to form; about 15 to 20 minutes. Chill
beaters from mixer in freezer as well. Using a hand held electric
mixer, beat nonfat dry milk solids into nonfat milk. Continue to beat
on high until soft peaks form, about 2 minutes. Add honey to taste
and vanilla and beat until stiff peaks form, about 2 additional
minutes. Use within 20 minutes to avoid cream separating. Makes 1 1/2
cups 1/4 cup of product contains 14 calories with 6% fat content!
