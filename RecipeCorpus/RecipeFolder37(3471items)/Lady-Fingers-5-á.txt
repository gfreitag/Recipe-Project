Title: Lady Fingers 5 รก
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/3    Flour
1/4    Salt
5 tb Powdered sugar
3    Egg whites
2    Egg yolks
Add powdered sugar to stiffly beaten egg whites.  Add 2 well
beaten yolks and vanilla extract.  Fold in flour, which has been
sifted twice with salt.  Line a pan with paper, don't grease it.
Press the batter through a pastry bag on the paper forming a strip 4
inches long and 1 inch wide.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar and bake
in slow oven (300 degrees) for 10 minutes.
