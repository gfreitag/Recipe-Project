Title: Creamy Cucumber Potato Salad^
Categories: Potatoes, Salads, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Seeded chopped cucumber
3/4 c  Light sour cream
2/3 c  Fatfree mayonnaise or that
Fake stuff
1 tb Prepared mustard
1/8 ts To 1/4 white pepper
3 c  Cooked, peeled, cubed
Potatoes
2    Coarsley chopped hard cooked
Eggs
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Chopped green onions
Combine vegetables. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over; stir
gently
to coat. Cover; chill until needed.

Per half cup serving:
110cal., 4g pro., 16g carb., 2g fiber, 3g fat, 46mg chol., 260mg sod.,
290mg
pot, 1 starch and 1/2 fat exchanges.
