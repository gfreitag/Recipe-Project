Title: GRAHAM WAFER DELIGHTS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Icing sugar
1 pk Graham wafers
1 pk Dates
1/2 c  Brown sugar
Place graham wafers in bottom of 9 x 9 inch pan, set aside. Cook dates
with the brown sugar till soft, remove from heat and add vanilla.
Cool. Spread on the layer of wafers in pan, then cover with more
wafers. Can be iced with chocolate icing.
