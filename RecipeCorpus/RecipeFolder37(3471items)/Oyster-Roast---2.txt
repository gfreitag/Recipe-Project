Title: Oyster Roast - 2
Categories: Fish/seafoo, White house
Yield: 1 batch
MMMMM---------------------------RECIPE--------------------------------
Put one quart of oysters in a basin with their own liquor and let them
boil three or four minutes;  the oysters should plump and the edges
curl. Season with a little salt, pepper, and three tablespoons of
butter.  Serve on buttered toast.

From The White House Cookbook, 1929 ed.  MMed by Dave Sacerdote

Dave's note:  Don't know why they called this an Oyster "roast."  The
oysters are simmered, not roasted.
