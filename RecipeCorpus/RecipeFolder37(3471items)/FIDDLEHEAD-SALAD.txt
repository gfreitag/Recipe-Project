Title: FIDDLEHEAD SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
2    Drops tabasco sauce
12 tb Olive oil
4    Green onions, chopped
2 pk 10 oz. frozen fiddleheads
5 tb Wine vinegar
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
Thaw Fiddleheads and mix with the vinegar and the two sauces and salt
& pepper to taste. Let marinate in fridge for 4-8 hours. Add oil and
onions before serving.
