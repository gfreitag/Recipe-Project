Title: MILK ROLLS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 servings
1 pk Yeast
2    Eggs beaten
3 c  Flour
1 c  Scalded milk
2 tb Shortening
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
To milk add shortening, salt and sugar.
Dissolve yeast in 1/2 c warm water with 1 tsp. sugar and add to milk
mixture, add beaten eggs. Add flour and let rise until very light then
shape into rolls and let rise again till light and bake in 375 oven
20 -30 minutes.
