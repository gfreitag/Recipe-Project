Title: Maple Walnut Butter
Categories: Syrups, Nuts/grains, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 1/4 cup
1/4 c  Maple syrup
1/2 c  Butter at room temperature
1/2 c  Walnuts, finely chopped
Stir butter, walnuts, and syrup together in a small bowl until
blended. Refrigerate until ready to use.

To soften, remove from refrigerator 15 minutes before serving.

NOTE:  I would just nuke it for 5 seconds. *:)

Recipe from Bobbie Kopf, FIDO: Cooking Conference, 12/22/95.
