Title: PEACHY SALSA
Categories: Main dish, Pasta/rice, Vegetables
Yield: 2 cups
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic, crushed
1 ts Olive oil
2 ts Balsamic vinegar
Cayenne, to taste
1/3 c  Cilantro, lightly packed
2 c  Peaches, peeled & diced
2 c  Tomatoes, diced
1 ts Lemon juice
1 pn Black pepper
Place all ingredients in a blender or a food processor & process for
a few seconds.  Serve immediately over hot, fresh pasta or
refirgerate until ready to serve.
