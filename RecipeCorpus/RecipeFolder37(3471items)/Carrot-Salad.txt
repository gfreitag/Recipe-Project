CARROT SALAD

Carrot salad is easy to make and refreshing.  When Israelis abroad
long for their homeland, they recall the incomparable tastes of
Israel's fruits and vegetables.

Peel and grate carrots.  Mix in a bowl with raisins and orange juice.
Add sugar to taste.
