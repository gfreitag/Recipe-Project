Title: RABBIT BOURGUIGNON
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
6 sl Bacon, lean
18    Baby onions or shallots,pee
18    Button mushrooms
3 tb Flour
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh parsley, chopped
1 md Rabbit, cut-up fryer
2 c  Dry red wine
1 md Onion, cubed
2 lg Carrots, chopped
Bouquet garni *
3    Garlic cloves, minced
1/4 ts Cloves, ground
* See Bouquet garni recipe for ingredients.
Calories 386; Protein 27 g. Carbohydrate 21 g.; Fat 12 g/serving.
Wipe meat pieces and place in shallow glass or ceramic pan. Combine
wine, onion, carrot, Bouquet garni, garlic and cloves; pour over meat
and toss to fully coat all pieces. Cover and let stand 4 hours at
room temperature ~--
