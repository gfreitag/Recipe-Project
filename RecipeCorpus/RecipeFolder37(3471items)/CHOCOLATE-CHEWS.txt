Title: CHOCOLATE CHEWS
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 30 servings
1/8 ts Salt
3 tb Powdered sweetener
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Skim milk
1 c  Flour; MINUS:
2 tb -Flour, Sifted
2 tb Cocoa
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/8 ts Cinnamon
Sift together flour, cocoa, soda, cinnamon, salt and sweetener. Blend
together oil and milk in seperate small bowl. Stir milk mixture into
dry mixture gradually. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto a Pam sprayed pan.
Bake at 350 for 10 mins. Cool and serve.
Each 2 cookies = 1 fruit
