Title: FRIED TURKEY BREAST
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
Pepper (to taste)
1 c  Flour
1    Breast of young turkey
Salt (to taste)
Slice turkey cross-grain in 1/4 inch thick slices.  Season with salt
and pepper.  Roll in flour.  Deep fry in hot fat until lightly
browned. Serves 4 to 6.
