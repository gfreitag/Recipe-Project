Title: HONEY DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Prepared mustard
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Minced onion
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 ts Minced parsley
Salt, pepper
1/2 c  Oil
Mix vinegar, mayonnaise and mustard until smooth. Add sugar, onion,
honey, and parsley and season to taste with salt and pepper. Slowly
beat in oil until well blended.

Makes 2 cups
