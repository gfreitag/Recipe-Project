Title: HONEY ALMOND DIP
Categories: Dip, Fruit
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
3 tb Slivered almonds -- toasted
Chopped
2 1/2 tb Honey
In small bowl, combine all ingredients. Stir or whisk until smooth.
Makes about 3/4 cup.
