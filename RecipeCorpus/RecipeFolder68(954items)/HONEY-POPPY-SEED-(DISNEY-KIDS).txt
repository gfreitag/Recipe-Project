Title: HONEY POPPY SEED (DISNEY KIDS)
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 24 Servings
1 c  Oil
1/3 c  Vinegar
2 tb Water
2 tb Honey
1 1/2 tb Poppy seeds
Blend the oil, vinegar, water, and honey in a blender until creamy,
then stir in the poppy seeds. Store in the refrigerator. Makes 1-1/2
cups.
