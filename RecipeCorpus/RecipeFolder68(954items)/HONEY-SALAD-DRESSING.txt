Title: HONEY SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Powdered Dry Mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Celery Salt
1/2 c  Honey
3 tb Lemon Juice
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 c  Salad Oil
Mix the dry ingredients.  Add the honey, lemon juice and vinegar.
Slowly add the salad oil, beating until well blended. Makes about 2
cups.
