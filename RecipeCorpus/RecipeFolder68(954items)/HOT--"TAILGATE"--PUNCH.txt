Title: HOT  "TAILGATE"  PUNCH
Categories: New, Text, Import
1 ga apple cider
Yield: 4 Servings
1 sm can condensed orange
juice(frozen)
:
1/3 c  brown sugar
5    whole cloves-to 6 cloves
2    cinnamon sticks-to 4 sticks

Combine ingredients and simmer 1 to 2 hours.  Serve in crock pot or
in a cleaned out pumpkin.  Pumpkin keeps punch warm but does not
change the flavor of the punch.
