Title: HOT CRAB DIP #3
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 5 Servings
8 oz Cream cheese
1 tb Milk
1/2 ts Horseradish
Pepper
1 pk Crabmeat
2 tb Onions chopped
1/4 ts Salt
Mix all of the ingredients (not in a blender or food processor). Put
in a baking dish and top with either sliced almonds or paprika. Bake
in a 375F oven for 15 minutes. Serve with chips.
