Title: HONEY BRAN MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Breakfast, Low-fat, Snacks
Yield: 12 Servings
1 1/3 c  Flour
3/4 tb Baking powder
1 1/8 c  Milk
3/8 c  Honey
1    Eggs
2 tb Oil
2 1/4 c  Cereal, bran, unsweetened
3/8 c  Raisins
If using sweetened cereal, only use half the amount of honey.

Preheat oven to 400.  Stir together flour and baking powder. In large
mixing bowl, beat together milk, honey, egg, and oil, until thoroughly
mixed.  Add flour mixture, cereal, and raisins, stirring only until
combined.  Portion batter evenly into greased muffin cups. Bake about
25 minutes, until lightly browned. Serve warm.

Per muffin: 210 calories, 2 g dietary fiber, 4 g fat
