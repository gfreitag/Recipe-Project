Title: HONEY DOUGH (EDIBLE)
Categories: Kids
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Powdered milk
1/2 c  Creamy peanut butter
1/2 c  Honey
Mix together well. Butter the childs hands, and have them shape dough
into balls, trees, animals, etc. Then eat and enjoy. (Note: This
dough is somewhat sticky, so a child who gets frustrated easily may
need the parent to help.)
