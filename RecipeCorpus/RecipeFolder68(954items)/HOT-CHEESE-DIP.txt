Title: HOT CHEESE DIP
Categories: Sauces, Cheese/eggs, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 8 Servings
7 oz Can minced clams, drained
1/4 c  Green pepper, finely chopped
4    Scallions, finely chopped
2 dr Tabasco sauce
10 oz Sharp cheese spread
Garlic salt, to taste.
1. Combine all ingredients in a deep, 1 1/2-quart, heat- resistant,
non-metallic casserole. 2. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 5
minutes or until cheese melts. Stir at the end of each minute. 3.
Serve very hot with crisp corn chips for dipping. Makes 1 1/2 cups.
