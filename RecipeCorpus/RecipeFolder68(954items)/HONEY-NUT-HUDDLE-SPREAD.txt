Title: HONEY-NUT HUDDLE SPREAD
Categories: Dip, Nuts, Super bowl
Yield: 2 Cups
2 tb Orange juice
1 tb Honey
8 oz Soft cream cheese
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Honey roasted nuts,chopped
Blend orange juice, honey and cream cheese.  Stir in raisins and
chopped nuts.  Chill for at least one hour before serving. Serve with
crackers and potato chips. Source: Cincinnati Enquirer 1/19/97
