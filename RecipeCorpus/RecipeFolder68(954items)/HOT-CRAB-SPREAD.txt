Title: HOT CRAB SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz Cream cheese; at room temper
1 tb Milk
2 ts Worchestershire sauce
7 1/2 oz Crabmeat; -or-
7    Imitation crab
2 tb Green onion; chopped
Recipe by: Kathy Cox <coxk@WSU.EDU> Cream cream cheese and add milk
and worchestershire sauce. Blend well. Drain and flake crabmeat. Add
to cheese mixture with green onions.

Turn into greased 8 inch pie plate or small shallow baking dish. Top
with 2 tablespoons toasted slivered almonds. Bake at 350 degrees for
15 minutes. Serve warm on crackers.
