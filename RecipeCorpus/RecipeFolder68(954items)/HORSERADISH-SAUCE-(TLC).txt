Title: HORSERADISH SAUCE (TLC)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
50 g  Grated horseradish
150 ml Double cream
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Dry mustard powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts White pepper
2 ts White wine vinegar
Fresh horseradish must be soaked in cold water one hour and then
washed prior to use.

1. Scrape the horseradish into very thin shreds with a sharp knife.
2. Whip the cream into stiff peaks and then fold in the horseradish,
sugar, mustard, salt, pepper and vinegar. 3. Pour into a jar
