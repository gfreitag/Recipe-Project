Title: HOT SPINACH ARTICHOKE DIP
Categories: Dips
1 Cn (14 oz) Artichoke hearts, --
drained and
chopped
Yield: 6 Servings
:
:
1 c  Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
4 Oz Parmesan cheese -- shredded
1 Cn (4 oz) chopped green chilies
drained
:
1    Clove Garlic -- minced
1 pk frozen chopped spinach --
thawed and drained
:

Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Mix all ingredients. Spoon into 9 inch pie
plate or shallow ovenproof dish. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until
lightly browned. Sprinkle with sliced green onions and chopped
tomatoes, if desired. Serve with tortilla chips, crackers or assorted
cocktail bread slices. Makes 2 cups.
