Title: HONEY BAKED/GLAZED CHICKEN
Categories:
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ea Onion soup mix
2 tb Honey
1/2 c  Water -- `
6    Pieces chicken

Optional;
1 ea Onion -- sliced thin

Mix soup, honey, and water.  Pour over chicken in a shallow baking
dish sprayed w/ pam.   Top with sliced onion. Cover and bake at 350
for about 30 to 45 min. Uncover and continue to bake untill chicken
is done and golden brown. Baste if needed.
