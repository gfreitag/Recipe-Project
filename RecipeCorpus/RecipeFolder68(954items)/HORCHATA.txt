Title: HORCHATA
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Rice, washed
2 qt Water
1    Cinnamon stick
Sugar to taste
Mix together all ingredients; let stand 3 hours. Simmer for 1/2 hour.
Puree in a blender and strain through a cloth. Taste for sweetness
and add sugar if necessary. Chill and serve over ice.

That's it.
