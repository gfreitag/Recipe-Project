Title: HOMEMADE PAN COAT (PAN GREASE)
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Shortening -- such as
Crisco
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Flour
Blend the shortening, oil and flour until smooth. Cover and store in
the refrigerator.  Use to grease cake pans, muffin pans and casserole
dishes. Note: A processor, blender or mixer may be used to blend
ingredients smoothly.
