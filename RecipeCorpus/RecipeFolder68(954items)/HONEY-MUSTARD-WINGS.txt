Title: HONEY MUSTARD WINGS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 36 Appetizers
1 pk Campbell's Dry Onion with
-Chicken Broth Soup and
-Recipe Mix
1/3 c  Honey
2 tb Spicy-brown mustard
18    Chicken wings
1. In large bowl, mix soup mix, honey and mustard. Set aside. 2. Cut
wings at joints and discard tips. Add to soup mixture. Toss to coat.
3. Place chicken in baking pan. Bake at 400'F. 45 minutes or until
chicken is done, turning once.
