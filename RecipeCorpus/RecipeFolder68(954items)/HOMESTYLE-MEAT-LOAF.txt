Title: HOMESTYLE MEAT LOAF
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
12 oz Heinz HomeStyle Brown Gravy
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1/2 ts Salt
Dash pepper
Combine 1/4 cup gravy with beef and next 5 ingredients. Shape into 8"
X 4" loaf in baking pan. Bake in 350 F oven 1 hour. Let stand 5
minutes. Meanwhile, heat remaining gravy; serve over meat loaf.
