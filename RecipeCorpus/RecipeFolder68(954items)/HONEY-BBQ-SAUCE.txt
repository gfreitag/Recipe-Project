Title: HONEY BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Onion -- minced
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1    8 oz
1/2 c  Honey
2 tb Cider or sherry wine
Vinegar
2 tb Fresh parsley -- minced
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Cayenne
Tomato sauce
Heat oil in medium skillet. Add onion and garlic and saute on medium
~ high heat until onion is transparent. Add remaining ingredients and
simmer for 5 min. Remove from heat. Makes 1 cup.
