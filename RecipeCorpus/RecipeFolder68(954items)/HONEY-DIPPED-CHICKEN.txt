Title: HONEY DIPPED CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Quick meals
Yield: 6 Servings  ~--------DISH INGREDIENTS-----------
1 c  Flour
1/4 lb Butter
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ts Paprika
3 lb Chicken breasts, skinned,  -
- boned
:

~-----------SAUCE----------------------------------
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Lemon juice

Preparation:: Melt butter in shallow baking dish. Coat chicken pieces
with dry ingredients by shaking in a bag. Place chicken in pan and
bake for 30 minutes at 400f. Mix sauce and pour over chicken and
return to oven. Bast occasionally and bake for 15 minutes more.
