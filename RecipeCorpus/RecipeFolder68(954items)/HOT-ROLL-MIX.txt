Title: HOT ROLL MIX
Categories: Mixes, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
5 lb Unbleached All Purpose Flour
4 ts Salt
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Instant Non Fat Dry Milk
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.  Stir together to distribute
evenly.  Put in a large airtight container. Lable as HOT ROLL MIX and
store in a cool dry place.  Use within 6 to 8 months.  Makes about 22
cups of HOT ROLL MIX.
