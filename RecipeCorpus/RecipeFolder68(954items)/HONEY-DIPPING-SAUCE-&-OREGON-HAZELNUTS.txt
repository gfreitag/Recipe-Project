Title: HONEY DIPPING SAUCE & OREGON HAZELNUTS
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 12 Servings
1/4 c  Honey
1/8 c  Soft butter
1/8 c  Apricot nectar
1/4 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 ts Soy sauce
Chopped Oregon hazelnuts
Heat all ingredients, except Oregon hazelnuts, until well blended.
Cool to room temperature. Serve with barbecued pork, turkey filets or
chicken wings. Dip meat into sauce and then into Oregon hazelnuts.
