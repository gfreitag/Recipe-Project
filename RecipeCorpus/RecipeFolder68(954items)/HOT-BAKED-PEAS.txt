Title: HOT BAKED PEAS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
1 cn Peas, drained
1    Green pepper, mango cut in
Rounds
1 sm Onion
1 cn Tomatoes
Pepper
Place in buttered casserole peas, green pepper, onion, tomatoes and
pepper in layers.  Cover with juice from the tomatoes. Bake,
uncovered at 350 degrees until mango is done or about an hour and a
half. Randy Rigg
