Title: HOMINY CASSEROLE
Categories: Grains, Casseroles
Yield: 1 Servings
1 md Onion, chopped
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 tb Cooking oil
1 c  (8 ounces) sour cream
3/4 c  Shredded cheddar cheese,
-divided
1/4 c  Milk
1 cn (4 ounces) chopped green
-chilies
1/4 ts Ground cumin
3 cn (15 ounces each) golden
-hominy, drained
In a small skillet, cook onion and garlic in oil until tender. Remove
from heat. In a bowl, combine onion mixture, sour cream, half of the
cheese, milk, chilies and cumin. Add hominy; mix well. Pour into a
greased 2-qt. baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at 350! for 30 minutes or
until heated through. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Serve
immediately.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
