Title: HONEY GARLIC CHICKEN WINGS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lb Chicken wings
1/2 c  Honey
3 tb Vinegar, cider
1/4 ts Pepper, black
x  Garlic salt
1/4 c  Soy sauce
5    Garlic cloves; minced
Trim wings and Season with garlic salt; arrange in a 13"x9"x2"
baking dish. Cover with foil and bake at 325F for one hour until
tender; drain all liquid.
Combine remaining ingredients in a saucepan and simmer five mins.
Drizzle honey mixture over spareribs and bake, uncovered, at 350F for
30 mins., baste every 10 mins. Submitted By DONW1948@AOL.COM On MON,
