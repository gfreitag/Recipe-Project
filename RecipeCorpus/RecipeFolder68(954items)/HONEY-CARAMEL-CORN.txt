Title: HONEY CARAMEL CORN
Categories: Db, Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Sugar, brown
1/3 c  Honey
2 tb Butter
1 x  Popcorn, salted
1/2 c  Nuts
Combine all except nuts and popcorn and melt until sugar is bubbling
around edges. Pour over popcorn and nuts.

From: The Silverbow Honey Co., Moses Lake, WA 98837
