Title: HONEY-CURRY SAUCE
Categories: Jaw, Sauces, Low-fat
Yield: 6 Servings
2 ts Vegetable oil
1 tb Flour
2 ts Curry powder
1 ts Salt
1 c  Low fat milk
6 tb Honey
In small saucepan, heat oil. Add flour, curry powder and salt; cook
and stir over medium heat 1 minute. Stir in milk and honey until
mixture is smooth; cook and stir until thickened. Serve over steamed
vegetables or mix vegetables with sauce and use as a topping for
baked potatoes.
