Title: HONEY MUSTARD - COUNTRY LIVING
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
3/4 c  Dijon-style prepared mustard
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Honey
2 tb Dry mustard
1. In 1-quart saucepan, heat prepared mustard, oil, honey, and dry
mustard just until boiling, stirring constantly.

2. Remove from heat; cool and spoon mustard into jars or crocks. Seal
and store in the refrigerator.
