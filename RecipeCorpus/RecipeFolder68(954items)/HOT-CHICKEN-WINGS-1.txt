Title: HOT CHICKEN WINGS 1
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lb Chicken wings
4 pk Taco seasoning mix
Flour
Oil
Celery stalks
Blue cheese dressing
Mix together the flour and taco seasoning, get oil hot in pan. Flour
chicken wings and fry until done. Serve with celery and blue cheese
for dipping.
