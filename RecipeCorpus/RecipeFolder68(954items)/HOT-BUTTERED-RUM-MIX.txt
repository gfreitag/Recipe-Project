Title: HOT BUTTERED RUM MIX
Categories: Beverages
1/2 lb better -- room temperature
Yield: 48 Servings
1 qt vanilla ice cream -- barely
softened
:
1 lb confectioner's sugar
1 lb light brown sugar
light rum
:
8 1/2 qt hot water
ground nutmeg for garnish
:

This drink is very good without the rum so the kids can enjoy it too.
In a 2 qt. bowl mix the butter and sugars with an electric mixer.
Stir in the soft ice cream. Put mixture in freezer container and
store in freezer.

To mix drinks: In a large metal or plactic bowl use all of the mix
from this recipe and add 8 1/2 quarts of HOT water. Serve from punch
bowl. Mix with rum to taste and garnish with a dash of nutmeg.
