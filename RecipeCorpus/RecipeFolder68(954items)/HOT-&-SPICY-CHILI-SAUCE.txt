Title: HOT & SPICY CHILI SAUCE
Categories: Pickles, Sauces
Yield: 1 Serving
5 lb Ripe tomatoes
2 md Onions
3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1 ts Crushed red pepper
1 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Curry powder
Chop vegetables, add remaining ingredients and simmer for about 1
hour or a little more until thick. Bottle in sterilized jars.
