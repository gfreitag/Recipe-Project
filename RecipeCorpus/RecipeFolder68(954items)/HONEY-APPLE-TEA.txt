Title: HONEY APPLE TEA
Categories: Beverages, Teas
Yield: 8 Servings
4    Tea bags
3 c  Boiling water
1/3 c  Sue Bee Honey
Lemon slices
3 c  Unsweetened apple juice
In a 2-quart pot, brew tea bags in boiling water. Remove tea bags, add
honey and apple juice. Simmer until hot. Ladle into cups and garnish
with lemon slices.
