Title: HONEY CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Butter
2 c  Honey
2    Eggs
1 c  Buttermilk
1/4 ts Salt
4 c  Flour
2 ts Soda
1 ts Ginger
1 ts Cinammon
Cream butter and honey well. Add beaten eggs and buttermilk, sifted
four with soda, spices and salt. Mix well, and bake in two layers, in
a slow (275Ã¸) oven, about thirty minutes.  Watch oven closely because
this cake burns easily.
