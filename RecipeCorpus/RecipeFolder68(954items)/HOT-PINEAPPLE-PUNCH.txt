Title: HOT PINEAPPLE PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 9 Servings
1 cn 46-oz unsweetened pineapple
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Lime juice
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
3 1/4 c  Dry white wine
1 x  Cinnamon Sticks
1 x  Fresh pineapple
In large saucepan, combine pineapple juice, sugar, lime juice, and
nutmeg; bring to boiling, stirring till sugar dissolves. Reduce heat;
stir in wine. Heat through but DO NOT BOIL.  Pour into heat-proof
glasses or mugs. Garnish each serving with cinnamon stick stirer and
pineapple wedge. Makes about 9 cups.
