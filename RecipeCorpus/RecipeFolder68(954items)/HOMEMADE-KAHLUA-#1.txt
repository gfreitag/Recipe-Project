Title: HOMEMADE KAHLUA #1
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 2 Fifths
3 c  White sugar
12 ts Rounded Yuban instant coffee
4 c  Water
1    Fifth Vodka
3 ts Vanilla
Bring sugar, instant coffee and water to boil.  Simmer gently for one
hour. Cool.  Stir in Vodka and Vanilla. Bottle in two empty Fifths.
Ready to use immedietly.
