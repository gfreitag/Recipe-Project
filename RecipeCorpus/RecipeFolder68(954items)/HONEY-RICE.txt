Title: HONEY RICE
Categories: Rice, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Rice, cooked
1/2 c  Raisins
2 1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Honey
2 tb Butter
1 ts Grated Lemon peel
1 tb Lemon Juice
Combine rice, raisins, milk, honey, and margarine. Bring to a boil,
reduce the heat and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Stir in lemon peel and juice.

For a festive touch, put a Tbsp of Whipped cream or other topping on
each serving, and top with cherry or strawberry.

From: Rice Council of America 1971
