Title: HONEY-YOGURT DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Firm Tofu Drained
2 tb Lemon Juice
2 tb Honey
1/2 ts Ground Cumin
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Plain Yogurt
Combine All Ingredients Except Yogurt in Food Processor. Cover &
Process Until Smooth.  Place in A Bowl. Add Yogurt, Stirring Well.
Cover & Chill.
(Fat 0.3, Chol. 0)
