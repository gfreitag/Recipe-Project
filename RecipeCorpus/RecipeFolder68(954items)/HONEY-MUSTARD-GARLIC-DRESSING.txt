Title: HONEY-MUSTARD GARLIC DRESSING
Categories: Condiments, Salads
Yield: 1 Cup
2    Heads roasted garlic
2 tb Dijon mustard
1 1/2    -2 tbsp mild honey
3 tb Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Apple juice, unsweetened
1/4 ts Salt
Black pepper to taste
Squeeze the garlic puree from the skins into a blender. Add remaining
ingredients and puree until smooth and creamy. Taste for seasoning.
Covered tightly and refrigerated this dressing should keep 4 days.
