Title: HOT CROSS BUNS GLAZE
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Milk
2 tb Water
3 tb Superfine Sugar
Stir ingredients together in a small saucepan over medium heat until
sugar is dissolved.

*If currents are not available, substitute sultanas (golden raisins)
or just use 1 c. regular raisins.
