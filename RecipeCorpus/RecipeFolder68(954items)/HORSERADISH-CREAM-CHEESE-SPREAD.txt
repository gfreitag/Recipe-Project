Title: HORSERADISH-CREAM CHEESE SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Dairy, Spreads
Yield: 2 Cups
8 oz Cream cheese; room
-temperature
1/4 c  Fresh chives; finely chopped
2 tb Buttermilk
1/2 c  Horseradish; grated and
-drained
Mash all ingredients together until well combined, using just enough
buttermilk to get the desired consistency.
Use as a spread on rye or pumpernickel bread, on bagels or
crackers.
