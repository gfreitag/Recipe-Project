Title: HOT STUFFED AVOCADO
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 8 Servings
4 lg Avocados (peel and halve)
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/16 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Butter or Margarine
2 tb Green onion (chopped)
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/16 ts Cayenne pepper
1/4 c  Flour
2 c  Light cream
2 c  Chicken (cooked & minced)
1 c  Sharp Cheddar (grated)
Roll outside of peeled avocados in lemon juice to keep color. Put in
large baking pan. Sprinkle with lemon juice and garlic powder. Melt
butter; add green onions, celery salt and pepper. Cook 5 minutes.
blend in flour. Add cream. Cook while stirring until thickened. Fold
in chicken and 1/2 cup cheese. Bake avocados at 350 degrees for 15
minutes. remove to serving plates. Ladle filling over avocados. top
with remaining cheese.
