Title: HORSERADISH & BEET RELISH
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Diced or julienne cooked bee
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Horseradish
2 tb Onion juice
1/2 c  Vinegar
Pour all into saucepan, toss lightly and bring to a boil. Serve hot
or cold with meat or fish. If served cold, allow to chill overnight.
