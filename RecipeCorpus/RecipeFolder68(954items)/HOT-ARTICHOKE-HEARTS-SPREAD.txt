Title: HOT ARTICHOKE HEARTS SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Grated parmesan cheese
14 oz Artichoke hearts; drained
1 ts Garlic salt
Chop artichoke and combine with other ingredients. Place mixture in
shallow baking pan and bake uncovered at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.
Serve with crackers.
