Title: HONEY-MUSTARD DRESSING (EATING WELL)
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
--from PNFF51A--
1/4 c  Nonfat yogurt
1 1/2 ts Cider vinegar
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 ts Honey
Salt
Black pepper; freshly ground
In a small bowl, whisk together ingredients until well blended.
Alternatively, combine all ingredients in a small jar, secure lid and
shake until blended.

Good on salads of mixed bitter greens, such as arugula, chicory,
escarole, radicchio and watercress.

Makes about 1/4 cup.
