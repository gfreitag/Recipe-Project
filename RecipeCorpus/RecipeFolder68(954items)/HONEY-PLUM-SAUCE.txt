Title: HONEY PLUM SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Plums pitted -- chopped
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Onion -- minced
1 ts Lemon zest -- grated
1/2 ts Fresh ginger root
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cloves -- ground
1/4 ts Allspice
Salt and pepper -- to taste
Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan, bring to boil, reduce
heat and simmer for 15 min. or until plums are completely cooked.
Puree mixture in a blender or food processor. Taste and adjust
sweetness, adding more honey if nessary. return to saucepan and
simmer for 5 min.
