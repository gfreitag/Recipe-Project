Title: HONEY SPICED BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/4 c  Catchup
2/3 c  Salad oil
3/4 c  Vinegar
5 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Honey
2 tb Dry mustard
3 ts Ginger, fresh grated
1 ea Lemon, sliced thinly
3 tb Butter
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and heat to blend together.

Source:  Best Barbeque Recipes by Mildred Fischer
