Title: HOT MULLED ORANGE JUICE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 Servings
2 c  Orange juice
1    Stick cinnamon
2    Whole cloves
Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan and simmer for 15 to 20
minutes.  Remove cinnamon stick and cloves before serving.

Source:  Reed Mangels, Ph.D., R.D.
Vegetarian Journal  January/February 1997
