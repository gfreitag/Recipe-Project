Title: HOT DOG PROVENCALE
Categories: Meats
Yield: 5 Servings
8 ea Hot dog
8 ea Bacon slices
1 c  Onion, minced
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
1 cn Tomatoes (8-oz)
1/2 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
Fry bacon, onion and garlic until bacon is crisp. Drain most of bacon
fat. Stir in tomatoes, salt and pepper. Heat to a boil, reduce heat
and simmer about 10 minutes. Add hot dogs; cover and simmer about 15
min.
