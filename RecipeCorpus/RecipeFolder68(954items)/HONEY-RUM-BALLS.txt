Title: HONEY-RUM BALLS
Categories: Candies, Snacks, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Vanilla wafer crumbs(7.5 oz)
1/2 c  Rum
1/2 c  Honey
1 lb Walnuts, ground
Confectioners Sugar
Mix 1st 4 ingredients, shape in balls and roll in confectioners sugar.
Store in airtight container for up to 6 weeks. Dust with sugar again
before serving.

Makes 5 dozen.

Christmas 1980
