Title: HOMEMADE KETCHUP
Categories: Sauces, Relishes
Yield: 1 Servings
10 lb Tomatoes (pref. overripe)
1 1/4 c  Wine vinegar
2 tb Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1 tb Allspice
1 tb Mustard powder
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 c  Dark brown sugar
PLACE TOMATOES AND VINEGAR in a large saucepan over medium heat.
Cover and cook until soft, about 30 minutes. Strain the tomatoes or
place through a food mill. Discard seeds and skins and replace tomato
pulp in a clean saucepan. Add all other ingredients and let simmer
over medium heat about 2 hours, or until reduced to 6 cups. Stir
occasionally. The mixture should be thick. Makes 6 Cups
