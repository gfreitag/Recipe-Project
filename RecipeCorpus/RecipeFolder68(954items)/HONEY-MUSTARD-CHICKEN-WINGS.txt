Title: HONEY MUSTARD CHICKEN WINGS
Categories: Appetizers, Nachos, Lf
Yield: 5 Servings
24    Chicken wings
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Butter; melted
5 tb Dijon mustard
2 tb Sesame seeds
1 pn Salt and pepper
Preheat oven to 325.  Rinse wings well, drain on paper towels, Season
with salt and pepper.  Blend honey, butter and mustard in bowl. Dip
wings a few at a time, set in shallow baking pan. Bake 45 min. and
baste occasionally.
