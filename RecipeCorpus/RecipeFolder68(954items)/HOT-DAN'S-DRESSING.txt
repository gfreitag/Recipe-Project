Title: HOT DAN'S DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Mustard; Prepared
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Vinegar
2 tb Half And Half; OR
2 tb Undiluted Evaporated Milk
1/4 ts Salt
Combine all the ingredients together and beat with a rotary beater
until light and fluffy.  Serve on potato salad, cole slaw and use in
deviled eggs for a great treat.

Makes 1/4 Cup of Dressing.
