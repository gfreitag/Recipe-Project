Title: HONEY BUTTER
Categories: Jams
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Butter (no margarine substit
1/4 c  HONEY
Soften butter and cream in HONEY.  Serve on breads, muffins or
crackers. Variations: add 1 teaspoon grated orange or lemon rind or
substitute peanut butter for butter. From Connecticut Honey by
Eastern Connecticut Beekeepers Association
with the support of The Connecticut Department of Agriculture
