Title: HOT ARTICHOKE APPETIZERS.
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 10 Servings
1 pk Frozen artichoke hearts; (9
1/4 c  Chicken broth
1    Cl Garlic; minced
1/2 lb Bacon (slices)
Recipe by: JACQUELINE MALLORCA Preparation Time: 0:30 Simmer frozen
artichoke hearts in chicken broth with the garlic until tender, about
6 minutes. Drain.  When cool enough to handle, wrap in thinly cut
bacon and secure with a toothpick. Broil until bacon is crisp, and
serve warm. Alternatively, wrap in prosciutto and serve cold.
