Title: HOT ARTICHOKE DIP --- WHNP81A
Categories: Appetizers, Family
Yield: 8 Servings
14 oz Artichoke hearts
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Parmesan cheese
1 ds Garlic powder
1 ds Hot pepper sauce
1 x  Paprika
Preheat oven to 350 deg.  Mix artichoke hearts (chopped),
mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder and hot pepper sauce.
Place in a 9-in. baking dish, and sprinkle paprika on top. Bake 20-30
min. Serve hot with bland crackers.
