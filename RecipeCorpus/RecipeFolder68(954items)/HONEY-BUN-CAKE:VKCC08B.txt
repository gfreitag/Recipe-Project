Title: HONEY BUN CAKE:VKCC08B
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 10 Servings
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Buttermilk
3/4 c  Oil
4 x  Eggs
1 c  Raisins
5 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Brown sugar
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
2 c  Confectioners sugar
4 tb Milk
2 tb Vanilla
Mix 1st 5 ingredients together and pour into greased and floured 9x13
pan. Mix raisins, cinnamon and brown sugar, swirl into batter in pan.
Bake at 350F for 30 minutes. GLAZE: Combine all ingredients until
smooth and creamy, pour over cake.
