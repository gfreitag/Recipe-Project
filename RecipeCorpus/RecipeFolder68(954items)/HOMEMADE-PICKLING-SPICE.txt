Title: HOMEMADE PICKLING SPICE
Categories: Pickles, Spices
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Mustard Seed
1 tb Whole Allspice
2 ts Coriander Seeds
2    Whole Cloves
1 ts Ground Ginger
1 ts Dried Red Pepper Flakes
1    Bay Leaf, crumbled
1    Cinnamon Stick (2 inches)
Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight jar or container.
Use in favorite pickle recipes.

Yield: 1/3 Cup
