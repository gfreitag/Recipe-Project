Title: HOT SPICY BEAN CURD
Categories: Vegetarian, Beans, Oriental
Yield: 2 Servings
2 tb Oil
2 ea Green onions, chopped
1 ts Garlic, chopped
1 ea Green chilies, chopped
1 tb Salted black beans
1 lb Bean curd, cubed
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Brown sugar
1/2 c  Stock
1 ts Cornstarch dissolved in 1
-- tb water
Heat oil in a wok & fry the onion, garlic & chili for 30 seconds. Add
salted beans & bean curd, fry gently till bean curd turns golden. Add
the rest of the ingredients & cook until the sauce thickens. Serve
hot over noodles or fried rice.

Jack Santa Maria, "Chinese Vegetarian Cookery"
