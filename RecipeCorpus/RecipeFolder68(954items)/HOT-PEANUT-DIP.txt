Title: HOT PEANUT DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/4 c  Soy sauce, low sodium
2 tb Water -- hot
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Honey
1 ea Garlic clove
1 ts Red pepper flakes
Combine all ingredient in a blender and process using on/off method.
Add a little more water if necessary to make a smooth dipping sauce.
Serve at room temperature as a dip for assorted raw vegetables.
