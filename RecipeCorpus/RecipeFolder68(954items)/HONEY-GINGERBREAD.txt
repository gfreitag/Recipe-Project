Title: HONEY GINGERBREAD
Categories: Breads, Desserts, Honey
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Egg -- beaten
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Honey
2 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
2 ts Ginger -- ground
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In small bowl, beat sour cream and honey.
Blend into beaten eggs. Sift together dry ingredients. Mix the wet
and dry mixtures together and beat well. Blend in oil. Bake 30-40
minutes or until done in center. Cool on rack 5 minutes. Remove from
pan. Serve warm with ice cream or whipped cream.
