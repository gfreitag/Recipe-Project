Title: HOT CHOCOLATE SPECIAL
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
2 1/2 c  Milk, scalded
2 oz (2 squares) unsweetened
Chocolate, quartered
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1 ds Salt
Rinse an electic blender container with hot water. Put into
container=7F about 1/2 cup scalded milk, chocolate, sugar, vanilla
and salt. Cover and blend about 1 minute, or until smooth and color
is even throughout. Add remaining scalded milk and blend until
thoroughly mixed. Serve immediately.
