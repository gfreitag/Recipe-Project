Title: HONEY COUNTRY ICE CREAM
Categories: Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Honey
2 c  Light cream
1    Egg
2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Heavy cream.
Honey Country Ice Cream

Combine honey with 1 cup light cream. Beat in the rest of the light
cream, the egg and the vanilla.  Stir in the heavy cream. Chill and
freez in an ice-cream maker Variations: Add 1 ripe mashed banana and
1/2 teaspoon of lemon juice or 1/2 cup chopped dates and 1/2 cup
chopped walnuts.

Makes 1 quart
