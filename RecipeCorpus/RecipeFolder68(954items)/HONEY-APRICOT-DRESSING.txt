Title: HONEY-APRICOT DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads, Fruits
Yield: 10 Servings
8 1/4 oz Unpeeled Apricot Halves;
-Drained
1 c  Dairy Sour Cream
1/4 c  Honey
1 tb Lemon Juice
1 ds Salt
Put all of the ingredients into a blender container and blend until
smooth. Chill, if desired.

Makes 1 3/4 cups.
