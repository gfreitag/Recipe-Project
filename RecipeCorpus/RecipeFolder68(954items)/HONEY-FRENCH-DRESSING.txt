Title: HONEY FRENCH DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Celery seeds
1/3 c  Honey
5 tb Vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Grated onion
1 c  Oil
Mix sugar, mustard, paprika, salt and celery seeds. Add honey,
vinegar, lemon juice and onion. Slowly beat oil into dressing with
rotary beater.

Makes 2 cups
