Title: HOMEMADE ICE CREAM
Categories: Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 1 Gallon
1 2/3 c  Sugar
5    Eggs
2 cn Condensed milk
1/2 ga Milk
Flavoring to taste
Combine all ingredients together in large mixing bowl; mix well with
electric mixer.  Pour into can of ice cream churn; pack with crushed
ice and ice cream salt. Turn until frozen. Fruits may be crushed and
added to mixture before freezing, if desired.

SOURCE: 1979 New Orleans Time-Picayune Recipe Contest Cookbook Typed
