Title: HONEY GLAZED CARROTS
Categories: Vegetables, Famfavorite
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  CARROTS, WHOLE OR SLICED
1/4 c  WATER
1/4 c  HONEY
2 tb BUTTER
2 tb CHOPPED PARSLEY FLAKES
1 ds SALT
COOK CARROTS UNTIL TENDER AND DRAIN. STIR REMAINING INGREDIENTS IN
UNTIL CARROTS ARE WELL COATED. SALT TO TASTE. MAKES 6 SERVINGS. PREP
TIME, 5 MINUTES.
