Title: HOT & SPICY PARTY MIX
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 9 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Chili powder
4 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Hot pepper sauce
2 c  Corn chex
2 c  Rice chex
2 c  Wheat chex
2 c  Bran chex
1 c  Salted mixed nuts
Preheat oven to 250 deg f. Melt butter in shallow roasting pan. Mix
in all seasoning ingredients. Add cereal and stir until well coated.
Bake for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Store in airtight
container. Makes 9 cups snack mix.
