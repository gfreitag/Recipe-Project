Title: HOT OLIVE CHEESE PUFFS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 25 Puffs
1 c  Cheddar cheese (sharp),
-grated
3 tb Butter, softened
1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Paprika
25    Olives (approx)
Blend cheese with butter.  Add flour, salt, paprika. Mix well.

Dry olives.  Wrap a spoonful of dough around each and seal well. Bake
at 400 degrees F. for 15 minutes.

NOTES:

*  Delicious hot appetizers.  Yield:  Makes about 25 puffs.

*  You can freeze the unbaked cheese puffs on a cookie sheet and then
store them in the freezer in a plastic bag.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  30 minutes preparation, 15 minutes baking.
: Precision:  measure the ingredients.
