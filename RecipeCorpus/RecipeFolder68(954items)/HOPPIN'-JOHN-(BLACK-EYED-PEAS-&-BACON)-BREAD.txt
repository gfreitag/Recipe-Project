Title: HOPPIN' JOHN (BLACK-EYED PEAS & BACON) BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 pk Yeast
1/4 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Sugar
2 1/4 c  Flour, bread
1/2 c  Flour, wheat
1/4 c  Bran
2 tb Gluten
1/2 ts Pepper, white
1/2 c  Bacon bits
1 ts Onion, dried minced
1 c  Blackeyed peas, canned and
-drained
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 1/2 c  Water; very warm
Add all ingredients into pan in the order listed. Select white
bread setting and press "Start".

Source: DAK Gourmet Gazette, Vol 1, Issue 2.
