Title: BRUSH-ON SAUCE FOR CHICKEN
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Melted butter or margarine
1 ts Celery salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 ts Salt
Mix ingredients thoroughly and use as brush-on sauce for chicken
halves during broiling.  Brush chicken frequently with sauce, turning
pieces from time to time.
