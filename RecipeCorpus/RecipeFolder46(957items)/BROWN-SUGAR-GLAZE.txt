Title: BROWN SUGAR GLAZE
Categories: Ham
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Rown sugar, packed
1/2 ts Ustard, dry
1 tb Inegar
Yields 3/4 cup for use on baked ham. Mix together ingredients. 30
minutes before ham is done, pour off drippings. Score surface in
criss- cross fashion - brush glaze and continue baking.
