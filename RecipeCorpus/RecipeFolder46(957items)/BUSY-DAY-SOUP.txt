Title: BUSY DAY SOUP
Categories: Soups
2 lb ground beef
Yield: 1 Servings
32 oz mixed vegetables
56 oz tomatoes
12 oz tomato sauce
1    onion -- chopped
1 c  barley
1/2 c  sugar
8 c  water -- more if needed
salt -- to taste
pepper -- to taste
:
:

Brown ground beef and onions.  Drain.  Add rest of ingredients and
cook for at least 1 hour.

MAKES ALOT !!!!!!!!
