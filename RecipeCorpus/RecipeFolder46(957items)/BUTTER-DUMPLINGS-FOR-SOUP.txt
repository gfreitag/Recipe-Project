Title: BUTTER DUMPLINGS FOR SOUP
Categories: Dumplings
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Eggs
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
6 tb Butter; room temp
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Nutmeg
2 qt Beef or chicken broth
1. In a medium bowl, beat the eggs. Add the flour and mix.

2. Into the flour mixture beat the butter, salt, and nutmeg to make a
smooth batter.

3. Bring the soup to a boil. Drop half-teaspoonfuls of the batter
into the gently boiling soup. Cook 6 to 7 minutes. Serve dumplings in
the soup.

Source: "The Dumpling Cookbook" by Maria Polushkin
