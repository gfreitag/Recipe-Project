Title: BROILED CHICKEN QUARTERS OR TURKEY LEGS
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
*
4 ea CHICKEN QUARTERS
1/4 c  WATER
1 x  DRY POULTRY SEASONING  **
*    CHICKEN QUARTERS OR TURKEY LEGS CAN BE USED. ** POULTRY SEASONING
RECIPE IS LISTED UNDER "SAUCES" PLACE POUTRY PIECES IN MICROWAVE-SAFE
DISH AND POUR WATER OVER THE TOP. COVER THE DISH AND COOK ON HIGH FOR
8 TO 10 MINUTES. REARRANGE PIECES AFTER 5 MINUTES. DRAIN THE POULTRY
AND BRING TO ROOM TEMOERATURE. LIGHTLY DUST WITH SEASONING AND BROIL
FOR 5 TO 7 MINUTES OR COOK OVER HOT COALS.
