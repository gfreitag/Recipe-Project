Title: BROCCOLI LOVERS CASSEROLE
Categories: Vegetables, Kathy's, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 Servings
2 pk (10 oz) frozen chopped
Broccoli, drained
2 c  Round butter flavored
Crackers, crushed
1/3 c  Butter, melted
1/4 c  Onion, grated
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 cn (10 1/2 oz) cream of
Mushroom soup
2    Eggs, beaten
1 1/2 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
place packages of frozen broccoli in microwave safe dish and
microwave on high power 5-6 min. until defrosted. drain. set aside.
combine crackers and melted butter. place 1/2 of the crackers in the
bottom of a 10 oz quiche dish or pie plate. combine broccoli with
remaining ingredients. mix well and pour over the prepared cracker
base. top with remaining crackers. microwave on med. (50%) power for
18 to 21 min. or until mixture is set. let stand 6-8 min. recipe from
"Wisconsin Cheese in a Flash"
