Title: BROWN-EYED SUSANS
Categories: Muffins, Fruits
Yield: 12 Muffins
1 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Bran
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1/2 ts Ground allspice
1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1    Egg, beaten
3/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Pumpkin pie filling
Combine flour, bran, baking powder, salt and spices. Add brown sugar
and mix until no lumps remain.  Stir in chocolate chips. Combine
beaten egg, milk, oil and pumpkin; mix thoroughly. Add to dry
ingredients all at once stirring just until moistened. Do not
overmix. Spoon into greased muffin tins, filling 2/3 full. Bake at
375 F. for 25 minutes. Remove from pan to cool.
