Title: BRUCE'S BBQ RUB (CIRCA 1993)
Categories: Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Light brown sugar, packed
1/2 c  White vinegar
1/4 c  Seasoned salt
1/4 c  Onion powder
1/4 c  Paprika
2 tb Pepper
2 tb Chile powder
2 tb Dry mustard
1 ts Poultry seasoning
1 ts Thyme
1 ts Tarragon
1 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Allspice
Place all ingredients in a resealable gallon-size freezer bag. Make
sure bag is sealed.  Shake and tumble the mixture until all
ingredients are thoroughly mixed.  Yield: 2 cups

Source:  The Great Barbecue Companion, Mops, Sops, Sauces, and Rubs by
Bruce Bjorkman
