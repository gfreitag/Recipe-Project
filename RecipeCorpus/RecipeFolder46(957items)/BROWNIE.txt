Title: BROWNIE
Categories: Ww, Desserts, Chocolate, Mw
Yield: 1 Servings
-Shirley Hopkins  FRTW03B
1 pk Chocolate Alba mix
3 ts Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
6 ts Boiling water or coffee
1 pk Art. sweetner
1 ts Vanilla
Spray small microwave proof bowl.  Pour mixture into bowl and bake in
microwave 45 sec or reg oven 7 min 1 M, 35 opt cal. Reformatted for
MM by Penny Schenck NFSM70B
