Title: BROWNIE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Sifted flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Cocoa
3/4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
2 tb Shortening; melted
3/4 c  Nut meats, chopped
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
3/4 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Cocoa
1 3/4 c  -Hot water
Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, sugar and cocoa. Add milk,
vanilla and shortening; mix until smooth. Add nut meats. Pour into
greased 8-inch square cake pan. Mix brown sugar and cocoa; sprinkle
over batter. Pour hot water over entire batter. Bake.
Temperature--350 F. Time--45 minutes. Amount--6 to 8 servings.
