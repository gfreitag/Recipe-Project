Title: BROCCOLI CHEESE APPETIZER SPREAD
Categories: Crs, Totest, Post
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk (10oz) frozen chopped
Broccoli, thawed and
Drained
1 c  Mayonnaise
2/3 c  Grated Parmesan
1/2 c  Chopped chives
1/2 c  Chopped fresh parsley
1 tb Basil
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Chili powder
Combine and bake in a greased 1-qt. baking dish for 20 minutes at
350F. Makes
2 1/2    cups.

Southern Living Home for the Holidays 1994
