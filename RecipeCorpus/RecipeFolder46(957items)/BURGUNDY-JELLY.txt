Title: BURGUNDY JELLY
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Red current jelly
1/2 c  Burgendy
1 tb Powdered mustard
Melt the jelly in a heavy saucepan then stir in the wine & mustard.
Mix well & continue to heat for 2-3 minutes. Pour the jelly into 1
1/2 cup mold, allowing to cool. Chill until set.
