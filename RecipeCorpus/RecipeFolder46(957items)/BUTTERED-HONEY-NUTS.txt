Title: BUTTERED HONEY NUTS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
6 oz Shelled, unsalted nuts
1 tb Butter
1 tb Honey
DIRECTIONS: Spread nuts on a cookie sheet. Bake at 300-F for 15
minutes. Mix butter and honey; pour over nuts. Stir until nuts are
completely coated with the mixture. Store in airtight container.
