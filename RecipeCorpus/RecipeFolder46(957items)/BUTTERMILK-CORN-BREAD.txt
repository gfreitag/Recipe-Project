Title: BUTTERMILK CORN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Pan
1    Egg
2 c  Buttermilk
1 c  Water
1 ts Baking soda
2 ts Salt
Corn meal
Combine egg, buttermilk and water; add salt and soda. Stir in enough
corn meal until of cake batter consistency. Bake at 500 degrees for
15 to 20 minutes in a well greased 10" pan.
