Title: BURNT SUGAR CANDY
Categories: Candies
Yield: 40 Servings
1 c  Evaporated milk
1/2    Butter
3 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Nuts
Mix milk, butter and 2 cups sugar in saucepan, heating to boiling
point. Melt 1 cup sugar in saucepan stirring constantly. Cook to soft
ball stage. Add vanilla and nuts; beat until very firm. Pour into
buttered 9- inch square pan. Yield: 40 pieces.
