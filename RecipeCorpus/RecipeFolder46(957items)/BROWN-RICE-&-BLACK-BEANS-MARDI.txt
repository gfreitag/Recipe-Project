Title: BROWN RICE & BLACK BEANS MARDI
Categories: Beans
Yield: 6 Servings    2 1/4 c  instant brown rice
1 3/4 c  boiling water
2    15-oz can cooked black beans
or pinto beans -- undrained
:
2 TB margarine
1 lg onion -- chopped
3    cloves garlic -- chopped
1 md green pepper -- chopped
1/2 ts ground cumin
1 1/2 c  salsa Cook rice as per instructions on the box and set
aside. In a large frying pan, saute' onion, garlic and green pepper
in margarine. Add rice, beans, cumin and salsa and simmer about 20
minutes.
