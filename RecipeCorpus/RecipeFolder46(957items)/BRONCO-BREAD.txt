Title: BRONCO BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
Jim Vorheis
2 tb Softened butter
1/4 c  Hot water
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 1/2 tb Freshly grated orange rind
1    Egg
1 c  Sugar
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Baking soda
1 c  Fresh blueberries, or frozen
-or canned, well-drained
Combine butter, water, orange juice and rind in a bowl. Add egg and
mix well.  Add dry ingredients and fold in blueberries by hand. Bake
at 325 F in greased 9x5-inch baking pan for 1 hour and 10 minutes.
Cool on rack.

Colorado Cache Cookbook (1978) From the collection of Jim Vorheis
