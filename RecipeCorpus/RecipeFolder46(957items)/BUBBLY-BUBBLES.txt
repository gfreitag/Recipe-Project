Title: BUBBLY BUBBLES
Categories: Crafts, Kids
Yield: 1 Batch
-Susan Klapper KCXJ08A
1/4 c  Liquid detergent
2 1/2 ts Sugar
9 tb Oil
Food coloring
2 3/4 qt Water
Mix ingredients together.  Shake of dissolve sugar. Use with empty
spool, straw, bubble pipe, or wire blower.
