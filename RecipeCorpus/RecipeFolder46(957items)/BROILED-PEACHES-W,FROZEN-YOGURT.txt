Title: BROILED PEACHES W/FROZEN YOGURT
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lg Peaches
2 tb Orange juice
Brown sugar
Frozen yogurt
Halve and pit peaches; place, cut side up, in shallow baking dish.
Spoon orange juice into hollows; sprinkle with sugar to taste.

Broil for 3 to 5 minutes or until sugar is lightly caramelized.
Transfer to serving dishes; top with scoop of frozen yogurt. Makes 4
servings. Tip: To grill combine orange juice and brown sugar and
brush over cut sides of peaches, using any remaining juice to drizzle
over peaches as they cook. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com
