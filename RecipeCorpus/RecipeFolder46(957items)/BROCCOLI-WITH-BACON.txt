Title: BROCCOLI WITH BACON
Categories: Crs, Totest, Post
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Fresh broccoli
6 sl Bacon
3/4 c  Coarsely chopped walnuts
(optional)
1/2 c  Sliced green onions
Remove broccoli leaves, cut tough stalk ends off. Wash and cut into
spears.

Cook in boiling water for 6 to 8 mins. or until tender;drain. Keep
warm in serving dish.

Cook 2 tb drippings in skillet. Crumble bacon and put aside.

Cook walnuts, if desired, in drippings over medium-high heat, stirring
constantly, 3 mins. Add green onions, and cook for 2 mins. Stir
constantly. Spoon over broccoli. Sprinkle bacon on.

Southern Living Home for the Holidays 1994
