Title: BROILED ONION TOPPING
Categories: Vegetables, Relishes, Hamburger
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Butter Or Margarine
1 c  Onion; Chopped, 2 Medium
1/8 ts Nutmeg
2 tb Dairy Sour Cream
Melt the butter or margarine in a small skillet.  Add onions, cook
and stir until tender.  Stir in remaining ingredients; spread on
cooked patties. Broil 2 inches from the heat until hot, about 1
minute.
