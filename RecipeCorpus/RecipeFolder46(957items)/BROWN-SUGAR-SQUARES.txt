Title: BROWN SUGAR SQUARES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 3/4 c  Evaporated milk
2 tb Corn sirup
1 ts Vanilla
2 tb Butter or butter substitute
1 c  Coconut
1 c  Chopped almonds
Few grains salt
Boil sugar, sirup, and milk to soft ball stage (234 - 238 F). Remove
from fire.  Add butter.  Cool to room temperature. Beat until thick
and creamy. Add coconut, nuts, salt, and flavoring. Pour into
well-buttered, shallow pan.  Cut in squares. Mrs. M. Carello, Cato,
WI.
