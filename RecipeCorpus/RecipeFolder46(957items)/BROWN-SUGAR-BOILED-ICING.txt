Title: BROWN SUGAR BOILED ICING
Categories: Candies
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Water
2 ea Egg whites
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Boil to-gether sugar and water  until soft ball forms when tested in
small amount of cold water. Beat egg whites stiff and gradually add
syrup in a steady stream beating constantly, until frosting holds
shape.Add salt and vanilla.
