Title: BROCCOLI DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Jaw
Yield: 1 Party
1    Bunch fresh broccoli (about
1 pound)
1    8 oz jar processed cheddar
Cheese spread
1    Can mushroom soup
1    Can Ro-Tel tomatoes with
Green chilies
Dash Worcestershire sauce
Slice and cook broccoli until slightly tender; drain. Heat remaining
ingredients and then add broccoli. Put in fondue pot to keep warm and
serve with chips.
