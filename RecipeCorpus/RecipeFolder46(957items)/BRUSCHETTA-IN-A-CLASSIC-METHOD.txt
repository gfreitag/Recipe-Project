Title: BRUSCHETTA IN A CLASSIC METHOD
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lg Slices  good quality bread
Toasted
Cut clove of peeled garlic
About 1/2 cup very thinly
Sliced mushrooms -- or
Roasted eggplant
Or chopped fresh tomatoes
Extra virgin olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
Black pepper
Chopped parsley
Right after toasting, rub the bread with garlic and cover it with
mushrooms. Drizzle with oil, season with salt and pepper and garnish
with parsley.

Yield: 2 servings
