Title: BUCKAROO BAR-B-Q SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ea Medium onion, sliced
1/2 c  Chopped celery
2 tb Fat
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Prepared mustard
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Catsup
1 ea 8 oz can seasoned tomato
-sauce
Cook onions and celery in hot fat till soft and yellow. Add remaining
ingredients; Mix thoroughly. Makes 2 cups, enough to barbeque 4 lbs
spareribs.

Source:  Better Homes and Gardens Barbeque Book
