Title: BURGUNDY STEAK SAUCE A LA WORCESTER
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Butter Or Margarine
1/4 c  Onion; Chopped
21 oz Beef Gravy; 2 cn
3 tb Burgundy; *
4 ts Worcestershire Sauce
2 tb Parsley; Chopped
*    You can use Burgundy or any other type of dry red wine in this
recipe. +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
+++++++++++++++++

In a small saucepan, melt the butter.  Add the onion and saute for 3
minutes.  Stir in the gravy, wine, and Worcestershire Sauce. Cook and
stir until hot, about 2 minutes. Stir in the parsley. Serve over
steak or hamburgers.

Makes approximately 3 cups of sauce.
