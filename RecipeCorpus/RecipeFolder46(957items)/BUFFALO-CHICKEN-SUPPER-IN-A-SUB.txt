Title: BUFFALO CHICKEN SUPPER IN A SUB
Categories: Sandwiches, Poultry, Jaw
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Chicken breast tenders
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Paprika
2 tb Flour
1 tb Olive oil
2 tb Bottled hot sauce
3/4 c  Sliced red onion
3/4 c  Sliced cucumber
1/2 c  Bottled ranch dressing
4    Long sub buns, split
4    Red bibb lettucer leaves
2    Stalks celery, sliced in
Strips
4 tb Crumbled bleu cheese
In plastic bag, mix together garlic powder, chili powder, paprika and
flour; add chicken and turn to coat. In large nonstick frypan, place
olive oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook, turning, about
7 minutes or until fork can be inserted with ease. Drizzle hot sauce
over chicken, toss to coat; set aside. In medium bowl, mix together
onion, cucumber and ranch dressing. Inside each bun, place 1 lettuce
leaf and top with chicken and celery; spoon onion-cucumber mixture on
top. Sprinkle with bleu cheese.
