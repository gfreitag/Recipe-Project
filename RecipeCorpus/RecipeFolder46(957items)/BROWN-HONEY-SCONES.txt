Title: BROWN HONEY SCONES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Wholemeal
4 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
2 tb Honey
1 c  Milk, approx
Mix wholemeal, salt and bakingpowder. Rub in the butter. Dissolve the
honey in the milk, add to dry ingredients and mix to a soft dough.
Rollout, cut out, and bake on a cookie sheet for 10 - 12 minutes at
425 degrees. (recipe from Edmonds Sure To Rise NZ Cookbook)
