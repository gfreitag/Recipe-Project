Title: BROCCOLI-RICE CASSEROLE II
Categories: Vegetables, Main dish, Cajun
Yield: 1 Servings
2 pk Chopped broccoli OR
1    Bunch fresh broccoli
1    Stick oleo
1 c  Cooked rice
Paprika
1/2 cn Cream of chicken soup
1    Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1 c  Chopped mushrooms
1 sm Jar Cheez-Whiz
1/2 cn Cream of Mushroom soup
Saute onion, celery and mushrooms in oleo until soft. Set aside.
Heat soups and Cheez Whiz.  Add rice and broccoli to vegetables. Add
soup mixture. Sprinkle with paprika. Bake at 350 F. for 25 minutes.
