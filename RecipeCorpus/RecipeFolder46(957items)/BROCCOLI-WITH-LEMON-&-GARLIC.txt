Title: BROCCOLI WITH LEMON & GARLIC
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
24 oz Ready-cut broccoli florets
2 cl Garlic
3 tb Olive oil
3 tb Fresh lemon juice
Salt
Steam broccoli till tender but firm, 4-6 minutes. Meanwhile, mince the
garlic.  Heat the oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat, add the
garlic saute one minute. Add the cooked broccoli, lemon juice and
salt to taste. cooking briefly to combine.

Nutritional info per serving: 281 cal; 10g pro, 21g carb, 21g fat
