Title: BUTTERMILK PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Melted margarine
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Flour
2    Eggs
1 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Lemon extract
1    9" unbaked pie shell
Mix sugar and flour together.  Add eggs, then margarine. Stir in
buttermilk.  Add lemon extract. Pour into pie shell. Bake at 400 F 30
to 35 minutes until pie is set. Serve at room temperature.
