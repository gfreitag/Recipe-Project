Title: BROKEN SHRIMP JAMBALAYA
Categories: Cajun, Main dish, Seafood
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Broken shrimp (cooked)
1 c  Peanut oil
4    Onions, chopped
5    Cloves garlic
2    Bunches shallots
1    Bell pepper, chopped
2 ts Paprika
Red, black, white pepper
Salt
3 c  Ri
1/4 lb Smoked sausage
5 c  Water
Heat oil, add onions, garlic, shallots, bell pepper, smoked sausage,
paprika, salt, and peppers and saute well. Add shrimp pieces, rice and
water.  Bring to boil, cover, and over very low heat, steam for 20 to
25 minutes.  Stir with fork and replace cover. Serves 6
