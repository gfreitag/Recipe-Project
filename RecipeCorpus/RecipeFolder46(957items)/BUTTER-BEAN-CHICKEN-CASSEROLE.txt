Title: BUTTER BEAN-CHICKEN CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Butter beans(15oz)
1 c  Chicken,cubed cooked
1/4 c  Green pepper,chopped
Cheddar cheese,shredded
1/4 c  Tomato catsup
1 ts Onion,instant minced
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cn Onion rings,French-fried
1. Put beans, catsup, chicken, green onion, pepper and Worcestershire
sauce in a 1-quart casserole.
2. Bake casserole, uncovered, in preheated 325'F. oven
20    minutes.
3. Top with desired amount of cheese and onion rings; bake until
cheese melts and onion rings are crisp.
