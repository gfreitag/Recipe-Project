Title: BURNT SUGAR BUTTERCREAM FROSTING FHMN87A
Categories: Amish, Cakes, Frostings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Butter, softened
16 oz Powdered Sugar
1    Speck of salt
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 tb Light cream/evaporated milk
1/4 c  Chopped nuts
1/4 c  Burnt sugar syrup from cake
Cream the butter in a large mixing bowl.  Beat in the powdered sugar,
salt, vanilla, and burnt sugar syrup.
Add cream to make the frosting good consistency for spreading.(May
need up to 2 Tablespoons or more)
