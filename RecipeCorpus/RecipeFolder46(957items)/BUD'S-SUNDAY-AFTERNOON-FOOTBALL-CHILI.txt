Title: BUD'S SUNDAY AFTERNOON FOOTBALL CHILI
Categories: Chili, Meats
Yield: 10 Servings
1 lb Beef (tip steak) cubed
1 lb Ground beef
2    Med. Onions, coarse chopped
2    Sm. Green Peppers, chopped
-med.
1/2 c  Burgundy wine
6 tb Chili powder (Mexene)
4 tb Cumin
3 cl Garlic (small)
1 ts Oregano
1 tb Chilantro leaves
2 tb Hot sauce (La Preferida)
1 ts Sugar
2 ds Salt
1 cn Peeled whole tomatoes with
-juice (16 oz.)
1 cn Stewed tomatoes (16 oz.)
1 cn Tomato sauce (8 oz).
1 cn Chili hot beans (15 1/2 oz.)
- Note: beans optional!
Brown meat with 1 of the chopped onions. Drain grease. Place meat
onions, remainder of onions, green peppers, and the rest of the
ingredients into stockpot.  Bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer 1
or 2 hrs, covered. Cool
1    hr.  Reaheat before serving.

Concocted by Bud Cloyd (who had nothing better to do on a particular
Sunday afternoon.)
