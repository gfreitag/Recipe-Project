Title: BROCCOLI SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 12 Servings
10    Strips bacon -- fried and
Crumbled
1 1/2 lb Broccoli -- cut into
Flowerets
1 c  Red onions -- chopped
1 c  Raisins
1 cn Waterchestnuts -- sliced
1 c  Sunflower seeds
DRESSING--
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb White vinegar
Mix dressing and let stand for several hours. Shake or stir before
adding to salad mixture just before serving.
