Title: BROWN-BUTTER FROSTING
Categories: Icings
Yield: 1 Recipes
1/2 c  Butter
2 c  Powdered sugar
Hot water, approx 2 Tb
1 ts Vanilla
Melt and stir butter in a heavy pan over medium heat till golden
brown. Blend in sugar gradually. Add vanilla and hot water to make
spreading consistency. Spread over warm rolls.
