Title: Cucumber Sweet Pickle (Short Process)
Categories: Pickles, Can/preserv, Vegetables, Mennonite
Yield: 4 quarts
1 c  Water
1 1/2 qt Vinegar
2 tb Mixed spices
1 ga Cucumbers
6 c  Sugar
1 c  Salt
Wash cucumbers and wipe them dry. Dissolve salt in 1 gallon cold
water and pour over cucumbers. Let stand 24 hours and drain. Puncture
each cucumber 3 times with a needle. Combine 1/2 of the sugar,
vinegar, water, and spices. Simmer liquid for 30 minutes. Add
cucumbers to liquid and continue to simmer 15 minutes. Let stand in a
jar for 2 days. Drain off the liquid and pack pickles in hot jars.
Add remaining sugar to liquid and boil for 5 minutes. Pour over
pickles and seal.

Mrs. Roy E. Blosser of Ohio _Mennonite Community Cookbook_ Mary Emma
