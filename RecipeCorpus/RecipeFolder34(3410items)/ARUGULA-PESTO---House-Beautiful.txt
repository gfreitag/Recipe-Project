Title: ARUGULA PESTO - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Herbs/spice
Yield: 3 /4 cup
2 lg Garlic doves, peeled and
-crushed
3 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
2 tb Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese, preferably Reggiano
1 1/3 c  Packed fresh arugula leaves
1/3 c  Packed fresh flat-leaf
-parsley sprigs
1/3 c  Packed fresh spinach leaves
2 tb Walnuts or pistachios
In a blender or food processor, place the arugula, parsley, spinach,
nuts, and garlic. Process until finely minced. Add oil and cheese and
process until blended. Transfer to a small bowl, cover and
refrigerate. Makes about 3/4 C.
