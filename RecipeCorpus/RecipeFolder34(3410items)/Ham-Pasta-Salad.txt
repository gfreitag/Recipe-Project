Title: Ham Pasta Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise-type salad
Dressing
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
2 tb Milk
1/4 ts Salt
Additional Parmesan cheese
1    Box shell pasta -- macaroni
- 7 oz., cooked & drained
2 c  Cooked ham -- cubed
1 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1 c  Tomato -- chopped
1/4 c  Chopped onion
Dressing:
In a large bowl, toss macaroni with ham. green pepper, tomato and
onion. In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, parmesan cheese, milk and
salt. Pour over pasta mixture and stir to coat. Cover and chill.
sprinkle with additional parmesan before serving. Yield: 4 to 6
servings.
