Title: Spicy White Pizza
Categories: Snacks, Pizza
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Basil leaves
1/4 ts Crushed hot red pepper
Flakes (optional)
2 tb Kraft grated parmesan
Cheese
8 oz (2cups)J.L. Kraft Select
Shredded part-skim
Mozzarella cheese
10 oz Can Hungry Jack Flaky
Biscuits
2 tb Vegetable oil
2    To 4 cloves garlic --
Minced
(about 1 t) or 1/4 t
Instant
Minced garlic
3/4 ts Oregano leaves
Preheat oven to 350F. Separate dough into 10 biscuits; separate each
biscuit into 2 layeres. Place in an ungreased 12-inch pizza pan or
13x9 inch pan; press over bottom and 1/2 inch up sides to form crust.
In a small bowl, combine oil, garlic, oregano, basil and pepper
flakes; drizzle over crust. Sprinkle with cheeses. Bake in preheated
oven for 18 to 22 minutes or until crust is golden brown. Per
serving: 421 calories, 20gm protein, 32 gm carbo, 27gm fat, 34mg
