Title: Hot Cheese Puffs
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Freeze dried chives
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
2 c  Shredded sharp cheese
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  All purpose flour
Mix cheese and butter; stir in flour, chives and seasoned salt until
blended.  Shape into 1 inch balls, place on ungreased baking sheet.
Cover and refrigerate up to 48 hours.  Thrity minutes before serving,
heat oven to 400 degrees.  Bake until cheese puffs are set and light
golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Randy Rigg
