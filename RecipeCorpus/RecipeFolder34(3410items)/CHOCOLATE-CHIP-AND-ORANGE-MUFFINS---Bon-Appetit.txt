Title: CHOCOLATE CHIP AND ORANGE MUFFINS - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Muffins, Chocolate
Yield: 10 muffins
1/3 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1 tb Baking powder
1 tb Salt
2/3 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
2 tb Grated orange peel
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1    Egg, beaten to blend
1 tb Vanilla
2 c  All purpose flour
Preheat oven to 400'F. Grease ten 1/2-cup muffin cups. Combine first 4
ingredients in bowl. Mix together flour, sugar, baking powder and
salt. Stir into liquid ingredients. Fold in chocolate chips and
orange peel. Divide batter among prepared cups. Bake until tester
inserted in centers comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Let stand 5
minutes. Remove muffins from cups. (Can be prepared 1 day ahead.
Cool. Store airtight.) Serve muffins warm or at room temperature.
