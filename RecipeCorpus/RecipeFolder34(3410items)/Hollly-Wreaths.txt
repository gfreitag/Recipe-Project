Title: Hollly Wreaths
Categories: Cookies, Country wom
Yield: 36 servings
2 c  All-purpose flour
Cherries -- green cut thin
Slice
Red-hot candies -- cinnamon
Frost and decorator gel
1 c  Butter -- softened
1 pk Cream cheese -- softened 3
Oz
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and cream cheese. Add sugar; blend
well. Stir in vanilla. Gradually beat in flour. Using a cookie press
fitted with star tip form dough into 2 1/2 inch wreaths on ungreased
baking sheets. Bake at 375 deg. for 10 to 12 min. or until set but
not brown. Cool on wire racks. Decorate wreaths with green cherry
"leave"and cinnamon candy "berries" attached with a drop of frosting.
Add bows with decorator gel. Yield; about 3 doz.
