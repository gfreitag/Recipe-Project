Title: Middle Eastern Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
8 oz Can tomato sauce
1/4 c  Raisins
1    12" pizza crust
2/3 c  Feta cheese -- crumbled
1    Plum tomato -- chopped
1 tb Fresh parsley -- minced
1 tb Vegetable oil
3/4 lb Eggplant -- 1/2" dice
1 ts Vegetable oil
3/4 c  Onions -- chopped
1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1 ts Garlic -- minced
1 ts Cumin
3/4 ts Cinnamon
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat 1 tb oil and add eggplant; cook, stirring,
until softened, 10 to 15 minutes; set aside. Add 1 ts oil and onions
to skillet; cook untiil tender, 3 minutes. Add beef, garlic, cumin,
cinnamon and salt; cook until meat is no longer pink. Stir in sauce,
raisins and eggplant. Spoon on crust. Sprinkle with feta, plum tomato
and parsley. Bake 10 minutes. Per Slice: 320 Cal, 14 gr Fat.
