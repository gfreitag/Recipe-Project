Title: Dllly Cucumbers
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
Or 1 tablespoon dill weed
1 ts Sugar
4 sm Cucumbers -- peeled/sliced
1/4 c  Vinegar
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Fresh dill -- minced
In a bowl, combine vinegar, oil, dill and sugar. Add cucumbers and
stir well. Refrigerate until serving. Yield: 6 servings
