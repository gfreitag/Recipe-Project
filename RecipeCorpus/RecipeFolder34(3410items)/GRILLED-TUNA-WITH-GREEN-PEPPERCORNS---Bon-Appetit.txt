Title: GRILLED TUNA WITH GREEN PEPPERCORNS - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Fish/seafoo, Bbq/grill
Yield: 2 servings
2    7-oz tuna steaks
1 tb Lemon juice
Olive oil
Lemon wedges
2 tb (1/4 stick) butter, room
-temperature
1 tb Green peppercorns in brine,
-drained and chopped
Prepare barbecue (medium-high heat) or preheat broiler. Combine
butter and peppercorns. Rub tuna with lemon juice. Brush with oil.
Place on grill. Cook to desired degree of doneness, about 4 minutes
per side for medium. Transfer to plates. Spread with peppercorn
butter. Serve with lemon wedges.

Bon Appetit/August/89  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
