Title: Quick Golden Stew
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Ham -- cubed
1 cn Cream of celery soup,
Condensed -- undiluted 10
-3/4 oz.
1    Jar processed American
Cheese -- 8 oz
4 ea Carrots -- cut 1 in. pieces
1 1/2 c  Potatoes -- peeled &
Chopped
2 md Onion -- cut into chunks
Water
1 pk Frozen peas -- defrosted 10
Oz
In a large saucepan or dutch oven, combine carrots, potatoes, onions
and just enough water to cover. Cook, covered until vegetables are
tender, about 10 min. Add peas and ham; continue to cook 5 more min.
Drain water. Stir in soup and cheese; heat through. Yield: 4 to 6
servings.
