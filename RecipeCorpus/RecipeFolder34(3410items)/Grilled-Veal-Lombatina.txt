Title: Grilled Veal Lombatina
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
-ground
2 1/4 ts Balsamic Vinegar
2 tb Parsley -- coarsely chopped
2    Lemons -- halved and seeded
4    14-Oz Veal Loin Chops
- 2 inches thick
4 tb Olive Oil, Extra-Virgin
1/2 ts Kosher Salt
1/4 ts Black Pepper -- freshly
1. Preheat a grill, grill pan, broiler, or outdoor barbecue to very
hot.

2. Rub the veal chops with 1 Tbs of the oil. Season with half the
salt and pepper.

3. Place the remaining salt, pepper, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar
in a jar. Cover tightly and shake well to combine.

4. Grill the chops 10 mins a side and transfer to a warm serving
platter. Drizzle with the olive oil and balsamic mixture, sprinkle
with the parsley, and serve with the lemon halves.
