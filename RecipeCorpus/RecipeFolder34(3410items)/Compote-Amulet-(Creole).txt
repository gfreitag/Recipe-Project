Title: Compote Amulet (Creole)
Categories: Salads, Fruit, Cajun/creol
Yield: 6 servings
-undrained
Lettuce leaves
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
3 ea Maraschino cherries; chopped
6 ea Oranges; peeled and
-sectioned
3/4 c  Grated coconut
1 1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
11 oz Can mandarin oranges;
Combine first 4 ingredients.  Toss; chill.  Spoon onto lettuce
leaves. Spread mayonnaise over fruit; garnish with cherries.
