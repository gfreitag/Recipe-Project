Title: Chamberlain's Homemade Steak Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 1/3 cups
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
3 tb Tomato paste
2 ts Powdered garlic
2 tb Raisins
1 1/2 ts Black peppercorns
3/4 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Onion(s), chopped
2 tb Honey
1/4 c  Apple-cider vinegar
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Ruby Red grapefruit juice
1/4 c  Beef broth
Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan. Saut the onions over medium
heat until soft. Add the honey and continue cooking for 2 minutes.
Add the remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil and reduce the heat to
medium low. Simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Remove and
cool. Pure in blender until smooth. Refrigerated, the sauce will keep
indefinitely.
