Title: No Cholesterol Bean Dip Spread
Categories: Vegetarian, Low cal/fat
2 c  Canned Garbanzo Beans (Well Drained)
Yield: 1 batch
1/4 c  Oil
2 ts Onion, minced
1 ts Sesame Seeds
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Garlic Powder
2 Tb Lemon Juice
1 Tb Low Cholesterol Mayonnaise

Place all ingredients into a blender or food processor. Blend at high
speed until ingredients are well mixed and mixture has a smooth,
creamy texture. Serve with fresh vegetable spears such as celery,
carrot sticks, or raw cauliflowerets, cucumber or raw zucchini
chunks. May also be used as a spread on your favorite snack crackers.
