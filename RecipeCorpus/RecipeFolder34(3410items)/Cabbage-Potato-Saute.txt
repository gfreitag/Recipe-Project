Title: Cabbage-Potato Saute
Categories: Vegetables, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Margarlne
pn Salt and pepper
1 tb Vinegar
4 c  Shredded cabbage
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Grated raw potato
In a saucepan, cook cabbage in water until tender, stirring
occasionally; drain. Add potato, margarine, salt and pepper. Stir in
vinegar. Cook and stir over low heat for 5 minutes. Yield: 4
servings. Nutritional Informatlon: One serving equals 50 calories,
179 mg sodium, 0 cholesterol, 6 gm carbohydrate, 1 gm protein, 3 gm
fat
