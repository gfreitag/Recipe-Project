Title: Squash Custard Pie
Categories: Pies, Pastry, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ds Salt
1 ea Unbaked pie pastry -- 9
Inches
Whipped cream or topping for
Garnish
1 c  Winter squash -- mashed
Cooked
1 c  Whipping cream -- or heavy
Cream
1 c  Sugar
3 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
1 ts Ground ginger
In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients except for the pie pastry
and additional whipped cream. Pour into the pastry; bake at 375 deg.
for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 deg.; bake for 45 minutes or until
set. Cool on a wire rack. Chill. Serve with a dollop of whipped
cream. Yield: 8 servings.
