Title: CARAMELIZED ONION RELISH - Country Living
Categories: Condiments, Vegetables, Country liv
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt (opt.)
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1/4 c  Olive oil
4    Large (2 lb) onions, thinly
-sliced
1. In large, nonstick skillet, heat oil over high heat. Stir in
onions and salt, if desired; cover and cook 5 minutes or until onions
have softened slightly.

2. Uncover and saute onions, stirring frequently, until tender and
very well browned-about 20 to 25 minutes. Remove from heat and stir
in vinegar. Serve hot or cold.
