Title: Greek Style Pizzas
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Onion -- sliced
1 cn Anchovies -- drained
1/2 c  Feta cheese -- crumbled
8    Pitted ripe olives --
Sliced
1 c  Basic pizza sauce
1 ts Oregano -- crushed
4    Pita bread rounds
1 cn Artichoke hearts --
Quartered
4 oz Ham julienne strips
Prepare basic pizza sauce.  Preheat broiler.  Mix sauce and oregano;
spread on pitas.  Arrange artichoke hearts, ham, onions and anchovies
and olives evenly over sauce; sprinkle with cheese. Broil 3 minutes
or until cheese is melted.
