Title: LIME SHERBET - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 8 servings
Pinch of salt
2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Lime juice
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Lemon juice
1. Chill the container of an ice-cream maker or a 13 by 9-inch baking
pan in the freezer.

2. In medium-size bowl, combine lime juice, sugar, lemon juice, and
salt until sugar dissolves, Stir in milk until well blended.

3. Pour mixture into chilled container of ice-cream maker and process
following manufacturer's directions, or pour into chilled baking pan
and freeze until almost firm-1 1/2 to 2 flours, depending on freezer
temperature.

4. If freezing in pan, cut frozen mixture into squares. With
heavy-duty electric mixer or food processor, beat until smooth. Pack
into freezer containers for storage.
