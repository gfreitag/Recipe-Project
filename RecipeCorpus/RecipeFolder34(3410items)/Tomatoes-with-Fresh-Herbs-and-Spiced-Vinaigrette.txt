Title: Tomatoes with Fresh Herbs and Spiced Vinaigrette
Categories: Salads, Condiments, Bon appetit
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM------------------------VINAIGRETTE-----------------------------
1 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 c  Olive oil
3 tb Balsamic vinegar
2 tb Lime juice
1 tb Hungarian sweet paprika
MMMMM--------------------------TOMATOES-------------------------------
2 tb Chives, chopped
-or green onions (opt)
Basil and cilantro leaves
2 1/2 lb Tomatoes
-use red, green and yellow
-if possible
Combine first 5 ingredients in small bowl. Gradually whisk in oil.
SEason with salt and pepper.

Overlap tomatoes on platter. Spoon dressing over. Top with chives or
green onions if desired. Garnish with basil and cilantro leaves.
