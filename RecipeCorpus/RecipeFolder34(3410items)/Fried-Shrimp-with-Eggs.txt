Title: Fried Shrimp with Eggs
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Vegetable oil
2    Eggs beaten
Salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 lb Shrimp,peeled aqnd deveined
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Paprika
1) Place shrimp in large bowl. Add soy sauce, lemon juice, and
paprkia; marinade 15 minutes.

2) Heat oil in large frying pan. Meanwhile, dip shrimp in beaten egg
and add to pan. cook shrimp 2 minutes each side over medium heat.
Season well.

3) When shrimp are cooked, drain on paper towel. If desired, serve
with dipping sauce such as plum sauce.
