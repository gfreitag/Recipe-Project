Title: Easy Chicken Pot Pie^
Categories: Poultry, Pie/tart
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
1/4 ts Pepper
1 cn (10oz) refrigerated
Buttermilk biscuits
2 c  Chopped cooked chicken
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 pk (10oz) frozen mixed
Vegetables, thawed
1/2 c  Milk
Combine all ingredients except biscuits in a slightly greased 8"
square baking dish. Set side.

Separate biscuits and place on top of chicken mixture.

Bake at 400F. 12 minutes, cover with foil and bake an additional 8
minutes.
