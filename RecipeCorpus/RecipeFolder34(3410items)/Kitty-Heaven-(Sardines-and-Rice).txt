Title: Kitty Heaven (Sardines and Rice)
Categories: Pets
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Liver
1/4 c  Parsey, chopped
2    Flat cans of sardines in oil
2/3 c  Cooked rice
Combine all ingredients. Stir with a wooden spoon to break up
sardines into bite-sized pieces.

Yields: 2 - 3 servings.

Store unused portion in refrigerator, tightly covered.

Source: the Shampoo-chez Good Earth Petalog typed by J. Matthews
