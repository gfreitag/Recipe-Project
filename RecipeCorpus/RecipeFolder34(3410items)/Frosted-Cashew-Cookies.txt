Title: Frosted Cashew Cookies
Categories: Cookies, Country wom
Yield: 36 servings
1 3/4 c  Cashews -- salted halves
Brown Butter Frosting:
1/2 c  Butter -- no substitutes
3 tb Light cream
1/4 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Confectioner's sugar
Additional cashew halves, --
Optional
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1 ea Egg
1/3 c  Sour cream
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 ts Baking powder
3/4 ts Baking soda
3/4 ts Salt
In a mixing bowl, cream the butter and brown sugar. Beat in egg, sour
cream and vanilla; Beat in egg, sour cream and vanilla; mix well.
Combine dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture and mix wll. Fold in
the cashews. Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto greased baking sheets.
Bake at 375 deg. for 8-10 min. or until lightly browned. Cool on a
wire rack. For the frosting. lightly brown butter in a small
saucepan. Remove from the heat; add cream and banilla. Beat in
confectioner"s sugar until smooth and thick. Frost cookies. Top each
with a cashew half if desired. Yield: about 3 dozen.
