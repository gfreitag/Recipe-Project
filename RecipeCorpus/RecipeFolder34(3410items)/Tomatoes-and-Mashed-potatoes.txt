Title: Tomatoes and Mashed potatoes
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
Parsley (optional)
Stuffed olives (optional)
6 md Tomato cases
Mashed potatoes
1/2 c  Nut meats (optional)
Place tomatoes (prepared as described in making Tomato Cases) in a
pan with enough water to keep them from scorching and bake for 10
minutes at 350 F. If you wish you may add 1/2 cup broken nut meats.

Fill the tomato cases and garnish with whole nut meats, a sprig of
parsley or sliced stuffed olives.

Return to oven and bake until done.
