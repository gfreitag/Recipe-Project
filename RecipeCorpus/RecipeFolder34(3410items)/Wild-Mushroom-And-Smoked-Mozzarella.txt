Title: Wild Mushroom And Smoked Mozzarella
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    12" pizza crust
2 tb Parmesan -- grated
6 oz Smoked mozzarella -- slice
Thin
2 tb Butter
1 ts Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Shallots -- minced
1/2 lb Shiitake mushrooms -- slice
Thick
1/4 lb Cremini mushrooms -- slice
1/4 lb White mushrooms -- sliced
1 tb Fresh parsley -- minced
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat butter and oil. Add shallots; cook 2
minutes. Add mushrooms; cook until liquid is absorbed, 5 to 7
minutes. Add parsleyi, garlic, salt and pepper. Spread over crust.
Sprinkle with parmesan. Top with mozzarella. Bake 10 minutes. Per
Slice: 265 Cal, 12 gr Fat.
