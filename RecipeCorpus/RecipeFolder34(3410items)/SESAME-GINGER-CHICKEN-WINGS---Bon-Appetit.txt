Title: SESAME GINGER CHICKEN WINGS - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Poultry, Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 tb Freshly ground pepper
1/8 tb Dried red pepper flakes
1    Egg, beaten to blend
1/2 c  Dry broadcrumbs
1/3 c  Sesame seeds
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter, melted
2 lb Chicken wings, tips
-discarded, cut in half at
-joint
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1    2-inch-square piece fresh
-ginger, peeled
2    Garlic cloves
Place chicken wings in large nonaluminum bowl. Blend soy sauce,
ginger, garlic, freshly ground pepper and pepper flakes in blender or
processor until ginger and garlic are minced. Pour over chicken and
mix well. Cover and mannate at room temperature 2 hours or in
refrigerator overnight, stirring chicken occasionally.

Preheat oven to 400'F. Butter large baking dish. Stir egg into chicken
marinade. Combine bread crumbs and sesame seeds in another bowl.
Dredge chicken in breadcrumb mixture. Place in prepared dish. Drizzle
with melted butter. Bake until chicken is brown and crisp, about 45
minutes. Serve hot.

Bon Appetit/August/89  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
