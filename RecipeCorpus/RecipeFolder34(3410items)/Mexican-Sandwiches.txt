Title: Mexican Sandwiches
Categories: Sandwiches, Country wom
Yield: 24 servings
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 15 oz.
1 cn Olive -- ripe 4 oz chopped
1 cn Salsa -- 4 oz chopped
24 ea Hard rolls
3 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1 lg Chopped onion
1 pk Longhorn cheese -- 12 oz
Grated
Brown ground beef and onion together; drain. Add remaining ingredients
excepts  rolls. Pinch bread out of center of rolls; fill with the beef
mixture. Wrap each roll in foil. Bake at 350 deg. for 30 min. Yield:
24 sandwiches.
