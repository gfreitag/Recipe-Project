Title: Evelyn's Orlglnal Roquefort Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressings, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Onion salt
Few drops Tabasco sauce
1 pk Roquefort cheese -- 4 oz.
Crumbled
1 ct Sour cream -- 8 oz.
1/2 c  Miracle Whip salad dressing
1 ts Tarragon vinegar
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Garlic salt
Mix together ingredients in the order given. Store, covered, in the
refrigerator. Yield: about 1 pint
