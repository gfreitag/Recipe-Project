Title: SALMON WITH GARLIC MAYONNAISE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
1    Garlic clove, minced
Salt and pepper
2    7-oz salmon fillets
Olive oil
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Chopped fresh parsley
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Dijon mustard
Preheat broiler. Combine first 5 ingredients in bowl. Season with
salt and pepper. Brush salmon with oil. Broil to desired degree of
doneness, about 3 minutes per side for medium. Transfer to plates.
Spoon garlic mayonnaise on side.
