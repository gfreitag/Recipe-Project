Title: ROSE-PINK GRAPEADE - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Beverages
Yield: 8 servings
-sparkling pink Catawba or
-other sparkling red grape
-juice, chilled
Ice cubes
1 cn (12-oz) frozen white
-grape-juice concentrate
3 1/2 c  Cold water
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1    750-milliliter bottle
In 1-gallon jar or very large pitcher, combine undiluted grape-juice
concentrate, water, and lemon juice, stirring until juice thaws. Add
sparkling grape juice. Serve immediately in glasses filled with ice
cubes.
