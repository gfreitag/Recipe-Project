Title: CHEESE-COATED GRAPES - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Appetizers
Yield: 16 servings
-pistachios
1/4 c  Chopped fresh dill, patted
-dry on paper towels
3 tb Poppy seeds
Fresh grape leaves (opt.)
1/2 lb Seedless green grapes
-(about 48 grapes)
1    8-oz container spreadable
-light cream cheese
1/3 c  Finely chopped shelled
1. Rinse and stem grapes; pat grapes dry. Spread about 1 t cream
cheese around a grape, covering it completely. Roll coated grape in
pistachios and place on plate or tray. Repeat to cover one-third of
the grapes.

2. Coat the remaining grapes the same, except roll one-third of the
grapes in dill and remaining grapes in poppy seeds. Cover and
refrigerate grapes until the coating is firm.

3. Just before serving, arrange grapes on serving plate to resemble
bunches of grapes. Garnish with grape leaves, if desired.
