Title: Gilbertini
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Pasta/rice, Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1 ts Oregano
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
2 pk Mozzarella cheese, shredded
2 lb Italian sausage
3 c  Mixed noodles, cooked
Spirals, pinwheels, etc.
3 c  Chicken bouillon/broth
6 tb Flour
Brown and drain sausage.  To thicken bouillon/broth, mix with flour;
add salt and pepper and oregano.  Add sausage to gravy.  Layer in a
9x13 inch pan the noodles, gravy mixture and mozzarella cheese.
Repeat layers, as necessary.  Top with Parmesan cheese.  Bake at 350
degrees for 30 to 40 minutes.  Let stand for 15 minutes. Randy Rigg
