Title: California Spa Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Olive oil
1/4 ts Garlic -- minced
4    Plum tomatoes -- slice thin
2 sm Zucchini -- slice thin
1    12" pizza crust
pn Crushed red pepper flakes 3/4 c  Nonfat ricotta cheese 2 tb
Parmesan; grated Preheat oven to 450~. Arrange slices of tomatoes and
zucchini alternately in concentric circles over crust. Sprinkle with
salt. Combine olive oil, garlic and red pepper flakes and brush on
veggies. Spoon dollops of ricotta cheese over top. Sprinkle with
Parmesan. Bake 10 minutes. Per Slice: 195 Cal; 5 Gr Fat.
