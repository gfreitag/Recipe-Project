Title: Parmesan Noodles
Categories: Pasta, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
2 tb Fresh parsley -- minced
8 oz Wide noodles
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Cook noodles according to package directions; drain. Place in a bowl.
Immediately add remaining ingredients and toss well. Yield: 4 servings
