Title: Easy Apricot Jam
Categories: Fruit, Preserves, Etc., Country wom
Yield: 30 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground ginger
16 oz Dried apricots
2 1/2 c  Orange juice
3/4 c  Sugar
In a large kettle, combine apricots, orange juice and sugar. Cover and
simmer for 30 min. Mix in lemon juice, cinnamon and ginger. Remove
from the heat and cool to room temperature. Puree in a food processor
or blender until smooth. Spoon into jars or freezer containers,
leaving 1/2 in. headspace. top with lids. Refrigerate or freeze.
Yield: 4 cups
