Title: Minted Beets
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Margarine;
1    Recipe whole cooked beets;
3 tb Mint jelly;
2 tb Butter -=OR=-
Combine jelly, butter and beets.  Heat slowly, stirring occasionally
until beets are glazed.
