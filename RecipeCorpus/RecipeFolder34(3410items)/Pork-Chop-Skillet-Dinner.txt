Title: Pork Chop Skillet Dinner
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Beef Bouillon Cube
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Sherry, Optional
16 oz Mixed Vegetables *
4 ea Pork Chops, 1/2" Thick
2 tb Oil
1 ea Med. Onion, Chopped
2 ts Caraway Seed
*  Mixed Vegetables should be 1 16 oz package Birdsey Broccoli,
Cauliflower and Red Peppers.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Brown pork chops on both sides in hot oil.  Add onion and caraway
seed and cook until onion is tender but not brown.  Add bouillon
cube, water and sherry.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat, cover and
simmer 15 minutes.  Stir in vegetables, cover and simmer 5 minutes
longer or until vegetables are heated through.
