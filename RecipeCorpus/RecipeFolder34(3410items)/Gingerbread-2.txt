Title: Gingerbread 2
Categories: Breadmaker, Desserts, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Ground ginger
3/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 1/4 c  Milk
3 tb Unsalted butter
6 tb Brown sugar
2 1/4 ts Active dry yeast
2 2/3 c  Bread flour
3/4 c  Rye flour
Scald the milk.  Stir in the butter and brown sugar.  Let cool to room
temp.  Add the milk mixture and all remaining ingredients in the order
suggested by the manual.  Bake on basic bread setting.
