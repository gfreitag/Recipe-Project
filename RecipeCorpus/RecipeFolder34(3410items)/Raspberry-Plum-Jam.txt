Title: Raspberry Plum Jam
Categories: Fruit, Preserves, Etc., Country wom
Yield: 30 servings
10 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Lemon juice
2 pk Liquid fruit pectin -- 3 oz
Each
4 1/2 c  Pitted plums -- coarsely
Chopped
2 pk Raspberries, frozen -- 10 oz
Each
In syrup thawed
In a large kettle, combine plums, raspberries, sugar and lemon juice.
Bring to a full rolling boil over high heat, stirring constantly.
quickly stir in pectin; return to a full rolling boil. boil for 1
min. stirring constantly. Remove from the heat; skim off any foam.
Pour hot into hot jars, leaving 1/4 in. headspace. Adjust caps.
Process for 15 min. in a boiling-water bath. Yield: 6 pints.
