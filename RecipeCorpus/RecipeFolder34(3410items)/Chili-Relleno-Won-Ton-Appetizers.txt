Title: Chili Relleno Won Ton Appetizers
Categories: Appetizers, Country wom
Yield: 72 servings
1 cn Green chiles -- 4 oz
Chopped
Add 1 more can for
Additional zip
1 pk Won-ton wrappers
2 lb Ground beef, extra lean
2 md Chopped onion
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 ts Oregano -- leaf
1/2 ts Cumin
1 ds Garlic salt
Brown and drain beef. Combine beef, onions, cheese, oregano, cumin,
garlic salt and chilies. Place a spoonful of the meat mixture on each
won ton wrapper and follow package directions for folding and
sealing. Deep-fry 1 1/2 min. or until golden; drain on paper towels.
Serve warm. Yield; 6 dozens appetizers.
