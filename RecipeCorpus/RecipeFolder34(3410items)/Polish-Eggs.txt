Title: Polish Eggs
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
8    Eggs
1/4 c  Milk
Salt & pepper, to taste
1/4 lb Polish sausage
6 sl Bacon
1 tb Finely chopped onion
Brown sausage, bacon and onion in skillet.  Mix eggs and milk.  Add
to meat mixture in skillet.  Add salt and pepper, to taste.  Cook
until eggs are done. Randy Rigg
