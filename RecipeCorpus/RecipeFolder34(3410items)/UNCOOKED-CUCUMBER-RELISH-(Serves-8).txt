Title: UNCOOKED CUCUMBER RELISH (Serves 8)
Categories: Relishes, Vegetables, Can/preserv
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Salt
1 ts White pepper
6 lg Cucumbers
3 sm Onions, chopped fine
1 pt White vinegar
Pare cucumbers and chop fine through food chopper.  Add salt.  Keep
cucumbers in a colander for 1 hour and add onions, pepper & vinegar.
Stir well. Pack in glass jars or bottles. Seal tight and let stand a
month before using.
