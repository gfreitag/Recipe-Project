Title: Chocolate Logs
Categories: Candies
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Jif crunchy peanut butter
1 lb Chocolate chips
1 lb Powdered sugar
1    Stick of margarine
4 c  Rice Krispies
Work powdered sugar, margarine, Rice Krispies and crunchy peanut
butter together and form into logs (2 x 1 inch).  Chill in
refrigerator. Melt chocolate chips over hot water.  Dip chilled logs
into melted chocolate. Randy Rigg
