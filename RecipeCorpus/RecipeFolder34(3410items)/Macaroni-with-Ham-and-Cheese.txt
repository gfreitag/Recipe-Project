Title: Macaroni with Ham and Cheese
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
2    Egg yolks, beaten
1 c  Swiss cheese, grated
Salt and pepper to taste
4 c  Macaroni
2 tb Butter
1 c  Cooked ham, julienned
1 c  Heavy cream
Cook macaroni. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter.
Saute ham until brown, approximately 2minutes. Add cream, bring to a
boil. Remove from heat and mix in egg yolks. Add grated swiss cheese
and return to low heat, stirring constantly until the cheese is
melted and sauce is creamy. Season to taste. Add macaroni and toss
well. Serve immediately. Submitted By JANE KNOX   On   09-13-95
