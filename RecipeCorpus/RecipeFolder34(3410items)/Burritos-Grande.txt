Title: Burritos Grande
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
4 lg Flour tortillas (10 inch)
1 c  Cheese; shredded
-Colby or Monterey Jack
1 lb Ground beef
1 1/4 oz Taco seasoning mix
1 c  Sour cream
16 oz Can refried beans; heated
Brown meat, drain. Add taco seasoning mix prepared as directed on
package. Stir in sour cream, heat thoroughly.

Spread beans down center of each tortilla. Top with meat mixture and
cheese; fold

Serve with salsa, lettuce, pitted ripe olive slices, sliced avocado
and additional sour cream.

Makes 4 servings
