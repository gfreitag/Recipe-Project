Title: Epping Sausage
Categories: Sausage, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
13 ea Ounces of salt
1/4 ea Of an ounce each of ground
-nutmeg and ginger
1/4 ea Of an ounce of rubbed
-marjoram
23 lb Of lean beef
7 lb Of fat pork
8 lb Of bread, soaked and
-pressed dry
4 ea Ounces of white pepper
Mix the seasonings with the meat, mince fine, then work in the bread;
place into the filler, fill into sheep casings, link them, and use by
frying and broiling. From: The Culinary Handbook Shared by: Pat
Stockett
