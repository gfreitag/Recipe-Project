Title: Black Forest Brownies
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 36 servings
Chopped
1/2 c  Nuts -- chopped
Icing:
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Confectioner's sugar
6 tb Cocoa powder
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Nuts -- chopped
1 1/3 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Cocoa powder
4 ea Eggs -- beaten
2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 ts Almond extract
1 c  Maraschino cherries --
Combine flour, baking powder and salt; set aside. In a large
saucepan, melt butter. Remove from the heat and stir in cocoa until
smooth. Blend in eggs, sugar and extracts. Stir in flour mixture,
cherries and nuts. Pour into a greased 13x9x2 inch baking pan. bake
at 350 deg. for 35 min. or until brownies test done. For icing blend
butter, vanilla, sugar, cocoa and milk until smooth; spread over hot
brownies. Sprinkle with nuts. Cool . Yield: 3 doz.
