Title: Peach of a Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
3 oz Sliced Canadian bacon
1/2 c  Thinly sliced red onion
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1 cn (10-oz.) Refrigerated pizza
-crust
2 c  Fresh peaches; divided
6 oz Sliced provolone cheese
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.  Grease 12-inch pizza pan or
13-by-9-inch pan. Unroll pizza dough and press into pan.  Top with 1
cup peaches, then cheese.  Arrange Canadian bacon, onion, green
pepper and remaining peaches. Bake on lowest rack for 20 to 25
minutes or until crust is golden brown. Serve hot.  Makes 8 slices.
