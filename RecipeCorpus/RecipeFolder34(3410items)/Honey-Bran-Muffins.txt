Title: Honey Bran Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 20 servings
5 ea Eggs -- beaten
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
4 c  All-BranÂ® Cereal
2 c  Pineapple juice -- lukewarm
2 c  Golden raisins
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Honey
In a small bowl, combine pineapple juice and raisins; set aside. In a
large mixing bowl, combine brown sugar, oil, honey and eggs; mix
well. Combine flour, baking soda and salt; stir in cereal. Add to
sugar mixture and mix well. Fold in the raisin mixture (batter will
be thin). Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours. or overnight. Stir
(batter will thicken). Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups
three-fourths full. Bake at 400 deg. for 20-25 min. or until muffins
test done. Cool in pan 10 min. before removing to a wire rack. Yield:
about 12 jumbo muffins or 20 standards-size muffins.
