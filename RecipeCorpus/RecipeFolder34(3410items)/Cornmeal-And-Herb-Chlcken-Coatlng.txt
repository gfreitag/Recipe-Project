Title: Cornmeal And Herb Chlcken Coatlng
Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Pepper
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Celery salt
1 ts Onion salt
1 ts Dried oregano
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
1 c  Powdered milk
1 c  Cornmeal
1 c  All-purpore flour
3 tb Salt
1 tb Dried thyme
Combine all the ingredients. To use. place 1 cup mixture in a plastic
bag; shake chicken pieces until coated. Place on a baking pan and
bake at 350 deg. for 1 hour. Store unused mixture in an airtight
container. Yield: Recipe makes enough coating for three chickens,
each 2-1/2 to 3 Ibs
