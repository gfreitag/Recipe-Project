Title: Fried Green Tomatoes SL (Shaker)
Categories: Side dish, Tomatoes
Yield: 6 servings
6 md Green tomatoes; cut into
-1/4-inch slices
1/2 c  (approx) vegetable oil
1/2 c  Cornmeal
1/4 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Combine cornmeal, sugar, salt, and pepper; stir well.  Dredge
tomatoes in cornmeal mixture.
Heat oil in a large skillet to 375 degrees F.; add tomatoes and fry
until golden brown on each side.  Drain on paper towels.
