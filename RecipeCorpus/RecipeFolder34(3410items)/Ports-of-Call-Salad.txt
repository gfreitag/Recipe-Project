Title: Ports of Call Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Hot sauce
1/2 cl Garlic; minced
1 oz Anchovy fillets; minced
2 ea Heads romaine lettuce; torn
1 lb Yellow squash; cut into
-julienne strips
1/2 c  Chopped fresh parsley
3 ea Egg yolks
1/2 c  Peanut oil
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Buttermilk
2 tb Parmesan cheese; grated
Combine first 3 ingredients in a large salad bowl, tossing well.
Beat egg yolks until thick and lemon colored.  Add oil, 1 tablespoon
at a time, to egg yolks, beating constantly until mixture begins to
thicken. Add mayonnaise and buttermilk, beating until smooth.  Add
remaining ingredients, and beat until smooth.  Toss dressing with
salad.
