Title: Pizza Muffins
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Pepperoni -- chooped fine
2 1/4 oz Black olives
3/4 c  Nonfat milk
1    Egg -- large
3/4 c  Mozzarella,cheese --
Shredded
2 c  All purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Basil -- dried
1 ts Oregano -- dried
2 tb Salad oil
1/2 c  Tomato -- chopped
Mix flour,baking powder,basil and oregano.  Add milk,egg and oil,stir
to moisten.  Add tomatoes,pepperoni,olives,and 1/2 the cheese,mix
well. Divide batter equally among 10 well oiled muffin cups (2 to 2
1/5 in wide). Top with remaining cheese. Bake in a 350 oven until
muffins are well browned (30-35 minutes). Let stand about 5 minutes,
then remove form pan. Serve warm or cool.
