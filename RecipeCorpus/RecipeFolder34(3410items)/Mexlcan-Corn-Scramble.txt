Title: Mexlcan Corn Scramble
Categories: Vegetables, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Ham -- cubed
Fully cooked smoked or
Sausage
3/4 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
Torilla chips
Picante sauce
1 sm Onion -- chopped
3 tb Butter or margarine
1 cn Whole kernel corn with
Peppers -- 11 oz. drained
1 cn Ripe olives
2 1/4 oz Sliced, drained
8 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
In a medium skillet, saute onion in butter until tender. Stir in corn
and olives. Add eggs; cook and stir over medium heat until eggs just
begin to set. Add ham or sausage and cheese. Cook until eggs are
fully cooked and cheese is melted. Serve with tortilla chips and
picante sauce. Yield: 6 servings
