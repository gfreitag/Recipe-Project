Title: Parmesan Potato Stlcks
Categories: Potatoes, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Black pepper
2 lb Russet potatoes
1/2 c  Butter -- melted
1/2 c  Bread crumbs -- fine dry
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
Scrub and peel potatoes: cut lengthwise into quarters. Cut each
quarter into 3 strips. Roll in melted butter, then roll in combined
remaining ingredients. Place potato sticks in a single layer on a
cookie sheet. Drizzle melted butter over potatoes. Bake at 400 deg.
for 30-35 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Yield: 6 servings
