Title: BERRY COBBLER - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Desserts, Fruit, Microwave
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
2 tb Buttermilk or lowfat yogurt
2 tb Butter, melted
3 c  Mixed berries (we used
-blueberries & raspberries)
6 tb Sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Grated lemon rind
1. In 1 1/2-quart microwave-safe dish, stir berries, 4 T sugar, the
comstarch, lemon juice, and lemon rind until well combined. Microwave
on high (100 percent) 6 to 7 minutes or until mixture bubbles. (If
microwave does not have carousel, rotate dish midway through
cooking.) Cool berry mixture 20 to 30 minutes.

2. Meanwhile, in small bowl, mix together 1 t of remaining sugar and
the cinnamon; set aside. In medium-size bowl, combine flour,
remaining 5 teaspoons sugar, the baking soda, and nutmeg; stir in
buttermilk and butter just until combined. Divide dough into 6
pieces; form each piece into a 1 1/2-inchround biscuit. Place in
microwave-safe shallow dish or pie plate and sprinkle tops of
biscuits with sugar-cinnamon mixture. Microwave on high (100 percent)
2 to 3 minutes or until centers spring back when lightly touched.

3. Place biscuits onto cooled berry mixture and serve.
