Title: Teriyaki Chicken Breasts
Categories: Poultry, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
-pieces
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1 tb Gingerroot, minced
1 tb Sherry
6    Chicken Breasts
1 c  Soy Sauce
3 tb Brown Sugar
1/8 c  Butter or Oil
6 oz Can  Pineapple juice or
Place chicken breasts in bowl or dish; combine remaining ingredients
in saucepan or bowl and heat or microwave just until sugar and butter
or oil dissolve; cool; pour over chicken; marinate for at least 2
hours, or longer, in refrigerator. Remove chicken and place directly
on grill and cook over medium heat until done, turning and basting
frequently with marinade; keep grill lid closed except as othewise
necessary.
