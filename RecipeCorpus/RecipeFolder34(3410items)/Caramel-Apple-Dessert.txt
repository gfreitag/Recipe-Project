Title: Caramel Apple Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Topping -- 12 ounces
1 c  Peanuts -- roasted chopped
6 ea Apples
Cored and cut into wedges
1    Jar caramel ice cream
Just before serving, cut apples and arrange on a platter or individual
plates. Heat caramel ice cream sauce according to package directions.
Place peanuts in a small bowl. To serve, dip apples in warm caramel
sauce and then in chopped peanuts. Yield: 6 servings
