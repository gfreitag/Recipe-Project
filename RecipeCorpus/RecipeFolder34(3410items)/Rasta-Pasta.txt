Title: Rasta Pasta
Categories: Pasta/rice, Salsa/sauce, Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts White pepper
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Salt
1    Bell pepper; chopped
4    Mushrooms; chopped
1/2    Zucchini; small; chopped
1/4 c  Corn; kernels
4    Broccoli; florets
4 oz Angel hair; or linguine; raw
1/4 c  Butter
1 sm Onion; diced
2    Cl Garlic; minced
2 tb Fresh ginger; minced
1    Chile pepper; seed; minced
2 c  Pumpkin; calabaza, butternut
1 c  Water or chicken stock
1 c  Coconut milk
1 ts Coriander
1 ts Cumin
Cook pasta, set aside.

Melt butter over medium heat, add onion, garlic, ginger, and chile
pepper and saute 4-5 mins. Add pumpkin and water to pan and cook
15-20 mins, until pumpkin is tender. Add coconut milk, coriander,
cumin, thyme, white pepper, nutmeg, allspice, and salt and simmer
another 4-5 mins. Place in food processor and process until smooth.

Melt remaining butter add pepper, mushrooms, and zucchini.  Saute 5
mins. Add corn and broccoli and saute 2 mins. Pour pumpkin sauce into
skillet and stir. Bring to a simmer. Cook 1 minute; add pasta. Serve
steaming.
