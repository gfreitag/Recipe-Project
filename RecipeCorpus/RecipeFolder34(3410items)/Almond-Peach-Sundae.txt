Title: Almond Peach Sundae
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Almond extract
1/4 c  Almond slivers
Vanilla ice cream
1 cn Sliced peaches -- 16 oz
1 tb Cornstarch
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
Drain peaches and place the liquid in a saucepan. Stir in cornstarch,
cinnamon and nutmeg. Cook and stir over med-high heat until thickened
about 2-3 min. Stir in extract, almonds and peaches. heat through.
Serve warm over ice cream. Yield: 4-6 servings.
