Title: Lemon Ice Cream
Categories: Ice cream
Yield: 4 cups
Juice of three lemons,
-strained
Grated peel of two lemons
1 c  Sugar
4 c  Water
1 c  Heavy cream
Boil the water, sugar, and grated lemon peel. Cook over medium heat
for 5 minutes. Allow to stand until it reaches room temperature, then
strain well and refrigerate for several hours.

Add cream and lemon juice.  Pour into ice cream machine and process
according to manufacturer's directions.

Recipe by Gabi Shahar.
