Title: Pineapple Chess Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 cn (8 1/2oz) crushed pineapple
1    9-inch pie shell
3/4    Stick oleo
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
Drain pineapple. Cream oleo and sugar. Add eggs and beat well. Blend
in vanilla and drained pineapple. Pour into unbaked pie shell. Bake
45 minutes in 350 degree oven.
