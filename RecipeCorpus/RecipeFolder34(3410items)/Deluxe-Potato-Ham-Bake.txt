Title: Deluxe Potato Ham Bake
Categories: Vegetables, Country wom, Meats, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
1 md Onion -- chopped
2 c  Ham -- cubed fully cooked
1    Bag frozen Southern-style
Hash brown potatoes -- 32 oz
Thaw
2 cn Condensed cream of chicken
Soup -- undiluted 10-3/4 oz
4 tb Butter or margarine
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz.
1 1/2 c  Cheddar cheese -- 6 oz.
Shredded
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
3/4 c  Cornflakes -- crushed
4 tb Butter or margarine
In a large bowl, combine first five ingredients; mix well. Stir in
ham and potatoes. Spread into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking
dish. Combine topping ingredients; sprinkle over casserole. Bake,
uncovered, at 350 deg. for 1 hour or until potatoes are tender.
Yield: 10-12 servings
