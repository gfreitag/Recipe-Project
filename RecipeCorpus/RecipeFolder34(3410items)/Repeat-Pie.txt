Title: Repeat Pie
Categories: Meats, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
Potatoes, diced
Carrots, diced
1 cn Biscuts, refrigerator
Roast beef
Roast beef gravy
Onions
Use leftovers from roast beef dinner.  All the ingredients are normaly
found in such a meal.

Cut Beef into cubes, thin gravy with water and combine all ingredients
except biscuts.  Place in greased casserole and bake at 400 F. for
15-20 minutes or until brown.
