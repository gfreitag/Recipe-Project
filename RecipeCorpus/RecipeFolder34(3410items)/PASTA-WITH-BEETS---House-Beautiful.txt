Title: PASTA WITH BEETS - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Pasta/rice, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Coarsely chopped fresh
-Italian parsley
1/2 c  Coarsely chopped fresh curly
-parsley
Freshly ground black pepper
-to taste
5 sm Fresh beets, about 1 lb,
-washed, stems removed and
-root ends trimmed
Water
1 lb Fresh linguine
1/2 c  Extra-virgin olive oil
In a saucepan cover beets with water, bring to a boil and cook,
covered, for about 30 minutes or until tender. Drain and reserve
cooking liquid. When beets are cool enough to handle, slip skins off,
slice into even rounds and set aside.

In a large pot, boil at least 4 quarts water and cook hnguine for
about 2 minutes or until al dente. Drain, place in a warm serving
bowl and toss with beets, olive oil, parsley and pepper. If desired,
toss pasta with 2 to 3 tablespoons ofthe reserved cooking liquid.
Serves 4 to 6.

House Beautiful/April/94  Scanned & fixed by DP & GG
