Title: Skillet Beans
Categories: Vegetables, Stove top, Fruit
Yield: 1 batch
3 tb Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Cornstarch
3 tb Vinegar
1 tb Soy sauce
1 cn 20 oz baked beans
4 sl Bacon
1/2 md Green pepper, chopped
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 cn 8 oz pineapple chunks
Drained
Pan fry bacon, green pepper, onion and pineapple in a 10 inch skillet
until lightly browned.  Combine brown sugar and cornstarch and stir
in vinegar and soy sauce.

Slowly stir into meat mixture and cook until thickened.  Stir in the
baked beans.  Simmer in skillet on top of range over low heat for
about 15 minutes.

Typed, but not tested
