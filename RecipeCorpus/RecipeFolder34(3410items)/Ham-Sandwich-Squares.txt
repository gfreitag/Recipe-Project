Title: Ham Sandwich Squares
Categories: Snacks, Low cal/fat, Appetizers
Yield: 12 snacks
-deli-style ham
2 md Tomatoes; thinly sliced
8 ea 2/3-oz. slices Healthy
-Choice American singles
-cheese
2 ea Cucumbers; peeled and thinly
-sliced lengthwise
1/2 c  Sliced green onions
4 oz Alfalfa sprouts; washed and
-drained
1/4 c  + 2 Tbsp lite ricotta cheese
2 tb Nonfat mayonnaise
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Garlic powder
x  Vegetable cooking spray
1 lb Loaf frozen white bread
-dough; thawed
1 tb Reduced-calories margarine;
-melted
6 ea 1-oz. slices Healthy Choice
Combine first 5 ingredidents in container of an electric blender;
process until smooth.  Set aside.

Coat a 15- x 10- x 1-inch jellyroll pan with cooking spray; set
aside. Roll dough on a lightly-floured surface  in a 15-x 10-inch
rectangle; transfer to pan.  Pat to the edge of pan; brush entire
surface with margarine. Let rise in a warm place (85 degrees), free
from drafts, 30 minutes or until doubled in bulk.  Bake at 450
degrees for 10 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from oven, and
cool 3 minutes. (Crust may be left in pan to serve or may be
carefully removed to a tray.) Spread crust with ricotta mixture.

Arrange remaining ingredients over ricotta mixture.  Cut into squares.
Yield:  12 servings.

PER SERVING:  CALORIES 160; FAT 2.7g; CARB 24.2g; PROTEIN 10.4g;
CHOLESTEROL 11mg; SODIUM 554mg.

From:  Healthy Choice CHOICES FOR LIVING HAPPIER AND HEALTHIER, Fall
1995.
