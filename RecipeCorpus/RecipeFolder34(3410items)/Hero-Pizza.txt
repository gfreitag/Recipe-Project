Title: Hero Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
3 oz Sliced provolone cheese
2    Plum tomatoes -- sliced
1 c  Lettuce -- shredded
Italian Dressing
1/4 c  Marinara sauce
1    12" pizza crust
3 oz Sliced capicola
3 oz Sliced salami
2 oz Sliced pepperoni
Preheat oven to 450~. Spread sauce over crust. Arrange capicola over
sauce. Layer with salami and pepperoni. Top with provolone. Bake 10
minutes. Top hot pizza with tomatoes and lettuce. Serve with Italian
Dressing. Per Slice: 320 Cal, 17 gr Fat.
