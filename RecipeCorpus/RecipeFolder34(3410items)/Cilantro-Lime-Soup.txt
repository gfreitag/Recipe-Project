Title: Cilantro-Lime Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
-chicken broth
1 c  Corn kernels
-fresh or frozen
1 c  Tomatoes
-seeded and chopped
1/2 bn Cilantro sprigs tied
-together with kitchen twine
2 tb Olive oil
1    Onion(s), chopped
2    Garlic clove(s), minced
1 tb Chile powder
2    Chicken breast halves
-skinned and boned
-cut into 3/4" pieces
5 c  Canned low-salt
MMMMM--------------------------TO SERVE-------------------------------
Salt and pepper to taste
Sour cream
1/4 c  Cilantro, chopped
1/4 c  Lime juice
Heat oil in heavy large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add onion and
garlic; saut until slightly softened, about 3 minutes. Add chile
powder; stir 1 minute. Add chicken; stir 2 minutes. Add broth, corn,
tomatoes and cilantro sprigs to saucepan; bring to a boil. Reduce
heat and simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 10 minutes.
Discard cilantro sprigs.

Add chopped cilantro and lime juice to soup. Season with salt and
pepper to taste. Garnish with sour cream.
