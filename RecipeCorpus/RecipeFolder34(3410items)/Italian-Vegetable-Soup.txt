Title: Italian Vegetable Soup
Categories: Vegetables, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 12 servings
1 tb Dried parsley -- flaked
1 ts Salt -- 1 oz
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Basil -- sweet
1/4 ts Black pepper
2 c  Cabbage -- shredded
1 c  Frozen green beans -- or
Fresh cut 1 in
1/2 c  Elbow macaroni -- small
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 c  Onion -- diced
1 c  Celery -- sliced
1 c  Carrot -- sliced
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1 cn Tomatoes -- 16 oz
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 15 oz
1 cn Red kidney beans -- 15 oz.
Undrained
2 c  Water
5 ts Beef bouillon -- granules
Brown beef in large kettle; drain. Add all the ingredients except
cabbage, green beans and macaroni. bring to boil. Lower heat; cover
and simmer 20 min. Add cabbage, green beans and macaroni; bring to
boil and simmer until vegetables are tender. If you prefer a thinner
soup, add additional water or broth. Sprinkle with the parmesan
cheese before serving.  Diabetic Exchange.
