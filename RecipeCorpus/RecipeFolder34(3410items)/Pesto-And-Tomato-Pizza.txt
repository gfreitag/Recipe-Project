Title: Pesto And Tomato Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 8 servings
1    Plum tomato -- sliced
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper
1/3 c  Prepared pesto
1    12" pizza crust
1 1/2 c  Mozzarella; shred -- divide
Preheat oven to 450~. Spread pesto over crust; sprinkle with 1 c
cheese. Top with tomato, 1/2 c cheese and red pepper. Bake 10
minutes. Per Slice 260 Cal, 13 Gr Fat.
