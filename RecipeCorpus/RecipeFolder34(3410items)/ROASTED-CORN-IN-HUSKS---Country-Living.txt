Title: ROASTED CORN IN HUSKS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
3 tb Butter, melted
8    Ears yellow corn with husks
1. Heat oven to 325'F (see Note). Carefully pull back husks and
remove all silk from corn. Rinse corn and thoroughly moisten husks.
Pull the husks back into place.

2. Place corn on oven rack around turkey roasting pan. Roast 40 to 45
minutes or until tender. Unwrap and brush corn kernels with melted
butter before serving.

Note: If you have a second oven or are not roasting a turkey, heat
oven to 400'F and arrange corn on top rack of oven; roast 35 to 40
minutes or until tender.
