Title: Zhug - Yemenite Hot Sauce
Categories: Condiments, Middle east, Hot/spicy, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
1/2 ts Cumin powder
Salt to taste
Olive oil to cover
1 lb Serrano peppers
5    Garlic HEADS, peeled
1    Bunch coriander, washed
1 ts Dried red pepper flakes
Place the serrano chilies (any other hot chili may be used), garlic,
and coriander in a food processor and chop finely.  Add the hot
pepper, cumin, and salt and pulse a few times to mix. Place in a
clean glas jar and cover with a little olive oil. Refrigerate.
