Title: Chocolate Cake Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Low cal/fat
Yield: 16 brownies
1/3 c  Soft margarine
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  Unsweetened applesauce
1/3 c  Chopped walnuts,optional
2/3 c  Splenda granular sweetner
2/3 c  All purpose flour
1/3 c  Cocoa
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
Combine splenda,flour,coca,baking powderand salt; set aside.In mixer
bowl,beat margarine,eggs and vanilla 1 minute.Add applesauce and beat
just until blended.Stir in flour mixture and nuts just until
blended.Spread evenly in greased 8" square pan.Bake at 350 degrees
for about 15 minutes or until tester inserted in centre comes out
clean.Cool on rack.Store in covered container in refrigerator.

Per serving Energy 75 calories Protein 1.7g Fat 4.9g Carbohydrates
6.7g
