Title: Lemon Dill Fish Fillets^
Categories: Low cal/fat, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Lemon dill coating
Lemon wedges, optional
4    (4oz) orange roughy or other
Lean white fish fillets
Vegetable cooking spray
MMMMM---------------------LEMON-DILL COATING--------------------------
1 tb PLUS (see next line)
1 ts Dried whole dillweed
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
2 c  Finely crushed plain Melba
Toast rounds
2 tb Paprika
2 tb Grated lemon rind
Coat both sides of fish with spray; sprinkle with salt. Place coating
is a shallow dish; dredge fish.Place in a spayed 11 x 7 x 2" baking
dish. Bake at 400F for 15 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a
fork. Serve with lemon wedges, if desired.

Per serving: 125 calories, 18.3 g protein, 2.2 g (16%), 7.6 g carb.,
23 mg chol., .9mg iron, 307 mg sodium, 8 mg calcium

Coating:

combine in a ziplock bag and shake well. Store in refrigerator and
shake well before each use. Use as a coating for fish and chicken.
Makes 2 cups.

Per 2 tablespoon serving: 43 calories, 1.6 g protein, .9 g (19%) fat,
7.5 g carb., .2 g fiber, 0 mg chol., .7 mg iron, 89 mg sodium, 6 mg
calcium.
