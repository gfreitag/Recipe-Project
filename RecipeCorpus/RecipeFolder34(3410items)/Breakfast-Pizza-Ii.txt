Title: Breakfast Pizza Ii
Categories: Breakfast, Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Mushrooms
1 cn Cresent roll dough
1 lb Breakfast sausage
x  Diced onions, peppers 2 tb Ranch or creamy Italian -dressing x
Grated sharp cheese 4    Eggs beaten with 1 T. milk Open roll of
dough and press into 12 inch pizza pan. Spread dressing on dough.
Cover with cooked sausage, onions, peppers, and mushrooms. Pour
beaten eggs over all. Top with cheese. Bake 20 to 25 minutes at 350
degrees.
