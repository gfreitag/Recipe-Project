Title: Chicken 'n' Rice Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
1/2    Green pepper -- chopped
1/2    Red pepper -- sliced
1/4 lb Fresh mushrooms -- sliced
1/2 c  Sliced ripe olives
1 cn Pizza sauce
1 ts Oregano -- crushed
1 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
4 c  Rice -- cooked
1/2 c  Walnuts -- ground
1    Egg -- beaten
1 1/2 c  Swiss cheese -- shredded
1/2 c  Grated parmesan cheese
2 tb Olive oil
2    Chicken breasts -- bite
Sized
1 sm Onion -- chopped
reheat oven to 375F.  Combine rice, walnuts, egg, 1/2 c swiss cheese,
and 1/4 cup parmesan cheese in large bowl. Press evenly over bottom
and 1/2 inch up the side of a greased 14" pizza pan. Bake 10 minutes.
Set aside to cool. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat
until hot. Add chicken, onion, peppers, mushrooms and olives; cook
and stir 7 minutes or until chicken is tender and no longer pink;
drain off excess liquid. Spread pizza sauce over cooled rice crust.
Layer remaining 1 cup Swiss cheese, chicken mixture, basil and
oregano over sauce. Top with mozzarella cheese and remaining 1/4 cup
parmesan cheese. Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until heated through. Let
cool slightly bbefore serving.
