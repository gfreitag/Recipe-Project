Title: Baked Cranberry Sauce
Categories: Condiments, Country wom
Yield: 20 servings
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 ts Ground cloves
2 c  Cranberries -- fresh or
Frozen
Place cranberries in a 1 qt. baking dish. Sprinkle with brown sugar
and cloves. Bake, covered at 350 deg. for 30 to 35 min. Stir after 15
min. Yield: 1 1/2 cup
