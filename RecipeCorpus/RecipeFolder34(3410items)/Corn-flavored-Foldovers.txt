Title: Corn-flavored Foldovers
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Dried thyme
1 ea Egg, beaten
3/4 c  Sour cream
3 tb Melted butter
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 tb Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees F. Blend dry ingredients, add sour cream
and egg.  Mix to a soft dough, knead 2 minutes. Roll 1/4" thick, cut
into 2" circles. Brush circles lightly with melted butter, fold in
half and brush top lightly with butter.  Arrange with edges touching
on baking sheet. Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until golden.  Makes about
24.
