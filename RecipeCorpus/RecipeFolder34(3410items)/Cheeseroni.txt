Title: Cheeseroni
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Cheese/eggs, Pasta/rice
Yield: 3 servings
3/4    Chopped green pepper
2 c  Cubed Colby cheese
1 sm Can French fried onion rings
2 c  Elbow macaroni, uncooked
1 lb Ground beef
1 cn Tomato soup
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
Cook macaroni; drain.  Set aside.  Brown ground beef; drain.  Stir in
soups, green pepper and macaroni.  Pour half of mixture into greased 2
quart baking dish.  Top with 1 cup cheese.  Top with remaining
mixture and cheese.  Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.  Top with
onion rings and bake 10 minutes more. Randy Rigg
