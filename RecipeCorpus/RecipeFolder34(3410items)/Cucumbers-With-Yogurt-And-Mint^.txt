Title: Cucumbers With Yogurt And Mint^
Categories: Vegetables, Salads, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/8 ts Pepper
4 c  Sliced seeded peeled
Cucumber
Cooking Light 9-95
Carolyn Shaw 8-95
1 c  Nonfat plain yogurt
3 tb Chopped fresh mint
1/2 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients except cucumbers in a bowl and stir well. Add
cucumbers; toss gently to coat. Cover and chill 30 minutes.

Per serving: 57 cal., 4.3 g protein, .4 g (6%) fat, 9.8 g carb., 1.9 g
fiber, 1 mg chol., .8 mg iron, 340 mg sodium, 144 mg calcium.
