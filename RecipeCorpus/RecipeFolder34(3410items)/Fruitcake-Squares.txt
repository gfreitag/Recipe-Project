Title: Fruitcake Squares
Categories: Cookies, Country wom
Yield: 72 servings
1/2 c  Candied pineapple --
Chopped
3/4 c  Chopped dates
1 c  Pecan halves
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
6 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
4 c  Vanilla wafer crumbs
3/4 c  Candied cherries -- green
Halved
3/4 c  Candied cherries -- red
Halved
Pour melted butter in a 15x10x1 inch baking pan. Sprinkle evenly with
wafer crumbs. Arrange cherries, pineapple, dates and pecans evenly
over the crumbs; press down gently. Combine milk and vanilla; pour
over fruit. Bake at 350 deg. for 20-25 min. Yield: about 6 dozen.
