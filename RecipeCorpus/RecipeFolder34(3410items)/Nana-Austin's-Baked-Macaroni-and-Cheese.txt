Title: Nana Austin's Baked Macaroni and Cheese
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Velveta cheese grated
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Buttered bread crumbs (opt.)
1 lg Box elbow macaroni
1 lb Cheddar cheese, grated
1 pt Sour cream
1 c  Milk, scalded
Boil macaroni until tender and rinse under cold water.  In a suace
pan, scald milk, add cheeses and stir until melted.  Remove from the
heat and add sour cream and mix well.  Add to cooled macaroni and
place in a greased baking dish.  Dot with the butter and sprinkle
with the buttered bread crumbs if desired.  Bake in a 375 degree oven
45-50 minutes or until golden brown.
