Title: GRILLED LAMB AND MELON KEBABS - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 30 kebabs
1/4 tb Ground coriander
1 lb Lean lamb (leg or well-
-trimmed shoulder), cut
-into 1-inch cubes
30    (about) 1-inch cubes
-cantaloupe
30    (about) 6-inch-long bamboo
-skewers, soaked in water 1
-hour and drained
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Balsamic vinegar* or red
-wine vinegar
1 1/2 tb Chopped fresh rosemary OR
1/2 tb Dried, crumbled
1    Garlic clove, finely chopped
1/2 tb Ground cumin
1/2 tb Salt
1/2 tb Freshly ground pepper
1/4 tb Cayenne pepper
Combine first 9 ingredients in small bowl. (Can be prepared 2 days
ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Place lamb in glass baking dish. Pour
marinade over lamb, turning to coat well. Cover and marinate 2 hours
at room temperature, or up to 8 hours in refrigerator.

Drain lamb, reserving marinade. Thread 1 piece of melon and 1 piece
of lamb on each skewer. Return to bakinc, dish, Spoon reserved
marinade over meat. (Kebabs can be prepared 4 hours ahead. Cover and
refrigerate.)

Prepare barbecue grill (medium heat). Arrange skewers around edges of
grill. Cook until lamb is slightly charred on outside and pink on
inside, turning occasionally, about 5 minutes. Arrange skewers on
platter.

*Available at specialty foods stores, Italian markets and some
supermarkets.

Bon Appetit/August/89  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
