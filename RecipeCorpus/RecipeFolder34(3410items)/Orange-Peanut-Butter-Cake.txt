Title: Orange Peanut Butter Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Peanut butter
2    Eggs, large
1 1/3 c  Fresh orange juice
1    Orange
1/3 c  Brown sugar, packed
2 2/3 c  Sifted flour
1 c  Granulated sugar
2 ts Baking soda
3/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
2/3 c  Brown sugar, packed
From: Arizona Cookbook

Sift together flour, granulated sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, and
salt into large bowl. Add 2/3 cup brown sugar, peanut butter, eggs,
and orange juice. Stir gently to mix, then beat about 100 times until
batter is smooth. (Or, beat at medium speed with electric mixer until
batter is smooth.)

Grate 1 tbsp of peel from orange. Peel orange, cut into fine pieces
and drain. Stir orange pieces and peel into batter. Pour batter into
greased and floured 13x9x2 in cake pan. Sprinkle 1/3 cup brown sugar
evenly over top of batter.

Bake at 325 for 45 to 50 minutes, until cake tests done. Cake is done
if springs back when touch lightly in center. Serve warm or cool.
