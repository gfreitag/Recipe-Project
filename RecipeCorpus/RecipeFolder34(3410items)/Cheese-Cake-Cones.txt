Title: Cheese Cake Cones
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 filled con  1 1/2 c  Berry or Cherry Pie Filling
1 cn Condensed Milk 14 oz.
(Not Evaporated Milk)
:
8 oz Cream Cheese,
-at room temperature
:
1/3 c  Lemon Juice
1 t  Vanilla
Package of Sugar or Plain Cones
:

1. Beat Cream Cheese
2. Beat In Milk Gradually
3. Blend in Juice and Vanilla
4. Alternately layer Cheese filling and pie filling in cones
5. Serve to family thats all.
