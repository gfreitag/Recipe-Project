Title: Peach Fruit Cup
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
3 lg Ripe peaches; halved, pitted
1 c  Fresh raspberries
1 c  Fresh blueberries
1/2 c  Lemon yogurt
1 ts Honey
1/8 ts Cinnamon
In a small bowl, combine yogurt, honey and cinnamon; blend.  Spoon 1
tablespoon yogurt mixture onto each serving plate.  Top each with
peach half, cut side up; spoon raspberries and blueberries over peach
halves. Drizzle with remaining yogurt mixture.

Makes 6 servings.
