Title: CARROT-VIOLA SALAD - Country Living
Categories: Salads, Country liv
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Small violet leaves or
-watercress sprigs
1/4 c  Pesticide-free viola
-blossoms without stems
-(or violets, pansies,
-or Johnny-jump-ups)
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 tb Red-wine vinegar
1 tb Honey
1/4 ts Salt
3 c  Coarsely shredded carrots
12    Leaves Boston lettuce, each
-torn in half
1. In medium-size bowl, whisk oil with vinegar, honey, and salt. Add
the carrots and toss until well mixed.

2. Line a shallow serving bowl or rimmed plate with lettuce leaves and
violet leaves. Arrange carrots in center and sprinkle with the viola
blossoms. Toss lightly and serve immediately.
