Title: Blueberry Jello Mold
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Blueberries, drained
1/4 c  Reserved blueberry syrup
3/4 c  Boiling water
3 oz Box lemon jello
1 pt Sour cream
1 c  Boiling water
3 oz Box black cherry Jello
Mix together lemon jello, sour cream and 1 c. boiling water.  Pour
into mold (preferably ring type) and chill until set. Mix together
remaining ingredients and pour over top.  Chill until set.  Unmold
and serve.  Great for bbq's.
