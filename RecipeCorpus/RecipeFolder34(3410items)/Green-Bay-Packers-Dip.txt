Title: Green Bay Packers Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Tarragon
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Parsley -- chopped
1/4 c  Green onion -- sliced
Place all ingredients in blender.  Blend until smooth.  Chill 30
minutes before serving.   Serve with chicken nuggets.
