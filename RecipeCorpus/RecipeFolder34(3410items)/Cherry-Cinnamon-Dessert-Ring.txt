Title: Cherry Cinnamon Dessert Ring
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 20 servings
1 ts Almond extract
2    Envelopes
Sweetened whipped cream
Gelatin -- unflavored
3 c  Sweet cherries, frozen --
Fresh pitted dark
3 1/4 c  Water -- divided
1 c  Sugar
2    Sticks cinnamon
In a saucepan, combine cherries with 3 cups of water, sugar and
cinnamon. bring to a boil; reduce heat, cover and simmer 15 min.
Remove from the heat. Stir in extract. Set aside. Place remaining
water in a saucepan. Sprinkle gelatin over water; let stand 1 min.
Heat until dissolved. Stir into cherry mixture and mix well.
Refrigerate until mixture begins to thicken, stirring occasionally to
distribute cherries evenly. Spoon into a 5 1/2 cup ring mold.
Refrigerate until set, about 3-4 hours. Turn onto a serving platter.
Serve with whipped cream. Yield; 20 min.
