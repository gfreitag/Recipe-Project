Title: Veggie Appetizer Squares
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 24 servings
1/4 c  Sour cream
2 c  Finely chopped assorted veg.
1 cn 8 oz crescent dinner rolls
6 1/2 oz Garlic & herb cream cheese
Heat oven to 375 dgrees. Separate rolls into 2 rectangles. Press onto
bottom and 1/4 inch up sides of 13x9 inch baking pan to form crust.
Firmly press preformations to seal. Bake 10 minutes, cool. Mix cheese
and sour cream. Spread over crust. Top with vegetables, cover,
refrigerate. Cut Into squares.
