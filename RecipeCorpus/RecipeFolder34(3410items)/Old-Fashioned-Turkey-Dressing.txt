Title: Old Fashioned Turkey Dressing
Categories: Main dish, Dress/stuff
Yield: 20 lb turkey
3 lg Onions
1    Hot Pepper
2    Sweet Peppers
Partly cooked Giblets
Dressing Seasoning
1 lg Loaf of White Bread
10    Eggs
1    Stick of Butter
1    Stalk Celery with leaves
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
(This recipe is for a 20 lb turkey. Cut ingredients down according
to bird size.) Grind or chop fine the bread, giblets, celery, onions
and peppers. Beat the eggs, melt the butter and add to bread mixture.
Add seasoning and mix well. Leftover stuffing may be baked in a
casserole with the bird.
