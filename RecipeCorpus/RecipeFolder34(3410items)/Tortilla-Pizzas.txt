Title: Tortilla Pizzas
Categories: Appetizers, Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
8 tb Green onions -- thinly
Sliced
4 tb Green pepper -- chopped
8 tb Pizza sauce
1 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
Chili peppers -- optional
2 tb Butter
2 c  Cooked chicken -- chopped
1 ts Oregano leaves -- crushed
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Black pepper
Vegetable oil
4    Corn tortillas -- 6 inch
8 tb Tomato -- chopped
Preheat oven to 375 F.  Melt butter in medium skillet over medium
heat. Add chicken, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook and stir for 3 to
5 minutes. Remove chicken from skillet with slotted spoon; place in
bowl and reserve. Pour oil into same skillet to 1/4 inch depth; heat
oil over medium-high heat.  Cook 1 tortilla in hot oil for about 1
minute or until crisp. Remove tortilla from skillet; drain on paper
towels, and place on a large baking sheet.  Repeat with remaining
tortillas. Spoon 1/4 of chicken mixture evenly over each tortilla.
Top each with 2 tablespoons tomato, 2 tablespoons onion, 1 tablespoon
green pepper, 2 tablespoons pizza sauce and 1/4 cup cheese. Bake 5
minutes or until cheese melts. Garnish with chili peppers if desired.
