Title: RAISIN BUNS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Breakfast, Breads
Yield: 12 buns
1/3 c  Butter
1 1/4 c  Milk
1/3 c  Dark seedless raisins
Sugar Glaze (recipe follows)
1/3 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
2 1/2 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1. Heat oven to 400'F. Lightly grease large baking sheet. In small
bowl, combine 1 T  sugar and the cinna- mon; set aside. In large
bowl, combine flour, remaining sugar, the baking powder, and salt.
Melt 1 T butter; set aside. With pastry blender or 2 knives, cut
remaining butter into flour mixture until mixture resembles coarse
crumbs.

2. Add milk and mix just until dry ingredients are moistened and a
stiff dough forms, being careful not to overmix.

3. Between floured sheets of waxed paper, roll out dough to a 12- by
8-inch rectangle. Brush dough with melted butter; sprinkle with
raisins and cinnamon-sugar mixture.

4. Using waxed paper for support, roll up dough to forri1 a 12-inch
roll. With knife, cut roll crosswise into twelve 1 -inch buns. Place
buns on greased baking sheet.

5. Bake buns 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown. Meanwhile, make
Sugar Glaze. When buns are removed from oven, brush immediately with
glaze. Cool buns on wire rack before serving.

Sugar Glaze: in 1-quart saucepan, heat 1/4 C  water and 1/4 C sugar to
boiling. Cook, stirring, 1 minute. Remove from heat and set aside
until ready to use.

Nutrition information per bun-protein: 4 grams; fat: 6 grams;
carbohydrate: 34 grams; fiber: 1 gram; sodium: 227 milligrams;
cholesterol: 17 milligrams; calories: 206.
