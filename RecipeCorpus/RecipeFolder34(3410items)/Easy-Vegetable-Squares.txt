Title: Easy Vegetable Squares
Categories: Appetizers, Pizza
Yield: 32 servings
Chopped
1 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1 c  Tomato -- diced
1 c  Ripe olives -- sliced
1 c  Carrot -- shredded
1/2 c  Celery -- diced finely
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
2 cn Refrigerated crescent rolls
12 oz Cream cheese -- softened
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Dried dill weed -- crushed
1 ts Buttermilk salad dressing
Mx
1 c  Broccoli; blanched --
Unroll crescent rolls and pat into a 15 1/2x 10 1/2 x 1 inch baking
pan. Bake according to package directions. Cool on wire rack.
Meanwhile blend cream cheese, mayonnaise, dill weed, and salad
dressing mix in small bowl. Spread evenly over crust.  Sprinkle with
desired toppings, then shredded cheese.  To serve, cut into squares.
