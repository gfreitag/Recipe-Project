Title: Provencal Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Parmesan; grated -- divided
1/2 c  Kalamata olives; pit --
Chop
2 oz Can flat anchovies -- drain
2 tb Basil -- julienne
1/4 ts Cracked black pepper
2 tb Olive oil
4 md Onions -- slice thin
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Rosemary -- crushed
1/4 ts Pepper
3    Plum tomatoes -- sliced
1    12" pizza crust
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat olive oil and add onions, salt, rosemary
and pepper; cook, stirring occasionally, until onions are tender and
golden. Arrange tomatoes over crust. Sprinkle with 1/4 c Parmesan.
Top with onions, anchovies, basil 3/4 c Parmesan and pepper. Bake 10
minutes. Per Slice 315 Cal, 14 Gr Fat.
