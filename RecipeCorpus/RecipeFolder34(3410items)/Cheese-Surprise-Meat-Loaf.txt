Title: Cheese Surprise Meat Loaf
Categories: Meats, Cheese/eggs, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Onion, finely chopped
1/2 c  Catsup or A-1 Sauce
1 c  Cubed velveta cheese
1/8 ts Garlic powder
2 lb Ground beef
2    Eggs slightly beaten
1 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Mix all ingredients together except for the cheese.  Take 1/2 of the
mixture and pat into a loaf pan bringing some of the meat up the
sides of the pan approximately 1/2 inch.  Sprinkle the cubed velveta
in the center and pat down the remaining half of the meat mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 to 55 minutes until nicely brown.
