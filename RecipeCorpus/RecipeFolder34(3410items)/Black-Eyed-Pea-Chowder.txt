Title: Black-Eyed Pea Chowder
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
Each
Rinsed and drained
1 cn Beef consomme -- 10 1/2 oz
2 cn Stewed tomatoes -- 14 1/2 oz
Each
1 lb Bacon
1 c  Celery -- chopped
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Green pepper -- chopped
2 cn Blackeyed peas -- 16 oz
In a saucepan, cook bacon until crisp. Remove bacon; crumble and set
aside. Discard all but 2 tablespoons of drippings. Saute celery,
onion and green pepper until tender. Add bacon and all remaining
ingredients; heat through. Yield: 2 1/4 qts.
