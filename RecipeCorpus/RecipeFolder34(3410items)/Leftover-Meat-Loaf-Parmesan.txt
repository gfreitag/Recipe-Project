Title: Leftover Meat Loaf Parmesan
Categories: Ground, Beef, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Leftover (each about
3/8    -inch thick)
Shortening for pan-frying
Ketchup, optional
1 c  Saltines -- crushed
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 ea Egg
3 tb Water
4 sl Meat loaf
Combine the saltine crumbs and Parmesan cheese in a shallow dish: set
aside.Beat egg with water Dip meat loaf slices in egg mixture, then in
crumb mixture. Heat shortening in a skillet. Brown meat loaf slices
on both sides. Serve with ketchup if desired. Yield: 2-4 servings
