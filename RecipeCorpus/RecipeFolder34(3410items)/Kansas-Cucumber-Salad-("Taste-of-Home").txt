Title: Kansas Cucumber Salad ("Taste of Home")
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Low cal/fat, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Salt, optional
4 md Cucumbers, peeled/sliced
Thin
3    Green onions, chopped
1 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/4 c  Sugar
4 ts Vinegar
1/2 ts Dill weed
In a large bowl, combine mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, dill and salt if
desired; mix well.  Add cucumbers and onions; toss.  Cover and chill
for at least 1 hour.  Yield: 8 servings. Diabetic Exchanges: One 2/3
cup serving (prepared with low fat mayonnaise and without added salt)
equals 2 vegetable, 1-1/2 fat; also, 126 calories, 197 mg sodium, 6
mg cholesterol, 14 gm carbohydrate, 1 gm protein, 8 gm fat.
