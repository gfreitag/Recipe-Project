Title: Brussels Sprouts & Carrots in Lemon Butter
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
4 tb Butter
1    Small clove garlic, minced
Juice and grated peel
-of 1/4 lemon
1/2 lb Brussels sprouts,
-trimmed and halved
1/2 lb Carrots, scrubbed and
-halved lengthwise
-and crosswise
Steam sprouts and carrots for 10 minutes.  Drain and arrange in a
heated serving dish.  Melt butter with garlic, add lemon juice and
peel, and pour over vegetables.  (Save stock from vegetables for
other recipes.) Serves 4 Submitted By JULIE GLEITSMANN   On   09-20-95
