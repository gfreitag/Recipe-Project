Title: Tuna Divan
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Tuna, drained
1 1/2 ts Cornstarch
pn Pepper
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
2 ts Chopped parsley
1/2 bn Broccoli
2 tb Mayonnaise
pn Salt
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Dry bread crumbs
2 ts Margarine, melted
In a saucepan, bring about 1 cup water to boil. Add broccoli. Cook
uncovered, 5 minutes or until tender-crisp. Drain. Arrange broccoli
in a small greased casserole. Top with tuna. In a small saucepan,
stir together mayonnaise, cornstarch, salt and pepper until smooth.
Gradually stir in milk until smooth. Stir in cheese. Stirring
constantly, bring to a boil over medium heat and boil 1 minute. Pour
over tuna. In a small bowl, stir together bread crumbs, parsley and
margarine. Sprinkle over sauce. Bake in a 350 degree oven 20 to 25
minutes or until hot and bubbly.
