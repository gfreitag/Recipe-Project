Title: Fudge Brownies (reduced calories)
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Low cal/fat
Yield: 16 servings
1/3 c  All-purpose flour
1/3 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
x  Nonstick cooking spray
1/2 c  Sugar
6 pk Sweet One(R)
1/4 c  Margarine, melted
2 lg Eggs
2 tb Butter Buds(TM) Sprinkles
1 ts Vanilla extract
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Spray 8-inch square baking pan with
nonstick cooking spray.  In medium-size bowl, stir together sugar,
Sweet One, margarine, eggs, Butter Buds Sprinkles and vanilla.  In
small bowl, stir together dry ingredients; stir into liquid
ingredients just until blended.  Spread into pan.  Bake 25 minutes.
Cool on wire rack. Cut into 2-inch squares.  Makes 16 servings.

(This recipe saves you 1/2 the calories of a regular brownie.)

Source:  coupon circular advertisement for Sweet One and Butter Buds.
