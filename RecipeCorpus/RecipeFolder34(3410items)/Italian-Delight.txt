Title: Italian Delight
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Pasta/rice
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Cream style corn
1 pk Medium egg noodles
Shredded Longhorn cheese
2 lb Ground beef
1/2    Green pepper
1 md Onion
1 cn Tomato soup
Brown ground beef, green pepper and onion.  Drain excess fat.  Add
tomato soup and creamed corn; let simmer.  Cook noodles as package
directions. Mix meat mixture and noodles in a large baking dish,
about 9 x 13 inch cake pan or glassware.  Top with cheese, bake at
350 degrees until cheese is melted and hot. Randy Rigg
