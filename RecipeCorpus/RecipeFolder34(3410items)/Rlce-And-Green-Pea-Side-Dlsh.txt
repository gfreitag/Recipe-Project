Title: Rlce And Green Pea Side Dlsh
Categories: Rice, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Carrot -- peeled/shredded
1 ts Chicken bouillon granules
1 ts Salt-free herb seasoning
Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Uncooked long grain rice
2 c  Water
1 md Onion -- chopped
2 c  Frozen peas -- defrosted
Place all ingredients in a 3-qt. saucepan. Cover and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to simmer and continue to cook 15 minutes or until rice is
tender. Yield: 6 servings
