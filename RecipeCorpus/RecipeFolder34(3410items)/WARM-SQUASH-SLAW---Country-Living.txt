Title: WARM SQUASH SLAW - Country Living
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Country liv
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
2 tb Firmly packed light-brown
-sugar
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
1/4 ts Caraway seeds
1 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Chopped green onions
1    Small (1 lb) cabbage,
-coarsely shredded
1    (1 1/4-lbs) butternut
-squash, coarsely shredded
1. In large skillet, heat oil; add green onions and saute until
wilted. Add cabbage; stir-fry 5 minutes or until it is slightly
tender.

2. Stir in squash, vinegar, brown sugar, salt, pepper, and caraway
seeds. Cover and cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes
or until cabbage is wilted. Serve slaw immediately.
