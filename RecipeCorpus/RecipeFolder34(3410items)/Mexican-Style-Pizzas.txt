Title: Mexican Style Pizzas
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Canned chicken -- drained
1/2 c  Canned pinto beans
1 cn Green chiles -- cut strips
1 c  Monterey jack cheese --
Shredd
1/2 c  Pizza sauce
1/2 c  Mild salsa
1 tb Parsely -- minced
4 lg Flour tortillas
1 cn Whole kernel corn
Preheat broiler.  Mix pizza sauce, salsa and parsley. spread on
tortillas. Arrange corn, chicken, pinto beans and chiles over sauce;
sprinkle with cheese. Broil 3 minutes or until cheese is melted.
