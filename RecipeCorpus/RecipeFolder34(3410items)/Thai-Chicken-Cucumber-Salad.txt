Title: Thai Chicken Cucumber Salad
Categories: Newspaper, Asian, Salads, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-----------------------THAI  DRESSING----------------------------
1 1/2 ts Garlic; chopped
1 tb Asian fish sauce (nam pla)
1/2 ts Sugar
3 tb Vegetable oil
Juice of 1/2 lemon or lime
2    Dried red chile peppers  **
.   * OR *
. 1 fresh serrano; sliced
.   * OR *
. cayenne pepper to taste
1/2 ts Ground pepper
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
4 sm Nodes of ginger; cut into
. fine slivers
1    Green onion; thinly sliced
2    Stalks celery; cut julienne
1 tb Cilantro leaves
4    Chicken breast halves;
. sliced very thin
2 md Cucumbers; sliced 1/4-inch
. thick; cut in half &
. seeded
**  Red chile peppers; seeded and soaked in water for about 5 minutes.

Make the dressing.

In a blender or food processor, blend the chile peppers, black pepper,
garlic, nam pla, sugar, vegetable oil and lemon juice.

Toss the chicken and vegetables in the dressing.  Sprinkle with the
cilantro leaves.

Nutritional Information per serving:  273 calories, 16g fat, 72mg
cholesterol, 365mg sodium
