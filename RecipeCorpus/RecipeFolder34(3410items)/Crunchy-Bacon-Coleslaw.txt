Title: Crunchy Bacon Coleslaw
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Red cabbage -- shredded
1/2 c  Peanuts -- chopped
4 sl Bacon -- cooked & crumbled
3/4 c  Miracle Whip+
1 tb Sugar
4 c  Cabbage -- shredded
Mix dressing and sugar in large bowl.  Add remaining ingredients; mix
lightly.  Refrigerate.
