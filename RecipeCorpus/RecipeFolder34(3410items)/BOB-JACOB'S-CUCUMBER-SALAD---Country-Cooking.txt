Title: BOB JACOB'S CUCUMBER SALAD - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
3 tb Chopped fresh dill
4 md Cucumbers, peeled and cut
-into 1/4-inch-thick slices
1 sm Red onion, thinly sliced
1 c  Distilled white vinegar
1  In large serving bowl, combine cucumbers and onion; set aside,

2. In 1-quart saucepan, combine vinegar, sugar, and water. Heat to
boiling over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until sugar
dissolves.

3. Pour vinegar mixture over cucumbers and onion. Add dill and toss to
combine. Cover and refrigerate several flours or overnight before
serving.
