Title: Dessire's Lemon-Chicken Soup
Categories: Poultry, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
-chopped
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts White Pepper -- ground
5 c  Chicken Stock
1/2 c  Rice, Quick-Cooking
2 tb Lemon Juice
1 tb Fresh Parsley -- finely
In a large stockpot over high heat, bring the chicken stock to a
boil. Add the rice; reduce the heat to a simmer and cook, covered,
for 15 mins, or until the rice is tender. Add the remaining
ingredients and cook 2 mins. longer. Serve hot. Makes 4 1/2 cups.
