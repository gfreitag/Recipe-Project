Title: FRESH BLACKBERRY MUFFINS - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Muffins, Breads, Fruit
Yield: 18 muffins
1/2 tb Salt
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Fresh blackberries or frozen
-blackberries, thawed and
-drained
4 tb Sugar
1/2 c  (1 stick) unsalted butter,
-room temperature
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2 lg Eggs, room temperature
2 c  All purpose flour
2 tb Baking powder
Preheat oven to 375'F. Grease eighteen 1/2-cup muffin cups. Using
electric mixer, cream 1/2 C butter and 1 1/4 C sugar in large bowl
until light. Add eggs 1 at a time, beating well after each addition.
Sift flour, baking powder and salt into small bowl. Mix dry
ingredients into butter mixture al@ temately with milk. Fold in
berries.

Divide batter among prepared cups. Sprinkle 4 T sugar over. Bake until
tester inserted in centers comes out clean, about 30 minutes. Serve
warm or at room temperature.
