Title: Greek Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Feta cheese -- crumbled
1 tb Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
2 ts Green onion -- chopped
1    Pita bread -- halved
Olive oil
1/4 c  Pizza sauce -- divided
1 tb Chopped greek olives
Brush pita halves lightly with oil.  Bake on baking sheet in
preheated 425F oven for 5 minutes.  Spread with pizza sauce; sprinkle
with olives, feta cheese, mozzarella cheese and green onion. Bake at
425F for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
