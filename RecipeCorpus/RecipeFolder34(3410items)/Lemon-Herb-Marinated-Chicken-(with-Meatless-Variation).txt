Title: Lemon Herb Marinated Chicken (with Meatless Variation)
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Boneless, skinless chicken
-breast halves
2 tb Mazola RightBlend(R) canola
-& corn oils
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Rosemary or tarragon;
-crushed
In cup, mix first 5 ingredients; pour over chicken.  Cover; let stand
at room temperature no longer than 30 minutes, turning once.  Broil
10 minutes or until thoroughly cooked, turning once.  Makes 4
servings.

Each serving (with chicken) provides: 200 calories; 25g protein; 1g
carbohydrate; 10g total fat (3g poly, 1g sat); 70mg cholesterol; 300mg
sodium.

Meatless Variation:  ROASTED VEGETABLES:  Mix first 5 ingredients as
above; omit chicken.  In 13" x 9" baking dish toss 6 cups cut-up
vegetables with marinade.  Roast in 450 degree F. oven 20 minutes or
until tender, stirring once.  Makes 6 servings.
