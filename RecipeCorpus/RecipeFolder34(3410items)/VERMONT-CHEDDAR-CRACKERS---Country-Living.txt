Title: VERMONT CHEDDAR CRACKERS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Holiday, Breads
Yield: 48 crackers
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb To 2 T ice water
1/2 c  (1 stick) butter, softened
1/2 c  Shredded yellow Vermont
-Cheddar cheese
1 1/4 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
1. In medium size bowl, with electric mixer on medium speed, beat
butter and cheese until well blended. Reduce speed to low and beat in
flour, baking powder, salt, and 1 T  water until dough is smooth. Add
more water, if necessary, to make dough manageable. Wrap and
refrigerate dough 30 minutes.

2. Heat oven to 350'F. Grease 2 baking sheets, Between 2 pieces of
floured waxed paper, roll out dough to 1/4-inch thickness. Using
2-inch holiday-shape cookie cutters, cut out dough. Reroll scraps and
cut out more shapes until all dough has been used.

3. Place cut dough, or crackers, 2 inches apart on greased baking
sheets. With fork, pierce tops of each several times.

4. Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until edges are golden brown. Remove
crackers to wire racks and cool completely. Store in airtight
container.
