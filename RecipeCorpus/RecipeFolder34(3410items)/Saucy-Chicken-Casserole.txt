Title: Saucy Chicken Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Poultry seasoning
1/2 ts Garlic salt
2 c  Bisquick mix
1 1/2 c  Milk
3 c  Cut up cooked chicken
2    10 3/4 oz. cans of cream of
Mushroom soup
16 oz Frozen mixed vegetables
Heat oven to 450 degrees.  Mix chicken, soup, vegetables, seasoning,
and garlic salt in an ungreased 9 x 13 x 2 inch baking dish.  Stir
Bisquick mix and milk.  Blend well.  Pour over chicken mixture.  Bake
30 to 32 minutes, or until crust is golden brown. Randy Rigg
