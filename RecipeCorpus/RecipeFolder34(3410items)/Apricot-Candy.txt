Title: Apricot Candy
Categories: Candies
Yield: 3 dozen
1/2 c  Chopped apricots
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1 c  Chocolate chips
Put nuts in glass bowl and microwave on high 2 minutes.  Put
chocolate in glass bowl and microwave on high 1 to 2 minutes until
melted. Stir until smooth and fold in apricots.  Drop by teaspoonfuls
onto waxed paper. * CROSSPOSTED Submitted By HOME_COOKI  On   09-16-95
