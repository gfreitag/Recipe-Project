Title: SPICY-SWEET GRILLED SHRIMP - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Main dish, Fish/seafoo, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Large shrimp, shelled and
-deveined
Salt to taste
12-inch wooden skewers
-immersed in water at least
-30 minutes before using
1/2 c  Lime jelly
1/4 c  Garlic jelly
1/3 c  Fresh lime juice
1 tb Plus 1 t Worcestershire
-sauce
1 ts Minced fresh jalapeno
-pepper
Note: The lime and garlic jellies can be substituted by combining 3/4
C apple or guava jelly with 1 large minced garlic clove.

In a small saucepan, combine jellies, lime juice, Worcestershire
sauce, and jalapeno pepper. Cook m@e over medium heat until jellies
melt. Remove from heat and set aside.

Thread 4 shrimp on each wet wooden skewer so the shrimp lay flat.
Brush both sides of shrimp liberally with jelly mixture and place on
a tray. Allow shrimp to marinate for 15 minutes. Just before
grilling, salt to taste. Grill or broil shrimp 2 minutes on each
side, brushing on more of the jelly mixture before turning. Serve hot
on skewers with remaining sauce.
