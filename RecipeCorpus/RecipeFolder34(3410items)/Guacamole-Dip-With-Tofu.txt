Title: Guacamole Dip With Tofu
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 cup
1/3    (10.25-ounce)block silken
. tofu, inpieces
Corn chips or steamed
. artichoke
1/4 c  Onion, roughly chopped
3 cl Garlic, roughly chopped
Juice of 1 lemon
1    Avocado
1/4 ts Salt
In a blender, mix all ingredients except chips or artichoke to a
smooth texture.  Serve with chips or artichoke.

Per serving: Calories 108, Fat 9g, NO cholesterol, sodium 141mg,
percent calories from fat 67%
