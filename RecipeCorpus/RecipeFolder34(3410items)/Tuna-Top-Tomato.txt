Title: Tuna Top Tomato
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Tomatoes
Yield: 4 servings
2    Green onions (with tops)
Sliced thinly
1 pk Frozen chopped broccoli,
Thawed and drained well
6 1/2 oz Can tuna, drained
4 lg Tomatoes - cut horizontally
Into halves
1/2 c  Bisquick mix
1/2 c  Shredded Swiss cheese
1/4 c  Mayo
2 lg Eggs
Preheat oven to 400.

Spray 9 x 9 inch pan with non stick vegetable oil.  Place the
tomatoes cut side up in the pan.

Mix remaining ingredients.  Spread that mixture evenly on the cut
side of each tomato half.

Bake uncovered until topping is puffed and golden brown.  About 20
minutes.
