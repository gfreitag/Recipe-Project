Title: Fruited Chops
Categories: Pork, Ham, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Ground ginger
1 lg Apple -- cored and chopped
2 c  Cooked rice
1 bn Green onion -- sliced
1 tb Cooking oil
4 ea Pork chops -- 1 inch thick
Lean
1 cn Chicken broth -- 10 3/4 iz
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Vinegar
1/2 c  Apple juice
In a 10 in. skillet, heat oil over medium-high. brown chops on both
sides. Stir in chicken broth, soy sauce and vinegar; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat cover and simmer 20 min. or until chop are tender.
Meanwhile in a small bowl, combine apple juice, brown sugar,
cornstarch and ginger; stir until smooth. Remove  chops from skillet
and keep warm. Increase heat to medium. Stir cornstarch mixture into
skillet; cook and stir until thickened. Add chopped apple and heat
through. On a platter, arrange chops over rice. Spoon sauce over
chops and top green onions. Yield: 4 servings.
