Title: Cheesy Asparagus Soup
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1    Jar sliced mushrooms --
4 1/2 oz Drained
3 c  Cheddar cheese -- 12 oz
Shredded
3 lb Fresh asparagus -- trimmed
1 sm Onion -- chopped
2 cn Cream or asparagus soup
10 3/4 oz Ea undiluted
2    Soup cans milk
In a large kettle, cook asparagus and onion in a small amount of water
until tender. Drain liquid. Add all remaining ingredients; heat over
medium until the cheese is melted and the soup is hot. Yield: 8-10
servings
