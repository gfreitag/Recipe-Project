Title: Cherry Ice Cream
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 qt Cream
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk --
14    Ounces
1 tb Vanilla extract
1 ts Almond extract
6 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 cn Cherry pie filling -- 21
Ounces
In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until thick and
lemon-colored. Scald milk; slowly pour into egg mixture, stirring
constantly, until sugar is dissolved. Cool. Chop the cherries by hand
or in a food processor. Combine cherries with egg mixture and all
remaining ingredients. Freeze in an ice cream maker. Yield: about 1
gal
