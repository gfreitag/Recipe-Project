Title: Easy Sauerkraut Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1    Jar pimientos -- 2oz chopped
Drained
1 c  Sugar
1 cn Sauerkraut -- 27oz rinsed &
Draine
1 c  Finely chopped celery
1 c  Finely chopped onion
In a large bowl, combine the first four ingredients. Pour sugar over
and mix well. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Serve chilled. Yield:
8-10 servings
