Title: Moroccan Beet Salad
Categories: Israeli
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Cumin
1/2 c  Parsley, chopped
Salt and pepper
6 sm Beets
1/2 c  Olive oil
2 lg Lemons, juiced
Don't peel the beets.  Cook in water 40 minutes. Drain, cool slightly
and peel.  Cut into quarters.  Slice each quarter into 1/5 inch
slices. Add the other ingredients and let rest for 1 hour. Serve at
room temperature.

Origin: "Yediot Aharonot" newspaper, November 1994. Translated by Gabi
Shahar, November 1994.
