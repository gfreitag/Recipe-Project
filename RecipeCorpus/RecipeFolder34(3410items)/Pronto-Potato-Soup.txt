Title: Pronto Potato Soup
Categories: Potatoes, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Soup -- 10-3/4 ounces
1    To 2 soup cans of milk
1/2 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
2 tb Parsley -- chopped
8 ea Bacon strips -- cut in
Pieces
1 sm Onion -- chopped
2 c  Mashed potatoes -- leftover
1 cn Condensed cream of chicken
In small frying pan, brown bacon until crisp. Remove and let drain on
paper towel. Add onion to drippings in pan and saute 2-3 minutes.
Drain fat off. Meanwhile, in a 3-qt. saucepan, mix cold mashed
potatoes and soup until smooth. Add milk gradually to desired
consistency, stirring constantly. Add bacon and onions. Season with
salt, pepper and parsley. Heat through. Yield: 3-4 generous servings
