Title: Meaty Spanish Rice  #
Categories: Rice, Dishes, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Hot pepper sauce
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper -- optional
1 ds Pepper
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 lb Ground turkey
1 md Chopped onion
1 md Green pepper -- chopped
2 c  Water
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 8 oz
1 c  Long-grain rice -- uncooked
In skillet melt butter over medium heat. Add the ground turkey, onion
and green pepper; cook until meat is brown and vegetables are tender.
Add all of remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat,
cover and simmer until rice is tender, about 30 min. Yield: 4
servings.
