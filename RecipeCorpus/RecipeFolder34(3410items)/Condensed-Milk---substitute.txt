Title: Condensed Milk - substitute
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Powdered milk plus 2 T
1/2 c  Warm water
Mix powdered milk and warm water together and place in a double
boiler. Stir in 3/4 cup of sugar and stirr until sugar is melted and
all absorbed. Cool and use in place of canned sweetened condensed
milk. Submitted By DOTTIE THERIAULT   On   09-14-95
