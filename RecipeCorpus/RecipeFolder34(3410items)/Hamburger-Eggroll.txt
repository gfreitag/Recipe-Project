Title: Hamburger Eggroll
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
6    Water chesnuts, chopped
1 sm Onion, chopped
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Accent
1 lb Hamburger, cook and drain
2    Eggs
2 c  Cabbage, sliced fine
1 cn Bean sprouts
2    Jars of mushroom slices 2oz.
1/4    Bell pepper, chopped
Mix and place desired amount on eggroll wrapper.  Deep fry until
golden brown. Randy Rigg
