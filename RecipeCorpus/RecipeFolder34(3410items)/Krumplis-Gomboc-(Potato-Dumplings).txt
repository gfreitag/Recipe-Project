Title: Krumplis Gomboc (Potato Dumplings)
Categories: Hungarian, Soup/stew, Potatoes, East europe
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Flour, all-purpose; heaping
1 ea Eggs
1 pn Salt
2 md Potatoes
2 qt Water
1 tb Butter, unsalted
Boil unpeeled potatoes in 2 quarts of water. When done, peel and
puree while still warm. Add butter, flour, egg, and salt. Mix the
dough and knead it well.
With a spoon, cut off pieces of dough. Flour your hands and roll the
pieces into round dumplings. Drop dumplings into boiling soup and
cook for about 5 minutes, until tender.
