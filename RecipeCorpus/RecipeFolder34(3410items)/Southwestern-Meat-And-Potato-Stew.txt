Title: Southwestern Meat And Potato Stew
Categories: Meats, Stews, Country wom, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
3 md Potatoes -- cubed
1 c  Salsa
1 ts Salt -- optional
1 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Pepper
2 tb All-purpose flour
2 lb Lean ground beef -- or
Chuck
1 lg Onion -- chopped
1 c  Water -- divided
1 cn Tomatoes -- 28 oz liquid
Cut-up
1 pk Frozen corn -- 16 oz
In a dutch oven , brown beef and onion; drain. Add 3/4 cup water and
all remaining ingredients except flour. Bring to a boil; reduce heat.
Cover and simmer for 1 1/2 hours. Combine flour and remaining water;
stir into stew. Cook and stir until boiling and slightly thickened.
Yield: 6 servings. Diabetic Exchanges; One serving prepared with
ground chuck and low-sodium tomatoes and without added salt) Yield 8
servings.
