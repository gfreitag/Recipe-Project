Title: BUNUELITOS DE QUESO (CHEESE BUNS) - Country Living
Categories: Breads, Cheese/eggs, Appetizers, Country liv
Yield: 16 buns
1 ts Salt
2    Large eggs, lightly beaten
1 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
Ketchup (opt.)
Vegetable oil for deep-fat
-frying
2 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
2 tb Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1. In large skillet, heat 1/2 inch oil until deep-fat thermometer
registers 325'F.

2. In large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add
eggs and milk; stir until mixture is just combined. Fold in cheese.

3. Drop heaping tablespoonfuls of cheese batter into hot oil and fry
6 to 8 minutes or until buns are browned and cooked through, turning
buns over halfway through cooking time. Drain buns on paper towels
and serve warm on serving platter with ketchup, if desired.
