Title: Cranberry Sloppy Joes
Categories: Ground, Beef, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
Oz
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Chili powder
1 ds Hot pepper sauce
8 ea Hamburger buns -- split &
Toasted
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Chopped onion
1 cn Tomato soup, condensed -- 10
3/4    Oz
Undiluted
1 cn Cranberry sauce -- jellied 8
In a skillet, cook beef, celery and onion until meat is brown and
vegetables are tender. Drain. Stir in soup, cranberry sauce, salt,
chili powder and hot pepper sauce. Simmer, uncovered about 30
minutes, stirring occasionally. Spoon into buns. Yield: 8 servings.
