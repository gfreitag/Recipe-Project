Title: No Guilt Refried Beans^
Categories: Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Finely chopped onion
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Margarine
1 cn (16oz) pinto beans, rinsed
And drained
1/4 c  Thick and chunky salsa
Combine beans, salsa, onion and garlic powder in a 2-quart saucepan.
Bring mixture to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally.

Reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer 8-10 minutes or until onion is
translucnt, stirring occasionally. Add margarine, stir until melted.

In a food processor or blender, process mixture until smooth. Serve
as a taco ingredient or chip and vegetable ingredient. 4 servings.

Per serving: 123 calories, 5 g protein, 3 g fat (21%), 18 g
carbohydrate, 3 mg cholesterol, 675 mg sodium, 6 g fiber.
