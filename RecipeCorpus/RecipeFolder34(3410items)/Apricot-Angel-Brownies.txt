Title: Apricot Angel Brownies
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Dried apricots -- finely
Chopped
1/4 c  Almonds -- sliced
1/4 c  Coconut flakes
2 ea White chocolate -- baking 2
oz Each
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
2 ea Eggs -- beaten
1/4 ts Vanilla extract
3/4 c  All-purpose flour
In a saucepan, melt chocolate and butter over low heat, stirring
constantly until all the chocolate is melted. Remove from the heat;
stir in brown sugar, eggs and vanilla until blended. Set aside. In a
bowl, comine flour, baking powder and salt. Stir in chocolate
mixture. Combine apricots, almonds and coconut; stir half into the
batter. Pour into a greased 9 inch square baking pan. Sprinkle
remaining apricot mixture on top. Bake at 350 deg. for min. or until
golden brown. Cool. Yield: about 2 dozen.
