Title: Poultry Seasoning Butter
Categories: Dips/spread, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 batch
1 ts Fresh chopped rosemary
1 ts Fresh chopped lemon thyme
1 ts Fresh chopped oregano
1 ts Fresh chopped sage
1/2 lb Unsalted butter
-- at room temp.
1    To 2 pressed garlic cloves
A little salt and pepper
Blend all ingredients thoroughly.

From Lynn Alley's "Herbs in Butter" article in "The Herb Companion."
April/May 1993, Vol. 5, No. 4.  Pg. 47.  Posted by Cathy Harned.
