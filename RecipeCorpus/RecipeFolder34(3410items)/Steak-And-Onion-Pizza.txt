Title: Steak And Onion Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
2 ts Fresh tarragon -- minced or
1 tb Fresh parsley -- minced mix
W/
1/2 ts Dried tarragon -- crumbled
1    12" pizza crust
1 c  Fontina cheese -- diced
1 ts Vegetable oil
3/4 lb Flank stead
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1    Red onion -- cut 1/2"
Wedges
1/2 c  Green onions -- sliced
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat oil over high heat. Sprinkle steak with
salt and pepper. Cook 1 minutes per side. Slice thin. Add onion to
skillet; cook 2 minutes. Transfer onion to bowl with green onions and
tarragon. Arrange evenly on crust. Top with cheese. Bake 10 minutes.
Per Slice: 300 Cal, 13 Gr Fat.
