Title: Creamy Chicken Divan^
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Poultry seasoning
1 ct (8oz) sour cream
1 c  (4oz) shredded cheddar
Cheese, divided
2 pk (10oz) frozen broccoli
Spears
1 cn Cream of chicken soup,
Undiluted
2 c  Chopped, cooked chicken
Place broccoli spears in a shallow 2-qt. baking dish. Cover tightly
with heavy duty plastic wrap and fold back a small corner of wrap to
allow steam to escape. Microwave at high 3 minutes. Rearrange spears;
cover and microwave 3-4 minutes. Drain and set aside.

Combine soup, chicken and poultry seasoning in a microwave safe bowl;
cover and cook 2-3 minutes. Stir in sour cream and 1/2 cup cheese.
Cook an additional 2 minutes.

Spoon chicken over broccoli and sprinkle with the rest of the cheese.
Cook 1 minute.

Note:

To reduce fat and calories, substitute reduced fat soup, cheese and
sour cream.
