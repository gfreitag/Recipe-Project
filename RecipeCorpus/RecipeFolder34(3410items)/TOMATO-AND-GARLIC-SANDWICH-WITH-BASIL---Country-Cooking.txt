Title: TOMATO AND GARLIC SANDWICH WITH BASIL - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Sandwich, Appetizers
Yield: 4 sandwiches
1    Ripe plum tomato, sliced
-into quarters
4    Fresh basil sprigs
4    Carrot mums (opt.)
1 tb Olive oil
2    Cloves garlic, chopped
1 sm Club roll or baguette, cut
-diagonally in quarters
1. In large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add garlic and saute
until tender-1 minute; remove 1/4 t garlic and set aside.

2. Add bread to skillet and cook until lightly toasted. On serving
platter, place bread quarters. Top each with a tomato slice and basil
sprig, then sprinkle with reserved garlic. Garnish with carrot mums,
if desired.
