Title: Bob's Kitchen Sink Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Green Bell Pepper --
-chopped
1/2 c  Pimientos -- chopped &
-drained
1/4 c  Celery -- chopped
3 tb Oil
1 ts Salt
4 md Potatoes, Peeled --
-diced in 3/8" cubes
2    Garlic Cloves -- minced
1/4 c  Beef Broth
1/2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over high heat until hot. Add the
potatoes, garlic, and salt and saute for 3 to 4 mins, turning
frequently. 2. Combine the broth with the Worcestershire sauce, and
pour over the potatoes. Bring to a boil. Add the vegetables, stir to
blend, cover, and steam for another 5 to 6 mins. 3. When broth is
absorbed and vegetables are crisp-tender, serve immediately. Makes 4
to 6 servings.
