Title: PORTUGUESE ROAST BEEF ** (RRBM10B)
Categories: Main dish, Meats, West europe
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Pickling spice
Salt to taste
4    A potatoes; quartered
23    A large onions; sliced
1    Chuck roast; bone in or bone
1 cn Whole tomatoes; large
1/2 c  Red wine
3    Cloves garlic; sliced
Place the roast in a baking pan. Place the potatoes around it. Put the
sliced onions on top of this. Pour the tomatoes on next, cutting them
up a bit.  Pour on the wine, add the spices and add enough water to
slightly cover.  Bake at 350 degrees for about 2 1/2 hours or until
everything is tender. You can use sweet potatoes if you like. FROM:
SHERRYL PERRY (RRBM10B)
