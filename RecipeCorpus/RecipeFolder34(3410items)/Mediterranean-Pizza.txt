Title: Mediterranean Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 8 servings
1/3 c  Marinara sauce
1    12" pizza crust
6 oz Fresh mozzarella -- slice
Thin
1/3 c  Kalamata olives; pit --
Chop
1 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
1 tb Olive oil
1 c  Onions -- sliced
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper flakes
1 ts Garlic -- minced
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat oil. Add onions and spices. Cook, stirring
frequently, until onions are tender, 5 minutes. Stir in garlic; cook 1
minute. Spread sauce over crust; layer with mozzarella, onion mixture
and olives. Bake 10 minutes. Sprinkle with parsley. Per Slice: Cal
310, Fat 15gr.
