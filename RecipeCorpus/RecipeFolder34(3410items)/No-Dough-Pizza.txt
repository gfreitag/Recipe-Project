Title: No Dough Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 1/2 ts Italian seasoning
1 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
Optional toppings
2 c  Wheat chex cereal --
Crushed to 1 cup.
1 lb Lean ground beef
1    Egg
1 c  Prepared pizza sauce
Preheat oven to 375 F.  In medium bowl combine crushed cereal, egg,
meat, 1/4 cup sauce, parmesan cheese and seasonings. Add salt and
pepper if desired.  Press mixture into ungreased 12 inch pizza pan to
form a "crust". Bake 10 minutes.  Remove from oven and top with
remaining 3/4 cup sauce, mozzarella cheese and desired toppings.
Return to oven for an additional 10 to 15 minutes until cheese is
melted.
