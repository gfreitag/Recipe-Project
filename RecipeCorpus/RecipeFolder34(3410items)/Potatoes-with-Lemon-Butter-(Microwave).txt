Title: Potatoes with Lemon Butter (Microwave)
Categories: Side dish, Potatoes, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
5 tb Fresh lemon juice
2 tb Chopped fresh dill
3 tb Butter
1 3/4 lb Sliced, waxy potatoes
Salt and pepper
Melt the butter in a bowl in the microwave for 1 minute on high.
Put the potatoes on a dish and season with salt and pepper.  Pour
the melted butter and lemon juice over.  Cover and microwave on high
for 17 minutes.
Leave to stand for 5 minutes, sprinkle with chopped dill and serve.
