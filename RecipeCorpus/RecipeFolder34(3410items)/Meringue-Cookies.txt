Title: Meringue Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 ts Vanilla
2    Egg whites
2/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Chocolate chips
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Beat egg whites until stiff.  Gradually
add sugar, then fold in chocolate chips, nuts and vanilla.  Drop by
teaspoonfuls onto a foil lined, greased cookie sheet.  Turn oven off
and put cookies in oven overnight, or for at least 6 hours. Randy Rigg
