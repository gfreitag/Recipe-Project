Title: BANANA ORANGE SHERBET - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Ice cream, Desserts
Yield: 2 quarts
1 tb Vanilla extract
3    Ripe medium bananas (about 1
-lb 5 oz), sliced
3 tb Fresh orange juice
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
Coarsely chopped walnuts
3 c  Milk (do not use nonfat or
-lowfat)
3/4 c  Sugar
3    3-inch-long strips orange
-peel (orange part only)
6 lg Egg yolks
Scald milk, sugar and orange peel in heavy large saucepan over medium
heat, stirring until sugar dissolves. Whisk yolks in medium bowl
until smooth. Gradually whisk in hot milk mixture. Return to
saucepan. Stir over low heat until custard thickens and coats back of
spoon, about 10 minutes; do not boil. Strain into large bowl. Stir in
vanilla. Cool custard.

Puree bananas, orange juice and lemon juice in processor. Transfer to
large bowl. Stir in custard 1 C at a time. Refrigerate until well
chilled.

Transfer banana mixture to ice cream maker and process according to
manufacturer's instructions. Freeze in covered container several
hours. (Can be prepared 1 day ahead. If frozen solid, soften slightly
in refrigerator.) Scoop into bowls and garnish with walnuts.

Bon Appetit/August/89  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
