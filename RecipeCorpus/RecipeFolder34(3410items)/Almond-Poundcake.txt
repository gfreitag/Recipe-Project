Title: Almond Poundcake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Almond extract
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Butter, softened
1 c  Margarine, softened
3 c  Sugar
5    Eggs, beaten
3 c  Flour
Cream butter, sugar and margarine, then add beaten eggs.  Sift flour
and baking powder.  Add flour mixture and milk alternately to the egg
mixture. Add flavorings.  Pour batter into a greased and floured
bundt cake pan. Place pan in cold oven and turn on to 325 degrees.
Bake 1 1/2 hours. Randy Rigg
