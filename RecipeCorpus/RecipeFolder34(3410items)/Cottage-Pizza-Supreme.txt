Title: Cottage Pizza Supreme
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1/2 lb Mushrooms -- sliced
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 pk Hot roll mix
1/2 lb Bulk italian sausage --
Cooked
2 c  Cottage cheese
1 cn Seasoned tomato paste
Prepare roll mix according to package directions. Press into bottom
and sides of buttered 15x10 inch jelly roll pan. Let rise 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 375F. Combine all remaining ingredients except
parmesan cheese in large bowl.  Spread on prepared dough. Top with
remaining parmesan. Bake 35 minutes or until cheese is hot and bubbly.
