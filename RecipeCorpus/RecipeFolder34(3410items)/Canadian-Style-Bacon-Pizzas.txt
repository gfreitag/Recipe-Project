Title: Canadian Style Bacon Pizzas
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
3 oz Roasted peppers -- strips
1 c  Jack cheese w/jalapeno --
Shrd
1/4 c  Olives -- sliced
8 oz Canadian bacon -- sliced
6 ts Olive oil
1 md Onion -- wedges
1/2 ts Oregano -- crushed
4    6" Pitas -- split horiz
Preheat oven to 450 F.  Cut bacon onto 1/2 inch strips. Heat 2 t oil
in small skillet over med-high heat until hot. AAdd onion; cook and
stir 3 minutes. Combine remaining 4 t oil and oregano in small bowl.
Place pita breads smooth side up, on baking sheet and brush evenly
with oil mixture. Top with onion, bacon and red peppers.  Sprinkle
cheese and olives evenly over tops.  Bake 5 minutes or until cheese
is melted.
