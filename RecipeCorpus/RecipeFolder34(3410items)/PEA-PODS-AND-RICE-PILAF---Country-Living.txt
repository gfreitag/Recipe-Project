Title: PEA PODS AND RICE PILAF - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Chopped fresh tarragon
-leaves or 1 t dried
-tarragon
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Ground black pepper
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 1/2 c  Long-grain white rice
1/4 ts Saffron threads, crushed
2 1/2 c  Water
1/2 lb Sugar-snap pea pods
1/2 lb Fresh green peas (in pods)
1. In 4-quart saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion; saute
until translucent. Stir in rice and saffron until grains are coated
with oil. Add water and heat to boiling over high heat. Stir rice
mixture; cover pan; reduce heat to low and simmer rice until tender
and all liquid has been absorbed-about 15 minutes.

2. Meanwhile, remove tips and any strings from pea pods. Shell green
peas.

3. When rice is tender, remove from heat and let stand, covered, 5
minutes. Meanwhile, in large skillet, heat butter over medium heat.
Add pea pods and shelled peas; saute until tender-crisp. Stir in
tarragon, salt, and pepper.

4. With fork, lightly toss pea-pod mixture into rice. Transfer rice
mixture to serving bowl.
