Title: Pizza Con Cannellini
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
-fine
1 sm Jalapeno pepper; chopped
-fine
2 tb Olive oil
4 tb Coarsely chopped fresh basil
2 tb Chopped fresh mint
1 oz Grated Parmesan cheese
3 oz Shredded Fontina cheese
Pizza dough (see separate
-recipe "Pizza Dough
-'Crucial Crust'")
1 c  Cannellini beans (about 1/2
-can), drained
1 lb Plum tomatoes; seeded and
-chopped fine
4 ea Cloves garlic; minced very
Preheat oven to 500 degrees.  Press out pizza dough.  Combine all
remaining ingredients in a bowl and mix well.  Spread evenly over the
dough, and pop into the oven for 20 to 25 minutes.  Presto, a pizza.

Per slice:  333 calories; 10g fat; 22mg cholesterol.
