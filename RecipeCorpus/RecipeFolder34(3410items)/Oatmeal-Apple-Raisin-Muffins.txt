Title: Oatmeal Apple Raisin Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Sugar
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 ts Nutmeg
2 ts Cinnamon
Apple
1    Egg
3/4 c  Milk
1 c  Raisins
1    Chopped
1/2 c  Oil
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Quick oats
Beat egg: stir in remaining ingredients, mixing just to moisten. Pour
into 12 greased muffin cups until 3/4 full. Bake at 400 deg for 15 to
20 minutes. Serve cool or piping hot with butter
