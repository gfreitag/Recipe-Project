Title: Corned Beef and Cabbage
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Corned beef
1 ts Salt
1 md Cabbage
16 oz Tomatoes (canned)
Wash cabbage; chop medium fine.  Heat tomatoes in saucepan to
boiling. Add cabbage, cover.  Cook 5-10 minutes or until cabbage is
tender. Submitted By JJ JUDKINS   On   09-05-95
