Title: PEPPER SLAW - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Dijon-style prepared mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
1/4 ts Celery seeds
1 ea Large sweet red, green
-and yellow pepper
2 c  Firmly packed shredded green
-cabbage
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Olive or vegetable oil
1. Quarter and seed peppers; cut into very thin strips. in medium-size
bowl, combine peppers and cabbage.

2. In jar with tight-fitting lid, combine vinegar, oil, sugar,
mustard, salt, black pepper, and celery seeds. Shake vigorously to
combine. Pour over peppers and cabbage mixture. Cover tightly and
refrigerate at least 1 hour. Toss before serving.
