Title: CREAMY RAINBOW ROCKS
Categories: Dress/stuff, Beverages
Yield: 24 drinks
1 1/2 c  Plain yogurt
1/2 c  Drink crystals; any flavour
2 c  Water
Mix drink crystals, water and yogurt.  Freeze in ice cube trays.

Several different flavours can be made ahead of time and stored
separately in plastic bags in freezer.

Serve in your favorite juice.
