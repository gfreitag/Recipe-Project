Title: Melon Balls Wlth Llme Sauce
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Cubes
2 c  Cantaloupe balls -- or
Cubes
2 c  Honeydew balls -- or cubes
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar
Juice and grated peel of 1
Lime
2 c  Watermelon balls -- or
In a small saucepan, heat water, sugar, lime juice and peel; bring to
a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes. Cool. Pour sauce over fruit
and toss to coat. Chill 4 hours. Yield: 12-16 servings
