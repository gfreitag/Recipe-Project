Title: Herbed Ricotta And Walnuts
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
Thin
2 tb Parmesan -- grated
1/2 c  Gorgonzola cheese --
Crumble
1/3 c  Walnuts -- chopped
3/4 c  Ricotta cheese
1/2 c  Fresh basil -- chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    12" pizza crust
2    Plum tomatoes -- sliced
Preheat oven to 450~. Combine ricotta, basil, salt and pepper. Spread
evenly over pizza crust. Arrange tomato slices in a circle 1" in from
the cheese border. Sprinkle with Parmesan, Gorgonzola and nuts. Bake
10 minutes. Per Slice 255 Cal, 11 gr Fat. VARIATION-Mozzarella,
Gorgonzola and Walnuts-Spread 1-1/2 c shredded mozzarella over crust.
Prepare and bake as directed but omit ricotta, basil, salt and
pepper. Per Slice: 280 Cal, 14 gr Fat.
