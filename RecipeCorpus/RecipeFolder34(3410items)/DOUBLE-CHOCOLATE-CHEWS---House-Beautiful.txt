Title: DOUBLE-CHOCOLATE CHEWS - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Holiday, Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 3 dozen
4 lg Eggs
1 3/4 ts Vanilla extract
1/8 ts Almond extract
3/4 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1/4 c  Confectioners' sugar, to
-garnish
1 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
4 oz Unsweetened chocolate
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 2/3 c  Sugar
In a medium bowl combine flour, baking powder and salt; set aside.

In the top of a double boiler, melt chocolate with oil, stirring
occasionally with a wooden spoon. Remove from heat and stir in sugar.
Add eggs, 1 at a time stirring  until blended. Stir in vanilla and
almond extracts.

Pour the chocolate mixture into dry ingredients and stir until
thoroughly mixed. Add chocolate chips. Cover dough and chill at least
1 1/2 hours or up to 48 hours.

Roll chilled dough to form 1 1/4 balls. Space balls 2 inches apart on
greased cookie sheets. Bake in upper third of preheated 350'F oven 11
to 12 minutes, or just until firm in the center. Remove from oven and
let stand 1 minute; transfer cookies to wire racks to cool
completely. Lightly dust cookies with confectioners' sugar before
serving.

Store cookies in an airtight container up to 1 week or freezer up to 1
month. Makes about 3 dozen 2 1/2-inch cookies.
