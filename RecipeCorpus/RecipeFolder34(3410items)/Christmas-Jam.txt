Title: Christmas Jam
Categories: Canning, Preserves, Etc., Country wom
Yield: 14 servings
Frozen
5 lb Sugar
2 ea Pectin -- 3 oz each
Liquid fruit pouches
2 pk Frozen strawberries -- whole
-20 oz.
-OR-
2 1/2 qt Fresh strawberries
1 lb Cranberries -- fresh or
Grind strawberries and cranberries in a food processor or grinder;
place in a large kettle. Add sugar. Bring to a full rolling boil;
boil for 1 min. Remove from the heat; add pectin. Cool for 5 min.;
skim off foam. Pour hot into hot jars, leaving 1/4 in. headspace.
Adjust caps. Process for 15 min. in a boiling-water bath. Yield;
about 14 half-pints
