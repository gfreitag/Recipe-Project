Title: BBQ Corned Beef
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Chili powder
1 cn Corned beef
3 md Onions, chopped
1 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Worchestershire sauce
1 ts Vinegar
Brown onions in fat,  Add remaining ingredients with 2 cups water.
Simmer 2 hours or until thick.
