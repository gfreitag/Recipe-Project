Title: Cran-Apple Sauce
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1/3 c  Apple juice
1/4 ts Ground mace
1/8 ts Coriander -- ground
1 c  Cranberries -- fresh or
Frozen
6 lg Apples -- peeled & chopped
1/2 c  Sugar
In a large saucepan, combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce
heat; cover and simmer for 10 to 15 min. or until apples are tender.
Remove from heat and cool slightly. Puree mixture in food processor.
Cover and refrigerate. Yield: about 4 cups
