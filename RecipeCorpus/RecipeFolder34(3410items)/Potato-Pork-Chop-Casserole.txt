Title: Potato Pork Chop Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Potatoes
Yield: 3 servings
Salt and pepper to taste
6    Pork chops
2 c  Potatoes
1 cn Mushroom soup
1 sm Carton of sour cream
1/4 c  Water
1 tb Parsley
Mix above ingredients.  Brown pork chops.  Slice 2 cups of potatoes
thin. Alternate layers of mix and potatoes.  Salt and pepper to
taste. Cover with pork chops.  Bake 1 hour and 15 minutes at 375
degrees covered. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   09-09-95
