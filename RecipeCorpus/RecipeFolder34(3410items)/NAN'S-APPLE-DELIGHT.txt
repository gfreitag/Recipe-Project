Title: NAN'S APPLE DELIGHT
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 1 pie
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Apples, diced
1/2 c  Chopped black walnuts **
1 ts Vanilla (scant)
2 x  Eggs, well beaten
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
5 tb Flour
** 1 ts. black walnut extract or maple extract can be substituted
Beat eggs well and sift in sugar, flour, salt and baking powder. Mix
well Add apples, nuts (or extracts) and vanilla. Mix well. Pour into
a 9 in. pie plate that has been sprayed with Pam. Bake at 350 degrees
for 40 minutes.

* Serve with dollops of whipped cream or vanilla ice cream while warm
from the oven.
