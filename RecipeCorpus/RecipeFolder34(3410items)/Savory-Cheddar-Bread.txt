Title: Savory Cheddar Bread
Categories: Breads, Country wom
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/4 c  Sharp cheddar cheese --
Shredded
1 ea Egg -- beaten
1 c  Milk
1 tb Butter -- melted
2 c  All-purpose flour
4 ts Baking powder
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Onion salt
1/2 ts Oregano -- leaf
1/4 ts Dry mustard
Combine flour, baking powder, sugar, onion salt, oregano, dry mustard
and cheese; set aside. Combine egg, milk and butter; add all at once
to dry ingredients, stirring just until moistened. Spread batter in a
greased 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 inch loaf pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 45 min.
Yield: 1 loaf.
