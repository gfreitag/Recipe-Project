Title: Aunt Ruth Packard's Potato Chip Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 5 dozen
1/2 c  Crushed potato chips
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
2 c  Flour
2    Sticks of butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix margarine and sugar and vanilla.  Add
crushed potato chips and nuts mixed with flour.  Roll into balls
about the size of a walnut and place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Flatten down with a glass that has been dipped into sugar.  Bake for
14-16 minutes. Makes approximately 5 dozen cookies
