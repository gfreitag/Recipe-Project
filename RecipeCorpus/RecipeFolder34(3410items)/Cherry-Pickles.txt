Title: Cherry Pickles
Categories: Can/preserv, Pickles, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
6 tb White sugar
3 tb Coarse pickling salt
8 c  Cherries, with stems
2 1/4 c  White vinegar
2 1/4 c  Water
Pack cherries into hot sterilized jars.  Combine the remaining
ingredients, bring to boil and boil for 4 minutes.  Pour syrup over
cherries and seal. Allow to stand for at least 2 weeks before
serving. Makes 4 pints.

Origin: Preserves, by Jean Pare. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Aug/95.
