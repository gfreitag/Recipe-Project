Title: Sweet Cranberry Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
2    Oranges, grind rind & pulp
1 c  English walnuts
2 c  Sugar
2 pk Lemon Jello
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
1 1/2 c  Cold water
1 lb Ground cranberries
Mix Jello with boiling and cold water.  Allow to thicken.  Meanwhile,
grind cranberries, oranges and walnuts.  Mix in sugar.  When Jello is
ready, mix with remaining ingredients.  Pour into mold and
refrigerate until solid. Randy Rigg
