Title: Jalapeno Vodka
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Vodka
1    Jalapeno
Jalapeno vodka: Split the jalapeno in half, remove seeds and place in
a glass container with the vodka for four hours or until desired
spiciness is achieved.  Strain and place vodka in freezer. Use in
bullshots (jalapeno vodka, beef bouillon, Worcestershire sauce, salt
and pepper) or in Bloody Marys.
