Title: Beefy Spanish Rice
Categories: Rice, Meats, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Long-grain rice -- uncooked
1 ts Salt
1 ts Chili powder
1 1/4 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Tomato paste
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1 md Onion -- chopped
1 md Green pepper -- chopped
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1 cn Stewed tomatoes -- 14 1/2
Oz
1 1/2 c  Water
In large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, green pepper and garlic
until the meat is browned; drain. Stir in the next eight ingredients;
bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20 min. or until
the rice is tender. Stir in tomato paste and heat through. Serve with
French bread. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
