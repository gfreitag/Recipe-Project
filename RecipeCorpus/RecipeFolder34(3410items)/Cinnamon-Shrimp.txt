Title: Cinnamon Shrimp
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Herbs/spice, Tomatoes, Wok
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Lime juice
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Sugar
1 tb Chopped cilantro
2 ts Olive oil
1/2 lb Shell,devein shrimp
1 c  Sliced green onions
2 lg Plum tomatoes
1 sm Jalapeno
Seed and cut into thin strips the pepper and tomatoes. In a 9"
nonstick skillet heat oil; add shrimp and green onions and cook over
high heat, stirring frequently, until shrimp just turn pink, about 2
minutes. Add remaining ingredients except cilantro and stir to
combine; cook, stirring constantly, until flavors blend, about 2
minutes. Sprinkle with cilantro.

1 fat, 3 protein,2 1/4 vegetables, 3 opt. cal. 192 cal 7 g.fat
