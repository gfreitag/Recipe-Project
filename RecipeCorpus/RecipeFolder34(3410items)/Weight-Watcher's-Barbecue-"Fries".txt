Title: Weight Watcher's Barbecue "Fries"
Categories: Side dish, Bbq/grill, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 ts Tomato paste
1 1/2 ts Cider vinegar
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 lb Plus 14oz pared Idaho
-potatoes, cut into 1/2
-inch strips
1 tb Vegetable oil
Preheat oven to 450.  Line baking sheet with foil; spray with nonstick
cooking spray.

In medium bowl, combine potatoes, oil, tomato paste, vinegar, chili
powder and salt; toss well to thoroughly coat.  With slotted spoon,
arrange potatoes in a single layer on prepared baking sheet.  Bake
until cooked through, 15-20 mins.

Each 1/2c serving provides 136 calories, 3g fat (0 sat)
