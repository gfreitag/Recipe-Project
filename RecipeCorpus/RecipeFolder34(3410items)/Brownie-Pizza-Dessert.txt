Title: Brownie Pizza Dessert
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 12 servings
4    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Flour
4    Squares baking chocolate
3/4 c  Margerine
2 c  Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
Assorted sliced fruit
2    Squares semi-sweet
Chocolate
8 oz Cream cheese -- softened
1/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1/2 ts Vanilla
Heat oven to 350F.  Line 12x1/2 inch pizza pan with foil. Grease
foil. Melt unsweetened chocolate aand margerine in microwave on high
for 2 minutes. Stir until chocolate is completely melted. Stir 2 c
sugar into chocolate mixture.  Mix in 4 eggs and 1 t vanilla until
well blended. Stir in flour. Spread in prepared pan. Bake 30 min. Mix
cream cheese, 1/4 c sugar. 1 egg and 1/2 t vanilla in bowl until well
blended. Pour over baked brownie crust. Bake 10 minutes longer or
until toothpick inserted into center comes out with fudgy crumbs. DO
NOT OVERBAKE. Cool in pan. Lift brownie pizza out of pan.  peel off
foil. Place brownie pizza on serving plate. Arrange fruit over cream
cheese layer. Drizzle with semi-sweet chocolate.
