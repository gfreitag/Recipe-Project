Title: Buttered Beets and Celery
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 - 8 folks
2 c  Celery; cooked
1/2 ts Salt;
1 1/2 tb Vinegar;
3 tb Onion; minced
3 tb Fat;
2 c  Beets; cooked
Saute onions in fat until tender, add remaining ingredients and heat
thoroughly.  Serves 6-8
