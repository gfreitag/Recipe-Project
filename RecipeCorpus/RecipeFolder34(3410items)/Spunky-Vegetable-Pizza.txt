Title: Spunky Vegetable Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Sliced bell pepper
6 oz Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
3/4 c  Pizza sauce
1 lg Pizza shell
1 c  Broccoli -- chopped
1 c  Carrots -- shredded
Spoon pizza sauce on pizza shell.  Place on cookie sheet. Arrange
vegetables over sauce.  Sprinkle with cheese. Bake in 450F oven 10
minutes. Cut into 8 wedges.
