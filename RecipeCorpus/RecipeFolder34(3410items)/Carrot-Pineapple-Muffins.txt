Title: Carrot Pineapple Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 1 dozen
1/8 ts Cloves
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Oil
1 c  Crushed pineapple
1 1/2 c  Grate carrot
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
Preheat oven to 425.  Grease a 12 cup muffin pan.  In a bowl sift
together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and spices.  In
another bowl beat eggs with sugar until thick and light.  Beat in oil
until well blended. Stir in crushed pineapple and carrots.  Pour
liquid ingredients into dry ingredients and stir only until all
ingredient are moistened. Spoon batter into greased muffin tins. Bake
15 - 20 minutes at 425. Remove from muffin tins and cool on wire
rack. From Empress Food Flair Recipes Pamphlet by Lucerne Foods
