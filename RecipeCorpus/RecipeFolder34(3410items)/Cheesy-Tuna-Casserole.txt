Title: Cheesy Tuna Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Main dish, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Onion -- chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Fresh bread cubes --
-toasted
2 cn Tuna (6.5 oz) -- drained
8 oz Cheddar cheese -- shredded
4 oz Noodles -- cooked & drained
10 oz Green peas
1 c  Celery -- sliced
3/4 c  Miracle Whip+
Heat oven to 350.  Mix all ingredients except bread cubes.  Spoon
mixture into 1 qt casserole; sprinkle with bread cubes.  Bake 35 to
40 minutes.
