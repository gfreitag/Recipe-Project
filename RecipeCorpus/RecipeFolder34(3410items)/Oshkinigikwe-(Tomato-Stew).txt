Title: Oshkinigikwe (Tomato Stew)
Categories: Soup/stew, Native amer, Wampanoag, Vegetables, Tomatoes
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Cucumber, minced
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/3 c  Corn flour
4 lb Miniature Mexican tomatoes,
-- washed & deseeded
12 ea Shallots, chopped
1/3 c  Water
Place tomatoes in a large saucepan, add the shallots & water & simmer
for 30 minutes, stirring gently on occasion.

Add the cucumbers & salt & pepper, simmer for 10 minutes, stirring,
until the tomatoes break up.

Quickly add the corn flour, 1 tablespoon at a time, stirring slowly.
Simmer for 5 minutes & serve.

Bernard Assiniwi, "Indian Recipes"
