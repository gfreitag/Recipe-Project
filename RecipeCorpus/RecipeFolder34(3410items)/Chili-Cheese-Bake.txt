Title: Chili-Cheese Bake
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1 md Chopped onion
2 ts Ground cumin
1 ts Dried oregano
1 ts Creole seasoning
2 c  Sharp cheddar cheese -- 8 oz
Divided
Shredded
3 c  Cooked rice
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1 cn Green chiles -- chopped 4
Oz
2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Red kidney beans -- 16 pz
Rinsed and drained
In a greased 2 qt. saucepan, combine all ingredients except cheese.
Top with 1 1/2 cups of cheese. Bake covered at 350 deg. for 25 min.
Top with remaining cheese, then bake uncovered 10 min. more. Yield: 6
servings.
