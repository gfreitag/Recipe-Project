Title: Steak Sauce
Categories: Condiments, Country wom
Yield: 20 servings
1/2 c  Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
2 tb Mustard -- prepared
2 c  Ketchup
2    Cloves garlic cloves --
Minced
2/3 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Water
Combine all of the ingredients in a 3 qt. saucepan; bring to a boil
over medium heat. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered for 30 min.
Strain if desired. Cover and refrigerate. Yield: about 3 cups.
