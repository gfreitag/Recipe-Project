Title: Pineapple Mandarin Salad^
Categories: Salads, Low cal/fat, Fruit, Healthy
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn (8 oz) unsweetened pineapple
Chunks, drained
1/4 c  Fat free honey Dijon
Dressing
6 c  Torn Boston lettuce
1 cn (11 oz) mandarin orange
Sections in light syrup,
Drained
Divide lettuce evenly among plates. Divide oranges and pineapple among
plates; drizzle with dressing. Makes 4 servings.

Per serving: 71 cal., 1.3 g protein, .2 g (3%) fat, 15.8 g carb., .6 g
fier, 3 mg chol., .5 mg iron, 104 mg sodium, 4 mg calcium.
