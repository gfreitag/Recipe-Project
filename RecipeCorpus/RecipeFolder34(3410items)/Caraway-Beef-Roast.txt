Title: Caraway Beef Roast
Categories: Beef, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
2 ts Caraway seeds
2    Bay
2 tb All-purpose flour
1/4 c  Water
Cooked potatoes and carrots
Optional
Leaves
3 lb Beef rump or chuck roast
3 tb Cooking oil
1 c  Hot water
1 1/2 ts Instant beef bouillon
1/4 c  Ketchup
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Instant minced onion
1 ts Salt
In a Dutch oven, heat oil over medium high. Brown roast on all sides.
Drain. Combine water, bouillon, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, onion,
salt, pepper and caraway. Pour over roast. Add bay leaves. Cover and
bake at 325 deg. for 3 hours or until tender. Remove roast to a warm
serving platter. Combine flour and water. Stir into pan juices. Bring
to a boil, stirring conslantly. Cook until thickened, adding
additional water if necessary. Remove bay leaves. Serve with cooked
potatoes and carrots if desired. Yield: about l2 servings
