Title: Spinach, Red Pepper And Pine Nut Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
10 oz Spinach; trim -- chop
Coarse
1    12" pizza crust
1 c  Mozzarella -- shredded
1/2 c  Mozzarella -- shredded
2 tb Pine nuts -- toasted
2 tb Olive oil
1    Red pepper -- cut thin
Strips
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Garlic -- minced
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat oil. Add red pepper, onion, salt and
pepper; cook until tender, 3 to 5 minutes. Add garlic; cook,
stirring, 1 minute. Add spinach; cook, stirring, until wilted.
Sprinkle crust with 1 c mozzarella. Layer with veggie mixture, 1/2 c
more mozzarella and pine nut. Bake 10 miinutes. Per slice: Cal, 265,
Fat 12 gr.
