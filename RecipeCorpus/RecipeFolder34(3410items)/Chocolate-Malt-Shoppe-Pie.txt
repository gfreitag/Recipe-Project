Title: Chocolate Malt Shoppe Pie
Categories: Pies, Pastry, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Instant chocolate malted
Milk powder
3 tb Marshmallow creme topping
1 c  Whipping cream
Additional whipped cream
Additional malted milk
Balls
1 1/2 c  Chocolate cookie crumbs
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 pt Vanilla ice cream --
Softened
1/2 c  Crushed malted milk balls
2 tb Milk -- divided
Combine crumbs and butter. Press into a 9-in. pie pan. Freeze while
preparing filling. In a mixing bowl, blend the ice cream, crushed
malted milk balls and 1 tablespoon milk. Spoon into crust. Freeze for
1 hour. Meanwhile, blend malted milk powder, marshmallow creme and
remaining milk. Stir in whipping cream; whip until soft peaks form.
Spread over ice crearn layer. Freeze several hours or overnight.
Before serving, garnish with whipped cream and malted milk balls.
Yield: 6-8 servings
