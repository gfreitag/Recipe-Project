Title: Granola Biscuits
Categories: Breads
Yield: 18 servings
1/2 ts Baking Powder
1/2 ts Imitation Vanilla Butter-Nut
-Flavoring
1/4 ts Baking Soda
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Dry Granola Cereal
3    Egg Whites
1    Egg
1 ts Brown Sugar Substitute
1/8 c  Margarine, Reduced-Calorie
2 c  All-Purpose Flour
1 cn Evaporated Skim Milk
Preheat the oven to 375 F. In a large bowl, combine the egg whites,
egg, brown sugar substitute and margarine. Beat with an electric
mixer on high speed for 1 min, or until the mixture turns light
yellow. Add the next 6 ingredients and mix well. Stir in the granola.
Spray a baking sheet with nonstick vegetabke cooking spray. Using a
small ice cream scoop, drop scoops of dough onto the baking sheet
about 1 inch apart. Bake for 15 mins, or until the biscuits are
lightly browned. Makes 18 biscuits.
