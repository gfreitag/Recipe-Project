Title: Carrot Bran Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1 1/4 c  Dark brown sugar
1/4 c  Molasses
3 c  Finely grated carrots
1 c  Raisins -- OR currants
3 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt -- optional
1 tb Cinnamon
2 c  Bran
Sift together flour, soda, baking powder, salt and cinnamon. Add
bran; set aside. Beat eggs; add oil, sugar and molasses. Add carrots,
flour mixture and raisins. Fill 24 greased muffin tins 3/4 full. Bake
at 350 deg for 25 minutes. Yield: 24 large muffins
