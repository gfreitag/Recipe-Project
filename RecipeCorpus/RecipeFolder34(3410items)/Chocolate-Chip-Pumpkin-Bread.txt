Title: Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Bread
Categories: Breads, Country wom
Yield: 15 servings
1/2 c  Water
2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cloves
2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips --
12 oz.
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
3 ea Eggs
2 1/3 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 c  Pumpkin -- canned
In a mixing bowl, cream sugars, butter and eggs. Add flour, pumpkin,
water, baking soda, cinnamon, salt and cloves. Mix thoroughly. Fold
in chocolate chips. Pour into four greased and floured 6x3x2 inch
loaf pans. Bake at 350 deg. for 45 min. or until breads test done.
Yield: 4 mini loaves.
