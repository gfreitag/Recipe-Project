Title: Apricot Glazed Turkey Breast
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 10 servings
Vegetable cooking spray
1/3 c  Reduced calorie apricot
Spread
1 tb Brown mustard
2 ts Reduced sodium soy sauce
1 ea 5lb turkey breast skinned
And boned
4 ea Clove garlic
2 tb Peeled and slice gingerroot
1/2 c  Dry white wine
Cut 10 slits into turkey breast.  Stuff slits with garlic and
gingerroot slices.  Place turkey breast in a shallow dish and pour
over wine over top. Cover and marinate in refridgerator about 8
hours. Transfer turkey breast to rack of roasting pan coated with
cooking spray. Combine apricot spread, mustard, and soy sauce
stirring well. Brush one fourth of mixture over breast.  Cover with
aluminum foil and bake at 325 for 1 1/2 hours. Baste with apricot
mixture. Bake uncovered an additional 30 minutes or until meat
thermometer registers 185.  Baste with apricot mixture every 10
minutes. Transfer turkey to a serving platter let cool slightly
before slicing. Per serving 165 calories From Safeway's Nutrition
