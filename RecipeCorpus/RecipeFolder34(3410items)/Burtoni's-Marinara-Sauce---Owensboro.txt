Title: Burtoni's Marinara Sauce - Owensboro
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn (16 oz) tomato sauce
1 cn (6 oz) tomato paste
1 tb To 2 tb sugar
1 lb Thin spaghetti
Parmesan cheese; freshly
-grated
1 cl Garlic; minced
Oregano
Basil
Thyme
3 tb Olive oil
1 cn (16 oz) stewed tomatoes;
-drained and mashed
Saute garlic and spices in olive oil for 2 to 3 minutes. Add stewed
tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste; stir. Add sugar; stir well.
Simmer 30 minutes. While sauce is simmering, bring 4 quarts of salted
water to boil; add
spahetti and cook until firm.  Drain. Serve spaghetti with sauce
on top; sprinkle with cheese.
