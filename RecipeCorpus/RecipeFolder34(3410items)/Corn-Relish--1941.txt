Title: Corn Relish -1941
Categories: Relishes, Oldies
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Salt
2 lg Green peppers
1 ea Dozen ears corn
2 lg Onions
2 tb Mustard
1 ea Red sweet pepper
1 sm Head cabbage
1/2 ts Turmeric
Cut corn from cob.  Chop peppers, cabbage, and onions.  Mix
thoroughly. Heat 2 cups vinegar to boiling.  Add sugar, salt,
mustard, flour, and turmeric which have been combined with 2 cups
cold vinegar. Heat to boiling.  Stir constantly until slightly
thickened. Add chopped vegetables. Stir until well blended. Cook
slowly 1/2 hour. Florence Taft Eaton, Concord, MA.
