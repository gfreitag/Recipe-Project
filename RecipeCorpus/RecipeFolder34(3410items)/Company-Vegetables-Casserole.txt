Title: Company Vegetables Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Country wom, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
3/4    Oz
Undiluted
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
3/4 c  Saltine crackers -- crumbs
1/4 c  Almonds -- sliced toasted
1 cn Green beans -- cut drainied
15    Oz
Or 2 cups frozen cut green
Beans -- thawed
1 cn Corn, whole kernel, canned
Drained 15 oz
Or 2 c. cooked fresh or
Frozen whole -- kernel corn
1 cn Cream of celery soup -- 10
In a bowl, combine beans, corn, soup, sour cream, cheese and onion.
Pour into an ungreased 2 qt. baking dish. Combine butter, crumbs and
almonds; sprinkle over vegetables. Bake uncovered, at 350 deg. for 35
to 40 min. or until bubbly. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
