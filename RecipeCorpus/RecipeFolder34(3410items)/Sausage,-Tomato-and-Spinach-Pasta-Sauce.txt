Title: Sausage, Tomato and Spinach Pasta Sauce
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
9 oz Pkg frozen creamed spinach,
-not thawed, removed from
-pouch
12 oz Sweet Italian turkey sausage
-removed from casing
14 oz Tomatoes, can Pasta-ready
-chunky
Cook 12 ounces pasta according to package directions.
Cook sausage in a large skillet over medium-high heat, breaking up
clumps with a wooden spoon, 3 to 4 minutes, until no longer pink.
Drain off fat.
Reduce heat to medium-low.  Stir in tomatoes and add frozen creamed
spinach.  Cover and simmer about 6 minutes, stirring often, until
spinach is thawed and sauce is hot.
Before draining pasta, scoop off about 1/4 cup of the pasta cooking
water.  After draining, return pasta to pot along with reserved
cooking water.
Add sauce and toss to mix. Serves 4. Per serving: 547 cal, 29 g
pro, 74 g car, 14 g fat, 74 mg chol, 1,127 mg sodium. Prep: 5 min
Cook: 10 min Cost per serving:  $1.28 Source: Woman's Day 9/1/95
