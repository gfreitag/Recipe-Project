Title: Cabbage, Potato, And Turnip Soup With Roquefort Toasts
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
3 md All-Purpose Potatoes --
-peeled and cubed
4 md Turnips -- peeled and cubed
2 lg Carrots -- peeled and cubed
Salt
Black Pepper -- freshly
-ground
2 tb Unsalted Butter
1 tb Corn Oil
2 lg Onions, Peeled, Quartered,
-thinly Sliced
7 c  (To 8) Beef Bouillon
1 lb Savoy Cabbage -- cored and
-sliced
MMMMM------------------------GARNISH----------------------------------
-Bread
4 oz Roquefort Or Other Mild Blue
-Cheese
3 tb Fresh Parsley -- finely
-minced
6    Thin slices Toasted French
1. Melt the butter with the oil in a large casserole over med-low
heat. Add the onions and cook until soft and lightly browned. Add the
bouillon and vegetables, season with salt and pepper, and simmer,
partially covered, for 45 mins.

2. Correct the seasoning. Serve hot in individual shallow soup bowls,
sprinkled with the parsley and topped with toasted French bread
spread with a thick layer of Roquefort.
