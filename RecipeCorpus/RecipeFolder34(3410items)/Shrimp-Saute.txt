Title: Shrimp Saute
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 md Zucchini, thinly sliced
1    Clove garlic, minced
Juice of 1/2 a lemon
2 tb Shredded coconut
2 tb Olive oil
1 lb Shrimp, peeled and deveined
3    Scallions, chopped
1    Stalk celery sliced
Salt and pepper to taste
1) Heat 2 tb oil in skillet. Add shrimp and cook over high heat for
3- 4 minutes.

2) Add celery and scallions to skillet. Season with salt and pepper;
stir well and cook 2-3 minutes.

3) Add zucchini to shrimp mixture. add a little more oil if mixture
is too dry. stir in garlic; saute for 2-3 minutes.

4) Sprinkle mixture with lemon juice and coconut; mix well. Serve hot
with white rice.
