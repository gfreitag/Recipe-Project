Title: Three Cheese Spread
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Milnot
1 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
2    3 oz. pkg. blue cheese,
Crumbled
1/2 lb Kraft Old English cheese
1/2 lb Velveeta cheese
Melt all ingredients together in a double boiler, stirring until
smooth and creamy.  Pour into 3 or 4 small containers (plastic
margarine tubs work well) and refrigerate.  Keeps well in
refrigerator for one month. Serve with Triscuit or Keebler crackers.
