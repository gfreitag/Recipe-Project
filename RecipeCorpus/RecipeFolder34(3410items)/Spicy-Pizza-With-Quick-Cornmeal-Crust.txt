Title: Spicy Pizza With Quick Cornmeal Crust
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 lb Lean ground beef
1 pk Taco seasoning
3/4 c  Salsa
1    Green pepper -- sliced
1 md Tomato -- sliced
2 c  Mozzarella cheese -- grated
1 1/4 c  Flour
3/4 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 1/2 tb Baking powder
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
3/4 c  Milk
2 1/2 tb Vegetable oil
In medium bowl combine flour, cornmeal, baking powder and cayenne
pepper. Add milk and oil; stir with a fork until dough forms a ball.
Sprinkle greased bottom of a 15x10x1 inch jellyroll pan with 1 T
cornmeal. Press dough into pan.  Bake in 425 F oven 12 to 15 minutes
until lightly browned. In medium skillet, brown groiund beef,
stirring until thoroughly cooked; drain fat. Add taco spices and
salsa; blend well. Spread meat mixture over prepared crust.Layer
green pepper and tomato over top.; sprinkle with cheese.  Bake 15
minutes or until heated through and cheese is melted.
