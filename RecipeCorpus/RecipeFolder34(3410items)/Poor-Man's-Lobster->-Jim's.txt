Title: Poor Man's Lobster > Jim's
Categories: Main dish, Appetizers, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Sugar or honey
Butter or Garlic butter
Lemon wedges
4 lb Ling Cod
3 qt Water
1/2 c  Salt
Cut ling cod fillets diagonally into 3/4" wide strips. Bring water,
salt and sugar to boiling and add cod. Boil one scant minute and
remove pot from the heat. Cover and let stand for 15 minutes or until
the fish strips rise to the top of the pot. Drain and pat dry with
paper towels.

Serve immediately with melted butter and lemon wedges.
