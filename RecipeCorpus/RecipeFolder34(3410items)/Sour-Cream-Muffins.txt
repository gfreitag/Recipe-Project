Title: Sour Cream Muffins
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Sour cream
2 c  Bisquick baking mix
1    Stick of butter, softened
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  In a large bowl, mix all ingredients well
with wooden spoon.  Put into greased muffin tins.  Bake 20 minutes or
until light golden brown. Randy Rigg
