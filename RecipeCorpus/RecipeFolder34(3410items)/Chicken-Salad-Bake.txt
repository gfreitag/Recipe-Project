Title: Chicken Salad Bake
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Salads
Yield: 3 servings
Salt and pepper to taste
6 c  Cooked chicken
2    5 oz. cans sliced water
Chestnuts
2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 c  Sliced celery
1/2 c  Chopped onion
3 tb Butter
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Lemon juice
Saute the celery and onion in the butter.  Pour into a large mixing
bowl. Add rest of ingredients.  Mix well.  Pour into greased 9 x 13
inch casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Randy Rigg
