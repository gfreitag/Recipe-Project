Title: Corn/Chili Casserole
Categories: Meats, Casseroles, Chili
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Corn chips
1/2 c  Cheddar Cheese, shredded
1/4 c  Ripe olives, minced
1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 c  Onion, minced
1 ea Packet chili Seasoning
8 oz Tomato sauce
Brown ground beef and onion, stir in chili mix and tomato sauce.
Grease a one quart casserole. Coarsely crush corn chips. Layer 1/2
beef mixture, 1/2 corn chips, 1/2 cheese, repeat. Top with minced
olives. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 minutes. Note: I usually
microwave this for about 5 min. unless I am using the oven for bread.
