Title: Mustard-Broiled Shrimp
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Dry mustard
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4    Cloves garlic, minced
2 lb Large shrimp, peeled and
Deveined
1/2 c  Corn oil
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
2 tb Lemon juice
1) Place shrimp in bowl. Combine oil, parsley, lemon juice, mustard,
salt, pepper, and garlic in separate bowl; pour over shrimp and
marinade for 30 minutes.

2) Preheat broiler.

3) Place shrimp and marinade in shallow pan. Broil 4-in from heat
source for 5 minutes. Turn shrimp and broil for 5 minutes more.

4) Remove shrimp from oven, and serve at once.
