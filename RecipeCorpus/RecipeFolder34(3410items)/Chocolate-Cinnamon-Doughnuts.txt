Title: Chocolate Cinnamon Doughnuts
Categories: Breads, Country wom
Yield: 24 servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Baking soda
3/4 c  Buttermilk
Oil or shortening for
Deep-fat frying
2 ea Eggs -- beaten
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 ts Vanilla extract
4 c  All-purpose flour
1/3 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
4 ts Baking powder
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE:--------------------------------
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
6 tb Milk
4 c  Confectioner's sugar --
Sifted
1 ts Vanilla extract
In a mixing bowl, beat eggs. Add sugar and beat until mixture is
thick and lemon-colored. Stir in oil and vanilla. In another bowl,
combine flour, cocoa, baking powder, cinnamon, salt and soda. Stir
into egg mixture alternately with buttermilk. Chill. Divide dough in
half and put half in the refrigerator. On a lightly floured board,
roll to 1/2 inch thickness. Cut with a 2 1/2 in. floured doughnut
cutter. Repeat with remaining dough. Deep-fry in fat heated to 375
deg. for 3 min. turning once. Place on a paper towels. Combine glaze
ingredients and dip tops of warm doughnuts. Yield: 2 dozen.
