Title: Wreath Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  Corn flakes
Red cinnamon drops
1    Stick of margarine
30    Marshmallows
1 1/2 ts Green food coloring
Melt margarine in a pan over medium heat.  Add marshmallows, several
at a time, stirring constantly.  Remove pan from heat when all
marshmallows have melted.  Stir in green food coloring.  Pour
marshmallow mixture over corn flakes.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto
waxed paper. Put 2 or more red cinnamon drops on each cookie. Randy
