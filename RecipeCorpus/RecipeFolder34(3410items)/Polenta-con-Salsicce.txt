Title: Polenta con Salsicce
Categories: Italian
Yield: 20 servings
4 c  Marinara sauce
5 lb Spicy sausage link
9 c  Water
1 tb Salt
1 lb Polenta
COMBINE WATER AND SALT IN A SAUCEPOT, OVER A HIGH FLAME BRING TO THE
BOIL AND SLOWLY STIR IN POLENTA HEAT AND STIR FOR 20-30 MINUTES,
UNTIL MIXTURE PULLS AWAY FROM SIDES OF POT POUR OUT ONTO A LIGHTLY
MOISTENED PLATTER SIMMER SAUSAGES IN MARINARA SAUCE, UNTIL DONE
ARRANGE SAUSAGES AROUND POLENTA POUR MARINARA SAUCE OVER ALL SERVE HOT
