Title: Buttered Beets SL
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
4 c  Pared, diced fresh beets
3 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Sugar
Place beets in a large saucepan; add water to cover.  Bring to a
boil; cover and cook 15 minutes or until tender.  Drain, reserving
1/2 cup liquid.
Combine next 2 ingredients in a saucepan; slowly stir in reserved
liquid. Cook over medium heat, stirring until thickened and bubbly.
Remove from heat; stir in vinegar and butter.  Pour over beets.

Dinner on the James River. Suggested Menu: Cold Vegetable Soup,
Roasted Leg of Lamb with Oven-Browned Potatoes, Buttered Beets,
Braised Broccoli in Tomato Cups, Sally Lunn Muffins, Pineapple Pound
Cake, Plum Pudding with Sherried Hard Sauce, Bourbon Cake
