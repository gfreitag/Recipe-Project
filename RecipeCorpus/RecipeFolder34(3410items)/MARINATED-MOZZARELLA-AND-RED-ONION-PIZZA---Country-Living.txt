Title: MARINATED-MOZZARELLA AND RED-ONION PIZZA - Country Living
Categories: Pizza, Country liv
Yield: 1 pizza
Parmesan variation of Basic
-Pizza Dough
1 tb Cornmeal
3    To 4 plum tomatoes, cut
-crosswise into 1/4-inch
-thick slices
1 md Red onion, cut lengthwise
-in half and thinly
-sliced crosswise
2 tb Thinly shredded arugula
1 pk (9-oz) bite-size mozzarella
-(ciliegine), packed in
-water, drained and thinly
-sliced
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Red-wine vinegar
1/2 ts Dried oregano leaves
1/2 ts Dried marjoram leaves
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper
1. In large bowl, combine mozzarella, olive oil, vinegar, oregano,
marjoram, salt, and black and red peppers; cover and refrigerate to
marinate.

2. Meanwhile, prepare Parmesan variation of Basic Pizza Dough. Set
dough aside to rise.

3. Heat oven to 450'F. Lightly dust large baking sheet with cornmeal.
On baking sheet, shape dough into 14-inch round; roll edge to make
border.

4. Just inside dough border, arrange a circle of cheese, placing
slices side by side. Alternating tomato and onion, make a concentric
circle inside cheese. Repeat with another cheese and another
tomato-onion circle; fill middle of pizza with remaining cheese.

5. Bake pizza on lower oven rack 10 to 12 minutes or until crust is
golden brown and cheese melts. Arrange shredded arugula over cheese
between the two circles of tomato on pizza and serve immediately.
