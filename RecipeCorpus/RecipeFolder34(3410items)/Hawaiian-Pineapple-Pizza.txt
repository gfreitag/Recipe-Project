Title: Hawaiian Pineapple Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Pineapple tidbits --
Drained
2 c  Mozarella cheese --
Shredded
1    Long loaf French bread
1 1/2 c  Pizza sauce
4 oz Canadian bacon -- slivered
1 sm Green pepper -- sliced
Preheat broiler.  Cut bread lengthwise.  Spread with pizza sauce. Top
evenly with remaining ingredients. Broil until cheese melts.
