Title: Leafy Greens With Lime & Chilies
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Lime juice
1 tb Red pepper flakes
1 ts Sugar
Salt and pepper
6 tb Virgin olive oil
1 1/2 lb Spinach -- washed and
-chopped
1 ts Garlic -- minced
In your largest saute pan, heat the oil over high heat until very hot
and just beginning to smoke.  Add the spinach and stir furiously
until the spinach has turned bright green and becomes slightly
wilted, about 1 minute.  Add the garlic and cook for 20 seconds more.
Add the lime juice, red pepper flakes, sugar, salt and pepper.  Cook,
still stirring vigorously, for an additional 10 to 15 seconds.
Remove from the heat and serve at once.
