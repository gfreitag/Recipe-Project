Title: Layered Fresh Fruit Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------CLTRUS SAUCE-----------------------------
1    Cinnamon stick
1/2 ts Grated orange peel
1/2 ts Grated lemon peel
2/3 c  Fresh orange juice
1/3 c  Fresh lemon luice
1/3 c  Packed brown sugar
MMMMM------------------------FRULT SALAD-----------------------------
2    Oranges -- peeled and
Sectioned
1    Red grapefruit -- peeled &
Sectioned
1 c  Seedless red grapes
2 c  Cubed fresh pineapple
1 pt Fresh strawberries -- hulled
& sliced
2    Kiwifruit -- peeled and
Sliced
3 md Bananas -- sliced
In a saucepan, bring all sauce ingredients to a. boil; simmer 5
minutes. Cool. Meanwhile, in a large clear glass salad bowl, arrange
fruit in layers in order listed. Remove cinnamon stick from the sauce
and pour sauce over fruit. Cover and refrigerate several hours.
Yield: 10-12 servings
