Title: Church Supper Sloppy Joes
Categories: Ground, Beef, Country wom
Yield: 50 servings
3 ts Garlic -- minced
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 1/2 c  Sharp cheddar cheese --
Shredded 6 oz
Hamburger buns
7 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1 1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
2 tb Mustard
1 1/2 qt Tomato puree -- or sauce
1/2 c  Vinegar
In a large skillet, brown beef, onion and celery. Cook in batches if
necessary. Drain any fat. Add all remaining ingredients except last
two; cook 10 min. Stir in cheese; cook until melted. Serve on buns.
Yield: 50 servings. Editor's Note: Recipe can be made ahead of time
and frozen in batches when serving a crowd.
