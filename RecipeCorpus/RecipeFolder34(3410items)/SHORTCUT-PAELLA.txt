Title: SHORTCUT PAELLA
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry, Pasta/rice, Fish/seafoo, Paella
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Mushrooms; drained; sliced
1    Envelope onion soup mix
1 ts Paprika
2 c  Chicken; cooked
1 1/2 c  Chicken broth
10 oz Shrimp; shelled
2 c  Rice; uncooked
Cut chicken into 1 inch pieces. Combine all ingredients in 3 qt
casserole; bake at 350~F, covered, for 1 1/4 hrs until rice is tender.
