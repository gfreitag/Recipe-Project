Title: Cheese Pudding
Categories: Side dish, Cheese/eggs, Vegetarian, Puddings
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
pn Nutmeg
3 ea Eggs,separated
1 1/2 c  Milk
2 1/2 c  Stale bread, cubed
1 1/2 c  Cheese, coarse grated
1 1/2 tb Butter
Combine milk, bread, cheese, butter and seasonings; cook over low
heat until cheese is melted.  Remove from heat.  Blend in egg yolks
which have been beaten until lemon colored.  Then fold in egg whites
which have been beaten until stiff but not dry.  Pour into 2 quart
buttered baking dish and bake in 350 F. oven 30 to 35 minutes.
