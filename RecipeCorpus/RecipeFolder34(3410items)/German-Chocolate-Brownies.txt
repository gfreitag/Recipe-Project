Title: German Chocolate Brownies
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 16 servings
2 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
1 c  All-purpose flour -- about 1
1/2    Pounds
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 pk Sweet chocolate squares -- 4
oz Broken square
German
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
2 tb Corn syrup
2 tb Milk
2 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1 c  Coconut flakes
In a saucepan, melt butter and chocolate, stirring until smooth. Cool
slightly. Add sugar and vanilla; mix. Beat in the eggs. Mix in flour,
baking powder and salt. Pour into a greased 9 inch square baking pan.
Bake at 350 deg. for 18-20 minutes or until brownies test done. For
topping, combine butter and brown sugar in a bowl. Add coconut,
pecans, corn syrup and milk; mix well. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto warm
brownies; spread evenly. Broil several inches from the heat for 2-4
min. or until top is browned and bubbly. Yield: 16 brownies.
