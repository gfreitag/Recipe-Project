Title: Philly Burger
Categories: Sandwich, Beef, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Cream cheese -- 3 oz.
Softened
1    Jar mushrooms
2 1/2 oz Sliced drained
1 ts Parsley flakes
4 ea Kaizer rolls
1 lb Ground beef
2 tb Worcestershire sauce --
Divided
4 ts Dijon mustard -- divided
1 cn French-fried onions -- 2.8
oz Divided
Combine ground beef, 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce. 3 teaspoons
mustard and half the onions. Form into four patties and broil or
grill to desired doneness. Meanwhile, in small bowl blend cream
cheese, remaining Worcestershire sauce and mustard, mushrooms and
parsley. Spread the cheese mixture on cooked patties; top with
reserved onions. Broil or grill 30 seconds more or until the onions
are golden. Serve on Kaiser rolls. Yleld: 4 burgers
