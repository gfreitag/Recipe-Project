Title: Pork And Cabbage Sandwiches
Categories: Sandwich, Country wom, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar
1/4 c  Sour cream
Hamburger buns or pita
Bread
Onion -- chopped
1 lb Bulk pork sausage
7 c  Shredded cabbage
1    Medlum
1 md Sweet red pepper -- thinly
Sliced
1/2 c  Water
In a large skillet, brown sausage; drain. Add cabbage, onion, red
pepper, water, salt and sugar; mix well. Cover and cook for 20
minutes or until the vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally.
Stir in sour cream and heat through. Serve on buns or in pita bread.
Yield: 8-12 servings
