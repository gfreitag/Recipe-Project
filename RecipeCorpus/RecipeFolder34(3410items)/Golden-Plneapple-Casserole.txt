Title: Golden Plneapple Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
3 ea Eggs -- beaten
4 sl Bread
(crusts removed), buttered
And cubed
2 cn Crushed pineapple -- 20 oz.
Each
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb All-purpose flour
Drain pineapple, reserving 1 cup juice. In a mixing bowi, combine
pineapple and juice with sugar, flour and eggs; mix well. Spoon into
a 2-qt. baking dish: top with bread cubes. Bake at 350 deg.,
uncovered, for 45-50 minutes or until set and  browned. Serve as a
side dish with ham  or poultry. Yield: 8 servings
