Title: CHEESE SPOON BREAD
Categories: Breads, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Fat
2 c  Milk
1/2 lb Process cheese, sliced
3 ea Eggs, beaten
3/4 c  Corn meal
1 ts Salt
ds Pepper
1 tb Sugar
1 c  Water
In sauce pan mix corn meal, salt, pepper, sugar, water, fat, and 1
cup milk.  Cook over medium heat until thick and boiling, stirring
constantly. Remove from heat; add sliced cheese, reserving a few
slices to put on top of mixture before baking.  Stir until cheese
melts.
Add remaining milk and beaten eggs.  Pour into greased 1 1/2 qt
shallow baking dish.  Put reserved slices of cheese on top. Bake at
325 F. until almost set, about 50 minutes.  Serve immediately.
