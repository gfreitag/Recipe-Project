Title: Baked Cheese Gnocchi
Categories: Italian
Yield: 50 servings
3/4 c  Parsley
1/3 c  Scallions
18    Egg
1 tb Freshly ground white pepper
9 c  Swiss cheese
1 c  Olive oil
1 tb Ground cinnamon
3 qt Water
9 c  Milk
2 tb Kosher salt
1 ts Freshly grated nutmeg
6 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 c  Unsalted butter
3 c  Parmesan cheese
3/4 c  Cooked bacon
COMBINE WATER, MILK, SALT, AND NUTMEG IN A SAUCEPOT, OVER A MODERATE
FLAME REDUCE HEAT VERY SLOWLY STIR IN CORNMEAL, AND CONTINUE TO STIR
UNTIL THICKENED REMOVE FROM HEAT STIR IN BUTTER, PARMESAN, BACON,
PARSLEY, SCALLIONS, EGGS, AND BLACK PEPPER-MIX WELL POUR INTO A SHEET
PAN, TO A THICKNESS OF 1/4-INCH CUT INTO 2-INCH ROUNDS WITH A BISCUIT
CUTTER REMOVE ROUNDS TO A BUTTERED SHEET PAN TOP EACH ROUND WITH 1
TABLESPOON OF GRATED SWISS CHEESE DRIZZLE LIGHTLY WITH OLIVE OIL BAKE
@ 350 DEGREES UNTIL CRISP AND GOLDEN GARNISH WITH GROUND CINNAMON
SERVE HOT Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   08-20-95
