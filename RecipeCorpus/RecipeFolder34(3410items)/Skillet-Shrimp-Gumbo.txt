Title: Skillet Shrimp Gumbo
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts White pepper
2 c  Water
1 c  Canned tomatoes
2    Whole bay leaves
6    Drops tabasco sauce
1 1/2 c  Cooked rice
1/3 c  Oil
2 c  Fresh siced okra
1 lb Shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/2 c  Chopped scallions
3    Cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 ts Salt
1) Heat oil in a large frying pan. Add okra and cook over medium heat
for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add shrimp, garlic, scallions,
salt and pepper; simmer for 5 minutes.

2) Add water, tomatoes, and bay leaves to frying pan; cover and
simmer for 20 minutes. Remove bay leaves; stir in tabasco sauce.

3) To serve, place generous scoop of rice in each of 6 bowls. Spoon
gumbo on top of rice.
