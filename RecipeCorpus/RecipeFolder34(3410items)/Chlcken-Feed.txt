Title: Chlcken Feed
Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Cubed leftover cooked
4 c  Gravy -- leftover
1 ea Chicken bouillon cube --
Crushed
1 ds Pepper
3 c  Potatoes -- mashed
1 sm Onion -- sliced
2 c  Fresh mushrooms -- sliced
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
1/2 ts Dried thyme -- divided
1 tb Butter or margarine
4 c  Chicken
In a skillet, saute the onion, mushrooms, garlic and 1/4 teaspoon
thyme in butter. Stir in the chicken, gravy, bouillon and pepper;
spoon into a greased 3-qt. casserole. Combine potatoes and remaining
thyme; spoon over mixture. Bake, uncovered, at 350 deg. for 45
minutes. Yield: 6-8 servings
