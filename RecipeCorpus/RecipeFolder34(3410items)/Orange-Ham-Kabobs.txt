Title: Orange Ham Kabobs
Categories: Pork, Ham, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Cut into 16 pieces
1 lg Sweet red pepper
Cut into 16 pieces
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 tb Tomato paste
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1 1/2 lb Fully cooked ham
Cut into 24 cubes (l-inch
Pieces)
2 md Oranges
Peeled and cut into eighths
1 lg Green pepper
On eight metal skewers, alternately thread three ham cubes, two orange
pieces, two green pepper pieces and two red pepper pieces. Place on a
broiler pan with rack; broil 4-5 in, from the heat for 8 minutes,
turning occasionally. In a small bowl, combine the orange juice,
tomato paste and ginger; mix well. Brush half over kabobs; broil 2-3
minutes. Turn kabobs and brush with the remaining sauce; broil 2
minutes more or until the vegetables are tender. Yield: 8 servings.
Diabetic Exchanges: One serving equals 3 lean meat; also, 150
calories, 1,248 mg sodium, 40 mg cholesterol, 10 gm carbohydrate, 17
gm protein, 5 gm fat
