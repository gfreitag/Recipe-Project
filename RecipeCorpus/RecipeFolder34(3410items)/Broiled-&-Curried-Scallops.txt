Title: Broiled & Curried Scallops
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Syrups
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ts To 3 tsp curry powder
2 lb Fresh scallops
1/4 c  Maple syrup
1/4 c  Prepared mustard
Line broiling pan with foil and arrange scallops on it. Combine syrup,
mustard, lemon juice and curry powder. Brush half of sauce over
scallops and slide pan under broiler for 5 minutes. Turn scallops,
brush with remaining sauce and broil 5 more minutes. Serves 4 to 6.
