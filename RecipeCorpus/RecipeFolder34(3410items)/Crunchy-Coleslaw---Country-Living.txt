Title: Crunchy Coleslaw - Country Living
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Country liv
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Grated onion
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Honey
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground white pepper
3 qt Thinly sliced green cabbage
3/4 c  Coarsely chopped walnuts,
-toasted
1/2 c  Dark seedless raisins
1/2 c  Shredded peeled carrots
1/2 c  Sour cream
1. In large bowl or container, combine cabbage, walnuts, raisins, and
carrots.

2. For the dressing, in medium size bowl, whisk together sour cream,
mayonnaise, onion, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper; pour over
cabbage mixture. Stir until well mixed; cover and refrigerate until
well chilled.

Country Living/October/90  Scanned & fixed by DP & GG
