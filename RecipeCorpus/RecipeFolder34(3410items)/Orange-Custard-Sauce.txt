Title: Orange Custard Sauce
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 2 .5 quarts
1 c  Orange Juice
-fresh squeezed
4 tb Sweet Butter
1 tb Orange Liqueur
4    Egg Yolks
2 oz Sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
2 tb Lemon Peel
Beat egg yolks with sugar until mixture becomes thick and pale
yellow. Add cornstarch and continue beating well. Add the lemon peel.
Gradually mix in the Orange Juice. Transfer the mixture to a 2 quart
saucepan and cook over medium heat until the mixture thickens up.
Remove from heat immediately after thickening. Cool by placing pan in
cold water, beating continually. Beat in the butter and the orange
liqueur. Then when mixed, cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours.
Serve it with Swiss Chocolate Cake or German Chocolate Cake.
