Title: Pickled Mixed Vegetables
Categories: Vegetables, Pickles
Yield: 5 pints
3 tb Salt
1 tb Mustard seed
1 tb Celery seed
1/4 ts Ground tumeric
3/4 c  Honey
2 x  Cauliflower heads, medium
2 x  Green peppers, medium
2 x  Sweet red peppers, medium
1 1/2 lb Onions
2 1/2 c  White vinegar
1 1/2 c  Water
Break cauliflower into small flowerets, cook in unsalted water 5
min, drain. Cut peppers into 1/4 inch strips; quarter onions. Combine
remaining ingredients except honey in a pot; heat to boiling. Add
veggies; simmer 2 min then add honey.
Continue simmering while packing one clean, hot pint jar at a time.
Fill to within 1/2 inch of top; making sure vinegar solution covers
vegetables. Cap each jar at once. Process 5 minutes in a boiling
water bath. Stocking Up-Rodale Press1977 Typos by M.Miklas
