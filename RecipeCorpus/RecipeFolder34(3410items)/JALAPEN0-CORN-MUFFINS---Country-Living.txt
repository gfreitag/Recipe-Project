Title: JALAPEN0 CORN MUFFINS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Muffins
Yield: 24 muffins
No ingredients found
Prepare Cheddar Muffins, substituting 1/4 C masa harina or yellow corn
flour for 1/4 C of the flour and omitting the Cheddar and chives.
Fold in 1 small red jalapeno pepper, stem, ribs, and seeds removed
and discarded, finely chopped, 1 C fresh or frozen yellow
whole-kernel corn, and 1 T chopped cilantro before spooning into
cups. Serve immediately; store any left- over muffins in the
refrigerator and reheat before serving.
