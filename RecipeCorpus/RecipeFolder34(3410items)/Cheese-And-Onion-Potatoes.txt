Title: Cheese And Onion Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
1 ds Pepper
1/3 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
Chopped parsley -- fresh
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
4 md Red potatoes -- sliced 1/4"
-thick, unpeeled
2 tb Dried onions -- minced
In a skillet, melt butter over med.-high heat. Add potatoes, onion and
pepper; toss to coat. Cover and cook, stirring occasionally, until
potatoes are tender about 10 min. Uncover; cook until potatoes are
browned, about 5-7 min. Top with cheese. Cover and remove from the
heat; let stand for several minutes. sprinkle with parsley before
serving. Yield: 4 servings.
