Title: Warren's Oatmeal Cookies~
Categories: Cake/cookie, Healthy
Yield: 36 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Raisins
3/4 c  Oleo or shortening
3/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Cornmeal
Mix oleo and sugar in a bowl. Add egg and beat well. Add rest of
ingredients and mix well. Drop dough by teaspoonsful on greased cookie
sheet. Bake at 350~F about 15 minutes. Makes about 3 dozen.

Per cookie:  Calories 88; fat 4.5 g; cholesterol 6 mg; carbohydrate
11.3 g; fiber 0.2 g; protein 0.9 g; sodium 27 mg; potassium 26 mg;
calcium 12 mg.
