Title: MINIATURE CHIVE MUFFIN AND SMOKED HAM SANDWICHES - Bon Ap
Categories: Bon appetit, Sandwich, Appetizers
Yield: 36 sandwiches
MMMMM------------------------CHIVE MUFFIN-----------------------------
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter, melted
1    Egg
1/2 c  Chopped fresh chives
2 c  All purpose flour
1 tb Baking powder
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Golden brown sugar
3/4 tb Salt
MMMMM-------------------------SANDWICHES------------------------------
Coarsely ground pepper
Fresh chives, cut into 2
-inch pieces
6 tb (3/4 stick) unsalted butter,
-room temperature
1/2 lb Cooked smoked ham, sliced
-1/8 inch thick
For muffins: Preheat oven to 400'F. Lightly butter 36 miniature
muffin cups (3 tins). Sift first 5 ingredients into medium bowl.
Whisk milk, melted butter and egg in small bowl; stir in chives. Make
well in center of flour mixture. Slowly add liquid ingredients and
mix until just blended.

Spoon batter into prepared muffin tins, filling each 3/4 full. Bake
until light brown on top, about 18 minutes. Remove muffins from tins
and cool on rack. (Can be prepared ahead. Seal in plastic bags and
refrigerate 2 days or freeze up to 3 weeks. Bring to room temperature
before continuing.)

For sandwiches: Slice muffins in half crosswise. Spread with butter.
Place 2 to 3 small pieces ham on bottom half of each muffin, draping
excess ham over sides. Grind pepper over ham. Place 1 or 2 chive
pieces on each. Replace muffin tops. Arrange sandwiches on platter.
(Can be prepared 3 hours ahead. Cover tightly with plastic wrap.
Refrigerate.) Serve at room temperature.
