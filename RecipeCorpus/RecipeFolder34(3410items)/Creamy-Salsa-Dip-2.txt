Title: Creamy Salsa Dip 2
Categories: Appetizers, Salsa/sauce, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1    Envelope vegetable soup mix
12 oz Chunky salsa
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Sour cream
Stir all ingredients until well mixed.  Cover; chill 2 hours.  Serve
with tortilla chips.
