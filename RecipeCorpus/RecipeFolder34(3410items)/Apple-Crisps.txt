Title: Apple Crisps
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
3 c  Sliced apples
1/2 c  Flour
1/4 c  Butter
Arrange apples in 8 inch glass baking dish.  Combine remaining
ingredients, mixing until crumbly.  Sprinkle over apples.  Microwave
on high 5 to 6 minutes rotating once during cooking. From 365
Microwave Recipes Calendar 1990
