Title: Blackberry-Blueberry Cobbler
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Pie/tart
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar; divided
3/4 c  Milk
2 c  Blackberries
1 c  Blueberries
2 c  Grape juice or water
1 c  Unbleached flour
1 c  Wheat flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter; softened
In a bowl stir together both flours, baking powder and salt.  Beat
butter and 1 cup sugar with an electric mixer until fluffy.  Add
flour mixture alternately with milk.  Beat until smooth.  Spread
batter evenly over the bottom of a greased 13- x 9- x 2-inch baking
dish or a 3-quart round dish. Sprinkle blackberries and blueberries
over batter, then sprinkle them with the one-half cup sugar.  Pour
grape juice or water over fruit. Bake in a preheated 350-degree oven
for 40 to 45 minutes or until a pick inserted in middle comes out
clean. Serve warm with cream or ice cream. Makes 12 servings.

Each serving has:  418 calories; 16g fat; 31mg cholesterol; 257mg
sodium.
