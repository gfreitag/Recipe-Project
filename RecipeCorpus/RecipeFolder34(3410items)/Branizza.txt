Title: Branizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 18 servings
Seasn
1 cn Pizza Sauce
2 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
1 g  Green pepper -- thin sliced
1 cn Sliced ripe olives
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 1/2 c  All bran cereal
1 1/2 lb Lean ground round
2    Egg whites -- sl beaten
1/4 c  Skim milk
1 c  Onions -- chopped
2 ts Italian seasoning
1 cn Tomato sauce w/Italian
In large bowl combine; All-bran, beef , egg whites, milk, onions and
seasoning.  Let stand 5 minutes or until cereal is softened. Evenly
press meat mixture into an 11x15x2 inch baking pan. Stir together
tomato and pizza sauces; spread over meat mixture.  Sprinkle sauce
with mozzarella cheese, pepper slices, olives, and Parmesan cheese.
Bake at 400F about 30 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and edges
begin to brown. Cut into 18 slices.  Serve hot.
