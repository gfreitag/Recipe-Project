Title: Chili Skillet
Categories: Chili, Beef, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Dried oregano
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Long-grain rice -- uncooked
1 c  Frozen corn -- or canned
1/2 c  Black olives -- sliced
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese --
Or monterey jack
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1 c  Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1/2    Clove garlic -- minced
1 c  Tomato juice
1 cn Red kidney beans -- 8 oz
Undrained
4 ts Chili powder
In a large skillet over medium heat, cook beef, onion, pepper and
garlic until meat is brown and vegetables are tender. Drain fat. Add
tomato juice, kidney beans, chili powder, oregano, salt and rice;
cover and simmer about 25 min. or until rice is tender. Stir in corn
and olives; cover and cook 5 min. more . Sprinkle with cheese, cover
and cook only until cheese melts, about 5 minutes . Yield: 6 servings.
