Title: Ginger Bread #2
Categories: Breads
Yield: 9 servings
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Salt
Whipped cream
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Light molasses
5 tb Butter or margarine, melted
2/3 c  Cold water
Combine egg, sugar, molasses, butter, & water.  Mix well.  In lg
mixing bowl,stir together flour, baking soda, ginger, & salt.  Add
molasses mixture.  Beat until well-mixed.  Pour into a greased, 8"
square baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 min. or until cake
tests done. Serve warm with whipped cream. From: Mark Barnett Date:
