Title: Cheddar Dill Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 12 servings
oz Chopped
1 3/4 c  Milk
3 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
3 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
3 tb Sugar
2 tb Baking powder
2 ts Dill weed
1 ts Salt
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese -- 4
In a bowl, combine the first six ingredients. Combine milk, eggs and
butter; stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Fill greased
or paper-lined muffin cups almost full. Bake at 400 deg. for 25 to 30
minutes or until muffins test done. Cool in pan 10 min. before
removing to a wire rack. Yield: 12 muffins.
