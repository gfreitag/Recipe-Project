Title: JICAMA WITH LIME VINAIGRETTE - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Dried oregano
Salt & freshly ground
-black pepper to taste
1    Jicama, 3/4 to 1 lb, peeled
-and cut into quarters
Juice of 2 large limes
4 tb Light olive oil
Using a food processor fitted with a fine shredding disc, or a hand
grater, finely grate jicama and transfer to a medium serving bowl.
Set aside.

In a cruet or a small bowl combine remaining ingredients for
vinaigrette. Shake or whisk to combine well. Pour over jicama and
toss to coat. Cover bowl and chill in refrigerator until ready to
serve. This salad can be made up to 24 hours in advance. Serves 4 to
6.

House Beautiful/May/94 Scanned by DP & GG
