Title: Spiced Pear Jam
Categories: Fruit, Preserves, Etc., Country wom
Yield: 30 servings
4 c  Sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cloves
8 c  Pear -- about 5 lbs.
Chopped or coarsely ground
Peeled
Combine all ingredients in a large kettle. simmer, uncovered, for 1
1/2 to 2 hours or until thick, stirring occasionally. Stir more
frequently as the mixture thickens. Remove from the heat; skim off
foam. Pour hot into hot jars, leaving 1/4 in. headspace. Adjust caps.
Process for 10 min. in a boiling-water bath. Yield: 6 half pints.
Editor's Note: this recipe does not require pectin.
