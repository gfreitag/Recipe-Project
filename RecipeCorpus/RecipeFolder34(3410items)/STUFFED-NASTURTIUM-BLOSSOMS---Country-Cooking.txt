Title: STUFFED NASTURTIUM BLOSSOMS - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 appetizers
24    Capers
24    Pesticide-free nasturtiums
1/4 c  Cream cheese, at room temp.
1. Place nasturtiurm on a tray or large platter. With butter knife,
press about 1/2 t cream cheese into center of each nasturtium. Pat
capers dry on paper towels. Push a caper into the center of the cream
cheese in each flower.

2. Serve stuffed nasturtiums immediately or refrigerate until ready to
serve, no more than 1 hour.
