Title: SPLINTER'S EASY PIZZAS
Categories: Pizza, Kids
Yield: 1 servings
Mozzerella cheese
Your favorite pizza toppings
1    English muffin; sliced
Tomato sauce
Toast the English muffin.

Spread each half with sauce.

Top with cheese. (and ham, or jam, or peas- or whatever you like!)

Bake in the oven at 400 degrees F. until the cheese melts.

HAVE A GROWNUP HELP YOU WITH THE SLICING AND THE OVEN.

Origin:  Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles Pizza Party

From Autumn's Favorite Recipes.
