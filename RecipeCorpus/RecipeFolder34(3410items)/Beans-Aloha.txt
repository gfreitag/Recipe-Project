Title: Beans Aloha
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn 16 oz baked beans/molasses
1/4 c  Green pepper, chopped
8    Buns, split, toasted
1 c  Ring Bologna-cubed
1 cn 8 oz pineapple tidbits,
Drained
1/2 c  French dressing
Combine first five ingredients in skillet - simmer slowly about 10
minutes until right consistency for sandwich filling.

Spoon mixture on bun.

Serve with chips for a very filling lunch or light dinner.

Typed, but not tested.
