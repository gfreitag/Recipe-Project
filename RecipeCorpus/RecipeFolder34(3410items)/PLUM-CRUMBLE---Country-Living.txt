Title: PLUM CRUMBLE - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Desserts, Fruit, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
-sugar
2 ts All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
1 tb Butter
4 md (1 1/4 lb) plums, pitted
-and sliced
2 tb Honey
1 tb Cornstarch
3 tb Quick rolled oats
3 tb Firmly packed light-brown
1. In medium-size bowl, combine plums, honey, and cornstarch. Divide
plum mixture into four 8-oz microwave-safe custard cups.

2. In same medium-size bowl, combine oats, brown sugar, flour,
cinnamon, and nutmeg. With pastry blender or 2 knives, cut butter
into oat mixture; sprinkle evenly into plum-filled custard cups.
Microwave on high (100 percent) 6 to 7 minutes or until plum juices
bubble; rotate cups midway through cooking. Cool crumble 20 to 30
minutes before serving.
