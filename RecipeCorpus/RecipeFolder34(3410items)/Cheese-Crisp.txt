Title: Cheese Crisp
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 24 servings
1 ts Salt
2 c  Rice crispies
1/4 c  Nuts, finely chopped ;opt
1/2 lb Margarine; at room temp
1/2 lb Sharp cheddar; grated
2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Tabasco
Mix margarine or butter with sharp cheese. Add tabasco sauce, blend
well. Mix salt with flour, mix thoroughly. Add rice crispies and mix
gently but well. Roll into small balls, place on greased cookie
sheet. Crisscross with a fork.

Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
