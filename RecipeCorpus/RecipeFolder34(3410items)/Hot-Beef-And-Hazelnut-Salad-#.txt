Title: Hot Beef And Hazelnut Salad #
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
Strips
1 lb Sirloin steak -- sliced thin
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
Regular
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Water
1/4 c  Green onion -- sliced
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
2 tb Soy sauce, low sodium -- or
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/4 ts Ground ginger
8 c  Lettuce -- or 10 cup torn
1/4 c  Hazelnuts -- coarsely
Chopped
Toasted almonds
1 c  Sweet red peppers -- or
Green chopped
2 tb Cider vinegar
2 tb Soy sauce, low sodium -- or
Regular
2 tb Vegetable oil
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Curry powder
Place beef in a glass mixing bowl. Combine marinade ingredients; pour
over beef. Allow to stand at room temperature 30 min. Meanwhile,
combine dressing ingredients; set aside. Place greens in a large
salad bowl; refrigerate. In a skillet over high heat, half the beef
and marinade. Remove and then brown remaining beef. Drain and add all
beef to greens. In the same skillet, heat dressing. Pour over salad
and quickly toss. top with hazelnuts, onions and peppers. Serve
immediately. Yield: 4 servings.
