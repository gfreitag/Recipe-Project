Title: Shrimp Etouffee A La Sherry
Categories: Main dish, Fish/seafoo, Cajun/creol
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 tb Cornstarch
1 1/2 c  Cold Water
1 1/2 c  Cooking Sherry
1 tb Lea & Perrins Sauce
1 ds Red Hot Sauce
Salt, Black Pepper and
Cayenne Pepper To Taste
4 c  Fresh Peeled Shrimp
2 c  Chopped Onion
2 c  Chopped Celery
1/2 c  Chopped Parsley
3 ea Cloves Garlic, Chopped
1 c  Chopped Green Pepper
1 c  Chili Sause
3/4 c  Cooking Oil
Peel shrimp, season generously with salt, black pepper and cayenne
pepper and set aside. Heat cooking oil; add onions, celery, green
pepper,parsley and garlic. Cook slowly in uncovered heavy pot until
tender and wilted. Add chili sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
Dissolve cornstarch in water and add to mixture. Add seasoned shrimp
and cook over medium heat for about 20 minutes; add sherry and cook
an additional 20 minutes. Serve over steamed rice. Will serve about 8
or 4 Cajuns :-).
