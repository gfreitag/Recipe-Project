Title: Dllled Vegetable Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
-OR-
1 ts Dried dill weed
2 tb Salad oil
1 tb Vinegar
2 ts Sugar
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground white pepper
1 md Cucumber
Peeled and sliced 1/8 inch
Thick
2 md Tomatoes
Sliced 1/4 inch thlck
1 md Sweet onion -- thinly
Sliced
2 tb Fresh snipped dill
Place first three ingredients in a bowl. Combine dill, oil, vinegar,
sugar, salt and pepper; pour over vegetables and toss. Refrigerate
until ready to serve. (Can be prepared the day before, if desired.)
Yleld: 6 servings
