Title: Mennonite Beet Pickle
Categories: Pickles, Can/preserv, Vegetables, Mennonite
Yield: 2 quarts
1/2 c  Grated horseradish
2 c  Vinegar
3 c  Sugar
3 ts Salt
2 qt Diced or sliced beets;
-cooked
3 sm Onions
3 ea Green peppers
Dissolve sugar and salt in hot vinegar
Add horse-radish and bring to a boil.
Add beets and chopped onion and simmer 20 minutes.
Place in 2 sterilized quart jars or keep in refrigerator.
