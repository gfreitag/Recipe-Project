Title: Basic Pizza Sauce
Categories: Pizza, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Basil -- crushed
1/4 ts Tarragon -- crushed
1/4 ts Oregano -- crushed
1 cn Tomato puree
1 cn Whole tomatoes -- drained
1/2 c  Onion -- fine chopped
1    Clove garlic -- minced
Combine all ingredients in large skillet; heat to boiling. Reduce
heat, simmer for 10 minutes.  Cool.
