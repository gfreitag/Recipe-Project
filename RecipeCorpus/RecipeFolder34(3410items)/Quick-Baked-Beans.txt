Title: Quick Baked Beans
Categories: Beans, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 cn Baked beans -- 16 oz
2 ea Hot dogs -- sliced
4    Strip bacon
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1 md Chopped onion
1/2 md Green pepper -- chopped
1/2 lb Ground beef, extra lean
Cook the bacon until crisp; set aside. Saute garlic, onion and green
pepper in bacon dripping until golden. Add beef. Brown well; drain.
Season with salt and pepper, if desired. Stir in beans and hot dogs;
cover and simmer over low heat for 10 min. Crumble bacon over top and
serve. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
