Title: Mozzarella Pasta Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 12 servings
Bacon
1 cn Green chiles -- 4 oz
Drained
2 c  Mozzarella cheese -- cubed
3/4 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1 cn Black olives -- 5 3/4
Sliced
2 c  Pasta -- corkscrew (twist)
Cooked and drained
10 oz Fresh spinach -- washed &
Drained
And torn in bite-size
Pieces
2 c  Cooked ham -- chopped or
MMMMM---------------------PARMESAN DRESSING:--------------------------
White vingar
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1    Clove garlic -- minced or 2
1 ea Egg
1 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1/4 c  White wine vinegar -- or
Toss together pasta and remaining salad ingredients; cover and chill.
To make dressing, place egg in blender; cover and blend 5 seconds.
With blender running, remove top and slowly add oil to egg, blending
until thick. Add remaining ingredients; blend until smooth. Pour
dressing over salad ingredients; toss to coat. Serve immediately.
Chill leftovers. (If salad dressing is made in advance, keep chilled
till used.) Yield 12 servings.
