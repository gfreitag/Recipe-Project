Title: Pasta And Alaska Salad
Categories: Appetizers, Salads
Yield: 1 salad bowl
30    Fresh basil leaves
- finely chopped
1/2 c  Parsley, finely chopped
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice
Salt and pepper
1/2 kg Curly shaped pasta
- cooked and cooled
3/4 kg Alaska bits
- or smoked salmon
3/4 c  Olive oil
Put the pasta and Alaska bits in a bowl, and mix well. Process the
remaining ingredients in a food processor until smooth. Pour the
dressing over the pasta and Alaska bits, mix well, and chill
overnight.

Serve at room temperature.

Source: Complete Meals, by Israel Aharoni.

Modified by Gabi Shahar: the original recipe called for sauteed
squid, but I used Alaska bits (a sort of smoked salmon substitute)
for Kosher reasons.
