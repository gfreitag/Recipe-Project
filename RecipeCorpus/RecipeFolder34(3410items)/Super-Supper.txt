Title: Super Supper
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
2 1/2 c  Condensed tomato soup
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/4 c  Butter
6 sl Bread, toasted
2 1/2 c  American cheese, shredded
3 ea Eggs, slightly beaten.
Spread butter over toasted bread; cut each slice into 6 squares.
Grease a 2 quart baking dish; place alternate layers of toast
squares and cheese, ending with top layer of cheese.
Combine eggs, soup, salt, and mustard and pour over bread and cheese
layers.  Bake in a 325 F. oven for about 1 hour.
May be garnished with finely chopped green pepper and sprigs of
parsley.

Serve hot.

Condensed cream of mushroom soup may be used instead of tomato soup
for a variation of this dish.
