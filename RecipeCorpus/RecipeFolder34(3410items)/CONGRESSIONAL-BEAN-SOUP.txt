Title: CONGRESSIONAL BEAN SOUP
Categories: Soup/stew, Crockpot, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Tb Parsley, chopped
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ea Bay leaf
1 lb Small white beans
8 c  Water
2 c  Ham, diced
1 c  Onion, diced
1 c  Celery, chopped
Assemble ingredients in Slow Cooker.  Cover and cook on low 8-10
hours or until beans are tender. Serves: 6 Posted by: Dorothy Flatman
- Cooking
