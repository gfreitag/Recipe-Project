Title: Red Onion Relish - Country Living
Categories: Relishes, Vegetables, Condiments, Country liv
Yield: 3 cups
2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground white pepper
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 lb Large red onions, sliced
-& separated into rings
1/4 c  Red-wine vinegar
In large skillet, heat oil over medium heat.  Add onions and saute 5
minutes.  Reduce heat to low, cover and cook onions 15 minutes or
until tender, stirring occasionally.

Stir vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper into onions.  Cook 5 minutes
longer or until mixed.  Transfer to serving dish and serve warm or at
room temperature.

Country Living/Jan/93   Typed by Didi Pahl
