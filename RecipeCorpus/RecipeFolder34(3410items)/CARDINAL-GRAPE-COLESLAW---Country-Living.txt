Title: CARDINAL GRAPE COLESLAW - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Salads, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
2/3 c  Low-calorie mayonnaise
1/3 c  Thawed frozen white
-grape-juice concentrate
1 tb White-wine vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
2 qt Thinly sliced green cabbage
1/2 lb Cardinal grapes, halved
-& seeded (or use another
-red grape variety)
1 c  Sliced celery
2 tb Chopped fresh chives
1. In large bowl, lightly toss cabbage grapes, celery, and chives. In
small bowl, with wire whisk, combine mayonnaise, undiluted grape-juice
concentrate, vinegar, salt, and pepper.

2. Stir mayonnaise mixture into cabbage mixture; toss and transfer to
serving bowl or dish.
