Title: LOBSTER ROLLS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Fish/seafoo, Main dish, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Cooked lobster meat (about
-2 1 3/4-lb lobsters)
4    Frankfurter rolls
4    Lemon wedges (opt.)
Coleslaw (opt., separate
-recipe)
Yankee Fries (opt., separate
-recipe)
1/2 c  Finely chopped celery
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Chopped fresh basil or
-parsley leaves
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
1/4 ts Salt
2    To 3 drops hot red-pepper
-sauce (opt.)
1. In medium-size bowl, combine celery, mayonnaise, basil, lemon
juice, black pepper, salt, and red-pepper sauce, if desircd. Fold in
lobster until combined; cover and refrigerate at least 20 minutes.

2. To serve, split rolls and toast until golden. Divide lobster
mixture among rolls and serve with lemon wedges, Coleslaw, and Yankee
Fries, if desired.

Nutrition information per serving without lemon, Coleslaw, and Yankee
Fries-protein: 19 grams; fat: 19 grams; carbohydrate: 31 grams;
fiber: 2 grams; sodium: 838 milligrams; cholesterol: 63 milligrams;
calories: 376.
