Title: Fruit Pizza
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 1 pizza
1 pk Cookie dough
Fresh or canned fruit
8 oz Cream cheese
1/2 c  Confectioners sugar
For the dough, use 1 tube Pillsbury Slice & Bake sugar cookie dough,
or instant Brownies mix, or pastry dough.

Fresh fruits: use blueberries, kiwi, strawberries, peaches, grapes,
etc. If using canned fruits, drain completely before use.

Heat oven to 400 F (200 C).

Slice cookie dough into thin pieces and arrange on a pizza stone to
form a crust.  Bake for 10-15 minutes until golden brown. Loosen from
stone but do not remove until completely cool.

Combine cream cheese with confectioners sugar. Spread over cooled
dough. Arrange fruit over the top.

Refrigerate until ready to serve. Presentation: cut like pizza slices.
