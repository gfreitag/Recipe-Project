Title: Charleston Oyster Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 cups
1/2 ts Pepper
1/8 ts To 1/4 tsp ground mace
2 c  Whipping cream
1/4 c  To 1/3 cup sherry (optional)
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 qt Oysters; undrained
1 c  Water
1 tb All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Salt
Melt butter in a Dutch oven; add undrained oysters.  Cook over
medium heat, stirring constantly, until hot.  Add next 5 ingredients.
Gradually add cream, stirring constantly.  Continue cooking over
medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is heated and oyster
edges curl. Stir in sherry, if desired. Yield: 6-1/2 cups.
