Title: Cottage Cheese Loaf
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Breadcrumbs
1 ea Lemon (juice of)
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 ea Eggs, well beaten
1 lg Bell pepper
3 md Carrots
3 tb Margarine
1 lg Onion, chopped
2 c  Cottage cheese, sieved
Drop green pepper into boiling water for 5 minutes.  Drain, cool
remove ribs and seeds, chop.  Wash and scrape carrots.  Grate
coarsely.
Melt margarine (or butter), add onion, and cook for 3 minutes.  Add
green pepper, carrots, cottage cheese, breadcrumbs, lemon juice, and
seasonings.
Blend very well, add beaten eggs and mix.  Pour into well buttered
loaf pan and bake at 350 F. 35 to 40 minutes.  Serve with tomato
sauce.
