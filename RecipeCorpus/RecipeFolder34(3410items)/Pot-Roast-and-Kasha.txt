Title: Pot Roast and Kasha
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
Garlic powder
1/2 c  Water
12 oz Kasha (buckweat groats)
5 ea Onions, sliced
4 lb Chuck roast
Salt
Pepper
Place all ingredients except kasha in a large pot. Cook on low heat
until tender, about 3 hours. Prepare kasha according to package
instructions. To serve: pour meat, onions and gravy over kasha.
