Title: SHRIMP AND PEACH SALAD - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2 lg Peaches, peeled, halved,
-pitted, and sliced
2 sm Cucumbers, peeled, and
-thinly sliced
1/2 md Sweet red pepper, chopped
Balsamic vinegar or prepared
-salad dressing
1 lb Jumbo shrimp, cooked,
-shelled, deveined, and
-chilled
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 tb Cilantro leaves, finely
-chopped
1/2 sm Head green-leaf lettuce
1. In medium-size bowl, combine shrimp, lemon juice, and cilantro;
cover and refrigerate 30 minutes.

2. Just before serving, divide lettuce among 4 chilled plates. Arrange
marinated shrimp, peaches, cucumbers, and red pepper on lettuce. Pass
balsamic vinegar or prepared salad dressing to serve on salad.
