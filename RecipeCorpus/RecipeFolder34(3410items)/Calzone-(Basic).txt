Title: Calzone (Basic)
Categories: Main dish, Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Mozzarella shredded
1/4 c  Parmesan grated
1 ts Salt
Pizza dough
1 cn Pizza sauce
1/4 lb Itallian sausage
Roll out dough and cut into 6 inch rounds.  Mix all other ingrediants
in a bowl.  Place a scant 1/4 cut of filling on each round. Fold over
and pinch closed with a fork and water. Bake on a greased cookie
sheet, brush tops with oil (lightly). Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or
till golden brown.
