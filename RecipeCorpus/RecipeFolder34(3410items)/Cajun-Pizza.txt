Title: Cajun Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Cajun/creol
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 lb Andouille or garlic
Sausage -- sliced thin
1 c  Frozen cut okra -- thaw
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    12" pizza crust
1 1/2 c  Muenster cheese -- shredded
1 sl Bacon -- diced
1 c  Onions -- chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1 ts Garlic -- minced
1/8 ts Thyme
1/8 ts Oregano
14 1/2 oz Can stewed tomatoes --
Drain
Preheat oven to 450~. Cook bacon until just crisp. Add onions and
green pepper; cook until softened, 6 minutes. Add garlic, thyme and
oregano; cook 30 seconds. Add tomatoes, sausage, okra, salt and
pepper. Spoon on crust. Top with cheese. Bake 10 minutes. Per Slice:
335 Cal, 16 gr Fat.
