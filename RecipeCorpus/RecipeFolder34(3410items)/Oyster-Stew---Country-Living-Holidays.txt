Title: Oyster Stew - Country Living Holidays
Categories: Country liv, Holiday, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
Pepper
1 1/2 pt Heavy cream
6 c  Milk
Paprika
2 pt Fresh oysters
4 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Flnely chopped peeled onion
Worcestershire sauce
Tabasco sauce
1. Drain oysters, reserving liquid.

2. Heat butter or margarine in a large heavy pan over moderate heat.
Add onion and cook until soft. Add oysters and cook until edges begin
to curl.

3. Add a few dashes of Worcestershire, Tabasco and pepper. Then add
cream, milk and oyster liquid.

4. Heat over moderately low heat until stew is hot and bubbles form
around the edges of pot. Do not boil. Taste and add more seasoning if
needed.

5. Serve in heated soup plates. Sprinkle with paprika and, if
desired, add a thin sliver of butter or margarine to the top of each
serving. This is oystery stew; more milk and cream may be added.
