Title: Bacon And Cheese Breakfast Pizza
Categories: Breakfast, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
4    Eggs
12 oz Sour cream
2 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
1    9" siingle-crust pastry
1/2 lb Bacon; cook -- crumble
8 oz Swiss cheese -- shredded
Roll pastry to fit into a 12" pizza pan. Bake at 425~ for 5 minutes.
Sprinkle bacon and cheese evenly over crust. In a bowl, beat eggs,
sour cream and parsley until smooth; pour over pizza. Bake for 20 to
25 minutes or until pizza is puffy and lightly browned. Yield 6
main-dish or 18 appetizer servings.
