Title: Hot-Fudge Sauce - Country Living Holidays
Categories: Country liv, Holiday, Desserts, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Water
1 ts Vanilla
3    1-oz squares unsweetened
-chocolate
3    1-oz squares semisweet
-chocolate
1/2 c  Sugar
1. In a medium-sized heavy saucepan, combine chocolates, sugar, and
corn syrup. Heat over low heat until chocolates melt and sugar
dissolves.

2. Add butter, water, and vanilla, stirring until mixture is well
blended and smooth.

3. Serve immediately or refrigerate and rewarm before serving.
