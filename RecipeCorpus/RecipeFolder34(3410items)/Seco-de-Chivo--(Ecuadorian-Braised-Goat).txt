Title: Seco de Chivo  (Ecuadorian Braised Goat)
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables, Meats, So. america
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 ea Fresh aji chile; seeds and
-stems removed
1 ea Or substitute yellow wax hot
-chile or jalapeno
Water to cover
2 tb Olive oil
4 lb Goat or lamb; cut into
-3/4 inch cubes
1 c  Chicha or raspberry juice
4 ea Tomatoes; peeled and diced
1 ea Large onion; diced
1 cl Garlic; minced
Heat the oil in a heavy casserole and brown the meat.  Add the
remaining ingredients and bring the pot to a boil.  Cover the pot and
reduce the heat to a simmer and cook for 2 to 3 hours until the meat
is tender and the water is consumed.  Serve over hot rice.

Authors' heat scale: Mild
