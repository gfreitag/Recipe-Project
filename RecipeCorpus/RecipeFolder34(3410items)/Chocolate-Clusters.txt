Title: Chocolate Clusters
Categories: Cookies, Country wom
Yield: 110 servings
Salted
3 c  Miniature marshmallows --
Pastel
4 c  Crispy rice cereal
2 lb White chocolate -- or almond
Bark
1 c  Creamy peanut butter -- or
Chunky
2 c  Peanuts, dry-roasted --
Melt white chocolate and peanut butter in microwave or double boiler,
stirring often to mix well. Add all remaining ingredients; stir with
wooden spoon to coat evenly. Drop by teaspoonsful onto waxed paper.
Yield 11 dozen.
