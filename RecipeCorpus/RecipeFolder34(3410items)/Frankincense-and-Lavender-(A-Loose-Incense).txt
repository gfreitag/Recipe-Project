Title: Frankincense and Lavender (A Loose Incense)
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 batch
1 oz Rosemary
1/2 oz Sandalwood
1/4 oz Frankincense
1/4 oz Ground cinnamon
1 oz Lavender
Use with charcoal briquettes.

From Sandy Maine's "Herbal Incense" article in "The Herb Companion."
Dec. 1992/Jan. 1993, Vol. 5, No. 2.  Pg. 40.  Posted by Cathy Harned.
