Title: Mushroom And Bacon Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Rosemary -- crushed
1/2 ts Pepper
1 1/2 c  Fontina cheese;shred --
Divide
1    12" pizza crust
1    Plum tomato -- diced
6 sl Bacon; crisp, drain --
Crumble
2 tb Drippings
1 lb Mushrooms -- slice thin
1/4 c  Shallots -- minced
1/2 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 450~. Add mushrooms, shallots, salt, rosemary and
pepper to drippings in skillet; cook until liquid is evaporated.
Sprinkle 3/4 c cheese over crust; top with mushroom mixture, bacon,
3/4 c cheese and tomato. Bake 10 minutes. Per Slice 305 Cal, 15 Gr
Fat.
