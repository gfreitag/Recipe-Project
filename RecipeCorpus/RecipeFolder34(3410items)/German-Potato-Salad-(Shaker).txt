Title: German Potato Salad (Shaker)
Categories: Salads, Side dish, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 tb Sugar
2 ts Cornstarch
1 ts Salt
7 md New potatoes
3 sl Bacon
1 sm Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Water
Scrub potatoes.  Cook in boiling water 30 minutes or until tender.
Drain, and chill well.  Peel and cut potatoes into thin slices.
Cook bacon in a large skillet until done but still limp. Remove
bacon, reserving drippings in skillet.  Tear bacon into bite-sized
pieces; set aside.
Saute onion in bacon drippings until tender.  Stir in remaining
ingredients; cover over medium heat until slightly thickened.  Add
bacon and pour over potatoes.  Toss gently.
