Title: Italian Breaded Chops
Categories: Italian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Water
2 c  Dry breadcrumbs
1/4 c  Unsalted butter
1/4 c  Sage
4    Pork loin chop
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts White pepper
1    Egg
COMBINE BEATEN EGG AND WATER-MIX WELL PLACE BREADCRUMBS INTO A PAPER
BAG DIP CHOPS INTO EGG MIXTURE PLACE INTO PAPER BAG AND SHAKE TO COAT
EVENLY SHAKE OFF ANY EXCESS PLACE ONTO A SHEET OF WAXED PAPER CHILL
FOR 60 MINUTES TO SET COATING COMBINE CLARIFIED BUTTER AND SAGE IN A
SKILLET, OVER A MODERATE FLAME ADD CHOPS AND BROWN WELL ON BOTH SIDES
PLACE INTO A BAKING DISH BAKE @ 350 DEGREES FOR 30 MINUTES SERVE HOT
