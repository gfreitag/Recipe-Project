Title: QUICK TORTELLINI SOUP
Categories: Soup/stew, Pasta/rice, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
4 ts Cilantro leaves; dried
Coarse black pepper
Parmesan cheese; grated
14 c  Chicken broth; hot
1 lb Tortellini; fresh; mixed
2 c  Spinach leaves; drained &
-chopped
Pour hot chicken broth into a large saucepan, add tortellini; boil
gently for 10 minutes.  Add spinach and cilantro; reduce heat; simmer
for 2 minutes.

Ladle soup into serving bowls; sprinkle each bowl with pepper and
cheese.

Preparation time:  30 minutes.
