Title: FRUIT AMBROSIA SALAD
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 10 servings
500 g  Plain yogurt
10 tb Mayonnaise
Salad greens
10 tb Coconut, long-thread
5    Fresh peaches; peeled
2 1/2 c  Fresh cherries; pitted
2 1/2    Banana; sliced
35 oz Canned pineapple tidbits
--drained
Pit peaches; chop.  Mix with cherries, banana and pineapple.  Mix
yogurt and mayonnaise.  Toss with fruit until coated.  Place on a bed
of salad greens in a shallow salad bowl.  Sprinkle with coconut.
