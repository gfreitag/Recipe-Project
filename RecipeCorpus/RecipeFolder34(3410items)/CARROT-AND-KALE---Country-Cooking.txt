Title: CARROT AND KALE - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
-coarsely chopped (8 C)
1 tb Rice vinegar
1 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Unsalted peanuts, chopped
1 tb Peanut oil
1/2 lb Large carrots, sliced thinly
-crosswise
3/4 lb Kale, stems removed and
1. In 5-quart Dutch oven, heat oil. Add carrots and saute 1 minute.
Add kale, vinegar, and soy sauce. Cover and cook 2 minutes, or until
kale just begins to wilt.

2. Stir in chopped nuts and transfer to serving dish. Serve
immediately.
