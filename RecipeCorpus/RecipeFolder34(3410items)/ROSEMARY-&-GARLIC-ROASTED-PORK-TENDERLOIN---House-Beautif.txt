Title: ROSEMARY & GARLIC ROASTED PORK TENDERLOIN - House Beautif
Categories: House beaut, Main dish, Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 lb Pork tenderloin
MMMMM------------------------FOR MARINADE-----------------------------
-minced
2 tb Fresh rosemary leaves
Salt & freshly ground black
-pepper to taste
1/2 c  Olive Oil
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
Juice of 1 lemon
4    Cloves garlic, peeled and
Combine all ingredients for marinade and marinate pork for 4 hours or
overnight in the refrigerator covered. Remove pork from marinade,
discarding marinade. Roast in a shallow pan in a 400'F oven for 20 to
30 minutes, until meat thermometer inserted reads 155'F. Remove from
pan and place on a serving platter. Slice thinly. Serves 4.
