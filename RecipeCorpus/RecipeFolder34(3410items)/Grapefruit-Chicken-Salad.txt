Title: Grapefruit Chicken Salad
Categories: Main dish, Salads, Poultry, Fruit
Yield: 3 to 4 serve
1 ea Minced scallion
1/4 c  Reduced-calorie mayonnaise
1/4 c  Nonfat or low-fat plain
-yogurt
1/4 c  Minced fresh parsley
1 1/2 c  Sunfresh Brand grapefruit
-segments; cut into bite-
-sized pieces
2 c  Diced cooked chicken
1/4 c  Chopped celery
Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly.  Serve on a bed of salad
greens.  (Serves 3 to 4.)

Source:  Sunfresh Brand grapefuit segments advertisement in coupon
circular.
