Title: Tomatsmor (Tomato Butter)
Categories: West europe, Salsa/sauce, Condiments, Tomatoes
Yield: 1 cup
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar, granulated
16 tb Butter, unsalted
4 tb Tomato paste
Cream butter in electric mixer. When light and fluffy, beat in
tomato paste, salt and sugar. Transfer to serving bowl, chill until
ready to serve. Serve cold, with hot grilled or fried fish.
