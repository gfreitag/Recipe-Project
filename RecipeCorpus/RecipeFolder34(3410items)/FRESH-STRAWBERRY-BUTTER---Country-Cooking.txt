Title: FRESH STRAWBERRY BUTTER - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Dips/spread, Fruit
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
1 c  (2 sticks) butter, softened
2 tb Confectioners' sugar
1 c  Sliced fresh strawberries,
-pureed
1. In food processor fitted with plastic blade, or in medium-size
bowl with electric mixer, beat strawberry puree into butter until
fairly smooth (mixture should have a few little pieces of fruit).

2. Beat in sugar; pack strawberry butter into serving bowl or mold.
Refrigerate until firm.
