Title: Ella McGinty's Dirty Rice
Categories: Newspaper, Main dish, Meats
Yield: 50 servings
5 pk Lipton Cajun Rice & Sauce
.  mix; (4.5 oz)
2 cn Ro-tel tomatoes; 10 oz
5 lb Ground beef
5 c  Water
2 tb Vegetable oil
Brown the beef lightly and drain well.  Transfer to a large stock
pot. Add the water and oil and bring to a boil.  Add the rice mix and
tomatoes, stirring well.  Let mixture come to a boil, then cover and
reduce the heat. Simmer for about 25 minutes or until the rice is
done.

Nutritional Information per serving:  199 calories, 8g fat, 16g
carbohydrates, 37mg cholesterol, 429mg sodium
