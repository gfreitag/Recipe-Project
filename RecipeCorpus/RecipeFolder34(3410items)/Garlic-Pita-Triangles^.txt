Title: Garlic Pita Triangles^
Categories: Breads, Low cal/fat, Healthy
Yield: 8 servings
2    (6") pita rounds
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Paprika
Cooking Light 9-95
Carolyn Shaw 8-95
1 tb Reduced calorie margarine
1 cl Garlic, minced
Combine margarine and garlic in a small microwave safe bowl. Microwave
until margarine mlts, about 30 seconds. Split pita rounds and brush
margarine mixture evenly over halves. Cut each half into 6 wedges and
sprinkle with cheese and paprika. Arrange in a single layer on a
baking pan and broil 1 minute or until golden. Serving size is 3
triangles.

Per serving: 40 cal., 1.2 g protein, 1.5 g (34%) fat, 5.6 g carb., .2
g fiber, 1 mg chol., .3 mg iron, 79 mg sodium, 17 mg calcium.
