Title: Lemon Cream Dessert
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 9 servings
6 oz Lemonade, frozen
-concentrate -- slightly
-thawed
2 c  Vanilla wafer crumbs
1 c  Miracle Whip+
4 1/2 c  Cool Whip+
Heat oven to 350.  Mix crumbs and 1/2 cup dressing; press onto bottom
of 8" square baking pan.  Bake 10 minutes.  Cool.  Mix remaining 1/2
cup dressing, whipped topping and concentrate; spoon over crust.
Freeze 4 hours or until firm.
