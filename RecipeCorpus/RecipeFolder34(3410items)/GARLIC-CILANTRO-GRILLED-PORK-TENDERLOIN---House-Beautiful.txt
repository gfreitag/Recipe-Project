Title: GARLIC-CILANTRO GRILLED PORK TENDERLOIN - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Main dish, Meats, Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
-pieces & flattened slightly
1 1/2 lb Pork tenderloin, cut
-crosswise into 1-inch
MMMMM------------------------FOR MARINADE-----------------------------
1/2 c  Fresh cilantro, chopped
3 tb Freshly squeezed lime juice
Freshly ground black pepper
4    Cloves garlic, peeled
-and minced
1/4 c  Olive oil
Combine all ingredients for marinade and marinate pork covered for 4
hours or overnight in the refrigerator. Remove pork from marinade,
discarding the marinade. In a heavy skillet over medium-high heat,
pan-broil the pork 2 to 3 minutes per side. Serve immediately.
