Title: Oriental Beef And Cauliflower Stew  #
Categories: Beef, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Low sodium soy sauce -- or
Regular
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1 1/2 ts Fresh ginger root -- grated
Optional
3 tb Cornstarch
1/2 ts Sugar
1/4 c  Water
1 c  Green onion -- sliced
Cooked rice
2 tb Cooking oil
1 1/2 lb Round steak -- cut into 1 in
Cubes
Lean
3 c  Beef broth
1    Head cauliflower --
Separated into
Florets
1 lg Green pepper -- cut into
Chunks
In a skillet, heat oil over medium-high. brown meat on all sides. Add
broth; cover and simmer until beef is tender, about 1 hr. Add
cauliflower, green pepper, soy sauce, garlic, and gingerroot if
desired. Cover and simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 5 to
7 min. Combine cornstarch, sugar and water. Stir into meat mixture.
Bring to a boil, stirring constantly; cook 2 min. or until thickened.
Stir in green onion. Serve on rice.
