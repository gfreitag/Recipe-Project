Title: AMBROSIA - Country Living
Categories: Desserts, Side dish, Country liv, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
-cherries
2 tb Freshly shredded or
Packaged shredded coconut
10    Navel oranges
1    Fresh pineapple
1 c  Fresh or canned Bing
1. With sharp knife, cut peel and all membranes off oranges. Cut
oranges crosswise into 1/4-inch thick slices.

2. Peel and core pineapple; cut into 1/4- inch chunks. In large bowl,
toss together orange slices, pineapple chunks, and the cherries (if
using fresh cherries, you may leave stems on). Sprinkle top with
shredded coconut and serve immediately or cover and refrigerate until
ready to serve.
