Title: Cranberry Short Ribs
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Orange marmalade
1/2 c  Bottled chili sauce
6 lb Short ribs
1 c  Whole cranberry sauce
1/3 c  Vinegar
Trim excess fat from ribs and place them in large kettle with
sufficient water to cover.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and
simmer for 2 hours or until ribs are tender when pierced with a fork.
Drain. Combine marmalade, cranberry sauce, chili sauce and vinegar in
a bowl. Preheat gas barbecue on high for 10 to 15 minutes. Reduce
heat to low. Grill short ribs for 20 to 25 minutes brushing with
glaze and turning frequently. Ribs should be richly glazed. Serve
with remaining glaze Submitted By GRANT AMES   On   08-22-95
