Title: Gasali's Marinated Tuna Fish or Swordfish
Categories: Italian, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 1 servings
70 g  Fresh oregano
100 ml Olive oil
Salt and pepper
1 1/2 kg Fresh tuna sliced
100 ml Lemon juice
50 ml Vinegar
"Carpaccio Di Tonno O Pescespada Marinato"

Spread in a plate, slices of tuna. Marinate with lemon, vinegar salt
and pepper for 1 hour. Take out the tuna and put on plate. Put
oregano and olive oil on the top. Serve.

Source:  Given to share with the express permission of Chef de Cuisine
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.
