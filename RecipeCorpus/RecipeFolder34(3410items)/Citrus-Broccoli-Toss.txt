Title: Citrus Broccoli Toss
Categories: Vegetables, Dishes, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 ts Lemon peel -- grated
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 pk Frozen broccoli -- cut,
Thawed 10 oz
1 1/2 ts Orange peel -- grated
In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Saute broccoli until
crisp-tender. Sprinkle with orange and lemon peel, salt and pepper.
then toss to coat. heat through. Yield: 4 servings.
