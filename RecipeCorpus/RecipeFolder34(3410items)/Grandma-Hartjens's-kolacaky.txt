Title: Grandma Hartjens's kolacaky
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Lemon extract
1/2 ts Salt
4 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Butter or oleo
1/4 c  Sugar
1 pk Yeast
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Warm water
2    Eggs
2    Egg yolks
1 c  Cream or canned milk
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
1/2 ts Vanilla
2    Egg whites, beaten stiff
1 1/2 c  Powder sugar
disolve yeast, sugar in water. add eggs, butter, milk, lemon extract
and 2 cups flour, let rise 1 hour. add rest of flour, knead, roll out
1/2 inch thick. cut with biscuit cutter, let rise 1 hour in greased
pan. make a dent in each with back of spoon. add filling. bake 12
min. in 375 degrees oven and let cool. frost around filling not on
it. presonal note: in this recipe grandma lists 1/4 cup sugar with
nothing to do with it. Submitted By KATHY LOFTUS   On   09-12-95
