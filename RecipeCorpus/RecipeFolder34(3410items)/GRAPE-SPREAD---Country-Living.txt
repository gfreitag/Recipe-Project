Title: GRAPE SPREAD - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 cup
1/3 c  Quartered blue-black grapes,
-seeded if necessary, and
-patted dry on paper towels
1/2 pk (8-oz) Neufchatel or light
-cream cheese, softened
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) unsalted butter
-or margarine
1. In small bowl, with electric mixer at medium speed, beat cheese and
butter until smooth and fluffy.

2. With rubber spatula, fold in grapes until well combined. Spoon into
serving dish or bowl.
