Title: Pasta with Mascarpone
Categories: Italian, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
4 tb White wine
Salt and pepper
Olive oil
Basil leaves
Grated parmesan
1 lb Pasta
1/2 lb Mascarpone cheese
1 c  Milk
4    Garlic cloves, minced
2 tb Tomato paste
In a blender, using low speed, mix the milk and mascarpone. Gradually
add the tomato paste, white wine, salt and pepper.

Fry the garlic in olive oil until golden.  Add the milk and cheese
mixture to the pan, bring to a slow boil.

Prepare the pasta as needed.  Pour the sauce over the pasta.

Sprinkle with olive oil, garnish with basil strips and grated
parmesan, and serve immediately.

Origin: Israeli Mascarpone "Gad" package.  Recipe by Phyllis Glazer.
Translated by Gabi Shahar, December 1994.
