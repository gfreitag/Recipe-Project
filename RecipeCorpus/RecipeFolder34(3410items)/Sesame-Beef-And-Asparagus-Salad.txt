Title: Sesame Beef And Asparagus Salad
Categories: Beef, Country wom, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Rice wine vinegar
1/2 ts Ginger root -- grated
1 ds Sesame seeds
Lettuce leaves -- optional
1 lb Top round steak
4 c  Asparagus -- fresh sliced 2
In
3 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Sesame oil
Broil steak to desired doneness. Cool and cut into thin diagonal
strips. Cook asparagus in a small amount of water 30 to 60 seconds.
Drain and cool. Combine beef and asparagus. Blend all remining
ingredients except the sesame seeds and lettuce; pour over beef and
asparagus. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and toss lightly. Serve warm or
at room temperature-on lettuce leaves if desired. Yield: 6 servings.
