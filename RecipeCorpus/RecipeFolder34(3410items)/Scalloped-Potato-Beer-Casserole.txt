Title: Scalloped Potato Beer Casserole
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes, Casseroles
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb All purpose flour
2 ts Sugar
4 tb Margarine
1 lb Swiss cheese,grated
4 lg Russett potato w/skin
1 c  Onion sliced
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Garlic salt
2 ts Paprika
Peel potatoes and slice into 1/8 inch slices. Layer a buttered
casserole dish with 1/4 of potatoes spread evenly in dish. Sprinkle
potatoes with 1/4 of onions. Combine in a small bowl salt, garlic
salt, sugar, paprika, and flour. Blend well. Sprinkle 2 1/2 teaspoons
of this mixture evenly over first layer. Dot with 1 tbs butter cut
into pieces. Continue the procedure for 3 more layers. Pour beer over
casserole and top with grated cheese. Bake at 350 oven for 1 hour.

Source: The Vegetarian Times Magazine
