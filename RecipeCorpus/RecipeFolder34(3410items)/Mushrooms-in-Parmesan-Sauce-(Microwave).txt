Title: Mushrooms in Parmesan Sauce (Microwave)
Categories: Side dish, Microwave, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 ts Cornstarch
3 tb Madiera
1 ts Fresh lemon juice
1 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
2 tb Sour cream
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
2 oz Smoked streaky bacon; cut
-into strips
2 lg Shallots; chopped
1 1/4 lb Mushrooms; sliced
1/2 ts Garlic powder
2 tb Olive oil
Salt and pepper
Put the bacon and shallots in a casserole dish.  Microwave,
uncovered, on High for 3 minutes.
Add the mushrooms to the casserole dish, with the garlic powder,
olive oil, and salt and pepper to taste.  Stir well.  Cover and
microwave on High for 10 minutes.
After 10 minutes of cooking, mix the cornstarch to a smooth paste
in a bowl with the Madiera and add to the mushrooms.  Microwave
uncovered on High for 10 minutes.
Add the lemon juice, grated Parmesan, sour cream, and chopped
parsley to the casserole.  Stir, leave to stand for 2 minutes, and
serve.
