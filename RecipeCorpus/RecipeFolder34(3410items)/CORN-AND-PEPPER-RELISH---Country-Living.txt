Title: CORN AND PEPPER RELISH - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Relishes
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Celery seeds
1/2 ts Mustard seeds
1/4 ts Salt
Lettuce leaves (opt.)
2    Ears fresh yellow corn,
-shucked and silk removed
1 md Sweet red pepper, chopped
1 md Sweet green pepper, chopped
1/4    2-lb head green cabbage,
-chopped into 1/4-inch
-pieces (3 C)
1. With knife, cut corn kernels from cobs. In medium-size bowl,
combine corn kernels, red and green peppers, and cabbage.

2. In 1-quart saucepan, heat vinegar, sugar, turmeric, celery and
mustard seeds, and salt to boiling. Boil 2 minutes.

3. Immediately pour boiling vinegar mixture over corn mixture and
stir to combine. Cool relish mixture to room temperature; refrigerate
until ready to serve. If desired, remove relish with slotted spoon
and serve on lettuce.

Nutrition information per serving without lettuce protein: 2 grams;
fat: .6 gram; carbohydrate: 22 grams; fiber: 3 grams; sodium: 78
milligrams; cholesterol; 0; calories: 93.
