Title: GINGER GRILLED PORK TENDERLOIN - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Meats, Main dish, Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 lb Pork tenderloin
MMMMM------------------------FOR MARINADE-----------------------------
2 tb Sesame oil
2    Cloves garlic, peeled &
-minced
2 c  Dry white wine, or chicken
-broth
4 tb Grated fresh ginger
2 tb Soy sauce
Combine all ingredients for marinade and marinate pork for 2 hours or
overnight covered in the refrigerator. Remove tenderloin from
marinade, discarding marinade. Grill tenderloin over medium-hot
coals, turning occasionally, for 15 to 20 minutes, until thermometer
inserted reads 155'F. Slice to serve.

House Beautiful/June/94  Scanned & fixed by Di & Gary
