Title: OLIVE PESTO - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 cup
2 tb Walnuts or pistachios
2    Garlic cloves, peeled and
-crushed
3 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
1/4 c  Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese, preferably Reggiano
3/4 c  Black or green Nicoise
-olives, pitted
1/2 c  Packed fresh flat-leaf
-parsley sprigs
1/4 c  Packed fresh basil leaves
2    Shallots or green onions
-(white part only), chopped
In a blender or food processor, place the olives, parsley, basil,
shallots, nuts, and garlic. Process until finely minced. Add oil and
cheese and process until blended. Transfer to a small bowl, cover and
refrigerate. Makes about 1 C.
