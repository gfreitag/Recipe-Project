Title: Savoury Tomato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Tomatoes
Yield: 8 cups
2 tb Chablis or other dry white
-wine
5 lg Tomatoes; peeled, seeded,
-and chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Chopped green onion
-(optional)
1 c  Minced leek
2 tb Butter or margarine
4 ea 10-1/2 ounce cans beef broth
-(undiluted)
1 1/2 c  Tomato juice
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
2 1/2 tb Red wine vinegar
Saute leek in butter until tender and transparent.  Add broth,
tomato juice, and sugar, stirring well.  Bring mixture to a boil,
reduce heat, and simmer 10 minutues.
Combine cornstarch, vinegar, and wine, mixing well; stir into tomato
juice mixture.  Cook, stirring constantly, until slightly thickened.
Stir in chopped tomato, salt, and pepper.  Serve hot; garnish with
chopped green onion, if desired.
