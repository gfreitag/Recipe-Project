Title: Saucy Mocha Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE:--------------------------------
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Hot strong coffee
1/4 c  Baking cocoa
1/2 c  Sugar
MMMMM---------------------------CAKE:--------------------------------
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Milk
Ice cream or whipped cream,
Optional
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
2/3 c  Sugar
ea Egg
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  All-purpose flour
In a saucepan, combine all sauce ingredients and keep warm.
Meanwhile, in a large mixing bowl, beat butter, sugar, egg and
vanilla until light and fluffy. Combine flour, baking powder and
salt; add alternately with milk to the egg mixture. Spread into a
greased 8-in. x 8-in. baking pan. Pour sauce over the batter. Do not
stir. Bake at 350 deg. for 45-50 minutes or until the cake tests
done. When finished, the cake will float in the hot mocha sauce.
Serve warm with ice cream or whipped cream, if desired. Yield: 9
servings.
