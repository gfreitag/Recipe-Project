Title: Double Peanut Butter Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 servings
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
4 c  Nut & Honey Crunch --
-crushed to 2 cups
1 c  Peanut butter chips
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
-softened
1/2 c  Peanut butter
Stir together flour, salt and baking powder; set aside.  In large
mixing bowl, beat together butter, peanut butter and sugar until
combined.  Add eggs and vanilla; beat well.  Stir in dry ingredients,
cereal and peanut butter chips.  Drop by slightly rounded tbsp onto
ungreased baking sheet, about 2 inches apart.  With fork, flatten
cookies to 2 1/4" in diameter. Bake at 350 about 12 minutes.  Cool on
wire rack before storing in airtight container.  130 calories per
cookie.
