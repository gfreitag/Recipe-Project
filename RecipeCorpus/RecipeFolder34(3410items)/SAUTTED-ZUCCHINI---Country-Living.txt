Title: SAUTTED ZUCCHINI - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
-(use lemon, orange, or
-nutmeg thyme, if available)
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground white pepper
3 sm (about 1 lb) zucchini
1 tb Olive oil
1 ts Butter or margarine
1 lg Clove garlic, finely chopped
1 tb Fresh thyme leaves
1. Trim ends off the zucchini. Cut zucchini crosswise into 1/4-inch
slices.

2. In large skillet, heat oil and butter over medium-high heat. Add
zucchin1 and saute until lightly browned-about 3 minutes. Add garlic,
thyme, salt, and pepper. Cook, stirring, until the zucchini is well
mixed. Transfer to serving dish.

Nutritional information per serving-protein: gram; fat: 5 grams;
carbohydrate: 4 grams; fiber: 1 gram; sodium: 144 milligrams;
cholesterol: 3 milligrams; calories: 56.
