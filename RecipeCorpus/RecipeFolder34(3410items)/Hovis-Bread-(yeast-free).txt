Title: Hovis Bread (yeast free)
Categories: Breads, Healthy, Diabetic
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Cold pressed oil (???)
2 1/3 c  Water
4 c  Whole wheat flour
1 1/2 c  White flour
1 c  Wheat germ or bran
4 ts Baking powder
Mix dry ingredients together.  Add oil and water and mix.  Use 1/4
cup more water if dough to stiff.  Bake at 350F for 60-75 minutes.
Makes 2 loaves. use liquid lecithin to grease the pans Origin:
Pamphlet on yeast free diet, Guidelines and Recipes Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Jan/94
