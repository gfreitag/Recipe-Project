Title: Snickers Style Dessert Low Fat
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Chunky peanut butter
1 pk Weight watchers instant
Chocolate pudding
1 1/2 c  Weight Watchers chocolate
Ice Cream
1 c  Frozen cool whip
Combine icecream and coolwhip until softened.  Add peanut butter and
dry pudding mix.  Stir until smooth.  Freeze until firm.  Each
serving is 1/2 protein 1/2 fat 1/2 milk and 25 optional calories.
From a weight watcher meeting I attended in 1989.
