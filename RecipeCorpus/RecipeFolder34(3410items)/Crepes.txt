Title: Crepes
Categories: Pcake/waffl
Yield: 12 servings
3    Eggs
2 tb Oil
1 c  Milk
3/4 c  Pancake mix
Combine all ingredients, mixing until smooth.  Let stand 5 minutes.
Heat 6 to 7" crepe pan over medium high heat; grease lightly.  For
each crepe pour about 2 tbsp batter (1/2 of a 1/4 cup measure) into
hot, prepared pan; immediately tilt pan to coat bottom evenly with
thin layer of batter. Cook 45 seconds or until top looks dry.  Turn;
continue cooking about 20 seconds.  Stack crepes between sheets of
wax paper.  May be frozen up to 3 months.  Thaw in refrigerator.
