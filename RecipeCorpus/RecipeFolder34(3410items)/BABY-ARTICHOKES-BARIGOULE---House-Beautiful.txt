Title: BABY ARTICHOKES BARIGOULE - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 12 appetizers
1 c  Basil leaves, chopped
12    Baby artichokes, trimmed
3/4 c  Dry white wine
Coarse salt & freshly ground
-black pepper to taste
1/2 c  Extra-virgin olive oil
1 md Onion, diced
2    Cloves garlic, crushed
1    Slice pancetta
1 sm Carrot, peeled and diced
In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil and saute onion, garlic,
pancetta, carrot and basil until the onions are soft. Do not brown.
Add artichokes and cook until tender, about 5 to 10 minutes.

Place artichokes in a shallow dish and set aside.

Pour wine into skillet and deglaze pan. Sea- son sauce and pour over
the artichokes. Serves 12 as an appetizer.

House Beautiful/September/93  Scanned & fixed by DP & GG
