Title: Beef Onion Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Tomato sauce, small can
1 tb Flour
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 pk Dry onion soup mix
Brown beef and drain.  Add ingredients and simmer for 5 to 10
minutes. Add to 1 cup of cooked noodles or macaroni.  Top with grated
cheese. Bake for 30 minutes in a 350 degree oven. Randy Rigg
