Title: Poppy Taco Twisters
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Cheese curls
1 c  Pretzel twists (small)
1/4 c  Pumpkin seeds -- shelled
3 tb Oil
1 ts Poppy seeds
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Garlic salt
4 c  Crispix cereal
Stir together poppy seeds, chili powder, cumin and garlic salt.  Set
aside. In a 2 gallon storage bag, combine Kelloggs Crispix cereal,
cheese curls, pretzels and pumpkin seeds.  Pour oil over cereal
mixture.  Close bag and gently toss cereal mixture until well coated.
Add poppy seeds and spices; close bag.  Gently toss cereal mixture
until well coated.  Store in airtight container.  Per 1/2 cup
serving: 100 calories and 4 grams fat.
