Title: Citrus Slaw
Categories: Salads, Low cal/fat
1 c  Light or Cholesterol Free
Reduced Calorie Mayonnaise
Yield: 5 cups
:
1/2 c  Frozen Orange Juice
Concentrate Thawed
:
2 ts Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
6 c  Shredded Red or Green
-Cabbage
:
2 cs Shredded Carrots
1    Green Pepper thinly sliced
1/3 c  Golden Raisins (optional)

Combine mayonnaise, orange juice concentrate, sugar, salt and pepper.
Stir in remaining ingredients. Cover, chill. Makes about 5 cups.
