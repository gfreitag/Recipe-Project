Title: Thai Vegetable Salad w/ Mint
Categories: Newspaper, Asian, Salads
Yield: 2 servings
.  to taste (opt)
1 1/2 c  Lettuce leaves; torn
1/2 c  Carrots; shredded
1/2 lg Tomato; chopped
1/2 c  Dalkon radish; shredded
1    Green onion; thinly sliced
Cilantro for garnish
2 tb Lime juice
2 tb Olive oil
1 1/2 tb Fresh mint leaves; minced
1/2 tb Basil leaves; minced
1/2 ts Lemon peel; grated
pn Cayenne pepper
Nam Pla (Thai fish sauce)
Combine the lime juice, oil, 1/2 tsp mint, basil, lemon peel and
cayenne in a salad bowl, mixing well, then add the nam pla to taste.
Toss with the remaining ingredients.

Nutritional Information per serving:  105 calories, 9g fat, 1g
protein, 6g carbohydrates, 0mg cholesterol, 23mg sodium
