Title: Qulck Rlce Puddlng
Categories: Rice, Country wom, Puddings
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Raisins
Whipped topping
Maraschino cherries
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding mix
3 3/8    Ounces
2 c  Cold milk
1 c  Rice -- cold cooked
In a mixing bowl, combine pudding mix and milk; beat for 1-2 minutes
until well blended. Stir in rice and raisins. Spoon pudding into
individual bowls. Chill. Garnish with whipped topping and a cherry.
Yield: 6 servings
