Title: Chlcken Salad On Buns
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Salad dressing -- or
Mayonnaise
2 tb Onion -- chopped
2 tb Green pepper -- chopped
Kaiser rolls
2 c  Chicken
Diced left over cooked
1/4 lb Process American cheese --
Diced
2 tb Pickle relish
In a bowl, combine first six ingredients; mix well. Spoon about 1 /3
cup onto each roll. Wrap each tightly in foil. Bake at 300 deg. for
20-30 minutes or until heated through. Yield: 6-8 servings
