Title: Spinach Ham And Pasta Salad^
Categories: Meats, Salads, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
Cheese slices, cut in 2x1/4"
Strips
DRESSING
1/4 c  White wine vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Olive oil
1 tb Water
1/4 c  Chopped fresh basil
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 cl Garlic, minced
Fast & Healthy 9/92
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
SALAD
1 pk (8oz, 5 1/2c) uncooked
Spinach noodles
4 oz Cooked xtra lean ham slices,
Cut in 2 x 1/4" strips
1 sm Red onion, thinly sliced,
Separated into rings
4 c  Fresh spinach, cut into
3    X1/4" strips
4 oz Lowfat part skim mozzarella
Cook noodles to desired doneness as directed on package. drain; rinse
with cold water. Meanwhile, in jar with tight fitting lid, combine
all dressing ingredients; shake well.

In large bowl, combine cooked noodles and remaining salad
ingredients; toss lightly. Pour dressing over salad mixture; toss
lightly to coat. 6 (1 1/2 cup) servings.

Per serving: 260 calories, 16 g protein, 30 g carbohydrate, 4 g
fiber, 8 g fat, 55 mg cholesterol, 520 mg sodium, 460 mg potassium, 2
starch and 2 lean meat exchanges.
