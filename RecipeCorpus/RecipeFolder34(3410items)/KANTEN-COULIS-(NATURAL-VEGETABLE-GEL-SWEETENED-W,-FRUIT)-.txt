Title: KANTEN COULIS (NATURAL VEGETABLE GEL SWEETENED W/ FRUIT)-
Categories: House beaut, Fruit, Information
Yield: 1 quart
1    10-oz bag each of frozen
-strawberries, blueberries
-and raspberries, thawed
1 qt Apple juice
3 tb Agar Agar flakes or Kanten,
-available in health
-food stores
In a large saucepan add apple juice and Agar Agar flakes and bring to
a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes or until the flakes
dissolve. Stir in berries and return mixture to a boil. Remove pan
from heat and chill mixture until set.
