Title: Quesadilla Pizza
Categories: Appetizers, Pizza
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Green chiles -- diced
1 tb Green onion -- thinly
Sliced
1 ts Ripe olives -- sliced
1 tb Monterer Jack cheese --
Shred
2    Flour tortillas
1/4 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
Olive oil
1/4 c  Pizza sauce
1/4 ts Ground cumin
2 tb Refried beans
Sprinkle one tortilla with cheddar cheese, top with second tortilla.
Brush lightly with olive oil; place on baking sheet. Bake in
preheated 450 F oven for 4 minutes. In small bowl combine pizza sauce
and cumin. Spread on tortilla.  Spoon on refried beans; sprinkle with
chiles, green oniln, olives and Monterey Jack cheese. Bake in
preheated 450 F oven for 3 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
Cut into wedges to serve.
