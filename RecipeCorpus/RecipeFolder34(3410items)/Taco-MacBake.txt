Title: Taco MacBake
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Water
1 1/4 oz Taco seasoning mix
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1 c  Crushed taco chips
1/2 lb Elbow macaroni (uncooked)
1 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper, chopped
2 cn Tomato soup
Prepare elbow macaroni. Darin. Brown ground beef, onion and green
pepper, spoon off excess fat, add soup, water, and seasoning mix.
Simmer 10 minutes. Combine elbow macaroni and meat mixture. Pour into
a 3 quart casserole, top with cheese and taco chips. Bake in 350
degree oven 30 minutes.
