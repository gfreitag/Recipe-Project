Title: Dilled Cucumbers
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Chopped
1/8    Teabpoon
1/4 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Dried dill weed
Pepper
2 md Cucumbers
Peeled and thinly sliced
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Green onion -- finely
In a small bowl, toss cucumbers with salt. Allow to stand for 10
minutes. Meanwhile, combine all remaining ingredients. Drain
cucumbers and combine with sour cream mixture. Chill until ready to
serve. Yield: 6 servings
