Title: Herbed Cheese Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Appetizers, Low cal/fat, Snacks
Yield: 1 1/2+ cups
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/8 ts Dried marjoram
1/8 ts Dried thyme
1/8 ts Dried basil
1/8 ts Ground savory
1 ea Clove garlic; minced
2/3 c  1% low-fat cottage cheese
1 pk (8-oz.) Healthy Choice fat-
-free cream cheese;
-softened
1 tb Finely chopped fresh parsley
1 ts Red wine vinegar
Beat cottage cheese in a small bowl at medium speed of an electric
mixer until creamy.  Add cream cheese and remaining ingredients,
stirring well. Cover and chill thoroughly.

Transfer to a serving bowl; serve with melba toast rounds or unsalted
crackers.

Yield:  1-1/2 cups plus 2 tablespoons.

PER TABLESPOON:  CALORIES 12; FAT 0.1g; CARB 0.6g; PROTEIN 2.0G;
CHOLESTEROL 2mg; SODIUM 77mg

From:  Healthy Choice "CHOICES FOR LIVING HAPPIER AND HEALTHIER," Fall
1995.
