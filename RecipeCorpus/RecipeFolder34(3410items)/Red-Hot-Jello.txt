Title: Red Hot Jello
Categories: Salads, Fruit, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Chopped celery
2 c  Chopped apple
3/4 c  Chopped pecans
2    3 oz. pkgs. Cherry Jello
4 c  Hot water
1/2 c  Red hots
Dissolve Jello in 3 cups of hot water.  Dissolve red hots in 1 cup
water. Mix Jello and red hots, and cool.  Add remaining ingredients
to Jello. Refrigerate in a 9 x 9 inch pan. Randy Rigg
