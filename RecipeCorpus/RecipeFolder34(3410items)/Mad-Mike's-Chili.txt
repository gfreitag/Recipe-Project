Title: Mad Mike's Chili
Categories: Meats, Tex/mexican, Chili
Yield: 1 servings
2 cn Enchilada sauce; mild
10    Bay leaves
4 tb Brown sugar
2 1/2 oz Chili powder
3 tb Cumin
2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 tb Oregano
5 lb Round steak; cubed
2 lg Onions; chopped
4 lg Carrots; shredded
46 oz V-8 juice
6 oz Tomato paste
28 oz Tomatoes; whole peeled
1 cn Beef broth
Dice the meat into 1/2 inch cubes, cutting across the grain. In a 3
gallon pot, saute the onions in some olive oil until they are soft.
Add the meat and continue cooking until meat is grayish brown, about
20 minutes. Mix in all the other ingredients, bring to a boil and
simmer for 3 hours.
