Title: Chicken and Cheese Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
Stuffing
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
8 oz Mild Cheddar cheese, shred
1    Chicken, cut in small pieces
2 c  Chicken broth
1 pk Stove Top chicken flavored
Cook chicken in water until done.  Save 2 cups of broth, debone and
cut chicken into small pieces.  In a bowl, combine Stove Top stuffing
and seasoning with 2 cups broth.  Let set 3 to 4 minutes, and then
place in a 9 x 13 inch glass dish sprayed with Pam, then layerin
chicken, followed by condensed cream of chicken soup, and top with
cheese. Bake covered for 15 minutes at 350 degrees and 20 minutes
uncovered. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   09-09-95
