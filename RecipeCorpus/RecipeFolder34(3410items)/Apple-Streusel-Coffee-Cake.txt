Title: Apple Streusel Coffee Cake
Categories: Breakfast, Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
3/4 c  Nuts -- chopped
2/3 c  Pancake mix
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
3 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
2 1/2 c  Pancake mix
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Water
1    Egg -- beaten
2 tb Oil
1 1/2 c  Apples -- coarsely chopped
Heat oven to 350.  Grease 13 x 9 baking pan.  Combine pancake mix,
brown sugar and cinnamon.  Add water, egg, and oil; mix until smooth.
Spread into prepared pan.  Sprinkle apples evenly over batter.
Combine nuts, pancake mix, brown sugar and cinnamon.  Add margarine,
mixing until crumbly; sprinkle over apples.   Bake 25 to 30 minutes.
Serve warm.
