Title: Laskateszta Levesbe (Soup Noodles)
Categories: Soup/stew, Hungarian, East europe
Yield: 6 servings
3 qt Water
2 tb Salt
1/2 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 ea Eggs
1 pn Salt
Knead a hard dough from flour, egg and salt. Roll it out very thin
and cut thin vermicelli noodles.
Bring 3 qt water and 2 tablespoons salt to a boil. Cook noodles in
rapidly boiling water for 3 minutes, or until done. Drain and rinse
with cold water.
