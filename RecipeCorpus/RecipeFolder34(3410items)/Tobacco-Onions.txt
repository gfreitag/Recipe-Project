Title: Tobacco Onions
Categories: Vegetables, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Paprika
Salt and pepper to taste
Oil for deep frying
2 lg Red onions sliced thinly
2 lg Yellow onions sliced thinly
6 c  Flour
2 ts Cayenne
Combine flour, cayenne, paprika, salt and pepper.  Dredge onions in
the seasoned flour.  Shake off the excess flour.  Fry onions until
golden brown.

Use as a "bed" for chicken breasts.
