Title: Mandamin (Corn -- Sioux Style)
Categories: Side dish, Native amer, Quapaw, Vegetables
Yield: 1 recipe
3 tb Oil
3 ea Shallots, chopped
Salt & pepper
1 1/2 c  Corn
1/4 c  Green bell pepper, diced
1/4 c  Mild red pepper, diced
Heat the oil & fry the peppers with the shallots for 3 minutes or so.
Add the corn, salt & pepper.  Cook for a further 5 to 10 minutes at
low heat & serve.

Bernard Assiniwi, "Indian Recipes"
