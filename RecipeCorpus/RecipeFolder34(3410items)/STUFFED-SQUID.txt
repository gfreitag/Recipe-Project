Title: STUFFED SQUID
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
2    Sprigs parsley; minced
1 tb Uncooked rice
1    Very small tomato; minced
1 tb Olive oil
1    Canned anchovy filet; choppe
B christensen (cpvm71a)
2 tb Bread crumbs
2 lb Squid
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/2 ts Salt
1    Clove garlic
~Peel squid and remove insides, keeping body whole. ~Cut off
tentacles and heads, remove eyes and put heads and tentacles through
meat chopper. ~Add parsley, salt, pepper, rice, tomato, anchovy and 1
Tablespoon oil and mix well. ~Stuff squid bodies with this mixture,
place in oiled baking dish, sprinkle with bread crumbs and pour oil
over all. ~Bake in hot oven 400 35 minutes. Slice and serve. serves 4
