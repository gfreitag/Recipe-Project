Title: Pasta with Fresh Tomato Sauce
Categories: Pasta/rice, Tomatoes, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Finely chopped drained, sun-
Dried tomatoes
3 tb Chopped fresh basil
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper to taste
1/2 c  Grated Asiago cheese
3/4 lb Fusilli lunghi (long curly
Pasta) or other pasta
1 lb Ripe tomatoes
1 tb Oil drained from sun-dried
Tomatoes
1 md Onion;chopped
2    Garlic cloves;chopped fine
Cook pasta in large amount of boiling salted water until tender;
drain.
Meanwhile, halve, seed and chop tomatoes; set aside. In large heavy
saucepan, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add onion and garlic; saute
until onion is tender, about 3 minutes. Stir in sun-dried tomatoes
and cook for 1 minute. Add tomatoes, basil and salt and pepper; stir
until heated through, about 1 minute.
Add tomato sauce to pasta and toss. Sprinkle cheese on top.
Source: Vancouver Sun  Oct 26/94
