Title: COLESLAW - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Caraway seeds (opt.)
1/4 ts Cracked black pepper
2 c  Finely sliced green cabbage
2 c  Finely sliced red cabbage
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
3 tb Cider vinegar
2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1. In large bowl, combine mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, salt, caraway
seeds, if desired, and pepper. Add green and red cabbage; toss until
cabbage is well coated with mayonnaise mixture.

2. Cover tightly and refrigerate at least one hour before serving for
ingredients to marinate, to allow cabbage to soften, and for flavors
to blend.

Nutrition information per serving-protein: 1 gram; fat: 1 1 grams;
carbohydrate: 7 grams; fiber: 2 grams; sodium: 357 milligrams;
cholesterol: 8 milligrams; calories: 125.
