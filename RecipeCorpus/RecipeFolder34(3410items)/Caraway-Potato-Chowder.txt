Title: Caraway Potato Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables, Herbs/spice, Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Caraway seed
1/4 ts Lemon-pepper seasoning
Pepper to taste
1/2 c  Cooked ham, cubed
1/3 c  Sauerkraut, rinsed & drained
Chives, chopped
2 md Potatoes, peeled & chopped,
-about 2 cups OR 2 cups
-loose packed hash browns
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
1 ts Instant chicken boullion
1 1/4 c  Milk
Combine potatoes, water, onion & boullion in a medium saucepan; bring
to boil, reduce heat, cover & simmer for 10 minutes. Do not drain,
slightly mash the potatoes.

Stir together the milk and flour; then mix into the potato mixture.
Add caraway, lemon-pepper, and pepper. Cook and stir until thickened
and bubbly. Add ham and sauerkraut. Cook until heated through. Serve
topped with chopped chives.
