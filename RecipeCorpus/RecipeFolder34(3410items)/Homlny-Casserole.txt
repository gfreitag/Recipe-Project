Title: Homlny Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Green chilies -- 4 oz.
Chopped
1/4 ts Ground cumin
3 cn Golden hominy -- 15 oz. ea.
Drained
Clove -- minced
1 md Onion -- chopped
1    Garlic
1 tb Cooking oil
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz.
3/4 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
Divided
1/4 c  Milk
In a small skillet, cook onion and garlic in oil until tender. Remove
from heat. In a bowl, combine onion mixture, sour cream, half of the
cheese, milk, chilies and cumin. Add hominy; mix well. Pour into a
greased 2-qt. baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at 350 deg. for 30
minutes or until heated through. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Serve immediately. Yield: 8-10 servings
