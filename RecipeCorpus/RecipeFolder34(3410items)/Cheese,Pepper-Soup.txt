Title: Cheese/Pepper Soup
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 12 servings
4 c  Milk
4 c  Chicken broth
1    Jar Cheese Whiz (16 oz)
1/8 ts Red pepper or cayenne or
-to taste
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 tb Mustard
1/3 c  Carrots -- finely chopped
1/3 c  Celery -- finely chopped
1 c  Green onion -- thinly
Sliced
2 c  Water
1 md Chopped onion
3/4 c  Butter
1 c  Flour -- plus 2 tablespoons
Combine carrots, celery and green onion in water; cook until tender.
Set aside. Saute onion in butter until limp; stir in the flour and
blend well. Do not brown. Combine milk and broth; bring to boil.
Whisk in onion/flour mixture. Add cheese whiz, cayenne, salt and
pepper if desired and mustard. Slowly stir in vegetables and water
they were cooked in. Bring just to a boil; serve immediately. Add
cayenne gradually to taste. Yield: 12 servings.
