Title: Garlic Festival Garlic Roast
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
-or other herbs
1 tb Sea salt
1/2 ts Fresh ground pepper
10    Whole heads of garlic
2 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
2 tb Salad oil
1 tb Thyme
Slice the top third off of each garlic pod. Combine the oils in a
small shallow baking dish or pie pan. Distribute the seasonings
equally in the oil. Arrange the pods, cut side down, in the pan.
