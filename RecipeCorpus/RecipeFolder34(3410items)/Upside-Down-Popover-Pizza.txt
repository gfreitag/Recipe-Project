Title: Upside Down Popover Pizza
Categories: Main dish, Pizza
Yield: 5 servings
8 oz Mozzarella cheese -- sliced
Batter
2    Eggs
1 c  Flour
1 c  Milk
1 tb Oil
1/4 ts Salt
1 lb Pork sausage
1 md Onion  -- chopped
1/2 md Green pepper -- chopped
2 tb Flour
1/2 ts Each dried basil and
Marjoram leaves crumbled
15 oz Can tomato sauce
In a  large skillet, cook sausage until browned. Pour off excess fat.
To skillet, add onion and green pepper. Cook 3-4 minutes. Stir in
flour, basil, marjoram and tomato sauce. Heat well. Pour into a 13x9
" baking pan> Top with mozzarella slices. To make popover batter;
beat together eggs,flour, milk, oil and salt.Pour over ingredients in
pan. Sprinkle top with parmesan cheese.Bake at 425 for 25 to 30
minutes until well browned. Serve immediately.
