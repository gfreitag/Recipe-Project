Title: Snappy Asparagus Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 ds Lime juice
Salt and pepper to taste
1 ds Cayenne pepper
1 lb Fresh asparagus -- trimmed
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz.
1/2 c  Salsa
(mild, medium or hot)
Cook asparagus in a small amount of water until tender. Drain and
cool. In a blender or food processor, puree asparagus until smooth.
Stir in all the remaining ingredients. Chill. Serve with assorted raw
vegetable dippers or tortilla chips. Yield: 2 cups
