Title: Dilled Garden Dip^
Categories: Dips/spread, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 24 servings
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1 1/2 ts Chopped fresh dill
OR
1/2 ts Dried dill weed
1 ts Sugar
ds Pepper
Fast & Healthy 8/92
Carolyn Shaw 8-95
1 ct (12oz) lowfat cottage
Cheese
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Shredded carrot
1 tb Sliced green onion
In blender, combine cottage cheese and lemon juice. Cover; blend 3-5
minutes or until smooth. Spoon imto bowl; stir in remaining
ingredients. Serve with raw vegetable dippers. Makes 1 1/2 cups.

Per tablespoon: 14 calories, 2 g protein, 1 g carb., 0 g fiber and
fat, 1 mg chol., 60 mg sodium, 20 mg potassium, free exchange.
