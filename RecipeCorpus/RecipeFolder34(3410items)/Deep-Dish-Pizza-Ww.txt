Categories: Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM--------------------------<SAUCE>-------------------------------
1 pk Beef broth, instant -- low
Sod
1/2 ts Oregano -- dried
1/4 ts Basil -- dried
Black pepper -- ground
1 ea Garlic cloves -- minced
3 ea Tomatoes, medium -- chopped
1/3 c  Spaghetti sauce --
Reduced-cal
Sugar substitute
MMMMM--------------------------<PIZZA>-------------------------------
Diced
1 1/2 c  Bell pepper, green --
Sliced thin
6 oz Cheese, mozzarella --
Part-skim
Italian seasoning
15 oz Turkey sausage patties --
Hot-
1 ts Fennel seed
1/2 ts Garlic powder
9 ea Rifrigerator rolls (1 oz)
1/2 c  Mushrooms, sliced, canned
1. Spray a large nonstick skillet with nonstick cooking spray. Cook
onion and garlic over medium-high heat until tender, about 3 minutes.
Add 3/4 cup water and remaining sauce ingredients. Bring to a boil;
reduce heat to low and simmer, uncovered, 55 to 60 minutes. 2. To
make pizza, in medium nonstick skillet, brown sausage patties over
medium-high heat. Add fennel and garlic powder; stir to break up
sausage. With slotted spoon, remove turkey to plate. 3. Preheat oven
to 400F. Spray a 13x9 baking dish with nonstick cooking spray. With
hands, press biscuits onto bottom of dish, pinching together to form
a crust that covers entire surface. 4. Spread 1/2 cup sauce over
biscuits. Sprinkle evenly with turkey sausage. Top with remaining
sauce, the mushrooms, 1 cup bell pepper, the sliced cheese, and
Italian seasoning. Top with remaining bell pepper. Bake 25 to 30
minutes, or until crust is brown. Let stand 10 to 15 minutes before
cutting.
