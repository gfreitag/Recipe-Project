Title: Jellied Pig's or Calve's Feet
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
4    Peppercorns
6    Cloves
2    Dried chile peppers; opt'l
1 tb White vinegar
6    Pig's or calve's feet
1 lg Onion; sliced
1 cl Garlic; sliced in half
1    Lemon; sliced
2    Bay leaves
Wash, split and blanche the feet. In a large pot add the feet, cover
with water and add the other seasonings. Simmer 4 hours. Remove the
feet; skin and bone them. Set in a mold. Strain the stock, correct
the seasoning and pour over the feet. Chill until the stock gels.
Slice and serve with salt, pepper, mustard and vinegar to taste.
