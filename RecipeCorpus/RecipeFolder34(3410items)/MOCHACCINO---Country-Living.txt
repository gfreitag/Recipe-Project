Title: MOCHACCINO - Country Living
Categories: Beverages, Country liv
Yield: 1 serving
1 ts Sugar
1 c  Skim or low-fat milk
Ground cinnamon
1 tb Hot water
1 1/2 ts Instant coffee granules
1 ts Unsweetened cocoa powder
1. In microwave-safe mug or cup, combine water, coffee, cocoa, and
sugar. Microwave, uncovered, on high (100 percent) 30 seconds.

2. Add milk to coffee mixture and stir to blend. Microwave,
uncovered, on high (100 percent) 1 1/2 minutes. If desired, add more
froth to mochaccino by placing small whisk in cup and quickly rubbing
handle between fingers. Sprinkle lightly with cinnamon and serve.
