Title: Hawaiian Flours Lemon, Poppy seed, Sour Cream Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Oil
2 tb Poppy seeds
1 lg Egg
1/3 c  Sour cream
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Liquid--3T lemon juice
Plus water.
1 c  Bread flour
1 c  All Purpose flour
1 c  Lemon Cake Mix
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
3 tb Gluten flour
3 tb Sugar
2 ts Yeast
Add ingredients in accordance with ABM guidelines. Makes a moist and
sweet loaf suitable for breakfast or desert.
