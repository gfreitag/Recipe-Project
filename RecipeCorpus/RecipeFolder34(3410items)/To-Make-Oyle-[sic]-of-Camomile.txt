Title: To Make Oyle [sic] of Camomile
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 batch
3 oz Camomile [sic] flowers
1 1/2 pt Oyle
"- take oyle a pint and an halfe [sic], and three ounces of camomile
flowers dryed [sic] one day after they be gathered.  Then put the
oyle and the flowers in a glasse [sic] and stop the mouth close and
set into the Sun by the space of forty days."

Cathy's note:  I do not know if this is edible.

From _The Good Housewife's Handbook_, 1588.  In Steven Foster's
"Chamomile" article in "The Herb Companion."  Dec. 1992/Jan. 1993,
Vol. 5, No. 2. Pg. 68.  Posted by Cathy Harned.
