Title: Creamy Noodles And Cabbage
Categories: Pasta, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb All-purpose flour
1 pk Noodles -- 12oz
Or fettuccine cooked and
Drained
1 c  Shredded Parmesan cheese --
(4 oz)
1/2 c  Crumbled cooked bacon
6 tb Butter or margarine
1    Head cabbage -- chopped abt
1    Pound
1 md Onion -- chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Light cream
In a Dutch oven, melt butter. Add cabbage, onion, salt and pepper.
Cook and stir until vegetables are crisp-tender, about 10 minutes.
Combine cream and flour. Add to vegetables: bring to a boil and boil
1 minute. Stir In noodles and Parmesan cheese: mix well. Pour into
serving dish and sprinkle with bacon. Serve immediately. Yield: 10-12
servings
