Title: Sour Cream Pound Cake
Categories: Cakes, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 ounces
3 c  All-purpose flour
Confectioners' sugar,
Optional
1 c  Butter
3 c  Sugar
6 ea Eggs
1/4 ts Baking soda
2 ts Vanilla extract
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time,
beating well after each addition. Mix soda and vanilla with sour
cream. Add alternately with the flour to the butter mixture. Mix
well. Pour batter into a greased and floured tube pan. Bake at 325
deg. for80 minutes or until cake tests done. Cool in pan for 15
minutes before removing to a rack. Sprinkle with confectioners' sugar
before serving, if desired. Yield: about 16 servings
