Title: Macaroni with Danish Butter Sauce
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Graded parmesan cheese
1 1/2 tb Fresh chives, minced
Salt and pepper to taste
4 c  Macaroni
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Mushrooms, sliced
1/2 c  Danish blue cheese
1 c  Chicken broth
Cook macaroni, melt butter in large skillet, over low heat and saute
mushrooms for 1 minute. Add blue cheese and stir until melted. Add
chicken broth and lemon juice and simmer over low heat for 2 minutes.
Add macaroni and salt and pepper. Mix thoroughly. Add parmesan cheese
and chives.

Serve.
