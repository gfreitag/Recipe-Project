Title: Golden Ham Croquettes
Categories: Pork, Ham, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
Ground
2/3 c  Dry bread crumbs
1 ea Egg -- beaten
2 tb Water
Cooking oil for deep-fat
Frying
3 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 c  Milk
3 ts Mustard
1 ts Onion -- grated
2 c  Cooked ham -- coarsely
MMMMM------------------------CHEESE SAUCE-----------------------------
1 1/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Swiss cheese -- shredded
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 tb All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
In a saucepan, melt butter; stir in curry powder and flour. Gradually
add milk; cook and stir until bubbly. Cook and stir 2 min. more.
Remove from the heat. Stir in mustard and onion; add ham and mix
well. Cover and chill thoroughlty. With wet hands, shape mixture into
10 balls. Poll ballsin bread crumbs; shape each into a cone. Whisk
together egg and water. Dip cones into egg mixture; roll again in
crumbs. Heat oil in a deep-fat fryer to 365 deg. Fry croquettes, a
few at a time, for 2 to 2 1/2 min. or until golden brown. Drain on
paper towels; keep warm. For the cheese sauce, melt butter in a
saucepan; stir in flour, salt and pepper. Gradually add milk; cook
and stir until thickened and bubbly. Cook and stir 2 min. more. Add
cheeses; heat and stir until melted. Spoon over croquettes. Yield: 6
servings.
