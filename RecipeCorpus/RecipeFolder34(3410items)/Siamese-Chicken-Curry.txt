Title: Siamese Chicken Curry
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Dried crushed red pepper
1 1/2 c  Canned low-fat
-chicken broth
3/4 c  Whipping cream
3/4 ts Coconut extract
2 1/2 c  Cooked chicken
-cut in 1/2" pieces
3 tb Curry powder
2 tb Cider vinegar
3 tb Butter
1    Onion(s), chopped
2    Garlic clove(s), chopped
2 tb Basil, chopped or
2 ts Dried basil
Stir curry powder and vinegar in small bowl to blend. Melt butter in
heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; saut
until onion is translucent, about 6 minutes. Add curry mixture; stir
2 minutes. Add basil and crushed red pepper, stir 1 minute. Add
broth, cream and coconut extract; boil until reduced to sauce
consistency, about 6 minutes. Add chicken; stir until heated through.
Season with salt and pepper.
