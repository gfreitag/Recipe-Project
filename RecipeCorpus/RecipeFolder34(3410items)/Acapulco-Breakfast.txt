Title: Acapulco Breakfast
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Salsa
1 ts Chopped onion
1/4 c  Shredded jack cheese
1 ea Flour tortilla
1/4 c  Bean dip
1 ea Egg
Put tortilla on glass plate.  Spread with bean dip.  Make a well in
center of beans and break egg into it piercing yolk.  Microwave on
medium 2-2 1/2 minutes.  Top with salsa, onion and cheese. From
Recipes Calendar Microwave cooking 1990
