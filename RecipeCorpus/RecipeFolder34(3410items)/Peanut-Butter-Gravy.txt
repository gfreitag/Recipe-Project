Title: Peanut Butter Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Oldies
Yield: 1 gravy
Few drops onion water;
1/2 ts Onion juice;
1/2 ts Salt; (OPTIONAL)
1/8 ts Pepper;
1/2 ts Poultry; seasoning
2 tb Butter  -=OR=-
2 tb Margarine;
1/2 c  Peanut butter;
3 tb Flour;
1 1/2 c  Boiling water;
Melt the butter or margarine and add the peanut butter and flour. Cook
together slowly until the mixture begins to brown and gradually stir
in the in the boiling water.  Add the seasonings and cook and stir
until boiling point is reached.

Source: The Service Cook Book NO. 2 by Mrs. Ida Bailey Allen,
Published exclusively for F. W. Woolworth Co., Copyright 1935.
