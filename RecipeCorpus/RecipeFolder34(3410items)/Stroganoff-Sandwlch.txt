Title: Stroganoff Sandwlch
Categories: Sandwich, Country wom, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
Softened
1    Loaf
Cut in half lengthwise
2 md Tomatoes -- thinly sliced
1 md Green pepper -- cut in
Rings
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- 4 oz.
Shredded
French bread
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 cn Mushrooms -- 4 oz.
Sliced/drained
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz.
Butter or margarine
In a skillet, brown beef with onion. Drain. Season with the garlic
powder, salt, pepper and Worcestershire sauce. Remove from the heat
and stir in mushrooms and sour cream; set aside. Butter the cut
surface of the bread. Place it with the buttered side up on a baking
sheet; broil until light golden brown. Reset oven to 375 deg. Spread
bread with ground beef mixture. Top with the tomatoes, pepper rings
and cheese. Bake for 5 minutes or until the cheese melts. Serve
immediately. Yield: 6-8 servings
