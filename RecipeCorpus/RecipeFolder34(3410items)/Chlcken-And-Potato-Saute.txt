Title: Chlcken And Potato Saute
Categories: Poultry, Country wom, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Chicken bouillon cube
1 c  Hot water
1 cn Whole potatoes -- 16 oz.
Drained
1/4 c  Red wine -- or water
Snipped fresh parsley
Clove -- minced
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
6 ea Boneless chicken breast
Halves
1 md Onion -- sliced
1    Garlic
2 tb All-purpose flour
Salt and pepper to taste
In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Saute chicken until
browned on both sides. Add onion and garlic; cook about 5 minutes. In
a small bowl, combine flour and seasonings. Dissolve bouillon in
water and stir into flour mixture; pour over chicken. Cover; simmer
20 minutes. Add potatoes and wine or water; heat through. Sprinkle
with parsley. Yield: 4-6 servings
