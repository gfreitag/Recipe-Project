Title: Cheese Strata
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Prepared mustard
1 tb Onion, minced
1/4 ts Pepper
12 ea Slices bread
1/2 lb Cheddar cheese, thin sliced
4 ea Eggs
2 1/2 c  Milk
Cut crusts from bread.  Place 6 slices in bottom of greased baking
dish. Top bread with cheese slices.  Cover cheese with remaining
bread sliced. Combine eggs, milk, mustard, onion and pepper. Pour
over bread. Refrigerate for one hour.  Bake, uncovered, at 325
degrees F for 50 minutes. Serve immediately.
