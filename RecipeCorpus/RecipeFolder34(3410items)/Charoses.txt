Title: Charoses
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 tb Red wine
Ginger, dash
1/2 ts Lemon rind, grated
1 ea Apple, pared
1/2 c  Nuts, chopped
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Sugar
Grate the apple coarsley and mash throughly with remaining
ingredients. Beat until mixture is smooth and free of lumps. Correct
seasoning to taste.
