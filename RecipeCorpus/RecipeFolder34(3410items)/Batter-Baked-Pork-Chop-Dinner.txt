Title: Batter Baked Pork Chop Dinner
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Bread crumbs
1 x  Salt and pepper
4 ea Pork chops
1 ea Eggs
Season chops, dip in egg wash then crumbs.  Refrigerate 1 hour. Place
on rack.  Bake 350 F for 20 minutes.  Turn, bake additional 20
minutes.
