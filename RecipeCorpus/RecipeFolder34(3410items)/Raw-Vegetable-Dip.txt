Title: Raw Vegetable Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Dry green onions
1 ts Garlic salt
2 dr Tabasco sauce
1 ts Accent
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Hellmann's mayonnaise
1 ts Parsley flakes
1/2 ts Dried dill weed
1 ts Beau Monde seasoning
Mix all ingredients together until thick.  Let stand in refrigerator
for 2 to 6 hours.  Serve with an assortment of raw veggies. Randy Rigg
