Title: Tony Palermo's Michigan Champ Chili
Categories: Chili, Meats
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 c  Crushed rosemary
1 tb Salt
1 tb White pepper
1 tb Cumin powder
2    No.10 cans whole tomatoes
(13 lbs)
1 c  Tomato paste
1 ts Tasbasco
Sour cream for garnish
5 lb Ground beef
3 tb Oil
60    Garlic; crushed
3 lg Onions; chopped
3    Green peppers; chopped
6    Jalepeno peppers; chopped
3/4 c  Chili powder
1/4 c  Oregano
1/4 c  Sweet basil
1/4 c  Thyme
Brown ground beef, drain and set aside. Heat oil and saute garlic, 2
onioins, green peppers, and jalapeno peppers. Cook on medium heat for
5 mins. Add all spices. Continue to cook for about 3mins. Add whole
tomatoes and juice, tomato paste, Tabasco, and beef and cook for 2
1/2 hours. Garnish with sour cream.
