Title: Southwestern Rice Casserole
Categories: Tex/mexican, Pasta/rice, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Sour cream
4 oz Green chiles -- chopped
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
1 c  Water
1 c  Chunky salsa -- divided
1 1/2 c  Instant rice
Preheat oven to 350.  In a medium saucepan, bring water and 1/2 cup
salsa to a boil.  Stir in rice; remove from heat, cover and let stand
5 minutes. Stir in sour cream and remaining 1/2 cup salsa into the
rice.  Pour into an 8" square baking pan.  Spread green chilies over
tice and top with cheese. Bake 20 minutes.
