Title: Spicy Rice
Categories: Newspaper, Side dish, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Sesame oil
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Long-grain white rice
1 2/3 c  Water
1/3 c  Orange juice
1 tb Ginger; grated
1/4 ts Hot chili oil
In a medium pan, combine the water, orange juice, ginger, chili and
sesame oils and salt.  Bring to a boil and stir in the rice. Continue
to boil, stirring occasionally, until the liquid just covers the
rice. Cover, turn the heat to low and cook 15 minutes.  Fluff with a
fork. Nutritional Information per serving: 188 calories, 1g fat, no
cholesterol, 272mg sodium
