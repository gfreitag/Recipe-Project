Title: Fruit Vodka
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Vodka
1 c  Fresh fruit **
**  use melon or pear cubes, pitted cherries, strawberry halves or
other berries.

Place the fruit in a glass container with the vodka. Set aside one to
two days.  Remove the fruit and chill the vodka. Use over ice cream
or as a spirit to enjoy with spicy ethnic foods or a fruit dessert.
Try it in any vodka cocktail that uses a fruit juice.
