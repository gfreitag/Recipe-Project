Title: JOE'S KEY WEST SALSA APPETIZER WITH BLACK BEANS - HB
Categories: House beaut, Appetizers, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM----------------------FOR BLACK BEANS---------------------------
Freshly ground black pepper
-to taste
Juice of half a lemon
2 tb Finely chopped scallions
1    15-oz can black beans,
-drained & rinsed well
-under running water
2 tb Olive oil
1    Garlic clove, minced
MMMMM-------------------------FOR SALSA------------------------------
1 ts Minced fresh jalapeno OR
2 oz Canned chopped green chilies
2 ts Olive oil
2 ts Balsamic vinegar
Salt & freshly ground black
-pepper to taste
Tortilla chips
2    Fresh tomatoes, skinned,
-seeded, & chopped or one
-14 1/2-oz can chunky
-tomatoes, drained
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 lg Garlic clove, peeled and
-minced
Prepare black beans: In a small bowl, combine black beans, oil,
garlic, and pepper. Transfer to a serving bowl and add lemon juice.
Garnish with scallions. Beans can be prepared in advance and
refrigerated, but should be served warm or at room temperature.

Prepare salsa: In a medium-size glass bowl, combine tomatoes, onion,
garlic, jalapeno, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and salt and pepper to
taste. Refrigerate for several hours. To serve: Place a spoonful of
beans on a tortilla chip and top with salsa. Serves 8.
