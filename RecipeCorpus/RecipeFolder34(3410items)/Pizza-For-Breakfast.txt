Title: Pizza For Breakfast
Categories: Pizza, Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
Ham chopped -- or sausage
Or

1 cn Flakey style biscuits --
(layered kind)

1 c  Grated mozorella cheese
In a pizza pan that is lightly greased press biscuits pulled apart to
make 3 to 4 biscuits per 1 biscuit and cover pan. Add remaining
ingredients and bake at 350 till done and begining to brown on edges.
