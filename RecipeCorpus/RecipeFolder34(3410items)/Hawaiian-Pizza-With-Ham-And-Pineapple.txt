Title: Hawaiian Pizza With Ham And Pineapple
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
1    Ready made pizza crust
2 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
3 sl Cooked ham -- 1/2" strips
1    Green pepper -- rings
1/3 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1/2 ts Basil -- crushed
1 cn Pineapple slices
1 cn Tomato paste (6 oz)
2 tb Onion -- chopped
1/2 ts Oregano -- crushed
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Sugar
1    Clove garlic -- crushed
Black pepper
Preheat oven to 400 F.  Drain pineapple reserving juice. Combine
reserved juice with tomato paste, onion, oregano, basil, salt, sugar,
garlic and black pepper. Mix well. Spread over crust. Sprinkle with
mozzarella cheese. Arrange pineapple, ham and green pepper on top.
Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Place on baking sheet. Bake 8 to 10
minutes or until hot and bubbly.
