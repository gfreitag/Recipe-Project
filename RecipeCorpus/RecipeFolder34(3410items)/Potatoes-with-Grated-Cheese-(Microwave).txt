Title: Potatoes with Grated Cheese (Microwave)
Categories: Side dish, Microwave, Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Grated Cantal or Gruyere
-cheese
3/4 c  Sour cream
2 tb Butter
Salt and pepper
1 3/4 lb Grated potatoes
Put the butter in a small dish, 9 inches in diameter and melt in
the microwave on high for 1 minute.  Make sure the butter covers the
bottom of the dish.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Add the potatoes and season with
salt and pepper.  Pour 1-1/4 cups water over.  Cover and microwave on
High for 8 minutes.
Add the cheese and sour cream to the potatoes.  Stir carefully
with a fork, making sure that no cheese is left on the surface. Cover
and microwave on High for 6 minutes.  Serve very hot in the same dish.
