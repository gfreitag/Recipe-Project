Title: Ginger Minted Chicken
Categories: Diabetic, Poultry
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Ground coriander;
1 pn Chicken breasts; skinless
-- boneless (5-oz ea)
2    Sprigs fresh mint; for
-- garnish
1/2 c  Nonfat yogurt;
1/4 c  Fresh mint; coarsely chopped
1/2 tb Ginger; chopped fresh
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1 md Clove garlic; crushed
Mix together yogurt, chopped mint, ginger, onion, garlic, coriander
and cayenne.  Remove as much fat as possible from chicken.  With a
skewer or knife, poke several holes in chicken.  Add to yogurt
mixture and marinate 20 minutes, turning once.  Preheat broiler. Line
a baking tray with foil. Broil chicken on to shelf for 5 minutes per
side, or until cooked through. Serve on bed of Lemon Barley with a
sprig of fresh mint as a garnish.

Per serving: 225 calories; 4 g fat; 91 mg chol; 127 mg sod;
