Title: Chicken Crescents
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Milk
1 tb Onions
8 oz Can crescent rolls
3/4 c  Seasoned croutons, crushed
3 oz Cream cheese
3 tb Margarine
2 c  Cooked chicken
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Blend cheese and 2 tablespoons margarine.  Save 1 tablespoon.  Add
chicken, salt, pepper, milk and onions; mix.  Separate dough into 4
rectangles; press peforations to seal.  Place 1/2 cup of mixture in
center of each. Bring all 4 corners to center and seal edges.  Brush
tops with margarine and sprinkle croutons over them.  Bake 20 to 25
minutes at 350 degrees on an ungreased cookie sheet. Randy Rigg
