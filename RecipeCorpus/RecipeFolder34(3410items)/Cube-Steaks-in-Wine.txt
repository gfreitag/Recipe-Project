Title: Cube Steaks in Wine
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Water
1/2 c  Seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 c  Sifted flour
1 ts Cajun seasoning
2 tb Olive oil
6    Cube (minute) steaks
1    Onion, chopped course
2    Cloves garlic, chopped
3/4 c  Dry red wine
2 cn Golden mushroom soup
1. mix bread crumbs, flour, & cajun seasoning together & coat steaks
with mixture. Place olive oil in electric skillet & brown cube steaks
on both sides & set aside, saute onions & garlic in skillet.

2. In a sauce pan, mix soup & water bring to a boil. Pour soup into
skillet add wine & stir. Add steaks cover & simmer about 10 - 12
minutes turning steaks over occasionally.

Makes a great sauce to serve over rice, noodles, or mashed potatoes.
