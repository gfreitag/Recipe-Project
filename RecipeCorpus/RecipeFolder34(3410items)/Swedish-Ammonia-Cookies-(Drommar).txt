Title: Swedish Ammonia Cookies (Drommar)
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 batch
1 ts Vanilla
ts Ammonia salt, scant ts`
1 c  Butter
1 1/8 c  Sugar
2 c  Enriched flour
Cream butter; add sugar and creamthoroughly, using an electric
beater if possible.  Beat in sifted flour, ammonia salt, and vanilla.
Refrigerate for a few hours then shape into small balls and bake at
300 F. *JJ* 8/29/95
