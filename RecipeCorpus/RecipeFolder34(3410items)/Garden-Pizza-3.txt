Title: Garden Pizza 3
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Dill weed
1/2 ts Onion powder
Vegetables **
2 cn Crescent rolls
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
8 oz Cream cheese
Use two cans of crescent rolls and cover sheet pan. Bake according two
directions on back of rolls.  Let cool, must be cool!! Mix 8 ounce
package of cream cheese, 1/2 cup of mayo mix in teaspoon of dill
weed, 1/2 teaspoon of onions powder and spread on topped of cooled
crust. ** Top with chopped veggies such as broccoli, tomatoes,
peppers, scallions, mushrooms, etc..
