Title: Tomato Sauce with Dried Porcinis
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 quart
1 qt Classic Fresh Tomato Sauce
2 oz Dried porcini mushrooms
Cover the mushrooms with 2 cups warm water and let soak until
softened, about 45 minutes. Drain the mushrooms, reserving 1/3 cup of
the mushroom soaking liquid. Gently squeeze the mushrooms dry and
chop them/ Strain the reserved soaking liquid through a coffee filter.

In a large saucepan, combine the mushrooms, reserved mushroom liquid,
and the tomato sauce. Bring to a simmer and let cook until thick and
not at all watery, 15-25 minutes.
