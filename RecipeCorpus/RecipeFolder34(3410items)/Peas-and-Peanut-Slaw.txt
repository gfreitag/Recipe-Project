Title: Peas and Peanut Slaw
Categories: Salads, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1 ds Pepper;
1/4 ts Curry powder;
1 ts Prepared mustard;
1 ts White-wine vinegar;
3/4 c  Salted peanuts;
10 oz Frozen peas;
2 c  Cabbage; finely shredded
1    Green onions; thinly sliced
1/2 c  Slaw dressing; (purchased
-- homemade)
1/4 ts Salt;
Place peas in colander and run hot water over them until thawed. Drain
well.  In a large bowl, combine peas, cabbage and onion.   In a large
bowl, combine remaining slaw dressing, salt, pepper, curry, mustard
and vinegar. Blend dreesing into cabbage mixture and toss lightly.
Cover and chill at least 1 hour.  Just before serving, pour peanuts
on top of salad.
