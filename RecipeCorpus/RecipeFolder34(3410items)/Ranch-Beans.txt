Title: Ranch Beans
Categories: Vegetables, Crockpot
Yield: 1 recipe
1 c  Catsup
2 tb Prepared mustard
2 ts Vinear
1 lb Ground beef
1    Envelope onion soup mix
2 cn (16 oz) pork & beans
1 cn (16 oz) kidney beans;drained
Brown meat, then combine all ingredients in a slow cooker.  Cook
on low all day.

recipe by Dr. Norbert Peil, as published in Brownwood Bulletin,
