Title: Multifloral Vinegar - Country Living
Categories: Dress/stuff, Country liv, Salads
Yield: 1 1/2 pint b
1 c  White-wine vinegar
Few sprigs lavender or
-nasturtium
1/4 c  Torn nasturtiums or
-calendula flower petals
2 tb Lavender flowers
2 tb Borage flowers
1. In small jar, place nasturtiums, lav- ender, and borage flowers;
pour vinegar over flowers and seal jar. Let stand in a cool, dark
place for at least 3 to 4 weeks. Check after a few weeks to make sure
the flowers are still covered by the vinegar, adding more if
necessary and resealing.

2. Strain the vinegar and pour into 1/2- pint bottle. Insert flower
sprigs into the bottle and seal.
