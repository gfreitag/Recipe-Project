Title: Buttered Sliced Beets
Categories: Diabetic, Vegetables, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Lemon juice;
Salt and pepper;
1    Bunch tender beets;
2 tb Butter -=OR=-
2 tb Margarine;
Peel beets, slice thinly, and place in center of square of Heavy Duty
Reynolds Wrap.  Add remaining ingredients and close Reynolds Wrap into
bundle.  Bake in 350 F oven for 1 hour.
