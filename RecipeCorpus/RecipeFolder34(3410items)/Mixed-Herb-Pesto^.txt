Title: Mixed Herb Pesto^
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Fresh oregano leaves
1/4 c  Fresh thyme leaves
1/4 c  Minced fresh chives
2 tb Freshly grated Parmesan
Cheese
1/4 ts Salt
3 tb Extra vigin olive oil
Fast & Healthy 9-95
Carolyn Shaw 8-95
2 tb Pine nuts, toasted
2 cl Garlic, large
2 c  Torn spinach
3/4 c  Fresh flat leaf parsley
1/2 c  Arugula leaves
Drop pine nuts and garlic through food chute with food processor on
and process until minced. Add remaining ingredients except oil;
process until finely minced. With processor on, slowly pour oil
through food chute. Process until well blended. Spoon into a zip loc
bag. Store in refrigerator. Makes 3/4 cup.

Per tablespoon: 53 cal., 1.6 g protein, 4.8 g (82%) fat, 2.1 g carb.,
1 g fiber, 1 mg chol., 1.3 mg iron, 83 mg sodium, 55 mg calcium.
