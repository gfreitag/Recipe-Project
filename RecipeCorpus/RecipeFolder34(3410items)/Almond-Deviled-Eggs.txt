Title: Almond Deviled Eggs
Categories: Eggs, Country wom
Yield: 12 servings
3 ts Roasted almonds -- finely
Chopped
12    Whole roasted almonds
Fresh parsley
6 ea Hard-boiled egg
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 ts Garlic salt
Slice eggs in half lengthwise; remove yolks and set whites aside. In a
small bowl, mash yolks, mayonnaise, mustard, garlic salt and chopped
almonds. Evenly fill the egg whites, Garnish with whole almonds and
parsley. Chill until read to serve.Yield: 12 servings.
