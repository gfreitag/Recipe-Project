Title: Bayou Shrimp
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Bon appetit
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
12 lg Uncooked shrimp
-in their shells
1/4 c  Butter (1/2 stick)
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Black pepper
1 1/2 ts Dried rosemary
from Pascal's Manale in New Orleans

Melt butter in heavy medium saucepan over medium heat. Mix in water,
lemon juice, pepper, rosemary, paprika and Worcestershire sauce.
Simmer sauce until reduced to  cup, about 3 minutes. Season with
salt. Add shrimp to sauce. Cover pan; cook until shrimp turn pink and
are just cooked through, about 4 minutes. Transfer shrimp and sauce
to shallow bowls, dividing equally, and serve.

Bon Apptit September 1995
