Title: Fried Zucchini Blossoms
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 recipe
-- grated
1 tb Finely chop'd fresh cilantro
16    Zucchini blossoms; pistils
-- removed if desired *
Canola oil for deep-frying
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Curry powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Chilled club soda
1/4 lb Mozzarella cheese; coarsely
Mix together flour, curry powder, and salt.  Add club soda; mix
until smooth.  Let batter stand 10 minutes.
In a small bowl toss mozzarella and cilantro.  Stuff 1 tablespoon
into each blossom, pressing ends of blossom closed.
Heat 1 inch oil in skillet (375Ã¸ F).  Dip the filled blossoms one
at a time in batter.  Coat completely.  Fry, turning once, until
golden and crisp.  Drain on paper towels; sprinkle with salt.  Serve
warm.
