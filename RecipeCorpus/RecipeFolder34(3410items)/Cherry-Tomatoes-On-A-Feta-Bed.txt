Title: Cherry Tomatoes On A Feta Bed
Categories: Israeli, Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Zaatar (hyssop) *
-olive oil
2 c  Feta cheese
30    Cherry tomatoes, halved
Crumble the cheese, and spread on a 9 inch by 13 inch pan or a nice
circular clay pan. Sprinkle half of the zaatar over the cheese.
Arrange the tomatoes, cut side down, on the cheese. Sprinkle with
olive oil and the remaining zaatar. Bake at 450 degrees for 15
minutes; serve warm.

Original recipe by Gabi Shahar, February 1994.

* Zaatar is a popular middle-eastern condiment.
