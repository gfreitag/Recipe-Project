Title: Flavored Mocha Mix *
Categories: Mixes, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
1/2 c  Cocoa unsweetened
1/4 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Almond extract
1 1/2 c  Non-dairy creamer
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Instant coffee
In large bowl, combine all ingredients except extracts; mix well.
Place 1 1/2 cups of mixture in small bowl. Add vanilla extract; mix
well. Add almond extract to remaining mixture; mix well. Store each
in tightly covered container. To serve, combine 3 tbls of desired mix
with 1 cup boiling water; stir to blend. Garnish with whipped cream.
