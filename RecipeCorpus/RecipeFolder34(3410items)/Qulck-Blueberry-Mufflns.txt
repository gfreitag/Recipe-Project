Title: Qulck Blueberry Mufflns
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
2 tb Sugar
1 c  Vanilla ice cream --
Softened
1 c  Self-rising flour
1 c  Fresh blueberries
In a medium bowl, mix ice cream and flour. Fold in blueberries. Spoon
into six greased muffin cups. Bake at 375 deg. for 20-25 minutes or
until muffins test done. While hot, brush muffin tops with butter and
sprinkle with sugar. Serve warm. Yield: 6 servings.
