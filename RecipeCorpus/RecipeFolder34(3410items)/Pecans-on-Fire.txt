Title: Pecans on Fire
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 pound
1 lb Extra-large pecan halves
1 c  Confectioners' sugar
1 qt Canola oil
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 1/4 ts Sweet paprika
1 1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 1/2 ts Black pepper
1. Mix the seasonings in a large bowl. Blanch the pecans in boiling
water for 5 minutes, then drain well. Coat the nuts with the
confectioners' sugar.

2. Heat the oil in a large pot over moderately high heat until it
reaches 350F. Add the pecans and fry until golden brown, about 4
minutes. With a slotted spoon, transfer the pecans to the seasoning
mixture and toss until well coated. Let cool completely before
serving.
