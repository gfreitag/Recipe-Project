Title: Pizza Bianco~
Categories: Low cal/fat, Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Chopped fresh broccoli or
Cauliflower
1 sm Onion -- chopped
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Mild yellow pepper rings --
Drained (optional)
Refrigerated pizza crust
1 ts Oil
1    10 oz can
1 ts Italian seasoning
1 c  (4 oz) shredded part-skim
-Mozzarella cheese
8 oz Turkey & cheddar smoked
Sausage -- cut into 1/4"
Slices
Cooking Time  15 min.
Prep Time:  15 min.

Heat oven to 425 degrees. Brush 1/2 tsp oil on shallow 10x15-inch
baking pan. Unroll pizza dough and place on pan; press dough evenly
across pan bottom to form crust, rolling up edges. Lightly brush top
of dough with remaining oil; sprinkle with seasoning. Prick crust
generously with fork. Bake 5 minutes. Remove crust from oven;
sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Top with sausage slices and
remaining ingredients. Bake 12 minutes or until crust is browned.
Makes 6 servings.

Per serving:  Cal 315; fat 10 g; protein 19 g; cholesterol 35 mg;
carbohydrate 36 g; sodium 845 mg.
