Title: Onion and Cider Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Port
2 ts Thyme leaves
Salt & pepper to taste
12    Thick slices sourdough
Baguette
3 c  Grated Oka cheese
1/4 c  Butter
2 lb Spanish onions; sliced
1 ts Sugar
1/4 c  Flour
8 c  Beef stock
2 c  Apple cider
In Dutch oven, melt butter over medium-high heat. Saute onions and
sugar 25 minutes, or until golden in colour.
Sprinkle with flour; cook, stirring 3 minutes. Stir in stock, cider,
ort and thyme; bring to boil, stirring frequently. Reduce heat and
simmer, covered 10 minutes. Add salt and peppr to taste.
Ladle soup into 6 ovenproof soup bowls; set on baking sheet. Top
each with 2 bread slices; sprinkle with 1/2 cup of the cheese. Broil
until cheese melts. Serve immediately.
Source: Vancouver Sun  March 15/95
