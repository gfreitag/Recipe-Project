Title: Cheesy Vegetable Orzo
Categories: Vegetarian, Pasta/rice
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Dried basil
1/2 c  Orzo
2 oz Cheddar cheese, shredded --
-extra sharp
1    Onion -- chopped
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
2 c  Chicken broth
1/2 c  Green beans
Heat a skillet, then add half a teaspoon oil if skillet isn't
nonstick. Saute the onion and garlic about two minutes, then add
broth, vegetable, and basil. Bring to a boil. Add the orzo, reduce
the heat to a simmer, cover the skillet and cook about 15 minutes,
stirring occasionally. Remove from heat, stir in cheese until melted,
cover the skillet again, and let sit for about 2 minutes. Serves 2.
