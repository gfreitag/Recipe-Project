Title: Spicy Hard Cooked Eggs In Coconut Milk
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
6 tb Lime juice
1 c  Coconut milk -- unsweetened
4 ds Worcestershire sauce
3 tb Curry powder
Salt and pepper
8    Hard-boiled egg -- shelled
3 tb Virgin olive oil
1 sm Red onion -- diced
1 tb Ginger root -- minced
1 ts Garlic -- minced
1 tb Chili pepper -- minced
1 tb Brown sugar
1/2 c  Pineapple juice
In large saute pan, heat the oil over medium high heat until hot but
not smoking.  Add the onion and saute, stirring frequently, for 3 to
4 minutes. Add the ginger, garlic, and chili pepper and saute,
stirring for 1 additional minute.  Add the brown sugar, pineapple
juice and lime juice and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes.
Add the coconut milk, worcestershire sauce, curry powder, salt and
pepper.

Bring to a boil, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 10 minutes;
the mixture should thicken slightly.  Add the whole shelled eggs,
cook for 3 minutes and serve.
