Title: "Smothered" Steak
Categories: Crockpot, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
3 tb Soy sauce
1 lg Onion, sliced
10 oz Package frozen
Green beans, French-style
(optional)
1    To 2 green peppers, sliced
16 oz Can tomatoes
1 1/2 lb Chuck or round
Steak, cut in strips
1    4 oz can mushrooms,
Drained
1/3 c  Flour
2 tb Molasses
(optional)
1 ts Salt
Put steak strips, flour, salt and pepper in CROCK-POT. Stir well to
coat steak. Add all remaining ingredients. Cover and cook on Low 8
hours. (High: 4 hours). Serve with rice. From Rival Crock-pot cook
book, date unknown
