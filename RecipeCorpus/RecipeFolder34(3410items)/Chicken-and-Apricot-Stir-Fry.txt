Title: Chicken and Apricot Stir Fry
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 2 servings
3 tb Orange juice
1 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Cornstarch
1/4 ts Grated gingerroot
1 ts Peanut or canola oil
1 ea Garlic clove
2 ea Chicken breast halves
3 ea Apricots
1 tb Scallion, chopped
Cube boned, skinned chicken breast.  Pit and slice apricots. Saute
garlic in oil, add chicken, stir fry.  Add ginger, stir fry. Add
apricots, and scallions.  Stir fry. Combine orange juice, soy sauce,
and cornstarch. Stir to coat.
