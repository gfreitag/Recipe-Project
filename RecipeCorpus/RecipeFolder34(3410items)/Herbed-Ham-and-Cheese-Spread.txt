Title: Herbed Ham and Cheese Spread
Categories: Dips/spread, Appetizers, Low cal/fat, Snacks
Yield: 1 cup
-drained
2 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/2 ts Dried basil
3/4 c  1% low-fat cottage cheese
1/2 pk (8-oz.) Healthy Choice fat-
-free cream cheese; softened
1/3 c  Finely chopped Healthy
-Choice deli-style ham
2 oz (1 jar) Diced pimiento;
Place cottage cheese in container of an electric blender; process
until smooth.  Transfer to a small bowl; add cream cheese and
remaining ingredients, stirring well.  Cover and chill thoroughly.
Serve with melba toast rounds or fresh raw vegetables.  Yield:  1 cup.

PER TABLESPOON:  CALORIES 20; FAT 0.3g; CARB 0.9g; PROTEIN 3.1g;
CHOLESTEROL 4mg; SODIUM 123mg.

From:  Healthy Choice CHOICES FOR LIVING HAPPIER AND HEALTHIER, Fall
1995.
