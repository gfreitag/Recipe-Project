Combine all ingredients except pasta in a large sauce pan.  Cover and
bring in to a boil.  Cook until clams just open, stirring
occasionally. Keep warm.  Cook pasta according to cooking
instructions. Drain, add to clams mixture.  Toss together and season
to taste. (If using canned clams, drain and reserve.  Add when
tossing pasta with sauce, just to heat through.) Enjoy.
