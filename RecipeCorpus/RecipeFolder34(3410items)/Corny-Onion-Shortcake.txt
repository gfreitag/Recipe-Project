Title: Corny Onion Shortcake
Categories: Vegetables, Country wom
Yield: 9 servings
1 c  Corn, cream-style
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz.
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- 4 oz.
Shredded divid
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 lg Onion -- sweet finely
Chopped
1 1/2 c  Corn muffin mix
1 ea Egg
1/3 c  Milk
In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Saute onion until tender.
Remove from the heat and set aside. In a bowl, combine muffin mix,
egg, milk and corn. Spread into a greased 9-in. baking pan. Combine
sour cream, salt and half the cheddar cheese; spread over the batter.
Sprinkle with the remaining cheese. Bake at 425 deg. for 30 minutes.
Cut into squares; serve warm. Yield: about 9 servings
