Title: Cheap Chili
Categories: Main dish, Chili
Yield: 9 servings
1 lb Gr. beef or turkey
Chili seasoning of your
-choice
1    Can of tomato juice
2    Cans of kidney beans
2    Cans of fr. cut gr. beans
1    Can of corn
DUMP IT ALL TOGETHER, HEAT TO BOILING, SERVE WITH CORNBREAD,
CRACKERS, OR ROLLS.  THIS FEEDS ABOUT 9 HUNGRY PEOPLE.
