Title: Persimmon Roll
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Chopped dates
1 c  Persimmon pulp
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Vanilla wafer crumbs
Mix all ingredients and bake 1 hour in a 300 degree oven in 1 pound
coffee tin.  Remove from can when cool,  roll in crushed nuts.
