Title: Grilled Chicken Breasts With Cilantro Pecan Pesto
Categories: Poultry, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
Salt and pepper
4    Boneless skinless chicken
-breasts
1/4 c  Oil
Salt and pepper
1 c  Cilantro -- loosely packed
1 tb Garlic -- minced
1/4 c  Pecans -- chopped
6 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Lime juice
6 ds Tabasco sauce
Make the pesto: combine first 7 ingredients in a food processor and
process until a rough paste is formed.  Cover and set aside.  Coat
the chicken lightly with oil on both sides and sprinkle with salt and
pepper. Grill the chicken over a medium fire, 7 to 9 minutes per
side.  Remove the chicken from the fire, top each breast with a
generous tbsp of pesto. Serve with Red Cabbage Slaw.
