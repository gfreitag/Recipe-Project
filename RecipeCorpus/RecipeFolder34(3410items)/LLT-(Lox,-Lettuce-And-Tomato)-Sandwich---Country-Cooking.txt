Title: LLT (Lox, Lettuce And Tomato) Sandwich - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 lb Thinly sliced smoked saimon
-(lox)
1 sm Red onion, thinly sliced
1 1/2 ts Drained capers (opt.)
Fresh dill sprigs
Lemon wedges (opt.)
4    Poppy seed or plain bagels,
-split in half
3 tb Light cream cheese or
-Neufchatel cheese
Lettuce leaves (Boston,
-bibb, escarole, or romaine)
1 lg Or 4 cherry tomatoes, sliced
1  Place bottoms of bagels on 4 sandwich plates. Divide cream cheese
among bagels.

2. Top each bagel bottom with lettuce, cover with tomato slices,
salmon, onion, capers (if desired), and dill; add remaining bagel
half.

3. Serve "LLT" with lemon wedges, if desired.
