Title: La Raiders Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Green pepper -- chopped
1 tb Red pepper -- chopped
1 ts Dijon mustard
ds Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Onion -- chopped
2 tb Dill pickle relish
2 tb Parsley flakes
Combine all ingredients.  Chill 30 minutes before serving.  Serve with
chicken nuggets.
