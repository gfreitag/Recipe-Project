Title: Lo Cal Chili Con Carne
Categories: Low cal/fat, Main dish, Chili
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Salt
2 ts Chili powder
1    Bay leaf
2 c  Tomato sauce
16 oz Beans; cooked
8 oz Ground beef; cooked
4 oz Onion; chopped
Combine ingredients in a med. covered saucepan and simmer, covered,
for 30 minutes. Freezes well.
