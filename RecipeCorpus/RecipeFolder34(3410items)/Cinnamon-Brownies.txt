Title: Cinnamon Brownies
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 36 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 1/3 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips --
(6 oz)
3/4 c  Cocoa powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
2/3 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted divided
1/2 c  Boiling water
2 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs -- beaten
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
1 ts Ground cinnamon -- or 1 1/2
Teaspoons
1 1/3 c  Evaporated milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
6 tb Butter or margarine --
Softened
1/2 c  Cocoa powder
2 2/3 c  Confectioner's sugar
In a mixing bowl, combine cocoa and baking soda; blend in 1/3 cup
melted butter. Add boiling water, stirring until thickened. Stir in
sugar, eggs, vanilla and remaining butter. Add flour, cinnamon and
salt. Fold in chocolate chips. Pour into a greased 13x9x3 inch baking
pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 40 min. or until brownies test done. Cool.
For frosting, cream butter in a mixing bowl. Combine cocoa, sugar and
cinnamon; add alternately with the milk. Beat to a spreading
consistency; add vanilla. Add more milk if necessary. Spread over the
brownies. Yield: 3 dozens.
