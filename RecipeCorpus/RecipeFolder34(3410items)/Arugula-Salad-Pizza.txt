Title: Arugula Salad Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Red onion -- chopped
2 tb Parmesan -- grated
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Marinara sauce
1    12" pizza crust
6 oz Fresh mozzarella -- slice
Thin
4 c  Arugula or watercress;
Wash -- chop coarse
3/4 c  Plum tomatoes -- diced
Preheat oven to 450~. Spread sauce over crust; sprinkle with
mozzarella. Bake 10 minutes. Meanwhile combine arugula, tomaotes,
onions, Parmesan, lemon juice, olive oil, salt and pepper in bowl;
toss. Spoon salad on center of hot pizza. Per Slice: 310 Cal, 9 gr
Fat.
