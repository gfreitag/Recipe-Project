Title: BENNE-SEED WAFERS - Country Living
Categories: Breads, Country liv, Appetizers
Yield: 4 dozen wafe
3 tb Vegetable shortening
2/3 c  Sesame seeds, toasted
1    Large egg
3 tb Milk
1 1/2 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter
1. In medium-size bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt. With pastry
blender or 2 knives, cut in butter and shortening until mixture
resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in sesame seeds.

2. In small bowl, beat together egg and milk; reserve 2 T mixture for
glaze. Mixing lightly with fork, add remaining egg mixture to flour
mixture until soft dough forms. Turn dough out onto lightly floured
surface and knead for 1 minute.

3. Heat oven to 350'F. Grease 2 large baking sheets. On floured
surface, roll out dough to 1/8-inch thickness. Using lightly floured,
2 1/2-inch round or holiday cookie cutters, cut out dough into
wafers. Knead trimmings together and re-roll to cut out more wafers.

4. Arrange wafers on greased baking sheets; brush tops with reserved
egg mixture. Bake 12 to 14 minutes or until bottoms brown slightly.
Cool wafers completely on sheets on wire rack. Store wafers in
airtight container.
