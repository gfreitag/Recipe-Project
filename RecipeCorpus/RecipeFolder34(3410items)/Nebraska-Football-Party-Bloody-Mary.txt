Title: Nebraska Football Party Bloody Mary
Categories: Beverages, Mixed drink, Alcohol
Yield: 1 bunch
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Lime juice
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Soy sauce
10 dr Tobasco sauce (or to taste)
46 oz Can of V8
6 oz Vodka
6 oz Kraut juice (if you can't
-find juice, drain some
-sauerkraut)
1 ts Horseradish
MMMMM----------------------FOR RIM OF GLASS---------------------------
Margarita salt
1    Fresh lemon
1.  Mix all INGREDIENTS together. Rub rim of glass with fresh lemon
slice. Dip rim of glass into Margarita salt, shaking off excess salt.

2. Put ice in glass.  Pour in Bloody Mary mix.

3. DRINK!

4. FALL DOWN!

5. GET UP!

6. REFILL GLASS!

REPEAT STEPS 3, 4, 5, and 6 until you cannot repeat number 5!

Recipe from Troy Green, FIDO Recipes Echo.  Typed in Meal-Master
format by Arthur Cloninger, Huntington, WV.
