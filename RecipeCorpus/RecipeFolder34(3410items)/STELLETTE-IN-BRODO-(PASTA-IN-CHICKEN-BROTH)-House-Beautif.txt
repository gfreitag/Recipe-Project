Title: STELLETTE IN BRODO (PASTA IN CHICKEN BROTH)-House Beautif
Categories: House beaut, Soup/stew, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Freshly chopped chives,
-tarragon, basil, or chervil
Salt & freshly ground black
-pepper to taste
1/4 c  Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese, preferably Reggiano
4 c  Good-quality chicken broth,
-preferably homemade (see
-separate recipe, OR low
-sodium canned
6 oz Dried niiniature soup pasta:
-small bowties, alphabets,
-or stellette
In a stockpot boil chicken broth and cook pasta until it is al dente,
about 3 to 4 minutes.

Remove pan from heat. Evenly divide soup among 4 warmed soup plates,
garnish each portion with herbs, salt, pepper, and cheese. Serve
imniediately. Serves 4.

House Beautiful/May/94  Scanned & fixed by DP & GG
