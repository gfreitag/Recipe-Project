Title: Yankee Baked Hash
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 3 servings
Salt and pepper
Milk and any leftover gravy
Butter
2 1/2 c  Leftover beef roast
1 sm Onion
1    Sprig of parsley
3    Freshly baked potatoes
Put meat, onion and parsley through your food processor.  Add baked
potato. Do not chop too fine!  Salt and pepper to taste.  Put meat
mixture in a casserole dish and fill almost to top with milk and
gravy or just milk. Dot with butter and bake in a 325 degree oven for
1 1/2 hours, until a brown crust forms on top.  Do not overbake! Use
about a 2 quart casserole. Randy Rigg
