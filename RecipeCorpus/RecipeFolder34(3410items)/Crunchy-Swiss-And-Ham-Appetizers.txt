Title: Crunchy Swiss And Ham Appetizers
Categories: Appetizers, Country wom
Yield: 96 servings
1/4 c  Minced onions
1 ea Eggs -- well beaten
1 tb Mustard -- prepared
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 1/2 c  Corn flake crumbs
2 c  Mashed potatoes -- very
Stiff
2 c  Cooked ham -- finely
Chopped
1 c  Swiss cheese -- shredded 4
Oz
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
Combine all ingredinets except corn flakes; chill. Shape into 1 inch
balls and roll in corn flakes. place on greased cookie sheet and bake
at 350 deg. for 25 to 30 min. Serve while hot. Yield: about 8 dozen.
