Title: Fresh Apricot Salsa^
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit, Condiments
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Apricot preserves
1 md Tomato, chopped
1 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
1 ts Finely chopped gingerroot
1/8 ts Cinnamon
Fast & Healthy 9/92
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
2/3 c  Chopped fresh apricots
**OR**
1 cn (16oz) apricots, drained and
Chopped
Combine; mix well. chill until serving time. Makes 2 cups.

Per two tablespoons: 20 calories, 5 g carbohydrate, 50 mg potassium,
1/2 fruit exchange.
