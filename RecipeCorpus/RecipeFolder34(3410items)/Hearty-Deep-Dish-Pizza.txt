Title: Hearty Deep Dish Pizza
Categories: Main dish, Low cal/fat
Yield: 12 servings
-crushed
8 oz Fat-free natural Mozzarella
-cheese, Healthy Choice
1 c  Onion rings
1 c  Green pepper rings
8 oz Fat-free Healthy Choice --
-shredded pizza chees
1    Loaf frozen bread dough
-thawed to room temperature
1 c  Pasta sauce -- Healthy
-Choice
1    Garlic clove -- minced or
1 ts Dried oregano leaves --
-crushed
1 ts Dried basil leaves --
Heat oven to 450 F.  Press bread dough onto bottom and 1" up sides of
13" x 9" baking pan that has been sprayed with non-stick cooking
spray. Bake at 450 F. for 10 minutes. Top wtih mozzarella cheese.
Combine pasta sauce, garlic, oregano and basil; spread over cheese.
Top with onions, green peppers and mushrooms; sprinkle with shredded
pizza cheese. continue baking at 450 F. for 10 to 15 minutes, or
until cheese melts and crust is deep golden brown. 12 servings, each
206 calories, 16 g protein, 24 g carbo., 1 g total fat, 0 g saturated
fat, 10 mg cholesterol, 469 mg sodium.
