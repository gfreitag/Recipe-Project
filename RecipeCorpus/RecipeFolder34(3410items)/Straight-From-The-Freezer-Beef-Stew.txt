Title: Straight-From-The-Freezer Beef Stew
Categories: Crockpot, Soup/stew, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1    12-ounce can beer
1    1O-ounce package frozen
-mixed vegetables
1    4-ounce can mushrooms,
-drained
2 1/2 lb Frozen stew meat
1    1 -3/8-ounce package
-dry onion soup mix
1    1 1/2 -ounce package
-brown gravy mix
In CROCK-POT combine soup and gravy mixes with  beer. Add frozen stew
meat, vegetables and mushrooms, pushing down into liquid to moisten.
Cover and cook on Low 10 to 14 hours. From Rival Crock-pot cook book,
