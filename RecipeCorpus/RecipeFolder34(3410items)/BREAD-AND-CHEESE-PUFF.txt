Title: BREAD AND CHEESE PUFF
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
4 ea Eggs, beaten
1 qt Milk
1/2 ts Worchestershire sauce
ds Tobasco sauce
8 sl Bread, enriched
8 sl American cheddar cheese
Salt
Pepper
1 ts Accent
Place 4 slices of bread on bottom of shallow baking dish, cutting to
fit bottom.
Cover bread with half the cheese; sprinkle with salt, pepper, and
1/2 tsp Accent.  Repeat.
Combine remaining ingredients. Pour over bread and cheese.  Bake in
350 F. oven 40 minutes or until top is golden brown, puffed and
shiny.  Serve immediately.
