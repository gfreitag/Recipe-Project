Title: Meatza Pizza Pie
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
1    Jar sliced mushrooms
2 tb Green pepper -- chopped
2 tb Onion -- chopped
1 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
1 lb Ground round
1/2 c  Fresh breadcrumbs
1    Egg
2 ts Instant beef bouillon
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
1/2 c  Pasta  sauce
Preheat oven to 350 F.  In medium bowl, combine meat, crumbs, egg,
bouillon and seasoning.  Mix well.  Press evenly on bottom and up
side to rim of 9 inch pie plate to form crust. Bake 15 minutes; pour
off fat. Spoon pasta sauce over crust.  Top with mushrooms, green
pepper, onion ans cheese. Bake 10 minutes longer or until cheese
melts. Garnish as desired. Refrigerate leftovers.
