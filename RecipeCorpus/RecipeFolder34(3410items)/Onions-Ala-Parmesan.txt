Title: Onions Ala Parmesan
Categories: Diabetic, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Margarine; (OR LESS)
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese;
8 md Onions; sliced
1/4 c  Butter (OR LESS) -=OR=-
Saute onion slices in butter 10 - 12 minutes.  Remove to baking dish.
Sprinkle with cheese over top.  Broil 5" from heat until cheese melts.
Makes 6 servings.
