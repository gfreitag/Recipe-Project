Title: Easy Pizza Crust 3
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
2/3 c  Beer or club soda
1 tb Oil
1 c  Self-rising flour
3 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
In 2qt bowl mix 1st 3 ingredients just till combined. Pour in beer
(Club Soda) & oil & stir until smooth & thick (like biscuit dough).
Spread over 12 or 13" round pizza pan sprayed with Pam. Bake 375 for
15 min (without toppings).
