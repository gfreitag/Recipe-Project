Title: Beef And Cabbage Stew
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Chopped cabbage
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/2 ts Dried marjoram
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 cn Kidney beans -- 16oz rinsed
& draine
1 cn Beef broth -- 14oz
1 cn Crushed tomatoes -- 16oz
In a Dutch oven, cook beef and onion until meat is browned and onion
is tender; drain. In a small bowl, mash 1/4 cup beans with 1/4 cup
beef broth. Add to Dutch oven with remaining beans and broth,
tomatoes, cabbage and seasonings. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes or
until the cabbage is tender. Yield: 6-8 servings
