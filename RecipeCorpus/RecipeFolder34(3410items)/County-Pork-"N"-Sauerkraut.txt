Title: County Pork "N" Sauerkraut
Categories: Vegetables, Meats, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Brown sugar
2 ts Caraway seed
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Pepper
2 lb Pork country-style ribs
1 md Onion -- chopped
1 tb Cooking oil
1 cn Sauerkraut -- 14 oz drained
1 c  Applesauce
In a dutch oven, cook ribs and onion in oil until ribs are browned and
onion is tender. Remove from the heat. Combine remaining ingredients
and pour over ribs. Cover and bake at 350 deg. for 1 1/2 to 2 hours
or until ribs are tender. Yield: 4 servings.
