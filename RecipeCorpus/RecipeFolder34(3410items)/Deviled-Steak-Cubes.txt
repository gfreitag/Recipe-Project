Title: Deviled Steak Cubes
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea Onion
16 oz Tomato sauce
2 ts Dry mustard
2 tb Worchestershire Sauce
1 lb Round steak
2 tb Flour
1/2 ts Pepper
2 ts Canola oil
Cut round steak into cubes, dredge in mix of flour and pepper. Brown
in oil. Add onion, tomato sauce, mustard, worch. sauce. Cover, and
simmer 30 to 40 minutes or until tender.
