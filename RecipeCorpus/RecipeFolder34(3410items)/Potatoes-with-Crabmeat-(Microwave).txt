Title: Potatoes with Crabmeat (Microwave)
Categories: Side dish, Potatoes, Microwave, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
Pepper
1/3 c  Sour cream
2 tb Chopped fresh dill
1 3/4 lb Firm, waxy potatoes; thickly
-sliced
Salt
1/4 lb Crabmeat
Pour 1 cup of water into the lower compartment of a microwave
steamer and heat on high for 2 minutes.
Put the potatoes in the upper compartment of the steamer and add
salt. Put the steamer into the microwave, cover, and cook on HIGH for
17 minutes.
Put the crabmeat in a bowl and pour the lemon juice over it.  Season
with salt and pepper.  Add the sour cream and stir well.  Heat for 1
minute on high.
Drain the potatoes and transfer to a vegetable dish.  Add pepper.
Pour the crab sauce over and stir carefully.  Sprinkle with chopped
dill and serve hot.
