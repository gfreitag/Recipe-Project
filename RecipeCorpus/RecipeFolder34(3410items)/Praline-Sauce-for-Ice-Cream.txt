Title: Praline Sauce for Ice Cream
Categories: Ice cream, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Vanilla
1 c  Heavy cream
Pecans
1 lb Light brown sugar
1 ea Pt white Karo syrup
1/2 c  Butter
Melt sugar, karo, and butter on low fire until smooth.  Cool.  Add
vanilla and cream.  Stir in really well.  Serve over ice cream with
pecans on top of sauce.  1 qt.  Keeps for 1 year in refrigerator.
(by Ellen Cleary)
