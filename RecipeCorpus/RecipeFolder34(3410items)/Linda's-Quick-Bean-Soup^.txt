Title: Linda's Quick Bean Soup^
Categories: Soup/stew, Low cal/fat, Healthy
Yield: 13 servings
2 cn (14 1/2oz) unsalted whole
Tomatoes, undrained,
Chopped
1 cn (15oz) chick peas,
Undrained
1 cn (13 3/4oz) no salt chicken
Broth
6 c  Tightly packed chopped fresh
Kale
1 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Onion powder
3 cn (16oz) red kidney beans,
Undrained
Combine all ingredients in a large Dutch oven; bring to a boil. Cover;
reduce heat and simmer 1 hour. Serving size: 1 cup

Per serving: 210 cal., 12.9 g protein, 1.3g (6%) fat, 38.6 g carb.,
4.9 g fiber, 0 mg chol., 4.3 mg iron, 438 mg sodium, 102 mg calcium.
