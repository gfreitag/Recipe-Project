Title: Apple Compote
Categories: Desserts, Israeli, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
Juice from a half lemon
1    Vanilla bean
- split lengthwise
2 lb Golden Delicious apples
4 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar
Peel, core and quarter the apples. Put all the ingredients in a large
pan. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and cook 15 minutes,
until apples are slightly soft. Cool and refrigerate. Serve cold.

Recipe by Gabi Shahar.
