Title: Spicy Pepperoni Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
2 oz Pepperoni -- sliced
8    Ripe olives; pitted --
Sliced
2 tb Green onions -- sliced
4    6 inch prepared pizza
Crusts
1 cn Pizza style tomatoes
2 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
Preheat oven to 450 F.  Place crusts on baking sheet. Spread tomatoes
evenly over crusts.  Layer 1/2 of the cheese, then pepperoni, olives
and green onions.  Top with remaining cheese. Bake 6 to 8 minutes or
until hot and bubbly.
