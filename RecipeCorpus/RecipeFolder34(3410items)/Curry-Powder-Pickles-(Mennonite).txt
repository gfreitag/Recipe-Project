Title: Curry Powder Pickles (Mennonite)
Categories: Pickles, Can/preserv, Vegetables, Mennonite
Yield: 1 bunch
3 c  Sugar
1 tb Celery seed
2 tb Mustard seed
2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Powdered alum
1/2 ea Peck of cucumbers; each
-about 2-inches long
1/2 c  Salt
Water to cover
1 qt Vinegar
Wash cucumbers and cover with salt water. Let stand 3 hours and then
drain. Combine vinegar, sugar, alum, and spices. Bring to a boil and
add cucumbers. When the boiling point has been reached again, pack
pickles in hot
jars and seal.

Mrs. Paul Weldy of Indiana. _Mennonite Community Cookbook_ Mary Emma
Showalter  1950 The Mennonite Community Association ISBN 87883-0411
