Title: TURKEY WITH MUSTARD SANDWICH - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Sandwich, Poultry, Appetizers
Yield: 4 sandwiches
-crusts removed, cut in half
1 tb Whole-grain prepared mustard
Parsley sprigs
1/2    6 oz pk thin-sliced turkey
-breast, rolled up
2    Slices whole wheat bread,
On serving platter, place one slice of rolled turkey on each piece of
bread; top with a dab of mustard and sprig of parsley.

Country Cooking/Summer/94  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
