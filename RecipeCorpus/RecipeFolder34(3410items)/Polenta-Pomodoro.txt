Title: Polenta Pomodoro
Categories: Italian
Yield: 20 servings
1 lb Polenta
20 oz Marinara sauce
9 c  Water
1 tb Salt
COMBINE WATER AND SALT IN A SAUCEPOT, OVER A HIGH FLAME BRING TO THE
BOIL AND SLOWLY STIR IN POLENTA HEAT AND STIR FOR 20-30 MINUTES,
UNTIL MIXTURE PULLS AWAY FROM SIDES OF POT POUR OUT ONTO A LIGHTLY
MOISTENED PLATTER HEAT MARINARA SAUCE AND POUR OVER POLENTA GARNISH
AS DESIRED SERVE HOT Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   08-20-95
