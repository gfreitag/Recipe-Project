Title: Molded Beet Salad #1
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Onion; minced
1/4 c  Vinegar; or lemon juice
Lettuce
Mayonnaise
Celery salt
1 tb Gelatin
1/4 c  Water; cold
3/4 c  Beets; diced
1 c  Oranges, mandarin
1/4 ts Salt
Soften gelatin in cold water for 5 minutes. Drain juice from beets and
oranges, ading enough water to make 1-1/4 cups liquid.  Heat juice to
boiling and add gelatin to this; then add salt, onion, and vinegar or
lemon juice (or combination of the two).  Chill.  when mixture is
slightly thickened, fold in beets and oranges.  Pour into 6
individual molds and chill until firm.  Unmolk on lettuce and serve
with mayonnaise with dash of celery salt.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
Coleman. Submitted By MICHELLE HOWE   On   08-27-95
