Title: Date Balls
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Rice Krispies
2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Pecans
2    Sticks margarine
1 c  Sugar
1 lb Dates, chopped
Mix margarine, sugar and dates.  Cook over low heat for 10 minutes.
Add Rice Krispies, pecans and vanilla.  Mix well.  Shape into balls
and roll twice in powdered sugar or coconut. Randy Rigg
