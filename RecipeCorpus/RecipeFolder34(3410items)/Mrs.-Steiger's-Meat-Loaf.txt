Title: Mrs. Steiger's Meat Loaf
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles, Low cal/fat, Poultry
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM-----------------C) 1994 BY SYLVIA STEIGER----------------------
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
1 c  Applesauce
1 lb Ground chicken
1 c  Oatmeal
1 tb Salt
Corn meal or bread crumbs may be substituted for oatmeal.  Preheat
oven to 350.  Mix together well and bake 1 hour.

Vary with chopped tomato, green pepper, parsley, celery.

Sylvia's comments:  The kids liked this, but it still tasted like
meat loaf to me, even made with ground chicken.
