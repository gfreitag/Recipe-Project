Title: Creamed Ham
Categories: Vegetables, Meats, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Dried parsley -- flaked
1 1/2 lb Cooked ham -- cubed
3 ea Hard-boiled eggs -- chopped
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
Toast points or biscuits
1 lb Asparagus -- fresh or
Frozen
Cut into 1 inch pieces
1 tb Cornstarch
1 1/2 c  Milk -- divided
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 ts Salt
In a saucepan cook asparagus in a small amount of water until tender;
drain and set aside. In a medium saucepan, mix cornstarch and 2
tablespoons milk. Add butter, salt, pepper and remaining milk; cook
and stir over medium heat until thickened and bubbly. Add parsley,
ham, eggs, cheese and asparagus; cook and stir over low heat until
ham is warmed and cheese is melted. Serve over toast point or
biscuits. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
