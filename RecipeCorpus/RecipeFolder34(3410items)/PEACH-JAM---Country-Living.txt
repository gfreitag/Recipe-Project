Title: PEACH JAM - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Dips/spread
Yield: 2 cups
1/4 ts Ground allspice
Pinch salt (opt.)
1 ts Fresh lemon juice
2 c  Chopped peeled peaches
3 tb Sugar
1 tb Cornstarch
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1. In 1-quart microwave-safe bowl; combine peeled peaches, sugar,
cornstarch, ginger, allspice, and salt, if desired. Microwave on high
(100 percent), uncovered, 3 minutes.

2. Stir mixture well and microwave on high 3 to 4 minutes or until
mixture thickens. With fork, coarsely mash any large chunks of fruit
and stir until mixture is spreadable.

3. Stir lemon juice into jam and set aside to cool slightly; cover and
refrigerate several flours or overnight, allowing mixture to thicken.

Nutritional information per T  without optional salt-protein: .07
gram; fat: .01 gram; carbohydrate: 3 grams; fiber: .2 gram; sodium: 1
milligram; cholesterol: 0; calories: 10,
