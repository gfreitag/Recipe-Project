Title: Mango and Grapes With Lime and Coconut
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Sweetened shredded coconut
1 tb Fresh lime juice
1 tb Honey
2    Ripe mangos (14 ounces each)
-peeled and cut in bite-
-size chunks
1 c  Seedless red grapes
Put all ingredients in a medium-size bowl and toss gently to mix.
Serves 4.  Per serving:  143 cal; 1g pro; 34g car; 2g fat; 0mg chol;
15mg sod. Total time 10 minutes.  Cost per serving:  63 cents.
