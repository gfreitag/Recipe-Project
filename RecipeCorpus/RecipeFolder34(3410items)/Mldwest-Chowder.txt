Title: Mldwest Chowder
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Flour
Black pepper to taste
2 c  Milk
10 oz Sharp cheddar cheese --
Grated
2 c  Cream-style corn
2 c  Potatoes -- diced
1/2 c  Carrots -- sliced
1/2 c  Celery -- sliced
1/4 c  Onions -- diced
1 ts Salt -- optional
2 c  Boiling water
1/4 c  Butter
Cook potatoes, carrots. celery and onions in boiling salted water for
10 minutes. While vegetables cook, melt butter, stir in flour and
pepper and cook for 1 minute. Gradually stir in milk; cook, stirring
constantly, until thickened. Add cheese, stirring until melted. Stir
in corn. Heat through but do not boil. Add reserved vegetables and
liquid. Yield: 8 servings.
