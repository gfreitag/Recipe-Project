Title: Greek Pasta Salad^
Categories: Greek, Salads, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
Fast & Healthy 8/92
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
Strips
1 md Tomato, cut in wedges
6 oz Feta cheese, crumbled (1
1/2    C)
8 oz (3c) uncooked tri-colored
Sprial macaroni
1 sm Green bell pepper, cut in
1/4    " strips
1 sm Red bell pepper, cut in 1/4"
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2 tb Chopped green onions
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Ried oregano leaves
3 tb White vinegar
1 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Chopped fresh basil
**OR**
2 ts Dried basil leaves
Cook macaroni to desired doneness. Drain; rinse with cold water.
Meanwhile, in blender or food processor, combine all dressing
ingredients and process until smooth.

Combine salad ingredients. Pour dressing over salad, toss gently.
Serve immediately. 5 (1 1/3 cup) servings.

Per serving: 310 calories, 12 g protein, 39 g carbohydrate, 2 g
fiber, 12 g fat, 32 mg cholesterol, 650 mg sodium, 230 mg potassium,
2 starch, 1 high fat meat, and 2 vegetable exchanges.
