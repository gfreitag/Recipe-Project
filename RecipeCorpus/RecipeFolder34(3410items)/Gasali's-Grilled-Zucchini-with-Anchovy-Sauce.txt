Title: Gasali's Grilled Zucchini with Anchovy Sauce
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
70 g  Parsley
Salt and pepper
1 1/2 kg Zucchini sliced
150 g  Preserved anchovy chopped
200 ml Olive oil
Grill the slices of zucchini, with salt and pepper. Beat the olive
oil with anchovy. Dress zucchini with oil. Sprinkle with parsley.

Source:  Given to share with the express permission of Chef de Cuisine
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.
