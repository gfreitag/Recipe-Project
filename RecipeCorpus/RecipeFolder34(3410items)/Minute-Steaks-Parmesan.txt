Title: Minute Steaks Parmesan
Categories: Beef, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
4 ea Cube steaks -- abt 4 oz. ea
2    Tablepoons
1 cn Pizza sauce -- 8 oz.
Butter or margarine
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
2 ts Water
1 ds Pepper
1/2 c  Saltine crackers -- finely
Crushed
In a shallow bowl, combine egg white, water and pepper. Set aside. On
a plate, combine cracker crumbs and cheese. Dip each cube steak into
the egg mixture, then coat with cracker/cheese mixture. In a large
skillet, melt butter; brown steaks on both sides. Add pizza sauce and
simmer for 3-5 minutes. Garnish with remaining crumb mixture. Serve
immediately. Yield: 4 servings
