Title: Cauliflower, Bean and Squash Curry
Categories: Indian, Curries, Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Dark raisins
1/2 c  Low sodium chicken broth
-or veg. broth
1 lb Green beans; trimmed
1    Yellow squash; cut in half
-lengthwise and sliced
1/2 c  Apple juice
1 tb Cornstarch
3 tb Butter, marg. or veg. oil;
-[divided]
2 tb Curry powder
1    Onion; cut into wedges
1 cl Garlic; minced
1 tb Fresh ginger; chopped
1/2    Cauliflower head; cut into
-florets
In a skillet over med high heat melt 2 tb butter. Add curry; cook 2
min stirring constantly. Add the remaining butter, onion, garlic and
ginger. Cook until onion is tender about 5-8 min. Add cauliflower,
raisins and broth. Cover; cook 7 min. Add beans and squash. Cover;
cook 7 min more. Combine apple juice and cornstarch; add to the
skillet and cook until sauce thickens, 1 min. 237 cal. From Women's
World magazine, July 3, 95. Adapted and posted by Jim Weller.
