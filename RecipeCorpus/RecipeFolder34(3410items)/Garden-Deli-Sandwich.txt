Title: Garden Deli Sandwich
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 6 servings
6    French rolls -- split
1 1/2 c  Lettuce -- shredded
12 oz Turkey breast slices
6 sl Cheese
1 sm Green pepper -- in rings
1 sm Red onion -- sliced
3/4 c  Miracle Whip+
1/3 c  Cucumber -- seeded & diced
1/3 c  Tomato -- seeded & diced
1/2 ts Dried basil -- crushed
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Mix dressing, cucumber, tomato and seasonings.  Spread cut side or
rolls with dressing mixture; fill with remaining ingredients.
