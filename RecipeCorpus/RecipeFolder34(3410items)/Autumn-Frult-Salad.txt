Title: Autumn Frult Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Drained
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Walnuts -- chopped
4 tb Frozen whipped topping
2 tb Mayonnaise
2 ea Bananas -- sliced
1 ea Apple
Cored and cut into chunks
2 tb Orange juice
1 cn Mandarin oranges -- 11 oz.
Toss banana and apple pieces with orange juice to prevent
discoloration. Drain any excess juice. Toss with all remaining
ingredients. Chill. Yield: 6 servlngs
