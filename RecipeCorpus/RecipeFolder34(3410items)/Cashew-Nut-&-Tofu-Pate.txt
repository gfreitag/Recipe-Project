Title: Cashew Nut & Tofu Pate
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
6 oz Tofu, drained
4 tb White wine -=OR=- water
2 tb Parsley, chopped
Salt, to taste
1 tb Olive oil
1 sm Onion, diced
1 sm Garlic clove, chopped
4 oz Cashews, toasted & ground
Heat the oil in a shallow skillet & saute the onion & garlic until
softened, about 5 minutes.  Add this to the nuts in a mixing bowl.
Mash in the tofu.  Stir in the wine or water, parsley & salt.  Press
the pate into indivdual ramekins, smooth the top of the mixture &
serve.

David Scott & Claire Golding, "The Vegan Diet"
