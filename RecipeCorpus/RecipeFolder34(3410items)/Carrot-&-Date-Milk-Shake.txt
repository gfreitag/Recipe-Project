Title: Carrot & Date Milk Shake
Categories: Beverages, Desserts
Yield: 5 cups
-softened slightly
x  Carrots and their green tops
-(optional)
2 c  Skim milk
1 c  Coarsely shredded carrots
1/2 c  Pitted dates; cut up
1 pt Frozen vanilla yogurt;
1.  In electric blender on low speed, blend milk, carrots, and dates
just until carrots and dates are very finely chopped.  Increase speed
to high and blend mixture until smooth.  Turn off blender.

2.  Add yogurt to carrot mixture.  Cover and blend just until frothy
and well mixed.  Pour into glasses; garnish with carrots and some
green tops, if desired.

Per 1-cup serving:  PROTEIN 10g; FAT 3g; CARBOHYDRATE 41g; FIBER 2g;
SODIUM 99mg; CHOLESTEROL 42mg; CALORIES 229; VITAMIN A 679 retinol
equivalents.

From:  COUNTRY LIVING magazine, 8/94.
