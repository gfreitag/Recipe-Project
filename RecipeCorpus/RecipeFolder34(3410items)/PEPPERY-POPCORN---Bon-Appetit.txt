Title: PEPPERY POPCORN - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Snacks
Yield: 4 quarts
1/8 tb Cayenne pepper
4 qt Unseasoned popped popcorn
-(about 3/4 C unpopped)
3 tb Melted butter
1/2 tb Salt
1/2 tb Paprika
1/8 tb Ground black pepper
1/8 tb Ground white pepper
Combine first five ingredients in small bowl. Place popcorn in large
bowl. Drizzle butter over popcorn, tossing until evenly coated.
Sprinkle 1/3 of seasoning over popcorn and combine well. Repeat 2
more times until popcorn is well coated. (Can be prepared hour ahead.
Store at room temperature.)

Bon Appetit/August/89  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
