Title: ASIAN GREEN BEAN SALAD
Categories: Vegetables
1 lb long green beans, trimmed,
cooked, -- and cooled
Yield: 1 Servings
:
1 ts crushed red pepper flakes
2 TB rice vinegar
1 ts chopped garlic
1/4 c  olive oil
2 TB sesame oil
2 c  cherry tomatoes, -- halved
2 TB crushed toasted peanuts
Salt and pepper
:

In a bowl toss all ingredients together. Season to taste with salt and
pepper. Serve at room temperature.
