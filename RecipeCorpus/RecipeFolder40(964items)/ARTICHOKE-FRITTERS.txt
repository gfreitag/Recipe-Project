Title: ARTICHOKE FRITTERS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 lb Artichoke hearts, cooked and
-diced
4    Eggs, separated
1 ts Baking powder
3    Green onions, chopped (green
-parts only)
1 tb Grated lemon peel
1/2 c  Flour
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tb Cornstarch
4 c  Oil for frying, Peanut or
-corn oil
Place artichoke hearts in a large bowl and stir in egg yolks and
baking powder.  Add green onion.  Fold in lemon peel. Mix in flour,
salt and pepper. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites and cornstarch
together until peaks form. Fold egg whites into artichoke mixture.
Heat oil to 360 degrees.  With a tablespoon, drop half dollar sized
dollops of fritter batter into oil. Fry until golden brown,
maintaining oil at 360 degrees. Remove fritters with a slotted spoon
and drain on paper towels. See recipes for Sfuzzi's Asparagus
Mushroom Salad and Basil Vinaigrette.
