Title: APPLESAUCE CINNAMON BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 999 Servings
1/3 c  Applesauce
3/4 c  Buttermilk
2 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 tb Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Butter
1 1/2 ts Yeast
Place all ingredients in pan in order listed (Hitachi machine).
Adjust as needed for your machine.

From Automatic Home Bakery Cookbook, Hitachi
