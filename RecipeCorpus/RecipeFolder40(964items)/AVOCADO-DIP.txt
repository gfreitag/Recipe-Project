Title: AVOCADO DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 10 Servings
1 ea RIPE AVOCADO (PEELED AND SEE
1 ea MEDIUM ONION (FINELY CHOPPED
1 c  MAYONNAISE
1/4 ts THYME
1/4 ts BASIL
1/4 ts ROSEMARY
1/4 ts SAGE
1/4 ts MARJORAM
8 oz SOUR CREAM
1 lb CRACKERS OR CHIPS
In a mixing bowl, mash avocado. Add onion,
mayonnaise, thyme, basil, rosemary, sage,
marjoram, and sour cream. Mix well. serve with
crackers or chips.
Yields 2 cups.
