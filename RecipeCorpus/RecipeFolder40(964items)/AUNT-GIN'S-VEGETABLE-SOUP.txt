Title: AUNT GIN'S VEGETABLE SOUP
Categories: Soups, Vegetables
Yield: 12 Servings
1 lb Flank steak cut up
-(or similar cut)
2    Potatoes; chopped
1 qt Canned tomatoes
1 pt Canned corn
1 pt Canned green beans
1    Onion, chopped
2 c  Chopped cabbage
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Celery
Salt & pepper to taste
Combine in crockpot.(you may not need all the juice from the corn &
gr. beans) Cook on low at least 8-10 hours. You can use home canned
or store bought vegetables. Walt MM
