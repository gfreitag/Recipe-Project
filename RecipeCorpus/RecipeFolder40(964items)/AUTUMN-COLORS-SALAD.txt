Title: AUTUMN COLORS SALAD
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
-aka Sweet Potato/Apple
-Salad
about 4 cups peeled, cubed sweet potatoes or yams 1 apple, skin on,
chopped 2 stalks celery, sliced 1 small red onion, chopped 1 teaspoon
fresh ginger, grated rice vinegar to taste

steam potatoes til soft, not mushy toss all ingredients lightly dress
with vinegar to taste chill From: Jagordon@agsm.Ucla.Edu
