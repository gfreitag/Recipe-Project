Title: AVOCADO FILLING:
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
45    Avocados, peeled and finely
-chopped
1 sm White onion, peeled and,
-finely chopped
1/4 c  Cilantro,, finely chopped
3 sm Serrano chiles or 1 small
-jalapeno chile,, finely
-chop
Juice of 2 limes
Sea salt, to taste
Freshly ground black
-pepper,, to taste
Mash the avocados with a fork and stir in the onions, cilantro,
chiles, and lime juice. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
