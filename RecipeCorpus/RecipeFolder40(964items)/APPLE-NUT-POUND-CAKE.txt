Title: APPLE-NUT POUND CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
3 ea Eggs
2 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 c  Oil
1 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Diced apples
3 c  Flour
1 c  Walnuts
2 c  Sugar
Beat eggs.  Add oil, vanilla & dry ingredients.  Fold in apples &
walnuts. Bake in greased & floured tube pan at 350 for 1 1/4 hrs.
