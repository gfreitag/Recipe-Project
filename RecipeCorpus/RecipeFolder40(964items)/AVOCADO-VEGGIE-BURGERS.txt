Title: AVOCADO VEGGIE BURGERS
Categories: None
Yield: 2 Servings
1 md Avocado
1 c  Cooked soybeans
1/2 sm Onion, chopped
1 ts Prepared mustard
1 tb Tomato puree
Salt, to taste
Whole wheat bread crumbs
1. Mix all the ingredients except bread crumbs together in a blender
or food processor, and blend until smooth.
Put mixture into a bowl and add bread crumbs until the mixture sticks
together enough to be formed into patties 2. Make two burger shaped
patties and fry in oil until they're browned. Serve on buns with your
favorite burger fixings!
