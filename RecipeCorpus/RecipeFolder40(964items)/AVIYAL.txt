Title: AVIYAL
Categories: Vegetarian, Indian
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Vegetarian, mixed
1/2 c  Coconut (grated)
2    Green/Red Chillies
2 ts Kozhambhu powder
2 c  Yogurt
Boil vegetables for about 5 minutes.  Add yogurt and powder to
vegetables. Bring to boil.
