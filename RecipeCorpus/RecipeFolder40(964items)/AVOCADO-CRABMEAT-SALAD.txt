Title: AVOCADO CRABMEAT SALAD
Categories: Seafood, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Crabmeat
2 tb Mayonnaise
3 tb Pickapepper sauce
1/4 lb Jalapeno cheese -- grated
1/4 lb Cheddar cheese -- grated
8    Hearts of palm
8    Artichoke hearts
1    Basket cherry tomatoes
1 lg Ripe avocado -- in 1/8's
2    Hard boiled eggs --
Quartered
Lettuce with:
Lemon wedges
Combine all ingredients and mix together. Add a little oil from the
artichokes as you mix.  Chill and serve on lettuce with lemon slices.
