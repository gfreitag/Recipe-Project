Title: AVOCADO ICE CREAM
Categories: Desserts, Fruits, Harned 1994
Yield: 1 Batch
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Lemon juice
14 1/2 oz Can evaporated milk
-- well chilled
1 c  Mashed ripe avocados
1 1/2 c  Sugar
Mix juices.  Beat milk in chilled bowl until almost doubled in
volume. Add all ingredients; blend well. Freeze. From _Our Best
Recipes_ by Lena E. Sturges, Food Editor. Birmingham, AL: Oxmoor
House, Inc., 1970. Pg. 132. Library of Congress Catalog Number
70-140493. Typed for you by Cathy Harned.
