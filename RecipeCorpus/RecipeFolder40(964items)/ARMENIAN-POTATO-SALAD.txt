Title: ARMENIAN POTATO SALAD
Categories: Armenian, Salad, Mint, Posted-mm
Yield: 4 Servings
4 md White potatoes
1 ts Salt
1 tb Dried mint
1/2 lg Purple onion, sliced
1/4 c  Salad oil
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
Source: Armenian and Selected Favorite Recipes
=======================================================
================== Boil potatoes. Slice when cool.
Add all the ingredients.
Toss and chill.
