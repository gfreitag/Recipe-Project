Title: APPLES & OAT CEREAL
Categories: Breakfast, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Water
1 1/3 c  Oat bran
1/2 c  Raisins
1    Apple, shredded
1 tb Maple syrup
1/2 ts Ground caraway seeds
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 c  (1-2) Skim milk
In a 2-quart saucepan, bring the water and oat bran to a vigorous
boil, stirring constantly.  Reduce the heat to low and cook for 2
minutes, stirring frequently, until thick. Remove from heat and stir
in the raisins, apples, maple syrup, caraway, and cinnamon. Let stand
5 minutes. Spoon into bowls and serve with the milk.
