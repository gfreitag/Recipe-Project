Title: APPLESAUCE TEA BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 20 Servings
2 1/2 c  White flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Cinnamon
8 oz Applesauce
1 ea Egg
1 c  Skim milk
Mix the dry ingredients together and add the applesauce, then the egg
and milk.  Pour into nonstick loaf pan and bake at 350F for 50 min.
Remove and cool. Cal: 77 (slice); Fat: 1/10g.
