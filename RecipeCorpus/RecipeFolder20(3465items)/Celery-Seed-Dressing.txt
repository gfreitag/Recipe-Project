Title: Celery Seed Dressing
Categories: Salad
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
1 t  Celery Seed OR Poppy Seed
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Lemon Juice
2 t  Cider Vinegar
1 t  Dry Mustard
In blender container, combine all ingredients except oil and seed;
blend until smooth.  On low speed, continue blending, slowly adding
oil. Stir in seed.
Chill to blend flavors. Makes about 1 cup. Sugguested Serving is to
serve with all types of fresh fruit salads.
