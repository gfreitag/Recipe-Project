Title: Rindergulasch (Beef Goulash)
Categories: German, Beef, Main dish, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  Paprika
1/4 t  Sugar
2 c  Water; Hot
1 T  Unbleached Flour
1/4 c  Water; Cold
1/2 c  Cream; Heavy
3 T  Vegetable Oil
1 lb Round Steak; Cubed
3 ea Onions; Medium, Chopped
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1/2 t  Garlic Salt
Heat vegetable oil in a large fry pan or Dutch oven.
Add meat cubes and brown well, approximately 10 minutes. Stir in
onions; cook until soft. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, garlic salt,
paprika, and sugar.
Blend thoroughly.  Pour in hot water; cover and simmer gently about
1 1/2 hours.  In a small jar or container, shake or blend flour with
cold water. Be sure to break up all lumps. Add to meat about 7
minutes before the end of the cooking time.  Stir constantly until
sauce is thickened and bubbling. Remove from heat; stir in cream.
Serve with noodles, accompanied by a tomato salad if desired.
