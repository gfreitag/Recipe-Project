Title: Cottage Cheese-Spinach Casserole
Categories: Casserole
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Low Fat Cheese Cubed
1/2 pk (10 Oz.) Frozen Chopped
Spinach, Partially Thawed
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Low Fat Cottage Cheese,
Rinsed & Drained
Egg Substitute Equal To 3
Eggs
3 tb Flour
Preheat Oven To 350 F. Combine Cottage Cheese, Egg Substitute &
Flour. Add Cheese.  Add Spinach in Chunks Along With Salt. Place in
Baking Dish & Bake Uncovered 60 Min.
Fat 5.0.  Chol. 15.
