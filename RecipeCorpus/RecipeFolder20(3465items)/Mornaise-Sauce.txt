Title: Mornaise Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 t  Pepper
2 ea Egg yolks
1 T  Whipping cream
1/4 c  Swiss cheese
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Flour
2 c  Milk
1/2 t  Salt
In microwave melt butter, add flour, stir until smooth, 1 minute.
Gradually add milk.  Beat egg yolks, add whipping cream. Gradually
add hot mixture. Add cheese at the end.
