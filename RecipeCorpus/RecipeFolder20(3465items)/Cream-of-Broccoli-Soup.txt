Title: Cream of Broccoli Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 12 servings
16 oz Sour cream
1 lb Cheddar cheese, grated
6 c  Water
1 c  Cashews, ground
1 ea Bunch broccoli
1 ea Vegetable bouillon cube
1/2 c  Butter
1/8 c  Soy sauce
3/4 lb Swiss cheese, grated
Saute  broccoli in butter for 10 minutes.   Using blender combine all
of the ingredients together.  As blender fills, transfer liquid to
soup pot and simmer for 20 to 30 minutes. Good with muffins or corn
bread.
