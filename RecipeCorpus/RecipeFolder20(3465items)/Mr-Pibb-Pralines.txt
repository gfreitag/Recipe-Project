Title: Mr Pibb Pralines
Categories: Candy, Beverage
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Marshmallows; Miniature
1 x  Pecan Halves; *
1 c  Sugar; Granulated
1 c  Brown Sugar; Dark, Packed
1 c  Mr. Pibb
*  Use 2 to 3 cups of pecan halves.
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++ Cook sugars and Mr. Pibb in a saucepan over low
heat, stirring constantly, then gradually birng to the soft ball
stage (240 degrees F on candy thermometer).  Remove mixture from
heat; stir in marshmallows and pecans. Mix vigorously until
marshmallows dissolve. Quickly drop by tablespoon on wax paper to
cool. Makes 2 dozen pralines. NOTE: If Mr Pibb is not available in
your area, use Dr. Pepper.
