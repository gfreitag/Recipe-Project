Title: Curried Garlic Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 4 servings
2 t  Curry Powder
2 t  Garlic Powder
1 c  Plain Yogurt
1 c  Sour Cream
1 t  Dry Mustard
Add each ingredient in the order listed, blending constantly until
all are well blended and the mixture is smooth. Cover and chill.
Makes about 2 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS: Zucchini, Mushrooms,
Cherry Tomatoes, Deli Meats, Chicken Drumettes
