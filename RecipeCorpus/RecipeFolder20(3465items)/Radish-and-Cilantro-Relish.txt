Title: Radish and Cilantro Relish
Categories: Relish, Vegetable, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
2 T  Fresh Cilantro; Fine Snipped
2 T  Vegetable Oil
1/4 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper; Freshly Ground
2 c  Radishes; Thinly Sliced
1/2 c  Onion; Chopped, 1 Med
3 T  Orange Juice
2 T  Lime Juice
Mix all ingredients in glass or plastic bowl.  Cover and refrigerate
at least 1 hour. Makes 3 cups of relish.
