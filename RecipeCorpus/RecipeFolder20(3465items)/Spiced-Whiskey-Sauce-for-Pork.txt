Title: Spiced Whiskey Sauce for Pork
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2    Cloves GARLIC; minced
1 ts WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE
1/4 ts POWDERED SUGAR
1/4 c  CORN OIL
1/4 c  JACK DANIEL'S WHISKEY
1/4 c  DIJON-STYLE MUSTARD
1/2 c  HONEY
1/4 c  SOY SAUCE
Combine all ingredients; mix well. Marinate pork in refrigerator
overnight. Grill or broil, basting often with marinade. Makes 1-1/2
cups. Shared by ELLIE COLLIN, Prodigy ID# CMKD93F.
