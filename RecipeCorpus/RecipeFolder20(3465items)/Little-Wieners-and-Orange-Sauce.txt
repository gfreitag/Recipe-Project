Title: Little Wieners and Orange Sauce
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 5 servings
8 ea Cloves whole
1/4 t  Cinnamon
1/4 c  Vinegar
2 pk Hot Dogs (cut into bites)
1/2 c  Sugar
3 T  Cornstarch
1 1/2 c  Orange juice
Put sugar, cornstarch, orange juice, cloves, cinnamon and vingar
in a saucepan and thicken on low heat. Once sauce has thickened, put
in hot dogs, just until heated through.
