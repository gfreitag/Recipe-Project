Title: Herb-Baked Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts MSG (optional)
1/4 ts Ground oregano
1/4 ts Ground rosemary
1/4 ts Ground marjoram
1    (2 1/2-lb) chicken
3 tb Oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Black pepper
Place chicken in baking dish. Brush with oil and sprinkle with salt,
pepper, MSG, oregano, rosemary and marjoram. Bake at 450F 30 to 35
minutes.

Created by: Scarantino's Italian Inn, Glendale, Calif.
