Title: John Beresford Tipton Bars
Categories: Candy, Snack
Yield: 10 servings
1 c  Rice Krispies
1 c  Miniature Marshmallows
1 lb Golden Almond Bars; (5 bars)
1 c  Froot Loops Cereal
NOTE:  One pound of almond bark can be substituted for the candy bars
if +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++ Melt candy bars in a double boiler.
Remove from heat, add cerals, mix until coated, then add
marshmallows. Mix well.  Pour into a buttered pan about 7 by 10
inches. Let cool until set. Cut into 20 to 30 small squares.
