Title: Mexican Tea Punch
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Lime juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 1/2 t  Cinnamon; ground
1 1/2 t  Aromatic bitters
2 c  Tequila
2 c  Tea; strong, cold
1 c  Pinapple juice
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Water
Mix all ingredients; refrigerate until chilled.  Stir before serving.
Serve over ice.
