Title: Grunkohlsuppe (Kale and Potato Soup)
Categories: German, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 t  Pepper
2 lb Kale; fresh
1/2 lb Garlic Sausage; *
4 ea Potatoes; Medium
2 T  Vegetable Oil
8 c  Water
1 t  Salt
*  Garlic Sausage should be the smoked kind, cooked and sliced.
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++ Peel and chop potatoes.  Combine with vegetable
oil and water. Cook for 20 to 30 minutes or until potatoes are
tender. Remove potatoes and reserve liquid.  Mash potatoes through a
sieve and return to potato liquid. Add salt and pepper and simmer for
20 minutes. Wash kale discarding all tough leaves and cut into
shreds.  Add to potatoes and cook for 25 minutes. Add sausage. Simmer
gently for 5 minutes.
