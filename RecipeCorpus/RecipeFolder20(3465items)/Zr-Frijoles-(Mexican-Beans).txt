Title: Zr Frijoles (Mexican Beans)
Categories: Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Clove Garlic
1 T  Red Chili Powder
1/4 t  Ground Cumin
1/2 t  Oregano
Meats should include only one of the following:
2 c  Dry Pinto Beans
1 x  Water To More Than Cover
1/2 lb Meat *
1 ea Large Onion Chopped
*
Saltpork, Ham Hock,
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Add salt to taste if other than Salt Pork is uxed.
Soak beans in water overnight.
Add salt pork, onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin and oregano;
simmer on low heat at least 4 hours or water is absorbed and mixture
is thick.
