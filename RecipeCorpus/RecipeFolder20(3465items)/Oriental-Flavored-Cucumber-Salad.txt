Title: Oriental Flavored Cucumber Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Sugar
1/4 t  Hot chinese chili paste
1 ea Small clove garlic, minced
1 ea English cucumber
1 T  Rice vinegar
1 T  Soy sauce
1 T  Vegetaqble oil
1/2 t  Oriental sesame oil
Trim ends off cucumber and cut into 1 inch chunks.
Combine rice vinegar with soya sauce, vegetable oil, sesame oil,
salt, sugar, hot chili paste and garlic.
Mix dressing with cucumbers and serve at room temperature.
