Title: French Dressing Mix
Categories: Misc/food, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Paprika
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Dry Mustard
1/8 ts Onion Powder
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl; stir until well blended.  Put
mixture in a foil packet or 1-pint glass jar and label as French
Dressing Mix.  Stor in a cool, dry place and use within 6 months.

Makes enough mix (5 Tablespoons) for 1 1/4 cups of French Dressing.

VARIATION:  Sweet Italian Dressing:  Increase sugar to 1/2 cup.
Substitute 1 T celery seed for paprika. French Dressing: Combine 1
pkt of mix, 3/4 cup vegetable oil, and 1/4 cup vinegar in a glass
jar. Shake until well blended.  Chill before serving.

Makes about 1 1/4 cups of French Dressing.
