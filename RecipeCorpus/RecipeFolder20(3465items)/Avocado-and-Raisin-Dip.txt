Title: Avocado and Raisin Dip
Categories: Vegetable, Appetizer, Fruit, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  Sugar
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Freshly Ground Pepper
2 ea Avocados; Peeled & Chopped
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
1/4 c  Lime Juice
Place all ingredients in blender container.  Cover and blend on high
speed until smooth, about 45 seconds.
Serve with raw vegetables, assorted crackers or fried tortillas.
Makes 1 2/3 cups of dip.
