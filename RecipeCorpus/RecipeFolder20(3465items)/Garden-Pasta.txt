Title: Garden Pasta
Categories: Main dish, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 t  Garlic powder
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Pepper
1/2 t  Oregano
1 T  Vegetable or salad oil
1 lb Spaghetti
5 ea Tomatoes, chopped
2 ea Celery, chopped
2 ea Carrots, chopped
1 ea Onion, medium, chopped
8 ea Green onion, chopped
1 pk Equal
1 t  Basil
Put the vegetables in a pot and cover tightly.  Cook over medium heat,
stirring occasionally, for 10 min.
Add seasonings.  Cover pot, cook medium-low heat for 5 minutes. Add
oil and simmer for 30 min. or until carrots are tender. Cook
spaghetti, drain. Toss with the sauce. Cal: 150; Fat: 1/2g.
