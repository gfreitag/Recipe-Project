Title: Crawfish Chili
Categories: Cajun/creol, Main dish, Ground beef, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 16 servings
3 T  Chili powder
1 cn (8 oz) tomato sauce
1 c  Dry white wine
1 x  Water
1 t  Lemon or lime juice
1 c  Chopped onions
1 x  Bacon drippings
2 lb Lean ground beef
2 lb Crawfish tails
1 t  Garlic, chopped fine
2 t  Salt
1 T  Soy sauce
1 t  Cayenne pepper
1 t  Dried mint
1 T  Dried parsley
Brown meat in bacon drippings.  Combine all other ingredients with
meat and bring to a boil.  Simmer for a few hours. From Justin
Wilson's "Gourmet and Gourmand Cookbook"
