Title: Bean and Garlic Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 t  Red Chiles; Ground
1/4 t  Salt
1 x  Pepper; Dash of
2 c  Pinto Beans; *
1/4 c  Mayonnaise Or Salad Dressing
1 ea Clove Garlic; Finely Chopped
*  Pinto beans can be home cooked or canned.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Mix all ingredients.  Cover and refrigerate 1
hour. Serve with tortilla chips. Makes 2 cups of dip.
