Title: Citrus Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetable, Fruit, Tex/mexican
Yield: 5 servings
1/2 c  Lime Juice
2 T  Sugar
2 T  Lemon Juice
1 T  Fresh Cilantro; Snipped
1 t  Salt
1 ea Onion; Large, Finely Chopped
1 T  Ground Red Chiles
1/4 t  Ground Red Pepper
1 ea Ancho Chile; *
1 T  Vegetable Oil
1 c  Orange Juice
*  Ancho chile should be seeded and finely chopped.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Cook onion, ground red chiles, red pepper and
ancho chile in oil, stirring frequently, until onion is tender, about
5 minutes. Stir in remaining ingredients.  Heat to boiling, reduce
heat to low. Simmer uncovered, about 10 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Makes about 2 1/3 cups of sauce.
