Title: Wonderful Layered Peach Dessert
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Whipped topping
2 c  (3c) Peaches & sugar
1 3/4 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 t  Almond flavoring
1 1/2 c  Plain flour
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Melted butter
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
FOR CRUST: Combine flour, sugar and pecans. Add the butter. Mix well
and press mixture in bottom of 13 x 9 baking dish. Bake at 350F for
18-20 minutes. Cool. FOR FILLING: Beat cream cheese and powdered
sugar at medium speed until light and fluffy. Fold in whipped topping
and almond flavoring. Drain peaches and add to whipped topping
mixture. Spoon over cooled crust and chill for several hours.
