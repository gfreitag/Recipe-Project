Title: Hickory-Smoked Cheese Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 t  Salt; Hickory-Smoked
1 tb Green Onion; Minced, Use All
1 c  Cottage Cheese
1/2 c  Sour Cream
1/4 t  Garlic; Minced
Blend the cottage cheese and sour cream until smooth.
Add the remaining ingredients blending well.  Cover and chill. Makes
about 1 1/2 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS: Potato Chips, Water
Crackers, Ham, Mushrooms, Snow Peas
