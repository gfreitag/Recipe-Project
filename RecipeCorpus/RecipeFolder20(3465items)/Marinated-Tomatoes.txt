Title: Marinated Tomatoes
Categories: German, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Garlic; clove, large, minced
1 T  Basil; fresh, chopped
2 ea Thyme; sprigs, fresh,chopped
1 ea Marjoram; fresh, sprig, chop
1 T  Scallion; minced
4 ea Tomatoes; large *
1 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Wine vinegar
1/4 t  Mustard; dry
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Black pepper
*    Tomatoes should be peeled and sliced.
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++ ++++ Place tomato slices in serving bowl. Combine
remaining ingredients and pour over tomatoes. Toss lightly. Chill for
1 hour or longer before serving.
