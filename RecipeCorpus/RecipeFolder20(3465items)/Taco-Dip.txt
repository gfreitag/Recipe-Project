Title: Taco Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Relish
Yield: 10 servings
2 T  Milk
1 pk Cheddar cheese
1/2 ea Head of lettuce
1 1/2 ea Tomatoes, cubed
1 pk Cream cheese - 8 oz
1 pk Taco mix
1 pk Small sour cream
2 T  Taco sauce (hot)
Mix cream cheese, sour cream, taco mix, taco sauce and milk together.
Spread on serving dish.  Top with cheddar cheese, shredded. Top again
with 1/2 head of lettuce shredded. Sprinkle with tomatoes cut into
small cubes. Refrigerate.  Serve with tostito or nacho chips.
