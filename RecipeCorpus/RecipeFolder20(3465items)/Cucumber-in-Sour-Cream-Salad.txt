Title: Cucumber in Sour Cream Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Drops of tabasco
2 T  Chopped chives
1 t  Dill seed or fresh chop dill
1 ea Dash of pepper
1 ea Med cucumber thin sl 3 cups
1 t  Salt
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 T  White vinegar
Sprinkle the cucumber with salt.  Let stand 30 minutes. Drain
thoroughly. Dry on peper towel.
Combine sour cream, vinegar, tabasco, chives, dill seed and pepper.
Pour over cucumber.  Chill well before using. Garnish with fresh dill.
