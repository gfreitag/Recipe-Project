Title: White Gazpacho
Categories: Appetizer, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
16 oz (1 pk) Sour Creem
2 T  Lemon Juice
1/4 t  Garlic Powder
1/4 t  Pepper
4 t  Instant Chicken Bouillion OR
4 ea Cubes Chicken Bouillion
2 c  Boiling Water
3 ea Med Cucumbers
Pare and seed cucumbers.  Cut into cubes so that you have about 3
cups of cubes. In small saucepan, dissolve bouillion in water. Cool
completely. In blender or food processor, blend cucumber with 1/2 Cup
Bouillon liquid until smooth.
In medium bowl, combine cucumber mixture, remaining bouillon liquid,
sour cream, lemon juice, garlic powder and pepper; mix well. Chill
thoroughly.
Garnish as desired.  Serve with condiments.
Refrigerate left overs. SUGGESTED CONDIMENTS: Chopped Fresh Tomato
Chopped Green Onions Chopped Green Peppers Toasted Slivered Almonds
Toasted Croutons.
