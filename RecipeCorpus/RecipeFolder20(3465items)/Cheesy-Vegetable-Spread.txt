Title: Cheesy Vegetable Spread
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Spread
Yield: 6 servings
2 T  Finely chopped Celery
2 T  Chopped Pecans
2 T  Currants
1 T  Toasted Wheat germ
1/2 t  Curry powder
1 c  Shredded Farmer Cheese(4 oz)
3 T  Plain Yogurt
1 t  Lemon juice
1 t  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Chopped Apple
1/4 c  Chopped Carrot
In food processor, process cheese, yogurt, lemon juice, and soy sauce
until smooth.
Stir in remaining ingredients. Use as a spread on crackers, whole
wheat bread, or pita crisps, or stuffed into pita pockets. Makes 1
1/2 cups Variations: - substitute shredded Cheddar for the farmer
cheese Spread may be made in advance, and refrigerated 3-4 days.
