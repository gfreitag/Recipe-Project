Title: Sangria
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 12 servings
1/3 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 c  Ginger ale; chilled
25 oz Red wine; dry, 1 bottle
1/3 c  Frozen lemonade; *
1/2 c  Brandy
1/2 c  Orange-flavored liqueur
*  Use 1/2 of a 6-oz can of concentrate.
~------------------------------------------------------
~------------- ---- Mix all ingredients except ginger ale;
refrigerate until chilled. Just before serving, stir in ginger ale.
Garnish with fruit, if desired.
