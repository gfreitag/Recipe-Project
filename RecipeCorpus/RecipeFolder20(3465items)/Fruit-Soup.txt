Title: Fruit Soup
Categories: Fruit, Soup/stew
Yield: 2 servings
1 T  Honey
1/8 t  Ground Allspice
1 c  Peeled & Sliced Fruits *
2/3 c  Apricot Nectar
1 t  Cornstarch
1 T  Brandy
*  Peeled and sliced fruits can be papayas, peaches or pineapples;
cut up apricots, nectarines, or plums; or halved and pitted dark
sweet cherries.
~------------------------------------------------------
~---------------- In a nonmetal bowl stir together apricot nectar and
cornstarch. Stir in brandy, honey, and allspice. Micro-cook,
uncovered, on 100% power for 2 to 3 minutes or till the mixture is
thickened and bubbly, stiring every 30 seconds.  Stir in fruit.
Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power for 45 seconds to 1 minute or
till heated through.  Chill thoroughly, if desired. Serve hot or cold.
