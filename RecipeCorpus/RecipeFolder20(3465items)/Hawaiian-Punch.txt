Title: Hawaiian Punch
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 25 servings
1 qt Rasberry sherbert
2 qt Ginger ale
3 cn Large frozen Hawaiian Punch
3 cn Large frozen Lemonade
2 cn Large frozen Orange juice
In a LARGE punch bowl, follow can directions for each frozen juice.
Spoon in rasberry sherbert and whip it up. Pour the ginger ale slowly
around the edge of the punch bowl (2 bottles).
