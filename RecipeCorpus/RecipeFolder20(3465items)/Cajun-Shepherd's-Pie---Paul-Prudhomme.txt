Title: Cajun Shepherd's Pie - Paul Prudhomme
Categories: Cajun/creol, Main dish, Beef
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Finely Chopped Celery
1/2 c  Green Bell Peppers, Chopped
1 tb + 1 t Minced Garlic
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 ts Tabasco Sauce
1 1/2 lb Ground Beef
1/2 lb Ground Pork
2    Eggs, Lightly Beaten
1/2 c  Very Fine Bread Crumbs
1/4 lb + 3 T Unsalted Butter
3/4 c  Finely Chopped Onions
MMMMM---------------------MEAT SEASONING MIX--------------------------
3/4 c  Evaporated Milk
2 lb Potatoes, Peeled & Quartered
1 ts Salt
1 ts White Pepper
1 1/2 c  Julienned Carrots (See Note)
1 c  Julienned Onions  (See Note)
2 ts Cayenne Pepper
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Black Pepper
1 1/4 ts White Pepper
3/4 ts Ground Cumin
3/4 ts Dried Thyme Leaves
MMMMM------------------VEGETABLE SEASONING MIX-----------------------
1/4 ts Garlic Powder
1/4 ts Cayenne Pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts White Pepper
1/4 ts Onion Powder
MMMMM-------------------------VEGETABLES------------------------------
Very Hot Cajun Sauce/Beef
1 1/2 c  Julienned Zucchini(See Note)
1 c  Julienned Squash (See Note)
NOTE:  To julienne squashes, cut peelings 1/8 inch thick and cut
these into strips 1/8 inch wide and 2 inches long; use only strips
that have skin on one surface.  Cut carrots and onions into similar
strips.
