Title: Cream Cheese Tarts
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 36 servings
1 t  Vanilla
1 pk Sunshine vanilla wafers
1 cn Thank You Cherry Pie filling
2 pk Softened cream cheese
3/4 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs
1 T  Lemon juice
Beat all ingredients with electric mixer.  Put papers in tart tins
(use tart pan and paper wrappers).  Put wafers in papers, FLAT side
up. Fill 3/4 full of cheese mixture. Put cherry on top, (no juice).
Bake 15 minutes at 350*.  Cool in tins.  Makes three dozen.
If storing for awhile, refrigerate.
