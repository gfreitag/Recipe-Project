Title: Everyday Salad Dressing
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1    Garlic Clove Minced
1/4 ts Dillweed OR Basil
1/8 ts Pepper
Low Calorie Sweetener To
Taste
1/2 c  Rice Vinegar
2 tb Vegetable Oil OR Olive Oil
1 tb Soy Sauce
1 tb Water
1/2 ts Dijon Mustard
In Medium Jar With Lid, Combine All Ingredients.  Shake Vigorously
Until Well Blended.  Store in Refrigerator Until Ready To Use. Makes
3/4 Cup.
