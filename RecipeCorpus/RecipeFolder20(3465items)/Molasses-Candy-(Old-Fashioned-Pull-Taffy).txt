Title: Molasses Candy (Old-Fashioned Pull Taffy)
Categories: Penndutch, Candy
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Water
1 pn Baking soda
2 c  Molasses
2 T  Butter
1 T  Vinegar
Boil all together until a little tried in cold water becomes brittle.
Pour on a buttered dish and allow to cool. When cool enough to
handle, rub butter on hands and pull the candy (small portions at a
time) until light in color. Cut in small pieces.
