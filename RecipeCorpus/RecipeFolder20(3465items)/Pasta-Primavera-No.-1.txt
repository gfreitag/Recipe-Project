Title: Pasta Primavera No. 1
Categories: Italian
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Sliced Mushrooms
1/4 c  Chopped Onions
1/4 c  Frozen Peas
1 oz Fat Free Ham, Julienned
1 tb Sliced Pimentos
1 c  Uncooked Pasta Twists
1 c  Nonfat Dry Milk
1/2 ts Basil, 1 Clove Garlic
1/4 ts Salt, Dash Of Pepper
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan
1/2 c  Diced Zucchini
1/2 c  Broccoli Flowerettes
In Saucepan, Boil Milk, Basil, Salt & Pepper. in Cup Mix Cornstarch&
Water. Add To Milk & Boil, Stirring Constantly.  Stir in Parmesan&
Cover. Cook Pasta According To Package Directions.  in Skillet, Saute
Garlic, Zucchini, Broccoli, Mushrooms, Onions & Ham in Oil Until
Tender. Add Peas, Pimentos & Pasta & Heat.  Pour Sauce Over, Toss &
Serve Immediately.
