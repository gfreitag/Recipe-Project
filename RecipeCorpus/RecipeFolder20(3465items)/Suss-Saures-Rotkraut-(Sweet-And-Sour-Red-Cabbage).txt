Title: Suss-Saures Rotkraut (Sweet-And-Sour Red Cabbage)
Categories: German, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Brown Sugar
2    Bay Leaves
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Butter
Lemon Juice;Of 1/2 Med.Lemon
1/4 c  Butter
4    Apples; Med., Peel, Slice
1/2    Onion; Red, Chopped
1    Red Cabbage; Head,Fine Shred
1 c  Red Wine
4    Cloves; Whole
Melt butter in 4-quart Dutch oven.  Add apples and onions, saute
slightly. Add finely shredded cabbage, red wine, cloves, sugar, and
bay leaves. Simmer, covered, for 1 hour, then add the remaining
ingredients.  Heat to melt the butter and serve immediately.
