Title: Marinated Ginger Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  Grated Ginger Root OR
1 T  Ground ginger
1 t  Onion Salt
1/4 t  Garlic Powder
1 ea 2 1/2-3 lb Frying Chicken
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Vegtable oil
1/4 c  Imported Soy Sauce
NOTE:  When making this recipe, it tastes much better if you find
sources ~------------------------------------------------------
~------------------ Cut chicken in to serving parts if not already in
that form. Place chicken in shallow baking dish. In small bowl,
combine remaining ingredients; pour over chicken.  Cover, refrigerate
4 hours or overnight, turning occasionally.  Grill or broil as
desired, basting frequently with marinade.
Refrigerate leftovers
