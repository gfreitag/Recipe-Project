Title: Clinton Hot Chicken Salad
Categories: Poultry, Salad, Herbs/spice, Chicken
Yield: 12 servings
6 tb Lemon juice
6 x  Eggs, hard-boiled, chopped
3/4 c  Almonds, slivered
1/4 c  Butter, melted
1 c  Cornflakes, crushed
2 c  Chicken, cooked & cubed
2 c  Celery, cooked & cubed
2 c  Rice, cooked in chicken brot
2 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 1/4 c  Mayonnaise
Onion, chopped
Prepare chicken, celery, and rice.  Combine cream of chicken soup,
mayo., onion, lemon juice, and chopped eggs.  Add first three
ingredients and mix well.  Place in casserole for baking.
Heat almonds in melted butter and pour on top of cassrole.  Top with
cornflakes.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
