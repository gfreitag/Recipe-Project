Title: Traditional Calabacitas Con Leche
Categories: Tex/mexican, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Dash pepper
1 ea 4 oz can chopped green chili
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Grated cheddar cheese
4 ea Medium summer squash, sliced
1/4 c  Butter or maragarine
1 ea 15 oz can corn, drained
1/2 c  Onion, thinly sliced
1/2 t  Salt
Saute' squash in butter until soft. Reduce heat and add corn, onions,
salt, pepper and green chili. Mix well and add milk. Simmer until
well blended. Add cheese and cover until cheese is melted.
