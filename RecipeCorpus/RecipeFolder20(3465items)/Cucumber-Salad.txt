Title: Cucumber Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Cottage cheese
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 pk Lime gelatin (3 oz.)
1 c  Hot water
1 c  Cucumber, scrubbed & grated
1/2 c  Onion, grated
Dissolve gelatin in hot water. Let cool. Add cucumber and onion to
gelatin. Fold in sour cream, cottage cheese, and mayonnaise. Pour
into 6 cup mold and refrigerate at least four hours before serving.
