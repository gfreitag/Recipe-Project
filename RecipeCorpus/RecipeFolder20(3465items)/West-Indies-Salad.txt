Title: West Indies Salad
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Olive oil
4 oz Vinegar
4 oz Ice water
1 lb Crabmeat
3 T  Chopped onion
1 x  Salt and pepper
Put onions in bowl, add meat, salt, and pepper.  Then oil, vinegar,
and ice water in that order, let set at least 1 hour before serving.
