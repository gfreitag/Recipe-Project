Title: Potato Salad Recipe
Categories: Salad, Appetizer
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Basil
1 ts Salt
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ds Red pepper
10 md Potatoes, diced & cooked
1/2    Green bell pepper, diced
2    Celery stalks, diced
3    Scallions, chopped
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 ts Hungarian paprika
Cool potatoes completely in the fridge or better leave to stand
overnight. Mix in the diced vegetables.  Whisk together the oil with
the spices & herbs.  Ensure it is well blended.  Pour over potatoes &
toss gently. Refrigerate till ready to be served.
