Title: Florida Red Snapper
Categories: Seafood, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 t  Grated orange rind
1/2 t  Salt
1/8 t  Nutmeg
2 lb Red snapper fillets
1/4 c  Grated onion
2 T  Orange juice
2 T  Lemon juice
Thaw fillets if frozen.  Cut fish into 6 portions.
Place in a single layer, skin side down,  in a well-greased baking
dish, 12x8x2 inches. Combine onion, orange and lemon juice, ornage
rind, and salt.
Pour over fish; cover and place in refrigerator to marinate 30
minutes. Sprinkle fish with nutmeg and pepper. Bake in a moderate
oven, 350F, for 25 to 30 minutes or until fish flakes easily when
tested with fork.
