Title: Cream Cheese Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Pecans
1 c  Drained fruit (pineapple)
2 ea Graham cracker pie shells
8 oz Cream Cheese
14 oz Condensed Milk
1/3 c  Lemon juice
9 oz Whipped cream
Combine cream cheese, milk, lemon juice, and whipped cream. Mix
together with mixer.  Add pecans, and fruit. Refrigerate.
