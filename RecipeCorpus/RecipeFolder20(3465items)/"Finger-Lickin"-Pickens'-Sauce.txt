Title: "Finger Lickin" Pickens' Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Horseradish
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Sage
1 ts Salt
1 c  Beer
2 sm Onions finely chopped
2 c  Ketchup
2 ts A-1 steak sauce
1 ts Whole celery seed
1 ts Tabasco sauce
2 tb Soy sauce
2/3 c  Dark brown sugar
Juice of 2 lemons
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan. Bring mixture to boil. Simmer
sauce over medium-low heat, uncovered, for 5 minutes, stirring
constantly..

Nutrition information per Tbsp: Calories, 18 Fat, 0.03 gram
Carbohdrate, 4 gram Cholesterol, 0 milligram Sodium, 155 milligram
