Title: Meatballs
Categories: Meatballs
Yield: 30 servings
2 tb Garlic salt
1 tb Oregano
1 tb Parsley
2 lb Ground beef
1 c  Bread crumbs
2 tb Grated cheese
2    Eggs
Prior to browning,mix all ingredients together thoroughly. Form into
meatballs.Place in a bowl,separating with wax paper. Refrigerate
overnight. Brown meatballs in a 250 degree oven for 15 minutes.Place
in sauce.Cook on low heat for 3 hours.Turn off and let thicken. Note:
Placing meatballs in refrigerator overnight,holds meatballs
together.Otherwise,you have meat sauce.
