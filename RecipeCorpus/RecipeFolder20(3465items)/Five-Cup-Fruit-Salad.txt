Title: Five Cup Fruit Salad
Categories: Salad, Londontowne
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Sour cream
11 oz Can mandarin oranges, draind
13 1/2 oz Can pineapple chunks, draind    3 1/2 oz Flaked coconut
1 x  Grapes/cherries for garnish
1/2 c  Juice from pineapple
1 1/2 c  Miniature marshmallows
Combine all ingredients except garnish, and chill several hours or
overnight. Serve on lettuce cups garnished with grapes or cherries.
Mrs. Robert F. Lewis
