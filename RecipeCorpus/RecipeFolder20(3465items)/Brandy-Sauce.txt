Title: Brandy Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  Nutmeg
1/4 c  Brandy
1 t  Real Vanilla
1 c  Water
2 T  Corn Starch
2 T  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
Mix dry ingredients and then stir them into a cup of boiling water.
Boil for 5 minutes and then add butter, brandy, and vanilla. Serve
hot over mince pie or gingerbread.
