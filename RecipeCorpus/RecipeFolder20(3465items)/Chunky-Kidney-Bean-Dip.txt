Title: Chunky Kidney Bean Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
1 t  Hot Sauce
1 tb Cumin; Ground
1 tb Coriander; Ground
1 1/2 c  Kidney Beans; Cooked
1/4 c  Sour Cream
1 t  Lemon Juice
Mash the beans by hand, using a fork or the back of a wooden spoon.
Blend in the sour cream, mixing until smooth. Add all the other
ingredients and blend well.
Cover and chill. Makes about 1 3/4 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS:
Polish Sausage, Celery, Corn Crackers
