Title: Not Miss Lily's Olives Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  Dried Parsley; Crushed
2 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1 tb Paprika
1 tb Fresh Parsley; Chopped, OR
1 c  Cream Cheese; Softened
1 c  Sour Cream
1/4 c  Black Olives; Chopped
1/2 t  Garlic Powder
Beat the cream cheese to a smooth consistency.  Blend in the sour
cream and then the remaining ingredients.
Blend well.  Cover and chill. Makes about 2 1/4 cups of dip.
SUGGESTED DIPPERS:  Scallions, Jalapeno Peppers, Celery Root, Potato
Chips, Duck Sausage
