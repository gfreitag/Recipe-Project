Title: Fettuccine Alfredo
Categories: Pasta, Italian, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1 x  Salt and pepper to taste
8 oz Fettuccine
4 T  Butter
1/2 c  Heavy cream
6 T  Parmesan cheese
Melt  butter in a small saucepan over a low heat.
Off the heat add the cream and the grated parmesan.
Put back on the fire to heat the  sauce through and melt the
cheese. Do not boil.  Stir in the salt and pepper. Cook the
fettuccine in plenty of boiling salted water. Drain well. Combine the
noodles and the sauce and leave to stand covered  for  two minutes
before serving.
