Title: Fruit Pizza
Categories: Appetizer, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
3 T  Cornstarch
1 x  Dash of salt
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Orange juice
3/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Lemon juice
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/2 c  Sugar
8 oz Cream cheese
1 t  Vanilla
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1 pk Sugar cookie dough
MMMMM---------------------------FRUIT--------------------------------
1 x  Strawberries
1 x  Kiwi
1 x  Peaches
1 x  Grapes
1 x  Bananas
Mix ingredients of glaze- cook over medium heat til boils, and
thickens, then cool. Spread cookie cough on buttered pizza pan. Bake
350 degrees for 10 minutes. Cool. Mix sauce and spread over cooled
dough. Arrange fruit on top and pour glaze over. Refrigerate.
