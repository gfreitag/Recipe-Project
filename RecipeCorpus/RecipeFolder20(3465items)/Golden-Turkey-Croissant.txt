Title: Golden Turkey Croissant
Categories: Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 1 servings
1    Croissant; split
1/4 c  Chef-Mate Golden Cheese
- Sauce; heated
2 oz Turkey; cooked, sliced thin
2 sl Tomato; sliced thin
1/2 oz Alfalfa sprouts
1.  Place turkey, tomato slices and alfalfa sprouts on croissant.
Ladle Golden chese sauce over sandwich. Serve.

Makes 1 sandwich

Source: Carnation Company
