Title: Tropical Chicken Salad
Categories: Salad, Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  White Raisins (opt.)
3 T  Chopped Chutney
2 t  Curry Powder
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 c  Chicken, white meat, *
2 x  Apples, peeled and diced
1 c  Pineapple Chunks
1/3 c  Chopped almonds
1/2 c  Shredded Coconut
* Cooked and cubed
In a bowl, combine chutney, curry, and mayonnaise. Combine all other
ingredients in a separate bowl. Stir
curry, chutney, and mayonaisse mixture into the chicken mixture.
Serve on watercress or lettuce leaves, with slices of avocado.
