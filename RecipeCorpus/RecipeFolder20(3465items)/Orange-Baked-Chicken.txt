Title: Orange Baked Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 t  Rosemary
1/4 t  Pepper
2 T  Flour
2 c  Orange juice
3 ea Chicken breasts, skin, split
1/4 c  Minced onion
1/2 t  Paprika
1/2 t  Salt
Arrange chicken in shallow baking pan, breast side up, not
overlapping. Sprinkle with onion and seasonings. Blend flour with 1/2
c orange juice, stir in the remaining juice and pour over the
chicken. Bake, uncovered, basting occasionally at 350F for 1 hr. or
until tender. Serve the chicken over noodles or rice on a warm
platter. Stir the pan juices to blend and pour over the chicken.
