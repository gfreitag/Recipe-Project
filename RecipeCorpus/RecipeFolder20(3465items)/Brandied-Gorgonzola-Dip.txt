Title: Brandied Gorgonzola Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 t  White Pepper
3/4 t  Brandy
1/2 c  Gorgonzola Cheese
3/4 c  Sour Cream
Blend the gorgonzola cheese and sour cream until smooth. Add the
remaining ingredients and blend well.
Cover and chill. Makes about 1 1/4 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS:
Cherry Tomatoes, Green Zucchini, Jicama, Pearapple, Anjou Pear,
Nectarine
