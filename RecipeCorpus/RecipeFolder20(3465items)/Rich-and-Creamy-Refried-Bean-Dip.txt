Title: Rich and Creamy Refried Bean Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs, Dessert
Yield: 2 servings
1 t  Salsa Verde; Hot Green Salsa
1/8 t  Salt
1 x  Black Pepper; To Taste
1 c  Refried Beans
1/2 c  Sour Cream
1/4 c  Fresh Tomato; Chopped
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
2 tb Black Olives; Chopped
1 x  Cheddar; Sharp, Shredded
Mix the beans and sour cream, blending thoroughly.
Blend in all of the other ingredients except the garnish, blending
well. Cover and chill. Garnish with the cheddar cheese just before
serving. Makes about 1 3/4 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS: Cucumber,
Cheese Crackers, Jicama, Broccoli
