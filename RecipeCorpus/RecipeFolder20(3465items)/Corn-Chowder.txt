Title: Corn Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Milk
2 T  Butter
1 ea Egg slightly beaten
1 ea Can (8 oz) corn
1 ea Medium potato diced
1 ea Onion, chopped
1 ea Can (14 oz) chicken broth
1 ea Can (16 oz) cream-style corn
Saute  onion  in butter until golden brown.   Add broth and potato and
simmer until potato is tender.
Add corn and milk,  blend well.  Season with salt and pepper. Bring
to a boil.  Remove from heat.  Stir some of chowder into egg. Add
mixture to sauce pan and blend well. Simmer until heated through.
Serve.
