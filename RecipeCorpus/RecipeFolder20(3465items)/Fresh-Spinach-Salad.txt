Title: Fresh Spinach Salad
Categories: Salad, Londontowne
Yield: 1 servings
1 x  Fresh spinach
2 T  Caesar salad dressing
4 T  Cottage cheese
Two hours before serving, marinate Caesar salad dressing with cottage
cheese. Prepare fresh spinach by washing, cutting out large vein in
center of leaves, and tearing into desired size pieces. Drain. Pour
dressing over spinach, toss, and serve. Mrs. John P. Elberti
