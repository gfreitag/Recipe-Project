Title: Pudding Icing
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 t  Vanilla
1 c  Milk
1 pk Instant pudding (small)
1/2 c  Butter/margarine
Mix together 1 cup milk and 1 box of pudding and let stand. Mix
together butter, shortening, powdered sugar and vanilla thoroughly.
Add both mixtures together and blend well. Will ice 2 9-inch cakes.
