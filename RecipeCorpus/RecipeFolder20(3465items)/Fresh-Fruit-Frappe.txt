Title: Fresh Fruit Frappe
Categories: Fruit, Beverage, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Strawberries; Halved
1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Orange Juice
1 x  Crushed Ice
1 c  Watermelon; Cut Up
1 c  Cantaloupe Or Honeydew; *
1 c  Pineapple; Cut Up
1 c  Mango; Cut Up
*  Melon should be cut up.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Mix all ingredients except ice.
Fill blender container 1/2 full of mixture.  Add crushed ice to fill
to the top.  Cover and blend on high speed until of a uniform
consistency. Repeat with remaining mixture. Serve immediately;
garnish with fruit, if desired.
