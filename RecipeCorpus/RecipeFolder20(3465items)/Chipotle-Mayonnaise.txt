Title: Chipotle Mayonnaise
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Tex/mexican, Chilies
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 t  Oregano Leaves; Dried,(Opt.)
2 ea Chipotle Chiles; *
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Dairy Sour Cream
*  Chipotle Chiles shoud be the ones that are canned in adobo sauce
and should be finely chopped.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Mix all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate until
chilled, about 1 hour. Makes about 1 cup Mayonnaise
