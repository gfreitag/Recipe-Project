Title: Smoked Prime Rib   (Rich Davis, K.c. Masterpiece Resturan
Categories: Bbq/grill, Main dish, Meat
Yield: 8 servings
1 x  Dry rub seasonings
8 lb Prime Rib Roast
Place dry rub seasonings over entire roast surface.

Grill roast in smoker (indirect heat) for 3 to 4 hours at 200 to 250
deg. or until a meat thermometer indicates 140 deg (for M-Rare).

Sprinkle crushed rosemary leaves 3/4 of the way thought the cooking
time.
