Title: Margarita Sunrise
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Powdered sugar
1 c  Tequila
6 oz Frozen orange juice; *
1 ea Lime; cut into halves
1 x  Granulated sugar
3 c  Cracked ice
*  Use 1 6-ounce can of thawed frozen concentrate.
~------------------------------------------------------
~------------- ---- Rub rims of 4 stemmed glasses with 1 line half;
dip rims of glasses in granulated sugar.
Squeeze juice from both lime halves into blender container. Add
remaining ingredients.  Cover and blend on high speed until foamy.
