Title: Vidalia Onion Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Vegetable, Appetizer, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
6    Vidalia onions, large,
-sliced thinly
1/4 c  Grated Parmesean cheese
4    Shakes of Tabasco sauce
1/4 c  Grated cheese for top
2    9" Pie shells, baked
4    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Sour cream, (generous cup)
Salt & pepper to taste
1    Stick of butter
Cook onions in butter until clear and soft (about 15 min.), stirring
often. Add all other ingredients. Mix and pour into the 2 baked pie
shells. Sprinkle grated cheese on top. Bake in 375 deg. oven for 20
minutes, then lower temp. to 350 deg. for an additional 40 minutes
until lightly browned on top.
