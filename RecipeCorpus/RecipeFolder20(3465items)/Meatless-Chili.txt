Title: Meatless Chili
Categories: Chili, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Oil, 2 1/2 T. Flour
2 tb Chili Powder
1 tb Ground Cumin
1/4 ts Garlic Powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 c  Dried Kidney Beans
(5-6 C. Cooked)
2/3 c  Chopped Onion
1 cl Garlic Minced
1    Bay Leaf
1 cn (6 Oz.) Tomato Paste
1. Place Dried Beans in Water & Soak Overnight. Change Water. Add
Water 3 OR 4 Times The Volume Of Beans. Simmer For 3 Hours & Drain.
2. Combine Beans, 6 Cups Water, 1/3 C. Chopped Onion,  Minced Garlic,
Bay Leaf & Tomato Paste & Simmer 2 Hours.3. Heat Oil in A Separate
Pan, Gradually Stir in Flour, Chili Powder, Cumin, 1/3 C. Onion &
Garlic Powder. Heat 5 Min.4. Stir Into Beans & Cook 1 Hour Until
Beans Are Soft & Chili Is Thickened.5. Add Salt & Pepper.6. May Be
Served Over Rice OR With Tortillas If Desired.
(Fat 4.5  Chol. 0.)
