Title: Working Woman's Chicken and Rice
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Rice-A-Roni (Chicken
2 cn Cream of chicken soup
Salt and pepper to taste
6    Bonless chicken breasts,
-skinned
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
In slow cooker put chicken breast with canned soups, alt and ppper.
Cook all day on LOW (approx. 10 hrs. or until chicken is tender).

Fix Rice-A-Roni per directions on box.  Put on plate and place
chicken and gravy on top.
