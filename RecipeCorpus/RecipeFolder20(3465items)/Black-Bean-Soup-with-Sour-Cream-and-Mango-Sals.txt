Title: Black Bean Soup with Sour Cream and Mango Sals
Categories: Soup/stew, Appetizer
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Chicken stock
1 ea Salt and pepper
4 ea Bay leaves
1 T  Honey
2 c  Black turtle beans
1 c  Diced onion
1 ea Diced carrot
2 ea Diced celery stalks
3 ea Strips of bacon
Combine all the ingredients and add enough water to cover the beans.
Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook until beans are
thoroughly cooked.
Add more water as required.  Remove bay leaves and puree the soup in
a blender.  Then pass the soup through a fine sieve to remove the
skins of the beans.
