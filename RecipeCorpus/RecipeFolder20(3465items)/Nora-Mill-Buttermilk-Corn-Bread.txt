Title: Nora Mill Buttermilk Corn Bread
Categories: Bread
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Buttermilk
2 tb Liguid shortening
1 1/2 c  NORA MILL buttermilk cb mix
1    Egg
Beat egg until light, add buttermilk and shortening.  Mix this
together, then add NORA MILL mix.  Blend until lumps are gone.  Pour
into well greased pan.  Bake at 450 degrees F. for 20-25 minutes or
until golden brown.
