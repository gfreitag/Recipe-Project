Title: Walnut Shortbread
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
12 ea Large walnut pieces
1 c  Softened butter
3/4 c  Icing sugar
1 t  Vanilla
Cream butter, icing sugar and vanilla until light. Stir in flour and
chopped walnuts. Pat dough into 9-inch pan with removable bottom.
Score with fork into 12 pie-shaped wedges. Place one walnut piece on
each wedge. Set pan on cookie sheet. Bake in oven heated to 325
degrees for 40 minutes or until lightly brown. Cool. Cut into wedges.
Makes 12 servings. Store in tightly covered tin container. From The
Gazette 90/12/12.
