Title: Variations on Rice Krispies Marshmallow Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie, Snack
Yield: 8 servings
Recipe Of Marshmallow Sqs.
VARIATIONS:

Use Cocoa Krispies instead of Rice Krispies. Melt 2 squares of
unsweetened chocolate with the marshmallows. Add 1/4 cup peanut
butter to marshmallows. Add 1 cup raisins with Rice Krispies. Add 1
cup of salted peanuts with Rice Krispies.
