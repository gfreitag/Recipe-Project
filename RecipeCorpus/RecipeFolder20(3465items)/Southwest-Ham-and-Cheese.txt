Title: Southwest Ham and Cheese
Categories: Appetizer, Pork, Tex/mexican, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
3 ea Cheddar Cheese; Mild
6 T  Vegetable Oil
6 ea Cooked Smoked Ham Slices
6 ea Flour Tortillas; 7" Dia.
Place 1 slice ham on each of 3 tortillas.  Top each with cheese,
another slice of ham and a tortilla.
Heat 2 T of the oil in a 10-inch skillet over medium heat until hot.
Cook 1 sandwich in oil, turning once, until golden brown and cheese
is melted, about 3 minutes. Cut into 4 wedges. Repeat with remaining
oil and sandwiches.
