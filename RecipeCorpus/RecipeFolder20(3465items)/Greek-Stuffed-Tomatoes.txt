Title: Greek Stuffed Tomatoes
Categories: Greek, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Feta cheese, crumbled
Salt, to taste
Pepper, to taste
6    Tomatoes, firm and with
Insides scooped out
2 tb Olive oil
1 sm Onion, diced
2 c  Spinach, chopped and cooked
2 ts Dried basil
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1    Egg
In a saucepan, heat oil and saute onion until translucent. In a bowl,
combine the sauteed onions with all remaining ingredients except
tomatoes. Stuff filling into tomatoes.  Bake in a preheated, 350
degree F oven for 15 minutes.  Make 4 to 6 servings.
