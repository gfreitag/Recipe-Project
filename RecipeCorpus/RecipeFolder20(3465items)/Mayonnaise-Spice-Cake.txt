Title: Mayonnaise Spice Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla Extract
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Cinnamon
2 c  Unbleached Flour
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Nuts, Chopped (Optional)
1 ts Soda
1 c  (Scant) Hot Water
1 c  Mayonnaise
Combine the raisins, nuts, soda and hot water and allow to soak for 10
minutes.  blend together the mayonnaise, sugar, vanilla extract,
cloves and cinnamon.  Fold this mixture alternately with the raisin
mixture into the flour.  Pour the batter into a greased and floured
9-inch square baking pan and bake in a preheated 350 degree F. oven
for 35 to 40 minutes or until the cake tests done.

From The Farmers Almanac 1976
