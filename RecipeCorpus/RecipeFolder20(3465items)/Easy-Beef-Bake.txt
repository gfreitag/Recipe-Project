Title: Easy Beef Bake
Categories: Beef
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 c  Ketchup
1 md Egg
3 c  Hot mashed potatoes
Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 pk Onion and mushroom or beef
Flavor mushroom soup mix
1/4 c  Water
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Soft bread crumbs
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.In a medium bowl,combine soup mix,
water,beef,bread crumbs,ketchup and egg.Press beef mixture into a 9"
pie pan.Bake @ 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
