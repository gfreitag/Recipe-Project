Title: Dutch Pancake
Categories: Penndutch, Breakfast, Pancakes
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Milk
3 ea Egg, well beaten
1/2 t  Salt
Beat the eggs thoroughly and add the flour and salt. Continue to beat
and add the milk while beating. Pour into a well-buttered pan and
bake for 25 minutes. Serve with powdered sugar and lemon juice.
