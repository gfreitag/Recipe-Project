Title: Cafe Mexicano
Categories: Beverage, Tex/mexican
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Coffee; Regular Grind, Dry
1 t  Vanilla
1/4 c  Brandy
8 T  Coffee Liqueur
8 c  Water; Cold
1/3 c  Dark Brown Sugar; Packed
1/2 oz Baking Chocolate; Fine Chop
2 ea Cloves; Whole
1 ea Cinnamon Stick;Broken In 1/2
Heat water, brown sugar, chocolate, cloves, and cinnamon to boiling in
3-quart saucepan; reduce heat.
Simmer, uncovered, for 15 minutes.  Stir in coffee.
Remove from heat; cover and let stand 5 minutes.
Stir in vanilla.  Strain coffee through 4 thicknesses of
cheesecloth. CAFE DIABLO: Cafe Mexicano Prepare Cafe Mexicano; keep
hot in saucepan after straining.  Heat brandy just until warm in
small, long-handled saucepan. Remove from heat; ignite.
Pour flaming brandy over coffee.  Allow flame to burn out; stir. Pour
coffee into cups or mugs.  Top each with whipped cream and 1 T
liqueur. Garnish with cinnamon stick, if desired.
