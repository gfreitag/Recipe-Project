Title: Red Meats Marinade*
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Marinade
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chianti
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
4 tb Honey
1 tb Chili powder
1/2 c  Peanut oil
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 tb White pepper ground
1 tb Black peppercorns crushed
1 ts Oregano crushed
2    Garlic cloves minced
Mix well. Pour over meat cover and refrigerate for 6-24 hours. Use as
a basting sauce also. Origin: the ktichen of Don Houston
