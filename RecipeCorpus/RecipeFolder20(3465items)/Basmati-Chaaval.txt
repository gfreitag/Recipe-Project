Title: Basmati Chaaval
Categories: Asian, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 T  Unsalted butter
2 2/3 c  Water
2 c  Basmati rice
3/4 t  Salt
Pick the rice over and put in a bowl.  Wash in several changes of
water. Drain.   Pour 3 times the water you have rice over the rice
and let soak for 30 minutes hour. Drain thoroughly. Combine the rice,
salt, butter and the water in a pot.  Bring to a boil. Cover
with a tight fitting lid,  turn heat to very low and cook for 20
minutes. Lift  the lid,  mix gently but quickly with a fork and cover
again. Cook for 5-10 minutes until tender.
