Title: Cheddar-Olive Bread
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 ea French Bread; Loaf, Unsliced
3 c  Cheddar; Sharp, Grated
3 oz Pimento-StuffedOlives;Sliced
Mix the cheese, olives, and mayonnaise together.
Spread on the cut surface of the French Bread, which has been sliced
horizontally.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 to 30 minutes, then slice
into thick slices and serve hot.
