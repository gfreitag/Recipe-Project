Title: Cheddar Dumplings
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
3 qt Boiling Water
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 pt Sour Cream
16 oz Cheddar; Md, Shredded
2 ea Eggs; Lg
1 c  Unbleached Flour
1 t  Salt
MMMMM-------------------------GARNISHES------------------------------
1 x  Parsley
1 x  Paprika
Mash the cheddar cheese and add the eggs mixing well.
Stir in the flour and salt.  Drop by TBLS into the rapidly boiling
water then cover and boil for 15 minutes. Drain and serve with melted
butter and sour cream. Sprinkle with chopped parsley or paprika, if
desired.
