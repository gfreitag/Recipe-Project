Title: Suet or Carrot Pudding
Categories: Penndutch, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Suet, chopped
1 ea Egg
1 x  *dissolved in:
2 T  Brown sugar
1/2 ea Lemon, grated rind of
1 t  Cinnamon
1 t  Cloves
1/2 c  Currants
1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Carrot, cooked, mashed
1 c  Molasses
1 t  Baking soda
1/4 c  Water, hot
1/4 t  Salt
1 t  Allspice
1 t  Nutmeg
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Citron, chopped
Mix all the ingredients together, adding the dissolved baking soda
last. Put into a buttered mold and steam for 3 hours. (May need to
use extra flour to thicken the batter).
