Title: Lebaneese Eggplant
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Tomatoes (drained) 16 oz.
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Brown sugar
1/4 t  Dried whole basil
1/8 t  Pepper
2 T  Grated Parmesean cheese
1 ea Medium eggplant -peel & dice
1/2 c  Shopped onion
1 ea Clove of garlic
1/2 c  Sliced mushroom
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 T  All purpose flour
Cook eggplant in boiling water (salted) 8-10 min., drain well. Saute
onion, garlic, mushrooms in olive oil. Add flour, stir until smooth.
Add tomatoes and other ingredients. Bring to a boil. Remove from
heat. Layer in lightly grease 1 qt. casserole, top with Parmeasan
cheese. Bake at 375 degree for 25 minutes.
