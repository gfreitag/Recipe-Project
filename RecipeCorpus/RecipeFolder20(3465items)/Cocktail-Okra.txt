Title: Cocktail Okra
Categories: Vegetable, Can/preserv
Yield: 5 servings
1/2 c  Water
6 T  Pickling Salt
1 T  Celery Seed
1 T  Mustard Seed
2 lb Fresh Tender Okra Pods
5 ea Hot Red/Green Peppers
5 ea Cloves Garlic, Peeled
1 qt Distilled Vinegar
Wash Okra and pack into clean jars with screw tops.
Into each jar put 1 red or green hot pepper and 1 clove garlic. Bring
remaining ingredients to a boil and pour over the okra, filling the
jars to overflowing.  Seal while hot and let age for 2 months before
using. Makes 5 pints
