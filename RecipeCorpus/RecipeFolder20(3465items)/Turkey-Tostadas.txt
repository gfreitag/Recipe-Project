Title: Turkey Tostadas
Categories: Poultry, Tex/mexican, Turkey
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Dried Oregano
1/2 ts Thyme
Dash Of Hot Sauce
1/4 c  + 2 t. Mozzarella Cheese
(1 1/2 Oz.)
1/4    C +2 T. Plain Low Fat Yogurt
6    (6 in.) Flour Tortillas
1/2 lb Ground Turkey
1/4 c  Minced Onion
2 cl Garlic Minced
1    Jalapeno Pepper, Seeded &
Minced
1 cn (8 Oz.) Tomato Sauce
Place Tortillas On A Baking Sheet, Bake At 350 F. For 7 To 10 Min. OR
Until Crisp.  Set Aside. Combine Turkey, Onion, Garlic & Jalapeno
Pepper in A Large Skillet Coated With Cooking Spray; Cook Over Medium
Heat Until Meat Is No Longer Pink, Stirring To Crumble Meat. Add
Tomato Sauce, Oregano, Thyme & Hot Sauce; Stir Well.  Simmer 10 Min.
OR Until Thickened. Spread About 1/3 C. Turkey & Tomato Sauce Mixture
Over Each Reserved Tortilla; Spinkle Each With 1 T. Mozzarella
Cheese.  Broil 8 Inches From Heat Until Cheese Melts.  Top Each
Tostada With 1 T. Lowfat Yogurt. Garnish With Jalapeno Pepper Slices
If Desired.
