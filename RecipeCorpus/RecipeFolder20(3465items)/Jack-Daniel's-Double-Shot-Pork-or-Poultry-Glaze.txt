Title: Jack Daniel's Double Shot Pork or Poultry Glaze
Categories: Meat, Poultry, Salsa/sauce, Chicken
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb LEMON JUICE
1/2 ts BLACK PEPPER, freshly ground
1/2 c  JACK DANIEL'S WHISKEY
1 1/3 c  HONEY
Combine all ingredients; mix well. Serve as a sauce or glaze for
grilled chicken or pork. Makes 2 cups. Shared by ELLIE COLLIN,
Prodigy ID# CMKD93F.
