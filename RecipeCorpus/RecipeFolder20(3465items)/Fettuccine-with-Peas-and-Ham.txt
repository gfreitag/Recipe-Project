Title: Fettuccine with Peas and Ham
Categories: Pasta, Main dish, Pork
Yield: 4 servings
1 x  Salt and pepper
1 1/4 c  Whipping cream
1 ea 10 oz pk frozen tiny peas
4 oz Boiled ham chopped
5 T  Unsalted butter
1 c  Parmesan cheese
6 ea Green onions
1 lb Fettuccine cooked
8 oz Mushrooms sliced
Melt  butter in heavy large skillet over medium head.
Add shallots and saute until soft.   Add mushrooms,
increase heat to high and cook until mushrooms  are very lightly
browned. Add cream and boil  two
minutes. Stir  in peas and cook about 30 seconds.
Reduce heat to low;  blend in ham cheese and fettuccine and toss
until heated, well combined and sauce clings to pasta. Season to
taste. Serve immediately.
