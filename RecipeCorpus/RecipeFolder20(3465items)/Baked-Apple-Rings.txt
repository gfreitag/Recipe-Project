Title: Baked Apple Rings
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Water
4 x  Baking Apples (ex. Rome)
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
1/2 t  Powdered Cloves
Preheat oven to 400 deg F.
Core and slice apples into 1/2" rings. place in shallow baking dish.
In saucepan, combine and heat remaining ingredients. Pour over apples
and bake 15 minutes or until tender, turning to baste once or twice.
Variation: Very good with red-hot candies put in the center of each
ring.
