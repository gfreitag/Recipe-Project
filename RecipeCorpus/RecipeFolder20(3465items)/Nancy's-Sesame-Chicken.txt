Title: Nancy's Sesame Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Asian, Chicken
Yield: 2 servings
2 ts Tobasco sauce (to taste)
Flour
2 x  Chicken cutlets
2 tb Sesame Oil
2 ts Sugar
Marinate chicken cutlets in sesame oil, sugar, and tobasco sauce.
Dredge in flour.
Spray med skillet with Pam. Place chicken cutlets in hot pan, and
brown slightly on both sides.
Delicious served with Nancy's Sesame Noodles!
