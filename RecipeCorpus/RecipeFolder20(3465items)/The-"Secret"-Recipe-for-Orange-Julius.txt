Title: The "Secret" Recipe for Orange Julius
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Simple or vanilla syrup.
1 ts Instant vanilla pudding mix
16 oz Orange Juice
Ice
Water
Put orange juice in blender.  Fill with ice to 3/4 full.  Add water
to just cover ice.  Add syrup and "secret powder" (i.e. instant
vanilla pudding mix; use a good brand).  Blend until smooth.
