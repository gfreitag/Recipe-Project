Title: Red Pepper-Sour Cream Sauce
Categories: Vegetable, Salsa/sauce, Tex/mexican, Chilies
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Dairy Sour Cream
1 t  Sugar
3 ea Red Bell Peppers; *
1 ea Red Jalapeno Pepper; *
*  Peppers should be roasted and peeled.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Place bell peppers and chile in food processor
workbowl fitted with steel blade or in a blender container; cover and
process until well blended. Stir in sour cream and sugar. Makes about
2 cups of sauce
