Title: Eggless Corn Muffins
Categories: Penndutch, Muffin
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Flour
1 t  Salt
1 c  Milk
1 c  Cornmeal
1/4 c  Sugar
2 t  Baking powder
2 T  Shortening, melted
Sift and mix together the dry ingredients. Add the milk and
shortening. Pour into greased muffin tins and bake at 350-F for 30
minutes.
