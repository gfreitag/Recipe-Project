Title: Sourdough Pancakes #1
Categories: Bread, Sourdough, Pancakes
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  Cooking Oil
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 t  Soda
1/2 c  Active Starter
1/2 c  Pancake Mix
1 ea Large Egg
Mix all ingredients well.  Be careful not to over mix.
Small lumps are ok. Lightly grease a hot cast iron griddle. Drop onto
griddle with a large spoon while the batter is still rising.
