Title: Chinese Cabbage & Cilantro Slaw
Categories: Salad
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Lime Juice
2 tb Vegetable Oil
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Red Pepper Flakes
1 cl Garlic Sliced
1 lb Chinese Cabbage
Finely Shredded
3/4 c  Thinkly Sliced Red Pepper
2 tb Finely Chopped Green Onions
2 tb Finely Chopped Fresh
Cilantro
Combine Cabbage, Red Pepper, Green Onions & Cilantro in A Large Bowl.
Toss & Set Aside. Combine Lime Juice, Oil, Salt, Red Pepper Flakes &
Garlic in A Smallbowl, Stirring With A Wire Whisk Unitl Well Blended.
Pour Lime Juicemixture Over Cabbage  Mixture, Tossing Well. Cover &
Chill Throughly.
(Fat 4.8. Chol. 0.)
