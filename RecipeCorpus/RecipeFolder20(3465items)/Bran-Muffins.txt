Title: Bran Muffins
Categories: Bread
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/4 c  Skim milk
1/2 c  Molasses
1 t  Baking soda, dissolved in wa
1 c  Raisins (optional)
1 c  White flour
2 c  Bran
1/4 c  Cornmeal
1 t  Salt
Mix all ingredients together and pour into a muffin tin, using either
nonstick pan or paper liners.  Bake 325F for 25 min.
