Title: Strawberry Shake
Categories: Fruit, Beverage
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Plain Yogurt
2 x  Whole Strawberries
1/2 c  Strawberries  (or more)
2 T  Honey
1 c  Cold Milk
Puree 1/2 cup strawberries and honey in blender or food processor.
Add milk and yogurt; blend until smooth. Pour into glasses; garnish
each with a whole strawberry. Yield about 2 cups.
