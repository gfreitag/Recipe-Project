Title: Shrimp or Oysters Brochette
Categories: Appetizer, Fish
Yield: 8 servings
1 ea Juice of lemon
1/2 ea Bacon for each shrimp
1 ea Stick of butter
2 ea Cloves of garlic
Heat lemon juice, butter an garlic to boiling.  Put 1/2 slice of bacon
around each shrimp.  Salt and pepper (red) to taste. Broil 15-20
minutes Turn once.
