Title: Spinach Quiche - Beverly's
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Bisquick
3 ea Eggs
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1 ea Box chopped spinach or broc
1/2 ea Onion
1/2 c  Bell pepper
1 c  Cheese
Spray pam,  Put first 4 ingredients in bottom of pie plate. Mix rest
with mixer to pour over other ingredients. Bake 400 degree for 35
minuutes.
