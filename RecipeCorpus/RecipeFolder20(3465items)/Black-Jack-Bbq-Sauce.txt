Title: Black Jack Bbq Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 servings
3 T  Chili powder
2 t  Salt
2 c  Chopped onions
1/4 c  Minced hot chili peppers
6 x  Cloves garlic, minced
1 c  Strong black coffee
1 c  Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and simmer 25 mins. Strain or
puree in a blender or food processor. Refrigerate between uses.
