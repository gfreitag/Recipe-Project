Title: Halibut and Cucumber Sauce
Categories: Fish, Main dish, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 ds Pepper
2 lb Halibut, Fresh
1/4 c  Butter
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1    Paprika
1/2 c  Cucumber, Shredded
1/2 c  Sour Cream
1/4 c  Miracle Whip
1 tb Chives, Snipped
2 ts Lemon Juice
1/4 ts Salt
Blend shredded cucumber with sour cream, Miracle Whip, chives, lemon
juice, 1/4 t salt and dash of pepper. Mix well and chill until
serving time. Place the halibut in a well greased wire grill basket.
In a sauce pan melt the butter. Stir in the 1 t salt and 1/8 t
pepper. Grill the halibut over medium-hot coals for 5-8 minutes
depending on thickness of steaks. Brush with butter mixture
occasionally. Turn and baste with the remaining butter mixture. Grill
the halibut until it flakes easily with a fork, 5-8 minutes more.
Sprinkle halibut with paprika and serve with chilled cucumber sauce.
From: Syd's Cookbook.
