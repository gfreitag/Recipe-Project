Title: Turkey Burgers
Categories: Poultry, Turkey, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Unsalted butter
1 lb Mushrooms; thinly sliced
4    Toasted hamburger buns
Mayonnaise
2 lb Ground turkey
1/4 lb Bacon
1 ts Chopped fresh sage leaves
-=OR=-
1/2 ts -Dried sage leaves
COARSELY CHOP THE BACON, place in a food processor and process until
smooth. Add the ground turkey and sage and process just until well
mixed. Scrape into a bowl and form the mixture into 4 patties. Place
in the refrigerator, covered to chill for 20 minutes. Heat the butter
in a medium skillet over medium heat. Add the patties and cook 4
minutes. Turn, add the mushrooms and continue to cook 5 to 7 minutes
more. Remove the patties from the heat and place on hamburger buns.
Garnish with some sauteed mushrooms. Serve immediately and offer
mayonnaise.
