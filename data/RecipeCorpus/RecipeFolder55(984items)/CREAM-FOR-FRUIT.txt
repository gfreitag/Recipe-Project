Title: CREAM FOR FRUIT
Categories: Fruits, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Sweet milk
2    Egg whites
1 tb Sugar
Butter size of a nutmeg
1 ts Cornstarch
This recipe is an excellent substitute for pur cream, to be eaten on
freh berries and fruit.

Heat one cup of milk until boiling.  Beat whites of two eggs together
with sugar, butter and then add half a cupful of cold milk and a
teaspoonful of cornstarch; stir well together until very light and
smooth, then add it to the boiling milk.  Cook until it thickens --
IT MUST NOT BOIL--. Set aside to cool.  It should be of the
consistency of real fresh cream. Serve in a creamer.
