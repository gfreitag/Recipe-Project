Title: CORNMEAL MUSH
Categories: Penndutch, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Cornmeal
2 qt Water, boiling
1 ts Salt
Moisten cornmeal with enough cold water to make a paste. Stir paste
into boiling salted water, beating thoroughly. Let cook over slow
fire, stirring constantly, about 10 minutes. Pour into a double
boiler and cook slowly for 1 hour. Source: Pennsylvania Dutch Cook
Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
