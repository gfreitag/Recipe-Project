Title: COTTAGE CHEESE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Lime jello
1 c  Boiling water
1 pt Cottage cheese
3 ea Carrots grated
1 ea Onion grated
3 ea Stalks celery grated
1/2 c  Salad dressing
1/2 c  Milk
1 pk Knox geletin
Dissolve jello in water. Mix the gelatin in a little bit of cold
water.Mix rest of ingredients to-gether then mix gelatin into jello
and add to other ingredients. Put in 13" x 9 " pan and refrigerate
till set.
