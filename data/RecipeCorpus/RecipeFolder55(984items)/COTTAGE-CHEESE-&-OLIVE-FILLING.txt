Title: COTTAGE CHEESE & OLIVE FILLING
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Cottage cheese
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Chili sauce
1/2 c  Chopped ripe olives
Salt and white pepper
:    Put the cottage cheese through a sieve. To it add the
Worcestershire sauce, chili sauce and olives. Season to taste with
salt and white pepper. Jeannie Allen
