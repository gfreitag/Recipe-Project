Title: CRANBERRY - RASBERRY SALAD
Categories: Salads, Veg
Yield: 1 9x13 pan
1 pk Rasberry jello
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
1 c  Dream whip
2 c  Marshmellows
1 cn 14 oz. pineapple tidbits
Drained
1 c  Whole cranberries
Mix jello as per instructions and let set until starts to gel, then
whip and add 1 cup dreamwhip, 2 c marshmellows, the drained pineapple
and can of cranberries. Put in greased 9x13 pan and let set, cut in
squares to serve.
