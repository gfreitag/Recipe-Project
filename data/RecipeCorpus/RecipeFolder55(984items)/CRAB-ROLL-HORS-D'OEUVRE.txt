Title: CRAB ROLL HORS D'OEUVRE
Categories: Appetizers, Fish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Backfin crabmeat
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
Season slightly with your choice of the following: salt cream onion
tabasco
mustard
sherry pepper
:
from crabmeat. Very gently mix crab with cream cheese, being careful
not to break the pieces. Shape into a log, sprinkle with parsley,
chill, and serve with crackers. Mrs. James Olfson
