Title: CRAN CAKE W/SAUCE
Categories: C, K, E
Yield: 1 Servings
DAWN TURNBLAD
3 Tablespoons Butter 1 Cup Sugar 2 Cups Flour 1 Cup Milk 3 Teaspoons
Baking Powder 1/4 Teaspoon Salt 3 Cups Fresh (Whole) Cranberries

Mix ingredients and pour into a prepared 9x9" pan. Bake at 350
degrees for 35-45 minutes.
