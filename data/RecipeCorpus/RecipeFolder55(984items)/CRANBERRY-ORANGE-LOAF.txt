Title: CRANBERRY-ORANGE LOAF
Categories: Desserts, Loaves, Fruit, L.b.
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 c  Cranberries
1 3/4 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg
3/4 c  Orange juice concentrate
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
Coarsely chop cranberries. Mix dry ingredients, stir in cranberries.
Mix liquids together, then add to dry, beat for about a half minute
(batter will be lumpy). Greased loaf pan. 350 degrees for 60 - 65
minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean.
