Title: CORNISH HENS WITH GLAZED ORANGES
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
3    Rock Cornish Hens (about 1
-1/2 lb Ea)
2 tb Butter, melted
GLAZED ORANGES
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 tb Honey
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/8 ts Paprika
Place hens, breast sides up, on rack in shallow roasting pan; brush
with butter. Roast uncovered in 350 oven for 30 minutes. Prepare
Glazed Oranges. Mix remaining ingredients; brush half of the orange
juice mixture over hens. Roast, uncovered, brushing with remaining
mixture, until hens are done, about 45 minutes.
