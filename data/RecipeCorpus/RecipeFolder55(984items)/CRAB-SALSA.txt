Title: CRAB SALSA
Categories: Fish
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 lb Crabmeat
1/2 c  Lime juice
1 ts Tangerine peel, grated
2/3 c  Tangerine
1/2 c  Tomatoes, seeded and chopped
2 tb Purple onions, chopped
1 tb Cilantro, chopped
1 tb Jalapeno, chopped
1/8 ts Salt
Combine ingredients in a bowl, toss gently. Cover and Chill Posted to
