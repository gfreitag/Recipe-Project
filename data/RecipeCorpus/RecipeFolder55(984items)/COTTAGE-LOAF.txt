Title: COTTAGE LOAF
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Finely minced lean beef
Seasoning
Pinch of mixed herbs
1    Egg white
1    Small carrot grated
1    Medium tomato finely chopped
1    Medium onion finely chopped
Pre-heat the oven to 190 degrees c or 375 degrees f or gas 5. Place
all the ingredients in a mixing bowl and blend well until combined.
Place a strip of foil on a baking sheet and place the mixture onto
it. Shape into a loaf or use a lined loaf tin. Bake for one hour.

Serve hot or cold.
