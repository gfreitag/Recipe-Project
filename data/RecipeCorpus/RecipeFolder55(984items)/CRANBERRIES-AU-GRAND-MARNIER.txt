Title: CRANBERRIES AU GRAND MARNIER
Categories: Condiments, Fruits, Harned 1994, Pork, Poultry
Yield: 3 Cups
16 oz Fresh cranberries (1 pkg.)
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Grand Marnier or
1/2 c  Other orange-flavor liqueur
Wash cranberries; drain well.  Place cranberries in an ungreased 13 x
9 x 2" baking dish; sprinkle with sugar. Cover and bake at 350 F. for
one hour. Remove from oven and let cool. Stir in Grand Marnier. Chill
until ready to serve.

Serve as a condiment with beef, pork or poultry.

From _The Gathering_ by The Blue Bird Circle/Houston, TX. In
_America's Best Recipes: A 1989 Hometown Collection_. Birmingham, AL:
Oxmoor House, Inc., 1989. Pg. 279. ISBN 0-8487-0765-6.
