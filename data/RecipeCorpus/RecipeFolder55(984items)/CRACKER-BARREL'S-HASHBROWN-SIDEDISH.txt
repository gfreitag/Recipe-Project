Title: CRACKER BARREL'S HASHBROWN SIDEDISH
Categories: Potatoes, Pasta, Salsa
Yield: 6 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
2 lb Bag frozen hashbrowns --
Thaw
1 md Onion -- chop fine
6 tb Margarine -- melted
1 lb Sour cream
8 oz Cheddar cheese-shredded
10 oz Cream chicken soup
Corn flakes -- crushed
4 tb Margarine -- melted
In a 9x13 pan, make single layers of the following: potatoes, onions,
6 tb melted margarine, sour cream, cheddar cheese and soup. Sprinkle
top with corn flakes. Drizzle 4 tb margarine over top. Bake 350~ for
1 hour or until bubbly and piping hot. Source: Gloria Pitzer's Make
Alike Recipes
