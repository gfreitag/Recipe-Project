Title: CREAM CHEESE & APRICOT FILLING
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings
Yield: 1 Servings
Cream cheese
Stewed or canned apricots
Mayonnaise
Nuts ( opt )
:    Mix together equal parts cream cheese and apricot pulp ( made by
pressing through a sieve the stewed or canned apricots, that have been
throughly drained. )Add mayonnaise to taste and chopped nuts if
desired. Jeannie Allen
