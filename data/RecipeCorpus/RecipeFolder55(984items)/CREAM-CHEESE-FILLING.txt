Title: CREAM CHEESE FILLING
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/2 c  Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
1    Egg
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
Recipe by: SUMMER DESSERTS Beat cream cheese, sugar, salt and egg in
small bowl on medium speed about 1 minute, scraping bowl constantly,
until smooth and creamy. Stir in chocolate chip.
