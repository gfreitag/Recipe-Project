Title: CRANBERRY ORANGE SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
36 oz Cranberries -- fresh
12 oz Orange Juice, From Frozen
Concentrate
12 oz Water
1 1/2 c  Sugar -- 1 1/4 for a more
Tart flavor
4 lg Oranges -- to 6, peel,
Section,
Cut into bite size
12 oz Walnuts -- coarsely chopped
3 tb Cornstarch
1/4 c  Water -- to 1/3 C
Mix cranberries with orange juice, 12 oz water and sugar. Cook mixture
until it boils.  Mix cornstarch w/ 1/4 c water; stir into boiling
berry mixture. Reduce heat and cook until the cranberries are as open
as desired (3 min for partially open; 10 minutes for mostly open).
Remove from heat, add oranges and walnuts. Chill and serve.
