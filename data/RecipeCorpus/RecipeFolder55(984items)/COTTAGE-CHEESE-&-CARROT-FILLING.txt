Title: COTTAGE CHEESE & CARROT FILLING
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings
Yield: 1 Servings
Cottage cheese
Grated raw carrot
1 ts Sweet pickles, finely choppd
Salt and pepper
To 3 parts cottage cheese, add 1 part carrot and the sweet oickles.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Variation; Chopped nuts may be substituted for sweet pickles. Jeannie
