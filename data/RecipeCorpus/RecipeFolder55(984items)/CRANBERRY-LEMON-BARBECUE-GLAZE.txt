Title: CRANBERRY LEMON BARBECUE GLAZE
Categories: Bbq sauces, Other sauce
Yield: 1 Cup
1 cn Cranberry sauce, whole,
-8 oz can
1/2 ts Lemon peel, finely shredded
2 tb Lemon juice
1/8 ts Rosemary, dried, crushed
In saucepan, combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil. Use to baste
lamb or pork last 15 minutes of grilling.
