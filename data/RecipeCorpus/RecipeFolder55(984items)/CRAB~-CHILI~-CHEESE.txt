Title: CRAB~ CHILI~ CHEESE
Categories: Appetizers, Chili
Yield: 10 Servings
8 oz Cream cheese
1 cn Crabmeat
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1 ts Horseradish
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Tobasco sauce
1 ts Chopped chives
1/4 ts Parsley
1 ts Salt
Slice cream cheese in half and cover with crabmeat. Just
before serving top with chili sauce mixture. Serve with
crackers.
