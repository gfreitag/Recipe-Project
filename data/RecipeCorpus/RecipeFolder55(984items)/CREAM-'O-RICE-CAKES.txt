Title: CREAM 'O RICE CAKES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Uncooked Cream Of
-Rice
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Baking powder
3/4 ts Baking soda
2 tb Lemon juice, plus
-enough milk to make
3/4 c  Total (milk & juice)
1    Egg
2 tb Oil
In a bowl stir together dry ingredients & make a well in the center.
In another bowl, mix together liquids. Stir liquid into the dry
ingredients and let stand 10 minutes. Heat lightly greased griddle
over medium heat. Stir mixture and pour 1/4 to 1/3 c on griddle. Cook
carefully turning once to brown both sides. [Note: watch 'em close,
they cook fast] ~--
