Title: CRANBERRY SORBET
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar
1 lb Cranberries, fresh or frozen
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Chambord or other raspberry
-- liqueur
Make a syrp by combining the water & suagr in a non-corrosive pot &
bring to a boil.  Add cranberries & orange juice & simmer until the
berries burst from their skins. This should take about 25 to 30
minutes. Remove from the heat & puree in a food processor or blender.
Strain through a fine sieve to remove the skins. Let cool. Combine
with the rest of the ingredients & freeze following the instructions
for making Lemon Sorbet.
