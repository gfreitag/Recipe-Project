Title: CREAM CHEESE BARS
Categories: Misc
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar; packed
1/4 c  Butter
1 c  Bisquick baking mix
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
1 pk Cream cheese; 8 oz softened
1/4 c  Sugar; granulated
1 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
1    Egg
Approx. Cook Time:
Fat grams    per serving:
:45
Preheat oven to 350F. Grease 8" square pan. Mix brown sugar and
butter till well blended. Stir in Bisquick and walnuts till mixture
is crumbly; reserve 1 cup. Press remaining mixture in pan with
fingers. Bake 12 minutes.   Mix cream cheese and granulated sugar;
beat in remaining ingredients till smooth. Spread cream cheese
mixture over layer in pan; sprinkle with crumbly mixture. Bake till
centre is firm, about 25 minutes. NB. Results aren't always
consistent (sometimes it's too crumbly or the bottom layer sticks in
the pan).
