Title: CRANBERRY WALNUT SQUARES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 60 Servings
1    Recipe cookie crust
4    Eggs
1 c  Corn syrup, light or dark
1 c  Sugar
3 tb Butter or margarine, melted
2 c  Cranberries, coarsely
-chopped
1 c  Walnuts, chopped
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Prepare and bake the cookie crust.
Meanwhile, in a large bowl, beat eggs corn syrup, sugar, and butter
until well blended. Stir in cranberries and walnuts. Immediately pour
over hot crust; spread evenly. Bake until set, about 25 to 30
minutes. Cool completely. Cut into 1 1/2 inch squares. Posted to
