Title: CORNMEAL WAFFLES #2
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 3 Servings
1 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
3 ea Egg yolks; beaten
4 tb Melted butter or oil
1 1/2 c  Milk
3 ea Egg whites,; beaten until
-stiff
1/2 c  Cornmeal
Mix all dry ingredients together.  Mix wet ingredients together and
combine with dry ingredients.  Do not over mix. Carefully, fold in
beaten egg whites.  Cook according to your waffle iron instructions.
