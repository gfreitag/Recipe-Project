Title: CRANBERRY DIJON CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Can jellied cranberry sauce
3/4 c  GREY POUPON dijon mustard
1 ts Grated orange peel
6    Chicken thighs
In small saucepan, over medium heat, cook and stir cranberry sauce,
mustard and orange peel until blended and smooth; cool completely.
Grill or broil chicken 6" from heat source for 25 to 30 minutes,
turning occasionally and brushing with sauce during last 10 minutes.
