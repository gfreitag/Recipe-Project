Title: COTTAGE MASHED POTATOES
Categories: Cheese, Low-fat, Potatoes
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Servings potato flakes
1 1/2 tb Minced onions
8 oz Cottage cheese, 1/2% fat
Paprika
Prepare potatoes as directed, adding oniion and using 1/2 cup less
liquid. Mix in cheese.  Put into 1 quart casserole. Sprinkle with
paprika. Bake about 30 min. at 350.
