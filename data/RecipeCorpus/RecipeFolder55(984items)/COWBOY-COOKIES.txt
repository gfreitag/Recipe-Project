Title: COWBOY COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 96 Mm#: 2077
2 c  Flour, sifted
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
2 md Eggs
2 c  Rolled oats
1 c  Nuts (optional)
1 pk (6oz) semisweet choc. bits
Sift together flour, soda, salt and baking powder; set aside. Blend
together shortening and sugars. Add eggs and beat until light and
fluffy. Add flour mixture, blend well. Add rolled oats, vanilla, nuts
and chocolate bits. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto a greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Yields 8-10 dozen cookies.
