Title: CRANBERRY-APPLE CIDER
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 8 Servings
1 qt Apple Cider
1 qt Cranberry Juice Cocktail
1/2 ts Ground Allspice
1/2 ts Ground Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground Cloves
1/4 ts Ground Nutmeg
Combine all of the ingredients in a 4-quart Dutch oven. Warm, over
high heat, until the mixture comes to a boil. Remove from the heat
and serve.

Makes 2 quarts or eight 8-oz servings
