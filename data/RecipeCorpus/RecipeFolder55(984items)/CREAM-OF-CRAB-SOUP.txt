Title: CREAM OF CRAB SOUP
Categories: Soups, Appetizers, Fish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Crabmeat
1/4 ts Celery salt
1    Chicken bouillion cube
1 c  Boiling water
Dash pepper
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 qt Milk
1 c  Butter
Chopped parsley
3 tb Flour
Dissolve boullion cube in water. Cook onion in butter until tender,
blend in flour and seasonings. Add milk and bouillion gradually; cook
until thick, stirring constantly. Add crabmeat, heat. Garnish with
parsley.
