Title: CRAB DIP :::GWHP32A
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood, Dips
Yield: 12 Servings
24 oz Cream cheese
1 ts Onion; chopped
1 lb Crab meat
1 x  Salt & pepper to taste
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sherry
2 ts Mustard
1 ts Sugar; powered
1. Combine all the ingredients but the sherry in the top of a double
boiler and heat until creamy. Add sherry to taste; half a cup is not
too much, depending on whether it is to be served from a chafing dish
or as a dip, when the mixture should be slightly thicker.
