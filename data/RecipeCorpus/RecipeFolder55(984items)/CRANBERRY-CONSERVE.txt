Title: CRANBERRY CONSERVE
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
2 qt Cranberries
1/2 lb Raisins
1 c  Diced orange pulp
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
6 c  Sugar
Wash cranberries.  Remove stems.  Cover fruit with water. Cook until
soft. Add raisins, sugar, and orange. Simmer slowly until thick. Add
nuts and cook 5 minutes. The Household Searchlight
