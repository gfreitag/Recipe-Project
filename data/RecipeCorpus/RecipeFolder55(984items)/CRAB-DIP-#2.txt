Title: CRAB DIP #2
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Crabmeat
1/2 c  Mayonnaise Or Salad Dressing
Garlic Salt, To Taste
2 tb Onion, Grated
2 ts Prepared Mustard
2 ts Powdered Sugar
2/3 c  White Wine
Mix together all ingredients except crabmeat.  Heat slowly. Add
Crabmeat. Serve warm with crackers.
