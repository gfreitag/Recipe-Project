Title: CRAZY CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 9 Servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
3 tb Cocoa
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1 tb Vinegar
6 tb Oil
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Cold water
Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, salt and baking soda in an 8-inch square
pan. Make three holes in the mixture. Put the vinegar in one, the oil
in another and the vanilla in the third. pour cold water over all and
stir to mix well. Bake at 350 deg f. for 25 minutes. Dust top with
confectioners sugar.
