Title: CREAM CHEESE TOPPED CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIE
Categories: Cookies, Kwc, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 Pies
8 oz Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
8 oz Cream Cheese
1 lb Powdered Sugar
4 oz Coconut(optional)
2 ts Vanilla
First make your favorite chocolate chip cookie dough. Then put 8 oz
into a 9" pie tin.  Spread it evenly around. Mix the topping well and
divide it equally between the 3 cookie pies. Spread the topping
evenly and bake in 350^ oven til done.  15-30 minutes.

Enjoy!!!
