Title: CRANBERRY FRUIT NUT BREAD
Categories: Breads, Fruits, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 1/4 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 c  Shortening
1 ts Grated orange peel
1 ea Beaten egg
3/4 c  Orange juice
1 c  Chopped cranberries
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Sift together dry ingredients.  Cut in shortening. Combine peel,
juice and egg.  Add to dry ingredients, mixing just enough to
moisten. Fold in cranberries and nuts. Turn into greased 9x5x3 inch
loaf pan. Bake at 350Ã¸ for 60 minutes.  Cool, wrap, and store
overnight before slicing. From "Make It Better with CRANBERRIES" Cape
