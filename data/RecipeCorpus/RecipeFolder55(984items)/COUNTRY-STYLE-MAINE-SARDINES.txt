Title: COUNTRY-STYLE MAINE SARDINES
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 6 Servings
2 cn (4 oz each) Maine Sardines
2 tb Parsley; chopped
3 c  Mashed potatoes; seasoned
- with salt and pepper
2    Tomatoes; thinly sliced
Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Cheese; shredded
Drain sardines.  Add parsley to potatoes and mix well. Spread
potatoes in the bottom of a well-greased baking dish (8 x 8 x 2)
Arrange sardines over potaotes. Cover with tomato slices. Sprinkle
with salt and pepper. Top with cheese.  Bake in a moderate oven (350
F) for 25 to 30 minutes or until heated through and cheese melts.

From "Flavor of Maine - Sardine Secrets", US Dept. of the Interior,
1968 MM by Dave Sacerdote
