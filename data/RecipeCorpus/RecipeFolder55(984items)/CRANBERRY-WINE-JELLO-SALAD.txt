Title: CRANBERRY WINE JELLO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lg Pkg. rasberry jello
1 1/4 c  Boiling water
1 lg Can whole cranberry sauce
1 lg Can undrained crushed
Pineapple
1 c  Chopped nuts (walnuts or
Pecans)
3/4 c  Port wine
8 oz Cream cheese
1 c  Sour cream
Dissolve jello in boiling water.  Stir cranberry sauce in thoroughly.
Add pineapple, nuts and wine.  Pour into a 9x 13 inch glass dish and
chill for 24 hrs. When ready to serve, stir cream cheese until soft,
add sour cream and beat well.  spread on top of Jello
