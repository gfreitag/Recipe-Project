Title: CRAWFISH CHILI
Categories: Cajun, Main dish, Meats, Fish, Chili
Yield: 16 Servings
2 lb Lean ground beef
2 lb Crawfish tails
1 ts Garlic, chopped fine
2 ts Salt
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Dried mint
1 tb Dried parsley
3 tb Chili powder
1 cn (8 oz) tomato sauce
1 c  Dry white wine
1 x  Water
1 ts Lemon or lime juice
1 c  Chopped onions
1 x  Bacon drippings
Brown meat in bacon drippings.  Combine all other ingredients with
meat and bring to a boil.  Simmer for a few hours. From Justin
Wilson's "Gourmet and Gourmand Cookbook"
