Title: CRANBERRY FIG RELISH
Categories: Relishes, Fruits
Yield: 3 Cups
3/4 lb Cranberries (3 cups)
1 c  Finely chopped dried figs
1 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1 tb Grated orange rind
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Maple syrup
In heavy saucepan, combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce
heat and simmer, uncovered, for about 50 minutes or until thickened,
stirring frequently. Remove from heat; let cool. Source: The
Vancouver Sun Oct 5/94
