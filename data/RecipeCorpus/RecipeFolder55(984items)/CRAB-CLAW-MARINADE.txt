Title: CRAB CLAW MARINADE
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 ts Tarragon
3/4 ts Black pepper
3/4 ts Salt
3/4 ts Sugar
MMMMM-----------------------FOOD PROCESSOR----------------------------
1 c  Parsley
1 c  Celery
1 c  Scallions
4    Cloves of garlic (4-10)
Mix all together, pour over claws and serve.
