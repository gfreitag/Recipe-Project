Title: CRAB MEAT BACON ROLLS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Tomato juice
1    Egg well beaten
1 c  Crab meat drained and
Flaked
1/2 c  Dry fine bread crumbs
1 tb Chopped parsley
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
Dash pepper
9    Slices bacon cut in half
Mix tomato juice and egg. Add crab meat, crumbs, parsley, lemon juice
and seasonings; mix thoroughly. Roll into 18 fingers, about 2 inches
long. Wrap each roll with 1/2 slice bacon; fasten with toothpick.
Broil 5 inches from heat about 10 minutes, turning often to brown
evenly.
