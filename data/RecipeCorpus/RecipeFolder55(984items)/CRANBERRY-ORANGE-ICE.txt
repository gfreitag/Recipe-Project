Title: CRANBERRY-ORANGE ICE
Categories: Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 2 Quarts
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Orange juice
4 c  Cranberry juice cocktail
In a medium saucepan, combine sugar and orange juice. Stir over
medium heat until sugar dissolves.  Cool to room temperature. Stir in
cranberry juice cocktail. Pour into ice cream canister. Freeze in ice
cream maker according to manufacturer's directions.  Makes 2 quarts
