Title: CORRERO'S RED BEANS & RICE
Categories: Beans, Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Dried red beans -- soaked
Overnight
1    Meaty ham bone
2 lg Onions -- chopped
1    Bell pepper -- chopped
2    Ribs celery -- chopped
1/4 c  Parsley -- chopped
2    Cloves garlic -- finely
Chopped
Salt and pepper -- to taste
pn Sugar
Bay leaf
2 lb Sausage links -- your
Choice
Combine all except parsley and cook until beans are done. Serve over
rice. Add parsley just before serving. Variation: Omit ham bone and
sausage and use a center slice of ham, cut in small pieces.
