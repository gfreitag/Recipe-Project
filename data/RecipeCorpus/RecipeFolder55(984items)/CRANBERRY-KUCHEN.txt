Title: CRANBERRY KUCHEN
Categories: Cakes, Fruits
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  Margarine
1 1/4 c  Granulated sugar; divided
1 ts Vanilla
3    Eggs
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/4 c  Sour cream
2 c  Chopped cranberries
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/4 c  Lightly packed brown sugar
2 tb All-purpose flour
2 tb Chopped almonds
1 tb Margarine
1/2 ts Cinnamon
CREAM thoroughly margarine, 1 cup sugar and vanilla in large bowl with
electric mixer.  ADD eggs, one at a time, beating until light and
fluffy. COMBINE flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add flour
mixture and sour cream alternately to creamed mixutre, ending with
flour. (Batter will be fairly thick). Combine cranberries with
remaining 1/4 cup sugar. Spread half the batter in greased 10 "
springform pan, sprinkle with cranberries; top with remaining batter.
COMBINE topping ingredients; sprinkle over batter. BAKE at 350 F for
50 to 55 min. Serve warm or cool.
