Title: CRANBERRY/RASPBERRY PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 50 Servings
3 1/2 ga Raspberry sherbet
2 1/2 qt Vanilla ice cream
2 qt Cranberry cocktail
2 qt Sprite
Soften sherbet and ice cream.  Add 1 qt. of juice; mix. Add remaining
juice; blend well.  Just before serving, add Sprite.
