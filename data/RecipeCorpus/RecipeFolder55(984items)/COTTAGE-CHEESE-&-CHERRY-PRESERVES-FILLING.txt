Title: COTTAGE CHEESE & CHERRY PRESERVES FILLING
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings, Preserves
Yield: 1 Servings
Cottage cheese
Cherry preserves
Worcestershire sauce
Salt and pepper
:    Combine and mix throughly equal parts of cottage cheese and well
drained, chopped, cherry preserves. Season to taste with a few drops
Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper. Jeannie Allen
