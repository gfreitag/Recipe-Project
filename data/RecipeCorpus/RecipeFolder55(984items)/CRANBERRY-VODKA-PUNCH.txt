Title: CRANBERRY VODKA PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 15 Servings
1/2 qt Vodka
1 1/2 qt Cranberry juice (48 oz.)
3/4 qt Orange juice (24 oz.)
3/4 qt Pineapple juice (24 oz.)
Mix all ingredients together. If you wish, you can add as much ginger
ale as you want. This looks very nice with a frozen ring made with
one part cranberry juice, 1/2 part orange juice, and 1/2 part
pineapple juice. Recipe from Martha Kahn. Format into Meal-Master by
Karen Adler, SPXB77A@Prodigy.com..
