Title: CORNISH HENS & DRESSING IN WINE SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Finely chopped onions
1 c  Chopped green pepper
3 tb Bacon drippings
6 sl Fried bacon -- crumbled
3 c  White bread cubes
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Sage
6    Cornish hens
1/2 c  Melted butter
1/2 c  White wine
1    Clove garlic -- crushed
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Sage
Saute onion and green pepper in bacon drippings. Add bacon, bread
cubes, walnuts and seasonings. Stuff hens, place in roaster pan.
Combine remaining ingredients; pour in roaster around hens. Bake at
4oo degrees for 1 hour; baste occasionally with sauce. Turn off heat
and cool hens in sauce for 30 minutes before serving.
