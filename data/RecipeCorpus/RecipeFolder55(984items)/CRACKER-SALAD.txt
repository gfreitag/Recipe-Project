Title: CRACKER SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Saltine Cracker stack crush
1    Cup  Onion, minced
1 sm Pimentos, canned, drained
5    Boiled eggs, chopped
1    Cup  Sweet Pickle, chopped
1 md Green pepper, chopped
1 pt Salad dressing
Combine and blend well.  Spread onto bread for sandwiches, or use as a
salad.
Source:  Gail Reed
