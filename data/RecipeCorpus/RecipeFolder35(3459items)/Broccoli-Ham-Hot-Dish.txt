Title: Broccoli-Ham Hot Dish
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
3 c  Milk
1 1/2 lb Cooked ham -- cubed
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese --
Or swiss cheese
2 pk Frozen broccoli -- cut 10 oz
Each
2 c  Cooked rice
6 tb Butter or margarine
2 c  Bread crumbs
1 md Onion -- chopped
3 tb All-purpose flour
Cook broccoli according to package directions; drain. Spoon rice into
a 13x9x2 inch baking pan. Place broccoli over rice. Melt butter in a
large skillet. sprinkle 2 tablespoons of melted butter over the bread
crumbs and set aside.  In remaining butter, saute onion until soft.
Add flour, salt and pepper, stirring constantly until bubbly. Stir in
milk and continue cooking until sauce thickens and bubbles. Cook and
stir for 1 min; add ham and heat through. Pour over rice and
broccoli. Sprinkle the crumbs over all. Bake at 350 deg. for 30 min.
or until heated through. Sprinkle with cheese; let stand 5 min.
before serving. Yield 8 servings.
