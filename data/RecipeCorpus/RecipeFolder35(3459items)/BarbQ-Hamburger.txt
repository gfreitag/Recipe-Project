Title: BarbQ Hamburger
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Vinegar
1 ts Chili powder
2 ts Mustard
Chopped onion
1 lb Hamburger
2 tb Brown sugar
1 cn Tomato soup
Brown hamburger and onions.  Add other ingredients and simmer 20
minutes. Randy Rigg
