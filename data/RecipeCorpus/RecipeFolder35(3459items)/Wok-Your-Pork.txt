Title: Wok Your Pork
Categories: Meats, Country wom, Vegetables, Wok
Yield: 4 servings
(green beans, broccoli,
-mushrooms red pepper),
-defrosted
1 lg Tomatoes -- cut int wedges
1 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Soy sauce
Cooked rice or chow mein
Noodles
2 tb Cooking oil
1 lb Boneless pork top loin --
Thinly sliced
1 1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 pk Pea pods, frozen --
Defrosted 6 oz
1 pk Japanese vegetables --
Frozen 16 oz
In a wok or skillet, heat oil on high. Cook the meat with ginger and
garlic powder until meat is no longer pink. Add pea pods, Japanese
vegetables and tomato; cook until vegetables are heated through. Mix
cornstarch, water and soy sauce; pour over meat/vegetable mixture.
Cook and stir until sauce thickens. Serve immediately over rice or
over chow mein noodles. Yield: 4 servings.
