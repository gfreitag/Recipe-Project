Title: Deviled Eggs on Watercress
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs, Information
Yield: 6 servings
Dash of pepper
Paprika
4 ea Pitted ripe olives; halved
1 bn Watercress
4 ea Hard-cooked eggs
2 tb Mayonnaise
1 tb Capers; drained
4 ea Pitted ripe olives; chopped
2 ts Lemon juice
Slice eggs in half lengthwise, and carefully remove yolks.  Mash
yolks, and combine with next 5 ingredients.  Mix well.  Stuff egg
whites with yolk mixture.  Garnish eggs with paprika and olive
halves. Serve on a bed of fresh watercress.
