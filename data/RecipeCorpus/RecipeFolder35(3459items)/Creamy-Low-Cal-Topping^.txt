Title: Creamy Low-Cal Topping^
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Vanilla
2 tb Oil -=OR=-
3 tb Tahini -=OR=-
1/4 c  Cashews
1 1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  SWEETENER
1/4 c  White flour
1/4 ts Salt
Simmer together all ingredients except the last one, 5-10 minutes,
stirring. Scrape into blender. While blending add oil, nuts or tahini.
Chill.

Variations: Leave out vanilla and use one of the following: 1/4
teaspoon cherry, butterscotch, almond orange, or 18 teaspoon anise,
lemon or pure mint-peppermint. Source: Of These Ye May Freely Eat: A
Vegetarian Cookbook by JoAnn Rachor . ISBN 1-878726-02-1 Shared by
Carolyn Shaw 9-95.

Note: Ingredients in capital letters refer to either another recipe
or a cooking method which can be found elsewhere in the series.
Unfamiliar ingredients can be found in most large grocery stores or
health food stores.
