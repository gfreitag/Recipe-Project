Title: Parmesan Ranch Loaf
Categories: Breads, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 loaf
1/3 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
Garlic powder; opt
2 cn Pillsbury buttermilk
.  biscuits; 7.5oz
1/4 c  Salad dressing; ranch flavor
Preheat the oven to 375øF.  Spray a 9x5-inch loaf pan with nonstick
spray.

Separate the dough into 20 biscuits.  Stretch each biscuit slightly.
Dip one side of each biscuit in the salad dressing, then the Parmesan
cheese; fold in half so the filling is inside.  Place the biscuits
with the fold down in the spray coated pan, forming 4 crosswise rows
of 5 biscuits each. Sprinkle any remaining cheese over the top of the
biscuits.

Bake at 375øF  for about 25 to 35 minutes or until deep golden
brown.** Cool for 5 minutes; remove from the pan.  Serve warm.

(Garlic powder or minced garlic may be added with the salad dressing
for a garlic bread taste for Italian meals.)

**  Cover the pan with foil during the last 5 to 10 minutes of baking
time if necessary to prevent excessive browning.

Nutritional Information: per serving: 140 calories, 4g protein, 5g
fat, 19g carbohydrates, 1g dietary fiber, 5mg cholesterol, 440mg
sodium
