Title: Lowfat Brown Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Flour
2 tb Celery, diced
1 c  Beef broth (fat skimmed)
1 ea Bay leaf
1 x  Pepper, dash
1 tb Margarine
2 tb Carrot, diced
2 tb Onion, diced
2 tb Tomato puree
1/2 ea Garlic clove
1 x  Thyme leaves, dash
Melt margarine. Add flour and cook for 3 minutes. Add vegetables and
cook for 5 minutes until lightly browned. Remove from heat and stir
in broth. Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 4 to 5 minutes.
Let cool slightly. Remove bay leaf and process in blender until
smooth.
