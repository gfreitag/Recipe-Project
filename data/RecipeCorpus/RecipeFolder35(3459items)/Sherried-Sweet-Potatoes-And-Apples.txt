Title: Sherried Sweet Potatoes And Apples
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Fruit, Holiday
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/3 c  Dry sherry
3 lg Sweet potatoes
3    Granny Smith Apples --
Peeled and cored
1/4 c  Unsalted butter
Put sweet potatoes in a large kettle with enough water to cover. Boil
for 35 minutes, until tender.

Drain and cool.  (May be prepared in advance and refrigerated
overnight.

Peel the sweet potatoes, cut lengthwise into sixths, and crosswise
into 1/2-inch pieces.

Cut apples lengthwise into sixths and crosswise into 1 1/2-inch
peices.

In a large skillet, cook the apples in the butter over moderate heat,
stirring, for three minutes.  Stir in the lemon juice, brown sugar,
cinnamon and sherry. Bring to a boil, and simer three minutes. Add
sweet potatoes and cook, stirring gently, for two minutes, until
heated through.

Transfer to heated serving dish.
