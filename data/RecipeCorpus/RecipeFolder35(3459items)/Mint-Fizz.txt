Title: Mint Fizz
Categories: Beverages, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1    Egg white
3 1/2 c  Sparkling water
2 c  Crushed ice
3    (or 4) lemons
1/4 c  Mint leaves plus
4    Mint sprigs for garnish
1/3 c  Sugar
Juice the lemons. There should be 3/4 cup juice. Wash and stem the
mint leaves. Combine the lemon juice, mint leaves, sugar, and egg
white in a blender. Blend at high speed for 1 minute. Add the
sparkling water and ice. Blend until the mixture is smooth. Pour into
glasses and garnish with mint sprigs.
