Title: Apple Toast - SL 11/81
Categories: Breakfast, Breads, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1/4 c  Butter; divided
4 sl (1" thick) French bread
2 lg Cooking apples; peeled and
-thinly sliced
2 tb ;water
1 tb Sugar
Combine apples, water, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and 2 tablespoons
butter in a saucepan; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer
5 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally.
Melt remaining 2 tablespoons butter in an electric skillet at 350F;
place bread in skillet, and cook 4 minutes on each side or until
browned. Spoon apple mixture over toast. Serve hot.  Yield: 4
servings.

G. Pedersen of Mississippi, in November, 1981 "Southern Living".
