Title: Cool Whip Pie
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 3 servings
2    9 inch graham cracker crusts
Canned fruit of any kind,
Your choice, but drain it!
1 ct Cool Whip (12 oz.)
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 cn Condensed milk (small)
Mix together Cool Whip, lemon juice and condensed milk.  Mix in
drained fruit.  Pour into pie crusts and chill overnight. Randy Rigg
