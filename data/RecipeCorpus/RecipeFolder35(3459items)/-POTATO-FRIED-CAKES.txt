Title:  POTATO FRIED CAKES
Categories: Potatoes, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
Salt
Nutmeg
1 c  Mashed potatoes
2 c  Sugar
3 tb Melted lard
3    Eggs
3 c  Flour
Mix all ingredients together.  Roll out on floured board and cut with
round cookie cutter.  Drop one-by-one in a deep fryer and cook to a
light brown. Drain.
