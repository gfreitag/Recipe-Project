Title: Cajun Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome, Cajun/creol, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
6 tb Olive oil -- or vegetable
Oil
1 tb Dijon mustard
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
2 lb Red potatoes -- small
1/2 c  Red onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Green onion -- sliced
1/4 c  Fresh parsley -- minced
6 tb Cider vinegar -- divided
1/2 lb Kielbasa -- cooked & smoked
Or cajun sausage sliced
Cook the potatoes in boiling salted water for 20 to 30 min. or until
tender; drain. Rinse with cold water; cool completely. Cut into 1/4
in. slices; place in a large bowl. Add onions, parsley and 3
tablespoons vinegar; toss. In a med. skillet cook sausage in oil for
5-10 min. or until it begins to brown. Remove with slotted spoon and
add to potato mixture. To drippings in skillet, add mustard, garlic,
pepper, cayenne pepper and remaining vinegar; bring to a boil,
whisking constantly. Pour over salad; toss ga=ently. Serve
immediately. Yield: 6 servings.
