Title: Basic Shortbread
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 3 dozen
3 c  Flour
1/4 c  Cornstarch
1 lb Butter
1 c  Icing Sugar
Cream butter, add icing sugar and cream well.  Add flour and
cornstarch. Beat until light and fluffy.  Drop from teaspoon onto
ungreased cookie sheet.  Flatten with a fork or fingers. Bake at 350
for 12 minutes.
