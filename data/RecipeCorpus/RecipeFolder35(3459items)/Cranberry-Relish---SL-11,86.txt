Title: Cranberry Relish - SL 11/86
Categories: Side dish, Relishes, Fruit
Yield: 2 -1/2 cups
1 md Apple; unpeeled and chopped
8 oz Can unsweetened pineapple
-tidbits; drained
1/2 ea Thin-skinned orange; seeded
-and chopped
1 1/2 c  Fresh cranberries
Position knife blade in food processor bow; add orange.  Cover
with top; process 3 minutes or until orange peel is finely chopped.
Add remaining ingredients, and pulse 4 times, scraping sides of bowl
between each pulse. Cover and chill.  Yield: about 2-1/2 cups (about
14 calories per 2 tablespoons).

Joanne Nanney of North Carolina, in November, 1986 "Southern Living".
