Title: Basil Mashed Potatoes
Categories: Side dish, Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Kosher salt
2 c  Basil leaves
4 c  Mashed potatoes
2 tb Pine nuts
1/4 c  Extra virgin olive oil
3    Cloves garlic -- peeled &
-sliced
In a blender, combine the pine nuts, olive oil, garlic, salt and
pepper; puree.  With the motor running add the basil leaves and
continue to puree until smooth.  Add the basil puree to the warm
mashed potatoes and over low heat, stir with a wooden spoon until the
puree is completely incorporated and the mixture is piping hot.
Serve.
