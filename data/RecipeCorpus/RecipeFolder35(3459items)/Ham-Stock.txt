Title: Ham Stock
Categories: Soup/stew, Meats
Yield: 1 quart
MMMMM--------------------FINE COOKING-NOV 95-------------------------
2    Bay leaves
2    Sprigs thyme
1 tb Ground ginger
1/2 ts Crushed black peppercorns
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Mace
3 qt Chicken stock
2 lb Smoked ham hocks
2 md Celery ribs
Coarsely chopped
1 md Onion, coarsely chopped
1 md Leek, coarsely chopped
5    Sprigs flat-leaf parsley
Combine all the ingredients in a stock pot and bring to a boil over
moderately high heat. Lower the heat and simmer until reduced by
two-thirds, about 1Â¬ hours. Strain the stock. Let cook, then cover and
refrigerate for up to 3 days or freeze for up to 1 month. Strain off
the fat before using.
