Title: White Beans with Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Main dish, Spanish, Vegetarian, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Green bell pepper, chopped
2 ea Sweet potatoes, peeled &
-- cubed
1 c  Water
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Parsley, chopped
1 ea Onion, chopped
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  White rice, uncooked
2 ea Tomatoes, chopped
1 c  Great Northern beans, cooked
Saute onion & garlic in oil until translucent, about 2 minutes.  Add
rice & saute for 3 minutes.  Add the rest of the ingredients, with
the exception of the parsley & bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover &
simmer until the rice is tender, about 25 minutes.  Garnish with
parsley & serve.
