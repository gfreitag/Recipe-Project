Title: Qulck Italian Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
2 tb Fresh parsley -- minced
1 tb Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 1/2 ts Dried basil
1 ts Dried oregano
1/2 ts Pepper
Salad greens
Sliced tomatoes, zucchini,
Mushrooms and green  pepper
Or vegetables of your
Choice
1 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  White wine vinegar
Arrange greens and vegetables in a salad bowl or on individual salad
plates. In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine all remaining
ingredients; shake well. Serve over salad. Yield: 1-1/2 cups dressing
