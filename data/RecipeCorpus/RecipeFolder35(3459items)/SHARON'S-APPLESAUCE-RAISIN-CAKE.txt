Title: SHARON'S APPLESAUCE RAISIN CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 1 cake
1 c  Walnuts, chopped
1 ts Soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Dark brown sugar
1 c  Butter
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Applesauce
1 c  Raisins
Cream butter and sugar.  Add applesauce, raisins and nuts.  Sift
flour, soda and spices together and add to mixture. Gradually beat
after each addition. Pour into greased 9" x 13" pan and bake for 30
mins. at 375 deg.
