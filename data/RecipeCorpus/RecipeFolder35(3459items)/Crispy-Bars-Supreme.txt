Title: Crispy Bars Supreme
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 42 servings
1    Egg
2 tb Water
2 c  Rice Krispies+
1 c  Nuts -- chopped
7 oz Raisinets
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Butter -- softened
2/3 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Sugar
Preheat oven to 350.  In small bowl, combine flour, baking powder and
cinnamon; set aside.  In large bowl, combine butter, brown sugar and
sugar; beat until creamy.  Beat in egg and water.  Gradually add
flour mixture. Stir in cereal, nuts and raisinets.  Spread into
greased 13 x 9 pan. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
