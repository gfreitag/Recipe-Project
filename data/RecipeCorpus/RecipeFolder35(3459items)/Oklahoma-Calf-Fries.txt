Title: Oklahoma Calf Fries
Categories: Route 66, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
*****  NONE  *****
Slice 2 pounds of calf fries (mountain oysters) about 1/4 inch thick
(they are easier to slice if slightly frozen.) Soak in an egg and
milk mixture about 10 mins. Roll each fry in a mixture of 1 1/4 cups
cornmeal, 3 cups flour, and 1 tsp salt. Fry in hot oil (350 F) until
golden brown. Enjoy????
