Title: Rhubarb Strawberry Crisp
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM---------------------------FRUIT--------------------------------
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2    Lemon -- juice only
1/4 c  Cold water
1 1/2 lb Rhubarb -- cut into 3/4"
Pieces
1 pt Strawberries -- halved
1/4 c  Sugar
MMMMM------------------CINNAMON WALNUT TOPPING-----------------------
3 tb Sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
2/3 c  Walnuts -- coarsely chopped
Ice cream or frozen yogurt
3 tb Unsalted butter -- cut into
Pieces
1/4 c  Flour
1/4 c  Brown sugar
Preheat the oven to 375.  Butter an 8" pie pan.  In a mixing bowl
combine the rhubarb, strawberries, sugars, lemon juice and water.
Transfer the mixture to the buttered pan.  In a small bowl, combine
the butter, flour, sugars and cinnamon.  Cut together until the
mixture forms large crumbs. Crumble in the walnuts.  Scatter the
topping over the fruit, pressing it in lightly.  Bake about 35
minutes.  Cool briefly.  Serve warm.
