Title: Cheese Scalloped Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1 sm Cheez whiz
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Cream of celery soup
2 lb Hash brown potatoes, thawed
2 tb Minced onion
1 sm Sour cream
Mix all together in 9 x 13 inch baking dish.  Dot with margarine and
sprinkle with cornflake crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees until brown (45
minutes or so). Randy Rigg
