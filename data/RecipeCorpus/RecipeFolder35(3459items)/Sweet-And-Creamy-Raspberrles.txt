Title: Sweet And Creamy Raspberrles
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Confectioners' sugar
Fresh mint, optional
1 pt Fresh raspberries
Light cream
Place raspberries in individual bowls. Just before serving, pour
cream over each serving. Sprinkle with sugar: garnish with mint if
desired. Yield: 4 servings
