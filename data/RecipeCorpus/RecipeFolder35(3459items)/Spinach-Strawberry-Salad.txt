Title: Spinach-Strawberry Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1/4 ts Pepper
4 c  Spinach leaves
2 c  Strawberries, halved
2 tb Sugar
Wash the spinach leaves under cool running water; pat dry, remove the
stem, and tear the leaves into bite-size pieces. Place torn spinach
into a large bowl; cover and chill. combine strawberries, sugar, and
vinegar in a small bowl; toss gently. Cover and chill at least 2
hours, stirring occasionally.

Add the strawberry mixture and pepper to the torn spinach; toss well.
Serve immediately.
