Title: Cranberry-Peach Punch
Categories: Beverages, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Honey or to taste
2 c  Cranberry
-or lingonberry juice
2 1/2 c  Water
2    Cinnamon sticks
2    Bags peach-flavored tea
1 tb Lime juice
Bring the water to a boil in a large pan with the cinnamon sticks and
tea bags. Remove the pan from the heat and let the tea steep for 10
minutes. Remove the tea bags and cinnamon sticks. Stir in the lime
juice, honey and cranberry juice. Transfer the mixture to a pitcher
and chill.
