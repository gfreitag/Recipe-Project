Title: Corn Puddlng Supreme
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/3 c  Corn
Fresh, frozen or canned
Sweet corn
1 c  Milk
2 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1 pk Cream cheese -- 8 ounces
Softened
2 ea Eggs -- beaten
1/3 c  Sugar
1 pk Corn bread muffin mix --
8 1/2    Ounces
1 cn Cream-style corn -- 16-1/2
Ounces
In a mixing bowl, blend cream cheese, eggs and sugar. Add all of the
remaining ingredients and mix well. Transfer to a greased 13-in. x
9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 45-50 minutes or until
set. Yield: 12-16 servings
