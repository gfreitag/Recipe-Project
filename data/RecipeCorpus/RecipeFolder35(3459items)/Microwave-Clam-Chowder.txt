Title: Microwave Clam Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb All-purpose flour
1 1/2 c  Milk -- divided
1/2 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 ts Butter or margarine
Minced fresh parsley
4 sl Bacon slices -- cut 1/2 in.
Pieces
2 cn Clams, canned -- 6 1/2 oz
Each
Chopped
1 1/2 c  Potatoes -- raw peel, diced
1/3 c  Chopped onion
In a covered 2 qt. microwave-safe dish, cook bacon on high for 4-5
min. or until crisp. Remove with a slotted spoon to drain; set aside.
Drain clam juice into the drippings. Stir in potatoes and onion.
Cover and cook on high for 8-10 min. or until potatoes are tender,
stirring once or twice. Stir flour into 1/4 cup of milk; add to
potato mixture. Stir in salt, pepper and remaining milk. Cover and
cook on medium for 6 min. stirring once or twice. Let stand for 3-5
min. Stir in clams and butter. Garnish with bacon and parsley. Yield:
4 servings. (1 quart).
