Title: Mashed Turnip
Categories: Side dish, Indian, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 ea Green chilies, chopped
1/2 ts Cumin
1 ts Garam masala
Salt
1 pn Sugar
1 tb Cilantro
1 lb Turnip, peeled, boiled &
-- mashed
2 tb Ghee
1/2 ts Grated ginger
1 md Onion, finely chopped
2 md Tomatoes, quartered
Heat ghee in a skillet.  Saute ginger & onion till golden.  Add
tomatoes, chilies, cumin & garam masala.  Mix in turnip, add salt &
blend.

Keep pan over moderate heat & stir for about 10 minutes.  Add sugar &
blend again.  Serve hot, topped with cilantro.

Michael Pandya, "Indian Vegetarian Cooking"
