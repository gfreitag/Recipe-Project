Title: Hot Spiced Crab Soup [Nov 9, 1995 (16:01)]
Categories: Soup/stew, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 or 4
1 13/16 qt Wine; *dry* white
-drinking wine
-(26.5 oz. bottle)
14 oz Chicken broth
2 lg Dungeness crabs; cooked
-(about 2 lbs. each),
-cleaned and cracked
Lemon wedges
1/8 lb Butter
1 lg Onion, chopped
2    Garlic cloves, crushed
1/2 ts Tobasco +|-
-or other pep sauce
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper; +/-
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1    Bay leaf
In a large kettle (at least 8 qt.) heat butter until bubbly;
stir in onion and garlic and cook over medium heat until onion is
soft. Stir in hot pepper seasoning, cayenne, parsley, bay leaf, broth
and wine; heat to simmering.
Add crab, cover, and simmer about 10 minutes or until crab is heated
through.

Eating this soup is a wonderful and messy affair.  Serve with hot
Parmesan garlic bread, a salad and another bottle of the wine used.
