Title: Fruit Juice Shake
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 servings
2    Juice cans milk
1 1/2 cn Crushed ice
1    Envelope Dream Whip
6 oz Frozen orange juice
-concentrate
Combine all ingredients in blender and blend 30 seconds at high speed.
(Variation: Can also use apple juice or cranberry juice cocktail.  Or
try pineapple juice and 1/4 cup coconut.)
