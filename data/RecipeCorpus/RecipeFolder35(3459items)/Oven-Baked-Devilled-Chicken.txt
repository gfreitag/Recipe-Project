Title: Oven Baked Devilled Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Meats, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
4 ea Chicken thighs, skinned
1/4 c  Yogurt
3 tb Dijon mustard
2 tb Mayonnaise
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs
In small bowl, mix together the yogurt, mustard, mayo and salt and
pepper to taste.  In another bowl, mix together the bread crumbs,
paprika, and cayenne. Dip chicken pieces into the yogurt mixture,
then into the crumb mixture and arrange on a greased baking sheet.
Bake at 400F for about 50-60 minutes, or until juices run clear.
Makes 4 servings. Origin: Adapted from Canadian Living, Sept/95.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/95.
