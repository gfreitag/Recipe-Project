Title: Chocolate - Peanut Cookies
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 5 dozen
2/3 c  Cocoa
3/4 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Finely chopped peanuts
1 c  Butter / margarine; softened
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs
2 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  All-purpose flour
Cream butter; gradually add the sugar, beating until light and
fluffy. Stir in eggs and vanilla.
Combine flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt; add to creamed
mixture, beating well.  Stir in the chopped peanuts.
Drop dough by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.  Bake at
350 degrees F for 12 to 14 minutes.  Cool cookies on wire racks.
Yield: about 5-1/2 dozen
