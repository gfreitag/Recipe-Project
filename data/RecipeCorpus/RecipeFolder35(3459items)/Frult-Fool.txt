Title: Frult Fool
Categories: Beverages, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Flaked coconut -- toasted
Optional
Fresh mint leaves, optional
1 cn Peach pie filling -- or
Apricot 21 oz.
2 c  Whipped topping
Combine the pie filling and whipped topping. Spoon into individual
dessert dishes. If desired, sprinkle each with coconut and/or garnish
with mint. Yield: 4-6 servings
