Title: Dried Beef Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Mustard
1/2    Stick of margarine, melted
1    Jar of dried beef, shredded
8 oz Philadelphia cream cheese
1 sm Jar of pimento cheese
1 tb Horseradish
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
Mix cheeses, melted margarine, horseradish.  Worcestershire and
mustard and 1/2 of shredded dried beef, may form into cheese ball or
serve in chip dip bowl, top with rest of dried beef.  Serve with dip
crackers. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   10-08-95
