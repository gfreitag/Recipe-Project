Title: Dieter Strawberry Shortcake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 ts Liquid sucaryl
1/4 c  Skim milk
Strawberry Sauce
4 c  Hulled strawberries
4 tb Liquid sucaryl
2 tb Water
Biscuit-type shortcake
1 c  Sifted flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
pn Salt
3 tb Butter
1    Egg
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.Combine flour,baking powder,and
salt.Cut in butter until of consistency of coarse corn meal.Combine
remaining ingredients; stir into flour mixture.Knead gently about 10
times.Roll 1/4" thick on lightly floured board.Cut into 6 3"
circles.Place on ungreased baking sheet; bake 12 to 15 minutes.Cool.
Crush 1 cup strawberries and sucaryl and water.Pour over remaining
halved berries and chill. Split biscuits and fill with sauce; top
with remaining sauce.If desired,garnish with low calorie whipped
topping.

Per serving    169 calories protein 4g fat 7g carbohydrate 23g

(with sugar   331 calories)

= 1 fruit exchange
