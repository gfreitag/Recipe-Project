Title: Cabbage With Meat Sauce
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
Dried
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 md Head of cabbage -- thinly
Sliced
1 lb Pork sausage
1 ea Green pepper -- chopped
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Tomato juice
1 cn Tomatoes -- 14 1/2 oz
Diced with liquid
2 tb Fresh oregano -- or 2 tsp.
In a skillet, cook sausage, green pepper and onion until the sausage
is browned; drain. Stir in the tomato juice, tomatoes, oregano, salt
and pepper; simmer for 1 hr. Melt butter in a dutch oven; saute
cabbage over med. heat until tender, about 15 min. Transfer to
serving platter; top with the meat sauce. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
