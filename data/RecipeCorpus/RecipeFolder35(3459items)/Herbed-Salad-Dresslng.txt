Title: Herbed Salad Dresslng
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Fresh tarragon -- minced
Or 1/2 teaspoon dried
Tarragon
1 ts Brown sugar
1 ts Salt
Salad greens and vegetables
Choice
1 1/3 c  Tarragon vinegar
1 c  Vegetable oil
2/3 c  Olive oil
4 ts Mayonnaise
3 ea Garlic cloves -- minced
1 tb Fresh thyme -- minced
Or 1 teaspoon dried thyme
2 ts Dijon mustard
In a jar with tight-fitting lid, combine the first 10 ingredients;
shake well. Refrigerate. Serve over salad. Yield: 3 cups
