Title: Grilled Peppers
Categories: Vegetables, Bbq/grill
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Basil
8 ea Assorted bell & hot peppers
1 x  Salt & pepper to taste
2 tb Olive oil
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Thyme
Mix all marinade ingredients together and set aside. Wash peppers,
cut in two and remove seeds. Place peppers on plate and season well.
Pour marinade over and marinate 15 minutes. Preheat barbecue at
medium. Oil grill. Place peppers on grill and cover; cook 12 minutes.
Turn peppers over 4 to 5 times during cooking and baste occasionally
with marinade. Avoid charring peppers. Serve at once. From Morning
Star electronic magazine - July 1994
