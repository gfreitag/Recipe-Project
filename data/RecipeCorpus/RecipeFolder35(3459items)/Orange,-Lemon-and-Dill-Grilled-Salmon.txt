Title: Orange, Lemon and Dill Grilled Salmon
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
40 g  Butter
2 tb Dill
Salt
Ground Black Pepper
Watercress
1    Zest and juice of orange
1    Zest and juice of lemon
1 tb Olive Oil
1 ts Whole Grain Mustard
4    Salmon Steaks
Preparation Time: 25 minutes Cooking Time: 15-20 minutes

Preparation: grate the zest of the oranges and lemons

1. Blend together the orange and lemon zest and juice, oil, mustard
and
seasoning.

2. Place the salmon steaks in a dish, pour ove the marinade and leave
for
1-2 hours.

3. Meanwhile, blend together the butter, dill and seasoning. Place on
a
sheet of food wrap and roll up to form a "sausage" shape. Chill
until
firm then cut into 8 rounds.

4. Grill the salmon under a preheated moderate grill for 15-20
minutes,
turning and basting occasionally with the marinade.

Serve immediately on a bed of watercress with the herb butter rounds.
