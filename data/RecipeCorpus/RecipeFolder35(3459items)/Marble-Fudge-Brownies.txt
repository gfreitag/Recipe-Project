Title: Marble Fudge Brownies
Categories: Masterchefs, Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1 pn Salt
Additional butter for
-greasing molds
Cocoa powder
Powdered sugar
Chocolate sauce
Ice cream
Mint sprigs
4 1/2 oz Semisweet chocolate
9 oz Sweet butter
2    Eggs
3 c  + 1 Tb Sugar
8 oz Cream cheese
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Flour
Over hot water bath or in microwave; melt chocolate and butter. Beat
eggs and 3 cups plus 1 tablespoon sugar together.

With an electric mixer combine cream cheese, 1/4 cup sugar and
vanilla.

Add chocolate mixture to egg mixture and combine. Add flour and stir
to combine. (Do not over stir) Add salt and stir.

Pour chocolate mixture into 4 molds that have been buttered and cocoa
powdered. Fill half way to top. (Note: All this time the cream cheese
mixture has been mixing in the electric mixer)

Pour cream cheese mixture on top of chocolate mixture. With a spoon,
using a few swirling motions mix the brownie to get a marbled look.
(Do not over mix, you just want a marbled effect)

Bake at 350~ for 35 minutes. Cool a little; then unmold. Sprinkle with
powdered sugar.

To serve:  Spoon some chocolate sauce (prepared or commercial) onto
plate. Swirl plate to coat. Place brownie in center of sauce. Put
scoop of ice cream on top. Garnish with mint sprigs.
