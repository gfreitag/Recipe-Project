Title: Bread Dressing - SLC
Categories: Dress/stuff, Poultry, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
-undiluted
2 ea Eggs; slightly beaten
3/4 c  Onion; chopped
3/4 c  Celery; chopped
1/2 c  Butter or margarine; melted
1 ea (1 pound) loaf day old bread
-;crumbled
1 1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Canned chicken broth;
Combine first 5 ingredients in a large bowl; set aside.
Saute onion and celery in butter until tender.  Add to bread
mixture, stirring well.  Spoon into a lightly greased 2-quart
casserole dish. Cover and bake at 325 degrees F for 20 minutes.
Yield: 6 servings.

Note: Dressing is too moist to bake in turkey. <<<<<<<<<
