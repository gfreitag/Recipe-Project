Title: Amish Oatmeal Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Light corn syrup
1 ts Grated orange rind
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Quick oats
1    Unbaked 9 inch pie shell
1 c  Sugar
3 tb Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
4    Eggs
1 1/2 tb Melted butter
Mix sugar, flour, cinnamon and salt.  Beat 4 eggs until frothy.  Add
dry mixture.  Add corn syrup, melted butter, orange rind, vanilla and
the quick oats.  Mix well.  Pour into shell.  Bake at 350 F for 45
minutes.

Source: Amish Cooking in Quilt Country TV show
