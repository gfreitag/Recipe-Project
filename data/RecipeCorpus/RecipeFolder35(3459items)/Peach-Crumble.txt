Title: Peach Crumble
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking pawder
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ea Egg -- beaten
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted and cooled
Vanilla ice cream, optlonal
6 c  Ripe peaches -- sliced
Peeled
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
3 tb All-purpose flour
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Grated lemon peel
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
TOPPING:
Place peaches in a greased shallow 2-1/2-qt. baking dish. Combine
brown susar, flour, lemon juice and peel and cinnamon; sprinkle over
the peaches. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and
nutmeg. Stir in egg until the mixfure resembles coarse crumbs.
Sprinkle over the peaches. Pour butter evenly over topping. Bake at
375 deg for 35-40 minutes. Serve with ice cream if desired. Yield:
10-12 serving
