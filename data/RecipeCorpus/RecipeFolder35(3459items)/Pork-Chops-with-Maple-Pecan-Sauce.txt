Title: Pork Chops with Maple-Pecan Sauce
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
Vegetable cooking spray
3 ts Vegetable oil
2 tb Reduced-calorie
-maple syrup
1 tb Chopped pecans, toasted
4    (4oz) lean boneless
-center-cut loin pork chops
2 ts Dijon mustard
2 tb All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Ground ginger
Trim fat from chops. Place chops between 2 sheets of plastic wrap;
flatten to Â¬-inch thickness, using a meat mallet or rolling pin.
Spread mustard on both sides of chops. Combine flour and ginger;
dredge chops in flour mixture.

Coat a non-stick skillet with cooking spray; add oil and place over
medium heat until hot. Add chops and cook 3 minutes per side or until
browned. Combine syrup and pecans; add to skillet, stirring to coat
chops. Cover and simmer 4 minutes or until chops are tender, turning
once.
