Title: Spice Delight Cake with Seafoam Icing
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Salt
2/3 c  Milk,plus 1/4 cup
Seafoam icing:
2    Egg whites,large
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar; packed
5 tb -water,cold
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cream of tartar
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Butter; softened
1 1/3 c  Sugar
2    Eggs,large
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Cake flour; sifted
1 ts Cinnamon,ground
1/2 ts Nutmeg,ground
1/4 ts Allspice,ground
2 1/2 ts Baking powder,double-acting
Recipe by: Jo Anne Merrill 1-For cake: cream butter (or margarine) and
gradually add sugar. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating well after each.
Add vanilla. Sift dry ingredients together; add to batter alternately
with the milk. Divide batter in half. Into one half, add the spices
and mix well. 2-Grease and flour two 8-inch cake pans. Pour batter
into one pan and spice batter into the other. Bake in preheated 350
degree oven 25-30 minutes. Cool and then frost.

Seafoam Icing:

Put all icing ingredients except vanilla into the top of a double
boiler; place over boiling water. Beat with an electric mixer set on
low speed until blended, then beat on high until mixture stands in
peaks. Remove from heat and add vanilla; beat about 5 minutes until
of spreading consistency. This cake recipe was a third place winner
at the Del Mar county fair. Jo Anne Merrill
