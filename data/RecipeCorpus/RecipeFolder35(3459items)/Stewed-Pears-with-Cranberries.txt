Title: Stewed Pears with Cranberries
Categories: Condiments, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2    Lemon, juiced
2 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Fresh cranberries
-(see note)
6    Firm pears, peeled
-cored, and chopped
-in large chunks
1/2 c  Apple juice
1/4 ts Five-spice powder
Serve with black beans or any spicy bean dish.

1. In a large pot, cook the pears, apple juice, five-spice powder,
cinnamon, lemon juice, and sugar over low heat until pears are soft
but not mushy, 30-45 minutes.

2. Add the cranberries and cook until the cranberries begin to burst,
about 5 minutes. Serve warm, at room temperature, or cold.

Can be prepared up to 2 days ahead and refrigerated.
