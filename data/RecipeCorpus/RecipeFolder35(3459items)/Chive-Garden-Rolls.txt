Title: Chive Garden Rolls
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
Each
1/2 c  Warm water
1/4 c  Wheat germ
3 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
3 tb Fresh chives -- or dried
Chopped
1 ea Egg
1 c  Nonfat cottage cheese -- 8
oz Softened
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 ts Honey
1 ts Salt
1 pk Active dry yeast -- 1/4 oz
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 sm Onion -- finely chopped
1 ea Egg -- beaten
In a mixing bowl, combine the egg, cottage cheese, oil, honey and
salt. Dissolve yeast in warm water; add to egg mixture. Add wheat
germ and 1 1/2 cups flour. Mix on med. speed for 3 min. Add chives
and enough remaining flour to form a soft dough. Turn onto a floured
board; knead until smooth and elastic, about 10 min. Place in a
greased bowl, turning once to grease top. Cover and let rise in a
warm place until doubled about l hr. Punch dough down; roll out to
3/4 in. thickness.Cut with a 3 in. round cutter. Place on greased
baking sheets. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 45 min. Brush
tops with egg and sprinkle with onion. Bake at 350 deg. for 15 to 20
mon or until the rolls are golden brown. Yield; about 1 doz.
