Title: Kid-Size Plzzas
Categories: Pizza, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Garlic powder
2 c  Mozzarella cheese -- 8
oz Shredded
Refrigerator biscuits -- 10
oz Each
2    Tubes
-
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 8 ounces
1 1/2 ts Dried minced onion
1 ts Dried oregano
1 ts Dried basil
Roll or pat biscuits into 2-1/2-in. circles. Place on two greased
baking sheets. In a small bowl, combine tomato sauce. onion, oregano,
basil and garlic powder: spread over the biscuits. Sprinkle with
cheese. Bake at 400 deg. for 8-10 minutes. Yield: 20 mini pizzas.
