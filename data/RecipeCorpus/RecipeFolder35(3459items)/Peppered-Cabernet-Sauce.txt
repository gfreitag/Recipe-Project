Title: Peppered Cabernet Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 batch
2 tb Flat-leaf parsley, chopped
1 tb Butter
2 ts Cracked black pepper
Salt
1 c  Cabernet Sauvignon
1 lb Strawberries
-medium to large
-hulled, sliced 1/4" thick
Put the wine in a medium saucepan, bring to boil and reduce over high
heat until only about Â¬ cup remains, about 6-8 minutes. When the wine
is reduced, add the strawberries and reduce the heat to very low.
Stir in the parsley, butter, cracked pepper and salt. The
strawberries should be softened, but still hold their shape. Serve
with broiled salmon.
