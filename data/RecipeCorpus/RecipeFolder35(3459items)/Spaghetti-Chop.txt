Title: Spaghetti Chop
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
Inch
2 cn Tomato soup -- 10 3/4 oz
Undiluted
1 c  Ketchup
2 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1 lg Onion -- chopped
1/2 ts Onion salt
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
8 oz Spaghetti -- broken into 4
In a skillet brown ground beef; drain. Add onion, onion saltand
seasoned salt; cook until onion is tender, about 5 min. Remove from
heat. In large saucepan, cook spaghetti according to package
directions; drain and return to pan. Add beef mixture, soup and
ketchup; mix well. Simmer for 5 to 10 min. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
