Title: Basic Pastry Mix Variations:
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------SPICY PASTRY-----------------------------
1 tb Light molasses
1    To 3 tablespoons cold water
8    Inch to 10-inch pie:
1 1/2 c  Basic Pastry Mix
1 ts Pumpkin pie spice
In a medium bowl, combine pastry mix and pie spice. Combine the
molasses and water; gently mix in just enough to moisten dry
ingredients. Proceed as for regular pie pastry
