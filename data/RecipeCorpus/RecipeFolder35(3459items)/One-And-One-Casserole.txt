Title: One And One Casserole
Categories: Meats, Casseroles, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
Soup
1 c  Water
1 c  Sliced green onion tops
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 c  Uncooked rice
1 pk Dry onion soup mix
1 cn Condensed cream of mushroom
Crumble ground beef in buttered 2 1/2 quart casserole.  Sprinkle with
rice and soup mix.  Blend mushroom soup and water.  Pour over rice.
Spread onion tops over mixture.  Cover and bake at 350 for one hour.
Stir with fork.
