Title: Blueberry Bran muffins
Categories: Low cal/fat, Breads, Muffins
Yield: 1 dozen
1/3 c  Molasses
1/3 c  Sugar
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 ea Egg
1 ea Egg white
1/2 c  Fresh or frozen blueberries
Not thawed
1 c  All bran cereal
1/2 c  Skim milk
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Unsweetened apple sauce
In large bowl combine bran cereal, and skim milk.  Let stand 5
minutes. In medium bowl stir together flour, baking powder, salt, and
cinnamon; set aside.  Add apple sauce, molasses, sugar, oil, egg, and
egg white to bran cereal, stir until well combined.  Add dry
ingredients and stir until just moistened.  Fold in blueberries. Pour
into 12 muffin cups sprayed with non stick cooking spray.  Bake at
400 for 20 to 25 minutes. Remove from pan and cool completely. From
