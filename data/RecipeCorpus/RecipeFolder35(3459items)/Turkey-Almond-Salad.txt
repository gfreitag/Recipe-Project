Title: Turkey Almond Salad
Categories: Poultry, Country wom, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
2/3 c  Miracle WhipÂ® light -- or
Mayonnaise
1 tb Milk
2 ts Mustard -- prepared
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 c  Cooked turkey -- cubed
2 c  Shredded cabbage
3/4 c  Diced celery
1/4 c  Green onion -- sliced
1 1/2 c  Chow mein noodles
1/2 c  Almond slivers -- toasted
2 tb Sesame seeds -- toasted
Dressings:
In a large bowl, toss together turkey, cabbage, celery and green
onions. In another bowl, combine dressing ingredients. Pour over the
turkey mixture. Chill for several hours. Just before serving, add the
chow mein noodles, almonds and sesame seeds; toss to mix. Yield: 6
servings.
