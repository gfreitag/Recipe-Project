Title: Orange Pecans
Categories: Holiday, Snacks, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
2 c  Pecan halves -- (about 10
-ozs)
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
2 tb Unsalted butter
4 ts Grated orange peel
2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground coriander
1/2 ts Ground cloves
Preheat oven to 300 F. Melt butter in heavy medium saucepan over low
heat. Add orange peel, cinnamon, coriander, cloves and cayenne and
stir until aromatic, about 30 secs. Add pecans, then sugar and salt
and stir to coat evenly.
Transfer nut mixture to baking sheet. Bake until nuts are toasted,
stirring occasionally, about 20 mins. Cool (Can be prepared 3 days
ahead. Store in airtight container at room temp.)
