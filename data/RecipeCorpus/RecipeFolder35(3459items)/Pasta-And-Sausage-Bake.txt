Title: Pasta And Sausage Bake
Categories: Pasta/rice, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Cooked/drained
1/3 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 pk Mozzarella cheese -- 4 oz
Shredded
1 lb Italian sausage
Cut into 1/4-inch slices
1    Jar spaghetti sauce -- l5
Ounces
8 oz Mostaccioli --
In a skillet, brown sausage. Drain. In a greased 2-qt. baking dish,
combine sausage, sauce, mostaccioli and Parmesan cheese. Top with
mozzarella cheese. Bake at 350 deg. for 15-20 minutes or until heated
through. Yield: 4-6 servings
