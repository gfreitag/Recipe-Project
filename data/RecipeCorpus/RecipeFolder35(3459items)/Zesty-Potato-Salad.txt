Title: Zesty Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
3 ea Bacon strips -- cooked and
Crumbled
4 ea Hard-cooked eggs -- chopped
2 ea Green onions -- sliced
2 lb Red potatoes
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 tb Prepared horseradish
1 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
1/2 ts Salt
Peel potatoes; cook in boiling salted water for 20 minutes or until
done. Drain and cool. Cut potatoes into cubes. In a large bowl,
combine mayonnaise, sour cream, horseradish, parsley, salt and
pepper; mix until smooth. Stir in potatoes, bacon, eggs and onions.
Cover and chill up to 24 hours. Yield: 6 servings
