Title: Eggnog (Vegan)
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Brown sugar
1/4 ts Tumeric; ground
1/2 c  To 1c rum or brandy
;nutmeg, opt
2    10 oz pkg silken regular
-or low fat tofu
16 oz Vanilla soymilk; or rice
-milk
1 tb Vanilla extract
In blender or food processor, combine all ingredients except nutmeg;
blend thorougly stopping occasionally to scrape down sieds.
Serve well chilled and dusted with nutmeg.
