Title: Nao's Peas & Basil Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1    Head boston lettuce -- bite
-size pieces
8    Pieces red leaf lettuce --
-bite size
3 tb Raspberry vinegar
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 sm Cucumber -- seeded and
-sliced
1 sm Green bell pepper -- seeded
-and sliced
3    Green onion -- sliced
1/2 c  Parsley -- finely chopped
1/2 c  Peas
Mix the vinegar and oil together in a small container.  Add salt and
pepper to taste.  Gently toss remaining ingredients in a large salad
bowl. Swirl on dressing.  Gently toss again.
