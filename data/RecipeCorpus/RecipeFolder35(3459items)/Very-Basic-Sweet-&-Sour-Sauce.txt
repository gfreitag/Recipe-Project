Title: Very Basic Sweet & Sour Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Vinegar
3 tb Sugar
1 cn Chicken broth;(10 3/4 oz)
3 tb Corn starch
2 tb Soy sauce
Combine chicken broth with other ingredients. Add to stir fry recipe
and cook until sauce thickens.
