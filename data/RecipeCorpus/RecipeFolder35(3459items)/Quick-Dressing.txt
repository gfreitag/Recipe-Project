Title: Quick Dressing
Categories: Side dish, Dress/stuff, Poultry
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
1 1/2 tb Onion flakes, instant
1 1/3 tb Chicken-seasoned stock base
3 c  Water; boiling
1    Egg; well beaten
8 c  Bread cubes; 1/2 inch
1/4 ts Parsley flakes, dried
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Celery seeds
1/2 ts Salt
Combine first 7 ingredients in a large bowl.  Combine stock base and
water; add to bread cube mixture. Add egg, and mix lightly.

Stuff turkey lightly with dressing.  Or spoon dressing into a buttered
13x9x2" pan; bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until lightly
browned.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in 1975.
