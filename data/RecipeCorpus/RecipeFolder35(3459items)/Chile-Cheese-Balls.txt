Title: Chile Cheese Balls
Categories: So. america
Yield: 2 servings
8 oz Cream cheese
1 c  Chopped green chile
1 lb Longhorn cheese
1 ea Tabasco sauce to taste
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
Put cheese in large bowl and mix until smooth. Add the rest of the
ingredients. Shape into balls and roll in chopped pecans. Wrap in wax
paper and refrigerate.
