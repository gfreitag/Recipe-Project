Title: Shrimp Saag
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1    Bay leaf
1 c  Clam broth
-or fish stock (approx)
2    12oz bags spinach
-finely chopped or
2 pk Frozen spinach
1 1/2 lb Large shrimp
Peeled and deveined
Salt and pepper
3 tb Cilantro or parsley
-finely chopped
-for garnish
1 tb Olive oil
1    Onion(s)
-finely chopped
2 ts Ginger, minced
2    Garlic clove(s)
-minced (2 tsp)
1/2 ts Cumin seeds
1/2 ts Ground coriander seeds
1/2 ts Turmeric
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cardamon
1 pn Ground cloves
Heat the oil in a sautÂ‚ pan. Add the onion, ginger, garlic, spices,
and bay leaf, and cook over medium heat for 3 minutes, or until soft
but not brown. Add the clam broth and bring to a boil. Stir in the
spinach and cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the spinach is soft and
mushy. If the mixture dries out too much, add more clam broth.

Stir in the shrimp and simmer for 1-2 minutes, or until cooked.
Remove and discard the bay leaf. Season with salt and pepper.
Transfer to a serving dish, garnish with cilantro or parsley, and
serve at once.
