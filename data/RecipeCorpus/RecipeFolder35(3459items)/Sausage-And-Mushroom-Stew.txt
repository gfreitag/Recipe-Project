Title: Sausage And Mushroom Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Green beans -- fresh &
Halved
3/4 lb Fresh mushrooms -- halved
1/2 md Head of cabbage -- coarsely
Chopped
2 cn Cream of mushroom soup --
Undiluted 10 3/4 oz
1 1/2 lb Kielbasa -- smoked cut 1 in
5 md Potatoes -- cut 1in chunks
4 lg Carrots -- cut 1 in. piece
3 md Onions -- coarsely chopped
In an ovenproof 5 qt. Dutch oven or casserole, combine all ingredients
except cabbage. Cover and bake at 350 deg. for 1 1/4 hours. Uncover
and stir. Add the cabbage. Cover and bake 30 min. longer or until
vegetables are tender. Stir again before servings. Yield: 6-8
servings.
