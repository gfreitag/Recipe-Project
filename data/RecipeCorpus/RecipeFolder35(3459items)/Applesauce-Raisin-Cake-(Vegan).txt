Title: Applesauce Raisin Cake (Vegan)
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------MIX TOGETHER-----------------------------
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Raisins
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 tb Baking powder
ts Baking soda
1 tb Cinnamon
MMMMM----------------INTO THE BLENDER TIL SMOOTH---------------------
1/2 c  Chopped pitted dates
3 ts EnerG egg replacer
6 tb Water
1 c  Soy or rice milk
1 c  Applesauce
2 sm Overripe bananas
MMMMM----------------------------AND---------------------------------
-to peel
2 sm Apples, diced, no need
Prepare a 9x13 pan with veg spray.  Preheat oven to 375.

Toss the apples into the flour mix, pour in the blended stuff, mix
gently until mixed.  Pour into prepared pan, smooth out top, and
sprinkle nuts all over (if desired).  Bake 30 mins, until sides pull
away from pan and it passes toothpick test.

Lee's note: this is pretty sweet as is, but if you have a real sweet
toth, add some maple syrup in place of the 6T water, or use vanilla
flavored soy milk.
