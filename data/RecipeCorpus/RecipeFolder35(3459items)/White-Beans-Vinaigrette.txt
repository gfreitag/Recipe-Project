Title: White Beans Vinaigrette
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Parsley; finely chopped
2 tb Basil; finely chopped
;black pepper to taste
3 ts Red wine vinegar
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 c  White beans; dried, such
-as white kidneys or pea
-beans
4 c  Water
1 c  Onions; finely chopped
1 ts Garlic; minced
Unless the package specifies no soaking, place beans in a bowl and add
water to cover about 2 inches above top of beans.  Soak overnight.

Drain beans.  Put in pot and add 4c water.  Bring to boil, simmer 50
mins to 1 hour, til beans are tender.  Drain well.

Put beans in a mixing bowl and add remaining ingredients.  Blend
gently but well.  Serve warm.
