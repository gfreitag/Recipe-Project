Title: Strawberries with Raspberry Sauce
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Orange juice or to taste
1 tb Maple syrup or to taste
4 c  Strawberries
1 c  Raspberries
1. Wash and hull the strawberries and put them in an attractive bowl.

2. In a separate bowl, press the raspberries 2-3 times with the back
of a fork to mash some of them. Stir in the orange juice and maple
syrup.

3. Pour the raspberry mixture over the strawberries, mix gently, and
cover the bowl with plastic wrap. Let stand at room temperature for
at least 1 hour. Serve at room temperature.

Can be prepared up to 4 hours ahead. Refrigerate any leftovers for 1
day.
