Title: Chocolate Macadamla Nut Cookles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Baking soda
1/8 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
3/4 c  Macadamia nuts -- coarsely
Chopped
3/4 c  Pecans -- coarsely chopped
10 tb Butter
(no substitutes), softened
3/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
1 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 ts Baking powder
MMMMM-----------------------CARAMEL GLAZE:----------------------------
Candies
12    Caramel
2 tb Whipping cream
In a mixing bowl, cream butter, sugar and vanilla. Add egg. Combine
flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; add to creamed mixture
and mix well. Fold in chocolate chips and nuts. Drop by teaspoonfuls
2 in. apart onto greased baking sheets. Bake at 350 deg. for 10-12
minutes or until golden. Cool on a wire rack. For glaze, melt the
caramels and cream in a saucepan over low heat, stirring until
smooth. Drizzle over cooled cookies. Yield: 2 dozen.
