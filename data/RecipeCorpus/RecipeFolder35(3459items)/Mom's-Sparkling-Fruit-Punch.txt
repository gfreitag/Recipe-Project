Title: Mom's Sparkling Fruit Punch
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 7 servings
1    Bottle sparkling red grape
.  juice or 1 fifth
,  sparkling wine; chilled,
.  25 oz
1 cn Frozen apple-grape-raspberry
.  juice concentrate; thawed
.  12 oz
1 cn Frozen lemonade concentrate;
.  thawed,  6 oz
Combine the frozen juice concentrates in a 1-quart container.  Cover
and store in the refrigerator.  Give with serving instructions.

Makes about seven 6oz servings

To Serve:  Combine the blended concentrates and the sparkling grape
juice or wine.  Serve chilled.

Source:  A pamphlet Mom received in the mail.
