Title: Beans and Franks
Categories: Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Worchestire sauce
8 ea Hot Dogs cut in 1 inch
Pieces
2 tb Molasses
1 cn 16oz pork and beans
2 ts Mustard
1/4 c  Ketchup
Combine all ingredients in glass bowl.  Cover and microwave on high
5-7 minutes.  Stirring twice during cooking
