Title: Master Mix Quick Rolled Biscuits
Categories: Breads
Yield: 10 servings
2/3 c  Milk
2 c  Master Baking Mix
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Combine mix and milk, beat until
smooth.  If dough feels too sticky, add up to 1/4 c more mix.  Turn
dough onto floured surface, knead gently 10 times.  Roll dough to
1/2" thickness, cut into 2 inch circles or squares.  Place on
ungreased baking sheet.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes.
