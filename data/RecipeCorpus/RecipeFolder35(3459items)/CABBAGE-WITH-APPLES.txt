Title: CABBAGE WITH APPLES
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Butter or margarine
3 tb Lemon juice
1 ea Cabbage, head, wedge cut
1 ea Red apple, cored, sliced
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
* Cut cabbages into 6 wedges. Cook in small amount of boiling salted
water 5 minutes.  Add apple and cook for 3 minutes more, until
tender.  Drain. * Combine remaining ingredients.  Heat through but do
not boil.  Pour on cabbage and serve hot.
