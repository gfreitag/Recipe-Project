Title: Savory Steak Rub
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Dried thyme
1 ts Dried rosemary -- crushed
3/4 ts Dried oregano
1 tb Dried marjoram
1 tb Dried basil
2 ts Garlic powder
Combine all ingredients; store in a covered container. Rub over steaks
before grilling or broiling. Will season four to five steaks. Yield:
1/4 cup
