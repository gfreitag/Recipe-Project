Title: Pumpernickel Pus Lumps^
Categories: Kids, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
8 sl Pumpernickel bread
3 3/4 oz Jar capers
4 oz Tub whipped cream cheese
MMMMM---------------------------TOOLS--------------------------------
Toaster
Bread knife
Colander
Bowl
Spoon
Carefully drain the capers through a colander over the sink. Place the
drained capers through a bowl.

Scoop the cream cheese into the bowl of capers and mix gently.

Toast the bread. Spread each slice with a generous glop of pus lumps
and serve, open faced, immediately. Serves 4 infectous friends.
