Title: Inside-Out Stuffed Peppers
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Water
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 cn Stewed tomatoes -- 16 oz cut
Up
1 lg Green pepper -- chopped
1/2 c  Long-grain rice -- uncooked
In a skillet, brown beef; drain. Transfer to a greased 2 qt.
casserole. Add the next eight ingredients. Cover and bake at 350 deg.
for 1 hr. or until rice is tender. Uncover and sprinkle with the
cheese; return to the oven until cheese melts, about 5 min. Yield 4-6
servings.
