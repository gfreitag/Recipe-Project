Title: Peach Butter 2
Categories: Dips/spread, Oldies
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Water
4 c  Sliced peaches
2 c  Sugar
Combine peaches and water.  Cover and cook until fruit is soft.
Add sugar.  Boil slowly, stirring constantly, until thick and
clear.
