Title: Southwestern Seasoning Mix
Categories: Tasteofhome
Yield: 40 servings
2 tb Dried oregano
2 tb Dried basil
1 tb Dried thyme
1 tb Garlic powder
1/4 c  Chili powder
1/4 c  Onion powder
2 tb Ground cumin
2 tb Coriander
Combine all ingredients. Store in an airtight container. Use as a
seasoning for cooked vegetables, grilled meats or chip dips. Yield: 1
cup. editor"s Note: for use as a dip, combine 1 tablespoon mix with 1
cup sour cream and salt to taste. Chill for 1 hr.
