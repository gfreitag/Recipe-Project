Title: Whole Grain Bread Mix
Categories: Breads, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Unprocessed bran
2 tb Packed brown sugar
1 ts Salt
1 pk Quick-rise dry yeast
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1/4 c  Old-fashioned oats
2 tb Yellow cornmeal
2 tb Wheat germ
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl; stir until well blended.
Store in

an airtight container in a cool place. Give with baking instructions.
Yield: about 2 - 1/2 cups bread mix, makes 1 loaf bread. To bake
bread: Place mix in a large bowl.  In a small saucepan, heat 1 cup
milk and 2 T. oil until very warm (120 to 130).  Add to bread mix;
stir until a soft dough forms. Turn onto a lightly floured surface
and knead 5 minutes or until dough becomes smooth and elastic. Cover
and allow dough to rest 10 minutes. Shape dough into a loaf and place
in greased 4 x 8 inch loaf pan. Spray top of dough with cooking
spray, cover and let rise in a warm place (80 to 85) 50 minutes or
until doubled in size. Preheat oven to 375. Brush top of loaf with a
beaten egg. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until bread is golden brown and
sounds hollow when tapped. Serve warm or transfer to wire rack to
cool completely. Store in an airtight container. Source: Leisure
