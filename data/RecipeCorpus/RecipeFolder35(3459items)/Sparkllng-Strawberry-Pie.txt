Title: Sparkllng Strawberry Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Boiling water
4 c  Frozen whole strawberries
1 ea Prepared pie crust -- (8 or
9    Inches)
Whipped topping
2 pk Gelatin
3 oz Ea regular or sugar-free
Raspberry
Or strawberry flavored
Gelatin
In a mixing bowl, dissolve gelatin in water. Add frozen berries and
allow to set a few minutes to thicken. When partially set, spoon into
pie shell. Refrigerate. Garnish with whipped topping. Yield: 6
servings
