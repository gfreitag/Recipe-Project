Title: Creamy Peach Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Fresh peaches -- diced
Peeled
1 ea Deep-dish pastry shell --
9    In baked
Sliced peaches and or mint
Leaves, optiona
1 pk Peach-flavored gelatin -- 3
Ounces
2/3 c  Boiling water
1 c  Vanilla ice cream
1 ct Frozen whipped topping --
8 oz Thawed
In a large bowl, dissolve gelatin in boiling water; stir in ice cream
until melted and smooth. Add whipped topping and mix well. Fold in
peaches. Pour into pastry shell. Chill until firm, about 3 hours. If
desired, garnish with peaches and/or mint leaves. Yield; 6-8 servings
