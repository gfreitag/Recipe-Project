Title: Horseradlsh Cheese Spread
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Hot pepper sauce
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 lb Process American cheese --
Cubed
1/2 c  Prepared horseradish
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
Melt cheese in the top of a double boiler over simmering water until
smooth. Add remaining ingredients and stir until smooth. Spoon into
containers and refrigerate. Serve with crackers or raw vegetables.
Yield: 4 cups
