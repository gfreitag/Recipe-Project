Title: Jesse's Cafe Beet Pickles
Categories: Route 66, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
-cheesecloth
1 c  Sugar
1 ga Canned Beets -- undrained
1 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Pickling Spices -- tied in
Combine all ingredients and bring to a slow boil. Simmer 10 mins,
then let cool with the pickling spice remaining in the liquid. When
cool, remove spices and store beets in refrigerator until ready to
serve.
