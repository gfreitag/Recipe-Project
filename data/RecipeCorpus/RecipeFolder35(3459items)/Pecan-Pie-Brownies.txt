Title: Pecan Pie Brownies
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 1/2 c  Pecans -- coarsely chopped
1 pk Brownie Supreme mix
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
2 tb Flour
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Corn syrup
Heat oven to 350.  Melt margarine in 2 qt saucepan.  Stir in flour
until smooth.  Mix in brown sugar, corn syrup and eggs until well
blended.  Cook over medium heat 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat.  Stir in vanilla and pecans.  Prepare brownie mix
as directed for fudgelike brownies except spoon pecan mixture over
batter before baking.  Bake 35 minutes.  Cool completely.
