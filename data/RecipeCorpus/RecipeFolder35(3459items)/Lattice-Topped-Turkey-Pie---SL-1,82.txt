Title: Lattice-Topped Turkey Pie - SL 1/82
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1 c  Mayonnaise
3 ds Hot sauce
8 oz Can crescent dinner rolls
2 ts Sesame seeds
Radish slices (optional)
Fresh parsley sprigs (opt'l)
2 c  ;cooked chopped turkey
10 oz Package frozen English peas
1 c  (4 oz) sharp Cheddar cheese;
-shredded
1 c  Celery; diced
1/2 c  Soft breadcrumbs
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1/4 ts Salt
Combine first 10 ingredients; mix well.  Spoon into a 12" x 8" x 2"
baking dish.
Separate crescent dough into 2 rectangles; press perforations to
seal. Cut into 4 long strips and 4 short strips.  Arrange strips in
lattice design across top of casserole.  Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
Bake at 350F for 35 minutes.  Garnish with radish slices and parsley,
if desired. Yield: 6 servings.

Hortense Callaway of Florida in January, 1982 "Southern Living".
