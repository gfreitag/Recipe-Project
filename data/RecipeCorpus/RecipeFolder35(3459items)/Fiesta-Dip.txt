Title: Fiesta Dip
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2 ea Green chili pepper, chopped
1/2 cn Refried beans
1/4 c  Salsa
Combine all ingredients and place in crock pot.  Cover and heat 30 to
60 minutes until cheese is melted.  Serve with tortilla chips or corn
chips.
