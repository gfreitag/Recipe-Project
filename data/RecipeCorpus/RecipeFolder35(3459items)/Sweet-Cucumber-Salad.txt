Title: Sweet Cucumber Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb White sugar
1/2 ts Salt (optional)
pn Pepper, or to taste
1 sm English cucumber
1 sm Onion, sliced thinly
3 tb White vinegar
2 tb Lemon juice
With fork, score cucumber lengthwise.  Cut in half, lengthwise, and
slice thinly crosswise.  Slice onion thinly and combine in a bowl
with the cucumber.  Whisk together remaining ingredients and pour over
cucumber/onion mixture.  Refrigerate for at least 4-5 hours to allow
flavours to blend.  Makes about 4 servings. Origin: Adapted from
Canadian Living, Sept/95. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/95.
