Title: Caramel Apple Dip (Halloween)
Categories: Holiday, Fruit, Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Caramel Flavoring
8 oz Cream cheese -- softened
1/2 c  Brown sugar
Blend all ingredients with mixer.  Serve with apple slices.  Tastes
like caramel apples, but doesn't stick to the roof of your mouth or
your teeth.
