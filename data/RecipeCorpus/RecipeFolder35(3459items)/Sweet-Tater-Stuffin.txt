Title: Sweet Tater Stuffin
Categories: Side dish, Low cal/fat, Vegetables, Potatoes
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 ts Marjoram, dried
1/2 ts Sage
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Skim milk
2    Egg whites, slightly beaten
1 tb Tamari soy sauce
3 c  Onion, chopped
2 c  Celery, chopped
4 c  Sweet potato, mashed
8 c  Whole wheat bread, cubed
1 1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
1 1/2 ts Thyme, dried
Spray nonstick frying pan with PAM.  Saute onion and celery until
onion is browned.

In large bowl mix sweet potato, bread cubes, pecans, Thyme, marjoram,
sage and pepper.  Mix well.

Add onion and celery.  Toss to mix.

In separate bowl mix skim milk, egg whites and Tamari.  Beat lightly
and pour over rest of ingredients.  Toss.

Stuff chicken or turkey. This is enough stuffing to do a 12 pound
bird. If you are not going to use this to stuff a bird spray a large
casserole dish with PAM and pour mixture into casserole.  Bake in
preheated oven at 350 F. for 30 to 35 minutes, covered.
