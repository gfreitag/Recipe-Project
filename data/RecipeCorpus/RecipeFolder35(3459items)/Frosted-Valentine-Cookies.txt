Title: Frosted Valentine Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 lb Semisweet chocolate
Or milk chocolate
Confectionery coating, m
Confectioners' sugar icing,
Optional
2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1 c  Confectioners' sugar
4 c  All-purpose flour
2 c  Quick-cooking oats
2 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Almond extract
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add flour, oats, extracts
and salt; mix well. Roll out dough to 1/4-in, thickness. Cut with a
3-in. heart-shaped cookie cutter; place on ungreased baking sheets.
Bake at 350 deg. for 12-15 minutes. While cookies are warm, spread
melted chocolate on tops. Cool. Using a pastry tube, decorate with
confectioners' sugar icing if desired. Yield: 3-1/2 dozen
