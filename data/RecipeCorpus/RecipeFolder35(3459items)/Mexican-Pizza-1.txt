Title: Mexican Pizza-1
Categories: Pizza, Tasteofhome
Yield: 2 servings
Drained
1 c  Monterey jack cheese --
Shredded
1 c  Lettuce
2 ea Tortilla flour
3 oz Pepperoni slices
2 md Tomatoes -- chopped
1/2 c  Salsa
1 cn Olives -- ripe 2 1/4oz
Place one tortilla on a microwave-safe plate. Layer with half of the
pepperoni, tomatoes, salsa, olives and cheese. Microwave on high for
1 1/2 to 2 minutes or until cheese melts. Top with half of the
lettuce. Make a second pizza with remaining ingredients. Yield 2
servings.
