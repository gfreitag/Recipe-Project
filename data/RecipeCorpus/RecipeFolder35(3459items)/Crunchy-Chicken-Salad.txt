Title: Crunchy Chicken Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
Oil
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Sesame seeds -- toasted
1 1/2 ts Soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt -- optional
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 ds Hot pepper sauce
4 c  Cooked chicken -- shredded
2 c  Shredded lettuce
1 c  Carrots -- julienned
1 c  Cucumber -- julienned
2/3 c  Green onion -- strips
1 c  Bean sprouts
Dressing
2 tb Peanut oil -- or vegetable
In large salad bowl, toss the chicken, lettuce, carrots, cucumber,
green onion and bean sprouts. Refrigerate,. In a small bowl, combine
dressing ingredients. Refrigerate. Just before serving pour dressing
over salad and toss gently. Serve with breadsticks. Yield: 10
servings.
