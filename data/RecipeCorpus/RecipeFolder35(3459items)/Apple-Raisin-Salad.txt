Title: Apple Raisin Salad
Categories: Salads, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
1/8 ts Pepper
1 pk Equal sweetner
1 ea Head Boston Lettuce
Separated into leaves
2 md Red Delicious Apples, cubed
1 c  Raisins
3/4 c  Walnuts, chopped
1/4 c  Skim milk
1/4 c  Cottage cheese, low fat
1/2 ts Horseradish sauce
Place apples, raisins, and walnuts in a large bowl Place remaining
ingredients except lettuce in blender or food processor mixing till
smooth. Pour blended ingredients over apple mixture. Toss to coat.
Arrange lettuce leaves in 6 bowls and spoon apple mixture into center
of bowls.

255 Calories 6.1 g. Protein 51 mg. Sodium
33.2 g. Carbohydrates 9.8 g. Fat 1 mg carbohydrate
