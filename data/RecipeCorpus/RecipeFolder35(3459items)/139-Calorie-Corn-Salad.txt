Title: 139 Calorie Corn Salad
Categories: Vegetables, Salads, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Vinegar, red wine
1 ts Tamari soy sauce
1/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Parsley, fine chopped, fresh
1 pk Equal
34 oz Corn, canned *1 (2 17oz can)
1 md Potato, diced
1 md Celery rib, diced
1/4 c  Green onion. chopped
1/4 c  Cottage cheese, low fat
Mix first 4 ingredients in large bowl. Place remaining ingredients in
food processor or blender and mix until smooth.

Pour over corn mixture and toss.  Chill
