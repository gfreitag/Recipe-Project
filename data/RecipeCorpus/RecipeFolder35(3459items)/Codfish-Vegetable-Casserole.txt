Title: Codfish Vegetable Casserole
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Casseroles, Main dish, Canadian, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Butter
1/2 ts Pepper
2 c  Flaked codfish (fresh)
1 c  Cooked vegetables
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 c  White sauce
1/4 ts Paprika
1 c  Corn kernels
Fry onion in butter until tender.  Combine with other ingredients and
season with salt to taste.  Pour into greased casserole and cover with
pastry dough.  Bake in 425f for 30 minutes.

This recipe comes from Eva Combden of Barr'd Islands (Labrador?) from
FATBACK AND MOLASSES, 1974
