Title: Kahlua Mudslide
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 oz Vodka
1 oz Cream
1/2 oz Kahlua
1/2 oz Irish Cream
Mix all ingredients well.  Serve over ice cubes.
