Title: Brushetta for 6
Categories: Breads, Cheese/eggs, Vegetables, Italian
Yield: 6 servings
6 ts Olive paste
2 c  Vegetable strips
1/2 c  Cheese, parmesan
12 sl Bread, italian
Olive oil
6 ea Garlic cloves, split
Brush both sides of each thick slice of bread with oil, broil both
sides to toast. rub slices with halved garlic. sprad one side with
1/2 tsp each olive paste  Top with roasted vegetable strips ( see
Roasted Vegetable Lasagna for instructions), top with cheese and serve
