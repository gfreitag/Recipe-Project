Title: Fiesta Ranch Green Chile Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
4 1/4 oz Black olives -- chopped &
Drained
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese -- finely
Shredded
1 1/8 oz Hidden Valley Fiesta Ranch
Dip mix
16 oz Sour cream
4 oz Green chiles -- diced &
Drained
Combine dip mix with sour cream.  Stir in remaining ingredients.
Serve with chips, vegetables or crackers.
