Title: Maple Cherry Syrup
Categories: Condiments, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
1/2 ea Lemon -- juice of 1/2 lemon
1/2 ts Lemon peel -- grated
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 c  Tart cherries -- dried
Chopped
3/4 c  Maple syrup
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Honey
1 ea Orange -- juice of one
Orange
1 ts Orange peel -- grated
In a medium saucepan, combine the first seven ingredients. Bring to
boil over med. heat, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat and simmer 10
min. Add cherries. Cook for 2 min. Serve over cornmeal pancakes,
waffles or French toast. Yield: 1 1/2 cups.
