Title: Handy 'Crisp' Topping Mix
Categories: Mixes, Nuts/grains, Cake/cookie
Yield: 2 servings
Saves time.
2 c  Flour
2 c  Oatmeal, quick cook
1 1/3 c  Packed brown sugar
1 tb Cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg
This handy topping mix can
-be kept in the fridge for
-up to 2 months.
Keep a supply on hand for
-when you want to whip up a
-quick fruit crisp.
Combine all ingredients and store in covered container or airtight
ziploc type bag.  Keeps in fridge for up to 2 months.
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%% %%%%% TO USE  %%%%% %%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%% Fill an
8 inch baking dish with sliced fruit (tossed with a bit of sugar if
desired) Combine 1-1/4 cups of MIX along with 1/3 cup melted butter.
Pat crumb mixture on top of fruit and bake as usual. Coconut can be
added for a change of pace also. Origin: Adapted from Canadian
Living, Sept/95. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/95.
