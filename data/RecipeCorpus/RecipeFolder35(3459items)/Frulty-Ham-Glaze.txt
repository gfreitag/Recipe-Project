Title: Frulty Ham Glaze
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Preserves
1/4 c  Cranberry-orange sauce
1/4 c  Apricot -- or peach
Combine ingredients in a small bowl. About 30 minutes before ham is
done, remove from the oven. Score surface; spoon glaze over entire
ham. Return to the oven, basting occasionally during the last 30
minutes of baking. Yield: 1/2 cup
