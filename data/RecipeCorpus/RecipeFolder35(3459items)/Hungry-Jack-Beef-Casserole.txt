Title: Hungry Jack Beef Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Boston baked beans; 16oz
1 cn Hungry Jack flaky biscuits;
.  refrigerated, 10oz
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
1 1/2 lb Ground beef or turkey
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1 c  Barbecue sauce
1 tb Brown sugar
Preheat the oven to 375øF.

In a large skillet, cook the ground meat and onion over medium-high
heat until browned; drain.  Stir in the barbecue sauce, brown sugar
and beans; heat until bubbly.  Pour into an ungreased 2 1/2-quart
casserole dish.

Separate the dough into 10 biscuits; cut each biscuit in half
crosswise. Place the biscuits, cut side down, over the hot meat
mixture around the edge of the casserole.  Sprinkle the cheese over
the top of the biscuits.

Bake at 375øF  for 22 to 27 minutes or until the biscuits are golden
brown.

protein, 43g carbohydrates, 5g dietary fiber, 80mg cholesterol,
