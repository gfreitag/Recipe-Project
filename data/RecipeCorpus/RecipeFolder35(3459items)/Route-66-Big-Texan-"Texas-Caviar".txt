Title: Route 66 Big Texan "Texas Caviar"
Categories: Dips/spread, Route 66
Yield: 1 servings
-chopped
1 tb Seasoned Salt
2 tb Chili Powder
2 tb Ground Cumin
1/4 ts Ground Red Pepper
2 cn (16 Oz) Black-Eyed Peas,
-drained
1 md Jalapeno -- minced
1/4 sm White Onion -- chopped
1/3 c  Italian Dressing
1/2    Green Bell Pepper --
Combine black-eyed peas with remaining ingredients. Serve chilled
with corn chips. Makes 5 cups.
