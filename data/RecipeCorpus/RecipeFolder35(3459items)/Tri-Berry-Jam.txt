Title: Tri-Berry Jam
Categories: Can/preserv, Tasteofhome
Yield: 30 servings
Fresh
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 pk Powdered fruit pectin -- 1
3/4 oz Each
4 c  Frozen blueberries -- or
Fresh
2 1/2 c  Raspberries -- or fresh red
2 1/2 c  Frozen strawberries -- or
Combine the berries and lemon juice in a large kettle; crush fruit
slightly. Stir in pectin. Bring to a full rolling boil over high heat,
stirring constantly. Stir in sugar; return to a full rolling boil.
Boil 1 min. stirring constantly. Remove from the heat; skim off any
foam. Pour hot into hot jars, leaving 1/4 in. headspace. Adjust caps.
Process for 15 min. in boiling-water bath. Yield: about 6 pints.
