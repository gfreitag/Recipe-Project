Title: Acorn-Corn Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1    Egg
1 tb Maple syrup
1 tb Cooking oil
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Acorn meal
1/2 c  Cornmeal
2 tb Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
Mix together the dry ingredients. In a separate bowl beat egg, and add
the liquid ingredients. Combine with a few swift strokes. Pour batter
into a small square greased pan and bake at 425 for 20 min or until
firm to the touch. Serve hot with butter.

From: Edible Wild Fruits and Nuts of Canada, published by the National
Museums of Canada, ISBN 0-660-00128-4
