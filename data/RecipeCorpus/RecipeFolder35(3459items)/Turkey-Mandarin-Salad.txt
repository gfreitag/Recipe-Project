Title: Turkey Mandarin Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Drained
1 c  Cooked macaroni
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
3/4 c  Whipping cream -- whipped
1/3 c  Slivered almonds
Toasted almonds, optional
2 c  Cooked turkey -- cubed
1 tb Onion -- finely chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Seedless red grape --
Halves
1 c  Celery -- diced
1 cn Mandarin oranges -- l5 oz
In a large bowl, combine turkey, onion and sait: mix well. Add grapes,
celery, orange; and macaronl; toss lightly to mix. Cover; and
refrigerate. Just before serving, combine mayonnaise and whipped
cream; fold into salad along with almonds. Top with toasted almonds
if desired. Yield: 6-8 servings
