Title: Pecan Clusters - SL 11/81
Categories: Desserts, Nuts/grains
Yield: 12 dozen
1 ea 13 oz can evaporated milk
1/2 c  Butter
6 c  Pecan halves
1 ea 7 oz jar marshmallow creme
1 1/2 lb Milk chocolate kisses
5 c  Sugar
Place marshmallow creme and kisses in a large bowl; set aside.
Combine sugar, milk, and butter in a saucepan.  Bring mixture to a
boil; cook for 8 minutes. Pour over marshmallow creme and kisses,
stirring until well blended.  Stir in pecans.  Drop by teaspoonfuls
onto waxed paper. Yield: about 12 dozen.

Margaret H. King of Georgia, in November, 1981 "Southern Living".
