Title: Blueberry Cobbler
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Biscuit mix
1/2 c  Skim milk
1 ts Liquid sucaryl
3 c  Blueberries
2 tb Liquid sucaryl
2 tb Quick-cooking tapioca
3/4 c  Water
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.Combine blueberries,the 2 tbsps
sucaryl,tapioca,and water; turn into a shallow 6 cup baking
dish.Combine biscuit mix,milk,and 1 tsp of sucaryl,mixing only until
flour is dampened.With spoon,form into 6 biscuits on top of the
berries.Bake 30 to 40 minutes,or until the biscuits are golden brown
and the berries bubbling hot.

Per serving: Calories 163 Proteins 3.5g Fat 3.5g Carbohydrates 30.5g
(with sugar  247 calories)
