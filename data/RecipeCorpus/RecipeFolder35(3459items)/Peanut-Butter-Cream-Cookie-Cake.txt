Title: Peanut Butter Cream Cookie Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
Cookies -- crushed
FROSTING:
1/2 cn Vanilla frosting
1/2 c  Creamy peanut butter
2 tb Milk -- (or more)
1 pk White cake mix
1 c  Water
1/3 c  Oil
2    Eggs
1 1/4 c  Peanut butter sandwich
Heat oven to 350.  Grease 13 x 9 pan.  In large bowl, combine cake
mix, water, oil and eggs at low speed until moistened; beat 2 minutes
until moistened; beat 2 minutes at high speed.  Gently fold in
crushed cookies. Pour batter into greased pan.  Bake at 350 for 30 to
40 minutes.  Cool completely.  In small bowl, combine frosting,
peanut butter and milk; beat until smooth.  Spread evenly over cake.
Garnish with additional crushed peanut butter sandwich cookies, if
desired.
