Title: German Pork Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Meats, Aust/german
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg
1 lb Fat pork
1 c  Coffee
2 c  Brown sugar
1    Box of raisins
1 1/2 c  Nuts
Mix well.  Grease and flour tube cake pan.  Preheat oven at 350
degrees. Baking time is 1 to 1 1/2 hours.  Moisten white cloth with
wine and put it over the cake for 1 or 2 weeks before you plan to
serve it. Dampen cloth with wine when it gets dry.  My grandmother
also used 1 lb of pork sausage if pork fat was unavailable. Randy Rigg
