Title: Berber Marinade
Categories: Salsa/sauce, African, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 cup
1 ts Fenugreek seeds (opt)
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Allspice berries
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 tb Salt or to taste
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Chicken stock
-or water if needed
1 md Onion(s), diced (1 cup)
3    Garlic clove(s)
-minced (1 tbs)
2 tb Ginger, minced
1/2 c  Hungarian paprika
1 tb Coriander seeds
2 ts Cracked black peppercorns
2 ts Cardamon pods
1 ts Hot red pepper flakes
-or to taste
Place the onion, garlic, spices, and salt in a dry skillet. Cook over
medium heat for 2-3 minutes, or until the spices are lightly roasted
and fragrant.

Combine the spice mixture, lemon juice, and oil in a blender, and
pure to a smooth paste. If the mixture is too dry to pure, add a
little stock or water.

Spread the paste on the seafood, poultry or meat, and marinate
overnight.

Makes enough for 1«-2 pounds seafood, poultry, or meat.
