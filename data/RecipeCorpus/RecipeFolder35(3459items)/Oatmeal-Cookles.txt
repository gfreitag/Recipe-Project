Title: Oatmeal Cookles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Egg substitute
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
1 ts Vanilla extract
3 c  Quick-cooking oats
2/3 c  All-purpose flour
2/3 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Packed brown sugar
1 ts Baking powder
In a mixing bowl, mix oats, flour, sugars, baking powder and salt.
Add egg substitute, corn syrup and vanilla; mix. Drop by rounded
teaspoonfuls onto cookie sheets that have been sprayed with non-stick
vegetable cooking spray. Bake at 350 deg. for 10-12 minutes. Yield: 2
dozen. Nutritional Information: One serving (1 cookie) has 101
calories, 47 mg sodium, trace cholesterol, 21 gm carbohydrate, 3 gm
protein, 1 gm fat
