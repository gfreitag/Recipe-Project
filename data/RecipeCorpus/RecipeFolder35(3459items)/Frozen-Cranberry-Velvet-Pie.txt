Title: Frozen Cranberry Velvet Pie
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart, Holiday
Yield: 8 servings
6 tb Butter or margarine, melted
1 1/4 c  Fine vanilla wafer crumbs
MMMMM-----------------------------OR----------------------------------
1 pk Store bought crust
MMMMM----------------------------PIE---------------------------------
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 cn 16 oz whole cranberry sauce
8 oz Pkg cream cheese, softened
1 c  Whipping cream
1/4 c  Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------OPTIONAL-------------------------------
Whipping cream for garnish
If making own crust Combine crumbs and melted butter; press firmly
into bottom and sides of buttered 9 inch pie pan.  Chill until firm.

Beat cream cheese until fluffy. Set aside

Combine whipping cream, sugar and vanilla, whip until thickened but
not stiff.  Gradually add to the whipped cream cheese, beating until
smooth and creamy.

Set aside a few whole cranberries from the sauce for garnish.

Fold the remaining cranberry sauce into whipped mixture.  Spoon into
chilled crust.  Freeze until firm.

Remove from the freezer 10 minutes before serving.

To serve, top with the additional whipped cream and the reserved whole
cranberries.
