Title: Deluxe German Potatoe Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
3/4 ts Dry mustard
5 lb Red new potatoes -- unpeeled
Cooked/slic
2 ea Carrots -- shredded
2 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
Additional salt to taste
1/2 lb Sliced bacon
1 c  Celery -- thinly sliced
1 c  Onion -- chopped
1 c  Sugar
2 tb All-purpose flour
1 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Water
In a skillet, cook bacon until crisp. Drain, reserving 1/4 cup
drippings. Crumble bacon and set aside. Saute the celely and onion in
drippings until tender. Combine sugar and flour; add to skillet with
vinegar, water, salt and mustard. Cook, stirring constantly, until
mixture thickens and bubbles. In a large bowl, combine potatoes,
carrots and parsley; pour the sauce over and stir gently to coat.
Season to taste with additional salt. Spoon into a serving dish;
garnish with crumbled bacon. Serve warm. Yield: 14-16 servings
