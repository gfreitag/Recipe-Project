Title: Onion Jam
Categories: Condiments, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 cup
3 tb (to 4 tbs) honey
2 tb Sugar
1 pn Cayenne pepper
1 pn Ground cloves
Salt and pepper
1 lb Red onions, peeled
-very thinly sliced
-(abt 4 cups)
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
2 c  Dry red wine
2 c  Water
1. Place the onions, vinegar, wine, water, honey, sugar, cayenne, and
cloves in a large nonreactive sautÂ‚ pan. the onions should be
completely covered. Bring the mixture to a boil, reduce the heat, and
gently simmer until the liquid is completely absorbed, about 45
minutes. Stir from time to time, more frequently at the end, to keep
the onions from burning.

2. Correct the seasoning, adding salt and pepper, vinegar, or honey to
taste. the mixture should be a little sweet, a little sour, and very
highly seasoned.

Serve with pita chips or bruschetta.
