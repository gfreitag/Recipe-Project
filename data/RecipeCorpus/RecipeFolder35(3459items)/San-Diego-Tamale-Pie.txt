Title: San Diego Tamale Pie
Categories: Tex/mexican, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Whole kernel corn;
2 cn Tomato paste; (6-oz ea)
1 cn Enchilada sauce; (4-oz)
1 cn Green chili, (4-oz)
1 tb Chili powder;
1 c  Milk;
1 1/2 lb Lean ground meat;
1 lg Onion; chopped
2 cl Garlic; minced
1 c  Yellow cornmeal;
1 cn Ripe olives; pitted (6-oz)
1 1/2 lb Longhorn cheese; grated
Brown meat, drain off fat.  Add onion and garlic, heat, add everything
except cheese.  Pour into 3 qt casserole and bake at 350 F. for 30
minutes, uncovered.  Add cheese and cook 5 - 10 minutes longer.

Source: June Gustatson, Hope Kountry Kitchen, Hope Christian School,
Albuquerque, New Mexico
