Title: Itallan Herb Salad Dresslng
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Dried oregano
pn Pepper
3/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1 tb Parmesan cheese -- grated
Or Romano cheese
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine all ingredients; shake
well. Refrigerate. Shake well again before serving over greens.
Yield: 1-1/4 cups
