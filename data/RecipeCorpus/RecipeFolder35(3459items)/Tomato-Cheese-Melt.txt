Title: Tomato Cheese Melt
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
2 ea Tomato slices
1 tb Parmesan cheese -- shredded
1 ea Onion bagel
Or English muffin, split
1/4 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
On each bagel or muffin half, sprinkle half of the cheddar cheese and
cayenne pepper. Top wifh a tomato slice. Sprinkle half of the Parmesan
cheese over each tomato. Broil 6 in. from the heat for 4-5 minutes or
until cheese is bubbly. Yleld: 2 servings
