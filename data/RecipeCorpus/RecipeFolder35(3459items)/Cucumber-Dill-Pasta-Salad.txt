Title: Cucumber-Dill Pasta Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
3/4 c  Bottled cucumber salad
Dressing
1 ts Dill weed
Salt and pepper to taste
3 c  Cooked pasta
1/2 c  Carrot -- thinly sliced
1/2 c  Celery -- thinly sliced
1 c  Broccoli florets --
Parboiled
1 ea Green -- thinly sliced
Combine all ingredients in a large salad bowl. Chill until serving
time. Yield: 4-6 servings
