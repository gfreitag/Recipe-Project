Title: Buttery French Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Celery seed
1    Loaf French bread, abt 20",
-sliced
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1/4 ts Paprika
In a smal bowl, combine butter, paprika and celery seed; spread
between bread slices and over top. Wrap bread tightly in foil. Bake
at 375 deg. for 15 minutes. Open the foil and bake 5 minutes longer.
Yield: 6-8 servings
