Title: Spicy Tomato Steak
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Olive oil
3 md Tomatoes -- into wedges
2 md Potatoes -- thinly sliced
2 cn Green chile -- 4oz each
Chopped
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1 ts Dried basil
2 tb Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1 lb Round steak -- cut 1/4 in
Strips
Trimmed
1/4 c  Flour
In a mixing bowl, combine vinegar, salt and pepper; toss with beef.
Cover and marinate for 30 min, drain. Place flour in a bowl; add beef
and toss to coat. In a skillet, cook beef in oil over medium heat for
15 to 20 min. or until tender. Add remaining ingredients. Cover and
simmer for 20 to 30 min. or until the potatoes are tender, stirring
occasionally. Yield; 6 SERVINGS.
