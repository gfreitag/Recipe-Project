Title: Oak Hill Icebox Hash
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Applesauce
1/2 c  Cocoanut
2 ea Egg whites - beaten
12 ea Marshmallows
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ea Lemon - juice
Salt - a few grains
Dissolve marshmallows in heated applesauce, add rest of ingredients,
stir and place in refrigerator to chill. Vanilla may be used instead
of lemon juice.

Source: Hazel Scobie, Union Grange, Summit County, OH
