Title: Breakfast Sherbet
Categories: Breakfast, Beverages
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1 tb Lime juice
5 c  Honeydew melon; chopped
3 md Kiwi fruit; peeled & quarter
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1. In a food processor, combine all ingredients; process until smooth.
Pour the mixture into a medium bowl; cover with plastic wrap and
freeze 3 to 4 hours, until firm.

2. Remove from the freezer and let stand 15 minutes.  With an electric
mixer, beat until smooth.  Cover and freeze 3 to 4 hours longer, until
firm.

Each serving (1/2 cup) provides: 1 FR, 35 C.  Per serving: 90 cal, 1g
pro, 0g fat, 22g car, 29mg sod, 1mg chol.
