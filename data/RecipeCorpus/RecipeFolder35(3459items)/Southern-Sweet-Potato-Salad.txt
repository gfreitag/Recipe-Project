Title: Southern Sweet Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
1 c  Sliced -- celery
1/3 c  Dates -- chopped
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
Lettuce leaves
1 cn Mandarin oranges -- 11
oz Drained
2 lb Potatoes
Sweet, peeled and cut into
1/2    -inch cubes
2 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Orange juice
1 tb Honey
1 ts Orange peel -- grated
1/2 ts Ground ginger
In a medium saucepan, cook sweet potatoes in boiling salted water just
until tender, about 5-8 minutes (do not overcook). Drain; toss with
the lemon juice. In a large bowl, combine mayonnaise, orange juice,
honey, orange peel, ginger, salt and nutmeg. Add the warm potatoes,
celery and dates. Toss to coat well. Cover and chill. Before serving,
gently stir in the pecans. Spoon salad onto a lettuce-lined platter.
Arrange oranges around salad. Yield: 6-8 servings
