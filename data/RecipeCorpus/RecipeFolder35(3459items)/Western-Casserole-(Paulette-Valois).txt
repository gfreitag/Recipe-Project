Title: Western Casserole (Paulette Valois)
Categories: Meats, Casseroles
Yield: 4 to 6 servi
1/2 ts Chili
1 ts Onion; minced
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Cornmeal; yellow
10    Biscuits
1 lb Beef; Ground
1 cn Red Kidney Beans
1 c  Cheese, Shredded Sharp
1 cn Corn; Kernel, drained
1 cn Tomato Soup
1/4 c  Milk
Brown Beef.  Stir in corn, Beans, Soup, Cheese, Milk, Onion, and Chili
powder.  Bring to Boil.  Turn mixture into 2 quart casserole. Bake
uncovered at 400F for 10 minutes.  Dip Biscuits in cornmeal. Place on
edge of boiling hot casserole.  Bake 20 minutes longer or until
biscuits are golden.
Source: Rochon Family Cookbook, 1990 From: Cousin Paulette Valois
