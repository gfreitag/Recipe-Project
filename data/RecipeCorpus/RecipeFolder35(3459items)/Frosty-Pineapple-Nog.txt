Title: Frosty Pineapple Nog
Categories: Beverages, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/4 ts Salt
5 ea Ice cubes
Fresh mint sprigs, optional
3 c  Buttermilk
1 cn Crushed pineapple -- 8 oz
W/juice chilled
1/4 c  Sugar -- or honey
In a blender container, combine buttermilk, pineapple, sugar or honey,
vanilla and salt. Cover and blend about 30 seconds or until combined.
With blender running, add ice cubes, one at a time, through opening
in lid. Blend until mixture is nearly smooth and frothy. Pour into
chilled glasses. If desired, garnish with a sprig of mint. Yield; 6
servings
