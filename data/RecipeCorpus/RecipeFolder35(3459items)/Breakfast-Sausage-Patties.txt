Title: Breakfast Sausage Patties
Categories: Sausage, Breakfast, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Rosemary; crushed
1/4 ts Salt
2 ts Olive oil
10 oz Ground turkey
1/2 ts Dried sage
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1. In a medium bowl, with a fork, combine all the ingredients except
the oil.  Shape into 8 equal 1/4" thick patties.

2. In a large nonstick skillet, heat the oil.  Add the patties; cook
10 minutes, turning once, until browned on both sides.

Each serving (2 patties) provides: 1/2 FA, 2 P.  Per serving: 116
cal, 15g pro, 6g fat, 0g car, 182mg sod, 41mg chol,
Night Before:  Mix and shape patties.  Cover and refrigerate.
