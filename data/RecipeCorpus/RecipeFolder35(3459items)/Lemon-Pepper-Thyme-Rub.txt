Title: Lemon Pepper Thyme Rub
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Monosodium glutamate --
Optional
1/4 ts Ground coriander
1/8 ts Ground cumin
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
6 tb Lemon pepper
2 tb Ground thyme
2 tb Paprika
2 ts Granular garlic
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients; mix well. Apply generously to steaks or
hamburgers. Store unused portion in a covered glass container. Yield:
about 1/2 cup
