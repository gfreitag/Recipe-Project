Title: Terry Genger's Pinto Bean Fiesta Fudge
Categories: Candies, Route 66
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Vanilla
2 lb Powdered Sugar
1 c  Pecans -- chopped
1 c  Pinto Beans, Warm Cooked
3/4 c  Butter -- melted
1 c  Cocoa
Mash or sieve beans. Add melted butter, cocoa, and vanilla. Mix
thoroughly. Stir in powdered sugar gradually. Add nuts and press
mixture into a 9 x 13 buttered pan. Store in refrigerator until ready
to serve.
