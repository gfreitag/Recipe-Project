Title: Quickie Clam Chowder+
Categories: Soup/stew, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
2 cn Minced clams with juice
-(8 oz. each)
1 qt Half & Half
2 cn Cream of potato soup,
-undiluted
2 cn Cream of celery soup,
-undiluted
Mix all ingredients. Heat and serve.
