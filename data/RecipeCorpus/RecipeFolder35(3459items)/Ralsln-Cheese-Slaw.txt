Title: Ralsln Cheese Slaw
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Onion powder
Or dried minced onion
2 c  Finely shredded cabbage
1 c  Finely shredded carrot
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Raisins
1/3 c  Salad dressing -- or
Mayonnaise
2 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Prepared mustard
In a small bowl, combine salad dressing, lemon juice, sugar, salt,
mustard and onion powder. In a large bowl, combine cabbage, carrot,
cheese and raisins. Fold in dressing. Cover and refrigerate for at
least 1 hour. Yield: 8 servings
