Title: Harvest Sausage Dressing   [for Poultry]
Categories: Dress/stuff, Poultry
Yield: 9 cups
2 ea 8-oz packages herb-seasoned
-stuffing mix
1 1/3 c  Mincemeat
1 ea 8-oz can sliced water
-chestnuts; drained and
-chopped
2 ts Poultry seasoning
1 lb Bulk pork sausage
2 c  Celery; chopped
2 c  Mushrooms; sliced
1 1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1 tb Plus 1 tspn chicken-flavored
-bouillon granules
1 3/4 c  Boiling water
Brown sausage in a Dutch oven, stirring to crumble; drain.  Return
sausage to Dutch oven; add celery, mushrooms, and onion.  Cook over
low heat until vegetables are crisp-tender.  Add bouillon granules
and water; bring to a boil.
Add stuffing mix and remaining ingredients to Dutch oven.  Stir
well, and lightly stuff 2 cups of dressing into body cavity of
turkey. SPoon remaining 7 cups of dressing into a lightly greased 12"
x 8" x 2" baking dish.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 45
minutes. Yield: 9 cups.

Dorothy L. Akers of Virginia in November, 1988 "Southern Living"
