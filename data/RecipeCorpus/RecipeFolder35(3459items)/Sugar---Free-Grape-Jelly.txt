Title: Sugar - Free Grape Jelly
Categories: Dips/spread, Fruit
Yield: 2 half-pints
1 1/2 c  Unsweetened grape juice
4 tb Liquid sucaryl
4 ts Unflavoured gelatin
1/2 c  Water
Soften gelatin in water.Bring grape juice to the boil,remove from
heat.Add softened gelatin,stirring to dissolve.Add sucaryl.Bring to
the full rolling boil.Ladle into clean half - pint jars; seal.Keep in
refrigerator.

Per tbsp: Calories 9 protein 0.5g no fat carbohydrates 2g (with sugar
~ 36 calories)

Note:If no more than 1 tbsp is used,need not be calculated as
exchanges. If larger amounts are used: (2 tbsp = 1/2 fruit exchange)
