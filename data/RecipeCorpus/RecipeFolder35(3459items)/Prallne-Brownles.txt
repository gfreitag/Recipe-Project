Title: Prallne Brownles
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Packed light brown sugar
2 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Packed dark brown sugar
3/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Divided
2 tb Evaporated milk
1/2 c  Pecans -- coarsely chopped
In a saucepan. cnmbine the dark brown sugar, 1/4 cup butter and milk.
Stir over low heat just until butter is melted. Pour into an
ungreased 8-in. square baking pan; sprinkle evenly with pecans. In a
mixing bowl, cream light brown sugar and remaining butter; add eggs.
Stir in flour, vanilla and salt until moistened. Spread over pecans.
Bake at 350 deg. for 30-35 minutes or until brownies test done. Cool
5 minutes in pan: invert on to a tray or serving plate. Cool slightly
before cutting. Yield: 16 brownies
