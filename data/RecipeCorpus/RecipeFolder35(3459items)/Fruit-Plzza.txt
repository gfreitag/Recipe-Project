Title: Fruit Plzza
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
oz Drained
1/2 c  Red grape halves
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Orange juice
2 tb Water
1 tb Lemon luice
1 1/2 ts Cornstarch
1 ds Salt
Peeled & thinly sliced
1 pk Refrigerated sugar cookie
Dough -- 20 ounces
1 pk Cream cheese -- softened 8
Ounces
1/4 c  Confectioners' sugar
1 ct Frozen whipped topping -- 8
oz Thawed
3    Kiwifruit
2 ea Bananas -- firm sliced
1 cn Mandarin oranges -- 11
Pat cookie dough into an ungreased 14-in. pizza pan. Bake at 375 deg.
for 10-12 minutes or until browned; cool. In a mixing bowl, beat the
cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Fold in whipped topping. Spread
over crust. Arrange fruit on top. In a saucepan, combine the sugar,
orange juice, water, lemon juice, cornstarch and salt; bring to a
boil, stirring constantly for 2 minutes or until thickened. Cool;
brush over fruit. Chill. Store in refrigerator. Yield: 16-20 servings
