Title: Mexican Cranberries - SL 11/94
Categories: Side dish, Fruit, Holiday
Yield: 2 -1/2 cups
10 1/2 oz Jar jalapeno pepper jelly
2 tb Fresh cilantro; chopped
16 oz Can whole-berry cranberry
-sauce
Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan; cook over low heat,
stirring often, until jelly melts.  Cool.   Yield: 2-1/2 cups

Carolyn Gaskins of North Carolina, November, 1994 "Southern Living"
