Title: Nippy Pork Chop Platter
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Melted fat
1 cn Chicken rice soup, condensed
6 ea Pork chops, lean 3/4 inch
Prepared mustard
1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
Spread pork chops with thin coating of mustard.

Combine flour, salt and pepper in paper bag; shake chops in bag to
coat with flour then brown in fat in heavy skillet.

Place chops in baking dish; cover with soup.  Cover and bake in 350
F. oven 40 minutes.  A relish tray of pickle and carrot sticks and a
dessert of sherbet would complete the meal.
