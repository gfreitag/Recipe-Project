Title: Peanut Butter Oatmeal Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Kids, Desserts, Snacks
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 c  Brown Sugar; Packed
1 lg Egg
1/4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla Extract
1 c  Quick Cooking Rolled Oats;
(Oatmeal)
1 c  Raisins
1 1/2 c  Unbleached All-Purpose Flour
1/2 ts Baking Powder
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter; Softened
1/2 c  Peanut Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
Sift the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt together,
blending well then set aside.  Using an electric mixer, beat the
butter and peanut butter on low speed.  Add the sugars.  Beat on
medium speed until fluffy. Add the egg and milk and vanilla extract
and beat for 1 minute. Add the dry ingredients, blending well.  Stir
in the oats and raisins. Place rounded Ts of the dough 2-inches apart
on cookie sheets. Bake in a preheated 375 degree F. oven for 10 to 15
minutes. From: Rich Harper ~-- Submitted By GAIL SHIPP   On   10-11-95
