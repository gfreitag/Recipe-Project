Title: German Leberknoedel (Liver Dumplings)
Categories: Soup/stew, Aust/german, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
2    Eggs; well beaten
2 tb Parsley; chopped
1 tb Flour
1/2 lb Liver; ground
1    Medium onion; minced
2 tb Butter
1/4 lb Raw spinach
Mix all ingredients thoroughly. Shape into small balls. Drop into
boiling soup and cook for 5 minutes. From: Dorie Villarreal Date:
10-09-93
