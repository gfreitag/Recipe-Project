Title: Snack Crackers
Categories: Snacks, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Dressing mix -- l ounce
2 pk Oyster crackers -- 10 ounces
Each
3/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 1/2 ts Dill weed
1    Envelope dry ranch salad
Combine oil, dill and salad dressing mix. Place the crackers in a
large bowl: pour dressing mixture over and toss gently. Allow to
stand at least 1 hour before serving. Yield: 12 cups
