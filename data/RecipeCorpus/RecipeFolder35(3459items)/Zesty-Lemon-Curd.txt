Title: Zesty Lemon Curd
Categories: Condiments, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
Tabespoon-size pieces
3/4 cn Lemon juice -- fresh
Grated peel of 1 lemon
3 ea Eggs -- beaten
2 c  Sugar
1/2 lb Butter -- cut into
In the top of a double boiler, combine all ingredients. Cook and stir
over boiling water until thickened like pudding. Remove from heat and
cool. Chill until ready to serve. Spread on muffins or rolls or serve
over waffles or ice cream. Keep well in the refrigerator up to 2
weeks. Yield: 3 cups.
