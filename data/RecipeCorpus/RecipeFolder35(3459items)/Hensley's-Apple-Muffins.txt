Title: Hensley's Apple Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Breads, Route 66
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Nuts -- chopped
1/2 c  Orange Juice
1/3 c  Cooking Oil
2    Eggs -- beaten
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Raw Apple -- grated
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking Powder
Preheat oven to 375 F. Coat twelve 2 1/2 inch muffin tins with
nonstick spray. In a mixing bowl, combine dry ingredients and stirn
in  nuts. Blend thoroughly. In a separate bowl combine remaining
ingredients. Make well in dry ingredients and stir in apple mixture
just until moistened. Do not overmix. Spoon batter into tins, filling
3/4 full. Bake for 20 mins. Makes 12 large muffins.
