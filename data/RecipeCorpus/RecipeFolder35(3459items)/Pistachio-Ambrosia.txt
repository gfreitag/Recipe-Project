Title: Pistachio Ambrosia
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
Pistachio
2 c  Sour cream -- 16 oz
1 pk Whipped topping -- 12 oz
Frozen
Thawed
Chopped pecans, -- optional
2 cn Fruit cocktail -- 17oz each
2 cn Pineapple chunks in juice --
20 oz Each
2 cn Mandarin oranges -- 11 oz
Each
4 pk Instant pudding mix -- 3.4
oz Each
Drain fruit cocktail, pineapple and oranges, reserving 3 cups juice.
Set fruit aside; pour juice into a 4 qt. bowl. Add pudding mix and
mix until smooth. Stir in sour cream. Add whipped topping and mix
until  smooth. Fold in fruit; chill for several hours. Top with
pecans just a before serving if desired. Yield: 16-20 servings.
