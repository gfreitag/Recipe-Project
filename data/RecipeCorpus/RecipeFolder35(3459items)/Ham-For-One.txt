Title: Ham For One
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Crushed pineapple -- with
Juice
1 sm Ham steak
Or slice of leftover baked
Ham
Line a 9-in. pie pan with foil. Place ham in pan, sprinkle with brown
sugar. Top with pineapple. Bake, uncovered, at 350 deg. for 20-25
minutes or until heated through. Yield: 1 serving
