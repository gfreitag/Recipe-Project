Title: Spiced Apple Cider - SL 11/85
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 2 -1/2 quart
2 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Apple brandy (optional)
Cinnamon sticks
1/2 ga Apple cider
1 c  Sugar
2 ts Whole cloves
1 ea Whole nutmeg
Combine apple cider and sugar in a Dutch oven.  Tie cloves and
nutmeg in a cheesecloth bag; add to cider mixture.  Bring to a boil;
reduce heat, and simmer 15 minutes.  Remove spice bag, and add
juices. Stir in brandy, if desired.  Simmer until heated.  Serve with
cinnamon-stick stirrers. Yield: 2-1/2 quarts.

From Linda Gellatly of North Carolina, in November, 1985 "Southern
