Title: Chocolate Cupcakes
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1 3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Unsifted flour
4 lg Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
4    Squares of semi sweet or
Unsweetened chocolate
1/2 lb Margarine
2 c  Broken nutmeats
Melt chocolate and margarine, add nuts in pan.  In mixing bowl put
sugar, flour, and unbeaten eggs.  Do not beat but mix well.  Stir in
chocolate mixture.  Do not beat.  Add vanilla.  Fill paper cups full.
Bake at 325 degrees for 25 to 35 minutes.  Cool 8 to 10 minutes
before removing from the pan.
