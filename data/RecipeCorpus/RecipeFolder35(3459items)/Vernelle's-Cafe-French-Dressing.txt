Title: Vernelle's Cafe French Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Route 66, Salads
Yield: 12 servings
3 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1 tb Heinz 57 Sauce
1    Bottle Oil (17 Oz)
1/2 tb Prepared Mustard
1/2 ts Tabasco Sauce
17 oz Vinegar
1 3/4 c  Sugar
2 1/2 tb Curry Powder
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
1 tb Salt
1/3 ts Black Pepper
1 cn (51 Oz) Tomato Soup
Combine sugar with curry powder, garlic, salt and pepper. Add
remaining ingredients and beat with an electric mixer until well
blended. Store in refrigerator until ready to use. Makes 12 cups.
