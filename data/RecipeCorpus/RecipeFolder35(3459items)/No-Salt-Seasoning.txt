Title: No-Salt Seasoning
Categories: Tasteofhome
Yield: 30 servings
1 tb Dry mustard
1 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Celery seed
5 ts Onion powder
1 tb Garlic powder
1 tb Paprika
Combine all ingredients in a small jar with a shaker top. Use for
seasoning broiled fish, poultry, cooked vegetables, soups and stews
or place it on the table to be used individually. Yield: about 1/3
cup.
