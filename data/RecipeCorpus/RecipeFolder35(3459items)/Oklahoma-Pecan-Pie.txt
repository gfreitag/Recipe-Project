Title: Oklahoma Pecan Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Route 66
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Pecan Halves Or Pieces
1    9 Inch Pie Shell -- unbaked
3    Eggs -- lightly beaten
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Light Corn Syrup
1 tb Butter -- melted
Beat eggs, add sugar, corn syrup, and butter and mix together until
well blended. Stir in vanilla and pecans. Pour mixture into pie shell
and bake in a preheated 350 F oven for 45 to 55 mins or until a knife
inserted halfway between center and edge comes out clean. Cool well
on wire rack. Serve plain or with whipped cream. Makes 6 to 8 slices.
