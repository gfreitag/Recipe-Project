Title: Baked Pumpkin - SL 10/82
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Ground mace
1 ds Pepper
1/2 c  Pecan halves
1/2 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
1/4 c  Maple or maple-flavored
-syrup
3 c  Pumpkin; cooked and mashed
2 ea Eggs; beaten
1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Butter or margarine; melted
1/2 ts Salt
Combine first 8 ingredients, stirring well.  Pour pumpkin mixture
into a lightly greased 1-1/2 quart casserole.  Arrange pecan halves
on top of casserole.
Combine brown sugar and syrup in a small saucepan; cook, stirring
constantly, over medium heat until mixture reaches a boil.  Cool
slightly. Spoon glaze evenly over casserole.  Bake, uncovered, at 350
degrees F for 40 minutes.  Yield: 6 servings.

Carla C. Hunter of Georgia in October, 1982 "Southern Living" Typos
