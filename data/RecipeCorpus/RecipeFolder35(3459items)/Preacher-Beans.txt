Title: Preacher Beans
Categories: Vegetables, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
1 md Onion, chopped
1/8 ts Garlic salt
Red pepper, ground, to taste
Salt to taste
1 sm Package of pinto beans
1    Link of Italian sausage,
Sliced
3 sl Baked ham, chopped
Soak beans overnight or bring to a boil and drain.  Cook for 45
minutes. Saute meat, add meat, onion, and seasonings to beans.
Continue to cook until beans are tender. Randy Rigg
