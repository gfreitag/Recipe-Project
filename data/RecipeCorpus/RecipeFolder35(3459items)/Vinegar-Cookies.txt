Title: Vinegar Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  White sugar
2 tb Vinegar
1 ts Maple flavoring
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Shortening
1 ts Soda
2    Eggs, beaten
4 1/2 c  Flour
Cream shortening and sugars well.  Add flavoring, vinegar, and eggs.
Add flour, mix well.  Roll in balls.  Press flat with hand.  Bake at
350 degrees until lightly browned.
