Title: Kodiak Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
10 3/4 oz Undiluted
1    Jar picante sauce -- 8
Ounces
1    Bottle
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
4 c  Medium egg noodles -- cooked
& drained
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- 4 ounces
Shredded
Barbecue sauce -- 18 ounces
2 lb Ground beef
4 c  Onions -- diced
2 ea Garlic cloves -- minced
3 md Green peppers -- diced
4 c  Celery -- diced
1    Jar green olives -- 5 3/4 oz
Stuffed/und
1 cn Mushroom -- 4 oz
Stems/pcs/undra
1 cn Condensed tomato soup --
In a Dutch oven, brown ground beef with onions and garlic; drain. Add
remaining ingredients except cheese; mix well.Cover and bake at 350
deg. for 1 hour or until hot and bubbly. Sprinkle with the cheese
just before serving. Yield: 16-20 servings
