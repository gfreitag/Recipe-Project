Title: Hamburger Vegetable Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Carrots -- sliced
1 c  Celery -- sliced
1 c  Potatoes -- cubed peeled
2 ea Bay leaves
1 ts Salt
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Onion -- chopped
3 c  Beef broth
1 cn Tomatoes -- 28 oz w/liquid
Cut u
In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, brown ground beef: drain. Add
onion and cook until tender. Add remaining ingredients: bring to a
boil. Reduce heat: cover and simmer until vegetables are tender,
about 45-60 minutes. Yield: 8-10 servings (2-1/2 quarts)
