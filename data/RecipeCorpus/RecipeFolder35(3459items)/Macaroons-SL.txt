Title: Macaroons SL
Categories: Desserts, Candies, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 dozen
3 ea Egg whites
3 c  Sifted powdered sugar
16 oz Blanched almonds; finely
-chopped
1 tb Almond extract
Combine almonds and almond extract.  Set aside.
Beat egg whites (at room temperature) until foamy; gradually add
sugar, beating until stiff peaks form.  Fold in almond mixture.  Drop
mixture by heaping tablespoonfuls onto unglazed brown paper on cookie
sheet. Bake at 300 degrees F for 30 minutes or until lightly browned.
Remove from paper with spatula while warm. Yield: about 3 dozen.
