Title: CREAMY PEANUT BUTTER PIE
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1    Pie shell, 9"; baked
1/2 c  Sugar, brown
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Peanut butter, creamy
2    Egg
In a saucepan, combine the sugars, peanut butter, eggs and flour.
Gradually stir in the milk. Cook over moderate heat, stirring con-
stantly, until thick. Don't boil.

Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla. Pour into the baked pie
shell and chill. Serve with whipped cream.
