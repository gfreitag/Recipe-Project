Title: Frozen Punch
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Water
1 1/4 lb Sugar
1 ga Orange sherbet
4    Bottles of 7 Up
4 lg Cans orange juice
4 lg Cans pineapple juice
1 lg Can of crushed pineapple
1/2    Bottle of ReaLemon
Mix juices in very large container, add pineapple.  Cook water and
sugar until dissolved add to juice.  Stir well and freeze.  To serve:
thaw to slush consistency, add sherbet and 7 Up and whip. Randy Rigg
