Title: Tangy Citrus Dresslng
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Juice and grated peef of 1
Orange
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg -- well beaten
Juice and grated peel of 1
Lemon
Juice and grated peel of 1
Lime
In a small saucepan, combine all ingredients. Over medium heat, bring
mixture to a boil and boil for 1 minute. Remove from the heat and
cool. Serve with assorted fresh fruit. Yield: about 1-1/2 cups
