Title: Mom's Chocolate Chlp Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips --
12    Ounces
1 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
3/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Sugar
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding mix
3 3/8    Ounces
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars. Add pudding mix, eggs and
vanilla. Combine flour and baking soda: add to creamed mixture and mix
well. Fold in chocolate chips. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased
baking sheets. Bake at 375 deg. for 10-12 minutes or until lightly
browned. Yield: 4 dozen
