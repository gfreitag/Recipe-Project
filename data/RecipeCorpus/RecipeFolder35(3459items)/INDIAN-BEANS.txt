Title: INDIAN BEANS
Categories: Side dish, Amerindian
Yield: 10 servings
3/4 c  Celery, sliced
1 1/2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Cinnamon, ground
6 tb Sugar
3 tb Vinegar
1 lb Pinto beans, dried
3 qt Water
1 lb Tomatoes, canned
1 lg Onion, chopped
8 sl Bacon, cut in 2 inch lengths
* Wash beans, place in 6 qt kettle with 3 qts water and bring to
simmer. Cover; cook for 4 hours or until tender.
* Mix remaining ingredients with beans and cooking water and place in
4 quart casserole; cover and bake in 350 F. oven 2 to 3 hours or
until well done.  Add water as necessary.
