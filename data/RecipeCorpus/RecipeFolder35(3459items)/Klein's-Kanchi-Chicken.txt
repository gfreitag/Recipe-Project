Title: Klein's Kanchi Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Crockpot, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1    Enevlope dry onion soup mix
Garlic salt to taste
6    Chicken breasts
1/4 c  Italian salad dressing
1/4 c  Water
Place chicken in slow cooker. Sprinkle with garlic salt and onion
soup mix. Pour water and salad dressing over chicken.cover and cook
on low or medium for 8 to 10 hours.
