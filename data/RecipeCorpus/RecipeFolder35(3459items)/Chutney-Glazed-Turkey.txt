Title: Chutney-Glazed Turkey
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 tb Dry sherry
1 ts Curry powder
8    (2oz) turkey breast cutlets
Vegetable cooking spray
1/4 c  Mango chutney
Place the turkey breast cutlets on a broiler pan coated with cooking
spray. Combine the chutney, mustard, sherry, and curry powder in a
bowl, and stir well. Brush half of the chutney mixture over the
turkey cutlets and broil for 4 minutes.

Turn the turkey cutlets over and brush with the remaining chutney
mixture; broil an additional 3 minutes or until done.
