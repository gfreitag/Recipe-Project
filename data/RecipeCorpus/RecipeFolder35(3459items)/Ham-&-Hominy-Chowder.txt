Title: Ham & Hominy Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Meats, Potatoes, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn White or yellow hominy; 14oz
1 cn Green chilies; chopped, 4oz
1 tb Flour; all-purpose
1 1/4 ts Dried thyme leaves
1 1/2 ts Salt  (optional)
1/4 ts Black pepper
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 lb Red potatoes; cubed
1 md Onion; chopped
1 md Red or green bell pepper;
.  chopped
1 1/4 c  Cooked ham; cubed
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
In a 3-quart saucepan, combine the oil, potatoes, onion, bell
peppers, ham and celery.  Cook over low heat 10 minutes stirring
occasionally.  Add the hominy, chilies, flour, thyme, salt and
pepper.  Gradually stir in the milk, and bring to a boil.  Reduce the
heat, simmer, covered, for 30 to 40 minutes or until soup is
thickened and potatoes are tender.
