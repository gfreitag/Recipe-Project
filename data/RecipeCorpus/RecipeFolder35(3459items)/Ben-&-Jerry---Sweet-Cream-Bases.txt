Title: Ben & Jerry - Sweet Cream Bases
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 versions
MMMMM---------------------SWEET CREAM BASE 1--------------------------
2 c  Heavy OR whipping cream
1 c  Milk
2 lg Eggs
3/4 c  Sugar
MMMMM---------------------SWEET CREAM BASE 2--------------------------
2/3 c  Half and half
2 c  Heavy Or whipping cream
3/4 c  Sugar
MMMMM---------------------SWEET CREAM BASE 3--------------------------
Cold
2 c  Light cream
1 c  SWEETENED condensed milk
Base 1 has creamy texture, medium body and subtle taste.  Good as a
background for fruit, cookies, and candy

Whisk the eggs in a mixing bowl until light and fluffy, 1 to 2
minutes. Whisk in the sugar a little at a time, then whisk until
completely blended about one minute more.  Pour in the cream.

Base 2 made with a minimum of ingredients and requires no cooking.  It
makes a creamy ice cream with 25 percent butterfat.  Does not store
well in home freezers.

Pour the cream into mixing bowl.  Whisk in the sugar a little at a
time until completely blended, about one minute.   Pour in the half
and half and blend completely.

Base 3 Makes a less creamy less rich ice cream.

Whisk the light cream and the SWEETENED condensed milk together in a
mixing bowl until blended.

All these bases make one quart for each recipe.
