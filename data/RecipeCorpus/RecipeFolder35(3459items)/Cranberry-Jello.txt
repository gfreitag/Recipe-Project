Title: Cranberry Jello
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Sugar
2 sm Box of cherry Jello
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 lb Raw cranberries
2    Apples
2    Oranges
Grind together raw cranberries, apples and oranges.  Add sugar and
let set overnight in refrigerator.  Next day strain juice off ground
fruit, cook juice and dissolve Jello.  Add nuts and chill till firm.
