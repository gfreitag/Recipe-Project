Title: Vegetable Mafe
Categories: African, Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 sm Cabbage, chopped
2 lg Tomatoes, chopped
1 bn Chard
2 ea Chili peppers
2 c  Tomato sauce
3/4 c  Peanut butter
2 lg Onions, chopped
4 tb Peanut oil
2 c  Squash
4 ea Turnips
4 md Potatoes, quartered
2 lg Carrots, chopped
Brown the onions in a moderately hot oil in a large skillet.  Add
vegetables one at a time sauteeing each one for a minute or two before
adding the next.  Stir in tomato sauce & about a cup of water.
Reduce heat & simmer till all the vegetables are tender.  Spoon out
half a cup of the broth & mix it with the peanut butter to make a
smooth paste.  Return to the pot & simmer for 15 minutes.  Serve over
rice.
