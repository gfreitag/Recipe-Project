Title: Oregon's Hazelnut Chocolate Chip Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 36 servings
2 1/3 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips --
6    Oz
3/4 c  Hazelnuts -- chopped
1 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Packed brown sugar
2 ea Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars on med. speed for 3
min. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add
vanilla. Combine flour, baking soda and salt; gradually add to
batter. Fold in chocolate chips and nuts. Drop by heaping
tablespoonfuls 3 in. apart onto lightly greased baking sheets.
Flatten lightly with a fork. Bake at 350 deg. for 10 to 12 min. or
until light brown.Remove to a wire rack to cool. Yield: 3 doz.
