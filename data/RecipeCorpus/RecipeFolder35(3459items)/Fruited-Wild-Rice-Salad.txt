Title: Fruited Wild Rice Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
1 c  Golden raisins
1 c  Seedless grapes -- red
Halved
2 tb Fresh mint -- minced
2 tb Parsley -- minced
1 ts Chives
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 c  Pecan halves
Dressing:
1/3 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Olive oil -- or vegetable
Oil
2 tb Honey
Salad
1 c  Wild rice -- uncooked
2 ea Golden delicious apples --
Chopped
1 ea Lemon -- juice from one
Combine dressing ingredients; set aside. Cook rice according to
package

directions; drain if needed and allow to cool. In a large bowl, toss
apples with lemon juice. Add raisins, grapes, mint, parsley, chives
and rice. Add dressing and toss. Season with salt and pepper. Cover
and chill several hours or overnight. Just before serving, add pecan
and toss lightly. Yield: 8-10 servings.
