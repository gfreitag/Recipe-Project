Title: Red Cedar Inn French Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Route 66, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
5 ts Paprika
3 3/4 c  Corn Oil
1 cn Tomato Soup (51 Oz)
3 3/4 c  Sugar
5 c  White Vinegar
5 ts Salt
Blend 1st six ingredients well, then add corn oil very slowly and mix
well again. Makes 1 gallon.
