Title: Washington State Apple Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb All-purpose flour
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1/8 ts Ground ginger
1/8 ts Salt
Pastry for double-crust pie
(9 inches)
6 c  Baking apples
Sliced peeled(5 to 6
Medium)
2 tb Water
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
In a saucepan, combine apples, water and lemon juice; cook over
medium-low heat jusl until the apples are tender. Remove from the
heat and cool (do not drain). In a large bowl, combine sugars, flour,
cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger and salt; add apples and toss to coat. Place
bottom pastry in pie plate; add apple mixture. Cover with top pastry;
seal and flute edges. Cut slits in top crust. Bake at 450 deg. for 10
minutes. Reduce heat to 350 deg.; bake 35-45 minutes longer or until
golden brown. Yield: 6-8 servings
