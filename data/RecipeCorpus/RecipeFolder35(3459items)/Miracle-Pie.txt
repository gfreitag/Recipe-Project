Title: Miracle Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Vitamin D milk
1 c  Coconut
4    Eggs
1    Stick of butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
Blend all these ingredients in your blender and pour into a greased
pie pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  You don't need a pie crust
as this recipe cooks its own. Randy Rigg
