Title: Shrimp kebabs
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Bbq/grill
Yield: 2 servings
-thick and blanched
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Teriyaki sauce
1 tb Lime juice
2 tb Chopped fresh ginger
1 x  Salt & pepper to taste
20 lg Shrimp, shelled, deveined
-and cleaned
3 ea Green onions, cut into
-2-inch lengths
1 c  Blanched snow pea pods
2 ea Onions, cut into wedges
2 lg Carrots, pared, sliced 1/2"
Alternate shrimp and vegetable on skewers. Mix remaining
ingredients together in a bowl and brush over kebabs. Place skewers
in oven about six inches from top element. Broil 3 to 4 minutes on
each side. If desired, they can be cooked on grill in the same amount
of time. Be sure to baste several times during cooking. From Morning
Star electronic magazine - July 1994
