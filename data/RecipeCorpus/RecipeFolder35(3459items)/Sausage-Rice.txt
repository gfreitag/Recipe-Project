Title: Sausage Rice
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Pasta/rice
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Cream of celery soup, not
Diluted
3/8 oz Package of dry chicken
Noodle soup
1 1/2 c  Water
2 sm Sweet pickles, chopped
1 lb Hot sausage
3 c  Cooked rice
1/4 c  Chopped nuts
1    Onion, chopped
3    Stalks celery, chopped
4 oz Can of chopped mushrooms
Brown sausage, stir in remaining ingredients and simmer until
vegetables are tender.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
