Title: Candied Sweet Potato (Mw)
Categories: Holiday, Potatoes, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Butter or Margarine -- cold
1    To 1.5 lbs Sweet Potatoes
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
Peel and slice the sweet potatoes. Place in casserole dish.

Coarsely chop butter and sprinkle pieces over sliced potatoes.

Sprinkle brown sugar on top of potatoes.

Cover dish, and bake in 350F (170 C) oven for approximately 40 mins or
until sweet potatoes are soft.

Serve as a side dish with either fowl or beef.

Note: You can cut the cooking time of this dish by pre-cooking the
whole sweet potatoes (I microwave on high for 10 mins).
