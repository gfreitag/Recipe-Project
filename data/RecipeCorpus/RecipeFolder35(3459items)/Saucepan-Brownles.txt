Title: Saucepan Brownles
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk --
14    Ounces
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
1 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips --
5    Ounces
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
2 c  Biscuit mix
In a large saucepan, melt chocolate chips and butter over low heat.
Stir until well blended; remove from the heat. Add biscuit mix, milk
and egg; stir well until all lumps are gone. Add nuts. Pour into a
greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 25
minutes. Yield: 2-1/2 dozen
