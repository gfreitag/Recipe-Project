Title: Cherry Whip
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Eagle Brand milk
1 lg Carton of Cool Whip
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 cn Cherry pie filling
1 md Size can of pineapple
Chunks, well drained
In a large serving bowl, mix cherries and pineapple, add Eagle Brand
milk and stir well.  Fold in Cool Whip, add nuts while mixing well.
Let set in the refrigerator overnight or make early in the day.
Garnish with cherries.
