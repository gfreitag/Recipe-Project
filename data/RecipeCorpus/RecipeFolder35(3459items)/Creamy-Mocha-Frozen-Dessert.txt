Title: Creamy Mocha Frozen Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 24 servings
2 pk Cream cheese -- 8 oz
Softened
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk --
14    Oz
1/2 c  Chocolate syrup
1 ct Whipped cream -- topping,
Thawed
2 ts Instant coffee -- granules
1 tb Hot water
1 c  Cookie crumbs
Cream-filled chocolate
3/4 c  Pecans -- chopped divided
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
In a small bowl, dissolve coffee granules in hot water; set aside. In
another bowl, combine cookie crumbs, 1/2 cup pecans and butter. Pat
into the bottom of a 13x9x2 inch baking pan. In a mixing bowl, beat
cream cheese until light and fluffy. Blend in coffee mixture, milk
and chocolate syrup. Fold in whipped topping and spread over crust.
Sprinkle the remaining pecans on top. Freeze. Yield: 24 servings.
