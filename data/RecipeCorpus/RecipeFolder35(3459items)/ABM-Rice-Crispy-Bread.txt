Title: ABM Rice Crispy Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads, Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 1/2lb loaf
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Yeast
9 oz Water
1 tb Veg oil
1 3/4 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Ground Rice Crispies
It takes about 2 cups of Rice Crispies to make 1 cup of ground rice
in the blender.

Add liquid ingredients, then dry ingredients, then yeast. Set machine
for regular/basic and medium crust. Watch the first mixing and add
more flour if necessary for the dough to form a ball that lifts from
the tub.
