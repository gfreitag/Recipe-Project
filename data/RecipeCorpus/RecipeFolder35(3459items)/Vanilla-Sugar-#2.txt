Title: Vanilla Sugar #2
Categories: Herbs/spice, Condiments
Yield: 4 cups
1    Vanilla bean
4 c  Sugar
Split vanilla bean lengthwise and chop coarsely.  Place with sugar in
a food processor and process until the beans are well chopped.  Store
in an airtight container and use as needed, putting the sugar through
a sieve or sifter first.

For dusting on cakes or rolling freshly-baked cookies in.

Source:  Old Farmer's Almanac, 1996
