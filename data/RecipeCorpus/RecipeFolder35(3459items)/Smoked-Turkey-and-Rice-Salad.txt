Title: Smoked Turkey and Rice Salad
Categories: Salads, Poultry, Pasta/rice, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
Makes 6 servings
MMMMM-------------------PREP TIME: 10 MINUTES------------------------
MMMMM-------------------COOK TIME: 20 MINUTES------------------------
2 ea Thick slices smoked deli
-turkey breast, cut into
-strips
1 c  Frozen peas, thawed
1 sm Tomato, chopped
1 sm Sweet yellow pepper,
-chopped and seeded
4 ea Green onions
1 c  Rice, cooked and cooled
1/2 c  Low fat yogurt
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 1/2 ts Dried tarragon
1 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Sugar
Salt to taste
In small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, yogurt, tarragon, pepper
salt, mustard and sugar.  In salad bowl, toss together the rice,
turkey, peas, tomato, peppers and onions.  Drizzle dressing over top,
and toss gently to coat.  Chill until thoroughly cool. Origin: Uncle
Ben's pamphlet on rice Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/95.
