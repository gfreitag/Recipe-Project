Title: Cheery Cherry Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 48 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Maraschino cherries --
Drained & chopped
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
1/2 c  Coconut flakes
1 c  Packed brown sugar
3/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1 ea Egg
2 tb Milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  All-purpose flour
In a large mixing bowl, cream brown sugar, butter, egg, milk, and
vanilla. Combine flour, salt and baking soda; gradually add to
creamed mixture. Fold in cherries, pecans and coconut. Drop by
teaspoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake at 375 deg. for 10-12
min. or until golden brown. Yield: 4 dozen.
