Title: Brownle Mix-Brownies
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------BROWNLE MIX:-----------------------------
8 c  Sugar
1 cn Baking cocoa -- 8 ounces
2 c  Vegetable shortening
6 c  All-purpose flour
4 ts Baking powder
4 ts Salt
MMMMM---------------------T0 MAKE BROWN1ES:--------------------------
1/2 c  Chopped nuts -- optional
Confectioners' sugar,
Optional
2 1/2 c  Brownie mix
2 ea Eggs -- beaten
1 ts Vanilla extract
BROWNIE MIX: In a large bowl, combine first five ingredients. With a
pastry blender, cut in shortening until evenly distributed. Store in
a large airtight container in cupboard. Keeps 10-12 weeks. Yield:
about 17 cups of mix. T0 MAKE BROWN1ES: In a mixing bowl, combine
first four ingredients. Beat with spoon until smooth. Spread into a
greased 8-in. square baking pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 20-25 minutes
or until top tests done. Cut into squares while still warm. Dust with
confectioners' sugar if desired. Yield: 9 brownies
