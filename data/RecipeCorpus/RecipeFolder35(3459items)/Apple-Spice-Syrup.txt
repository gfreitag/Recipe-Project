Title: Apple Spice Syrup
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
1 3/4 c  Apple juice -- or cider
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1/4 ts Ground allspice
In a saucepan, combine brown sugar, cornstarch, allspice and nutmeg;
mix well. Add juice or cider. Cook and stir over medium heat until
syrup is bubbly and slightly  thickened. Yield: 1-3/4 cups
