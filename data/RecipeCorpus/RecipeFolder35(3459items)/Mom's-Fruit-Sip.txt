Title: Mom's Fruit Sip
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Dried chopped dates
1/2 c  Raisins
2 ts Vanilla extract
1    Bottle apple-cherry juice
.  48oz
3    3-inch cinnamon sticks
1/2 c  Dried apples
In a large saucepan, combine all the ingredients.  Bring to a simmer
and remove from the heat.  Allow to cool.  Keep in the refrigerator
overnight. Drain the liquid through a strainer or double layer of
cheesecloth into a bowl.  Pour the liquid into a glass bottle.  Cap
the bottle tightly. Store in the refrigerator.  May be served hot or
cold.

Makes about six 8oz servings.

Source:  A pamphlet Mom received in the mail.
