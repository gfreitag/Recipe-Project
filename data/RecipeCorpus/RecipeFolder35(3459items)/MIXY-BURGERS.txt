Title: MIXY-BURGERS
Categories: Sandwich, Meats
Yield: 5 servings
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Worchestershire sauce
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ts Salt
5 ea Hamburger buns, toasted
Split into halves
1 tb Shortening
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper, chopped
1/4 c  Celery, chopped
1 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Cheddar cheese, diced
8 oz Tomato sauce
Melt shortening in top of chafing dish or heavy skillet directly over
heat. Add onion, green pepper, and celery.  Cook till lightly
browned. Add beef, stirring with fork while beef browns. Add
remaining ingredients except buns.  Simmer 10 minutes, stirring
occasionally. To serve, spoon on warm buns.  Eat with fork
