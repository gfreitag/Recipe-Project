Title: Sopaillas
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Cooking oil for deep-fat
Frying
Honey, optional
Confectioners' sugar,
Optional
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Shortening
1/3 c  Warm water
In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt. Cut in shortening
unlil mixture resembles fine crumbs. Gradually add water, stirring
with a fork. The dough will be crumbly. On a lightly floured surface,
knead the dough for 3 minutes or until smooth. Cover and let rest for
10 minutes. Roll out into a l2-in. x 10-in, rectangle. Cut into 12
squares. In a deep-fat fryer, heat 2 in. of oil to 375 deg.. Fry
sopaipillas for 1-2 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels; keep hot
in a warm oven. Serve with honey or dust with confectioners' sugar if
desired. Yield: 6-8 servings
