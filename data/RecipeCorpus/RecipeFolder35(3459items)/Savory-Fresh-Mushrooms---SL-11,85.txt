Title: Savory Fresh Mushrooms - SL 11/85
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Seasoned salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Chopped fresh parsley
1 lb Fresh mushrooms; sliced
2 tb Dry sherry
1 ts Margarine
1/4 ts Paprika
Combine all ingredients in a large skillet.  Cover and cook over
medium heat 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Yield: 4 servings.
(about 43 calories per serving).
