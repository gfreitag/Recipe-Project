Title: Quick Garam Masala
Categories: Herbs/spice, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 tablespoon
1/4 ts Black peppercorns
1/2 ts Kosher salt
1    Cardamon pod
1 ts Cumin seeds
1 ts Coriander seeds
1/4 ts Sesame seeds
Place the cumin seeds, coriander seeds, sesame seeds, black
peppercorns, salt and cardamon pod in a dry skillet and toast lightly
over medium heat until lightly browned and very fragrant, about 2
minutes. Grind the mixture in a spice mill or pulverize with a mortar
and pestle.
