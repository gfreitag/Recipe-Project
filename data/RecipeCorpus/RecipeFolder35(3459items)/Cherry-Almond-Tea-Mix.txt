Title: Cherry-Almond Tea Mix
Categories: Beverages, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
-kool aid
2 ts Almond extract
2 1/4 c  Artificially sweetened
-instant tea
2 pk Unsweetened cherry-flavored
Place all ingredients in a food processor; process until well blended.

Store in an airtight container.  Give with serving instructions.
Yield about 1 - 1/2 cups tea mix.  To serve: Stir 2 teaspoons mix
into 8 ounces hot or cold water. Source: Leisure Arts - Quick and
Tasty Christmas Gifts.
