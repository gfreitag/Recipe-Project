Title: Turtle Sundae Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Pecans -- chopped
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips --
6    Ounces
Vanilla ice cream and pecan
Halves, option
1 pk German chocolate cake mix --
L8-1/4 ounces
1 pk Caramels -- 14 ounces
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
6 tb Butter or margarine
Mix cake according to package directions. Set aside half of the
batter; pour remaining batter into a greased and floured 13-in. x
9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 18 minutes. Meanwhile,
in a saucepan over low heat, melt the caramels, milk and butter.
Remove from the heat and add nuts. Pour over cake. Sprinkle with
chocolate chips. Pour reserved batter over top. Bake 20-25 minutes
more or until cake springs back when lightly touched. Cool, Cut into
squares. If desired, top each with a scoop of ice cream and a pecan
half. Yield: 20 servings
