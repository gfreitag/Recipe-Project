Title: Peanut Butter Brownle Cups
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
18 ea Miniature peanut butter
Cups
1 pk Fudge brownie mix -- 21-1/2
Ounces
Mix brownie batter according to package directions. Fill paper-lined
or foil-lined muffin cups two-thirds full. Remove wrappers from
peanut butter cups; set one in each muffin cup and press down until
batter meets the top edge of the candy. Bake at 350 deg. for 20-25
minutes. Yield: about 1-1/2 dozen. Editor's Note: The Test Kitchen
staff found Betty Crocker's Fudge Brownie mix bakes best for this
recipe
