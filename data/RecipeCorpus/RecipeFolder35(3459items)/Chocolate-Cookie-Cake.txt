Title: Chocolate Cookie Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
3    Eggs
1 c  Chocolate sandwich cookies
(about 10) -- crushed
1 cn Vanilla frosting
1 pk Chocolate cake mix
1 1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
Heat oven to 350.  Generously grease and flour 2 9" pans.  Beat cake
mix, water, margarine and eggs in large bowl on low speed 30 seconds.
Beat on medium speed 2 minutes.  Reserve 2 tbsp of the crushed
cookies.  Fold remaining crushed cookies into batter.  Pour into
pans.   Bake 30 to 37 minutes.  Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans.
Cool cake completely.  Crush reserved cookies finely; mix into
frosting.  Fill layers and frost side and top of cake with frosting.
If, desired, garnish with additional chocolate cookies cut in half.
