Title: Soynuts
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Seasonings
1 ea Soaked soybeans
Spread soaked soybeans on an oiled cookie sheet and roast in 350
degree oven for about 30 mins, stirring occasionally, until they are
golden brown.

Remove from oven and season to taste with a sprinkle of plain or
garlic salt; for a spicier version, add some cayenne pepper, or Cajun
seasoning mix.

Cool completely and store in an airtight container.
