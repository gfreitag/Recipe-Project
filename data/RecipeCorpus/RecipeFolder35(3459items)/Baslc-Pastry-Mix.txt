Title: Baslc Pastry Mix
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/3 c  Vegetable shortening
6 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Salt
In a mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. cut in half of the
shortening until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Cut in
remaining shortening. Place in an airtight container and store in the
refrigerator for up to 6 weeks. Yield: 8 cups
