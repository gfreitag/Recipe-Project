Title: Bonnie's Blueberry Mufflns
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Fresh blueberries -- or
Frozen
Additional butter or
Margarine, melted
Additional sugar
2 c  All-purpose flour
2/3 c  Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Milk
1/3 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. In
another bowl, beat eggs. Blend in milk, butter, nutmeg and vanilla;
pour into dry ingredients and mix just until moistened. Fold in
blueberries. Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full.
Bake at 375 deg. for 20-25 minutes. Brush tops with melted butter and
sprinlcle with sugar (*lf using frozen blueberries, rinse and pat dry
before adding to batter.) Yield: 1 dozen.
