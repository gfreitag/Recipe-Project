Title: Fish Flllets In Garllc Butter
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Whitefish or cod
1/4 c  Green onion -- thinly
Sliced
Lemon wedges
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 sm Garlic cloves -- minced
4 ea Salmon fish flllets
About 6 ounces salmon (or
In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Saute garlic 7 minute.
Place fish over garlic, cover and cook over low heat 3 minutes.
Carefully turn fish; sprinkle with onions. Cover and continue to cook
until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 2-3 minutes. Squeeze
lemon over fish. Serve immediately. Yield: 4 servings
