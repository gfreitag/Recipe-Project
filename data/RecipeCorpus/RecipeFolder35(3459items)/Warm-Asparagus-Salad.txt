Title: Warm Asparagus Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Fresh parsley -- chopped
Salt to taste
1 md Tomato -- chopped
1 qt Mixed greens -- torn
2 c  Asparagus pieces
4 ea Slices bacon
1 sm Red onion
Thinly sliced into rings and
Separated
2 tb Vinegar
Cook the asparagus until crisp-tender. Drain. In a skillet, cook bacon
until crisp. Remove to paper towel. In drippings, saute onion until
soft; stir in vinegar. sugar, parsley and salt. Stir in asparagus,
crumbled bacon and tomato. Pour mixture over mixed greens and toss.
Serve immediately. Yield: 4 servings.

Microwave: In 1-qt. dish, microwave asparagus on high 3-5 minutes or
until crisp-tender. Drain. Microwave bacon on high until crisp, about
3-5 minutes. Remove bacon. Microwave onion in bacon drippings 2-3
minutes or until soft. Stir in vinegar, sugar, parsley and salt. Stir
in asparagus, crumbled bacon and tomato. Toss with mixed greens in
salad bowl
