Title: Pecan Sandies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  All-purpose flour
2 c  Chopped pecans
Additional confectioners'
Sugar
2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1 c  Confectioners' sugar
2 tb Water
4 ts Vanilla extract
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add water and vaniila; mix
well. Gradually add flour; fold in pecans. Roll dough in to 1-in.
balls. Place on ungreased baking sheets and flatten with fingers.
Bake at 300 deg. for 20-25 minutes. Cool on a wire rack. When cool,
dust with confectioners' sugar. Yield: about 5 dozen
