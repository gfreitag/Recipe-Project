Title: Taco Tartlets
Categories: Appetizers, Country wom, Pie/tart
Yield: 32 servings
MMMMM------------------------MEAT SHELLS:-----------------------------
2 tb Ice water
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean
2 tb Taco seasoning mix
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING:-------------------------------
1 c  Tortilla chips -- crushed
Divided
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1 c  Sour cream
2 tb Taco sauce -- reduced
Calorie
2 oz Olive -- ripe chopped
To make meat shells, combine ingredients; mix well. Press meat
mixture into bottom and sides of tiny tart pans and set aside.
Combine sour cream, taco sauce, olives and 3/4 cup tortilla chips.
Spoon filling into each shell, mounding slightly. Combine remaining
chips and cheese; sprinkle over each tartlet. Bake at 375 deg. for 10
min. Garnish with taco sauce. Yield 32 tartlets.
