Title: Halloween Burgers for Kids
Categories: Kids, Sandwich, Meats, Holiday
Yield: 6 children
-- cut into desired shapes
Pitted olives;
6    Lettuce leaves;
6    Hamburger buns; split
1 1/2 lb Lean ground meat;
Salt & pepper TO TASTE;
6 sl Cheese; cut into desired
-- shapes
Red/green bell peppers;
Light shape ground beef into 1-1/2" patties. Do not over-handle.
Choose a cooking method.

TO BROIL: Place patties on a rack in broiler pan so surface of meat
is 3 to 4" from heat.  Broil 10 minutes, or until centers are no
longer pink, turning once.

TO PAN BROIL: Place patties in a preheated heavy skillet over
medium-low heat.  Cook 7 - 8 minutes, or until centers are no longer
pink, turning once.

Season with salt and pepper after turning.  Top with cheese slices,
bell peppers and olives to create jack-o-lanterns or ghosts during
the few minutes of cooking.  Serve on lettuce-lined buns.

The picture showed a hamburgers with cheese cut into pumpkin and
ghosts shapes. The bell pepper cut into shapes for eyes and mouths
for pumpkin. Olives were used as eyes for ghosts.
