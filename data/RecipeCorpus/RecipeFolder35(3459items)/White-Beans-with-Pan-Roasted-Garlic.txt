Title: White Beans with Pan-Roasted Garlic
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Butter
Salt and pepper
Roasted peppers
-and roasted shallots
-for garnish
1 tb Olive oil
5    Garlic clove(s),
-lightly crushed
2 1/2 c  Cooked white beans, warmed
-homemade or canned
1 tb Heavy cream
1. Place a cast-iron skillet over high heat for 1 minute. Then
transfer the skillet to the lowest heat possible. Add the olive oil
and then the garlic. SautÂ‚ the garlic VERY slowly, stirring often,
until golden brown, 20-30 minutes. Remove the garlic from the pan and
mash it with a fork.

2. Mash one-third of the beans (you can mash some, all, or none as
desired). Add the cream and butter, then stir in the garlic. Season
with salt and pepper. Gently reheat.

3. Garnish with roasted peppers and roasted shallots and serve.
