Title: Hot Spiced Tea - SL 10/83
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 10 cups
1/2 c  Sugar
3 c  Orange juice
3 c  Unsweetened pineapple juice
Orange slice quarters
-(optional)
4 ea 3-inch cinnamon sticks;
-halved
1 ts Whole cloves
1/2 ts Allspice
1 qt ;Hot tea
Combine first 3 ingredients in a tea ball or cheesecloth bag; set
aside.
Combine hot tea and sugar in a large Dutch oven; stir in juice. Add
spice mixture and bring to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 30
minutes.  Garnish each serving with fresh orange, if desired. Serve
hot. Yield: 10 cups.

From Joyce Dean Garrison of North Carolina in Otober, 1983 "Southern
