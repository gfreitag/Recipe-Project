Title: Spiced Carrot Strips
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
5 lg Carrots -- julienned
2 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
Place carrots in a saucepan; cover with water. Cook until tender,
about 8-10 minutes. Drain, Combine butter, sugar, salt and cinnamon;
pour over carrots and toss to coat. Serve immediately. Yield: 4-6
servings
