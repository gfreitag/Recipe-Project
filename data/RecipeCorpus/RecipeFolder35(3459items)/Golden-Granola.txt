Title: Golden Granola
Categories: Breakfast, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1/3 c  Honey
1/3 c  Water
1 tb Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Golden raisins
Or chopped dried apricots
4 c  Old-fashioned oats
1 c  Flaked coconut
1/2 c  Wheat germ
1/2 c  Sesame seeds
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds
1/2 c  Slivered almonds
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
In a large bowl, combine the first eight ingredients; mix well. In a
saucepan, cook oil, brown sugar, honey, water and vanilla until sugar
is dissolved. Pour over dry ingredients and mix well. Spoon into a
greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Bake at 275 deg. for 1
hour or until golden, stirring every 15 minutes. Cool completely.
Stir in raisins or apricots. Yield: about 9 cups
