Title: Nutty Burgers
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 x  Parsley, to taste
2 x  Eggs
1 ts Salt
1 pn Sage
1 x  Bread crumbs
1 c  Nuts, ground fine
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds, ground fine
1/2 c  Grated carrots
1 x  Celery stalk, ground
1 x  Small onion, ground
1/2 x  Green pepper, ground
Put nuts, seeds, carrots,celery, onion, pepper and parsley in a food
grinder or blender with enough liquid(a tomato or 1/2 c broth) and
grind; then add egg, salt, and sage. Blend. If they are too dry, add
more broth or tomato pulp. If too moist, add bread crumbs. Shape into
patties and broil until browned on each side. Stocking Up,Rodale
