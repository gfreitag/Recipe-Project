Title: Bebe Nunn's Chicken And Rice Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Route 66
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Water
1 tb Dry Onion Soup Mix
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
1    Frying Chicken, Skinned --
-cut in pieces
1 c  Uncooked Rice
1 cn Mushroom Soup (10 1/2 Oz)
Spray an 8 x 11 baking dish with nonstick spray. Add uncooked rice and
place chicken pieces over rice. Salt and pepper to taste. Combine
soup, water and onion soup mix and pour over chicken and rice. Bake
uncovered in preheated 350 F oven until done, about 1 hour. Casserole
should be nicely browned on top.
