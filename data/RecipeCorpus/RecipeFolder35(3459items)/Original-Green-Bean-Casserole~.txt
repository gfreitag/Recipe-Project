Title: Original Green Bean Casserole~
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
-condensed
1/8 ts Ground black pepper
1 1/2 c  French fried onions
2    9 oz pkgs Frozen cut green
-beans -- thawed/drained
3/4 c  Milk
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup,
Combine all ingredients except 1/2 cup french fried onions; pour into
a 1 1/2-quart casserole. Bake, uncovered, at 350~ for 30 minutes or
until heated through. Top with reserved onions; bake, uncovered 5
minutes or until onions are golden. Makes 6 servings.

Per serving:  Calories 168; fat 11.9 g (64.2%); cholesterol 5 mg;
carbohydrate 12.8 g; fiber 0.7 g; protein 2.2 g; sodium 547 mg;
potassium 119 mg; calcium 59 mg.
