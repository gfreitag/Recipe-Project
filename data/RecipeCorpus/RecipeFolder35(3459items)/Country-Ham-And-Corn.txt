Title: Country Ham And Corn
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
11 oz Can Green Giant Mexicorn
1 pk Corn muffin mix (7 oz)
4 oz Chredded cheddar cheese; (1
Cup)
2 c  Cubed booked ham
1/2 c  Chopped green onions
1/2 c  Margarine or butter; melted
3    Eggs; well beaten
15 oz Can cream style corn
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 2 qt baking dish.  In large bowl,
combine all ingredients except cheese.  Pour into greased baking
dish. Bake at 350 degrees fo 45 -55 minutes or until golden brown and
set. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake an additional 1-2 minutes to melt
cheese. Let stand five minutes - before serving -- cut into squares.

For less fat, use fat free cheese, low fat margarine and egg beaters.
