Title: Apple Cobbler
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Vanilla extract
4 tb Milk
8 c  Baking apples --
Thinly/sliced/peeled
2 tb Quick-cooking tapioca
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
3 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Sugar -- divided
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
2 ea Eggs
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING:-------------------------------
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1 tb Milk
3/4 ts Sugar
In a bowl, combine flour, 1/4 cup sugar, baking powder and salt. Cut
in butter until crumbly. In another bowl, lightly beat eggs and
vanilla; add to crumb mixture. With a fork, gently mix in milk to
moisten. Stir until dough forms a ball. Press half of the dough into
the bottom of a greased 13-in. x 9-in, x 2-in, baking pan. Chill the
remaining dough. toss apples with tapioca, cinnamon and remaining
sugar; place over dough in pan. On a lightly floured surface, roll
chilled dough to fit top of pan. Place over apples. Brush with milk.
Combine sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle on top. Bake at 350 deg. for
45-50 minutes or until apples are tender and crust is golden.Yield:
12-16 servings
