Title: Callfornla Cheese Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Vegetables -- 16 ounces
2 cn Condensed cream of chicken
Soup
10 3/4 oz Each undiluted
1 lb Process cheese -- cut in
Cubes
1 qt Water
2 ea Chicken bouillon cubes
1 c  Celery -- diced
1/2 c  Onion -- diced
2 1/2 c  Potatoes -- diced peeled
1 c  Carrots -- diced
1 pk Frozen Callfornia Blend
Bring water to a boil in a large kettle; add next six ingredients.
Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until all vegetables are tender,
about 30 minutes. Stir in soup and cheese; cook until soup is heated
through and the cheese is melted. Yield: 10-12 servings (3 qts.)
