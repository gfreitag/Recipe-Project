Title: 7 Up Christmas Bowl
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 3 servings
Concentrate, thawed
46 oz Can of pineapple juice
2    32 oz. bottles of chilled
7 Up
2 pk 10 oz. frozen red
Raspberries in syrup,
Partially thawed
1 cn 12 oz. frozen lemonade
Place raspberries and lemonade in blender, process till smooth.
Strain into punch bowl.  Discard seeds.  Stir in pineapple juice.
Just before serving, add 7 Up. Randy Rigg
