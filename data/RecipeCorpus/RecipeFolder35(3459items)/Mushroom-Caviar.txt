Title: Mushroom Caviar
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 2 servings
Pepper
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
2 tb Dill, chopped
1/3 c  Sour cream
Tomato slices
1/2 c  Green onions, chopped w\tops
2 tb Butter
1 c  Mushroom, chopped
1 tb Lemon Juice
Salt
Saute the green onions in butter for 1 minute.  Add mushrooms, lemon
juice, salt, pepper and cayenne.  Saute 4 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Remove from heat, stir in dill and sour cream.  Serve
garnished with tomato slices.
