Title: Instant Stock
Categories: Soup/stew, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 quart
1 ts Soy sauce
4 c  Warm water
2 tb Fish sauce
Combine all the ingredients in a large pan or bowl and whisk to mix.

Note: To make instant vegetarian stock, replace the fish sauce with
miso. High-Flavor, Low-Fat Cooking by Steven Raichlen ISBN
