Title: Thyme-Lime Chlcken
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 ea Chicken breast halves
4 ea Chicken thighs
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 tb Olivs oil
2 tb Fresh lime juice
1 tb Minced fresh thyme
Or 1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 ts Grated lime peel
In a small saucepan over low heat, melt butter in olive oil; add lime
juice, thyme, lime peel, garlic, salt and pepper. Place chicken on a
greased broiler pan. Brush with sauce. Broil, basting frequently, 6-7
in. from the heat for 15-20 minutes on each side or until the chicken
juices run clear. Yield: 4-6 servings
