Title: Lemon Sherry Pudding
Categories: Puddings
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Sherry
2 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Skim milk
3 tb Liquid sucaryl
2    Egg whites,stiffly beaten
2 tb Flour
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg plus
2    Egg yolks
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.combine flour,cinnamon,and salt in a small
bowl.Combine egg plus yolks,sherry,lemon juice,milk and sucaryl.Add
to dry ingredients,beating well.Fold in stiffly beaten egg
whites.Pour into a lightly buttered 4 cup casserole.Set in a pan of
hot water and bake 55 to 60 minutes.Serve warm or chilled.

Per serving: calories 109 protein 7g fat 4g carbohydrate 7.5g

(with sugar  271 calories)
