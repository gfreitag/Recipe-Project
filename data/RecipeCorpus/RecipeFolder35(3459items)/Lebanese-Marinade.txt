Title: Lebanese Marinade
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Middle east, Herbs/spice, Hot/spicy
Yield: 3 servings
2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Cayenne
4 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Oil
Mix together with blender or processor.  Make about 2/3 cup, enough
for 5 chicken breast halves, or 10 thigh/drumsticks. Origin: Appeal
Magazine, Fall 1995.  Booklet by Overwaitea Foods. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Oct/95.
