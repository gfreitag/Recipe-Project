Title: Delicious Potato Doughnuts
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
7 c  All-purpose flour
Cooking oil
FAST FUDGE FROSTING:
4 c  Confectioners' sugar -- l
Pound
1/2 c  Baking cocoa
1/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Boiling water
1/3 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Hot mashed potatoes --
Mashed w/milk & butt
2 1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  Buttermilk
2 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
2 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1/2 ts Salt
In a large bowl, combine potatoes, sugar, buttermilk and eggs. Stir in
butter, baking soda, baking powder, nutmeg, salt and enough of the
flour to form a soft dough. Turn onto a lightly floured surface; pat
out to 3/4-in. thickness. Cut with a 2-1/2-in. floured doughnut
cutter. In an electric skillet, heat 1 in. of oil to 350 deg.. Fry
the doughnuts for 2 minutes per side or until browned. Place on paper
towels. For frosting, sift sugar, cocoa and salt into a large bowl.
Stir in water, butter and vanilla. Dip tops of warm doughnuts in
frosting. Yield: 4 dozen
