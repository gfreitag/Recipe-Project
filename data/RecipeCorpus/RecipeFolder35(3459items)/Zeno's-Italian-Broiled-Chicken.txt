Title: Zeno's Italian Broiled Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Route 66
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Sweet Basil
2 ts Chopped Parsley
1 tb Garlic -- freshly minced
1/2    Chicken* -- cut up
1    Stick Butter
1/4 c  Lemon Juice
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
* 1 breast, 1 thigh, 1 leg, 1 wing

Place chicken, skin side up, in a shallow baking dish. Pour melted
butter and lemon juice over chicken and season with salt and pepper.
Place chicken under broiler and cook approximately 10-12 mins. Watch
carefully to avoid burning. Turn chicken over and season with basil,
parsley and garlic. Continue broiling and basting, turning chicken
several times until done.

Remove chicken to hot platter. Discard most of the liquid in pan, but
pour a little of the seasoning over the chicken. Garnish with lemon
wedges to serve.

Makes 2 to 4 servings.
