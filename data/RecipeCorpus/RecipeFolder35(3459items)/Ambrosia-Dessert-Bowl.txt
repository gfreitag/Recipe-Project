Title: Ambrosia Dessert Bowl
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1    Loaf
(10-314 ounces) thawed and
Cubed (about 4
6 lg Navel oranges -- peeled and
Sectioned
1/4 c  Slivered almonds -- toasted
Frozen pound cake
20 ea Large marshmallows
2 c  Heavy cream -- divided
2 tb Sugar
2 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Almond extract
1 cn Crushed pineapple -- 20
oz Well drain
1 c  Flaked coconut
Place marshmallows and 1/4 cup cream in the top of a double boiler;
heat over boiling water until the marshmallows are melted and mixture
is smooth. Cool completely. Meanwhile, whip the remaining cream until
thick. Add sugar. Fold into marshmallow mixture. Fold in extracts,
pineapple and coconut. Place half of the pound cake cubes in the
bottom of a 2-1/2- to 3-qt. clear glass bowl. Top with half of the
orange sections. Top with half of the cream mixture. Repeat layers.
Sprinkle with almonds. Chill until serving time. Yield: 10-12 servings
