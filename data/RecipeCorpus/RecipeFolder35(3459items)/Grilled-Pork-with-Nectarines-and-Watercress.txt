Title: Grilled Pork with Nectarines and Watercress
Categories: Meats, Salads, Main dish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Pork chops; boneless,
-center-cut
2    Nectarines; pitted,in wedges
1 lb Watercress; coarsely chopped
1/2 c  Orange juice
3 tb Fresh cilantro; or parsley,
-chopped
3 tb Olive oil
1 ts Orange zest; grated
1 cl Garlic; minced
Combine orange juice, cilantro, oil, zest, garlic, salt and pepper.
Place chops in a non-reactive dish and drizzle with 3 tbs of the
dressing. Cover, chill and marinade at least one hour.

Prepare grill and cook chops to medium done. Slice into strips and
toss with nectarines, watercress and dressing. Serve.
