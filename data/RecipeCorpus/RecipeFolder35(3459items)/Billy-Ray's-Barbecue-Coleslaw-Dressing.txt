Title: Billy Ray's Barbecue Coleslaw Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Route 66, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 lb Cabbage -- shredded
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Cider Vinegar
6 tb Salad Oil
Combine sugar with vinegar, oil, and salt until sugar is dissolved.
Add mayonnaise and mix well. Pour over cabbage and stir to combine.
Chill well. Makes 8 to 10 servings.
