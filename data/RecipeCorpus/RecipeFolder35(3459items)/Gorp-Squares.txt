Title: Gorp Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------PREP TIME: 15 MINUTES------------------------
MMMMM-------------------BAKE TIME: 20 MINUTES------------------------
1 c  Flaked coconut
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Chopped apricots
3/4 c  Butter or margarine
3/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
2 tb Corn syrup
3 c  Hillsborough Mills cereal
-(I don't have a clue what
-this even
Might be close to)
1 c  Quick cooking oats
3/4 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
Combine all dry ingredients in a large bowl and set aside.  Cook the
butter, sugar and corn syrup in a small saucepan until boiling,
stirring constantly.  Remove from heat and pour over dry ingredients.
Press into a greased jelly roll pan, and bake at 350F for about 12-15
minutes. Cool slightly and cut into squares while still warm.  Makes
about 30 squares. Origin: What's Cooking, 13th issue, booklet by
Kraft Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/95.
