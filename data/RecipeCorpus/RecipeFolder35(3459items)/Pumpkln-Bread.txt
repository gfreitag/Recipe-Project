Title: Pumpkln Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1/2 c  Raisins -- optional
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Cooked pumpkin -- or canned
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Water
2 ea Eggs
1 2/3 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
In a mixing bowl, combine sugar, pumpkin, oil, water and eggs; beat
well. Combine dry ingredients; gradually add to pumpkin mixture and
mix well. Stir in nuts and raisins if desired. Pour into a greased
9-in. x 5-in. x 3-in. loaf pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 65-70 minutes or
until bread tests done. Cool 10 minutes in pan before removing to a
wire rack. Yield: 1 loaf.
