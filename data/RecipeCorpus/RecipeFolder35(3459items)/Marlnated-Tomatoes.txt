Title: Marlnated Tomatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Sugar
1/8 ts Lemon pepper
3 ea Tomatoes -- thickly sliced
2 tb Fresh basil -- chopped
2 tb Olive oil
Place tomato slices in a shallow serving dish; sprinkle with basil.
In a small bowl, combine remaining ingredients; pour over tomatoes.
Let stand at room temperature for 1 hour before serving. Yield: 6
