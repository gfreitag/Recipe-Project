Title: Qulck Beef Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
10 3/4 oz Undiluted
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup --
10 3/4 oz Undiluted
1/2 ts Dried thyme -- optional
1/4 ts Dried rosemary -- optional
Pepper to taste
2 c  Roast beef
Leftover diced cooked
1 cn Mixed vegetables
16    Ounces, liquid drained and
Reserved
1 cn Cream of celery soup --
In a saucepan, combine beef, vegetables, soups and seasonings. Heat
through. If desired, add the reserved Vegetable liquid to thin the
stew. Yield: 4 servings.
