Title: Oatmeal Waffles
Categories: Breakfast, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
Milk  optional
2 ea Eggs -- beaten
2 c  Buttermilk
1 c  Quick-cooking oats
1 tb Molasses
1 tb Vegetable oil
In a large bowl, mix eggs and buttermilk. Add oats and mix well. Stir
in molasses and oil. Combine flour, salt, baking soda and baking
powder; stir into the egg mixture. If batter becomes too thick, thin
with a little milk. Pour about 3/4 cup batter onto a greased
preheated waffle maker. Bake according to manufacturer's directions.
To make pancakes: Drop batter by 1/4 cupfuls onto a hot greased
griddle. Turn when bubbles begin to form on top of pancake. Yield: 5
waffles (7 inches) or about 15 standard-size pancakes
