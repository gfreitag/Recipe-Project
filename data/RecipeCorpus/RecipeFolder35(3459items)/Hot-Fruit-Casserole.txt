Title: Hot Fruit Casserole
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
Liquid for use in sauce
Sauce:
1    Stick of butter
2 tb Flour, heaping TB
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Reserved fruit juices, mixed
1 cn Sliced pineapple, reserve
Liquid for use in sauce
1 cn Peach halaves, reserve
Liquid for use in sauce
1 cn Pear halves, reserve liquid
For use in sauce
1    Jar apple rings, reserve
Use a shallow long casserole dish.  Arrange fruit in layers, with
apple rings on top.  Cook sauce until thick and smooth.  Pour hot
sauce over fruit.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.  Bake uncovered
at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, or until hot and bubbly. Randy Rigg
