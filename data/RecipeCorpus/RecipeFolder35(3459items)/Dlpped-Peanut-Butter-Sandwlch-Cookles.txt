Title: Dlpped Peanut Butter Sandwlch Cookles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Ounces
1 tb Shortening
Round butter-flavored
Crackers -- 4 ounces
1/2 c  Creamy peanut butter
1    Sleeve
-
1 c  White semisweet chocolate
Or milk chocolate chips 6
Spread peanut butter on half of the crackers; top with remaining
crackers to make sandwiches. Refrigerate. In a double boiler over
simmering water, melt chocolate chips and shortening, stirring until
smooth. Dip sandwiches and place on waxed paper until chocolate
hardens. Yield: 1-1/2 dozen
