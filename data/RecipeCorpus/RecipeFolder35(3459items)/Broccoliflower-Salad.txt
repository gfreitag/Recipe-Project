Title: Broccoliflower Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Sour cream
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese -- cubed
1    Head cauliflower flowerets
1 bn Broccoli flowerets
1 pk Peas, Frozen -- uncooked
1 pk Hidden Valley Ranch Salad
Dressing mix
2 c  Mayonnaise
Cut vegetables into flowerets (use an equal size bunch of both).  Add
frozen peas.

Blend together:  Mayonnaise or Miracle Whip, sour cream, dressing mix,
salt, sugar, and garlic powder.  Pour mixture over vegetables.
Refrigerate for several hours (overnight, if possible).

Add cheese just before serving.

NOTES : This is a "secret" recipe stolen from a restaurant in Peshtigo
Wisconsin, when my brother lived up there.  It's really delicious!
Serves a crowd.
