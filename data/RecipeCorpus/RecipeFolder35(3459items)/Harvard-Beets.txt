Title: Harvard Beets
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Vinegar
2 tb Orange juice
1 tb Orange peel -- grated
1 cn Sliced beets -- l6 ounces
1/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Cornstarch
Drain beets, reserving 2 tablespoons juice; set beets and juice
aside. In a saucepan, combine sugar and cornstarch. Add vinegar,
orange juice and beet juice; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer
for 3-4 minures or until thickened. Add beets and orange peel; heat
through. Yield: 4-6 servings
