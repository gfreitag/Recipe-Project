Title: Norma Alloway's Satellite Cafe Oatmeal Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Route 66
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Baking Soda
1 1/2 ts Baking Powder
1 c  Old-Fashioned Oatmeal
1 1/4 c  Boiling Water
1    Stick Margarine
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown Sugar
2    Eggs
MMMMM-----------------------FROSTING---------------------------------
3/4 c  Coconut -- flaked
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
1/4 c  Margarine
4 tb Milk
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
In a large bowl, combine oatmeal with boiling water and set aside for
20 mins. Cream margarine; add sugar and blend. Stir in eggs and
vanilla. Add dry ingredients to the oatmeal mixture, then stir into
the shortening (margarine), sugar and eggs. Bake in a greased 8 x 12
inch pan in a preheated 350 F oven for 35 mins.

For the frosting, bring the margarine, milk, and sugar to a boil. Add
remaining ingredients and cool slightly before spreading over cake.
Place under broiler just long enough to coast coconut if desired.

Makes 12 servings.
