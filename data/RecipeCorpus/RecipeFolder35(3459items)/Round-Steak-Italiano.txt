Title: Round Steak Italiano
Categories: Crockpot, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Oregano
1 cn Spaghetti sauce; 15 oz
1 1/2 lb Round steak
1 ts Salt
Cut the steaks into 5 or 6 serving pieces. Coat with salt, oregano,
and pepper.  Place the meat in a crockpot and pour spaghetti sauce
over it. Cook 7 to 9 hours on LOW. Serve with noodles so you can
spoon the sauce over them!
