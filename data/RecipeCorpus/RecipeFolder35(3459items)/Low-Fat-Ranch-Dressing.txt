Title: Low-Fat Ranch Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
1/4 c  Sour cream -- non-fat
1/4 c  Mayonnaise-type salad
Dressing -- fat-free
1 pk Ranch salad dressing --
Low-fat mix 2 oz.
1 1/2 c  Skim milk
In a small bowl, combine all ingredients until smooth. Cover and
chill. Serve over salad greens. Keep refrigerated.
