Title: Spam Macaroni Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
1/2    - cup grated Swiss cheese
1 md Green pepper, diced
1 c  Soft bread, diced
1    2 oz jar pimento, diced
1 c  Grated American cheese
1/4    - cup onion, minced
1 c  Green peas or mixed
-vegetables, optional
1    7 oz box elbow macaroni (2
-cups uncooked)
2    Eggs, beaten
1/2    - cup milk
1 ts Seasoned salt
1 ts Prepared mustard
1    12 oz can Spam, diced
-(reserve 3 whole slices)
Prepare macaroni as package directions. Drain. Beat together eggs,
milk, salt and mustard. Mix with macaroni. Combine with remaining
ingredients (except 3 slices Spam). Pour into 2 qt.  casserole that
has been lightly greased with margarine. Cut Spam slices in half
lengthwise. Arrange on top. Bake @ 350 degrees 25 to 30 minutes.
