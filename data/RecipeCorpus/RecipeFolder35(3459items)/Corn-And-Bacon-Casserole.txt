Title: Corn And Bacon Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Sour cream
3 1/2 c  Whole kernel corn, frozen --
Or fresh
1 tb Chopped parsley -- fresh
1 tb Chives -- chopped fresh
6 ea Bacon slices
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 tb All-purpose flour
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
In a large skillet, cook bacon until crisp. Drain reserving 2
tablespoons of drippings. Crumble bacon; set aside. Saute onion in
dripping until tender. Add flour, garlic, salt and pepper. Cook and
stir until bubbly; cook and stir 1 min. more. Remove from the heat
and stir in sour cream until smooth. ADD corn, parsley and half of
the bacon; mix well. Pour into a 1 qt. baking dish. Sprinkle with
remaining bacon. Bake uncovered at 350 deg. for 20 to 25 min. or
until heated through. Sprinkle with chives. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
