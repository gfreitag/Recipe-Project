Title: Terlyakl Marlnade
Categories: Chinese, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 c  Soy sauce
3 tb Sugar
2 tb Dry cooking sherry
Combine all ingredients; mix well. Pour over beef; cover and
refrigerate for 2 to 24 hours. Yield: 1/2 cup
