Title: Zesty Carrot Bake
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
2 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
1/2 c  Sharp cheddar cheese --
Shredded
1 lb Carrots -- cut 1/2 in.
Slices
2 tb Onion -- minced
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/3 c  Water
1 tb Prepared horseradish
On stovetop or in a microwave oven, cook carrots until tender. Place
in a 1 qt. baking dish; set aside. In a small bowl, combine the next
five ingredients; mix well. Pour over carrots. Combine bread crumbs
and butter; sprinkle on top. Bake uncovered at 350 deg. for 25 to 30
min. Sprinkle with cheese. Return to the oven for 2 to 3 min. or
until the cheese melts. Yield: 6 servings.
