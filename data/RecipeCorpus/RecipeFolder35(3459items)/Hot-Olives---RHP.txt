Title: Hot Olives - RHP
Categories: Chilies, Vegetables, Appetizers, Hot/spicy
Yield: 1 jar
-jar or can of jalapenos
1 ea Jar whole or stuffed olives
1 ea Juice from the same-sized
Drain the juice from the olives and replace it with the juice from
the jalapenos.  Recap the jar and keep it in the refrigerator for 3
weeks before using.  Replace the liquid in the jalapeno jar with
equal amounts of vinegar and water.  Recap and store in the
refrigerator.
