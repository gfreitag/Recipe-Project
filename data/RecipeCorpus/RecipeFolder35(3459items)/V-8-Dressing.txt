Title: V-8 Dressing
Categories: Condiments, Low cal/fat, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Marjoram, dried
1/4 ts Thyme, dried
1/4 ts Mustard, dry
1 ts Cornstarch
1 pk Equal sweetener
1 c  V8 vegetable juice
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Basil, dried
1 ts Oregano, dried
Place all ingredients except equal in sauce pan.  Stir until
cornstarch is dissolved.  Turn to medium high heat and stir
constantly until mixture thickens.  Remove from heat, add Equal and
chill
