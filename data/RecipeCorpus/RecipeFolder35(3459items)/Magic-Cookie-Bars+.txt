Title: Magic Cookie Bars+
Categories: Cake/cookie, Breads, Desserts
Yield: 54 bars
1 pk Nestle Toll House chocolate
-semi-sweet morsels (6 0z.)
1 1/3 c  Flaked coconut
1 c  Walnuts, chopped
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
-(14 oz.)
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a 13 x 9" baking pan, melt butter in
oven. Remove from oven; add graham cracker crumbs. Mix well and press
into pan. Pour sweetened condensed milk evenly over crumbs. Sprinkle
chocolate chips, coconut and walnuts on top; press down firmly. Bake
at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes. Cool completely on a wire rack.
Cut into 2 x 1" bars.

Origin; "Sweet Treats, Nestle Toll House"
