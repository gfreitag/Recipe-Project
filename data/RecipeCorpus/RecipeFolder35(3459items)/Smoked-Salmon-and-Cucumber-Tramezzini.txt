Title: Smoked Salmon and Cucumber Tramezzini
Categories: Sandwich, Fish/seafoo, Italian
Yield: 4 sandwiches
6 oz Thinly sliced smoked salmon
Or lox
1/2    Cucumber, peeled and thinly
Sliced
1 c  Watercress or basil leaves,
Or mixed baby lettuces
1 tb Lemon juice
2 1/2 tb Olive oil
Pinch salt
Freshly ground black pepper
To taste
8    Thin slices good quality
Italian bread
1. Whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt and several
grindings black pepper.

2. Lay out 4 slices of the bread and brush lightly with some of the
dressing.  Top with the smoked salmon, cucumber and greens.  Drizzle
with the remaining dressing and cover with the remaining slices
bread.  Press down lightly and cut into halves.

Data per sandwich: Calories.....329 Carbohydrates.....37g
Monounsaturated fat.....8g Protein......14g Sodium..........772mg
Polyunsaturated fat.....2g Fat..........12g Saturated fat......2g
Cholesterol...........10mg
