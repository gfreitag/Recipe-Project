Title: Banana Date Pudding^
Categories: Puddings, Vegetarian, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Cornstarch
1 c  Water
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
2    Sliced bananas
1 c  Chopped dates
2 c  Boiling water
2/3 c  Cashews
1/8 ts Salt
Pour boiling water over dates, cover. Let stand about 5 minutes then
blend smooth. Empty into a saucepan. BLEND smooth remaining
ingredients, except bananas. Add to saucepan, lightly boil until
thick, stirring, about 10 minutes. Stir in sliced bananas. Po

Variations: Add 2 tablespoons cornstarch for a pie filling. May use
other varieties of fruits or nuts. Source: Of These Ye May Freely
Eat: A Vegetarian Cookbook by JoAnn Rachor . ISBN 1-878726-02-1
Shared by Carolyn Shaw 9-95.
