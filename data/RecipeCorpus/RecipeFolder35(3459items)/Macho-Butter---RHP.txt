Title: Macho Butter - RHP
Categories: Chilies, Dips/spread, Appetizers
Yield: 5 cups
3 c  Chunky peanut butter
1 1/2 c  Sesame seeds
Ground cayenne pepper; as
-needed
3 ea Chipotles; rehydrated and
-seeded
4 ea Anchos; lightly toasted,
-rehydrated, and seeded
1 cl Garlic; peeled
Place the peppers and garlic in a blender and process until smooth,
adding a little of the soaking water to make a paste.  Gradually add
the peanut butter, mixing well.  Add the sesame seeds and mix until
distributed throughout.  Season with cayenne pepper.  Spread as a
spread with tortilla chips, bread rounds, or celery sticks.

_Red Hot Peppers_  Jean Andrews, 1993
