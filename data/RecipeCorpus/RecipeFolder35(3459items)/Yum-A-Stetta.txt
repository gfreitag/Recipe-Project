Title: Yum-A-Stetta
Categories: Meats, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
Undiluted
1 pk Egg noodles -- 16 oz
1 pk Processed American cheese --
8    Oz
1/4 md Onion -- chopped
2 lb Lean ground beef
1 ds Pepper
1 ds Salt
2 tb Brown sugar
1 cn Tomato soup -- undiluted
1 cn Cream of chicken soup --
Brown hamburger with salt, pepper, brown sugar and onion. Add tomato
soup. Cook egg noodles; drain. Add cream of chicken soup. Layer
hamburger mixture and noodle mixture in casserole dish with processed
cheese between layers. Bake at 350 deg for 1/2 hr. Servings 8.
