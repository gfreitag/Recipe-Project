Title: Downtown Route 66 Diner Brown Beans
Categories: Route 66, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Garlic -- minced
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Brown Beans
4 qt Water
1 lg Ham Bone
1 1/2    Onions -- chopped
Wash and pick over beans. In a large pot, cover beans with water. Add
other ingredients and simmer over low heat for 6 hours, adding water
as needed. Do not stir.
