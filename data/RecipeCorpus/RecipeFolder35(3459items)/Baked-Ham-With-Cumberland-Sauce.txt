Title: Baked Ham With Cumberland Sauce
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Dry mustard
Whole cloves
1/2 ea Ham
With bone 4 to 5 pounds
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
MMMMM---------------------CUMBERLAND SAUCE:--------------------------
1/4 c  Red wine -- or apple juice
2 tb Honey
1 tb Cornstarch
1 c  Red currant -- or apple
Jelly
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Remove skin from ham; score the sulface with shallow diagonal cuts,
making diamond shapes. Mix brown sugar and mustard; rub into fat of
ham. Insert a whole clove in center of each diamond. Place ham in a
large roaster with a baking rack. Bake, uncovered, at 325 deg. for
20-22 minutes per pound. For sauce, combine all of the ingredients in
a medium saucepan. Cook over medium heat until thickened, stirring
often. Serve over the sliced ham. (Sauce recipe can be doubled if
desired.) Yield: 8-10 servings (1-3/4 cups sauce)
