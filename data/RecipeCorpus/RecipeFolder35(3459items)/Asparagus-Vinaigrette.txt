Title: Asparagus Vinaigrette
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
3 tb Dill pickles -- relish
1 tb Fresh parsley
1 tb Fresh chives
2 lb Asparagus -- fresh
Cooked and drained
Lettuce leaves
3 ea Hard-boiled egg -- sliced
2 md Tomatoes -- cut in wedges
1 1/2 c  Olive oil -- or vegetable
Oil
1/2 c  White wine vinegar
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
3 ea Radishes -- sliced
1/4 c  Green pepper -- chopped
In a bowl, whisk together the first five ingredients. Add radishes,
green pepper, relish, parsley and chives. Place asparagus in a glass
baking dish; pour dressing over asparagus. Cover and chill at least 4
hours or overnight. To serve arrange the lettuce on a serving
platter; remove asparagus from dressing with a slotted spoon and
arrange over lettuce. Garnish with eggs and tomatoes. Drizzle with
some of the dressing. Yield; 6 to 8 servings.
