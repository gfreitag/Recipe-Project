Title: Candied Acorn Squash Rings
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
2 ea Acorn squash
Cut into 1-inch rings and
Seeded
Arrange squash in a shallow baking pan; cover with foil. Bake at 350
deg. for 35-40 minutes or until tender. Combine sugar and butter;
spread over squash. Bake, uncovered, for 15-20 minutes, basting
occasionally. Yield: 6 servings.
