Title: Spinach Rice Casserole
Categories: Vegetarian, Casseroles, Main dish, Pasta/rice, Healthy
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Low sodium tamari
1 ts Cumin
2    Tomatoes, sliced
1    Onion, chopped
2 c  Spinach, chopped
3 c  Brown rice, cooked
Saute onin in a small amount of water until translucent. Add spinach
and steam (covered) until barely tender (about 5 minutes). Remove
from heat. Mix the rice with the onions and spinach. Press into
non-stick baking dish
(9-inch square or 12-inch by 7-inch). Layer sliced tomatoes over
the top. Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
