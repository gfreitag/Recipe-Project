Title: Cherry Cranberry Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 16 servings
3 c  Cranberries fresh or freeze
Chopped
1 1/2 c  Apple -- diced
1 c  Celery -- chopped
1 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 pk Gelatin powder -- cherry
1 c  Sugar
2 c  Water -- boiling
1 cn Crushed pineapple in juice
Undrained
In a large bowl, combine gelain and sugar. Add water; stir until
gelatin and sugar are dissolved. Stir in pineapple, cranberries,
apple, celery and walnuts. Pour into 2 qt. serving bowl; chill until
firm, 3-4 hours. Yield: 12 to 16 servings.
