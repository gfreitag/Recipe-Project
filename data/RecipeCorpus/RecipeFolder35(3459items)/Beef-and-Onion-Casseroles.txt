Title: Beef and Onion Casseroles
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
2 tb Pimiento
1 cn 3oz french fried onions
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1 c  Sour cream
Combine beef and chopped onion in glass bowl and microwave on high 7
to 8 minutes.  Drain.  Add sour cream, soup, and pimiento.  Microwave
on high 5 to 6 minutes more stirring once during cooking.  Top with
French fried onions
