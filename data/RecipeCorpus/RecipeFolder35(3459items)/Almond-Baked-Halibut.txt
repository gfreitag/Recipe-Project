Title: Almond Baked Halibut
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 ea Cloves of garlic crushed
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 c  Sliced almonds
1 tb Butter or margerine
3 lb Halibut
1 tb Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
3 ea Juice of 3 limes
Heat oven to 500.  Arrange fish in a shallow low baking pan.  Season
with salt and pepper.  Blend together lime juice, garlic and oil and
brush over fish.  Bake for 10 minutes; turn over, baste with juice.
Lower oven temperature to 425 and bake 15 to 20 minutes longer or
until fish flakes easily with a fork.  Baste several times during
cooking. Saute almonds in butter or margerine until golden brown.
Serve fish with almonds sprinkled on top and garnish with lime
wedges. 1973 Curtin Publications. * CROSSPOSTED Submitted By
