Title: Sugar 'n' Spice Nuts
Categories: Appetizers, Country wom
Yield: 20 servings
1 tb Orange peel -- grated
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Ground allspice
3 cn Mixed nuts -- lightly
Salted
1 ea Egg white
1 tb Orange juice
2/3 c  Sugar
Place nut in alarge bowl. In a small bowl, beat egg white and orange
juice with a fork until foamy. Add sugar, orange peel, cinnamon,
ginger and allspice; mix well. Pour over nuts and stir to coat.
Spread into an ungreased 15x10x1 inch baking pan. Bake at 275 deg.
stirring every 15 min. for 45 to 50 min. or until nuts are crisp and
lightly browned. cool completely. Store in an airtight container.
Yield: 4 cups.
