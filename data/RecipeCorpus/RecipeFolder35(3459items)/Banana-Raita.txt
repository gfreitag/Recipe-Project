Title: Banana Raita
Categories: Condiments, Low cal/fat
Yield: 2 cups
1    Banana, peeled
-coarsely chopped
Salt and pepper
1/4 c  Mint leaves, chopped
2 ts Sugar or to taste
2 c  Non-fat yogurt
1/4 ts Ground cardamon (opt)
1 sm Cucumber, peeled
-seeded and grated
-or  coarsely chopped
Drain the yogurt for 2 hours.

Fold the remaining ingredients into the yogurt. correct the seasoning,
adding more salt to taste.

Serve in a bowl with your favorite curry.
