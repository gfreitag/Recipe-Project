Title: New Fangled Potato 'Chips'
Categories: Potatoes, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Cider vinegar
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
1/2 ts Salt and pepper, or to taste
4 ea New potatoes
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Butter, melted
Scrub potatoes and cut into 1/2 inch thick slices.  In large bowl,
combine oil, butter, vinegar, garlic, salt and pepper.  Stir to mix.
Add sliced potatoes and toss to coat.  Saving butter mixture for a
basting sauce, cook potatoes on grill over medium heat.  Turn and
baste frequently for about 15-20 minutes or until potatoes are tender
and golden brown. Makes about 4 servings. Origin: Canadian Living,
June 1995. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/95.
