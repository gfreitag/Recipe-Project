Title: Tangy Meatballs-1
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Egg -- beaten
Salt and pepper to taste
3 tb Cooking oil
2    Bottles
1    Jar grape jelly -- 8 oz.
1 1/2 c  Beef broth
Cloves -- minced
Chili sauce -- 10 oz. ea.
3 sl White bread -- torn in small
Pcs
1 c  Milk
2 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 lb Bulk pork sausage
1 md Onion -- finely chopped
1 tb Mustard seed
2 ts Seasoned salt
2    Garlic
In a large bowl, combine bread and milk. Squeeze excess milk out of
bread: discard milk. To the bread, add beef, sausage, onion, mustard
seed, seasoned salt, garlic, egg, salt and pepper: shape into
1-1/2-in. balls. In a large skillet, brown meatballs in oil; drain.
In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, combine chili sauce, jelly and
beef broth; slowly bring to a boil. Add meatballs; simmer for 30-45
minutes. Yield: 10 serving
