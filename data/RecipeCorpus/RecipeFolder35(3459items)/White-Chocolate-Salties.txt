Title: White Chocolate Salties
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 lbs
3 c  Pretzels sticks
1 c  Salted Spanish Peanuts
1 lb White chocolate
2 tb Shortening
Combine chocolate and shortening in top of a double boiler; bring
water to boil.  Reduce heat to low and cook until chocolate melts.
Pour chocolate mixture into a large mixing bowl.  Stir in Pretzels
and peanuts and spread into a buttered 15x10x1 in jelly roll pan.
Chill 20 minutes or untilfirm.
Break into pieces.  Store in an airtight container.  Yields 1 1/2
lbs. Submitted By SUE TOLENE   On   09-28-95
