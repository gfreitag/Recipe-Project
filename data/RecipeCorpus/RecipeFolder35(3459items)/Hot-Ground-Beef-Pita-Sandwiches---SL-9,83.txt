Title: Hot Ground Beef Pita Sandwiches - SL 9/83
Categories: Sandwich, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 ds Pepper
6 ea 6-inch pita bread rounds
1 1/2 c  Tomatoes; peeled and chopped
5 ea Green onions with tops;
-chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper; chopped
1/2 c  Cucumber; peeled & chopped
1/2 c  Feta cheese; crumbled
3/4 c  Onion; chopped
1 tb Olive oil
1 lb Ground beef
3 tb Catsup
2 1/2 tb ;Water
1/2 ts Ground allspice
1 ds Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
Saute 3/4 cup onion in hot oil in a large skillet until tender. Add
ground beef and cook until browned, stirring to crumble; drain off pan
drippings.  Stir in next 6 ingredients.  Reduce heat and simmer 5
minutes, stirring occasionally.
Place bread rounds on a baking sheet; bake at 250 degrees for 10
minutes or until warm.  Cut bread rounds in half; fill each pocket
about half full with meat mixture.  Top each with tomatoes, onion,
green pepper, cucumber, and cheese.  Yield: 6 servings.

Mrs. Bronwen M. Gibson of Texas in September, 1983 "Southern Living"
