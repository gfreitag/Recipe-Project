Title: Cheesy Sun Crisps
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 96 servings
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Oatmeal, instant
2/3 c  Sunflower seeds, roasted
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
3 tb Water
In a mixing bowl, combine cheddar and parmesan cheeses, butter and
water until well mixed. Stir in oats and sunflower seeds. Knead dough
until it hold together. Shape into a 12 in. roll. Cover with plastic
wrap; chill for 4 hr. or overnight. Allow to stand at room temp. for
10 min. before cutting into 1/8 inch slices. Place on greased
foil-lined baking sheets. Bake at 400 deg. for 8 to 10 min. or until
edges are golden. Slide crackers and foil off baking to wire racks to
cool. Yield: 8 doz.
