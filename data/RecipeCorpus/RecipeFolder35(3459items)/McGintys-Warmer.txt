Title: McGintys Warmer
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
Hot coffee
Whipped Cream
Cinnamon sugar
1/2 oz Creme de cacao
1/2 oz Ameretto
1/2 oz Baileys
Pour the creme de cacao, ameretto, and baileys into coffee mug.  Top
off with hot coffee and a dollop of whipped cream.  Sprinkle with
cinnamon sugar. From Canadian Living Nov 1995
