Title: Appetizer Meatballs
Categories: Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 96 servings
1 tb Ketchup
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Italian seasoning
1 ts Dried basil
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 lb Lean ground beef
2 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
1 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded 4 oz
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1/4 c  Onion -- finely chopped
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1    Jar apple jelly -- 12 oz
1    Jar currant jelly -- 12 oz
1    Bottle ketchup, regular or
-hot, 14 oz.
2 tb Cornstarch
In a bowl, combine the first 12 ingredients; mix well. Shape into 1
inch balls. Place on a rack in a shallow roasting pan. Bake at 350
deg. for 10 to 15 min. remove the meatballs and rack; drain. Combine
ketchup and cornstarch in roasting pan. Stir in jellies; add the
meatballs.Cover and bake for 30 min. Yield: about 8 doz.
