Title: Coconut Macaroons #3
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Salt
2 ea Egg whites
1/2 ts Almond extract
cn Cherry halves
1 1/2 c  (about) Baker's Angel Food
-coconut
1/3 c  Sugar
3 tb Flour
Combine coconut, sugar, flour and salt in bowl. Stir in egg whites and
almond extract; mix well. Drop from teaspoon onto lightly greased
baking sheets. Add cherry halves. Bake at 20-25 minutes, or until
edges are golden. Remove from sheets immediately.
