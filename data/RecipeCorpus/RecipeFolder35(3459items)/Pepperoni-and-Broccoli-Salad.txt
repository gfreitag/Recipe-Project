Title: Pepperoni-and-Broccoli Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  (4 oz) Swiss cheese; diced
3 1/2 oz Package sliced pepperoni
1 ea Green pepper; chopped
3/4 oz Package Italian salad
-dressing mix
1 lb Bunch broccoli
1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms; sliced
Prepare dressing mix according to package directions; set aside.
Trim off large leaves of broccoli.  Was broccoli, and break off
flowerets; reserve stalks for use in other recipes.
Combine broccoli flowerets and next 4 ingredients in a large bowl.
Pour dressing over salad; toss gently.  Cover and refrigerate 8 hours
or overnight.

Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
