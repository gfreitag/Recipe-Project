Title: LEMONY TUNA STROGANOFF
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------YIELD: 4 OR 5 SERVINGS------------------------
-peel (zest) only
ds Pepper
2 cn (6-7 oz) tuna
1 cn (4 oz) sliced mushrooms,
-drained
Hot cooked noodles or rice
Paprika to taste
4 ea Green onions
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 cn (10 3/4 oz.) condensed
-cream of mushroom soup
2/3 c  Sour cream (I use lite)
1/3 c  Milk (I use skim)
1/2 ea Lemon, juice and grated
Cut green onions into 1-inch pieces.  Place in skillet with butter and
garlic and saute until tender.  Add soup, sour cream, milk, lemon
zest, lemon juice, and pepper.  Stir in tuna and mushrooms until
heated through. Serve over pasta or rice; sprinkle with paprika.
