Title: Fried Pecans
Categories: Holiday, Snacks, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
Powdered sugar
Pecans
Beaten egg whites
Dip pecans in beaten egg whites and then roll in powdered sugar and
deep fry.
Or just deep fry them and salt, with no coating.
