Title: Cottage Potato Bake
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Butter
Paprika
Parsley
Salt and pepper
6 md Potatoes, peeled, diced, and
Cooked as you would for
Mashed potatoes
1 c  Cream style cottage cheese
1    Egg
When potatoes are cooked, drain well, return to warm, no heat burner.
Let set for 5 to 10 minutes.  Place potatoes in a large bowl.  Mash
and mix with the rest of the ingredients except paprika.  Turn into a
well greased 1 inch baking dish.  Sprinkle with paprika.  Top with
buttered bread crumbs and bake uncovered in a 350 degree oven for 35
minutes. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   10-20-95
