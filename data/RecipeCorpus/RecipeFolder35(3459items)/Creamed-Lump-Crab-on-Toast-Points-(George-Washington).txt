Title: Creamed Lump Crab on Toast Points (George Washington)
Categories: Appetizers, Fish/seafoo, Oldies
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Brandy
2 ts Fresh parsley; chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Toast points
4 md Fresh mushrooms; sliced
1/4 c  Butter or margarine; melted
1 lb Fresh crabmeat; drained and
-flaked
1 c  Whipping cream
Saute mushrooms in butter in a large skillet 3 minutes or until
tender.
Add next 6 ingredients, stirring gently; heat thoroughly.  Serve on
toast points.

From a formal dinner at George Washington's Mount Vernon, Virginia?
