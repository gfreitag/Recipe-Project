Title: Golden Spread Pecan Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Route 66
Yield: 8 servings
pn Salt
1/2 c  (Or More) Pecans
1    9 Inch Pie Shell -- unbaked
3    Eggs
1/2 c  White Sugar
1 1/2 c  Dark Karo Syrup
Preheat oven to 350 F. In a medium mixing bowl, beat eggs until
creamy. Add sugar, syrup and salt. Blend well to dissolve sugar. Pour
mixture into unbaked pie shell and top with pecans. Bake 45 mins to 1
hour or until set. Makes 8 servings.
