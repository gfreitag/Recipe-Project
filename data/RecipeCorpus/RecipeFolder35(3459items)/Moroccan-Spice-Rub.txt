Title: Moroccan Spice Rub
Categories: Moroccan, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 /2 cup
1/2 ts Ground cloves
2 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Chopped garlic
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 tb Ground cumin
1 tb Paprika
1 tb Ground black pepper
1 tb Ground coriander
1 ts Cayenne
Moroccan Spice Rub: Put all the ingredients in a food processor and
blend, scraping the sides occasionally, until you have a paste.

Note: Leftover spice rub is great for grilled pork chops.
