Title: Creamy Pineapple Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 ct Whipped cream, light --
Frozen 8 oz thawed
1 ea Graham cracker pie crust, 9
Inch
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk --
14    Oz
1 cn Crushed pineapple -- 8 0z
Undrained
1/4 c  Lemon juice
In a bowl, mix milk, pineapple and lemon juice. Fold in whipped
topping. Pour into the crust. Chill until ready to serve. Yield: 8
servings.
