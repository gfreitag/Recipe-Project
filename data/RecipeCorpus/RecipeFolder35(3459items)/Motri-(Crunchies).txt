Title: Motri (Crunchies)
Categories: Appetizers, Indian, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 ts Sambhar spice
5 tb White vinegar
Oil for deep-fat frying
1 1/4 c  Biscuit or pancake mix
1 pn Salt
1 ds Cumin seeds
1 ds Cayenne
Combine the biscuit mix with the salt, cumin, cayenne & sambhar spice.
Gradually add in the vinegar to form a soft dough.  Knead to ensure
it is well blended.  Divide in hal & form 2 balls.

On a lightly floured board, roll out each ball to a thickness of
1/4". Cut into very small diamond shapes (1/2" long).  Heat vegetable
oil & fry the diamond shapes.  Drain & serve while warm.

NOTE: These may be frozen.  To serve from frozen place on a cookie
sheet & bake at 400F for 8 to 10 minutes.  Do not defrost first.

Pranati Sen Gupta, "The Art of Indian Cuisine"
