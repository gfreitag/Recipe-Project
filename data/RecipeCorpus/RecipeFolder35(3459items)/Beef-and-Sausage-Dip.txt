Title: Beef and Sausage Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Meats, Sausage
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
8 oz Sour cream
4 tb Old El Paso jalepeno relish
1 lb Ground beef
1 lb Sausage
1 md Onion, chopped
1 lb Velveeta cheese
Brown beef, sausage and onion.  In saucepan, melt 1 lb. Velveeta
cheese cut into chunks.  Add cream of mushroom soup, 8 oz. sour
cream, and relish. Drain meat and add other ingredients.  Add
additional relish for more spice. Randy Rigg
