Title: Crock Pot Tomato Sauce
Categories: Crockpot, Salsa/sauce, Tomatoes
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Salt
1 ts Oregano
1 cn Tomato sauce (12 oz)
20 x  Tomatoes; peeled
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Pepper
5 tb Corn starch
Mix all ingredients in crock pot, excluding the corn starch. Cook on
high 8 hours. Approximately 1 hour before done, mix corn starch with
1/2 cup water and add to crock pot mixture. Finish cooking. Cool and
put in resealable bags or seal in jars.
