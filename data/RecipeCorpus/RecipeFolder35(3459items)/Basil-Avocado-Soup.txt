Title: Basil-Avocado Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Fruit, Herbs/spice
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
2 1/2 c  Chicken stock
Avocado slices, sour cream
And Basil sprigs to garnish
3    Avocados; peeled & pitted
1 c  Reduced-fat sour cream
1/2 c  Fresh basil; chopped
2 tb Lemon juice
In a processor or blender, combine avocados, sour cream, basil, lemon
juice and pepper; puree until smooth. Add chicken stock; process
until smooth. Serve and garnish with avocado slices, a dab of low-fat
sour cream and basil. Per serving: 233 cal. 5 g protein, 19 g fat, 7
mg chol., 14 g carbs, 367 mg sodium.
