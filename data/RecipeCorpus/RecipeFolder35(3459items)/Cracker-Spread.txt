Title: Cracker Spread
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
8 oz Liverwurst
4 oz Cream cheese
1 x  Small onion, finely minced
Mix all ingredients well with beater. Add a little milk to thin if
desired. SUGGESTIONS:
A good smoky braunschwieger makes this good.
A spoonful of horseradish adds zip.
Serve on triscuts, toast rounds, or your favorite crackers.
