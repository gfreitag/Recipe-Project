Title: Banana Glace~
Categories: Low cal/fat, Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Vanilla
Freshly ground nutmeg
2    Frozen ripe Bananas
1 1/2 c  Skim milk
2 tb Honey
Slice bananas and place them in a blender or food processor with 1/2
cup of the milk and honey. Blend until smooth. Add remaining milk,
cinnamon and vanilla. Blend. Freeze until slushy, about 1 1/2 hours.
Rmove from freezer, blend again until smooth. Refreeze. Sprinkle with
nutmeg and serve.

Makes 4 servings.

Per serving:  121 calories; 0.6 g fat (4%).
