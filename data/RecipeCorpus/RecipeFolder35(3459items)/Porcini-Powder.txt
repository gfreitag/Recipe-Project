Title: Porcini Powder
Categories: Vegetables, Information
Yield: 1 batch
None
Grind the small bits and pieces and the dregs of dried porcini
mushrooms in a clean coffee or spice grinder. Use in pasta, beans,
soups, and stocks for a fast, easy way to get mushroom taste in to a
dish.

Keeps for 1 year in a glass jar at room temperature.

The Occasional Vegetarian by Karen Lee ISBN 0-446-51792-5 pg 42
