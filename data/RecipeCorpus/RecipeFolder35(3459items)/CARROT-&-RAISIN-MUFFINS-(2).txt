Title: CARROT & RAISIN MUFFINS (2)
Categories: Muffins, Breads
Yield: 9 servings
-cup cottage cheese, 1/4
-cup raisins
1/2 c  Sugar, 3/4 cup milk
1 c  Bran, 3/4 cup flour, 1 tsp
-baking soda, 1 Tbsp golden
-syrup, 1 egg
1 sm Carrot, 1/4 tsp salt, 1/4
Place bran, milk, golden syrup & egg into a mixing bowl. Peel & grate
carrot  & add with cottage cheese & raisins. Leave to stand for 5
mins. Add flour, sugar, b/soda & salt. Mix until just combined. 3/4
fill greased muffin pans & bake at 350oF for 20 mins or until
well-risen & golden. Makes 9.
