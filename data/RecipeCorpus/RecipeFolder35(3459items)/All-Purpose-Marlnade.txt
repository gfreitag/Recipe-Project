Title: All-Purpose Marlnade
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
1 tb Pepper
2 ts Fresh parsley -- chopped
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Hot pepper sauce --
Optional
1 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Dry mustard
Combine all ingredients in a blender container or food processor.
Puree until parsley is finely minced. Can be used as a marinade or
baste for beef or game. Yield: 2-1/2 cups
