Title: Frozen Cranberry Banana Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Whole-berry l6 ounces
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ct Frozen whipped topping -- 12
oz Thawed
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 cn Pineapple tidbits -- 20
Ounces
5 md Bananas
Firm  halved lengthwise and
Sliced
1 cn Cranberry sauce
Drain pineapple juice into a medium bowl; set pineapple aside. Add
bananas to the juice. In a large bowl, combine cranberry sauce and
sugar. Remove bananas, discarding juice, and add to cranberry
mixture. Stir in pineapple, whipped topping and nuts. Pour into a
13-in, x 9in. x 2-in. dish. Freeze until solid. Remove from the
freezer 15 minutes before cutting. Yield: 12-16 servings
