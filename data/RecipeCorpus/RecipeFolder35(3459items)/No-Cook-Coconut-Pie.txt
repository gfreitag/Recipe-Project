Title: No-Cook Coconut Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
oz Thawed
1/2 c  Flaked coconut
1    Graham
Toasted coconut
Cracker crust -- 9 inches
2 pk Instant vanilla pudding mix
3 3/8 oz Ea
2 3/4 c  Cold milk
1 ts Coconut extract
1 ct Frozen whipped topping -- 8
In a large mixing bowl, beat pudding mixes, milk and coconut extract
on low speed until combined. Beat on high for 2 minutes. Fold in
whipped topping and coconut. Pour into the crust. Sprinkle with
toasted coconut. Chill until serving time. Refrigerate leftovers.
Yield: 6-8 servings
