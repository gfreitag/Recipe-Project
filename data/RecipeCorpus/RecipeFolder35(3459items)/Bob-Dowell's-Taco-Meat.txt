Title: Bob Dowell's Taco Meat
Categories: Main dish, Route 66, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Black Pepper
1 1/4 lb Lean Ground Beef
1 tb Garlic Powder
1/2 c  Onions -- chopped
Thoroughly mix all ingredients. Cook well, either on top of stove or
in a slow oven. Drain any excess grease. Break meat into small pieces
with fork or spoon. Serve on buffet with crisp fresh taco shells,
grated cheese, chopped tomatoes and lettuce, more onion if desired,
and a big bowl of taco sauce. Makes 8 Texas size tacos.
