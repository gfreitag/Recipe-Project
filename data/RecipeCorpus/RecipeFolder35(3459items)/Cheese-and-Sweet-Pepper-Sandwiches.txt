Title: Cheese and Sweet Pepper Sandwiches
Categories: Sandwich, Cheese/eggs, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
Hot pepper sauce
Salt and pepper
4 oz Cheese
-(softened goat cheese or
Thinly sliced sharp Cheddar
-or Monterey Jack)
4    Long sandwich buns,
-sliced open lengthwise
2 tb Olive oil
1    Red bell pepper
-sliced lengthwise
-into 1/2" strips
1    Yellow bell pepper
-sliced lengthwise
-into 1/2" strips
1 md Onion(s), thinly sliced
1 ts Dried thyme
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet. Add the bell peppers and
cook over moderate heat, stirring occasionally, until wilted, about 6
minutes. Add the onion and cook until all the vegetables are
softened, about 15 minutes. Stir in the thyme and season with hot
pepper sauce and salt and pepper to taste.

2. Spread or layer the cheese on the bottom half of the buns and heap
the vegetables on top. close the sandwiches, then transfer them to
plates and serve.
