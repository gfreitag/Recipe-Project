Title: Spaghetti With Sausage And Peppers
Categories: Pasta/rice, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
3    Cloves garlic -- minced
8 dr Hot pepper sauce
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 tb Cayenne pepper
1/2 c  Chicken broth
2 tb Cornstarch
1 pk Spaghetti -- 12 oz
Cooked and drained
1 lb Turkey sausage -- cut 1/4 in
Slices
Smoked
2 md Green pepper -- thinly
Sliced
2 md Sweet red peppers -- thinly
Sliced
1 md Onion -- thinly sliced
2 cn Tomatoes -- 14 1/2 oz
Diced with liquid
In a Dutch oven, brown sausage. Add peppers and onion; saute for 2
minutes. Add tomatoes, garlic, hot pepper sauce, paprika, salt and
cayenne pepper; cook and stir until the vegetables are tender.
Combine broth and cornstarch; add to sausage mixture. Bring to a
boil. Cook and stir until thickened. Add spaghetti; cook for 5
minutes stirring occasionally. Yield; 6 - 8 servings.
