Title: Grandma Wyckoff's Squaw Bread
Categories: Breads, Route 66
Yield: 10 servings
3 tb Lard
Fat -- for frying
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking Powder
1 c  Sour Milk
Combine flour, baking powder, milk and lard. Knead on bread board
until soft. Use more flour to hold together if needed. Cover and let
stand 10-15 mins. Pinch off dough and roll into thin strips 6-8
inches long and 3-4 inches wide. Heat fat to about 400 F and fry
strips to a pretty dark brown color. Makes 10 pieces.
