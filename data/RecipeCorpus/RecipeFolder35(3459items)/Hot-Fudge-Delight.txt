Title: Hot Fudge Delight
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1    Jar fudge sauce
1 sm Carton Cool Whip
Chopped nuts
30    Oreo cookies
1/4 c  Melted butter
1/2 ga Ice cream
Crush Oreo cookies.  Add butter, mix in a large bowl.  Press cookie
mixture into a 9 x 13 inch pan.  Freeze crust for 30 minutes.  Cut
ice cream in slices and place over cookie layer.  Freeze 30 minutes.
Spread fudge sauce over ice cream layer and freeze 30 minutes. Spread
Cool Whip over fudge sauce layer.  Sprinkle with chopped nuts and
return to freezer. Freeze for 24 hours before serving. Randy Rigg
