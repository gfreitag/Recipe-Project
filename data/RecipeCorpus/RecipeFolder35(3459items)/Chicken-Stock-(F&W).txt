Title: Chicken Stock (F&W)
Categories: Soup/stew, Poultry
Yield: 3 quarts
MMMMM-------------------FOOD AND WINE- OCT 95------------------------
1 tb Herbes de Provence
-or dried thyme
1 tb Soy sauce
3 lb Chicken bones
1 lg Onion, quartered
12    Whole cloves
4    Bay leaves, pref imported
1. In a large stockpot, cover the chicken bones with 6 quarts of
water. Bring to a boil over high heat. Lower the heat and simmer,
skimming often, for 30 minutes. Add the onion, cloves bay leaves,
herbes de Provence and soy sauce and simmer for 2Â« hours. Strain the
stock into a large bowl. Let cool, then cover and refrigerate for up
to 3 days. Skim off fat before using. Can be frozen for up to 1 month.
