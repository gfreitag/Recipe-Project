Title: Oatmeal-Raisin Muffin Mix
Categories: Muffins, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Orange peel
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Old-fashioned oats
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1/4 c  Whole-wheat flour
2 tb Dry buttermilk powder
Place oats in a food processor; process till coarsely ground.  Add
sugars, flour, buttermilk powder, baking powder, orange peel, baking
soda, and salt; pulse process just until well blended.  Stir in
raisins.  Store in an airtight container.  To serve:  Preheat oven to
400. Combine muffin mix, 1/2 cup water, 1/3 cup oil, and 2 eggs, stir
just until moistened. Fill lightly greased muffin cups about
three-fourths full.  Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until a toothpick
inserted in center of muffin comes out clean. Source:  Leisure Arts:
Quick & Tasty Christmas Gifts.
