Title: Blackeyed Peas With Bacon
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Dried thyme
Salt to taste
Additional crunbled bacon,
Optional
1 lb Blackeyed peas -- rinsed and
Sorted
1/2 lb Bacon -- cooked & crumbled
1 lg Onion -- chopped
1    Garlic clove -- minced
Place peas, bacon and enough water to cover in a large kettle; bring
to a boil. Boil for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat; cover and let
stand for 1 hour. Do not drain. In a skillet, saute onion and garlic
in butter until tender. Add to pea mixture with thyme and salt.
Return to the heat; simmer, covered, for 30 minutes or until peas are
soft. Top with crumbled bacon if desired. Yield: 6-8 servings
