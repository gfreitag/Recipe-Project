Title: Applesauce Walnut Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
Softened
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Walnuts -- finely chopped
2 tb Powdered sugar
1 pk Fudge cake mix
3    Eggs
1 1/3 c  Applesauce
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Preheat oven to 375.  Grease and flour bundt pan.  Combine cake mix,
eggs, applesauce, butter and cinnamon in large bowl.  Beat at medium
speed with electric mixer for 4 minutes.  Stir in walnuts.  Pour into
pan.  Bake at 375 for 45 to 50 minutes.  Cool in pan for 25 minutes.
Invert onto serving plate.  Cool completely.  Dust with powdered
sugar.
