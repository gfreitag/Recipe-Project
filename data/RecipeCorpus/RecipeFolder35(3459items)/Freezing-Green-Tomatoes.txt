Title: Freezing Green Tomatoes
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------YIELD VARIES-----------------------------
Yield varies widely depending on the size of the tomatoes.

1. Select firm, sound, green tomatoes.

2. Wash, core, and slice 1/4 inch thick.

3. Tray freeze, without blanching, then pack in containers or bags
with layer of freezer wrap between slices. Seal, label, and freeze.

4. Thaw and coat to fry.
