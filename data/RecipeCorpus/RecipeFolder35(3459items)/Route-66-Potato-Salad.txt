Title: Route 66 Potato Salad
Categories: Route 66, Salads, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Salad Dressing
1/4 c  Mustard
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
2 lb Potatoes
3    Hard-Cooked Eggs -- sliced
1/4 c  Onion -- finely chopped
1/2 c  Sweet Relish -- with juice
Follow directions for Bob's Barbecue Potato Salad. Makes 6 to 8
servings.
