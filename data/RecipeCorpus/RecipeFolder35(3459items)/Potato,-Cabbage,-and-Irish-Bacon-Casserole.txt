Title: Potato, Cabbage, and Irish Bacon Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Potatoes, Vegetables, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Chopped parsley
1/4 ts Leaf thyme
Salt and pepper
1 lb Boiling potatoes, pared and
-thinly sliced
3/4 c  Defatted chicken broth
2 oz (1/2 cup) shredded lowfat
-cheddar cheese
Non stick cooking spray
6 c  Shredded green cabbage
-(about 3/4 lb)
1 lg Onion, chopped
1/4 lb Slab bacon (Shannon
-traditional Irish brand if
-available) cut
Into 1/4 inch cubes
Spray 3 quart casserole dish with cooking spray.  Layer with ahlf of
cabbage and onions, sprinkle with half of bacon.  Sprinkle with half
of parsley, thyme, and salt and pepper to taste.  Arrange half of
potatoes over.  Repeat layers ending with potatoes.  Pour chicken
broth over, sprinkle with cheese.  Cover.  Bake in 350F oven for 45
minutes. Uncover, bake 15 more minutes or until top is brown.  Serves
4. Origin: Cookbook Digest, Mar/Apr 93 Shared by: Sharon Stevens.
