Title: Peanut Dipping Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 cup
2 tb Rice vinegar
2 ts (to 3 tsp) honey
-or sugar or to taste
3 tb Cilantro, minced
1 ts Thai hot sauce
-or your choice hot sauce
3 tb Chunky-style peanut butter
1 ts Ginger, minced
1    (to 2) garlic clove(s)
-minced
1/3 c  Vegetable stock
3 tb Soy sauce or tamari
Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl and whisk until smooth.
Correct the seasoning, adding soy sauce, vinegar, or honey to taste.
The mixture should be a little sweet, sour, and salty.
