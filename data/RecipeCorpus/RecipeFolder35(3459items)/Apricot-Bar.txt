Title: Apricot Bar
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 30 servings
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
2/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Coconut -- shredded
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 ea Egg
1 tb Milk
2 c  Apricot preserves
Topping
In a bowl, combine flour and baking powder. Cut in butter until the
mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Beat the egg and milk; stir into
flour mixture. Spread in a greased 9 inch square baking pan. Spread
preserves over crust. Combine topping ingredients; carefully drop by
tablesspoonfuls over apricot layer. Bake at 350 deg. for 25 to 30
min. or until golden brown. Cool; cut into small bars. Yield: 2 to 2
1/2 dozen.
