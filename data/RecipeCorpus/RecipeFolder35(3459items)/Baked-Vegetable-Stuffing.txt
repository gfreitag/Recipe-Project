Title: Baked Vegetable Stuffing
Categories: Side dish, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
;salt and pepper to taste
1/4 c  Vegetable stock
1/4 c  Wine; can sub with more
-vegetable stock
1/2 ts Sage
1/2 ts Thyme
2 tb Fresh parsley; chopped
-OR 1T dried
2    Carrots; peeled, chopped
1    Celery stalk; fine chopped
1 md Onion; fine chopped
1    Zucchini; cubed
1 c  Mushrooms; chopped
5 sl Bread; crusts removed,
-cubed
3 tb Lemon juice
Combine the first 8 ingredients in a casserole dish.
In small bowl, combine the rest.

Whisk the above together and then pour over the vegetable mixture.
Cover and bake 35 mins at 350.  A baked sweet potato dish would go
great with this.
