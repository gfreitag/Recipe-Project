Title: Cranberry Molded Relish
Categories: Relishes, Holiday
Yield: 12 servings
DRAINED
2 c  Fresh cranberries
1 sm Orange, peeled, quartered
And seeded
1 c  Diced celery
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1 pk 3 oz lemon jello
1 pk 3 oz cherry jello
1/2 c  Sugar
3 c  Boiling water
1 tb Lemon juice
1 cn 8 3/4 oz crushed pineapple
Dissolve jellos and sugar in three cups boiling water.  Add lemon
juice and undrained crushed pineapple.  Chill until partially set.

Put cranberries and orange through grinder.  Fold into gelatin mixture
along with the celery and nuts.

Pour into 8 1/2 cup mold.  Chill until firm.  Unmold on bed of lettuce
leaves.
