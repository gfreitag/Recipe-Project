Title: Zesty Jalapeno Vinegar - RHP
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chilies
Yield: 3 cups
3 cl Garlic; peeled and crushed
1/2 ts Fresh or dried oregano
1 sl Onion
1 ts Black peppercorns; crushed
6 ea Fresh jalapenos, or to
-taste
1 c  ;water
2 c  Cider or white vinegar
2 tb Olive oil
Cut the jalapenos in several places with a knife.  Place all the
ingredients in a nonreactive saucepan and bring to a boil.  Reduce the
heat to medium and simmer for 5 minutes. Cool and place in a
sterilized glass jar or bottle with a tight lid or cork.  Store in
the refrigerator.
Use on vegetables, cooked greens, in salad dressings, or
substitute for vinegar or lime juice in sauce recipes.
