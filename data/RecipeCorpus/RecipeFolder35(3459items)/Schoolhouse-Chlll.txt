Title: Schoolhouse Chlll
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Chili powder
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Pepper
Cooked spaghetti, optional
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1 cn Tomatoes -- 16 oz w/liquid
1 cn Mild chili beans -- 16 oz
W/sauce
1 lb Ground beef
Puree onion, green pepper, tomatoes and beans in a blender or food
processor until smooth. In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, brown the
beef; drain. Add seasonings and the pureed vegetables. Simmer over
low heat for 1 hour. Add cooked spaghetti before serving if desired.
Yield: 4-6 servings (1-1/2 quarts)
