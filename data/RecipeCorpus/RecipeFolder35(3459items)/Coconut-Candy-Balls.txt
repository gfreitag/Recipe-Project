Title: Coconut Candy Balls
Categories: Desserts, Candies
Yield: 12 servings
2 lb Confectioners sugar
12 oz Chocolate chips
2    Inch square paraffin
2    Sticks margarine melted
1 cn Condensed milk
3 c  Coconut
Combine first 5 ingredients in order given.

Shape into balls.  Place on waxed paper on cookie sheet, let harden.
Chill

Melt chocolate chips and paraffin in top of double boiler.

Keeping chocolate mixture on stove over water, dip balls into
chocolate mixture.  Place on waxed paper to cool, or place in waxed
paper candy cups.
