Title: Balogna
Categories: Meats, Sausage
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Liquid smoke
1 c  Water
4 ts Morton Quick Cure
2 lb Ground beef
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Garlic powder
Mix together well.  Shape in two rolls, wrap in heavy foil and
refrigerate for 24 hours.  Cover with water and boil 1 hour. Yes
