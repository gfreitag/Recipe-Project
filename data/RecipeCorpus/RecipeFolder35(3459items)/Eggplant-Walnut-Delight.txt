Title: Eggplant-Walnut Delight
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 x  Salt, to taste
1 x  Oregano, to taste
1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1 x  Medium eggplant
1 x  Medium onion
1/2 c  Walnut meats
Peel eggplant and cut into chunks. Put eggplant, onion, and nut
meats through a food grinder or chop in a blender. Add seasonings and
tomato sauce and mix thoroughly. Form into a loaf and bake in an
oiled casserole for 20 minutes. Serve hot. Stocking Up,Rodale
