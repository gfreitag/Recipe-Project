Title: Splced Pecans
Categories: Snacks, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ea Egg white
1 ts Cold water
1 lb Pecan halves -- 4 cups
In a mixing bowl, beat egg white lightly. Add water; beat until
frothy but not stiff. Add pecans; stir until well coated. Combine
sugar, salt and cinnamon. Sprinkle over pecans; toss to mix. Spread
in a 15-in. x 10-in. x 1in. greased baking pan. Bake at 250 deg. for
1 hour, stirring occasionally. Yield: about 4 cups
