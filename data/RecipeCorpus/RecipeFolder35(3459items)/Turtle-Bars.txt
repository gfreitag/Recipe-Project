Title: Turtle Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 96 servings
Topping
2/3 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1 c  Pecan halves
In a mixing bowl, beat flour, sugar and butter on medium speed for 2
to 3 min. Press firmly into a ungreased  13x9x2 inch baking pan.
Arrange pecans over crust. Combine butter and brown sugar in a
saucepan. Bring to boil; boil for l mi. stirring constantly. Pour
over pecans. Bake at 350 deg. for 18 to 20 min. or until bubbly.
Sprinkle chocolate chips on top; let stand for 3 min. Spread
chocolate but allow some chips to remain whole. Cool completely; cut
into small squares. Yield: about 8 doz.
