Title: Cheese Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Milk
2 c  Cheddar cheese -- 8 oz
Shredded
Chopped fresh parsley
3 tb Butter or margarine
6 ea Large potaloes -- peeled &
Thinly slic
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Melt butter in a large nonstick skillet. Cook potatoes until almost
tender and lightly browned. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Pour milk
over all; boil gently until milk is absorbed. Sprinkle with cheese
and allow to melt. Stir; sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately.
Yield: 6 servings
