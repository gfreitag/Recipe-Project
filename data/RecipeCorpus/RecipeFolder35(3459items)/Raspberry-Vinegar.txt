Title: Raspberry Vinegar
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chambord liqueur
Fresh raspberries
3 c  Red wine vinegar
2    Or 3 cloves of garlic
Combine all ingredients in a tightly sealed jar.  Allow to sit for 2-3
weeks.  Remove garlic cloves before serving.
