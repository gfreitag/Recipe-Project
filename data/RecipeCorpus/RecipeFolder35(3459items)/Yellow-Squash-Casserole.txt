Title: Yellow Squash Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
10 ea Saltine crackers -- crushed
Salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 c  Cheddar cheese -- 6 ounces
Shredded
Summer squash -- sllced
3 tb Butter or margarine
4    Yellow
1 md Onion -- chopped
1 cn Green chilies -- 4 ounces
Chopped
In a skillet, melt butter over medium high heat. Saute squash and
onion until crisp-tender. Remove from the heat; stir in chilies,
crackers, and salt and pepper. Spoon into a greased 1-1/2-qt.
casserole. Top with cheese. Bake at 350 deg. for 15-20 minutes.
Yield: 4-6 servings
