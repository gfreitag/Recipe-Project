Title: Crispy Skin-On Oven Fries
Categories: Vegetables, Low cal/fat, Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Olive oil spray
3 lg Baking potatoes; scrubbed
1/2 ts Salt
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Spray a baking sheet lightly with
olive oil spray.  Cut each potato into 8 wedges and place, skin-side
down, on the baking sheet.  Spray lightly with olive oil spray.

2. Combine the salt, pepper, paprika and garlic powder.  Sprinkle
over the potatoes.  Bake about 30 to 40 minutes, or until browned and
tender.

Data per serving:  200 calories, 4g protein, 0g fat, 46g
carbohydrates, 281mg sodium, 0g saturated fat, 0g monounsaturated
fat, 0g polyunsaturated fat, 0mg cholesterol.
