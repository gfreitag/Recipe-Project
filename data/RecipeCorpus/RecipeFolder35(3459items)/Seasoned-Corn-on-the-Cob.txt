Title: Seasoned Corn on the Cob
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
6    Ears corn
2 tb Reduced-calorie margarine
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Rubbed sage
Place the margarine in a 1-cup glass measure; microwave on medium (50%
power) 20-30 seconds or until melted. Stir in onion powder, sage and
pepper. Brush margarine mixture onto each ear of corn. Wrap each ear
of corn in heavy-duty plastic wrap, twisting ends to seal. Arrange
corn on paper towels in microwave oven. Microwave on high 16 minutes,
rearranging corn after 8 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes.
