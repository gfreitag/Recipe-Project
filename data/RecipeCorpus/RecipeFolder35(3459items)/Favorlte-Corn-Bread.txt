Title: Favorlte Corn Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 9 servings
3/4 ts Salt
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Shortening
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Cornmeal
1/4 c  Sugar
4 ts Baking powder
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, corn meal, sugar, baking powder and
salt. Add the milk, eggs and shortening; beat for 1 minute. Pour into
a greased 9-in. square baking pan. Bake at 425 deg.  for 20-25
minutes or until bread is golden brown and tests done. Yield: 9
servings
