Title: Funk's Grove Peach Cobbler
Categories: Desserts, Route 66, Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
1 cn Sliced Peaches, (29 Oz) --
-drained
1    Stick Margarine
1 c  Flour
3 ts Baking Powder
1/2 c  Maple Syrup
Melt margarine in 9x13-inch baking pan. Stir flour, baking powder,
syrup, sugar, and milk into margarine. Place drained peaches to top
of batter. Bake in preheated 350 F oven for 45 mins.

Makes 6 to 8 servings.
