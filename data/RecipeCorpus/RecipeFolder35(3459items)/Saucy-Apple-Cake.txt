Title: Saucy Apple Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Peeled
1/4 c  Walnuts -- chopped
VANlLLA SAUCE:
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Light cream
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Shortening
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Apples -- tart shredded
In a mixing bowl, cream sugar and shortening. Add egg and mix well.
Add the dry ingredients; mix well. Fold in the apples and walnuts.
Spread in a greased 8-in. square baking pan. Bake at 350 deg. for
35-40 minutes or until cake tests done. For sauce, combine sugar,
cornstarch and cream in a saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat;
boil for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat. Add butter and vanilla;
stir until butter is melted. Serve warm over warm cake. Yield: 9
servings
