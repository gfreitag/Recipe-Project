Title: Keftedes [Greek Meat Balls]
Categories: Greek, Meats, Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Mint; fresh, chopped
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Allspice
Salt and fresh ground pepper
Shortening for frying
1 1/2 lb Ground round steak
2    Eggs; lightly beaten
1/2 c  Bread crumbs; fine, soft
2 md Onions; finely chopped
2 tb Parsley; fresh, chopped
Combine all the ingredients except the shortening and mix thoroughly.
Refrigerate for several hours. Shape into small balls and fry in the
melted shortening. Serve hot.

From the New York Times Cook Book by Craig Claiborne.
