Title: French Garlic Dressing
Categories: Condiments, Low cal/fat
Yield: 2 cups
1 ts Mustard
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Garlic, dried, minced
1 1/2 c  Vinaigerette dressing *1
3 tb Chili sauce
2 ts Tamari soy sauce
1 pk Equal
1 ts Horseradish
Place all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth.
