Title: Millet, Nuts and Steamed Dates Pilaf
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Ground mixed walnuts and
-whole almonds (6oz total)
1/3 c  Light brown sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
Heavy cream (opt)
24    Whole dried dates
3 c  Cooked millet (use Method 1)
1/2 ts Almond extract
2 tb Soft butter,
-cut in small pieces
Steam the dates in a vegetable steamer for 5 minutes and set aside.
In a large bowl, mix the hot millet lightly with the almond extract
and butter. In another bowl, combine the nuts, brown sugar and
cinnamon and toss with the millet using two forks. Lightly mound on a
platter and ring the platter with the steamed dates. Serve warm with
a pitcher of cream if desired.
