Title: Dellclous Apple Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
4 md Red Delicious apples --
Peeled & sliced
Pastry for double-crust pie
9    Inches
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Milk
1 tb Cinnamon-sugar
3 tb Water
4 ts Cornstarch
1 ea Egg -- beaten
3/4 c  Light cream
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
In a small bowl, mix water and cornstarch until dissolved. Add egg,
cream, sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg. Place apples in a large bowl; pour
cream mixture over and stir to coat. Line a 9-in. pie pan with the
bottom pastry. Pour apple mixture into crust; dot with butter. Top
with remaining pastry; flute edges and cut slits in fop. Brush with
milk and sprinkle with cinnamon-sugar. bake at 350 deg. for 55
minutes or until golden brown. Yield: 6-8 servings
