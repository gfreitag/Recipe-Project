Title: Cooking Fresh Pumpkin
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 pumpkin  Wash pumpkin and cut in half, crosswise.

Remove seeds and strings, reserving seeds for roasting, if desired.

Place pumpkin in a baking pan face down, shell up, in 1/2-inch of
water. Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour, or until tender. Large
pumpkins may require longer baking time.

Scrape the pulp from the shell and put through a strainer or ricer.

Source:  Carroll's Pumpkin Farm
