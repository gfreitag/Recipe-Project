Title: All in One Macaroni
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Basil
1/8 ts Thyme
2 c  Elbow macaroni
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Granulated sugar
1 lb Ground beef
2 c  Chopped onions
1    Minced garlic
1 tb Cooking oil
1 cn Crushed tomatoes
1 1/2 c  Water
1 tb Soy sauce
In large saucepan brown meat, onions and garlic in cooking oil.  Add
remaining 9 ingredients bring to a boil. Cover and simmer stirring
occasionally until macaroni is tender but firm.  About 15 minutes.
