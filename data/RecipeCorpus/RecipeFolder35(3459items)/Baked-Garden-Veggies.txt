Title: Baked Garden Veggies
Categories: Main dish, Low cal/fat, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Butter
3 tb Parsley, fresh, minced, *5
1/4 ts Salt free seasoning or Spike
1/2 ts Sea salt (optional)
Fresh ground black pepper
1 c  Carrots *1
2 c  Potatoes *2
1 pk Lima beans, frozen
2 lg Zucchini *3
2 c  Cabbage *4
*1 cut into fine matchsticks *2 peeled, quartered, and cut into 1/4
inch slices OR 1 small cauliflower, cut into 1/2 inch florets. *3
quartered and cut into 1 inch cubes. *4 coarsely chopped. (Sub. bok
choy leaves coarse chopped) *5 fresh parsley, minced (optional)
