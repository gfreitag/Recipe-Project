Title: Turkey Sausage Stew
Categories: Sausage, Soup/stew, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
In
1/4 c  Ketchup
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Pepper
1 pk Frozen vegetable medley --
For stew
1 cn Tomato soup, condensed --
Low-fat 10 3/4 oz.
2 c  Water
1 lb Turkey sausage -- smoked 1/4
In a large saucepan, combine vegetables, soup and water; bring to a
boil. Reduce heat. Add remaining ingredients; simmer for 35 to 45
min. or until vegetable are tender. Yield: 8 servings.
