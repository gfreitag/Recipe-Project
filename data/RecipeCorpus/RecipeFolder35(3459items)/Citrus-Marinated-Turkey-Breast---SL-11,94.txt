Title: Citrus-Marinated Turkey Breast - SL 11/94
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 8 servings
2 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
2 ts Dried oregano
Garnishes: fresh sage,
-oregano, & rosemary sprigs
6 lb To 7 pound turkey breast
1 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lime juice
1/4 c  Olive oil
3 tb Apple cider vinegar
Remove and discard skin and breast bones from turkey.  Place
breast halves in a jumbo, heavy-duty zip-top plastic bag; set aside.
Combine orange juice and next 6 ingredients in a jar; close
tightly, and shake vigorously. Set 1/2 cup marinade aside.  Pour
remainder over turkey; seal bag.  Chill 8 hours, turning bag
occasionally.
Remove turkey from marinade; discard marinade.
Cook turkey, covered with grill lid, over hot coals (400 degrees F
to 500 degrees F) 18 minutes on each side or until meat thermometer
registers 170 degrees F, brushing occasionally with [the 1/2 cup]
marinade.  Let stand 10 minutes before slicing.   Yield: 8 servings.

Note: to cook turkey in oven, place on a rack in a roasting pan; bake
at 325F for 1 hour and 10 minutes or until a meat thermometer
registers 170F, brushing occasionally with reserved marinade.

Carolyn Gaskins of North Carolina, in November, 1994 "Southern Living"
