Title: Grandma's Apple Roll
Categories: Pastry, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
4 c  Diced apples
Cinnamon and nutmeg
2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Lard
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
2/3 c  Milk
Flour
Mix together lard, baking powder, salt and milk.  Add enough flour to
mixture to make a soft dough.  Roll dough out in oblong shape.
Sprinkle apples over dough.  Sprinkle cinnamon and nutmeg over apples
as desired. Roll dough and apples up as for a jellyroll.  Cut into 1
1/2 inch slices and place in a greased 13 x 9 inch pan.  Mix sugar
and water together to make syrup and pour over slices.  Bake at 375
degrees for 45 minutes.
