Title: Gabriel's Waffle House Blue Cheese Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Route 66, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
4 oz Blue Cheese
1    Bottle Catsup (8 Oz)
1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Cider Vinegar
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
Combine catsup, sugar, vinegar, garlic powder, and Worcestershire
sauce. Blend with mixer, blender or food processor. Add vegetable oil
slowly, beating until smooth. Crumble blue cheese and stir into
dressing. Makes 1 1/2 cups.
