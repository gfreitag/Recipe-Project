Title: Sour Cream Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
4    Eggs
1/3 c  Oil
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 pk Instant lemon pudding
1 c  Sour cream
Combine all ingredients in large mixing bowl.  Blend on low speed to
moisten.  Then beat 4 minutes at medium speed.  Pour into prepared
pans and bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes.
