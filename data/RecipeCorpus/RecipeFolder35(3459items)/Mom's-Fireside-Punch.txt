Title: Mom's Fireside Punch
Categories: Beverages, Holiday, Alcohol
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Peach schnapps
1 c  Rum
Fresh lemon peel for garnish
6 c  Apple cider
1 cn Frozen lemonade concentrate;
.  12oz
1 c  Granulated sugar
In a Dutch oven, combine the first three ingredients, bring to a boil.
Remove from the heat; stir in schnapps and rum.  Serve hot in mugs,
garnished with lemon peel.

Makes about twelve 6oz servings.

Source:  A pamphlet Mom received in the mail.
