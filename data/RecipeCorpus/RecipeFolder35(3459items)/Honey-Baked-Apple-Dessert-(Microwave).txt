Title: Honey-Baked Apple Dessert (Microwave)
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Microwave
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ds Ground cloves
Vanilla ice cream or whipped
-topping
3 lg Unpeeled cooking apples
1/4 c  Light brown sugar; firmly
-packed
1 tb Pecans; chopped
3 tb Honey
1 tb Brandy
Cut a 1/4" slice from top of apples; core each apple to within
1/8" of bottom.  Place each apple in a 10-ounce custard cup; set
aside.
Combine brown sugar and next 6 ingredients; stir well.  Spoon into
apple cavities, drizzling excess over apples.  Cover each cup tightly
with heavy-duty plastic wrap; fold back an edge of wrap to allow
steam to escape.  Arrange cups in a triangle in microwave.  Microwave
at HIGH 4 to 7 minutes or until almost tender, rotating triangle at
2-minute intervals. Top with vanilla ice cream or whipped topping.
Yield: 3 servings.
