Title: Eggplant Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Black olives, sliced, pitted
2 ts Frutcose, granulated
1 ts Oregano, dried
1/2 ts Basil, dried
1 tb Soy sauce
1/8 ts Black pepper
28 oz Tomato Puree
3 c  Eggplant, peeled and cubed
1 md Onion, chopped
4 1/2 oz Mushrooms, canned, drained
1 ts Garlic, minced
1/3 c  Burgundy
Place all ingredients in sauce pan and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat
to medium and cook for 30 minutes or until sauce is of desired
consistency. Serve over pasta.
