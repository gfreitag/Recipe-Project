Title: Mlnl Apple Plzzas
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Peeled, cored and shredded
1 c  Cheddar cheese
4 oz Shredded optional
Refrigerator biscuits -- 10
Biscuits
1    Tube
-
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
2 tb All-purpose flour
1 ts Ground cinnamon
2 ea Cooking apples
Roll or pat biscuits into 3-1/2-in. circles: place on a lightly
greased baking sheet. In a mixing bowl, combine brown sugar, flour
and cinnamon; mix well. Add apples and mix well; spoon rounded
tablespoonfuls onto biscuits. Bake at 350 deg. for 15-20 minutes or
until edges begin to brown. If desired, sprinkle each pizza with 1
tablespoon cheese. Serve warm. Yield: 10 servings
