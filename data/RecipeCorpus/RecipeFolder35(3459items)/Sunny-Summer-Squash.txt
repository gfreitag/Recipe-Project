Title: Sunny Summer Squash
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Oregano, minced or
1/4 ts Dried oregano
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/2 c  Sliced green onions
2 ts Reduced-calorie margarine
2 1/2 c  Sliced yellow squash
-(abt 2 medium)
Combine the green onion and margarine in a 2-quart casserole. Cover
and microwave on high 2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute. Add the
remaining ingredients and stir well. Cover and microwave on high 4-5
minutes or until squash is tender, stirring after 2 minutes.
