Title: Spiced Shortbread Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 30 servings
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground ginger
2 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Margarine, softened
2/3 c  Sifted powdered sugar
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
Cream butter; gradually add sugar, beating at medium speed of an
electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add spices, and beat well.
Stir in flour. Dough will be stiff. Shape dough into 1 1$ inch balls,
and place 2 inches apart on lightly greased cookie sheets. Lightly
press cookies with a floured cookie stamp or fork to flatten to 1/4
inch thickness. Bake at 325 for 15 to 18 minutes or until done. Let
cool on wire racks. 2 1/4 dozen. Submitted By JULIE GLEITSMANN   On
11-01-95
