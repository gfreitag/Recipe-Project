Title: Rice's Dining Salon Turkey Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Route 66
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Turkey Broth
2    Eggs -- well-beaten
2 ts Sage
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
Additional Broth As Needed
4 c  Cornbread -- crumbled
4 c  Lightly Toasted Bread --
Cubed
1 lg Onion -- chopped
1 c  Celery -- chopped
1 lg Dill Pickle -- diced
Combine cornbread and bread in large mixing bowl. In medium saucepan,
add onion, celery, and dill pickle to turkey broth. Bring to a boil,
cooking until barely tender. Cool slightly and pour over bread
mixture. Add beaten eggs, sage, salt and pepper, and enough
additional broth to make a slightly moist mixture. Pour dressing in a
greased 9 x11 baking dish. Bake in preheated 350 F oven for 30 mins.
Makes 8 to 10 servings.
