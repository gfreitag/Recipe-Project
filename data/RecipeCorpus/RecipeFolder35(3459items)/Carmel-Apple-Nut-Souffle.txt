Title: Carmel Apple Nut Souffle
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 10 servings
Chopped
2 tb Butter Or Margarine
1 1/2 ts Vanilla Extract
1 1/2 c  Heavy Cream -- whipped
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
2    Env Unflavored Gelatin
10 tb Brown Sugar -- divided
3    Eggs -- separated
3 c  Milk
2    Apples, Peeled, Cored &
In a medium-sized saucepan, mix gelatin with 8 Tbsp sugar; blend in
egg yolks beaten with milk. Let stand 1 min. Place over low heat and
stir until gelatin is completely dissolved, about 5 mins. Add apples,
butter and vanilla; continue cooking, stirring constantly, 5 mins or
until apples tender. Pour into large bowl and chill, stirring
occasionally, until mixture mounds slightly when dropped from spoon.
In a medium-sized bowl, beat egg whites until soft peaks form;
gradually add remaining 2 Tbsp sugar and beat until stiff. Fold egg
whites, then whipped cream and pecans into gelatin mixture. Turn into
a 1-qt souffle dish with a 3-inch collar or a 2-qt bowl; chill until
set. Remove collar; garnish, if desired, with additional apples and
pecans.
