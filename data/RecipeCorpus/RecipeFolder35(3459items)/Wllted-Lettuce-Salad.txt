Title: Wllted Lettuce Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2    Teapoons
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1    Hard-cooked egg -- chopped
Lemon juice
8 c  Torn leaf lettuce -- or
Spinach
1/4 c  Sliced green onions
Pepper to taste
3    Bacon -- diced
1 tb White wine vinegar
Place;lettuce or spinach and onions in a large Salad bowl. Sprinkle
with pepper; :set aside. In a large skillet, cook bacon Until crisp.
Do not drain off drippings. Stir in vinegar, lemon juice, sugar and
salt. Pour over lettuce and toss gently until Well coated. Top with
hard-cooked egg. Serve immediately. Yield: 6 servings
