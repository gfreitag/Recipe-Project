Title: Oyster Sauce for Baked Fish
Categories: Game, Fish/seafoo, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Onion, minced
1/2 pt Oysters, drained
1/2 c  Cream
1 x  Salt & pepper to taste
1 ts Parsley, minced
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1 pt Milk and oyster liquor
Parboil the oysters. Mix oyster liquor and milk. Cook flour in butter
to make a roux. Add oyster liquor and milk. When smooth add cream.
Stir in oysters, season and serve hot. Use as hot sauce for baked
black drum, redfish, sheepshead. Source: FISHES AND FISHING IN
LOUISIANA, pub 1933 Recipe date: 01/17/33
