Title: Tangy Corn
Categories: Vegetables, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Corn -- cream-style 15 oz
3 tb Barbecue sauce -- bottled
1 cn Corn, whole kernel, canned
16 oz Drained
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan; cook over med. heat for 5 min,
or until heated through. Yield 4 to 6 serving.
