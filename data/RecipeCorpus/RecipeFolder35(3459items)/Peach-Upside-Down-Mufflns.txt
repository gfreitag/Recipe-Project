Title: Peach Upside-Down Mufflns
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Milk
6 tb Butter or margarine
1 c  Packed brown sugar -- plus 2
Tbs
3 c  Ripe peaches -- sliced
Peeled
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Shortening -- melted
2 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar. baking powder and salt. Add
shortening, eggs and milk; mix until smooth. In the bottom of 18
greased muffin cups, place 1 teaspoon of butter and 1 tablespoon
brown sugar. Place in a 375 deg oven for 5 minutes. Arrange peaches
in the muffin cups. Fill each half full with batter. Bake at 375 deg
for 25 minutes or until browned. Turn out of pans immediately. Yield:
1-1/2 dozen
