Title: German Cucumber Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Mustard -- prepared
2 tb Fresh dill -- minced
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Tomatoes -- sliced
2 md Cucumber -- thinly sliced
4 ea Green onion -- thinly
Sliced
3    Scoops
2 tb Fresh parsley -- snipped
Dressing:
1/4 c  Sour cream
In a bowl, combine cucumbers, onions, tomatoes and parsley. Combine
dressing ingredients; pour over cucumber mixture and toss gently.
cover and chill for at least 1 hr. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
