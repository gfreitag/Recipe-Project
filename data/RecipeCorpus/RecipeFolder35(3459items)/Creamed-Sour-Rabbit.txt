Title: Creamed Sour Rabbit
Categories: Game, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings  "... Now ain't thet somethin'!  Never did know rabbits was like
people - sweet ones an' sour ones.  All I gotta do now is learn how
to tell the diference.  Anyways- here's how to fix 'em...."

Cut rabbit in small pieces and soak overnight in a solution of 1 cup
vinegar and 1/2 cup water.  Next morning cook rabbit in the vinegar
solution until done and thicken the broth with a little flour.  salt
and pepper to taste and serve hot.  Good with bisquits.
