Title: Summer Squash And Potato Saute
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Sliced
Minced fresh persley
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 md Summer Squash -- sliced
2 sm Red potatoes -- thinly
In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Saute squash and potatoes
until tender. Sprinkle with parsley, salt and pepper to taste. Yield:
4 servings
