Title: Pork And Spinach Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Celery -- sliced
1    Jar pimientos -- sliced
Drained
3 tb Olives -- ripe sliced
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1 tb Olive oil
1/2 lb Pork tenderloin -- cut into
Thin strips
10 oz Fresh spinach -- washed &
Stems
1 cn Peas -- rinsed and drained
Black-eyed
1/3 c  Low calorie Italian salad
Dressing
1/4 c  Green onion -- sliced
1/2 c  Fresh mushrooms -- sliced
Line four plates with spinach leaves; set aside. In a bowl, combine
peas, Italian dressing, green onions, mushrooms, celery, pimiento and
olives; set aside. In a a med. skillet, saute garlic in oil for 30
seconds. Add pork and stir-fry for 2 to 3 min. or until no pink
remains. Remove from the heat; add vegetable mixture and mix well.
Divide among spinach-lined plates. Serve immediately. Yield: 4
servings.
