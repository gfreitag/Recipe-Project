Title: Ravioli with Artichoke Sauce+
Categories: Pasta/rice, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
-Swiss, provolone, or
-mozerella cheese (3 oz.)
2 tb Dry white wine or milk
1 cn Artichoke hearts (14 oz.)
-drained and quartered
1/4 c  Pine nuts or slivered
-almonds, toeasted
1 pk Frozen beef or chicken
-ravioli (50 count)
1 c  Mushrooms, fresh and sliced
1 cl Garlic, minced
2 tb Olive or cooking oil
3 tb All-purpose flour
1 2/3 c  Milk
3/4 c  Choice of Gruyere, process
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
-(optional)
Italian flat leafed parsley
Cook Ravioli according to package directions, drain.

Meanwhile, for sauce, in a medium saucepan cook mushrooms and garlic
in hot oil until mushrooms are tender. Stir in flour. Add the 1 2/3
cup milk; cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. Add cheese and
wine or milk; heat and stir until cheese is melted. Add artichoke
hearts and pine nuts or almonds; heat through.

Serve sauce over ravioli. If desired, garnish with parsley.
