Title: Easy Layered Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Thawed
2 c  Salad dressing -- or
Mayonnaise
3 c  Cheddar cheese -- 12 ounces
Shredded
1    Head lettuce
Torn into bite-size pieces
1 c  Green pepper -- diced
1/4 c  Green onions -- sliced
1 pk Frozen peas -- l0 ounces
In a 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. pan, layer lettuce, green pepper, onions
and peas; do not toss. Spread salad dressing over all; sprinkle with
cheese. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Yield: 8-10 servings
