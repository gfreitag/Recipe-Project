Title: Tossed Itallan Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Or zucchini thinly sliced
1 lg Tomato -- cut in wedges
Ltalian salad dressing
1 ea Garlic clove -- peeled
6 c  Assorted salad greens
1/2 ea Cucumber
Rub the inside of a salad bowl with the garlic. Add the greens,
cucumber or zucchini and tomato. Just before serving, toss with
enough salad dressing to coat. Yield: 4 servings
