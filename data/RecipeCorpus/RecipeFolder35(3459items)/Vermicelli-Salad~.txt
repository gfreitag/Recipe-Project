Title: Vermicelli Salad~
Categories: Pasta/rice, Salads, Vegetarian, Low cal/fat
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Caraway seed
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 lb Diced celery
1 bn Green onions -- chopped
1/4 c  Fresh parsley -- chopped
12 oz Vermicelli
1/2 c  Sweet pickle juice
6 oz French salad dressing
2    Garlic cloves -- mashed
1 tb Poppy seeds
1 ts Celery seed
Break vermicelli into 1" pieces, and cook according to package

directions. Mix pickle juice, French dressing, garlic, and seasonings
and pour over warm vermicelli. Chill. One hour before serving, add
celery, green onions and parsley.

Per serving:  Calories 177; fat 6.4 g; cholesterol 8 mg; carbohydrate
27.6 g; fiber 1 g; protein 3.3 g; sodium 304 mg; potassium 140 mg;
calcium 48 mg.
