Title: Blockbuster Brownies
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 servings
3 c  Granulated sugar
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 tb Vanilla
1 c  Chopped walnuts
8 ea Squares unsweetened
Chocolate
1 1/2 c  Butter or margerine
6 ea Eggs
Melt chocolate and butter or margerine over hot water or in microwave
on medium 4 minutes.  Cool.  Beat eggs until lemon coloured.
Gradually add sugar, beating until thick about 3 minutes.  Stir in
chocolate mixture, fold in flour, vanilla and nuts.  Pour into two
greased 2 l square pans. Bake at 350 for 35 to 40 minutes.
Toppings:  Sprinkle with chopped nuts and semi sweet chocolate chips
before baking.  Sprinkle cool Brownies with icing sugar
Glaze:  Melt 1 square of unsweetened chocolate with 1 tb of butter
and 1/4 cup of milk, blend until smooth.  Add 1 1/4 cup of icing
sugar, blend well.  Spread over pan cooled brownies.
Rocky Road:  Sprinkle 2 cups miniature marshmallows over 1 pan of
brownies.  Broil under preheated broiler until golden brown.  Drizzle
with 1 square melted semi sweet chocolate.
From Bakers Chocolate Square box
