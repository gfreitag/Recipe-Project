Title: Honey-Curry Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Poultry
Yield: 4 -6 serving
-melted
6    To 8 Cooked skinless chiken
-breast halves or legs  or
12    To 14 cooked skinless thighs
-or drumsticks
2/3 c  Liquid honey
1/2 c  Dijon mustard
1 1/2 ts Curry Powder, (or to
-taste)
1/2 c  Butter or margarine,
Instructions:

In a large bowl, combine first 4 ingredients; whisk well to blend.
Heat in saucepan over medium heat until heated through or microwave
at 50% (MEDIUM) power several minutes until heated.  Pour over hot
cooked chicken pieces and serve.
