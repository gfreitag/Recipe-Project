Title: Poppy Seed French Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 tb Poppy seeds
8 sl French bread slice -- 1 in.
Thick
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
Combine butter, Parmesan cheese and poppy seeds; spread on both sides
of each piece of bread. Place on a baking sheet. Bake  at 350 deg.
for 12 -16 min. turning once. Yield : 6-8 serving.
