Title: Baked Rice Custard
Categories: Puddings
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Cooked rice
3 tb Raisins
3    Eggs,well beaten
1 1/2 c  Skim milk
2 tb Liquid sucaryl
1/4 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 350 degrees,Blend together the eggs,skim
milk,sucaryl,salt and vanilla.Stir in cooked rice and raisins.Pour
into a 4 cup casserole.Place casserole in a pan of hot
water.Bake,uncovered,60 minutes stirring after one half hour of
baking.Serve hot.

Per serving: Calories 98 Protein 6g Fat 3g Carbohydrates 12.5g (with
sugar 170 calories)

1 serving = 1 bread exchange
= 1/2 meat exchange
