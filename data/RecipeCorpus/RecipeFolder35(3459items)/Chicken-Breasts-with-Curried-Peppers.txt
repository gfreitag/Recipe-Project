Title: Chicken Breasts with Curried Peppers
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Pitted prunes
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 md Sweet red pepper; cut into
-strips
1 md Green pepper; cut in strips
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 cl Garlic; crushed
1 ts Curry powder
4 ea Chicken breast halves;
-skinned and boned
1/2 c  Dry white wine
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat; stir in garlic and
curry powder.  Add chicken, and cook 5 minutes on each side.  Add
wine, prunes, salt, and pepper; cover and simmer over low heat 15 to
20 minutes. Remove chicken to a serving platter; keep warm.
Add peppers to skillet; cook 3 to 4 minutes or until crisp-tender;
stirring occasionally.  Spoon over chicken.   Yield: 4 servings
