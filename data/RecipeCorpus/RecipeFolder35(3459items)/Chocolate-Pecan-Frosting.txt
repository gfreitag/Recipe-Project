Title: Chocolate-Pecan Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
4    Egg yolks
1 1/2 c  Flaked coconut
1 1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Evaporated milk
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Plus 2 tb butter or
Margarine
Combine first four ingredients in a heavy saucepan; bring to a boil
and cool over medium heat 12 minutes stirring constantly.  Add
Coconuts, pecans and vanilla; stir until cool and thick enough to
spread.

from Chocolate Fantasies by Oxmoor House
