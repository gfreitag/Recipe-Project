Title: New Log Cabin Marinated Pork Chops
Categories: Main dish, Route 66, Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------MARINADE AND CHOPS----------------------------
1 1/2 ts Molasses
1 1/2 ts Salt
6    To 8 Pork Chops, Thick-Cut
1 c  Soy Sauce
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
MMMMM--------------------BARBECUE SAUCE------------------------------
7 oz Ketchup
6 oz Chili Sauce
1/4 c  Brown Sugar
1 1/2 ts Dry Hot Mustard
3 tb Water
COMBINE all ingredients for marinade and bring to a boil. let cool.
Place 6-8 thick-cut pork chops in pan and pour marinating sauce over
chops. Cover and let stand overnight in refrigerator. The next day,
take pork chops out of marinade, place in a single layer in a baking
pan and cover tightly with foil. Bake in a preheated 375 F oven until
tender, about 2 hours.

FOR BARBECUE SAUCE, combine sugar and mustard with water. Blend until
smooth. Combine with ketchup and chili sauce in saucepan and bring to
a boil. When chops are tender, remove from oven and dip in barbecue
sauce. Return to uncovered baking pan and continue cooking in a 350 F
oven until lightly glazed, about 30 mins. Serve hot.

Makes 6 to 8 marinated pork chops.
