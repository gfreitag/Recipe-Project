Title: Herbs De Provence Spice Mix 2
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------COMBINE-------------------------------
1/2 ts Dried sage and
1/2 ts Fennel seeds.
----------------
Makes 3/4 cup
3 tb Dried marjoram,
3 tb Dried thyme,
3 tb Dried savory,
1 ts Dried basil,
1 ts Dried rosemary,
Mix well and spoon into small jars. Use to season chicken, vegetables
or meat.
