Title: Asparagus Sandwich
Categories: Vegetables, Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Egg separated
1 tb Mayonnaise
2 sl White bread
2 sl Cheese
12 ea Cooked asparagus spears
Put toast on glass plate.  Layer with cheese and asparagus.   Mix
yolk with mayonnaise.  Beat white until stiff and fold into yolk
mixture. Spread over asparagus.  Microwave on high 2 minutes rotating
dish twice during cooking. From 365 Microwave Calendar 1990
