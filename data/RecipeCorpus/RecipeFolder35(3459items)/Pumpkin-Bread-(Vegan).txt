Title: Pumpkin Bread (Vegan)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Soda
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Cloves; scant
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Canned pumpkin
1/2 c  Applesauce
1/2 c  Water
Egg substitute equivalent
-to 2 eggs
1 2/3 c  Flour
Combine first 5 ingredients in a small bowl and beat well.
In a separate bowl, combine remaining ingredients and mix well.
Gradually add to pumpkin mixture and beat well.

Use a nonstick 9x5 loaf pan (or a non teflon pan very lightly sprayed
with canola oil).  Pour batter into loaf pan and bake at 350 for
65-70 mins or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

Cool 10 mins in pan before removing to wire rack.
