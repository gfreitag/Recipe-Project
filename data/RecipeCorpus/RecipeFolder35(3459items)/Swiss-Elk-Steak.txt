Title: Swiss Elk Steak
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Ripe olives -- 2 1/4 oz
Sliced/drai
1 c  Fresh mushrooms -- sliced
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
4 sl Swiss cheese -- optional
Cooked noodles
2 lb Elk steak
All-purpose flour
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 15 oz
1/2 c  Red wine -- or beef broth
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Onion -- diced
1/2 c  Green pepper -- diced
Dredge elk steak lightly in flour; shake off excess. Melt butter in a
large skillet; brown steak on both sides. Place in a shallow baking
pan. Combine the next nine ingredients; pour over steak. Cover and
bake at 350 deg. for 1-1/2 hours or until cooked to desired
tenderness. If desired, place cheese over steak before serving. Serve
over noodles. Yield: 4 servings
