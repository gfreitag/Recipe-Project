Title: Amarillo Double Chocolate Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1 1/2 c  Water
12 oz Package chocolate chips
Chocolate icing (opt)
1 pk Yellow cake mix
2 sm Packages instant chocolate
Pudding
4    Eggs, beaten
Beat together first five ingredients until well blended. Fold in
chocolate chips. Bake in a 9 x 13 inch pan for one hour at 350
degrees F. Frost when cool.
