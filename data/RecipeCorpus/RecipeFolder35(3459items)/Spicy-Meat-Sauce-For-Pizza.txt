Title: Spicy Meat Sauce For Pizza
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
-chopped
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1    Bay leaf
1 ts Black pepper
8 oz Italian sausage
28 oz Tomato puree -- italian
-style
2 ts Hot chili peppers -- finely
Saute the sausage meat in a skillet over medium heat until browned,
breaking it up with a fork as it cooks.  Drain and set aside.
Combine the remaining ingredients in a 3 qt saucepan, then stir in
the reserved sausage.   Cover, and bring to a boil.  Uncover, lower
the heat, and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Adjust
the seasonings to taste. May be frozen if using fresh sausage.
