Title: Double Chocolate Chews~
Categories: Healthy, Cake/cookie
Yield: 32 servings
1 ts Pure vanilla extract
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Dutch-processed cocoa
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Mint chocolate chips
Nonstick cooking spray
Total time:  28 minutes
4 tb Unsalted butter -- softened
4 tb Nonfat cream cheese -- (1/4
-cup)
1/3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Dark brown sugar -- firmly
-packed
Prep Time:  20 minutes

Preheat oven to 350~. Spray 2 sheetpans with nonstick cooking spray.
In a medium mixing bowl, cream together butter, cream cheese, sugar
and brown sugar. Beat in vanilla. Into a small bowl, sift flour,
cocoa and salt. Blend dry ingredients into butter mixture. Stir in
chocolate chips. Divide dough into quarters and taking one-eighth of
a quarter, roll it between the palms of two hands to form a ball and
place it on a sheetpan. Repeat to form 32 dough nuggets. Using the
bottom of a glass, press the nuggets into 2-inch rounds. Bake for 8
minutes, until slightly puffed and very soft to the touch. Let cool
on the pans for 1 minute before transferring to racks to cool
completely. Makes 32 2-inch cookies.

Per cookie:  Calories 63; fat 2 g (29%); sat fat 1 g; carbohydrate 10
g; protein 1 g; cholesterol 4 mg; sodium 31 mg.
