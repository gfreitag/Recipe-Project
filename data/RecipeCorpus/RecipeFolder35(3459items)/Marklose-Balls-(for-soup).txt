Title: Marklose Balls (for soup)
Categories: Breads, Soup/stew, Penndutch
Yield: 1 batch
1 ts Onion; grated
Pepper and salt to taste
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
2 tb Beef marrow (from uncooked
- beef soup bones)
2    Eggs
Cut marrow fine, add eggs, onion, salt and pepper, and mix well.
Stiffen with bread crumbs and shape into balls the size of marbles.

Drop into hot broth or soup and cook uncovered 15 to 20 minutes.

From "Cooking With the Pennsylvania Dutch", A. Monroe Aurand, Jr.
(ed.), 1946
