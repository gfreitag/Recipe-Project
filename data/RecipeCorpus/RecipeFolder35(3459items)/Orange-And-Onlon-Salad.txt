Title: Orange And Onlon Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Mandarin oranges -- 11
oz Drained
Sliced almonds
Bottled poppy seed dressing
1    Head Boston lettuce
Separated into leaves
1 md Red onion
Thinly sliced into rings
Arrange lettuce leaves, onion and oranges on salad plates. Chill. Just
before serving, sprinkle with almonds. Serve with poppy seed dressing.
Yield: 4-6 servings
