Title: Creamy Sweet Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Peas
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
Or sweet red pepper
1/2 c  Green onions -- sliced
1 bn Fresh spinach
3 ea Bacon strips -- cooked &
Crumbled
2 c  Mayonnaise
1/3 c  Buttermilk
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 ts Sugar
3 lb Sweet potatoes
Cooked, peeled and cubed
In a bowl, blend mayonnaise, buttermilk, lemon juice, mustard and
sugar until smooth. In a large bowl, combine sweet potatoes, peas,
pepper and onions. Pour dressing over all; toss to blend. Chill 2
hours or overnight. To serve, spoon salad into a large bowl lined
with spinach; top with bacon. Yield: 12-16 servings
