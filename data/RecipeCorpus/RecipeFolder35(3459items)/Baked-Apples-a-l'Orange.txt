Title: Baked Apples a l'Orange
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Holiday
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea (4-inch) stick cinnamon;
-broken in half
1/3 c  Reduced-calorie orange
-marmalade
6 sm Gingersnap cookies; crushed
6 md Cooking apples
1/4 c  Unsweetened apple juice
1/4 c  ;water
1 tb Lemon juice
2 ea Whole cloves
Core apples to within 1/2 inch from bottom; peel top third of each
apple.  Place apples in a 12" x 8" x 2" baking dish; add apple
juiceand next 4 ingredients.  Bake, uncovered, at 350F for 30
minutes, basting often with cooking liquid.  Remove apples from oven;
spoon equal amounts of marmalade into center of each apple, and
sprinkle with gingersnap crumbs. Bake an additional 5 minutes or
until apples are done.

Yield: 6 servings. (127 calories per serving.)

:0.9g protein, 1.9g fat, 29g carbohydrate, 3mg cholesterol, 14mg
sodium, and 36mg calcium
