Title: Beans And Things Chili
Categories: Chili, Route 66
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Red Chili Pepper -- crushed
1 cn (10 Oz) Rotel Tomatoes
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Ground Cumin
1 ts Salt
1 tb Paprika
4 lb Coarse Ground Beef
1 tb Garlic -- chopped
1    Onion -- chopped
1 tb Chili Powder
1 ts Red Pepper
1 ts Black Pepper
Braise meat until browned. Add garlic and onions and continue cooking
until onions are clear. Add remaining ingredients and cover with
about 2 inches of water. Cook until meat feels heavy on the bottom,
about 2 hours. Makes 12 to 16 servings.
