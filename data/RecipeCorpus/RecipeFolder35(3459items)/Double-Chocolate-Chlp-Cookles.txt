Title: Double Chocolate Chlp Cookles
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Milk
1 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Baking powder
1 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips --
6    Ounces
1 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Packed dark brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 ea Egg
1/3 c  Baking cocoa
In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter, sugars and vanilla. Beat in
egg. Add cocoa and milk. Combine flour and baking powder; fold into
creamed mixture with walnuts and chocolate chips. Roll teaspoonfuls
of dough into balls; place 2 in. apart on ungreased baking sheets.
Bake at 350 deg. for 10-12 minutes. Cool for 5 minutes before
removing to wire racks to cool. Yield: 3-4 dozen
