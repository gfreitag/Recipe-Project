Title: Banana Brunch Punch
Categories: Beverages, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
2 c  Sugar -- divided
1 cn Pineapple juice -- 46 oz
3    Bottles Lemon-lime soda, 2
-liters each
Orange slice -- optional
6 md Banana -- ripe
1 cn Frozen orange juice
Concentrate -- 12 oz thawed
1 cn Lemonade, frozen concentrate
6 oz Thawed
3 c  Warm water -- divided
In a blender or food processor, blend bananas, orange juice and
lemonade until smooth. Remove half of the mixture and set aside. Add
1 1/2 cup of warm water and 1 cup sugar to mixture in blender; blend
until smooth. Place in a large freezer container. Repeat with
remaining banana mixture, water and sugar; add to container. Cover
and freeze until solid. One hour before serving, take punch base out
of freeze. Just before serving, place in a large punch bowl. Add
pineapple juice and soda; stir until well blended. Garnish with
orange slices if desired. Yield: 16 to 20 servings. (3 quarts).
