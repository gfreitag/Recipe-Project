Title: Bacon-Avocado Potato Salad~
Categories: Potatoes, Vegetables, Salads, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
Salt
Black pepper
Paprika
1/4 ts Mustard powder
2 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
1 tb Fresh cilantro -- chopped
6 md Boiling potatoes
2    Avocados -- cubed
8 sl Bacon
1/2 c  Chopped onions -- chopped
1 tb Fresh lime juice
1/2 c  White wine
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
Boil potatoes in their skins. While potatoes boil, cube avocados and
toss with lime juice. Chop bacon into one inch pieces and fry until
crisp in a large skillet. Remove bacon to paper towels to drain. In
bacon fat, saute onions until golden. Remove pan from heat and stir
in wine, vinegar, mustard, and salt, pepper, and paprika to taste.

When potatoes are tender, drain, peel, and dice. While still warm,
pour dressing over potatoes and toss. Allow to cool to room
temperature.

Fold in avocado, bacon, parsley, and cilantro. Serve at room
temperature or chill one hour or longer.

Per serving:  Calories 201; fat 11 g; cholesterol 5 mg; carbohydrate
20.8 g; fiber 4.6 g; protein 4.9 g; sodium 114 mg; potassium 846 mg;
calcium 23 mg.
