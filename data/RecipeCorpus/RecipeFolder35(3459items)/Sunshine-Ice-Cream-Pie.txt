Title: Sunshine Ice Cream Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Whipped topping
1 cn Mandarin oranges -- drained
11    Oz
2 tb Coconut -- toasted
1 pt Vanilla ice cream --
Softened
1 ea Graham cracker pie crust, 9
Inch
1 pt Orange sherbet -- softened
Spread ice cream into the crust; spread sherbert over ice cream.
Freeze for at least 3 hours. top with whipped topping. Cover and
freeze. Thirty min. before serving, remove pie from the freezer;
arrange on top sprinkle with coconut. Yield 6-8 servings.
