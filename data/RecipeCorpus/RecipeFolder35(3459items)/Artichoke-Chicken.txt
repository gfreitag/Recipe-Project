Title: Artichoke Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Dried rosemary
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Chicken broth
Or 1 cup broth & 1 cup dry
White wine
Cooked noodles
Chopped fresh parsley
Artichoke hearts
8 ea Skinless boneless chicken
Breast halves
2 tb Butter or margarine
2    Jars
(6 ounces each) marinated
Drained
1    Jar whole mushrooms -- 4-1/2
oz Drained
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1/3 c  All-purpose flour
In a skillet, brown chicken in butter. Remove chicken to an ungreased
13- n. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish; do not drain pan juices. Cut the
artichokes into quarters. Arrange artichokes and mushrooms on top of
chicken; set aside. Saute onion in pan juices; blend in flour,
rosemary, salt and pepper. Add chicken broth; cook until thickened
and bubbly. Remove from the heat and spoon over chicken. Cover and
bake at 350 deg. for 50-60 minutes or until chicken is tender. Place
noodles on serving platter; top with chicken and sauce. Sprinkle with
parsley. Yield: 8 servings
