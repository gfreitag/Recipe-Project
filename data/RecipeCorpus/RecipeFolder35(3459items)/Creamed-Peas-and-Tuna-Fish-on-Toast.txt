Title: Creamed Peas and Tuna Fish on Toast
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 cn Tuna fish, drained
1 c  Peas
6 sl Toast
2 tb Margarine or butter
2 tb All purpose flour
In a 10" fry pan, melt margarine.
Stir in flour.
Add milk all at once.
Stir until flour and margarine is mixed in.
Add tuna and peas.
Cook until sauce thickens.
Serve over toast.
