Title: Scalloped Plneapple Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
4 c  White bread
Firmly packed cubed (crusts
Removed)
3/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 1/4 c  Sugar
3 ea Eggs
1 cn Crushed pineapple -- 20 oz
Well drained
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time,
beating well after each addition. Stir in the pineapple and lemon
juice. Gently fold in bread cubes. Spoon into a greased 2-qt. baking
dish. Bake, uncovered, at 350 deg. for 40-45 minutes or until top is
lightly golden. Serve warm. Yield: 6 servings
