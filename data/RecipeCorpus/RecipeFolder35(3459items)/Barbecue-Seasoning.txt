Title: Barbecue Seasoning
Categories: Condiments, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 cup
2 tb Pepper, black
2 tb Chili powder
4 tb Paprika
2 tb Salt
4 tb Sugar, granulated
2 tb Cumin; ground
Combine all ingredients, mix thoroughly. Place in jar with tight
fitting lid, use as desired.
From "Brochure 010008 10/92" National Pork Producers Council
