Title: Bratwurst and Cabbage
Categories: Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Shredded carrot
1/3 c  German mustard
1 lb Bratwurst cut into 1/2 inch
Pieces
1 c  Shredded cabbage
Combine all ingredients in glass bowl.  Microwave on high 3 to 4
minutes, stirring once during cooking
