Title: Red Cabbage Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
1 ts Salt
1 md Apples -- or 2 chopped
1/4 c  Red currants -- jelly
Lemon slices -- optional
1 tb Shortening
8 c  Shredded red cabbage
1 md Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Lemon juice -- or vinegar
1/4 c  Sugar
In a dutch oven melt shortening. add the cabbage, onion, lemon juice
or vinegar, sugar and salt; mix well. Cover and cook over medium heat
for 10 to 15 min. or until cabbage is crisp-tender, stirring
occasionally. Add apples; cook 10 to 15 min. more or until cabbage
and apples are tender. Stir in jelly until melted. Garnish with lemon
slices if desired. Yield: 8 to 10 servings.
