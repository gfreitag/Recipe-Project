Title: Terlyakl Chlcken
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Garlic powder
12 ea Chicken drumsticks -- abt
2 1/2    Pounds
3/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
3 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Optional
In a large glass dish, combine the soy sauce, oil, brown sugar,
cooking sherry if desired, ginger and garlic powder; mix well. Add
drumsticks; turn to coat. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour or
overnight, turning occasionally. Drain and discard marinade. Place
chicken in a single layer on a foil-lined baking sheet. Bake at 375
deg. for 35-45 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink. Yield: 6
servings
