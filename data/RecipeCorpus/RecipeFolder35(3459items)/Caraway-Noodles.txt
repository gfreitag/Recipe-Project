Title: Caraway Noodles
Categories: Side dish, Pasta/rice
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Caraway seeds
1/8 ts Salt
2 c  Uncooked wide egg noodles
2 tb Reduced-calorie
-margarine, melted
Cook egg noodles according to package directions, omitting salt and
fat; drain. Add melted margarine, lemon juice, caraway seeds and salt
to the noodles; toss well.
