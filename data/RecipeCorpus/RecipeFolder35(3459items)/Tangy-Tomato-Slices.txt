Title: Tangy Tomato Slices
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Spoon garlic powder
1 md Sweet onion -- thinly
Siiced
6 lg Tomatoes -- thinly sliced
1 c  Vegetable oil
1/3 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Fresh parsley -- minced
3 tb Fresh basil -- minced
Or 1 tablespoon dried basil
1 tb Sugar
In a small bowl or a jar with a tight-fitting lid, mix the first nine
ingredients. Layer onion and tomatoes in a shallow glass dish. Pour
the marinade over; cover and refrigerate for several hours. Yield: 12
servings
