Title: Creamy Nutella Cheesecake
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
Makes 12 slices
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1/3 c  Granulated sugar
1 1/4 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/3 c  Margarine, melted
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Vanilla extract
2 ea 8oz packages cream cheese,
-softened
1 c  Nutella
MMMMM-------------------------FOR CRUST------------------------------
In a small bowl, stir crumbs, melted margarine and sugar. Into 8 1/2
inch springform pan, press crust mixture acrosss bottom and 1 1/2 in
up sides. Set aside.
For Filling:
With electric mixer on medium, beat cream cheese and sugar until
smooth. Pour into prepared crust and chill at least 4 hours. Run
sharp knife around edge to loosen before serving.
