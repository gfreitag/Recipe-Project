Title: Hoosier Goulash
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Soup/stew
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Basil
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Garlic clove, crushed
1 c  Dry elbow macaroni
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion, chopped
1 cn Tomatoes, cut up (16 oz.)
1 cn Tomato paste (6 oz.)
1 cn Tomato sauce (8 oz.)
1 ts Sugar
Place a plastic colander over a bowl or baking dish.  Crumble meat in
colander.  Add onion and microwave at full power for 6 minutes,
stirring once during cooking.  Remove from microwave and crumble.
Place hamburger and onions in a large casserole that has been sprayed
with Pam. Add remaining ingredients including dry macaroni.  Cover
and cook on full power for 10 minutes, stirring twice during cooking.
