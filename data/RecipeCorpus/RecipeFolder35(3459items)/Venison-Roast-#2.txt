Title: Venison Roast #2
Categories: Main dish, Game
Yield: 1 roast
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Chopped onion
2 c  Water (boiling)
Slab of venison about 2 in
Thick
4 ts Bacon fat
1/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Chopped celery
Lay venison on board and pound flour into it.  Then melt fat in very
large frying pan and brown roast on it.  Add all of the seasoning and
1/2 the water.  Cover and let simmer for 55 minutes.  Then pour in
the rest of the water and simmer until done to taste.
