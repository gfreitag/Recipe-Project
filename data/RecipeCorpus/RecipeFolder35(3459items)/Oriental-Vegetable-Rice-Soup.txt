Title: Oriental Vegetable Rice Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables, Tomatoes, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
Makes 6 servings
MMMMM-------------------PREP TIME: 15 MINUTES------------------------
MMMMM-------------------COOK TIME: 10 MINUTES------------------------
1/4 ts Hot pepper flakes
4 c  Chicken stock
1 ea 28oz can tomatoes, diced
1/4 c  Soy sauce (or to taste)
2/3 c  Minute rice (or cooked,
-cooled regular rice)
1 ea Tbps sesame seeds
1 tb Vegetable oil
2 ea Carrots, thinly sliced on
-the diagonal
1 c  Snow peas, ends trimmed and
-then halved
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
1 ts Ground ginger
Cook carrots, snow peas and garlic until softened.  Stir in the
ginger and the red pepper flakes and continue to cook for about 1
minute.  Stir in the stock, tomatoes and soy sauce and then bring to
boil.  Stir in cooked rice (or minute rice if using)  Bring back to
boil, turn heat off and allow to sit with lid on for a few minutes.
Serve immediately. Garnish with sesame seeds. Origin: Uncle Ben's
Rice pamphlet. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/95.
