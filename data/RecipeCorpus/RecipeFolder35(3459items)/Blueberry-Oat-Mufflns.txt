Title: Blueberry Oat Mufflns
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 ea Egg -- beaten
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 c  Fresh blueberries
1 c  Quick-cooking oats
1 c  Milk -- sour
1 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
In a bowl, combine oats and milk: let stand a few minutes. In another
bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda and
salt; mix well. Stir egg and butter into oat mixture. Add oat mixture
all at once to dry ingredients. Stir just until moistened. Gently
fold in blue-berries. Fill well-greased muffin tins 3/4 full. Bake at
400 deg. for 15-20 minutes. Yield: 12 muffins. (*To sour milk, place
1 tablespoon white vinegar in a mesureing cup; add enough milk to
equal 1 cup.)
