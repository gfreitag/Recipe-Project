Title: Lime Gelatin Salad
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
1 cn Crushed pineapple -- drained
8    Oz
1 cn Lemon-lime soda
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
1 ct Cool WhipÂ® -- thawed &
Divided
1 pk Lime gelatin powder -- 6 oz
1 c  Hot water -- boiling
1 pk Cream cheese -- 8 oz
Softened
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 cn Mandarin oranges -- drained
Dissolve in gelation in water. In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese
until fluffy. Stir in gelatin mixture and beat until smooth. Stir in
vanilla, oranges, pineapple, soda and pecans. Chill until mixture
mounds slightly when dropped from a spoon. Fold in 3/4 of the whipped
topping. Pour into a 13x9x3 inch dish. Refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours
or until firm. Cut into squares; garnish with the remaining whipped
topping. Yield: 16 to 20 servings.
