Title: Quick Couscous
Categories: Nuts/grains, Side dish, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
Salt and pepper
2 c  Vegetable stock
1 c  Quick-cooking couscous
1. Bring the stock to a boil. Stir in the couscous and salt and
pepper. Bring the mixture back to a boil.

2. Cover the pan and remove from the heat. Let stand for 5 minutes.
Correct the seasoning and fluff the couscous with a fork before
serving.
