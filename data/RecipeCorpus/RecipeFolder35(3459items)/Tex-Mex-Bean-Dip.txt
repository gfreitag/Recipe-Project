Title: Tex-Mex Bean Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Country wom, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- grated
1 c  Monterey Jack cheese --
Grated
1 bn Green onions
(6 onions), chopped,
Divided
1 cn Refried beans -- 16 oz.
1 c  Dairy sour cream
1 pk Cream cheese -- 8 oz.
1 cn Green chilies -- 4 oz.
Chopped
1 pk Taco seasoning
Mix all ingredients except 1 tablespoon of onion in fondue pot or
chafing dish. Heat and stir until cheese melts. Top with reserved
onions. Serve warm with tortilla chips. Yield: 30 appetizer servings
