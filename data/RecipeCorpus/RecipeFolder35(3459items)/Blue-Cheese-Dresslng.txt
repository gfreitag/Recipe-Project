Title: Blue Cheese Dresslng
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce --
Optional
Salt and pepper to taste
3/4 c  Blue cheese -- crumbled
3/4 c  Sour cream -- divided
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Lemon juice
In a small bowl, mash blue cheese with a fork. Add 2 tablespoons sour
cream; beat until smooth. Stir in all remaining ingredients. Serve
over mixed greens. Yield: 1 cup
