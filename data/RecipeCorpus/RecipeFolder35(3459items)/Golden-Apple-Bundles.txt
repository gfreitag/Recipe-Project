Title: Golden Apple Bundles
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Lemon peel
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
Pastry for double-crust pie
Milk
Sugar
2 c  Apples -- chopped peeled
1/3 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Raisins
1 tb All-purpose flour
In a bowl combine the apples, walnuts, brown sugar, raisins, flour,
lemon peel and cinnamon; set aside. Roll pastry to l/8-in. thickness.
Cut into 5-in. circles. Spoon about 1/4 cup apple mixture into center
of each circle. Moisten edges of pastry with water. Fold over and
seal edges with a fork. Place on a greased baking sheet. Bake at 450
deg. for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 400 deg.; bake 10 minutes longer.
Brush each with milk and sprinkle with sugar; return to oven for 5
minutes. Yield: 10-12 servings
