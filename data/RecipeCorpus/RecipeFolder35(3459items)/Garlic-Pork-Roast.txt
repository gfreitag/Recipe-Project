Title: Garlic Pork Roast
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Sliced
1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
8 ea Garlic cloves -- minced
ts Salt
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ea Pork loin roast
Backbone loosened (about 5
Pounds)
1/2 md Green pepper -- finely
Chopped
1/2 c  Green onions -- thinly
With a sharp knife, cut a deep pocket between each rib on meaty side
of roast. Combine green pepper, green onions, celery and garlic;
stuff deeply into pockets. Season roast with salt and cayenne pepper,
insert meat thermometer. Place roast, rib side down, in a shallow
roasting pan. Bake, uncovered, at 325 deg. for 2-3 hours or until the
thermometer reads 170 deg.. Let stand for 15 minutes before carving.
Yield: 6-8 servings
