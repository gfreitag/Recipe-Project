Title: Self rising flour
Categories: Information
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM---------------------------
MMMMM----------------GLORIA PITZERS SECRET RECIPE---------------------
1/4 c  Salt
5 lb Flour
1/2 c  Baking powder
1/4 c  Baking soda
Add baking powder, baking soda and salt to flour.

Using 2 large bowls, sift the mixture 3 to 4 times, using the largest
sifter that you have, or use a large strainer and a wooden spoon to
sift it. Store at room temperature and use in any recipe that calls
for self-rising flour.
