Title: Praline Pumpkin Pie
Categories: Holiday, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Ground Ginger
1/4 ts Ground Cloves
1 2/3 c  Evaporated Milk Or
-Half-And-Half
Praline Topping -- (see
-below)
1    9 Inch Pie Shell -- unbaked
2    Eggs
1 cn (16 Oz) Pumpkin
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Ground Cinnamon
Prepare pie shell. Heat oven to 425 F. Beat eggs slightly with hand
beater; beat in remaining ingredients except Praline Topping. Place
pastry-lined pie plate on oven rack; pour in filling. Bake 15 mins.
Reduce oven temperature to 350 F. Bake 35 mins; sprinkle with Praline
Topping. Bake until knife inserted in center comes out clean, about
10 mins longer; cool. Serve with sweetened whipped cream, if desired.

PRALINE TOPPING: Mix 1/2 cup packed brown sugar, 1/3 cup chopped
pecans and 1 Tbs margarine or butter, softened.
