Title: Rice, Corn And Cheese Casserole
Categories: Vegetarian, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Skim milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Chili powder -- to taste
1/4 ts Black pepper
Paprika
3 c  Cooked brown rice -- or
-white rice
2 c  Corn
1 sm Onion -- finely chopped
2 c  Sharp cheddar cheese --
-grated
In a large mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients except the
paprika, and mix them well.

Pour the ingredients into a greased 2-quart casserole.  Sprinkle with
paprika, and bake the casserole in a 350 degree oven for 40-45
minutes.
