Title: Swedish Creme
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Almond extract
2 c  Sour cream -- 16oz
1 c  Raspberries, frozen -- or
Fresh red crushe
2 c  Heavy cream
1 c  Sugar -- plus 2 teaspoon
1 pk Gelatin -- unflavored
1 ts Vanilla extract -- clear
Imitation
In a saucepan, combine cream and 1 cup sugar. Cook and stir
constantly over low heat until candy thermometer reads 160 deg. or
steam rises from pan (do not boil). Stir in gelatin until dissolved;
add extracts. Cool 10 min. Whisk in sour cream. Pour into eight
dessert glasses or small bowls; chill at least 1 hour. Before
serving, combine raspberries and remaining sugar; spoon over each
serving. Yield 8 servings.
