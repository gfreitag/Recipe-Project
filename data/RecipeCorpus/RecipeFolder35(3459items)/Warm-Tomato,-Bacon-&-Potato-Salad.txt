Title: Warm Tomato, Bacon & Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Tomatoes, Potatoes
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Frozen peas -- thawed
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar
2 tb White vinegar
3 tb Bacon -- cooked & crumbled
2 lg Tomatoes
8 oz Potatoes -- cooked &
Peeled
2 tb Oil
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
Core and cut tomatoes into 3/4" cubes; place in large bowl.  Cut
potatoes into 3/4" cubes; add to tomatoes.  In medium skillet heat
oil until hot. Add onion; saute about 5 minutes.  Stir in peas, salt,
sugar and vinegar; pour over tomato mixture, tossing gently.
Sprinkle with bacon.  Serve warm.
