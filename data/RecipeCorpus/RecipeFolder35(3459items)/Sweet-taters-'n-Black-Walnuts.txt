Title: Sweet taters 'n Black Walnuts
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Game
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Black walnuts
Pinch salt
6 lg Sweet taters
1 c  Hot milk
3 tb Sweet butter
2 tb Melted butter
Peel them taters, boil and mash.  Add hot milk, sugar , and 3 tb
butter. Then add black walnuts and a pinch of salt.  Place in greased
baking dish 'n pour 2 tb melted butter over the top.  Bake 20-30
minutes in moderate oven.  (325)

This is a recipe thet's good to set with most anything on th' table.
They say it's good with 'possum.  Course I personaly think about
anything would be an improvement on 'possum.  (jest kiddin')
