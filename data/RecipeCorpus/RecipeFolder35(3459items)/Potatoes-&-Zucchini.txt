Title: Potatoes & Zucchini
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
3    Cloves garlic -- minced
1/3 c  Water or chicken broth
2 ts Fresh thyme -- chopped
Salt and pepper
3 md Potatoes -- scrubbed
2 md Zucchini -- trimmed and
Cubed
2 ts Oil
Cut potatoes into 3/4" pieces.  Heat oil in skillet.  Add potatoes and
stir-fry for 1 minute.  Pour in water or broth, cover and cook over
low heat 4 to 5 minutes.  Add garlic, zucchini, thyme and season with
salt and pepper to taste.  Stir, re-cover and cook until vegetables
are tender.  96 calories and 2 grams fat.
