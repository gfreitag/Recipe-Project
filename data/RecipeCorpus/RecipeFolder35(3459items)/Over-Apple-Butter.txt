Title: Over Apple Butter
Categories: Can/preserv, Tasteofhome
Yield: 30 servings
1 c  Cider vinegar
4 ts Ground cinnamon
1    Stick cinnamon -- 3 inch
40 lg Apple -- about 13 pound
Quartered and cored
1/4 c  Water
8 c  Sugar
In a large covered kettle, simmer the apples and water until tender.
Press through a sieve or food mill. Measure 1 gallon of pulp. place
in a large roaster. Add sugar, vinegar, cinnamon and cinnamon stick.
Cover and bake at 400 deg. for 3 hours, stirring occasionally.
Uncover and bake 1 hour longer, stirring occasionally, until very
thick. Remove cinnamon stick. Pour hot into hot jars, leaving 1/4
inch. headspace. Adjust the caps. Process for 10 min. in a
boiling-water bath. Yield; 8 pints.
