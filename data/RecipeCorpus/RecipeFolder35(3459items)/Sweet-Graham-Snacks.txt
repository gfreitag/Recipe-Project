Title: Sweet Graham Snacks
Categories: Snacks, Tasteofhome
Yield: 48 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Nuts -- ground
12 ea Graham crackers -- broken
Into quarters
1 c  Butter or margarine
Line a 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan with graham crackers. In a
saucepan, bring butter and sugar to a boil; boil for 2 minutes.
Remove from the heat; stir in nuts. Spoon over the graham crackers.
Bake at 325 deg. for 10 minutes. Immediately remove from pan onto
foil. Cool. Break apart. Yield: 3-4 dozen
