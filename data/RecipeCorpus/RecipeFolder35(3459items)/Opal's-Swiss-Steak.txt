Title: Opal's Swiss Steak
Categories: Main dish, Route 66, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
2 c  Tomato Sauce
2 lb Round Steak
Shortening -- for browning
1 c  Green Pepper -- chopped
Brown steak in a large skillet using a small amount of shortening. Add
green pepper and onion and season with salt and pepper. Pour enough
sauce over steak to cover it. Reduce heat to simmer, cover container,
and cook slowly for 1 hour. Makes 8 servings.
