Title: Golden Drumstick Blue Cheese Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Route 66, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1    Tbsp (Scant) Lemon Juice
1 c  Sour Cream
Garlic Powder -- to taste
1/4 lb Blue Cheese -- melted
1 c  Salad Dressing
1/2 c  Oil
Melt the blue cheese on simmer or use the microwave on low power. Add
remaining ingredients and stir vigorously to blend. Store in covered
container in refrigerator until needed. Makes 2 1/2 cups dressing.
