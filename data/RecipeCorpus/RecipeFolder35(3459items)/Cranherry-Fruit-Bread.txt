Title: Cranherry Fruit Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Divided
2 c  Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Shortening -- melted
1/4 c  Warm water
1    Bag cranberries -- 12
Ounces
Fresh or frozen, halved
2 c  Pecan halves
1 c  Mixed candied fruit --
Chopped
1 c  Dates -- chopped
1 c  Golden raisins
1 tb Grated orange peel
4 c  All-purpose flour --
Combine cranberries, pecans, fruit, dates, raisins and orange peel
with 1/4 cup flour; set aside. In another bowl, combine sugar, baking
powder, baking soda. salt and remaining flour; set aside, in a large
mixing bowl, beat eggs. Add orange juice, shortening and water. Add
flour mixture; stir just until combined. Fold in cranberry mixture.
Spoon into three greased and waxed paper-lined 8-1/2in, x 4-1/2Â·in, x
2-1/2-in. loaf pans. Bake at 350 deg. for 60-65 minutes or until
breads test done. Yield: 3 loaves
