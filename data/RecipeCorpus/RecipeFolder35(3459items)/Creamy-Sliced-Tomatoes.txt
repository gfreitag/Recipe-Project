Title: Creamy Sliced Tomatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Lettuce leaves
6 md Tomatoes -- sliced
1 md Red onion -- thinly sliced
In rin
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Light cream
3/4 ts Dried basil
Or 1-1/2 teaspoons chopped
Fresh basil, di
In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, cream and half of the basil; mix
well. Refrigerate. Just before serving, arrange lettuce, tomatoes and
onions on individual salad plates. Drizzle dressing over. Sprinkle
with remaining basil. Yield: 12 servings
