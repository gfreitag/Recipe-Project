Title: Swiss Potato Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Potatoes, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Hellmans's mayonnaise
1/4 c  Minced onion, fresh
1    Egg, slightly beaten
3 c  Prepared instant mashed
Potatoes
4 oz Shredded Swiss cheese
In mixing bowl combine all ingredients and beat until well mixed.
Spoon into a 9 inch buttered pie plate.  Bake 35 to 40 minutes at 400
degrees or until puffed and golden brown. Randy Rigg
