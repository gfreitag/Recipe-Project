Title: Easy Chicken Taco Salad
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Tomato -- chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
-guacamole
4 oz Cheddar cheese -- shredded
-lettuce leaves
10 oz Swanson chicken -- drained
1/2 c  Salsa
1/3 c  Catalina dressing
3 c  Tortilla chips
15 oz Kidney beans, canned --
-drained & rinsed
Mix chicken, salsa and dressing.  For each serving, arrange chips,
beans, chicken mixture, tomato green pepper and guacamole on lettuce
lined serving plates.  Top with cheese.
