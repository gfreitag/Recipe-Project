Title: Spicy Breaded Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/4 c  Skim milk
2 lb Boneless skinless chicken
Breast halves
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs
1 tb Nonfat dry milk -- powder
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Garlic powder
.In a plastic bag, mix bread crumbs, milk powder,  chili powder,
garlic powder and dry mustard; set aside. Place milk in a shallow
pan. Dip chicken pieces into milk, then in bag and shake to coat.
Place chicken, in a 13x9x2 baking pan coated with vegetable cooking
spray. bake, uncovered at 375 deg/ for 50-55 min. or until juices run
clear. Yield: 6  servings.
