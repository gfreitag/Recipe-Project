Title: Holiday Nut Stuffing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Nuts/grains, Vegetables, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------HOLIDAY NUT STUFFING-------------------------
Here's a luxurious twist on the old fashioned (but still great) bread
stuffing from teh turkey folks.

INGREDIENTS:

1 lg Onion; chopped
8 oz Mushrooms; thickly sliced
1/2 c Butter
1/4 c Pecans
1/4 c Hazelnuts (Filberts)
1/4 c Almond slivers
2 c Dried Bread Cubes
1/2 c Celery; chopped
1 tb Poultry seasoning

DIRECTIONS:

1. In hot skillet, saute onion and mushrooms in butter until tender.
Add nuts and mix well. Remove from heat. In large bowl, add bread
cubes, celery and poultry seasoning. Gently mix the two together and
stuff into prepared turkey cavity.

2. To cook extra stuffing, place in a greased casserole, cover and
bake during the last 40 minutes of roasting time.
