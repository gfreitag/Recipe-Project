Title: DROP DOUGHNUT HOLES
Categories: Pastry
Yield: 50 servings
1 2/3 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
1/3 c  Milk
1/4 ts Lemon extract
1/4 ts Nutmeg
2    Eggs
1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
2 tb Melted margarine
Or cooking oil
Beat eggs until light; add sugar, salt, oil, extract, and nutmeg and
mix well.
Mix flour with baking powder; add alternatively with milk to egg
mixture. Drop by 1/2 teaspoonsful into deep hot oil and fry about
2-1/2 minutes, or until brown. Doughnut will rise to the surface, so
continue submerging them to assure even cooking.
Drain on paper towels. Shake with sugar in a bag.
Makes about 50.
