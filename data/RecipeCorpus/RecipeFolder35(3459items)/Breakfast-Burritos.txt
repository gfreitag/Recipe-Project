Title: Breakfast Burritos
Categories: Breakfast, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Browned/drained
12 ea Flour tortillas -- 10 inches
Warmed
3 c  Cheddar cheese -- l2 ounces
Shredded
Salsa, optional
1    Bag frozen Southern-style
Hash browns -- l6 ounces
12 ea Eggs
1 lg Onion -- chopped
1 ea Green pepper -- chopped
1/2 lb Bulk pork sausage --
In a large skillet, fry hash browns according to package directions;
remove and set aside. In a large bowl, beat eggs; add onions and
green pepper. Pour into the same skillet; cook and stir until eggs
are set. Remove from heat. Add hash browns and sausage; mix gently.
Place about 3/4 cup of filling on each tortilla and top with about
1/4 cup cheese. Roll up and place on a greased baking sheet. Bake at
350 deg. for 15-20 minules or until heated through. Serve with salsa
if desired. Yield: 12 servings
