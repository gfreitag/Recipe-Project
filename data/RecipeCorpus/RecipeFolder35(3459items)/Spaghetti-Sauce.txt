Title: Spaghetti Sauce
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
8 oz Tomato sauce
6 oz Tomato paste
1 ea Med-lge. onion, chopped
1/2 ea Green pepper, chopped
1/2 c  Fresh parsley,chopped
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Fennel seed
1 tb Olive oil
1 ea Clove of garlic, chopped
1 1/2 lb Sweet or hot italian sausage
4 oz Mushrooms,sliced
2 oz Parmesan cheese, shredded
2 oz Mozzarella cheese,
Shredded
28 oz Whole tomatoes pureed in
-blender
In large sauce pan or dutch oven, lightly brown garlic in oil. Add
sausage and brown.  Drain fat, remove sausage from pan and setaside.
Add paste to drippings, cook about 5 minutes stirring constantly.
Add pureed tomatoes and sauce.  Cook over medium heat 15 minutes,
stirring occasionally. Return sausage to pan.  Add remaining
ingredients except cheeses. Simmer 15 minutes.  Add cheeses
gradually, stir until dissolved in sauce. Cover and simmer 1 1/2 hrs.
Serve over 1 lb spaghetti. Submitted By DENISE KILROY   On   09-30-95
