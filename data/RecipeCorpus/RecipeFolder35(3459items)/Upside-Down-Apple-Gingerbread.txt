Title: Upside-Down Apple Gingerbread
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Egg
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
3/4 c  Hot tea
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
2 lg Apples -- peeled/cored/slice
1/3 c  Packed brown sugar
GINGERBREAD
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1/2 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Packed brown sugar
Pour butter into a 9-in, square baking pan. Arrange apples over
butter; sprinkle with brown sugar and set aside. For gingerbread,
combine butter, molasses, sugars and egg in a mixing bowl; mix well.
Combine dry ingredients; add to sugar mixture alternately with hot
tea. Mix well; pour over apples. Bake at 350 deg. for 45-50 minutes
or until the cake tests done. Cool for 3-5 minutes. Loosen sides and
invert onto a serving plale. Serve warm. Yield: 9 servings
