Title: Pecan Cranberry Muffins
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 18 servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
2 ea Eggs -- lightly beaten
1 c  Milk
1 c  Pecans -- chopped
1 tb Lemon peel -- grated
1 1/2 c  Chopped cranberries -- fresh
Or frozen
1 1/4 c  Sugar -- divided
3 c  All-purpose flour
4 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
In a bowl, toss cranberries with 1/4 cup sugar; set aside. Combine
flour, baking power, salt and remaining sugar. Cut in butter until
the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Combine eggs and milk; stir into
flour mixture just until moistened. Fold in pecans, lemon peel and
cranberries. Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full.
Bake at 400 deg. for 20 to 25 minutes. ou until muffins test done.
Yield: about 18.
