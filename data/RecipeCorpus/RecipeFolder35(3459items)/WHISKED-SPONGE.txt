Title: WHISKED SPONGE
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 cake
4 oz Plain flour, sifted
2    Eggs
4 oz Caster sugar
Grease and base-line an 8-in. sandwich tin. Whisk the eggs and sugar
in a bowl over hot water until thick and leaving a trail. Remove from
the heat. Sprinkle the sifted flour over the egg mixture and
carefully fold in, using a metal spoon, until the flour is evenly
distributed. Pour into the prepared tin and bake just above the
centre of the oven at 350F. for 25-30 minutes. Turn out and cool on a
wire rack and use as required. Submitted By JAMES LEAR   On   10-08-95
