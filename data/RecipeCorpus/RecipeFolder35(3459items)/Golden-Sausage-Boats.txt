Title: Golden Sausage Boats
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Breakfast
Yield: 6 servings
ds Salt
6 ea Hotdog buns
12 ea Pork sausage links
6 ea Eggs
1/3 c  Milk
Place links in unheated skillet.  Do NOT prick skins. Add 3 Tbs water.
Cook, covered 5 minutes over low heat.  Uncover, drain liquid and
finish cooking links over low heat, turning till well browned on all
sides.

Meanwhile, beat eggs together with milk and salt.  Dip buns into
mixture. Grill on both sides till browned.  Place sausages in buns.
Serve with syrup.
