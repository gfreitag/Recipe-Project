Title: Noodles Alfredo
Categories: Side dish, Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Parmesan Cheese
1 tb Parsley flakes
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
8 oz Noodles (your favorite - I
Like fettucinni or
Linguini)
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Light cream
Cook your noodles according to package directions;  meanwhile

heat butter and cream over low heat in a small saucepan add cheese,
parsley, salt, pepper.  Mix well pour sauce over noodles, toss to mix
