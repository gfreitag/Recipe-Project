Title: Cherries N Cream Cakes
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla
21 oz Cherry pie filling
1 pk Cherry chip cake mix
1 c  Sour cream
2 tb Sugar
Heat oven to 350.  Prepare and bake cake in 2 round pans as directed
on package.  Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans.  Cool cake
completely.  Mix sour cream, sugar and vanilla.  Spread over top of
cakes.  Spoon pie filling over sour cream mixture.  Store cakes in
refrigerator.
