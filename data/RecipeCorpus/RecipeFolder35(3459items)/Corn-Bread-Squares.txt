Title: Corn Bread Squares
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 9 servings
3/4 ts Salt
2 ea Eggs -- beaten
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Yellow cornmeal
1/4 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder and
salt. Add eggs, milk and oil. beat just until moistened. Spoon into a
greased 8 in. square baking pan. Bake at 400 deg. for 20 to 25 min.
or until bread tests done. Yield: 9 servings.
