Title: Icebox Sandwiches
Categories: Cake/cookie, Tasteofhome
Yield: 24 servings
1 c  Miniature chocolate chips --
Semisweet 6 oz
48 ea Graham crackers -- squares
1 pk Instant pudding mix --
Vanilla 3.4 oz
2 c  Milk
2 c  Whipped topping
Mix pudding and milk according to package directions and refrigerate
until set. Fold in whipped topping and chocolate chips. Place 24
graham crackers on a baking sheet; top each with about 3 tablespoons
filling. Place another graham cracker on top. Freeze for 1 hour or
until firm. Wrap individually in plastic wrap; freese. Serve
sandwiches frozen. Yield: 24 servings.
