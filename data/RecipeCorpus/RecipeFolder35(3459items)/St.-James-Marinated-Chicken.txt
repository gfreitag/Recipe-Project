Title: St. James Marinated Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Route 66
Yield: 4 servings
4    To 6 Boneless Chicken
-Breasts
Monterey Jack Cheese -- for
-garnish
Bacon Slices -- for garnish
2 c  Kraft Creamy Golden Salad
Dressing
1 tb Vanilla
ds Basil
ds Oregano
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
Combine all ingredients. Marinate boneless breasts for 12 hours.
Remove from marinade and grill chicken until done. To serve, sprinkle
generously with Monterey Jack cheese and a slice of bacon that has
been cooked to the rare stage. Slide chicken under broiler to melt
cheese and crisp bacon. Serve on a bed of rice. Makes 4 to 6 servings.
