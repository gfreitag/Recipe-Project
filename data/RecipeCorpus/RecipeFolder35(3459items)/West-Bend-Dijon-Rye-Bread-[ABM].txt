Title: West Bend Dijon Rye Bread [ABM]
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 1/2lb loaf
3/4 c  Whole wheat flour
3/4 c  Medium rye flour
1 ts Instant coffee
1 ts Salt
2 ts Active dry yeast
1 c  Water [80 deg]
1 tb Veg oil
2 tb Molasses
1/4 c  Dijon mustard
1 3/4 c  All purpose flour
Add water, oil, molasses and mustard to the bread pan. Add flours,
coffee and salt to the pan. Level the dry ingredients. Make a small
well and add the yeast. Program the machine for basic mode not whole
wheat.

From the West Bend ABM manual. Posted by Jim Weller.
