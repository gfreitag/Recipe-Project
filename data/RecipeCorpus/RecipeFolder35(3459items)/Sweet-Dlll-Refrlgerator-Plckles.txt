Title: Sweet Dlll Refrlgerator Plckles
Categories: Can/preserv, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 qt Cucumbers -- sliced
Unpeeled
1 lg Onion -- sliced
1 c  Fresh dill -- minced
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Vinegar
2 c  Water
1/4 c  Salt
In a saucepan, combine sugar, vinegar, water and salt. Bring to a
boil and boil 1 minute. In a large nonmetallic container, combine
cucumbers, onion and dill. Pour dressing over; cool. Cover and
refrigerate at least 3 days before serving. Stir occasionally. Yield:
3-1/2 quarts.
