Title: Favorite French Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tasteofhome
Yield: 25 servings
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Paprika
12 oz Chili sauce -- one bottle
1 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  Vinegar
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Onion -- grated
1 1/2 ts Salt
In a bowl or jar with tight-fitting lid, mix vingar, sugar and onion.
Combine salt, mustard, paprika and 2 tablespoons chili sauce to form a
paste. Add remaining chili sauce and mix well. Pour into vinegar
mixture; add oil and mix or shake well. Store in the refrigerator.
Yield 3 1/2 cups.
