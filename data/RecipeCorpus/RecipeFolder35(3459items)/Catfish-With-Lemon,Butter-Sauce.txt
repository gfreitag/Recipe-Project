3/4 c  Butter
8 oz Fresh mushrooms -- sliced
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
1/2 c  Chicken broth
Or dry white wine
2 tb Lemon juice
1/3 c  Fresh parsley -- chopped
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
In a large skillet, melt butter over mediurn heat. Cook mushrooms and
garlic, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes. Add broth or wine, lemon
juice, parsley, salt and pepper; cook 3 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Add catfish; simmer, uncovered, for 6-8 minutes or
until fish flakes easily with a fork. (Butter sauce will be thin.)
Serve over spaghetti. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Garnish with
lemon and parsley if desired. Yield:6-8 servings
