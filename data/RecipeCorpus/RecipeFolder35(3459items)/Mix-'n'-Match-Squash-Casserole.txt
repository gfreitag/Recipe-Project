Title: Mix 'n' Match Squash Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
2 ea Eggs -- beaten
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
4 c  Summer squash -- cubed
(yellow, zucchini, pattypan
& or sunburst
1 lb Pork sausage -- cooked &
Drained
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
Place squash and a small amount of water in a large saucepan; cover
and cook for 8 to 10 min. or until tender. Drain. Add all remaining
ingredients; mix well. transfer to greased 11x7x2 inch baking dish.
Bake uncovered, at 325 deg. for 30 to 35 min. Yield 6 to 8 serving.
