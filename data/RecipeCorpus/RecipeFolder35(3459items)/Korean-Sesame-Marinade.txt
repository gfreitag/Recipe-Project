Title: Korean Sesame Marinade
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Korean, Low cal/fat
Yield: 3 /4 cup
1/3 c  Soy sauce
3 tb Sugar or honey
1 1/2 tb Sesame oil
1 ts Hot red pepper flakes
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 c  Toasted sesame seeds
3    Garlic clove(s)
-minced (1 tbs)
1 tb Ginger, minced
3    Scallions, minced
Lightly toast the sesame seeds in a dry skillet over medium heat.
Combine the sesame seeds and the remaining ingredients in a shallow
bowl. Marinate fish for 30 minutes, chicken breasts for 1 hour, and
thinly sliced beef for 1-2 hours, turning once or twice.
