Title: Pizzelles
Categories: Herbs/spice, Cake/cookie, Desserts, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Anise oil
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
Vegetable oil
3    Eggs
3/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Butter or margarine,
Softened
1 ts Vanilla extract
Beat eggs at medium speed of an electric mixer until foamy;
gradually add sugar, beating until thick and lemon colored.  Add
butter, vanilla, and anise oil; mix well.  Add flour and baking
powder; beat until smooth.

Brush pizzelle iron lightly with oil; preheat iron over medium heat 2
minutes. Place 1 tablespoon batter in center of iron; close iron and
cook 30 seconds on each side or until pizzelle is lightly browned.
Repeat with remaining batter; cool on wire racks.
