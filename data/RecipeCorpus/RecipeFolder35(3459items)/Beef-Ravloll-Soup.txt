Title: Beef Ravloll Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes --
Crushed
1 cn Beef ravioli -- 15 ounces
c  Fresh parsley -- chopped
Grated Parmesan cheese
1 c  Celery -- sliced
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1 c  Carrots -- sliced
2 tb Vegetable oil
4 c  Beef broth
1/8 ts Pepper
In a large kettle, saute celery, onion and carrots in oil for 3
minutes; add broth and seasonings. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and
simmer, covered, about 15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
Stir in the ravioli and heat through. Garnish with parsley and
Parmesan cheese. Yield: 4-6 servings (1-1/2 qts.)
