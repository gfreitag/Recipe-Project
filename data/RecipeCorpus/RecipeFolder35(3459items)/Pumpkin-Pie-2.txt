Title: Pumpkin Pie 2
Categories: Pie/tart, Vegetables
Yield: 1 pie
1/4 ts Ginger
Few grains salt
1 3/16 c  Prepared pumpkin
3/4 c  Hot milk
2    Eggs
2/3 c  White sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Nutmeg
To prepare pumpkin:
1) Remove seeds (not necessary to peel)
2) Cut into chunks, place in covered container
3) Bake in oven, microwave, on stove (with a little water), or in a
pressure cooker (with a little water), until soft. Drain water.
4) Remove peel
5) Mash- place in strainer to remove extra water.

Make pie crust- The one on the Tenderflake box is good.

Mix filling ingredients and pour into unbaked shell.  Bake at 450
degrees for 10 minutes, then at 350 for 25-30 minutes (until a
toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean).

Whipped cream or ice cream makes a great topping.
