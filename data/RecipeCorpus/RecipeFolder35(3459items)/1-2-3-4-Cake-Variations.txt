Title: 1-2-3-4 Cake Variations
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
See 1-2-3-4 Cake recipe
Tube or bundt pan: Bake in a greased and floured 10 by 4 inch (18 cup
capacity) tube, bundt or kugelhopf pan for 60 to 70 minutes. After
the cake has cooled, sprinkle with confectioners' sugar and fill the
center with fresh berries or glaze as desired.

Citrus cake: Add 2 tablespoons finely grated ornage rind and 1
tablespoon grated lemon rind to batter.

Buttermilk cake: Decrease baking powder to 2 teaspoons and add 1
teaspoon baking soda; substitute buttermilk for whole milk.

Spice cake: Substitute paced light brown sugar for the granulated
sugar. Add 1 teaspoon each cinnamon, ginger and cocoa, 3/4 teaspoon
ground pepper and 1/8 teaspoon each allspice, cloves and nutmeg,
sifting them with the flour mixture. Use the batter to make 24
cupcakes in greased muffin or egg-shaped tines. Frost if desired.
