Title: Glazed Sweet Potato Wedges
Categories: Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
-each cut lengthwise
-into 8 wedges (abt 1 lb)
Orange slices (opt)
Parsley sprigs (opt)
4 ts Margarine
4 ts Orange juice
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
2 md Sweet potatoes, peeled
Place margarine in an 8-inch square baking dish and microwave on high
30 seconds or until butter melts. Stir in orange juice and cinnamon;
add potato wedges and toss. Cover with wax paper; microwave on high
6-8 minutes or until tender, stirring after 4 minutes. Garnish with
orange slices and parsley if desired.
