Title: Molly's Landing Marinated Mushrooms
Categories: Route 66, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Sugar
2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1 tb Salt
1 lb Fresh Mushrooms -- cleaned
1 1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
1/2 ts Dry Mustard
1 1/2 c  Vinegar
1 1/2 tb Fresh Garlic -- minced
2 md Onions -- minced
Combine all ingredients except mushrooms. Pour mixture over mushrooms
and marinate for 24 hours in the refrigerator. Makes 5 to 6 servings.
