Title: Layered Chicken Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
1 lb Pea pods -- fresh halved
4 c  Cooked chicken -- cubed
2 c  Miracle WhipÂ® light
1 tb Sugar
2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Ground ginger
Cherry tomatoes & fresh
Parsley sprigs -- optional
4 c  Iceberg lettuce -- shredded
Or 5 cups
1 md Cucumber -- thinly sliced
1 c  Bean sprouts
1 cn Waterchestnuts, canned --
Drained
1/2 c  Green onions -- thinly
Sliced
Place lettuce in the bottom of a 4 qt. glass salad bowl. Layer with
cucumber, bean sprouts, water chestnut, onion, pea pods and chicken.
In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, sugar, curry and ginger. Spread
over top of salad. Garnish with cherry tomatoes and parsley if
desired. Cover and chill several hours or overnight. Yield: 8-10
servings.
