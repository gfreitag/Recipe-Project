Title: Cream Cheese Brownies~
Categories: Low cal/fat, Holiday, Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 servings
1/3 c  Sugar
1    Egg
2 tb Flour
1    Recipe One Bowl Brownies
(see recipe)
1    8 oz pkg Cream cheese
- softened
Prepare One Bowl Brownies as directed, using 4 eggs. Spread in
prepared pan. Beat cream cheese, sugar, egg and flour in same bowl
until well blended. Spoon mixture over brownie batter; swirl with
knife to marbleize. Bake 40 minutes. Makes 24 brownies.

NOTE:  For a festive twist, cut brownies into holiday shapes with
metal cookie cutters. For easy cutting and removal, bake in a greased
foil-lined pan.

Per brownie including One Bowl Brownie recipe:  Calories 251; fat
14.8 g; cholesterol 61 mg; carbohydrate 27.4 g; fiber 1.6 g; protein
4.1 g; sodium 77 mg; potassium 113 mg; calcium 22 mg.
