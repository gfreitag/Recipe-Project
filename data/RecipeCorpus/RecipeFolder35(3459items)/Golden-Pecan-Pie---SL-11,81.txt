Title: Golden Pecan Pie - SL 11/81
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 9" pie
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 tb Margarine; softened
1 c  Pecan halves
1 ea Unbaked 9" pastry shell
3/4 c  Sugar
1 tb All-purpose flour
1 pn Salt
3 ea Eggs; well beaten
1 c  Light corn syrup
Combine dry ingredients in large mixing bowl.  Add eggs, syrup,
vanilla, and margarine; beat with electric mixer until blended.  Stir
in pecans. Pour mixture into pastry shell.  Bake at 350F for 55 to 60
minutes. Yield: one 9" pie.

Mrs. J.W. Hutchison of Tennessee, in November, 1981 "Southern Living".
