Title: Rigatoni With Zucchini Sauce
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
-peeled
1/2 ts Salt
1    Bay leaf
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Oregano
8 oz Rigatoni
3 tb Olive oil
1 lg Onion -- sliced
2 md Zucchini -- sliced & halved
3 c  Fresh tomatoes -- diced &
In large skillet, saute onion in hot oil until tender.  Add zucchini,
tomatoes, bay leaf and seasonings.  Simmer covered for 15 minutes;
uncover and simmer for 10 minutes longer.  Discard bay leaf.
Meanwhile, cook rigatoni as directed on package; drain.  Serve
rigatoni topped with zucchini sauce.
