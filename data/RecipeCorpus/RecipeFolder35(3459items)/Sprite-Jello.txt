Title: Sprite Jello
Categories: Salads, Fruit, Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Mini marshmallows
1 sm Can of crushed pineapple
1 c  Chopped nuts
10 oz Sprite
1 sm Package of lime Jello
3 oz Cream cheese
Heat Sprite and melt Jello and cream cheese with marshmallows.  Add
crushed pineapple and nuts.  Chill till firm. Randy Rigg
