Title: Ltallan Vegtable Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Beef broth -- 14-1/2 ounces
1 1/2 c  Water
2 md Zucchini
Cut into 1/4-inch slices
1/2 ts Dried basil
Grated Parmesan cheese
1 lb Bulk ltalian sausage
1 md Onion -- sliced
1 cn Whole tomatoes
6 oz With liquid, chopped
1 cn Garbanzo beans -- l5 ounces
Drained
In a 3-qt. saucepan, cook sausage and onion; drain fat. Stir in
tomatoes, beans. broth. water, zucchini and basil. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes or until the zucchini is tender.
Sprinkle each serving with cheese. Yield: 6-8 servings (2 qts.)
