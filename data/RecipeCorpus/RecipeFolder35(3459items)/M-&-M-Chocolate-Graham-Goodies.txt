Title: M & M Chocolate Graham Goodies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 3 dozen
1 1/4 c  All Purpose Flour
1 1/4 c  Graham Cracker Crumbs
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cinnamon
12 oz M&M's Semi Sweet Chocolate
Mini Baking Bits
3/4 c  Butter Flavor Crisco
1/2 c  Granulated Sugar
1/2 c  Light Brown Sugar, firmly
Packed
1 lg Egg
2 tb Milk
2 tb Vanilla Extract
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a large bowl with electric mixer,
cream Crisco & sugars until light and fluffy. Add egg, milk, and
vanilla extract, blending well. Combine flour, graham cracker crumbs,
baking soda, salt and cinnamon, blend well into creamed mixture. By
hand, stir in M&M bits. Drop by heaping tablespoonfuls about 2 inches
apart on ungreased cookie sheets. Bake 8 to 10 minutes until set. DO
NOT OVERBAKE! Cool 1 minute before removing to wire rack; cool
completely. Store in tightly covered container.

VARIATION: To make Chocolate Graham Goodies S'mores, sandwich two
cookies together by spreading bottoms of half the cookies with 1
teaspoon of marshmallo cream; place the bottom of a second cookie on
the cream.
