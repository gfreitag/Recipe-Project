Title: Southwestern Raw Vegetable Salad - RHP
Categories: Chilies, Salads, Side dish
Yield: 10 servings
-chiles; seeded & julienned
1 ea Fresh jalapeno; seeded and
-chopped
1 md Red onion; thinly sliced
1 c  Seedless grapes
1 md Jicama; peeled and julienned
2 ea Red bell peppers; seeded and
-julienned
2 ea Poblano peppers; seeded and
-julienned
2 ea Fresh green New Mexican
MMMMM----------------------FOR THE DRESSING---------------------------
1 tb Fresh lime juice
1 ts Grated lime zest
2 cl Garlic; minced
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Extra virgin olive oil
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1 tb Cumin seeds; toasted and
-ground in a spice mill or
-coffee grinder
1 tb Cilantro; chopped (fresh
-coriander)
1 tb Red New Mexican chile flakes
Combine all the salad ingredients in a serving bowl.  Combine the
dressing ingredients in a small bowl or glass jar.  Pour the dressing
over the salad, toss, and serve.
