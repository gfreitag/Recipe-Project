Title: Egg Balls for Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Breads, Penndutch
Yield: 12 sm. balls
2 tb Flour
Salt and pepper to taste
2    Yolks from hard-boiled eggs
Dab of butter; softened
1    Egg white; raw
Mash the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs fine and smooth with just a
touch of soft butter.  Beat the white of 1 egg and add to the yolks
with the flour, salt, and pepper.  Mix all together, adding if
necessary a little flour to mold the mixture into balls the size of
quite small marbles. Do not make too stiff.  Drop these into hot
broth or soup and cook about five minutes.

From "Cooking with the Pennsyvania Dutch", A. Monroe Aurand, Jr.
(ed.), 1946
