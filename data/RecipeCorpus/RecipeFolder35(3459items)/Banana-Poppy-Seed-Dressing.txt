Title: Banana Poppy Seed Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Dry mustard
3/4 ts Salt
Salad greens
Orange and grapefruit
Sections
1 ea Ripe banana
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 ounces
1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Poppy seeds
1 tb Lemon juice
In a small bowl, finely mash banana. Add sour cream, sugar, poppy
seeds, lemon juice, mustard and salt. Chill for at least 30 minutes.
Arrange salad greens and fruit on a platter or individual salad
plates. Serve with dressing. Yield: 1-3/4 cups
