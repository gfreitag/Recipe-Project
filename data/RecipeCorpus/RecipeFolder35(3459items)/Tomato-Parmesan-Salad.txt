Title: Tomato Parmesan Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 14 servings
1    Head iceberg lettuce --
Torn
2 sm Red onion -- thinly sliced
2 lg Tomatoes -- diced
1    Jar pimiento -- diced 4 oz
2/3 c  Parmesan cheese -- shredded
1 1/3 c  Olive oil -- or vegetable
Oil
1 c  Red wine vinegar
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
2 bn Romaine lettuce -- torn
In a small bowl or jar with a tigh-fitting lid, combine oil, vinegar,
garlic, salt and pepper; set aside. In a large salad bowl, combine all
remaining ingredients. Chill until ready to serve. Just before serving
whisk or shake dressing; pour over salad and toss. Yield: 14 servings.
