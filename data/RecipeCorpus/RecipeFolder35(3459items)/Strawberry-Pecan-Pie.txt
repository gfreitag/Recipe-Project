Title: Strawberry-Pecan Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Pecans -- chopped
Pastry for double-crust pie
(9 inches)
1 tb Butter or margarine -- or 2
Tablespoons
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Ground cinnamon
2 c  Fresh strawberries --
Chopped
In a bowl, combine sugar, flour, nutmeg and cinnamon. Add
strawberries and pecans; toss gently. Line pie plate with bottom
crust. Add filling; dot with butter. Top with a lattice crust. Bake
at 375 deg. for 50 min. or until crust is golden brown and filling is
bubbly. Yield: 6-8 servings.
