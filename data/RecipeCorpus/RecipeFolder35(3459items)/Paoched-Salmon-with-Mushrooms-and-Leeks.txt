Title: Paoched Salmon with Mushrooms and Leeks
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1    Leek
75 g  Mushrooms
142 ml Double cream
1/2 ts French mustard
2 ts Cornflour
Salt
Ground Black Pepper
500 g  Salmon fillet
75 ml White wine
75 ml Water
2 tb Chopped Parsley
25 g  Butter
4    Shallots
2    Garlic Cloves
Preparation Time: 20-25 minutes Cooking Time: 20-25 minutes

Preparation: chop the shallots, crush the garlic cloves, cut the leek
into 7cm fine strips, slice the mushrooms, blend the cornflour with
cold water.

1. Place the salmon fillet in a saucepan with the wine, water and
parsley.
Bring to the boil and simmer for 12-15 minutes.

2. Remove the salmon from the pan, place in a serving dish and keep
warm.
Boil the fish liquor until reduced by half and reserve.

3. In a separate pan heat the butter, add the shallots, garlic, leeks
and
mushrooms and cook for 4-5 minutes.

4. Add the reserved fish liquor, cream, mustard, blended cornflour and
seasoning and bring to the boil.

5. Pour over the salmon fillet and garnish with parsley.

Serve with new potatoes and seasonal vegetables.
