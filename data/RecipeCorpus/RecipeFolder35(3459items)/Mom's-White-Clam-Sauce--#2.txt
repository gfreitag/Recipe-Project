Title: Mom's White Clam Sauce  #2
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Italian, Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Black pepper
48    Littleneck clams
1/3 c  Olive oil
4 cl Garlic; minced
3/4 c  Parsley; chopped
Heat the oil in a large pot.  Simmer together the first four
ingredients for about 2 minutes.  Add the clams, cover and simmer
until shells open about 5 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
