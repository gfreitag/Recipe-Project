Title: Broccoli Cheddar Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Tasteofhome
Yield: 2 servings
3/4 c  Chicken broth
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Chopped broccoli, frozen --
Or fresh cooked
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
In a saucepan, saute the onion in butter until tender. Stir in flour,
salt and pepper; cook and stir until smooth and bubbly. Add broth and
milk all at once; cook and stir until the mixture boils and thickens.
Add broccoli. Simmer stirring constantly until heated through. Remove
from the heat and stir in cheese until melted. Yield 2 servings./
