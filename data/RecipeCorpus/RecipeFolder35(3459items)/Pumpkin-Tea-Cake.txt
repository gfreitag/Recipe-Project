Title: Pumpkin Tea Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
3 c  Sugar
1 c  Oil
3    Eggs
2 c  Pumpkin
3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
Blend sugar and oil.  Beat eggs.  Add eggs, then pumpkin.  Beat well.
Sift all dry ingredients together.  Gradually add to mixture.  Beat at
medium speed.  Line bottom of 9 inch tube pan.  Bake 1 hour and 15
minutes at 350 degrees.  Can substitute 1 cup of applesauce for 1 cup
of pumpkin.
