Title: Pork With Cashew Nuts
Categories: Chinese, Meats, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Bamboo Shoots. Thin Sliced
And -- cut in 1/2" strips
1 c  Unsalted Roasted Cashew
Nuts
4 tb Oil
1 lb Pork Loin -- thinly sliced
1 c  Broccoli Flowerets
1    Carrot -- shredded
3    Scallions, Split & Cut In 1"
Segments
MMMMM----------------------SEASONINGS--------------------------------
1    Clove Garlic -- minced
2 sl Ginger -- minced
MMMMM-----------------------MARINADE---------------------------------
2 ts Cornstarch
2 ts Soy Sauce
2 ts Sherry
MMMMM-------------------------SAUCE-----------------------------------
1/2 ts Sugar
1 tb Soy Sauce
1 ts Cornstarch
1. Marinate the pork slices.

2. Heat the wok and add 2 Tbs of the oil.

3. When the oil begins to smoke add the ginger and garlic, stirring
only a few seconds.

4. Add the broccoli and carrots. Stir-fry for 1 min and add the bamboo
shoots. Stir-fry briefly, and then lower the heat slightly. Cover the
wok and cook the vegetables for 2 mins. Then remove them from the wok.

5. Add the remaining 2 Tbs oil to the wok. Again turn the heat to
high and stir-fry the pork until it loses its pink color.

6. Return the vegetables to the wok, adding the mixed sauce and the
scallions. Stir until it is thickened.

7. Mix in the cashew nuts and serve.
