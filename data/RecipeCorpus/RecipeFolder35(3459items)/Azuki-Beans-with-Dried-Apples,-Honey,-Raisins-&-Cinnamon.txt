Title: Azuki Beans with Dried Apples, Honey, Raisins & Cinnamon
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Honey
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Lemon juice
Vanilla ice cream
3 c  Cooked azuki beans
-liquid reserved
1 c  Dried apples
-cut in 3/4" pieces
1/2 c  Raisins
Place cooked beans in a 3-qt. non-stick saucepan and set aside. In a
small bowl, mix the apples and raisins together and add 1« cups of
the reserved bean liquid. Let stand for 15 minutes, then add to
saucepan along with the honey and cinnamon. Simmer, covered, for
about 8 minutes. Stir in the lemon juice and serve warm, spooned over
vanilla ice cream.

If you reheat the beans and fruit, add « cup boiling water to dilute,
since most of the liquid will have been absorbed.
