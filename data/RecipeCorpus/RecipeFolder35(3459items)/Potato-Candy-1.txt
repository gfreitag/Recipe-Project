Title: Potato Candy 1
Categories: Candies, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Salt
4    Squares Baking chocolate
3/4 c  Cooked cold mashed potatoes
4 c  Shredded coconut -- chopped
4 c  Confectioners' sugar
Mix the potatoes, (Plain mashed, no butter, milk, or salt added)
and the confectioners' sugar.  Stir in coconut, vanilla and salt;
blend well.  Press into one large or two small pans so that candy
will be about 1/2 inch thick.

Melt chocolate over hot water. (Do not allow water to boil If
chocolate gets too hot it may be streaky when hardened.)   Pour
chocolate on top of candy. Cool and cut into squares.
