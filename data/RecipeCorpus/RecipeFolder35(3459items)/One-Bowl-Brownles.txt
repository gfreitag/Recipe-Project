Title: One-Bowl Brownles
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
6 tb Baking cocoa
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ds Salt
1/2 c  Nuts -- chopped
1 c  Butter or margarine
2 c  Sugar
4 ea Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar Beat in eggs and vanilla.
Combine cocoa, flour and salt; stir into creamed mixture. Add nuts.
Pour into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Bake at 375
deg. for 20-25 minutes. Yield: 2-1/2 dozen
