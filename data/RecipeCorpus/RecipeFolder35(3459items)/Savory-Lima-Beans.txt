Title: Savory Lima Beans
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Brown sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Celery salt
1/4 ts Paprika
2 c  Fresh lima beans; shelled
2 sl Bacon
1/2 c  Water
1 tb All-purpose flour
2 tb Onion; chopped
Cook lima beans, covered, in boiling salted water 20 minutes or
until tender; drain well.
Cook bacon until almost crisp; drain and set aside.
Gradually add water to flour, stirring until smooth.  Combine lima
beans, flour mixture, onion, and next 4 ingredients, mixing well.
Pour into a lightly greased 1-quart baking dish.  Place bacon on top.
Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees F for 20 minutes.  Yield: 4 servings
