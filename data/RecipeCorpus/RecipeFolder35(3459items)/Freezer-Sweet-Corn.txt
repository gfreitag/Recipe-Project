Title: Freezer Sweet Corn
Categories: Can/preserv, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
2 ts Salt
4 qt Fresh-cut sweet corn -- 18
To 20 ears
1 qt Hot water
combine all ingredients in a large kettle; simmer for 5-7 minutes,
stirring occasionally. Pour into large shallow containers to cool:
stir occasionally. Spoon into freezer bags or containers; freeze.
Yield: 3 quarts
