Title: Praline Dream Pie
Categories: Holiday, Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
-topping mix
2 3/4 c  Cold milk -- divided
1 ts Vanilla
2 pk Vanilla instant pudding &
-pie filling -- (4-serving
-size)
1/2 c  Chopped pecans or walnuts
1/3 c  Margarine or butter
1/3 c  Firmly packed light brown
Sugar
1    9-inch Baked pastry shell --
-cooled
2    Envelopes Dream Whip whipped
Heat nuts, margarine and sugar in small saucepan until margarine and
sugar are melted and mixture comes to boil. Boil 30 seconds. Spread
on bottom of pastry shell. Cool. Beat whipped topping, 1 cup of the
milk and vanilla in large bowl with electric mixer on high speed
about 6 minutes or until topping thickens and forms peaks. Add
remaining milk and pudding mix; blend on low speed. Beat on high
speed 2 minutes, scraping bowl occasionally. Spoon into pastry shell.
Refrigerate at least 4 hours or until ready to serve. Garnish as
desired. Makes 8 servings.
