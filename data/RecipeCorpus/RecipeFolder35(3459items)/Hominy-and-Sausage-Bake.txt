Title: Hominy and Sausage Bake
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Tex/mexican
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
1/2 c  Green onion; sliced
1 cn Green chilies; chopped, 4oz
2 cn White or yellow hominy; 14oz
4    Eggs; beaten
8 oz Chorizo or pork sausage;
.  cooked and drained
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a 9-inch glass baking dish.
In a large bowl combine the hominy, eggs, chorizo, cheese, onion and
chilies if desired.  Pour into the prepared dish.  Bake for 30 to 35
minutes or until set.  Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
