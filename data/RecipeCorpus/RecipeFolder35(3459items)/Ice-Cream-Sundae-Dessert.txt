Title: Ice Cream Sundae Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 16 servings
Or peppermint ice cream
8 oz Whipped topping -- frozen
Thawed
3 tb Chocolate syrup
1/4 c  Pecans -- chopped
20 ea Chocolate cookies cream
Filled -- crushed
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1/2 ga Vanilla ice cream --
Softened
In a bowl, mix cookie crumbs with butter. Press into the bottom of a
greased 13x9x2 in. pan. Carefully spread ice cream over crust. Spread
whipped topping over ice cream. Dizzle chocolate syrup on top;
sprinkle with nuts. Freeze until firm, about 2 to 4 hours. Yield: 12
to 16 servings.
