Title: Beef With Sour Cream
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Sharp cheese, shredded
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
2 tb Shortening
2 md Onions, thin sliced
1/2 c  Beef broth
3 c  Brown beef cubes
Melt shortening in skillet.  Add onions and saute until soft and
clear. Add broth, beef, cheese and seasonings.  Cover, simmer gently
to heat, Blend in sour cream just before serving.
