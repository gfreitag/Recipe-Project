Title: Scrum-Delicious Burgers
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
6 ea Bacon strips -- cooked &
Crumbled
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
6 ea Hamburger buns -- split
Lettuce leaves and tomato
Slices, optional
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
3 tb Onion -- finely chopped
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- 4 ounces
Shredded
1/3 c  Canned  mushrooms -- sliced
In a medium bowl, combine beef, onion, garlic salt and pepper; mix
well. Shape into six patties, 3/4 in. thick. In a small bowl, combine
the cheese, mushrooms, bacon and mayonnaise; refrigerate. Grill
burgers over medium-hot coals for 10-12 minutes, turning once. During
the last 3 minutes, spoon 1/4 cup of the cheese mixture onto each
burger. Serve on buns with lettuce and tomato if desired. Yield: 6
servings
