Title: Pineapple Beets
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Sliced beets -- l6 ounces
Drained
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Cornstarch
1/4 ts Salt
1 cn Pineapple tidbits -- 8
oz Undrained
In a saucepan, combine brown sugar, cornstarch and salt; add
pineapple and bring to a boil, stirring constantly until thick, about
2 minutes. Add the beets, butter and lemon juice; cook over medium
heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Yield: 4 servings
