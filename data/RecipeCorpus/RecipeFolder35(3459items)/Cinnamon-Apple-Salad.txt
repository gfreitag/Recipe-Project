Title: Cinnamon Apple Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
ds Salt
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Red hot candy
3 c  Boiling water
2 c  Apple sauce
1 lg Box cherry Jello
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
2 tb Mayonnaise
8 oz Cream cheese
1/4 c  Milk
Dissolve gelatin and candy in boiling water, stir in apple sauce,
lemon and salt.  Chill until partially set.  Add nuts.  Pour into 8 x
8 x 2 inch pan. Allow to set.  TOPPING: Blend cream cheese, milk and
mayonnaise. Spread on top of Jello. Randy Rigg
