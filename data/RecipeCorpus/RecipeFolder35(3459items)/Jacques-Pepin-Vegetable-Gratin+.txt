Title: Jacques Pepin Vegetable Gratin+
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese/eggs, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Parsley, minced
3 tb Parmesan, grated
1 1/2 c  Day old hearty bread cubes
1 1/2 c  Cherry tomatoes (mixed
-colors if you can get them)
MMMMM--------------------------OPTIONAL-------------------------------
Eggplant
Leeks
Or what have you?
Zucchini
Sweet bell pepper
Scallions
MMMMM------------------------IN ADDITION-----------------------------
3 tb E.V. olive oil
Fresh herb of your choice
Freshly cracked black pepper
Toss all in a bowl and put into a gratin dish of accomodating size.
Bake in a 350 degree oven till cooked and somewhat browned on top.

We have had this dish and it is lovely. We must try it again, soon.
