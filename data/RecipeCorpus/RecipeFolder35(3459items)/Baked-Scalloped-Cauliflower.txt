Title: Baked Scalloped Cauliflower
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------PREP TIME: 20 MINUTES------------------------
MMMMM-------------------COOK TIME: 30 MINUTES------------------------
-flowerets
1/3 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/3 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1 ea 10oz can cream of celery
-soup
1/2 c  Peppercorn ranch dressing
1 md Califlower, broken into
In large bowl, mix the soup and the dressing together.  Add
cauliflower and toss to coat.  Arrange in a greased casserole dish.
Blend the cheese and bread crumbs together and sprinkle over
cauliflower.  Bake in a 350F oven for about 20 minutes covered, then
uncover and bake an additional 10 minutes. Origin: What's Cooking,
13th issue, booklet by Kraft. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/95.
