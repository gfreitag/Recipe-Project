Title: Cherry Fluff
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 16 servings
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk --
-14 oz.
1/2 c  Coconut flakes
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
1 pk Whipped topping -- frozen
Thawed
1 cn Crushed pineapple -- 20 oz
Drained
1 cn Cherry pie filling -- 21 oz
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl; mix. well. Chill overnight.
Yield: 12-16 servings.
