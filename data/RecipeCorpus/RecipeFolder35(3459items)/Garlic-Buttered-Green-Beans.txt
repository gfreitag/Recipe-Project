Title: Garlic-Buttered Green Beans
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 ts Onion powder
1 1/2 ts Garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
1 lb Green beans -- fresh or
Frozen
1/2 c  Fresh mushrooms -- sliced
6 tb Butter or margarine
Cook green beans in water to cover until tender. Meanwhile, in a
skillet, saute mushrooms in butter until tender. Add onion powder and
garlic powder. Drain beans; add to skillet and toss. Season with salt
and pepper. Yield: 6 servings
