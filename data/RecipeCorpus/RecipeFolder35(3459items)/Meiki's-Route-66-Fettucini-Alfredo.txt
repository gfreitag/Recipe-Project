Title: Meiki's Route 66 Fettucini Alfredo
Categories: Main dish, Pasta/rice, Route 66
Yield: 1 servings
ds White Pepper
1 tb Sour Cream
1 ts Parmesan Cheese -- ground
1 ts Romano Cheese -- ground
5 oz Fettucine -- pre-cooked
1 1/2 ts Unsalted Butter
1/3 ts Garlic -- freshly ground
4 oz Heavy Whipping Cream
ds Salt
Briefly heat medium skillet; add butter. Over low flame, coat bottom
of skillet with butter and add garlic. Stir until sauteed, then add
cream. Increase flame slightly and heat, stirring constantly. Add
salt, pepper, and sour cream and bring to heat while stirring. When
sour cream is completely blended, combine and stir in cheeses. Stir
pasta into sauce over low flame. Serve immediately. Makes 1 serving.
