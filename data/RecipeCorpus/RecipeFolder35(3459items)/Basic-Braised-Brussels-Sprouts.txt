Title: Basic Braised Brussels Sprouts
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
Leaves removed
1/2 ts Salt
1 lb Small firm bright green
Brussels sprouts, rinsed
Stem ends and discolored
Bring sprouts, « cup water and salt to boil in 2-quart saucepan over
medium-high heat. Lower heat, cover and simmer, shaking pan once or
twice, until knife tip inserted into brussels sprout center meets no
resistance, 8-10 minutes. Drain well and serve or continue with
another recipe.

Variation for Brussels Sprouts Braised in Cream

Follow recipe for Basic Braised Brussels Sprouts. substituting 1 cup
whipping cream for « cup water. Increase cooking time to 10-12
minutes. Season with a pinch of grated nutmeg and ground black
pepper. Serve without draining.
