Title: Round Steak Parmesan
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
Crumbs
3 tb Parmesan cheese
Olive oil
Round steak (cut into long
Thin strips 2 inches)
1 c  Flavored Progresso bread
Dip steak strips into olive oil then into bread crumb cheese mixture.
Roll and hold with toothpick.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
Serve with pasta and sauce combination. Randy Rigg
