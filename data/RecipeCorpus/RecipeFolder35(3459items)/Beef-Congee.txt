Title: Beef Congee
Categories: Chinese, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1    Scallion -- chopped
2 c  Beef -- shredded
1 c  Rice -- wash well & drain
3 qt Water
2 c  Beef Broth
MMMMM----------------------SEASONINGS--------------------------------
1/2 ts Sesame Oil
Salt And Pepper
1 tb Soy Sauce
1 ts Sugar
1. Marinate the beef shreds in the soy sauce.

2. Bring the rice and water to a boil in a large saucepan. Cover and
simmer for 1 1/2 hrs without stirring.

3. Add the two cups of broth and sugar, bringing it to a boil. Stir
in the beef and when the rice again boils turn down the heat.

4. Simmer for 15 mins more. Stir in the chopped scallions, sesame
oil, salt and pepper.
