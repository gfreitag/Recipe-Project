Title: Fried Quail with Onion Gravy
Categories: Main dish, Game
Yield: 15 servings
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  ;Water
2/3 c  Onion; minced
Dash of garlic salt
30 ea Quail; cleaned
Salt and pepper
All-purpose flour
8 sl Bacon
2 c  Peanut oil
Sprinkle quail with salt and pepper to taste; dredge in flour, and
set aside.
Fry bacon in a large skillet.  Remove bacon, reserve for other uses.
Add peanut oil to drippings in skillet; heat over medium heat.  Add
quail; cook 10 to 12 minutes on each side or until done.  Remove
quail from pan; drain on paper towels.  Reserve 1/4 cup drippings in
pan.
Add flour to drippings, stirring until smooth.  Cook 1 minute,
stirring constantly.  Gradually add water, stirring well.  Add onion
and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened and
bubbly. Stir in 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/8 teaspoon pepper, and garlic
salt.

Yield: 15 servings
