Title: Mom's Aglio E Olio
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Italian, Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 /2 cup
Salt & black pepper to taste
1/2 c  Olive oil
4 cl Garlic; minced
MMMMM--------------------OPTIONAL INGREDIENTS-------------------------
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
1/2 c  Parsley; chopped
While the pasta is cooking, heat the oil in a small skillet and saute
the garlic over low heat for a few minutes, until it begins to turn a
light golden color (NOT Brown).

Remove from the heat.  Pour over hot pasta.  Stir in the salt &
pepper and any optional ingredients you may desire.
