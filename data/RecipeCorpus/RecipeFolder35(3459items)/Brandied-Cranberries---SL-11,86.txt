Title: Brandied Cranberries - SL 11/86
Categories: Side dish, Fruit, Holiday
Yield: 2 cups
1/4 c  Brandy
1/4 c  Sugar
4 c  Fresh cranberries
2 c  Sugar
Place cranberries in a shallow baking pan; sprinkle with 2 cups
sugar. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove
from oven; stir in brandy and 1/4 cup sugar.  Chill.  Yield: 2 cups.
