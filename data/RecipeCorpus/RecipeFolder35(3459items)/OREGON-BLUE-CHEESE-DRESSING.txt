Title: OREGON BLUE CHEESE DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Salsa/sauce, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 cups
MMMMM--------------------NORMA WRENN NPXR56B-------------------------
1 tb Milk;
1 ts Cider vinegar;
1 cl Garlic; minced
1/8 ts Dry mustard;
1/8 ts Freshly ground pepper;
1 c  Mayonnaise;
8 oz Carton commercial sour cream
1 1/2 pk (4-oz ea) blue cheese;
-- crumbled
1/4 c  Olive oil;
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl; stir until well blended.
Cover and chill.  Serve over fruit or salad greens.

Source:  Oregon Sample:  Resorts & Recipes, The Assistance League of
Corvallis, Oregon, America's Best Recipes, A 1988 Hometown Collection,
Oxmoor House.
