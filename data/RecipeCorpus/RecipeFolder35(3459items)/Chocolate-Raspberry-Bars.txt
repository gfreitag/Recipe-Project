Title: Chocolate Raspberry Bars
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 36 servings
2 tb Milk
1 c  Vanilla-flavored chips --
Melted 6 oz.
Glaze:
3/4 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
2 tb Shortening
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 c  Confectioner's sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
Filling
1/2 c  Raspberry jam -- seedless
4 oz Cream cheese -- softened
In a bowl combine flour and confectioners' sugar; cut in butter until
crumbly. Press into an ungreased 9 in. square baking pan. Bake at 375
deg. for 15 to 18 min. or until browned. Spread jam over warm crust.
In a small mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and milk until smooth. Add
vanilla chips; beat until smooth. Spread carefully over jam layer.
Cool completely. Chill until set about 1 hour. For glaze, melt
chocolate chips and shortening; spread over filling. Chill for 10
min. Cut into bars; chill another hour. Store in the refrigerator.
Yield: 3 dozen.
