Title: Basil Chicken Medley
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
1 md Zucchini -- cut into cubes
2 md Tomatoes -- cut into chunks
1 tb Dried basil
2 tb Vinegar
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Rice -- or pasta cooked
1 tb Olive oil
3    Cloves garlic cloves --
Minced
2    Whole chicken breasts
Without skin -- boned 1 in
Striped
Heat oil in a skillet; saute garlic. Add chicken and cook until no
longer pink; removed and keep warm. Combine zucchini, tomatoes, basil
and vingar; toss to coat vegetable well. Add to skillet and stir-fry
about 3 to 5 min. Return chicken to skillet and heat through. Serve
immediately over rice or pasta. Yield: 4 servings.
