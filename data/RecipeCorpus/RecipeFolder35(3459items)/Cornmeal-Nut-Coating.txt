Title: Cornmeal Nut Coating
Categories: Poultry, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------BINDER--------------------------------
1 tb Vegetable oil
3/4 c  Buttermilk
1 lg Egg
MMMMM--------------------------COATING-------------------------------
1 c  Cornmeal
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Nuts, finely chopped
-(pecans, walnuts,
-pistachios, etc)
Dip the chicken in the binder, then dredge it in the coating.
Refrigerate the coated chicken for 20 minutes before cooking to help
the coating adhere. Coated chicken breasts are best sautÂ‚ed.

Makes enough for 2 whole chicken breasts.
