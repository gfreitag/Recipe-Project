Title: Peanut Dressing - SLC
Categories: Dress/stuff, Holiday, Side dish
Yield: 10 servings
1 ea Egg; beaten
1 tb Rubbed sage
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
4 1/2 c  ;water
1 1/2 c  Celery; finely chopped
3/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
1/2 c  Fresh parsley; chopped
3/4 c  Butter or margarine; melted
12 c  Soft breadcrumbs
2 c  Salted peanuts; chopped
Saute first 3 ingredients in butter in a large Dutch oven until
tender. Add remaining ingredients; stir well.  Spoon dressing into a
lightly greased 13" x 9" x 2" pan; bake at 350 degrees F for 45
minutes to 1 hour or until lightly browned around edges.  Yield: 10
to 12 servings.

Note: Dressing may be spooned into turkey cavities.  Spoon any
remaining dressing into a lightly greased 2-quart casserole dish;
bake at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes.
