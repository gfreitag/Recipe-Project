Title: Easy Cherry Tarts
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Frozen whipped topping,
Thawed
Chocolate sprinkles
1 cn Cherry pie filling -- 21
Ounces
1 pk Single-serve graham cracker
Crusts -- 4 oz 6 crusts
Divide pie filling among the crusts. Top with whipped topping and
chocolate sprinkles. Chill. Yield: 6 servings
