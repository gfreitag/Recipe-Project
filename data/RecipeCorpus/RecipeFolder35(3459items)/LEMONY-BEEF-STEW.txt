Title: LEMONY BEEF STEW
Categories: Crockpot, Soup/stew, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM--------------------------NPFN03A-------------------------------
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Rosemary Leaves; crushed
3 ea Med. Potatoes; peeled, cubed
2 c  Carrots; peeled and sliced
2 c  Celery; sliced diagonally
1 ea Sm. Whole Onions; drained
1 ea Pk Frozen Peas; slightly
-thawed
2 tb Flour
3 lb Beef Stew Meat
1 x  Salt and Pepper
1 x  Flour
1 x  Oil
8 oz Tomato Sauce
1 ts Lemon Peel; fresh grated
2 tb Lemon Juice; fresh squeezed
2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1 ts Celery Seed
1/2 ts Salt
Season beef with slat and pepper, lightly coat with flour, and brown
in 2 parts in large skillet. Pour off fat, put meat in crock pot. Add
everything except last 3 ingredients. Cover and cook on low 8 - 10
hours. Add onions and peas and cook 1 - 2 hours longer. Gradually
blend 1/4 c water into the 2 T flour. Add to stew mixture and cook on
high, stirring until thickened. From "Woman's Day Crockery Cook Book"
