Title: Pork And Turkey Sausages Or Burgers.
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
Half level tsp black pepper
2    Level tsp paprika
Pn Ground cloves.
450 g  Or 1 lb lean pork.
450 g  Or 1 lb turkey meat.
2    To 3 garlic cloves
Place all ingredients in a food processor or liquidiser and process
until smooth. Shape into sausages or burgers. Dry fry slowly, grill
or bake in an oven at 200 c, 400 f, or gas mark 6.
