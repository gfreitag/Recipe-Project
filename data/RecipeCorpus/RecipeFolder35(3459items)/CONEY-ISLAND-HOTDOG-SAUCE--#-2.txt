Title: CONEY ISLAND HOTDOG SAUCE  # 2
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 1 SERVINGS--------------------------
1 ts White wine vinegar
1 ts Allspice, ground
1 ea Tb Salt
1 ts Black pepper; freshly ground
1 ts Cinnamon, ground
1 ea Onion; chopped
3 ea Bay leaves
1 qt Water, hot
1/4 ts Garlic powder
2 lb Beef, lean ground
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1 cn Tomato paste, (12 oz)
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Cumin, ground
3 ea Tb Chili powder
Pour water in a large sauce pan.  Crumble in beef (do not brown). Add
rest of ingredients.  Simmer for about 3 hours, or to desired
consistency, stirring occasionally.  Remove bay leaves before
serving. Source: ORIGIN UNKNOWN  -  From John Schlueter's Database
