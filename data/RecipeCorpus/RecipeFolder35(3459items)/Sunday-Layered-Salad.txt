Title: Sunday Layered Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Drained sliced
2 c  Mayonnaise
Or salad dressing
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
8 ea Bacon strips --
Cooked/crumbled
1    Head iceburg lettuce --
Chopped
1 pk Frozen peas -- l0 ounces
Thawed
1/4 c  Red onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper -- diced
1 cn Water chestnuts
In a 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan, layer all ingredients in order
given. Cover and refrigerate until serving time. Do not toss. Cut into
squares to serve. Will keep for several days. Yield: 8-10 servings
