Title: Brown Beef Cubes
Categories: Meats
Yield: 5 quarts
Salt
Pepper
4 tb Shortening
12 lb Beef, cut for stew
Heat shortening in 2 heavy skillets;  brown beef on all sides in hot
fat.

Place beef in large heavy kettle; add seasonings and water to a depth
of 1 inch.

Cover, simmer until tender, 1-2 hours.  Cool, pack meat in quart,
pint and half pint containers ( for various recipes ), packaging
broth separately. Freeze
