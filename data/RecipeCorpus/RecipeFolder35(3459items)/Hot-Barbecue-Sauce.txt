Title: Hot Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Hot/spicy, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 cups
1/2 c  Catsup
1    Beef bouillion cube
1 ts Cider vinegar
1/2 ts Tobasco
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/2 ts Paprika
1 tb Butter
1 md Onion,chopped
1 ts Dry mustard
1 tb Cornstarch
1 c  Water
1 tb Liquid sucaryl
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
In small saucepan,melt butter over low heat until frothy.Add onion and
saute until soft and golden in colour.Blend in mustard and cornstarch
to make a smooth paste.Add remaining ingredients; bring to the
boil.Reduce heat and simmer,stirring occasionally,for at least 60
minutes.Use to baste hot dogs,hamburgers,spareribs or chicken.

Per each tbsp serving: calories 16 trace of protein fat 0.5g
carbhydrate 2.5g (with sugar 26 calories)

Need not be calculated as exchanges.
