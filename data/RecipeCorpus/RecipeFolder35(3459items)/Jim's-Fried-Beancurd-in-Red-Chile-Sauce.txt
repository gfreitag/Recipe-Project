Title: Jim's Fried Beancurd in Red Chile Sauce
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian, Korean
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Sesame oil
1    Garlic clove, minced
3    Spring onions, sliced
19 oz Tofu, firm
2 c  Oil, for frying
1 ts Sesame seeds
2 ts Red chile flakes
1 ts Ginger, grated
2 tb Soy sauce
1/4 ts Chinese 5 spice
1 tb Water
1/2 ts Sugar
In a bowl, combine red chiles, soy sauce, water, sesame oil, garlic,
onions, ginger, 5 spice powder and sugar.

Cut tofu into strips 3" long by 1" wide by 1/2 "thick, and pat dry
with paper towels.  Heat 1-2 " of vegetable oil in a skillet or wok
set over moderately high heat. Fry tofu pieces until golden brown on
all sides, turning once. Remove tofu from oil and place in a serving
dish . Pour chile mixture over tofu, turning pieces to coat evenly.
Sprinkle with the sesame seeds and serve warm with steamed rice, Kim
Chi and assorted vegetable relishes and pickles.
