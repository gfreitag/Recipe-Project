Title: Fresh Spinach Salad Delight
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Vegetables
Yield: 16 servings
MMMMM-----------------SOUTHERN LIVING; JULY 1980----------------------
6 sl Bacon; cooked and crumbled
4    Green onions; thinly sliced
3    Tomatoes; cut into wedges
2    Eggs; hard-cooked; chopped
1 lb Spinach, fresh; torn in bite
-size pieces
1 c  Palm, heart of; sliced thin
14 oz Artichoke hearts; drained
-and diced
MMMMM--------------------TANGY SALAD DRESSING-------------------------
1/3 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Oil, vegetable
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 sm Onion; minced
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Vinegar
Combine all ingredients except dressing in a large salad bowl; toss
lightly.  Cover salad, and chill until serving time.  Serve with Tangy
Salad Dressing.

Tangy Salad Dressing:
Combine all ingredients in container of electric blender; blend well.
Chill before serving. Yield: 2 cups
