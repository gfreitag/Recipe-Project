Title: Tuna Egg Scramble
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Tuna (7 oz)
2 tb Butter
6 ea Eggs
1/3 c  Light cream or milk
1/2 ts Salt
Blend eggs, cream, and salt.  Stir in tuna.  pour into hot skillet
with butter.  Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally until eggs are
thickened, but still moist.
