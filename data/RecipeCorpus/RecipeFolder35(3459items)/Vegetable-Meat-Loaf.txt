Title: Vegetable Meat Loaf
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 ds Garlic powder
5 tb Chili sauce
Or ketchup, divided
2 ea Baked potatoes -- optional
Shredded cheddar cheese,
Optional
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 sl Bread
Torn into small pieces
1 ea Egg -- beaten
1/4 c  Carrot -- shredded
2 tb Onion -- finely chopped
2 tb Green pepper -- finely
Chopped
2 tb Celery -- finely chopped
In a bowl, combine ground beef, bread, egg, carrot, onion, green
pepper, celery, seasonings and 2 tablespoons chili sauce or ketchup.
Form into a loaf in an ungreased 5-3/4-in. x 3-in. x 2-in. loaf pan.
Spoon remaining chili sauce or ketchup over loaf. Bake, uncovered, at
350 deg. for 45-50 minutes or until meat is no longer pink. Serve
with baked potatoes topped with shredded cheddar cheese if desired.
Yield: 2 servings
