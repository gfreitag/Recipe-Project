Title: Jim's Salsa
Categories: Tomatoes, Vegetables, Chilies
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Fresh ground black pepper
3 sm Fresh habanero peppers,
-minced
20 oz Fresh tomatoes, minced
2 oz Boiling water
1/4 ts Onion powder*
1/4 ts Garlic powder*
1/4 ts Basil*
1/8 ts Tarragon*
In a small bowl with a cover, combine onion and garlic powder with
basil, tarragon, salt and pepper. Pour boiling water over ingredients
and stir. Cover and let sit for 20 minutes. Meanwhile, mince
habaneros and tomato. Add to spice mixture, gently stir to mix. Cover
and refrigerate at least 2 hours, overnight is better. *This items
are home grown, dried, powdered and/or crushed. The flavor is more
intense than store bought. If using store bought ingredients you may
want to use more. SOURCE:*Jim Bodle POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 9/95
