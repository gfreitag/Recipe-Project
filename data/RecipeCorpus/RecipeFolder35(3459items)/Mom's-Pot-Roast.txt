Title: Mom's Pot Roast
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Water
10 sm Whole onions
10 sm Carrots, halved
2 cn Tomato sauce, 8 oz.
4 lb Chuck roast
1 tb Paprika
Salt and pepper
2 tb Oil
Sprinkle meat with paprika, salt and pepper.  Brown in oil in a Dutch
oven. Add water; cover and simmer for 1 1/2 hours or until meat is
tender. Arrange vegetables around the meat; pour in tomato sauce.
Cover and simmer for 1 hour. Randy Rigg
