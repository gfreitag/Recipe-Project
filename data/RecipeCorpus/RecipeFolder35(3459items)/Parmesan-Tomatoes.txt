Title: Parmesan Tomatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Dried basil
Or 1 tablespoon snipped
Fresh basll
1/2 ts Fresh parsley -- chopped
1 ds Pepper
2 lg Tomatoes
3 tb Dry bread crumbs
2 tb Parmesan cheese -- grated
2 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
Remove stems from the tomatoes and halve crosswise. Place, cut side
up, in a small baking dish. Combine all remaining ingredients;
sprinkle over tomato tops. Bake at 375 deg. for 15 minutes or until
heated through. Yield: 4 servings
