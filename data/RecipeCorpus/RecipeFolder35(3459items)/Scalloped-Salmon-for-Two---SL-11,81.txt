Title: Scalloped Salmon for Two - SL 11/81
Categories: Main dish, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
1 tb All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb ;buttered soft breadcrumbs
2 tb Onion; chopped
1 tb Green pepper; chopped
2 tb Butter or margarine; melted
1 ea (7-3/4 oz) can salmon;
-drained and flaked
Saute onion and green pepper in butter until tender but not brown.
Add next 4 ingredients; mix well.  Spoon mixture into buttered
individual baking shells; top with breadcrumbs.  Bake at 400F for 15
minutes. Yield: 2 servings.
