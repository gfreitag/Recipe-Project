Title: Cranberry Pineapple Salad
Categories: Salads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 12 servings
3/4 c  Orange juice
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
Lettuce leaves
Mayonnaise or salad
Dressing
1 pk Gelatin -- 6 ounces
Raspberry
1 3/4 c  Boiling water
1 cn Cranberry sauce -- 16 ounces
Jellied
1 cn Crushed pineapple -- 8 oz
Undrained
In a bowl, dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Break up and stir in
cranberry sauce. Add pineapple, orange juice and lemon juice. Chill
until partially set. Stir in nuts. Pour into an 11-in. x 7-in. x
2-in. dish. Chill until firm. Cut into squares; serve each on a
lettuce leaf and top with a dollop of mayonnaise. Yield: 12 servings
