Title: Clam Pie (Mrs. Kitching's Smith Island Cookbook)
Categories: Pie/tart, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
10 x  Chowder clams
2 x  Eggs, well beaten
1 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
2 x  9 inch pie crusts
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Soda cracker crumbs
1/4 ts Pepper
Prepare pie crust, setting aside top crust.  In bowl, combine clams,
milk, eggs, crumbs, salt asnd pepper.  Pour into pie shell and dot
with butter. Cover with top crust, trimming overhanging pastry to 1/2
inch.  Fold it under and flute to insure no leaking.  Cut several 1
inch slits near center of pie.  Place in 450 F degree oven for 15
minutes, then reduce heat andbake at 350 F for 30 minutes.
