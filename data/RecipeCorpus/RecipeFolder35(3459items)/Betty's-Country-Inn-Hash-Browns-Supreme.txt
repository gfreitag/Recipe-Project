Title: Betty's Country Inn Hash Browns Supreme
Categories: Route 66, Vegetables, Potatoes, Sausage
Yield: 2 servings
*****  NONE  *****
In separate skillets or on the grill, fry 2 cups hash brown potatoes
and 4 oz sausage. Scramble together. Add 2 Tbs each of finely chopped
onions and bell peppers. Stir and turn until done. Shape potato
mixture like an omelet and sprinkle grated cheddar cheese on top.
Allow cheese to melt then turn out on two platters and add freshly
chopped tomatoes on top. Makes 2 servings.
