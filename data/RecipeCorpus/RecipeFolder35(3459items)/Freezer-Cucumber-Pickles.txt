Title: Freezer Cucumber Pickles
Categories: Can/preserv, Tasteofhome
Yield: 10 servings
3/4 c  Walter
4 c  Sugar
2 c  Cider vinegar
4 lb Cucumbers -- sliced
8 c  Onions -- thinly sliced
1/4 c  Salt
Combine cucumbers, onions, salt and water in two large bowls. Let
stand at room temperature for 2 hours. Add sugar and binegar; stir
until sugar dissolves. Pack into 1 pint freezer containers, leaving 1
in. headspace. Cover and freeze for up to 6 weeks. Thaw at room
temperature for 4 hours before serving. Yield; 10 pints.
