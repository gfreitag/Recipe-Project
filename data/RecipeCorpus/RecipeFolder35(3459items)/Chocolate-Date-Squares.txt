Title: Chocolate Date Squares
Categories: Desserts, Tasteofhome
Yield: 24 servings
2 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Chopped dates
1 c  Hot water
1 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Shortening
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips --
6    Oz
In a bowl, combine dates and water; set aside to cool (do not drain).
In a mixing bowl, cream sugar and shortening. Add eggs, flour, baking
soda and salt; mix well. Add dates. Pour into a greased and floured
13x9x2 inch. baking pan. Combine the topping ingredients; sprinkle
over batter. bake at 350 deg. for 40 min. or until a wooden pick
inserted in the center comes out clean. Yield: 24 servings.
