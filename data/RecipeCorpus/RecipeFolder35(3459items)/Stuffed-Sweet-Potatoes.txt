Title: Stuffed Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
3/4 ts Salt
6 md Sweet potatoes
1 cn Crushed pineapple -- 8
oz Drained
1/2 c  Orange juice
Pierce sweet potatoes with a fork. Bake at 400 deg. for 55-65 minutes
or until tender. Cool Slightly. Remove pulp from potatoes, keeping
the skins intact. In a bowl, combine the pulp with pineapple, orange
juice, butter and salt. Refill potato shells and place in a greased
13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Bake at 400 deg. for 20 minutgs
or until heated through. Yield: 6 Servings
