Title: Peach Pie Alaska
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Diced, fresh peaches
3 1/2 c  Cool Whip topping, thawed
1    9 inch deep dish pie shell,
Baked and cooled
3 oz Package peach gelatin
(Jello brand)
2/3 c  Boling water
1 c  Vanilla ice cream
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water.  Add ice cream by spoonfuls.  Stir
until melted and smooth.  Blend in whipped topping and fruit.  Chill
if necessary, until mixture will mound.  Spoon into crust.  Chill
about 3 hours or freeze until firm. Randy Rigg
