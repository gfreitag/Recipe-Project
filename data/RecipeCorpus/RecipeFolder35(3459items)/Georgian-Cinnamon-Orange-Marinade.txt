Title: Georgian Cinnamon-Orange Marinade
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
-minced (1 cup)
2    Cinnamon sticks
3 tb Paprika
1 ts Cracked black peppercorns
1/4 ts Grated nutmeg
1 ts (to 2 tsp) sugar
-or to taste
1/2 ts Salt or to taste
3 tb Olive oil
4    (or 5) oranges
-(2 cups juice)
2    (to 4) lemons
-(1/2 cup juice)
1/2 c  Dry white wine
3    Garlic clove(s)
-minced (1 tbs)
1 tb Ginger, minced
1 md Onion(s)
Grate the zest of 1 orange and 1 lemon. Juice the oranges and lemons.
Combine the orange juice, lemon juice, and wine in a saucepan and boil
until only « cup liquid remains. Let cool.

Combine the garlic, ginger, and onion in a bowl. Stir in the juice
mixture, grated zests, spices, sugar, salt, and oil. Marinate
swordfish steaks or boneless chicken breasts for 1-2 hours, whole
chickens or game hens overnight, turning once or twice.

Makes enough for 1«-2 pounds seafood or poultry.
