Title: 4-H Corn Speclal
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Or frozen or canned sweet
Corn
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 c  Crushed saltines
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 lb Ground beef
1 sm Onion -- finely chopped
1 1/2 c  Rice -- cooked
2 c  Fresh tomatoes -- seeded
Chopped
Or 1 can (16 ounces)tomatoes
With liquid,
2 c  Fresh corn
In a large skillet, brown beef and onion; drain. Stir in rice,
tomatoes, corn, salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce and hot pepper
sauce. Pour into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish.
Combine cracker crumbs and butter; sprinkle on top. Bake at 350 deg.
for 30 minutes. Yield: 6-8 servings
