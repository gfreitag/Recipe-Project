Title: Pita Chips
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 48 chips
1 tb Extra-virgin olive oil (opt)
3 lg (or 4 small) pita breads
1. Preheat the oven to 350Ã¸F.

2. Separate the pita breads lengthwise into halves. Lightly brush
each half with olive oil. Cut each half into 8 wedges (6 for small
breads).

3. Arrange the pita chips on a baking sheet and bake for 8-10
minutes, or until golden brown. Transfer to a cake tin to cool.

Note: For extra color, you could sprinklw the chips with white or
black sesame seeds before baking.
