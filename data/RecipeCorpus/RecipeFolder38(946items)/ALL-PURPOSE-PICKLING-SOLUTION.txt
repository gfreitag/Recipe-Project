Title: ALL PURPOSE PICKLING SOLUTION
Categories: Pickles, Garlic
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Salt Per Quart Of Water
1    Clove Garlic
1    Stalk Of Celery, Sliced
1    Carrot, Sliced
1    Hot Chili Pepper
1    Bay Leaf
8    Spice Balls
1    Bud Of Dill
1 lb Washed Pickling Cucumbers
Combine all ingredients, except cucumbers, in a pan and bring to a
boil. Then prick the cucumbers with a fork and add to pickling
solution. Put in jars and seal. Let stand for 3 weeks before eating.
Typed by Syd Bigger.
