Title: ALMOST COCO'S SOUR CREAM DRESSING
Categories: Copy cat, Restaurant, Salad dress, Copycat
Yield: 24 Servings
1 1/4 c  Milk
1 c  Buttermilk
2 1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Garlic salt
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
In a mixing bowl, combine milk, buttermilk, mayonnaise, vinegar,
Parmesan cheese, pepper, and garlic salt. Mix well with wire whisk.
Fold in sour cream, leaving lumps intact. Refrigerate in a tightly
closed container. Makes 6 cups.
