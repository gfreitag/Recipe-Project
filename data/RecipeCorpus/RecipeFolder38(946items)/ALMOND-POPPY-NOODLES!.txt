Title: ALMOND POPPY NOODLES!
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
8 oz Medium egg noodles
4 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Slivered almonds
1 tb Poppy seeds
Cook noodles according to package directions. In large skillet, melt
1 T butter and lightly brown almonds. Stir in remaining butter,
noodles and poppy seeds; heat through. Serve, if desired with pork
roast.[1-2]
