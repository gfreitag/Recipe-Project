Title: AJILIMOJILI (GARLIC & PEPPER SAUCE)
Categories: Sauce/gravy, Puerto rico, Caribbean, Upload
Yield: 3 Cups
3    Peppers, red, hot; seeded
3    Peppers, bell, red; seeded
4    Peppercorns, white
4 lg Garlic cloves; peeled
2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Lime juice
1/2 c  Oil, olive
Pound peppers, peppercorns, garlic and salt in a mortar, or chop in
processor finely. Transfer to a bowl and beat in lime juice and oil.
Served with suckling pig.
