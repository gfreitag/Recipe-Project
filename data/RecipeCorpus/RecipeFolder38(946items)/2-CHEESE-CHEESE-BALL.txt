Title: 2-CHEESE CHEESE BALL
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 8 Servings
16 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 c  Grated sharp cheddar cheese
1 pk Hidden Valley Original--
Ranch Dressing (small size)
Walnuts or Pecans
Blend together cheeses and Hidden Valley dressing package contents
with hands and shape into ball or log. Roll in walnuts or pecans.
Refrigerate.
