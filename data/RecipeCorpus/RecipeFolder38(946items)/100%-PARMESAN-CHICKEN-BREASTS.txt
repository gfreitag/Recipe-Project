Title: 100% PARMESAN CHICKEN BREASTS
Categories: Poultry, Cheese
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Chicken breasts without skin
2 tb Shedd's spread country crock
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
1/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Parsley
1/4 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
Recipe by: Kraft recipe Preparation Time: 0:30 Heat oven to 400F.
Spray 15x10x1" baking pan with no-stick cooking spray.
Dip chicken in Shedd spread; coat with combined remaining
ingredients. Place in prepared pan.  Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until
tender.
