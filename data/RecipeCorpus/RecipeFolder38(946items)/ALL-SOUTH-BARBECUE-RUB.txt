Title: ALL-SOUTH BARBECUE RUB
Categories: Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Salt
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Ground cumin
2 tb Chili powder
2 tb Black pepper -- freshly
1    Cracked
1 tb Cayenne pepper
4 tb Paprika
All you do is throw them together and mix them well.
