Title: ALMOND~ BACON~ CHEESE SPREAD
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  ROASTED ALMONDS
2 x  STRIP CRISP FRIED BACON
1 c  GRATED AMERICAN CHEESE
1 tb CHOPPED GREEN ONION
1/2 c  MAYONNAISE
1/4 ts SALT
CHOP ALMONDS VERY FINE. CRUMBLE BACON TO FINE TEXTURE. MIX ALL
INGREDIENTS UNTIL WELL BLENDED. PLACE IN SMALL SERVING DISH.
