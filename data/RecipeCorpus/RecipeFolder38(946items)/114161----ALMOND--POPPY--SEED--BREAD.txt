Title: 114161 -- ALMOND  POPPY  SEED  BREAD
Categories: B, R, E, A
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Flour
2 3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Salt
3 ts Each poppy seeds, vanilla,
Butter and almond flavoring
3    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Oil
Blend together in one bowl and beat 2 minutes with electric beater.
Turn into 2 greased loaf pan (or 4 mini loaf pans). Bake
approximately 60 minutes at 350 degrees. A toothpick inserted in
center and coming out clean indicates bread is done. Glaze: If
desired, blend in saucepan 1/2 cup orange juice, 3/4 cup sugar and
1/2 teaspoon of above flavorings. Bring to a boil, simmer for 5
minutes. Brush on loaves while warm.
