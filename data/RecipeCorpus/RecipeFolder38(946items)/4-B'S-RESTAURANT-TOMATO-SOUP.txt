Title: 4 B'S RESTAURANT TOMATO SOUP
Categories: Soups, Copycat
Yield: 8 Servings
1 cn 28 0z diced tomatoes
1 c  Chicken broth
1/4 c  Butter
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Chopped onion
1/8 ts Baking soda
2 c  Milk
In a saucepan, combine the first 6 ingredients.  Cover and simmer for
1 hour.  Heat milk, add to tomato mixture just before serving. makes
about
1 1/2    quarts.
