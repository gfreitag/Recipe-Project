Title: 7 OR 9 GRAIN BREAD (ABM)
Categories: Digest, Oct., Abm
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/8    Water/Milk
1/2 tb Fruit Juice Concentrate
3 tb Honey/Maple Syrup
1/3    To 3/4 tsp Salt
1 1/2    To 3 Tbs Vital Gluten (opt.)
3/4 c  7/9 Grain Cereal
2 1/4 c  Whole Wheat Flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
an easy low-rising bread - 7/9 grain cereal may be found at your HFS.
~ Flour Equivalent 3 cups
