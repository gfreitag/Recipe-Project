Title: 150 YEAR OLD OATMEAL COOKIES::GWHP32A
Categories: Cookies, Dairy, Katesmith, Family
Yield: 36 Servings
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Lard
3 ea Egg yolks
2 c  Flour
2 c  Oatmeal; old fashioned
1/2 c  Raisins; chopped
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
MMMMM------------------DISOLVE IN 1/2 C. WATER-----------------------
1 ts Soda
1 ts Cinnamon
3 ea Egg whites; well beaten
Mix ingredients, add the egg whites last.  This is a very stiff
mixture - but do not moisten.  Drop with tsp. 2 inches apart. Bake in
moderate oven.---Barb
