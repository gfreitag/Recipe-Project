Title: 461743 -- BAKED  BEANS
Categories: D, I, A, B, E
Yield: 1 Servings
2    (16 oz.) cans French style
Beans
1 tb Dehydrated onion flakes
1 c  Tomato juice
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Dry mustard
Artificial sweetener to
Equal 12 tsp.
Sugar
Drain beans and empty into bowl.  Add remaining ingredients. Mix
lightly and turn into baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
