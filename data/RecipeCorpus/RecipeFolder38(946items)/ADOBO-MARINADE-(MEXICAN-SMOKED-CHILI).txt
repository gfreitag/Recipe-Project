Title: ADOBO MARINADE (MEXICAN SMOKED CHILI)
Categories: Sauces, Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
3 ea ORANGES
1 ea LIME
2 ea CANNED CHIPOTLE CHILIES
1 ts GARLIC POWDER
1/2 ts CUMIN
2 ts OREGANO
1/2 ts BLACK PEPPER
2 tb WINE VINEGAR
1/2 ts SALT
JUICE THE LIME AND ORANGE. FINELY CHOP THE CHILIES. PLACE JUICE,
GARLIC, CHILIES AND REMAINING INGREDIENTS IN BLENDER. PUREE UNTIL
SMOOTH. MAKES ENOUGH MARINADE FOR ABOUT 2 LBS. BEEF, PORK, CHICKEN.
