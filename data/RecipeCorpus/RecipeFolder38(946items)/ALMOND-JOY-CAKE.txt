Title: ALMOND JOY CAKE
Categories: Box cakes, Chocolate
Yield: 10 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
1 pk Devil's food cake mix
24    Large marshmallows
14 oz Coconut
1 c  Evaporated milk
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Almonds; chopped
Make chocolate cake according to package directions in 2 round pans.
Make favorite chocolate frosting for cake. While cake is cooling, heat
evaporated milk, sugar and marshmallows in a very large pan to avoid
spilling over. When marshmallows are melted, add coconut and almonds.
Spread this on top of the bottom cake layer. Top with other cake
layer and frost with chocolate frosting.
