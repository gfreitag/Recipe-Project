Title: ABM FOOLPROOF RICE BREAD FOR THE R2 D2
Categories: Breads
Yield: 16 Servings
1/2 tb Yeast
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
1 tb Sugar
1/2 tb Salt
1 tb Oil
1 c  Rice; cooked
1 c  Water
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order. Set "baking control" at 10 o'clock. Select "white bread" and
push Start. For a richer bread, use 1 egg and 3/4 c water instead of
1 c water. In hot, humid weather, use 1/8 c less water. WARNING: the
dough will look a little "wet." DO NOT ADD EXTRA FLOUR.
