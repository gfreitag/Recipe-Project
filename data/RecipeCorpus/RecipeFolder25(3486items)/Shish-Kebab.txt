Title: Shish Kebab
Categories: Main dish, Middle east, Greek, Bbq/grill, Meat
Yield: 1 servings
1    Garlic;clove
1/4 c  Red Wine
1/2 ts Soy Sauce
1/2 ts Basil
1 tb Lemon Juice
2 lb Beef;cubed
1 lb Mushrooms
1 c  Tomato;wedges
1 c  Onion;quartered
2    Green Pepper;quartered;
1/2 c  Salad Oil
Marinate beef in oil, red wine, soy sauce, garlic, lemon juice,
basil, salt and pepper to taste - from 3 to 4 hours.
Place on skewer, alternating mushroom caps, quartered tomatoes,
peppers and onions.
Roast, basting with marinade.
