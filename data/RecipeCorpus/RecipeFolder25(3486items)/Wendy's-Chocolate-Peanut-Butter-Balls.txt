Title: Wendy's Chocolate Peanut Butter Balls
Categories: Dessert, Nuts/grains, Chocolate
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Powdered sugar
2 c  Chocolate chips
1/2    Stick parafin wax
3 c  Rice krispies cereal
2 c  Crunchy peanut butter
1/2 c  Margerine, melted
Mix all but chips and wax, make into balls, set on waxed paper.
Freeze 1/2 hour.

Melt chips and wax in double boiler and dip cold balls into chocolate.
