Title: Malasadas (Sweet Doughnuts)
Categories: European, Dessert, Doughnut
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Lukewarm water
1/4 c  Butter; melted
2 3/4 c  Flour
1 qt Salad oil for frying
Sugar for rolling
1/2 c  Milk
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
3    Eggs
1    Cake compressed or dry yeast
eat milk to lukewarm. MOisten yeast in lukewarm water. Add the
yeast, salt, sugar, and melted butter to the milk. Stir in part of
the flour, beating well with a wooden spoon to prevent lumps. Add
beaten eggs and remaining flour to form a soft dough. Cover the dough
and set in a warm place until it doubles in bult. Heat oil to 350
degrees. Drop bough by tablespoonful into the hot oil and fry until
evenly browned. Drain on absorbent paper and roll in sugar.  Makes 24
