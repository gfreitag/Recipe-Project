Title: Teriyaki Seafood Kabobs
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Crab/shrimp, Clam/oyster, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
-1 1/2" chunks
16    Cherry tomatoes with stems
1/2 c  S&W Teriyaki Cooking Sauce
-and Marinade
8    Skewers
1 lb Raw medium-large prawns
1 lb Raw large scallops
1 sm Pineapple trimmed, cored
-and cut into 1 1/2" chunks
2    Bell peppers, cut into
Place seafood in boiling water for approximately 1 minute; drain.
Shell prawns, leaving tails in place. Alternate seafood with
pineapple, bell pepper and tomato on 8 skewers. Thoroughly coat each
skewer with marinade and let stand 20-30 minutes. Grill or broil 3-4
minutes on each side. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   10-31-94
