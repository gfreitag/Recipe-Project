Title: Knot Tying Club Noodles
Categories: Pasta, Cheese/eggs, Casserole
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Onion, chopped
1 T  Worcestershire sauce
ds Tabasco sauce
Salt to taste
Knot bad at all...
5 oz Package fine noodles
1 c  Cottage cheese
1 c  Sour cream
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
Butter a casserole dish.  In a larg pot cook the noodles in boiling
salted water for 10 minutes.  Drain.  In the prepared dish combine
the remaining ingredients, and add the noodles.  Toss until coated.
Bake in a 350F oven for 45 minutes.  Serves 6. Origin: Aunt Bee's
Mayberry Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
