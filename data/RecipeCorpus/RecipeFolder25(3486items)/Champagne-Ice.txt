Title: Champagne Ice
Categories: Dessert, Ice cream, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 2 1/2 quarts
1/3 c  Lemon juice; fresh
2    Bottles champagne;(75 ml ea)
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3 c  Water
1 1/2 tb Lemon rind; grated
Combine all ingredients, stirring well; pour into freezer container
of a 1-gallon hand-turned or electric freezer.  Freeze according to
manufacturer's instructions.

Pack freezer with additional ice and rock salt, and let stand 1 hour
before serving, if desired.
