Title: Orange Freeze
Categories: Dessert, Diabetic, Low-cal/fat, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Lo cal orange drink mix
3 c  Crushed ice
1/2 c  Granulated sugar replacement
Combine all ingresients in a blender and whip at high speed. Eat as
is or freeze for later.
