Title: Baked Apples Farcis
Categories: Fruit, Side dish, Sausage, Meat, Apples
Yield: 1 servings
8    Apple;green;med.
1/4 c  Brown Sugar
8    Bacon;slices
1 lb Sausage;ground
1    Garlic;crushed;clove
2 tb Onion;chopped
In skillet saute sausage, garlic and onion. Core apples and stuff
with this mixture. Place in a baking dish. Sprinkle with brown sugar
and top with bacon.
Bake 350 degrees about 40 minutes or until apples are soft.
