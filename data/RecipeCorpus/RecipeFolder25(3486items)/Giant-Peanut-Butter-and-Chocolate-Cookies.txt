Title: Giant Peanut Butter and Chocolate Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 18 cookies
1 t  Baking soda
1 t  Baking powder
1/4 t  Salt
1 c  Large chocolate chips
-or chocolate chunks
1/2 c  Peanut butter chips
1 c  Butter
1 c  Light-brown sugar, packed
1 c  Granulated sugar
2    Eggs
1 c  Chunky peanut butter
3 c  Flour
In bowl beat butter until light and creamy. Beat in brown and
granulated sugars. Beat in eggs, 1 at a time, until blended. Beat in
peanut butter. Stir together flour, baking soda, baking powder and
salt. Stir into peanut butter mixture. Stir in chocolate and peanut
butter chips.

Spoon about 1/4 cup dough for each cookie onto lightly greased baking
sheet about 2" apart. Bake at 350'F. 14-16 minutes or until golden
brown. Remove from oven. Let stand on baking sheet few minutes, then
remove to wire rack to cool.

Each cookie contains about: 415 calories; 187 milligrams sodium; 53
milligrams cholesterol; 22 grams fat; 50 grams carbohydrates; 7 grams
protein; 0.41 gram fiber.
