Title: Peach and Cherry Jam
Categories: Fruit, Can/preserv, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------MAKES--------------------------------
12 oz Bottle maraschino cherries,
-cut in pieces, juice
-reserved
1 lg Orange, unpelled
6 lb Peaches
6 lb Sugar
Put peaches and orange thru meat grinder after the peaches have been
peeled and stoned. Boil peach pulp with sugar for 3/4 of an hour.
Then add the cherries and boil 15 minutes longer. Pour into jars,
cover and process. Source: ??? In the "big pile" Carolyn Shaw's
Collection
