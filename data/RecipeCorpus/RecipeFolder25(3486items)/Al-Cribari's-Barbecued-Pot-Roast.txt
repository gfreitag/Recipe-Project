Title: Al Cribari's Barbecued Pot Roast
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Bbq/grill, Beef
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Curry powder (good brand)
4 tb Olive oil
1 ea 4 1/2-5lb chuck roast
Freshly ground black pepper
-to taste
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
Several drops of liquid
-smoke
Dash of tabasco sauce
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 md Yellow onion; chopped
1 c  Dry red Burgundy wine
1 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
Rub the roast with the freshly ground pepper and the curry powder.
Heat a large frying pan and add the oil. Brown the meat on all sides,
using high heat. Prepare the sauce by simmering all the sauce
ingredients until the onions are soft. Place the roast in a large
piece of aluminum foil and place on a baking sheet. Pour the sauce
over the roast and fold the foil over to seal the roast well.  Place
on a grill or in a 350 oven for 2 hours, or until tender. You may
wish to add a bit of salt when serving. Wine suggestion: Fruity
Cabernet. from: The Frugal Gourmet Cook's with Wine. Contributed by
Janie B. Submitted By JANIE BOURKE   On   10-19-94
