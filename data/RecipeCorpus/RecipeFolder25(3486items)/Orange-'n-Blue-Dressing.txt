Title: Orange 'n Blue Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Cheese, blue; crumbled
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Orange juice
1 ts Orange rind; grated
1 c  Sour cream
Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly.  Cover; chill at least 1
hour before serving to allow flavors to blend.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, March 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
