Title: Pecan Delight
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Finely chopped pecans
1 t  Vanilla extract
1 x  Whipped cream
3 ea Egg whites
20 ea Round buttery crackers; roll
1 c  Sugar; fine
1/4 t  Baking powder
Grease 9" glass piepan.  Beat egg whites til stiff; fold in sugar,
baking powder, and vanilla extract.
Beat until stiff.  Fold in cracker crumbs & chopped pecans. Pour mix
into piepan. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 mins. Top with==>> whipped
cream. Yield: 6-8 servings.
