Title: Turkey Italian Sausage with Red Sauce
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Turkey, Rice
Yield: 4 servings
-wine
1 cn Tomato sauce (15 oz)
1 cn Stewed tomatoes, drained
-(14 1/2 oz)
2 tb Tomato paste
1/2 t  Italian seasoning
1 c  Cooked Mahatma Rice
2 tb Olive oil, divided
1 pk Butterball Turkey Italian
-Sausage (1 lb)
1 lg Onion, chopped
1/2 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1/2    Green bell pepper, chopped
1    Garlic clove, minced
1/4 c  Chicken broth or dry white
Heat 1 tablespoon of the oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add
turkey sausage and brown 8-10 minutes. Remove from skillet; set
aside. Add the remaining 1 tablespoon oil and onion to skillet; cook
over medium-low heat 5 minutes. Then add mushrooms, pepper and
garlic; cook 5 minutes more. Pour in broth and cook over medium-high
heat about 2 minutes. Stir in tomato sauce, stewed tomatoes, tomato
paste and Italian seasoning. Bring to a boil. Return sausage to
skillet. Cover and simmer over low heat 15 minutes. Serve with hot
Mahatma Rice. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   10-31-94
