Title: Sherried Seafood Bisque
Categories: Soup/stew, Seafood, Clam/oyster, Potato, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 8 servings
10 1/2 oz Can minced clams, drained
2 c  Flaked cooked fish
1/4 lb Shrimp, cleaned
2 tb Dry sherry
2    Egg yolks
1/2 c  Heavy whipping cream
5 c  Water
5 md Potatoes, peeled and diced
1/2 c  Onion, coarsley chopped
4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Salt
Boil water. Add potatoes, onion, 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce, garlic,
thyme, and salt. reduce heat and simmer 15-20 minutes, or until
potatoes are almost soft. Add clams, fish, shrimp, sherry, and
remaining Worcestershire sauce. Simmer 5 minutes or until seafood is
hot. Remove pan from heat. Combine egg yolks and cream; stir into
fish mixture. Return pan to heat and cook until well heated; do not
boil. Serve hot or cold.
