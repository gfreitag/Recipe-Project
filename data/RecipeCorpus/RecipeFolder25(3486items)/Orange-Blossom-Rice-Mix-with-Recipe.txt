Title: Orange Blossom Rice Mix with Recipe
Categories: Herbs/spice, Mixes, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
-RICE:
2 1/2 c  Water
2 tb Butter
1 pk Rice mix
1 c  Long grained white rice
1/2 ts Dried orange peel
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Dried marjoram
1/4 ts Dried thyme
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Orange Blossom Rice: Bring water and butter to a boil. Add rice,
reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 20 minutes. 6 servings.
