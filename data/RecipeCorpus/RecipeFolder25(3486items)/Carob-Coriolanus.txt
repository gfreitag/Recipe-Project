Title: Carob Coriolanus
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Carob powder
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
2 tb Chopped walnuts
1 c  Black mission figs
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Dates, pitted
1/8 c  Water
Soak the black figs for 2 hours in the 1/2 c water. Soak the dates in
1/8 c water for 2 hours.

Blend all the ingredients, except the wamnuts, in a blender at medium
speed until thick and creamy. Pour into small dessert cups and
sprinkle with the walnuts. Refrigerate until you are ready to serve
it. Can be kept tightly covered in the refrigerator for 5 to 7 days.

Frances Kendall, "Sweet Temptations"
