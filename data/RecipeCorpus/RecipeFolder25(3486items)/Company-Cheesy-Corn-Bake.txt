Title: Company Cheesy Corn Bake
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
-small pieces
2 T  Onion flakes
1 ea Package (30-23 oz) frozen
-corn kernals, thawed and
-drained
2 T  Diced red pepper
2 T  Diced green pepper
3/4 c  Lean cooked ham, trimmed
-and diced
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
3 T  Margarine or butter
3 T  All-purpose flour
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1/8 ea To 1/4 teaspoon cayenne
-pepper (or to taste)
3/4 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Coarsely grated American
-cheese (About 6 ounces)
3 ea Ounces cream cheese,
-softened and cut in to
In a 3-quart saucepan over low heat, melt margarine or butter, Whisk
in flour, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper.  Blend well and slowly
add milk, shisking constantly until smooth. Increase heat to medium
and cook 3 to 4 minutes, stirring constantly, until the mixture is
tickened an bubbling. Stir in American cheese, cream cheese and onion
glakes, Then reduce heat to low. Cook and stir until cheese is melted
and mixture is smooth. Remove from heat and stir in corn, red and
green peppers, and ham. Pour mixture into a 2-quart casserole disk.
Bake 20 minutes covered or until heated through.  Uncover and bake 15
minutes more.  Remove from oven and let stand 5 minutes.  Using a
paper towl blot off any excess oil and serve. Serves 10 to 12 From
Renee Reeves, Milford, Mi. Via COMPANY SHOW on WXYZtv and printed in
the 11/16/94 edition of the Detroit Free Press. Nutrition Details per
serving (margarine, 12 serving) Calories: 209 Percent of calories
from fat:   52% Fat: 12 gm Protein: 10 gm Carbohydrate: 18 gm
Sodium: 447 mg Diabetic exchanges: 1
Cholesterol: 32 mg
