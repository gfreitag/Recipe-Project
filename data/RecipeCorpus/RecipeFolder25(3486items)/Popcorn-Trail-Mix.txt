Title: Popcorn Trail Mix
Categories: Fruit, Snack, Popcorn
Yield: 2 quarts
-- apricots, apples, etc.
2 qt Popped popcorn
8 oz Raisins
6 oz Diced dried fruit
Combine raisins, diced fruit and popcorn.  Toss until mixed.

Nutritional info per 3 cup serving: 651 calories, 5 g fat (3
saturated), 162 g carbohydrates, 15 g fiber, 7.9 g protein, 420 mg
sodium, no cholesterol.

From Food Columnist's Mary Alice Holt's 10/06/94 "October Perfect
Month for Popcorn Provisions" article in "The (Elizabethtown, KY)
News-Enterprise." Pg. D1.  Posted by Cathy Harned.
