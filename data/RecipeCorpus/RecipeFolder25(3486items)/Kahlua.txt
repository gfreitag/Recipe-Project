Title: Kahlua
Categories: Beverage, Alcohol
Yield: 1 recipe
1 qt 100 proof vodka
1/4 c  Pure vanilla extract
4 c  Water
3 3/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Instant coffee
Bring water and sugar to a rolling boil. Remove from heat. Add instant
coffee. Stir well to dissolve completely. Let cool till lukewarm. Add
vodka and vanilla. Stir well and bottle in brown bottles. Let stand
for 2 weeks. Makes about 3 1/2 fifths.
