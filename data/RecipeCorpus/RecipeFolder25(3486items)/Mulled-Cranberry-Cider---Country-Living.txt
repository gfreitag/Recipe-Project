Title: Mulled Cranberry Cider - Country Living
Categories: Apples, Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
8    Whole cloves
1/4 t  Ground nutmeg (opt.)
8    8-inch cinnamon sticks
-(opt.)
Apple slices (opt.)
1 qt Apple cider
1 qt Cranberry-juice cocktail
1 T  Firmly packed light-
-brown sugar
2    3-inch cinnamon sticks
1. In 4-quart saucepan, heat cider, cranberry juice cocktail, sugar,
3-inch cinnamon sticks, the cloves, and nutmeg, if desired, to
boiling over high heat. Reduce heat to medium; cover and simmer 10
minutes.

2. Ladle hot cider into mugs or cool to room temperature and serve
over ice. Garnish each serving with a cinnamon stick and slice of
apple, if desired,
