Title: Venison Sausage Balls
Categories: Appetizer, Game, Sthrn/livng, Sausage
Yield: 5 dozen
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Allspice, ground
1/4 ts Nutmeg, ground
1/8 ts Cloves, ground
1/8 ts Ginger, ground
1/4 c  Butter (or marg.); melted
1 1/2 lb Venison; ground
1/2 c  Breadcrumbs, dry
1    Egg; beaten
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Potatoes; mashed
1/2 t  Sugar, brown
Orange slices; opt.   Lemon slices; opt.   Parsley sprigs; opt.

Combine first 11 ingredients, mixing well.  Shape into 1" balls.
Brown well in butter, stirring occasionally.  Cover and cook over low
heat 15 minutes. Arrange on serving platter; garnish with fruit and
parsley, if desired.
