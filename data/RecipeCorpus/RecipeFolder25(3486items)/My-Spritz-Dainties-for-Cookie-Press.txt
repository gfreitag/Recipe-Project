Title: My Spritz Dainties for Cookie Press
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 60 servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM---------------------------
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Baking soda
1 c  Soft butter
3/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
1    Egg yolk
1/2 ts Vanilla
Preheat oven to 375

Combine butter and brown sugar and beat til creamy. Add egg yolk and
vanilla and beat well.  Stir together sifted flour, salt and baking
soda. Blend into creamed mix. Press dough through desired disk onto
ungreased cookie sheet.  Brush tops with egg white, slightly beaten.
Decorate. Bake 8 to 10 minutes.
