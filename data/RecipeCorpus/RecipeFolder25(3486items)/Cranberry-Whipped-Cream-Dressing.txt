Title: Cranberry Whipped Cream Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Salsa/sauce, Sthrn/livng, Fruit
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
1/3 c  Cranberry sauce; strained
1/2 c  Cream, heavy; whipped
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Combine whipped cream and mayonnaise.  Fold in cranberry sauce.
Serve over turkey salad.
