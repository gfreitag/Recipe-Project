Title: Autumn Pear Salsa
Categories: Fruit, Side dish, Condiment
Yield: 3 cups
1/2 ts Garlic; finely chopped
1/2 ts Jalapeno; finely chopped
1/2 ts Grated lime zest
2 tb Fresh lime juice
1 lg Grapefruit; preferably pink
2 md Pears; ripe
1/2 c  Cherries or cranberries;
-dried
2 tb Red onion; finely chopped
1. Peel the grapefruit, removing all white pith. Carefully section the
fruit then cut each section crosswise into thirds. Place in a bowl
with any accumulated juices.

2. Peel and core the pears, then cut into 1/4" dice. Add at once to
the grapefruit to prevent discoloration.

3. Toss with the remaining ingredients.

Per Tablespoon: 11 calories, .1g fat, no cholesterol.
