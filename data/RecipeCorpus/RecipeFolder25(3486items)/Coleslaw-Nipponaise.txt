Title: Coleslaw Nipponaise
Categories: Side dish, Salad, Vegetable, Asian
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Rice Wine Vinegar
3 tb Soy Sauce
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sunflower Seed;hulled
1    Cabbage;head
1 c  Carrot;grated
2    Green Onion;minced;
2 tb Parsley;chopped
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
Chill and serve.
