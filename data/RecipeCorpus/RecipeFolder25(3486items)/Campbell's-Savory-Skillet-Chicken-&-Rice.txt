Title: Campbell's Savory Skillet Chicken & Rice
Categories: Poultry, Chicken, Main dish, Rice, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Onion flakes
1/4 t  Dried thyme leaves, crushed
1/8 t  Pepper
2 c  Frozen cut green beans
2 c  Uncooked instant rice
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 lb Skinless, boneless chicken
-breasts, cut up
1 cn Campbell's condensed cream
-of mushroom soup (10.75 oz)
1 c  Milk
Prep Time: 5 minutes. Cook Time: 15 minutes. Stand Time: 5 minutes.

1. In skillet over medium-high heat, heat margarine. Add chicken and
cook until browned, stirring often. Set chicken aside.

2. Add soup, milk, onion, thyme, pepper and beans. Heat to a boil.
Return chicken to pan. Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or
until chicken is done.

3. Add rice. Cover and remove from heat. Let stand 5 minutes. Fluff
with fork.
