Title: Church Windows
Categories: Dessert, Candy, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 3 dozen
-multicolored
1 c  Pecans; finely chopped
Coconut, flaked
12 oz Chocolate chips
1/2 c  Butter (or marg.)
10 oz Marshmallows, miniature
Melt chocolate and butter over low heat; cool.  Add marshmallows and
nuts to chocolate mixture.  Shape into 2 rolls 1-1/2 to 2 inches in
diameter; roll each in coconut.  Refrigerate.

When rolls are thoroughly chilled, slice into 1/2" slices.
