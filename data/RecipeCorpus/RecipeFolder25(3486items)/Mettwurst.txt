Title: Mettwurst
Categories: Beef, Sausage, Pork, Homemade
Yield: 5 pounds
1/2 ts Ground allspice
1/4 ts Fresh ground gingerroot
1/4 ts Ground marjoram
1/4 ts Ascorbic acid
1/2 ts Saltpeter
4    Feet medium hog casings
3 lb Lean beef chuck, cubed
2 lb Pork butt sith fat, cubed
5 ts Salt
1 ts Finely ground white pepper
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1/2 ts Ground celery seed
Grind meats separately and mix together. Mix with remaining
ingredients. Prepare casings, stuff and twist into 6" links. cure in
the refrigerator 24 hours. Smoke at 110-120 or two hours, raise
temperature to 150 and smoke for two more hours. Simmer in a kettle
of 180-190 water for thirty minutes or until they float. Place in a
kettle of cool water for 30 minutes, remove, dry thoroughly and
refrigerate up to two weeks.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: -88266-477-8
