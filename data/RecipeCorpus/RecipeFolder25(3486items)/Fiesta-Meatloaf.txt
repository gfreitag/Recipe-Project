Title: Fiesta Meatloaf
Categories: Main dish, Meatloaf, Ground beef, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Soft bread crumbs (add more
-bread crumbs, to taste)
1    Egg, slightly beaten
2 tb Heinz Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 cn Heinz Chili Sauce (12 oz)
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
Saute onion, celery and green pepper in oil until vegetables are
tender; stir in chili sauce. Combine 1/2 cup vegetable sauce mixture
with ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, Worcestershire Sauce, salt and
pepper. Shape into a loaf (8x4x1-1/2") in shallow baking pan. Bake in
350'F. oven, 1 hour. Let stand 5 minutes before slicing. Serve
remaining sauce, cold or heated, over meat loaf.
