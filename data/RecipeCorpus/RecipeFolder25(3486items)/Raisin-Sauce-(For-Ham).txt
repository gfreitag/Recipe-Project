Title: Raisin Sauce (For Ham)
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce, Sthrn/livng, Pork
Yield: 2 cups(aprx)
2 tb Sugar, brown; firmly packed
1 tb Cornstarch
ds Salt
ds Allspice, ground
1/3 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Wate
1/3 c  Currant jelly
1/2 ts Orange peel; grated
1/2 c  Orange juice
Combine raisins, water, jelly, orange peel, and orange juice in
saucepan; bring to a boil.  Combine remaining ingredients; stir into
orange mixture. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until
thick and clear. Serve warm over ham.
