Title: Calzone Filling
Categories: Sausage, Herbs/spice, Italian, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Parsley;chopped
Pepper;
2    Egg;large
1/2 lb Mushrooms;sliced
1/2 c  Bell Pepper;chopped
1/2 c  Onion;sliced
1    CALZONE DOUGH;recipe
1/2 lb Feta Cheese;chunks
1/4 lb Mozzarella Cheese;cubes
1/4 c  Ricotta Cheese
1/2 c  Pepperoni;sliced
1/4 c  Parmesan Cheese;grated
1/4 ts Rosemary;chopped
2 ts Basil;chopped
1/2 ts Oregano;ground
Prepare CALZONE DOUGH per recipe.
Set aside 2 baking sheets.  Mix cheeses, meats, vegetables, 1 egg
and herbs.  Set oven to 400F.
Divide dough in half.  Roll each into 6' circles.
Spoon filling over 1/2 of each circle, leave edges free for sealing.
Fold dough over filling and press edges.  Place on sheets, slit
tops and brush with egg beaten with 1 Tbl. water.
Bake 30 minutes, or until golden brown.
