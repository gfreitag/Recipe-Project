Title: Peanut Patties
Categories: Candy, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Butter
1 t  Vanilla
Few drops red food coloring
2 1/2 c  Sugar
2/3 c  White corn syrup
1 c  Cream
3 1/2 c  Raw peanuts or pecans
Combine sugar, syrup, cream and peanuts in a heavy saucepan. Cook
over low heat, stirring constantly, until a few drops of mixture
forms a soft ball when dropped in cool water. Remove from heat. Add
butter, vanilla and coloring. Beat until mixture is thick and creamy.
Drop by spoonfuls onto waxed paper.

*Note: milk can be used instead of cream.
