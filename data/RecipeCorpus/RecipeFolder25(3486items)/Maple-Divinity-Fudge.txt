Title: Maple Divinity Fudge
Categories: Candy, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Vanilla
1/2 c  Maple syrup
1/4 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg white
1/2 c  Water
Mix sugar, syrup and water together and boil to the hard boil stage.
Remove, add vanilla and pour hot syrup over stiffly beaten egg white.
Add nuts.  Beat until stiff enough to hold shape and drop from
teaspoon on wax paper. From: The Vermont CookBook Shared By: Pat
