Title: Ginger Glaze for Ham
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Pork, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 1 cup
1 1/2 ts Ginger, ground
1 1/2 ts Orange peel; grated
1/4 c  Dressing, Russian
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Sugar, brown; firmly packed
Combine all ingredients in small saucepan; cook over low heat until
smooth. spoon over ham during last 30 minutes of baking time.
