Title: Duck Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Game, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 4 SERVINGS--------------------------
2 ea Stalks celery, with leaves,
-chopped
6 ea Chicken bouillon cubes
1 lg Or 2 small ducks
1 lg Onion, diced
8 c  Water
1 t  Salt
Place all in a large pot, simmer gently for 2 or 3 hours. If too
thick, add one cup water, add 2 or 3 oz tin of thin noodles, cook
very slowly half hour longer and serve. Serve with fresh hot buns.
Origin: Anna Heppner
