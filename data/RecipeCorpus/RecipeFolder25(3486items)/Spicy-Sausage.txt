Title: Spicy Sausage
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  Prepared mustard
1/4 t  Ground ginger
1 c  Golden raisins
1 lb Polish sausage,2" pieces
1 lb Kielbasa,2" pieces
1 t  Instant beef bouillon
1 c  Dry white wine
Heat oven to 400'. Place sausage in ungreased baking dish, 10x6x2
inches. Dissolve bouillon in boiling water. Stir remaining
ingredients into bouillon; pour over sausage. Bake uncovered until
done, 25 to 30 minutes. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On
11-21-94
