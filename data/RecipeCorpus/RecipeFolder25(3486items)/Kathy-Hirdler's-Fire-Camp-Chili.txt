Title: Kathy Hirdler's Fire Camp Chili
Categories: Tex/mexican, Meat, Ground beef, Chili, Main dish
Yield: 120 servings
48 x  Onions, large, chopped
40 lb Meat (ham, sausage, pork,
4 c  Chili powder
100 lb Pinto beans
4 c  Jalapeno chiles with juice
Bacon, ground beef, etc)
Salt to taste
Soak Beans overnight, then raise to a bOil on high heat. Add all
ingredients and simmer until tender (about 6 hours). Add Water as
necessary.  Stir occasionally.  Makes 60 gallons. Winner, 1987 World
Championship
