Title: Superb Baked Meatloaf
Categories: Main dish, Meatloaf, Ground beef, Meat
Yield: 3 servings
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Warm water
2    Strips of bacon
2 lb Ground beef
1 pk Onion soup mix
3 sl Bread, diced
3/4 c  Catsup
Mix together all ingredients, except the bacon, in a large bowl. After
mixed well, place in a 9x13 inch baking dish and shape in a loaf
form. Put the bacon strips on top and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
Remove bacon and lightly cover the top of the meatloaf with catsup
and bake another 15 minutes.  Promptly remove from baking dish, let
stand 10 minutes before serving. Randy Rigg
