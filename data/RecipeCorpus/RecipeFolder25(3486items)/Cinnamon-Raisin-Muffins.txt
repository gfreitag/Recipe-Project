Title: Cinnamon-Raisin Muffins
Categories: Muffin
Yield: 12 muffins
1    Egg, lightly beaten
2 T  FLEISCHMANN'S Margarine,
-melted
11 t  Vanilla extract
1 c  California Raisins
2 T  Fruit juice, any flavor
11 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Regular, Quick or Instant
-CREAM OF WHEAT Cereal
1/2 c  Sugar
1 T  Baking powder
2 t  Ground cinnamon
1 c  Milk
In medium bowl, combine flour, cereal, 1/4 cup sugar, baking powder
and cinnamon; set aside.

In small bowl, combine milk, egg, margarine and vanilla; stir into dry
ingredients just until moistened. Stir in raisins. Spoon mixture
evenly into 12 greased 2 1/2" muffin-pan cups. Bake at 400'F. 18-20
minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Remove
from pan; brush tops of muffins with fruit juice and roll in
remaining sugar. Serve warm. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On
10-23-94
