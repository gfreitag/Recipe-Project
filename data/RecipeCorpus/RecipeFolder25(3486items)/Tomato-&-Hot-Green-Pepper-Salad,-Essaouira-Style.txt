Title: Tomato & Hot Green Pepper Salad, Essaouira Style
Categories: Salad, Moroccan, Appetizer, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
Salt & pepper
1 tb Lemon jiuice
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Parsley, chopped
1/4 ts Cumin
2 ea Garlic cloves, chopped
4 lg Tomatoes, peeled, seeded &
-- cubed
4 ea Green bell peppers, grilled,
-- seeded & diced
1 ea Cucumber, peeled, centre
-- core removed & diced
1 tb Chili peppers, seeded & dice
Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl & blend gently.
Refrigerate one hour before serving.

Paula Wolfert, "Couscous & Other Good Food From Morocco"
