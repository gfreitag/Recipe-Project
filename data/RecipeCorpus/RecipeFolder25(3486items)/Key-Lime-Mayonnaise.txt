Title: Key Lime Mayonnaise
Categories: Condiment, Salad, Dressing
Yield: 1 cup
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: THE NATIONAL CULINAR---------------------
1 c  Canola or olive oil
1 1/2 T  Key lime juice
1/2 T  Very hot water
1 ea Egg white (or 1/4 cup egg
-substitute)
1/2 t  Dry mustard
1/2 t  Salt
Method: In blender, combine egg white, mustard, sugar and salt; blend
2 seconds.  With motor running, gradually add 1/2 cup oil, then 1
tablespoon juice, then remaining 1/2 cup oil, then remaining 1/2
tablespoon juice, then hot water.  Stop motor and scrape blender as
needed. Blend till consistency of mayonnaise.  Refrigerate covered
till needed.
