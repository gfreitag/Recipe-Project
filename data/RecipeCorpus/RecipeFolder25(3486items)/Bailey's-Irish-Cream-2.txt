Title: Bailey's Irish Cream 2
Categories: Beverage, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Of whiskey (more if desired)
1/2 t  Coffee powder
3 ea Eggs
1 t  Coconut essence
5 1/2 T  Choc. ice-cream topping
Blend all ingredients in a blender for no longer than 2mins. Pour
over ice and enjoy straight away.
