Title: Honey Mustard Sauce for Pasta
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cheese/eggs, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  Grated Romano cheese
2 pn Dried basil
1 t  Dry mustard
3/4 c  Olive oil
1 T  Red wine
1 T  Prepared mustard
2 T  Honey
Put all ingredients in a jar and shake well. Toss with hot pasta or
drizzle over fresh salad greens. Serves 4. SOURCE:*Yankee Magazine,
April 1994 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 12/94
