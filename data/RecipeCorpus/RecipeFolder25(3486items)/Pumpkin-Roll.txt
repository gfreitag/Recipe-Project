Title: Pumpkin Roll
Categories: Dessert, Pumpkin
Yield: 10 servings
2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Walnuts
FILLING
1 c  Powdered sugar
1/4 c  Margarine
1/2 ts Vanilla
6 oz Cream cheese
3    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Pumpkin
1 ts Baking powder
3/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Pumpkin spice
Beat eggs at high speed for 5 minutes. Slowly add sugar and stir in
pumpkin. Add dry ingredients. Grease and flour a 15x10in pan. Spread
mixture and top with nuts, bake for 15 min at 375 degrees. Cool
slightly and turn on towel sprinkled with powdered sugar. Roll up and
let cool. When cool unroll and spread filing mixture. Roll back up
and refridgerate, seam side down before slicing.
