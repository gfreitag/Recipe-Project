Title: Cocktail Water Chestnuts
Categories: Appetizer, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
12 sl Bacon, halved
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Catsup
8 1/2 oz Can of water chestnuts
Save 1/2 cup of liquid
1/2 c  Vinegar
Marinate chestnuts in liquid and vinegar for 1 hour.  Drain.  Mix
brown sugar and catsup; then spread on bacon.  Roll chestnuts in
bacon. Fasten with toothpicks.  Broil unti bacon is crisp. Randy Rigg
