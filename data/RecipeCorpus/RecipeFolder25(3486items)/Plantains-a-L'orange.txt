Title: Plantains a L'orange
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Caribbean
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/3 c  Brown sugar
Plantains
3 tb Butter
Bake plantains in the skin at 350'F. until tender. Peel, then cut
into 1/2" chunks. Then, saute cut plantains over medium heat with
butter and orange juice. Sprinkle with brown sugar. Cook until butter
is melted and plantains are softened. Makes 4 serving for dessert.
