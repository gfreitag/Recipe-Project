Title: Roast Potatoes Poupon
Categories: Vegetable, Potato, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
-Dijon Mustard
1/3 c  Margarine, melted
2 tb Chopped parsley
12 sm Red skinned potatoes,
-quartered
2    Garlic cloves, peeled
1/3 c  GREY POUPON Dijon or Country
In large heavy pot with tight-fitting lid, place potatoes and garlic
in a single layer. Cook, covered, over low heat 45-60 minutes or
until done, stirring occasionally (low heat required for stove-top
roasting).

Mix mustard, margarine and parsley; toss with hot potatoes. Serve
immediately.
