Title: Refries & Cheese Bread
Categories: Tex/mexican, Breadmaker, Bread, Beans, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 loaf
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1 c  Refried beans
4 oz Cheeze Whiz, "mild Mexican"
1 1/4 c  Water; very warm
1 pk Yeast
1/4 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Sugar
3 c  Flour, bread
1/3 c  Flour, wheat
2 tb Gluten
Add ingredients to pan in order listed.  Select white bread
setting and press "Start".

Source: DAK Gourmet Gazette, Vol 1, Issue 2.
