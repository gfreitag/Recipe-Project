Title: Chocolate Peanut Butter Balls
Categories: Candy, Dessert, Chocolate
Yield: 60 servings
12 oz Choc. chips
2 tb Crisco
1 lb Powdered sugar
1 lb Peanut butter; smooth
1 c  Margarine
Cream margarine and sugar. Add peanut butter. Mix well. Roll into 1
inch balls. Chill 1 hour. Melt choc. & Crisco in top of double
boiler. With a fork roll balls (one at a time) in choc. mixture. Let
drip a few seconds and then put on waxed paper to dry. When
completely dry put in airtight container.

Note:  Roll the balls when you have plenty of time or while watching
TV. They are easy to do, but do take some time. Judy Garnett/NC

These are very creamy, and good. If you prefer, you may use crunchy
peanut butter.
