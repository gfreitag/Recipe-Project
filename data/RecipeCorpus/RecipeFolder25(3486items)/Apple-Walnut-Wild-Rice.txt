Title: Apple Walnut Wild Rice
Categories: Side dish, Apples, Nuts/grains, Rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Apple juice
1 c  Water
1/4 t  Ground cinnamon
1 md Red apple, cut into 1/2"
-pieces, unpeeled
1/4 c  Chopped walnuts
1 tb Margarine or butter
1 md Onion, chopped
1 pk NEAR EAST Long Grain & Wild
-Rice Pilaf Mix (6 oz)
1. In medium saucepan, add nuts and heat oven medium heat. Cook 5-6
minutes or until nuts are light golden brown and fragrant, stirring
frequently. Remove from pan; set aside

2. In same saucepan, melt margarine over medium heat. Add onion; cook
3 minutes, stirring frequently. Add rice pilaf mix; cook 30 seconds,
stirring occasionally.

3. Add juice, water, contents of Spice Sack and cinnamon; bring to a
boil.

4. Cover, reduce heat to low. Simmer 30 minutes or until most of
liquid is absorbed.

5. Stir in apple and walnuts just before serving.
