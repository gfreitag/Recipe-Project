Title: Cranberry Mousse
Categories: Dessert, Holiday, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
16 oz Cranberry sauce, jellied
2 c  Cool whip; thawed
1 c  Cranberry Juice Cocktail
1 pk Jello, raspberry; (3 oz)
Heat cranberry juice to boiling.  Remove from heat and stir in jello
until dissolved.

Beat cranberry sauce in a small bowl for one minute at high speed.
Stir into jello mixture.  Chill until jello begins to thicken (not
set). Gently fold in Cool Whip.  Spoon into individual serving dishes
or into a pie shell.  Chill until firm.
