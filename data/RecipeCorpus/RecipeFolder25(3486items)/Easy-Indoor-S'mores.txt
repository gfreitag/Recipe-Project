Title: Easy Indoor S'mores
Categories: Dessert, Snack, Chocolate
Yield: 24 squares
1 1/2 c  Milk chocolate chips
1 t  Vanilla
8 c  Golden Grahams cereal
6 c  Minature marshmallows
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
6 tb Margarine or butter
1. Melt 5 cups miniature marshmallows, the corn syrup, margarine and
chocolate chips in 3-quart saucepan over low heat, stirring
constantly; remove from heat. Stir in vanilla. Pour over cereal in
large bowl.

2. Mix melted ingredients with cereal QUICKLY until completely coated
with chocolate. Stir in 1 cup marshmallows.

3. Make squares by pressing mixture evenly in buttered 13x9x2"
rectangular pan with buttered back of spoon. Let stand at least 1
hour, or refrigerate if a firmer bar is desired. Cut into 2" squares.

MICROWAVE DIRECTIONS: Place 5 cups miniature marshmallows, the corn
syrup, margarine and chocolate chips in large microwavable bowl.
Microwave uncovered on High 2 to 3 1/2 minutes, stirring after each
minute, until mixture is melted and smooth when stirred. (Be careful
when removing mixture from microwave; it will be hot!) Stir in
vanilla. Pour over cereal in large bowl. Continue as directed in step
2 above. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   11-22-94
