Title: Sugar Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------FROM THE KITCHEN OF: GARY &---------------------
1 1/2 t  Salt
2 t  Baking Powder
1 ea Orange (grate the rind only)
1 T  Orange juice
2/3 c  Shortening
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs
3 c  Flour
Mix well; roll out and cut. Bake about 20 minutes.
