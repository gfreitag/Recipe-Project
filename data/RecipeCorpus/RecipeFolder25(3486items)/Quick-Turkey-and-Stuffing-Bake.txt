Title: Quick Turkey and Stuffing Bake
Categories: Turkey, Poultry, Main dish, Stuffing, Vegetable
Yield: 6 - 8 folks
-stuffing
3/4 c  Bisquick Original baking mix
1/2 ts Salt; if desired
1 1/2 c  Milk; low fat
3    Eggs;
1 c  Turkey; cooked cubed
1/2 c  Frozen green peas;
1/4 c  Green onion; sliced w/ tops
1/4 c  Dried cranberries;
1 c  Prepared & baked turkey
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Grease 9" pie pan.  Mix turkey, peas,
onion and cranberries in pie pan.  Separate stuffing into small
pieces and place on turkey mixture.  Stir remaining ingredients with
fork until blended. Pour into plate.   Bake 30 to 35 minutes, or
until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.  Serve with hot
turkey gravy, if desired. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune Food
Section, Nov. 25, 1994. Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion
and her Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On   11-25-94
