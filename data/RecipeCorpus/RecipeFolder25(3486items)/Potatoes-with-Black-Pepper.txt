Title: Potatoes with Black Pepper
Categories: Side dish, Indian, Vegetable, Potato
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
2 tb Cilantro, chopped
5 md Potatoes, boiled, cooled &
-- diced
4 tb Vegetable oil
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  When hot, put in
potatoes & stir-fry them for a minute or so.  Sprinkle in the salt.
Cover & let the potatoes heat through gently for 5 minutes, reducing
heat as necessary. Add pepper & mix gently.  Cook for a few more
minutes, stirring every now & then.  Sprinkle in the cilantro, mix &
serve.

Madhur Jaffrey's "Indian Cooking"
