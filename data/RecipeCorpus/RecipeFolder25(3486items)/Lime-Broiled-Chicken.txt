Title: Lime-Broiled Chicken
Categories: Fruit, Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: THE NATIONAL CULINAR---------------------
1/4 ea To 1/2 teaspoon Tabasco
-sauce
8 ea To 9 pounds skinless
-chicken breasts
Salt and pepper
1/2 c  Canola oil
1/2 c  Lime juice
2 T  Minced onion
1 t  Fresh tarragon (or 2
-teaspoons dried)
Method: Mix oil, juice, onion, tarragon and Tabasco.  Season chicken
with salt and pepper.  Arrange in 1 layer on broiler rack.  Brush
with sauce. Broil slowly about 6 inches from heat, turning and
brushing on more sauce often, till chicken is done.
