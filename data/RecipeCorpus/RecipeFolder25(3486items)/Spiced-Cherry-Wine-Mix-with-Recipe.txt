Title: Spiced Cherry Wine Mix with Recipe
Categories: Fruit, Mixes, Beverage, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
4    Cinnamon sticks
12    Whole cloves
1/2 c  Dried cherries
1 c  Sugar
MMMMM---------------------SPICED CHERRY WINE--------------------------
2    Bottles dry red wine
1 pk Mix
1    Lemon, sliced thinly
Mix: Combine dry ingredients and store in an airtight container.

Spiced Cherry Wine: Heat all ingredients, reserving a few lemon
slices, but do not allow to boil. Keep warm over low heat 1 hour.
Serve warm garnished with remaining lemon slices.
