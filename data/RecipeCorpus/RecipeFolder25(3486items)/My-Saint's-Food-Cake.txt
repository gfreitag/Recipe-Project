Title: My Saint's Food Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM---------------------------
1 ts Salt
1/3 c  Cocoa
1 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Margarine
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 3/4 c  Flour
1 1/4 ts Baking soda
Cream marg and sugar.  Add eggs and beat til fluffy. Sift dry
ingredients together and add alternately with milk and vanilla. Bake
350 in greased 8x8 pans.  Ice with white vanilla icing.
