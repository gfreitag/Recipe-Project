Title: Rich Lemon Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
2 lg Eggs
2 T  Finely grated lemon rind.
Makes 12 regular size
-muffins.
Heat oven to 400.  Grease
-12 muffin cups.
2 c  Unsifted all purpose flour
1/2 c  Plus 2 tbsp. sugar
1 T  Baking powder
1 t  Salt
1/2 c  (1 stick) butter or
-margarine
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice
Combine flour, 1/2 cup sugar, baking powder, and salt; blend well Melt
butter.  Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice, eggs, and lemon
rind. Stir egg mixture into dry ingredients and blend until well
moistened. Spoon into muffin cups; sprinkle top of batter with 2
tbsp. of sugar. Bake until lightly browned  15 - 20 minutes. Shared
