Title: Stuffed Mushrooms *Sss*
Categories: Mushroom, Italian, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Parsley, minced
Salt and pepper
1/2 ts Fresh basil, minced
2 cl Garlic, minced
-Sherilyn Schamber
1 lb Mushrooms
5 sl Bacon
1/2 c  Onion, minced
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
Preheat oven 425. Remove and mince stems of mushrooms. Fry bacon until
crisp. Remove with slotted spoon and crumble. Saute onion in bacon
fat. Place cream cheese in bowl and combine with mushroom stems,
bacon, onion, parsley, salt, pepper, basil, and garlic. Stuff
mushroom caps with mixture. Place on greased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10
mins. Source: Mama D's Submitted By JR BYERS   On   12-10-94
