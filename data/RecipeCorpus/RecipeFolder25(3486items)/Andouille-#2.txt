Title: Andouille #2
Categories: Cajun/creol, Sausage, Information
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  Pepper
1 t  Cayenne pepper
1 t  Chili pepper
1/2 t  Ground mace
1/2 t  Ground cloves
1/2 t  Ground allspice
1 T  Minced thyme
1 T  Minced marjoram
1 T  Minced parsley
4 lb Pork (2 lbs fat, 2 lbs lean)
1 lb Inner lining of pork
-stomach or largest
-intestine
- (chitterlings)
2 ea Garlic cloves
3 ea Bay leaves
2 ea Lge onions
1 T  Salt - (not iodized)
(you can use an extra pound of pork instead of the tripe.) Chop, do
not grind the meat.  Mix with seasonings.  Stuff into casings. Age at
least overnight and then smoke several hours using hickory, hackberry
or ash. (Do not use pine.)  Throw anything sweet, such as cane sugar
or syrup, raw sugar, molasses, sugar cane or brown sugar on the wood
before lighting. Source: Ellen Cleary
