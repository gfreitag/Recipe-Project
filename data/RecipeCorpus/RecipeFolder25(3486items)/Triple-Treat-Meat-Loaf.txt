Title: Triple-Treat Meat Loaf
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Pork, Veal, Meatloaf
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Milk
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Chili sauce; divided
1/4 c  Breadcrumbs, dry
1/4 c  Cheese, Parmesan
1/4 c  Peanuts; chopped
2 tb Sugar, brown
2 tb Fruit juice
1 ts Mustard, prepared
1 lb Beef, ground
1 lb Pork, ground
1 lb Veal, ground
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
1/4 c  Celery; finely chopped
1 c  Oatmeal; quick-cooking
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Eggs; beaten
Combine all the ground meat and mix thoroughly. Add onion, celery,
oatmeal, salt, and pepper.  Add beaten egg to milk; add
Worcestershire sauce and add to meat mixture; mix well.  Form into 3
loaves and place in a greased 13x9x2" pan.  Spread 1/2 cup chili
sauce over 1 loaf and sprinkle on a topping made by combine dry
breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese.

Spread second loaf with 1/2 cup chili sauce and sprinkle with chopped
peanuts.  Top third loaf with a glaze made by combine brown sugar,
fruit juice, and mustard.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
