Title: Sybil Carter's Secret Salad Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing
Yield: 8 servings
2 T  Salt
1 t  Black pepper
1    Garlic clove,peeled
1 c  Peanut oil
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 T  Sugar
Place contents in a Mason jar, screw on lid, and shake vigorously.
Chill for several hours. Remove galic clove and discard. Add dressing
to salad before serving.
