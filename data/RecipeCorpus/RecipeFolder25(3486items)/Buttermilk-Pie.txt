Title: Buttermilk Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
3 ea Eggs
1 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Vanilla
1 ea 9-inch crust, unbaked
1/2 c  Butter, melted
2 c  Sugar
3 tb Flour, rounded
Mix all ingredients, pour in pie crust, and bake in moderate oven (350
degrees) until set. (Flora Gardner, Abilene)
