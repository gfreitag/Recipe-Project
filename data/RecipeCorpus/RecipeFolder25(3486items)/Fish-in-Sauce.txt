Title: Fish in Sauce
Categories: Stove top, Fish, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2 T  Chopped onions
1 cn Whole tomatoes peeled
2 T  Lemon juice
1 c  Water
4 lg Fillets of fish
2 T  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Sour cream
Salt and pepper
Simmer all ingredients except cream over a slow fire about 20 minutes
or until fish is done. Remove fish, add cream and continue to cook
until sauce is thick. Pour sauce over fish when serving. Just about
any fish that can be filleted can be used in this recipe.
