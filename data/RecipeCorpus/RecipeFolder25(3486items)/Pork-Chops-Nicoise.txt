Title: Pork Chops Nicoise
Categories: French, Pork, Meat, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1    Green Pepper;chopped;
1 ts Basil
1/2 c  Olives;black
1/4 c  Red Wine
6    Pork Chops;
1 tb Olive Oil
4    Tomato;chopped;
2    Garlic;minced;cloves
Heat oil in a large skillet and fry chops until light brown. Add
tomatoes, garlic, pepper, basil and wine.
Cover and simmer 30 minutes, turn chops once. Add olives and cook
over low heat 10 minutes.
Serve with rice.
