Title: Herbed Popover Mix with Recipe
Categories: Bread, Mixes, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
8 tb Butter
1 pk Mix
2 c  Milk
6    Eggs
2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Crumbled sage
-POPOVERS:
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Herbed Popovers: Preheat oven to 400 and place 1 tb butter in each of
8 custard cups or popover molds. Place in oven to melt the butter. In
a large bowl, combine mix, milk and eggs and stir with awire whisk
until smooth. Pour into the prepared cups until 2/3 full. Return
