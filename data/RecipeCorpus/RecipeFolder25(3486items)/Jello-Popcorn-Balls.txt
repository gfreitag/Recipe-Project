Title: Jello Popcorn Balls
Categories: Dessert, Popcorn
Yield: 8 balls
1/4 c  Butter
2 tb Jello powder
3 c  Pre-popped Popcorn
2 c  Mini Marshmallows
Put popcorn in greased 13 x 9 x 2" pan.  Place pan in 300Ã¸ oven.

Put butter in sauce pan over medium heat.  When the butter is melted
pour in marshmallows.  When marshmallows are melted, stir in jello
powder. When the jello powder is mixed in, take out popcorn.

Pour marshmallow mixture over popcorn.  Stir.  Roll into balls.
