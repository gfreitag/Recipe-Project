Title: Cauliflower, Broccoli & Bell Pepper Salad
Categories: Salad, Middle east, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Tahini
1/4 c  Water
1/3 c  Lemon juice
Salt & pepper
Parsley, chopped
Sesame pita, cut into wedges
1/2 sm Head cauliflower, cut into
-- florets
1/2 bn Broccoli, cut into florets
1 sm Yellow bell pepper, sliced
1 sm Red bell pepper, sliced
1 ea Garlic clove, crushed
Parboil & drain the cauliflower & broccoli.  Combine with bell
peppers in a medium salad bowl.

Whisk together the garlic, tahini, water, lemon juice, salt & pepper
in a small bowl.  Pour dressing over the salad & toss gently to coat.
Check seasonings & adjust if necessary.  Refrigerate covered until
well chilled. Serve on a platter garnished with fresh parsley & pita
wedges.

Virginia Habeeb, "Pita the Great"
