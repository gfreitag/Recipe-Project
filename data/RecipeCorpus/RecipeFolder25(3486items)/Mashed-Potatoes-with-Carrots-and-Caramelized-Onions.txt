Title: Mashed Potatoes with Carrots and Caramelized Onions
Categories: Vegetable, Potato, Side dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 servings
-Cheese (you can use Cottage
-Cheese instead or Cottage
-Cheese drained in a sieve
-to let liquid run out.  The
-final mixture will be
-wetter
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 ts Butter (or non-fat cooking
-spray)
1 lg Baking Potato
1 lg Carrot (or 2 medium)
1/4 c  Non-fat or part-skim Ricotta
Saute onion in butter or non-fat cooking oil, stirring frequently
until onions are golden brown -- 8 to 10 minutes.  Peel potato and
carrots. Cut potato into 8 pieces and each carrot into 6 pieces. Cook
in boiling water until tender, about 8 to 10 minutes.

Mash cooked potato and carrots together until the carrots are in
small chunks.  Mix in the cooked onion, Ricotta cheese, and salt and
pepper to taste.  Serves 2.
