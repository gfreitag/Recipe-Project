Title: Cherry Drop Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 48 servings
2 ea Eggs
1 x  Few drops red food coloring
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 x  Quartered maraschino cherry
1 ea Pk Cherry Supreme Deluxe
-Cake
1/2 c  Cooking oil
2 T  Water
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Blend cake mix, oil, water, eggs and
food coloring.  Stir in nuts.  Drop from a teaspoon onto an ungreased
cookie sheet.  Top each cookie with a quarter maraschino cherry.
Bake for 10-12 minutes. Cool on cookie sheet for about 1 minute, then
to rack to finish cooling.
