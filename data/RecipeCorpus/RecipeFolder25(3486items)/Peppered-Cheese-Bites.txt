Title: Peppered Cheese Bites
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Snack
Yield: 5 dozen
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 c  Unsalted butter; at room
-temperature
6 oz Extra-sharp Cheddar cheese;
-grated (1 1/2 cups)
1/3 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
1.  Preheat oven to 350F.  Combine butter, Cheddar cheese, Parmesan
cheese, paprika, salt and pepper in food processor.  Whirl until
blended. Add flour.  Pulse with on-and-off motions until blended.

2.  Put dough into cookie press, using a flower or Christmas tree
shape. Press out dough onto ungreased cookie sheet, spacing 1 inch
apart.

3.  Bake in preheated 350F oven 14 to 16 minutes until dark golden in
color.  Cool on wire rack.  Makes about 5 1/2 dozen.

*These crackers freeze well.

Nutrient value per bite:  42 calories, 1 g protein, 3 g fat, 2 g
carbohydrate, 34 mg sodium, 9 mg cholesterol.  Exchanges:  1/8
starch/bread, 1/8 lean meat, 1/3 fat.
