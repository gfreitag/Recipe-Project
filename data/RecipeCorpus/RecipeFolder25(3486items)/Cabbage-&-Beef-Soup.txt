Title: Cabbage & Beef Soup
Categories: Ground beef, Vegetable, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1    Tomato can water
4    Beef bouillon cubes
1    Chopped onion
1 cn Kidney beans, undrained
1/2 md Head cabbage, chopped
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Celery stalks, chopped
1    28 oz can tomatoes
In Dutch oven, brown meat; add remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil;
reduce heat and simmer for 1 1/2 hours.

Frozen left overs are wonderful heated in a double boiler
