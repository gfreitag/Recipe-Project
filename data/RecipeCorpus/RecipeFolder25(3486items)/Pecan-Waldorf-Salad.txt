Title: Pecan Waldorf-Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Celery; diced
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Lettuce leaves
1 c  Apples; chopped, unpeeled
2 tb Cherries, maraschino; choppd
1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
Combine fruit, pecans, celery, and mayonnaise.  Chill and serve on
crisp lettuce leaves.
