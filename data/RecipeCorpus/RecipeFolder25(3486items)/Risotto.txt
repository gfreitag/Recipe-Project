Title: Risotto
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Green peas
1 t  Dried rosemary
1 t  Dried oregano
1/2 c  Chopped scallions
1 c  Uncooked brown rice
2 T  Olive oil
2 c  Chicken broth
saute scallions and rice in hot oil in large skillet for about 3
minutes. Stir in broth and bring to a boil. Remove from heat. Pour
mixture into a 1 1/2 qt casserole. Stir in peas and herbs. Cover,
bake at 400 for 20-25 minutes. Liquid should be absorbed.
