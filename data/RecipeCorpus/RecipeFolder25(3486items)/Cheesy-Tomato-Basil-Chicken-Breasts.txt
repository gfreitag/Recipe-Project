Title: Cheesy Tomato Basil Chicken Breasts
Categories: Chicken, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM---------------------MAKES: 6 SERVINGS--------------------------
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
2 t  Minced fresh garlic
3 ea Boneless, skinless chicken
-breasts, halved
3 ea Tb butter or margarine
2 c  Tomatoes, cut in 1" cubes
1/3 c  Chopped onion
6 oz Can tomato paste
1 ea Tb basil leaves
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
2 ea Butter or margarine, melted
6 oz Mozzarella, cut into strips
1 c  Fresh bread crumbs
1/4 c  Chopped fresh parsley
Heat oven to 350~. In 13X9" baking pan, melt 3 Tb butter in oven. Mix
together remaining sauce ingredients except chicken; set aside. Place
chicken in baking dish, turning to coat with butter. Spoon sauce
mixture over chicken. Bake for 30-40 minutes until chicken is no
longer pink. Meanwhile, in small bowl, stir together all topping
ingredients except cheese. Place cheese strips over chicken; sprinkle
with topping mixture. Continue baking 5-10 minutes or until chicken
is fork tender and bread crumbs are browned Source: Land O Lakes
