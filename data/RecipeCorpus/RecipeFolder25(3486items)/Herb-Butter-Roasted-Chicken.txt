Title: Herb Butter Roasted Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------MAKES: 4 SERVINGS--------------------------
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
2 ea Tb chopped fresh parsley
1 t  Minced fresh garlic
4-5 lb whole roasting
-chicken
1/4 c  Butter or margarine,
-softened
1 t  Rosemary leaves, crushed
Heat oven to 350~. Secure wings to body of chicken. stir together
remaining ingredients. Rub chicken with butter mixture. Place on rack
in roasting pan. Bake for 2-2 1/2 hours or until fork tender. Source:
Land O Lakes Treasury of Country Recipes Carolyn Shaw's Collection
