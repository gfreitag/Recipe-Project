Title: Homemade Winter Vinegar
Categories: Can/preserv, Homemade, Condiment, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Onion powder
1/4 t  Garlic powder
1/4 t  Mustard seed
3 ea Whole cloves
1 ea Bay leaf
3 c  White vinegar
Flavour your salad dressing
-with this.
2 T  Celery seed
1/3 c  Parsley flakes
1 t  Black peppercorns
1 ea Tbps granulated sugar
Measure first 9 ingredients into a quart bottle.  Add vinegar.
Cover. Let stand 2 weeks in cool place.  Strain through double layer
of cheesecloth before using.  To use same day, bring mixture to a
boil Remove from heat. Let stand 2 hours.  Strain.  Keeps at least 1
year. Make 1-1/2 pints. Origin: Jean Pare's Preserves Book. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Aug/94.
