Title: Individual Swiss Steaks
Categories: Main dish, Sthrn/livng, Beef, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Thyme
1 md Onion; sliced
1/2 c  Celery; sliced
16 oz Tomatoes; canned
1    Round steak; cut 3/4" thick
1/4 c  Flour, all-purpose
2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
3 tb Shortening
Cut steak int 4 pieces.  Combine flour, salt, and pepper;  Pound
seasoned flour into steaks.  Brown in shortening.  Pour off
drippings. Add thyme, onion, celery, and tomatoes.   Cover tightly
and cook slowly for 1-1/2 to 2 hours or until meat is tender. Thicken
liquid for gravy, if desired.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
Coleman. Submitted By NANCY COLEMAN   On   11-30-94
