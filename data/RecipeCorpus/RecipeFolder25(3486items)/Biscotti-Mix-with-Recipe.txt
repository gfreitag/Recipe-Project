Title: Biscotti Mix with Recipe
Categories: Italian, Mixes, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
-BISCOTTI:
1 pk Mix
3 lg Eggs
1/2 c  Buter, melted
1/2 ts Anise extract
1 lg Egg, separated
2 3/4 c  Flour
2 c  Chopped almonds
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Dried lemon peel
1/4 ts Dried orange peel
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Biscotti: Preheat oven to 375. Combine mix with eggs, butter, anise
and yolk from separated egg. Beat on low speed until dough is smooth.
Roll into 2 12-14" logs. Place on greased cookie sheet. Brush with
the egg white. Bake 30 minutes, until golden brown. Reduce h
