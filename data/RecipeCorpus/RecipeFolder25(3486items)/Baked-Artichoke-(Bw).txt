Title: Baked Artichoke (Bw)
Categories: Main dish, Vegetable
Yield: 10 - 12 servi
6 tb Garlic salt
2 c  Bread crumbs, seasoned
2 ts Basil
5 ea Artichokes (15 oz. cans)
1 3/4 c  Olive oil
8 tb Parmesan cheese
Mix bread crumbs,cheese,garlic salt, & basil.add olive oil,if mixture
is too dry add more olive oil until it is as moist as stuffing would
be. Drain artichokes, Cut bottom of artichokes off & cut each
artichoke in half. In a 2 quart casserole dish, (using all of
artichoke) alternate layers of artichoke & bread crumb mixture ending
with bread crumb mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 - 45 minutes.
