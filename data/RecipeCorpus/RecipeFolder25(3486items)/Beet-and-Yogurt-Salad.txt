Title: Beet and Yogurt Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable, Middle east
Yield: 4 servings
MOST O LABOO
Housewives in Teheran often buy cooked beets from vendors who hawk
their wares.  The beets are purchased in quantity and stored in the
refrigerator to be used when wanted in salads, soups, or traditional
dishes. 1 lb cooked beets, peeled and grated 2 cups yoghurt 1. Mix
the beets and yoghurt together.  Refrigerate. Serve cold or at room
temperature as a snack or as a salad with dairy dishes. Serves 4.
