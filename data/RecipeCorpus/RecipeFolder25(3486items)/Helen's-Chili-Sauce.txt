Title: Helen's Chili Sauce
Categories: Can/preserv, Vegetable, Salsa/sauce, Tomato
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Cinnamon
1 tb Allspice
1 c  Vinegar
2    Red peppers, chopped fine
9    Tomatoes, ripe, peeled
1    Onion, chopped fine
1 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Salt
1 tb Ginger
Put everything in a large kettle and boil until thick, stirring often
so it doesn't stick.  Put in sterilized jars and seal.  Boiling takes
a couple of hours until it is good and thick.
