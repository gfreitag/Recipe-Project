Title: Wilted Spinach Salad
Categories: Salad, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
4    Feta Cheese;crumbled;Oz.
8    Croutons;Oz.
4 tb Olive Oil
2 tb Wine Vinegar
1 lb Spinach;cleaned
12    Greek Olive;
1    Garlic;chopped;clove
1 ts Oregano;chopped
Chop cleaned spinach. Remove pits from olives. Combine all
ingredients except olive oil in a metal bowl.
Heat olive oil in a saucepan till almost smoking. Pour hot oil over
salad and toss quickly. Add more vinegar if necessary.
