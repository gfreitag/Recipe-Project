Title: Hearty Cheese and Toast
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Turkey, Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
-cheese (8 oz.)
4-6 Slices White or whole
-wheat bread, toasted
4-6 Slices Cooked turkey,
-chicken, beef or ham,
-optional
Chopped fresh parsley
1 T  Butter or margarine
1 T  All-purpose flour
2/3 c  Milk
1 t  Worcestershire sauce
1/2 t  Dry mustard
1 t  Whole caraway seeds
Pepper to taste
2 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
In a saucepan, melt butter over medium-high heat. Stir in flour to
form a smooth paste. Gradually stir in milk. Cook and stir until the
mixture is smooth and thickened. Add the Worcestershire sauce,
mustard, caraway and pepper. Stir until well blended. Add the cheese
stir and cook over low heat until well melted. Cut toast diagonally
and place on individual plates. If desired, top each with a slice of
meat. Spoon cheese sauce over meat and toast. Sprinkle with parsley.
Serve immediately. Serves 4. SOURCE:*Dawn Supina, Edmonton, Alberta,
