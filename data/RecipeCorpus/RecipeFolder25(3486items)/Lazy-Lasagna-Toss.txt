Title: Lazy Lasagna Toss
Categories: Pasta, Sausage, Cheese/eggs, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
Makes a great partner.
1 lb Hot Italian sausages
3 c  Chunky vegetable spaghetti
-sauce
1 c  Ricotta cheese
1/4 c  Freshly grated parmesan
-cheese
12 oz Pasta shells or fusilli,
-cooked
Here are all the flavors of
-lasagna - zesty sausage,
-tomato sauce, pasta
And a dollop of creamy
-ricotta - in a no bake, no
-layer, no fuss toss.
A sprinkle of chopped
-parsley adds a fresh note
-and crisp Caesar salad
Remove sausages from casing.  In large skillet, heat 1 tsp vegetable
oil over medium high heat.  Cook sausage, stirring and breaking up
with fork, for 5-6 minutes or until meat is no longer pink.  Drain
off fat. Stir in spaghetti sauce, cook for 3-4 minutes or until
bubbling. Meanwhile, in small microwaveable bowl or saucepan, mix
ricotta with parmesan. Microwave at high for 45 seconds, or cook over
mediumheat for 3 minutes, or just until parmesan begins to melt.
Toss spaghetti sauce with hot pasta. Divide among bowls.  Spoon
ricotta mixture evenly over top. Makes 4 servings. Per serving: 875
calories, 40 gr protein, 36 gr fat, 96 gr carbo, very high source
fibre, excellent source calcium, and good source iron. Origin:
Canadian Living, Nov/94. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
