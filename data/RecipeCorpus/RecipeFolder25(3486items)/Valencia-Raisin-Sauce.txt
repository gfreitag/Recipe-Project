Title: Valencia Raisin Sauce
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Raisins
1 T  Sliced green onions
1 t  Cider vinegar
1/3 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
2 T  Cornstarch
1 t  Grated orange peel
2 c  Orange juice
In saucepan, combine brown sugar, cornstarch and orange peel.
Blend in orange juice and raisins.  Cook until mixture comes to a
boil and thickens, stirring occasionally.  Stir in green onions and
vinegar.  Makes about 2 1/2 cups sauce.

Microwave instructions:  In 4-cup glass measure, combine brown
sugar, cornstarch, orange peel, orange juice and raisins.  Microwave
at HIGH (100%) for 8 to 10 minutes, or until thickened; stir every 2
minutes. Stir in green onions and vinegar.

An all-around sauce that adds zest to any dish. Serve over grilled
salmon, cooked poultry, pork or lamb.
