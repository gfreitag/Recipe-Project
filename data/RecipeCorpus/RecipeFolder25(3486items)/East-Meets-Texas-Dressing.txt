Title: East-Meets-Texas Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Soy sauce
1 t  Shredded fresh ginger root
1/3 c  Pace Picante Sauce
1/4 c  Orange marmalade
Combine Pace Picante Sauce, marmalade, soy sauce and ginger root.
Toss with any mixed green salad for far-out Far Eastern flavor. Makes
about 2/3 cup. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   11-21-94
