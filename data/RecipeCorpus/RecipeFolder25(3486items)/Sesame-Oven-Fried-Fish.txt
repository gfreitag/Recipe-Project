Title: Sesame Oven-Fried Fish
Categories: Main dish, Fish
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Snipped fresh coriander; or
-1 ts. dried cilantro; crshd
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Garlic powder
14 ts Pepper
4    Catfish fillets (1 lb)
1/4 c  Skim milk
1/4 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
1/4 c  Yellow corn meal
1 tb Toasted sesame seed
Rinse fish and pat dry with paper towels.  Pour milk into shallow
pan. In another shallow pan, combine crumbs, corn meal, sesame seeds,
coriander, mustard, garlic powder and pepper.  Dip fish into milk,
then coat with crumb mixture.  Spray a 13x9x2 inch pan with non-stick
cooking spray. Arrange fish in pan.  Bake at 450 degrees until golden
and fish flakes easily when tested with a fork (allow 4 to 6 minutes
per 1/2 inch thickness).
