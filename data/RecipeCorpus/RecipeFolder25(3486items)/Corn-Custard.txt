Title: Corn Custard
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 3 servings
3    Eggs
1 tb Flour
Salt and pepper to taste
3    Ears of corn, cut off the
Cob
2 c  Milk
2 tb Sugar
Combine sugar and flour, stir in eggs and milk.  Add corn, salt and
pepper. Bake 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees. Randy Rigg
