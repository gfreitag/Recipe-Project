Title: Insalata Alassio
Categories: Salad, Side dish, Italian, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
Wine Vinegar;
Salt;
Pepper;
12    Tomato;slices
12    Mozzarella Cheese;slices
1/4 c  Basil;chopped, fresh
4 tb Olive Oil
On a platter, arrange alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella.
Sprinkle with basil and dribble oil and vinegar. Salt and pepper to
taste.
Great with a barbecue or any hot weather meal.
