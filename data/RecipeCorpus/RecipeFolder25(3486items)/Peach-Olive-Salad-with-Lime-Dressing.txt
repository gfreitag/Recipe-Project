Title: Peach-Olive Salad with Lime Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 4 servings
29 oz Peach halves
Paprika
Nuts; chopped
12 oz Cottage cheese
3/4 c  Olives, ripe; chopped
1/4 ts Salt
Salad greens
MMMMM-----------------------LIME DRESSING----------------------------
2 tb Honey; or sugar
ds Cayenne pepper
1/2 c  Oil, salad
1/2 c  Lime juice
1/2 ts Salt
Mix cottage cheese, olives, and salt and mound on salad greens with
drained peach halves arranges on side.  Fill peach centers with
additional cheese mixture.  Sprinkle wiht paprika and nuts.  Serve
with Lime Dressing.

Lime Dressing: Combine all ingredients and shake well. Yield: 1 cup.
