Title: Teriyaki Shark Steaks
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Fish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Pineapple Juice
1 c  Teriyaki Sauce
6    Shark;1 in. thick;steaks
1 tb Gelatin
Heat teriyaki sauce and pineapple juice; add gelatin and simmer 5
minutes. Refrigerate until thickened, about 1 1/2 hrs.
Broil shark for 4 minutes each side; while broiling, brush glaze
over steaks.
