Title: Pecan Tassies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains
Yield: 2 dozen
1 tb Soft butter
1 ts Vanilla
pn Salt
2/3 c  Coarsely broken pecans
1    3 oz package cream cheese
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  All-purpose flour
1    Egg
3/4 c  Brown sugar
CHEESE PASTRY; Let cream cheese and 1/2 c butter soften at room
temperature; blend together.  Stir in flour. Chill about 1 hour.
Shape in 2 dozen 1" balls; place in ungreased 1 3/4 inch muffin pans.
Press dough against bottoms and sides.

PECAN FILLING: Beat together egg, brown sugar, 1 tb. butter, vanilla
and salt just till smooth.  Divide half the pecans among pastry-lined
pans; add egg mixture and top with remaining pecans. Bake in slow
oven ( 325 ) 25 minutes or till filling is set.  Cool. remove from
pans.
