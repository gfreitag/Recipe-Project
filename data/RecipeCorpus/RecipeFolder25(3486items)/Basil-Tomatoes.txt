Title: Basil Tomatoes
Categories: Salad, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
Salt;pinch
Pepper;pinch
Garlic Powder;pinch
3    Tomato;sliced;medium
2 tb Olive Oil
1 ts Basil
2 ts Vinegar
Combine oil, vinegar, basil, salt and pepper. Add tomatoes and
evenly coat. Add pinch garlic powder.
Chill at least 2 hours.
