Title: Seasoned Salt Mix
Categories: Mixes, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Paprika
1/8 ts Celery seed
1/4 ts White pepper
1/4 ts Dry mustard
3/4 c  Salt
1/4 c  Garlic salt
1 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Oregano
Mix ingredients together and store in airtight containers.

Use with Father's Day Burgers and Grilled Vegetables (separate
recipes).
