Title: Cottage Cheese Cake
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Sugar
2 ts Grated lemon rind
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Whipping cream
Topping of your choice
1 c  Fine rusk crumbs
3 tb Melted butter
3 tb Sugar
3/4 ts Sinnamon
2    16 oz. cartons cottage cheee
5    Eggs
1/2 c  Sifted all-purpose flour
Heat oven to 350 F.  Lightly butter a 9 inch spring-form pan. Combine
rusk crumbs, melted butter, 3 tb. sugar and cinnamon.  Press evenly
in bottom of prepared pan.  Bake 10 minutes. Press cottage cheese
through a sieve into mixer bowl.  Add eggs, one at a time, and beat
well after each addition. Mix flour and 1 c. sugar and beat into
cheese mixture. Stir in lemon rind, lemon juice and vanilla. Whip
cream and fold in. Pour on crust in the spring-form pan. Bake about 1
hour and 10 minutes or until set.  Cool in pan, then chill. Top with
strawberry topping or your choice. Chill again. Submitted By FLORENCE
