Title: Frosty Cranberry Tiptops
Categories: Salad, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
3 tb Lemon juice
3 oz Cream cheese, whipped
1/4 c  Sifted confectioners sugar
1 lb Jellied cranberry sauce
1 c  Heavy cream, whipped
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Chopped walnuts
Crush cranberry sauce with fork.  Add lemon juice.  Pour into paper
cups or refrigerator tray.  Combine remaining ingredients and spread
over cranberry mixture.  Freeze firm.  Unmold on crisp lettuce. Randy
