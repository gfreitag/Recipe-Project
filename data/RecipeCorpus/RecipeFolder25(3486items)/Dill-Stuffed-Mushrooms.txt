Title: Dill Stuffed Mushrooms
Categories: Appetizer, Mushroom, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
1/2 ts Dried dillweed
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Worcestershire Sauce
24 lg Mushrooms
2 tb Sliced green onion with
-tops
2 tb Butter or margarine
Remove stems from mushrooms; chop stems.  Cook stems and onion in
but-
ter until tender.  Remove from heat.  Stir in bread crumbs,
dillweed,
salt, and Worcestershire; fill mushroom crowns with bread crumb
mix-
ture.  Bake on a greased baking sheet at 425F for 6 to 8 minutes.
Makes 24.
