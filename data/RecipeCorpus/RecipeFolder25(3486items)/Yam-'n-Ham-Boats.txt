Title: Yam 'n Ham Boats
Categories: Vegetable, Meat, Pork, Main dish, Sweetpotato
Yield: 1 servings
-chopped
Salt
Pepper
Cheese, grated
Sweet Potatoes, baked
Ham, cooked and chopped
Onions, sauteed and chopped
Bell Peppers, sauteed and
Scoop out the flesh of baked sweet potatoes, leaving the skins
intact. Mash the potatoes and add ham, onions, green peppers, and
salt and pepper to taste.  Stuff the mixture back into the shells,
top with grated cheese, and put under the broiler until the cheese
melts.

Source:  S.H.A.R.E. Colorado Nov./Dec. 1994
