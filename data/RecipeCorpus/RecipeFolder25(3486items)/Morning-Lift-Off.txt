Title: Morning Lift Off
Categories: Vegetarian, Breakfast, Beverage
Yield: 5 cups
MMMMM--------------------VEGGIE LIFE; JULY 94-------------------------
1/4 c  Grape Nuts
1/4 c  Firm tofu; sliced
1 tb Wheat germ
2 c  Fresh squeezed orange juice
1/2 c  Oatmeal; cooked, hot
1 tb Honey or alternative
3 sm Bananas; sliced
In a blender, mix orange juice, oatmeal and honey with bananas 1
minute on medium speed.

Add cereal, tofu and wheat germ and blend for 1 more minute.
