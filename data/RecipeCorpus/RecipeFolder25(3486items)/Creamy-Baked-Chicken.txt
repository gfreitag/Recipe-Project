Title: Creamy Baked Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Cream of Chicken Soup
1 c  Stuffing Mix
1/4 c  Butter, Melted
8    Skinless, Boneless
Chicken Breasts
8 sl Swiss Cheese
Arrange Chicken in a lightly greased 13 X 9 inch oven proof dish.
top with cheese, spoon soup over all.  Sprinkle with the stuffing
Mix, drizzle Butter or Margarine over the crumbs, Bake at 350 for 45
~ 55 minutes. Serve over rice or Noodles.
