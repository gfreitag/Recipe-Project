Title: Southern Black-Eyed Peas
Categories: Cajun/creol, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
1    Celery rib,chopped
1    Ham bone
1    Chunk of salt pork
Salt to taste
Black pepper to taste
1 lb Black-eyes peas,dried
2 qt Water
1    Onion,chopped
1/3 c  Green bell pepper,chopped
1/2    Jalapeno pepper,diced
1/3 c  Red bell pepper,chopped
1. Rinse peas; soak overnight in cold water to cover.

2. Drain and put in Dutch oven with 2 quarts fresh water.

3. Add remaining ingredients; cover and simmer about 2 hours, or
until peas begin to soften enough to mash easily.

4. Add water as needed.
