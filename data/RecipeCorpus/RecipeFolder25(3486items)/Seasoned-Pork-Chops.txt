Title: Seasoned Pork Chops
Categories: Main dish, Pork
Yield: 4 servings
4    Center cut pork chops
2 T  Oil
1 pk Beef stew seasoning
1/4 c  Flour
Combine beef stew seasoning and flour. Coat pork chops with seasoning
mixture. Pan fry in oil until thoroughly coated.
