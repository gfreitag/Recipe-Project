Title: Tripe Patties
Categories: Meat, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Onion juice
1 t  Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 lb Boiled tripe
1    Egg
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
Grind tripe and combine with remaining ingredients.  Shape into
patties and fry in greased skillet until browned.  Variations- Make
patties 1/2 inch thick and brown under low broiler heat.Baked Tripe
with Bacon- Wrap each patty with slice of bacon and bake in hot oven
(425 F.) 8 to 10 minutes, or until bacon is crisp.  Tripe Fritters-
Sift 1 c sifted flour with 1/2 tsp salt.  Combine 1 c milk, 1 T
melted shortening and 1 egg, slightly beaten. Add to flour and mix
until smooth. Dip tripe patties into the batter and fry in hot deep
fat (380 F.) until browned. Submitted By BRENT WILLIAMS   On
