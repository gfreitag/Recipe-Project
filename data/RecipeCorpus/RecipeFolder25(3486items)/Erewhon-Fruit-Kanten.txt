Title: Erewhon Fruit Kanten
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 1 recipe
1 tb Erewhon Kuzu
1 pt Strawberries
1/2 lb Seedless grapes
2 tb Honey (optional)
1 qt Apple juice or cider
1    Orange; juiced
1    Lemon; juiced
1/3 c  Erewhon Agar Flakes
Pour juices into large pot.  Stir in Agar.  Dissolve Kuzu in 1/4 cup
water. Blend until smooth white paste, then add to juice.

Bring liquid to boil, stirring frequently.  Lower flame and simmer
for 20 minutes.  Remove from heat.

Slice fruit and add to juice.  Pour mixture into shallow, heat-proof
serving dish.  Allow to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate
for 2-3 hours.

Garnish with chopped nuts or coconut if desired.
