Title: Double Fudge Brownie Mix with Recipe
Categories: Cake/cookie, Mixes, Chocolate
Yield: 6 servings
-BROWNIES:
1 c  Butter, softened
4    Eggs
1 pk Mix
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Cocoa (not Dutch process)
1 c  Flour
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 c  Chocolate chips
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Double Fudge Brownies: Preheat oven to 325. grease a 13X9" pan. Cream
the butter. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each. Add
mix, continue to beat until mixture is smooth. Spread into prepared
pan and bake 40-50 minutes.
