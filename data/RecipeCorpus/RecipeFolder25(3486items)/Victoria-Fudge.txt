Title: Victoria Fudge
Categories: Candy, Fruit, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Chopped candied pineapple
1 ts Almond or rose flavoring
1/4 c  Chopped figs
3 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Sweet cream
1 c  Coconut
1/4 c  Chopped candied cherries
Combine cream, butter, and sugar.  Boil to soft ball stage (234 - 238
F). Remove from fire.  Cool to room temperature. Beat until creamy.
Add chopped fruit and coconut.  Add flavoring. Pour into
well-buttered shallow pan. Cut in squares. Mrs. Martin Nelson,
Homestead, MI. Submitted By LAWRENCE KELLIE   On   11-12-94
