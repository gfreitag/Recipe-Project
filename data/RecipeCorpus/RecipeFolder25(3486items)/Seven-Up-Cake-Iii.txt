Title: Seven-Up Cake Iii
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
3 c  Self-rising Flour
2 tb Lemon Extract
3/4 c  Seven-Up
1 1/2 c  Margarine
3 c  Sugar
5 ea Eggs
Cream together margarine and sugar for 20 minutes. THIS IS IMPORTANT!
Add eggs, one at a time. Add flour, unsifted. Blend in Seven-Up and
lemon extract. Pour into a well-greased Bundt pan and bake at 325
degrees for 1 to 1 1/4 hours or until cake tests done. Very easy and
very good. Shared by MARY BOWLES, Prodigy ID# DNSR31A.
