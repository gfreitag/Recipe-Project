Title: Basic Cranberry Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Relish, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Fruit Liqueur
1    Bag Cranberries
1 c  Sugar
Put the cranberries in a heavy bottomed pot with just enough water to
cover. Add 1 cup of sugar per bag of cranberries and bring to a boil.
When the berries begin to pop, turn off heat, cover and cool to room
temp. Stir in a little liquer of your choice and chill
