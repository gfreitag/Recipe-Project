Title: Abm--Whole Wheat Bread
Categories: Bread
Yield: 1 servings
2 2/3 c  Bread flour
1 1/3 c  Whole wheat flour
5 tb Nonfat dry milk
2 1/2 ts Yeast
1 1/3 c  Water
5 tb Margerine; or butter
1 1/2    Eggs; optional
2 1/2 tb Sugar
2 ts Salt
Makes 1 1/2 pound loaf.

Bake on medium color setting.

Add 1t gluten for each cup of flour.
