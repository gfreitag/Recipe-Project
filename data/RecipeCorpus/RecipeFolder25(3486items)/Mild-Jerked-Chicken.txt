Title: Mild Jerked Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Bbq/grill, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Teriyaki sauce
2 1/2 t  Ground ginger
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Cayenne pepper
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
3 1/2 lb Chicken parts
Serve this gentle version
-of the Jamaican national
-barbecue the way Blue
Jays Pitcher Mike Timlin
-and Wife Dawn do - with
-rice a zesty mixed
Green salad
In large bowl, stir together teriyaki sauce, ginger, salt, cayenne
pepper and garlic. Add chicken parts, turning to coat well.  With
tongs, transfer chickend to greased rimmed baking sheet, skin side
down. Reserve sauce. Bake in 400F oven turnign once and basting often
with xauce, for about 45 minutes or until browned and juice run clear
when chicken is pierced. Makes 6 servings. Origin: Canadian Living,
December 1994. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
