Title: Peanut Butter Pudding
Categories: Pudding
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
Prepare vanilla or chocolate pudding according to package directions.
Add 1/4 cup smooth or chunk peanut butter.  Beat with rotary or
electric mixer until well blended.  Chill at least 1 hour. Garnish
with chocolate curls or bits. From: Lancaster Farming Shared By: Pat
