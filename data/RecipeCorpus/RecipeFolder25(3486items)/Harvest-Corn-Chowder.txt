Title: Harvest Corn Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Whole kernel corn
4 c  Diced peeled potatoes
1 cn Condensed cream of mushroom
-soup, undiluted
Jar    Sliced mushrooms, drained (6 oz.) 3 C Milk
1 md Onion, chopped
1 T  Butter or margarine
2 cn Cream-style corn (14 1/2
-oz. ea.)
(10 3/4 oz.) 1
1/2 Medium Green pepper, chopped 1/2-1 Medium Sweet red pepper,
chopped Pepper to taste 1/2 Lb. Bacon, cooked and crumbled In a
saucepan, saute onion in butter until tender. Add cream-style corn,
kernel corn, potatoes, soup and mushrooms. Stir in milk. Add green
and red peppers. Season with pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes or until
vegetables are tender. Garnish with bacon. Yield: About 12 servings.
(3 1/2 qts.) SOURCE:*Carolyn Lough, Medley, Alberta, Country Woman,
July/Aug. 1993 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 1/94
