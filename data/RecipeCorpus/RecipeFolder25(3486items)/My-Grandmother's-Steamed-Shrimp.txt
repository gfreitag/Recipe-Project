Title: My Grandmother's Steamed Shrimp
Categories: Cajun/creol, Seafood, Main dish, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Vegetable Cooking Oil
1 c  Chopped Celery
1/2 c  Parsley
1 ea Clove Garlic
5 lb Raw Headless Shrimp in Shell
1 c  Chopped Onion
1/2 c  Green Onion
1/2 c  Green Onion
1/2 c  Green Pepper
Heat oil and add shrimp. Cook until pink. Add all seasonings.
:
Cover and cook 10 minutes by clock. season to taste with salt, black
pepper, and red or cayenne pepper. Cover and cook 10 minutes more.
Serve with sliced French Bread.
