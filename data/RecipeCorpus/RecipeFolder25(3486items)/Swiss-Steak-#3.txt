Title: Swiss Steak #3
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 6 servings
2 ea Ribs Celery, chopped
1 cn Tomatoes with liquid, cut
-up (16 oz.)
2 T  Worcestershire sauce
1/4 t  Dried oregano
Hot cooked noodles
-(optional)
2 lb Beef round steak (1 inch
-thick)
3 T  All-purpose flour
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
2 T  Cooking oil
2 md Onions, sliced
Cut meat into serving size pieces and dredge in flour. Sprinkle with
salt and pepper. In a skillet or Dutch oven, brown meat on both sides
in oil. Top with onions and celery. Combine tomatoes, Worcestershire
sauce and oregano; spoon over vegetables. Cover and simmer 1 1/2-2
hours or until meat is tender. Serve over noodles if desired. Serves
6. SOURCE:* Country Magazine December/January 1995 POSTED BY: Jim
