Title: Coffee Continental Mix with Recipe
Categories: Mixes, Beverage
Yield: 13 servings
1 1/2 tb Mix
6 oz Boiling water
-Whipped cream
1 c  Cocoa mix
3/4 c  Instant coffee
-COFFEE:
Mix: Combine the ingredients and store in an airtight container.

Coffee Continental: Pour boiling water over mix in bottom of cup.
Garnish with whipped cream.
