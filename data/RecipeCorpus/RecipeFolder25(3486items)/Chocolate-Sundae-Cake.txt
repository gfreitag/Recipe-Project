Title: Chocolate Sundae Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
3 tb Cocoa
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Cold water
2 tb Shortening
1 c  Flour
1    Square of chocolate
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  MILNOT
Melt shortening with chocolate.  Then sift flour, salt, sugar, baking
powder and then add milk, vanilla and nuts.  Fold in chocolate
mixture. Put in 6 inch loaf pan. Combine cocoa, white sugar and brown
sugar and shake over the cake.  Then cover the cake with cold water
and bake about 1 hour at 325 degrees.  Serve warm or cold with
whipped cream. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   10-19-94
