Title: Broccoli-Shrimp Crisp
Categories: Main dish, Casserole, Vegetable, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Cheese, American; shredded
1 c  Breadcrumbs; toasted
2 tb Butter; melted
2    Eggs; hard-cooked, sliced
1 c  Shrimp; cooked
10 oz Broccoli spears
1 cn Soup, cream of mushroom
1/4 c  Milk; or water
Peel, devein, and cut shrimp in half.  Cook broccoli, according to
package directions, just under tender.  Heat soup in saucepan with
milk; stir until smooth.  Stir in shrimp.  Arrange layers of
broccoli, soup mixture, and cheese in a baking dish.  Sprinkle with
crumbs and spoon melted butter over top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20
minutes. Just before serving, slice hard-cooked eggs and arrange over
casserole.
