Title: Holiday Snow Peas
Categories: Diabetic, Side dish, Vegetable, Holiday
Yield: 4 sweet ones
2    Scallions; trimmed & sliced
2 oz (1 jar) pimentos; drained
1 tb Sherry -=OR=-
1 tb Sherry vinegar;
Nonstick cooking spray
1/4 lb Fresh snow peas; stem and
-strings removed -=OR=-
2 pk (6 oz)frozen snow peas;
Spray a wok or nonstick skillet lightly with cooking spray.  Place
snow peas and scallions in the wok or skillet, and stir-fry over
medium-high heat, stirring constantly for 2 minutes.  Add pimentos
and sherry. Stir-fry one minute longer.  Peas should be bright and
crisp. Serve hot. Food Exchanges per serving: 1 VEGETABLE EXCHANGE;
CAL: 39; FAT: 0g; CHO: 0mg; SOD: 4mg; CAR: 7gm; SUGARS: 4gm;  PRO:
3gm; Source: Diabetes Forecast, Dec. 1994 Brought to you and yours
