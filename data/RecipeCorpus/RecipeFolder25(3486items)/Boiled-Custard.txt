Title: Boiled Custard
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  Whipped Cream
2 qt Milk
6 ea Eggs (separated)
Bring milk and sugar to a boil, beat egg yolks, add to milk.  Blend
well, stiring for 5 minutes. Remove from heat, let cool well ++ Beat
egg whites well, add to cool mixture. Before serving add whipped
cream. The nog is added in the cups for the adults as it's served.
