Title: Smashed Potatoes
Categories: Diabetic, Potato, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 10 sweet ones
1/4 ts Salt;
1/2 ts Pepper;
4 ts Butter-flavored sprinkles;
2 ts Fresh chives; chopped
9 md Potatoes;
1/2 c  Nonfat sour cream;
-=OR=-
1/2 c  Nonfat yogurt;
1/4 c  Evaporated skim milk;
Wash, peel, and quarter the potatoes.  Place potatoes in a large
saucepan with just enough water to cover them. Cover the saucepan and
boil potatoes for 20 to 25 minutes or till tender.   Drain the
potatoes. Combine them with sour cream substitute, skim milk, salt,
pepper, butter-flavored sprinkles, and chives.  Mash with a potato
masher or an electric mixer at medium speed for 2 minutes or until
potato mixture is smooth.
