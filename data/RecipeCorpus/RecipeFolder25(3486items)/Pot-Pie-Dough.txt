Title: Pot Pie Dough
Categories: Pie/tart, Pasta
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Mashed potatoes
2 c  Flour
1 ea Egg beaten
Measure flour;  add potatoes.   Stir in eggs.  Mix well.  Roll 1/4
inch thick.  Cut in squares.   Dry.  Use in pot pie recipes. From:
Lancaster Farming Shared By: Pat Stockett
