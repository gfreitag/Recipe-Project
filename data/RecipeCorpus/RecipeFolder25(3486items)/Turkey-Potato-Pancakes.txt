Title: Turkey-Potato Pancakes
Categories: Main dish, Turkey, Potato, Poultry, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Cranberry Sauce
1/2 ts Sage;dried
ds Pepper
3    Egg;beaten;
3 c  Potato;shredded
1 1/2 c  Turkey;chopped, cooked
1 1/2 c  Onion;grated
1 tb Flour;white
Combine eggs, potato, turkey, and onion.  Add flour, salt, and
pepper; mix well.
Drop about 1/4 cup batter for each pancake onto hot, greased
griddle, spreading to about 4' in diameter.  Cook over medium-low
heat 3 - 4 minutes on each side.
Serve with cranberry sauce if desired.
