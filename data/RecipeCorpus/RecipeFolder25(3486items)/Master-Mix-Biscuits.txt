Title: Master Mix Biscuits
Categories: Bread
Yield: 2 servings
3/4 c  Water
3 c  Master mix
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Mix ingredients vigorously until all dry
ingredients are dampened. Turn out onto lightly floured surface and
knead gently 20 seconds. Pat to a thickness of 1/2-inch. Cut with
knife or cutter. Bake 12-16 minutes until golden brown.
