Title: Lemon Pasta Salad with Peppercorn-Coconut Glaze
Categories: Salad, Appetizer, Pasta
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Coconut, grated
2 c  Slivered snow peas
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Cilantro, chopped
1/4 c  Basil, chopped
14 oz Udon noodles
2 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Lemon zest
1 ts Coarsely ground black pepper
1/2 c  Vegetable stock
Cook udon noodles in salted water according to package instructions,
till tender but firm.  Drain.  Heat 1 tb oil in skillet, add lemon
juice, zest, pepper, stock & coconut.  Bring to a boil.  Cover & cook
over moderate heat for 2 minutes.  Add snow peas & continue to cook
for 1 minute. Remove pan from heat, add drained noodles & stir to
blend. Season with salt. When cooled, add herbs & mix.  Drizzle with
remaining oil & garnish with cilantro.

Yamuna Devi, "Yamuna's Table"
