Title: Spicy Ham Loaf
Categories: Main dish, Pork, Sthrn/livng, Meatloaf
Yield: 6 servings
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Apple juice
1 tb Onion, minced
1/2 ts Mustard; dry
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Pepper
3/4 c  Syrup, dark corn
2 ts Mustard, dry
1/3 c  Vinegar
3 c  Ham; ground (about 1-1/2lbs)
2 c  Pork; ground (1 lb.)
1 c  Breadcrumbs, fine dry
Combine corn syrup, 2 teaspoon dry mustard, and vinegar in a small
saucepan; bring to a boil.  Boil 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove syrup from heat, and set aside.

Combine remaining ingredients; mix well.  Place meat mixture in a
greased 13x9x2" baking pan, and shape into 12x4" loaf.  Bake at 300
degrees for 1-1/2 to 2 hours, basting every 15 minutes with syrup
mixture.
