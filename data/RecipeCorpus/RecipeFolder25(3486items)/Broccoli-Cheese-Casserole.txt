Title: Broccoli Cheese Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Vegetable, Casserole, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Soup can of milk
1/4 lb Velveta cheese
1 ea Can french fried onion rings
1 pk Broccoli
-frozen
-or one bunch
1 ea Can cream of mushroom soup
For fresh broccoli remove stems and place in boiling water for 3
minutes. Just thaw frozen broccoli. Place broccoli in casserole. Add
soup, milk, cheese and half of onion rings. Stir gently to prevent
busting broccoli buds. Bake for 20 minutes at 350 degrees. Add
remaining onion rings on top of dish and bake for 10 more minutes.
