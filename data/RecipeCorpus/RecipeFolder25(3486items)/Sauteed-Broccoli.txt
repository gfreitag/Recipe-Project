Title: Sauteed Broccoli
Categories: Side dish, Italian, Vegetarian, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
2    Garlic;minced;cloves
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 lb Broccoli;fresh
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Peanut Oil
Wash broccoli. Split ends of large stalks length wise into halves
or quarters, depending on size.
Place in large skillet. Sprinkle with water, peanut oil, garlic,
salt and pepper.
Cover tightly; cook over very low heat (15 to 20 minutes), or until
stalks are tender. Turn broccoli several times during cooking. Serve
with melted butter.
