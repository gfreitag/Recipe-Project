Title: A Taste of Thai Layered Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
2 T  EACH finely chopped green
-onions and peanuts
1 t  Ground ginger
Juice and rind of one lime
4 c  EACH blanched broccoli and
-cauliflower
3 c  Bean sprouts, divided
2 ea Green onions, thinly sliced
2 lg Carrots, sliced diagonally
In large bowl, layer broccoli, half of the bean sprouts, cauliflower,
remaining bean sprouts, and green onions.  Arrange the sliced carrots
around the edge of the salad.  Chill for 2 hours.  Mix the remaining
ingredients.  Just before serving, pour the dressing over the top of
the salad, avoiding the carrots.  Sprinkle with additional chopped
green onions and peanuts as garnish. Origin: KRAFT What's Cooking
Magazine, 10th issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
