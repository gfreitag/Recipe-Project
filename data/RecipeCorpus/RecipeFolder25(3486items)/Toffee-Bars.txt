Title: Toffee Bars
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Sifted all purpose flour
6 oz Semi sweet chocolate chips
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1 c  Butter
1 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Thoroughly cream 1 cup of butter, 1 cup of brown sugar and 1 tsp. of
vanilla.  Add 2 cups of sifted all purpose flour; mix well.  Stir in
one 6 oz. package of semi sweet chips and 1 cup of chopped walnuts.
Pat into an ungreased 15 1/2 x 10 1/2 x 1 inch pan.  Bake at 350
degrees for 15 to 18 minutes.  While warm, cut in bars.  Cool. Randy
