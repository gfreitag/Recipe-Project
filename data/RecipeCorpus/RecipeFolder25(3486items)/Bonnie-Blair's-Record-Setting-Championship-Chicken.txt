Title: Bonnie Blair's Record-Setting Championship Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 t  Dried tarragon (opt)
4    Chicken breast halves*
1/2 c  MIRACLE WHIP Salad Dressing
2 T  Dijon mustard
1 T  Honey
* - boneless, skinless (about 1 1/4 lbs.)

1. Mix salad dressing, mustard, honey and tarragon.

2. Place chicken breasts on grill or rack of broiler pan; brush with
half of salad dressing mixture.

3. Grill or broil for 8 minutes. Turn; brush with remaining salad
dressing mixture. Continue broiling or grilling 8-10 minutes or until
tender. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   10-12-94
