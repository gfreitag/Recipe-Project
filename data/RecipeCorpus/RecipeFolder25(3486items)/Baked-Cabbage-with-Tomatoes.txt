Title: Baked Cabbage with Tomatoes
Categories: Side dish, Moroccan, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Cumin
1 md Cabbage
3 lg Tomatoes, peeled & chopped
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 sm Onion, chopped
Preheat oven to 350F.

Quarter the cabbage & boil it for 5 minutes.  Drain & put the pieces
into a baking dish.  Combine the remaining ingredients, pour them
over the cabbage & bake for 20 minutes.
