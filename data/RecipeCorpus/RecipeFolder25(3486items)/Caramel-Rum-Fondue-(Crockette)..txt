Title: Caramel Rum Fondue (Crockette).
Categories: Dessert, Dip, Crockpot
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Marshmallows, miniature
1 tb Rum, or rum extract 1/2 ts.
1 pk Caramels (14 oz.)
2/3 c  Cream
Combine caramels and cream in crockette. Cover and cook until melted,
2 to 3 hours. Stir in marshmallows and rum. Cook another 1 to 1 1/2
hours. Serve with apple wedges, pound cake, angel food cake,
doughnuts, marshmallows. Submitted By JR BYERS   On   12-11-94
