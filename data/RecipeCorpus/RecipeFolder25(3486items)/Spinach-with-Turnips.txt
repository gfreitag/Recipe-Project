Title: Spinach with Turnips
Categories: Indian, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Yogurt
2 lb Fresh spinach
1 1/4 lb White turnips, pared and
Sliced 1/4-inch thick
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1    Tomato, diced
3 tb Butter or margarine
1 c  Finely chopped onion
2 ts Crushed coriander seed
1/2 ts Ground turmeric
1/8 ts Cayenne
1 ts Salt
1. In hot butter in large saucepan, saute onion until golden-about 5
minutes.
2. Add spices, salt, and yogurt; cook, over low heat and
stirring, 8 to 10 minutes.
3. Meanwhile, wash spinach. Remove stems, and chop leaves coarsely.
4. Add half of spinach to spice mixture, along with turnips, garlic,
and
tomato.
5. Cook, covered, over medium heat, 3 minutes. Stir in rest of
spinach and 1/2 cup water; cook, covered, 15 to 20 minutes, or
until
turnips are tender. SERVES 4 OR 6.
