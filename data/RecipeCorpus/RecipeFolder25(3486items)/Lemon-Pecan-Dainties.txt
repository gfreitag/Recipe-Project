Title: Lemon Pecan Dainties
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Sthrn/livng, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 dozen
2 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Baking powder
pn Salt
1 c  Pecans; chopped
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg; beaten
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Lemon rind; grated
Cream shortening; gradually add sugar, beating until light and
fluffy. Add eggs, lemon juice, and grated lemon rind; beat well.

Combine flour, baking powder, and salt; add to creamed mixture,
beating just until blended.  Stir in pecans.

Shape dough into a long roll, 2 inches in diameter; wrap in waxed
paper, and chill 2 to 3 hours or until firm.

Unwrap roll, and cut into 1/4" slices; place on lightly greased cookie
sheets.  Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.
