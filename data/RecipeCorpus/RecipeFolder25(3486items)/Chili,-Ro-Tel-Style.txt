Title: Chili, Ro-Tel Style
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Chili
Yield: 8 servings
15 oz Tomato puree
8 oz Tomato sauce
1 pk Chili seasoning
2 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion, chopped
1    Green pepper, chopped
2 cn 10 oz. ROTEL
Cook beef, onion and pepper in a large stock pot until meat is
browned. Drain excess drippings. Add remaining ingredients; cover and
simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
