Title: Orange-Pecan Couscous
Categories: Side dish, Pasta, Fruit, Low-cal/fat, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 servings
1 pn Turmeric
1 pn Ground red pepper
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Couscous
1/2 c  Raisins (more if desired)
1 tb Oil
1 c  Coarsely chopped pecans
1    Onion; chopped
2 c  Orange juice
3    Cinnamon sticks
5    Cloves, whole
Heat oil in a large skillet and cook pecans until lightly toasted. Add
onions and cook until soft.  Add orange juice, cinnamon sticks,
cloves, turmeric, red pepper and salt and bring to a boil.  Quickly
stir in couscous and raisins, then cover and turn off heat.  Let
stand 5 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed completely.  Fluff with
a fork before serving.
