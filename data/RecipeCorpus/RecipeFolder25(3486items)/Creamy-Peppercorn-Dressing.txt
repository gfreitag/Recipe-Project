Title: Creamy Peppercorn Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Garlic powder
1 t  Onion powder
1/2 t  Cayenne pepper
3/4 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 t  Freshly ground black pepper
Combine all ingredients in a medium-size mixing bowl. Mix well. Cover
and refrigerate until ready to use. Yield 1 1/2 cups. Source: More
Cajun Cooking, Modern Publishing, New York, NY Copyright 1990 SHARED
BY: Jim Bodle 1/93
