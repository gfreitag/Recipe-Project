Title: Tomato Sauce for Fish
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetable, Tomato, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 3 1/2 cups
1/2 c  Shrimp; cooked or canned
1/2 c  Mushrooms, sliced; canned
1/4 c  Celery; diced
2 c  Tomato sauce
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Parsley, fresh; chopped
1/4 c  Olives, stuffed; chopped
Combine all ingredients in saucepan and heat to the boiling point.
Taste and add more seasoning, if desired.  Serve this sauce with
baked or broiled fish.  It is best served from a platter that can be
placed under broiler for just a few minutes.
