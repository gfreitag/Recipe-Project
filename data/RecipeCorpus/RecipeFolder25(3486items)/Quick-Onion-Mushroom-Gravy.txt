Title: Quick Onion-Mushroom Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng, Mushroom
Yield: 1 cup
2 tb Flour, all-purpose
1 c  Broth, beef
6 oz Mushrooms, sliced
1 tb Onion, instant minced
1 tb Butter (or marg.); melted
Drain liquid from mushrooms into a small mixing bowl; add instant
minced onion and set aside.

Combine butter and mushrooms in a saucepan; cook over low heat until
mushrooms are golden.  Gradually stir in onion and beef broth; cook
until slightly thickened.  Serve hot over meat.
