Title: Homemade Clotted Cream
Categories: Condiment, Misc/food
Yield: 2 cups
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 1/2 c  Pasteurized heavy cream
-NOT ultra-pasteurized
Combine cream and buttermilk in jar or measuring cup. Stir, cover,
and let stand at room temperature until mixture has thickened to the
consistency of softly whipped cream, 12 to 24 hours. Refrigerate;
cream will continue to thicken as it chills. Clotted cream can be
kept refrigerated and covered for up to 10 days.
