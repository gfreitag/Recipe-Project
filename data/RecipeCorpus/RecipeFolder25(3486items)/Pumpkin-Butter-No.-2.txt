Title: Pumpkin Butter No. 2
Categories: Pumpkin, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 t  Ground cinnamon
16 oz Can solid-pack pumpkin  2/3
-cup firmly packed brown
-sugar
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1/8 t  Ground cloves
1 t  Lemon juice
In medium saucepan, combine pumpkin, brown sugar, honey, lemon juice,
cinnamon, and cloves.  Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring
frequently. Reduce heat; simmer 20 minutes, or until thickened,
stirring occasionally. Ladle mixture into clean jars or freezer
containers. Cover with lids. Store in refrigerator several weeks or
freeze for several months. Yield: 2 cups.
