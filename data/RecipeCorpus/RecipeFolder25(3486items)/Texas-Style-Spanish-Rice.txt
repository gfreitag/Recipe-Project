Title: Texas-Style Spanish Rice
Categories: Rice, Tomato, Main dish, Tex/mexican, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Cumin, ground
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Garlic powder
2 c  Water
2 1/2 tb Cilantro, fresh, chopped
(optional)
1/4 c  Chopped Onion 1/4 C Chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper, chopped
2 tb Cooking oil
1 c  Rice, long grain, uncooked
1/2 c  Tomatoes with green chilies
1/4 ts Tumeric, ground
In a skillet, saute onion and green pepper in oil for about 2
minutes. Add rice and stir until coated with oil. Add tomatoes,
turmeric, cumin, salt, garlic powder and water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer, covered, about 20 minutes or until liquid is
absorbed. Add cilantro if desired. Yield: 6 servings.

SOURCE: *Melissa Pride, Plano, TX, Country Magazine, Feb./Mar.93
