Title: Sole Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Mushroom, Casserole
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Flour
-Salt and pepper
-Lemon juice to taste
1/4 c  Bread crumbs
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
2 c  Milk
2 1/2 lb Sole fillets
2 1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms
10 tb Butter
2 c  Seedless green grapes
Preheat oven to 375. Heat milk in large fry pan. When just below
boiling, put fish in, Cook over low heat 5 miinutes; drain, reserving
liquid. Saute mushrooms in 1/4 cup butter 3 minutes. Transfer
mushrooms to lightly buttered casserole. Add grapes to casserole, lay
fish on top. Melt butter in saucepan, stir in flour. Add fish liquid
and cook, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens. Season with
salt, pepper, and lemon juice; pour over fish. Combine bread crumbs
and grated cheese together, sprinkle over fish. Dot with remaining
