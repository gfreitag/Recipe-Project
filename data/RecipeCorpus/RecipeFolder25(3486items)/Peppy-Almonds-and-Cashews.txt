Title: Peppy Almonds and Cashews
Categories: Nuts/grains, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Margarine or butter
2 t  Worcestershire sauce
Cayenne pepper
2 c  Mixed nuts (almonds,
-cashews, or combo)
2 T  Sugar
In two quart casserole, mix the sugar, margarine and worcestershire
sauce. Melt and then mix in the nuts. Microwave 2 minutes on high.
Stir to coat. Microwave 2 more minutes.  Stir.  Microwave 2 more
minutes. Place on cookie sheet, or tin foil to cool.  Sprinkle
cayenne over top, mix. Origin: Adapted from recipe Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Dec/94.
