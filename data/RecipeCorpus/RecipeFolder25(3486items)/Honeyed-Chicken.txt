Title: Honeyed Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Jewish, Chicken, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Peanut Oil
1/4 c  Honey
1    Salt;pinch
1 lb Chicken;cut-up
1/2    Lemon;
2 ts Ginger;ground
Rub the chicken pieces with lemon. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the ginger
and salt. Combine oil, honey, and the remaining ginger.
Brush chicken with honey mixture. Roast in oven (350 F) 1 1/2 to 2
hours, basting occasionally with the honey mixture.
