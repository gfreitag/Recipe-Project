Title: Pineapple Delight
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
Few drops almond extract
1/2 c  Whipped cream stiff
1 c  Shredded pineapple
Sliced almonds (optional)
Cherries to decorate
1 1/2 c  Vanilla or graham crumbs
1/2 c  Butter, melted
1 pk Unflavoured geletin
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Water
Blend crumbs with melted butter.  Pack in dish and chill. Blend 1 pk.
geletin in 3 tbsp. water.  Mix sugar and 1/3 c. water. Cook to soft
ball stage. Beat 1 egg white with a pinch of salt until fluffy.
Slowly add syrup beating constantly.  Add geletin mixture.  A few
drops of almond extract. Add stiffly beaten whipped cream and
pineapple. Spread over crumb base. Allow to set in refrigerator for 4
hours minimum. DELICIOUS!!! Submitted By FLORENCE THOMPSON   On
12-05-94
