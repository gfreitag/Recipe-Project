Title: Celery Fingers
Categories: Appetizer, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 12 fingers
1/16 ts Pepper, black
1/4 ts Paprika
4 sl Bread; thin
Butter (or marg.)
1 c  Celery; minced
1 tb Nuts; finely chopped
1 tb Olives; finely chopped
1 tb Mayonnaise
1/8 ts Salt
Combine first 7 ingredients.  Mix well.  Remove crust from bread.
Spread bread thinly with butter and then with celery mixture.  Cut
into 1-inch strips.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
