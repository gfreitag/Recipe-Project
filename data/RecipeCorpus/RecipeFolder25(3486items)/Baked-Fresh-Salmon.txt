Title: Baked Fresh Salmon
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
3 ea Onion slices
1 ea Garlic clove sliced in qtrs.
2 ea Cucumber peeled, strip cut
3 lb Salmon piece
1 t  Pepper
3 t  Butter
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
3 ea Parsley sprigs
1 ea Bayleaf
1 t  Salt
1/2 t  Thyme
1 1/2 c  Cream light
Combine salt, pepper and thyme then rub all sides of the salmon. Melt
butter in baking dish then add salmon and coat with the butter. Add
light cream, onion slices, parsley, garlic, bayleaf then arrange
cucumber strips around the fish. Bake covered for 40 minutes or until
center bone can be removed easily. Remove and discard bayleaf, onion,
parsley and garlic before serving.
