Title: Peanut Butter Balls No. 2
Categories: Candy, Nuts/grains, Chocolate
Yield: 20 servings
4 tb Melted butter
1 ts Vanilla
8 oz Semi-sweet chocolate
1 lb Dates
1 lb Peanut butter
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
Mix ingredients.  Shape into balls 1" round.  Chill overnite.  Melt
chocola te in double boiler.  Add 1/2 bar parafin wax.  Dip balls in
chocolate. Cool on waxed paper. Makes 100 balls.
