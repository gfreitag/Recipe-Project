Title: Pasta with Clam Sauce
Categories: Main dish, Italian, Pasta, Salsa/sauce, Clam/oyster
Yield: 1 servings
2    Garlic;minced;cloves
2 tb Parsley;chopped
4 c  Spaghetti;cooked
2 oz Clams;cans
1 tb Olive Oil
2 tb Butter
Combine all ingredients except spaghetti in a sauce pan. Simmer 5
minutes. Serve over cooked spaghetti.
