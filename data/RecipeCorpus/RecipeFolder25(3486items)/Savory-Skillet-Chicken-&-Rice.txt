Title: Savory Skillet Chicken & Rice
Categories: Chicken, Rice, Beans, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Milk
1 tb Onion flakes
1/4 ts Dried thyme leaves; crushed
1/8 ts Pepper
2 c  Frozen cut green beans
2 c  Uncooked instant rice
1 tb Margarine or butter
1 lb Chicken breasts; cut up
-skinless and boneless
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
-Campbell's condensed
-(10-3/4-oz can)
1. In skillet over medium-high heat, heat margarine.  Add chicken and
cook until browned, stirring often.  Set chicken aside.

2. Add soup, milk, onion, thyme, pepper and beans.  Heat to a boil.
Return chicken to pan.  Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or
until chicken is done.

3. Add rice.  Cover and remove from heat.  Let stand 5 minutes.
Fluff with a fork.

Serves 4

Prep time: 5 min. Cook time: 15 min. Stand Time: 5 min.

From a Campbell's advertisement, USA Weekend magazine, November 18-20,
1994.  APB
