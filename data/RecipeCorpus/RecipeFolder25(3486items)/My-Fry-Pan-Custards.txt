Title: My Fry Pan Custards
Categories: Dessert, Pudding, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM---------------------------
1 1/2 c  Milk
4 tb Sugar
Nutmeg
2    Eggs
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/8 ts Salt
Beat eggs and add salt, sugar, vanilla, and milk. Strain into custard
cups and sprinkle with grated nutmeg.(fresh is best). Cover each cup
with foil and place in electric fry pan in 3/4 inch water. Bring
water to boil and simmer at 250 about 10-12 minutes. (This is
dependant on accuracy of thermostat on fry pay). Test with a knife
and remove custards when it comes out clean.
