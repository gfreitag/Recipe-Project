Title: Crab and Cheese Bites
Categories: Diabetic, Cheese/eggs, Seafood, Microwave
Yield: 36 servings
1 t  Lemon juice
1/4 t  Curry powder
1/8 t  Pepper
36    Melba toast rounds
8 oz Crab meat
1 t  Sliced green onion
4 oz Shredded swiss cheese
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
Combine first seven ingredients and mix well. Spoon crab mixture on
toast rounds. Place half rounds on a microwave tray lined with paper
towels. Heat on 70% power 20-30 seconds or until mixture is warm and
cheese is melted. Repeat with other half of appetizers. These are
good on the onion or sesame melba rounds.

36 appetizers 1 appetizer = 1/4 fat-meat exchange
1/2 fat exchange
1/5 bread exchange
55 calories
3 g carbohydrate
3 g protien
3 g fat
