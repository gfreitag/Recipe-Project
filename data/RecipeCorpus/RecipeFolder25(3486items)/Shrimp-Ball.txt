Title: Shrimp Ball
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Appetizer, Snack, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 8 servings
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Horseradish
1/2 c  Finely chopped parsley
1/2 lb Cooked small shrimp,
-coarsley chopped
2 tb Minced onion
1/4 ts Salt
Mix shrimp, onion, salt, cream cheese, lemon juice, and horseradish
together well. Divide in half and shape into balls. Roll in parsley.
Cover with plastic wrap until ready to use. Serve with crackers
