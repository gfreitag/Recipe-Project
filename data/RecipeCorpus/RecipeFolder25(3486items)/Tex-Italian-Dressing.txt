Title: Tex-Italian Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Bottled Italian dressing
1/2 c  Pace Picante Sauce
Combine Pace Picante Sauce and bottled Italian dressing. Toss with
pasta salads or green salads or use as a marinade for bean salads or
mixed vegetable salads. Makes about 3/4 cup.
