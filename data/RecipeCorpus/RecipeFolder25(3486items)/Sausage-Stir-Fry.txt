Title: Sausage Stir Fry
Categories: Sausage, Stir fry, Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1    Red pepper thinly sliced
1    Green pepper thinly sliced
1 lb Mushrooms thinly sliced
1/2 lb Snow pea pods
1 c  Sweet & sour dressing
1 tb Soy sauce
2 c  Rice, uncooked
1 lb Hillshire Farm Beef Hot
-Links or any variety
-Hillshire Farm Smoked
-Sausage
4    Carrots peeled/sliced
1 sm Bunch bok choy chopped
-(or substitute)
Chop and slice all vegetables. Set aside. Cook rice according to
instructions. Saute Beef Hot Links in sweet & sour dressing and soy
sauce. Add vegetables.
