Title: Deviled Potato Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng, Potato, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
6 md Potatoes; cooked, peeled,
-and cubed (4-1/2 cups)
1 c  Celery; chopped
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
2 tb Pepper, green; chopped
2 tb Pimiento; chopped
Tomato wedges
Cucumber slices
8    Eggs; hard-cooked
2 tb Vinegar
1 tb Horseradish, prepared
2 1/2 tb Mustard, prepared
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Sour cream
1/2 ts Celery salt
1 ts Salt
Cut eggs in half and remove yolks.  Mash and blend yolks with vinegar,
horseradish, and mustard.  Add mayonnaise, sour cream, celery salt,
and salt; mix well.  Chop egg whites and combine wiht potatoes and
chopped vegetables.  Fold in egg yolk mixture; chill.  Garnish with
tomatoes and cucumbers.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
