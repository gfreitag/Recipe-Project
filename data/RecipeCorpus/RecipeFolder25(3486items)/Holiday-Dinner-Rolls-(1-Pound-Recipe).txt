Title: Holiday Dinner Rolls (1 Pound Recipe)
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker, Holiday
Yield: 8 rolls
3/4 t  Salt
2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 t  FLEISCHMANN'S Bread Machine
-Yeast
1/4 c  Milk (70-80'F.)
2 tb Water (70-80'F.)
2 tb Butter or margarine, cut up
1 lg Egg
4 t  Sugar
MMMMM-------------------------EGG GLAZE------------------------------
1 tb Water
1    Egg
ADD all ingredients except Egg Glaze into bread machine pan in the
order suggested by manufacturer.

PROCESS in dough/manual cycle. When cycle is complete, remove dough to
lightly floured surface. If necessary, knead in enough flour to make
dough easy to handle. Divide dough into 8 equal pieces. Divide each
into 3 equal pieces; roll into balls, making 24 balls. Place 3 balls
in each section of 8 greased 2 1/2" muffin pan cups. cover; let rise
in warm, draft-free place until doubled in size, about 30-45 minutes.
Brush Egg Glaze on rolls. Bake at 375'F. for 15 minutes or until
done. Remove from cups; cool on wire rack.

EGG GLAZE: Lightly beat 1 egg with 1 tablespoon water.
