Title: Garlic & Herb Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Oil
1 cn Hunt's Ready Sauce Chunky
-Garlic & Herb
Salt
Pepper
1    Whole cut-up chicken
Flour
Simply salt & pepper whole cut-up chicken. Coat lightly with flour.
Brown in skillet in oil. Pour Hunt's Ready Sauce Chunky Garlic & Herb
over chicken. Simmer, covered, 20 minutes or until cooked through.
