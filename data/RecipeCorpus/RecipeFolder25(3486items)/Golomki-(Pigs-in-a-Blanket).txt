Title: Golomki (Pigs in a Blanket)
Categories: Main dish, Ground beef, Vegetable, Rice
Yield: 4 servings
1    Cabbage
1 cn Plum Tomatoes
1 ts Sugar
1 pn Ground Allspice
1 ts Salt
1 lb Ground beef or Meat Loaf
1 c  Cooked rice
1    Onion
2    Parsley Sprig
1/2    Celery Stalk
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
Prepare cooked rice. Finely chop the onion, parsley and
:
celery. Combine, add Worcestershire and saute. Add salt & pepper to
taste. Knead with with meat and rice to form "pigs".
Remove and discard the core from a head of cabbage. Boil the
:
cabbage while carefully removing the leaves as they wilt from the
head. These are the "blankets".
Wrap the pigs in the blankets and place in a roasting pan.
:
Squash the plum tomatoes and add salt, sugar and allspice. Spread
mixture over and around pigs. Bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour
turning when the leaves begin turning brown and wilting.
ENJOY!
