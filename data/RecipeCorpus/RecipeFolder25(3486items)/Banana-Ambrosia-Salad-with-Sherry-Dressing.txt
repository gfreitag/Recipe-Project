Title: Banana Ambrosia Salad with Sherry Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing, Fruit, Vegetable, Caribbean
Yield: 6 servings
2    Bananas, such as manzano,
-burro or red, peeled and
-sliced
1 lg Orange, peeled, halved and
-sliced
Torn red leaf or radiccio
-lettuce
Torn Romaine lettuce
3/4 c  Finely chopped dried
-starfruit
1    Pear, thinly sliced
MMMMM----------------------SHERRY DRESSING---------------------------
1/4 t  Ground allspice
2 t  Shredded fresh ginger root
1/4 c  Salad oil
1/3 c  Dry sherry or vinegar
1 t  Thinly sliced orange peel
Arrange lettuce on a large serving platter or four salad plates. On
top of lettuce arrange fruit (dip banana slices in lemon juice to
prevent darkening). Place dressing ingredients in a shaker jar.
Cover, shake well, drizzle over salad. Makes 5-6 servings.
