Title: Golden Egg Bread (Abm)
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 loaf
6 tb Corn oil
1 1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Warm water
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
4 tb Sugar
2    Eggs
All ingredients except the water should be at room temperature before
starting.

Add the ingredients to the pan according to the manufacturer's
instructions.

Select "White Bread"; Press "start".
