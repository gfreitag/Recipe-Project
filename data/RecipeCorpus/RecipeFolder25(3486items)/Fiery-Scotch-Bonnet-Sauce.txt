Title: Fiery Scotch Bonnet Sauce
Categories: Chilies, Salsa/sauce, Tomato
Yield: 2 cups
2    Scotch bonnet chiles, seeded
-and minced
1 ts Black pepper, ground
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2    Garlic cloves, chopped
1 lg Onion, chopped
4 lg Tomatoes, peeled and seeded
1 1/2 ts Oregano
Heat the oil in a skillet.  Add the garlic and onion and saute until
soft. Add the remaining ingredients except the vinegar.  Cook for
about 20 minutes, until thick.  Add the vinegar.  Cook for 5 minutes
longer. Pour into a sterilized jar and store in the refrigerator. Use
within 2 to 3 weeks.

Makes about 1 1/2 cups.

Source: "The West Indies Cookbook - Classic Recipes from the Spicy
Caribbean" by Connie and Arnold Krochmal, 1992.  Foreword by Dave
DeWitt. Border Books, Alburquerque, New Mexico.
