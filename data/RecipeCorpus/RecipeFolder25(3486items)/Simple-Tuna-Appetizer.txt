Title: Simple Tuna Appetizer
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Chopped onion
-Rice crackers
6 1/2 oz Can tuna fish, drained
1 ts Soy sauce
In bowl, mix tuna with soy sauce and onion. Spread on rice crackers or
crackers of your choice and serve at once.
