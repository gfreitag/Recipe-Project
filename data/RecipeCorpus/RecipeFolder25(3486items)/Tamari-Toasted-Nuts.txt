Title: Tamari-Toasted Nuts
Categories: Snack, Salad, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 batch
1 tb Tamari
1/2 c  Raw nuts
For this recipe, you may use walnuts, cashews, almonds, pecans or
peanuts.

Over medium heat, toast the nuts in a large dry skillet, stirring
often, for about 3 to 5 minutes.  Remove from heat and quickly stir
in tamari until nuts are coated, then immediately transfer nuts to
serving plate. Allow to cool completely before serving.
