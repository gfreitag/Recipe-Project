Title: Baked Stuffed Pork Chops
Categories: Main dish, Pork, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
6    Pork chops; thick, cut with
-pockets
2 tb Shortening
1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Stuffing, herb-seasoned
2 tb Parsley; chopped
Heat water, add butter, and stir until butter melts.  Add stuffing and
parsley, tossing with fork until crumbs are thoroughly moistened. Fill
pockets of chops with stuffing; secure with toothpicks.  Brown chops
on both sides in melted shortening.  Arrange in baking dish. Stir 1/4
cup water into skillet drippings; pour over chops.  Cover. Bake at
350 degrees for 45 minutes.  Uncover; continue baking 15 minutes more
or until chops are tender.
