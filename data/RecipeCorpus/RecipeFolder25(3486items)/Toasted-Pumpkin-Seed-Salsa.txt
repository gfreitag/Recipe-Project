Title: Toasted-Pumpkin-Seed Salsa
Categories: Pumpkin, Salsa/sauce, Vegetable
Yield: 1 batch
1/4 c  Chopped cilantro leaves
1 ts Ground cumin
1 1/2 ts Coarse ground black pepper
1 tb Salt
2 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Whole shelled pumpkin seeds
3 md Tomatoes; cored
1 sm Onion; peeled and cored
1 md Poblano chile
1 md Jalapeno
1 tb Lime juice
Toast pumpkin seeds in shallow pan in 350 F. oven for 5 minutes. Over
gas flame or on hot grill, char tomatoes, onion and chiles. Seed and
stem chiles.  Place in bowl and break up with hands or spoon. Mix in
remaining ingredients, then roughly puree in food processor or
blender to a chunky consistency.  Serve warm.

Yield: About 3 cups.

From Executive Chef Jim Severson/Dakota's/600 N. Akard/Dallas, TX in
"Texas Monthly."  July 1993, Vol. 21, No. 7.  Pg. 136.  Typed for you
