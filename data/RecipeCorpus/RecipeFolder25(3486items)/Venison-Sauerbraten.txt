Title: Venison Sauerbraten
Categories: German, Marinade, Game, Main dish, Stove top
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2 ea Bay leaves
2 T  Margarine
2 t  Sugar
3-4 lb venison roast
1 c  Wine vinegar
1 t  Salt
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
2 ea Whole cloves
2 T  Flour
1/4 c  Water
1 c  Water
2 md Onions, chopped
6 ea Peppercorns

Bring MARINADE ingredients to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat
and simmer for 15 minutes. Cool marinade to room temperature. Place
meat in large glass bowl. Pour marinade over meat and refrigerate
three days. Turn meat at least 4 times each day. Remove roast from
marinade, dry and brown in margarine in heavy skillet. Place meat in
roaster, add marinade liquid, cover and roast in 325 degree oven
until tender (3-4 hours). Remove roast from juices and keep warm.
