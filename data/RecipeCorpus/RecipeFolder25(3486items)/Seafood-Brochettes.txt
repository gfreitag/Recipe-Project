Title: Seafood Brochettes
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Crab/shrimp, Clam/oyster
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  White Wine
1 c  Lime Juice
1/8 ts Tabasco Sauce
1/4 c  Olive Oil
1 tb Cilantro;chopped
12    Prawns;shelled;large
12    Sea Scallops;large
1 lb Swordfish;1' chunks
8    Pearl Onion;parboiled;
1    Bell Pepper;seeded;
8    Cherry Tomato;
Remove veins from prawns. Combine last 5 ingredients for wine
marinade. Add seafood to marinade and refrigerate 1 hour.
Cut bell pepper into 1 inch chunks. Thread seafood and vegetables
onto skewers and barbecue or broil until done, basting occasionally
with marinade. Serve over steamed rice.
