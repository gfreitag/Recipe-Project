Cut bananas into 1/4 inch thick slices.  Divide bananas, marshmallows,
pineapple and chocolate chips among four 12 inch squares double
thickness foil.  Shape foil to make cups but do not enclose
ingredients completely. Add pineapple juice.  Place on barbcue grill.
Grill over medium heat 15 minutes or until marshmallows begin to
brown and chocolate chips have melted.  Serve at once. Per serving:
224 calories, 8 g fat, moderate fibre. Origin: Homemaker's Recipe
Collection of the Year, Sizzling Barbecues. Shared by: Sharon Stevens
