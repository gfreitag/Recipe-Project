Title: Honey Nut Rice Mix with Recipe
Categories: Nuts/grains, Mixes, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
-RICE:
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Honey
2 c  Water
1 pk Rice mix
1 c  Long grain rice
1/2 c  Roasted peanuts
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 ts Ground ginger
1    Chicken bouillon cube,
-crumbled
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Honey Nut Rice: Boil butter, honey and water. Reduce heat, add rice
mix, cover and simmer 20 minutes. 6 servings,
