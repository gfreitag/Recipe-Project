Title: Picadillo
Categories: Game, Pork, Potato, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Ground cummin
4 c  Water
1 c  Chopped Onion
1/2 c  Celery
1/2 c  Bell pepper
4 c  Potatoes, cut into cubes
2 c  Frozen corn kernels
1 lb Ground venison
1 lb Ground beef or pork
3 tb Flour
6 oz Tomato paste
2 tb Salt Or To Taste
1 tb Black pepper
1 1/2 tb Granulated garlic
Instructions:

1.  Place venison and beef or pork in a large deep pot or Dutch oven
over medium heat.  Cook until meat is brown; drain any excess fat.

2.  Stir in flour, tomato paste, salt, pepper, garlic and ground
cumin to coat meat evenly.  Add water and vegetables.  Bring liquid
to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for about 45 minutes.  Serve over
rice, it desired.
