Title: Pu Pu Shrimp
Categories: Appetizer, Asian, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Flour
2 c  Tempura Batter
Peanut Oil;
24    Prawns;raw deveined;whole
2 c  Coconut;shredded
1 1/2 c  Water
2    Egg;beaten;
Place beaten eggs in a medium bowl.
Butterfly shrimp.
Put flour and coconut in separate dishes.
Blend tempura mix and water in a large bowl.
Dust shrimp in the flour, then dip in egg, then the tempura.
Dredge in the coconut and place on a buttered baking dish. Bake at
350F for 35-40 minutes, or until shrimp are done.
