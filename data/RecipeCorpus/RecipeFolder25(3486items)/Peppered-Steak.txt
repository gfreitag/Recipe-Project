Title: Peppered Steak
Categories: Beef, Mixes, Herbs/spice, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
5 tb Butter
1/2 c  Beef broth
1/4 c  Cognac -OR-
1/4 c  Dark rum
1 1/2 tb Ground Pepper mill combo mix
OR-
1 1/2 tb Caribbean Pepper mix
4    Rib eye steaks, 3/4" thick
