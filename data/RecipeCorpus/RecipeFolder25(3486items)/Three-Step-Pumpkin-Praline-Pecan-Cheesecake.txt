Title: Three-Step Pumpkin Praline Pecan Cheesecake
Categories: Pie/tart, Cheese/eggs, Pumpkin, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
Dash Ground Nutmeg
1    Ready to use Graham Cracker
Crumb crust (6oz or 9-inch)
3/4 c  Chopped Pecans
2 T  Maple-flavored syrup
2 pk Cream Cheese (16oz)softened
1/2 c  Pumpkin
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed firmly
1/2 t  Vanilla
1/2 t  Ground Cinnamon
Dash Ground Cloves
Mix cream cheese, Pumpkin, brown sugar, vanilla and spices at medium
speed with electric mixer until well blended.  Add Eggs; mix until
blended.

Pour into crust.  Sprinkle with pecans.

Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until center is almost set.
Cool. Refrigerate 3 hours or overnight.  Drizzle with syrup just
before serving. Makes 8 servings.
