Title: Cranberry Sauce with Raspberry Vinegar
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Side dish, Holiday, Fruit
Yield: 1 batch
1    Cinnamon stick
1 tb Thin orange peel strips
-- (orange part only)
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Raspberry vinegar
1/4 c  Water
12 oz Cranberries
In a heavy medium saucepan set over medium-high heat, bring first 3
ingredients to a boil, stirring to dissolve sugar.  Add cranberries,
cinnamon stick and orange peel.  Reduce heat, cover partially, and
simmer until berries start to pop, about 10 minutes.  Remove from
heat. Cool completely.  Discard cinnamon stick.

May be stored (refrigerated) up to 1 week before serving.
