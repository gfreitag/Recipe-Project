Title: Date & Nut Muffin Mix with Recipe
Categories: Fruit, Mixes, Nuts/grains, Muffin
Yield: 1 servings
-MUFFINS:
1 pk Mix
1    Egg
3/4 c  Milk
2/3 c  Chopped dates
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 c  Self rising flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
Mix: Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.

Date and Nut Muffins: Preheat oven to 400 and grease 12 muffin cups.
Combine all ingredients, mixing just until moistened. Pour into muffin
cups, filling them 3/4 full. Bake 15-18 minutes, until goldenbrown.
