Title: Marinated Havarti
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Whole green olives
1 c  Herb salad dressing
1/2 lb Havarti cheese, cubed
1 ea Red pepper, cubed
1 ea Yellow pepper, cubed
Cut the cheese and peppers.  Add the green olives and toss to mix
well. Heat the salad dressing and pour over top.  Toss to coat.  Can
be stored in fridge for 4-5 days.  Allow to sit out at room
temperature to an hour before serving to allow flavours to develop.
Origin: KRAFT What's Cooking Magazine, 10th issue. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Dec/94.
