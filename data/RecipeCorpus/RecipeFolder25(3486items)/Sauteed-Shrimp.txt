Title: Sauteed Shrimp
Categories: Main dish, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Parsley;chopped
1/2 ts Dill Weed;dried
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 1/2 lb Shrimp;raw peeled
6 tb Olive Oil
3    Garlic;chopped;cloves
2 tb Lemon Zest;shredded
Heat oil in a large frying pan; add shrimp, garlic and lemon zest
and saute 3 minutes over high heat, until shrimp is just done.
Add all other ingredients, toss together and remove from heat.
Serve over rice.
