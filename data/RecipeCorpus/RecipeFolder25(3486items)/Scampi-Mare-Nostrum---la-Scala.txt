Title: Scampi Mare Nostrum - la Scala
Categories: Main dish, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 6 servings
Juice of one lemon
1/2 c  Fish stock
2 lg Shallots
-chopped
2 ea Tb Chopped sweet basil
2 ea Tb Chopped fresh oregano
24 lg Prawns
-peeled, deveined, and split
-lengthwise
Salt & pepper
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  White wine
Arrange the prawns butterfly fashion in a baking dish.  Season with
slat and pepper.  combine oil, wine, lemon juice, fish stock,
shallots, basil and oregano.  Poru over prawns, turning to coat well.
Marinate for 1 hour. When ready to cook, remove prawns from marinade,
shaking off excess. Place under broiler and broil for 10 min. or
until golden. DO NOT OVER COOK. Source: California Cuisine ISBN
