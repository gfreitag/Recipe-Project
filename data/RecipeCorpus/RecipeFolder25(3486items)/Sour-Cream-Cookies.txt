Title: Sour Cream Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 18 cookies
2 c  Nuts, chopped
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Nutmeg
1/2 t  Baking soda
2 t  Baking powder
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sour cream
1    Egg
2 c  Flour
Cream shortening and sugar. Add beaten egg and nuts. Sift dry
ingredients and add to mixture alternately with cream mixture.  Drop
by spoonfuls on greased cookie sheet. Bake in moderate oven until
lightly browned. Submitted By ROBERT WHITE   On   11-16-94
