Title: Zucchini and Walnut Sour Cream Pancakes
Categories: Kids, Breakfast, Vegetable, Pancakes
Yield: 6 grandsons
2 c  Baking mix;
1 c  Zucchini; grated squeezed
-dry
1/4 c  Walnuts; chopped
1 1/2 c  Milk;
1 c  Sour cream;
2    Eggs;
2 tb Melted butter;
In a large bowl, combine the milk, sour cream, eggs and melted
butter. Mix thoroughly.  Add the baking mix, and stir until just
combined. Add the zucchini and walnuts.  Pour onto a lightly greased
400 degrees griddle or frying pan. Source: The San Diego
Union-Tribune Food Section, Nov. 3, 1994 and Kinniston Hill Inn in
Boothbay, Maine. Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her
Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On   11-04-94
