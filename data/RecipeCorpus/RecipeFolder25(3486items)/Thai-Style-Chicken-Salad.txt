Title: Thai-Style Chicken Salad
Categories: Salad, Chicken, Thai, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
-separated into rings
1 tb Minced fresh ginger root
Lime-Mint Dressing
1/2 c  Chopped fresh cilantro
-(leaves and stems)
1/4 c  Chopped peanuts, toasted
3 c  Chopped cooked chicken meat
1    Cucumber, peeled, seeded
-and shredded
2 c  Fresh bean sprouts
2    Fresh jalapeno chilies,
-seeded and minced
1/2 sm Red onion, sliced thin and
In a large bowl, mix chicken, cucumber, bean sprouts, chilies, onion
and ginger root. Toss with Lime-Mint Dressing. Gently toss in
cilantro. Serve immediately, sprinkled with peanuts.

NOTE: If not serving immediately, refrigerate ingredients and dressing
separately. Toss just before serving.

Nutrient data per serving (including dressing): 365 calories; 34 grams
protein; 17 grams fat (41 percent total calories); 15 grams
carbohydrates; 4 grams dietary fiber; 87 milligrams cholesterol; 443
milligrams sodium. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   11-25-94
