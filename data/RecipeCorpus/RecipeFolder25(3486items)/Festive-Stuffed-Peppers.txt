Title: Festive Stuffed Peppers
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
1 md Onion,chopped
1    Egg,slightly beaten
2    Garlic cloves,crushed
4    Peppers,any color
2 c  STOVE TOP Chicken Flavor
-Flexible Serving Stuffing
-Mix
1    Jar brown gravy (12 oz)
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 sm Green pepper,chopped
1. Heat oven to 375'F.

2. Mix all ingredients, except whole peppers, in large bowl. Cut
peppers lengthwise; remove seeds. Spoon stuffing mixture into pepper
halves. Place in baking pan; cover. Bake 50 minutes. Remove cover and
bake 10 minutes longer.

NOTE: For 2 servings, use 1 egg.
