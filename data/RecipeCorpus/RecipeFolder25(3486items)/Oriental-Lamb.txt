Title: Oriental Lamb
Categories: Main dish, Asian, Meat, Lamb
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Ginger;grated fresh
Salt;
Pepper;
4 lb Lamb;steak of
1 c  White Wine
2    Garlic;minced;cloves
2 tb Soy Sauce
Combine wine, garlic, soy sauce and ginger. Marinate lamb turning
occasionally.
Broil or grill to desired degree.
Salt and pepper to taste.
