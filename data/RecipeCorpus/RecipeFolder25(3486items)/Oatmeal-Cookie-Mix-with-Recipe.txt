Title: Oatmeal Cookie Mix with Recipe
Categories: Nuts/grains, Mixes, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
-COOKIES:
1 1/4 c  Butter, softened
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
1 pk Mix
1 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
3 c  Quick rolled oats
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Oatmeal Cookies: Preheat oven to 375. Cream butter, egg and vanilla.
Add mix and stir until well blended. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls
onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake 8-9 minutes. Cool 1 minute, remove
to rack to finish cooling.
