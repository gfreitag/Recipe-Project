Title: Snow Chili
Categories: Chili, Soup/stew, Beans, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Chopped chicken
4 oz Green chiles
4 ts Ground cumin
2 ts Ground oregano
1 ts Red pepper
3 c  Monterey Jack cheese
1 c  Sour cream
1 lb Dry white or Navy beans
6 c  Chicken broth
1 tb Chicken base
1 ts Pleasoning seasoning
1 tb Oil
2 c  Onions, chopped
7 ea Cloves garlic, minced
Cook beans in broth with base and seasoning for 2 hours or until
beans are tender. Brown onion, garlic and chicken in oil. Add to bean
mixture. Simmer for 30 minutes. Add cheese and sour cream.
