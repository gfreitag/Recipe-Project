Title: Bulgur Risotto Mix with Recipe
Categories: Nuts/grains, Mixes, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Cracked wheat bulgur
-RISOTTO:
2 1/2 c  Water
2 tb Butter
1 pk Risotto mix
1 tb Dried minced onion
3    Chicken bouillon cubes,
-crumbled
1 ts Dried chervil
1 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Black pepper
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Bulgur Risotto: Preheat oven to 350. Bring water and butter to a
boil. Add the risotto mix and cook, stirring, 5 minutes. Cover and
bake 25 minutes. 6 servings
