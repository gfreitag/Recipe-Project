Title: Wild Turkey Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Game, Poultry
Yield: 4 pounds
1/2 ts Powdered bay leaf
2 ts Lemon zest
2 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1/8 ts Ground cloves
1/4 c  Wild turkey Bourbon :)
5    Feet small hog or sheep
-casings
4 lb Wild turkey meat
2 ts Salt
1 ts White pepper
1 ts Celery seed
Prepare casings. Mix remaining ingredients, grind through the fine
disk and stuff into casings. Twist off into 3" links. Cook by
sauteing in oil over medium heat until nicely browned and cooked
through.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
