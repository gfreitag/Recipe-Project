Title: Pecan and Rice Loaf
Categories: Side dish, Rice, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Breadcrumbs, dry
1    Egg; beaten
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 c  Milk
2 c  Pecans; chopped
2 c  Rice; cooked
2 tb Butter; melted
1/4 c  Pepper, green; chopped
3 tb Flour, all-purpose
1/2 c  Celery; finely cut
Combine all ingredients and pack tightly in a well-greased 9x5x3"
loafpan lined with paper.  Bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
