Title: Glorified Grits
Categories: Nuts/grains, Cheese/eggs, Breakfast
Yield: 4 - 6 folks
1/2 ts Garlic powder;
1/2    Stick butter
1/2    Stick margarine;
1    Egg; beaten
Paprika
1 1/2 c  Quick-cooking grits;
6 oz American cheese; coarsely
-shredded
2 tb Worcestershire;
1 tb Tabasco Grits;
Cook grits as directed on the package.  While grits are hot, add the
other ingredients.  Place on top for color.  Bake at 300 degrees for
color. Bake at 300 degrees for about 40 minutes or until brown but
not to dry. Source: Southern Living, Our Best Recipes Brought to you
and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
