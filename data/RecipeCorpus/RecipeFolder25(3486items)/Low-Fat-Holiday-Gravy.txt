Title: Low Fat Holiday Gravy
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Holiday, Condiment
Yield: 4 cups
1 t  Kitchen Bouquet Browning &
-Seasoning Sauce
1/8 t  Pepper
4 1/2 c  Canned chicken broth
1/2 c  Flour
2    Chicken bouillon cubes
Blend in a bowl, 1 cup cold broth with flour until smooth. Heat
remaining broth with bouillon cubes in sauce pan. Stir flour mixture
into hot broth. cook and stir over moderate heat until mixture
thickens. Reduce heat. Add Kitchen Bouquet and pepper.

FLAVOR VARIATION: Replace 1/4 cup broth with dry sherry and add 1
tablespoon chopped parsley.

1% fat, less than 1 gram per serving.
