Title: Dilly Mashed Potatoes
Categories: Side dish, Potato, Herbs/spice
Yield: 6 servings
2 t  Dill weed
1 T  Dried minced onion
3/4 t  Seasoned salt
6 md Potatoes, peeled and cubed
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Sour cream (8 oz.)
2 T  Minced fresh dill OR
In a saucepan, cover potatoes with water; cook until very tender.
Drain; mash with milk. Stir in remaining ingredients. Serves 6-8.
