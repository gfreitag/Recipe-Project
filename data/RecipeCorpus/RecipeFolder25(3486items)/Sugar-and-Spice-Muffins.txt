Title: Sugar and Spice Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Herbs/spice
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Chopped nuts
1/4 c  Melted margarine
1    Egg, beaten
3/4 c  Milk
1 c  Flour
1/4 c  Sugar
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Oats
Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt; stir in oats, raisins and
nuts; add margarine, egg, and milk, mixing only until dry ingredients
are moistened.

Topping: 2 tb sugar 2 ts flour 1 ts cinnamon

Mix and sprinkle on muffins, then bake 15 minutes at 425 degrees.
