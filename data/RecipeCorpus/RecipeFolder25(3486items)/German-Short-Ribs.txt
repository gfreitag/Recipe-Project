Title: German Short Ribs
Categories: Beef, Main dish, Crockpot, German
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 T  Worcestershire sauce
1/2 t  Dry mustard
1/4 c  Cold water
3 lb Short ribs
1/2 c  Water
3 T  Brown sugar
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1/2 t  Chili powder
2 T  Flour
2 ea Onions
1/2 c  Chili sauce
3 T  Vinegar
Flour and pepper short ribs, brown in skillet.  Put in crockpot and
add sliced onion, water, chili sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, Worch.
sauce, chili powder and dry mustard.  Cover and cook on low 6-8
hours. Turn to high, skim excess fat.  Thicken sauce with flour mixed
with water. Serve with broad egg noodles and cooked carrots. From the
files of Earl Shelsby
