Title: Cheese-Baked Macaroni with Broccoli
Categories: Pasta, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs, Casserole
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Mozzarella, diced
2 ea Eggs
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
-F.
1 bn Fresh broccoli
3 T  Butter
1 T  Olive oil
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 lb Ziti, cooked and drained
Cook broccoli in a small amount of boiling salted water.  Drain, cool
and cut into small spears. Saute broccoli in butter and olive oil in
a medium size skillet until well saturated, stirring constantly.
Sprinkle the bottom of a 3-quart buttered casserole with Parmesan
cheese; cover with a layer of macaroni, then a layer of broccoli.
Top with a layer of mozzarella. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Continue alternating layers, finishing with a layer of macaroni.
Break eggs into a bowl, add 2 Tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese, and beat
well. Pour over macaroni, dot with butter, and bake, uncovered, for
20 minutes or until macaroni has a golden crust. Yield: Serves 4
