Title: Sour Cream and Dill Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Poultry, Main dish, Pasta
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Lemon juice
1 T  Fresh dill, chopped OR
1 t  Dill weed
1 cn Sliced mushrooms, drained
-(4 oz.)
Paprika
Cooked wide egg noodles,
-optional
8-10 Chicken pieces,
-skinned
Pepper to taste
1 cn Condensed cream of mushroom
-soup, undiluted (10 3/4
-oz.)
1 ea Envelope Dry onion soup mix
1 c  Sour cream (8 oz.)
Place chicken in a single layer in a 13" x 9" x 2" baking pan.
Sprinkle with pepper. Combine soup, soup mix, sour cream, lemon
juice, dill and mushrooms; pour over chicken. Sprinkle with paprika.
Bake uncovered, at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until chicken is tender.
Serve over egg noodles if desired. Yield: 4-6 servings. SOURCE:
*Rebekah Brown, Three Hills, Alberta, Country Woman Magazine May/June
93 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 11/93
