Title: Parmesan Pita Chips
Categories: Bread, Snack, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 48 servings
1/4 c  Butter or margaine
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 ea Clove garlic, finely
-choppped
1/2 t  Dried savory or thyme
-leaves, crumbled
1/3 c  Grated parmesan cheese
These homemade chips are
-tastier than any cheese
-flavoured chips you can
Buy. They'll quickly become
-a family favourite.
6 ea Pita breads, about 6 inches
-in diameter
Preheat the oven to 425F.  Using a sharp knife cut each pita bread
into quarters, cut through the middle and split each quarter into
two, for a total of 48 pieces.  Lay the pieces cut side up on an
ungreased baking sheet.  In a small saucepan, heat ubtter and oil
over moderate heat. Add garlic and savory, saute for 15 seconds.
Remove the butter mixture form the heat.  Brush the mixture over the
cut side of the pita pieces. Sprinkle grated cheese evenly over the
pita pieces.  Bake the pita until the pieces are crisp and lightly
browned, about 10 minutes.  Transfer the chips to a rack and let them
cool, and serve.  If not serving imediately, store the chips in an
airtight container.  Makes 48. Origin: Canadian Reader's Digest,
November/94. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
