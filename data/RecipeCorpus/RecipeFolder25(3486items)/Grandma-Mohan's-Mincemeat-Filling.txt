Title: Grandma Mohan's Mincemeat Filling
Categories: Game, Apples, Dessert, Pie/tart, Can/preserv
Yield: 2 servings
Well done.)
4 c  Sugar
10 c  Chopped, peeled apples
2 T  Cinnamon
1 1/2 t  Nutmeg
4 c  Water
2 c  Vinegar
2 c  Seedless raisins
6 c  Roast deer, elk, beef,...
-Ground in meat grinder.
(Grandma usually used
-venison or antelope rump
-roasts or
Other normally less tender
-cuts, salted and roasted
-'till
Combine all ingredients in LARGE pan and boil gently until apples are
tender but still firm.  Either use directly in pies or cookies or can
in quart jars. "Unknown"
