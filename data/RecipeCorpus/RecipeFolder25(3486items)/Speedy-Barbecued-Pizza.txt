Title: Speedy Barbecued Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Bbq/grill, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea 12 inch prepared pizza
-crust, thawed if frozen
2 ea Tomatoes, sliced
1 c  Grated parmesan cheese
Serve this easy pizza as an
-appetizer while your
-guests watch you
Barbecue the main course
1/3 c  Pesto sauce
Spread pesto sauce evenly over pizza crust.  Arrange tomato slices in
single layer over pesto sauce.  sprinkle evenly with parmesan.  Place
pizza on wire cake reack, place rack on foil pizza pan.  Place rack
and pan on barbecue grill.  Grill over medium heat for 15 minutes or
until cheese is bubbly and crust is beginning to brown around edges.
Serve at once cut into wedges. Per serving: 546 calories for 4, 364
for 6. 23 g fat for 4, 15 g for 6. Excellent source of calcium, good
soure of iron, moderate fibre for 4. Origin: Homemakers' Recipe
collection of the Year, Sizzling Barbecues. Shared by: Sharon Stevens.
