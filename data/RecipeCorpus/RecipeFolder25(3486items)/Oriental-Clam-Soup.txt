Title: Oriental Clam Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Seafood, Asian, Clam/oyster
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Minced parsley
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Sherry
2 c  Chicken broth
2 c  Clam juice
2 c  Minced clams
1 sm Onion, minced
Combine chicken broth and clam juice in large saucepan. Add clams and
onion; bring to a boil. Simmer for 10 minutes. Add parsley, soy
sauce, and sherry. Cook until heated through,serve at once
