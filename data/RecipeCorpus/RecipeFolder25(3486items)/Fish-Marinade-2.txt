Title: Fish Marinade 2
Categories: Cajun/creol, Salsa/sauce, Fish, Marinade
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 t  Ground cayenne pepper
2 c  Chablis wine
2 t  Salt
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
2 t  Creole mustard
2 t  Lemon juice
Mix all ingredients together and stir well.  Use as a marinade, Then
as a basting sauce when you cook fish. From Justin Wilson's "Outdoor
Cooking With Inside Help"
