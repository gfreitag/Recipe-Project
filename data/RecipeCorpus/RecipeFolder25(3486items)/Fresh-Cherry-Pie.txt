Title: Fresh Cherry Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 qt Tart Red Cherries
1    Plain Pastry
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2 1/2 tb Flour
1/4 ts Salt
Mix sugar, flour, salt and cherries together. Line piepan with
pastry, add cherry mixture and cover with top crust. Bake in a 450*F
oven for 10 minutes; reduce temperature to 350*F and bake 25 minutes
longer. Submitted By MICHELLE BRUCE   On   11-21-94
