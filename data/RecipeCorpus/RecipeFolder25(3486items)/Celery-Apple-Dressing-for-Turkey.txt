Title: Celery-Apple Dressing for Turkey
Categories: Side dish, Sthrn/livng, Apples, Dressing
Yield: 1 unknown
1/8 ts Pepper
6 c  Bread cubes, stale
2 tb Water; or liquid from cooked
-giblets
2 c  Apples; peeled and chopped
1 c  Celery; chopped
1 md Onion; chopped
1/3 c  Buttter (or shortening)
1 ts Salt
Prepare apples.  Saute celery and onion in butter; add salt and
pepper, bread and apples.  Sprinkle with water.  More moisture will
come from the apples, and the cooked dressing will not be dry.  Mix
dressing well and stuff turkey.

Yield: enough for a 10 to 12 pound turkey.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
