Title: Chicken Fajita Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Chicken, Tex/mexican, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
2 T  Lime juice
1 c  Thinly sliced onion
1 c  Green or red pepper strips
1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1/4 ea To 1/2 tsp salsa seasoning
12 ea Ounces (3 cups) shredded
-Monterey Jack cheese
Red pepper flakes for
-garnish
One pizza crust,
-commercial, or homemade
1 T  Vegetable oil
4 ea Skinless, boneless, chicken
-breat halves, cut into 2
-inch by 1/2 inch
Strips
1 1/2 T  Fajita seasoning mix
1/2 t  Mexican oregano
Prepare pizza crust according to directions.  Preheat oven to 400F.
Saute chicken in hot oil until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
Stir in next 5 ingredients.  Cook 1-2 minutes, or until vegetable are
crips tender. Spoon mixture over crust.  Stir salsa seasoning mix
into tomato sauce, spoon over pizza.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake on
middle oven rack until crust is golden brown, approximately 12-15
minutes.  Sprinkle to taste with red pepper flakes. Origin: Watkin's
