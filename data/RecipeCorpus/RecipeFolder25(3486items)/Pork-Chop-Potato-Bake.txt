Title: Pork Chop Potato Bake
Categories: Pork, Casserole, Potato
Yield: 4 servings
6    Pork chops
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
-up to 2 cans
5    Potatoes, peeled
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tb Instant minced onion
Slice potatoes 1/4 inch thich, place in large buttered casserole.
Sprinkle with salt, pepper and onion. Brown pork chops in skillet.
Place over potatoes. Spread soup over chops. Pour 1 soup can water
over soup. Cover, bake at 350 for 1 to 2 hours.
