Title: Chunky Beef over Rice
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Rice, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
2 t  French's Wrocestershire
-Sauce
1 pk Success Rice, cooked
1 cn Campbell's Chunky Beef Soup
-(19 oz)
1 c  Cooked broccoli flowerets
Prep Time: 10 minutes. Cook Time: 5 minutes.

In saucepan, combine soup, broccoli and Worcestershire. Over medium
heat, heat through, stirring often. Serve over rice.
