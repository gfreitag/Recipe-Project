Title: Sandy's Guacamole Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 2 cups
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ds Tabasco or more
4 md Avocados, peeled & mashed
1 ts Lawry's Seasoned Salt
Taste a small portion of EACH avocado before mashing to make certain
that it isn't bitter.  Combine & chill all ingredients.  Spoon on top
of your taco or serve as a dip, with corn chips or chilled vegetable
dippers.
