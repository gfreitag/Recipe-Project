Title: Chicken Provencale
Categories: Main dish, French, Chicken, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1    Garlic;minced;clove
1/2 c  White Wine
2 c  Tomato;chopped
2 tb Basil;chopped fresh
6    Chicken Breast;boned, skinne
4 tb Olive Oil
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Black Olives;chopped
2    Anchovies;minced;fillets
In large skillet heat butter and oil. Saute chicken on both sides
until brown.  Remove from pan.
In same skillet swirl wine; add garlic, tomatoes, anchovy, olives
and basil. Simmer 5 minutes.
Add chicken, cover, simmer until tender, about 30 minutes.
