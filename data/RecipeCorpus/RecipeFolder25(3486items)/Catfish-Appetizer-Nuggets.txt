Title: Catfish Appetizer Nuggets
Categories: Cajun/creol, Appetizer, Fish
Yield: 72 servings
MMMMM--------------------------NUGGETS-------------------------------
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Black pepper
1/2 c  Butter or margarine,melted
2 lb Catfish fillets
1 c  Round cheese crackers
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Sesame seeds
MMMMM-----------------SOUR CREAM BLUE CHEESE DIP----------------------
1/4 t  Salt
Chopped fresh parsley
1 pk Dairy sour cream(8oz)
2 T  Crumbled blue cheese
1/4 c  Minced onion
*** NUGGETS ***

1. Thaw frozen fish fillets according to package directions.

2. Cut fillets into 1-inch cubes and set aside.

3. Combine cracker crumbs, Parmesan cheese, sesame seeds, salt and
pepper in small bowl and set aside.

4. Dip fish cubes in melted butter; roll in crumbs mixture.

5. Place fish cubes 1/2 inch apart on fole-lined baking sheets.

6. Bake uncovered in preheated 400' oven for 20 minutes, until cubes
are golden brown.

7. Serve immediately with Sour Cream Blue Cheese Dip.

*** SOUR CREAM BLUE CHEESE DIP ***

1. Combine sour cream, blue cheese, onion and salt in a small mixing
bowl; cover and refrigerate.

2. When ready to serve, garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
