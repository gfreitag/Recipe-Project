Title: Caesar Tofu Dip
Categories: Dip, Vegetable, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Freshly grated parmesan
-cheese
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
1 t  Olive oil
Salt and pepper
Process tofu, mayonnaise,
-yogurt, lemon juice,
-worcestershire sauce,
1 c  Tofu
2 T  Light mayonnaise
2 T  Plain yogurt
1 T  Lemon juice
2 t  Worcestershire sauce
1 t  Dijon mustard
1 t  Anchovy paste
ds Hot pepper sauce
mustard, anchovy paste and hot pepper sauce until smooth.  Stir in
cheese, garlic and oil.  Season with salt and pepper.  Can be kept in
refrigerator for up to 8 hours.  Makes 1-1/3 cups. Origin: Candian
Living, December, 1994. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
