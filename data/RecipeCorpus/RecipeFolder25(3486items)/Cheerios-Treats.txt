Title: Cheerios Treats
Categories: Snack, Nuts/grains
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 c  Peanut butter smooth or
Crunchy
5 c  Cheerios ceareal
3 tb Margarine or butter
1 pk Miniature marshmallows)
(6 cups)  or
40    Marshmallows
Grease 13x9-inch pan. Microwave margarine or butter in large
microwavable bowl on HIGH 45 seconds or until melted. Add
marshmallows; toss to coat with margarine or butter. Microwave on
HIGH 1 1/2 minutes or until smooth when stirred, stirring after 45
seconds. Stir in peanut butter. Immediately add cereal; mix lightly
until well coated. Using greased spatula or wax paper, press mixture
into prepared pan. Cool; cut into squares. Makes about 24

TIP: for ease in preparation, spray pan and spatula or wax paper with
no stick cooking spray.

VARIATIONS: Mix 1 cup candy-coated milk chocolate candies OR 1 cup
raisins and 1/2 cup dry roasted peanuts with cereal; add to warm
marshmallow-peanut butter mixture.
