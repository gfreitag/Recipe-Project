Title: Lemon & Garlic Stuffed Mushrooms
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Mushroom
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Parsley, chopped
1/2 ea Lemon, peel freshly grated
1/4 ts Salt
Pepper, to taste
4 lg Flat mushrooms, wiped &
-- trimmed
2 oz Margarine
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
Lightly oil a baking sheet.  Preheat oven to 375F.

Chop the mushroom stalks.  Set the caps aside.

Beat the margarine until it is soft.  Stir in the garlic along with
the mushroom stalks, parsley, lemon peel, salt & pepper.  Divide this
mixture evenly between the mushroom caps.  Place on the baking sheet
& cook for 20 minutes.  Serve immediately either as is or else on top
of freshly toasted wholewheat bread.

Elizabeth Brand, "Vegetables"
