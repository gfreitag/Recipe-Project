Title: Fudge (Timkham)
Categories: Candy, Snack, Dessert, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 24 servings
6 tb Water
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Nuts (chopped)
1/8 ts Salt
3/4 c  Cocoa
3 c  Sugar
3 tb Corn syrup
1 1/8 c  Evaporated milk
Cook and stir the cocoa, sugar, corn syrup, milk and water until sugar
dissolves. Boil slowly to soft-ball stage. Add salt and vanilla.
Cool; beat until thick and creamy, then add nuts and pour onto
buttered dish.
