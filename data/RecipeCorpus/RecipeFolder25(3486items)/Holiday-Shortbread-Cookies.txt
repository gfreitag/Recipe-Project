Title: Holiday Shortbread Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
2 c  Cold butter (no substitutes)
5 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, suar and salt.  Cut in butter
until mixture resembles fine crumbs.  Pat into an ungreased 15x10x1"
baking pan. Prick all over with a fork. Bake at 325 degrees for 35
minutes or until center is set. Cool for 10-15 minutes. Cut into
small squares. Continue to cool at room temperature.  Cookies will
keep in a storage tin or airtight container for several weeks.
