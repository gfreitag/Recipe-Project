Title: Ranch-Style Dressing & Dip Mix with Recipe
Categories: Mixes, Dressing, Dip
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------MIX-MAKES ONE QUARTER CUP----------------------
1/4 tb Dried tarragon
1/2 tb Garlic powder
1/2 tb Lemon pepper
1 1/2 tb Dried parsley
1 tb Salt
1/2 tb Dried chives
1/4 tb Dried oregano
MMMMM----------------RANCH DRESSING-MAKES ONE CUP---------------------
1 tb Mix
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Buttermilk
MMMMM------------------RANCH DIP-MAKES TWO CUPS-----------------------
1 c  Sour cream
2 tb Mix
1 c  Mayonnaise
Mix: Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.

Dressing: Whisk all ingredients together. Refrigerate one hour before
serving.

Dip: Combine and chill 2 hours before serving. Serve with raw
vegetables or as a topping for baked potatoes or steamed vegetables.

Source: The Perfect Mix by Diane Phillips ISBN:0-688-12104-7
