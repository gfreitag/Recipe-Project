Title: Hungarian Meatballs - Hungarian
Categories: European, Ground beef, Main dish, Meatballs
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Marjoram Leaves
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Flour; Unbleached
Recipe of Basic Meatballs; *
1 tb Vegetable Oil
2    Onions; Md., Thinly Sliced
3/4 c  Water
3/4 c  Red Wine; Dry, **
1 ts Caraway Seed
* See Recipe 229. ** You can substitute a mixture of 3/4 cup of
water, 1 tsp of instant Prepare the basic meatballs and set aside.
Heat the oil in a large skillet. Add the onions and cook and stir
until they are tender. Add the cooked meatballs, 3/4 cup of water,
the wine, caraway seed, paprika, marjoram leaves and salt. Heat to
boiling then reduce the heat and cover. Simmer for about 30 minutes,
stirring occasionally. Mix 1/4 cup of water and the flour, stir into
the sauce mixture. Heat to boiling, stirring carefully. Boil and stir
for 1 minute. NOTE: Serve with either boiled potatoes or noodles for
a great main dish.
