Title: Tuna Alfredo with Angel Hair
Categories: Main dish, Fish, Seafood, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
-drained (6 oz ea)
1 c  Part skim ricotta cheese
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
3/4 c  Borden or Meadow Gold
-Lowfat Milk
1 t  Italian seasoning
3/4 t  Garlic salt
1 sm Red bell pepper, seeded and
-coarsely chopped
2 tb Margarine or butter
1/2 pk Creamette Angel Hair, cooked
-as package directs and
-drained
4 c  Small fresh broccoli
-flowerets, steamed
-OR
1 pk Frozen chopped broccoli,
-thawed (10 oz)
2 cn StarKist Select Solid Light
-Tuna in Spring Water, well
In small bowl, combine StarKist Select Tuna, cheese, milk, Italian
seasoning and garlic salt; mix well. In large skillet, over medium
heat, cook bell pepper in maragrine until tender. Stir in tuna
mixture then angel hair and broccoli. Mix well. Heat through. Serve
immediately. Refrigerate leftovers.
