Title: Honey-Hoisin Marinade
Categories: Marinade, Asian
Yield: 2 cups
2    Garlic cloves, minced or
-pressed
2 tb Ginger root, grated fresh
2 sm Chilies, red, dried and
-crumbled (seeds removed for
-less heat)
1/4 c  Soy sauce, low-sodium
2 tb Hoisin sauce
1/3 c  Honey, oriental or dark
1/4 c  Tomato sauce
2 tb Sherry, dry
2 tb Rice wine vinegar
1 ts Five spice powder
Combine all the ingredients in a nonreactive bowl or blender.

Source: "Dry Rubs, Pastes & Marinades for Poultry, Meat, Seafood,
Cheese & Vegetables" by Jim Tarantino, 1992.  The Crossing Press,
Freedom, CA 95019. Submitted By JIM MOREY   On   12-06-94
