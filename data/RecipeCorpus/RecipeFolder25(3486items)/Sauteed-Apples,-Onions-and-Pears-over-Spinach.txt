Title: Sauteed Apples, Onions and Pears over Spinach
Categories: Vegetable, Fruit, Apples, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
-into rings
1 tb Olive Oil
1    Pear, cored and sliced
1    Cooking Apple, cored& sliced
10 oz Fresh Spinach, washed and
-trimmed
2 tb Honey
1/4 c  White Wine Vinegar
1/4 c  Dry Sherry or Apple Juice
1 tb Lemon Juice
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Dried Thyme (optional)
1 sm Onion, sliced and separated
Combine the first six ingredients.  Add thyme, if desired.  Set
aside. Cook onion in olive oil in a large skillet over medium high
heat, stirring constantly, until tender.  Add pear, cooking apples
and honey mixture.

Cook, stirring often, 5 to 6 minutes or until fruit is tender and
liquid is slightly thickened.  Serve over fresh spinach.
