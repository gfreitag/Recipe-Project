Title: Venison Stew No. 2
Categories: Game, Soup/stew, Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
Salt to taste
6 ea Carrots, quartered
3 ea Potatoes, in 1/2 inch cubes
2 lb Venison, in one-inch pieces
1 t  Lemon juice
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 t  Sugar
8 sm Whole onions, peeled
1/4 lb Margarine
4 c  Hot water
1 t  Worcestershire sauce
2 md Onions, sliced
Brown meat thoroughly in margarine in large heavy kettle. Add all
ingredients, except carrots, whole onions and potatoes. Simmer for 2
hours, adding water if necessary. Add carrots, whole onions and
potatoes and cook about 30 minutes. Liquid may be thickened with
flour if desired.
