Title: Dumplings for Chicken and Dumplings
Categories: Pasta, Chicken
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 t  Salt
3 ea Eggs, beaten
1/4 c  Milk
Recipe from 1904.
Serves 6
3 c  Flour
1 t  Baking powder
Mix all ingredients well.  POur out onto well floured board.  With
rolling pin, roll out 1/2 batter at a time.  Cut into 1 inch squares.
Drop one at a time into 2 quarts of boiling chicken broth in a 4
quart Dutch oven. Cook 20 minutes. Origin: Women's Circle, for cooks
on the go. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/94
