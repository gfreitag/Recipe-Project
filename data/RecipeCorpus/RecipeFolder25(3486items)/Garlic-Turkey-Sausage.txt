Title: Garlic Turkey Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Turkey, Poultry
Yield: 5 pounds
2 ts Black pepper
1 ts Garlic powder
-OR-
2 cl Finely minced garlic
5    Feet small hog or sheep
-casings
5 lb Turkey-15% fat
1 tb Ground coriander seed
2 ts Salt, or to taste
Prepare the casings. Grind turkey through the fine disk; combine with
remaining ingredients and grind again. Stuff casings and twist of
into 3" links. Saute in vegetable oil until evenly browned and cooked
trhough.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
