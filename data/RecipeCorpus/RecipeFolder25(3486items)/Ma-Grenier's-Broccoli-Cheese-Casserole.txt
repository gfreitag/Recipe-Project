Title: Ma Grenier's Broccoli Cheese Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Side dish, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
2    Rows Ritz crackers
1    Stick butter, melted
2    Boxes frozen chopped
-broccoli
1 lb Velveeta, cubed
Cook broccoli halfway, drain well. Mix with Velveeta. Place in a
lightly buttered casserole dish. Crush crackers and mix with butter.
Spread over top of broccoli. Bake at 350 until the cheese is melted
and is nicely browned
