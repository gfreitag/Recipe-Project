Title: Pinto Bean Pie
Categories: Beans, Pie/tart, Main dish, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 - 9" pies
3 c  White sugar;
1 tb Vanilla;
2    Sticks margarine; melted
3    Unbaked 9" pie shells;
1 c  Pinto beans; mashed with a
-little liquid
1 cn Coconut;
1 c  Pecans;
4    Eggs;
Mix all ingredients together and pour into pie shells.  Bake for 1
hour in a 300 degrees oven. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune Food
Section, Nov. 10, 1994. & "Readers Ask" & "The Little Mountain Bean
Bible Cookbook". Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her
Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On   11-10-94
