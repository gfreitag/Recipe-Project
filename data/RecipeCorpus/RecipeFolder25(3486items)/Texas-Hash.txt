Title: Texas Hash
Categories: Casserole, Ground beef, Rice, Tomato, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 t  Pepper
3/4 c  Rice, uncooked
2 t  Chili powder
1 lg Onion, chopped
1 1/2 lb Hamburger
1 1/2 t  Salt
28 oz Can whole tomatoes
Cook onions in a little oil until yellow.  Add hamburger and saute'
until mixture falls apart.  Drain.  Set aside.  Pour tomatoes into a
2 or 3 qt. casserole and break apart or mash with a potato masher.
Add rice and seasonings.  Pour in hamburger/onion mixture.  Stir to
mix. Cover casserole and bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour.  Serves 4.
Variations: You can use pureed tomatoes instead of whole tomatoes.
Increase the chili powder for an added kick. Note: A great casserole
when money is tight and time is precious! Serve with fresh bread and
butter. From: Debbie's Mom, Dulcinea Ertz Posted by: Debbie Carlson
