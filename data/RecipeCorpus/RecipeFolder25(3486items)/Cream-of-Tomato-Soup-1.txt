Title: Cream of Tomato Soup 1
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
Pepper
2 1/2 c  Milk
2 1/2 c  Tomatoes
1 t  Grated onion
4 T  Flour
2-4 tbls. butter
Add grated onion to tomatoes, simmer for a few minutes and strain.
Make a cream sauce of the flour, seasonings, butter and milk. Remove
from heat. Pour strained tomatoes graually into cream sauce, stirring
quickly. Serve at once. Serves 6. AR/93
