Title: Oyster Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Clam/oyster
Yield: 2 pounds
1 ts Coarse grind black pepper
1    Egg, beaten
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
2 c  Fresh bread crumbs
4    Feet small hog casings
2 lb Shucked fresh oysters,
-drained, reserving liquid
1 cl Finely minced garlic
Prepare casings. Pulse oysters in food processor unti coarsley
chopped. Add remaining ingredients and process until well blended.
Add enough reserved liquid to form a stiff paste. Stuff casings and
twist off into 3-4" lonks.

Source: Home Sausae Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
