Title: Shrimp Cocktail
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Salsa/sauce, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Heavy whipping cream
1 1/2 tb Horseradish
1/2 ts Lemon juice
-Bibb lettuce or crushed
-ice
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Vinegar
2 ts Seafood seasoning
1 ts Salt
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1 lb Cleaned shrimp
Combine water vinegar, seafood seasoning, salt, and garlic in a lrage
saucepan; bring to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat for 10
minutes or until shrimp are pink and firm. Drain; rinse in cold
water. To make sauce, combine ketchup, cream, horseradish, and lemon
juice; mix well. Arrange shrimp on bed of lettuce or ice. Serve with
sauce.
