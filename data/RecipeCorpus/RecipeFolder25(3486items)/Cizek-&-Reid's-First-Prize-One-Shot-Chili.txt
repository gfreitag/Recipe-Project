Title: Cizek & Reid's First Prize One-Shot Chili
Categories: Chili, Meat
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Tomato sauce
8 oz Chicken broth
1 2/3 oz Green Chili peppers; canned
Whole
2    Onions; brown
Finely chopped
4 c  Water
4 lb London broil; cut
Into cubes
Karen Adler-FNGP13B
3 tb Paprika
1 tb Onion; powdered
1 ts Cayenne pepper
2 tb Cumin
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts White pepper
8 tb Chili powder; fresh
2    Bouillon cubes; chicken
Brown meat, drain and set aside. Combine the rest of the ingredients
in a suitable pot, add browned meat and simmer uncovered over low
heat at least three hours. Converted to MM format by Karen Adler -
FNGP13B.
