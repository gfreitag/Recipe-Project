Title: Spanish Rice 3
Categories: Casserole, Rice, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
2 x  Tomatoes; 16 oz can
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Grated American cheese
4 x  Slices bacon;cut up
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper; finely chopped
3 c  Rice; cooked
Fry bacon until crisp. Remove to 1 1/2 qt. baking dish and whisk
around to grease bowl. Add to bacon fat in skillet the onion and
green pepper. Saute until onion is yellow. Combine in baking dish
with cooked rice, tomatoes, salt and pepper. Sprinkle cheese ovr top.
Bake at 400 for 25-30 minutes. Serves 4-6    SOURCE: My Famous Little
