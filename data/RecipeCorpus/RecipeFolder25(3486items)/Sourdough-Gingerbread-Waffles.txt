Title: Sourdough Gingerbread Waffles
Categories: Pancakes, Sourdough, Herbs/spice, Breakfast, Waffles
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Cinnamon
4 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Molasses
6 tb Cooking oil
1/2 c  Sourdough starter
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
2 c  All purpose flour
Mix together molasses, oil, sourdough starter, milk and eggs. Combine
flour with baking powder, baking soda, salt, ginger, cinnamon, and
sugar. Add dry ingredients to starter mixture and blend well. Pour
desired amount of batter on hot waffle grids. cook about 5 to 6 min.
or until done.

From: Sourdough Cookery by Rita Davenport Makes 6 small waffles %%%
