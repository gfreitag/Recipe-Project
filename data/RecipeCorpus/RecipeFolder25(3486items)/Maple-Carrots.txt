Title: Maple Carrots
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 1 serving
1/2 ts Cinnamon (optional)
1 ds Salt (optional)
1    Carrot per person
2 tb Maple-flavored syrup
Peel and grate one carrot per serving.  Steam in a small saucepan
with a small abount of water, or in the microwave until carrots are
tender.

Add 1 to 2 tablespoons maple-flavored syrup, and a few shakes of
cinnamon and a dash of salt, if desired.  Stir to blend.  Heat
through and serve.

Source:  S.H.A.R.E. Colorado Oct./Nov. 1994 Submitted by Mary Tagge -
Aurora, Colorado
