Title: Smoky Chipotle Chile
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian, Low-cal/fat, Chili
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts -Red chili powder
2 c  Water
2    Bay leaves
2 ts Mexican oregano
1 ts Ground cumin
1 ts Salt
2 ts Tamari
2 ts Honey
3/4 c  Cracked wheat or bulghur
1 c  Frozen corn
1    Onion; chopped
1    Green pepper; chopped
2    Celery stalks; chopped
2 lg Garlic cloves; minced
30 oz Canned pinto or black beans
-- drained
28 oz Whole, peeled tomatoes
16 oz Tomato sauce
4    Canned chipotle chilies
-- drained, stemmed & pureed
-OR-
In a large, nonstick pot lightly coated with oil, saute onion, green
pepper, celery and garlic until tender.

Add beans, tomatoes, tomato sauce, water and seasonings.  Bring to a
boil and stir in bulghur and corn.  Reduce heat and simmer 20 to 30
minutes.

Calories per serving: 314 Grams of fat: 1 % fat calories: 3
Cholesterol: 0 mg Grams of fiber: 17.2
