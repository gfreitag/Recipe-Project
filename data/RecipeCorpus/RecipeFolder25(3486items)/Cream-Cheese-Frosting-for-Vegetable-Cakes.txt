Title: Cream Cheese Frosting for Vegetable Cakes
Categories: Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Package (1lb)
-confectioner's sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 ea - 8oz package cream cheese
2 t  Vanilla
Beat shortening with cream cheese and vanilla.  Gradually beat in
sugar. If mixture becomes too thick, add a little milk.  Spread on
cooled cake. Origin:  Hearth and Home Companion Shared by: Sharon
Stevens
