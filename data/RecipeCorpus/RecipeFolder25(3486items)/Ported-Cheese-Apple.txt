Title: Ported Cheese Apple
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Alcohol, Holiday, Apples
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk (125g) Cream cheese,
-softened
1 T  Port wine
Paprika
1 ea Cinnamon stick
1 ea Bay leaf
This looks like a big red
-apple. The cinnamon stick
-makes the stem, and the
Bay
Leaf an apple leaf.
1 1/2 c  Grated cheddar cheese
Blend together the cheeses and wine.  Wrap the cheese mixture in a
sheet of plastic wrap and shape into a ball.  Chill about 1 hour or
until ball in slightly set.  Form wrapped cheese ball into the form
of an apple. Unwrap; sprinkle (or roll) with paprika until completely
coated.  Use a small piece of cinnamon stick and the bay leaf toform
the stem and leaf of the apple. Serve with assorted crackers and
fruit. Origin: Canadian Living, December 1991 Shared by: Sharon
Stevens
