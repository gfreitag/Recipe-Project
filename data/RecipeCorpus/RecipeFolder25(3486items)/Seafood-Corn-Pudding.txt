Title: Seafood Corn Pudding
Categories: Native amer, Casserole, Pudding, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
3/4 c  Condensed milk
Salt and pepper to taste
4 lg Ears corn
2 T  Melted butter
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 1/2 lb Small shrimp, shelled
3 ea Eggs well beaten
1 t  Sugar
Preheat oven to 325oF. Grease well with butter and sprinkle with
cornmeal a 1-1/2 quart casserole. Set aside. Grate the fresh corn
into a medium bowl. In a separate bowl beat eggs and fold into the
corn. Add melted butter, sugar and condensed milk. Blend well. Add
shrimp, salt and pepper, and blend well. Pour into the prepared
casserole and bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Serve
immediately. Serves 6
