Title: Low Fat Chicken Parmesan
Categories: Chicken, Low-cal/fat, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Green onions, sliced
1 T  Margarine
1 T  Flour
1/2 c  Skim milk
5 oz Frozen spinach
1 T  Pimento, chopped
1/3 c  Parmesan cheese, grated
1/4 t  Italian seasoning, crushed
3 ea Chicken breasts, (about
-2-1/4 pounds total,
-skinless, boneless and
Halved lengthwise
PER SERVING:  202 cal., 30g Pro., 4g Carbo., 7g fat, 77mg Chol., 213mg
Sodium, 361mg Potassium In a small mixing bowl combine cheese and
Italain seasoning.  Roll chicken pieces in cheese mixture to coat
lightly; set remaining cheese mixture aside. ARRANGE pieces in an
8x8x2-inch baking dish.  In a small saucepan cook onion in hot
margarine till tender. Stir in flour; add milk all at once. Cook and
stir till bubbly; stir in drained spinach and pimiento.  Spoon
spinach mixture over chicken; sprinkle with remaining cheese mixture.
Bake, uncovered, in a 350F oven for 30 to 35 minutes or till tender.
