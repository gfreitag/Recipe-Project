Title: Raisin Compote with Rosemary and Thyme
Categories: Fruit, Herbs/spice, Can/preserv
Yield: 3 cups
1 1/2 ts Rosemary; minced
1 1/2 ts Thyme; minced
1 ts Sage; minced
1/4 c  Parsley; minced
Salt and pepper
1 c  Golden raisins (7 oz)
1 c  Dark raisins (7 oz)
1 c  Juice from 3 to 4 oranges
1/4 c  Juice from 1 to 2 lemons
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Cayenne pepper
Bring first six ingredients, with 1 1/2 cups water, to a simmer in a
medium-size, heavy-bottomed saucepan over medium heat. Reduce heat to
low and simmer, stirring frequently, until raisins plump, about 15
minutes. Add next four ingredients; simmer to blend flavors, about 5
minutes. Cool to room temperature, season to taste with salt and
pepper, then refrigerate. Serve chilled.
