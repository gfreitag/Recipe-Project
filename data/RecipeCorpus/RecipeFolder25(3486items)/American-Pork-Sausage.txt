Title: American Pork Sausage
Categories: Sausage, Pork, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Ground Mace
2 t  Coriander
1 ea Whole Nutmeg, grated
1 c  Water
5 lb Medium Ground Pork
1 T  Salt
2 T  Sage
2 t  Freshly Ground Pepper
1 t  Ground Cloves
Combine all ingredients, mix well and stuff into sheep casing or make
into patties. SOURCE: The Sausage Making Cookbook, by Jerry Predika
Collection of Clarence Fontish
