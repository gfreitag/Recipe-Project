Title: Catfish Gumbo
Categories: Main dish, Fish, Vegetable, Cajun/creol
Yield: 2 gallons
4 c  Boiling water
20 oz Tomatoes; undrained
2 pk Sliced okra (10 oz. ea)
4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Thyme
3    Bay leaves
6 oz Vegetable cocktail juic (V8)
8    Catfish; cut in 1" pieces
-(Farm-raised is best)
1 c  Celery; chopped
1 c  Green pepper; chopped
1 c  Onion; chopped
2    Garlic cloves; minced
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
4    Beef bouillon cubes
Prepare fish and set aside.  Saute celery, green pepper, onions and
garlic in vegetable oil until tender.  Dissolve bouillon cubes in hot
water; add remaining ingredients except fish.  Bring to a boi, lower
heat and simmer 30 minutes.  Add fish, mix well, and simmer, covered,
15 minutes, or until fish flakes easily.  Serve over rice.
