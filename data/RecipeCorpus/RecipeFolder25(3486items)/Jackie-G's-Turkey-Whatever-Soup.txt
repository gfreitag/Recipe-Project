Title: Jackie G's Turkey-Whatever Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Turkey, Vegetable, Canadian
Yield: 25 servings
2    Large onions
3    Stalks celery
Chicken soup base
Noodles or rice
Turkey drippings
Water
Turkey pieces
3    Carrots
Mix everything in order in VERY large pot.  Arrange to taste.  There
should be enough noodles or rice so that just after adding, every
scoop of soup has a lot of them.

Total time:  40 minutes (without counting turkey's time)

Recepie taken (with permission) from Jackie Grenier at Northgate
Restaurant, Montreal River, Ontario.
