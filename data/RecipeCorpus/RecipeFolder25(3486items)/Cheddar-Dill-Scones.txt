Title: Cheddar Dill Scones
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Butter
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
1/2 c  Half and half
2 1/2 c  Flout
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 c  Chopped fresh parsley
1 tb Tb
2 ts Dill weed
Makes: 16 Scones

Heat oven to 400~. Combine all ingredients except butter, eggs, and
half and half. Cut in butter until crumbly. Stir in eggs and half and
half just until moistened. Turn dough out onto lightly floured board;
knead until smooth (1 minute). Divide dough in half; roll each half
into an 8" circle. Cut each circle into 8 wedges. Place 1" apart on
cookie sheets. Bake 15-20 minutes or until lightly browned.
