Title: Jalapeno Salsa
Categories: Appetizer, Dip, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Onion;finely chopped
1/4    Lemon;juice and pulp of;
3    Tomato;finely chopped;
1    Green Bell Pepper;finely cho
3 tb Jalapeno Pepper;finely chopp
Combine chopped ingredients in a bowl. Add lemon juice and pulp
and mix thoroughly.
Refrigerate before serving.
Serve with crisp tortilla chips, on celery chunks or other raw
vegetables, as a sauce in tacos or whenever a spicy salsa is desired.
