Title: Shrimp Stuffed Peppers
Categories: Seafood, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1    Clove garlic, chopped
4 tb Cooking oil
1 1/2 lb Shrimp, clean & devein
2 c  Grated bread crumbs
4 tb Grated Parmesan
1 c  Cooked rice
6    Bell peppers
1    SAUCE RECIPE
2 1/2 c  Canned tomatoes
1    Onion, chopped
1    Clove garlic, chopped
Salt/pepper to taste
1    STUFFING RECIPE:
1    Onion, chopped
2    Stalks celery, chopped
Make sauce:  Combine tomatoes, onion, garlic, salt and pepper. Simmer
together for 45 minutes and set aside. Make stuffing: Saute' onions,
celery and garlic in pan with oil for about 10 minutes. Add shrimp,
chopped in small pieces.  Simmer about 30 minutes. Season to taste.
In a separate bowl, combine bread crumbs and cheese. Pour shrimp
mixture into crumbs and mix well.  Mix in cooked rice.  Stuff peppers
and arrange in baking dish. Pour sauce in dish and bake at 350
degrees for about 1 hour. Submitted By BOBBI ZEE   On   10-24-94
