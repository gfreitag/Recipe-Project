Title: Irish Cream Liqueur Iii
Categories: Alcohol, Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Irish whiskey
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
4 lg Eggs
1 t  Instant coffee
1/2 t  Pure vanilla extract
1/4 t  Almond extract
Add flavorings to whiskey and blend in blender. Add milk and blend on
low speed for 1 minute. Add eggs and pulse stir for 1 minute (use the
lowest speed on blender and turn on-off about eight times). Makes
about 4 cups.
