Title: Tomato and Fish
Categories: Main dish, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Water
1/4 ts Dill Weed
3 tb Butter
2 ts Oil
2 c  Rice;cooked
1 1/2 lb Fish;fillets
1    Tomato;chopped;
1 tb Lemon Juice
1 tb Onion;chopped sauteed
1/8 ts Basil
Preheat oven to broil. In an iron skillet, melt butter and saute
fish fillets for 5 - 10 minutes until fish is firm. Remove from heat.
In a bowl, combine tomato, lemon juice, onion, basil and dill.
Mound about 2 Tbl. of vegetable mixture onto each fillet, sprinkle
with oil, and broil until golden brown - 2 to 3 minutes.
Serve with rice.
