Title: Orange Scones with Raspberry Filling
Categories: British, Pastry
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 c  Whipping cream
3 T  Raspberry preserves
3 1/4 c  Flour
4 t  Baking powder
6 T  Butter or margarine
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 T  Grated orange peel
Icing sugar
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 t  Salt
3 ea Eggs, beaten
Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt;
cut in margarine until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Beat eggs,
cream and orange peel. Add to flour mixture, mixing just until
moistened. Shape dough into a ball. Knead 10 times on lightly floured
surface. Divide in half. Roll out each half into a 12x6-inch
rectangle. Spread 1 rectangle with preserves. Top with remaining
rectangle. Cut into 8 (3-inch) squares; cut each in half diagonally.
Place on lightly greased cookie sheet. Bake 12 to 14 minutes or until
lightly browned. Sprinkle with icing sugar.
