Title: Jell-O Nanaimo Bars
Categories: Chocolate, Nuts/grains, Dessert
Yield: 24 servings
-powder mix, any flavour
1/3 c  Each, butter and boiling
-water
2 c  Icing sugar
3 oz Semi sweet chocolate
1/2 c  Whipping cream
3 oz Unsweetened chocolate
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Graham wafer crumbs
1/2 c  Finely chopped toasted
-pecans
1 pk Jello Instant pudding
Melt unsweetened chocolate and butter over low heat, remove, add
crumbs and pecans, mix well.  Press into 9 inch square pan, chill.
Mix pudding powder, butter and water.  Blend in icing sigar imto;
s,pptj/  Spread over base.  Chill until firm.  Melt chocolate and
cream over low heat. Stir until smooth.  Spread over pudding layer.
Chill.  Store in refrigerator. Let stand at room temperature for
about 5 minutes before slicing. Makes 24 bars. Origin: Kraft What's
Cooking Magazine, 10th issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
