Title: Steak 'n Mushrooms
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Meat, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1    Garlic;chopped;clove
3 tb Parsley;chopped
Sherry;Optional:;
Worcestershire;
1 1/2 lb Sirloin Steak
2 lb Mushrooms;caps of
6 tb Butter
6 tb Olive Oil
Heat in skillet butter and oil. Add mushroom caps and saute about 3
minutes. Add garlic and cook until tender.
Sprinkle parsley and season to taste with salt and pepper. You may
wish to add a dash of Worcestershire sauce or sherry.
Serve over cooked steak. (See STEAK AND ONIONS)
