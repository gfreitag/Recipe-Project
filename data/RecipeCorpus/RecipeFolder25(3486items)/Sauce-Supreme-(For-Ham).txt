Title: Sauce Supreme (For Ham)
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce, Sthrn/livng, Pork
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
1 ts Lemon peel; slivered
1/8 ts Salt
16 oz Applesauce
2 tb Horseradish, prepared
Combine all ingredients; blend well.  Chill overnight.  Serve cold
with ham.
