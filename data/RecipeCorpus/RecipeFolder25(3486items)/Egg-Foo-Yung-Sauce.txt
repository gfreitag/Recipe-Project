Title: Egg Foo Yung Sauce
Categories: Asian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts 5-Spice;ground
1 tb Soy Sauce
2 tb Cornstarch
1 1/2 c  Water;boiling
1/2 ts Ginger;ground
Dissolve the cornstarch in 1/2 cup cold water, stir well.
Add the cornstarch to the boiling water, stirring constantly.
Reduce heat to medium, add the rest of the ingredients, cook 3 more
minutes.
Serve with EGG FOO YUNG, or in stir-fried vegetables.
