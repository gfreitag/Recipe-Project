Title: Cheap Cheap Chili
Categories: Chili, Main dish, Low-cal/fat, Turkey
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------LIZ JONES VXRF36B--------------------------
Pepper & salt or garlic salt
2 ts Or 3 t chili powder
1    Garlic clove; minced, opt
Dried oregano; opt
Cumin seed; opt
3/4 lb Ground raw turkey
1    Onion; chopped
1    Bell pepper; chopped
2 c  Red beans; can, undrained
6 oz Tomato paste
1/4 c  Water
Defrost ground turkey, if frozen. Spray a large non-stick skillet with
cooking spray for no-fat frying. Spread th turkey meat in a shallow
layer. Brown the meat over moderate heat; break into chunks and turn
to brown evenly.  Stir in remaining ingredients. Cover and simmer 45
minutes. Uncover and continue to simmer until thick. The Tampa
Tribune, Feb. 1993.
