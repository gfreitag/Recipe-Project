Title: Snappy Stuffed Tomatillos
Categories: Tex/mexican, Appetizer, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
-cream cheese, softened
2 ea Green onions (with tops)
-sliced
1 t  Ground red chilies
Ground red chilies
20 ea Tomatillos or cherry
-tomatoes (1-1/4 to 1-1/2
-inches in diameter)
2/3 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Whole kernel corn
2 ea Packages (3 ounces each)
Cut a thin slice from stem ends of tomatillos.  Remove pulp and seeds
with melon baller or spoon.  Mix Cheddar cheese, corn, cream cheese,
onions and 1 tsp. ground red chilies.  Fill tomatillos with cheese
mixture. Sprinkle with ground red chilies.  Cover and refrigerate
until serving time. Garnish with cilantro and green onions if
desired.  20 appetizers. From: Betty Crocker's Low-Calorie Cooking
