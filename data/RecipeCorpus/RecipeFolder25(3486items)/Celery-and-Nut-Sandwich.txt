Title: Celery and Nut Sandwich
Categories: Main dish, Sandwich, Vegetable, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 sandwiches
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
16 sl Bread
Butter (or marg.)
Lettuce
1 c  Celery; finely chopped
1 c  Nuts; medium-chopped
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper, black
1 ts Lemon juice; freshly squeezd
Combine celry, nuts, salt, black pepper, lemon juice, and mayonnaise.
Mix well.  Spread between bread slices, whick have been spread thinly
with butter.  Place a leaf of lettuce on top of the filling for each
sandwich.
