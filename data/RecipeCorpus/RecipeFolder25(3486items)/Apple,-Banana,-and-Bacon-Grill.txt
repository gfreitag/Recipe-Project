Title: Apple, Banana, and Bacon Grill
Categories: Breakfast, Fruit, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 4 servings
Lemon juice; freshly squeezd
4    Bacon strips
8    Apple rings
1/2 c  Sugar, brown; firmly packed
1/2 ts Cinnamon, ground
4    Bananas, firm
Combine sugar and cinnamon.  Peel bananas; brush with lemon juice and
dip in sugar mixture.  Wrap bacon strip around each banana; secure
with wooden picks.  Sprinkle apple rings with remaining sugar
mixture. Place fruit on broiler rack with surface of food 3 inches
below source of heat. Broil 8 to 10 minutes, turning fruit once.
