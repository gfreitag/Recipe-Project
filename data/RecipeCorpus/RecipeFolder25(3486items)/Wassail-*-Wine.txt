Title: Wassail * Wine
Categories: Beverage, Alcohol, British
Yield: 20 servings
MMMMM-----------------------SYRUP MIXTURE----------------------------
3    Lemon slices
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
4    Cinnamon sticks
MMMMM-----------------------PUNCH MIXTURE----------------------------
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 c  Dry sherry
2    Lemons, sliced.
2 c  Pineapple juice
2 c  Orange juice
6 c  Dry red wine
Boil the syrup mixture for 5 minutes and strain.  Discard the cinnamon
sticks and those lemon slices.

Heat, but do not boil the punch ingredients

Combine with the syrup mixture, garnish with the lemon slices, and
serve hot.

Source:  The Williamsburg Cookbook Typed by Dale/Gail Shipp
