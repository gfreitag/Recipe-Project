Title: One-Pan Potatoes and Chicken Teriyaki
Categories: Vegetable, Main dish, Potato, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
-1/2" slices
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Sliced green onions
1/4 c  Prepared teriyaki sauce
4 md Potatoes, cut into thin
-wedges & microwaved 8-10
-minutes until tender
1 lb Boned and skinned chicken
-breasts, cut into long
Time: 20 minutes.

While potaotes cook, in large skillet toss and brown chicken in oil
over high heat 5 minutes. Add potatoes; saute and toss until potatoes
are lightly browned. Add onions and teriyaki sauce; toss until heated
through.

Per Serving: 280 calories, 9 grams fat, 45 milligrams cholesterol, 740
milligrams sodium, 28 grams carbohydrates, 21 grams protein.
