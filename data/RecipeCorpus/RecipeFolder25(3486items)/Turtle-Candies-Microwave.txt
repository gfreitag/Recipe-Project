Title: Turtle Candies Microwave
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
28    Caramels, unwrapped
2 tb Whipping cream
1    (12 oz) pkg semisweet
-chocolate morsels
1 1/4 c  Pecan halves
Micro chocolate in a glass bowl at high for 1 1/2 min, stirring after
1 min.  Stir until smooth; cool until slightly thickened.  Drop
chocolate by tbsp onto a wax paper- lined baking sheet, shaping into
16 (1 1/2 in. ) circles; Reserve remaining chocolate.  Arrange 4
pecans over each circle; chill till firm.  Place caramel and whipped
cream in a glass bowl. Micro at high 2 mins or until caramel melts;
stir after 1 min.

Let stand 4 min or until slightly thickened.  Spoon caramel evenly
over pecans.  Microwave remaining chocolate 1 min, stirring after 30
seconds; quickly spread chocolate over caramel mixture.  Refrig until
firm. Yield 16 candies (great valentines gifts)
