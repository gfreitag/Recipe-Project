Title: Spicy Crab Curry
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Curries, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Peanut Oil
1 tb Curry Paste
3    Scallion;chopped;
2 lb Crab;unshelled, cooked
2 c  Coconut Milk
3 tb Soy Sauce
2 ts Honey
Leaving the shell on the crab, cut it into serving size pieces and
crack each piece.
In a large sauce pan, heat peanut oil and curry paste over high
heat until mixture bubbles. Add all other ingredients except
scallions and cook 8 minutes, until crab is heated through.
Add scallions, stir, and serve over steamed rice.
