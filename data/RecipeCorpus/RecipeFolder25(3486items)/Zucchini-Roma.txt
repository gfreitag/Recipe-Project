Title: Zucchini Roma
Categories: Main dish, Italian, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Olive Oil
2    Tomato;sliced
1 c  Sour Cream
1/2 c  Parmesan Cheese
5    Zucchini;med.
1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Pepper;ground
Wash and scrub zucchini well and cut into thin round pieces.
Combine flour with half the salt, pepper and oregano in a bowl.
Coat zucchini slices in mixture.
Heat oil in a skillet and saute zucchini until brown. Place
zucchini in a greased baking dish and top with tomato.
Combine sour cream and the rest of the salt, oregano and pepper and
spread over tomato slices.
Bake 30 minutes at 350F.
