Title: Sea Foam Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 3 servings
2 ts Cream
1 c  Whipping cream, whipped
1 lg Can of pears
1 pk Lime Jello
2 sm Packages of cream cheese
Heat 1 cup of pear juice and pour over 1 package of lime Jello.  Stir
until dissolved.  While hot, add 2 small packages of cream cheese,
which has been mashed with 2 teaspoons of cream.  When cool and
thick, add mashed pears and 1 cup of cream which has been whipped.
Keep refrigerated until served. Randy Rigg
