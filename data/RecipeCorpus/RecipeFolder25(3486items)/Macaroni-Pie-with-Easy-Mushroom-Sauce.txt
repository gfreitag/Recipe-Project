Title: Macaroni Pie with Easy Mushroom Sauce
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Mushroom, Microwave, Pasta
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Shredded sharp Cheddar
-cheese (about 3 ounces)
2 ea Eggs, slightly beaten
1/2 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
1/3 c  Dry bread crumbs
2 T  Grated Parmesan cheese
2 T  Margarine or butter
1 ea Package (7 ounces) macaroni
-rings, cooked and drained
1 c  Creamed cottage cheese
Mix bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese and margarine in 2-cup measure.
Microwave uncovered on high (100%), stirring every 30 seconds, until
light brown, 1-1/2 to 2 minutes; reserve. Mix hot macaroni, cottage
cheese, Cheddar cheese, eggs, salt and pepper. Turn into greased
9-inch pie plate. Cover with waxed paper and microwave on medium
(50%) 8 minutes. Sprinkle crumbs over pie.  Microwave uncovered on
medium (50%) until center is almost set, 8 to 10 minutes.  Let stand
10 minutes.  Serve with easy Mushroom sauce. Easy Mushroom Sauce 1
can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup 1 can ( 2
ounces) mushroom stems and pieces, drained 1/4 cup milk 1 Tablespoon
margarine or butter 1 teaspoon instant minced onion 1/8 teaspoon
Worcestershire sauce Mix all ingredients in 4-cup measure. Microwave
uncovered on high (100%), stirring every minute until hot and bubbly,
3 to 4 minutes. From: Betty Crocker's Microwave Cookbook Shared By:
Pat Stockett
