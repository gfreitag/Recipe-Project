Title: Ginger Cheese Gems
Categories: Breakfast, Muffin, Sthrn/livng, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 14 muffins
1/2 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  Butter (or marg.); melted
1 c  Cheese, Cheddar; shredded
1/4 c  Raisins, seedless; or dates
2 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ginger, ground
1    Egg; well beaten
Combine flour, baking powder, soda, salt, and ginger in large mixing
bowl. Mix egg, molasses, and buttermilk together, and add to dry
ingredients along with melted butter, shredded chees, and raisins.
Mix only until dry ingredients are moistened.  Fill greased muffin
pans two-thirds full. Bake at 400 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.
