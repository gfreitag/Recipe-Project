Title: Pizza Hors D'oeuvres
Categories: Appetizer, Pizza, Sausage, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1 lb Velveeta cheese
2    Loaves of Pumpernickle bread
1 lb Hot sausage
1 lb Mild sausage
1 ts Italian seasoning
Crumble sausage into pan; add seasoning and onions.  Cook until done.
Drain grease and add cheese.  Simmer until well mixed.  Spread on
bread. You can freeze them ahead.  Then just place in the oven at 425
degrees and bake for about 10 minutes. Randy Rigg
