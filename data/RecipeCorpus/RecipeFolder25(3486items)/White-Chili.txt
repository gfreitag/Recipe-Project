Title: White Chili
Categories: Turkey, Vegetable, Main dish, Chili
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 c  Flour
2 T  Ground coriander
3 T  Ground cumin
2 cn Cannelini beans
3 lb Ground turkey
2 ea Red onions, diced
1 bn Celery, sliced
4 ea Leeks, cleaned and diced
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
3 ea Qts chicken broth
3 T  Chili powder
2 t  Sugar
4 ea Cloves garlic, minced
3 oz Diced green chili peppers
2 ea Red bell peppers, diced
2 t  Oregano
In a skillet, brown the ground turkey with the garlic and onions.
Drain off the fat.  Place the turkey mixture in a stock pot over
medium heat. Add the next 6 ingredients and stir until the flour is
smooth- cooked. Add the stock and stir until the mixture is
thickened. Add the remaining ingredients and simmer for 1 hour.
