Title: Chicken & Potatoes in Sauce
Categories: Chicken, Potato, Main dish, Crockpot, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Potatoes
8    Chicken thighs
Spaghetti Sauce
1 md Onion
Place potatoes and onion at bottom of cooker.  Put in chicken thighs.
Pour sauce on top.  Cook.
