Title: California Corn Bread Mix with Recipe
Categories: Mixes, Bread
Yield: 3 servings
1 pk Mix
2    Eggs
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Butter, melted
2 c  Biquick
1/2 c  Cornmeal
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
-CORN BREAD:
Mix: Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.

California Corn Bread: Combine all ingredients until smooth. Bake at
400 in a greased 8" square pan 30 minutes. 4-6 servings.
