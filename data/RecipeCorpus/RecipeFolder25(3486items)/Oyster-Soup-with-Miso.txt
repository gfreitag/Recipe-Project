Title: Oyster Soup with Miso
Categories: Soup/stew, Japanese, Asian, Clam/oyster
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 ts Cornstarch
-Cold water
1/2 ts Crushed red pepper
3 1/2 oz Miso (soybean paste)
4 c  Dashi (Japenese bouillon
-stock)
8 oz Shucked oysters
Blend miso and dashi together in saucepan; bring just to a boil. Add
oysters and cook until oysters are plump and tender. Mix cornstarch
with just enough water to make a xommth paste. Add to oyster mixture;
cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens. Simmer
until soup is well heated. Season with crushed red pepper and serve.
