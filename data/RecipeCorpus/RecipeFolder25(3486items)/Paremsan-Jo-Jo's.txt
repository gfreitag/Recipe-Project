Title: Paremsan Jo-Jo's
Categories: Potato, Cheese/eggs, Appetizer, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese, freshly
-grated
Salt & pepper to taste
6 sm Baking potatoes, scrub and
-cut into 8 wedges
2 T  Butter
Preheat the broiler.  Line a broiler pan with aluminum foil.  Bring a
large pot of water to a boil over high heat. Drop the potatoes into
the boiling water and partially cover the pot. When the boil returns,
cook for 4 minutes, drain.  Toss in a large bowl with the butter.
Arrange the potato wedges in a single layer on the prepared pan.
Sprinkle half of the parmesan cheese on potatoes.  Broil for 5 - 10
minutes, until deep golden brown. Judge by color.  Turn the potatoes
and sprinkle with the remaining parmesan.  Broil for 5-8 minutes
longer, until deep golden brown. Serve hot with salt and pepper to
taste. May serve with ranch type dip or sour cream if desired. Shared
by: Bill Jernigan
