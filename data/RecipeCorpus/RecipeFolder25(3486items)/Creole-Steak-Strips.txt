Title: Creole Steak Strips
Categories: Cajun/creol, Meat, Vegetable, Main dish, Beef
Yield: 6 servings
1 md Green Pepper, chopped
1    (10-oz.) package frozen or
1 1/2 c  Fresh Okra
1    (2 1/2-oz.) can sliced
Mushrooms, drained
Carrot Curls (optional)
1 1/2 lb Boneless Round Steak
Salt & Pepper
1    Onion, chopped
1 c  Sliced Celery
1 c  Seasoned Tomato Juice
2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/8 ts Garlic Powder
Cut steaks into strips about 1/2-inch wide and 2 inches long.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Place in slow-cooking pot with onion,
celery, tomato juice, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic powder. Cover
and cook on low 6-8 hours. Turn control to high. Add green peppers
and partially thawed okra and mushrooms. Cover and cook on high for
30 minutes or until okra is done. Garnish with carrot curls if
desired. Serve over rice. Submitted By MICHELLE BRUCE   On   11-25-94
