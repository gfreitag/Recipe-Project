Title: Martha's Special Scones
Categories: Breakfast, Muffin, Fruit
Yield: 8 wedges
6 tb Milk
1/4 c  Butter or margarine, melted
Softened butter (opt) and
Sugar (opt)
2 pk Martha White Muffin Mix
-(7 oz ea; Blueberry,
-Strawberry, Apple Cinnamon,
-Blackberry or Raspberry)
Preheat oven to 400'F. Combine Muffin Mix, milk and melted butter;
stir until mixture forms a soft dough. Turn onto lightly floured
board or pastry cloth and knead gently, just until smooth. Pat out
into a 6" round. Brush with softened butter and sprinkle with sugar.
Cut into 8 wedges with sharp knife. Place on greased baking sheet
about 1" apart. Bake 14-16 minutes or until golden brown.
