Title: Broccoli with Bacon
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Walnuts, coarsely chopped
1/2 c  Onions, green; sliced
2 lb Broccoli, fresh
6 sl Bacon
Remove broccoli leaves, and cut off tough ends of stalks; discard.
WAsh broccoli, and cut into spears.

Cook in a small amoung of boiling water 6 to 8 minutes or just until
tender; drain.  Keep warm in a serving dish.

Cook, reserving 2 tablespoons drippings in skillet.  Crumble bacon,
and set aside.

Cook walnuts, if desired, in drippings over medium-high heat, stirring
constantly, 3 minutes.  Add sliced green onions, and cook, stirring
constantly, 2 minutes.  Spoon over broccoli.  Sprinkle with bacon.
