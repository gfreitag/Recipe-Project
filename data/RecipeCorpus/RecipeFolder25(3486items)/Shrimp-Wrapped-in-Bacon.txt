Title: Shrimp Wrapped in Bacon
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
8 sl Bacon
8 lg Shrimp, cleaned
Preheat oven to 350. Wrap shrimp with bacon; fasten with toothpicks.
Place in baking pan; bake 15-20 minutes. Remove from oven, drain and
serve.
