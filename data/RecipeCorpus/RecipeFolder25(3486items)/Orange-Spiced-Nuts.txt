Title: Orange Spiced Nuts
Categories: Appetizer, Nuts/grains, Holiday
Yield: 1 batch
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 ts Grated orange peel
1/4 ts Ground RED pepper
1 cn 12 oz mixed nuts
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Margarine
In a two quart glass measure, combine mixed nuts, sugar, margarine and
Worcestershire sauce.  Microwave on high 5 to 6 minutes until nuts are
browned, stirring at two minute intervals.  Stir in orange peel and
red pepper.

Spread on cookie sheet to cool.
