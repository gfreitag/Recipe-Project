Title: Apple Cookie Squares
Categories: Dessert, Sthrn/livng, Apples, Cake/cookie
Yield: 30 squares
-chopped
2 c  Flour, all-purpose
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon, ground
Whipped cream (opt.)
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
1    Apple, Winesap; coarsely
Cream shortening and sugar.  Add eggs, one at a time, beating well
after each addition.  Add vanilla, nuts, and apple, mix well. Combine
dry ingredients and add to creamed mixture; mix well.

Bake in a greased and floured 13x9x2" pan at 350 degrees for about 30
minutes.  Cool and cut in squares.  Serve with whipped cream, if
desired.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, March 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
