Title: Rasberry Dessert
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 dessert
2    Egg whites
1 c  White sugar
1 pk Rasberries frozen
1 c  35% cream
1 c  Sifted flour
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Melted butter
Mix flour br. sugar, walnuts, butter together.  Place in pan in 350
oven 20 minutes. Stir often to brown. Reserve some crumbs for top.

Beat 2 egg whites, sugar.  Add rasberries.  Fold in whipping cream,
beaten.

Crumbs on bottom & crumbs on top.
