Title: Sauteed Liver
Categories: Main dish, Meat
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Flour
2    Onion;sliced;med.
1/2 c  White Wine
1 lb Liver;calf
2 tb Butter
2 tb Salad Oil
Slice liver 3/4-inch thick. Season with salt and pepper and coat
with flour. In skillet heat butter and oil. Add onions and saute'
about 3 minutes. Remove onions. Add liver and fry over high heat,
about 3 minutes each side.
Replace onions; add wine. Turn heat up very hot; cook 1 minute.
Remove from heat and serve immediately.
