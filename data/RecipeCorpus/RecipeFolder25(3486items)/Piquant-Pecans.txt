Title: Piquant Pecans
Categories: Appetizer, Nuts/grains, Holiday
Yield: 3 cups
1 1/2 ts Hot Sauce (or to taste)
3 c  Pecan halves
3 tb Butter or margarine
3    Cloves garlic, minced
In a small skillet, met butter of margarine.  Add garlic and hot
sauce, cook one minute.  Add pecans and toss to coat with mixture.
Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet; bake at 250 degrees for
one hour, stirring occasionally.  Pecans will crisp when cool.
