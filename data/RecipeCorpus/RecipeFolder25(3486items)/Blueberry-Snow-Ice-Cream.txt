Title: Blueberry Snow Ice Cream
Categories: Ice cream, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Undiluted evaporated milk
1 t  Vanilla
Snow, new-fallen is best
2 ea Eggs
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Blueberries
Beat eggs, add milk, sugar and vanilla. Fold in snow until the mix
has the consistancy of (surprise) ice creamIngredients: Fold in the
berries. You might have to place it in the freezer for a bit to get
the consistancy right.
