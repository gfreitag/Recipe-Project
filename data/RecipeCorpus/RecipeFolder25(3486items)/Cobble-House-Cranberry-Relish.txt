Title: Cobble House Cranberry Relish
Categories: Relish, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
-chopped
1 ea Pear, peeled, cored and
-chopped
1 c  Chopped dates
1 c  Golden raisins
1/2 c  Maple syrup
1/4 c  Southern Comfort
3 T  Cider vinegar
2 lb Fresh or frozen
-cranberries, picked over
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Water
2 ea Navel oranges
2 c  Peeled, cored and chopped
-fresh pineapple, or canned
2 md Apples, peeled, cored and
Mix cranberries, sugar and water in a large nonaluminum pot and bring
to a boil.  Simmer until thick, stirring about 20 minutes. Meanwhile,
remove zest from oranges with a vegetable peeler and mince. Remove
and discard pith from oranges and chop fruit.  Place chopped oranges
and zest in a large bowl, add remaining ingredients and toss well.
Add to cranberries and simmer for 15 minutes, or until fruit is
softened. Makes 12 cups. This can be kept for a month, tightly
covered, in the refrigerator From: Sweet Maple by James M. Lawrence
and Rux Martin Asbury Park Press, 11/2/94 Shared By: Pat Stockett
