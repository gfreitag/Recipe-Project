Title: Hash-Browned Potato Casserole
Categories: Potato, Casserole, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 4 SERVINGS--------------------------
4 oz Colby cheese
1/4 c  Onion; diced
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 ea Pk frozen shredded potatoes;
-(12 or 14 oz)
2 T  Margarine; melted
Salt & pepper; to taste
Mix all thoroughly and pour into a casserole.  Bake in a pre-heated
350F oven until brown (about 25 - 35 min). As printed in "Naples (FL)
Daily News" Thurs. March 7, 1991.
