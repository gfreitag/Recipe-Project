Title: Raisin Coconut Loaf
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 1 loaf
1 c  Coconut, flaked; toasted
1    Egg
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
3 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 tb Baking powder
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 c  Raisins, dark or golden
Combine flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.  Stir in raisins and
coconut.  Beat egg until foamy and mix with milk and vanilla.  Stir
into dry ingredients and mix thoroughly, but do not beat.  Pour into
greased 9x5x3" loafpan, and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 10
minutes.
