Title: Buffalo Fire Flings
Categories: Chicken, Snack, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
Full Bag (5 lbs)
1 ea Bag WingFlings
1 c  Margarine
1 1/2 c  Frank's
Louisiana Hot Sauce*
4 pk (6 oz. ea.)
Dry Italian Salad
Dressing mix
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 t  Dry basil
1/2 ea Bag (2-1/2 lbs.)
1/2 ea Bag WingFlings
1/2 c  Margarine
1/2 ea - 3/4 cup Frank's
Louisiana Hot Sauce*
2 pk (6 oz. ea.)
Dry Italian Salad
Dressing mix
2 T  Lemon juice
1/2 t  Dry basil
Fry unbreaded WingFlings.  Preheat oil to 350.  Fry (15) wings for 10
to 12 minutes.  Fry smaller amounts in mini-fryer. (Thawed,
WingFlings will cook in 7 to 9 minutes.)  Melt margarine and stir in
remaining ingredients. Coat cooked WingFlings with sauce and bake 10
minutes with sauce and bake 10 minutes in oven at 350.  Serve with
Bleu Cheese dressing, celery sticks, and carrot sticks. * May
substitute another pepper sauce.
