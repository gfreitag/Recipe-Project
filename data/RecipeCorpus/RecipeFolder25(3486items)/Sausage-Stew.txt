Title: Sausage Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Sausage, Potato, Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 md White onion quartered
4 lg Carrots peeled/chopped
1 lb Mushrooms cleaned and cut
-in half
2 cn Diced tomatoes (12 oz)
12 sm Whole new potatoes
1 cn Tomato sauce (8 oz)
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
1 lb Hillshire Farm Polska
-Kielbasa or any variety
-Hillshire Farm Smoked
-Sausage
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 t  Fennel
2    Garlic cloves finely chopped
1 tb Oregano
1 c  Celery chopped
Heat the oil in a heavy pan and add the onions, garlic, celery,
fennel, salt and pepper. Saute until soft. Add all other ingredients
except for the sausage, and cook for 20 minutes over low heat. Add
sausage, simmer for another 15 minutes or until heated through.
