Title: Broccoli with Basil Mushrooms
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 tb Chopped fresh basil
1 cn Green Giant Whole Mushrooms,
-drained (4.5 oz)
1 pk Green giant Select Frozen
-Broccoli Spears (16 oz)
2 tb Butter
1. Cook Broccoli Spears according to package directions; drain well.

2. Meanwhile, melt butter and small saucepan; add basil and
mushrooms. Cook and stir until thoroughly heated. Spoon over broccoli.
