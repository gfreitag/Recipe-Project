Title: Squirrel Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Game
Yield: 2 pounds
2 tb Toasted pine nuts
1/8 ts Mace
1/8 ts Cardamom
1/8 ts Allspice
2    Feet small sheep casings
2 lb Squirrel meat
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts White pepper
Prepare casings. Grind meat through the fine disk. Mix with remaining
ingredients. Grind again and stuff into casings. Twist off into 2"
links resembling cocktail franks. cook by steaming or simmering for
about 30 minutes. Serve warm or cold.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
