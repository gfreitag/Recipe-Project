Title: Grannie's Peanut Butter Cookies *
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 7 dozen
2    Eggs, beaten
1 1/4 c  Flour, all purpose
1 c  Bran
3/4 c  Oats, rolled
2 t  Baking soda
1 c  Butter/margarine
3/4 c  Sugar, brown
3 c  Sugar, granulated
1 t  Vanilla
1 c  Peanut butter, creamy or
-chunky
Preparation time:  5 to 10 minutes Baking time: 15 to 18 minutes Oven
temperature:  350

Melt butter.  Beat together with the sugars, vanilla, penut butter and
eggs.

In a separate bowl, combine the flour, bran, oats and baking soda.
Stir mixture into butter mixture.

Drop by teaspoons onto ungreassed cookie sheet.  Bake at 350F for 15
to 18 minutes.  Remove to rack to cool.
