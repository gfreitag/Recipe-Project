Title: Holiday Double Chocolate Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday, Chocolate
Yield: 42 cookies
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
2    Eggs
1 1/3 c  (10 oz pkg) Hershey's
-Holiday Candy Coated Bits;
;divided
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  (1 stick) butter or
-margarine; softened
3/4 c  Light brown sugar; packed
Heat oven to 350F. Stir together flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
IN large mixer bowl, beat butter, brown sugar, sugar, and vanilla
until well blended. Add eggs; beat well. Gradually add flour mixture,
blending well. Stir in 1 cup candy coated bits. Drop by rounded
teaspoonfuls onto lightly greased cookie sheet. Press 8 to 9 of
remaining bits on dough before baking. Bake 7 to 9 minutes or until
cookies are set. Do not overbake. Cool slightly; remove from cookie
sheet to wire rack. Cool completely. Submitted By NANCY VAINE   On
10-24-94
