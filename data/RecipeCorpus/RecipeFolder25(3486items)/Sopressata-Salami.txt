Title: Sopressata Salami
Categories: Beef, Pork, Sausage, Homemade, Italian
Yield: 10 pounds
1 ts Finely minced garlic
2 ts Sugar
1 c  Dry white wine
1/2 ts Ascorbic acid
1 ts Saltpeter
4    Feet large beef casings
3 lb Lean beef
5 lb Pork
2 lb Pork fat
5 tb Salt
1 tb Finely ground black pepper
2 ts Finely ground white pepper
1 ts Finely ground coriander
Grind the beef, pork, and fat separately. Grind to a fine powder the
remaining ingredinets except the wine, garlic and casings. Mix in a
large pan with the meats and fat; cure in the refrigerator 24 hours.
Prepare casings, stuff and tie off into 6-7" length. Dry 8-12 weeks.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
