Title: Braised Cabbage
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
Salt;
Pepper;
2 tb Vinegar
1 1/2 lb Cabbage;shredded
3    Carrot;grated;
3 tb Butter
4    Chives;stalks
In skillet heat butter until foamy. Add chives and saute until
tender. Add green cabbage and carrots.
Saute over high heat until tender but crisp, about 4 minutes. Add
vinegar, salt and pepper to taste.
