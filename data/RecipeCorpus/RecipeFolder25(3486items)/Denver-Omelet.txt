Title: Denver Omelet
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Jack Cheese;shredded
1/2 c  Green Pepper;diced
1/4 tb Sage
4    Egg;
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Ham;diced
1/2 c  Onion;diced
Mix eggs well. Combine with ham, onion, peppers and cheese. Melt
butter in a saute pan, cook on one side for 3 minutes. Turn over,
cook briefly and serve.
