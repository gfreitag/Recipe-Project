Title: Sauce a la Chambord
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fish, Crab/shrimp, Clam/oyster
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
6 ea Mushrooms, sliced thin
2 ea Parsley, sprigs
2 ea Cloves, mashed
4 ea Allspice, ground
1 ea Crawfish tails, chopped-OPT
12 ea Oysters, chopped-OPT
1 T  Butter
1 ea Onion, large, chopped
1 ea Thyme, sprig
1 ea Bay leaf
3 ea Tomatoes, large
1 ea Pt oyster liquor
1 ea Salt & pepper to taste
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Brown onion in butter being careful not to burn. Put in 3 large
tomatoes and add chopped herbs. Brown. Now add a pint oyster liquor
which has been heated by blanching the oysters. Season sauce with
salt and pepper to taste. Recommended for Baked Grouper.
