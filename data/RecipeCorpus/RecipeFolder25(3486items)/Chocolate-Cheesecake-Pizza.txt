Title: Chocolate Cheesecake Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Chocolate, Cheese/eggs, Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 4 SERVINGS--------------------------
3 ea Tb Icing sugar
2 ea Tb Cocoa
2 ea Tb Milk
2 c  Strawberries, sliced
1 ea Pre-baked pizza crust
2 ea Tb Butter
1 ea Tb Granulated sugar
250 g  Cream cheese (package)
Dessert Pizza, why not? It's easy to make and it's a great finish for
a summer meal. Top with peaches, blueberries, plums or nectarines.
PREHEAT oven to 425F. Butter cookie sheet. Place on pizza crust and
butter it. Sprinkle with sugar. Bake 7 minutes or until crisp. Chill.
BEAT together remaining ingredients except strawberries, adding
enough milk to make a spreadable consistency. Spread on pizza base.
TOP with strawberries or other fruit. Serves 4.
