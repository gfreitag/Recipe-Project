Title: Turkey Tortilla Pizza
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Pizza, Turkey
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Fat Free Mozzarella Shreds
1 pk Healthy Choice Oven Roasted
1    Small green bell pepper
3/4 c  Tomato
1 t  Dried basil
1 t  Dried oregano
1/4 t  Garlic powder
2 t  Olive oil
6    Flour tortilla
1.  In a custard cup, combine basil, oregano and garlic powder with
olive oil.  Brush one side of each tortilla with olive oil mixture;
top with 2 tablespoons cheese. 2. Equally divide turkey, green pepper
and tomato; sprinkle over tortillas. 3. Bake at 400 degrees F. for 8
minutes, or microwave each tortilla pizza individually on medium-high
for 2 minutes. Submitted By ELIZABETH RODIER   On   11-17-94
