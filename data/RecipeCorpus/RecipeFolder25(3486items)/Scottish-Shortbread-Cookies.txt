Title: Scottish Shortbread Cookies
Categories: Bread, Cake/cookie, British
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------1/4    CUP    EACH: CONFECTI---------------------
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 1/4 c  Butter, refrigerated and
-cut into 8 to 10 pieces
Preheat oven to 275.  Place sugars in food processor and process
until very fine.  Add butter; pulse until sugar disappears.  add
flour; pulse until no dry flour particles remain.  Mixture will be
moist and crumbly. Place in plastic bag and press together to form
fough; knead lightly until dough holds together.  Remove from bag.
Shape dough into 1 inch balls. Place on unbuttered cookie sheets 2
inches apart.  Flatten each with a cookie press, fork or the bottom
of a glass lightly moistened with water. Bake 45 minutes to 1 hour or
until pale golden.  Remove from cookie sheets; cool completely on
wire racks.  Yield: 4 dozen Source: Rose's Christmas Cookies Shared
by: Marie Culver
