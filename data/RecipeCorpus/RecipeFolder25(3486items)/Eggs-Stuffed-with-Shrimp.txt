Title: Eggs Stuffed with Shrimp
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Cheese/eggs, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 8 servings
4 1/2 oz Can shrimp, drained,
-reserving liquid
8    Hard boiled eggs, halved
-lengthwise
2 tb Butter, softened
Remove yolks from eggs and mash with butter. Add enough shrimp liquid
to make a smooth paste. Fill egg halves with shrimp paste. Top with
as many shrimp as they will hold. Refrigerate until well chilled and
serve.
