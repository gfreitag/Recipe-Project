Title: Pumpkin Date Bars
Categories: Pumpkin, Fruit, Snack
Yield: 1 servings
-walnuts, sunflower seeds
-or dried
Pumpkin seeds
1 ea Sheet Pumpkin Pie leather,
-not dried
1/2 c  Finely chopped dates
1/2 c  Chopped pecans, almonds,
Prepare one tray of undried Pumpkin pie leather.  Sprinkle with dates
and nuts or seeds.  Dry 10 to 12 hours, or until mixture will pull
away from liner.  Using kitchen shears, cut into 1-1/2 by 2-1/2 inch
bars. Yield: approximately 30 bars. From: Food Drying at Home, The
Natural Way Shared By: Pat Stockett
