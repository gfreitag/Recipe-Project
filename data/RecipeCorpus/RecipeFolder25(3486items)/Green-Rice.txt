Title: Green Rice
Categories: Side dish, Rice, Sthrn/livng, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
2/3 c  Water
2 c  Rice, regular; uncooked
ds Garlic powder
ds Parsley flakes
Salt; to taste
Pepper; to taste
1 c  Onion; chopped
1 c  Celery; finely chopped
6 tb Margarine; melted
10 oz Broccoli, chopped; thawed
8 oz Cheese, pasteurized process;
-cubed
10 3/4 oz Soup, cream of mushroom
Saute onion and celery in melted margarine until clear.  Add broccoli
and cheese; heat until cheese melts.  Add remaining ingredients and
blend well. Spoon into a 2-quart casserole dish; cover and bake at
350 degrees for 45 minutes.  Uncover; reduce heat to 300 degrees and
bake an additional 10 minutes.
