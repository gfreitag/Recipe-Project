Title: Campbell's Sauteed Chicken Breasts
Categories: Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Vegetable oil
1 cn (10oz) Campbell's condensed
-Italian Style Tomato Soup
1/2 c  Water
2 T  All purpose flour
1/8 t  Pepper
4 ea Skinless boneless, chicken
-breasts
Combine flour and pepper.  Coat chicken lightly with flour mixture. In
large skillet over medium high heat.  Heat oil.  Brown chicken 5
minutes on each side.  Remove chicken from skillet, set aside.  Pour
off fat. In same skillet, combine soup and water.  Bring to boil.
Return chicken to skillet.  Reduce heat to low.  Cover, simmer 5
minutes or until chicken is no longer pink, stirring occasionally.
Makes 4 servings. Origin: Campbell's Soup Ad, Canadian Living,
Nov/94. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
