Title: Tomato-Grits Casseroles
Categories: Nuts/grains, Side dish, Vegetable, Casserole
Yield: 6 - 8 folks
3/4 c  Sugar; (or equal to 3/4 cup
- of sugar substitute)
1/4 c  Butter
-=OR=-
1/4 c  Margarine;
1/2 c  Quick-cooking grits;
2 c  Boiling water;
1 ts Salt;
1 qt Stewed tomatoes; fresh or
-canned
Add grits to rapidly boiling water to which salt has been added; stir
well and cook until thick, stirring often.  Combine tomatoes, sugar,
and butter; heat.  Combine with grits, blending well. Simmer gently
for 5 minutes. Source: Southern Living, Our Best Recipes. Brought to
you and your via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
