Title: Date N Nut Drops
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Lightly packed brown sugar
2 ea Eggs
2 t  Vanilla
1 c  Oatmeal
3/4 c  Coconut
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 1/4 t  Cinnamon
1/4 t  Nutmeg
A spicy old fashioned
-favourite drop cookie
-that's loaded with good
Taste.
3 c  All purpose flour
1 t  Baking soda
1 lb Dates, chopped
1 c  Shortening (crisco)
Toss flour, baking soda and dates together to coat dates.  Cream
shortening, sugar, eggs, and vanilla together in large bowl on medium
speed of electric mixer until light and creamy.  Stir in remaining
ingredients with date mixture.  Mix well.  Drop dough by tbsp's onto
greased baking sheet, leaving room for spreading.  Bake at 375F for
8-10 minutes, or until light golden.  Cool slightly, then remove to
cooling rack. Store in airtight container. Origin: Baking Festival
1994 Recipes, by Robin Hood and Baker's. Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
Nov/94.
