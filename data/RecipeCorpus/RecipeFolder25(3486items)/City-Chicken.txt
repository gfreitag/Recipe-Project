Title: City Chicken
Categories: Meat, Veal, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------SERVING SIZE  : 6  PREPARATI---------------------
1 ea Egg ++ slightly beaten
2 ea Tb milk
3 ea Tb shortening
1 t  Chicken bouillon
1/2 c  Boiling water
2 lb Veal ++ cut into 1 1/2"cubes
2/3 c  Cracker crumbs ++ fine
1 1/2 t  Salt
1 t  Paprika
3/4 t  Poultry seasoning
Thread veal cubes onto 6 wooden skewers. Combine crumbs, salt,
paprika, and poultry seasoning. Combine egg and milk. Dip veal into
egg mixture, then into crumb mixture to coat. Brown slowly on all
side in hot shortening. Dissolve bouillon in boiling water, add to
meat. Cover and bake at 350-degrees for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake
for another 30 minutes. Originally Posted By: RANDY POLLAK Posted By:
