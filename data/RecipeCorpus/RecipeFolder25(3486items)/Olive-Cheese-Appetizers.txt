Title: Olive Cheese Appetizers
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Spread
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 ts Paprika
1 c  Flour
60 sm Olives
1/2    Stick of butter, softened
5 oz Jar of sharp cheese spread
1/2 ts Onion powder
Beat butter and cheese together.  Add onion, paprika and flour; mix
well. Wrap enough dough around each olive to cover it, about 1/2
teaspoon. Chill 2 hours.  Place on a lightly greased baking sheet.
Bake at 400 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes. Randy Rigg
