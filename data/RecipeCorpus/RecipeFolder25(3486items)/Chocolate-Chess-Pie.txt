Title: Chocolate Chess Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert, Chocolate
Yield: 1 pie
3 1/2 tb Cocoa
1/2 cn Carnation milk
1 ea Unbaked 9" pie shell
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 ea Beaten eggs
1 ts Vanilla
Combine all ingreds. and pour into pie shell.  Bake at 325 F. for
45 min.
