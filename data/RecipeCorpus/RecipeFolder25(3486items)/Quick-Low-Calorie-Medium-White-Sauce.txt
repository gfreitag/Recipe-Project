Title: Quick Low Calorie Medium White Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 c  Cold Milk
3/4 c  Skim Milk
1 tb Cornstarch
Heat skim milk to boiling.  Gradually stir in cornstarch MIXED with
cold milk.  Mixture will thicken quickly. Season as you wish.

Protein: 8 grams; Calories: 125

Source: La Leche League International's Whole Food For The Whole
Family Cookbook Submitted by Roberta Bishop Johnson
