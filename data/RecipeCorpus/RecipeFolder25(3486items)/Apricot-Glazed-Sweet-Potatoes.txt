Title: Apricot Glazed Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Fruit, Side dish, Vegetable, Sweetpotato
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Shredded orange peel
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Brown sugar
6 ea Medium sweet potatoes,
-cooked and sliced
1 cn Apricots(16oz), reserve 1
-cup syrup
1/2 c  Pecans
1 1/2 tb Cornstarch
Arrange sweet potatoes in a buttered 9x13 inch baking dish.  Combine
brown sugar, cornstarch, salt, cinnamon, orange peel and butter; add
apricot juice and bring to a boil.  Remove from heat and pour over
sweet potatoes. Arrange apricots and pecans over sweet potatoes. Bake
at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
