Title: Giant's Holiday Bake
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Casserole, Holiday
Yield: 5 servings
-(10 3/4 oz)
1/3 c  Milk
1 cn French fried onion rings
-(2.8 oz)
1 pk Green Giant American
-Mixtures Heartland Style
-Frozen Vegetables (16 oz)
1 cn Cream of broccoli soup
1. In 2-quart microwave-safe casserole combine vegetables, soup, milk
and half of the onion rings.

2. Microwave on HIGH for 10-12 minutes (or bake in 375'F. oven for
30-35 minutes) until vegetables are crisp tender, stirring
occasionally.

3. Sprinkle with remaining onion rings before serving.
