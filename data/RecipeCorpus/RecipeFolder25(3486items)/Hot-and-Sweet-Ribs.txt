Title: Hot and Sweet Ribs
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meat, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Honey
1 tb Soy Sauce
1 tb Mustard
1 tb Lemon Juice
3 lb Short Ribs
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
1 tb Garlic;minced
2 ts Orange Peel;grated
2 ts Ginger;grated fresh
Trim excess fat from ribs.  Lightly score top of ribs.  Combine
1/4 cup soy sauce, garlic, orange rind, ginger and black pepper.
Marinate ribs in mixture for 8 hours or overnight, turning
occasionally.
Combine honey, 1 T soy sauce, mustard and lemon juice in small
bowl; set aside. Take ribs from marinade; place on grill 4-5' from
medium-low coals. Grill 15-18 minutes, turning and basting with
marinade.  Brush ribs with honey baste mixture.  Continue grilling
2-3 minutes or until desired doneness.
