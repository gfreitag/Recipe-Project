Title: Blue-Cheese Mushrooms
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Appetizer
Yield: 4 - 6 folks
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Blue cheese; crumbled
1/3 c  Bread crumbs; fine dry
12 lg To 14 lg fresh mushrooms;
-=OR=- canned mushrooms
1/4 c  Green onion; chopped
1/4 c  Margarine; -=OR=-
Remove stems from mushrooms; and chop stems.  Cook stems and onions in
butter till tender, but not brown.  Add cheese, 2 tablespoons of the
crumbs, and salt and pepper to taste. Fill mushrooms crowns with
mixture; sprinkle with remaining crumbs.  Place no baking sheet. Bake
in miderate oven 350 degrees for 12 minutes for fresh mushrooms or 8
minutes for canned.  Makes 4 to 6 servings. Source: Better Homes &
Gardens Vegetable Cook Book, by WHOM in 1968. Brought to you and
yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
