Title: Hersey House's Gingerbread Pancakes
Categories: Kids, Breakfast, Pancakes
Yield: 8 grandsons
1/4 c  Molasses;
2 c  Milk;
2    Eggs; lightly beaten
6 tb Butter; melted
1 c  Raisins;
2 1/2 c  All-purpose flour;
5 ts Baking powder;
1 1/2 ts Salt;
1 ts Cinnamon;
1/2 ts Ginger;
Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, soda and spices.  Combine
molasses, milk and eggs.  Stir to blend.  Stir in melted butter.  Add
molasses mixture to dry ingredients.  Stir only until moistened.  Mix
in raisins.  Cook on hot griddle, using 1/4 cup batter for each
pancakes. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune Food Section, Nov. 3,
