Title: Syrian Rice Pilaf Mix with Recipe
Categories: Mixes, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Turmeric
-RICE:
2 tb Butter
1 pk Rice mix
2 1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Orzo
2 tb Dried minced onion
2    Crumbled chicken bouillon
-cubes
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 c  Long grain rice
Mix: Combine and cover in an airtight container.

Syrian Rice Pilaf: Melt butter. Add rice mix, stirring to coat the
rice and pasta. Add water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and
simmer 20 minutes, until pilaf is tender and all water is absorbed. 8
servings.
