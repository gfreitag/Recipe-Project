Title: Chestnut Meatballs
Categories: Beef, Pork, Ground beef, Sausage
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Onion powder
1/2 c  Milk
9 oz Sweet & sour sauce
1 1/2 c  Bread crumbs; soft
1/2 lb Pork sausage; bulk
1/2 lb Groundbeef
8 oz Water chestnuts; chopped
1 tb Soy sauce
Combine all ingredients excedpt sweet & sour sauce; mix well and
shape into 1 inch balls.  Place in a lightly greased 13x9x2 inch
baking dish; bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes. Drain
meatballs and return to baking dish.  Add sauce; stir lightly to coat
meatballs. Bake meatballs and sauce an additional 15 minutes. Yield:
3 dozen.
