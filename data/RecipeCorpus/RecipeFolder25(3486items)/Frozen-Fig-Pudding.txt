Title: Frozen Fig Pudding
Categories: Dessert, Pudding, Fruit, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Sugar
1/4 lb Figs, dried; chopped
1/4 lb Walnuts; chopped
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 tb Vanilla extract
2 c  Milk
3    Egg yolks; beaten
1 pk Gelatin, unflavored
1/4 c  Cream
1/3 ts Salt
Scald milk and add to beaten egg yolks.  Sprinkle gelatin in cream;
let stand for 5 minutes.  Add egg mixture to disolved gelatin
mixture. Add salt, sugar, figs, walnuts, orange juice, and vanilla.
Stir well; pour into freezer tray.  Put in freezer.  Stir once or
twice during freezing process.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, March 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
