Title: Green Onion Dressing
Categories: Cajun/creol, Condiment, Dressing, Salad
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 t  Sugar
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  White pepper
1 T  Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Vegetable oil
1    Egg yolk
1 c  Chopped green onions
3 T  White vinegar
1 T  Creole mustard
1/4 t  Chopped garlic clove
Combine all ingredients in screw-top jar; shake well.
