Title: Marshmellow Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish, Nuts/grains, Sweetpotato
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Paprika
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 lb Marshmellows
8 md Sweet potatoes
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Hot milk
1/2 ts Salt
Cook potatoes until tender, remove skins, mash. Add next 5
ingredients. Beat until fluffy. Add nuts. Turn into a greased
casserole, cover with marshmellows; bake at 350*F for 30 to 40
minutes. Submitted By MICHELLE BRUCE   On   11-21-94
