Title: Creamy Low Fat Bechamel Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Condiment
Yield: 2 cups
1/4 c  White wine; or vegetable
-stock
3 tb Unbleached white flour; or
-whole wheat pastry flour
2 c  Milk; skim
1/8 ts Nutmeg; freshly grated
1/8 ts Thyme; dried
1 tb Onions; finely minced
1 ts Oil; safflower
In a saucepan over medium high heat, combine milk, nutmeg, thyme and
onions.  Bring just to the boiling point, remove pan from heat, cover
and set aside for 10 mins to allow flavors to blend.

In a heavy saucepan over med high heat, combine oil and wine or veg
stock. Heat to simmering.  Add flour and cook, whisking constantly
for 3 mins.

Slowly add warm milk mixture and continue whisking.  Sauce should
thicken to the consistency of heavy cream.  Serve hot.
Per Tablespoon:  10 cals, 1g prot, 1g fat, 1g carb, 0 chol, 8mg sod,
trace fiber

Recipe by Mary Carroll From the Best of Vegetarian Times

VT's note:  A good white sauce is indispensable to creative cooks.
But the fat is dispensable.  Here's creaminess without compromise.
