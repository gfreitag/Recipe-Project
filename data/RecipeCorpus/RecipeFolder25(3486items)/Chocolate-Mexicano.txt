Title: Chocolate Mexicano
Categories: Tex/mexican, Beverage, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
6    Clove;whole;
Salt;
6 c  Milk
2 tb Vanilla
1/2 c  Cocoa;powdered
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
2 c  Water
2    Cinnamon Sticks;3 inch
Combine cocoa, brown sugar, water, cinnamon sticks, cloves and a
pinch of salt in a pan over medium heat. Bring to a boil and reduce
heat to low; simmer for about 5 minutes.
Add milk to cocoa while whisking constantly; keep cocoa just below
boiling point. Remove from heat and add vanilla.
Strain to remove spices; beat or blend until foamy. Serve hot! -
You may optionally use cinnamon sticks for stirring.
