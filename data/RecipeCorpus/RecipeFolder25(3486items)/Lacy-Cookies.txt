Title: Lacy Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Flour;white
2 tb Milk
1 c  Almonds;blanched
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
Insert steel blade in food processor.  Process nuts, a half cup at
a time, until finely ground. Combine with remaining ingredients in a
small saucepan and stir and blend over moderate heat until butter is
melted.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto a greased and floured baking sheet.
Bake in a preheated 350F oven 6 to 8 minutes.  Remove carefully with
a spatula to a rack to cool.
Yields about 2 dozen cookies.
