Title: Persimmon Marmalade
Categories: Can/preserv, Fruit, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 half pints
1 c  Orange juice
1    Orange; grated rind of
2 qt Persimmons, ripe
1 c  Sugar
Cut up persimmons; discard seeds and cores.  Mash fruit and cook it
with sugar, orange juice, and orange rind until mixture is thick.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
Coleman. Submitted By NANCY COLEMAN   On   12-01-94
