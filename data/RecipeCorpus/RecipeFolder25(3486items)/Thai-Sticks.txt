Title: Thai Sticks
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Thai, Chicken, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
1/2    Jalapeno Pepper;seeded;
1 tb Cilantro;chopped
1 tb Mint;chopped
2 tb Peanut Oil
2 lb Chicken Breast;sliced, boned
1 c  Coconut Milk;unsweetened
1/2 c  Mango Chutney
1/4 c  Peanuts;chopped, roasted
Combine all ingredients except chicken in food processor or
blender. Blend until smooth.
Thread thin sliced chicken breast pieces on skewers; place in a
shallow dish. Pour marinade over chicken; refrigerate overnight.
Cook quickly on very hot barbecue or close to oven broiler.
