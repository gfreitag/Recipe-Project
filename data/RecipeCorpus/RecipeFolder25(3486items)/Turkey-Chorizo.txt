Title: Turkey Chorizo
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Turkey, Poultry
Yield: 5 pounds
1 ts Ground ginger
2 tb Sweet paprika
2 ts Cayenne
1/2 ts Celery seed
1/4 c  Dry red wine
2 ts Pepper
2 ts Salt, or to taste
5    Feet small hog or sheep
-casings
5 lb Turkey meat-15% fat
2 ts Oregano
2 ts Cumin
1 ts Freshly ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground coriander
Prepare casings. Grind turkey through the fine disk, mix with
remaining ingredients and grind again. Stuff into casings and tie off
into 3" links. Saute in oil until evenly browned and cooked through.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
