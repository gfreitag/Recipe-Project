Title: Curried Rice Mix with Recipe
Categories: Curries, Mixes, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Curry powder
-RICE:
2 1/2 c  Water
1 pk Rice mix
1 c  Long grain white rice
1    Chicken bouillon cube,
-crumbled
2 tb Dried minced onion
1/4 c  Raisins
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container.

Rice: Bring water to a boil. Add rice mix, reduce heat, cover and
simmer 20 minutes. 6 servings
