Title: Cranberry-Orange Relish Jewels
Categories: Diabetic, Fruit, Relish
Yield: 3 c - 48 sev
-frozen
Artificial sweetener to
-taste
1    Orange, unpeeled, cubed
1/4 ts Allspice; ground
3 c  Cranberries, fresh canned
Position knife blade in food processor bowl, add cubed unpeeled
orange. Process 15 seconds or until orange is coarsely chopped.  ( Or
use a sharp knife.)  Combine chopped orange, allspice, and
cranberries in a large saucepan; simmer over low heat, stirring
occasionally for 12 minutes or until cranberry skins pop and mixture
thickens.  Remove heat and let cool. Add sweeter (substitution) to
taste.  Store in air-tight container in refrigerator up to 1 week.
Serve with poultry. Exchanges per serving: FREE EXCHANGE; CAL: 4;
FAT: 0gm; SOD: 0mg; CAR: 1gm; SUGAR: 1gm; Source: The Diabetes
