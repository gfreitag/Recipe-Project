Title: Herbed Potatoes and Carrots
Categories: Potato, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 t  Dried thyme
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
Paprika
4 md Potatoes, unpeeled, cut in
-wedges
3 md Carrots, cut in large chunks
1/2 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
1 t  Dried rosemary
Heat oven to 450F.  Boil potatoes and carrots together 15 minutes or
until tender.  Drain.  Combine with dressing, herbs, salt and pepper.
Place in a asingle layer on a greased baking sheet.  Sprinkle paprika
on top. Bake 15 minutes or until browned.  Makes 6 servings. Origin:
Canadian Reader's Digest, November/94. Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
Nov/94.
