Title: Homemade Hot Cocoa Mix
Categories: Beverage, Chocolate
Yield: 3 servings
Mix together dry
-ingredients. Store in
-airtight container.
2 c  Nonfat Dry Milk
1/2 c  Powdered Non-dairy Creamer
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Hershey's Cocoa
Recipe makes 3 3/4 cups mix (15 to 16 servings). For single serving:
Add 1/4 cup mix to 3/4 cup boiling water. For Spiced Cocoa: Add 3 Tb
instant coffee, 1/2 ts allspice and 1/4 ts cinnimon to mix.
