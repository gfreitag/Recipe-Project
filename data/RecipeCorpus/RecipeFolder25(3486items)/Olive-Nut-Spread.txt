Title: Olive Nut Spread
Categories: Dip, Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Olives, pimento stuffed,
Chopped
8 oz Cream cheese
4 tb Milk
1 c  Walnuts, finely chopped
Soften cheese and add milk.  Blend in chopped olives and walnuts.
Spread on crackers. Randy Rigg
