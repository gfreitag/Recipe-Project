Title: Koushry (Rice & Lentils)
Categories: Side dish, African, Rice, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
3 md Onions, chopped
2 tb Oil
1 c  Dark lentils
1 c  Long-grain rice
1 ts Salt
Put lentils & rice in separate pots.  Pour enough boiling water over
the lentils to cover them.  Add 1 1/2 c boiling water to rice. Simmer
both pots, covered, over low heat for 20 minutes.  Drain & combine in
one large pot.  Addsalt & another 1/2 c water.  Cover & simmer 15 to
20 minutes until both rice & lentils are tender.

Meanwhile, fry the onions in oil until golden.  Stir them into the
rice before serving.
