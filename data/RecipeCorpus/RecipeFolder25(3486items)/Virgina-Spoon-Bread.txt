Title: Virgina Spoon Bread
Categories: Bread, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 3 servings
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
2    Eggs, separated
1 c  Corn meal
1 ts Salt
2 c  Milk, scalded
Mix corn meal and salt; stir into hot milk and cook over low heat
until thick and smooth.  Stir occasionally.  Cool slightly.  Stir in
baking powder and well beaten egg yolk.  Fold in stiffly beaten egg
whites. Turn into well greased casserole.  Bake at 375 degrees for 35
minutes or until firm and crusty. Randy Rigg
