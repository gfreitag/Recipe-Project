Title: Italian Herbed Mozzarella Ring
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Appetizer, Herbs/spice, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
4 ea Tomatoes, sliced
1 pk KRAFT part skim mozzarella
-cheese slices
1 ea English cucumber, cut in
-slices
1/3 c  KRAFT miracle whip salad
-dressing
1/3 c  Yogurt
2 T  Chopped fresh basil leaves
12 ea Romaine lettuce leaves
Mix salad dressing with the yogurt and the basil leaves.  Layer the
lettuce leaves, tomato slices, cheese slices and cucumber slices in a
nice pattern on a platter.  Drizzle the dressing over top of all and
garnish with some shredded lettuce on top. Origin: KRAFT What's
Cooking Magazine, 10th issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
