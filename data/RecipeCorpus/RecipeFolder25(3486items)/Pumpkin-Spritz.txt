Title: Pumpkin Spritz
Categories: Holiday, Cake/cookie, Dessert, Pumpkin
Yield: 60 servings
1/3 c  Canned pumpkin
1    Egg
1 t  Vanilla
2 3/4 c  Flour
Ground nutmeg (optional)
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 t  Baking powder
3/4 t  Ground cinnamon
1/2 t  Ground nutmeg
1/4 t  Ground ginger
Beat butter in a medium mixing bowl with an electri mixer on medium
to high speed for 30 seconfs.  Add sugar, baking powder, cinnamon,
nutmeg, and ginger; beat till combined.  Beat in pumpkin, egg and
vanilla till combined.

Beat in as much of the flour as you can with the mixer. Stir in any
remaining flour with a wooden spoon.  DO NOT CHILL DOUGH

Pack dough into a cookie press.  Force dough through the cookie press
onto an ungreased cookie sheet.   Sprinkle with nutmeg, if desired.
Bake in a 400 deg oven for 6 to 8 minutes, or till edges are firm but
not brown. Cook on wire rack.  makes about 60.

Source: Christmas cookies 1994 (Better Homes and Gardens) p.83 typos
