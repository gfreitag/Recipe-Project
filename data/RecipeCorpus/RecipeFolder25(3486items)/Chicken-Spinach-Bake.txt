Title: Chicken Spinach Bake
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Salsa/sauce, Chicken, Vegetable
Yield: 16 servings
MMMMM----------------KEYWORDS: MUSHROOM, BRIBE, F---------------------
3/4 c  Italian seasoned bread
-crumbs
16 ea Boneless skinless chicken
-breast halves
Salt and pepper to taste
5 T  Butter or margarine, melted
3 pk Frozen chopped spinach,
-thawed (10 oz. ea.)
3 ea Eggs
1/2 t  Onion salt
1/2 t  Ground nutmeg
3/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese,
-divided
MMMMM------------------------CHEESE SAUCE-----------------------------
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese (8
-oz.)
1 c  Sliced fresh mushroom
6 T  Butter or margarine, divided
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 t  Salt
2 c  Milk
Drain and squeeze out excess moisture from spinach. Beat eggs, onion
salt and nutmeg. Add spinach and 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese; mix well.
Combine bread crumbs and remaining Parmesan. Sprinkle chicken with
salt and pepper; coat with crumb mixture. Place in 2 greased 13" x 9"
x 2" greased baking pans. Spread 2 tablespoons of spinach mixture
onto each breast. Sprinkle with remaining crumb mixture; drizzle with
butter. Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes or until chicken juices
run clear. Sauce: Melt 4 tablespoons of butter; blend in flour and
salt. Stir to form a smooth paste. Add milk; cook and stir until
thichened and bubbly. Add cheese and stir until melted. Saute
mushrooms in remaining butter. Stir into cheese mixture. Carefully
pour sauce over chicken, or pour into a serving bowl and pass. Serves
16. SOURCE:* Taste of Home Magazine Aug./Sept. 94 POSTED BY: Jim
Bodle 10/94
