Title: Easy Tacos
Categories: Main dish, Tex/mexican
Yield: 12 servings
4 oz Cheddar Cheese, grated
1    Tomato, chopped
1 sm Onion, chopped
4 oz Pace Picante Sauce
4 oz Sour Cream
1 lb Ground Beef
1 pk Taco Seasoning Mix
1 c  Water
1 lb Tortilla Chips
Lettuce, shredded
Brown ground beef in skillet, drain off excess fat.  Add Taco
Seasoning Mix and water.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 10
to 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Serve on top of tortilla chips.

Makes about 2 cups of taco meat.

Add the following or any combination as desired:  lettuce, cheese,
tomatoes, onion, picante sauce, and sour cream.
