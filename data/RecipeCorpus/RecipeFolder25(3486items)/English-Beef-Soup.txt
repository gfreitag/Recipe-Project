Title: English Beef Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Beef, Vegetable, British
Yield: 2 quarts
1/2 c  Cooked barley
4 tb Unbleached all-purpose flour
1 c  Cold water
2 tb Tomato catsup
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tb Olive oil
1/2 lb Lean beef, cut in 1" cubes
1    Small onion, sliced rings
4    Stalks celery, chopped
2    Quarts beef broth
1/2 c  Chopped carrots
In a large soup pot, heat the oil and brown the beef and onion. Drain
off the fat.  Add the celery and beef broth.  Simmer, covered, for
1-1 1/2 hours, or until the meat is tender.

Add the carrots and barley and simmer for 20 minutes.  In a small
bowl, blend the flour and cold water.  Slowly add the flour mixture
to the soup pot and cook for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.  Add the
catsup, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper.  Stir well and serve
