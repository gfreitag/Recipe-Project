Title: Turkey Parmesan
Categories: Main dish, Italian, Poultry, Turkey, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Parmesan Cheese
1 ts Oregano
1/2 c  Olive Oil
3 tb Margarine
Lemon Juice;
2 lb Turkey;boned breast
1/2 c  Bread Crumbs
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2    Egg;beaten;
Pound turkey till thin. Pat dry and cut into 2' by 4' pieces.
Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, spices and salt. Set aside.
Beat eggs in a shallow dish. Dip turkey into egg, then into the
crumb mixture, coating both sides.
Heat oil and margarine in skillet. Saute turkey until brown -
about 1 - 2 minutes each side. Remove to a warm platter.
Sprinkle with lemon juice before serving.
