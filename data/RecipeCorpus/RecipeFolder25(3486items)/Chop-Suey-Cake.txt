Title: Chop Suey Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 cake
Icing
2 ts Soda
1    8 oz Cream Cheese
1    Stick Margarine
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Confectioners Sugar
2 c  Flour, Self Rising
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Coconut Flakes
1 c  Chopped Nuts
1    Crushed Pineapple 20 oz all
2    Eggs
2 ts Baking Powder
Mix Flour, Sugar, Pineapple and juice, 1/2 the nuts, some Coconut,
Soda and the Eggs with a fork. Bake in a greased and floured sheet
cake pan, Bake at 350 for 35 - 45 min.

Icing Combine Cream Cheese, Margarine, Vanilla and Confectioners
Sugar, whip well. Spread on warm cake. Good!!!
