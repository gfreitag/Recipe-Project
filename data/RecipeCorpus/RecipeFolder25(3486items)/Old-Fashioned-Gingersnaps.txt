Title: Old Fashioned Gingersnaps
Categories: Cake/cookie, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Ground ginger
-Additional sugar
3/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1/4 c  Molasses
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking soda
In a mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar.  Add egg and molasses;
beat well.  Sift together dry ingredients; gradually add to creamed
mixture. Mix well.  Chill the dough.  Roll into 1-1/4 in. balls and
dip into sugar. Place 2 in. apart on ungreased cookie sheets.  Bake
at 375 degrees F for about 10 minutes or until set and surface
cracks. Cool on wire racks.

Makes about 4 dozen.
