Title: Smoked Salmon Cracker Spread
Categories: Snack, Fish, Holiday, Appetizer
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM-----------------------MAKES: 2 CUPS----------------------------
1/2 t  Horseradish
16 oz Can salmon, drained, bones
-removed, flaked
Chopped pecans
Chopped fresh parsley
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
1/2 t  Salt
1 ea Tb lemon juice
2 t  Finely chopped onion
1 t  Liquid smoke
In small mixer bowl, beat cream cheese until light and fluffy. Add
sallt, lemon juice, onion, liquid smoke, and horsereadish. Continue
beating until well mixed. Stir in salmon by hand. Spoon into serving
bowl. If desired, garnish with pecans and parsley. Store
refrigerated. Source: Land O Lakes Treasury of Country Recipes
