Title: Betty's Cheese Wafers
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Bread, Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
1 t  Worcestershire sauce
ds Tabasco
1 c  Sifted all purpose flour
1/2 c  Butter
2 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
-cheese
In a food processor or electric mixer, combine all of the
ingredients. Roll into logs, cover, and refrigerate until firm.
Grease a cookie sheet. Slice into wafers and place on the cookie
sheet.  Bake in a 350F oven for 12 minutes.  Serves 6. Submitted by
Harvey Bullock, writer for Mayberry series. Origin: Aunt Bee's
Mayberry Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
