Title: Gimble's Cheese Torte/cake
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
2/3 c  Plus 1 tsp. Sugar
1/2 ts Almond flavoring
Topping
1 pt Sour cream
1/3 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Almond flavoring
Crust
2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/2 c  Melted butter
1/4 c  Sugar
Filling
16 oz Cream cheese-softened
2    Eggs
Crust: Melt butter. Add sugar to graham cracker crumbs and blend with
butter until well mixed.  Put in bottom of spring form pan.

Filling: Mix softened cream cheese with eggs, sugar, vanilla and
almond flavoring. Pour over crust and bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
When cold add sour cream topping.

Topping: Blend sour cream, sugar and almond flavoring until smooth.
Pour over cooled cake.  Bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees.  Chill
several hours before serving.
