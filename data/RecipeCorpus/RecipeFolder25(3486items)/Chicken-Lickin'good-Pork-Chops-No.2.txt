Title: Chicken-Lickin'good Pork Chops No.2
Categories: Crockpot, Pork, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Garlic powder
2 tb Oil
1 cn Chicken and rice soup
8    Lean pork chops
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
Mix flour, salt, dry mustard and garlic powder; dredge pork chops and
brown in wil. Put in crockpot and add soup. Cover, cook on LOW for 6
to 8 hours. Submitted By BOBBI ZEE   On   11-13-94
