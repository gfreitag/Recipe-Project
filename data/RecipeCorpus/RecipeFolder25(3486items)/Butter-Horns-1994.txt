Title: Butter Horns 1994
Categories: Holiday, Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 16 servings
1    Portion Vanilla Dough
-see Basic Cookie Dough
-recipe
2 T  Granulated sugar
1/8 c  Walnuts
1/4 t  Ground cinnamon
Process sugar, nuts, and cinnamon in food processor until nuts are
finely chopped.  roll dough on lightly floured surface into a 10-inch
circle. Cut into 16 wedges and sprinkle with nut mixture, leaving a
1-inch border all around.  Slide out 1 wedge at a time with a small
metal spatula. Roll up from wide edge to point.  Put on ungreased
cookie sheets and curve each into a crescent.  Bake 10 to 12 min. at
350degF , until golden. Remove to a wire rack to cool.

Per cookie: 65 ca., 1 g pro, 8 g car, 4 g fat, 11 mg chol with
butter, 3 mg cho with margarine, 31 mg sod. Exchanges: 1/4
starch/bread, 1/4 fruit, 2/3 fat
