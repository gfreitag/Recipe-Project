Title: Garden Squid Chowder
Categories: Seafood, Soup/stew, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sour cream
2 T  Flour
1/2 t  Dill weed
1/2 t  Salt
ds White pepper
Chopped parsley
3 lb Cleaned squid
2 T  Butter
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Fresh sliced mushrooms
1 c  Julienne carrots
3 c  Chicken bouillon
Clean squid, wash, pat dry and cut into small rings. Melt butter and
saute squid and vegetables five minutes. Add bouillon and simmer 30
minutes until squid is tender. Combine remaining ingredients except
parsley, and blend into soup. Cook, stirring constantly until
thickened. Serve garnished with parsley. Six servings. POSTED BY: Jim
Bodle 12/93, 10/94
