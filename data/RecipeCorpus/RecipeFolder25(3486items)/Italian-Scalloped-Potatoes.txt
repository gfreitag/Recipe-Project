Title: Italian Scalloped Potatoes
Categories: Potato, Cheese/eggs, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
-soup
1/4 c  Milk
1/2 t  Italian seasoning, crushed
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
4 md Potaotes, very thinly sliced
1 md Onion, chopped
1 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
10 cn Condensed cream of mushroom
In a greased 6 cup casserole, arrange half of potatoes and half of
onion. Sprinkle with ahlf of mozzarella and half parmesan.  Repeat
layers. In small bowl, combine soup, milk, italian seasoning, and
garlic. Spoon over potato mixture.  Cover and bake at 400F for 1
hour. Uncover and bake 10 more minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Makes 6 side dish servings. Origin: Campbell's soup ad, Canadian
Reader's Digest, November/94. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
