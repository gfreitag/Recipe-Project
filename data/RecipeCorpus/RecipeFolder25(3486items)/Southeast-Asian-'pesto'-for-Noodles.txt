Title: Southeast Asian 'pesto' for Noodles
Categories: Asian, Herbs/spice, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Fresh minced mint leaves
1    Or 2 jalapenos; (to taste)
-- coarsely chopped
1 tb Shredded fresh ginger or
1 ts Ground ginger
1/2 c  Double-strength chicken
-broth (not too salty)
2 tb Light soy sauce
2 tb Fish sauce
1/4 c  Fresh minced basil
1/4 c  Fresh minced cilantro
Combine ingredients; toss with hot noodles or rice.

Per sauce serving: 35 calories.
