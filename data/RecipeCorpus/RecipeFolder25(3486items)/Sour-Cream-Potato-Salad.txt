Title: Sour Cream Potato Salad
Categories: Salad, Potato
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM--------------------LORI NORMAN  RFHH52E-------------------------
-diced or sliced
2 lg Eggs, hard-boiled and
-chopped
1/2 c  Celery chopped
1 tb Onion, chopped
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Milk
1 pk Italian Salad Dressing Mix
6 c  Potatoes, hot cooked
Combine sour cream, vinegar, milk and salad dressing mix; blend well.
Place potatoes, eggs, celery and onion in a large bowl. Add dressing;
toss well.

Serve warm or chilled.  Source:  Lori Norman, Prodigy.
