Title: Lobster Boats
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Mushroom, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/8 ts Tabasco sauce
-Pepper to taste
24    Mushrooms, about 1 1/2"
-diameter, stems removed
-Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 lb Cooked lobster meat,
-chopped
1/4 c  Condensed cream of mushroom
-soup
2 tb Bread crumbs
2 tb Mayonnaise
Preheat oven to 400. Combine lobster, soup, bread crumbs, mayonnaise,
Worcestershire and Tabasco sauces and pepper. Stuff each mushroom cap
with 1 tb lobster mixture. Sprinkle with grated cheese. Place on a
15X10X1" bking pan. Bake 10-15 minutes or until lightly browned.
Serve hot.
