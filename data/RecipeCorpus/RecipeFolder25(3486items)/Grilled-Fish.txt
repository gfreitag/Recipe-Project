Title: Grilled Fish
Categories: Main dish, Fish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
Cheese (grated)
Catfish fillets
Italian dressing
Marinate fish in dressing skin side down.  Place fish in aluminum pan
lined with foil and cook on grill 10 to 20 minutes, depending on heat
of fire. When fish is done, sprinkle heavily with grated cheese and
return to heat until melted.
