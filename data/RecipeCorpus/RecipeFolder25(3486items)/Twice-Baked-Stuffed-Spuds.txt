Title: Twice Baked Stuffed Spuds
Categories: Potato, Stuffing, Side dish, Homemade, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1 T  Parsley flakes
1/8 t  Cayenne
Milk
Margarine
1 c  Grated cheddar cheese
4-5 medium sized potatoes,
-washed
1/2 c  Bacon, cut, cooked, and
-crumbled
1 sm Onion, chopped fine, and
-sauted
Wash potatoes and poke fork into them to allow steam an escape route.
Bake for 1 hour at 400F, or microwave until tender.  Cut in half
lengthwise. Scoop out potato, leaving a 1/4 inch behind.  Set aside.
Mash potatoes, adding margarine and milk as you would for mashed
potatoes. Add the bacon, garlic, onion, parsley and cayenne. Mix
thoroughly. Spoon mashed potatoes back into potato shells.  Arrange
on a cookie sheet that has been lined with foil.  Sprinkle grated
cheese over tops and bake at 375F for about 20-30 minutes or until
potatoes are heated all the way through, and cheese has melted and is
starting to bubble. Origin: Sharon Stevens Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
Nov/94.
