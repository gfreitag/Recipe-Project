Title: Fried Crab Canapes
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 10 servings
3/4 ts Salt
-Dash tabasco
8 sl Day-old firm white bread,
-crusts removed
-Oil for frying
1/2 lb Crabmeat, flaked
4    Water chestnuts, drained and
-finely chopped
1 1/2 tb Cornstarch
1    Egg, lightly beaten
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
In bowl, combine crab, water chestnuts, cornstarch, egg,
Worcestershire, salt, and tabasco. Cut each slice of bread into 4
triangles. Spread with crab misture. Preheat oil to 375 in deep
fryer. Add bread triangles, crab side down. Fry 1 minute on each
side. Drain on paper towels,; serve hot.
