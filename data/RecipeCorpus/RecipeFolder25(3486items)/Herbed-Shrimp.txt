Title: Herbed Shrimp
Categories: Main dish, Sthrn/livng, Herbs/spice, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Basil, sweet; crushed
1 ts Tarragon; crushed
1    Garlic clove; minced
1 c  Breadcrumbs, fine dry
2 lb Shrimp; uncooked in shells,
-or 1 lb. cooked, shelled,
-or canned deveined shrimp
1 c  Butter
1/2 ts Salt
Cook shrimp; remove shells and devein.  Divide shrimp between 4
individual shells or casseroles.  Cream butter and stir in remaining
ingredients. Mix well.  Spread crumb mixture ove shrimp.  Bake at 350
degrees until thoroughly heated (this takes about 20 minutes).
