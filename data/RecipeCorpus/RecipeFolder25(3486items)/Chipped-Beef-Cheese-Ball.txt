Title: Chipped Beef Cheese Ball
Categories: Appetizer, Beef, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 ball
1 pk Dried Chipped Beef chopped
5    Green Onions, chopped
1 pk Cream Cheese, 8 oz softened
1 ts Worchestershire Sauce
1 ts Milk
Mix cheese, Worchestershire Sauce and Milk to make a soft mixture.
Add 1/2 of the Beef and chopped Onion, shape into a ball and roll in
the remaining Beef.  Chill
