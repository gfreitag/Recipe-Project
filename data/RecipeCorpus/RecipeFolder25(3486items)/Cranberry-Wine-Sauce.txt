Title: Cranberry Wine Sauce
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce, Alcohol
Yield: 2 cups
1    Lemon; grated rind of
1/2 c  Wine, port
1 lb Cranberry sauce, whole berry
3 tb Sugar
ds Nutmeg, ground
1 ts Cinnamon, ground
1/4 ts Cloves, ground
Combine dry ingredients and wine; mix well.  Place in a saucepan over
low heat and simmer for 5 minutes.  Add cranberry sauce; stir with a
fork or wire whisk until mixture is smooth and heated thoroughly.
Serve with game, pork, or lamb.
