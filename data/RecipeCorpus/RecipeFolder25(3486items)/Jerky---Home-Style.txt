Title: Jerky - Home Style
Categories: Beef, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 t  Onion salt
1 t  Black pepper
1 lb Beef, cut in strips
1 T  Worcestershire sauce   1/4
-tsp hickory salt
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
ds Hot sauce
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 t  Garlic powder
Directions: Combine all the ingredients and marinate in the fridge
for at least 8 hours, mixing occasionally. Then, skewer the marinated
strips on bamboo skewers (or toothpicks) and hand the strips in the
oven with a pan below to catch the drippings. Set the oven on it's
lowest setting (100-150 Deg F) and use something to keep the door
ajar. The jerky should be dry after 8-10 hours.
