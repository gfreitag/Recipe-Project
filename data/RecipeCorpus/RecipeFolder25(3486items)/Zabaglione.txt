Title: Zabaglione
Categories: Dessert, Italian, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Marsala
4    Egg;yolks;
2 tb Sugar;powdered
Beat egg yolks and powdered sugar till thick. Put in a heavy bowl
over hot water. Beat constantly, adding a little marsala at a time,
until the mixture starts to hold shape.
Place immediately into heated cups for serving warm or cold.
