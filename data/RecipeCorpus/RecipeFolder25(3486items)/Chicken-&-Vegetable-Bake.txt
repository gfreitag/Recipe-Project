Title: Chicken & Vegetable Bake
Categories: Main dish, Casserole, Chicken, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
-(opt)
2 cn Swanson Premium Chunk White
-OR Chunk Chicken, drained
-(5 oz)
ds French's Worcestershire
-sauce
1 cn Durkee French Fried Onions
-(2.8 oz)
1 cn Campbell's Cream of Chicken
-Soup (10 3/4 oz)
1/2 c  Milk
3 c  Cooked cup-up vegetables
-broccoli flowerets,
-cauliflowerets, sliced
-carrots, cut green beans)
1/4 c  Cooked red pepper strips
1. In 1 1/2-quart casserole mix soup, milk, vegetables, chicken,
Worcestershire and 1/2 can onions.

2. Bake at 350'F. 25 minutes or until hot.

3. Stir. Sprinkle with remaining onions. Bake 5 minutes or until done.
