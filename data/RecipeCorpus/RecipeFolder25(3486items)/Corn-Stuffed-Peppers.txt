Title: Corn Stuffed Peppers
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Grated onion
2 ts Parsley flakes
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
6    Green or red bell peppers
2 c  Frozen corn, thawed
1/2 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/2 c  Cream
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
2 tb Melted margarine/butter
Remove tops and insides of peppers.  Wash and drain thoroughly. Place
the peppers upright in a shallow pan. Add 1/2 inch water to pan. Mix
all the remaining ingredients.  Spoon into peppers.  Bake at 350
degrees for 45 minutes or until peppers are tender.
