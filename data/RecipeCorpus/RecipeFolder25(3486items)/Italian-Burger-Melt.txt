Title: Italian Burger Melt
Categories: Ground beef, Burgers, Cheese/eggs, Sandwich
Yield: 6 servings
6 sl Mozzarella, or American
-cheese
6 ea Hamburger rolls
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 ea 10oz can Campbell's Italian
-tomato soup
Shape beef into 6 patties and brown in skillet.  Drain fat.  Add soup
and 1/3 cup water.  Cover and cook 10 minutes or until patties are no
longer pink in the middle.  top with cheese and serve on rolls.
Serves 6. Origin: Campbell's Soup Ad in Women's Day Magazine, Oct/94.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
