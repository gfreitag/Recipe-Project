Title: Rum Balls (Walter Kasbey)
Categories: Cake/cookie, Candy, Holiday, Alcohol, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Cocoa
1 c  Finely chopped nuts
1 c  Powdered sugar + more
1/2 c  Butter, melted
1/2 c  Rum

2 c  Vanilla wafer or graham

Mix crumbs, cocoa, nuts, powdered sugar, butter and rum. Form into
small balls, store in freezer and roll in powdered sugar just before
serving.
