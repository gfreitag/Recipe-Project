Title: Linda William's Turkey Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Turkey, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
5    Carrots, chopped
2    Onions, cut up
2 tb Parsley
1    Meaty turkey carcus
32 oz Canned tomatoes, undrained
1/3 c  Barley
2 c  Chopped celery
Cover turkey bones with water in a large pot.  Season with salt and
pepper to taste. Bring to a boil and simmer 3 hrs. Strain the broth
and save the meat. Add tomatoes and barley. Simmer 2 hrs. Add
vegetables and parsley. Simmer 1/2 to 1 hr. longer till vegetables
are cooked. Add meat and heat.
*******************************************************
****************** Although this soup takes a long time to cook, it's
worth it! For a richer flavor, crack the large turkey bones before
cooking them. Eileen Lamparelli CGDR39A
