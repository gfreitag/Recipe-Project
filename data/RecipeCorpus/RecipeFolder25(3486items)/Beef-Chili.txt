Title: Beef Chili
Categories: Beef, Main dish, Rice, Chili
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------BARB DAY - GWHP32A--------------------------
- Rice Salad for this mix
1 md Onion; chopped
28 oz Plum tomatoes; canned,
- undrained
2 c  Whole kernel corn; frozen
1 lb Cube steaks; lean
2 ts Vegetable oil
4 1/2 ts Spicy Seasoning Mix
- divided,  Note: See
- Recipe for Spicy Beef and
Preparation time:  30 minutes
Heat oil in deep large skillet over medium heat 5 minutes.
Meanwhile cut each steak lengthwise into 1" wide strips, then cut
crosswise in 1" pieces. Sprinkle meat with 2 ts. spicy seasoning.
Stir-fry meat and onion 2 to 3 minutes.  Season with salt if desired.
Add tomatoes (break up with back of spoon), corn and remaining 2 1/2
teaspoons of the seasoning mix.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat to
medium low and simmer, uncovered, 18 to 20 minutes.
Serving suggestions:  Tortilla chips and melon wedges.

Source: Beef Industry Council
