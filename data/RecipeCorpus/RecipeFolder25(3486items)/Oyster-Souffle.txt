Title: Oyster Souffle
Categories: Main dish, Sthrn/livng, Cheese/eggs, Rice, Clam/oyster
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Milk
3/8 c  Tomato sauce
1 1/2 tb Flour, all-purpose
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
Salt; to taste
Pepper; to taste
1    Onion; finely chopped
1/4 c  Pepper, green; minced
2 tb Shortening
6    Eggs; separated
1 1/2 c  Oysters, fried; chopped
3/4 c  Rice; cooked
Saute onion and green pepper in shortening in a saucepan.  Stir in
other ingredients except egg whites; heat.  Remove from heat and add
beaten egg whites.  Pour into lightly greased baking dish; bake at
350 degrees until lightly browned on top.
