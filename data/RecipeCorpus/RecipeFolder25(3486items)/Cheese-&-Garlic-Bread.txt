Title: Cheese & Garlic Bread
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 small
1 ts Garlic powder
2 tb Parmesan cheese grated
1 tb Oil
220 ml Water
1 1/2 ts Dry yeast
2 1/3 c  Bread flour
1 tb Milk powder
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
If using a bread machine make as per instructions for basic bake
mode.  If mixing by hand, warm water and add yeast and sugar. leave
to stand until froth appears.  Mix dry ingredients together.  Then
add liquid and oil. Mix using dough hook or knead dough thoroughly.
Oil bowl and leave dough covered with a damp cloth in a warm place
until it has doubled in size.
Knock back and knead dough lightly. Then put into grase loaf tin and
leave to rise.  Bake in a hot oven 20-25 minutes
