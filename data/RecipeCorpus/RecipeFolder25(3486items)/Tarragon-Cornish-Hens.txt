Title: Tarragon Cornish Hens
Categories: Poultry, Game, Chicken, Herbs/spice, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
1 t  Dried tarragon
1/4 t  Each of salt and white or
-black pepper
2 ea Cornish hens
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
2 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
Juice of 1 lemon
Preheat oven to 375F.  Split hens in half lengthwise.  Place hens,
skin side up, on a larg baking sheet with shallow sides.  In a small
bowl, whisk remaining ingredients togethers.  Generously baste hens
with mixture. Roast, uncovered, in centre of preheated oven for 40-45
minutes, basting frequently with oil mixture.  Serves 2-4. Origin:
Chatelaine Magazine, December/89. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
