Title: Indian Curried Chick Peas and Vegetables
Categories: Beans, Vegetable, Curries, Indian
Yield: 8 servings
-chopped
1/2 c  KRAFT miracle whip
1/2 c  Plain yogurt
1/3 c  Chopped fresh parsley
1-2 tbsp curry powder, or
-to taste
Serve with Turkey
19 oz Can chick peas, rinsed and
-drained
2 ea Red pepper, chopped
2 ea Yellow peppers, chopped
1 ea Long English cucumber,
Mix chick peas and vegetable into a large glass bowl.  Gently mix
remaining ingredients together and toss with the vegetables.  Chill
for 2 hours. Makes 8 servings. Origin: KRAFT What's Cooking Magazine,
10th issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
