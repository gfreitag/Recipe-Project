Title: Baked Spinach with Brown Rice and Cheese
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian, Rice, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
2    Large eggs, beaten
2 tb Chopped parsley
2 tb Bread crumbs
1 tb Melted butter
Cayenne
Nutmeg
Salt
Pepper
1 lb Fresh spinach leaves
Or
1/2 lb Frozen spinach, thawed
8 fl Brown rice
16 fl Hot water
1    Onion, chopped
1 tb Oil
1 ts Butter
4 oz Grated cheddar cheese
Melt the butter and oil in a saucepan and soften the onion it it, then
stire in the rice to get it coated with oil.  Add the hot water, stir
once, and simmer gently with a lid on for 40 minutes or until the
grain is cooked.

As soon as the rice is cooked, cool it in a bowl, then combine it
with the grated cheese and stir in the eggs and parsley followed by
the chopped spinach.  Season well and add a good grating of nutmeg.

Now place the mixture into a large oiled pie dish, mis the
breadcrumbs with the melted butter and cayenne and sprinkle that over
the top.

Bake in the centre of the oven for about 35 minutes.
