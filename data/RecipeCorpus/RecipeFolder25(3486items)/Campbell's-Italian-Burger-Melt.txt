Title: Campbell's Italian Burger Melt
Categories: Sandwich, Italian, Burgers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
6 sl Mozzarella or American
-cheese
6    Hamburger rolls
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 cn Campbell's Italian Tomato
-Soup
1. Shape beef into 6 patties and brown in skillet. Drain fat.

2. Add soup and 1/3 cup water.

3. Cover and cook 10 minutes or until patties are no longer pink.

4. Top with cheese and serve on rolls.

Prep Time: 10 minutes. Cook Time: 25 minutes.
