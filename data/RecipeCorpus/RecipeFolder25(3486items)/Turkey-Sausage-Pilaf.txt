Title: Turkey Sausage Pilaf
Categories: Poultry, Sausage, Rice, Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  Fresh chopped basil
3 ea Carrots, thinly slice
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1 ea Pinch red peppercorns (or
-green)
1 ea Chopped onion
1 lb Spicey turkey sausage
1 c  Long grain rice
1 1/2 c  Chicken broth
2 ea Chicken bouillon cubes
Combine onion and sausage in large non-stick skillet. Cook over medium
heat; turn to brown evenly. Add reamaining ingredients and heat to
boiling. Lower heat and cover; simmer 20 minutes until liquid has
been absorbed. Remove from heat and let stand 5 minutes then fluff
with fork. Jess Poling's Compu-Chef 2.0 database.
