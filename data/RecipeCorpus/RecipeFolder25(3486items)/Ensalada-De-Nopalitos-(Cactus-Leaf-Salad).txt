Title: Ensalada De Nopalitos (Cactus Leaf Salad)
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salad, Tomato, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
;gently in cold water
3 md Tomatoes; peeled, seeded and
;coarsely chopped
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
1 tb Coriander, fresh; coarsely
;chopped
3 tb Olive oil
1 tb Vinegar, white wine
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Black pepper; freshly ground
1/4 ts Dry mustard
20 oz Nopalitos (cactus leaves);
;diced, drained and rinsed
In a large mixing bowl, combine the oil, vinegar, salt, pepper and
mustard, and beat them together with a spoon or whisk.  Add the
nopalitos, tomatoes, onions and coriander, and toss with a large
spoon to coat them evenly with the dressing.  Refrigerate until
thoroughly chilled. Transfer to a salad or serving bowl and stir
again before serving.
