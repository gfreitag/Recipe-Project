Title: Hidden Valley Ranch Mashed Potatoes
Categories: Vegetable, Potato
Yield: 6 servings
-Dry Salad Dressing Mix
Butter or margarine (opt)
4 c  Instant mashed potatoes
1 pk Hidden Valley Ranch Milk
-Recipe Original Ranch
Prepare mashed potatoes from scrach, or instant mashed potatoes
according to package directions, omitting salt. Stir Hidden Valley
Ranch Milk Recipe Original Ranch Dry Salad Dressing Mix into
potatoes. If desired add butter or margarine. Serves 4-6.
