Title: Dried Peach Compote with Mint and Whiskey
Categories: Fruit, Herbs/spice, Alcohol, Can/preserv
Yield: 3 cups
2 c  Water
1 c  Mint leaves; minced
1/3 c  Bourbon or rye whiskey
2 1/2 c  Dried peaches (14 oz)
-cut in 1/4" strips
2 tb Honey
Bring peaches, honey and water to simmer in a medium-size,
heavy-bottomed saucepan over medium heat. Reduce heat to low and
simmer uncovered, stirring frequently, until peaches plump and liquid
thicken to slightly thinner than applesauce, about 20 minutes. Stir
in mint and whiskey; simmer until alcohol evaporates, 2 to 3 minutes.
cool to room temperature. SErve warm or at room temperature.
