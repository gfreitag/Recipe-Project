Title: Old-Fashioned Cranberry Pie
Categories: Dessert, Pie/tart, Fruit, Sthrn/livng, Pastry
Yield: 1 pie (9")
1    Pastry shell (9"); unbaked
Pastry for lattice topping
2 cn Cranberry sauce, whole
2 tb Butter; melted
1/4 c  Sugar, brown
Combine cranberry sauce, butter, and brown sugar.  Spoon into unbaked
pastry shell.  Cover with lattice topping.  Bake at 425 degrees for
30 to 40 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
