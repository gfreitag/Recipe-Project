Title: Reuben Roll
Categories: German, Ground beef, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 c  Thousand island dressing
1/2 t  Fresh ground pepper
3/4 c  Sauerkraut very well drained
1 c  Shredded swiss cheese plus
-some for garnish
1 1/2 lb Ground chuck
1 1/2 ea To 2 cup rye bread crumbs
1 ea Lightly beaten egg
1 T  Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 T  Sweet pickle relish
Combine beef, bread crumbs, egg, worcestershire, onion, pickle relish,
salad dressing and pepper. Place on a sheet of wax paper and shape
into a flat 6 x 14 inch rectangle. Leaving a 1/2 inch border on all
sides, sprinkle meat with sauerkraut and swiss cheese. Staring at a
short end, roll up, jelly roll fashion - using the paper to help
guide the mixture. Place roll in a shallow roasting pan and bake,
uncovered, for 45 minutes in a preheated 350 degree oven. Top with
additional swiss cheese before serving.
