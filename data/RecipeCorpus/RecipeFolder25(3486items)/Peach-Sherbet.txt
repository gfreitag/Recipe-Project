Title: Peach Sherbet
Categories: Dessert, Ice cream, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Sliced fresh peaches
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 T  Lemon juice
ds Salt

/ Recipe - Fresh Peach
-Sherbet \
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
-++++++-
2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Granulated sugar
1 ea Envelope (1 tsp.)
-unflavored gelatin
In a saucepan, combine milk, sugar, and gelatin.  Stir over low heat
until sugar and gelatin are dissolved.  Whirl milk mixture with all
the remaining ingredients in an electric blender until pureed. Turn
into metal ice cube tray or a 9x5x3 inch loaf pan.  Freeze until the
outer edge of mixture is solid but center is still mushy.  Turn
sherbet in- to a cool mixing bowl and beat until smooth and fluffy;
return to freezer and freeze until firm. Makes about 1 quart.
