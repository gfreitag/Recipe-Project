Title: Conch Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Fish, Clam/oyster
Yield: 2 pounds
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Rosemary
1 ts Lemon juice
2    Six ox cans conch, drained
4    Feet small hog casings
1 1/2 lb Whitefish fillets, cubed
1    Egg, beaten
1 ts Crushed red pepper flakes
Prepare casings. Pulse fish in food processor just until broken. add
egg, red pepper, oregano, rosemary, and lemon juice. Process until
well blended. Remove mixture to a bowl. Add the conch to the
processor and process until the texture of minced clams. Add to fish
misture and mis through well. Stuff into casings and twist off into
3-4" links.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
