Title: Lobster Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Clam/oyster
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Diced raw potatoes
1 sm Onion, sliced
Paprika
Salt & pepper
2 c  Lobster meat
4 T  Butter
1 c  Water
2 c  Hot milk
Simmer the lobster meat in the butter over low heat for 7 or 8
minutes. Combine potatoes, onion, and water and boil until tender.
Add hot milk and lobster mixture.  Season to taste and add a dash of
paprika. From: Red's Lobster Recipes Shared By: Pat Stockett
