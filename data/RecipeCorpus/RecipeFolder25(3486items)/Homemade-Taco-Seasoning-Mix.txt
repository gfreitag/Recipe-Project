Title: Homemade Taco Seasoning Mix
Categories: Tex/mexican, Herbs/spice, Mixes, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
3 ts Onion Powder
2 1/2 ts Garlic Powder
1/8 ts Cayenne Pepper
6 ts Chili Powder
5 ts Paprika
4 1/2 ts Cumin
In storage container with tight-fitting lid, combine all ingredients;
mix well. Seal tightly. Store in cool, dry place for up to 6 months.
Makes 21 teaspoons (about 1/2 cup). Seven teaspoons of mix equal a
1.25 oz. pkg. of purchased taco seasoning mix.

TIPS: Cumin, an herb, is ground from cumin seeds, and there is no
substitute for its flavor. Seven teaspoons mix contains 90mg Sodium.
