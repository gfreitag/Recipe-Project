Title: Refreshing Pea Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Onion; chopped
1 tb Pickle relish
1    Egg; hard-cooked, diced
1 1/2 tb Mayonnaise
17 oz Peas, green
2 tb Pepper, green; chopped
1 ts Pimiento; chopped
2 tb Celery; chopped
2 tb Cheese, sharp Cheddar; diced
Drain peas and set aside.  Combine next 6 ingredients and mix well.
Add peas, egg, and mayonnaise and toss gently.  Chill thoroughly.
