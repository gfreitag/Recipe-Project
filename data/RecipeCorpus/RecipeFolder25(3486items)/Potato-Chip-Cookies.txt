Title: Potato Chip Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla
6 oz Bag of potato chips,crushed
(about 1 3/4 Cups).
1 lb Margarine
1    Egg
1 1/4 c  Sugar
3 c  Flour
Cream margarine and sugar.  Add egg, vanilla and beat.  Add flour,
one cup at a time.  Fold in crushed chips.  Drop by spoonfuls on
ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake about 15 minutes in a 350 degree oven.
Let cool a minute or so on cookie sheet before removing.
