Title: Rice Stuffing for Turkey
Categories: Side dish, Sthrn/livng, Stuffing, Rice, Poultry
Yield: 6 cups
4 c  Rice; cold, cooked
1/2 ts Sage
1 ts Thyme
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 lg Onion; chopped
1 tb Shortening
1/2 lb Sausage
1 c  Breadcrumbs, soft
1 c  Milk
Brown onion in shortening; add sausage and cook until brown.  Soak
breadcrumbs in milk.  Combine all ingredients and stuff turkey for
baking.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
Coleman. Submitted By NANCY COLEMAN   On   12-16-94
