Title: Old Bay Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 2 pounds
1/4 ts Ground bay leaf
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Mace
1/4 ts Cardamom
1 ts Sweet paprika
1 ts Lemon juice
4    Feet small hog casings
2 lb Whitefish fillet, thawed if
-frozen
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/4 ts Crushed mustard seed
1/4 ts Fine grind black pepper
Prepare casings. Cut the fish into 2" cubbes and process in food
processor unti lthe fish is just broken, about 2-3 on/off cycles. Add
remaining ingredients and process just until everything is well
blended. Stuff casings and twist off into 3-4" lengths.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
