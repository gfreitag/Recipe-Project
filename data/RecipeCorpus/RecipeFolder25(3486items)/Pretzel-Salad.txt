Title: Pretzel Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Cool Whip
1 ea Six ounce box Strawberry
-Jello
2 ea Eight ounce boxes frozen
-strawberries
2 c  Hot water
2 c  Crushed pretzels
1 1/2 ea Sticks margarine (I use
-butter)
3 T  Sugar
8 ea Ounces cream cheese
1 c  Sugar
For a crust mix pretzels, margarine (or butter), and 3 T sugar.  Pat
into bottom of pan and bake 5-6 minutes at 400 degrees.  Cool. Blend
the cream cheese, 1 cup of sugar, and Cool Whip and spread on top of
the crust. Dissolve Jello in hot water, add frozen strawberries, wait
until almost set and pour over the top of the salad.
