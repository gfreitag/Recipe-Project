Title: Dry Beef Dip
Categories: Dip, Beef, Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Onion, chopped
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
2 tb Butter
1/4 ts Salt
1    8 inch pie plate
8 oz Cream cheese
2 tb Milk
3 oz Dry chopped beef
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Green pepper, chopped
Saute pecans in butter and salt; set aside.  Mix all other ingredients
together and place in a small casserole.  Top with pecan mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.  Spread on crackers. Randy Rigg
