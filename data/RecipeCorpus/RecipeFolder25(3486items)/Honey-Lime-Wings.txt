Title: Honey-Lime Wings
Categories: Chicken, Appetizer, Holiday
Yield: 36 servings
1    Clove of garlic
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 kg Chicken wings
1/4 c  Liquid honey
2 tb Lime juice
Finely grated peel of 1 lime
Tip and half wings. Preheat broiler. Set a rack on a foil-lined baking
sheet or broiler pan. Place wings on rack, spacing 1/2 in apart.
Broil 3 inches from element for 8 to 10 minutes, until golden brown
and skin is crisp. Turn and continue to broil 8 to 10 more minutes,
or until golden brown and chicken is cooked. Meanwhile, stir
remaining ingredients together in a large bowl. When wings are cooked
toss in honey mixture. Serve, drizzling mixture over wings. Source:
