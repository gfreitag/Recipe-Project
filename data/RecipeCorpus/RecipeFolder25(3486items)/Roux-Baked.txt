Title: Roux Baked
Categories: Cajun/creol, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Vegetable oil
Baked Roux
3 c  Flour
Mix the flour and oil in an iron skillet or heavy ovenproof
container. Bake in preheated 400 degree oven 1 1/2 to 2 hours,
stirring every 15 minutes. The color determines the doneness. Cool
before storing. May be frozen.
