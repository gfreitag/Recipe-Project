Title: Wammy Burgers
Categories: Kids, Burgers, Main dish, Hot dogs
Yield: 4 grandsons
4 sl Ham;
4    Hamburger buns;
1 lb Ground beef;
4 sl Cheese;
8    Hot dogs; halved lengthwise
Shape ground beef into patties; place cheese slice on top.  Cook in
frying pan.  Heat hot dogs and ham.  Place ham, hot dogs and
cheeseburger on bun. Add relishes.  Yield 4 servings. Source: The
Pahhellenic Cookbook Meats Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion
and her Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On   11-07-94
