Title: Old-Fashioned Apple Butter
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Spread, Apples
Yield: 1 servings
Dash of Cinnamon
1 c  Applesauce
In a small saucepan, heat ingredients to a boil; turn to low and
simmer, stirring occasionally, until mixture raches desired
thickness. Yields 1/2 cup. Source: Sweet & Sugarfree Shared by:
