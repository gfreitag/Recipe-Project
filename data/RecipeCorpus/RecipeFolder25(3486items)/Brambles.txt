Title: Brambles
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
Juice of lemon
1 tb Flour
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg yolk (whole egg too
Liquid) unbeaten
1 pn Salt
Pastry for two pies cut in
Rounds
Chop:
1 c  Raisins (small as possible)
Peel of one lemon
Mix:
Raisins, lemon
Blend raisin mixture.  Put small amount on each round. Fold over and
press edges with fork.  (Might use egg white to moisten edges) Bake
as pie 375 - 400.  Tip: Cover cookie sheet with foil for easy clean
up.

This recipe came from Graham Kerr when he was a young whipper-snapper
on TV in Canada.  I remember taking it down during his program.

I have made them, and they were really good.
