Title: Oriental Dressing - Bon Appetit
Categories: Asian, Dressing, Salad
Yield: 2 1/4 cups
3    Jalapeno chilies, seeded and
-minced
2 T  Sugar
2 T  Oriental sesame oil
1 c  Plus 2 T soy sauce
1/2 c  Plus 1 T rice vinegar
1/4 c  (generous) minced peeled
-fresh ginger
5 lg Garlic cloves, pressed
Whisk all ingredients in small bowl. (Can be prepared 1 day ahead.
Cover and let stand at room temperature.)

Bon Appetit/August/89  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
