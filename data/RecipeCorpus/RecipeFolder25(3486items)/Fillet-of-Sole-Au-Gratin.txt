Title: Fillet of Sole Au Gratin
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Grated American cheese
1 1/2 lb Sole fillets
2 tb Chopped onion
2 tb Chopped celery
-salt and pepper to taste
2 tb Water
Preheat oven to 450. Place fish in a baking pan, sprinkle with
mixture of onion, celery, salt, and pepper. Add water and lemon
juice; bake for 10 minutes. Melt butter in saucepan, stir in flour.
Add milk; cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until sauce
thickens. Season with salt and pepper. Set aside 2 tb cheese, stir
remaining cheese into sauce until it melts. Pour over fish. Sprinkle
fish with remaining cheese; bake 10-15 minutes.
