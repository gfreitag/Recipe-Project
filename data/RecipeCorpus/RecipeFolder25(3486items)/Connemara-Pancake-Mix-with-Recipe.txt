Title: Connemara Pancake Mix with Recipe
Categories: Pancakes, Mixes, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
-PANCAKES:
4    Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Molasses
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1 pk Mix
2 tb Oil
1 c  Maple syrup
1 1/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
2/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Irish oats
1 tb Sunflower seeds
1 tb Sesame seeds
2 ts Baking powwder
1/4 ts Cinnamon
Mix: Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.

Connemara Pancakes: Beat eggs, milkm molasses and vanilla until
light. Add mix. Refrigerate 2 hours or overnight. Whisk to blend when
ready to use. Cover bottom of griddle with oil. Pour 1/3 cup batter
