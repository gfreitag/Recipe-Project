Title: Turkey Pizza with Macaroni Crust
Categories: Pizza, Pasta, Turkey, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Pepper
1/2 t  Oregano
1/8 t  Garlic powder
1/2 c  Skim milk
1 ea Egg, beaten
Shredded low-fat mozzarella
-cheese
2 c  Uncooked elbow macaroni
1 lb Ground Turkey
1 md Green bell pepper, chopped
1 c  Sliced onion rings
1 cn Tomato sauce (15 oz.)
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Italian seasoning
Cook pasta and drain. In a skillet, combine ground turkey, green
pepper and onion rings. Cook until tender and meat is no longer pink.
Add tomato sauce and seasonings. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and
simmer 20 minutes. In a medium bowl, blend skim milk and egg; add
cooked macaroni and mix well. Spread pasta mixture evenly in a
greased 14 inch pizza pan. Spoon turkey mixture evenly over pasta and
top with cheese. Bake at 350 degrees until hot, about 20 minutes. Let
stand 5 minutes before cutting. Makes 4 to 6 servings. SOURCE:
