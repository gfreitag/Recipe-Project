Title: Espresso Crisps
Categories: Holiday, Dessert, Cake/cookie
Yield: 16 servings
1/4 c  Granulated sugar, mixed with
1 t  Ground cinnamon
1    Portion Chocolate-Coffee
-Dough (see Basic Cookie
-Dough recipe)
Roll dough into an 8x6 inch rectangle on a lightly floured surface.
Slice into four 1 1/2-inch-side strips and cut each strip into 10
triangels. Put on ungreased cookie sheets 1 inch apart.  Bake 8 to 10
min, at 350degF until tops look dry.  Remove to a wire rack. Sprinkle
with cinnamon-sugar while still warm.

Per cookie: 26 ca., 0 g pro, 4 g car, 1 g fat, 4 mg chol with butter,
1 mg chol with margarine, 12 mg sod. Exchanges: 1/8 starch/bread, 1/8
fruit, 1 fat
