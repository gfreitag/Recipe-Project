Title: Montana Sourdough Doughnuts
Categories: Doughnut, Sourdough
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
-fat for frying
2    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Sourdough starter
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 tb Melted lard or cooking oil
4 1/2 c  Sifted all purpose flour
Beat eggs and sugar together.  Add starter, buttermilk, lard, and
sifted dry ingredients.  Mix well.  Turn out dough and knead on a
lightly floured board until smooth.  Roll to 1/2 inch thickness and
cut with 2-3/4 inch doughnut cutter.  Put on greased cookes sheet and
let rise for 30 minutes. Fry in hot deep fat (370F) until golden
brown and done. Roll in sugar. Makes about 3 dozen. NOTE** Starter
may be purchased by mail order from The Gilded Cage, Anchorage
Chapter, Alaska Crippled Children's Association, 225 East Street,
Anchorage, Alaska. Origin: Women's Day Encyclopedia of Cookery, Vol
1, Montana Section. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Feb/94
