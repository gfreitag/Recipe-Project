Title: Bison Bologna
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Game, Beef
Yield: 5 pounds
2 cl Finely minced garlic
2 ts Finely minced shallots
1/2 ts Cayenne
2 ts Sweet paprika
5    Feet medium hog casings
4 lb Bison (buffalo) meat, with
-fat, cubed
1 lb Beef fat, cubed
2 ts Salt
fPrepare casings. Grind the meat and fat together through the coarse
disk. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Grind through the fine
disk twice. Stuff into casings, tie off into 3-4" links and cook as
for any fresh sausage.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
