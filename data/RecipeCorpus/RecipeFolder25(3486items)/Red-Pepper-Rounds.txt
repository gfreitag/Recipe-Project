Title: Red Pepper Rounds
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 8 rounds
2 tb Capers (opt)
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
8 sl Baguette-style French bread,
-toasted or grilled (1")
1 cn Roasted red peppers (7.5 oz)
1    Garlic clove, put through a
-garlic press
2 tb Extra virgin olive oil
2 tb Roughly chopped parsley
Dry peppers with paper towels. Chop coarsely. Mix all ingredients
except bread; heap atop bread.

Per round: 17 milligrams vitamin C, 85 calories, 2 grams protein, 11
grams carbohydrates, 0.5 grams fiber, 3.6 grams fat (0.5 grams
saturated), 105 milligrams sodium.
