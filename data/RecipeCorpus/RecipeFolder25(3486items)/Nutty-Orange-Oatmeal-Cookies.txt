Title: Nutty Orange Oatmeal Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Diabetic, Fruit
Yield: 18 servings
1 c  Rolled oats
1/4 t  Baking soda
2 T  Chopped walnuts
1/4 t  Grated orange peel
1/3 c  Softened unsalted margarine
1/3 c  Plain lowfat yogurt
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1 t  Vanilla
Preaheat oven to 325 F. Blend margarine, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla.
Mix in remaining ingredients. Shape dough by rounded teaspoons into 1
inch balls. Place 2 in apart on an ungreased cookie sheet; flatten
slightly with fork. Bake at 325 for 12 to 15 minutes. Remove
immediately from baking sheet. From the All-In-One Diabetic Cookbook
(no artificial sweeteners) Analysis of one serving = 3 cookies: 1
starch/bread exchange, 1/2 fat exchange - Cal 102, Pro 3, Fat 3, carb
12, Na 15, Chol 0 Posted by PHILLIP BOWER
