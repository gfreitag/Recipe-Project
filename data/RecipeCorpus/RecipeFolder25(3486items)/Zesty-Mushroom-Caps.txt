Title: Zesty Mushroom Caps
Categories: Appetizer, Mushroom, Vegetable, Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Red pepper, finely chopped
1 lg Carrot, grated
2/3 c  KRAFT three cheese Ranch
-dressing
40 lg Mushrooms, stems removed
300 g  Frozen chopped spinach,
-thawed and drained
2 ea Green onions, finely chopped
Wash mushrooms, pat dry and set aside.  Mix remaining ingredient
together, stuff mushrooms.  Cover and refrigerate until serving.
Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before serving.  Mkes 40 pieces.
Per mushroom: 20 cal, pro .7g, carbo 1.8g, fat 1.3 g, good source of
vitamin A Origin: Kraft What's Cooking Magazine, 10th issue. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
