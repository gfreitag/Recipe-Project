Title: Dried Beef Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Casserole, Sthrn/livng, Beef, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Olives, ripe; sliced
1/2 c  Cheese, Cheddar; shredded
2 c  Noodles; cooked
Cheese, Cheddar; shredded
5 oz Beef, dried
3 tb Butter (or marg.)
3 tb Flour, all-purpose
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
Tear dried beef into pieces and set aside.  Melt butter; stir in
flour. Add milk; cook, stirring constantly, until thickened.  Add
dried beef, celery, olives, 1/2 cup cheese, and noodles.  Spoon into
a 1-1/2 quart casserole dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
Sprinkle with additional cheese, and return casserole to oven until
cheese is melted.
