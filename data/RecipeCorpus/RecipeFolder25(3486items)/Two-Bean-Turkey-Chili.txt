Title: Two-Bean Turkey Chili
Categories: Chili, Turkey, Soup/stew, Beans
Yield: 6 servings
1 T  Ground cumin
2 1/2 T  Chili powder
MMMMM----------------1 TSP. EACH: DRIED OREGANO,---------------------
1 lg Onion, diced
1 T  Brown sugar
1/4 t  Ground red pepper
2 T  Olive oil
MMMMM----------------1 EACH DICED: RED AND GREEN---------------------
1 cn (28 ounces) tomatoes,
-diced, liquid reserved
1 ea To 2 jalapeno chili
-peppers, minced
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
MMMMM----------------1 CAN (16 OUNCES) EACH: PINT---------------------
Salt to taste
2 c  Cooked turkey
1/2 c  Chopped fresh cilantro
Combine seasonings in a small dish; set aside.  Heat oil in a large
pan over high heat.  Add half of the seasoning mixture.  Cook and
stir 20 seconds.  Add onion; cook, stirring often, until onion
softens, 8 to 10 minutes.  Add the bell peppers, jalapeno and garlic;
cook 2 minutes longer. Add tomatoes with their liquid and remaining
seasoning mixture; reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, until mixture
thickens slightly about 15 minutes. Add beans and turkey; cook 10
minutes longer.  Stir in cilantro and add salt to taste.  SErves 6.
