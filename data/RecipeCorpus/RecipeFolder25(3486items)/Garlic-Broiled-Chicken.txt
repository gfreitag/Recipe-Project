Title: Garlic Broiled Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM---------------------MAKES: 6 SERVINGS--------------------------
2 1/2-3 1/2 lb whole
-frying chicken, cut in half
1/4 c  Chopped fresh parsley
Cooked Rice
1/4 c  Butter or margarine, melted
1/4 t  Pepper
3 ea Tb fresh minced garlic
2 ea Tb soy sauce
Stir together butter, pepper, garlic, and soy sauce. heat broiler.
Place chicken on greased broiler pan. Broil chicken 6-8" from heat,
turning every 10 minutes and brushing with butter mixture during last
10 minutes for 30-35 minutes or until fork tender. Just before
serving, brush with butter mixture and sprinkle with parsley. Serve
with cooked rice. Source: Land O Lakes Treasury of Country Recipes
