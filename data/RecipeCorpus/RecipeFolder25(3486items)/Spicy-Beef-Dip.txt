Title: Spicy Beef Dip
Categories: Dip, Ground beef, Tomato, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Catsup
3/4 ts Oregano
8 oz Cream cheese
1/3 c  Parmesan cheese
1 lb Ground beef
1/2    Fine chopped onion
1 cl Garlic, powder
8 oz Can of tomato sauce
Brown the ground beef and onion together.  Add the garlic, tomato
sauce, catsup and oregano.  Mix well.  Then add the cream cheese and
Parmesan cheese.  Place over fire in chafing dish for serving.  Serve
hot. Randy Rigg
