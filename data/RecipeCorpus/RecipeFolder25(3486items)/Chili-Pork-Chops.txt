Title: Chili Pork Chops
Categories: Pork, Main dish, Chili
Yield: 6 servings
-undrained
1 c  Quick cooking rice
1/2 c  Coarsely chopped onion
2 T  Watkins chili seasoning mix
1/2 t  Garlic powder
6 ea Green pepper rings
Vegetable oil
2 T  Vegetable oil
6 ea Pork chops cut about 1/4
-inch thick, trimmed of fat.
1 t  Watkins beef soup mix
1/4 t  Black pepper
1 cn (10-3/4oz) tomato soup,
-undiluted
1 cn (15oz) kidney beans,
Heat the 2 tbsp vegetable oil in large oven'proof skillet; brown pork
chops on both sides. Remove chops and sprinkle with beefsoup mix and
pepper. Combine soup, beans, rice, onion, chili seasoning, and garlic
powder in skillet or in 11x7 inch baking dish. Place pork chops over
soup mixture. Cover and bake at 325F for 45-55 minutes or until chops
are tender. Top each chop with a green pepper ring that has been
brushed with oil the last 10 minutes of baking time. Makes 6
servings. Origin: Watkins "Traditions for Today" Catalogue Shared by:
Sharon Stevens.
