Title: Smothered Burritos
Categories: Main dish, Tex/mexican
Yield: 2 servings
1 cn Refried beans, heated (15oz)
4 md Flour Tortillas, heated
1 pk Howlin' Coyote Chili heated
-according to package
-directions
Spread refried beans down center of each tortilla. Spoon a few meat
and vegetable chunks from the chili over the beans. Save the sauce.
Roll up each burrito. Smother burritos with remaining chili sauce.
Garnish with sour cream, shredded cheese, green onions.
