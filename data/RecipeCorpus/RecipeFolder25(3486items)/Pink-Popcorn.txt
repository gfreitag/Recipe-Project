Title: Pink Popcorn
Categories: Popcorn, Candy, Snack
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Coloring
1/2 t  Salt
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1 T  Butter
1 1/3 1 ea 1/2 cups popcorn

Spray a large roaster with Pam, after popping corn pour into roaster.
set aside. Put the remaining ingredients in a large sauce pan. Boil 3
minutes, timing is very important.  Pour over the popped corn and
stir with a wooden spoon until the mixture is all mixed in.  You have
to keep mixing as the sugar will just go to the bottom. Origin: Home
