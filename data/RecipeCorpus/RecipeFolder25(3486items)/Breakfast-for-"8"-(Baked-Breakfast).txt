Title: Breakfast for "8" (Baked Breakfast)
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs, Sausage, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
6 lg Eggs, beaten
1 t  Salt
2 c  Milk
1 lb Sausage
1 c  Grated Cheese
1 t  Dry Mustard
2 sl Cubed Bread
Saute sausage; drain.  Beat Eggs with Milk, Salt and Mustard. Layer
bread, sausage and cheese in 9 X 13-inch pan. Pour egg mixture over
all. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. This is nice for overnight
company. Make ahead except for egg mixture.  Then when ready for use,
top with eggs and bake while you have your morning coffee!
