Title: Ma's Mincement
Categories: Can/preserv, Game, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Cloves
1 t  Crushed coriander seed
1 T  Cinnamon
1 t  Salt
1 t  Pepper
2 ea Whole grated nutmegs
1 ga Mashed sour cherries
2 lb Broken butternuts
2 c  Brandy
9 ea Qts. Sliced peeled apples
4 lb Venison*
2 lb Chopped beef suet
3 lb Sugar
2 ea Qts. Cider
4 lb Seedless raisins
3 lb Currants
1 1/2 lb Chopped orange with peel
1/2 lb Chopped lemon with peel
1 T  Mace
Combine all ingredients in a pot. Cook slowly for 2 hours or more,
stirring frequently. Seal in sterilized jars. Note: Mom always used
venison or bear. I have made this with lean beef and it came out
good. SOURCE:*Louise Bodle POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 12/94
