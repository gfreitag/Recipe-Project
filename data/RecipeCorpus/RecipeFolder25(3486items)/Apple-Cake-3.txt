Title: Apple Cake 3
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Apples
Yield: 1 cake
1 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cloves
Glaze
1/2    Stick Butter
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
2 T  Milk
1 c  Oil
3    Eggs
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
4 c  Chopped Apples
1 c  Chopped Nuts
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking Powder
1 ts Soda
Mix oil, eggs and vanilla.  Sift dry ingredients togeather,add to oil
and egg mixture, mix well.  fold in apples and nuts.  Bake in a tube
pan at 350 for 1 hour and 15 min. remove from pan while warm and
glaze.

Mix glaze ingredients togeather and boil for 1 min, pour over cake
