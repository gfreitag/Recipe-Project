Title: Spiced Lentils
Categories: Main dish, Moroccan, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
5 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
1 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Black pepper
1 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Chili pepper
2 tb Cilantro, chopped
2 c  Dark lentils
1 ea Onion, finely chopped
1/2 ts Turmeric
1 ts Salt
2 lg Onions, sliced
2 tb Ghee
2 ea Tomatoes, peeled & chopped
Soak lentils for 2 hours in cold water & drain.  Simmer them in enough
water to cover plus 1" along with the chopped onion, tirmeric & salt.
Cook for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.  Add more water as necessary.

When lentils are nearly done, saute the sliced onion in the ghee in a
large skillet.  Add remaining ingredients & cook over low heat for 5
to 6 minutes.  Stir in the contents of the lentil pot.  Add cilantro
& simmer for 10 minutes, stirring.
