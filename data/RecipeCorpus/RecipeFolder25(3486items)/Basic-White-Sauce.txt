Title: Basic White Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper, white
2 tb Butter (or marg.)
1 1/2 tb Flour, all-purpose; to 2 tb.
1 c  Milk; hot
Put butter in saucepan or small skillet and melt over low heat.  Stir
it carefully to see that it does not burn.  Add flour, a small amount
at a time, stirring constantly so that flour does not lump.  Stir in
milk, and continue stirring until mixture is desired thickness.  Add
salt and pepper.
