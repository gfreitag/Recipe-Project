Title: Ham-Cheese Ball
Categories: Holiday, Meat, Cheese/eggs, Appetizer, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Paprika;
1/2 ts Salt;
1 cn (2-1/4 oz) deviled ham;
2 tb Parsley flakes;
2 tb Pimento; chopped
2/3 c  Pecans; chopped
2 pk (8 oz) cream cheese;
1/2 lb Sharp cheese; shredded
2 ts Grated onion;
2 ts Worcestershare sauce;
1 ts Lemon juice;
1 ts Mustard;
Soften cream cheese in large bowl.  Add sharp cheese, onion,
Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, onion, mustard, paprika, salt,
deviled ham, parsley flakes, and pimento; mix well.  Chill until
nearly firm. Shape into ball; roll in pecans.  Wrap in foil;
refrigerate overnite. Sprinkle with additional paprika; slice to
serve. Source: The Holidays Cookbook; Favorite Recipes of Home
