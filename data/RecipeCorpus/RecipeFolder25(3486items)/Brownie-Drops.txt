Title: Brownie Drops
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Salt
3/4 c  Chopped nuts
2    Bars Germans sweet chocolate
1 tb Butter
2    Eggs
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Unsifted all purpose flour
Melt chocolate and butter over hot water.  Stir and cool.  Beat eggs
until foamy, add sugar 2 tablespoons as a time.  Beat until
thickened. Blend in chocolate, add flour, baking powder, salt and
cinnamon. Blend. Stir in vanilla and nuts.  Drop by teaspoon onto
greased cookie sheet. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 8 to 10 minutes.
