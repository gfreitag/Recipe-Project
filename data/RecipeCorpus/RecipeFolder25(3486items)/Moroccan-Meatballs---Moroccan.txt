Title: Moroccan Meatballs - Moroccan
Categories: African, Ground beef, Lamb, Main dish, Meatballs
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-------------------------MEATBALLS------------------------------
1 tb Chopped parsley
Cumin
2 ts Paprika
1 lb Ground beef or lamb
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Dried onions
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
Cayenne
Salt
1/4 ts Cumin
1 ts Paprika
1/2 c  Chopped parsley
1    Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  Water or stewed tomatoes
3 tb Sweet butter
1/2 c  Dried & mashed onions
3/4 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Turmeric
1 pn Saffron
Mix all of the ingredients for the meat. Knead well and form into 1"
balls. SAUCE: Place all ingredients in a skillet except lemon. Add 1
1/2 cup water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat cover and simmer for
15 minutes. Add meatballs and simmer for 30 minutes. Add lemon juice
and serve at once on heated platter with lots of Moroccan bread. Stan
