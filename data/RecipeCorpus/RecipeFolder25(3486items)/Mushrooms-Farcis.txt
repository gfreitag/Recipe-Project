Title: Mushrooms Farcis
Categories: Appetizer, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Bread Crumbs
1/2 c  Olive Oil
Pepper;ground;
Salt;
1 lb Mushrooms
2 tb Parsley;chopped
2    Garlic;minced;cloves
3 tb Parmesan Cheese
1 ts Oregano
Preheat oven at 350F. Wash mushrooms and remove stems. Chop the
stems and mix with garlic, parsley, cheese, oregano, bread crumbs,
salt and pepper.
Place mushrooms stem side up in baking dish. Fill mushrooms with
mixture. Add 1/4-inch of water to dish. Pour olive oil evenly over
caps and bake 30 minutes.
