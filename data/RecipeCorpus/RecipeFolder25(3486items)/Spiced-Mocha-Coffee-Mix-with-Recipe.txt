Title: Spiced Mocha Coffee Mix with Recipe
Categories: Mixes, Beverage
Yield: 21 servings
-COFFEE:
1 tb Mix
6 oz Boiling water
-Shaved Chocolate -OR-
-Cinnamon
1/3 c  Instant coffee
1/2 c  Cocoa powder
1/2 c  Nonfat dry milk
1 ts Ground cinamon
2 ts Dried orange peel
Mix: Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.

Spiced Mocha Coffee: Pour water over mix in cup. Garnish with shaved
chocolate or additional cinnamon.
