Title: Mandelkraenzchen (Almond Cookies)
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains, German
Yield: 24 servings
1 ea Egg white, unbeaten
1/4 c  Finely chopped almonds
1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
3 ea Egg yolks, beaten
4 c  Flour
Cream butter and sugar.  Add yolks then flour to make a stiff
dough. Roll out thing on floured board.  Cut with doughnut cutter.
Place cookie rings on greased cookie sheet.  Place small circles on a
seperate cookie sheet. Brush with egg white and sprinkle with
almonds, sugar and cinnamon. Bake at 350 degrees 12-15 minutes or
until golden brown. (sent in the the Abilene Distorter News by Mary
Jo Blankenship, Abilene) Submitted By STEVE KREITLER   On   11-15-94
