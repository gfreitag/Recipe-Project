Title: Holiday Hot Potatoes
Categories: Holiday, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 2 servings
-sweet potatoes or Yams
Toasted slivered almonds
Salt and pepper to taste
Butter
Honey
Lemon Juice
Thick slices of cooked
Heat butter, honey and a little lemon juice in a skillet and add the
potatoes. Then toss gently over medium heat until glazed and lightly
browned, adding a handful of toasted slivered almonds during the last
few minutes of cooking. Season with salt and pepper
