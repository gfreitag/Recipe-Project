Title: Caribbean Fruited Rice
Categories: Rice, Caribbean, Fruit, Vegetable, Apples
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Cashews
1/3 c  Dried apricots, chopped
1/3 c  Raisins
1/3 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
1/3 c  Orange juice
1/2 t  Allspice
1/2 t  Cayenne pepper
3 c  Cooked Instant Brown minute
-rice (or home cooked plain
-brown
Rice)
1 c  Chopped red apple
1 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Green onion, chopped
Mix all ingredients together into large bowl. Spoon into lightly
greased 6 cups ring mould.  Refrigerate.  To serve, unmold carefully
onto glass plate.  Makes 10 servings. Per serving: 207 cal, 4.1 gr
pro, 31.1 carbo, 8.5 gr fat. Origin: KRAFT What's Cooking Magazine,
10th issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
