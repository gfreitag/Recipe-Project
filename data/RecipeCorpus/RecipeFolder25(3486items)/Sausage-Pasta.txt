Title: Sausage Pasta
Categories: Sausage, Pasta, Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
5 lg Carrots peeled/sliced
2 c  Linguine, uncooked
1 c  Italian dressing
Garlic
Oregano
1 lb Hillshire Farm Smoked
-Sausage, any variety
5    Green or red peppers cut
-into strips
3 oz Black olives
1 sm Bag frozen peas
Cook pasta. Saute vegetables. Mix salad dressing, pasta, and
vegetables together with a pinch of garlic and oregano. Serve hot or
cold. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   11-22-94
