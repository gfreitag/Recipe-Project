Title: Minestrone Salad
Categories: Salad, Pasta, Beans, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 t  Dried basil
1/2 t  EACH salt, pepper and
-granulated sugar
14 oz Red kidney beans, drained
-and rinsed
2 ea Carrots, thinly sliced
2 sm Zucchini, thinly sliced
1 c  Sliced celery
1 c  Diced sweet red pepper
1/2 c  Freshly grated parmesan or
-shredded jarlsberg cheese
Enjoy this easy , colorful
-salad with chicken for
-supper, then pack it in
Individual containers for
-the next day's lunch and
-serve with halved
Cherry tomatoes and Italian
-bread.
1 c  Macaroni
1/3 c  Olive oil
1/3 c  White wine vinegar
1/4 c  Water
In saucepan of boiling salted water, cook macaroni for 8-10 minutes or
until tender but fimr.  Drain well.  In large bowl, whisk together
oil, vinegar, parsely, water, basil, salt, pepper and sugar.  Stir in
warm macaronio to coat well.  Add beans, carrots, zucchini, celery,
red pepper and cheese.  Toss to combine.  Makes 6 servings. Per
serving: 295 calories, 11 g protein, 15 g fat, 30 g carbo, very high
source fibre. Origin: Canadian Living, Nov/94. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Nov/94.
