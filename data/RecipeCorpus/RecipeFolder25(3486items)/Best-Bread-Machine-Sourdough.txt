Title: Best Bread Machine Sourdough
Categories: Breadmaker, Bread, Sourdough
Yield: 999 servings
1 tb Oil
1 ts Salt
3/4 tb Yeast
1/2 c  Water, warm
1 c  Sourdough Starter
2 1/4 c  Bread Flour
1 tb Sugar
Place ingredients in order in bread machine.  Makes large loaf in
Hitachi. This is the best sourdough for bread machine that I have
ever tried. Adapted from recipe of Helen Fleischer.
Submitted By JOAN MERSHON   On   10-20-94
