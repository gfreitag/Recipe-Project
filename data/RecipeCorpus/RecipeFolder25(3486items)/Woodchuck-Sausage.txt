Title: Woodchuck Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Game
Yield: 5 pounds
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
1 ts Dried rosemary, crushed
1/2 ts Sage
1/2 ts Thyme
2 tb Chopped parsley
5    Feet medium hog casings
5 lb Woodchuck, cubed
2 ts Salt
2 ts Black pepper
2 cl Finely minced garlic
Prepare casings. Mix remaining ingredients, grind through the coarse
diak, then through the fine disk. Stuff into casings, tie off into 3"
links. Saute by vegetable oil.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
