Title: Surprise Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Bread
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 t  Nutmeg
3/4 c  Of one of the following:
Raisins
Diced apple
Grated carrots
Grated zucchini
Blueberries
1 1/2 c  Unsweetened applesauce
1    Egg, slightly beaten
2 T  Oil
1/4 c  Honey
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 t  Baking powder
1 t  Baking soda
1/2 t  Cinnamon
Mix first four ingredients in a large bowl. Sift in remaining
ingredients. Stir until barely mixed. Add fruit. Spoon into lightly
greased muffin tins. Bake 20 minutes at 375'F.
