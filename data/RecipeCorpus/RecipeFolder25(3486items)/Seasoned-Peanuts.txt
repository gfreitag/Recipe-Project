Title: Seasoned Peanuts
Categories: Snack, Holiday, Appetizer
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM-----------------------MAKES: 2 CUPS----------------------------
1/8 t  Ground red pepper
2 c  Unsalted peanuts*
1 ea Tb butter or margarine
1 t  Onion salt
1/2 t  Garlic powder
Melt butter in small saucepan. Stir in onion salt, garlic powder, and
ground red pepper until combined. Stir in nuts till coated with
mixture. Spread in shallow baking pan; bake at 300~ for fifteen
minutes. Do not stir. Serve warm or cool. NOTE: To use raw peanuts,
coat them with the butter mixture and increase baking time to thirty
minutes or till peanuts are lightly browned, stirring once after
fifteen minutes. Source: BH&G Holiday Appetizers 1994 Carolyn Shaw's
Collection 11/94
