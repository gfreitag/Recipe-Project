Title: Banana Foster
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Creme de banana, liqueur
4    Ripe bananas, peeled, sliced
Vanilla ice cream
1/4 c  Unsalted butter
1/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 c  Dark rum
Stir butter with sugar and cinnamon in heavy skillet over medium heat
until melted. Add dark rum and liqueur and simmer until sauce
thickens, stirring occassionally, about 5 minutes. Add bananas and
stir to coat. Scoop ice cream into dishes. Spoon sauce and bananas
over. Serve immediately. Submitted By NANCY VAINE   On   11-08-94
