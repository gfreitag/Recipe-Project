Title: Bean Supreme Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Beans, Cheese/eggs, Main dish
Yield: 1 casserole
2 c  Bean Soup Mix
1 ds Cayenne pepper;
1 tb Worhcestershine Sauce;
1 1/2 c  Sharp Cheddar Cheese;
1    Spice packet;
2 lb Ground beef -=OR=-
2 lb Ground turkey;
1 lg Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Bell pepper; chopped
16 oz (1 cn) Stewed Tomatoes;
Prepare beans as directed on package, leaving out spice packet.  In
heavy skillet, brown meat in oil.  Add onion and green pepper and
cook until vegetables are light brown.  Mix cooked beans, meat
mixture, spice packet*, tomatoes, Worcestershire Sauce and cayenne
pepper. Pour in a three quart casserole dish.  Top with cheese, cover
and bake at 350 degrees for one hour. * For milder flavor reduce the
amount of spice packet. Please mix spices carefully when less than a
full packet. Source: Leaflet in The Old Farmers Almanac 16 Beans and
Spice Mix Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her
Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On   11-07-94
