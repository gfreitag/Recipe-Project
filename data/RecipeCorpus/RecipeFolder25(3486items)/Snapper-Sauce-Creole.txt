Title: Snapper Sauce Creole
Categories: Fish, Salsa/sauce, Seafood, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/4 t  Cloves
1/4 t  Allspice
10 ea Ripe olives, pitted
3 ea Tomatoes, chopped
1 ea Butter or margarine
1 ea Red snapper, 3-4 lb
1 ea Garlic clove
1 ea Onion
1/2 ea Bell pepper
1 T  Capers
1 T  Chili powder
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Mince and brown in one cup olive oil the garlic, onion and bell
pepper. Next add capers, chili powder, salt, cloves, allspice,
pepper, olives and tomatoes. Place fish in baking pan or dish. Brush
with melted butter or margarine. Pour sauce over the fish and bake
for 35 minutes at 375-F or until flaky, basting 2 or 3 times. Also
for: Any ole fish will do.
