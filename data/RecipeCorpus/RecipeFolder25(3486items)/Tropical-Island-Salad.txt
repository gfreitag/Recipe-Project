Title: Tropical Island Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Cactus pear, peeled and cut
-into 1 inch cubes
1/2 pt Raspberries
DRESSING
1/2 c  Yogurt
2 T  Honey
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1 ea Head lettuce
1 ea Papaya, peeled, seeded and
-cut into 1 inch chunks
1 ea Kiwano, halved and sliced
-(Is this the same as
-kiwi????)
1 ea Starfruit, sliced
Arrange lettuce on platter for individual servings.  Arrange papaya,
kiwano, starfruit, pear and raspberries on lettuce to serve each
person. In a small bowl, combine yogurt, honey and cinnamon, mixing
well.  Place a large spoonful on each salad. Origin: packaging on
sugar cane Shared by: Sharon Stevens ++_ End Stevens Recipe ++-
