Title: Tortilla Roll Ups
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
-(very finely)
2/3 c  Fresh broccoli, VERY finely
-chopped
8 ea 7-inch flour tortillas
250 g  Cream cheese, softened
1/3 c  KRAFT three cheese ranch
-dressing
1 ea Carrot, finely chopped
Mix together the cream cheese, dressing, carrot and broccoli.  Spread
evenly over the tortillas.  Roll tortillas up and store in fridge.
(Makes slicing easier when they are cold) To serve, cut on the
diagonal. Makes about 4-5 dozen pieces. Per roll: 20 cal, .5 gr pro,
2.2 gr carbo, 1.0 gr fat. Origin: KRAFT What's Cooking Magazine, 10th
issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/94.
