Title: Coffeepot Punch
Categories: Beverage, Holiday, Fruit
Yield: 64 (1/2 cup)
1/4 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
2 tb Whole cloves;
4    Sticks cinnamon;
1 ds Salt;
1 cn Unsweetened pineapple juice;
1    Bottle cranberry juice;
-cocktail   (48 oz)
Water;
3/4 c  Sugar;
Pour pineapple juice and cranberry juice cocktail into a large
coffeepot. Add enough water to bring to a liquid to the 32 cup mark
on the coffeepot. In the coffee basket, combine granulated sugar,
brown sugar, cloves, cinnamon sticks and salt.  Perk according to
manufacturer's directions. Serve hot. Source: Beverages, Food
