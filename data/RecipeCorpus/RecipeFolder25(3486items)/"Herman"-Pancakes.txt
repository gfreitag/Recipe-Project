Title: "Herman" Pancakes
Categories: Breakfast, Sourdough, Pancakes
Yield: 5 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1 ea Egg
2 tb Oil
1/2 c  Milk (or more for thinner)
1 c  "Herman" starter
1 c  Flour
2 1/4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
Mix well all ingredients.  (Up to 3/4 c milk depending on desired
thickness).  Using scant 1/4 cup batter per pancake, bake on well-
greased, preheated griddle or skillet about 3 minutes or until tops
are bubbly and edges brown.  Turn;  bake other side about 3 minutes
or until golden. Makes 10 large pancakes.
