Title: Betta' Bruschetta
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 10 servings
1 ea Tomato, chopped
1 sm Clove garlic, minced
1 t  Dried oregano leaves
1/2 t  Pepper
1 ea Vienna style loaf
1/2 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
1 c  Grated mozzarella cheese
1/2 c  Chopped pitted ripe olives
Only the tangy zip of Miracle Whip make Betta' Bruschetta a great
beginning to any meal.  It's a starter everyone's sure to finish.
Begin by heating the oven to 350F.  Cut bread into 20 slices.
Combine remaining ingredients; spread onto slices.  Place slices on a
baking sheet, and bake for 15 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
Serve warm. Makes 10 servings, two pieces each. Origin: Canadian
Living, Nov/94. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
