Title: Muffin Mix with Recipe
Categories: Muffin, Mixes, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
-MUFFINS:
1 pk Muffin mix
1    Egg
3/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 c  Self rising flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Mix: Mix together and store in an airtight container.

Muffins: Preheat oven to 400. Combine muffin mix, egg, milk and oil.
Stir just enough to combine. Spoon into greased muffin cups, filling
3/4 full. Bake 15-18 minutes, until golden brown.

Raspberry or Blueberry Muffins: add 1 cup fresh fruit to the batter,
bake as directed.
