Title: Cherry Delight
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 pk Frozen whipped topping
-(thawed)
1 cn Strawberry or cherry pie
-filling
1 c  Powdered sugar
2 T  Milk
1 ea (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese,
-softened
1 ea (9 in.) baked pie shell
Combine sugar, milk and cream cheese; beat until smooth.  Spoon
mixture into pie shell; sprinkle with pecans.  Refrigerate 30
minutes. Spread whipped topping over pecans.  Top with pie filling.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
