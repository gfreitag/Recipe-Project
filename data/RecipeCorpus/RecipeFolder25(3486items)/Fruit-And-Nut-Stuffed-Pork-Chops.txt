Title: Fruit-And-Nut-Stuffed Pork Chops
Categories: Main dish, Pork, Sthrn/livng, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Pecans, toasted; choppd fine
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Apple pie spice
2 tb Butter (or marg.); melted
1 c  Orange juice; divided
6    Pork chops; 1-1/4" thick
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Breadcrumbs, Italian;divided
1/2 c  Apple; peeled, chopped
1/3 c  Cheese, Cheddar; shredded
Cut a 3" horizontal pocket in each pork chop, cutting to, but not
through, opposite side.  Sprinkle with pepper; set aside.

Combine 1-1/2 cups breadcrumbs and next 6 ingredients; stir in 1/2 cup
orange juice.  Stuff each pork chop evenly with breadcrumb mixture,
and dredge in remaining breadcrumbs.  Place chops in a 13x9x2" baking
dish; add remaining orange juice.  Cover.

Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.  Uncover and bake 30 to 35
additional minutes.  Serve immediately.
