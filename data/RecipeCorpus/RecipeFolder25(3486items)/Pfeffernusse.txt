Title: Pfeffernusse
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Holiday
Yield: 1 recipe
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1 ds Pepper
Powdered sugar
3/4 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Butter
2    Eggs; beaten
4 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/4 ts Baking soda
Cook and stir the molasses and butter until melted.  Cool to room
temperature.  Stir in beaten eggs.  Sift together the flour, sugar,
baking soda, cinnamon, ground cloves, nutmeg, and pepper.  Add to the
molasses, butter, and eggs mixture.  Chill in refrigerator for an
hour or so. Roll dough in small balls.  Bake on greased cookie sheet
(350-degrees F. for 15 minutes for small balls, 375-degrees F. for 12
minutes for larger balls). Shake in powdered sugar in paper sack when
cool. Store in tight container.
