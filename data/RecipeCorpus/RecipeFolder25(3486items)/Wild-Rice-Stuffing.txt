Title: Wild Rice Stuffing
Categories: Stuffing, Side dish, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Marjoram;powdered
1/8 ts Thyme;powdered
1/8 ts Tarragon;crushed dried
1/4 c  Olives;chopped ripe
1/2 c  Butter
2 tb Onion;chopped
1/3 c  Celery;chopped
2 c  Bread Crumbs;dry
2 c  Wild Rice;cooked
1/2 ts Salt
Melt butter in large skillet. Saute onion and celery in butter
until tender.
Add remaining ingredients and toss. Makes 1 quart.
- from A Century of Mormon Cooking
