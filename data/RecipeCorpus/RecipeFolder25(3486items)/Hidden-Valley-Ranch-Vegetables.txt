Title: Hidden Valley Ranch Vegetables
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 pk Vegetable medley (32 oz)
1 pk Hidden Valley Ranch Milk
-Recipe Original Ranch
-Salad Dressing Mix (1 oz)
Combine dry salad dressing mix with oil in large casserole dish. Add
vegetable and toss to coat. Bake at 375'F. for 30 minutes, stirring
every 10 minutes.
