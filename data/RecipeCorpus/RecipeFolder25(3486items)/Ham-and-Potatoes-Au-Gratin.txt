Title: Ham and Potatoes Au Gratin
Categories: Potato, Pork, Casserole, Main dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 c  (4 oz) shredded cheddar
-cheese
3/4 t  Salt
ds White pepper
Chopped fresh parsley
2 c  Sliced peeled potatoes,
-cooked
1 c  Diced cooked ham
1 T  Minced onion
1/3 c  Butter
3 T  Flour
Combine potatoes, ham, and onion in a greased 1-quart casserole; set
aside. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat; stir in flour
until smooth. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until mixture
thickens and bubbles. Add cheese, salt and pepper; stir until the
cheese melts. Pour over potato mixture and stir gently to mix. Bake
at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes or until bubbly. Garnish with
parsley. Serves: 2 From: Reminisce Magazine Posted by:  Debbie
Carlson - Cooking
