Makes 4 cups

Melt butter in saucepan.  Remove butter from heat; add liquid
ingredients, stirring well.  Add pecan halves to mixture and stir to
coat.

Spread pecan halves on a cookie sheet or jellyroll pan.  Bake at 350
F for 13 to 15 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes. Place pecans on
paper towels; sprinkle lightly with salt, and cool completely.
