Title: Orange Glaze for Ham
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Pork, Sthrn/livng, Fruit
Yield: 1 cup
4 tb Mustard, prepared
1/4 c  Sugar
6 oz Orange juice concentrate;
-thawed and undiluted
1/2 ts Ginger, ground
Combine all ingredients, and stir unitl smooth.  Spoon over ham
during last 30 minutes of baking time.
