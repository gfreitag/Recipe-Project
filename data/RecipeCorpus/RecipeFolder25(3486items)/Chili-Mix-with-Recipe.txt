Title: Chili Mix with Recipe
Categories: Chili, Mixes, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Oil
2 lb Ground meat
1 pk Chili mix
16 oz Can tomato puree
2 c  Water
-Grated Cheddar cheese
-Chopped onions
2 ts Ground cumin
2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/3 c  Masa harina
-CHILI:
Mix: Combine and store in an airtight container

Chili: Saute meat in oil until no longer pink. Add chili mix, tomato
puree, and water. Simmer 45 minutes, stirring so it doesn't stick.
Serve with cheese and onions on side. 6 servings.
