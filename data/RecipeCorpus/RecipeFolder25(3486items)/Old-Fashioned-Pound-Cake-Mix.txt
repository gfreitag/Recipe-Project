Title: Old Fashioned Pound Cake Mix
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sour cream
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Orange extract
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 pk Mix
2 3/4 c  Sugar
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
-CAKE:
1 c  Butter, softened
6    Eggs
Mix: Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.

Old Fashioned Pound Cake: Preheat oven to 350. Grease and flour an
8-9 cup bundt pan. Cream butter until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one
at a time, beating well after each. Add the sour cream and
extracts.Add mix and blend at low speed until smooth. Pour into pan
and bake 1 hour a
