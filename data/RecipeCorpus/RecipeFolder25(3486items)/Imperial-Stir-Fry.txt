Title: Imperial Stir-Fry
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Asian, Stir fry, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Oyster Sauce;bottled
3 tb Peanut Oil
Rice;Steamed;
3 lb Beef;thin sliced sirloin
1    Asparagus;bunch
3    Scallion;chopped;
1    Garlic;chopped;clove
Steam asparagus until just done; do not overcook. Refresh under
cold water; cut into chunks.
In a wok, heat peanut oil very hot. Add beef sirloin and garlic;
cook 1 minute. Add scallions; cook 1 minute more. Add asparagus and
oyster sauce; cook 3 minutes or until sauce is bubbling and asparagus
heated through.
Serve with steamed rice.
