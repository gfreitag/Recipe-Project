Title: Watermelon Wally
Categories: Fruit, Alcohol, Beverage
Yield: 6 servings
3 T  Lime juice
6 c  Cubed watermelon
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Light rum
In a small bowl comine the sugar, rum, and lime juice.  Place the
watermelon in a serving dish and pour the rum mixture over all.
Cover and chill for 2-3 hours.  Serves 6. Origin: Aunt Bee's Mayberry
Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
