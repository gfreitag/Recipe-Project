Title: Southern Fried Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Sthrn/livng, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
Salt and pepper; to taste
1/2 ts Paprika
1 c  Bacon fat
1 c  Shortening
1 tb Butter
1 lg Fryer
1 c  Cream or milk
2 tb ;Water
2    Eggs
2 c  Flour
Cut chicken into frying pieces.  Add salt and pepper to chicken.  Set
aside for 15 minutes.  Mix cream, water and eggs; beat.  Pour cream
mixture over chicken and allow to soak for one hour.  Add paprika to
flour, then roll chicken in flour.  Melt bacon fat, shortening and
butter in skillet. Drop chicken in hot grease and fry on medium heat
until golden brown.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
