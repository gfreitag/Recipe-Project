Title: Kentucky Swiss Steak
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Tomatoes, 15 oz
1 ts Pepper
1 1/2 c  Water
2 ts Oil
8 ea Minute Steaks
1/4 c  Flour
1    Green Pepper
6    Carrots, sliced
1 sm Onion, chopped
Roll the Steaks in the flour, fry until brown on both sides, place the
browned steaks into a large roaster, top with Green Pepper, Carrots
and Onions.  Pour the Tomatoes over all and sprinkle with Pepper. Add
water and cover, bake at 350 for about 1 hour.

From Kentucky Favorites Paul Howard
