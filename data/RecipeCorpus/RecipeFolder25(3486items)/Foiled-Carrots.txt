Title: Foiled Carrots
Categories: Side dish, Bbq/grill, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 1 ?(depends)
Pepper; to taste
Parsley sprigs
3    Carrots; (aprox) per serving
1/2 c  Butter (or marg.); melted
Salt; to taste
Punch 3 or 4 holds in each carrot with a metal skewer.  Place carrots
for individual servings on squares of aluminum foil.  Brush with
melted butter, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and add a sprig of
parsley. Fold foil to seal packet and cook on grill at low heat for
45 to 50 minutes.
