Title: Applesauce-Bran Breakfast Cake
Categories: Bread, Cake/cookie, Breakfast
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Grated nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Chopped nuts
1/4 c  Margarine
2 c  Applesauce
1 c  Unbleached all-purpose flour
1 1/2 c  Oat Bran
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking soda
Preheat the oven to 350F.  Grease the bottom only of a 9" baking pan.

In a large saucepan, heat the margarine and applesauce together over
medium heat until the margarine is just melted, then remove from
heat. Add the remaining ingredients, stir quickly to moisten, then
pour the batter into the baking pan.  Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or
until toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
