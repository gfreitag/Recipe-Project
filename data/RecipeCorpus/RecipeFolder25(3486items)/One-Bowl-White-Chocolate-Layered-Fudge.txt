Title: One Bowl White Chocolate Layered Fudge
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 48 bars
2 t  Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Chopped DIAMOND Walnuts
6    Squares BAKER'S Premium
-White Chocolate
2 pk BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Chocolate
-(8 squares each)
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
-(14 oz)
1/2 c  Sweetened condensed milk
MICROWAVE chocolate and 1 can sweetened condensed milk in large
microwavable bowl on HIGH 2-3 minutes or until chocolate is almost
melted, stirring halfway through heating time. Stir until chocolate
is completely melted.

STIR in vanilla and walnuts. Spread in foil-lined 8" square pan. Melt
Premium White Chocolate as directed on package. Stir in 1/2 cup
sweetened condensed milk. Spread over fudge in pan. Refrigerate as
directed.

REFRIGERATE 2 hours or until firm. Cut into squares.
