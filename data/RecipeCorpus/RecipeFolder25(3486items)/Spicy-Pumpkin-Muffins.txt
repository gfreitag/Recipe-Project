Title: Spicy Pumpkin Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Pumpkin puree
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter or
-margarine, melted
1/2 c  Golden raisins.
Heat oven to 400.  Grease
-12 muffin cups.
1 1/2 c  Unsifted all purpose flour
1/3 c  Sugar
1 T  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Ground cinnamon
1/2 t  Ground nutmeg
1 lg Egg
In a bowl, combine dry ingredients and blend well. Beat egg with milk,
pumpkin and butter until smooth. Stir pumpkin mixture into dry
ingredients and blend until moistened.  Stir in raisins. Spoon into
muffin cups and bake until lightly browned++15 to 20 minutes. Serve
warm. Shared By: Pat Stockett
