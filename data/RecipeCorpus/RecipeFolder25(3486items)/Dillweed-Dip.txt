Title: Dillweed Dip
Categories: Herbs/spice, Mixes, Dip
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Lowfat yogurt
3 tb Dillweed dip mix
1 c  Regular or lowfat
-mayonnaise
1 c  Sour cream -OR-
Combine and refrigerate until ready to serve. Serve with raw
vegetables or as a potato topper.
