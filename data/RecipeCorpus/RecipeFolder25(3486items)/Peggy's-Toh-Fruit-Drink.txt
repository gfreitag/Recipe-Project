Title: Peggy's Toh-Fruit Drink
Categories: Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2    To 3 cups ice
1 c  Fresh/frozen strawberries*
1    Banana
1/2 pk Tofu
2 tb Honey; or to taste
*Frozen orange juice or any other berry fruits can be substituted for
the strawberries.

In food processor or blender, put banana, honey and fruit; blend until
smooth.  Slowly add tofu; continue to blend for 1/2 minute or until
smooth. Add 2 to 3 cups ice, until desired thickness is reached.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
