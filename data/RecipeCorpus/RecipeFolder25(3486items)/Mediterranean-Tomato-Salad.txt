Title: Mediterranean Tomato Salad
Categories: Salad, Middle east, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Celery, sliced
1/4 c  Mint, chopped
3 tb Parsley, chopped
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
4 ea Tomatoes, thinly sliced
1 lg Cucumber
1/4 c  Black olives, sliced
1/4 c  Scallions, thinly sliced
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
3/4 ts Salt
Black pepper, to taste
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Lemon juice
Place tomatoes in a large serving dish.  Peel the cucumber.  Halve
lengthwise, seed & slice thinly.  Add to the tomatoes.

Add the remaining salad ingredients, toss & set aside.

DRESSING: Whisk together all the dressing ingredients in a small
bowl. Pour this dressing over the salad, toss gently & refrigerate
until ready to use.
