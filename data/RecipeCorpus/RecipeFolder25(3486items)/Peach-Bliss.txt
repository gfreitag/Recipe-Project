Title: Peach Bliss
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 2 servings
ds Cinnamon, ground
1/4 ts Almond extract
Cake, white or yellow; slicd
8 oz Peach slices
1 1/2 ts Cornstarch
3/4 ts Lemon juice; freshly squeezd
Drain peaches, reserving 1/2 cup syrup.  Combine reserved syrup,
cornstarch, lemon juice, and cinnamon in saucepan.  Cook over medium
heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils; allow to boil 1
minute. Add almond extract and peach slices; heat thoroughly.  Serve
warm over cake slices.
