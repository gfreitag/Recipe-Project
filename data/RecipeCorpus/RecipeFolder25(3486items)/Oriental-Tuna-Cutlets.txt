Title: Oriental Tuna Cutlets
Categories: Seafood, Bbq/grill, Asian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
4 x  Tuna cutlets (steaks)
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1 ts Grated lemon rind (zest)
1 cl Garlic, crushed
1 ts Garam masala
1/4 c  Fresh orange juice
1/3 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Chopped parsley
1 tb Lemon juice
Combine all above ingredients in a shallow container, put 4 tuna
cutlets into the container, making sure the sauce is on all parts of
the cutlets. Marinate for 3 hours. Grill and baste with marinade
while grilling. About 3 mins. for each side. Serve with green salad
tossed in lemon juice and honey.
