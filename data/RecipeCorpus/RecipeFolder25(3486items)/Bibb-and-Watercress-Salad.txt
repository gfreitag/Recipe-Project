Title: Bibb and Watercress Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Watercress; torn
1/2 c  Vinegar, white wine
1 ts Mustard, Dijon
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper, coarsely ground
1/2 c  Oil, vegetable
1 md Pepper, green; finely choppd
1 md Cucumber; peeled, seeded,
-and finely chopped
1/2 c  Onion, green; sliced
2    Heads lettuce, bibb
2    Heads radicchio
Combine first 3 ingredients, and set aside.

Arrange Bibb lettuce and radicchio leaves evnely on individual salad
plates; top with vegetable mixture, and sprinkle with watercress.  Set
aside.

Combine vinegar and next 3 ingredients in contgainer of an electric
blender; process 1 minute.

Turn blender on high; gradually add oil in a slow, steady stream. Pour
dressing over salads, and serve immediately.
