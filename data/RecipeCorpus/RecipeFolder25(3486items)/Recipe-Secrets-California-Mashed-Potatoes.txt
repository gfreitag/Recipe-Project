Title: Recipe Secrets California Mashed Potatoes
Categories: Vegetable, Potato, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 pk Lipton Recipe Secrets Onion
-Soup Mix
3/4 c  Milk, heated to boiling
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 lb All-purpose potatoes,
-peeled, if desired, and
-cut into chunks
Water
2 tb Chopped fresh parsley (opt)
In 3-quart sausepan, cover potatoes with water. Bring to a boil over
high heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer 20 minutes or until potatoes
are very tender; drain. Return potatoes to saucepan. Mash potatoes.
Stir in parsley and onion soup mix blended with hot milk and sour
cream. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   11-22-94
