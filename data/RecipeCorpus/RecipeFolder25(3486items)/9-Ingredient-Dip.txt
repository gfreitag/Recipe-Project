Title: 9 Ingredient Dip
Categories: Dip
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Dill weed
1/2 ts Accent
2 dr Tabasco sauce
2/3 c  Hellman's mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb Dry minced onion
1 tb Dry parsley
1 ts Lowry's salt
Mix well and refrigerate.  This is a real good dip for veggies. Randy
