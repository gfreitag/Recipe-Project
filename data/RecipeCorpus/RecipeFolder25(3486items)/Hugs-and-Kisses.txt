Title: Hugs and Kisses
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie, Holiday, Dessert
Yield: 16 servings
12    Chocolate kisses, or Hugs
-candies, unwrapped
1    Portion Chocolate Dough
-see Basic Cookie Dough
-recipe
Roll dough into an 8-inch log and slice into 12 pieces.  Roll each
piece into a ball.  Put on ungreased cookie sheets  1 inch apart and
bake 10 to 12 min, until tops are slightly cracked.  Immediately push
a candy into center of each.  Remove to a wire rack to cool.

Per cookie: 95 cal, 1 g pro, 11 g car, 6 g fat, 16 mg chol with
butter, 5 mg chol with margarine, 46 mg sod. Exchanges: 1/4
starch/bread, 1/2 fruit, 1 fat
