Title: Zesty Breaded Chicken Breasts
Categories: Poultry, Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
-Herb with Red Pepper Soup
-Mix
1/2 c  Plain dry bread crumbs
4    Boneless skinless chicken
-breast halves (about 1 lb)
3 tb Margarine or butter, melted
1 pk Lipton Recipe Secrets Fiesta
Preheat oven to 350'F.

Dip chicken in melted margarine, then fielta herb with red pepper
soup mix combined with bread crumbs. In 13x9" baking or roasting pan,
arrange chicken; drizzle with remaining margarine. Bake 20 minutes or
until done.

Variation: For CHEESY CHICKEN BREASTS, substitute 1/4 cup grated
Parmesan cheese for bread crumbs.
