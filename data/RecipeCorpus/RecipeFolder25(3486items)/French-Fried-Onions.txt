Title: French-Fried Onions
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Flour, self-rising
Oil, salad
Salt
4 lg Onions, white
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs; beaten
Cut onions into 1/4" slices and separate into rings.  Combine milk and
eggs; soak onion rings in milk-egg mixture for 2 hours.  Drain. Dredge
onion rings in flour, and dip again in milk-egg mixture. Dredge in
flour again, and deep fry in oil heated to 365 degrees. Drain on
absorbent paper. Salt and serve immediately.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, March 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
