Title: Minted Fruit Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit
Yield: 8 cups
1/3 c  Water
1/4 t  Peppermint extract
8 c  Cut-up assorted fresh fruits
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Orange juice
1/3 c  ReaLemon Lemon Juice from
-Concentrate
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients except fruit; stir until sugar
dissolves. Place fruit in large shallow dish; pour ReaLemon brand
mixture over. Cover; chill 3 hours or overnight, stirring
occasionally. Refrigerate leftovers.
