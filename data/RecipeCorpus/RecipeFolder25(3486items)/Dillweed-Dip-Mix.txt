Title: Dillweed Dip Mix
Categories: Dip, Mixes, Herbs/spice
Yield: 2 servings
1/3 c  Spice Islands Beau Monde
-seasoning
1/2 c  Dried dillweed
1/2 c  Dried minced onion
1/2 c  Dried parsley
Combine all ingredients.

Use for Dillweed Dip and Shrimp and Pasta Salad (separate recipes).
