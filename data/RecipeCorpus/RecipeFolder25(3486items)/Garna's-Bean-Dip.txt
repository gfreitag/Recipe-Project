Title: Garna's Bean Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Beans
Yield: 2 cups
8 oz Cream Cheese
15 oz Chili, with or without beans
MMMMM--------------------------OPTIONAL-------------------------------
4 oz Nacho Cheese Sauce
4 oz Green Chili
Cut cheese into cubes and melt slowly on low heat in a small
saucepan. Add chili and heat until bubbly.  Serve as a soup with
flour tortillas or as a dip with corn tortilla chips.
