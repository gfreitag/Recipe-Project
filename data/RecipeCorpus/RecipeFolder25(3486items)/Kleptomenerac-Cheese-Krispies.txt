Title: Kleptomenerac Cheese Krispies
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains, Rice, Snack
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Self rising flour
2 c  Rice Krispies (breakfast
-cereal)
They'll steal your taste
-buds.
12 oz Sharp cheddar cheese
1 c  Margarine
In a large saucepan over low heat, melt the cheese and margarine
together. Remove from the heat and add the flour.  Stir in the Rice
Krispies and mix thoroughly.  Roll into small balls and place on a
cookie sheet. Bake in a 325F oven for 15 minutes.  Serves 6.
Submitted by: Bobbie Carson, Nashville, Tennessee Origin: Aunt Bee's
Mayberry Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
