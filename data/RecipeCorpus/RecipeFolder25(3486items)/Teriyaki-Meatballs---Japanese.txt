Title: Teriyaki Meatballs - Japanese
Categories: Japanese, Beef, Meatballs
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Onion; grated
1 c  Milk
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
-Arline Fifield
4    Eggs
4 lb Ground beef
1 c  Cornflake crumbs
MMMMM-----------------------TERIYAKI SAUCE----------------------------
4    Garlic cloves; minced
2 ts Sugar
2 c  Soy sauce
1 c  Water
4 ts Ginger
Beat eggs and mix thoroughly with remaining ingredients. Shape into
small balls about walnut size. Arrange in a layer in a shallow baking
pan. Mix Tariyaki sauce and pour over all.  Bake in slow oven (300
deg) about 45 minutes - turning and basting every 15 minutes.
