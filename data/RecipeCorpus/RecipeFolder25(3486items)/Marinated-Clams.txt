Title: Marinated Clams
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Clam/oyster
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Pepper
1 sm Head lettuce
2    Hard boiled eggs, cut into
-wedges
2    Tomatoes, sliced
Fresh dill sprigs for
-garnish
2 lb Shelled clams
2    Onions, minced
2 cl Garlic, crushed
1/3 c  Olive oil
5 tb Chopped parsley
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
Place clams in bowl. In seperate bowl combine onion, garlic, olive
oil, parsley, lemon juice, salt, and pepper; pour over clams. Cover
and refrigerate 2-3 hours. Drain clams; reserve marinade. Place
lettuce leaves on large platter. Arrange marinated clams with
marinade to taste on top of lettuce. Place egg wedges and tomato
slices around clams. Garnish with dill sprigs and serve.
