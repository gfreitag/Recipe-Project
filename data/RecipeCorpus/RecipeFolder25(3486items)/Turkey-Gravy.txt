Title: Turkey Gravy
Categories: Diabetic, Poultry, Salsa/sauce, Holiday
Yield: 7 sweet ones
1/4 c  Dry white wine
-=OR=-
1/4 c  White grape;
1/4 ts Salt;
1/3 ts Pepper;
1 tb Reduce-cal margarine;
3 tb All-purpose flour;
1 c  Low-sodium chicken broth;
1 c  Turkey drippings; defatted
-=OR=-
1 c  Low-sodium chicken broth
Melt margarine in a medium-sized heavy pan over medium heat.  Stir in
flour boil 1 minute, stirring constantly with a wire whisk. Gradually
add broth, turkey drippings, wine, salt and pepper, stirring
constantly with whisk. Bring to a boil, still constantly. Reduce heat
and simmer, uncovered, until slightly thickened. Food Exchanges per
serving: 1/2 FAT EXCHANGES; CAL: 1; TOTAL FAT: 1gm; CHO: 0mg; SOD:
29mg; SOD WITH ADDED SALT: 111mg; CAR: 3gm; Source: The Diabetic
