Title: Pili Pili
Categories: German, Chilies, Salsa/sauce, Condiment
Yield: 1 cup
1/2    Lemon, juice only
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 pn Oregano
10    Habanero chilies, stemmed
1/2 md Onion, yellow
1 md Garlic clove
Chop chilies, onion and garlic in a processor.  Add other ingredients
and blend.  Refrigerate (sauce keeps 2 weeks).  Makes 1 cup.

Variations: In Berlin, this sauce is made with fresh ginger.  Or, omit
olive oil and serve Pili Pili as a relish with grilled meat.
