Title: Goosewurst
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Game, Poultry
Yield: 5 pounds
1/4 c  Chopped fresh chives
1 ts Mace
1 ts Ground coriander
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Sweet paprika
5    Feet medium hog casings
5 lb Goose meat-15% fat
1/2 c  Drambuie
2 ts Salt, or to taste
2 ts White pepper
1/2 c  Very finely chopped onion
Prepare the casings. Grind goose through the coarse disk, combine with
Drambuie and refrigerate at least three hours. Mix with remaining
ingredients; grind using fine disk and stuff into casings. Twist off
into 3-4" links. Saute in vegetable oil until evenly browned and
cooked through.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
