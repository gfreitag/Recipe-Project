Title: Zippy Stuffed Celery Stickes
Categories: Appetizer, Vegetable, Sthrn/livng, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 24 sticks
-whole caraway seeds
24    Celry ribs (3")
Parsley, fresh; minced
8 oz Cheese, cottage (creamy)
1/4 c  Cheese, blue (or Roqueufort)
1 ts Salt
2 ts Dill seed, ground; OR 2 ts.
Blend cottage cheese with blue or Roquefort cheese.  Add salt and
dill. Mix well.  Stuff lightly into ribs of celery.  Garnish with
minced parsley.
