Title: Cod Soup with Orange
Categories: Soup/stew, Potato, Seafood, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Can crushed tomatoes,
-undrained
2    Cloves garlic, minced
-Salt and pepper to taste
1/2    Orange, juiced
2 tb Chopped parsley
4 c  Fish stock
2/3 lb Cod fillets, cut into 1"
-cubes
6    Potatoes, cubed
1    Fennel, cubed
1    Leek, white part only,
-shredded
Bring fish stock to a boil. Add remaining ingredients except orange
juice and parsley; simmer 10-12 minutes. Add orange juice; simmer 3-4
minutes more. Serve soup piping hot, sprinkled with parsley.
