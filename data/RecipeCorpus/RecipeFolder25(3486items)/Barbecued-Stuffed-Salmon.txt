Title: Barbecued Stuffed Salmon
Categories: Seafood, Main dish, Bbq/grill, Rice
Yield: 6 to 8 servi
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Tobasco sauce
1    Salmon (about 5 1/2 lb.)
1 c  Cooked rice (mixed brown &
-long grain)
1/4 c  Chopped green onion
1 c  Dry White Wine
1/2 c  Lemon Juice
1 md Medium onion chopped
2    Cloves Garlic, minced
1 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Thyme
Instructions:

In a small saucepan, combine the first nine ingredients for marinade;
bringing to boil.  Pour into a large shallow pan; cool.  Clean the
salmon; remove head and tail; wash inside and out.  Score the salmon
to make several 1/4 in. deep cuts diagonally in the skin.  Place in
the marinade, turning to moisten both sides.  Refrigerate, covered, 6
hrs, turning occasionally.  Remove salmon from marinade; pat dry with
paper towels. Reserve marinade.  Mix rice and green onions.  Loosely
fill the salmon with rice mixture, closing opening with skewers and
kitchen cord. Over coals on a barbecue or under a broiler in a stove,
broil salmon 4 in. from heat about 15 minutes a side, brushing
frequently with reserved marinade, or until salmon is lightly browned
and flakes easily.
