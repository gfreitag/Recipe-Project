Title: Country Chicken Piccata
Categories: Chicken, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------MAKES: 4 SERVINGS--------------------------
6 ea Tb butter or margarine
1 t  Minced fresh garlic
2 ea Tb lemon juice
1 c  Sliced green onions, about
-1" long
1 c  Fresh mushrooms, halved
Lemon slices
Fresh parsley
1/4 c  Milk
1 ea Egg, slightly beaten
1/3 c  Flour
1/3 c  Crushed corn flakes
1/4 t  Salt
pn Pepper
2 ea Boneless, skinless chicken
-breasts, halved
Combine milk and egg. Combine flour, corn flakes, salt and peppar.
Flatten each chicken breast half to 1/4" thickness. Dip chicken
pieces into milk then into crumbs. In skillet, melt butter. add
garlic and chicken. Cook, stirring occasionally, until golden brown.
Place chicken on serving platter, keep warm. Add remianing buttr to
drippings in pan. Stir until butter melts, stir in lemon juice. Add
green onions and mushrooms. Continue cooking, stirring occasionally,
until heated through. Spoon over chicken. garnish with lemon slices
and parsley. Source: Land O Lakes Treasury of Country Recipes Carolyn
Shaw's Collection 11-1-94
