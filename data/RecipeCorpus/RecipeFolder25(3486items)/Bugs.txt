Title: Bugs
Categories: Chocolate, Candy, Cake/cookie, Dessert, Holiday
Yield: 4 dozen
1 pk Cashews or peanuts
1 pk Chow mein noodles
12 oz Chocolate chips
12 oz Butterscotch chips
Melt chips in microwave then pour over nuts and noodles. Mix well and
drop by spoonfuls on ungreased cookie sheet. chill until firm.
