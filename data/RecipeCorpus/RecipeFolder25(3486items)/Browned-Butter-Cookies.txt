Title: Browned Butter Cookies
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie
Yield: 30 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Butter
1/3 c  Sugar
1    Egg
Put butter into heavy skillet and slowly heat until butter is browned
about 5 minutes.  turn into mixing bowl.  beat the butter with the
sugar, egg, vanilla until light.  Add flour and baking powder,
stirring to make a stiff dough.  Shape into little balls using about
1 level tablespoon dough for each.  Place on cookie sheet.  Press
center down with thumb to make and indentation.  Bake at 350 for 10
to 12 minutes or until cookies are firm but not overlybrowned.
