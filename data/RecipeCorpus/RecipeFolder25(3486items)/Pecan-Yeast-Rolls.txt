Title: Pecan Yeast Rolls
Categories: Pastry, Nuts/grains, Dessert
Yield: 3 dozen
2    Eggs; beaten
5 c  Flour, all-purpose
1/3 c  Butter (or marg.); melted
1 c  Sugar, light brown; firmly
-packed
1 c  Pecans; chopped
108    Pecan halves
2 lb Yeast, dry
1/4 c  Water; very warm
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Shortening
1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Lemon rind; grated
Dissolve yeast in very warm water.  Scald milk and add shortening,
sugar, salt, and lemon rind.  Cool to lukewarm.  Stir in beaten eggs
and dissolved yeast.  Add half the flour and beat thoroughly.  Add
remaining flour and mix well.  Turn out onto lightly floured board
and knead lightly. Place in greased bowl; cover and set in a warm
place.

Allow dough to rise until double in bulk.  When light, punch down and
turn out onto floured board.  Roll dough in large rectangle about 1/2
inch thick.  Spread with melted butter or margarine, brown sugar, and
chopped pecans.  Roll up jellyroll fashion, and cut in 3/4" slices.
Grease muffin tins and place 3 pecan halves in bottom of each cup;
put a slice of the roll on top, cut side down.  Allow to rise again
until double in bulk. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 to 18 minutes.
