Title: Nippy Cheese Log
Categories: Diabetic, Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Dip
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 t  Garlic powder
1/4 t  Paprika
pn Chili powder
2 dr Hot pepper sauce
1 c  Edam cheese, shredded
1/2 c  Cottage cheese, well mashed
1/4 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1/3 c  Parsley, chopped
Combine edam, cottage, cheddar and parmesan cheeses, 1 T parsley,
garlic poweder, paprika, chili powder and hot pepper sauce in a
mixing bowl. Mix well (A food processor works well). Form into a log;
roll in remaining chopped parsley. Wrap and refrigerate for several
hours. Remove from refrigerator 20 minutes before serving.

Makes 8 servings Each serving = 2 Tablespoons
1 protein choice
1/2 fat/oil choice
1 g carbohydrate
8 g protein
6 g fat
380 kilojoules
90 calories
