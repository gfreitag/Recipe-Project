Title: Honey Fudge
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
2 tb Butter or butter substitute
1/4 c  Honey
2 c  Sugar
2    Squares unsweetened
-chocolate
2/3 c  Evaporated milk
Dissolve sugar in milk and honey.  Add grated chocolate, butter, and
salt. Stir until well blended.  Cover until boiling point is reached.
Boil to soft ball stage (234 - 238 F).  Cool to room temperature. Add
flavoring. Beat until mixture is thick, creamy, and will hold its
shape when dropped from a teaspoon. The Household Searchlight
