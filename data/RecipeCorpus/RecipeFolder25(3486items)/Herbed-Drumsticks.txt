Title: Herbed Drumsticks
Categories: Chicken, Appetizer, Snack, Herbs/spice
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2 ts Basil
24    Chicken Drumsticks
3/4 c  Butter-melted
1 1/2 c  Dry Bread Crumbs
1/4 c  Parmesan Cheese-grated
2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Oregano
Combine bread crumbs, cheese, salt and herbs in shallow pan or
dish. Dip drumsticks in melted butter; then roll in crumb mixture.
Place on two foil-lined 15x10x1 inch baking pans. Bake at 350 F for
50 minutes to 1 hour. For uniform browning, exchange positions of
pans in oven after 30 minutes.
