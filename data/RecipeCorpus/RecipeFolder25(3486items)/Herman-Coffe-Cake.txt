Title: Herman Coffe Cake
Categories: Bread, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Mayonnaise
1 c  Chocolate chips
1 c  Nuts
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Melted butter
2 c  Herman
2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
2/3 c  Oil
~-------------------TOPPIMG---------------------------- ~--- Bake in
a 350 oven for at least 30 minutes. Mix all ingredients and put in
greased 9 x 13 pan and top with the topping mixture.
