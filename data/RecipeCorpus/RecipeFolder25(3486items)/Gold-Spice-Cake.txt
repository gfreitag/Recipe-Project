Title: Gold Spice Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, 1941, Herbs/spice
Yield: 6 servings
2/3 c  Chopped raisins
2/3 c  Chopped nuts
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Allspice
To the recipe for gold cake add:

Sift spices with flour, baking powder, and salt.  Dredge raisins in 2
tablespoons of the flour.  Add raisins and nuts.  Pour into
well-oiled loaf pan.  Bake in moderate oven (375 F) about 40 minutes.
