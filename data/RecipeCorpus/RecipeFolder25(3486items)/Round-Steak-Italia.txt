Title: Round Steak Italia
Categories: Main dish, Sthrn/livng, Beef
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Shortening
15 oz Spaghetti sauce w/mushrooms
10 oz Green beans, Italian; frozn
16 oz Onions, whole; drained
1 1/2 lb Steak, round; 3/4 to 1" thck
3 tb Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Pepper
Rub steak with mixture of flour and seasonings; reserve remainder of
flour mixture.  Cut steak into 6 pieces.  In large skillet over
medium heat, brown steak in hot shortening.  Place in baking dish,
11-1/2x7-1/2x1-1/2". Heat sauce and flour mixture to boiling,
stirring constantly. Pour over steak; cover.  Bake at 375 degrees for
45 minutes. Add vegetables; cover and bake 45 minutes.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, November 1972. Typos by Nancy
