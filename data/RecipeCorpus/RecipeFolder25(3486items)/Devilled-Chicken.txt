Title: Devilled Chicken
Categories: Casserole, Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  Water
1 t  Oil
1    Chicken,broiler-fryer,8ths
1/2 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 T  Brown sugar
1 T  Mustard,brown,prepared
1. Combine catsup, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, mustard and
water in a small bowl; blend well.

2. Heat oil in a large skillet; add chicken and brown well on both
sides, about 15 minutes.

3. Remove chicken to 10x6x2-inch baking pan.

4. Pour off fat in skillet; add catsup mixture, stirring to loosen
browned particles in bottom of pan.

5. Pour mixture over chicken.

6. Cover and bake in preheated 350'F. oven 30 minutes.

7. Remove cover; bake until chicken is tender, about 10 to 15 minutes
longer.
