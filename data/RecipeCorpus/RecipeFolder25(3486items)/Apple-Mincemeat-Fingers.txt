Title: Apple Mincemeat Fingers
Categories: Cake/cookie, Apples, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 batch
1 c  Butter
3 c  Apples peeled & chopped
1 c  Mincemeat
1 c  Nuts chopped
1/2 c  White sugar
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Nutmeg
2 c  Rolled oats
1 c  Brown sugar packed
MMMMM----------------------CHOCOLATE GLAZE---------------------------
1 c  Icing sugar, sifted
1 pn Salt
1/4 ts Vanilla or 1 tb rum.
1    Square unsweetened chocolate
Melted
2 tb Soft butter
1 1/2 tb Milk
Sift together flour, soda, salt & nutmeg. Add the oats, brown sugar &
butter.  Mix until grainy. Put 1/2 mixture in greased pans (two square
pans) Spread apples on top then mincemeat. Dot the whole with 3 tb.
butter. Cover with rest of mixture, then remaining nuts and sugar.
Bake at 300 for 1 hr. and 15 minutes. If used, spread glaze while
warm.

Origin: Mme Benoit, one of Quebec's popular chefs. Mme Benoit is not
known for low-fat recipes.  I would make this for Christmas. A great
way to use extra mincemeat.
