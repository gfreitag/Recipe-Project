Title: Fruit Salad Delight
Categories: Salad, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Broken nut meats
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
1    Heaping ts of flour
1    Egg
3/4    Of the drained pineapple
Juice
1 cn Pineapple chunks, drained
White seedless grapes
1 c  Chopped natural cheddar
Cheese
2    Heaping ts of sugar
1 tb Vinegar
Combine flour and sugar.  Add egg and beat in the vinegar.  Add
pineapple jice and cook over slow heat until thick (stir constantly).
Add other ingredients when almost cool. Randy Rigg
