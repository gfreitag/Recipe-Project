Title: Toasted Chili Pecans
Categories: Appetizer, Nuts/grains, Holiday
Yield: 1 batch
1 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Pecan halves
2 tb Margarine, melted
Toss pecans in margarine in a 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan.  Bake at 350
degrees for 20 to 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Sprinkle with
chili powder and salt; toss to coat.  Cool.
