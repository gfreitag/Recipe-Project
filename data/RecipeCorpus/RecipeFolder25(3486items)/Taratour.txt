Title: Taratour
Categories: Appetizer, Middle east, Salsa/sauce, Snack
Yield: 2 cups
Salt & pepper
1/4 c  Water
Fresh parsley, chopped
1 c  Tahini
2 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
1/3 c  Lemon juice
Combine tahini, garlic, lemon juice, salt & pepper in a blender.
Blend till smooth.  Gradually add the water until the sauce reaches
the desired consistency: it should be thicker than a dressing or
sauce. Add up to 1/2 c water.  Adjust seasonings.  Garnish with
parsley.

Virginia Habeeb, "Pita the Great"
