Title: Moroccan Sweet & Sour Carrots
Categories: Side dish, Moroccan, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb White vinegar
1/2 c  Seedless raisins
2 tb Brown sugar
4 md Carrots
1/4 c  Onion, minced
1/2 ts Nutmeg
6 tb Olive oil
Scrape the carrots & slice them into 1/4" rounds.  Heat oil & add
carrots, onions & nutmeg.  When onions are lightly browned, pour in
the vinegar. Cook, covered, over low heat, until carrots are tender.

Meanwhile, soak the raisins in 1/2 c warm water.  When carrots are
done, drain raisins & dump them into the pot with the carrots along
with the sugar.  Stir gently until the raisins are heated through.
Serve steaming hot.
