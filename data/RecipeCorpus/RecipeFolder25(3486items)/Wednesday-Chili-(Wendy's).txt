Title: Wednesday Chili (Wendy's)
Categories: Chili, Ground beef
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ds Tobasco
21 oz Can red kidney beans undrain
6 oz Can tomato paste
8 oz Can tomato sauce
-PNewton vkbb14a
2 tb Oil
1 1/2 lb Ground round
10 oz Can French onion soup
-not creamy
1 tb Chili powder
Blend soup and spices in blender then add to browned meat. Heat 20
min on low. Mash meat to rice size. Add kidney beans, tomato paste
and tomato sauce. Heat thoroughly, about 30 min.

STEAK & SHAKE STYLE: Stir in 2 t Hershey's cocoa powder & 10 oz Coke.
