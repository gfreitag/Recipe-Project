Title: Coca-Cola Chicken Wings
Categories: Chicken, Appetizer, Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 c  Catsup
1 ea Pk Chicken Wings
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1/4 c  Butter
1 c  Coca-Cola
Put wings in baking dish.  Mix ingredients and pour over them.  Bake
in a 325oF oven until done, approximately 1 hour.
