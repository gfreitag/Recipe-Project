Title: York Sensational Brownie Recipe
Categories: Holiday, Cake/cookie, Candy, Dessert
Yield: 36 servings
1 c  Hershey's cocoa
1 t  Baking powder
1 t  Salt
24 sm York peppermint patties,
-unwrapped
1 1/2 c  Butter or margarine, melted
(3 sticks)
3 c  Sugar
1 T  Vanilla extract
5    Eggs
2 c  Flour
Heat oven to 350degF.  Grease 13x9x2-inch baking pan.  In large bowl
with spoon or wire whisk, stir together butter, sugar and vanilla.
Add eggs; stir until well blended.  Stir in flour cocoa, baking
powder and salt; blend well. Reserve 2 cups batter; set aside. Spread
remaining batter in prepared pan.  Arrange peppermint patties in
single layer over batter, about 1/2 inch apart. Spread reserved 2
cups of batter over patties. Bake 50 to 55 min., or until brownies
begin to pull away from sides of pan. cool completely in pan on wire
rack; cut into squares. About 36 brownies.
