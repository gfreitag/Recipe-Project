Title: Oysters on the Half Shell
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Clam/oyster
Yield: 4 servings
2    Lemons, cut into wedges
-Tabasco sauce
24    Oysters
-lettuce or crushed ice
Wash oysters well. Prepare beds of lettuce or crushed ice on serving
plates. Open oysters just before serving. Discard top shell; loosen
oyster from bottom of shells by cutting ligaments but leave oyster on
shell. Serve oyster immediately, garnished with lemon wedges. Serve
Tabasco sauce seperately.
