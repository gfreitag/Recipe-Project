Title: Chocolate Layered Dessert
Categories: Dessert, Chocolate
Yield: 12 servings
1 pk Marble pound loaf, cut
-into 1/2" cubes (12 oz)
1/2 c  Chocolate syrup
4 pk Chocolate-covered English
-toffee bars, chopped
3 c  Cold milk
2 pk JELL-O Chocolate flavor
-Instant Pudding & Pie
-Filling (4-serving size)
1 pk COOL WHIP Non-Dairy
-Whipped Topping, thawed
POUR milk into large bowl. Add pudding mixes. Beat with wire whisk 2
minutes. Let stand 5 minutes. Gently stir in 2 cups of the whipped
topping.

ARRANGE 1/2 of the cake cubes in 3 1/2 quart serving bowl. Drizzle
with 1/2 of the chocolate syrup. Layer with 1/2 of the chopped candy
bars and 1/2 of the pudding mixture. Repeat layers ending with
pudding mixture.

REFRIGERATE 1 hour or until ready to serve. Garnish with remaining
whipped topping. Sprinkle with additional chopped candy bars, if
desired. Store leftover dessert in refrigerator.

NOTE: JELL-O Chocolate Flavor Sugar Free Instant Reduced Calorie
Pudding & Pie Filling and COOL WHIP LITE Whipped Topping can be
substituted in this recipe.
