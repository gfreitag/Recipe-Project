Title: Joan's Spare Rib Sauce (Country Style Ribs)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill, Pork
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Worchestershire sauce
1 c  Ketchup
2 c  Water
Drops of tobasco
2 pk Country style ribs
1 t  Salt
1 t  Chili powder
1 t  Celery seed
1 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Vinegar
Combine in a saucepan all ingredients, except ribs.  Stir, simmer on
low for 15-20 minutes (stir regularly)

Cook ribs slowly 3 hours at 300 degrees F. Turn the ribs often and
add more sauce.

original recipe from Joan Barnes, Bennington Vermont.  paraphrased and
typed by Charlotte Welch, Dec 1994.
