Title: Country Chicken Sausage
Categories: Can/preserv, Sausage, Chicken, Poultry
Yield: 2 pounds
1/2 ts Sage
1/2 ts Ground thyme
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Summer savory
1/2 ts Cayenne (optional)
2    Feet small hog or sheep
-casings
2 lb Chicken meat-15%fat
1 ts Salt, or to taste
1 ts Pepper
Prepare casings. Grind the chicken through the fine disk; mix with
remaining ingredients. Grind again and stuff. Twist off into 2-3"
links. To cook, saute in oil until evenly browned and cooked through.

Source: Home Sausage Making by Charles G. Reavis ISBN: 0-88266-477-8
