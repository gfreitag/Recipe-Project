Title: Big Bucket in the Sky Chicken (Kfc)
Categories: Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 pk Good Season Italian
-Dressing (dry)
1 t  Salt
-or marg
3 c  Self rising flour
2 pk Lipton's Tomato Cup of
-Soup**
1 T  Paprika
** If can't find Liptons, use Knorr's Tomato Basil Soup Mix Mix all
together then place some in a plastic bag and shake chicken pieces in
it. Melt butter and place in pan. Bake for 1 hour 350 degrees. Make
sure you coat the chicken twice. Can also be done skinless. From:
