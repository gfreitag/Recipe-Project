Title: Maryland Fried Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Salt
2 tb Pepper
1 tb Pepper;white
1/3 c  White Worcestershire
1 c  Vegetable Oil
1    Chicken;cut-up fryer;
2 c  Flour;sifted
3    Egg;whipped;
3 c  Crackers;crushed saltine
2 tb Poultry Seasoning
Combine flour, poultry seasoning, salt, and peppers in a bowl.
Whip eggs in another bowl. Crush crackers in a third bowl.
Coat each piece of chicken in flour, then egg, cracker crumbs, and
again in flour.
Pour oil into frying pan (two may be used for 6 or more servings);
heat to about 350F. Add chicken, cover, fry for 15 minutes. Turn
chicken; pour white Worcestershire over chicken, cover and cook
another 10 - 15 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
