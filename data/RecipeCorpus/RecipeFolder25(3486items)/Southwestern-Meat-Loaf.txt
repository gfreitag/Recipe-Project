Title: Southwestern Meat Loaf
Categories: Ground beef, Main dish, Meatloaf, Tex/mexican
Yield: 8 servings
-frozen or canned, drained
1    Green pepper; diced
2    Eggs
3/4 c  Water
1/3 c  Ketchup
Salsa
2 lb Chopped beef
1 pk Lipton Recipe Secret Onion
- Soup Mix
2 c  Crushed corn flakes; or,
Bran flakes (about 3 oz)
1 1/2 c  Whole kernel corn
Preheat oven to 350 deg.

In large bowl, combine all ingredients.  In a large shallow baking
pan, shape into loaf.  Bake 1-1/2 hours or until done.  Serve with
prepared salsa

Can also use Onion-Mushroom or Beefy Onion Soup Mix.
