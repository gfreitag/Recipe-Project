Title: Flan with Kahlua Sauce  (La Mansion Del Rio <s.a. Express
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
-Tablespoons Sugar)
1/2 t  Cinnamon
Kahlua Sauce  (See Recipe)
Shaven Chocolate, (Optional)
3 1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Heavy Cream
4 oz German's Sweet Chocolate
8 ea Eggs, at room temperature
1/2 c  Sugar (Add 2 more
Scald milk; set aside.  Heat heavy cream separately; stir in
chocolate to melt.  Set aside to cool to lukewarm.  Beat eggs with
sugar and cinnamon. Mix all ingredients together.  Strain into
remakins, souffle dish or casserole dish.  Bake in preheated 350
degree oven in a water bath for 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 hours.  Time will vary
depending upon type of mold used, with individual molds taking less
time.  Cool to room temperature. Refrigerate. Unmold before serving.
Top with Kahlua Sauce. Sprinkle with shaven chocolate if desired.
