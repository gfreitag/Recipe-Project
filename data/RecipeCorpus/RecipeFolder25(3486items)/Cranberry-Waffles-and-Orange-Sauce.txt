Title: Cranberry Waffles and Orange Sauce
Categories: Breakfast, Fruit, Pancakes, Waffles
Yield: 4 waffles
MMMMM--------------------------WAFFLES-------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Milk
3 tb Shortening; melted
1 c  Cranberries, raw; ground
2 c  Buttermilk biscuit mix
1/4 c  Sugar
3    Eggs; beaten
MMMMM------------------------ORANGE SAUCE-----------------------------
1 tb Butter (or marg.)
1 tb Orange rind; grated
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Water; boiling
Combine biscuit mix and sugar; stir in eggs and milk.  Add melted
shortening, mixing well; fold in cranberries.  Bake in a preheated
waffle baker, and serve with Orange sauce.

Orange Sauce: Combine sugar, cornstarch, and salt; add to boiling
water. Cook 3 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat; stir in
butter orange rind, orange juice, and lemon juice. Yield: about 2
cups.
