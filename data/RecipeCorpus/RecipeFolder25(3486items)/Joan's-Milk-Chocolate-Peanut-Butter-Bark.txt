Title: Joan's Milk Chocolate Peanut Butter Bark
Categories: Dessert, Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Chopped peanuts
2 c  Milk chocolate chips
2 c  Peanut butter chips
Line cookie sheet with foil.  In medium saucepan, over low heat, melt
chips together, stirring until smooth.  Remove from heat.  Stir in
peanuts. Spread on foiled cookie sheet.  Refrigerate for one hour.
Break into pieces, store in air tight container.

NOTE: Don't spread too thin.  Joan.

original recipe from Joan Barnes, Bennington Vermont.  paraphrased and
