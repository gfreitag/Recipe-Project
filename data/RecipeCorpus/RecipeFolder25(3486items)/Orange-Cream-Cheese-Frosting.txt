Title: Orange Cream Cheese Frosting
Categories: Fruit, Dessert, Cheese/eggs, Cake/cookie
Yield: 2 servings
-butter,
Room temperature
2 t  Grated orange peel
1 1/4 c  Finely chopped toasted
Walnuts (about 6 oz)
4 ea 8-oz packages cream cheese,
Room temperature
2 c  Powdered sugar
1 1/4 c  (about) orange marmalade
6 ea Tb (3/4 stick) unsalted
Using electric mixer, beat cream cheese and sugar in large bowl until
smooth.  Add 3/4 cup marmalade, butter and orange peel and beat just
until smooth.  If necessary, cover and chill until firm enough to
spread. SOURCE: BON APPETIT, May '93
