Title: Sen Barry Goldwater's Expert Chili
Categories: Chili, Ground beef
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-------------------MICHAEL GROSZ VBFF60A------------------------
3 tb Hot unspiced chili powder
1 tb Ground cumin
Salt to taste
Water
1 lb Ground beef; coarsely ground
1 lb Pinto beans; dried
1 cn 6 oz Tomato Paste
2 c  Onions; chopped
Soak beans in water, covered overnight.  In large Dutch oven, cook
beef until browned, stirring to keep crumbly; drain off drippings, if
needed. Add tomato paste, onions and drained beans. Mix chili powder,
cumin and season to taste with salt.  Stir into mixture. Bring to
boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer until beans are tender, about 5
hours. Serve 6.
