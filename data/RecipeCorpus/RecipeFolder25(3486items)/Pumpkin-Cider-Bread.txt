Title: Pumpkin Cider Bread
Categories: Bread, Pumpkin, Apples
Yield: 8 servings
1 t  Cinnamon
1 t  Cloves
1 t  Allspice
1 t  Lemon peel
1/4 t  Nutmeg
1/2 c  Walnuts
1 c  Mincemeat
2 c  Pumpkin
2 1/2 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Butter
4 ea Eggs, beaten
2 c  Apple cider
1/3 c  Milk plus 1 tsp vinegar
3 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 t  Baking powder
2 t  Baking soda
1 1/2 t  Salt
Grease two loaf pans.  In a deep mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar.
Beat in eggs.  Add pumpkin, cider and milk mixed with vinegar.  Blend
well Comber each of the other ingredients, one at a time, and stir
into mixture. Add walnuts and mincemeat.  Bake at 350 for 60 to 70
minutes. Keep one for your supper and give the other to a friend.
