Title: Sausage Salad
Categories: Salad, Sausage, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Black olives (15 3/4 oz)
1    Red pepper sliced
1    Yellow pepper sliced
1/4 c  Italian dressing
2 tb Olive oil
Rosemary
Chives
1 lb Hillshire Farm Turkey Smoked
-Sausage or any variety
-Hillshire Farm Smoked
-Sausage
1 1/2    Heads Bibb lettuce
4    Carrots sliced
1    Cucumber sliced
1/2    Red onion sliced
Clean lettuce. Slice vegetables. cut sausage into 1/2" slices and
saute in olive oil. Toss with Italian dressing. Arrange salad on
plates. Add rosemary and chives as desired.
