Title: Dropped Oatmeal Chippies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 48 cookies
1 1/2 c  Quick cooking oats
2 ea Eggs, lightly beaten
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Semi-sweet choco chips
1 c  All purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Margarine
1 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
Preheat oven to 350 F.  Sift flour with baking powder and salt. Stir
margarine and sugar together until just mixed; blend in flour,
oatmeal, eggs and vanilla. Stir in choco chips. Drop by teaspoonfuls
2" apart on greased baking sheets and bake 15-18 minutes until
lightly browned and tops spring back when touched. Transfer at once
to wire racks to cool. Source: The New Doubleday Cookbook 1985.
