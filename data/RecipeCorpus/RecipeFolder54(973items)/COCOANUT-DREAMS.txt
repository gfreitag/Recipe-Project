Title: COCOANUT DREAMS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Cocoanut
2 c  Icing sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Mashed potatoes
Mix first 4 ingredients and add the potatoes. Shape into tiny
haystacks and put in refrigerator for 2 hours. Melt 1 package of
unsweetened chocolate and dip candies in it. Put back in refrigerator
to harden.
