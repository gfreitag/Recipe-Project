Title: COOL PAPAYA SALSA
Categories: Condiments, Fruits, Sauces, Dips, Low-fat
Yield: 1 Recipe
3/4 c  Papaya, peeled & diced
1/4 c  Red onion, diced
1/4 c  Green bell pepper, diced
2 tb Cilantro leaves, chopped
2 tb Lime juice
1 pn Salt & pepper
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl.  Chill for 30 minutes before
serving.  Makes 1 1/2 cups
