Title: COCONUT POUND CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 16 Servings
1 1/2 c  Margarine
3 c  Sugar
6    Eggs
1 c  Evaporated milk
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 tb Lemon flavoring
1 cn Coconut (flaked)
Bake 1 1/2 hours at 350 F. in large tube pan.

Aunt Irene Conger

SOURCE: Mama's Favorite Recipes (Frances Turfitt) Shared by Nancy
