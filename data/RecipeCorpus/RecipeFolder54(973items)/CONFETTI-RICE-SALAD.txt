Title: CONFETTI RICE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Relishes, Rice
Yield: 28 Servings
1 pk Uncle Ben's Brand Chicken
Flavor Rice Pilaf
2 qt Water
1 c  Green onions, sliced
1/2 c  Pimentos, diced
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Salad oil
1/2 c  Sugar
1. Combine rice, contents of seasoning packet and water in cooking
pan. 2. Bring to a vigorous boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer until
water is
absorbed, about 5 minutes. 3. Cool. 4. Add green onion and
pimentos. 5. Combine vinegar, salad oil and sugar. Stir into rice
mixture. 6. Chill before serving. Measurements and timing are
designed only for Uncle Ben's Fast Cooking Rice Pilaf, Original Rice
Pilaf, Fast Cooking Spanish Rice, Long Grain & Wild Rice Blend, and
CONVERTED Brand Rice
