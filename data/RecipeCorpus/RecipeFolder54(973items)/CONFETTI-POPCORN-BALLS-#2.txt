Title: CONFETTI POPCORN BALLS #2
Categories: Candy
Yield: 8 Servings
5 c  Popcorn, air-popped
1/2 c  Candy coated pieces
1/3 c  Unsalted peanuts
2 tb Margarine -- melted
3 c  Miniature marshmallows --
Melted
In a prepared mixing bowl, combine popcorn, candy coated pieces, and
peanuts. Place margarine and marshmallows in a 4-cup microwave-safe
measuring cup. Microwave on high for 2 minutes or until melted and
smooth, stirring once halfway through cooking. Pour over popcorn
mixture; stir quickly to coat. With buttered hands, quickly shape
mixture into balls. Place on waxed paper. Let stand at room
temperature 1 hour.
