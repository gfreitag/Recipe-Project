Title: COLOSSAL CRANBERRY
Categories: Beverages, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Cran-Raspberry Juice
2 c  Frozen Mixed Fruit
1 1/2 c  Nonfat Vanilla Frozen Yogurt
Put all ingredients into blender and blend until smooth.

Cal 660 Fat 0

*Note: My mixed fruit contains raspberries, blueberries,
blackberries, and strawberries. Posted to Digest eat-lf.v096.n110
