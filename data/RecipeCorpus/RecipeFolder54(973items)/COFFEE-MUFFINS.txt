Title: COFFEE MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins, Breads, Uucp
Yield: 1 Servings  1 cup sugar, 2 eggs, 1 cup yoghurt 2.1/2 cups flour, 2 tsp baking
powder

Preheat oven to 400oF, prepare pans. Put the first four ingredients
into a bowl & mix well. Add the dry mix to the wet mix until just
combined. Spoon
