Title: COCOA MOCHA MINT
Categories: Mixed, Drinks, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz White creme de cacao
1 oz Kahlua
1 oz White creme de menthe
1 oz Sweet cream
Cracked ice
Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add creme de cacao, Kahlua,
creme de menthe, and cream. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail
glass.
