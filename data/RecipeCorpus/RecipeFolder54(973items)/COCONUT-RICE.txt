Title: COCONUT RICE
Categories: Side dish, Thai, Rice, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Long-grain rice
4 c  Coconut milk
1 1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
Wash the rice & drain.  Place in a heavy pot, add the milk, water,
salt & sugar, mix, cover & bring to a boil. Let it boil for 3 to 5
minutes. Reduce the heat to low & cook for 20 minutes, until done. A
richer flavour can be obtained by increasing the amount of coconut
milk & decreasing the amount of water. Serve with any type of curry.
