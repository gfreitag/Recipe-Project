Title: CONEY ISLANDS
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 12 Coneys
1/2 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Beef Stock
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
1 c  Seasoned tomato sauce
3/4 ts Cumin powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground ancho pepper
1 tb Green pepper flakes
Brown ground beef slowly, but thoroughly, breaking with a fork till
fine. Add remaining ingredients, simmer uncovered for 10 min.
