Title: CORAL REEF
Categories: Boat, Drinks, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Rum
1 oz Lemon juice
1 oz Simple syrup
1/2 oz Grenadine
1    Scoop crushed ice
A frozen drink  that looks as cool as its name Combine rum, lemon
juice, syrup, grenadine, and ice in a blender until smooth, serve it
in a martini glass and garnish with a flower. FOR 2 or MORE.... Call
'em CORAL REEFERS!
