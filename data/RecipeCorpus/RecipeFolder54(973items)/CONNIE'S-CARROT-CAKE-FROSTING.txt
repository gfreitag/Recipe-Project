Title: CONNIE'S CARROT CAKE FROSTING
Categories: Cakes, Desserts, Favorites
Yield: 18 Servings
1 1/2 ea Sticks of margerine
1 1/2 pk Confectioners sugar, (bags)
1 1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
1 1/2 pk Cream cheese
3 ts Vanilla
Cream sugar, butter, cream cheese and flavoring.  Add nuts last. Do
not cook.  Let layers cool before icing...(Can be frozen).
