Title: COLONEL SANDERS' MACARONI SALAD
Categories: Salads, Pasta, Salsa
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Box elbo macaroni -- (7
Ounces) cooked
2    Ribs celery minced fine
1 tb Dry minced onion
1/3 c  Diced sweet pickles
1 1/2 c  Fat free Miracle whip
1/2 c  Fat free mayo
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Sugar
Salt to taste
Combine everything just as listed. Refg. salad tightly covered several
hours before serving. Famous Favorites pg83 Formatted for MM by
