Title: COLD MUSTARD SAUCE FOR MEATS & FISH
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 1 Servings
8 tb Onion -- Grated
8 tb Vinegar
4 tb Dijon Or Dusseldorf Mustard
8    Hard
4 tb Cream
6 ts Sugar
8 tb Vegetable Oil
Egg Yolks -- Cooked
Combine the ingredients and use.

Yields 1 Cup.
