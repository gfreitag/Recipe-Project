Title: COCONUT CAKE FILLING (MILDRED HARTLEY)
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 10 Servings
1 ea Coconut, large   *
1 x  Salt, pinch
1 pt Milk, sweet
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Flour, heaping
* coconut should be split, peeled, and grated. Milk should be added to
coconut milk to make 1 pint.
^^-----------------------------------------------------
~------------- Cook all ingredients in a 3-quart saucepan except
coconut until thick. Add coconut and cook another 10 minutes on
low-heat. Cool before putting on any white cake. MILDRED HARTLEY -
this recipe has been in her family for over 100 years.
