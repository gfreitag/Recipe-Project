Title: CONGEALED SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
20    Marshmallows
1 c  Grated cheddar cheese
1/2 pt Whipping cream OR>>>
Cool Whip
2 c  Boiling water
1 pk Lime jello
1 cn Crushed pineapple, drained
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 sm Bottle maraschino cherries
Melt marshmallows in water.  Add lime jello.  Set to soft congeal.
Fold in whipped cream or Cool Whip and remainder of ingredients and
refrigerate.
