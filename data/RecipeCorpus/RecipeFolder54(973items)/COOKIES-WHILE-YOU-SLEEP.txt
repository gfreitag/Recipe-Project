Title: COOKIES WHILE YOU SLEEP
Categories: Cookies, Dessert
Yield: 60 Servings
2    Egg whites
Pinch of salt
1/4 ts Cream of tartar
2/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Pecans, chopped
3/4 c  Chocolate bits
Source: Stars In The Galley - Star of India Auxiliary Cookbook

Preheat oven to 350 degrees

Beat first 3 ingredients until stiff. Gradually add sugar, beating.
When shiny peaks form, add vanilla. Fold in pecans and chocolate bits.

Drop bite-sized mounds onto cookie sheets.

Place trays in oven; turn off heat and leave until morning. DO NOT
PEEK! These bake on retained heat.

Makes 60 or so cookies. Store in tightly covered container.
