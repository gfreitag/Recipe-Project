Title: COLESLAW/FAMOUS FAVORITES
Categories: Salads, Sandwiches, Salsa
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Bottle Italian Dressing-(8
Ounces)
3 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Corn oil
1 ts Dry minced parsley
1/4 ts Dry marjoram leaves
1 ds Tabasco Sauce
1 ts Onion Powder
1 md Cabbage -- finely chop
1 sm Carrot -- finely grated
Combine all ingredients, add to cabbage and toss lightly. Refg.
atleast 4 hours before serving. Famous Favorites pg 88 Formatted for
