Title: CORIANDER BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker, Party
Yield: 16 Servings
-ELLEN GORDON (JSBJ02B)
1 c  Milk
3 tb Margarine
1/4 c  Honey
3 c  Unbleached bread flour
1/4 c  High gluten flour
2 ts Dry yeast
1 ts Salt
2 ts Ground coriander
1/8 ts Ground cloves
1/8 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1    Egg (I use egg substitute)
1/2 tb Grated orange peel.
I bake overnight - family loves it. Enjoy Ellen / Iowa FROM: ELLEN
