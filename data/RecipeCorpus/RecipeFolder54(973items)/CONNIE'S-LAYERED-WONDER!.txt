Title: CONNIE'S LAYERED WONDER!
Categories: Candies, Chocolate, Cookies, Desserts, Favorites
Yield: 16 Servings
1 c  Graham crackers
12 oz Semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 cn Eagle brand milk
6 oz Can flaked cocoanut
1/2 c  Melted butter
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts or pecans
12 oz Package butterscotch chips
LAYER ALL SEVEN INGREDIENTS IN A 9 X 13 INCH PAN IN THIS ORDER:

1ST LAYER GRAHAM CRACKER CRUMBS
2ND LAYER MELTED BUTTER
3RD LAYER CHOCOLATE CHIPS
4TH LAYER CHOPPED WALNUTS OR PECANS
5TH LAYER EAGLE BRAND MILK
6TH LAYER BUTTERSCOTCH CHIPS
7TH LAYER COCOANUT
:
:
:
:
:
:
:

BAKE AT 350 DEGREES FOR 30 MINUTES.  COOL BEFORE SLICING.
