Title: CONEY ISLAND HOT DOG SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 10 Servings
3    Medium onions  chopped
2 tb Paprika
2 tb Chili powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2    L/2 lb Hamburger
l l/2 t  Oregano
l l/2 t  Cumin
l l/2 t  Celery salt
l l/2 t  Garlic salt
l T  Salt

Chop onions and boil in enough water to cover; boil until completely
cooked. Add spices. Break up hamburger into small pieces and put into
onion mixture. Cook about l hour, stirring occasionally.
