Title: COCOANUT CREAMS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Cocoa
2/3 c  Eagle brand milk
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Icing sugar
1 c  Coarsely cut walnuts
Sift cocoa  and   sugar to-gether, take out 1/4 cup to roll creams
in. To milk add vanilla, cocoa and sugar, blend well and add nuts.
Shape into small balls and roll in reserved cocoa & sugar mixture.
