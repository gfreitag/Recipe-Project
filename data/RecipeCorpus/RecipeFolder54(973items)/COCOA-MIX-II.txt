Title: COCOA MIX II
Categories: Mixes, Italian, Ceideburg 2, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
2 3/4 c  Nonfat dry milk powder --
Instant
1 1/2 c  Cocoa mix -- instant
1/2 c  Nondairy creamer
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1 c  Marshmallows -- optional
Miniature
Combine ingredients in a large bowl. Mix well. Store in airtight
containers.  To serve: Place 1/3 cup mix in a cup. Add 1 cup boiling
water and stir well. Makes 5 cups of mix. When you serve this cocoa,
add a cinnamon stick to each cup, if desired.
