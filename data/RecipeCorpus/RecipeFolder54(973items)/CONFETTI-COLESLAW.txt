Title: CONFETTI COLESLAW
Categories: Salads, Am
Yield: 10 Servings
2 c  Green cabbage
2 c  Red cabbage
1 c  Green pepper
1 c  Red pepper
1 c  Carrot
6 tb Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Celery seed, ground
Thinly shred or slice ingredients.  Combine milk, mayonnaise, vinegar,
sugar, and celery seed in small bowl. Combine other ingredients in a
large bowl. Pour liquid mixture over cabbage mixture; toss to coat.
Serve chilled.
