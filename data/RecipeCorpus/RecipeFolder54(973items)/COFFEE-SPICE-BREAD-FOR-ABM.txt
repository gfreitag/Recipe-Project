Title: COFFEE SPICE BREAD FOR ABM
Categories: Abm, Posted-mm
Yield: 8 Servings
2 1/2 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1/4 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
3 tb Sugar
1    Egg
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  Coffee -- prepared
Put all ingredients in the bread machine and turn on.

: "The Bread Machine Cookbook," Donna German
