Title: COCONUT-HAZELNUT PIE SHELL
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Crust
1 c  Oregon hazelnuts, sliced
1 1/2 c  Coconut, shredded
1/2 c  Margarine or butter, melted
Combine coconut and sliced hazelnuts. Add melted margarine or butter
and mix well. Press into greased pie tin and refrigerate until firm
(2-3 hours).
