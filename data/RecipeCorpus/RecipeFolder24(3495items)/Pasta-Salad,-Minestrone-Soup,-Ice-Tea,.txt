Title: Pasta Salad, Minestrone Soup, Ice Tea,
Categories: Salad, Pasta, Beverage, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Broccoli
1/4 c  Celery
1 c  Minestrone soup
1 c  Cooked pasta
1/4 c  Carrots
1/4 c  Oil-free dressing
Cook broccoli, carrots and celery, mix with pasta and dressing. Serve
with minestrone soup for lunch. From Cut Out Cholesterol Diet Posted
by: Sheila Exner - January 1992
