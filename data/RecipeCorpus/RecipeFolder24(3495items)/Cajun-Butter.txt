Title: Cajun Butter
Categories: Spread, Cajun/creol
Yield: 3 servings
2 t  Cajun pepper
1/4 ea - 1/2 tsp seasoning salt
1/4 t  Garlic powder
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Unsalted butter, softened
1 1/2 t  Parsley
2 t  Lemon juice
In food processor or mixer, combine all ingredients.  Process until
well blended, about 30 seconds.  Scrape butter onto a large sheet of
plastic wrap.  With rubber spatula, smooth butter into a cyclinder
shape about 1/2 inches in diameter.  Roll up, twisting both ends to
seal.  Keep refrigerated.  Can be frozen up to two months.  Makes 3/4
cup, 1 tbsp per serving. Keep in refrigerator or freezer. Serve with
grilled meat, poultry, or fish. Also good for stir frying, sauteeing
vegetables, making sauces, or enriching soups. Origin: Watkins Food
Gifts Cookbook Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
