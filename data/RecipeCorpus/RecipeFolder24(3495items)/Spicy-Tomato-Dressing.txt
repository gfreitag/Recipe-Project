Title: Spicy Tomato Dressing
Categories: Diabetic, Dressing
Yield: 2 servings
-of your choise
1 tb Wine vinegar;
1 c  Tomato Juice
3/4 c  Unflovored gelatin;
1 1/2 ts Dry salad dressing mix;
Bring 1/2 cup tomato juice to boil and add gelatin.  Stir until
dissolved. Add remaining ingredients, blend thoroughly.  Chill. Shake
berfore serving. (Gelatin makes the dressing thick.) Food Exchange
per serving: FREE FOOD
