Title: Halloween Mystery Punch with Frozen Hand Ice Ring
Categories: Beverage, Holiday
Yield: 16 servings
MMMMM-----------------------MYSTERY PUNCH----------------------------
3 c  Water
12 oz Orange juice concentrate
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 ts Ginger, ground
2 qt Apple cider or juice
MMMMM--------------------FROZEN HAND ICE RING-------------------------
1    New, disposable plastic or
-rubber glove*
Food coloring; red, blue or
-green
Water
*Make sure to use a glove that does not have an inside powdery
coating.

Mystery Punch: In a large pitcher or glass gallon jar, stir lemon
juice and ginger until blended. Add cider, water and orange juice
concentrate; stir until well blended.  Cover and chill at least 1
hour or until well chilled. Serve in a large chilled punch bowl.

Frozen Hand Ice Ring: Add food color to water until desired color.
Fill glove with water, fastening end with a twist tie or rubber band.
Hang glove from a shelf in the freezer and freeze at least four hours
or until frozen solid.  When frozen, cut glove from ice and place
"hand" in punch. Submitted By JEANNE GEAKE   On   10-17-94
