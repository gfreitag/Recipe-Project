Title: Shashlik with Vegetables (Sephardic/yemenite)
Categories: Lamb, Main dish, Rice, Bbq/grill, Middle east
Yield: 4 servings
3    Tomatoes; unpeeled
Hot cooked rice
2 lb Lamb; cut in 36 pieces
Salt, pepper and cumin
3    Onions; peeled
Season the lamb with salt, pepper and cumin.  Cut the onions and
tomatoes into quarters.  Alternate the lamb with the vegetables on
skewers; grill or broil for 5 minutes on each side.  Serve with rice.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings.

From "The Yemenite Cookbook" by Zion Levi and Hani Agabria.  New York:
Seaver Books, 1988.  Pg. 153.  ISBN 0-8050-0394-0.  Posted by Cathy
