Title: Seasame Spinach Salad
Categories: Diabetic, Salad, Vegetable, Side dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Walnut oil;
1 tb Sesame oil;
1 tb Toasted Sesame seeds;
1 lb Spinach; fresh
1 c  Mushrooms; sliced
1 tb Low-sodium soy sauce;
1 tb Water
Wash spinach thoroughly and trim off tough stems.  Spin or pat dry.
Mix remaining ingredients together and toss with spinch. Food
Exchange per serving: 1 VEGETABLE EXCHANGE + 1 FAT EXCHANGE CAL: 71;
CHO: Omg; CAR: 5g; PRO: 3g; SOD: 217mg; FAT: 5g;
