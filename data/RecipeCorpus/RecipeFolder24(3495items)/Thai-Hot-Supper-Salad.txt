Title: Thai Hot Supper Salad
Categories: Salad, Thai, Beef, Pork, Asian
Yield: 4 servings
-leaves
2 c  Shredded spinach leaves
1    Tomato, cut into thin wedges
1/4 c  Chopped peanuts
4 T  Chopped cilantro
2    Green onions w/tops, sliced
1 lb Lean gorund beef or pork
2    Cloves garlic, minced
3/4 c  Pace Picante Sauce
1 T  Soy sauce
1 T  Lime juice
2 t  Finely shredded fresh ginger
2 c  Shredded romaine lettuce
Savor the flavor of favorite Thai restaurants at home with Thai Hot
Supper Salad. Made without exotic specialty store ingredients, it's
ready to enjoy in about 20 minutes.

Brown meat with garlic in 10" skillet; drain. Add Pace Picante Sauce,
soy sauce, lime juice and ginger; cook and stir 4-5 minutes or until
sauce has thickened slightly. Arrange combined lettuce and spinach on
serving platter, placing tomatoes around edge; spoon hot meat mixture
over greens. Sprinkle with peanuts, cilantro and green onions. Serve
with additional Pace Picante Sauce. Makes 4 servings.
