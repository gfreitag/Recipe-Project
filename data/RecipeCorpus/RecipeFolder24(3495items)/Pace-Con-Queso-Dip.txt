Title: Pace Con Queso Dip
Categories: Dip, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Pace Picante Sauce
1 lb Velveta Process Cheese
- Spread, Cubed
Combine ingredients in saucepan.  Cook over low heat, stirring
frequently, just until sauce is smooth.  Serve with corn chips,
tortilla chips or vegetable dippers.  Makes 2-1/2 cups.

Microwave Directions: Combine ingredients in 1-quart microwave safe
bowl. Cook at high for 3 to 4 minutes or until cheese spread is
melted, stirring after each 1-1/2 minutes.

From: Pace booklet.
