Title: Creamy Bacon Dip
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dip, Snack
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Minced onion flakes
1 tb Imitation bacon bits
1 c  Plain lowfat yogurt
1/4 c  Plus (see next ingredient)
4 ts Lowfat mayonnaise
Combine all ingredients, chill to blend flavors.

Per serving: 2g protein, 3g fat, 3g carb., 111mg sodium, 5mg chol., 50
calories.
