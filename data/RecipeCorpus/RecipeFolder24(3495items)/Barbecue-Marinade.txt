Title: Barbecue Marinade
Categories: Bbq/grill, Marinade
Yield: 1 cup (aprx)
1/3 c  Catsup
1 tb Horseradish, prepared
1 tb Water
1/4 ts Black pepper, coarse grind
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1 1/2 tb Sugar, brown; packed
1 tb Oil, vegetable
1/3 c  Vinegar, cider
Cook onion and brown sugar in oil in small saucepan over medium heat
until onion is tender, about 3 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients
and continue cooking over medium heat 3 to 4 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Remove from heat; cool thoroughly before adding to
beef. Makes about 3/4 cup.

SOURCE: Marinate and Grill Beef Recipes, 1991 Beef Industry Council
