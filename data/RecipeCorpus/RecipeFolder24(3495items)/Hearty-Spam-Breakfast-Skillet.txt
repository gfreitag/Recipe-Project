Title: Hearty Spam Breakfast Skillet
Categories: Main dish, Breakfast, Spam, Cheese/eggs, Potato
Yield: 6 servings
-julienne strips (12 oz)
1 cn Frozen fat-free egg product,
-thawed, OR
4    Eggs
1/4 t  Dried basil
1/8 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
6 dr Hot pepper sauce
1/4 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
2 c  Frozen diced or shredded
-potatoes
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/4 md Green bell pepper, cut into
-1" thin strips
1/4 md Red or yellow bell pepper,
-cut into 1" thin strips
2 t  Oil
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cut into
In large non-stick skillet, cook potatoes, onion, and peppers in oil
over medium-high heat 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Add SPAM; cook
and stir 5 minutes. In small bowl, combine egg product, basil, salt,
pepper, and hot pepper sauce; blend well. Pour over mixture in
skillet. Cover. Cook over medium-low heat 8-12 minutes or until set.
Sprinkle with cheese; remove from heat.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 294; Protein 17 grams;
Carbohydrate 17 grams; Fat 18 grams; Cholesterol 50 milligrams;
Sodium 725 milligrams.
