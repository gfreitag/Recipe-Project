Title: Chicken a la Avacado
Categories: Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Parsley,chopped
1/2    Avacado
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
1/8 c  Olive oil
6    Chicken breasts,boneless*
1/2 c  Capers
3/4 c  Lemon juice
* - 3 oz. each

Saute the chicken in olive oil for 5 minues each side over low heat.
Remove chicken from the skillet, remove skin & set aside. In the same
skillet, saute capers for 1 minute. Remove from heat & add lemon
juice & parsley. Pour lemon juice & caper mixture over top of each
chicken breast. Serve with fanned avacado slices & rice.
