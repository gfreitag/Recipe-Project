Title: Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Chocolate, Kids, Cake/cookie, Snack
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Water
1 1/2 oz Corn or wheat flake cereal
2 tb Raisins
2 tb Peanut butter
2/3 c  Nonfat dry milk powder
2 ts Unsweetened cocoa
5 ts Sugar
In a small bowl, combine peanut butter, dry milk, cocoa, sugar and
water. Mix until well blended. Stir in cereal and raisins. Press
mixture into bottom of a 4X8 inch loaf pan. Chill 30 minutes or
longer, cut into bars and serve.

Per serving: 9g protein, 5g fat, 21g carb., 248mg sodium, 2mg chol.,
170 calories.
