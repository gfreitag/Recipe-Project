Title: Creamy Italian Dressing
Categories: Diabetic, Dressing, Salad
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Leaf oregano;
1/8 ts Salt;
Pepper to taste
1 tb Virgin Olive oil;
1 tb Vegetable oil;
4 tb Fresh lemon juice;
Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate.  Shake well before
using. Makes 1/3 cup = 6 servings. Food Exchange per serving: 1 FAT
EXCHANGE; CAL: 46; CHO: 0mg; CAR: 1g; PRO: 0g; FAT: 5g;

Source: Light & Easy Diabetes Cuisine by Betty Marks. Brought to you
and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
