Title: Marinade & Seasoning Sauce B1
Categories: Beverage, Marinade, Chinese, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 tb CIDER VINEGAR
1/2 ts SALT
1 ts SUGAR
2 ts CORNSTARCH
1/8 ts BLACK PEPPER
1/3 c  CHICKEN BROTH OR COLD
WATER
MARINADE:
1 tb CORNSTARCH
1 tb DRY SHERRY
1/2 ts SALT
SAUCE:
2 tb SOY SAUCE
1 tb DRY SHERRY
1 tb HOISIN SAUCE
Marinade -- combine all ingredients in a small bowl and mix well.
Sauce -- combine all ingredients in a small bowl and mix well.
Effort: EASY Time: :05 Source: ANNA KAO'S Comments: SERVE WITH ANNA
KAO'S KUO FAN GEE CHIU OR OTHER CHICKEN DISH.
