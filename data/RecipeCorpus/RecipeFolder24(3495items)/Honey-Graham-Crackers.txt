Title: Honey Graham Crackers
Categories: Cake/cookie, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Unsalted butter
3 1/2 T  Honey
2 ea To 4 Tbsp. milk
2 c  Freshly ground whole wheat
-flour, sifted
2 t  Baking powder
1 t  Cinnamon
Sift together flour, baking powder, and cinnamon.  Set aside.  Cream
together butter, honey, and 2 Tbsp. milk.  Cut butter mixture into
flour mixture, using 2 remaining Tbsp. milk if needed. Using a
rolling pin, roll to 1/4 inch thickness between sheets of waxed
paper. Remove top sheet of waxed paper; cut into 1-1/2 x 1-1/2 inch
square or diamond shapes and place on a dehydrator tray.  Dry 4 to 6
hours. Yield: about 4 dozen crackers. From: Food Drying at Home, The
Natural Way Shared By: Pat Stockett
