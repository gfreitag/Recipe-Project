Title: Dill Roasted Pork
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Pork, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Dill weed
Salt and pepper to taste
1 ea 3-4 lb pork loin Roast
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Preheat oven to 325. Place roast on rack in shallow baking dish.
Combine spices and rub over meat. Bake 30 minutes per pound or until
meat thermometer registers 185 degrees.

Per serving: 24g protein, 11g fat, 0g carb., 59mg sodium, 77mg chol.,
205 calories.
