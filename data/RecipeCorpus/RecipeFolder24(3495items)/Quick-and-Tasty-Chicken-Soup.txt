Title: Quick and Tasty Chicken Soup
Categories: Diabetic, Soup/stew, Main dish, Poultry, Crockpot
Yield: 4 cups
1/4 c  Celery; diced fine
1/2 ts Thyme;
1/2 tb Arrowroot;
1 tb Water;
1 1/2 c  Chicken; cooked diced.
1 1/2 c  Chicken broth;
1 c  Water
2 tb Onion; chopped fine
Simmer first seven ingredients for about 10 minutes.  Make paste of
arrowroot and 1 tb water, add to soup.  Cook until clear and smooth
(takes just a few seconds.

Food Exchange per serving: 2 3/4 LEAN MEAT EXCHANGES  CAL: 185 THAT'S
ALL!!
