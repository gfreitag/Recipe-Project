Title: Zucchini-Sausage Pie
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Vegetable, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Thyme
1/8 ts Rosemary
1/8 ts Garlic powder
Pepper; to taste
1/2 c  Cheese, Parmesan; divided
2 lb Squash, zucchini; coarsely
-chopped
1/2 lb Sausage, pork bulk
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Cracker crumbs
3    Eggs; slightly beaten
Cook zucchini in boiling salted water 15 to 20 minutes or until
tender; drain.  Saute sausage and onion until browned, stirring to
crumble sausage; drain well.

Combine all ingredients except 2 tablespoons Parmesan.  Spoon into
well-greased 9" deep-dish pieplate; sprinkle with reserved cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until browned.
