Title: Sour Curry Paste
Categories: Curries, Thai
Yield: 1 cup
3 tb Shallots, chopped
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Sugar
10 ea Dried red chilies, soaked,
-- seeded & chopped
1 1/2 tb Lemon grass, chopped
Place all ingredients in a blender or food processor & blend into a
paste.

Puangkram C. Schmitz & Michael J. Worman, "Practical Thai Cooking"
Date: 09-17-94
From: Mark Satterly
