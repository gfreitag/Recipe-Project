Title: Potato Stuffing for Dosa
Categories: Potato, Indian, Stuffing
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------YIELDS: 4 SERVES---------------------------
1 md Onion
1/4 t  Mustard seeds
Salt & pepper to taste
250 g  Potatoes
1/4 t  Turmeric
1/4 t  Chilli powder
30 g  Ghee
Boil the potatoes. Peel and dice. Chop the onion finely. Heat the
ghee and fry the mustard seeds. Add the onions and fry till half
done. Add potatoes, turmeric, chilli and salt. Cook for 5 mins till
dry. Add pepper. Place spoonful on each dosa. From: The Best of
Indian Booking, Bay Books Typed by: Joell Abbott 9/94
