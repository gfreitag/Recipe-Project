Title: Spicy Tofu Salad
Categories: Salad, Thai, Appetizer, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Mushrooms, sliced
1 sm Iceberg lettuce
1/2 lb Lima beans, lightly cooked
1 1/2 lb Tofu
1 c  Cucumber, julienned
1 c  Carrot, julienned
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
5 ea Red & green chilies, chopped
1/3 c  Peanuts, chopped
2 ea Green onions, chopped
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Rice vinegar
1/2 c  Tofu
1 1/2 tb Chili sauce, see recipe
3 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Sugar
Cut the tofu into cubes.  Drain in a colander.

Put the lettuce on the bottom of a shallow bowl or attractive serving
dish. Place the tofu in the centre & surround it with one mound each
of cucumber, carrot, mushrooms & peanuts.  Refrigerate until chilled.

Combine the TOPPING ingredients in a blender or food processor & whip
into a rough mixture.

Blend together the dressing ingredients

At serving time, sprinkle the topping mixture over the salad then
pour on the dressing.  Toss at the table.

Adapted from Puangkram C. Schmitz & Michael J. Worman, "Practical Thai
Cooking"
