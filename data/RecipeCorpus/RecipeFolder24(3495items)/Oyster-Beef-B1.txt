Title: Oyster Beef B1
Categories: Beef, Chinese, Meat, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ds SUGAR
2 tb VEGETABLE OIL
2 tb OYSTER SAUCE
1/4 c  CHICKEN STOCK
1/4 ts MSG (OPTIONAL)
1 tb EACH CORNSTARCH & WATER
-MADE INTO A PASTE
1 lb BEEF,SLICED 1/4-IN.
-THICK (2X1 IN. PIECES)
1/2 c  SCALLIONS,WHITE SECTIONS
-ONLY,CUT TO 1 IN. LENGTHS
1/4 ts SALT
1/2 ts SOY SAUCE
1 ts CORNSTARCH
In mixing bowl, combine salt,soy sauce,1 tsp. cornstarch & sugar.
Blend thoroughly & add sliced beef. Marinate 5 min. Add oil to pre-
heated wok or skillet. Add beef,scallions,& oyster sauce. Toss-cook
at high heat 3 min. Add chicken stock,MSG, and cornstarch/water
paste. Mix & toss until gravy thickens & has coated the beef &
scallions. Serve very hot. Temperature(s): HOT Effort: AVERAGE Time:

WINE: WAN FU WHITE WINE
