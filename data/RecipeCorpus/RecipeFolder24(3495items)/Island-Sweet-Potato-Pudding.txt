Title: Island Sweet Potato Pudding
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dessert, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts -reduced calorie margarine
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Rum extract
1    Egg white
12 oz Cooked sweet potatoes,
-peeled and cut in chunks
1 md Ripe banana, mashed
3 ts Firmly packed brown sugar
2 tb Plus
Preheat oven to 350. In a large bowl, beat all ingredients except egg
white until smooth. Add a small amount of water if necessary, to get
a smooth consistency. Beat egg white until stiff. Fold gently into
sweet potato mixture. Spoon into a casserole that has been sprayed
with a nonstick cooking srpay. Bake, uncovered, 45 to 50 minutes,
until lightly browned.

Per serving: 3g protein, 4g fat, 28g carb., 147mg sodium, 0mg chol.,
174 calories.
