Title: Super Seafood Salad
Categories: Salad, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
2    Celery stalks,chopped
3 T  Finely chopped onion
1 pk Louis Kemp CRAB DELIGHTS
1/2 c  Kraft(c) Mayonnaise
Cut CRAB DELIGHTS flakes in half or shred. Combine with mayonnaise,
celery and onion. Serve on lettuce or as a sandwich filling.

From: Michael Orchekowski
