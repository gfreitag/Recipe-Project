Title: Fried Eggplant Slices
Categories: Side dish, Indian, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
Black pepper
Oil for shallow frying
6 ea Lemon wedges
1 md Eggplant
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Turmeric
1/8 ts Cayenne
Cut eggplant into quarters lengthwise & then cut crosswise into 1/2"
thick wedges.

Mix together salt, turmeric, cayenne & black pepper in a small bowl.
Sprinkle over the eggplant & mix wll.

Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.  When hot, saute the eggplant
in batches on both sides until reddish brown.  Remove & drain on
paper towels. Repeat until all eggplant wedges have been cooked.
Serve garnished with lemon wedges.
