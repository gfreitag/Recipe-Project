Title: Artichokes with Peas (Cfa)
Categories: Greek, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Peas; fresh cooked or canned
1    Pimiento; sliced, canned
4 md Artichokes
Boiling salted water
Juice of 1 lemon
Slivers o fred pimiento across artichoke halves filled with peas
make this a coloful vegetable dish.

Cut off the tips of the artichokes, and cut in half lenghtwise;
scoop out the choke with a spoon.  Cook in boiling salted water with
the lemon juice until tender,  about 45 minutes.  Heat peas.  Arrange
cooked artichoke halves on a plattre, cavity side up.  Spoon over the
peas, filling the cavities, and arrange a strip of pimiento over the
top of each.

Source: "A Sunset Book": Cooking with a Foreign Accent  circa '58
