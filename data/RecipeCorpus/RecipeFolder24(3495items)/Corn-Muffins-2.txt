Title: Corn Muffins 2
Categories: Muffin, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 1/2 c  Granulated sugar
2 tb Baking powder
3 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Cornmeal
Combine sugar, baking powder, flour, cornmeal, eggs, and 1 cup of milk
until well blended.  Add remaining 1/2 cup of milk and the oil. Mix
for 3 minutes.  Place in well greased muffin tins and bake at 400
degrees (F) for about 20 minutes (until golden).  Makes 30 muffins.
