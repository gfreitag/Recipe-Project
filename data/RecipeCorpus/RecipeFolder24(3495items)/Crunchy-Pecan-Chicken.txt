Title: Crunchy Pecan Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Poultry, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1    Broiler-fryer; cut into
-serving sized pieces
1/2 c  Milk, evaporated
1/2 c  Butter (or marg.); melted
1 c  Biscuit mix, buttermilk
1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Paprika
1/2 c  Pecans; finely chopped
Combine biscuit mix, seasonings, and nuts; mix well.  Dip chicken in
milk and coat with dry mixture; place chicken in a greased 13x9x2"
baking pan. Pour butter over top and bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour
or until tender.

SOURCE: The Popular Pecan, pamphlet by the Mississippi Pecan Growers
Assn. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
Date: 09-28-94
From: Nancy Coleman
