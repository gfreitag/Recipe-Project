Title: Sun-Dried Tomato Pesto
Categories: Vegetable, Salsa/sauce, Low-cal/fat, Tomato
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Fresh italian parsley; chop
1 ts Capers
1 lg Fresh ripe tomato; seeded
1/2 c  Sun-dried tomatoes; softened
2    Garlic cloves; minced
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
1/2 c  Fresh basil; chopped
Blend all ingredients in blender at high speed until smooth. Bettie
Source: Dr. Dean Ornish's "Eat More, Weight Less" Cookbook. MM Format
