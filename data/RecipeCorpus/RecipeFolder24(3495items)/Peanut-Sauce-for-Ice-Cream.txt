Title: Peanut Sauce for Ice Cream
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Syrup
Yield: 2 servings
1 t  Peanut butter flavor
1/4 t  Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Chopped, blanched, roasted,
-salted peanuts, optional
1 c  Dark corn syrup
1 c  White sugar
1/8 t  Salt
2 T  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Milk
combine corn syrup, sugar, salt, butter, and milk in large heavy
saucepan. Cook over medium heat, stirring constatnly, until mixture
comes to a boil. Boil gently 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat. Stir in Peanut butter flavor and vanilla extract.
If desired, stir in chopped peanuts.  Cool completely.  Store sauce
in covered container in refrigerator.  Makes 2 cups, 2 tbsp per
serving. Delicious served with ice cream or over banana splits.
Store in refrigerator, stir well before using. Origin: Watkins Food
Gifts Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
