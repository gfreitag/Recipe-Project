Title: Shrimp or Crabmeat Canapes
Categories: Cajun/creol, Appetizer, Spread, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 T  Chili sauce
Hot pepper sauce to taste
1/2 c  Cooked shrimp or crabmeat
1 pk Cream cheese,softened(3 oz)
1/2 t  Worcestershire sauce
1. Mince or grind shrimp or crabmeat; combine with remaining
ingredients (thin, if necessary, with a little cream).

2. Spread on crackers or rounds of tossed bread.
Date: 09-26-94
From: Michael Orchekowski
