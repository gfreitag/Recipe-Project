Title: *Earl's Tangy Salad Dressing*
Categories: Salad, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Mustard yellow
1 t  Garlic minced
1 T  Soy sauce *
Mix all ingredients together. Use as a sandwich spread or a very
tatsy and tangy salad dressing. * NOTE: Worcestershire sauce my be
used instead of the soy sauce. ORIGIN: Earl Shelsby's kitchen, circa
1994 Submitted By DON HOUSTON   On   10-02-94
