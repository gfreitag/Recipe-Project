Title: Asparagus Lasagna
Categories: Vegetable, Pasta
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 t  Cayenne pepper
2 1/4 c  Milk
8 oz Lasagna noodles
Fresh ground black pepper
2 c  Cottage cheese
8 oz Monterey Jack cheese,
-shredded
1/2-1 C Parmesan cheese,
-grated
1-2 Lbs. Fresh asparagus,
-cleaned and cut into 1
-inch pieces
2 ea Green onions, chopped
4 c  Fresh mushrooms, washed and
-chopped
3 T  Butter
1/4 c  Flour
1 t  Salt
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Cook asparagus to crisp tender, drain
and let cool. Saute' onions and mushrooms in butter until soft, about
5 minutes. Blend in flour, salt and cayenne pepper. Gradually stir in
milk, and cook until sauce has thickened for an additional 5 minutes.
Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Spread 1/2 cup
of mushroom sauce in a greased 9" x 13" pan and add a layer of
noodles, seasoned with fresh ground black pepper. Add a third of
remaining ingredients (asparagus, cottage cheese, Montery Jack
cheese, a third of remaining mushroom sauce and Parmesan cheese),
then another layer of noodles. Repeat twice. Bake for 45 minutes. Let
stand for 20 minutes before cutting. Serves 8. SOURCE:*The Old
Farmer's 1995 Almanac POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 10/94
