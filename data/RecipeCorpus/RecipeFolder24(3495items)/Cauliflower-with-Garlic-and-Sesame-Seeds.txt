Title: Cauliflower with Garlic and Sesame Seeds
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
2 T  Sesame seeds
- seasoning to taste
- boiling salted water to
- cover florets
1 lg Cauliflower
- broken into florets
2 T  Vegetable oil
1 cl Garlic; minced
Place cauliflower in rapidly boiling salted water.  Boil 2 - 4
minutes. Drain well.

In large skillet, heat oil over medium heat.  When hot, add garlic and
saute until lightly browned.  Add sesame seeds and stir for one
minute.

Add cauliflower florets and stir to coat with seeds and garlic.

Add seasoning to taste.

Source: Entertaining Without Reservations by Donna-Joy Halliday.
