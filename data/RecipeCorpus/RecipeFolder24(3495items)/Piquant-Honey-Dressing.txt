Title: Piquant Honey Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
12 ea Ounces HONEY
32 ea Ounces vegetable oil
2 ea Ounces ice water
4 t  Dry mustard
4 t  Salt
1 T  Paprika
8 ea Ounce lemon juice
Dissolve mustard, salt and paprika in lemon juice.  Blend in HONEY.
Gradually add oil, mixing well after each addition, until mixture is
smooth and opaque.  Mix in ice water until blended. Cover and
refrigerate. Serve with fruit, tossed mixed greens, and edible flower
blossom salads.
