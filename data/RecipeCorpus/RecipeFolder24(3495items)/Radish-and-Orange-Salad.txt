Title: Radish and Orange Salad
Categories: Diabetic, Fruit, Salad, Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 4 sweet ones
1 tb Orange flower water;?
Salt;
2    Navel oranges;
12 oz Radishes; trimmed
2 tb Sugar or sugar sub:
2 tb Lemon juice;
Shred radishes in a food processor or "grate" with a blender.  Allow
excess liquid to drain of and add salt to taste.  Chill. Peel and
slice oranges. Just before serving.  Arrange all together and dust
with cinnamon.

Food Exchanges per serving: 1 FRUIT EXCHANGE. Source: Vegetable Magic
