Title: Double Chocolate Cookie Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 24 bars
-12 ounces
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Nuts; chopped
2 c  Finely crushed creme-filled
-chocolate sandwich cookies
-about 24 cookies
1/4 c  Margarine; or butter, melted
2 c  Semi-sweet chocolate chips
Preheat oven to 350.  Combine crumbs and margarine; press firmly on
bottom of 13x9-inch baking pan.  In medium saucepan, over medium
heat, melt 1 cup chocolate chips with sweetened condensed milk and
vanilla. Pour evenly over prepared crust; top with nuts and remaining
chips. Bake 20 minutes or until set.  Cool.  Chill if desired. Cut
into bars. Store tightly covered at room temperature.
