Title: Pineapple & Bananas with Coconut Syrup
Categories: Dessert, Thai, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Pineapple, peeled & chopped
2 c  Cantaloupe, seeded & cubed
2 ea Bananas, sliced
1 c  Brown rice syrup
1 c  Water
1 c  Coconut milk
2 ts Rosewater, optional
In a medium sized pot, combine syrup & water.  Bring to a boil,
reduce heat & simmer until the mixture thickens slightly, about 15
minutes. Remove from heat.

Stir in coconut milk & rosewater & chill for 1 to 2 hours.  Arrange
fruit in a large serving bowl.  Pour syrup over the top & serve
slightly chilled or at room temperature.
