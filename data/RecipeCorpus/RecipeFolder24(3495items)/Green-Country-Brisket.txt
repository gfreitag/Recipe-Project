Title: Green Country Brisket
Categories: Main dish, Beef
Yield: 10 servings
Salt & Pepper
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Catsup
4 ds Worchestershire sauce
6 ds Soy sauce
5 lb Trimmed brisket
1/4 c  Liquid smoke
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Onion salt
2 ts Celery seed
2 ts Worchestershire sauce
Combine liquid smoke, salts, garlic celery seed and 2 tsp
Worchestershire. Marinate brisket in this overnight.  Line broiler
pan with foil. Place meat in this.  Seal in foil.  Bake 1 hour at
350*, reduce heat to 300* and bake 2 hours.

Combine brown sugar, vinegar, catsup, Worchestershire sauce and soy
sauce. Open foil and drain grease from pan, pour sauce over meat.
Wrap foil loosely and cook another 1 1/2 hours.
