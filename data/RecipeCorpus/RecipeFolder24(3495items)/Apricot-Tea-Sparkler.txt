Title: Apricot Tea Sparkler
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/3 c  Water, sparkling, chilled
1 1/3 c  Tea concentrate (qv)
1 1/3 c  Apricot nectar, chilled
Combine concentrate, water and nectar, and pour into ice-filled
glasses.
Source: Sunset Magazine, Aug 1994
Date: 09-14-94
From: Karl Lembke
