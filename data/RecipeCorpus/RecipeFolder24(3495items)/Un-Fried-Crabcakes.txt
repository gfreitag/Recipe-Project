Title: Un-Fried Crabcakes
Categories: Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Old Bay seasoning
1 ts Italian seasoning
2 tb Jalape¤o pepper, (1 large)
1 ts Baking powder
2 tb Fresh parsley, chopped
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Bread crumbs, unflavored
- dried
1 lb Fresh crabmeat, (lump blue
- crabs)
Light vegetable oil cooking
- spray
1 ts Parmesan cheese, freshly
- grated
1 tb Fresh chives, snipped
1 lg Whole egg, beaten OR
2 lg Egg whites, beaten
Preheat the oven to 400ø.

Rinse the crabmeat under cold running water and drain, making sure to
remove any filament or shell.

Spray the vegetable oil over the baking sheet 3 times to coat.

In a large bowl, combine all remaining ingredients, except 1/2 cup of
the bread crumbs, and stir in the crabmeat.  Using 1/4 cup for each
crabmeat, form the mixture into 8 cakes.  Roll each in the reserved
bread crumbs and place on the prepared baking sheet.  Coat the
crabcakes lightly with the cooking spray.

Place the baking sheet on the bottom shelf of the oven and bake the
crabcakes for about 5 minutes per side, until brown.

Fat per serving = 2.4 grams Calories per serving = 252
