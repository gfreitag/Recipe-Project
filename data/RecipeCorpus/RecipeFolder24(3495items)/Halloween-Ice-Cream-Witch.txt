Title: Halloween Ice Cream Witch
Categories: Dessert, Holiday, Kids
Yield: 1 servings
Food coloring, green/orange
Chocolate chips
1    Gum drop
1    Sugar cone
1    Sugar cookie
Ice cream, green
Coconut
Make witch's hat by dipping a sugar cone in melted chocolate and then
sticking it on top of a sugar cookie. Make witch's hair by mixing
coconut with green or orange food coloring.  Put a round ball of lime
sherbet or chocolate chip mint or pistachio ice cream on a small
plate for the witch's head.  Put hat on top of head and hair on
witch's head. Use a gum drop for the nose and chocolate chips for the
eyes. Submitted By JEANNE GEAKE   On   10-17-94
