Title: Potato and Cheese Filling
Categories: European, Pasta, Potato, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Mashed potatoes
1/2 c  Shredded cheese (your
-choice of kind)
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Butter
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts White pepper
Saute onion in butter 5 minutes.  Stir in salt and pepper. Combine
potatoes, cheese and onion mixture.  Mix well.  Makes about 2 cups.
