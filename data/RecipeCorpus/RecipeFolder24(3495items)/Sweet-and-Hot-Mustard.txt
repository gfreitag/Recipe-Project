Title: Sweet and Hot Mustard
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
1    Clove Garlic, Minced
2/3 c  Dry Mustard
1 T  Honey
2    Or 3 Drops Tabasco
1    Onion, Chopped
1 c  Dry White Wine
1 1/2 t  Olive/Vegetable Oil
1 t  Salt
Combine onion garlic and wine in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
Simmer 5 minutes. Remove from heat and cool. Strain into dry mustard.
Add oil, honey, salt, and tabasco. Cook over low heat until smooth
and thick. Yield about 16 Oz.

NOTE: Homemade mustards must be stored in the refrigerator.
Date: 09-26-94
From: Earl Shelsby
