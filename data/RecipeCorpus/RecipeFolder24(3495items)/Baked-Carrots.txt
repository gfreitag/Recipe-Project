Title: Baked Carrots
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Finely shredded carrots
1 pk Instant chicken broth
1/8 ts Dill weed
2 tb Plus (see next ingredient)
2 ts -reduced calorie margarine
1/2 c  Chopped onions
Preheat oven to 350. fry onions in margarine until tender, about 5
minutes. Stir in remaining inredients, pour into casserole and bake,
covered, 30 minutes.

Per serving: 1g protein, 4g fat, 10g carb., 388mg sodium, 0mg chol.,
78 calories.
