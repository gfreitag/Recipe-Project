Title: Yogurt Cheese
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 cup
-yogurt
8 oz Container plain nonfat
Place the yogurt in a cheesecloth-lined seive or a fine mesh strainer
and set over a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap. Set aside at room
temperature overnight for at least 8 hours.

Per Tablespoon: 14 calories, 2g carb., 1g protein, 1mg chol., 0g/0%
fat.
