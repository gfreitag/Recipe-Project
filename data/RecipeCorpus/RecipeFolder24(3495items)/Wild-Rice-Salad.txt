Title: Wild Rice Salad
Categories: Salad, Seafood, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Mixed green and purple
-seedless grapes, halved
1/4 c  Light olive oil
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 pk Long grain and wild rice
-mix (6.25-6.75 oz)
1 pk Louis Kemp Crab Delights
Flakes or chunks (8 oz)
1 c  Sliced celery
Prepare rice mix according to package directions. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Chill 3 hours or overnight.

295 calories per serving.
Date: 09-26-94
From: Michael Orchekowski
