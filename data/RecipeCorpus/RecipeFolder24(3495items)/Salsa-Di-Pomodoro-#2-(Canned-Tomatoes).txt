Title: Salsa Di Pomodoro #2 (Canned Tomatoes)
Categories: Italian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Sugar
Salt
Pepper
2 tb Olive oil
1 lg Garlic clove, halved
14 oz Can peeled tomatoes
1 tb Tomato paste
Heat the oil and garlic in a saucepan over low heat for 5 minutes or
until oil is well flavored.  Discard the garlic.  Add the tomatoes
and their liquid, tomato paste, and salt and pepper to taste.  Bring
to boil; cover and simmer very gently for at least 40 minutes,
preferably longer.  Beat the sauce if necessary to break up any large
pieces of tomato, check the seasoning and use as required.

Makes about 1 1/4 cups sauce.
