Title: Pepper Cheese
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Vegetable, Appetizer, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 t  Garlic powder
3/4 t  Onion powder
2 T  Cracked black pepper
16 oz Reduced calorie cream
-cheese, softened
2 1/2 t  Italian seasoning
Combine cream cheese, Italian seasoning, garlic and onion powders.
Mix well.  Line a small square or round dish with plastic wrap.  Fill
with cheese mixture and refrigerate 6-8 hours.  Remove cheese from
dish and peel off plastic wrap.  Pat cracked black pepper evenly over
cheese. Cover and store in the refrigerator.  Makes 16 servings.

Store in refrigerator.  Serve with crackers or melba toast.
