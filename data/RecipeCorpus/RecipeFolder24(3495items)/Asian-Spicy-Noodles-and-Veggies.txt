Title: Asian Spicy Noodles and Veggies
Categories: Vegetable, Main dish, Asian
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Soy sauce
3 tb Vinegar
2 tb White wine
2 ts Sesame oil
2 ts Mustard, dry
Pepper to taste
Cayenne pepper to taste
6 qt Water
2 2/3 c  Carrots, sliced
9 c  Broccoli, cut into pieces
4 c  Fusilli
6    Onions green
2 tb Ginger, grated
4 tb Peanut butter
boil water for pasta in pot, boil water for vegetables in steamer.
Place carrots in steamer for 10 minutes, add broccoli for 7 minutes
longer. Cook pasta according to directions. Place onions in
processor, add ginger, peanut butter, soy sauce, vinegar, wine, oil,
mustard, peppers. Blend thoroughly.  Drain pasta, reserve 1/2 cup
water. Add the water to food processor and blend briefly. Pour pasta
into serving dish, toss with some of the sauce.  Top with vegetables
and pour on remaining sauce. Serve with whole grain bread
