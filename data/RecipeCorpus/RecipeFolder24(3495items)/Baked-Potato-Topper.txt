Title: Baked Potato Topper
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 ts Imitation bacon bits
1 c  Plain lowfat yogurt
1/2 ts Dill weed
1 1/2 ts Dried chives
Combine all ingredients and mix well. Chill

Per serving: 2g protein, 1g fat, 2g carb., 111mg sodium, 2mg chol., 20
calories.
Date: 09-13-94
From: Carolyn Shaw
