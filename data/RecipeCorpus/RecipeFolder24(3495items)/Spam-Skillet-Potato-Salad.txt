Title: Spam Skillet Potato Salad
Categories: Salad, Spam, Potato
Yield: 6 servings
3 md Potatoes, boiled and diced
1 1/4 c  Shredded sharp Cheddar
-cheese
1/4 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cut in
-strips (12 oz)
1/2 c  Chopped green onions
1/2 c  Chopped green bell pepper
In large skillet, cook SPAM, green onions, and green pepper over
medium heat until SPAM is lightly browned. Add potatoes, cheese, and
mayonnaise. Heat just until cheese begins to melt.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 353; Protein 18 grams;
Carbohydrate 15 grams; Fat 25 grams; Cholesterol 80 milligrams;
Sodium 792 milligrams.
