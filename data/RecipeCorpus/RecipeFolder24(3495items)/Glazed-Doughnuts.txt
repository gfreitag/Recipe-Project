Title: Glazed Doughnuts
Categories: Breakfast, Pastry, Cake/cookie, Doughnut
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Lard or crisco, melted
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Milk
1    Yeast cake
1/4 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Flour
Cook milk until warm, add yeast dissolved in 1/4 cup water, then beat
in 1 1/2 Cups flour.  Let rise for 2 hours or until doubled in size.
Then add remaining ingredients and let rise for 2 hours or until
doubled in size. Roll dough 1/4-inches thick, cut with donut cutter,
let rise until double.

Fry in hot grease. Then dip in hot water and roll in powdered sugar.

VARIATIONS/COMMENTS You can also dip them in sugar/cinnamon.
