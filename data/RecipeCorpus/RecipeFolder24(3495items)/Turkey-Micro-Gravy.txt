Title: Turkey Micro Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Microwave, Turkey
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 1/2 c  Drippings
3/4 c  Turkey broth
3/8 c  Flour
Micro drippings on high 1 - 2 minutes until hot.  Stir in flour, salt
and pepper.  Stir in broth until well blended. Micro on high
(stirring every minute) (5-6 minutes) or until thickened. Strain if
necessary. Submitted By CAROLYN STEWART   On   10-01-94
