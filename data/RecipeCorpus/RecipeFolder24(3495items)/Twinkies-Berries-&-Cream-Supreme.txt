Title: Twinkies Berries & Cream Supreme
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Whipped topping
1 c  Fresh berries or sliced
-fruit
4    Hostess Twinkies brand
-Golden sponge Cake with
-Creamy Filling
Slice unwrapped Twinkies in half diagonally. Arrange on a plate with
pointed ends toward the center to form a star shape. Top with whipped
topping and fruit. Garnish with mint leaves (optional).

From: Michael Orchekowski
