Title: Swedish Rutabagas
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
2 T  Butter
2 md Rutabagas, peeled, quartered
-and sliced 1/4" thick
2 T  Brown sugar
1/2 t  Ground ginger
Cook rutabagas in boiling salted water; drain.

Meanwhile, combine brown sugar, ginger, salt and pepper:  mix
thoroughly. Add with butter to rutabagas.  Stir gently over low heat
until sugar melts, 2 to 3 minutes.  Makes 6 servings.
