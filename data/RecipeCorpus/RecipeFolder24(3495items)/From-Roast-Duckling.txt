Title: From Roast Duckling: Duck Pilaf
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
-Broth)
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Cut-up dried apricots
1    Roasted duckling
1 c  Uncooked regular rice
3 tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Chopped celery
4 c  Broth (from (Duck Giblet
Description:

Saute rice first to give it a toasty flavor, then blend with duckling
and apricots to make this gourmetlike casserole.
Cooking Time: 1 1/2 Oven Temp: 350oF
Preparation Time:

Instructions:

1.  Remove skin from duckling; srip meat from frame, then dice. (There
should be 3 cups).  Set aside for step 4.

2.  Saute rice in vegetable oil in a large frying pan, stirring
often, just until golden; add celery and saute 5 minutes longer.

3.  Stir in broth, salt, and pepper; heat to boiling.

4.  Spoon into 80cup baking dish, stir in duckling and apricots;
cover.

5.  Bake in moderate oven until ruce is tender and liquid is absorbed.
