Title: All-Purpose Chili Sauce
Categories: Side dish, Thai, Salsa/sauce, Vegetarian
Yield: 2 pints
1/3 c  Salt
1/3 c  Sake
1 c  Garlic cloves, peeled
1 lb Red chili peppers, washed &
-- dried
2 c  Rice vinegar
1/2 c  Sugar
Place all ingredients in a blender or food processor & blend until a
thick paste is formed.

Use to add some pizzazz to stir-fries.

Puangkram C. Schmitz & Michael J. Worman, "Practical Thai Cooking"
Date: 09-17-94
From: Mark Satterly
