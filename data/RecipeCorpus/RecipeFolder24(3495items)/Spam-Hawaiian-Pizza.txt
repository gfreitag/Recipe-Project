Title: Spam Hawaiian Pizza
Categories: Main dish, Spam, Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
-thin squares (12 oz)
1 cn Chunk pineapple, drained
-(8 oz)
1/2 c  Thinly sliced red onion
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1 cn Refrigerated all-ready pizza
-crust (10 oz)
1 pk Sliced Provolone cheese
-(6 oz)
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cut in
Heat oven to 425'F. Grease 12" pizza pan or 13x9" baking pan. Unroll
dough; press in prepared pan. Top with cheese. Arrange remaining
ingredients over cheese. Bake 25-30 minutes or until crust is deep
golden brown.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 373; Protein 20 grams;
Carbohydrate 30 grams; Fat 21 grams; Cholesterol 65 milligrams;
Sodium 1192 milligrams.
