Title: Spanish Steak
Categories: Main dish, Burgers, Beef, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
16 oz Tomato wedges; drained
12    Olives, pimiento-stuffed;
-sliced
1/2 c  Cheese, Cheddar; shredded
1    Green pepper; cut in rings
1 md Onion; sliced and separated
-into rings
2 lb Ground chuck
1/2 c  Onion; finely chopped
4 sl Bacon; cooked and crumbled
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
1 ts Paprika
1/8 ts Pepper
ds Hot sauce
Combine first 8 ingredients; mix lightly.   Place meat mixture on a
lightly greased sheet of aluminum foil, and shape into an oval loaf,
2 inches thick. Top with remaining ingredients; seal foil and place
in a 13x9x2" baking pan.  Bake at 400 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes or
until done.
