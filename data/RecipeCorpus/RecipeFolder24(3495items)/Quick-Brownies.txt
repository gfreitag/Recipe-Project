Title: Quick Brownies
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
ds Salt
2/3 c  Flour
1 t  Vanilla
1    Stick of oleo
2 tb Cocoa
2    Eggs
Melt oleo; add cocoa. Beat in eggs, salt; sift in flour. Add vanilla.
Bake at 325' for 35 minutes in a greased and floured 8x8" pan. Pig
