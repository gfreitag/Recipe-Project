Title: Quick Crabmeat Bisque
Categories: Appetizer, Soup/stew, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Crabmeat
1/4 c  Sherry or dry white wine
Butter or margarine,
-at room temperature
Old Bay Seasoning
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup,
-condensed
1 cn Cream of asparagus soup,
-condensed
1 1/2 cn Milk (use soup cans)
1 c  Light cream
Combine soups; stir in milk and cream. Heat until mixture boils,
stirring frequently. Add crabmeat and heat through. Add sherry, mix
and heat well. Remove to serving bowls or soup turine. Sprinkle with
Old Bay Seasoning. Float butter pats on each bowl or float a few pats
of butter in soup turine.
Date: 09-16-94
From: Dale Shipp
