Title: Ginger, Shrimp and Rice Salad
Categories: Salad, Rice, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise (yeast free)
1 t  Ginger, finely chopped
Sesame seeds for garnish
5 ea Green onions, finely chopped
1 sm Onion, finely chopped
3 c  Cold cooked brown rice
1 c  Cooked small shrimp
Combine ingredients.  Serve on a bed of lettuce and sprinkle with
sesame seeds (optional). Origin: Yeast-Free Feast Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Oct/94.
