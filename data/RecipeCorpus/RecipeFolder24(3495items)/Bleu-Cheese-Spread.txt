Title: Bleu Cheese Spread
Categories: Spread, Cheese/eggs, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1    Tbps chopped pimentos
1 T  Minced green pepper
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1/2 t  Onion powder
-dash black pepper
1/2 c  Finely chopped walnuts
8 oz Reduced calorie cream
-cheese, softened
1/2 c  Reduced calorie sour cream
4 oz Bleu cheese, crumbled
Spread walnuts in shallow baking pan.  Toast in 350F oven 9 minutes,
stirring occasionally, until golden brown.  Cool slightly.  Beat cream
cheese in bowl until smooth and creamy.  Blend in sour cream, bleu
cheese, walnuts, and remaining ingredients;  mix well.  Spoon into
attractive serving dish. Keep refrigerated.  Makes 2 cups, 2 tbsp per
serving.

Store in refrigerator.  Serve with unsalted crackers, party rye, or
French bread.
