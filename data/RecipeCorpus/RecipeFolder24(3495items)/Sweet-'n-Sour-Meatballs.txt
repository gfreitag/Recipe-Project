Title: Sweet 'n Sour Meatballs
Categories: Appetizer, Meatballs, Ground beef
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Lemon juice from concentrate
1/4 c  Ketchup
2 tb Cornstartch
1/4 ts Salt
1    Red or green bell pepper
-cut into squares
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
8 oz Water chestnuts; drained
-chopped
2    Eggs
1/3 c  Plain dry bread crumbs
4 ts Beef-flavor instant bouillon
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Water
In large bowl, combine meat, water, chestnuts, eggs, crumbs,
vouillon, and Worcestershire;  mix well.  Shape into 1 1/4"
meatballs. In large skillet, brown meatballs.  Remove from pan; pour
off fat. In skillet, combine remaining ingredients except bell
pepper; cook and stir until thickened. Add meatballs; simmer
uncovered 10 minutes. Add bell pepper; heat through. Refrigerate
leftovers. Submitted By LAWRENCE KELLIE   On   10-02-94
