Title: Teriyaki Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Chinese, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
2/3 c  Sugar
1 t  Ginger
1 x  Chicken pieces
1 c  Soy Sauce
1 t  Garlic
1/4 c  Wine (White)
Mix and Marinate Chicken. Place marinated Chicken pieces on foil lined
Cookie sheet and Broil for about 10 minutes on each side. (I also
then bake for 1/2 hour at about 350 Deg.F. A Favorite Family Recipe.
