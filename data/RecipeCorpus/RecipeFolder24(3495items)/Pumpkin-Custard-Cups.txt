Title: Pumpkin Custard Cups
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dessert, Fruit, Holiday
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Canned pumpkin
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 pk Orange flavored jello
3/4 c  Boiling water
1 c  Part skim ricotta cheese
In saucepan, dissolve jello in boiling water. In blender, combine
jello with rest of ingredients. Blend until smooth. Divide among 4
dessert dishes. Chill until firm.

Per serving:5g protein, 3g fat, 7g carb., 102mg sodium, 10mg chol., 76
calories.
