Title: Fruity Oat and Bran Bread
Categories: Bread
Yield: 8 servings
2 ts Margarine, melted
3 tb Firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Skim milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1 c  Applesauce
1 ts Freshly grated orange peel
1/4 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Minus (see next ingredient)
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 1/2 oz Bran
3/4 oz Quick cooking oats
-uncooked
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
2 tb Plus (see next ingredient)
Preheat oven to 350. In a large bowlm combine both flours, bran, oats,
baking soda, and cinnamon. In another bowl, mix remaining ingredients
except raisins. Beat with a wire whisk until blended. Add to dry
ingredients with raisins and mix well. Place in a 4X8 nonstick loaf
pan. Bake 40 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool in pan
10 minutes, remove to rack to finish cooling.

Per serving: 4g protein, 5g fat, 28g carb., 163mg sodium, 34mg chol.,
171 calories.
