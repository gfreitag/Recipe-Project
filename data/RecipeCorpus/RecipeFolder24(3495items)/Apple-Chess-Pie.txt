Title: Apple Chess Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 3 servings
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Chopped apples
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1    Unbaked pie shell
1 c  Sugar
1    Stick of margarine
1 tb Flour
Cream sugar, margarine, flour, and eggs.  Fold in chopped apples and
cinnamon.  Pour into an 8 inch unbaked pie shell and bake 10 minutes
at 400 degrees then 40 minutes at 325 degrees. Randy Rigg
