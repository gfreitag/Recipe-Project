Title: Spicy Peanut Chicken Wings
Categories: Chicken, Appetizer, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
12    Chicken wings, disjointed
1 T  Soy sauce
2 T  Rice vinegar
2 T  Chopped roasted peanuts
1/3 c  Peanut butter
2 T  Honey or brown sugar
1/2 t  Crushed red pepper flakes  1
-medium clove garlic
Stir together the peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, vinegar, and pepper
flakes and finely chopped garlic. Place the wings in a single layer
on a baking pan and bake in a preheated 400 degrees F. oven 15
minutes. Turn and bake an additional 15 minutes. Remove the wings
from the oven and brush one side with the peanut sauce. Bake 5
minutes. Turn and brush the second side with the sauce and sprinkle
with the peanuts. Bake 7 minutes. Remove from the oven and cool.
Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve. From: Earl Shelsby
Date: 09-23-94
