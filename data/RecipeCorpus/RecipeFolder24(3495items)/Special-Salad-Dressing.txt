Title: Special Salad Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Salad, Down under
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: AUSTRALIAN VOGUE WIN---------------------
Ground pepper to taste
Thyme, chopped finely
Marjoram, chopped finely
Mint, chopped finely
Parsley, chopped finely
1/2 c  Hill Smith Family Estate
-Vinegar
1 c  Good quality olive oil
1 T  Honey
1/2 t  Salt
Blend vinegar, oil, honey, salt and pepper.  Add chopped herbs to
taste and blend again, or place all ingredients in a covered jar and
shake well. Arrange salad greens in season, in a large bowl; garnish
with fresh yellow rose petals and chopped hard-boiled eggs.  Just
before serving, pour in required amount of dressing and toss.
Michael Hill Smith. Bon-Appetit, Exec.Chef. Magnus Johansson
