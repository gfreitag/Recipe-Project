Title: Spam Italian Rice Salad
Categories: Salad, Italian, Rice, Spam
Yield: 10 servings
-drained (10 oz)
1    Green bell pepper, cut into
-1/4" strips
1    Red bell pepper, cut into
-1/4" strips
1 pk HORMEL sliced Pepperoni
-(3 1/2 oz)
1/2 c  Chopped fresh parsley
2 c  Rice
9    Green onions, chopped
1 1/2 t  Paprika
1/2 t  Cumin
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 cn Chicken broth (14 1/2 oz)
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cut into
-strips (12 oz)
1 pk Frozen peas, thawed and
In 3-quart saucepan over medium heat, cook rice, green onions,
paprika, and cumin in oil until onion is tender. Stir in broth. Cook
over low heat 20-30 minutes or until rice is tender and all liquid is
absorbed. Meanwhile, in skillet over medium heat, saute SPAM 4-5
minutes. Combine rice mixture, SPAM, peas, bell pepper, pepperoni,
and parsley.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 342; Protein 14 grams;
Carbohydrate 36 grams; Fat 15 grams; Cholesterol 35 milligrams;
Sodium 836 milligrams.
