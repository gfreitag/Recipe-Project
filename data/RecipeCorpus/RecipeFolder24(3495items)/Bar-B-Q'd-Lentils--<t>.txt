Title: Bar-B-Q'd Lentils  <t>
Categories: Main dish, Bbq/grill, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Worcestershire sauce; or veg
16 oz Tomato sauce
2 tb Minced onions
1/4 ts Liquid barbecue smoke.; opti
2 1/3 c  Lentils; rinsed
5 c  Water
1/2 c  Molasses
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Vinegar
1/2 c  Ketchup
Recipe by: USA Dry Pea & Lentil Council Add lentils to water, bring
to a boil and simmer for 30 min or until tender but whole. Add
remaining ingredients to the cooked lentils and bake at 350 deg F for
45 min.
