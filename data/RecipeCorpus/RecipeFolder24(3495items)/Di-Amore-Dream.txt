Title: Di Amore Dream
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 1 serving
2 oz Orange juice
1 1/2 oz Amaretto
1/4 oz Boston creme de cacao
Blend ingredients with two scoops of vanilla ice cream; serve in
parfait glass.

SOURCE: Amaratto di Amore Recipes, given out at Simply Southern, the
Fall 1994 Southern Living Cooking School. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
Date: 09-26-94
From: Nancy Coleman
