Title: Fairfax Chocolate Icing
Categories: Cake/cookie, British
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Chocolate, semi-sweet square
6 T  Water
1/2 c  Confectioners sugar
2 ea Chocolate, bitter squares
3 T  Butter
6 ea Marshmallows
1 t  Vanilla
Mix chocolate, sugar and water together. Cook about 5 minutes. Remove
from heat, add marshmallows, butter and vanilla. Beat until thick
enough to spread.

Mrs. Harold T. Cook
