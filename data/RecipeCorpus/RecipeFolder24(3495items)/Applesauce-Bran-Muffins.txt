Title: Applesauce Bran Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 36 muffins
3 c  Natural bran
6 c  Flour
1 T  Baking soda
1 T  Cinnamon
1 T  Salt
3 c  Chopped dates
3 c  Applesauce
2 c  Buttermilk
1 c  Packed br sugar
3/4 c  Molasses
3/4 c  Veg oil
1 T  Vanilla
3 x  Eggs
In bowl, whisk applesauce, buttermilk, sugar, molasses, oil, vanilla,
and eggs. Stir in bran, let stand for 5 minutes. In large bowl,
combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt; stir in applesauce
mixture. Stir in dates. Spoon into greased muffin cups filling to
tops. Bake in batches in 375 degree oven for about 25 minutes or
until tops are firm to the touch. Let cool in pan on rack.
