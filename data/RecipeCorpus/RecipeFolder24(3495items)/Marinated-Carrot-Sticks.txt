Title: Marinated Carrot Sticks
Categories: Can/preserv, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Seasoning salt
1/2 t  Dill weed
1/4 t  Garlic powder
-dash black pepper
3    4 medium carrots, cut into 4
-inch strips
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  White vinegar
2 T  Vegetable oil
Arrange carrots upright in clean pint jar.  In small bowl, combine
remaining ingredients.  Blend well.  Pour over carrots.  Cover and
refrigerate several hours or overnight, shaking occasionally.

Can be stored up to 4 week in refrigerator.  Great on hors d'oeuvres
tray.
