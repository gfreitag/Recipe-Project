Title: Spam Western Pasta Salad
Categories: Salad, Spam, Vegetable, Pasta
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Chopped green bell pepper
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
2 tb Dijonnaise-style mustard
1 1/2 tb Barbeque sauce
3 c  Cooked macaroni
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(12 oz)
1 c  Cubed Cheddar cheese
1 c  Shredded carrot
3/4 c  Chopped celery
STATE FAIR RECIPE Jodie Johnson, Jacksonville, NC.

In large bowl, combine macaroni, SPAM, cheese, carrot, celery, green
pepper, and onion. In small bowl, combine mayonnaise, mustard, and
barbeque sauce. Toss dressing with macaroni mixture. Cover and chill
1 hour.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 328; Protein 13 grams;
Carbohydrate 19 grams; Fat 22 grams; Cholesterol 57 milligrams;
Sodium 674 milligrams.
