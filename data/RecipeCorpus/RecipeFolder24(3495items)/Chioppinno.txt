Title: Chioppinno
Categories: Main dish, Crab/shrimp, Clam/oyster
Yield: 10 servings
1 x  Salt to taste
1/2 c  Sherry
1 c  Red wine
1 x  Oregano, bayleaf, pepper
1 x  Sweet basil, fresh garlic
2 lb Cracked crab, clams
1 x  Prawns, shrimp &/or scallops
1 c  Celery, chopped
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 tb Garlic, minced
1 tb Parsley, chopped
1 ea Onion, chopped
1 cn Tomatos (lg. can)
2 c  Tomato sauce
Braise onions, celery, chopped garlic & parsley in olive oil. Add all
other ingredients except seafood. Simmer at least 1 hour. Add
seafood, simmer at least 1/2 hour more. Serve with fresh french bread
and butter. Submitted By LINDA SATTERFIELD   On   10-14-94
