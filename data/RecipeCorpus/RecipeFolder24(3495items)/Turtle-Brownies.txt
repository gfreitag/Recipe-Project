Title: Turtle Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
3/4 c  Melted margarine
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 c  Chocolate chips
14 oz Bag of caramels
2/3 c  Milnot
1    Box chocolate cake mix
(devils food)
In a bowl, add cake mix, melted margarine, 1/3 cup Milnot, and nuts.
Stir until batter holds together.  Put 1/2 batter in greased cake pan
(9x13 inches) and bake at 350 degrees for 6 to 10 minutes, until it
appears slightly dry.  In a saucepan, melt caramels and rest of the
Milnot. Spread that over half of the baked batter.  Top with
chocolate chips and then remaining batter and bake at 350 degrees for
15 to 18 minutes. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   10-14-94
