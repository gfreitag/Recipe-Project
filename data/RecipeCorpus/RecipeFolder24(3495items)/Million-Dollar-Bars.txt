Title: Million Dollar Bars
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 servings
Dipping Chocolate
Butter
1 lb Nestle Caramel
2 1/2 c  Rice Crispies
1 c  Peanuts or Pecans
Melt Caramel, Remove from heat and add all ingredants except
Chocolate. Spread on Buttered Cookie sheet and try to even out the
thickness. after it has cooled, spread the Chocolate over it and
allow to cool and cut into squares.
