Title: Autumn Punch
Categories: Beverage, Fruit, Herbs/spice
Yield: 7 servings
2 t  Whole cloves
1 1/2 qt Cranberry juice
5 c  Apple juice
5 c  Apricot nectar
3 qt Ginger ale
Crushed ice
Makes 7-1/2 quarts
1 1/2 c  Honey
3/4 c  Lemon juice
6 ea Whole cardamom seeds
3 ea 3-inch sticks cinnamon
1 t  Whole allspice
Combine first 6 ingredients in a saucepan.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce
heat, simmer 10 minutes.  Strain and discard spices.  Chill.  Combine
chilled mixture with remaining juices and ginger ale.  Serve over
ice. Origin: Women's Circle, for cooks on the go. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens
