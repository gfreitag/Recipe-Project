Title: Oriental Deep Fried Chicken Wings
Categories: Chinese, Appetizer, Chicken, Poultry, Asian
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Dark soy sauce
1 ts Brown sugar
1/2 ts Msg or gourmet powder
Pinch of pepper
12    Pieces wings
1 ts Salt
1 tb Light soy sauce
1 tb Cooking wine
1. Marinate chicken wings with all ingr. mix well and let stand for 2
hrs stirring now and then. 2. In wok, heat 6 cups of oil until
smoking hot.
Deep fry  wings until brown, takes 15 to 20 min. Posted by Ann Benn.
