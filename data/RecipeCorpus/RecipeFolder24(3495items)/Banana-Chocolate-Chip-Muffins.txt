Title: Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins
Categories: Dessert, Muffin, Chocolate
Yield: 12 muffins
2 1/4 c  All purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Chocolate chips
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1 c  Extra ripe medium bananas
(approximately two med)
2    Eggs
1 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter, melted
1 ts Vanilla
Puree bananas in blender.  In medium bowl, beat pureed bananas, eggs,
sugar, butter and vanilla until well blended.

In large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon and salt.  Stir
in chocolate chips and nuts.  Make well in center of dry ingredients,
pour in banana mixture.  Mix until just blended.  Spoon into WELL
greased 2 1/2 inch muffin cups.  Bake in 350 oven for 25 to 30
minutes or until done. Remove from pans and cool.
