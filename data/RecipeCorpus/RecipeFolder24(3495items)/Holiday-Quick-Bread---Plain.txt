Title: Holiday Quick Bread - Plain
Categories: Bread, Holiday
Yield: 8 servings
4 tb Vegetable oil
1 ea Egg
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Salt
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
3 ts Baking Powder
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix together sugar, vegetable oil,
milk, egg and vanilla extract in a large bowl; beat well.  Add flour,
baking powder and salt.  Pour batter into a greased and floured 9"
loaf pan.  Bake 50 to 60 minutes or until toothpick inserted in
center comes out clean.
