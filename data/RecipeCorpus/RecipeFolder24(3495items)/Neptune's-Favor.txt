Title: Neptune's Favor
Categories: Casserole, Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 5 servings
2 T  Onion,grated
1 cn Mushrooms,sliced,drained(8oz
2 c  Milk
2    Eggs,hard-cooked,chopped
1 pk Alaska King Crab,frozen(6oz)
1/2 c  Swiss cheese,shredded
2 pk Spinach,chopped(10oz ea)
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Flour
3/4 t  Salt
1/4 t  Paprika
1/8 t  Rosemary,ground
1/8 t  Thyme,whole
1. Press spinach to remove excess moisture; place spinach in the
bottom and around the edge of a well-buttered 8-inch-square baking
dish.

2. In a medium-size saucepan, combine mayonnaise and flour to make a
paste.

3. Add salt, paprika, rosemary, thyme, onion and mushrooms to
saucepan; cook over medium heat until bubbly.

4. Gradually add milk; cook over medium heat, stirring constantly,
until thickened.

5. Stir in eggs.

6. Pour sauce into the center of the spinach-lined baking dish; top
with crab and cheese.

7. Bake in preheated 350'F. oven 20 minutes, or unitl bubbly and
cheese is melted.
