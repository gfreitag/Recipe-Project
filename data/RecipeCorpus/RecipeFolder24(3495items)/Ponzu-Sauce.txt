Title: Ponzu Sauce
Categories: Asian, Japanese, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 batch
3 tb Mirin; alcohol burnt off
10 g  Dried bonito flakes
-- (hana-katsuo)
-- (small handful)
1    5 cm square giant dried kelp
1 c  Strained lemon or
-- lime juice
1/3 c  Plus 2 tb rice vinegar
1 c  Dark soy sauce
2 tb Tamari
Combine ingredients and let stand for 24 hours.  Strain through
muslin and mature for three months in a cool dark place or in the
refrigerator. Keeps indefinitely but really best used within 12
months.

Yield:  2 1/2 cups.
