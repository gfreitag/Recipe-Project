Title: Honey-Garlic Pork Loin
Categories: Pork, Meat, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Honey
2 T  Soy sauce
1 T  Dry sherry
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
4 ea Boneless pork center loin
-chops, 1-1/4 to 1-1/2 inch
-thick
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Combine marinade ingredients and pour over chops in heavy plastic bag.
Seal. Refrigerate 4 to 24 hours.  Remove chops from marinade, reserve
marinade. Broil chops 5 inches from heat source, turning once, 12-15
minutes, and basting occasionally with reserved marinade.  DO NOT
OVERCOOK! Source: National Port Producers Council ad in Bon ApÂ‚tit
magazine, October 1990.
