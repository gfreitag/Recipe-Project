Title: Molasses Popcorn Balls
Categories: Candy, Kids, Snack, Popcorn
Yield: 10 servings
2 c  Molasses
1/2 t  Salt
1 t  Soda
4 qt Popped corn
1 c  Sugar
2 T  Butter
Boil Molasses, sugar, butter, and salt, stirring occasionally untill
mixture forms hard ball in cold water.  Remove from fire, add soda
and mix well.  Pour over popped corn, stirring so that each kernel
may be coated. Form into a ball with well buttered hands QUICKLY!
