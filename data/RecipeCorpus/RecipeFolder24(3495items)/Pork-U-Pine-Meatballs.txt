Title: Pork U Pine Meatballs
Categories: Ground beef, Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
1    Very large onion, chopped
2 cn Tomato sauce
3 lb Ground beef
1 1/2 c  Of rice
Mix ground beef, onions, rice, roll into balls. Place meatballs in
pan and pour tomato sauce over. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
