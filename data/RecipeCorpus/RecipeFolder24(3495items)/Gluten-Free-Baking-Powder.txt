Title: Gluten-Free Baking Powder
Categories: Diabetic
Yield: 1 servings
8 ts Cornstarch
1 ts Baking powder
2 ts Cream of tartar
Sift together 4-5 times.  Store in airtight container.  Use within 3
months.

Source:  A Guide for the Diabetic Celiac 1990, ISBN 0-921026-02-1
