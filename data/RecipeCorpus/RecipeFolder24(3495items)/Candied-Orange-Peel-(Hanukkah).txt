Title: Candied Orange Peel (Hanukkah)
Categories: Jewish, Dessert
Yield: 6 servings
4 tb Corn Syrup
3/4 c  Water
6 ea Oranges; peel of
1 1/2 c  Sugar
DIRECTIONS: Place the orange peel in a saucepan, and cover with water.
Bring to a boil, lower the heat, and simmer for 10 minutes. Drain.
Cover again with water and bring to a boil. Lower the heat, and
simmer for 20 minutes or until peel is soft. Drain, and slice the
peel into long strips.

Bring the sugar, corn syrup, and 3/4 cups water to a boil. Lower the
heat, and add the cooked orange peel. Cook the peel in the syrup
until most of the syrup is absorbed.

Spread the peel on a foil-lined cookie sheet covered with sugar.
Sprinkle more sugar all over the peel. Let dry. Store in a plastic
container.
