Title: Fresh Tomato Pasta with Ham, Cream and Mushroom Sauce
Categories: Pasta, Pork, Main dish, Down under
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------SOURCE:  AUSTRALIAN VOGUE WI---------------------
Butter
375 g  Fresh or packaged pasta
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
Salt and pepper
300 ml Cream
Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese
Chopped parsley
1 c  Sliced baby mushrooms
A little butter
4 sl Leg ham, chopped
1 ea Clove garlic, crushed
Chopped basil to taste
Cook pasta in boiling, salted water (2 minutes if fresh, 15 minutes if
dried).  Drain, return to saucepan and stir in some butter. To make
sauce: saute mushrooms lightly in butter.  Add ham, a little garlic,
basil, salt and pepper; stir in cream and heat.  Pour the sauce over
the noodles, sprinkle with cheese and chopped parsley and serve.
Sandra and Bruce McLean. Bon-Appetit, Exec.Chef. Magnus Johansson
