Title: Green Papaya Saald
Categories: Salad, Thai, Appetizer, Vegetable, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2 tb Soy sauce
1 ea Lemon, juiced
3 c  Papaya, unripe, grated
1/2 lb Green beans, julienned
2 ts Garlic chopped
2 ea Dried red chilies, soaked,
-- seeded & chopped
1 tb Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Cabbage leaves, torn
1/4 lb Cherry tomatoes
4 tb Peanuts, chopped
In a mortar, pound the garlic, chilies & suagr into a paste.  Mix in
the soy sauce & lemon juice.

Mix the papaya, beans & refrigerate until ready to serve.

At serving time, pour on the dressing & sprinkle the peanuts & cherry
tomatoes over the top.  Serve with chunks of raw cabbage leaves.

Adapted from Puangkram C. Schmitz & Michael J. Worman, "Practical Thai
Cooking"
Date: 09-21-94
From: Mark Satterly
