Title: Orange Juliana
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
1    Egg white
1 c  Orange juice
1 c  Crushed ice
Combine orange juice, ice, dry milk powder and egg white in blender
and blend until frothy. Pour into tall chilled glass.
