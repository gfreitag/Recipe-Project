Title: Rich Crumble Crust
Categories: 1941, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Butter, softened
1 1/2 c  Pastry flour
1/4 c  White sugar or mix with
Directions Sift, then measure. Re-sift with sugars. Work in with hand
butter. Spread some of thes mixture over the bottom of a greased deep
dish. Fill with fruit (sweetened and flavoured as for ordinary
pie)and cover with the crumble mixture. Or put the rich crumble over
the top only. Bake in a moxerate oven,375 degreesF, until the fruit
is tender and the crust browned.
Date: 09-08-94
From: Georgina Major
