Title: Shrimp Dunk
Categories: Appetizer, Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
3    Peppercorns
1    Bay leaf
1    Cl Garlic
1 lb Very large shelled shrimp, r
-aw
Approx. Cook Time: 2 hrs 1.
12 oz Great western beer
1 sm Onion, sliced
1    Celery, top and leaves,
-sliced
1 tb Salt
Fat grams    per serving:
Combine all ingredients except shrimp in a large saucepan. Cover;
heat to boiling. Boil 10 minutes. 2. Add shrimp. Cover and boil 5
minutes, or just until shrimp turn pink. Remove from heat; chill in
cooking liquid. 3. Serve cold with dunking bowls of cold beer.  Serve
as appetizer or as a summertime main entree. Makes 25 to 30 shrimp
