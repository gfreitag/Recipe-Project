Title: Orange Whip
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 3 servings
11 oz Mandarin oranges, drained
9 oz Cool Whip
2 c  Boiling water
3 3/4 oz Package of vanilla instant
Pudding
3 oz Package of orange Jello
15 oz Crushed pineapple, drained
Mix pudding and Jello in a bowl.  Add water and mix well. Refrigerate
15 minutes.  Add oranges and pineapple.  Refrigerate another 15
minutes. Fold in topping.  It tastes best when made 1 day ahead.
