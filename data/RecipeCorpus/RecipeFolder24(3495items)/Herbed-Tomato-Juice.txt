Title: Herbed Tomato Juice
Categories: Vegetable, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
Fresh parsley
Fresh basil
25 lb (11kg) tomatoes
Fresh dill
Wash and drain fresh ripe tomatoes.  Peel, core and cut into small
pieces, simmer until soft.  Press tomatoes through a food mill.
Reheat almost to boiling.  Pour into hot jars, leaving 1/4 inch (6mm)
headspace. Add a sprig of fresh herbs to each jar.  Adjust caps.
Process 35 minutes in boiling water bath.  Yield: about 6 quarts.
(5700 mL). 22 calories per 1/2 cup (120mL) serving. From: Ball Blue
