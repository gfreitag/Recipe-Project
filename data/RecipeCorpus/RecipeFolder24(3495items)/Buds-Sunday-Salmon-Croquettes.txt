Title: Buds Sunday Salmon Croquettes
Categories: Fish, Snack
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-----------------------THE CROQUETTES----------------------------
2    Eggs, large
1 tb Cajun Seasoning (Luzianne
-TM all-purpose w/lemon
1 c  Onions, finely chopped
1/2 c  Green Peppers finely chopped
1 c  Cracker crumbs
1 cn Salmon (14.75 oz)
MMMMM-------------------------THE SAUCE------------------------------
1 cn Peas (16 oz.)
Pepper to taste
1/2 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk
The croquettes------ Finely chop onions and green peppers. Microwave
them until tender (approx 3 min.) In medium bowl, beat eggs, add
drained salmon, cracker crumbs, and remainder of ingredients. Mix
thoroughly. Separate into balls and refrigerate 1 hr. Fry in oil
until brown, turning as neccessary. Serve with peas in white sauce.

The sauce In med. sauce pan, mix flour and milk and heat slowly until
thickened and sauce forms -- stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Add
peas. Serve over croquettes

Concocted by Bud Cloyd on a Sunday afternoon when the larder was
looking low.
