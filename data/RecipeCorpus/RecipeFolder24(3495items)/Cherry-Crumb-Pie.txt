Title: Cherry Crumb Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
Pet Ritz pie crust
1 cn Cherry pie filling
3/4 c  Flour
1/3 c  Sugar
6 tb Butter
Mix flour and sugar, add butter and mix until crumbly.  Place 1/2 of
the pie filling in pie crust.  Sprinkle with sugar.  Dot with butter;
repeat. Place crumb mixture atop pie filling.  Bake at 350 degrees
for 30 minutes. Randy Rigg
