Title: Three Veggies and Cheese
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
-combo
1 1/2 c  Skim milk
1 T  Margarine
1 T  Flour
1/2 t  Italian seasoning (or
-basil, rosemary, thyme
-combo)
2 c  Cauliflower florets
2 c  Broccoli florets (or stalk,
-sliced into sticks)
2 c  Carrots, cut into
-matchsticks
1 c  Calorie reduced swiss,
-mozzarella, cheddar, or
Make a white sauce by melting the margarine in saucepan.  Add flour,
and then gradually add the milk until completely mixed.  Heat slowly
until thickend.  Add grated cheese and italian seasoning and set
aside. Meanwhile, steam vegetables.  Spray a casserole dish with Pam,
and then toss the veggies together until mixed.  Arrange in casserole
dish, pour cheese sauce over top and bake in 350F oven for about 20
minutes, just until the cheese sauce melts down and around the
veggies. (can also zap it in the microwave.) Origin: Adaptation from
another recipe. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
