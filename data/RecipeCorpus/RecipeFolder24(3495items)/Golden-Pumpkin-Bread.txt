Title: Golden Pumpkin Bread
Categories: Holiday, Bread, Vegetable
Yield: 1 loaf
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1 1/2 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 c  Solid packed pumpkin
1/2 c  Butter, softened
2    Eggs
1 ts Baking powder
Directions In large bowl, combine all ingredients; beat until well
mixed. Pour into greased loaf pan. Bake in preheated 350 degrees F
for 45 to 55 minutes or until wooden pick inserted into center comes
out clean. Let cool in pan on wire rack 10 minutes. Loosen edges;
remove from pan. Cool completely on wire rack.
