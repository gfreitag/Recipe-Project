Title: Harvest Vegetables with Swiss Cheese Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
-vegetable.
This mild creamy sauce
-compliments almost any
MMMMM-------------------PREP TIME: 15 MINUTES------------------------
MMMMM-------------------COOK TIME: 10 MINUTES------------------------
8 c  Steamed vegetables
-(broccoli, cauliflower,
-carrots, green beans,
Etc)
g  Swiss cheese
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  All purpose flour
2 c  Milk
1 c  Grated swiss cheese
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1/2 t  Salt
Melt butter in saucepan over low heat.  Blend in flour.  Gradually add
milk; cook, stirring constantly until thickened.  Add cheeses and
salt. Stir until melted.  Pour over hot vegetables and top with
additional swiss cheese.  Serve immediately.  Makes 8-10 servings.
