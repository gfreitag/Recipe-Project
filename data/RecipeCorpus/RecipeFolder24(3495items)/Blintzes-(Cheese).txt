Title: Blintzes (Cheese)
Categories: Jewish, Cheese/eggs, Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
Few drops vanilla extract
Oil or shortening, then
-butter
Strawberry jam, or red
-currant jelly
Serves 4
1 c  Flour
pn Salt
2 ea Eggs
2/3 c  Milk
FILLING
1 c  Cottage cheese
Sift flour and salt, beat to a thick creamy batter with the eggs and
milk. Brush a thick skillet with oil or shortening.  Heat fry pan,
and fry pancakes until golden brown on both sides.  Keep hot or store
for later use.  Fill with the cottage cheese blended withthe sugar
and vanilla extract.  Place some fillin gin the center of each
pancake and roll up. Heat in hot butter for a few minutes.  Serve
topped with hot or cold jam, or with cream. Origin: Marguerite
