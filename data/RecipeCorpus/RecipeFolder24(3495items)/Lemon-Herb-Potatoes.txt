Title: Lemon Herb Potatoes
Categories: Diabetic, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM--------------------LEMOM HERB POTATOES-------------------------
1 ts Dill weed;
1/8 ts White pepper;
1 ts Lemon peel; grated
1 tb Parsley;
Paprika;
6 sm Baking potatoes;
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1 tb Unsalted margarine;
1 cl Garlic; Minced
1/4 c  Skimmed milk; hot
MMMMM-----------------CHEESE LEMON HERB POTATOES----------------------
MMMMM--------------------------VERATION-------------------------------
1/2 oz Cheese; for each potatoe
1.Bake potatoes until done. Cool. 2.Saute onions and garlic in
unsalted margarine. Scoop out potatoes and mash. Beat in onion
mixture, hot skimmed milk, lemon and pepper. Mix in dill and parsley.
Refill skins. Sprinkle with paprika. 3. Bake at 400F for 30 minutes.

Variation:  add 1/2 oz grated cheddar cheese on top of each potato
before baking the final time. Food Exchange per serving: 1
