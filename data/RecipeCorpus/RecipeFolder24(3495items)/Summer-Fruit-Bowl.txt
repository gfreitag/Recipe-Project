Title: Summer Fruit Bowl
Categories: Side dish, Fruit, Alcohol
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
2 T  Sweet red wine
1 T  Honey
3    Medium bananas
4    Medium oranges,sectioned
1 c  Strawberries,halved
1 c  Seedless green grapes,halved
Slice bananas into bowl. Cover completely with remaining fruit; cover
bowl and refrigerate.

Just before serving, mix remaining ingredients; pour over fruit. Toss
until fruit is coated.
