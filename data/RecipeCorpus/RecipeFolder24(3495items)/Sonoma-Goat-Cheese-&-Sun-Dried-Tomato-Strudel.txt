Title: Sonoma Goat Cheese & Sun Dried Tomato Strudel
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------BERINGER VINEYARDS--------------------------
2 tb Basil; chopped
2 tb Pine nuts; toasted
Salad baby greens
-tossed with both
Vinegar, Balsamic
Oil, Olive Extra Virgin
1 pk Phyllo dough
1/2 c  Oil, olive
8 oz Cheese, Goat (feta)
1/2 c  Tomatoes, sun dried
- (1/2 inch diced)
- soak in hot water if salty
1/4 c  Olives, Kalamata Greek
Thaw the phyllo dough; lay out 1 sheet and brush with olive oil.
Overlap another sheet on top and brush with olive oil again. Repeat
one more time.

Dice the cheese into 3/4 inch dices and toss with the diced sun dried
tomatoes, basil, olives, black pepper and pine nuts. Spoon a row of
this mixture on the bottom edge of the phyllo. Roll the phyllo up and
cut into desired portion size.  Brush exterior with olive oil and
place on a cookie sheet in the center of a preheated 425 degree oven
until golden brown, approximately 8 to 10 minutes.

Place on a salad plate and surround with baby greens.

Wine Suggestion: Serve as a first course with Beringer Sauvignon
Blanc or as a main course with Beringer Private Reserve Cabernet
Sauvignon or Knights Valley Cabernet Sauvignon.
