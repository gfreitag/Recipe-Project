Title: Apple Milk Drink (Sharbat)
Categories: Beverage, Fruit, Moroccan, Nuts/grains, Apples
Yield: 1 batch
Orange flower water (scant)
2 c  Milk, cold
Shaved ice (opt'l.)
2    Red eating apples
2 tb Sugar
2 ts Rosewater (scant)
-or-
Peel and cube the apples.  Place in the blender jar with the sugar,
perfumed water, and milk.  Whirl at high speed 15 seconds.  Serve,
with shaved ice if desired, in small glasses.

Yield: 2 to 4 servings.

From "Couscous and Other Good Food from Morocco" by Paula Wolfert. New
York: Harper & Row, Publishers, Inc., 1987.  Pg. 326.  ISBN
0-06-091396-7. Posted by Cathy Harned.
