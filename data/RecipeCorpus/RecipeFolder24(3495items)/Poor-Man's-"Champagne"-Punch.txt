Title: Poor Man's "Champagne" Punch
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 4 quarts
2 c  Unsweet. grapefruit juice
-chilled
48 oz Beer; chilled
Ice
1 qt Lemon juice from concentrate
-chiloled
4 c  Cold water
3 c  Sugar
In large punch bowl, combine lemon juice, water, sugar, and grapefruit
juice; stir to dissolve sugar.  Just before serving, add beer and ice.
