Title: Light Sour Cream Dip
Categories: Diabetic, Dip
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice; fresh squezzed
-or more to taste
1 c  1% low-fat cottage cheese;
2 tb Buttermilk;
In a blender, place cottage cheese, buttermilk and lemon juice. Blend
until smooth. Adapted from "Diet for a Happy Heart" and appeared in
the

San Diego Union Food Section, May 19, 1994 Brought to you and yours
via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
