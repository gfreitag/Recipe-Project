Title: Kay's Dressing & Poppy Seed Dressing
Categories: Diabetic, Dressing
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM-----------------------KAY'S DRESSING----------------------------
1/8 ts Ground pepper; fresh
1/16 ts Paprika
Sugar substitute equivalent
To 4 teaspoons sugar
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  White vinegar;
1/2 ts Salt;
1/2 ts Dry mustard;
MMMMM--------------------POPPY SEED DRESSING-------------------------
Omit paprika
1/2 c  Cider vinegar;
1 tb Poppy seed;
Combine all ingredients in a pint jar and cover tightly. Shake
vigorously and store in refrigerator.  Shake before using. Food
Exchange per serving: THIS IS A "FREE" FOOD   CAL: 4 CHO: 1g; PRO:
0g; FAT: 0g; Low-sodium diets: Omit salt

Variation Poppy Seed Dressing.  Follow recipe for Kay's
dressing(above), but use cider vinegar in place of white vinegar,
omit paprika, poppy seeds. Delicious over fruit salad. Food Exchange
per serving: THIS IS A "FREE" FOOD    CAL: 9 CHO: 2g; PRO: 0g; FAT:
0g; Low-sodium diets: Omit salt.

Source: The Art of Cooking for the Diabetic by Mary Abbot
