Title: Oriental Burgers
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Beef, Main dish, Burgers, Ground beef
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Grated lemon peel
1 tb Minced onion flakes
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Very lean ground beef
1 ea Egg white
1 tb Soy sauce
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Combine all ingredients, shape into 4 patties. Place on rack in
broiler pan or grill. cook 3 inches from heat about 5 minutes per
side, or until done to taste.

Per serving: 21g protein, 15g fat, 2g carb., 332mg sodium, 70mg
chol., 230 calories.
