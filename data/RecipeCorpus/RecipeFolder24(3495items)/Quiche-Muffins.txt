Title: Quiche Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin
Yield: 10 muffins
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Sharp cheddar, grated
2    Green onions
10 sl Bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 ct Cottage cheese (16 oz)
3    Egg whites
5    Eggs
1/4 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  Flour
Preheat oven to 400.  Place cottage cheese in a food processor fitted
with steel blade and process until smooth.  Transfer to a large bowl.
Process egg whites in food processor until foamy.  Add next 5
ingredients and process until smooth.  Add egg mixture to cottage
cheese. Stir in cheese, onions, and bacon.  Fill large greased muffin
tins 2/3 full and bake 12-15 minutes or until edges are light brown.

Yield: 10 muffins
