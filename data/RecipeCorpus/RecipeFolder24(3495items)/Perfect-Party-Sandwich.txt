Title: Perfect Party Sandwich
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Sandwich, Seafood, Chicken
Yield: 8 servings
2    Tomatoes, thinly sliced
12 oz Sliced LORRAINE cheese
1/2 lb Deli tuna salad
1/2 lb Deli chicken salad
1/2 lb Deli crab salad
1 lb Round herb bread, sliced
-horizontally into 4 layers
3/4 c  Italian dressing
Lettuce leaves
1    Red onion, thinly sliced
Sprinkle each layer of bread with dressing. Top with lettuce leaves,
onions, tomatoes, and Lorraine. Spread each salad over one layer of
bread. Reassemble loaf. Wrap tightly in plastic. Chill several hours.
Cut into 8 wedges.

From: Michael Orchekowski
