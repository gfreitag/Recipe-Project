Title: Spicy Tomato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Thai, Appetizer, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Red curry paste
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Lemon juice
30 oz Stewed tomatoes
5 oz Tofu, sliced
3 c  Coconut milk
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
2 tb Cilantro leaves, chopped
Puree the tomatoes.  Set aside.  Puree the tofu & the coconut milk.
Mix all the ingredients together in a soup pot.  Bring to a boil,
lower heat & simmer for 10 minutes.  Garnish with cilantro.

Puangkram C. Schmitz & Michael J. Worman, "Practical Thai Cooking"
