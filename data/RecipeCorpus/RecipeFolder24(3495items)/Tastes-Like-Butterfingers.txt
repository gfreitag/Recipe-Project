Title: Tastes Like Butterfingers
Categories: Candy
Yield: 3 bars
1 c  Peanut Butter
1 c  Coconut shreds  (opt)
Dipping Chocolate
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  White Syrup
1/3 c  Water
Combine all ingredants except Peanut Butter, cook to 300 degrees,
remove from heat and add Peanut Butter, stir very quickly only until
the Syrup and Peanut Butter is mixed, hurry on this part, pour on
counter top or Marble Slab and quickly form into three logs or three
ropes. Cut with Kitchen Shears.  When cool, dip in dipping Chocolate.
These are also good rolled in Coconut shreds while still warm.
