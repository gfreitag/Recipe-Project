Title: Peppered Cheddar Dip
Categories: Dip
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Red pepper sauce
1 tb Milk
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
1 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4 c  Finely chopped onions
3 tb Minced green pepper
Blend all ingredients.  If necessary, add another tbs of milk to make
a good dipping consistency.  Cover, refrigerate at least 1 hour.
Makes 2 cups Spicy as a Southwestern favorite should be.  Serve hot
with corn chips.
