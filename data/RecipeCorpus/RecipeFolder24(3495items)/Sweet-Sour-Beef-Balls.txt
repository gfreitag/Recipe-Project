Title: Sweet Sour Beef Balls
Categories: Main dish, Meatballs, Ground beef
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Pineapple juice
1 tb Soy sauce
3 tb Vinegar
6 tb Water
1/2 c  Sugar
4 sl Pineapple
3 lg Green peppers
1 lb Ground beef
1    Egg
1 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Salt
2 tb Onion, finely chopped
Few grains of pepper
1 tb Oil
Mix beef, egg, 1 tablespoon of cornstarch, salt, onion, and pepper.
Form into small balls, 20 or more.  Brown them in a small amount of
oil. Drain. To 1 tablespoon of oil, add pineapple juice and cook
slowly for a few minutes.  Add mixture of cornstarch, soy sauce,
vinegar, water, and sugar. Cook until it thickens, stirring
constantly. Add meat balls, cut up pineapple, and green pepper, cut
into strips. Heat thoroughly. Serve hot with steamed rice. Randy Rigg
