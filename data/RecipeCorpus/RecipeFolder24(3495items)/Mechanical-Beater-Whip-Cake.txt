Title: Mechanical Beater Whip Cake
Categories: 1941, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 cake
2    Eggs
1/2 c  +1 tbsp milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Butter
1 3/4 c  Pastry flour
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
Directions Soften butter to the consistency of mayonnaise. Sift and
measure flour; combine very thoroughly with sugar and salt.Sift the
dry ingredients over the butter in the bowl of mechanical mixer. Beat
at medium speed for about 1 minute okr until very well mixed. ( the
mixture will be very crumbly). Scrape down well from sides of bowl.
Add unbeaten eggs with milk and vanilla. Beat at high speed for 1 1/2
minutes or until a smooth, light batter is obtained. Again scrape
down bowl. Take baking powder and sift over the batter. Beat at low
speed for about 1 minute, being sure to get all the batter from the
bottom of the bowl. Turn into greased and floured square or layer
pans. Bake at 350 degrees F for 45 to 50 minutes for a square cake
375 degrees F for 25 to 30 minutes for layers. From: Georgina Major
Date: 09-08-94
