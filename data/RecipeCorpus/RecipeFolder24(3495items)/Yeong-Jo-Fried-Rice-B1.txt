Title: Yeong Jo Fried Rice B1
Categories: Rice, Chinese, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  GREEN PEAS
1 c  LETTUCE,SHREDDED
3 c  COLD COOKED RICE
2 tb VEGETABLE OIL
1/2 ts SALT
2 tb SOY SAUCE
1/4 c  RAW PRAWNS OR SHRIMP,
-DICED
1/4 c  BARBECUED PORK OR COOKED
-HAM,DICED
1/4 c  GREEN ONIONS, CUT 1/8
-IN. THICK
Place oil in preheated wok or skillet. Bring oil to sizzling point,
add prawns or shrimp & salt. Toss & turn 2-3 min. or until cooked.
Add pork or ham,green onions,peas,lettuce,rice and soy sauce. Press
rice gently into pan and fry for a few seconds, repeating the process
until all rice is hot completely through. Turn & mix rapidly 5-7 min.
Add a few drops of oil if necessary to prevent burning.
Temperature(s): HOT Effort: AVERAGE Time: 00:15 Source: KAN'S
Comments: GRAND ST., SAN FRANCISCO Comments: WINE: WAN FU WHITE WINE
