Title: Mary Anne's Spinach Dip
Categories: Dip
Yield: 2 cups
1 pk Frozen Chopped Spinach,
(cooked and drained)
1 pk Knorr Vegetable Soup
1 c  Sour Cream
1 c  Mayonaise
Mix all ingredients the night before using. Refrigerate overnight.
Great with crackers, pumpernickle bread etc... You can serve it
inside a hollowed out loaf of bread for something a little more
fancy. Typed for you by Peggy and Bruce Travers,Cyberealm BBS
