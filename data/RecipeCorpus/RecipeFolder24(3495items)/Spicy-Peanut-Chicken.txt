Title: Spicy Peanut Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1    Jalepeno
2 tb Lime juice
4 tb Peanut butter
1 ts Garlic, minced
1 c  Tomatoes, canned, crushed
Cilantro leaves, garnish
2 c  Onion, sliced
4    Chicken breasts, boned
2 ts Cumin,
1 ts Cinnamon
4 tb Oil
4    Tomatoes, plum
Slice onion, wash and dry chicken breasts, skin, rub cumin, and
cinnamon in each side.  Heat oil in skillet, saute onion and chicken
in oil, browning chicken on each side.  Cut tomatoes into large
chunks, half pepper and remove seeds and membrane, chop finely, chop
tomatoes, add peanuts, peppers,garlic and puree, stir well. when
chicken is browned, pour sauce over and cover. reduce heat, simmer
for 10 minutes, until cooked Serve over rice, with cilantro leaves.
