Title: Dessert Zucchini Fritters
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dessert, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1/8 ts Ground allspice
2 tb Raisins
1 c  Finally shredded zucchini
-unpeeled
2    Eggs
2/3 c  Lowfat cottage cheese
2 sl White or WW bread crumbled
6 ts Sugar
1 ds Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
2 ts Vegetable oil
In a blender container, combine all ingredients except raisins and
zucchini. Blend until smooth. Pour mixture into a bowl. Drain
zucchini on paper towels. Stir zucchini and raisins into egg mixture.
Preheat a nonstick skillet or griddle over medium high heat. Drop
batter onto griddle with a large spoon, making 4-inch cakes. Turn
fritters carefully when edges appear dry. Brown lightly on both
sides. Divide evenly.

Per serving: 18g protein, 12g fat, 25g carb., 659mg sodium, 278mg
chol., 334 calories.
