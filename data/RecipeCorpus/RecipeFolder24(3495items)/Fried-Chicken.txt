Title: Fried Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Evaporated milk
1    Broiler - fryer chicken
Cut up
1/4 c  Crisco oil
1/3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Pepper
Directions Mix flour, salt, paprika, garlic powder, and pepper in
large plastic food storage bag. Set aside. Pour milk into bowl. Dip
chicken in milk. Add a few chicken pieces to food storage bag. Shake
to coat. Remove chicken from bag. Repeat with remaining chicken. Heat
oil in large skillet. Add chicken. Brown over medium - high heat.
Cook over moderate heat about 25 minutes, or until meat near bone is
no longer pink and juices run clear, turning pieces over frequently.
