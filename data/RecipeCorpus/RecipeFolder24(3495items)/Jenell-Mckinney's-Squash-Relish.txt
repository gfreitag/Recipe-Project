Title: Jenell Mckinney's Squash Relish
Categories: Can/preserv, Condiment, Relish, Vegetable
Yield: 1 batch
1 c  Chopped green tomato
2 c  Vinegar
2 1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Mustard seed
1 ts Celery seed
2 ts Salt
8 c  Chopped squash
-- mixture of yellow and
-- zucchini for color
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped green pepper
Sprinkle salt over vegetables.  Let set for one hour; drain.

Boil rest of ingredients and add drained vegetables  Cook 8 to 10
minutes. Place in sterilized jars and seal.

Recipe from Jenell McKinney of the Kaufman County (TX) Electric
Cooperative, in the August 1992 Reader-Recipe Contest of "Texas Co-op
Power" magazine.  Vol. 49, No. 2.  Pg. 25.  Posted by Cathy Harned.
Date: 09-26-94
