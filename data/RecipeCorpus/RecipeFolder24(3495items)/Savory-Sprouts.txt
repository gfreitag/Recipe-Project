Title: Savory Sprouts
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Dried basil
1 pk Instant chicken broth
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ds Nutmeg
1    10 oz pkg frozen brussell
-sprouts
1 tb Plus
1 ts -margarine
1/8 ts Ground savory
Cook Brussell sprouts according to package directions. Drain. While
sprouts are cooking, melt margarine in a small saucepan over medium
heat. Add remaining ingredients and pour over sprouts. Toss and serve

Per serving: 3g protien, 4g fat, 6g carb., 264mg sodium, 0mg chol., 62
calories.
