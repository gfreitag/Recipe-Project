Title: Sage Cheese
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Herbs/spice, Spread
Yield: 1 batch
1 ts Minced fresh savory or
1/2 ts Dried savory
1 ts Tamari
1 tb Finely chopped chives
1 c  Ricotta cheese
2 tb Parmesan cheese
1 tb White wine
2 ts Minced fresh sage or
1 ts Rubbed sage
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Chopped chives
Fresh herbs or
Stir all the ingredients together vigorously until thoroughly blended.
Scrape into a serving bowl and smooth the top.  Cover and chill at
least 1 hour before serving.  Garnish.

Yield: About 1 1/2 cups.

Barker writes: "This cheese makes a great sandwich spread or cracker
topper. For a dip, add a little milk or extra wine."

From Jennifer Stein Barker/Canyon City, OR in "Kitchen Table: Where
Herbs and Spices Make a Difference" column in "The Herb Companion."
