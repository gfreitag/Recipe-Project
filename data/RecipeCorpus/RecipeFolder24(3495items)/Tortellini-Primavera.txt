Title: Tortellini Primavera
Categories: Side dish, Pasta, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Milk
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
1/8 ts Pepper, black
1/4 c  Cheese, parmesan; grated
9 oz Tortellini, cheese
2 c  Vegetables, frozen; any
Oil, olive
3 tb Margarine
1 pk Pasta prima alfredo sauce mx
Cook pasta in 3-qt. saucepan according to package directions, adding
vegetables to cooking water last 5 minutes.  Drain and toss with a
little olive oil to keep from sticking.  Set aside.

Melt margarine in same saucepan over medium heat.  Add Alfredo Blend,
milk, Italian Seasoning and Pepper with a whisk.  Bring to a boil,
reduce heat and simmer 3 minutes or until thickened, stirring
occasionally.

Toss in cooked pasta, vegetables and cheese.  Serve immediately.
