Title: Grandma Vavricek's Kolache Recipe
Categories: Fruit, Meat, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
2 t  Salt
4 ea Egg yolks plus 1 egg
-slightly beaten
1 t  Grated lemon rind
6 1/4 c  Sifted flour
Softened butter as needed
2 ea Env. active dry yeast
1/4 c  Lukewarm water
1 T  Sugar
1 cn Evap milk, add water to
-make 2 cups
3/4 c  Butter
3/4 c  Sugar
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
In a small bowl dissolve yeast in warm water.  Sprinkle with 1 tbsp
sugar and let stand.  Scald milk in sauce pan.  Remove from heat and
stir in butter and 1/2 cup sugar.  Cool to lukewarm.  Add salt and
egg yolks. Combine milk mixture to yeast in large bowl.  Bit by bit
add the flour until all has been used.  Knead dough on lightly
floured board until glossy. Place in bowl, cover to let rise in warm
place (80o) until doubled in bulk about 1 hour.  This dough will not
be quite as stiff as a roll dough.  Now using a tbs take egg-sized
bits of the dough and roll into balls on the floured board.  Place on
greased baking sheet about 1 inch apart.  Brush each with softened
butter.  Let rise again.  covered until light. Make an indentation in
each, large enough to hold 1 tsp of fruit filling. Sprinkle fruit
with some of the topping. Bake at 425o for 15 minutes. Remove from
oven. Brush with softened butter immediately. Cool kolaches on wire
rack. Makes about 3 1/2 dozen. TOPPING 1 CUP FLOUR 1/2 CUP SUGAR 1/4
CUP BUTTER OR MARGARINE Mix all ingredients together in mixer until
the consistency of cornmeal. POPPY SEED FILLING 1 CAN POPPY SEED 1/4
CUP VANILLA WAFER CRUMBS 1/4 CUP PECAN MEAL OR FINELY CHOPPED PECANS
(USE FOOD PROCESSOR) 1/3 CUP HONEY Mix well. Place by tsp full into
kolache indentations. CREAM CHEESE FILLING 8 OZ CREAM CHEESE 2 EGG
YOLKS 1/4 TO 1/2 CUP SUGAR GRATED RIND OF ONE LEMON Beat all
ingredients together and place by tsp full into kolache indentations.
