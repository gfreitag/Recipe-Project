Title: Lentils with Rice (Moujadara)
Categories: Middle east, Rice, Side dish
Yield: 1 side dish
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Rice (washed and drained)
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1 md Onion (julienned)
1 1/2 c  Lentils
-drained)
8 c  Water
1 1/2 ts Salt
Instructions:

1.  Wash lentils, drain and place in saucepan.  Cover and cook for 1
hour or until lentils are tender.

2.  Add salt and pepper to the lentils.  Cook at a steady boil on low
heat for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.

3.  Saute onions in oil until onions are a golden brown.  Add to
cooking lentil mixture, stir and remove from heat.

Serve hot or cold with cold yogurt, a salad, pickles and a squeeze of
lemon over each individual serving.

Source/Author: A Taste of Lebanon by Mary Salloum / type Bob Shiells
