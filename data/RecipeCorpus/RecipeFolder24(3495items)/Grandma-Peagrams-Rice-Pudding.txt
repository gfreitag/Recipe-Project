Title: Grandma Peagrams Rice Pudding
Categories: Dessert, Pudding
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM---------------------------
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 pn Salt
4 c  Milk, 2 %
1 tb Butter
1/3 c  Rice, short grain  *
In a large saucepan, heat milk over medium high heat til bubbles
appear around edge.  Remove from heat.  In a greased 8 cup baking
dish preferably glass, combine milk, rice, sugar, butter, vanilla and
salt. Bake in 300 F oven about 2 hours or until rice is just tender
and most of milk is absorbed, stirring twice during first hour.

I used long grain and it turned out fine. HP
