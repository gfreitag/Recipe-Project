Title: Russian Tea Mix
Categories: Beverage, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cloves
2 c  Orange drink mix (Tang)
3 oz Dry lemonade mix
3/4 c  Instant tea powder
Combine orange drink mix, lemonade mix, instant tea powder, sugar,
cinnamon and cloves and mix well. Store in airtight container. To
make tea, use 2 or 3 tablespoons per cup boiling water.

Makes about 4 1/2 cups mix
