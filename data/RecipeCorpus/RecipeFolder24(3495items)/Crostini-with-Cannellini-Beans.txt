Title: Crostini with Cannellini Beans
Categories: Italian, Appetizer, Beans
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Fresh parsley, minced
Fresh ground black pepper
1    Recipe basic crostini
1 cn White kidney beans
-Cannellini beans
1 sm Clove garlic, minced
3 tb Olive oil
Note: white kidney beans are also known as canellini beans and are
widely available in most supermarkets.

Drain and rinse the beans.  Place in a bowl and mix well with
remaining ingrdients.  Add fresh ground black pepper to taste. Serve
on crostini.
