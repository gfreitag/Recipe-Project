Title: Almond Butter Syrup
Categories: Syrup
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ea To 1 tsp almond extract
1/2 t  Butter flavor
1 c  Light corn syrup
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Water
In medium saucepan bring corn syrup, brown sugar, and water to a
boil. Cook 5 minutes.  Remove from heat and let cool slightly.  Add
Almond Extract and butter flavor.  Pour into suitable containers and
store in refrigerator. Serve over pancakes, waffles, French toast, or
hot homemade biscuits. Origin: Watkins Food Gifts Cookbook. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
