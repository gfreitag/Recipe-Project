Title: Menorah Salad (Candle)
Categories: Jewish, Salad, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Recipe
Cut a banana in half crosswise. Stand upright in round slice of canned
pineapple on lettuce to form the candle. Place a maraschino cherry on
top of banana to represent flame. Make handle from a strip of green
pepper looped into pineapple at base of candle. Pour a little
mayonnaise down the side of the candle and base to represent dripping
wax. From: The Complete American-Jewish Cookbook By: Anne London and
Bertha Kahn Bishov Posted By: S. Lefkowitz
