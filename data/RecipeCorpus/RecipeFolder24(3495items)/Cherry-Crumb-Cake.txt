Title: Cherry Crumb Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Low-cal/fat, Fruit
Yield: 24 servings
MMMMM---------------------LIZ JONES VXRF36B--------------------------
1/2 c  To 3/4 c liquid Butter Buds
1 cn Pie filling; strawberry/
-cherry/apple/or blueberry
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Quick oats
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
Mix everything together, except the pie filling. Reserve 1 cup of the
crumb mixture for topping. Pat into a 9x13x2" casserole dish that has
been sprayed with a non-fat cooking spray. Pour can of pie filling on
top og crumb mixture.  Sprinkle the rest of crumb mixture (1 cup) on
top of pie filling.  Bake 45 minutes for 1 hour in a slow oven.
300~F. Serve warm with vanilla ono-fat yogurt or Dream Whip.
Sprinkle Grape-Nuts cereal on top, if desired. Serves 8-12 Per
serving: Fat: 0.37g; Calories: 105; Protein: 3.2g; Cholesterol: 0mg;
Carbohydrate: 14.3g: Sodium: 63mg. Source: "Butter Busters" by Pam
Mycoskie - 1992 From: Dorie Villarreal ~-- From: Dale Shipp
