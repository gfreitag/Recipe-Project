Title: Eggplant Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  Basil
1/2 t  Oregano
2 T  Olive oil
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
1 md Onion, chopped
2 ea Bell peppers, chopped
1/2 sm Head cauliflower, thinly
-sliced
1 ea Spear broccoli, thinly
-sliced
This is an absolute must to
-try..
Serves 4.
3 c  Grated eggplant
3 ea Eggs, beaten
1 1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1/4 c  Wheat germ
16 oz Can stewed tomatoes
12 oz Tomato sauce
1/4 t  Freshly ground pepper
Preheat oven to 400F.  Mix eggplant, eggs, flour and wheat germ
together. Spread onto a greased pizza pan.  Bake in oven for 20
minutes, or until crust is lightly browned.  Meanwhile, in a large
mixing bowl, combine stewed tomatoes, tomato sauce, pepper, basil and
oregano. Set aside. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet, add garlic,
onion, peppers, cauliflower and broccoli.  Stir fry 5-10 minutes.
When crust is done, reduce oven heat to 350F.  Cover crust with
tomato sauce, then layer with vegetable mixture, and cook pizza 30
minutes.  Serve hot. Origin: Yeast Free Living. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Oct/94.
