Title: Peach Pickle Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Parsley, fresh; chopped
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
8    Peaches, pickled; chopped
Water
3 oz Gelatin, orange-flavored
Drain peaches and reserve liquid; add water to liquid to make 2 cups;
heat until boiling.  Pour liquid over gelatin and stir until
dissolved. Chill until slightly thick.  Add remaining ingredients.
Pour into a 5-cup mold or individual molds.  Chill until firm.
