Title: Creamy Cheese and Spinach
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Cheese/eggs, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
Salt and pepper to taste
3 oz Shredded muenster cheese
1 oz Grated parmesan cheese
2 ea 10oz boxes chopped spinach
-thawed and drained
2 tb Plus (see Next Line)
2 ts -reduced calorie margarine
1/2 c  Skim milk
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
1/2 ts Onion powder
In medium saucepan, combine all ingredients except spinach, and heat
until cheese is melted. Add spinach and heat gently till heated
through.

Per serving: 13g protein, 13g fat, 8g carb., 467mg sodium, 27mg
chol., 190 calories.
