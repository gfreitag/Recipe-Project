Title: Creamy Baked Cheesecake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
14 oz Eagle Brand sweetened milk
(not evaporated milk)
3    Eggs
1/4 c  ReaLemon juice concentrate
8 oz Sour cream
1/3 c  Butter, melted
1 1/4 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/4 c  Sugar
2    8 oz. packages of cream
Cheese, softened
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.  Combine butter, crumbs, and sugar; press
firmly on the bottom of a 9 inch springform pan.  In a large mixer
bowl, beat cheese until fluffy.  Gradually beat in sweetened
condensed milk until smooth.  Add eggs and ReaLemon; mix well.  Pour
into prepared pan. Bae 50 to 55 minutes or until set.  Cool; chill
thoroughly. Spread sour cream on top.  Garnish if desired,
refrigerate your leftovers. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On
10-01-94
