Title: Pecan Pralines
Categories: Dessert, Candy
Yield: 1 unknown
2 tb Butter
2 1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Sugar, white
1 ts Soda
1 c  Buttermilk
1/8 ts Salt
In large heavy saucepan combine sugar, soda, buttermilk, and salt.
Cook over high heat for 5 minutes, stirring often.  Scrape bottom of
pan; add butter and pecans and continue cooking, about 5 minutes,
until candy reaches soft ball stage.  Remove from heat and add
vanilla; cool slightly. Beat until creamy.  Quickly drop by
tablespoonfuls onto waxed paper and let cool.

SOURCE: The Popular Pecan, pamphlet by the Mississippi Pecan Growers
Assn. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
