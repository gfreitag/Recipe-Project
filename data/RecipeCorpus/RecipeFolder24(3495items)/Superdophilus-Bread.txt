Title: Superdophilus Bread
Categories: Bread
Yield: 2 servings
-flour
1/2 c  Oat bran
1/2 c  Yellow corn flour or soy
-flour
2 c  Lukewarm water
1 ea Level tsp lactobacillus
-acidophillus culture
2 1/2 ea To 3 cups whole wheat bread
Stir lactobacillus culture with water in a large bowl.  Add warmed
flours and bran and stir to form a soft dough.  Cover and leave in a
warm place for at least 4 hours.  Knead for about 5 minutes, adding
more flour if too sticky.  Shape into two small loaves and allow to
rise in a warm place on a cookie sheet for 1-3 hours.  Bake at 350F
for 40-45 minutes. This bread may be varied by using different flour,
eg. rye. Superdophillus is obtainable from health food stores, or
from: Natren Inc, No. Hollywood, CA, 91602. Origin: Yeast-Free Feast:
Recipes and Ideas to Help Control Yeast Related and other
Environmental Health Problems. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
