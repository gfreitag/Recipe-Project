Title: Chocolate Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Dessert, Down under
Yield: 8 servings
-Wine and Cookbook 1985
-Source:  Australian Vogue
2 cups milk 1 tablespoon castor sugar 5 egg yolks 75 g Small's dark
chocolate, broken in squares 2 tablespoons chocolate liqueur

Bring the milk to boil in a saucepan.  Beat together castor sugar and
yolks.  Pour on a little boiling milk and mix with wooden spoon.
Return mixture to saucepan with rest of milk and cook over low heat
until mixture coats back of spoon.  Strain into bowl, add chocolate,
stir until dissolved.  Allow to cool, add chocolate liqueur.  Chill,
covered, in refrigerator until serving time. Bon-Appetit, Exec.Chef.
Magnus Johansson
