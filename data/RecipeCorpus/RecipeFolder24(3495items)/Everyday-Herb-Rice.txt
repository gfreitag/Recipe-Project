Title: Everyday Herb Rice
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Rice, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1 pn Ground cloves
1/2 c  Wild OR brown rice
2 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Chopped scallion, green with
-some white
1 tb Dried onion flakes
1/2 tb Dried celery flakes
2 tb Dried parsley
1/2 ts Dried garlic flakes
1 tb Dried green pepper flakes
1/2 ts Ground tumeric
1 tb Chicken flavored granules
-or bouillion
In a small bowl, combine the first 10 ingredients. In a medium
saucepan, combine the rice, water, and the herb mixture. Bring to a
boil, partially cover, lower the heat, and simmer for 45 minutes, or
until the rice is cooked. Fluff with a fork. Fold in the scallions
and serve at once.

Per serving: 174 calories, 37g carb., 7g protein, 0mg chol., .6g/3%
fat.
