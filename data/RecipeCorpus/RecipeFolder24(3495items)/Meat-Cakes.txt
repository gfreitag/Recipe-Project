Title: Meat Cakes
Categories: Diabetic, Meat, Main dish, Fruit
Yield: 18 meat loave
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Dried sage
1 c  Unsweetened applesauce
1/2 c  Whole wheat bread crumbs
1/2 c  Unprocessed bran
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 lb Bulk Italian sausage
1/2 c  Minced onion
1 ts Italian seasoning
1 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Mix all ingredients together well.
Place 1/3 cup (2 oz.) mixture in each of 16 muffin cups. Bake at
350F. until done, about 1 hour.  Remove from muffin tins. Serve hot
or cold. Note: Nice for a picnic. Yield: 16 meat cakes Nutrient
analysis of 1 meat cake: 1/2 starch/bread exchange; 2 lean meat
exchanges; 150 Calories; 12 g protein; 8 g fat; 8 g carbohydrate; 360
mg sodium; 41 mg cholesterol.

Source: The Complete Diabetic Cookbook by PJ Palumbo,MD and Joyce Daly
Margie, MS
