Title: Orange Baked Pork Chops
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Pork, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Grated orange peel
1/4 ts Dry mustard
2 ts Onion flakes
4    5 0z. Pork loin chops
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Honey
Broil chops in preheated broiler, turning once, until lightly browned.
Place in a shallow baking pan. Combine remaining ingredients and por
over chops. Cover and bake 1 hour.

Per serving: 28g protein, 9g fat, 9g carb., 324mg sodium, 83mg chol.,
232 calories.
