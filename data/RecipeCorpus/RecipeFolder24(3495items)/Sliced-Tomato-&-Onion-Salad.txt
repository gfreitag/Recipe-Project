Title: Sliced Tomato & Onion Salad
Categories: Salad, Moroccan, Appetizer, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Vinegar
1 ea Garlic clove, crushed
Salt & pepper
Parsley, chopped
1 pn Sweet paprika
1 pn Cumin
6 ea Tomatoes, peeled & sliced
-- crosswise
1/2 ea Spanish onion -=OR=-
1 ea Red onion, sliced into thin
-- rings
1/4 c  Olive oil
Combine the tomato & onion slices.  Make a sauce with the remaining
ingredients & pour over the tomatoes & onion.

Paula Wolfert, "Couscous & Other Good Food of Morocco"
