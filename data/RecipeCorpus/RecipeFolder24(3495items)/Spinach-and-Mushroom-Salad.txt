Title: Spinach and Mushroom Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 1 batch
2    To 3 tb. rice wine vinegar
-or-
2    To 3 tb. champagne vinegar
1 ts Vietnamese/Thai fish sauce
-or-
1 ts Chinese oyster sauce
1/4 ts Sugar
1 sm Bunch of spinach
1 c  Sliced fresh mushrooms
1/4    To 1/2 tsp. mashed fresh or
-- rehydrated turmeric
-or-
1/4 ts Ground dried turmeric
1    Garlic clove; mashed
2 tb Olive oil
Rinse the spinach in several changes of water, drain it well, and pat
the leaves dry on toweling.  Shred the leaves coarsely and toss them
with the mushroom slices into a salad bowl.  Mash the fresh or
rehydrated turmeric to a paste in a mortar or with a garlic press and
put it, or the ground turmeric, in a small bowl.  Add the garlic,
then whisk in the oil, vinegar, fish sauce and sugar.  Pour over the
salad, toss lightly, and serve.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings.

Carlson writes: "Turmeric provides a warm note in this tart, earthy
salad."
