Title: Mom's Stuffed Mushrooms
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 2 servings
1/8 ts Salt
12 md Mushroom caps
1/4 c  Bacon grease
3 sl Bacon
1/3 c  Sharp Cheddar cheese, shred
1/2 ts Chopped green onions
Fry the bacon, then drain and crumble.  Combine bacon with cheese,
onions and salt.  (Mushroom stems can be chopped and added, also.)
Dip mushroom caps in bacon grease; place on broiler pan.  Fill caps
with bacon-cheese mixture.  Place tops of mushrooms 4 inches from
broiling unit; broil 6 to 8 minutes.  This recipe can be prepared
ahead of time and refrigerated. Do NOT broil until just before
serving as hor d'oeuvres. This recipe is especially good in
complimenting a steak or roast. Randy Rigg
