Title: Redhot Salsa Dressing
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 20 servings
6 1/2 c  Tomatoes; crushed, undrained    1 1/3 c  Mild green chilies; chopped,
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Cilantro; dried
1 c  Onions; chopped
3/4 c  Durkee redhot cayenne pepper
- sauce
Recipe by: Durkee RedHot Cayenne Pepper Sauce: Combine all
ingredients in large bowl.  Stir well.  Cover and allow to rest at
room temperature for 30 minutes before serving.

Serve at room temperature with Layered Taco Salad or use to top Macho
Nachos.
