Title: Christmas Ornament Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Unsifted flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
*INGREDIENTS*
3/4 c  Butter, softened
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 lg Eggs
*DIRECTIONS*

In large mixer bowl at medium speed, beat butter and sugar.  Beat in
eggs and vanilla.  Mix flour and baking powder and stir into butter
mixture. Divide dough into thirds; shape into disks; wrap in plastic
and refrigerate for 3 hours. Roll into 1/8" thickness and cut. Bake
at 375 degrees for 6 minutes or almost lightly browned. Cool and
decorate with Xmas Icing. Submitted By FRAN MCGEE   On   09-26-94
