Title: Sweet and Pungent Beets
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Cider vinegar
Salt to taste
2 c  Cooked, sliced beets
1 tb Plus (see next ingredient)
1 ts -honey
In saucepan, combine all ingredients and cookk until heated through.
Serve hot or cold.

Per serving: 1g protein, 0g fat, 12g carb., 42mg sodium, 0mg chol., 49
calories.

From: Carolyn Shaw
