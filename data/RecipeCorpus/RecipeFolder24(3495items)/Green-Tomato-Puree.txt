Title: Green Tomato Puree
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 6 cups
2 tb Sugar
3 lb Green tomatoes
1/2 c  ;Water
Core tomatoes and cut into 1" chunks.  Place in a stainless steel or
enamelware saucepan.  Add water and sugar.  Bring to a boil over
medium heat.  Lower heat to a simmer.  Cover.  Cook 2 hours or until
tomato bits are very soft.  Place mixture, 1/3 at a time, in blender
or food processor. Process until smooth.  Mixture can be frozen.

From Alice Colombo's 09/21/94 "Cook's Corner" column in "The
(Louisville, KY) Courier-Journal."  Pg. C6.  Posted by Cathy Harned.
