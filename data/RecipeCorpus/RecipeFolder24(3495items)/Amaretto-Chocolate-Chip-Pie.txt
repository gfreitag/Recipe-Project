Title: Amaretto Chocolate Chip Pie
Categories: Dessert, Pie/tart, Chocolate
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chocolate chips, semisweet
1/2 c  Almonds, sliced
1    Pie crust; unbaked
Whipped cream or ice cream
3    Eggs
3/4 c  Syrup, dark corn
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Amaretto
2 tb Butter; melted
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In large mixing bowl, beat eggs until
blended.  Stir in corn syrup, sugar, amaretto, butter and salt.  Add
chocolate chips and almonds.  Pour into unbaked pie crust.  Bake 50
to 60 minutes until knife inserted between center and pie edge comes
out clean. Cool completely.  Serve with whipped cream or ice cream.

SOURCE: Amaretto di Amore Recipes, given out at Simply Southern, the
