Title: Lemony Oriental Marinade
Categories: Marinade
Yield: 1 half cup
1 1/2 tb Oil, vegetable
3/4 ts Ginger, fresh; grated
1/4 ts Red pepper pods; crushed
1/4 c  Lemon juice, fresh
3 tb Green onion; chopped
1 1/2 tb Soy sauce, reduced-sodium
Combine all ingredients, stirring until well blended.

SOURCE: Marinate and Grill Beef Recipes, 1991 Beef Industry Council
From Simply Southern, the 1994 Southern Living Cooking School Typos
by Nancy Coleman.
Date: 09-26-94
From: Nancy Coleman
