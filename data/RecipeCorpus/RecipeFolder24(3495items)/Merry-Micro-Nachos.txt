Title: Merry Micro-Nachos
Categories: Tex/mexican, Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
Pace Picante Sauce or Pace
-Thick & Chunky Salsa
Tortilla chips
3/4 c  Shredded cheddar or
-monterey jack cheese
MMMMM-------------------------GARNISHES------------------------------
Chopped shrimp
Sour cream
Chopped cilantro
Guacamole
Sliced ripe olives
Shredded crab meat
Cover a 9" or 10" microwaveable plate with tortilla chips. Sprinkle
shredded cheese evenly over the chips. Microwave at HIGH (full power)
30-60 seconds or just until cheese is melted. Top with Pace Picante
Sauce or Pace Thick & Chunky Salsa and garnish generously with any or
all of the Garnishes.
