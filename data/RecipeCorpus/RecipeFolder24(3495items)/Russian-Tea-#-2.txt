Title: Russian Tea # 2
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 1 batch
2 c  Sugar
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cloves
1 c  Instant tea
2 c  Tang instant orange drink
1    Envelope dry lemonade mix
Mix all ingredients thoroughly. Keep in sealed jar. Mix with hot or
cold water -- 3 to 4 teaspoon to a glass, more or less to taste.
