Title: Sweet Potato Croquettes (Fritters)
Categories: Jewish, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Hot Cream
1 pn Salt
Cracker Crumbs; powdered
2 c  Mashed Sweet Potatoes
3 ea Eggs
3/4 c  Pecans; chopped finely
Press potatoes through ricer. Add 2 well-beaten eggs, nuts cream and
salt. More or less cream may be needed, depending on the consistency
of the potatoes. Shape into flat rounds. Dip lightly in beaten egg,
then in crumbs. Fry in deep fat (375F). SERVES 6. From: The Complete
American-Jewish Cookbook By: Anne London and Bertha Kahn Bishov
