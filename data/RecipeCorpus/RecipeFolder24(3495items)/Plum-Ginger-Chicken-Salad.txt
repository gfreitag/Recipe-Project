Title: Plum Ginger Chicken Salad
Categories: Salad, Chicken, Poultry, Asian
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Oil, vegetable
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Ginger, ground
1 pk Rice mix, long grain & wild
2 c  Chicken breast, boneless;
-cooked and cubed
1/2 c  Green onions; sliced
4    Plums; sliced
Prepare rice mix as directed; cool to room temperature.  Combine rice,
chicken, green onions and sliced plums in large mixing bowl.  To
prepare dressing, combine oil, lemon juice, soy sauce and ginger;
pour over rice mixture and toss lightly. Chill until served.

SOURCE: Everything's Better with Rice, USA Rice Council pamphlet
