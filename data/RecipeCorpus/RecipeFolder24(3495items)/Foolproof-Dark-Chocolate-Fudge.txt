Title: Foolproof Dark Chocolate Fudge
Categories: Candy, Chocolate
Yield: 2 pounds
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
-to 1 cup
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
3 c  Semi-sweet chocolate chips
-18 ounces
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
ds Salt
In heavy saucepan, over low heat, melt chips with sweetened condensed
milk and salt.  Remove from heat; stir in nuts and vanilla.  Spread
evenly into wax paper-lined 8- or 9-inch square pan.

Chill 2 hours or until firm.  Turn fudge onto cutting board; peel off
paper and cut into squares.  Store loosely covered at room
temperature. Submitted By LAWRENCE KELLIE   On   10-08-94
