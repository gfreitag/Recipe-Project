Title: Turbot Crown and Mushroom Salad
Categories: Salad, Fish, Seafood, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: THE NATIONAL CULINAR---------------------
2 T  Red onions, fine diced
1 t  Chopped dill
1 t  Chopped parsley
1/3 c  Olive oil
Juice of 1 1/2 lemons
Salt and pepper to taste
Hartmut Handke
1 lb Turbot crown (the fringe on
-1 edge of the fillet),
Poached in fish stock
1/2 lb Button mushrooms, cut in
-quarters and poached
In salted water
Method: Prepare marinade with diced onions, chopped dill, chopped
parsley, olive oil, lemon juice and salt and pepper.  Fold turbot
crown and mushrooms into marinade very carefully to avoid breaking up
turbot crown. Let marinate 1 hour.  Spoon 6-ounce portions in middle
of the plates. Serve with some seasonal greens and oven-roasted Roma
tomatoes.
