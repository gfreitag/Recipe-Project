Title: Apricot Nectar Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1    Box - lemon supreme cake mix
1 c  Apricot nectar
3/4 c  Wesson oil
1 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
Combine all ingredients and bake in a round or square pan for 45
minutes at 350 degrees.  Mix 1 cup of powdered sugar and juice of 4
lemons and pour over cake while warm. Randy Rigg
