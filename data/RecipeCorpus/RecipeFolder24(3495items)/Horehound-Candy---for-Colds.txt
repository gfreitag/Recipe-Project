Title: Horehound Candy - for Colds
Categories: Candy, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Cream of tartar
2 c  Best New Orleans sugar
1/2 c  Horehound tea - STRONG
Boil until crisp in cold water. Pour into buttered plates and when
almost cold cut in squares. If white or light brown sugar is used,
recipe makes a very good candy and not so strong. Source: Unknown
