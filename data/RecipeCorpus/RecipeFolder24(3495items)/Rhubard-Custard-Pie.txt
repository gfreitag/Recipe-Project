Title: Rhubard Custard Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 3 servings
8 inch pie
2    Eggs
2 tb Milk
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb Flour
1/2 ts Nutmeg
3 c  Cut rhubarb
2 ts Butter
2    Pie crusts
9 inch pie
3    Eggs
3 tb Milk
2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Flour
3/4 ts Nutmeg
4 c  Cut rhubarb
1 tb Butter
2    Pie crusts
Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Beat eggs slightly; add milk.  Mix sugar,
flour, and nutmeg.  Stir into egg mixture.  Mix in rhubarb.  Pour
into pie shell and dot with butter.  Cover with lattice top.  Bake 50
to 60 minutes, until nicely browned.  Tastes best served slightly
warm. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   09-29-94
