Title: Holiday Relish
Categories: Condiment, Fruit, Holiday, Nuts/grains, Relish
Yield: 1 batch
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 ts Red food coloring (opt'l.)
2 3/4 c  Sugar [I find this to be too
-much, we like only 1 cup]
1 pk Whole cranberries
2    Apples; unpeeled
2    Oranges; unpeeled
1 sm Can crushed pineapple
Place cranberries and 2 cups of water in blender.  Chop finely and
drain. Place cored and quartered apples (unpeeled) and 2 cups of
water in blender. Chop finely and drain.  Quarter oranges (unpeeled),
remove seeds, and chop finely in blender.  Do not add water, use only
juice from oranges. Mix all ingredients in a glass bowl and
refrigerate overnight to enhance flavor.

Yield:  Approximately 1 1/2 qts.
