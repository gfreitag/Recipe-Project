Title: 60 Minute Oven Stew
Categories: Main dish, Beef
Yield: 6 servings
-small wedges
1 cn Beef broth
1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Broiled steak seasoning
1/2 ts Thyme leaves
1/4 ts Black pepper
1 lb Steak, sirloin; uncooked,
-trimmed of fat and cut into
-thin strips
6 sm Potatoes, red; quartered
1/2 lb Green beans, fresh; trimmed
6    Carrots, peeled and sliced
1 sm Onion, yellow; cut into
Combine first 5 ingredients in a 13x9x2" baking dish.

Im a medium bowl, combine remaining ingredients with a whisk.  Pour
over meat and vegetables.

Cover baking dish with aluminum foil.  Bake in preheated 350 degree
oven for 40 minutes.  Carefully uncover and stir meat and vegetables.
Cover and bake an additional 20 minutes.  Uncover and stir stew
before serving.
