Title: Eggplant Spread
Categories: Appetizer, Caribbean, Spread, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Vinegar
2 tb Oil
Water
Salt & pepper
1 ea Eggplant, sliced
1 ts Onion, grated
1 ea Garlic clove, crushed
1 ea Tomato, finely chopped
1/2 ts Sugar
Start by cooking the eggplant slices in boiling, salted water for a
few minutes until tender.  Drain & put in a bowl.  Then mash them.

Add the onion, garlic, tomato, sugar, vinegar, oil, salt & pepper.
Beat to make a creamy dip consistency.  Chill & then serve with lemon
wedges.

Troth Wells, "The World in Your Kitchen"
