Title: Tomato and Fennel Soup - Country Living
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 cups
-chopped
1 sm Onion, chopped
1    Clove garlic, chopped
1 T  Butter (opt.)
1/4 t  Salt
1/4 t  Ground black pepper
2 c  Water
1 md Carrot, thinly sliced
1 c  Chopped fennel bulb
1    Chicken or vegetarian
-bouillon cube
3 T  Long-grain white rice
6 md Ripe tomatoes, seeded and
1. In 2-quart microwave-safe bowl, combine water, carrot, fennel,
bouillon, and rice. Cover and microwave on high (100 percent) 5
minutes.

2. Stir tomatoes and onion into carrot mixture; cover and microwave
on high 6 minutes. Add garlic, stir well, cover, and microwave on
high 5 to 6 minutes longer or until rice is very tender. Stir
in-butter, if desired, and salt and pepper. Set aside 10 minutes.

3. In batches, puree soup mixture in blender until very smooth. Serve.
