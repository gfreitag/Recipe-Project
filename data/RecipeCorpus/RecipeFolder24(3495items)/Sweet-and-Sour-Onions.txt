Title: Sweet and Sour Onions
Categories: Diabetic, Side dish, Casserole, Vegetable
Yield: 4 - 6 folks
1/4 c  Margarine; melted
1/4 c  Boiling water;
1/4 c  Sugar;
(Would use sugar substitute)
4 lg Onion; sliced
1/4 c  Cider vinegar;
1/4 c  Butter; melted
-=OR=-
Slice the onions and arrange in a 1 quart baking dish.  Mix the rest
of the ingredients and pour over the onions.  Bake at 300 degrees for
1 hour. Yield: 4 - 6 servings

Food Exchange per serving: 1 VEGETABLES EXCHANGES + 1 FAT EXCHANGE (I
would cut down the margarine to make it, if even that, lower then the
1 fat exchange to 1/2 fat. I WILL use SUGAR substitute, yes I would.)

Source: Southern Living, Our Best Recipes by Lena Sturges Brought to
you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master
