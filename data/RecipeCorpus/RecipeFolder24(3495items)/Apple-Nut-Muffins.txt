Title: Apple Nut Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Fruit, Bread, Apples
Yield: 10 servings
1/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 ea Tb Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Cinnamon sugar
1 ea Chopped apple
1 ea Egg
2/3 c  Apple juice or milk
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1 t  Vanilla
2 c  Flour
1/4 c  Sugar
In bowl, beat egg with juice, oil and vanilla.  Stir in flour, sugars,
baking powder, and salt just until flour is moistened. (will be
lumpy) Stir in nuts and apple. Fill prepared muffin tins. (greased or
paper lined.) Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.  Bake at 400 F for 20
minutes or until golden brown.   From Domino Back of the Box. Posted
