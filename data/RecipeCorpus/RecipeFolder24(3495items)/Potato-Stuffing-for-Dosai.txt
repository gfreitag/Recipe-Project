Title: Potato Stuffing for Dosai
Categories: Indian, Vegetable, Stuffing, Potato
Yield: 4 servings
1 md Onion
1/4 ts Mustard seeds,black
Salt & pepper to taste
250 g  Potatoes
1/4 ts Turmeric
1/4 ts Chilli flakes
30 g  Ghee
Peel and dice the potatoes, then boil. Chop the onion finely. Heat
the ghee in fry pan and fry the mustard seeds, add onions, and fry
til almost done. Add potatoes, turmeric, chilli, salt and pepper.
Cook for 5 mins till dry. Put a spoonful of this mixture in each
dosai and serve.
