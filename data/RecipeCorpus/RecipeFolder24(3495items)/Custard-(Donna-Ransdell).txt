Title: Custard (Donna Ransdell)
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1 ga Milk
8 ea Egg yolks
4 t  Cornstarch
Salt
5 ea Or 6 Tbsp vanilla
This is one of my husband,
-Warren's, favorite
-desserts. My mother
Always made it when I was
-growing up.
Cook milk, yolks, cornstarch, salt, and vanilla over hot water in a
double boiler it until coats a spoon.  Cool.  Chill in refrigerator.
It's good if put in freezer until it begins to freeze. Originally
from Mee-Maw, but Marilla gave me the recipe
