Title: Italian Baked Spaghetti Squash
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Plus
1 ts Parmesan cheese
2 c  Cooked, drained spaghetti
-squash
1    8 oz. can tomato sauce
1/2 ts Dried oregano
Preheat oven to 375. Place squash in a casserole that has been
sprayed with a nonstick cooking spray. Combine tomato sauce, oregano,
and garlic powder and pour evenly over squash. Sprinkle with parmesan
cheese. Bake 20 minutes or until hot and bubbly.

Per serving: 2g protein, 1g fat, 9g carb., 330mg sodium, 1mg chol., 47
calories.
