Title: Hot Spice Tea Mix
Categories: Beverage, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 batch
1/4 ts Ground allspice
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/3 c  Instant Tea powder
1 c  Tang
1/3 c  Sugar (I use less)
Blend well store in air tight container.  Use 1-1/2 tsp. or more per
cup of boiling water. Jason Leto's MOM
