Title: Stir Fry Linguine
Categories: Pasta, Beef, Cheese/eggs, Stir fry, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
12 ea Mushrooms, quartered
14 oz Spagetti sauce (canned or
-homemade)
1 c  Grated mozzarella cheese
1 lb Hot cooked linguine, drained
1 ea Onion, coarsely chopped
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
1 T  Olive oil
1 lb Sirloin stead, cut in very
-thin strips
1 ea Green pepper, julienned
Stir fry onion and garlic in oil.  Add beef.  Continue to stir fry
until beef is no longer pink.  Add vegetables, stri fry for 1
minutes.  Pour in sauce and heat through.  Toss sauce with pasta and
3/4 cup mozzarella. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top.  Makes 4
servings. Origin: Kraft's What's Cooking magazine, 9th issue. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
