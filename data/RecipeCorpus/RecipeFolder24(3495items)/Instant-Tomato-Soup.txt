Title: Instant Tomato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 1 servings
1/16 t  Salt
ds Pepper
4 T  Non-fat dry milk
2 T  Powdered dry tomatoes
1/8 t  Basil
Combine all ingredients and seal in small plastic bags using a vacuum
sealer, such as Decosonic Pak-n-Save. To use: Open package and place
in a bowl or mug. Add 8 ounces boiling water, stirring constantly.
Note: If you dry your own tomatoes, dry until just brittle. Powder in
a blender. Dried tomato can also be found in health food stores. If
using dried tomato slices powder in blender. SOURCE:* Gwynne Bodle
