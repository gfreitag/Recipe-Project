Title: Pamela Peters' Chili
Categories: Chili, Main dish, Beef, Beans
Yield: 8 -10
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: THE NATIONAL CULINAR---------------------
1 T  Cumin
1 t  Freshly ground black pepper
1 t  Salt
1/2 t  Crushed red pepper
2 lb Fresh tomatoes, peeled,
-seeded and chopped
1 ea (15-ounce) can tomato puree
8 ea Ounces black beans, cooked
1/4 bn Fresh cilantro, chopped
Juice from 1 whole lime
1 pt Sour cream
8 ea Ounces Monterey Jack
-cheese, shredded
Written by Linda Beaulieu
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
2 T  Olive oil
2 ea Ounces jalapeno peppers,
-cut into 1/4-inch dice
6 ea Ounces red pepper, cut into
-1/4-inch dice
6 ea Ounces yellow pepper, cut
-into 1/4-inch dice
8 ea Ounces onion, cut into
-1/4-inch dice
4 ea Cloves garlic minced
2 T  Chili powder
1/2 T  Oregano
Method: Brown the ground beef over medium heat.  Drain excess fat and
reserve beef.  In the olive oil, saute the peppers and onion until
translucent.  Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.  Add seasonings and
cook for another couple of minutes.  Add tomatoes and tomato puree.
Cook for 30 to 45 minutes.  Add cooked black beans and cilantro;
simmer an additional 10 to 15 minutes. Serve with generous amounts of
sour cream combined with lime juice. Garnish with Monterey Jack
cheese and sprigs of cilantro.
