Title: Slow-Cooked Pepper Steak
Categories: Beef, Crockpot, Main dish, Pasta
Yield: 1 servings
4    Tomatoes, cut into eighths
-OR
16 oz Can tomatoes w/ liquid, cut
-up
2 lg Green peppers, cut into
-strips
1/2 c  Cold water
1 tb Cornstarch
Cooked noodles or rice
1 1/2 lb Beef round steak
2 tb Cooking oil
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1 c  Chopped onion
1    Clove garlic, minced
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Ground ginger
"After a long day....I appreciate coming in to this hearty beef dish
for supper." - Sue Gronholz

Cut beef into 3x1" strips; brown in oil in skillet. Transfer to a slow
cooker.  Combine the next seven ingredients; pour over beef. Cover
and cook on low for 5-6 hours or until meat is tender. Add tomatoes
and green peppers; cook on low for 1 hour longer.  Combine the cold
water and cornstarch to make a paste; stir into liquid in slow cooker
and cook on high until thickened.  Serve over noodles or rice.

Serves:  6-8 From: "Taste of Home" Magazine Posted by: Debbie Carlson
