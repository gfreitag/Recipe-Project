Title: Peach Sorbet
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Sugar
5 tb Amaretto
4 c  Peeled, pitted and coarsely
-chopped frsh peaches
3/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
In a blender or food processor, puree the peaches. Add the remaining
ingredients, pulse to combine, and refrigerate until very cold. Place
the mixture in an ice crem machine and freeze according to
manufacturers directions.

Per serving: 164 calories, 40g carb., .7g protein, 0mg chol., 0g/0%
fat.
