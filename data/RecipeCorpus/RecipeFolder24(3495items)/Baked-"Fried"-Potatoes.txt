Title: Baked "Fried" Potatoes
Categories: Vegetable, Potato
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
4 lg Potatoes, baking
2 tb Olive oil
Scrub 2 large (8-10 oz) baking potatoes. Cut each in half lengthwise.
Cut each half into 4 spears.

Place potatoes in large pot of lightly salted boiling water, bring
water back to boil and boil 3 minutes. Drain in colander.

Spray cooking sheet with cooking spray.

Mix 1 Tablespoon olive oil and potatoes in bowl.

Mix 1/2 teaspoon paprika and 1/4 teaspoon salt together, toss over
potatoes.  Mix well.

Bake in preheated 400 oven for 15 minutes or until tender.

Place in paper towel lined basket or bowl.

Toss gently with 1 Tablespoon graded Parmesan Cheese

Serve hot.

Makes 2 servings Can add other spices to salt mixture for different
flavors.
