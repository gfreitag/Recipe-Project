Title: Golden Shrimp
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Cheese/eggs, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 6 servings
2    Eggs; well beaten
1 2/3 c  Milk, evaporated; undiluted
1 1/2 lb Shrimp; shelled and deveined
1 1/2 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
MMMMM-------------------3-MINUTE CHEESE SAUCE------------------------
2 c  Cheese, American process;
-shredded
1 2/3 c  Milk, evaporate; undiluted
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Mustard, dry
Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt.  Add eggs and evaporated
milk.  Blend.  Dip shrimp in batter and cook in hot shortening (375
degrees) for 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly  browned. Drain on
absorbent paper.  Serve with 3-Minute Cheese Sauce.

3-Minute Cheese Sauce: Simmer evaporated milk, salt, and mustard in
saucepan over medium heat to just below boiling (about 2 minutes). Add
cheese; stir over medium heat until cheese melts (about 1 minute
longer). Yield: 2-1/2 cups.
