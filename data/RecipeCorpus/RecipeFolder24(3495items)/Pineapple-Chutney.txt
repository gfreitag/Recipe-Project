Title: Pineapple Chutney
Categories: Can/preserv, Relish, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Ground cloves
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Garlic salt
1/4    To 1/2 tsp salt, to taste
1/8    To 1/4 tsp black pepper, to
-taste
1 c  Cider vinegar
29 oz Crushed pineapple,
-undrained
2    To 2-1/2 cups brown sugar,
-to taste
1 c  Seedless raisins
1 c  Chopped dates
1 T  Onion flakes
Combine all ingredients and mix well.  Cook and stir over low heat
for 1 to 1-1/2 hours or until very thick.  Cool.  Pour into clean
glass jars with covers.  Store in refrigerator.  Makes 4 cups, 1 tbsp
per serving.

Will keep in refrigerator up to 4 weeks.  SErve as an accompaniment
with hot or cold meats such as ham, chicken, or turkey.  Also good as
an accompaniment to spicy foods, such as curries.
