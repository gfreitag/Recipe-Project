Title: Kahlua Mudslide Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 servings
1 1/2 c  Granulated sugar
4 T  KAHLUA
2 T  Irish cream liqueur
1 T  Vodka
3/4 c  Coarsely chopped walnuts
Whole coffee beans (opt)
2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
2/3 c  Butter
4    Squares unsweetened
-chocolate, chopped (1oz ea)
3    Eggs
MMMMM------------------------KAHLUA GLAZE-----------------------------
3 T  KHALUA
1 1/4 c  Powdered sugar
Combine flour, baking powder and salt in small bowl. Melt butter and
chocolate in small saucepan over low heat; set aside. Beat eggs and
granulated sugar in large bowl until light. Beat in flour mixture,
chocolate mixture, 4 tablespoons Kahlua, Irish cream and vodka. Fold
in walnuts. Pour into greased 13x9" baking pan.

Bake in 350'F. oven just until toothpick inserted into center comes
out clean, about 25 minutes. DO NOT OVERBAKE. Cool in pan on wire
rack. Spread with Kahlua Glaze. Decorate with whole coffee beans, if
desired. Cut into squares.

Makes 24 brownies.
