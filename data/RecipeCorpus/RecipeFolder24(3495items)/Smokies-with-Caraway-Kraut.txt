Title: Smokies with Caraway Kraut
Categories: Main dish, Sausage
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Caraway seeds
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 lb Sausage, smoked links; cut
-diagonally into thirds
3 c  Onion; chopped
1    Garlic clove; minced
2 tb Shortening; melted
16 oz Sauerkraut
1 tb Paprika
2 ts Sugar
Saute onions and garlic in shortening.  Add remaining ingredients
except sausage; cover and simmer over low heat 20 minutes.  Add
sausage; cover and simmer 10 minutes.
