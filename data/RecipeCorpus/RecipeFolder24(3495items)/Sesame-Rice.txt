Title: Sesame Rice
Categories: Side dish, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
1 md Tomato; seeded and finely
-chopped
1/2 c  Green onions; sliced
2 tb Parsley, chopped;or cilantro
2 tb Sesame seeds; toasted
1 sm Onion; chopped
1    Garlic clove; minced
1 tb Oil, sesame or veg.
3 c  Rice; hot, cooked (cooked in
-chicken broth)
Cook onion and garlic  in oil in large skillet over medium heat until
tender.  Stir in rice, tomato, green onions and parsley; heat
thoroughly. Garnish with sesame seeds.

SOURCE: Everything's Better with Rice, USA Rice Council pamphlet.
