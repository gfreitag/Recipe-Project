Title: Caramel Icing
Categories: Cake/cookie, British
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  White sugar
1 c  Cream
2 c  Brown sugar
1 T  White syrup
1 t  Vanilla
Cook together all ingredients except vanilla until mixture forms soft
ball when dropped into cold water. Add vanilla and stir until desired
consistency to spread.

Mrs. Charles E. Phillips
