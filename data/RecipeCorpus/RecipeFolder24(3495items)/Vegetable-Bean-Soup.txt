Title: Vegetable Bean Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Beans, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Frozen soup vegetables
1/2 ts Celery seed
1 tb Dried onion flakes
1 cn Chick peas (drained)
1 ds Tabasco (or to taste)
Pepper/Salt to taste
1 pk Knoor Vegetable Soup Mix
4 1/2 c  Water
1    11.5 oz can vegetable juice
1    Vegetarian Veg. Bouillon
Cube (OR 2 beef bouillon
Cubes)
1/3 c  Pasta Flakes *
*Pasta Flakes can be found in Kosher section of grocery store.  They
are little squares of pasta.

In 3 quart sauce pan, add water, vegetable juice, soup mix, and
bouillon cube, bring to boil.  Add pasta flakes, dried onions, celery
seed, and frozen vegetables, lower heat and simmer slowly for 10
minutes. Add drained chick peas and tabasco, pepper and salt to
taste, heat and serve.

For non vegetarian soup you can add left over beef or chicken if
desired.

NOTE:   If pasta flakes are not available, use 1/2 leftover rice, or
1/2 cup macaroni (uncooked) cook according to package
:
directions
in soup.
