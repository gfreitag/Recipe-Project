Title: Pork-Rice Dish
Categories: Main dish, Pork, Rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Soup, tomato
1 c  Water
1 c  Rice, regular; uncooked
4    Pork chops
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
Sprinkle dry rice in 13x9x2" pan.  Arrange pork chops over rice.
Sprinkle with chopped onion.  Combine soup and water and pour over
mixture. Lift pork chops so some of liquid settles on bottom of pan.
Bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour.
