Title: Celeste's Salsa
Categories: Appetizer, Condiment, Dressing
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Wine vinegar
1 ts Mustard seed
1 ts Coriander; ground
1 ds Black pepper; coarse
4    Tomato; chopped fine
1/2    Onion; chopped fine
1/2    Celery; chopped fine
1/2    Green bell pepper; chopped f
3 tb Green chiles; chopped fine
Recipe by: Celeste Walther Preparation Time: 0:15 Finely chop all
vegetables. Mix well. Combine vinegar, mustard seed, coriander, and
pepper. Pour over vegetables and mix. Chill. Store tightly covered in
refrigerator. Keeps well.
