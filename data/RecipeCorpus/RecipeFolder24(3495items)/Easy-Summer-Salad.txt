Title: Easy Summer Salad
Categories: Salad, Seafood, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
1    Bottle buttermilk ranch
-salad dressing (8 oz)
1 pk Frozen peas, thawed (10 oz)
1 pk Wlbow macaroni (7-8 oz)
1 pk Louis Kemp Crab Delights
-flakes or chunks (8 oz)
Prepare macaroni according to package directions. Rinse with cold
water to cool. Combine macaroni with remaining ingredients.

370 calories per serving.
