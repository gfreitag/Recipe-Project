Title: Fire Station Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Beef, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Onion soup mix
2    Cans Campbell's Beef Broth
10 1/2 oz Can cream of mushroom soup
Mix ingredients and heat.  Serve with country fried steak, roast, pork
chops or hamburgers.
