Title: Spiced Tomato Soup
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Soup/stew, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 6 servings
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tb Sugar
3 tb Flour
6 c  Chopped, peeled tomatoes
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1 tb Mixed pickling spices
2 tb Margarine
In blender, blend tomatoes and onions until smooth. Strain into
saucepan. Place pickling spices in a tea ball or cheesecloth. Add to
saucepan with margarine, salt, pepper, and sugar. In a small bowl,
stir flour into a small amount of soup mixture until blended. Add to
soup. Cook, uncovered, over low heat 30 minutes, stirring frequently.

Per serving: 2g protein, 4g fat, 14g carb., 59mg sodium, 0mg chol., 95
calories.
