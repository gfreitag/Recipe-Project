Title: Cranberry Raisin Relish
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Relish, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Cranberries
1 ts Grated orange peel
3/4 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Unsweetened orange juice
1/3 c  Water
1/4 c  Sugar
in a small saucepan, combine raisins, orange juice, water, sugar, and
lemon juice. Bring to a boil over medium high heat. Reduce heat to
low, simmer 5 minutes. Add cranberries and orange peel, let cook
until cranberries pop. Then simmer, stirrine frequently, 20 minutes.
Chill

Per serving: 1g protein, 0g fat, 21g carb., 2mg sodium, 0mgchol., 79
calories.
