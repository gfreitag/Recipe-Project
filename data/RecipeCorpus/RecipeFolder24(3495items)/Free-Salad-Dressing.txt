Title: Free Salad Dressing
Categories: Diabetic, Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Onion; chopped finely
Salt and pepper;
1/2 c  Tomato Juice;
2 tb Lemon juice;
1/2 ts Horseradish;
Combine ingredients.  Refriegerate.  Shake well berfore serving. Food
Exchange per serving: FREE FOOD

yours by Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master
