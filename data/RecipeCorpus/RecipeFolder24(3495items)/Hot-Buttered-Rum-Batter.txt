Title: Hot Buttered Rum Batter
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 t  Ground cloves
Boiling water
Rum extract
Nutmeg, optional
Cinnamon sticks
1 c  Butter, room temperature
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Honey
2 t  Cinnamon
1 t  Nutmeg
In a small mixer bowl, cream butter until fluffy.  Gradually add brown
sugar, then honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves; beating well.  Spoon
into covered containers.  Keep refrigerated.  Makes 2 cups, 8
servings. DIRECTIONS:  Store in refrigerator up to 1 month.  To
serve; Bring batter to room temperature.  Place 1/4 cup of batter in
a warm 10 ounce mug. Pour about 1/2 cup boiling water into mug and
stir to dissolve batter. Add a few drops of rum extract, to taste,
and fill with boiling water; stir. Sprinkle with nutmeg, if desired.
Stir with cinnamon stick. Origin: Watkins Food Gifts Cookbook. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
