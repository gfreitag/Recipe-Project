Title: Sesame Turkey
Categories: Turkey, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
3 tb Oil, peanut
1    Garlic cloves, crushed
1/2 tb Oil, sesame
2 tb Soy sance
4 sl Turkey breast, cooked
3 tb Oil, sesame
1 c  Sesame seeds
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Black pepper
Brush turkey to taste with sesame oil and dredge in mixture of sesame
seeds, salt, black pepper and cayenne pepper.  In skillet, saute
slices in heated peanut oil until brown on both sides.

Remove to platter.  Deglaze skillet with garlic, sesame oil, and soy
sauce. Pour sauce over turkey.
