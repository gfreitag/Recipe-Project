Title: Top N Bottom Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
2 c  Old cheddar cheese, shredded
Reserve 6 flowerettes for
-garnish.
1 lg Broccoli, chopped
1 lg Carrot, diced
10 oz Chicken broth
10 oz Water
Cook vegetable in broth and water until tender.  Let cool 10 minutes.
POur into blender. Process until smooth; return to saucpan.  Whisk in
salad dressing and cheese.  heat over low heat to melt cheese.  Do
NOT BOIL. Garnish with broccoli.  Makes 6 servings. Origin: Kraft
What's Cooking magazine, 9th issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
