Title: Squirrel Jambalaya
Categories: Game, Cajun/creol, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/4 ea Green pepper, chopped
4 T  Parsley, chopped
2 c  Uncooked rice, washed
1 1/2 c  Water
2 t  Salt
1 md Squirrel
Salt and cayenne pepper
2 lg Onions, chopped
3 ea Celery stalks, chopped
1 ea Garlic clove, chipped
3 T  Oil
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Cut squirrel into serving pieces and season well.  Saute in oil until
brown; remove from skillet.  Saute onions, celery, garlic, green
pepper, and parsley in oil until wilted.  Put squirrel back into
skillet; cover. Cook slowly about 30 minutes, or until squirrel is
tender. Add rice and water.  Stir thoroughly.  Add salt; cook slowly
about 30 minutes or until rice is cooked. Serves 4. From the Official
Louisiana Seafood & Wild Game Cookbook Published by Louisiana
department of Wildlife and fisheries.
