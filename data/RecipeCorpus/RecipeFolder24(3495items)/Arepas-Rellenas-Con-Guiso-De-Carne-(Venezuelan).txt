Title: Arepas Rellenas Con Guiso De Carne (Venezuelan)
Categories: Beef, Ground beef, Pork, Bread, Sandwich
Yield: 10 sandwiches
1/2 ts Achiote powder
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Thyme leaves
1/4 ts Black pepper
1 ts Salt
8    Pimento-stuffed olives
-- sliced
1 tb Capers
1    Recipe basic arepa dough
2 tb Oil
1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 lb Ground pork
1 md Onion; chopped
1    Garlic clove; minced
1 sm Green chile; chopped
2 lg Tomatoes; peeled
-- seeded and chopped
*Stuffed Arepas from Venezuela.

Heat oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat.  Add meat, onions,
garlic, green chile, tomato, achiote, cumin, thyme, black pepper and
salt, and cook, stirring occasionally, for 20 minutes, or until
tomatoes have formed a thick sauce. Stir in olives and capers.

Prepare a recipe of basic arepas.  Shape into 3" discs (3/4" thick)
and cook. When done, split in half, fill with meat sauce, and serve
hot.

Yield: 10 sandwiches.
