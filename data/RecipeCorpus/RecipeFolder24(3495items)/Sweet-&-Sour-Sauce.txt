Title: Sweet & Sour Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chinese, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 T  Marmalade or pineapple
-chunks
1 ea Carrot; minced
1/2 c  Cucumber; finely minced
1/2 c  Onion; finely minced
1 c  ;Water, cold
2 T  Catsup
2 T  Rice vinegar
2 T  Cornstarch
2 T  Sugar
Combine all ingredients and heat to simmering.  Cool and store in
refrigerator. Joe Waring
