Title: Chickenless Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Diced fresh mushrooms
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
Onion salt to taste
Unbleached all-purpose
2 c  Boiling water
2 T  Vegetable oil
3 T  Nutritional yeast
1    Vegetable bouillon cube
flou

In a large saucepan, simmer all ingredients except the flour for
approximately 5 minutes. Slowly add the flour (tablespoon by
tablespoon), wisking after each addition, until desired thickness is
reached. Keep warm. Submitted By CAROLYN DOSS   On   10-05-94
