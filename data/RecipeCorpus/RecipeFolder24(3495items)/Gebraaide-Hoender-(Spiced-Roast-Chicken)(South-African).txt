Title: Gebraaide Hoender (Spiced Roast Chicken)(South African)
Categories: Chicken, Poultry, African, Main dish, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1/8 t  Each of:
-turmeric
-fenugreek
-cumin
-fennel
-allspice
1    Chicken, cut up
-Salt
1 c  Water
1 lb Potatoes, diced
-Oil
1    Onion, chopped finely
-Pinch ginger
Rub each piece of chicken with salt and place the pieces in a skillet
with 1 cup of water. Cover and simmer gently. After 15 minutes, add
the potatoes, a little oil if necessary, and the onion. Add also the
spices and the ginger and garlic. Now simmer for an additional 30
minutes. Serve with rice or vegetables. Serves 4.
Date: 09-26-94
From: Earl Shelsby
