Title: Gingered Chicken Nuggets
Categories: Poultry, Asian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
8    Green onions
4 ts Soya sauce
4 tb Sherry
3 tb Ginger, grated
4    Chicken breasts, skinned,
Boned, cubed, 1 inch
2 tb Oil
2 ts Oil,sesame
brown chicken in oils, saute until golden. cut green onions, cut into
rings, add to chicken with soy sauce and sherry. cook 1-2 minutes.
grate ginger and add. cook 2 minutes, to soften ginger slightly.
