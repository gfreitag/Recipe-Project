Title: Zucchini-Mozzarella Casserole
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/4 ts Dried basil
Salt and pepper to taste
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
6 oz Shredded mozzarella
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 tb Plus
1 ts -margarine
4 c  Thinly sliced zucchini
1/2 c  Chopped onions
2 tb Parsley flakes
1/8 ts Garlic powder
Melt margarine in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add
zucchini and onions and cook 10 minutes, stirring frequently until
vegetables are tender crisp. Remove from heat. Preheat oven to 350.
Add seasonings to zucchini mixture. In a large bowl, combine eggs,
cheese, and mustard. Add zucchini mixture and stir until combined.
Pour into a casserole dish that has been sprayed with a nonstick
cooking spray. Bake 35 minutes, or until lightly browned.

Per serving: 13g protein, 16g fat, 9g carb., 282mg sodium, 170mg
chol., 227 calories.
