Title: Seafood Salad
Categories: Diabetic, Salad, Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Almonds; chopped
1 c  Low-fat cottage cheese;
2 c  Lettuce; torn up
1    Tomatoe; cut in wedges
1 ts Parsley sprigs; chopped fine
1/2 lb Deveined shrimp; shelled,
-cooked chilled, and cut in
-2" pieces
1 tb Green onion; minced
3 tb Diet salad dressing;
3 tb Diet french dressing;
Mix first six ingredients.  Divide evenly on lettuce, garnish with
tomato and parsley. Food Exchange per serving: 2 FAT EXCHANGES + 2
LEAN MEAT EXCHANGES CAL: 280;

Source: Recipes for Diabetics by Billy Little(version 1985) Brought
to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
