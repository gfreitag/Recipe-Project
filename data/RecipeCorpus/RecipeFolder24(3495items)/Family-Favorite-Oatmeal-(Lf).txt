Title: Family-Favorite Oatmeal (Lf)
Categories: Diabetic, Low-cal/fat, Breakfast, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 md To large apple; at room temp
-cut into small chunks
1/3 c  Raisins;
2 tb Walnuts; or pecans, chopped
3 c  Hot cooked oatmeal;
1/4 c  Light brown sugar;
Brown sugar sub equal to
-1/4 cup
1 ts Cinnamon;
MMMMM------------------------PER SERVING-----------------------------
4 x  *gm Fiber
7 x  *mg sodium
2 7/8 x  *gm Total Fat
3/8 x  *gm Sat Fat
171 x  *Cals
Stir brown sugar and cinnamon into cooked oatmeal. Add apple chunks,
raisins, and chopped nuts.  Serve hot.

From One Meal At A Time by Martin Katahn
