Title: Peppermint Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 bn Peppermintleaves, fresh and
Finely chopped.
1 tb Sugar
1 pn Salt
1 1/16 c  Lemonjuice
1 .Mix salt, lemonjuice and sugar in pot, bring to a boil. 2. Add
peppermint, let cool, thin with a little water if nessesary.
