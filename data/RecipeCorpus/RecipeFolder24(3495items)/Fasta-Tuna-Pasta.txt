Title: Fasta Tuna Pasta
Categories: Pasta, Vegetable, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
6 1/2 oz Can tuna
1 pk Kraft dinner
1 c  Forzen peas
Cook Kraft Diner pasta according to package directions.  Drain pasta
over 1 cup frozen peas in colander.  Return to pot.  Add milk, butter
and cheese sauce packet as directed on package.  Add 1 can drained,
chunk tuna. Heat thoroughly.  Makes 4 servings. Origin: What's
Cooking Magazine, from Kraft Kitchens, 9th issue. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Oct/94.
