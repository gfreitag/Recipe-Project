Title: Three Bean Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable, Beans
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Salad oil
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 cn 16 oz cut green beans
1 cn 16 oz dark red kidney beans
1 cn 16 oz yellow wax beans
1 c  Diced celery
1/2 c  Minced onion
1/2 c  Minced green bell pepper
Drain beans, place in mixing bowl.  Add green bell pepper, celery and
onion.  Mix oil and vinegar with sugar, salt and pepper.  Pour over
bean mixture. Mix together. Transfer to large non metalic container.
Cover and refrigerate overnight or longer for flavors to blend.
Occasionally stir the mixture.

Serve with slotted spoon.
