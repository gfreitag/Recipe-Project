Title: Spam Mexican Extravaganza
Categories: Salad, Spam, Tex/mexican, Vegetable, Pasta
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Chopped tomato
1 cn Sliced ripe olives, drained
-(2 1/4 oz)
1 cn CHI-CHI's Salsa (8 oz)
3/4 c  Sour cream
1 tb Mayonnaise or salad dressing
4 c  Shredded lettuce
Tortilla chips
1 c  Rainbow rotini
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(12 oz)
1 cn Pinto beans, drained (15 oz)
1 c  Frozen corn, thawed
3/4 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1    Green bell pepper, chopped
1/4 c  Chopped onion
Cook rotini according to package directions; chill. In skillet, saute
SPAM over medium heat until lightly browned; cool. In large bowl,
combine rotini, SPAM, beans, corn, cheese, green pepper, onion,
tomatoes, and olives. In small bowl, combine salsa, sour cream and
mayonnaise. Toss together salsa mixture and SPAM mixture. Cover and
chill several hours. Spoon salad mixture over lettuce. Serve with
chips.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 403; Protein 20 grams;
Carbohydrate 31 grams; Fat 23 grams; Cholesterol 86 milligrams;
Sodium 1053 milligrams.
