Title: Walnut and Sweet Potato Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Rice
Yield: 2 servings
6 ea Green onions, chopped
1/2 c  Walnuts, chopped finely, in
-blender
1 c  Water or stock
4 c  Cooked brown rice
1 ea Sweet potato thickly peeled
2 ea Eggs
3-4 garlic cloves finely
-chopped
Puree sweet potato with water or stock in blender.  Add more water or
stock if necessary.  Add eggs and blend briefly.  Combine all
ingredients and bake for 30-45 minutes at 350F. Origin: Yeast Free
Feast. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
