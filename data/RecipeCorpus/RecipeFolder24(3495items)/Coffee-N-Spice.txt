Title: Coffee N Spice
Categories: Beverage, Herbs/spice
Yield: 2 servings
No Ingredients Found
Fill automatic coffee maker with 6 cups cold water.  Add 3/4 cup
coffee to brew basket.  Top with 3-4 cinnamon sticks, broken in half
and the peel from one large orange.  Brew as usual. Origin: Kraft
What's Cooking magazine, 9th issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
Submitted By SHARON STEVENS   On   10-02-94
