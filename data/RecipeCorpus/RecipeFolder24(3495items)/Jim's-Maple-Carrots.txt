Title: Jim's Maple Carrots
Categories: Syrup, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
Salt to taste
Paprika to taste
1 lb Baby Carrots
1/4 c  Pure maple syrup
3 T  Butter
Steam carrots until tender. Melt butter in a cast iron skillet. Add
carrots and maple syrup. Sprinkle salt and paprika over carrots. Cook
and stir until carrots are brown and glazed. Serves 2. Note: This
freezes well for later use. SOURCE:* Jim Bodle POSTED BY: Jim Bodle
