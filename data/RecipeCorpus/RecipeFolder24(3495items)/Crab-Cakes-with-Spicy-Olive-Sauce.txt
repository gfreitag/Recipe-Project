Title: Crab Cakes with Spicy Olive Sauce
Categories: Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 4 servings
-crumbs
2    Eggs
1 T  Butter or margarine
1 T  Vegetable oil
1/2 lb Fresh crabmeat, lumps broken
-up
2 T  Worcestershire sauce
6 T  Minced fresh parsley
1/2 c  Packaged fine dry bread
MMMMM---------------------SPICY OLIVE SAUCE--------------------------
-ripe olives
2 t  Louisiana hot sauce to taste
1 cn Unsalted tomato sauce
1/2 c  Slivered pitted California
Mix crab with Worcestershire sauce, parsley, bread crumbs and eggs.
Shape into 8 small cakes. Prepare sauce. Saute cakes in butter and
oil over medium-low heat until golden, turning once, about 2 minutes
on each side. Serve with sauce.

Spicy Olive Sauce: Combine tomato sauce, olives and hot sauce in
saucepan. Heat.

Cooking time: 4 minutes.

Total preparation time: 20 minutes.
Date: 09-26-94
