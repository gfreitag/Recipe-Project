Title: Oriental Poultry Patties
Categories: Poultry, Asian, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1    Ginger slice, 1 inch
12 oz Ground chicken or turkey
Pepper to taste
Cilantro to garnish
2 ts Sesame paste
1 ts Sesame oil
2 ts Soy sauce
1/2 cn Water chestnuts
Turn on broiler. Process sesame paste, oil, soy sauce to make smooth
paste. Add chestnuts,ginger and continue to process. Combine ground
poultry with sesame paste mixture, garlic and pepper, and shape into
4 patties. Broil 2 inches from source of heat for about 8 minutes.
Chop cilantro and garnish patties.
