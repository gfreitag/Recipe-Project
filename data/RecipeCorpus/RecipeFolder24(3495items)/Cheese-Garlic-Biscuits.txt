Title: Cheese Garlic Biscuits
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Dried parsley
2 c  Bisquick baking mix
2/3 c  Milk
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
Mix baking mix, milk, parsley, and cheese until soft dough forms. Beat
vigorously for 30 seconds.  Drop dough by spoonfuls onto ungreased
cookie sheet.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes, or until golden brown. Mix
butter and garlic powder; brush over warm biscuits before removing
from cookie sheet. Serve warm. Randy Rigg
