Title: Alfredo Supreme
Categories: Pasta, Cheese/eggs, Side dish, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
Hot cooked fettuccine
Black pepper
ds Nutmeg
1/3 c  Grated paremsan cheese
1/3 c  Margarine
1/3 c  Half and half cream
Mix the cheese, margarine and the half and half together and heat
slowly until smooth.  Toss with 8 oz hot cooked, drained fettuccine.
Sprinkle with freshly ground black pepper and nutmeg.  Makes 4
servings. Origin: Kraft What's Cooking magazine, 9th issue. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
