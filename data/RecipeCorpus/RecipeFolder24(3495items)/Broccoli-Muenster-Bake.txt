Title: Broccoli Muenster Bake
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Cheese/eggs, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Minced onion flakes
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 tb Skim milk
10 oz Pkg. frozen cut broccoli
4 oz Muenster cheese, cubed
1 tb Plus (see next ingredient)
1 ts -lowfat mayonnaise
Cook broccoli according to package directions until just tender-crisp.
Drain. Preheat oven to 350. Combine all ingredients together, place
in a casserole that has been sprayed with a nonstick cooking spray
and bake, covered, 20 minutes.

Per serving: 9g protein, 10g fat, 5g carb., 286mg sodium, 29mg chol.,
143 calories.
