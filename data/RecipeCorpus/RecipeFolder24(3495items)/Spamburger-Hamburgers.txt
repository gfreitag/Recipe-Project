Title: Spamburger Hamburgers
Categories: Sandwich, Spam
Yield: 6 servings
6    Lettuce leaves
2    Tomatoes, sliced
6 sl American cheese (1 oz)
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat (12 oz)
6    Hamburger buns, split
3 tb Mayonnaise or salad dressing
Slice SPAM into 6 slices (3x1/4"). In skillet, saute SPAM over medium
heat until lightly browned. Spread cut sides of buns with mayonnaise.
Layer lettuce, tomato, SPAM, and cheese on bun bottom. Cover with bun
top.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 405; Protein 20 grams;
Carbohydrate 25 grams; Fat 25 grams; Cholesterol 76 milligrams;
Sodium 1280 milligrams.
