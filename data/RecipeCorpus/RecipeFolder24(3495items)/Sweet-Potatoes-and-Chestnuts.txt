Title: Sweet Potatoes and Chestnuts
Categories: Vegetable, Nuts/grains, Sweetpotato
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/4 c  Hot water
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
4 md Sweet potatoes
1 lb Chestnuts
Salt to taste
Cook, peel and halve sweet potatoes.

Cover chestnuts with boiling water, simmer covered for about 20
minutes. Rinse in cold water and remove shells and skins.  Keep nuts
in large pieces and arrange with potatoes in shallow baking dish.
Sprinkle with salt. Bring hot water and brown sugar to a boil.  Add
butter. Pour liquid in casserole.

Bake uncovered in preheated moderate oven (350 degrees) for 30
minutes.
