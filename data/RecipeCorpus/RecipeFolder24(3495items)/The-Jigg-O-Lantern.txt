Title: The Jigg-O-Lantern
Categories: Kids, Dessert, Holiday
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------PREP TIME:  15 MINUTES PLUST---------------------
1 c  Boiling water
2 c  Vanilla ice cream, softened
1 pk (85 gram) jello orange
-jelly powder
assorted candies ( they show licorice whips, cut to form eyes, nose,
mouth, eyebrows and a nib for the hat) Dissolve jelly powder in
boiling water. Add ice cream by spoonfuls, whisking until smooth.
Pour into dessert dishes. Chill until set, about 30 minutes. Make
pumpkin faces on jelly with candies.  Makes 4 servings. Origin:
Jell-o recipe card from store Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
