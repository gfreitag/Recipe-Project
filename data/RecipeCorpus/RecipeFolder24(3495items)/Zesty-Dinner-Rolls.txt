Title: Zesty Dinner Rolls
Categories: Bread
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Salt
1    Egg
1/4 c  Margarine
3 1/2 c  Flour
1 pk Yeast
1/4 c  Warm water
3/4 c  Lukewarm milk
1/4 c  Sugar
Dissolve yeast in warm water.  Add milk, sugar, salt, egg, and
margarine. Beat until smooth.  Add enough flour to make the dough
easy to handle. Knead on floured surface; grease bowl and put the
dough in and turn greased side up.  Let rise 1 1/2 to 2 hours in a
warm place. Make into balls and place in a muffin pan and let ris 20
minutes before baking. Bake at 400 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes. Randy
Rigg
