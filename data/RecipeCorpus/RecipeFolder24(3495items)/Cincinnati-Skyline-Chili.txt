Title: Cincinnati Skyline Chili
Categories: Chili, Ground beef, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
3 c  Water
1/4 t  Garlic power
1 ea Onion, chopped
1 t  Worcestershire sauce
2 t  Cumin
1 T  Chili powder
1/2 t  Crushed red pepper
2 lb Ground beef
1 cn Tomato sauce (15 oz)
2 ea Bay leaves
1 t  Ground cinnamon
1 1/2 t  Salt
1 1/2 t  Vinegar
1 1/2 t  Ground allspice
Mix ground beef and water until soupy.  Mix in other ingredients.
Simmer, uncovered 3 hours.  Stir occasionally. "Makes 8 servings."
Note: Top with grated Cheddar, onions, chili beans or use as sauce
over noodles or spaghetti.
