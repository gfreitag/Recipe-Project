Title: Guerrini Chicken Wings
Categories: Poultry, Appetizer, Bbq/grill, Chicken
Yield: 3 servings
5 T  Soy sauce
4 T  Sugar
1 T  Toasted sesame seeds
Oil for frying
Green onions, chopped
2-3 lbs. chicken wings
1 1/2 t  Salt
7 T  Cornstarch
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
4 T  Flour
2 ea Eggs
Mix all ingredients except oil and chicken.  Add chicken to mixture
and marinate overnight in the refrigerator, turning occasionally to
coat. Deep fry until golden brown. Note: These go very fast so I
always double or triple the batch! Posted by: Debbie Carlson
