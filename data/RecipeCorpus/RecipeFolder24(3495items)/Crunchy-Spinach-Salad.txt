Title: Crunchy Spinach Salad
Categories: Diabetic, Salad, Vegetable, Dressing
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-----------------------SPIANCH SALAD----------------------------
3/4 c  Cabbage, purple; shredded
1/4 c  Raisins
1/2 lb Spinach, fresh; torn into
-bite size pieces
2 tb Almonds, slivered; toasted
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1 1/2 tb Onion, minced
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Oil, vegetable
1/4 c  Sugar
3/4 ts Mustard, dry
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Celery seeds
Combine all ingredients except dressing in a large bowl.  Toss and
serve with dressing.

Dressing: Combine all ingredients in a jar. Cover tightly, and shake
vigorously. Chill several hours.  Shake dressing again before serving
over salad. Yield: 1 cup.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, July, 1980. Typed for you by Nancy
Coleman. Nancy O'Brion's Note: This could be worked out for the
