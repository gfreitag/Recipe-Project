Title: Halibut & Peppers Julienne
Categories: Fish, Seafood
Yield: 4 -6 serving
1    Tomato, seeded and
-coarsely chopped
1 ts Chopped parsley
1 ds Paprika, curry powder, and
-cayenne
Salt And Pepper
2/3 c  White Wine
1    Red pepper, seeded
1    Green Pepper, seeded
1/2    Yellow pepper, seeded
-(optional)
1    Onion
2    Stalks celery, halved
2 tb Butter
Description:

Partner poached steamed Halibut steaks with a colourful potpourri of
peppers simmered in wine.  For 4-6 Halibut steaks, freshly poached or
steamed.

Instructions:

Cut peppers, onion, and celery into thin strips.  Melt butter in
skillet. Add prepared vegetables and all remaining ingredients,
except halibut. Simmer 5 minutes.  Spoon simmered vegetables and pan
juices over halibut just before serving.
