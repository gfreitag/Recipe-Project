Title: Saytah Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
3 T  Olive oil
1 sm Onion, minced
1 c  Roasted, peanuts, pulverized
1 t  Tumeric
3 chili peppers , minced 1 cup
1 large onion, cut into rings 1 tbsp
Rice Directions: 1. Cut chicken into
4-6 skinned chicken breasts
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
1 ea Piece ginger root, minced
1 T  Soy sauce
Juice of 1 lime
water
olive oil
small cubes. 2. Saute chicken in oil until cooked. Remove from heat.
3. In a blender, put garlic etc. to water ingredients and set on
"destroy." Blend until smooth. 4. Put mixture in double boiler, cover
and cook till somewhat thickened (at least 20 minutes). 5. Slice
remaining onion into rings; saute in remaining oil until translucent.
6. Make rice. 7. Mix chicken and sauce. 8. Serve over rice; place
onion rings on top. This dish should be spicey. If not, add more
peppers the next time.
