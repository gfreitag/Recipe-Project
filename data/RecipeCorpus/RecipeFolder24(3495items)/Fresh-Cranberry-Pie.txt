Title: Fresh Cranberry Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Flour
1    Stick of margarine
1/4 c  Butter Crisco
1    Bag of fresh cranberries
3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Pecans
2    Eggs
Wash cranberries; put in pie plate.  Sprinkle with 3/4 cup of sugar
and 1 1/2 cups of pecans.  Set aside.  Beat 2 eggs until foamy;
gradually add 1 cup of sugar and beat well.  Add 1/2 cup of flour;
beat in melted butter and Crisco.  Beat well.  Add 1/2 cup of flour;
beat again. Pour over cranberries in pie plate.  Bake at 350 degrees
for 50 to 60 minutes, until golden brown. Randy Rigg
