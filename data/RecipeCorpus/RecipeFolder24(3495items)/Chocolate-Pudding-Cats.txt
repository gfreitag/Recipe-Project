Title: Chocolate Pudding Cats
Categories: Kids, Holiday, Chocolate, Pudding
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-------------------PREP TIME: 10 MINUTES------------------------
2 c  Milk
1 pk (4 serving size) chocolate
-instant pudding
MMMMM----------------ASSORTED CANDIES: SHOESTRING---------------------
Ju jubes, etc
Prepare pudding according to package directions.  Spoon into 4 round
bowls. Let stand 5 minutes.  Decorate with candies to resemble cat
faces; shoestring licorice for whiskers, assorted licorice for eyes
and jelly beans for nose.  Makes 4 servings. Origin: Jell-o recipe
card in store. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
