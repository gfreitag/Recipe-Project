Title: Old Fashioned Fruit Punch
Categories: Beverage, Fruit, Holiday
Yield: 50 servings
12 c  Ginger ale
4 c  Soda water; or sparkling war
16 c  -Cracked ice
1 bn Mint; fresh
4 c  Tea; cold
4 c  Orange juice
4    Lemons; juice of
4    Oranges; thinly sliced
2 c  Sugar; to taste
Combine tea, juices, orange slices and sugar. Stir until the sugar
dissolves. Chill. Add ginger ale and soda just before serving.
Garnish with fresh mint.

SERVES: about 50 SOURCE: _The Non-Drinker's Drink Book_ by Gail
Schioler
