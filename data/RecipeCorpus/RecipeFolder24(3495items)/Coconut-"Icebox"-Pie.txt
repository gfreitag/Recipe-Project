Title: Coconut "Icebox" Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pumpkin
Yield: 3 servings
1 pk Dream Whip
1 cn Coconut
1 pk Instant coconut pudding
(small)
1 1/2 c  Milk
Beat milk and package of pudding; whip Dream Whip (according to the
box). Fold into pudding.  Add 1/2 can of coconut; pour into baked pie
shell. Beat 3 egg whites plus 5 tablespoons powdered sugar.  Spread
on top of pie. Spread on top of the pie.  Sprinkle other 1/2 of the
can of coconut on top. Randy Rigg
