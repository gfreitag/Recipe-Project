Title: Tangy Miracle Whip Slices
Categories: Sandwich, Cheese/eggs, Snack
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
Spread halved pita bread with 1 tbsp miracle whip salad dressing
mixed with 1/2 tsp horseradish.  Fill with 2 slices of each: tomato,
cucumber and red onion.  top with 3 slices swiss cheese. 440 cal,
23.8 pro, 26.8 carbo, 26.5 fat, excellent source of calcium. Origin:
Kraft What's Cooking magazine, 9th issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
October/94. Submitted By SHARON STEVENS   On   10-04-94
