Title: Festive Egg Nog Bread
Categories: Bread, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 1/2 t  Vanilla extract
1 1/2 t  Brandy extract
1 t  Almond extract
1/2 c  Chopped almonds
1/2 c  Maraschino cherries
2 1/2 c  All purpose flour
1 c  White sugar
1 T  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
3/4 t  Nutmeg
3    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Half and half (or cream)
In mixing bowl, stir together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and
nutmeg.  In separate container beat together eggs, half and half,
oil, and the three extracts. Stir into dry ingredients; mix well.
Fold in nuts and cherries.  Turn into two greased loaf pans.  Bake at
350F for 40-45 minutes. Cool in pans 10 minutes.  Remove from pans
and cool on rack. Makes 2 loaves, 10 servings each.
