Title: Peanut Butter Brownies #2
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 16 brownies
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
2    Eggs
1 c  Chopped peanuts
1/2 c  Sifted flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Crunchy peanut butter
1/4 c  Butter
Preheat oven to 350oF. Sift flour and salt together into bowl. In
separate bowl, cream peanut butter, butter and vanilla together;
gradually add brown sugar, beating until well blended. Add eggs one
at at time; beat well after each addition. Blend in flour. Stir in
peanuts.

Spoon batter into greased 8" square baking pan; spread evenly. Bake
for 30-35 minutes or until center is firm. Cool in pan for 5 minutes,
then cut into squares. Remove from pan and cool on wire rack.

Source: Marian Hoffman, ed., Creative Cooking - Desserts. Typed by
