Title: Spiced Orange Tea Mix
Categories: Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  White sugar
2 t  Cinnamon
1 t  Allspice
1/2 t  Ground cloves
1 c  Powdered orange breakfast
-drink mix (ie, tang)
1 c  Powdered unsweetened
-instant tea mix
Combine all ingredients; store in a tightly covered container.  Makes
about 2 cups dry mix, 32 servings. TO SERVE: Spoon approx 2-3 tsp mix
in a cup, more or less to taste. Add 6 ounces boiling water, stir
well. VARIATION: Substitute apple juice, or cranberry juice for the
water. Origin: Watkins Food Gifts Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Sept/94.
