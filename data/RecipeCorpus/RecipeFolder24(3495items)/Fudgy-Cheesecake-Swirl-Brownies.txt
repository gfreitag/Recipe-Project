Title: Fudgy Cheesecake Swirl Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 24 servings
MMMMM--------------------CREAM CHEESE BATTER-------------------------
1    Egg
1 t  Vanilla extract
1 pk Cream cheese, softened (8oz)
1/2 c  Sugar
MMMMM----------------------CHOCOLATE BATTER---------------------------
1 3/4 c  Sugar
3    Eggs, well beaten
1 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 c  Butter
2    Foil-wrapped bars NESTLE
-unsweetened chocolate
-baking bars
CREAM CHEESE BATTER: Preheat oven to 350'F. In small mixer bowl, beat
cream cheese and 1/2 cup sugar until smooth. Beat in 1 egg and
vanilla extract; set aside.

CHOCOLATE BATTER: In heavy-gauge, medium saucepan over low heat, melt
butter and Nestle unsweetened chocolate bars, stirring until smooth.
Stir in 1 3/4 cups sugar. Blend in 3 eggs. Stir in flour.

Spread Chocolate Batter into greased 13x9" baking pan. Smooth Cream
Cheese Batter over top. Swirl metal spatula through batters to
marbleize.

Bake 30-35 minutes until edges begin to pull away from side of pan.
Cool completely; cut into 2" bars.

Makes about 2 dozen brownies.
