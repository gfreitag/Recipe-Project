Title: Plum Sauce B1
Categories: Fruit, Chinese, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb HONEY
1/3 c  CIDER VINEGAR
A FEW DROPS GARLIC JUICE
1 c  RED PLUM PRESERVES
1/2 c  APRICOT PRESERVES
1/2 c  APPLESAUCE
Combine all ingredients in saucepan; bring to boil over medium heat.
Cook, stirring constantly for 5 minutes, until all ingredients are
well blended. Remove from heat; let cool.  To preserve, pour into hot
sterilized jar and seal at once. Temperature(s): HOT Effort: EASY
Time: 1:15 Source: ANNA KAO'S Comments: SERVE WITH ANY RECIPE CALLING
Comments: PLUM SAUCE.
