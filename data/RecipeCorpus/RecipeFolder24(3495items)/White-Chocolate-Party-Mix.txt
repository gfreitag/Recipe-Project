Title: White Chocolate Party Mix
Categories: Candy, Mixes, Nuts/grains, Snack
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Pretzel sticks
2 c  Dry roasted peanuts
12 oz M&M's, plain
1 lb White chocolate
3 c  Rice Chex cereal
3 c  Corn Chex cereal
3 c  Cheerios cereal
Slowly melt white chocolate in top of a double boiler over simmering
water. Combine cereals, pretzels, peanuts, and candy in a large bowl.
Slowly pour chocolate over mixture and stir until evenly coated.
Spread the mixture on wax paper and allow to cool.  Break into small
pieces. Store in an airtight container and refrigerate to keep fresh.
