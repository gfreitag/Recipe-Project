Title: Sole in Herbed Butter
Categories: Main dish, Diabetic, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 t  Salt (optional)
1/4 t  White pepper
2 lb Sole fillets
Fresh dill and lemon wedges
-(optional)
4 T  Butter or margarine,
-softened
1 t  Dillweed
1/2 t  Onion powder
1/2 t  Garlic powder
In a bowl, mix butter, dill, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, if
desired and pepper. Transfer to a skillet; heat on medium until
melted. Add the sole and saute for several minutes on each side or
until it flakes easily with a fork. Garnish with dill and lemon if
desired. Serves 6. Diabetic Exchanges: One serving (prepared with
light margarine and with out added salt) equals 4 1/2 lean meat, 1/2
fat; also, 256 Calories, 303mg Sodium, 72mg Cholesterol, trace
Carbohydrate, 35gm Protein, 12gm Fat. SOURCE:* Taste Of Home Magazine
