Title: Orange Sour Cream Dip
Categories: Dip, Appetizer
Yield: 10 servings
1/4 c  Sour cream
1 md Pineapple, fresh
Fruit, fresh; assorted
6 oz Orange juice, frozen conc.;
-thawed and undiluted
1 1/4 c  Milk
3 oz Pudding mix, instant vanilla
Combine orange juice concentrate, milk, and pudding mix; beat with
electric mixer until smooth (about 2 minutes).  Stir in sour cream;
chill 2 hours.

Cut pineapple in half lengthwise.  Scoop out pulp, leaving shells
intact; set aside shells.  Cut pineapple pulp into chunks.  Combine
pineapple chunks and other assorted fruits; spoon into 1 pineapple
shell. Spoon dip into remaining shell.
