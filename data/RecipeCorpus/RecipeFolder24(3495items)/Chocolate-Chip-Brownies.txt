Title: Chocolate Chip Brownies
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 50 brownies
3 lg Eggs
3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
Confectioners' sugar (opt)
3 oz Unsweetened baking chocolate
1/2 c  Butter/margarine, softened
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
1/4 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 375oF. Butter and lightly flour a 9"x13" pan. Set
aside. In double boiler over low heat, melt chocolate. Stir until
smooth; remove from heat. Cream butter and sugars in large bowl until
light and fluffy. Blend in milk. Beat in eggs, one at a time, until
well blended. Stir in melted chocolate. Fold in flour and chocolate
chips, just until moistened.

Pour into prepared pan. Bake 25-30 minutes. Cool in pan before
cutting into squares. Dust with confectioners' sugar, if desired.

Source: Marian Hoffman, Whitman's Chocolate Cookbook. Typed by Linda
