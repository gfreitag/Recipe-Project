Title: Fruit and Nut Cheese Ball
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Worcestershire sauce
1/2 t  Onion powder
1/8 t  Garlic powder
2/3 c  Chopped mixed dried fruit,
-divided
1/3 c  Sliced almonds, toasted
8 oz Shredded sharp cheddar
-cheese
1/3 c  Butter or margarine,
-softened
1    Tbps parisienne mustard
1 T  Milk
In food processor or with an electric mixer, process or mix first 7
ingredients until smooth.  Stir in 1/3 cup of the mixed dried fruit.
Chill 30 minutes.  Shape into a ball.  Roll in amixture of the
remaining dried fruit and almonds, pressing gently to make fruit and
almonds adhere. Chill for up to 2 days.  Makes 1 cheese ball, 12
servings.

Store in refrigerator.  Let stand at room temperature one hour before
serving.  Serve with assorted crackers.
