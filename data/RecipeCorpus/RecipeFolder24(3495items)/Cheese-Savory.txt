Title: Cheese Savory
Categories: Dessert, Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 unknown
1 c  Pecans; chopped
1/2 ts Red pepper
8 oz Cheese, Old English
1 c  Oleo
3 1/2 c  Flour
Melt oleo and cheese.  Add flour and pepper; stir in pecans.  Cool and
shalp into rolls.  Wrap in wax paper.  Chill overnight.  Slice thin.
Bake at 400 degrees for about 12 minutes.
