Title: Red Wine Marinade
Categories: Marinade
Yield: 1 half cup
2    Garlic cloves; minced
3/4 ts Italian seasoning, dried
1/4 ts Black pepper, coarse grind
1/3 c  Vinegar, red wine
2 tb Oil, vegetalbe
1 tb Mustard, Dijon-style
Combine all ingredients, stirring until well blended.
