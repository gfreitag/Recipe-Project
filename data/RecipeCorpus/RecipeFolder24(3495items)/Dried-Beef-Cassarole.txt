Title: Dried Beef Cassarole
Categories: Beef, Pasta, Meat, Casserole
Yield: 8 servings
1    Onion, chopped
2 c  Macaroni; cooked & drained
8 oz Cheddar; grated
1 pk Dried beef (Armour)
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 pk Small sour cream
Mix beef, soup, sour cream and onion together.  Add macaroni.  Mix
well. Pour into greased 13x9 pan.  top with cheese.  cover with foil
& bake @ 350F for 45-55 min.
Date: 09-25-94
From: Joan Mershon
