Title: Apple Streusel Mince Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 1 pie
-Ready-to-use
1/4 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/3 c  Margarine; or butter, cold
1/4 c  Nuts; chopped
1    Unbaked pastry shell; 9-inch
3    Apples; pared, thinly sliced
1/2 c  Flour; unsifted
3 tb Flour; unsifted
2 tb Margarine; or butter, melted
1    Jar None Such Mincemeat
In large bowl, toss apples with 3 Tablespoons flour and melted
margarine; arrange in pastry shell.  Top with mincemeat.  In medium
bowl, combine remaining 1/2 cup flour, sugar, and cinnamon; cut on
cold margarine until crumbly.  Add nuts; sprinkle over mincemeat.

Bake in lower half of 425 oven 10 minutes.  Reduce oven temperature
to 375; bake 25 minutes longer or until golden.  Cool
