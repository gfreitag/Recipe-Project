Title: Super Simple Carrot Stuffing
Categories: Stuffing, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
Prepare Stove Top Stuffing mix for chicken according to package
directions. Stir in 1 cup grated fresh carrot and 1/2 green pepper,
chopped. Stuff chicken and roast until done. Origin: Kraft What's
Cooking magazine, 9th issue. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
Submitted By SHARON STEVENS   On   10-02-94
