Title: Sweet Rice
Categories: Thai, Dessert, Rice, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Coconut milk
1 1/2 c  Short grain rice
Place the rice in a bowl with 2 c water.  Cover the bowl & soak the
rice overnight.

Thirty minutes to 1 hour before serving, place the rice & whatever
wateris left into a medium sized pot.  Stir in the coconut milk,
cover tightly & cook for 20 minutes over low heat.  After 10 minutes,
stir once. The finished grains will have a thick, sticky consistency.
Top with fruit & serve.

Pojanee Vatanapan's "Thai Cookbook"
