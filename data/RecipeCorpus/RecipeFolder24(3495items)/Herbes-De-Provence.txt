Title: Herbes De Provence
Categories: Herbs/spice, French
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2 t  Dried summer savory
1 t  Fennel seeds
2 t  Dried thyme
2 t  Dried basil
1/2 t  Dried lavender
Crush together in a mortar. Use on meats or poultry++ oven-roasted or
grilled; zucchini; potatoes.
