Title: Tomato Spinach Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2    3 cloves fresh garlic
2    3 cups water
1 pk Lipton Tomato-Herb soup
-(dry)
1/2 c  Ff spagetti sauce
1 1/2 c  Spinach
1 T  Each fresh dill, thyme, and
-marjoram
5    6 fresh basil leaves
Put all sauce in pot (rinse jar & use that too). Add soup powder.
Blenderize remaining ingredients using as much water as needed. Add
to the sauce and simmer until desired consistancy.
