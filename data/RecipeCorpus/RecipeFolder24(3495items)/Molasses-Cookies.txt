Title: Molasses Cookies
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 dozen
1/4 t  Cloves
1 t  Cinnamon
1 t  Ginger
2 1/2 c  Flour
1    Egg
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Milk
1 t  Salt
1 t  Baking soda
Cream shortning and sugar.  Add molasses, milk, salt, spices, flour,
well-beaten egg.  Bake at 350~, 12-15 min.
