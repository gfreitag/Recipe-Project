Title: Zucchini and Chicken Skillet
Categories: Vegetable, Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Cream of celery soup
1 t  Paprika
1 t  Basil
Salt and pepper to taste
2 md Zucchini, sliced
2 T  Shortening
1/2 c  Tomatoes, drained
2 lb Chicken
In skillet, brown chicken in shortening.  Pour off excess fat.  Add
soup, tomatoes and seasonings.  Cover.  Cook on low heat for 30
minutes. Add zucchini.  Cook about 15 minutes longer. Origin: Women's
Circle, for cooks on the go. Shared by: Sharon Stevens
