Title: Cranberry Celebration Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
Makes 1 13x9 inch cake
MMMMM-------------------PREP TIME: 15 MINUTES------------------------
MMMMM------------------COOK TIME: 30-35 MINUTES-----------------------
1 T  Grated orange rind
Vanilla frosting (homemade
-or commercial)
1/2 c  M & M's plain chocolate
-candies
2 pk (250 gram each) white cake
-mix
1 c  Water
2 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  Fresh or frozen cranberries
Grease and flour a 13 x 9 inch oblong pan, set aside.  In large bowl,
combine cake mix, water and eggs.  With electric mixer, beat at
medium high speed for 4 minutes until bater is thickened and smooth.
Stir in cranberries.  Bake in 350F oven for 30-35 minutes, until
toothpick inserted into center of cake comes out clean of cake
batter.  Let cake cool in pan for 5 minutes.  Remove cake from pan,
let cool on wire rack. Add orange rind to frosting; stir well.
Spread over cake; sprinkle with M&M's candies.  Cut into squares.
