Title:  Warm Mixed Berry Compote
Categories: Dessert, Ice cream, Masterchefs
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Water
2 oz Butter
Ice cream, vanilla
1 c  Strawberries, fresh
1 c  Raspberries, fresh
1 c  Blueberries, fresh
1/2 c  Sugar
In a large saucepan, bring the sugar and water to a boil.
Simmer for 10 minutes.  Add the strawberries and blueberries to the
pan and cook for 2 minutes.  At the last minute, add the mixed
raspberries and butter to the saucepan with the other ingredients.

Heat just until butter melts.  Spoon the mixture onto a flat
plate and serve with a scoop or ice cream in the center.

Source: Great Chefs of San Francisco, Avon Books, 1984

Chef:   Jeremiah Tower, Santa Fe Bar and Grill, Berkeley, CA
Date: 09-08-94
From: Rob Stewart
