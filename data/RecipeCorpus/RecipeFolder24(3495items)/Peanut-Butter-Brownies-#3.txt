Title: Peanut Butter Brownies #3
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 25 brownies
2 oz Unsweetened baking chocolate
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Peanut butter
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
Confectioners' sugar
1/2 c  Cornflake crumbs
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Nuts, coarsely chopped
1/4 c  Margarine or butter
Preheat oven to 350oF. Grease a 9"x9"x2" baking pan. Mix cornflake
crumbs with baking powder, salt and nuts. Set aside. Melt margarine
and chocolate together in medium-sized saucepan over very low heat,
stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Add granulated sugar and
peanut butter. Stir until well combined. Add eggs. Beat well. Stir in
crumbs mixture.

Spread in prepared pan. Bake about 30 minutes, or until wooden pick
inserted near center comes out clean. When cool, sift confectioners'
sugar over top. Cut into 1 3/4" squares.

Source: Marian Hoffman, Whitman's Chocolate Cookbook. Typed by Linda
