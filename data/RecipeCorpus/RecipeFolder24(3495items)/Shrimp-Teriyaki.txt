Title: Shrimp Teriyaki
Categories: Appetizer, Seafood
Yield: 10 servings
1 ts Minced Fresh Ginger Root
2 tb Water
2 lb Medium Sized Raw Shrimp,
-Peeled And Deveined
1/2 c  Soy Sauce
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Vegetable Oil
1 1/2 ts Cornstarch
1    Clove Garlic, Crushed
Blend soy sauce, sugar, oil, cornstarch, garlic, ginger and water inn
small saucepan.  Simmer, stirring constantly, until thickened, about
1 minute; cool.  Coat shrimp with sauce; drain off excess.  Place on
rack of broiler pan.  Broil 5 inches from heat source 3 to 4 minutes
on each side, or until shrimp are opaque and cooked.  Serve
immediately with wooden picks. Typed by Syd Bigger.
