Title: Flower Pot Walnut Bread
Categories: Bread, Nuts/grains, Down under
Yield: 3 x 15 cm di
MMMMM----------------SOURCE:  AUSTRALIAN VOGUE WI---------------------
320 ml Warm water
1 1/2 c  Walnuts, chopped coarsely
1 ea Egg, beaten
1 ea Packet Bird's Wholemeal
-Bread Mix
16 g  Sachet yeast (included)
Empty bread mix into bowl, stir in contents of yeast sachet and mix
well. Add warm (37'C) water and 1 cup of the walnuts.  Mix to take a
consistent dough.  Knead dough well on a bench for 7 to 10 minutes,
or mix in a machine with a dough hook.  Return dough to bowl, cover
with greased plastic food wrap and place in a warm position for 10
minutes. Divide mixture into three and place into well-greased
terracotta pots. Cover with greased plastic food wrap and leave to
prove in a warm place for 45 minutes, or until dough rises to tops of
pots. Remove plastic wrap and gently brush dough with beaten egg.
Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup of walnuts.  Bake bread on centre
shelf of a pre-heated 200oC oven for 25 mins.  Serve with an
assortment of cheese. Sue Morphet and Barbara Wooley, Bird's Country
Mill Bread Mixes. Bon-Appetit, Exec.Chef. Magnus Johansson
