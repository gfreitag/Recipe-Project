Title: Spiced Honey
Categories: Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
3 ea Sticks cinnamon
2 2/3 c  (620 mL) honey
1 ea Lemon
12 ea Whole cloves
Cut lemon into 6 thin slices.  Place 2 cloves in each side.  Place
lemon slices, cinnamon sticks, and honey in a saucepan.  Bring to
boiling, stirring occasionally.  Remove lemon slices from honey and
place 2 lemon slices and 1 cinnamon stick in each jar.  Pour honey
into hot jars, leaving 1/4" (6mm) head space.  Adjust caps.  Process
10 minutes in boiling water bath.  Yield:  About 3 half pints. (720
mL) From: Ball Blue Book Shared By: Pat Stockett
