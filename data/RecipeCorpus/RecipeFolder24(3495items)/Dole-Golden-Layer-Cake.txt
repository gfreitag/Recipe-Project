Title: Dole Golden Layer Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
16 oz Froz pound cake, thawed
1/3 c  Almond flavored liquer
1/4 c  Sliced toasted almonds
20 oz Can Dole crushed Pineapple
8 oz Carton whipped topping, thaw
4 pk Serving Vanilla inst pudding
Combine undrained pineapple, whipped topping and pudding mix. Let
stand 5 mins. Cut cake lengthwise in 1/3. Drizzle with liquer. Spread
1/3 pudding mixture over the bottom layer of cake. Top with seciond
layer. Repeat layering ending with pudding. Chill 30 minutes.
Sprinkle with toasted almonds and garnish with pineapple slices if
desired. Submitted By CAROL VERGE   On   10-03-94
