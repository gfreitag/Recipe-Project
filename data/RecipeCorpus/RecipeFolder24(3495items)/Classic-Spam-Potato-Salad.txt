Title: Classic Spam Potato Salad
Categories: Salad, Spam, Potato
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Chopped dill pickles
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
3/4 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
2 tb Dill pickle liquid
1 t  Prepared mustard
2 lb Potatoes (about 6 medium)
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(12 oz)
1/2 c  Cooked diced carrots
1/2 c  Frozen peas, thawed
Cook unpeeled potatoes in boiling water until tender; drain. Cool
slightly; peel. Cool to room temperature; cut into 1/2" cubes. In
large bowl, combine SPAM and potatoes. Add carrots, peas, pickles,
and onion; gently toss. Stir together mayonnaise, pickle liquid, and
mustard; gently stir into SPAM mixture. Cover and refrigerate several
hours.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 344; Protein 10 grams;
Carbohydrate 26 grams; Fat 23 grams; Cholesterol 46 milligrams;
Sodium 672 milligrams.
