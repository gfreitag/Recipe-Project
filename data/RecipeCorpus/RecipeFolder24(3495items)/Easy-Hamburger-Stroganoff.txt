Title: Easy Hamburger Stroganoff
Categories: Main dish, Burgers
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
8 oz Musrhooms; drained
1 cn Soup, cream of chicken
8 oz Sour cream
8 oz Egg noodles; cooked/drained
1 lb Beef, ground
1 md Onion; chopped
2 tb Flour, all-purpose
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic salt
Cook ground beef and onion until beef is browned and onion is tender.
Stir in flour, salt, garlic salt, pepper, and mushrooms; cook over
medium heat 5 minutes, stirring mixture constantly.

Stir soup into meat mixture; simmer 10 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Stir in sour cream, and cook only until thoroughly
heated.  Serve over hot cooked noodles.
