Title: Grilled Jalapeno Lime Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Bbq/grill, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
3 T  Jalapeno mustard
4 ea Skinless, boneless chicken
-breast halves (about 1 llb)    1 1/2 ea To 2 tsp liquid garlic spice
1/2 t  Mexican pepper
1/3 ea To 1/2 cup fresh lime juice
Flatten chicken breat to 1/4 inch thickness.  Combine lime juice and
next 3 ingredients.  Add chicken.  Marinate at least 1 hour.  Grill
or broil chicken until done, approzimately 7 minutes per side,
basting occasionally with marinade. Origin: Watkins Catalogue Shared
by: Sharon Stevens
