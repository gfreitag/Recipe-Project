Title: Marinated Vegetable Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable, Greek
Yield: 1 servings
Tomato, cut into small
-wedges
Black olives, cut in half
Feta cheese, crumbled
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  White vinegar
1 t  Minced garlic
1/2 t  Sweet basil
1/2 t  Parsley
1 lg Green pepper, cut into bite
-sized chunks
1 md Sweet red pepper, chunked
1 md Yellow pepper, chunked
1 md Orange pepper, chunked
2 ea Carrots, peeled and grated
Few broccoli florets
Few cauliflower florets
1/2 c  Kernel corn (frozen or
-canned)
Prepare all the vegetables.  Place in a container with a lid that will
seal.  Mix thoroughly.  Prepare dressing ingredients, and drizzle
over top of vegetables.  Seal.  Store in fridge.  Every 3-5 hours,
turn container over to allow the dressing to coat all vegetables.
Better flavour develops if you make the salad the day before serving.
