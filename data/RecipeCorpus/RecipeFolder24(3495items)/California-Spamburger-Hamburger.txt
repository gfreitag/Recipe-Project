Title: California Spamburger Hamburger
Categories: Sandwich, Spam
Yield: 6 servings
6    Lettuce leaves
2    Tomatoes, sliced
6    Green bell pepper rings
6    Onion slices
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat (12 oz)
6    Whole wheat hamburger buns,
-split
3 tb Thousand Island salad
-dressing
Slice SPAM into 6 slices (3x1/4"). In skillet, saute SPAM over medium
heat until lightly browned. Spread cut sides of bun with dressing.
Layer lettuce, SPAM, tomato, green pepper, and onion on bun bottom.
Cover with bun top.

NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION PER SERVING: Calories 287; Protein 14 grams;
Carbohydrate 28 grams; Fat 13 grams; Cholesterol 47 milligrams;
Sodium 891 milligrams.
