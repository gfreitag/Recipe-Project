Title: Sausage Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Sausage
Yield: 2 servings
3/4 c  Milk
1 c  Cubed cheese
Bread crumbs
1 lb Ground sausage
1 pk Noodles, 4 oz
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 sm Jar of pimiento
Fry sausage and drain off fat.  Cook noodles and drain.  Mix all
ingredients and put into casserole.  Put bread crumbs on top and cook
until brown and bubbly.  (About 25 minutes)  Bake at 375 degrees for
20 minutes then top with potato chips and bake 3 or 4 minutes longer.
