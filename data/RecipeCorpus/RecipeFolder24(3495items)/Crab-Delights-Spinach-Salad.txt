Title: Crab Delights Spinach Salad
Categories: Salad, Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 6 servings
Spinach or lettuce leaves
1 lb Deli spinach dip
1/2 c  Slivered almonds
1 pk Louis Kemp Crab Delights
-flakes or chunks (8 oz)
3    Tomatoes, sliced
Arrange Crab ?Delights, tomato slices and spinach on salad plates.
Top with 1/3 cup spinach dip; spinkle with slivered almonds.

230 calories per serving.

Alternate method: Combine Crab Delights, spinach dip and almonds.
Arrange spinach and tomato on salad plates; top with 1/2 cup salad
