Title: Peanut Butter Brownies
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2 t  Vanilla
3 c  Flour,all-purpose,sifted
1 T  Baking powder
1 t  Salt
1/4 c  Peanut,chopped
3/4 c  Peanut butter
1/3 c  Butter
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown sugar,firmly packed
4    Eggs
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 T  Honey
1 c  Peanuts,dry-roasted,chopped
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar,firmly packed
1/2 c  Butter
1/4 c  Milk
Cream the peanut butter, butter, and sugars. Add the eggs and vanilla
and beat until well blended. Sift dry ingredients and add to creamed
mixture along with peanuts; mix until smooth. Spread batter evenly
into a greased 13x9x2" pan. Bake in a preheated moderate oven
(350'F.) for 35 minutes.

*** TOPPING ***

Combine sugar, butter, milk, and honey. Bring to a boil and cook
slowly for 10 minutes. Remove from heat and add peanuts. Let cool and
spread on warm brownies. Cut into squares.
