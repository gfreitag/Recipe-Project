Title: Eggplant Salad Ii
Categories: Salad, Moroccan, Appetizer, Vegetable
Yield: 2 cups
3 ea Garlic cloves, chopped
1/2 ts Cumin
1 ts Sweet paprika
1 tb Cilantro, chopped, optional
3 tb Lemon juice
2 lb Eggplant
Salt
Olive oil, for frying
3 ea Tomatoes, peeled, seeded &
-- chopped
Remove three vertical strips of skin from each vegetable leaving the
eggplants striped.  Then cut into 1/2" thick slices.  Salt them &
leave them to drain in a colander for 30 minutes.  Rinse well &
drain. Dry on paper towels.

Heat oil in a skillet & fry the eggplant slices until brown on both
sides, removing them when cooked.  Mash the eggplants with the
tomatoes, garlic spices & cilantro.

Fry the puree in the oil that remains in the skillet until all the
liquid has evaporated.  Stirring the puree to prevent scorching,
saute for another 15 minutes.  Pour off the oil & season with lemon
juice. Taste for salt & serve warm or cold.

Paula Wolfert, "Cousocous & Other Good Food From Morocco"
