Title: Pepper Burgers
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Beef, Main dish, Burgers, Ground beef
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Tomato sauce
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 pk Beef broth mix
Pepper to taste
1 lb Very lean ground beef
1 ea Egg white
3/4 oz Wheat germ
1 c  Very finely chopped
-red and/or green pepper
Mix all ingredients, shape into 4 patties. Place on rack in broiler
pan or on grill and cook 3 incehs from heat about 5 minutes per side,
or until done to taste.

Per serving: 23g protein, 16g fat, 6g carb., 423mg sodium, 70mg
chol., 259 calories.
Date: 09-24-94
From: Carolyn Shaw
