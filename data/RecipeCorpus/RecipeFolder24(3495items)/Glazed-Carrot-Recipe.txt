Title: Glazed Carrot Recipe
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Grated orange peel
1 1/2 c  ;water
2 t  Cornstarch mixed with 3
-Tablespoons water
8 md Carrots, cut diagonally in
-thin slices
4 T  Apple juice concentrate
1/2 t  Ground cinnamon
1/8 t  Ground allspice
1) Put carrots and remaining ingred except corn starch mixture in med
size saucepan. 2) Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 20 min or
until carrots are tender, but not soft. 3) Add cornstarch mixture;
cook; stirring constantly, until mixture thickens

Per serving: 95 cals 2 g protein 23 g carbos 0 g fat 76 mg sodium
