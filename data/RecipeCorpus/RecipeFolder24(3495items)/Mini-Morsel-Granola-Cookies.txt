Title: Mini Morsel Granola Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 48 servings
2    Eggs
1 pk NESTLE Toll House semi-sweet
-chocolate mini morsels
2 c  Granola cereal
1 c  Raisins
2 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Butter, softened
1 1/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
Prehea oven to 375'F. In small bowl, combine flour, baking powder,
baking soda and cinnamon; set aside.

In large mixer bowl, beat butter and brown sugar until creamy. Beat in
eggs. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in Nestle Toll House
semi-sweet chocolate mini morsels, granola and raisins. Drop by
rounded measuring tablespoonfuls ont ungreased cookie sheets.

Bake 9-11 minutes until edges are golden brown. Let stand on cookie
sheets 5 minutes. Remove from cookie sheets; cool.

Makes about 4 dozen cookies.
