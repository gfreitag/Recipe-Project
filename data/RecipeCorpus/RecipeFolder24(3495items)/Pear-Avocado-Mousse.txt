Title: Pear Avocado Mousse
Categories: Appetizer, Dip
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Grated orange rind
2 tb Spiced or plain dark rum
1/2 c  Chopped peanuts or macadamia
-nuts
1 ds Allspice
2    Very ripe pears, peeled and
-chopped
1    Very ripe avocado, peeled,
-seeded and chopped
2 tb Lemon juice
mint sprigs for garnish

Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor and puree for
approximately 1 minute.  Pour the pureed mixture into a glass dessert
dish or parfait glasses and chill in the refrigerator for at least an
hour. Garnish with mint sprigs and serve cold.
