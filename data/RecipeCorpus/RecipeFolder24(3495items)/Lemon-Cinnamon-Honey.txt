Title: Lemon-Cinnamon Honey
Categories: Dessert, Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 1 SERVINGS--------------------------
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1 c  Mild-flavored honey
1/4 t  Cinnamon
In a small saucepan, combine the honey, lemon juice and cinnamon.
Heat, stirring, over low heat, just until the ingredients are warm
and blended. Serve warm over pancakes or French toast or keep cold
and drizzle on toast. Also goes good in a cup of tea or sipped from a
spoon when you feel a cold coming on. Shared by: Carol Verge
