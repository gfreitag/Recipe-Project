Title: Onion - Herb Baked Bread
Categories: Herbs/spice, Bread
Yield: 1 loaf
1 ts Basil leaves
1 ts Oregano
1/8 ts Pepper
(about 16" long, halved
Lengthwise
1 pk Onion recipe soup mix
1 md Clove garlic, finely chopped
1/2 c  Butter, softened
1    Loaf Italian or French
-bread
Directions Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. In small bowl, thoroughly
blend all ingredients except bread; generously spread on bread
halves. On baking sheet, arrange bread cut side up, and bake 15
minutes or until golden. Serve warm.

NOTE Store any remaining spread, covered, in refrigerator for future
use.
