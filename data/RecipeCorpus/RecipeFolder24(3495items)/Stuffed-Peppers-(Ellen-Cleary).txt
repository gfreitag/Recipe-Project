Title: Stuffed Peppers (Ellen Cleary)
Categories: Ground beef, Rice, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Onion, chopped
8 oz Tomato sauce
1 c  Raw rice
3-4 large peppers
1 lb Ground beef
Cook rice.  Remove top, seeds and any inner membranes from peppers.
Place in boiling water for 5 minutes to parboil.  Brown meat and
onions, then drain. Combine rice, meat and onion mixture, tomato
sauce, salt and pepper. Stuff into peppers. Place in a baking pan and
bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. If you have leftover spaghetti
sauce, it is great over these. From: ELLEN CLEARY
