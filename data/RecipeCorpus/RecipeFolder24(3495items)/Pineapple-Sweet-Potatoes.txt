Title: Pineapple Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Diabetic, Side dish, Vegetable, Fruit, Sweetpotato
Yield: 8 sweet ones
2 c  Pineapple tidbits;
-artificially sweetened
2 tb Diet margarine
24 oz Sweet potatoes;*
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Boil potaotoes until tender; peel; cool slightly.  Mash untill
smooth; stir in remaining ingredients, including syrup from pineapple
tidbits. Return to stove and reheat.

Food Exchange per serving: 1 STARCH/BREAD EXCHANGE + 1 FAT EXCHANGE +
1/2 FRUIT EXCHANGE  CAL: 122

Source: Recipes for Diabetics by Billie Little (1985 Version)
