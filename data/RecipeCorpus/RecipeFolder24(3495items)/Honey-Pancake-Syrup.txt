Title: Honey Pancake Syrup
Categories: Syrup, Salsa/sauce, Breakfast, Pancakes
Yield: 1 servings
-sweetnees of honey.
1/2 c  Water
1 c  HONEY
1 t  Vanilla
Honey can be used straight
-from the jar on pancakes -
-but this
Syrup pours nicely and
-dilutes the overwhelming
Heat water in a saucepan over medium heat.  Add honey just before
boiling. Stir well until combined- do not boil.  Remove from heat -
stir in vanilla. Store in covered jar or syrup dispenser in the
refrigerator.
