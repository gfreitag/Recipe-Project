Title: Curry Tomato Sauce
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
-tomatoes
1/4 t  Curry powder
1/2 t  Ground turmeric
1 bn Scallions, finely chopped
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1 T  Grated fresh ginger
1    28-ounce can crushed
Place a small amount of water in a saucepan. Add the scallions,
garlic & ginger. Cook & stir over medium heat for a few minutes. Add
remaining ingredients. Mix well. Cook for another 6 to 7 minutes to
blend flavors. Serve hot.
