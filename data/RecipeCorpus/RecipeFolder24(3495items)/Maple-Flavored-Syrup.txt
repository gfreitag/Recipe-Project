Title: Maple Flavored Syrup
Categories: Syrup
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 t  Cream of tartar
3-4 tsp maple flavor
1/4 t  Vanilla extract
2 1/2 ea To 3 cups white sugar
1 c  Water
3 T  White corn syrup
Mix together first four ingredients in 2 quart saucepan.  Stir over
medium heat until sugar dissolves and mixture is crystal clear.  Stir
in maple flavor and vanilla extract; continue to simmer for 2
minutes. Pour into attractive containers.  Makes 1-1/2 pints, 1 tbsp
per serving. Good with pancakes, waffles, French toast, etc.  Will
keep longer if kept in refrigerator.  Bring to room temperature
before serving. Origin: Watkins Food Gifts Cookbooks Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
