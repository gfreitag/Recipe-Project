Title: Sauerkraut Hot Dog Topping
Categories: Vegetable, Hot dogs, Sausage, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
"You'll relish this zesty condiment!  For years, I stirred this up by
guess and golly before finally figuring out the proper measure of
ingredients so I could share the recipe. It's great to have on hand
anytime that you grill hot dogs or smoked sausages." - Erlene
Cornelius 15 oz. 1/4 cup 2 Tbsp. brown sugar 1 Tbsp. prepared mustard
1/2 tsp. caraway seed can sauerkraut, rinsed and drained sweet pickle
relish

Combine all ingredients in a saucepan; cook on low heat until heated
through.  Serve over hot dogs. Yields: 2 cups From: "Taste of Home"
Magazine, October/November 1994 Posted by: Debbie Carlson - Cooking

Submitted By DEBBIE CARLSON   On   10-16-94
