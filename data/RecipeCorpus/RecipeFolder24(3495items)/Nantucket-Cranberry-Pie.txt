Title: Nantucket Cranberry Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Almond extract
2 c  Chopped cranberries
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Sugar
2 lg Eggs
3/4 c  Melted and cooled butter
Preheat oven to 350F. Combine the chopped cranberries, walnuts and
1/2 cup sugar and mix well. Put in the bottom of a buttered 10 inch
pie plate or springform pan.

Combine eggs, butter, sugar, flour, salt, and almond extract. Stir the
batter until smooth and pour over the cranberry-walnut mixture. Bake
the cake/pie in the middle of the oven for 40 minutes, or until
tester comes out clean.

Source: Yankee Cookbook, by Imogene Wolcott Published in Gourmet
