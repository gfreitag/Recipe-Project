Title: Overnight French Toast Croissants
Categories: Breakfast, Waffles
Yield: 8 servings
1    Orange peel; finely grated
8    Dry croissants
1/2 c  Maple Syrup
1    Orange; sliced
4    Eggs
1 c  Whipping cream; or cereal
2 T  Sugar
2 t  Cinnamon
1 1/2 t  Vanilla
Grease 13 x 9" baking pan and set aside.

In large bowl, combine egg mixture ingredients and beat until well
blended.

Dip croissants into egg mixture, turning once.  Place in prepared
pan. Pour any extra egg mixture over croissants.  Cover with foil and
refrigerate overnight.

Preheat oven to 375F.  Bake croissants, covered, 30 to 35 minutes or
until golden brown.

Serve with maple syrup and orange slices.
