Title: Heads Up Ranch Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Carrot, cut in julienne
-strips
1 lg Broccoli, cut into
-flowerettes
MMMMM----------------1/2 CUP EACH: CHOPPED RED ON---------------------
-(Kraft)
2/3 c  Three cheese dressing
Mix vegetables and raisins.  One hour before serving, pour dressing
over salad; toss lighly. Refrigerate until ready to serve.  Makes 4
servings. Origin: Kraft What's Cooking Magazine, 9th issue. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
