Title: Chicken with Honey
Categories: Chicken, Poultry, African
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 t  Pepper
1 1/2 c  Chicken broth
Juice of 1 lemon
6 ea Pieces chicken
2 T  Oil
2 ea Onions, chopped
1 c  Ground, slivered almonds
MMMMM------------------------PREPARATION-----------------------------
1 T  Basil
1/2 c  Honey
2 T  Cornstarch
2 T  Butter
Salt & pepper
1 ea Clove garlic, chopped
In casserole, heat butter and oil and brown chicken, season with salt
& pepper if desired.  Remove.  In remaining fat, cook onion and
garlic until translucent.  Add almonds, basil, pepper, and honey and
cook, stirring, until mixture is well blended.  Combine broth and
cornstarch and add to casserole; cook, stirring, over high heat until
sauce thickens. Remove from heat, stir in lemon juice, and replace
chicken, spooning sauce over. Bake, covered, at 350 degrees for 1
hour, or until chicken is tender.
