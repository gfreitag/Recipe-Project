Title: EASY M/W PEANUT FUDGE
Categories: Candies, Nuts, Chocolate
Yield: 36 Servings
2 oz Unsweetened chocolate; 2 sq
1/2 c  Butter; or margarine
1/4 c  Milk;or 1/2 & 1/2)
1 ts Vanilla extract
3 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1 c  Peanuts; dry roasted
Microwave chocolate, butter, and milk in a 2qt. microwave- safe bowl
for 1 1/2 to 2 1/2 minutes at MED-HIGH (70%) <see* below>, until
butter looks melted. Stir until smooth and choc. is melted. Stir in
vailla; beat in sugar gradually until mixture is smooth and creamy,
but not crumbly. Sir in nuts. Pour into a buttered 8 inch square pan.
Chill to firm (20 mins). Cut into squares.
