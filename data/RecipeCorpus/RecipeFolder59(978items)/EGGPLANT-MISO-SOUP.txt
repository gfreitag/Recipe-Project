Title: EGGPLANT MISO SOUP
Categories: Soups, Japanese, Low-fat
Yield: 1 Servings
-----waldine van geffen vghc
2 tb Miso
2    Scallions; chopped
1 qt Hot water
1/2 c  Tofu; cubed
1    Carrot; chopped
1 c  Eggplant; julienned
In boiling wash, add miso. Mash to dissolve. Add carrots, scallions,
tofu and eggplant. Cook until veggies are tender. Source: Lightly
Ethnic
