Title: DUTCH PEA SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Dried green split, peas
2 qt Water
1/2 c  Diced celery
1/2 c  Shredded carrots
1/2 c  Chopped leeks (white parts
-only)
1 c  Cubed cooked ham
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
1.   In a large saucepan or a slow-cooker, combine peas, water,
celery, carrots, leeks, ham, seasoned salt and pepper.

2.   Cover and cook over low heat about 10 hours.  If using a
saucepan, stir every few hours to keep mixture from sticking.

3.   The flavor is even better if you cool the soup, refrigerate it,
then reheat the next day.
