Title: EINSPANNER (GERMAN COFFEE WITH WHIPPED CREAM)
Categories: Beverages, Coffee, German
Yield: 6 Servings
5 c  Hot Strong Coffee
Sugar (To Taste)
Whipped Cream
Pour coffee in stemmed glasses and sweeten with sugar. Stir until
sugar is dissolved.  Top with whipped cream.
From Sheila Buff & Judi Olstein, "The New Mixer's Guide to
Low-Alcohol and Nonalcoholic Drinks." Published By HPBooks, Inc.,
1986, ISBN 0-89586-458-4.
