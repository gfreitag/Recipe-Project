Title: EASY AS APPLE PIE
Categories: Tvfn
*****  NONE  *****
Yield: 1 Servings  :

Place bottom crust in pan. Add apples, sprinkle with sugar and
nutmeg. Top with butter, place top crust over vent. Bake 375 degrees
for one hour.
