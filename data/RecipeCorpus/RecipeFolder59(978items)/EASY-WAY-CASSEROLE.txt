Title: EASY-WAY CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 4 Servings
2 pk (3oz ea) cream cheese
- softened
1/2 c  Milk
1 pk (10oz) frozen mixed -
Vegetables,thawed,drained.
2 1/4 c  (10oz) frozen potato puffs
1 c  (5oz) cubed cooked ham
1 c  (4oz) cubed American cheese
-divided
1 cn 2.8 oz. Durkee French Fried-
Onions, divided
Beat cream cheese with milk in large bowl; add mixed vegetables,
potato puffs, ham, 3/4 cup of the cheese and 1/2 can of the onions.
Mix throughly. Pour into 1 1/2 qt. casserole. Cover and bake at 375F
35 minutes or until heated through.  Top remaining 1/4 cup cheese and
1/2 can onions. Bake, uncovered, 3 minutes or until onions are golden
brown. Source: Favorite Recipes ch.
