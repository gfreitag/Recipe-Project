Title: EGGLESS TOFU-PESTO QUICHE
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 19 Servings
1/2 pk Soft tofu (16 oz)
1 c  Pesto
1    Vegan pie crust
1 c  Mushrooms; sliced
1/4 c  Fresh tomatoes; chopped
-(optional)
Process the tofu and pesto in a food preocessor until smooth. Set
the pie crust in a pie plate and arrange the mushrooms and tomatoes
on the bottom. Pour in the tofu-Pesto mixture. Bake in a preheated
375 degree oven for 35 to 45 minutes.
