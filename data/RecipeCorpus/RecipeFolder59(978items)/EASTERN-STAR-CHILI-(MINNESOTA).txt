Title: EASTERN STAR CHILI (MINNESOTA)
Categories: Chili, Group recip
Yield: 1 Servings
1 2/3 lb Coarse ground chuck
1 1/3 c  Chopped onion
1 tb Salt
1/2 tb Pepper
4 c  Chopped celery, precooked a
22 oz Tomatoes; chopped
22 oz Kidney beans
8 oz Tomato soup
2 tb Brown sugar
2 1/2 tb Chili powder
Brown ground chuck and onions in a large pot. Add all other
ingredients. Cover and simmer for about 1 hour.
Makes 1 gallon.

Note:  I suggest you cut down on the sugar and celery.
