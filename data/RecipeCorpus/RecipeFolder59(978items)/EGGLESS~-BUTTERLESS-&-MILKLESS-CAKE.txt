Title: EGGLESS~ BUTTERLESS & MILKLESS CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sugar
2 c  -Water
1/2 c  Shortening
2 c  Raisins
1 ts Cinnamon
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Cloves, ground
Into a saucepan, measure sugar, water, shortening and raisins. Boil
for 4 minutes. Cool. Sift together and add remaining ingredients.
Bake at 350F for 40 minutes.
