Title: EGGPLANT & TOMATOES OVER RICE NOODLES
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Eggplant (cubed to
1/4*to*1/2 inch cubes)
1 cn Tomatoes (either use whole
And chop, of buy chopped)
1/2 pk Rice noodles (glass noodles)
Precooked
x  Salt (optional)
In microwave container, layer eggplant, salt, tomatoes, and rice
noodles in that order. Microwave high until eggplant is to taste - it
takes me 10 minutes on high. Mix everything and enjoy.

Variation: Use sticky rice instead of the noodles.
