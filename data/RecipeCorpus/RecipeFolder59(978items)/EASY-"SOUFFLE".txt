Title: EASY "SOUFFLE"
Categories: Eggs, Cheese, Kids
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Butter
6    Bread slice
1 1/2 c  Cheese; grated
6    Egg
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Dry mustard
Approx. Cook Time:
Fat grams    per serving:
:45
Preheat oven to 250F. Spread butter on bread & cut into small
cubes. In casserole dish, alternate layers of bread cubes and cheese.
In a bowl, beat together egg, milk and dry mustard; pour over bread;
stir gently. Let stand at least 10 minutes. Bake 40-45 minutes or
till set in centre.

Serves 4 hungry people with 6 as a brunch dish.
