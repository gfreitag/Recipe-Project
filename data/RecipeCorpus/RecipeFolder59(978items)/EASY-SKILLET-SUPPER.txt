Title: EASY SKILLET SUPPER
Categories: Beef, Cheese/eggs, Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onions
Cooking oil
Salt and pepper
2 c  Hash brown potatoes
4    Eggs
1 c  Grated longhorn cheese
Crumble beef into skillet; brown in cooking oil. Add onions and
potatoes, mixing well. Season to taste. Cook until onions are
transparent and potatoes are brown. Stir frequently. beat eggs and
mix with grated cheese. Smooth meat and potato mixture in pan,
pressing down gently. Pour egg and cheese mixture over top and cover.
Cook until eggs are firm.
