Title: EGG SALAD & CRUSHED CRACKERS
Categories: Eggs, Sandwiches
Yield: 1 Batch
Sweet pickles; chopped
Dill pickles; chopped
Mustard or Durkee's dressing
Mayonnaise
Louisiana hot sauce
Salt
Lea & Perrins
6    Eggs; mashed or chopped
Crackers
Make egg salad.  Crush crackers and mix together. The reason for the
crackers is twicefold--it stretches the amount (like adding water to
soup or gumbo) and it changes the flavor somewhat. From Justin
Wilson's Gourmet & Gourmand Cookbook
