Title: EASY "NO-BAKE" COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
4 tb Cocoa
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
1/2 c  Peanut butter
3 c  Dry oatmeal
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
Combine sugar, butter, milk, salt, & cocoa, boil one min. Remove from
heat & add vanilla, peanut butter & oatmeal. Stir together, then drop
by spoonfuls onto waxed paper.
