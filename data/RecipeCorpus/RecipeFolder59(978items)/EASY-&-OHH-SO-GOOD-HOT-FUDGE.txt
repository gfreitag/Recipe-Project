Title: EASY & OHH SO GOOD HOT FUDGE
Categories: Chocolate, Sauces, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
6 oz Chocolate Chips
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
In your microwave (at 40%) melt chocolate chips into milk. Stir
frequently, about every minute or so. This usually takes about 4-6
minutes. Just time to have someone dish out the Ice cream. Serve hot
and enjoy
