Title: EASY COCOA MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 14 Servings
2 c  Nonfat dry milk powder
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Powdered coffee creamer
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients in 1 qt airtight container or decorative gift
jar; cover. Makes about 4 cups mix or 16 servings. For single
serving: Place rounded 1/4 cup Easy Cocoa Mix in mug or cup; add 3/4
cup boiling water. Stir until mix is dissolved. Top with sweetened
whipped cream and marshmallows, if desired. Serve immediately.
