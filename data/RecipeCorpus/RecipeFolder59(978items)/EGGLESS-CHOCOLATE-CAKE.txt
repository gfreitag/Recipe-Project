Title: EGGLESS CHOCOLATE CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 2 Servings
3 c  Flour
2 ts Baking Soda
2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Cocoa
2 c  Sugar
2 ts Vinegar
2 ts Vanilla
2/3 c  Butter or Margarine, melted
2 c  Cold Water
Preheat oven to 350oF.

Sift into an ungreased 9x13 pan, the flour, baking soda and salt.
Combine cocoa and sugar in a small bowl and stir into the flour
mixture. Make 3 holes in mixture. In the first hole, palce the
vinegar. The vanilla goes in the second hole. The third hole is
filled with the melted butter. Pour water over all. Stir with a fork,
mix in flour with a spatula. Bake 35-45 minutes until done, test done.

*** Can be halved and placed in a 8 or 9 inch square pan.

Source: "The Yankee Kitchen" 03-24-93 (#1) [Marjorie from Conneticut]
