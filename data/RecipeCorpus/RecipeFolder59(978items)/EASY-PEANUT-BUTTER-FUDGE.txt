Title: EASY PEANUT BUTTER FUDGE
Categories: Candies, Desserts
Yield: 36 Servings
2 1/2 c  SUGAR
1 1/2 c  HEAVY CREAM
1/8 c  CORN SYRUP
1/2 c  PEANUT BUTTER
COMBINE SUGAR, CREAM, AND CORN SYRUP. COOK UNTIL SOFT BALL STAGE IS
REACHED ( 235 DEG F.). REMOVE FROM HEAT, ADD PEANUT BUTTER. BEAT
UNTIL FIRM. POUR INTO BUTTERED 8" SQ. PAN. MAKES 36 SERVINGS.
