Title: DUNGEON BARS
Categories: Cookies, Davidson
Yield: 32 Bars
1 c  Butter, sweet
1/2 c  Sugar, brown
1/2 c  Sugar, white
2    Egg
2 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Baking soda
1 c  Oats, old-fashioned
1 c  Raisins
Preheat oven to 350 F.  Cream butter with the brown and white sugars.
Beat in the eggs and vanilla.  Add flour, salt and baking soda. Stir
in the oats and raisins. Spread in a greased 9"x13" baking pan; bake
for 30 minutes. Cool slightly and cut into bars.

---*Catering to Nobody*
Diane Mott Davidson
