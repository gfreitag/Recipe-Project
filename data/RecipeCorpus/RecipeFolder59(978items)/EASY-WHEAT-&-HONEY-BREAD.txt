Title: EASY WHEAT & HONEY BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker, Breakfast
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM------------------MICHAEL HATALA  RJHP21A-----------------------
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
1 pk Yeast
3/4 c  Whole wheat flour
2 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
2 tb Honey
2 tb Butter; at room temperature
1    Egg; at room temperature
1 c  Plus 1 Tbsp. warm water
Put all ingredients in pan, select white bread and push start.

From Loafing It by DAK
