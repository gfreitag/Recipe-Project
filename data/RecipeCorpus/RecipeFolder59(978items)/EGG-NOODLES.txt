Title: EGG NOODLES
Categories: Pasta, Low-cal
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Flour
1    Teaspoon salt
6 c  Water or broth
1    Teaspoon baking powder
2    Whole egg substitutes
In a medium bowl add flour, baking powder, salt,
CAROLYN TAYLOR
and egg substitute.  Mix well. (Dough will be sticky.) Place dough on
a heavily floured surface. Knead until smooth. (Not sticky.) With a
rolling pin, roll out 1/8-inch thickness.  Cut into 1/8 or 1/4-inch
strips. In a 3-quart saucepan, add broth or water. Bring to a boil.
Add noodles.
Reduce heat to medium.  Cook uncovered 8-10 minutes or until done,
stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat. Drain. Place in a serving
bowl. Serve. SERVES 6
