Title: EILEEN'S CORN-CHILIES-CHEESE BREAD
Categories: Breads, Abm, Chili, Corn
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Flour, bread
3/4 c  Cornmeal, yellow
1/2 c  Whole kernel canned corn,*
1 tb Chilies, Ortega, chopped
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 ea Egg
1 tb Soft butter
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Sugar
1 1/4 c  Water, warm
Drain corn. Put all the ingredients, select white bread, and push
start.
