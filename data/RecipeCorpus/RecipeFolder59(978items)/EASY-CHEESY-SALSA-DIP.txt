Title: EASY CHEESY SALSA DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Pasteurized process cheese
-spread (8 oz)
1/2 c  Pace Thick & Chunky Salsa
1/4 c  Dairy sour cream
MMMMM---------------------OPTIONAL TOPPINGS--------------------------
Sliced green onion tops
Chopped cilantro
Diced red bell peppers
Combine cheese spread, Pace Thick & Chunky Salsa and sour cream; mix
well. Transfer to serving bowl; top as desired. Serve chilled with
vegetable dippers or unsalted chips. Makes about 1-2/3 cups.
