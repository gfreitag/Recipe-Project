Title: EASY SLOPPY JOE SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches, Hamburger
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Ground beef, browned/drained
1/4 c  Onion, chopped fine
1/8 c  Green pepper, chopped fine
15 oz Can tomato sauce
1 ts Vinegar
1 ts Brown sugar
1/2 ts Chili powder
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Salt
6 oz Tomato paste
Mix all ingredients and simmer 10-15 minutes.  Add water if too thick.

Serve on hamburger buns.
