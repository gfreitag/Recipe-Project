Title: EASY VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Gifts, Salads, Sauces/dips
Yield: 1 servings
Olive oil
Vinegar (balsamic or herb)
Salt
Pepper
Mustard
Garlic
Herbs
Combine olive oil and vinegar (balsamic or herb) 3 parts to 1 part or
as desired..  Add salt, pepper, mustard, garlic and desired herbs to
taste. Transfer to clean wine bottle and stop with new cork.  This
will keep for weeks in fridge.

Contributor:  "Good Things" Martha Stewart
