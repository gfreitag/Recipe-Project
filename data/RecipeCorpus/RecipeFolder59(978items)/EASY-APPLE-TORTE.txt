Title: EASY APPLE TORTE
Categories: New, Text, Import
1/2 c  butter, -- softened
Yield: 4 Servings
1/3 c  sugar
1/4 ts vanilla
1 c  all-purpose flour
8 oz cream cheese
1/3 c  sugar
1    egg
1/2 ts vanilla
1/3 c  sugar
1/2 ts cinnamon
4 c  diced peeled apples
1/4 c  slivered almonds
Whipped cream for garnish
:

Heat oven to 440. Cream butter, 1/3 cup sugar and 1/4 teaspoon
vanilla. Blend in flour. Spread on bottom and sides of 9 inch
springform pan. Combine cream cheese, 1/3 cup sugar, egg and 1/2
teaspoon vanilla and blend until smooth. Spread evenly over pastry
lining. Combine 1/3 cup sugar and cinnamon and toss with apples to
coat evenly. Arrange apples over cream cheese filling. Sprinkle
almonds evenly over the top. Bake 10 minutes then reduce heat to 400.
Bake 25 minutes longer. Cool before removing springform pan rim.
Refrigerate until chilled then garnish with whipped cream if desired.
