Title: EGG BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Milk
2    Eggs, large
3 c  Bread flour + 2 Tablespoon
1 1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Sugar
3 ts Red star active dry yeast
1/8 c  Wheat germ
1/8 c  Bran
Makes 1 1/2 pound loaf.

Place all ingredients in bread pan. Select light crust setting. Press
start.

This recipe works in our B101 Hitachi.  It makes a good sandwich
bread. Other machines may use slightly different proportions.

Adapted from: Bread Machine Magic, ISBN 0-312-06914-6, 1992.
