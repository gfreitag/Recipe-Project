Title: EGG & SAUSAGE CASSEROLE
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles
Yield: 8 Servings
8    Bread slices
1 lb Sausage
2 c  Grated cheese
6    Eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 c  Milk
1 1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2 c  Milk
Trim crusts from bread.  Place in a 13x9 inch Pyrex dish. Cook sausage
until red is gone.  Drain on paper towel. Put sausage on top of bread.
Place cheese on top of sausage. Mix eggs, 1/2 cup milk and dry
mustard and pour over cheese.  Cover and refrigerate for 24 hours.
Mix soup with 1 1/2 cups milk and pour over casserole. Bake at 300
for 1 1/2 hours. May be frozen.
