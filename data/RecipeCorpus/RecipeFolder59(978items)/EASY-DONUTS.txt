Title: EASY DONUTS
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
2/3 c  Milk
2 tb Powdered sugar
sift dry ingredients to - gether and add egg and milk, batter will be
thick. If too thin add a little more flour. Drop by spoonfuls into
deep fat and cook till browned. The powdered sugar is sprinkled on
the donuts after they are cooked.
