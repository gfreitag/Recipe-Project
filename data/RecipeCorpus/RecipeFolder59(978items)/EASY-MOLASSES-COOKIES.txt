Title: EASY MOLASSES COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Crisco
3/4 c  Molasses
1/3 c  Brown sugar
2 ts Soda
1 ts Ginger
1 ts Salt
3 c  Flour
1    Egg
Blend all dry ingredients, cut in crisco. Add molasses & egg. Form
into a roll & refrigerate. Slice thin and bake at 375 for 8-10
minutes.
