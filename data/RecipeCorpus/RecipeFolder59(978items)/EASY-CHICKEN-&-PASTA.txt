Title: EASY CHICKEN & PASTA
Categories: Ground, Meat
1/2 ts olive oil
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb ground chicken breast,
skinless -- cooked
:
1/2 c  onions -- chopped
1/2 c  red and green bell peppers
chopped
:
10 3/4 oz low-fat cream of mushroom
soup
:
1/2 c  water
1 c  twist pasta -- cooked
1 1/2 c  frozen mixed vegetables --
thawed
:

In a skillet, heat oil over low heat. Add chicken, onions, and bell
peppers. Cook until chicken is no longer pink and vegetables are
tender. Stir in cream of mushroom soup, water, pasta, and mixed
vegetables. Cover and cook over low heat for 5 minutes.
