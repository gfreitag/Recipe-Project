Title: EDIBLE PLAY DOUGH #2
Categories: Kids, Crafts, Play dough
Yield: 1 Batch
1/3 c  Margarine
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 lb Powdered sugar
Food coloring (opt.)
1. Mix first 4 ingredients together. Add sugar. 2. Knead it. Divide
and add food coloring. Keep refrigerated to keep from spoiling and
when not in use. *You can add other flavored extracts to give flavor
other than just plain sweetness.*
