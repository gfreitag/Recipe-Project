Title: EASY YUMMY FUDGE
Categories: Candy
Yield: 5 Pounds
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
14 oz Can evaporated milk
4 1/2 c  Sugar
7 oz Jar marshmallow cream
24 oz Pkg semi sweet chocolate
Chips
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Chopped walnuts
Combine butter, sugar &amp; milk. Boil 5 minutes; remove from heat
&amp; add remaining    ingredients except nuts. Mix well and add
nuts. Spoon into buttered 9 x 13p pans. Cool and cut; makes 5 pounds.
