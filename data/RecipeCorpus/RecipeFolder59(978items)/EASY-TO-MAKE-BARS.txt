Title: EASY-TO-MAKE BARS
Categories: Candies, Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 lb Margarine
1 c  Crushed Soda Crackers
1 c  Coconut
1 c  Chocolate Chips
1 c  Chopped nuts (optional)
1    - 15 oz can Sweetened
-Condensed milk
Melt the margarine & add soda crackers; spread in a 13"x9" cake pan.
Sprinkle coconut, chocolate chips & nuts evenly over it. Pour milk
over it & bake in a 350 Degree oven for 20 minutes. Jane P.
