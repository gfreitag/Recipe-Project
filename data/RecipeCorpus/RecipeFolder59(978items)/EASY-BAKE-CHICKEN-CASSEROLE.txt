Title: EASY BAKE CHICKEN CASSEROLE
Categories: Poultry, Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Boneless chicken breast,
-cut into 1" cubes
1    jar any flavor Rag=FA Chicken
-Tonight Cooking Sauce
1 c  instant rice,uncooked
-(extra rice can be added for
-thicker consistency)

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Place chicken in an 11" x 7" baking
dish. Add sauce, stirring to coat chicken evenly. Cover tightly and
bake 35 to 40 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked. Remove
chicken from oven and immediately add rice to casserole; stir to mix
well. Cover tightly and let stand 5 minutes or until rice is tender.
