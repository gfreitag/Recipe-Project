Title: EASY DOES IT MOLASSES COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Crisco
3/4 c  Molasses
1/3 c  Brown sugar
2 ts Soda
1 ts Ginger
1 ts Salt
3 c  Flour
1    Egg
Blend all dry ingredients, cut in crisco, add molasses and egg. Form
into roll and chill in fridge. Slice thin and bake in 375 oven 8 to
10 min.
