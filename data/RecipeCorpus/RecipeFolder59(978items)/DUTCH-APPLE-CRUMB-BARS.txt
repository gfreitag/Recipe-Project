Title: DUTCH APPLE CRUMB BARS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 16 Bars
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Cold unsalted butter
1    Egg yolk
2 1/2 tb Cold water
MMMMM--------------------FILLING AND TOPPING-------------------------
3 c  Peeled cored and chopped
Apples
1/2 c  Chopped, pitted dates
1/4 c  Flour
1/3 c  Rolled oats
1/3 c  Packed light brown sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Pinch of salt
1/4 c  Cold unsalted butter
Cut into 4 pieces
Preheat oven to 350 and grease a 9x9 inch pan. Combine flour, sugar,
and salt in a food processor. Add butter in pieces and process to
make a fine meal. Blend yolk and water. Pour over dry ingredients and
process until damp crumbs form.  Pat crumbs into bottom and halfway
up sides of pan. Bake for 25 minutes. Mix apples, dates, sugar, and
lemon juice. Spread evenly over crust.  Combine flour, oats, brown
sugar, cinnamon and salt. Add butter and rub it to make damp crumbs.
Cover apples with topping and pat gently.  Bake 40 minutes until
golden brown. Cool completely before slicing. From Farmer's Almanac
Cookie Lovers Calendar 1997
