Title: EASY LEMON CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Puff pastry shells
1 tb Oil
1 lb Boneless skinless chicken
Breasts -- in cubes
2    12oz jars
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Dried thyme -- crushed
3 c  Cooked, cut up veggies
Chicken gravy
Bake shells according to package directions. In med skillet over med
hight heat, heat oil.  Add chicken in 2 batches and cook until
browned, stirring ofter.  Set aside. Pour off fat. Add gravy, juice,
thyme and veggies. Heat to a boil. Return chicken to pan. Reduce heat
to low. Cover and cook 5 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.
Serve in shells.
