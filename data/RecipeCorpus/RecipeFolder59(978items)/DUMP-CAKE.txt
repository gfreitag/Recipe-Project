Title: DUMP CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Cherry pie filling or any
Kind you desire
1 cn Crushed pineapple
1 pk Yellow cake mix
2    Sticks of butter
1 cn Coconut
1 c  Nuts
Spread pie filling in bottom of a 9x13 inch dish. Spread pineapple on
cherries.  Sprinkle cake mix on top. Melt butter and pour over cake
mix. Top with coconut and nuts. Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour. Randy
Rigg
