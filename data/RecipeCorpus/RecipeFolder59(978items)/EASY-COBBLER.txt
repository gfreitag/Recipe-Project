Title: EASY COBBLER
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flour
3/4 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
3/4 c  Milk
Pinch of salt
Pie filling
Mix all ingredients in a bowl except your favorite pie filling. Pour
into a greased 9x13 inch pan.  Spread mixture. Pour pie filling over
top and spread.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes. It will
rise as it cooks.  Cook until brown. Randy Rigg
