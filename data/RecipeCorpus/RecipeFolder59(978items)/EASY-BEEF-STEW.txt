Title: EASY BEEF STEW
Categories: Meats, Soups/stews, Crock pot
Yield: 8 Servings  1 cn veg-all #303 can 1 cn chopped mush rooms 8 ea potatoes quartered
1 cn early peas #303 can 1 cn carrots #303 can 1 1/2 lbs beef stew
meat 1 ea onion chopped 1 ea toe garlic chopped 1/2 ts cajun
seasoning 1 ea bay leaf 1/2 cup sherry or red wine (optional) 2 cn
golden mushroom soup & 1/2 cup water Put ingredients in crock pot in
the above order. Cook on high 5 hours on high or 6 1/2 hours on low.
serve over noodles or rice. From: Mahlon Wall
