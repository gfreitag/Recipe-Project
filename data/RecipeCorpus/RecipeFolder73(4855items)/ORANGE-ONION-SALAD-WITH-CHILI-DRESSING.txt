Title: ORANGE ONION SALAD WITH CHILI DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Low-fat
Yield: 6 Servings
4 qt Leaf lettuce pieces
- (rinsed & crisped)
1/2 lb Tiny cooked shelled shrimp
-=OR=-
2 c  Bite-sized pieces of cooked
- Chicken or Beef Steak
1/2 c  Thinly sliced red onion
1 sm Cucumber; ends trimmed,
- thinly sliced
3 sm Oranges
1 md Firm-ripe avocado
1 c  Green or red chili dressing
Place lettuce in a large, shallow salad bowl.  Arrange shrimp, onion,
and cucumber on lettuce. With a short, sharp knife, cut peel and
white membrane from oranges. Cut oranges crosswise into 1/4" thick
slices; arrange on salad.  Peel and pit avocado, thinly slice
lengthwise, and place slices on salad.  Pour dressing over salad and
gently mix. Makes 6 to 8 servings.
