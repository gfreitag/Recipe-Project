Title: PEANUT BUTTER FROSTING TXBJ93A
Categories: Icings
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Peanut butter
1 tb Butter, at room temperature
l c  powdered sugar (approximately) Warm Milk, enough to thin and
give icing "spreadability" Beat together all ingredients with
electric mixer until smooth and creamy, adding the milk as necessary.
This amount of frosting will serve 8-10 (about what is needed to
frost a loaf cake. Della Masia
