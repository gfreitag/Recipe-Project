Title: ORANGE-MINT YOGURT DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Plain low-fat yogurt
2 tb Honey
2 tb Orange peel; grated
1 1/2 tb Candied ginger; finely chopp
1 1/2 tb Fresh mint; chopped
1/8 ts Salt
Recipe by: Miss Information <Lisa.Sepa@MCCADA.MAUICC.HAWAII.EDU> Whisk
together all ingredients until well combined.
