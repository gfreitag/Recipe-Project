Title: PIZZA STYLE CUBE STEAKS
Categories: Beef, Italian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 x  Cube steak
1/2 ts Lemon
Dill weed
Rosemary
1 sl Tomato
1/2 ts Salad oil
Salt/black pepper
Oregano
1 sl Provolone cheese
Brush steak with the oil and lemon juice. Broil 5 min. quickly on one
side. Turn and sprinkle with herbs. Broil 5 min on the other side.
Turn off broiler and top with cheese and tomato. Serve on toast.
