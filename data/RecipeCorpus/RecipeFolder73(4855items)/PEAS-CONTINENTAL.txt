Title: PEAS CONTINENTAL
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Butter
1 c  Mushrooms, sliced
1/4 c  Onions, minced
1/4 ts Salt
1 pn Pepper
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/8 ts Marjoram, dried
2 tb Sherry
2 c  Peas, cooked
Heat butter in skillet. Saute' mushrooms and onions about 5 minutes;
or till tender. Add salt and pepper, nutmeg, marjoram, sherry then
peas.
