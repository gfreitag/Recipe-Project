Title: PIZZA BREAD - BREADMAKER
Categories: Breadmaker, Pizza
Yield: 2 Servings
-Posted by: Pegg
-Seevers, GEnie
1 pk Yeast
1 ts Sugar
3 c  Bread flour
1/2 c  Sliced pepperoni
1/2 c  Cooked sweet (I use hot)
-Italian sausage
1/2 c  Whole black olives
1/4 c  Mozzarella cheese
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1    Egg
1 c  Bottled pizza sauce
1 tb Olive oil
1 ts Whole fennel seed
1/4 ts Cracked black pepper
1 ts Oregano (I use more)
3 tb Hot water
4    Cloves garlic, minced
Put all ingredients in breadmachine & press "Start".
