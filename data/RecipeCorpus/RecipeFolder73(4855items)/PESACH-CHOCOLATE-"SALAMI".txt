Title: PESACH CHOCOLATE "SALAMI"
Categories: Chocolate, Cookies
Yield: 48 Servings
3/4 c  Sweet wine
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 pk Vanilla sugar
3/4 c  Cocoa
1/4 lb Margarine
1 1/2 c  Nuts -- broken
4 c  Matzo farfel -- or broken
Matzos
Melt wine, sugar, cocoa and margarine,  Add the rest. Cool. Form into
rolls in foil and chill. Slice when cold.
