Title: PEACH PRESERVES
Categories: Condiments, Jams
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Peaches
3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
Peel peaches.  Remove pits.  Cut each peach in 6 or 8 pieces. Combine
sugar and water.  Boil 5 minutes.  Add fruit. Boil slowly until fruit
is clear and juice is thick. The Household Searchlight
