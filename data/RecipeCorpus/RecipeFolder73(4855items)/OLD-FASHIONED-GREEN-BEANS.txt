Title: OLD-FASHIONED GREEN BEANS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Bacon strips, cut into 1/2"
- pieces
2 lb Fresh green beans
3 tb Brown sugar
1/2 c  Water
In a large skiller, cook bacon over medium heat until crisp-tender,
about 5 minutes. Add beans, brown sugar, and water. Stir gently;
bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15 minutes or
until beans are crisp-tender. Remove to a serving bowl with a slotted
spoon.
