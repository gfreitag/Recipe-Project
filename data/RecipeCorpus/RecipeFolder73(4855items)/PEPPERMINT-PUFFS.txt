Title: PEPPERMINT PUFFS
Categories: Cookies, Holiday
Yield: 40 Pieces
2/3 c  Butter flavor Crisco
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Brown sugar; packed
1    Egg
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Peppermint candy; crushed
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Lightly grease cookie sheets. Cream
Crisco and sugars in large bowl at medium speed.  Beat in egg.
Combine flour, baking powder, and salt.  Blend into creamed mixture.
Stir in crushed candy.  Shape into 1-inch balls.  Place 2 inches
apart on cookie sheets. Bake for 11-12 minutes.  Remove to cooling
racks.
