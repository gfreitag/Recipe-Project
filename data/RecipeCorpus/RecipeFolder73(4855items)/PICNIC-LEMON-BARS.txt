Title: PICNIC LEMON BARS
Categories: Cookies, Bars
Yield: 16 Servings
1/4 c  Powdered sugar; sifted
1/2 c  Butter or margarine;
-softened
1 c  Flour
Pinch of salt
2    Eggs; beaten
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Baking powder
Grated rind of 1 lemon
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Flour
Powdered sugar
Cream 1/4 cup powdered sugar and butter until light and fluffy; add 1
cup flour and salt, mixing well.  Press mixture into a lightly
greased 9 inch square pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 18 - 20 minutes.
Combine the next 6 ingredients, beating well.  Pour over baked crust.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
Cool; cut into bars

Makes about 16 bars.
