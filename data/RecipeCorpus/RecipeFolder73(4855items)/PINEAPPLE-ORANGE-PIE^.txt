Title: PINEAPPLE ORANGE PIE^
Categories: Crs, Healthy, Lowfat, Lowchol
Yield: 8 Servings
CRUST
2 c  Oat bran flakes, crushed to
One cup
3 tb Orange juice concentrate
1 ts No sugar added orange
Marmalade
FILLING
1    Envelope unflavored gelatin
1 ts Unflavored gelatin
1/2 c  Water
MIXED WITH
1/2 c  Orange juice concentrate
5    Ice cubes
1/2 c  Crushed unsweetened
Pineapple
2 ts No sugar added orange
Marmalade
1 c  Lowfat yogurt
Preheat oven to 400F. Spray a 7 or 8" glass pie plate with pam and set
aside. Combine crust ingredients and press into pie plate. Cover
edges with foil and bake 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 250F. and bake
another 4-5 minutes. Set aside to cool. Sprinkle gelatin over
water/orange juice and heat until dissolved. Add ice cubes and stir
until melted. Add pineapple and marmalade. Whisk in yogurt. Pour into
crust and chill until firm.

Per serving: Calories: 120 Protein: 4g Carbohydrates: 25g Fat: 0.8g
Sodium: 28mg Cholesterol: 2mg
