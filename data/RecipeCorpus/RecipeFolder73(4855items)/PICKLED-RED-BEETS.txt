Title: PICKLED RED BEETS
Categories: Canning, Preserves, Etc.
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Brown sugar
1 qt Vinegar
Salt -- to taste
Cook beets till tender, peel and cut into cubes or slice. Bring the
remaining ingredients to a boil. Pack the beets in hot sterilized
jars, cover with liquid and seal at once.
