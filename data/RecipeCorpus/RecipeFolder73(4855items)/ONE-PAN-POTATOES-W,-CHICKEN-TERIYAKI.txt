Title: ONE-PAN POTATOES W/ CHICKEN TERIYAKI
Categories: Magazine, Oriental, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
4 md Potatoes; cut into wedges,
.  microwaved 8 to 10 min
.  or until tender
1 lb Chicken breasts; skinless,
.  boneless, cut into strips
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Green onion; sliced
1/4 c  Teriyaki sauce
While potatoes cook, in a large skillet toss and brown the chicken
breasts in the oil over high heat, about 5 minutes.

Add the potatoes; saute and toss until the potatoes are lightly
browned. Add the onions and teriyaki sauce; toss until heated through.
