Title: ORZO WITH PARSLEY
Categories: Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
8 oz Orzo (1-1/4 cup)
1 tb Vegetable oil
3 tb Chopped parsley
1/2 ts Salt
Bring large pot of water to boiling. Add orzo, stirring constantly,
until boiling resumes. Cook 8 to 10 minutes until tender, but firm to
the bite, stirring often. Drain. Return to pot. Stir in oil, parsley,
and salt.
