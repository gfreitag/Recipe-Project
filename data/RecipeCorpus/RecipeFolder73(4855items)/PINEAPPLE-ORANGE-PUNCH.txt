Title: PINEAPPLE ORANGE PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Unsweetened pineapple juice
2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 pt Any flavor sherbet
Fresh mint sprigs
Recipe by: From The New York Times Cookbook Although the recipe calls
for making in glasses, it seems easy enough to change to a punchbowl.
Mix the juices. Divide the sherbet into six tall glasses, add the
juices and stir slightly.  Garnish with mint.JM
