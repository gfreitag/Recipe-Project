Title: PEACH & BERRY KANTEN
Categories: Desserts, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Peaches, sliced
1/2 c  Berries (fresh or frozen)
-- blueberries,
-or strawberries,
-or raspberries
2 c  Apple juice
1 pn Lima Sea Salt
1    EDEN Agar-Agar Bar; OR...
2 tb EDEN Agar-Agar Flakes
Arrange fruit in a mold or bowl. Add juice, salt, and Agar-Agar to a
sauce pan. Bring to a light boil, then stir occasionallY for 5-8
minutes until Agar-Agar is dissolved.

Pour over fruit. Let set in a cool place or chill for 1-2 hours,
uncovered, until firm.

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cooking Time: 15 minutes Yield: 4 servings
