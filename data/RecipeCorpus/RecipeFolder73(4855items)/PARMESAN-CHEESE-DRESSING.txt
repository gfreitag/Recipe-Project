Title: PARMESAN CHEESE DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads, Dairy
1/4 c  Milk
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 ts White wine vinegar
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce

Gradually blend milk into mayonnaise. Mix in cheese, vinegar and
worcestershire sauce. Chill. Serve on tossed salad greens or on a
"meaty" chef salad.  Makes
3/4    cup.
