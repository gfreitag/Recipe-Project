Title: ORANGE SUNRISE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 3 Servings
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Frozen Orange Juice
-Concentrate
2 tb Honey
2 c  Cold Milk
8 oz Orange Flavored Yogurt
Combine the eggs, orange juice and honey in the blender. Blend until
smooth (about 1 minute). Add the milk and yogurt. Blend until smooth
and frothy. Serve at once in tall, chilled glasses.
