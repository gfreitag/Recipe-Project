Title: OPRAH'S CHICKEN SALAD WITH DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Chicken
Yield: 2 Servings    1 1/2 c  Chicken breast -- skinless
boneless
:
1/4 c  Peas
1 TB Golden raisins
1/4 c  Carrots -- shredded
1 c  Red cabbage -- shredded
1/4 c  Apples -- diced
1/4 c  Scallions -- sliced
1/4 c  Celery -- diced
DRESSING-----
:
1/8 ts Celery seed
1 c  Nonfat yogurt
1 TB Nonfat mayonnaise
3 ts Curry powder -- optional
3 TB Lemon juice
1 TB Dijon mustard
2 TB Shallots -- diced
Black pepper
:

Toss salad ingredients. Put dressing ingredients in blender and blend.
Serve over salad.
