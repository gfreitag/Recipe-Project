Title: OVEN-FRIED CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 6 Servings
2    2.5 lb broiler-fryer chicken
-s, cut up
2 c  Seasoned bread crumbs,
Cornflake crumbs or
Packaged coating mix
2 ts Paprika
2/3 c  Milk
1. Wash chickens and pat dry. 2. Combine desired crumb coating with
paprika. Coat each piece of chicken with milk, then crumb mixture. 3.
Place coated chicken pieces in a shallow, heat-resistant, non-
metallic baking dish with the larger pieces along the outside edges
of the pan and the smaller pieces in the center. 4. Heat, uncovered,
in Microwave Oven 20 minutes. Rearrange chicken pieces and heat an
additional 10 minutes or until chicken is fork-tender. 5. Cover
chicken with aluminum foil and let stand at room temperature 5
minutes. 6. Serve either hot or cold.
