Title: PEPPER & LEMON SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Lemon juice, fresh
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
4    Jalapeno pepper,chopped
2    Garlic cloves, crushed
Combine ingredients in small bowl. Let stand uncovered at room
temperature for 1 hour, or refrigerate covered for 4 hour. Serve with
