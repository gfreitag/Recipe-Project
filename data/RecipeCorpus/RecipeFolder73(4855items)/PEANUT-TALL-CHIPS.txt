Title: PEANUT TALL CHIPS
Categories: Cookies, Peanut, Chocolate
Yield: 31 Cookies
1/2 c  Dark chocolate chips
1/2 c  Milk chocolate chips
1/2 c  Shortening
3/4 c  Creamy Peanut Butter
1/3 c  Granulated sugar
1/3 c  Packed light brown sugar
1    Egg
2 tb Milk
1 ts Real vanilla extract
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
Powdered sugar
Heat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.

In large mixer bowl, beat shortening and peanut butter until well
blended. Add 1/3 cup granulated sugar and brown sugar; beat until
light and fluffy. Add egg, milk and vanilla; beat well. Add chocolate
chips (both light and dark. Stir together flour, baking soda and
salt; gradually add to peanut butter mixture. Shape dough into 1-inch
balls. Roll in granulated sugar; place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned.
