Title: OREGON HAZELNUT SATE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Oregon hazelnuts
-- roasted and pureed
1 tb Vegetable oil
2/3 c  Water
2 tb Lime juice
1 tb Lemon juice
2 1/2 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Red pepper flakes
1    Garlic clove
Combine all ingredients and blend well. Let set for flavors to
mingle. Sate can be thinned with warm water if it becomes too thick
while refrigerated. Use as a dipping sauce for chicken wings,
barbecued pork, vegetables or cheese. Also good brushed on chicken,
pork cutlets or fish filets, rolled in finely chopped hazelnuts and
sautÂ‚ed.
