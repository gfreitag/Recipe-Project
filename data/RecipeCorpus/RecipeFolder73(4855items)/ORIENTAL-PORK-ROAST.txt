Title: ORIENTAL PORK ROAST
Categories: Pork
Yield: 1 6 servings
4 lb Pork roast
1 tb Flour
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Ketchup
Mix ingredients together. Place roast in crockpot and pour mixture
over it. Cook on low for 6-8 hours
