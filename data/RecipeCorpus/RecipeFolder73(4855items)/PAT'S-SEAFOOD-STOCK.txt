Title: PAT'S SEAFOOD STOCK
Categories: Cajun, Seafood
Yield: 2 Quarts
2 qt Water
6    Celery stalks, cut into
2 inch pieces
2 md Onions, peeled and quartered
2    Garlic cloves
1/2    Lemon
Fresh shrimp heads, and/or
Shells, lobster heads, and
Or shells, fresh fish bones.
Use ingredients singly or in
Combination.
Combine all ingredients in large sauce pan or stockpot and bring to
rapid boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer slowly 2 to 8 hours
depending on strength of flavor desired. Strain before using.
