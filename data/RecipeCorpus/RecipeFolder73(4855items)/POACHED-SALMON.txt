Title: POACHED SALMON
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 2 Servings
1    6 Oz. Salmon Steak OR
Filet
1 c  Water
Rinse Salmon Under Cold Water. Pat Dry.  Place Salmon & Water in A 10
X 6 X 2 in Baking Dish.  Cover With Plastic Wrap, Turning One Corner
Back To Vent. Microwave Salmon At High For 2 1/2 Min. Give The Dish A
Half Turn, Microwave 2 1/2 Min. OR Until Salmon Flakes Easily When
Tested With A Fork.
Fat, 3.2, Chol. 30.
