Title: PEACH ICE CREAM SODA-MARTHA STEWART LIVING
Categories: Beverages, Fruits, Soup/stews
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Peach mixture (see step 1 of
-Peach Ice Cream recipe)
4 ts Sugar (or to taste)
1/4 c  Heavy cream or milk
8    Scoops Peach Ice Cream
Sodium-free seltzer or
-sparkling water
Mash peach mixture and sugar with a potato masher and divide equally
among 4 soda glasses. Add 1 T cream or milk and 2 scoops ice cream to
each glass. Fill glasses with seltzer or sparkling water.

Martha Stewart Living/Feb. & March/94 Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
