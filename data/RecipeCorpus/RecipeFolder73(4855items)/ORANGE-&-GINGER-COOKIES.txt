Title: ORANGE & GINGER COOKIES
Categories: Desserts, Biscuits, Fruits
Yield: 1 Batch
3/4 c  Shortening
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Molasses
1    Egg
2 tb Orange zest, fine grated
2 1/4 c  Flour
1 tb Ginger
1 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
MMMMM--------------------------COATING-------------------------------
1 tb Zest
1/2 c  Sugar
Mix all in order given. Scoop out balls of dough and drop into
coating. Toss, then place on cooking sheet and flatten with the palm
of your hand. Crystal coating of sugar on moist orange-ginger cookies
.... heaven is a hot cookie!
