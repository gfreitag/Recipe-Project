Title: PEANUT BUTTER DRESSING
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Peanut Butter; Chunky
1/2 c  Dark Corn Syrup; OR
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Milk
Stir the peanut butter and syrup together in a small bowl until well
blended.  Add the milk and then cover. Refrigerate until serving time.
Serve over fresh fruit and fruit salads. Makes about 1 1/3 cups.
