Title: PEANUT BUTTER BARS FOR A CROWD
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Dozen
12    Eggs
6 c  Sugar
3 c  Packed brown sugar
2 c  Peanut butter
1 c  Shortening
2 tb Vanilla extract
8 c  All-purpose flour
3 tb Baking powder
2 ts Salt
Confectioners' sugar
Cream the eggs, sugars, peanut butter, shortening and vanilla until
smooth. Combine flour, baking powder and salt; gradually add to
creamed mixture and mix well. Press into 3 greased 15"x10"x1" baking
pans. Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes or until top is golden brown.
Cool. Dust with confectioners' sugar.
