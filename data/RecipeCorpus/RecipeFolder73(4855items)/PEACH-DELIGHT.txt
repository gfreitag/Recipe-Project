Title: PEACH DELIGHT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Flour
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
1/3 c  Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
1    Egg
6    Ripe peaches, peeled & slice
Sift together dry ingredients, cut in butter until crumbs form.
Combine Milk, Vanilla and egg; stir into flour until moistened,
spread dough into 8" square baking pan. Arrange peaches in rows
pressing lightly into dough. Bake at 425 degrees for 25 minutes.
