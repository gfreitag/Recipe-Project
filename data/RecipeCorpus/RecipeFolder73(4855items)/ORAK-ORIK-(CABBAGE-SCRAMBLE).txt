Title: ORAK ORIK (CABBAGE SCRAMBLE)
Categories: Vegetables, Indonesian
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Oil
1 tb Onion; chopped
3 ea Garlic clove
3 oz Shrimp, small; cooked & dice
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper, black
1/2 ea Cabbage, small; very finely
4 ea Egg; beaten
2 ea Scallion; finely sliced
per serving: 205 Fat grams per serving: 6 Approx. Cook
Calories
Time: 0:20 Heat the oil in a wok.  Add the onion, garlic, shrimp,
salt and pepper and stir-fry until the onions are translucent. Mix in
the cabbage and then add the eggs.  Cook, stirring, until the eggs
are set. Transfer to serving dish and garnish with scallions.
