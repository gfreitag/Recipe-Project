Title: ORANGE-AND-GINGER BUTTERNUT SQUASH PUREE
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 md Butternut squash, peeled &
-- cubed
1 1/2 ts Ginger, grated
1 ts Orange zest, grated
1 ts Salt
Pepper, to taste
Put the squash in a medium saucepan and cover with cold water. Place
over medium-high heat and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer
until tender, about 10 mins.

Drain well. Transfer the squash to a food processor. Add the ginger
and orange zest and process until smooth. Add salt and pepper. Divide
among 4 plates and serve.
