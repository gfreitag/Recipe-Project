Title: PLAY DOUGH #1
Categories: Kids, Play dough, Crafts
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM--------------------NORMA WRENN NPXR56B-------------------------
1 c  Flour
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Salt
2 ts Cream of tartar
1 tb Salad oil
Cook and stir continuously until a ball forms. Add a few drops of food
coloring.  Makes one big ball you can hald in your hand. Put in an
airtight container. Work food coloring in while warm.
