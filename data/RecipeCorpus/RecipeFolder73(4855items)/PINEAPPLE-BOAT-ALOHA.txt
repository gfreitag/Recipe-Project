Title: PINEAPPLE BOAT ALOHA
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 10 Servings
2 ea Pineapples
2 c  Strawberries, sliced
2 c  Bananas, sliced
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Mustard
1 ts Salt
1/3 c  Lime juice
1 1/2 tb Rum
1 c  Oil
Cut Pineapples in half lengthwise.  Scoop out pulp, leaving shells
1/2 inch thick; set shells aside. Cut pineapple pup into bite-size
pieces, discarding core. Add strawberries and bananas; toss gently.
Spoon into pineapple shells; spoon dressing over fruit. Dressing:
Combine first 5 ingredients in container of electric blender; process
on high speed 1 minute. Gradually add oil, and continue to blend.
