Title: PECAN CARMEL MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins, Basic mix, Missouri
Yield: 12 Muffins
1    BASIC RECIPE
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Soft butter or margarine
Pecan halves
Mix as for original recipe. Blend 1/2 cup brown sugar with 1/4 cup
soft butter or margarine. Pat mixture evenly in greased muffin cups.
Arrange pecan halves in each. Use basic muffin recipe. bake in
moderate oven 375 for about 20 minutes
