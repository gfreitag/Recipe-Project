Title: PEACH MOUSSE
Categories: Desserts, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Peaches; fresh - sliced
2/3 c  Sugar
3 dr Almond extract
2 c  Cream; whipped
Peel and slice peaches, cover with sugar, let stand one hour. Wash
and run through a sieve. Fold in cream, whipped until stiff, add
almond flavoring. Pour into tray and freeze without stirring.

Source: Phyllis Grim, Chatham Grange, Medina County, OH Deerfield
Grange, Morgan County, OH
