Title: PINEAPPLE FROSTING
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 20 Servings
1/2 c  Water
3 tb Cornstarch
20 oz Crushed pineapple, undrained
Egg yolk
3 pk Equal
1/3 c  Chopped nuts
Dash yellow food coloring. In med. saucepan combine water, cornstarch,
pineapple and water; Cook over medium heat til thickened, stirring
occasionally. Cool for 25 minutes. Add Equal, coloring and nuts.
