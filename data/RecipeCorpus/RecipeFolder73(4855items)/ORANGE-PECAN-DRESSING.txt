Title: ORANGE-PECAN DRESSING
Categories: Rice, Misc, Dressings
Yield: 16 Servings
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 tb Chopped pecans
1 tb Honey or sugar
1 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Celery salt
Shake all ingredients in tightly covered container. ABOUT 1 CUP
DRESSING; 75 CALORIES PER TABLESPOON..
