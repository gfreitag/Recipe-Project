Title: ORANGE BARBECUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Microwave, Relishes, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/8 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/3 c  Chopped onion
1 1/2 ts Grated orange peel
1/3 c  Orange juice
3/4 c  Ketchup
1. Mix all ingredients in a 1-quart glass measuring cup.

2. Cook in microwave oven 4 minutes at High.  Stir twice.

Yield: About 1-1/2 cups.

SOURCE: Adventures in Microwave Cooking.
