Title: ONE BOWL CHOCOLATE FUDGE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 24 Servings
8 oz Semi-Sweet Chocolate
2/3 c  Sweetnd condensed milk
1 ts Vanilla
1/8 ts Salt
1 c  Walnuts
-chopped
Microwave chocolate and milk in 1 1/2 quart microwavable bowl on HIGH
2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute. Stir until chocolate is
completely melted and smooth. Stir in vanilla, salt and walnuts.
Spread in greased 8 X 4" or 9 X 5" loaf pan. Refrigerate until firm,
about 2 hours; cut into small squares.  Makes about 2 dozen. Tip -
recipe can be doubled; spread in greased 8" square pan.
