Title: PLANTATION PLUM PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Flour
2 lb Raisins, seeded
1 lb Currants
1/4 lb Citron; finely cut
1 c  Sugar, brown
5    Egg; beaten
1 c  Suet; finely chopped
4 oz Sherry or brandy
1 ts Cloves, ground
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Soda
1 c  Bread crumbs
1/4 lb Almonds; coarsely chopped
Sift flour on fruit and mix. Mix eggs and brown sugar, and combine
with flour-fruit mixture. Add all other ingredients, mix well. Put in
four 1 lb containers. Tie waxed paper covers on with string, and
steam on a rack set in a large pot with the water level just below
the rack for 3 hours. Flame, garnish and serve with hard sauce or
sherry sauce. Makes four 1 lb molds. Submitted By SAM WARING
