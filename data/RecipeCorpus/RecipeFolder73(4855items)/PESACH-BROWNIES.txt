Title: PESACH BROWNIES
Categories: Chocolate, Cookies, Brownies
Yield: 24 Servings
1/2 c  Cocoa
2 c  Sugar
1 pk Vanilla sugar
1/2 c  Margarine
1 c  Matzo cake meal
1/4 ts Salt
Melt margarine, add cocoa  stir till dissolved.  Cool.

Beat eggs till light, beat in sugars.  Beat in cocoa mixture. Mix in
matzo meal.

Put into greased 9"x13" pan.  Bake 25-30 min. at 350F.
