Title: PILGRIM PIE
Categories: Pies, Misc
Yield: 8 Servings
2    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Margarine
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Baking coconut
1 c  Oats
1/2 ts Salt
Cream together eggs, sugar and margarine and then add rest. mix well.
Pour into your favorite unbaked pie shell. Bake at 450 for 10 min.
and then reduce heat to
350    and bake for 30 min.
