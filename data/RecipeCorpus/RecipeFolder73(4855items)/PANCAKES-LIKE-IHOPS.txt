Title: PANCAKES LIKE IHOPS
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/4 c  Flour
1    Egg
1 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1    Heaping tsp.  baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
c  Vegetable oil
Preheat a skillet over medium heat. Use a nonstick pan, or use the
nonstick spray. Combine everything else in a blender until smooth.
Pour batter into the pan to make 5-inch circles. When the edges
harden, flip the pancakes. They should be light brown. Cook the other
side for the same amount of time. Recipe By
