Title: ORANGE SYRUP
Categories: Desserts, Sauces
Yield: 2 Cups
2 c  Sugar
1 tb Orange rind; grated
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
1/4 c  Orange liqueur
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 ts Vanilla extract
Combine all ingredients in a heavy saucepan; bring to a boil over
medium heat. Reduce heat to low; simmer, stirring occasionally, 5
minutes or until sugar dissolves.

Pour through a wire-mesh strainer into a small pitcher, discarding
orange rind. Serve with orange sections over ice cream or waffles.

Yield: 2 cups.

Source: Beverly Justice, Columbiana, Alabama ** Southern Living
Magazine -- June 1996  **
