Title: PEANUT BUTTER FRUIT DRESSING
Categories: Salad dress
Yield: 1 Servings
1 sm Banana; peeled and sliced
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Honey
2 tb Creamy peanut butter
1/2 c  Vanilla low-fat yogurt
Recipe by: Cooking Light Cookbook 1993; Oxmoor House. Combine banana
and lemon juice in container of an electric blender; top with cover,
and process until smooth. Add honey and peanut butter; procrss until
smooth. Add honey and peanut butter; process until blended. Transfer
to a small bowl; stir in yogurt. Cover and chill thoroughly. Serve
with fresh fruit salad.  Yield: 1 cup (33 calories per tablespoon).
