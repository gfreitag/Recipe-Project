Title: PLAYDOUGH #5
Categories: Kids, Crafts, Play dough
Yield: 3 Cups
2 c  Flour
1 c  Salt
2 tb Cream of tartar
2 c  Water
1 tb Mineral oil
Food coloring
Mix all ingredients in a saucepan.  Cook over medium heat, stirring
until stiff.  Allow to cool; knead.

NOTES:

*  Homemade playdough -- I got this from a friend of mine. It even
smells like the real thing.  Yield:  3 Cups

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  10 minutes.
: Precision:  approximate measurement OK.
