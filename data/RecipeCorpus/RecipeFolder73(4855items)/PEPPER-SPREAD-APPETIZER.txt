Title: PEPPER SPREAD APPETIZER
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Sweet red peppers, roasted
3 tb Cream cheese
1/2 ts Cumin, or to taste
2 tb Finely chopped sun-dried tom
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/4 c  Minced sweet onion (Vidalia,
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Chopped cilantro
Salt & pepper to taste
*Note: Sun-dried tomatoes should be oil-packed type. Process all in
food processor until well blended. Serve chilled with crackers.
