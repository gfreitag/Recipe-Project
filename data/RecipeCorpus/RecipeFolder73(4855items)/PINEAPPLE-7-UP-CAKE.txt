Title: PINEAPPLE 7-UP CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 10 Servings
1    Box Duncan Hines Pineapple
-Supreme cake mix
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding mix
10 oz 7-Up
4    Eggs
MMMMM---------------------------ICING--------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 lb Margarine
2 cn Crushed pineapple in juice,
-SMALL
1 pk Angel Flake coconut  (don't
-ASK me what size package...
-what you have will work)
Mix cake & pudding mixes and 7-up.  Beat well.  Add eggs one at a
time, beating well after each.  Pour into greased 9x13 pan, bake at
350 degrees 40-50 minutes, until the cake tests done.

Bring all ingredients to a boil, pour over cake.  Poke holes in cake
with cake tester to help in absorbing icing.

This cake is so moist it's juicy...and delicious!
