Title: ONE BOWL DEVIL CAKE
Categories: Chocolate, Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Sifted cake flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Cream of tartar
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Shortening
1 1/2 c  Buttermilk
3    Eggs
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
4 oz Unsweetened Chocolate, melte
Sift cake flour, sugar, soda, cream of tartar and salt into large
mixer bowl. Add shortening, half the buttermilk, eggs and vanilla.
Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes. Mix in remaining buttermilk and
melted chocolate. Beat additional 2 minutes. Spread into 9x13"
greased cake pan. Bake at 350~ for 40-50 minutes. Cool in pan.
