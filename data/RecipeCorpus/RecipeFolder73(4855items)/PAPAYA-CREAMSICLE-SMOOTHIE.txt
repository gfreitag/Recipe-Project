Title: PAPAYA CREAMSICLE SMOOTHIE
Categories: Beverage, Fruit, Healthy
Yield: 2 Servings
2 ea Papayas; ripe
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Vanilla frozen yogurt
PREP TIME:  5 minutes

1.  Peel, seed and coarsely chop papayas.

2.  Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor and blend
until smooth.

PER SERVING:  251 calories; 1.5g fat; 5% calories from fat; 7mg
cholesterol; 2.8g fiber.

Source:  Karen Greene in "Fast!  Fresh!  Fruity!" (New Hope Press) as
seen in "Delicious!  Your Magazine of Natural Living," July 1995.
