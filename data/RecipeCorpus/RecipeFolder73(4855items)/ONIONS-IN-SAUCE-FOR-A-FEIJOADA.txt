Title: ONIONS IN SAUCE FOR A FEIJOADA
Categories: Vegetables, Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lg Onion, sliced thin
1/4 ts Salt
3 tb Tabasco sauce
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Vinegar
Cover the onion with boiling water, drain, & rinse in cold water.
Drain well. Add the remaining ingredients & let stand at room
temperature 30 minutes or longer.
