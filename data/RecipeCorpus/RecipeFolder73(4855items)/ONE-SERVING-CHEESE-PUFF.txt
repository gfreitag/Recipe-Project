Title: ONE-SERVING CHEESE PUFF
Categories: Cheese, Side dish
Yield: 6 Servings    1 1/2 sl White or whole wheat bread
buttered
:
1    Egg
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Shredded process American --
cheese
:
1/8 ts Onion salt
1/8 ts Salt
6    To 8 drops hot pepper sauce

"This is an easy and delicious main dish for one." - Sharon
McClatchey Cut bread into strips; place with buttered sides down
along the sides and bottom of a 10-oz. custard cup. In a bowl,
lightly beat the egg; add milk, cheese, onion salt, salt and hot
pepper sauce. Pour into custard cup. Place on a baking sheet. Bake at
350 degrees for 35-40 minutes or until puffy and golden brown. Serve
immediately.
