Title: PINA COLADA FOOL
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Drained unsweetened crushed
-pineapple
1 1/2 c  Whipping cream
1/2 c  Sweetened shredded coconut
1 tb Coconut liqueur or rum (opt)
Mint sprigs (opt)
In blender or food processor, puree half of the pineapple; add to
remaining pineapple.  In separate bowl, whip cream; fold in
pineapple, coconut and coconut liqueur (if using).

Divide among 6 long stemmed glasses.  Chill for 1 hour. Garnish with
mint (if using).  Makes 6 servings. Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Canadian Living
