Title: POACHED FISH
Categories: Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Fish fillet
1 tb Parsley, chopped
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Dill weed, chopped
1    Onion, chopped
4    Lemon slices
In a large skillet, heat to boiling 1 inch of water with all
ingredients except fish. Place fish in a single layer in skillet.
Cover and simmer until fish flakes easily (about 5 minutes). Try
adding 2 tbl of vermouth (for standard serving size).
