Title: PATTY ANN'S HOLIDAY CHERRY SQUARES
Categories: Christmas, Cookies, Holidays
Yield: 1 Servings    1 1/2 c  Corn Flake Crumbs
1/2 c  Margarine -- softened
4 TB Sugar
2 c  Miniature Marshmallows
1 1/3 c  Flaked Coconut (Angel
Flake)
:
3/4 c  Maraschino Cherries --
chopped
:
1 Cn Eagle Brand Condensed Milk
1 c  Walnuts Or Pecans --
chopped
:

Oven 350=B0 F.

In a 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan, combine cornflake crumbs, butter and
sugar. Press down firmly with back of spoon. Sprinkle coconut,
marshmallows, and cherries evenly over crumb crust. Pour sweetened
condensed milk over all evenly. Bake at 350=B0 for about 25 minutes.
Cool completely before= cutting into squares.

Store in tin box between layers of wax paper.
