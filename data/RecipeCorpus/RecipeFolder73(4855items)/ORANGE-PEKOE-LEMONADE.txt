Title: ORANGE PEKOE LEMONADE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Freshly squeezed lemon juice
(about 5 lemons), plus
-wedges for garnish
1/2 c  Honey
3 c  Freshly brewed Orange Pekoe
-tea, chilled
In a small bowl, combine lemon juice and honey; stir until honey has
completely dissolved.  Transfer to a large pitcher. Add tea and 1 cup
cold water; stir. Fill pitcher with ice; garnish with the lemon
wedges. Serve. Yield: 6  Typed in MMformat by cjhartlin@msn.com
Source: Martha Stewart Living Magazine June 98
