Title: PEANUT DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Spreads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sour cream
3 tb Peanut butter
1 tb Lemon or lime juice
1 tb Soy sauce
1    Garlic clove;minced
1 ts Ginger root;fresh, minced
1 ds Hot pepper sauce; optional
Blend all ingredients till smooth. Use as dip for vegetables, pita
crisps, chicken wings or chicken fingers. Source: _Cooking with Milk
Calendar 1993_, Ontario Milk Marketing Board
