Title: PESTO
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
4 ts PESTO SEASONING
1/4 c  PARMESAN CHEESE
3 tb PINE NUTS OR PISTACHIOS, FIN
1 x  FETTUCINI OR SPAGHETTI NOODL
1 x  (COOKED TO PRODUCT INSTRUCTI
Mix and blend oil. cheese, nuts, and Pesto Seasoning and toss
with cooked pasta.  A little cream can be added for a smooth and
creamier flavor.  Serve at once.
