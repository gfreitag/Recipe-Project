Title: PLUMP SUGAR BEARS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
Brown Sugar Sweeties Recipe
Beat 2 Tbls. unsweetened cocoa powder into half dough. Shape dough
into balls using 1 Tbls. for bodies & smaller amounts as necessary
for heads, ears, etc. Arrange on cookie sheets, pressing parts gently
together. Bake 12-15 minutes. Decorate cooled cookies. Submitted By
