Title: ORANGE JULIUS (REAL THING)
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Cup  Orange juice
1    Cup  Water
2    Eggs
3/4 ts Vanilla extract
1/4    Cup  Sugar
1    Cup  (heaping) ice
Combine all of the ingredients in a blender set on high speed for
exactly 1 minute. Makes: 2 cups Posted by: Debbie Carlson
