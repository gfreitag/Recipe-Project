Title: OREGON HAZELNUT GRANOLA
Categories: Breakfast, Cereal
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  Oatmeal
1 c  Oregon hazelnuts
-- roasted & chopped
1 c  Flaked coconut
1/2 c  Wheat germ flakes
1 c  Raisins
2/3 c  Honey
1 tb Water
1 ts Vanilla
2 tb Oil
Yield: 6 to 8 servings.

Put oatmeal, hazelnuts, coconut and wheat germ flakes in a bowl, mix
lightly. Stir together honey, water, vanilla, and oil, then combine
well with dry mixture. Spread in a 3-quart, lightly oiled baking pan.
Bake at 325 for 20-25 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes. Add raisins
after baking. When cool, store in airtight container. Serve with milk
or eat as a snack.
