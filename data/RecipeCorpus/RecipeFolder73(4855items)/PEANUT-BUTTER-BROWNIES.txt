Title: PEANUT BUTTER BROWNIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 36 Servings
3    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Peanut butter -- any kind
1/4 c  Margarine
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
12 oz Chocolate chips
Chopped peanuts -- optional
Combine all but chocolate chips and peanuts.  Spread in greased jelly
roll pan.  Bake 25 min. at 350F. Sprinkle with chips. When they
soften, spread them over to cover cookies. Sprinkle with peanuts, if
desired. Cool and cut into bars.
