Title: ONE-PAN POTATOES & CHICKEN TERIYAKI
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Vegetables, Low calorie
Yield: 4 Servings
4 md Potatoes
1 lb Chicken breasts; 1" slices
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Green onions; sliced
1/4 c  Teriyaki sauce
Cut potatoes into thin wedges, microwave on high for 8 to 10 minutes.

While potatoes cook, in large skillet toss and brown chicken in oil
over high heat 5 minutes. Add potatoes; saut, and toss until potatoes
are lightly browned. Add onions and teriyaki sauce; toss until heated
through. Submitted By KEN BLACKMORE <BLACKMOK@CADVISION.COM> On SAT,
