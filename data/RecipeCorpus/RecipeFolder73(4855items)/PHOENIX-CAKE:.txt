Title: PHOENIX CAKE:
Categories: Cake, Chiles, Desserts
Yield: 1 Cake
CAKE
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Flour
2    Beaten eggs
1/2 c  Water
3    Pureed Habaneros
(fresh or frozen)
2 lb Can crushed pineapple
7 oz Coconut
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Walnuts
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
Icing
1/4 lb Butter
8 oz Cream cheese
1/2 lb Powdered sugar
2 ts Chile powder
CAKE Mix 2 minutes. Bake 350 degrees 35 - 45 minutes (until toothpick
in center pulls out clean) WALT
