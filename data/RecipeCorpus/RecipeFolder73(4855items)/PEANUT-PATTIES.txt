Title: PEANUT PATTIES
Categories: Snacks, Candies
Yield: 50 Patties
2 1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
2/3 c  White Karo Syrup
1/2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Raw peanuts
6 dr Red food coloring
1 tb Butter
Combine sugar, milk, Karo and peanuts together; bring to a boil. Cook
on low heat for 1 hour and 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove
from heat; add butter, vanilla and food coloring. Beat until thick
and holds shape.  Drop from spoon onto greased waxed paper.

YIELD: 50 patties

SOURCE: 1979 New Orleans Times-Picayune Recipe Contest Cookbook Typed
for you by Nancy Coleman
