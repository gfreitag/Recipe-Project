Title: ONION COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
3 lg Onions
1 c  Oil
1 tb Salt
1 ts Soda
1/4 c  Water
2    Eggs
4 c  Flour
2 tb Poppy seeds
Cut onions very thin.  Add all other ingredients. Roll very thin on
floured board.  Cut into rounds or squares. Bake in 400 degree oven
10 to 15 minutes until golden brown. Yield: 3 dozen
