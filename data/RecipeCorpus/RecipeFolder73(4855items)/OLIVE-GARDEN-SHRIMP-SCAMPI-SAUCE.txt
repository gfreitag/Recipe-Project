Title: OLIVE GARDEN SHRIMP SCAMPI SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
1/2 c  Italian salad dressing
1/4 ts Dry mustard
Tabasco
1/3 c  Ketchup
With wire shisk combine all ingredients until smooth. Keep
refrigerated, tightly covered, to use within a week. Freeze to thaw
and use within 4 months. Source: Gloria Pitzer.
