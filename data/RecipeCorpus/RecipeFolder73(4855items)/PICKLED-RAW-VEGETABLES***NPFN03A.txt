Title: PICKLED RAW VEGETABLES***NPFN03A
Categories: Low-cal, Vegetables, Appetizers
Yield: 2 Servings
1 ts Slightly crushed dill seed
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
1    Dash of hot pepper sauce
1 c  Water
2 c  Raw vegetablesc cut up*
2 tb Finely chopped pimento
*Can be cauliflorlets, radish slices, carrot curls, green pepper
chunks etc. Combine first 6 ingredients in sauce pan. Bring to boil.
Pour over vegetables. Cool, add pimento, chill about 24 hours. Drain,
store covered in refrigerator. 63 calories total.
