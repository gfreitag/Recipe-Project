Title: PAT'S SQUASHED PORK CHOPS
Categories: Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Pork blade steaks,3/4" thk
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
3    Squash,crookneck,med,sliced
1 cn Cream/mushroom soup(10.5oz)
2 tb Corn oil
2    Onions,medium,thinly sliced
1 pk Cornbread stuffing mix(8oz)
3/4 c  Water
1. Brown steaks on both sides in oil; sprinkle with salt and set
aside.
2. Add onion slices to pan drippings; cook until golden, stirring
occasionally.
3. Place half the squash slices in a roasting pan or in a 13x9-inch
baking pan; spread half the onions over squash.
4. Set aside 2 tablespoons stuffing crumbs; place half the remaining
stuffing over vegetables.
5. Repeat layers of squash, onions and stuffing.
6. Combine mushroom soup and water; set aside 1/2 cup and pour
remaining portion over stuffing.
7. Arrange pork steaks on top of stuffing; pour reserved mushroom
misture over steaks and sprinkle with reserved stuffing crumbs.
8. Cover with foil and bake in preheated 325'F. oven
45    minutes.
9. Remove foil and continue to bake 20 to 30 minutes longer, or until
steaks are done.
