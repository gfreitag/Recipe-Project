Title: PLAY DOUGH (GOLD MEDAL FLOUR RECIPE)
Categories: Kids, Misc, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flour
2 ts Cream of tartar
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Cold water
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 ts Vanilla
15    Drops food coloring of   -
Your choice
Mix all ingredients in a 1 1/2 quart saucepan. Cook over medium heat
for 4 to 5 minutes, stirring vigorously, until dough forms a ball.
Remove from pan and let stand for 5 minutes. Knead for 30 to 45
seconds or until smooth. Cool completely. Store in airtight container
in refrigerator.
