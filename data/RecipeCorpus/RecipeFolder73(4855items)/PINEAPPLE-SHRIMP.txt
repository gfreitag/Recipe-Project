Title: PINEAPPLE SHRIMP
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
2 1/2 c  Pineapple chunks
3 tb Pickle syrup
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Pickles, chopped
1 tb Cornstarch
1 lb Shrimp, cooked
1 x  Rice, cooked
Put pineapple with syrup, pickles, pickle syrup and salt in saucepan.
Dissolve cornstarch in a little water and add to saucepan. Bring
mixture to a boil, stirring until clear and thickened. Add the cooked
shrimp and heat through. Serve on rice.
