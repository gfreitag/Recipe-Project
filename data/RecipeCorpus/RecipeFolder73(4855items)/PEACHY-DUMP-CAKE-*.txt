Title: PEACHY DUMP CAKE *
Categories: Box cakes, Fruits, Nuts, Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRRJ67A---------------------------
1 cn Sliced Peaches; undrained
1 c  Nuts; chopped
1 c  Flaked Shredded Coconut
1 pk Yellow or white cake mix
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Margarine; melted
Heat oven to 350~. Arrange peaches with the juice in botttom of a
greased and floured 9x13" pan. Sprinkle cake mix over top. Sprinkle
nuts mixed with coconut and brown sugar on top. Drizzle melted
margarine over all. Bake 35-40 minutes. Serve with Cool Whip or ice
cream.
