Title: PESTO BREAD - CHURCHILL
Categories: Breadmaker, Italian
Yield: 14 Servings
400 g  Flour
1 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
2 1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Pesto
1 tb Olive oil
250 ml Water
pn Ascorbic acid
1 tb Parmesan cheese; finely grat
1/4 c  Sun-dried tomatoes; chopped
Recipe by: Pat.Churchill@bbs.actrix.gen.nz At the beep add the
sundried tomatoes.  Great loaf to have with soup.
