Title: PATTY ANN'S CHOCOLATE DIAMONDS
Categories: Christmas, Cookies
1/2 c  Butter Or Margarine
Yield: 1 Servings
3    Squares Unsweetened Baking
Chocolate
:
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla Extract
2/3 c  Pecans -- chopped

Oven 400=B0 F.

Grease 9 x 13 x 2-inch pan.  Melt butter and chocolate. Stir in
sugar. Add eggs and beat well. Add remaining ingredients. Mix well.
Bake at 400=B0= for 15 minutes.  Let cool in pan.  Cut into diamonds.

Store in tin box between layers of wax paper.
