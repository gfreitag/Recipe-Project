Title: ORANGE SMOOTHEE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 Servings
6 oz Orange juice concentrate
-- frozen
1 c  Milk
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
10    Ice cubes
Place all ingredients in a blender.  Cover and blend until smooth.
Serve immediately.

Calories per 3/4 cup serving: About 115
