Title: PEANUT BUTTER BULGUR COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts, Snacks
Yield: 60 Servings
2/3 c  Shortening
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  White sugar
1 ea Eggs
1 ts Soda
1 tb Water (hot)
1 c  Peanut butter
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Reconstituted bulgar wheat
2 c  Flour
Cream shortening and sugars, Add eggs, beat well. Dissolve soda in
water. Add ingredients to sugar mixture. Add bulgar and flour, mix
well. Roll in balls, press with fork. Bake 325 degrees for 15 to 20
minutes.

From the Kitchen of: Gary & Margie Hartford, Eugene, OR (1:152/19)
