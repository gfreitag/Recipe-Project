Title: PARTY CHICKEN BAKE
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Dry Italian salad dressing
Mix. (Good Seasons, etc.)
2 tb Margarine, melted
3    Whole chick. breast, split
1 cn Cream mushroom soup
3 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 tb Chopped chives
1/3 c  Sauterne
1 c  Instant rice
Reserve 1/2 tsp. salad dressing mix.  In large skillet combine butter
and dressing mix; add chicken and brown slowly until golden. Place in
9 x 13 inch baking dish. Blend soup, cream cheese, chives, and
sauterne. Spoon over chicken.   Bake uncovered at 325 F. for 1 hr.
Baste with sauce occasionally.  Serve over rice which has been cooked
in water to which the reserved 1/2 tsp. salad dressing mix has been
added.
