Title: PEPPY SNACK MIX
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 4 Servings
7 c  Life Cereal
1 1/2 c  Pretzel Sticks
3/4 c  Peanuts
1/3 c  Butter, Melted
1 tb Parmesan, Grated
2 tb Chili Powder
Preheat oven to 325Ã¸F.  Combine cereal, pretzel sticks and peanuts.
Add butter and mix well.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and chili
powder and mix well.  Spread onto ungreased 10" X 15" jelly roll pan.
Bake for 20 minutes stirring occasionally during baking.
