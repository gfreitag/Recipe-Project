Title: OVEN BARBECUE SHRIMP
Categories: Fish
Yield: 2 Servings
24 lg Shrimp, shell on
3/4 c  Butter
1/3 c  Ground pepper (NOT misprint)
French bread
Arrange shrimp in a shallow baking dish and sprinkle w ground pepper
and top w sliced butter.

Bake in 450F oven for 10 minutes.  Serve w French bread for dipping
and plenty of napkins for peeling.

Russel DeCarle's recipe, from an article in Modern Woman, Nov 1994
