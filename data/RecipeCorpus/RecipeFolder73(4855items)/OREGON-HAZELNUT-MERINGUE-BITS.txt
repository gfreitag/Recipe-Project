Title: OREGON HAZELNUT MERINGUE BITS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 48 Cookies
3    Egg whites
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 c  Oregon hazelnuts
-(finely chopped)
1/2 c  Semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/3 c  Saltine cracker crumbs
Beat egg whites and salt until very stiff. Add sugar slowly and keep
beating. Fold in Oregon hazelnuts, chocolate and cracker crumbs. Drop
by teaspoonfuls onto a lightly greased cookie sheet. Bake 13 to 15
minutes at 350.
