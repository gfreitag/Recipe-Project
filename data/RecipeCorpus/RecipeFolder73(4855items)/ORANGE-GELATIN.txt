Title: ORANGE GELATIN
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Gelatin
3/4 c  Cold water
1/2 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/3 c  Sugar
Few grains salt
2 tb Lemon juice
Soften gelatin in cold water.  Dissolve in boiling water. Add sugar
and salt.  Stir until dissolved. Add strained fruit juices. Pour into
mold. Chill.  4 servings.
