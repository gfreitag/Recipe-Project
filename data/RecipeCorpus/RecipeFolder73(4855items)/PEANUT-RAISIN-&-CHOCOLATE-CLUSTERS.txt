Title: PEANUT RAISIN & CHOCOLATE CLUSTERS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Egg white
1/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Raisins
3/4 c  Raisins
3/4 c  Chocolate chips
Beat egg white till frothy, add sugar gradually; continue beating till
light. Stir in rasins, peanuts and chocolate chips. Place in mounds on
cookie sheet lined with parchment (or buttered and floured). Bake in
preheated 375F oven 8 to 10 minutes or till browned. Cool. makes: 24
candies Source: Lucy Waverman in the Toronto Star
