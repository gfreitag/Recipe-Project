Title: PINEAPPLE APRICOT JAM
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Dried apricots
2 c  Crushed pineapple
3 1/4 c  Sugar
Wash apricots.  Cover with cold water.  Heat slowly to boiling. Simmer
until soft.  Add pineapple and sugar. Simmer slowly, stirring
frequently, until thick. Mrs. Lena J. Roberts, Norheim, MT.
