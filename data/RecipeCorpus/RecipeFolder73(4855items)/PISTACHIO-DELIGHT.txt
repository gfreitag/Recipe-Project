Title: PISTACHIO DELIGHT
Categories: Desserts, Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
1 pk Pistachio instant pudding,lg
8 oz Cool Whip
16 oz Crushed Pineapple undrained
1 c  Walnuts Chopped
1 c  Colored Mini Marshmallows
In large bowl mix pudding and cool whip together. Add pineapple with
liquid and mix. Add walnuts and marshmallows. Refrigerate for 8 hours
before serving. This is a very colorful and delicious salad or
dessert. You will get a lot of compliments.
