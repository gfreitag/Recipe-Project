Title: ORANGE-JICAMA SALAD
Categories: Mexican, Salads
Yield: 2 Servings
Butter lettuce leaves
1 tb Fresh orange juice
1    Large orange, peeled/sliced
1 ts White wine vinegar
1 c  Julienne of peeled jicama
Salt & freshly ground pepper
1/2 c  Chopped red onion
1 tb Minced fresh cilantro
3 tb Olive oil
Line plates with lettuce.   Tob with orange slices.
Mound with jicama. Sprinkle with chopped red onion. Blend juice,
vinegar, salt & pepper in small bowl.
Whisk in oil in thin stream.  Spoon over salads.
garnish with minced cilantro.
