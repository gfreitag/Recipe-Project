Title: PINEAPPLE SALSA 2
Categories: Desserts, Sauces and
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Fresh Pineapple -- diced
1/4 c  Red Bell Pepper -- minced
1/4 c  Green Bell Pepper -- minced
1/4 c  Red Onion -- minced
1/4 c  Fresh Cilantro -- minced
1 tb Fresh Lime Juice
2 1/2 ts Jalapeno -- seeded and
Minced
1/4 ts Lime Peel -- grated and
Minced
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl. Season to taste with salt.
