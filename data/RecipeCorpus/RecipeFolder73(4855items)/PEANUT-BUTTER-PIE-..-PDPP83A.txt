Title: PEANUT BUTTER PIE .. PDPP83A
Categories: Pies
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------EVENING SHADE----------------------------
1 x  Anna Lee Little
1 ea Baked pie shell
1 c  Confectioners sugar;scant C.
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/4 c  Cornstarch
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
3 ea Eggs; separated
2 c  Milk
2 tb Butter
1/2 ts Vanilla
Mix powdered sugar and peanut butter together until it feels like
cornmeal. Mix cornstarch, sugar, salt and egg yolks together; add
milk, butter and vanilla. Cook over low heat until thickened. Pour
powdered sugar and peanut mixture into pie mixture and stir just a
little. Pour into baked pie shell; top with beaten egg whites. Bake
at 325 until light brown for about 15 minutes. From the cookbook
collection of Joyce Burton .. PDPP83A.
