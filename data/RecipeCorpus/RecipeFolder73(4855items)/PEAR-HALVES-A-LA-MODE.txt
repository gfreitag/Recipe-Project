Title: PEAR HALVES A LA MODE
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pt Frozen vanilla yogurt
2    Pear halves
Chocolate syrup
Place pear half in a dessert cup. Fill center with a scoop of frozen
yogurt. Drizzle chocolate syrup on top and serve immediately.
