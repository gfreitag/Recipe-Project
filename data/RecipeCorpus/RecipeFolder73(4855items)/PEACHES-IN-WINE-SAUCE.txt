Title: PEACHES IN WINE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
8    Peaches
3/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Water
1/3 c  White wine
PEACHES IN WINE SAUCE

Scald and peel skins off peaches; leave fruit whole. Combine sugar and
water in skillet; cook 5 minutes. Add peaches; simmer 5 minutes. Add
wine; simmer 5 minutes. Baste 3 times while cooking. Transfer cooked
peaches to bowl; cover with syrup. Refrigerate; serve when chilled.
