Title: PINEAPPLE IN BROWN SUGAR RUM SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Alcohol
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Fresh pineapples
2 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Rum
Peel pineapple, remove eyes, and core.  Cut into chunks. Combine
water and brown sugar.  Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 5
minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add pineapple; simmer in syrup until
heated through. Remove from heat; stir in rum.  Pack hot into hot
jars, leaving 1/4 inch head space. Remove air bubbles. Adjust caps.
Process 15 minutes in boiling water bath.  Yield: About 4 pints.

From: Ball Blue Book Shared By: Pat Stockett
