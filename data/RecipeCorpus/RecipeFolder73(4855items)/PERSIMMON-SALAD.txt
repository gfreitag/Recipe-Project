Title: PERSIMMON SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Batch
4 lg Persimmons, ripe
Lettuce
1 c  Pears, fresh; diced
3/4 c  Grapes, seedless
1 c  Pineapple, fresh; diced
4 ts Pignolias; chopped
4 ts Walnuts; finely chopped
1 c  Mayonnaise; thinned with
Pineapple juice, fresh; and
Lemon juice, fresh
Cut persimmons into quarters, almost to the stem end, and spread
apart. Place on a bed of lettuce.  Combine pears, grapes and
pineapple and mound within the persimmons. Sprinkle each one with 1
teaspoon pine nuts and 1 teaspoon walnuts.  Serve with the mayonaise.
