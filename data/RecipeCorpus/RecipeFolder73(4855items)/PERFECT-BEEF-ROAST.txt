Title: PERFECT BEEF ROAST
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Beef
Yield: 1 Servings  Pre heat oven to 375 degrees. Salt and pepper room temperature roast
after rubbing down meat with soy sauce. Place meat on rack in shallow
pan. Roast 1 hour at 375 degrees. Turn off oven. DO NOT OPEN OVEN
DOOR. Allow 3 hours in oven to complete coking time (a little longer
won't matter). About 20 minutes before meal time, turn oven to 300
degrees to warm roast. Works with any size or type of roast.

Excellent for football games - cooks while you watch the game.

Enjoy
