Title: PEANUTTY SQUASH
Categories: Casseroles, Grains, Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Casserole
8    To 9 squash, thinly sliced
2 c  Medium white sauce
-- (recipe on cornstarch
-- box)
4 tb Grated cheese
1 c  Crushed, unsalted peanuts
Boil squash in a small amount of salted water for 5 minutes; drain.
Put a layer of squash in a buttered casserole dish. Spread with white
sauce and sprinkle with cheese and peanuts. Repeat layers, ending
with peanuts. Bake at 350 F. for 1 hour.
