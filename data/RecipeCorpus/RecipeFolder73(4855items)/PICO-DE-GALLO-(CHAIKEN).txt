Title: PICO DE GALLO (CHAIKEN)
Categories: None
Yield: 18 Servings
6    Ripe tomatoes, cut up
Coarsely
2 lg Onions, cut up coarsely
1 tb Salt
1/2 c  Jalapeno, chopped
6    Green anaheim chilis, and/or
Combination of green and red
Bell peppers
1    Clove garlic
1/2 c  Chopped cilantro (vital)
Mix, age for 30 seconds, and
Dive in.
