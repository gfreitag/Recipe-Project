Title: PEACH & COTTAGE CHEESE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 24 Servings
24    Peach halves
1 qt Cottage cheese
24    Prunes cooked and pitted
1    Head lettuce shredded
wash lettuce and shred.  Place about 1/4 cup on a small plate or large
serving platter.  Top each portion with a peach half, spoon of cottage
cheese, and pitted prune.
