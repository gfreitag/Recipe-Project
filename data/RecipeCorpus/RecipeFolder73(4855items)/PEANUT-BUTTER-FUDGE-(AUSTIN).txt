Title: PEANUT BUTTER FUDGE (AUSTIN)
Categories: Candies, Christmas
Yield: 2 Servings
3/4 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Soft butter or margarine
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Salt
4 c  Sifted confectioners' sugar
3/4 c  Chopped peanuts, or other
-nuts
Servings: 2 pounds

DIRECTIONS: Mix well: peanut butter, butter, corn syrup, vanilla and
salt. Gradually stir in sugar. Knead until smooth. Gradually add
nuts. Pack into an 8x8x2" pan. When firm, cut in squares.
