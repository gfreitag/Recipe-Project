Title: PILAF
Categories: Appetizers, Pasta, Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Fine Noodles -- broken in
Pieces
2 tb Unsalted Butter
1 cn Chicken Broth -- or beef
Broth
1/3 c  Water
1/2 c  Long-Grain Rice -- raw
In saucepan, brown noodles in butter; stir often.  Add remaining
ingredients.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat. Cover and simmer 20-25
minutes or until liquid is absorbed.

Makes about 2 1/2 cups.
