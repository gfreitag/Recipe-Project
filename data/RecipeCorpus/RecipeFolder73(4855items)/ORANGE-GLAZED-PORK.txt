Title: ORANGE GLAZED PORK
Categories: Low-fat/low, Pork
Yield: 2 Servings
8 oz Pork tenderloin
1/2 ts Coriander
1/2 ts Dried rosemary
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/4 c  Frozen orange juice
Concentrate
2 sl Bread
Turn on broiler.  Cover pan with foil.  Wash and dry pork - cut into 4
equal pieces.

In a bowl large enough to hold the pork, combine rest of ingred except
bread. Season with pepper. Add pork and coat well.

Broil pork for 10-12 min.  Serve with bread.
