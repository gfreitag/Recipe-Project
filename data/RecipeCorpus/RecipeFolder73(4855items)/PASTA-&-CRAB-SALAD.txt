Title: PASTA & CRAB SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Pasta, cooked and chilled
1/2 pk Crabmeat
1 1/2 c  Mayo
1    Garlic, salt, pepper
1    Onion, diced
1    ~~~~optional ingredients~~~~
1    Broccoli
1    Peas
1    Cauliflower
1    Carrots
1    Celery
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Let sit in fridge till chilled
thoroughly.
