Title: ONION DILL BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 12 Servings
-CHRISTINE ERICKSON MMVH58B
1 pk Yeast
3 1/3 c  Flour
1/4 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg, unbeaten
MMMMM-----------------MIX TOGETHER; WARM AND ADD----------------------
1/4 c  Water
3/4 c  Cottage Cheese
3/4 c  Sour cream
3 tb Sugar
3 tb Dried onion; minced
2 tb Dill seed; whole
1 1/2 tb Butter
In the order listed, place dry ingredients and egg into inner pan.
Warm next set of ingredients, add them. Select white bread, and push
"start". Lightly brush the top with a little melted butter at baking
time if you wish.
