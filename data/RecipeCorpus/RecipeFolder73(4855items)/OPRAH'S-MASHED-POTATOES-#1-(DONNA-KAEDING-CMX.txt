Title: OPRAH'S MASHED POTATOES #1 (DONNA KAEDING CMX
Categories: Potatoes, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Idaho baking potatoes
1 1/2 lb Red potatoes
1/2 c  Prepared horseradish, or to
1 x  Salt
1/2 ea Stick butter
6 oz To 7 ounces milk
1 x  Butter
1 x  Snipped green onions*
*green part only (for garnish) Place potatoes in pan and cover with
salted water. Boil until tender and drain. (Leave on all or part of
peel.) Using a hand masher, mash potatoes with 1/2 stick of butter
and milk and 1/4 cup of horseradish. Taste and add rest of
horseradish by teaspoon until desired taste is achieved. Garnish with
a dollop of butter and snipped green onions. From:	matejka@bga.Com
