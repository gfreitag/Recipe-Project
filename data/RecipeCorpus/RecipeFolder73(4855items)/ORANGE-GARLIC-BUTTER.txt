Title: ORANGE GARLIC BUTTER
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
4 oz Butter
1 1/2 tb Zested orange peel
2 md Cloves garlic
Salt to taste
White pepper to taste
Leave butter out until soft and whip until it turns white. Add
remaining ingredients; set aside. Just before serving, place 1 TB
orange garlic butter on top of stuffed romaine; melt. Suggestions:
Use as an appetizer; garnish with sliced blanched carrots in Grand
Marnier. Serves 4-6.
