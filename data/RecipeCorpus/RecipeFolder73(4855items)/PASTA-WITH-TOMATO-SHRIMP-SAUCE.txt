Title: PASTA WITH TOMATO-SHRIMP SAUCE
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 lb Pasta*
1 1/4 lb Plum tomatoes,ripe
16    Shrimp,large,cooked,cleaned
1/4 c  Basil,fresh,chopped
2    Garlic cloves,finely chopped
* - angel hair pasta or spaghetti

Cook the pasta according to package directions. Chop tomatoes.
Combine the tomatoes with shrimp, basil and garlic in bowl. Drain
pasta. Place pasta on 4 dinner plates. Spoon sauce over the pasta.
Serve.
