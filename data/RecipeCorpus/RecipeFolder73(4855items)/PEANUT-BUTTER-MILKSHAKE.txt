Title: PEANUT BUTTER MILKSHAKE
Categories: Beverages, Desserts
Yield: 1 Serving
Milk
1 tb Creamy peanut butter
1 ts Vanilla
Vanilla ice cream
Fill a tall glass or mug with as much milk as you wish (I use 1 cup);
pour into blender, and add the vanilla and peanut butter. Whirl for 1
minute; add 2 scoops ice cream, then whirl 1 minute more.
