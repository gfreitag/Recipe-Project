Title: PEANUT BUTTER-CHOCOLATE BON BONS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 20 Servings
1/2 c  Margerine, softened
2 c  Creamy peanut butter
1 lb Confectioner's sugar
4 c  Rice Krispies
1 lb Dipping chocolate
Blend margerine, peanut butter and confectioner's sugar. Crush
cereal slightly in a plastic bag.
Add cereal to peanut butter mixture and mix well. Roll mixture
into 3/4 inch balls. Chill for one hour.
Melt chocolate slowly in top of double boiler. Stir well. With a
dipping fork, dip each ball into the chocolate to coat. Place on wax
paper to cool.
Makes 7-8 dozen.
