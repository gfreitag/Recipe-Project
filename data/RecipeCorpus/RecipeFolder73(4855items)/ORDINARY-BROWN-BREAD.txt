Title: ORDINARY BROWN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2    Cuyp cornmeal
1/2 c  Rye flour
1/2 ts Salt
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
2 tb Honey
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Raisins
Blend dry ingredients, add honey, milk and raisins. Stir well, turn
into buttered bowl or mold that will fit loosely into pressure cooker
and cover with wax paper. Blace covered bowl in cooker, allow steam
to flow from vent for 20 minutes, then put indicator weight on vent
and cook for 25 minutes. Let stem return to down position naturally.
