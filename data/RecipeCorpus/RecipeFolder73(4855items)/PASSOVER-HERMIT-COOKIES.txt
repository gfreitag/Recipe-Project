Title: PASSOVER HERMIT COOKIES
Categories: Passover, Desserts, Kids, Nuts
Yield: 12 Servings
-RHONA ALTOMARI (KXPV54B)
12 oz Pkg Honey Cake mix
1 tb Instant coffee
1 lg Egg
2 tb Peanut oil*
1 c  Walnuts; chopped
1 c  Raisins
* (any KP oil) water (or orange juice) Stir coffee into cake mix.
Break egg into measuring cup. Add oil and liquid to measure 1/2 Cup
Mix as directed on pkg. Fold in raisins and nuts. Chill in refridge
at least 1 hour. Drop by tsp onto greased cookie sheet. Bake 15
minutes @ 300. 3 dozen Formatted by Elaine Radis; 3/92
