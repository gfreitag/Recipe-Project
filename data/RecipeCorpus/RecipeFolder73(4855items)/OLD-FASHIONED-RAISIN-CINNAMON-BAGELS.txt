Title: OLD-FASHIONED RAISIN CINNAMON BAGELS
Categories: Bagels, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings

3/4 c  Water
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Malt syrup, molasses or
White sugar
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 3/4 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
1/2 c  Dark or golden raisins, at
Beep*
MMMMM------------------------1 1/2 POUND-----------------------------
1 1/4 c  Water
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Malt syrup, molasses or
White sugar
1 ts Salt
2 ts Cinnamon
3 1/3 c  Bread flour
2 ts Active dry yeast
Dark or golden raisins, at
Beep*
*Or five minutes before the end of the kneading phase, or work into
dough.

The familiar, or old-fashioned, raisin cinnamon white bagel is a
"can't miss" appetite-pleaser. Knead in another tablespoon or so of
cinamon before shaping the bagel for a marbleized look.

The Best Bagels are made at home by Dona Z. Meilach
