Title: PERSIMMON PUDDING 4
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Persimmon pulp
2 c  Flour
3 c  Milk
2 c  White sugar
1 c  Brown sugar
6 oz Margarine
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
3    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Pumpkin pie spice
1/2 ts Cinnamon
mix all ingred. together. pour into greased pyrex dish. Bake at 375
deg. for 40 min., reduce heat to
350    for another 10 min.
