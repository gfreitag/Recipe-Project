Title: PAPPA'S ITALIAN ANCHOVY GARLIC SPREAD
Categories: Breads, Spreads, Cake mix
Yield: 6 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
1    Tin anchovies
1 tb Olive oil
5 ml Garlic
Parmesan cheese
Saute garlic in oil. Add anchovies and cook for a few minutes. Put in
blender and puree. Spread on pieces of Italian bread, sprinkle with
Parmesan cheese and place under broilerfor a minute or until cheese is
bubbly. Depending on how thick you spread the bread, it will serve 6
to 8. Source:  Ask Your Neighbor
