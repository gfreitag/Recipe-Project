Title: ORANGE CREAMS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 36 Servings
2 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1 tb Grated orange peel
3 ts Frozen orange juice
-concentrate
-additional powdered sugar
-for rolling
Line baking sheet with buttered wax paper; set aside. Combine 2 1/4 c
sugar, orange peel and orange juice concentrate in small bowl, adding
more sugar if needed to make a stiff dough. Form dough into ball;
wrap in plastic wrap.  Refrigerate 30 minutes.  Roll out dough to 1/4
inch thickness on a surface covered with powdered sugar. Cut into 1
1/2 inch shapes.  [cook's note - canape cutters work well] Arrange on
prepared baking sheet.  Refrigerate, uncovered, overnight. Store in
refrigerator in tightly sealed container between layers of waxed
paper.
