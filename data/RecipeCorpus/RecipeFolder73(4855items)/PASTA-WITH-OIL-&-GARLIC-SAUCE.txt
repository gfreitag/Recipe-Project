Title: PASTA WITH OIL & GARLIC SAUCE
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 lb Uncooked Pasta
1/3 c  Olive Oil
4    Garlic Cloves
1/2 ts Basil (fresh, chopped)
1/4 c  Parsley (fresh, chopped)
1/2 c  Parmesan Cheese
1) Cook pasta until Al Dente, keep warm 2) Heat oil in 10" skillet
over low heat. Add garlic, cook slowly and
gently till light gold. Remove from heat and stir in parsley and
basil. 3) Add hot pasta to skillet, toss to coat evenly. 4) Serve
immediately with cheese.
