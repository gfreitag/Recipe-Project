Title: ONION SOUP (COOKS GARDEN)
Categories: Soups
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Sliced onions
1 tb Butter
2 qt Homemade beef stock
4 tb White wine
Cheese
Homemade French bread
Saute onions in butter til soft and golden.  Add onions to beef stock
and simmer 1 hour.  Season with white wine and salt and pepper to
taste.

Bake cheese-topped slices of French bread in a 350 F degree oven til
browned.  Place a slice in each soup bowl, add hot soup, and enjoy.

From: The Cook's Garden catalog - Spring/Summer 1990 - page 50
