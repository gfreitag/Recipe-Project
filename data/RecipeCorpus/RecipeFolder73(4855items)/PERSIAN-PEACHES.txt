Title: PERSIAN PEACHES
Categories: Fruits, Sauces
Yield: 5 Servings
4 c  Sliced peaches
1/2 c  Orange juice
3 tb Honey
2 tb Finely chopped
Candied ginger
1 ds Salt
Candied ginger adds delightful zingo. Serve plain or over vanilla ice
cream.

Combine all ingredients, mixing gently. Cover; chill thoroughly.
Spoon into chilled sherbet glasses. Makes
5    servings.
