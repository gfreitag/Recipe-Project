Title: PLANTATION HOT SAUCE #2
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Prepared mustard
1/2 c  Vinegar or lemon juice
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 ts Bottled hot pepper sauce
1 ts Salt
Blend molasses and prepared mustard; stir in remaining ingredients.
Heat to boiling.  Makes enough sauce for basting 4 pounds loin back
ribs or spareribs.

Source:  BH & G, Barbecue Book, 1965
