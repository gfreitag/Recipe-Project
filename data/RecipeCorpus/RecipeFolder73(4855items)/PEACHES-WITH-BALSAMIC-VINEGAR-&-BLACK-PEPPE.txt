Title: PEACHES WITH BALSAMIC VINEGAR & BLACK PEPPE
Categories: New, Text, Import
4    perfectly ripe fresh peaches
or nectarines
Yield: 4 Servings
:
4 TB high quality balsamic
vinegar
:
1 TB freshly ground black pepper
1 ts sugar

Wash and drain peaches. Slice peaches into 1/2-inch thick slices and
place in a large mixing bowl. Add vinegar, pepper and sugar and
gently toss to coat. Divide among 4 martini glasses and serve.

Yield: 4 servings
