Title: PARTY MIX (KAREN KUGELMAN'S)
Categories: Snacks, Entertain
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Cherrios
3 c  Rice chex
3 c  Wheat chex
3 c  Corn chex
3 c  Thin pretzels
1/3 c  Butter
4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Onion salt.
1 ts Celery salt
Melt butter, add rest of seasonings.  Pour over mixed cereals in
roaster pans.  Bake in 350 over for 1 hour, stirring occasionally
(every 10 minutes or so). Reformatted by: CYGNUS, HCPM52C
