Title: PINEAPPLE SHERBET #2
Categories: Desserts, Osg
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Water
1 c  Pineapple; crushed
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 c  Milk
Combine sugar, salt and water. Boil 5 minutes. Cool, add pineapple,
milk and lemon juice. Mix well. Freeze. Serves 6. Source: Mrs. Ben
Nelson, Union Grange, Richland County, OH
