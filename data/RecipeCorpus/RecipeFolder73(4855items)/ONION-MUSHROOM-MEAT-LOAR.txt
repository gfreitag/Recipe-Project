Title: ONION MUSHROOM MEAT LOAR
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Ground beef, Soups
Yield: 8 Servings
1    Envelope lipton onion mushrm
Soup mix
2 lb Ground beef
1 1/2 c  Fresh bread crumbs
2    Eggs
3/4 c  Water

in large baking pan, shaped into loaf. Bake 1 hour or until done
