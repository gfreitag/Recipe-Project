Title: OLIVE TAPENADE
Categories: Appetizers, Dips, Fish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 ea Anchovy Fillets
1 c  Black Olives; Chopped
4 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Fresh Parsley; Chopped, OR
4 ts Dried Parsley; Crushed
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Garlic; Minced
3 tb Fresh Basil; Chopped, OR
1 tb Dried Basil; Crushed
Chop and mash the anchovies on a cutting board.  Put them into a bowl
and mix with the olives and worcestershire sauce. Blend in the
mayonnaise and add all the rest of the ingredients, blending well.
Cover and chill. Makes about 2 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS:
Scallions, French Bread, Seafood
