Title: PEANUT BUTTER 'N HONEY COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 4 Dozen
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg, well-beaten
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1 ts Salt
2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
Cream shortening, honey and sugar together till light and fluffy. Add
well-beaten egg. Add peanut butter and salt. Stir in flour and soda
sifted together and mix well. Form into small balls of dough and
place on greased cookie sheet. Press with fork. Bake at 350F for 8 to
10 minutes.
