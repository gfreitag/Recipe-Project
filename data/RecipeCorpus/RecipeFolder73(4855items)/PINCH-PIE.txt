Title: PINCH PIE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
3    Egg whites
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Vinegar
1 ts Water
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Sugar
Combine in bowl of mixmaster first 6 ingredients. Beat at high speed
until stiff. Add 1 cup of sugar a little at a time. Shape like a pie
shell on greased cookie sheet or form small amounts like birds nests
and bake in 275 oven 1 hour, turn off oven and leave in oven to dry.
Serve with ice cream or fruit.
