Title: ONION-CHEESE DIP MIX
Categories: Beef, Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Instant Minced Onion
1/2 ts Instant Beef Bouillon
1 tb Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/4 ts Garlic Salt
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl; blend well. Spoon mixture
onto a 6-inch square of aluminum foil and fold to make airtight.
Label as Onion-Cheese Dip Mix. Store in a cool dry place and use
within 3 to 4 months.

Makes 1 package (about 3 T) of mix.  This recipe may be increased to
make more packages of mix.

Onion-Cheese Dip:  Combine 1 cup sour cream with 1 package of mix.
Chill at least 1 hour before serving.

Makes about 1 cup of dip.

VARIATION:  Substitute 1 cup cottage cheese or 1 (8 oz) package of
softened cream cheese for sour cream.
