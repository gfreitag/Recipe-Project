Title: PET PARTY MIX
Categories: Pets, Pet food
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Cheerios
2 c  Chex or Crispex
2 ts Dry gravy mix
1/2 c  Imitation bacon bits
2 c  Spoon-size Shredded Wheat
1/2 c  Melted butter or margerine
1/2 c  Grated American Cheese
Powder
1 c  Dog treats (pupperoni, Jerky
Treats, etc.)
1    Preheat oven to 130 =F8C .
2.  Pour melted butter/margerine into a 33x23 cm baking pan. Stir in
cheese, bacon bits, and gravy mix. Add cereal and stir until all
pieces are coated.

3.  Heat until crisp, approx. 45 min.

4.  Let cool and store in tightly sealed container.

Difficulty    : very easy. Precision
: measure ingredients.
