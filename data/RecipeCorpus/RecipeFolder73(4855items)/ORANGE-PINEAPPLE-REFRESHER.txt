Title: ORANGE PINEAPPLE REFRESHER
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM--------------------BEST OF GOURMET III-------------------------
4 c  Fresh orange juice
1 1/2 c  Unsweetened pineapple juice
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice
Grenadine to taste
In pitcher stir together the orange juice, pineapple juice, and lemon
juice. Fill tall glasses with ice, drizzle a little grenadine into
each glass, and pour orange juice mixture. S: Best of Gourmet III
