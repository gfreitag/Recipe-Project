Title: ONION CHEDDAR SOUP - (WINNER)
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
4 c  Low fat milk
2 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
-process cheese
Cook onion in butter until tender, but not brown. Blend in flour,
salt and pepper. Add milk all at once. Bring to a boil, stirrlng
constantly. Remove from heat and add shredded cheese, stirring until
melted. Garnish each serving with paprika and snipped chives. Makes 6
servings.

Source: Kitty Graden, Winner of 16th Annual Cookbook The Cameron
Citzen Observer, MO.
