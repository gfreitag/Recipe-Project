Title: ORIENTAL SPICE MIXTURE
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
3/4 ts Turmeric
2 ts Paprika
1 1/2 c  Sugar
A few grains dry mustard
2 ts Salt
MMMMM------------------------PREPARATION-----------------------------
Mix all ingredients and shake well.  Makes 1 1/2 cups. Source:
Cookbook USA
