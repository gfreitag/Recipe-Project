Title: PHEASANT & WILD RICE
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 5 Servings
1 c  Raw wild rice
1 cn Cr. of chicken soup
1 cn Cr. of mushrooms soup
1 cn Mushrooms
2 1/2 c  Water
Water chestnuts
2    Pheasants; cut up, flour and
Brown.
1 pk Instant onion soup mix
Mix rice, canned soup, water, mushrooms and water chestnuts in 9x13"
glass casserole.  Add pheasant. Sprinkle with onion soup mix. Cover
lightly with foil.  Bake 2 - 2 1/2 hours at 300 degrees.

Andrea Leafblad, St Could MN Herberger's cookbook.
