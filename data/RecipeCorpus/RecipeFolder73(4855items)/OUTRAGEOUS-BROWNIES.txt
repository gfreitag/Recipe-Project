Title: OUTRAGEOUS BROWNIES
Categories: Brownies, Chocolate
Yield: 24 Servings
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
2    Eggs; beaten
1/4 c  Water; cold
1    Fudge Brownie mix
7 oz Chocolate bars
1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
Preheat oven to 350~. Mix together miracle whip, eggs and water until
well beaten. Add brownie mix, mixing until moistened. Coarsely chop
two chocolate bars; stir into brownie mix along with chopped nuts.
Pour into a greased 13 x 9 baking dish. Bake 30-35 minutes or until
edges pull away from sides. Immediately top with the remaining
chopped chocolate bar. Let stand 5 minutes or until chocolate is
melted; spread evenly over brownies. Garnish with chopped nuts if
desired. Cool. Cut into squares.
