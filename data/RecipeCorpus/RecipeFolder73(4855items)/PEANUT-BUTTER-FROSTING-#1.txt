Title: PEANUT BUTTER FROSTING #1
Categories: Icings
Yield: 16 Servings
3 c  Powdered sugar
1/3 c  Peanut butter
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Milk; or more
Mix powdered sugar and peanut butter. Stir in vanilla and milk. Beat
until smooth and of spreading consistency. Frosts a 13 X 9 inch cake
or fills and frosts two 8- or 9-inch cake layers. 16 SERVINGS; 125
CALORIES PER SERVING.
