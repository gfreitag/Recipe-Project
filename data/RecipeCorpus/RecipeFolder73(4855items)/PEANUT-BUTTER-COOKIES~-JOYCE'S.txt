Title: PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES~ JOYCE'S
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Peanut butter
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown sugar
2    Eggs
2 3/4 c  Flour
2 ts Soda
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
Mix all to - gether and form into small balls and flatten with fork.
Bake in 350 oven 12 - 15 minutes
