Title: PEACHES & CREAM RICE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Rice
Yield: 50 Servings
1 pk Uncle Ben's Brand Chicken
-Flavor Rice Pilaf
2 qt Water
5 c  Diced Peaches, drained
4 c  Whipped topping
2 c  Shredded coconut
1. Cook rice accoding to package directions. 2. Allow rice to cool.
3. Fold in diced peaches, whipped topping and coconut. 4. Refrigerate
until ready for serving. Measurements and timing are designed only
for Uncle Ben's Fast Cooking Rice Pilaf, Original Rice Pilaf, Fast
Cooking Spanish Rice, Long Grain & Wild Rice Blend, and CONVERTED
Brand Rice
