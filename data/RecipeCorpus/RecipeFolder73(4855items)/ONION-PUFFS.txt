Title: ONION PUFFS
Categories: Appetizers, Breads, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 Recipe
1    Loaf bread, white or rye
8 oz Cream cheese
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Cheese,Parmesan
1 1/2 tb Onion, grated or minced
1 1/2 tb Chives; chopped
Salt
Pepper
Cut slices of bread into circles with a donut cutter/cookie cutter.
Place circles on cookie sheet.
Place in 250-deg. oen or let stand on cupboard to dry. Mix cream
cheese with mayonnaise and add remaining ingrdients. Spread over
bread circles.
