Title: PENOCHE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Cream
2 tb Butter
1/3 c  Coconut
1/3 c  Chopped dates
1/3 c  Chopped nuts
Combine sugar, cream, and butter.  Boil to soft ball stage (234 - 238
F). Remove from fire.  Cool to room temperature. Beat until creamy.
Add fruit, coconut, and nuts. Continue beating until mixture will
hold its shape. Pour into well-buttered, shallow pan. Cut in squares.
Myrtle Howell, Gillett, WI.
