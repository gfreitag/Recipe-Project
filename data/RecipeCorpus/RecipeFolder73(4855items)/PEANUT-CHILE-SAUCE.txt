Title: PEANUT-CHILE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ea Green serano chile, seeds
1 x  Removed, roasted and peeled
1/4 ts Piquins, or crushed red
1 x  Pepper, seeds included
2 ea Scallions, chopped,
1 x  White part only
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
1/4 ts Ginger, minced
1/2 ts Peanut oil
1/2 c  Broth
1/2 ts Soy sauce
1 ts Dark brown sugar
1 x  Juice of 1/2 lime
1/4 c  Peanut butter
Saute the green onion, garlic and ginger in the oil, 3 to 4 minutes,
until soft but not browned.  (I used the micro.)
Add the stock and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and stir in the
remaining ingredients.  Simmer until thickened (5 minutes in micro,
or 10 on stove).
