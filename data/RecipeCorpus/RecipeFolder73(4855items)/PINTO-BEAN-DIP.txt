Title: PINTO BEAN DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Dips, Snacks, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Cup
16 oz Pinto beans, cooked
1/2 c  Salsa
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Cumin, ground
1/2 ts Coriander, ground
Salt & black pepper
Puree all the ingredients except the salt & pepper in either a food
processor or a blender.  Seasn with salt & pepper & serve.

"Vegetarian Gourmet" Winter, 1995
