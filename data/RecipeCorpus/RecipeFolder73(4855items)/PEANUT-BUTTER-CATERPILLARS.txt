Title: PEANUT BUTTER CATERPILLARS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
Banana
2 tb Peanut Butter
Grape
Peel and slice a banana. Sprad slices with peanut or sesame butter and
connect the slices. Add a grape up front for the head ("gluing" with
more peanut butter.) If desired, make this a game with the child -
what veggie can we add to make antennas? etc. (maybe a strip of
celery.)
