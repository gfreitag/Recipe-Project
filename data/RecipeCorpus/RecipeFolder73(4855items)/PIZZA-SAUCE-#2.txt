Title: PIZZA SAUCE #2
Categories: Pizza, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Tomato paste
1/4 c  Raw onion (chopped)
1 cn Tomato sauce
1    Clove garlic (chopped)
3 c  Water
2 pt Oregano
3 pt Cooking oil
1 pt Brown sugar
Combine ingredients in a 2 qt sauce pan and cook for 30-40 minutes.
Makes enough sauce for 5 12" Pizzas.
