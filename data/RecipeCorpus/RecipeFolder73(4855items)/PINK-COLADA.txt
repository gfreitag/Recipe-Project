Title: PINK COLADA
Categories: Beverages, Crocker
Yield: 24 Servings
24 oz Frozen limeade
-concentrate, thawed
15 1/2 oz Coconut cream
1/2 c  Grenadine syrup
56 oz Sparkling water, chilled
Mix limeade concentrate, coconut cream and grenadine syrup; add
sparkling water. Serve over ice and garnish with lime slice and
maraschino cherry ifdesired. 24 servings (about 1/2 cup each);120
calories per serving.

Source: Betty Crocker's Cookbook, 6th Edition
