Title: PESTO POPCORN SEASONING MIX
Categories: All newly t, Snacks
Yield: 10 Servings
3 tb Butter flavored sprinkles
2 tb Grated parmesan cheese
1 ts Dried basil; crushed
1/2 ts Dried parsley flakes;
-crushed
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Recipe by: Holiday Cooking 1996 Combine butter-flavor sprinkles,
Parmesan cheese, basil, parsley flakes, an garlic powder in a small
bowl. Give as a gift with unpopped popcorn.
(Seasoning mixture will coat about 10 cups of popped popcorn.) Store
mix the refrigerator.
