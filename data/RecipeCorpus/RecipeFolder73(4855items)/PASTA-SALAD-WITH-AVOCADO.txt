Title: PASTA SALAD WITH AVOCADO
Categories: Salads
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 x  Avocado
2 x  Tomatoes, diced 3/4 inch
2 c  Shell macaroni, cooked
1/4 c  Red onion, sliced
1 cn Water-packed albacore tuna,
Drained
3 tb Bottled vinegar based dress.
Cut avocado half into large 3/4 inch dices.  Combine all ingredients
and gently toss together.
