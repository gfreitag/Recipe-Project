Title: PASTA FAZOOL
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Kidney beans
6 c  ; water
2 tb Oil
1 c  Onions; chopped
1/2 c  Celery; diced
3/4 c  Carrots; chopped
1 c  Canned crushed tomatoes
Salt; pepper other seasoni
-to taste
2 c  Pasta; cooked
Soak and cook beans in water until tender. To the cooked beans and
remaining liquid, add oil, onions, celery, carrots, tomatoes and any
seasonings. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 45 minutes or
until vegetables are tender. Add cooked pasta.
