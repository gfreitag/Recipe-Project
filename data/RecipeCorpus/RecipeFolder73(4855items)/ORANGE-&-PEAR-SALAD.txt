Title: ORANGE & PEAR SALAD
Categories: New import
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Navel oranges, peeled, cored
And diced
2    Ripe pears, cored anddiced
12 c  Minced green onion
14 c  Julienned fresh basil
1/2    Julienned fresh mint
1 bn Fresh watercress, arugula or
Spinach leaves
Sweet Sesame Orange Dressing
(recipe follows)
Toss salad ingredients with dressing. Makes 6 side-dish servings.

STREET SESAME ORANGE DRESSING: Whisk together sesame oil, 2
tablespoons minced shallot, 1 teaspoon minced garlic, 2 tablespoons
orange marmalade, 1 tablespoon cider vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon salt and
1/2 teaspoon black pepper. Whisk in 2 tablespoons hot water and set
aside to combine flavors. Makes 1/2 cup.

Per serving: Calories 200 Fat 12g No cholesterol Sodium 183mg Percent
calories from fat
