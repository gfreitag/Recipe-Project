Title: PITCAITHLY BANNOCK (SCOTTISH)
Categories: Breads, Scottish
Yield: 1 Recipe
8 oz Flour
4 oz Butter
2 oz Caster Sugar
1 oz Choped Almonds
1 oz Mixed Candied Peel
Set Oven to 325F/Gas 3. Grease a baking sheet. Sift the flour
:
into a bowl. Add the sugar and butter and rub in to form a dough. Add
the almonds and mix in the peel, making sure they are evenly
distributed. Form into a thick round on a lightly floured surface and
prick all over with a fork. Place on the sheet and bake for about
45-60 minutes. Allow to cool and serve sliced thinly and buttered
