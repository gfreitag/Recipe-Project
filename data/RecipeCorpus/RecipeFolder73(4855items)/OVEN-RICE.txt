Title: OVEN RICE
Categories: Rice, Misc
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Uncooked regular long
-Grain rice
2 c  Water
1/4 ts Salt, if desired
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Heat water to boiling. Mix rice, water and
salt in ungreased 1-quart casserole. Cover and bake 25 to 30 minutes
or until water is absorbed. 6 SERVINGS (1/2 CUP EACH); 120 CALORIES
PER SERVING.
