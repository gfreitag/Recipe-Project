Title: PETTI DI POLLO ALLA BOLOGNESE
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Ethnic
Yield: 2 Servings
2    1/2 chicken breasts*
1 tb Butter
1 tb Olive oil
2 sl Cooked ham
2 tb Grated parmesan
1 ts Parsley
1/4 c  Chicken broth
*Can also be made with veal

Heat the oil and butter in a frying pan. Brown the chicken on both
sides. Put a slice of ham on each 1/2 breast. Springle with parmesan,
parsley. Pour the chicken broth in the pan. Cover let simmer for five
minutes. Submitted By MARTIN DELISLE <DELISLE@CAM.ORG> On THU, 16 NOV
1995 120742 ~0500
