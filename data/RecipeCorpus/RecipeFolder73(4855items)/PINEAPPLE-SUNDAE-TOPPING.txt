Title: PINEAPPLE SUNDAE TOPPING
Categories: Sauces, Desserts, Cake mix
Yield: 1 Servings
Vkbb14a
1 cn Crushed pineapple -- (8
Ounces)
8 oz Clear apple jelly
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
Strain pineapple thoroughly. Heat with apple jelly just until jelly
melts. Pour into refg. container with corn syrup. Refg. in covered
container to use within a month.
