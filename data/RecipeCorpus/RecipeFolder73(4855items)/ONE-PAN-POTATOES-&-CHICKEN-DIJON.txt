Title: ONE-PAN POTATOES & CHICKEN DIJON
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Low calorie, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
4 md Potatoes sliced 1/4" thick
1 lb Chicken breasts 1/2"thick
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Honey-Dijon BBQ Sauce
1 ts Dried tarragon
Microwave potatoes 8 - 10 minutes until tender.  While potatoes cook,
in large skillet toss and brown chicken in oil over high heat 5
minutes. Add potatoes; saute and toss until potatoes are lightly
browned. Add BBQ sauce and tarragon; toss until heated through.  Time:
20    minutes.
