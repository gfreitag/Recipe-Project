Title: PICKLED MUSHROOMS ("TASTE OF HOME")
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Cups
2/3 c  Tarragon vinegar
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Water
1 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 ds Hot pepper sauce
1 lb Fresh mushrooms
1 md Onion, thinly sliced rings
Finely diced sweet red
Pepper
In a glass bowl, combine the first seven ingredients. Add mushrooms
and onion; toss to coat.  Cover and refrigerate 8 hours or overnight.
Sprinkle with red pepper before serving. Yield: 4 cups

Typed by Dottie Theriault from "Taste of Home" Sept 1995
