Title: PINEAPPLE CURRY SAUCE
Categories: New import
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 ts Each cardamom, turmeric,
Ground coriander and cumin
1/8 ts Each ground cloves & nutmeg
1 tb Brown sugar
1/2 ts Paprika
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 ts Fresh ginger, grated
1/4 c  Pineapple juice
1/2 c  Pineapple, finely diced
Stir spices together in a small skillet over low heat for a minute or
two. Add garlic, ginger, sugar, and pineapple juice and cook over
medium heat, stirring frequently, for 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from
heat and add diced pineapple. Stir. Makes eight 2-Tablespoon servings
