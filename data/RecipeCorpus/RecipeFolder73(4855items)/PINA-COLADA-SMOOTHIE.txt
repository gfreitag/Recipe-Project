Title: PINA COLADA SMOOTHIE
Categories: Beverages, Fruits, Low-fat, Deal-a-meal, My book
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Evaporated skim milk; chill
1/2 ts Coconut extract
1/4    Ripe banana; peeled and
-frozen
1/4 c  Pineapple chunks; frozen
Ice cubes; 2-3
In blender, combine evaporated milk and extract. With blender
running, add frozen banana and pineapple pieces, a few at a time. Add
ice cubes one at a time. Blend until smooth.
