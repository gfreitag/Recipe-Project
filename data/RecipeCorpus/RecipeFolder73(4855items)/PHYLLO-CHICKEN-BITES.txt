Title: PHYLLO CHICKEN BITES
Categories: Poultry, Appetizers, Chicken
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Boneless, skinless chicken
Breast, cut into bite size
Pieces
3 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Olive oil
3 lg Garlic cloves-minced
1 lb Phllo dough, cut into 4-inch
Strips
Melted butter
Mix chicken pieces, lemon juice, olive oil and garlic together and
refrigerate overnight. Place a piece of chicken on the end of a strip
of phyllo dough. Roll up, tucking the ends to make a little pillow.
Dip in a bowl of melted butter, then bake for 15 minutes at 400 F/200
C.

Source: Eaton's Great Canadian Recipe Challenge 1993 From the
collection of K.Deck
