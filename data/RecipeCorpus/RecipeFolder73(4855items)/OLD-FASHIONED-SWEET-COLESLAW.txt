Title: OLD-FASHIONED SWEET COLESLAW
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 sm Cabbage, finely chopped
2    Carrots, shredded
1/3 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Combine cabbage and carrot. Combine mayonnaise and remaining
ingredients. Toss mayonnaise mixture with cabbage mixture. Cover and
chill.
