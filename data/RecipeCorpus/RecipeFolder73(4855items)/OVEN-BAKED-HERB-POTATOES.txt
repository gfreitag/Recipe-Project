Title: OVEN-BAKED HERB POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Holiday, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
2 md Baking Potatoes
1 tb Vegetable Oil
1/2 ts Dried Thyme
1/2 ts Dried Oregano
Slice the potatoes about 1/4 inch thick.  Brush the slices with the
vegetable oil.  Place in a baking pan. Sprinkle with the thyme and
oregano. Bake in a 400-degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until
tender.

Serves 4
