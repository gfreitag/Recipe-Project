Title: PINK LEMONADE [A]
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 8 Servings
6 oz Can frozen lemonade
-concentrate; thawed
6 oz Can grenadine
6    Cans water
Combine lemonade concentrate, grenadine and water in bowl; mix well.
Pour over ice in punch bowl. This is a nice substitute for a Bacardi
Cocktail.

Source: Great Recipes from Great Gardeners
