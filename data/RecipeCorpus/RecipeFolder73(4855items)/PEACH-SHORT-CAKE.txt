Title: PEACH SHORT CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Snacks
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM--------------------------DESSERT-------------------------------
1 cn Peaches
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
MMMMM---------------------------SYRUP--------------------------------
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
Line pan with peaches. Mix all dessert ingredients. Pour over
peaches. Mix syrup ingredients and pour over batter. Bake at 350 deg
for 1 hour. Serves 6 to 8 people.
