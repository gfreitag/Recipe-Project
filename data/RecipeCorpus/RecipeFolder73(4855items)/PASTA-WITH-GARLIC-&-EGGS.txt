Title: PASTA WITH GARLIC & EGGS
Categories: Main dish, Cheese
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 lb Dry pasta
1/2 c  Olive oil
2 tb Parmesan cheese
2    Eggs, beaten
4    Cloves of garlic
1    Salt and pepper, taste
Cook pasta. Heat the oil in small frying pan, add garlic. Saute for
just a moment. Drain pasta, toss all ingredients together. Salt and
pepper to taste.
