Title: OSHKINIGIKWE (TOMATO STEW)
Categories: Main dish, Native, Wampanoag, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
4 lb Miniature Mexican tomatoes,
-- washed & deseeded
12 ea Shallots, chopped
1/3 c  Water
1/3 c  Cucumber, minced
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/3 c  Corn flour
Place tomatoes in a large saucepan, add the shallots & water & simmer
for 30 minutes, stirring gently on occasion. Add the cucumbers & salt
& pepper, simmer for 10 minutes, stirring, until the tomatoes break
up. Quickly add the corn flour, 1 tablespoon at a time, stirring
slowly. Simmer for 5 minutes & serve.
