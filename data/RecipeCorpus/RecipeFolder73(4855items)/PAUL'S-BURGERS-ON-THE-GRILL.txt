Title: PAUL'S BURGERS ON THE GRILL
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Onion -- finely chopped
2 ts Italian seasoning
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Salt
4    Hamburger buns -- split
Lettuce leaves and tomato
sl -- optional
Combine the first six ingredients in a medium bowl; mix well. Shape
into four patties.  Grill over medium coals for 4-5 minutes per side
or until no longer pink. Serve on buns; top with lettuce and tomato
if desired.

: Taste Of Home June/July '96
