Title: PICNIC SPECIAL
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 12 Servings
2 lb Ground beef
1 c  Quaker Oats, uncooked
-- (quick or old-fashioned)
1 3/8 oz Onion soup mix
1 c  Milk
1    Loaf Vienna bread
-- cut into 24 slices
12    Tomato slices
Leaf lettuce
6 sl Process American cheese
-- cut in half
Combine ground beef, oats, soup mix and milk thoroughly. Shape to
form 12 flat patties.  Place in broiler or over hot coals about 5
minutes. Turn and cook about 5 minutes longer.

To serve, place a tomato slice, leaf of lettuce, half a cheese slice
and a hamburger between 2 slices of Vienna bread. Reassemble loaf in
bread basket.
