Title: ONION BROTH
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Calf's feet or soup bones
2 tb Oil or butter
5 lb Yellow onions
- peeled and roughly chopped
2 tb Minced garlic
1/4 ts Ground mace
4 c  Chicken stock or water
4    Bay leaves
Salt and pepper; to taste
PLACE BONES, oil and onions in a large pot and place over low heat.
Cook, stirring, until the onions change color from light to dark,
about 1 hour. Add garlic, mace, stock, bay leaves, salt and pepper.
Cover and simmer for 45 minutes. Remove the bones and bay leaves and
serve the soup.
