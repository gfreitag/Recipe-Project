Title: OVEN-CURED TOMATOES
Categories: Vegetables, June 1996 i
Yield: 24 Servings
8 lg Tomatoes
Extra virgin olive oil
Salt and pepper
1 ts Sugar
2 ts Marjoram -- finely chopped
Heat oven to 250 F. Cut tomatoes crosswise into thirds. PLace cut
side up on parchment-lined baking sheet. Brush with oil; season with
salt and pepper, sugar and marjoram. Place in oven. For a gas oven
with pilot light, turn off heat for 2 hours; leave overnight. For an
electric oven, bake 2 hours, then turn to warm, leave overnight.
