Title: ONION BREAD (LERNER)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Grated onion
1 ts Salt
1 tb Dry yeast
1/2 c  Warm water
2 1/2 c  Unbleached white flour
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
In a bowl, mix onion & salt.  Add yeast dissolved in the warm water.
Stir in flour & oil.  Beat well.

Spoon the dough into an oiled loaf tin & let the dough rise to the
top of the tin.  Preheat oven to 350F & bake for 1 hour.

Anne Lerner, "Breads You Wouldn't Believe"
