Title: OLIVE GARDEN HOUSE DRESSING (UPDATED)
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese/eggs, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
8 oz Paul Newman's Vinegar and --
Oil Dressing
10 ml Garlic; peel -- halve
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/2 ts Dried oregano
3 pk Sweet and Low -- or
1 tb Sugar
Put ingredients into the bottle of dressing and shake well.
Refrigerate 24 hours before using. Source: Gloria Pitzer.
