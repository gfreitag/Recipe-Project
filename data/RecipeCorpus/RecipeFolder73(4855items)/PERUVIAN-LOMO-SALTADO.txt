Title: PERUVIAN LOMO SALTADO
Categories: Hot & spicy, Main dish, Mexican, Potatoes
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Vegetable Oil
1/2 c  Onion -- sliced
2    Cloves Garlic -- minced
1/2 c  Bell Pepper, Red Or Yellow
Chopped
1 1/2 c  Potato -- cubed and cooked
1 tb Jalapeno Pepper -- diced
1/2 lb Sirloin Steak -- sliced
Thin
1    Tomato -- chopped
1 ts Fresh Oregano
Salt And Pepper -- to taste
Heat oil in a non-stick pan and saute onion, garlic and bell pepper.
Add potato and cook, stirring occasionally, until potato just begins
to brown. Add chile and steak. Saute a few more minutes. Stir in
tomato, oregano, salt and pepper.
