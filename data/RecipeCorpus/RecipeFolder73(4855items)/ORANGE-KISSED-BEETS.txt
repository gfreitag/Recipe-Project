Title: ORANGE-KISSED BEETS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
From the files of W. Long
Tulsa, Oklahoma
1/3 c  Orange juice
2 tb Light brown sugar
1 tb Margarine
1/2 ts Cornstarch
1/8 ts Ground ginger
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 cn 8-1/4 oz sliced beets,
Drained
1 ts Golden raisins
Strips of orange peel
In a saucepan over medium heat, cook and stir orange juice, brown
sugar, margarine, cornstarch, ginger, salt and pepper until thick.
Add the beets and raisins, heat through. Garnish with orange peel.
