Title: OVEN BROWNED POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
2 tb Butter
2    Potatoes, boiled and
Chilled
2/3 c  Milk
1    Egg, beaten
Preheat oven to 350 Grease shallow casserole. Shred potatoes in dish
and spread evenly.  Mix milk and egg, then pour over potatoes. Stir
slightly. Dot with butter. Bake 30 minutes.
