Title: PEANUT BUTTER FUDGE #3
Categories: Desserts, Candies
Yield: 2 Dozen
12 oz Semisweet chocolate morsels
12 oz Crunchy peanut butter
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
Put chocolate chips and peanut butter into a microwave-safe dish.
Microwave on high 1-2 minutes until chips have completely melted. Add
sweetened condensed milk; stir until mixture is of a uniform
consistency. Pour into a 8"x8" square pan lined with wax paper.
Refrigerate until firm. Cut into 1" pieces.  Yield:1 1/2 pounds or 16
servings
