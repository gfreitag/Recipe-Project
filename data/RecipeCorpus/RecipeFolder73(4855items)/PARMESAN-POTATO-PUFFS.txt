Title: PARMESAN POTATO PUFFS
Categories: Cheese, Side dishes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 md Baking potatoes; peeled and
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese; freshly gra
2 tb Skim milk
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Ground white pepper
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1/4 c  Frozen egg substitute; thawe
Vegetable cooking spray
Recipe by: Dora Guerra <dguerra@LONESTAR.JPL.UTSA.EDU> Cook potato in
boiling water until tender, drain, and mash. Add 1/4 c. parmesan
cheese and next 4 ingredients; beat at med. speed of mixer just until
mixture is smooth. Stir in egg substitute.  Pipe or spoon potato
mixture into 6 mounds on a baking sheet coated with veg. spray;
sprinkle evenly with 1 tbsp. parmesan.  Bake potato mounds at 350
degrees for 25 - 30 min. or until lightly browned
