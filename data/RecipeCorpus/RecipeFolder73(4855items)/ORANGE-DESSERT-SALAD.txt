Title: ORANGE DESSERT SALAD
Categories: Salads, African-amr
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Oranges, sliced in rounds
-and seeded
6    Large pitted dates, chopped
2 tb Slivered almonds, toasted
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1 ts Cinnamon
Combine oranges, dates and almonds in a serving dish. Gently stirin
lemon juice and chill for an hour. Just before serving, sprinkle with
cinnamon.
