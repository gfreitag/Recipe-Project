Title: PAPAYA MANGO RELISH
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Serrano chiles; minced
Or 3 Habaneros; minced
1    Juice of 2 limes
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Fresh cilantro; minced
1    Garlic clove; minced
1/2 c  Firm mango; diced
1/2 c  Papaya; diced
Combine the chiles, lime juice, olive oil, cilantro, and garlic to
make a marinade. Toss the diced fruit in the mixture and allow to sit
for at least an hour to blend the flavors. Chile Pepper Magazine
