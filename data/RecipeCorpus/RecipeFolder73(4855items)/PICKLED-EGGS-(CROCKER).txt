Title: PICKLED EGGS (CROCKER)
Categories: Appetizers, Crocker
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Hard-cooked eggs
1 c  Cider vinegar
1 c  Beet liquid
1/3 c  Granulated sugar or packed
-brown sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 sm Onion, chopped
4    Whole cloves
Shredded greens
Place peeled eggs in bowl or jar. Mix vinegar, beet liquid sugar,
salt, onion and cloves; pour over eggs. Cover and refrigerate at
least 2 days. Slice eggs; serve on greens. 6 servings;145 calories
per serving.

Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
