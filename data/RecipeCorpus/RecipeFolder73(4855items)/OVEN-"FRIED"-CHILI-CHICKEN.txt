Title: OVEN "FRIED" CHILI CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Chicken thighs without skin
About 6
1 lb Chicken breasts without skin
About 3
2/3 c  Corn flake crumbs
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Ground red pepper
1 ds Garlic powder
Remove all visible fat from the chicken pieces. Combine crumbs and
spices in a plastic bag.  Rinse chicken pieces in water and then toss
a few at a time in the crumbs until well coated. Place pieces on a
rack in a shallow pan and bake in a 375 oven for 40-45 minutes or
until tender and no longer pink.

per breast:  122 Kcal   1.4g fat (0.3g sat fat)   11% CFF 126mg Na per
thigh:  58 Kcal   1.8g fat (0.4g sat fat) 28% CFF 63mg Na
