Title: PEANUT BUTTER FUDGE #2
Categories: Candies, Desserts
Yield: 2 Dozen
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
Dash of salt
1/2    Stick of butter (2 oz)
1 ts Vanilla
2 tb Heaping tb peanut butter
Cook sugar, milk, and salt until hard ball stage, then add butter,
vanilla, and peanut butter.  Beat until thick. Pour into a buttered
pan.
