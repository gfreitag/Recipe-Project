Title: OREGON HAZELNUT APPETIZER
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 48 Appetizers
10    Strips sliced bacon
3/4 c  Chopped Oregon hazelnuts
1 c  Grated cheddar cheese
1 tb Parsley, snipped
1 ts Grated onion
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Cayenne pepper
12    Slices thin wheat bread
Cook bacon until crisp; drain and crumble. Blend with Oregon
hazelnuts and remaining ingredients, except bread. Remove crusts from
bread, cut into quarters and spread mixture on top of squares. Place
on cookie sheet and bake at 350 for 10 minutes. Serve hot.
