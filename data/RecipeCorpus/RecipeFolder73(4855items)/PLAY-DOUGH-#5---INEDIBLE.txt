Title: PLAY DOUGH #5 - INEDIBLE
Categories: Kids, Crafts, Play dough
Yield: 1 Batch
2 c  Flour
1 c  Salt
1 ts Salad oil
Water
SOURCE:  Posted on Delphi - Cust 229 by Lynne Sammon (MICROWIZ). MM
format by Ursula R. Taylor
Mix ingredients together; knead until very smooth and workable. To
color just knead food coloring into small balls of dough as desired.
