Title: ORANGE & GRAPEFRUIT MARMALADE~ COTTAGE CHEE
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Orange marmalade
1/2 c  Grapefruit marmalade
1 c  Well drained cottage
Cheese, sieved
1 tb Minced parsley  or chives
Salt and pepper
:    Combine and mix well the orange and the grapefruit marmelades and
cottage cheese. Season with salt, pepper, and chives or parsley.
Store in a jar in the refrigerater for 1 week. Makes 2 c.
Serve on toast spread with peanut butter. Jeannie Allen
