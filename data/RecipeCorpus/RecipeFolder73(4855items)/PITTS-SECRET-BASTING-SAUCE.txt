Title: PITTS SECRET BASTING SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Texas beer
2 tb Liquid smoke
2 tb Soy Sauce
1/4 c  Wine vinegar
1/4 ts Tabasco
Mix all ingredients in a shaker-type bottle.  Shake liberally on ribs,
chops, etc. while cooking.

From the kitchen of Kathy Pitts Bryan, TX
