Title: OLGA'S MICROWAVE PEANUT BRITTLE
Categories: Candies, Microwave
Yield: 1 Batch
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Corn syrup, light
1 c  Peanuts, roasted salted
1 ts Butter
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Baking soda
Combine sugar and corn syrup in a 2-quart microwave-safe dish, and
micro-cook on HIGH 4 minutes. Stir in roasted peanuts, and micro-cook
on HIGH three to five minutes, or until lightly brown. Add butter and
vanilla to syrup blending well; micro-cook on HIGH one or two minutes
more. Add soda to mixture and gently stir until foamy. Pour onto a
greased cookie sheet.  Let cool; break into pieces when fully cooled.
