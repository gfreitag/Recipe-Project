Title: PLUM GINGER SAUCE (SERVED WITH ECREVISSE STRU
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 10 Servings
1 tb Butter
12 oz Plum preserves -- purreed
2 oz Ginger -- grated
4 oz Red wine vinegar
2 oz Granulated sugar
1/2 ts Granulated garlic
Heat Skillet, melt butter.  Add grated inger, saute 2 minutes. Add
remaining ingredients.  Bring to a boil. Strain. Serve Hot. Serve with
Ecrevisse Strudel with two Sauces. Can add less ginger... strong
ginger taste. Source: Brit Shockley
