Title: PLUMS WITH HONEY
Categories: Dessert, Norwegian
Yield: 1 Servings
6    To 8 plums depending on size
2 c  Orange-spice tea or your
Choice
Honey
Pecan halves
Vanilla ice cream
Kornmo kjeks or graham
Crakers
Place plums into boiling tea. Remove from heat and let stand 1 hour.
Remove skins. Pour honey into heavy-bottomed pan and heat on low; add
plums and turn until all sides are covered. When ready to serve, add
pecans. Serve with ice cream and crackers.
