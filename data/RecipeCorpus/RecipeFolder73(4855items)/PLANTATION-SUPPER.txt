Title: PLANTATION SUPPER
Categories: Hamburger
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 c  Egg Noodles
1 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion, Chopped
1 cn Cream of Mushroom Soup
8 oz Cream Cheese
1 cn Corn
Brown the ground beef and onions.  Separately, prepare the noodles
and then add them to the beef/onions. Soften the cream cheese and
slowly stir it in. Then add the remaining ingredients and let it
simmer for approx. 20 minutes.
