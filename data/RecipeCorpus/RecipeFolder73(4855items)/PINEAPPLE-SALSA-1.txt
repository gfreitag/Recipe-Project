Title: PINEAPPLE SALSA 1
Categories: Salsa
1/2 md Pineapple -- 1/2 dice
Yield: 1 Servings
1 Sm Red Bell Pepper -- 1/2 dice
3 md Green Onions -- Minced
1    Serrano Pepper -- Minced
1    Ginger Root Slice -- 1/2-in
minced
:
1 TB Lime Juice
1/8 ts Salt

Mix all ingredients including reserved pineapple juices in medium
bowl. Cover and let stand at least 1 hour at room temperature.
