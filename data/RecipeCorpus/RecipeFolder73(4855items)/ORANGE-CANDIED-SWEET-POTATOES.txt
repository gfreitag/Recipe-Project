Title: ORANGE CANDIED SWEET POTATOES
Categories: Side, S.potato, Holiday
Yield: 4 Servings
Recipe
6 medium sweet potatoes 1 cup orange juice 1 cup sugar 1/2 teaspoon
grated orange rind 1 cup water 1/4 cup butter 1/2 teaspoon salt

Peel uncooked potatoes and slice in 1/4 inch slices. Arrange in a
buttered baking dish.  Make a syrup of the remainging ingredients and
pour over potatoes. Cover and bake at 350 degrees until tender. Baste
occasionally. Remove the lid the last 10 minutes and allow to brown.
If desired, a layer of marshmallows may be added and browned just
before removing dish from the oven.

I just cook until tender, cool and refrigerate over night, heat in
the micro, add marshmallows and brown in oven before serving.
