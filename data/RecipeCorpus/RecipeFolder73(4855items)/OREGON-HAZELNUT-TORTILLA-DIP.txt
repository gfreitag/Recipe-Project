Title: OREGON HAZELNUT TORTILLA DIP
Categories: Dips, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Recipe
2    Ripe avocados
-- peeled, pitted & diced
8 oz Cream cheese
-- room temperature
2    Green onions; finely sliced
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
1    Garlic clove; minced
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Salt
1    Tomato; seeded & diced
Chopped parsley
1/3 c  Roasted & chopped hazelnuts
-(Oregon hazelnuts)
Combine softened cream cheese, avocado, onion, lemon juice, garlic,
chili powder and salt. Blend well. Add half the nuts and tomato;
blend. Mold into ball. Top with remaining nuts and parsley. Chill to
improve flavor. Serve with tortilla chips.
