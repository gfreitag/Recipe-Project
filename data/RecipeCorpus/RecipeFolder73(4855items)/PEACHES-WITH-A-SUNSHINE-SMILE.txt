Title: PEACHES WITH A SUNSHINE SMILE
Categories: Salads, Kids
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn Of peach halves
2    To 3 cups cottage cheese
-(low-fat)
Lettuce leaves
Arrange lettuce in a bowl or plate. Spoon a layer of cottage cheese
"sun rays" on lettuce. The peach half is placed round end up. Press
raisins gently into peach to form a sunshine face. Sprinkle extra
raisins on cottage cheese.
