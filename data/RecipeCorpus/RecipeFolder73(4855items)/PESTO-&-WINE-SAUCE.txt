Title: PESTO & WINE SAUCE
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 1/2 serv.
1/2 c  Dry white wine
1 c  Purchased pesto
1/2 c  Chopped roasted red bell
-peppers (from 7.25 oz.jar)
In small saucepan, heat wine just until warm.  Remove from heat. Stir
in pesto and bell peppers.  Serve over hot cooked pasta. Yield: 4 1/2
cups Source: Pillsbury Pasta Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com
