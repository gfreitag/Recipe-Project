Title: PARSLEY & POTATO SOUP
Categories: Soups, Appetizers, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 md Onion, chopped
1 ts Olive oil
2 c  Chopped potatoes
1 c  Sliced leeks, white only
4 c  Stock
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts White pepper
2 tb Chopped fresh chives
1/4 c  Chopped fresh parsley
In a large pot, combine all the ingredients except the salt, pepper,
chives & parsley.  Cover the pot & bring to a boil. Lower heat &
simmer for 30 minutes.  Cool slightly, then puree. Season with salt &
pepper & garnish with chives & parsley.
