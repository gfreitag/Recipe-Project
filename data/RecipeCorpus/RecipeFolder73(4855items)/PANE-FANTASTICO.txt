Title: PANE FANTASTICO
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Butter softened
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 ts Dried basil
1 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1    Loaf soft Italian seeded
Bread OR thick sliced bread
In a bowl, cream the butter with the garlic and the basil until
smooth. Stir in the three cheese.  Spread mixture evenly on each
slice of Italian seeded bread or thick bread and place cheese side
together forming sandwiches. Stack the sandwiches and warp in foil.
Bake at 375F for 20 minutes.  Open foil and bake about 10 minutes
longer or until it is crispy on the edges. Serves 6 - 8 people.
