Title: PEANUT BUTTER SUNDAE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Peanut butter
2 tb Honey
1/3 c  Milk
Few grains salt
3 c  Vanilla ice milk
Wheat germ; as desired
Stir peanut butter, honey, milk, and salt together in a saucepan.
Cook over low heat, stirring constantly. Remove from heat when peanut
butter is melted.  Sauce should be smooth. Cool. Serve sauce over ice
milk. Sprinkle each serving with wheat germ, as desired.

Calories per 1/2 cup serving: About 195
