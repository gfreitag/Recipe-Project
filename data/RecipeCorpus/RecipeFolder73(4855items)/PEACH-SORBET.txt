Title: PEACH SORBET
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar
8 sm Peaches
Juice of 1 lemon
1 tb Marsala or peach brandy,
-- optional
Make a syrup by combining the water & sugar in a non-corrosive pot &
bring to a boil.  Simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from the heat & let
cool. Puree the eaches in a food processor or blender to yield about
1 c. Combine the syrup, peaches, lemon juice & marsala. Freeze
accroding to the instructions for making Lemon Sorbet.

Serve over fresh raspberries or blackberries.
