Here's our no-fat alternative for that old familiar ring.

Dip 1/4-inch-cut sweet onion rings in beaten egg white and dredge in
seasoned bread crumbs or in cornflake crumbs mixed with chili powder
to taste. Then bake the coated rings on alightly sprayed baking sheet
at 375Â°F. for 15 minutes, or until brown and crispy.
