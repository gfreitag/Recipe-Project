Title: OLIVE & ANCHOVY BITES
Categories: Tapas, Spanish, Appetizers
Yield: 40 Pieces
1 c  All-purpose Flour
1/2 c  Chilled butter
1 c  Grated cheese (any kind)
2 oz Chopped Anchovy Fillets
1/3 c  Chopped black olives
1/2 t  Cayenne pepper
1.  Place flour, butter, cheese, anchovies, olives, and cayenne in a
food processor and mix until forms a firm dough.

2.  Wrap dough in plastic wrap and chill for 20 minutes.

3.  Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.  Roll out dough on lightly floured
surface.

4.  Cut dough into 2 inch strips.  Cut strips diagonally to make small
triangles.  Bake 8-10 minutes on baking sheets.  Cool.

May keep in airtight container for up to 2 weeks.
