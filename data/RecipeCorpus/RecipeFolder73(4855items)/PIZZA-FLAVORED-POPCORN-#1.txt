Title: PIZZA FLAVORED POPCORN #1
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Grated parmesan cheese
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Italian herb seasoning
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
ds Pepper
2 qt Hot popcorn
In blender, blend cheese, garlic powder, italian seasoning, paprika,
salt and pepper about 3 minutes. Place popcorn in large bowl;
sprinkle with cheese mixt ure. Toss to coat evenly. Makes 3 quarts
