Title: PEANUT BUTTER BROWNIE CUPS
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 15 Servings
1 pk Betty Crocker Fudge
-Brownie Mix
15 ea Up to 18 Reeses Mini
-Peanutbutter Cups
Make brownie mix per directions.  Fill paper-lined muffin cups 2/3
full. Unwrap peanutbutter cups & press into the center of each
'muffin' until the batter meets the top edge of the candy. Bake at
350 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes.  Makes 15 to 18.
