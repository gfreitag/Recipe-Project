Title: PEACH-GINGER-GRAPE FIZZ
Categories: Digest, Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 ts Grated fresh gingerroot
3 c  White grape juice, chilled
3 lg Peaches or nectarines,
-peeled, pitted, and cut
-into chunks
3/4 c  Seltzer water, chilled
Place ginger, 1.5 cups of the grape juice, and the peaches or
nectarines in a blender or food processor and puree. Combine the
remaining grape juice, the blened peaches, and the seltzer.

4    servings, 1.5 cups each
Cal:145,Fat:0.2g,Chol:0mg,Prot:1.5g,Carb:35g,Fib:1g,Sod 5.3mg

From _500 Fat-Free Recipes: A Complete Guide to Reducing the Fat in
Your Diet: 500 recipes from soup to dessert containing one gram of
fat or less_ by Sarah Schlesinger. Villard Books: New York. 1994.
