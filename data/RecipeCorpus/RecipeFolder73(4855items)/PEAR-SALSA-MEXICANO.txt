Title: PEAR SALSA MEXICANO
Categories: Condiments, Fruits, Mexican
Yield: 2 .5 cups
16 oz Bartlett pear; drained &
-chopped
1 c  Tomato; chopped
4 oz Chiles, green canned;
-chopped
1/4 c  Scallion; chopped
1/4 c  Bell pepper, green; chopped
1 tb Olive oil
1 tb Lime juice
1 tb Cilantro; chopped (opt)
1 1/2 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Oregano; crushed
1 sm Hot pepper sauce; bottle
Combine all ingredients; mix well.  Refrigerate at least one hour.
