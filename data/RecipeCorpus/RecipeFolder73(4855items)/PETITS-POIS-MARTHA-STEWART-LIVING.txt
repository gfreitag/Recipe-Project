Title: PETITS POIS-MARTHA STEWART LIVING
Categories: Vegetables, Sides, Soup/stews, Pets
Yield: 2 Servings
2 ts Unsalted butter
1/2    10-oz box frozen petits pois
-(also called baby or early
-peas)
1 c  Shredded Boston lettuce
Salt & freshly ground pepper
Heat butter in a small saute pan over medium heat. Add peas; cook
until tender, about 2 minutes. Add lettuce, salt, and pepper and cook
until just wilted, about 2 minutes more.

Martha Stewart Living/Feb. & March/94 Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
