Title: PLAY DOUGH #6
Categories: Kids, Play dough
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ds Food colouring
1 c  Water
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Salt
2 ts Cream of tartar
2 tb Cooking oil
In a heavy saucepan, stir food colouring into water. Add all remaining
ingredients.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly with a wooden
spoon until mixture thickens and draws away from the sides of the
pan. Form into a ball and cool. Pack play dough in a zip-lock bag or
air-tight container.

NB: Can also be used as a treat for birthday parties, just pack it
into individual decorative containers.

Source: Modern Woman magazine, June 1993 issue Shared by: Sharon
Herrington
