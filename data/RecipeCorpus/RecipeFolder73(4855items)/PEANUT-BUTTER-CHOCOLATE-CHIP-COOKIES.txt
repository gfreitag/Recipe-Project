Title: PEANUT BUTTER CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Chocolate
Yield: 24 Servings
1 c  Peanut Butter
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg -- lightly beaten
1/2 c  Semisweet Chocolate Chips --
(miniature)
In a mixing bowl, combine peanut butter, sugar and egg (batter will be
stiff).  Stir in chocolate chips. Scoop level tablespoonfuls and roll
into balls.  Place on ungreased cookie sheets and flatten with a
fork. Bake at 350 degrees for 15-18 minutes or until browned.

Yield:  2 dozen.

Editor's Note:  This recipe does not use any flour.

Source: April / May issue of "Taste of Home.  "
