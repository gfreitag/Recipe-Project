Title: PEST POWDER (TLC)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
8 ts Crushed Coriander Seeds
8 ts Powdered orris root
3    Cinnamon sticks
Dried peel of 1 orange
Handful of nutmeg
Handful of cloves
1/2 ts White sugar
sm Handful of lavender flowers
sm Handful of wormwood or
-southern wood
1. Put the cinnamon sticks, cloves and nutmeg in a grinder. Add all
the other ingredients and mix in a large bowl. Fill the linen sachet
with the mixture and secure.

2. To make little spice bags simply put a small amount of mixture in
centre of handkerchief and tie up. Decorate with ribbon.
