Title: PEPE'S GARLIC SOUP
Categories: Soups, Garlic
Yield: 4 Servings
Bread, French, stale; sliced
4 c  ;Water, salt, boiling
8    Garlic clove
1    Onion; chopped
1 ts Parsley; chopped
2 tb Oil
1 ts Paprika
4    Egg
Preheat oven to 450 F.  Place bread in with boiling water. Fry onion,
garlic and parsley in oil.  Remove garlic and add paprika then turn
heat off.  Add to bread/water mixture. Place in oven uncovered for 15
minutes. It will form a crust.  Place in bowls and break an egg into
each while soup is still hot.
