Title: PINK PASTEL PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Recipe
-JUDI M. PHELPS
2 pt Raspberry sherbet
1    (12 oz.) can pink lemonade
4 c  Water
1    Liter bottle lemon lime soda
OR
Ginger ale
10 oz Pkg. frozen raspberries;
-thawed
Soften sherbet and lemonade and combine with water. Stir in thawed
raspberries.  Add lemon lime soda or ginger ale at last minute. Nice
for weddings.
