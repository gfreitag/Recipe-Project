Title: ORANGE & DATE BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 2 Loaves
1 c  Butter or margarine, soft
2 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
4 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 1/3 c  Buttermilk
1 c  Chopped dates
1 c  Chopped pecans or walnuts
1 tb Grated orange rind
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Grated orange rind
Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs, beat well.  Combine dry
ingredients and add alternately with the buttermilk. Fold in walnuts,
dates and orange rind. Pour into two 8 1/2 inch greased and floured
pans and bake at 350F. degrees for about an hour.  Comb

NOTES: Can be made ahead and frozen. From Taste of Home magazine,
