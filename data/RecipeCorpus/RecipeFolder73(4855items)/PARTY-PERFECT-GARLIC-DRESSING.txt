Title: PARTY-PERFECT GARLIC DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Garlic
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/3 ts Curry powder
1 1/3 ts Dry mustard
2 ts Cayenne
1 tb Dehydrated parsley
1 tb Dehydrated chives
1 ts Instant granulated garlic
1/4 c  Salt
1 tb White pepper
1    Lemon rind, chopped
1/3 c  Olive oil
1 c  White vinegar
2 1/2 qt Salad oil
1 1/4 qt Cider vinegar
Moisten instant granulated garlic, parsley and chives with 1/4 cup
water. To this add curry, mustard, cayenne, salt, pepper and lemon
rinds. While beating, first add olive oil, then vinegar. Chill well.
Shake before serving.  Makes about 1 gallon.
