Title: ORANGE-CRANBERRY BREAD (ABM)
Categories: Digest, Dec., Holiday, Abm
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Dried cranberries
x  Zest from 1 orange
2 c  White bread flour
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1    Packet/2 1/4 tsp yeast
1 tb Sugar or honey
1 ts Salt
2    3 tsp cinnamon
x  Juice from the orange plus
Water to make 1 1/2 cups
Follow usual procedures (oat bread cycle helps).  I think this one's
a bit higher, something like 80 cal,
0.6 g  fat/ounce.
