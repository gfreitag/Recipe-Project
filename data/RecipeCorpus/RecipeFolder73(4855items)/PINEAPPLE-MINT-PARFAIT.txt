Title: PINEAPPLE MINT PARFAIT
Categories: Desserts, Text
Yield: 8 Servings
Text Only
Prepare the recipe for strawberry parfait, substituting crushed
pineapple for strawberries. Serve with sweetened whipped cream.
Garnish with bits of candied pineapple which have been colored green
and flavored with mint. Fill mold.  Pack in ice and salt. Let stand 4
hours. 8 servings. The Household Searchlight
