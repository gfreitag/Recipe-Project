Title: PIGEON HOUSE PATIO KEY LIME CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/3 c  Sugar
2 c  Flour
2/3 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
3 oz Lime gelatin
5    Eggs
1 1/3 c  Oil
3/4 c  Orange juice
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 ts Lemon extract
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
1/3 c  Key lime juice
1/3 c  Powdered sugar
Whipped cream garnish Lime slices garnish

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Place dry ingredients into mixing bowl. Add eggs, oil, orange juice,
vanilla and lemon extract. Beat until well blended. Pour batter into a
9x13x2-inch pan; bake 25 to 30 minutes.

Remove cake from oven. Let stand in pan 15 minutes or until almost
cool. Prick cake all over with fork. Drizzle thoroughly with lime
juice mixed with powdered sugar. Cover and refrigerate.

To serve, cut into squares, top with whipped cream and garnish with
lime slices.
