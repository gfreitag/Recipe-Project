Title: PASTA WITH TOMATO MINT SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 8 Servings
16 oz Pasta -- rotini
Chopped
Garlic clove -- minced
1/2 c  Mint leaves, fresh --
Chopped
1/3 c  Oil -- olive
1 ts Salt
3 lb Tomatoes, peeled -- seeded
And
Prepare pasta as directed on package; drain. Meanwhile, cook and stir
garlic in hot oil until golden brown. Stir in tomatoes, mint and
salt. Cook over low heat 5 minutes or until heated through. Serve
over pasta.
