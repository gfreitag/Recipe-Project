Title: PEPPER & HERB MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 tb Instant minced onion
1 ea Clove garlic, finely minced
1 tb Sugar
2 ts Tabasco sauce
2 ts Chicken or beef bouillon
1 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Oregano dried
In a shallow dish or plastic bag, combine ingredients. Add chicken,
beef or pork.  Cover.  Marinate in refrigerator 4 hours or overnight,
turning occasionally.  Remove meat from marinade. Grill or barbeque
as desired, basting with marinade.
