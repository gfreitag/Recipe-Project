Title: PIZZA MARIETTA
Categories: Boboli, Meats, Vegetables, Cheese
Yield: 4 Servings
-Waldine Van Geffen VGHC42A]
1    12" or 4 6" Boboli shells
1/2 c  Pizza or tomato sauce
1 c  Mozzarella; shredded
4    Italian sausages; cook,
-slice
1 c  Green pepper; thin strips
8 sl Red onion; thin, separated
Spread sauce on Boboli shell. Sprinkle with cheese. Arrange remaining
ingredients on top. Bake at 450~ 10-12 minutes. (wrv)
