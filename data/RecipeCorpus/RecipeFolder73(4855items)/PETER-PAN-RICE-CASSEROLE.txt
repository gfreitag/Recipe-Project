Title: PETER PAN RICE CASSEROLE
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 oz Peanut butter
1 1/2 tb Sunflower oil
1/4 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Tarragon
1/2 ts Salt
3 oz Brown rice; cooked
1 1/2 oz Sunflower seeds
1 1/2 oz Shallots; chopped medium
-fine
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Lightly grease 4 x 8 baking dish with
sunflower oil. In a blender, combine peanut butter, oil, thyme,
tarragon, salt and 2 ounces water. Blend until mixture achieves sauce
consistency.
Combine with rice, sunflower seeds, adn shallots. Mix well. Transfer
to baking dish and bake for 20 minutes in oven.
