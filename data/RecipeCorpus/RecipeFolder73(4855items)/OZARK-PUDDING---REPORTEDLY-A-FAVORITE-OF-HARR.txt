Title: OZARK PUDDING - REPORTEDLY A FAVORITE OF HARR
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  All-purpose flour
1 tb Baking powder
1/8 ts Salt
2 c  Apples, peeled and chopped
1/2 c  Walnuts, chopped
Unsweetened whipped cream
Poached apple slices
In large mixer bowl, beat together eggs, sugar, and vanilla until
light and fluffy. Stir together flour, baking powder, and salt. Blend
into creamed mixture. Fold in apple and walnuts. Turn into greased
and floured 9x9x2" baking pan. Bake in 325F oven 30-35 min. Spoon
into dessert dishes. Serve warm with whipped cream and apple slices.

Serves 6-8.
