Title: PINK-GRAPEFRUIT GELATIN
Categories: Desserts, June 1996 i
Yield: 6 Servings
2 3/4 c  Pink grapefruit juice --
Freshly sqeezed, str
5    Packets unflavored gelatin
1/2 c  Sugar
Pour 1 cup grapefruit juice into a bowl. Sprinkle gelatin over and let
stand to soften, about 5 minutes. Bring sugar and 1 1/2 cups water to
a boil over medium-high heat, stirring to dissolve sugar. Pour hot
syrup over gelatin; mix well to dissolve gelatin. Stir in remaining
juice. Pour into a 9x13 inch Pyrex pan, cover and refrigerate until
set, about 4 hours. When ready to serve, cut gelatin into 1-inch
cubes and serve in tall glasses.
