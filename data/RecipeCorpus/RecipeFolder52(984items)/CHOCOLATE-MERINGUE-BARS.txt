Title: CHOCOLATE MERINGUE BARS
Categories: Chocolate, Bars
Yield: 36 Servings

1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2    Eggs -- separated
1 ts Vanilla
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salr
3 tb Milk -- or water
6 oz Chocolate chips
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Nuts -- , optional
