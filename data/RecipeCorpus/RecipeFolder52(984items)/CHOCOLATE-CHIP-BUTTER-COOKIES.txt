Title: CHOCOLATE CHIP BUTTER COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 64 Servings
1 lb Butter or margarine
Powdered sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Vanilla
4 1/2 c  Flour
12 oz Semisweet chocolate pieces
Cream butter and 2 cups powdered sugar. Add salt, vanilla and flour.
Mix to blend. Stir in chocolate pieces. Pinch off pieces of dough.
Roll into 1-inch balls and flatten with fork on baking sheet. Bake at
350F 15 minutes. Sprinkle with powdered sugar while hot.
