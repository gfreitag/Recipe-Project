Title: CHOCOLATE SAUCE FUDGY
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
6 oz Semisweet chocolate
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1/4 c  Half and half or cream
1 tb Unsalted butter
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Coarsely chop the chocolate, then melt it over hot water in the top
of a doubler boiler along with the syrup. Mix well, and add
half-and-half. Blend well, and stir in the butter. Remove pan from
heat and add vanilla and mix.

Serve warm or allow to cool and refrigerate.

Makes about 1 1/2 cups
