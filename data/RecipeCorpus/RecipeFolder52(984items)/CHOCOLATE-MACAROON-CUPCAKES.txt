Title: CHOCOLATE MACAROON CUPCAKES
Categories: Desserts, Passover, Nuts, Chocolate
Yield: 12 Servings
-SHERI KONOPKO (GXCH63B)
1 oz Bitter chocolate
3/4 c  Condensed milk
1/8 ts Salt
2 c  Shredded coconut
1 ts Vanilla
Melt chocolate; condensed milk and salt. Cook 10 min. Add shredded
coconut, and vanilla. Place in greased cupcake tin Bake at 350 for 30
min.
