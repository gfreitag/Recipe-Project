Title: CHOCOLATE BANANA PINA COLADAS
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Fruits, Mix recipes
Yield: 1 Servings
6 oz Light rum
1/2    Banana
3 tb Chocolate syrup
1/4 cn Coco lopez
Pineapple juice
Whipped cream
Shaved coconut; optional
Fill Blender with ice Add light rum Add banana Add chocolate syrup
Add Coco Lopez Cream of Coconut Fill the rest of the way up with
pineapple juice Blend until smooth and serve in daquari glasses
topped with whipped cream. (optional: Shaved coconut on top of the
whipped cream)
