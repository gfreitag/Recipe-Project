Title: CHOCOLATE MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
pn Salt
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
2    Eggs
2/3 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  Butter, melted
3 1/2 oz Semi-sweet baking chocolate,
-melted
1/2 c  Vanilla chips
Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Grease muffin pan or line with paper
liners.

Sift flour, salt, and baking powder into a bowl.  Add brown sugar and
pecans.

In another bowl, combine eggs, milk, vanilla, butter, and melted
chocolate.

Stir dry mixture into wet mixture.  DO NOT OVERMIX. Lightly stir in
vanilla chips.
