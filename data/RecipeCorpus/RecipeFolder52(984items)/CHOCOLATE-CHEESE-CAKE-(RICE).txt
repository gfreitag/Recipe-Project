Title: CHOCOLATE CHEESE CAKE (RICE)
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
12 oz Semi-sweet chocolate.melt
-in dbl. boiler.
5 tb Butter.
1 1/2 lb Cream cheese at room temp.
3 ts Vanilla.
4    Eggs beaten.
5 tb Cocoa sifted.
-Crust I Refer to cheese
-cake crust.
INSTRUCTIONS: Mix all ingredients for fifteen minutes. See cheese
cake for baking instructions.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
