Title: CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES~ JOYCE'S
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg beaten
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Salt
Beat first five ingredients to-gether and add flour, soda and salt,
blend well and add 1/2 pkg. chocolate chips. Drop by spoonfuls on
baking sheet and bake 8 -
10    min. in 375 oven.
