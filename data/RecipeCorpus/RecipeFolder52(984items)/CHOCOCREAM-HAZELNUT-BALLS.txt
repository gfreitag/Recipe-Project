Title: CHOCOCREAM HAZELNUT BALLS
Categories: Candy
Yield: 24 Pieces
1/2 c  Finely chopped hazelnuts
-(Oregon hazelnuts)
1 c  Vanilla cookie crumbs
1 c  Powdered sugar
2 tb Cocoa
2 tb Corn syrup
1/4 c  Cream
1/2 ts Vanilla
Combine all ingredients and mix well. The mixture should be moist to
the touch. Roll into balls about the size of a walnut. Roll in
powdered sugar or finely chopped hazelnuts. Store for at least 2 days
in covered container.
