Title: CHOCOLATE CHERRY CAKE & FROSTING
Categories: Cakes, Frostings, Favorite
Yield: 18 Servings
MMMMM----------------------------CAKE---------------------------------
1 pk Devils food cake mix (with
-pudding in mix)
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
2    Eggs; beaten
1 ts Almond extract
21 oz Cherry pie filling
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
6 oz Semi-sweet chocolate chips
4 tb Margarine or butter
2 1/2 c  Powdered sugar; sifted
1/2 ts Almond extract
3 tb Milk (to 5 tb)
CAKE: Mix together cake mix, sour cream, eggs, almond extract and pie
filling BY HAND. Pour into a greased and floured 9 x 13-inch pan.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick
inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.  Cool 5-10
minutes and top with frosting.

FROSTING: Melt chocolate chips and margarine or butter together. Stir
in powdered sugar, almond extract and milk to spreading consistency.
Spread warm frosting on warm cake.
