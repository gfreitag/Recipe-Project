Title: CHOCOLATE SHORTBREAD
Categories: Chocolate, Cookies
Yield: 36 Servings
1/4 c  Cocoa
1/2 c  Powdered Sugar
1 c  Unsalted Butter -- cut in
1    In. cubes
2 c  Flour
Preheat oven to 350.

Put all ingredients in a food processor and process until a ball of
dough forms.  Roll out the dough to 1/4-inch thickness and cut into
pieces (rectangular). Place on ungreased cookie sheets and bake 13-15
minutes.

Makes about 36.
