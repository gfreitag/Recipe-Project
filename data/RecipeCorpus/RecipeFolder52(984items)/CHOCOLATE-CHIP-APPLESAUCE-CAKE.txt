Title: CHOCOLATE CHIP APPLESAUCE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Chocolate, Apples
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  Margarine -- or applesauce
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
3 tb Cocoa
2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 c  Applesauce, unsweetened
3/4 c  Chocolate chips
TOPPING
2 tb Sugar
1 c  Nuts -- chopped - opt.
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1 ts Cinnamon
Mix cake ingredients as usual.  Put into greased 9"x13" pan. Mix
topping ings. and sprinkle over. Bake about 40 Min. at 350F.
