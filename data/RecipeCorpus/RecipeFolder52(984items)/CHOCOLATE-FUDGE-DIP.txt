Title: CHOCOLATE FUDGE DIP
Categories: Dip, Fruit
Yield: 1 Servings
6 tb Plain nonfat yogurt
6 tb Chocolate fudge sauce --
Bottled
1 1/2 ts Frozen orange juice
Concentrate -- thawed
In small bowl, combine all ingredients. Stir or whisk until smooth.
Makes about 3/4 cup.
