Title: CHOCOLATE PECAN ORANGE BALLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 100 Servings
1 lb Pecans; shelled and ground
8 1/2 oz Chocolate wafers; crushed
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 ts Orange peel
1/3 c  Honey
Powdered sugar
Combine all ingredient, except powdered sugar, in a large bowl. Mix
well/ chill for 20 minutes. Shape by spoonful into round balls. Store
in a tightly covered, airtight container. Just before servings or
giving, roll
