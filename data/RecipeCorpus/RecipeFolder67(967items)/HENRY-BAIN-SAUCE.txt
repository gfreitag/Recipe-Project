Title: HENRY BAIN SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
12 oz Bottle chili sauce
14 oz Bottle ketchup
11 oz Bottle A-1 sauce
10 oz Bottle worcestershire
8 oz Bottle good chutney
Tabasco sauce to taste
(about 1/2 bottle)
Large pieces fruit, chopped
Mix all together.  Taste until you get it right.
