Title: HELLEN HOLDER'S ZUCKINNI BREAD
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 2 Loaves
3    Eggs
2 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Oil
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour (plain)
2 c  Grated zuckinni
3 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
1 ts Soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Nuts (Optional)
Beat eggs well, add oil and vinegar.  Then add flour, zuckinni, soda
and powder. Bake at 325 degrees for 60 minutes. NOTE: If you use self
rising flour omit salt, soda and baking powder. Variations: Use
dates. Allspice instead of cinnamon.
