Title: HOMEMADE BABY WIPES
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Roll Bounty Paper Towels
1    White
2 1/4 c  Water
2 tb Baby Shampoo OR Liquid Baby
1    Soap
1 tb Baby Oil
Cut roll in half and remove center cardboard.  In Baby Wipe
Container, mix liquid ingred. and place half of roll in container.
Place on lid and tip upside down. To use, pull out from center.
