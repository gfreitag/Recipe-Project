Title: HIDDEN VALLEY RANCH BAKED CHICKEN
Categories: Chicken
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Chicken pieces (to 3 lbs)
1 pk Ranch Salad Dressing Mix
-Hidden Valley Ranch
Dredge 2-3 lbs. chicken pieces in Hidden Valley Original Ranch Salad
Dressing Mix, then bake skin side up at 325-degrees for 45 minutes
until brown and crispy.
