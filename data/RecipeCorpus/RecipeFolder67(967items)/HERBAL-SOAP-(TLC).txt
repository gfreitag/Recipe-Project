Title: HERBAL SOAP (TLC)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lg Block olive or veg soap
25 g  Loosely chopped herbs
3 dr Essential oil - thyme
- rosemary, horsechestnut
1 tb Oatmeal, finely ground
1. Grate the soap into basin and add the remaining ingredients. 2.
Heat gently in a bain-marie until it melts and mix well. 3. Line an
egg box with waxed paper and pour the soap into each section
