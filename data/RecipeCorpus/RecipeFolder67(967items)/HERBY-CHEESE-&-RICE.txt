Title: HERBY CHEESE & RICE
Categories: Grains
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  White rice
2 tb Olive oil
3 tb Fresh basil; parsley, or
-mix of the two or 1T dried
-herbs
1 1/2 c  Mozzarella; grated
2 tb Parmesan; grated
Cook rice. Empty into large bowl and stir in olive oil and herbs. Stir
mozzarella into hot rice--it must melt. Sprinkle parmesan over top and
serve immediately.
