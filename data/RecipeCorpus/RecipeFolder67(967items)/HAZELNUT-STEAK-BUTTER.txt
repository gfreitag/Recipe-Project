Title: HAZELNUT STEAK BUTTER
Categories: Sauces, Condiments, Meats
Yield: 1 Recipe
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Oregon hazelnuts, chopped
1 1/2 ts Chopped chives
1/4 ts Tabasco sauce
Yield: about 2/3 cup.

Melt 2 tablespoons of the butter; add hazelnuts and SautÂ‚ until
lightly brown. Turn into small bowl and cool thoroughly. Add the
remaining butter, chives and Tabasco. Beat until creamy. This is
enough butter to serve with 6 hamburgers or 4-6 steaks.
