Title: HEARTY WHOLEGRAIN COFFEECAKE
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  All-purpose biscuit mix
-(Bisquick)
2/3 c  Milk
1/2 c  Quaker Oats (Quick or
-Old-Fashioned, uncooked)
1    Egg
2 tb Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Topping:
2/3 c  Quaker Oats *(quick or old
-fashioned, uncooked)
2/3 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/4 c  Butter or margarine, melted
1/2 ts Cinnamon
From: Quaker Oats WholeGrain Cookbook

CoffeeCake:

For coffee cake, combine all ingredients; mix just until dry
ingredients are moistened.

For topping, combine all ingredients, mix well, Spread half of batter
into greased 8-inch square baking dish; sprinkle half of topping over
batter. Top with remaining batter and topping. Bake in preheated
moderate oven (375) 35 to 40 minutes or til wooden pick comes out
clean. Serve warm.

Microwave directions: Cook in microwave oven at HIGH 6 to 7 minutes
or til wooden pick comes out clean, rotating after 3 minutes of
cooking.
