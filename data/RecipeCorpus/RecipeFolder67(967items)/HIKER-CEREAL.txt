Title: HIKER CEREAL
Categories: Outdoors, Camping
Yield: 6 Cups
3 c  Oatmeal
1 c  Wheat germ
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 c  Slivered almonds
3/4 c  Packed brown sugar
At home, mix ingredients and spread on cookie sheets. Toast in 250
F8F oven for an hour.  Cool, and store in plastic bag. Chopped dried
fruit can be added.  Makes about 6 cups. Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Trail Mix
