Title: HEALTH BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1/3 c  Sugar (white)
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Ground quick cooking -
Rolled oats
1/2 c  Molasses
2 tb Melted shortening
2 c  Sour milk
1 c  Raisins
Sift the first five ingredients together.  Add the oats, molasses,
melted shortening and milk.  Mix thoroughly and add the raisins. Pour
into a large greased loaf pan.  Let it set for 20 minutes then bake
about 1 hour at 350F. Note: The rolled oats may be ground with a
rolling pin or in the meat grinder. Source: Chatelaine Golden Anniv.
Recipes.ch.
