Title: HAZELNUT PRALINE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
2 tb Chopped toasted hazelnuts
In small saucepan heat sugar over med heat without stirring but
shaking pan occas for 3 mins oruntil sugar has melted and is golden.
Sprinkle nuts onto buttered plate andpour syrup over. Let cool until
hardened; break into pces. Process in food processor until finely
ground or place in heavy plastic bag and crush with rolling pin.
