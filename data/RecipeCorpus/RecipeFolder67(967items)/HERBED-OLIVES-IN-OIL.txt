Title: HERBED OLIVES IN OIL
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
11 oz Cracked green olives in
Brine -- drained and rinsed
6    Thin lemon slices-quartered
3    Cloves garlic
2 ts Thyme -- dried
1 ts Peppercorns -- crushed
2    Bay leaves
Olive oil
Arrange all ingredients except oil in a 1-pint jar. Pour oil into jar
to cover olives. Store covered at room temperature at least 1 week
but no more that 1 month.
