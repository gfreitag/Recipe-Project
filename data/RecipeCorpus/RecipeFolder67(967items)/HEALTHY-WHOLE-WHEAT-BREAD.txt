Title: HEALTHY WHOLE WHEAT BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
-Conrad Feierabend
MMMMM------------------------MEDIUM LOAF-----------------------------
1 c  -Water
2 tb Vegetable oil

3/4 ts -Salt

1 tb Wheat germ
1 tb Oat bran
1 tb Wheat flakes
1 1/2 tb Oats

1 1/2 c  Bread flour
2 ts Yeast

Medium
