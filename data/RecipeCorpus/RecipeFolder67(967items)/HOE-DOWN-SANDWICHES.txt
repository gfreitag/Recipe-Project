Title: HOE DOWN SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 8 Sandwiches
-Sue Woodward
4    Eggs; hard cooked; chopped
3/4 c  Ham; chopped
1/4 c  Celery; chopped
1/4 c  Dill pickle; chopped
Miracle Whip salad dressing
16 sl Whole wheat bread
16 sl Kraft American Singles
-Pasteurized Process Cheese
Soft Parkay margarine
Combine eggs, ham, celery, pickle, and enough salad dressing to
moisten; mix lightly.  For each sandwich, cover slice of bread with
slice of cheese, egg mixture, slice of cheese, and second slice of
bread. Spread bread with margarine.  Place sandwiches on cookie
sheet; broil on each side until golden brown cheese melts.

Source:  Kraft advertisement torn out of old TV Guide, rec'd from Joni
Cloud (5/95)
