Title: HEALTHY VEGGIE BURGER
Categories: Main meal, Sandwiches, Grilling/ba
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Hamburger Buns, Low Calorie
1    Hamburger Patty (Morningstar
Fat Free)
1 oz Red Onion -- sliced
1/2 oz Roma Tomato -- sliced
3/4 oz Romaine Lettuce
1 1/2 ts Ketchup
1 1/2 ts Nonfat Mayonnaise
1 1/2 tb Bac'n Pieces
Put hamburger patty and onion on GRILL EXPRESS. Grill for 1 to 1 1/2
minutes. Pile on bun spread with mayonaise, ketchup. Add lettuce and
sprinkle bacon pieces over all.
