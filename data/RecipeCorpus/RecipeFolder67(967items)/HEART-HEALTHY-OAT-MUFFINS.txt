Title: HEART HEALTHY OAT MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Diabetic
Yield: 12 Muffins
2 c  Oat bran
1/4 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt or substitute (opt)
1 ts Nutmeg; heaping (opt)
1/2 c  Skim or 1% milk
2    Egg whites; slightly beaten
1/4 c  Honey
2 tb Vegetable or canola oil
1/2 ts Vanilla
Heat oven to 425 degrees.  Coat muffin pan with cooking spray or line
with paper baking cups.  Combine oat bran, brown sugar, baking powder
and salt and nutmeg. Add milk, egg whites, honey and oil. Stir in
vanilla. Do not over mix.  Bake 15-20 minutes, until lightly browned.
Variations: Add 1/2 c raisins, a mashed banana, 1/2 c blueberries or
raspberries or a chopped apple tossed with 1 teaspoon cinnamon.

Nutritional info per serving: 105 cal; 4g pro, 21g carb, 3g fat(23%)
Exchanges: 1 bread, .1 meat, .5 fat
