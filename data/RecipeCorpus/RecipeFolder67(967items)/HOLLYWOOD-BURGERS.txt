Title: HOLLYWOOD BURGERS
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Lean Ground Beef
1 1/2 c  Cheddar Cheese; Shredded
1/2 c  Tomato;Fresh,Seeded,Chopped
1/2 c  Green Pepper; Seeded,Chopped
1/2 c  Onion; Chopped
1/2 ts Sea Salt Or Kosher Salt
Freshly Ground Black Pepper
Combine the beef, cheese, tomato, green pepper, onion, salt, and
pepper to taste, blending well.  Shape into patties, pressing gently
to make the meat and vegetables cling together. Grease the grill
well. Place the patties on the grill, 3 to 4 inches above the hot
coals, and grill until well browned, turning as needed to cook
evenly. Cook until done as desired, rare in about 12 minutes, medium
in about 15, and well done in 18 to 20 minutes.
