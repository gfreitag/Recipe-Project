Title: HOGAHOLICS DRY RUB
Categories: Bar-b-q, Spices, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Lemon peel
1 tb Garlic powder
1 tb Onion powder
1 tb Chili powder
1 tb Paprika
1 tb MSG
1/2 tb Black pepper
1/2 tb Cayenne pepper
1/2 tb White pepper
2 tb Salt
2 tb Sugar
Combine all ingredients and rub into meat before cooking.
