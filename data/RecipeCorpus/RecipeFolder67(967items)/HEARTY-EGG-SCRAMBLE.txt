Title: HEARTY EGG SCRAMBLE
Categories: Breakfast, Potatoes
Yield: 6 Servings
1/3 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
2 md Potatoes, peeled, cooked and
-cubed
1 1/2 c  Julienned fully cooked ham
6    Eggs
2 tb Water
1 ds Pepper
"Hearty Egg Scramble is a filling and flavorful dish. The ham and
potatoes are comforting additions.  The cost for this dish is
estimated at $.94 [per serving]."

In a large skillet, cook onion and green pepper in butter until
crisp-tender.  Add potatoes and ham; cook and stir for 5 minutes. In a
bowl, beat eggs, water and pepper; pour over ham mixture. Cook over
low heat, stirring constantly, until eggs are set.
