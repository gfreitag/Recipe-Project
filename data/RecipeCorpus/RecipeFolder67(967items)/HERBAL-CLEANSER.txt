Title: HERBAL CLEANSER
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ts Anhydrous lanolin
1/4 c  Almond oil
1/4 c  Elderflower infusion
1/4 c  Glycerin
2 ts Witch hazel
Melt lanolin and almond oil in a glass or enamel saucepan over boiling
water.  Remove from heat and slowly beat in elderflower tea with an
electric beater until the mixture is almost cool and very] well
blended. Transfer to a sterilized jar.  This is good for dry skin
(for oily skin, substitute an infusion of sage or yarrow, for normal
skin, try one with rose water, lady's mantle, or chamomile.) Source:
Victoria Magazine
