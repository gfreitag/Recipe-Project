Title: HIDDEN VALLEY RANCH MASHED POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Mashed potatoes
1 pk Ranch Salad Dressing Mix
-Hidden Valley Original
Butter or margarine (opt.)
Prepare 4 cups mashed potatoes from scratch, or instant mashed
potatoes according to package directions, omitting salt. Stir in
Hidden Valley Original Ranch Salad Dressing Mix and if desired add
butter or margarine.
