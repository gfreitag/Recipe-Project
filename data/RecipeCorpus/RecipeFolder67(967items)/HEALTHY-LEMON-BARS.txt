Title: HEALTHY LEMON BARS
Categories: Bakery, Bar cookies
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Brown sugar
3    Eggs -- well beaten
2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Coconut -- finely shredded
1/2 c  Raisins
1 c  Walnuts -- optional
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Grated lemon rind
Heat oven to 350F. Mix flour, butter and 1/4 cup sugar thoroughly.
Press= firmly into bottom of lightly greased oblong pan 13x 9 1/2 x
2. Bake 10= minutes. Mix eggs, 2 cups sugar and salt together. Stir
in rest of= ingredients, spread over the partially baked bottom
layer. Bake about 25= minutes. Top will be lightly brown.
