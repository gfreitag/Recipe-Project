Title: HAZELNUT HEAVEN
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 c  Chopped Oregon hazelnuts
6    Eggs
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Whipping cream
Beat eggs; add sugar. Combine with hazelnuts and flour. Bake at 350
in flat pan until golden brown. When cool, spread whipping cream,
then roll into a roll. Keep frozen.
