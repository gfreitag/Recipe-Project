Title: HERMAN COFFEE CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
T tsp. Baking Powder
1/4 ts Each Salt and Baking Powder
1 c  Herman starter
1/3 c  Oil
1    Egg
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Chopped Nuts
Topping (recipe follows) In large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking
powder, salt, and baking soda. Add starter, oil, egg, raisins and
nuts; stir to mix well. Spread in greased and floured 8" or 9" square
pan; sprinkle with topping. Bake in preheated 350 deg. oven, 30 to 35
mins., or until browned and pick inserted in center comes out clean.
Cool slightly in pan on rack; cut into squares. Best served warm.
Note: The original coffee cake recipe was doubled and baked in a 13 x
9 x 2 inch pan about 45 mins. TOPPING: In a small bowl, mix 1/2 C.
packed brown sugar,m 1-1/2 tsp. each flour, cinnamon and granulated
sugar. Cut in 2 Tbs. butter (or margarine), at room temperature,
until crumbly.
