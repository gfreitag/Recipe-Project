Title: HOMEMADE BEEF STICK
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
5 lb Cheap lamb or beef (ground)
2 1/2 ts Liquid smoke (Wright's)
2 1/2 ts Garlic salt
2 1/2 ts Mustard seed
5 ts Heaped Morton's tender quick
Mix all ingredients together well and refrigerate. Remove and kneed
for 5 minutes each of the next three days. On day 4, divide into 6
segments and form into logs (~1 1/2-2 inch diameter). Place on
broiler pan and bake in oven at 160 F for 9 hrs. When done, pat with
paper towel to remove excess grease.  Keeps in refrigerator for 3
wks, and can be frozen.
