Title: HOME ON THE RANGE TUNA SALAD
Categories: Salads, Lunch, Low-fat
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Water-packed Tuna, drained
- and flaked
1 c  Low-fat cottage cheese
2 ea Hard-boiled eggs, peeled &
- chopped
1/4 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Chopped green onion
2 tb Chopped parsley
1 ts Lemon pepper seasoning
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Diet mayonaise (4 tbs)
1.  In mixing bowl, combine all ingredients. Chill. Use as salad or
sandwich filling. 2. May be stored in refrigerator up to one week.
