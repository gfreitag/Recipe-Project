Title: HAZELNUT PIE CRUST
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Crust
1 c  Oregon hazelnuts,
-- roasted and ground
1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Allspice, ground
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
Mix together hazelnuts, crumbs, sugar and allspice; blend in butter
to form a crumbly mixture. Press against bottom and sides of a 9" pie
plate. Bake in 400 oven 5 minutes, or until lightly browned. Cool
thoroughly.
