Title: HOMEMADE CAJUN SEASONING
Categories: Spices
Yield: 999 Servings
2 ts White pepper
2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Onion powder
2 ts Ground red pepper
2 ts Paprika
2 ts Black pepper
Stir all ingredients together.  Store in an airtight container in a
cool, dry place.  Use this seasoning to add a little zip to many main
dishes and meats.
