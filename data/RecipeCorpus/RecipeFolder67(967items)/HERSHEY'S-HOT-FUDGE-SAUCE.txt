Title: HERSHEY'S HOT FUDGE SAUCE
Categories: Desserts, Sauces, Chocolate
Yield: 2 Servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Hershey's cocoa
1/3 c  Butter
5 oz Evaporated milk
1 ts Vanilla
Combine sugar and cocoa in small saucepan; blend in evaporated milk
and corn syrup.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until
mixture boils; boil and stir one minute. Remove from heat; stir in
butter and vanilla.  Serve warm.  From Hershey's.

Makes 2 1/2 cups
