Title: HOMEMADE BUNS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Lukewarm water
1 ts Sugar
1 pk Yeast
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Water
1 ea Egg beaten
5 c  Flour
Put yeast in the water with the tsp. sugar, Let sit for 10 minutes.
Heat 3/4 c water, add shortening to melt, add rest of water,sugar,
salt, vinegar and egg. Add yeast mixture and 2 1/2 cups of the flour,
beat until smooth, add rest of flour. Let rise until double in bulk.
Make into buns and rise again. Bake 20 - 25 min. in 375 F oven
