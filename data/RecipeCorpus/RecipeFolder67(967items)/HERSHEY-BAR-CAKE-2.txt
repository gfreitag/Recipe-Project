Title: HERSHEY BAR CAKE 2
Categories: Cakes, Chocolate
Yield: 15 Servings
1    HERSHEY'S milk chocolate bar
-(7 oz), broken into pieces
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
-(1/2 stick)
1 2/3 c  Boiling water
2 1/3 c  All-purpose flour
2 c  Packed light brown sugar
2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
1 ts Vanilla extract
Heat oven to 350'F. Grease and flour 13x9x2" baking pan. In bowl,
combine chocolate bar pieces, butter and boiling water; stir until
chocolate is melted and mixture is smooth. In large mixer bowl, stir
together flour, brown sugar, baking soda and salt; gradually add
chocolate mixture, beating until thoroughly blended. Blend in eggs,
sour cream and vanilla; beat 1 minute on medium speed. Pour batter
into prepared pan. Bake 35-40 minutes or until wooden pick inserted
in center comes out clean. Cool completely in pan on wire rack; frost
as desired. Makes 12-15 servings.
