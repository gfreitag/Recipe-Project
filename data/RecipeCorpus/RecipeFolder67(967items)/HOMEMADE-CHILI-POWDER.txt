Title: HOMEMADE CHILI POWDER
Categories: Seasonings
3 TB paprika
Yield: 1 Servings
1 TB ground cumin
2 TB oregano
1 ts red or cayenne pepper
1/2 ts garlic powder

Mix well. Place in an airtight container. Adjust red pepper to taste
for a hot or mild blend. This makes
2/3    cup.
