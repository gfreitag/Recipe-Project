Title: HIBISCUS-GRAPE ICED TEA
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  White grape juice
1 c  Hibiscus tea
Ice cubes
1/2 c  Carbonated water
Orange slices for garnish
Stir the juice into the tea and pour over ice in 2 large glasses. Add
carbonated water and garnish with slices of orange.
