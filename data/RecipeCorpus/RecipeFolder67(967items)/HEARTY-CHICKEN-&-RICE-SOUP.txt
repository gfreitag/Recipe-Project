Title: HEARTY CHICKEN & RICE SOUP
Categories: Cyberealm, Soups, Low-cal, Low-fat
Yield: 8 Servings
10 c  Chicken broth
1 md Onion
1 c  Celery [sliced]
1 c  Carrots [sliced]
1/4 c  Parsley [snipped]
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Thyme leaves [dried]
1    Bay leaf
1 1/2 c  Chicken [cubed]
2 tb Lime juice
Lime slices for garnish
1) Combine the broth, onion, celery, carrots, parsley, pepper, thyme,
and bay leaf in a dutch oven, and bring to a boil over high heat,
stirring once or twice... 2) Reduce heat to low and simmer,
uncovered, 10 to 15 min., then add the chicken and simmer for 5 to 10
min. more or `til the chicken is done... 3) Remove and discard bay
leaf, stir in the rice and lime juice just before serving... garnish
with lime slices...
