Title: HOLLY'S CHEESY ONION RICE
Categories: Side dish, Rice, Velveeta, Cheese
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Water
2 c  Minute Rice
4    Chicken bouillon cubes
4    Slices Velveeta
4 oz Chives & Onion
-Cream Cheese
Bring water to full boil in medium sauce pan. Add bouillon cubes;
stir till cubes fully disolve. Stir in rice. Cover and remove from
heat. Let stand 5 minutes. Add velveeta and cream cheese; stir till
well mixed.
