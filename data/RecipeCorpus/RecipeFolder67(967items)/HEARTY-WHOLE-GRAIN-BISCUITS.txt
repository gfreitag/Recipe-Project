Title: HEARTY WHOLE GRAIN BISCUITS
Categories: Bakery, Breakfast, Tofu
Yield: 24 Biscuits
8 oz Firm silken tofu
1/4 c  Apple juice
1 c  Wholewheat flour
1/2 c  Rye flour
1/2 c  Wheatgerm, toasted
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt, optional
1/4 ts Sesame seeds, toasted
Preheat oven to 450F & oil cookie sheets. Blend tofu until smooth &
blend in the apple & lemon juices. Transfer to a medium-sized mixing
bowl & set aside. Sift together remaining ingredients, except the
sesame seeds. Fold flours & seeds into tofu mixture. Turn out onto a
lightly floured board, roll out dough to 1/2" thickness, cut with
cookie cutter & bake for
12    minutes. Serve immediately.
