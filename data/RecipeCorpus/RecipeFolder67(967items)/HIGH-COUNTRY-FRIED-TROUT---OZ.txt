Title: HIGH COUNTRY FRIED TROUT - OZ
Categories: Fish, Oz
Yield: 2 Servings
2 ea Trout,fresh
1/2 c  Plain flour
Cooking oil
Pour small amount of cooking oil in pan and heat. Clean trout and
roll them in flour and place in pan. Cook for approx 3 or 4 minutes
on each side. Serve when both sides are cooked.

Source: Bush Cooking by Max Bryant Published by the Kangaroo Press
ISBN 0 86417 230 3
