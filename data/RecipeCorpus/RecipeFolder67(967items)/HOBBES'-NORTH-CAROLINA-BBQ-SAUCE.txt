Title: HOBBES' NORTH CAROLINA BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Apple cider vinegar
2 ts Red pepper
3/4 c  Water
1 ds Sugar
2/3 c  Minced onions
1    Bay leaf
1    Clove garlic, crushed
2/3 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Peanut oil
1 ts Black pepper
Combine all the ingredients in a small saucepan. Bring to a rapid
boil, then simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat, cool, and store
your bottle in the frig.

You can play around with the ingredients but the vinegar and red
pepper are the main jewels.

: Hobbes
