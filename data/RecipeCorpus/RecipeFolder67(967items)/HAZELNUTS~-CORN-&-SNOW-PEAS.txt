Title: HAZELNUTS~ CORN & SNOW PEAS
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Fresh-cut corn kernels
8 oz Fresh Chinese pea pods
-- sliced into thirds
3 tb Diced pimento; drained
2 tb Margarine
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
1/3 c  Roasted & chopped hazelnuts
-(Oregon hazelnuts)
In a saucepan, melt margarine. Add corn, salt and pepper, and cook
for 4 minutes. Add pimento and pea pods and continue to cook for 4 to
6 minutes. Add hazelnuts and serve.
