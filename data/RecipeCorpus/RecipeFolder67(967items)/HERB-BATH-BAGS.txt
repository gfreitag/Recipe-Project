Title: HERB BATH BAGS
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
Cheesecloth or muslin
Needle or sewing machine
Thread
1    Rib bons, lace -- trim etc
Make bags out of muslin or cheesecloth.  Decorate with trims and
notions. Add ribbons so that the bags can hang from the faucet into
the bath water. Fill bags with herbal bath mixtures. If you are
really handy with machine or needle, bags can be made into shapes.
