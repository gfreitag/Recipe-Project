Title: HERBED PECAN RUB
Categories: Sauces, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Pecans  -- broken
3    Cloves garlic  -- cut up
1/2 c  Fresh oregano
1/2 c  Fresh thyme
1/2 ts Lemon peel  -- finely
Shredded
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Cooking oil
In a blender or food processor, combine all ingredients EXCEPT oil.
Cover and blend several times, scaping sides, until a paste forms.
With machine running, gradually add oil until mixture forms a paste.
Rub onto fish or chicken.

Makes 1/2 cup, enough for 3 pounds of meat.

: Better Homes & Gardens, July 1996
