Title: HERB RICE
Categories: Rice, Stuffing
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Raw rice
1 c  Margarine or butter
1 c  Sliced onions
1 ts Thyme, sage,
-marjoram (each)
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Chopped parsley
3/4 c  Celery and leaves.
Cook rice and let it cool. Melt margarine and add the rest ot the
ingredients. Cook together for 5 min. Add rice and mix well. This is
also good for stuffing. Aviva from Petach Tikva, Israel
