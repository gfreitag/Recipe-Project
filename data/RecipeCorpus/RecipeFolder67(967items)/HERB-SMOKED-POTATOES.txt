Title: HERB-SMOKED POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Side dishes
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Olive oil
1/4 ts Liquid smoke
16    Red new potatoes,
-cut in half
1 tb Rosemary, minced
Black pepper
-to taste
Combine the oil and liquid smoke.  Add to the potatoes with the
pepper and rosemary.  Stir until potatoes are well coated. Wrap the
potatoes in foil and place on a baking sheet. Bake in a preheated
400-degree oven 25 minutes, or until tender. Serve warm or at room
temperature.
