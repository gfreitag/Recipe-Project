Title: HERSHEY'S COCOA CREAM PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Pie
1/2 c  Hershey's Cocoa
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Cornstarch
1/4 ts Salt
3 c  Milk
3 tb Butter or margarine
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1    Baked 9-inch pie crust
-OR-
-graham cracker crumb crust
- (cooled)
Sweetened whipped cream
In medium saucepan, stir together cocoa, sugar, cornstarch and salt.
Gradually add milk to dry ingredients, stirring until smooth. Cook
over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils; boil l
minute. Remove from heat; stir in butter and vanilla. Pour into baked
pie crust. Carefully press plastic wrap directly onto pie filling.
Cool; refrigerate 6 to 8 hours. Top with whipped cream. Cover;
refrigerate leftover pie. 6 to 8 servings.
