Title: HIDDEN VALLEY RANCH PASTA PRIMAVERA
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
6 oz Rotelle pasta
3 tb Olive oil
1 pk Ranch Salad Dressing Mix
-Hidden Valley Original
16 oz Vegetable medley, thawed
Cook rotelle pasta.  Drain.  Stir in olive oil, one package Hidden
Valley Original Ranch Salad Dressing Mix and thawed vegetable medley.
Heat and serve.
