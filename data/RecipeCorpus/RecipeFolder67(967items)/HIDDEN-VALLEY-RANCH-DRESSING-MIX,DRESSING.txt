Title: HIDDEN VALLEY RANCH DRESSING MIX/DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dressings, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
POWDER MIX
15    Square saltines -- (2")
2 c  Dry minced parsley flakes
1/2 c  Dry minced onions
2 tb Dry dill weed
1/4 c  Onion salt
1/4 c  Garlic salt
1/4 c  Onion powder
1/4 c  Garlic powder
SALAD DRESSING
1 tb Mix
1 c  Mayo
1 c  Buttermilk
Put crackers through blender on high speed until powdered. Add
parsley, minced onions and dill weed. Blend again until powdered.
Dump into bowl. Stir in onion salt, garlic salt,onion powder and
garlic powder. Put into container with  tight-fitting lid. Store at
room temp for 1 year. Makes 42 1 tb servings. TO USE MIX-Combine mix,
mayo and buttermilk. Yield 1 pint. Source: Gloria  Pitzer
