Title: HEARTY BEEF STEW
Categories: Stews, Beef
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Beef, cut in 1" cubes
2    Carrots, sliced
1 lg Onion, diced
3    Stalks celery, sliced
1 cn Tomatoes; 28oz
1/2 c  Quick-cooking tapioca
1    Whole clove
2    Bay leaves
Salt and pepper

(Old Fashioned flavor Iike Grandma's thanks to the tapioca!)

Trim all fat from meat. Put all ingredients in CROCK-POT. Mix
thoroughly. Cover and cook on Low 12 hours. (High: 5 to 6 hours).
From Rival Crock-pot cook book, date unknown
