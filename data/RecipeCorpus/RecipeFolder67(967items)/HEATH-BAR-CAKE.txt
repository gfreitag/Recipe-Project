Title: HEATH BAR CAKE
Categories: Box cakes, Cakes
Yield: 15 Servings
1 pk Chocolate fudge cake mix
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
1    Jar caramel ice cream toppin
12 oz Cool Whip
4    Heath bars; crushed *
* eaisier to crush if frozen ***************************** Prepare
cake according to directions. Bake in 9x13" pan. While still hot,
poke the top full of holes with a wooden spoon handle. Pour condensed
milk over the top, then ice cream topping. REFRIGERATE OVERNIGHT.
Spread the Cool Whip over the top, then sprinkle with Heath bars.
