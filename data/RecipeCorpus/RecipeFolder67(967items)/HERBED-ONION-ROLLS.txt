Title: HERBED ONION ROLLS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 tb Instant chopped onion
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
1/2 ts Garlic powder
Combine Stir until well mixed.

Separate 1 (11 ounce) package of soft bread sticks leaving sticks
rolled. Place on a cookie sheet.  Press each bread round into a 3
inch circle. Brush bread rounds with olive oil mixture. Bake at 350 F
for 15 to 18 minutes or until golden brown.  Makes 8 servings.

From: Durkee-French Foods Shared By: Pat Stockett
