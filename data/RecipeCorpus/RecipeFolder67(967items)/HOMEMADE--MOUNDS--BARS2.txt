Title: HOMEMADE  MOUNDS  BARS2
Categories: Ew text imp
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Light corn syrup
14 oz Bag; coconut
12 oz Bag; chocolate chips
3/4    Bar Canning wax
Preparation Time: Preparation
: Boil syrup for 1 minute. Add coconut and stir. Let sit for 2 hours.
Wet hands and form into balls. Freeze. Then melt chocolate chips and
wax together and dip coconut balls into mixture. Place on greased
cookie sheet.
