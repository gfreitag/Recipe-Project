Title: HEARTY POTATO CHIVE BREAD
Categories: Herb, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
-Large Loaf
3/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Buttermilk
3 c  Bread flour
1/2 c  Instant potato flakes
2 1/2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Chopped dried chives
3 tb Applesauce
2 1/2 ts Yeast or
2 ts Fast acting yeast
