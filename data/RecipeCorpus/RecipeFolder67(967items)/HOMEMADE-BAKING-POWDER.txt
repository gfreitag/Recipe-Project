Title: HOMEMADE BAKING POWDER
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Soda
1 1/2 lb Cream of tartar
1/4 lb Cornstarch
The make the best (and double the strength of any on the market)
Baking Powder known. Try it. A. E. Crosby, chemist.

Twentieth Century Cookbook, 1906.

Origin: A Taste of Oregon Shared by: Sharon Stevens
