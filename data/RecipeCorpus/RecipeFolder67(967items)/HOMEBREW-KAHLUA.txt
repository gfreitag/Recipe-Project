Title: HOMEBREW KAHLUA
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Mix recipes, Bourbon
Yield: 1 Servings
8 c  Sugar
2    Vanilla Beans
4 oz Jar Freeze Dried Coffee
3/8 l  Bourbon
Mix sugar with 6 cups water; bring to a boil.  Boil for 6 minutes;
divide into 2 parts.  Chop vanilla beans into small pieces; add to 1
part sugar syrup with coffee. Add bourbon to second half; stir well.
Combine coffee mixture with bourbon mixture; blend well. Age for 6
weeks. Yields 1 gallon.
