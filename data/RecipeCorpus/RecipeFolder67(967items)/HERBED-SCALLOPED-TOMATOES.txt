Title: HERBED SCALLOPED TOMATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Casserole
Yield: 8 Servings
7 lb Ripe tomatoes, peeled,
Chopped and seeded
2 1/2 c  Herb seasoned stuffing
1 sm Onion chopped
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Freshly  ground pepper
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Powdered oregano
1/4 ts Powdered rosemary
5 tb Butter
Preheat oven to 375 F. Drain tomatoes well.  Toss with 2 cups of the
stuffing, onion, sugar, salt, pepper, nutmeg, oregano, and rosemary.
Place in a buttered 3 quart casserole. Sjprinkle with the remaining
1/2 cup stuffing and dot with butter.  Bake for 45 minutes submitted
by marina source: San Francisco A la Carte recipe book
