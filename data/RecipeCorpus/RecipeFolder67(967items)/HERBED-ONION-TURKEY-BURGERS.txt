Title: HERBED-ONION TURKEY BURGERS
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Ground turkey
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1    Envelope (1-ounce) dry onion
-soup mix
1 tb Minced fresh or 1 teaspoon
-dried thyme
2 ts Minced fresh or 1/2 teaspoon
-dried rosemary
1/2 ts Pepper
8    Hamburger buns
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPINGS-------------------------------
Honey mustard, mayonnaise,
-lettuce, sliced tomato
Combine first 6 ingredients. Shape into 8 patties. Grill over
medium-high heat (350F to 400F) 5 minutes on each side or to desired
degree of doneness. Serve on buns with desired toppings.
