Title: HEARRINGS WITH BEET SALAD FOR SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches, Fish
Yield: 1 Salad
1 lg Beet
1/2 lb Hearring filets (preserved)
1/2 c  To 1 cup cream (used as need
Salt and pepper
Bread in slices for to make
The sandwiches
Cook the beet and when is soft cut in thiny cubes, also cut the
hearring filets in little pieces, mix with cream as needed and add
salt and pepper. Fill the bread, add more cream if the bread is dry.
Patricia Wriedt
