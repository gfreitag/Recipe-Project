Title: HEAVENLY HERO
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  BEST FOODS Mayonnaise
2 tb Wine vinegar
2 ts Dried Italian seasoning
1 ds Garlic powder
1 lb Assorted deli meats
1 lb Assorted cheese
Lettuce
Tomato
Combine mayonnaise, wine vinegar, Italian seasoning and garlic powder.
Layer split loaf Italian bread with deli meat and cheese, lettuce,
tomato and mayonnaise mixture.
