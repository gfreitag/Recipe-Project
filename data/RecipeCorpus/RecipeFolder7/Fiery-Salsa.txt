Title: Fiery Salsa
Categories: Mexican, Hot, Appetizers, Sauces, Condiment
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Oregano
1 lg Onion
8    Tomatoes
1/2 sm Bunch cilantro (no stems)
10    Jalapeno peppers
Juice of 1 lime
Juice of 1 lemon
Dice onions, tomatoes, jalapenos, and cilantro as small as possible.
Combine the rest of the ingredients. Chill overnight.
