Title: Prune Pickles
Categories: Pickles
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Whole allspice
1 lb Prunes
1 tb Stick cinnamon
1 ts Whole cloves
Wash prunes.  Cover with cold water.  Cover and simmer until tender. Add
sugar, vinegar, and spices.  Simmer 20 minutes. Edith Johnson, Genesee, PA.
