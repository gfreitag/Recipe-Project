Title: Uncooked Cucumber Relish
Categories: Can/preserv
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Salt
1 ts Pepper
6 lg Cucumbers
3 sm Onions, Chopped Fine
1 pt Vinegar
Peel cucumbers and chop fine through food chopper.  Add salt.  Keep
cucumbers in a colander for 1 hour and add onions, pepper and
vinegar. Stir well.  Pack in glass jars or bottles.  Seal tight and
let stand for 1 month before using.
