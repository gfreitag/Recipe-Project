Title: Sesame Fried Trout
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Water
1 c  Bread crumbs
4    Trout (about 3 lb.)
1    Egg
4 T  Sesame seed
1/4 c  Flour
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
Beat egg with water. Mix sesame seeds and bread crumbs. Coat fish with
flour. Dip fish in egg and water mixture and roll in sesame seeds and
bread crumbs. Pan fry in hot oil about ten minutes on each side or
until flesh flakes from bones.
