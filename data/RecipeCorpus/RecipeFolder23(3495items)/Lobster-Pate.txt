Title: Lobster Pate
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1/8 ts Dill weed
1 1/2 c  Lobster meat, finely chopped
Jim Vorheis
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
1/4 c  Dry white wine
1/2 ts Onion salt
Beat cheese and wine until smooth and creamy.  Blend in salts and
dill; add lobster.  cover and refrigerate several hours or overnight
to mellow. Serve with crackers.

Colorado Cache Cookbook (1978) From the collection of Jim Vorheis
