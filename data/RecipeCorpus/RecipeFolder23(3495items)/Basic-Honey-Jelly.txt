Title: Basic Honey Jelly
Categories: Spread
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 c  Liquid fruit pectin
1 c  Honey
1/4 c  Water
Heat honey and water to boiling, stirring constantly.  Add liquid
fruit pectin and bring back to boil. Allow to cool in jelly jars.

Origin: Homemaking Booklet, Mormon Church, 1978. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens
