Title: Cucumber Dill Dip #2
Categories: Dip
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Lemon Juice
2 ts Snipped Fresh Dill Or
1/2 ts Dried Dill Weed
1/2 ts Tabasco Sauce
8 oz Cream Cheese, Softened
1 c  Miracle Whip
2 md Cucumbers *
2 tb Sliced Green Onion
* Cucumbers should be peeled, seeded and chopped.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Beat cream cheese until smooth.  Stir in remaining ingredients
until well blended.  Cover and chill until serving time.  Makes 2-1/2
cups.  From: Los Angeles Times.
