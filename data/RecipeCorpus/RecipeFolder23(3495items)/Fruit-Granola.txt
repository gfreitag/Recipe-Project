Title: Fruit Granola
Categories: Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Honey
1/2 c  Raisin
1/2 c  Chopped dried apricot
1/2 c  Chopped dried peach
1/2 c  Chopped dried apple
4 c  Rolled oats
1 3/4 c  Rolled wheat flakes
1 c  Sunflower seed
1 c  Chopped nuts
1/3 c  Toasted sesame seed
1/3 c  Sunflower oil
Preheat oven to 400 deg. Blend oats, wheat flakes, sunflower seed,
nuts (almonds, walnuts, pecans or combination), sesame seed, oil and
honey in large bowl. Transfer to large roasting pan, spreading
evenly. Bake until lightly browned at edges, stirring occasionally,
about 15 minutes. Let cool completely. Stir in fruit. Store in
airtight container.
