Title: Pungent Salt Substitute
Categories: Misc/food
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Sage
1 ts Thyme
2 ts Marjoram
3 ts Basil
2 ts Summer savory
2 ts Celery seed
2 ts Ground cumin
Put ingredients into a blender, food processor or clean coffee
grinder. (Or crush with a mortar and pestle.)  Blend well.  Store in
a glass container in a cool, dry place.  To prevent caking, add a few
grains of rice.
