Title: Garlic Shrimp - Goong Gratiem
Categories: Thai, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Fish Sauce (Nam Pla)
Fresh Cilantro Leaves
3 tb Vegetable Oil
1/2 lb Shrimp, Peeled And Deveined
2 tb Cilantro Pesto (See Recipe)
Heat a wok or medium skillet over medium-high heat.  Add the oil and
swirl to coat the surface.  When the oil is very hot but not smoking,
add the shrimp and stir-fry until they begin to color on all sides,
about 1 minute. Add the pesto and stir-fry until it coats the shrimp
and begins to cook, about 1 minute.  Add the fish sauce and toss the
shrimp for another 15 seconds to mix it in.  Transfer the shrimp and
sauce to a serving platter. Sprinkle with cilantro leaves and serve.
