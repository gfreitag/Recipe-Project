Title: North Beach Garlicky Meat Loaf
Categories: Beef, Meatloaf, Meat, Italian
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Fresh ground black pepper
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Water
Catsup, for coating
1 1/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
5 tb Dry cheese, grated (see Note
-below)
5 tb Parsley, minced
2 tb Garlic, finely minced
1 1/2 lb Ground beef, lean
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Mix bread crumbs, cheese,
parsley, and garlic.  Add meat, and salt and pepper, andmix together.
Pour in eggs and a little of the water; knead mixture to an even
consistency, adding more water if mixture seems dry.
2.  Mold into a greased loaf pan, top with a very thin coat of
catsup, and bake for 70 minutes.

Note:  Paramesan cheese or a mixture of Parmesan, Romano, and dried
Jack cheeses may be used.

Comments:

This richly seasoned meat mixture captures the flavors of San
Francisco's North Beach area, home to many wonderful Italian
Restaurants and cafes. Leftover meat loaf makes delicious sandwiches
on a flavorful, homestyle bread.  Do not slice the loaf until needed;
it slices best when cold.
