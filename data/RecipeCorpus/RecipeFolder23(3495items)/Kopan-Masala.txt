Title: Kopan Masala
Categories: Asian, Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
25    Cloves
2    Cinnamon sticks,broken up
1 ts Black peppercorns
1/4 ts Freshly ground nutmeg
1/3 c  Coriander seeds
1/4 c  Cumin seeds
10    Black cardamom pods,peeled
15    Green cardamom pods,peeled
Combine coriander, cumin, black and green cardamom, cloves, cinnamon,
peppercorns and nutmeg and grind finely, but not to powder, with
mortar and pestle, rolling pin, coffee grinder or food processor.
Store in airtight jar. Makes about 1/2 cup.
