Title: Hot 'n Spicy Artichoke Shrimp Dip
Categories: Dip, Seafood, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan Cheese
Finely Julienned Red Pepper
- Strips
Thinly Sliced Green Onion
- Tops
14 oz Artichoke Hearts
4 1/4 oz Shrimp, Rinsed And Drained
3 oz Cream Cheese, Softened
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Pace Picante Sauce
Drain artichoke hearts; dice.  Add shrimp, cream cheese, mayonnaise,
Pace Picante Sauce and Parmesan cheese; mix well.  Spoon into 9-inch
round pie plate or shallow baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees F for
about 20 minutes or until heated through.  Garnish with red pepper
and green onion.  Serve with chips and assorted vegetable dippers.
Makes about 2-1/2 cups dip.

Microwave Oven Directions:  Cook in microwave oven at high for about 3
minutes or until hot, stirring after each minute of cooking.

From: Pace booklet.
