Title: Orange Jelly
Categories: Spread
Yield: 1 servings
-peeling & all
Sugar
3/4 c  Lemon juice
2 1/4 c  Water
6    Whole oranges, sliced,
Servings: 1

Combine lemon juice and water.  Pour the lemon juice mixture over the
orange slices.  Let stand overnight.  Boil until soft.  Press through
jelly bag and strain.  To each 2 c of juice add 1 3/4 c sugar.  Boil
rapidly to jelly stage (220 degrees).  To test, dip a spoon in the
boiling jelly.  It should drop from the side of the spoon in two
drops which run together and slide of in a sheet from the side of the
spoon.  Remove from the heat at once.  Skim off any foam and pour
into sterilized half-pint jars.  Fill to within 1/2 inch of top. Put
on cap & screw band firmly tight.  Process in boiling water bath 5
minutes.
