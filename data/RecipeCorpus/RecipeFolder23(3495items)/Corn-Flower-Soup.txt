Title: Corn Flower Soup
Categories: Chinese, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1    Egg, Beaten
1 g  Salt And Pepper To Taste
1    Pork Chop, Chopped Fine
1 sm Can White Cream Corn
1 c  Water
Add a small amount of oil into saucepan and brown the chopped pork.
Add the water and simmer 10-15 minutes.  Add corn, salt and pepper
and simmer 10 minutes longer.  Remove from heat and gently stir in
the beaten egg. Serve piping hot.
