Title: Mondongo Elegante - Elegant Tripe
Categories: Pork, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
1    Sprig Parsley
2    Cloves Garlic
8    Peppercorns
Salt
1 lb Tripe
2    Pigs' Feet, Thoroughly Wash
1 qt Water
1 lg Onion
1    Bay Leaf
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1 c  White Table Wine
1 tb Capers Or To Taste
3 oz Pimento Stuffed Olives
2 tb Olive Oil
8 oz Tomato Sauce
1/4 lb Chopped Uncooked Ham
Place tripe, pigs' feet, water, onion, bay leaf, parsley, garlic,
peppercorns, and salt in kettle.  Cover and simmer until tender.
Cool. Remove meat from broth, strain and reserve 2 cups of broth.
Remove bones from pigs' feet.  Coarsely chop pigs' feet and tripe.
Set aside.  Wilt onion in oil, add tomato sauce, cook for 5 minutes.
Add broth, ham, tripe, pigs' feet, and wine.  Adjust seasoning,
simmer a few minutes longer, then add capers and olives.
