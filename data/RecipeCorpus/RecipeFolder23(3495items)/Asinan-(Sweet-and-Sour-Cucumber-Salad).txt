Title: Asinan (Sweet and Sour Cucumber Salad)
Categories: Asian, Salad
Yield: 4 servings
1    Thai Chile
3 lg Cucumbers
1 md Onion
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
1/4 c  Vinegar, White
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 ts Salt
Peel and thinly slice the cucumbers. Slice the onion thinly. Seed and
thinly slice the chile.  Put the cucumber slices in a shallow bowl,
arrange the onion slices on top and sprinkle with the chile slices.
Combine all the ingredients with the dressing, mixing well. Taste and
adjust seasoning with salt. Pour the dressing over the onions and
cucumbers and refrigerate a few hours or overnight if possible to
allow flavors to blend.
