Title: Marinated Black-Eyed Peas
Categories: Tex/mexican, Beans
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Celery; Chopped
1/2 c  Red Onion; Chopped
1/2 c  Green Bell Pepper; Chopped
1 ea Jalapeno Pepper; Chopped
30 oz Black-eyed Peas; Drained,2Cn
1/2 c  Olive Oil
1/4 c  Red Wine Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ea Clove Garlic, Crushed
Mix the oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, and garlic then pour the mixture
over the remaining ingredients in a large bowl.  Cover and
refrigerate at least 2 hours but no longer than 24 hours, stirring
occasionally.
