Title: Caviar Mousse
Categories: Appetizer, Dip
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 pn Onion Powder
Pepper To Taste
3 1/2 oz Caviar
7    Hard-Boiled Eggs
2 tb Lemon Juice
2 tb Water
1 sm Package Unflavored Gelatin
6    Anchovy Fillets
Combine all ingredients and refrigerate until ready to serve.
