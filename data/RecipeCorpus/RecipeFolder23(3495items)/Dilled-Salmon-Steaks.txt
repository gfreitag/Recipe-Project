Title: Dilled Salmon Steaks
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Lemon juice
1/2 t  Dried dill
4    Salmon steaks or fillets (1
1/2 lb )  1/4 cup of margarine,
-melted
Mix margarine, lemon juice and dill in dish. Dip salmon in mixture
and turn so all sides are covered. Broil about 5 inches from heat for
five minutes on each or until meat flakes easily. Can also be baked
for about 10 minutes at 450 degrees.
