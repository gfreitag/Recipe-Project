Title: Guacamole Dip #2
Categories: Appetizer, Dip
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Chili Powder
1/3 c  Miracle Whip
2 lg Ripe Avocados
1 tb Grated Onion
1 tb Lemon Juice
Mix all ingredients together except the Miracle Whip.  Spread the
Miracle Whip over the top, sealing edges.  Chill for 2 hours.  Mix
the Miracle Whip into the dip.  Serve with potato chips, crackers or
corn chips. From: Syd's Cookbook.
