Title: Corncob Jelly
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 6 servings
4 c  Sugar
Yellow fruit coloring
12 lg Corncobs
4 c  Water
1    Box Powdered fruit pectin
Cut corn kernels from cobs and reserve for another recipe. In a large
kettle place corncobs and water; bring to a boil. Boil for 10 minutes.
Remove and discard the cobs; strain liquid through cheesecloth. Liquid
should measure 3 cups. Add additional water if necessary. Return to
the kettle and stir in pectin. Bring to a full rolling boil. Add
sugar and bring back to a boil. Skim foam and add a few drops food
coloring. Pour into hot jars. Cool and refrigerate until ready to
use. Yield: About 2 pints.

SOURCE:*Marge Hagy, Brewster, WA, Country Woman, July/Aug. 1993
POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 9/93
