Title: Couche-Couche
Categories: Snack
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Cornmeal (preferably yellow)    1 1/2 c  Milk or water
1/4 c  Lard, melted and heated hot
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
{ Submitted by Eunice F. V. Dedebant }

Use a heavy iron pot or skillet.  Mix the cornmeal, salt, baking
powder and milk thoroughly and put into the heated shortening.  Let a
crust form. Give a good stir and lower the heat to simmer.  Cover and
cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Serves 6.
