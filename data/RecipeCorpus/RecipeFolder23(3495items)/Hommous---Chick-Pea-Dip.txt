Title: Hommous - Chick Pea Dip
Categories: Dip
Yield: 1 servings
2    Cloves garlic
1    Parsley sprigs
2 c  Cooked or canned chick-peas;
2/3 c  Tahini (sesame paste)
3/4 c  Lemon juice (to taste)
Place all ingredients but parsley in food processor or blender. Blend
to a smooth paste. Place in a small platter or shallow bowl. If
preferred, sprinkle olive oil on top and garnish with parsley sprigs
and lemon wedges. Shared by Rita Taule, Prodigy ID# BTVC62A.
