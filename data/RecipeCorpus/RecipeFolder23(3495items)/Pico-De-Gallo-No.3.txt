Title: Pico De Gallo No.3
Categories: Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts White vinegar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Cilantro leaves, finely
-chopped
2 lg Tomatoes, diced
2 lg Onions, chopped finely
3 md Tomatillos, chopped
2 ea Serrano peppers finely
-chopped
1 ea Tb oil
Mix all ingredients together. Can be served right away ro chilled for
several hours. It is best the same day because of the fresh tomatoes,
but keeps well for several days.  Yields 2 cups.  Serve as a relish
or better yet, as a condiment on fajitas.
