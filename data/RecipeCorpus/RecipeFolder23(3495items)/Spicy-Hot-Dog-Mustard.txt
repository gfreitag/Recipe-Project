Title: Spicy Hot Dog Mustard
Categories: Spread
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Dijon Mustard
1/4 c  Prepared Mustard
Stir together 1/4 cup Dijon mustard, 1/4 cup French's prepared
mustard, and 1/4 cup honey. Refrigerate.

From Gloria Pitzer.(this is supposed to be like one at Howard
Johnson's)
