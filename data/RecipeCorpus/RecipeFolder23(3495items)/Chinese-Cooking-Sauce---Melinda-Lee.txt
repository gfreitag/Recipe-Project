Title: Chinese Cooking Sauce - Melinda Lee
Categories: Chinese, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Soy sauce
3 tb Chicken broth
2 ts Cornstarch
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Distilled vinegar
1 tb Dry sherry
Combine all ingredients and mix well.  When your stir-fry is just
about done, move the stir-fry to the outside of the wok.  Add the
cooking sauce to the center and mix with juices.  When mixed toss
with stir-fry to coat.
