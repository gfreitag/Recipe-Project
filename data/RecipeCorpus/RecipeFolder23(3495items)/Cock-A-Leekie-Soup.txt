Title: Cock-A-Leekie Soup
Categories: British, Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 12 servings
Pepper, to taste
2 qt Chicken broth
Milk
1/2 c  Margarine
3 bn Leeks
3 lb Potatoes, fresh mashed, or
-reconstituted instant
4    Green onions
1    Head celery
Slice vegetables and cook in a large pan with the margarine until
soft. Add the chicken stock and allow to simmer for 1 1/2 hours.  Add
the potatoes to the mixture (you will have a thick paste).  At this
point the mixture may be frozen in the freezer in quart size or
smaller containers. When ready to use, thaw a portion, add milk to
desired thickeness and pepper to taste.  Heat and serve.
