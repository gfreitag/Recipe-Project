Title: Basbousa Bil Loz (Basbousa with Almonds)
Categories: Dessert, Middle east
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Semolina
Whole blanched almonds
Whipped cream
1/2 c  Butter
2 1/4 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Lemon juice
3/4 c  Blanched almonds
Bring water, sugar and lemon juice to a boil. Simmer for few min. Chop
almonds fine and fry them in hot butter together w/ semolina until
golden. Add hot syrup slowly stirring constantly over low heat until
thickened. Remove from heat & cover. Let it cool. Pour into indiv.
greased molds. Unmold & serve warm. Decorate w/ almond and dollop of
cream.
