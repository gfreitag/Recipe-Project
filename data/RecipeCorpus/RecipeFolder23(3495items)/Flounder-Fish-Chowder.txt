Title: Flounder Fish Chowder
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Water or 1 1/2 cans of milk
2 lb Fish fillets cut up
-salt and pepper to taste
1 cn Cream of potato soup
1 sm Grated onion
2 T  Butter or margarine
Boil fish for about ten minutes. Combine all ingredients in a pot and
bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer occasionally for about ten
minutes.
