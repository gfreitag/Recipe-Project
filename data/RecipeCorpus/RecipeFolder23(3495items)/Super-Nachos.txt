Title: Super Nachos
Categories: Tex/mexican, Snack, Kids
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Sliced jalapeno peppers
3/4 c  Milk
2 pk Queso Quesadilla Cacique
-(Cheese), sliced
2    Dozen tortillas, taco size
From: Cacique, 14940 Proctor Ave., Industry, CA 91744

chili powder to taste

Cut tortillas into triangles and cook in hot oil until golden brown.
In saucepan, combine Queso Quesadilla with milk and cook until
melted. Add chili powder. Mix and pour over nachos.

Decorate with slices of jalapeno peppers. That's it!
