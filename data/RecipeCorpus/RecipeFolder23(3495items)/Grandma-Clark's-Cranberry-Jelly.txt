Title: Grandma Clark's Cranberry Jelly
Categories: Spread
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM---------------------------
2 c  Sugar
4 c  Cranberries, fresh or frozen
1 c  Water
Cook berries in water til mushy and put through seive.  Add sugar and
boil 15 minutes til it jells.  Bottle.

Super! HP H. Peagram
