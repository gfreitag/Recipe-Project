Title: Peach Jam
Categories: Condiment, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
7 1/2 c  Sugar
4 c  Prepared fruit
1    Bottle fruit pectin
Peel peaches.  Pit, and grind or crush.  If peaches lack flavor, add
juice of 1 lemon.  Combine sugar and fruit.  Heat rapidly to full
rolling boil. Stir constantly before and while boiling. Boil hard 1
minute. Remove from fire.  Stir in fruit pectin. Skim and stir
alternately for 5 minutes to cool jam slightly and to prevent
floating fruit. The Household Searchlight
