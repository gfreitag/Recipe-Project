Title: Apple Lokshen (Noodle) Kugel (Sweet)
Categories: Dessert, German
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Vanilla
8 oz Medium Egg Noodles
1/4 c  Melted Margarine or oil
4    Eggs, beaten
3 md Apples
1/4 c  Dark Seedless Raisins
Cook and drain noodles and put in a large bowl. Stir in margarine and
eggs. Peel and chop apples. If using raisins, rinse in hot water and
drain. Combine all ingredients together, add seasoning and mix well.
Preheat oven to 350 deg F. Pour kugel mixture into a greased 9" x 13"
baking pan. Bake for 40-60 minutes or until lightly browned. Makes 12
servings. Note: for extra softness, add another 2 eggs.

Variation:  Pineapple Kugel Replace apples and raisins with one 16 oz
can crushed pineapple..Add an additional 1/2-3/4 cup of sugar and 1
tbs cinnamon.
