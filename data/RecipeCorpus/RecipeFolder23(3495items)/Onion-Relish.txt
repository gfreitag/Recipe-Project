Title: Onion Relish
Categories: Relish
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Onions
2 tb Unsalted butter
2 ts Sugar
Cut onions into thin wedges. Heat butter in a large skillet over
medium heat. Add onion, saute until browned, 10 to 15 minutes.
Sprinkle with sugar. Stir in vinegar, salt, and pepper. Serve with
cube steaks.
