Title: Ginger Sherry Pork Chops **
Categories: Pork, Bbq/grill, Alcohol, Marinade
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
1 ts Honey
1    Garlic clove; finely chopped
4    Butterfly pork chops;
- 1" thick
1/3 c  Dry sherry
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Gingerroot; finely chopped
Mix all ingredients except pork in shallow glass dish. Place pork in
dish; turn to coat with marinade. Cover and refrigerate at least 1
hour. Remove pork from marinade; reserve marinade. Cover and grill
4-5" from medium coals, 14-16 minutes, brushing occasionally with
marinade and turning once, until no longer pink in center.
