Title: Egg Plant Fritters
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Flour
1 lg Egg plant
2 ts Butter
1    Egg, beaten
Boil egg plant, scoop out the meat and mix with butter, egg, salt,
pepper and enough flour to drop from a spoon.  Heat shortening (375
degrees). Drop batter from spoon and fry.  Drain on paper towel.
Cooking time about 6 minutes.
