Title: Salata Lahano-Karota-Domates
Categories: Salad, Greek
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Vinegar or lemon juice
Salt to taste
Calamata or black olives
Parsley
1 ea Cabbage
6 ea Tomatoes
5 ea Large, tender carrots
1/2 c  Oil
Shred cabbage.  Place in strainer and wash under running water for
several minutes; drain well.  Wash tomatoes and cut into wedges.
Wash and scrape carrots. Grate. Place carrots in center of a platter;
surround with ring of cabbage and then with ring of tomatoes, or put
tomatoes in the center and lay the carrots around them.  Sprinkle
with salt.  Before serving, add a dressing of oil and vinegar or oil
and lemon.

Garnish Calamata olives and parsley.
