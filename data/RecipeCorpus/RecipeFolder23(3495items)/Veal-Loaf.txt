Title: Veal Loaf
Categories: Meat, Mennonite
Yield: 1 servings
1    Egg
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/4 c  Hot milk
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 lb Ground veal shoulder
1/2 lb Sausage or fresh pork
2 tb Catsup
2 ts Minced parsley
1    Onion, sliced
Mix all the ingredients together and shape into a loaf.
Place in a loaf pan and bake at 35o degrees for 1 1/2 hours.
Brush occasionally with bacon drippings.
