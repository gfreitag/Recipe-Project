Title: Simple Fish Chowder
Categories: Fish, Potato, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
-grouper
Ingredients: 1 can cream of potato soup 1 can of water or 1 1/2 cans
of milk 1 small grated onion 2 lbs fish fillets cut up 2 tbs of
butter or margarine   salt and pepper to taste

Boil fish for about ten minutes. Combine all ingredients in a pot and
bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer occasionally for about ten
minutes.
