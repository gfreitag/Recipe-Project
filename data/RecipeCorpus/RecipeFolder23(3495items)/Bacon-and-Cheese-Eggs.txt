Title: Bacon and Cheese Eggs
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Vinegar
1/4 c  Grated cheddar
1/8 ts Salt
6    Hard cooked eggs
3 tb Mayonnaise
2 sl Crisp bacon
Mash egg yolks and stir with bacon, cheese, mayonnaise, vinegar and
salt. Fill egg white halves.  Top with crumbled bacon.
