Title: Gelatin Pizza
Categories: Kids, Pizza
Yield: 10 servings
Non-stick cooking spray
1 c  Cool-whip
2 c  Cut-up fruit
4 pk (4 serving size) or
2 pk ( 8 serving size) jello
-powder, any flavor
2 1/2 c  Boiling water
Servings: 10 to 12

Pour gelatin into bowl. Add 2 1/2 cups boiling water to gelatin. Stir,
until gelatin is completely dissolved, about 2 minutes. Spray pizza
pan with non-stick cooking spray. Pour gelatin mixture into pizza
pan. Put pan into refrigerator to chill until firm, about 3 hours.

Remove pan from refrigerator when ready to serve. Put about 1 inch of
warm water in sink. Carefully dip just bottom of pan into warm water
for 15 seconds. Spread whipped topping over gelatin just before
serving, leaving about 1 inch of space around outside edge of gelatin
for pizza "crust".

Top pizza with fruit, arranging fruit in whatever design you like. Cut
pizza into wedges. Posted by Linda Davis
