Title: Rainbow Trout with Herb Stuffing
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sliced onions
1/4 c  Parsley, chopped
3 T  Pimento, chopped
3 t  Lemon juice
1/2 t  Marjoram
6    Dressed trout
1 t  Salt
1 c  Fresh bread cubes
2/3 c  Butter or oleo
1 sm Can mushrooms
Sprinkled salt over inside and outside of trout. Saute bread in 1/4
cup butter until brown. Stir often. Ad onions and mushrooms, Cook
until mushrooms are tender. Stir in parsley, lemon juice, marjoram
and dash of salt. Mix and stuff fish with mixture. Place in greased
baking dish and bake ate 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
