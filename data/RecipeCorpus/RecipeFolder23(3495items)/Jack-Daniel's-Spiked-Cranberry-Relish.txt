Title: Jack Daniel's Spiked Cranberry Relish
Categories: Relish
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Jack Daniel's Whiskey
4 c  (1 pound) fresh cranberries
1    Orange, seeds removed and
-quartered
Using food processor, chop cranberries and orange. Add sugar; chop
again to combine. Place in covered container. Chill for 24 hours. Add
Jack Daniel's Whiskey just before serving. Makes 1 quart.
