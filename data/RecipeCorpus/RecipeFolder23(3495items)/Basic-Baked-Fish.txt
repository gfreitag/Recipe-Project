Title: Basic Baked Fish
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Melted margarine
1/4 t  Lemon-pepper season
1/2 lb Dressed fish fillets
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Paprika
Placed dry fish in foiled lined baking pan. Mix rest of ingredients
and pour over fish. Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes or until meat
flakes easily.
