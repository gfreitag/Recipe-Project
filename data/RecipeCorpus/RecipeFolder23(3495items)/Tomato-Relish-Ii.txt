Title: Tomato Relish Ii
Categories: Relish, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Grated rind 1 lemon
4 c  Crushed and peeled tomatoes
6 1/2 c  Sugar
1    Bottle fruit pectin
Boil tomatoes 10 minutes in an uncovered kettle.  Stir occasionally.
Add lemon juice and grated lemon rind.  Heat rapidly to full rolling
boil. Stir constantly before and while boiling. Boil hard 2 minutes.
Remove from fire. Stir in fruit pectin. Skim. This recipe requires
about 2 1/2 pounds tomatoes. The Household Searchlight
