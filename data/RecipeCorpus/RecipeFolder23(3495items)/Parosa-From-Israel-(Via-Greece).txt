Title: Parosa From Israel (Via Greece)
Categories: Casserole, Jewish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ds Salt
1/4 c  Plain yogurt
2 tb Butter or marg., melted
2 tb All-purpose flour
1 lb Creamed cottage cheese
2    Eggs (separated)
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Blend thoroughly the cheese, egg yolks, sugar, cinnamon and salt.
Add the yogurt to make a thick paste.  Add the melted butter or
margarine.  Stir in the flour.  Beat the egg whites stiff and fold
into the mixture.  Pour into a greased quart casserole.  Set the
casserole into a shallow pan of water and bake uncovered in a 350F
Oven for 1 hour.  chill. May be served with more yogurt.  Serves 4 - 6
