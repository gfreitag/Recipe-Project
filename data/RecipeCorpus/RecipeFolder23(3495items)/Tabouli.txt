Title: Tabouli
Categories: Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
COMBINE:  1 c. bulgur wheat (available at healthfood stores)

Let stand 20 minutes.

ADD:   2 cloves of garlic, peeled & smashed, still in one piece 1/4 c.
lemon or lime juice 1/2 t. dried peppermint leaf or 1 T. fresh
chopped mint 1/4 c. olive oil Mix lightly with a fork

REFRIGERATE AT LEAST 3 HOURS or OVERNIGHT

IMMEDIATELY BEFORE SERVING:

Remove the cloves of garlic and mix in: 1 c. chopped fresh parsley
1/2 c. chopped scallions 2 medium tomatoes (chopped) Black pepper to
taste 1 cucumber (peeled and chopped)

Adapted by Mike Hackmann from Andrew Bishop (Moosewood Cookbook)
