Title: Olive Cheese Balls
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Margarine, melted
36    Pimento stuffed olives
1 pk Cheddar cheese (8 oz.)
1 1/4 c  Flour
Mix cheese and flour.  Add margarine and mix thoroughly.  If dough
seems dry, work with your hands.  Mold 1 teaspoon of dough around
each olive, shape into ball.  Place 2 inches apart on greased baking
sheet.  Cover, chill for 1 hour.  Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Bake 15
to 20 minutes.  Can make ahead and refrigerate or freeze.
