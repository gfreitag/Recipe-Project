Title: Steak Port Antonio **
Categories: Beef, Alcohol, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
2 ts Lime juice
1/2 ts Tabasco pepper sauce
4    Beef steaks
1/4 c  Rum
1 tb Shallots; chopped
4 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Parsley; chopped
In saucepan, combine rum and shallots; bring to a boil. Reduce heat;
simmer 2 minutes. Stir in butter, parsley, lime juice and tabasco
sauce. Brush steaks with rum butter. Grill or broil 6" from heat.
Cook about 8 minutes, turning steak once. Brush with rum butter
occasionally. Heat any remaining rum butter to boil and serve with
steaks. Serve with additional tabasco sauce if desired.
