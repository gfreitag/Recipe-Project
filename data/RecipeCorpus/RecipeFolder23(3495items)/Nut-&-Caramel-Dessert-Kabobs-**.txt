Title: Nut & Caramel Dessert Kabobs **
Categories: Dessert, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
2 sm Nectarines; cut in fourths
-=OR=- peaches
1 md Banana; diagonal cut 1" thik
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Caramel ice cream topping
2 sl Frozen pound cake; thawed
- 1" thick each
1/4 c  Creamy peanut butter -=OR=-
- cream cheese; softened
1/4 c  Peanuts; chopped
Heat grill. Cut each cake slice into 6 cubes. Spread peanut butter (or
cream cheese) lightly on 2 opposite sides of cake cubes. Dip coated
sides in peanuts. In medium bowl, toss nectarine pieces and banana
chunks with lemon juice. Thread 3 cake pieces, 2 nectarine pieces and
2 banana chunks alternately on each of 4 (12-14" long) skewers. Heat
caramel topping in small saucepan until warm. When ready to grill,
brush kabobs with caramel topping. Place kabobs on gas grill over
medium heat or charcoal grill 4-6" from medium coals. Cook 4-7
minutes or until well heated, turning and brushing with caramel
topping. Serve warm.

BROILER METHOD: Prepare recipe as above. Place kabobs on broiler pan;
broil 4-6" from heat, for 4-6 minutes or until well heated, turning
and brushing with caramel topping. Serve warm.
