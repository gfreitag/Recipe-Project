Title: Freezer Log
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Dry mustard
2 ts Mayonnaise
1/2 lb Sharp Cheddar cheese
8 sl Cooked bacon
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
Combine in food processor - 1/2 lb. sharp yellow cheese, 8 slices
bacon, 1/2 tsp. Worchestershire sauce, 1 tsp. dry mustard, 2 tsp.
mayonnaise. Blend until bacon is minced. Form into a log shaped like
party rye bread. Wrap in plastic wrap or foil and freeze til firm. To
