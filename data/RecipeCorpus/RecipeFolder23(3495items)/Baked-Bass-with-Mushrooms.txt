Title: Baked Bass with Mushrooms
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Half and half
1 c  Slice mushrooms
1/2 c  Dry white wine
8    Striped or black bass
-fillets
1 c  Parsley
1    Onion, sliced
1/4 c  Margarine
1    To 2 tsp flour
-
Saute onions in margarine until soft. Add parsley and mushrooms
slices and saute about 2 to 3 minutes. Place 4 fillets in greased
baking pan. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Thicken half and half with
a little flour and add to saute mix. Spread the sauteed mixture on
the fish and top with remaining fillets. Pour wine over fish. Bake
for 15 minutes or until done at 350 degrees. - Earl
