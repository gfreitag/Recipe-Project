Title: Spinach Parmesan
Categories: Italian, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Butter
3 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Salt
1 lb Spinach, washed and chopped
1/2 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Nutmeg
Place spinach in pan with butter and cook 8 minutes, or until tender.
Add salt, pepper, and nutmeg and Parmesan cheese and mix well.  Serve.

Serves 4

From: The Talisman Italian Cook Book Shared By: Pat Stockett
