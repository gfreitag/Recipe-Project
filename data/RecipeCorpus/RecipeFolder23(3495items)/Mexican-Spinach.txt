Title: Mexican Spinach
Categories: Tex/mexican, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Prepared horseradish
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ea Egg, hard-cooked, sliced
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 pk Spinach, chopped, frozen
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Light cream
Melt butter or margarine in a saucepan; add spinach and salt. Cover.
Place on a high heat until steaming; turn to a lower heat and cook
until just tender. Drain. Add light cream, horseradish and pepper;
heat. Garnish with sliced hard cooked egg. Serves 6.

From: Florence P. Hanford's Television Kitchen Meals, 1964.

Shared by: June Hoffman, 9/93
