Title: Spicy Nachos Supreme
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1    Avocado
1 ts Lemon Juice
1/2 c  Sour Cream
Jalapeno Slices, Optional
8 oz Tomato Sauce
4 oz Diced Green Chiles
1/2 c  Chopped Green Bell Pepper
1    Green Onion, Sliced
1/4 ts Hot Pepper Sauce
10 oz Tortilla Chips
Combine tomato sauce, chiles, green pepper, green onion and hot pepper
sauce in a bowl; let stand for 15 minutes.  Place tortilla chips in a
shallow 8" X 10" baking dish.  Pour sauce over chips; sprinkle grated
cheese over all.  Broil nachos for 3 minutes or until cheese melts.
Just before serving, seed, peel and mash avocado.  Stir in lemon
juice.  Spoon avocado mixture and sour cream on hot nachos and top
with jalapeno slices. Serve immediately.
