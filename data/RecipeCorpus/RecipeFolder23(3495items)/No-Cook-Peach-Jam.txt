Title: No-Cook Peach Jam
Categories: Spread, Condiment
Yield: 3 servings
3 oz Liquid Fruit Pectin; 1 Pouch
2 tb Lemon Juice
1 lb Peaches: Peeled, Pitted And
-Mashed, 2 Cups
2 c  Sugar
Stir the mashed peaches and sugar together in a large bowl, blending
well, and let stand 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add the
liquid fruit pectin and lemon juice.  Stir, constantly, for 3
minutes.  Spoon into jars prepared for freezer jams.

YIELD:

3  Eight Ounce Jars

Each Tablespoon Contains:

Calories:  36  Protein: 0 Grams  Cholesterol: 0 Milligrams  Fat:  0
Grams Carbohydrates: 9 Grams  Sodium: 1 Milligram

NOTE:

Again this is one jam that cannot be sealed with the cookbook
directions and must be frozen.
