Title: Vegetable Pork Stir-Fry
Categories: Vegetable, Pork, Asian
Yield: 6 servings
-sliced
1 c  Carrots, thinly sliced
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 c  Chicken broth
2 tb Soy sauce
1 1/2 tb Cornstarch
3 c  Hot cooked rice
3/4 lb Pork tenderloin, cut in
-strips
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 1/2 c  Mushrooms, fresh, sliced
1 lg Green bell pepper, cut in
-strips
1    Zucchini, thinly sliced
2    Celery stalks, diagonally
Brown pork strips in oil in wok or large skillet over high heat.  Push
meat to side of wok or skillet; add vegetables.  Stir-fry vegetables
approximately 3 minutes.  Combine broth, soy sauce, and cornstarch;
add to wok or skillet and cook until clear and thickened.  Serve over
hot rice.
