Title: Veal Delicious
Categories: Meat, Mennonite
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 c  Sour cream
2 lb Veal steak 3/4 inch thick
3 ts Fat
1    Onion
1/2 c  Flour
Cut veal into serving pieces.
Rub with salt and pepper and roll in flour
Brown on both sides in hot fat.
Place in a roasting pan or casserole and cover with sour cream.
Slice onion in very thin slices and lay on top.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for approximately 2 hours.
