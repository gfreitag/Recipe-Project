Title: Savory Oven Brisket
Categories: Main dish, Jewish, Beef
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/2 c  Honey
1/3 c  Worchestershire sauce
1 c  Garlic; minced
-GRAMMIE'S KITCHEN
2 tb Soy sauce
1/2 c  Coffee; black
3 lb Brisket
1/2 c  Catsup
Sear meat on all sides and place in a dutch oven with the above
combined ingredients.  Simmer on stove top until done- 2 1/2 - 3
hours or in a 325 degree oven for the same time. To serve, slice on
the diagonal. Recipe from PURITY SUPERMARKET FLYER. SEPTEMBER 1991

Prepared this for the first time. ROSH HASSHONNA 1991.
