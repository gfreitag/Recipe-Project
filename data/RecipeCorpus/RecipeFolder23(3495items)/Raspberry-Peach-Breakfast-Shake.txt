Title: Raspberry Peach Breakfast Shake
Categories: Snack
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 tb Honey
10 oz Frozen Raspberries *
1 c  Canned Peach Nectar
* Frozen raspberries should be thawed and in light syrup.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Place all ingredients in a blender bowl and process until smooth.
Pour into glasses and serve.  109 calories per serving.  From: Syd's
Cookbook.
