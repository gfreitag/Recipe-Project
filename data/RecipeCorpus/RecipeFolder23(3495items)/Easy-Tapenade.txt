Title: Easy Tapenade
Categories: Relish
Yield: 1 servings
8    Anchovies
1 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Chopped parsley
1/2 c  Pitted nicoise olives
-=OR=- Calamata olives
This is a fast, simple version of this classic olive relish.

COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS in a food processor and process until a paste
forms.

Makes 1/2 Cup
