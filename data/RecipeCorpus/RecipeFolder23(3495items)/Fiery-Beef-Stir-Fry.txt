Title: Fiery Beef Stir-Fry
Categories: Chinese, Beef, Stir fry
Yield: 6 servings
4 ts Cornstarch
1 1/2 ts Distilled white vinegar
1/4 ts Ground red pepper (cayenne)
3 tb Oil, divided
3 c  Bite-size cauliflowerets
Salt
1    Onion, chunked and separated
1    Green pepper, chunked
1/2 lb Boneless tender beef steak
(sirloin, rib eye or top
-loin)
1 tb Cornstarch
4 tb Soy sauce, divided
1/2 ts Sugar
1    Clove garlic, minced
1 1/4 c  Water
Cut beef across grain into thin strips.  Combine 1 Tb. each
cornstarch and soy sauce with sugar and garlic in small bowl; stir in
beef.  Let stand 15 minutes.  Meanwhile, combine water, remaining 3
Tb. soy sauce, 4 tsp. cornstarch, vinegar and red pepper; set aside.
Heat 1 Tb. oil in hot wok or large skillet over high heat.  Add beef
and stir-fry 1 minute; remove. Heat remaining 2 Tb. oil in same pan.
Add cauliflowerets; lightly sprinkle with salt and stir-fry 2
minutes.  Add onion and green pepper; stir-fry 4 minutes.  Stir in
beef and soy sauce mixture; cook and stir until sauce boils and
thickens.
