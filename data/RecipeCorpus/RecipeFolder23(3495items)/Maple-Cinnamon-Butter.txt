Title: Maple Cinnamon Butter
Categories: Spread
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Salt
1 lb Unsalted Butter, softened
2 tb Maple Syrup
2    Drops Vanilla
Combine all ingredients, mixing thoroughly. pack into crock or pottery
dish.

Refrigerate. Garnish with cinnamon stick when serving.
