Title: Grilled Fish Fillets
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Pepper
2    Green peppers, sliced
3 T  Margarine or butter
2 lb Fish fillets
2 sm Onions, sliced
2 T  Lemon juice or juice of one
-lemon
Cut fillets into serving portions. Place each piece of fish skin down
on about a 12in x 12in sheet aluminum foil.Put a little dab of
margarine or butter on each piece of fish. Mix remaining ingredients
and pour over fish. Wrap and seal fish. Cook on hot grill about four
inches from coals about 10 minutes for each inch of thickness or
until fish flakes with a fork.
