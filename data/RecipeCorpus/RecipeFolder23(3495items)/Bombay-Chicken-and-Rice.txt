Title: Bombay Chicken and Rice
Categories: Indian, Chicken, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
4 ts Curry powder
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
2 c  Water
2 tb Butter or margarine, melted
1/2 ts Paprika
Chopped parsley, optional
3 lb Broiler-fryer, cut up
Salt
1 c  Uncle Ben's Converted Brand
-rice
1 pk (6 ounces) dried mixed fruit
-pieces OR 1-1/3 cups diced
Dried fruit and raisins
1/2 c  Chopped onion
Posted by Sonya Whitaker-Quandt   9/22/92   National Cooking Echo

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Remove excess fat from chicken; season
with salt. Combine rice, fruit, onion, 2 tsp of the curry powder,
sugar, salt and water in a 12x8x2-inch baking dish; stir well.
Arrange chicken over rice mixture. Combine melted butter, paprika and
remaining 2 tsp curry powder; brush on chicken. Cover tightly with
aluminum foil and bake in 375 degree oven 1 hour, or until chicken is
tender and most of liquid is absorbed. Garnish with chopped parsley,
if desired. Makes 6 servings, 520 calories per serving.
