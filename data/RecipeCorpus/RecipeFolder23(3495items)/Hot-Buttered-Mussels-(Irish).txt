Title: Hot Buttered Mussels (Irish)
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1    Salt and pepper
2 T  Chopped chives
2 pt Mussels
4 oz Butter
Wash mussels thoroughly under running water.  Remove "beards" and
discard any open shells.  Place mussels in pan and cook over a high
temperature for 7 or 8 minutes, until the shells open.  Season with
salt or pepper. Place in a serving dish and pour cooking juices over.
Dot with knobs of butter and sprinkle with chopped chives.  Serve
with fresh brown bread and butter.
