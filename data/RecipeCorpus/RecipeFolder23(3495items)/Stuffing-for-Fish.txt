Title: Stuffing for Fish
Categories: Fish, 1941
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Celery salt
1/8 t  Pepper
2 c  Soft bread crumbs
1    Egg, well beaten
1 T  Minced onion
Combine ingredients.  Add sufficient warm water to make the stuffing
pack easily.  If desired, 1 cup chopped oysters may be added to the
ingredients. The Household Searchlight
