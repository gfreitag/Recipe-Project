Title: Beet Cabbage Relish
Categories: Relish, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Chopped cauliflower
3 tb Mustard seed
1 tb Celery seed
2    To 2 1/4 cups vinegar
10    Beets, chopped
1 c  Chopped onions
2 c  Chopped cabbage
1 c  Red sweet pepper
1    Stalk celery, chopped
2 tb Salt
Combine ingredients.  Heat to boiling.  Cook slowly, stirring
frequently, until vegetables are tender. M.B. Horsky, Simpson, PA.
