Title: Kokt Orrett (Boiled Trout)
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Cider vinegar
1 T  Salt
1/2 c  Chopped parsley
4 sm Whole trout
-water
1/2 c  Butter , softened
-lemon wedges
Wash trout.  Put trout in a shallow pan and add vinegar and enough
water just to cover the fish.  Add salt and place on stove over
medium-high heat. When water just begins to boil, remove from heat
and let stand, covered, for 10 -15 minutes.  Drain and carefully lift
fish to a platter. Cream butter until light and fluffy and blend in
parsley. Serve parsley butter with the fish and garnish platter with
lemon wedges.
