Title: Rhubarb-Strawberry Fruit Leather
Categories: Kids
Yield: 6 servings
-strawberries with
1 1/2 tb Honey, unless you use frozen
Rhubarb-Strawberry Fruit Leather

Combine 1 cup rhubarb puree and 1 cup strawberry puree (Strawberries
may be fresh, frozen or frozen with sugar added.) Add sugar already
added. Spread on plastic wrap and dry as for fruit leather.
