Title: Hollandaise Snack Mix
Categories: Snack
Yield: 6 servings
2    Teaspoons. garlic powder
2 c  Unsalted dry-roasted peanuts
2 c  Ritz Bits crackers
2 c  Stick pretzels
1/2 c  Butter
2 pk (0.9 ounces each)
-hollandaise sauce mix
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.  In a small saucepan, melt butter over
medium heat.  Stir in next 3 ingredients.  In a large bowl, mix
together peanuts, crackers, and pretzels.  Pour butter mixture over
peanut mixture. Toss until well coated.  Spread evenly on a baking
sheet.  Bake 15 to 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Transfer to
paper towels to cool completely.  Store in an airtight container.
Yield:  about 6 cups snack mix
