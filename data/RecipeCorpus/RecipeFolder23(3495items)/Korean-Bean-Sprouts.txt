Title: Korean Bean Sprouts
Categories: Asian, Salad
Yield: 5 servings
1/2 ts Garlic powder
-Karen says: use REAL garlic
1 pn Cayenne pepper, optional
1/4 c  Finely chopped green onions
Karen Mintzias
1/2 lb Bean sprouts
1 ts Salt; or to taste
2 tb Sesame seeds, toasted
Clean the bean sprouts.  Drop into boiling water and boil 5 minutes.
Drain well.  Return to the pan.  Stir in the salt, sesame seeds,
sesame oil, garlic powder, cayenne, and green onions.  Simmer 2
minute.  Serve hot or cold.
