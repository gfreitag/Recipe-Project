Title: Crab Stuffed Flounder
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
3 T  Flour
2 T  White wine
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
-paprika
4 T  Margarine or butter
1 1/2 c  Chicken broth
1/2 lb Crab meat
1 lb Flounder fillets
Melt margarine or butter in small saucepan. Blend in flour. Stir in
chicken broth and cook until thick, stirring constantly. Mix in wine.
Combine 1/2 cup of this sauce with crab meat and bread crumbs. Spread
on fillets. Roll up and place seam side down in greased baking pan.
Dot with margarine and bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes or until
done. Heat remaining sauce and pour over fillet rolls. sprinkle with
paprika (optional).
