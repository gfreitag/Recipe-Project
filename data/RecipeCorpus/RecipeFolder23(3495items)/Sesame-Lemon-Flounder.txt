Title: Sesame-Lemon Flounder
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Flounder fillets
2 T  Sesame seeds
1/8 t  Salt
2/3 c  Onions, sliced
1 T  Cooking oil
1/4 t  Garlic powder
2 T  Lemon juice
Place onion slices in lightly greased baking dish. Toast sesame seeds
lightly in a heavy skillet, stirring constantly. brush fillets with
oil. Coat with sesame seeds. sprinkle fillets with garlic powder and
salt. Place on top of onions.  Sprinkle with lemon juice. Bake at 450
degrees for 10 minutes or until done.
