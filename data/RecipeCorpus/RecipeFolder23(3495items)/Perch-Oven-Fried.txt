Title: Perch Oven Fried
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Milk
2 T  Grate parmesan cheese
1 T  Margarine, melted
1 lb Perch fillets
1/2 c  Dried bread crumbs
1/2 T  Thyme
Dip fillets in milk. Combine crumbs, parmesan cheese, and thyme. Coat
fish with bread crumbs. Lay on single layer on greased pan or baking
dish. Drizzle with melted butter or margarine.  bake at 450 degrees
for 10 minutes per inch of thickness.
