Title: Mexican Salsa Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Tex/mexican, Condiment, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
2    Chopped medium tomatoes
3 tb Olive oil
1 tb Brown vinegar
1 cn Chopped olives
1 cn Chopped Ortega green chilis
4    To 6 chopped green onions
From: Marcy Moreno Posted by: Donna Ransdell

lots of salt

Mix together and serve with tortilla chips. It is usually better if
made just an hour or so before serving.
