Title: Deep-Fried Eel
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/8 t  Pepper
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
2 lb Eel, skinned
1 t  Salt
2    Eggs, lightly beaten
Directions:  Stir together flour, salt and pepper. Coat eel with the
mixture; dip into eggs, then coat with the bread crumbs. Fry each
piece for about 4 minutes or until golden brown.
