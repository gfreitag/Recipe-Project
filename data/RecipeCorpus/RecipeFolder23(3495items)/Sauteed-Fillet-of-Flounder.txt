Title: Sauteed Fillet of Flounder
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Milk
2 T  Butter
2 T  Chopped parsley
4    Fillets of flounder
1/2 c  Flour
-salt and pepper
1    Lemon, cut in wedges
Soak fillets in milk for 15 minutes. Drain and dredge in flour. Put
oil in heavy frying pan and heat until almost smoking. Put fillets in
pan and cook for 4 or 5 minutes. Turn gently. Cook for another two
minutes. Salt and pepper to taste and remove fish to heated platter.
Serve garnished with parsley.
