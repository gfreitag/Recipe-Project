Title: Tomato Catsup
Categories: Condiment, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Black pepper
3 tb Salt
1 lg Onion, chopped
3 tb Cold water
1 1/4 c  Sugar
5 qt Tomato pulp
2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Paprika
1 tb Mixed spices
1 tb Mustard
2 1/2 c  Vinegar
Prepare tomato pulp by washing and chopping tomatoes, cooking until
soft, and rubbing through a sieve.  Tie whole spices and onion in a
thin bag. Mix mustard with cold water.  Combine all ingredients. Cook
slowly, stirring frequently, until thick. M.B. Horsky, Simpson, PA.
