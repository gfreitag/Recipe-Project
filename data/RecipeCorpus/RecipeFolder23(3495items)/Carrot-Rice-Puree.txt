Title: Carrot-Rice Puree
Categories: Rice
Yield: 1 servings
-chopped in small pieces
1 1/3 c  Water
2 tb Brown rice, uncooked
6    Carrots, scrubbed and
From "Baby's First Helpings" by Chris Casson Madden ISBN:
0-385-19143-X

A nutritious, smooth dish with a bit of texture for older infants.

(or broth or leftover ooking liquid from cooking vegetables) 1
teaspoon sweet butter (optional)

Place rice and carrots in a saucepan with the water and cover.  Simmer
until the water is absorbed--about 30 to 40 minutes.  When cool
enough to handle, puree in blender or food processor with butter
until smooth Refrigerate, or freeze leftovers in ice cube tray.

Makes 1-1/ cups
