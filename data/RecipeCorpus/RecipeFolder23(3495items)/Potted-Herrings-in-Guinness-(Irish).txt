Title: Potted Herrings in Guinness (Irish)
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
-Guinness (pref. draft)
1    Onion cut into rings
10    Cloves
-Salt as required
10    Filleted fresh herrings
5 fl Vinegar
2    Bay leaves
4    White and black peppercorns
1 t  Brown sugar
Wash herring fillets and roll up, starting from the tail.  Place in a
77v77 baking dish and add the stout-and-vinegar mixture.  Add all
other ingredients.  Cover with baking parchment or foil and bake in a
moderate oven (about 350 degrees) for 20 minutes.  Remove from oven,
allow to cool, and serve.
