Title: Thai-Style Beef Salad
Categories: Beef, Salad, Thai, Asian
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Hot red pepper flakes
4 c  Chinese cabbage, or Iceberg
-lettuce, shredded
1 1/2 c  Carrots, coarsley grated
1 c  Green onions, thinly sliced
1 c  Spaghetti, cooked
1/4 c  Cilantro, fresh, loosely
-packed
3 tb Soy sauce, divided
1 1/2 tb Brown sugar, divided
2 tb Water
3/4 lb Beef top round steak, in
-strips 2 1/4 x 1/4-inch
2 tb Seasoned rice vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 lg Garlic clove, minced
1 ts Toasted Oriental sesame oil
Combine 1 tablespoon soy sauce, 1 tablespoon brown sugar, and water;
pour over beef.  Marinate beef 30 minutes.  Drain beef, discarding
marinade. In a non-stick skillet, stir-fry beef over high heat until
browned, about 3 minutes.  Remove from pan; cool to room temperature.
Mix remaining 2 tablespoon soy sauce and remaining 1/2 tablespoon
brown sugar with vinegar, lemon juice, garlic, sesame oil and red
pepper.  Combine beef with remaining ingredients.  Pour on reserved
dressing and toss.
