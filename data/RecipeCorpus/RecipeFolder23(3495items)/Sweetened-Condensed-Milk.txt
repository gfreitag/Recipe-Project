Title: Sweetened Condensed Milk
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Sugar
4 tb Butter, melted
Jim Vorheis
4 c  Instant powdered milk
1 c  Hot water
Mix in blender and store in refrigerator. Note: Costs approximately
1/2 that of commercial brands.

Colorado Cache Cookbook (1978) From the collection of Jim Vorheis
