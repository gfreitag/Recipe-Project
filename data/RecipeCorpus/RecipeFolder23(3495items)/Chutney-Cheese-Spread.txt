Title: Chutney Cheese Spread
Categories: Appetizer, Indian
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Finely chopped chutney
1 tb Snipped chives
Crackers
Fresh fruit
1 1/2 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
-cheese (6-8 oz)
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
2 1/2 tb Dry sherry
1/2 ts Curry powder
Combine first five ingredients and blend well.  Stir in chutney.
Turn into serving dish.  Top with chives if for appetizer and serve
with crackers. If served as adessert, serve with fresh fruit to dip
into mixture.

Origin: A Taste of Oregon Shared by: Sharon Stevens
