Title: Spicy Seasoning Mix
Categories: Herbs/spice, Mixes
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Dried oregano leaves
1/2 ts Ground red pepper
3 tb Chili powder
2 ts Ground cumin
1 1/2 ts Garlic powder
Combine all ingredients.  Store. covered. in airtight container.
Shake before using to blend.  Yield:  about 1/3 cup
