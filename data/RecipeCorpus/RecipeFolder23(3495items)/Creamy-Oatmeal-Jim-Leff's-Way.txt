Title: Creamy Oatmeal Jim Leff's Way
Categories: Nuts/grains, Kids
Yield: 6 servings
Salt (opt)
1 tb Maple syrup or honey (opt)
3/4 c  Old-fashioned oatmeal
1 1/3 c  Cold water
In an earthenware dish or heavy ovenproof casserole with
tight-fitting lid, combine oatmeal, water, a pinch of salt and maple
syrup, honey or brown sugar if using them. Cover tightly and place in
225'F. oven for 7 hours, or overnight, until the oatmeal reaches
desired consistency.
