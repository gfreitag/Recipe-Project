Title: Italian-Style Pistachio Pasta
Categories: Italian, Salsa/sauce, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 lb Prosciutto, sliced 1/8-inch
-thick, diced
1 c  Natural pistachios, coarsely
-chopped
1 1/2 ts Rosemary, crumbled
1/2 c  Extra virgin olive oil
4 c  Cooked penne-type pasta, hot
1 oz Blue cheese, crumbled
2 tb Butter or margarine
1    Yellow onion, cut into thin
-wedges
1/4 c  Green bell peppers, finely
-diced
1/4 c  Red bell peppers, finely
-diced
2 tb Garlic cloves, minced
Melt butter in skillet.  Add onion ans saute until nearly tender.  Add
bell peppers, garlic, prosciutto, pistacios, rosemary and olive oil to
skillet.  Continue stirring until hot and sauteed.  Add crumbled blue
cheese; spoon over hot pasta.
