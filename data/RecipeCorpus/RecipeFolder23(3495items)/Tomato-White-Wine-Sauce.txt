Title: Tomato White Wine Sauce
Categories: Fish, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
-large garlic clove,
1/3 c  Dry white wine
-Thyme
-salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 T  Shallots, minced
-minced
1 1/3 c  Fish or chicken stock
1    Bay leaf
1 1/2 c  Tomatoes, peeled and seeded
Simmer the shallots and garlic, in a saucepan with the stock or wine
and herbs. When the liquid has reduced to about 2 tbs, in 5 minutes
or so, stir in the tomatoes. Cover the pan for several minutes so the
tomatoes render their juices, then uncover and boil several minutes
until the sauce thickens to the consistency you want .
