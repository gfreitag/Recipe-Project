Title: Plum Chutney
Categories: Indian
Yield: 6 servings
7 c  White vinegar
2    Chunks fresh ginger
7 ts Dry mustard
7 ts Curry powder
7 ts Salt
25 lb Plums
8 lb Onions
4 lb Apples
5 lb Currants
8 lb Brown sugar
Cook all except sugar.

Add sugar to dissolve.

Cook and bottle.

This is a recipe I used to make up in a restaurant I worked in when
we had a glut of blood plums. It is pretty simple but good,
especially if left to mature a while in the bottle.
