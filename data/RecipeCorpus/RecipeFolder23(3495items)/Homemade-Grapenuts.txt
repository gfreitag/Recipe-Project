Title: Homemade Grapenuts
Categories: Nuts/grains
Yield: 15 servings
1 1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Vanilla
2 tb Soda
2 1/2 qt Buttermilk or sour milk
5 lb Brown sugar
3/4 lb Oleo (melted)
8 lb Whole wheat flour
1 1/2    Tsps. maple flavor
Put dry ingredients in a bowl, except soda which should be added to
milk just before adding milk to dry ingredients.  Last add oleo and
flavorings. Mis well.  Dough should be fairly thick.  If too thick,
add milk, if too thin, add flour.  Put in pans and spread even.  Bake
in 350 degree oven till baked.  Take out and let cool.  Rub over wire
netting, or over grater. Dry in oven till crisp.  Makes 15 pounds of
grape nuts.

Source:  Homecoming Cookbook by Phila Hach Posted by Dar Rains
