Title: Snapper a L'orange
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 servings
-salt and pepper
1 t  Grated orange rind
1 1/2 lb Snapper fillets
2 T  Orange juice
3 T  Cooking oil
Combine orange juice. rind and oil. Place fish in oil baking pan and
sprinkle with salt and pepper. Pour sauce on top of fish and bake at
350 degrees for 20 or until fish is done. Note: The American Heart
Association recommends replacing the salt with nutmeg.
