Title: Crockpot Fish Chowder
Categories: Fish, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
7 T  Margarine
4 md Potatoes, peeled and diced
1 lg Can evaporated milk
-salt and pepper
1    Onion, chopped
1    To 1 1/2 lb fish
-water to cover
1 pt Milk
Use any fish or combination that includes flounder, ocean perch,

haddock, halibut, or sole. Directions: Saute onions in 4 tbs of
margarine. Cut fish into bite size pieces and add to onion and in
crockpot (set on high). Cook until fish is flaky (about 4 hours). Add
potatoes , cover with water and boil for 40 minutes. Add milk and
evaporated milk and cook on low for 20 minutes. salt and pepper.
