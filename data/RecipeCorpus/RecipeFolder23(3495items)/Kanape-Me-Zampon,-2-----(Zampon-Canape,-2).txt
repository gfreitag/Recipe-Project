(Zampon Canape, 2)
Title: Kanape Me Zampon, 2
Categories: Appetizer, Greek
Yield: 1 servings
Cooked beet slices
Pitted black olives
Zampon
Crackers
Cut zampon to fit shape of the crackers you are using.  With a
sharp knife, cut out small hearts and diamonds from the beet rounds;
cut out small clubs and spades from the olives.  Decorate the zampon
canapes with these so they resemble playing cards.
