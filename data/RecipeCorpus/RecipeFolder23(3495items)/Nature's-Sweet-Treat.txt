Title: Nature's Sweet Treat
Categories: Snack, Kids
Yield: 6 servings
-peanut butter
1/2 c  Nonfat dry milk
1/3 c  Mild flavored honey
1/2 c  Creamy or chunky style
Combine honey and peanut butter in bowl. Gradually stir in dry milk
until thoroughly blended. Shape into narrow roll. Wrap in waxed paper
and chill until firm. Cut into bite-sized pieces and wrap in plastic
wrap or waxed paper.
