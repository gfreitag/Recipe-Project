Title: Frozen Apple Sauce and Fruit Cup
Categories: Kids, Snack
Yield: 6 servings
11 oz Can mandarin orange
-segments, drained
1 c  Grapes, if desired
2 tb Orange juice concentrate
1 c  MOTTS Chunky or Regular
-Apple Sauce
10 oz Pkg frozen strawberries,
-thawed
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients. Spoon fruit mixture into
individual dishes or paper cups. Freeze until firm. Remove from
freezer about 30 minutes before serving. 7 (1/2 cup) servings.
