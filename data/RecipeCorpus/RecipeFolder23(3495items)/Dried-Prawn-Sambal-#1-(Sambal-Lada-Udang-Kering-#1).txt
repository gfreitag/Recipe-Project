Title: Dried Prawn Sambal #1 (Sambal Lada Udang Kering #1)
Categories: Asian, Condiment
Yield: 6 servings
2 oz Oil
1/4 lb Fresh chillies, ground
1    Lemon, juice only
Stephen Ceideburg
2 oz Dried prawns
2    Onions, thinly sliced
Soak prawns in hot water until soft. Rinse and mince. Stir-fry onion
in hot oil until golden; add finely ground chillies, salt to taste.
Stir and reduce heat. Let simmer until chillies become tender. Add
prawns and lemon juice; simmer gently another 10 minutes.

From "Dishes From Indonesia", Yohanni Johns, Thomas Nelson  Ltd.,
Sydney, 1971. SBN 17 001929 2 [It says "SBN", not "ISBN" in  the
book. S.C.]
