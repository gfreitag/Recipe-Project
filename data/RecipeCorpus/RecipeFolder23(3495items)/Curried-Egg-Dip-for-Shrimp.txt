Title: Curried Egg Dip for Shrimp
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Dip
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Chopped Green Onion & Tops
1 ts Curry Powder
1 ts Lemon Juice
Cooked Shrimp
1 c  Mayonnaise
1    Hard Cooked Egg *
1 ts Ground Ginger
1    Clove Garlic
1/2 ts Salt
* Very finely chopped.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Mix together thoroughly the mayonnaise, chopped egg and ginger.
Peel garlic and crush thoroughly with the salt (or mash garlic and
mix with salt, or add separately).  Add garlic and salt, onion, curry
powder and lemon juice, blend well.  Put in small bowl surrounded by
critters.  Also good with crackers.
