Title: Gooseberry Relish
Categories: Relish, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Mustard
1 ts Ginger
1 ts Turmeric
1 qt Vinegar
5 c  Gooseberries
1 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Raisins
1    Onion, peeled and sliced
3 tb Salt
Chop gooseberries, raisins, and onion.  Add other ingredients. Heat
slowly to boiling.  Simmer 3/4 hour.  Stir frequently.  Rub through
coarse sieve. Reheat. Harriet Hinzinga, Pella, IA.
