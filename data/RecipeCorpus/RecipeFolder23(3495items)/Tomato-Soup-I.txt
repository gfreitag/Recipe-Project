Title: Tomato Soup I
Categories: Soup/stew, 1941
Yield: 4 servings
1/2    Bay leaf
1    Clove
2 tb Melted butter
Salt and pepper
2 c  Canned tomatoes
1 tb Minced onion
2 c  Milk
2 tb Flour
Combine tomatoes, bay leaf, onion, and clove.  Cover.  Simmer 10
minutes. Rub through sieve.  Combine butter and flour.  Add to tomato
puree. Simmer 5 minutes.  Add slowly to cold milk.  Heat to boiling.
Season to taste. Serve at once.  4 servings.
