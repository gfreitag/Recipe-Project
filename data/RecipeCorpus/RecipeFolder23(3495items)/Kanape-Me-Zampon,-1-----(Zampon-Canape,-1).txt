(Zampon Canape, 1)
Title: Kanape Me Zampon, 1
Categories: Appetizer, Greek
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Relish
6 ea Buttered bread rounds, >>>
Crusts removed.
6 sl Zampon
2 ea Hard cooked eggs
1 tb Mayonnaise or sweet butter
1 sm Pickle, chopped OR >>>>>
Roll the zampon into horn shapes.  Add mayo. (or butter) to chopped
eggs, then add chopped pickle (or relish); mix well.  Stuff zampon
horns with the filling, and place horns on the bread rounds.
