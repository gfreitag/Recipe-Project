Title: Egg Drop Soup #6
Categories: Chinese, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Basic Stock
2 ts Finely chopped green onions
2 ea Eggs
1 ts Salt
In a small bowl, beat eggs with a pinch of salt; set aside.

In a medium saucepan, bring stock to a boil.  Slowly pour beaten eggs
into boiling stock, stirring constantly.  Cook for 1 minute.

Place remaining salt and onions in a serving bowl.  Add soup; serve
hot.

Makes 4 servings.
