Title: Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry
Categories: Chicken, Vegetable, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Dill weed, fresh, snipped
(Optional)
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Dijon mustard
1/8 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Wish-Bone Italian Dressing
1 lb Chicken breasts, boneless,
-cut into strips
4 c  Assorted fresh vegetables
1/2 c  Green onions, coarsely
-chopped
In large skillet, heat Italian dressing and brown chicken over
medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes.  Add Assorted
Fresh Vegetables and green onions and cook, stirring occasionally, 5
minutes or until chicken is done and vegetables are crisp-tender.
Add remaining ingredients and heat through.

Notes:  Assorted Fresh Vegetables:  Use any combination of the
following to equal 4 cups - sliced zucchini, yellow squash, carrots,
red, yellow, or green peppers.
