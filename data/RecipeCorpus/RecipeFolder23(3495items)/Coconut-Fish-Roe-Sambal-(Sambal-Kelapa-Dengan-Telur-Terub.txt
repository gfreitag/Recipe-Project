Title: Coconut Fish Roe Sambal (Sambal Kelapa Dengan Telur Terub
Categories: Asian, Condiment
Yield: 6 servings
1    Lemon, juice only
2 oz Hot water to moisten coconut
1    Onion, grated
1    One-inch piece ginger,
-scraped and grated
Salt, to taste
Stephen Ceideburg
1 c  Desiccated or freshly grated
-coconut
1 oz Fish roe, chopped
1/4 ts Turmeric
2 ts Ground chilli
Mix everything together in a saucepan. Cook on low heat for 20
minutes with the lid on. Stir occasionally to prevent it from burning.

From "Dishes From Indonesia", Yohanni Johns, Thomas Nelson  Ltd.,
Sydney, 1971. SBN 17 001929 2 [It says "SBN", not "ISBN" in  the
book. S.C.]
