Title: WHOLE WHEAT WAFFLES
Categories: Breads, Breakfast
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/3 c  Whole wheat flour
3/4 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
2 tb Sugar
1 c  Milk
4 tb Melted shortening
2    Eggs
Beat egg yolks well;p add milk and shortening. Combine salt, sugar,
and flour.  Add to first mixture. Beat until smooth. Fold in stiffly
beaten egg whites. Sift baking powder lightly over the mixture and
fold in quickly. Bake in hot waffle iron. 6 servings. The Household
Searchlight - 1941
