Title: TOFU SOUP WITH LEMON GRASS
Categories: Soups, Thai, Appetizers, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Tofu
2 c  Straw mushrooms
MMMMM------------------------A SEASONINGS-----------------------------
3 sl Galanga or dried/powdered
-- equivalent
2 ea Cilantro roots, chopped
3 ea Lemon grass stalks, cut into
-- 3" lengths
4 ea Kaffir lime leaves
2 1/2 tb Soy sauce
MMMMM------------------------B SEASONINGS-----------------------------
5 ea Guinea peppers, mashed
1 1/2 tb Chili sauce, see recipe
2 tb Lime juice
1/2 ts Sugar, optional
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
2 ea Green onions, chopped
2 tb Cilantro leaves, chopped
Cut tofu into 1/2" cubes.  Cut mushrooms in half. Put 6 c cold water
in a large pot & bring to a rapid boil. Add the A SEASONINGS, reduce
heat to medium low & simmer for 15 minutes. Remove any solid pieces
of galanga, cilantro, lemon grass or kaffir that there are. Add the
tofu & mushrooms & simmer another 3 minutes. Mix in the B SEASONINGS
& heat through. Place soup in a tureen & garnish. To make the soup
less hot use milder chilies.
