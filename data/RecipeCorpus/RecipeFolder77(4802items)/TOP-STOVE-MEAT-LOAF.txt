Title: TOP STOVE MEAT LOAF
Categories: Meat, Loaf
Yield: 6 Servings    1 1/2 lb ground chicken breast,
skinless
:
1/2 c  white bread crumbs
10 3/4 oz tomato soup, condensed
1/2 c  onions -- chopped
1    egg white -- whipped
1/4 ts black pepper
1/2 ts olive oil
1/4 c  water
1/2 ts prepared mustard
2 sl fat-free american cheese
singles
:

In a mixing bowl, combine chicken, bread crumbs, 1/4 cup tomato soup,
onions, egg white, and black pepper. Shape firmly into 2 loaves. In a
skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add loaves. Brown on both sides.
Cook, covered on low heat for 25 minutes. Stir in remaining tomato
soup, water, and mustard.  Top loaves with cheese slices. Cook,
uncovered for 10 minutes.
