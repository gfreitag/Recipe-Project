Title: WATERMELON & PEPPER SAUCE
Categories: Digest, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Cubed, seeded watermelon
1 c  Fresh lime juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Hot pepper sauce (can be
Adjusted to taste)
Combine lime juice, salt & pepper sauce. Put watermellon cubes on
serving tray and juice mixture in a bowl in the middle of the
watermelon cubes. Dip watermelon cubes in juice.

I must warn you that my husband and son did NOT like this. I thought
it was great. If anyone tries it, let me know what you think about it.
