Title: VIRGIN STRAWBERRY DAIQUIRI
Categories: Non-, Alcohol, Mixed, Drinks
Yield: 1 Servings
3 oz Frozen strawberries -- in
Syrup
1 oz Fresh lime juice
1 ts Sugar
Cracked ice
1    Strawberry
1    Orange slice
Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add lime juice, frozen
strawberries, and sugar. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail
glass. Garnish with strawberry and orange slice.
