Title: YUMMY CHEESE FILLING
Categories: Sandwiches, Appetizers, Snacks, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Cheese, grated - cheddar,
Longhorn, jack or combo.
1 lg Onion, chopped
4    Cans chopped ripe olives
2    Cans diced green chilies or
Half jalapeno and reg chilis
2 sm Cans tomato sauce
1/2 c  Oil
4 tb Vinegar
Garlic powder to tast
Mix all ingredients in large bowl.  Mix well!   Remove some of the
dough from inside rolls, or a loaf of french bread that has been cut
in half, vertically. Fill with cheese filling, wrap in foil and bake
in 300~ oven for 45 mins or 350~ for 30 mins. formatted for mm by
PSeevers
