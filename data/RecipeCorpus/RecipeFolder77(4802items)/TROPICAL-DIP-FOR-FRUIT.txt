Title: TROPICAL DIP FOR FRUIT
Categories: Appetizers, Fruits, Holiday, Do ahead, Quick\easy
Yield: 1 Dip
MMMMM------------------------NORMA WRENN-----------------------------
8 oz Carton sour cream
1/2 c  Apricot preserves
1/2 c  Macadamia nuts; chop; toast
1/4 c  Flaked coconut
Combine all ingredients; cover and chill.  Serve with assorted fresh
fruit. Yield: 1-3/4 cups.

Source:  Southern Living 1991 Annual Recipes - Oxmoor House
