Title: TRANSYLVANIAN STEW
Categories: Main dish, Low fat
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Cloves garlic
1 lg Onion.
3 tb Paprika (preferably Szego)
1 tb Hot paprika
-
-
2 lb Chicken (amount not
-critical)
2    Chicken boullion cubes
-(optional)
2 lb Sauerkraut
4    Potatoes peeled and chunked
-(raw)
-(or more paprika + a shot
-of tabasco or
-other heat)
Chop garlic and onion. PAM and heat stew pot, add garlic and then
onion. Add paprika When onions cooked, add chicken (1-2 lbs)

Add juice from Sauerkraut and enough water to mostly cover the
chicken (2 cups)

After simmering for 20 minutes, pull out chicken & remove bones.
(Alternatively use boneless chicken or turkey) Add boullion cubes Add
sauerkraut & potatoes Cook for another hour or two.
