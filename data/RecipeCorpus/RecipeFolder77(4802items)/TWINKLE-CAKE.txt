Title: TWINKLE CAKE
Categories: Snacks, Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
CONKLIN(VXBD11A) --
FILLING:
1/2 c  Butter or marg
1/2 c  Crisco
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Evap milk
1 tb Vanilla -- CAKE
3 lg Eggs
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
3 1/2 ts Baking powder
2 c  Flour
Mix together and put into 13 x 9 pan or you can use a yellow cake
mix. Cook for 25 to 30 min at 350. then cut into squares and put
filling on cake that has been split and top with other piece.
