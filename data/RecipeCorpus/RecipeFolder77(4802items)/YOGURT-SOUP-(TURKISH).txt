Title: YOGURT SOUP (TURKISH)
Categories: Soups, Turkish
Yield: 1 Servings
8 c  Meat or chicken stock
-or water
80 g  Flour
80 g  Rice
3 c  Yogurt
2    Egg yolks
3 tb Butter
2 tb Dried mint
1- Boil the rice in meat or chicken broth, on low heat 2- Beat the
yoghourt with the egg yolks and flour. Stirring constanly, slowly add
2 cups of boiling stock. Add it to the meat stock with rice. Let
simmer for 10 minutes. 3- Heat the butter. Add dried mint. Leave on
low heat for 1 minute. Pour it over the soup and serve.
