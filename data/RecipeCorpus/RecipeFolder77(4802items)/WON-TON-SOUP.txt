Title: WON-TON SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Won tons
1 lb Ground Pork
1 ea Egg beaten
1 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
6 c  Chicken broth
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Chopped shallots
Mix pork,egg,soy sauce,salt & pepper. Fill won - tons. Cook in boiling
water until won-tons float to top. Remove from water. Heat chicken
stock and  add won tons, ladle into soup bowls and add shallots and
soy sauce. Sandra A - Young - Chee's  recipe.
