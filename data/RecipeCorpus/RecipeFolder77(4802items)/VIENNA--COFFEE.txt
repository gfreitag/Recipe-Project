Title: VIENNA  COFFEE
Categories: Beverage
1 c  Instant coffee
Yield: 5 1/2 cups d
2 c  Instant cocoa (Nestle's)
2 c  Creamora
1/2 c  Sugar or 1 c. powdered sugar
1 t  Cinnamon

Mix all ingredients.  Store in tightly covered jar. Add 2 teaspoons
to a cup of boiling water.  Top with tablespoon whipped cream.
