Title: VANILLA PECAN BRANDY
Categories: Misc., Liquers
Yield: 1 Serves
1 qt Brandy
2    Vanilla beans (2 inch )
6 oz Pecans, chopped
2 c  Sugar syrup (see recipe )
1 pn Cinnamon
Prep time: 0 mins   Cook time: 0 mins   Difficulty: **

Pour brandy, pecans, cinnamon, and vanilla beans (extract may be
alternated ) in jar and cover. Let stand for 2 - 3 weeks. Add sugar
syrup to taste. Let stand 1 week. Strain and steep 2 more weeks.
Ready to serve. Excellent for baking. Yield: 1 1/2 quarts Container:
1/2 gallon jar
