Title: TUNA CLUB
Categories: Fish, Sandwiches
Yield: 2 Servings
6 sl Low-fat bacon -- cooked and
Drained
6 sl 7 grain bread
1/2 c  Fat-free mayonnaise
1    Recipe
4    Lettuce leaves
Naked Tuna Salad
Toast bread as desired. Lay 2 slices of bread on work surface. Spread
with mayonnaise. Divide tuna salad mixture between the 2 slices,
spreading evenly over the surface of the toast. Arrange 3 strips of
bacon on top of each. Spread a little more mayonnaise on 2 more
slices of the toast and place a slice, mayonnaise-side down, on each
serving of bacon. Spread a little more mayonnaise on top of the 2
slices and divide lettuce over the tops. Spread more mayonnaise on
the remaining 2 slices of bread and invert one toast onto either
sandwich. Invert 4 toothpicks into each sandwich and cut into=
quarters.
