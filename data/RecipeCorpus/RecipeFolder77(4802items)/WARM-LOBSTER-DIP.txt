Title: WARM LOBSTER DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 2 Cups
1 cn (5 oz.) lobster
1/2 lb Light cream cheese, cubed
1/4 c  Sauterne or other white wine
1 tb Light mayonnaise
1    Clove garlic, minced
1 ts Finely chopped onion
1 ts Granulated sugar
1 ts Dijon mustard
Pinch salt & cayenne pepper
Drain lobster; flake meat with fingers and set aside.

In small saucepan, melt cheese over low heat until smooth, stirring
often. Stir in lobster, wine, mayonnaise, garlic, onion, sugar,
mustard, salt and cayenne; heat through, stirring occasionally.
Serve in warmed bowl. Makes about 2 cups.  Variation: Crab Dip:
Substitute 1 pkg. (7 oz.) frozen crab for the lobster. Yield: 2 cups
