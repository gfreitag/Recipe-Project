Title: TURKEY DRESSING
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 12 Servings
2 ts Salt
2 ts Sage -- ground
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts White pepper
6 c  Soft bread crumbs --
Toasted
6 c  Cornbread stuffing
2    Eggs -- beaten
1/2 c  Butter
2 tb Bacon fat
1    Onion -- chopped
2 tb Fresh parsley -- minced
2    Whole celery stalks --
Chopped
Turkey giblets stock
Combine salt, sage, thyme, baking powder, pepper, bread crumbs, and
eggs. Melt butter with bacon fat in skillet on low heat; add onion,
celery and parsley. Simmer for about 15 minutes. Mix together the
butter and crumb mixtures. Soften to your taste with stock made from
stewed giblets.

: Elizabeth Powell
