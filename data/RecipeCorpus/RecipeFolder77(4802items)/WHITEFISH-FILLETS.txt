Title: WHITEFISH FILLETS
Categories: Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb White fish fillets
1 tb Lemon juice
Salt & pepper
2    Shallots, thin slice OR
4    Scallons, thin slice
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1/4 c  Cheese, grated
White wine
Moisten fish with lemon juice (sprinkle on). Toss. Place in a shallow
dish (buttered). Add salt & peppper, cover with shallots, crumbs,
cheese. Dot with butter. Pour on wine to about 1/3 way up fish. Bake
uncovered at 450 for 25 min. until opaque
