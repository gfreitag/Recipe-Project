Title: WILD BASMATI RICE WITH ORANGE-SOY
Categories: Rice, Restaurant
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Basmati rice
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Fresh orange juice
1 tb Soy sauce, low sodium
1 tb Dry sherry
1/4 ts Hot pepper sauce
SAUCE
1 tb Butter flavored canola oil
1/4 c  Chopped pecans -- pan
Roasted
2 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
1 tb White wine
Use steamer to steam rice in the liquids listed. Roast nuts in a dry
pan, add the oil, and the wine. Stir in the cilantro and stir in
cooked rice. Stir to mix. Serve with milder meat entrees and/or
salads.

PANTRY - try  Sri-ra-cha, an Asian chili pepper sauce (Recommended by
KM at McRecipe, 1996) Recipe By
: adapted from Wild Chinese Rice (ChopStix)
