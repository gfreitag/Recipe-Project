Title: VELVEETA HAMBURG DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs, Beef
Yield: 1 Batch
1 lb Hamburg
1 lb Velveeta cheese, cut up
Soul seasoning
1 md Onion, chopped
1    0r 2 Jalepeno with seeds
Chopped
Cook meat, onions and pepper. Drain off fat. Melt cheese in crockpot
and add meat mixture, add soul seasonings to taste. Serve warm with
nacho chips or chilled like a pate with crackers.
