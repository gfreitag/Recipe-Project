Title: TO ROAST INDIVIDUAL CLOVES OF GARLIC
Categories: Info/tips, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Batch
1    Head of garlic
1/2 c  Olive oil
2    Or 3 thyme sprigs
1    Bay leaf
Salt & freshly ground pepper
Preheat oven to 250 F. Break garlic head into cloves; leave inner
skins on the cloves.  Place garlic in a small oven-proof dish and add
the olive oil, thyme and bay leaf.  Season lightly with salt and
pepper. Bake garlic until it is very tender, about 40 minutes. Turn
the cloves over from time to time.  Remove from the oven and let
garlic come to room temperature.
