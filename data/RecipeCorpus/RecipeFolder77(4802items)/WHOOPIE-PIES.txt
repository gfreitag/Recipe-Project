Title: WHOOPIE PIES
Categories: Pies, Pastries
Yield: 36 Servings
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Cocoa Powder
1/2 ts Baking Powder
1 1/2 ts Baking Soda
Filling-----
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Powdered Sugar
1 c  Marshmallow Fluff
1/2 ts Vanilla
Few Drops Of Milk
Preheat oven to 375.

Cream shortening and sugar.  Mix in egg, milk, and vanilla. Sift dry
ingredients together and add.  Drop by teaspoonful on a cookie sheet,
flatten with the bottom of a glass and and bake for 8 minutes. Cool
and put filling between cakes.

Makes 3 dozen pies.
