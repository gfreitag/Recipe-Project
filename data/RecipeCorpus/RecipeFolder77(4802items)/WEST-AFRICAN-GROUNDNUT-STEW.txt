Title: WEST AFRICAN GROUNDNUT STEW
Categories: Vegetarian, Soups/stews, African
Yield: 4 Servings
2 ea Sweet potatoes
2 tb Vegetable oil
3 ea Garlic cloves, minced
3 tb Grated ginger
2 tb Coriander
1/2 ts Cayenne
1 md Onion, chopped
2 md Toamtoes, chopped
4 c  Eggplant
1/2 c  Stock
1 c  Zucchini
2 ea Green peppers, chopped
2 c  Tomato juice
1/2 c  Peanut butter
Boil the potatoes till they are just tender.  Saute the garlic &
ginger with the spices for 1 minute & then add the onion. Saute for a
few minutes longer. Add tomatoes, eggplant & a splash of the water.
Simmer for 10 minutes.  Add the zucchini & peppers & simmer for 20
minutes.

Drain the potatoes, mash roughly & add to the stew. Add the juice &
peanut butter.  Stir well.  Simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.

Serve on rice or millet & garnish with pineapple or banana slices.
