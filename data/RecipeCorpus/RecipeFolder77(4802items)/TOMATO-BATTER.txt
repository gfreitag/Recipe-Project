Title: TOMATO BATTER
Categories: Italian, Batters
Yield: 1 Chicken
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Chopped tomatoes, well
-drained
1/2    Onion, chopped
2    Parsley sprigs, minced
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Black pepper
In bowl, beat flour and milk into smooth batter. Mix in tomatoes,
onion, parsley, salt and pepper. Makes enough to coat one chicken.
