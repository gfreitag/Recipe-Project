Title: TIPS OVER RICE/NOODLES
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Grains
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Sirlion tips
2 tb Vegetable oil
1    Thin slice ginger root
1    Garlic clove; minced
3/4 c  Beef broth
2 tb Soy sauce
2    Green onions; 1in pieces
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
DIRECTIONS ~------------------------------------------------------
~--- CUT MEAT INTO 1 1/2 IN CUBES. IN LARGE SKILLET OR WOK HEAT OIL.
ADD BEEF GINGER AND GARLIC; STIRFRY UNTIL MEAT IS BROWN ON ALL SIDES,
ABOUT 2 MINUTES. MIX REMAINING INGRED. AND ADD TO BEEF. HEAT TO
BOILING, REDUCE HEAT. COVER AND SIMMER, STIRRING OCCASIONALLY UNTIL
BEEF IS TENDER, ABOUT 30 MINUTES . SERVE OVER RICE OR NOODLES.
