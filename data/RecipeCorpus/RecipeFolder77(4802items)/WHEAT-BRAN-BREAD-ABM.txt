Title: WHEAT BRAN BREAD ABM
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Buttermilk
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
1/2 c  Oatbran
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Salt
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Butter
1 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
Put ingredients in bread pan in order suggested by manufacturer. Use
whole wheat setting if machine has that option.

Deb's notes: Good and fairly light for a wheat bread.

Source: Hitachi Home Bakery Cookbook.
