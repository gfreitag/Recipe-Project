Title: YELLOW SPLIT PEA SOUPS
Categories: Soups, Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Dried yellow split peas
8 c  Vegetable stock
2 md Potatoes
2 lg Carrots
3    Celery stalks
1 lg Onion
1    Turnip, peeled
2    Parsnips, peeled
3 ts Mustard powder
1 ds Allspice
1 ts Cumin
1 ts Marjoram
1 ts Thyme
2 ts Salt
Freshly ground pepper
Croutons
Rinse split peas, bring to a boil in the stock. Coarsely chop the
vegetables & add to the water as it comes to a boil. Lower heat &
simmer for about 1 1/2 hours. Puree soup in batches until it is quite
smooth. Add spices & herbs & season with salt & pepper. Reheat gently.
