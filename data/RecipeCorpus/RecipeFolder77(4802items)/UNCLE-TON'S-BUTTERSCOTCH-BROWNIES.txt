Title: UNCLE TON'S BUTTERSCOTCH BROWNIES
Categories: Desserts
1/2 c  melted butter
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  dark brown sugar
2    egg
1 1/2 c  flour
2 ts baking powder
1 ts vanilla

Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

Butter a 9" square cake pan.  Mix all of the ingredients together,
combining them well. Spread mixture in the cake pan and bake for 35-40
minutes, or until dry on top and almost firm to the touch. Let cool
for 10-15 minutes, then cut in 2" squares.

: Nancy Atherton "Aunt Dimity's Good Deed
