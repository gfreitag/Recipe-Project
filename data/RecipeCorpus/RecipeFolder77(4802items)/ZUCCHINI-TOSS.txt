Title: ZUCCHINI TOSS
Categories: Side dish, Red wine
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Vegetable/olive oil
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/2    Head lettuce
1    Medium zucchini,thin sliced
2    Green onions,sliced
2 tb Dry red wine
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Paprika
1/2    Small bunch romaine
1/2 c  Sliced radishes
2 tb Crumbled blue cheese
Shake oil, wine, vinegar, salt, mustard and paprika in tightly
covered jar. Refrigerate until chilled.
Tear greens into bite sized pieces in large salad bowl. Mix in
remaining ingredients. Shake dressing; pour over salad. Toss until
greens glisten.
