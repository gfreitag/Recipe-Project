Title: ZUCCHINI SOUP 3
Categories: Soups
Yield: 1 Servings
1 md Onion
1    Clove garlic
8 md Zucchini, cut into 1 1/2
-inch slices
1    Can veggie broth
2 1/2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Parsley
1 ts Basil
1/8 ts Black pepper
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
Saute onion and garlic in some oil. Add everything else. Bring to a
boil, then reduce temperature to simmer and cook for 15 minutes.
Place 2 C of soup in blender at a time. Puree. Serve with garnish or
parmesan cheese.
