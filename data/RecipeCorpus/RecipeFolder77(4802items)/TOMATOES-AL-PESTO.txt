Title: TOMATOES AL PESTO
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 2 Servings
1    Small lettuce
2 tb Vinaigrette
3 tb Pesto
1 lg Tomato; sliced
Fresh basil leaves
-(to garnish)
Toss the inner lettuce leaves in the vinaigrette and put 3-4 leaves
on each plate. Spread some pesto on to each slice of tomato and place
on top of the lettuce. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve.
