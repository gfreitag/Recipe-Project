Title: WHISKEY SOURS [A]
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 Servings
1    6-oz can frozen lemonade
-concentrate, thawed
1    6-zo can frozen orange juice
-concentrate, thawed
1/4 c  Confectioners' sugar
4    Jiggers (6 oz) Southern
-Comfort
2 c  Crushed ice
Combine lemonade concentrate, orange juice concentrate, sugar,
Southern Comfort and ice in pitcher. Stir until ice is almost melted.
May add additional ice cubes. Serve with orange slice and cherry.

Source: Great Recipes from Great Gardeners
