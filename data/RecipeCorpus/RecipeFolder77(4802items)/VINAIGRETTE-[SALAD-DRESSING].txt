Title: VINAIGRETTE [SALAD DRESSING]
Categories: Salad dress
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Balsamic vinegar
2 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Honey
1 tb Dijon mustard
2 tb Oil
1    Clove garlic
1 c  Water
Salt & pepper to taste
In a covered jar combine all ingredients and shake well. Refrigerate
until needed. Serve on mixed salad greens.
