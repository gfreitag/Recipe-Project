Title: ZUCHINI & SAUSAGE SOUP
Categories: Soups/stews
Yield: 1 Servings

1 c  Celery, chopped
1    Onion, chopped
1/2 lb Sausage
1/2 ts Oregano
Salt
Pepper
1/2 ts Basil
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1    Green pepper, chopped
28 oz Tomatoes
