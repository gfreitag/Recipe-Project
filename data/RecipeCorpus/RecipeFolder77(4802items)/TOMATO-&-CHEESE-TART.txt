Title: TOMATO & CHEESE TART
Categories: New imports, Side dishes, Vegetables
1    sheet puff pastry
Yield: 4 Servings
6    eggs
1 c  cream
2 TB melted
2 c  tomatoes, peeled -- seeded
and diced
:
1 c  Parmesan or Swiss cheese
1 TB oregano -- chopped
Salt and pepper
:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Roll out puff pastry to fit inside a
10-inch shallow tart pan. Crimp edges and thoroughly prick bottom of
shell with a fork. In a small bowl lightly beat eggs, add cream and
mix well. Add remaining ingredients and adjust seasonings to taste.
Pour into tart shell and bake for 45 minutes to
1    hour, or until firm.
