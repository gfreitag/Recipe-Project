Title: TURKEY & HAM RILLETTE
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Cooked chopped ham
1 lb Leftover chopped cooked
Turkey
1/4 lb Soft butter
1/2 ts Fresh ground pepper
1/2 ts Tarragon
tb melted butter
2-3

Put ham and turkey into the bowl of your food processor and process
using a pulsing action. Do not puree. Stir in softened butter, pepper
and tarragon. Put into 3-4 small crocks. Refrigerate for an hour
before covering with a thin layer of melted butter. Store in
refrigerator.
