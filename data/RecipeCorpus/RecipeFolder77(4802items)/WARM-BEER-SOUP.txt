Title: WARM BEER SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  Beer
1 c  Milk
1 ea Egg yolk
1 tb Sugar
2 ea Cloves, whole
1 pn Cinnamon
1/2 ts Lemon rind
1 tb Flour
1 ds Salt
Bring beer to boiling, mixed with cloves, sugar, cinnamon, and lemon
rind. Brink milk to boiling point, mixed with flour, salt and add
this to beer. Add egg yolk. A nourishing soup!
