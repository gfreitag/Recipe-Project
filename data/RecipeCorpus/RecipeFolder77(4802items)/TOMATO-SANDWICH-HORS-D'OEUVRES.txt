Title: TOMATO SANDWICH HORS D'OEUVRES
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
8 tb Deviled ham
3 tb Mayonnaise
1/2 ts Minced onion
4 lg Tomatoes
Parsley
Combine the ham, mayonnaise and onion.  Rinse, dry and cut each
tomato into four 1/4 inch crosswise slices. Spread deviled ham
mixture on half the tomato slices and top each with a second tomato
slice to make sandwiches. Garnish with mayonnaise and a sprig of
parsley and serve as a first course. Serves 8  Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source: The Culinary Arts Cookbook.
