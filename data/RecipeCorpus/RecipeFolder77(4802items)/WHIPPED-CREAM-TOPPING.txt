Title: WHIPPED CREAM TOPPING
Categories: Desserts, Toppings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Heavy cream
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Whip cream until almost stiff. Add sugar and vanilla and beat
until cream just holds a peak.
Sprinkle 3/4 teaspoon unflavored gelatin over 2 tablespoons milk
and stir thoroughly. Place over boiling water and stir until
dissolved. Add to cream warmed to room temperature. Chill and whip.
Tops 1 (9") pie. Source: Culinary Arts Institute
