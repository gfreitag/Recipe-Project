Title: WAY OF LIFE STEW
Categories: Stews
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Stewing beef, in bite size
1/2 pk Lipton's Onion soup mix -
- stir before dividing
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cn Sherry or wine
Mix all together.  Bake at 300 for 3 hours in covered casserole. Just
as good the second time around.. Great for the cold months.

Mrs Muriel Aldwinckle, Hamilton
