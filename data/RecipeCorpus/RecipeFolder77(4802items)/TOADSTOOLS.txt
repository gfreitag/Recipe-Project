Title: TOADSTOOLS
Categories: Microwave, Snacks, Kids
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Saltine cracker
1    Small piece of Hershey bar
1 ts Peanut butter
1/2    Large marshmellow
Spread peanut butter on saltine cracker. Top with Hershey bar piece
and marshmellow. Microwave 20 seconds. Grown ups will love these as
much as the kids do. They are addicting!!
