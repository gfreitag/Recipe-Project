Title: VEGETABLE BURGERS
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables
1 x  potato
Yield: 6 Servings
1 x  onion
1/2 c  walnuts or pecans (ground)
4 T  oil
1 c  cooked oatmeal
1 c  bread crumbs
1 t  salt
1/2 t  sage
2 T  soy sauce

In blender,grind together potato,onion and nuts. Saute mixture in oil
until soft. Add remaining ingredients. Mix throughly. Form into
burger patties and fry until brown on both sides, or bake on oiled
sheet in 350 degree oven until brown. ~--
