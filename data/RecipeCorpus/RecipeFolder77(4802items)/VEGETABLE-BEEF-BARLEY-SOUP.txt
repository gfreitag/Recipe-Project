Title: VEGETABLE BEEF BARLEY SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 1 Servings
1 3/4 c  Lean beef round, 1 cubes
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1 c  Tomato sauce
1 1/2 c  Carrots, thinly sliced
1 1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1 c  Mushrooms, chopped
7 tb Pearl barley
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Spray saucepan with non stick, add beef and onion. Saute 5-7 mintues.
Add 3 quarts water adn bring to a boil. Stir in celery, carrots,and
mushrooms. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover partially, and simmer 1
hour.

Add remaining ingredients. Cover and simmer 1 hour. Stirring
occassionally.
