Title: ZESTY 'FRIED' CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb CORNMEAL
1 ts PAPRIKA
1/2 ts SALT
1/2 ts GARLIC POWDER
1/2 ts PEPPER
1/4 ts GROUND CUMIN
4 ea CHICKEN BREAST HALVES
IN A MEDIUM BOWL, COMBINE THE CORNMEAL, SALT, CUMIN, GARLIC POWDER,
PAPRIKA AND PEPPER. DEBONE AND SKIN THE CHICKEN BREASTS AND RINSE
THROUGHLY BUT DO NOT PAT DRY. COAT BREASTS EVENLY WITH CORNMEAL
MIXTURE ON ALL SIDES. SPRAY A COLD SKILLET WITH NO STICK COOKING
SPRAY AND HEAT OVER MEDIUM HIGH HEAT. COOK CHICKEN FOR 8 TO 10
MINUTES TURNING OCCASIONALLY TO BROWN ON ALL SIDES. COOK UNTIL NO
LONGER PINK.
