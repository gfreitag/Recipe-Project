Title: WALDORF SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Diced apples
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Diced celery
Mayonnaise dressing
Few grains salt
Combine apples, celery, nuts, and salt.  Moisten with mayonnaise. Mix
lightly with 2 forks.  Serve on crisp lettuce. 6 servings.
