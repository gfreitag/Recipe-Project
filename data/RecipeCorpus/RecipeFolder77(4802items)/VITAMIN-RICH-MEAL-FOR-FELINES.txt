Title: VITAMIN RICH MEAL FOR FELINES
Categories: Cyberealm, Pets
Yield: 1 Cat bowls
1/3 c  Cottage cheese
2 tb Bisquick
1 tb Chopped liver
1 tb Corn oil
1    Dash iodized salt
Mix all together for 1-2 delicious cat servings.
