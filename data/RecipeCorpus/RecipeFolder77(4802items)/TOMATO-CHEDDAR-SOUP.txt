Title: TOMATO CHEDDAR SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 8 Cups
-JUDI M. PHELPS
4 c  Chicken stock
3 c  Cheddar cheese; grated
2 lg Cans tomatoes
1 ts Basil
1/4 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Tarragon
1    (6 oz.) can tomato paste
Put tomatoes through a food processor and then through a strainer to
remove seeds.  Add all other ingredients and simmer for 20 minutes,
stirring occasionally so that cheese doesn't settle on bottom and
burn. Makes about 8 cups.  Source:  Cookbook USA
