Title: WEDNESDAY'S CHILI
Categories: Chili, Hamburger
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM----------------------MM BY H. PEAGRAM---------------------------
MMMMM----------------GLORIA PITZERS SECRET RECIPE---------------------
1 1/2 lb Ground round
2 tb Corn oil
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Onion soup, 10 oz
1 tb Chili powder
2 ts Cumin powder
1/2 ts Pepper
21 oz Kidney beans, undrained
1 cn Tomato paste, 6 oz
1 cn Tomato sauce, 8 oz
Heat oil in 10" skillet, add crumbled beef, sprinkle with salt. Pack
beef firmly in pan, cover, cook over low heat for 20 min. Put onion
soup thru blender for 1 min., add to beef, mashing with fork till it
looks like rice. Simmer, covered another 5 min. Add chili powder,
cumin, pepper. Transfer to a 3 qt pot, add kidney beans, tomato paste
and sauce. Heat for another 20 min to allow spices to blend.
