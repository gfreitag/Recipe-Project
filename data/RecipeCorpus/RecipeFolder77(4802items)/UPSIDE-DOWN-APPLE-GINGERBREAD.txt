Title: UPSIDE DOWN APPLE GINGERBREAD
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Margarine, melted
2 lg Apples, sliced
1 1/3 c  Sugar
1 1/4 c  Margarine
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg
2 c  Flour
1 1/4 c  Molasses
1 1/3 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Soda
1 ts Ginger
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Cloves
1 1/4 ts Salt
3 1/2 c  Hot tea
Pour melted margarine into a 9" dish & place apples in dish, sprinkle
with brown sugar.  Mix other ingredients together & pour over apples.
Bake at 350 degrees about 45 min. Cool in pan 4 or 5 min., invert
onto serving plate.
