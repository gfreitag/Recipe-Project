Title: ZUCCHINI BARS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 24 Servings
3 x  EGGS
1 1/2 c  SUGAR
1 c  SALAD OIL
2 c  GROUND ZUCCHINI (UNPEELED)
2 c  FLOUR
1 ts SALT
2 ts BAKING SODA
1 ts CINNAMON
1/4 ts BAKING POWDER
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS IN A LARGE BOWL UNTIL WELL BLENDED. PRESS
EVENLY INTO A JELLY ROLL PAN. BAKE AT
350    DEG F. FOR 25 TO 30 MINUTES.
