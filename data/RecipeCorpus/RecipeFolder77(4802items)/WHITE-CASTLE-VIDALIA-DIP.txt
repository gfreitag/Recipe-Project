Title: WHITE CASTLE VIDALIA DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 14 Servings
14    White Castle hamburgers,
Chopped fine; buns set aside
2 1/2 c  Chopped Vidalia onions
1 3/4 c  Hellmann's (R) mayonaise
2 c  Shredded Swiss cheese
1/2 c  Crumbled bacon
Crushed crackers (topping)
Mix all above ingredients (except buns and crackers). Bake in
casserole dish at 350 deg F. for 30-35 minutes. Let stand for 10
minutes. Put open buns under broiler until toasted. Serve dip on buns
or crackers.
