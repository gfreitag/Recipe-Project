Title: TWICE-BAKED SWEET POTATOES WITH RAISINS & P
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings    1 3/4 lb unpeeled sweet potatoes --
(4 small)
:
1/2 c  golden raisins
2 TB brown sugar
1/4 ts ground cinnamon
8 oz unsweetened crushed
pineapple -- (1 can)
drained
:
:
2 TB chopped pecans

Place potatoes on a baking sheet. Bake at 400 deg for 1 hour or until
done. Let cool 15 minutes.

Cut each potato in half lengthwise; carefully scoop pulp into a bowl,
leaving shells intact. Mash pulp; stir in raisins, sugar, cinnamon,
and pineapple. Spoon into shells; sprinkle with pecans.

Bake at 400 deg for 15 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Yield: 8
servings (serving size: 1 potato half).

: Cooking Light, Mar/Apr 1993, page 155
