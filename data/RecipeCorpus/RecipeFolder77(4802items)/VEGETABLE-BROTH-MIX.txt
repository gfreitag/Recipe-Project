Title: VEGETABLE BROTH MIX
Categories: Starters, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Cup
4 tb Celery salt
4 tb Dried parsley flakes
6 ts Garlic powder
6 ts Salt
6 ts Ground savory
2 ts Dried marjoram
2 ts Dried thyme
1 ts Pepper
1 ts Turmeric
1 ts Ground sage
Combine all ingredients and mix well. Mix again before using. Store
in a jar.
