Title: TUNA SANDWICH FILLING
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 6 Servings
227    Gram pkg. cream cheese
1 tb Lemon juice
125    Ml.mayonnaise
125    Ml.chopped ripe olives
198    Gm. can tuna drained flaked
125    Ml. peanuts chopped
Lettuce
Mix cheese and lemon juice to-gether until fluffy. stir in
mayonnaise, ripe olives, tuna and nuts. Blend well. Spread on bread
and lay lettuce on top.
