Title: TOMATO BROCCOLI (QUICK LUNCH) <T>
Categories: Digest, Sept., Quick
Yield: 1 Servings
1 bn Broccoli
1    Dozen Cherry Tomatoes
3 tb Minced Parsley
2 tb Chopped Chives
Salt
Into a lunch box put as follows: Clean and cut up the Broccoli until
the box is almost full. Quarter the Cherry Tomatoes and put on top.
Add Parsley and Chives and Salt to Taste. Do not mix!

At work, mix, then nuke for 8 minutes for soft Broccoli, or 6 minutes
for crunchier Broccoli.
