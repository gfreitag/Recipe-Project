Title: TRANQUILLITEA
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Dry peppermint leaves
1 tb Rosemary leaves
1 ts Sage leaves
Store in tightly closed jar. Use heaping tsp. in a cup of boiling
water. Steep one minute, strain, sweeten with honey and sip.
