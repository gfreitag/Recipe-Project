Title: WILD RICE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Wild rice, uncooked
Seasoned salt to taste
2 c  Chicken, cooked, diced
1 1/2 c  Grapes, green, halved
1 c  Water chestnuts, sliced,
-drained
3/4 c  Mayonnaise, light
1 c  Cashews; optional
Lettuce leaves
Cook rice according to package directions, omitting salt or
substituting seasoned salt if desired. Drain well; cool to room
temperature.
Spoon into a large bowl; add chicken, grapes, water chestnuts and
mayonnaise. Toss gently with a fork. Cover and chill.
Just before serving, add cashews if desired. Serve on lettuce
leaves or line a bowl with lettuce leaves and fill with salad.
