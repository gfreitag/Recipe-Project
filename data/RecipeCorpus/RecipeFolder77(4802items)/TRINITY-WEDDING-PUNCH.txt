Title: TRINITY WEDDING PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 25 Servings
2 qt 7 up, chilled
1 pt Sherbet (flavor=color)
12 oz Lemonade, undiluted
Soften sherbet, stir in lemonade that is partially thawed. Pour in
7-up and serve.
