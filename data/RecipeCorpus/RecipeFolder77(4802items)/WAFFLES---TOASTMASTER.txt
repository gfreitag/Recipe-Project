Title: WAFFLES - TOASTMASTER
Categories: Breakfast, Famfavorite
Yield: 7 Servings
1 3/4 c  FLOUR
2 ts BAKING POWDER
1 tb SUGAR
1/2 ts SALT
3 ea EGG YOLKS, BEATEN
3 ea EGG WHITES, BEATEN STIFF
1 1/2 c  MILK
1/3 c  OIL
MEASURE DRY INGREDIENT INTO A MIXING BOWL. COMBINE EGG YOLKS AND
MILK. STIR INTO DRY MIX. STIR IN OIL. CAREFULLY FOLD IN EGG WHITES.
DO NOT OVER MIX. POUR INTO WAFFLE IRON BY 2/3 CUPFULS. CLOSE IRON AND
BAKE ACCORDING TO INSTRUCTIONS WITH IRON.
