Title: VICKI'S CREAM CHEESE BALLS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 Balls
4 pk Cream cheese
;8 oz each
1    Armour Dried beef
;jar, in small pieces
1 bn Green onions
;sliced thin
3 ts Accent
Green olives
;diced
Black olives
;diced
Soften cream cheese.  Mix with cut up meat and onions and accent.
Seperate into thirds. in one third, mix black olives, the green in
another third. Form all three into balls. May roll "plain" third in
nuts.

Source: Joan Mershon
