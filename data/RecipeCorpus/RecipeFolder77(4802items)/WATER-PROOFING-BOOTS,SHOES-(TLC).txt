Title: WATER-PROOFING BOOTS/SHOES (TLC)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
200 g  Beeswax granules
100 g  Beef/meat fat
Traditionally mutton fat, but any red meat fat will do.

1. Melt rge beeswax and the fat in a double boiler. 2. blend well
with a wooden spoon and transfer into an old jar to cool slightly.
