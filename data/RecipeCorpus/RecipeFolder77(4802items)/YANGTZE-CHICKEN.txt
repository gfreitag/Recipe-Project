Title: YANGTZE CHICKEN
Categories: Barbeque, Low fat, Chicken
Yield: 16 Servings
2 lb Boneless skinless chicken
Breasts -- cut in 2-inch
Cubes
1    Pineapple -- cut in 2-inch
Chunks
Marinade:
1/2 c  Soy sauce
3/4 c  Ketchup
1 c  Dry white wine
1 c  Orange marmalade
4 tb Honey
16    Skewers
Mix marinade ingredients in large bowl. Add chicken and mix well to
coat. Marinate for at least 1 hour in refrigerator. Alternate chicken
and pineapple pieces on skewers. Grill 1 to 2 minutes on each side or
to desired doneness.
