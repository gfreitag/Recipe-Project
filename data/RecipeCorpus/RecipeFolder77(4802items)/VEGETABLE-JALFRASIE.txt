Categories: Vegetables, Vegetarian, Indian
Yield: 4 Servings

2    Green peppers
2 lg Tomatoes
1/2 lb Broccoli
1/2 lb Cauliflower
4 tb Butter
1 ts Red chili powder
1/2 ts Turmeric
Ginger, fresh; 1"
3    Garlic clove

2 tb Tomato puree
