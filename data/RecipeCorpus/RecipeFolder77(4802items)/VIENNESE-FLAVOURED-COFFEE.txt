Title: VIENNESE FLAVOURED COFFEE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Dry instant coffee,
-scant
2/3 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Dry nondairy creamer
1/2 ts Cinnamon
-dash each of ground
-allspice,
-cloves, & nutmeg.
Mix all ingredients & Store in air tight jar. To make mix 4 tsp with
one cup hot water.

20 servings From: Ronda Eikenberry
