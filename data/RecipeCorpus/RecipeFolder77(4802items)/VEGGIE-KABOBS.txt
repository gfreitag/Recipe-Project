Title: VEGGIE KABOBS
Categories: Barbeque, Low fat, Vegetables
Yield: 16 Servings
2    Green peppers -- cut in 1"
Pieces
4 sm Yellow squash -- sliced in
Thick rnds
2    Dozen mushrooms, large
2 md Onions -- cut into wedges
1 pt Cherry tomatoes
Marinade:
1 ts Salt
1 tb Italian herb mix (such as
Oregano,sage
Marjoram,thyme,savory, basil
Rosemary)
4    Cloves garlic -- crushed
2/3 c  Water
2/3 c  Balsamic vinegar
16    Skewers
Place vegetables in a large bowl. Add remaining ingredients and
marinate for 2 hours at room temperature. Arrange vegetables on
skewers and grill or broil for 7 to 10 minutes, turning frequently.
