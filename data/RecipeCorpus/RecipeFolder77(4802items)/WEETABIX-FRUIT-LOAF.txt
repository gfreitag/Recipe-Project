Title: WEETABIX FRUIT LOAF
Categories: Breads, Breakfast
Yield: 1 Loaf
2    Weetabix
6 oz Sugar
6 oz Sultanas
8 oz Self-Raising Flour
8 fl Milk
1. crush the weetabix into fine crumbs 2. add sugar, fruit and flour
3. add milk and mix well 4. Pour into greased lined loaf tin 5. Bake
at Gas Mark 4 / 350F for 60 to 70 minutes 6. Cool for 10 minutes 7.
When cold store in an airtight tin
