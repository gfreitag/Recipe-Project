Title: ZESTY HAMBURGERS
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Ground beef or turkey
4 ts Prepared horseradish
2 ts Dijon mustard
1 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Salt -- optional
4    Hamburger buns -- split
In a bowl, combine the first six ingredients; mix well. Shape into
four patties.  Pan-fry, grill or broil until no longer pink. Serve on
buns.
