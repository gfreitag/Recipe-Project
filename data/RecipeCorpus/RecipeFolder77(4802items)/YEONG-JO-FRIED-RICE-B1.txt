Title: YEONG JO FRIED RICE B1
Categories: Grains, Chinese, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Raw prawns or shrimp,
-diced
1/4 c  Barbecued pork, diced
-OR- cooked ham, diced
1/4 c  Green onions,
-cut 1/8" thick
1/4 c  Green peas
1 c  Lettuce, shredded
3 c  Cold cooked rice
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Soy sauce
Place oil in preheated wok or skillet. Bring oil to sizzling point,
add prawns or shrimp & salt. Toss & turn 2-3 minutes or until cooked.
Add pork or ham, green onions, peas, lettuce, rice and soy sauce.
Press rice gently into pan and fry for a few seconds, repeating the
process until all rice is hot completely through. Turn & mix rapidly
5-7 minutes. Add a few drops of oil if necessary to prevent burning.
