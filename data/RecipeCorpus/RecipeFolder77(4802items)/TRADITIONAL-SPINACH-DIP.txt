Title: TRADITIONAL SPINACH DIP
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Serving
1 pk (10 oz) frozen spinach,
Thawed & drained well
8 oz Sour cream
8 oz Mayonnaise
1 pk Knorr's dry vegetable soup
Mix
2 bn Green onion, chopped
1 sm Can chopped Waterchestnuts
(optional)
1 lb Loaf of light or dark rye
Bread, round and uncut
Mix together all ingredients (except bread) and chill. To serve,
hollow out the round, unsliced loaf of rye bread. Fill center with
dip, and use pieces of the bread to scoop and eat. Crackers may also
be used instead of the bread for serving.
