Title: VEGETARIAN QUICHE SPUD
Categories: Stuft spuds, Vegetarian
Yield: 2 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------STUFFED SPUDS----------------------------
2    Baked potatoes
1 tb Butter
1/4 c  Onion, chpd
1/2 c  Sour cream
2    Eggs, lightly beaten
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
3/4 c  Swiss cheese, cut 1/4" cubes
1 c  Leftover vegs, assorted chpd
Parsley
Cut a thin slice from the top of each potato. Remove pulp being
careful not to tear shels. Place potato pulp in mixing bowl; mash;
cover and set aside. Set shells aside. heat butter in skillet; saute
onion until tender, low heat, 10 mins. Add sour cream, eggs, salt and
pepper, stirring constantly. Add mashed potatoes; mix well. Add
cheese and vegs; mix lightly. Heap mixture into shells. Bake 350 F,
45 mins. Garnish with parsley.
