Title: VARNISHED CHICKEN *** PUNKY
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Casserole
Yield: 4 Servings
1 x  Chicken ; cut up
1 ea Dry onion soup mix
1 c  Catsup
2 tb Brown sugar
1 c  Water
Place chicken pieces in single layer in large baking pan. Combine
onion soup mix, catsup, brown sugar and water and pour over chicken.
Cover with foil.  Bake at 300 degrees for 20 minutes. Uncover and
bake 20 minutes longer or until chicken is done. LA TIMES Author:
