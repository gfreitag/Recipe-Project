Title: ZUCCHINI QUESADILLAS
Categories: Main dish, Mexican, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
3 ea Flour or corn tortillas
1/2 c  Green onions, thinly sliced
1 md Jalapeno chile, seeded &
-- minced
2 c  Zucchini, julienned
1 c  Corn niblets
1/4 c  Black olives, sliced
Preheat oven to 400F.  Place one tortilla on an ungreased oven baking
sheet & layer with half the remaining ingredients. Top with a second
tortilla & the rest of the filling.  Finish with the final tortilla.
Bake about 10 minutes, till the tortilla are browned around the
edges. Cut into quarters & serve hot.

"Vegetarian Gourmet" Spring, 1995
