Title: VEAL PARMESAN
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Veal
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper, ground
2 ea Eggs, beaten
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1 ts Oregano
1 c  White wine
3/4 c  Olive oil
Pound veal till thin. Pat dry, cut into pieces. Combine bread crumbs,
cheese, spices and salt. Set aside. Beat eggs in a shallow dish. Dip
veal into eggs, then crumb mixture, coating on both sides. Heat oil
in skillet. Saute' veal until golden brown. About 3 minutes per side.
Remove to warm platter. Drain fat, stir in wine, scraping up brown
residue till boiling.Pour over veal. Serving hint - cover with tomato
sauce. Top with mozzarella cheese slices. Broil till cheese melts.
