Title: TROUT ITALIANO
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
4    8-oz. trout fillets
Italian salad dressing
Place the trout fillets in a shallow bowl and cover with Italian salad
dressing.  Marinate for about 2 hours.

Place trout on a well oiled grill about 5 inches from the heat. Cook
for 5 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.

Serve with new potatoes and sauteed zucchini.  Garnish with fresh
parsley.

Serves 4.

(Adapted from a National Fisheries Institute recipe)
