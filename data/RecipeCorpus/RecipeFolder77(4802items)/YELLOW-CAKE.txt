Title: YELLOW CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts, Microwave
Yield: 6 Servings
9 oz Package of yellow cake mix
Ingredients called for
On package label
Mocha Frosting (See recipe)
1. Mix cake according to package directions. 2. Pour batter into 1
8-inch round, wax paper lined, heat- resistant, non-metallic baking
pan. 3. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 6 minutes or until a cake
tester comes out clean. 4. Frost with Mocha Frosting.
