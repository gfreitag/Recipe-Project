Title: TOMATO-ONION SOUP WITH RIGATONI (VEGAN)
Categories: Soups, Mcdougall, Prodigy, Dec.
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lg Onion; chopped
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 c  Water
28 oz Canned tomatoes; chopped, w/
1 tb Dried parsley
1 c  Rigatoni
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1    Bay leaf
1/2 ts Dried basil
1 tb Soy sauce
Recipe by: The New McDougall Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:10
:    Saute the onion and garlic powder in the water for 15 minutes.
Add the remaining ingredients, except the soy sauce. Simmer, covered,
for 1 hour. Add the soy sauce and remove the bay leaf just before
serving. 150 calories, 1.0 gram fat per serving.
