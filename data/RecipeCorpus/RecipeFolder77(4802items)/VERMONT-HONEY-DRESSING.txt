Title: VERMONT HONEY DRESSING
Categories: Fruits, Cheese
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Paprikw
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Celery seed
1/3 c  Honey
5 tb Vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Salad oil
Combine all ingredients in a blender, adding oil last and pouring in
slowly while other ingredients are blending. Blend until smooth.
Serve over cottage cheese and fruit. From The Taste of Vermont,
Vermont Agricultural Enterprises, Inc.
