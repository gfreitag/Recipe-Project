Title: TOMATO & BELL PEPPER SALAD
Categories: Try, It
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lg Tomatoes, peeled, seeded,
-and diced
2    Green peppers, diced and
-lightly fried
1 bn Cilantro, chopped fine
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1 1/2 ts Cumin
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 ts Salt
1    Garlic clove, chopped fine
1 c  Lemon juice
1/2    Onion, chopped fine
Mix all ingredients and serve
