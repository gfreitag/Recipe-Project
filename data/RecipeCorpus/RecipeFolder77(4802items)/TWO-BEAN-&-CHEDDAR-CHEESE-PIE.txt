Title: TWO-BEAN & CHEDDAR CHEESE PIE
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 Servings
3/4 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Milk
1    Egg, slightly beaten
16 oz Can garbanzos, drained
16 oz Can kidney beans, drained
8 oz Tomato sauce
1/2 c  Chopped green bell pepper
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Dried oregano leaves
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Heat oven to 375 degrees.  Grease pie plate, 10 x 1 1/2 inches. Mix
flour, 1/2 c cheese, the baking powder and salt in medium bowl. Stir
in milk and egg until blended. Spread over bottom and up sides of pie
plate. Mix 1/2 c of the remaining cheese and the remaining
ingredients; spoon into pie plate.  Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 c
cheese. Bake about 25 minutes or until edges are puffy and light
brown.  Let stand 10 minutes before cutting.
