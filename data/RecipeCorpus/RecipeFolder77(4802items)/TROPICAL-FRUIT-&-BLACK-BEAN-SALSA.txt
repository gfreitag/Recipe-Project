Title: TROPICAL FRUIT & BLACK BEAN SALSA
Categories: Sauces, Dips
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Pineapple; diced
1/2 c  Mango; diced
1/2 c  Papaya; diced
1/2 c  Onions, red, chopped
1/2 c  Onions, red, chopped
1/2 c  Beans, black; cooked
1    Jalapeno; minced
Mix all ingredients.  Allow flavors to develop for at least 30
minutes.

Recipe from Norman Van Aken, A Mano, Miami Beach Source: Orlando
Sentinel, August 15th 1991
