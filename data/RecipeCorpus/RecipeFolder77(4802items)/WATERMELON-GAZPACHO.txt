Title: WATERMELON GAZPACHO
Categories: Soups, Fruits, Dkuhnen msn
Yield: 10 Cups
1/2 sm Watermelon
1    Red onion
1    Red pepper
1    Jalapeno pepper
1    Field cucumber
Salt and pepper
Cayenne pepper
Seed watermelon, cut into chunks and puree in food processor until
smooth. Discard rind.  Finely chop onion, red pepper and jalapeno
pepper. Peel and seed cucumber, chop into cubes. In large bowl,
combine watermelon and vegetables.  Season with salt, pepper and
cayenne. Chill 2 hours, serve. Source:  Toronto Star Newspaper, July
2nd, 1996.
