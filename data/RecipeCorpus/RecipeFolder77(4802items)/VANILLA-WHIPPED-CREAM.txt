Title: VANILLA WHIPPED CREAM
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Heavy cream
1    Vanilla bean -- split
Lengthwise
1 tb Confectioners' sugar
Over medium heat, scald cream and vanilla bean; remove from heat.
Cover and steep for 10 minutes. Scrape seeds into cream; discard pod.

Strain cream into a bowl set in an ice-water bath. Let stand, stirring
occasionally, until cold, 10 to 15 minutes.

Transfer to a large chilled metal bowl, add sugar and whip by hand
into soft peaks, about 5 minutes.

Makes 1 3/4 cups.

Total prep and cooking time: 30-45 minutes.

Per tablespoon: 30 calories; 3.2 g fat (2 g saturated fat; 96 percent
calories from fat); 11 mg cholesterol;
3 mg sodium; 0.5 g carbohydrates
