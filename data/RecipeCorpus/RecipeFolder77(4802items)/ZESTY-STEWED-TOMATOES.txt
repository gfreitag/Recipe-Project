Title: ZESTY STEWED TOMATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 6 Servings
3 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1/4 c  Finely chopped green pepper
6 lg Tomatoes, peeled & quartered
3/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Sugar
1/8 ts Garlic salt
1/2 ts Dried oregano leaves
1. In a deep, 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole melt
butter or margarine in Microwave Oven 30 seconds. Add onion and green
pepper, and heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 3 minutes or until
lightly browned. 2. Add remaining ingredients and stir to combine. 3.
Heat, covered, in Microwave Oven 5 to 6 minutes or until tomatoes are
very tender. 5tir occasionally. Serve hot or cold
