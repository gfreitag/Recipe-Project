Title: TINA'S HEARTY ROAST BEEF
Categories: Beef
Yield: 1 Servings
1    3to4 lb.roast
3 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
2    Bayleaves
3    Beef boulion cubes
Brown roast in oil. Drain off any excess oil.  Add water boulion
cubes, salt, pepper and bayleaves. Add carrot chuncks and sliced
onions. Bring to boil while covered, turn downto a low simmer for 2
to 3 hours or untill tender. Thicken juices to make gravy.
