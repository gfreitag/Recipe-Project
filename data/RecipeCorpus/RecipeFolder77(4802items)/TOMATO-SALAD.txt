Title: TOMATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, German
Yield: 4 Servings
5 md Tomatoes, Chopped
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Basil, Dried
1/4 ts Thyme, Dried
1/4 ts Pepper, Freshly Ground
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
6 tb Vinegar
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1 lg Onion, Diced
Blend all ingredients together and chill for 1 hour before serving.
Serve on lettuce leaves.
