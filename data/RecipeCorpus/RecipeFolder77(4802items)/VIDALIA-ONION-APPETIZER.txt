Title: VIDALIA ONION APPETIZER
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
4 lg Onions, sweet
3/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Vinegar
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 ts Celery salt
Thinly slice onions.  Combine onions, water, sugar, and vinegar;
marinate overnight. Before serving, drain and pat dry. Toss with
mayonnaise and salt. Serve on party rye.
