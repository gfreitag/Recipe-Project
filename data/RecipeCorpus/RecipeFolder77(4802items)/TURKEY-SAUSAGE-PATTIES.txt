Title: TURKEY SAUSAGE PATTIES
Categories: Poultry, Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM--------------------NATHAN KOPEL WDVB56A-------------------------
1/2 ts Fennel seed
1/2 ts Red pepper, crushed,optional
1/2 ts Rosemary
1 lb Ground turkey
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Salt
Crush fennel, red pepper and rosemary and mix into turkey along with
pepper and salt.   Form 8 patties. Coat non-stick skillet with
cooking spray. Cook patties over medium heat about 15 to 20 minutes,
turning to brown both sides.

This is easy and good.  Of course you can use ground pork instead of
turkey.
