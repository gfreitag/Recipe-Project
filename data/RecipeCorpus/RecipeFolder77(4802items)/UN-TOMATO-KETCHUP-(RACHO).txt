Title: UN-TOMATO KETCHUP (RACHO)
Categories: Appetizers, Sauces, Spreads, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Cooked carrots
1/4 c  Cooked beets
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Sweetener
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Oregano
Blend all ingredients till smooth.  Keep in the refrigerator.

JoAnn Racho, "Of These Ye May Freely Eat"
