Title: YANKEE APPLE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Fruit
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Flour
4    Eggs
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Oil
3 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  Orange juice
1/4 ts Salt
APPLE MIXTURE:
4 lg Apples, peeled -- cored and
Sliced
4 ts Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
Beat everything but Apple Mixture with mixer for 10 minutes. Grease 2
loaf pans or 1 tube pan and make several layers of batter and apples,
beginning with batter and ending with apples. Bake at 350^ for 1 hour
or until tester is dry when inserted in center.  A tube pan will take
10-15 minutes longer. (If glass pans are used, turn oven to 325^
after first 10 minutes)
