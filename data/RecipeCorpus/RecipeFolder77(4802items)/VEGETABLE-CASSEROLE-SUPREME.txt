Title: VEGETABLE CASSEROLE SUPREME
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
4 ea POTATOES, SLICED
3 ea CARROTS, SLICED DIAGONALLY
1 cn PEAS
1 lb GROUND BEEF
*
USE CREAM OF MUSHROOM OR TRY CHICKEN SOUP OR ?
1 cn MUSHROOM SOUP
*
*------------------------------------------------------
~------------- * LAYER ALL VEGETABLES AND BEEF IN A 2 QUART CASSEROLE
DISH. POUR THE SOUP (DILUTED WITH 1 CAN OF WATER) OVER THE TOP OF THE
DISH. BAKE AT 350 DEG F. UNTIL ALL LAYERS ARE DONE.
