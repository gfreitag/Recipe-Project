Title: WALT DISNEY'S OWN CHILI & BEANS
Categories: Chili, Legumes, Ground beef, Restaurants
Yield: 6 -8 servs
MMMMM-------------------MICKEY'S GOURMET CKBK------------------------
2 lb Dried pinto beans
2 md Onions, sliced
1/4 c  Veg oil
2 cl Garlic, diced
1 c  Celery, chpd
2 lb Ground beef
1 ts Chili powder, or more
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Dried thyme
28 oz Cn tomatoes, about 3c
Salt and pepper
MMMMM---------------------SPICIER VARIATION--------------------------
1/8 ts Coriander
1/8 ts Turmeric
1/8 ts Chili seeds
1/8 ts Fennel
1/8 ts Cloves
1/8 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Dried ginger
1 sm Mexican chili pepper
Walt Disney's personal recipe. Wash, sort and soak beans in cold water
overnight. Drain; place in 2 qt saucepan. Add water to cover 2" over
beans. Add onions and simmer in covered pot until tender, 2 hrs.
Meanwhile, heat oil in lg sauce pot; saute garlic until lightly
browned. Add celery and beef; cook. Add chili powder, paprika, and
thyme. Break up tomatoes with spoon and mix with meat mixture; cover,
simmer 1 hr. When beans are tender, combine with meat, stirring
gently. Salt and pepper and add additional spices if desired, cook 30
mins.
