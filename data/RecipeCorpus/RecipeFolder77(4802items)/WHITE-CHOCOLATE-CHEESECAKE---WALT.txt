Title: WHITE CHOCOLATE CHEESECAKE - WALT
Categories: Cakes, Cheesecakes
Yield: 8 Servings
8 oz White chocolate
16 oz Cream cheese
1/3 c  Sugar
2    Egg
1    Chocolate cookie crust
Melt chocolate in a double boiler.  Cream together cream cheese
and sugar. Beat in eggs one at a time. Mix in melted choclate. Place
in cookie crust.  Cook 30 minutes at 350 F. Cool before cutting.
