Title: TWIN MOUNTAIN MUFFINS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
5 ts Baking powder
2 c  Flour
1 c  Milk
1    Egg
Cream butter.  Add sugar and well beaten egg, then dry ingredients,
alternately with milk.  Bake in buttered muffin pan for 20 to 25
minutes in 400 degree oven.
