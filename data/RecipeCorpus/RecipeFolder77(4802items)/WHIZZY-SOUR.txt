Title: WHIZZY SOUR
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  ORANGE JUICE
1/4 c  LEMON JUICE
1/4 c  MARASCHINO CHERRY JUICE
1 l  LEMON-LIME SODA
2 ea SLICES OF ORANGE
2 ea MARASCHINO CHERRIES
COMBINE JUICES, POUR INTO ICE CUBE TRAYS. FREEZE. PLACE 3 TO 4 CUBES
IN GLASS AND FILL WITH SODA. GARNISH WITH ORANGE AND OR CHERRY.
