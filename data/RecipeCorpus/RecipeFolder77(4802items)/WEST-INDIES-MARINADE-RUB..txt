Title: WEST INDIES MARINADE RUB.
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
SUITABLE for all meats.
15 ml 1 tbsp curry powder. 15 ml 1 tbsp ground cumin. 1,25 ml quarter
tsp chili powder. 7,5 ml half tbsp ground black pepper. 15 ml 1 tbsp
paprika. 2,5 ml half tsp ground mixed spice.

Mix all the ingredients together. Coat the meat in the mixture. Cook
under the grill or on the barbecue. Brush lightly with oil during
cooking.
