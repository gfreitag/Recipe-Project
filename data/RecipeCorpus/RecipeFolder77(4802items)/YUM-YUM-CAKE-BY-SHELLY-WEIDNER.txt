Title: YUM YUM CAKE BY SHELLY WEIDNER
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Cherry pie filling
1 pk Cake mix-white or yellow
1 c  Pecans-chopped
1 c  Apple pie filling
1 1/2    Margarine-melted (sticks)
1. In 9 x 13 inch pan, spread pie fillings. Spread dry cake mix over
top of
pie fillings. Drizzle melted margarine over cake mix. Sprinkle
pecans
on top. Do not stir or mix. 2. Bake in 325 degree oven at 1 hour
and 10 minutes. Leave in pan. 3. Serve hot or cold with whip cream or
ice cream.
