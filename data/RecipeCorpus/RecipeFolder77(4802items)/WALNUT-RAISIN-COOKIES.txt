Title: WALNUT RAISIN COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 48 Servings
2 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Butter or margarine,softened
1 1/3 c  Packed light brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
2    Eggs
1 1/3 c  Walnut pieces,chopped
1 c  Sliced dried apples,chopped
1/2 c  Seedless raisins
In a small bowl,combine flour and baking soda;set aside.In a medium
bowl,using an electric mixer,cream butter,brown sugar and
vanilla.Beat in eggs,one at a time.Stir in flour mixture.Mix in
walnuts,apples and raisins.Drop by tablespoons onto greased baking
sheets.Bake @ 350 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.Cool on wire racks.
