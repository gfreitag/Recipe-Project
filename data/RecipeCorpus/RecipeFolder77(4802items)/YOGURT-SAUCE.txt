Title: YOGURT SAUCE
Categories: Ethnic, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pt Yogurt
1/2    Cucumber  ,peeled, grated,
Drained
2    Cloves garlic -- crushed
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Salt and white pepper -- to
Taste
pn Cayenne pepper
Mix all the ingredients together and serve as a dip or sauce.
