Title: WHATSIT
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 lg Onion, grated
1 pk Noodles, small package
1 cn Mushroom soup
1/2 lb Hoop cheese, grated
Salt
1 cn Tomato soup
Brown meat and onion slowly while noodles are cooking. When both are
done, add cheese to meat.  Add noodles and stir until cheese melts.
Add soup; mix slightly. Pour into baking dish. Cover with cracker
crumbs. Dot with butter and brown. Randy Rigg
