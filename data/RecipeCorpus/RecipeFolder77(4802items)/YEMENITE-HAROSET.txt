Title: YEMENITE HAROSET
Categories: Jewish, Fruits, Nuts, Ethnic, Holiday
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 lb Pitted dates
1/4 lb Raisins
4    Figs, dried
1 1/2 c  Sweet wine
1/4 lb Walnuts
1/4 lb Almonds
2 tb Sesame seeds
1/2 ts Cumin
1/2 ts Ginger, ground
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cardomom
Place fruits in food processor or blender.  Finely chop. Add 1/2 cup
of the wine.  Mix again, at low speed. Add remaining ingredients and
mix at low speed until nearly smooth.
