Title: ZUCCHINI TABBOULEH
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
5 sm Zucchini; cut in small diced
-very small
2 c  Minced parsley leaves
4    Scallion; minced
1/4 c  Fresh mint; minced
1/2 c  Lettuce leaves; minced
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Lemon juice
3/4 c  Olive oil
1    Cl Garlic; minced
Recipe by: The Daily Breeze Food Section, July 1, 1991 Combine all
ingredients, mixing well. Refrigerate before serving.

Makes 8 servings.
