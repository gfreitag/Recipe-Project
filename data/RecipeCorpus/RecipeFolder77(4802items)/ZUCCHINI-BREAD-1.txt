Title: ZUCCHINI BREAD 1
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
3    Eggs
1 c  Oil
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Grated zucchini
2 ts Vanilla
3 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Baking powder
3 c  Flour
1 c  Walnuts, optional
Mix wet ingredients, then add dry ingredients. Pour into loaf pans
(2). Bake for an hour at 350.

Recipe from Charlo Zauss, El Cajon.
