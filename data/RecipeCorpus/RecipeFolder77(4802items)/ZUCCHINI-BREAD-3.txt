Title: ZUCCHINI BREAD 3
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Grated zucchini
3    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Oil
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
3 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Chopped walnuts
Mix well.  Bake at 350 for 40-45 mins in a 9x13 greased pan.

Good luck! Ellen
