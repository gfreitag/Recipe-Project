Title: ZUCCHINI & TOMATOE CASSEROLE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Zucchini
3    Tomatoes (sliced and peeled)
1    Small onion sliced
Dash of worchestershire
Salt and pepper to taste
Dots of butter
Layer zucchini and tomatoe and onion, then repeat. Put dots of butter
on top.  Bake 350 degree 45 min. to 1 hour. Top with cheese if
desired.
