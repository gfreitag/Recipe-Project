Title: YOGURT SHAKES
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Plain yogurt
2/3 c  Orange juice
1    Banana
1 c  Fruit*
4    Ice cubes (optional)
Combine all ingredients in blender.  Blend until smooth. Serves 2.

*Use 1 or more fresh or frozen fruits - my favorites are peach,
blueberry, strawberry, mango-peach, nectarine-raspberry, mixed
berries, etc.
