Title: ZUCCHINI CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Corn oil
3 c  Sugar
3 c  Zucchini; grated
3 c  Flour
1 1/4 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
1 c  Nuts
1 c  Raisins
Beat eggs until they become light yellow. Add next 3 ingredients. Add
to egg mixture and blend well. Add nuts and raisins. Bake in a well
greased tube pan for 1 1/2 hours or a 9 x 13" pan for 1 hour @ 350
degrees.
