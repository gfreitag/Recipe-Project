Title: WITCH'S BREW
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Hamburger
1 lb Onion, sliced
1 cn Pork and beans
1 cn Mushrooms, sliced
1 c  Catsup
Salt and pepper to taste
Brown hamburger and onion; stir often to break up hamburger. Add
beans, mushrooms, catsup, salt and pepper. Blend well. Simmer until
ready to serve. Randy Rigg
