Title: TORTIERE
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 lb Ground pork
1    Onion, chopped
1    Clove, garlic
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Savory or thyme
1/4 ts Celery salt
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/2 c  Water
In a pot mix together 1/2 lb each lean ground beef and 1/2 lb ground
pork and all other ingredients and bring to a boil. Turn down to
simmer for 20 minutes.  Add 1/2 cup breadcrumbs spoon by spoon until
the fat is absorbed. Cool mixture.  Pour into uncooked pie shell. Add
the top crust. Cook 25 minutes in a preheated 450F oven. Source: The
Canadian Heritage Cookbook.ch
