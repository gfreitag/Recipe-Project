Title: TOM'S SUMMER VEGGIES
Categories: Digest, August
Yield: 5 Servings
2    Yellow squash
2    Green zucchini
2    Red tomatoes
1 lg Onion
x  Veg stock for sauteing
x  Basil, oregano, pepper to
Taste
x  Salt (just a touch)
Optional
Saute thinly sliced onion until almost clear.  Add sliced squash &
zucchini when almost done add tomatoes wedges and herbs. Stir and
saute until tomatoes are heated.

This is very simple, fast, & yummy as a side dish, or if you like
serve it over spiral pasta for a main dish (just add more stock for a
sauce).
