Title: YELLOW DELICATE CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
2/3 c  Milk
2 c  Pastry Flour
2 ts Baking Powder
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
3    Eggs
Cream butter and sugar. Add milk, then flour sifted with the baking
powder and salt. Add vanilla. Add eggs (unbeaten) one at a time and
beat each one thoroughly into the mixture. Bake for 45 min at 375 deg.
