Title: VEGGIE PIZZA APPETIZER
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings  tube pillsbury cresent rolls-flatten on a cookie sheet. Brush with
beaten egg-bake 375 about 11 minutes. cool mix together: 8 oz. soft
cream cheese, 1 pkg dry Italian dressing or ranch, your choice, 1/2
cup Mayonnaise. Spread this mixture on dough. Note: I used light
everything, cheese, dressing, mayo. Top with broccoli, onion, olives,
pepper, shredded carrot, shredded cheese and other veggies of your
choice. Chill and cut int squares to serve.
