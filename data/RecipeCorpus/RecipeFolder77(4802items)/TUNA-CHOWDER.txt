Title: TUNA CHOWDER
Categories: Soups, Low cal, Low fat
Yield: 3 Servings
10 1/2 oz Can cream of potato soup
Condensed
1/2 c  Skim milk
1/2 c  -- water
2 tb Onion -- minced
