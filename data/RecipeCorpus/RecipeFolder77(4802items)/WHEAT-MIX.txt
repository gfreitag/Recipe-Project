Title: WHEAT MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
6 c  Whole-wheat Flour
1 1/2 c  Instant Non Fat Dry Milk
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Baking Powder
3 c  Unbleached All-purpose Flour
1 tb Salt
1/2 c  Wheat Germ
2 c  Vegetable Shortening
In a large bowl, combine whole-wheat flour, unbleached flour, dry
milk, salt, wheat germ, sugar, and baking powder. Mix well. With a
pastry blender, cut in shortening until evenly distributed. Put in a
large airtight container.  Label as WHEAT MIX and store in cool, dry,
place. Use within 10 to 12 weeks.  Makes about 14 Cups WHEAT MIX.
