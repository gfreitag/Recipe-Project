Title: WEST VIRGINIA HOT PEPPERS
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 Servings
5 lb Jalapenos
1 qt Tomato sauce
1 c  Vinegar
1 c  Cooking oil
2 tb Salt
2 tb Sugar
Wear rubber gloves when handling peppers.  Cut peppers in half and
remove seeds.  Put tomato sauce, vinegar, oil, salt and sugar to
boil. Add peppers and cook exactly 8 minutes. Put in hot clean
canning jars and seal. Process in hot water bath for 15 minutes.
