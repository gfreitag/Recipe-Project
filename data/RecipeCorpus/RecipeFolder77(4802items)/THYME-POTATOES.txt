Title: THYME POTATOES
Categories: Side dishes
Yield: 1 Servings
3 lb Potatoes; boiled
1/2    Olive oil
2 tb Unsalted butter
1/2 md Onion; chopped
1 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Salt and pepper
1 tb Red wine vinegar
Saute onion in 1/2 cup oil and butter.  Add thyme, garlic powder,
salt and pepper, and red wine vinegar. Dice potatoes and pour sauce
over them. Add additional oil if necessary.
