Title: WEINGELEE (DESSERT WINE JELLY)
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 10 Servings
4 pk Unflavored gelatin
2 c  Cold water
1 c  Sugar
3 c  Boiling white grape juice
1 c  Medium dry white wine
Sweetened whipped cream
Strawberries
Sprinkle gelatin on cold water to soften. Stir in sugar and grape
juice; stir until gelatin is dissolved. Stir in wine. Pour into 6-cup
mold. Refrigerate until firm, at least 4 hours. Unmold and serve with
whipped cream. Garnish with strawberries.
