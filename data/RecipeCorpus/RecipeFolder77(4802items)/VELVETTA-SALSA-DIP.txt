Title: VELVETTA SALSA DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Mexican, Condiments, Salsa
Yield: 3 Servings
1 lb VELVETTA CHEESE SPREAD
1    JAR PACE PICANTE SAUCE
2 tb CILANTRO (CHOPPED)
microwave cheese and picante sauce in bowl on high 5 minutes stirring
after 3 minutes.  stir in cilantro Serve hot with tortilla chips
