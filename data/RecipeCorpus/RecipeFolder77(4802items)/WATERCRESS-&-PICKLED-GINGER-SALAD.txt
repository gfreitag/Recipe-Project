Title: WATERCRESS & PICKLED GINGER SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Cups
1 cl Garlic, crushed
1/8 ts Kosher salt
1 tb Liquid from a jar of pickled
-ginger
1 tb Fresh lime juice or
-rice-wine vinegar
1 tb Canola oil
1 ts Honey
Freshly ground black pepper
-to taste
6 c  Stemmed, washed and dried
-watercress
4    Scallions, chopped
1/3 c  Drained pickled ginger
1. With the side of a chefÂ’s knife, mash garlic with salt. Place in a
small bowl or a jar with a tight-fitting lid. Add ginger liquid, lime
juice (or vinegar), oil, honey and pepper; whisk or shake until
blended.

2. Place watercress, scallions and pickled ginger in a large bowl.
Just before serving, toss with dressing.

Per serving: 45 Cal, 1 G Pro, 4.0 G Fat, 3 G Carb, 90 MG Sod,0 MG
Chol, 1.0 G Fiber
