Title: WOK MADE RICE
Categories: Rice
2 c  water
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  rice

Bring water to a boil in wok on high setting. Stir in rice and cover.
Reduce heat to medium and simmer 15 minutes or until all water is
absorbed. Turn off heat and allow rice to sit 5 minutes. Fluff with a
fork and serve.

Yield: 3 cups of rice
