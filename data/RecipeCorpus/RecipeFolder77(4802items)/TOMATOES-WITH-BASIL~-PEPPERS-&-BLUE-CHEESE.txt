Title: TOMATOES WITH BASIL~ PEPPERS & BLUE CHEESE
Categories: Mc, Salads, Vegetables
3    tomatoes -- coarsely
chopped
Yield: 1 Servings
:
1/2    onion -- chopped
1    pepper, hot or sweet --
chopped
:
4    basil leaves -- chopped
1/2 c  blue cheese -- crumbled
2 TB red wine vinegar
3 TB olive oil
salt and pepper -- to taste
:

Put vegetables in bowl.  Mix dressing ingredients and pour over
vegetables. Chill in refrigerator for at least one hour.
