Title: WHITE BEAN SALAD WITH GRILLED TOMATOES
Categories: New, Text, Import
6 lg plum tomatoes -- halved and
seeded
Yield: 6 Servings
:
3 TB extra-virgin olive oil
Kosher salt and freshly
ground black peppe
:
:
2 ts mashed anchovy fillets or
paste -- (or to taste)
:
1 TB roasted garlic
2 c  cooked white beans (3/4 cup
dried)<<OR>>
:
2 c  canned beans -- drained and
rinsed
:
1 c  red onions -- chopped
2 TB capers -- drained and
minced
:
1 TB fresh mint -- minced
3 c  young arugula leaves
Garnish--
Mint sprigs
:
:

Prepare a charcoal fire. In a bowl, lightly toss the tomatoes with 1
tablespoon of the olive oil. Season with salt and pepper. Grill the
tomatoes over hot coals until lightly colored but still firm. Or broil
them. Set aside.

In a large bowl, combine the remaining 2 tablespoons olive oil, the
anchovy fillets, and the roasted garlic. Add the cooked white beans,
red onions, capers, mint, and plenty of freshly ground black pepper.
Toss to combine.

Serve the bean salad on the arugula leaves, surrounded by the grilled
tomatoes. Garnish with mint sprigs.

Yield: 6 servings
