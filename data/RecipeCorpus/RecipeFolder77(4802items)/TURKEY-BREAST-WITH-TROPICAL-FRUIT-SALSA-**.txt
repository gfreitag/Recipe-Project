Title: TURKEY BREAST WITH TROPICAL FRUIT SALSA **
Categories: Turkey, Fruits, Bar-b-q
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Turkey breast slices; about
-  1-1/2 lbs
MMMMM--------------------TROPICAL FRUIT SALSA-------------------------
17 oz Mango slices; drain, cubed
1 c  Strawberries; sliced
1/2 c  Kiwi; diced
3 tb Red onion; finely chopped
3 tb Fresh lime juice
1 ts Grated lime peel
1/4 ts Ginger
In medium bowl, gently combine all salsa ingredients. Cover;
refrigerate at least 1 hour to blend flavors. Heat grill. When ready
to cook, oil grill rack. Place turkey on gas grill over low heat or
on charcoal grill, 4-6" from medium coals. Cook 8-10 minutes or until
mo longer pink, turning once. Serve with salsa.
