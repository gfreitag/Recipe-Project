Title: VEGETABLE BURGER SOUP
Categories: Soups/stews, Hamburger, Beef
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Ground beef
2 c  Water
1 cn (16-oz) stewed tomatos
1 cn (8-oz) tomato sauce
1 pk (10-oz) frozen mixed
Vegetables
1/4 c  Dry onion soup mix
1 ts Sugar
In large saucepan, brown ground beef. Drain off excess fat. Stir in
water, stewed tomatos, tomato sauce, vegetables, onion soup mix and
sugar. Bring to boiling. Cook and simmer 20 minutes.
