Title: WHITE CLAM SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Fish, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
1    To 3 cloves garlic,
-peeled and quartered
16 oz Canned chopped clams,
-undrained
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1 ts Dried oregano leaves
1. Place oil and garlic, to taste, in a small, heat-resistant,
non-metallic bowl and heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 3 minutes.
2. Remove garlic pieces. 3. Add remaining ingredients and heat,
uncovered, in Microwave Oven 4 minutes or until heated through. Stir
occasionally. 4. Serve over spaghetti.
