Title: ZESTY COCONUT CREAM DRESSING
Categories: Vegetables, Fruits, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Coco Lopez cream of coconut
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1 ea .70 ounce pk. italian
-dressing mix
In small jar with tight-fitting lid or bowl, combine ingredients; mix
well. Chill to blend flavors. Stir before serving. Serve with tossed
green salads, fruit salads, or with coleslaw. Refrigerate leftovers.
