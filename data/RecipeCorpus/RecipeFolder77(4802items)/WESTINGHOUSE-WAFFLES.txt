Title: WESTINGHOUSE WAFFLES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Flour
4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Sugar
2 ea Eggs
1 1/4 c  Milk
6 tb Butter; melted
Sift dry ingredients together. Beat egg yolks, add with milk to dry
ingredients. Beat until batter is smooth. Add melted butter and folk
in stiffly beaten egg whites. Preheat iron and bake 3 or 4 minutes.
This recipes makes 6 waffles.

Source: Mrs. E. A. Williams, Grand Prairie Grange, Marion County, OH
