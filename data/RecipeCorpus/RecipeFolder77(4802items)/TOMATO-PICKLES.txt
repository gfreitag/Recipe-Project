Title: TOMATO PICKLES
Categories: Pickles
Yield: 1 Recipe
30 lg Ripe tomatoes
4 lg Onions
6 lg Pears
6 lg Peaches
6 lg Apples
4 c  Granulated sugar
2 tb Salt
1 pk Mixed pickling spices, tied
-in cloth
1 pt Vinegar
Peel and chop fine the tomatoes, omions, pears, peaches and apples.
Mix all ingredients and boil until thick. Pour into sterilized KERR
jars and seal.
