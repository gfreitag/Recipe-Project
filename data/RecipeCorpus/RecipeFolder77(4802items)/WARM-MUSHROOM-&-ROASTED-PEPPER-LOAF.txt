Title: WARM MUSHROOM & ROASTED PEPPER LOAF
Categories: Jaw, Sandwiches
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Loaf (8 ounces) Italian or
French Bread
1/4 c  Butter
12 oz Fresh white mushrooms,
Thickly sliced
4 oz Fresh shiitake mushrooms,
Trimmed and sliced
1 ts Minced garlic
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
4 oz Sliced baked ham
1    Jar (7 oz) roasted red
Peppers, drained and patted
Dry
4 oz Provolone cheese, thinly
Sliced
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Halve bread horizontally. Howwow out each
half, leaving 1/2 inch thick shells. In large skillet, melt butter;
add mushrooms, garlic, salt and black pepper. Cook about 6 to 8
minutes, stirring frequently, until mushrooms are tender and liquid
evaporates; set aside. Arrange ham in bottom half of bread shell; top
with mushroom mixture, red peppers and cheese, cutting to fit shell
if necessary. Cover with remaining shell. Place bread on baking
sheet; cover loosely with foil tent. Bake about 7 minutes, until
cheese melts. Cut into serving-size pieces; serve with a knife and
fork.
