Title: WATERMELON COOLER
Categories: New, Text, Import
3 c  watermelon
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Yield: 4 Servings
:
3    scoops lemon sherbet

Remove seeds from watermelon and slice into chunks. Place on cookie
sheet and freeze. Once frozen put in blender. Add sherbet and lemon
juice. Blend until smooth. Pour into cups and serve.
