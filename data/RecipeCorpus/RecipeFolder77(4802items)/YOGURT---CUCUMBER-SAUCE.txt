Title: YOGURT - CUCUMBER SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 3 Cups
1    Large seedless cucumber,
---peeled and finely diced
2 c  Plain low-fat yogurt
2 tb Honey
2 ts Finely shredded fresh mint
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1 ts Olive oil
Pinch cayenne pepper
Kosher salt
In a medium bowl, combine the cucumber, yogurt, honey, mint garlic,
olive oil and cayenne.  Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
Season with salt just before serving.

Reprinted from Food and Wine Magazine, July 1996.
