Title: UKRAINIAN POTATO PANCAKES <DERUNY>
Categories: Ukrainian, Ethnic, Sidedish, Vegetable, Resort
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ea Onion large grated
6 ea Potatos peeled and grated
2 tb Flour
2 ea Eggs
2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Black pepper
1 pt Sour cream
1/2 pt Cream
In a large bowl use a mixer to puree the ingredients except the sour
cream & cream. You may do this in a food processor as well or a
blender. Heat oil in a skillet and when hot drop large spoonsful of
the micture. Cook until browned on one side. Turn and repeat. When
done remove, drian, and place in a warm oven. Mix the sourcream &
cream together. Serve warm with a large dollop of the the cream
mixture! This a staple in Ukrainian homes and these pancakes will
store well in the refrigerator for 2-3 days. In many homes preserves
or jam is also served on these delicious pancakes. ORIGIN: Galina
Shovkoshytny, Kiev-Ukraine, circa 1996
