Title: ZUCCHINI RAISIN BREAD
Categories: Breads
3    eggs
Yield: 24 Servings
1 2/3 c  sugar
1 c  oil
1 1/2 ts vanilla extract
3 c  all-purpose flour
1 ts double-acting baking powder
1 ts baking soda
1 ts salt
1 ts ground nutmeg
2 ts cinnamon
3/4 c  nuts -- chopped
2 c  zucchini -- coarsely
shredded
:
1 c  seedless raisins

Beat eggs until light. Gradually add sugar. Sift dry ingredients
together. Add to egg-oil-sugar mixture and beat until smooth. When
well blended, stir in remaining ingredients. Place batter in two
buttered and floured loaf pans (8-1/2" x 4-1/4"). Bake at 350 degrees
for one hour.
