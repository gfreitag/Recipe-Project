Title: WHOLE WHEAT SUNFLOWER BREAD
Categories: Breads, Bmm, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pound loaf
1/3 c  Old fash rolled oats
5/8 c  Buttermilk
1    Egg
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Bread flour
2 ts Wheat gream
1 ts Salt
1 tb Applesauce
1 tb Brown sugar
Grated rind 1 orange
1/4 c  Sunflower seeds
1/4 ts Baking soda
3 ts Yeast
Crust light. Bake.
