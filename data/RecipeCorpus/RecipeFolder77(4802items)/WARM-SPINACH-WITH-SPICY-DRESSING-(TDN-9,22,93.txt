Title: WARM SPINACH WITH SPICY DRESSING (TDN-9/22/93
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Fresh spinach leaves
1 tb Olive oil
1 ts Hot pepper sauce or to taste
1 tb Mild white vinegar
1 ts Reduced-sodium soy sauce
1    Clove garlic, finely minced
1/2 ts Finely minced fresh ginger
1 tb Minced fresh chives
Rinse the spinach well under running water.  Then put the still-wet
leaves into a 4 cup measure and cover. Microwave on full power until
the spinachis just beginning to wold and soften, about 2 inutes.

Meanwhile, combine the oil, hot pepper sauce,vinegar, soy sauce,
garlic, ginger and honey in a small bowl and whisk until well mixed.

Pat the spinach dry and tip into a medium bowl.  Pour on the dressing
and toss well.  Serve warm, sprinkled with the chives.
