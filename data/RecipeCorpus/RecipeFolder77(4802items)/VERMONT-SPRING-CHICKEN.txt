Title: VERMONT SPRING CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Maple, Val's
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ea Chicken,2-1/2to 3 lb, cut up
1/4 c  Butter, melted
1/2 c  Maple syrup
1/2 ts Grated lemon rind
1 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1/4 c  Chopped almonds
2 ts Lemon juice
Place chicken pieces in shallow buttered baking dish. Mix remaining
ingredients and pour evenly over chicken. Bake uncovered 50-60
minutes at 4008F. Baste occasionally. Delicious on bed of rice.
