Title: VOODOO JERK CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
2 3/4 lb Split broiler chickens
2 tb *Uncle Billys VooDoo Jerk
Slather  Sauce
2 tb Molasses or maple syrup
Slather Chicken, marinate at least one hour. Bake at 350 for 1 1/2
hours or cook on BBQ

*The sauce contains: Peppers, onion, garlic oil, honey, molasses,
cider vinegar & spices. It's produced by: The Zombies at Starche
