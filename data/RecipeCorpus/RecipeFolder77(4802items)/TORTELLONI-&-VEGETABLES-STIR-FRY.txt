Title: TORTELLONI & VEGETABLES STIR FRY
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Small head green cabbage
1    Large red bell pepper
1    Large yellow bell pepper
1/4 c  Olive or salad oil
1/2 ts Dried basil leaves
1    Large onion
1    Large green bell pepper
2    9 ounce cheese tortelloni
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
Cut out tough core from cabbage.Coarsely slice cabbage.Slice
onion.Cut each pepper in half;cut off stems and white membrane and
discard seeds.Cut peppers into 1/2" wide strips. Meanwhile prepare
tortelloni as label directs.In a 8 quart Dutch oven or sauce pot over
high heat,in hot oil,cook cabbage,onion,peppers,salt,basil and black
pepper until tender crisp,stirring frequently. To serve,drain
tortelloni;toss with vegetables.
