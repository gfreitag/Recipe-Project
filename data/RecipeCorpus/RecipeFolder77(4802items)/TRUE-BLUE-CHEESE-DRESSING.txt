Title: TRUE BLUE CHEESE DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 1 Dressing
MMMMM--------------------NORMA WRENN NPXR56B-------------------------
1 c  Mayonnaise
4 oz Pkg blue cheese; crumbled
3 tb Milk
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Onion; chopped
2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
Combine all ingredients; stir well.  Cover and chill. Serve dressing
over salad greens. Yield: About 1-3/4 cups. Source: Betty Anderson,
America's Best Recipes A 1989 Hometown Collection, Oxmoor House
