Title: TOURTIERE IV
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lg Potato Boil and mash/save w
1 lb Minced pork
1    Onion chopped
1    Garlic clove minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Each tyme & sage
1/4 ts Each cloves and dry mustard
Boil and mash potato and save the water it was cooked in. In large
fry pan combine pork, onion, garlic and seasonings and potato water.
Simmer 25 min. Stir in mashed potato.Cool mixture and put in pastry
lined pie plate and cover with top crust. Bake in 450 oven 10 min.
then turn heat down to 350 for 20 min.
