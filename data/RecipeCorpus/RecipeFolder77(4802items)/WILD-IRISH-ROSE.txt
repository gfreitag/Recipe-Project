Title: WILD IRISH ROSE
Categories: Alcohol, Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Lime juice
1 oz Grenadine
1 oz Bushmill's
Mix and pour over ice. top with ginger ale in a 12 oz glass. will
look like a sunset, red at the bottom fading to pink at the top.

NOTES : Only 1/2 oz Bushmill's is a Mild Irish Rose... Recipe by:
Sarah Steelmon
