Title: WHITEBAIT FRITTERS (NZ)
Categories: New zealand, Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
4 oz Raw whitebait
2    Eggs
1 ts Salt
2 tb Fresh breadcrumbs
Whitefish are tiny 2 inch white fish which are the young of a fish of
the smelt family. They swim in large shoals and are netted around the
New Zealand coastline. The Maori name is Inanga.

1. Beat the eggs well and then mix in the other ingredients until
they are well blended.

2. Drop spoonfuls of the mixture into a pan with a thin layer of hot
butter. They should be fried like pancakes.

3. Serve hot with lemon juice squeezed over the fritter. Serves 4
