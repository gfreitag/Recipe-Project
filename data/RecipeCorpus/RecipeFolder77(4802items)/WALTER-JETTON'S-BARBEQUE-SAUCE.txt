Title: WALTER JETTON'S BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Tomato ketchup
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Chili powder
1/8 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Water
3    Stalks celery -- chopped
3    Bay leaves
1    Clove garlic
2 tb Chopped onion
4 tb Butter
4 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Paprika
1 ds Black pepper
Combine all the ingredients and bring to a boil. Simmer about 15
minutes. Remove from the heat and strain.

(note - the celery pieces that you strain out from the sauce are
among the most delicious things I have ever tasted - really
wonderful. In fact, I sometimes made the sauce just to have some of
the yummy celery. - Bruce)

: Walter Jetton
