Title: WHITE CHOCOLATE FUDGE
Categories: Fudge, Candies, Desserts
8 oz cream cheese,
softened
Yield: 12 Servings
:
4 c  powdered sugar
1 1/2 ts vanilla
12 oz white chocolate, melted
:
1 1/2 c  pecans (or walnuts
or macadamia nuts)
:

Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla in large mixing bowl at medium
speed with electric mixer until well blended. Gradually add
chocolate; mix well. Stir in nuts. Spread in greased 8-inch square
pan. Chill several hours.

NOTE:  You can also use 3/4 c. nuts and 3/4 c. dried apricots or other
dried fruit. Walt MM Margaret Johnson
