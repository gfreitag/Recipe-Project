Title: TORTILLAS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Corn-meal
3 ts Baking powder
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Shortening
Pour boiling salted water over corn-meal and stir. When corn-meal is
cool enough to handle, shape into thin flat cakes and cook on an
un-oiled hot griddle until brown on both sides. 8 servings. Lillian
Barker Smith, Memphis, TN.
