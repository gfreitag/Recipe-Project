Title: ZITI WITH CREAMY HERB SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 8 Servings
16 oz Pasta -- ziti
2 ts Oregano leaves
15 oz Cheese -- ricotta
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Cheese, parmesan -- grated
1/4 ts Pepper
14 c  Parsley -- chopped
Chicken bouillon cube
1/4 c  Basil, fresh -- chopped
1/2 c  Water -- boiling
Prepare pasta as directed on package; drain. Meanwhile, stir cheeses
with parsley and seasonings. Dissolve bouillon cube in boiling water;
stir into cheese mixture.  Pour over pasta; toss to coat.
