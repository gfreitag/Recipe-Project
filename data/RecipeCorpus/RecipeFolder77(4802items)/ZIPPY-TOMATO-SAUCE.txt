Title: ZIPPY TOMATO SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Vegetables, Hamburger
Yield: 8 Servings
2 tb Margarine Or Butter
1/2 c  Green Bell Pepper; Chopped,*
1 ea Onion; Small, Thinly Sliced
8 oz Tomato Sauce; 1 can
1 c  Chili Sauce
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 ts Chili Powder
*    You can use canned chopped Green Chiles or Jalapenos for a hotter
sauce. ~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Melt margarine in a small saucepan. Add green
peppers and onion.  Cook and stir until the onion is tender. (Note:
If using the canned peppers, just cook the onion alone.) Stir in all
the remaining ingredients and heat to boiling, stirring occasionally.
Serve hot over patties.
