Title: TURKEY MEATBALLS
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Turkey; ground
1 1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1 c  Onions; chopped
1 c  Carrots; chopped
1 c  Tomato sauce
3/4 ts Sage
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Recipe by: Cooking For Good Health Preparation Time: 0:20 Preheat
oven at 375. Combine all ingredients in a medium-size bowl and mix
well. Shape into balls and place on a non-stick baking sheet. Bake 35
minutes until browned.
