Title: ZUCCHINI CASSEROLE (EHRLICH)
Categories: None
Yield: 4 Servings
3 md Zucchini, Grated Coarsely
1/3 c  Onion, Minced
1 c  Sharp Cheddar, Grated
1 c  Soft Bread Crumbs
2    Eggs, Beaten
3 tb Butter, Melted
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
MMMMM------------------------HERB MIXTURE-----------------------------
1/4 c  Celery Leaves, Chopped Fine
1 ts Mixed Dry Herbs: Sage,
-Marjoram, Thyme, etc.
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Drain the zucchini. Combine the
zucchini, onion, cheese, bread crumbs, eggs, butter, salt and pepper.
Combine the ingredients for the herb mixture. Re chop or rub the
leaves together to freshen the flavor. Stir into the zucchini
mixture. Spoon the mixture into a buttered 9" pie plate. Bake until
golden (40-45 minutes). Let cool for
10    minutes before serving.
