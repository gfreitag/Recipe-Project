Title: ZIPPY TOMATO APPETIZER
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 9 Servings
10 1/2 oz Can condensed beef broth
1/2 ts Dried marjoram -- crushed
46 oz Can vegetable juice
Cocktail
1 tb Lemon juice
In slow-cooking pot, combine broth, marjoram, vegetable juice
cocktail, and lemon juice.  Cover and heat on low for 2 to 3 hours.
Serve hot from slow-cooking pot.
