Title: VEPROVY GULASH (PORK GOULASH)
Categories: Pork
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Fresh pork shoulder, cubed
1    Medium onion, chopped
1/4 c  Lard
1/4 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Caraway seeds
Salt
2 c  Water
2 tb Flour
Fry onion in lard.  Add paprika, meat, caraway seeds, and salt; brown
well; add 1/2 cup water; simmer in a covered pan until meat is tender
(about 1 hour). Dust drippings with flour; stir until brown. Add
remaining water, and simmer for 10 to 20 minutes. Serves 4.
SOURCE: THE CZECHOSLOVAK COOKBOOK
