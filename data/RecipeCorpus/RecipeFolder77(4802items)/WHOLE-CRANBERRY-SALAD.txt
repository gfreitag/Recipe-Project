Title: WHOLE CRANBERRY SALAD
Categories: Salads, Nov96
Yield: 10 Servings
2 sm Cranberry Jello
2 c  Boiling water
1 cn Whole cranberry sauce
20 oz Crushed pineapple in juice
6 oz Frozen orange juice, thawed,
-undiluted
Nuts, if desired
Mix together and chill.  Good with turkey.
