Title: TOFU SOUP
Categories: Soups, Thai, Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
5 c  Vegetable broth
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 lb Tofu
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ea Scallions, chopped
2 ea Cilantro sprigs, chopped
3 tb Soy sauce, or more to taste
Salt, if necessary
In a medium sized soup pot, bring the broth to a simmer. In a
skillet, heat the oil.  While it is heating, slice the tofu into
strips. When the oil is hot, fry the tofu.  Add the pepper, scallions
& cilantro stems. Let cool slightly.  Drop the tofu mixture into the
broth. Stir in the soy sauce & simmer for 3 or 4 minutes & serve.
