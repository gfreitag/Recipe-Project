Title: YOGURT CHEESE
Categories: Desserts, Spreads, Toppings
Yield: 1 Servings
16 oz Low-fat plain yogurt
Line colander with large paper coffee filter or double-layered
cheesecloth. Place over large bowl. Pour yogurt into sieve. Cover
with plastic wrap. Refrigerate 8 hours or overnight; drain liquid
from bowl. Transfer cheese to clean bowl. Refrigerate, covered, up to
1 week.
