Title: WAFFLES (MORGAN)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Eggs
3 c  Milk; sour
3 3/4 c  Flour
1 tb Sugar
4 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
2 tb Butter; melted
Note: No directions given. Combine ingredients, bake on waffle iron
until crisp and brown.

Source: Mrs. F. A. Morgan, North Lima Grange, Mahoning County, OH
