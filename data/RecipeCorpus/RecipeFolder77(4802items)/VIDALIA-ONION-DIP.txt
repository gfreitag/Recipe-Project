Title: VIDALIA ONION DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
3 lg Chopped Vidalia Sweet Onions
2 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/3 c  To 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
1 ts Celery Seed
Soak onions overnight in sugar, water and vinegar. Drain well. Add
mayonnaise and celery seed to spreading consistency. Serve on
crackers.
