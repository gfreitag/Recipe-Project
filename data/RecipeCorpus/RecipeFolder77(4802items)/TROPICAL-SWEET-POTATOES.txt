Title: TROPICAL SWEET POTATOES
Categories: Creole, Vegetables
Yield: 10 Servings
4 c  Sweet potatoes,cooked
Butter or margarine
1 cn Cream of coconut(15oz)
1/2 c  Flaked coconut(toasted)
1. Arrange sweet potatoes in well-buttered rectangular baking dish.
2. Bring coconut cream to a boil; lower heat and reduce cream to 2/3
original volume.
3. Pour hot coconut cream over prepared sweet potatoes; top with
flaked coconut.
4. Bake in preheated 350'F. oven until bubbly, about
30    minutes.
