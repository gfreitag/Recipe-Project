Title: TUNA SALAD (ZLATKIN)
Categories: Fish, Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
14 oz Tuna, drained and flaked
1/2 c  Chopped green onion
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Chopped pimiento-stuffed
-green olives
1/2 c  Chopped green bell pepper
1/2 ts Salt (optional)
1/8 ts Black pepper
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Lemon juice
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix thoroughly. Makes six
servings.

Melody Zlatkin
