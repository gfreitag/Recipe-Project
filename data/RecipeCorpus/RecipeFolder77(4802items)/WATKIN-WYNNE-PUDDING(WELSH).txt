Title: WATKIN WYNNE PUDDING(WELSH)
Categories: Puddings, Welsh
Yield: 4 Servings
8 oz Breadcrumbs
4 oz Chopped suet
2 oz Sugar
4 oz Currants
1 oz Peel
1    Egg
4 pt Milk
(PWDIN WATCYN WYNNE) Mix the dry ingredients. Beat the egg and add to
the milk.  Bind the pudding together with the liquid. Cover and boil
for 3 hours. Serve with Madeira Sauce
