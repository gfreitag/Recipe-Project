Title: VERY "UNFRENCH" FRENCH TOAST
Categories: Frenchtoast, Breakfast, Brunch
Yield: 6 Servings
6 sl Non-fat sourdough bread
1 c  Egg Beaters
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Vanilla
1 pt Fresh strawberries
1/2 c  Unsweetened natural apple
-juice
Pam spray
SOURCE:  Recipes for Fat Free Living Cookbook by Jyl Steinback,
copyright 1993, ISBN #0-9636876-6-2.  MM format by Ursula R. Taylor.
Combine all ingredients - except bread and Pam in a shallow bowl.
Dip each bread slice in Egg Beaters until coated evenly. Set aside.
Spray pan with Pam and heat on medium heat.  Place 2 slices of
bread in pan at a time and cook until lightly browned.
Meanwhile, wash and hull strawberries.  Blend berries with apple
juice until pureed.  Pour over toast and serve immediately.
NUTRITION per serving:  Calories 82, Protein 5 g, Carbohydrate 17 g,
Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 163 mg, Dietary Fiber 3.7 g.
EXCHANGES per serving:  1/2 bread and 1/2 fruit.
