Title: YORKSHIRE PUDDING WITH ROAST BEEF
Categories: Beef
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs
1    5 to 7 lb. rump roast
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.Cook rump roast in 9 x 9" pan for 25 to
30 minutes or until internal temperature in meat is 150 to 170
degrees. Mix and sift flour and salt.Add milk to make a smooth
paste.Then add eggs,beat 2 minutes in electric mixer. Cover bottom of
9 x 9" pan about 1/4" with drippings from roast. Pour mixture 1/2"
deep on drippings in pan.Bake 20 to 30 minutes. Delicious when served.
