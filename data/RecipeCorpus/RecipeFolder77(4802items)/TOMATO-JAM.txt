Title: TOMATO JAM
Categories: Sauces, Relishes
Yield: 9 Servings
1 lb Tomatoes (green or red)
1 lb Sugar
2 oz Ginger root
1    Cinnamon stick
1    Grated rind(lemon or orange)
Peel, core and slice tomatoes. Cover with sugar and let stand for 12
hours. Strain off the juice and boil it until syrup falls from a
spoon in heavy drops. Add the tomatoes, spices and rind. Cook until
jam thickens then put in hot jars. Process for 10 minutes in hot
water bath or seal with paraffin.
