Title: WONDROUS LOWFAT VANILLA SAUCE FOR DESSERTS
Categories: Sauces, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Boiling water
1 tb Cornstarch
2 tb Diet unsalted margarine
1 ts Pure vanilla flavoring
1 pn Salt
Combine sugar and cornstarch, then gradually stir in the boiling
water, stirring for 5 minutes on low heat. Remove from heat and add
margarine, vanilla and salt. Serve over Angel Food Cake, lowfat
custards and puddings, fat-free pastries, fruit gelatins or fresh
fruit, especially bananas, mangos, and other non-citrus fruits. Makes
about 1 1/2 cups.
