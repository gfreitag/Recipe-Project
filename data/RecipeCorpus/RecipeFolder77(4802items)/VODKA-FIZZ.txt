Title: VODKA FIZZ
Categories: Alcohol, Flora's dri, Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Vodka
4 oz Pineapple juice
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Powdered sugar
Club soda
Comgine ingredients except club soda. Shake. Fill glass with ice
cubes and drink. Top with club soda.

Recipe by: The Complete Bartender - pg. 449
