Title: ZUCCHINI NOODLES
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Zucchini
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Basil leaves
Using vegetable peeler, cut zucchini lengthwise into thin strips. Or
shred zucchini in food processor. Coat a large non-stick skillet with
cooking spray and heat over medium-high heat. Add zucchini, salt and
basil; saute until tender.
