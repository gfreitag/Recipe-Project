Title: YAKIMANDU DIPPING SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Oriental, Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
4 tb Soy sauce
1    Garlic clove; minced
1/2 ts Sugar
1 1/4 ts Rice vinegar
1/4 ts Ground chile pepper
4 ts Minced green onion
1 1/4 ts Sesame seeds; toasted
1 1/4 ts Sesame oil
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and stir until everything is
combined.
