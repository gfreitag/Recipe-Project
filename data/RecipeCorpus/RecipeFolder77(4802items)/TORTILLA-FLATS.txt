Title: TORTILLA FLATS
Categories: Desserts, Tex-mex, Fruits, Quick/easy, Party
Yield: 1 Servings
Judy Garnett- pjxg05a
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1    Wheat Flour tortilla; see *
Veg. oil; (for frying)
1 tb Butter; melted
1    Scoop vanilla ice cream
-Strawberries; sliced
-OR fresh peaches; sliced
1 tb Slivered almonds; toasted
*Please note: Cornmeal tortillas CAN'T be substituted because they
do not take on the texture of pastry when fried.
Combine sugar and cinnamon and reserve.  Heat oil in a skillet and
fry tortilla on both sides until golden brown and puffy. Drain on a
paper towel. Brush tortilla with melted butter, and sprinkle with
cinnamon sugar. Top with ice cream and garnish with fruit and nuts.
Serves 1. Source: Lawry's Calif. Center
