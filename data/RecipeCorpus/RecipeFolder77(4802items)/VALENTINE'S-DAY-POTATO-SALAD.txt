Title: VALENTINE'S DAY POTATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
5    Red Rose potatoes
Boiled and cubed, with skins
5    Eggs, hard boiled and sliced
1    Apple, diced
1 c  Mayonnaise (add as desired)
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
1/4 c  Sweet relish, with juice
1/4 c  Mustard, Spicy
1 tb Dill
Salt & Pepper to taste
Combine all ingredients, folding together gently. Serve chilled.
