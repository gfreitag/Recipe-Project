Title: WORCESTER HASH
Categories: Beef, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Lean ground beef
2 c  Pasta
3 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ea Chili powder to taste
1/2 c  Jack cheese, grated
1 c  Frozen or leftover vegetable
Brown ground beef in skillet; drain.  Meanwhile cook pasta in boiling,
unsalted water until tender; drain. Add Worcestershire sauce to meat
in skillet; stir until mixed. Mix in chili powder to taste. Add pasta
and cheese; stir.  Add vegetables and heat through. Serves 4.
