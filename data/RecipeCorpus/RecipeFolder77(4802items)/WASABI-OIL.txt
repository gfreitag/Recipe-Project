Title: WASABI OIL
Categories:
1/2 c  wasabi powder
Yield: 4 Servings
1 TB mirin
1 ts sugar
Water
:
1/2 c  neutral oil

In a stainless steel bowl, whisk wasabi, mirin and sugar. Add water
until a loose puree is achieved. Whisk in oil. Recipe By
: Chef du Jour DJ 9306
