Title: TOLL HOUSE CHOCOLATE CHIP PIE
Categories: Chocolate, Pies & past
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown Sugar -- firmly
Packed
1 c  Butter -- * see note
6 oz Pk Semisweet Chocolate Chips
-Nestle's)
1 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1    9 Inch Pie Shell --
(unbaked)
* melted and cooled to room temperature

Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  In large bowl, beat eggs until foamy.
Add flour, sugar and brown sugar; beat until well blended. Blend in
melted butter.  Stir in semi-sweet chips and walnuts. Pour into pie
shell. Bake at 325 for 1 hour.  Serve warm with whipped cream or ice
cream. Makes one pie.

If using frozen pie shell, ony use deep dish style and thaw
completely. Place on cookie sheet; bake 10 minutes longer.
