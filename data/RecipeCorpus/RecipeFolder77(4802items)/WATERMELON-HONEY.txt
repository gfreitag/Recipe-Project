Title: WATERMELON HONEY
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
10 lb Chopped melon
2    Lemons
2 qt Cold water
1/4 lb Preserved ginger or ginger
-root
10 lb Sugar
Pare watermelon.  Discard rind and all of the pink portion. Grind
white portion through food chopper, using the coarse knife. Use the
following proportions:

Combine melon and water.  Cook slowly until melon is clear. Add sugar,
sliced lemon, and ginger, cut in small pieces. Cook slowly until
thick. Mrs. Mary V. Jones, Scottsville, VA.
