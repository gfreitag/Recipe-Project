Title: TV COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
Cream together...
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown sugar
Add...
2    Eggs, beaten
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Sifted flour
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
Add...
2 c  Quick oatmeal
2 c  Rice Krispies
1 c  Coconut
1/2 c  Nuts
Blend and drop by spoonfuls on cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees.
Randy Rigg
