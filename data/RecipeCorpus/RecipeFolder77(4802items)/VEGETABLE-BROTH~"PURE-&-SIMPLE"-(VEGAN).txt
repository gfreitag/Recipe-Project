Title: VEGETABLE BROTH~"PURE & SIMPLE" (VEGAN)
Categories: Vegetarian, Soups, Fat-free, Prodigy, Dec.
Yield: 3 Quarts
2 c  Tomato juice; or V-8
2 1/2 c  -Water
2/3 c  Soy sauce
2    Garlic cloves; or more mince
2 lg Onions; chop; or flakes
1 tb Basil
Use for cooking seitan - or whatever. It can be reduced by boiling and
thickened with flour and water, or arrowroot, and used as a gravy.
