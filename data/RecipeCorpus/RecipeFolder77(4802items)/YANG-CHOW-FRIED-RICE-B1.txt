Title: YANG CHOW FRIED RICE B1
Categories: Grains, Chinese, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Green onions, chopped
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 c  Green peas, uncooked
1/4 c  Cooked shrimp, diced
3 c  Cold cooked rice
1/4 c  Barbecued pork or ham,
-diced
1/2 ts Light soy sauce
1 tb Dark soy sauce
Swirl vegetable oil around bottom and sides of a heated wok. Add
onions & eggs, quick-fry, then add peas and shrimp and blend
together. Immediately add rice & press gently to sides & bottom to
separate kernels. Add pork or ham, salt, & soy sauces. Mix together
quickly & serve.

Temperature(s): HOT Effort: AVERAGE Source: MADAME WU'S GARDEN
Comments: WILSHIRE BLVD, LOS ANGELES Comments: WINE: WHITE DRY
