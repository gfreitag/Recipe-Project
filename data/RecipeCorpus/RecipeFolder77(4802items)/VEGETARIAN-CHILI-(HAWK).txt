Title: VEGETARIAN CHILI (HAWK)
Categories: Soups, Vegetarian
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 md Onion, diced
1 cn Refried beans (watch out
-for lard)
1 tb Chili powder
1/2 ts Garlic powder
&1/2 cup water
1 cn Tomato sauce
1 ts Sugar
Bring water to boil.  Add onion and cook on med. boil till fairly
transparent.  Reduce heat to low.  Add remaining ingredients. Stir
till beans are smooth and heated, a minute or two. I ate it with a
dollop of sour cream and some raw diced onion but it is good without
any garnish at all. I hope you like it, Connie.
