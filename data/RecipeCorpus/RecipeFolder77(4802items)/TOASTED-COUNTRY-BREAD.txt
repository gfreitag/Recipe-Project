Title: TOASTED COUNTRY BREAD
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Thick Slices  crusty country
Bread
1 tb Extravirgin olive oil
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Brush bread slices lightly with olive
oil on both sides. Place on rack placed on a cookie sheet and place
in oven. Toast for 10-15 minutes, or until just beginning to brown.
Spoon tomato salad on top and serve. Copyright, 1996, TV FOOD
