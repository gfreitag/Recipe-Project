Title: VEGETABLE SOUP FOR 50
Categories: Soups
Yield: 50 Servings
2 ga Soup stock
3    Bay leaves
3 c  Carrots, diced
3 c  Celery, diced
2 c  Onions, chopped
1/2 c  Rice
2 qt Tomatoes
1 cn Peas, no. 2 size
2 tb Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Heat the stock with the bay leaves; boil for 5 minutes. Remove the bay
leaves.  Add vegetables and seasoning to stock and simmer gently until
vegetables are tender and the flavors are blended. Other vegetables
may be added if desires.  Add rice and cook until tender. Randy Rigg
