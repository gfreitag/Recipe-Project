Title: TROPICAL COMPOTE WITH HONEY LIME DRESSING
Categories: Fruits, Salads
2    avocados  -- seed, peel,
cube
Yield: 1 Servings
:
2    kiwi fruit  -- peel, slice
1    banana  -- peel, slice
1    papaya  -- peel, slice
1/2 c  coconut flakes  --
sweetened
Honey Lime Dressing-----
:
:
3/4 c  plain yogurt
2 TB lime juice
1/4 c  honey
1/4 ts lime peel  -- grated

Prepare Honey Lime Dressing by mixing all those ingredients together.
Mix fruit together in serving bowl; pour Honey Lime Dressing over
top. Sprinkle with coconut and serve.
