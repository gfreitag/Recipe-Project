Title: WHOLE WHEAT BISCUITS
Categories: Breads, Biscuits, Breakfast
Yield: 16 Biscuits
1 c  Whole wheat
7/8 c  All-purpose flour
1 tb Baking powder
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Shortening; (to 1/3 cup)
3/4 c  Water (or milk)
Combine the dry ingredients and mix well.  Cut in the shortening and
add liquid to make a soft dough.  Place the dough on a lighlty
floured board, and pat out or roll until 1/2 inch or 3/4 inch thick;
or knead gently, and roll to the desired thickness.  Cut into
biscuits. Bake at 450 degrees for about 15 minutes.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in the 1970's. Typed for
you by Nancy Coleman.
