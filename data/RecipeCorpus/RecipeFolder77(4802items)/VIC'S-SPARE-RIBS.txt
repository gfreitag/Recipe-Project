Title: VIC'S SPARE RIBS
Categories: Pickell, Main dish, Aggregation, Bar-b-q
Yield: 2 Pounds
2 lb Spare ribs
2 cn Choy sweet and sour sauce
2 ts Chili powder
2 ts Curry powder
2 tb Vinegar
Salt & pepper
Cook the Spare Ribs slowly.  When the ribs are done add the other
ingredients and simmer for 45 minutes.
