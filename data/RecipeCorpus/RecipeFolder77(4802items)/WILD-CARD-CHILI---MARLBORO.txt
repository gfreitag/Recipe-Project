Title: WILD CARD CHILI - MARLBORO
Categories: Chili, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Chopped beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 cn Red beans - 16 oz.
1 cn Refried beans - 16 oz.
1 cn Tomato sauce - 8 oz.
1 c  Water
1 ts Chopped hot red peppers
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
3 tb Chili powder
1 tb Molasses
Brown beef with onions in a Dutch oven; pour off fat. Add remaining
ingredients; cover and simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
Makes 6 servings.
