Title: YAM VIETNAM (SPICY SHRIMP & PORK SALAD)
Categories: Salads, Vietnam
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 ts Coriander seeds; ground
3    Garlic cloves; minced
1 ts Fresh ground black pepper
3 tb Vinegar
3 tb Lime or lemon juice
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Dried ground chili peppers
2 c  Cooked pork; cubed small
1 lb Cooked shrimp; diced small
1    Cucumber; peeled and sliced
1 ts Mint
Combine in a bowl the coriander, garlic, pepper, vinegar, lime juice,
salt, sugar and chili peppers. Add the pork, shrimp and cucumber.
Toss lightly. Sprinkle with the mint.  From AOL, June 1993.
