Title: TRAFFIC LIGHT2
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 oz Green Creme de Menthe
1/3 oz Creme de Banana
1/3 oz Sloe Gin
1. Pour green creme de menthe into a liqueur glass or a brandy
snifter. 2. Float creme de banana. 3. Float sloe gin.

Recipe by: The Complete Bartender
