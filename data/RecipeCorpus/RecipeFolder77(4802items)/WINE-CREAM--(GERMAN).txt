Title: WINE CREAM--(GERMAN)
Categories: Desserts, German
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Dry white wine
1/2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
1 ts Grated lemon rind
1 ts Grated orange rind
Wine Cream--(German)

Combine all of the ingredients in top of a double boiler. Cook,
beating with a whisk or fork until frothy and thickened. Pour into
serving dishes and chill. Serve with whipped cream if you like.
