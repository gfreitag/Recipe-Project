Title: VEGETABLE STEW WITH NUT DUMPLINGS
Categories: Vegetarian, Soups/stews
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 lb Potatoes
1 ea Parsley sprig
1/4 lb Carrots
1 ea Celery leaf
1/4 lb Parsnips
2 oz Onions
1/4 lb Turnips
2 tb Oil
1/2 tb All purpose flour
---------DUMPLINGS----------
2 tb Oil
4 tb Wholewheat flour
1/4 c  Wholewheat breadcrumbs
1/4 c  Finely chopped walnuts
Cut peeled & washed vegetables into small cubes. Combine oil & 1 1/2
cups of vegetables.  Cook till ight brown. Add flour & mix well. Add
a pint of water & the rest of the vegetables.  Cook slowly for about
90 minutes. Season to taste & serve with the dumplings.

DUMPLINGS: Work oil into flour, crumbs & nuts.  Add enough water to
make a stiff dough.  Roll & cut into walnut sized pieces. Cook for 25
minutes in rapidly boiling water.
