Title: TOMATO-ROSEMARY SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
3    Tomatoes
1    Red onion
2 ts Fine chopped fresh rosemary
1/4 c  Chopped Italian parsley
Salt
Virgin olive oil
Balsamic vinegar
Cut tomatoes into 1/2-inch dice.  Chop red onion into 1/4-inch
pieces. Combine tomatoes, onion, rosemary, parsley and salt to taste.
Toss with vinaigrette dressing composed of 3 parts olive oil and 1
part balsamic vinegar.  Makes 4 servings.
Each serving contains about: 119 calories; 84 mg sodium; 0
cholesterol; 10 grams fat; 7 grams carbohydrates; 1 gram protein;
0.79 gram fiber.
