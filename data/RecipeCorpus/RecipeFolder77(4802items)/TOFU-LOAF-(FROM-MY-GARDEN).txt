Title: TOFU LOAF (FROM MY GARDEN)
Categories: Wasnone
1 lb tofu
Yield: 1 Servings
5    carrots -- grated (5 to 6)
1    onion -- chopped
1/4 c  bread crumbs
4 oz cream cheese
Salt
Pepper
:
:
1 c  chopped parsley

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In large bowl combine all ingredients,
except 1/2 cup of parsley, and mix well. Pour into a greased loaf
pan. Sprinkle remaining parsley on top. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes,
until firm ..
