Title: WHEAT BERRY TOMATO SALAD (VEGAN)
Categories: Digest, Dec.
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Cooked wheat berries
(cooled)
2 c  Chopped tomatoes
1/2 c  Chopped red onion
Toss above together, serve with your favorite dressing
