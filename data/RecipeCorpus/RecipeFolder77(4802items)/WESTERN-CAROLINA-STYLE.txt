Title: WESTERN CAROLINA STYLE
Categories: Barbeque, Sauces and
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Ketchup
1 c  Brown sugar -- firm packed
1/2 c  Lemon juice
3 tb Butter
1/4 c  Onion -- minced
1 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Place all ingredients in saucepan. Bring to boil and simmer 30 min.,or
until slightly thickened. Makes 1
1/2    cups.
