Title: WHEAT GERM SQUARES
Categories: Cookies, Snacks, Desserts
Yield: 16 Servings
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Molasses
3/4 c  Honey
2 ea Eggs
2 tb Vanilla
1 c  Wheat germ
1/2 c  Powdered dry milk
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ea Pinch salt
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Melt butter, molasses and honey together.  Remove from heat. Add eggs
and vanilla.  Stir in the remaining ingredients until just moistened.
Spread mix in oiled 8-inch square pan, and bake at 350 degrees, 25-30
minutes. Cool and cut into 2-inch squares.  Makes 16
