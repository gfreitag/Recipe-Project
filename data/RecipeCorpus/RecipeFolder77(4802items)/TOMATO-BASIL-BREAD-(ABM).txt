Title: TOMATO BASIL BREAD (ABM)
Categories: Digest, Dec.
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/4 c  Tomato juice
1 1/3 tb Honey
1 3/4 ts Basil (I assume dry is meant
Here)
1 2/3 ts Salt (omit if using
Commercial tomato juice)
1 1/2    To 3 T gluten
3 1/3 c  Whole wheat flour
2 ts Yeast
Mix/add per directions on your bread machine, timer: medium

per 1 oz slice: 68 cal 0.3 g fat 3.1 g protein 14.1 g carb 63.4 mg
sodium

Let me know how it turns out!  I don't have time to bake it just now.
