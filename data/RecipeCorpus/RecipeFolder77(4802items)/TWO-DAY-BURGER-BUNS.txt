Title: TWO DAY BURGER BUNS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 36 Servings
2 c  Sugar
2 pk (2 tbsp) yeast
1 c  Oil
4 c  Water
2 ts Salt
13 c  Flour
1    P.m. - Mix, knead and set to
-raise.
6    P.m. - Punch down.
9    P.m. - Knead and then shape
-1/2 cup-size balls of dough
-and
arrange on cookie sheet-size pieces of foil. Set to raise in warm
place overnight. In the morning, begin baking them - 15 minutes at
400 F.

Makes 36 - 40 buns.
