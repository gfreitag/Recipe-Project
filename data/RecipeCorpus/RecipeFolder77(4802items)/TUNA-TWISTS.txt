Title: TUNA TWISTS
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
5 tb Olive Oil
8 oz Tricolour Pasta Twists
2 oz Butter
1    Garlic Clove, peeled and
-crushed
4 oz Button Mushrooms, Wiped
14 oz Tuna Chunks in Oil,
-Drained and Flaked
Salt and Pepper
2 tb Snipped Fresh Chives
Bring a large pan of salted water to the boil and add a tablespoon of
olive oil to prevent pasta from sticking. Add pasta twists, bring
back to the boil and simmer for about 10-12 min or al dente. Drain.

Meanwhile, heat the remaining oil and butter in a saucepan and add the
garlic and mushrooms. Cook for 2-3 minutes, add tuna and heat
thoroughly, season to taste.

Mix the pasta and the sauce together. Serve garnished with the chives
