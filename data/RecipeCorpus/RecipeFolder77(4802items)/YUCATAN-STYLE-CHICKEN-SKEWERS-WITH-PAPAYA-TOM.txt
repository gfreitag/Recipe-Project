Title: YUCATAN STYLE CHICKEN SKEWERS WITH PAPAYA TOM
Categories: New, Text, Import
1 c  Yucatan Marinade
Yield: 4 Servings
9    skinless and boneless
chicken thighs
:
1 md jicama, -- julienned
36    6inch skewers, -- soaked
2 c  Papaya Tomatillo Salsa

Rub the marinade into the chicken thighs. Cover the chicken and
refrigerate for 4 to 6 hours or overnight. Prepare a wood or charcoal
fire and let it burn down to embers.

Thread each piece of chicken onto 2 skewers so that the meat stays
flat on the grill. Grill for about 4 minutes on each side or until
done to taste. Serve with PapayaTomatillo Salsa.
