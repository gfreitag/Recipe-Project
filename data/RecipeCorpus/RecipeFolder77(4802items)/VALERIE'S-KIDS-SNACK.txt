Title: VALERIE'S KIDS SNACK
Categories: Kids, Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Honey
1 c  Wheat germ
Shredded coconut or:
Chopped nuts
1. In bowl, blend peanut butter, honey and wheat germ. Roll into small
balls

2. Roll balls in coconut or nuts.  Refrigerate.

Eat when chilled and hardened.
