Title: VANILLA PUDDING
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Milk
6 tb Cornstarch
6 tb Sugar
Dash of salt
6 tb Cold milk
1 ts Vanilla
Place the 3 c milk into a saucepan and over medium heat until hot.
Mix the sugar, cornstarch and salt to-gether and add the 6 T milk and
blend well, stir this into the hot milk slowly and cook till thick.
Cool slightly and add the vanilla.
