Title: TORTILLAS DE HARINA DEL NORTE (WHEAT TORTILLA
Categories: Breads, Mexican
Yield: 12 Tortillas
2 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1 tb Lard
3/4 c  Water; cold
Thoroughly mix dry ingredients. Cut in lard and enough water to
make a stiff dough. Divide into balls about the size of small eggs
and roll out on a lightly floured board, making them as thin as
possible. Bake about 2 minutes on each side on an ungreased comal or
griddle.
