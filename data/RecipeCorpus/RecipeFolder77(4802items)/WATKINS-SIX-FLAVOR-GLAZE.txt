Title: WATKINS SIX-FLAVOR GLAZE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 1 Cake
1/2 c  White sugar
1/4 c  Water
1/2 ts WATKINS Coconut Flavor
1/2 ts WATKINS Rum Extract
1/2 ts WATKINS Butter Flavor
1/2 ts WATKINS Lemon Extract
1/2 ts WATKINS Vanilla Extract
1/2 ts WATKINS Almond Extract
Combine all ingredients in heavy saucepan.  Bring to a boil over
medium heat, stirring until sugar is dissolved.
