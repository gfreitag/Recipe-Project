Title: ULTIMATE CHOCOLATE CHUNK COOKIES......REALLY!
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 40 Cookies
2 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Baking soda
1    Egg
1 c  Softened butter
12 oz Semi sweet chocolate
1/2 c  Gran. sugar chunks
3/4 c  Packed brown sugar
Preheat oven to 375. Mix dry ingredients in small bowl (except
chips). In large bowl mix moist ingredients, then add dry
ingredients. When blended add chips. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto
UNgreased cookie sheets 2 inches apart. Bake 8 to 10 minutes. Makes36
to 48 really good cookies.
