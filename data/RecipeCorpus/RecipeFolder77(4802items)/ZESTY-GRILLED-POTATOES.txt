Title: ZESTY GRILLED POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Grill, Cookbook
Yield: 4 Servings
4 md Potatoes; unpared
1/2 c  Italian dressing
Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
~Scrub Potatoes till clean.

~In boiling water, cook potatoes in skins till just tender.

~While still hot carefully slice diagonally into 1/2" slices. Place in
shallow glass dish and pour dressing over hot slices. Turn to coat
thouroughly & let stand 1 hour.

~Remove from dressing and place on grill 3" from hot coals. Grill 8-10
minutes on each side till golden brown.

~Season after turning.
