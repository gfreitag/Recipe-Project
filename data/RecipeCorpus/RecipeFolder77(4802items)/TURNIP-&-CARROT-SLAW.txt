Title: TURNIP & CARROT SLAW
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Vegan
Yield: 2 Servings
100 g  Raw turnip; grated
100 g  Carrots; grated
4 tb Vinaigrette
2 tb Snipped dill
Soft-leaved lettuce
Salt
Freshly ground pepper
Serves 2-3

In a bowl combine the grated turnips and carrots and dress with the
vinaigrette. Toss well, then add the dill and season to taste with
salt and pepper. Arrange on salad plates lined with the lettuce.
