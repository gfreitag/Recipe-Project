Title: WARREN'S BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Catsup
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
3 ds Tabasco or other bottled hot
Pepper sauce
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Celery seed
Combine all ingredients.  Heat to boiling.  Let simmer 30 minutes.
Makes enough sauce for basting 4 pounds loin back ribs.

Source:  BH & G Barbecue Book, 1965
