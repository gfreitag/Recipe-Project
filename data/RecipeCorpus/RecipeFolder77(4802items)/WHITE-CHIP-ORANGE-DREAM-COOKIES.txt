Title: WHITE CHIP ORANGE DREAM COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/4 c  Flour
3/4 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
2    Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg
3 ts Orange peel; grated
2 c  White chocolate chips
Beat butter, sugar, and brown sugar in large bowl until creamy. Beat
in egg and orange peel.  Gradually beat in flour, baking soda and
salt. Stir in morsels. Drop by teaspoons onto greased baking sheets.
Bake at 350

for 10-12 minutes.
