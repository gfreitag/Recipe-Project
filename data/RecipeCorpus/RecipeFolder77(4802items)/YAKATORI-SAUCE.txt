Title: YAKATORI SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Ground ginger
1 1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 1/2 ts Corn starch
Mix these ingredients in small saucepan. Heat until boiling. Let
stand, until thickens a little bit.  Pour sauce over boneless chicken
breasts. Marinate on chicken 30 minutes before cooking, and continue
basking the chicken until it is done!  That is it! Let me know how it
turns out for you!  Have a great day!!!
