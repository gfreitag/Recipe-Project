Title: ZUCHINNI-LEMON BARS
Categories: Cookies, Mimi
Yield: 24 Servings
EVA ESTES BXGT29B
1 1/4 c  SUGAR
1 c  OIL
3    EGGS
2 ts LEMON RIND; GRATED
1/4 c  LEMON JUICE
1 3/4 c  ZUCCHINI; SHREDDED
2 c  FLOUR
1/4 ts BAKING POWDER
2 ts SODA
1 ts SALT
1 c  WALNUTS; CHOPPED
POWDERED SUGAR; SIFTED
Combine 1 1/4cups sugar, oil, eggs, lemon rind, and lemon jice in a
large mixing bowl; mix well. Stir in zucchini.
Combine flour, baking powder, soda, and salt; gradually add to
zucchini mixture, stirring well. Stir in walnuts. Pour batter into a
greased 13x9x2-inch baking pan. Bake at 350d for 45-50 minutes or
until a wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool; dust
top with sifted powdered sugar.  Cut into bars. Yield: 2 dozen
