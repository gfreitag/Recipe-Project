Title: TOMATO SOUP CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
Cake:
1 cn Tomato soup
1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Dates
1 c  Nuts
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Soda
1 tb Shortening
Frosting:
1 c  Philadelphia Cream Cheese
1 c  Powdered sugar
1 tb Butter
1 ts Vanilla and Nuts
Mix all cake ingredients together and bake for 35 minutes at 350
degrees. After cake has cooled, mix all frosting ingredients together
and spread over cake.
