Title: ZUCCHINI CAKE *XWCG89A
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 10 Servings
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cloves
3    Eggs
1/2 c  Oil
1 1/3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 ts Almond extract
1 1/2 c  Shredded zucchini
Orange icing
In large bowl mix flour, baking powder, soda, salt, cinnamon and
cloves. Set aside. With wisk, beat eggs. Stir in oil, sugar, juice,
extract and zucchini. Mix well. Add in flour mixture, stirring just
to moisten. Pour into greased pan or pans. Bake in preheated 350F
oven.

Tube cake = 55-60 minutes 1 - 15x9x2 pan = 35-40 minutes 4 loaf pans,
6x3 foil loaf pans = 40 minutes
24    cupcakes = 20-25 minutes

ORANGE ICING: In small bowl beat together until blended: 2 T softened
butter, 3 cups confectioner sugar, 1/4 c OJ, and 1 tsp lemon juice.
