Title: UNBELIEVABLE PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 2 Dozen
1 c  Peanut butter
-smooth or chunky-
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
1 ts Vanila
Mix together with fork.  Roll into balls. Put on ungreased cookie
sheet and press with fork. Bake at 350 degrees for about 10 minutes.
Remove from sheet while still warm.
