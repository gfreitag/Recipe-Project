Title: TORTILLA CHEESE WRAP-UPS
Categories: Appetizers, Tex-mex, Party, Guests, Posted/mm
Yield: 2 Dozen
MMMMM----------------MM FORMAT BY MARY ANN YOUNG---------------------
1/4 c  Philadelphia soft cream
-cheese
1 tb Chopped cilantro
1/2 ts Mexican seasoning
4    Flour tortillas (6-inch)
8    Kraft American cheese
-singles
Salsa
Mix cream cheese, cilantro and seasoning until well-blended.

Spread 1 tb cream cheese mixture over each tortilla. Place 2 process
cheese food slices on each tortilla. Tightly roll up.

Wrap each tortilla securely in plastic wrap and refrigerate. Cut each
tortilla in sixths. Secure ends with wooden toothpicks.

Serve with salsa in a dipping bowl.

Prep time: 15 minutes plus refrigeration time.
