Title: TROUT AMANDINE
Categories: Fish
Yield: 2 Servings
2    Dressed freshwater trout
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 tb Clarified butter; divided
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Slivered almonds
Lemon slices
Chopped fresh parsley
Rinse fish thoroughly in cold water; pat dry, and set aside. Combine
flour, salt, and pepper in a small bowl; dredge fish in flour. Fry
fish in 2 tbs clarified butter in a large skillet 5 minutes on each
side or until golden brown.  Transfer to a warm serving dish;
sprinkle with lemon juice.

Add remaining clarified butter to skillet.  Add almonds, and saute
until golden brown.  Spoon almonds over fish. Garnish with lemon and
parsley.
