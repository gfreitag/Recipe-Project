Title: VIDALIA ONION DILL CHEESE BREAD
Categories: Breads, Cheese
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Tap Water
1    Egg
3 c  Bread Flour
2 1/2 c  Grated Cheddar Cheese
1 tb Heaping Dill Weed (dry
-fresh)
1 tb Dill Seed
1 1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Sugar
1 tb Butter
1 1/2 ts Yeast (Red Star)
1 md Vidalia Onion (chopped fine)
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Put all ingredients in bread machine except onion. Select MIX BREAD
press Start. When beeping sounds during first kneading, add onion.

Machine: Hitachi Home Bakery Plus Setting: Mix Bread Crust Setting:
Light

SOURCE: Jim and Gwynne Bodle 6/92
