Title: VEGETABLE PIZZA
Categories: Pizza, Appetizers
Yield: 24 Appetizers
1 cn Refrigerated crescent
-dinner rolls (8 oz)
1 pk PHILADELPHIA BRAND Cream
-Cheese, softened (8 oz)
1/2 c  MIRACLE WHIP or MIRACLE WHIP
-LIGHT Dressing
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
3/4 c  Chopped red pepper
3/4 c  Chopped radishes
1/2 c  Sliced pitted ripe olives
2 tb Sliced green onions
1/2 c  KRAFT Natural Shredded
-Sharp Cheddar Cheese (2 oz)
Heat oven to 375'F. Unroll dough into 2 rectangles. Press onto bottom
and 1/4" up sides of 13x9" baking pan to form crust. Bake 10 minutes;
cool. Mix cream cheese, dressing and seasoning until well blended.
Spread over crust. Top with remaining ingredients. Refrigerate. Cut
into squares.

PREP TIME: 20 minutes plus refrigerating.
