Title: WHIPPED AVACADO DESSERT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 Servings
2    Avacados, peeled and cut
-into pieces
1/3 c  Powdered sugar
1/4 c  Whipping (heavy) cream
3 tb Lime juice
Whipped Avacado Dessert

This unusual dessert is popular in Brazil.

Place all ingredients in a blender or food processor. Cover and
process on high speed until smooth and creamy. Makes 3 servings.
(about 340 calories per serving)

Recipe from Betty Crocker's "30 Minutes or Less" Cookbook
