Title: WALNUT MISO CONDIMENTS
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 Cup
1/2 c  Roasted walnuts
2 tb EDEN Genmai or Shiro Miso
1/4 c  -water; or up to...
1/2 c  Water
Roast walnuts in a heavy skillet over medium heat. Blend with
remaining ingredients into a smooth paste in a blender or food
processor. Delicious served with grains and vegetables, or as a
spread.
