Title: TOMATO~ PICKLED ONION. & CILANTRO SANDWICHE
Categories: New text im
Yield: 1 Servings
8 sl Whole-grain bread,; toasted
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 lg Tomatoes,; sliced
1/2 c  Pickled onions
1 c  Fresh cilantro sprigs
Recipe by: Gourmet-August 1994 Spread bread with mayonnaise and make 4
sandwiches with tomato slices, pickled onions, and cilantro sprigs,
pressing together gently. Makes 4 sandwiches.
