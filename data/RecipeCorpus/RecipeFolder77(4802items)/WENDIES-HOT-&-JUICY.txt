Title: WENDIES HOT & JUICY
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Batch
10 oz Beef broth
3 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Vinegar
1 1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1 tb Worchestershire sauce
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb A-1 sauce
1 tb Corn oil
Marinate hamburgers for 4 hours.  Turn every 1/2 hour. Seems to lock
in juiceness and flavor enhanced.
