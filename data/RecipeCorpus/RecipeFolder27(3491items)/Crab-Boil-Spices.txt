Title: Crab-Boil Spices
Categories: Herbs/spice, Seafood
Yield: 1 cup
2 ts Ground ginger
5    Bay leaves
2 ts Dried oregano
1 tb Minced dried chives
1/4 c  Sea salt
1/4 c  Pickling spices
2 tb Mustard seeds
2 tb Black peppercorns
1 tb Celery seeds
2 tb Dried hot red pepper flakes
In food processor combine all ingredients and pulse to a fine powder.
Store in an air-tight container.
