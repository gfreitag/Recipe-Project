Title: Horseradish Dip
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
4 ts Prepared horseradish
2 pk Sweet 'N Low
1 c  Yogurt Cheese
1/4 c  Onion(s), finely chopped
3 ts Prepared horseradish to
In a small bowl, combine all ingredients; stir until well blended.
Stir in a few tbs chicken broth or low-fat milk to thin for sauces or
salad dressings.
