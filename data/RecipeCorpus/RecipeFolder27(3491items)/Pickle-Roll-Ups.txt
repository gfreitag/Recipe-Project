Title: Pickle Roll Ups
Categories: Appetizer, Pickles
Yield: 1 servings
3 pk Smoked meat, thin sliced
1 ea Jars Pickles; dill
1 pk Cream cheese
Approx. Cook Time: none Roll
Fat grams    per serving:
pickle completely in cream cheese between hands. Wrap in 1 layer of
meat. Chill for at least 1 hour. Then slice rolls into wheels, the
pickle will be surrounded by the cream cheese and then the meat.
from Neil's mom, originally from Florida
