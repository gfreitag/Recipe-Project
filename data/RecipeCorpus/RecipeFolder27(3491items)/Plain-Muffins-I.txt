Title: Plain Muffins I
Categories: Muffin
Yield: 6 servings
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg, well beaten
2 c  Flour
4 tb Sugar
3 tb Melted shortening
1 c  Milk
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder and salt. Combine
egg, shortening, milk, and sugar.  Add dry ingredients. Beat only
until smooth. Fill well-oiled muffin tins 2/3 full. Bake in hot oven
(425Ã¸ F) 15-20 minutes.  10 servings. The Household Searchlight - 1941
