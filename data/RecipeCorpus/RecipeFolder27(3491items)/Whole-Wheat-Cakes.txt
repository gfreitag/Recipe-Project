Title: Whole Wheat Cakes
Categories: Bread, Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
1    Egg, well beaten
1 c  Milk
3 tb Melted shortening
1 1/3 c  Whole wheat flour
3 ts Baking powder
3/4 ts Salt
3 tb Sugar
Combine egg, shortening, and milk.  Blend sugar, salt, and baking
powder with whole wheat flour.  Beat until smooth.  Bake on hot
griddle. 8 servings. The Household Searchlight - 1941
