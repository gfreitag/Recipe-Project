Title: Green Beans in Sour Cream
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sour Cream;
2 c  French-style green beans;
-(canned or frozen)
1/2 c  Cheese; grated
1/2 c  Onion; minced
2 tb Butter -=OR=-
2 tb Margarine;
2 tb Flour;
1 ts Salt;
Simmer onion in butter.  Add flour, salt and pepper.  Mix well, add
sour cream, and then beans, (if frozen beans are used, cook according
to directions.) Pour in small casserole and top with grated cheese.
Heat in a 350 degrees  oven for 20 to 30 minutes.  Serves 4 Source:
Mrs. Wm B. Moore, Jr. (Lillian), The Stuffed Cougar, Richmond, VA.
