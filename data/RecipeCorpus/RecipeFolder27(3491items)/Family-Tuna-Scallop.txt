Title: Family Tuna Scallop
Categories: Main dish, Fish, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Pepper;
3 1/2 c  Milk;
2 cn (7-oz) tuna; flaked drained
1 c  Cut green beans; -=OR=-
1 pk Frozen green beans;
6 md -size Potatoes; pared sliced
1 lg Onion; chopped (1 cup)
6 tb Or 3/4 stick butter; -=OR=-
6 ts Or 3/4 stick margarine;
6 tb Flour;
1 1/4 ts Salt;
Partially boil potatoes approximately 10 minutes, drain.  Saute onion
in butter or margarine until soft in medium-size saucepan.  Stir in
flour, salt and pepper; cook, stirring constantly until bubbly.  Stir
in milk. Continue cooking and stirring sauce thickens or boils one
minute. Layer 1/3 potatoes, tuna and beans and sauce into 10 cup
baking dish. Repeat ot make 2 more layers of each.  Cover.  Bake 45
minutes or until bubbly hot at 350 degrees. (want to try Spam)
