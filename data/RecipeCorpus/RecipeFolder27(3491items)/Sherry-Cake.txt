Title: Sherry Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Vanilla
2 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Sherry
1/4 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding
3/4 c  Chopped nuts
3/4 c  Cooking oil
1 tb Butter flavoring
Mix all ingredients and beat 10 minutes.  Grease bundt pan.  Bake 1
hour in a 350 degree oven.  Cool for a few minutes.  Turn out.

Glaze: 1 cup powdered sugar 2 tb sugar 3/4 cup of cream 1/4 cup of
melted butter

Mix powdered sugar, vanilla, 3 tb of wine and butter flavoring. Punch
holes in cake with a toothpick.  Pour glaze over warm cake slowly, so
it can soak in. Randy Rigg
