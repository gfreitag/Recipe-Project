Title: Shrimp Louis Dip - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizer, Pickles
Yield: 12 servings
1 ea Tb prepared horseradish
1/4 t  Lite salt, optional
1/8 t  Ground pepper
2 c  Finely chopped cooked shrimp
1 c  Kraft fatfree mayonnaise or
-Miracle Whip
1 c  Fatfree sour cream
1/3 c  Chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Chile sauce
Stir all ingredients until well mixed. Cover and chill. Per 1/4 cup
serving: 76 cal., 0.4g fat (5%), 65mg chol., 0g fiber, 9g pro., 6g
carb., 391mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-446-67040-5
