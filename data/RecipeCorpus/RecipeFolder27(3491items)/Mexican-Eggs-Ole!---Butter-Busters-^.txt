Title: Mexican Eggs Ole! - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Breakfast
Yield: 8 servings
-and drained
1 c  Nonfat Cheddar cheese,
-grated
1 1/2 c  Skim milk
1 c  Egg Beaters
1 ea Taco Sauce or salsa,
-optional
4 ea Corn tortillas, halved
1 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1/2 t  Minced garlic
1 t  Ground oregano leaves
1/2 t  Ground cumin
1 ea Tb fatfree chicken broth
2 c  Canned kidney beans, rinsed
Arrange the tortillas on the bottom of a 12 x 8 x 2" casserole dish
that has been sprayed with a nonfat spray. Set aside. In a nonstick
skillet over medium heat, cook onion, pepper, garlic, oregano, and
cumin in chicken broth until tender. Stir in the kidney beans. Spoon
1.2 of the mixture over the tortillas; repeat layers once. Sprinkle
with the cheese and set aside. In a bowl, combine milk and Egg
Beaters; pour evenly over cheese. Bake at 350F 30-40 minutes or until
puffed and golden brown. Let stand 10 minutes before serving. Serve
with taco sauce or salsa if desired. Per serving: 146 cal., 1g fat
(6%), 4mg chol., 4g fiber, 15g pro., 22g carb., 412mg sod. Butter
Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw
