Title: Chocolate Cream Cheese Raspberry Fudge
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 25 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract
ds Salt
4    1-oz unsweetened chocolate
Squares; melted
1 c  Pecans; chopped
1 pk (8 oz) cream cheese
-- softened
3 c  Powdered sugar
2 tb Chambord or other
Raspberry-flavor liqueur
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:10 1. In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese
and powdered sugar until smooth. Mix in Chambord, vanilla, and salt
until well blended. Stir in melted chocolate until thoroughly mixed.
Mix in nuts. 2. Spread evenly in a buttered 8-inch square baking pan.
Refrigerate several hours, or until firm. Cut into 25 squares. Store,
covered, in refrigerator.
