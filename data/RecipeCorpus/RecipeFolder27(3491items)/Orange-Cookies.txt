Title: Orange Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
6 c  Flour; sifted
Frosting:
1 ea Orange; grind rind and all
1 tb Butter
1 lb Sugar; powdered
1 ea Orange; grind rind and all
1 c  Butter; or other shortening
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk; sweet or sour
1 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
Drop on cookie sheet, bake in 400 F. oven. When entirely cooled, ice
with frosting.

Note: Cream butter or shortening and sugar, combine remaining
ingredients. For frosting, combine ingredients, mix till smooth.

Source: Mrs. Ernest Clapp, Chatham Grange, Medina County, OH
