Title: Almond Crumb Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 2 servings

1 tb Skim milk
1 tb Vegetable oil
2    Lemon wedges
1 oz Blanched almonds; ground
1 tb Bread crumbs; plain, dried
1 1/2 ts Parmesan cheese; grated
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Basil leaves
