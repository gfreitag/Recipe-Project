Title: HEAVENLY TOPPING
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Sugar
1 cn Pet milk
1/2 c  Butter
4 oz Unsweetened chocolate
Melt butter and chocolate in top of double boiler. Stir in sugar
gradually about 4 tbsp. at a time. Add milk slowly. Cook until smooth
and satiny. Heat as need to pour over ice cream, pound cake, etc.
Stores well in refrigerator for a long period of time.
