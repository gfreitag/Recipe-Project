Title: Beef Teriyaki - gas grill
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Sugar
2 ts Dry mustard
4 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 lb Beef tenderloin
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Dry sherry
Partially freeze beef. Thinly slice across the grain into bite-size
strips. Combine soy sauce, wine, sugar and mustard and garlic; add
beef and let stand 15 minutes at room temperature. Thread meat,
accordian style, on small skewers. Pre-heat both sides of gas grill
on HIGH for 10 minutes. Place skewers on grids; close hood and cook
for 5 to 7 minutes or until meat is done, turning and basting often
with marinade. Submitted By GRANT AMES   On   02-03-95
