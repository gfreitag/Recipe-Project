Title: Mackerel In Tomato Sauce
Categories: Fish, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
3 lb Mackerel fillets, cut
-into 4 pieces
1 tb Chopped parsley
1    8-ounce can tomatoes,
Undrained
Parsley sprigs, for
Garnish
2 tb Olive oil
1 sm Onion, sliced
1/2    Clove garlic, peeled and
-chopped
2 tb Water
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1. Place olive oil, onion, garlic, and parsley in large skillet; saute
until onion is browned. 2. Add tomatoes, water, salt, and pepper to
onion mixture; simmer for 5 minutes. Add mackerel, cover, and simmer
for 5 minutes. Turn mackerel over; cook for 10 minutes more. 3.
Transfer fish to serving platter; garnish with parsley sprigs. *From
Creative Cooking Fish and Other Seafood Cookbook*
