Title: Garlic and Dill Tomato Soup
Categories: Vegetable, Low-cal/fat, Tomato, Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
If necessary
1 tb Frozen apple juice
Concentrate
ds Freshly ground pepper
1 tb Fresh dillweed or
1 ts Dried dill
1 1/2 c  Minced onion (1 Spanish)
3 cl Garlic, minced
1 tb Vegetable stock
6 c  Canned tomatoes, chopped
2 c  Liquid from the canned
Tomatoes, or tomato juice
Saute the onion and garlic in the stock until the onion becomes
translucent and slightly brown at the edges. Do this over low heat so
the garlic doesn't burn. Combine with remaining ingredients in a soup
pot and simmer 30 minutes. Serve hot or chilled. Serves 8 Calories:
73 Fat: 1.7 gm 21% fat
