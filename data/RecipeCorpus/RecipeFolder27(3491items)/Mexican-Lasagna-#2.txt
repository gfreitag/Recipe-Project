Title: Mexican Lasagna #2
Categories: Tex/mexican, Casserole
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
1 pk Tortilla chips (10 oz.)
-crushed
1 c  Sour cream
1/2 lb Cheddar cheese, grated
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 1/2 lb Ground chuck
1 c  Cottage cheese
1    Sm. Onion, chopped
1 cn Green chiles (sm. can)
3 cn Tomato sauce (8 oz. ea.)
1 cn Slice black olives (sm. can)
Preheat oven to 350 deg.  Combine sour cream, cottage cheese,
garlic powder and green chiles and set aside. Brown Meat and onions.
Add tomato sauce, olives and salt.  Layer in greased casserole --
tortilla chips, meat, sour cream mixture and cheese.  Bake 20 -30
min. until hot and bubbly.
