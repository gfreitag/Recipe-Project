Title: Parmesan wingflingers
Categories: Appetizer, Chicken, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Oregano
1/2    Bag wingflinger
1/2 lb Butter, melted
1    Can (8 oz) parmesan cheese
preheat oven to 400 dip wingflingers in melted butter then roll in
mixture of parmessan cheese and seasonings. place on greased sheet
pan and bake for 45 mins
