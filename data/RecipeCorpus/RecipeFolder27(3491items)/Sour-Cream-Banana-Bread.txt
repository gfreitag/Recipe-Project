Title: Sour Cream Banana Bread
Categories: Bread, Fruit
Yield: 2 loaves
4 1/2 c  Flour
3 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
2 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Butter
3 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
7    Ripened Bananas
1/2 ts Salt
Cream butter and sugar until smooth.  Mix together with remaining
ingredients until well moistened.  DO NOT OVERMIX.  Pour into two well
greased 9 x 5 x 3 loaf pans.

Bake in 300 degree oven for about one hour or until tested done.
