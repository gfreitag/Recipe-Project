Title: Chocolate Coconut Pecan Pie
Categories: Chocolate, Pie/tart, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Coconut; shredded
1 c  Pecans; chopped
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
1    Pie crust  (9 inch)
(deep-dish); unbaked
Whipped cream
3    Eggs
2/3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Dark corn syrup
4 tb Butter; melted
1 ts Vanilla extract
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 1:30

1. preheat oven to 325Â°F. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, and
sugar until well blended. Add light and dark corn syrups, butter, and
vanilla. Whisk until combined. Stir in coconut, pecans, and chocolate
chips.

2. Turn into pastry crust. Bake 70 to 75 mins, or until center is
almost set. Let cool before serving, topped with whipped cream.
