Title: Cherry Dessert Cake
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 cake
1 c  Butter or margarine, melted
3 oz Slivered almonds
2 cn 21 oz cherry pie filling
3 1/3 c  Basic cake mix
Preheat oven to 350.  Pour cherry pie filling into a 13 x 9 pan.
Sprinkle basic cake mix on top of pie filling. Drizzle melted
margarine over top of mix. Top with slivered almonds. Bake about 45
minutes, until lightly browned.  Cool in pan.
