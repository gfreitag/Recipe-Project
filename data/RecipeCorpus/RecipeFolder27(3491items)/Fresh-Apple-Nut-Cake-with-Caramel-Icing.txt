Title: Fresh Apple Nut Cake with Caramel Icing
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Pecans, finely chopped
Caramel Icing:
2 1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Milk
1    Egg
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2    Stick butter
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Sifted flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Oil
4 c  Apples, finely chopped
(about 6)
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a lg mixing bowl, sift flour, baking
powder, soda, salt, & cinnamon.  Add oil, apples & vanilla.  Beat at
md speed for 2 min. until well blended.  In another bowl, beat eggs
until light yellow in color.  Gradually add sugar & continue beating
until light & fluffy.  Fold egg mixture into the apple mixture,
blending thoroughly. Stir in nuts.  Pour into lightly greased 10"
tube pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hr. & 10 min. or until done.

Caramel Icing:

MIx 2 c sugar, milk & egg & bring to a boil.  Brown 1/2 c sugar in
skillet, then add it to egg mixture while boiling.  Boil until cool,
then add a little butter & vanilla.  Cool & spread over cake. From:
