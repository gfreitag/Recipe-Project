Title: Steak with Livers - gas grill
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Meat, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  White wine, dry
2 lb Beef sirloin steak
-1 to 1 1/2 inch thick
1/2 c  Chicken livers, sliced
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Green onion, chopped
1 c  Mushrooms, fresh, sliced
In skillet cook livers in butter; remove from pan. In same pan cook
onion and mushrooms until almost tender; return livers to pan. Stir
in wine, 1/4 teaspoon of salt and dash of pepper. Pre-heat both sides
of gas grill on HIGH for 10 minutes. Turn both to MEDIUM. Grill steak
with hood closed for 20 to 25 minutes turning once. Serve with livers.
