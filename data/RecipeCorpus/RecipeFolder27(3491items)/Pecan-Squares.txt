Title: Pecan Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Pecan meats
1 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Butter
1 ea Egg
1 c  Flour
Melt butter, blend with sugar, add eggs, mix and let stand for few
minutes, add dry ingredients sifted together. Stir in pecans, spread
in greased pan. Bake and cut in squares.

Note: No temperature or time given. Assume a 325 F. oven. Source:
Mrs. H. G. Stevenson, Lewistown Grange, Logan County, OH
