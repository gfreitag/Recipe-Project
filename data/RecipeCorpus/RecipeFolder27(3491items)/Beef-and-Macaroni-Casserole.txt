Title: Beef and Macaroni Casserole
Categories: Crockpot
Yield: 6 servings
2 ea Stalks celery, chopped
1 cn Tomato paste
3/4 c  Water
2 tb Sherry
1 cn Tomato soup
Grated Cheddar or
Parmesan cheese
1 ts Leaf oregano
1 pk Package
Macaroni,
Cooked and drained
2 tb Oil
1 1/2 lb Ground beef,
-browned and drained
Salt and pepper
1 lg Onion, chopped
Toss cooked noodles in oil. Place in greased CROCK-POT. Add all
remaining ingredients,  except grated cheese.  Stir thoroughly.
Sprinkle cheese over top. Cover and cook on Low 4 to 8 hours. (High:
2 to 3 hours). From Rival Crock-Pot cookbook, date unknown
