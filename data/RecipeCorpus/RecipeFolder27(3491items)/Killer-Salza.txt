Title: Killer Salza
Categories: Relish
Yield: 4 servings
2    Cloves Garlic (3 If Wanted)
1 ts Oregano
1 c  Tomato Juice(Can Use Upto 2)
2 pk Dried Chile Pequins *
1 ts Cumin (Crushed)
1/2 ts Salt (Optional)
*  Or other hot peppers. NOTE: Add juice of one lemon if you are
canning. ~------------------------------------------------------
~------------------ Soak The chile pequins 3 to 4 hours in vinegar to
cover; drain. Throw into the blender with all other ingredients and
blend well.
Let set in the refrigerator to cool off. NOTE:
This sauce is very hot.  Chile Pequins are the hottest of the
Chile Peppers, so watch out.
