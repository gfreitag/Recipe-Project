Title: Pollo Loco Marinade
Categories: Marinade, Chicken, Bbq/grill, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Sugar
2 ts Garlic powder
Tabasco to taste
2 tb Oil
1    Lemon ; juiced
1    Orange; juiced
2 tb White vinegar
2    Worchestershire sauce
1/2 c  Chili sauce
Just marinate chicken in this mixture for 4-24 hours. Grill 20
minutes on each side. Serve with flour tortillas and fresh salsa.
