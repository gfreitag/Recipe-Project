Title: Heavenly WW Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 pound loaf
1 1/8 c  Water
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
4 tb Butter or marg
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Instant potato flakes
1 1/2 ts Yeast
1-pound loaf
3/4 c  Water
1 1/3 c  Whole wheat flour
2/3 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
3 tb Butter or marg.
1 tb Sugar
3 tb Instant pot. flakes
1 1/2 ts Yeast
Place all ingredients in bread pan, select light crust setting.
