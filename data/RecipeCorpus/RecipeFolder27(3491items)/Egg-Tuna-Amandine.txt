Title: Egg-Tuna Amandine
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Sthrn/livng, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Lemon juice; freshly squeezd
7 oz Tuna; drained and flaked
1/4 c  Almonds; chopped
4    Eggs, hard-cooked; diced
1/2 c  Breadcrumbs, buttered
4 tb Butter (or marg.); melted
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 c  Milk
Combine butter and flour in saucepan over medium heat; cook, stirring
constantly, until bubbly.  Add seasonings and milk; cook until smooth
and thick, stirring constantly.  Add lemon juice, tuna, almonds,
pimiento, and eggs.

Spoon into a greased casserole dish; top with buttered breadcrumbs.
Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
