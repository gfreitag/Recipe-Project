Title: Glazed Onions with Raisins
Categories: Condiment, Vegetable, Fruit, Moroccan, African
Yield: 2 cups
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Sugar
3 tb Butter
Salt and pepper
2 c  Moroccan lamb broth,
-strained
1 lg Spanish onion, quartered
-sliced thin lengthwise
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
Bring broth to boil in saucepan. Add remaining ingredients, including
salt and pepper to taste. Simmer, covered, for 45 minutes. Uncover
and simmer until liquid boils away and onions have a glazed
appearance, 30-45 minutes. Can be covered and refrigerated for up to
2 days st this point. Warm before serving.
