Title: Puff Oven Pancakes
Categories: Mixes, Breakfast, Pcake/waffl, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Milk
2/3 c  Pancake mix
4 T  Butter or margarine
4    Eggs
MMMMM--------------------FIESTA FRUIT TOPPING-------------------------
1    Banana, sliced
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Sour cream
10 oz Frozen raspberries or straw-
Berries, thawed
1 c  Pineapple chunks, drained
Preheat oven to 450.  Put 2 Tbsp butter or margarine each in two 9"
pie pans.  Put in preheating oven to melt. In blender, combine eggs,
milk and pancake mix. Pour batter into pie plates. Bake about 18
minutes, until pancakes are puffy and browned.  Do not open oven
while pancakes are cooking.   Spoon raspberries or strawberries on
top of each warm pancake. Top with pineapple chunks and banana.
Sprinkle brown sugar over top. Top with sour cream.  Serve
immediately. Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   02-08-95
