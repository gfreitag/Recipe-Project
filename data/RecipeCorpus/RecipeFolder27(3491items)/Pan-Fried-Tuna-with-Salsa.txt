Title: Pan-Fried Tuna with Salsa
Categories: Fish, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
Tuna steaks
MMMMM-----------------------FLAVORED FLOUR----------------------------
1 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Dill, dried
OR
1 tb Parsley, dried
1 c  Flour
1 ts Cayenne
1 ts Black pepper
1 ts Onion powder
MMMMM-----------------------FRYING MIXTURE----------------------------
1 tb Butter
1 c  Oil
1/2 ts Cayenne
Dip each tuna steak or fillet portion in the flavored flour to coat.
Then dip in egg-milk wash and then in stale crushed bread crumbs.
Place on a rack for 10 min for coating to set.

Heat frying mixture in a skillet. Pan fry tuna til golden. Turn and
finish. Serve with a few drops of pan liquor and top each piece with
a spoon full of Salsa. Garnish with lemon or lime wedges.
