Title: BLACK FOREST TORTE
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Baking powder
2/3 c  Margarine, soft
4 oz Unsweetened chocolate
3    Eggs
1 3/4 c  Flour
1 3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/4 c  Water
1 1/4 ts Soda
1 ts Salt
Heat oven to 350Ã¸. Brush sides and bottom of four 9" round layer pans
with butter. Bake only two layers at a time. Measure into a large
mixer bowl all ingredients except eggs. Beat at low speed to blend;
beat 2 minutes at medium speed, scraping bowl frequently. Add eggs;
beat 2 minutes more. Pour 1/4 of batter into each pan; layers will be
thin. Bake 15-18 minutes or until done. Cool slightly and remove from
pan; cool thoroughly on greased waxed paper to prevent sticking.
Alternate layers with chocolate filling and cream filling, ending
with chocolate filling on top. Do not frost sides of torte.
