Title: Double Deluxe All American Brownie
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 16 servings
1 c  Butter or margarine
4    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 c  Nuts; chopped
2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Flour
3/4 c  Cocoa powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Soda
Preheat oven to 350~F. Grease lightly 13x9" pan. Stir sugar, flour,
cocoa, soda, and salt; set aside. Mix melted butter, eggs and vanilla
until smooth. Add flour mixture and stir until moistened. Fold in
chocolate chips and nuts. Spread batter in prepared pan. Bake for
35-40 minutes. Do not overbake.
