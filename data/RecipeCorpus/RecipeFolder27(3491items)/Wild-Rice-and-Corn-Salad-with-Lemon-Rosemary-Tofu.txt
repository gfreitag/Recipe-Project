Title: Wild Rice and Corn Salad with Lemon-Rosemary Tofu
Categories: Rice dishes, Vegetable, Salad, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
1    Piece Lemon-Rosemary Tofu,
...cut into 3/8-inch cubes
...(about 2 cups)
1/4 c  Pine nuts
1 tb Canola oil
2 1/2 tb Lemon-Rosemary Marinade
Salt and ground black pepper
2 c  Cooked wild rice
1/2 c  Canned or frozen corn,
...drained and defrosted
1/2 c  Thinly sliced scallions,
...green part only
1 lg Shallot, finely minced
...(about 2 tb)
1. In large bowl, combine wild rice, corn, scallions, shallots, and
cubed tofu.

2. In small dry skillet, toast pine nuts over medium heat until golden
brown, about 10 minutes, stirring frequently to prevent burning.

3. Add nuts and canola oil to rice mixture, toss to blend.

4. Add enough marinade to moisten and flavor.  Season to taste with
salt and pepper.
