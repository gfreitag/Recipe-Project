Title: Eggnog Cheesecake (Not Just for Christmas)
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  WATER
24 oz CREAM CHEESE, unwrapped and
-warmed in
Microwave 1-1/2 minutes on
-high
1/2 c  GRANULATED SUGAR
2 c  EGGNOG
1 pt WHIPPING CREAM, whipped
1 pk GRAHAM CRACKERS, crushed
1/4 c  SUGAR
1/4 ts NUTMEG
1/2 ts CINNAMON
4 tb BUTTER, melted
Mix and pat into 10"
-springform pan.
CAKE
2    Envelopes KNOX GELATIN
** CRUST
