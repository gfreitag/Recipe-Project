Title: No-Bake Chocolate Ricotta Pie
Categories: Chocolate, Pie/tart, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
-- melted
1/2 c  Pistachio nuts; chopped
1    Chocolate wafer crust
(9-inch); baked
Whipped cream
Shaved chocolate
1    15-16 oz ricotta cheese,
Low-fat
1/4 c  Powdered sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 tb Heavy cream
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:10

1. In a medium bowl, beat together ricotta cheese, powdered sugar,
vanilla, and cream with an electric mixer on medium until light and
fluffy, 2 to 3 mins. Beat in melted chocolate until well blended.
Stir in pistachio nuts.

2. Turn into Chocolate Wafer Crust. Refrigerate until set, several
hours or overnight. Decorate with whipped cream and shaved chocolate.
