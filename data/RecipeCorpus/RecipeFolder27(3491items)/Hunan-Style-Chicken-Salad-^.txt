Title: Hunan Style Chicken Salad ^
Categories: Salad
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Ground ginger
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Red wine vinegar
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Crushed red pepper
1 tb Sesame seeds, toasted
1 lb Cooked chicken, shredded
3    Cucumbers, julienned
1    Leek, white part only, very
-thinly sliced
1 tb Minced fresh ginger
-OR-
Combine chicken and cucumbers in serving dish. Sprinkle with leek
pieces and ginger. Refrigerate until well chilled. Mix soy sauce,
vinegar, sugar, red pepper and oil together, blending throoughly.
Stir in sesame seeds. Just before serving, toss chicken mixture with
dressing.
