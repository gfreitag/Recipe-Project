Title: Sukiyaki Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/4 c  Japanese rice wine (sake)
2 c  Canned or homemade beef stoc
1 c  Japanese soy sauce
Combine sauce ingredients in a saucepan and heat just to dissolve
sugar. Cool and refrigerate until needed. 01/16 07:43 pm Florence -
Sequim, WA
