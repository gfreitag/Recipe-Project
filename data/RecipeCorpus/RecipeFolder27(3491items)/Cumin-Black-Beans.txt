Title: Cumin Black Beans
Categories: Vegetable, Herbs/spice
Yield: 2 servings
-rinsed and drained
2 tb Orange juice
1 tb Cilantro; chopped
1    Onion(s), chopped
1 tb Vegetable oil
3/4 ts Ground cumin seeds
1 cn Black beans (16-19 oz)
In small saucepan cook onion in oil over moderately high heat,
stirring occasionally until softened and lightly browned. Add cumin
and cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Stir in beans and orange juice and
cook until beans are heated through and most of the liquid has
evaporated. Stir in the cilantro.
