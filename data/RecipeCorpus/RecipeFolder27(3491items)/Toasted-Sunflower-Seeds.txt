Title: Toasted Sunflower Seeds
Categories: Snack, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 ts Salt, if desired
1 c  Sunflower seeds
1 ts Oil, if desired
Preheat oven to 325 F (slow).

Mix sunflower seeds with oil only if salt is used.  Spread plain or
oiled seeds on baking sheet.  Bake about 8 minutes or until lightly
browned. (Watch carefully, these seeds brown quickly.)

Sprinkle oiled seeds with salt while hot.

Calories per tablespoon: About 20 with oil, 15 without oil
