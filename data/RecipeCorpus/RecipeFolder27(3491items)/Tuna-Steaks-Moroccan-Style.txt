Title: Tuna Steaks Moroccan Style
Categories: Fish, Moroccan, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
4 ea Tuna steaks (1-1/2# total,
-1-1/2" thick)
1 ea Tb lemon juice
2 ea Tb olive oil
2 ea Tb melted butter
4 ea Tb coarsely chopped
-coriander
1 t  Paprika
1/2 t  Ground cumin
1 t  Turmeric
1/4 t  Ground anise seed
1/2 t  Ground ginger
1/8 t  Ground cinnamon
1/4 t  Red hot pepper flakes
Salt and white pepper
Combine spices. Rub steaks with mixture, sprinkle with lemon juice and
olive oil. Cover with plastic wrap and let stand till ready to cook.
If broiling: Arrange on rack and place under very hot broiler, 4"
from heat. Broil 4 minutes with door partly open, turn and cook on
other side 3-4 minutes. For rare, cook less. Steaks should be well
browned. If pan frying: Heat large cast iron skillet. Do not add fat.
When hot, add steaks in one layer and cook until well browned, 3
minutes per side. Place steaks on warm plates, brush with melted
butter, sprinkle with coriander. Serve with couscous. Yield: 4
servings
