Title: Date Filled Drop Cookies
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 30 cookies
1 t  Vanilla
1/4 c  Water or buttermilk
Walnut halves
4 c  Basic cookie mix
1/4 t  Cinnamon
2    Eggs, beaten
MMMMM------------------------DATE FILLING-----------------------------
3 T  Water
1/4 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Chopped dates
3 T  Sugar
In small saucepan combine dates, sugar and water. Cook over medium
heat about 5 to 10 minutes, stirring until thick. Remove from heat.
Cool slightly.  Stir in chopped nuts. Set aside to cool. Preheat oven
to 375. Lightly grease baking sheets.  In a large bowl, combine
cookie mix, cinnamon, eggs, vanilla and water or buttermilk. Blend
well. Drop by tsp onto prepared baking sheets. Spoon 1/2 tsp date
filling on top of each cookie, depressing dough slightly.  Cover each
with another tsp of dough. Top with walnut half.  Bake 10 to 12
minutes. Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   02-10-95
