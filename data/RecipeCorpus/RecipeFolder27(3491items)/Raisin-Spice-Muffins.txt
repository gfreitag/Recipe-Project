Title: Raisin-Spice Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin, Fruit
Yield: 12 muffins
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/2 c  Molasses
1    Egg, beaten
1/3 c  Butter, melted
1 c  Milk
3/4 c  Raisins
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Nutmeg
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

Sift the first seven ingredients together.  Stir in the molasses, egg,
butter and milk, just until moistened.  Fold in the raisins.  Fill
paper lined muffin tins full and bake approximately 20 minutes.
