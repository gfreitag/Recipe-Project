Title: Nam Prik for Cooked Vegetables
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------FAR EASTERN-----------------------------
3    Garlic clove(s)
1    Onion(s)
1 tb Fish sauce (nam pla)
1 tb Brown sugar
Lime juice
4 oz Unripe fruit such as
-green mango, goseberries,
-tart plums, or grapes
6    Dried red chilies
1 sm Piece trassi (shrimp paste)
Chop the fruit. Seed and chop the chilies. Wrap the trassi in foil and
grill until it darkens or cook in a preheated 350Ã¸F oven for a few
minutes. Crumble and pound the trassi or process it with the other
ingredients, adding lime juice to dilute and to taste. Stored in a
jar, it will keep in the refrigerator for a few days.
