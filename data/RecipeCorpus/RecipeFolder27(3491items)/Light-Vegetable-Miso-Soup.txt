Title: Light Vegetable Miso Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
1 pk Ramen or
-2 vermicelli nests
1 1/2 ts Miso
1/2    Carrot, slivered
1    Scallion, sliced very thin
1 tb Frozen peas
2 c  Water
Bring water to a boil. Put in  the carrot, scallion, noodles and
peas. Let simmer for about 3 minutes, till the noodles are done. Put
the miso in a cup and add about 2 tablespoons hot water from the pot.
Stir till blended and then put back in the pot. Serve.
