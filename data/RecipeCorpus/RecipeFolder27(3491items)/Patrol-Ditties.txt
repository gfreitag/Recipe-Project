Title: Patrol Ditties
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 ts Vanilla
20    Graham crackers
1/2 c  Sugar
2    Sticks of butter
Break graham crackers in half and spread in a pan.  Mix sugar and
butter and boil for 2 minutes.  Add pecans and vanilla and pour over
crackers. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.  Remove from pan
immediately. Randy Rigg
