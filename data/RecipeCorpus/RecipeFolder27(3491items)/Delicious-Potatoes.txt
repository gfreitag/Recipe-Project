Title: Delicious Potatoes
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 6 - 8 folks
Sm amount pimento;
5    Green onions; 3" of green
1 ts Salt;
1 1/2 c  Milk;
1    Stick butter
1    Stick margarine;
6 md Green Pepper;
Melt butter in the milk. (do not boil.)  Grate or grind the potatoes,
peppers and onion.  Put into a 1-1/2 quart casserole.  Pour on milk.
Preheat oven 250 degrees.  Bake casserole 4 to 5 hours.  Add more
milk is necessary.  Any leftovers can be used for breakfast. Source:
Lucille Prine, Our Daily Bread, Westminster Presbyterian Church,
Escondido, CA. Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her
Meal-Master. Submitted By NANCY O'BRION   On   02-11-95
