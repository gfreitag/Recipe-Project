Title: Special Shrimp Pasta Salad - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Chopped bell pepper
1 sm Onion, diced
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/2 t  Papa Dash lite salt,
-optional
1/4 t  Ground pepper
1 ea Pt Kraft fatfree mayonnaise
-or Miracle Whip
12 oz Sprial pasta, cooked and
-drained
1 lb Shrimp, cooked and cleaned
1/2 c  Liquid butter buds
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 t  Accent
1 t  Curry powder
2 t  Dill
Combine all of the spices with the liquid Butter buds, fatfree
mayonnaise and lemon juice; set aside. Combine shrimp, pasta, green
pepper, onion and celery. Mix dressing with salad, cover and chill
overnight. The longer it sets, the better the flavor. Per serving:
123 cal., 0.7g fat (5%), 74mg chol., 1g fiber, 10g pro., 19g carb.,
560mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-446-67040-5 Entered
