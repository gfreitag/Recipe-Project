Title: Turkey Basting Agent
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Poultry
Yield: 10 servings
1/4 ts Gravy Master
Or Kitchen Bouquet
-----INGREDIENTS-----
3 tb Butter OR Margarine
1/2 ts Paprika
USE FOR BASTING A TURKEY IN THE MICROWAVE OVEN

Microwave on HIGH (100%) for 45 seconds. Use this mixture to
paint the turkey to be baked in a microwave oven.

Directions for baking a turkey in the microwave oven is found in the
recipe for Microwave Turkey.

Deidre Anne Penrod, Prodigy Food & Wine Board
