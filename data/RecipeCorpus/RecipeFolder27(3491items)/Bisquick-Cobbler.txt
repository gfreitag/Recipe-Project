Title: Bisquick Cobbler
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
Drain off 1/2 of the syrup
1 c  Milk
1    Stick of margarine
1 c  Bisquick
1 c  Sugar
1 cn Sliced peaches, large can
Melt margarine in a 13 x 9 inch pan.  Mix milk, sugar and Bisquick in
a bowl.  Pour into melted margarine.  Spread peaches on top.  Bake
for 45 minutes at 350 degrees. Randy Rigg
