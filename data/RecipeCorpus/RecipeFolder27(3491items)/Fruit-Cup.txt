Title: Fruit Cup
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 md Banana; peeled, sliced
1/2 c  Seedless grapes; halved
1/2 c  Blueberries
2 tb Walnuts, finely chopped
3 tb Frozen lemonade concentrate
1 md Apple; cored, diced
1 md Orange
-- peeled, sectioned & diced
1 md Peach; pitted, diced
Place lemonade concentrate in a large bowl and mix lightly with
fruits as they are prepared.  Chill.

Garnish each serving with chopped walnuts.

NOTE: Skins of apple and peach may be removed, if desired.

Calories per 2/3 cup serving: About 95
