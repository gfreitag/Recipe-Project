Title: Rosy Peach Sherbet
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 1 quart
2/3 c  Sugar
2    To 3 cups milk
2 lg Fresh peaches
2 ts Lemon juice
Scrub peaches, cut and remove pits. You may peel them if desired, but
then it won't be "rosy." Place in jar of blender. Add lemon juice,
sugar, and 1 cup milk. Blend till smooth. Add milk till mixture
measures 4 cups. Blend to mix that in. Churn freeze. Makes 1 quart.

To substitute mango, use 1 large, peeled, pitted mango, or 2 small
ones. From: Helen Fleischer
