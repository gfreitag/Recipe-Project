Title: DAHL PALAK (SPINACH AND SPLIT PEAS)
Categories: Indian, Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Ginger, fresh and chopped
4 ea Garlic cloves, chopped
1 tb Garam Masala
1 tb Lemon juice
1 pn Salt
1/2 c  Split peas
2 c  Water
1/4 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Salt
1 lb Spinach, chopped
3 tb Butter/oleo
Soak split peas in 2 cups water 1 hour.

Add turmeric and 1/2 ts salt to split peas.  Boil 10 mins in same
water used to soak peas.

Add spinach and cook until most of the liquid has been absorbed and
split peas are done.

Meanwhile, heat butter/oleo in a skillet.  Add ginger and garlic.
Cook until garlic is golden.  Add Garam Masala (see recipe called
"Garam Masala").

Immediately add the seasoned ginger and garlic to the spinach-split
pea mixture.  Add lemon juice and salt to taste and serve.
