Title: Fettuccine Alfredo (Lo-Fat)
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Italian, Pasta, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Molly McButter Natural
-Butter Flavor Sprinkles
4 c  Hot cooked fettuccine
2 ts Chopped fresh parsley
Freshly ground pepper
2 sm Cloves garlic, minced
1 1/3 c  Skim milk, divided
2 ts All purpose flour
2 tb Fat free cream cheese
1 c  Fat free grated parmesan
-cheese
(1) In medium saucepan, combine minced garlic and 1 cup skim milk.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat to simmer. (2) Stir in flour and
remaining 1/3 cup milk. Continue stirring for about 2 minutes, until
thickened. (3) Add fat free cream cheese. Whisk vigorously until
smooth. Cook for 2 minutes. (4) Add grated Parmesan cheese; whisk
until smooth. (5) Remove from heat. Stir in Molly McButter. (6) Pour
the sauce over hot cooked fettuccine. Garnish with fresh parsley and
black pepper. Submitted By ED FAUSS   On   02-05-95
