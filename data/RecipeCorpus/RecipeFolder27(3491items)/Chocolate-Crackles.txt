Title: Chocolate Crackles
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 48 cookies
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  C and H Powdered Sugar
-- (approximately)
1 c  Semi-sweet chocolate pieces
1 c  C and H Brown Sugar; packed
1/2 c  Salad oil
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  All-purpose flour
Melt chocolate.  Combine with sugar and oil.  Add eggs one at a time;
beat well.  Add vanilla.  Combine flour, baking powder and salt.  Add
to chocolate mixture.  Stir in nuts.  Chill dough.  Drop teaspoonfuls
of dough in powdered sugar.  Roll to coat.  Place on greased cookie
sheet. Bake in 350 degree oven 10 to 12 minutes.  Cool on rack.

Makes 4 dozen cookies.
