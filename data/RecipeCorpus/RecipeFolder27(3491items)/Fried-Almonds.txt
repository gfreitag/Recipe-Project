Title: Fried Almonds
Categories: Condiment, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 cup
1 c  Whole blanched almonds
2 tb Vegetable oil
Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add almonds; fry
until golden brown, 3-4 minutes. Drain on paper towels and set aside.
Can be stored in an airtight container for up to 2 days.
