Title: My Oatmeal Cookies *****
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 5 dozen
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sour milk
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
2 c  Rolled oats
Directions Stir first 5 ingredients together.Melt fats and mix with
dry ingredients.Stir in milk, mixing well. Chill dough 1/2 hour. Roll
out to 1/4" thickness on lightly floured board. Cut with cookie
cutter, about 2" in diameter.Place on greased baking sheet and bake
in a moderate oven 350 degrees 10 to 15 minutes or until lightly
brown.

Note; When I make these I put 2 together with jam between and they
are a favorite with my grandchildren.
