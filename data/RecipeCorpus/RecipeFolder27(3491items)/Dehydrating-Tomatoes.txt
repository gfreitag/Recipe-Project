Title: Dehydrating Tomatoes
Categories: Can/preserv, Tomato
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
Cut into small wedges and let each rest on itsskin side to dry.  This
keeps the juice in so flaor and nutrition are enhanced.  Fascinating
to chew and a great flavor addition to broths, etc. Origin: Dry It -
You'll Like It! circa 1973. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
