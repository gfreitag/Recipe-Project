Title: Red Beet Eggs
Categories: Side dish, Cheese/eggs, Sthrn/livng, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Mixed pickling spices
6    Eggs, hard-cooked
1 md Onion; thinly sliced and
-separated into rings
16 oz Beets
1 c  Vinegar
3/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Salt
Drain beets, reserving liquid.  Pour liquid into a glass jar or bowl;
add vinegar, sugar, salt, and spices; stir until sugar dissolves. Add
beets, eggs, and onion; cover.

Chill 2 hours or longer before serving.  Drain eggs and serve in one
dish, beets and onions in another.
