Title: Chili Dip *
Categories: Dip, Cheese/eggs, Appetizer, Tex/mexican, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Picante sauce
2 cn Chili with no beans, large
1 lb Cheddar cheese; shredded
Microwave chili until hot. Add shredded cheddar cheese and mix until
cheese is melted. Return to microwave if needed. Add sour cream and
picante sauce (I use hot). Mix well. Serve with nacho chips or plain
corn chips.
