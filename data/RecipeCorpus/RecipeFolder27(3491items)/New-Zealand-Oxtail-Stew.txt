Title: New Zealand Oxtail Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Down under, Beef
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2 ea Carrots
2 ea Beefstock cubes
1 ea Ox tail
1 oz Crisco
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
2 c  BOILING water
Pepper and salt
1 oz Flour
2 ea Onions
Disjoint the oxtail, roll each piece in flour and brown in hot fat.
Lift out meat and brown sliced vegetables. Put ALL into a saucepan,
add seasonings and only enough liquid (beef stock or stock cubes
crumbled and dissolved in boiling water) to cover the meat and
vegetables. Simmer for 3 hours.
