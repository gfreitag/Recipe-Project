Title: Roasted Potatoes (Geoffrey's style)
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Onion powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cumin
1 1/2 ts Paprika
2 lb Red potatoes
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts White pepper
1/2 ts Cayenne
Boil peeled potatoes until semi-cooked, very starchy.

Add spices to oil-mix well. Pour half of oil mixture onto a baking
sheet and add potatoes. Pour remaining spice mixture over potatoes to
coat them thoroughly.

Bake in preheated oven at 450 degrees F. until potatoes start to
crisp, about 15 to 20 minutes.
