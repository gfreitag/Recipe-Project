Title: Japanese Chicken Soup ^
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Cooked thin egg noodles
1    Cooked boneless chicken
-breast, thinly sliced
4    Thin slices lemon with rind
5 c  Chicken broth
4 oz Can mushroom stems and
-pieces, drained, reserving
-liquid
2 ts Soy sauce
Bring broth to a boil. If there is not enough broth, add mushroom
liquid to measure 5 cups. Cover and simmer 5 minutes. Add mushrooms
to broth and simmer until heated through. Add soy sauce and noodles;
simmer 3 minutes. Divide chicken among 4 bowls. Pour soup over and
garnish with a lemon slice.
