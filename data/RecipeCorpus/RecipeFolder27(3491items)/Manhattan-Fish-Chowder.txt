Title: Manhattan Fish Chowder
Categories: Fish, Soup/stew, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Chopped carrots
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Catsup
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Chopped parsley
1 lb Fresh or frozen fish fillets
1/4 c  Chopped bacon or salt pork
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 lb Can tomatoes, chopped
1 c  Chopped potatoes
Fry bacon or salt pork till crisp. Remove all but 2 tb. fat. Saute
onion till tender.

Add everything but fish. Cover and simmer for 45 minutes till vegies
are done. Add fish and simmer covered for 10 more minutes.

Can also be done in the microwave.
