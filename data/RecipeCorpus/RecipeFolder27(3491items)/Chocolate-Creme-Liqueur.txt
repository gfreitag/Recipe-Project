Title: Chocolate Creme Liqueur
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 tb Vanilla extract
1 tb Instant espresso powder
1 tb Coconut extract
2 c  Heavy cream
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
1 c  Whiskey
1/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:10 1. In a food processor, combine cream,
sweetened condensed milk, whiskey, cocoa, vanilla, espresso powder,
and coconut extract. Process until well blended and smooth. 2. Serve
immediately over ice. Or place in glass container, cover tightly, and
store in refrigerator up to 3 weeks. Stir before using.

Note: Makes a good gift and dessert drink. Similar to commercial cream
liqueurs, but cheaper to make and very delicious. Serve over ice.
