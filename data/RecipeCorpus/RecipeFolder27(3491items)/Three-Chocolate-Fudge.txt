Title: Three-Chocolate Fudge
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 5 1/2 pounds
2    Milk chocolate candy bars,
(7 oz each), broken
2 oz Semisweet baking chocolate,
-chopped
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Pecans, chopped
3 1/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter/margarine
1 c  Dark brown sugar, packed
12 oz Evaporated milk
32 lg Marshmallows, halved
12 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
In a large saucepan, combine the first four ingredients.  Cook and
stir over medium heat until sugar is dissolved.  Bring to a rapid
boil; boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Stir in chocolate
chips until melted. Add chocolate bars and baking chocolate; stir
until melted. Fold in vanilla and pecans; mix well.  Pour into a
greased 15x10x1 inch baking pan. Chill until firm.  Cut into squares.
