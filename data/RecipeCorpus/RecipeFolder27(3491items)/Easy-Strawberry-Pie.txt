Title: Easy Strawberry Pie
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 19 servings
1 c  Water
3 tb Cornstarch
2 tb White corn syrup
1 ea 9" pie shell, baked
4 c  Strawberries
1 c  Sugar
Boil together the sugar, water, cornstarch & syrup.  Cook until it
starts to thicken.  Cool.  When cool, add the strawberries & pour
into the pie shell.  Refrigerate before serving.

From Lindley Farms.
