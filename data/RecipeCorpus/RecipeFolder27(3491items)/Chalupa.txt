Title: Chalupa
Categories: Vegetable, Tex/mexican, Crockpot
Yield: 8 servings
1 T  Cumin
1 ts Oregano
1 cn (4-oz.) chopped green
.chilies
Corn chips
1 lb Dry pink beans
3 lb Pork roast
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
1 T  Salt
2 T  Chili powder
Sort and rinse beans.  Put all ingredients except corn chips in dutch
oven, an electric crockery cooker or heavy kettle.  Add 7 cups water.
Cover and simmer about 5 hours, or until roast falls apart and beans
are done. Uncover and cook about 1/2 hours to desired thickness.
Serve with corn chips and pass condiments for choice of toppings:
(chopped tomato, avocado, onion, shredded lettuce, grated cheddar
cheese, taco sauce or other hot sauce).

From "Cooking with California Dry Beans," California Dry Bean Advisory
