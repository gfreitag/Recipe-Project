Title: Oatmeal Crisps
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  Sifted flour
2 ts Salt
2 ts Baking soda
6 c  Quick cooking rolled oats
2 c  Raisins
2 c  Butter
2 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
2 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla
4    Eggs
Cream together butter and brown and white sugars until fluffy.  Stir
in vanilla, then add eggs, one at a time, beating after each
addition. Sift together flour, salt, and baking soda.  Add to creamed
mixture. Stir in rolled oats and raisins.  Drop by teaspoonfuls about
2 inches apart onto a well greased baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees
for 10 to 15 minutes. Randy Rigg
