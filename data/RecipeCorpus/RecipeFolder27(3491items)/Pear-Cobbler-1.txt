Title: Pear Cobbler 1
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
3/4 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Baking soda
2 T  Butter
6 T  Buttermilk
Whipped cream or vanilla
-ice cream, optional
1 lg Pear, cored and sliced into
-thin wedges
3 T  Sugar
1 T  Flour
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/4 t  Salt
Toss together pear wedges, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1 tablespoon flour and
cinnamon.  Spoon into 1-1/2 quart glass bowl and set aside.  Stir
together remaining 1/2 cup flour, salt, remaining 1 tablespoon sugar,
baking powder and baking soda in medium bowl. Cut in butter until
crumbly. Stir in buttermilk to make sticky dough. Drop dough, using
tablespoon, around inside edge of bowl to make ring of batter. Bake
at 350 degrees 30 to 35 minutes, or until dough is golden brown and
pear filling is juicy and bubbly.  Remove from oven and let set 10
minutes. Serve plain, with cream or ice cream.
