Title: Award Winner Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 16 servings
2/3 c  Unsifted flour
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
2    Eggs
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Butter or margarine; melted
3/4 c  Chocolate; ground
Heat oven to 350~. Using a spoon, stir eggs with sugar and vanilla;
add butter. Sift Ground Chocolate with flour, baking powder and salt.
Stir into egg mixture; add nuts. Spread into greased 8 or 9" square
pan. Bake at 350~ for 20-30 minutes. For extra chewy brownies, use 8"
pan and less baking time. For cake like brownies use 9" pan and
longer baking. Cut into squares.
