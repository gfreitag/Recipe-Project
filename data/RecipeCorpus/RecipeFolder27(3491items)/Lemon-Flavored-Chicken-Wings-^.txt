Title: Lemon Flavored Chicken Wings ^
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
4 tb Butter or margarine
16    Chicken wings
2 c  Lemon juice
1 ts Salt
Cut off wing tips and save for a later use. Combine lemon juice, salt
and pepper. Pour over wings and marinate 4 hours, stirring to coat
well on all sides. Drain wings on paper towels. Place on foil lined
broiler pan and rub each piece liberally with the butter. Broil 15-20
minutes, or until crisp or bake at 500 30-45 minutes. Serve.
