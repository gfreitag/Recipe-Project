Title: Sweet & Sour Meatballs
Categories: Mixes, Beef
Yield: 6 servings
6 T  Water
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 pk Meatball mix (about 30)
1    Green pepper, sliced
4 c  Cooked rice
1 T  Oil
20 oz Pineapple chunks, drained
Reserving juice
3 T  Cornstarch
1 T  Soy sauce
3 T  Vinegar
In large skillet, combine oil and 1 cup drained pineapple juice,
adding water if needed to make 1 cup. In small bowl, combine
cornstarch, soy sauce, vinegar, water and brown sugar. Stir into
juice mixture and cook over medium heat about 5 to 7 minutes until
thick, stirring constantly. Add meatball mix, pineapple chunks and
green pepper. Simmer 20 minutes until heated through.  Serve over
hot, cooked rice. Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   02-14-95
