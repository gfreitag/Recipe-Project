Title: Corn  Velvet  Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Chicken broth
3 c  Water
3 T  Cornstarch in 1/2 c. water
1 T  Sherry (optional)
-Salt and pepper to taste
1 lg Can creamed corn
3    Eggs, beaten
1 sl Cooked ham (diced)
-Some cooked chicken
-(chopped)
Put broth and water in deep pan and boil.  Add Sherry, salt and
pepper. Add corn and bring back to boil.  Cook 3 minutes, stirring
constantly. Add ham and chicken, reduce heat.  While stirring,
dribble beaten eggs in soup "to form flowers".  Dissolve cornstarch
in water by stirring with finger. Add to soup last and cook and stir
until thickened. Serves 6. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
02-13-95
