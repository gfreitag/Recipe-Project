Title: Orange Taffy
Categories: Candy, Fruit
Yield: 6 dozen
Salt, pinch
1 c  Light cream
1/2 c  Butter/margarine
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Light corn syrup
6 oz Orange juice, frozen
-concentrate, untiluted
In a heavy saucepan, combine first four ingredients.  Cook and stir
over medium heat until sugar is dissolved.  Bring to a rapid boil and
cook until candy thermometer reads 245 degrees (firm ball stage).
Add cream and butter, heat and stir until reaches 24 again.  Pour
into a greased 15x10x1 inch pan; cool.  When cool enough to handle,
roll into 1 1/2 inch logs or 1 inch balls.  Wrap individually in foil
or waxed paper; twist ends. Yield: about 6 dozen
