Title: Lemon Butter Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 /2 cup
1 1/2 ts Herbes de Provence
-or Fines Herbes
-or your favorite blend
1 pk Butter Buds Mix, liquefied
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Lemon juice
1    Shallot, minced (2 tbs)
1 1/2 tb Parsley, minced
In small saucepan over medium-high heat, cook water, lemon juice,
shallots,and herbs until reduced to 1 tbs. Gradually add Butter Buds,
stirring constantly, until the mixture comes to a boil; boil 1 minute.
Strain if desired.

Tarragon Sauce: Replace the water and lemon juice with 2 tbs each
white wine and white vinegar. Replace the herbs with 1 tbs fresh or
1« tsp dried tarragon. cook as directed.

Dill Sauce: Replace the water and lemon juice with ¬ cup white wine.
Replace the herbs with 1« tbs minced fresh dill. cook as directed.
