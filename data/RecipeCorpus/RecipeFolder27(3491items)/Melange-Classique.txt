Title: Melange Classique
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------FRANCE--------------------------------
2 ts Cloves
1 ts Cayenne
1 1/2 ts White peppercorns
1 1/2 ts Coriander seeds
5    Bay leaves
2 ts Dried thyme
1 ts Dried marjoram
1 ts Dried rosemary
2 ts Grated nutmeg
Crumble the bay leaves. Grind all the ingredients to a fine powder.
Stored in an airtight continer, it will keep for 3-4 months.
