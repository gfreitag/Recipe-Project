Title: Itallian Pasta Salad - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
1 ea Tb sugar
**OR**
1 1/2 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
1 t  Minced garlic
1 t  Italian seasoning
1/8 t  Ground black pepper
8 oz Sprial pasta, cooked in
-unsalted water and drained
2 md Tomatoes, cut in wedges
2 c  Broccoli florets, blanched
1/2 c  Black beans, rinsed and
-drained
1/4 c  Kraft fatfree mayonnaise or
-Miracle Whip
In a large bowl, combine the pasta, tomatoes, broccoli and black
beans. In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, garlic,
Italian seasoning and pepper. Pour over salad and toss to coat well.
Cover and chill 2 hours. Per sugar serving: 68 cal., 0.4g fat (5%),
0mg chol., 2g fiber, 3g pro., 14g carb., 67mg sod. Per S&L serving:
63 cal., 13g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-446-67040-5
