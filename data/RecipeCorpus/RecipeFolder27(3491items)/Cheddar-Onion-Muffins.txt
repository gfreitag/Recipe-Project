Title: Cheddar-Onion Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 12 muffins
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/3 c  Butter, melted
1 c  Cheddar cheese, grated
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
1/4 c  Fresh parsley, finely
-chopped
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Paprika
2 tb Sugar
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  Milk
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

Sift together the first 5 ingredients.  Add the remaining ingredients
except for the onions and parsley.  Mix just until blended.  Fold in
the onion and parsley.  Fill paper lined muffin tins full and bake
about 20 minutes.  Best served hot or warm while the cheese is still
gooey.
