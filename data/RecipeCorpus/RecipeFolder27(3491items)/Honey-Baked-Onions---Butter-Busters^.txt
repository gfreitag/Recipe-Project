Title: Honey Baked Onions - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 6 ----------
1 t  Ground coriander
1/4 t  Lite salt, optional
1/8 t  Cayenne pepper
3 lg Red onions, halved crosswise
1/3 c  Honey
1/4 c  Liquid Butter Buds
1 t  Sweet Hungarian paprika
Place onions, cut side down, in a baking dish just large enough to
hold all the onions in one layer. Sprinkle with water. cover with
foil and bake at 350F. for 30 minutes. turn onions cut side up.
Combine remaining ingredients and spoon half the mixture over the
onions. Return to oven and bake uncovered 15 minutes. Baste with
remaining honey mixture and bake 15 more minutes. this is a good side
dish that is very low in calories as well as low in fat. Per serving:
148 cal., 0.5g ft (3%), 0mg chol., 4g fiber, 3g pro., 36g carb., 63mg
sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by
