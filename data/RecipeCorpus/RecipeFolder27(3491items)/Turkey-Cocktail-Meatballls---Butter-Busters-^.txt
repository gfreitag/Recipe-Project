Title: Turkey Cocktail Meatballls - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizer, Pickles
Yield: 10 servings
1 ea Tb Worcestershire sauce
1/4 t  Lite salt, optional
1/2 t  Poultry seasoning
**SPICY SAUCE**
1/2 c  Chile sauce
2 ea Tb vinegar
2 ea Tb brown sugar*
1 lb Lean ground white meat
-skinless turkey
1/2 c  Crushed fatfree SnackWells
-Wheat crackers
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Egg Beaters
1 ea Tb chicken broth
MMMMM-------------------------MEATBALLS------------------------------
Mix all of the ingredients together and roll into bite sized balls.
Place meatballs on a broiler pan so any fat will fall through. Bake
at 375F 15-20 minutes. Sauce: Mix together. *Or use 1 tablespoon
brown sugar and 1/4 teaspoon Sweet 'n Low Brown Makes 50 meatballs.
Per 5 meatball serving: 106 cal., 1.5g fat (13%), 27mg chol., 0g
fiber, 13g pro., 9g carb., 278mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie
