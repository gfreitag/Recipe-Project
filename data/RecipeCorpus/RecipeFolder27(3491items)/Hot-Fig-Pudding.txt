Title: Hot Fig Pudding
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 10 servings
MMMMM----------------DOUBLE HOT WINE SAUCE RECIPE---------------------
MMMMM-----------------FOR MORE GENEROUS SERVINGS----------------------
2 1/2 c  Sifted flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter
2    Eggs
1 c  Molasses
2 c  Finely chopped dried figs
1/2 ts Grated lemon peel
1 c  Buttermilk
MMMMM-----------------------HOT WINE SAUCE----------------------------
3/4 c  Dry sherry
1 ts Grated lemon peel
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
Cream 1/2 cup butter until soft.  Beat in 2 eggs and molasses until
fluffy. Stir in figs, 1/2 ts lemon peel and buttermilk. Resift flour
with soda, baking powder, ginger and salt. Stir dry ingredients into
pudding mixture. Pour into greased 9 inch tube pan. Bake at 325
degrees 1 hour until done.

Meanwhile, to make sauce, cream 1/2 cup butter and sugar until light.
Beat in 2 eggs.   Stir in sherry, 1 ts lemon peel and nutmeg. Shortly
before serving, beat sauce over hot water in double boiler. Heat
thoroughly. Serve with hot pudding.
