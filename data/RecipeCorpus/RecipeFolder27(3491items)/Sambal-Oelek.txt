Title: Sambal Oelek
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------------INDONESIA------------------------------
1 ts Brown sugar
8 oz Red chilies
1 ts Salt
Heat a heavy frying pan and put in the chilies after 2 minutes. Dry
roast over medium heat for a few minutes. Cool, then seed if desired,
finely chop and pound to a paste with the salt and sugar. Can be kept
tightly stored in the refrigerator for a week or so.
