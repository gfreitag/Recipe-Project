Title: Quick Cajun Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Herbs/spice, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------PREP. TIME: 10 MINUTES, COOK---------------------
-black pepper
3/4 ts Spice Islands Ground white
-pepper
4 ea Boneless chicken breasts
2 T  Butter or olive oil
1 1/2 ts Spice Islands Thyme
1 ts Spice Islands Cayenne pepper
1 ts Spice Islands Garlic salt
1 ts Spice Islands Onion powder
3/4 ts Spice Islands fine grind
In small bowl, combine herb and spices.  Sprinkle herb and spice
mixture on both sides of the chicken breasts; press into meat.
Reserve.  In large skillet, heat butter over medium-low heat.  Cook
chicken in hot butter for 15 minutes; turn and cook for another 10
minutes or until cooked through. Serve with rice, if desired. From:
Spice Islands Shared By: Pat Stockett
