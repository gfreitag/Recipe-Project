Title: Kindergarten Donuts
Categories: Kids, Doughnut
Yield: 8 doughnuts
1 ts Cinnamon
Vegetable oil
1 pk Refrigerated biscuits
1 c  Sugar
NOTE: Adult supervision is needed for this recipe! Mix sugar and
cinnamon and set aside. Put a finger through each biscuit to make a
very large hole in the center. Heat one-inch deep vegetable oil to
375F in a frying pan. Drop in doughnuts. Cook to a golden brown. Roll
in sugar mixture. *By Sue Hibbard* -Posted for you by Michelle Bruce
