Title: My Salmon Patties  *****
Categories: Main dish, Fish, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 1 servings
20 oz Can of tomatoes
1 md Onion,chopped
1 tb Butter
1 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Soda crackers,crushed
-fine
1 lg Can of red salmon
1/2 c  Mashed potatoes
1    Egg
1/4 ts Sage
Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Bread crumbs
Sauce;
Directions: Drain salmon,add mashed potatoes,salt and pepper,sage and
egg.Mix together and shape into patties.Roll patties in bread crumbs
and place in skillet and cook until done. SAUCE Put tomatoes in dish
and add remaining ingredients,bring sauce to a boil and heat. Pour
over patties and serve.

Note; We had these often as we were growing up and still do.
