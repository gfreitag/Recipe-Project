Title: Filipino Fish Soup
Categories: Filipino, Fish, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
3 c  Water
1 1/2 lb Fish, cut into 1 1/2-inch
Slices
1/3 c  Watercress, chopped
2 md Tomatoes, sliced 1/2-inch
Thick
2 md Green peppers, cut into
Rings 1/4-inch wide
4 oz Onion, cut into thin slices
In saucepan combine tomatoes, green peppers, onion, salt and water and
bring to a boil. Add fish and cook 5 to 8 minutes, or until fish is
tender. Add watercress, heat 1 minute and serve at once. *From Weight
Watchers International Cookbook*
