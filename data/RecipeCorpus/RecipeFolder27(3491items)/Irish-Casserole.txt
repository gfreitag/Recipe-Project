Title: Irish Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Vegetable, Sausage
Yield: 4 - 6 folks
1 pk Green beans; frozen cut
-cooked
2 c  To 4 cup mashed potatoes;
-(you can use instant mashed
-if you wish)
1/2 c  Cheese; grated
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/3 c  Green onions; chopped
1/4 c  Mushrooms; sliced;(4-oz can)
3 tb Margarine;
6    To 8 weiners; (depending
-on size of family) cut into
-1/2" slices
Saute onion, green pepper and mushrooms in margarine about 5 minutes.
Add weiners, brown lightly.  Remove from heat; add beans.  Stir hot
mashed potatoes into weiner-bean mixture, mixing well.  Sprinkle
cheese over mixture.  Bake a 375 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes. Serves
4 to 6 minutes. Source: Betsy Wood, Our Daily Bread, Westmister
Presbyterian Church. Escondido, CA. Brought to you and your via Nancy
O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
