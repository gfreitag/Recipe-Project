Title: Jellied Cranberry Turkey Salad ^
Categories: Salad
Yield: 5 servings
2 tb Chopped scallion
3/4 ts Seasoned salt
1/8 ts Pepper
4 tb Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Seedless grape halves
Romaine lettuce
1 c  Bottled cranberry juice
3 oz Pkg raspberry or cherry
-jello
1/2 c  Port wine
1 1/2 c  Diced cooked turkey
3/4 c  Chopped celery
Place cranberry juice in a pan and bring to a boil. Add jello and stir
until dissolved. Remove from heat. Add port wine and let cool. Pour
into oversized wine glasses or goblets, filling half full. Chill
until firm. Combine turkey, celery, scallion, salt and pepper. Chill
for several hours. Add mayonnaise and grapes, tossing gently. Arrange
small lettuce leaves in each glass. Spoon in turkey salad and serve.
