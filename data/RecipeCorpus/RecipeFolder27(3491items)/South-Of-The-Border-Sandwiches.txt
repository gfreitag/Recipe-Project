Title: South Of The Border Sandwiches
Categories: Sandwich, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
ds Pepper
ds Ground cumin
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese (4
-oz.)
4 ea French OR submarine rolls,
-split
2 ea Hard cooked eggs, chopped
1 T  Chopped green chilies
1/3 c  Salsa
1 ea Green onion, sliced
2 T  Sliced ripe olives
ds Salt
In a large bowl, mix eggs, chilies, salsa, onion, olive and
seasonings. Fold in cheese. Spoon 1/2 cup onto bottom of rolls.
Replace tops; wrap each sandwich tightly in foil. Bake at 350 degrees
for 20 minutes. Serves 4. SOURCE:* Taste of Home Magazine, Feb./Mar.
1995 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 2/95
