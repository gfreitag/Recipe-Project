Title: Apricot Oatmeal Squares
Categories: Fruit, Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 25 servings
1 c  Cold butter
1 ea « cups rolled oats
« cup desiccated coconut
1 c  Apricot jam
1 ea « cups all purpose flour
« cup white sugar
« cup brown sugar
1 t  Baking powder
Combine flour, sugars, and baking powder. Cut in butter until it is
in tiny bits. Stir in oats and coconut. Reserve about 1 cup of the
mixture for topping. Press remainder into buttered 9" square baking
dish. Spread base with jam. Sprinkle with reserved crumb mixture on
top. Bake at 375F for 30-40 minutes or till lightly browned. Cool.
Cut into squares. Makes 25 AR/95
