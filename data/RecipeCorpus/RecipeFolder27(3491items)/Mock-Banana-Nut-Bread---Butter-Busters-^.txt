Title: Mock Banana Nut Bread - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Bread
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 c  Buttermilk**
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
1 t  Vanilla
3 sm (to4)mashed ripe bananas
1 c  Grape nuts cereal
*Or 1/2 c sugar and 6
-packets S&L
2 c  Flour or bread flour
1 t  Baking soda
1/2 t  Lite salt, optional
1 c  Sugar*
1/2 c  Liquid Butter buds or
-Promise Ultra fatfree
-margarine
*or 1/2 cup skim milk and 1 teaspoon lemon juice Mix all ingredients
except bananas and cereal with a hand mixer. Beat until smooth. fold
bananas and cereal in. Spray two loaf pans with Pam. Pour batter in
and bake at 300F about an hour or until golden brown. Per sugar
serving: 114 cal., 0.4g fat (3%), pmg chol., 1g fiber, 3g pro., 25g
carb., 119mg sod. Per S&L serving: 99 cal., 21g carb. Butter Busters
by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 2-95.
