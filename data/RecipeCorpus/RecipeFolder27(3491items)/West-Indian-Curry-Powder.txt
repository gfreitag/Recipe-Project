Title: West Indian Curry Powder
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------WEST INDIES-----------------------------
1 tb Black peppercorns
1    Piece of cinnamon
2 tb Ground ginger
2 tb Turmeric
1 oz Coriander seeds
1 tb Aniseed
1 tb Cumin seeds
1 tb Black mustard seeds
1 tb Fenugreek seeds
Dry roast all the whole spices for about  5 minutes. Cool, then grind
and blend with the ginger and turmeric. Stored in an airtight
container, it will keep for 3-4 months.
