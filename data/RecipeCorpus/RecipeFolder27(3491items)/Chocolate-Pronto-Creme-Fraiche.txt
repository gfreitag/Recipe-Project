Title: Chocolate Pronto Creme Fraiche
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Powdered sugar
1/4 c  Sour cream
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Heavy cream
2 tb Unsweetened cocoa powder,
-- (to 3 tbs)
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:10 In a medium bowl, whip cream with cocoa powder
and sugar until stiff. Add sour cream and vanilla and beat just until
blended. Cover and refrigerate for up to 2 days before using.

Variation: PRONTO CREME FRAICHE Omit cocoa powder and vanilla; reduce
powdered sugar to 2 Tbs.
