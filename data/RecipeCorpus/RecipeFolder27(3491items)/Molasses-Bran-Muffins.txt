Title: Molasses Bran Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Bread, Breakfast
Yield: 12 muffins
1    Egg
1 1/4 c  Muffin mix
1 c  Bran cereal
3/4 c  Milk
2 T  Molasses
Preheat oven to 400.  Butter muffin pans.  In a small bowl combine
bran and milk.  Combine molasses and egg in another bowl. Stir into
bran mixture. Put muffin mix into medium bowl. Add bran molasses
mixture all at once to muffin mix.  Stir just until moistened; batter
should be lumpy. Fill prepared muffin pans 2/3 full. Bake 15 to 20
minutes, until golden brown. VARIATION: Add 1 cup raisins or chopped
dates to bran mixture. Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   02-06-95
