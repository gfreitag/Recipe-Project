Title: Chicken with Citrus Cream Sauce
Categories: Chicken, Salsa/sauce, Stove top
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
3 T  Butter - OR margarine
1 ea Lemon - sliced in half
-crosswise
1 ea Orange - sliced in half
-crosswise
1 t  Orange marmalade
1 1/2 lb Chicken breasts, cubed
3 T  Flour - all-purpose
1 c  Chicken broth
1 ea Lime - sliced in half
-crosswise
1 c  Whipping cream
1 sm Onion - finely chopped
1 pkg Puff Pastry Shells, baked
salt and white pepper to taste
Directions: Coat chicken pieces with flour. In a large saucepan, saute
chicken in 1-1/2 tbs butter until half cooked, about 2 to 3 minutes.
Remove chicken and set aside.  Saute onion in remaining butter.
Squeeze half of the lemon, lime and orange into onion mixture. Cook
while stirring, until sauce reduces slightly.  Add chicken broth and
marmalade. Increase heat while stirring, reduce liquid to half. Add
whipping cream and reserved chicken; cook, stirring constantly until
sauce thickens and just comes to a boil. Season with salt and pepper.
Serve in warm pastry shells. Slice remaining citrus fruit into wedges
and use as garnish.
