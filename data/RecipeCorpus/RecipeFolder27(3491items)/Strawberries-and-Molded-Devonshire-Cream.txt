Title: Strawberries and Molded Devonshire Cream
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
-temperature   1 cup
-whipping cream
1/2 c  Sugar
2 pt Fresh strawberries, sliced
1 ea Envelope (1 Tbsp)
-unflavored gelatin  3/4
-cup cold water
1 c  Sour cream, at room
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 t  Vanilla
In saucepan, sprinkle gelatin over water; then dissolve over low
heat. Stir into sour cream, whisking lightly to smooth out lumps.
Set aside. In mixing bowl, beat cream, gradually adding sugar, until
soft peaks form. Stir in vanilla and sour-cream-gelatin mixture. Pour
into 1-quart mold, rinsed first with cold water.  Chill until firm.
Unmold on serving plate. Surround with sliced berries, sweetened to
taste, if needed. Slice mold to serve and top each serving with
berries.
