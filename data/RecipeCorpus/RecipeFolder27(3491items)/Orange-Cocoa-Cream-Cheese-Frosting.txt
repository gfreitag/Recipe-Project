Title: Orange Cocoa Cream Cheese Frosting
Categories: Chocolate, Fruit, Cheese/eggs, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Triple sec or other
-orange-flavor liqueur
3/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
3 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
4 tb Butter; softened
1 pk Cream cheese, (8 oz)
-- softened
1 ts Orange extract
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:05 In a medium bowl, beat together butter and
cream cheese with an electric mixer on low speed until smooth. Add
orange extract, Triple Sec, and cocoa; beat until smooth. Add
powdered sugar and beat on high speed until smooth and fluffy.

From:  Dan Klepach
