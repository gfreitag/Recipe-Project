Title: Green Masala
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
4    To 6 green chilies
1 sm Bunch fresh cilantro
1 sm Piece of ginger
1    To 2 garlic clove(s)
Peel and chop the garlic and ginger; seed and slice the chilies.
Remove the cilantro stalks. Pound or blend all the ingredients to a
paste with a little water. Use with fish or chicken.
