Title: Mozzarella Meat Pie
Categories: Meat, Main dish
Yield: 5 servings
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Salt
1 x  Pepper
8 oz Crescent rolls
1 lb Ground beef
1/3 c  Chopped onion
1/3 c  Chopped celery
6 oz Tomato paste
4 oz Shredded mozzarella cheese
Brown meat and drain.  Add onion and celery and cook until tender. Add
tomato paste, 1/2c cheese and seasonings.  Mix well.  Separate rolls
into 8 triangles.  Place in 9" pie plate.  Press together to form
crust. Flute edge.  Fill with meat mixture and top with remaining
cheese. Bake at 375 for 15-20 minutes. VERY GOOD
