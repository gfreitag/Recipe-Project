Title: Chocolate Chip Cake #2
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Salad oil
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Graham crackers; crushed
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
2 tb Cinnamon
1 c  Walnuts; chopped
3/4 c  Chocolate chips
1/2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
1 pt Sour cream
Combine cinnamon, walnuts, chips and white sugar; set aside. Blend
eggs, sour cream, brown sugar and salad oil until creamy; add
vanilla. Combine cracker crumbs, baking powder and soda; stir into
batter. Pour half the batter into an oiled 13x9" baking pan. Sprinkle
batter with half the cinnamon/sugar mix. Pour rest of batter in pan.
Sprinkle top with remaining cinnamon/sugar mix. Bake at 350~ for 45
minutes.
