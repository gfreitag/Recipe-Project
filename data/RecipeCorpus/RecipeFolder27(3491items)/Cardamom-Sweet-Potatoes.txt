Title: Cardamom Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Sweetpotato, Herbs/spice, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 t  Ground nutmeg
1/2 t  Ground cardamom
2/3 c  Milk
2 T  Butter or margarine, melted
1 cn Sweet potatoes (40 oz.) OR
1 1/2 lb Fresh sweet potatoes, cooked
9 T  Brown sugar, divided
1 T  Vanilla extract
1/2 t  Salt
In a mixing bowl, beat sweet potatoes, 6 tablespoons brown sugar,
vanilla, salt, nutmeg and cardamom. Add milk; mix well. Spoon into a
greased 1 quart casserole. Drizzle with butter; sprinkle with
remaining brown sugar. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until
top is lightly browned. Serves 6. SOURCE:*Taste of Home Magazine,
December/January 1995. POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 2/95
