Title: Spaghetti Alla Carbonara
Categories: Pasta, Pork, Mushroom, Italian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1/8 ts Red pepper
1 lb Spaghetti
Butter and olive oil
1 lb Bacon cut in pieces
1/2 lb Onions cut in pieces
3/4 lb Mushrooms halved
1/2 c  Whipping cream or half&half
1/3 c  Parsley chopped
1.  Cook bacon and drain on a towel 2. Cook onion in olive oil until
soft - drain 3. Cook mushrooms in butter - drain 4. Put bacon,
onions, and mushrooms together 5. In a bowl beat eggs, cream, cheese,
parsley, and pepper. 6. Cook spaghetti until al dente and drain DO
NOT RINSE 7. Place hot spaghetti in a bowl. 8. Put bacon mixture on
top. 9. Pour on egg mixture and toss thoroughly. Heat from the
spaghetti will cook the eggs. Submitted By LAWRENCE KELLIE   On
02-04-95
