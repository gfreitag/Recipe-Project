Title: Elegant White Cake
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 cake
3    Egg whites
1 t  Vanilla
3 1/3 c  Basic cake mix
3/4 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 375.  Grease and flour two 8" round pans. Combine
cake mix and milk in a large bowl. Beat at medium speed 2 minutes.
Scrape bowl and beaters.  Add egg whites and vanilla.  Beat 2 more
minutes. Pour into prepared pans.  Bake 20 to 25 minutes. Cool in
pans 10 minutes, then cool on wire racks. Frost as desired.
