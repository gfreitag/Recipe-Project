Title: Apple Crisp
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
5 c  Sliced tart apples
-- (5 to 6 apples)
1 c  C&H Golden Brown Sugar
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Mix sugar, flour, spices, and salt.  Cut in butter until mixture is
crumbly.  Spread apples in bottom of buttered 8- or 9-inch square
baking pan.  Sprinkle crumbly mixture over fruit; pat lightly with
fingers.  Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes, or
until apples are tender. Serve warm with cream or ice cream.  Makes 6
to 8 servings.
