Title: Panocha Walnuts
Categories: Candy
Yield: 1 batch
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
2 1/2 c  Walnut halves
1 c  C&H Brown Sugar
1/2 c  C&H Granulated Sugar
1/2 c  Water
Combine 1 cup C&H Brown Sugar, 1/2 cup C&H granulated Sugar, 1/2 cup
water, 1/4 teaspoon salt in 3-quart saucepan.  Slowly bring to a
boil, stirring occasionally until sugar dissolves.  Boil slowly to
246 degrees (firm-ball stage).  Remove from heat.  Add 1 teaspoon
vanilla and 2-1/2 cups walnut halves; stir until nuts are coated and
sugary.  Quickly spread on waxed paper or foil and separate nuts with
two forks.  Let cool.
