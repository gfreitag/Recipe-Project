Title: GRAHAM CRACKER PEANUT BUTTER BARS
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Nuts/grains
Yield: 36 servings
1 lb Powdered sugar
1 lb Graham crackers, crushed
12 oz Milk chocolate morsels
-FHMN87A Phill Bower
1    18 oz jar peanut butter
3/4 c  Butter
Graham crackers are to be coarsely crushed. Using an electric mixer,
cream together peanut butter and butter until soft and smooth. Beat
in powdered sugar until smooth, then beat in graham crackers. Beat
until well blended and moist. Spread mixture in a 13x9x2 inch pan.
Melt the chocolate morsels and pour over mixture. Refrigerate 3 or 4
hours. Cut into bars. from the Ultimate Peanut Butter Cookbook by
Arthur Boze
