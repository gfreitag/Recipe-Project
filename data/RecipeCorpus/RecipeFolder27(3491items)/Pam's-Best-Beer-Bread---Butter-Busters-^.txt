Title: Pam's Best Beer Bread - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Breakfast
Yield: 24 servings
1 l  Beer
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
1 ea Tb liquid Butter Buds
3 c  Self rising lfour
1 c  Sugar*
1 t  Baking powder
*Or use 1/2 cup sugar and 6 packets Sweet 'n Low In a large bowl
combine all of the ingredients and mix well with a hand beater. Pour
into two loaf pans that have been sprayed with a nonstick cooking
spray. Bake at 350F for 40 minutes. Brush liquid Butter Buds on top
after removing from oven. Serve warm. Serving size is one slice. Per
sugar serving: 94 cal., 0.2g fat (2%), 0mg chol., 0.4g fiber, 2g
pro., 20g carb., 223mg sod. Per S&L serving: 78 cal., 16g carb.
