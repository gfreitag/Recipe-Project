Title: Pickling Spice with Cayenne
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------ENGLAND-------------------------------
1 oz Cloves
3 oz Allspice
1 1/2 oz Ginger
2 1/2 oz Yellow mustard seeds
2 oz Cayenne pods
2 1/2 oz Black peppercorns
1 1/2 oz White peppercorns
Leave spices whole. Use for chutneys, pickled fruits and vegetables,
and to spice vinegar. Tie in a small muslin bag and remove after
pickling, or add straight to vinegar, depending on the type of pickle.
