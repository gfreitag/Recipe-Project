Title: Mexican Tortilla Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Tex/mexican, Main dish, Beef
Yield: 1 casserole
2 ts Chili powder;
Corn tortillas
3/4 lb Cheddar cheese;
1 sm Can olives;sliced or chopped
1 lb Ground beef;
1 sm Onion;
1 sm Can Mexicorn;
1 cn (8-oz) tomato sauce;
1 ts Cumin;
Brown meat and onion. Drain excess grease.  Add corn, tomato sauce,
cumin and chili powder.  Layer in casserole dish meat then tortillas
cut up and cheese - 3 times.  Top with chopped olives.  Bake at 350 F
for 1 hour. Source: Suzanne Graeher; Our Daily Bread, Westminster
Presbyterian Church, Escondido, CA Brought to you and your via Nancy
O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
