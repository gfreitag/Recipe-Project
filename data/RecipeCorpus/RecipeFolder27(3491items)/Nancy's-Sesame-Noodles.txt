Title: Nancy's Sesame Noodles
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 ts Sugar
1 ds Tobasco, to taste
3 c  Cooked Spaghetti noodles
4 tb Peanut Butter
3 ts Water (see directions)
2 ts Sesame oil
1 ds Soy sauce
Cook spaghetti, drain and rinse with cold water (adjust how much
spaghetti you make depending upon how hungry you are!) Mix peanut
butter with a few teaspoons of warm water until creamy and light in
color. Add sesame oil, dash soy sauce, dash Tobasco to taste.
Pour over spaghetti.
Serve with Nancy's Sesame Chicken.
