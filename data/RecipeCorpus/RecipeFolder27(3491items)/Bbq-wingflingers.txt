Title: Bbq wingflingers
Categories: Appetizer, Chicken, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  White vinegar
3 tb Worchestershire sauce
4 tb Chili powder
2 tb Salt
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/2    Bag wingflingers (frozen
-chicken wings)
1/4 c  Salad oil
5 md Onions, diced
3 c  Tomato sauce
1 1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
preheat over to 400. in hot salad oil over med high heat cook onion
til tender (5 mins) add rem ingred, heat to boiling, stirring
constantly reduce heat and simmer 30 min, stirr occasionally. pour
over wings and bake in sauce for 1 hour
