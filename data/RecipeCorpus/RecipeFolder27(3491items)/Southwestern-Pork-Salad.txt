Title: Southwestern Pork Salad
Categories: Salad, Pork, Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Sugar
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 t  Dry mustard
1 t  Dried oregano
2 T  Minced fresh parsley
2 c  Cooked pork strips
1 cn (16 ounces) kidney beans,
Rinsed and drained
1/2 c  Sliced, ripe olives
1 ea Medium onion, chopped
1 lg Green pepper, chopped
1 lg Tomato, chopped
In a large bowl, toss pork, beans, olives, onion,green pepper and
tomato. Combine remaining ingredients in a jar with tight fitting
lid; shake well. Pour over pork mixture; toss gently. Cover and
refrigerate for 4-6 hours, stirring occasionally.
