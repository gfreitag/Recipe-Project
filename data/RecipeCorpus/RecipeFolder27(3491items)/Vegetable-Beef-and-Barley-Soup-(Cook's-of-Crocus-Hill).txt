Title: Vegetable Beef and Barley Soup (Cook's of Crocus Hill)
Categories: Beef, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 soup
3 qt Beef stock, warmed
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Dried leaf thyme
2 tb Fresh dill weed, minced (or
-1 ts. dried)
2 tb Parsley, minced
Salt and pepper to taste
3 tb Unsalted butter
1 1/2 c  Onions, diced
1 c  Carrots, diced
1 c  Celery, diced
2 ts Garlic, minced
1 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1 lb Beef stewing meat, diced
YIELD; 3 quarts

Melt the butter in a large soup or stockpot. Add onions, celery,
carrots, and garlic and saute until tender, but not browned.

add diced beef and saute briefly. Add mushrooms, and cook till soft.

Add warmed stock, nutmeg and barley. Bring to a boil, skimming any
foam from the top. Reduce heat and simmer for 2 hours, or until
barley is tender.

Just before serving, stir in dill weed and parsley. Season with salt
and pepper to taste.

from a class at Cook's of Crocus Hill attended by- Mary Riemerman
