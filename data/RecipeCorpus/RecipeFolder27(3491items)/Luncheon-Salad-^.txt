Title: Luncheon Salad ^
Categories: Salad
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Salt and pepper to taste
Lettuce leaves
1 tb Chopped parsley
2 c  Chopped cooked chicken
2 c  Grated carrot
2 tb Finely chopped onion
2    Unpeeled sweet apples, cored
-and chopped
3    Stalks celery, chopped
Combine chicken, carrot, onion, apples and celery in bowl. Mix
mustard with mayonnaise. add to ingredients in bowl and toss lightly.
Season with salt and pepper. Arrange lettuce on individual plates.
Pile chicken mixture in center and sprinkle with parsley.
