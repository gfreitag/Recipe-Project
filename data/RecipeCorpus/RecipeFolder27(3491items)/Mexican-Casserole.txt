Title: Mexican Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Beef, Tex/mexican, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Enchilada sauce
1 sm Bottle taco sauce
1    Bag of cheese doritos
2 c  Cheddar cheese; grated
1 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion; chopped
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
Saute meat and onion. Drain. Mix soups and sauces with meat. Layer
chips, soup/meat mix and cheese in a 2 qt casserole dish. Repeat
layers. Bake at 350~ for 30 minutes, uncovered.
