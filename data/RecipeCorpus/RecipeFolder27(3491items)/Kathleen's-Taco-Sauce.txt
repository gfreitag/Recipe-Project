Title: Kathleen's Taco Sauce
Categories: Appetizer, Low-cal/fat, Relish, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 T  Salt
1/4 T  Cumin
1/4 T  Oregano
1/4 t  Red pepper
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 1/4 qt Tomatoes
1/8 c  Green peppers
3/16 c  Hot peppers
1/2 c  Onions
1/2    Clove garlic
Peel tomatoes, chop in food processor, then add next 4 ingredients.
Put into big pot and add the rest of the ingredients.  Simmer until
thick.  (2 hours or longer).
This salsa can be very hot.  My Mother-In_Law usually makes a larger
batch to can.
