Title: Green Chile Cheese Corn Bread - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Bread
Yield: 16 servings
-drained
1/4 c  Liquid Butter Buds or
-Promise Ultra fatfree
-margarine
1/4 c  Drained green chiles,
-chopped
2 c  Self rising flour
1/4 c  Sugar*
1/4 c  Egg Beaters, beaten
1 c  Skim milk
1/2 c  Grated nonfat Cheddar Cheese
1 cn (7oz) whole kernal corn,
*Or 2 tablespoons sugar and 1 1/2 packets Sweet 'n Low Heat oven to
400F. Spray 9" square pan with Pam. Measure all ingredients in order
listed in bowl; stir just until moistened. Batter should be lumpy.
Spread evenly in pan. Bake 35 minutes or until golden brown. Per
sugar serving: 95 cal., 0.4g fat (4%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 4g pro.,
19g carb., 266mg sod. Per S&L serving: 89 cal., 18g carb. Butter
Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw
2-95.
