Title: Strawberry Cheese Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1/3 c  Lemon Juice from concentrate
1 t  Vanilla extract
1 qt Strawberries, cleaned & hull
1 ea Pk Prepared strawberry glaze
1 ea Pk 9-in baked pastry shell
1 ea Pk 8 oz cream cheese,
-softened
1 c  Eagle Brand Condensed Milk
In large mixer bowl, beat cheese until fluffy.  Gradually beat in
Eagle Brand until smooth.  Stir in lemon juice and vanilla.  Pour
into prepared crust.  Chill 3 hours or until set.  Top with
strawberries and desired amount of chilled glazed.  Refrigerate
leftovers.
