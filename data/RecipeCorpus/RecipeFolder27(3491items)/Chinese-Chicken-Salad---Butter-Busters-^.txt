Title: Chinese Chicken Salad - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Fatfree sour cream
1/4 c  Fatfree ranch dressing
-(Weight Watchers or Kraft)
1 t  Curry posder
1 t  Soy sauce
1 t  Lemon juice
2 c  Cooked chicken breasts,
-boned and skinned
4 oz Sliced water chestnuts,
-drained
1/2 c  Diced celery
1/2 lb Seedless green grapes
Cut chicken into 1" cubes. Mix together chicken, water chestnuts and
celery. Blend together the sour cream, dressing, curry powder, soy
sauce and lemon juice. Pour over the chicken mixture. Stir in grapes.
Chill several hours. Per serving: 158 cal., 2.2g fat (13%), 42mg
chol., 2g fiber, 17g pro., 14g carb., 166mg sod. Butter Busters by
