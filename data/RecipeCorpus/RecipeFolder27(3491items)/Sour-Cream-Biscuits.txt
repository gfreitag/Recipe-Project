Title: Sour Cream Biscuits
Categories: Bread
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Sour cream
3 tb Baking powder
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
Sift flour, measure, and sift with salt, baking powder, and baking
soda. Mix with cream to the consistency of a roll dough, adding 1
teaspoon sweet milk or water if too stiff.  Turn onto lightly floured
board. Knead lightly.  Pat into sheet 1/2 inch thick. Cut with
floured cutter. Place in well-oiled pan and bake in hot oven (450Ã¸ F)
about 12 minutes. 12 servings. Crace Viall Gray, Glen Ellyn, IL.
