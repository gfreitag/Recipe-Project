Title: Chinese Celery
Categories: Vegetable, Asian, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Onion; chopped
1 ts Accent;
1/8 ts Garlic powder;
1/8 ts Ginger;
2 tb Butter    -=OR=-
2 tb Margarine;
1/3 c  Almonds; sliced
4 c  Celery; coarsely chopped
1    Chicken bouillon;
Melt butter or margarine and bouillon.  Add other ingredients.  Stir
and cook 10 minutes. Source: Junie Scherzer, Cooking with Love,
