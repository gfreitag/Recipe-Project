Title: Slow Cook Spanish Rice
Categories: Crockpot, Beef
Yield: 1 servings
1 cup
-sauce
1 ea 8-ounce can tomato sauce
-1 cup raw rice (converted)
2 1/2 t  Chili powder
2 t  Worcestershire
2 lb Ground chuck
-water
Or beef
2 md Onions, chopped   2 1/2
-teaspoons salt
2 ea Green peppers, chopped
Brown beef in skillet and drain off fat. Put all ingredients in
CROCK-POT. Stir thoroughly. Cover and cook on Low 6 to 8 hours.
(High: 3 hours). From Rival Crock-pot cook book, date unknown From
the files of Earl Shelsby
