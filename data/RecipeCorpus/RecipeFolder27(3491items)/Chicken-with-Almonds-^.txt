Title: Chicken with Almonds ^
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Chicken, Tomato, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Cinnamon
Salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 c  Chicken broth
5 tb Blanched almonds
1/2 c  Seedless golden raisins
4 c  Hot rice
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Butter
2 1/2 lb Chicken pieces
2    Onions, thinly sliced
3    Tomatoes, skinned, seeded
-and chopped
Preheat oven to 375. Heat oil and butter in frying pan. Add chicken
pieces and brown on all sides. Add onions, tomatoes, cinnamon, salt
and pepper. Cook over low heat 3-4 minutes, then transfer to
ovenproof casserole. Add chicken broth, cover and cook 30 minutes.
Add almonds and raisins and cook 30 minutes more. Line a large oval
platter with hot cooked rice. Pile chicken pieces in center of
platter. To serve, season sauce to taste and pour over chicken.
