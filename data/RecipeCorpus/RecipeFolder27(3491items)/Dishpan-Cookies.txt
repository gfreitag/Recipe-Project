Title: Dishpan Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 c  Oats
2 ts Baking soda
3 c  Corn flakes
2 c  Coconut flakes
1 1/2 c  Nuts
1 ts Salt
2 c  Brown sugar
2 c  White sugar
2 c  Liquid shortening
4    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
4 c  Flour
Cream sugars, oil, eggs, and vanilla.  Mix in gradually the last 7
ingredients.  Drop by teaspoon on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake in a
350 degree oven for 8 to 10 minutes. Randy Rigg
