Title: Monte Criso Sandwiches
Categories: Mixes, Sandwich, Pork, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 sandwiches
6 sl Thin turkey
2    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Milk
1 c  Pancake mix
12 sl White bread
Mayonaise
12 sl Thin swiss cheese
6 sl Thin ham
Preheat griddle to 350.  Spead 1 side of each slice with thin coating
of mayonaise.  Assemble each sandwich using 2 slices of swiss cheese,
1 slice ham and 1 slice turkey. Trim crusts with a sharp knife,
making edges even. Cut each sandwich in half.  Set aside. Combine
eggs and milk in a shallow dish.  Add pancake mix. Butter griddle.
Dip each sandwich into the batter. Grill about 3 to 4 minutes, until
lightly browned on both sides and cheese begins to melt.
