Title: Raspberry Almond Cheesecake
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 30 servings
-warmed in
Microwave 2 minutes on high.
1 c  SUGAR
5    JUMBO EGGS, out of shells
-and warmed in
Microwave 25 seconds.
1/2 c  HEAVY CREAM
1/4 c  CORNSTARCH
1 ts ALMOND EXTRACT
2 c  GRAHAM CRACKERS, crushed
1 c  ALMONDS, chopped
1/3 c  SUGAR
6 tb BUTTER, melted
Mix and pat into bottom and
-sides of 10" springform
-pan.
CAKE
2 lb CREAM CHEESE, unwrapped and
** CRUST
