Title: Chocolate Rum Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
3 oz Melted unsweetened chocolate
1/4 c  Rum
Milk, if needed
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter, softened
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
16 oz Powdered sugar, divided
With electric mixer at medium speed, beat butter and cream cheese
until creamy.  Add half the powdered sugar and the chocolate; mix
well. On low speed, add remaining powdered sugar and rum;  beat until
mixture is of spreading consistency, adding a few drops of milk if it
is too thick. Use as directed to frost chocolate rum cake.
