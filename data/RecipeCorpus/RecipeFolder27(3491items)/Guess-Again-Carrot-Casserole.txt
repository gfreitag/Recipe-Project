Title: Guess Again Carrot Casserole
Categories: Vegetable, Casserole
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Butter
1    Onion, medium, grated
-Green pepper is optional
-Bread crumbs with butter an
2 lb Carrots, cooked and mashed
8 oz Sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
You can't put your finger on the taste right away, hence, the name.
Add cheese , salt and pepper, & butter to mashed carrots. Place in a
1 qt. buttered baking dish. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and dot with
the additional butter.  Sprinkle with parsley flakes. Bake @ 350 for
40 min. Lisa/Cedar Rapids
