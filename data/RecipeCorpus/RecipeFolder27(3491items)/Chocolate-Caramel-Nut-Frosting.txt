Title: Chocolate Caramel Nut Frosting
Categories: Chocolate, Nuts/grains, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
Coarsely chopped
6 oz Semisweet chocolate
Chips
1 c  Brown sugar; packed
4 tb Butter
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1/3 c  Blanched almonds;
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:08

1. In a medium saucepan, combine brown sugar, butter, and cream.
Bring to a boil, stirring often, over medium heat. Reduce heat to low
and simmer 3 mins. Stir in nuts.

2. Let cool completely. Stir in chocolate chips; frosting will be
chunky.
