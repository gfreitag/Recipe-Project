Title: Sweetheart Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 2 servings
3/4 c  Cooked or canned beets, chop
6    Iceberg lettuce slices, 1/2-
6    Red pepper rings, cut in hal
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sour cream
1 c  Red Delicious apple, chopped
1 ts Lemon juice
Combine mayonnaise and sour cream; mix well. Toss apple with lemon
juice. Add mayonnaise mixture and beets; mix lightly. Chill. For each
serving, place 1/2 cup salad on lettuce slice. Surround with 2
half-slices of red pepper, molding the salad o form a heart shape.
Source: Chef Keith Keogh, Executive Chef, Epcot Center, Walt Disney
World
