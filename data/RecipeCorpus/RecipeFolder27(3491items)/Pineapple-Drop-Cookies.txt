Title: Pineapple Drop Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/4 c  Flour; sifted
Salt; a pinch
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 c  Nut meats
1/4 c  Butter
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
1/4 c  Pineapple; drained - crushe
-d
Cream butter, sugar, add remaining ingredients. Mix well, drop 1/2
teaspoon on cookie sheet. Bake in oven at 375 F.

Source: Florence Yohey, Tallmadge Grange, Summit County, OH
