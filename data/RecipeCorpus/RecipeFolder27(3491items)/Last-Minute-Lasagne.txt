Title: Last Minute Lasagne
Categories: Mixes, Pasta, Vegetarian, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 lb Grated mozzarella cheese
1 c  Grated romano and parmesan
6 c  Italian cooking sauce
8 oz Lasagne noodles, cooked
1 lb Ricotta cheese
Preheat oven to 350.  Lightly butter a 13 x 9 pan. Spread one third of
cooking sauce in bottom of pan. Cover with one third of lasagne
noodles. Add one third of ricotta cheese thinned with water for
easier spreading if needed.  Add one third mozzarella and one third
grated cheeses. Repeat layers twice, ending with grated cheeses.
Cover with foil. Bake 30 to 35 minutes, until heated through.  Let
stand 10 minutes before serving. Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On
02-12-95
