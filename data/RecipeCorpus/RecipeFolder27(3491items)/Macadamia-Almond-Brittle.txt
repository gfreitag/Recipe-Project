Title: Macadamia Almond Brittle
Categories: Candy, Nuts/grains, Microwave
Yield: 1 pound
1 tb Butter/margarine
2 ts Vanilla extract
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
34 c  Macadamia almonds, coarsely
-chopped
Combine sugar and corn syrup in a 1 1/2 qt. microwave safe bowl.
Microwave on high for 5 minutes.  Stir in nuts.  Microwave on high
for 4 to 5 minutes or until a candy thermometer reads 300 degrees
(hard-crack stage). Quickly stir in butter, vanilla and baking soda
until mixture is light and foamy. When bubbles subside, pour onto a
greased cookie sheet, spreading as thinly as possible with a metal
spatula.  cool completely; Break into pieces. Store in an airtight
container with waxed paper between layers. Yield: about 1 lb.

NOTE:  This recipe was tested using a 700-watt microwave.
