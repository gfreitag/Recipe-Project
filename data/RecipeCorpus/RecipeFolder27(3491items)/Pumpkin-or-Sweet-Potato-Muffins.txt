Title: Pumpkin or Sweet Potato Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin, Vegetable, Sweetpotato
Yield: 12 muffins
2/3 c  Sugar
1    Egg, beaten
1 c  Sour cream
3 tb Sweet orange marmalade
1/3 c  Oil
1 c  Mashed pumpkin or sweet
-potato
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ds Ground cloves
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

Sift together the first 8 ingredients.  Stir in the remaining
ingredients, just until blended.  Spoon into paper lined muffin tins
and bake for about 20 minutes.
