Title: Ginger Crisps
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Flour
2 ts Soda
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Ginger
3/4 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg
4 tb Molasses
Roll in small balls and dip in sugar.  Flatten with glass.  Bake for
8 to 10 minutes at 350 degrees. Randy Rigg
