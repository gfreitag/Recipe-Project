Title: Broccoli-Tofu Stir-Fry
Categories: Vegetable, Stir fry, Low-cal/fat, Mushroom
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2 T  Rice vinegar
1 T  Fresh ginger, grated
2 c  Broccoli flowerets
1 md Red bell pepper, thin strips
1 1/2 c  Mushrooms, sliced
1 T  Sesame oil (PLUS 1 tsp)
1 sm Onion, sliced
12 oz Firm tofu, drained,
-1/2-inch cubes
1/4 c  Low-sodium soy sauce
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Heat oil in a 12-inch skillet and stir-fry onion until slightly
tender. Add tofu and stir-fry until lightly browned.  Add the
remaining ingredients. Gently toss to coat all the ingredients with
the sauce. Cover and simmer over medium heat 5 minutes until the
vegetables are tender.
