Title: Wilted Greens
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Horseradish
1/2 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 lb Greens, leaf lettuce, escaro
3 ea Sl Bacon
1 ea Onion, chopped
1/4 c  Vinegar, white wine
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Prepared mustard
Approx. Cook Time: :09 Place
Fat grams    per serving:
bacon and onion in 8 cup casserole. Cover with lid.  Cook at High 5
to 6 minutes or until bacon is lightly browned; stir once.  Add
vinegar, sugar, mustard, horseradish, salt and pepper, stir.  Add
greens cut into bite size pieces; toss well. Cook at high 2 to 3
minutes.
