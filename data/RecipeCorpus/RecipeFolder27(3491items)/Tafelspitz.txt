Title: Tafelspitz
Categories: Beef, Aust/german
Yield: 8 servings
2    Carrots
3    Sprigs parsley
6    Peppercorns
1    Bay leaf
3 lb Top or bottom round of beef
-or (better yet) brisket
2 ts Salt
2    Onions
Place water, onions, carrots, parsley, peppercorns and salt in kettle
large enough to accomodate the beef. Bring to a boil. Add the beef,
cover, and simmer for 2 or 3 hours. Remove beef from the kettle when
tender and cut in 1/2" slices. Arrange beef on a heated platter and
pour 1 cup of broth over it.

Serve with 2 sauces.

A mixture of horseradish and applesauce and A mixture of horseradish,
sour cream and mayonnaise
