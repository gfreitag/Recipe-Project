Title: Korean Beef - gas grill
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Sesame oil
2 tb Dry sherry
1/8 ts Pepper
1 1/2 lb Sirloin steak
-cut 1/2 inch thick
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 ea Green onions, finely chopped
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
Cut meat into serving size pieces; score on both sides. Place meat in
shallow pan. Mix soy sauce, onions, garlic, sugar, oil, sherry and
pepper. Pour over beef and let stand at room temperature for 1 hour;
turn once. Drain. Pre-heat both sides of gas grill on HIGH for 10
minutes. Turn both sides to MEDIUM. Grill meat with hood closed for
about 15 minutes or until done, turning once.
