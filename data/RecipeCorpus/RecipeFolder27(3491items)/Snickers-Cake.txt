Title: Snickers Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 12 servings
1/3 c  Milk
6 oz Chocolate chips
1 c  Nuts; chopped
1 pk German chocolate cake mix
1 pk Caramels (small pack)
1/2 c  Butter
Mix cake mix as directed. Pour half of the batter into a greased 9x13
pan. Bake in preheated 350~ oven for 15-20 minutes. Melt caramels,
butter and milk together. Pour over baked mixture. Sprinkle chocolate
chips and nuts over caramel mixture. Pour remaining cake batter over
top of all and bake 20 minutes at 250~ then 10 minutes at 350~.
