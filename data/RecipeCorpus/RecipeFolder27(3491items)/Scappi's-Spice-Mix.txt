Title: Scappi's Spice Mix
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------ITALY--------------------------------
-or pepper mixed with
-a little ginger
1/4 oz Saffron
1/2 oz Brown sugar
24    Cinnamon sticks
1 oz Cloves
1/2 oz Dried ginger
1/2 oz Grated nutmeg
1/4 oz Grains of paradise
Break the cinnamon into pieces and grind all the ingredients to a fine
powder. Stored in an airtight container, it will keep for 3-4 months.

The Complete Book of Spices by Jill Norman ISBN 0-140-23804-2 pg 98
