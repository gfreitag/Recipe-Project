Title: Paprika BBQ Spice
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------AMERICA-------------------------------
1 tb Paprika
2 ts Ground cumin seeds
1 sm Piece of ginger
1    Garlic clove(s)
Pound the ginger and garlic in a mortar and mix with the paprika and
cumin.

A pungent blend that goes well with chicken.

The Complete Book of Spices by Jill Norman ISBN 0-140-23804-2 pg 106
