Title: Very Vanilla Cookies
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 cookies
1 1/2 t  Vanilla
1    Egg
3 c  Basic cookie mix
1 t  Milk, more if needed
Preheat oven to 375.  Lightly grease baking sheets. In a large bowl,
combine cookie mix, milk, vanilla and egg. Blend well. Drop tsp onto
prepared baking sheets. Sprinkle with sugar, if desired. Bake 10 to 15
minutes.

VARIATIONS:

SPICE VANILLA COOKIES: Add 2 tsp pumpkin pie spice and 1/2 cup nuts to
dough.

COCOA COOKIES: Add 2 Tbsp cocoa and increase milk to 2 TBSP.

HOLIDAY PINWHEELS:  Divide dough in half. Color half green and half
red. Cover each half with wax paper roll out to rectangles of equal
size. Remove wax paper. Place one rectangle on top of the other and
roll like a jelly roll.  Chill 2 to 3 hours.  Cut into 1/8" slices
