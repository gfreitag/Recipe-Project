Title: Green Beans Provencal
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Dried basil leaves
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 1/2 lb Fresh green beans
1    Large tomato, peeled and cho
1/2 c  Green onion, chopped
Melt butter in medium skillet. Add beans, then remaining ingredients.
Cover and cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until beans are
tender (10-15 minutes) serves
6
