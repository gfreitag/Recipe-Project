Title: White Salsa
Categories: Appetizer, Dip, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
4    Cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 c  Chopped scallions
1 cn (15 oz) sliced black olives
1/2 t  Tabasco
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Sour cream
3    Limes, juice only
1 1/2 c  Chopped cilantro
Mix and refrigerate. Let flavors marry for at least 4 hours
