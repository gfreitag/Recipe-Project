Title: Banana-Chip Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin, Fruit, Chocolate
Yield: 12 muffins
1/3 c  Butter, melted
1 c  Milk
1    Banana, chopped
1 c  Chocolate chips
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Sugar
1    Egg, beaten
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

Sift the first four ingredients together.  Stir in the egg, butter and
milk, just until blended.  Fold in the banana and chocolate chips.
Fill paper lined muffin tins full and bake appx. 20 minutes.  Better
eaten cool.
