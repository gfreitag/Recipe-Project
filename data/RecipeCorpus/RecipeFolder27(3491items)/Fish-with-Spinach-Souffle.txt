Title: Fish with Spinach Souffle
Categories: Fish, Main dish, Vegetable
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
4 oz Cheddar cheese, grated
1    Frozen spinach souffle
4    Fish fillets
I credit this recipe to Mary Brent, once director of the New Beginning
Center that I used to volunteer at.

Thaw spinach souffle, halfway. cut into 4 pieces. wrap a fillet
around each piece of souffle. Place in a greased 8 x 8" baking dish.
Pour soup over the top. Sprinkle cheese on top of soup. Bake in 350
degree oven for 25 to 30 minutes.
