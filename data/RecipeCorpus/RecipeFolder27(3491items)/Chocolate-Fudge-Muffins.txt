Title: Chocolate Fudge Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 12 muffins
1    Egg, beaten
1/3 c  Shortening, melted
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Sour cream
3/4 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
3/4 c  Boiling water
1 c  Chocolate chips
1/4 c  Butter
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sugar
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

Combine the boiling water, chocolate chips and butter and melt over
low heat, stirring constantly.  Set aside.

Sifr together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.  Add the egg,
shortening, milk, sour cream and melted chocolate mixture.  Blend
until just moistened.  Fold in the walnuts.  Fill paper lined muffin
tins full with batter, sprinkle with remaining chocolate chips and
bake for about 20 minutes.
