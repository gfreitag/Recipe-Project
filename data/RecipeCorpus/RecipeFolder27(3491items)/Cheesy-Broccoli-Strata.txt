Title: Cheesy Broccoli Strata
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
2 1/4 c  Milk
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 ts Barbecue spice
1/2 ts Garlic powder
Paprika
2    Tomatoes, cut in wedges
12 sl Day old bread, trimmed and
-cubed
1 pk (10 oz.) frozen, chopped
-broccoli, thawed & drained
1 pk (8 oz.) shredded mozzerella
3    Eggs, slightly beaten
Layer bread, broccoli, and half the cheese in buttered 8 x 12" glass
baking dish. Combine eggs, milk, mustard, spice and salt; pour over
strata. Refrigerate, covered, for several hours or overnight.
Sprinkle with paprika. Bake, uncovered, in a 350 degree oven for 35
minutes. Add remaining cheese and tomato wedges; bake until knife
inserted in middle comes out clean, 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
Cut in squares. Submitted By MARY RIEMERMAN   On   02-10-95
