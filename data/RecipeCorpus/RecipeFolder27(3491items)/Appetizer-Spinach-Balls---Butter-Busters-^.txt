Title: Appetizer Spinach Balls - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizer, Pickles
Yield: 20 servings
1/3 c  Alpine Lace fatfree
-Parmesan cheese
1/2 t  Lite salt, optional
1/2 t  Pepper
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1/2 t  Thyme
2 c  Pepperidge Farms stuffing
-mix
2 ea Pk chopped spinach, cooked
-and drained
1 c  Egg Beaters
1 c  Liquid Butter Buds
Mix all ingredients and chill for one hour. Shape into one inch balls
(no larger) and bake immediately or freeze on a cookie sheet. Store
frozen balls in a plastic bag. Bake 15 minutes at 325F, or 30 minutes
if frozen. Makes 80 balls (4 balls per serving) Per serving: 53 cal.,
0.3g fat (5%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 3g pro., 6g carb., 200mg sod.
