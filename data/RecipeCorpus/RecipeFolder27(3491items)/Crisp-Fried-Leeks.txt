Title: Crisp-Fried Leeks
Categories: Vegetable, Filipino
Yield: 4 servings
-salt
-oil for deep-frying
1 bn Leeks (white part only)
-flour
Wash leeks and cut into fine julienne. Season to taste with salt.
Dredge in flour. Heat oil to 375 degrees. Fry leeks, small handful at
a time, until crisp and golden. Do not over-brown. Drain on paper
towels. Makes 4 servings. Source: Unknown (snagged from the echo)
