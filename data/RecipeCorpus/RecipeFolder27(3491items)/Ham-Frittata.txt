Title: Ham Frittata
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Meat, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Black Pepper
1 c  Potatoes; cubed
1/4 c  Sliced Mushrooms
2 tb Green Pepper; chopped
8 oz Egg Substitute
1/2 c  Lean Ham
1 tb Dijon Mustard
1 ts Parsley Flakes
Preheat broiler.  In bowl, combine egg product, ham, mustard,
parsley and black pepper.  Set aside.
Preheat skillet, sprayed with vegetable oil spray, over medium heat.
Saute potatoes, mushrooms and green pepper until tender, stirring
occasionally. Pour egg product mixture over vegetables. Cook four to
five minutes or until bottom of skillet is set. Place skillet in
broiler 6 inches from heat.  Broil four or five minutes or until
lightly browned. Calories 84 Fat 2g This recipe is from the Houston
Post - Janice Schindeler.
