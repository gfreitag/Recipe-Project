Title: HAMBURGER TRIO SKILLET
Categories: Beef
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  COOKED RICE
1/4 t  THYME
1 pt MEAT SAUCE MIX, THAWED
17 oz WHOLE KERNAL CORN
1/2 c  CHOPPED GREEN PEPPER
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS IN A MEDIUM SKILLET.  COVER AND COOK OVER
MEDIUM HEAT ABOUT 10 TO 15 MINUTES, UNTIL HEATED THROUGH. SERVE FROM
SKILLET. *** Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   02-19-95
