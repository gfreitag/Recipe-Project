Title: Raw Tomato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Extra virgin olive oil
2 T  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Shredded basil leaves, fresh
1 c  Sour cream
6    Whole fresh basil leaves
3 lb Very ripe tomatoes
1 t  Thyme leaves (optional)
1/2 t  Tabasco sauce
1/2 t  Freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 t  Salt
Cut the tomatoes into pieces and puree them in a food processor.
Strain the pruee through a food mill fitted with the fine-mesh screen
(this should yield about 5 cups).  Stir in the thyme leaves, Tabasco,
pepper, salt, oil, vinegar, and shredded basil.  Set aside in a cool
place or refrigerate.

At serving time, divide the soup among six bowls, and serve each with
a dollop of sour cream in the center and a whole basil leaf on top.

Source: Jacques Pepin
