Title: Pudding Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 18 servings
2/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
2    Eggs
4 oz Chocolate pudding
1/2 c  Unsifted flour
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/3 c  Butter
Combine: Flour, pudding mix and baking powder. Mix well. Melt butter
in sauce pan. Remove from heat. Beat eggs in one at a time. Blend in
vanilla and pudding mixture. Spread in 8" square pan and bake at
350~F for 35 minutes. Do not overbake. Cool in pan. Ice or cut into
bars.
