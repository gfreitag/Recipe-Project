Title: Sweet & Sour Pork Philippine Island
Categories: Filipino, Main dish, Low-cal/fat, Pork
Yield: 1 servings
Equal 1/2 ts sugar
1 tb Vinegar
1/2 c  Cooked enriched rice
1 md Tomato, cut into 6 wedges
Salt to taste
1/2 ts Chopped fresh parsley
8 oz Lean boneless pork loin
1/2 c  Chicken bouillon
1/4 md Green pepper, diced
ds Garlic powder
1/2 c  Canned crushed pineapple,
No sugar added
Artificial sweetener to
Broil pork 4 inches from source of heat 5 minutes on each side.
Remove; cool; cut into 1-inch cubes. Place in a nonstick skillet with
bouillon, pepper and garlic. Simmer, stirring, until liquid has
reduced to half its volume. Add pineapple, sweetener and vinegar.
Fold in rice and tomato. Season to taste. Heat thoroughly over low
flame, stirring gently, until liquid is absorbed. Sprinkle with
parsley. *From Weight Watchers International Cookbook*
