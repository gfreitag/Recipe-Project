Title: Orange and Anise (Fennel) Salad
Categories: Salad, Fruit, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/4    Red onion thinly sliced
2 T  Olive oil
Salt and fresh ground pepper
1 md Anise bulb, thinly sliced
(also called fennel)
4    Oranges, peeled and sliced
Combine anise, oranges, and red onions.

Drizzle with olive oil and season to taste with salt and pepper.

Toss gently and serve.

1 Cup = 137 calories, 7g fat (1g saturated), 0 mg cholesterol, 5 g
fiber, and 53 mg sodium.
