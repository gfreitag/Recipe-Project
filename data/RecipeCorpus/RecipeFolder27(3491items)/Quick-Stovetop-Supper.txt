Title: Quick Stovetop Supper
Categories: Main dish, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Dry mustard
MEAT (see below)
1/2 c  VEGETABLE (see below)
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms
1/4 c  Chopped onions
1 cn Condensed SOUP (See below)
MMMMM----------------------SOUP (CHOOSE ONE---------------------------
Cream of chicken
Cheddar cheese
Cream of mushroom
Cream of celery
MMMMM----------------------MEAT (CHOOSE ONE---------------------------
1 c  Cooked chicken cut into
Strips
1 cn Tuna, drained and flaked
4 oz Dried beef, rinsed and
Cut into strips
1 c  Cooked ham cut into strips
1 c  Cooked turkey cut into strip
MMMMM-------------------VEGETABLE (CHOOSE ONE------------------------
Drained, cooked diced
Carrots
Packed chopped raw spinach
Drained cooked peas
Drained, cooked cut green
Beans
MMMMM-----------------ACCOMPANIMENT (CHOOSE ONE----------------------
Hot cooked noodles
Baked patty shells
Toast points
Baked potatoes, split
In two quart saucepan over medium heat, in hot butter, cook mushrooms
and onion until tender, stirring occasionally.  Stir in SOUP, milk
and mustard until smooth.  Stir in MEAT and VEGETABLE.  Heat through,
stirring occasionally.  Serve over ACCOMPANIMENT.
