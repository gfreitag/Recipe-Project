Title: Molly McButter Lite* Fettucine Alfredo
Categories: Pasta, Salsa/sauce, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  + 2 tsp. Molly McB Natural
-Butter flavor sprinkles
4 c  Hot cooked fettuccine
2 t  Chopped fresh parsley
Freshly ground pepper
2 sm Cloves garlic, minced
1 1/3 c  Skim milk, divided
2 t  All purpose flour
2 T  Fat free cream cheese
1 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
In medium saucepan, combine minced garlic and 1 cup skim milk.  Bring
to a boil.  Reduce heat to simmer. Stir in flour and remaining 1/3
cup milk. Continue stirring for about 2 minutes until thickened. Add
fat free cream cheese.  Whisk vigorously until smooth  Cook for 2
minutes. Add grated Parmesan cheese; whisk until smooth. Remove from
heat. Stir in Molly McButter. Pour the sauce over hot cooked
fettuccine. Garnish with fresh parsley and black pepper. (Makes 4 1
cup servings) * 66% less fat than a regular Fettucine Alfredo recipe.
Fat content has been reduced from 15 g to 5g per serving.  Calories
310, Fat 5g, Sodium 550 Shared By: Pat Stockett
