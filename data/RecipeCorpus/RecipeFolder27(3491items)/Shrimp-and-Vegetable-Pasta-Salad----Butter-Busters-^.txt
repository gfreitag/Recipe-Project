Title: Shrimp and Vegetable Pasta Salad -  Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
1 ea Tb wine vinegar
1 ea Tb plus
1 t  Yelow mustard
1 c  Kraft fatfree mayonnaise or
-Miracle Whip
2 t  Dried oregano
1 t  Minced garlic
3/4 t  Papa Dash lite salt,
-optional
1/2 t  Pepper
1 ea Pk (8oz) macaroni twists
1/2 ea Bn broccoli, broken into
-florets
1/2 md Head cauliflower, broken
-into florets
1 ea Dz cherry tomatoes, halved
1/3 c  Alpine Lace or Weight
-Watchers fatfree Parmesan
-cheese
1 sm Onion, diced
1/2 lb Cooked peeled shrimp
**DRESSING**
Combine all of the dressing ingredients in a blender and blend until
smooth (about 1 minute). Cover and refrigerate until ready to use.
Cook macaroni according to package directions. Cook broccoli in
boiling water until just tender. Run under cold water and drain.
Separate florets into smalll pieces. Cook cauliflower until fork
tender. Rinse under cold water. Drain until dry. Mix vegetables,
shrimp, macaroni, Parmesan cheese and onion. Mix with dressing and
chill at least one hour. Per serving: 123 cal., 0.8g fat (6%), 55mg
chol., 2g fiber, 11g pro., 18g carb., 469mg sod. Butter Busters by
