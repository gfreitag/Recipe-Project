Title: Sweet Potato Fruit Salad - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 10 servings
1 ea Tb lemon juice
1 ea Tb sugar
**OR**
1 1/2 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
1/4 t  Grated orange peel
1 ea Tb orange juice
3 c  Cooked, peeled and cubed
-sweet potatoes
2 c  Chopped red delicious apples
2 c  Seedless green grapes
2 c  Sliced bananas
3/4 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
Place the potatoes and fruit in a large bowl and mix together. Mix
all the other ingredients in a smaller bowl and pour over potatoes
and fruit to coat. Chill before serving. Per sugar serving: 127 cal.,
0.6g fat (4%), 0mg chol., 4g fiber, 2g pro., 30g carb., 20mg sod. Per
S&L serving: 123 cal., 29g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN
