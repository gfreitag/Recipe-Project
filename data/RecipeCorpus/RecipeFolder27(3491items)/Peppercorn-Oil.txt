Title: Peppercorn Oil
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 batch
3 tb Cracked black peppercorns or
-drained green peppercorns
20    Fluid ounces olive or
-vegetable oil
Place the ingredients in a bottle or glass jar.  Keep for at least a
week before using.
