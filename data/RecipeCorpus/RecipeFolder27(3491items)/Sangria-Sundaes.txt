Title: Sangria Sundaes
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
-peeled, segmented, halved
1/2 ts Lime juice
3 c  Vanilla frozen yogurt
1/2 c  Dry red wine
3 tb Orange marmalade
2    Clementines or tangerines
In small saucepan, bring wine to a boil over medium-high heat. cook
until reduced to 2 tbs, 6-8 minutes. Add marmalade and cook until
thickened, 1-2 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in clementines or
tangerines and lime juice. Let cool slightly.

To serve, scoop frozen yogurt into 4 dessert dishes and spoon reserved
sauce on top.
