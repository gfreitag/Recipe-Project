Title: Onion Bread (Machine)
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Dry onion soup mix (1 oz)
1 1/2 tb Nonfat dry milk
1 1/2 tb Butter/margarine
1 c  Plus 3 T + 1 t water
1 3/4 ts Active dry yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
Add ingredients in the order listed. All ingredients must be at room
temperature. Select yeast bread and baking control for medium. Press
start. Bread will be ready in 4 hours and 4 minutes.

The humble onion is found in every cuisine. Its most famous role is in
French onion soup. Today dried onion soup mix is added to recipes more
often than served as soup. So, we tried it in yeast dough. The
resulting bread satisfies the most extreme craving for onions.

From "Easy Baking" by Claudia Burns and Tom Lacalamita for Welbilt
