Title: Foil Grilled Potatoes (Miracle Whip)
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 t  Pepper
3 ea Potatoes, cut in 1/4" slices
1 lg Onion, sliced
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
3 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 t  Paprika
1/4 t  Salt
Heat Grill.  Mix dressing, garlic and seasonings in large bowl.
Add potatoes and onion; toss to coat.
Divide potato mixture evenly among six 12" square pieces of
foil. Seal each to form packet.
Place foil packets on grill over medium-hot coals.  Grill,
covered, 25 - 30 min. or until potatoes are tender.

VARIATION:  Omit grilling. Heat oven to 425.  Place packets on
center rack in oven.  Bake 30 - 35 min. or until tender.
