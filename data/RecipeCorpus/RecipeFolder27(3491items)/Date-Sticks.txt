Title: Date Sticks
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
3 ea Eggs
1 ts Baking powder
Salt; a pinch
1 c  Dates
1 c  Nut meats
1 c  Sugar
1 1/4 c  Flour
Bake in sheet 15 minutes, let cool. Cut into strips. Dust with
pulverized sugar.

Note: No temperature given. Assume a moderate 350 F. oven.

Source: Margaret Butcher, Wadsworth Grange, Medina County, OH
