Title: Jelly Top Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Cloves
1 ts Baking soda
2 tb Water; hot
2 ea Egg whites
Firm jelly; for centers
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Molasses
1 c  Lard
2 ea Egg yolks
1 tb Vinegar
1/2 ts Ginger
Use 1 level t. soda dissolved in 2 T. hot water. Flour to make a very
stiff dough. Roll, cut and dip in egg white, then in granulated
sugar. Put firm jelly in center or each cookie. Watch closely as they
will burn easily.

Note: Combine all ingredients except egg whites. Use additional
granulated sugar for dipping. No temperature given, but probably 375
F.

Source: Laura Lehew, Maple Grange, Hardin County, OH
