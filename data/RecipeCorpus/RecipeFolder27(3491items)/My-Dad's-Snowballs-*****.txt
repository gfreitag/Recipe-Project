Title: My Dad's Snowballs *****
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Nuts/grains
Yield: 5 dozen
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1 c  Rice krispies
1 c  Peanut butter
1 c  Icing sugar
2 tb Butter
Directions Mix ingredients together and form a ball. Dip in thin
icing made with icing sugar,a little butter and a few drops of
milk.Then roll in coconut. Chill.
