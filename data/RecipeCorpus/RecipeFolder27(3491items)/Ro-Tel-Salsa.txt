Title: Ro-Tel Salsa
Categories: Relish
Yield: 1 servings
1    Large Tomato; chopped
1/2    Lime; juice only
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Ro-Tel Tomatoes w/Chilies
2    Green Onions; chopped fine
1    Garlic Clove; minced
Mix and enjoy.  To increase heat, add canned or fresh jalapenos to
taste. This recipe came from the back of a can of Ro-Tel Tomatoes
w/Green Chilies.
