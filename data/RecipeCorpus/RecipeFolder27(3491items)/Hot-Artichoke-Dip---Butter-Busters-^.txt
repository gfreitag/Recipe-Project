Title: Hot Artichoke Dip - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Appetizer, Pickles
Yield: 12 servings
1 t  Lemon juice
1/4 t  Garlic powder
1 ds Tabasco
1 c  Nonfat mozzarella cheese,
-grated
2 cn (14oz) artichoke hearts,
-drained and chopped
1 c  Kraft farfree mayonnaise or
-Miracle Whip
1/2 c  Allpine Lace fatfree
-Parmesan cheese
In a large bowl, stir al of the ingredients together. Spoon into a
casserole dish that has been sprayed with a nonfat cooking spray.
Bake at 350F 15-20 minutes. Serve with fatfree saltine crackers as a
spread. Per 1/4 cup serving: 52 cal., 0.1g fat (2%), 0mg chol., 1g
fiber, 3g pro., 11g carb., 271mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie
