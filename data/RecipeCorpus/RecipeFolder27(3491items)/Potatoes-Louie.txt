Title: Potatoes Louie
Categories: Vegetable, Casserole
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Stock or water
Salt and pepper to taste
Bouquet garni
6 md Potatoes
2 tb Butter
1/4 lb Lean bacon
2 tb Flour
Wash, peel and cube the potatoes. Melt butter in a heavy saucepan.
Dice the bacon and fry lightly in the butter till golden brown. Stir
in flour, and when it begins to darken, add stock, bouquet garni and
then potatoes. cover and simmer for 25 to 30 minutes.
