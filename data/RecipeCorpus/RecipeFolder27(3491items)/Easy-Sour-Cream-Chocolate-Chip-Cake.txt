Title: Easy Sour Cream Chocolate Chip Cake
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Baking soda
ds Salt
1 c  Walnuts or pecans
-- chopped
1 1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate
Chips (9 oz)
4 oz Butter, softened
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 c  Sour cream
2 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:55

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. In a large bowl, beat together butter,
sugar, and eggs with an electric mixer on medium speed until light
and fluffy, 3 to 4 mins. Beat in sour cream and vanilla until well
blended. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt; beat until
well mixed. Stir in nuts and chocolate chips. Turn batter into a
well-greased 12-cup bundt pan.

2. Bake 45 to 55 mins, or until golden and a cake tester inserted in
center comes out clean. Let cake cool in pan 15 mins, then unmold
onto a serving plate to cool completely.
