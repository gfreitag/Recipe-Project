Title: Peanut Butter Milk Chocolate Chippers
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 20 servings
2 ts Vanilla extract
1    Egg
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Milk chocolate chips
1/2 c  Chunky peanut butter
1    Stick butter or margarine
-- softened
3/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:25 1. Preheat oven to 350 F. In a medium bowl,
beat peanut butter, butter, and sugars with an electric mixer on
medium until light and fluffy, 2 to 3 mins. Add vanilla and egg; beat
until well blended. Add flour and baking soda; mix well. Stir in
chocolate chips. 2. Drop dough by heaping tablespoonfuls into mounds
2 inches apart on lightly greased cookie sheets. Flatten cookies
slightly. Bake 10 to 12 mins, or until golden. 3. Let cookies cool 5
mins on sheets before removing to wire racks to cool completely.

NOTES:  A great combination that is irresistible when served warm.
