Title: NO PEEK BEEF CASSEROLE
Categories: Crockpot, Beef
Yield: 4 servings
Combine all ingredients in
-CROCK-POT. Stir together
-well
1 ea 10 1/2-ounce can cream of
Mushroom soup
1 ea 4-ounce can whole
2 lb Stew beef,
Cut into 1 -inch pieces
1 1 3/8-ounce envelope
Dry onion soup mix
-mushrooms
1/2 c  Red wine
Cover and cook on Low 8 to 12 hours. Serve over noodles or rice.
(High: 3 to 4 hours). Serves 4.  This recipe may be doubled. From
Rival Crock-pot cook book, date unknown From the files of Earl Shelsby
