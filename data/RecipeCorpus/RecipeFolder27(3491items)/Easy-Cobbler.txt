Title: Easy Cobbler
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Milk
2 c  Fruit
1/2 c  Oleo, one stick
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Flour, self-rising
Melt oleo in casserole pan. Mix sugar and flour in separate bowl. Add
milk and fruit to dry mixture. Pour into pan and bake for about 25
minutes.

HINT: for fresh fruit increase sugar to 1 and 1/2 cups.

Recipe from Grandmother Murray and Aunt Nell
