Title: Spicebox Cookies
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 30 cookies
1/4 t  Cloves
1/4 t  Allspice
1/4 t  Vanilla
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
2 c  Basic cookie mix
1    Egg
1/4 t  Baking soda
1/4 t  Cinnamon
In a medium bowl, combine cookie mix, egg, baking soda, cinnamon,
cloves, allspice, and vanilla.  Mix well. Stir in chopped nuts. Shape
into a long 2" diameter roll. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate
3 hours or overnight.  Preheat oven to 375. Lightly grease baking
sheets. Cut roll into 1/8" slices and place on prepared baking
sheets. Bake 8 to 10 minutes. Cool on wire rack.
