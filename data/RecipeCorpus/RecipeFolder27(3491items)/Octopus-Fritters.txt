Title: Octopus Fritters
Categories: Seafood, Native amer, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
2 md Onions, peeled and minced
2    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Flour or more as needed
Salt and pepper no taste
Oil for fryiwg
Ingredients:
2    Octopus about 1 1/2 poands
-each, cleaned
1 ts Salt
2 qt Water
2 qt Ice water with ice
Directions: Drop the octopus into a large kettle with rapidly boiling
salted water. Cook on medium-high heat for about 25 minutes. Drain and
plunge into a bowl filled with ice and ice water. With a coarse brush
scrape away act of the purple skin. Cut off the legs and chop fine.
Discard the heads. In a bowl mix together onions, eggs, flour and
salt and pepper. Add chopped octopus, and blend well. Form mixture
into 2 1/2 - 3 inch flat patties. Heat about 1/2 inch of oil in a
large heavy skillet, and fry the octopus fritters until well browned
on each side. Serve immediately.

Serves 8
