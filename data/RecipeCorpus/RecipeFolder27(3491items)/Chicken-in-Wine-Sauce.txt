Title: Chicken in Wine Sauce
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 cl Garlic, minced
1 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Dried oregano
1 tb Firmly packed brown sugar
1 1/4 lb Boneless skinless chicken
1 c  Rose wine
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Plus 2 ts vegetable oil
2 tb Water
In a cover baking dish or casserole, combine all ingredients. Bake,
covered, one hour. Refrigerate overnight. Reheat before serving.

Per serving: 34g protein, 11g fat, 5g carb., 1419mg sodium, 82mg
chol., 275 calories.
