Title: Chocolate Whipped Cream
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Heavy cream
1 ts Vanilla extract
3 tb Unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tb Powdered sugar
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:10 In a small bowl, whisk together cocoa and
powdered sugar until well blended. Place cream in a medium bowl and
whisk in cocoa and powdered sugar. Whisk in vanilla. With chilled
beaters, beat cream with and electric mixer on high speed until stiff
or desired consistency.
