Title: Marinated wingflingers
Categories: Appetizer, Chicken, Marinade
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Dry white wine
2 c  Chicken broth
1/2    Bag wingflingers
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  White sugar
preheat oven to 400 mix sauce ingredients tog. stir in wingflingers
and marinate-1 hour before cooking. back in sauce for 45 mins
