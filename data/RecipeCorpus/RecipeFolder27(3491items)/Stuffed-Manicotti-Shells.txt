Title: Stuffed Manicotti Shells
Categories: Mixes, Pasta, Cheese/eggs, Meat, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1 T  Parsley flakes
4 c  Italian meat sauce mix
Romano and/or parmesan cheee
12    Manicotti shells, cooked
Water
Salt
1 pt Ricotta cheese
1    Egg, beaten
Cook manicotti in boiling salted water according to package
directions. In medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, eggs, parmesan
cheese and parsley flakes. Blend well. Stuff into cooked manicotti
shells. Preheat oven to 350.  Place 1 cup of italian cooking sauce in
bottom of 13 x 9 baking pan. Place stuffed manicotti shells on top of
sauce. Pour remaining sauce over top of shells. Sprinkle with romano
and/or parmesan cheese, for garnish. Cover with foil and bake 30
minutes, until heated through. Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On
02-12-95
