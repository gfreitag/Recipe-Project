Title: Macaroni and Bean Bake - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 9 ----------
-stewed tomatoes
1 ea Green pepper, diced
1 ea Red pepper, diced
2 ea Tb fatfree Parmesan cheese
-(Alpine Lace or Weight
-Watchers)
1 ea Pk (7oz) macaroni, cooked
-and drained
2 c  Nonfat Cheddar cheese,
-grated
1 cn (16oz) pinto, Ranch or red
-beans, undrained
1 cn (16oz) Italian or Mexican
Heat oven to 375F. Spray a 9" square glass baking dish with a nonfat
cooking spray. Combine macaroni, 1 1/2 cups Cheddar cheese, beans,
tomatoes and peppers; mix well. Spoon into prepared dish. Sprinkle
with remaining cheddar and Parmesan cheese. Bake 25-30 minutes or
until heated through. Let stand 10 minutes before serving. Per
serving: 155 cal., 0.7g fat (4%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 9g pro., 28g
carb., 434mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5
