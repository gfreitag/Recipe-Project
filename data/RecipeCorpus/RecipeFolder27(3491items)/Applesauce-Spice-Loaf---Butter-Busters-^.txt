Title: Applesauce Spice Loaf - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Bread
Yield: 14 servings
1/2 c  Liquid Butter Buds or
-Promise Ultra fatfree
-margarine
1/2 c  Raisins
**POWDERED SUGAR GLAZE**
1/2 c  Sifted powdered sugar**
2 t  Hot water or skim milk
2 c  Flour
1 t  Baking soda
1 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Baking powder
1 c  Applesauce
1 c  Sugar*
3/4 c  Egg Beaters
*OR 1/2 cup sugar and 6 packets Sweet 'n Low **1/4 cup powdered sugar
and 2 packets S&L Glaze: blend together and drizzle over top of
cooled cake In a small bowl, combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon and
baking powder. In a large bowl, blend applesauce, sugar, Egg Beaters
and liquid Butter buds; stir inflour mixture just until blended
(batter will be lumpy). Mix in raisins. Spoon batter into a 9 x 5 x
3" loaf pan that has been sprayed with Pam. Bake at 350F 55-60
minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool
and top with glaze. Per sugar serving: 153 cal., 0.6g fat (3%), 0mg
chol., 1g fiber, 3g pro., 34g carb., 138mg sod. Per S&L serving: 131
cal., 28g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5
