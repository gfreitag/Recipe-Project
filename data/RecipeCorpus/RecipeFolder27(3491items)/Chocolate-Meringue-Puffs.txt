Title: Chocolate Meringue Puffs
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 42 cookies
-- unsifted
1/2 ts White vinegar
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
6 oz Semi-sweet chocolate pieces
2    Egg whites
1 ds Salt
3/4 c  C and H Powdered Sugar
Melt chocolate over low heat.  Set aside.  Beat egg whites with salt
until foamy.  Gradually add sugar beating continuously until stiff
and glossy. Beat in vinegar and vanilla.  Fold in melted chocolate
and nuts.  Drop by teaspoonfuls on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake in
350-degree oven 10 minutes.  Cool on rack.  Makes about 3-1/2 dozen
cookies.
