Title: Baked Lasagne (Traditional)
Categories: Italian, Pasta, Main dish, Meat, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
32 oz Spaghetti sauce
15 oz Ricotta dry curd or drained
-cottage cheese
3/4 lb Mozzarella cheese, cubed or
-shredded
1/4 c  Romano cheese
8 oz Lasagna noodles
1 lb Ground beef, Italian
-sausage or Pork sausage
1/4 c  Onion, finely chopped
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic salt
Cook lasagne according to package direction. In large skillet, saute
meat, onion, salt and garlic salt; drain off excess fat. Add
spaghetti sauce. Bring to boil, reduce heat, simmer 15-20 minutes. In
greased 9 X 13 inch baking pan, layer 3 strips of lasagne,
lengthwise, spread on 1/3 meat sauce, 1/3 dry curd (or cottage
cheese), and 1/3 Mozzarella. Repeat layering process twice, sprinkle
Romano cheese on top. Bake 350 degree F oven for 35-40 minutes. Let
set 10 minutes before cutting. Submitted By ED FAUSS   On   02-05-95
