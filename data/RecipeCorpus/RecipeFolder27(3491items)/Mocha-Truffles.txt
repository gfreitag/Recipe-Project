Title: Mocha Truffles
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 5 1/2 dozen
1 lb Dark chocolate confectionary
-coating
White confectionary coating,
-optional
24 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
3 tb Instant coffee granules
2 ts Water
In a microwave-safe bowl or double boiler, melt chocolate chips.  Add
cream cheese, coffee and water; mix well.  Chill until firm enough to
shape. Shape into 1 inch balls and place on a waxed paper-lined
cookie sheet. Chill for 1 to 2 hours or until firm.  Melt chocolate
coating in microwave-safe bowl or double boiler.  Dip balls and place
on waxed paper to harden.  If desired, melt white coating and drizzle
over truffles. Yield: about 5 1/2 dozen

NOTE:  Truffles can be frozen for several months before dipping in
chocolate.  Thaw in the refrigerator before dipping.
