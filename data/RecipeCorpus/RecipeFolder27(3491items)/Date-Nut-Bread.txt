Title: Date Nut Bread
Categories: Bread
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Sliced dates
1 c  Coarsely chopped nuts
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Molasses
1 1/2 c  White flour
5 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 c  Graham or whole wheat flour
Pit dates and cut into 5 or 6 slices with scissors or sharp knife.
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder, salt, and baking
soda. Add sugar, milk, and molasses.  Add graham flour in which dates
and nuts have been dredged.  Beat until thoroughly mixed. Pour into
well-oiled loaf pan. Bake in moderate oven (375Ã¸ F) 1 hour. 1 loaf.
