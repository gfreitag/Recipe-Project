Title: Walnut Raspberry Brownie
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
1/3 c  Raspberry jam
3    Unsweetened chocolate sqs
1/2 c  Crisco
3    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
Melt chocolate and Crisco over warm water; cool slightly. Blend
together eggs, vanilla, sugar and salt; stir in chocolate mixture,
then flour. Fold in walnuts. Turn into well greased 8-inch square
pan. Bake at 325~ for about 40 minutes. Spoon jam over hot brownies;
spread carefully. Let cool. Top with Velvet Chocolate Glaze.
