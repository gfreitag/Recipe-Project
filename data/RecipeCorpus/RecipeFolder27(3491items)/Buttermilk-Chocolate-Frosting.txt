Title: Buttermilk Chocolate Frosting
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Buttermilk
4 c  Powdered sugar
4 tb Butter; softened
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:05

In a medium bowl, beat together butter and cocoa with an electric
mixer on low speed until smooth, 1 to 2 mins. Add buttermilk and
powdered sugar and beat on high until smooth and fluffy, 1 to 2 mins.
