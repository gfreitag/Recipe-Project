Title: Peanut Butter Fudge Balls
Categories: Candy
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Sesame seeds
1/4 c  Wheat germ
1/4 c  Honey
1 c  Crunchy peanut butter
1/2 c  Milnot
3/4 c  Raisins
Mix all ingredients and roll into 1 inch balls.  Store in
refrigerator. Randy Rigg
