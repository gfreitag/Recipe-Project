Title: French Toast
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs, Fruit, Breakfast
Yield: 2 servings
2 ts Strawberry jam, low sugar or
-any low sugar fruit jam
4    Egg whites (lightly beaten)
1/4 c  Cinnamon applesauce
4 sl Whole grain bread
Lightly oil non-stick skillet and heat on medium. Lightly beat
together egg whites and applesauce by hand. Soak each slice of bread
on both sides. Brown bread in non-stick skillet on both sides. Top
each slice with 1 teaspoon strawberry jam. Serves 2 Calories: 100 (1
slice) Fat: 1 gm 9% fat
