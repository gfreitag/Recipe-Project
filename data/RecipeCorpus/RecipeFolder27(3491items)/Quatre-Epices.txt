Title: Quatre-Epices
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------FRANCE--------------------------------
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Dried ginger
5 ts Black peppercorns
2 ts Grated nutmeg
Grind all the ingredients to a fine powder. Use in dishes that need
long simmering, such as stews. Stored in an airtight container, it
will keep for 3-4 months.
