Title: Sour Cream Apple Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert
Yield: 1 pie
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
5 lg Tart apples
1    Pastry-lined 9" pie pan
1 tb Lemon juice
3/4 c  C and H Granulated Sugar
1/3 c  Flour
Peel and cut apples into thick slices.  Arrange slices in overlapping
rows in pastry-lined pie pan; sprinkle with lemon juice.  Mix sugar,
flour, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg; cut in butter until crumbly; spoon
over apples. Spread sour cream over top.  Bake in 350 F oven 50 to 55
minutes until apples are tender.
