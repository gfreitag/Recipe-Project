Title: Gumbo
Categories: Soup/stew, Main dish, Vegetable, Seafood, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 servings
Hot peppers to taste
Hot sauce to taste
1 md Onion, chopped
1 ea Bay leaf
2 tb Flour
4 ea Crabs
3 lb Okra, cut up
1 cl Garlic, minced
1 cn Tomato paste (small)
1 qt Water
2 lb Shrimp, peeled
1 cn Stewed tomatoes
Combine okra, garlic, tomato paste, stewed tomatoes, onion , bay
leaf, and water.  Cook slowly until done.  Brown flour, add enough
water to make a roux.  Add roux, shrimp, crabs, and season to taste.

Cook slowly until done.
