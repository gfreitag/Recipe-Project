Title: Orange Chocolate Crunch
Categories: Chocolate, Fruit, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Walnuts; chopped
6 oz Semi sweet chocolate chips
2 c  Orange sections; about 6
- oranges
1/2 c  Orange juice
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Almond extract
1 2/3 c  Flour
Preheat oven to 350~. Peel and section oranges. Cut sections into
small pieces. Combine the orange pieces and orange juice. Beat eggs
and add oranges, vanilla and almond extract. Mix well. Sift dry
ingredients together and add all at once. Pour into a greased and
floured 9x13 pan. Combine the brown sugar, chocolate chips and nuts.
Sprinkle on top of cake. Bake at 350~ for 45-50 minutes.
