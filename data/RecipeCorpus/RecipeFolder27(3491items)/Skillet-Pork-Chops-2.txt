Title: Skillet Pork Chops 2
Categories: Pork, Main dish, Tomato, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
6    Onions, sliced
6    Green pepper rings
6    Pork chops
1 cn Tomato soup
1/4 c  Water
In skillet, brown chops; pour off fat.  Season with salt & pepper.
Stir in soup & water. Top each chop with onion and green pepper.
Cover; simmer 30 min. or until done. Stir occasionally.  Makes 6
servings. Submitted By SUE LASTER   On   02-08-95 (1745)
