Title: Polverones (Mexican wedding cookies)
Categories: Tex/mexican, Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 2 doz.  serv
2 c  All-purpose flour, sifted
3/4 c  Nuts, finely chopped
Confectioner's sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Shortening
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Confectioners' sugar
Preheat oven to 425 deg f.  In medium mixing bowl, cream butter or
margarine, shorthening, and vanilla until fluffy.  Mix the 1/2 cup
confectioner's sugar, flour, and nuts togethert; add to creamed
mixture, stirring to form a soft dough.  Shape dough into small balls
and place on ungreased baking sheet.  Bake cookies about 10 minutes,
or until lightly brown.  Roll warm cookies in confectioner's sugar.
