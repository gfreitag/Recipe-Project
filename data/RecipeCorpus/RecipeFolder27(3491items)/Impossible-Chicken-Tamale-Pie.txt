Title: Impossible Chicken Tamale Pie
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Main dish, Chicken, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Frozen Corn; Thaw, Drain
3/4 c  Bisquick
3 x  Eggs
Sour Cream
Shredded Lettuce
1 1/2 c  Cut-up Cooked Chicken
1 pk Taco Seasoning
1 cn 4oz Green Chilies; Chop,
-Drain
1 1/4 c  Milk
Heat oven to 400~.  Grease 10" pie plate.  Mix chicken, corn and Taco
mix; spread in plate.  Sprinkle with chilies and cheese. Beat
remaining ingredients until smooth, 15 seconds in blender on high or
1 minute with hand beater.  Pour into plate.  Bake until knife
inserted halfway between center and edge comes out clean, 25 to 30
minutes. Cool 5 minutes. Serve with sour cream and lettuce.
