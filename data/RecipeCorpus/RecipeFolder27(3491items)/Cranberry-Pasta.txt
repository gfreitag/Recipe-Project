Title: Cranberry Pasta
Categories: Pasta, Fruit, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
2    2 1/2 Tbs cranberry juice
-concentrate
1 c  Durum semolina
1    Egg
Mix by hand, then shape and cook.  If using an extruder, add a little
more flour and a little less juice concentrate.
