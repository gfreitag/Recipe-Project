Title: Strawberry Fluff
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Cheese/eggs, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar, brown
4 T  Corn starch
1 c  Pecans, chopped (sm can)
2 pk Dreamed Whip
4 c  Strawberries, frozen
8 oz Cheese, PHILADELPHIA C.
3 T  Strawberry jello
1 c  Sugar, conf.
4 dr Food coloring, red
12 c  Butter, melted
1 c  Sugar
TOP LAYER DRAIN 2 CANS OF STRAWBERRIES,MIX JUICE WITH 3 TABLESPOON OF
STRAWBERRY JELLO 4 DROPS OF RED FOOD COLORING, 1 CUP OF SUGAR, 1/2 CUP
WATER, 4 TABLESPOONS CORNSTARCH. BRING TO A BOIL, STIRRING CONSTANTLY.
REMOVE FROM HEAT.  ADD THE BERRIES STIR. CHILL FOR 30 MINUTES.

THE FLUFFY MIX 2 PACKAGES OF DREAMED WHIP FOLLOWING PACKAGE
DIRECTIONS. MIX CREAM CHEESE WITH 1 CUP CONFECTIONERIES SUGAR, THEN
FOLD INTO DREAMED WHIP, SPREAD ON TOP OF CRUST. THE CRUST MIX 2
STICKS OF BUTTER MELTED, 1 CUP OF FLOUR, 1/2 CUP OF BROWN SUGAR 1
SMALL CAN OF PECANS CHOPPED. SPREAD INTO A 9x11 INCH PAN, BAKE AT 350
deg. FOR 10 TO 15 MINUTES. MAKE YOUR STRAWBERRIES , THEN YOUR CRUST,
THEN YOUR FLUFF
