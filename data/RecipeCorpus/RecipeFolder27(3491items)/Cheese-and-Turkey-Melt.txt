Title: Cheese and Turkey Melt
Categories: Main dish, Sandwich, Sthrn/livng, Turkey, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 sandwiches
12 sl Cheese, Cheddar
24 sl Turkey breast; cooked
12 sl Tomato
18 sl White bread
8    Eggs; beaten
Butter
Dip each slice of bread in beaten eggs; cook in butter on preheated
griddle until lightly browned.  Cover each of 12 bread slices with 1
slice cheese, 2 slices turkey, and 1 slice tomato. Continue grilling
the 12 bread slices slowly in butter untl cheese melts.  Stack 2
slices together and top with remaining bread.  Serve hot.
