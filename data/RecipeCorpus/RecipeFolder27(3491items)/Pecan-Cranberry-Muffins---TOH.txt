Title: Pecan Cranberry Muffins - TOH
Categories: Breakfast, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Muffin
Yield: 1 1/2 dozen
1/2 c  Butter/margarine
3    Eggs, lightly beaten
1 c  Milk
1 c  Pecans, chopped
1 tb Lemon peel, grated
1 1/2 c  Cranberries, chopped, fresh
-or frozen
1 1/4 c  Sugar, divided
3 c  Flour
4 1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
In a bowl, toss, cranberries with 1/4 cup sugar; set aside.  Combine
flour, baking powder, salt and remaining sugar.  Cut in butter until
the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  Combine eggs and milk; stir in
pecans, lemon peel and cranberries.  Fill greased or paper-lined
muffin cups 2/3's full. Bake at 400 for 20 to 25 minutes or until
muffins test done. Yield: about 1 1/2 dozen
