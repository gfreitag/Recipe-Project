Title: Shichimi Togarashi
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------JAPAN--------------------------------
-tangarine peel
3 ts Chili powder
1 ts Black sesame seeds
1 ts Poppy seeds
2 ts White sesame seeds
3 ts Sansho (Japanese pepper)
1 ts Small pieces dried laver
-(a seaweed called nori)
3 ts Flakes of dried
(Seven-Flavor or Seven-Spice Mix)

Grind the white sesame seeds and sansho coarsely. Add the laver and
dried tangarine peel and grind again briefly. Stir in the remaining
spices qand blend well. Stored in an airtight container, it will keep
for 3-4 months. Use as a table condiment to flavor soups, noodles and
grilled meats.
