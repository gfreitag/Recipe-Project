Title: Chocolate Quick Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
16 oz Chocolate Syrup
1 ts Vanilla
4    Egg
1 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking Powder
1/2 c  Butter
per serving:
Approx. Cook Time: 0:45 Cholesterol
Calories
grams per serving:
per serving: Marks:   E

*DIRECTIONS*

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a large bowl, mix all ingredients at
low until dry ingredients are moistened and then at medium until
smooth. Pour batter into 2 well-greased, 8-inch round layer cake pans
or one 9- x 13-inch pan. Bake for 25 minutes for layers or 30 minutes
for large cake or until toothpick inserted near center comes out
clean. When cool, frost with desired frosting.
