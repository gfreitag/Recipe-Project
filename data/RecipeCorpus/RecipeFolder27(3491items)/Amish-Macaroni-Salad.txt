Title: Amish Macaroni Salad
Categories: Salad, Pasta
Yield: 20 servings
1/4 c  Onions, finely chopped
6    Hard cooked Eggs, sliced
Paprika
1 lb Macaroni or baby sea shells
1 1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Chopped Celery
1/2 c  Carrots, finely grated
Cook macaroni as directed; drain and cool.  Set aside 1 hard cooked
egg; then stir together gently the cooked macaroni, celery, carrots,
Onion and the remaining eggs.

DRESSING: 1 pint Miracle Whip 1/4 cup Vinegar 3/4 cup Sugar 2
Tablespoons prepared mustard

Blend together; then fold into macaroni salad mixture.  Garnish with
the 1 hard cooked egg and paprika From "Amish and Mennonite Kitchens"
