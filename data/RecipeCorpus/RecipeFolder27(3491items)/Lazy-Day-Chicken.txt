Title: Lazy Day Chicken
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Instant beef broth
1/2 c  Water
1 1/4 lb Boneless skinless chicken
8 oz Can tomato sauce
2 tb Minced onion flakes
Preheat oven to 350. Place chicken in a baking pan that has been
sprayed with a nonstick cooking spray. In a small bowl, combine
remaining ingredients and pour over chicken. Cover and bake 30
minutes. Remove cover and bake 30 minutes longer.

Per serving: 31g protein, 5g fat, 6g carb., 595mg sodium, 99mg chol.,
196 calories.
