Title: Rice Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Rice dishes
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Milk
4 tb Melted shortening
2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Cold cooked rice
2    Eggs, well beaten
1/2 ts Salt
3 ts Baking powder
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder and salt. Combine
rice, milk, eggs, shortening, and sugar.  Add dry ingredients. Beat
only until smooth.  Fill well-oiled muffin tins 2/3 full. Bake in hot
oven (435Ã¸ F) about 30 minutes. 8 servings. Mrs. W.B. Baker, Pueblo,
CO.
