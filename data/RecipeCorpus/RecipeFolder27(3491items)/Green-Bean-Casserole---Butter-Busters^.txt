Title: Green Bean Casserole - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 6 ----------
2 c  Green beans, cut in
-inch-and-a-half pieces
1 1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Nonfat sour cream
4 t  Cornstarch
2 ea Tb liquid Butter buds or
-fatfree chicken broth
1/4 c  Wheat germ
4 ea Tb quick oats oatmeal
4 t  Italian seasoning
1/2 t  Garlic powder
In a 8 or 9" pie plate, microwave Butter Buds or chicken broth, wheat
germ, oatmeal, Italian seasoning and garlic powder on high for 2-3
minutes, stirring after each minute. Remove mixture from pie plate;
set aside. In the same plate, combine beans, mushrooms and onion.
Microwave on high 3-4 minutes, stirring after 2 minutes. Stir in
combined sour crean and cornstarch, coating vegetables thoroughly.
Microwave on high 1 1/2 to 2 1/2 minutes or until sauce is slightly
thickened; stir. Sprinkle reserved wheat germ mixture over the top
before serving. Per serving: 106 cal., 1g fat (8%), 0mg chol., 3g
fiber, 6g pro., 12g carb., 92mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie
