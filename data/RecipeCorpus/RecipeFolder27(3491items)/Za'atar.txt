Title: Za'atar
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------NORTH AFRICA, TURKEY-------------------------
1 oz Ground sumac
1 oz Dried powdered thyme
-and Jordan
2 oz Sesame seeds
Dry roast the sesame seeds over medium heat for a few minutes,
stirring frequently. Cool, then mix with the sumac and thyme. Stored
in an airtight container, it will keep for 3-4 months.
