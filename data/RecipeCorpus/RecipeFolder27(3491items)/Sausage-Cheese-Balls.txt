Title: Sausage Cheese Balls
Categories: Mixes, Appetizer, Sausage, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 75 appetizers
1 lb Lean ground pork sausage
1 1/2 c  Quick mix
1 lb Grated cheddar cheese
Preheat oven to 350.  Combine ingredients until dough sticks
together. Roll into 1 1/2" balls.  Bake about 25 minutes.  Drain on
paper towels. Serve warm on toothpicks.
