Title: Marzipan Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 bars
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
-- (2 cups)
1    Egg white
2 tb Water
2 c  C and H Powdered Sugar
-- unsifted
1 1/2 c  Blanched almonds, ground
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Vanilla
1    Egg
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  C and H Powdered Sugar
-- unsifted
Filling: Combine sugar and ground almonds and mix thoroughly.  Add egg
white and mix.  Stir in water to thick consistency.  Set aside.

Crust: Combine flour, baking powder and sugar.  Cut butter into flour
mixture with pastry blender until evenly distributed.  Mix vanilla
with egg; stir into flour mixture.  Set aside 3/4 cup dough.  Press
remaining dough evenly into 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan.  Spread marzipan
over dough.  Form remaining dough into 1/2-inch strips and arrange in
large lattice pattern over filling.  Bake in 350-degree oven 35
minutes.  Cool completely before cutting into 36 bars (about 2-inches
by 1-1/2-inches).
