Title: Kirghiz Baked Beef
Categories: Beef, Main dish, Fruit, Ussr
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Chopped Dried Apricots
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Prunes dried, pitted
- & chopped
2 T  Curry Powder
1 t  Salt
2 t  Pepper
2 lb Flank steak, Chopped 1/2"
1 ea Lg. Onion, Chopped
1 ea Med. Green Pepper, Chopped
2 T  Olive oil
1 c  Yogurt (Plain)
2 ea Large Eggs
2 ea Apples Diced
Sautee onion & green pepper in olive oil in lg. heavy skillet until
onion is transparent. Add chopped beef and brown lightly. Pour off
drippings. Add eggs, apple, apricots, curry powder, salt and pepper
to beef. Add yogurt & mix well. Place in a large, deep casserole &
bake in a 350 degree F. for 45 minutes. Origin: Chef Sergei
Kuanishev, Hotel Kazakhstan, Almaty, Kazakhstan
