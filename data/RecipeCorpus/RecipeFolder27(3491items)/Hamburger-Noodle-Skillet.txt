Title: Hamburger Noodle Skillet
Categories: Mixes, Beef, Casserole
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Tomato sauce
1/2 c  Grated cheddar cheese
1 t  Chopped parsley
1 pt Meat sauce mix
2 c  Cooked noodles
2 c  Mixed vegetables w/liquid
In medium skillet, combine meat sauce mix, cooked noodles, cooked
mixed vegetables and tomato sauce. Cover and cook over medium heat
about 10 to 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Sprinkle grated
cheese and parsley on top. Do not stir.  Cover and heat just long
enough to melt cheese. Serve from skillet.
