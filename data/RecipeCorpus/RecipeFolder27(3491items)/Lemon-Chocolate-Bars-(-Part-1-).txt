Title: Lemon Chocolate Bars ( Part 1 )
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Alcohol
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM-----------------CHOCOLATE SHORTBREAD BASE----------------------
1/4 c  Unsweetened Cocoa
1 c  Flour
10 tb Butter; cold
1/3 c  Powdered Sugar
1/3 c  Sugar
MMMMM---------------------LEMON CURD TOPPING--------------------------
4 tb Butter
pn Salt
2 ts Lemon Zest
4 lg Egg Yolks
3/4 c  Sugar
3 oz Lemon Juice
- about 2-1/2 lemons
MMMMM-------------------ORANGE GANACHE TOPPING------------------------
2 tb Heavy Cream
1 1/2 ts Grand Marnier
1 oz Bittersweet Chocolate;
-Finely Chopped
Heat oven to 325~F. Use 8x8" pan and line bottom and two sides with a
8x16" strip of foil. Whisk together the sugars and cocoa. In a large
bowl, cream the butter and cocoa mixture until light and fluffy. Mix
in the flour, adding in two parts, until incorporated. Pat dough into
prepared pan. Use fork to prick dough all over. Bake for 40 minutes.
While shortbread is baking, prepare the Topping.
