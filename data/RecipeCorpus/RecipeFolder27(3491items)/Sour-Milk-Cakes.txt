Title: Sour Milk Cakes
Categories: Bread, Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Sour milk
1/2 c  Sour cream
1    Egg, well beaten
2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 1/4 ts Baking soda
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder, baking soda, and
salt. Add sour cream, egg, and sour milk.  Beat until smooth. Bake on
hot griddle.  16 servings. The Household Searchlight - 1941
