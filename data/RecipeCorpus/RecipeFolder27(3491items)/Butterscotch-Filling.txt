Title: Butterscotch Filling
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  CORNSTARCH
1/2 c  WATER
1/2 c  BROWN SUGAR
1/4 t  SALT
1 T  BUTTER
STIR TOGETHER SUGAR, CORNSTARCH AND SALT.  STIR IN WATER. COOK,
STIRRING CONSTANTLY, UNTIL MIXTURE THICKENS AND BOILS. BOIL AND STIR
1 MINUTE. BLEND IN BUTTER. COOL
