Title: Pudding Spice
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Grated nutmeg
1 tb Coriander seeds
1 tb Allspice berries
1 sm Piece of cinnamon
1 tb Cloves
1 tb Mace
Grind all the spices to a fine powder. Use is cakes, cookies, and
puddings. Stored in an airtight container, it will keep for 3-4
months.
