Title: Don's Lime-Peanut-Chili Seafood Marinade
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Seafood, Marinade, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2    Jalapeno peppers, minced
3 tb Peanut oil
1 ts Garlic clove, minced
1 tb Dill, dried
1 lg Dill pickle, minced
1/3 c  Lime juice, fresh
1 ts Lime rind, minced
1/2 c  Scallions, chopped
2 tb Cider vinegar
2 tb Peanut butter
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Put the vinegar, lime juice, peanut oil, & peanut butter in a blender
and mix on puree setting until smooth. Add in the other ingredeints
and blend for 5 seconds. Spread over fish, cover, and refrigerate for
4-12 hours. Heat the sauce before using as a serving sauce. Origin:
Don Houston's kitchen, circa Aug. 1991
