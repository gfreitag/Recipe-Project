Title: Date Nut Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 48 bars
5    Eggs; separated
1 c  C and H Brown Sugar; packed
1 ts Vanilla
C and H Powdered Sugar
1 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Chopped dates
1 c  Chopped walnuts
Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in bowl.  Add dates and nuts
and toss until well-coated and separated.  Set aside.  Beat egg yolks
until thick (about 3 minutes).  Gradually beat in sugar and vanilla.
Blend in date-nut mixture.  Beat egg whites until stiff; fold into
batter.  Pour into 15 by 10 by 1 inch greased and floured pan.
Spread evenly.  Bake in 325 degree oven 30 minutes until light brown.
Cool.  Cut into 48 bars and roll in powdered sugar.
