Title: Applesauce Snack Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Herbs/spice, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea EGG
3/4 c  APPLESAUCE
1/2 t  ALLSPICE
1/2 c  CHOPPED NUTS
1 pk SNACK CAKE MIX
1/3 c  OIL
1 1/2 t  CINNAMON
1/8 t  CLOVES
1/2 c  RAISINS
PREHEAT OVEN TO 325.  POUR SNACK CAKE MIX INTO AN UNGREASED 9" SQUARE
BAKING PAN; SET ASIDE.  IN A SMALL BOWL, COMBINE EGG, OIL, APPLESAUCE,
CINNAMON, ALLSPICE AND CLOVES, BEATING WITH A FORK TO BLEND. STIR INTO
SNACK CAKE MIX UNTIL SMOOTH AND BLENDED. STIR IN NUTS AND RAISINS.
BAKE 35 TO 45 MINUTES UNTIL DONE.  COOL ON RACK. (***)
