Title: Banana Coconut Delights
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 36 cookies
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Rolled oats
2 c  Basic cookie mix
1 c  Coconut
1    Medium banana, mashed
1 t  Vanilla
Preheat oven to 375.  Lightly grease baking sheets. In medium bowl,
combine cookie mix, coconut, banana, vanilla and egg. Beat well. Stir
in chopped nuts and oats. Drop by tsp onto prepared baking sheets.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes.
