Title: Country Oat Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 16 servings
MMMMM----------------------------CAKE---------------------------------
3/4 c  -Egg substitute
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Finely chopped apple
-- (about 2 medium)
1 pk Spice cake mix (18.5 oz.)
1 c  Quaker Oats, uncooked
-- (quick or old-fashioned)
8 oz Plain low-fat yogurt
3    Eggs; OR...
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/4 c  Margarine or butter; melted
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
Whipped cream (optional)
1 c  Quaker Oats, uncooked
-- (quick or old-fashioned)
1/2 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
Heat oven to 350 F.  Grease and flour 13 x 9-inch baking pan.  For
cake, combine first six ingredients.  Blend on low speed of electric
mixer until moistened; mix at medium speed 2 minutes.  Stir in apple.
Pour into prepared pan.  For topping, combine oats, brown sugar,
margarine and cinnamon; mix well.  Sprinkle evenly over batter.  Bake
40 to 45 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out
clean.  Serve with whipped cream, if desired.

Nutrition Information (1/16 of recipe): * Calories 290 * Fat 11g *
Sodium 300mg * Dietary Fiber 1g
