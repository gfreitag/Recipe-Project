Title: Strawberry Banana Dessert
Categories: Dessert, Pudding, Fruit
Yield: 10 servings
2 ea Bananas, see note
1 ea Loaf pound cake, see note
1 x  Additional strawberries
1 x  Additional Banana slices
1 c  Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 c  Cold water
1 ea Pt Whipping cream, see note
1 ea Pt Fresh strawberries, note
NOTE: Strawberries; cleaned, hulled and sliced. MOTE: Bananas; sliced
and dipped in lemon juice from concentrate. NOTE: Whipping Cream; 2
cups, stiffly whipped NOTE: Pound Cake: 10-3/4 or 12 ounce cut in 12
slices. In large bowl, combine sweetened condensed milk and water.
Add pudding mix; beat well.  Chill 5 minutes.  Fold in whipped cream
then strawberries and bananas.  Line side and bottom of 3-1/2 quart
glass serving bowl with cake slices.  Spoon pudding mixture into
prepared bowl. Cover; chill. Garnish with additional strawberries and
banana slices. Refrigerate leftovers.
