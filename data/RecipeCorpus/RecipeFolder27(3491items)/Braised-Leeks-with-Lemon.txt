Title: Braised Leeks with Lemon
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Chicken broth
1 ts Grated lemon zest
4 sm Or 3 medium leeks
-trimmed to 7" long
-cut lengthwise into
-quarters or eighths
In a shallow dish soak leeks in cold water to cover for 15 minutes,
rubbing occasionally to remove any grit. In a heavy skillet, melt
butter over moderate heat. Lift leeks out of water and with water
still clinging to them add to skillet. Cook, stirring occasionally, 5
minutes and add broth and zest. Braise leeks, covered, 5 minutes, or
until very tender and season with salt and pepper.
