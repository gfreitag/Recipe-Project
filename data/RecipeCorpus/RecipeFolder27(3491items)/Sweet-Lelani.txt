Title: Sweet Lelani
Categories: Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 8 -10  servi
1 lg Bottle ginger ale
32 oz Pineapple spears
Marascino cherries
2 cn Orange juice, frozen
1 lg Can pineapple juice
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Lemon juice
Combine all ingredients except ginger ale, pineapple spears, and
cherries. Stir.  Add ginger ale just before serving.  Pour drink into
ice filled glasses.  Garnish each with a pineapple spear and a
maraschino cherry. Submitted By FLEURETTE DELISLE   On   02-06-95
