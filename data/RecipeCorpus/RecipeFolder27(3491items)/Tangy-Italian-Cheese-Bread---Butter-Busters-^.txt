Title: Tangy Italian Cheese Bread - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Bread
Yield: 6 servings
-cheese
1 ea Tb minced fresh parsley
2 t  Minced fresh basil
1/4 c  Nonfat Parmesan cheese
6 ea Sl (1oz) French bread,
-toasted
2 ea Tb Weight Watchers nonfat
-Caeser dressing
1/2 c  Grated nonfat mozzarella
Place bread slices on a broiler rack. Brush top side of each bread
slice lightly with salad dressing. Sprinkle cheeses, parsley and
basil evenly over bread slices. Broil 6" from heat until cheese
melts. Serve immediately. Per serving: 113 cal., 1g fat (8%), 3mg
chol., 1g fiber. 6.2g pro., 19g carb., 306mg sod. Butter Busters by
Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 2-95.
