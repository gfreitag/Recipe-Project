Title: Westport Room Salad - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ea Head cauliflower
1/2 ea Head lettuce
1 bn Fresh spinach, cleaned and
-torn
1 c  Pepperidge Farms seasoned
-stuffing cubes
1 cl Garlic, crushed
1 ea Tb (to3) fatfree Parmesan
-cheese (Alpine Lace or
-Weight Watchers)
1 c  Kraft fatfree mayonnaise or
-Miracle Whip
2 ea Tb lemon juice
Blend all ingredients except cauliflower, lettuce, spinach and
stuffing cubes. Refrigerate at least one hour. Grate half a head of
cauliflower and refrigerate covered. Pour dressing over lettuce and
spinach. Sprinkle cauliflower over top tand toss. Top with stuffing
cubes. Per serving: 54 cal., 0.2g fat (3%), 0mg chol., 2g fiber, 2g
pro., 12g carb., 450mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN
