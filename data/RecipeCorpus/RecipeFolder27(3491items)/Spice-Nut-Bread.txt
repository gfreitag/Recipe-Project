Title: Spice Nut Bread
Categories: Mixes, Bread, Herbs/spice, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 loaf
1/4 t  Nutmeg
3/4 c  Chopped nuts
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1 c  Milk or water
3 c  Quick mix
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Granulated sugar
1 t  Cinnamon
1/4 t  Cloves
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease a 9" loaf pan.  In a large bowl, combine
quick mix, brown sugar, granulated sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves,
and nuts until blended.  Combine egg and milk or water in a small
bowl.  Add all at once to dry ingredients.  Blend.  Pour batter into
prepared pan. Bake 45 to 50 minutes.  Let stand 5 to 10 minutes in
pan.  Remove from pan and cool on a wire rack.
