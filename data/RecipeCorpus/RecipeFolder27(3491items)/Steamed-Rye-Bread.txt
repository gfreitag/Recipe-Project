Title: Steamed Rye Bread
Categories: Bread
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Raisins
1 c  Graham flour
1 c  Rye flour
1 c  Corn-meal
3/4 c  Molasses
2 c  Water
Combine ingredients.  Fill well-oiled 1-pound cans 2/3 full. Cover
closely and steam 2 hours. Florence Taft Eaton, Concord, MA.
