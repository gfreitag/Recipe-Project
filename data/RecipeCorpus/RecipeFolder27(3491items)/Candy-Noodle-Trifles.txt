Title: Candy Noodle Trifles
Categories: Candy
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Chow mein noodles
12 oz Butterscotch bits
1 c  Spanish peanuts
Melt bits in a pan or bowl over pan of hot wate.  Slowly add nuts and
noodles; stir until coated with butterscotch.  Drop by tablespoon onto
waxed paper.  Chill for 2 hours in the refrigerator.  Keep cool.
