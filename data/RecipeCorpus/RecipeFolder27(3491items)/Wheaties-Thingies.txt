Title: Wheaties Thingies
Categories: Candy
Yield: 3 servings
4 c  Wheaties
12 oz Milk chocolate bits
Melt chocolate in a bowl or pan over hot water, a double boiler.
Gradually add cereal.  Drop by teaspoonfuls on waxed paper.  Chill in
refrigerator, 2 hours, keep cool. Randy Rigg
