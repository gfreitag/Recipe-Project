Title: Mrs. Dash Roasted Lemon Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
2 t  Instant chicken bouillon
3 T  Mrs. Dash Lemon pepper
1 T  Dried oregano
One whole chicken (2-1/4 -
-2-1/2 lbs.)
1/4 c  Hot water
Preheat oven to 400 F.  Place chicken in roasting pan.  Combine hot
water, and bouillon; pour over chicken. Combine Mrs Dash Lemon Pepper
and oregano. Coat chicken with mixture. Bake (covered with aluminum
foil) 50 minutes. Remove foil.  Baste. Bake uncovered for 15 minutes
or until golden brown and chicken is done. (Serves 3 - 4) Shared By:
Pat Stockett
