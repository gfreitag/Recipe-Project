Title: Pepper Steaks - gas grill
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Cracked pepper
2 lb Beef sirloin steak
-1 1/2 inches thick
*Hickory chips
1/4 c  Butter
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 ts Garlic powder
*Hickory chips. Soak Hickory chips in water to cover for 1 hour; drain
chips and place on a 12 inch square of heavy foil. Wrap foil around
chips to make a log leaving both ends open. Pre-heat gas grill on
HIGH for 10 minutes. While grill heats, place butter in a saucepan on
warming rack to melt. Remove from heat; stir in lemon juice,
Worcestershire, garlic powder and salt. Rub pepper into both sides of
steak. Place Hickory chip log directly on lava rocks. Turn both
burners to MEDIUM-HIGH. Place steak on cooking grid; close hood and
cook for 17 to 20 minutes, brushing occasionally with lemon-butter
mixture. Turn meat and cook 15 to 17 minutes longer for rare,
slightly longer for medium. Heat remaining lemon-butter on warming
rack; pass with steak. Submitted By GRANT AMES   On   02-03-95
