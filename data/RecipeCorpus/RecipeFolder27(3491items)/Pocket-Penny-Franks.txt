Title: Pocket Penny Franks
Categories: Kids, Sausage, Sandwich
Yield: 8 servings
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 pk Hot dogs, sliced into
1/4-inch rounds
1 pk Pita bread, sliced in half
And lightly toasted
1/2 c  Catsup
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Liquid smoke
1 tb Vinegar
2 ds Tabasco sauce
To make the barbecue sauce, combine the first eight ingredients in a
saucepan. Cook and stir over low heat for 5 minutes. Place the hot
dogs in a nonstick frying pan and lightly brown over medium-high
heat. Add the barbecue sauce and simmer for 10 minutes. Spoon into
toasted pita bread halves. *By Annemarie Rose Selness, taken from
October 1994 issue of Parenting magazine* -Posted for you by Michelle
