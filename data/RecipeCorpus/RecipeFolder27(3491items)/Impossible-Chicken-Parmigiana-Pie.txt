Title: Impossible Chicken Parmigiana Pie
Categories: Bread, Chicken, Cheese/eggs, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 8 SERVINGS--------------------------
1/2 t  Basil, dried
6 oz Tomato paste
1 c  Milk
2 ea Eggs
2/3 c  Bisquick baking mix
1/4 t  Pepper
3/4 c  Creamed cottage cheese
-(small curd)
1/3 c  Parmesan; grated
1 1/2 c  Chicken; cooked cut-up
5 oz Mozzarella ; shredded
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1/2 t  OrÂ‚gano, dried
Heat oven to 400 degrees. Grease pie plate, 10x1-1/2". Layer cottage
cheese and Parmesan cheese in pie plate. Mix chicken, 1/2 cup of the
mozzarella cheese, the garlic powder, oregano, basil and tomato
paste. Spoon over Parmesan cheese. Beat remaining ingredients in
blender on high speed 15 seconds, with hand beater 1 minute or until
smooth. Pour into pie plate. Bake 30 minutes. Top with remaining
mozzarella cheese. Bake 5 to 8 minutes longer or until knife inserted
in center comes out clean. Cool 5 minutes. High altitudes: No
adjustments are necessary
