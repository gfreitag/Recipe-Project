Title: Polo Loco Marinade II
Categories: Chicken, Marinade, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Tabasco
1 tb Parsley ; chopped or cilant
2    Chickens; halved
1 c  White vinegar
1 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  White wine ; or vermouth
Garlic; to taste
1 ts Salt
Combine ingredients, marinate chicken at least one hour.
