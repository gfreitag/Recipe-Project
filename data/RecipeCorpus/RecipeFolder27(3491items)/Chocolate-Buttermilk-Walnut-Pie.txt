Title: Chocolate Buttermilk Walnut Pie
Categories: Chocolate, Pie/tart, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 servings
3    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Walnuts; chopped
Vanilla or coffee
Ice cream
1 2/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Buttermilk baking mix
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
4 tb Butter or margarine
-- melted
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 1:00

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. In a food processor, combine sugar,
buttermilk, baking mix, cocoa, melted butter, eggs and vanilla/
Process until smooth and well blended, scraping down sides of bowl
once or twice, 1 to 2 mins.

2. Pour filling into a well-greased 9-inch pie pan. Sprinkle walnuts
on top. Bake 40 to 45 mins, or until set. Serve pie warm or cold,
topped with scoops of ice cream.
