Title: Dehydrator Sesame Pumpkin Seed Bits
Categories: Can/preserv, Nuts/grains, Vegetable, Syrup
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------------STIR TOGETHER----------------------------
1/2 c  Honey and 1/4 cup hot water
MMMMM---------------------------MIX IN--------------------------------
1 c  Pumpkinseeds
2 c  Sesame seeds
Spread to about 1/4 inch thick.  Pat gently into place.  The mixture
will be crumbly.  Score (cut partially into squares)  When firm,
remove from tray and turn over to continue drying until crisp.  Break
into squares along scored lines and dry a little more. VARIATIONS:
Use maple syrup in place of honey.  Substitute sunflower seeds and/or
nuts for pumpkinseeds. Add chopped dates or raisins. Origin: Dry It -
You'll Like It! circa 1973. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
