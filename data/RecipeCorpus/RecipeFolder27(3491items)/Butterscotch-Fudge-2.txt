Title: Butterscotch Fudge 2
Categories: Cake/cookie, Candy
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Salt
1/2 c  Nuts
1 c  Brown Sugar
2 T  White Syrup
1/4 c  Butter
3/4 c  Sour Cream
1 c  White Sugar
1 t  Vanilla
Melt butter. Add brown sugar and heat to boiling. Add white sugar,
sour cream and salt. cook to 236 degrees. Cool. Add vanilla and
nuts then beat until thick. Pour into buttered pan.
