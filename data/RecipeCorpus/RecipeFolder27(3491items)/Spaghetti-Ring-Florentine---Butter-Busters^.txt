Title: Spaghetti Ring Florentine - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 10 ----------
6 ea Tb liquid Butter Buds
2 cn (8oz) sliced mushrooms,
-rinsed and drained
1 cn (26 3/4oz) Hunts light
-spaghetti sauce
1 c  Nonfat mozzarella cheese,
-grated
8 oz Uncooked spaghetti
2 ea Pk (10oz) frozen chopped
-spinach
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
1/3 c  Alpine Lace or Weight
-Watchers fatfree Parmesan
-cheese
Line a tube or angel food cake pan with folil. Spray with Pam. Set
aside. Cook spaghetti according to package directions; drain. Cook
spinach according to directions, adding onion; drain, pressing out
liquid with back of spoon. Stir together Egg Beaters, spaghetti and
spinach mixture, Parmesan, mozzarella, 4 tb liquid Butter Buds and
1/2 of the mushrooms. Press into mold. Cover and bake at 375F. 25
minutes. Let stand 5 minutes. For sauce: heat remaining mushrooms in
2 tb Butter Buds. Add sauce and heat through. Cut ring into wedges
and pour sauce over each serving. Per serving: 129 cal., 0.5 g fat
(3%), 0mg chol., 3g fiber, 9g pro., 23g carb., 505mg sod. Butter
Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw
2-95.
