Title: Sweet-and-Sour Pork II
Categories: Meat, Pork, Main dish, Asian
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Cooking oil
1    Green pepper,cut into
-thin strips
2 T  Each, soy suace,vinegar,
Brown sugar,and corn starch
3/4 t  Salt
*** Cooked Rice
1 lb Pork tenderloin
2 cn 8 ounce each, pineapple
Tidbits
1/3 c  Ketchup
1/3 c  Water
1/4 t  Pepper
1/4 t  Ground ginger
1 md Onion, chopped
Cut the tenderloin into 1-1/2 in x 1/4-in strips;set aside. Drain
pineapple, reserving juice in a small bowl; set pineapple aisde. To
juice, add ketchup,water,soy sauce,vinegar,brown sugar,corn
starch,salt, pepper and ginger;stir until smooth. Heat oil in large
skillet or wok on high; stir fry pork and onion for 5-7 minutes or
until pork is no longer pink. Stir in pineapple juice mixture to Wok.
Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. Reduce heat;cover and cook
5 minutes. Serve immediately over rice..
