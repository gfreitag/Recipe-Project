Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 30 servings
Minature
1/3 c  Pecans; chopped
1    Egg white; lightly beaten
1 pk Puff pastry, frozen

Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:27 1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F. With a rolling pin,
on a floured board, roll out each (2) pastry sheet, 1 at a time, to a
9 x 15-inch rectangle. Cut each pastry sheet into 15 (3 oz)
rectangles. 2. Place 1 tsp chocolate chips and 1/2 tsp pecans in
center of each dough square. Moisten edges with egg white and fold in
half diagonally to form a triangle. Press edges together firmly to
seal. Place on buttered baking sheets. Brush tops with egg white. 3.
Bake 10 to 12 mins, or until golden brown. Serve warm.
