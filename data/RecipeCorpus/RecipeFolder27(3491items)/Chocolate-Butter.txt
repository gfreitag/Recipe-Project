Title: Chocolate Butter
Categories: Spread, Chocolate
Yield: 2 to 4
1 oz Semisweet chocolate
4 ea Tb Butter
Melt chocolate, set aside to cool.  Cream butter, beat in cooled
chocolate. Pour into 4 small ramekins and chill until firm.  Serve
with warm brioche or croissants. Posted by Linda Davis
