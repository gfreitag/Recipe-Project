Title: Nightcap Coffee Mix
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Ground cardamom
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
2/3 c  Non dairy coffee creamer
1/3 c  Instant coffee granules
1/3 c  Granulated sugar
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl; stir until well blended.
Store in airtight container. Yields 1 1/3 cups coffee mix To serve:
spoon 1 heaping tablespoon coffee mix into 8 ounces hot water. stir
until well blended.
