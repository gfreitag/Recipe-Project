Title: Chocolate Pecan Pastry
Categories: Chocolate, Nuts/grains, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Pecans; ground
1    Stick butter; cut in tbs.
1 tb Butter-flavored shortening,
1 c  Flour
1/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:10

In a food processor, combine butter, shortening, flour, cocoa, sugar,
salt, and pecans. Process with on and off turns until mixture
resembles coarse meal, about 30 secs. With machine on, add 2 to 3 Tbs
cold water through feed tube. Process until dough forms a ball.
Refrigerate until ready to use.
