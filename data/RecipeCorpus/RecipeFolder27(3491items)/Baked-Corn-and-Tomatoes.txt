Title: Baked Corn and Tomatoes
Categories: Side dish, Casserole, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Black pepper
3 tb Butter (or marg.); melted
1 c  Breadcrumbs, fresh dry
2 c  Corn; freshly grated
2 c  Tomatoes, fresh; chopped
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
Combine corn and tomatoes; add sugar, salt, pepper, and melted butter.
Spoon into a greased 1-quart baking dish.  Cover with breadcrumbs and
bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes or until corn is done.
