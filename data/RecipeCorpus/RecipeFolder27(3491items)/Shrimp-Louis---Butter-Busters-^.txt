Title: Shrimp Louis - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 t  Prepared horseradish
6 c  Boston lettuce
2 lb Shrimp, shelled, deveined
-and cooked
1 c  Kraft fatfree Miracle whip
1/2 c  Chile sauce
3/4 t  Worcestershire sauce
1 ea Tb gratd onion
1 ea Tb chopped parsley
MMMMM-----------------------MAKE DRESSING----------------------------
Combine Miracle Whip, chile sauce, Worcestershire, grated onion,
parsley and horseradish. Beat with a rotary beater until smooth and
well combined. Refrigerate, covered, several hours until very well
chilled. To serve: Arrange lettuce cups on individual small plates.
Chop shrimp coarsley. Divide evenly itno lettuce cups. Spoon some of
the dressing over shrimp. Serve immediately. Per serving: 214 cal.,
2g fat (8%), 295mg chol., 33g pro., 16g carb., 1046mg sod. Butter
