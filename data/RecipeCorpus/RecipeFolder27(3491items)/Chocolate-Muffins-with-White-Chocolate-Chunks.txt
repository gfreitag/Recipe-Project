Title: Chocolate Muffins with White Chocolate Chunks
Categories: Chocolate, Muffin, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
Melted and cooled
1 lg Egg, at room temperature,
-lightly beaten
1 t  Vanilla extract
6 ea Ounces white chocolate, cut
-into 3/4-inch chunks
1 ea Position a rack in the
-center of the oven and
-preheat to 375 F.
1 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Unsweetened nonalkalized
-cocoa powder
2 t  Double-acting baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Baking soda
1 c  Milk, at room temperature
8 T  (1 stick) unsalted butter,
Lightly butter twelve 1 1/8-by-2 3/4-inch (3-ounce) muffin cups and
the edges surrounding the cups. 2. In a large bowl, stir together the
flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, salt, and baking soda. In another
bowl, stir together the milk, butter, egg, and vanilla until blended.
Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients. Add the liuid
ingredients and stir just to combine. Stir in half of the white
chocolate chunks. 3. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin cups.
Sprinkle the tops with the remaining white chocolate chunks. Bake for
20 to 25 minutes or until a cake tester toothpick inserted in the
center of one of the muffins comes out clean. 4. Cool the muffins in
their pans set on a wire rack for 5 minutes. Remove the muffins from
the cups and finish cooling on the wire rack. Serve warm, or cool
completely and store the muffins in an airtight container at room
temperature. Yield: 12 muffins Source: Glorious Chocolate by Mary
Goodbody and the Editors of Choclatier Magazine
