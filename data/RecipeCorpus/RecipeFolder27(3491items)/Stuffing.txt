Title: Stuffing
Categories: Side dish, Low-cal/fat, Vegetable, Dress/stuff
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Chopped fresh thyme
1 ts Salt
1 ts White pepper
1 tb Onion and garlic powder mix
1/2 c  Defatted chicken broth
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped mushrooms
1 pk Stuffing mix
1/4 c  Parsley
2 ts Chopped fresh sage
Mix all together and put into baking dish. Bake at 400 degrees for 30
minutes. 8 % Fat
