Title: Tofu Fiesta
Categories: Vegetable, Diabetic, Holiday
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 ea Garlic Clove, minced
1 c  Broccoli Pieces
1 1/2 c  Mushrooms, diced
8 oz Tofu, drained
1/2 c  Ricotta Cheese
2 T  Parsley Leaves, chopped
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
1 T  Vegetable Oil
2 ea Carrots, sliced thin
1 c  Celery, chopped
1/2 t  Ground Turmeric
1 ea Ripe Tomato, cubed
2 T  Sesame Seeds, toasted
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Saute the onion and garlic in the oil until the onion is transparent.
Add the broccoli, carrots, pepper, mushrooms and celery and cook
until tender. Break the tofu into chunks.  Add the tofu to the
vegetables with the turmeric and cheese.  Simmer to heat thoroughly.
Just before serving add the tomato and parsley and sprinkle on the
sesame seed. Serves 2.
