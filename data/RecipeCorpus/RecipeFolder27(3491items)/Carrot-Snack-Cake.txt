Title: Carrot Snack Cake
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie, Vegetable, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 cake
3/4 c  Orange juice
1 t  Cinnamon
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 pk Snack cake mix (2 1/4 cups)
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1/3 c  Oil
1 c  Grated carrots
MMMMM-------------------CREAM CHEESE FROSTING------------------------
1 2/3 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 t  Vanilla
3 T  Butter or margarine
3 oz Cream cheese, softened
Preheat oven to 350.  Pour snack cake mix into an ungreased 8 or 9"
square baking pan.  In medium bowl, combine egg, oil, carrots, orange
juice and cinnamon, beating with a fork to blend. Stir into snack
cake mix until blended.  Stir in nuts.  Bake 35 to 45 minutes. In
small bowl, cream margarine and cream cheese until light and fluffy.
Beat in powdered sugar and vanilla until smooth. Set aside.  Cool
cake on rack. Spread frosting over cooled cake.
