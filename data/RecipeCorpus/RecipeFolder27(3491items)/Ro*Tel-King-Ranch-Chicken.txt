Title: Ro*Tel King Ranch Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 10 servings
Chilies  (10 oz)
3 lb Chicken
12 ea Soft corn tortillas torn
Into bite-size pieces
1 1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 c  Chopped green pepper
1 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Margarine or butter
2 cn Cream of chicken soup
2 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn RO*TEL tomatoes and green
Stew chicken with salt, pepper, and bay leaf. After cooling and
boning cut chicken into bite-size pieces. In a large sauce pan, cook
green pepper and onion in margarine or butter until tender. Stir in
soups, tomatoes and chicken in 3 quart shallow baking dish (13"x9"),
Arrange in 3 alternating layers. Tortillas, soup mixture and ending
with a topping of cheese. Bake at 325 F for 40 minutes or until hot
and bubbly and cheese is browned. Can be frozen before or after
cooking.

Source:"RO*TEL Recipe"; a promotional booklet.
