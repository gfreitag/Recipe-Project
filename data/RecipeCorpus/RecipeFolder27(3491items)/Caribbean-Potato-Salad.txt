Title: Caribbean Potato Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Scallions, sliced
1/2 c  Green bell pepper, sliced
2 c  Papaya or cantaloupe, cubed
2 lb Red Bliss potatoes
2 c  Cherry tomatoes, halved
1    Celery rib, sliced
MMMMM---------------------TROPICAL DRESSING--------------------------
2 tb JalapeÂ¤o, chopped
2 tb White wine vinegar
Salt to taste
2 tb Lime juice
1/4 c  Canola oil
1/3 c  Mango chutney
Place potatoes in large saucepan with enough salted cold water to
cover. Boil until tender when pierced with a fork, about 30 minutes.
Drain.

When cool enough to handle, cube potatoes and combine with dressing
ingredinets.

To make dressing: combine all dressing ingredients in a small bowl.
pour over vegetables and toss to coat. Serve immediately or
refrigerate in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
