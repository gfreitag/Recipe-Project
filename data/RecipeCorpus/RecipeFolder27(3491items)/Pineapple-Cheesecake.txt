Title: Pineapple Cheesecake
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
Warmed in microwave on high
-for 1-1/2
Minutes.
1 c  SUGAR
1 ts VANILLA
5    JUMBO EGGS, removed from
-shell and warmed 25
Seconds in the microwave.
1/2 c  HEAVY CREAM
1/4 c  CORNSTARCH
1 1/2 pk GRAHAM CRACKERS, crushed
1/2 c  SUGAR
6 tb BUTTER, melted
Mix and pat onto bottoms and
-sides of 10-inch
-springform.
CAKE
20 oz PINEAPPLE, crushed (natural
-juice packed)
1 1/2 lb CREAM CHEESE, removed from
-wrappers and
** CRUST
