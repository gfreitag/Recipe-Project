Title: Jalapeno Corn Bread - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Healthy, Bread
Yield: 10 servings
1/4 c  Salt
1/2 c  Lowfat buttermilk
1/2 c  Liquid Butter Buds
1 cn Rotel tomatoes, drained
1 c  Cornmeal
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
8 oz Cream style corn (1/2 #8
-can)
1 c  Grated nonfat cheddar cheese    1 1/2 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Lite salt
Beat Egg Beaters and ad remaining ingredients, mixing well. pour into
a large cast iron skillet that has been sprayed with a nonstick
cooking spray. Bake 50-60 minutes at 350. Per serving: 103 cal., 0.8g
fat (7%), 2mg chol., 1g fiber, 6g pro., 18g carb., 333mg sod. Butter
Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw
2-95.
