Title: Apple Treat Muffins
Categories: Bread, Muffin, Fruit
Yield: 12 muffins
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Cinnamon
1    Egg, beaten
1 c  Evaporated milk
1/3 c  Butter, melted
1 1/2 c  Finely chopped apples
-(peeled and cored)
1 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Brown sugar, packed
3 tb Butter, melted
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 ts Cinnamon
Mix together in a small saucepan the chopped apples, 1 ts cinnamon, 2
tb brown sugar and 3 tb butter.  Cover and stean over low heat for 2
minutes.

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

Sift the next 6 ingredients together, then add the egg, milk and
butter and blend just until moistened.  Fold in the apple mixture and
spoon into paper lined muffin tins.

Combine the 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1 ts cinnamon and 1/4 ts ground
cloves. Sprinkle over the muffin batter and bake for about 20 minutes.
