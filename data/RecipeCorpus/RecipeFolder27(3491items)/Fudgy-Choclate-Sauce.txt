Title: Fudgy Choclate Sauce
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
ds Salt
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
3 oz Unsweetened chocolate square
-- broken up
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:08 1. In a 1-qt glass bowl, combine sweetened
condensed milk, chocolate and milk. Heat in microwave on High about 2
1/2 mins, stirring twice during cooking time, until mixture is
smooth, blended, and thickened. Whisk in vanilla and salt. 2. Serve
hot or cold over ice cream. Refrigerate any leftovers and reheat in
microwave as needed.
