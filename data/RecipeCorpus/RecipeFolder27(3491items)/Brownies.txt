Title: Brownies
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 36 servings
4    Eggs
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Flour; sifted
3/4 c  Pecans; chopped
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
5 oz Unsweetened chocolate square
-- cut-up
2    Sticks butter or margarine
1 3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Light corn syrup
2 ts Vanilla extract
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:48 1. Preheat oven to 350 F. In a 2-qt glass
bowl, combine unsweetened chocolate and butter. Heat in microwave on
High 1 1/2 to 2 1/2 mins, or until chocolae is melted and smooth when
stirred. 2. With a fork, beat in sugar, corn syrup, vanilla, and eggs
until well blended. Mix in salt and flour until well mixed. 3. Spread
evenly in a greased, foil-lined 9 x 13-inch baking pan. Sprinkle nuts
and chocolate chips over top. Bake 30 to 35 mins, or until set. Do
not overbake. Let cool before cutting into bars.
