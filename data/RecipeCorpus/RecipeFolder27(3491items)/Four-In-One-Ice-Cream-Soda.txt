Title: Four-In-One Ice Cream Soda
Categories: Kids, Beverage, Ice cream
Yield: 4 servings
1 qt Ice cream
1 pt Soda pop
1 lg Bowl
4    Kids
4    Straws
4    Spoons
Place ice cream and soda in large bowl. Top with glob of whipped
cream. Gather four kids around and give each a spoon and straw and
tell them to dig in! *By Lou Roehr* -Posted for you by Michelle Bruce
