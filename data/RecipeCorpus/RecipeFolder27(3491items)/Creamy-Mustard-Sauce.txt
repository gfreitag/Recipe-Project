Title: Creamy Mustard Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
-or chicken broth
1 1/2 tb Dijon mustard
1 tb Parsley, minced
Black pepper to taste
1 c  Basic White Sauce
2 tb Onion(s), minced
2 tb Butter Buds Mix, liquefied
1/2 c  White wine
Prepare Basic White Sauce; keep warm. In a small saucepan over medium
heat, cook the onion in the Butter Buds until softened. Add the wine
and cook until reduced to 2 tbs. Whisk the mixture into the sauce
with the remaining ingredients. Stir over low heat until warmed
through.
