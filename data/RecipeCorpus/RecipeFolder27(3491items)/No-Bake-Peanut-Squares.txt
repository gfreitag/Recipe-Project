Title: No-Bake Peanut Squares
Categories: Kids, Snack
Yield: 10 servings
4 c  Corn flakes
4 c  Cherrios
1 c  Peanuts
1 c  Light corn syrup
1 c  White sugar
1 c  Peanut butter
In a 2 qt. pan melt together just until smooth the first three
ingredients, stirring constantly. Do not boil. (Warning: Don't use
glass pan on top of electric stove!) Remove from heat. Measure the
last three ingredients into a large bowl. Pour over peanut butter
mixture. Stir, coating cereal and nuts. Spread into buttered large
pan. Cut into squares. Press down. *By 9 year old Travis Williams*
