Title: Char Masala
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------AFGHANISTAN-----------------------------
1 tb Cumin seeds
1 tb Black cardamon seeds
1 tb Cinnamon
1 tb Cloves
Mix all the spices together. Use to flavor rice dishes. Stored in an
airtight container, it will keep for  3-4 months.
