Title: Chocolate Drop Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 batch
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Sifted all-purpose flour
1/4 ts Soda
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  C&H Brown Sugar
1    Egg; unbeaten
2 oz Unsweetened chocolate
-- melted
1 c  Buttermilk or sour milk
Combine shortening, sugar and egg in mixing bowl; beat well.  Add
melted chocolate, buttermilk and vanilla; blend.  Sift together
flour, soda, and salt and stir in.  Add walnuts; chill soft dough.
Drop by teaspoonful on greased baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees
for 12 to 15 minutes or until done.  Makes 3 to 4 dozen soft, rich
cookies.  Frost if desired.
