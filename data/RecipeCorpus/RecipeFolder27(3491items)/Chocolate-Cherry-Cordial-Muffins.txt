Title: Chocolate Cherry Cordial Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Chocolate, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chopped candied cherries
-maraschino cherries OR
-raisins
3/4 c  Milk
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1    Egg
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 c  (11 1/2 oz pkg) Nestle Toll
-House Milk Chocolate
-Morsels, divided
1/4 c  Light brown sugar; firmly
-packed
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
2 ts Baking powder
In large bowl, combine flour, 1 3/4 cups morsels, brown sugar,
granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  Stir in
cherries. In small bowl, combine milk, oil and egg until well
blended. Add liquid ingredients to dry ingredients; stir just until
moistened. Spoon batter into 12 greased or paper-lined muffing cups;
muffin cups will be full. Sprinkle with *remaining* 1/4 cup morsels.
Bake in preheated 375F oven for 18 to 20 minutes or until golden.
Cool for 5 minutes. Remove from pan; serve warm, or cool completely
on wire rack.
