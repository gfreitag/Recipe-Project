Title: China Moon Wontons
Categories: Chinese, Appetizer, Snack, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Pepper oil
1 tb China moon infusion
1 lb Ground pork
1 pk Wonton wrappers
1/4 c  Scallions, thinly sliced
1 tb Ginger, minced
1 tb Garlic, minced
2 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Chinese wine or sherry
1/2 ts Kosher salt
Mix and wrap. Source: China Moon cookbook
