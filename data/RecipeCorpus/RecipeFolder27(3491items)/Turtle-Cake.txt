Title: Turtle Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
1 c  Semi sweet chocolate chips
1 c  Pecans; chopped
1 pk German chocolate cake mix
14 oz Caramels
3/4 c  Margarine or butter
Preheat oven to 350~. Grease and flour 9x13 baking dish. Prepare cake
according to package directions. Pour half of the cake mix into
prepared dish and bake for 15 minutes. In a small saucepan over low
heat, melt caramels with butter and evaporated milk. Remove cake from
oven, pour melted caramel mix over top of cake. Top with chocolate
chips and pecans. Pour rest of batter over all and bake 20 minutes
longer.
