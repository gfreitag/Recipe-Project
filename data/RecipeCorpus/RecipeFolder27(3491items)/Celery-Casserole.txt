Title: Celery Casserole
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 1 casserole
1 cn Cream of chicken soup;
1    Section of Ritz creakers;
3 c  Celery; diced
1 cn Water chestnuts;
Boil diced celery in boiling salted water for 7 minutes.  Drain. Won't
overcook.  Drain and slice water chestnuts.  Combine with celery and
soup and put in casserole.  Melt margarine, crumble Ritz crackers in
margarine and sprinkle over casserole. Bake 350 F for 1 hour.  NOTE:
For large casserole, the celery can be increased. Source: Rachel
Miller, Our Daily Bread, Westmester Persbyterian Chruch. Brought to
you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
