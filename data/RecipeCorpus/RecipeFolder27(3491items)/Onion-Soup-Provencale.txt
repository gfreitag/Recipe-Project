Title: Onion Soup Provencale
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Soup/stew, Vegetable, French
Yield: 8 servings
6 oz Tomato paste
1/2 c  Red wine; optional
1/4 c  Fresh basil; chopped
2 tb Soy sauce
Freshly ground pepper; to ta
5 c  Water
4 lg Onions; sliced in rings
2 sm Zucchini; sliced
1 md Eggplant; peel/chop
- or 2 small japanese eggpla
2    Garlic cloves; crushed
Recipe by: The New McDougall Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:15 Combine
all of the ingredients in a large soup pot.  Bring to a boil, cover,
reduce the heat, and simmer over medium-low heat for about 1 hour, or
until the vegetables are tender.
