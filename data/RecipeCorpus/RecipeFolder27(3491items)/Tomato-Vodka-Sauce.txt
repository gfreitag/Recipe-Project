Title: Tomato-Vodka Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Alcohol, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Prepared spaghetti sauce
3 tb Vodka
1/2 ts Red pepper flakes
2 ts Olive oil
1/4 c  Diced Canadian bacon
3    Garlic clove(s)
-finely chopped
Heat oil in saucepan over medium heat. Add bacon and cook, stirring,
until lightly colored, about 2 minutes. Add garlic and cook,
stirring, until it is golden, about 30 seconds. Add spaghetti sauce,
vodka and red pepper flakes and stir until heated through. SErve over
cooked pasta.

Makes 2 cups.
