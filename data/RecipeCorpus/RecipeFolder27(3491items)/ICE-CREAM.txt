Title: ICE CREAM
Categories: Dessert, Ice cream
Yield: 1 servings
2    Eggs
1/4 ts Salt
Rock salt
Ice cream freezer
1 qt Half and Half; plus
3 c  Half and Half
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb Vanilla
Beat eggs.  Add sugar, half and half, vanilla, salt. Mix together.
Freeze in ice cream freezer.

Yield 2.5 quarts.
