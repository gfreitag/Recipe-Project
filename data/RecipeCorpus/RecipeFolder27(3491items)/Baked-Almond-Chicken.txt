Title: Baked Almond Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Chicken, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
7 tb Butter (or marg.); melted,
-divided
3/4 c  Almonds, sliced
1 1/2 c  Half-and-half
1/2 c  Sour cream
3 tb Breadcrumbs, dry
1    Chicken, fryer; cut into
-pieces
Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Celery salt
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Oregano
Wash chicken and pat dry.  Shake pieces of chicken in bag containing
flour. Combine celery salt, paprika, salt, curry powder, oregano,
pepper, and 6 tablespoons melted butter.  Roll floured chicken in
seasoned butter.

Arrange chicken in a single layer in a 13x9x2" baking dish; sprinkle
with almonds and pour half-and-half over chicken.  Cover with foil;
bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.

Uncover, spoon out 1/2 cup liquid and combine with sour cream.  Pour
mixture over chicken.  Bake, uncovered, 30 minutes longer or until
tender.
