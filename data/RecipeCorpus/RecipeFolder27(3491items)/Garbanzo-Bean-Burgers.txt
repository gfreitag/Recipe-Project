Title: Garbanzo Bean Burgers
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Beef, Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Flour, whole wheat
2 ts Oil
Salt
Pepper
2 c  Garbanzo beans (chickpeas),
-mashed
1 ea Carrot, finely chopped
1/4    Small onion, minced
Mix the ingredients (except oil) in a bowl. Form 6 flat patties.
Fry in oiled pan over medium-high heat until burgers are golden brown
on each side.

Per serving: 133 calories; 3g fat (17% of calories from fat); 5g
protein; 23g carbohydrates; 34mg calcium; 1mg iron; 250mg sodium; 2g
dietary fiber.
