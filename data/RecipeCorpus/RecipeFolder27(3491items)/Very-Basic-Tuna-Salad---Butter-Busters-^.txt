Title: Very Basic Tuna Salad - Butter Busters ^
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Salad, Pickles
Yield: 8 servings
6 ea Hard boiled egg whites,
-chopped
1/4 t  Celery salt
1/2 c  Chopped celery, optional
2 c  (6 1/8oz) tuna in water,
-drained and chopped
1/2 c  Kraft fatfree mayonnaise or
-Miracle Whip
1/2 c  Sweet pickle rellish
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and chill. Serve on sandwiches or
stuffed in a tomato. Per serving: 100 cal., 0.4g fat, (4%),27mg
chol., 0g fiber, 15g pro., 9g carb., 740mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam
