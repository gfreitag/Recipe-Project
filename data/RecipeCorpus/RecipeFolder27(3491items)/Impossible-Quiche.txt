Title: Impossible Quiche
Categories: Bread, Pork, Chicken, Cheese/eggs, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 6 SERVINGS--------------------------
4 oz Cheddar; shredded
1 1/2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Bisquick
3 ea Eggs
Salt and pepper
1 c  Green beans; cooked
4 oz Mushroom pieces; drained
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1 c  Ham or chicken; cooked &
-chopped
Sprinkle green beans, mushrooms, onion, ham or chicken, and cheese in
a deep 10" pie plate.  In blender place milk, Bisquick, eggs, salt
and pepper to taste.  Blend 15 seconds on high speed.  Pour over the
above. Bake in preheated 400 degree oven for 30 minutes.  Remove from
oven and let set 5 minutes.  Serve.
