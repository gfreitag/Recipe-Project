Title: CARMELS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
1 c  Brown Sugar
1 c  Butter
1 c  White Syrup
1 tb Vanilla
1 c  Heavy Cream
Cook slowly to 247 degrees, 1 1/2 to 2 hours cooking time. Pour into
8 x 8 inch heavily buttered pan. Cool, cut, and wrap each piece in
wax paper.

From the Kronborg Kitchen Kapers Cookbook 1975 Contributed by - Sena
Hermansen
