Title: CATHERINE WHEEL COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour; all purpose
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 c  Currants
1/2 c  Almonds; ground
2 ts Anise seeds
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
5/8 c  Butter; melted or 10 Tbsp
1    Eggs; beaten
Preheat oven to 400F. Mix together flour, baking powder, currants,
almonds, anise seeds, sugar and cinnamon. Add cooled melted butter
and egg, stir to form soft dough. Pinch off walnut sized pieces, roll
into balls, and place on ungreased cookie sheets. Flatten with fork
to make crisscross pattern and bake in preheated 400F oven for 10 to
12 minutes or till lightly browned. Cool on wire rack. Yield: 2 1/2
dozen
