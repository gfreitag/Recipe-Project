Title: CHAMPIONSHIP CHICKPEA DIP
Categories: Dip, Bean:garban, Super bowl
Yield: 2 Cups
19 oz Can chickpeas,rinse & drain
1/3 c  Prepared pesto
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper, ground
Process all ingredients until smooth.  Serve with crackers and potato
chips. Source: Cincinnati Enquirer 1/19/97
