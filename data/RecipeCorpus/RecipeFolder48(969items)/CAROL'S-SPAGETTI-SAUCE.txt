Title: CAROL'S SPAGETTI SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Pasta
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lg Can tomato juice
1 sm Can tomato paste
Garlic
Oragano
Basil
1 lb Hamburger
1    Egg
Bread crumbs
Garlic
Med italian sausage
In large saucepan mix juice, tomato paste and seasonings. In bowl mix
hamburg, bread crumbs, garlic and egg. Make into small balls and add
to sauce and cook until thickened. I boil medium spiced italian
sausage then cut up and add to sauce.
