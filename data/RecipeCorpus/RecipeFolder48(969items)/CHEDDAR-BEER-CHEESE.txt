Title: CHEDDAR BEER CHEESE
Categories: Spreads, Cheese/eggs, Gifts
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  (16 ounces) grated sharp
-Cheddar Cheese at room
-temperature
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
1/3 c  Flat beer
Place all in blender or food processor. Process till smooth. Cover and
refrigerate overnight. Serve at room temperature with crackers. Yield:
about 2 cups spread.
