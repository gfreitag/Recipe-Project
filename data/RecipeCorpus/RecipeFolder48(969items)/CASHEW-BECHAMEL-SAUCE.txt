Title: CASHEW BECHAMEL SAUCE
Categories: Vegetarian, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Cashews, raw, whole
2 tb Whole wheat pastry flour
1 c  ;water or vegetable stock
1/4 ts Sea salt, or to taste
pn Cayenne
1/4 c  Parsley; finely minced
Place the cashews, flour, water or stock, salt, and cayenne in a
blender (a food processor will not work as well). Blend until very
smooth and creamy.

Pour the nixture into a small saucepan. Bring to a boil over high
heat, stirring constantly. Add the parsley and mix.

Serve over steamed vegetables such as broccoli or cauliflower.
