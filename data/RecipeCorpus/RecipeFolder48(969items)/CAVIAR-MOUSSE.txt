Title: CAVIAR MOUSSE
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
7    Hard-Boiled Eggs
2 tb Lemon Juice
2 tb Water
1 sm Package Unflavored Gelatin
6    Anchovy Fillets
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 pn Onion Powder
Pepper To Taste
3 1/2 oz Caviar
Combine all ingredients and refrigerate until ready to serve.
