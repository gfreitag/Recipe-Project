Title: CATSKILL MOUNTAIN FRENCH
Categories: Jewish, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
-DONNA CELIERO
7 oz Warm water
3 c  Bread flour
2 ts Sugar
2 ts Salt
1 tb Butter/margarine
2 ts Red star yeast
2 tb Sesame seeds
The flour is an almost 3 cups -- go light on the measuring and watch
the dough ball. Use the crispy (french) mode.
