Title: CARDIGANSHIRE SAVOURIES-TOCYN Y CARDI(WELSH)
Categories: Brakefast
Yield: 2 Servings
1 tb Rolled oats
1 tb Flour
2 tb Milk
1 ts Baking powder
Salt
Mix the dry ingredients together in a basin and bind together with the
milk. Shape the mixture into small cakes and fry in bacon fat. Serve
with fried bacon and tomatoes. Serves two.
