Title: CHARLIE BROWN'S MOTHER'S BUTTERED OVEN-POTATO
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
4 md Potatoes, Peeled
2 tb Butter, Melted
Ice Water
Salt And Pepper
Slice the potatoes into lengths (like French fries). Soak for 1 hour
in ice water.  Remove and dry with paper towels.  Dip each piece of
potato into the melted butter and arrange in a shallow baking pan.
Salt lightl with a salt shaker.  Cook in a preheated 375 - 400 Degree
F. oven until the potatoes are brown and tender when poked with a
long fork.
