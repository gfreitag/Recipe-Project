Title: CELERY SEED - HONEY DRESSING
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Paprika
4 tb Vinegar
1 ts Celery seed
1 ts Dry mustard
1/3 c  Honey
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Grated onion
1 c  Salad oil
Mix dry ingredients.  Add honey, lemon juice, vinegar and onion.
Pour oil slowly into mixture, beating constantly. Serve with fruit
salad.
