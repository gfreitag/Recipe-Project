Title: CARROT-RAISIN SALAD WITH PEANUT BUTTER
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Grated carrots
1/4 c  Peanut butter
1 tb Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Raisins
Place carrots in a medium size mixing bowl. Add peanut butter and
mayonnaise. Stire gently until well combined. Add raisins and mix
gently but well. Refrigate until serving time.

makes about 4 serving
