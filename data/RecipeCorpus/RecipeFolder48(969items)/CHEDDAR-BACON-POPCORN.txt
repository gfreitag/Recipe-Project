Title: CHEDDAR BACON POPCORN
Categories: Popcorn
Yield: 10 Cups
-Sue Woodward
5 sl Bacon; cooked crisp and
-crumbled
10 c  Popped corn
1/4 lb Sharp cheddar cheese; finely
-grated
Preheat oven to 250~.  In a lg. baking pan, combine bacon, popcorn,
and cheese.  Bake for 3 to 5 mins., or until cheese is just melted.

Source:  Theatre Popcorn Popper leaflet
