Title: CARNE DE PUERCO EN CHILE VERDE (PORK WITH GRE
Categories: Mexican, Meats, Ethnic
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Pork (or beef)
Oil or lard
4    Green chiles, toasted,
- peeled and chopped
2    Garlic cloves; crushed
1 md Onion; chopped
1 md Tomato; chopped
1 c  Boiling water
Salt, pepper
Cut pork or beef into small cubes. Saute in oil until well browned.
Drain off all but 1 tablespoon fat. Add chopped chiles, garlic,
onion, tomato and water and season to taste with salt and pepper.
Cover tightly and simmer 1 hour.
