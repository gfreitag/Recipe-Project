Title: CAROB MINT ICE CREAM
Categories: Vegetarian, Mint, Ice cream
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Frozen bananas
1/2 c  To 3/4 water
2 tb Nuts - OR -
1 tb Peanut butter
1/16 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Carob powder
1/8 ts Mint-peppermint flavor.
