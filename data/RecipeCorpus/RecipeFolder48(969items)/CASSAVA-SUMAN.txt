Title: CASSAVA SUMAN
Categories: Desserts, Phillipines
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 c  Cassava; grated
1/2 c  Coconut; grated or chopped
3/4 c  Sugar
Banana leaves
Mix all ingredients and wrap mixture in 2 layers of wilted banana
leaf in size desired.  Tie by twos with string. Boil in water until
done. *Cassava is also know as Yucca root.
