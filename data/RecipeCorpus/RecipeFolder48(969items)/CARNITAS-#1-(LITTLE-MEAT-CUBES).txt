Title: CARNITAS #1 (LITTLE MEAT CUBES)
Categories: Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Lean pork
Salt and pepper to taste
A dusting of pasilla pepper,
-ground
Cut the pork into 1" cubes.  Dust with salt, pepper, and pasilla
pepper, and let rest for 1 hour.  Place the meat in a shallow baking
pan in a 300 degree oven for about 2 hours. Pour or siphon off the
fat from time to time. Drain on paper towels and serve with Salsa
Cruda and/or Guacamole. Makes 25 to 30 carnitas.

Source: "Authentic Mexican Cooking" by Paula Holt and Helene Juarez
:: MM by Sue Woodward
