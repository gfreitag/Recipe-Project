Title: CARROT CAKE 3
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 1 Cake
2 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
2 ts Cinammon
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Applesauce
4 c  Grated carrots
1 1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
MMMMM---------------------------ICING--------------------------------
1    Stick butter, softened
8 oz Cream chese, softened
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Almond extract
1 lb Powdered sugar (up to 1.5
-lbs may be needed)
cake directions: sift together dry ingredients. beat in applesauce
and eggs until well blended. stir in carrots and nuts. pour into a
greased and floured pan and bake at 350F for 50-60 minutes.

icing directions: beat first 4 ingredients until smooth. gradually
beat in powdered sugar until desired consistency.

Enjoy!
