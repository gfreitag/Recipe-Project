Title: CHEDDAR CHEESE BREAD NVN
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
-DEICHMA1   [Diana/SysOp]
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1/4 c  Nonfat dry milk
1 tb SOFT butter
1 ts Salt
2 tb Sugar
1 1/4 c  Warm water
1 1/2 c  Grated sharp cheddar cheese
-(at room temperature)
1/3 c  Parmesan cheese
1 ts Coarse black pepper
In the order listed, put ingredients in pan, select white bread, and
push start.
