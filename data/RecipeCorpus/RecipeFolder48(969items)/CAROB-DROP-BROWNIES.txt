Title: CAROB DROP BROWNIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Batch
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil
1/2 c  Maple syrup
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Carob powder
1 3/4 c  AM Barley Flour
1 ts Non-alum baking powder
1/4 ts Sea salt (optional)
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Mix first four ingredients together until smooth. Stir dry ingredients
together.  Combine mixtures. Drop dough from a teaspoon onto oiled
baking sheets, spaced about 2 inches apart. Bake 8-12 minutes at 375
F. Cool on sheets.
