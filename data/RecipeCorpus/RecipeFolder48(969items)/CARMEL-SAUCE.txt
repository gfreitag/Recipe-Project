Title: CARMEL SAUCE
Categories: A.b.c., Cakes, Candies, Desserts, Sauces/dips
Yield: 2 Quart
1 lb Brown sugar
1 lb Granulated sugar
2 oz Flour
1 qt Water
8 oz Butter
1 tb Vanilla extract
Combine sugars, flour, and water.  Boil until thick( this could take
as long as 30 - 40 minutes). Add margarine and vanilla stir in til
absorbed.
