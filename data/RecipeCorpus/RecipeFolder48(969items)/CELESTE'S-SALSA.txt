Title: CELESTE'S SALSA
Categories: Appetizers, Condiments, Dressings
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Tomato; chopped fine
1/2    Onion; chopped fine
1/2    Celery; chopped fine
1/2    Green bell pepper; chopped f
3 tb Green chiles; chopped fine
2 tb Wine vinegar
1 ts Mustard seed
1 ts Coriander; ground
1 ds Black pepper; coarse
Preparation Time: 0:15 Finely chop all vegetables. Mix well. Combine
vinegar, mustard seed, coriander, and pepper. Pour over vegetables
and mix. Chill. Store tightly covered in refrigerator. Keeps well.
