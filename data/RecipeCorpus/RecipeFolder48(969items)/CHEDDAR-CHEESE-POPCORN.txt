Title: CHEDDAR CHEESE POPCORN
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Unpopped popcorn
1/3 c  Butter
1 c  Finely grated cheese, (use
-more if you want it
-cheesier)
-
-salt to taste
-pepper
-(cheddar, brick, or colby)
Pop the popcorn.  Melt the butter.  Grind some pepper into the butter.
Stir.  Layer the cheese into the popcorn. Pour the butter mixture on
top, and salt.
