Title: Bread or Invalid Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Egg
Seasoning
2 ea Bread; slices
3 c  Water; hot
Cut bread in 1/2 inch cubes, brown in butter in frying pan. When
brown, pour hot water over bread. Break egg into this and stir.
Season with salt and nutmeg. Source: Mabel Miller, Genoa Grange,
Ottawa County, OH
