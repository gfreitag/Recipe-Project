Title: Asparagus and Bulgur
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Basil
1/4 ts Salt
3 oz Asparagus; cut into 1/2-inch
-pieces
3 oz Bulgur; cooked
1 tb Sunflower oil
Steam asparagus for about 10 minutes. Combine all ingredients and mix
well. Serve at room temperature.
