Title: Bloody Mary Bread
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
6 oz Can of spicy V-8 juice; at
-room temperature
1 tb Vodka
1 tb Water
1    Egg
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Soft butter
Place all ingredients into pan, select white bread and push start.

Serve with a stalk of celery.

From Loafing It by DAK
