Title: San Francisco Vegetable Soup
Categories: Vegetarian, Soup/stew
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Vegetable bouillon powder
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Granulated garlic OR
2 ts Fresh garlic; minced
1/4 c  Snow pea pods; diagonally
-sliced
1/4 c  Onions; sliced
1/4 c  Carrots; thinly sliced
1/4 c  Bok choy; thinly sliced
1/4 c  Red bell pepper; thinly slid
1 ts Sesame or vegetable oil
3 c  ;water
In a medium saucepan, saute onons, carrots, bok choy and bell
peppeer in oilover medium heat until vegetables soften, about 5
minutes.

Add water, bouillon powder, ginger and garlic. Simmer until
vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.

Add snow peas, cook for one minute and serve hot.

Per serving: 67 cal; 2 g prot; 182 mg sod; 12 g carb; 2 g fat; 0 mg
chol; 27 mg calcium

Source: Vegetarian Gourmet, Winter 93/MM by DEEANNE
