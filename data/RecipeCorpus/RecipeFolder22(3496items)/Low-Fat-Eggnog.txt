Title: Low-Fat Eggnog
Categories: Beverage, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Light rum
1 ts Vanilla
Ground nutmeg
4 c  Skim milk
12 oz Evaporated skim milk
8 oz Frozen egg product, thawed
1/2 c  Sugar
Over medium heat stir skim milk, evaporated skim milk, egg product and
sugar about 10 minutes or until slightly thickened.  DO NOT BOIL.
Stir over a bowl of ice water 2 minutes.  Cover; chill 4 to 24 hours.
Stir in rum and vanilla.  Stir in more skim milk till desired
thickness. Sprinkle with ground nutmeg.

Serving size: 1/2 cup

99 calories (2% from fat); 1 g fat
