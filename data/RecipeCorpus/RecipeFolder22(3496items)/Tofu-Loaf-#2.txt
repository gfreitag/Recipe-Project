Title: Tofu Loaf #2
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Peanut butter
4 tb Tamari
2    Garlic cloves; pressed
1/2 ts Sage
2 c  Tofu; mashed
1 c  Onion; chopped
1 c  Rolled oats
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
Place all the ingredients in a large bowl. Mix well, using your
hands if necessary.

Press the mixture into a well-oiled loaf pan.

Run a knife around the edges of the pan and unmold th eloaf onto a
serving platter. Surround the platter with colorful ssteamed
vegetables and serve with a sauce.
