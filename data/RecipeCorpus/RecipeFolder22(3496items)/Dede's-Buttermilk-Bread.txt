Title: Dede's Buttermilk Bread
Categories: Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Salt
1 T  Butter or margarine
2 T  Honey
1/4 t  Baking soda*
1 1/2 t  Red Star active dry yeast
7/8 c  Buttermilk or
3 T  Dry buttermilk powder and
7/8 c  Water
(for Welbilt/Dak machines
Add 1 T more buttermilk)
2 c  Bread flour
* Prodigy "research" has shown that if using powdered buttermilk we
should omit the baking soda.

Place all ingredients in bread pan, select Light Crust setting, and
pres Start.

From Bread Machine Magic by Linda Rehberg and Lois Conway.
