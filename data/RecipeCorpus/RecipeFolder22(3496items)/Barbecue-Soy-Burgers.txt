Title: Barbecue Soy Burgers
Categories: Vegetarian, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Fine bread crumbs
1    Egg
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Sesame seeds
1 sm Onion, minced
2    Garlic cloves, mashed
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 c  Cooked soybeans, drained
These nutty burgers are a barbecue favorite, great on whole-wheat
onion buns, with the traditional toppings of tomato, cheese, lettuce
and so on.

Freshly ground black pepper to taste

In a small pan, saute onion and garlic in butter or margarine, and
cook until translucent. Set aside. In food processor, whirl beans,
bread crumbs, egg, soy sauce and sesame seeds to a coarse meal.
Transfer to a medium bowl, add garlic- onion mixture and black
pepper, and mix well. Shape into 4 patties. Heat on grill till brown,
giving them about 5 minutes each side.

Makes 4 burgers. Source: Boutique Bean Pot
