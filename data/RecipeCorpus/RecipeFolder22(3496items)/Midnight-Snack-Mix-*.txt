Title: Midnight Snack Mix *
Categories: Snack
Yield: 11 cups
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
1 c  Pecan halves
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Honey
1 ts Grated orange peel
4 c  Mini cinnamon bun cereal
3 c  Cinnamon bear graham cracker
2 c  Chow mein noodles
1 1/2 c  Raisins
Heat oven to 350~. In large bowl, combine cereal, graham crackers,
chow mein noodles, raisins and pecan halves. Melt margarine in small
saucepan; stir in honey and orange peel. Pour over cereal mix; toss
to coat well. Spread evenly in ungreased jelly-roll pan. Bake for
11-13 minutes or until mixture is glazed, stirring once during
baking. Spread on foil to cool completely. Store in tightly covered
container.
