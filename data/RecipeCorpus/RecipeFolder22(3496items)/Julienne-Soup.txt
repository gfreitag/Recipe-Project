Title: Julienne Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
Text Only
Shred carrots, turnips, celery, potato, and onion; or cut in very
narrow strips.  Cook in boiling salted water until tender. Drain. Add
to hot bouillon or consomme in the proportion of 2 tablespoons cooked
vegetables to each cup of bouillon or consomme. Heat thoroughly.
