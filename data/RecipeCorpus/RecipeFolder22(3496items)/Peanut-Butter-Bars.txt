Title: Peanut Butter Bars
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 20 servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar,firmly packed
1 c  Flour,sifted
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/4 c  Butter
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 c  Coconut,flaked
1 pk Chocolate pieces,semisweet
1 ts Vanilla
3    Eggs,well beaten
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar,firmly packed
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1. Preheat oven to 350'F.

2. Blend peanut butter and butter together; gradually beat in sugar;
work in flour with back of a spoon. Pat into bottom of greased 8"
square pan. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, make
the tortelike topping.

*** TOPPING ***

Beat eggs with brown sugar until creamy. Blend in flour and baking
powder, then fold in remaining ingredients. Pour over baked peanut
butter mixture and bake 30 minutes more at same temperature. Cool
thoroughly in pan. Cut in squares.
