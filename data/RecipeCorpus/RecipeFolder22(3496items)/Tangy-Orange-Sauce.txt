Title: Tangy Orange Sauce
Categories: Vegetarian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Ginger
1/4 c  Maple syrup
2    Oranges; peeled, segmented
1/2 c  Orange juice
Combine all the ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a simmer over
medium-high heat. Cook for 1 minute and serve hot. Good over vanilla
Rice Dream and any kind of kebob.
