Title: Tomatillo Guacamole
Categories: Tex/mexican, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
Diced, 1/2" cubes.
Juice of 1/2 lime
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes
Salt and pepper to taste
1 sm Clove garlic
1    Shallot, medium, peeled
2 tb Cilantro, coarse chop
2    Tomatillos,cleaned,quartered
1    Avocado, cleaned, pitted>>>
Mince together garlic, shallot, cilantro and tomatillos in food
processor or by hand.  Spoon into serving bowl.  Stir in avocado, lime
juice, red pepper flakes and salt and pepper to taste.  Set aside 5
minutes for flavors to blend.

Goes good with MM#: 32--Black Bean Roll-ups
