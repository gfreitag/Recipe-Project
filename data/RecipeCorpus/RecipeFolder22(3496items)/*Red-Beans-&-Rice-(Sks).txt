Title: *Red Beans & Rice (Sks)
Categories: Cajun/creol, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 t  Cajun Spice Mix (McDougall)
1/2 t  Cilantro
3/4 t  Chili Powder
2 t  Salt
1 ea Large red onion, diced
4 ea Stalks of celery, sliced
1/4 c  Red pepper, diced
1 ea 28 0z Can crushed tomatoes
2 ea 16 oz cans red beans
Grease non-stick stew pot with 2 tsp olive oil. Saute onions, celery
and red peppers. Add crushed tomatoes. Add spices. Simmer for 15
minutes. Add RINSED kidney beans. Heat through. Spoon over brown rice.
