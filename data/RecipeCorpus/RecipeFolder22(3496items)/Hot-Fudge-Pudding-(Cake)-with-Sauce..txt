Title: Hot Fudge Pudding (Cake) with Sauce.
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Milk
*Sauce*
3/4 c  Sugar
3 tb Cocoa
2 tb Water
2 c  HOT water
1 c  Flour
1/3 c  Cocoa
3/4 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking Powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Shortening
1    Egg
Servings:  4

Preheat to 350F

Sift dry ingrediants in a bowl. Cut in shortening. Add egg & milk.
Stir til flour is moist. Put batter in 9" cake pan. Mix sauce well,
pour over batter slowly. Bake for 40 min. Serve with milk or cream
(while warm)
