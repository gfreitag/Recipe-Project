Title: Zesty Cilantro, Chili, Pea Dip
Categories: Vegetarian, Snack
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Pepper
Salt to taste
4 c  Raw vegetables of choice
1/4 c  Cilantro; chopped
1 cn Green Ortega chilis; diced,
-drained (4 oz)
1 pk Mori-Nu silken firm tofu;
-drained (10.5)
In a food processor or blender, combine all ingredients until smooth.
Whirl approximately 30 seconds on high.

Refrigerate 1 hour. Mound over overlapping tomato slices. Rim with
fresh cilantro. Serve with fresh cut vegetables.

Makes 8 servings of 1/4 cup each.
