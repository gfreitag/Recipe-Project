Title: I Can't Believe It's All Veggie Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetarian, Vegetable, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  Pinto beans, cooked
Pepper to taste
1/2 ts Basil, dried
(or 2 T fresh)
1 cn Green beans, cut
1 c  Okra, frozen or fresh, cut
Salt to taste, optional
1/2 c  TVP Chunks
2    Garlic cloves
1 c  Chopped onions
1 c  Carrot chunks
5 ts Vegetable Broth Powder
1/4 c  Uncle Ben's Conv. Rice
2 cn Hunt's No-Salt Tomatoes
3 1/2 c  Water
Directions:
Place all ingredients in crockpot. Cover and cook until done, about
6 hours.  Serving size: 1 Cup
12 Servings: 120 Cal, 8.25 Prot, Chol 0, Carb 21.5, Sodium 255,
Fiber 2, Fat .5, Iron 5, Calcium 65, % Cal from Prot: 27% / Carb: 71%
/ Fat 2%
Update of old family favorite - Alice in Houston 5/07/93
