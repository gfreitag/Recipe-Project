Title: *Don's Piquant Salad Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Salsa/sauce, Salad, Diabetic
Yield: 6 servings
1    Garlic clove minced
1 tb Chopped chives
2 tb Plain yogurt
1 ts Tabasco sauce
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/3 c  Basil vinegar
1 tb Black pepper fresh ground
2 tb Romano cheese, grated
Mix all ingredients together. Chill for 1-3 hours. Shake well before
pouring over salad. Origin: Don's kitchen circa 1979
