Title: Walnut Burgers
Categories: Vegetarian, Asian, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Tamari
2 tb Brewers yeast
1/2 ts Basil
2 c  Herb seasoned Pepperidge
Farm stuffing
1    10.5 oz package More-Nu
Silken/soft tofu
1 c  Walnuts, chopped
1/4 c  Sunflower seeds
1/4 c  Scallions
In a food processor or blender, puree tofu, then add rest of
ingredients except stuffing.

Put stuffing into a bowl and pour tofu mixture over it. Mix well.

Form into 8 burger patties and bake on a greased cookie sheet in a
350 degree oven for 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown.

Serve on a burger bun with your favorite condiments.

Note:  The mixture may also be formed into 24 balls and served in
spaghetti sauce over pasta.

Recipe by Shirley Wilkes-Johnson
