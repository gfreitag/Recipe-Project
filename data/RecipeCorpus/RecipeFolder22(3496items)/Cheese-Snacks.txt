Title: Cheese Snacks
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 lb Butter
1 c  Grated Cheese
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cayenne Pepper
Sift the first 3 ingredients together.  Work in butter and cheese.
Form into a long roll.  Chill and slice.  Bake in a 350Ã¸F oven for 12
to 15 minutes.  May be kept in refrigerator, sliced and baked as
