Title: Whole Wheat Pizza Dough
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 16 servings
1 ts Salt
2 1/2 c  Whole-wheat flour
Cornmeal
1 pk Yeast, dry active
1 c  Warm water (110 degrees)
1 ts Honey
1. In medium sized bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water with honey.
Stir remaining ingredients; beat vigorously 20 strokes. Be sure all
flour is absorbed.
Form into ball and let rest 5 minutes.

2. Spray pizza pan with PAM or use non stick 14 inch pizza pan;
sprinkle with cornmeal.

3. Divide dough in half.  (This recipe makes enough dough for 2
crusts. If your only making one pizza, freeze extra crust after
rolling out) With lightly floured rolling pin, rolldough on very
lightly floured surface into 15 inch circle.  Place dough over pizza
pan and press down to fit, leaving edges overlappin about 1 inch.
COntinue with pizza recipe for toppings and bakeing instructions. ==
