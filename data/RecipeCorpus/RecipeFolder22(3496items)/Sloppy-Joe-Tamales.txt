Title: Sloppy Joe Tamales
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Tex/mexican
Yield: 9 servings
-papers
1    Basic Masa Dough recipe
18    Corn husks or parchment
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
5 ts Canola or safflower oil
3 tb Onion flakes, dried
2 ts Oregano
1 1/2 ts Basil
1 lb Tempeh burgers; prepared;
-crumbled
1 1/2 c  Tomato paste
2 ts Vinegar, brown rice
3 ts Tamari
To make filling: Combine filling ingredients in a mixing bowl. Stir
to combine.

Fill and cook tamales according to "Tamales: Basic Procedure".

Per serving (2 tamales): 452 cal; 12 g prot; 277 mg sod; 78 g carb;
9 g fat; 0 mg chol; 69 mg calcium

Vegetarian Gourmet, Summer 1993/MM by DEEANNE
