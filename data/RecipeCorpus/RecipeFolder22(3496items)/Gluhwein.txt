Title: Gluhwein
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 10 servings
Fresh grated nutmeg to taste
3    Mace blades
4 c  Red wine
10    Orange slices
10    Cinnamon sticks
1    Orange
8    Whole cloves
1 1/4 c  Cold water
1/4 c  Sugar
3    Cinnamon sticks (3")
Stud orange with cloves and put into a saucepan. Add water, sugar,
cinnamon, nutmeg and mace.

Heat gently, stirring to dissolve sugar. Bring to a boil, then reduce
heat and simmer 5 minutes.

Add red wine and heat through gently. Strain and serve hot in
heatproof glasses. Garnish with orange slices and cinnamon sticks.
