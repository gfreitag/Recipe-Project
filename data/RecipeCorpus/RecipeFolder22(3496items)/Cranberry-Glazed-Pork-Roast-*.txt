Title: Cranberry Glazed Pork Roast *
Categories: Pork, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
1/2 ts Grated orange peel
2 tb Orange juice
2 tb Dry sherry
16 oz Whole berry cranberry sauce
4 lb Boneless pork loin
2 ts Cornstarch
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Salt
In smalll saucepan stir together all ingredients except pork. Cook,
stirring over medium heat, until thickened; set aside. Place roast in
shallow baking dish. Roast at 325~ for 45 minutes. Spoon 1/2 cup
glaze over the roast and continue baking for 30-45 minutes more,
until internal temperature is 155~-160~. Let stand 10 minutes before
slicing and serving with remaining sauce.
