Title: Court Bouillon
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
BOUQUET GARNI:
4    Parsley sprigs
1    Bay leaf
1/2 ts Dried thyme
2 md Onions
2 md Carrots
2 md Celery stalks
3 c  Dry white wine
2 tb Salt
Tie the parsley, bay leaf and thyme in washed cheesecloth.  Peel and
mince the onions and carrots.  Mince the celery.  Place the minced
vegetables and bouquet garni in a medium stockpot.  Add the wine,
salt and 8 quarts water. Bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 15
minutes.

Use for poaching lobster or whole fish (simmer for about 8 minutes,
covered)
