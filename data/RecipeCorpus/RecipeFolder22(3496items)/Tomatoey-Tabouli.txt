Title: Tomatoey Tabouli
Categories: Salad, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Parsley
2 tb Onions, dried
Cucumber salad dressing
1 c  Bulgur wheat
1 cn Tomatoes, whole
Water
1 tb Garlic salt
1/2 c  Oil, olive
Drain tomatoes well, reserving liquid.  Add enough water to reserved
liquid to measure 1 cup and bring to boil. Mix together bulgur,
boiling liquid, and garlic salt. Cover for 30 minutes.

Add remaining ingredients to bulgur mixture and refrigerate. Serve
with cucumber dressing.
