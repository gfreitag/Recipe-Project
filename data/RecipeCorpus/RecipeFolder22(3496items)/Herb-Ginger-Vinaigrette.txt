Title: Herb Ginger Vinaigrette
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Mustard
1 t  Chopped parsley
1/4 t  Chopped shallots
1/4 t  Salt
1/4 t  Freshly ground black
-pepper
2    -inch cube fresh ginger,
-minced
1 bn Chervil, washed and patted
-dry, leaves & tops only
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Rice wine vinegar
2 T  Lemon juice
Place everything in a jar and shake well.
