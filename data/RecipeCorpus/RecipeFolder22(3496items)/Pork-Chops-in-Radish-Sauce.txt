Title: Pork Chops in Radish Sauce
Categories: Tex/mexican, Meat, Pork, Salsa/sauce, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Tomatoes; Chopped, 2 Medium
Hot Cooked Rice
2 tb Vegetable Oil
6    Pork Loin Or Rib Chops; *
1 ts Salt
MMMMM-----------------RADISH AND CILANTRO RELISH----------------------
2 tb Fresh Cilantro; Snipped Fine
2 tb Vegetable Oil
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper; Freshly Ground
2 c  Radishes; Thinly Sliced
1/2 c  Onion, Chopped, 1 Medium
3 tb Orange Juice
2 tb Lime Juice
*    Pork Chops should be about 1/2-inch thick.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Prepare Radish and Cilantro Relish and set aside.  Heat oil in a
10-inch skillet until hot.  Cook pork chops in oil over medium heat
until brown; sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Remove pork from the
skillet.  Cook and stir relish and tomatoes in the same skillet for 5
minutes.  Add pork chops and heat to boiling; then reduce the heat.
Cover and simmer until pork is done, about 45 minutes.  Serve with
rice.  Garnish with the snipped fresh cilantro if desired. RADISH AND
CILANTRO RELISH: Mix all ingredients in a glass or plastic bowl.
Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour.
