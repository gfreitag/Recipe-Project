Title: Cookie & Candy Snack Mix *
Categories: Snack, Chocolate
Yield: 10 cups
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
3 c  Candy bar minatures; unwrap
10 oz Mini butter cookies; -=OR=-
- mini chocolate chip cookie    1 1/2 c  M&M's, plain or peanut
7 1/2 oz Bite-size oreos
In large bowl, combine all ingredients; toss gently. Store in tightly
covered container.
