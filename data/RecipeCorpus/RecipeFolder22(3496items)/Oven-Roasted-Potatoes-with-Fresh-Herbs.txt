Title: Oven-Roasted Potatoes with Fresh Herbs
Categories: Vegetarian, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Paprika
1/8 ts Dried sage leaves
2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1 ts Salt
Vegetable oil spray
2 lb Red potatoes, small
2 tb Vegetable stock
1 ts Fresh rosemary; chopped OR
1/4 ts Dried rosemary
2 ts Fresh parsley; chopped OR
1 ts Dried parsley
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.

Clean and quarter the potatoes. Toss with the remaining ingredients
until well coated.

Lightly wipe or spray a baking pan with vegetable oil.
Place the potatoes in a single layer on the pan and bake for 30
minutes, until the potatoes are soft and brown.
