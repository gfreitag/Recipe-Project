Title: Broccoli Bud Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Chicken or vegetable stock
2 c  Skim milk
2 c  Coarsely chopped broccoli
1/2 c  Cooked macaroni
1 ds Nutmeg
1 c  Low-fat yogurt (garnish)
1 sm Carrot, sliced
1    Stalk celery, sliced
1 sm Onion, chopped
1    Garlic clove, minced
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/4 ts Basil
Servings:  4

Place carrots, celery, onions, garlic, marjoram, basil and stock in
medium size saucepan.  Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover, simmer for
10 minutes. Add the milk and broccoli.  Return to boil, reduce heat,
cover and simmer for 5 to 7 minutes or until broccoli is tender.  Add
macaroni and nutmeg and heat through.

Remove some broccoli buds from the soup and set aside.  Place
remaining soup in a blender and process on low speed, until the soup
is smooth.

Return soup to pot with reserved broccoli buds and heat through.
