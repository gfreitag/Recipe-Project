Title: Parsley ``gravy''
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetarian
Yield: 10 servings
-soy sauce
2    Celery sticks
1/8 t  Celery seed
-powder
1 3/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Cashew nuts
1 1/2 T  Arrowroot powder
-(or 2 tablespoons whole
-wheat flour)  1/4 teaspoon
Using a blender, blend everything till smooth.  Carefully simmer in a
saucepan until thick.  Add 1/4 chopped parsley and serve. From: Jay
Stevens Date: 10-11-93
