Title: Snack Mix Bars *
Categories: Snack, Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
1/2 c  Margarine or butter
1/3 c  Peanut butter
10 oz Marshmallows
3 c  Corn squares cereal
2 c  Mini pretzels
1 c  Peanuts
1/2 c  M&M's
Grease 13x9" pan. In large bowl, combine cereal, pretzels, peanuts and
M&M's. Melt margarine in large saucepan over low heat; add peanut
butter and marshmallows. Heat until smooth and completely melted,
stirring occasionally. Pour over cereal mixture; toss to coat. With
wet fingers, press mixture evenly in greased pan. Cool completely.
Cut into bars.
