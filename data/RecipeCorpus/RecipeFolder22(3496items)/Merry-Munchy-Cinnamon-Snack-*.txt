Title: Merry Munchy Cinnamon Snack *
Categories: Snack
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
2 tb Brown sugar; packed
1 1/2 c  Cheese flavored crackers
1 c  Cinnamon roasted peanuts
4 c  Apple cinnamon cheerios
1 1/2 c  Mini pretzel twists
6 oz Dried fruit; diced
2 tb Margarine or butter; melted
Heat oven to 300~. Mix cereal, pretzels and dried fruits in
jelly-roll pan. Heat margarine and brown sugar over low heat. Pour
over cereal mixture; toss to coat. Bake 10 minutes, stirring once;
cool. Add cheese crackers and peanuts. Store in airtight container.
Makes 8 cups.
