Title: Navy Bean Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, 1941
Yield: 8 servings
Salt and pepper
2 c  Navy beans
1/3 lb Salt pork, diced
Wash beans.  Cover with water.  Add pork.  Cover.  Simmer until beans
are tender.  Add more water if necessary.  If desired, milk or cream
may be added just before beans are served.  Season to taste. 8
servings.
