Title: Whitewatergate Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 1 cake
MMMMM----------------------------CAKE---------------------------------
1 pk Instant pistachio pudding
3 ea Eggs
1 c  Club soda
1 pk White cake mix
1 c  Salad oil
1/2 c  Choppen pecans
MMMMM---------------------PISTACHIO FROSTING--------------------------
1 pk Instant pistachio pudding
2    Env. Dream Whip
1 1/2 c  Cold milk
Combine ingreds., beat 4 min. at med. speed of elec. mixer.  Pour
batter into prepared 10" tube pan.  Bake at 350 F. for 45-50 min.
Turn out on wire rack to cool.  Frost with Pistachio Frosting,
garnish with pecans.

PISTACHIO FROSTING:  Combine whipped topping mix and milk, beat at
high speed of elec. mixer until peaks form.  Add pudding mix and beat
until fluffy.   M-M-M GOOD!!!  (All calories are automatically baked
out.)
