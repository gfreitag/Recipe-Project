Title: Tofu Burgers 3
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  (or more) sesame seeds
1 sm Scrubbed potato
1/4 ts (or more) garlic powder
1 sm Onion OR onion powder to
Taste
1 lb Tofu
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
3 tb Nutritional yeast
2 tb Vegetable bouillon powder
Pepper to taste
2 tb Soy sauce
Grind the tofu, potato and onion (if using) in a food processor.
Don't let it get too fine. Mix in remaining ingredients, shape into
burgers and bake at around 350 degrees on a very lightly oiled cookie
sheet for 15-20 minutes, turning once. They can also be fried.

This recipe was the Judge's Choice award winner in the "Build a
Better Burger Contest" sponsored by the North American Vegetarian
Society.

Submitted by Helen Dessart, Otterburn Park, Quebec

From _Vegetarian Voice_, Vol. 19, No. 1
