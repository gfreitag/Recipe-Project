Title: Lemon Coconut Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 30 servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar; granulated
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Lemon juice from concentrate
3/4 c  Coconut; flaked
1 1/2 c  Flour; unsifted
1/2 c  Confectioners sugar
3/4 c  Margarine or butter; cold
4    Eggs; slightly beaten
In medium bowl, combine flour and confectioners' sugar, cut in
margarine until crumbly. Press onto bottom of lightly greased 13 x 9"
baking pan; bake @ 350 degrees for 15 minutes. In large bowl, mix
eggs, granulated sugar, baking powder and lemon juice. Pour over
crust; top with coconut. Bake 20 minutes or until golden. Cool. Cut
into bars. Store in refrigerator.
