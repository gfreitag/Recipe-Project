Title: Cranberry Pecan Stuffing *
Categories: Stuffing, Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 14 servings
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
16 oz Herb stuffing mix
1/2 c  Cranberries
1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
1/4 c  Margarine
2    Celery stalks; chopped
1 lg Onion; chopped
1 cn Chicken broth
In 4 quart saucepan over medium heat, melt margarine; add celery and
onions and cook until tender. Add broth. Heat to boiling. Remove from
heat. Add stuffing mix, cranberries and nuts; toss to coat well. Use
to stuff 12-14 lb turkey according to directions on turkey package.
Bake any remaining stuffing in covered casserole dish along with
turkey, for 30 minutes.
