Title: Sear's Vegetable Burgers
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Instant Rolled Oats
1/4 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Potatoes; mashed
1/4 c  Hazelnuts, chopped
1/3 c  Rice; cooked
Salt
Pepper
Olive Oil for Sauteing
1 tb Olive Oil
1 c  Onion; minced
1 c  Carrots; grated
1 c  Turnips; grated
1 c  Zucchini; grated
1 c  Beets; grated
1/2 tb Garlic; chopped
1/2 ts Cumin; ground
3/4 tb Dill & Tarragon; mixed
Heat oil in a large skillet.  Add onion, carrots, turnips, zucchini,
beets, garlic, cumin and herbs.  Saute until vegetables begin to
wilt, about 3 to 5 minutes.  Allow to cool slightly.

Soak oats in water for 5 minutes, then drain and press out excess
water. Add to vegetable mixture along with potatoes, hazelnuts and
rice. Season with salt and pepper.  Form mixture into 8 patties.

Grill patties on a well-oiled vegetable grill, or saute in olive oil
for 3 to 4 minutes on each side, until golden.

Nutrition:  106 Cal; 2G Prot; 4G Fat; 56G Carob; 0 Chol; 150MG sod; 2G
