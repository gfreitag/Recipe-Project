Title: Cream of Vegetable Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
Worcestorshire sauce to
-taste
2 ts Flour
1/2 c  Any cooked vegetable
1/4 ts Chicken bouillon
1 tb Celery
Salt & pepper to taste
1 tb Onion
1 c  Milk
1 tb Butter
Cook celery and onion in butter until soft.  Stir in flour, bouillon,
salt and pepper.  Add milk and worcestorshire sauce. Cook until
thickened plus 1 minute.  Blend vegetable with soup until smooth.
Makes 1 serving. Suggested combinations:  carrots and nutmeg, peas
and mint, potatoes and dill.
