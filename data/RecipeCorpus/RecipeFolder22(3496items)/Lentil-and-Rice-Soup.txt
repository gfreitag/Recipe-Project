Title: Lentil and Rice Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Ground cumin
1 ts Ground coriander
3    Chicken (or vegetable)
-bouillon cubes
6 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Lentils, washed
3 c  Brown rice, freshly cooked
1 lg Onion, chopped
2 tb Sunflower oil
2    Cloves garlic, diced
1 ts Fresh ground ginger
1 ts Curry powder
1 ts Turmeric
1 ts Chili powder
In a soup pot, cook onion in oil for about 10 minutes.  Add garlic and
spices; cook for 2 more minutes.  Add bouillon cubes, water and
lentils; bring soup to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for about 1
hour. Ladle into bowls over rice.

Serves 4.
