Title: Bulgur with Dried Berries and Scallions
Categories: Rice
Yield: 6 servings
1 sm Bunch scallions,
-trimmed and thinly sliced
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 1/2 c  Bulgur wheat
3 c  Cold water
1/4 c  Dried cranberries
1 1/2 ts Light olive oil
Combine bulgur and water in a deep bowl, cover and let stand for 30
minutes. Drain, squeeaing as much water out of bulgur with your hands
as possible. Transfer to a salad bowl.

Add cranberries, oil, scallions, salt, and pepper. Fluff with a fork
to distribute ingredients. Serve on lettuce-lined plates.
