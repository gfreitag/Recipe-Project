Title: Country-Style Risotto
Categories: Rice, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
1 3/4 c  Rice
5 tb Butter
6 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
Vegetable oil
Chicken or beef stock
1 lb Tomaotoes, chopped
1/2 c  Cooked white beans
1    Onion
1/2 c  Shelled fresh peas
1/2 c  Asparagus tips
3/4 c  Sliced zucchini
Heat a little oil in a pan, add onion and cook until soft.  Add peas,
aparagus and zucchini and cook for about 5 minutes.  Add a little
stock and cook over low heat for 10 minutes.  Add tomatoes and beans;
season with salt and pepper.  Cook for 15 minutes, then add rice.
Stir and add more stock, as necessary, and cook until al denre.  Stir
in hte butter and 3 tablespoons  parmesan.  Serve sprinkled with
remaining 3 tablespoons parmesan.
