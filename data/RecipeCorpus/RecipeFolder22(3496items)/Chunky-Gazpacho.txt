Title: Chunky Gazpacho
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 ts Liquid hot sauce
1    Cucumber, peeled and diced
1    Green pepper, diced
2    Carrots, diced
3    Celery stalks, diced
3    To 4 green onions, diced
3    Tomatoes, diced
46 oz Can tomato juice
1    Clove garlic, pressed
1 tb Sugar, heaping
1 ts Salt
1 ts Seasoned salt
1/4 c  Olive oil
3 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
In a large mixing bowl, mix tomato juice, garlic, sugar, salt,
seasoned salt, olive oil, lemon juice, worcestershire sauce, and hot
sauce.  Mix until oil and seasonings are dissolved.  Pour into a
large refrigerator pitcher.  Stir chunky vegetables into juice
mixture and add diced fresh tomatoes.  Chill overnight or 4 hours.
Serve cold with seasoned croutons on top.

Yield:  12 servings

From:  "Celebrate San Antonio - A Cookbook" by the San Antonio Junior
Forum, 1986.  ISBN 0-961917-0-0 Posted by: Karin Brewer, Cooking
Echo, 8/92
