Title: Mushroom Barley Soup W/ Short Ribs
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
2/3 c  Pearl barley
1 tb Dried dill weed
Salt and pepper to taste
2 lb Mushrooms
2 lb Beef short ribs
1 md Onion; diced
2 tb Minced garlic
6    Celery ribs; finely sliced
6 c  Water or chicken stock
PLACE RIBS, ONION, GARLIC and celery in a soup pot. Add the liquid,
cover, bring to a boil and simmer over low heat for 1 hour. Add the
barley, dill, salt and pepper and cook another 50 minutes. Add the
mushrooms and cook another 10 minutes. Serve piping hot.

MICHAEL ROBERTS - PRODIGY GUEST CHEFS COOKBOOK
