Title: Vegetarian Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Italian, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
Salt to taste, optional
2    Deep-dish crusts
12 oz Skim milk mozzarella OR
-other light cheese
-shredded
4 oz Romano/Parmesan cheese
1 ts Vegetable oil
1    Small chopped onion
1    Large minced garlic clove
12    Lg mushrooms (8oz total)
3    Lg peeled & sliced carrots
2 cn Plain tomato sauce
1 1/2 ts Dried oregano
Spray small nonstick skillet with cooking spray; add
oil, onion and garlic. Cook over moderate heat for two
minutes. Slice mushrooms. Add mushrooms and cook over
moderate heat for 15 minutes more, or until liquid from
mushrooms has almonst completely evaporated. Add carrots;
cook over moderate heat for 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat
and stir in tomato sauce, oregano and salt, if desired.
Spread vegetable mixture evenly over crusts, leaving rims
exposed. Distribute mozzarella over vegetable mixture;
sprinkle with Romano (or Parmesan).
Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Bake pizzas on lower
shelves for 20-22 minutes, until bottom of crusts is deep
brown. Remove from oven and let stand for 10-15 minutes
before serving.
