Title: Garlic Parmesan Pasta
Categories: Pasta, Main dish, Low-cal/fat, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Fettuccine; Cooked & Drained
1 1/2 c  Broccoli Floweretts; Cooked
-Tender Crisp
3 tb Walnuts; Chopped
1/2 c  Margarine
2 ts Dried Basil; Crushed
2 ts Lemon Juice
1 1/4 ts Garlic Powder W/Parsley
3/4 ts Seasoned Salt
Melt the marg in a large skillet and add the basil, lemon juice,
garlic powder and seasoned salt, blending well.  Add the fettuccine,
broccoli, walnuts, blending well and tossing to coat the fettuccine.

PRESENTATION:

Serve with a fresh spinach salad.
