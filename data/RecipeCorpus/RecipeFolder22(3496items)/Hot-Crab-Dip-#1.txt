Title: Hot Crab Dip #1
Categories: Appetizer, Seafood, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Milk
2 tb Worcestershire Sauce (Opt.)
Salt And Pepper
Almonds, Sliced
1 lb Crabmeat
8 oz Cream Cheese, Softened
1 md Onion, Finely Diced
2 ts Horseradish
