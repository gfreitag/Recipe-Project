Title: "Champagne"
Categories: Vegetarian, Beverage
Yield: 10 servings
-concentrate (6 oz. each);
-thawed
32 oz Club soda or seltzer
2 cn Frozen white grape juice
Stir together club soda or seltzer and grape juice concentrate. Pour
into two soda bottles. Cap tightly and chill. Serves 8 to 10.

Per serving: 30 cal; 0 prot; 0 fat; 7 g carb; 0 chol; 29 mg sod; 0
fiber; vegan

Source: Vegetarian Times, Jan 94/MM by DEEANNE
