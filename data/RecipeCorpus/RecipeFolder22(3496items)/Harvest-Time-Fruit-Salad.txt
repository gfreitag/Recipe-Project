Title: Harvest-Time Fruit Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Celery, chopped
6    Red lettuce leaves
1 c  Walnuts, chopped
4 c  Apples, quartered, sliced>>>
Thin.  Peel if desired.
MMMMM------------------DELICIOUS SALAD DRESSING-----------------------
pn Salt
Sprinkle of white pepper
1 c  Thick sour cream
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Lemon juice, strained
In a medium bowl, combine walnuts with apple slices and chopped
celery.
Add enough salad dressing to moisten the ingredients.  Just before
serving, spoon salad mixture onto salad plates covered with washed and
drip-dried crisp lettuce leaves.

DELICIOUS SALAD DRESSING:  In a small bowl, combine sour cream,
sugar and lemon juice.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.  Chill,
covered, for about one hour.
