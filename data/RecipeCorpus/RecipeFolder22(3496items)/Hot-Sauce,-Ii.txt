Title: Hot Sauce, Ii
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
6 ea Jalapeno peppers, cleaned>>>
Seeded & chopped fine.
1 ts Cumin seed
Salt to taste
1 ts Bacon grease
2 cn Whole tomatoes
Black pepper to taste
1 ts Oregano
2 ea Onions, minced
Saute onions in grease.  Add tomatoes and jalapeno.  Add all
ingreds. in blender and blend.  Pour into saucepan and cook on low
heat for 45 min.
