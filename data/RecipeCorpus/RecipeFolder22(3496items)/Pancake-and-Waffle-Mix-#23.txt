Title: Pancake and Waffle Mix #23
Categories: Pancakes, Mixes, Waffles
Yield: 3 servings
1/3 c  Sugar
2 tb Baking posder
2 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Rye flour
1 c  Whole-wheat flour
2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Yellow cornmeal
Sift the flours, corn meal, sugar, baking powder, salt and soda into a
bowl, using a wide-meshed sieve.  With lge. whisk, mix them very
thoroughly for at least 30 seconds.

Measure mix into 3 plastic bags or other containers, putting 1 3/4
cups into each.  If any mix is left, divide equally.  Close
containers and store at room temperature.
