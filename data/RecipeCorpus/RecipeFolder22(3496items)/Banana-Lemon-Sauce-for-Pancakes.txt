Title: Banana Lemon Sauce for Pancakes
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Cinnamon
3    Bananas; diced
1 tb Honey; or to taste
1/4 c  Butter
1/2    Lemon
1    Orange
Grate orange to to produce grated rind. Squeeze juice from orange
and 1/2 lemon. In small saucepan, melt butter. Add bananas. Simmer
till bananas are soft. Add remaining ingredients and serve hot. from
_The Berton Family Cookbook_ posted by Anne MacLellan
