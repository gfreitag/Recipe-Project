Title: Buttermilk Oat Bread
Categories: Bread
Yield: 14 servings
One/1/2 pound Loaf
1 1/3 c  Buttermilk
5 tb Butter
4 tb Molasses
2 ts Salt
1 1/3 c  Oatmeal
2 2/3 c  Bread flour
2 1/2 ts Yeast
One pound loaf
1 c  Buttermilk
4 tb Butter
3 tb Molasses
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Oatmeal
2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
Bake according to manufacturer's instructions.

Source: The Bread Machine Cookbook - Melissa Clark
