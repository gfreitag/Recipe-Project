Title: Garlicky Mashed Potatoes
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Milk
White pepper
per serving: 224 Fat grams per serving: Approx. Cook
6    Potatoes
8    Cl Garlic
1/2 ts Salt
Calories
Time: :30 Peel Potatoes and cut into quarters. Place in medium
saucepan with garlic and salt; add enough cold water to cover. Bring
to boil over high heat. Reduce heat to medium; simmer, covered 25 to
30 minutes or until potatoes are tender.  Drain well; return saucepan
to turned-off burner to dry potatoes.  Mash roughly.  With electric
hand mixer or wooden spoon, beat in butter and enough milk to make a
creamy consistency. Season with pepper to taste.  Serve at once.
