Title: Yentl Lentil Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Olive oil
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Each garlic powder & cumin
Lettuce leaves
Mint leaves (optional)
2 c  Cooked lentils
1 1/2 c  Peeled, seeded, chopped
Tomatoes
1 c  Chopped parsley
1/2 c  Diced celery
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1/2 c  Chopped red onion
1/4 c  Chopped mint
1.  In large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients, except lettuce and
mint leaves, mixing thoroughly.  Chill.

2.  Serve on a bed of lettuce.

3.  Garnish with mint leaves if desired.

DEAL-A-MEAL CARDS USED:  2 Meat  1 Vegetable  1 Fat 243 Calories
