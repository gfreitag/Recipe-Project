Title: Muffins
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/3 c  Dry powdered milk
1 pk Sweet'n Low
1/4 ts Baking soda
-Nancy Erlich (XVPK76B).
1/2 c  Pineapple; crushed
1 oz Cornmeal
1    Egg
Put everything in blender to mix.  Bake in muffin tins at 400 for 15
minutes.  All 5-6 muffins are ONE serving and provide: 1 fruit, 1
milk and 25 optional calories.
