Title: Impossible Brownie Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar; packed
1/2 c  Bisquick or baking mix
1/2 c  Sugar; granulated
3/4 c  Nuts; chopped
4    Eggs
1/4 c  Margarine or butter; melted
4 oz Chocolate; cooking
-melted & cooled
Prehat oven to 350F. grease pie plate 9x1 1/4". Beat eggs, margarine
and chocolate till smooth, 10 seconds in blender on high or 30
seconds with hand beater. Add brown sugar, Bisquick and granulated
sugar. Beat till smooth, 1 minute in blender on high (stopping
occaisionally to scrape down sides) or 2 minutes with hand beater.
Pour into plate; sprinkle with nuts. Bake till knife inserted in
centre comes out clean; about 35 minutes. Cool.
