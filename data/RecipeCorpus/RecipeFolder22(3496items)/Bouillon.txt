Title: Bouillon
Categories: Soup/stew, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Diced turnips
1/2 c  Diced onions
4    Cloves
Salt
4 lb Lean beef from shank
2 lb Bone
2 qt Cold water
1/2 c  Diced celery
1/2 c  Diced carrots
Dice meat.  Crush bone.  Add water and cloves.  Heat slowly to
boiling. Cover.  Simmer 5 hours.  Season to taste.  Add vegetables.
Simmer 1 hour. Strain.  simmer until broth is reduced to 6 cups.
Cool. Remove fat. Reheat bouillon. 6 servings.

Florence I. Miller, Connersville, IN.
