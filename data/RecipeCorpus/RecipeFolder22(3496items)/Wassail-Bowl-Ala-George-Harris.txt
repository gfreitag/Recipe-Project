Title: Wassail Bowl Ala George Harris
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 24 servings
1 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Cloves
1    3" Cinnamon sticks
1/4 ts Nutmeg
2 qt Apple cider
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sugar
Combine all ingredients, bring to a boil, and simmer for 5 min. Serve
hot.
