Title: Soft Vegetable Tacos
Categories: Vegetarian, Tex/mexican, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Sesame seeds
1 c  Picante (or to taste)
2 c  Guacamole (optional)
8    Flour tortillas
2 c  Pico de gallo
2 c  Fresh mushrooms; sliced
1 c  Alfalfa sprouts
Wash and slice mushrooms. Fold flour tortillas like tacos and fill
with mushrooms, pico de gallo, alfalfa sprouts and guacamole (if
desired). Garnish with sesame seeds and picante to taste.

Makes 8 tacos.
