Title: Chicken Spectacular
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Rice, Cajun/creol, Chicken
Yield: 16 servings
Rice, cooked.
1 ea Med. onion, chopped
1 c  Hellman's mayo.
1 cn Water chestnuts, diced
Salt & pepper to taste
3 c  Cooked chicken
1 cn Cream celery soup
1 ea Jar sliced pimiento (4 oz.)
2 c  French style green beans>>>
Drained.
1 pk Uncle Ben's White & Wild>>>
Mix all ingreds.  Pour into 2 1/2 or 3 quart casserole.  Bake 25
to 30 min. at 350 F.  Serves 16.

Freezes well, but do not cook prior to freezing.
