Title: Curry Lime Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Dip
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 x  Cayenne Pepper To Taste
1 tb Curry Powder
4 tb Lime Juice
2 tb Dijon Mustard
1 ts Garlic; Minced
Mix the lime juice and mustard, blending well.  Add the garlic.
Whisk in the mayonnaise and then blend in the spices, blending until
almost smooth. Cover and chill. Makes about 1 1/2 cups of dip.
SUGGESTED DIPPERS: Chicken Drumettes, Onion Crackers, Chicory,
Cauliflower
