Title: Vegetable Consomme
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 5 servings
3    Sprigs parsley
10    Basil leaves
10    White mushrooms; diced
1    Sprig thyme
2    Bay leaves
2    Sprigs cilantro
2 pn Chervil
Freshly ground pepper
2 md Leeks; white part only,
-diced
1/2 sm Celery root (or 2 celery
-stalks); peeled and diced
4    Shallots; coarsely chopped
1 lg Tomato; peeled and seeded
3 md Carrots; diced
3    Garlic cloves; coarsely
-chopped
Combine all the ingredients in a stockpot, cover with 2 quarts cold
water, and bring to a boil, then turn the heat down to a light
simmer. Simmer for about 20 miknutes. Turn off the heat, keep
covered, and set aside to infuse for 20 more minutes. Strain the
broth carefully through a fine strainer.

Serving size: 1 cup 19 cal; .2 g fat; 0 mg chol; 10.6 mg sod

Source: Eat More, Weigh Less, by Dean Ornish, M.D./MM by DEEANNE
