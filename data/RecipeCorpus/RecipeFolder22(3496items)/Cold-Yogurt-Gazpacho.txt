Title: Cold Yogurt Gazpacho
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 servings
3    Garlic cloves; minced
1 tb Finely chopped mint
1 1/2 ts Finely chopped dill
Salt
3 md Cucumbers
6 c  Yogurt
2 tb White vinegar
1 tb Olive oil
Peel cucumbers and slice lengthwise into halves. Scoop out seeds from
each half with spoon. Coarsely grate cucumbers to measure about 3
cups.

Place yogurt in deep bowl and whisk or stir until completely smooth.
Gently but thoroughly stir in grated cucumbers, vinegar, oil, garlic,
mint and dill. Season to taste with salt. Refrigerate soup 2 hours or
until thoroughly chilled. Place ice cube in each serving, if desired.

Makes 2 to 2 1/2 quarts
