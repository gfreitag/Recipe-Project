Title: Zucchini Mandarin
Categories: Vegetable, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Diabetic, Holiday
Yield: 4 servings  1 lb Zucchini 1 8-oz Can Mandarin Oranges 1/4 ts Nutmeg 1/4 c Almonds,
slivered and toasted

Cut the zucchini into slices.  Steam until tender.  Add the orange
slices and nutmeg. Sprinkle with the almonds just before serving. ___
