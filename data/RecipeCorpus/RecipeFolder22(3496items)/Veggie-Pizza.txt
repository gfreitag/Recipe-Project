Title: Veggie Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Italian, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
-dresing mix
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 pk Lg pkg crescent dinner rolls
1 pk 8-oz pkg cream cheese
1/2 pk Hidden valley ranch
Unroll one package of crescent dinner rolls and bake
them in a 9 x 13 inch pan. Cool. Mix the cream cheese,
mayonnaise and dressing. Spread over the baked dinner
rolls. Cover and refrigerate several hours or overnight.
An hour or less before serving time, cover with chopped
vegetables. USe any combination od broccoli, green onions,
radishes, cauliflower, celery, etc. Sprinkle with grated
cheese. GArnish with sliced olives, pieces of tomato or
whatever you desire. Almost anything goes well. Cover with
saran wrap and press down a little to set vegetables in
the cream cheese.
