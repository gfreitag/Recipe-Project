Title: Hershey Bar Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
3 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Buttermilk
1/2 ts Soda
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 ts Vanilla
8    Plain Hershey bars
15 1/2 oz Hershey syrup
2 c  Sugar
2    Sticks oleo
4    Eggs
