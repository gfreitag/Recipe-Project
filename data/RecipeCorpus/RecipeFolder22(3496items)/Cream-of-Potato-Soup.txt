Title: Cream of Potato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, 1941
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Minced onion
Celery salt
Salt, pepper, and paprika
1 c  Diced potatoes
1 c  Boiling water
2 c  Thin white sauce
Combine potatoes, water, and onion.  Cook until vegetables are
tender. Rub through sieve.  Add white sauce.  Season to taste with
salt, pepper, and celery salt.  Heat to boiling.  Garnish with
paprika. 4 servings.
