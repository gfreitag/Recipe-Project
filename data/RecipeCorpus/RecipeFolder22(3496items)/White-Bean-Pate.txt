Title: White Bean Pate
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
1    Can (15oz.) white beans
Salt and pepper
Red bell pepper strips
Carrot slices
1 lg Onion finely chopped
2    Garlic cloves
1/2 c  Vegetable broth
1/4 c  White wine vinegar
Saute onion, garlic in broth.  Stir often over med. heat. Add small
amounts of broth until a nicely browned. Add beans to onion mixture
and coarsely mash.  If needed, add a little broth to give a creamy
textrue. Add salt and pepper to taste.  Place pate in a bowl and
chill for up to 3 days. Scoop onto pepper strips and carrot slices.
