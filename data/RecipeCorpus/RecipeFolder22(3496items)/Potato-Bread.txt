Title: Potato Bread
Categories: Bread, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
-GAYE LEVY DTXT63A
MMMMM------------------------MEDIUM LOAF-----------------------------
1 T  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 c  Whole Wheat flour
1 1/2 t  Yeast
3/4 c  Mashed Potatoes
3/4 c  Potato water
2 1/4 T  Vegetable oil
3/4 t  Salt
This recipe is from Donna German's Book II. She states that you may
substitute bread flour for the whole wheat flour. Also; Dick Bequette
(PKMP64A) posted privately that he cut back the whole wheat flour by
1 cup (and added bread flour) plus added 1/3 cup of sunflower seeds.
He said it was terrific.... German Book II)
