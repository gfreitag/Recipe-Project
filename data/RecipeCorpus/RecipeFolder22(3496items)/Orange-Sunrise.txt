Title: Orange Sunrise
Categories: Vegetarian, Beverage
Yield: 6 servings
-frozen; thawed
1 qt Orange juice
10 oz Strawberries in syrup,
In a blender, combine orange juice and strawberries; process until
smooth. Strain if desired. Chill. Serve over chipped ice. Makes 6
cups.

Per serving: 104 cal; 1 g prot; 0.1 g fat; 28 g carb; 0 chol; 3 mg
sod; 1 g fiber; vegan

Source: Vegetarian Times, Jan 94/MM by DEEANNE
