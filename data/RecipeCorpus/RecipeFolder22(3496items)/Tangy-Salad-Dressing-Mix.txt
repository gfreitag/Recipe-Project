Title: Tangy Salad Dressing Mix
Categories: Salad, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
To Serve:
1/2 c  Cold water
1 ts Lemon Juice
3/4 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
1 c  Skim milk powder
1/4 c  Sugar
4 ts Dried basil, crushed
4 ts Dried onion flakes
2 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Garlic powder
1. Combine all dry ingredients. Store in an airtight container. Makes
about 1 cup. 2. To make up, combine 1/4 cup of dry dressing mix with
1/2 cup cold water and lemon juice. Blend in mayonnaise or salad
dressing. Shake well before serving.

makes 1/2 cup dressing.
