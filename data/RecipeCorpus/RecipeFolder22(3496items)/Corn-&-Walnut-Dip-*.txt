Title: Corn & Walnut Dip *
Categories: Appetizer, Tex/mexican, Nuts/grains, Vegetable
Yield: 4 cups dip
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
8 3/4 oz Whole kernel corn; drained
1 c  Walnuts; chopped
1 sm Onion; chopped
16 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Lime juice
1 tb Ground red chiles
1 tb Cumin
Beat all ingredients except corn, walnuts and onion, in a large bowl,
on medium speed, until smooth. Stir in corn, walnuts and onions.
Serve with tortilla chips.
