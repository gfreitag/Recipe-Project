Title: Bean Burrito Casserole
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Casserole, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/2 ts Onion powder
3 tb Chili powder
4 tb Cornstarch or arrowroot
2 c  Tomato sauce
3 c  ;water
1/4 ts Garlic powder
MMMMM-------------------------CASSEROLE------------------------------
1/2 c  Black olives; chopped
12    Whole wheat tortillas
5 c  Pinto beans; mashed
1 c  Green onions; cooked
Sauce: Combine all sauce ingredients in a saucepan.
Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture boils and thickens, about 7
minutes.

Casserole:  Place casserole ingredients in separate bowls.

To assemble: Spread 1 cup sasuce in the bottom of a coverfed
casserole dish. Fill each tortilla with a mixture of the beans, green
onions and olives. Roll up and place seam side down in the casserole
dish. Pour remaining sauce over the rolled-up tortillas. Cover and
bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Variation: Substitute 2 cups of rice for 2 cups of beans.

402 cal per serving
