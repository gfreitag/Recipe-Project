Title: Corn Bread or Muffin Mix
Categories: Bread, Cake/cookie
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Sugar, or more to taste
2 ts Salt
2 tb Baking powder
2 2/3 c  Cornmeal (stone ground if>>>
Possible).
2 c  A/P flour
Whisk all ingreds. together in lge. bowl.  Mix very thoroughly.
Divide mix into two zip lock bags.  You should have 2 1/2 c. per bag.
Store at room temp.
