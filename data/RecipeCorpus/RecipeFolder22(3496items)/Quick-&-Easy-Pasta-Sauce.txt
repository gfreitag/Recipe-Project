Title: Quick & Easy Pasta Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Pepper
3 tb Garlic powder w/Parsley
1 cn Tomato Puree (28 oz)
1 cn Tomato Sauce (16 oz)
2 tb Italian seasoning
Put all the ingredients in a saucepan and simmer for 15 minutes.
Serve over your favorite pasta.  By Anne Morelli
