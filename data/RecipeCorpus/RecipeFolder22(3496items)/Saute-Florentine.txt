Title: Saute Florentine
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
3 oz Spinach; coarsely chopped
3 oz Artichoke hearts, marinated;
-chopped into bite-size
-pieces
3 oz Celery; chopped into bite-
-size pieces
3 oz Brown rice; cooked
3 oz Sunflower flour
2 ts Onions; minced
1/3 ts Thyme
1/3 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Soy sauce
2 tb Sunflower oil
Combine flour with onion, thyme, oregano, salt, soy sauce, and oil.
Mix well. Saute flour mixture with vegetables and sunflower oil in
skillet or wok. Add rice and saute for 7 minutes.
