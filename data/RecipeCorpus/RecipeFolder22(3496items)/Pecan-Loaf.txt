Title: Pecan Loaf
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Soy flour
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sage
Non-dairy margarine
1 c  Pecans
1 c  Bread crumbs, dry
1 sm Onion
2 md Potatoes
1 1/2 c  Soy milk
Grind nuts and bread crumbs separately in blender.

Chop onion and potatoes in soymilk in the blender. Add to nut
mixture.

Add soy flour, salt and sage; mix well. Pour into greased loaf pan.
Dot the top with small pea-sized pieces of margarine. Bake at 350
degrees fro 30 minutes. Serve with brown gravy.
