Title: "Beef" and Bean Burrito
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Cheese, cheddar, low-fat;
--shredded
1 c  Taco sauce
1 pk Natural Touch Taco Mix
1 c  Refried beans
4    Tortillas, flour
Prepare taco mix according to package directions. Place 3 ounces of
Taco Mix and 1/4 cup of refried beans on each flour tortilla. Roll up
tortilla. Pour sauce over burritos and sprinkle with cheddar cheese.
Heat in microwave oven for 2 minutes or conventional oven for 10
minutes at 350 degrees or until hot.

From "Recipes, Natural Touch"
