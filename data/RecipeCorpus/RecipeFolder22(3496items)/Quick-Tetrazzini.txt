Title: Quick Tetrazzini
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
2 cn Tuna; drained, flaked
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
2 tb Butter; melted
1/2 lb Thin spaghetti
10 1/2 oz Condensed cream of mushroom
2 oz Mushrooms; sliced, canned
1 c  Light cream; or
1 c  -canned milk
Break spaghetti into small pieces (2 to 3 inches) and cook according
to package directions.  Meanwhile, combine soup, mushrooms, cream,
and tuna. Drain spaghetti and combine with mushroom-tuna mixture.
Put into greased 2 to 2 1/2 quart casserole.  Mix bread crumbs,
cheese, and melted butter and sprinkle on top of mixture in
casserole.  Bake in hot oven (400F) 45 minutes to 1 hour or until
browned and bubbly.

From:  The Tuna Cookbook
