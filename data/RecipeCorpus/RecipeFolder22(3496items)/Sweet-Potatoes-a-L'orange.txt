Title: Sweet Potatoes a L'orange
Categories: Vegetable, Fruit, Diabetic, Holiday, Sweetpotato
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Cinnamon, ground
16    Apricot halves, dried
Orange slices, fresh
2 lb Sweet potatoes, cooked
-OR-
2 lb Vacuum-packed sweet potatoes
2 tb Margarine, melted
Arrange the sweet potatoes in a shallow baking dish.  Combine the
margarine and cinnamon.  Pour over the potatoes.   Arrange the
apricot halves on top. Cover the dish and bake in a 425-degree oven
for about 15 minutes.  Add the orange slices and serve.
