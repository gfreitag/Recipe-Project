Title: Spiced Tomato Sunset
Categories: Vegetarian, Beverage
Yield: 8 servings
1 sm Lemon; thinly sliced
Hot pepper sauce to taste
Freshly ground pepper to
-taste
1 lg Can V-8 or tomato juice
2 ts Horseradish, prepared
1 tb Vegetarian Worcestershire
-sauce
Combine all ingredients and stir well. Cover and chil.
May be served hot in mugs or cold over lots of ice.
Serves 8.

Per serving: 29 cal; 0.8 g prot; 0 fat; 7 g carb; 0 chol; 59 mg sod;
1 g fiber; vegan

Source: Vegetarian Times, Jan 94/MM by DEEANNE
