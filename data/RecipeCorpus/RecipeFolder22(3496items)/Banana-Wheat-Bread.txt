Title: Banana Wheat Bread
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Warm water
1    Egg
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 ts Poppy seeds;(optional)
1 pk Yeast
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 c  Wheat flour
1/2 ts Salt
2    Medium ripe bananas; sliced
-right into pan
Add ingredients into the pan in the order listed, select white bread,
and push start.

From Loafing It by DAK
