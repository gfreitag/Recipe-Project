Title: Yogurt Dressing
Categories: Salad, Salsa/sauce, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Minced celery leaves
1/4 c  Minced parsley
1/2 ts Salt
8 oz Plain yogurt
1/3 c  Finely chopped onion
1/2    Garlic clove; minced
Blend yogurt, onion, garlic, celery, parsley and salt. Chill to blend
flavors. Stir well just before serving.

Note: 9 calories per tablespoon.

Makes 1 1/4 cups
