Title: Sweet Popcorn Mix *
Categories: Snack, Popcorn
Yield: 8 cups
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
1 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Cinnamon
6 c  Popped popcorn
2 c  Cocoa puffs cereal
2 tb Margarine; melted
Mix popcorn and cereal in a large bowl. Drizzle with margarine; toss
to coat. Mix sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle on popcorn mixture; toss to
coat.
