Title: Overstuffed Mushrooms
Categories: Vegetarian, Appetizer
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Parsley; chopped
1 tb Wheat germ
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Salt
Pepper to taste
3/4 lb Mushrooms, medium to large
2 tb Oil
1 lg Garlic clove; minced
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Fresh bread crumbs
1/4 c  Sunflower seed meal
Preheat oven to 400 deg. Remove stems from mushrooms and chop; set
caps aside. Heat 1 tbs oil in skillet.
Add chopped mushroom stems, garlic and onion. Fry for 5 minutes or
until dry. Add crumbs, seed meal, wheat germ and seasoning. Drizzle
remaining oil over stuffed mushrooms.

Bake for 10 minutes.

225 cal per serving
