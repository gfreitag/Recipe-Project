Title: French Bread # 2
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Soft butter
1 c  Plus 1 Tbsp. warm water
1 pk Yeast
2 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
Put all ingredients in inner pot, select "French" bread and push
start.

From Loafing It by DAK
