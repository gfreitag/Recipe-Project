Title: Mix and Match Bean Mix
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
--flakes
2 tb Onion, dried, minced
1 tb Parsley flakes; dried
2 ts Instant vegetable bouillon
--granules
2 ts Chili powder OR
2 ts Curry powder OR
1 ts Italian seasoning
1 ts Garlic, dried, minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Beans, navy or Great
--Northern, dry
1/4 c  Beans, garbanzo or pink, dry
1/4 c  Beans, red or kidney, dry
1/4 c  Beans, black or pinto, dry
1/4 c  Rice, brown; OR
1/4 c  Wheat, rye or triticale
--berries
2 tb Tomatoes, sun-dried;snipped
--(optional)
2 tb Celery or green pepper
In a plastic bag or airtight container combine the beans. (They need
to soak, so are packaged separtely from seasonings.) In another bag or
container combine remaining ingredients. Seal tightly and store until
needed. Makes 1 2/3 cups total.
