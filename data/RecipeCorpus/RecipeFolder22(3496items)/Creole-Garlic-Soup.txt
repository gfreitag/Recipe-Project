Title: Creole Garlic Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Cajun/creol, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Water
1 c  Dry Sherry
4    French Bread Slices
4 lg Garlic Cloves, Peeled
1/4 c  Butter Or Margarine, Soft
2 c  Beef Broth
2 tb Parmesan Cheese, Grated
Add garlic cloves to beef broth in suacepan; cover and simmer 15
minutes, or until garlic is soft.  Remove garlic and reserve; add
water and sherry to broth and heat to serving temperature.  Toast
bread on one side under broiler; remove and spread untoasted side
with butter.  Mash reserved garlic and spread over bread; sprinkle
with cheese.  Broil toast until brown and bubbly, about 30 seconds.
Place a piece of toast in each of the four soup bowls; ladle hot soup
over and serve.  NOTE: This soup shows the Spanish influence on
Creole cooking.
