Title: Zesty Micro-Baked Beans
Categories: Vegetable, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Onion, chopped fine
1 ts Prepared mustard
1 cn Baked beans, undrained (1lb)
1 cn Kidney beans, drained
1/2 c  Thick & chunky style salsa
In a 1 1/2 qt. microwave safe casserole, combine all ingreds; mix
well. Cover with waxed paper.

Microwave on high for 7 to 9 1/2 min. or until onion is tender and
flavors are blended, stirring twice during cooking.
