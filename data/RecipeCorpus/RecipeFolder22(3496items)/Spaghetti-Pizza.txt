Title: Spaghetti Pizza
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 1 servings
1    15 oz. tomato sauce
1 ts Italian seasoning
2 c  Mushrooms
2 c  Mozzarella cheese
Vegetable cooking spray
1    7 oz. uncooked spaghetti
1/2 c  Skim milk
1    Egg
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 lg Onion
1 md Green pepper
Prepare spaghetti as directed and drain.  Blend milk and egg and add
spaghetti and toss to coat.  Spray 15 x 18 inch pan with cooking
spray. Spread spaghetti mixture in pan.  Brown beef, onion pepper and
drain. Add sauce and seasoning.  Simmer 5 minutes.  Spread over
spaghetti. Top with mushrooms and mozzarella cheese.
