Title: Pea and Peanut Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
Text Only
Substitute 1 cup pea puree or sieved peas for corn in the recipe for
cream of corn soup.  Prepare as cream of corn soup.  Add 1/2 teaspoon
finely ground peanuts to each serving.  6 servings.
