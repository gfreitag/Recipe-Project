Title: Impossible Cherry Pie
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Bisquick
1/4 c  Sugar
21 oz Cherry pie filling
1 c  Milk
2 tb Margarine or butter;softened
1/4 ts Almond extract
2    Eggs
MMMMM--------------------------STREUSEL-------------------------------
1/2 c  Brown sugar; packed
1/2 ts Gr. cinnamon
2 tb Margarine; firm
1/2 c  Bisquick
Heat oven to 400. Grease pie plate, 10 x 1 1/2".  Beat all ingred.
except pie filling and Streusel until smooth, 15 sec. in blender on
high or 1 min. with hand beater. Pour into plate. Spoon pie filling
evenly over top. Bake 25 min.  Top with Streusel.  Bake until
streusel is brown, about 10 min. longer. Cool; refrig. any remaining
pie. STREUSEL: Cut margarine into Bisquick, brown sugar and cinnamon
until crumbly.
