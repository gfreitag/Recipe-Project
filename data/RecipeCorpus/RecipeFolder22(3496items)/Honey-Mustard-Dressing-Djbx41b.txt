Title: Honey-Mustard Dressing Djbx41b
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------JANET MEERSMAN (DJBX41B-----------------------
1/4 c  Vinegar; white
1 T  Onion powder
1 c  Oil
Parsley; fresh/dried
3 c  Mayonaise
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Grey Poupon mustard
Combine all ingredients with wire wisk, except oil.
Whip, until well blended.  Next blend in oil. Chill at least 1 hour
before serving.  Makes 4 cups.
