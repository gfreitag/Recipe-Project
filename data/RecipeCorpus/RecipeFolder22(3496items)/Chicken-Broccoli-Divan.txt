Title: Chicken Broccoli Divan
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
4    Saltines, crushed, lo-salt
1 tb Parmesan cheese, grated
1/2 ts Paprika
1 lb Broccoli, fresh, cut into>>>
Spears, cooked.
1 c  Chicken, cooked, cubed
1 cn Cream of broccoli soup
1/2 c  Skim milk
In 2-qt. shallow casserole, arrange broccoli; top with chicken.
Combine soup, milk and pepper; pour over chicken.

Combine crushed crackers, cheese and paprika; sprinkle over
chicken. Bake at 450 F. for 20 min. or until hot and bubbling.

Nutritional values per serving:  Calories 160, Protein 16g,
Carbohydrate 13g, Fat 5g, Cholesterol 35mg, sodium 390mg.
