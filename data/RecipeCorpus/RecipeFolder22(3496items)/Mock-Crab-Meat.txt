Title: Mock Crab Meat
Categories: Seafood, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
1    Lemon; sliced
1 lg Onion; quarter
2 cl Garlic; peel
1 lg Drum or sheephead; cut large
-filets
1    Box crab boil
Boil fish with crab boil, lemons, onions, and garlic until fish
starts to flake. Cool. Remove skin and use as you would crabmeat. In
a salad, it is hard to tell the difference.
