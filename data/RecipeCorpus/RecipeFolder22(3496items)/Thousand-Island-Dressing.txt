Title: Thousand Island Dressing
Categories: Dressing, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Minced pimiento
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts MSG (optional)
3/4 c  Buttermilk (or more)
1    Hard-cooked egg; chopped
1/2 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Tomato puree
2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Dill pickle relish
1 tb Chopped green olives
2 ts Minced chives
Combine egg, catsup, tomato puree, mayonnaise, relish, olives, chives,
pimiento, salt, pepper, mustard and MSG and beat. Gradually beat in
buttermilk until desired consistency is reached.

Makes about 4 1/2 cups
