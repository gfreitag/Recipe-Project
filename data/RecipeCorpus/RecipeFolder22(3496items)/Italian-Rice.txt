Title: Italian Rice
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Soy sauce
3 oz Alfalfa sprouts
1 1/2 oz Cashews; chopped
3 ts Cider vinegar
3 oz Celery; chopped
1 1/3 oz Shallots; chopped finely
3 oz Brown rice; cooked
2 tb Sunflower oil
1 1/2 ts Tarragon
1/4 ts Dill
Saute celery and shallots with brown rice in a skillet with
sunflower oil for 5 minutes. Add herbs, salt, and soy sauce. Transfer
to bowl; add sprouts, cashews and cider vinegar. Mix well and serve
at room temperature.
