Title: Bay Country Crab Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Seafood
Yield: 5 servings
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
10 oz Package frozen mixed
-vegetables
1    Pckg fresh or frozen mixed
-soup vegetables
1 lb Maryland crab meat
1/2 lb Maryland claw crabmeat
1 lb Beef shin, bone in
3 qt Water
1 lg Onion, chopped
2 lg Stalks celery, chopped
1 lb Can of tomatos
1 tb Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
Put first 8 ingredients in a large pan and simmer, covered, until
meat is very tender, about 3 hours.  Add rest of ingredients and
simmer, covered, until vegetables are done.

Makes about 5 quarts of soup.
