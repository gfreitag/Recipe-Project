Title: Bloody Mary W/tabasco-Spiked Ice Cubes
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Dried oregano
4    Green onions; trimmed
4 c  Tomato juice
6 tb Fresh lemon juice
3 tb Fresh lime juice
2 ts Tabasco
3/4 c  Vodka
Combine 1 c tomato juice, 2 tb lemon juice, 1 tb lime juice and 1 ts
Tabasco. Pour into ice cube tray; freeze overnight. Combine vodka,
garlic salt, Worcestershire, oregano and remainging 3 c tomato juice,
4 tb lemon juice and 2 tb lime juice and 1 ts Tabasco in pitcher.
Divide Tabasco ice cubes among 4 tall glasses. Pour tomato juice
mixture over. Garnish each glass with 1 green onion. Source: Bon
Appetit
