Title: Fresh Fruit Compote
Categories: Fruit, Salad, Dessert
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk Raspberries, frozen, sweet.
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Kirschwasser (optional)
2    Apples
2    Pears
1    Persimmon
Cut up fresh apples, pears and persimmon.  Place in a bowl.
Defrost raspberries, drain and add to bowl.  Stir gently to mix.
Pour orange juice and kirsch over all.  Cover and refrigerate.  Serve
cold.

NOTE:  You may substitute any fresh fruit in season to prepare this
compote.
