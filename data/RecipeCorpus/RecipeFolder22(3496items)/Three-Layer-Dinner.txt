Title: Three-Layer Dinner
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Vegetable, Mennonite
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 13/16 ts Pepper
1 c  Milk
1 lb Hamburger
1    Small head cabbage
3 c  Diced raw potatoes
Shred cabbage and put 1/2 of it in the bottom of a greased casserole.
Next add 1/2 of the diced potatoes and then 1/2 of the hamburger.
Sprinkle salt and pepper over each layer.
Add remainder in the same order, having hamburger on top.
Add milk and bake at 350 degrees for 2 hours.
