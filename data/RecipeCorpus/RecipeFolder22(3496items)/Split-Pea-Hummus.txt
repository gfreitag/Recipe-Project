Title: Split Pea Hummus
Categories: Vegetarian, Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
2    Garlic cloves; minced
1 1/2 ts Dried mint
4 tb ;water
1 c  Dried green split peas
1/2 c  Tahini
1 tb Olive oil
1/3 c  Lemon juice
Cook the split peas in 3 cups of water for 45 to 60 minutes on the
stovetop, or 8 to 12 minutes in a pressure cooker.

Place all ingredietns in a food processor and blend until smooth and
creamy, adding enough water to achieve desired consistency. Yields: 2
1/2 cups.

Serve at room temperature.

Per tablespoon: 27 cal; 1 g prot; 1 mg sod; 2 g carb; 2 g fat; 0 mg
chol; 3 mg calcium

Source: Vegetarian Gourmet, Winter 93/MM by DEEANNE
