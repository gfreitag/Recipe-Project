Title: Dinner-In-A-Bowl
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 lg Onion, sliced
1/4 c  Parsley flakes
2    Bay leaves
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Thyme leaves
Pepper to taste
4    Ham hocks
1/2 lb Sweet Italian sausage
1/2 lb German sausage
Water
4 md Potatoes - quartered
4 md Carrots - large chunk
1 sm Cabbage - quartered
Servings:  4

Put ham hocks and sausages in large pot, almost cover with water.
Simmer for one hour.  Remove hocks, cut off meat, discard bones.  Add
remaining ingredients, cover and simmer until vegetables are tender.
Cut meat into bite size pieces.  Remove bay leaves.

Serve in soup bowls.
