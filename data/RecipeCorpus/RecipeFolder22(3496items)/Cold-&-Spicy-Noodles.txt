Title: Cold & Spicy Noodles
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Vegetarian, Pasta
Yield: 8 servings
4 1/2 tb Orange juice;
3/4 ts Pepper, red; crushed
3 ea Scallions; finely chopped
3/4 md Pepper, red bell; minced
6 c  Udon or Soba noodles; cooked
1/2 c  Cilantro; finely chopped
1 1/2 tb Tahini;
1 1/2 tb Mayo, fat-free; -=or=-
-Soy Mayonnaise
3 tb Miso;
1 1/2 ts Sesame oil, dark;
MMMMM------------------------PER SERVING-----------------------------
35 x  *gm carbo
34 x  *mg sodium
4 x  *gm fiber
196 x  *cals
7 x  *gm protein
3 x  *gm fat
IN a large bowl, combine cilantro, tahini, mayo, miso, oil, orange
juice and crushed red pepper.  Toss in scallions, bell pepper and
noodles. Cover bowl tightly and chill for 3 hours or overnight. Serve
cold, tossing again lightly before serving.  Serves 8.
