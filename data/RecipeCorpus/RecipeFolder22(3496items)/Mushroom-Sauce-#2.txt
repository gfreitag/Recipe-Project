Title: Mushroom Sauce #2
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Light vegetable oil
3    Onions, chopped fine
1    Stalk celery, diced
3 c  Vegetable stock or water
1 t  Parsley
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
3    Cloves garlic, diced
1    Green pepper, diced
1/2 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1/2 c  Tamari
1 t  Garlic powder
In a medium  saucepan, on medium heat, stir  in the flour and 1/4 cup
oil. Fry until golden brown.  Remove this mixture and set aside. In
the same pan,  add the rest of  the oil, garlic and other vegetables,
and saute for about 7 minutes. Add the mushrooms and cook another 3
minutes. Place stock and  flour mixture  in a  blender, and blend
until homogenized. Add blended mixture  to  sauted vegetables. Season
with tamari, garlic powder, salt, and parsley. Cook on low heat for
10 minutes. You may add more flour for a thicker sauce. 	Makes 1
quart.
