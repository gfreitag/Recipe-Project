Title: Pasta Mexicana
Categories: Vegetarian, Tex/mexican, Pasta, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/4 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Chili powder
Cayenne to taste
1/4 c  Minced cilantro
1/2 lb Thin, green spaghetti
1 1/2 c  Cubed tomatoes
1 c  Cucumber matchsticks
1 md Avocado, peeled, seeded, and
-thinly sliced crosswise
1 c  Salsa
1. Prepare pasta according to package directions.
Rinse under cold water and set into large, shallow serving bowl.

2. Add tomatoes, cucumber, and avocado to serving bowl.

3.  Combine salsa, oil, oregano, chili powde,r and cayenne to taste.
Mix well, then pour over pasta. Ad cilantro and toss gently to
distribute vegetables evenly.

From _The American Vegetarian Cookbook_ by Marilyn Diamond
