Title: Cauliflower and Potato Curry
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 t  Salt
-big pinch
2    Tomatoes, chopped
1 1/4 c  Water
1/2 t  Garam masala
1/2 c  Wheat berries (optional)
-of asafetida
-of sugar
4 c  Potatoes, peeled and
-quartered
1 sm Cauliflower, cut into
-florets
-a pinch
3/4 t  Ground turmeric
1/2 t  Chilli powder
1 1/2 t  Ground cumin
Add all ingredients to a crockpot and cook on low for approximately
six hours.  If you're adding wheat berries, cook them on high with an
additional cup of water for an hour, then add remaining ingredients
and cook on low.

With the wheat berries, if things start drying out, add more water.
