Title: Rice Crust for Pizza
Categories: Vegetable, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Grated Mozzarella Cheese
3 c  Cooked Brown Rice
2    Eggs; beaten
Mix the rice with eggs and cheese.  Press into 10" pizza pan.  Bake
for 20 minutes at 450*. Put on sauce and toppings of your choice;
bake 10 minutes longer. PROTEIN: 45.4 grams; CALORIES: 1066
