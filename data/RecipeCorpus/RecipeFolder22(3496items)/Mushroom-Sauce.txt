Title: Mushroom Sauce
Categories: Vegetarian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Flour
2 c  Vegetable broth
2 tb Soy margarine
1 tb Onion or shallot; minced
4 oz Mushrooms; thinly sliced
Heat a heavy bottomed pan and saute the onion and mushrooms in the
margarine. Cook about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle with
the flour. Stir well, then slowly add the broth while stirring. Cook
until sauce begins to bubble.

Makes about 2 cups.

Per 1/4 cup: 54 cal; 1 g prot; 5  carb; 4 g fat
