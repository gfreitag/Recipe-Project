Title: Cream of Pea Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Finely minced onion
2 2/3 c  Thin white sauce
Salt and pepper
1 1/3 c  Canned pea soup, pea puree,
-or sieved peas
2 tb Butter
Brown onion in the butter.  Add pea soup and white sauce.  Heat to
boiling. Season to taste.  Beat with a dover beater 1 minute. Serve
at once. 6 servings.
