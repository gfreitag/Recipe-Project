Title: Herb Bean Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Green onions, chopped
1/4 lb Whole tender green beans
6    Cherry tomatoes, halved
Lettuce leaves
3 c  Cooked white beans (or can)
1 c  Cucumber,pared,seeded,diced
1 c  Sweet red pepper, diced
2 tb Celery, chopped
2 tb Parsley, chopped
MMMMM-----------------------HERB DRESSING----------------------------
1/2 ts Grated lemon peel
1/2 ts Salt
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Olive or vegetable oil
1/4 c  Lwmon juice
1/4 c  White wine vinegar
1 ts Basil (1 tb. if fresh)
1/2 ts Thyme (1 1/2 ts. if fresh)
1 tb Honey
1 tb Dijon-style mustard
Combine white beans, cucumber, pepper, celery, green onions and
parsley. Toss well with 1/2 c. herb dressing.  Let stand 1 hr. at rm.
temp.

Steam green beans about 2 min. or until crisp-tender.  Pour 2 tb.
dressing over green beans while they are still warm and toss.

Arrange white bean mixture, green beans and tomatoes on lettuece lined
salad plates.  Serve with additional dressing.

HERB DRESSING:  Combine all ingredients except oil.  Gradually beat
in oil until slightly thick.  Use in the herb bean salad recipe
above. Yield: 1 cup.
