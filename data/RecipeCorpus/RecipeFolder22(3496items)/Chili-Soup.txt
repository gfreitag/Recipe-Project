Title: Chili Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Chili
Yield: 1 servings
2 cn Kidney beans
1 qt Tomatoes; strained
1 c  Cream
Chili powder
2 lb Hamburg
1 ea Onion
1 tb Butter
2 qt Water
Fry meat and onions until done, add other ingredients, salt and
pepper and let simmer - the longer the better. Source: Mrs. Dill
Rambo, Deavertown Grange, Morgan County, OH
