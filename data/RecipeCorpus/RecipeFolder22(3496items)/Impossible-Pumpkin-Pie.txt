Title: Impossible Pumpkin Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pumpkin
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Buttermilk biscuit mix
1 c  Canned pumpkin
Pumpkin pie spice
Whipped cream (optional)
1 1/3 c  Milk
3 tb Butter or margarine
- softened
4    Eggs
1/2 c  Sugar
Combine milk, butter, eggs, sugar, biscuit mix, pumpkin and pie spice
to taste in blender. Blend until mixed. Turn into greased 9-inch pie
pan and bake at 400F 25 to 35 minutes, or until knife inserted
halfway between center and rim comes out clean. Cool. Serve with
whipped cream, if desired.
