Title: Marshmallow Popcorn Christmas Tree *
Categories: Holiday, Snack, Popcorn
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
Green food color
Candy pieces to decorate
- tree with
12 c  Popped popcorn
1/2 c  Margarine or butter
3 c  Mini marshmallows
1/2 pk Lime flavored jello
Spread popcorn evenly in jelly-roll pan or on waxed paper-lined
counter. In medium saucepan, combine margarine and marshmallows; cook
over medium heat until melted. Add jello; continue to cook until
jello is dissolved. Add food color and mix well. Pour evenly over
popcorn; stir quickly to coat. With dampened hands, shape popcorn
into cone shape. Decorate tree with candy pieces.
