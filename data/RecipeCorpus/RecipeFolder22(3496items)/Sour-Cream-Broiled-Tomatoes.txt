Title: Sour Cream Broiled Tomatoes
Categories: Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Parsley
1/2 ts Basil
2 ts Flour
1 ts Sugar, granulated
4    Tomatoes; large firm ripe
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Cut tomatoes in half crosswise. Squeeze gently to remove juice and
seeds. Broil 6 inches from heat for 5 minutes. Mix remaining
ingredients together. Spread over tomatoes. Broil about 2 minutes
more until tinged a golden color. Serves: 8
