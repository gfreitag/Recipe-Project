Title: Mustard Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 20 servings
1/2    Inegar
1/2    Ry mustard (= 2 oz can)
1 c  Brown Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Flour
2 a  ggs (beaten) Combine all ingredients bring to boil..reduce
temp to simmer til thick.. at this time you can add sliced hot dogs
or those cute party sausages.. This sauce is great for party's...
Place in fondue or crock pot to keep warm.. put some cheese squares
along side as they are delicious dipped in this tangy sauce also..
