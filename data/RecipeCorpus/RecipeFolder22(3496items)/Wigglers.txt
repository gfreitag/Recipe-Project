Title: Wigglers
Categories: Beef, Vegetable, Casserole, Mennonite
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Chopped celery
1    Small can mushrooms
1/2 lb Spaghetti, cooked
10 oz Grated cheese (mild/sharp)
1 lb Hamburger
3    Slices bacon
1    Small onion, chopped
1 c  Cooked peas
2 c  Tomatoes
Fry bacon; when brown, remove from pan.
Add onion and hamburger to bacon fryings and heat through.
Arrange meat, vegetables, and spaghetti in alternate layers in
casserole. Pour tomatoes over mixture; cover with grated cheese and
bacon. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
