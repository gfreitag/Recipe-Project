Title: Roasted Eggplant
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Mustard oil
1 lg Eggplant
1 sm Onion; finely chopped
2    Green chilis; finely chopped
Place the eggplant under a preheated broiler for about 15 minutes,
turning frequently, until the skin becomes black and the flesh soft.

Peel the skin and mash the flesh.

Add the restof the ingredients to the mashed eggplant and mix
thoroughly.
