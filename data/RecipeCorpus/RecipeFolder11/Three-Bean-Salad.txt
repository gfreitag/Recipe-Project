Title: Three Bean Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Salad oil
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 cn 16-oz green beans
1 cn 16-oz kidney beans
1 cn 16-oz wax beans
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1/2 c  Sugar
Drain the three kinds of beans and place them in a bowl. Add all
ingredients EXCEPT the onion. Mix well and chill several hours. Add 1/2 cup
onion before serving.
