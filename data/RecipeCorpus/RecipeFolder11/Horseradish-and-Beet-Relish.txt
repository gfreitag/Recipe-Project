Title: Horseradish and Beet Relish
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Onion juice
1/2 c  Vinegar
2 c  Diced or julienne cooked bee
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Horseradish
Pour all into saucepan, toss lightly and bring to a boil. Serve hot or cold
with meat or fish. If served cold, allow to chill overnight.
