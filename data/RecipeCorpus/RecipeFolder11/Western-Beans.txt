Title: Western Beans
Categories: Misc.
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Dried red pepper flakes
1    Bay leaf
1 cn Whole tomatoes with liquid,
-chopped (16 oz)
1 cn Pinto beans, drained (15 oz
1 cn Kidney beans, drained
-(16 oz.)
4    Bacon strips, diced
1 lg Onion, chopped
1/3 c  Dry lentils
1 1/3 c  Water
2 tb Ketchup
1 ts Garlic powder
3/4 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Ground cumin
Lightly fry bacon in a heavy 3-qt. saucepan. Add onion; cook until
transparent. Stir in remaining ingredients. Cook over medium heat for 45
minutes or until lentils are tender, stirring only once or twice. Remove
bay leaf before serving.
