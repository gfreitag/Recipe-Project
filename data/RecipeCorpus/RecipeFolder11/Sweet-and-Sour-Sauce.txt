Title: Sweet and Sour Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 c  Pineapple juice
2 tb Soy sauce
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring,
until it thickens and becomes clear. From Chuck Ozburn.
