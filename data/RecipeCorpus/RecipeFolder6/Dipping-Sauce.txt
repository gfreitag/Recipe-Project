Title: Dipping Sauce
Categories: Appetizers, Sauces
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Chili oil
1/4 ts Honey
1 tb Water
1 tb Tamari soy sauce
1 ts Dark sesame oil
1 ts Rice vinegar
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Will keep indefinitely if stored in the
refrigerator.
