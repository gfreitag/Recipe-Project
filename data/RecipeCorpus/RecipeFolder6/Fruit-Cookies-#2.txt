Title: Fruit Cookies #2
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Dates
Or
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Nut meats
5 c  Flour; sifted
3 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Lard
3 ea Eggs
4 tb Milk; sweet
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
Drop by teaspoonful on cookie tin, bake in moderate oven.

Note: Combine ingredients. Moderate oven is 350 - 400 F.

Source: Laura Kistler, Riverside Grange, Hancock County, OH
