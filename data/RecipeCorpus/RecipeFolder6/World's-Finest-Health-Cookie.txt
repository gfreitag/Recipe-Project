Title: World's Finest Health Cookie
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Milk; sour
1 ea Egg
1/2 ts Salt
3 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
3 c  Oats; rolled
Note: No directions. Combine ingredients, spread in shallow pan. Bake in
325 F. oven.

Source: Frank Boester, Hicksville Twp. Grange, Defience County, OH
