Title: Whole Wheat Cookies
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Nut meats
1 c  Raisins
Salt; a pinch
1 c  Honey; strained
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 ea Eggs
1 ts Shortening; heaping
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 c  Flour
Bake in quite a hot oven. Makes 4 dozen drop cookies.

Note: Cream sugar, shortening, add remaining ingredients. Hot oven is 400 -
450 F.

Source: Bridgewater Grange, Williams County, OH
