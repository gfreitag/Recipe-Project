Title: CHICKENLESS GRAVY
Categories: Sauces, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Recipe
2 c  Boiling water
2 tb Vegetable oil
3 tb Nutritional yeast
1 ea Vegetable bouillon cube
1/2 c  Mushrooms, diced
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
Onion salt, to taste
Unbleached allpurpose flour
In a large saucepan, simmer all ingredients except the flour for
approximately 5 minutes. Slowly add the flour (tablespoon by
tablespoon), wisking after each addition, until desired thickness is
reached. Keep warm.
