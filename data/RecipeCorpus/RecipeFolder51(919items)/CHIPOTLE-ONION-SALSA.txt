Title: CHIPOTLE-ONION SALSA
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
8    Cloves Garlic, chopped
2    Onions, chopped
2    Chipotle Chiles, chopped
1 pn Ground Cloves
1/4 ts Thyme
Black Pepper
1    Lemon, juiced
2 tb Cider Vinegar
Salt
Combine all ingredients and serve as desired. Makes about 1 cup.

Source: San Francisco Chronicle
