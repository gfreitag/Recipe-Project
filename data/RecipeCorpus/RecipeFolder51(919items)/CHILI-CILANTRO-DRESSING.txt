Title: CHILI-CILANTRO DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Sauces, Digest
Yield: 1 Servings
1    4oz  can chopped green
-chilis
1/4 c  Chopped fresh cilantro
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
1    2 cloves garlic
2 ts Honey
-freshly ground pepper to
-taste
Place all of the ingredients in a food processor or blender. Blend
until smooth.  (one of those Braun hand blenders works really well-ac)
