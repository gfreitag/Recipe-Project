Title: CHILI CHEESE BALL
Categories: Mexican, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Chili seasoning
2 pk Cream cheese (8 oz ea)
3 tb Salsa
Shredded Monterey Jack
Chopped nuts
Beat chili seasoning, cream cheese and salsa. Shape into a ball and
chill until firm. Roll in shredded cheese and/or chopped nuts.
