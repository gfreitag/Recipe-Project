Title: CHINESE BARBEQUE SAUCE - CHAR SUI
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 13 Servings
1/2 c  Sherry
4 ea Cloves garlic
6 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Plum sauce
2 tb Black bean paste
6 tb Hoisin Sauce
2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Chinese 5-spice
1/2 c  Sugar
Mix together well.  Heat in double boiler - add cornstarch for
thickening.
