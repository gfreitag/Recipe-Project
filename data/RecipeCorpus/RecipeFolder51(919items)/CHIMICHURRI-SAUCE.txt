Title: CHIMICHURRI SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Oil
1 c  Vinegar
1 ts Salt
2    Cloves crushed garlic
2 tb Finely chopped parsley
1    Scallion, chopped
1 sm Tomato, peeled, seeded
-and chopped
1 sm Sweet pepper, finely chopped
1/2 ts Cumin
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Chili powder
2    Bay laurel leaves, broken in
-small pieces
1/2 ts Oregano
Mix ingredients together in a bottle or jar.  Let stand for at least
12 hours, or a full day in a dry place. Shake prior to use.
