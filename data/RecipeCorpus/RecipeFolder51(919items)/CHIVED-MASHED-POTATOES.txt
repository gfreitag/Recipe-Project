Title: CHIVED MASHED POTATOES
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs, Microwave
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Hot water
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
3 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Milk or light cream
1 1/2 c  Instant mashed potato flakes
1/3 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Finely chopped parsley
3 tb Freeze-dried chives
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1. Combine water, salt, pepper and butter in a deep, 1 1/2-quart
heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole. 2. Heat uncovered, in
Microwave Oven 4 minutes or until mixture boils. 3. Add milk. 4. Stir
in potato flakes with a fork. 5. Stir in remaining ingredients until
well blended. 6. Heat uncovered, in Microwave Oven 3 minutes or until
heated through.
