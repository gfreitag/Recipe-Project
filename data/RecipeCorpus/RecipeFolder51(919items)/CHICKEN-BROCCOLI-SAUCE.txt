Title: CHICKEN-BROCCOLI SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Condensed Cream of Chicken
-& Broccoli Soup
1/2 c  Milk
1/8 ts Pepper
In 1-quart saucepan, combine soup, milk and pepper. Over medium heat,
heat through, stirring often. Serve over broiled or grilled chicken
or hot cooked rice or noodles. Makes about 1 1/2 cups sauce.
