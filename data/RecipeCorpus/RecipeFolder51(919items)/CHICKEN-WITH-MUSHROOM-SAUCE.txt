Title: CHICKEN WITH MUSHROOM SAUCE
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Low-cal
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Chicken, cut up, skinned
1 cn Mushroom soup
1/2 cn Milk
1/2 lb Mushrooms
1/2 c  Sherry
Place chicken in casserole dish.  Mix together remaining ingredients
and pour over chicken.  Cover.
Bak at 350F for 45 minutes.
