Title: CHILE RELLENOS
Categories: Tex-mex, Vegetable, Hot, Soup, Chile
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Whole green chiles --
Peeled
1    Bisquick pancake mix -- mix
As with pancakes
1/2 lb Mozzarella cheese
After mixing up a batch of pancake mix, place cheese in the chiles
and dip the chiles in pancake mix.  Fry until golden brown turning
only once if possible. ~ - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
