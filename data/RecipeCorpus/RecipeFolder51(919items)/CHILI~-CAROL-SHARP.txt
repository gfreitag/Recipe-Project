Title: CHILI~ CAROL SHARP
Categories: Main dish, Chili
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Ground beef, browned and
-drained
1 md Onion, diced
1 sm Or 1/2 large green pepper
-diced
1    1# can diced tomatoes
1    1# can chili beans (Brooks
-ot Chili beans are my
-favorite)
1 sm Can tomato paste
1/2 c  Red wine
1 pk Chili seasoning mix
1 tb Chili powder
Mix everything together in the crockpot and cook on low 6 or more
hours. This is really good with some cheese stirred in just before
serving and a dab of sour cream on top.
