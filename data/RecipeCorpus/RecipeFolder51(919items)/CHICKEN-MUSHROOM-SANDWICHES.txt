Title: CHICKEN-MUSHROOM SANDWICHES
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Minced cooked chicken
1/2 c  (4 oz.) can mushrooms,
-chopped
1/4 c  Salted almonds, chopped
3 tb Salad dressing
2 tb Chopped green olives
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Paprika
6    Frankfurter buns, cut in
-halves
Combine the filling ingredients and blend well. Spread between halves
on buns.  Serves 6  Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: The
Culinary Arts Cookbook.
