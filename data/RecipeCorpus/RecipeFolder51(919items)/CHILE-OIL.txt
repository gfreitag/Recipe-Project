Title: CHILE OIL
Categories:
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Chile powder
1 tb Cumin
34 c  Oil
Heat chile and cumin together in a saute pan until barely smoking.
Whisk in oil. Transfer to glass jar and let stand overnight for
proper separation.
