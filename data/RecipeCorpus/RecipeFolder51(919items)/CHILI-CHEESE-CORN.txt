Title: CHILI CHEESE CORN
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 24 Servings
8 c  Corn well drained
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 tb Salt
1 1/2 c  Grated cheddar cheese
1 tb Chili powder
place corn in large skillet and lightly aute'.  Add cheese and cook
on low heat until cheese melts.  Add chili powder to hot corn and
cheese.
