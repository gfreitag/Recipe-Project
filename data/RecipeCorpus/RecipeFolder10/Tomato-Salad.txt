Title: Tomato Salad
Categories: Salads, German
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Pepper, Freshly Ground
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
6 tb Vinegar
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1 lg Onion, Diced
5 md Tomatoes, Chopped
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Basil, Dried
1/4 ts Thyme, Dried
Blend all ingredients together and chill for 1 hour before serving.  Serve
on lettuce leaves.
