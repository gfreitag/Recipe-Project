Title: Cornmeal Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
--------------------------------MEDIUM LOAF--------------------------------
1 ts Salt
1 c  Yellow Cornmeal
2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Vegetable Oil
1    Egg
2 tb Sugar
This loaf rises very high and is suprisingly good.  We have tried other
breadmaker cornmeal breads but none have equaled this one. Gaye (from Donna
German CB I)
