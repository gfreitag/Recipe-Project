Title: Kahlua-Colada Parfaits *
Categories: Ice cream, Dessert, Alcohol
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
8    Macaroon cookies
1/2 c  Kahlua
1 qt Vanilla ice cream
8 ts Rum
20 oz Crushed pineapple, in juice;
- well drained
1/4 c  Shredded coconut; toasted
In each of 4 parfait glasses or 12oz wine glasses, crumble 1 cookie.
Sprinkle each with 1 tbls Kahlua. Top with 1/4 cup ice cream in each
glass, then spoon a layer of pineapple over ice cream and sprinkle
with 1 tsp rum. Repeat layers using remaining ingredients, ending
with ice cream and sprinkling toasted coconut over top. Serve
immediately.
