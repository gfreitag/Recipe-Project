Title: Salsa Cruda
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 servings
2 c  Peeled,seeded,chopped tomat.
1/2 c  Finely chopped green onion>>
Including tops
1/4 c  Finely chopped hot pepper
1/4 c  Finely chopped bell pepper
2 ts Cilantro, chopped fine
1/8 ts Sugar
Salt & pepper
Chop each ingredient by hand or in food processor.  Chop separately.
Do not over process.  Mix all the ingredients together.  Taste and
adjust the seasonings.  Let the salsa stand for at least 30 minutes.
Stir well before serving.

Yield: approx. 2 cups.
