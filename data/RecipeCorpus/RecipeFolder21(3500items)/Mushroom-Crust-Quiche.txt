Title: Mushroom Crust Quiche
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms,
Coarsely chopped
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Chopped green onions
3    Eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
1 c  Cottage cheese
1 c  Shredded Monterey Jack
Cheese
Melt butter in skillet,add mushrooms and saute until golden,about 10
minutes.Stir in bread crumbs,then pat into a 9" pie pan or divide
among eight 4" tart pans. Combine cheeses and remaining
ingredients;spoon into prepared crust.Bake @ 350 degrees 35 to 45
minutes,until puffy and golden. Cool about 10 minutes before
serving.Makes one 9" pie.
