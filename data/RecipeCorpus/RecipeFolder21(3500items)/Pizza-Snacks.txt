Title: Pizza Snacks
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Italian sausage
1 tb Crushed oregano
1 cl Garlic, minced
1 pk Refrigerated biscuits
Tomato paste
1 c  Shredded sharp process
Cheese
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
Olives sliced for garnish
Brown sausage; drain. Add oregano and garlic. On greased cookie sheet,
flatten biscuits to 4 inch circles with floured custard cup; leave
rim. Fill with tomato paste and sausage. Sprinkle with cheeses. Bake
at 425 degrees for about 10 minutes. Trim with olive slices.
