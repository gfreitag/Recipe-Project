Title: Fat-Free Knaidlach
Categories: Jewish, Low-cal/fat, Pasta
Yield: 12 servings
3    Eggs (separated)
3/4 c  Matzo meal
1/2 ts Salt
Beat egg whites until stiff, add beaten yolks.  Fold in matzo meal
and salt into eggs.  bring 4 qts. salted water to a boil.  With
moistened hands form balls the size of a walnut and drop into boiling
water.  Cover and boil for 20 min. or longer.

Yield:  10 - 12
