Title: Southern Pound Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 8 servings
-Lisa Hlavaty fdgn81a
2    Sticks margarine or butter
3 c  Sugar
3 c  Flour
6    Eggs; separated
1 c  Sour cream
1/4 ts Soda
2 ts Vanilla extract
2 ts Almond extract
Cream butter and sugar; add egg yolks one at a time. Combine soda and
sour cream; add alternately with flour to the mixtre. Add extracts.
Beat egg whites until stiff and fold into cake batter.Bake in a
greased and floured tube pan or angel food cake pan at 325 for 1 1/2
hours.
