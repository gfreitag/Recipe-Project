Title: Beef Barley Soup***
Categories: Italian, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
- G. Granaroli XBRG76A
- MM:MK VMXV03A
3 lb Beef shin-(or more)
2    Carrots diced
2    Stalks celery diced
2    Onions chopped
2    Cloves garlic crushed
1    White turnip diced
1 lg Parsnip diced
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1 3/4 qt Water.
16 oz Can peeled tomatoes - crush
-by your fingers
Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Pearl barley
Place all ingredients, except barley, in a large pot. Bring to a boil.
Lower to simmer and cook 2 1/2 hours. Add barley and cook until
tender, Check package of barley for approximate timing. The meat can
be served as a side dish to the soup with a dressing of mayonnaise
and horseradish. Serve a crusty bread also. Makes a great one dish
meal.
