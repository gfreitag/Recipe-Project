Title: Corn Meal Scrapple
Categories: Meat, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 lg Onions, chopped
1/2 ts Shortening
1 c  Corn meal
1 sm Can mushrooms
1/2 tb Butter
2 lb Ground round steak
1 lg Can tomatoes
Salt and pepper to taste
Brown onions in fat, add meat.  Add corn meal, tomatoes,
mushrooms, salt and pepper.  Cook for 2 hrs. in skillet over slow
fire.

U.W.M.--Shreveport, La.
