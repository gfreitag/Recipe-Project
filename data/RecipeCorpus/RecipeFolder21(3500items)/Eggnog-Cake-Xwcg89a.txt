Title: Eggnog Cake Xwcg89a
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Alcohol
Yield: 10 servings
1 pk Plus Yellow Cake Mix
1/4 c  Oil
3 x  Eggs
1 c  Whipping cream
1/4 c  Rum
1/2 ts Nutmeg
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
1/3 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Cutter
2 tb Rum
1/8 ts Nutmeg
2 tb Water
Heat to 350F. Grease and flour 12 cup fluted tube pan. Blend cake
ingredients until moistened. Beat 2 min. at highest speed. Pour into
pan. Bake for 30-35 minutes. In small saucepan, heat sugar, nutmeg,
butter and water until mix boil and sugar is melted. Remove from heat
and add rum. Pour 1/2 of glaze around edges of hot cake. Cool upright
in pan 5 min. then turn onto serving plater. Pour remaining glaze
over top of cake.
