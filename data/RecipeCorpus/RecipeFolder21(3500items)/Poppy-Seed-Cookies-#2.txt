Title: Poppy Seed Cookies #2
Categories: Jewish, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Flour (or more)
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Poppy seeds
3    Eggs
1 c  Oil
1 c  Sugar
Juice of 1 orange
1 ts Grated orange rind
Cream sugar and eggs.  Add oil and juice and then poppy seeds.  Mix
thoroughly.  Add dry ingredients gradually, mixing well.  Roll out a
small amount at a time on a well floured board, to 1/8th inch
thickness.  Cut with cookie cutter or glass into desired shapes.
Place on greased cookie sheet and bake in a 350 F. oven for 10 to 15
min or until nicely browned.
