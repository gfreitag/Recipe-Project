Title: Tomatillo Salsa
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1    Small onion, finely chopped
2    Cloves garlic, finely chopp.
1/4 c  Pickled jalapenos, chopped
2 tb Fresh cilantro, choppped
1 cn Tomatillos (13 oz.), drained
Pinch of sugar
Chop each ingredient by hand.  Combine the ingredients, taste, and
adjust the seasonings.  Allow to stand for at least 30 min. for
flavors to blend.

Moderately hot--excellent with chips--bean burritos--served with
tacos, added to guacamole--whatever!!

Yield:  Approx. 1 1/4 cups.
