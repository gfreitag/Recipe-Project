Title: Kentucky Kernels - S.a. Express News - Karen Haram
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 10 servings
4 tb Butter (1/2 Stick)
1/3 c  Bourbon
1    Box of Powdered Sugar
1 c  Pecans, chopped
Cream butter by hand.  By hand, alternately blend in powdered sugar
and bourbon.  DO NOT USE MIXER.  Fold in pecans.  Drop by 2/3
teaspoonful onto wax-paper lined cookie sheets.  Cover with wax
paper.  Refrigerate until set.  Cover and store in refrigerator.
