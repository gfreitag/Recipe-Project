Title: Devil's Food Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
1 3/4 c  All purpose flour
3 ts Cocoa powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 ts White vinegar
1 c  Water
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
6 ts Vegetable oil
Sift dry ingredients into a bowl.  Add the liquid in order, stirring
well after each addition.  Mix until smooth.  Bake at 350F for 35
minutes in a square pan.
