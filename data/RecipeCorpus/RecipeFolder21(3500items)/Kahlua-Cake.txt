Title: Kahlua Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Alcohol
Yield: 10 servings
MMMMM------------------MARIE BARANDICA GJJF24B-----------------------
Chocolate Fudge cake w/puddi
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Kahlua; or Amaretto
1/4 c  Oil
1 pt Sour cream
12 oz Chocolate chips (2 cups)
Mix together and pour into Bundt pan that has been lightly greased.
Bake at 350 for 50 - 60 minutes until sides of cake begin to pull
away from pan.
