Title: Cheese and Beef Ball
Categories: Spread
Yield: 1 servings
2 pk (8-oz) cream cheese
1 pk (10-oz) sharp cheddar
Cheese
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 pk (3-oz) chopped shredded
Dried beef
1/2 c  Chopped pimento olives
Combine all of the above and mix well. Shape into a ring or ball.
Serve with crackers or party breads. This can be made into two balls
rolled in pecans and be used at a later date.
