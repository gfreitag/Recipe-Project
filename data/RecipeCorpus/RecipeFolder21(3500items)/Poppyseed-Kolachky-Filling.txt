Title: Poppyseed Kolachky Filling
Categories: Bread
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Milk, scalded
1 lb Ground poppyseeds
1 1/2 c  Sugar
Contributed to the echo by: Leti Labell Originally from: "More
Favorite Recipes" from the Catholic Universe Bulletin Poppyseed
Filling Mix together filling ingredients. Set aside until milk is
absorbed and mixture is cooled.
