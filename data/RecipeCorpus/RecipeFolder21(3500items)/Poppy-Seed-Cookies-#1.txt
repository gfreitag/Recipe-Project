Title: Poppy Seed Cookies #1
Categories: Jewish, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
3    Eggs
1/3 c  Poppy seed, washed and >>>
Soaked in warm water.
1 ts Vanilla or Almond flavor
1/2 c  Oil
1/2 c  Sugar
3 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
Beat eggs and sugar; add oil, poppy seeds and flavoring.  Mix in flour
thoroughly.  Roll out on a floured board to 1/8th inch thickness.
Cut into desired shapes and place greased cookie sheet.  Sprinkle
tops with a little sugar and bake at 375 F. for abt. 10 min.
