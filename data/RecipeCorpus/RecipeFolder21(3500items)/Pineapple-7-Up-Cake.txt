Title: Pineapple 7-Up Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 servings
Duncan Hines Pineapple
Supreme cake mix
1    Instant vanilla pudding mix
10    Bottle 7-Up
4    Eggs
MMMMM---------------------------ICING--------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1    Stick margarine
2 cn Crushed pineapple in juice
1    Angel Flake coconut
Contributed to the echo by: Marge Clark Originally from: a church lady
Pineapple 7-up Cake: Mix cake & pudding mixes and 7-up. Beat well.
Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each. Pour into greased
9x13 pan, bake at 350 degrees 40-50 minutes, until the cake tests
done. Icing: Bring all ingredients to a boil, pour over cake. Poke
holes in cake with cake tester to help in absorbing icing.

This cake is so moist it's juicy...and delicious!
