Title: Poppyseed Dressing
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Salad oil
1/2 c  Sugar (I use less)
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
1 tb Poppyseeds
1 tb Grated onion
1 ts Salt
1 ts Dry mustard
Contributed to the echo by: Julie Purvis POPPYSEED DRESSING Blend all
ingredients in blender or mix by hand; will keep in frig for a week. I
serve this with raw spinach, fresh strawberries or mandarin oranges,
and sliced mild onion, Vidalia if possible.
