Title: Spaghetti and Tofu
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian, Pasta
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Tofu, mashed
2 tb Nutritional yeast
1 tb Oil
2 tb Tamari
2 c  Cooked spaghetti
3 c  Tomato sauce
In a medium sized bowl, mix tofu, nutritional yeast, oil & tamari.
Stir in the spaghetti & tomato sauce.  Heat through in a moderate
oven till hot. Serve.
