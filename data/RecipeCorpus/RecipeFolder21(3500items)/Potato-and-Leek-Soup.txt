Title: Potato and Leek Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
-G GRANAROLI   (XBRG76A)
4 tb Butter
5    Leeks sliced
6 tb Flour
4 c  Hot water
5    Potatoes; peel, dice
1/2 c  Milk
Butter and chives; garnish
In a large pot over medium heat saute leeks in butter. Remove from
heat, let cool a bit. Stir in flour. Mix well. Add water and mix well
again. Return to heat and add potatoes, salt, and pepper. Bring to a
boil. Then lower heat, cover and simmer for 30 min. When potatoes are
tender break up potatoes with a masher, add milk and heat just under
boiling. Garnish individual serving dishes with butter and chives and
serve with a large bowl of crusty croutons. (wrv)
