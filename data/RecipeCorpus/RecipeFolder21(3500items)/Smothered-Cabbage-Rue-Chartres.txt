Title: Smothered Cabbage Rue Chartres
Categories: Jewish, Cajun/creol, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1    Small onion, chopped
1/4 lb Salami OR hot sausage
1    Head cabbage, coarse chop
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 tb Pareve margarine
Saute' onion and meat in margarine.  Add cabbage (with water still
clinging to leaves from washing), salt, and pepper.  Cover and cook
over low heat for abt. 2 hrs.
