Title: Stuffed Artichokes Ii
Categories: Main dish, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
4    Artichokes
1/2 lb Swiss cheese, grated
1/4 lb Mozzarella cheese, grated
1/2 c  Celery, chopped fine
1/2 c  Onion, chopped fine
1 lb Crabnmeat
4 oz Hot pepper cheese, grated
Salt & Pepper & Red pepper
1/4 c  Bell pepper, chopped fine
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1    Stick butter
2    Eggs, well beaten
Wash artrichokes and cut off tips.  Boil until tender.  Drain.
Saute celery, bell pepper and onions in butter.   Remove from heat.
Add crabmeat and grated cheeses.  Add bread crumbs and eggs.  Mix
well.  Clean out heart of artichokes and stuff with crab mixture.
Stuff each leaf. Wrap in foil and bake at 375 F. for 35 min.

A.F.R--Terrebonne Parish, Houma, La.
