Title: Cherry Salad Supreme
Categories: Salad, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Raspberry jello
1 pk Lemon jello
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Minature marshmallows
2 c  Boiling water
1 cn Cherry pie filling
1 pk Cream cheese (small)
1 cn Crushed pineapple & juice
1/4 c  Finely chopped nuts
1/2 c  Whipping cream, whipped OR>>
1 c  Dream Whip
Dissolve raspberry jello in 1 cup boiling water.  Stir in pie
filling. Turn into 9 x 9 x 2 inch lightly oiled baking pan.  Chill
until partially set.  Dissolve lemon jello in 1 cup boiling water.
Beat together cream cheese and mayonnaise, gradually add lemon jello.
Stir in undrained pineapple.  Whip the whipping cream or use Dream
Whip.  Fold into lemon mixture with marshmallows.  Spread atop cherry
layer.  Sprinkle nuts over top.  Chill until set.  Cut into squares
to serve.  Good as salad or dessert.
