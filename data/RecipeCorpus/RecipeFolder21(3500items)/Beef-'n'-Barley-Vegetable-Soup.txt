Title: Beef 'n' Barley Vegetable Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Soup bone - 1/2 meat
2 tb Fat
2 qt Water
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Minced parsley
1/4 c  Barley
1 c  Cubed carrot
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped celery
2 c  Cooked tomatos
1 c  Fresh/frozen peas
Remove meat from the cracked soup bone; cut meat into cubes and brown
slightly in hot fat. Place meat, soup bone, water, seasonings and
parsley in soup kettle. Cover tightly and cook slowly 1 hour. Add
barley and cook 1 hour longer. Cool and skim off excess fat. Remove
soup bone. Add carrots, onion, celery and tomatos; cook 45 minutes.
Add fresh peas and cook 15 minutes.
