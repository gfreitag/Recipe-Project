Title: French Style Herbed Cheese
Categories: Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk (8-oz) softened cream
Cheese
2 tb Milk
1/4 ts Crushed dried
Oregano leaves
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Onion powder
1/8 ts Pepper
Combine all ingredients, mix until well blended. Chill. Serve with
crusty french bread or crackers.
