Title: Pink Pickeled Eggs
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
6    Hard cooked eggs, shelled
1 c  Canned beet juice
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1/2    Bay leaf
1/4 ts Allspice
1 ts Salt
Freshly ground pepper
1 c  Cider vinegar
Place eggs in quart jar. Combine remaining ingreds in enameled
saucepan and bring to boil. Pour over eggs. Cover, cool and
refrigerate for at least 8 hrs.
