Title: Cajun Couche Couche
Categories: Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Oil
1 1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Milk
2 c  Corn meal
1 ts Baking powder
3/4 c  Water
Heat oil in heavy iron pot.  Mix dry ingredients, add milk and
water; batter will be soft.  Pour batter into hot oil, let crust form
on bottom before stirring.  Reduce to low heat and stir occasionally
until cooked, about 15 minutes.  Serve in bowl with milk and sugar as
