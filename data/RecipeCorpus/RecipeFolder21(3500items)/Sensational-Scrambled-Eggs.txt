Title: Sensational Scrambled Eggs
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Butter or margarine
6    Eggs,beaten
1/4 lb Velveeta Pasteurized
Process Cheese Spread,
Cubed
Melt butter or margarine in a skillet over low heat.Add eggs and
cheese.Cook,slowly,stirring,occasionally,until eggs are set and
cheese is melted.Makes 4 servings.
