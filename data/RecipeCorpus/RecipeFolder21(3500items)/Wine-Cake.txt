Title: Wine Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Alcohol
Yield: 12 servings
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 pk Sm vanilla inst. pudding
4    Eggs
3 tb Butter
1/2 c  Oil
1 c  Wine, your choice*
1/4 c  Hot water
1 ts Nutmeg
Combine all ingredients and beat well on med speed about 2 minutes.
Pour into greased bundt pan and bake at 350 for 40-45 minutes. *Apple
juice can be substituted for the wine. TSPN00B
