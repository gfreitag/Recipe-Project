Title: Lemon Drop Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Shortening
2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cream of tartar
3 c  Flour
Beat shortening for 30 seconds.  Add sugar & continue to beat till
light & fluffy.  Combine lemon juice & water.  Combine the dry
ingredients. Alternately mix the dry & wet ingredients with the
beaten mixture.  Mix well.  Drop by spoonful onto a greased cookie
sheet.

Bake for 8 to 10 minutes at 450F.

Makes about 60 to 70.
