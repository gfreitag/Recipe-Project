Title: Tuna Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Seafood, Italian, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
-G GRANAROLI (XBRG76A)
7 oz Italian tuna packed in oil
1/4    Stick butter
2 cl Garlic chopped
2 tb Fresh chopped parsley
1/4 c  White wine
x  Salt and pepper to taste
28 oz Can chopped peeled tomatoes
Melt butter and tuna oil in pan. Sautee garlic for 2 min, until
golden. Add tuna and cook 5 min. Add tomatoes, wine and parsly, salt
and pepper and cook on low heat 30 min. Serve over cooked, drained
pasta. (wrv)
