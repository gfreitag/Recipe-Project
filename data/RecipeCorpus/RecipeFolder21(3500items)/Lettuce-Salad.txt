Title: Lettuce Salad
Categories: Salad, Appetizer, Greek, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Lettuce
A few spring onions
3 tb Olive oil
1 tb White vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
1 pn Black pepper
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Chopped garlic
Wash lettuce & separate the leaves.  Allow to drain.  Beat the rest
of the ingredients in a bowl.  Add lettuce leaves & turn them gently
so that all the leaves become coated with the dressing.

Jack Santa Maria, Greek Vegetarian Cookery
