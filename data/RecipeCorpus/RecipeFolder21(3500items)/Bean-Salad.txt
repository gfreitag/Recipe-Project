Title: Bean Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Kernel corn, drained
1    Jar diced pimentos, drained
1 lg Onion, chopped
1/4 c  Vinegar
Salt and pepper to taste
1 cn Green beans, drained
1 cn Ranch style beans, drained
1 lg Bell pepper, chopped
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Oil
Mix ingredients together and refrigerate at least 24 hrs. before
serving.
