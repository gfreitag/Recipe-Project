Title: Shrimp New Orleans
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Salad oil
2 tb Vinegar
2 tb Prepared mustard
1 ts Paprika
2    Green onion with tops,
Minced
1    Celery heart minced
2 c  Cleaned cooked shrimp
Shredded lettuce
Combine ingredients except shrimp, season; pour over shrimp, toss
lightly. Chill 2 hours. Serve on shredded lettuce.
