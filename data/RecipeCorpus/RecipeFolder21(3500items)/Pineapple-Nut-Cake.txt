Title: Pineapple Nut Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
14 oz Can crushed pineapple
2 1/2 c  All purpose flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 c  White sugar
Mix above ingredients together & Bake at 325F for 30 to 40 minutes.
Ice with nut icing.
