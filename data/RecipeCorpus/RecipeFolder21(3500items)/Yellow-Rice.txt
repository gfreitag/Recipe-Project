Title: Yellow Rice
Categories: Indian, Side dish, Rice, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Ghee
1 md Onion, chopped
1/2 ts Turmeric
2 ea Bay leaves
4 ea Cloves
4 ea Black peppercorns
2 ea 1" cinnmaon sticks
2 ea Black cardamom pods
2 c  Brown rice, presoaked
3/4 c  Water
3 tb Green peas
1 ts Salt
1 pn Saffron
Heat ghee in a skillet.  Saute onion over a medium heat till golden.
Reserve some of the onion for garnishing later.

Add turmeric to the skillet.  Stir & heat briefly.  Add the rest of
the spices in order.  Mix well.

Add the rice & stir a few times.  Pour over water, stir & bring to a
boil. Reduce heat, mix in peas & salt, cover & cook for 10 to 15
minutes. Sprinkle with saffron & reserved sauted onion.  Serve hot.

Michael Pandya, "Indian Vegetarian Cooking"
