Title: Pad Thai Lite
Categories: Asian, Seafood, Poultry, Pasta, Thai
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Uncooked linguine
1/2 lb Chicken breast, boneless,
& skinless
3/4 c  No-salt-added tomato juice
3 tb Kikkoman light soy sauce
1 tb Vinegar
2 ts Sugar
3/4 ts Cornstarch
3 tb Vegetable oil, divided
1/2 lb Fresh bean sprouts, rinsed
& drained
1/3 c  Sliced green onion tops
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1/2 lb Baby shrimp, cooked, rinsed,
& drained
1 tb Minced fresh cilantro
Lime wedges
Cook linguine accrding to package directions, omitting drain.  Cut
chicken into thin strips.  Combine tomato juice, lite soy sauce,
vinegar, sugar and cornstarch; set aside.  Heat 1 tablespoon oil in
hot wok or large skillet over high heat.  Add chicken and stir-fry 1
minute; remove.  Heat remaining 2 tablespoons oil in same pan.  Add
bean sprouts, green onions and garlic; stir-fry 1 minute.  Stir in
linguine and cook 2 minutes, or until heated through.  Add chicken,
shrimp, cilantro and tomato sauce mixture.  Cook and stir until sauce
boils and thickens.  Serve with lime wedges.  Garnish with additional
cilantro.
