Title: Hush Puppies I
Categories: Bread, Cajun/creol, Main dish
Yield: 18 servings
1/2 c  Sifted flour
1    Onion, minced
1    Egg
1 ts Sugar
1 c  Corn meal
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
Milk
Mix all ingredients with just enough milk to moisten dough to a
stiff consistency, but soft enough to spoon.  Dip with tbl. spoon and
drop into hot fat.  Cook until golden brown.  If you need more,
