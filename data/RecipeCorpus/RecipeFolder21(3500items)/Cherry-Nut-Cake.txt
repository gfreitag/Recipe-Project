Title: Cherry Nut Cake
Categories: Fruit, Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 8 servings
-Lisa Hlavaty fdgn81a
-MM: nstm54b
2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Oil
1 tb Vanilla
3    Eggs
3 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Soda
1 lg Can pet milk
1 cn Almond filling
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Maraschino cherries & juice
-chopped fine
10 x  Sugar; optional
Grease tube or bundt pan. Flour. Preheat oven to 350. Combine sugar,
oil and vanilla. Add eggs one at a time. In separate bowl, combine
flour, soda, and salt. Add to sugar mixture alternately with pet
milk. Stir in remaining ingredients one at a time. Pour batter into
pan. Bake at 350 for 1 1/2 hours. Cool about 10 to 15 minutes, then
remove from pan. No glaze is required, but can be dusted with 10x
sugar before served.
