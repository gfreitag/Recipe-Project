Title: Passover Pancakes
Categories: Jewish, Breakfast, Bread, Pancakes
Yield: 4 servings
BARBARA ALHADEFF   (JKBC19B)
1/3 CUP MI
1    EGG
1/3 c  MATZAH MEAL
1/2 ts SUGAR
1/4 ts SALT
MIX INGREDIENTS TOGETHER.  MELT MARGARINE OR OIL IN PAN AND FRY
PANCAKES. MAKES 6 - 7 PANCAKES  SERVE WITH HONEY/MARG Reformatted by
