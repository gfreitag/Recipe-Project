Title: Cranberry Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 1 servings
2 pk Cherry jello
2 c  Raw cranberries
1    Orange with peel
1    Orange without peel
2    Med. size apples
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Chopped nuts
Fix jello by directions on box.  Grind cranberries, apples and
oranges.
Add sugar and nuts, mix with jello after it congeals.
