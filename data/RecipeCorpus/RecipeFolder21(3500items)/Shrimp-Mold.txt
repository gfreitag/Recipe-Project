Title: Shrimp Mold
Categories: Appetizer, Cajun/creol, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
3    Doz. fresh shrimp boiled>>>>
In crab boil (Zatarain's)
1 lg Pkg. cream cheese
1 sm Pkg. cream cheese
1 c  Cold water
4 pk Knox gelatine (plain)
1 cn Tomato soup
1 c  Mayonnaise
3    Ribs celery, chopped fine
1    Bunch green onions, chopped
Salt,pepper and cayenne to>>
Taste.
Warm undiluted soup until simmering. Remove from stove. Mix
gelatine in cold water. Add pkgs. of cream cheese, mix well with
soup. Cheese will be a little lumpy; stir well. Add cleaned cooked
shrimp, seasoning, mayonnaise. Mix well and put in Tupperwzare ring
mold. Chill in 'fridge until ready to serve. Serve on Ritz or Encore
crackers.
