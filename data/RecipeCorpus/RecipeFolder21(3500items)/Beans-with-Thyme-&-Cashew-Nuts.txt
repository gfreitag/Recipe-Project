Title: Beans with Thyme & Cashew Nuts
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Green beans
3 tb Sunflower oil
3 oz Cashew nuts
1 ea Garlic clove, chopped
1 tb Chopped thyme
Slice beans.  Boil them in lightly salted water until they are just
tender. Drain them.

Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.  Put in the cashew nuts & stir
until they are golden brown.  Remove from the skillet.  Increase the
heat. Put in the beans & garlic.  Stir until the garlic begins to
brown.  Mix in the nuts & thyme & remove the pan from the heat.

Gail Duff, "A Book of Herbs & Spices"
