Title: Venison Stew***
Categories: Italian, Salsa/sauce, Meat
Yield: 1 servings
- G. Granaroli XBRG76A
- MM:MK VMXV03A
2 1/2 lb Venison cubes
4 tb Olive oil
1 lg Onion coarsly chopped
1    Stalk celery sliced
1 lg Carrot sliced
8    To 10 oz fresh mushrooms
-sliced
3 tb Fresh chopped parsley
28 oz Can tomato puree
1 c  Dry red or white wine
Salt and pepper to taste
In a heavy pot, (dutch oven) saute onion, carrot and celery until
golden. Add meat and brown on all sides. Add tomato puree and wine
and bring to a boil. Lower to a simmer. Cook partially covered for
2-2 1/2 hours. Check and stir often to make sure it doesn09:49 pm
stick or it might need a bit more liquid. You can add wine, water, or
broth. When meat is tender, adjust salt and pepper. This is wonderful
served over Polenta and topped with grated cheese. **Polenta is a
yellow corn meal cooked like a heavy farina.
