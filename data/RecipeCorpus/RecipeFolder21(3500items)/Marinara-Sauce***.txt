Title: Marinara Sauce***
Categories: Italian, Salsa/sauce, Pasta
Yield: 1 servings
- G. Granaroli XBRG76A
-MM:MK VMXV03A
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 lg Onion chopped
3    Cloves garli minced
2 tb Tomato paste
28 oz Tomato puree
28 oz Can Italian tomatoes
-crushed (by your hands)
1/2 c  Red wine
1 ts Dried sweet basil
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/2 c  Water
Salt and pepper to taste
Sautee garlic and onion in oil til lightly browned.( add garlic last
minute) Add all other ingredients and simmer 2 hours stirring often to
prevent sticking.
