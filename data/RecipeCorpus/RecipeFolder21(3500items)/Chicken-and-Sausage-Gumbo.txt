Title: Chicken and Sausage Gumbo
Categories: Soup/stew, Cajun/creol, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Parsley, chopped
1/4 c  Green onions & tops, chopped
3    Cloves garlic, minced
1/4 c  Cooking oil
Salt & red pepper to taste
Sausage
1/4 c  Onions, chopped
1/4 c  Bell pepper, chopped
3 tb Flour
1 1/2 lb Chicken wings, or those >>>
Parts which please you.
10 c  Water
Cut sausage in thin pcs. Combine oil and flour to make a roux.
Stir over low heat until brown. Add parsley, onions, green onions,
bell pepper, garlic, sausage and 1 cup water. Bring to a boil, reduce
heat and simmer for 3 minutes. Add remaining 9 cups water and bring
to a boil.  Add chicken, salt and cayenne pepper.  Bring to boil,
reduce heat and simmer for abt. 2 hrs. Serve over rice.
