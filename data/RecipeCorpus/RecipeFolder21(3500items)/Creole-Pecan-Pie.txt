Title: Creole Pecan Pie
Categories: Jewish, Cajun/creol, Pie/tart, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1    9" pie crust,unbaked
1 c  Karo syrup, blue label
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Pecans
2    Eggs, beaten
1/8 ts Salt
2 tb Pareve margarine, melted
Mix ingreds. together, adding pecans last.  Pour into pastry shell.
Bake in 400 F. oven for 40 min. or until a knife inserted in center
of filling comes out clean.  Cool on wire rack.
