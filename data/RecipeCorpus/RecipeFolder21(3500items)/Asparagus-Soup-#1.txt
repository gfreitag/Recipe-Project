Title: Asparagus Soup #1
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Asparagus, cut diag 1"
Pieces
1 cl Pressed garlic
3    Sliced leeks
4    Chopped thinly green
Onions
3 tb Butter
1    Chopped small red potato
4 c  Water
1    Vegetable, beef, chicken
Cube
1 tb Soy sauce
1 c  Cream
2 tb Tarragon
2 tb Basil
Saute vegetables in butter for 5 minutes. Add water and bouillon
cube; simemr 5 minutes. Add remaining ingredients and simmer 10
minutes more. Serve.
