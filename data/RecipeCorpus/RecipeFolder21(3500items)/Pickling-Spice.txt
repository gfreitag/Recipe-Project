Title: Pickling Spice
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Mace blades
1 tb Allspice berries
1 tb Whole cloves
2    Cinnamon sticks (3"), broken
-into small pieces
12    Black peppercorns
1    Dried bay leaf, crumbled
In a small bowl, mix all ingredients.Store in a small, airtight jar
up to 2 months.
