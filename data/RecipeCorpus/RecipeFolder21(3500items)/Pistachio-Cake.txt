Title: Pistachio Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 12 servings
1 pk Yellow cake mix
4    Eggs
1 c  Orange juice
1/3 c  Oil
1 pk Ins. pistachio pudding(3 1/2
1/2 c  Chocolate syrup
MMMMM----------------------CHOCOLATE GLAZE---------------------------
3/4 c  Powdered sugar
1/4 c  Cocoa
1 1/2 tb Maybe 2--T milk
In a large bowl, combine cake mix, eggs, o.j., oil & p. mix; blend at
low speed for 1 min, then beat at high speed for 3 min. Pour 2/3 of
the batter into a greased & floured 12" bundt pan. Add chocolate
syrup to remaining 1/3 of the batter & mix at med speed til well
blended. Pour over top of batter & marbleize by cutting through
batter with a knife. Bake at 350 deg for about 1 hour or til cake
tests done. Cool in pan 10-15 minutes; turn out on a wire rack or
serving plate to complete cooling. Top with chocolate glaze.
Glaze---Combine all ingredients til well blended. Spoon over top of
cake, allowing some to run down sides of cake. Mona BTMF00B
