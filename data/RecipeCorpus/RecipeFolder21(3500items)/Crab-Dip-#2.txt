Title: Crab Dip #2
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Crab meat
2 pk (8-oz) cream cheese
1    Bottle bennet chili sauce
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
Mix all ingredients except crab meat and chili sauce. Let stand
overnight. Three hours before serving add crabmeat, then mold onto
serving dish. Pour chili sauce over molded dip and refrigerate. Serve
with crackers and triscuts.
