Title: Summer Sausage
Categories: Meat, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Ground beef
1/4 ts Coarse pepper
2 tb Curing salt (kosher salt)
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1 tb Liquid smoke
1/4 ts Mustard seed
1/4 ts Onion salt
Mix all ingreds. well.  Form into 2 rolls and wrap tightly in foil.
Referigerate 24 hrs.  Bake over broiler pan with water at 350 F. for
1 1/2 hrs.  Remove from foil after baking.  Good served hot or cold.
