Title: Shrimp Fried Rice
Categories: Asian, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
2    Eggs
2 tb Water
2 tb Vegetable oil
3    Green onions & tops, chopped
3 c  Cold, cooked rice
1/4 lb Cooked baby shrimp, chopped
3 tb Kikkoman Soy Sauce
Beat eggs with water just to blend; set aside.  Head oil in hot wok or
large skilled over medium heat.  Add green onions; stir-fry 30
seconds. Add eggs and scramble.  Stir in rice and cook until heated;
gently separating grains.  Add shrimp and soy sauce; cook and stir
until heated through.  Serve immediately.
