Title: Chili #3
Categories: Chili
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped green pepper
1 1/4 lb Ground veal, beef, pork
1 1/2 tb Minced garlic
3 tb Chili powder
1 ts Ground cumin
1 ts Oregano
Bay leaf
1/2 ts Black pepper
4 c  Canned tomatos
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1/4 ts Red hot pepper flake
Heat oil in skillet, add onion and green pepper. Cook until wilted.
Add the meat and, using the edge of a heavy kitchen spoon, stir and
chop the meat to break up any lumps. Sprinkle the meat with garlic,
chili powder, cumin and oregano. Stir to blend. Add the bay leaf,
pepper, tomatos, vinegar and crushed hot pepper. Bring to a boil and
cook for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
