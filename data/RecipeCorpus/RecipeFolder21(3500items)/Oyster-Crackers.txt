Title: Oyster Crackers
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Oil, Crisco, Wesson, etc
1 pk Hidden Valley Ranch dry
Dressing
1 ts Dill weed
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Lemon and pepper
Seasoning
2    Bags small oyster crackers
Add 2 bags of oyster crackers, small ones to rest of ingredients. Mix
well. Spread on cookie sheets to dry, approximately 2 hours.
