Title: Guacamole #1
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
2    Avocados
2    Diced fine tomatos
1/2    Diced fine onion
1 cn Diced fine Ortega chilis
1/4 cn (7-oz) diced fine olives
2    Bags corn/tortilla chips
1 tb Soy sauce
Halve and pit avocados; remove from skins with spoon. Mash with other
ingredients, combining thoroughly. Chill 1-2 hours (if longer,
avocado may start to discolor). Serve as dip with chips.
