Title: Vegetable Relish
Categories: Relish
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 kg Ripe tomatoes
2 md Onions
1 kg Zucchini
500 g  Red bell peppers
500 g  Green bell peppers
2 ea Garlic cloves
2 tb Salt
2 c  Cider vinegar
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Cornflour
2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Pepper
Peel & coarsely chop the tomatoes & onions.  Coarsely chop the
zucchini & peppers.  Combine in a bowl.  Process in a food processor
in several batches till they are finely chopped.  Place in a large
bowl, sprinkle with salt & let stand, covered, overnight.

Place vegetables in colander & drain excess liquid.  Place in a large
pot, add vinegar & sugar & stir over low heat till the sugar is
dissolved. Bring to a boil & simmer for 3 minutes.  Combine the
remaining ingredients & blend till smooth.  Add to the vegetables &
bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Simmer till thickened.  Remove
from heat & pour into warm sterile jars.  Seal.

Letts, "Jams, Pickles & Chutneys"
