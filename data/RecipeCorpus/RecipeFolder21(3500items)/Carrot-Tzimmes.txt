Title: Carrot Tzimmes
Categories: Side dish, Jewish, Vegetable, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 servings
COOKIE CRIDEN   (NBVX45B)
1 lb Carrots, peeled and grated
1    Large yam, peeled and grated
1 lb Granny Smith apples, peeled
1/4 lb Prunes coarsely chopped
1/4 c  White raisins
1/4 c  Dried, pitted cherries
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 c  Crushed pineapple--don't dra
1/2 c  Matzo meal
Pinch nutmeg
Pinch salt
Pinch Cardamom (optional)
1/4 lb Margarine, melted and divide
1 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1 tb Matzo meal
Reserved melted margarine
1. Mix together all ingredients except the topping ingredients and
only half of the margarine.
2. Coat a terrine, souffle dish or individual ramekins with
margarine.
3. Pour mixture into prepared dish.
4. Combine topping ingredients and spread over top.
5. Bake at 350 deg. for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
6. It can be prepared a day ahead, but bake the day you want to eat
it.
7. Serves 6 to 8. Enjoy!
