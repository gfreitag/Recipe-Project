Title: Fettucini Alfredo
Categories: Italian, Pasta, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
- G. Granaroli XBRG76A
- MM:MK VMXV03A
1    Stick sweet butter
1/2 lb Chopped ham
1 pt Heavy cream
1 pt 1/2 and 1/2
3/4 c  Parmegiana cheese
12 oz Fettucini noodles (green are
- best - the fresher the
- better)
In a pan, saute the ham in butter for 5 min. Turn off heat. Add the
rest of ingredients and serve with HOT pasta. Serve immediately.
