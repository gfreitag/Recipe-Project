Title: Creamy Spinach Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1    8-oz dairy sour cream
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/2 ts Dill weed
1/4 ts Onion salt
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
3 c  Frozen leaf spinach thawed
And well drained
1 cn (8-oz) water chestnuts
Drained and finely chopped
3 tb Chopped red bell
Pepper if desired
In medium bowl, combine sour cream, mayonnaise and seasonings. Stir in
onions, spinach, water chestnuts, and red pepper. Cover; refrigerate
to blend flavors. Serve with crisp vegetable dippers, assorted
crackers or bread.
