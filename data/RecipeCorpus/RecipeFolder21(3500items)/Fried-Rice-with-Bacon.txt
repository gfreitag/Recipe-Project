Title: Fried Rice with Bacon
Categories: Pasta, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Cold, cooked rice
1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 lb Bacon, fried crisp, crumbled
3 tb Vegetable oil
1 ts Salt
3    Eggs, scrambled very soft
2    Green onions & tops, chopped
Stir-fry rice in hot oil for 1 minute.  Add sugar, salt and soy
sauce. Stior until heated through.  Add eggs, green onions, and bacon
pcs.  Stir to mix and serve.
