Title: Cream Cheese Ball
Categories: Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk (8-oz) cream cheese
Softened
1 tb Milk
1 pk Corned/chipped beef cut in
Strips with scissors
Chopped ripe/green olives
As desired
Salt and pepper
Add the milk to the softened cream cheese first. Blend together. Roll
in ground buts for ball. Cool and spread on crackers.
