Title: Red Chile Chipolte Salsa (Cooked)
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 3 servings
4    Chipolte chiles NO SUBST.
1 c  Boiling water
1 tb Oil
3/4 c  Diced onion
3/4 c  Bell pepper
2 c  Fresh toamto.,peeled,diced
1/2 ts Oregano
Combine chiles and boiling water--let soak 30 min.  Remove stems and
seeds and chop.

In nonaluminum saucepan, heat the oil and saute the onion, sweet
pepper, and chipoltes until onion is slightly tender, 1-2 min.  Add
the chiles, soaking water, tomatoes and oregano and remove from heat.
Process briefly in blender until ingredients are well mixed, but
still chunky.  Use for BBQ, or any cooked dish.  Great when mixed
with sour cream for dip.

Yield:  approx. 3-3 1/2 cups.
