Title: Party Mix
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) margarine or
Butter
1 1/4 ts Seasoned salt
4 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
8 c  Your favorite chex cereals
1 c  Salted mixed nuts
1 c  Pretzel sticks
Melt margarine in open roasting pan in preheated 250 oven. STir in
seasonings. Gradually add cereals, nuts and pretzels; stir to coat
evenly. Bake 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Spread on absorbent
paper to cool. Store in airitght container.
