Title: Hamburger Corn Casserole
Categories: Beef, Casserole
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Hamburger
1    Diced medium onion
8 oz Pkg cooked noodles
12 oz Can corn
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
Servings: 1

Salt and pepper to taste

Brown hamburger and onion. Layer noodles, corn and hamburger in
buttered casserole. Cover with soup. Repeat layers. Season with salt
and pepper. White sauce may be used with soup. bake for 1 hour at 350
degrees. Makes 6-8 servings.
