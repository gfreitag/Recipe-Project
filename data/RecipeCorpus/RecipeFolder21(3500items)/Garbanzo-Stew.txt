Title: Garbanzo Stew
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Dried Garbanzo Beans
10 c  Water
2    Potatoes Chunked
2    Carrots Thickly Sliced
2    Stalks Celery Thickly
Sliced
2    Onions Chopped
2 tb Low Sodium Tamari (Opt.)
1. Place the Garbanzos and Water in a Large Pot.  Bring To a Boil,
Reduce Heat To Medium Low, Cover and Cook At Least 2 Hours.
2.  add Remaining Ingredients and Cook until Tender, Usually 1 Hour
More, Depending on How Soft You Like Your Vegetables.
