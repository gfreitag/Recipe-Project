Title: Tater Tot Casserole
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 12 servings
1 lg Onion
-chopped
3    Celery stalk
-chopped
6    Morning Star Grillers
-cut up
Tater Tots
2 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Water
Cheese
Parsley
Chop up onion and celery, put in bottom of 9x13 pan.  Cut up Grillers
and place on top of onion/celery.  Cover with frozen tater tots.  Mix
the 2 cans mushroom soup with the one can water, pour over the top of
mixture in pan.  Sprinkle cheese and parsley.

Bake 1 hour at 350F.
