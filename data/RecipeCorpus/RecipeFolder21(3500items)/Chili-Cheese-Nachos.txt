Title: Chili Cheese Nachos
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Prepared chili mix
1    (8-oz) jar pasteurized
Process cheese spread
1 pk (8-oz) tortilla chips
2 c  Shredded lettuce
1 c  Chopped tomatos
1/2 c  Sour cream
Microwave chili and process cheese spread on high 5 minutes or until
thoroughly heated, stirring every 2 minutes. Layer half of chips,
half of chili mixture, remaining chips and remaining chili mixture on
large serving platter. Top with remaining ingredients.
