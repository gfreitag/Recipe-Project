Title: Cheese Ball #1
Categories: Spread
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Cream cheese
1/4 lb Sharp cheddar cheese
Spread
1 ts Minced onion
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1 cn (4-oz) deviled ham
Mix together at room temperature. Roll into ball and roll in chopped
nuts, parsley, or chipped beef. Pack cheese down in waxed paper lined
bowl. Chill well.
