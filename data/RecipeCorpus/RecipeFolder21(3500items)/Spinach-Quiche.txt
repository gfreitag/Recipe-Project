Title: Spinach Quiche
Categories: Main dish, Pie/tart, Cajun/creol
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Butter
Dash salt & pepper
4    Eggs
1 c  Chopped ham
1    Pie shell, 9" unbaked
3 tb Onion
3 tb Flour
1 c  Half & half
1 pk Creamed spinach
1 c  Swiss cheese, grated
Pat parmesan cheese on bottom of shell.  Melt butter and add to
remaining ingreds.   Mix well and pour into pastry shell.  Bake at
375 F. about 25-35 min., or until knife inserted comes out clean.
Serve hot.
