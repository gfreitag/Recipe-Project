Title: Buttery Onion Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM--------------------COUNTRY WOMAN 01/93-------------------------
2 c  Onions; thinly sliced
1/2 c  Margarine
1/4 c  Flour
2 c  Chicken broth
2 c  Milk
1 3/4 c  Mozzarella cheese shreded
-(6-8 oz)
Salt & pepper; to taste
Croutons; optional
In a large kettle, saute onions in margarine over low heat until
tender and transparent, about 20-30 minutes. Blend in flour.
Gradually add broth and milk; cook and stir over medium heat until
bubbly. Cook and stir for 1 minute more; reduce heat to low. Add
mozzarella cheese and stir constantly until melted (do not boil).
Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve with croutons if desired.
Yield: 6 servings (about 1 1/2 qts.)  A recipe of Sharon Berthelote
of Sunburst, Montana.
