Title: Rich Delicious Orange Tea Muffins
Categories: Bread
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Sugar,
2 ts Baking powder,
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter or margarine melted,
1/2 c  Fresh orange juice
2    Eggs,
1 1/2 c  Flour,
Grated rind of 1 orange
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
Sugar cubes
Orange juice
Preheat oven to 375Ã¸F, prepare pans. Combine first 4 ingredients &
blend well. Melt butter, take off heat & stir in orange juice, rind &
eggs. Beat. Stir dry mix into wet mix and blend until just moistened.
Spoon into pans, soak 1 sugar cube in orange juice for each muffin
and place on top of batter. Bake for 15-20 mins or until done. Makes
8-9 large muffins.
