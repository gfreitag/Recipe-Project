Title: Chicken Corn Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Chicken broth OR
1 c  Chicken broth and 1 bouillon
Cube dissolved in 1 cup hot
Water
1 1/2 c  Fine diced potato
1/2 c  Fine diced onion
1/2 c  Fine diced celery
1 ts Salt
1 sm Bay leaf
1 c  Cut up chicken breast
3    Strips crumbled fried bacon
Cook until vegetables are just tender over medium heat. Just before
serving, add 2 cup milk and 2 cup cream style corn or 1 cup cream
style and 1 cup whole kernel corn. Return to a boil.
