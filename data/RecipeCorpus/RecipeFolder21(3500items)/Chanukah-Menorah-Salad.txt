Title: Chanukah Menorah Salad
Categories: Jewish, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 lb Cream cheese, softened
8    Maraschino cherries
1/2 lb Walnut halves
1 cn Pineapple spears
Cover an oval platter with a thin smooth layer of cream cheese.   On
this arrange the spears to represent  a Menorah with candles.  On top
of each candle place a cherry for a flame.  Trim edge of platter with
walnut halves.
