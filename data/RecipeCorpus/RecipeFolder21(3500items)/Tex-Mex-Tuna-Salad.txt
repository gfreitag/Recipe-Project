Title: Tex-Mex Tuna Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 6 servings
2 cn Solid white tuna in water,
-drained and flaked (6.5 oz)
1/2 c  Sliced ripe olives
1/2 c  Sliced green onions w/tops
1/2 c  Thinly sliced celery
2/3 c  Pace Picante Sauce
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
1 ts Ground cumin
Lettuce leaves
-OR
Shredded lettuce
12    Taco shells
-OR
3 c  Tortilla chips
Combine tuna, olives, green onions and celery in medium bowl. Combine
Pace Picante Sauce, sour cream and cumin; mix well. Pour over tuna
mixture; toss lightly. To serve, line taco shells with lettuce
leaves; spoon tuna mixture into shells. Or, line individual serving
plates with shredded lettuce; top with tuna mixture and surround with
tortilla chips. Drizzle with additional Pace Picante Sauce; top with
additional sour cream, if desired.
