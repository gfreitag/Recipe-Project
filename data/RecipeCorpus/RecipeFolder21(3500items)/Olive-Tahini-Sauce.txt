Title: Olive-Tahini Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Unbleached white flour
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Liquid from canned black
-- olives
1/3 c  Tahini
1 c  Finely chopped black olives
Juice of 1 lemon
1/4 ts Thyme
Black pepper
Dissolve flour with just enough water to make a smooth, flowing
paste. Set aside.  Heat water & olive liquid till just under boiling
point. Whisk in tahini, a littel at a time, then the flour paste.
Simmer overl ow heat til the sauce is thick.  Stir in the remaining
ingredients & serve immediately.

Nava Atlas, "Vegetariana"
