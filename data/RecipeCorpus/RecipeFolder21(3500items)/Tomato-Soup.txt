Title: Tomato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Butter
10    Fresh skinned chopped
Tomatos
3    Diced large onions
2    Diced stalks celery
3 tb Chopped fresh basil
2 tb Chopped fresh thyme
2 c  Homemade chicken stock
32 oz Tomato juice
1 c  Cream
20 oz Tomato puree
3 1/2 c  Gin
1/4 c  Sugar
Saute vegetables and herbs in butter. Add stock and reduce to one
third. Add tomato juice and cream. Bring to a simmer for 45 minutes.
Add gin and sugar and thicken with tomato puree. Simmer for 1 hour.
Garnish with chives, black olives and artichoke hearts.
