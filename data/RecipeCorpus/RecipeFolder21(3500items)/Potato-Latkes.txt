Title: Potato Latkes
Categories: Side dish, Appetizer, Vegetable, Jewish
Yield: 4 servings
-NATHAM KOPEL   (WDVB56A)
6 md Potatoes; grated
1    Onion; grated
2    Eggs
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Flour; to 1/2 Cup
Here are some recipes from "simple Jewish Cookery" by Edna Beilenson
which are typical for Chanukah. Squeeze liquid from potatoes and add
remaining ingredients, mix thoroughly. Fry pancakes, from spoon into
hot shortening, until brown on underside. Turn and brown other side.
Use moderate heat.
