Title: Tunnel of Fudge Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Butter
6    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  Flour
1 pk Fudge Buttercream Frosting
-Mix
2 c  Chopped walnuts
Cream butter at high mixer speed.  Add eggs one at a time, beating
well. Gradually add sugar, beating at high speed. Stir in flour,
frosting mix and walnuts by hand. Pour into a greased bundt pan. Bake
60-65 minutes at 350 degrees.  Cool 2 hours.  Remove from pan.
Cake will have dry, shiny brownie-type crust when you take it out of
the oven.  It will be soft in the center when you cut it.
