Title: Black Eyed Pea Salad
Categories: Salad, Appetizer, Greek
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Black eyed peas
3 ts Ground coriander
3    Garlic cloves, minced
3 tb Fresh ginger, grated
1/2    Red pepper, chopped OR
-- cayenne to taste
Olive oil
Wine vinegar
Salt
Hungarian paprika
Soak peas.  Put in a pot with water to cover, plus 1 inch & add
coriander, garlic, ginger & red pepper.  Cook for a little over 1
hour or until still a little chewy.

While the peas are cooking, prepare a dressing by mixing together oil,
vinegar, salt & parika.  Use 2:1 oil-vinegar ratio & add seasonings
according to taste.  Add to peas as soon as you remove them from the
heat. Allow to cool to room temperature & adjust seasoning then serve.

Vera Gewanter, "A Passion for Vegetables"
