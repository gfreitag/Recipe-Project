Title: Salmon Cakes
Categories: Main dish, Cajun/creol, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Salmon
2 c  Soft bread crumbs
2    Well beaten eggs.
1 tb Chopped onion
1 tb Chopped parsley
2 tb Butter
Salt & pepper to taste
2 ts Baking powder
Mix thoroughly.  Chill.  Shape and fry in deep fat.  365 F.
