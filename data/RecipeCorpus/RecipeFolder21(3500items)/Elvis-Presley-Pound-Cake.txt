Title: Elvis Presley Pound Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Sugar
1/2 lb Butter, softned
7    Eggs, room temperature
3 c  Cake flour, sifted twice
1 c  Whipping cream
2 ts Vanilla extract
Butter and flour a 10-inch tube pan.  Thoroughly cream together sugar
and butter. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Mix in half the flour, then the whipping cream , then the other half
of the flour.  Add vanilla.  Pour batter into prepared pan. Set in
COLD oven and turn heat to 350 degrees. Bake 60 to 70 minutes, until
a sharp knife inserted in cake comes out clean.  Cool in pan 5
minutes. Remove from pan and cool thoroughly.

SOURCE: BUFFALO EVENING NEWS - Reportedly Elvis' favorite and original
recipe.
