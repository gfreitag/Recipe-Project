Title: Shrimp Au Gratin 1
Categories: Main dish, Cajun/creol, Cheese/eggs, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Cooked, cleaned shrimp
2/3 c  American cheese, grated
1 c  Thick white sauce
1 tb Chopped parsley
1/2 c  Buttered bread crumbs
Alternate layers of shrimp, white sauce and 1/2 of the cheese in
greased deep baking dish.  Sprinkle top with remaining cheese and
parsley, then with buttered crubms.  Bake at 400 F. for 20 minutes or
until crumbs are brown. Courtesy Telephone Pioneers BillSpalding *P
