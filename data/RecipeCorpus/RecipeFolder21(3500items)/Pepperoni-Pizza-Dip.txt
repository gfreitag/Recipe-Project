Title: Pepperoni Pizza Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk (8-oz) softened cream cheese
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
1 ts Crushed dry oregano
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Crushed red pepper
1/2 c  Pizza sauce
1/2 c  Chopped pepperoni
1/4 c  Sliced green onion
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1/2 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
Sweet pepper strips,
Broccoli florets
Or crackers, optional
In a small mixer bowl beat together cream cheese, sour cream, oregano,
garlic powder, and (optional) red pepper. Spread evenly in a 9 or
10-inch quiche dish or pie plate. Spread pizza sauce over the top.
Sprinkle with pepperoni, green onion, and green pepper. Bake in a 350
degree oven for 10 minutes. Top with cheese; bake 5 minutes more or
till cheese is melted and mixture is heated through. Serve with sweet
pepper strips, broccoli flowerets, or crackers.
