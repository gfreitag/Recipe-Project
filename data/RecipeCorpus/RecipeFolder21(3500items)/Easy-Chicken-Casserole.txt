Title: Easy Chicken Casserole
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 6 servings
1    Pgk. frozen broccoli (10oz.)
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Wild rice
4    Chick. breast--chopped/cookd
1 cn Cream of chicken coup
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1    4 oz. jar Cheez-Whiz
Cook broccoli according to pkg. directions, adding onion and celery
while cooking. Drain.  Cook rice according to pkg. directions.  Cook
chicken breasts in salted water.  Cook, bone, and chop chicken.   In
a large casserole, place the rice in layer, next the chicken, and
then the broccoli.  Mix soups together and pour over the chicken.
Drop the cheese spread by spoonsful over the top.  Bake at 350 F. for
40 min.
