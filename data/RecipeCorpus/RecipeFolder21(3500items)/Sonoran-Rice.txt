Title: Sonoran Rice
Categories: Rice
Yield: 4 servings
28 cn Whole peeled tomatos
2 tb Tomato juice
1 1/2 c  Finely chopped onions
1 ts Diced green chilies
2 tb Safflower oil
1    Jalapeno chili
2 ts Chopped fresh basil
2 ts Garlic powder
1 1/2 ts Ground cumin
1 ts White pepper
3/4 ts Ground coriander
1 1/2 c  Uncooked rice
Contributed to the echo by: Keith Ker Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Drain and chop the whole, peeled tomatos. Remove the seeds from the
jalapeno chili and chop fine. Cover the bottom of casserole with oil.
Combine all ingredients but rice in casserole. Stir over high heat 1
minute, reduce heat to medium. Add rice, cover, and transfer to oven.
Bake until liquid is absorbed, about 35 minutes.

Servings: 4
