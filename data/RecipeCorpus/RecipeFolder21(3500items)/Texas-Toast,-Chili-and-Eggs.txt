Title: Texas Toast, Chili and Eggs
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Chili
Butter
8    Eggs, fried or poached
8    Thick slices bread
Heat chili;keep eggs warm.Spread bread slices with butter.Fry or
grill on both sides until golden brown.To serve,spoon chili over
bread.Drop 2 eggs on each slices.Makes 4 servings.
