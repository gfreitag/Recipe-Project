Title: Easy Chocolate Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
2 2/3 c  Flour
2 ts Baking soda
2/3 c  Cocoa powder
2 c  White sugar
1 ts Salt
2 c  Water
2 ts Vanilla
2/3 c  Vegetable oil
2 ts White vinegar
Sift dry ingredients into a bowl.  Add liquid.  Mix well.  Transfer
to 13 X 9 X 2-inch pan.  Bake at 350F for 35 minutes.
