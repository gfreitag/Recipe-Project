Title: Stuffed Crabs
Categories: Fish, Cajun/creol
Yield: 8 servings
3    Medium bellpeppers
2    Large onions
2 c  Bread crumbs
1 tb Flour
1 pn Parsley
2    Stalks celery
1    Stick butter
1/2 lb Claw crab meat
1 pn Green onions
3    Eggs
Saute' vegetables until wilted,season to taste with salt and red
pepper. Mix bread crumbs,flour,eggs,green onions,and parsley. Combine
with other ingredients and fill crab shells. Dip in a batter of egg
and milk,then in flour and finally in bread crumbs. Fry until golden
brown.
