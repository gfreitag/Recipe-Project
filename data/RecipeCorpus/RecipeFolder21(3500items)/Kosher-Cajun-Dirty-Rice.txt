Title: Kosher Cajun Dirty Rice
Categories: Jewish, Main dish, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Uncooked long-grain rice
1/2 lb Mushrooms,cooked,chopped,OR
1 cn Chopped mushrooms (4 oz.)
Liquid from mushrooms.
1/2 c  Chopped bell pepper
1/2 lb Kosher smoked sausage
Freshly chopped parsley
2    Onions, chopped
1 1/2    Sticks margarine, pareve
1 c  Celery, chopped
3    Chicken livers
3    Chicken gizzards
2 c  Clear chicken broth
1 c  Water
Pepper to taste.
Boil gizzards until tender (abt. 30-35 min.); broil livers until
brown; and saute sausage until slightly brown.  Drain meats and dice.
Saute onions in heavy iron skillet until light brown.  Add bell
pepper, celery, and mushrooms and cook until onions are transparent.
Add remaining ingredients except sausage.  Cover and cook on low heat
40 minutes, stirring often. Add sausage and place in 2-qt. chafing
dish.  Garnish with parsley.
