Title: Ketjap Manis
Categories: Asian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Water
2/3 c  Molasses
1 c  Soy sauce
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Cumin
1/2 ts Coriander
Bring water and sugar to a boil, then add the remaining ingredients.
Simmer for 5 minutes.  Let cool.  Store in refrigerator in
tightly-covered container.  Always shake before using.
