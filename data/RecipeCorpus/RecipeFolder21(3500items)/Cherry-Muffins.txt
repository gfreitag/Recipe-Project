Title: Cherry Muffins
Categories: Bread
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Sugar,
2 ts Baking powder,
1/2 ts Salt
1/2    Bottle maraschino cherries
Cut up,
2    Eggs,
3 tb Melted butter
1 c  Cherry juice & milk
Combined
1 1/2 c  Flour,
Prepare pans. Stir together dry ingredients & add the cherries. Mix
eggs, cherry liquid & melted butter. Add the dry mix to the wet mix
until just combined. Bake for 15-20 mins at 375oF. Makes 9-10 large
muffins.
