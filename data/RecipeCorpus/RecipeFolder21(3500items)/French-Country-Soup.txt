Title: French Country Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Cajun/creol
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Beef shanks
2 cn Tomato sauce (small)
2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Med. onion, choppped
1    Wedge cabbage, 1"thick,chop
1 lb Cut green beans
2 qt Water
2    Beef bouillon cubes
1 ts Salt
2    Whole cloves
3    Turnips, pared and diced OR>
1    Rutabaga,small
1 c  Uncooked elbow macaroni
In dutch oven or kettle, combine beef, water, onion, tomato sauce,
cabbage, bouillon cubes, sugar, salt, pepper and cloves.  Bring to
boil, simmer covered for 2 hrs.  Add turnips, macaroni and beans.
Simmer uncovered for 20 or 30 minutes more, or until tender.
