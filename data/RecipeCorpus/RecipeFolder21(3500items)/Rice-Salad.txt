Title: Rice Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 6 servings
8 sl Bacon, cooked, crumbled
1 c  Celery, diced
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 c  Cooked rice
1 c  Cooked English peas
1/4 c  Diced pimento
1/4 c  Fresh chives, minced, OR>>
Green onion tops
Stuffed olives (optional)
Combine all ingredients, except olives; toss.  Garnish with olives
if desired.  Chill.  Makes 6 servings.
