Title: Watermellon Rind Preserves
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Watermelon cubes
2 ts Water
2 tb Lime (calcium oxide)
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Water
1/2    Lemon, sliced
Contributed to the echo by: Merrilyn Vaughan WATERMELON RIND
PRESERVES 1. Soak melon in limewater for 3+ 1/2 hours. Drain. 2. Soak
in clear water for 1 hour. Drain. 3. Cover with water and boil for 1+
1/2 hour. Drain. 4. Make syrup fo2 c. sugar and 1 quart water. 5. Add
rind and boil for 1 hour. 6. As syrup thickens add lemon [half ob.
for each lb. of melon]. 7. When syrup is thick and rind clear, pack
in hot, sterilized jars,
top with syrup and seal.
