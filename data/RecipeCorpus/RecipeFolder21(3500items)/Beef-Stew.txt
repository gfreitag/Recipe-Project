Title: Beef Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Beef
Yield: 4 servings
3    Tomatos, chunked
2 lb Stew meat
2 c  Beef broth
1 ts Worchestershire sauce
1    Crushed garlic clove
1    Bay leaf
1 ts Paprika
1    Chopped onion
1    Stalk celery, chunked
2 ts Kitchen bouquet
Salt and pepper to taste
Contributed to the echo by: BOYD NARON BEEF STEW Combine all
ingredients in slow cooker and cook on low 10 to 12 hours or on high
for 4 to 5 hours.
