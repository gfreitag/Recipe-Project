Title: Canadian Flapjacks
Categories: Breakfast, Pancakes
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Flour
1    Pinch sea salt
1 1/2 c  Apple juice or milk
2 tb Oil
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Honey
2 ts Baking powder
1    Pippin apple, grated
Combine all ingredients except apple with mixer.  Add apple and mix by
hand. Spoon into oiled fry pan, at medium heat. Turn when bubbles
appear and harden. Brown other side. Serve immediately with your
choice of topping.  We like honey and maple syrup best.
