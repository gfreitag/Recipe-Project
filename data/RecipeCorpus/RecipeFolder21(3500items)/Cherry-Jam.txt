Title: Cherry Jam
Categories: Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Sweet cherries
3 c  Warmed sugar
Stone cherries.  Crush the fruit.  Boil in their juice till tender,
about 10 minutes.  Add sugar, stir well to dissolve.  Boil for
another 5 to 7 minutes.  Remove from heat & let stand, covered, for 2
to 3 minutes.  Stir & skim if necessary.  Pour into sterile jars &
seal.
