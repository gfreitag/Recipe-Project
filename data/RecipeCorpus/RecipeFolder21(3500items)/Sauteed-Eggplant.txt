Title: Sauteed Eggplant
Categories: Vegetable, Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 md Eggplant
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Oil
1 ts Chili powder, optional
Cut eggplant into slices 1/4" thick.  Sprinkle with salt & let sit
for 1/2 hour.  Rinse, squeeze & pat dry. Saute in oil till soft,
adding more oil if you need it.  Place on paper towels.

Serve sprinkled with hot chili powder if desired.
