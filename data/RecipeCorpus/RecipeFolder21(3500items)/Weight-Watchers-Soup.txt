Title: Weight Watchers Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Can V-8 juice
1    V-8 can water
1 lb Hamburger
1/2 sm Head cabbage
3    Cubes beef bouillon
1 pk Frozen mixed vegetables
Brown hamburger. Shred or chop cabbage. Add cabbage to hamburger. Add
V-8 juice and 1 can water. Add bouillon cubes and mixed vegetables.
Simmer, for about 2 hours until cabbage is tender.
