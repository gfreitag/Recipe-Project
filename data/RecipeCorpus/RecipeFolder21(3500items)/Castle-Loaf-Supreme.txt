Title: Castle Loaf Supreme
Categories: White castl, Pork, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
12    White Castle hamburgers
(Pickles & ketchup included)
1 lb Ground pork
2    Cloves garlic
8    Sprigs chopped parsley
1/2 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Milk
1    Pinch oregano
1/2    Can tomato sauce
Salt to taste
Mix all ingredients except tomato sauce in a large bowl. Grease loaf
pan. Shape mixture and place in pan; smooth meat with oiled hands.
Pour tomato sauce over meat loaf. Bake at 350 deg F. for one hour.

Joseph J. Garofalo, Bronx, New York
