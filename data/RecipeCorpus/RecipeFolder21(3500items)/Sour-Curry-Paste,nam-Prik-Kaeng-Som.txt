Title: Sour Curry Paste/nam Prik Kaeng Som
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
10    Dried chilies
5    Shallots, chopped
1 tb Shrimp paste
1 tb Salt
Coarsely chop the chilies and soak in water for 10 minutes. Drain.
Pound all the ingredients together to produce a fine paste which goes
well with fish and vegetables.

SOURCE: National Cooking Echo 04/18/90 Contributed to the echo by:
Stephen Ceideburg Originally from: "Discover Thai Cooking", by
Chaslin, Canungmai and Tettoni; Times Edition, Singapore, 1987
