Title: No-Meat Burgers-In-A-Hurry
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Firm tofu; mashed
1 c  Quick rolled oats
1/2 c  Wheat germ
1    Onion (optional)
-finely minced
2 tb Onion powder
2 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Black pepper
Vegetable oil; for frying
Mix ingredients together. Knead for a few minutes.  Shape into six
patties. Fry in lightly oiled cookie sheet at 325 F for 25 minutes.
Serve on whole wheat bread or buns. Top with lettuce, tomato, alfalfa
sprouts, grated carrot, onion, pickles, catsup or mustard.
