Title: Banana Pudding Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------PHYLLIS SMITH WCDT64B------------------------
1 x  Bananas; 1 or 2 small, ripe
1 ea Yellow cake mix
1 pk Banana cream instant pudding
1 x  - mix (4 serving size)
4 ea Eggs
1 c  ;water
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 c  Nuts; finely chopped, opt.
Slice bananas into mixer bowl. Beat until well mashed. Add remaining
ingredients. Blend; then beat at med speed for 2 minutes. Pour into
well greased and floured 10" tube or Bundt pan. Bake at 350 for 60-70
minutes. Sprinkle with confectioners sugar when cool. Cake may be
baked in a 9x13" pan for 50-55 min.
Source: My Famous Little Black Box
