Title: Skillet Corn and Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Margarine
4    Skinless,boneless chicken
Breast halves
1 cn Campbell's Golden Corn Soup
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Broccoli florets
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/8 ts Pepper
1.  In skillet, in hot margarine, cook chicken 10 minutes or until
browned. Remove; set aside. Spoon off fat.

2.  In skillet, combine remaining ingredients. Heat till boiling.
Return chicken to skillet.

3.  Cover; cook over low heat 10 minutes or until chicken is no
longer pink and broccoli is tender-crisp; stirring often. Garnish
with tomato wedges and fresh thyme, if desired.
