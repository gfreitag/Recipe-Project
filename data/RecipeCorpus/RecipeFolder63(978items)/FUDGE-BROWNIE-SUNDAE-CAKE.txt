Title: FUDGE BROWNIE SUNDAE CAKE
Categories: Digest, Feb95
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Flour1/2
2 ts Baking powder1/2
1/2 c  Soy milk1/4
1 ts Vanilla extract3/4
1 3/4 c  Hot water
c  Unsweetened cocoa
ts Salt
c  Unsweetened applesauce
c  Brown sugar
Preheat oven to 350 F. Spritz 8x8 pan with cooking spray. Combine
flour, baking powder, salt, & 1/4 cup cocoa. Blend in apple sauce,
vanilla, and milk. Spoon batter into pan. Combine brown sugar, 1/4
cup cocoa, and hot water. Stir to blend. Pour over batter in pan.
Bake for 40 minutes. Remove and cool in pan. You may cover with your
favorite topping, or plain, but spoon chocolate sauce that has formed
on the bottom, over the top of the brownie

Preparation   :15    Cook   :40    Stand   :20
Total   1:15
