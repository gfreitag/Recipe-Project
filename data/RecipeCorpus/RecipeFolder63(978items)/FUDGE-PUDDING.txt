Title: FUDGE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
3/4 c  Sugar
2 ts Cocoa
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Melted butter
sift 1st 5 ingredients in bowl, stir in milk and butter. Spread
mixture in 9" pan. Over this sprinkle 1 cup brown sugar mixed with 3
tblsp. cocoa. Spread on batter and over this pour 1 3/4 cups hot
water. Bake in 350 oven for 45 min. Serve warm or cold with or
without ice cream.
