Title: FRUIT PIZZA
Categories: Appetizers, Desserts, Fruits/nuts
Yield: 12 Servings
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
1 c  Orange juice
3/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Lemon juice
3 tb Cornstarch
Dash of salt
1 c  Sugar
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
8 oz Cream cheese
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Sugar
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1 pk Sugar cookie dough
MMMMM---------------------------FRUIT--------------------------------
Peaches
Grapes
Bananas
Strawberries
Kiwi
Mix ingredients of glaze- cook over medium heat til boils, and
thickens, then cool. Spread cookie cough on buttered pizza pan. Bake
350 degrees for 10 minutes. Cool. Mix sauce and spread over cooled
dough. Arrange fruit on top and pour glaze over. Refrigerate.
