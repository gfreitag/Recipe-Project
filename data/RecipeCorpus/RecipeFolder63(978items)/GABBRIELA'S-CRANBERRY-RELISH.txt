Title: GABBRIELA'S CRANBERRY RELISH
Categories: Thanksgivin, Side dishes
3 c  cranberries, fresh
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  sugar
2 TB water
few raspberries
lemon zest or orange
ginger -- grated
:
:
:

Put sugar, water, and cranberries into a medium saucepan. Boil ten
minutes, the add remainder of ingredients. Cool
