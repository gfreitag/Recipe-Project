Title: GARDEN VEGETABLES
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 md Onions, sliced
2    Garlic cloves, minced
2 md Zucchini, cut into 1/4"
-slices
1 md Yellow squash, cut into
-1/4" slices
1 ts Oregano
1 cn RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and
-Green Chilies (10 oz)
1 cn Crushed tomatoes (16 oz)
In a large skillet, saute onions and garlic in hot oil until tender.
Add zucchini, eggplant, squash and oregano. Saute util vegetables are
tender. Stir in RO*TEL and crushed tomatoes; simmer 10 minutes.
