Title: GEORGE'S PICO DE GALLO
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 bn Fresh cilantro, chopped
3    Tomatoes, peeled, seeded and
-chopped
1 md Red onion, chopped fine
4    Heads garlic,minced optional
2 sm Jalapenos,seeded and chopped
1 ts White vinegar
Juice of one lime
Salt (optional)
Mix everything together and chill for a least an hour for the flavors
to blend.

For a quick and easy dip, mix with one container of fat-free sour
cream and serve with tostada chips, and margaritas!

Ole!
