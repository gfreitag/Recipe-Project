Title: GARDEN VEGETABLE SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Spreads
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz Soft cream cheese
1/2 c  Shredded carrot
1/2 c  Shredded zucchini
1 tb Chopped parsley
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1 ds Pepper
Combine ingredients; mix well. Chill. Serve with party rye or
pumpernickel bread slices or assorted crackers. Makes 1 1/3 cups.
