Title: GARLIC PEANUTS
Categories: Garlic, Snacks, Spicy
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Raw Peanuts
1/4 ts Garlic Powder
For each cup of raw peanuts, start with a small amount of garlic
powder, about 1/4 teaspoon, stirring well to blend. Increase garlic
powder to taste as the peanuts warm. Spread out on a cookie sheet and
