Title: GAMBAS AL AJILLO TAPAS (GARLIC SHRIMP)
Categories: Tapas, Seafood, Appetizers, Spanish
Yield: 1 Servings
-waldine van geffen vghc42a
24 md Shrimp in the shells
2/3 c  Olive oil
60 ml Garlic; chop fine
Lemon slices
Remove heads from shrimp if desired.  Heat oil to smoking point. Saute
shrimp with the garlic over fairly high heat until they turn pink,
about 2 minutes.  Serve immediately with bread for dunking or on a
bed of boiled rice.  Garnish with lemon wedges. Source: The Complete
Spanish Cookbook (wrv)
