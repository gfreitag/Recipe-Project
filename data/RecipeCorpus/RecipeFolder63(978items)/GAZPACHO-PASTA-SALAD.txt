Title: GAZPACHO PASTA SALAD
Categories: Salads, Pasta, Meatless
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Rotini, Twists or Spirals
- uncooked
4 md Ripe tomatoes
- peeled, seeded & chopped
1/2 c  Sliced green onions
1/2 c  Cucumber
-(peeled, seeded & chopped)
1    Garlic clove; minced
2 tb Chopped cilantro; OR...
2 ts -Dried cilantro
2 tb Olive oil
1 tb Red or white wine vinegar
1 ts Salt
Cayenne pepper to taste
Prepare pasta according to package directions; drain. Combine pasta
with remaining ingredients. Cover and chill at least 1 hour.

Serves 6-8

Each serving provides: 127 Calories; 3.2 g Protein; 19.1 g
Carbohydrates; 4.4 g Fat; 0 mg Cholesterol; 312 mg Sodium. Calories
from Fat: 31%
