Title: GARLIC LOVER'S CREAMY CUCUMBER DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dressings, Garlic, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Cucumber, pared, seeded
-chopped
1 c  Mayonnaise (light- low fat)
1    Garlic clove, minced
2 tb Parsley, minced
1 tb Chives or scallions, minced
1 c  Yogurt, plain non-fat
Stir together cucumber, mayonnaise, garlic, parsley, chives and
pepper. Fod in yogurt. Chill.  Makes approximately 2 3/4 cups.
