Title: GARLIC HERB DIP
Categories: Garlic, Dips, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Real mayonnaise
4 lg Cloves fresh garlic, minced
4    Full sprigs fresh parsley
Finely chopped
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1    Heaping Tbsp finely minced
Onion
1 tb Dill weed
1 ts Seasoned salt
3    Drops Tabasco
Combine all ingredients thoroughly, cover and refrigerate overnight
or up to four days for more potent flavor. Serve with dippers of your
choice. Makes about 1 1/2 cups.
