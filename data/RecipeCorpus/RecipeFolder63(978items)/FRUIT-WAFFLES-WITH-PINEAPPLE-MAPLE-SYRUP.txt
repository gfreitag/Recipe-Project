Title: FRUIT WAFFLES WITH PINEAPPLE MAPLE SYRUP
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 Cup
1 cn DOLE Pineapple Chunks in
-juice, drained (20 oz;
-reserve 3/4 cup juice)
1/4 c  Maple syrup
2 ts Cornstarch
DOLE Mandarin Orange
-Segments (drained)
DOLE Raisins
Add maple syrup and cornstarch in a saucepan. Mix well. Cook, stirring
until mixture boils and thickens. Serve over waffles with the
pineapple chunks, DOLE Mandarin Orange Segments (drained), and DOLE
Raisins.
