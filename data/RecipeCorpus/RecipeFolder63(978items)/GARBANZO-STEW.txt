Title: GARBANZO STEW
Categories: Main dish, Stews, Low-fat, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Vegetable stock
1 c  Dried garbanzo beans
-- soaked overnight
1 c  Diced onion
1 c  Diced tomatoes
1 tb Minced fresh ginger
2 ts Ground coriander
1 ts Minced garlic
1/4 ts Ground cumin
Freshly ground black pepper
1 ts Fresh lime juice
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
In a large saucepan, combine the stock and garbanzo beans. Bring to a
boil, reduce the heat, and simmer, covered, for 1 hour, or until just
tender. Drain, reserving 1 cup of the cooking liquid.

In a large saucepan, combine the cooked garbanzos, reserved liquid,
onion, tomato, ginger, coriander, garlic and cumin. Bring to a boil.
Reduce the heat and simmer, covered, for 20 minutes.

Season to taste with freshly ground black pepper. Garnish with the
lime juice and chopped parsley.
