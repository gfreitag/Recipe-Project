Title: GEISHA CUP
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------JUDI M. PHELPS----------------------------
1 1/2 oz Gin
1 oz Apricot brandy
2 oz Orange juice
2 oz Grapefruit juice
Maraschino cherry
Mix all ingredients, except maraschino cherry, with cracked ice in a
shaker or blender and serve in a chilled Collins glass. Garnish with
cherry.
