Title: GARBANZO & CARROT SALAD
Categories: Vegetarian, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
15 oz Garbanzo beans, Eden organic
-drained
1 md Carrot; cut into matchstick
-pieces
1/4 c  Cilantro or parsley; finely
-chopped
2 tb Sesame seeds; lightly
-toasted
1    Garlic clove; pressed
2    Onions, green; finely
-chopped
2 tb Tahini, mixed with 1 T water
1 ts Vinegar, ume plum, Eden
Mix beans, carrot, cilantro, sesame seeds, garlic and green onions.
Stir tahini mixture and vinegar together and pour over ingredients.
Serve on a bed of lettuce.
