Title: GARLIC MASHED TATERS (LACTO)
Categories: Digest, Jan.
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Tiny new potatoes, unpeeled
3    Cloves garlic, peeled
4 tb Nonfat yogurt
3 tb Nonfat sour cream
1/8 ts Salt
Fresh gound black pepper
Place potatoes and garlic in pot with enough water to cover and cook
until potatoes are tender. Drain and mash, then stir in yogurt, sour
cream, and salt and pepper.

The recipe actually called for light sour cream, and nutritional
analysis was: 270 cals, 2 g. fat, 8 milligrams cholesterol, 185
milligrams sodium, 7 g. protein, 60 g. carbohydrate.
