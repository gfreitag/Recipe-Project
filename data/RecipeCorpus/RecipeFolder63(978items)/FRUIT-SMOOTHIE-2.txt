Title: FRUIT SMOOTHIE 2
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
8 oz Fruit cocktail, can --
Chilled
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Nonfat dry milk powder
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Ice cubes
Cinnamon -- ground (dashes)
PER SERVING:  87 cal., 5g Pro., 16g Carbo., 0g fat, 3mg Chol., 81
Sodium 298mg Potassium In a blender container combineundrained fruit
cocktail and remaining ingredients.  Cover; blend till combined. Add
ice cubes; cover and blend till smooth. Sprinkle with additional
cinnamon (for garnish), ifdesired. Serve immediately. BETTER HOMES
AND GARDENS
