Title: GARLIC KNOTS
Categories: New, Text, Import
1    recipe Curtis's Pizza Dough
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  extra virgin olive oil
2    cloves garlic, -- chopped

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Once dough has risen, separate into two
separate pieces. Set half aside for use another time. Divide remaining
dough into golf ball-sized pieces. Roll each ball into a 4 -6 inch
long piece. Loop dough into a pretzel-shaped knot. Add garlic to
olive oil. Roll knot in oil. Bake for 8 - 10 minutes, until golden
brown.
