Title: FUDGY PEANUT-ICE CREAM DESSERT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Chocolate wafer crumbs
(about 36 wafers)
1/3 c  Margarine or butter,melted
1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Chopped peanuts
1/2 c  Chocolate fudge topping
1/2 c  Caramel-flavored topping
1    Half gallon vanilla or choc
-ice cream, softened
1/4 c  Chocolate fudge topping
1/4 c  Caramel-flavored topping
1/4 c  Peanuts, if desired
Mix wafer crumbs, margarine and sugar.  Press firmly in ungreased
square pan, 9x9x2 inches.  Mix 1 cup peanuts, 1/2 cup fudge topping
and 1/2 caramel topping. Spread over crumb mixture. Spoon ice cream
onto peanut mixture; spread evenly.

Drizzle with 1/4 cup fudge topping and 1/4 cup caramel topping. Cover
and freeze at least 12 hours until firm. Serve with peanuts and
additional chocolate fudge topping if desired. Yield: 12 servings.
