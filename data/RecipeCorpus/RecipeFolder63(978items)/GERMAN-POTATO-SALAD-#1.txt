Title: GERMAN POTATO SALAD #1
Categories: Salads, Germany, German
Yield: 2 Servings
8 md Potatos boiled and peeled
1 ea Onion
1/4 lb Bacon diced
3 tb Vinegar
Pepper
Salt
Slice potatos and put in large bowel. Fry bacon,not too crisp.Dice
onion and cook in bacon drippings.Pour all over potatos,drippings
still hot.Add vinegar,salt and pepperand mix. Let sit several
hours.Enjoy! German Potato Salad
