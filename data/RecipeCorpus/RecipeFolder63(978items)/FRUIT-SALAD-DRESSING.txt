Title: FRUIT SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Fruits, Salads, Cheese
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Orange Juice
1/2 ts Orange Peel; Grated
1/4 c  Mayonnaise Or Salad Dressing
1 tb Sugar
3 oz Cream Cheese; Softened And
-Cut Into Cubes
1 ds Cayenne Pepper
Put all of the ingredients into a blender container and blend until
smooth.

Makes 1 cup.
