Title: GARBANZO SALSA
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Garbanzos (8 oz)
1 c  Cilantro,fresh
1/3 c  Yogurt,lowfat,unflavored
1/3 c  Green onion,chopped
1/4 c  Lime juice
Salt to taste
Pepper to paste
Drain garbanzos; whirl smooth in a food processor or blender with
cilantro, yogurt, onions, and lime juice. Add salt and pepper to
taste.
