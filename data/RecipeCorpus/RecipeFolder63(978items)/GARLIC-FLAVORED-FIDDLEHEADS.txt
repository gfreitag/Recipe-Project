Title: GARLIC FLAVORED FIDDLEHEADS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Fresh fiddleheads
Or 1 pkg. frozen
6    Cloves garlic
2    Green onions
1 tb Soya sauce
1/2 ts Sugar
3 tb White wine
Cook fiddleheads in water or steam. Meanwhile in frypan heat some
butter and saute garlic till softened then add the sliced green
onions for the last 1/2 minute. Blend in the soya sauce, sugar and
wine. Coat the fiddleheads in the sauce and serve.
