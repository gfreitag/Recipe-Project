Title: FUDGE TRUFFLE CHEESECAKE
Categories: Cheesecakes
Yield: 12 Servings
12 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
24 oz Cream cheese -- softened
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
4    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
1    Pie crust, chocolate wafer
Heat oven to 300. In heavy saucepan, over low heat, melt chips,
stirring constantly. In large bowl, beat cream cheese till fluffy.
Beat in milk until smooth. Add melted chips and remaining
ingredients. Pour into prepared crust.  Bake 1 hour and 5 minutes,
until center is set. Yummm!
