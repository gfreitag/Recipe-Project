Title: GERMAN GINGERBREAD COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, German
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Soft margarine
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1    Egg
3/4 c  Molasses
3 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
Mix all ingredients together well.  Make balls of dough the size of
walnuts, and place them on a greased cookie sheet 3-4" apart. Flatten
with a glass dipped in sugar. Bake at 325 F for 6-8 minutes. Or, if
you like, refrigerate overnight.  Roll out cold dough for cookie
shapes cut with cookie cutters.  If dough is sticky, add flour.
