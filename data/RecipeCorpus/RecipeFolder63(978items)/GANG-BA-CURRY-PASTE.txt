Title: GANG BA CURRY PASTE
Categories: Spice mix
Yield: 1 Cup
13 ea Dried red chili peppers,
-- soaked, seeded & chopped
2 tb Shallots -=OR=- red onion
1 tb Garlic, chopped
1 tb Galanga
3 tb Kaffir
1 tb Lemon rind, grated
1 1/2 tb Lemon grass, chopped
1 ts Ginger, chopped
Soak all dried ingredients until sufficiently soft.

Place all ingredients in a blender or food processor & blend to form a
smooth paste.  It may be necessary to add a few drops of water to
make the paste smooth.

Puangkram C. Schmitz & Michael J. Worman, "Practical Thai Cooking"
