Title: FUDGE FROSTING
Categories: Frostings
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Heavy cream
1 lb Semisweet chocolate; cut up
2 ts Vanilla
Heat the heavy cream in a saucepan until just boiling. Remove from the
heat. Add the semisweet chocolate and vanilla. Stir mixture until
smooth and the chocolate is melted. Transfer to a glass bowl. Place
the glass bowl over ice water. Stir until spreadable. ---
