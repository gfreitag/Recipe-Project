Title: FRUITY DROP COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 3 Dozen
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Roger's Golden syrup
1/2 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Chopped dates
1/2 c  Candied cherries or
1/2 c  Mixed glazed fruits
Sift flour with salt.  Cream shortening, gradually add sugar, Roger's
Golden Syrup and flavouring.  Beat until light and fluffy. Add eggs
one at a time beating vigourously after each addition. Fold in sifted
dry ingredients alternately with combination fruit and nuts. Drop
batter by teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet. Bake 8 to 10
minutes at 375-400.
