Title: GARLICKY HUMMUS (MAKES 1-3/4 CUPS)
Categories: Oriental, Vegetables, Spreads
1/2 ts Salt
Yield: 2 Servings    2 cn Chick-peas 15 oz, drained
1/4 c  Tahini (sesame seed paste)
Chopped parsley
Paprika
:
2 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
1 1/2 tb Fresh lemon juice
Pita Bread
:
2 lg Garlic cloves, crushed

1. In a food processor, combine chick-peas, tahini, olive oil, lemon
juice, garlic, and salt. Puree until smooth. If made in advance,
transfer to a bowl, cover and refrigerate. 2. Before serving, top
with chopped parsley, and a dusting of paprika. Serve with pita bread.
