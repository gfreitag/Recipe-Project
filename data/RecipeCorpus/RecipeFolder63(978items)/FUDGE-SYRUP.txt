Title: FUDGE SYRUP
Categories: Sauces, Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  BUTTER OR MARGARINE
3 oz UNSWEETENED CHOCOLATE
3 c  WATER
1/2 ts SALT
15 oz CAN OF EVAPORATED MILK
MELT BUTTER IN THE TOP OF A DOUBLE BOILER OVER BOILING WATER. ADD
CHOCOLATE, HEATING UNTIL MELTED. ADD SUGAR, 1/2 CUP AT A TIME,
STIRRING UNTIL DISSOLVED. STIR IN SALT. SLOWLY ADD MILK. MIX WELL.
CHILL SEVERAL HOURS BEFORE USING.
