Title: GELATO DI FRUTTA  (ICE CREAM WITH FRUIT)
Categories: Desserts, Italian, Db
Yield: 1 Add
2 c  Fruit, diced
1 c  Sugar
1 qt Gelato alla vaniglia recipe
To add fruit to a basic vanilla ice cream, mix fruit and sugar and let
stand for two hours untill sugar has dissolved. Then place in
refrigerator until thoroughly chilled. When ice cream is about half
way through the freezing process, stop and add fruit. Continue until
frozen.

From: Great Italian Cooking by Luigi Carnacina, Milan, Italy Circa
196? Posted by Dick Baker Nov 96
