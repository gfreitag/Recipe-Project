Title: GERMAN CHOCOLATE CAKE FROSTING
Categories: Desserts, German
Yield: 1 Cake
1 c  Evaporated milk
1 c  Sugar
3    Egg yolks
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Pecan pieces
1 1/3 c  Coconut
1 ts Vanilla
Cook milk, sugar, egg yolks, butter and vanilla over medium heat. Stir
constantly until thick.  Add 1-1/3 cup coconut and 1 cup chopped
pecans. Beat until thick to put on cake.
