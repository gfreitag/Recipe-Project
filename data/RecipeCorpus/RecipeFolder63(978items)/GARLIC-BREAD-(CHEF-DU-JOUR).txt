Title: GARLIC BREAD (CHEF DU JOUR)
Categories: New, Text, Import
1    loaf Italian bread
Yield: 4 Servings
1    stick butter, -- softened
6    cloves garlic, -- minced

Preheat broiler. Cut loaf lengthwise. Smear with butter. Sprinkle
garlic on top. Brown under broiler until golden. Serve hot.
