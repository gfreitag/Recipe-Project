Title: GERMAN DISH
Categories: Potatoes, Germany, German
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sauerkraut
1-16 oz. can applesauce
1/2 ea Dry wine
2 ea Tb. brown sugar
1-8 oz. can onions
9 ea Polish sausage
2 c  Drained potatoes, boiled
Combine all ingredients. Simmer 20 minutes and serve hot.Subj: German
Baked German Dish
