Title: GARLIC DILL RICE
Categories: Garlic, Rice, Sidedish, Pasta
Yield: 6 Servings
4    Cloves fresh garlic, minced
2 tb Olive oil
1 c  Rice
1 cn (14 1/2 oz.) chicken broth
1 ts Each dried dill and cilantro
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Onion powder
Saute garlic in hot oil until golden.  Add rice and saute for 4 to 5
minutes over high heat.  Add broth and seasonings. Bring back to boil,
cover and simmer over low heat for 30 minutes. Fluff and mix rice and
allow to stand uncovered for 5 minutes.
