Title: GARLIC BROTH
Categories: Soups/stews, Appetizers, Vegetarian, Garlic
Yield: 4 Servings
2 ea Garlic heads, unpeeled but
-- heads separated
1 ea Bay leaf, crumbled
6 c  Water
1/2 c  Chopped fresh parsley
1/2 ts Sage
1/2 ts Curry powder
1 pn Saffron
1 lg Onion, peeled & quartered
2 ea Celery stalks, cut to thirds
Wrap garlic & bay leaf in a cheesecloth.  Place with all other
ingredients in a soup pot.  Cover & bring to a boil. Loweer heat &
simmer for 25 minutes.  Remove from heat & let cool slightly. Remove
garlic & bay leaf. Puree the broth & vegetables in a blender. Season
to taste &f desired & serve.
