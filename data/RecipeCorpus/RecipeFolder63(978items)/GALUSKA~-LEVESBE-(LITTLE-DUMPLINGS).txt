Title: GALUSKA~ LEVESBE (LITTLE DUMPLINGS)
Categories: Soups/stews, Hungary, Submitted, Hungarian
Yield: 2 Servings
1 ea Eggs
3 tb Flour, all-purpose
1 pn Salt
1 ts Oil, vegetable
Mix egg with flour and salt. Spoon mixture into boiling soup,
using 1/4 teaspoon at a time. Cook in the soup for 2-3 minutes just
before serving.
