Title: FRUIT SHAKES
Categories: Kids, Beverages, Fruits, Dkuhnen msn
Yield: 2 Servings
1    Medium banana
1/2 c  Quartered strawberries
1/2 c  Pineapple or mango cut into
Chunks
3/4 c  Orange juice
1 c  Ice
Put all ingredients into blender; puree until very smooth and thick.
Source:  The Toronto Star Newspaper.
