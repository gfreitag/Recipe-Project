Title: GARLIC PUFFED SPUDS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
4 tb Melted butter
2 c  Mashed potatoes
2    Egg yolks
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1    Garlic clove, crushed
Melt butter in saucepan and mix potatoes, egg yolks and butter
together. Add all other ingredients, mixing well. Place spoonfuls of
the potato mixture on greased serving dish and place under preheated
broiler for 5 minutes, or until golden brown.
