Title: GARLIC MACKEREL
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
4 x  Large mackerel (or 8 small)
Clove garlic
Seasoned flour
Butter for frying
Lemon juice
Mince garlic very finely  Divide between the fish and rub in well.
Roll mackerel in beaten egg and then in flour. Fry in butter for 4-5
minutes each side.
Sprinkle with lemon juice and serve.
