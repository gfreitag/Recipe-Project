Title: GARLIC & MINT MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Mint leaves, fresh or dry
4 ea Garlic close, crushed
1/3 c  Peanut oil
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
4 tb Sherry
2 tb Soy sauce
Chop the mint finely.  Mix in the remaining ingredients in a large
bowl. Place meat in marinade for several hours, turning occasionally.
Drain the meat before placing on the grill.

Source:  Mesquite Cookery, By John 'Boog' Powell
