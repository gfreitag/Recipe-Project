Title: GARLIC HONEY COUGH SYRUP
Categories: Misc.
Yield: 1 Servings
12    Garlic cloves.peeled&crushed
3/4 c  Honey
3 tb Brandy
1 tb Lemon juice
In a small sauce pan, combine garlic and honey. Cook over low heat 15
minutes. Stir in brandy and lemon juice. Let stand until cool and
strain into jar. Makes
1    cup
