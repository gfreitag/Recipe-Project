Title: FUNFETTI COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Snacks, Desserts
Yield: 18 Servings
1 pk Funfetti cake mix
1/3 c  Oil
2    Eggs
1 cn Funfetti frosting
In a large bowl, combine cake mix, oil and eggs. Mix by hand until
well blended. Stir candy bits from package. Shape into 1" balls.
Place on ungreased cookie sheets, two inches apart, flatten to 1/4"
thick. Bake at 375 deg f for 6 to 8 minutes. Cool one minute on
cookie sheet, then move to wire rack. Spread warm cookies with
frosting. Makes about 3 dozen cookies.
