Title: FUDGE OATMEAL (NO BAKE) COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 lb Butter
Dash of salt
Combine ingredients.  Bring to a boil and boil hard for one minute.
Remove from fire and add 1 tablespoon of vanilla. Pour into a bowl
containing 3 cups of raw oatmeal (quick) and 1/2 cup of peanut
butter. Mix well. Drop quickly by tablespoons onto waxed paper. Randy
Rigg
