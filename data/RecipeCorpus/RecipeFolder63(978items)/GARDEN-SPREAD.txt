Title: GARDEN SPREAD
Categories: Sandwiches, Spreads
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  MIRACLE WHIP or MIRACLE WHIP
-LIGHT Dressing
1/3 c  Finely chopped cucumber,
-seeded
1/3 c  Finely chopped tomato,
-seeded
1/2 ts Dried basil leaves, crushed
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Mix all ingredients. Refrigerate.

Makes 1 cup.
