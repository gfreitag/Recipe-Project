Title: GARLIC GREEN VEGETABLE DIP
Categories: Dip, Appetizers
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Dijon mustard
1 ts Dried dill
1/4 c  Green onions - chopped
2 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Garlic salt
Pepper to taste
Directions: Combine all ingredients and blend well and chill. Serve
with fresh vegetables.
