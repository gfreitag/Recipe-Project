Title: GARDEN PIZZA
Categories: Boboli, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
-Waldine Van Geffen VGHC42A
1    16" or 4 6" boboli shells
2 tb Margarine or butter
1    Zucchini; slice very thin
1 sm Red onion; chopped
1 sm Stalk Broccoli; chopped
4 oz Chinese pea pods
1    Sweet Red pepper; diced
1    Yellow pepper; diced
1 1/2 ts Thyme
Black pepper to taste
1 oz Parmesan; grated
Preheat oven to 450~. Saute zucchini onion and broccoli in margarine
or butter until slightly tender. Add pea pods and stir 2 minutes. Add
red and yellow peppers, thyme and pepper as desired. Stir mixture
another 1 minute. Pour mixture onto Boboli shell. Sprinkle with
Parmesan and heat for 5-7 minutes. (wrv)
