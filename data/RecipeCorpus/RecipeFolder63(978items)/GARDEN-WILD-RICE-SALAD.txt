Title: GARDEN WILD RICE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Rice
Yield: 50 Servings
1 pk UNCLE BEN'S Long Grain &
-Wild Rice Blend  (36 oz)
3 qt Water
1/4 c  Margarine or butter
1 1/2 c  Cooking oil
1 c  Lemon juice
3 c  Tomatoes, fresh, diced
1 1/2 c  Green pepper, diced
1 1/2 c  Cucumber, pared and diced
1 c  Parsley, fresh, finely
-chopped
1.  Cook rice in water with margarine or butter according to package
instructions, for 20 minutes. 2. Transfer rice and remaining
liquid to shallow pan, cover and chill. 3. Fold in remaining
ingredients. Chill before serving. Measurements and timing are
designed only for Uncle Ben's Fast Cooking Rice Pilaf, Original Rice
Pilaf, Fast Cooking Spanish Rice, Long Grain & Wild Rice Blend, and
CONVERTED Brand Rice
