Title: GARLIC-PARSLEY FINGER SANDWICHES
Categories: Garlic, Appetizers, Sandwiches
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Bunches chopped parsley
2    Cloves fresh garlic, pressed
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1    Large loaf thin white bread
Combine parsley, garlic and mayonnaise; mix well. Spread mixture
between two slices of bread and cut diagonally to make four finger
sandwiches. Refrigerate until ready to use. These can be made early
on the day to be used, but not the day before.
