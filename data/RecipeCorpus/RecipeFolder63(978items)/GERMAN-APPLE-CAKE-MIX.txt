Title: GERMAN APPLE CAKE-MIX
Categories: Mixes, German
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Bake it all mix
1    Egg
1/3 c  Granulated sugar
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Bake it all mix
1/4 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1 ts Ground cinnamon
3 c  Sliced apples*
Break egg into bowl; beat. Blend in
*3 medium, pared and cored
granulated sugar and milk.  Add 1 cup Mix. Beat until thoroughly
mixed. Pour batter into an 8-inch round cake pan, bottom rubbed with
shortening. Blend 1/4 cup Mix, brown sugar, and cinnamon. Sprinkle
half of mixture over batter.  Arrange apple slices in rows on top.
Sprinkle remaining mixture over apples.  Bake in a 350 F. oven about
40 minutes. Serve warm with cream or whipped cream.  Yield:  One
8-inch round cake
