Title: GARDEN BLEND SALT SUBSTITUTE
Categories: Spices
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Parsley, dried
3 tb Basil, dried
3 tb Thyme, dried
3 tb Marjoram, dried
2 tb Rosemary, dried
2 tb Chives, dried
2 tb Paprika
1/2 ts Garlic powder
Mix the ingredients together.  Pulverize in blender or use as is.
Store in a jar in a cool, dark place. Makes about 1 cup.

Source:  Herbal Pantry by E. Tolley ISBN 0-517-58331-3 Shared by
