Title: GARLIC PITA TRIANGLES
Categories: Crs, Totest, Post, Lowfat
Yield: 12 Servings
1 tb Reduced calorie margarine
1 cl Garlic, minced
2    (6") pitaa rounds
1 tb Grated Parmesan
1/2 ts Paprika
Melt margarine and garlic in microwave.

Split pitas; brush with margarine and acut into 6 wedges each. Broil
until golden, about 1 minute.

Per 3 triangle serving: 40 cal., 1.2g pro., 1.5g (34%) fat,a 5.6g
carb., 0.2g fiber, 1mg iron, 0.3mg iron, 79mg sodium, 17mg calcium.

Cooking Light 9-95
