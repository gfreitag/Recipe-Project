Title: GARLIC SOUP (VEG TIMES)
Categories: Soups, Appetizers, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 qt Water
4    Potatoes, diced
1    Carrot, diced
2    Celery stalks, diced
1    Onion, diced
2 lg Bulbs garlic, peeled
1/2 ts Thyme
1 ds Cayenne
Salt to taste
Bring water to a boil.  Add vegetables, garlic & spices. Cook over
medium heat for 20 to 30 minutes. Puree & serve.
