Title: GARLIC & ROSEMARY ROASTED POTATOES
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb New potatoes (the small,
-red ones)
1 tb Fresh rosemary
1 ts Salt
3    Cloves garlic, chopped
Olive oil cooking spray
Wash and srub potatoes. Cut in potatoes in half. Spray a baking dish
with the cooking spray. Spray potatoes with cooking spray. Sprinkle
potatoes with garlic, rosemary, and salt and stir to mix well. Bake
uncovered for 20 to 35 minutes at 400 degrees, stirring occasionally.
