Title: GARLIC & MUSTARD BURGERS
Categories: Barbecue, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
3 tb Country-style Dijon mustard
5    Cloves garlic, finely
-chopped
4    Hamburger buns, split
4    Slices ( 1 ounce each)
-Monterey Jack cheese
Lettuce leaves
1    Jar ( 7 oz.) roasted red
-bell peppers, drained
Heat coals or gas grill.

Mix beef, mustard and garlic.  Shape mixture into 4 patties, about
3/4 inch thick.

Cover and grill patties 4 to 5 inches from medium heat 12 to 15
minutes, turning once, until no longer pink in center and juice is
clear. Add buns, cut sides down, for last 4 minutes of grilling or
until toasted. Top burgers with cheese.

Serve burgers on buns with lettuce and red bell pepper. Makes 4
