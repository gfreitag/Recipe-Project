Title: GARLIC SAUCE #1
Categories: Sauce/gravy, Phillipines
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Broth
1 1/2 c  Water
3 tb Corn starch
6 ea Cloves garlic, crushed
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/3 c  Sugar
Salt and pepper to taste
Mix together corn starch and sugar, then add the broth, soy sauce, and
water to make a gravy. Stir well, add the garlic. Heat over low flame
until thickened. GARLIC SAUCE
