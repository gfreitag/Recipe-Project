Title: FRUIT SALAD (M_C-TX2)
Categories: Salads, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
4    Apples peeled and diced
3 lg Bananas cut in small pieces
1 cn Crushed pineapple (drained)
1/2 c  Maraschino cherries (cut)
4    Oranges cut in small pieces
MMMMM----------------------SAUCE FOR ABOVE---------------------------
1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
4 c  Milk
4    Eggs
Mix the sauce ingredients. Cook over low heat until the sauce
thickens (as for custard).  Cool and pour over fruit and stir. Add 1
teaspoon vanilla. Cool in refrigerator.
