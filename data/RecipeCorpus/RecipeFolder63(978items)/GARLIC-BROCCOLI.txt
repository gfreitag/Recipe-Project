Title: GARLIC BROCCOLI
Categories: Vegetables, China, Update
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 lb Broccoli
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2 tb Sesame oil
1 1/2 ea Vegetable oil
1/2 ts Crushed red pepper
1 1/2 tb Minced garlic
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Lemon juice
Chop broccoli into 1-1/2 pieces, both the florets and the peeled
stems. Heat oils in a saucepan, add red pepper. Remove from heat, let
stand for 10 minutes, then add garlic, soy, sugar and lemon juice,
stir to dissolve the sugar. Steam the broccoli until almost tender.
Cool under running water, toss with dressing in a bowl and serve at
room temperature or chilled.Subj: Sesame Garlic Broccoli
