Title: Corned Beef Cheese Ball
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
3 oz. packages
3 tb Worcestershire sauce
6    Green onion tops chop fine
3 pk Philadelphia cream cheese
8 oz. packages
2 pk Corned beef, chopped fine
Mix well together.  Form a ball and roll in chopped pecans.
