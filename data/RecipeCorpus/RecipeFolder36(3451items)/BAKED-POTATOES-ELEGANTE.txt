Title: BAKED POTATOES ELEGANTE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
3 oz Softened Cream Cheese
2 tb Milk
Salt & Pepper to taste
6    Medium Baking Potatoes
3 tb Softened Butter
1/2 c  Sour  Cream
1 ts Onion Salt
Bake potatoes at 375 degrees until done. Cut slice from top of each
potato and scoop out insides.
Mash potato insides and mix with sour cream, cream cheese, butter,
onion salt, salt, and pepper. Beat until smooth and fluffy. Fill
potato shells with mixture.
Return to oven and bake 15 minutes or until potatoes are heated
through.
