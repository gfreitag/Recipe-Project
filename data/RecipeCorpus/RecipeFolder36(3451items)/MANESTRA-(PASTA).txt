Title: MANESTRA (PASTA)
Categories: Side dish, Pasta/rice, Greek, Holiday
Yield: 10 servings
16 oz Tomato bits
1/2 c  Romano cheese; grated
6 c  Lamb (or chicken) broth
16 oz Manestra or rice
Combine lamb broth, manestra, and tomato bits; cook over medium heat
for 20 minutes.  Sprinkle with cheese, and serve.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in the early 1970s. Typed
