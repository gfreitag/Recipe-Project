Title: Homemade Vanilla
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 servings
FRESH Vanilla beans; few
md To high quality BOURBON
Mix some medium good to high quality BOURBON with a few FRESH VANILLA
BEANS.  Cut open beans length-wise (slit them). Put in a big crockor
jar with boubon (lots). Let age for 2-3 months. Transfer to smaller
bottles. Decorate with cute label and red/green ribbon. Good homemade
vanilla. Include a recipe using Vanilla (cookies, french toast, etc.)
