Title: CHICKEN BREASTS OVER RICE
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 servings
Oz.
1/2 c  Cooking sherry
1 cn Mushroom soup
4    Chicken breasts, halved
3/4 c  Converted rice
1 cn Clear chicken broth, 13 1/2
Mix mushroom soup, chicken broth and cooking sherry together.  Add
rice and pour into well buttered 9 x 13 inch pan.  Place chicken
breasts in mixture. Sprinkle salt, pepper and paprika on top of
chicken breasts. Cover with foil.  Bake covered for 1 hour at 350
degrees.  Uncover and cook another 30 minutes.  Add water if
necessary.
