Title: Mexican Platter
Categories: Potatoes, Cheese/eggs, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Salsa
2/3 c  Sliced pickled jalapeno
-peppers (or to taste)
Sour cream
1 pk (750 gram) McCain Gold n
-Crisp Cross Trax Potatoes
1 1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar or Monterey
-Jack
Spread potatoes in single layer on two 15x10 inch baking sheets.  Bake
at 450 F for about 10 minutes.  Turn potatoes over.  Continue to bake
for an additional 5 minutes, or until nice and crisp.  Place on
platter, sprinkle cheese over top.  Spoon salsa over top and garnish
with sliced pickled jalpeno peppers. Serve with sour cream.

Origin: 1996 Recipes Plus Calendar by Eagle Brand and Realemon.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/95.
