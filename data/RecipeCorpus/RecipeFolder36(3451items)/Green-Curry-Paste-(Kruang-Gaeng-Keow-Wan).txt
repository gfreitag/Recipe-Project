Title: Green Curry Paste (Kruang Gaeng Keow Wan)
Categories: Thai, Curries, Condiments
Yield: 1 cup
15    Fresh green chilies
10    Black peppercorns; cracked
2 ts Ground coriander
2 ts Ground cumin
2 ts Salt
3    Cloves
3    Bay leaves
2 tb Vegetable oil; for blending
4 tb Roughly chopped lemon grass
1 tb Galangal (dried)
- presoaked for 30 minutes
2 tb Chopped garlic
1 md Onion; chopped
2    Whole coriander plants
-- (including root & stems)
1 ts Chopped lime or lemon zest
Blend ingredients together in blender or food processor until a smooth
paste is formed.  Refrigerate and use curry paste within a week.
Or... spread paste in a plastic zipper bag and freeze flat on a
baking sheet; break off pieces of the frozen paste as needed for
curry recipes.

Adapted by Karen Mintzias from a recipe in:
