Title: HONEY BAKED CHICKEN WINGS
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Chili sauce 1 cup honey
1 ts Garlic powder
3 lb Chicken wings
Salt and pepper
2 tb Oil
Season chicken well. Rub with oil and bake in a pre-heated 400 oven
for 15 minutes.Combine all remaining ingredients and mix with wings,
reduce heat to 375 and bake another 45 minutes. If the sauce start to
caramelize, reduce the heat again.
