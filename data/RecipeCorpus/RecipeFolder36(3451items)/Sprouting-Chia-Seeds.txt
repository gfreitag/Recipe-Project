Title: Sprouting Chia Seeds
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Chia seeds
1 ea Prepared clay saucer -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1/4 cup seeds in a clay saucer or tray, which will yield about 2
cups of sprouts.

There is no need to soak or rinse and drain; just mist the seeds
regularly to keep them moist. After 3 to 5 days, move into sunlight
to green.

Harvest when sprouts are 1 to 1-1/2 inch long. Use in salads,
sandwiches, casseroles, or as a garnish.
