Title: ASIAN BLACK BEAN MARINADE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------FOR POULTRY, PORK, LAMB-----------------------
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Sesame oil
2 tb Black bean sauce
1/2 ts Dry mustard
2 tb Dry sherry
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Makes about 1/2 cup
