Title: OKLAHOMA MILLIONAIRES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1/4    Bar paraffin *
3 c  Nuts
12 oz Chocolate chips
24 oz Butterscotch chips
* 1/4 of quarter stick.

Melt chips in double broiler, with wax, shaved thin.  Stir in nuts.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper and chill.
