Title: Corn Pasta
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
2    Beaten eggs
1/3 c  Water
1 ts Olive oil or cooking oil
1 1/3 c  Flour
1 c  Masa Harina tortilla flour
1/2 ts Salt
Stir together 1 cup of the all purpose flour, the tortilla flour, and
salt. Combine eggs, water, and olive oil; add to flour and mix well.
Sprinkle remaining flour on kneading surface.  Knead 10 minutes, let
rest 10 minutes,
roll and cut into desired shapes.

Origin:  Better Homes and Gardens Pasta Book
Shared by:  Sharon Stevens  Oct/91
