Title: Anisette Toast 2  รก
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
2/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking powder
2    Eggs
2 ts Anise seed
1 c  Flour
Beat the eggs and sugar together.  Add rest of ingredients and
mix well.  Bake in a greased loaf pan 20 minutes at 370 degrees.
When cool, remove from pan and cut into 1 inch slices.
