Title: Bongo Bongo Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Whipping cream -- whipped
1 c  Fresh oysters -- shucked
1/2 c  Spinach -- chopped
1 qt Milk
2 tb Butter
1 ts A-1Â¨ Steak Sauce
Puree oysters and spinach in a blender.

Heat milk and add puree, butter and A-1 sauce.  Bring to
simmering point.  DO NOT BOIL!
Mix cornstarch with water and add to soup to thicken.
Season.

Ladle into 4 oven-proof bowls and garnish each with
whipped cream and put bowls under broiler until cream
is browned.
