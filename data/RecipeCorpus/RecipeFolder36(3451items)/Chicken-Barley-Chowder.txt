Title: Chicken Barley Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 recipe
2 lg Carrots, cubed
Frozen corn
Frozen peas
1 cn Evaporated milk
Pepper to taste
2 lg Potatoes, cubed
4 lb Chicken
2    Stalks celery
1 ds Parsley
4 qt Water
1 tb Salt
1/2 c  Barley, uncooked
1/2 lb Bacon, cooked/crumbled
Combine all ingredients in large pot and simmer until
chicken is tender.
