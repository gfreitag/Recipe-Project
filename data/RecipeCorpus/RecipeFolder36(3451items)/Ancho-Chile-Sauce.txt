Title: Ancho Chile Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chilies
Yield: 4 cups
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Oregano
1 tb Tomato paste
1/4 ts White vinegar
1 ts Turbinado sugar
1 ds Vanilla extract
1 ds Salt and white pepper
1 c  Water
2 c  Chicken stock
3 tb Ancho chile powder
2 tb Paprika
2 tb Green tomato chile sauce
1/2 c  Onion(s), minced
1 ts Ground cumin
In a deep saucepan, combine all ingredients and, stirring constantly,
bring to a low boil. cover and, stirring often, let simmer for 45 min.

Serve over enchiladas, tamales, burritos, etc.
