Title: MARINATED TOMATOES
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Oregano leaves
1/8 ts Pepper
5 lg Tomatoes, ripe
1/4 c  Salad oil
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
1/2    Garlic clove, minced
Peel tomatoes, cut in thick slices.  Combine remaining ingredients.
Pour over tomatoes.  Chill thoroughly, stirring once or twice.
