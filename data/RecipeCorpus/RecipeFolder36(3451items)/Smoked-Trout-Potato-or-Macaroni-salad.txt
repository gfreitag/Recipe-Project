Title: Smoked Trout Potato or Macaroni salad
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Bbq/grill, Salads, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Parsley; minced
1 tb Dill weed, dried
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Lemon juice
12    Cherry tomatoes
12 sl Cucumber
Parsley; for garnish
1 lb Hot smoked lake trout
6 c  Cooked diced potatoes or
-macaroni
1/2 c  Green onions; chopped
1 c  Cucumber; diced
1 c  Celery; diced
2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Milk
Flake fish and mix with potatoes or macaroni, onions, cucumber,
celery, parsley and dill. Combine mayo, milk, pepper and lemon juice.
Pour over fish mixture. Toss salad gently. Garnish with cucumber,
tomatoes and parsley.

From: Smoking Salmon & Trout by Jack Whelan. Published by: Airie
