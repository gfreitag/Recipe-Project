Title: Fruit Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Finely chopped celery
1/2 c  Raisins (optional)
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sugar
3 lg Bananas, sliced
2 lg Red Delicious apples, peeled
-and
-diced
1/2 c  Chopped pecans, toasted
Combine all ingredients in a bowl; mix well.  Cover and chill.

Serves:  8 to 10 From: "Original Whistle Stop Cafe Cookbook" Posted
