Title: Thanksgiving Bread
Categories: Breads, Holiday
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 c  Chopped celery
1 1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Chopped pimiento
3    Eggs, beaten
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/3 c  Melted margarine
1 1/2 c  Flour
4 ts Baking powder
2 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Sage
1 ts Thyme
1 1/2 c  Corn meal
Sift the first 6 ingredients.  Add onion, celery and pimento.  Stir in
eggs, milk and margarine; stir only until blended.  Pour into 10 inch
skillet and bake for 40 minutes at 400 degrees.  To store, wrap in
foil and refrigerate.
