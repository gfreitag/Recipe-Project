Title: Crab Dip
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Dips/spread, Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt and pinch of garlic
-salt.
2    Finely chopped hard boiled
-eggs
1 cn Crabmeat, shredded
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/2 c  Mayonaise
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
Mix well and serve with your favorite crackers or potato chips.
