Title: Scallops and Snow Peas I.E.S.JJGF65A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM----------------------PHILLY.INQUIRER---------------------------
1/2 lb Sea scallops
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
1/2 ts Balsamic vinegar
1 tb Minced scallion
Salt to taste
Fresh ground pepper to taste
2 tb BUTTER
1 ts Grated gingerroot
1    Medium red jalapeno pepper
Seeded and minced
4 oz Trimmed snow peas,about
1 1/2 cups
Heat butter in large skillet.Add gingerroot and jalapeno pepper and
saute over medium heat 1 to 2 minutes.Do not allow to burn.Add snow
peas and saute until they glisten,2 to 3 minutes...
Add scallops and saute over medium high heat until cooked
through,turning over once,5 to 7 minutes.Add lemon juice,vinegar and
scallion.Season with salt and pepper.Cook over high heat until
mixture is hot and flavors blend,about 1 minute.Makes 2 servings....
