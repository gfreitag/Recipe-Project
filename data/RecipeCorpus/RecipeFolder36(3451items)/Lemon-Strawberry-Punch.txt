Title: Lemon-Strawberry Punch
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 20 servings
-concentrate
1 qt Carbonated water
1 qt Ginger ale
Thinly sliced oranges and
-lemons, for garnish
1 cn (16-oz.) Frozen orange juice
-concentrate; thawed
1 pk Frozen sliced strawberries;
-half thawed with juice
1 cn (6-oz.) Frozen lemonade
Combine orange juice, strawberries, and frozen lemonade in a punchbowl
with ice.  Just before serving, add carbonated water and ginger ale.
Garnish with orange and lemon slices.  Makes 20 servings.

Source:  AAA of Washington "Motorist"
