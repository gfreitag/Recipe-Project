Title: Applesauce Cake
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Guestchefs
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Baking Soda
1 1/2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Allspice
1/2 c  Soft Shortening
1 lg Egg
2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Applesauce
2 1/2 c  Sifted Flour
Mix shortening, sugar and egg.  Add the applesauce.  Mix flour with
spices. Add to applesauce mixture.  Stir in 1/2 cup chopped nuts.
Add 1 cup raisins.  Pour into oblong cake pan.  Bake in a 350Ã¸F oven
for 45 to 50 minutes.  If you use a square dish bake for 35 to 40
minutes.  If deeper bake longer.  Test for doneness with a toothpick.
From: Syd's Cookbook.
