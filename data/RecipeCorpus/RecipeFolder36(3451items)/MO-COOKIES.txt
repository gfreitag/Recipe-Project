Title: MO COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 72 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Baking Soda
6 c  Flour
2 c  Chocolate Chips
2 c  Nuts (Your Choice)
1 lb Butter (Softened)
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  Brown Sugar
3 ea Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
Mix together butter, sugar, borwn sugar until well creamed.  Add eggs,
vanilla, salt and baking soda and mix well. Start adding flour a
little at a time until all is in.  The batter will be pretty stiff
while adding the last 2 cups!  (Using the hands is ok)  Add chips and
nuts now.  Drop spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Leave room
for the cookie to spread out.  These get big.  Bake 10-12 minutes at
350 degrees.  Enjoy!
