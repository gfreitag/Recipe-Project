Title: Israeli Wheat Berry Stew
Categories: Main dish, Soup/stew, Vegetarian, Israeli
Yield: 8 servings
4 ea Garlic cloves, minced
5 ts Cumin
3 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 ea Green bell peppers
5 c  Trader Joe's maranara and
-- the rest water
1 1/2 c  Great northern beans
1 c  Wheat berries
6 sm Potatoes, halved
1 lg Onion, sliced
Mix together all ingredients in crockpot.  Cook at high 8 to 10
hours.
