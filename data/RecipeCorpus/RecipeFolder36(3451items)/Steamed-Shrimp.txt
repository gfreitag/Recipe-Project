Title: Steamed Shrimp
Categories: Appetizers, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Salt
1 lb Shrimp
In a saucepan, combine first four ingredients; bring to a
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 tb OLD BAY seasoning
:
boil. Add shrimp, stir gently.  Cover, steam until tender.  Drain and
serve with cocktail sauce
