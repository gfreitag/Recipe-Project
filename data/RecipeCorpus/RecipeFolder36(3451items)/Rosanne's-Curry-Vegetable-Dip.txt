Title: Rosanne's Curry Vegetable Dip
Categories: Appetizers
1 C mayonnaise
Yield: 8 servings
1 t tarragon vinegar
1/4 t salt
1/4 t curry powder
1/8 t thyme
1/2 t Chili Sauce
1/2 sm Onion, grated

Make 24 hours ahead to meld flavors.  Serve with:
Ripe olives, green pepper slices, carrot slices,
plum tomatoes, cauliflower chunks, radishes, celery,
cucumber spears, fresh mushrooms, broccoli, etc.
