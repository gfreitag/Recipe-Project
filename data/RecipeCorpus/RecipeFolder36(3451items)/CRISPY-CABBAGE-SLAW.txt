Title: CRISPY CABBAGE SLAW
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 10 servings
3/4 ts Dry mustard
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Celery seed
2 ea Carrots, grated (optional)
1 ea Cabbage, finely sliced
1 ea Large onion, finely sliced
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/3 c  HONEY
Place cabbage and onions in a large bowl.  Mix together the vinegar,
vegetable oil, HONEY, dry mustard, salt, and celery seed in a large
pan and bring to a boil.  Simmer 1-2 minutes.  Pour over cabbage
mixture and mix well.  Let stand for a few hours. Will keep
refrigerated for several days. Serves 10-12.
