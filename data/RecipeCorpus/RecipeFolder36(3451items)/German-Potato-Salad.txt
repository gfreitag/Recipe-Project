Title: German Potato Salad
Categories: Aust/german, Potatoes, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Water
1/3 c  Sugar
1 md Onion, chopped finely
1/2 ts Celery seed
2 1/2 lb Potatoes, boiled & peeled
8 sl Bacon, fried and chopped
2 tb Bacon drippings
2 tb Flour
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
In a large skillet (the one you fried the bacon in) add the
flour to the bacon drippings.  Brown slightly, then add the
vinegar and water.  When this comes to a boil add the sugar,
onion, celery seed, and bacon.  Stir in the potatoes that
have been sliced or diced.  Make sure the potatoes are coated
with the sauce.  Serve warm.
