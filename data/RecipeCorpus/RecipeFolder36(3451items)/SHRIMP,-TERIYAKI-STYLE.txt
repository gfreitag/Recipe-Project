Title: SHRIMP, TERIYAKI STYLE
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 ts Ground rosemary
1/4 ts Ground thyme
1/4 ts Ground oregano
1/4 ts Ground marjoram
2 lb Shrimp, shelled and deveined
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Sherry
2 tb Lemon juice or vinegar
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/2 ts Ground ginger
Mix soy sauce, oil, sherry, lemon juice, garlic salt, ginger,
rosemary, thyme, oregano and marjoram. Pour over shrimp. Let stand 1
to 2 hours. Remove shrimp from marinade and broil 5 to 7 minutes.
Makes 12 to 14 servings
