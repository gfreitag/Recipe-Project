Title: Bodle Open Face Spam Sandwich
Categories: Sandwich, Spam, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
2 sl Tomato
1 sl Provolone Cheese
Horseradish to taste
1 sl Bread
2 sl Spam
2 sl Onion
Place Spam on bread, top with onion, tomato and provolone cheese. Add
horseradish to taste. Microwave on high until cheese starts to melt,
about 2 minutes.
