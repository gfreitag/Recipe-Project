Title: NEPTUNE'S SURPRISE
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1 c  Mayonaise
1 tb Dill, chopped
1 tb Lemon or lime juice, fresh
1/2 c  Green Bell pepper, chopped
1 c  Potato chips, crushed
1/2 lb Shrimp, steamed & SWhelled
1/2 lb Scallops, steamed & shelled
1/2 lb Crabmeat
1/2 lb Oysters, steamed & shelled
1/4 c  Pimientos, chopped
1/4 c  Green olives, chopped
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
Mix all ingredients except the potato chips. Put into a greased
casserole. Cover and bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees F. Add potato
chips and return to oven for 20 minutes more.
