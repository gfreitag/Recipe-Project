Title: BLAFF
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Minced scallions
4    Cloves garlic, minced
1    Chili pepper (any hot
-pepper, up to ...
2    Chili peppers
2 tb Minced fresh parsley
4    Allspice berries
1 ts Minced fresh thyme, OR...
1/2 ts Dry thyme
4 ea 1 pound firm white fish
-scaled, cleaned & cut
-lengthwise in halves
-OR ...
4 ea 10 oz fish steaks, about
-1" thick
5 c  Water
1    Lime, juice of
2 ts Salt
Place fish in a glass of china dish.  In another bowl, combine
2 cups of water, the lime juice, 1 1/2 t salt, and 1 T scallions.
Pour the marinade over the fish, and marinate in the refrigerator
for 1 hour.  Drain the fish and discard the marinade.  Place the
remaining scallions, garlic, chili peppers, and parsley into a
heavy pot (preferable cast iron).  With a wooden spoon, mash the
mixture to extract the juices.  Add the remaining 3 cups of
water, allspice berries, and thyme.  Cover the pot and place on
high heat.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for about 5
minutes.  Add remaining 1/2 t salt and the fish to the broth.
Cover and bring to boil again.  Reduce heat and simmer for about
10 minutes or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.  With a
slotted spoon, transfer the fish onto heated dinner plates.  Boil
the liquid on high heat for 2 to 3 minutes and spoon over the
fish.  Garnish with lime quarters.
