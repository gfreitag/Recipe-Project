Title: CARROTS VICHY WITH THYME
Categories: Masterchefs, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1    Bay leaf
1 bn Fresh thyme
Salt and freshly ground
-pepper
500 g  Small carrots cleaned and
-left whole
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Butter
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Sprigs of thyme
Place the carrots in a saucepan with enough cold water to cover.  Add
the sugar, butter, bay leaf, half the thyme, salt and pepper to taste
and cook over medium heat for about 20 mins or until the carrots are
tender. Halfway through the cooking time, increase the heat and
reduce the liquid until it is golden brown and caramelised.
Serve the carrots garnished with the remaining sprigs of thyme.
