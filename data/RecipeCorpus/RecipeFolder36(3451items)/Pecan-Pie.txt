Title: Pecan Pie
Categories: Nuts/grains, Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
3    Eggs, well beaten
1 c  Pecans
1    Unbaked regular size (not
-deep dish) pie crust
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Dark corn syrup (KARO)
-(not pancake syrup)
1/4 c  Butter, melted
Combine sugar, corn syrup, and melted butter in medium size bowl

Add beaten eggs and pecans to sugar mixture.

Mix thoroughly.

Pour mixture into pie shell.

Place pie on pizza pan.

Bake 375 degrees  45-50 min or until knife inserted in center of pie
comes out clean.

Cool on rack

Serves 8
