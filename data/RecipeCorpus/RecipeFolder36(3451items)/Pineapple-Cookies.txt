Title: Pineapple Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 20 servings
1 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Crushed drained pineapple
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Butter
1 x  Egg, well beaten
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
Cream sugar and butter and add egg.  Then pineapple.  Sift dry
ingredients, add to first mixture.  Drop by spoonfuls. bake in oven
at 350 degrees.
