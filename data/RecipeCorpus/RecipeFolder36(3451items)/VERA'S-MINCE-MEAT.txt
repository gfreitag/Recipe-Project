Title: VERA'S MINCE MEAT
Categories: Oldies, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 recipe
1/4 lb Citron
2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Spice
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Cider
2 1/2 lb Beef
2 1/2 lb Pork
2 lb Raisins
1 1/2 lb Apples
1 lb Dried apricots
1 lb Dried peaches
Grind and mix all ingredients together.  Simmer two hours.  Can and
seal.
