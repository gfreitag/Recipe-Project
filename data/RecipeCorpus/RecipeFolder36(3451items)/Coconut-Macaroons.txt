Title: Coconut Macaroons
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 30 servings
1/2 ts EACH, vanilla and almond
-extracts
3 1/2 oz Flaked coconut
2    Egg whites
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cream of tartar
3/4 c  Sugar
red or green food coloring, optional
candied cherries, optional

Beat egg whites until foamy.  Add salt and cream of tartar.  Beat
until soft peaks form.  Gradually beat in sugar, 1 tbsp at a time,
beating until stiff and glossy.  Fold in the extracts and coconut.
Tint different shades if desired.  Drop from teaspoon onto foil
covered cookie sheet.  Put a cherry in the center of each.  Bake in a
slow oven (300F) for about 20 minutes.  Makes about 30 macaroons.

Origin: Woman's Day Encyclopedia of Cookery, Volume 3.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/95.
