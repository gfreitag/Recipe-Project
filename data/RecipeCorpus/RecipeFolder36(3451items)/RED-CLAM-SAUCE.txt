Title: RED CLAM SAUCE
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Lemon Juice
1 tb Fresh Parsley; Chopped
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Rosemary
1/4 ts Thyme; Ground
1 ea Onion; Md, Chopped
1 ea Clove Garlic; Minced
2 tb Olive Oil
13 oz Clams; Minced, 2 Cns
6 oz Tomato Paste; 1 Cn
1 c  Water
Saute the onion and garlic in the oil in medium skillet, add the
clams and their juice, the tomato paste, water, lemon juice, parsley,
sugar, rosemary and thyme.  Simmer, uncovered, for 15 minutes.  Serve
over cooked linguine or other pasta (8 oz) with grated Parmesan
Cheese, if desired.
