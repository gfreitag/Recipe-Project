Title: Best Of The Border Dry Rub Austin American/7/94
Categories: Bbq/grill, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Garlic powder
3 tb Cayenne pepper
8 tb MSG, optional
12 tb Salt
8 tb Black pepper
2 tb Chili powder
Combine all ingredients and store in airtight container.  Use as a
rub for brisket.  Makes about 2 1/4 cups.  Recipe comes from the Del
Rio Chamber of Commerce Chuck Wagon Gang.  ---- Texas Barbecue
