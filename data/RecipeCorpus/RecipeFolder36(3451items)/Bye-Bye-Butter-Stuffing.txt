Title: Bye Bye Butter Stuffing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Breads, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 sm Onion; coarsely chopped
4 c  Pepperidge Farm Herb Season
.  Stuffing
1 cn Swanson Chicken broth; 14oz
ds Pepper
1    Stalk celery; chopped
In a medium saucepan mix the broth, pepper, celery and onion.  Over
high heat, heat to a boil.  Reduce heat to low.  Cover and cook 5
minutes or until the vegetables are tender.

Add the dry stuffing.  Mix lightly.
