Title: GARLIC-ANCHOVY RUB
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------BEEF,POULTRY,PORK,LAMB,FISH---------------------
2 ts Olive oil
1/4 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1    Garlic clove; crushed
1 ts Anchovy paste
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mash until smooth. Makes about
1-1/2 Tbsp.
