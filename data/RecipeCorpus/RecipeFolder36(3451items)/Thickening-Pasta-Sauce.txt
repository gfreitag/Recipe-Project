Title: Thickening Pasta Sauce
Categories: Information, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 info
None
If your tomato sauce turns out thinner then you like, try adding a
tablespoon or two of dry breadcrumbs. They give body and substance by
absorbing some of the sauce without changing the flavor.
