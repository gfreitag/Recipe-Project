Title: Brussel Sprouts with Chestnuts
Categories: Vegetables, Holiday, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 servings
-and cooked, (kept warm)
6 tb Butter
4 c  Brussels sprouts
1 1/2 c  Chestnuts, whole, peeled
Parboil the brussel sprouts until tender crisp, drain and refresh.  In
large skillet, melt the 6 tbsp butter and stir fry the chestnuts until
they are starting to brown.  Quickly add the brussel sprouts and
continue to cook until brussel sprouts and chestnuts are warm all the
way through.  Serve immediately.  Makes 8-10 servings.
Origin: Adapted from Woman's Day Encyclopedia of Cookery, Volume 3.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/95.
