Title: Rum Balls 2
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 servings
2 tb Light corn syrup
1/4 c  Rum
2 tb Cocoa
1 c  Chocolate or Vanilla cookie
Crumbs
1 c  Confectioners' sugar
1 1/2 c  Finely chopped walnuts
1. Combine cookie crumbs, confectioners' sugar, 1 cup walnuts, corn
syrup, rum, and cocoa in bowl; mix well. Form into 1-inch balls.
2. Roll each ball in remaining walnuts. Air-dry about 1 hour. Store in
airtight container in cool place; these will keep for several weeks.
3. VARIATION: Reduce nuts to 1 cup. Roll finished balls in sugar or
shredded coconut.
