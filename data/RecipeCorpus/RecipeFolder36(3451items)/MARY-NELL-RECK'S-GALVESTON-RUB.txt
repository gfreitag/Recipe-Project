Title: MARY NELL RECK'S GALVESTON RUB
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------FOR POULTRY AND PORK-------------------------
2 tb Paprika
1 tb Lemon juice
6    Garlic cloves; crushed
1 tb Cayenne pepper
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mash until smooth.
Makes about 1/3 cup.
