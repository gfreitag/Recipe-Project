Title: SNOWBALLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 100 balls
6 oz Orange juice, frozen
Flaked coconut
1 lb Vanilla wafers
1/4 lb Butter, soft (1 stick)
1 lb Powdered sugar
Place the Vanilla wafers in a paper bag and crush with rolling pin to
make very fine crumbs.  (don't eat any, you need them all!)

Mix crumbs, butter, powdered sugar and OJ into large bowl.  Roll into
small balls and roll balls in coconut.  refrigerate.
