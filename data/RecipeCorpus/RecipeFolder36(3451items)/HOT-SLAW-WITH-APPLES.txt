Title: HOT SLAW WITH APPLES
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 servings
3 tb BUTTER OR SALAD OIL
1 1/2 ts SALT
1/2 ts CARAWAY SEEDS
1 lg APPLE, UNPEELED, THIN SLICED
6 c  CABBAGE, FINE CHOPPED
3 tb WATER
3 tb LEMON JUICE
2 tb SUGAR
Place all ingredients except apple slices in skillet; cook, uncovered,
over medium high heat, stirring until cabbage is tender, about 3
minutes.

Reduce heat to low; add apple, cover and cook 1 minute longer.  Serve
at once.
