Title: Sprouting Hulled Sunflower Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar
1 c  Hulled sunflower seeds
Use 1 cup in a quart jar which will yield about 3 cups of sprouts.
Soak for 10 hours.

Rinse 2 to 3 times daily for 2 to 5 days.

Harvest when sprouts are 1 to 1-1/2 inches long. Use in salads,
sauces, and dips.
