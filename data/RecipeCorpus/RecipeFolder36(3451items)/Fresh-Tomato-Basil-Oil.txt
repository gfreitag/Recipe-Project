Title: Fresh Tomato-Basil Oil
Categories: Condiments, Tomatoes
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
1 ts Red pepper flakes
5    Garlic clove(s), peeled
3 c  Olive oil
2 lg Tomatoes (abt 1 lb)
Seeded, coarsely chopped
1 c  Packed basil leaves
Washed and dried
In a deep pot, bring all ingredients to 220 F to 250 F and heat for
20 min. Let cool, and then strain through cheesecloth into a clean,
dry bottle.

Serving Suggestion: Boil pasta, toss it with fresh tomatoes and
basil, and add tomato-basil oil to match. Serve with salad and bread
for a quick main course.
