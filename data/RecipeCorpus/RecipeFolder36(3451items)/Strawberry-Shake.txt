Title: Strawberry Shake
Categories: Snacks, Guestchefs
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Light Brown Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla Extract
1 c  Fresh Strawberries *
3/4 c  Skim Milk
1/2 c  Low Fat Yogurt
* Strawberries should be rinsed and hulled.  Frozen strawberries can
be used and should be thawed and drained.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Place all ingredients in a blender bowl and process until smooth.
Serve at once.  133 calories per serving.  From Syd's Cookbook.
