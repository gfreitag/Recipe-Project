Title: Chop Suey^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Main dish, Chinese
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Water
1 cn (28oz) chop suey vegetables,
Drained
2 tb Soy sauce
Cooked rice
Chow mein noodles
1 lb Ground beef, browned and
Drained
2    Beef bouillon cubes
Dissolved in
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
2 tb Cornstarch dissolved in
Combine beef and bouillon; stir in cornstarch mixture. Bring to a
boil; reduce heat and simmer until thickened. Add veggies and soy
sauce; stir until heated through. Serve with rice and noodles.
