Title: FLORIDA COLESLAW (FLORIDA COOKING)
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Freshly ground pepper
3 tb Red wine vinegar
2 tb Vegetable or olive oil
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/2    Head (3/4 to 1 pound)-
-cabbage
3 ea Onions or scallions with-
-tops, sliced very thin
1 1/2 ts Dried dillweed or 2 tb-
-minced fresh dill
Shred the cabbage fine with a sharp knife or in a food processor.
Cut out the core; this is the cook's bonus if it is sweet, discard it
if it is bitter.  In a medium-size bowl, combine the cabbage with the
green onions, dill, salt, pepper, and vinegar.  Toss lightly, add the
oil and mayonnaise, and toss again.  Cover and chill 1 hour before
serving.

FROM:  The Florida Cookbook, by Jeanne Voltz and Caroline Stuart.
