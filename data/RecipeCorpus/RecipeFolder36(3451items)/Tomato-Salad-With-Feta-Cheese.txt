Title: Tomato Salad With Feta Cheese
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Olive oil
1 tb Red wine vinegar
Salt and pepper
Leaf lettuce -- optional
1    " chunks
Coarsely chopped
3 md Tomatoes -- cut into
-
1 md Cucumber -- peeled & diced
7    Imported black olives
-
4 oz Feta cheese -- crumbled
Add cucumbers and olives to tomatoes.  Crumble feta cheese over
vegetables; drizzle with olive oil and vinegar.  Add salt and pepper
to taste just before serving.  Serve on a bed of lettuce, if desired.
