Title: DROP BISCUITS II
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 biscuits
2    Eggs, beaten
3/4 c  Heavy cream
2 c  Flour, sifted
1 tb Baking powder
1 ts Salt
Sift together all dry ingredients.  Add eggs and cream.  Stir to mix
(dough should be lumpy and soft)  Drop from tablespoon onto ungreased
baking sheet

Bake at 400 F. for 15 minutes.
