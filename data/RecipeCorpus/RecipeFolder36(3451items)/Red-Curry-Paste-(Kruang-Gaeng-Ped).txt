Title: Red Curry Paste (Kruang Gaeng Ped)
Categories: Thai, Curries, Condiments
Yield: 300 ml
2 tb Chopped garlic
4 tb Roughly chopped lemon grass
1 tb Galangal, dried
-- soaked 1/2 hour
-- in hot water
4 tb Chopped coriander roots
2 ts Chopped lime zest
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 tb Ground red pepper
15 md Dried red chilies
10    Black peppercorns
1 tb Ground coriander
1 tb Ground cumin
2 ts Salt
4    Whole cloves
3    Bay leaves
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1 md Onion; chopped
Grind first 8 ingredients in a blender or spice mill.

Process all ingredients together in blender or food processor until a
smooth paste is formed.  Refrigerate and use curry paste within a
week, or spread paste in a plastic zipper bag and freeze flat on a
baking sheet, breaking off pieces of the frozen paste as needed for
curry recipes.
