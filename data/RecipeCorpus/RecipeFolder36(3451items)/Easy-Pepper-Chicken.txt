Title: Easy Pepper Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
4    Skinless boneless chicken
-breast halves
2 tb Olive oil
1 tb Paprika
1/2 ts Ground pepper
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Salt
Combine seasonings and sprinkle evenly over chicken.  Heat oil in  a
large skillet over medium high heat.  Brown chicken for 2 minutes on
each side. Reduce heat to medium, cover skillet and until chicken is
done, about 10 minutes.
