Title: Brown Sugar Ginger Snaps
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1 1/2 c  Flour
3/4 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Butter, unsalted at room tem
1 c  Brown sugar
1    Large egg yolk
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Finely chopped crystallized
Cream together the butter and brown sugar.  Beat in the egg, vanilla,
ginger.  Sift the flour, baking powder and salt, and then combine
with the other ingredients. This may be put in the frig for a half
hour or so to make the dough easier to handle. Roll out cookies and
cut.  Put on an ungreased baking sheet at 350 degrees for 10-12
minutes. From Gourmet Magazine. Source: mom
