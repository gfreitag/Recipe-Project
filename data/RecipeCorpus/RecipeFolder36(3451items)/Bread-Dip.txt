Title: Bread Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Dill weed
2 ts Beaumonde spice
1 cn Corned beef
Pumpernickel or Rye bread
1 1/3 c  Sour cream
1 1/3 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Parsley flakes
2 tb Onion flakes
Mix together all ingredients except for bread. Hollow out the bread,
leaving a bowl. Put the dip in the "bowl". Cut up the scooped out
bread into chunks for dipping.
