Title: Baked Rockfish With Ginger-Lime Sauce
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 tb Margarine
1 tb Fresh ginger root -- grated
2 tb Lime juice
2 tb Ketchup
1 1/3 lb Rockfish fillet
1/4 ts Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
-to taste
Place the fish fillets on a rack set in a pan (or use broiling pan),
and sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Bake in a preheated
450-degree oven for 12 minutes per inch of thickness.
While the fish is cooking, melt the margarine in a small
saucepan.  Add the ginger, lime juice and ketchup.  Stir
until blended and hot.

Spoon sauce over fish and serve.
