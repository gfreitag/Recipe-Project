Title: STRAWBERRY-GLAZED FRUIT SALAD
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
4    Firm large bananas, sliced
1    Container (16 oz.)
-strawberry glaze
1 qt Fresh strawberries,
-stemmed and halved
1 cn (20 oz.) pineapple chunks,
-drained
In a large bowl, gently toss together strawberries, pineapple
chunks and banana slices.  Fold in strawberry glaze.  Chill 1 hour
before serving.
