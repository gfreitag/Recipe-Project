Title: CHICKEN AND SPAGHETTI
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1    Stick of margarine
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
Velveeta cheese  10 oz.
1/2 c  Milk
8    Chicken breasts
1 lg Onion, chopped
1    Celery stalk, chopped
1/2 c  Mushrooms, chopped
1 pk Spaghetti, cooked  10 oz.
Boil chicken until done.  Debone chicken.  Cut cheese and margarine
into squares and let stand at room temperature for about 15 minutes.
Mix chicken and spaghetti.  Mix other ingredients.  Bake at 350
degrees for about 20 minutes or until brown. Randy Rigg
