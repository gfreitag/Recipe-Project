Title: COTTAGE/BLUE CHEESE DRESSING
Categories: Condiments, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 cup
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Blue cheese, crumbled
1/3 c  Cottage cheese
2 tb Vinegar
1/8 ts Salt
3 dr Tobasco sauce
In small bowl, blend vinegar, salt, and Tobasco sauce; blend in
Mayonnaise and beat until smooth.

Stir in blue cheese and cottage cheese.   1 1/3 cups
