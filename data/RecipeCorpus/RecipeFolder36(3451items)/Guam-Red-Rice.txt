Title: Guam Red Rice
Categories: Stove top, Guam, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Rice; uncooked
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Peas; frozen
4 sl Bacon;cut into small pieces
1 md Onion; chopped
2 c  Water; divided
2 tb Achiote seeds
This is a Guam national dish that is rather mild.

Saute bacon and onion about 5 minutes, drain off fat and set aside.
Add 1/2 cup water to achiote seeds to color the water, about 10 to 15
minutes.
Strain the seeds from the water and pour the red water into a
saucepan; discard the seeds.  Add remaining water, rice, salt, bacon
and onion. Bring to a boil, cover and turn heat down to a simmer;
cook 20 to 25 minutes. When rice is almost cooked, stir in frozen
peas.  Serve warm.

Source: Peter Cruz Benavente, Oregonian Food Day Nov 1994
