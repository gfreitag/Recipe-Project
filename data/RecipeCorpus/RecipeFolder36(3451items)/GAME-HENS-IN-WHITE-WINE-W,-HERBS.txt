Title: GAME HENS IN WHITE WINE W/ HERBS
Categories: British isl, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Freshly ground pepper
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Olive oil
2    Shallots, finely chopped
1/2 c  Dry white wine
2    Cornish game hens, 1/2'd
-lengthwise, backbones
-removed
1/4 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Dried oregano
1/4 ts Salt
Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 25 minutes

Simple & elegant. Serve w/ California Sauvignon Blanc or an Italian
Pinot Grigio.

1. Preheat oven to 350o. Season hens w/ basil, oregano, salt & pepper.

2. In a large frying pan, melt butter in oil over moderately high
heat. Add hens & saute, turning once, 4 minutes a side, until
browned. Transfer to a small baking dish.

3. Add shallots to pan, reduce heat to low & saute until soft, 1 1/2
minutes.
Add white wine & bring to a boil, scraping up browned bits from
bottom of pan.
Pour over hens.

4. Cover w/ foil & bake 20 minutes, basting occasionally w/ pan
juices.

5. Reduce heat to 425o, uncover & bake 5 minutes until hens are
tender & skin
is crisp.
