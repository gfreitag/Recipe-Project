Title: BURTA
Categories: Vegetables, Indian
Yield: 1 batch
1 ts Ground cumin seeds
1 ds Pepper
Salt; to taste
Chili powder; to taste
2 tb Ghee or oil
2 lg Black eggplants
1 lg Onion; minced
2 md Tomatoes; blanched & sliced
4    Green chilies; minced
1    Handful of coriander leaves
Broil eggplants over gas flame until skin is charred.  Discard peel
and mash eggplant to a pulp.
Heat ghee or oil and cook onion and chilies until soft. Add tomatoes
and spices and continue to cook until tomatoes are soft.  Mix in
eggplant and cook until mixture is almost dry.  Serve hot garnished
with chopped coriander.
