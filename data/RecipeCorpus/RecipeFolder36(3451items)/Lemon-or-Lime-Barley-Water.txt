Title: Lemon or Lime Barley Water
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
3 tb Honey or sugar
Or to taste
3 tb Pearl barley
4 tb Lemon juice
Or lime juice
Place 4 1/2 cups of water in a heavy pot with the barley and bring to
a boil. Reduce the mixture by 50% over high heat. Strain the liquid
into a clean glass container. Mix in the lemon or lime juice and
honey or sugar; stir well. Serve well chilled.
