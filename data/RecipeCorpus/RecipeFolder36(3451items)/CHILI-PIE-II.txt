Title: CHILI PIE II
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Main dish, Casseroles, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts To 2 t dried chili powder
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Sliced stuffed olives
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 1/2 c  Tortilla chips or corn chips
-coarsely crumbled
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef round
1 md Onion, thinly sliced
12    Ounce, whole kernel corn,
Vacuum-packed can, drained
16 oz Can whole peeled tomatoes,
-undrained
8 oz Tomato sauce with cheese
1. Crumble meat into a deep, 1 1/2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic
casserole.
2. Add onions and heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 7 minutes or
until meat
is browned and onions are tender. Stir occasionally. Drain off excess
fat. 3. Add corn, tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder to taste and
salt and pepper. Stir to combine.
4. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 4 minutes, stirring
occasionally. 5. Arrange olive slices on top of meat mixture and
sprinkle with cheese. 6. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 3 minutes.
7. Sprinkle with tortilla chips and heat, uncovered, in Microwave
Oven 1 minute.
