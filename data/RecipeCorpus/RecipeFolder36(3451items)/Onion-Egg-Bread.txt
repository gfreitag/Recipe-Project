Title: Onion Egg Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
4 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
2 tb Unsalted butter
1 c  Water
1 lg Egg
1 ts Lemon juice
2 1/4 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
3 tb Onion flakes
1 1/2 ts Poppy seeds
1 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
Use basic cycle.  Do not use timer.
