Title: MCCARTHY FAMILY FISH CHOWDER
Categories: Soup/stew, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
1 pt Boiling water
1 1/4 qt Milk
1 tb Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ea Sliced onion (chopped)
6 ea Cubed potatoes (diced)
1 1/2 lb Fresh haddock (back skinned)
1/2 c  Diced salt pork
Cook salt pork until brown, remove salt pork pieces and place in
separate dish leaving salt pork oil in kettle.  Now saut diced onion
in the same kettle until brown.  Add potatoes and 1 pint boiling
water, simmer 10 minutes, add fish and simmer 20 minutes. Add milk,
salt, pepper and pices of salt pork previously sauted.  Heat to
boiling and serve.
