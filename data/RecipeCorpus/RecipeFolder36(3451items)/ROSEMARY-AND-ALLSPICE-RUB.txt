Title: ROSEMARY AND ALLSPICE RUB
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------FOR BEEF,POULTRY,PORK,LAMB----------------------
1/2 ts Finely chpd.rosemary leaves
2 tb Olive oil
1    Garlic clove; crushed
10    Allspice berries; crushed
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mix until smooth.
Makes about 1/4 cup.
