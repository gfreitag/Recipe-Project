Title: ORIENTAL PLUM SAUCE MARINADE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Asian, Poultry, Chinese
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Fresh Cilantro leaves
- chopped
1/2 ts Dried red chili pepper
- crushed
2 ts Hoisin sauce
1 tb Soy sauce - low sodium
1 tb Ginger root - grated
2/3 c  Plum sauce - OR Duck sauce
2 tb Dry Sherry
1 ts Oriental Sesame sauce
2 tb Peanut oil
- Asian or domestic cold
-pressed
2    Clove garlic
- minced or pressed
Combine the plum sauce, hoisin, sherry and soy sauce in a nonreactive
mixing
bowl.  Whisk in the oils a little at a time.  Add the ginger, garlic,
cilantro and chili pepper. Makes 2 cups.
Recommended cuts:
Chicken Breasts (4 to 6 hours)
Chicken Wings   (6 to 8 hours)
(8 hours to overnight)
Spare Ribs
