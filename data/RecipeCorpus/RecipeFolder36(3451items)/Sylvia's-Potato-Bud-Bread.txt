Title: Sylvia's Potato Bud Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Potatoes
Yield: 16 servings
MMMMM-----------------COPYRIGHT 1995 S. STEIGER----------------------
1/2 tb Salt
1 tb Oil
1 lg Eggs
7/8 c  Water; warm
1/2 tb Yeast
2 1/4 c  Flour, bread
3 tb Potato buds
3 tb Milk, powdered
1/2 tb Sugar
Bring bread ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order.  Set "baking control" at 11 o'clock.  Select "white bread" and
push Start.

In hot & humid weather, use 1/8 c less water.

Tested in DAK R2D2.  Sylvia's comments: coarse bread, but tasty.
