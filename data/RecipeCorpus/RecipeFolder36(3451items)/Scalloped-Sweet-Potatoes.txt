Title: Scalloped Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 1 recipe
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Flour
1 c  Beer
Salt & pepper to taste
3 lg Sweet potatoes or yams
1 lg Onion, thinly sliced
3    Tart-sweet apples, washed
4 tb Butter (divided use)
Preheat oven to 350.

Thinly slice potatoes and layer them in a agreased 3
quart casserole. Slice onions and place over potatoes.
Core apples and slice.  Cover onion layer. Melt 2 T of
the butter and drizzle over casserole; sprinkle with
brown sugar.  Melt remaining butter in a sauce pan and
stir in flour, salt and pepper; cook over low heat for
1 to 2 minutes.  Slowly add beer and cook until
thickened.  Pour over layered potatoes, onions and
apples. Cover with foil and bake at 350 for 30 to 45
minutes.  Uncover and cook 15 - 20 minutes more.
