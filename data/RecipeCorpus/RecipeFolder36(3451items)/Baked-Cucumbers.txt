Title: Baked Cucumbers
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Flour
Margarine
2 lg Cucumbers, sliced
2    Spanish onions
1 c  Chili sauce
1 ts Salt
Arrange cucumbers and onions in layers, adding to each layer, salt,
pepper and flour.  Cover with chili sauce and dot with butter.  Bake
at 350 degrees until tender, about 30 minutes.
