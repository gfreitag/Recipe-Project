Title: MACARONI SALAD FOR 100
Categories: Salads, Pasta/rice
Yield: 100 servings
2 bn Celery, chopped (12 cups)
2 lg Onions, chopped (2 1/2 cups)
2 cn Pitted ripe olives, drained
-and sliced
6 lb Fully cooked ham, cubed
6 lb Macaroni, cooked & drained
3 lb Shredded cheddar cheese
2    Bags, frozen peas, thawed
-20 ounces each
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1 c  Light cream
1 1/2 ts Onion salt
1 1/2 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
2 qt Mayonaise
1    Bottle western or French
-salad dressing
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Sugar
Combine first 7 ingredients. Combine all dressing ingredients; pour
over the ham mixture and toss. Refrigerate.
