Title: THAI-STYLE MARINADE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------FOR POULTRY AND PORK-------------------------
2    Garlic cloves
1 tb Fresh cilantro; chopped
1/4 ts Freshly ground pepper
1    Small bay leaf
2 tb Honey
Peel of 1 lemon
Peel of 1 orange
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Oyster sauce
1/2 ts Ginger root; chopped
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Makes about 1-1/4 cups
