Title: Fast Clam Chowder
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 pot
2 c  Milk
2 ts Salt
1 tb Cornstarch
2 ts Parsley
1 pn Sage
1 pn Thyme
2 tb Butter
4    6.5-oz cans minced clams
Pepper to taste
5    Potatoes; diced
1 c  Onions; chopped
2 c  Water
Melt butter in saucepan.  Add onions and saute until amber.  Drain the
liquid from clams and add to the saucepan, reserving meat.  Add water,
salt, pepper, spices, and cornstarch and stir well.  Add potatoes.
Cover and cook at a quick simmer for 10 minutes.  Uncover and add
milk and clams.  Cook, stirring constantly, until the chowder
simmers.  Do not boil.

This makes a tolerably good chowder.
