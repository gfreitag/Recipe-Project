Title: KOREAN BBQ TOFU
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
4    Cloves garlic or 1/2 tsp
Garlic powder
2 ts Onion powder
1/2 c  Soy sauce
5    6 T sugar or sweetener
2 ts Dry mustard
a  little water

Cut tofu in strips 1/2 inch thick and marinate, then fry and garnish
with vegetables of your choice.  I like to cut tofu in squares and
throw in a vegetable stir-fry.  Enjoy! -
