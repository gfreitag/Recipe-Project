Title: Chocolate Rum Dums
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 batch
1 c  Xx sugar
1/4 c  Light corn syrup
1/4 c  Dark rum
8 1/2 oz Pkg chocolate wafers,
-crushed
1 c  Pecans, chopped
Combine crushed wafer, pecans, & sugar, syrup & rum.
Mix well. shape by tspfull into round balls. Let stand
10 minutes. Roll in extra XX sugar.
