Title: Sprouting Oats
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Oat seeds
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared towel
Use 1-1/2 cups seeds in a quart jar, or sprout on towels, which will
yield about 2 to 3 cups of sprouts. Soak for 1 hour.

Rinse once or twice daily for 3 days. The sprouts will be the length
of the seed.

Use in salads, granola, and baked goods.
