Title: Herb Vinaigrette
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1    Clove Garlic -- minced
1 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Chopped fresh basil
2 tb Dry red wine
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 tb Minced shallots
1 tb Chopped chives
Whisk together first 10 ingredients.  Gradually whisk in oil.  Season
to taste with salt and pepper.  Drizzle over lettuce quarters with
feta or roquefort cheese.

Ruth Quinn  20 Oct 1994
