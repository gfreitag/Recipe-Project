Title: LAMB CHOP AND VEGETABLE SKILLET
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Sliced zucchini or summer sq
1/2 c  Sliced green onions
1 ea Large tomato, cut into wedge
1/2 c  Beef or chicken bouillon
4 ea Lamb loin -OR-
1 x  8 rib chops, cut 3/4-inch th
1 x  Salt
1 x  Pepper
2 tb Butter or margarine
Sprinkle both sides of lamb chops with salt and pepper.  Melt butter
or margarine in skillet over medium heat.  Add lamb and quickly cook
until browned on both sides.  Add squash, onions, tomatoes, and
bouillon.  Cover and cook about 5 minutes, or until vegetables are
tender-crisp.
From NEW and Natural Fresh American Lamb Recipes for Special Occasions
