Title: Double Chocolate Fantasy Bars
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 32 servings
1 3/4 c  (12 Oz) M&Ms Semi-Sweet
Chocolate Baking Bits
1 c  Shredded Coconut
1 c  Walnuts Or Pecans --
Chopped
1/3 c  Butter Or Margarine --
Melted
2 c  Chocolate Cookie Crumbs
(Oreos Work Fine)
1 cn (14 Oz) Sweetened Condensed
Milk
Preheat oven to 350 F (325 F for glass pan).
In a bowl combine butter and cookie crumbs; press mixture into the
bottom of a 13 x 9-inch baking pan. Pour sweetened condensed milk
evenly over the cookie crumbs. Combine the baking bits, coconut and
nuts. Sprinkle mixture evenly over condensed milk; press in lightly.
Bake 25 to 30 mins or until set. Cool completely before cutting.
Store in tightly covered container. Makes 32 bars.
