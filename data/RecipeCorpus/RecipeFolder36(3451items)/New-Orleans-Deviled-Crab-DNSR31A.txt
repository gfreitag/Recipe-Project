Title: New Orleans Deviled Crab DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 1/2 ts Lemon juice
1 tb Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Hot sauce
1 ea Egg, beaten
2 tb Butter, melted
16 oz Crabmeat, drained and flaked
2 c  Breadcrumbs, soft, divided
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
2 tb Green pepper, chopped
2 tb Parsley, fresh, chopped
2 tb Chives, chopped
1/2 ts Salt
2 ea Eggs, hard cooked, chopped
Combine crab, 1 cup breadcrumbs and remaining
ingredients except butter. Spoon into lightly greased
2 qt baking dish.  Combine butter and 1 cup
breadcrumbs and sprinkle on top.  Bake at 375 degrees
for 35 minutes. Formatted by Mary Dishongh Bowles.
