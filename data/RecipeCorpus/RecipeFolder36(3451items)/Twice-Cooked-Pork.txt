Title: Twice Cooked Pork
Categories: Chinese, Meats, Asian
Yield: 4 servings
3    Cloves garlic, sliced
1 tb Bean paste
1/2 tb Chili paste
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Sugar
3 tb Oil
1/2 lb Pork leg
1    Scallion
2 sl Ginger
1/4 c  Rice wine
2    Green peppers, cut in 1"
-sq
2    Red peppers, cut in 1" sqs
Place the pork (in one piece) in a pot, combine with ginger, scallion,
wine, and water to cover. Boil 30 min. Remove, drain, and when cool,
slice pork in thin slices. Mix bean paste, chili paste, soy sauce,
and sugar. Stir-fry pork in 3 Tbs oil for 1 min; add garlic and green
and red pepper; stir-fry 1 min. Remove from wok. Usw remaining oil to
stir-fry the bean paste mixture for 10 sec. Return pork, stir to mix,
and serve.
