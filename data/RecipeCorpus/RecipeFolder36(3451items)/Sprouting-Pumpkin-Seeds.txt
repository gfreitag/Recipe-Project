Title: Sprouting Pumpkin Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Pumpkin seeds
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1-1/2 cups in a quart jar, or tray sprout, which will yield about
3 cups of sprouts. Soak for 10 hours.

Rinse twice daily for 2 to 3 days.

Harvest when sprouts are 1 to 1-1/2 inches long; pick off hulls and
rinse. Use in sauces, dips, and baked goods.
