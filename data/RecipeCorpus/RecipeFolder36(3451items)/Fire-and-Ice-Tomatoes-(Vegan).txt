Title: Fire and Ice Tomatoes (Vegan)
Categories: Salads, Tomatoes
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Mustard seeds
1 lg Cucumber, sliced
3/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/8 ts Black pepper
6 md Tomatoes, quartered
1 md Onion, sliced
1 md Green pepper, cut in strips
1/4 c  Water
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Celery salt
In a large bowl combine tomatoes, onion, green pepper, and cucumber.
In a small saucepan combine vinegar, water, sugar, celery salt,
mustard seeds, salt, cayenne pepper and black pepper.  Bring to a
boil, boil 1 minute. Pour hot vinegar mixture over tomato mixture.
Cover and refrigerate 8 hours or overnight to blend flavors.  Serve
with slotted spoon.
