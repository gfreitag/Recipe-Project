Title: Frozen Fruit Dessert
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
Thawed
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
2    Mashed bananas
12 oz Cool Whip
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
3/4 c  Sugar
1 lg Can of drained pineapple
Tidbits
10 oz Frozen strawberries, barely
Beat cream cheese and sugar.  In a separate bowl, mix remaining
ingredients.  Combine mixtures in freezer-safe container.  Freeze
overnight.  Remove from freezer approximately 30 minutes before
serving.
