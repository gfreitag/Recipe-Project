Title: HINT OF GINGER MARINADE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------FOR POULTRY, PORK, FISH-----------------------
1 tb Sweet red pepper; minced
1 1/2 ts Ginger root; minced
2    Small scallions; chopped
1 ts Lemon peel; grated
3/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Dry sherry
1    Small carrot; peeled, minced
1    Garlic clove; minced
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Makes about 1-1/4 cups
