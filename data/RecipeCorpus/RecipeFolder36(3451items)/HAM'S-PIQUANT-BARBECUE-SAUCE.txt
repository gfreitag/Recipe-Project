Title: HAM'S PIQUANT BARBECUE SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Mustard
4 ts Extra hot chile powder
1 ts Cumin
1 tb Capers, chopped
1    Glass red wine
2 md Onions, chopped
Olive oil
1    Green bell pepper, chopped
2    Beef tomatoes, chopped
2 ts Allspice
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
2 ts Mild paprika
2 ts Oregano
2 ts Olive paste
1 tb Soy sauce
Black pepper
4    Cloves Garlic, chopped
1    Red bell pepper, Chopped
12 oz Tomatoes, chopped
1    Sml can concentrated tomato
-paste
1 ts Ginger
Sweat the onions and garlic in a saucepan with the olive oil, taking
care not to brown (a pot with close fitting lid helps), add the sweet
peppers cook a little longer, add the tomato, cook a little more,
then chuck everything in and cook for another 45 minutes. Liquidize.
That's it.
