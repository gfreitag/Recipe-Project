Title: SPICY SOUTHWESTERN MARINADE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------BEEF, POULTRY, PORK, LAMB----------------------
2 tb Mild canned green chiles
-finely chopped
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
1 c  Bloody mary mix
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1 tb Prepared horseradish
1 tb Dijon mustard
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Makes about 1-1/3 cups
