Title: Vegetable-Beef Soup
Categories: Crockpot, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
2    Carrots sliced
1 pk Frozen vegetab1es,
3    Stalks celery with tops
2 md Onions, diced
2 md Potatoes diced
1 lb Beef shanks,
3 c  Water
1 ts Salt
4    Whole pepper corns
1 cn Tomatoes
3    Beef bouillon cubes
Put all ingredients in CROCK-POT. Cover and cook on Low 12 to 24 hours
(High: 4 to 6hours. *May be added during last 2 hours of cooking From
