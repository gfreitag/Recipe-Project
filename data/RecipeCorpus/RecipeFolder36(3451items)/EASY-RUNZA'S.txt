Title: EASY RUNZA'S
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk Lipton onion soup mix
1    Small head of cabbage
2    Tubes Crescents rolls
1 lb Hamburger
Shred and cook cabbage till transparent and tender. Spray cookie
sheet with oil and roll one tube of roll mix don't separate. Brown
hamburger and add dry soup mix, add cooked cabbage and spread on roll
mix that is on the cookie sheet top with second tube of roll mix.
Pinch edges together and bake according to directions on the roll
tube until golden brown. Cut into squares.
