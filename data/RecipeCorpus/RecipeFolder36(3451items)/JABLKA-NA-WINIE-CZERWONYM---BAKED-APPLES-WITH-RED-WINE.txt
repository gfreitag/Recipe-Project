Title: JABLKA NA WINIE CZERWONYM - BAKED APPLES WITH RED WINE
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Mace or nutmeg
1 c  Red wine
1/2 ts Vanilla
8    Apples, cored
Cherry or strawberry jam
1/2 c  Sugar
1. Place apples in a buttered casserole or baking dish.  Fill each
with jam.
2. Blend sugar and mace and stir in wine and vanilla. Pour over
apples and cover.
3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
4. Refrigerate for 2 to 4 hours before serving.
