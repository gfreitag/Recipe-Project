Title: RED AND GREEN COLESLAW
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Green cabbage; finely sliced
1/4 c  Thinly sliced red onion
1 c  Dried cranberries
2 c  Red cabbage; finely sliced
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Celery seeds
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
1/3 c  Oil
Combine cranberries, red and green cabbage and red onion in large
bowl. Toss with Dressing.  Cover and chill about 3 hours before
serving.  Stir and drain off all liquid before serving.  Makes 6
servings.
Each serving contains about: 173 calories; 8 mg sodium; 0
cholesterol; 13 grams fat; 17 grams carbohydrates; 1 gram protein;
0.69 gram fiber. DRESSING:
Combine vinegar, oil, sugar and celery seeds and mix well to
combine. Makes about 1 cup.
