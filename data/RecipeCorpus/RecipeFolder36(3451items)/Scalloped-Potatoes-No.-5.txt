Title: Scalloped Potatoes No. 5
Categories: Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
-dash of paprika
1/4 c  Milk
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 c  Onion, sliced
1 tb Margarine
1 cn Cream of celery or mushroom
-soup
1/8 ts Black pepper
4 c  Potatoes, sliced
1 c  Cheddar Cheese, shredded
Combine soup, milk, salt and pepper into a Pam sprayed 1 1/2 quart
shallow baking dish.  Arrange alternate layers of onion, potatoes,
cheese and soup mixture.  Dot with margarine.  Sprinkle with paprika.
Cover and bake for 1 hour at 375 degrees.  Uncover and bake another
`15 minutes until done.  Serves 4-6.
