Title: ATOLE
Categories: Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Water
3 c  Milk
1/2 c  Honey
1 c  Atole blue cornmeal
-(See NOTE)
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
Mix together cornmeal, cinnamon, salt and water in 3-quart saucepan.
Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Reduce heat to low. Stir in
milk and honey. Heat to simmering, stirring constantly (do not boil).
NOTE: Regular blue cornmeal can be substituted for the atole blue
cornmeal. Spread cornmeal in ungreased shallow baking pan. Bake, at
400F, stirring occasionally, until light brown, about 10 minutes.
