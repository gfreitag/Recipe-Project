Title: Asian Pesto Pasta
Categories: Herbs/spice, Main dish, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Finely chopped fresh basil
1/4 c  Finely chopped fresh
-cilantro
1/4 c  Finely chopped fresh mint
12 oz Linguine; cooked without
-salt or fat
1/2 c  Condensed chicken broth
-- undiluted
2 tb Reduced-sodium soy sauce
2 tb Fish sauce
1 tb Grated fresh ginger
1    Jalapeno; seeded and chopped
Combine first 8 ingredients; pour over linguine and toss gently.
Serve immediately.

Per serving: 234 calories (4% from fat), 0.9 g fat (0.1 g saturated),
0 mg cholesterol, 272 mg sodium, 46.7 g carbohydrate, 1.5 g fiber and
8 g protein.

From Adelyne Smith/Dunnville, KY in _Southern Living 1995 Annual
