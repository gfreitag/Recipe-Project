Title: CHEESE-NUT FUDGE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
- unsweetened
Chopped nuts
3 oz Cream cheese
2 c  Powdered sugar
1    Square, chocolate, melted
Mix well.  You might want to squeeze with your hands to get it mixed
well. Pat onto plate and cut into squares or roll into small balls.

Refrigerate
