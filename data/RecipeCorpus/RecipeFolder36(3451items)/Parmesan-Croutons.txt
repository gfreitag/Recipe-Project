Title: Parmesan Croutons
Categories: Breads, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 cups
2 ts Cheese, Parmesan; grated
4 sl Bread; 3/4" thick
1/4 c  Butter (or marg.); melted
Remove crust from bread, and cut bread into 3/4" cubes.  Brush or
drizzle with butter, tossing lightly to distribute evenly.  Sprinkle
with cheese, and toss again.

Spread cubes in a single layer on a generously buttered cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes or until dry and lightly browned.

NOTE: To make bread rounds to use as appetizers or with soups and
salads, cut bread slices with a 3" round cutter, brush with butter,
and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Toast as directed for croutons.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, October 1977.
