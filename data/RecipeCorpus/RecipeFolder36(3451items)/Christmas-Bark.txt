Title: Christmas Bark
Categories: Candies, Microwave
Yield: 18 servings
3/4 c  Red candied cherries, halved
3/4 c  Green candied cherries,
-halved
16 oz Vanilla-flavored candy
-coating, cut into pieces
1 c  Pecan halves
Microwave Directions:  Line cookie sheet with waxed paper.
Place candy coating in 2 quart microwave safe bowl.
Microwave on MEDIUM for 3 to 4 1/2 minutes, stirring once
halfway through melting.  Stir until smooth,  Add remaining
ingredients; toss to coat.  Spread mixture on waxed paper-
lined cookie sheet.  Let stand until set.  Break into
pieces.  Yield: 18 ounces
Source: Pillsbury, Family Christmas Cookbook
