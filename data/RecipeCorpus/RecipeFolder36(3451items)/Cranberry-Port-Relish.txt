Title: Cranberry Port Relish
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 1 recipe
1 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Port
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Ground cinnamon
3 c  (12-oz pkg) fresh or
-frozen cranberries
1 md Onion minced
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
1 c  Golden raisins
Combine all ingredients except cranberries in heavy
saucepan.  Bring to boil then simmer for 15 minutes.
Add cranberries and simmer another 20-30 minutes.
Towards the end the relish gets very thick.  Make sure
you stir it often, since it will stick to the pan.
