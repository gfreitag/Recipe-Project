Title: Sprouting Chick Peas
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chick peas
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1 cup in a jar, or tray sprout which will yield about 3 cups of
sprouts. Soak for 14 hours.

Rinse 3 to 4 times daily for 3 to 4 days.

Harvest when sprouts are 1/2 inch long. Use in casseroles, soups,
salads, steamed, or as a base for dips.
