Title: PINEAPPLE CHEESE BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Undrained crushed pineapple
1    Egg beaten
1 tb Veg. oil
1/2 c  Mild cheddar cheese grated
1/2 c  Walnuts optional
2 c  Flour
3/4 c  Sugar
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Soda
1 ts Salt
Blend and sift dry ingredients Combine pineapple, egg and oil and add
to dry mixture. Fold in cheese and walnuts. Pour into greased loaf
pan and let sit 10 min. Bake in 350 oven 45 - 50 min. or until tooth
pick comes out clean when tested with it.
