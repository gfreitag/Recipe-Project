Title: Dried Tomato & Rosemary Bread^
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads, Tomatoes
Yield: 10 servings
3 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
2 ts Granulated sugar
1/2 ts Dried rosemary, crushed
1/2 ts Paprika
1 1/2 ts Bread machine yeast
2/3 c  Water
2 tb Snipped dried tomatoes,
Unsalted, not packed in oil
1 tb Olive or vegetable oil
3/4 ts Salt
2 c  Bread flour
Make according to machine directions, but add the tomatoes with the
water.

Per serving: 106 cal., 4g pro., 19g carb., 2g (14%) fat, 1 mg chol.,
199mg sod., 0g fiber.

Woman's Day Holiday Baking 1995
