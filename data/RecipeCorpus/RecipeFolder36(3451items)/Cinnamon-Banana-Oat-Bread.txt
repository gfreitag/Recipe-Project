Title: Cinnamon Banana Oat Bread
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1 tb Cinnamon
3/4 c  Quick-cooking oatmeal
3/4 c  Whole-wheat flour
1 1/2 c  Unbleached white flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
1 c  Mashed banana
1    Egg
2 tb Vegetable oil
3 tb Molasses
1 ts Salt
Place all ingredients in the machine in the order
suggested by the manufacturer.  Program for white
bread or bread and press start. Makes 1 lb loaf.
