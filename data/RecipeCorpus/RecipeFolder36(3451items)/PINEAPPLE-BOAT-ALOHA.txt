Title: PINEAPPLE BOAT ALOHA
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 10 servings
2 c  Bananas, sliced
2    Pineapples
2 c  Strawberries, sliced
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/3 c  Lime juice
1 1/2 tb Rum
1 c  Oil
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Mustard
1 ts Salt
Cut Pineapples in half lengthwise.  Scoop out pulp, leaving shells
1/2 inch thick; set shells aside.
Cut pineapple pup into bite-size pieces, discarding core.  Add
strawberries and bananas; toss gently.  Spoon into pineapple shells;
spoon dressing over fruit.
Dressing: Combine first 5 ingredients in container of electric
blender; process on high speed 1 minute. Gradually add oil, and
continue to blend.
