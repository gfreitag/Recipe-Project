Title: Sprouting Fenugreek Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Fenugreek seeds
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1/4 cup in a quart jar, or tray sprout, which will yield about 1
quart of sprouts. Soak for 10 hours.

Rinse 2 to 3 times daily for 3 to 5 days. Mist with water if tray
sprouting to keep damp.

Harvest when 1/2 to 2 inches long. Use in salads and sandwiches.
