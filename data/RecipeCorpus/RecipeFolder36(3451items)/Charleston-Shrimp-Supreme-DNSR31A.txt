Title: Charleston Shrimp Supreme DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
4 tb Butter
4 tb Flour
8 tb Tomato catsup
1 1/2 c  Milk
4 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 lb Shrimp
1 ea Bay leaf
1 ds Red pepper
1/2 ea Juice of 1 lemon
1 x  Salt and pepper to taste
Cream butter and flour.  Add milk and make a thick
cream sauce.  Add Worcestershire sauce, catsup, bay
leaf, red pepper and lemon juice.  Salt and pepper to
taste.  Add cooked shrimp and heat until very hot.
Serve with rice.  "Charleston Receipts" formatted by
Mary Bowles.
