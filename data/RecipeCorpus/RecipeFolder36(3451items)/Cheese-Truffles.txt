Title: Cheese Truffles
Categories: Holiday, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
(Maclaren's)
1/2 c  Butter, softened
2 tb Brandy
1 pk (250 gram) cream cheese,
Softened
1 pk (250 gram) Imperial cold
Pack cheddar cheese
toasted almonds, chopped very fine.

Using food processor, on pulse, blend until smooth.  Remove from
processor. Working quickly, shape into 1 inch balls.  Chill for at
least 3 hours. Just before serving, roll in chopped almonds to coat.
Makes about 60 balls.

Origin: Adapted from What's Cooking, 14th issue. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Nov/95.
