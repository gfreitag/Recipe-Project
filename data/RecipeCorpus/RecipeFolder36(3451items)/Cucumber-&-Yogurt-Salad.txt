Title: Cucumber & Yogurt Salad
Categories: Salads, Middle east
Yield: 6 servings
2 ea Cloves garlic
1 ts Mint, dried
2 ea Cucumbers, peeled
3 c  Yogurt, plain
Finely dice cucumbers into small bowl.  Mince or press garlic, add to
cucumbers.  Add yogurt and dried mint.  Mix thoroughly.  Allow to sit
for at least 30 minutes or refrigerate overnight for flavors to
blend.  Serve with bread, meats, or rice.

Barghouti family recipe.
