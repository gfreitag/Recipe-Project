Title: NEW-MEXICO CHILE SALVE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------FOR BEEF, POULTRY, & PORK----------------------
Pinch of coriander
2 tb -=OR=- 3 Tbsp. vegetable oil
3 tb Mild ground chiles,
-=OR=- chili powder
1 ts Hungarian hot paprika
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mash until smooth.
Makes anout 1/3 cup
