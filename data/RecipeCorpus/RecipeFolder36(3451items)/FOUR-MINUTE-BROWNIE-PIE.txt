Title: FOUR-MINUTE BROWNIE PIE
Categories: Pie/tart, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
4 tb Unsweetened cocoa powder
1 ts Vanilla
1 ds Salt
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
Ice cream
2    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
- softened
1/2 c  Flour
Place eggs, sugar, butter, flour, cocoa, vanilla and salt in small
mixer bowl. Beat 4 minutes. Stir in nuts and pour into greased 8-inch
pie pan. Bake at 325F 30 minutes, or until done. Pie will settle like
meringue when cool. Cut in wedges and serve with ice cream.
