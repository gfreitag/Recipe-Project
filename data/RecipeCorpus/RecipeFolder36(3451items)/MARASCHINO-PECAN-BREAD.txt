Title: MARASCHINO PECAN BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------LISA CRAWLEY  TSPN00B------------------------
1/4 c  Red and green Maraschino
-cherries, whole
1/4 c  Sunflower seeds
1/4 c  Pecans; chopped
1/8 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Ground ginger
3 ts Yeast
1 1/8 c  Water
3 c  Flour
1 1/2 tb Nonfat dry milk
2 tb Sugar
1 1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
1/4 c  Raisins
Ingred. are to be added according to manufacturer's suggestion. Drain
cherries well on a paper towel; add whole for best appearance. Add the
fruit and nuts at the beep at the end of the first kneading cycle.
Taken From: McCall's Holiday Cookbook/Electric Bread recipe.
