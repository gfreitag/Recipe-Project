Title: Chili Lime Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Caribbean
Yield: 1 cup
(abt 6 limes)
1 tb Salt
1    (to 3) Scotch bonnets
Or other hot chilies
1 c  Lime juice
Stem, wash, seed, and thinly slice the peppers. Combine the lime
juice and salt in a mixing bowl and whisk until the salt is
completely dissolved. Stir in the peppers.

Transfer the sauce to a clean jar or bottle. Let the mixture stand at
room temperature for at least 24 hours, preferably 5 days to a week.

Spoon Chili Lime Sauce over grilled vegetables, chicken, or seafood.
Add a few drops to soups and stews. It's also great in salad
dressing. Keeps for several months at room temperature.
