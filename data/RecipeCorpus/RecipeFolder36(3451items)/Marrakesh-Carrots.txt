Title: Marrakesh Carrots
Categories: Indian, Vegetables, Side dish, Masterchefs
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Finely chopped preserved
-ginger
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
500 g  Whole baby carrots, washed
1 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Olive oil
1 ts Salt
The seasoning used on these carrots would also be delicious with
broccoli, asparagus or beans.

Place the carrots in a pot of cold water over a medium heat and bring
to a boil. Cook until soft but not mushy, for about 10 minutes. Drain
and cool under cold running water.

Meanwhile, place the lemon juice in a medium bowl. Whisk in the olive
oil to make a vinaigrette. Whisk in the ginger, salt, cinnamon and
nut- meg. Add carrots to the vinaigrette and toss. Season with
additional salt or spices to taste. Serves 6.
