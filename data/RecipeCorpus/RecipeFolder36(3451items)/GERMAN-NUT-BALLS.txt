Title: GERMAN NUT BALLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Nuts, fine chopped
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Confectioners sugar
2 1/4 c  Flour, sifted
Combine all ingredients; shape into balls.  Bake on cookie sheet 10
to 12 minutes at 375 F.  While hot, roll in Confectioners sugar.

< Guidepost Family Christmas Book 1980 >

May be stored for a long period of time in airtight tins.
This would make a great Christmas gift.
