Title: My Jambalaya
Categories: Main dish, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Tobasco
1 ts Garlic powder
8 oz Can tomato sauce
2 c  Converted instant brown
-rice
3 c  Vegetable stock
1/4 c  Chopped scallions
2 tb Parsely
1 1/2 c  Onions; finely chopped
1 1/2 c  Green peppers; fine chopped
1 1/2 c  Celery; finely chopped
2    Bay leaves
1 ts Salt; or to taste
1/2 ts Thyme
1 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
Sautee onions, peppers and celery in 1T oil.  Sautee until lightly
browned.  Add seasonings and continue cooking for 5 mins, stirring
frequently.

Add tomato sauce and rice, and mix well.  Cook 5 mins.

Add stock and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover pot, and simmer 20
mins.
Add scallions, and parsley and cook 5 mins.

My note:  I at least double the cayenne and tobasco, as well as garlic
powder.
This stuff is awesome!
