Title: HFS2- Cornish Fairings - BA 2/83
Categories: Desserts, British isl, Bon appetit
Yield: 2 dozen
1/2 c  (1 stick) unsalted butter;
-cut into pieces, room
-temperature
1 ts Orange peel; grated
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  English golden syrup -OR-
1/3 c  Light molasses
1 c  Unskinned almonds; coarsely
-chopped
1 1/2 c  Plus 2 tbsp all purpose
-flour
2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg; freshly grated
1/2 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Ginger
Sift together flour, soda, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves,
allspice, and ginger.  Transfer to processor.  Add butter and orange
peel and blend using on/off turns until mixture resembles coarse
meal. Mix in sugar.  Add syrup and 1/2 cup chopped almonds and mix
using on/off turns until dough just gathers into balls.
Preheat oven to 400F.  Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
Place remaining 1/2 cup almonds in small bowl.  Working quickly to
prevent oversoftening, roll dough into walnut-sized balls.  Press one
side of each ball into almonds, flattening slightly.  Arrange on
prepared baking sheet nut side up, spacing evenly.  Bake until golden
brown, 9 to 10 minutes. Cool 4 to 5 minutes.  Transfer to rack using
spatula. Cool completely before serving.  Store in airtight container.

"Hearty Farmhouse Suppers" (England) "Bon Appetit"  February, 1983.
