Title: Diabetic Mock-Turtles
Categories: Diabetic, Candies
Yield: 18 servings
1 tb Granulated sugar replacement
1/2 ts Maple flavoring
1 oz Diabetic chocolate coating
18 ea Pecan halves
1/2 c  Cold water
1 pk Unflavored gelatin
1/2 c  Skim milk
place 1 pecan half in paper cupcake holder,inside muffin tin.Dissolve
gelatin in water for 5 minutes.Cook and stir over medium heat till it
comes to a full boil.Boil 2 minutes.Remove from heat,stir in all but
the chocolate.blend well.set aside until thick,syrup
consistency.spoon 2 tsp.over each pecan half.chill till firm.melt
chocolate in double-boiler and divide evenly in each cup.store in
refrigerator. yield:18 turtles exchange:1 turtle = 1/5 bread
calories:1 turtle = 16 calories
