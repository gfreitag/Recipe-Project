Title: Cornish Pasties^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Pastry, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
Dissolved in
1/4 c  Water
1 c  Diced peeled potatoes
1/2 c  Diced carrots
2 tb Finely chopped onion
2    Pie crusts
1/2 lb Ground beef, browned and
Drained
2 tb Flour
1 ts Seasoned salt
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1 ts Instant beef broth granules
Combine beef, flour, salt, and parsley; stir until well coated. Add
vegetables, cook until vegetables are crisp tender. Roll each pie
crust into a 12" square; divide into 4-6" squares. Place about 1/3
cup meat into each. Fold into triangle and seal with e
