Title: Candied Pecans (Mike Pew))
Categories: Nuts/grains, Candies, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Brown sugar
2 c  Pecan halves
1 ea Egg white
1/2 ts Vanilla
Preheat oven to 250 degrees.  Beat egg white until it stands in
soft peaks.  Gradually mix in brown sugar and vanilla.  Fold in
pecans.  Place pecan halves, well coated in egg-sugar mix, on
greased cookie sheet about 1 inch apart.  Bake 30 mins.  Turn off
oven and let set for 30 mins.  Store in airtight container.
Freezes well.
