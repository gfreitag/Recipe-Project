Title: HEARTY ALPHABET BEEF SOUP
Categories: Crockpot, Soup/stew
Yield: 2 servings
1 pk Onion soup mix
1 pk Frozen mixed vegetables;
-partially thawed
1/2 c  Alhabet noodles;uncooked
1/2 lb Stew beef
-or round steak
1    Stewed tomatoes 1 pound can
1 cn Tomato sauce, 8 oz can
1 c  Water
Using the beef stew meat which is already cut up, place the meat,
combined with the stewed tomatoes, tomato sauce, water and soup mix
into crockpot. Cover and cook on LOW for 6 to 8 hours. Turn to HIGH,
add vegetables that have been partially thawed, and nookles. Cover
and cook on HIGH for 30 minutes or until vegetables are done.
