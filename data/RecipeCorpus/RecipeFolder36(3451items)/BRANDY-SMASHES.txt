Title: BRANDY SMASHES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 30 servings
1 cn Frozen orange juice
1/2 pt Apricot brandy
4 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
1 cn Frozen lemonade
Bring water to boil, stir in sugar and return to boil.  Set off
burner and wait until it quits steaming.  Add juices and brandy and
freeze.  Form into balls with melon baller and return to freezer.
Remove from freezer immediately prior to serving.
