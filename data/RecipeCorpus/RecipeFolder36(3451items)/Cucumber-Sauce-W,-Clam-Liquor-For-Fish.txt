Title: Cucumber Sauce W/ Clam Liquor For Fish
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 cups
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Cream
1/4 c  Cucumber; chopped
3 tb Butter
3 tb Flour
1/4 c  Bottled clam juice
Melt Butter and add the flour. Cook, stirring with a wire whisk, 2 or
3 min. without letting the flour brown. meanwhile bring the clam
juice and milk to a boil and add all at once to the flour-butter
mixture whisking vigorously until the mixture is smooth and has
thickened. Cook over low heat for 10 min.

Just before serving stir in the cream and cucumber. Serve hot with
poached fish.

From The New York Times Cook Book,
