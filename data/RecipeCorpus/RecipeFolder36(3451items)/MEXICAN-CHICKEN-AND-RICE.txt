Title: MEXICAN CHICKEN AND RICE
Categories: Poultry, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
-10 oz package
1 c  Chicken broth
1 c  Mild salsa
1 1/2 c  Minute original instant rice
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 tb Oil
1 lb Chicken breasts, cubed
-boneless, skinless
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 sm Green pepper, chopped
1 pk Frozen sweet corn, thawed
Heat oil in large skillet on medium-high heat.  Add chicken, onion and
pepper; cook and stir until chicken is cooked through.  Add corn,
broth and salsa; bring to boil.
Stir in rice; cover.  Remove from heat.  Let stand 5 minutes.  Fluff
with fork.  Sprinkle with cheese; cover.  Let stand 2 minutes, or
until cheese melts.  25 minutes; makes 4 servings.
