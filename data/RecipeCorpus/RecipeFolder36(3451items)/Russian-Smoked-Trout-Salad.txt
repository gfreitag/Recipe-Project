Title: Russian Smoked Trout Salad
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Bbq/grill, Salads, Russian
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Celery salt
2 tb Parsley; chopped
Lettuce leaves
1 lb Hot smoked lake trout
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 tb Green onions; chopped
Fresh ground pepper
Break salmon into bite size pieces and add sour cream, onion, parsley
and celery salt. Season with pepper. Serve in lettuce cups.

From: Smoking Salmon & Trout by Jack Whelan. Published by: Airie
