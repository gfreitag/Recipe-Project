Title: TENNESSEE BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1    Onions, chopped
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Celery seed
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 c  Water
Combine ingredients in small saucepan and bring to boil.  Simmer until
reduced to thick sauce, stirring occasionally.
