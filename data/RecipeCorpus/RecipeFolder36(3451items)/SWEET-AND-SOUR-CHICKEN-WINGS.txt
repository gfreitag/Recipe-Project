Title: SWEET AND SOUR CHICKEN WINGS
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
24    Whole chicken wings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Preheat oven to 350 F. Season wings with salt, pepper and garlic
powder. PLace in a shallow baking dish and bake for 15 minutes. in a
small saucepan, combine ketchup, vinegar, sugar and water. Heat,
stirring until well blended. Brush sauce over wings and continue
baking for 20 to 30 minutes, or until wings are tender. Remove from
oven, and serve. Source: Creative Cooking-Poultry
by Marian Hoffman
