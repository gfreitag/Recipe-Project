Title: THE COACH HOUSE WINE MARINADE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------POULTRY, FISH, SHELLFISH-----------------------
2 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 tb Dijon mustard
10    Peppercorns; crushed
1 c  Dry white wine
3    Large shallots; minced
2    Large garlic cloves; minced
2 tb Olive oil
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Makes about 1-1/2 cups
