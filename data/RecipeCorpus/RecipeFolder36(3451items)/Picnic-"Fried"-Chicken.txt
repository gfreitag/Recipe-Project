Title: Picnic "Fried" Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Ground cumin
1 pk (3 lb) fresh skinless
*chicken parts
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
3 tb Canola oil
3 c  Cornflakes, crushed
1/2 ts Ground red pepper
1 c  Plain low-fat yogurt
1 tb Lemon juice
1. Preheat oven to 400F. With oil, grease shallow baking pan large
enough to hold chicken without crowding; place in oven to heat.

2. On large dinner plate, spread crushed cornflakes and ground red
pepper. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice and cumin.
Beginning with large pieces, generously coat chicken with yogurt
mixture and roll in cornflakes.

3. Place chicken pieces, meat side up, in prepared baking pan;
sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake 30 to 40 minutes without turning,
or until coating is crisp and chicken is cooked through. (Check
breasts for doneness after 30 minutes.) Serve warm or cold.
