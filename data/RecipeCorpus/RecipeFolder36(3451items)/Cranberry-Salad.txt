Title: Cranberry Salad
Categories: Salads, Guestchefs
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Lemon Juice
2 tb Knox Gelatin Soaked In
1/2 c  Cold Water, Then Dissolved
Over Hot Water
16 oz Cranberry Sauce (Not Jelly)
6 oz Ginger Ale
1/2 c  Fine Chopped Celery
1/2 c  Finely Chopped Apples
1/2 c  Broken Nutmeats
Mix all ingredients together and pour into  a mold.  Place in
refrigerator and chill until set.  Serve with Miracle Whip or
mayonnaise.
