Title: COTTAGE CHEESE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 5 servings
1 lg Box Raspberry jello
1 lg Container cottage cheese
1 lg Can crushed pineapple
8 oz Cool whip
Drain pineapple well.  In bowl combine cottage cheese, dry jello, cool
whip, and drained pineapple.  Mix well.  Refrigerate and serve.
