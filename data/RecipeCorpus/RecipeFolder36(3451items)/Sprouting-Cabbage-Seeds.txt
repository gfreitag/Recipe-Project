Title: Sprouting Cabbage Seeds
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar
3 tb Cabbage seeds
Use 3 tablespoons seeds in a one quart jar, which will yield about 1
quart of sprouts. Soak for 10 hours.

Rinse 2 to 3 times daily for 3 to 5 days. Move into sunlight to green.

Harvest when sprouts are 1 to 1-1/2 inches long. Use in salads and
sandwiches.
