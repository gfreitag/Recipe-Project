Title: PIZZA MEXICANA
Categories: Pizza, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
1 cn Sliced black olives; drained
-2-1/4 oz. can
12    Nacho sliced Jalapeno pepper
-drained
1/4 c  Sliced tomato
Chopped cilantro
1 cn Vegetarian Refried Beans
-Rosarita brand (16oz can)
1    Pre-baked pizza/bread crust
-12-inch
1 c  Rosarita Picante Sauce Mild
1/2 c  Monterey Jack cheese
-shredded
Evenly spread beans over top of pizza/bread crust.  Top with remaining
ingredients in order given, EXECPT tomato and cilantro.  Bake at
450-degrees F for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Top with
tomatoes and cilantro.  Makes 4 servings.  Additional toppings: sour
cream, diced green onions, guacamole and Rosarita salsa.
