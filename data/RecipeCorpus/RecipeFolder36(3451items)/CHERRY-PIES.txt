Title: CHERRY PIES
Categories: Snacks, Pie/tart
Yield: 2 servings
1    Package, prepared pie crust
1/2 c  Cherry pie filling
Unfold one pie crust and form dough into a 10x10" square.  Cut in
half and place one piece into sandwich maker.  Place 2 tablespoons of
mixture in each triangular pocket.  Top with the other half of the
pie crust.  Lower lid, latch and bake 5-7 minutes or until done.
Drizzle with icing. Variations:  Peach, apricot, apple, blueberry,
strawberry, or minced meat pie filling.
