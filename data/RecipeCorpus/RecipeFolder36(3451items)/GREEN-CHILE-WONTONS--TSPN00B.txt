Title: GREEN CHILE WONTONS  TSPN00B
Categories: Appetizers, Tex/mexican, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 72 servings
1 lb Frozen wonton skins;thawed
1    Peanut oil
Salt
4 c  Monterey Jack cheese;shred
2/3 c  Green chiles; diced
1/3 c  Jalapeno peppers
Combine cheese, chiles, and pepper; stir well. Working with one
wonton skin at a time, spoon about 1 1/2 tsp. cheese mixture in
center of each wonton. Moisten edges of wonton lightly with water.
Fold wonton in half to form a triangle, pressing edges together to
seal. Repeat procedure with remaining wonton skins and cheese
mixture. Chill 20 min. Fry wontons in a small amount of hot oil (375)
until golden brown, turning once. Drain on paper towels. Sprinkle
with salt. Serve with avacado dip. Yield: 6 1/2 doz. LISA CRAWELY
