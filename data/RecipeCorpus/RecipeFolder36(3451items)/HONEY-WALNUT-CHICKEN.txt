Title: HONEY WALNUT CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Ground walnuts
1 x  Salt to taste
1 x  Pepper to taste
2 lb Boneless chicken breasts
1 c  HONEY
2 c  Bread crumbs
Roll chicken breasts in HONEY and then into mixture of walnuts and
bread crumbs.  Season with salt and pepper.  Place in refrigerator for
1 hour.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45-50 minutes.
From Connecticut Honey by Eastern Connecticut Beekeepers Association
