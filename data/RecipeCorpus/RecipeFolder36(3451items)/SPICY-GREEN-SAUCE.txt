Title: SPICY GREEN SAUCE
Categories: Condiments, Vegetables, Salsa/sauce, Dips/spread, Low cal/fat
Yield: 3 cups
1 1/2 c  Tomatillos, peel & chop
1 ts Cumin
2 tb Jalapenoes, chopped
1 tb White vinegar
1/4 c  Cilantro leaves, chopped
1/4 c  Vegetable stock
1 ts Olive oil
1 c  Onion, chopped
1/3 c  Green bell pepper, chopped
1 ts Garlic, minced
Heat the stock in a large & heavy skillet over medium-high heat.
Saute onion, bell pepper & garlic until soft.  Add tomatillos & cumin
& saute for 10 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients & saute a further
3 minutes. Serve warm.
