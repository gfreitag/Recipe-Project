Title: Broiled Pork Chops In Black Bean Sauce
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Hot/spicy
Yield: 1 servings
100 g  Pork chop with bone
20 g  Black bean mashed to form a
-sauce
:
30 g  Steamed rice
30 g  Vegetables

Season pork chop and broil.

Prepare black bean sauce with sauteed shallot, stock and reduce to
consistency.

Serve the sauce below pork, accompanied with steamed rice.
