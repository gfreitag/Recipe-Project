Title: CATSKILL MOUNTAIN JALAPENO FRENCH BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 12 servings
-("Better for Bread")
2 ts Salt
2 ts Sugar
2 tb Sesame seeds; or more
2 ts Active dry yeast
-(Red Star is best)
-DONNA CELEIRO   (DGSK75A)
7 oz Water
1 tb Butter/margarine
1/2 c  Jalapeno cheese; shredded
1/4 c  Jalapeno peppers; chopped
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
I use "El Rio Jalapeno strips" for this bread.

Use French/crusty mode; otherwise; standard/white bread mode.
