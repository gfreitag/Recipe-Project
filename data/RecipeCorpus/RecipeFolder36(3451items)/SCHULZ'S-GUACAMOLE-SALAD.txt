Title: SCHULZ'S GUACAMOLE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Scallion; grated
8    Tomato, cherry; halved
1 c  Chili Cheese Fritos; crushed
1    Iceberg lettuce, head
1/4 c  Cheddar; grated
1/4 c  Monterey Jack; grated
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/3 c  Oil
1 tb Picante sauce
1    Avocado; peel/pit/mashed
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sour cream
Tear lettuce into small pieces and combine with cheeses, scallions,
and tomatoes.  Cover and refrigerate in salad bowl until serving time.
Combine all ingredients for dressing and mix well.  Toss salad with
dressing and sprinkle top with crushed chips.
