Title: Wilted Lettuce Salad (Vegan)
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 servings
-and basil
1    Head Boston lettuce
Favorite vinegar
1    Onion
1    Bell pepper
1    Potato
Fresh chives, rosemary
Slice the potato, the onion and the pepper.  Put them in the oven and
bake at 400 about 20 mins or until done.  Meanwhile, clean and tear
the lettuce and put on a plate.  Sprinkle the lettuce lightly with
your favorite vinegar.

When the veggies are done, spray lightly with Pam then sprinkle with
salt and herbs.  Stir to mix.  Dump warm veggies over lettuce and
sprinkle with vinegar.
