Title: Baked Crabmeat DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
6 ea French bread sliced 1" thick
1/4 c  Milk or enough to soak bread
1 ea Egg, well beaten
1 x  Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1/2 c  Butter
1 ea Onion, large, finely chopped
1 ea Celery rib, finely chopped
1 lb Crabmeat
1 ts Parsley, chopped
2 ea Garlic cloves, finely minced
In a skillet, saute onions and celery in butter until
clear and tender. Add crabmeat, parsley, and garlic.
Stir fry about 10 minutes.  Add French bread and egg.
Salt and pepper to taste.  Mix well.  Place in a
buttered baking dish.  Cover with bread crumbs.  Bake
at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes.  From "Jambalaya,
Crawfish Pie, File Gumbo"  formatted by Mary Dishongh
Bowles.
