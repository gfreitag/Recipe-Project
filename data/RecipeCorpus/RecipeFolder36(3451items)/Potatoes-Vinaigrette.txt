Title: Potatoes Vinaigrette
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1    Stalk celery -- minced
1/2 md Onion -- chopped
1/2 ts Dried rosemary -- minced
Salt and pepper -- to taste
1 lb New potatoes -- with skin
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1/3 c  Olive oil
In a large pot of boiling, salted water boil potatoes until tender,
about 20 minutes.  Drain and set aside; cut them into bite-size
chunks, if they are large.  In a medium bowl, beat mustard and
vinegar together.  Using a whisk or fork. beat in oil until smooth.
Add celery, onion and rosemary. Add potatoes; toss to coat with
dressing.  Season with salt and pepper.
