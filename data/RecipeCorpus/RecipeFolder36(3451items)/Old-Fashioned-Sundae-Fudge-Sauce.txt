Title: Old Fashioned Sundae Fudge Sauce
Categories: Chocolate, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 /3
2 tb Liqueur (orange, almond or
-coffee)
OR 1 tbsp vanilla
2 tb Butter
4 oz Semi sweet chocolate,
-chopped
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Sugar
Melt chocolate with water over low heat.  Add sugar and bring to boil
over medium heat for about 2 minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove
from heat and add liqueur and butter.  Store in fridge.  Makes about
2/3 cup.

Origin: Baker's Festive Recipe pamphlet.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Dec/95.
