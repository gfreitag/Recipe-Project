Title: Citrus Collins
Categories: Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Lemon juice
1 oz Simple syrup
Ice cubes
2 oz Orange juice
Fill a 10-12 oz. glass with all ingredients.

Fill remainder with club soda.  Garnish with an orange slice or a
cherry.

Source:  AAA of Washington "Motorist"
