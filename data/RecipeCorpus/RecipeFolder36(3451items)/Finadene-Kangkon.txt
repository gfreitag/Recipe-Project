Title: Finadene Kangkon
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Guam
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 ea Hot pepper (if more is
-desired add 2)
4 tb Lemon juice
Salt and pepper to taste
1 lb Kangkon leaves, parboiled
-and cut into 2 inch pieces
-(similar to
Spinach)
1 c  Cherry tomatoes
Prepare finadene sauce with tomatoes and pour over cooked leaves.
Serves 4.
From: Real Guamanian Recipes By Way Of Dorothy's Kitchen
By: Dorothy Horn
