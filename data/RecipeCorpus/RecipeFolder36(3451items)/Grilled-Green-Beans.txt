Title: Grilled Green Beans
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
Horseradish
1 ts Salt
1 tb Brown sugar
1/8 ts Pepper
1 lb Can of green beans
1 sm Onion, chopped
2    Tomatoes, sliced
1/4 c  Oleo
3 ts Prepared mustard with
Drain green beans and place on large square of heavy duty foil.  Put
chopped onions and tomato slices over beans.  Mix together remaining
ingredients until well blended.  Spoon over beans and fold foil
tightly. Cook 30 to 35 minutes over medium hot coals.
