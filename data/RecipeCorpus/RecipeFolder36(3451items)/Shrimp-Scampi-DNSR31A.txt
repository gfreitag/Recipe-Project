Title: Shrimp Scampi DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Oil
1 lb Shrimp, large, peel & devein
1 x  Rice, cooked, hot
1 ea Garlic clove, crushed
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dry Mustard
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Parsley, chopped
Combine garlic, salt, mustard, pepper, parsley, lemon
juice, and oil for marinade.  Add shrimp to marinade
and marinate for 2 hours.  Broil, brushing with
marinade 4 inches from heat until done.  Serve with
rice. From  "A Taste of Indiana"  formatted by Mary
Dishongh Bowles.
