Title: SOOTHING CORDELIA
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Dried peaches
3/4 c  Water
1 tb Lemon juice
3 ea Dried pineapple rings
1/4 c  Dates, pitted
1/4 c  Dried apricots
Put all the dried fruit in a bowl.  Cover with lemon juice & stir to
mix. Let stand for one hour.  Then transfer the fruit & its liquid to
a blender.  Blend at medium speed until thick & creamy, about 2
minutes. Pour into small custard cups.  Refrigerate until ready to
serve.
If sotring for longer than 24 hours, cover tightly & keep in the
refrigerator for 5 to 7 days.
