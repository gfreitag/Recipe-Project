Title: Hot Cranberry Punch
Categories: Crockpot, Beverages
Yield: 10 servings
MMMMM------------------RIVAL CROCK-POT COOKING-----------------------
1 c  Water
1 ts Whole cloves
1    Cinnamon stick
4 c  Unsweetened pineapple juice
4 c  Cranberry juice
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
Tie cloves and cinnamon stick in cheesecloth. Combine all
ingredients in crock-Pot. Cover and cook on Low setting for 4
to 10 hours. Serve hot, in punch cups.
