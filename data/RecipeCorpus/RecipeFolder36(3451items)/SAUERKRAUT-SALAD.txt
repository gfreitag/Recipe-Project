Title: SAUERKRAUT SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Green pepper in thin strips
1/2 c  Carrot, shredded
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
1 lb Sauerkraut
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Celery, thin sliced
Cut kraut strands in shorter pieces with scissors. Stir in sugar and
let stand for 30 minutes.

Add remaining ingredients, cover bowl tightly, and chill in
refrigerator at least 12 hours.  Serve on lettuce.  8 1/2 cup servings

Great salad for picnics...
