Title: Cranberry Horseradish Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 recipe
1/3 c  Prepared Horseradish
2 c  Cranberries, finely chopped
1/4 c  Sugar
In a small saucepan over medium heat, cook cranberries
and sugar, stirring constantly until mixture is fully
blended, about 2 mins.  Mixture does not need to cook.
