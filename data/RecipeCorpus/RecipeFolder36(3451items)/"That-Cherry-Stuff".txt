Title: "That Cherry Stuff"
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
(14 ounces)
1 cn Crushed Pineapple, drained,
(8 ounces)
1 c  Nuts, coarsely chopped
(any kind)
1 cn Cherry Pie Filling,
(21 ounces)
1 ct Frozen Whipped Topping,
(8 ounces), thawed
1 cn Sweetened Condensed Milk,
In a large bowl, fold together all the ingredients.
Spoon into a serving bowl or individual dessert
glasses.  Chill before serving

Source: Mr. Food Cooks Real American
by Art Ginsburg
ISBN 0-688-12637-5
