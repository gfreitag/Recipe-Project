Title: CHIFFON ICE BOX DESSERT
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Boiling water
1 qt Vanilla ice cream
12    Ladyfingers, split
2 pk (4 serving size) OR 1 pkg
(8 serving size) gelatin
Black Raspberry, Orange or
Black Cherry
Completely dissolve gelatin in boiling water.  Add ice cream by
spoonfulls, stirring until completely melted.  Chill until thickened
but still spoonable (Not completely set)

Meanwhile, trim off about 1 inch of the ladyfingers and place cut
ends down around side of 8 inch springform pan.  Have rounded sides
of ladyfingers facing outside of pan.  Spoon thickened gelatin
mixture into pan.  Chill until firm, about 3 hours.  Remove side of
pan.

Garnish with whipped topping, fresh fruit and mint leaves if desired.

Source:  Jello Company Submitted by Gail/Dale Shipp
