Title: Kid's Fish Taco's
Categories: Kids, Fish/seafoo, Tomatoes, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
Cheddar cheese, grated
Sour cream
Taco shells
Frozen breaded fish sticks
Taco sauce
Lettuce
Tomato, diced
Cook the fish sticks according to package instructions.  When cooked,
place one fish stick in each taco.  Add the various toppings and serve
immediately.

Origin: Adapted from Jean Pare's Kid's Cooking. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Nov/95.
