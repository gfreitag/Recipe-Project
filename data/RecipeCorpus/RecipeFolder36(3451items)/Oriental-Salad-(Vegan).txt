Title: Oriental Salad (Vegan)
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
2    Carrots, shredded or
-julienned
1/2 c  Green onions, diced
Any other veggies you'd
-like, such as red bell
-pepper
1 c  Sliced or shredded cabbage
1/2 c  Snow peas
1    Can chinese baby corn,
-drained and rinsed
1    Can water chestnuts,
-drained and rinsed
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1 ts Chinese chili paste or
-some hot pepper flakes
1/4 c  Mirin (sweet rice wine)
2 tb Soy sauce
Add sauce to veggies and let marinate for about an hour.  Serve with
pitas or rice.
