Title: Sprouting Cress Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Cress seeds
1 ea Prepared clay saucer -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1 tablespoon of seeds in a clay saucer or tray, which will yield
about 1-1/2 cups of sprouts. There is no need to soak or rinse and
drain; just mist with water 3 times daily for 3 to 5 days. Move into
sunlight to green.

Harvest when sprouts are 1 to 1-1/2 inches long. Use as a spice (very
peppery flavor), in salads, sandwiches, or baked goods.
