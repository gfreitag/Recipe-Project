Title: Sprouting Turnip Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar
3 tb Turnip seeds
Use 3 tablespoons in a quart jar which will yield about 1 quart of
sprouts. Soak for 12 hours.

Rinse twice daily for 3 to 4 days. Move into sunlight to green.

Harvest when sprouts are 1 to 1-1/2 inch long. Use in salads and
sandwiches.
