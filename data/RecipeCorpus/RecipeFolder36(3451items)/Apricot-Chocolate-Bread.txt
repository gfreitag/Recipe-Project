Title: Apricot Chocolate Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 loaf
2 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Cocoa
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
9 oz Cut up dried apricots
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
1/3 c  Veg. oil
2    Eggs
Mix first 5 ingredients in a large bowl. Stir in the
rest until just moistened. Pour into greased loaf pan
and bake for 50-60 mins in a pre-heated 350 degree
oven. Cool for 10 mins. and remove from pan.
