Title: French Meat Pie^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Allspice
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Double crust pie shell
1    Egg, beaten, optional
2 tb Oil
1 lg Onion, thinly sliced
1 lb Ground beef
1 lb Ground pork
1 c  Mashed potatoes
1 cn (8oz) veg-all, drained
Saute onion in oil until tender. Remove from pan and set aside. Brown
beef and pork; drain. Combine meat, onion and seasonings; mix well.
Pour into pastry lined pie plate; cover with top crust, fluting edges
to seal. Make steam vents, brush with egg if de

Bakae at 375F. unti crust is golden, 30-35 minutes.
