Title: Shrimp Remoulade DNSR31A
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
5 tb Parsley, chopped
3/4 c  Creole mustard
2 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 c  Paprika
2 lb Shrimp, cooked and peeled
1 c  Olive oil (or salad oil)
1/2 c  Tarragon vinegar
1 1/4 c  Celery, chopped
2 1/2 ts Green pepper, chopped
1 tb Onion, chopped
Mix all ingredients and let marinate in refrigerator
for twenty-four hours, stirring often.  Use as salad,
or on toast or crackers. "Charleston Receipts"
formatted by Mary Bowles.
