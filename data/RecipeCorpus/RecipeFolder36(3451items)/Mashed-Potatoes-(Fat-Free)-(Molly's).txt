Title: Mashed Potatoes (Fat-Free) (Molly's)
Categories: Side dish, Potatoes, Fatfree
Yield: 4 cups
-natural butter flavor
-sprinkles
6 md Potatoes (2 lbs.)
1/2 c  Skim milk
3 tb + 1 tsp. Molly McButter
Peel and cut potatoes into quarters.  Boil until tender (20-25
minutes). Drain and mash.  Add milk to desired consistency.  Mix in
Molly McButter. Makes 4 cups (8 servings).  Garnish as desired.

For variety, try:

Garlic Mashed Potatoes:  substitute Molly McButter Garlic & Herb for
Molly McButter Natural Butter Flavor Sprinkles.

Mashed Potatoes Dijon:  add 2-1/2 tsp. Dijon mustard to original
recipe.

Per Serving:  120 cal; 0g fat; 0mg chol; 180-250mg sod; 27g carb; 3g
pro.

Source:  Molly McButter advertisement in Sunday coupon circular.
