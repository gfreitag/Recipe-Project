Title: Mascarpone Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 cups
1 tb Honey
2 c  Mascarpone cheese
2 tb Wasabi powder
Combine all of the ingredients in  blender set on medium-high for 1
min. Serve at room temperature.
