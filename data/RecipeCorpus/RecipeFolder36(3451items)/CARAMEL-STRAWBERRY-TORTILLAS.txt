Title: CARAMEL STRAWBERRY TORTILLAS
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Packed brown sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Butter
2    10-inch tortillas
2 md Bananas; sliced
1 c  Strawberries; sliced
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/4 c  Sour cream
- or plain yogurt
1/3 c  Whipping cream
2 tb Granulated sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Place tortillas on large baking sheet. Top with bananas. Combine brown
butter. bake at 350F for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, beat cream with
granulated sugar and vanilla until stiff peaks form. Fold in sour
cream. Chill. Serve tortillas warm, cutting each into quarters.
Arrange 2 quarters on each dessert plate and top with sauce.
