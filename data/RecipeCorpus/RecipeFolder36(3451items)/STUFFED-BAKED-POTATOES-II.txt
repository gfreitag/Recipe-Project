Title: STUFFED BAKED POTATOES II
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
Salt and pepper, to taste
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
Paprika
4    6 oz baked potatoes
2 tb Butter or margarine
3/4 c  Milk
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1. Allow potatoes to stand at room temperature 2 to 3 minutes after
baking. 2. Cut a thin slice from the top of each potato., Caution:
potato will be hot.
3. With a spoon, remove the potato pulp from each potato, leaving a
thin shell.
4. Mash potato pulp with a fork or potato masher.
5. Add butter, milk, egg, salt, pepper and cheese. Whip potato
mixture with a rotary beater or electric mixer until light and fluffy.
6. Spoon mixture into shells and sprinkle with paprika.
7. Place potatoes on a paper plate and heat, uncovered, in Microwave
Oven 5 minutes.
8. If desired, after cooking in Microwave Oven, potatoes may be placed
under
the broiler unit of a conventional oven to brown for a few minutes.
Transfer
from paper plate before browning.
