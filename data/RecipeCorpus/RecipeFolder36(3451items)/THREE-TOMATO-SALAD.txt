Title: THREE TOMATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Side dish, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Prepared mustard
2 tb Basil, minced
2 ts Cider vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Mushrooms, thinly sliced
2 lg Tomatoes, thickly sliced
1/2 c  Yellow pear tomatoes, halved
1/2 c  Cherry tomatoes. halved
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 ts Sugar
Layer mushrooms & sliced tomatoes down the centre of a large platter.
Ring with the halved yellow & cherry tomatoes.
In a small bowl, whisk together the remaining ingredients & drizzle
this over the tomatoes.  Cover & marinate at room temperature for 30
minutes.
