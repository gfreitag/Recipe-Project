Title: ORANGE ROAST BRISKET
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 10 servings
1 ts Sugar
Salt
Pepper
Garlic
3    Onions
4 lb Brisket of Beef; first cut
1/2 c  Red Wine
1 c  Orange Juice
3 tb Tomato Sauce
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Use covered roasting pan. Place onions on
bottom and brisket on top, fat side up. Combine wine, orange juice,
tomato sauce and sugar. Pour over meat. Add spices and roast in
covered pan for 2 1/2 hours.  Raise heat to 350 degrees and roast for
1/2 hour uncovered. Slice against grain when cooled.
