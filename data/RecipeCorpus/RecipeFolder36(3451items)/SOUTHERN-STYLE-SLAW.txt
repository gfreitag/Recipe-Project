Title: SOUTHERN-STYLE SLAW
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Celery seed
1/4 ts Pepper
6 c  Shredded cabbage
1 c  Shredded carrots
1 c  Maries buttermilk ranch drsg
3 tb Cider vinegar
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Prepared mustard
In medium bowl, combine salad dressing, vinegar, sugar, mustard,
celery seed and pepper.  Add cabbage and carrots; toss gently to
coat.  Cover; refrigerate until serving time, at least 2 hours.
