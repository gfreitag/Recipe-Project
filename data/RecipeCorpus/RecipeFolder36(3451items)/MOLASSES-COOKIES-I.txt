Title: MOLASSES COOKIES I
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 40 servings
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Shortening
3/4 c  Dark-brown sugar
1    Egg
MMMMM----------------------OPTIONAL SPICES---------------------------
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Ground cloves
PREHEAT THE OVEN TO 350F. Mix the molasses, shortening, brown sugar
and egg in a bowl, combining well. Mix the flour, salt and baking
soda together, add to the first mixture and blend thoroughly. Arrange
by teaspoonfuls on ungreased cookie sheets, about 1 inch apart, and
bake for 6 to 8 minutes or until crisp and lightly browned.

FOR SPICED MOLASSES COOKIES: Add ground ginger, ground cloves,
cinnamon, and nutmeg to the flour mixture.
