Title: 24 HOUR SLAW
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2 md Onions, finely chopped
2    Red peppers, chopped
1 c  Cider vinegar
2 c  Sugar
2 ts Mustard seed
3 lb Cabbage, shredded
2    Green peppers, chopped
1 tb Salt
1 c  Water
2 ts Celery seed
Combine salt, vinegar, water, sugar, celery seed and mustard seed and
bring to a boil.  Pour over vegetables.  Chill.  Store, covered, in
the refrigerator.  Keep 24 hours before serving.  Will keep well if
kept tightly covered and refrigerated. Randy Rigg
