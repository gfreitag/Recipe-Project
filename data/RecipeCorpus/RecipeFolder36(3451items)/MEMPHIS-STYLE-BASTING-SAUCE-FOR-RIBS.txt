Title: MEMPHIS STYLE BASTING SAUCE FOR RIBS
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 5 cups
1/4 c  Worcestershire
1/2 ts Tabasco sauce
2 x  Neem leaves
1/4 c  Brown Sugar; broken up
1 1/2 tb Memphis Dry Marinade
2 c  Red Wine Vinegar
2 c  Water
Combine Well. Baste the ribs every half hour or so. This baste
:
keeps well in a large gallon glass bottle. (Like a vinegar Bottle).
