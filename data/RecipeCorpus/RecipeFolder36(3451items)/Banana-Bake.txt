Title: Banana Bake
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 recipe
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Light corn syrup
1/4 c  Water
1 tb Butter
4    Bananas, peeled
Lemon juice
1/4 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Finely chopped pecans
Dip bananas in lemon juice; arrange in a greased 2 qt.
baking dish. Sprinkle with raisins & pecans.

In a small saucepan, combine sugar, syrup and water
and cook over medium heat until bubby. Spoon syrup
over bananas, dot with butter. Cover and bake at 350
degrees for 25 minutes. Serve with whipped cream if
desired.
