Title: Williamsburg Turkey Soup - SL 11/90
Categories: Soup/stew, Poultry
Yield: 5 quarts
2 ea Stalks celery, diced
1 c  Long-grain rice, uncooked
2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Half-and-half
1 ea ;Turkey carcass
4 qt ;Water
1 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  All-purpose flour
3 ea Onions, chopped
2 lg Carrots, diced
Place turkey carcass and water in a large Dutch oven; bring to a
boil. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 1 hour. Remove carcass from
broth, and pick meat from bones. Set broth and meat aside. Measure
broth; add water, if necessary, to measure 3 quarts.
Heat butter in a large Dutch oven; add flour, and cook over medium
heat, stirring constantly, 5 minutes. (Roux will be a very light
color.)
Stir onion, carrot, and celery into roux; cook over medium heat 10
minutes, stirring often. Add broth, turkey, rice, salt, and pepper;
bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes or until
rice is tender. Add half-and half, and cook until thoroughly heated.
Yield: 4-1/2 quarts.

Recipe from Rhea Hatch in November, 1990 "Southern Living".
