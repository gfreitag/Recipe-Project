Title: BEAURITA
Categories: Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
6 oz Crushed ice
Garnish: jalapeno pepper, li
Salt (for rim of glass)
2 oz Vodka
2 oz Tequila
2 oz Lime juice
2 ds Tabasco sauce
Recipe by: Beau Nash Restaurant, Hotel Crescent Court, Dallas
Mix ingredients in a blender. Pour into salt-rimmed martini glass.
Garnish.
