Title: Fry Bread
Categories: Breads, Amerindian
Yield: 12 servings
1 tb Baking powder (heaping)
1 ts Salt
4 1/2 c  Flour
2 tb Powdered milk (heaping)
1 1/2 c  Water
Mix dry ing., stir in water until it is absorbed, then knead with
hands about five minutes on a floured board. Let stand about 30
minutes.
Pinch off dough about the size of a lemon and roll flat on floured
surface.  Fry in hot shortening that is about 1/4" deep in skillet.
