Title: Greek Christopsomo Anise Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Water
2 lg Egg
1 ts Lemon juice
GLAZE:
1 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 ts Anise extract
3 ts Water -- or more
2 1/4 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1 ts Anise seed -- crushed
3/4 ts Salt
4 tb Sugar
4 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
3 tb Unsalted butter
Add all ingredients except those for glaze.  Use basic cycle.  While
the bread cools to room temperature prepare glaze.  Glaze cooled
bread on top and sides.  Let dry before slicing.
