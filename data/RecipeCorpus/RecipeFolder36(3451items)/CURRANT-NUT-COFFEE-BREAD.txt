Title: CURRANT-NUT COFFEE BREAD
Categories: Fruit, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Brown sugar
3/4 ts Salt
1 ts Yeast
1/2 c  Currants or dried cherries
1/3 c  Toasted chopped almonds or
Pecans
1 1/2 pound loaf
1 1/2 ts Instant coffee crystals
1 c  Water
1    Egg
1 tb Applesauce
3 c  Bread flour
dissolve coffee in water. add all ingredients to machine. use light
setting. 101 cal/oz
