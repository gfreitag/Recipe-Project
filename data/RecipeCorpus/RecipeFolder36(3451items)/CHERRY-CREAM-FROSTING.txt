Title: CHERRY CREAM FROSTING
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
5    Maraschino cherries
- finely chopped
Maraschino cherries
- for garnish
4 c  Confectioners' sugar, sifted
1/4 c  Milk
2 tb Softened margarine
1 tb Maraschino cherry juice
Beat until smooth and creamy (about 2 minutes) the confectioners'
sugar, milk, margarine, cherry juice and chopped cherries.  Garnish
as desired with maraschino cherries.  Goes well with Maraschino
Cherry Cake.
