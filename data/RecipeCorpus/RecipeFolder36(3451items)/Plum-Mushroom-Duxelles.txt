Title: Plum-Mushroom Duxelles
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 cup
1/2 ts Soy sauce
1 sm Prune plum
Pitted and chopped
Skin left on
3 tb Butter
2/3 c  Onion(s), chopped
2 c  Button mushrooms
Chopped (abt 8oz)
1/2 ts Salt
Note: Use this in dishes made with duck, scallops, or rich, oily fish
such as mackerel or shad.

Melt the butter in a large saute pan over medium heat and add the
onion. Saute for 1 min, then add the mushrooms. Continue to saute as
the mushrooms give off their liquid and the liquid begins to
evaporate. As the mixture begins to dry out, in a bout 5 min, add the
salt and soy sauce. Finally, add the plum and saute for another 2 min.

Can be kept for about a week in the refrigerator or frozen for several
months.
