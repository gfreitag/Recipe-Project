Title: Shor's Cafe Tartar Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
1 ts Dry minced onion
1 ds Black pepper
Few drops ofTabasco sauce
1/4 c  Sweet pickle relish
1/4 c  Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sour cream
1 tb Prepared yellow mustard
1 ts Granulated sugar
In mixing bowl, add all ingredients except relish; blend well. Fold
in relish. Chill completely before using.  Keep covered in
refrigerator up to 2 weeks.
