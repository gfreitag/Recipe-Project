Title: Pork with Cabbage Shreds
Categories: Chinese, Meats, Wok
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 lb Finely shredded cabbage
1    Minced garlic clove
1/4 ts Pepper
4 tb Soy sauce
1 1/2 lb Pork
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 tb Sherry
3 tb Oil
Cut the pork in matchlike pieces; mix with sugar, salt, sherry and 2
T Soy sauce. Let stand 10 minutes.

Heat 2 T oil in a skillet; saute' the cabbage 5 min, stirring
frequently. Remove from pan. Heat the remaining oil in skillet; saute
the pork and garlic 10 min, stirring frequently. Add cabbage, pepper
and remainng soy sauce; cook over low heat 3 minutes.

from The Complete Book of Oriental Cooking by Myra Waldo
