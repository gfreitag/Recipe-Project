Title: Sprouting Millet Seeds
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Millet seeds
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1-1/2 cups in a quart jar, or tray sprout which will yield about 2
cups of sprouts. Soak seeds for 8 hours.

Rinse 3 times daily for 3 to 5 days.

Harvest when sprouts are 1/4 inch long. Use in salads, soups, baked
goods, casseroles, and juices.
