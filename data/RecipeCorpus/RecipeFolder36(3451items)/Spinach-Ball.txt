Title: Spinach Ball
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Mayonnaise
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Tabasco sauce
1/4 c  Red onion, finely chopped
3 oz Blue cheese (crumbled)
3 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 pk Frozen spinach thawed &
Liquid squeezed out, 10 1/2
Oz. size
Combine the above ingredients until thoroughly mixed.  Form into ball
shape and roll in pecan pieces.  For best results store in the fridge
overnight before serving.
