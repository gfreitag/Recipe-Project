Title: ORANGE & CREAM-CHEESE SPREAD
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Powdered cugar
3 ts Grated orange peel
1 pk Cream Cheese (8 oz)
1/3 c  Orange juice
In small bowl of electric mixer or food processor fitted with a metal
blade, process all ingredients until smooth and light. Spoon into a
small serving dish. Cover and refrigerate. Let stand at room
temperature 20 to 30 minutes before serving.  Makes about 1 cup.
VARIATIONS:
Ginger-Orange Spread: Add 1 tablespoon minced crystallized ginger.
Prune-Cheese Spread:  Add 1/2 cup finely chopped prunes.
