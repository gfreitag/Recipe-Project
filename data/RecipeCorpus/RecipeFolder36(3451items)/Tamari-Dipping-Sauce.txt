Title: Tamari Dipping Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Sugar
1 pt Sake
1 c  Tamari
Combine all of the ingredients in a heavy saucepan and reduce over
medium heat until the sauce coats the back of a spoon. Remove from
heat and let cool slightly.

Serve with grilled poultry or hot or cold buckwheat noodles.
