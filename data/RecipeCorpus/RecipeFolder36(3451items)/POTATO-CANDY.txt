Title: POTATO CANDY
Categories: Candies, Potatoes
Yield: 1 batch
pn Salt
Peanut butter
Nuts
1 sm Potato, boiled and mashed
Powdered sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Boil small potato and peel jacket while hot.  Mash. Add powdered
sugar, a pinch of salt, and vanilla.  Keep adding powdered sugar
until like pie dough.

Sprinkle counter top with powdered sugar and roll out like pie dough.
Spread with peanut butter (creamy or crunchy) and nuts.  Roll up
dough and slice into pieces.
