Title: Potatoe Chip Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Potatoes, Nuts/grains
Yield: 60 servings
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Brown sugar
1 ea Egg
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 c  Crushed potatoe chips
Cream sugar, shortening and egg.  Stir in flour, soda and vanilla.
Mix well.  Add chips and nuts.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased
cookie sheet.  Bake 10 minutes.
