Title: Succotash
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Water
Salt and pepper
1/8 ts Sugar
2 tb Bacon drippings
2 c  Okra, sliced
2 c  Corn, fresh
4 lg Tomatoes, diced
In a skillet heat bacon drippings, add fresh okra and corn, saute for
a few minutes until tender crisp.  Add tomatoes (diced) and water,
salt and pepper to taste.  Add sugar and simmer for about 1 hour on
very low heat.
