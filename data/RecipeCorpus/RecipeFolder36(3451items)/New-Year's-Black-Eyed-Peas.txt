Title: New Year's Black-Eyed Peas
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Molasses
6    Sun-dried tomatoes
(not oil-packed), chopped
2 ts Salt or to taste
Black pepper to taste
1 lb Dried black-eyed peas
Rinsed and picked over
1    Onion(s), chopped
1 cn (14oz) stewed
Tomatoes with juices
In a large bowl, cover the peas with cold water; let soak overnight.
Drain and rinse the peas; place in a large pot and add onions, stewed
tomatoes, molasses, sun-dried tomatoes, and 2 cups water. Bring to a
boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer until the peas are very
tender and the sauce is thick, 2 1/2-3 hours. Add more water as
needed. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
