Title: Baked Apples
Categories: Desserts, Microwave, Guestchefs
Yield: 1 servings
1    Cinnamon
1    Butter
1    Red Cooking Apple
1    Brown Sugar
Remove the core from each apple leaving a bottom in each apple. Make a
mixture of the brown sugar and butter. Fill each apple with the
mixture. Pre-heat oven to 325Ã¸F. Bake apples on a rimmed cookie sheet
