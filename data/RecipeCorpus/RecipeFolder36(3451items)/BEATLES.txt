Title: BEATLES
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 100 servings
1 tb Baking powder
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate pieces
2/3 c  Sugar
1 tb Ground cinnamon
3/4 c  Butter or margarine
2 1/4 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
5 1/4 c  Flour
3/4 ts Salt
3/4 ts Baking soda
Cream butter and first amount of sugar. Add eggs and mix well. Sift
flour with salt, soda and baking powder. Alternately add to egg
mixture with sour cream. Add vanilla and fold in chocolate pieces.
Drop by heaping teaspoons onto greased baking sheets. Combine
remaining sugar with cinnamon and sprinkle over each cookie. Bake at
375F 12 minutes.
