Title: TANGY VIETNAMESE-STYLE MARINADE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------FOR POULTRY, PORK, SEAFOOD----------------------
2 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Bouillon powder
1/4 ts Freshly ground pepper
2    Garlic cloves; crushed
3    Shallots; minced
1/4 c  Peanut oil
3 tb Nuoc mam sauce
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Makes about 3/4 cup
