Title: Honey Baked Onions
Categories: Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 12 servings
Honey
2 tb Butter
6    Yellow onions, peeled
-and cut in half
Place cut side up in microwave pan.  Dribble with
honey and dot with butter.  Add a little water to the
pan and cover with plastic.

Microwave on high for 8-10 minutes.  Turn 1/4 after 3
minutes,   Turn 1/4 after 3 minutes.
