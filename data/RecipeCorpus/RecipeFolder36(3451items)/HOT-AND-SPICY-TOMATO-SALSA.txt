Title: HOT AND SPICY TOMATO SALSA
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Dry white wine or
Unsweetened apple juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/2 ts (1/2-1 tsp) ground red
Pepper (cayenne)
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ts Olive oil
1/4 c  Chopped fresh cilantro
3    Ripe medium tomatoes,
Coarsely chopped (3 cups)
15 oz Can, (1 3/4 cups) tomato
Puree
6    Medium garlic cloves,
Minced
2    Small jalapeno chile peppers
Seeded, finely chopped
3 tb Finely chopped onion
In a medium saucepan, combine all ingredients except cilantro.  Bring
to a boil over high heat.  Reduce heat to medium; cook, uncovered, 15
minutes, stirring occasionally.  Stir in cilantro.  Serve hot or cold
as a dip for chips or as a relish. Makes 4 Cups.  Can be frozen for
up to 2 months in tightly covered container.
