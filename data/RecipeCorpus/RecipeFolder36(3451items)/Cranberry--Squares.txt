Title: Cranberry  Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 recipe
1 ts Almond extract
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Fresh or frozen cranberries
1/2 c  Chopped almonds or pecans
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 lg Eggs
3/4 c  Melted butter or margarine
-cooled slightly
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Butter 9-in. square
baking pan.  Using mixer beat sugar and eggs in large
bowl until slightly thickened, about 2 min. Beat in
melted butter and extract.  Add flour and stir until
well blended. Stir in cranberries and pecans.  Pour
batter into prepared pan. Bake until tester comes out
clean, about 1 hr.  Cool completely before cutting. If
doubling recipe, use two 9-in. pans (not a 9x13 pan).
