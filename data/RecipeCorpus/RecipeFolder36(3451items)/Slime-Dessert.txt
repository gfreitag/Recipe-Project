Title: Slime Dessert
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
Cool whip Lite
M&ms plain chocolate candy
Cherry flavored gelatin
Lime flavored gelatin
Make equal quantities of cherry and lime gelatin. When set, mash each
color up separately.  Place alternate layers of slimy gelatin into a
large glass bowl.  Top with "eyeballs" of whipped cream blobs with M
& M centers.
