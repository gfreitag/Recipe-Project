Title: Smoked Trout Crepe Appetizer #2
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Bbq/grill, Appetizers, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 10 servings
1 tb Chives; finely chopped
1 tb Green pepper; finely chopped
1 tb Pimento; finely chopped
Pepper
5    Precooked 6-8" crepes
1/4 lb Hot smoked lake trout
3 oz Cream cheese; softened
2 tb Mayonnaise
Mix the ingredients and spread on the crepes. cut crepes into
quarters and roll up. Refrigerate and serve chilled.

From: Smoking Salmon & Trout by Jack Whelan. Published by: Airie
Publishing, Deep Bay, B.C. ISBN: 0-919807-00-3 Posted by: Jim Weller
