Title: Kidney Stew: Cocido De Rin~ones, Adapted From T-L Caribbe
Categories: Meats, Soup/stew, Caribbean
Yield: 4 servings
-vinegar or malt vinegar
1 1/2 lb Lamb kidneys, cleaned,
-trimmed, quartered
1/2 ts Salt
Pepper to taste
7 tb Olive oil, divided 3 and 4
1/2 c  Onion, minced
1 ts Garlic, minced
1 c  Tomatoes, minced
1 tb Red wine or substitute wine
Wilt onion and garlic in 3 T of oil. Add tomatoes and wine. Cook until
thick.

Rinse and dry the kidneys. Season with salt and pepper. Stir-fry in 4
T of hot oil until lightly browned on all sides. Add tomato mixture.
Cook a couple minutes. Correct seasoning. Serve hot with or over rice.

From Time Life Caribbean and posted by Michael Loo.
