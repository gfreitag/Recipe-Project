Title: Corn Relish
Categories: Relishes, Can/preserv
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Chopped cabbage
1/2 ts Turmeric
1 c  Chopped celery
2 c  Corn, cut from the cob
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Choped cucumber
2 c  Vinegar
2 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Celery seed
2 c  Chopped tomatoes
Delicious Orchards

In a Dutch oven or heavy sauce pan, combine first five ingredients.
Heat to a boiling; add vegetables.  Cook, uncovered, 25 minutes,
stirring occasionally. Pack in hot scalded jars and seal.  Makes 3
pints.
