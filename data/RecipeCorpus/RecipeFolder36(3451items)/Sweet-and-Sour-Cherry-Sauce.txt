Title: Sweet and Sour Cherry Sauce
Categories: Chinese, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cloves, Ground
1 ts Lemon Rind, grated
1 c  Sour Cherries; pitted
- and pureed
1 tb Sugar; or honey, to taste
1 tb Vinegar
Combine cherries, sugar, vinegar, cinnamon and lemon rind and
bring to a boil. Lower heat and cook 5 mins. Add a little water
to thin, if desired.

An attempt to approximate the cherry sauce used in Chinese-
Canadian restaurants for Chicken Balls. Posted by Jim Weller.
