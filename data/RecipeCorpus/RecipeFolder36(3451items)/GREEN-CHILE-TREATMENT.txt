Title: GREEN CHILE TREATMENT
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------BEEF,POULTRY,PORK,LAMB------------------------
-=OR=- chili powder
2 tb Vegetable oil
4 oz Mild or hot green chiles
2 ts Mild ground chiles
Combine all ingredients in a food processor. Process
until smooth. Makes about 1/3 cup.
