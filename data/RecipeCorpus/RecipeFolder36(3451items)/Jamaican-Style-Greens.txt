Title: Jamaican-Style Greens
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
Seeded and finely chopped
1/8 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Salt
Freshly ground black peppere
2 ts Lemon or lime juice
2 lb Collard greens, kale or
Mustard greens
1/4 c  Water
1 1/2 tb Olive oil
1 c  Finely chopped onion
1 sm Red jalapeno pepper, stemmed
1. Wash the greens well in several changes of water.  Remove the
thick rib from the center of the leaves; chop coarsely.
2. Put the greens with 1/4 cup water into a large pot.  Place over
medium heat, cover and cook until wilted, about 10 minutes.  Stir
often.  Drain. 3. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
Add the onion and jalapeno; saute 5 minutes.  Stir in the greens,
allspice, salt and pepper; saute 5 minutes.
4. Stir in the lime juice and serve.
