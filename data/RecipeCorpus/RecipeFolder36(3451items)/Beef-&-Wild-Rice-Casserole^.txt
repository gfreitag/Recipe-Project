Title: Beef & Wild Rice Casserole^
Categories: Casseroles, Pasta/rice, Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Chicken noodle soup
2 cn Water
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 md Chopped onion
1 c  Uncooked wild rice, rinsed
Brown beef and onion. Drain; place in a 3 qt. casserole. Combine
remaining ingredients and mix well. Cover and bake 1 hour 20 minutes
at 375F., stirring occasionally.
