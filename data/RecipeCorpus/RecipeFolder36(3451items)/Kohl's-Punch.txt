Title: Kohl's Punch
Categories: Vegetables, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
2    Juice of 2 Lemons
Mint leaves
4 c  Sugar
7-Up to stretch it
1 cn Pineapple juice (46-oz)
2 c  Orange juice
6 c  Water
5    Bananas; mashed
1 pk Strawberries; frozen
Mix sugar and water. Chill.  Add fruit juices and bananas right away.
Freeze and then break into pieces.  Add strawberries and 7-Up.

Taken from: IT NEVER TURNS OUT THE SAME WAY COOKBOOK
A Collection of Recipes from the Kitchen of Joyce and Clem Kohl
