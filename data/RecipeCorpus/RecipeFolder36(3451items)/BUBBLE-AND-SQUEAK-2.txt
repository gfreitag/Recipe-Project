Title: BUBBLE AND SQUEAK 2
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
-boiled first
2 c  Potatoes; mashed
1 pt Butter
1    Onion; fine chopped
2 c  Cabbage; shredded
Heat butter in large skillet & fry onion over low heat for 5  min or
till softened.  Add cabbage (should be boiled for 15 minutes
previously) & stir over low heat for 2  min. Fold in mashed potatoes
till well blended. Press mixture lightly with  back of spoon to form
large pancake. Cook for 5 min till browned lightly. Turn & brown 2nd
side 5 min.
