Title: English Muffin Bread
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1 ts Salt
2 ts Sugar
1 1/4 c  Water
2 ts Yeast
3 tb Nonfat dry milk
3 c  Bread flour
1/4 ts Baking soda
Add ingredients to the ABM in the order listed.  Water should be
between 105 and 125 degrees F. Feels warm to touch but not hot
Makes a 1 1/2 lb loaf excellent for toast in morning.

Source: The Bread Machine Cookbook by Donna Rathmell German
ISBN 1-55867-025-4
