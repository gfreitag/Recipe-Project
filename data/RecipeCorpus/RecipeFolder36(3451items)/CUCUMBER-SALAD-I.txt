Title: CUCUMBER SALAD I
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Vinegar
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Water
Watercress
3    Cucumbers
- peeled and sliced thin
1    Garlic clove; peeled
Salt
COVER THE CUCUMBERS WITH WATER. Add the garlic and sprinkle well with
salt, stirring to dissolve the salt. Let stand 1 hour. Drain,
pressing gently to get rid of excess liquid, and discard garlic. Stir
the vinegar, sugar and 2 tablespoons water together until the sugar
is dissolved. Pour over the cucumbers and stir. Add salt to taste.
Chill well, drain and serve on a bed of watercress.
