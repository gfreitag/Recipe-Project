Title: Puff Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Flour
1/4 c  Grape-Nuts cereal
1 c  Milk
2    Egg whites, stiffly beaten
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar or honey
1 ts Grated lemon rind
2    Egg yolks
3 tb Lemon juice
Heat oven to 325 deg. F.  Beat butter with sugar and
lemon rind in large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat
in egg yolks. Stir in lemon juice, flour, cereal and
milk.  (Mixture will look curdled.)

Fold in beaten egg whites.

Pour into greased 1-quart baking dish.  Place dish in
pan of hot water. Bake 1 hour and 15 minutes or until
top springs back when lightly touched. When done,
pudding has a cake-like layer on top with custard
below. Serve warm or cold. Makes 6 servings.
