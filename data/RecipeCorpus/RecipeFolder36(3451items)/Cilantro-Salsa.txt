Title: Cilantro Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 3 /4 cup
Lightly packed
3 tb Canola oil
1 tb Lime juice
1 tb Mock Sour Cream
(see pg 53)
2 tb Green chiles, diced
1    Garlic clove(s), minced
3/4 c  White onion(s), minced
1 ds Salsa habanero
1/2 c  Cilantro, chopped
Combine all ingredients in a blender and puree. Set aside to cool or
store in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
