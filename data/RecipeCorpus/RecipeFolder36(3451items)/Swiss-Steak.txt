Title: Swiss Steak
Categories: Meats, Guestchefs
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
3 tb Oil
1 sm Chopped Onion
2 lb Round Or Chuck Steak
1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Salt
Mix flour, salt and pepper.  Pound thoroughly into steak with meat
pounder or edge of saucer.  Brown the meat and onion in hot oil.
Cover; cook over low heat, or bake in a 350Ã¸F oven until tender,
about 1-1/2 hours or more. From: Syd's Cookbook.
