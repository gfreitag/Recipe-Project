Title: Stewed Okra and Tomatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Tomatoes
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Tomatoes, 16 oz.
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 md Onion, chopped
2 tb Oil
1 pk Okra, 10 oz.
Cook onion in fat in saucepan over moderate heat until lightly
browned. Add remaining ingredients and cook until okra is tender and
mixture thickens, 10 to 15 minutes.  Stir occasionally to prevent
sticking.
