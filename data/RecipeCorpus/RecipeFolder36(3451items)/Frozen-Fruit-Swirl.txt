Title: Frozen Fruit Swirl
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Frozen berries; slightly
.  thawed
4    Bananas; peeled, sliced
.  and frozen solid
Place frozen banana pieces in a food processor. Pulse with on and off
motions until finely chopped; whirl until pureed. Spoon puree into a
bowl and place in the freezer.

Add berries to the food processor. Whirl until pureed. Remove bananas
from the freezer. Swirl in berry puree. Serve at once. Makes 6
servings.

* Approximate nutritional analysis: 83 calories per 1/2-cup serving;
1g protein; 21g carbohydrate; .4g fat (4% of calories); 2g fiber;
1.5mg sodium; 37% of the Daily Value for vitamin C, 22% for vitamin
B6.
