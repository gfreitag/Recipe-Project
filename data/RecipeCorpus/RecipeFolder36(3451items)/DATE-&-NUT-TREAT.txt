Title: DATE & NUT TREAT
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Rice crispies
1 c  Walnuts cut up
1 c  Or more of coconut
1/2 c  Margarine
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Pitted dates
1/3 c  Cherries cut up
1    Egg slightly beaten
Put first 5 ingredients in saucepan and bring to a boil, boil 3-5 min.
Remove from heat and add the vanilla, rice crispies and walnuts. Drop
by tblsp. in coconut & roll. Refrigerate.
