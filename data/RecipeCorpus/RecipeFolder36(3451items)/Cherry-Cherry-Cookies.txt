Title: Cherry Cherry Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 4 dozen
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Maraschino cherries, well
-drained and chopped
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1/2 c  Flaked coconut
1 c  Packed brown sugar
3/4 c  Butter or margarine
1    Egg
2 tb Milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  All-purpose flour
In a large mixing bowl, cream brown sugar, butter, egg, milk and
vanilla. Combine flour, salt and baking soda; gradually add to
creamed mixture. Fold in cherries, pecans and coconut. Drop by
teaspoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake at 375 for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
