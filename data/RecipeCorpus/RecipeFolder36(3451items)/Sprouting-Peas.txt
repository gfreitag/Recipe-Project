Title: Sprouting Peas
Categories: Sprouts, Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Black-eyed or shelling peas
Water to 3 times seeds
1 ea Quart jar -OR-
1 ea Prepared tray
Use 1/2 cups black eyed or shelling peas in a quart jar, or tray
sprout which will yield about 1 cup of sprouts. Soak for 12 hours.

Rinse 2 or 3 times daily for 3 days.

Harvest when sprouts are 1/4 to 1/2 inch long. Use in salads, soups,
omelets, and casseroles.
