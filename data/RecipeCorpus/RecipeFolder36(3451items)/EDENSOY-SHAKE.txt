Title: EDENSOY SHAKE
Categories: Beverages, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Strawberries or blueberries
- fresh or frozen
1/8 c  Maple syrup (optional)
3 c  EDENsoy
-OR- any EDEN food beverage
1    Ripe banana
Blend all ingredients until smooth. You can also pour into popsicle
molds and freeze.

Prep Time: 2 minutes * Yield: 4 servings
