Title: Peel A Pound Soup (Vegan)
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Can diced tomatoes
2 md Potatoes
4    Carrots
1    Box (2pk) onion soup mix
3 c  Vegetable stock
1 sm Head cabbage
4 lg Onions
2 cl Diced garlic
1    Bunch celery
1 lg Green pepper
Clean veggies and cut into medium sized pieces.  Add all ingredients
to soup kettle and finish filling with water until it just covers the
vegetables.  Bring to a boil, and then reduce to a simmer and cook for
2 1/2 - 3 hours.
