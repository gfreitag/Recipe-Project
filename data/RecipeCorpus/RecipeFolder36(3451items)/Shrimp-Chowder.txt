Title: Shrimp Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
2 c  Shrimp (frozen)
3 oz Cream cheese
2 cn Cream of chicken soup
2 cn Potato soup
1 cn Corn, drained
2 c  Evaporated milk
Brown onions.  Saute in 1 tablespoon butter.  Combine remaining
ingredients except cream cheese in crock pot.  Cook on medium
high heat for 3 hours.  In the last 15 minutes add cream cheese.
