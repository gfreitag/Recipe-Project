Title: Steamed Rockfish With Hoisin And Spinach
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/3 lb Rockfish fillet
8    Spinach leaves -- stems
-removed and
-washed and dried
4    Lemon wedges
2 tb Hoisin sauce
2 ts Sesame oil
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Orange peel -- finely
-grated
Combine the hoisin sauce, sesame oil, salt and orange peel.
spread on fish fillets.  Top with spinach leaves.

Place the fish on a rack set above boiling water.  ( A roasting
pan fitted with a rectangular cake rack, set above the water,
or use a bamboo steamer.)  Cover with foil and steam 12
minutes per inch of thickness, or until fish tests done (140
degrees on and instant read thermometer.)
