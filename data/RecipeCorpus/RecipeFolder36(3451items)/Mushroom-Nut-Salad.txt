Title: Mushroom-Nut Salad
Categories: Vegetarian, Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
-into bite-size chunks
3/4 c  Toasted filberts -- chopped
(walnuts or pecans may be
-substituted)
3/4 ts Savory
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms -- sliced
3/4 c  Olive or salad oil
1/2 c  Wine vinegar
2 tb Water
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Onion powder
1 sm Head Romaine lettuce, torn
Combine mushrooms, oil, vinegar, water and seasonings in a bowl. Chill
about 1 hour.

To toast filberts, spread nuts in shallow pan and bake in 400F oven
for 10-15 min, stirring occasionally.

Toss undrained mushrooms with lettuce and filberts in salad bowl.

6-8 servings.

Jan McEwen, Hawaii  21 Oct 1994

: From _Mother Earth's Vegetarian Feasts_ by Joel Rapp
