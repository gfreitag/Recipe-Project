Title: SPICY SKEWERED SHRIMP
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Mint; chopped fresh
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
2 lb Shrimp; 16-20 size, cleaned,
-peeled and deveined
Bamboo skewers; soaked in
-warm water for 30 minutes
-CHUCK OZBURN   (HBWK07A)
2/3 c  Olive oil
1 tb Tumeric
1/4 ts Black pepper; freshly ground
3    Garlic cloves; minced
1 tb Basil; chopped fresh
2 Combine all ingredients and marinate for 6 to 8 hours; skewer
decoratively  and grill,  turning and basting, only until pink; DO NOT
OVERCOOK!!
