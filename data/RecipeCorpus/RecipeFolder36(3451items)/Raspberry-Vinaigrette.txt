Title: Raspberry Vinaigrette
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
4 ts Cider vinegar
1 tb Dry white wine
1 ts Coarse grained mustard
1/3 c  Raspberry preserves
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
4 ts Rice vinegar
Place preserves in medium bowl.  Gradually whisk in oil.  Stir in rest
of ingredients.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Makes 3/4 cup.
