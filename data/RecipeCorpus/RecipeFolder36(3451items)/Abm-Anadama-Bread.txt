Title: Abm Anadama Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
4 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
2 tb Unsalted butter
1 1/4 c  Water
3 tb Molasses
1 ts Lemon juice
2 1/4 ts Yeast
2 1/2 c  Bread flour
1/2 c  Whole-wheat flour
1/3 c  Cornmeal
1 1/2 ts Salt
Use basic cycle.  Do not use timer as water will be absorbed.
