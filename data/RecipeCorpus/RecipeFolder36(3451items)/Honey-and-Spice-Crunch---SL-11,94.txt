Title: Honey-and-Spice Crunch - SL 11/94
Categories: Desserts, Snacks
Yield: 3 quarts
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
2 ea (0.9-oz) packages dried
-fruit bits; (about 1/2 cup)
3 qt Popcorn; popped
Vegetable cooking spray
3 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Light corn syrup
3 tb Sugar
Pour popcorn into a bowl coated with cooking spray; set aside.
Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat; stir in next 4
ingredients. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.
Boil, without stirring, 7 minutes or until a candy thermometer
reaches 250F.
Remove from heat.  Stir in cinnamon, soda, and vanilla; pour over
popcorn, stirring to coat.  Spoon mixture into a lightly greased 15"
x 10" x 1" jellyroll pan.
Bake at 250F for 25 minutes or until golden, stirring every 5
minutes. Stir in dried fruit; cool.  Store in airtight containers.
Yield: 3 quarts.

Peggy Fowler Revels of South Carolina, in November, 1994 "Southern
