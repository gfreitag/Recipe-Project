Title: EASY SWEET AND SOUR SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Microwave, Fruit, Relishes
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Ketchup
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 c  Apricot or peach preserves
2 tb Cider vinegar
1. Combine all ingredients in a 1-quart glass measuring pitcher.

2. Cook uncovered in microwave oven 2-1/2 to 3 minutes at High; stir
once.

Yield: About 1-1/4 cups.

SOURCE: Adventures in Microwave Cooking
