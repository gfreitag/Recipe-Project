Title: Christmas Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 36 cookies
2 1/2 c  Flour - sifted
1 c  Almonds - sliced lengthwise
1/2 c  Each red and green cherries
Whole
1 c  Soft butter
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/8 ts Salt
Cream butter, add sugar and then add vanilla, salt and flour.
Finally add nuts and cherries and form into rolls.  Leave in ice box
overnight.  Slice thinly with a sharp knife and bake on greased
baking sheet in moderate oven (375 F) for 10 minutes.
