Title: Green Tomato Relish
Categories: Tomatoes, Relishes, Can/preserv
Yield: 6 servings
3 lb Sugar (about 6 3/4 cups, a
-little less is better)
1/4 c  Salt
3 tb Mustard Seed
3 tb Celery Seed
2 ts Tumeric
3 qt Green Tomatoes (about 14
-large)
1 qt Cooking Onions (about 5 or
-6)
3    Sweet Red peppers
Boiling Water
1 qt Vinegar
Grind and measure tomatoes, onions, and peppers (removing seeds). Pour
boiling water over the mixture and let stand 5 minutes. Drain well
and add vinegar, sugar, salt, mustard seed, celery seed, and tumeric.
Cook 30 minutes or until thickened, let simmer (might take more than
30 minutes to thicken). While hot, pour into sterilized jars and
process.

SOURCE: Syracuse Herald-Journal, 9/20/92
