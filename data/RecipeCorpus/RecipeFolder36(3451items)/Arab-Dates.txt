Title: Arab Dates
Categories: Fruit, Holiday, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1 lb Pitted dates
Sugar
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Chopped toasted almonds
1/4 c  Chopped candied citron
Mix first 4 ingredients.  Stuff dates with this mixture.  Roll in
sugar and store in airtight container.
