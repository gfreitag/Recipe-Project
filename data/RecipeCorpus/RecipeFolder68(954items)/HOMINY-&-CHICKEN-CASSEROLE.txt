Title: HOMINY & CHICKEN CASSEROLE
Categories: Main dish, Casserole
Yield: 8 Servings
4 c  Cubed stuffing mix
2 c  Chicken, cooked and diced
1 ea Golden hominy, 15oz can
1 ea Green chiles, 4-1/2 oz can
1/2 ea Red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 c  Parsley, chopped
1 ea Cream of mushroom soup, can
1 ea Whole kernel corn, can
1 c  Cream or milk
2 ts Cumin
1 c  Monterey Jack cheese
Combine stuffing and next five ingredients in large bowl. Stir
together remaining ingredients (except cheese)and combine with
stuffing mix. Pour into lightly greased 2-quart shallow baking dish.
Cover lightly with foil and bake at 350f for 35 minutes. Uncover,
sprinkle with cheese and bake an additional 5 minutes or until cheese
is melted and bubbly
