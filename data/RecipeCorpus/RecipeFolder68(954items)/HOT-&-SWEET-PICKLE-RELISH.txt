Title: HOT & SWEET PICKLE RELISH
Categories: Canning, Chile heads, Condiments, Relishes
Yield: 8 Half pings
1 qt Cucumbers, fineley chopped
1 c  Hot peppers, finely chopped
2 c  Onions, finely chopped
1 c  Green pepper, finely chopped
1/4 c  Salt
3 1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  Cider vinegar
1 tb Celery seed
1 tb Mustard seed
Combine cucumbers, onions, and peppers in a large bowl; sprinkle with
salt and cover with cold water. Let stand 2 hours. Drain thoroughly;
press out excess liquid. Combine sugar, vinegar and spices in a large
sauce pot. Bring to a boil.  Add drained vegetables and simmier 10
minutes. Pack hot into hot jars, leaving 1/4-inch head space. Adjust
caps. Process 10 minutes in boiling water bath.

Note:  For hotter relish, substitute hot peppers for the sweet green
peppers.
