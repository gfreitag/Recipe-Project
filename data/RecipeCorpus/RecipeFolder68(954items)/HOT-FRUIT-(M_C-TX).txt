Title: HOT FRUIT (M_C-TX)
Categories: Fruits
Yield: 12 Servings
5 cn Any fruit, drained
-(pineapple, peaches,
-apricots, pears, plums
-cherries, apples)
1    Stick of melted butter or
-margarine
1 c  Of brown sugar
1 cn Of apple sauce
Mix fruit together and arrange in a baking dish.  Mix together melted
butter, brown sugar and apple sauce and pour over fruit. Bake at 325
for one hour.  Serve hot or cold. Before baking, you may put on top
either crumbled macaroon cookies or chopped nuts.
