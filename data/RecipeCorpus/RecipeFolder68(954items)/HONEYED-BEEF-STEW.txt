Title: HONEYED BEEF STEW
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Stwing beef
1 lg Onion
2 ts Mustard powder
2 tb Honey
3    Celery stalks
3/4 pt Water
2 tb Lemon juice
1 md Green pepper
6 oz Ketchup
Heat a little oil in a pan,, brown cubed meat, slice onion and
pepper, chop celery, add to beef, mix the rest and pour over. Cover
and cook at Mark 4, 350 F, 1
1/2    hour or until tender.
