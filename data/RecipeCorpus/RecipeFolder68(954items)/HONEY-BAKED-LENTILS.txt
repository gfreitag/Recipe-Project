Title: HONEY BAKED LENTILS
Categories: Appetizers, Pulses and
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Lentils
1 sm Bay Leaf
2 1/2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
-----Xxxxx-----
1/2 ts Dry Mustard
1/8 ts Powdered Ginger
1 1/2 ts Tamari
1/4 c  Chopped Onion
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Honey
Combine first 4 ingredients in a saucepan. Bring to a boil. Cover
tightly and reduce heat. Simmer 30 minutes. Do not drain. Discard bay
leaf.

Preheat oven to 350.

Combine remaining 5 ingredients separately and add to lentils.

Place lentils in casserole dish and pour honey over top. Cover
tightly and bake 1 hour.  Uncover last 10 minutes.
