Title: HONEY-GARLIC SPARERIBS
Categories: Entree, Meat
Yield: 4 Servings
lb Spareribs
1/2 c  Honey
1 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
4    Cloves Garlic -- Crushed
Cook ribs in boiling salted water till tender 1 to 2 hrs. Combine
Iingredients and pour over ribs, turning to coat (can be refrigerated
or frozen.  To cook, heat in sauce in 400ooven, turn often.
