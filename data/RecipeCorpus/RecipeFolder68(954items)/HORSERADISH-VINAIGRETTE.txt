Title: HORSERADISH VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Dressings, Salads, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1 ts Fresh lemon juice
-- (OR up to double amount)
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Soy sauce
1/2 ts Freshly grated horseradish
-- (or prepared)
1    Garlic clove; minced
2 ts -to
3 ts Fresh herbs *
1/2 c  Olive oil
* Fresh herbs mix should be equal parts basil, dill, parsley and
chervil.

Place all ingredients in a jar.  Cover and shake jar vigorously until
mixed.

From: The Cook's Garden catalog - Spring/Summer 1993 (page 65)
