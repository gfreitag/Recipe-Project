Title: HONEY HERMITS
Categories: Cookies, Snacks
Yield: 6 Servings
1/3 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Milk
2    Eggs, beaten
1/2 ts Cloves
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 1/3 c  Honey
1 c  Raisins, chopped
1/2 ts Salt
4 tb Baking powder
3 1/2 c  Flour
Cream shortening & honey together; add eggs, and mix in. Add milk,
salt & raisins; mix well.  Sift & mix dry ingredients together; then
mix into batch.  Drop by spoon on greased pan. Bake in a moderate
oven until brown.
