Title: HONEY PEANUT BUTTER FUDGE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  Sugar
2    Egg whites
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Water
1 c  Honey
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1 ts Vanilla
Boil 1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup water to soft ball stage (234 - 238 F).
Pour slowly over stiffly beaten egg whites, beating constantly until
stiff. Boil together 3 cups sugar, honey, peanut butter, and 1/2 cup
water to soft ball stage (234 - 238 F). Slowly add to first mixture.
Beat constantly until the mixture will hold its shape when dropped
from a teaspoon. Add nuts and flavoring.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto
waxed paper. Mrs. L.H. Frey, Elgin, NE.
