Title: HOT MUSHROOM BACON SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Spreads, New england
Yield: 3 Cups
4    Slices bacon
3    Mushrooms, chopped
1    Small onion, chopped
1    Clove garlic
2 tb Flour
1    8oz cream cheese
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Soy sauce
1/2 c  Sour cream
Cook bacon crisp and crumble.  Saute mushrooms, onion and garlic in 2
T of bacon fat until soft, (5 minutes). Stir until smooth. Add flour,
pepper and cream cheese; stir until smooth.  Stir in Worcestershire
sauce, soy sauce and sour cream.  Serve warm or chilled on crackers.
Nanc Kenney
