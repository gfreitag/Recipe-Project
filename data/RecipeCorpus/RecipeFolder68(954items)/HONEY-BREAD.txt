Title: HONEY BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM------------------------MEDIUM LOAF-----------------------------
2/3 c  Water
2 ts Vegetable oil
3 tb Honey
3/4 ts Salt
3/4 c  Oatmeal
1 1/4 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
This bread is sweet but not too sweet. It is not a tall loaf. We like
it a lot and plan to make it often, perhaps adding some sunflower
seeds next time. From the Donna German Bread Machine Cookbook #2.
