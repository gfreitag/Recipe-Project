Title: HOSPITAL RECIPE
Categories: Seasonings, Herb/spice
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Parsley
2 1/2 ts Rosemary
2 1/2 ts Paprika
4 1/2 ts Chives
2 1/2 ts Onion powder
3 tb Marjoram
3 tb Basil
3 tb Thyme
Grind in a morat and pestle and bottle.
