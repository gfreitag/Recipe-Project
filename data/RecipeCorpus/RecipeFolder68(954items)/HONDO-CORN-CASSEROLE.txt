Title: HONDO CORN CASSEROLE
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Cream corn
1    16 oz. can whole kernal corn
1 c  Grated cheddar cheese
1 c  Ritz crackers, crushed
1    Onion, chopped
1    Egg
3 tb Sugar
2/3 c  Milk
1/4 lb Margarine
Salt & pepper to taste
Tabasco sauce to taste
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix all ingredients in large bowl. Bake
in a 3-qt baking dish for 50 to 60 min.
