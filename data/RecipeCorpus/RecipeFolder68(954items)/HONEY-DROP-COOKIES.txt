Title: HONEY DROP COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Melted butter or butter
-substitute
1 3/4 c  Flour
1    Egg, well beaten
1/8 ts Cloves
3/4 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Chopped raisins
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
Heat honey.  Add butter.  Add spices.  Cool.  Add egg. Sift flour,
measure, and sift with salt and baking soda. Add to honey mixture.
Add raisins. Mix thoroughly.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto well-oiled
baking sheet. Bake in hot oven (400 F) about 15 minutes. 30 servings.
