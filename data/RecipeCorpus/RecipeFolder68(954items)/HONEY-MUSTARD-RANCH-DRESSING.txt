Title: HONEY MUSTARD RANCH DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
1 ea Ranch dressing mix, pkg
4 tb Dijon mustard
1/2 c  Honey
Mix thoroughly.  Allow at least one hour for flavors to blend. Serve
as a salad dressing or as a condiment for chicken or fish.
