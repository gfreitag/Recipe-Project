Title: HONEY GINGER CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Shortening
3 tb Margarine or butter
1/3 c  Gold Medal all-purpose
-flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Pepper
3 lb Chicken drumsticks and
-thighs
1/3 c  Honey
1/3 c  Chili sauce 1/3 Cup soy
-sauce
t   ground ginger
/2

Heat oven to 425!. Heat shortening and margarine in rectangular pan,
13 x 9 x 2 inches, in oven until melted. Mix flour, salt, V2 teaspoon
ginger and the pepper. Coat chicken thoroughly with flour mixture.
Place chicken, skin sides down, in pan. Bake uncovered 30 minutes;
turn chicken. Bake 15 minutes longer; remove chicken. Drain fat from
pan. Line pan with aluminum foil. Return chicken to pan.

Mix remaining ingredients; pour over chicken. Bake uncovered 15
minutes, spooning honey mixture onto chicken every 5 minutes. Serve
immediately or refrigerate and serve chilled. 6 servings.
