Title: HONEY TARTS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 24 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Water
2    Eggs, beaten
1 tb Honey
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Boil sugar and water to a light syrup.  Cool.  Add eggs, honey and
nutmeg and beat thoroughly.  Fill unbaked tart shells 2/3 full. Bake
for 20 minutes at 400F. Source: Chatelaine Golden Anniversary ch.
