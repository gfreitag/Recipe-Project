Title: HONEY MUSTARD CHICKEN CASSEROLE
Categories: Poultry, Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Boneless chicken breasts
1 tb Vegetable oil
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1    Jar Chicken Tonight Light
-Cooking Sauce for Chicken
-Honey Mustard
1 lb Red potatoes, boiled and
-thinly sliced
10 oz Frozen broccoli spears,
-cooked and drained
1/2 c  Shredded low-fat
-Cheddar cheese
Preheat oven to 350 F. In a large skillet, lightly brown chicken in
oil on both sides. Add garlic and saut=E9 lightly. Add sauce; simmer,
covered, 20 minutes over low heat. Place cooked potato slices evenly
in an 11 x 7-inch baking dish. Spoon chicken and sauce over potatoes.
Evenly layer broccoli over chicken. Top with Cheddar cheese. Bake,
covered, 30 minutes or until bubbly.
