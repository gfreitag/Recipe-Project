Title: HONEY MUSTARD (FROM JANE BRODY'S GOOD FOOD GO
Categories: Mustard, Condiment, Gifts
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Coarse-grain Dijon-style
-mustard
2 tb Cider vinegar
2 tb Honey
1 1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
In a small bowl, whisk together the ingredients until they are
thouroughly combined.  If desired, the mustard can be made hotter by
adding cayenne.
