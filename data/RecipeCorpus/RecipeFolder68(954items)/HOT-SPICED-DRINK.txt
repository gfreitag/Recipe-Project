Title: HOT SPICED DRINK
Categories: Beverages, Microwave
Yield: 4 Servings
1 qt Milk
1/4 c  Molasses
4    (3-inch) cinnamon sticks
Whipped cream (sweetened)
Nutmeg
1. In a 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic pitcher or casserole,
combine milk and molasses. Add cinnamon sticks. 2. Heat, uncovered,
in Microwave Oven 6 minutes or until hot. Remove cinnamon sticks. 3.
Serve in mugs, garnished with whipped cream and a sprinkling of
nutmeg.
