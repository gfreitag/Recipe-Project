Title: HONEY MINTS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Warm honey
2 3/4 c  Powdered milk (non-instant)
4    Drops oil of peppermint
Green food coloring
Mix ingredients and knead until all milk is absorbed. Pull like
taffy,stretch, cut.

Origin: Homemaking Booklet, Mormon Church, 1978 Shared by: Sharon
Stevens
