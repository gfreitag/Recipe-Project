Title: HOT SPINACH & MUSHROOM SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings    1 1/2 lb spinach
2 TB corn oil
4 oz mushrooms -- slice thin
1 c  onions -- diced
4 ts lemon juice -- fresh
4 ts white vinegar
2 ts sugar
black pepper -- fresh
ground
:
:
2 TB yogurt -- plain, low-fat

Wash fresh spinach and remove all tough stems; drain well. In hot oil,
saute mushrooms and onions until onions are wilted. Add lemon juice,
vinegar, sugar and pepper; mix well. Stir in spinach and cook only
until spinach begins to wilt.  Remove from heat and stir in yogurt.
Serve immediately. Jo Anne Merrill, recipe from my personal files.
