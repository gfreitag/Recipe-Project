Title: HOT PEPPER & GARLIC SPAGHETTI
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
3 ea Chili peppers, fresh or
-- dried, chopped
9 ea Garlic cloves, chopped
1/2 c  Water
1 lb Spaghetti
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Parsley, chopped
Salt, to taste
Process the chili peppers, 6 of the garlic cloves & the water in a
food processor or blender until it makes a pungent, soup-like
mixture. Take care when opening up the top, as the fumes will be
strong. Bring a large pan of boiling salted water to the boil. Pour
the chili & garlic mixture through a sieve into the boiling water;
save or discard the solids as desired. Add the pasta to this seasoned
boiling water & cook until *al dente*. Drain. Toss the hot, drained
pasta with the olive oil & the parsley. Season with salt & serve.
