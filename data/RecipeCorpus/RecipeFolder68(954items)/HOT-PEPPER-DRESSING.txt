Title: HOT PEPPER DRESSING
Categories: Cajun, Dressings
Yield: 1 Cup
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
2 tb Olive oil
1 ts Hot pepper flakes
1 ts Hot sauce
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Black pepper
Combine vinegar, oil, red pepper flakes, hot sauce, garlic powder and
black pepper in a small bowl or cruet. Mix well. Use as a dressing
for you favorite salad.

Prep. time: 10 minutes
