Title: HONEY SOAP
Categories: Misc, Ref
Yield: 1 Servings  Take four ounces (125 g) of white soap and as much honey, half an
ounce (15 g) of salt of tartar and two or three drachms (10 ml) of
the distilled Water of fumitory; mix the whole together. Tis soap
cleanses the skin well, and renderes it delicately white and smooth.
It is also used advantageously to efface the marks of burns and
scalds. (The Toilet of Flora".) From Hemphill's Herb Collection
