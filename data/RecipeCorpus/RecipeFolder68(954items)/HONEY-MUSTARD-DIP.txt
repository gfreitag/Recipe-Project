Title: HONEY-MUSTARD DIP
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Cup
1 c  Yogurt Cheese
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 tb Honey
3 pk Sweet 'N Low
In a small bowl, combine all ingredients; stir until well blended.
Stir in a few tbs chicken broth or low-fat milk to thin for sauces or
salad dressings.

Savor the Flavor by Gail L. Becker ISBN 0-517-10026-6 pg 95
