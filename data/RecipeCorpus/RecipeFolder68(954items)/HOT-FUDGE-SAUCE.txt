Title: HOT FUDGE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Margarine
3 tb Cocoa
1/2 c  Boiling water
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Corn syrup
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 pn Salt
Melt margarine, add cocoa and stir over very low heat until melted.
Add boiling water slowly, stirring constantly and bring to a boil.
Add sugar and syrup. Stir and cook for 5 min. Add vanilla and salt.
Serve hot or cold on ice cream, cornstarch pudding, etc.
