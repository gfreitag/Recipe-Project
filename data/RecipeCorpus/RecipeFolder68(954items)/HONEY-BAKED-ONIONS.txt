Title: HONEY BAKED ONIONS
Categories: Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 12 Servings
6    Yellow onions, peeled
-and cut in half
Honey
2 tb Butter
Place cut side up in microwave pan.  Dribble with honey and dot with
butter.  Add a little water to the pan and cover with plastic.

Microwave on high for 8-10 minutes.  Turn 1/4 after 3 minutes, Turn
1/4 after 3 minutes.
