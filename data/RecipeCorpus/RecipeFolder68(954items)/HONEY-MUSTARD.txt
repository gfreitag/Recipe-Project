Title: HONEY MUSTARD
Categories: Sauces, Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Dry mustard
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/4 c  Cider or red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Oil
1 ds Worcestershire sauce
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl with a wire whisk or beater,
till smooth.  Put into a small non-metal container and refrigerate
overnight. Stir before serving.
