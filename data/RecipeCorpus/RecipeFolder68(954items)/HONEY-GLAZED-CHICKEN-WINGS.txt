Title: HONEY-GLAZED CHICKEN WINGS
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry, Holiday
Yield: 8 Appetizers
MMMMM------------------------NORMA WRENN-----------------------------
2 lb Chicken wings
1 c  Honey
1/2 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Sesame or vegetable oil
2 tb Fresh gingerroot; minced
2 tb Scallions; minced
2 tb Catsup
1    Clove garlic; minced
Cut off and discard wingtips; cut wings in half at joint. Place wing
pieces in a 13- x 9- x 2-inch baking dish.

Combine honey, soy sauce, oil, fresh gingerroot, scallions, catsup,
and minced garlic in a small bowl; pour mixture over wing pieces,
turning to coat.  Cover and chill 1 hour.

Transfer wings to a lightly greased 15- x 10- x 1-inch jellyroll pan;
bake uncovered, at 375 degrees for 45 minutes, turning once. Transfer
to a serving platter. Yield 8 to 10 appetizer servings.

Source: Southern Living 1991 Annual Recipes
