Title: HONEY & OATS BREAD (1 1/2 LB. LOAF)
Categories: Bread, Breads
1 c  milk -- 75-85 degrees F
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lg egg
3 TB honey
2 TB sweet butter
1 c  rolled oats
2 3/4 c  bread flour
1 1/2 ts salt
1 1/2 ts active dry yeast

Add all ingredients to bread pan and bake at light crust 1 1/2 lb.
loaf. Found at Betty Crocker Breadmaker WWW site.
