Title: HORSEY SAUCE
Categories: Vegetarian, Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Water
8 oz Silken tofu
1 ts Freshly grated horseradish
1/3 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Honey
2 ts Olive oil
Place all ingredients in a blender. Puree on high until smooth and
creamy.

Serve as a dipping sauce with turnip chips.

Per serving (1/4 cup): 71 cal, 5 g prot, 5 mg sod, 3 g carb, 5 g
fat, 0 chol, 106 mg calcium

Hint:  If using preserved or creamed horseradish, you may want to
increase the amount used. Remember that horseradish is rather
pungent--so add according to your taste.

From _Vegetarian Gourmet_, Winter 1992
