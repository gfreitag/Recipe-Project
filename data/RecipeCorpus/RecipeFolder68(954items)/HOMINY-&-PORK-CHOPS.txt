Title: HOMINY & PORK CHOPS
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Shoulder pork steaks*
1 c  Chicken broth,reg strength
4    Large carrots,thinly sliced
1 cn Golden hominy,drained (15oz)
Chopped parsley
Salt
Pepper
MMMMM--------------------PINK PICKLED ONIONS-------------------------
1    Medium-size red onion
1/4 c  White wine vinegar
2 tb Sugar
1/3 c  Water
* - fat trimmed
=======================================================
============== ===
1. Place pork in a 10-12" frying pan; cover tightly. Cook over medium
heat for 20 minutes. Uncover; cook over high heat, turning pork
occasionally until liquid evaporates and meat browns, 5-10 minutes.
Drain off fat.
2. Add broth, carrots and hominy. Cover and simmer until meat is
tender when pierced, 20-25 minutes. Transfer pork and hominy mixture
to a platter; top with pink pickled onions and parsley. Add salt and
pepper to taste.

*** PINK PICKLED ONIONS ***
Thinly slice onion; rinse well. Mix onion with vinegar, sugar, and
water. Cover and chill for 30 minutes to 1 hour; drain.
