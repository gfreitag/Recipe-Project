Title: HONEY WHEAT BREAD (FOR MACHINE)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/8 c  Water, lukewarm
2 1/2 c  Flour (Bread)
1/2 c  Flour, Wheat
1 1/2 tb Dry Milk
1 1/2 tb Honey
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
3 ts Yeast (active dry)
-or-
2 ts Yeast (fast rise)
Put the ingredients in the machine according to manufacturer's
recommendation.
6 Servings = Large Loaf, reduce to 4 or 3 if your machine is smaller
