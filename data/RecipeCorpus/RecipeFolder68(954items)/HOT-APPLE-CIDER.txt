Title: HOT APPLE CIDER
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 8 Servings
4 c  Apple Juice, or cider
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
3 c  Orange Juice
3 tb Lemon Juice
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1    3 In. Cinnamon Stick
Mix together. Bring to boil. Simmer. Can be made in 30 cup coffee pot.
