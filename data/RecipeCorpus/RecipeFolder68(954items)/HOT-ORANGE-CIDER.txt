Title: HOT ORANGE CIDER
Categories: Beverages, Crocker
Yield: 12 Servings
12 oz Frozen orange juice
-concentrate
1 1/2 qt Apple cider
1    Orange, cut into 12 slices
Prepare orange juice as directed on can. Heat orange juice and apple
cider just to boiling over low heat. Pour about 1 cup orange juice
mixture into each mug. Stir in 2 ts rum if desired; top with orange
slice. Garnish with stick cinnamon if desired.  12 servings;135
calories per serving.

Source: Betty Crocker's Cookbook, 6th Edition
