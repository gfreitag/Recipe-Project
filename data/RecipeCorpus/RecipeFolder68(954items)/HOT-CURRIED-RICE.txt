Title: HOT CURRIED RICE
Categories: Side dish, Indian, Rice, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Ghee
2 md Yellow onions, thinly
-- sliced
3/4 ts Ginger, thinly sliced
3 ea Green chiles, seeded &
-- halved
1 ea Bay leaf
2 c  Mixed vegetables, cut into
-- bite-sized pieces
2 c  Basmati rice
1/2 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Cayenne
1/2 ts Garam masala
1 1/4 ts Cumin, ground
1 1/4 ts Coriander, ground
2 ts Salt
3 c  Vegetable stock
Heat ghee in a large pot & fry onionns until they are lightly
browned. Add ginger, chiles, bay leaf & vegetables & cook, stirring
for about 2 minutes. Add the rice, turmeric, cayenne, garam masala,
cumin, coriander & salt. Stir in the stock & bring to a boil, allow
to boil hard for 2 minutes. Reduce heat & simmer for 12 to 15
minutes. Remove from heat & let stand for 10 minutes.  Fluff rice
with a fork & serve.

Pranati Sen Gupta, "The Art of Indian Cuisine"
