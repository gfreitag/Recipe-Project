Title: HOT MUSTARD SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Holiday, Pork
Yield: 8 Servings
2 ts Mustard prepared, heaping
1 ts Butter
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Cream
1 ds Cinnamon
1 ds Salt
1 ts Flour
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Apple Cider Vinegar
1 ds Nutmeg
1 ds Cayenne Pepper
Beat eggs.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cook in double boiler,
stirring constantly until mixture thickens. This is great served with
a ham. It is also good with turkey on sandwiches, with some of the
leftover dressing.
