Title: HOT MUSHROOM SANDWICH FILLING
Categories: Healthy and, Sandwiches, Vegetables
Yield: 3 Servings
30 md Mushrooms; sliced
1/2 c  Mozzarella cheese, part skim
1/4 c  Parsley, fresh; minced
1/4 c  Italian salad dressing, fat
Recipe by: Light and Easy Cooking (Oxford House) Mix all ingredients
in shallow 8x8 baking dish. Microwave on high, uncovered, for 3-4
minutes until cheese melts.

Serve open faced on whole grain bread.
