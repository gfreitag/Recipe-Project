Title: HOT BEEF SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1 tb Butter
2 tb Dry white wine
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 1/2 oz Dried beef; finely chopped
1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
Saute onion in butter in a skillet until tender. Stir in wine;
simmer 2 minutes.  Add cream cheese, sour cream, mayonnaise and beef;
mix well.
Spoon mixture into an 8-in. square baking dish; sprinkle with
pecans. Bake at 350 deg. for 15 to 20 minutes. Serve with assorted
crackers. Yield: 4 cups
