Title: HONEY ROASTED PEANUTS
Categories: Snacks, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Peanuts
2 tb Butter
2 tb Honey
Preheat oven to 350F. Place peanuts in bowl. Heat butter and honey
together. Toss with peanuts. Lay on cookie sheet and bake at 350F for
5 to 10 minutes or until peanuts are golden brown. MAKES:2 Cups

Source: The Toronto Sun, Lucy Waverman's column, a recipe from the
Rose & Crown pub in Toronto. posted by Anne MacLellan
