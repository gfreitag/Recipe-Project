Title: HOT MULLED CIDER
Categories: Beverages, Microwave
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Apple cider or juice
1    Whole clove to
2    Whole clove
1    1 inch cinnamon stick
Whipped cream
Nutmeg
1. Combine apple cider, whole cloves and cinnamon in a heat-
resistant, non-metallic mug, cup or glass. 2. Heat, uncovered, in
Microwave Oven 2 to 3 minutes. 3. Garnish with a dollop of whipped
cream and a sprinkling of nutmeg.
