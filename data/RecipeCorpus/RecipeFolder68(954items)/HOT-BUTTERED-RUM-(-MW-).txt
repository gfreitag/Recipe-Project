Title: HOT BUTTERED RUM ( MW )
Categories: Microwave, Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Brown Sugar
2 ts Butter Or Margarine,Softened
Dash Ground Cinnamon
Dash Ground Nutmeg
3/4 c  Warm Water
3 tb Rum
Lemon Slices (Optional)
In a nonmetal mug stir together the brown sugar, butter or margarine,
cinnamon and nutmeg.  Stir in the warm water. Micro-cook, uncovered,
on 100% power for 1 to 1 1/2 minutes or till steaming hot. Stir in
the rum. Garnish with lemon slices, if desired.
