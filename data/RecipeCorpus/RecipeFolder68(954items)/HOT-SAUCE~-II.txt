Title: HOT SAUCE~ II
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Bacon grease
2 cn Whole tomatoes
Black pepper to taste
1 ts Oregano
2 ea Onions, minced
6 ea Jalapeno peppers, cleaned>>>
Seeded & chopped fine.
1 ts Cumin seed
Salt to taste
Saute onions in grease.  Add tomatoes and jalapeno. Add all
ingreds. in blender and blend.  Pour into saucepan and cook on low
heat for 45 min.
