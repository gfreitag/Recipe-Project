Title: HONEY DIJON SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/8 c  Honey
1/8 c  Dijon mustard
1/8 ts Curry powder
1/8 ts Celery seed
1/4 c  White vinegar
Mix the honey and the mustard and adjust to taste. Add in the curry
powder and the celery seed and mix. Finally, mix in the vinegar.
