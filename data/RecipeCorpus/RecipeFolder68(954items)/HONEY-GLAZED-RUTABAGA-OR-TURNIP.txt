Title: HONEY-GLAZED RUTABAGA OR TURNIP
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Rutabaga or turnips
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Liquid honey
1/4 ts Ground ginger
Salt and pepper
Peel rutabaga and cut into 1/2 inch thick slices; halve or quarter
slices. In pot of boiling salted water, cook rutabaga for 15 minutes
(turnips for 8 minutes) or just until tender; drain.

Stir in butter; cook over high heat, shaking pan often, for 1 minute
or until coated.  Stir in honey, ginger, and salt and pepper to
taste; cook, stirring often, for 1 minute or until glazed. Makes 4
servings Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Canadian
Living's Best Vegetables.
