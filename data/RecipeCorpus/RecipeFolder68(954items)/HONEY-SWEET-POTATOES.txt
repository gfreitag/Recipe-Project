Title: HONEY SWEET POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2/3 c  Honey
1/2 c  Burtter
1 ts Salt
8    Sweet potatoes
In a well-greased baking dish, spoon the combined honey, butter, and
salt between layers of sliced sweet potato. Bake in a moderate (350^)
oven for 30 minutes, basting frequently.
