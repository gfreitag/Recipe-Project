Title: HOT CHOCOLATE MILK
Categories: Beverages, Chocolate, Microwave
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Chocolate syrup to
2 tb Chocolate syrup
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
3/4 c  Milk
1. Combine chocolate syrup and vanilla in a heat-resistant,
non-metallic mug or cup. 2. Stir in milk and heat, uncovered, in
Microwave Oven 1 1/2 to 2 1/2 minutes or until hot. Do not allow milk
to boil.
