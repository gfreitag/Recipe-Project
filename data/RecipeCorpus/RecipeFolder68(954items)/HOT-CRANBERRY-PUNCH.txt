Title: HOT CRANBERRY PUNCH
Categories: Try it, Crockpot, Beverages
Yield: 10 Servings
MMMMM------------------RIVAL CROCK-POT COOKING-----------------------
4 c  Unsweetened pineapple juice
4 c  Cranberry juice
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1 c  Water
1 ts Whole cloves
1    Cinnamon stick
Tie cloves and cinnamon stick in cheesecloth. Combine all ingredients
in crock-Pot. Cover and cook on Low setting for 4 to 10 hours. Serve
hot, in punch cups.
