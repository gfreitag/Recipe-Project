Title: HOMEMADE SWEETENED CONDENSED MILK
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Water
3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/4 c  Instant skim milk
Heat water and sugar in medium saucepan until sugar dissolves,
approximately 5 minutes.  Place instant milk in food processer or
blender. Pour water\sugar mixture in blender\processor. Process until
well blended. Makes about 1 1/2 cups.  Store, covered, in
refrigerator if not used immediately.  Can be used in all recipes.
