Title: HONEY CORN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 9 Servings
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Yellow cornmeal
1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2    Eggs
1 c  Whipping cream
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Honey
In a bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
In a small mixing bowl, beat the eggs. Add cream, oil, and honey;
beat well. Stir into the dry ingredients just until moistened. Pour
into a greased 9-inch square baking pan.  Bake at 400 degrees for
20-25 minutes or until a wooden pick inserted in the center comes out
clean.
