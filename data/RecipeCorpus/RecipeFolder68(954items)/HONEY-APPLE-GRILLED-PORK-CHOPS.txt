Title: HONEY APPLE GRILLED PORK CHOPS
Categories: Grilled, Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
4 ea Pork Loin Chops (8 oz each)
1 1/2 c  Apple Cider
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
2 tb Honey
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
1/4 ts Pepper
Combine all ingredients, EXCEPT pork chops.  Mix well. Place chops in
a shallow dish; pour marinade over chops. Cover and refrigerate
overnight, turning meat occasionally.

Remove pork chops from marinade.  Place on grill approximately six
inches above low to medium coals. Grill for 40 to 50 minutes, turning
and basting with marinade every 10 to 15 minutes.

Source:  Best Barbecue Recipes by Mildred Fischer
