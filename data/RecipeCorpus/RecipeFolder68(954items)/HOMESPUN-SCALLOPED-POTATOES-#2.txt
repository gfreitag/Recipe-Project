Title: HOMESPUN SCALLOPED POTATOES #2
Categories: Potato cass, Try soon
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Cream cheese, cubed
1 1/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
4 c  Potatoes, thinly sliced
2 ts Chives, chpd
In lg saucepan, combine cream cheese, milk, salt and pepper; stir
over low heat until smooth. Add potatoes and chives; mix lightly.
Spoon into 1 1/2 quart casserole; cover. Bake 350 deg, 1 hour, 10
mins or until potatoes are tender. Stir before serving. Can make this
ahead but don't cook, refrigerate. When ready to serve, bake as
directed.
