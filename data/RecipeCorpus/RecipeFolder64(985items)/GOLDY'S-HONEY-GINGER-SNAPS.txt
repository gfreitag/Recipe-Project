Title: GOLDY'S HONEY-GINGER SNAPS
Categories: Cookies, Goldilocks
Yield: 48 Servings
1/2 c  Honey
8 tb Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cloves
Set oven temp to 375F. Cream honey, butter, sugar and egg. Sift
together and the flour, soda, salt, and spices. Chill one hour. Roll
level tablespoons of dough into balls and place 2 inches apart on
greased cookie sheet. Bake 10 to 12 minutes. Makes 4 dozen.

: Diane Mott Davidson. Catering to Nobody
