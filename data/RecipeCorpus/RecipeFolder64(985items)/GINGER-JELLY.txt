Title: GINGER JELLY
Categories: Jams
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Finely chopped peeled ginger
1    Apple peeled
- cored and finely chopped
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS in a heavy pot, place over medium heat and
cook until the mixture begins to thicken, about 30 minutes. To can,
follow regular canning instructions. Makes 3 Cups
