Title: GIRL SCOUT THIN MINT COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Ritz crackers
1    Wilton dark chocolate candy
-melts
Peppermint oil
Recipe by: www.topsecretrecipes.com/hotmail.htm In double boiler melt
the candy melts. Add several drops of peppermint oil to the melted
chocolate. Dip Ritz crackers, one at a time in chocolate/mint
mixture. Place on waxed paper to dry.

Believe it or not, these taste exactly like the real thing!
