Title: GO-LITE: TOMATO SOUP
Categories: Soups, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Olive oil
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1 lg Onion, chopped
1 c  Celery, sliced
3 c  Tomato juice
1 c  Chicken stock
1 tb Red wine vinegar
4 lg Tomatoes, chopped
1    Zucchini, sliced (optl)
1/4 c  Fresh basil, chopped
2 tb Fresh parsley, chopped
2 tb Fresh oregano, chopped
1/4 ts Pepper
In a large saucepan, heat oil over medium heat; cook garlic, onion and
celery for about 5 minutes or until tender. Add tomato juice, chicken
stock and vinegar. Bring to boil; reduce heat and simmer gently for
10 minutes. Stir in tomatoes and zucchini; simmer for 5 minutes.

Add basil, parsley, oregano and pepper; simmer for 2 minutes.

Per serving: about 85 calories, 3 g Protein, 3 g fat,
14 g  carbohydrate
