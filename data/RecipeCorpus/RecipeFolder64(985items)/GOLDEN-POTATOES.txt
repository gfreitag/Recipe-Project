Title: GOLDEN POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 5 Servings
1/2 c  Cornflake Crumbs
1 ts Salt
5 md Potatoes, pared
2 tb Butter, melted
Heat oven to 375Ã¸.  Mix cornflake crumbs and salt. Brush
potatoes with butter, coat with crumbs. Arrange in ungreased baking
pan, 9x9x2". Bake uncovered 1 to 1 1/4 hours or until potatoes are
tender.
