Title: GHOSTLY  GREEN  BREW
Categories: New, Text, Import
2 l  chilled ginger ale
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 ga lime sherbet

Allow sherbet to soften for about 1 hour in bottom of punch bowl. Pour
ginger ale over and stir until all sherbet is dissolved. For a great
Halloween treat, serve in a large well-cleaned pumpkin with a great
face painted in it!
