Title: GINGER CHICKEN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Main dish, Dkuhnen msn
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lb Roasted or bbq'd chicken
12 oz Spinach or romaine lettuce
1 c  Bean sprouts
1    Yellow bell pepper
Seeded and slivered
1/2 c  Toasted almonds
3/4 c  Thinly sliced sweet red
Ginger
Optional...Enoki Mushrooms
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
3 tb Red wine vinegar
1 tb Light soy sauce
2 tb Oriental sesame oil
1 tb Juice from a jar of sweet
Red ginger
2 ts Hoisin sauce
1/4 ts Chinese chili sauce
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Minced green onions
1 tb Finely minced ginger
Discard chicken skin.  Remove chicken from bones and cut into
bite-sized pieces.  Set aside.  Bunch greens together and cut into
thin slivers. Place all salad ingredients into a large bowl. Mix
dressing well. Toss with salad and serve immediately. Source: TV
Guide Magazine, July 20th, 1996.
