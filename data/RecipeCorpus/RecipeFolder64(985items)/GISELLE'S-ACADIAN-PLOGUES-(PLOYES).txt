Title: GISELLE'S ACADIAN PLOGUES (PLOYES)
Categories: Breakfast/b, Pancakes/wa, Recipes fro, Breakfast
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  White buckwheat flour
1 c  Regular flour
4 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Cold water
1/2 c  Boiling water
Mix dry ingredients. Add cold water and let stand for 10 minutes. Add
boiling water and drop to make thin 6" pancakes on hot griddle,
400=B0. Bake on one side only, until bubbled and firm. Serve on warm
platter, cover with napkins.
