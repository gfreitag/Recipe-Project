Title: GOOSNARGH
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 36 Servings
4 c  Flour
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1 ts Coriander
1 3/4 c  Butter
Sift dry ingredients together three times, add the butter and work
into a firm dough.  The hands work for this. Knead on a lightly
floured board and roll to 1/8" thick. Cut with a 3 inch cutter and
bake at 325 until lightly browned, (about 10 minutes).  Dust with
confectioners sugar.
