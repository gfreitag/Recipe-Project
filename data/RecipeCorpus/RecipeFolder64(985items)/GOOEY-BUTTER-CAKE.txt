Title: GOOEY BUTTER CAKE
Categories: Marie, Desserts, Kids love, Great
Yield: 12 Servings
2 pk Yellow cake mix
1/2 lb Butter melted
4    Eggs
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 lb Powdered sugar
2    Eggs
8 oz Cream cheese
Combine the first three ingredients.  Mix well.  Place into a 9x13
pan. Press the layer in evenly.

Beat the next 3 ingredients together very well, until very light.
Pour over the top of the dough layer in the baking pan.

Bake 45 - 60 minutes at 350x.
