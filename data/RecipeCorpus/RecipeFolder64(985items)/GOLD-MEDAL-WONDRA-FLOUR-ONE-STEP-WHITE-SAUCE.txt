Title: GOLD MEDAL WONDRA FLOUR ONE-STEP WHITE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Cold milk
2 tb Gold Medal Wondra Flour
2 tb Margarine or butter
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Heat all ingredients to boiling over medium heat, stirring
constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute.  1 cup sauce. CHEESE SAUCE: Add
1/4 teaspoon dry mustand and stir 1/2 cup shredded Chedder or
American cheese into hot sauce until melted. High altitude (3500 to
6500 feet): For Cheese Sauce, stir cheese into sauce over low heat
until melted.
