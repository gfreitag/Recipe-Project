Title: GINGER GARLIC BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Cloves garlic
1/4    Inch thick slice of
Peeled fresh gingerroot
1    Stick (1/2 cup) unsalted
-butter, softened
1 12"    baguette

In a small food processor or blender puree the garlic and the
gingerroot with the butter until the mixture is smooth. Cut the
baquette lengthwise with a serrated knife, spread the butter mixture
onto the cut sides of the baguette, and broil the baguette, cut sides
up, under a preheated broiler about 4 inches from heat for 3 minutes,
or until the tops are golden brown. Serves 4.
