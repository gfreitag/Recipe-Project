Title: GOOD MORNING PIZZA
Categories: Breads, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
Buttermilk baking mix
4    Eggs, lightly beaten
2 sm Sausages, cut into 1/2 inch
-pieces
2    Slices crisp cooked bacon,
-crumbled
2    Slices cooked ham, cut into
-thin strips
1    Fresh tomato, diced
2 c  Grated American cheese
Preheat oven to 350Ã¸degrees.  Prepare biscuit mix for 1 large pizza
crust according to amounts and directions on package. Roll or pat
into a 10-12 inch round pan or 10 x 7 x 1 1/4 inch oblong pan. Bake
crust according to box directions.

Meanwhile prepare scrambled eggs; remove from pan. Set aside. Saute
sausage, bacon and ham.  Place scrambled eggs on baked crust; scatter
cooked meat across eggs. Sprinkle chopped tomatoes and grated cheese
across top.  Bake for 7 minutes, or until cheese has melted. Serves 6
