Title: GOLDEN BBQ SAUCES
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Prepared mustard
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 ts Rosemary leaves
Mix all ingredients. Baste meat during the last half of cooking
period. Just before serving, top meat with remaining sauce.
