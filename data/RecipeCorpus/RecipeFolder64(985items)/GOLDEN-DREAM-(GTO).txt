Title: GOLDEN DREAM (GTO)
Categories: Mixed, Drinks, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 oz Galliano
1/2 oz Creme de cacao
1/2 oz Triple sec
1/2 oz Orange juice
1 1/2 oz Heavy cream
Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add galliano, creme de cacao,
triple sec, orange juice, and cream. Shake and strain into a highball
glass filled with ice.
