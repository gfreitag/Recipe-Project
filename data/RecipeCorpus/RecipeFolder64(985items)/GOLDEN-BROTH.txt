Title: GOLDEN BROTH
Categories: Digest, Jan.
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Onion chopped
1    Clove garlic
1/2 c  Yellow split peas.
1/2 ts Turmeric
2 qt Hot water
Put all ingredients in a large pot and simmer at least half an hour.
Strain for a thin stock or puree for a thick one (I haven't tried
pureeing).
