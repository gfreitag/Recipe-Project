Title: GRAMMEE FLO'S SPECIAL SPAGHETTI SAUCE
Categories: Meats, Main dishes
Yield: 6 -8  1 can whole tomatoes (crushed up) 1/2 pkg. bacon slices (fry &
crumble) 1/3 to 1/2 pkg. deluxe American slices (cut into small
strips) Season salt and pepper

Combine all and heat until cheese is melted and HOT! Pour over hot
spaghetti or angel hair pasta.
