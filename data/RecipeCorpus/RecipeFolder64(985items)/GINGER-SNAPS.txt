Title: GINGER SNAPS
Categories: Cookies, Appetizers, Desserts
Yield: 120 Servings
-------- CREAM --------
3/4 c  Butter with:
2 c  Sugar, Stir In:
2    Eggs, Well Beaten
1/2 c  Molasses
2 ts Vinegar
------ SIFT AND ADD ------
3 3/4 c  All Purpose Flour
1 1/2 ts Soda
3 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Cloves
þ Mix ingredients until well blended. þ Form dough into 3/4-inch
balls. þ Preheat oven to 325 degrees. þ Bake on a greased cookie
sheet for about 12 minutes. þ As the ball melts down during baking,
the cookie develops the character-
istic crinkled surface. NOTE: A topping to delight the kids is 1/2 a
marshmallow, cut side down, on the almost baked cookies. Return to
oven about 4 minutes. When cool, ice to taste. ANOTHER NOTE: This
makes 10 dozen, 2-inch cookies. ANOTHER NOTE: If you dunk these in
coffee...don't dring the
coffee..
