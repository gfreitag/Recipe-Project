Title: GRANDMA'S BARBQ SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
4 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Black pepper
Pinch of red pepper
1/2 ts Vinegar
1 c  Catsup
2 c  Water
5 dr Lemon juice
Add all ingredients together; bring to boil and pour over ribs and
bake about 2 1/2 hours at 300 degrees or until ribs are done and
sauce is thick.
