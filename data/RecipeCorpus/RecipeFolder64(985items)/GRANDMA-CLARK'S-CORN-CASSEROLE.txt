Title: GRANDMA CLARK'S CORN CASSEROLE
Categories: Veggies, Sidedish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Margarine
14 oz Cream corn
14 oz Kernel corn drained  OR
-frozen kernel corn
1 c  Sour cream or yogurt
2    Eggs, beaten
2 1/2 c  Biscuit mix
1/4 c  Cornmeal
Melt marg in pan.  Spread melted marg to cover pan, then pour into a
bowl. Add remaining ingredients to bowl and blend til just mixed.
Pour into pan and bake for 40 to 50 minutes. Test as you would cake.
Serve warm as a vegetable or as potato replacement
