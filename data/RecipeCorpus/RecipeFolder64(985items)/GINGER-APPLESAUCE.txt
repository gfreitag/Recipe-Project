Title: GINGER APPLESAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
10 md Granny Smith Apples *
2    2-1/2" Cinnamon Sticks
1 c  Apple Juice
1 ts Grated Lemon Zest
1/3 c  Packed Brown Sugar
1 1/2 tb Fresh Ginger
1/4 ts Grated Nutmeg
* Granny Smith apples should be pared, cored and coarsely chopped.
~------------------------------------------------------
~------------- ~-- Place apples, cinnamon, apple juice and lemon zest
in a large saucepan or Dutch oven. Simmer covered, stirring
occasionally, over medium low heat until apples are soft, 20-25
minutes.  Stir in sugar to taste, the ginger and the nutmeg.  Cook
until sugar is dissolved and sauce thickens, about 5 minutes.  Serve
warm or cold.  Remove cinnamon sticks just before serving. Makes
about 6 cups.  92 calories per 1/2 cup. From: Syd's Cookbook.
