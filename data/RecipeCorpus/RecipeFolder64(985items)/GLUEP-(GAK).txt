Title: GLUEP (GAK)
Categories: Kids
Yield: 1 -some
6 tb Water
1 tb Borax
4 oz Elmers craft glue
-White or colored
Make a solution of the water and borax mixing well. In a seperate
bowl mix 1 Tb glue and 1 Tb water. Stir in a scant 2 ts of the borax
solution into the glue water. Continue stirring until the mixture
gets thick. Knead the Gluep until thick. Roll it Pop it Strech it
Bounce it Put in a plastic bag in the refrigerator to keep pliable.
