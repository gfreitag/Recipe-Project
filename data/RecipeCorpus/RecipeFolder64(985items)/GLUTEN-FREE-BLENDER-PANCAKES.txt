Title: GLUTEN FREE BLENDER PANCAKES
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Buttermilk
2    Eggs
1 tb Cooking Oil
2 tb Brown Sugar
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1 ts Baking Powder
1/2 c  Rice Flour
1/2 c  Potato Flour
1/4 c  Corn Meal
pn Salt
Place Milk, Eggs and Oil in Blender. Blend at low speed. Add Sugar
and Dry Ingredients. Pour 6" rounds into greased skillet or non-stick
pan. Flip when bubbles appear through out and edge of `cake looks
dull. Add more milk for thinner pancakes or more flour for thicker
ones.
