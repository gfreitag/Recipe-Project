Title: GIANT HONEY & OAT COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 18 Each
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Shortening, softened
2/3 c  Honey
3    Egg whites
4 c  Quick-cooking or old fash.
Oats
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease cookie sheet.  Mix sugar,
shortening, honey and egg whites in large bowl. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Drop dough by level 1/4 cupfuls about 3 inches apart
onto cookie sheet. Bake 11 to 14 minutes or until edges are light
brown (centers will be soft). Cool 3 to 4 minutes; remove from
cookies sheet. Cool on wire rack
