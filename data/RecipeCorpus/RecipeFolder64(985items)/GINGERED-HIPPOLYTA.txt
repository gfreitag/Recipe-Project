Title: GINGERED HIPPOLYTA
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 2 Servings
3 md Pears, very ripe, seeds &
-- stems removed
1 tb Lemon juice
1 sm Sliver, ginger
Cut the pears into small chunks.  Put in a blender with the lemon
juice & ginger.  Blend thoroughly at medium speed. Pour into dessert
bowls & serve immediately..  Otherwise, cover the dessert bowls
tightly & store in the refrigerator.  Will keep for 3 to 5 days.
