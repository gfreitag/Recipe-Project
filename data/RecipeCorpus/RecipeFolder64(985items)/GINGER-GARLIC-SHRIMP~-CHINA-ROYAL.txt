Title: GINGER-GARLIC SHRIMP~ CHINA ROYAL
Categories: Garlic, Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
16 ea Jumbo Shrimp, With Shell
1/4 c  Vegetable Oil
1 ea Piece Ginger *
3 ea Cloves Garlic Peeled/Crushed
2 ea Green Onions/Scallions **
1 x  Salt & Pepper To Taste
Ginger should be 1 inch long, peeled and minced. ** Green
*
Onions/Scallions should be trimmed and thinly sliced (Green
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Slit the shrimp up the back with a sharp knife and
remove the vein. Do not shell the shrimp. Heat the oil in a wok or
wide heavy skillet. When the oil is rippling, throw in the shrimp and
stir-fry over high heat for 2 minutes. Drain very well. Return 1/2 T
oil to the wok. Heat. Throw in the shrimp along with the ginger,
garlic, and onions. Stir-fry for 20 seconds. Season with salt and
freshly ground pepper. Stir-fry for an additional 20 seconds. Serve
at once.
