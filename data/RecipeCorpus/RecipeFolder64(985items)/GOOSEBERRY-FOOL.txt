Title: GOOSEBERRY FOOL
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pt Gooseberries, stemmed
Sugar
2 tb Water (to 3 tb)
1 c  Heavy cream, whipped
Put the gooseberries in a nonreactive saucepan with 1/4 cup of sugar
and the water. Cook very gently until the gooseberies are thoroughly
done and soft enough to mash. Put them through a sieve or food mill
and add sugar to taste. Fold the gooseberry puree through the whipped
cream. Chill for several hours.

Source: Time-Life "Good Cook" series, Fruits.
