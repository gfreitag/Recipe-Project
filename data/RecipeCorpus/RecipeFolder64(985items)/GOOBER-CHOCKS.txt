Title: GOOBER CHOCKS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
3    Sticks margarine
1 c  Peanut butter
2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs -- beaten
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 c  Water
3 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Peanuts -- chopped
6 oz Pk semisweet chocolate chips
Cream margarine, peanut butter and sugar; add eggs, vanilla, salt and
cinnamon; add water.  Add flour and baking powder; mix well. Add
peanuts and chocolate chips. Press well into a 14x10 inch or larger
pan; the larger the pan, the thinner the squares will be. Bake in
350F oven for 25 - 35 minutes or until firm. Cool; cut into squares.

: Bell's Best #2 (Mississippi)
