Title: GOURMET BEEF TENDERLOINS **
Categories: Beef, Grill, Cheese, Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
4    Beef tenderloin steaks:
- 1-1/2 to 2" thick
- about 6-8 oz each
MMMMM----------------HERBED GARLIC CHEESE TOPPING---------------------
7 oz Herb & garlic cream cheese
2 tb Half & half
1/2 ts Coarsely ground black pepper
2 1/2 oz Sliced mushrooms; drained
Heat grill. When ready to grill, place steaks on gas grill over
medium heat or charcoal grill 4-6" from medium coals. Cook 14-25
minutes depending on desired doneness, turning once. In small
saucepan, combine cheese spread, half & half and pepper; blend well.
Cook over medium heat until cheese melts and mix is smooth, stirring
constantly. Stir in mushrooms until heated, stirring constantly.
Serve over steaks.
