Title: GOOD REASONS ITALIAN DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Italian, Desserts, Mix
E  *****
Yield: 4 Servings

Yield: 1 servings  1 tb Garlic salt  1 tb Onion powder 1 tb Sugar 2 tb
Oregano  1 ts Pepper  1/4 ts Thyme  1 ts Basil 1 tb Parsley 1/4 ts
Celery salt  2 tb Salt 1 Env Cup of Soup. -Cream of Chicken mix 1/4 c
Cider vinegar  2 tb Water  2/3 c  Oil   Combine all dry ingr. Store
covered at room temp. For dressing: Mix vinegar, water, oil and 2 oz
of the dry mix. Shake well. From: GLORIA PITZER "EATING OUT AT HOME"
(CB018) *Also good as a italian spice blend
