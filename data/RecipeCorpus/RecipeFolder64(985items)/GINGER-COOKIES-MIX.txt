Title: GINGER COOKIES-MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 42 Servings
3 c  Bake it all mix
1/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground allspice
1/4 ts Baking soda
1    Egg
1/4 c  Milk
3/4 c  Molasses
Combine mix, sugar, spices and baking soda. Add egg, milk and
molasses. Beat thoroughly. Drop by spoonfuls onto a cooky sheet. Bake
in a 375 F. oven 12 to 14 minutes. When cool, store in a covered
dozen cookies
container. Yield: 3-1/2
