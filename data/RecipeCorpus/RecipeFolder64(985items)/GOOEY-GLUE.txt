Title: GOOEY GLUE
Categories: Kids, Information, Crafts
Yield: 1 Batch
-Virginia HCMC24B
-Formatted by L. Crouch
1 c  Love
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
3 tb Cornstarch
4 tb Cold water
2 c  Boiling water
GOOEY GLUE: Mix 3 tb. cornstarch dissolved in the 4 tb. cold water.
Pour into pan with the 2 cups boiling water, stirring constantly
until it becomes clear and thickened. Let cool and pout into a
plastic container.
