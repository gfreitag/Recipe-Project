Title: GINGER LIQUEUR
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 1 Pint
1/2 c  Crystalized ginger
2 c  Vodka
1/2 c  Sugar syrup (see recipe)
Add all ingredients together. Steep for 2 weeks. Strain and its ready
to serve. This liqueur has a real zing to it and will be enjoyed by a
true ginger lover. Makes a unique addition to meat and poultry
Container: Quart jar
marinades. Yield: 1 pint plus
