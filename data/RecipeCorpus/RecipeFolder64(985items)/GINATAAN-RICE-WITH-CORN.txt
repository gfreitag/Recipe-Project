Title: GINATAAN RICE WITH CORN
Categories: Grains/rice, Phillipines
Yield: 3 Servings
1/2 c  Mochi rice
5 c  Coconut milk
1 c  Young cork kernels
Sugar to taste
1 ts Salt
1 c  Coconut cream (first
Extract from grated coconut)
Boil the washed mochi rice in cocout milk until half done. Add corn,
sugar and salt; continue cooking, stirring occasionally until rice
and corn are cooked. Add coconut cream, or use it to tope each serv
ing. ++++- Ginataan Rice with Corn
