Title: GOLDEN ORANGE CAKE USING BASIC CAKE MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Basic Cake Mix
1 1/4 c  Buttermilk
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking soda
3 tb Butter or margarine*
3    Eggs
1 tb Grated orange peel
1 ts Vanilla
2/3 c  Golden raisins -- cut up
1/3 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1    Orange Glaze (Follows)
Heat oven to 350. Grease and flour 9-cup bundt

sugar, baking soda, butter, eggs, orange peel and vanilla in large
mixer bowl an low speed, scraping bowl constantly, 3O seconds. Beat
on medium speed, scraping bowl occasionally, 4 minutes. Beat in
raisins and walnuts. Bake until wooden pick inserted in center comes
out clean, 50 to 55 minutes. Remove from pan; cool.  Drizzle with
glaze. ORANGE GLAZE Mix 3 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted, I
