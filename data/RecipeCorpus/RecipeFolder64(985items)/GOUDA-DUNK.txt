Title: GOUDA DUNK
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 2 Cups
2 tb Butter
2    Green onions, finely chopped
2 tb Flour
2 ts Chicken bouillon mix
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 c  Milk
2 1/2 c  Grated Gouda cheese
1 ts Chopped parsley
Assorted fresh vegetables
Cubed bread
Melt butter in a medium saucepan.  Add green onions. Cook until
softened. Blend in flour, bouillon mix and Worcestershire sauce.
Gradually stir in milk.  Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture
comes to a boil and thickens.  Reduce heat, add cheese and stir until
melted. Pour into a small pot over a candle warmer. Serve with
vegetables and bread for dipping. Yield: 2 cups  Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source:Ontario Milk Marketing Board Cookbook
