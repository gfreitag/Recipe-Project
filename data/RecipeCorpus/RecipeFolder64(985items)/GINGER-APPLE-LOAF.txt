Title: GINGER-APPLE LOAF
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk (100g) Crystallised Ginger
2 md Cooking apples
Place in bowl:
2 c  Self raising flour, sifted
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Castor sugar (*)
3/4 c  Natural low-fat yoghurt
60 g  Butter, melted
2    Eggs
Peel, core, and roughly chop apples.

Place in a food processor, and add crystallised ginger. Blend until
ginger is coarsely chopped.

Add to loaf ingredients and mix together thoroughly.

Pour into two well buttered 18cm x 8 cm (7 1/2 x 3 inches) loaf pans.

Bake in a moderately hot oven at 190ø C for approx 40 minutes or
until a skewer when inserted comes out clean.

Turn onto a wire rack and cool. Spread over yoghurt icing and
decorate with extra sliced crystallized ginger.

Yoghurt Icing: Mix together 1 cup icing sugar and 2 tablespoons
natural low-fat yoghurt.ÿ
