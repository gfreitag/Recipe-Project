Title: GRAHAM CRACKER CANDY
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
1/4 lb Butter
10    Large marshmallows
16    Graham crackers (crushed)
1 c  Nuts (chopped)
Boil sugar, milk and butter for 10 minutes. Add marshmallows; beat
until dissolved. Add graham crackers; stir well. Add nuts; mix. Drop
on waxed paper.
