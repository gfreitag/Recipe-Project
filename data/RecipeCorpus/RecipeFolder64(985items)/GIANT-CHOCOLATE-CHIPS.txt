Title: GIANT CHOCOLATE CHIPS
Categories: Desserts, Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 c  Butter - softened
1 1/2 c  White sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
12 oz Chocolate chips
Mix flour, salt, baking soda in small bowl.

In large bowl cream butter until fluffy.  Gradually beat in sugar.
Add egg and vanilla until fluffy.

Stir in flour mixture and chips.

Shape into 1 1/2 to 2 inch  balls; place 3 inches apart on ungreased
cookie sheet.  (5-6 per large sheet)

Bake 350 degrees   on middle rack 25-30 mins.  Cool on cookie sheet.

Makes about 18 cookies
