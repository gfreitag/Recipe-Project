Title: GINGER-MUSHROOM SAUTE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Mushrooms
1 ts Each butter & Vegetable oil
1 tb Soy sauce
2    Whole green onions, sliced
2 ts Finely grated gingerroot
Freshly ground black pepper
1.  Clean mushrooms and slice.  Set aside.  In a large frying pan,
heat butter and oil over medium heat.  Add mushrooms and remaining
ingredients. Saute for 3 to 5 minutes or until mushrooms are tender.
Serve with grilled steak or chicken. Source: Chatelaine Magazine.
Sept. 96 Issue
