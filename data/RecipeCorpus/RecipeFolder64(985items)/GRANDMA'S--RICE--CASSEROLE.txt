Title: GRANDMA'S  RICE  CASSEROLE
Categories: Kaz
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Egg
1 c  Grated Cheddar cheese
1    (10 oz.) pkg. frozen chopped
Spinach
2 c  Cooked rice
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Fresh ground pepper
1/4 c  Grated Cheddar -- save for
The top
In medium bowl beat egg.  Add rest and mix.  Grease 1 1/2 quart
casserole baking dish.  Spoon in mix. Smooth top. Sprinkle with
cheese. Cover. Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees. Serves 4-6. Microwave:
Save sprinkle Cheddar. Bake 8 minutes.  Stir, rotate and sprinkle.
Bake 5 minutes.
