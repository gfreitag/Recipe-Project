Title: GOOD REASONS RANCH STYLE SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Pitzer
Yield: 1 Servings
-MM BY H PEAGRAM
4 tb Cremora Powder
4 ts Accent
4 ts Onion Salt
2 tb Dry Minced Parsley
1 ts Garlic Salt
4    Env Lipton C Of Chicken
Combine all of the ingredients and store in a covered container at
room temperature.  To Use: Remove 1/4 of the mixture and combine
with
2 cups mayonnaise and 2 cups of dairy buttermilk. Makes 1 pint of
dressing. From: Earl Shelsby
