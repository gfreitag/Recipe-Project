Title: GINGER GARLIC SOY LIME DRESSING
Categories: Powter, Low fat
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Tamari or light soy sauce
4 tb Rice vinegar
1/4 c  Lime juice
8 cl Garlic
2 tb Fresh grated ginger =OR=
1 ts Dried ginger
1 1/2 tb Spike seasoning
1 1/2 ts Dark sesame oil
1/4 ts Chili oil
Combine all ingredients in a blender. Pulse until garlic is chopped
fine. Per serving:  16 calories, .255g fat. Source: C'mon America,
Let's Eat! by Susan Powter. Typos by Elizabeth Wood and her ScanMan
Date: 05 Sep 96
8/96. From: Elizabeth Wood
