Title: GLAZED ORANGES
Categories: Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
3 md Oranges
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Light corn syrup
1 tb Honey
Cut ends off oranges; cut each into 1/8" slices. Heat butter in 12"
skillet over med heat until melted. Stir in corn syrup & honey. Heat
to boiling; add oranges & reduce heat. Simmer uncovered, spooning
sauce frequently over oranges till they are tender & glazed, about 25
minutes.
