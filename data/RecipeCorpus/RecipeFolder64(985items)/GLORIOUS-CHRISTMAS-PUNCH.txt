Title: GLORIOUS CHRISTMAS PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Christmas
Yield: 1 Servings
1    3 oz. pkg. cherry gelatin
1 c  Boiling water
1    6 oz. can frozen lemonade
3 c  Cold water
1 qt Cranberry juice cocktail
-chilled
1    12 oz. bottle ginger ale,
-chilled
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water; stir in lemonade. Add cold water
and cranberry juice.  Place two trays of ice cubes in a large punch
bowl; pour punch over ice. Pour in ginger-ale. Source: Quick & Easy
Cooking ch.
