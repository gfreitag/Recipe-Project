Title: GINGER & SCALLION SAUCE
Categories: Oriental, Main dish
Yield: 1 Cup
4 tb Toasted Sesame Oil
4 tb Minced Fresh Ginger
4 tb Minced Scallion
6 tb Soy Sauce
4 tb Dark Chinese rice Vinegar,
-Lt. rice vinegar or
-Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Homemade or low salt canned
-chicken stock
2 tb Sugar
Mix well. Serve with steamed dumplings.
