Title: GOLDEN CHICKEN & NOODLES
Categories: Poultry, Easy, Pasta, Crockpot
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Boneless skinless chicken
-breast halves ( 1 1/2 lbs)
2 cn (10 3/4 oz. each) condensed
-broccoli cheese soup,
-undiluted
2 c  Milk
1 sm Onion, chopped
1/2    To 1 tsp. salt, optional
1/2    To 1 tsp. dried basil
1/8 ts Pepper
Hot cooked noodles
Cut chicken pieces in half; place in a 5 qt. slow cooker. Combine the
soup, milk, onion, salt if desired, basil and pepper; pour over
chicken. Cover and cook on high for 1 hour.  Reduce heat to low;
cover and cook 5-6 hours longer or until the meat juices run clear.
Serve over noodles. Yield: 6 servings.  Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Quick Cooking
