Title: GOURMET SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetarian, Pasta
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk EDEN Spaghetti
-- (Parsley Garlic)
14 1/2 oz Can EDEN Diced Tomatoes
-- drained
1 sm Jar artichoke hearts, diced
1 sm Jar capers
1 bn Green onions; chopped
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2 tb EDEN Olive Oil
3 tb EDEN Mustard
1/4 c  Water
1/4 ts Cayenne (optional)
Break spaghetti into pieces, cook as package directs, set aside.
Combine remaining ingredients, mix with spaghetti and serve.

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cooking Time: 10 minutes Yield: 6-8 servings
