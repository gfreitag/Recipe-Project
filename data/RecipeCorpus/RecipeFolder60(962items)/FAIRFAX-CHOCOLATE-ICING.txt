Title: FAIRFAX CHOCOLATE ICING
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Chocolate, bitter squares
2    Chocolate, semi-sweet square
3 tb Butter
6 tb Water
6    Marshmallows
1/2 c  Confectioners sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Mix chocolate, sugar and water together. Cook about 5 minutes. Remove
from heat, add marshmallows, butter and vanilla. Beat until thick
enough to spread. Mrs. Harold T. Cook
