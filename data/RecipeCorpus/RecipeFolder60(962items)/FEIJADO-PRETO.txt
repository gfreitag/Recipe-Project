Title: FEIJADO PRETO
Categories: Beans
1 lg onion -- chopped
Yield: 8 Servings
1    clove garlic -- minced
2 TB oil
1 c  black beans
1    bay leaf
3 1/2 c  water
1/2 ts coriander seed
2    stalks celery -- chopped
1    tomato -- chopped
1    orange -- sliced - save 1/2
for garnish
:
1 c  brown rice -- uncooked
1/2 c  bread crumbs -- toasted

Saute onion and garlic in oil until tender, in large pot. Add beans,
water, bay leaf and cover. Soak overnight. The next day add,
coriander, celery, tomato and 1/2 orange. Simmer 2-3 hours until
beans are tender, adding water if necessary. Cook rice, about 45
minutes before serving, using 2 1/2-3 cups of water. Serve beans over
rice, garnish with orange slices and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
