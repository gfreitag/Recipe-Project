Title: ELDORADO COUNTRY CLUB TOLL HOUSE PIE
Categories: Pies & past, Chocolate, Nuts
Yield: 8 Servings
Butter -- for pan
2    Eggs
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Melted butter
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
Preheat oven to 3250. Rub 9" pie plate with butter. Beat eggs until
foamy. Add flour and sugars; beat until well-blended. Blend in melted
butter. Stir in chocolate chips and walnuts. Bake 30 minutes or until
set and light golden. Serve in wedges with ice cream.

: Eldorado Country Club, Texas
