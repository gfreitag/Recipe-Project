Title: FAT-FREE  MARINADE  FOR  BAKING  OR  BROILING
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Dry red wine
1 1/2 tb Soy sauce
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Marjoram
1/8 ts Pepper
2 lb Lean meat (flank or round
Steak, poultry, fish)
Crumble oregano and marjoram into wine and soy sauce. Add pepper.
Place meat in a plastic bag, pour marinade over, and seal bag. Chill
12 to 18 hours. Baste meat with marinade while cooking.
