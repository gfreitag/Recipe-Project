Title: EVERGLADES HUSH PUPPIES
Categories: Breads
Yield: 10 Servings
2 c  Corn meal
1 c  Flour
1 lg Onion, chopped
2 tb (rounded) baking powder
4    Eggs
2 ts Salt
1    13 oz. can evaporated milk
In a medium bowl, stir all ingredients together and drop by
teaspoonsful into hot oil and fry until golden. (The Orange Bowl
