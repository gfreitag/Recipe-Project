Title: FATFREE RESTAURANTS IN SANTA FE~ NEW MEXICO
Categories: Digest, Nov., Restaurants
Yield: 1 Servings
None
Try Healthy David's in Santa Fe (good low-fat vegan food), or for a
more upscale but not always low-fat version, the Natural Cafe. In
Albuquerque, a great diner-style vlf juice bar and restaurant is
called Forty Carrots.
