Title: FABULOUS CARAMEL CLUSTERS
Categories: Desserts, Cookies, Kathy's
Yield: 10 Cups
1 pk (14oz)caramels
1/4 c  Water
6 c  Bite size crispy rice cereal
Squares
2 c  Pecan halves (or your
Favorite nut)
heat oven to 350 degrees. melt caramels with water in 2 1/2 to 3 quart
heavy saucepan over low heat, stirring frequently until smooth. add
cereal and pecans. mix lightly until well coated. spread into greased
15 x 10 x 1 inch baking pan. bake 25 min. stiring after 15 min. turn
out onto greased wax paper. cool thoroughly, break apart. recipe from
"Kraft Creative Kitchens"
