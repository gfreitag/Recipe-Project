Title: FETTUCCINE WITH TUNA SAUCE
Categories: Fish, Main dish, Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Red Onion; Md, Sliced
1    Garlic Clove; Minced
1/2 c  Regular Margarine
6 1/2 oz Tuna In Water; Drain, Flake
1/4 ts Black Pepper
8 oz Fettuccine; Cooked, Drained
3 c  Red Leaf Lettuce; Torn
In a medium skillet, over medium heat, cook the onion and garlic in
the margarine for 2 minutes.  Add the tuna and pepper; cook and stir
for an additional 2 minutes more. In a large bowl, toss the hot
fettuccine with the tuna sauce and lettuce until well mixed. FROM THE
BACK OF A BLUE BONNET BOX.
