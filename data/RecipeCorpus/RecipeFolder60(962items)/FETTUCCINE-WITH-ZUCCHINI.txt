Title: FETTUCCINE WITH ZUCCHINI
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables, Pasta
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Small zucchini
2    Red peppers
1/2 c  Olive oil
2    Onions, chopped
2    Garlic cloves, minced
4    Tomatoes, seeded & chopped
Or 1 - 16oz can tomatoes
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Dried red pepper flakes
1 lb Fettucine
Trim the ends of the zucchini and slice into long julienne strips.
Cut the peppers into strips.  Heat the olive oil in a large skillet
and saute the onions, garlic, zucchini, and peppers for 5 minutes.
Add the tomatoes, salt, and red pepper flakes and cook over low heat
for another 5-10 minutes.  Serve over cooked fettucine.
