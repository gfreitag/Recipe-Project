Title: FAMILY FRENCH DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Tomato Catsup
1/2 c  Vegetable Or Salad Oil
1/4 c  Apple Cider Vinegar
2 ts Confectioners' Sugar
1 ea Clove Garlic; Split
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
Combine all of the ingredients in a jar.  Cover; shake vigorously.
Chill to blend flavors, about 2 hours. Remove the garlic and shake
again just before serving.

Makes 1 1/4 cups of dressing.
