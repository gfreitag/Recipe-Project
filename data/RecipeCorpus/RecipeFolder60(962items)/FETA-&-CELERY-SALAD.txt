Title: FETA & CELERY SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 2 Servings
175 g  Feta, crumbled
3    Stalks of celery
-- thinly sliced
1    Canned pimento
-- chopped small
2 tb Chopped fresh thyme
-OR oregano
2 tb Vinaigrette
1    Oak-leaf lettuce
-- washed and trimmed
Mix the feta with the celery, pimento and chopped herbs. Dress with
vinaigrette. Place the lettuce leaves on two separate plates, and
spoon half of the feta and celery salad in the centre of each one.
