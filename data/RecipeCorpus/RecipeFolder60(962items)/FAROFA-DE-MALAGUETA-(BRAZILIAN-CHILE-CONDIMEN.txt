Title: FAROFA DE MALAGUETA (BRAZILIAN CHILE CONDIMEN
Categories: Condiments, Sauce/gravy, Brazil, Upload
Yield: 3 Cups
1/4 c  Oil, palm
1 c  Onions; chopped
2    Eggs; hard cooked, chopped
1 ts Chile, malageuta; minced
2 c  Flour, manioc
MMMMM--------------------HEAT SCALE - MEDIUM-------------------------
Heat palm oil in a frying pan and fry the onions until golden
brown. Add hard-boiled eggs and saute the mixture for 1 minute. Next
add the chiles and manioc flour, stirring constantly until the
mixture turns golden.
Substitute piquin if malagueta chiles are unavailable.
Note: if palm oil is unavailable, or a healthier alternative is
desired, substitute vegetable oil with
3    teaspoons of paprika added.
