Title: ELEGANT OATMEAL CUSTARD
Categories: Breakfast, Gourmet
Yield: 6 Servings
8    Egg yolks
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 qt Milk; scalded
1 ts Vanilla
1 qt Oatmeal; cooked and cooled
2 c  Berries (for garnish)
Recipe by: Ritz-Carlton Hotel, Houston, Texas Beat together yolks,
sugar, and cinnamon. Stir in milk and vanilla. Fold in oatmeal. Ladle
16oz of mixture into 2 cup buttered ramekins and bake at 350-degrees,
in water bath, for 55-60 minutes. Serve hot or chilled, top with
berries.
