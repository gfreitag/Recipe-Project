Title: EMMENTHAL CHEESE FRITTERS
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Person
1 lg Slice bread
1    Slice ham
1 tb Butter
1    Slice Emmenthal cheese
Salt
Pepper
1    Egg
Lightly toast the bread.  Fry the ham briefly, place on bread, cover
with cheese and season.  Place in fairly hot oven and allow cheese to
melt, or in covered frying pan on top of the cooker. At the last
moment, top cheese with a fried egg.
