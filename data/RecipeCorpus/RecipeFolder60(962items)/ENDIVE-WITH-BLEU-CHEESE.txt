Title: ENDIVE WITH BLEU CHEESE
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 2 Servings
3 oz Cream cheese
3 oz Bleu cheese
3 tb Unsalted butter
1/4 c  Whipping cream
2 lg Bunch Belgian endive
paprika

(Makes about 2 dozen appetizers.)

Combine cheeses, butter and 1/4 cup whipping cream in processor or
blender and mix until creamy, adding more cream as necessary.

Separate endive leaves; wash and drain. Spoon cheese mixture into
pastry bag fitted with star tip and pipe into endive leaves. Arrange
leaves in spoke pattern on large platter and sprinkle with paprika.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
