Title: FETTUCINI WITH BROCCOLI PESTO
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Broccoli (flower part only)
1 c  Walnuts
3/4 c  Small chunks Parmesan
1 c  Olive oil
1 pn Salt
8 oz Fettucine
1    Garlic clove
1/4 c  Butter
1 tb Lemon juice
1    Garlic clove
4 c  Cream
In food processor combine 1st 6 ingredients to make pesto.In a large
pan saute one crushed garlic clove in butter, stir in lemon juice.
When thoroughly blended add the cream. Cook about 20 seconds.Add to
cooked fettucini, and lightly toss with a pinch of parsley.Add the
broccoli pesto to taste, and serve with extra grated Parmesan cheese.

Posted by Randall Crisman, Fido "Cooking", 5/14/92.
