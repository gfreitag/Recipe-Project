Title: FARMER EGG
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Ethnic, Pork
Yield: 2 Servings
3 1/2 oz Bacon
1    Potato; in small pieces
1    Union
5    Eggs
5 tb Milk
Salt
Green pea's or other vege's
Bake the bacon, put slices of union and potato in. Eventually you can
put some green pea's in it, or other vegatables. Bake it brown. Mix
the eggs with the milk and pour it above the bacon, union and potato.
Let it bake until the whole thing is light-brown. Serve it with some
garden vegetables.

Serve it with bread if it's a fast-food meal.
