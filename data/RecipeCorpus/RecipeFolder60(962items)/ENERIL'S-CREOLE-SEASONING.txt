Title: ENERIL'S CREOLE SEASONING
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 tb Paprika
2 tb Salt
2 tb Garlic powder
1 tb Black pepper
1 tb Onion powder
1 tb Cayenne pepper
1 tb Dried leaf oregano
1 tb Dried leaf thyme
Combine all and store in an airtight container. Source: Emeril's New
New Orleans Cooking, Chef Emeril Lagasse
