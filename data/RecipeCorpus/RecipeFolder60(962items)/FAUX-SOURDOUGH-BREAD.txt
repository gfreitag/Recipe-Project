Title: FAUX SOURDOUGH BREAD
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 pk Active Dry Yeast
2 1/2 c  Bread Flour
1 tb Dark Molasses
1 c  Sour Cream
1 ts Salt
1 ts Lemon Juice
4 tb Warm Water
All ingredients but the water should be at room temperature before
starting.  Add ingredients to the pan in the order listed. Select
"White Bread".  Press "Start".
