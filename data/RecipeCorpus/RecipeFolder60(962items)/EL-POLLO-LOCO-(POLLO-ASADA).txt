Title: EL POLLO LOCO (POLLO ASADA)
Categories: Chicken, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  White wine vinegar
1 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  White wine
Oregano
Thyme
Salt
10 ml Garlic -- mince
1 1/2 ts Hot sauce
Mix all ingredients and marinate two whole chicken which have been
cut in half. Marinate several hours in refrigerator. Grill chickens
slowly until done. Copycat for El Pollo Loco
