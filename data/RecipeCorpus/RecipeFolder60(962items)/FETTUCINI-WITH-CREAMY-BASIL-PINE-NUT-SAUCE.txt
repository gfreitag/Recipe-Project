Title: FETTUCINI WITH CREAMY BASIL-PINE NUT SAUCE
Categories: Pastas, Rice
Yield: 3 Servings
1    Clove garlic
1 c  Fresh basil leaves
1/2 c  Light ricotta cheese
1/2 c  Nonfat yogurt
2 oz Blue cheese -- crumbled
2 ts Sherry vinegar
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Pine nuts
9 oz Fettucini
****GARNISH****
Fresh basil leaves --
Optional
Place the garlic in a food processor and pulse 2-3 times. Add basil
and the next 5 ingredients (basil through pepper); process until
smooth. Stir in pine nuts.

Cook pasta according to package directions, omitting salt and fat -
drain. Combine pasta and sauce in a large bowl....toss well. Garnish
with fresh basil leaves, if desired.
