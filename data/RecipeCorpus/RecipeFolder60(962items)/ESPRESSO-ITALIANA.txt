Title: ESPRESSO ITALIANA
Categories: Beverages, Italian, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
6 c  Coffee; * see note
1 ds Nutmeg; freshly grated
1    Twist Lemon Peel
1/2 ts Anise Seed
1/2 ts Anisette Liqueur
* Use dark roast Italian espresso coffee.

Into each of 6 demitasse cups filled with coffee, pour mixture and
serve at once.

Serve with paper-wrapped macaroons.

Serves 6.
