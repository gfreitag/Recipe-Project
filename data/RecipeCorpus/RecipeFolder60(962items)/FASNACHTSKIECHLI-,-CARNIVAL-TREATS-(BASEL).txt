Title: FASNACHTSKIECHLI / CARNIVAL TREATS (BASEL)
Categories: Swiss, Cookies
Yield: 10 Servings
10    Eggs
100 g  Sugar
1    Grated lemon rind
1/2 dl Cream
50 g  Butter (melted)
1 kg Flour
Mix well and roll out 1/2 cm thick.  Cut out rounds and fry in deep
fat (200 C).  Dust with powdered sugar.

E Guete!  (More or less, Switzerdeutsch for "Bon appetit!".)
