Title: FAT FREE MARSHMALLOW TREATS
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 16 Servings
10 oz Marshmallows
3 tb Molly McButter butter
-flavor sprinkles
6 c  Rice cereal
1 c  Raisins
Spray a 13 x 9 x 2" pan with cooking spray; set aside. In a large
saucepan over low heat, melt marshmallows . Stir until smooth. Remove
from heat. Add sprinkles, cereal, and raisins, stir until well
coated. Press into prepared pan. Cool; cut into bars.
