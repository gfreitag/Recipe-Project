Title: ETHIOPIAN HONEY BREAD
Categories: Bread_mach, Bread/rolls
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Milk
3 tb Water
6 tb Honey
1    Egg
3 1/2 tb Butter
3 1/4 c  Flour
2 ts Coriander
1/2 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Yeast
All ingredients should be room temperature.  Add ingredients
according to the bread machine directions.  Bake on full cycle, light
crust.

* I ususally add about 1/3 c raisins for a more interesting taste
