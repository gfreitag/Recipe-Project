Title: ESSENCE OF EMERIL (SOUTHWEST SPICE)
Categories: Spice mix
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Chili powder
2 ts Cumin, ground
2 tb Paprika
1 tb Oregano, dried
1 tb Coriander, ground
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1 tb Garlic powder
1 ts Crushed red pepper
1 tb Salt
1 ts Black pepper
Combine all ingredients thoroughly.
