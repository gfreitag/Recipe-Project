Title: EXOTIC GREEN SALAD
Categories: Appetizers, Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Belgian endives
1    Head radicchio or ruby
-lettuce
1    Head Boston lettuce
DRESSING:
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Liquid honey
1/2 ts Minced gingerroot
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Each chopped fresh coriander
-and basil
Dressing:  In bowl, whisk together vinegar, soy sauce, lemon juice,
honey, ginger, salt, pepper and garlic; gradually whisk in oil. Stir
in coriander and basil.

Trim endives; separate into leaves.  Separate and tear radicchio and
Boston lettuce into bite-size pieces. In salad bowl, toss green with
dressing. Makes 6 servings.  Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com
Source: The Canadian Living 20th Anniversary Cookbook.
