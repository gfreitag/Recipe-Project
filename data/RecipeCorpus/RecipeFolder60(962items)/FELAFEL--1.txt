Title: FELAFEL -1
Categories: Pulses and, Sauces and, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
-----Felafel-----
2 c  Chickpeas -- cooked
1    Egg, Beaten
3    Cloves Garlic -- crushed
1/4 c  Celery -- finely minced
1/4 c  Scallions -- finely minced
1/2 ts Ground Cumin
1/4 ts Turmeric
1 1/2 tb Flour
1/8 ts Cayenne Pepper
1 ds Pepper
3/4 ts Salt
------Felafel Sauce-----
1 c  Yogurt
2 tb Tahini
1 tb Lemon Juice
3    Cloves Garlic -- crushed
1/4 ts Cumin
Put ingredients in food processor.  Chill well.  With floured hands,
make the batter into one dozen patties. Dust each lightly with flour.
Heat a 2-inch pool of oil in a heavy skillet to 365 degrees. Deep fry
felafel until golden brown and serve in pitas with sauce, lettuce,
and tomatoes.
