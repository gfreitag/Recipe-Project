Title: FAKE DAIRY QUEEN BLIZZARD
Categories: Fake, Beverages, Ice cream, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Health candy bar
1/4 c  Milk
2 1/2 c  Vanilla ice cream
1 ts Fudge topping
Freeze the Heath bar.  Break the candy into tiny pieces with a knife
handle before removing form wrapper. Combine all of the ingredients
in the blender and blend for 30 seconds on med. speed. Stop the
blender to stir the mixture with a spoon; repeat until well mixed.
Pour into a 16-oz. glass. Your homemade blizzard may not be as thick
as its commercial counterpart. If you would like a thicker treat,
after pouring the mixture into your cup, simply place it in the
freezer for 5 to 10 minutes, or until it reaches the desired
consistency. VARIATIONS: Try substituting different candies.
