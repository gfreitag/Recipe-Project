Title: FETTUCCINE WITH BLACK BEAN-AND-GARLIC SAUCE
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
12    Cloves garlic; chopped
3 tb Chopped onion
4 tb Chopped carrot
1 1/2 ts Olive oil
3/4 c  Low-sodium V-8 juice
1 1/4 c  Cooked black beans
2    Green onions; chopped
1 lb Fettuccine; cooked
1 tb Minced cilantro
1/4 c  Diced tomatoes, fresh
-or canned and drained
1. Saute garlic, onions, and carrot in oil in saucepan until soft.
Add 2 Tb. V-8 juice. 2. In food processor or blender, puree garlic
mixture with half of beans. 3. In bowl, combine remaining beans and
bean puree. Add remaining V-8 juice and green onions. 4. Return to
saucepan and cook, stirring, over medium-low heat until heated
through, about 2 to 3 minutes. 5. Serve over pasta.  Garnish with
cilantro and tomatoes. Note: Can be frozen.
