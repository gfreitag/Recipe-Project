Title: FATFREE ITALIAN DRESSING
Categories: Digest, Oct.
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Apple cider vinegar
1 tb Chopped onion
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Frozen apple juice
Concentrate
1    Clove garlic, cut in half
1 ts Honey
1/2 ts Crushed dried basil
1/2 ts Crushed dried oregano
1 pn White pepper
2    Canned artichoke hearts
(packed in water),
Rinsed and drained
(about 1/2 cup)
Combine all ingredients in blender or food processor and puree until
smooth.

Chill at least 15 minutes before serving.

Yields:  1 cup
