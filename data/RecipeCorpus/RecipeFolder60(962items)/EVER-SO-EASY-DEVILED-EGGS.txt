Title: EVER-SO-EASY DEVILED EGGS
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs, Low-carb
Yield: 12 Appetizers
6    Hard-boiled eggs
1/3 c  Dairy sour cream
1/3 c  Shredded cheese w/taco seaso
Preparation:

Cut eggs in half lenghtwise.  Remove yolks and set whites aside.  Mash
yolks with fork.  Blend in remaining ingredients.  Refill whites,
using one heaping teaspoon yolk mixture for each egg half.
