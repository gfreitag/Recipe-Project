Title: ENGLISH MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Muffins, Breakfast
Yield: 6 Muffins
1 c  Warm water
1 tb Dry yeast
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 ts Salt
2 c  Unbleached white flour
1 c  Whole wheat flour
In a bowl, mix water, yeast, oil & salt.  Beat in the white flour,
followed by the whole wheat flour.  Mix well. Oil the dough's surface
& allow to proof till light, about 25 minutes. Knead dough slightly,
till soft. Cut into 6 pieces & roll each piece in cornmeal. Form each
ball into muffins, 3 3/4 inches in diameter. Place on a cookie sheet.
Preheat oven to 350F & bake for 15 minutes.
