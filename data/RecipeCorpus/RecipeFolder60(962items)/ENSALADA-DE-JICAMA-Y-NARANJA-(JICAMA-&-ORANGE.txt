Title: ENSALADA DE JICAMA Y NARANJA (JICAMA & ORANGE
Categories: Appetizers, Nicaragua, Upload
Yield: 6 Servings
Juice of 2 limes
1/4 ts Chile powder, habanero
2 sm Oranges; cut in segments
1 sm Jicama; juilenne cut
3    Scallions; thinly sliced
Cilantro; chopped
MMMMM--------------------HEAT SCALE = MEDIUM-------------------------
Combine the lime juice and habanero powder. Arrange the orange
segments on a platter and top with the jicama and onions. Pour the
lime juice mixture over the salad, garmish with the cilantro, and
serve.
