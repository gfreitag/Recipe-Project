Title: FERMENTED FRUIT FRIENDSHIP CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Yellow cake mix, 2 layer
1    Pudding mix
4    Eggs
2/3 c  Oil
2 ts Orange extract
1 1/2 c  Fermented Fruit Starter
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Golden raisins
For pudding, use 4-serving size;
Use cake mix without pudding.
vanilla, lemon or pistachio
instant
pudding.
Preheat oven to 350 deg. Combine cake and pudding mixes, eggs, oil,
and orange extract in a large bowl. Add fruits, nuts and raisins, mix
thoroughly. Pour into greased, floured tube pan and bake one hour.
Let cool 15 minutes before removing from the pan. Makes 12-16
servings.
