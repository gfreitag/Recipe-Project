Title: FAVORITE BROCCOLI SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 bn Broccoli; separated into
-florets
1    Cauliflower; separated into
-florets
8    Bacon strips; fried and
-crumbled
1/3 c  Onion, chopped
1 c  Tomatoes; chopped, seeded
2    Eggs, hard boiled; sliced
1 c  Mayonnaise or Salad Dressg
1/3 c  Sugar
2 ts Vinegar
In a large salad bowl, combine broccoli, cauliflower, bacon, onion,
tomatoes and eggs. Set aside.
In another bowl, combine mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar; mix until
smooth. Chill all. Just before serving, mix dressing, pour over salad
and toss.
