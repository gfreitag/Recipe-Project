Title: SHRIMP AU GRATIN~ III
Categories: Main dish, Shellfish, Cajun
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Butter or oleo
1 c  Milk
Dash of Tabasco sauce
1 c  Sharp cheese, grated
Buttered bread crumbss
3 tb Flour
1 ts Lea & Perrins sauce
1    Green onion, chopped fine
2 c  Shrimp, cleaned, boiled
Salt & pepper to taste
Make white sauce of butter, flour and milk.  Add seasonings,
cheese and shrimp.  Place in casserole or ramekins. Cover with
buttered bread crumbs. Bake at 325 F. about 30 minutes or until hot
