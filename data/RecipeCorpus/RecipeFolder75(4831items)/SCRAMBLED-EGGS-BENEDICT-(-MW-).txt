Title: SCRAMBLED EGGS BENEDICT ( MW )
Categories: Microwave, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Hollandaise Sauce Mix  *
8    Thin Slices Canadian Bacon
4    Large Eggs
1/4 c  Milk
2 tb Chopped Green Peppers (Opt.)
1/8 ts Salt
1    Dash Pepper
2    English Muffins, Split
*    Sauce packet should weigh 1 1/4 ounces.

Mix sauce as directed on package in 2-cup glass measure. Microwave
uncovered on high (100%), 1 minute; stir. Microwave until mixture
boils and thickens, 1 to 1 1/2 minutes, stirring every 30 seconds.
Cover and microwave bacon on high (100%) on high (100%) until hot, 1
1/2 to 2 minutes. Beat eggs in 1-Qt casserole. Beat in milk, green
pepper, salt and pepper. Cover and microwave on high (100%) 2
minutes; stir. Cover and microwave until eggs are set but still
moist, 1 to 1 1/2 minutes. Place muffins, cut sides up on serving
plate. Top each with bacon slices and large spoonfull of eggs; spoon
sauce over eggs. Microwave uncovered on high (100%) until hot, 1 to 1
1/2 minutes. NOTE:
