Title: SOUTHWEST SMOOTHIE
Categories: Beverages, Fruits, Mexican
Yield: 3 Servings
1/2 c  Banana; Sliced
1/2 c  Mango, Papaya, Or Guava; *
2 c  Milk
1 tb Honey
*  Fruit should be of one kind listed and be chopped.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Place all ingredients in food processor workbowl
fitted with steel blade or in blender container; cover and process on
high speed until smooth. Strain if using mango.
