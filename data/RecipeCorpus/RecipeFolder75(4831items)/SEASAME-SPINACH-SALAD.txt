Title: SEASAME SPINACH SALAD
Categories: Diabetic, Salads, Vegetables, Side dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Spinach; fresh
1 c  Mushrooms; sliced
1 tb Low-sodium soy sauce;
1 tb Water
1 tb Walnut oil;
1 tb Sesame oil;
1 tb Toasted Sesame seeds;
Wash spinach thoroughly and trim off tough stems. Spin or pat dry. Mix
remaining ingredients together and toss with spinch. Food Exchange per
serving: 1 VEGETABLE EXCHANGE + 1 FAT EXCHANGE CAL: 71; CHO: Omg;
CAR: 5g; PRO: 3g; SOD: 217mg; FAT: 5g;

Source: Light & Easy Diabetes Cuisine by Betty Marks Brought you and
yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master
