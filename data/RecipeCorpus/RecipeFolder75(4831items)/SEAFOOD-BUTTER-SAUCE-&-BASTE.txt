Title: SEAFOOD BUTTER SAUCE & BASTE
Categories: Fish, Bar-b-q, Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 ts Dried rosemary
1/2 ts Dried tarragon
3/4 ts Salt
1 tb Lemon juice
Melt butter in a small saucepan.  Add remaining ingredients. Brush on
seafood; baste occasionally during grilling. May also be served warm
as a nice dipping sauce with shrimp, crawfish, crab and lobster.
