Title: SEA DREAM SALAD
Categories: Jello, Salads, Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
3 oz Pkg lime Jello
1 1/4 c  Boiling water
1 c  Grated cucumber
1 tb Vinegar
3/4 ts Grated onion
1/2 ts Salt
Dash of cayenne
1 lb Shrimp, cooked and cleaned
(or use tuna, salmon or
Chicken salad)
Dissolve Jello in boiling water.  Add remaining ingredients, except
shrimp. Force through sieve. Pour into individual ring molds or a
3-cup or 1-quart ring mold.  Chill til firm.  Unmold onto salad
greens. Fill rings with shrimp (or salmon, tuna or chicken salad).
