Title: SPANISH BEANS (HABICHUELAS GUISADAS)
Categories: Main dish, Beans
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Water
1    Small potato, diced
1    Chicken broth cube
1 pn Salt
1 1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 pn Black pepper
2 tb Sofrito
1 pk Sazon
3 tb Tomato sauce
1 cn Pinto beans
Place all ingredients in medium saucepan and boil until the potatoes
are tender and cooked, about 20 minutes. Serve over white rice.
