Title: SHRIMP DIP DELIGHT
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 4 Cups
1 lb Shrimp, raw
8 oz Cream cheese
2 c  Green onion, chopped fine
3 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Parsley, chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
Boil, peel and devein shrimp.  Chop finely and set aside. In a bowl,
blend cream cheese with onion, celery, lemon juice, parsley, salt,
cayenne pepper, and mayonnaise. Fold in chopped shrimp and
refrigerate overnight.
