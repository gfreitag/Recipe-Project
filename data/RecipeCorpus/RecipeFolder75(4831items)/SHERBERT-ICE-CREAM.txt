Title: SHERBERT ICE CREAM
Categories: Ice cream, Desserts
Yield: 2 Servings
qt Sugar, heaping
8 oz Evaporated milk
4    Oranges, juice of
3    Lemons, juice of
1 qt Heavy cream
1 cn Pineapple, small, drained
Milk, whole
Mix to dissolve sugar, evaporated milk, and juice of fruit. Add
cream and pineapple. Put mixture in a 2 qt. ice cream freezer and top
to line with whole milk. Source: Gwen Pitts
