Title: SOUR CREAM DRESSING A LA RON'S PLAICE
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
Soured thick cream
Salt & Pepper to season
French Mustard
Castor Sugar
Stir cream until smooth.  Season with salt & pepper. Add mustard and
castor sugar.  Stir well.  Add a little milk if the dressing is too
thick for your taste. Ron's notes: You can add finely chopped chives
for a little color if you wish. From Ron in Blackpool:)
