Title: SOUTHWESTERN VINEGAR
Categories: Sauces, Preserves
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Bottle White Wine Vinegar
1    Clove Garlic -- minced
2    Jalepeno Pep  Quartered
3    Sprigs Cilantro
1/2    Lime, -- halved
5    Sun-Dried Tomatoes --
Halved
Heat vinegar to boiling.  Put remaining ingredients in a glass
bottle. Pour hot vinegar into bottle.  Let cool, then cap and store
in a cool place, letting steep for 2 weeks. It turns a pretty red
color and is great in Southwestern dishes
