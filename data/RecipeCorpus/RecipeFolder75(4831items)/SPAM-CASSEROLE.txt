Title: SPAM CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 8 Servings
1 cn Spam
1 c  Bread crumbs
3    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Milk
1/2    - lb sharp cheese, cubed
1 md Onion
1    Green pepper
Grind Spam, cheese, onion and pepper together. Combine with the
remaining ingredients. Bake in a casserole, covered @ 350 degrees for
1 1/4 hours. Uncover and bake 15 minutes more to brown. From: Jim
