Title: SOURDOUGH PANCAKES #2
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Active Starter
1 ea Large Egg
2 tb Cooking Oil
1/4 c  Instant Or Evaporate Milk
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking Soda
2 tb Sugar
Mix ingredients together and let the mixture bubble and foam a minute
or two, then drop on hot griddle in large spoonfuls.
