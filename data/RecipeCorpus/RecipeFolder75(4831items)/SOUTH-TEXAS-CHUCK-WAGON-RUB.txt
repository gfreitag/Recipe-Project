Title: SOUTH TEXAS CHUCK-WAGON RUB
Categories: Marinades, Bar-b-q
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM--------------------------FOR BEEF-------------------------------
1    Garlic clove; crushed
1 ts Seasoned pepper
1 ts Ground dried mild chiles
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Seasoned salt
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mash until smooth. Makes about 2
Tbsp.
