Title: SHRIMP COCKTAIL
Categories: Appetizers, Crocker
Yield: 6 Servings
36    Shrimp
Cocktail Sauce
Prepare Cocktail Sauce (page 603). To serve as individual appetizers,
mix 36 shrimp with sauce. For a party snack, fill large bowl with
crushed ice and place a dish of sauce in center; arrange shrimp on
ice. Serve with wooden picks for dipping shrimp into sauce. 6
servings; 80 calories per serving.

Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
