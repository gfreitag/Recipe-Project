Title: SOPA DE MELON ESCRIBE (CANTALOUPE SOUP)
Categories: Soups, Fruits, Mexican
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Half and half
1 c  Potato; cooked, peeled &
-diced
3 c  Cantaloupe; peeled & diced
1/4 c  Dry sherry
1 pn Salt
Place the half and half, potato, and cantaloupe in the blender. Blend
to a smooth puree.  Stir in sherry. Season to taste. Serve chilled.
If desired, garnish with a sprinkle of nutmeg or a lime slice.
