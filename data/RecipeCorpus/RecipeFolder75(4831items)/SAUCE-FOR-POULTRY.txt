Title: SAUCE FOR POULTRY
Categories: Sauces, Condiments
1/4 c  white wine
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  chicken broth
1 TB tomato paste
2 TB honey
1    bag fresh cranberries
1 TB butter

butter. This is very nice for Cornish Game Hens
