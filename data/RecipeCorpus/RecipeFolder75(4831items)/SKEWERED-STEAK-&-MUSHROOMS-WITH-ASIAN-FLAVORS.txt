Title: SKEWERED STEAK & MUSHROOMS WITH ASIAN FLAVORS
Categories: Asian, Beef, Grill
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Medium fresh white mushrooms
1 lb Flank steak
1/2 c  Ketchup
2 tb Light soy sauce
Optional: 2 teaspoons sesame
- oil
2    Cloves garlic; minced
1 c  Snow peas (about 18); halved
1 c  Sweet red or yellow pepper;
-cut into squares
1/4 c  Sesame seeds
Preliminaries:  Preheat outdoor grill or broiler.  Trim mushrooms; set
aside.

1. Chill steak in freezer for 20 minutes; cut into thin diagonal
slicesacross the grain.

2. In a small bowl, mix ketchup, soy sauce, sesame oil, if using, and
garlic; set aside.

3. Thread steak on individual skewers (if using bamboo skewers, soak
in water for 15 minutes before using), weaving around mushroom caps,
snow peas and sweet peppers; brush all sides with ketchup mixture
andf sprinkle with sesame seeds.

4. Place kebabs on a rack.  Grill or broil until cooked and browned,
turning occasionally, about 5-8 minutes.  Serve on bed of rice, if
desired.

Contributor:  The Mushroom Council
