Title: SOUR CREAM MARINADE FOR CHICKEN
Categories: Marinades, Chicken
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Italian Salad Dressing Mix
2 tb Water
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Salad oil
**This delicious marinade makes enough for 3 lbs chicken parts.**

In a bowl, blend together salad dressing mix, water, and lemon juice.
Add sour cream and combine thoroughly. Slowly add salad oil, whipping
until creamy. Marinate chicken pieces for about 2 hours. Bake in
moderate oven until tender. Source: Creative Home Cooking #425
By Mona Brun
