Title: SAUTEED ZUCCHINI WITH HERBS
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Zucchini,sliced 1/2" thick
2 tb Water
1/4 ts Dried leaf thyme,crumbled
1/8 ts Dried leaf mint,crumbled
1 ts Butter or margarine
Coat nonstick skillet with nonstick cooking spray. Saute zucchini
until lightly browned, 1 minute. Add water, thyme, mint; cook 2-3
minutes until tender. Add butter.
