Title: SOUTHERN TEA CAKES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 36 Servings
1 c  Shortening
1 3/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Flour, self-rising
In a mixing bowl, cream together shortening and sugar. Beat in eggs.
Add milk and vanilla; beat well. Stir in flour; mix well. Drop by
tablespoonsful 2 1/2" apart onto greased cookie sheets. Bake at 350Ã¸
for 15-20 minutes. (very good)
