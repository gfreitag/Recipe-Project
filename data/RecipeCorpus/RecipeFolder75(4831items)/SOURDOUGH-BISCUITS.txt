Title: SOURDOUGH BISCUITS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Sourdough starter
1 c  Milk
2 1/2 c  Unsifted flour
3/4 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Double acting baking powder
1/2 ts Soda
1 x  Salad oil, butter or bacon g
Mix the starter milk and 1 cup of the flour in a large bowl. (prepare
the night before for breakfast.) Cover and keep0 at room temp. to let
rise.
Turn this out onto 1 cup flour on a bread board. Combine salt,
sugar, b.p. and soda with remaining 1/2 cup of flour and sift over
the top. With hand knead lightly until the proper consistency. Roll
to a 1/2 inch thick. Cut out and dip in either warm bacon grease or a
mixture of half salad oil and melted butter
Place close together in a 9 x 9 pan and set in warm place to rise
for about half hour  Bake in a 375 f oven makes about 14 biscuits.
