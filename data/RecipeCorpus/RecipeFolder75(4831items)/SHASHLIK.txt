Title: SHASHLIK
Categories: Lamb, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lb Lamb, cubed
1 c  Olive oil
3 ea Onions, chopped
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper, ground
You can use cubed leg or shoulder of lamb. Combine oil, onion, salt
and pepper and marinate lamb for at least one day in refrigerator.
Skewer the meat and broil until brown all over. Serve with Rice Pilaf
and broiled tomatoes.
