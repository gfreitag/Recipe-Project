Title: SCOTTISH OAT CAKE
Categories: Cakes/frost, Scotland
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Oats or quick-cooking oats
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
2    To 3 tbsp cold water
Cut shortening into next four ingredients until mixture resembles fine
crumbs. Add water, 1 tbsp at a time, until it forms a stiff dough.
Roll until 1/8 inch thick on lightly floured surface. Cut into 2.
inch rounds or squares. Place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake at
375 until they just start to brown - 12 to 15 mins.
