Title: SMOKED TURKEY SANDWICH W/APRICOT MAYO
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 8 Servings
Apricot Mayonnaise:
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Apricot jam
1 1/2 ts Curry powder
H
Sandwich:
4    Whole pita bread rounds OR
8    Slices French baguette
1 lb Smoked turkey; sliced
2    Bunches watercress; washed
-and trimmed
4    Italian plum tomatoes; slice
1    Avacado; peeled, seeded and
-sliced
1 c  Walnuts; chopped, toasted
1/3 c  Currants or raisins
In small bowl, combine mayonnaise, jam, and curry powder. Whisk
until well blended.  Cover and chill up to 2 days. If using pita
bread, preheat oven to 350 degrees. Warm pita about 5 minutes. Cut
rounds in half cross wise, forming pockets.  Spread inside of pita or
baguette with Apricot Mayonnaise.  Stuff or top with turkey,
watercress, tomatoes, avocado, walnuts, and currants. From Colorado
