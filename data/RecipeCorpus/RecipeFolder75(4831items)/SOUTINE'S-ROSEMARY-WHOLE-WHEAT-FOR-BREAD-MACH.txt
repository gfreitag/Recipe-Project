Title: SOUTINE'S ROSEMARY WHOLE WHEAT FOR BREAD MACH
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
3/4 c  Bread flour
2 2/3 c  Whole wheat flour
3 tb Wheat bran
1 tb Dried rosemary
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Honey
1 c  PLUS 2 tablespoons Water
Add all ingredients in the order suggested by your bread machine
manual and process on the basic bread cycle according to the
manufactur's directions. Makes
1-1/2    pound loaf.
