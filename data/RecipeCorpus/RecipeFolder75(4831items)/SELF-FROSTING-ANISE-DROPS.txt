Title: SELF-FROSTING ANISE DROPS
Categories: Desserts, Cookies
Yield: 90 Drops
3 ea Eggs (room temperature)
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Sugar
1 3/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
3 ts Anise seed (or 1 t anise
-extract or 1/4 t anise oil
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Grease and generously flour the
cookie sheets.
Beat eggs with electric mixer until fluffy.  Add sugar gradually,
beating constantly; continue to beat for 20 minutes more. Reduce
speed of mixer and add flour that has been sifted with baking powder
and salt; beat 3 minutes.  Add anise.
Drop by teaspoonsful on prepared cookie sheets, swirling dough to
form round cookies.  Let stand uncovered at room temperature for at
least 8 hours.
Bake 10 minutes or until cookies are a creamy golden color on the
bottom (do not allow bottoms to brown). Cookies form a cake-like
layer on the bottom with a crisp "frosting" on the top.
Store in a tightly covered container.
