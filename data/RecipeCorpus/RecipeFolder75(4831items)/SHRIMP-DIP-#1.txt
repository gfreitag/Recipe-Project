Title: SHRIMP DIP #1
Categories: Dips, Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Milk
1 c  Mayonnaise
3 dr Tabasco Sauce
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 ts Garlic Salt
1    Onion (Chopped)
8 oz Cheddar Cheese (Cubed)
5 oz Shrimp (Drained)
Place all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth. Served
chilled.
