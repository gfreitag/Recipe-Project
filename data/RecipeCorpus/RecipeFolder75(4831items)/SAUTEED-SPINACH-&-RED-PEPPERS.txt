Title: SAUTEED SPINACH & RED PEPPERS
Categories: Low-cal, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
1 tb Olive oil
1 ts Olive oil
2 md Red bell peppers
2 ea Garlic cloves, sliced
1 pn Crushed red pepper flakes
8 c  Steamed rinsed spinach
1/4 ts Salt
* Seed bell peppers and cut into strips. ** Do not dry spinach.

1.  In large skillet, heat oil.  Add bell pepers, garlic, and pepper
flakes.  Cook stirring frequently for 3-4 minutes.

2.  Add spinach, with water still clinging to the leaves to the
skillet. Sprinkle with salt.  Increase heat to medium-high and cook,
stirring occasionally, 3 minutes or until vegetables are tender.

Each half cup serving provides: 1/2 FA and 2V.
