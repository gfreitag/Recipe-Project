Title: SAVOURY MASHED SWEET POTATO PATTIES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
3 md Sweet potatoes
3 tb Ground almonds or cashews
3 tb Ground sunflower seeds
3 tb Grated coconut
1/2 ts Cayenne
1/2 ts Cumin
1 ts Coriander
1 tb Maple sugar
1 ts Salt
3 tb Chick pea flour
Ghee for frying
Boil the sweet potatoes for 20 to 30 minutes or until tender. Drain &
peel while still warm.  Mash.

Mix in all the remaining ingredients except the ghee. Knead till
thoroughly blended.  Divide into 8 portions.

Wash & dry hands & then rub with oil.  Roll each ball in your hands &
then flatten into a patty.

Brush with ghee a griddle or skillet.  Over the lowest heat possible,
heat the ghee & then fry the patties for 15 to 20 minutes each side
until a crisp reddish brown crust forms. Serve hot.

Yamuna Devi, "The Art of Indian Vegetarian Cooking"
