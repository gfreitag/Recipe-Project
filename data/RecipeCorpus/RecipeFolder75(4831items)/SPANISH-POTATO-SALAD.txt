Title: SPANISH POTATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb New potatoes
4 tb Olive oil
1 tb Wine vinegar
2 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
1 sm Red pepper
1 tb Chives, chopped
Scrape the potatoes (I don't).  Cook until tender, but do not
overcook them or they will break up. Mix together the oil & vinegar.
Toss the potatoes while still hot. Stir in the garlic & red pepper.
Sprinkle with the chopped chives. Serve while still warm.
