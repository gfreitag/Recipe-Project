Title: SAUSAGE CASSEROLE DAKOTA
Categories: Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Pork sausage links
1/2 c  Green pepper,chopped
1 cn Tomatoes(16oz)
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Basil
3/4 c  Rice,uncooked
1/2 c  Onion,chopped
1 cn Mushrooms,sliced(4oz)
1/4 ts Pepper
1. Brown sausage links in a skillet; remove sausage and set aside.
2. Add rice to drippings; cook 5 minutes, or until rice is golden.
3. Add green pepper and onion; cook until tender.
4. Pour off drippings; add tomatoes, mushrooms, salt, pepper and
basil to rice mixture.
5. Pour misture into a 2-quart casserole; arrange sausage links over
rice.
6. Bake in preheated 350'F. oven 45 minutes.
7. Remove cover; continue baking 10 minutes longer.
