Title: SOURGHUM BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Blackstrap molasses
1 c  Apple cider vinegar
1 c  Balsamic vinegar
2 c  Chopped onions
1 c  Chipotles in adobo
2    Heads garlic
1 tb Black pepper
Sweet spices (cinnamon,
Allspice, etc)
Stir fry the onions a bit in their own moisture, then add the rest,
cook for a few hours, cool, blend. Makes a great marinade, and the
leftovers can be used for sauce.
