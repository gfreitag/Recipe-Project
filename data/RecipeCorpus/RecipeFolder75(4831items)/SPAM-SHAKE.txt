Title: SPAM SHAKE
Categories: Beverages, Meats, Weird
Yield: 4 Spamshakes
1 cn Spam
1 cn Anchovies
2 cn Beer (12 oz cans)
4 oz Tomato juice
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 ts Catsup
1/2 c  Parsley; chopped
1/4 c  Scallions; chopped
1 ts Tabasco
1 ds Salt and pepper
Put everything into a blender and blend until smooth. Serve chilled
with a celery rib.
