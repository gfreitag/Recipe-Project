Title: SIR WALTER RALEIGH
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------JUDI M. PHELPS----------------------------
1 oz Brandy
3/4 oz Yellow Chartreuse
3/4 oz Triple sec
Angostura bitters; several
Mix with cracked ice in a shaker or blender and strain into a chilled
cocktail glass.
