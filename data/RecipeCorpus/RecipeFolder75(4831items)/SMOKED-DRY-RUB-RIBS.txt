Title: SMOKED DRY RUB RIBS
Categories: Bbq/grill, Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
Six 3-pound pork ribs
Dry Rub #6
Jalapeno Dippin' Sauce
Rub ribs side to side and top to bottom and place in the refrigerator
overnight. Allow ribs to sit out at room temperature for 1 hour before
smoking. Smoke at 200 degrees for 4 1/2 to 5 hours. Allow to sit
covered for 1/2 hour before eating
