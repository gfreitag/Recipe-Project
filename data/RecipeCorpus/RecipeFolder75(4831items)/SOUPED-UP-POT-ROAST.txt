Title: SOUPED UP POT ROAST
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1    3 1/2 lb pot roast
1 pk Lipton Soup Mix
2 1/4 c  Water
In Dutch oven, brown roast over medium heat. Add soup mix blended with
water. Simmer covered, turning occasionally, 2 1/2 hours or until
tender. Thicken gravy.

MICROWAVE: In 3 quart casserole, blend soup mix with 1 1/4 cup water.
Microwave at HIGH 5 minutes. Add roast; microwave uncovered 10
minutes, turning once. Microwave covered at DEFROST, turning
occasionally, 50 minutes, or until tender. Let stand 10 minutes.
