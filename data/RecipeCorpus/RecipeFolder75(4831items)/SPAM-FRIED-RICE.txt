Title: SPAM FRIED RICE
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Oil, divided
2    Eggs, beaten
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(12 oz)
1/4 c  Finely chopped mushrooms
1/4 c  Chopped green onion
2 c  Cooked rice
3 tb Soy sauce
In large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon oil. Add eggs. Cook, stirring, to
desired doneness. Remove from skillet. In same skillet, heat
remaining 1 tablespoon oil. Cook SPAM, mushrooms, and green onion 4
minutes or until vegetables are tender. Stir in rice and egg.
Sprinkle with soy sauce. Heat thoroughly.
