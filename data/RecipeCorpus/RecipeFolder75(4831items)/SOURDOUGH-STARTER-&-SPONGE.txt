Title: SOURDOUGH STARTER & SPONGE
Categories: Breads, Sourdough
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
2 c  Warm water
1 pk Yeast
Combine all ingredients-mix well-cover and let stand 8-10 hours.
Place 1/2 cup of this in refrigerator. Remainder can be used to bake.
Remove from refrigerator, add 2 cups flour, 2 cups warm water. Repeat
above.
