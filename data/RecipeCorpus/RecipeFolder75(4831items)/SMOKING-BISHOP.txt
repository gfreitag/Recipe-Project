Title: SMOKING BISHOP
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Wine
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lg Orange
6    Cloves
1/2 c  Sugar; to 1 cup
1 1/2 c  Water
750 ml Dry red wine
Stud orange with cloves and bake it in a 3500 oven until skin is
browned., about 40 minutes. Quarter orange and place in a heavy
saucepan with water and sugar. Bring to a boil, dissolve sugar and
reduce heat. Simmer for 20 minutes. Add wine and heat u ntil the
mixture is "smoking hot." Pour in a heated punch bowl and serve.
