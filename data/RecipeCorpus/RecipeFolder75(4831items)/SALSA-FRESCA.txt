Title: SALSA FRESCA
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Tomato juice
1 1/2 md Tomatoes
1/2    Onion; chopped
2 tb Chopped cilantro
1    Jalapeno chile; minced
1    Serrano chile; minced
1 1/2 ts Salt
1    Garlic clove; minced
1/8 ts Sugar
Blend together well and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.

Source: Bon Appetit, date unknown
