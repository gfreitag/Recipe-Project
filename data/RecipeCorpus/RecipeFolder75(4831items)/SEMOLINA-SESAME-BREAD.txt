Title: SEMOLINA SESAME BREAD
Categories: Breads, Abm
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/4 ts Yeast
2 1/3 c  Bread flour
2/3 c  Semolina flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Sugar
2 tb Powdered milk
1 1/2 ts Butter or margarine
1 c  Water
1 lg Egg
1 ts Lemon juice
1/3 c  Sesame seeds -- lightly
Toasted
Place all ingredients in machine and push start! Do not use timer. Add
sesame seeds during second kneading (or use raisin bread cycle and
add at the beep).

From THE ULTIMATE BREAD MACHINE COOKBOOK by Lacalamita  Yield: 1 loaf
