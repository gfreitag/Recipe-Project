Title: SLOPPY JOES 2 (VEGAN)
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian, Tvp
Yield: 8 Servings
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1 ea Onion, chopped
1 ea Green bell pepper, chopped
2 c  TVP granules or flakes mixed
3 3/8 c  Hot water (for TVP)
6 oz Tomato Paste
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Oregano
1/4 c  Catsup
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Worcestershire sauce,
-vegetarian
1 tb Honey, optional
Salt, optional
8 lg Rolls
Shredded lettuce
Saute garlic, onion, pepper & TVP.  In a bowl, mix tomato paste with
water, oregano, ketchup, cayenne, worcestershire sauce & honey is
using. Stir into skillet.  Bring to a boil & add salt if desired.
The sauce should be thick but spreadable. If desired, toast rolls.
Spoon the sauce over the bottom half of the roll, pile on the lettuce
& top with remaining roll half.
