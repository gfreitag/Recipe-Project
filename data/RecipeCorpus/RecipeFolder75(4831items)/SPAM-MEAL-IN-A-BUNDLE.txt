Title: SPAM MEAL IN A BUNDLE
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Stone ground mustard
1/4 c  Beer
1/2 ts Celery seed
8    New potatoes, sliced 1/4" tk
2 c  Carrots, sliced 1/4" thick
1    Onion, thinly sliced
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, sliced
-(12 oz)
Heat oven to 375'F. In bowl, combine brown sugar, mustard, beer, and
celery seed. Divide vegetables and SPAM on four 12" square pieces of
foil. Drizzle mustard mixture over vegetables. Fold foil to form
packets. Bake 45-60 minutes or until hot.
