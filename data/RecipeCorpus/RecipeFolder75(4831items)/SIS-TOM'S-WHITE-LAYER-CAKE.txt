Title: SIS TOM'S WHITE LAYER CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 16 Servings
1 c  Crisco
2 c  Sugar
3 c  Flour (sifted)
2 ts Lemon extract
2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Milk
8    Egg whites (unbeaten)
Cream Crisco and sugar.  Sift flour and baking powder. Add flour and
milk alternately.  Add egg whites one at a time and beat 2 minutes
after each addition.  Add flavoring. Bake in layers or a tube pan.
Bake at 350 F.

Aunt Tom Davidson

Source: Mama's Favorite Recipes (Frances Sykes Turfitt) Shared by
