Title: SCINTILLATING LEMON PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Pastry shell *
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Butter, softened
3    Eggs, well beaten
2 tb Lemon rind, fresh grated
3    Lemons, peeled, sliced thin
1/2 c  Cold water
* Make triangular sections for top - dust with sugar and nutmeg. Bake

Mix all ingredients and pour into the unbaked pastry shell. Bake at
400 deg F for 25 min. Add the prebaked triangular sections and bake
another 10 min.
