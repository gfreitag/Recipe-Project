Title: SOUTHWESTERN THREE BEAN SOUP (MIAMI HERALD)
Categories: Vegetarian, Soups/stews, Main dish
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 md Onion, red; diced
1 md Carrot; diced
2 sm Parsnips, diced
1 1/4 c  ;water
1 c  Kidney beans; drained
1 c  Chickpeas; drained
1 c  Baby limas; frozen
4 c  Peeled tomatoes, canned
1 c  Vegetable stock
1 ts Cumin, ground
2 ts Chili powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Place onion, carrot, parsnips, and 1/4 c water in a large saucepan.
Saute 5 min.  Add kidney beanns, chick peas, lima beans, stock,
remaining water, cumin and chili powder. Break up tomatoes with a
spoon or knife and bring to a simmer.  Simmer 20 min.  add salt and
pepper to taste.
