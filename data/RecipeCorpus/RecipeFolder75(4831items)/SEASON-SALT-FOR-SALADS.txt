Title: SEASON SALT FOR SALADS
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ts Coarse salt
1 ts Grated parmesean cheese
3/4 ts Tarragon
1/2 ts Freeze dried chives
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Sesame seeds
1/4 ts Dried lemon peel
Blend ingredients well. Use on salads From: Mary Riemerman Date: Mon,
