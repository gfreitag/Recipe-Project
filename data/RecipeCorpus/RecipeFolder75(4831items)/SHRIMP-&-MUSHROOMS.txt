Title: SHRIMP & MUSHROOMS
Categories: Seafood, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Butter
2 c  Chopped Celery
1 cn (6oz) Mushrooms *
8 oz Shrimp
1 tb Soy Sauce
1/8 ts Pepper
1 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Water
1/2 c  Beef Broth
2 c  Hot Cooked Rice
1 ts Ginger
* Sliced and drained.
In large skillet, melt butter; cook and stir celery, mushrooms, soy
sauce, ginger and pepper until celery is tender, about 5 mins. Mix
cornstarch with water. Stir cornstarch mixture and broth into celery
and mushrooms. Cook, stirring frequently, until mixture thickens.
Stir in shrimp; heat through. Spoon each serving over 1/2 cup rice.
