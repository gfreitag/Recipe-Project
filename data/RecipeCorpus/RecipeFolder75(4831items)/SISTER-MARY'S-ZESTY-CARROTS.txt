Title: SISTER MARY'S ZESTY CARROTS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
-BETTE LELAND
6    Carrots
2 tb Grated onion
2 tb Horseradish
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Buttered bread crumbs
Clean and cut carrots into thin strips.  Cook until tender in salted
water. Place in 6 x 10" baking dish. Mix together grated onion,
horseradish, mayonnaise, salt, pepper and water. Pour over carrots.
Sprinkle with a 1/4 cup of butter crumbs.  Bake about 15 min. at
375    degrees F.
