Title: SORORITY CAKES
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 25 Servings
4    Egg white
1 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Pecans; broken small
1 c  Coconut flakes
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Corn flakes
Beat eggs stiff, then add the sugar and coconut.  Add vanilla and
pecans, and corn flakes.  Mould into small cakes and cook in a slow
oven 1-1/2 hours.

Mrs. Howard Kilpatrick
Comanche Study Club cookbook
Comanche, TX, 1927
