Title: SNOWY TOPPED GINGERBREAD/USING MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 9 Servings
1    Recipe gingerbread
TOPPING-
11 oz Can mandarin oranges*
1/2 c  Sour cream -- dairy
1/2 c  Dates -- chopped
2 tb Almonds -- chopped
1/2 c  Miniature marshmallows --
Opt
1/4 c  Flaked coconut
*Drained Prepare and bake Gingerbread as directed. Combine mandarin
oranges, sour cream, dates, almonds, and marshmallows. Spread over
gingerbread and sprinkle with coconut. Chill to set topping. Yield:
One 8-inch square,    about 9 servings
