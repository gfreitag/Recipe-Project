Title: SESAME-RICE COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts, No-gluten
Yield: 1 Batch
1 c  AM Sesame Tahini
1/2 c  Honey
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Almond extract
1/2 c  Chopped AM Sunflower Seeds
-OR- ground seeds, or nuts,
-- (almonds are best)
1 c  AM Brown Rice Flour
1 ts Non-alum baking powder
Cream tahini, honey, vanilla, and almond extract together, then mix in
ground or chopped nuts.  Mix rice flour and baking powder. Add to
sesame mixture. Form into balls and place on lightly oiled baking
sheet. Flatten slightly.  Bake at 350 F. for 15-20 minutes or until
barely golden.
