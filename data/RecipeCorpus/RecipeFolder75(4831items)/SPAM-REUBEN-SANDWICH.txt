Title: SPAM REUBEN SANDWICH
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Sauerkraut, drained (8 oz)
1 c  Shredded Swiss cheese
1/4 c  Thousand Island salad
-dressing
8 sl Rye bread
3 tb Butter or margarine,softened
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cut into
-8 slices (12 oz)
In small bowl, combine sauerkraut, cheese, and dressing. Spread each
bread slice on one side with butter. Spread half of sauerkraut
mixture on unbuttered side of 4 bread slices; top each with 2 slices
SPAM. Cover with remaining sauerkraut mixture. Top with remaining
bread slices, buttered-side up. In skillet or griddle over medium
heat, grill sandwiches until cheese melts and both sides are golden
brown.
