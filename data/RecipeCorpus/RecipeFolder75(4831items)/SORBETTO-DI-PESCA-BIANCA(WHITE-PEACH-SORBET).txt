Title: SORBETTO DI PESCA BIANCA(WHITE PEACH SORBET)
Categories: Ice cream
Yield: 4 Servings    1 1/2 lb White peaches
1/2 c  Granualted sugar
2 tb Freshly squeezed lemon juic

Wash peahces, then peel them and chop coarsely after removing pit. Put
peaches and sugar in food processor;blend until liquified. Add lemon
juice and blend for a few seconds longer. Transfer to ice cream maker
and freeze according to manufacturer; instruction
