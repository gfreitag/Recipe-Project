Title: SHERRY EGG NOG
Categories: Penndutch, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ts Sugar
1 ea Egg
1 x  Wine, sherry
1 x  Cracked ice (1/2 glass)
1 x  Milk (1/2 glass)
Measure 2 wine glasses of sherry, add other ingredients, shake
thoroughly and serve with dash of nutmeg on top. Source: Pennsylvania
Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
