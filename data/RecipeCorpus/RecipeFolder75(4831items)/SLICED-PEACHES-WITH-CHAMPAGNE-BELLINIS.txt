Title: SLICED PEACHES WITH CHAMPAGNE BELLINIS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Granulated Sugar
2 tb Lemon Juice
5    Peaches, peeled, sliced
1    Bottle of Champagne
Servings:  4

Place 4 of the slices peaches in a bowl, sprinkle with sugar and lemon
juice, then arrange slices decoratively on 4 serving plates. Make
"bellinis" by pureeing the remaining peach in a food processor or
blender and placing an equal amount of the puree in each of 4
Champagne flutes or tall wine glasses. Pour Champagne over the pureed
peaches in each glass. Serve sliced peaches with the "bellinis".
Serves 4.

From The Gazette, 91/07/31.
