Title: SISTER LIZZIE'S SHAKER SUGAR PIE
Categories: Desserts, Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
1/3 c  Flour
1 c  Brown sugar
1    Unbaked pie shell
2 c  Light cream
1 ts Vanilla
2 tb Butter
1 ds Nutmeg
Preheat oven to 350 F.

Thoroughly mix flour and sugar.  Spread evenly in bottom of pie shell.

Combine cream and vanilla.  Pour over sugar mixture. Slice butter into
pieces and distribute evenly over top of pie. Sprinkle with nutmeg.
Bake for 40 to 45 minutes or until firm.

From Herman Sommerville II on 03/09/93.  He indicates the recipe is
from The Golden Lamb Inn, Lebanon/OH. Posted by Cathy Harned.
