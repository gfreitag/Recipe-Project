Title: SAZON PREPARADO (PREPARED SEASONING)
Categories: Herbs/spice, Dom repub, Caribbean, Upload
Yield: 6 Cups
3 md Onions; chopped
1    Pepper, bell, green; seeded,
-chopped
1    Pepper, bell, red; seeded,
-chopped
4 lg Garlic cloves; peeled,
-chopped
1 tb Oregano
1 c  Scallions; chopped, both
-green and white
1/2 c  Parsley; coarsely chopped
1/2 c  Coriander; chopped
1 ts Tabasco sauce
1 tb Paprika, sweet
1 c  Tomato paste
1 c  Oil, olive
1/2 c  Vinegar, white
Salt; to taste
Combine all ingredients and blend, bit by bit, to a puree in
blender. Pour into saucepan and simmer, stirring constantly, for 5
minutes. Cool and bottle.
Used to flavor stews, beans, rice and vegetables. Easy to make and
useful to have on hand.
