Title: SKEWERED CHEESE BALLS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 24 Appetizers
1/2 lb Cream cheese
1/2 c  Finely chopped fresh parsley
Form cheese into 24 balls about 1 inch round.  Roll in parsley.
Skewer on long bamboo picks.  Refrigerate on waxed paper-lined tray,
covered, for at least 2 hours or overnight. Makes 24 appetizers.
*Substitute finely chopped nuts, a mixture of dill and parsley, or a
mixture of equal parts celery seed and coarsely ground black pepper.
