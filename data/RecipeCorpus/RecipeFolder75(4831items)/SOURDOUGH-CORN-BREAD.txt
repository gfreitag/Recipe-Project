Title: SOURDOUGH CORN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sourdough starter
1 1/2 c  Yellow corn meal
1 1/2 c  Evaperated milk
2 ea Eggs beaten
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Melted butter (warm)
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Soda
Thoroughly mix the starter, cornmeal, evap. milk, eggs, and sugar in a
large bowl. Stir in melted butter, salt, and soda. Turn into a 10 inch
greased pan ( iron skillet works real well) and bake at 450f for 30
mins
