Title: SAD CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
3    Eggs
1 lb Brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Biscuit mix
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Coconut
Beat eggs; add brown sugar and beat until creamy.  Add remaining
ingredients and mix well.  Bake at 350 degrees in a greased and
floured 9 x 13 x 2 inch pan until done.
