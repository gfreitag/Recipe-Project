Title: SHISH  TAOUK
Categories: Chicken
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Boneless chicken breasts
3    Cloves garlic, crushed
1/4 c  Olive oil
1    Lemon, squeezed
1 tb Paprika
1    (6 oz.) tomato paste
1 c  Yogurt
2 tb Sumac (Lebanese Spice,
Optional)
1 ts Each salt, pepper & ginger
Cut chicken in small cubes.  Mix remaining ingredients and marinate
chicken overnight (in refrigerator, or at least 2 hours). Thread
chicken on skewers and barbeque on charcoal grill or under broiler.
