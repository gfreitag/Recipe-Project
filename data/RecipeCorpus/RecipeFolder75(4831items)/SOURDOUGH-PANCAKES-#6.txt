Title: SOURDOUGH PANCAKES #6
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Sourdough starter
2 c  Flour
2 c  Lukewarm water
2 tb Sugar, level
1 ts Salt
3 tb Melted shortening or oil
2 ea Eggs
1 ts Baking soda
To the 1/2 cup starter and the flour and water. Beat until smooth and
let stand in a warm place overnight.
To the dough, add sugar, salt, and shortening. Beat in eggs.
Dissolve soda in one tablespoon of water and fold in gently, Do not
stir after soda has been added.
