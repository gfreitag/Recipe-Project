Title: SHORT CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 6 Servings
Text Only
Prepare one-egg cake.  Bake in shallow pan.  Cut in rounds. Split each
round and place sweetened fresh fruit or well-drained and sweetened
cooked fruit between the layers and on the top. Serve with whipped
cream. Garnish with some of the fruit sections. The Household
Searchlight
