Title: SNOW PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 Cups
3    Ripe bananas
1 c  Light cream
1/2 c  Sugar
3    7-oz bottles lemon-lime soda
-chilled
1 c  Lemon sherbet
3 tb Flaked coconut
Advance preparation:  Pour lemon juice into blender container. Slice
bananas into container.  Cover; blend till pureed. Combine banana
mixture, cream, and sugar; chill. Before serving: Add lemon-lime
soda, stirring gently to blend. Serve in small glasses or punch cups.
Top with spoonfuls of lemon sherbet; sprinkle with flaked coconut.
