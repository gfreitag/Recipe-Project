Title: SEAFOOD MARINARA
Categories: Pasta, Seafood
1 TB olive oil
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2    onion -- finely chopped
2    cloves garlic -- finely
chopped
:
1/2 c  white wine
3 c  good quality tomato sauce
1/4 ts red pepper flakes
10 lg shrimp -- peeled and
deveined
:
10    scallops -- rinsed
1/4 c  chopped parsley
12 oz angel hair pasta  or
vermicelli -- cooked and
drained
:
:

In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Stir in onion and
cook for 2 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Add white
wine and simmer for 2 minutes. Stir in tomato sauce and red pepper
flakes. Bring sauce to a simmer and stir in the shrimp. Cook for 1
minute and stir in scallops. Simmer for 2 minutes. Stir in parsley
and toss with pasta. Serve immediately.

Yield: 2 servings
