Title: SOUTHWESTERN SPICE MIX
Categories: Seasonings
Yield: 1 Cup
1 tb Ancho powder
1 tb Powdered cumin
1 tb Coriander
1 tb Kosher salt
2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Onion powder
2 ts Cayenne
Combine above in a small bowl. Store in a tightly sealed container.
