Title: SLIGHTLY POTTED COCKTAIL FRANKS
Categories: Appetizers, Alcohol
Yield: 36 Franks
1/2 lb Cocktail frankfurters; cut
-in half
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/4 c  Bourbon
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/4 c  Dark brown sugar
2 ts Dijon mustard
2 tb Tomato preserves
Combine sauce ingredients and bring to a boil.  Simmer 5 minutes. Add
the frankfurters, cover and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Transfer to a chafing dish and have toothpicks nearby. Makes 36
little franks. Source:  Just For Starters by Gloria Edwinn.
