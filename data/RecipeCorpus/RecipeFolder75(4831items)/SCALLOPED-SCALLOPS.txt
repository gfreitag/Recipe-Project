Title: SCALLOPED SCALLOPS
Categories: Seafood, Main dish, Casserole
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pt Scallops
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Cracker crumbs
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
2/3 c  Heavy Cream
Salt and Pepper, to taste
Wash and pick over scallops.  Melt butter and add crumbs. Put layer of
crumbs in buttered dish.  Cover with scallops, add half the cream and
season with salt, pepper and Worcestershire sauce. Repeat; cover with
buttered crumbs.  Bake at 350F. for 25 minutes until crumbs are golden
brown.
