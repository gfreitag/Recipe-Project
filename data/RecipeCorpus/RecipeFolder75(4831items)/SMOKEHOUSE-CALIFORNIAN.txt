Title: SMOKEHOUSE CALIFORNIAN
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 1 Servings
2 sl Whole grain bread
2 tb Mayonaise
1 tb Thick and chuncky salsa,
Drained
6    Fresh spinach leaves
2 sl Tomato
3 oz Sliced smked turkey
2 sl Cheddar cheese
2 sl Cooked turkey bacon
1/2 md Avocado, sliced
Combine mayonaise and salsa; spread on bread.  Layer remaining
ingredients in order listed. Of course, Reynolds recommends that
these sanwiches be wrapped fresh and tight in Reynols Wrap aluminum
foil ;) Enjoy! From: Paul Edward Mayer <mayer@wagner.Pdate:
