Title: SOFT MOLASSES COOKIES 2
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Margarine, softened
1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Molasses
1    Egg
2 1/2 c  Flour, all purpose
1 1/2 ts Ginger, ground
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts -Salt
2 ts Baking soda
14 c  -Water
Cream margarine and sugar until light and fluffy. Blend in Molasses,
1 egg and water. Add dry ingredients beat well. Drop from teaspoon on
well greased baking sheet.  Bake about 7-10 minutes in 375 F oven.
Source: Gail Teichmann
