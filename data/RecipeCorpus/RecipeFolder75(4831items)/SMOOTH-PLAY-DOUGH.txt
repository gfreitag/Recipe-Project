Title: SMOOTH PLAY DOUGH
Categories: Kids, Play dough, Crafts
Yield: 1 Batch
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Salt
2 tb Alum
2 tb Oil
Food coloring as desired
2 c  Water
SOURCE;  From the kitchen of Bill & Syd Kerr and posted on NVN Food
& Wine Board - note #18576....Formatted into MM by Ursula R. Taylor.
Bring water to a boil.  Mix other ingredients and add to water. Add
more flour if to sticky....Cool to use.
