Title: SCHULZ'S GUACAMOLE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Davidson
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Iceberg lettuce, head
1/4 c  Cheddar; grated
1/4 c  Monterey Jack; grated
1/2 c  Scallion; grated
8    Tomato, cherry; halved
1 c  Chili Cheese Fritos; crushed
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1    Avocado; peel/pit/mashed
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/3 c  Oil
1 tb Picante sauce
Tear lettuce into small pieces and combine with cheeses, scallions,
and tomatoes.  Cover and refrigerate in salad bowl until serving
time. Combine all ingredients for dressing and mix well.  Toss salad
with dressing and sprinkle top with crushed chips.

---*Dying for Chocolate*
Diane Mott Davidson
