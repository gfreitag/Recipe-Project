Title: SESAME WINGS
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
5 lb Chicken wings
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Corn oil
1/2 c  Sesame seeds
4 tb White wine; or sherry
2    Cl Garlic; peeled and halved
1 md Onion; chopped
4 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Accent; (optional)
1 ts Red chili pepper; * see note
1/2 ts Powdered ginger
Recipe by: "Joanne L. Schweikj" <SCHWEIKJ@FREDONIA.BITNET> * or 3, 5,
or 8 dried chili pods, depending on how hot you like your wings.

In blender container, place soy sauce, corn oil, sesame seeds, wine,
garlic, onion, brown sugar, Ac'cent, chili pepper and ginger. Cover
and blend until liquified. Pour marinade over wings in a shallow dish
and marinate for several hours or overnight, mixing occasionally.
Then place wings in a single layer in a large, shallow baking pan (or
two, if needed). Bake, uncovered, in 350-degree oven, basting
occasionally, for 45 minutes to one hour.  (Wonderful!)
