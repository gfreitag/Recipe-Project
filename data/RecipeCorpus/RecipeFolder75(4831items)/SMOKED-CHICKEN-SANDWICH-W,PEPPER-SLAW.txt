Title: SMOKED CHICKEN SANDWICH W/PEPPER SLAW
Categories: Sandwiches, Poultry
Yield: 4 Sandwiches
-Robbie Shelton
1 c  Thinly Sliced Cabbage
1/2 c  Red Pepper, seeded and
-sliced thin (1/2 pepper)
1 tb Mayonnaise
1 ts Mustard
Salt and Pepper (opt)
8 sl Seeded Rye Bread
1 1/2 c  Shredded Smoked Chicken
Combine the cabbage, red pepper, mayonnaise, and mustard in a bowl
and let the mixture stand for approximately 30 minutes. Taste for
seasoning and add salt and pepper to taste, if necessary.

Spread the cabbage mixture on half of the bread slices. Add a thick
layer of shredded chicken to each, and top with a second slice of
bread.

This recipe is from THE ARE OF THE SANDWICH by Jay Harlow.
