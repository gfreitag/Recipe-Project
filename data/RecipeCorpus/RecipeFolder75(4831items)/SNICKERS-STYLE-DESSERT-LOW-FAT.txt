Title: SNICKERS STYLE DESSERT LOW FAT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 c  Weight Watchers chocolate
Ice Cream
1 c  Frozen cool whip
1/4 c  Chunky peanut butter
1 pk Weight watchers instant
Chocolate pudding
Combine icecream and coolwhip until softened.  Add peanut butter and
dry pudding mix.  Stir until smooth. Freeze until firm. Each serving
is 1/2 protein 1/2 fat 1/2 milk and 25 optional calories. From a
weight watcher meeting I attended in 1989.
