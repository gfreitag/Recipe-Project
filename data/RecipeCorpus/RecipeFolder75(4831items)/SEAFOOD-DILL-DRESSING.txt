Title: SEAFOOD DILL DRESSING
Categories: Seafood, Other sauce
Yield: 2 1/2 c
MMMMM----------------------LA TIMES CA CKBK---------------------------
2 c  Mayonnaise
4 ts Dried dill weed or
1/4 c  Chopped fresh dill
1/4 c  White vinegar
2 1/2 tb Prepared mustard
2 1/2 tb Sugar
* For chilled seafood. The Assistance League of So. CA Tearoom Place
mayo in bowl and stir in dill, vinegar, mustard and sugar. Beat until
fluffy. Chill. Makes 2 1/2c
