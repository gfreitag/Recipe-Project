Title: SPECIAL PARTY DIP
Categories: Dips, American
Yield: 4 Servings
6 tb Cream cheese, Phila. brand
- Note: 6tb = 3oz -
2 ts Cream or Milk
2 ts French Dressing, Miracle
2 tb Ketchup
1 ts Grated onion; finely grated
1 ds Salt
Blend cream cheese with milk (or cream) until smooth Add remaining
ingredients and mix well Serve with crackers or potato chips!
