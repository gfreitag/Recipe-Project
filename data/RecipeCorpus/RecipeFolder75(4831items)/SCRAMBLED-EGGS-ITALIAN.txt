Title: SCRAMBLED EGGS ITALIAN
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Olive oil
1 tb Chopped fresh basil Garlic
-bread
12    Eggs
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
Heat olive oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat until very hot.
Beat the eggs with basil and cheese. Scramble them quickly and
lightly. Serve on slices of toasted Italian garlic bread.
