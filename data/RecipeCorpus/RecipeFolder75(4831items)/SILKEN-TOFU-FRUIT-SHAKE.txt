Title: SILKEN TOFU FRUIT SHAKE
Categories: Miamiherald, Vegan, Beverages
Yield: 2 Servings
1 pk Silken tofu (10 1/2oz)
1    16 oz bag frozen fruit
1 1/2 tb Honey or to taste
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Mix all ingredients in a blender until smooth.

Nutritional info per serving: 357cal; 25g pro, 43g carb, 13g fat (31%)
Exchanges: 1.7 fruit, 3.3 meat, .6 fat

Source: Miami Herald, 8/31/95 format: Lisa Crawford, 8/5/96
