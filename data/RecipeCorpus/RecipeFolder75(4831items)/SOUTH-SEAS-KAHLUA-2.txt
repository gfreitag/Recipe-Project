Title: SOUTH SEAS KAHLUA 2
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Mix recipes, New (to be
Yield: 1 Servings
2 oz Instant Coffee
3 1/2 c  Sugar
2 1/4 c  Brandy
1    Vanilla Bean -- cut in half
Combine instant coffee, sugar and 2 cups boiling water; stir in
brandy. Pour into 2 bottles; add vanilla bean to each bottle. Seal
and let age for at least 30 days. Yields 2 fifths.
