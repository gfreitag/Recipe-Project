Title: SCALLOPS    (DXDG05A)
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Chinese
1 lb scallops -- rinsed/
drained)
Yield: 4 Servings
:
1 TB cornstarch
1 TB soy sauce
sesame oil -- drops
:
6 TB cooking oil
1/2 ts garlic -- fresh minced
5    ginger -- slices
1/2 lb snow peas -- fresh
1    carrot -- thinly sliced
1/4 ts salt
1/4 c  water
2    green onions -- 2" pieces

Scallops with snow Peas (from"The Stephen Yam Seafood Wokbook) 1.
Combine cornstarch,  soy and sesame oil, stir well and set aside 2.
Heat wok with 1/2 the oil when the smoke begins to rise, add garlic
and ginger stir fry 30 sec,add snow peas, car- rots salt and water,
stir fry for 3 min remove to a platter 3. Heat remaining oil, stir
fry green onion for 30 sec then add scallops stir fry constantly for
4 min 4. Return snow pea mixture and sauce, Bring to a boil Serve at
once Enjoy Jane FROM: JANE HARRIS (DXDG05A)
