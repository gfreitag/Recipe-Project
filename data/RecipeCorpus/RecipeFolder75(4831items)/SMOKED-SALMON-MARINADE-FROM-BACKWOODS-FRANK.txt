Title: SMOKED SALMON MARINADE FROM BACKWOODS FRANK
Categories: Sauces, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 ga Hot water
1/2 c  Kosher salt
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
3 tb Garlic powder
3 tb Coarse ground black pepper
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1 tb Bay leaves -- crushed
Stir until dissolved. Allow brine to cool. Add salmon fillets, soak
covered for 3 hours in refrigerator. Remove fillets and air dry for
at least 1 hour. Smoke in a single layer for about 2 hours at 250
degrees or until firm and golden.
