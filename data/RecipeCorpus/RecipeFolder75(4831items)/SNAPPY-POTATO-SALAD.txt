Title: SNAPPY POTATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Potatoes, Tasteofhome
Yield: 8 Servings
8 md Potatoes, peeled, cubed
And cooked
8    Bacon strips, cooked and
Crumbled
1 c  Chopped onion
3/4 ts Salt
2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Prepared mustard
1/4 c  Prepared horseradish
1 c  Sliced celery
1/4 c  Shredded carrot
In a large bowl, combine potatoes, bacon, onion and salt; set aside.
IN a small saucepan, combine mayonnaise, mustard and horseradish;
cook until heated through, stirring constantly.  Add celery and
carrot; mix well. Pour over potato mixture and toss lightly to coat.
Serve warm.

Yield: 8-10 servings
