Title: SIX LAYER BEEF DINNER
Categories: Beef, Casseroles
Yield: 4 Servings
3    Peeled, sliced,
-raw potatoes
1/2 c  Chopped onion
3    Sliced raw carrots
8 oz Can tomato sauce
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
3/4 c  Puncooked rice
1 lb Ground beef
1    Tomato sauce can of water
Take a large rectangular, butter baking dish, then layer all the
ingredient in the order listed.  Cover and bake at 1-1/2 hours at 350
F. degrees.
