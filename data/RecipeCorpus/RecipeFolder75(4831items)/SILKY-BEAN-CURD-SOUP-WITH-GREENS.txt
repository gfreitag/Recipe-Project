Title: SILKY BEAN CURD SOUP WITH GREENS
Categories: New, Text, Import
2 c  chicken broth
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  water
1 ts soy sauce
2 TB miso paste
4 oz silky soft bean curd, -- cut
into 1/2-inch dice
:
8 oz spinach, -- stemmed and
leaves washed
Garnish crushed red pepper
flakes; toasted sesame
seeds
:
:
:
:

Bring chicken broth and water to simmer with soy sauce. Whisk a few
tablespoonfuls of the hot broth into the miso paste and reserve. Add
bean curd and spinach and cook over low heat for a minute to wilt.
Return miso paste to soup and simmer a few seconds to thicken, but DO
NOT BOIL. Adjust the seasoning. Garnish and ladle out.

Yield: 2 servings
