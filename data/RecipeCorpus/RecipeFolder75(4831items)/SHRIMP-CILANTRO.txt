Title: SHRIMP CILANTRO
Categories: Appetizers, Salads, Seafood, Jaw
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Margarine or butter
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 md Onion, chopped
2    Cloves garlic, finely
Chopped
16    Uncooked peeled, deveined
Medium shrimp
2 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
Salad greens
Heat margarine and oil in 10 inch skillet over medium-high heat. Cook
onion and garlic in margarine mixture, stirring occasionally, until
onion is tender. Stir in shrimp. Cook 1 minute; turn shrimp. Cook
about 2 minutes longer or until pink and firm. Remove shrimp to
seving dish; sprinkle with cilantro. Pour pan juices over shrimp.
Serve over salad greens.
