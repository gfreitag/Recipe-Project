Title: SPAM CANTINA APPETIZERS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 12 Servings
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat, cubed
-(12 oz)
1/2 c  Chopped green onion
1    Tomato, chopped
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1    Avacado, peeled and chopped
1/2 c  CHI-CHI's Picante Sauce
3 tb Chopped parsley
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Peanut oil
1 ts Garlic salt
12    Flour tortillas (8")
In skillet, saute SPAM until lightly browned. In bowl, combine green
onion, SPAM, tomato, celery, and avacado. In small bowl, stir
together picante sauce, parsley, lemon juice, peanut oil, and garlic
salt. Toss dressing with SPAM mixture. Serve with tortillas.
