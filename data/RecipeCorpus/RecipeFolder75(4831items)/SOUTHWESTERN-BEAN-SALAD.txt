Title: SOUTHWESTERN BEAN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Beans, Lois'
Yield: 10 Servings
1 cn 15 oz. kidney, wax, green or
Whatever you prefer, beans
1 cn 15 oz. black beans
1 cn 15 oz. garbanzo beans, rinsd
2    Celery ribs, sliced
1    Medium onion, diced
1    Medium tomato, diced, or can
Stewed or diced tomatoes
1 c  Corn, frozen or canned
3/4 c  Thick and chunky salsa
1/4 c  Veg oil
1/4 c  Lime juice
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground cumin
combine beans, celery, onion, tomato and corn. combine dressing
ingredients, mix well.  chill for at least two hours.
