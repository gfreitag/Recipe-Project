Title: SPARKLING CRANBERRY PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Crocker
Yield: 25 Servings
2 qt Cranberry juice cocktail,
-chilled
6 oz Frozen pink lemonade
-concentrate, thawed
32 oz Sparkling water, chilled
Mix cranberry juice cocktail and lemonade concentrate in punch bowl.
Just before serving, stir in sparkling water. 25 servings (about 1/2
cup each); 65 calories per serving.

Source: Betty Crocker's Cookbook, 6th Edition
