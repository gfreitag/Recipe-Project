Title: SIMPLE SALISBURY STEAK
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 lb Ground Beef
1/3 c  Dry Bread Crumbs
1    Egg Beaten
1/4 c  Onion Finely Chopped
1 1/4 c  Fresh Mushrooms Sliced
1.  In bowl, mix THOROUGHLY 1/4 cup of the soup, beef, bread
crumbs, egg and onion.  Shape FIRMLY into
6    patties.
2.  In skillet over medium-high heat, cook patties, a few at a
time, until browned on both sides.
Spoon off fat.
3.  Stir in remaining soup and mushrooms; return patties to
skillet. Reduce heat to low.  Cover; simmer 20 min. or until done,
turning patties occasionally. Prep time: 10 min. Cook time: 30 min.
