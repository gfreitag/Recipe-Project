Title: SOUR CREAM POTATOES
Categories: Misc
Yield: 12 Servings
2 lb Frozen hash browns,thawed
8 oz Cheddar cheese,grated
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 lg Carton sour cream
1 ts Salt
1    Onion, diced
Garlic salt to taste
2 c  Cornflakes,crushed
1    Stick margarine or butter
Mix all ingredients together except for cornflakes and butter. Place
in large casserole and bake @ 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Remove from
oven.Top with cornflakes and butter or margarine. Return to oven and
bake 15 minutes longer.
