Title: SOUR SHRIMP SOUP
Categories: Soups, Poultry, Thai
Yield: 6 Servings
-O.Evans VPRJ01A
1    Slice dried galanga, soaked
For 20 minutes
3    Stalks fresh lemon grass,
Bruised
2    Kaffir lime leaves
2 md Stalks coriander with roots
5    Guinea peppers lightly
Pounded w/ mortar
1 tb Roasted chili sauce
6 c  Chicken stock
10 oz Med shrimp, shelled
1 oz Straw mushrooms
2 tb Fish soy
1 1/2 tb Sugar
Lemon juice
Place all soup ingredients except last 3 in a pot over medium heat
and boil for 5 minutes.  Add the shrimp and mushrooms and just return
to a boil. Mix in seasonings. Garnish with green onion
