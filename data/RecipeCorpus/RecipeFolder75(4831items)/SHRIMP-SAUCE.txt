Title: SHRIMP SAUCE
Categories: Creole, Fish, Seafood, Sauces
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Chili sauce
1/4 c  Pickle relish
1/2 ts Mustard,prepared
1 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 ea Egg,hard-cooked,chopped
1/2 ts Onion,grated
1. Combine all ingredients; chill.
2. Serve with baked or broiled shrimp.
