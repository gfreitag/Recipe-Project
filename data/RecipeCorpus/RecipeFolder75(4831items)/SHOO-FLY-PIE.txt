Title: SHOO FLY PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
1 x  Pastry for 1 - 9" crust pie
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 c  Hot water
1/2 c  Molasses
1 ea Egg yolk,well beaten
3/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Mace
1/8 ts Ginger
1/8 ts Cloves
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Shortening,melted
1/2 ts Salt
Line 9" pie plate with pastry;chill.Dissolve soda and water; beat
into molasses.Blend in egg yolk;pour into pie crust.Combine remaining
ingredients to make crumbs.Sprinkle over pie.Bake in preheated 400
degree oven for about 10 minutes or until crust begins to
brown.Reduce temperature to 325 degrees;bake until top is firm.
