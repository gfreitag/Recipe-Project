Title: SPAM BREAKFAST BAGELS
Categories: Sandwiches, Breakfast
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat (12 oz)
2 tb Butter or margarine
6    Eggs, beaten
6 sl American cheese (1 oz)
6    Bagels, sliced
Slice SPAM into 6 slices (3x1/4"). In skillet, saute SPAM over medium
heat until lightly browned. Remove from skillet; keep warm. In same
skillet, melt butter; pour into beaten eggs. Cook and stir to desired
doneness. Layer scrambled eggs, SPAM, and cheese on bagel bottom.
Cover with bagel top.
