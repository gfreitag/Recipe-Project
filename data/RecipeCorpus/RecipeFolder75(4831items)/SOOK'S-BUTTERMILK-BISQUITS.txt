Title: SOOK'S BUTTERMILK BISQUITS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Of plain flour
3/4 ts Of baking soda
1/2 ts Of salt
4 tb Of pure lard
2/3 c  Of fresh buttermilk
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees.  Mix the flour, soda, and salt
together. Cut in the lard with 2 forks until the mixture has the
appearance of small white gravel.

Pour all the buttermilk in at one time.

Gently mix, and put the dough on a floured board.

Knead 6-7 times

Roll to a half inch thickness

Cut to desired size (we use floured water glasses) and put on a
greased baking sheet

cook 'til golden brown.
