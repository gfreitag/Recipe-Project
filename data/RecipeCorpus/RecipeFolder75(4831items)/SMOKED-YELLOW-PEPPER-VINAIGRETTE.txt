Title: SMOKED YELLOW PEPPER VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Sauces, Vegetables
2    yellow bell peppers -- cold
smoked, peeled
and seeded
Yield: 1 Servings
:
:
1/2 cn chipotle
1/4 md red onion -- coarsely
chopped
:
3 TB fresh lime juice
1/4 c  olive oil
1 ts sugar
salt
freshly ground black pepper
:
:

In a blender, combine the peppers, chipotle, onion, and lime juice and
blend. While the blender is still running, slowly add the olive oil
until emulsified. Add the sugar and season with salt and pepper to
taste.

Yield: about 1 cup
