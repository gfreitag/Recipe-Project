Title: SESAME MEAL
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sesame seeds
1/2 ts Sea salt
Place 2 tablespoons of the sesame seeds in a blender and blend well.
Add the remaining seeds by 2 tablespoon measurements, blending well
after each addition. Mix in the salt.

Use as a dressing on salads, casseroles, and broled vegetables, or
as a condiment.

Makes 1 cup

From _The Cookbook for People Who Love Animals_ from Gentle World
