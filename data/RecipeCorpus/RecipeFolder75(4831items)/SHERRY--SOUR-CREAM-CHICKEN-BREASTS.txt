Title: SHERRY--SOUR CREAM CHICKEN BREASTS
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Chicken breasts
1 cn Sliced mushrooms (3 oz.)
1 cn Cream mushroom soup
1/2 c  Sherry
1 c  Sour cream
Paprika
Arrange chicken in a shallow baking dish.  Cover with mushrooms.
Combine undiluted soup, sherry, and sour cream. Blend. Pour over
chicken, completely covering it; dust with paprika. Bake at 350 F.
for 1 1/2 hrs.
