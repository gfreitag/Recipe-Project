Title: SPANISH STYLE PORK CHOPS
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Cajun
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Pork chops
2 lg Bell peppers, sliced
2 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Crushed basil
1 ts Sugar
2 lg Onions, sliced
1 ts Salt
1 cn Whole tomatoes (16 oz.)
1/2 ts Beau Monde seasoning
1 ts Water
Trim fat from 1 chop.  Rub fat over bottom of skillet on med heat.
Dirscard fat.  Brown chops on both sides. Add onion, green pepper,
tomatoes and their liquid, basil, Beau Monde seasoning, salt and
sugar. Boil, reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 45 min. Remove
chops to serving dish. Blend cornstarch and water in cup until
smooth, gradually stir into liquid and cook until thickened, abt.
2-3 min. Courtesy Telephone Pioneers BillSpalding *P CRBR 38 A
