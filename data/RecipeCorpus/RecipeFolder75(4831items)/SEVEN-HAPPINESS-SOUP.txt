Title: SEVEN HAPPINESS SOUP
Categories: Soups, Low-cal
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Chicken Stock
1    Slice ginger
1    Garlic clove, halved
1/2 c  Shredded chinese cabbage
1/3 c  Coarsely chopped tofu
1/3 c  Thinly sliced mushrooms
1/3 c  Snow peas
1/3 c  Slivered bamboo shoots
1/3 c  Thin sliced water chestnuts
1/3 c  Sliced carrots
Combine stock, ginger and garlic.  Simmer 15 minutes. Remove garlic
and ginger. Add remaining ingredients to flavored stock, simmer
gently for 20 minutes.
