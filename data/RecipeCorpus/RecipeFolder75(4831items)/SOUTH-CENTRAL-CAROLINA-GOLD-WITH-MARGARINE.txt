Title: SOUTH CENTRAL CAROLINA GOLD WITH MARGARINE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Mustard
3/4 c  Red wine Vinegar
1 1/2 tb Margarine
2 ts Salt
1 1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Tabasco
1/2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 c  Sugar granulated
Mix thoroughly and simmer 10-15 minutes until sugar is dissolved.
