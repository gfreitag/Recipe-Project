Title: SAUSAGE LOAF
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Loaf French bread
1 lb Ground pork sausage
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
3    Stalks celery, chopped
2    Eggs
1/3 c  Milk
1 lb Velveeta cheese, grated
Slice loaf lengthwise with more on bottom.  Scoop out all of the
bread. Crumble and combine with milk and eggs. Fry sausage, onions
and celery; drain.  Mix bread and sausage together. Put mixture in
bottom half of loaf. Put cheese on top of meat mixture.  Add top of
loaf; brush with melted butter.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 25
minutes. Randy Rigg
