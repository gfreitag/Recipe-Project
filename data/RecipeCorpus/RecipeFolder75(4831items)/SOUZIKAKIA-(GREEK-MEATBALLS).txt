Title: SOUZIKAKIA (GREEK MEATBALLS)
Categories: Beef/veal, Greece
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Hamburger
1/2 sm Onion
Salt and pepper to taste
Olive oil
2 ea (8 oz) cans tomato sauce
1/2-1 cup water
2-3 garlic cloves
2-3 slices bread, dampened
-with wine
Flour
Cinnamon
1-2 bay leaves
Combine the hamburger, garlic, onion, bread, salt and pepper, and a
dash of cinnamon.  Form into meatballs. Roll in a little flour and
brown in the olive oil. Place directly in casserole. Combine tomato
sauce, bay leaves, water, and cinnamon to taste.  Pour over
meatballs; cover and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
Uncover the last 10-15 minutes of cooking. Souzikakia (Greek
Meatballs)
