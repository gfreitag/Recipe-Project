Title: SAUSAGE 'N APPLES
Categories: Meats, Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Sausage, ground
1 ea Garlic clove, crushed
2 tb Onion, chopped
8 ea Apple, medium green
1/4 c  Brown sugar
8 ea Bacon slices
In skillet saute' sausage, garlic and onion. Core apples and stuff
mixture into hole. Place in a baking dish. Sprinkle with brown sugar
and top with bacon. Bake 350 deg about 40 minutes or until apples are
soft.
