Title: SPAGETTI SAUCE WITH MEATBALLS
Categories: Beef
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Mazola oil
2    Cloves garlic
Pinch basil
Crushed chillies to taste
1/2    51/2oz size can tomato paste
19 oz Can tomato juice
1/2 ts Soda or sugar
Put oil, basil, garlic and chillies in pot on low heat to warm then
add tomato paste, tomato juice and soda or sugar. Make meatballs:
1lb. hamburg, 1 clove garlic chopped, salt & pepper and pinch of
basil and 1 egg, mix well and make into small balls and add to sauce.
Cook on low heat for about three hours.
