Title: SOYMILK YOGURT
Categories: Vegetarian, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 1 Servings
3 1/4 c  Soymilk
1 ts Soymilk yogurt
Use soy milk at room temperature.  Stir yogurt into soymilk, then
pour into a clean jar.  Cover & allow to stand at room temperature
for 14 to a8 hours.  Set aside a tablespoonful or two for starting
future yogurts. Use in place of dairy based yogurt.

Shurtleff & Aoyagi, "The Book of Tofu"
