Title: SPAGHETTI WITH GARLIC & OIL***
Categories: Italian, Pasta, Mrs. g, Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
- G. Granaroli XBRG76A
-MM:MK VMXV03A
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/4    Stick butter
3 tb Chopped garlic
2 tb Fresh chopped parsley
1/2 c  White wine
Salt and pepper to taste
16 oz Thin spaghetti- cooked
-according to dir.
Gratd cheese for serving
Melt the butter in olive oil. Add garlic and sautee for 5 min on low.
Add wine and simmer for 5 min. Toss with pasta and garnish with
parsley. Serve hot with grated cheese on the side. Serve with crusty
bread and salad.
