Title: SNOW PEAS WITH MUSHROOMS
Categories: China, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Snow peas
1 c  Mushrooms-sliced
2 tb Sesame oil
****seasonings****
2 tb Sherry
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Soy sauce
Heat the wok and add the oil. When it begins to smoke, add snow peas
and mushrooms.  Stir fry 2 minutes, then add seasonings. Stir fry 1
minute and serve immediately.
