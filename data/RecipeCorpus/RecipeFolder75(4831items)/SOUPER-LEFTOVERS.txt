Title: SOUPER LEFTOVERS
Categories: Leftover, Turkey
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Turkey light meat, skinless
* see note

Thawed * see note

1/2 c  Bell peppers -- chopped
10 3/4 oz Low-fat cream of mushroom
Soup -- * see note
1 tb Skim milk -- or more if
Needed

peppers, cream of mushroom soup, and milk. Heat and serve on toast
