Title: SHRIMP DIP *** (JHMB81A)
Categories: Dips, Appetizers, Party, Seafood
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Shrimp; cooked  shredded
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Brockles or french dressing
2 ea Celery; chopped very fine
1/2 c  Onion; chopped very fine
Combine all ingredients and let stand in refrigerator overnight.
NOTE: Have not had good luck using food processor so hand chopping is
recommended. Also, you may add garlic to taste if you desire. FROM:
JACK BENSON (JHMB81A)
