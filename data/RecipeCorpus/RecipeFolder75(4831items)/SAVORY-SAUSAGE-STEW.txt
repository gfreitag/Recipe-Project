Title: SAVORY SAUSAGE STEW
Categories: Sausages, Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Italian sausage links,
-casings removed
2 tb Olive or vegetable oil
1 md Onion, sliced
2    Garlic cloves, minced
2    Fresh Idaho potatoes, cut
-into 1-inch pieces
1    Green or red pepper, cut
-into strips 1 teaspoon
-dried basil leaves
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 md Tomato, cut into chunks
1/4 c  Chopped fresh parsley
In 3-quart microwave-safe casserole combine sausage, oil, onion and
garlic. Cover loosely with plastic wrap; cook on High 7 to 9 minutes
or until sausage is cooked, stirring twice to break up meat. Stir in
potatoes, green pepper, basil, salt and pepper. Re-cover; cook on
High 14 to 16 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Stir in tomatoes
and parsley. Re-cover; let stand 5 minutes .

Makes 4 servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
