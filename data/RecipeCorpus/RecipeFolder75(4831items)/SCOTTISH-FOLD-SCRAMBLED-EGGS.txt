Title: SCOTTISH FOLD SCRAMBLED EGGS
Categories: Petfood
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Butter
2    Eggs, beaten
1/4 c  Cottage cheese
Melt the butter in a frying pan, and remove from heat. Combine beaten
eggs and cottage cheese, and pour into frying pan. Return pan to low
heat, and stir the egg mixture with a for until set. Serve separately
or over the cat's regular food.  Typed in MMFormat by
