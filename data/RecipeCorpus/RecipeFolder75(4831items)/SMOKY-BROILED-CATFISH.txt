Title: SMOKY BROILED CATFISH
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
3 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Liquid wood-smoke flavoring
4 cn Fillets
In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, lemon juice, garlic powder, and
liquid wood-smoke flavoring. Preheat broiler pan for five minutes.
Coat pan with shortening spray. Place catfish on broiler pan; brush
with mixture. Broil three inches from heat for 4-6 minutes, basting
occasionally for 3 minutes or until catfish flakes easily.

Makes 4 servings.
