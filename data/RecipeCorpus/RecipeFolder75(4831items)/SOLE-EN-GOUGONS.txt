Title: SOLE EN GOUGONS
Categories: Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Fillet of sole
1 ea Egg yolk
1/4 c  Milk
1/8 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
3/4 c  Bread crumbs, dry
3/4 c  Peanut oil
Cut fillet of sole diagonally into 1/2 inch strips. Beat together egg
yolk, milk, salt and pepper until well blended. Soak fish strips in
egg mixture for a few minutes. Coat fish with bread crumbs. Roll each
piece between palms to get a rounded strip. Fry in shallow hot oil
(375 deg) until golden brown (3 min). Drain on paper towels. Serve on
warm platter.
