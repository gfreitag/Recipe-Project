Title: SOUR BEET JUICE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1 kg Beetroots, peeled and sliced
Place the beetroots in a glass jar and cover with 2 litres of lukewarm
(boiled) water. Place a slice of whole meal bread on top to speed up
the process of pickling. Tie a piece of gauze on top of jar and leave
to stand in a warm place. After 4 to 5 days remove the froth.

(Use to make borsch and cold borsch)
