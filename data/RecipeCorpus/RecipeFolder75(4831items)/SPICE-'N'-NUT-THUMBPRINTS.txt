Title: SPICE 'N' NUT THUMBPRINTS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 24 Servings
3/4 c  (1 1/2 sticks) butter,
Softened
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
1 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Finely ground pecans or
Almonds
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 c  Jam, such as strawberry,
Raspberry, or peach
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  cream butter & sugar in lg mixer bowl
until light & fluffy.  Beat in egg & vanilla. Add 1/2 of flour; mix
well. Add remaining flour, nuts & cinnamon; mix well. Shape dough
into 1" balls. Place 1" apart on unbuttered cookie sheets. Lightly
press center of each ball with thumb, or the handle of a wooden
spoon, leaving cup-like indention. Fill each indention w/ about 1/4
ts jam. Bake 18 min. or until lightly browned around edges.  Transfer
to wire racks; cool completely.
