Title: SCALLOPED CANNED SOUP
Categories: Soups, Text
Yield: 6 Servings
Text Only
Thicken canned vegetable soup slightly.  Fill a well-oiled baking
dish with alternate layers of the vegetable soup and diced cooked
meat or condensed beef soup. Cover with buttered crumbs. Bake in
moderate oven (375 F) about 55 minutes.  6 servings.
