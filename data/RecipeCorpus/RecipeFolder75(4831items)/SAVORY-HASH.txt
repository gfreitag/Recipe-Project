Title: SAVORY HASH
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Lean ground beef
2 tb Veg. oil
1 c  Cellery, chopped
1 c  Onion, chopped
1 c  Green pepper, chopped
2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Chili powder
2 c  Canned tomatoes
1/2 c  Uncooked rice
Cook cellery, onion and green pepper in the veg. oil until just
tender. Add ground beef and seasonings. Cook until the ground beef is
crumbly. Add tomatoes and rice. Blend thoroughly. Cook in covered
casserole on low heat for 45 to 60 minutes.
