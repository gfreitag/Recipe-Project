Title: SEAFOOD SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
5 oz Precooked shrimp
10    Imitation crab sticks
1    Red pepper
2 tb Miracle whip
1    Stalk of celery
1 sm Onion
5 c  Macaroni, cooked
3 tb Slaw dressing
Put 5 cups of macaroni in to a large bowl; add cut up crab,
vegetables and shrimp.  Add the Miracle Whip and slaw dressing. Mix
all together and chill thoroughly. Randy Rigg
