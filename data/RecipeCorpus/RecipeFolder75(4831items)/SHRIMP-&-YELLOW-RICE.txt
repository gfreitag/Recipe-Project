Title: SHRIMP & YELLOW RICE
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 3 Servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
2    Garlic cloves
1    Small onion
1    Green pepper
1 lb Raw shrimp;peeled & deveined
3/4 c  Tomatoes
1 pn Saffron
1 tb Salt
1/2 ts Yellow food coloring
2 c  Water
1 c  Rice
1/2 c  Green peas; tiny
1 cn Pimentos; small jar
1    Parsley
Heat olive oil and saute the chopped garlic, onion, and green
pepper. When half done, add the shrimp. When shrimp turn pink, add
the tomatoes, saffron, salt, coloring and water. When mixture starts
boiling, add 1 cup rice. Bake in oven at 350 deg. for 15 minutes.
Garnish with peas, pimiento and parsley leaves.
From:  "The Gasparilla Cookbook"
Recipe from:  The Columbia Restaurant; Tampa, Fl
