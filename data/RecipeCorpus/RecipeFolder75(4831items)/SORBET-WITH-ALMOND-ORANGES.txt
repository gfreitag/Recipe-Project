Title: SORBET WITH ALMOND ORANGES
Categories: New, Text, Import
4 lg Navel oranges, peeled, --
thinly Sliced rounds
Yield: 4 Servings
:
1 pt lime sorbet
1/4 c  mango juice
Fresh shredded mint leaves
:

Sprinkle orange slices with mango juice and toss well. Chill. Drain
slices and arrange in overlapping fan pattern on plates. Add scoops
of sorbet to plate and garnish with mint. Serve immediately. You may
substitute orange juice for mango juice if desired.
