Title: SKILLET STICKY CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Oil
1    Pouch onion soup mix
1/4 c  Barbecue sauce or ketchup
3 lb Broiler-fryer chicken,cut up
1/2 c  Apricot or peach preserves
1/4 c  Water
In a 10" skillet over medium heat,in hot oil,cook chicken pieces
until browned on all sides.Pour off fat.In a small bowl,combine
remaining ingredients;stir into skillet.Reduce heat to low;cover and
simmer 30 minutes until chicken is tender,stirring occasionally.
