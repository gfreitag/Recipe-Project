Title: SLAVEDRIVER JELL-O
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lg Box Orange Jell-O; * see
-note
2 c  Boiling Water
1 1/2 c  Cold Water
1/2 c  Prospector Joe's Bourbon; or
-equivalent
* (the 4-cup, or 8 half-cup servings, size)

Follow package directions, using the bourbon with the cold water

NOTES : I tried this recently.  It's a nice sweet jell-o, but there
are probably better flavors to use with your Bourbon... such as
Peach, or Lemon (for a whisky sour)
