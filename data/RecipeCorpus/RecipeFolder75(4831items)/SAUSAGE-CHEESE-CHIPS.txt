Title: SAUSAGE-CHEESE CHIPS
Categories: Appetizers, Crocker
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Fully cooked kielbasa
36    Tortilla chips
1 1/2 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
4 oz Chopped green chilies,
-drained
Coarsely chop kielbasa; reserve. Arrange chips on greased 12-inch
pizza pan. Sprinkle kielbasa and cheese evenly over each chip; top
with chilies. Bake uncovered in 375F oven until cheese is melted, 3
to 5 minutes. 6 servings; 40 calories per serving.

Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
