Title: SHORTBREAD WITH AMARANTH
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Recipe
1/8 c  AM Amaranth Seed
3/4 c  AM Whole Wheat Flour
1/4 c  Honey
3/4 c  AM Rice Flour
1/4 c  Butter
Mix all ingredients well to make a smooth dough.  Roll out 1/4 to 1/2
inch thick into an approximate 8" circle. Score halfway through the
dough. Bake on an ungreased cookie sheet for 17 minutes at 350 F. Cut
into wedges after cooled and serve.
