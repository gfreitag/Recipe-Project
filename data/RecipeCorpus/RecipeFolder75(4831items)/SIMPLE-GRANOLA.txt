Title: SIMPLE GRANOLA
Categories: Snacks, Honey
Yield: 3 Servings
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Coconut
6 c  Rolled oats
Chopped nuts -- optional
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Oil
1 tb Salt
1 c  Honey
2 ts Vanilla
Mix the wet ingredients and blend well. Toss the dry ingredients
together, add to the wet mixture and toss to coat. Spread out on two
greased cookie sheets and bake 1 hour or until dry and golden. Store
in airtight container.
