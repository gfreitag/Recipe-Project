Title: SIX CAN CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Mushrooms, small
1 cn Evaporated milk, small
1 cn Chow mein noodles, large
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 cn Cream of celery soup
2 cn Boned chicken, small
Mix all together and add a dash of garlic powder. Cover with 1 cup of
shredded Cheddar cheese and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
