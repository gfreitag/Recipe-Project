Title: SOLE IN WINE
Categories: Fish
Yield: 2 Servings
FROM: New  Mexico BBS
1.5 lb fillet of sole or other white fish .33 lb mushrooms, whole 1 c
dry sauterne .25 c cream 2 x egg yolks Place sole in glass baking
dish. Add mushrooms and wine. Bake until tender at 350 degrees, about
30 minutes. Drain liquid into sauce pan and reduce over medium heat
until amount left is equal to initial amount of sauterne. In a cup,
mix yolks and cream together thoroughly; add to sauce pan, using
whisk to blend. Pour over fish and return to oven briefly to glaze. =
