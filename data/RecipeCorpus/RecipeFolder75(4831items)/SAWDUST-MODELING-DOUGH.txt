Title: SAWDUST MODELING DOUGH
Categories: Crafts, Kids
Yield: 1 Batch
1 c  Sawdust
1/2 c  Wallpaper paste
Water
SOURCE:  Posted on Delphi Cust 229 board on July 26, 1995 by Lynne
Sammon.  MM format by Ursula R. Taylor.
Mix together sawdust and paste.  Add enough water to make a mixture
like soft pie crust or cookie dough. Make a crude wire frame for your
animal or whatever. Squeese and pat the modeling mixture onto this
form and shape as desired..  When dry, can be left as is, shellaced
or painted.
