Title: SKILLET COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Egg yolks
1    Stick of butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Pecans, chopped
1 pk 8 oz. dates, chopped
2 c  Rice Krispies
Coconut, shredded
Cook the first three ingredients in skillet for 5 minutes. Add dates
and pecans.  Cook for 5 minutes longer. Remove from heat; add Rice
Krispies and mix well. Cool enough to handle, then shape into small
balls and roll in coconut. Randy Rigg
