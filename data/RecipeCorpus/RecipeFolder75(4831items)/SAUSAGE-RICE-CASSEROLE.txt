Title: SAUSAGE-RICE CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 10 Servings
1 lb Sausage, bulk
1 c  Celery; chopped
1 c  Onion; chopped
1 cn Soup, cream of mushroom
1 cn Soup, cream of chicken
1 cn Water
1 c  Rice, regular; uncooked
Brown sausage; drain.  Combine all ingredients in a large bowl; stir
gently to mix.  Spoon into a 3-quart casserole; cover and bake at 325
degrees for
2    hours.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, May, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy
Coleman
