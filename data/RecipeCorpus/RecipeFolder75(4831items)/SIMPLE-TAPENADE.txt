Title: SIMPLE TAPENADE
Categories: Appetizers, Other sauce, Relishes
Yield: 6 Servings
4    Anchovy fillets, rinsed
1/2 c  Pitted kalamata olives
2 tb Capers, minced
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 tb Dijon mustard
In a blender or food processor, combine all ingredients. If too thick,
drizzle more oil into mixture as you blend. Serve with bread or over
vegetables.
