Title: SHRIMP ARNAUD
Categories: Seafood, Appetizers
Yield: 8 Servings
1 ea Shrimp, frozen (bag)
1/4 c  Peanut oil
1/4 c  Vinegar, white
1 tb Chive, chopped
1 tb Chili sauce
2 ts Mustard, hot
1 ea Garlic clove (medium)
1/4 ts Paprika
Cook bag of shrimp according to package directions. Drain. Cool.
Combine oil, white vinegar, chives, chili sauce, hot mustard, garlic
and salt. Add cooled shrimp; Toss well. Cover and refrigerate at
least 3 hours, tossing occasionally. To serve, drain, sprinkle
lightly with paprika.
