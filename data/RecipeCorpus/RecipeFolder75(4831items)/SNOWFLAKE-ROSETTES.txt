Title: SNOWFLAKE ROSETTES
Categories: Miamiherald, Desserts
Yield: 4 Dozen
MMMMM-----------------FORMATTED BY LISA CRAWFORD----------------------
4    Eggs
2 c  Milk
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Oil for frying
Beat eggs until just before they begin to foam.  Add the milk a
little at a time.  Add flour gradually. Beat until smooth. Add sugar,
salt and vanilla. Heat hot oil/ Place iron in hot oil, then in batter
Fry one at a time until golden.  Sprinkle with granualated sugar.

Nutrtional info per serving w/o oil:  34 cal; 1g pro, 5g carb, 1g
fat(22%), .1g fiber, 19mg chol, 33mg sodium

Source: Cook's Corner, Miami Herald, 12/14/95
