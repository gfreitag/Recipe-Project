Title: SLIM & SAVORY SPINACH POTATO SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Chicken broth
3 md Leeks white part only
Sliced OR
3 md Onions chopped
6 oz Spinach trimmed and chopped
3 c  Cubed potatoes
2 tb All natural sour cream and
Butter flavor sprinkle
Freshly ground black pepper
Bring broth, leeks and spinach to a simmer. Cover and cook 5
minutes. Add potatoes and cook, covered, 20 minutes, or until
potatoes are tender. Puree half the mixture; return to saucepan. Stir
in butter sprinkles and pepper. Serve hot or cold.
