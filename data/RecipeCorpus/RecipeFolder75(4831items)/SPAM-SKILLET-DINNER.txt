Title: SPAM SKILLET DINNER
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 8 Servings
3 md Zucchini
1    Onion, thinly sliced
1 tb Oil
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat (12 oz)
3 md Potaoes, peeled, sliced
1 cn Tomatoes (16 oz)
1/2 ts Garlic powder
3/4 ts Basil
1/2 ts Oregano
Cut zucchini in 1/2" slices. In large skillet over medium-high heat,
saute zucchini and onion in oil 5 minutes, stirring often. cut SPAM
into 8 slices; halve each slice. Add potatoes, carrots, and SPAM to
skillet; pour tomatoes over all. Sprinkle with herbs. Cover and
simmer 25 minutes or until potatoes are tender, stirring occasionally.
