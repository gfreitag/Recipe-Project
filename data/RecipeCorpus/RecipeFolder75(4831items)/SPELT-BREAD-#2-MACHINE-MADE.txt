Title: SPELT BREAD #2 MACHINE MADE
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 14 Servings
2 tb Canola oil
4 oz Milk
5 oz Water
3 tb Molasses
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Rolled oats
1 c  Spelt flour
2 c  Bread flour
1 ts Active dry yeast ; red star-
Recipe by: Deborah Rech I originally had put a little gluten in the
above recipe and it came out a little too light.  This further
confirmed my belief that spelt must have a good gluten content.
