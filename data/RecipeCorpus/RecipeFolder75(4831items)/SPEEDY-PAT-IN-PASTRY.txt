Title: SPEEDY PAT-IN PASTRY
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Sugar
3 ts Milk; cold
3/4 ts -Salt
1/2 c  Oil; corn
Sift flour, sugar and salt directly in 9-10" pie plate. Combine oil
and milk in measuring cup and beat with fork till creamy. Pour all at
once over flour in pie plate. mix with a fork till flour is
completely dampened. Push the dough with your fingers to line bottom
and sides of pan. If you're fussy, press it down
