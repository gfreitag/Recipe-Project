Title: SIMPLE SAUSAGE BALLS
Categories: Appetizers, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Skinless sausage (Owen's Hot
-works well)
1 lb Grated Colby or Mild Chedar
-Cheese
2 c  Pioneer or Bisquick
**

Mix together (someone suggested using a zip-lock bag for mixing. Roll
into about 1" balls.  Place on a shalow baking pan (sides required to
catch the grease) and bake at 350 deg F until done. Both cooked and
uncooked balls may be frozen.
