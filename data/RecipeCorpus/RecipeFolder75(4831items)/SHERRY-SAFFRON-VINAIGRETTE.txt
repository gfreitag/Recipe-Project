Title: SHERRY SAFFRON VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Sauces, Dressings
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 ts Sugar
1/8 ts Crumbled saffron threads
2 tb Sherry vinegar (available
-at specialty foods shops
-and some supermarkets)
1 ts Minced pimiento-stuffed
-olive
1/3 c  Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
In a bowl whisk together the sugar, the saffron, the vinegar, the
olive, and salt and pepper to taste, add the oil in a stream whisking
and whisk the vinigrette until it is emusified. Makes about 1/2 cup.
