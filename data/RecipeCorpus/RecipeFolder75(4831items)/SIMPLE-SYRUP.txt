Title: SIMPLE SYRUP
Categories: Syrup, Cocktail, Beverages
Yield: 3 Cups
3 c  Water
4 c  Granulated sugar
1.  Bring water to boil in a large saucepan.  Reduce heat to medium,
add sugar and stir until completely dissolved. 2. Reduce heat to low
and simmer 5 minutes until mixture has a light syrup consistency. 3.
Cool, pour into jars, cover and refrigerate.  Refigerated in a
tightly closed jar, it keeps indefinitely. Source: Woman's Day
12/17/96
