Title: SAUSAGE BALLS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 10 Servings
1 lb Bulk Sausage (Hot Or Mild)
2 c  Sharp Chedder Cheese Grated
3 c  Bisquick
Combine all ingredients and shape into balls the size of walnuts.
Bake on cookie sheet at 350 degrees F. for 15 minutes. Makes about 5
dozen. NOTE:
Sausage Balls may be frozen before or after baking. If frozen
after, defrost and reheat at 350 degrees F.
