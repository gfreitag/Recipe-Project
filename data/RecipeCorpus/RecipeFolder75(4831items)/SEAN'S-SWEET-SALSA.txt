Title: SEAN'S SWEET SALSA
Categories: Condiments
1    clove garlic -- diced
Yield: 1 Servings
1 TB ginger root -- diced
1/2 md Spanish onion -- diced
1    splash Rose's lime juice
1    tangerine -- peeled and
diced
:
1/2    pear -- cored and diced
4    strips dry mango -- diced
3    sprigs watercress -- finely
chopped
:
2 TB dried mint -- crumbled
1 TB olive oil
Salt and pepper
:

In a medium bowl mix together the garlic, ginger and onion with the
lime juice. Stir in remaining ingredients and season to taste. Serve
with chips, seafood and vegetables.
