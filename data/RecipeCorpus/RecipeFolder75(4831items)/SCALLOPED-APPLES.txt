Title: SCALLOPED APPLES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Recipe
6    Tart apples
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Butter
3 c  Bread crumbs
Peel and slice apples.  Mix apples, sugar and spices. Melt butter and
pour over bread crumbs.  Place layer of apples in buttered casserole,
then bread crumbs. Alternate layers, ending with crumbs on top. Bake
at 350 F for 45 minutes.  Serve with pork chops or ham. But some
whipped cream on it and serve it as a dessert.
