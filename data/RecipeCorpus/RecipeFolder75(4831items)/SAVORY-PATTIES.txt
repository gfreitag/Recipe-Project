Title: SAVORY PATTIES
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings    2 1/3 c  soybeans, cooked
1/4 c  onion, chopped
2 x  eggs or egg substitute
1 1/4 c  cracker crumbs
1/2 t  salt
1/3 t  Vegex (herbed salt)
1/3 c  tomato juice
0 x Salt and celery salt to taste

Grind the beans through the food chopper.  Combine all ingredients
and mix well. Shape into patties and fry. If desired, mixture may
also be placed in an oiled baking dish and baked. ~--
