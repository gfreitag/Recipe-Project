Title: SO EASY BANANA CARAMEL PIE (ALTERNATE FOR MOC
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Baked 8" pie shell
1/4 c  Cold water
2    Egg yolks
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Boiling water
1 tb Butter or
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
3    Or 4 ripe bananas
1/2 c  Heavy cream
-
Early in day: In saucepan, mix cold water with egg yolks; stir in
combined sugars, flour, and salt. Gradually add boiling water,
stirring briskly. Cook, stirring, 3 to 5 min., or until smooth and
thick. Add butter and vanilla; cool 5 min., stirring occasionally.
Pour into baked pie shell. Refrigerate till serving time.

To serve: Slice bananas over filling. Whip cream; spread over all.
