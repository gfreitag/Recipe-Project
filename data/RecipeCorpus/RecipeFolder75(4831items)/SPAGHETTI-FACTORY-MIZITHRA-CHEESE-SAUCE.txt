Title: SPAGHETTI FACTORY MIZITHRA CHEESE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Pasta
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Flour
4 tb Butter
1 ts Olive Oil
10 ml Garlic -- crush
2 tb Parsley -- chopped
1/2 c  Half and Half
1 c  Milk
3/4 c  Romano Cheese
White Pepper to taste
Make a light roux over a medium fire with olive oil and butter. Add
flour and cook flour out of raw state; add garlic. Pour milk and half
and half in the roux with pepper and parsley. At the last moment add
the cheese. Place over hot pasta.
