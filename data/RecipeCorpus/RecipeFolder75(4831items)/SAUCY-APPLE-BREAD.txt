Title: SAUCY APPLE BREAD
Categories: Abm, Breads, Misc
Yield: 1 Loaf
MMMMM------------------------1 POUND LOAF-----------------------------
1/2 c  Apple cider
1 1/3 c  Bread flour plus 3/4 cup
1 tb Wheat flour
1 ts Salt
2 1/2 tb Yogurt, plain
2 1/2 tb Honey
1/3 ts Vanilla
2 1/2 tb Walnuts, unchopped
2 1/2 tb Egg, beaten
1/3 c  Applesauce
1/3 c  Apples, unpeeled
1 1/4 ts Yeast
Place all ingredients in machine and push Start!
