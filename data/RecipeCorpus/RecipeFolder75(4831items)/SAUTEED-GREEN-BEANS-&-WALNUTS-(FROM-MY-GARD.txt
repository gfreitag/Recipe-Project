Title: SAUTEED GREEN BEANS & WALNUTS (FROM MY GARD
Categories: Wasnone
2 TB olive or walnut oil
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  walnuts
1 lb green beans -- tipped and
blanched
:

In large pan or wok heat oil. Add green beans and walnuts. Saute for 3
minutes. Serve.
