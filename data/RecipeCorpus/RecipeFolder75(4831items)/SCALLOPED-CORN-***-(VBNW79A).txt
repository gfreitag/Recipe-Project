Title: SCALLOPED CORN *** (VBNW79A)
Categories: Vegetables, Bar-b-q, Casserole
Yield: 8 Servings
2 ea Butter; melted
4 ea Egg; beaten
2 c  Sour cream
2 ea Flako cornbread mix
Mix above ingredients together. Stir in 1 can creamed corn & 1 can
niblets corn.  Pour into ungreased 13" x 9" pan & bake at 375 for 45
minutes. Hope this helps! FROM: N ARNOLD-MONTGOMER (VBNW79A)
