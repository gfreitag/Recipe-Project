Title: SOUR CREAM LEMON MUFFINS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Butter, melted
1 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Grated lemon peel
1 c  Chopped pecans
1/2 c  Milk
1    Egg beaten
In a large bowl combine all the dry ingredients but the 1 T sugar. In
a small bowl combine the wets.  Stir til moistened. Spoon into muffin
cups- sprinkle remaining sugar on tops. Bake at 400 for 20-25 min.
Makes 12.
