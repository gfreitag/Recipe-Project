Title: SCARLET O'HARA
Categories: Mixed, Drinks, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
2 oz Southern Comfort
6 oz Cranberry juice
1 ds Fresh lemon juice
Fill a highball glass with ice cubes. Add Southern Comfort and
cranberry juice. Stir and top with a dash of lemon juice.
