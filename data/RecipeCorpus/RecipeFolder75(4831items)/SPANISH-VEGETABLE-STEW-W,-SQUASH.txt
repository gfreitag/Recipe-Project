Title: SPANISH VEGETABLE STEW W/ SQUASH
Categories: Main dish, Spanish, Vegetarian, Vegan, Easy
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lg Onions, chopped
4 lg Green bell peppers, chopped
4 lg Tomatoes, peeled & chopped
1 lb Yellow squash, chopped
2 md Potatoes, cubed
5 tb Olive oil
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
2 tb Chopped parsley
1 1/2 ts Salt
Heat oil and saute onions for a few minutes. Add remaining
ingredients and a little water. Cover & simmer for 40 minutes,
stirring occasionally.

Source: "A Passion for Vegetables" by Vera Gewanter
