Title: SPAGHETTI NAPOLI
Categories: Pasta, Low-fat, Low-cal
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Corn Oil (or preferred oil)
1 lg Onion, cut into thin wedges
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
2 ea Medium green pepper, chopped
1 ea Meduim red pepper, chopped
2 1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms (8 oz)
1 cn Diced tomatoes in juice
1 1/2 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Pepper
8 oz Spaghetti or egg noodles
2 tb Grated parmesan cheese
In large skillet heat oil over medium high heat.  Add onion and
garlic; saute 1 minute.  Add green and red peppers and mushrooms;
saute 2 minutes.
Add tomatoes and juice, basil and pepper; bring to boil. Reduce heat
to low; simmer 5 minutes.  Toss with noodles and parmesan cheese.
Makes 4 servings.
