Title: SHRIMP HORS D'OEUVRES
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 lb Cooked Cocktail Shrimp
Worcestershire Sauce
Make sure shrimp are clean and deveined.  Pat dry on paper towels.
Place shrimp in a small bowl and cover with Worcestershire Sauce to
marinate. Allow to marinate covered in the refrigerator for 4 hours.
Drain from marinade.  Skewer shrimp with toothpicks and serve on
platter.
