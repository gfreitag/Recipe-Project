Title: SMOKED OYSTER DIP
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 Servings
18 oz Package cream cheese
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Ketchup
2 ds Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Tabasco sauce
1 cn Smoked oysters, chopped
Combine ingredients. Serve with chips or crackers. From the files of
Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
