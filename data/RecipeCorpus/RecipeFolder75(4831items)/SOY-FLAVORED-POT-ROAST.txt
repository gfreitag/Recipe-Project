Title: SOY FLAVORED POT ROAST
Categories: Low-cal, Beef
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM--------------------------NPFN03A-------------------------------
3 lb Pot roast type meat, boned a
2 tb Dry sherry
1 1/2 ts Ginger
2    Medium ribs celery, sliced
1/3 c  Each, soy sauce and water
1    Garlic clove, minced
1    Medium onion cut in wedges
Brown meat in broiler on broiler pan sprayed with non-stick spray. In
Dutch oven bring to boil soy sauce, water, sherry, garlic and ginger.
Add meat, onion and celery. Cover and simmer, turning occasionally, 1
1/2 to 2 hours or until meat is tender. Serve sliced with pan liquid.
215    calories per serving
