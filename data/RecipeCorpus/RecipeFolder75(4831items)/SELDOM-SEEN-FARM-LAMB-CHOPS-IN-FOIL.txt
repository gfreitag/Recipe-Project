Title: SELDOM SEEN FARM LAMB CHOPS IN FOIL
Categories: Lamb
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Lamb chops
1    Zucchini -- unpeeled,
Sliced
1 sm Onion -- sliced
1 sm Green pepper -- cored,
Sliced
1 sm Tomato -- quartered
ds Basil
Salt and pepper
Garlic powder
Make a packet with the above in each, wrapped tightly. Grill on low
about 25-30 minutes.  Be sure to turn several times. Another great
way to use this recipe is to bake at 350^ for 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 hours.
Serves 1
