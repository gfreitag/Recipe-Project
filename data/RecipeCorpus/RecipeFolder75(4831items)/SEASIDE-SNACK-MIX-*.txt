Title: SEASIDE SNACK MIX *
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 10 Cups
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
7 oz Cheddar snack crackers
6 oz Parmesan fish crackers
2 c  Oyster crackers
1 1/2 c  Thin pretzel sticks; break
- in half
In large bowl, combine all; toss gently. Store tightly covered.
