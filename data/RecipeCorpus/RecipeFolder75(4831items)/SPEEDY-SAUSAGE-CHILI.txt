Title: SPEEDY SAUSAGE CHILI
Categories: Meats, Turkey, Poultry, Chili, Low-fat
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 lb Spicy turkey sausages
1 cn Tomato soup
2 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Dried oregano
1 ds Worcestershire sauce
1 cn Beans, drained
Grill the sausages under the broiler until browned. Drain and discard
any melted fat. Combine with reamaining ingrediants in a saucepan.
Simmer 5 minutes, until heated through.
