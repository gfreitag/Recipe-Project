Title: SHISH KEBAB
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Beef, cubed
1 lb Mushrooms
1 c  Tomato wedges
1 c  Onions, quarterd
2 ea Green pepper, quarterd
1/2 c  Salad oil
1 ea Clove garlic
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 ts Soy sauce
1/2 ts Basil
1 tb Lemon juice
Marinate beef in oil, vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, lemon juice, basil,
salt and pepper to taste - from 3 to 4 hours. Place on skewer,
alternating with mushroom caps, quartered tomatoes, peppers and
onions. Roast, basting with marinade.
