Title: SOUTH CENTRAL CAROLINA GOLD IV
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Mustard
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Cider vinegar
2 tb Chili powder
1 ts Black pepper
1 ts White pepper
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
5 dr Tabasco sauce
1/2 ts Soy Sauce
2 tb Butter
Mix together all but the Soy Sauce, butter and simmer 15 minutes.
Stir in Soy Sauce and butter.
