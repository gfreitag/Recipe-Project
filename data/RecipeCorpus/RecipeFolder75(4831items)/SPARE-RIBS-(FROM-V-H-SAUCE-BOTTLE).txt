Title: SPARE-RIBS (FROM V-H SAUCE BOTTLE)
Categories: Meats
Yield: 2 Servings
2 lb Spare Ribs
1    V-H Sauce, 12 fl. oz.
Boil 2 pounds of spare ribs about 15 minutes, until tender, in
separate pot, boil V-H spare ribs sauce about 3 minutes, until it
starts to thicken. Add drained spare ribs and simmer to desired
thickness.
