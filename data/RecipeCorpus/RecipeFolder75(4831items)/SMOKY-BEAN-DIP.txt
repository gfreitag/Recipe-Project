Title: SMOKY BEAN DIP
Categories: Digest, August
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Garbanzo beans
1    4-oz can chopped green
Chilies
1/4 c  Bottled no-fat barbecue
Sauce (I used Healthy Choice
Ickory)
1 ts Ground cumin
1 ts Minced garlic
Rinse and drain garbanzo beans.  Puree with remaining ingredients in
a food processor or blender. Cover and chill at least one day. Makes
2 cups.
