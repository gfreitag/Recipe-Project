Title: SIDEWALK CHALK
Categories: Kids, Misc
Yield: 1 Recipe
MMMMM---------------------LISA CRAWLEY/TSPN--------------------------
Plaster of Paris
Water
Colored Tempra Paint OR
Food Colors
Mix 2 qts. plaster of Paris w/ 1 part water.  Mix in desired color.
Pour into paper towel or TP tubes (about 3" high). Let dry thoroughly
(may take several days). Remove from tubes and give to your favorite
artist. Martelle Christian Church Cookbook/Iowa.
