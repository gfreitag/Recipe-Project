Title: SPAM QUESADILLAS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 18 Servings
1 cn SPAM Luncheon Meat,
-chopped (12 oz)
4 c  Shredded Monterey Jack
-cheese with peppers
6    8" flour tortillas
Guacamole
CHI-CHI's Salsa
Combine SPAM and cheese. Spoon meat mixture over 3 tortillas. Top with
remaining tortillas. In lightly greased griddle, heat filled tortilla
over medium-high heat until soft and cheese is melted, turning once.
Repeat with remaining tortillas. Cut each tortilla stack into 6
wedges. Serve with guacamole or salsa.
