Title: SCALLOPS IN SZECHWAN SPICY SAUCE
Categories: Chinese, Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Peanut oil for deep-frying
-up to 5 c
3/4 lb Large scallops, cut in half
-through the middle
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
2 ea Dried red chile peppers,
-seeded, soaked for one
-hour in water, then
-drained and chopped
2 tb Light soy sauce
1 pn Sugar
1/2 tb Rice wine vinegar
1 tb Dry sherry
MMMMM---------------------------FINAL--------------------------------
2 tb Fresh peanut oil
1/2 ts Finely grated fresh ginger
2 ea Cloves garlic, chopped fine
6 ea Green onions, cut chinese
-style
Heat deep frying oil to 360 degrees and deep-fry the scallops for
just a few minutes.  Drain and set aside. Mix the ingredients for the
sauce and set aside.  Heat a wok and add the oil, ginger, and garlic
and chow for a moment.  Add the green onions and the scallops. Toss
for a few seconds and add the sauce mixture. Stir fry until all is
hot and serve immediately.
