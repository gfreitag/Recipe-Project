Title: SOUTHWEST SOFT TACOS
Categories: Vegetarian, Tex-mex, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Corn tortillas
1 1/3 c  Frozen mixed vegetables:
-broccoli, corn and red
-peppers, or other Southwest
-blend
Salt and pepper
Sprouts (optional)
4 ts Salsa or picante
Microwave frozen vegetables for 5 minutes. Microwave tortillas for
20-30 seconds until hot and soft. Top each tortilla with vegetables,
salt and pepper, 1 ts of your favorite salsa and sprouts if desired.
Fold in 3 sides like flaps and eat. These are quick and easy.
