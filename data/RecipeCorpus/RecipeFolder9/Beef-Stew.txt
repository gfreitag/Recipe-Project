Title: Beef Stew
Categories: Beef, German, Soups/stews
Yield: 6 servings
Pepper, To Taste
2 ts Mustard, Dry
1/2 ts Celery Seed
1/4 c  Water
1 lb Tomatoes, (1 can)
1/4 c  Shortening
3 lb Rump Roast, Boneless
2 c  Onions, Sliced
1/4 c  Unbleached Flour
2 tb Salt
2 tb Sugar
Melt shortening in a Dutch oven.  Add the meat and brown on all sides.
Place the onions on top of the meat.  Mix the flour and seasonings with 1/4
c water.  Blend with the tomatoes and add the misture to the dutch oven.
Bake at 325Ã¸F, about 2 hours, until the meat is fork-tender.  Serve with
oven-browned potatoes.
