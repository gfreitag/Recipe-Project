Title: DATE BALLS
Categories: Appetizers, Christmas, Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
8 oz Pitted dates
1 c  Sugar
1    Butter
1 tb Karo syrup
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Pecans
3 c  Rice krispies
Mix dates, sugar, butter and Karo syrup.  Cook together. Let cool for
5 minutes.  Add vanilla, pecans and rice krispies. Roll in
Confectionary sugar or coconut.
