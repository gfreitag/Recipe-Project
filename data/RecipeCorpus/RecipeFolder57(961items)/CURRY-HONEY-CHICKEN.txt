Title: CURRY HONEY CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Dijon mustard
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Curry powder
2 tb Soy sauce
1/4 c  Butter
Chicken breasts for 5
Put skinned chicken in 9x13 inch pan.  Blend rest of ingredients and
heat to melt butter and honey.  Pour over chicken; cover. Refrigerate
overnight. Cover pan with foil. Bake at 350 F for 1 hour. Remove
foil; bake 15 minutes more.  Serve over hot rice; spoon on extra
sauce.
