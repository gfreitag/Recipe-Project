Title: CUCUMBERS IN CREAM SAUCE
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
4 md Cucumbers
1 md Onion, sliced
2 ts Salt
MMMMM------------------------CREAM SAUCE-----------------------------
1/2 c  Heavy Cream
2 tb Vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
In a large bowl, combine peeled and sliced cucumbers, onion, and
salt. Let stand at room temperature about 1 hour. Drain well. Combine
ingredients for cream sauce, add to cucumber/onion mixture, and stir.
Chill several hours before serving.
