Title: CUCUMBER IN SOUR CREAM SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ea Med cucumber thin sl 3 cups
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb White vinegar
2 ea Drops of tabasco
2 tb Chopped chives
1 ts Dill seed or fresh chop dill
1 ea Dash of pepper
Sprinkle the cucumber with salt.  Let stand 30 minutes. Drain
thoroughly. Dry on peper towel. Combine sour cream, vinegar, tabasco,
chives, dill seed and pepper.  Pour over cucumber.  Chill well before
using. Garnish with fresh dill.
