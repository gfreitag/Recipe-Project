Title: CRUSTLESS QUICHE
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 Servings
1/4 c  Butter (1/2 stick)
1/2 c  Flour
6 lg Eggs
1 c  Milk
1 lb Monterey Jack cheese, cubed
3 oz Cream cheese, softened
- (1 package)
2 c  Cottage cheese
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
Bacon, cooked crisp and
- crumbled (optional)
Melt butter in small saucepan.  Add flour and cook until smooth; set
aside. Beat eggs; add milk, Monterey Jack cheese, cream cheese and
cottage cheese. Stir in baking powder, salt, sugar and butter-flour
mixture, mixing until well blended.  Pour into a well-greased
9x13x2-inch pan. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
Preparation time: 15 minutes Baking time: 45 minutes
