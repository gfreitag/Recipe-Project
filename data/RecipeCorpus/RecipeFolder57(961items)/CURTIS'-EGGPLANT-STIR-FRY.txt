Title: CURTIS' EGGPLANT STIR FRY
Categories: Stir fry, Vegetables
4    Asian eggplants -- sliced
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2    " thick
on bias
:
2 TB sesame oil
1 TB vegetable oil
1/4 c  soy sauce
2 ts dark brown sugar
1 1/2 c  snow peas
5    scallions -- cut on bias in
1 1/2    inch long
pieces
:
2    red peppers -- sliced
thinly
:

Heat a wok or large non-stick skillet over medium high heat. Heat the
oils and when almost smoking, add eggplant and peppers. Cook for 4
minutes, until softened, and add snow peas and scallions. Dissolve
the sugar in the soy sauce, and add to pan. Cook for 2 minutes and
serve over rice.
