Title: DANDELION & LETTUCE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Penndutch
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Lettuce
1 pt Dandelion
4 sm Onion
1/2    Bell pepper, green
2 md Tomato
2    Egg; hard cooked, sliced
1/3 lb Swiss cheese
Salt & pepper
3 tb Olive oil
4 tb Vinegar
Cut the lettuce, dandelion, onions, pepper and cheese into small
pieces. Add salt and black pepper. Mix well. Add the olive oil and
vinegar. Mix thoroughly, then add the tomatoes cut in quarters, and
the eggs. Mix lightly so tomatoes and eggs will not be mashed or
broken.

Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes
Culinary Arts Press, 1936
