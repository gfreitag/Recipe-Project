Title: DATE NUT COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 24 Servings
1 c  Shortening
2 c  Brown sugar
2 ea Egg
To above mix
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Vanilla
In another bowl mix;
3 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1/8 ts Cinnamon
Mix well, then combine bowls
MMMMM-----------------FILLING; COOK UNTIL THICK----------------------
Stir constantly
2 c  Dates -- chopped
3/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Water
When thick add
1 c  Pecans -- chopped
drop 1 teaspoon dough, add 1/2 teaspoon filling and 1/2 teaspoon
dough on top. Bake at 375
