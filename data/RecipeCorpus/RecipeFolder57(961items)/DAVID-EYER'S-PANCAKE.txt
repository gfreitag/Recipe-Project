Title: DAVID EYER'S PANCAKE
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 2 Servings
MMMMM-----------------BON APPETIT; OCTOBER 1987----------------------
1/2 c  Flour
1/8 ts Nutmeg, ground
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Oil
2 tb Sugar, powdered
1 1/2 tb Juice, lemon
Preheat oven to 425.  Combine first 4 ingredients in medium bowl. Melt
butter in heavy 12" skillet in oven. Pour batter into skillet. Bake
until golden brown, about 15 minutes. Sprinkle with sugar and lemon
juice. Bake 3 more minutes.  Fill with ice cream or fruit, if desired.
