Title: DAK'S ORANGE-RAISIN WHEAT BREAD (BREAD MACHIN
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Salt
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Plus 2 tb fresh orange juice
Warm, or
2 tb Orange juice concentrate,
And 1 cup warm water
2    Egg whites
Put all the ingredients into pan in the order listed, select "whole
wheat" and push "start."
