Title: DANISH RYE CEREAL
Categories: Breakfast, Danish
Yield: 5 Servings
1 c  Whole rye berries, unprocess
2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Caraway seeds
1 tb Vanilla extract
3 c  Water
1/4 c  Raisins
Ricotta cheese; optional
Sugar; optional
Combine all ingredients except raisins, ricotta and sugar in
saucepan; mix well. Heat to a boil. Reduce heat to simmer and cook,
covered, for 1 hour. Stir occasionally; add more water, if needed, to
prevent scorching.

During last 15 minutes of cooking time, add raisins. Top each serving
with a dollop of ricotta cheese and sugar, if desired.

Source: Chicago Tribune, January 15, 1997
