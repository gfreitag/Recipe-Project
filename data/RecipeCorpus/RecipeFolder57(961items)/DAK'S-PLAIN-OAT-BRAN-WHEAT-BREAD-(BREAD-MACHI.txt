Title: DAK'S PLAIN OAT BRAN WHEAT BREAD (BREAD MACHI
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Oat bran
3 tb Gluten
1 ts Salt
2 tb Honey
2 tb Margarine, soft
1 1/2 c  Plus 3 tb warm water
Set machine's browning control to light. Put all the ingredients into
pan in the order listed, select "Oat/French" and push "start."
