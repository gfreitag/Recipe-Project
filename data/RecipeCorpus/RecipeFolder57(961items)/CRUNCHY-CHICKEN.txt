Title: CRUNCHY CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
3/4 c  Stove Top Stuffing Mix
4    Chicken breasts,boneless
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese-grated
6 ts Margarine, melted
Place stuffing mix in a food processor and process until fine crumbs;
combine with cheese.  Dip boneless and skinless chicken breasts into
margarine, then into stuffing-cheese mixture. Place on cookie sheet.
Drizzle any leftover margarine over top.  Bake at 350 degrees about 1
hour, or until done.
