Title: DATE NUT BARS
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 10 Servings
1 lb Brown sugar
1/3 c  White sugar
1 1/2    Stick of butter
3    Eggs
2 1/2 c  Flour-plain
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Vanilla
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 pk Dates
Mix all together - put in greased cookie sheet which has edge. bake
at 375 degrees for 20 minutes.  Cut while warm. Makes about 4 dozen.
Caot dates with a little flour before mixing in.
