Title: CUCUMBER VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Spice mix, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Peeled, diced cucumber - in
Sherry
sm Sprig of dill
1/4 ts Dijon mustard
Pepper; to taste
Recipe by: "Michelle M. Davis" <mdavis@PINEGOV.CO.PINELLAS.FL.US>
Blend until smooth. Chill. Serve over sliced cucumber.

Serving size = 1 cup 70 calories, 7 g fat, 0 cholestrol
