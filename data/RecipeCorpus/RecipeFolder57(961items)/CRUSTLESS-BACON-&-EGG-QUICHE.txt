Title: CRUSTLESS BACON & EGG QUICHE
Categories: Breakfast
6 sl fat-free turkey bacon --
cooked and crumbled
Yield: 6 Servings
:
1/2 c  skim milk
1/2 c  low-fat baking mix
3    eggs
1/4 c  Fleischmann'sÂ® Fat Free
Spread -- liquid
:
1/8 ts black pepper
1 c  fat-free cheddar cheese --
grated
:

Preheat oven to 350. Prepare a 9" pie pan with cooking spray and
flour; set aside. In a food processor, combine milk, baking mix,
eggs, spread, and black pepper. Process for 15 seconds. Pour into
prepared pan. Sprinkle bacon and cheese on top; gently press below
surface with a fork. Bake for 30 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before
serving.
