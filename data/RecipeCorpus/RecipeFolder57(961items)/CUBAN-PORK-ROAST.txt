Title: CUBAN PORK ROAST
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
Posted by Stewart Hopper
-
3 lb Pork shoulder - seasoned
1/3 c  Lime or lemon juice
1    Onion, sliced
1/2 ts Oregano
3    Garlic cloves
Season meat with salt and pepper.  Pour lime or lemon juice over
roast. Place onion slices over roast.  Let stand in refrigerator 1-2
hours. Split meat in places and add garlic plugs in slashes. Roast at
325 degrees for 1 1/2 - 2 hours or 35-40 minutes per pound.
