Title: CUCUMBER & ONION SALAD #2
Categories: Low-fat, Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 c  Cucumber, sliced paper thin
1/2 c  Onion, sliced paper thin
1/3 c  Vinegar
5 tb Water
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
15 ts Sugar
Layer cucumber and onion in a jar or bowl. Combine remaining
ingredients and pour over vegetables. Chill overnight, turning jar or
stirring several times.
