Title: CURRIED EGGS
Categories: Eggs, Easy
Yield: 4 Servings
6    Eggs; hard boiled
4 ts Butter
1    Apple; peeled, cored & fine
1    Onion; minced
2 ts Curry powder
2 ts Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk
2 ts Parsley; minced
Preheat  oven  to  350F.  Slice  eggs & place  in double layer,
slightly overlapping  in  greased baking dish. Set aside. SAUCE:Melt
butter in pan over low heat.  Add apple & onion. Cook, stirring till
onion is limp & apple reduced to a puree (10  min). Stir in curry
powder, then flour & salt. Stir till smooth. Slowly add milk,
stirring as added. Cook, stirring till sauce is thick & smooth. Pour
sauce over eggs & place in oven till bubbly hot. Top with parsley.
Serve with chutney.
