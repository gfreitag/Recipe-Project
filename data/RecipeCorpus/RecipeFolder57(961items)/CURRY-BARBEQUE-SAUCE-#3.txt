Title: CURRY BARBEQUE SAUCE #3
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
2 ea Cloves mashed garlic
1/4 c  Minced onion
1 tb Curry powder
3 tb Milk
1/4 c  Chopped raw apple
1/2 c  Beef stock
-Salt to taste
Melt butter and saute garlic and onions until light golden. Stir in
curry powder.  Add milk and apples. Simmer 10 minutes, stirring
often. Add stock and simmer 1/2 hour. Add more stock if sauce gets to
thick. Brush on kebabs
