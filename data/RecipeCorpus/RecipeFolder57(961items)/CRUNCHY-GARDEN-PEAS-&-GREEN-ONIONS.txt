Title: CRUNCHY GARDEN PEAS & GREEN ONIONS
Categories: Vegetable, Dupree
5 TB olive oil
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  shelled green garden peas
3    green onions with stems --
chopped
Salt and freshly ground
black pepper
:
:
:
3 ts finely chopped fresh basil
3 TB grated imported Parmesan
cheese
Hot pepper
:
:

Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan. Add the peas and green
onions and saute 2 to 3 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste. Add
the optional basil, cheese and/or hot pepper flakes to taste. Cover
the pan for 1 minute for young peas, longer for older ones. Serve
hot. Yield: 4 servings
