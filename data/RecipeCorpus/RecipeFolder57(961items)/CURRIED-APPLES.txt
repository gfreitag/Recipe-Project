Title: CURRIED APPLES
Categories: Spices
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Apples,cut into 1/4" rings
2/3 c  Dry red wine
1/4 c  Butter or margarine,melted
1/4 c  Brown sugar,packed
1/4 ts Curry powder
Soak apple rings in wine in covered shallow boal or baking dish 3
hours, stirring occasionally; drain.
Set oven control to broil and/or 550'. Place apple rings on rack in
broiler pan. Broil rings 5 inches from heat 3 minutes; turn.
Mix remaining ingredients; spoon over rings. Broil until glazed and
light brown, about 1 1/2 minutes.
