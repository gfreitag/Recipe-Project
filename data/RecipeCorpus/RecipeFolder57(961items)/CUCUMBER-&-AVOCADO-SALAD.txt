Title: CUCUMBER & AVOCADO SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1    English Seedless Cucumber
Salt
Chili Pepper
1 tb Fresh Lime Juice
1 ts Minced Onion
Slices, Sweet Red Pepper
Lettuce
1    Ripe Avocado, Sliced
Fresh Parsley
Peel and slice the cucumber paper-thin.  Season with salt, chili
pepper and lime juice.  Sprinkle with onion and red pepper slices.
Arrange lettuce on 6 serving plates. Pile cucumber mixture in center
of each plate. Add avocado slices and parsley.  From: Syd's Cookbook.
