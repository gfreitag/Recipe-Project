Title: DAK'S APPLE WHEAT BREAD (BREAD MACHINE)
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 c  Kellogg's Bran Flakes
(or eqivalent)
3 tb Gluten (optional)
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Plus 1 Tbs apple juice,
Warmed
1/2 c  Applesauce, warmed
1    Golden delicious apple,
Grated with skin (measures
About 1 1/4 cups)
Put all the ingredients into pan in the order listed, select 'whole
wheat' and push 'start'
