Title: DATE PUDDING
Categories: Penndutch, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ea Egg, well beaten
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Dates, chopped
1 ts Baking powder
2 tb Flour
1 c  Nuts, chopped
Mix in the order given. Bake at 325-F for 30 minutes. Serve with
cream. Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes,
Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
