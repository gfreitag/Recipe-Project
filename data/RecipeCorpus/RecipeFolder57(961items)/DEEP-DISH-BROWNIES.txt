Title: DEEP DISH BROWNIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 16 Brownies
3/4 c  Butter or margarine melted
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
3    Eggs
3/4 c  All purpose flour
1/2 c  Hershey's cocoa
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
Heat oven to 350F Grease 8 inch square baking pan. In medium bowl
blend butter, sugar  and vanilla. Add eggs; using spoon, beat well.
Combine flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt; gradually add to egg
mixture, beating until welll blended. Spread into prepared pan. Bake
40 to 45 minutes or until brownies begin to pull away from the side
of the pan. Cool completely; cut into squares. (16 brownies)

Variation Stir one cup of Reese's peanut butter Chips or Hershey's
Semi sweet chocolate chips into batter.
