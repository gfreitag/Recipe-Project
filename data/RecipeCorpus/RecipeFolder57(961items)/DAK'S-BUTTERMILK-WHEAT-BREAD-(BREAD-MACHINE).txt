Title: DAK'S BUTTERMILK WHEAT BREAD (BREAD MACHINE)
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Plus 2 TBS whole wheat flour
1 ts Salt
2 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Baking soda
1    Egg
1 c  Buttermilk, warmed
Put all the ingredients into pan in the order listed, select, "Whole
Wheat" and push "Start."
