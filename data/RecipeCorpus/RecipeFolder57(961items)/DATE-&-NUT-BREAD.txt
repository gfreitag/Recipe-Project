Title: DATE & NUT BREAD
Categories: Breads, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Dates; chopped
1 c  Water; boiling
1 ts Soda
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg; beaten
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Nut meats; chopped
Add soda and boiling water to dates. Let stand and cool. Then mix with
remaining ingredients. Bake in moderate oven 1 hour. Bake in long loaf
bread pan.

Note: No exact temperatur give. Moderate oven is 350 - 400 F. Source:
Cora Van Allen, Bugby Grange, Astabula County, Oh
Mabel Purdy, Pleasent Grange, Knox County, OH
