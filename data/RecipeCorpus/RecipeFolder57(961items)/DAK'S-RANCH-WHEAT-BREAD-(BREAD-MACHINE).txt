Title: DAK'S RANCH WHEAT BREAD (BREAD MACHINE)
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 pk Hidden Valley Original Ranch
Dressing mix (1 1-oz. pkg.)
1 tb Honey
1 1/4 c  Lowfat buttermilk, warmed
1 tb Water
Put all the ingredients into pan in the order listed, select "whole
wheat" and push "start."
