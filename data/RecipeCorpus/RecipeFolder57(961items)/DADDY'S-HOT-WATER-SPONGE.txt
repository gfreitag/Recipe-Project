Title: DADDY'S HOT WATER SPONGE
Categories: Cakes, Family
Yield: 1 Cake
Egg yolks (from Angel Food
-cake)
1/2 c  Water, boiling
1 tb Vanilla extract, or more
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Cocoa
Prepare loaf pan with Pam spray; preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Sift together the flour and sugar with the baking powder, salt and
cocoa. Beat eggs; pour in boiling water and vanilla. Stir eggs into
flour mixture. Pour batter into pan and bake until done.
