Title: DAVE & DEBI'S BARBECUED BEEF
Categories: Beef
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Stew Beef
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
1 1/2 lb Pork -- Cubed
1/4 c  Chili Powder
3    Green Peppers -- Chopped
1/4 c  Cider Vinegar
2    Onions -- Chopped
2 ts Salt
6 oz Tomoto Paste
1 ts Dry Mustard
1 c  Water
2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
In a 5 Quart Dutch Oven, combine all ingredients. Heat to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer, covered for approximately 3 hours, stirring
often.
