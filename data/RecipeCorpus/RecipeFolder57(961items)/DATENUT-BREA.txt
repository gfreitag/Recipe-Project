Title: DATENUT BREA
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
16 oz Dates,pitted
1 3/4 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking soda
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Chopped walnuts
mix dates and water let sit 10 minutes then add soda and flour. In a
seperate bowl mix butter sugar and eggs. Add date mixture to sugar
mixture and then add walnuts. Pour into 2 greased and floured loaf
pans and bake 325-350 for 1 hour. Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.10
