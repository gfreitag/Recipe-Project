Title: CURRANT CONSERVE
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
5 lb Currants
2 lb Seeded raisins
5 lb Sugar
3    Oranges
2 c  Raspberry juice
Wash and stem currants.  Add raisins, raspberry juice, sugar, and
oranges which have been seeded and chopped. Simmer slowly, stirring
constantly, until juice sheets from spoon. Florence Taft Eaton,
Concord, MA.
