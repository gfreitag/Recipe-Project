Title: CUMIN - CHILI BURGERS
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 Burgers
1 lb Lean ground beef
2 tb Ground cumin
2 tb Chili powder
1 tb Minced garlic
Salt and pepper
4 sl Jalapeno cheese
1/2 c  *Roasted garlic mayonnaise
1 c  Favorite salsa
4 ea Favorite rolls or buns
Preheat the grill. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef with the
cumin, chili powder and garlic. Mix until thoroughly incorporated.
Form into 1/4 pound patties and season with salt and pepper. Place on
the grill and cook for 4-6 minutes or medium-well done. Place the
cheese on the burgers for about 1-2 minutes to melt.

Remove from the grill and drain on paper towel. Spread the garlic
mayonnaise on the buns and add the burgers. Top with salsa and serve.

*Slice the top from a head of garlic and rub with olive oil. Wrap in
foil and roast at 325F for 25-35 minutes. When cool, squeeze the
cloves into the mayonnaise and mix well.
