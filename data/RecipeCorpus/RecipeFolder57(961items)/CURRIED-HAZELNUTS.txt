Title: CURRIED HAZELNUTS
Categories: Appetizers, Fruits/nuts, Snacks
Yield: 2 Cups
2 c  Oregon hazelnuts
3/16 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Salt
Melt butter in sauce pan; stir in curry powder, salt and hazelnuts.
Turn into shallow baking pan. Bake in a
275    oven for 20 minutes.
