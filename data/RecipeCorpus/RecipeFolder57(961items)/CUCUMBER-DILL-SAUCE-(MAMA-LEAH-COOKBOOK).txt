Title: CUCUMBER-DILL SAUCE (MAMA LEAH COOKBOOK)
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 999 Servings
2    Cucumbers peeled seeded chop
1 c  Sour cream
1 ts Horseradish
6 x  Sprigs fresh dill
1/2 c  Mayonaisse
Place peeled, seeded cucumber in food processor and chop for 1 minute.
Serve as an accompaniment to fish.
