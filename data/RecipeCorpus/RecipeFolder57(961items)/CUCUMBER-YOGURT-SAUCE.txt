Title: CUCUMBER YOGURT SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 md Cucumber, Peeled, Seeded
And Shredded
1    (8 Oz.) Carton Plain Low
Fat Yogurt
2    Green Onions, Finely
Chopped
1 tb Fresh Chopped Parsley
1 ts Vinegar
1/2 ts Dried Dillweed
1/8 ts Garlic Powder
Pat Cucumber With Paper Towels Until Barely Moist. Combine Cucumber
and Remaining Ingredients in A Medium Bowl. Cover & Chill At Least 2
Hours. Serve With Greek Stuffed Footballs.
