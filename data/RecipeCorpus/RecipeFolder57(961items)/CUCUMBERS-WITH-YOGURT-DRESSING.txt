Title: CUCUMBERS WITH YOGURT DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings    1 1/2 c  lowfat yogurt
6 TB scallions -- chopped
1/2 ts salt
1/2 ts black pepper
1/2 ts mustard
3    cucumbers -- sliced
dill weed sprigs -- for
garnish
:
:

In small bowl, combine the yogurt, scallions, salt, pepper and
mustard. Blend. Add thinly sliced cucumbers and mix well to coat with
dressing. Lay about 2 or 3 dill sprigs on top but do not mix in.
Cover tightly and refrigerate for one hour.

: Jo Merrill
