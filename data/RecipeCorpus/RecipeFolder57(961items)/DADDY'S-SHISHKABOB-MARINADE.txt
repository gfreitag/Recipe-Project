Title: DADDY'S SHISHKABOB MARINADE
Categories: Meats, Holiday
Yield: 1 Batch
1 c  Oil
3/4 c  Wine vinegar
1 tb Garlic salt
1/4 c  Worchestershire Sauce
1 tb Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Black Pepper
Mix above ingredients together in a large container and marinade
Shishkabob ingredients for a minimum of 24 to 48 hours in the
refrigerator. Turn occasionally to insure that the liquid is
distributed throughout. Use onions, fresh mushrooms, green peppers,
tomatoes, beef cubes, etc. *I serve this with consommed rice, a green
salad and french bread.
