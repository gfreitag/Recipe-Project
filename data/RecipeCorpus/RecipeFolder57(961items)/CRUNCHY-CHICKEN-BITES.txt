Title: CRUNCHY CHICKEN BITES
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 1 Recipe
1/4 c  Cornstarch
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar
2 tb Dry sherry
2    Egg whites
1    Whole chicken breast
-- cut into 1-inch pieces
3/4 c  Chopped Oregon hazelnuts
-- (roasted)
Oil for frying
MMMMM-----------------------MUSTARD SAUCE----------------------------
1/4 c  Dairy sour cream
1/4 c  Salad dressing
-OR- mayonnaise
1 tb Dry mustard
1/4 ts Sugar
Combine cornstarch, salt and sugar. Stir in sherry. Beat egg whites
until foamy. Stir in cornstarch mixture. Dip chicken in mixture and
roll in nuts, pressing nuts into chicken. Place on rack to dry. Fry
in hot oil until done. Drain and serve with a mustard sauce, if
desired.

For Mustard sauce: Blend all ingredients together and serve.
