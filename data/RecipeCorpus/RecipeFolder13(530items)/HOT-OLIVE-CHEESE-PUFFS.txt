Title: HOT OLIVE CHEESE PUFFS
Categories: Appetizers, Usenet
Yield: 25 puffs
1/4 t  Salt
1/2 t  Paprika
25    Olives (approx)
1 C  Cheddar cheese (sharp),
-grated
3 T  Butter, softened
1/2 C  Flour
Blend cheese with butter.  Add flour, salt, paprika.  Mix well.

Dry olives.  Wrap a spoonful of dough around each and seal well.  Bake at
400 degrees F. for 15 minutes.

NOTES:

*  Delicious hot appetizers.  Yield:  Makes about 25 puffs.

*  You can freeze the unbaked cheese puffs on a cookie sheet and then store
them in the freezer in a plastic bag.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  30 minutes preparation, 15 minutes baking.
: Precision:  measure the ingredients.
