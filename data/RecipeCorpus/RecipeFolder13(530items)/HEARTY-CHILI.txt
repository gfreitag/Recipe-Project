Title: HEARTY CHILI
Categories: Entrees, Usenet
Yield: 6 servings
6 C  Kidney beans
4 oz Green chili peppers,
-chopped (2 cans)
2 T  Chili powder
2 t  Oregano (or less)
1/4 C  Oil
1 lb Beef, ground
2 lb Onions sliced
6    Garlic cloves,
-minced (or less)
6 oz Tomato paste (or use
-about 8 oz cream of
-tomato soup)
Saute onions in oil.  Add ground beef and cook until brown.  Add remaining
ingredients including liquid from kidney beans.

Simmer covered for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.  (You may need to add
more water during simmering.)

NOTES:

*  A hearty, hot and tasty chili.

: Difficulty:  Easy.
: Time:  30 minutes preparation, 20 minutes cooking
: Precision:  Approximate measurement OK.
