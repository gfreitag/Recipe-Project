Title: LAMB MARINADE
Categories: Condiments, Meats, Usenet
Yield: 1 /2 cup
3/4 t  Onion salt
1/4 t  Black pepper
1/4 t  Ginger, ground
1/4 t  Garlic, minced
-(instant)
1/4 C  Salad or olive oil
2 T  Wine vinegar
2 t  Mustard, prepared
1 t  Rosemary leaves,
-crushed
1 1/2 t  Salt
Mix together.  Refrigerate.  Marinate lamb overnight.

NOTES:

*  A marinade for any cut of lamb.

: Difficulty:  trivial.
: Time:  5 minutes preparation.
: Precision:  no need to measure.
