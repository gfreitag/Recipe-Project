Title: KLUSKI Z MAKIEM I
Categories: Entrees, Polish, Holiday, Usenet
Yield: 4 servings
12 oz Poppy seed cake
-and pastry filling
1 t  Lemon juice
1 1/2 t  Lemon peel, grated
1/3 C  Raisins
6 C  Water, boiling
1 t  Salt
3 C  Egg noodles
2 T  Butter, melted
1 t  Vanilla extract
Combine boiling water and salt in a large saucepan.  Add noodles and cook
until tender, then drain and cool.  Toss noodles with butter in the
saucepan.

Combine poppy-seed filling with vanilla extract, lemon juice and lemon peel
and raisins.  Add to noodles and mix well.  Cook just until heated through.

NOTES:

*  RODZYNKAMI Noodles with Poppy Seed and Raisins -- This recipe could be
used as part of a 12-course meal known in Polish as Wigilia, or on its own.
Wigilia is eaten after sundown on Christmas Eve.  Yield:  Serves 4-6.

: Difficulty:  easy.
