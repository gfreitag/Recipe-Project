Title: MA FOSTER'S SLOPPY JOES
Categories: Sandwiches, Usenet
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Mustard
2 T  Worcestershire sauce
1 t  Celery seed
Salt and pepper,
-to taste
1 lb Beef, ground
1    Onion, chopped
1 C  Catsup
2 T  Vinegar
2 T  Sugar
In a frying pan, brown ground beef and onion.  Add remaining ingredients
and cook slowly for 15 minutes or more. Serve hot by scooping the mixture
onto toasted buns.

NOTES:

*  A spicy and simple sloppy joe -- This recipe comes from my wife's mom,
who got it from her mother-in-law in western New York state. It's very easy
to make and tastes a lot better than any mix.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  5 minutes preparation, 15 minutes cooking.
: Precision:  approximate measurement OK.
