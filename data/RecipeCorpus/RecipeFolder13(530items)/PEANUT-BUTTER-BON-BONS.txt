Title: PEANUT BUTTER BON BONS
Categories: Candy, Christmas, Usenet
Yield: 1 batch
1 lb Butter
1 lb Chocolate chips
1/2 lb Paraffin block
2 C  Peanut butter,
-crunchy
3 lb Sugar, powdered
Mix together the sugar and peanut butter in a bowl.  Melt the butter and
pour it over this mixture. Stir and knead until smooth. Roll into
walnut-sized balls.

Melt together the chocolate chips and paraffin over water.  Dip balls into
chocolate coating, set on waxed paper to harden.

NOTES:

*  Chocolate and peanut butter holiday treat.  Yield:  3-4 dozen.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  15 minutes.
: Precision:  Approximate measurement OK.
