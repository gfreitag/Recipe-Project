Title: MARY'S GARLIC BREAD
Categories: Breads, Usenet
Yield: 6 servings
3 T  Parsley (fresh), chopped
1/2 t  Oregano
1    Loaf French bread
-(baguette), cut
-lengthwise
1 C  Butter, softened
1 C  Parmesan cheese, grated
1/2 C  Mayonnaise
5    Garlic cloves,
-minced or pressed
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.  Mix all ingredients except the bread
together in a bowl. Spread the mixture onto the French bread. Wrap bread in
aluminum foil. Bake for 20 minutes. Unwrap and place under broiler to
brown.

NOTES:

*  Addictive and fattening garlic bread -- This recipe is from "The Garlic
Lover's Cookbook," which is a must for all folks interested in keeping
vampires away.

*  We never finish the last step.  By the time we have the stuff unwrapped,
we just have to eat it!

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  preparation:  10 minutes, cooking:  25 minutes.
: Precision:  No need to measure.
