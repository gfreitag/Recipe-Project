Title: OATMEAL PANCAKES
Categories: Breakfast, Usenet
Yield: 3 servings
1 T  Honey
1 T  Butter, melted
1 1/2 C  Milk
1    Apple, peeled,
-cored and shredded,
OR
1/2 C  Raisins, or other
-fruit (optional)
1/2 C  Flour, whole wheat
1 1/2 C  Rolled oats
1 T  Baking powder
1 t  Ground cinnamon,
-or more to taste
1/2 t  Nutmeg, or more
-to taste (optional)
1    Egg, beaten
Combine the dry ingredients in a bowl.

Add the honey, butter, milk and fruit to the egg, then combine with the dry
mixture.  The batter should be fairly thick.  If it seems too thick, add a
bit more milk. Cook on a hot, lightly buttered griddle.

NOTES:

*  Oatmeal and whole wheat pancakes -- I got this recipe from a friend.  As
she was relating it to me, we agreed on some modifications.  Yield:  Serves
3-4.

: Difficulty:  easy.
: Time:  20 minutes.
: Precision:  approximate measurement OK.
