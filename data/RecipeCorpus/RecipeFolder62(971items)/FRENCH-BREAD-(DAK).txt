Title: FRENCH BREAD (DAK)
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM------------------MICHAEL HATALA  RJHP21A-----------------------
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
1 pk Yeast
2 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Soft butter

start. From Loafing It by DAK
