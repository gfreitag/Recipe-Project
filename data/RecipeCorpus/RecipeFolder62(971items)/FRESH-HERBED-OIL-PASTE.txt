Title: FRESH HERBED OIL PASTE
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lg Cloves garlic -- peeled
2 lg Green onions -- trimmed, cut
Into 1-inch lengths
1 c  Olive oil
1/4 bn Cilantro -- cleaned
1/4 bn Mint
1/4 bn Parsley
1/4 bn Thyme
1    Sprig rosemary
1/2 ts Sea salt
1 pn Crushed hot red pepper
Flakes
In a blender, combine the garlic, green onions, olive oil, herbs,
salt and crushed pepper. Blend until smooth. Store in an airtight
container, in the refrigerator. This paste may be kept refrigerated,
for up to a week. Brush country bread or boned chicken breasts with
the herbed oil paste and grill. Yield: 2/3 cup Copyright, 1996, TV
