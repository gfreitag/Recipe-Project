Title: FRENCH TOMATO DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Oil
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Sweetener
3 tb Tomato paste
3 tb Water
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Onion powder
Blend all ingredients except oil & lemon juice till smooth. Keeping
the blender running, slowly pour in the oil. Stir in the lemon juice.
Chill.
