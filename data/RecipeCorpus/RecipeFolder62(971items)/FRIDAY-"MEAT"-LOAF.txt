Title: FRIDAY "MEAT" LOAF
Categories: Beans, Main dishes
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Beans -- cooked
1/2 lb Shredded cheddar cheese
1 c  Tomatoes -- chopped
1    Egg
1 ts Onion -- grated
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1 tb Butter
Combine beans and cheese, add tomatoes and beaten egg, seasonings,
crumbs to make stiff, and melted butter or milk to moisten if too
dry; bake. Serve with tomato sauce.
