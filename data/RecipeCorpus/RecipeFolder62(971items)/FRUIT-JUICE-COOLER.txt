Title: FRUIT JUICE COOLER
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Peach nectar
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Grapefruit juice
2 tb Lemon juice
12 oz Seltzer or sparkling mineral
--water;chilled
Combine juices in a 1-qt. pitcher; chill.  Just before serving, stir
in seltzer or mineral water.  Serve over ice. Source: Taste of Home
mag, June/July 1996
