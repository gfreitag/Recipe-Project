Title: FRIENDSHIP BREAD - SOUR DOUGH
Categories: Breads
Yield: 10 Servings
2/3 c  OIL
2 c  FLOUR
1 c  SUGAR
3 ea EGGS
1 ts BAKING POWDER
1/8 ts BAKING POWDER
2 ts BAKING SODA
1/2 ts SALT
1 ts CINNAMON
1 ts VANILLA
1 x  CHOPPED NUTS OR FRUIT
ADD ALL THE INGREDIENTS TO THE "FRIENDSHIP MIX". STIR WELL AND POUR
INTO 2 WELL GREASED AND FLOURED BREAD PANS. A BUNDT PAN CAN BE USED
IF YOU WISH. BAKE AT 325 DEG F FOR ABOUT ONE HOUR. COOL IN PANS FOR
TEN MINUTES. REMOVE AND COOL COMPLETELY.
