Title: FRIED-BRAIN OYSTER SHOOTERS
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Seafood, Weird, Vodka
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Vodka
1    Shucked Oyster
Tabasco
Cocktail Sauce
In a large shot glass pour vodka, place oyster on top. Then add
cocktail sauce with a few drops of tabasco. Bottoms up!
