Title: FRIED MUSH
Categories: Penndutch, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1 x  *cornmeal mush
1 x  Oil
1 x  Flour
Pour hot cornmeal mush into a well-oiled narrow baking pan and let
stand until cold and solid. Cut in thick slices, dip in flour, and
fry on both sides on a well-buttered hot griddle until nicely
browned. Source: Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes,
Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
