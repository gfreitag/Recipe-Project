Title: FRESH APPLESAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
4 lg Eating apples
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
Cut up apples.  Put half of the apples and the remaining ingredients
in blender container.  Cover and blend on high speed about 30 seconds,
stopping blender a few times to scrape sides, until smooth. Add
remaining apples. Cover and blend on high speed about 30 seconds
longer, stopping blender a few times to creape sides, until smooth.
Refrigerate any remaining applesauce. About 3 cups applesauce.
