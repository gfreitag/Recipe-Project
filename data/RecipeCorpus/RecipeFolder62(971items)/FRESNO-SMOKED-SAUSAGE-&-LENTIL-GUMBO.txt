Title: FRESNO SMOKED SAUSAGE & LENTIL GUMBO
Categories: Beans, Soups, Vegetables, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 lb Smoked sausage
1/3 c  Alad oil
1/3 c  Flour
4    Stalks celery; chopped fine
3 cl Garlic; minced
2 md Onions; diced
2 lg Carrots; chopped fine
2 qt Chicken broth
1 3/4 c  Lentils
1 tb Worcestershire
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Cayenne
In a 5 to 7 quart pan, over medium heat, cook sausage, stirring, until
brow.  Spoon from pan; reserve.  Add oi to drippings in pan to make
1/3 cup fat.  Add flour; stir until mixture is red brown, abouth 10
minutes.

Add celery, garlic, onion and crrots; cook, stirring until vegetables
begint o soften, about 3 minutes. Add reserved sauseage, chicken broth
lentils, worcestershire, pepper and cayenne. Bring to boiling over
high ehat; redue to simmer, cover and cook until lentils ar soft when
pressed, about 40 minutes.
