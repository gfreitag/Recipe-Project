Title: FRONTIER CHOWDER
Categories: Crs, Totest, Post, Chopmeat
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 md Chopped green pepper
1 md Chopped onion
1 cn Tomato soup, undiluted
1 cn Bean n bacon soup,
Undiluted
1 cn Corn, drained
1 c  Water
Cook beef, onion and pepper until vegetables are tender. Drain off
fat, add remaining ingredients. Cover and simmer a half hour.
