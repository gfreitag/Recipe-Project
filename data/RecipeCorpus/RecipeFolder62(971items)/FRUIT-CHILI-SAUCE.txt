Title: FRUIT CHILI SAUCE
Categories: Perserves, Preserves, Heirloom
Yield: 1 Servings
30    Large tomatoes, peeled
6    Onions, peeled
6    Pears, peeled
6    Peaches, peeled
3    Green pepper
3    Sweet red peppers
1    Large bunch celery
4 c  Granulated sugar
2 tb Salt
1 qt Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Mixed pickling spices (tied
In a bag)
Prepare and chop the vegetables and fruit.  Add sugar, salt, vinegar
and spices.  Simmer slowly uncovered until thick - about two hours.
Discard spices and pour into sterlized jars and seal. Source:
Heritage Recipes ch.
