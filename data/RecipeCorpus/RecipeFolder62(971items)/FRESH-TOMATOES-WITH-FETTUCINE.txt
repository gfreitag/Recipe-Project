Title: FRESH TOMATOES WITH FETTUCINE
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
5 md Ripe tomatoes, diced
1 md Red onion, chopped
1 ea Garlic clove, pressed
3/4 c  Basil, shredded
1 c  Parsley, chopped
3 tb Extra virgin olive oil
2 ts Balsamic vinegar
Salt & pepper, to taste
1 lb Fettucine
1/4 c  Black olives, chopped
Combine all except fettucine.  Cook fettucine al dente and add to
tomatoes. Top with olives, if desired. Serve warm, cold or at room
temperature. Serves 8.

Jean Carper in "Eat Smart," USA WEEKEND, August 1995, Posted by Iris
Grayson.
