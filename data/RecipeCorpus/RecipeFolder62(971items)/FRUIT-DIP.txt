Title: FRUIT DIP
Categories: Fruits, Desserts, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding mix
1 pt Dairy sour cream
Combine both ingredients and mix well.  Serve as a dip for sliced
apples, pears, melon, and other fruits. From Massachusetts Dairy
Sampler by Massachusetts Dairy Industry
Promotion, Inc.
