Title: FRESH SPINACH SALAD (M_C-TX)
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Oil
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/3 c  Catsup
1    Onion grated (or onion
-flakes)
Pinch of salt
MMMMM-------------------------VEGETABLES------------------------------
1 pk Spinach
MMMMM---------------------------EXTRAS--------------------------------
8    Slices bacon, fried &
-crumbled
2    Eggs, boiled & chopped
Mix dressing ingredients. Clean and tear spinach. Pour dressing over
spinach and add bacon and eggs before serving. Serves 6-8.
