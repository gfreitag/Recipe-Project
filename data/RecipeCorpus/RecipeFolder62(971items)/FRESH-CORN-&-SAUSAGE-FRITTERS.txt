Title: FRESH CORN & SAUSAGE FRITTERS
Categories: Appetizers, Meats
Yield: 24 Servings
1 c  All-purpose Flour, sifted
1 ts Baking Powder
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
1 c  Sausage, cooked and crumbled
1 c  Fresh Corn off the cob
2    Egg Yolks, beaten
2 tb Milk
2    Egg Whites, beaten stiff
Oil, for frying
Sift flour, baking powder, and spices together in a mixing bowl. Add
sausage, corn, egg yolks, and milk; mix until blended. Fold in stiffly
beaten egg whites. Drop by heaping teaspoonfuls into oil heated to
360 - 365 degrees. Cook 3 to 5 minutes, turning to brown on all
sides. Drain on paper towels.

Makes about 24 fritters
