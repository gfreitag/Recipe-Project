Title: FRENCH STYLE SWISS CHARD
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Coarsley shredded swiss
-chard
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1 tb Olive oil
4 tb Grated sapsago or parmesan
-cheese
Saute the chard and garlic in the olive oil until wilted, about 5
minutes. Sprinkle with the cheese and serve immediately.   Typed in
