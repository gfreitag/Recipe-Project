Title: FRIED GREEN TOMATOES FROM MY GARDEN
Categories: Wasnone
2 c  yellow cornmeal
Yield: 1 Servings
2    eggs
4    green tomatoes -- thickly
sliced
:
2 c  vegetable oil
Salt
Pepper
:
:

Season cornmeal with salt and pepper. In bowl beat eggs. Dip tomatoes
in the beaten egg and then in the cornmeal. In a cast iron or
non-stick pan heat oil and place tomatoes. Fry for 3 minutes or so on
each side, or until golden. Serve.
