Title: FRIJOLES REFRITOS
Categories: Side dishes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 kg Drained cooked pinto beans
Broth from cooked pinto bean
30 ml Vegetable oil
Recipe by: Pamela McGarvey UCLA Comrehensive Epilepsy Program 1. Heat
oil in a medium skillet.

2.  Add drained cooked pinto beans to the skillet and mash up into a
paste.

3.  Saute the beans over medium heat for about 5 minutes, stirring
frequent

Author's Notes:
You will first need to make a batch of frijoles en olla for this
recipe

Difficulty    : easy. Precision
: no need to measure.
