Title: FRESH STRAWBERRY CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Box white cake mix
1/2 c  Crushed strawberries (fresh
-or frozen)
1    Stick butter/margarine
1    3 oz. box strawberry Jello
4    Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
Cream Mararine.  Add eggs one at a time, beating after each addition.
Add cake mix, jello, strawberries and milk. Mix well. Bake in three 8
or 9 inch pans at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes. Let cool for 10
minutes. Remove from pans, cool and ice.

ICING

1 1lb. box of powdered sugar 1/2 stick margarine, softened 1/2 c.
crushed strawberries (if frozen, DRAIN!!!)

Cream margarine and sugar and mix well.  Add crushed strawberries and
mix to spreading consistancy, using juice if needed/ Stread onto
cake. Arrange strawberry slices on top of cake.
