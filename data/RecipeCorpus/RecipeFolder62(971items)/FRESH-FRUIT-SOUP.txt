Title: FRESH FRUIT SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
3 tb Sugar
3 tb Cornstarch
1/8 ts Salt
1 c  Water
1 1/4 c  Rose wine
1 1/2 c  Cranberry juice
3 c  Fresh fruit
Mix sugar, cornstarch and salt in 3-quart saucepan; stir in water and
wine. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute.
Remove from heat. Stir in cranberry juice. Cover loosely and
refrigerate until chilled.
Stir in fruit. Serve with sour cream or whipped cream if desired.
