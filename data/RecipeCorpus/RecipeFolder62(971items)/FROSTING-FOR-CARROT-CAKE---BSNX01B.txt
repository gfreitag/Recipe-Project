Title: FROSTING FOR CARROT CAKE - BSNX01B
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
3 oz Creamed cheese (room temp.)
1/4 c  Butter  (1/2 stick)
1 ts Vanilla
1/8 ts Salt
3/4 lb Confectioner sugar
Beat cream cheese and butter until fluffy. Blend in milk, vanilla and
salt. Gradually add confectioner sugar. Beat until smooth and creamy.
