Title: FROZEN CREAMED HORSERADISH
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Heavy cream
4 tb Horseradish
2 ts White vinegar
1/2 ts Black pepper
Whip well-chilled cream with chilled beaters in chilled bowl. Fold in
remaining ingredients.  Lightly oil a sheet of wax paper and roll
horseradish mixture in the paper, making a cylinder about 1-1/2" in
diameter.  Freeze horseradish cream. To serve, unwrap and slice
cylinder 1/4" thick.  Use to garnish tomato, salmon, trout, ham, or
veal. Keep frozen, removing slices as needed.
