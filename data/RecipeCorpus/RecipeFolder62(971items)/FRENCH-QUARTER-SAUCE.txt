Title: FRENCH QUARTER SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Onion, grated
1/4 c  Salad oil
1 ea 8 oz can tomato sauce
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
3 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Prepared mustard
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Cook onion in hot oil until tender.  Add remaining ingredients; simmer
uncovered 15 minutes.  Makes enough sauce to baste 4 pounds loin back
ribs or spareribs.

Source:  BH & G, Barbecue book, 1965
