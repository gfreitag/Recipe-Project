Title: FRENCH ONION-MUSHROOM SOUP
Categories: Favorites, Soups, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
3 tb Margarine
2 1/2 c  Onions; sliced
1 lb Mushrooms; sliced
2 tb White wine vinegar
4    Vegetable bouillon cubes
French bread; (4 slices)
1 c  Swiss cheese; shredded
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
Saute onions and mushrooms till soft. Add broth and vinegar. Boil,
cover, and simmer for 10 minutes. Heat oven to 400 and heat bread for
10 minutes; remove. Heat broiler. Puree 1/2 the soup mixture; add
back to pot, and heat. Divide into 4 broiler proof crocks, top with
bread and cheese and broil 3-5 minutes.
