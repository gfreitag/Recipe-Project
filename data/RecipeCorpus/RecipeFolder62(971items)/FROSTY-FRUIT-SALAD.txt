Title: FROSTY FRUIT SALAD
Categories: Fruits, Salads
2 c  sugar
Yield: 9 Servings
1/8 ts salt
1 qt buttermilk -- 4 cups
1 ts vanilla
1 cn crushed pineapple -- (20 oz)
drained
:
1 cn fruit cocktail -- (17 oz)
drained
:

In large bowl, combine sugar, salt, buttermilk and vanilla til well
mixed. Gently stir in drained fruits. Pour into 9" square pan. Freeze
til firm.
