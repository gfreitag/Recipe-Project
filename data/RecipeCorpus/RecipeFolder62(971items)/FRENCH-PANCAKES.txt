Title: FRENCH PANCAKES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 2 Servings
3/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
2 tb Powdered sugar (optional)
2    Eggs
1/3 c  Water
2/3 c  Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
Mix dry ingredients, beat the eggs and add the liquid ingredients.
Make a well in dry ingredients and add the liquids, combine with
little mixing and ignore any lumps, they will take care of themselves.
