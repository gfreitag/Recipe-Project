Title: FRIED RICE (MORGAN)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Cooked brown rice
6 c  Vegetables*
1/2 c  Water
x  Garlic
1/4 ts Ginger
1/4 c  Soy sauce.
Boil water garlic and ginger, add veggies *(yellow, green, red bell
peppers, broccoli, anything), cook 10 min. or however long you like
and add rice and soy sauce.
