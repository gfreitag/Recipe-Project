Title: FRIED RICE WITH PEPPERY CURRY
Categories: Side dish, Thai, Rice, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
3 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Onions, chopped
1 tb Garlic, chopped
1/2 c  Green beans, chopped
2 tb Peppery curry paste, see
-- recipe
5 c  Cooked long-grain rice
2 tb Soy sauce
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
1 tb Cilantro leaves, chopepd
1/2 c  Cucumber, sliced
3 ea Green onions, sliced
Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat.  Add the onion & stir-fry
for 3 minutes, then add the garlic & continue to stir-fry for a few
minutes, until both are golden. Add the beans & curry paste &
stir-fry for 3 minutes.  Add the rice & soy sauce & stir-fry until
hot through, be careful not to overcook.  Serve with the garnishes.
