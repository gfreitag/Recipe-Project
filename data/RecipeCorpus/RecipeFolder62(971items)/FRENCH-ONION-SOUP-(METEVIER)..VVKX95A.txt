Title: FRENCH ONION SOUP (METEVIER)..VVKX95A
Categories: Soups, Side dish, Family
Yield: 8 Servings
6 c  Onions
4 tb Margarine
8 c  Beef broth
2 ts Worcestershire
1/2 c  Carrots
2 ea Parsley sprigs
2 ea Bay leaves
1/4 ts Thyme leaves
1/2 c  Celery; opt.
COOK ONIONS IN BUTTER FOR 1/2 HOUR BEFORE ADDING OTHER INGREDIENTS.
