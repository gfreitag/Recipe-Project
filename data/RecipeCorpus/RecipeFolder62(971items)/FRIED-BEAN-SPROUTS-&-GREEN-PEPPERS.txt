Title: FRIED BEAN SPROUTS & GREEN PEPPERS
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, China, Update
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 4 SERVINGS--------------------------
1 lb Bean sprouts
3 ea Green peppers
5 ea Tb Oil
2 ea Tb Wine
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts MSG (optional
Remove heads and tails of bean sprouts and soak sprouts in water until
ready to use.  Drain before using. Wash, seed, and shred green
peppers. Heat 5 tbsps. oil and fry bean sprouts and shredded green
pepper. Add wine, salt (I prefer garlic salt) and MSG to taste. Serve
hot. Do not overfry bean sprouts.
