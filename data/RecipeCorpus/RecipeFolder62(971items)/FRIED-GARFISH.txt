Title: FRIED GARFISH
Categories: Main dishes, Fish
Yield: 1 Servings
Bite size pieces of garfish
Tabasco  sauce
Salt and pepper -- to taste
Mustard
Corn flour
Sprinkle Tabasco  sauce on fish and soak for 5 minutes. Add salt and
pepper to taste. Add mustard to cover all of the fish. Roll in corn
flour. Deep-fry until cooked thoroughly. Festival: Le Festival du
Poisson Arm (Garfish Festival); May 5-7, 1995.
