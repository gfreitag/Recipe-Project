Title: FROTHY ORANGE DRINK
Categories: Easy, Beverages
Yield: 4 Cups
1 cn (6 oz.) frozen orange juice
-concentrate, unthawed
1 c  Water
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
8    To 10 ice cubes
Combine all ingredients in a blender; cover and process until drink is
thick and slushy.  Yield: 4 cups. Typed in MMFormat by
