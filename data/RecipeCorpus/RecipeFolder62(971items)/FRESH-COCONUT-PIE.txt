Title: FRESH COCONUT PIE
Categories: Pies
1 c  sugar
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2    stick butter -- melted
2 TB flour
3    eggs -- beaten
1 ts vanilla
1 c  buttermilk
1 c  coconut -- grated
1    9 inch pie crust -- fluted &
unbaked
:

Mix all the ingredients together. Pour into pie crust and bake in a
preheated 300 degree oven for one hour. Cool before serving.
