Title: FROZEN CAPPUCCINO
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Scoops vanilla frozen yogurt
--divided
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Hershey's choc. milk mix
1 1/2 ts Instant coffee granules
Place 1 scoop frozen yogurt, milk, chocolate milk mix and coffee
granules in food processor or blender. Process 30 seconds or until
smooth. Pour into tall glass; top with remaining scoop of frozen
yogurt.
