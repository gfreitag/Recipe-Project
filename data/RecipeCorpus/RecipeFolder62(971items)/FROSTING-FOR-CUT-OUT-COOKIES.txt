Title: FROSTING FOR CUT-OUT COOKIES
Categories: Icings
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 lb Box confectioner's sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1    Stick butter, softened
3    To 4 ts cream
1/8 ts Salt
Mix all ingredients until smooth. Add cream if too stiff, add
confectioner's sugar if too runny. Use a decorator's tube to "draw"
on the cookies.
