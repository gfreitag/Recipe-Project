Title: FRUIT CRISP
Categories: Vegetarian, Desserts, Fruits, Microwave
Yield: 8 Servings
3 c  Apples, cooking; peeled,
-cored, and sliced
3 c  Pears; peeled, cored, sliced
1 tb Lemon juice
5 tb Margarine, nondairy
1 c  Flour, pastry, whole wheat
2/3 c  Oats, rolled
1/2 c  Sugar, brown OR
1/2 c  Maple syrup granules
1 ts Cinnamon, ground
1/4 ts Nutmeg, freshly ground
1/2 c  Walnuts; coarsely chopped
Place fruit in an 8 1/2-inch round glass dish.
Sprinkle wiht lemon juice.

In a large bowl, cut butte into flour with a pastry cutter. Stir in
remaining ingredients. Sprinkle mixture evenly over apples and pears.
Microwave, uncovered, on high for 11 to 13 minutes, or until fruit is
tender, rotating twice. Let stand until cool enough to serve. Serves
6-8.

Per serving: 411 cal; 6 g prot; 16 g fat; 63 g carb; 0 chol; 107 mg
sod; 6 g fiber
