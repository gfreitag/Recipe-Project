Title: FRESH FIGS CURA#AO
Categories: Fruits, Alcohol
Yield: 4 Servings
12    Fig, fresh; peeled &
-quartered
1 tb Cognac
1 c  Heavy cream, whipped
1/3 c  Curacao
Marinate the figs in the cognac 30 minutes or longer. Mix the cream
and Cura#ao. Fold in the figs and any cognac that they have not
absorbed.
