Title: FRENCH ONION SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
3 lb Peeled onions
4 oz Butter or margarine
1 1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
2 tb Paprika
1 ea Bay leaf
1/4 c  Flour
3 qt Beef stock
1 c  White wine
2 ts Salt (optional)
Slice onions 1/8 inch thick.  Melt butter, place onions in it and cook
slowly for 1 1/2 hours.  Add Beef stock and simmer 2 hours. Salt to
taste. Can color with Kitchen Bouquet. Refrigerate over night. Reheat
and serve with croutons and Swiss Cheese.
