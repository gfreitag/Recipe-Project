Title: FRESH APPLE CREPES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 Crepes
MMMMM-----------------------CHEESE TOPPING----------------------------
3 oz Cream cheese
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
MMMMM-----------------------APPLE FILLING----------------------------
3/4 c  Water
3/4 c  Sugar
5 c  Cooking apples
* 4 medium apples thickly sliced, pared and cored.

Prepare cheese topping:  In a mixing bowl allow cheeses to come to
room temp; beat until fairly smooth. Add sour cream. To prepare apple
filling: In a 2-qt. saucepan bring water and sugar to boil, add
apples and simmer until tender. Cover to keep warm.

To assemble:  Place 3-5 apple slices on each crepe; roll up. Place in
single layer, seam side down, in chafing dish; pour over left-over
apple syrup. Heat. Serve two crepes per serving. Top each with cheese
topping. Submitted By RHOMMEL <RHOMMEL@IX.NETCOM.COM> On TUE, 21 NOV
