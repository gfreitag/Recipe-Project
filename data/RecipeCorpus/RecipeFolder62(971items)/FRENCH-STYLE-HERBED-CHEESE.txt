Title: FRENCH STYLE HERBED CHEESE
Categories: Appetizers, Spreads, French
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Pkg softened cream cheese
2 tb Milk
1/4 ts Crushed dried oregano leaves
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Onion powder
1/8 ts Pepper
Combine all ingredients, mix until well blended. Chill. Serve with
crusty french bread or crackers.
