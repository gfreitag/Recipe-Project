Title: FRENCH MARKET SOUP MIX
Categories: Soups, Beans
Yield: 14 Servings
1 lb Each of the following
Navy beans
Great northern beans
Green split peas
Yellow split peas
Black-eyed peas
Lentils
Baby limas
Black beans
Red beans
Soy beans
Barley pearls
Combine the dried beans and peas.  Divide into 14 - 2 cup packages.
