Title: FRENCH ONION SOUP AUSSIE STYLE
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 Servings
30 g  Butter
6    Onions; sliced
6 c  Beef stock
430 g  Can* beef consomme
1    Pepper to taste
1/2 c  Grated cheese
1    * nearest equiv. can size
Melt butter in a saucepan, add onions and cook  over a medium heat
until tender and lightly browned. Add stock, beef consomme, and
pepper. Bring to boil and simmer for 20 mins. Serve hot, sprinkled
with cheese.
