Title: FRUIT & NUT CHILI
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
2 cn Green chilies -- diced 4 oz
Ea
4    Onions -- chopped
2    Apples -- chopped (Granny
Smit
3    Garlic cloves -- minced
3 tb Chili powder
32 oz Tomatoes -- chopped
2 tb Unsweetened cocoa
15 oz Tomato sauce
1 tb Curry powder
1 cn Chicken broth -- (14-1/2
Oz)
1 ts Cinnamon
3    Green peppers chopped
2/3 c  Slivered almonds
In large dutch oven; cook ground beef, onions and garlic until meat is
browned; drain. Stir in tomatoes, (undrained) tomato sauce, chicken
broth, chopped peppers, green chilies, apples, chili powder, cocoa,
curry and cinnamon. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer
for 1 hour. Add almonds. Heat through. Serve with raisins, cheddar
cheese, yogurt or sour cream. Makes 8 servings.
