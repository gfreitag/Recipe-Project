Title: FROZEN FRUIT SALAD (M_C-TX)
Categories: Salads, Fruits
Yield: 8 Servings
1 tb Gelatin
1/4 c  Water
1 c  Crushed pineapple
2    Bananas, sliced
1    Apple
3 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Whipped cream or cool whip
Soak gelatin in cold water for 5 minutes. Dissolve over boiling water.
Combine the fruits, lemon juice and sugar. Add dissolved gelatin to
above ingredients and chill. As mixture begins to gel, fold in
mayonnaise and whipped cream. Pour in ice tray and place in upper
freezer and freeze. Cut in squares and serve on lettuce cups.
