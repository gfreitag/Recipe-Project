Title: FROZEN RASPBERRY SAUCE
Categories: Desserts, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
For Desserts
In food processor or blender, puree thawed 10 oz pack frozen
raspberries in syrup until all pulp is removed from seeds, about 30
seconds. Press through a fine sieve to remove seeds. Add
confectioners' sugar to taste & 1 Tbls. raspberry or blackberry
liqueur. Submitted By THESERVS@GATE.NET (THOMAS E. HAUG)  On MON, 13
NOV 1995 155854 -0500
