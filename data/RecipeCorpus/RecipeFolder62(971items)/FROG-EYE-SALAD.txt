Title: FROG EYE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Pasta
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  SUGAR
2 tb FLOUR
1/2 ts SALT
1 sm BAG MINI MARSHMALLOWS
1 sm COOL WHIP, DEFROSTED
2    EGGS
1 lb ACINI DE PEPE
40 oz CRUSHED PINEAPPLE
- DRAINED & SAVE JUICE.
33 oz MANDARIN ORANGES; DRAINED
Mix in saucepan: sugar, flour, salt, and 1 3/4 c. reserved juice.
Bring to a boil. Beat eggs well. Add to boiling mixture, cook 1-2
minutes longer at reduced heat, stirring constantly. Cool completely.
Mix cooled pineapple juice mixture and cooked pasta. Refrigerate 3-4
hours add marshmallows, mandarin oranges, pineapple and cool whip.
Mix well. Chill and serve.
