Title: FROZEN PICKLES
Categories: Canning
Yield: 1 Servings
2 qt Cucumbers; sliced unpeeled
1 md Onion; sliced thin
2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Vinegar
Mix first 3 ingredients and refrigerate for 2 hours. Drain
thoroughly, but do not rinse.
Mix together until completely dissolved the following: 1 1/2 cup
sugar and 1/2 cup vinegar.  Pour over pickles. Juice should cover
them. Will keep in refrigerator for a couple of weeks or put in
containers and freeze.
KAREN STANOWSKY   (KGZV75A) Everyone really enjoys them.They can
also be frozen if you wish.
