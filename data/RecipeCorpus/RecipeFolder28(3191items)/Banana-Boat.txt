Title: Banana Boat
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 serving
Chocolate chips
Raisins (optional)
1    Banana
Mini marshmallows
Partially peel banana.  Cut wedge-shaped section in banana.  Remove
wedge. Place in hollow:  marshmallows, chocolate, and raisins.  Cover
mixture with banana peel and wrap in foil.  Place in coals for about
5 minutes, until chocolate and marshmallows are melted.
