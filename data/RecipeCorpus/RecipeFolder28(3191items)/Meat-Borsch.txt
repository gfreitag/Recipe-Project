Title: Meat Borsch
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
1 T  Salt
3 T  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Lemon juice
2 ea Eggs, beaten
3 qt Water
2 lb Brisket
Beef bones
8 ea Beets, grated
2 ea Onions, diced
Combine the water, meat and bones in a deep saucepan.  Bring to a
boil and skim.  Add the beets, onions, garlic and salt.  Cover and
cook over medium heat 2 hours.  Add the brown sugar and lemon juice.
Cook 30 minutes. Taste to correct seasonings if necessary. Beat the
eggs in a bowl. Gradually add a little hot soup, beating steadily to
prevent curdling. Return to saucepan.  Serve with pieces of meat as
garnish. Serves 8 - 10 From: The Art of Jewish Cooking by Jennie
Grossinger Shared by: Pat Stockett
