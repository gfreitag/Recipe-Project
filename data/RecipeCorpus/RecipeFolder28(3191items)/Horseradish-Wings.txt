Title: Horseradish Wings
Categories: Main dish, Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Olive oil
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
5 1/2 ts Tobasco sauce
1/2 ts Pepper ,black,ground
2 1/2 lb Chicken wings
1 ea Lemon ,juice only
1/2 c  Ketchup
5 tb Horseradish ,prepared
Combine all ingredients in a plastic bag. Knead occasionally for 2
hours. Cook in a grill or under a broiler. Cook 10 minutes then turn,
baste and cook 10 minutes more, or until done. Serve immediately, or
refrigerate until needed.
