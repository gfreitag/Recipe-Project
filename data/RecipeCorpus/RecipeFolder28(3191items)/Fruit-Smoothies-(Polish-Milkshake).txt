Title: Fruit Smoothies (Polish Milkshake)
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 pt Milk; (2 c.)
Ice cubes
1 ts Lemon peel; grated -=OR=-
1 ts Orange peel grated
1 lb Frozen strawberries; -=OR=-
1 lb Frozen blueberries -=OR=-
1 lb Frozen pitted cherries
4    Egg yolks;
3 tb Powdered sugar;
Place frozen fruit in food processor until pureed.  Place egg yolks
and sugar in a large bowl; beat until fluffy.  Add milk, beating
until smooth. Add pureed fruit, beat until fluffy.  Add milk, beating
until smooth. Add pureed fruit, beat until smooth.  Place 2 ice cubes
in each of 4 to 6 serving glasses.  Pour fruit drink into glasses.
Garnish with lemon or orange peel.  Serve immediately. Source: Katie
Hitt, Corona Cooks, Corona del Sol High School, Tempe, AZ Brought to
you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
