Title: Mushroom Saute with Zucchini and Italian Sausage
Categories: Italian
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 lb Zucchini; cut in 1/2" cubes
1/2 c  Parsley; chopped
Salt and pepper
2    Link poultry italian sausage
1 T  Oil
2 T  Garlic; chopped
3/4 lb Mushrooms; sliced
Poke sausage in serveral places; place in skillet with 1/2 cup water.
Cover and steam cook over low heat for 5 minutes.  Uncover and
continue cooking until water has evaporated and sausage is lightly
browned and cooked throughout.  Remove sausage from skillet and slice
into 1/2" rounds. Heat the oil in the skillet and stir fry the
garlic, mushrooms and zucchini until crisp tender.  Return sausage to
skillet and season to taste. Stir fry 1 more minute.  Toss in the
parsley and serve. 132 calories and 6 grams fat.  Source:  Store ad
