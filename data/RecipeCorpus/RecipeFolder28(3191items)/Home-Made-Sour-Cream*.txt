Title: Home Made Sour Cream*
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 pint
2 tb Yogurt
1 pt Sweet cream (scant)
Place sweet cream in a clean jar. Add yogurt. Mix and set in a warm
place overnight or until thickened. Refrigerate.
