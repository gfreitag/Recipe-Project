Title: Ranger Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
2 c  Oats, uncooked
1 c  Coconut
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Flour
2 c  Cornflakes
1/2 c  Nuts
Form into balls about walnut size.  Bake on lightly greased baking
sheet. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes. Randy Rigg
