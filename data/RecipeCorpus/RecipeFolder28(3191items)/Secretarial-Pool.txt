Title: Secretarial Pool
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 punch bowl
2 1/4 c  Water
1/2 ga Strawberry ice cream
1 l  Ginger ale
1 qt Pineapple juice
3/4 c  Sugar
6 oz Pink lemonade concentrate
The night before combine the pineapple juice, sugar, lemonade
concentrate and water.  Refrigerate overnight.  Add a few drops pf
red food coloring. Pour the mixture into a punch bowl, add the ice
cream and stir. Add the ginger ale last.  Source: Beverly Hillbillies
Cookbook. Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   03-12-95
