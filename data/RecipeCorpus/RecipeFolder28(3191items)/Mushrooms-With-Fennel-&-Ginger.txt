Title: Mushrooms With Fennel & Ginger
Categories: Side dish, Indian, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Sont (ground ginger)
1/4 ts Turmeric
1/4 ts Cayenne
1/2 ts Salt
12 oz Mushrooms
4 tb Oil
2 sm Tomatoes, chopped
1/4 ts Fennel seeds
Wipe mushrooms with a wet cloth & cut into slices.

Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.  When hot, put in the
mushrooms.  Stir & fry the mushrooms for about 2 minutes.  Add all the
other ingredients.  Stir & cook until the tomatoes are soft.

Madhur Jaffrey, "A Taste of India"
