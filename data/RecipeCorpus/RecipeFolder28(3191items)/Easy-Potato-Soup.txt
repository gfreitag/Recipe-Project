Title: Easy Potato Soup
Categories: Vegetables, Soup/stew
Yield: 7 servings
-Granules
2 c  Milk
2 tb Dried Chives
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
1 c  Sour Cream*
2 tb Flour
6    Frozen Gourmet Choice Hash
-Brown Patties
2 1/2 c  Water
2 tb Dried chopped Onion
3 tb Dried Celery Flakes
1/2 c  Water
1 tb Chicken flavored Bouillon
In large saucepan or stockpot, combine hash brown patties and 2 1/2
cups water.  Cover and place over medium heat, stirring occasionally
to break up patties.  Meanwhile, in a small bowl, place dried onion
and celery flakes in 1/2 cup water; let water absorb.  Add onion
mixture and bouillon granules to potato mixture.  Reduce heat and
simmer 5 minutes. Stir in milk, chives, salt and pepper; mix well. In
a small bowl blend sour cream and flour until smooth.  Gradually stir
sour cream mixture into soup. Cook until thickened, stirring
constantly.

Makes 7 (1-cup) servings.

NOTE: *Lite sour cream may be used in place of sour cream.
