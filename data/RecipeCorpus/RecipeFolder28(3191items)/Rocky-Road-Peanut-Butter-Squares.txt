Title: Rocky Road Peanut Butter Squares
Categories: Snacks, Kids, Nuts/grains
Yield: 24 squares
2 c  Mini marshmallows
1/2 c  Peanuts; coarsely chopped
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1 T  Shortening
3/4 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Peanut butter
6 c  Triples cereal
Heat syrup and sugar to boiling in heavy 2 quart saucepan over medium
heat, stirring constantly. Immediately remove from heat; stir in
peanut butter until melted.  Place cereal in large bowl.  Pour peanut
butter mixture over cereal, stirring until well coated. Cool 2
minutes. Stir in marshmallows. Press mixture in ungreased 13 x 9 pan.
Sprinkle with peanuts. Heat chocolate and shortening over low heat
until melted; drizzle over squares. Let stand until chocolate is set.
