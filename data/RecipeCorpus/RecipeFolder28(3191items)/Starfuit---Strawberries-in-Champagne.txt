Title: Starfuit - Strawberries in Champagne
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Strawberries, cut in half
3/4 c  Champagne
2    Starfruit
1/4 c  Sugar
Cut starfruit crosswise into 1/4 inch slices.  Sprinkle with sugar,
let stand 30 minutes.

Spoon starfruit and strawberries into dessert dishes.  Pour champagne
over fruit.
