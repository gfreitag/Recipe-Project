Title: Wheatie Pancakes
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
Salt; pinch
1 tb Butter
Flour
1 c  Wheaties
1 c  Milk; sweet
2 ea Eggs
2 ts Baking powder
Combine ingredients using enough flour to make a thin batter. Bake on
hot griddle. Source: Howard Behnke, North Elyria Grange, Lorain
County, OH
