Title: Easiest barbecue sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------YIELD: 1 CUP-----------------------------
3 ea Tb Brown sugar
1 t  Salt
1 t  Dry mustard
1 t  Chili powder
1 t  Paprika
1/2 t  Red pepper
3 ea Tb Catsup
2 ea Tb Vinegar
1 t  Lemon juice
2 ea Tb Worcestershire sauce
4 ea Tb Water
2 ea Tb Cooking oil
Combine all ingredients in a quart jar; shake well and refrigerate
until ready to use as a basting sauce for barbecue chicken, beef or
pork. Southern Living...June, 1972
