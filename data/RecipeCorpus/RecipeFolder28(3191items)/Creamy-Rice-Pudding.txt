Title: Creamy Rice Pudding
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------COMBINE IN TOP OF DOUBLE-----------------------
MMMMM---------------------------BOILER--------------------------------
Remove from heat.
Add
1 T  Butter or Margarine
1 t  Vanilla
1/3 c  Raisin,currants or chopped
Dates
1 1/2 c  Cooked rice
1 c  Milk
2    Slightly-beaten eggs
1/3 c  Sugar
Cook over simmering water,
Stirring contantly, until
Mixture coats a metal spoon.
Pour into serving bowl and allow to stand, without stirring, for 30
minutes Serve warm or chilled, with cream.   (I sprinkle cinnamon
and/or nutmeg on top.) Taken from an "Old" Purity Flour Cookbook.
