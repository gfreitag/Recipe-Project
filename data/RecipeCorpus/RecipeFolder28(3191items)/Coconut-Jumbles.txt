Title: Coconut Jumbles
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 dozen
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla extract
1    Egg white; slightly beaten
Coconut, shredded
1/2 c  Butter (or marg.); softened
1/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg; beaten
1 1/4 c  Flour, all-purpose
1/2 ts Baking powder
Cream butter; add sugar gradually, continuing to cream.  Add eggs;
blend well.  Combine flour, baking powder, and salt; add to creamed
mixture, beating well.  Stir in vanilla.

Roll out dough about 1/8" thick on a lightly floured board; cut with
doughnut cutter.  Brush tops with egg white; sprinkle with coconut.
Bake at 400 degrees for 8 minutes or until a delicate brown.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, May 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
