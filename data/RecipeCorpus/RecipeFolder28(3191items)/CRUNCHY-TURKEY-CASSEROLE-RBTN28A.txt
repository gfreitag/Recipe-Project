Title: CRUNCHY TURKEY CASSEROLE RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Milk
1 cn Mushrooms, well drained
3 oz Cn chow mein noodles
1 cn French fried onion rings
2 c  Chopped turkey or chicken
15 oz Cn french style green beans
Well drained.
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
Combine all ingredients in a casserole dish except the onion rings.
Cover. Microwave on roast, medium or 60 percent power for 8 minutes,
or until hot. Top with onion rings. Re-cover. Let stand for 5
minutes. ...
