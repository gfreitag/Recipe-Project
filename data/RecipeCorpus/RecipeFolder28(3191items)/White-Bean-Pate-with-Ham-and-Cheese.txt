Title: White Bean Pate with Ham and Cheese
Categories: Dips/spread, Vegetables, Pork, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
Fresh or dried rosemary;
-to taste
1    To 2 green onions; trimmed
-minced
1/2 c  Ham;
1/4 c  Walnuts; toasted chopped
1/4 c  Green olives; pimiento
-stuffed minced
1/2 c  Jack cheese; grated
1/2 c  Cottage cheese; -=OR=-
1/2 c  Ricotta cheese
2 oz Fat-free light cream cheese;
-=OR=-
2 oz Regular cream cheese -=OR=-
2 oz Neufchatel cheese; softened
1 c  White beans; cooked cooled
1 ts Dijon-style mustard; to
-taste
In a food processor fitted with a metal blade, puree the cottage or
ricotta cheese, cream cheese, white beans, mustard, salt, ground
white pepper, rosemary, green onions and ham.  Add and pulse tin
walnuts, green olives and jack cheese. Per 1-tablespoon serving 25
cal; 2g fat; 3mg chol; 43mg sod; NOTE: According to the story in the
paper, Bagels is the fastest growing bakery item in the country.
This recipe was featured in the artictle as spread to be used for
bagels. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Food Section,  Feb. 23,
