Title: Idaho Potato Cake
Categories: Potato, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 c  Baking cocoa
1 ts Baking soda
1 c  Milk
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Butter or margarine,
-softened
2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 c  Cold mashed potatoes
1 ts Vanilla extract
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs, one
at a time, beating well after each addition. Blend in potatoes and
vanilla. Combine flour, cocoa, and baking soda; add alternately with
milk, blending well after each addition. Stir in nuts. Pour into a
greased 1 3-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Bake at 350! for 40-45
minutes or until cake tests done. Cool on a wire rack.

Yield: 12-16 servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
