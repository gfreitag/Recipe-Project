Title: Quick Skillet Meat Loaf
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 loaf
1 pk Enchilada sauce (or other
. spicy sauce mix)
1 lb Ground meat
1    Egg
1 c  Cooked whole millet
Combine all ingredients on the baking sheet or in a skillet by making
a well in the meat.

Knead into a loaf shape, place in preheated 350 deg. F. oven, and
bake for 1 hour.  Cool slightly, slice and eat.

From "The Book of Whole Grains," by Marlene Anne Bumgarner, (C) 1976
St. Martin's Press, ISBN: 0-312-90072-4, Can. ISBN: 0-312-90073-2.

(Rearranged slightly by Iris Grayson to accommodate MealMaster format)

(Iris' Note: For a lowered-fat version, let the "meat" be ground
turkey breast and use egg substitute.)
