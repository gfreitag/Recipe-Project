Title: Shrimp Newburg - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 t  Tabasco
1/2 ea Egg Beaters, beaten
1 lb Cooked peeled shrimp, halved
2 ea Tb sherry or cooking sherry
1/2 c  Liquid Butter Buds
2 ea Tb flour
3/4 t  Lite salt, optional
1/8 t  Cayene pepper
1 1/2 c  Evaporated skim milk
Combine Butter buds, flour and seasoning. Add evaporated skim milk
gradually and cook until thickened, stirring constantly. Stir small
amount of hot sauce into Egg Beaters; add remaining sauce, stirring
constantly. Add shrimp; heat through. Remove from heat and stir in
sherry. Serve over rice or toast points. Per serving: 163 cal., 1.5g
fat (8%), 118mg chol., 0g fiber, 23g pro., 12g carb., 363mg sod.
