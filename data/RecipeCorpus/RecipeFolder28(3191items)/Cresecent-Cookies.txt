Title: Cresecent Cookies
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 1/2 dozen
2 1/4 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 c  Pecans; chopped or almonds
1 ts Red food coloring
Sugar
1 c  Butter (or marg.); softened
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla extract; OR
1/2 ts Almond extract
1 tb Water
Combine butter, sugar, vanilla, water and flour; blend well.  Add
nuts and food coloring.  Chill for several hours.

Shape into crescents or half-moons.  Place on greased cookie sheets
and bake at 300 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes.  Do not let cookies
brown. Roll in white or colored sugar.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, May 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
