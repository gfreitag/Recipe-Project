Title: Seven Minute Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 batch
1    Egg white, unbeaten
1/2 ts Vanilla
7/8 c  White sugar
3 tb Cold water
MMMMM-----------------------FOR CHOCOLATE----------------------------
OR
4 1/2 tb Cocoa
1 1/2    Squares melted bitter
Chocolate
Beat sugar, water and egg white in upper part of double boiler.

Place over boiling water and beat seven minutes with egg beater.

Remove from fire and add vanilla.

For Chocolate version of frosting, and bitter chocolate or cocoa after
removing from fire.
