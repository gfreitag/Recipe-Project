Title: Pistachio Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 3 servings
Cool Whip
1 c  Milk
1    Baked pie shell
2    Boxes of instant pistachio
Pudding
2 c  Vanilla ice cream
Mix all ingredients with mixer.  Pour in a baked pie shell and top
with Cool Whip, sprinkle with chocolate shavings. Randy Rigg
