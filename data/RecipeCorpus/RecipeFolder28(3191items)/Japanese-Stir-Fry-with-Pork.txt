Title: Japanese Stir Fry with Pork
Categories: Meats
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Oil
1/2 ts Cornstarch
2 ts Dry sherry
1 ts Soy sauce
2 c  Hot cooked rice
10 oz (1 pkg) frozen stir-fry
Japanese vegetables with
Seasonings
1    Boneless pork chop, cut into
Julienne strips
1>. Empty frozen vegetables into a 1-quart casserole; reserve
seasoning packet. Add pork and oil to vegetables. Cover with lid or
vented plastic wrap and microwave on high three minutes. 2>. Empty
contents of seasoning packet into a small bowl. Stir in cornstarch;
blend in sherry and soy sauce. Stir mixture into vegetables; recover.
Microwave on high three to four minutes; or until vegetables are
crisp tender and pork is no longer pink. Stir and serve over rice.
