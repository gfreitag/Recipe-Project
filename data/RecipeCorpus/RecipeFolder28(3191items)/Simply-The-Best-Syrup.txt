Title: Simply The Best Syrup
Categories: Breakfast, Syrups
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Allspice, whole
1/2 ts Whole cloves
1    Zest of 1 orange or lemon
1 ts Maple extract; optional
1/4 ts Almond extract
3 c  Brown sugar
(one 1-lb box)
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
1    Or 2 cinnamon sticks
-- broken in pieces
Recipe by: The Cook's Book of Uncommon Recipes - ISBN 0-940367-14-9

Mix brown sugar and water together. Add whole spices and citrus zest.
Cover and let steep for several hours. Taste to see if spicy enough
and if so strain into container with cover. Stir in maple and almond
extracts. Store in refrigerator. Makes 2 cups.

(Author's Note) Try this and store-bought syrup will not satisfy you
again. Recipe is flexible and you should feel free to adjust to your
taste by varying the flavors.
