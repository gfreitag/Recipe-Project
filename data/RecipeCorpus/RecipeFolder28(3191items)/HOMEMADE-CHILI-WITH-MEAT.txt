Title: HOMEMADE CHILI WITH MEAT
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 c  Tomatoe juice
10 1/2 oz Can cond. beef broth
1 tb Chile powder
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
16 oz Red kidney beans
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Chopped onions
1 c  Chopped celery
2 cn Tomatoes,undrained,cut up
Soak beans as directed on package. Drain; reserve 2 quarts liquid in
large deep pot, bring beans and reserved liquid to boil; reduce heat
and simmer gently, covered for 2 hours. In large skillet, brown
ground beef; drain. Add ground beef and remaining ingredients to
beans; simmer gently, uncovered an additional 1 to 1 1/2 hours until
beans are tender. Makes 8 (1 1/2-cups) servings You might like to add
some of the following; sugar, cummins, Roi Tel hot tomatoes, & even
more ideas.
