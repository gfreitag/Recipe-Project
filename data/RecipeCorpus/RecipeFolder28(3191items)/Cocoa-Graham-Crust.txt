Title: Cocoa Graham Crust
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
1 1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
4 tb Butter; melted
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:05 In a medium bowl, mix together graham cracker
crumbs, butter, sugar, and cocoa until well blended. Press firmly
into bottom and up sides of 9-inch pie pan. Freeze while preparing
pie filling and topping.
