Title: Jim's Horseradish BBQ Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
2 t  Worcestershire sauce
1 t  Hot pepper sauce
1/2 c  Prepared horseradish
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Sugar
1 t  Cornstarch
Make a slurry of the lemon juice, vinegar and cornstarch; add it to a
sauce pan with the Worcestershire, sugar and pepper sauce. Bring just
to a boil. Lower heat immediately and simmer 10 min. Remove from heat
and stir in the horseradish. Use as a BBQ sauce or chill and use as a
condiment with cold sliced roast meats .
