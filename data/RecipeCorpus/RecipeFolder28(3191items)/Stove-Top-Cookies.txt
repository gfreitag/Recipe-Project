Title: Stove Top Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 pn Salt
1/2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Raw quick cooking oats
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/2 c  Salad oil
1/2 c  Milk
In saucepan combine sugar, cocoa, oil and milk.  Bring to boiling
point and boil 3 minutes.  Remove from heat and quickly stir in salt,
vanilla and oats.  Blend thoroughly.  Immediately drop by tablespoon
onto waxed paper one inch apart.  Let cool thoroughly before storing
in an air tight container. Randy Rigg
