Title: Valentine Sugar Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 cookies
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 ts Almond extract
2 1/2 c  Flour; all purpose
Red decorators sugar ( opt.)
1 c  Butter or margarine;
- softened
1 1/2 c  Sugar; confectioner's
1    Egg; lightly beaten
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add egg and extracts. Stir
in flour; mix well. Chill for several hours. On a lightly floured
surface, roll dough to 1/4 inch thickness; cut with a 2 1/2 or 3 inch
heart shaped cookie cutter. Place on ungreased baking sheets;
sprinkle with red sugar, if desired. Bake at 375F for 8 to 10 minutes
or until lightly browned.
