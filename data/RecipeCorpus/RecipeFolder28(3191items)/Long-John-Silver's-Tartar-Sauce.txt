Title: Long John Silver's Tartar Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
Or sweet gherkins
1/2 ts Dry dill weed if using dill
Pickles
1 ts Onion powder
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
3 tb Creamy horseradish sauce
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Minced dill pickles
mix all well and refrigerate in covered container till time to serve.
Makes 2 cups.
