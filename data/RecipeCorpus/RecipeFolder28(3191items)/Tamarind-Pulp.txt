Title: Tamarind Pulp
Categories: Fruit, Indian
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
2 1/2 c  Hot water
11 oz Dried tamarind
Soak dried tamarind overnight in hot water or boil it for 15 minutes
over medium heat.

Rub tamarind through your fingers to reduce it to a pulp, then rub
through a sieve and collect the pulp. Discard the husks. Excess pulp
can be frozen.
