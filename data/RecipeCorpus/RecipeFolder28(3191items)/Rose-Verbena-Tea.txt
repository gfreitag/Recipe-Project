Title: Rose Verbena Tea
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 batch
1    Heaped teacup verbena (more
-if you want a stronger
-lemon taste)
1/2 lb Good black or Chinese green
-tea
2    Level teacups dried rose
-petals or buds
Mix together in a large bowl with your hands.  Pack in tins or jars
with air-tight lids and use 1 heaped tablespoon per pot.

from Edible Gifts by Claire Clifton and Martina Nicolls typed by
