Title: Oriental Bar-B-Q Pork Tenderloin
Categories: Bbq/grill, Asian, Pork, Marinades
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Ground ginger (fresh)
1 tb Msg (if desired)
19 oz Bottled bar-b-q sauce
8 lb Pork tenderloin (boneless)
1 1/3 c  Soy sauce
2/3 c  Oriental toasted sesame oil
4    Minced garlic cloves (large)
Preparation Time: 6:00 Trim pork tenderloin of all fat (the sesame
oil will prevent burning on the grill.

Combine 1/3 c soy sauce, 1/3 c sesame oil, 3 minced garlic cloves,
ginger, and MSG (if used) in a deep bowl and mix well (make sure the
ginger is not not clumping.

Place pork into marinade and place in refrigerator for 6 to 8 hours
(over- night if you prefer).

Remove pork from marinade and place on covered grill.

Add wet wood to grill firepan to insure adequate supply of smoke.

Prepare sauce: Combine bar-b-q sauce, 1/3 c sesame oil, 1/3 c soy
sauce and 1 minced garlic clove in a bowl and mix well. Serve over
sliced pork tenderloins.

NOTES:  Marinate 6-24 hours.
