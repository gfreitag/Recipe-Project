Title: Rice and Hamburger Oriental
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Cream of chicken soup
1 c  Celery, chopped fine
2 c  Water
2 ts Soy sauce
1 c  Raw rice
1 lb Hamburger
1    Onion, chopped fine
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
Brown onion and hamburger.  Combine with other ingredients and place
in a casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours. Randy Rigg
