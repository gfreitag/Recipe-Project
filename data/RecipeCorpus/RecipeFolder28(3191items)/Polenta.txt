Title: Polenta
Categories: Italian, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1 7/8 oz Yellow cornmeal
7/8 oz Parmesan cheese
Dash of salt
5 1/2 oz Milk
5 1/2 oz Water
3/4 oz Butter
Recipe by: Team USA

Place milk, water and butter in pot; heat to boiling. Combine
cornmeal, cheese and salt; sift slowly into boiling mixture. Cook for
10 minutes. Portion mixture into buttered individual molds;
refrigerate for 3 hours. Unmold onto buttered sheet pans. Bake at 350
degrees F for 10 minutes.

SERVES SIX
