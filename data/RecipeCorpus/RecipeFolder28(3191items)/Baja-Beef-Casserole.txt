Title: Baja Beef Casserole
Categories: Beef, Casseroles, Main dish
Yield: 6 casserole
1 tb Chili powder;
2 c  Crushed tortilla chips;
-divided
2 c  (8-oz) jack cheese;
-(low-fat/cal)
1 lb Ground Beef;
-(chicken or turkey)
12 oz (1 jar) chunky salsa;
1 c  Sweet corn;
3/4 c  Mayonnaise; (low-fat/cal)
Heat oven to 350 F.  Brown meat and drain.  Stir in salsa, corn,
mayonnaise and chili powder.  Layer 1/2 mixture, chips, and cheese in
2 quart casserole.  Repeat layers.  Bake 20 minutes or until
thoroughly heated. Top with shredded lettuce and chopped tomato if
desired. Serves 6.

Source: Violette Walsh, The Joy of Giving Cookbook, Kaiser Permanente
Volunteer Assiciation, San Diego, Calif. Brought to you and yours via
Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
