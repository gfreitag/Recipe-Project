Title: Hawaiian Wedding Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1    8 oz pkg cream cheese
1 cn Fruit cocktail in juice
1 cn Crushed pineapple in juice
(both 20 oz each)
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1    Any flavor cake mix
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding
1 1/3 c  Juice drained from fruit
1/2 c  Coconut
1    9 oz container Cool Whip
Make cake mix (flavor of your choice) as directed on package. Bake in
a 13 x 9.  Cool.

Combine pudding, softened cream cheese, and the juice drained from the
fruit equivalent to 1 1/3 cups. Add milk if needed to equal 1 1/3
cups Beat together at medium speed well, and spread on top of cake.

Combine fruit cocktail and pineapple and sprinkle on top of pudding
mixture. Then, sprinkle nuts and coconut on top of fruit. Top with
cool whip, and finish with additional sprinkles of coconut and nuts.
Store in fridge.

(Note, you can vary the fruit and the pudding flavors, as you wish)
