Title: Hungarian Beef Gulya
Categories: Beef, East europe
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 t  Salt
1 ea Flour
4 sl Bacon
2 lg Onions, sliced
1 1/2 T  Paprika
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 cn Beef broth
1/2 t  Caraway seed
3 lb Beef round steak
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
Saute bacon until crisp, remove. Cut beef into 1" pieces.  Brown in
bacon fat.  Remove. Saute onion in bacon fat until golden.  Add
garlic and paprika. Cook stirring constantly for 2 minutes.  Return
beef to pan. Add beef broth, salt and caraway seed. Cover and simmer
2 hours. Thicken sauce with flour mixed with water. Serve over cooked
noodles.
