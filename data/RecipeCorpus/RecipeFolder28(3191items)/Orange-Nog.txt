Title: Orange Nog
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
Dash of angostura bitters
Drop of vanilla extract
1    Part orange juice
1    Egg, beaten
1 ts Sugar
Put all ingredients in a blender and whiz until smooth.  Chill and
serve.
