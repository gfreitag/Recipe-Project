Title: Lane Cake Filling
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Nuts
8 ea Egg yolks
1 c  Sugar
1    Wine glass of wine
1 ts Vanilla
Beat yolks fo eggs with sugar and butter.  Cool in double boiler
until thick.  Add raisins, nuts, wine and vanilla.

Usually used as filling with at least 2 regular cake layers cut in
half.
