Title: Corn Balls
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Cooked whole kernel corn
3    Eggs, beaten
1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
3 1/2 c  Herb-seasoned stuffing
-croutons
In a saucepan, cook onion and celery in butter until tender; set
aside to cool. In a bowl, combine croutons, corn, eggs, water, salt,
pepper and onion mixture; mix well. Shape into eight to 10 balls.
Place in an ungreased shallow baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at 375!
for 25-30 minutes. Yield: 8-10 servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
