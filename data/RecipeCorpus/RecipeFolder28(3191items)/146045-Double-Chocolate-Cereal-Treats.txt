Title: 146045 Double Chocolate Cereal Treats
Categories: Desserts, Snacks, Chocolate
Yield: 12 servings
-butter chips
4 c  Cocoa Puffs cereal
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1 c  Milk chocolate or peanut
Butter square pan, 9x9x2 inches.  Heat corn syrup and chocolate chips
in 2 quart saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly, until chips
are melted. Stir in cereal until evenly coated.  Press mixture in pan
with buttered back of spoon; cool.  Cut into about 2 1/4 x 1 1/2 inch
bars. Yields 2 dozen bars.
