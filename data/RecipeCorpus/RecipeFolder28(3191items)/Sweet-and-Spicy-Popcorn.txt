Title: Sweet and Spicy Popcorn
Categories: Snacks, Healthy
Yield: 6 portions
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
1/8 ts Ground ginger
Nonsick spray coating
6 c  Popped popcorn (no oil)
2 tb Sugar
2 ts Water
Spray a COLD 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan with nonstick spray coating.
Place popcorn in the banking pan.

In a small mixing bowl, stir together, sugar, water, cinnamon, nutmeg
and ginger.  Add spice mixture to popcorn in banking pan.  Toss
popcorn until coated.

Bake in a 350 oven for 15 minutes, stirring once or twice.

Transfer popcorn from baking pan to a large piece of foil.   Cool
popcorn completely.

If desired, store in a tightly covered container.

Makes six - 3/4 cup servings.

76 calories; 2 g protein, 16 g carbo, 1 g fat, 0 chol, 1 mg sodium
