Title: BRIE EN CROUTE
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 5 servings
1    Egg
2 T  Milk
1    Brie round
1    Pastry dough; or filo
Completely enclose cheese in pastry.  Make egg wash of egg and milk.
Put brie on baking sheet with seam side of dough down.  Brush with
egg wash. Cut garnish of leaves, flowers, etc., from scraps of pastry
and decorate top of Brie.  Bake at 350F about 30 minutes or until
golden brown. Submitted By SAM WARING   On   02-26-95
