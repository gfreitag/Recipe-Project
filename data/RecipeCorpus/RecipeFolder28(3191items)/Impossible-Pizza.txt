Title: Impossible Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Bisquick
2 tb Cheese, parmesan, grated
1 c  Milk
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPINGS-------------------------------
Bell peppers, chopped
2 tb Cheese, parmesan, grated
3/4 c  Cheese, mozzarella, shredded
1/2 c  Prego
Sausage; or ground meat
Onions, chopped
Heat oven to 425.  Grease 1 pie plate per 4 servings. Sprinkle onion
and Parmesan cheese in pie plate.  Beat milk, eggs, and bisquick 15
seconds in blender on high. Pour into pie plate. Bake 20 minutes.

Spread pizza sauce over top.  Top with remaining ingredients. Bake
10-15 minutes, until cheese is light brown.

Cool 15 minutes.

Sylvia's comments: still rose too much for my preference. Try reducing
bisquick.
