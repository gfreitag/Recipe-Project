Title: 598511 Keftathes  (Greek  Meatballs)
Categories: Greek, Beef
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Oregano
1    Beaten egg
-Salt and pepper to taste
1 lb Hamburger
4 sl Dampened bread
1 sm Onion chopped or grated
Mix all ingredients together.  Make into small balls and roll in flour
until thoroughly covered.  Fry in pan containing 1/8 inch vegetable
oil. Cook on one side and then turn over.  Add oil as needed. Heat
oil to medium heat.  Should make approximately 20 meatballs.
