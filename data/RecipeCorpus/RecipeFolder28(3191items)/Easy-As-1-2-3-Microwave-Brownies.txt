Title: Easy As 1-2-3 Microwave Brownies
Categories: Chocolate, Microwave
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
2/3 c  Flour
1 c  Pecans; chopped
Powdered sugar
1    Stick butter or margarine
1 c  Granulated sugar
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:16 1. In a medium bowl, beat together butter,
sugar, eggs, and vanilla with an electric mixer on medium until light
and fluffy, 1 to 2 mins. Beat in cocoa. Add flour and beat until well
blended. By hand stir in pecans. Spread evenly in a wax paper-lined
8-inch square glass dish.

2. Cook in a microwave on High 3 mins. Turn dish a quarter turn and
cook 2 1/2 to 3 mins longer. Let cool. Sift powdered sugar over top.
Cut into 16 or 25 squares.
