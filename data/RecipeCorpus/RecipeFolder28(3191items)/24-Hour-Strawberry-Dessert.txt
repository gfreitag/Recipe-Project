Title: 24-Hour Strawberry Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sour cream
ds Salt
Strawberries, whole
2 c  Strawberries, fresh; sliced
2 tb Sugar, brown
1 c  Marshmallows, miniature
Sprinkle strawberries with sugar and let stand 10 minutes.  Fold
marshmallows and sour cream into strawberry mixture.  Add salt and mix
gently.  Place in a covered container and store in refrigerator
overnight.

To serve; spoon into individual serving dishes; garnish with a whole
strawberry.
