Title: Peanut Butter Snack
Categories: Snacks, Kids, Nuts/grains
Yield: 8 cups
1 c  Small pretzel twists
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Honey roasted peanuts
5 c  Kix cereal
1/4 c  Peanut butter
2 T  Margarine
Heat oven to 350.  Pour cereal into large bowl.   Heat peanut butter
and margarine to boiling in 1 qt saucepan, stirring occasionally.
Pour over cereal; toss until evenly coated.  Spread in ungreased 13 x
9 pan. Bake 10 to 12 minutes, stirring occasionally; remove from
oven. Stir in pretzels, raisins and peanuts.  Spread on waxed paper;
cool 2 hours. Store in airtight container.
