Title: Austrian Pork Loin With Apples & Cranberry Sauce
Categories: Healthy
Yield: 5 servings
2 lg Tart apples, peeled,
-- cored, and
-- thinly sliced
1 cn Cranberry sauce, whole
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Apple cider vinegar
2 tb Packed brown sugar
-----to serve-----
8 oz Reduced-fat egg noodles
-- cooked as directed
-----pork & fruit-----
1 lb Boneless pork loin,
-- trimmed of all fat
-- cut in 1/4" slices
1/2 ts Dried thyme leaves
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Salt; optional
1 c  Onion; chopped
2 ts Margarine, non-diet,
-- tub-style corn oil
--or canola
Recipe by: Skinny One-Pot Meals - ISBN 0-940625-75-X Sprinkle pork
with thyme, pepper, and salt, if desired. In Dutch oven or similar
large, heavy pot, combine pork, onion, and margarine. Cook over
medium heat, stirring frequently, until pork has changed color.

Add apples, cranberry sauce, Worcestershire sauce, vinegar, and brown
sugar and stir to mix well. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and
simmer 40 mins, stirring occasionally, until pork is tender.

Serve over cooked noodles.
