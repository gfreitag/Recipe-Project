Title: Rice Pudding
Categories: Puddings
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/8 ts Vanilla
2 tb Butter
1/3 c  Rice, short-grain,
-- uncooked
1 qt Milk
Recipe by: Alison Meyer

Preheat oven to 300 degrees.

Sprinkle rice evenly over the bottom of a 1 1/2 quart casserole.  Mix
milk, sugar, and vanilla, pour over rice, sprinkle with nutmeg, and
dot with butter.  Bake uncovered for 2 1/2 hours, stirring every 15
minutes for the first 2 hours.  Serve warm.
