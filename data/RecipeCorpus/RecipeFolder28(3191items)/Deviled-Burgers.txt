Title: Deviled Burgers
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Salt
3 ds Tobasco
1 lb Ground beef
2 tb Cold water
2 tb Spicy brown prepared mustard
1 ts Grated onion
Mix all ingredients together well by hand.  Shape into 4 patties and
cook by your favorite method.
