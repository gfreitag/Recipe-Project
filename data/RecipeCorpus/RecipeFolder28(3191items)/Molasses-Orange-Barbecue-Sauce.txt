Title: Molasses Orange Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 3 cups
1 tb Seasoned salt
1 tb Dry mustard
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Orange peel, finely shredded
1 1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Pepper, black
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 cn Tomato soup, condensed
-10 3/4 oz size
1 cn Tomato sauce, 8 oz can
1/2 c  Molasses, light
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 tb Minced onion, instant
In a saucepan, combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil; reduce heat
and simmer uncovered fo 20 minutes. Use to baste beef or poultry last
15 minutes of grilling.
