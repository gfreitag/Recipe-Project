Title: Homemade Cajun Seasoning
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 999 servings
2 ts Ground red pepper
2 ts Paprika
2 ts Black pepper
2 ts White pepper
2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Onion powder
Stir all ingredients together.  Store in an airtight container in a
cool, dry place.  Use this seasoning to add a little zip to many main
dishes and meats.

From: 25 Years of Festive Foods, Southwestern Public Service Company
