Title: Scalloped Apples
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Salt
4 tb Butter
1/2 ts Cinnamon
8    Apples
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
Quarter and core unpeeled apples.  Mix all ingredients in Dutch oven,
or casserole dish if done in microwave.  Cook until apples are tender
and sauce is clear and thickened. Randy Rigg
