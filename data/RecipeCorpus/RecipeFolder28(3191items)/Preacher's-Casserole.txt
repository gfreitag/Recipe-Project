Title: Preacher's Casserole
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn French cut green beans
(16 oz); drained
1 sm Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
1    Stack Round buttery crackers
1/4 c  Butter; melted
1 cn Cream of chicken soup,
(10 3/4 oz)
1 cn Cream of celery soup,
(10 3/4 oz)
4 tb Sour cream
1 cn Shoe peg corn (16 oz) can
-- use whole kernel; drained
Recipe by: Come and Get It! by Jr. Welfare League of Talladega, Al Mix
soups and sour cream. Add corn, beans, onion, and cheese. Mix well.
Pour into casserole dish. Crush crackers and put on top. Pour melted
butter over top. Cook about 20 mins. at 350 degrees or until crackers
are brown.

Originally submitted to book by Mrs. Randall Stewart (Reba).
