Title: SAUERKRAUT CASSEROLE  RBTN28A
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
6 sl Slices bacon
1 ea Onion, chopped
16 oz Can kraut
1 cn Tomatoes
1/2 c  Brown sugar
Drain sauerkraut, add tomatoes and sugar. Chop bacon and brown with
onions. Combine with kraut mixture and bake in 375F oven for 1 hour.
