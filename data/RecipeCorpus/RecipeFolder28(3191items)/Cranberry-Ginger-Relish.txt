Title: Cranberry-Ginger Relish
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Finely chopped peeled fresh
Ginger
12 oz Fresh cranberries
1 1/2 c  Orange juice
1 c  Sugar
Combine all ingrdients in a heavy, large saucepan.  Bring to a boil
over high heat, stirring until sugar dissolves.  Reduce heat to low
and simmer until relish is thick, stirring often and mashing berries
slightly, about 15 minutes.  Transfer relish to small bowl.  Cover
and chill.  Can be prepared up to 1 month ahead.  Keep chilled.
