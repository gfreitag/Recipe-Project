Title: Baked Eggs
Categories: Jewish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 serving
4    Raw eggs;
Cheese of your choice;
-grated (optional)
1 c  Matzo farfel;
1 ts Salt;
1/8 ts White pepper;
1 1/4 c  Tomato sauce w/ mushrooms
In a small bowl, combine matzo farfel with salt, pepper and the tomato
sauce.  Divide the mixture evenly into 4 well-greased 8 or 10 oz
baking dishes.  Make a well in the center of each dish and drop an
egg into each depression.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes,
until the eggs are firm.  If desired, sprinkle the top of each egg
with grated cheese and bake until the cheese melts. Source: The
Complete Passover Cookbook by Frances R. AvRutick Brought to you and
