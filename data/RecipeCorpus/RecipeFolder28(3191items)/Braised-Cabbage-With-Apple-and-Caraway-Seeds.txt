Title: Braised Cabbage With Apple and Caraway Seeds
Categories: Vegetables, East europe
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 t  Caraway Seeds
1 ea Salt
2 T  Butter
1 t  Garlic, minced
1 ea Apple, peeled, cored, sliced
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1/2 c  Chicken Broth
1 ea White Pepper, freshly ground
1 c  Onion, coarsely sliced
1 ea Cabbage Head, shredded
1 T  White Vinegar
Melt the butter in a skillet and add the onion and garlic. Cook,
stirring, until wilted. Do not brown. Add the cabbage, apple,
vinegar, caraway seeds, chicken broth, salt and pepper. Bring to a
boil, stir, cover and simmer for 15 minutes. Uncover and if there is
too much liquid, reduce it accordingly. Serves 4.
