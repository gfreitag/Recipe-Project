Categories: Healthy, Beverages
Yield: 24 ----------
1/2 c  Brown sugar
AND
2 t  Sweet 'n Low Brown
4 t  Whole cloves
5 ea Cinnamon sitcks
9 c  Cranberry juice cocktail
9 c  Unsweetened pineapple juice
