Title: Super-Easy Chocolate Mousse
Categories: Desserts, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 ts Vanilla extract
10 1/2 oz Silken tofu, extra-firm,
-- drained & crumbled
Blend tofu until smooth.

Melt chocolate over medium heat in a pot placed in a larger pot
filled with water. Blend chocolate & vanilla into tofu until there is
no white visible. Spoon evenly into 4 parfait glasses or wine goblets
& chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
