Title: Rowe's Spicy Strawberry Nut Loaf
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Breads
Yield: 2 servings
2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
4    Eggs; beaten
1 1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  Pecans; chopped
2 c  Fresh strawberries; sliced
3 c  All-purpose flour; sifted
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
In a large mixing bowl, sift together flour, sugar, salt, soda and
spices.

In a separate smaller bowl, blend eggs and oil, then gradually stir
into dry ingredients alternately with strawberries. Stir in nuts.
Pour batter into two greased, floured pans.  Bake in preheated 350
oven 45-50 minutes.
