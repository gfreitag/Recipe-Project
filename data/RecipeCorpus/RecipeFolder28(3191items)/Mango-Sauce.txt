Title: Mango Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
1 1/2 ts Ginger, peeled and grated
1 ts Serrano chile
-seeded and minced
2/3 c  Mango, peeled and chopped
2/3 c  Chicken broth
2 ts Lime juice
Combine all ingredients in small saucepan and cook over medium heat
for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Pour into a blender and
process until smooth.
