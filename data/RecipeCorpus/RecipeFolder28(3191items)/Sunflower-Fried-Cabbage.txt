Title: Sunflower Fried Cabbage
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Sunflower seeds, hulled
2 tb Butter or bacon fat
1 ds Vinegar, white or wine
1/4 x  Head cabbage
1 x  Small onion
1 x  Salt & pepper to taste
Chop onion and cabbage coarsely. Melt butter or bacon grease in
medium frypan, add sunflower seeds, toss to coat and saute lightly.
Add cabbage and onion, toss to coat. Simmer 10 to 15 minutes. Season
to taste with salt,pepperand dash of vinegar. Serve very hot.
