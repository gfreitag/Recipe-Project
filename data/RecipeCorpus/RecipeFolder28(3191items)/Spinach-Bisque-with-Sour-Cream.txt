Title: Spinach Bisque with Sour Cream
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
2    Parsley sprigs
1 lg Sprig thyme
1 sm Serrano chili
2 tb Dry sherry
Sour cream
1/4 c  Butter (1/2 stick)
1 lg Onion(s), chopped
6 c  Canned chicken broth
10 oz Pkg ready-to-use
-fresh spinach leaves
-large stems removed
Melt butter in heavy large Dutch oven over medium heat. Add onion;
saut until almost tender, about 8 minutes. Add broth, spinach,
parsley, thyme and whole chili; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer
45 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Discard thyme sprig and chili. Puree soup in blender in batches.
Return to pot. Add sherry and simmer 5 minutes. Ladle soup into
bowls. Top with dollop of sour cream and serve.

Source: Norma at the Wharfhouse
Montego Bay, Jamaica
