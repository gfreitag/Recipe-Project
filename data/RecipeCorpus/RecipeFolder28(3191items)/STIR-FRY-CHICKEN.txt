Title: STIR FRY CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 pk MANDARIN"FROZEN FOODS"
2 tb MARGARIN
1/2 pk ORIENTAL"FROZEN FOODS"
1 x  CHICKEN AS DESIRED
1 x  SOY SAUCE
Step #1  Boil chicken & bone it, cut into chunks. Step #2 Put one T.
margarin in skillet & add chicken. Stir while browning. Step #3 While
browning chicken, Put one T. margarine & remaining ingredients in
another skillet & STIR FRY, then combine the 2 together and add broth
& soysauce. Cover & simmer until tender, serve hot...
