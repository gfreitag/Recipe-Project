Title: Savory Shrimp
Categories: Seafood, Beer
Yield: 1 servings
1    No  2 can tomatoes
1/2 c  Catsup
1 cn Beer
1 lb Shrimp, cooked
-salt and pepper
4 c  Cooked rice
4 sl Bacon, diced
1    Stalk celery, diced
2    Onions, sliced
4    Green peppers, cut in
-rings
1    Clove garlic, minced
2    No  2 cans okra
Cook bacon; reserve. Saute celery, onions, green peppers and garlic in
bacon fat until soft. Drain okra; add with tomatoes, catsup and beer.
Simmer till thick. Add shrimp and bacon. Heat well. Season to taste
with salt and pepper. Serve with rice. From the files of Al Rice,
North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
