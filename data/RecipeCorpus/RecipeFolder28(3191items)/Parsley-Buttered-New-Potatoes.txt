Title: Parsley Buttered New Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Irish
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 lb Butter
1 cl Garlic, minced
3 tb Parsley, chopped
2 lb Red potatoes, small, washed
Salt
Water
Peel a small strip around the middle of each potato. Boil potatoes in
salted water until tender. Drain. Melt butter in a saucepan. Saute
garlic briefly. Toss hot potatoes in butter and garlic; sprinkle
generously with chopped parsley. Serve hot.
