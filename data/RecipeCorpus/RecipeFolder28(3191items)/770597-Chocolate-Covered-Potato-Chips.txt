Title: 770597 Chocolate Covered Potato Chips
Categories: Snacks, Chocolate, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
2    (12 oz.) pkgs. milk
-chocolate pieces
7 oz Can Ripple potato chips
2 T  Cooking oil
Place chocolate and cooking oil in casserole.  Microwave at 50% power
for 4-7 minutes, stirring occasionally until chocolate melts and will
stir into smooth sauce. Dip chips in chocolate, one at a time,
leaving one end uncovered.  Tap gently to remove excess chocolate.
Place on wax paper. Repeat.  Refrigerate chips until chocolate is
set. Store in tightly covered container.
