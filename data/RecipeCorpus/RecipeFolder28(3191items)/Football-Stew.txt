Title: Football Stew
Categories: Crockpot
Yield: 4 servings
4    Carrots
1    Celery stalk
4    Potatoes
1 lb Beef stew meat
2 c  Tomato juice
2 tb Sugar
3 tb Tapioca
2 ts Salt
1    Onions
Combine tomato juice sugar, tapioca, and salt in crockpot on Low. Dice
onion.  Slice carrots.  Chop celery.  Cube potatoes and beef.  Add
vegetables to crockpot, then meat. Cover with tomato juice mixture.
Cook on Low for 8-10 hours.
