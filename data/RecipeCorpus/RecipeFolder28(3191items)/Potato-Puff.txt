Title: Potato Puff
Categories: Healthy, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Lowfat cheese; grated
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 c  Green onions; chopped
2    Egg whites
6    Potatoes; cooked
-- and drained
3/4 c  Skim milk
3/4 ts Lite salt or less
Recipe by: The New American Diet - ISBN 0-671-66375-5

Add the skim milk and Lite Salt to potatoes and mash until smooth. Add
cheese, mustard and green onions to the mashed potatoes. Mix well.
Place in a lightly oiled 1 1/2-qt baking dish. May be prepared ahead
to this point and refrigerated.

Just prior to baking, preheat oven to 375 F. Beat egg whites until
very stiff and spread over potatoes. Bake 50-60 mins and serve
immediately.

Makes 4-6 servings.
