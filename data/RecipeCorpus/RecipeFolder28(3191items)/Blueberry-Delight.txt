Title: Blueberry Delight
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
350 degrees for 25 minutes.
Cool.
Filling:
2 pk Dream Whip
1    Box of powdered sugar
8 oz Cream cheese
Topping:
1 cn Blueberry pie filling
Crust:
2    Sticks of butter
2 c  Flour
1 c  Walnuts, chopped
1 ts Salt
Cream butter and flour; add
Nuts and salt.  Pat into a
9 x 13 inch pan and bake at
Prepare Dream Whip according to the directions on the package.
Combine with sugar and cream cheese.  Mix until smooth.  Pour onto
crust. Spoon pie filling on top of cheese filling.  Chill. Randy Rigg
