Title: Herbed Onions
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Sage
1/2 ts Basil
1 tb Minced celery leaves
1/4 c  Defatted chicken or vegetabl
4    Med onions; peeled and quart
1/4 c  Chopped mushrooms
3    Tomatoes; peeled seeded chop
1    Clove garlic; mashed through
Toss all of the ingredients into a round, medium sized casserole dish
and combine well. Cover and microwave on full power until the onions
are just tender, about 5 minutes. Let the onions stand for 3 minutes
before lifting the cover nd serving. The onions are even tastier when
reheated and eaten the second day. Great as a side dish to turkey or
roast pork. Printed in the Detroit News.
