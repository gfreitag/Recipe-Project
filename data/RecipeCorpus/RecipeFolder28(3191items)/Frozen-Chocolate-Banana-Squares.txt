Title: Frozen Chocolate Banana Squares
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 9 servings
1/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
3 tb Milk
2 md Banana; sliced
2 1/2 c  Whipped topping, frozen
-- thawed
1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
1    Stick butter or margarine,
(+ 3 tbs); softened
1 c  Chocolate wafer cookie crumb
-- crushed
1 pk (8 oz) cream cheese; softene
1 1/4 c  Powdered sugar
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:30 1. To make crust, melt 3 Tbs butter. In a
small bowl, mix together cookie crumbs and melted butter. Press
mixture evenly over bottom of an 8-inch square baking pan.

2. In a medium bowl, combine remaining 1 stick butter and cream
cheese. Beat with an electric mixer on medium until smooth. Add
powdered sugar, cocoa, and milk. Beat until smooth and blended.

3. Spread cream cheese mixture evenly over prepared crust. Top with
banana slices. Spread whipped topping over dessert and sprinkle nuts
on top. Cover and freeze at least 6 hours before serving. To serve,
cut into 9 squares.
