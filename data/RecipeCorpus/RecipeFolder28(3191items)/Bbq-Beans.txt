Title: Bbq Beans
Categories: Vegetables, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Turmeric; dried
1 c  Tomato sauce
1 1/2 tb Molasses
1/2 ts Apple cider vinegar
1 ds Tabasco sauce
4 c  Beans; cooked
1 lg Onion; chopped
1 lg Pepper; chopped
1    Garlic clove; crushed
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Cumin
Recipe by: The McDougall Plan Preparation Time: 0:35 Saute onion,
pepper and garlic in 1/4 c water for 5 minutes. Add mustard, chili,
cumin, tumeric and stir well to mix. Add remaining ingredients and
mix well. Cook over low heat until heated through, about 15 minutes.
HELPFUL HINT: This is great on a bun like sloppy-joe mix, with
ketchup and mustard. Good take-along for picnics. Use either hot or
cold. Also good over whole grains or as a sandwich spread. From the
collection of Sue Smith, S.Smith34, Uploaded June 16, 1994
