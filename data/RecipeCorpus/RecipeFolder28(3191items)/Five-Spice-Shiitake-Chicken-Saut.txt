Title: Five-Spice Shiitake Chicken Saut
Categories: Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Soy sauce
2/3 c  Water
1 lb Chicken cutlets
-thinly sliced
2 ts Chinese five-spice powder
3/4 lb Shiitake mushrooms
1. Sprinkle both sides of the chicken cutlets with t he five-spice
powder, rubbing it in well. Trim and slice the mushrooms.

2. Heat 3 tablespoons of the oil in a large frying pan. Add the
chicken and cook over medium-high heat until browned and cooked
through, about 1« minutes per side. Remove to a plate, leaving the
drippings in the pan.

3. Heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil in the pan. Add the
mushrooms and cook over medium-high heat, stirring, until lightly
browned and quite tender, about 3 minutes.

4. Add 2/3 cup water and the soy sauce to the pan. Boil, uncovered,
until the sauce is slightly reduced, 1 to 2 minutes. Return the
chicken and any accumulated juices to the pan, heat through and serve.
