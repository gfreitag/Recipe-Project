Title: Mock Cracker Torte
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 40 servings
1 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1 c  Walnuts; chopped
40    Soda crackers
2    Sticks butter (do not
-- substitute margarine)
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:35 1. Preheat oven to 400 F. Place crackers
side-by-side in a single layer without overlapping in a foil-lined 10
x 15-inch jelly-roll pan; set aside.

2. In a medium saucepan, combine butter and brown sugar. Heat to
boiling over medium heat, stirring often. Cook, stirring, 2 mins.

3. Pour butter mixture evenly over crackers and using a knife, spread
until evenly covered. Bake 5 to 7 mins, or until very bubbly.

4. Remove from oven and let stand 1 min. Sprinkle chocolate chips
evenly over crackers and let stand until melted, 5 mins. With a
knife, spread chocolate evenly over all. Sprinkle nuts on top. Let
stand until cool. Then refrigerate until candies are cold and
chocolate is set. Cut into about 40 pieces before serving.
