Title: Baked Corn Casserole
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
-corn
1 cn (4 ounces) chopped green
-chilies
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 cn (2.8 ounces) french-fried
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
2 pk (3 ounces each) cream
-cheese, softened
1 cn (17 ounces) whole kernel
-corn, drained
1 cn (16-1/2 ounces) cream-style
In a large bowl, beat together butter and cream cheese. Stir in the
kernel corn, cream-style corn, chilies and onion; mix well. Pour into
a greased 8-in. square baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at 350! for 15
minutes. Remove from oven; stir in half of the fried onions. Sprinkle
remaining fried onions on top. Bake 15 minutes longer. Yield: 8-10
servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
