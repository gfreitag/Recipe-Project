Title: Chinese Fish^
Categories: Healthy, Seafood
Yield: 2 servings
9 oz (to 10) salmon or halibut
-steak with bone
4    Green onions, with green
-tops, diced
4    Or 5 snow peas
1 ts Grated gingerroot
1 tb Dry sherry
1 tb Reduced sodium soy sauce
2 tb Water
1 1/2 ts Apple juice concentrate
Preheat oven to 375F. Mix gingerroot, sherry, soy sauce, water and
apple juice in a small bowl for sauce; set aside. Spray a baking dish
with Pam. Place fish in dish. Combine sauce, green onions and snow
peas; pour over fish. Cover and nake 20-25 minutes until fish turns
opague. Remove sauce from pan; set aside. Broil fish 3-4 minutes
until crispy. Pour sauce over fish. Serve with rice. Makes 2 servings.

Per serving without rice: Calories: 180 Protein: 29g Carbohydrates:
7g Fat: 4g, 20% Sodium: 389mg Cholesterol: 61mg
