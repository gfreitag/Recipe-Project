Title: Pineapple Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Cold milk
2 pk Dream Whip
1 cn Crushed pineapple
1    White cake mix
2 lg Egg whites
Drain pineapple, reserving juice.  Prepare cake mix as directed on
the box, using the reserved pineapple juice and water to make the
necessary amount of water.  Add 1/2 cup of the pineapple to cake mix
and bake in a 13 x 9 inch pan as directed on the box.  Set aside to
cool. Mix and beat until stiff 1 cup of cold milk and 2 packages of
Dream Whip. Fold in remaining pineapple and spread on top of the
cake. Keep cake cold until you're ready to serve. Randy Rigg
