Title: Extra Special Spinach Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 3 cups
10 oz Frozen spinach, thawed and
-squeezed dry
8 oz Water chestnuts; drained
-and chopped
1    Envelope vegetable soup mix
16 oz Sour cream
1/2 c  Mayonaise
1/2 t  Lemon juice
Blend soup mix, sour cream, mayonaise and lemon juice. Stir in
remaining ingredients.  Chill.  Variations: Add 2 T chopped green
onion, radishes or crumbled bleu cheese. Or omit water chestnuts and
add 1 cup chopped apple. Source:  Lipton box
