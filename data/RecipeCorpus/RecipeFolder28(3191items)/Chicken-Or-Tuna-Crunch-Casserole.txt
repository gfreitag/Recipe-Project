Title: Chicken Or Tuna Crunch Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  (3 oz.) chow mein noodles
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
2 1/2 c  Chopped cabbage
1/2    Bag sliced almonds
1 sm Onion, chopped
1    Rib celery, diced
1 cn Tuna or 2 c. cooked chicken
Brown almonds, onion, celery in butter.  Mix all ingredients together.
Reserve 1/2 of noodles to sprinkle on top.  Bake in casserole 25 to 30
minutes at 350 degrees.
