Whipped butter

Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 1:15 1. Preheat oven to 325 F. Spread undrained
pineapple over bottom of a 9 x 13-inch baking dish. Add undrained
cherry filling, distributing evenly. Sprinkle dry cake mix over
fruit; smooth top. Sprinkle pecans and then coconut over cake mix.
Drizzle butter evenly over all.

2. Bake 55 to 60 mins, or until edges are bubbly and top is golden
brown. Serve warm or cold topped with whipped cream.
