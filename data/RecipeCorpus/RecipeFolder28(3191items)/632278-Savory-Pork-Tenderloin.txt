Title: 632278 Savory Pork Tenderloin
Categories: Pork
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
1/8 t  Garlic powder
1/2 lb Pork tenderloin
1 T  Oil
1/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/2 t  Dried rosemary, crushed
Heat oven to 375 degrees.  Brush pork with oil.  In shallow dish
combine all remaining ingredients; blend well.  Coat pork with crumb
mixture; place roasting rack, tucking small end of tenderloin under.
Insert meat thermometer so the bulb reaches the center of the meat.
Bake at 375 degrees for 30-40 minutes or until internal temperature
reaches 165 degrees. 2-3 servings.
