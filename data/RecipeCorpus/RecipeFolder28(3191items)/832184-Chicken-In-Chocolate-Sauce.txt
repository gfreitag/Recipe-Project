Title: 832184 Chicken In Chocolate Sauce
Categories: Chicken, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
2    Cloves garlic
16 oz Can tomato sauce
1/2    To 1 tsp. salt
1 T  Chili powder (minimum)
1 oz Sq. baking chocolate
1/4 t  Pepper
1    Chicken, cut up or
-equivalent (turkey, pork,
-2 pounds)
1/4 c  Oil (Crisco, Wesson, etc.,
-but NOT olive oil)
1 lg Bell pepper
1 lg Onion
Skin chicken and brown lightly in oil.  Remove chicken.  Coarsely chop
peppers and onion, mince garlic, and cook in oil until onion becomes
translucent.  Add tomato sauce and salt.  Add seasonings and
chocolate. Stir until chocolate is melted, add chicken, and simmer on
low heat until chicken is very tender. Remove and discard bones.
Adjust seasonings to taste.  Serve over rice or noodles.
