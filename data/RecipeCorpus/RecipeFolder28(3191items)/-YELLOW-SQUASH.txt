Title:  YELLOW SQUASH
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 c  Butter
-Pepper to taste
2 lb Yellow summer squash,
-sliced
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Water
Combine first 4 ingredients in a large saucepan and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium; cook, uncovered, 15 to 20 minutes or until
squash is tender, stirring frequently. Drain well in a colander;
transfer to a bowl, and use a potato masher to mash coarsely with
butter and pepper.
