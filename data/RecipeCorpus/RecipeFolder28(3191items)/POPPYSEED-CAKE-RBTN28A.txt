Title: POPPYSEED CAKE RBTN28A
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
4 ea EGGS
1 c  SOUR CREAM
1/4 c  POPPYSEED
1 ea * D.H. BUTTER CAKE MIX
1/2 c  SUGAR
3/4 c  OIL
PREHEAT OVEN 350 DEGREES. IN MIXING BOWL PUT IN DUNCAN HINES BUTTER
CAKE MIX, SUGAR, OIL, & EGGS (ADD ONE AT A TIME BEATING BY HAND),FOLD
IN SOUR CREAM, & REST OF INGREDIENTS. GREASE & SPRINKLE BUNDT PAN
WITH SUGAR. BAKE 55 MINUTES.
