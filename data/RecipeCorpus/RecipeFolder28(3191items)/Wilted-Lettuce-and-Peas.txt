Title: Wilted Lettuce and Peas
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Green peas
-fresh or frozen
1 tb Butter
8 c  Iceberg lettuce
-thinly sliced
1/2 ts Sugar
Melt butter in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add lettuce
and cook for 3 minutes or until lettuce wilts, stirring frequently.
Stir in sugar and remaining ingredients; cook for 2 minutes and serve.
