Title: Red Raspberry Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 pt Raspberries
1/2 pt Whipping cream
1 c  Powdered sugar
4 tb Butter
Add butter to powdered sugar and put in a pie shell.  Pat down with
your hands until smooth.  Add berries and top with whipping cream.
Chill for 2 hours before serving. Randy Rigg
