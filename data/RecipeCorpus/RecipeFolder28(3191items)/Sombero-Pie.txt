Title: Sombero Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 t  Pepper
Cornmeal Pastry
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 c  Cornmeal
1/2 t  Salt
1/3 c  Shortening plus 1 T
3 T  Cold water
1/2 lb Ground beef
1/2 lb Ground lean pork
1 lg Onion, sliced
2 1/2 c  Tomato juice
1 1/2 c  Frozen corn
2 T  Chili powder
1 t  Salt
In a large skillet cook and stir the meat and onion until meat is
brown and onion is tender.  Drain off fat.  Stir in remaining
ingredients. Heat to boiling; reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes. Heat
oven to 400 degrees. Prepare pastry. CORNMEAL PASTRY: Measure flour,
cornmeal and salt into bowl.  Cut in shortening thoroughly. Sprinkle
in water, 1 tablespoon at a time, mixing until flour is moistened.
Gather into a ball; roll into rectangle 12 by 8 on lightly floured,
cloth-covered board.  Fold length-wise in half; cut slits on folded
edge. Pour meat mixture into ungreased baking dish, 11-1/2 by 7-1/2
by 1-1/2 inches. Cover with pastry; seal to edges of baking dish.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes.
