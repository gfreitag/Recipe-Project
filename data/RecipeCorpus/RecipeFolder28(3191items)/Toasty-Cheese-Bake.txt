Title: Toasty Cheese Bake
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1    Egg, slightly beaten
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
Dash of pepper
1/8 ts Dry mustard
8 sl White bread
Butter
1/2 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Chopped celery
1 tb Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Salt
Heat your oven to 350 degrees.  Toast the bread; buttering both
sides. Cook and stir meat, onion, celery, prepared mustard and 1/2
tsp. of salt until meat is brown and onion is tender.  Alternate
layers of toast, meat mixture and cheese in a greased baking pan, 9 x
9 x 2 inches. Mix remaining ingredients, pour over layers in the pan.
Sprinkle with paprika. Bake, uncovered, for 30 to 35 minutes. Randy
