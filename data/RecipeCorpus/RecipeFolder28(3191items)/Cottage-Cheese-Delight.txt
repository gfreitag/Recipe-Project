Title: Cottage Cheese Delight
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Size can pineapple; crushed
-(well-drained)
1/2 sm Carton cool whip;
1 pt Sm curd cottage cheese;
1 pk Strawberry flavor jello;
-use dry
Blend in dry Jell-O, little at a time, with cottage cheese.  Add
well-drained.  Fold in Cool whip.  May be used as a salad or dessert.
