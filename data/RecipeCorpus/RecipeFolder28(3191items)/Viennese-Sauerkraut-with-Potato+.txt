Title: Viennese Sauerkraut with Potato+
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
-replaced by water
1/2 ts Caraway seeds
3/4 c  White wine
1 md Potato, peeled and grated
1 md Onion, chopped
2 tb Bacon or pork fat
2 c  Sauerkraut, (16 oz. can)
-drained, rinsed and liquid
Saute onion in fat until tender. Add remaining ingredients except
potato. Simmer, covered, for 30 minutes. Add grated potato and simmer
for another 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
