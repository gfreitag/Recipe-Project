Title: Raisin Penny Pinchers
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 5 dozen
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Oats, old-fashioned; uncookd
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
1 c  Raisins, seedless
1 ts Lemon rind; grated
3/4 c  Butter (or marg.); softened
1 c  Sugar, brown; firmly packed
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Milk, evaporated; undiluted
2 c  Flour, all-purpose
Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs, beating well.  Blend in milk,
flour, and salt.  Add oats, walnuts, raisins, and lemon rind; mix
well. Drop level tablespoonfuls onto greased baking sheets.  Bake at
375 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, May 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
