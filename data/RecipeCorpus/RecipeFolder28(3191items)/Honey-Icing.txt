Title: Honey Icing
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Diced marshmallows
1/4 c  Water
1    Egg white, stiffly beaten
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Honey
1/8 ts Salt
Combine sugar, honey, water, and salt.  Boil to soft ball stage (236
F). Add marshmallows.  Pour slowly, beating constantly, onto egg
white. Beat until thick and creamy.
