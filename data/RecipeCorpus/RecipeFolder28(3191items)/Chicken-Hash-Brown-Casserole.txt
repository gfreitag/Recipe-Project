Title: Chicken-Hash Brown Casserole
Categories: Poultry, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1 c  Frozen Gourmet's Choice Baby
-Carrots, thawed & sliced
1/2 c  Celery, diced
2 c  Frozen Schwan's Diced
-Chicken Meat
1 c  Sour Cream*
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Bread Crumbs
4 sl Haugin's Farm Brand Thick
-Sliced Bacon, crumbled
6    Frozen Gourmet's Choice Hash
-Brown Patties
2 c  Water
2    Chicken flavored Bouillon
-Cubes
1/4 c  Margarine
6 sl Schwan's American Processed
-Cheese
Preheat oven to 350Ã¸F.  Prepare bacon in skillet over medium heat;
drain and crumble.  In large saucepan or stock pot combine potato
patties, water and bouillon cubes.  Cover and place over medium-low
heat until patties are thawed and crumbled.  Add margarine, cheese,
onion, carrots, celery, chicken and bacon; mix well.  Gradually add
sour cream; stirring until well blended.  Stir in salt and pepper.
Pour into a greased 2-qt. casserole dish.  Sprinkle with bread
crumbs. Bake 45 minutes.

Makes 6-8 servings.

NOTE:  *Lite sour cream may be used in place of sour cream.
