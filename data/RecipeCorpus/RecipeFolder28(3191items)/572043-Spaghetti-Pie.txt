Title: 572043 Spaghetti Pie
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
2    Eggs, well beaten
12 oz Ground turkey, veal or
-chicken
1 c  Onion, chopped
1 t  Oregano
4 T  Parmesan cheese
1 c  Cottage cheese
1/4 c  Green pepper, chopped
1/4 c  Or 2 oz. Mozzarella cheese,
-shredded
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1    Jar Weight Watchers
-spaghetti sauce
1 t  Sugar
3 c  (7 oz. box) thin spaghetti,
-cooked
2 T  Margarine
Cook spaghetti.  Stir in margarine into hot spaghetti.  Stir in eggs
and Parmesan cheese.  Pour into a 9 inch pie dish sprayed with Pam.
Spread cottage cheese next.  In a skillet cook turkey, onion and
green pepper until tender and brown.  Drain.  Stir in sauce, sugar,
oregano and garlic. Heat thoroughly, pour over cottage cheese. Bake
in a 350 degree oven for 20 minutes.  Add Mozzarella cheese and bake
until cheese melts (5 to 7 minutes).  Makes 6 servings.
