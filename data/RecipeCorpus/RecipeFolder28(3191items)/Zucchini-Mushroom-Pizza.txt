Title: Zucchini-Mushroom Pizza
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1/2 ts Oregano
1 tb Olive oil
5 tb Warm water
1/4 c  Whole wheat flour
1/4 c  White flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 pn Salt
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
4 c  Zucchini, grated
2 c  Mushrooms, sliced
Salt & pepper
1 tb Olive oil
1 md Onion, sliced
1 ea Garlic clove, chopped
MMMMM-----------------------SPICE MIXTURE----------------------------
1 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Marjoram
1 ts Basil
2 tb Parsley
1/2 ts Black pepper
CRUST: Combine flours, baking powder, salt & oregano.  Mix & add
olive oil. Add water, 1 tb at a time, mixing after each addition. Add
only enough water to enable the dough to hold together.  Knead the
dough for 1 to 2 minutes.  Form into a ball & wrap in a damp cloth.
Let it sit for 30 minutes. TOPPING: Heat olive oil in skillet over
moderate heat. When hot, add onion & garlic & stir fry for 5 minutes.
Add zucchini & mushrooms & saute until the mushrooms soften.  Add
salt & pepper.  Remove from skillet. SPICE MIXTURE: Combine all
ingredients & mix well. TO ASSEMBLE: Roll out dough & brush both
sides with olive oil.  Place the dough on a baking sheet & bake at
350F for 5 minutes.  Arrange the sauteed vegetables on the crust &
top with the spice mixture. Bake at 350F for 8 to 10 minutes. Recipe
by Mark Satterly
