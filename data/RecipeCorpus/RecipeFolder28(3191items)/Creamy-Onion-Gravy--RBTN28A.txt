Title: Creamy Onion Gravy  RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1 pk Campbells soup & recipe mix
2 tb All-purpose flour
2 tb Pan drippings from roast etc
1 1/2 c  Water
1. In 2-qt. saucepan, stir flour into drippings until smooth. Over
medium heat, cook until mixture begins to bubble, stirring
constantly. 2. Gradually stir in water, milk & soup mix. Heat to
boiling. Reduce heat to low; simmer 5 min., stirring occasionally.
Makes about 2-1/4 cups. VARIATIONS: In 2-qt. saucepan, heat 1 soup
mix pouch and 1-1/2 cups water to boiling. Reduce heat to low. Cover;
simmer 8 min. In cup, combine 2 tbsp. flour and 1/2 cup water.
Gradually stir into onion mixture; cook until mixture boils and
thickens, stirring often.
