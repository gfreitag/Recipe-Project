Title: Leftover Rice RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 8 servings
4 c  Cooked rice
3 c  Cooked boned chicken,bite si
1 c  Shredded cheese
2 tb Chopped pimiento
1 c  MUSHROOM SOUP
1 c  CREAM OF CHICKEN SOUP
1/2 c  Soup can of milk
1 ts Onion powder
Mix soups and milk. Heat. Add rest. pour into a casserole and bake at
375f 30 minutes. (Chicken should be bite size). You can also re-heat
the rice (microwave OK) and serve tossed with onions or mushrooms or
green peas or parmesan cheese or cheddar cheese or sour cream and
chopped bacon or any combination of the above ingredients..
