Title: Low-Fat Bananas Foster - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Desserts
Yield: 6 ----------
6 ea Tb (to 8) brown sugar
OR
1/4 c  Brown sugar
AND
1 t  Sweet 'n Low Brown
1/4 c  Banana liqueur
4 ea Bananas
1 ea Or 2 lemons, juice of
6 ea Tb liquid Butter buds
Cinnamon to taste
3 ea Tb light rum
1 1/2 qt Nonfat vanilla frozen yogurt
2 ea Tb Grape Nuts
Slice bananas in half lengthwise; cut into 4" lengths. Brush with
lemon juice. Blend Butter Buds, brown sugar, and liqueur in skillet.
Add bananas. Cook over medium heat for 3 minutes, turning once. Place
in serving dish. Sprinkle with cinnamon. Heat rum to lukewarm in
saucepan. Pour over bananas; ignite. Spoon over frozen yogurt.
Sprinkle Grape Nuts over top if desired. Per sugar serving: 360 cal.,
0.4g fat (1%), 0mg chol., 3g fiber, 9g pro., 77g carb., 285mg sod.
Per S&L serving: 347 cal., 73g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie
