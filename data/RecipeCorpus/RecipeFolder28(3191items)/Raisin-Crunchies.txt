Title: Raisin Crunchies
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 2 1/2 dozen
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
1 c  Raisin bran cereal
1/2 c  Butter (or marg.); softened
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Flour, all-purpose
Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.  Add egg and vanilla;
beat well.  Combine flour, baking powder, and salt.  Add dry
ingredients, nuts, and cereal to creamed mixture, mixing well.

Shape dough into balls about 1" in diameter; place on lightly greased
cookie sheets.  Flatten with fork.  Bake at 400 degrees about 10
minutes.
