Title: Hot Garlic Cheese Loaves
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 mini loave
1/4 t  Ground red pepper
1 1/4 c  Milk
2    Eggs, beaten
3 1/2 c  Buttermilk baking mix
2 1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1 t  Garlic powder
Preheat oven to 350.  In a large bowl, combine baking mix, cheese,
garlic powder, and red pepper.  Add milk and eggs to dry ingredients;
mix only until ingredients are moistened. Spoon batter into 4 greased
and floured 3 1/4 by 6" baking pans.  Bake 30 to 35 minutes. Cool in
pans 5 minutes. Remove from pans and cool completely on a sire rack.
Store in airtight container.  Source:  Quick Gifts of Good Taste.
