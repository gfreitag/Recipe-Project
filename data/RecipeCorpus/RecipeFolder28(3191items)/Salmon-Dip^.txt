Title: Salmon Dip^
Categories: Healthy
Yield: 1 servings
1 ct (2oz) pimentos
1 tb Onion powder
1 1/2 ts Chopped fresh dill
1 ts Paprika
2 c  Lowft cottage cheese
1 c  Lowfat yogurt
2 ts Lowfat yogurt
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 ts Dried dill weed
1 cn (17oz) salmon
1/2 c  Water packed artichoke
-hearts
Place everything in the blender except the 1 cup yogurt and blend
until smooth. Add yogurt and mix well. Serve with brown rice crackers
or toasted pita triangles. Makes about 4 cups.

Variation:

Add 1 (1/4oz) package unflavored gelatin according to package
directions and make a mold.

Per tablespoon: Calories: 21 Protein: 3g Carbohydrates: 1g Fat: 0.5g,
21% Sodium: 35mg Cholesterol: 3mg
