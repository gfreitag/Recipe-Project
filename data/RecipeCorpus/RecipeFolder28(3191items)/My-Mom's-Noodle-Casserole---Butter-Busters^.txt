Title: My Mom's Noodle Casserole - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 12 ----------
3 ea Tb flour
1/2 c  Liquid Butter Buds or
-fatfree chicken broth
Salt and pepper to taste
2 c  Grated nonfat Cheddar cheese
1 ea Pk (12-14oz) cooked and
-drained shells
1 cn (16oz) whole peeled tomatoes
1 cn (15oz) tomato sauce
1 md Onion, chopped
In a sauce pan, saute onions in liquid Butter Buds or broth. Add
peeled tomatoes and juice; break apart with a spoon. Add tomato
sauce. Pour a small amount of the sauce in a bowl. Add flour mixture
to that bowl. When this is thoroughly blended, mix it in with the
other tomato mixture. Add salt and pepper to taste and simmer until
heated. In a 9 1/2 x 13" casserole that has been sprayed with a
nonstick cooking spray, layer sauce, noodles and cheese. Repeat with
cheese on top. Bake at 350F. for 30 minutes. Per serving: 149cal.,
0.6 g fat (4%), 0mg chol., 2g fiber, 7g pro., 29g carb., 390mg sod.
