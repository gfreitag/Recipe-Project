Title: Rujak Manis (Fruit in Soy Sauce)
Categories: Fruit, Asian
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Lime juice
3 c  Assorted fresh fruit
1/4 c  Ketjap manis
1/2 ea Thai chile
1/2 ea Inch Ginger, fresh; peeled
Cut up fruit into bite-sized chunks.  Grind ginger and pepper.  Add
ketjap manis and lemon juice.  Pour over the fruit and mix gently.
Let stand an hour or more before serving.
