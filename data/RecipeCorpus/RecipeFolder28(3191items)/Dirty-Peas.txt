Title: Dirty Peas
Categories: Italian, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Chicken stock or
-- reduced-sodium broth
3 tb Cream
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 tb Butter
1 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1/4 c  Prosciutto or ham, chopped
1 pk (10 oz) peas, frozen
Recipe by: 365 Easy Italian Recipes - ISBN 0-06-016310-0 Preparation
Time: 0:17

1. In a medium frying pan, melt butter with olive oil over medium
heat. Add onion and prosciutto or ham and cook 2 to 3 mins, or until
onion is softened. Add peas and stock, increase heat to medium-high,
and cook uncovered 3 mins.

2. Add cream and cook 3 to 5 mins, until liquid is reduced and peas
begin to collapse and turn brown. Season with salt and pepper.
