Title: Quick Potato Salad For One
Categories: Potato, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Cider vinegar
1 ts Dijon-style mustard
1/4 ts Salt
-Dash pepper
1    Fresh Idaho potato, baked,
-cubed
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Drained chopped
-roasted red pepper or
-pimiento, drained
-(optional)
1 tb Finely chopped onion
In small bowl combine first seven ingredients; mix well. Add
potatoes; toss to coat well. Cover; refrigerate. Makes 1 serving.
From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
