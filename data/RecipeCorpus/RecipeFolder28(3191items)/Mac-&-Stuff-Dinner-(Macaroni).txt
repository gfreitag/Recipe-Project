Title: Mac & Stuff Dinner (Macaroni)
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Kids
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Margarine
2 c  Stove Top Stuffing Mix-
Chicken flavor- in canister
1 pk Mac & Cheese Dinner 7 1/4 oz
1 1/4 c  Water
1 c  Frozen peas, thawed
4 ea Hot Dogs, sliced
Prepare Macaroni & Cheese Dinner as directed on the package.
While it is cooking, in a seperate pan bring water, peas, hot dogs
and margarine to a boil (Large Saucepan).  Stir in Stuffing Mix;
cover. Let stand 5 minutes.
Stir stuffing mixture into Macaroni & Cheese Dinner.  Serve
Immediately.
This is a quick and easy meal to prepare for kids (unless they
are really picky about what they eat <G> ).

Preparation time:  10 min.
Cooking time:  10 min.
:
:

NOTE:  I am assuming the stuffing is prepared without the use of
the stuffing seasoning which comes with it in the canister.
