Title: Beet pickles RBTN28A
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 pints
3 qt Peeled cooked beets about 24    1 1/2 ts Salt
3 1/2 c  Vinegar
1 1/2 c  Water
2 c  Sugar
2 ea Sticks cinnamon
1 tb Whole allspice
Combine all ingredients, except beets, in a large sauce pot. Simmer 15
minutes. Pack beets into hot jars, leaving 1/4 inch head space. Cut
larger beets in half if necessary. Remove cinnamon. Bring pickling
liquid to a boil. Pour hot liquid over beets, leaving 1/2 inch head
space. Remove air bubbles. Adjust caps. Process pints and quarts 30
minutes in boiling water bath.;;
