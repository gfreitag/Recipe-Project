Title: Lobster Newburg No. 1
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Salt
-paprika
-patty shells or toast
-cups
6 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1 1/2 c  Light cream
3    Egg yolks, beaten
1/2 lb Lobster meat  (or more)
3 tb Dry sherry
DIRECTIONS   Melt butter in skillet and blend in flour.  Add cream
all at once. Cook, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens and
bubbles. Stir a small amount of hot mixture into egg yolks, return to
hot mixture; cook, stirring, constantly, until mixture thickens. Add
lobster; heat. Add wine, lemon juice, and salt.  Sprinkle with
paprika. Serve in patty shells or toast cups.

Turn all into a buttered casserole.  Top with a few buttered bread
crumbs and bake at 350f for about 30 minutes or until bubbly and
lightly browned.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
