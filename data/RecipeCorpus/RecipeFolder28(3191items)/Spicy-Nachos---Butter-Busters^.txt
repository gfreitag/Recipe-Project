Title: Spicy Nachos - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Pk Taco seasoning mix
1/2 ea Bn lettuce, chopped
2 lg Tomatoes, chopped
1 ea Pk Smart Temptations Chips*
2 c  Grated nonfat Cheddar Cheese
1 lb Skinless white turkey meat,
-ground
Cook turkey in a waxed papaer covered bowl in the microwave 5 minutes.
Remove and stir. Cook a couple of more minutes if needed. Drain meat
and set aside. Prepare taco mix as directed substituting turkey for
ground beef. Spoon taco mixture over chips. Sprinkle cheese on top.
Heat in microwave for 3 minutes. Sprinkle with lettuce and tomatoes.
Top with hot sauce of desired. Option: cook one package Country Pride
seasoned chicken strips (Teriyaki, Mesquite or Mexcali flavor) in
microwave as directed on package. Use instead of ground turkey or
chicken. You can find these seasoned strips at albertson's and
Kroger. Per serving: 231 cal., 2.5g fat (10%), 38mg chol., 3g fiber,
27g pro., 25g carb., 580mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN
