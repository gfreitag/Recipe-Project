Title: SIZZLIN' BEEF KABOBS
Categories: Beef, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 t  Hot Shot! Glack & Red
-Pepper Blend
1 lb Sirloin steak, cut into 1
-1/2" cubes
Assorted vegetables
3 T  Veg. oil
2 T  Soy sauce
1 T  Red wine vinegar
1 t  California StyleBlend
-Garlic Powder
Combine first 5 ingredients in large self closing plastic bag or
shallow glass dish.  Add steak; toss to coat.  Refrigerte 3 or more
hours.  Spear with veggies on metal skewers. Grill or broil 12-15
minutes, turning frequently. Source: McCormick*/Schilling* recipe
