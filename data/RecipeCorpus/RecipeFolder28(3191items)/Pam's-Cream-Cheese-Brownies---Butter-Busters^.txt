Title: Pam's Cream Cheese Brownies - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 ----------
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
1 t  Vanilla
3/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Hershey's Chocolate syrup
1/2 t  Baking powder
1/4 t  Lite salt, optional
3 ea Tb unsweetened cocoa
Nonfat cooking spray
1/2 c  Liquid Butter Buds or
-Promise Ultra fatfree
-margarine
1/3 c  Fatfree cream cheese
2/3 c  Sugar
OR
1/3 c  Sugar
AND
4 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
MMMMM-----------------CHOC. MARSHMALLOW FROSTING----------------------
1 ea Tb light corn syrup
1/3 c  Water
3/4 ea Marshmallow creme
OR
16 ea Marshmallows
1/4 c  Cocoa
2 ea Egg white
1 1/2 c  Sugar
OR
1 c  Sugar
AND
6 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
1/2 t  Cream of tartar
MMMMM--------------------BROWNIES DIRECTIONS-------------------------
Cream butter buds and cream cheese, gradually adding sugar until
light and fluffy. Add Egg Beaters and vanila. Combine flour, baking
powder and salt. Add to creamed mixture. Beat well. Divide Batter in
half. Sift cocoa over half of batter and fold in gently. Stir in
chocolate syrup. Spoon cocoa mixture into a 9" square pan that has
been sprayed with a nonfat coking spray. Pour remaining batter into
pan. Cut through mixture with a knife to create a marbled effect.
Bake at 350F. for 25 minutes or until wooden toothpick inserted in
center comes out clean. Cool brownies on a wire rack. Frost.
Frosting: Combine egg whites, sugar, cream of tartar, syrup and water
in top of a double boiler. Place over boiling water; beat with a
rotary beater until stiff peaks form, scraping pan occasionally.
Remove from heat. Add sifted cocoa and fluff or marshmallos and
continue beating until of spreading cocsistency. Spread over cooled
brownies. Per sugar serving: 130 cal., 0.4g fat (3%), 0mg chol., 0g
fiber, 2g pro., 31g carb., 83mg sod Per S&L serving: 105 cal., 24g
carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by
