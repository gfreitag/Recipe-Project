Title: Australian Crock-Pot Chop Suey
Categories: Down under, Main dish, Beef
Yield: 1 servings
2    Onions;
1 pk Chicken noodle soup;
2 ts Curry powder;
2 c  Water;
Salt & pepper;
1 lb Ground beef;
3    Stalks celery;
1/2    Head cabbage;
3    Carrots;
1    Green pepper;
1 cn Green beans;
Brown beef and onions in a pan.  Chop vegetables in small pieces.  Mix
soup, water, curry powder and salt and pepper.  Turn all into into
crock-pot and simmer for 4 to 5 hours. Source: By Dulcuie McVicker,
Cherished Recipes, Olde St. John's Church, Colliers, West Virginia.
