2 ea Crisp lettuce cups
1 c  Sliced radishes
2 ea Deviled eggs
Mayonnaise
1 c  Cooked or canned crab or
-lobster meat
1 t  Lemon juice
1 t  Grated onion
1/4 t  Celery seeds
Combine first 4 ingredients; arrange in 2 lettuce cups; alongside each
mound of crab meat, heap half of radishes. Place cups, side by side,
on attractive small tray, garnish with deviled eggs; add small bowl of
dressing. Add tiny baking-powder biscuits. Makes 2 servings
CRAB-AND-DEVILED-EGG SALAD. Toss first 4 ingredients with radishes (or
celery), 2 tablesp. mayonnaise. Serve as above. Double recipe if
desired.
