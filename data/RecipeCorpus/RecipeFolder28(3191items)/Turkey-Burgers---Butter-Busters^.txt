Title: Turkey Burgers - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 8 ----------
1 ea Pk dried onion soup mix
1/4 c  (to1/2) BBQ sauce
8 ea Sl Kraft or Borden fatfree
-cheese
8 ea Wonder fatfree buns
3 lb Fresh ground white meat
-turkey, without skin
3/4 c  Peeled, grated zucchini
2 cn (8oz) sliced mushrooms,
-chopped finely, rinsed and
-drained
Combine the turkey, zucchini, mushrooms and onion soup mix in a bowl.
Form into paties. Place patties in the refrigerator for about 30
minutes before cooking. Grill outside on outdoor or on a Jen-Aire (we
ALL have one of these, don't we????) grill inside for best results.
Baste patties with BBQ sauce while cooking. cook about 5-7 minutes on
each side. Place a slice of fatfree cheese on each patty for the last
minute of cooking time. Serve on buns with lettuce, tomato and any
other garnish you desire. If using mayonnaise, be sure and use the
Kraft fatfree variety. Per serving: 325 cal., 2.5g fat (7%), 104mg
chol., 3g fiber., 52g pro., 18g carb., 749mg sod. Butter Busters by
Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 2-95.
