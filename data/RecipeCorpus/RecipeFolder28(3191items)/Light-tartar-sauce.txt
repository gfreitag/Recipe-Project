Title: Light tartar sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 10 SERVINGS--------------------------
1 t  Prepared horseradish
1 t  Dried parsley flakes
1 ea Dr Hot pepper sauce
1/2 c  Plain lowfat yogurt
1 T  Sweet pickle relish
2 t  Minced onion
In small bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well.  Cover; refrigerate
2 to 3 hours to blend flaveors before using.  Stir before servings.
Makes 2/3 cup. Per 1 Tablespoon: 10 cal, 1 g protein, 1 g carbo, 0 g
fat, 1 mg chol, 20 mg sodium Source: Pillsbury Most Requested Recipes
(chicken & fish), v.2,no.2,p.38 typed by Charlotte Welch
