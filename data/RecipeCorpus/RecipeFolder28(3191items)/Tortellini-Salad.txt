Title: Tortellini Salad
Categories: Tex/mexican, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Dried basil leaves
2 c  Bite-size pieces spinach
1/4 c  Pine nuts or slivered almond
-Toasted
1 md Bell pepper, cut into strips
1 pk (7 ounces) dried
-Cheese-filled tortellini
1/4 c  Olive or vegetable oil
1/4 c  Wine vinegar
2 tb Grated parmesan cheese
1 tb Chopped fresh or
Cook tortellini as directed on package. Rinse in cold water and drain.
Shake oil, vinegar, cheese and basil in tightly covered container.
Pour over tortellini. Cover and refrigerate about 2 hours or until
chilled. Toss with remaining ingredients. Serve with freshly ground
pepper and additional Parmesan cheese if desired. 4 SERVINGS (1-1/4
CUPS EACH); 250 CALORIES PER SERVING.
