Title: Cucumber Salad  RBTN28A
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Apple cider vinegar
Salt & Pepper
2 ea Cucumbers
1 ea Onion, sliced
1/2 c  Sugar
Peel & thinly slice 2 to 3 Cucumbers, generously sprinkle with salt
and let set for 30 to 45 minutes. Drain cucumbers, add onions and
apple cider(1 to 1 1/2 cups) and sugar, mix thoroughly and add pepper
for taste.
