Title: Citrus Dipping Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Each grated orange and
-lemon peel
1/2 c  Orange juice
3 tb Honey
2 tb Lemon juice
2 ts . cornstarch
Whisk together all ingredients in small saucepan. Bring mixture to a
boil, stirring frequently, until sauce thickens. Serve warm.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
