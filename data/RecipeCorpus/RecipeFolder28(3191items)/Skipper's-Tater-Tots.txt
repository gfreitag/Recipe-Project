Title: Skipper's Tater Tots
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Milk
1 T  Margarine
1/4 c  Green pepper; finely chopped    1 1/2 t  Worcestershire sauce
6 1/2 oz Tuna
1 c  Peas; partially thawed
2 c  Tater tots
2 T  Onions, chopped
4 t  Flour
1/8 t  Dill seed
2 1/2 oz Mushrooms; sliced
In 8" round microwave dish combine first three ingredients. Cook,
covered, on high for 4 to 5 minutes. Stir in flour, dill seed, 1/2 t
salt and dash of pepper.  Drain mushrooms; reserve liquid. Stir
reserved liquid, milk, worcestershire sauce into baking dish. Cook,
uncovered, on high 11 to 12 minutes, giving dish a half turn after 6
minutes.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
