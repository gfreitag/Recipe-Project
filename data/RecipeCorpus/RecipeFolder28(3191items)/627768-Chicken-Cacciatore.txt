Title: 627768 Chicken Cacciatore
Categories: Italian, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 T  Parsley flakes
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Red wine
-Parmesan cheese
-Approximately 1/2 to 1 c.
-tomato juice
1    Or 2 bay leaves
4    Chicken breasts
-Olive oil Pam
1    (16 oz.) can stewed
-tomatoes
1/2 t  Oregano or basil
1/4 t  Garlic powder
1 t  Salt
1 t  Sugar
1 T  Flour
Remove skin from chicken; wash and dry.  Spray skillet with Pam and
brown chicken until golden.  Remove chicken from skillet and place in
baking dish.  Blend all remaining ingredients (except Parmesan
cheese) in skillet and simmer for 5 minutes.  Pour over chicken
breasts; cover and bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees.  Sprinkle with
Parmesan cheese before serving. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
03-02-95
