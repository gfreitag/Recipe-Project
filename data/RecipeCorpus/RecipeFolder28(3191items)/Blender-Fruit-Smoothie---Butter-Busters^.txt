Title: Blender Fruit Smoothie - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Beverages
Yield: 16 ----------
2 cn (6oz) pineapple-orange
-juice, undiluted
1 cn (6oz) limeade, undiluted
1 cn (6oz) lemonade, undiluted
4 ea Ct (8oz) frozen
-strawberries, partially
-thawed
5 ea Bananas, cut up
Blend in blender until smooth. Serve in tall chilled galsses. Garnis
with an orange slice on the side of the glass. Per sugar serving: 128
cal., 0.5g fat (3%), 0mg chol., 3.5g faiber, 1.5g pro., 32g carb.,
2.5mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered
