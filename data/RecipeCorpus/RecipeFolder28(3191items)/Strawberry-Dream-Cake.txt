Title: Strawberry Dream Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
10 oz Strawberry pop
Dream Whip
1    White cake mix
6 oz Strawberry Jello
1 c  Boiling water
Prepare and bake white cake as directed, using a 9 x 13 inch pan.
After cake is baked, punch holes all over the cake with a straw or
fork. Mix Jello, boiling water and strawberry pop together and pour
over the cake. Cool cake, then spread with Dream Whip. Randy Rigg
