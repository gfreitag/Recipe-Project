Title: Simple Sangria - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Beverages
Yield: 6 ----------
1 ea Lime, sliced
12 ea Maraschino cherries
1 qt Red wine, optional
1 qt Cranberry cocktail
1 c  Grape juice
1 ea Lemon, sliced
1 ea Orange, sliced
Combine juices. Add wine if desire. Place one slice of each fruit, 2
cherries and cracked ice to fill in each of 6 glasses. Pour juice
mixtre over and serve immediately. Makes 12 servings if using wine.
Per wine serving: 157 cal., 0.2g fat (1%), 0mg chol., 1g fiber, 1g
pro., 40g carb., 9mg sod. Per no wine serving: 132 cal., 0.1g fat
(1%), 0mg chol., 0.4g fiber, 1g pro., 20g carb., 12mg sod. Butter
Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw
3-95.
