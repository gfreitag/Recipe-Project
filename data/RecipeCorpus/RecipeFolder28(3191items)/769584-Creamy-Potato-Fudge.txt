Title: 769584 Creamy Potato Fudge
Categories: Desserts, Snacks, Chocolate, Vegetables
Yield: 16 servings
4    To 5 cups icing sugar
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
-Walnuts, almonds or pecans
1 md Potato
5 oz Semi sweet chocolate
1/4 c  Butter
1 t  Vanilla
Boil potato until cooked.  Peel and mash.  Measure 2/3 cup mashed
potatoes. In a saucepan over low heat or in microwave proof dish in
microwave oven on low, melt together butter and chocolate.  Combine
with hot potatoes, salt and vanilla.  Blend well.  Stir in icing
sugar until smooth and firm. Mix in nuts.  Spread into greased 9"x9"
pan. Cool and cut into squares. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
03-04-95
