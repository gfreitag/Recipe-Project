Title: Florida Shrimp A La King
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
-points
3 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Chopped green pepper
3 tb All-purpose flour
3/4 lb Cooked, deveined shrimp
1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1 1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Chopped pimiento
-patty shells or toast
Thaw shrimp if frozen.  In a large skillet, saute mushrooms and green
pepper in butter until tender.  Blend in flour, salt and cayenne
pepper. Gradually add milk.  Cook until thickened, stirring
constantly. Stir in pimiento and shrimp.  Heat.  Serve in patty
shells or on toast points.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
