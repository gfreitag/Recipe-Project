Title: Season Salt for meat, veggie, or soup
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 ts Coarse salt
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Thyme
1/8 ts Black pepper
2 ts Coarse salt
3/4 ts Dried parsley flakes
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Sage
1/4 ts Marjoram
1/4 ts Paprika
Or try the following
Pick desired combination of seasonings and blend well.  Store tightly
covered.  For use with meats, soups, and vegetables.
