Title: 523683 Chocolate Covered Fruit Balls
Categories: Snacks, Fruit, Chocolate
Yield: 24 servings
1 c  Chocolate bits
1 c  Coconut
1 c  Maraschino cherries,
-chopped
6 T  Orange juice
1 c  Peanut butter
2 c  Powdered sugar
2 c  Chopped nuts
2 c  Chopped dates
2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1 c  Raisins
Mix all ingredients.  Make into balls the size of walnuts. Refrigerate
overnight.  Coat balls with 2 pounds melted chocolate. Place balls on
wax paper.  Refrigerate until set.  Keep cold.  Makes approximately 8
dozen. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-03-95
