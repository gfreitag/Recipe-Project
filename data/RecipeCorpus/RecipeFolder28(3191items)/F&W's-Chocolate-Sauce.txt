Title: F&W's Chocolate Sauce
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 3 servings
MAKES 3/4 CUP
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: CHOCOLATE DESSERTS,---------------------
1/4 c  Strong, freshly brewed
-coffee
2 T  Unsalted butter, cut into
-small pieces
Supplement to Food & Wine
-magazine, 03/95
4 ea Ounces semisweet chocolate
2 T  Light cream
1. In a double boiler, melt the chocolate in the light cream and
coffee over hot, not simmering, water, stirring until smooth. 2. Add
the butter and stir until it is thoroughly incorporated. 3. Serve
warm; or cover tightly with plastic wrap and store in the
refrigerator for up to 3 days. To rewarm, stir in a double boiler
over hot water.
