Title: Oven Barbecued Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Healthy
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 tb Onion; optional
1    2 1/2-3 lb frying chicken;
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Vinegar
3 tb Oil
1/2 c  Chili sauce or catsup
Recipe by: 1975 American Heart Association Brochure Preheat oven to
350 F. Combine all ingredients except the chicken in a saucepan;
simmer 10 mins. Wash and dry chicken and place in a large baking pan.
Pour half of the barbecue sauce over chicken and bake, uncovered, for
50-60 mins; basting with remaining sauce every 15 mins. Or, chicken
may be immersed in sauce, then removed and cooked over charcoal,
basting frequently. Yield: 4 servings. Approx. 250 cal/serving
