Title: Sesame Seed Powder
Categories: Herbs/spice, Indian
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
1 ts Asafoetida
4 ts Oil
Salt
4 oz Sesame seeds
1/2 c  Copra
-grated or shredded
10    Dried red chilies
Fry sesame seeds, copra, chilies and asafoetida in oil for a few
minutes until lightly browned. Grind to a coarse powder, adding salt
to taste. Store in an airtight container. Serve with rice and gee.

Note: I don't know what copra is.
