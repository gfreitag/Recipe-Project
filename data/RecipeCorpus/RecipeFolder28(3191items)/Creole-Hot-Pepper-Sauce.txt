Title: Creole Hot Pepper Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Condiments, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Salt
2 tb Pepper
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice (optional)
5 qt Vinegar
5 qt Hot red peppers; washed
3 lb Onion; peeled, quartered
3    Whole garlic buds; peeled,
-chopped
Combine 4 quarts vinegar, peppers, onions, and garlic in a large Dutch
oven; bring to a boil.  Cook over low heat until vegetables are
tender, about 1 hour.  Drain vegetables, reserving liquid.  Place
vegetables in blender; blend until chopped.  Press vegetables through
a rice strainer and discard seeds.

Combine pureed vegetables, reserved vegetable liquid, and remaining
ingredients in a large Dutch oven.  Cook until thickened over medium
heat. Let mixture simmer 30 minutes.

Quickly pour into sterilized jars, leaving 1/8-inch headspace.  Cover
at once with metal lids and screw metal band tight.  Process in
boiling water bath for 10 minutes. ~Mrs. Floyd Zaunbrecher, Vermilion
Parish (Gueydan)
