Title: 286847 Pierogi Casserole
Categories: East europe, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
-Longhorn (your favorite)
2 1/2    Sticks butter or margarine
2    Onions, finely diced
1 lb Lasagna noodles, cooked
8    10 potatoes, boiled and
-mashed
3/4 lb Grated American cheese or
-Cheddar, or Colby, or
Preparation :

Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.  Mash potatoes;
add cheese to potatoes and beat with a mixer.  Saute onions in butter
until tender.  Add 1/3 of onions and butter to potato mixture and
salt to taste. Generously butter 9 x 13 inch baking dish. Place a
layer of noodles in dish, then a layer of potato mixture. Repeat
ending with a layer of noodles.  Pour remaining butter and onions on
top. Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees until heated through.
