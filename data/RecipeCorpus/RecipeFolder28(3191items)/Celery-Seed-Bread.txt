Title: Celery Seed Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Dried parsley flakes
4    Hot dog rolls, sliced
6 tb Butter or margarine,
-softened
1/2 ts Celery seed
In a mixing bowl, combine butter, celery seed, paprika and parsley
flakes, stirring well to blend. Spread on cut sides of each roll.
Place on baking sheet; broil until golden brown.

Yield: servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
