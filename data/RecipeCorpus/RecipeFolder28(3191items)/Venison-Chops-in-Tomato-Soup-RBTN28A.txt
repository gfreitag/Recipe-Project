Title: Venison Chops in Tomato Soup RBTN28A
Categories: Alcohol, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ea Green pepper, chopped
1/2 ea Med. onion
1 1/3 c  Minute rice
8 ea Chops
1 x  Salt & Pepper
1 x  Garlic powder
1 cn Tomato soup
Put salt, pepper & garlic powder on chops.  Brown in oil in electric
skillet.  Pour tomato soup in skillet.
Add green peppers & onions. Simmer 1 or 1 1/2 hrs.
Turn skillet off & add minute rice.  Let stand until sauce is
absorbed.
