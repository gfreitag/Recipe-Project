Title: Skillet Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Rice Krispies
1/2 c  Nuts
1/2 ts Vanilla
Coconut
1    Stick of margarine
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Chopped dates
1    Egg, beaten
Melt margarine in a large skillet; add brown sugar, dates and egg.
Stir well and boil for 5 minutes.  Remove from heat; add Rice
Krispies, nuts and vanilla.  Stir well.  Spoon on wax paper.  Shape
into balls and roll in coconut. Randy Rigg
