Title: Chocolate Lover's Snack
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 8 cups
6 oz M & M's
4 1/2 oz Chocolate covered peanuts
10 1/2 oz Mini Chips Ahoy cookies
7 1/2 oz Mini Oreos
In large bowl, combine all ingredients.  Store in airtight container.
