Title: Kidney Bean Hamburger
Categories: Beef, 1941
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/3 c  Cooking fat
Salt and pepper
2 lb Round, or flank, ground
1    Onion, chopped
2 c  Canned kidney beans
2 tb Catsup
Prize winning recipe Brown meat in cooking fat. Stir to prevent
sticking. Add onion, catsup, and Worcestershire sauce. Cover with hot
water. Simmer 20 minutes.  Add beans.  Season to taste.  Simmer 20
minutes. Serve hot. If desired, chili powder may be added.  8
servings.

Mrs. H.H. Darland, Cogswell, N.D.
