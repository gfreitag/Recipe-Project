Title: Quick 'N Chewy Crescent Bars (1972)
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 48 bars
8 oz Can Pillsbury Refrigerated
Crescent Dinner Rolls
14 oz Can sweetened condensed
Milk (NOT evaporated)
1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Coconut
3/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1/4 c  Butter
1972 Pillsbury Bake Off Winner

Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup;
level off.  In medium bowl, combine flour, coconut, brown sugar and
pecans. Using pastry blender or fork, cut in butter until mixture
resembles coarse crumbs.  Set aside.

Unroll dough into 2 long rectangles.  Place in ungreased 15 x 10 x 1
inch baking pan; gently press dough to cover bottom of pan. Firmly
press perforations to seal.  Pour condensed milk evenly over dough to
within 1/2 inch of edges.  Sprinkle coconut mixture over condensed
milk; press in lightly.

Bake at 400 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes or until deep golden brown.
Cool. Cut into bars.
