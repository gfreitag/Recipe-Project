Title: No-Fat French-Fries from Oprah's Chef Rosie Daley*
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 lg Egg whites
1 tb Cajun spice
5 lg Baking potatoes
Vegetable oil cooking spray
Slice potatoes lengthwise into 1/4" thick ovals; then into
matchsticks. Spritz baking sheet with 3 sprays of the vegie spray.

Combine egg whites and spice lightly with a whisk. Add potatoes and
stir to coat. Pour on sheet and spread as best you can.

Place on bottom shelf of stove and bake for 45 mintues at at (this is
a guess, as it didn't state) 350 degrees F.
