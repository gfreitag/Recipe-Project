Title: 635067 Lynn's Pork Tenderloin
Categories: Pork
Yield: 2 servings
-Morton's Nature seasoning
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1/2    To 3/4 c. milk
-Mozzarella cheese, shredded
-(as desired)
1    Pork tenderloin, in 1 inch
-chunks
1    Egg
1/2 c  Ritz cracker crumbs
1/2 c  Corn Flakes, crushed
1/2 c  Flour
Pound meat flat; dip into beaten egg mixed with 1 tablespoon milk,
then dip into mixture of crackers, Corn Flakes and flour.  Fry until
brown. Season with Nature seasoning.  Arrange in baking dish.
Sprinkle with Mozzarella cheese.  Pour over soups and milk.  Bake
covered for one hour at 350 degrees.  Can put under broiler
(uncovered) to brown for 5 minutes if desired.
