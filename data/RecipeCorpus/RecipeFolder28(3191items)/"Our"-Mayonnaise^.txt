Title: "Our" Mayonnaise^
Categories: Healthy
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Tb fresh lemon juice
1 t  No salt added Dijon Mustard
3/4 t  Onion powder
1 t  Low sodium soy sauce
1 1/2 c  Lowfat cottage cheese,
-drained
1 ea Tb apple juice concentrate
2 ea Tb apple cider vinegar
1 ea Egg white
Place all ingredients in a food procesor fitted with the metal blade.
Process 4 Pour into a glass container, cover and refrigerate
overnight. Makes 2 1/2 cups. Variations: Season with curry powder,
fresh minced garlic, grated horseradish, crushed pine Per tablespoon:
