Title: TUPPERWARE BOWL BREAD "BILL'S" RBTN28A
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  SUGAR
2 ts Salt
2 ea STICKS OLEO
1 1/2 c  MILK, SCALDED
1 1/2 c  WATER (TO COOL)
4 ea EGGS
2 pk YEAST
Scald milk and add water to cool. Add eggs, yeast, sugar and salt.
Mix all ingredients with a mixer. Add 9 cups flour. Make a pond in
flour and add liquid mixture. Do not mix! Place lid on bowl. When lid
breaks seal, add 2 sticks oleo. Mix flour and liquid and reseal until
lid breaks seal again. Put in 3 loaf pans or make cinnamon rolls.
Bake bread at 350F for 1 hour. Bake cinnamon rolls for 10 to 15
minutes..... Enid State School Employee Association, Cook book
(DESIGNS FO COOKING).
