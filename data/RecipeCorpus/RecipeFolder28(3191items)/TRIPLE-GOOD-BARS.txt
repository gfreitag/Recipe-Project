Title: TRIPLE GOOD BARS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Condensed milk(14oz)
1 tb Lemon juice
1 1/3 c  Brown sugar, packed
2 1/2 c  Oats, uncooked
1 1/2 c  Walnuts, chopped
1/4 ts Salt(optional)
2 c  Raisins
1 tb Grated lemon peel
1 c  Butter or margarine
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Grease a 13 x 9 inch baking pan. In a medium
saucepan, combine raisins, milk, lemon peel and lemon juice. Cook over
medium heat, stirring constantly, just until mixture begins to bubble.
Remove from heat; cool slightly; set aside.
Beat butter or margarine, sugar and vanilla until fluffy. Add
remaining ingredients; blend until evenly mixed and crumbly. Reserve
2 cups. Press remaining mixture onto bottom of greased pan. Spread
raisin mixture to within 1/2 inch of pan. Sprinkle with remaining oat
mixture; pat lightly. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown.
Cool completely. Cut into 1 x 2 inch bars. Makes about 4 dozen.
