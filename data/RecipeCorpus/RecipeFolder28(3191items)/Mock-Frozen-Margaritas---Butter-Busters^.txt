Title: Mock Frozen Margaritas - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Beverages
Yield: 6 ----------
3 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
2 ea Egg whites
2 c  Soda water
2 c  (to 3) ice cubes
6 ea Sl lime
1 ea Coarse salt for glass rims,
-optional
1 cn (6oz) frozen lemonade,
-partially thawed
1 cn (6oz) frozen limeade,
-partially thawed
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
OR
1/4 c  Powdered sugar
AND
Place lemonade, limeade, powdered sugar and egg white in a blender.
Blend until smooth and frothy. Add cracked ice gradually to thicken.
Add enough club soda to cut tartness to your taste. Pour into
prepared glasses (rimmed with salt if desired). this is done by going
around the rim with a lime wedge and dipping into salt. Per sugar
serving: 110 cal., 0.1g fat (1%), 0mg chol., 0g fiber, 1g pro., 28g
carb., 27mg sod. Per S&L serving: 97 cal., 24g carb. Butter Busters
by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 3-95.
