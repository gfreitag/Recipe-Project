Title: Sloppy Joe Casserole - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 9 ----------
1 cn (8oz) tomato sauce
1 c  Water
1 cn (16oz) nonfat cottage or
-ricotta cheese
1 c  Grated nonfat Cheddar
1 ea Pk (8oz) shell macaroni
1 ea Pk (11/2oz) French's sloppy
-joe mix
1/2 lb Ground white meat skinless
-turkey, cooked and drained
1 cn (6oz) tomato paste
Preheat oven to 350F. Cook macaroni as package directs; drain.
Meanwhile, prepare seasoning mix with ground turkey, tomato paste,
tomato sauce and water. In a 2 1/2-qt. casserole that has been
sprayed with a nonfat cooking spray, layer half the macaroni, half
the cottage cheese and half the meat sauce; repeat. top with grated
cheese. Bake uncovered 40-50 minutes or until bubbling. Per serving:
200 cal., 1.1g fat (5%), 18mg chol., 2g fiber, 19g pro., 29g carb.,
676mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered
by Carolyn Shaw 2-95.
