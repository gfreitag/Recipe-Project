Title: 630097 Herbed Pork Tenderloin
Categories: Pork
Yield: 2 servings
1 t  Hot Hungarian paprika
-Salt to taste
1 lb Lean line pork tenderloin
2 T  Fresh marjoram leaves
Preparation : Place pork onto roasting rack inside glass 8 x 13 inch
glass baking dish.  Sprinkle on marjoram and paprika.  Cover pan with
aluminum foil and bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes.  Remove foil,
cover and continue baking 20 to 30 minutes longer, until tenderloin
is lightly browned without becoming too dry.  Salt to taste, slice
and serve promptly. Serves 2 grown ups and 2 small children.
