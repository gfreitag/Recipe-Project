Title: Pecan Pie  RBTN28A
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Pecans
1 ea Unbaked (9") pie shell
3 ea Eggs
1 c  Karo corn syrup light/dark
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Mazola margarine melted
In large bowl stir first 5 ingredients until well blended . Stir in
pecans. Pour into pie shell. Bake in 350F oven 50 to 55 min. or until
knife inserted halfway between center and edge comes out clean. Cool.
Serves 8
