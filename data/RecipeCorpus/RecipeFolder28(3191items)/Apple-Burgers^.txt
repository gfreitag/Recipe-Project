Title: Apple Burgers^
Categories: Healthy, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Pepper
1 ts Low sodium soy sauce
1 lb Lean flank steak, ground
1 c  Whole wheat bread crumbs
1 1/2 c  Unsweetened applesauce
1 sm Onion, grated
MMMMM-------------------------MIXED WITH------------------------------
2 ts Water
Preheat oven to 350F. Combine all ingredients in a medium size bowl.
Spray 6 muffin cups with Pam. Divide meat among muffin cups. Bake 25
minutes or until cooked through.

Calories: 196 Protein: 19g Carbohydrates: 18g Fat: 4.5g Sodium: 178mg
Cholesterol: 47mg
