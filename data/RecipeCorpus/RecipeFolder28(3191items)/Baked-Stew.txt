Title: Baked Stew
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
1 pk Onion soup mix
1 c  Stewed tomatoes
3 tb Tapioca
6    Potatoes, cut up
6    Carrots, cut up
1 c  Celery
2 lb Stew meat
Mix all ingredients in a large casserole.  Cover and bake at 250
degrees for 5 hours. Randy Rigg
