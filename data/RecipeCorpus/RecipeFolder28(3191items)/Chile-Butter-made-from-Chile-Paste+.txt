Title: Chile Butter made from Chile Paste+
Categories: Condiments, Chilies
Yield: 4 ounces
1    Lime, zest only
1    Lime, juice only
3 tb Dried chile paste
1/2 c  Butter, softened, unsalted
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Fresh Cilantro
This is good on corn on the cob, warm tortillas, or just on a slice
of good bread.

In a samll bowl, work the paste together with the butter. Add the
lime zest and the lime juice. Turn into a small crock and garnish
with the cilantro.
