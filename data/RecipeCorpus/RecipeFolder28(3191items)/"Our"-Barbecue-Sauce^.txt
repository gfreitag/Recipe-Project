Categories: Healthy
Yield: 1 servings
-seasoned with chili powder
1/4 t  Paprika
1 t  Dijon Mustard
2 t  Garlic powder
Saute onion in pan sprayed
-with Pam.
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Water
2 ea Tb vinegar
1 ea Tb Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
2 ea Tb brown rice syrup
1 c  No salt added ketchup

chicken or b Variation: For those who prefer more tang, add 1
