Title: Rice and Corn Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetarian
Yield: 20 servings
1/4    Bell pepper; chopped
-- (red or green)
10    Water chestnuts; chopped
6 oz Spanish green olives
-- chopped
1 tb Minced parsley
4 c  Cold cooked rice
2 c  Frozen corn; thawed
-- drained
4    Scallions; sliced
- white part & some of green
2    Celery stalks; chopped
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
Black pepper to taste
1/4 ts Dried thyme; crumbled
1/4 c  Mild vegetable oil
1/4 c  Mild vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
In a large bowl, toss together all ingredients except dressing.

In another bowl, whisk dressing ingredients together until well
blended. Pour over rice mixture and toss gently to distribute
dressing evenly.
