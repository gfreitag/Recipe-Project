Title: Lazy Day Cobbler
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 cn Unsweetened cherries
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Stick of margarine
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Flour
3/4 c  Milnot
Place margarine in a deep dish, or 8 x 8 inch pan in the oven to
melt. Beat together sugar, flour, milk, baking powder and salt, to a
smooth batter. Pour over melted butter, do not stir.  Empty cherries
and include the juice over batter and sprinle with 1/2 cup of sugar.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes. Randy Rigg
