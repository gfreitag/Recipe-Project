Title: Mrybova Pidlyva (Mushroom Gravy)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Russian, Holiday
Yield: 10 servings
1 c  Half and half
1 tb Cornstarch; (optional)
Salt and pepper
1 c  Nachynka z hrybiv
-- (mushroom filling)
1 c  Stock
1 c  Light cream; or
Heat the stock in a saucepan and add the mushroom mixture, bringing
the mixture to a boil.  Add the cream and heat through.  Season to
taste.

NOTE: For a thicker gravy, add the cornstarch to a little of the
cream and then   add to the hot gravy.

Use this gravy with cabbage rolls and roasts.  It is also very good
over toast points.
