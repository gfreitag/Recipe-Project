Title: Pineapple Custard Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Pineapple - crushed
1 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
Boil water and sugar together. Add yolks, cornstarch and pineapple.
Pour in baked shell, cover with whites of 2 eggs, brown.

Source: Mrs. Wade Clark, Pleasent Valley Grange, Columbiana County, OH
