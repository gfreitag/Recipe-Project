Title: Clear Mushroom Soup^
Categories: Healthy, Soup/stew
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Dry sherry
1 ts Light miso
3 c  Chicken stock
6    Fresh mushrooms, sliced
3    Green onions, diced
Heat stock, add mushrooms and onions and simmer 3-4 minutes just to
wilt vegetables. Add sherry. Mix 3 tablespoons of the broth with miso
to blend. Add miso to soup mixture. cok 3 minutes; serve hot. Makes 3
servings.

Calories: 4 Protein: 4g Carbohydrates: 4g Fat: trace Sodium: 276mg
Cholesterol: 0mg
