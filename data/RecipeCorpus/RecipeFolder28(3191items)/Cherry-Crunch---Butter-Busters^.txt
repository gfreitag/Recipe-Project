Title: Cherry Crunch - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Desserts
Yield: 16 ----------
2 c  Cornflakes cereal
1/2 t  Almond extract
1 cn )21oz) cherry pie fillinf,
-regular or lite
1 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low, if using
-lite pie filling
1 t  Almond extract, if using
-lite pie filling
1 qt Nonfat frozen vanila yogurt
1/3 c  Light brown sugar
OR
3 ea Tb brown sugar
AND
3/4 t  Sweet 'n Low Brown
1/4 c  Liquidbutter buds or
-Promise Ultra fatfree
-margarine
1 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Nutmeg
Preheat oven to 375F. In a medium bowl, combine sugar, Butter Buds,
cinnamon and nutmeg. Blend until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Crush cornflakes slightly. With a fork, mix into sugar mixture.
Combine almond extract and pie filling. Turn into a shallow 1-qt.
baking dish that has been sprayed with Pam. Top with corng\flake
mixture, spreading evenly. Bake 20 minutes or until top is golden and
filling is bubbly. cool 2-30 minutes. Serve warm, topping each piece
with a scoop of frozen yogurt. Per sugar serving: 105 cal., 0.1g fat
(1%), 0mg chol., 0g fiber, 2g pro., 23g carb., 106mg sod. Per S&L and
lite pie filling serving: 84 cal., 17g carb. Butter Busters by Pam
