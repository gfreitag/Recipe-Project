Title: Calamari Piquante
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 3 servings
1 ea Small red pepper
1 tb Bread or Cracker crumbs
1 tb Margarine
1 tb Parsley, minced
1 lb Squid (Calamari steaks)
1/2 c  Oil
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 ts Salt
Clean squid and cut into 1 inch squares, slice tentacles. Or use
pre-cleaned Calamari steaks cut into 1 inch squares.  Brown garlic in
oil, add squid, salt and pepper.  Cover and cook over high heat until
liquid is evaporated.  Add crumbs, margarine and parsley.  Cook 2 min
more, stirring well to keep from sticking.

Adapted by Dorothy Flatman from recipes on package of calamari steaks.

Note: Very good served with plain white rice.
