Title: Simple Turkey Meat loaf - Butter busters^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 10 ----------
3/4 c  Ketchup
1 t  Accent
1 cn (8oz) tomato sauce
1 ea Pk Lipton's onion soup mix
1 lb Skinless white turkey meat,
-ground
1/2 c  Egg Beaters
1 1/2 c  SnackWell's Wheat crackers,
-crushed
Mix all ingredients except tomato sauce thoroughly. Place in a loaf
pan that has been sprayed with Pam. Pour tomato sauce over and bake 1
hour at 350F. Per serving: 112 cal., 0.7g fat (6%), 27mg chol., 1g
fiber, 14g fiber, 11g carb., 435mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam
Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by Carolyn Shaw 2-95.
