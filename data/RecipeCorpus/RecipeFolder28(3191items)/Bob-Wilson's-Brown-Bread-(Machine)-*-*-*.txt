Title: Bob Wilson's Brown Bread (Machine) * * *
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Strong White Bread Flour
1 c  Strong Wholemeal Bread Flour
1 1/2 tb Dry Skimmed Milk
1    Sachet Blend Dried Yeast
1 1/4 c  Lukewarm Water
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Sugar
4 1/2 ts Olive Oil
Directions:

Place ingredients in bread machine in the above order.  Select White
Bread setting.  (This has got to be the best brown bread ever!)
