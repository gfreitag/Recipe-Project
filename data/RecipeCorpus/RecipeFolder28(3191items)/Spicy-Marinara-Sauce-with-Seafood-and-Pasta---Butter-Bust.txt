Title: Spicy Marinara Sauce with Seafood and Pasta - Butter Bust
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
-torn apart
2 t  Minced garlic
1 cn (27 1/4oz) Hunts light
-spaghetti sauce
1 cn (8oz) sliced mushrooms,
-rinsed and drained
1/2 c  Liquid Butter Buds or
-fatfree chicken broth
2 t  Basil
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 t  Crushed red pepper
1 cn Whorle tomatoes and juice,
Saute onion and galric in Butter Buds until onion is golden brown. Add
tomatoes and juice, basil, red pepper, mushrooms and can of spaghetti
sauce. Simmer about 15 minutes. You can add shrimp, scallops, crab or
any other seafood to this sauce and serve with the pasta of your
sauce. It is quick and easy. Add 155 calories for every cup of pasta
used. Note: I make this sauce recipe in large quantities and freeze.
It comes in handy on a busy day. Per serving: 76 cal., 0.4g fat (5%),
0mg chol., 3 g fiber, 3g pro., 17g carb., 900mg sod. Butter Busters
