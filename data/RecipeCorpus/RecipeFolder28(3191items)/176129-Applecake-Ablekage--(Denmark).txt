Title: 176129 Applecake Ablekage  (Denmark)
Categories: West europe, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Applesauce
1 c  Whipping cream, whipped
-Red jelly
2 c  Dry bread crumbs
3 T  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
Saute bread crumbs and 1 tablespoon sugar in butter until browned.
Place in serving dish in alternate layers with applesauce.  Serve
topped with whipped cream sweetened with remaining sugar.  Decorate
with red jelly. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-05-95
