Title: Thai Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Asian
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Chili paste
1 ts Honey
3    Scallions; chopped
1/8 c  Soy oil
1    Chicken, med.; cut up
3/4 c  Peanut butter; chunky
1/2 ts Oil; sesame
1 ts Ginger, minced; fresh
1/8 c  Water; warm
Mix all ingredients except chicken to make sauce. Alternate adding
soy oil and water to finish. Brown chicken 10 minutes each side at
400 degrees. Coat with sauce and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
