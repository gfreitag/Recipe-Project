Title: Apple, Celery and Sesame Seed Salad (Sim-Sim Cos)*
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
-toasted
3 tb Mayonnaise
3    Apples, unpeeled, diced
2    Celery stalks, diced
3 tb Sesame seeds, lightly
Combine all ingredients. Toss lightly. serve on lettuce leaves.

Recipe gives no indication what language Sim-Sim Cos is a part of nor
what ethnic group it is from.

From Deaf Smith country cookbook, Natural Foods for Natural Kitchens
