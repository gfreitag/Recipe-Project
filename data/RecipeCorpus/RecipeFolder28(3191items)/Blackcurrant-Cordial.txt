Title: Blackcurrant Cordial
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 batch
2 oz Sugar (to taste)
Few scented geranium leaves
12    -fluid ounces Spanish brandy
-or vodka
2    -fluid ounces fresh black-
-currant juice
1    1/2-inch stick of cinnamon
1 oz TO
Place the juice, cinnamon, sugar and geranium leaves in a suitable
bottle. Fill up with brandy or vodka.  Cap firmly and shake well.
Leave in a warm place for a few days to dissolve the sugar and then
keep in a cool place for a month before straining through muslin or
flannel into clean bottles.

The authors say that this cordial is best served chilled or mixed
with a crisp white wine.  It can also be heated and served as a sauce
over cream cheese or ice cream.

from Edible Gifts by Claire Clifton and Martina Nicolls typed by
