Title: Speedy Chocolate Peanut Butter Fudge
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 64 servings
1/4 c  Heavy cream or milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
3/4 c  Smooth or crunchy peanut but
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
2 c  Miniature marshmallows
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:12 1. In a 2-qt glass bowl, combine peanut
butter, chocolate chips, marshmallows, cream, and vanilla. Place in
microwave and heat on High 1 1/2 to 2 mins, or until marshmallows are
melted and mixture is smooth when stirred.

2. Turn into a foil-lined 8-inch square baking pan. Let cool 1/2
hour, then refrigerate until firm. Cut into 1-inch squares.
