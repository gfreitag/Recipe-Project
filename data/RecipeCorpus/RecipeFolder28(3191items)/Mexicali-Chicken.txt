Title: Mexicali Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Chicken breasts; skinned and    1 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Converted rice
4 t  Chicken bouillon
-bonesless; in strips
15 oz Black beans; drain and rinse
14 1/2 oz Mexican style stewed tomato
In large non stick skillet, over medium high heat, cook chicken in Pam
until no longer pink, about 5 minutes. Add remaining ingredients;
bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 20 minutes. Remove
from heat; let stand 5 minutes before serving.
