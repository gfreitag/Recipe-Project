Title: Quik Taters 'N Chicken Combo
Categories: Vegetables, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Cond. Cream of Chicken Soup
Salt - to taste
Pepper - to taste
2 c  Schwan's Diced Chicken Meat
4 c  Schwan's Quik Taters
1 c  Celery, diced
1/2 c  Onion, diced
1 tb Schwan's Pace Fancy Blend
-Margarine
1 cn Cond. Chicken Rice Soup
In a skillet, saute celery and onion in margarine until tender.
Remove from heat.  Add soups, salt and pepper.  Mix well.  Fold in
Diced Chicken and Quik Taters.  Pour into a greased pan or casserole
dish and bake at 350Ã¸F. for 45 minutes.  Makes 6 servings.
