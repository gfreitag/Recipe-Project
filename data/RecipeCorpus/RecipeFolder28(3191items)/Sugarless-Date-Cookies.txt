Title: Sugarless Date Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Diabetic
Yield: 28 servings
1 tb Liquid sugar substitute
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Chopped dates
1 c  Water
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Margarine
Combine in saucepan raisins, dates, and water. Boil 3 minutes,
stirring constantly. Cool Cream together eggs, margarine, liquid
sugar substitute, and vanilla. Sift together cinnamon, flour, and
soda. Add dry ingredients to creamed mixture alternately with date
mixture. Beat well. Chill several hours. Drop from teaspoon onto
greased baking pan. Bake at 350 F. for 10 to 12 minutes.
