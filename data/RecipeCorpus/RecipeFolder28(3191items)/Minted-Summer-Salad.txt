Title: Minted Summer Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Fresh mint; chopped
1/4 c  Parsley; chopped
2 T  Oil
2 T  White wine vinegar
2    Zucchini
1/2    Seedless cucumber
6    Plum tomatoes or 3 beefsteak
3/4 c  Red onion; chopped
Chop unpeeled squash, cucumbers and tomatoes into 1/2" pieces. Combine
vegetables with onion, herbs, oil, vinegar, salt and pepper; toss.
Keeps well several days in the refrigerator.  Each 2/3 cup serving
contains 53 calories and 4 grams fat.  Source: grocery ad
