Title: Spicy Snack Mix
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 cervings
2 1/2 c  Peanuts (12 oz)
2 c  Small pretzels
2 cn French fried onions
1/2 c  Margarine, melted
1 1/4 oz Taco seasoning mix
4 c  Cheez-It
4 c  Corn Chex
Preheat oven to 250.  In a small bowl, blend margarine and seasoning
mix. In a large roasting pan, mix remaining ingredients. Pour butter
mixture over snacks; stir until well coated. Bake 1 hour, stirring
every 15 minutes.  Cool completely.  Store in airtight container.
