Title: Zesty Grilled Chops
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Brown sugar
6 ea Rib/loin chops. 1 1/2" thick
3/4 c  Soy sauce
1 tb Chili sauce
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
Combine the first five ingredients. (read left to right). Place chops
in a glass baking dish and pour marinade over. Cover and refrigerate
3-6 hours or overnight. To cook, remove chops from marinade and grill
or broil 4 inches from  the heat to desired doneness. Brush
occasionally with the marinade...
