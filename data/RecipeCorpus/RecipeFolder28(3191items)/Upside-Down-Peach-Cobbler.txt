Title: Upside Down Peach Cobbler
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
16 oz Sliced peaches, drained
Water
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
2 tb Margarine
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Milk
Mix well flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.  Stir in milk just
until blended.  Spread in a greased 9 x 9 inch baking pan.  Top with
peaches. Add enough water to reserved syrup to make 1 1/2 cup.  Heat
with sugar and margarine until margarine melts and sugar dissolves.
Pour over peaches. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 to 40 minutes.
