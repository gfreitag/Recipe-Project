Title: Rice Florentine
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
Dash nutmeg
1 1/2 c  Minute Long Grain Rice
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
9 1/2 oz Frozen creamed spinach,
-thawed
1 1/2 c  2% lowfat milk
1/4 ts Pepper
Bring spinach, milk, pepper and nutmeg to a boil in saucepan.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer 5 minutes.

Stir in rice; cover.  Remove from heat.  Let stand 5 minutes.

Stir in 2 tablespoons of the cheese.  Serve with remaining cheese
sprinkled over rice.

Recipe can be doubled. From: Shari Domke Date: 11 Apr 94
