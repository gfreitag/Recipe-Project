2/3 c  Heavy cream, whipped
Day before, soften gelatine
-in cold water in glass
-measuring cup. Add
Salt, pepper, and paprika.
-Set measuring cup in pan
-of boiling water,
Stirring, till gelatine
-dissolves. Add to turkey
-combined with rest of
Ingredients. Pour into 8" x
-8" x 2" cake pan. Chill
-till firm. Cut into
Squares; serve on crisp
-greens. Sprinkle with
-minced parsley and paprika.
Makes 6 servings.
1 ea Env. plus 1 1/2 teasp.
-unflavored gelatine
2 c  Cold water
1/2 t  Salt
ds Pepper
ds Paprika
1 1/2 c  Cooked or canned turkey or
-chicken, cut into medium
-pieces
1 c  Cold cooked rice
3/4 c  Finely diced celery
2 T  Chopped green pepper
1 T  Pickle relish
2 T  Chopped stuffed olives
1/4 c  Chopped pecans
1 T  Chopped parsley
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
~--To vary: Substitute cooked, cleaned fresh shrimp, crab meat,
lobster, ham or tuna for turkey.
