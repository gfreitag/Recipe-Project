Title: Banana Split Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 7 cups
1 c  Dried banana chips
6 oz Maraschino cherries, chopped
18 oz Cherry preserves
48 oz Chocolate syrup
1 c  Chopped pecans, toasted
In large microwave bowl, microwave preserves on medium-high power
(80%) 4 to 5 minutes or until melted. Stir in remaining ingredients.
Store in airtight container in refrigerator.  Serve warm or cold with
ice cream or cake.
