Title: SUPER TUPPER CINNAMON ROLLS
Categories: Breads, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  SUGAR
1/4 c  MARGARINE
1/3 ea 1/3 OF TUPPER BREAD DOUGH
1 x  CINNAMON AS DESIRED 1 T.?
Roll dough to about 22 X 14. Mix ingredients & pour all over batter.
Roll into a log & slice, placing slices in a shallow pan. Let rise
about 20 minutes. Bake at 350^ for about 30 minutes.
