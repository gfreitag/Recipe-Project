Title: Autumn Apple Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
-softened
1 c  Diced unpeeled apples
1/2    To 1 cup chopped nuts
1 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Whipped topping Lettuce
-leaves
1 cn (20 ounces) crushed
-pineapple, undrained
2/3 c  Sugar
1 pk (3 ounces) lemon-flavored
-gelatin
1 pk (8 ounces) cream cheese,
In a saucepan, combine pineapple and sugar; bring to a boil and boil
for 3 minutes. Add gelatin; stir until dissolved. Add cream cheese;
stir until mixture is thoroughly combined. Cool. Fold in apples,
nuts, celery and whipped topping. Pour into a 9-in. square baking
pan. Chill until firm. Cut into squares and serve on lettuce leaves.

Yield: 9-12 servings. From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.
Feb 1994
