Title: 633471 Pork Tenderloin Wrapped In Bacon
Categories: Pork
Yield: 3 servings
1 T  Oil
1 md Onion, chopped
1 T  Finely chopped parsley
2 c  Sliced mushrooms (1/2 lb.)
1 1/2 c  Rich beef stock
4 oz Dry white wine
-Dash of cayenne pepper
1 t  Fresh lemon juice
2 t  All-purpose flour
1    Pork tenderloin
-(approximately 1 lb.)
-Bacon to wrap tenderloin
-Olive oil
-Italian seasoning
-Salt, pepper and garlic
-powder or crushed garlic
--SAUCE:--
1 T  Butter or margarine
Sear meat in frying pan approximately 2-3 minutes over high heat
quickly, then spread olive oil over meat and season with Italian
seasoning and rest of ingredients.  Wrap tenderloin completely in
bacon. Broil or grill 6-8 inches from source of heat for 40 minutes,
turning meat every 10 minutes. Prepare sauce while cooking
tenderloin. Place butter and oil in large skillet over low heat, add
onion, parsley and mushrooms. Saute over medium heat until tender.
Add stock, wine, cayenne and lemon juice. Bring to a boil. Whisk in
flour and cook until thickened. Pour sauce over tenderloin and serve.
Serves 3-4. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   02-25-95
