Title: Justin Wilson's Crawfish or Shrimp Cocktail
Categories: Appetizers, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Lemon or lime juice
1/2 c  Parsley, chopped fine
Louisiana hot sauce
Boiled crawfish or shrimp
1/2 c  Heinz chili sauce
1/2 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Horseradish
1 tb Lea & Perrins Worcestershire
1/2 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients to make sauce.  Pour over crawfish or shrimp,
or dip the crawfish or shrimp in the sauce. Justin Wilson's "Gourmet
and Gourmand Cookbook"
