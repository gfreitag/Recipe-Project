Title: Gingered Sole^
Categories: Healthy, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
-concentrate
1 cl Garlic, minced
2 tb Slivered gingerroot
2 tb Dry sherry
2    Green onions with tops, cut
-in 1" pieces
1 lb Sole fillets, or other
-similar fish
1 tb Low sidoum soy sauce
-MIXED WITH
1 tb Water
1/2 ts Fresh lemon or lime juice
1 ts Apple or pineapple juice
rinse and dry fillets. Place fish on a heatproof plate. Combine soy
sauce mixture, apple juice concentrate, gingerroot, sherry and green
onions. Pour 1/2 of this mixture over fish using a brush to coat all
fish surfaces. Reserve the rest for sauce. Place fish on a steamer
rack in a wok or large steamer in which 2" of water has come to a
boil. Steam fish until it turns opague, 10-15 minutes. Serve with
accumulated juice mixed with reserved sauce. Serve over rice or angel
hair pasta. Makes 4 servings.

Per serving without rice or pasta: Calories: 109 Protein: 17g
Carbohydrates: 5g Fat: 0.8g, 0.06% Sodium: 214mg Cholesterol: 61mg
