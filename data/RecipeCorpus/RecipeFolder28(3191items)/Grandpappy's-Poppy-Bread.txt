Title: Grandpappy's Poppy Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 2 loaves
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 1/2 T  Poppy seeds
2 t  Vanilla; divided
2 t  Almond extract; divided
2 t  Butter flavoring; divided
1/4 c  Orange juice
3 c  Flour
1 1/2 t  Salt
1 1/2 t  Baking powder
3 c  Sugar; divided
1 1/8 c  Oil
3    Eggs
Combine the flour, salt, baking powder and 2 1/4 cups of sugar in a
large bowl.  Add the oil, eggs, milk, poppy seeds, 1 1/2 t of butter
flavoring. Beat with an electric mixer for 1 to 2 minutes.  Pour into
2 greased loaf pans.  Bake at 350 for 1 hour.  Combine the OJ and the
remaining sugar, vanilla, almond and butter flavorings.  Mix until
smooth. Spread the glaze over the bread.  Source:  Beverly
