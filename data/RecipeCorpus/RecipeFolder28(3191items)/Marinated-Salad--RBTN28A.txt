Title: Marinated Salad  RBTN28A
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Raw carrots, sliced thin
1/2 c  Onion, chopped large
2 c  Salad tomatoes (drain well)
Or use fresh if available
1/2 c  Chopped green peppers
2 c  Caulifloweretts (raw)
2 c  Broccoli (broken into bite
Size pieces)
1 c  Ripe black olives (pitted
And drained)
Cover with Italian Dressing. Marinate several hours or over-night
until ready to serve. Omit any of the veggies you don't like or add
others that you do like....
