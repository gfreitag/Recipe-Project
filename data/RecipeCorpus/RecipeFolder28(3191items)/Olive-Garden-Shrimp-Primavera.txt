Title: Olive Garden Shrimp Primavera
Categories: Pasta, Vegetables, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Black pepper
Vegetables
2 tb Butter
1/2 lb Mushrooms; halve
1 c  Green peppers; 1" squares
1 c  Red peppers; 1" squares
1/2 c  Yellow onion; 1" squares
1 lb Fresh or dry linguini; cook
1 lb Md to lg shrimp; cook, peel
-- devein
-----waldine van geffen vghc
Sauce
1/3 oz Butter or margarine
1 oz Pk knorr romaglio pasta
-- sauce mix (or similar)
28 oz Canned tomatoes; crush
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Red chili pepper; crush
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Dried marjoram
Melt butter in 3-qt pan over medium heat. Add remaining sauce
ingredients. Stir well and simmer for 10 minutes. In a large saute
pan, melt 2 tb butter.

Saute veggies about 3 minutes until crisp-tender. Ad to the sauce and
simmer 5 more minutes. When pasta is almost done, stir shrimp into
sauce to heat through.

Do not boil. Spoon Shrimp Primavera sauce over hot linguini. Sprinkle
with Parmesan.

Source: The Olive Garden.
