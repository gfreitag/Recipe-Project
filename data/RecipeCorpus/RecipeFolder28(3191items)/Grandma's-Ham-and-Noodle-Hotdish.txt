Title: Grandma's Ham and Noodle Hotdish
Categories: Spam, Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 1 casserole
2 c  Cooked egg noodles;
1    Egg; beaten
1 cn Cream chicken soup;
1 cn Cream mushroom soup;
1 sl Ham in cubes;
1 cn Whole kernel corn;
1/2 c  Green pepper; chopped
1/4 c  Melted margarine;
1 1/2 c  Cornflakes;
3/4 c  Diced cheese;
SPAM can be used in place of ham.  Mix in order given and bake 1 hour
at 350 degrees. Source: Our Favorite Recipes, Barb Gerarda, Ladies
Auxiliary, V. F. W., Blaine, Minnesota Brought to you and yours via
Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
