Title: Country Wedding Soup
Categories: Question?
Yield: 10 servings

1/4 c  Scallions; chopped
1 1/2 c  White rice; cooked
Salt
Pepper
6 c  Chicken broth
1/2 c  Carrots; cut in 1/2" dice
1/2 c  Celery; cut in 1/2" dice
1/2 c  Onion; cut in 1/2" dice
1/2 c  Tomatoes, fresh; seeded
-- and diced
1    Cl Garlic; chopped
2 c  Red leaf lettuce
MMMMM-----------------TURKEY MEATBALL - MAKES 16----------------------
1/4 c  Unseasoned bread crumbs
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 lb Ground turkey meat
2 tb Onion, finely diced
1/8 ts Garlic powder
Recipe by: Cooking With Mickey-Gourmet Mickey Cookbook Vol II

In a 4-qt soup pot, add chicken broth, carrots, celery, onion,
tomatoes, and garlic. Bring to a boil and simmer 20 mins until
vegetables are tender but not mushy. Add lettuce, ham, scallions, and
rice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add turkey meatballs.
Serve immediately.

YIELD: 16 meatballs
TURKEY MEATBALLS

Preheat oven to 350 F.  Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and
mix well.  Form into 1/2-inch meatballs and place on a greased baking
sheet. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until firm and fully cooked.  Remove
from oven and add to soup.
