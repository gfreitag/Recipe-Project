Title: Ground Beef Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1 md Onion, chopped
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 cn Water
3/4 c  Rice
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 c  Celery, chopped
1 cn Cream of celery soup
1/4 c  Soy sauce
Brown meat and onion; add rest of the ingredients and bake at 350
degrees for 80 minutes.  Top with chow mein noodles the last 10
minutes. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   03-14-95
