Title: Sugarless Raisin Cookies Ada
Categories: Cake/cookie, Diabetic
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Margarine
1    Egg
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
3/4 c  Raisins
2/3 c  Water
1 tb Liquid sugar substitute
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Combine raisins,water, sweetner, spices, and margarine in saucepan.
Bring to a boil and cook 3 minutes. Cool. stir in egg. Mix flour,
baking powder, and salt. Add to fruit mixture and mix well. Drop by
teaspoon onto a lightly greased baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for
9 to 10 minutes.

From ADA Family Cookbook vol. II.

Exchanges per 2 cookies: 1 bread, 1 fat Cal 95, cho 16, pro 2,fat 3,
Na 110, K 86.
