Title: Fish in skillet  RBTN28A
Categories: Fish
Yield: 6 servings
1 x  Cloves of garlic
2 c  Italian stewed tomatoes
1 x  Onions
1 x  Green peppers
For the in kitchen method. Lightly saute the onion, peppers and a few
cloves of garlic in a heavy skillet. Pour in 2 cans of Italian stewed
tomatoes and cook until bubbling. Lay fillets cut into bite-sized
pieces on top if vegetables. Put a lid on it and simmer for 10
minutes. Dig in..
