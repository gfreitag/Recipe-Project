Title: Orange Chocolate Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Ice cream
Yield: 3 cups
1/2 c  Frozen OJ concentrate, thaw
2 c  Sifted powdered sugar
1 t  Orange extract
12 oz Evaporated milk
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
In medium saucepan, combine milk, chocolate chips, butter, and orange
juice concentrate.  Stirring constantly, cook over low heat until
smooth. Increase heat to medium-high. Gradually stir in powdered
sugar and bring to a boil.  Stirring contantly, reduce heat to
medium-low and boil 8 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in orange
extract. Cool to room temperature and store in airtight container in
refrigerator. Serve warm or cold over ice cream or cake.  Source:
