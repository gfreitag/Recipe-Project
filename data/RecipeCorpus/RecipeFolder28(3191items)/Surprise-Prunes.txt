Title: Surprise Prunes
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 1 batch
Crystallized citron
Grated peel of 2 oranges
1/2    -teacup granulated sugar
1 lb Pitted or tenderized prunes
Crystallized ginger or
-drained stem ginger OR
Candied violets OR
Pit the prunes if necessary.  Use a sharp knife to pry out the stones
without savaging the prunes.  Cut the ginger or other filling into
thick slivers and fill each prune.  Mix the grated peel with the
sugar and roll the prunes one at a time in the mixture until they are
covered. Leave on a plate to dry for several hours, then pack between
layers of waxed or greaseproof paper in air-tight tins.
