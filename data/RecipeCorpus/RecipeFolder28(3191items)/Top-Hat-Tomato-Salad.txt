Title: Top-Hat Tomato Salad
Categories: Herbs/spice, Cheese/eggs, Mom's, Salads
Yield: 4 servings

1/4 t  Dried basil (optional)
1/8 t  Worcestershire
1 T  Minced onion
4 ea Chilled, peeled big juicy
-tomatoes
Cheese-and-egg topper
1 c  Cottage cheese
6 ea Chopped, shelled,
-hard-cooked eggs
3/4 t  Salt
1/2 t  Prepared mustard
Combine all ingredients except tomatoes. Slice tomatoes; arrange on
individual beds of crisp greens; top with mounds of cheese-egg
mixture. Pass French dressing. Makes 4 servings.
