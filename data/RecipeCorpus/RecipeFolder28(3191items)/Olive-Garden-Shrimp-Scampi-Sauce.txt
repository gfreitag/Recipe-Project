Title: Olive Garden Shrimp Scampi Sauce
Categories: Seafood, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Dry mustard
Tabasco
1/3 c  Ketchup
-----waldine van geffen vghc
1/2 c  Bottled wishbone italian
-- dressing
With wire whisk combine all ingredients until smooth.

Keep refrigerated, tightly covered, to use within a week. Freeze to
thaw and use within 4 months.

Source: Gloria Pitzer.
