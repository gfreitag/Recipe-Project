Title: Baked Chicken Breast on Rice
Categories: Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1    Soup can milk or
-- substitute water or
-- onion soup mix undiluted*
1    Envelope dry onion soup mix
3/4 c  Regular rice; uncooked
1 cn Condensed cream of
-- mushroom soup
1 cn (4 oz) mushroom stems
-- and pieces
2    Chicken breasts
-- split in halves
Recipe by: Dorothy Gothard

* If using onion soup, do not use the dry onion soup mix.

Heat oven to 350~. Blend soup and milk; reserve 1/2 cup of the
mixture. Stir together remaining soup mixture, rice, mushrooms (with
liquid) and half onion soup mix. Pour into greased 11 1/2x7 1/2x1
1/2-inch baking dish.

Arrange chicken breasts on rice mixture over chicken and sprinkle
with remaining onion soup mix. Bake for 1 hour. Uncover and bake for
15 minutes longer.
