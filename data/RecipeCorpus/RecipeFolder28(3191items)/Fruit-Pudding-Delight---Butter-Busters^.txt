Title: Fruit Pudding Delight - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Desserts
Yield: 16 ----------
-pieces
1 c  Grape Nuts
1 ea Pk (6oz) instant regular
-vanilla pudding
OR
1 ea Pk (2.1oz) instant
-sugarfree vanilla pudding
4 cn (20 oz) chunk pineapple,
-drained, reserving 1cup
-juice
2 cn (11oz) mandarin oranges,
-drained
2 c  Maraschino cherries, drained
6 lg Bananas, cut in bite size
Combine fruit and Grape Nuts in a large bowll; toss gently. Combine
pudding and reserved pineapple juice; beat on high speed one minute.
Pour over fruit to coat welll. Serve in small dessert dishes. Per
sugar and regular pudding serving: 234 cal., 0.4g fat (3%), 0mg
chol., 1g fiber, 3g pro., 23g carb., 116mg sod. Per S&L and sugarfree
pudding serving: 207 cal., 20g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie
