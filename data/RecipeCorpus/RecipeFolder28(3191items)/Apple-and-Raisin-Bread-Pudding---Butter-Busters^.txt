Title: Apple and Raisin Bread Pudding - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Desserts
Yield: 16 ----------
OR
1/2 c  Sugar
AND
3 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
1 t  Vanilla
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/4 t  Nutmeg
4 c  French bread, cut in cubes
1 1/2 c  Diced red and/or yellow
-delicious apples
1/2 c  Raisins
1 1/2 c  Skim milk
3/4 c  Egg Beaters
3/4 c  Sugar
In a 2-qt casserole that has been sprayed with Pam, combine bread
cubes, apples and raisins; set aside. Combine milk, Egg Beaters,
sugar, Butter Buds, vanilla, cinnamon and nutmeg, pour over bread
mixture. Bake at 350F 45-50 minutes or until knife inserted in center
comes out clean. Serve warm with Dream Whip or nonfat frozen yogurt
on top. Per sugar serving: 108 cal., 0.4g fat (3%), 0mg chol., 1g
fiber, 3g pro., 23g carb., 116mg sod. Per S&L serving: 96 cal., 20g
carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN 0-466-67040-5 Entered by
Carolyn Shaw 3-95.
