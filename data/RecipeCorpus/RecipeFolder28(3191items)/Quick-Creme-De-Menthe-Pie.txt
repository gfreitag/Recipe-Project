Title: Quick Creme De Menthe Pie
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 6 servings
1    8-inch chocolate crumb crust
-- baked
Whipped cream
Shaved semisweet chocolate
1 c  Heavy cream
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Creme de menthe
1 pk (4 oz) instant chocolate
-- fudge pudding mix
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:15 1. In a medium bowl, beat cream with an
electric mixer on high until stiff, 2 to 3 mins.

2. In a medium bowl, combine milk, creme de menthe, and pudding mix.
Beat with electric mixer on low speed 1 min. Fold in whipped cream
until well blended.

3. Turn mixture into crumb crust, spreading evenly. Refrigerate 1
hour or longer, until set. Top with whipped cream and shave
chocolate. Refrigerate any leftovers.
