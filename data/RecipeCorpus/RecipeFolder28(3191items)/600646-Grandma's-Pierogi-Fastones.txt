Title: 600646 Grandma's Pierogi Fastones
Categories: East europe, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
1 pt Sour cream
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Pepper
2/3 c  Green onions, chopped
1 lb Box noodles, cooked
8 oz Cream cheese, room
-temperature
1 lb Carton sm. curd cottage
-cheese
Mix all together and put in buttered casserole; sprinkle with Parmesan
cheese, dot with butter.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, covered
and 30 minutes more, uncovered.  If you prefer it crusty, leave in a
little longer.  May substitute lite cheeses, lite sour cream and Pam
in this recipe.
