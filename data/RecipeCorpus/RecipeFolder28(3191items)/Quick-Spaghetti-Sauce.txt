Title: Quick Spaghetti Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Beef, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 ts Brown sugar; packed
3/4 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/8 ts Marjoram
1/2 lb Ground beef; lean
24 oz Tomato sauce; 3 cn
1/2 c  Water
2 oz Mushrooms; sliced,
-- drained; 1cn
2 tb Minced onion flakes
Saute the ground beef in a skillet and drain off the excess fat.
Add the remaining ingredients and heat to boiling.  Simmer for 10
minutes, stirring occasionally.  Serve over hot cooked spaghetti or
other pasta.
