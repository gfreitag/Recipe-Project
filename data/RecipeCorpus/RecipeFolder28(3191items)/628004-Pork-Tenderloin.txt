Title: 628004 Pork Tenderloin
Categories: Pork
Yield: 2 servings
2 t  Caraway seed
2 t  Dill weed
12 oz Pork tenderloin
1 T  Soy sauce
2 t  Aniseed
Preparation : Brush soy sauce over meat, distributing evenly. On a
sheet of wax paper combine aniseed, caraway seed and dill seed.  Roll
meat in seeds to coat evenly.  Wrap meat in foil; chill 1 to 2 hours
or overnight. Remove foil.  In a covered grill arrange preheated
coals around a drip pan. Place roast on rack over drip pan but not
over coals. Close grill hood and grill 45 minutes.
