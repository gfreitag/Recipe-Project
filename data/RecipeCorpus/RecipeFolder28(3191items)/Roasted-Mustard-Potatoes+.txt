Title: Roasted Mustard Potatoes+
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Chili powder
1/8 ts Cayenne
16 sm New potatoes, red
4 tb Dijon mustard
2 ts Paprika
1 ts Cumin, ground
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.

Spray roasting pan with oil, 3 spritzes. Put the mustard, paprika,
cumin, chili powder and cayenne into a large bowl. Whisk to blend.
Prick the potatoes with a fork several times and add them to the
bowl. Toss to coat the potatoes evenly. Pour the coated potatoes into
the prepared roasting pan, leaving a little space between them.

Bake for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, until the potatoes are fork
tender.
