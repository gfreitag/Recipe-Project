Title: BEAN SOUP the ultimate
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Water
1 x  Medium carrot, diced
2 c  Chicken broth
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1 x  For garnish.
1 c  Dry navy beans
1 x  Medium onion, chopped
1 x  Stalk celery; leaves chopped
1/2 c  Milk or half-and-half
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Dried marjoram
1 x  Chopped chives or parsley-
Place beans in a large sauce pan with water. Bring to a boil; cook 2
min. Remove from heat; cover and let stand one hour. Add onion,
carrot and celery. Simmer until beans are tender, about 2 hours.
Drain, reserving 1/4 cup liquid. Whirl beans in blender or food
processor, a few cups at a time, adding a little of the reserved
cooking liquid as needed, until mixture is smooth. Add broth, milk
and seasonings. Cover and chill. Garnish with chives or parsley
before serving. Or serve hot with splash of fresh lime juice or
sherry.
