Title: Maple-Glazed Parsnips
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Water
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 tb Parsley, chopped
1/3 c  Maple syrup
1/4 ts Prepared mustard
2 ts Butter
5 1/2 c  Parsnips
-1/4" diagonally sliced
Combine maple syrup and mustard; stir well and set aside. Coat large
nonstick skillet with cooking spray; add butter and place over medium
heat until butter melts. Add parsnips and next 3 ingredients; cover,
reduce heat to medium-low, and cook for 12 minutes or until tender,
stirring occasionally. Add syrup mixture; cook, uncovered, over
medium-high heat, for 1 minute or until lightly glazed, stirring
constantly. Remove from heat; sprinkle with parsley and serve.
