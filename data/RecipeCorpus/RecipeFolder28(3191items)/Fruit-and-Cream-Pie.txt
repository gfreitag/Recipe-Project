Title: Fruit and Cream Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1 pk 4 serving size cook and
-serve pudding
1 1/4 c  Water
2 c  Sliced fruit
4 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 c  Cool whip; thawed
6 oz Graham cracker crust
1 pk 4 serving size gelatin
Mix cream cheese and sugar in large bowl until well blended. Stir in
cool whip.  Spread in crust. Refrigerate. Mix gelatin, pudding mix
and water in medium saucepan until smooth.  Stirring constantly, cook
on medium heat until mixture just comes to boil. Remove from heat.
Cool 5 minutes. Stir in fruit. Spoon over cream cheese layer.
Refrigerate 4 hours or until set. Note:  Can substitute sugarfree
varieties and lite cool whip. Source: Jello ad
