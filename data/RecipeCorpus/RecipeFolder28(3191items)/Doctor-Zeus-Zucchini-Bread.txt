Title: Doctor Zeus Zucchini Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 2 loaves
1/2 c  Raisins
3 c  Flour
1 1/2 t  Cinnamon
1 t  Salt
3/4 t  Nutmeg
2 t  Baking soda
1/4 t  Baking powder
3    Eggs
1 c  Oil
2 c  Sugar
2 t  Vanilla
2 c  Zucchini; shredded
8 1/2 oz Crushed pineapple; drained
1 c  Nuts; chopped
Beat the eggs, oil, sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl until thick.
Stir in the remaining ingredients.  Mix well.  Pour into 2 greased
loaf pans. Bake at 350 for 1 hour.  Source: Beverly Hillbillies
