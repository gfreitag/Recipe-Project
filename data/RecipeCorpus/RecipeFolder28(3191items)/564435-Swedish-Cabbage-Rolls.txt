Title: 564435 Swedish Cabbage Rolls
Categories: West europe, Crockpot, Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 c  Minute rice
1    (8 oz.) can tomato sauce
1 T  Brown sugar
1 T  Lemon juice
1 t  Worcestershire sauce
12 lg Cabbage leaves (or more.
2    Beaten eggs
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 t  Pepper
1 t  Salt
Immerse cabbage leaves in large kettle of boiling water for 3 minutes;
drain.  Combine eggs, milk, onion, salt, pepper, beef and rice. Place
about 1/4 cup meat mixture in center of each leaf; fold in sides and
roll ends over meat.  Secure with toothpick.  Place in slow cooker.
Combine rest of ingredients.  Pour over cabbage rolls. (If you like a
lot of sauce, you may want to double this part of the recipe.) Cover
and cook in slow cooker for 7 to 9 hours on low.
