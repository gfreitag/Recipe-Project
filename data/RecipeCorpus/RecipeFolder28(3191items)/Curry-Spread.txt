Title: Curry Spread
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/3 c  Tomato ketchup
1 ts Cider vinegar
2 ea Bay leaves
12 ea Whole cloves
1 ea Onion
2 ea Garlic cloves
1/4 c  Margarine
1 tb Curry powder
Slice onions & chop garlic finely.  Heat margarine & saute the onion &
garlic in it until they brown.  Stir in the curry powder & add the
ketchup with the rest of the ingredients.  Simmer for about 20
minutes, until it thickens.  Remove bay leaves & cloves.

This is best served hot on wholewheat toast or chilled on crackers.
It is quite rich, so spread thinly.

Leah Leneman, "365 plus One Vegan Recipes"
