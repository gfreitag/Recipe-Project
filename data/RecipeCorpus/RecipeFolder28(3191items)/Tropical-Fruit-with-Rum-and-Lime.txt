Title: Tropical Fruit with Rum and Lime
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
-peeled, pitted, sliced
1    Ripe banana, peeled
-thinly sliced on diagonal
2    Lime wedges
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Dark rum
1/4 ts Grenadine syrup (opt)
1    Ripe mango
Bring water and sugar to simmer in heavy small saucepan over medium
heat, stirring until sugar dissolves. Simmer syrup 1 minute. Remove
from heat; mix in run and grenadine. Cover syrup and refrigerate
until chilled.

Arrange mango and banana slices on 2 plates, dividing equally. Spoon
syrup over fruit. Squeeze 1 lime wedge over each and serve.
