Title: Prawn And Garlic Sauce
Categories: Seafood, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
3 oz Butter
8 oz Peeled prawns
6    Teasp chopped fresh chives
6    Teasp olive oil
2    Cloves garlic, finely
-chopped
-salt/pepper
Heat the oil and the butter until butter is melted Add garlic and cook
gently for 2-3 minutes, stirring  occasionally

Stir in the prawns, cook gently until heated through. Season with
salt and pepper. Stir in the chopped chives then  pour sauce over hot
pasta

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
