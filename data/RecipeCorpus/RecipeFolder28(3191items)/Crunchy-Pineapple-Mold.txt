Yield: 4 servings

-pineapple
1/4 c  Diced celery
1/2 c  Diced unpared apple
1/4 c  Chopped walnuts
2 t  Pickle relish
1 1/4 c  Hot water
1 pk Lemon flavored gelatin
1/2 c  Syrup drained from canned
-pineapple
1 ea Drained No. 2 can crushed
Pour hot water over gelatin- stir till dissolved; stir in syrup.
Chill, stirring occasionally, till consistency of unbeaten egg white.
Fold in pineapple and rest of ingredients. Pour into 4 to 6
individual molds. Chill until firm. Unmold on crisp greens; serve
with Fluffy Mayonnaise. Makes 4 to 6 servings.
