Title: Western Style Broiled Fish
Categories: Main dish, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
Western French Dressing
2 tb Ornage juice
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1 lb Orange roughy fillets
-=or=-
1 lb Whitefish fillets
8 oz Original, lite or fat free
Cut fish into serving-size pieces. Combine dressing, orange juice,
Worcestershire sauce, and ginger. Reserve 1/3 cup as sauce. Pour
remaining mixture into fat dish. Dip fish pieces into mixture,
turning to coat well. Cover and let stand to marinate for 15 minutes.
Remove fish from marinade and place on oiled broiler pan. Broil 4
inch from heat source for about 4 to 6 minutes, or until fish flakes
easily. Serve with reserved sauce.

Source:"Western Chilled or Grilled" a promotional booklet by Western
Dressing Inc.
