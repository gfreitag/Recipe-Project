Title: Chocolate Graham Cracker Pudding Cake RBTN28A
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Milk
8 oz Cool whip
1 ea Chocolate fudge icing
1 x  Graham crackers *
1 x  Melted butter  *
1 ea Instant pudding mix
Crumble crackers with melted butter to form crust in bottom of cake
pan. Mix pudding with milk until thichens. Add cool whip, mix well.
Pour over crust. Lay graham crackers on top & cover with chocolate
fudge icing (comes in a can). Serves 12 * means as needed..
