Title: Quik Taters 'N Beef Combo
Categories: Vegetables, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Schwan's Frozen Vegetables
-corn or peas (optional)
1/2 c  Onion, diced
4    Schwan's Beef Patties
1 cn Cond. Cream of Mushroom Soup
Brown Beef Patties and onion in a skillet.  Using a spatula, chop
patties into small chunks.  Drain grease.  Add soups, salt and
pepper. Mix well. Fold in Quik Taters and vegetables.  Pour into a
greased pan or casserole dish and bake at 350Ã¸F. for 45 minutes.

Makes 6 servings.
