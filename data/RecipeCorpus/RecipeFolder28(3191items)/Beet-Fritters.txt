Title: Beet Fritters
Categories: Doughnut, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 lg Egg, beaten
1/4 t  Ginger
Salt and pepper to taste
2 c  Grated raw beets
1/4 c  Onion, diced
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
Mix all ingredients. Spoon out pancake sized portions onto a hot,
oiled griddle. Cook until brown, turning once. Serve topped with
butter, sour cream, yogurt, or any combination of these. Serves 6-8.
NOTE: Turnips or rutabagas can be substituted for or mixed with
beets. Also a little hot pepper can be added if desired.
