Title: Shrimp Curry - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 9 servings
2 ea Tb lime juice
2 t  Grated lime peel
2 lb Raw shrimp, shelled and
-deviened
1 qt Water
1/2 t  Lite salt, optional
1 sm Onion, sliced
1/2 ea Lemon, sliced
5 ea Black peppercorns
1/4 c  Chopped chutney
1/4 c  Chopped tomato
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
3 c  Cooked white rice
3 ea Tb liquid Butter Buds or
-fatfree chicken broth
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped pared apple
1 cl Garlic, crushed
2 t  (to 3) curry powder
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 t  Lite salt, optional
1/4 t  Ground ginger
1/4 t  Ground cardamom
1/4 t  Pepper
2 cn (10 3/4oz) low sodium
-fatfree chicken broth,
-undiluted
In a large nonstick frying pan that has been sprayed with Pam, saute
onion, apple, garlic and curry powder in liquid Butter Buds. Remove
from heat; stir in flour, 1/2 teaspoon lite salt, ginger, cardamom
and pepper. Gradually stir in 2 cans broth, lime juice and peel.
Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Reduce heat and simmer
uncovered 20 minutes stirring occasionally. Meanwhile, cook shrimp.
Bring 1 quart water, 1/2 teaspoon salt, onion, lemon and peppercorns
to a boil. Add shrimp, return to boil. Reduce heat and simmer,
uncovered, 5-10 minutes or just till tender when tested with a fork.
Drain; add to curry sauce with chutney. Heat gently just to a boil.
Serve with white rice and individual bowls of chutney, tomatoes and
green pepper. Per serving: 240 cal., 2.5g fat (9%), 153mg chol., 2g
fiber, 23g pro., 29g carb., 191mg sod. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie
