Title: Mexican Lasagna  RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Enchilada sauce
1 c  Milk
12 ea Flour tortillas,broken up
2 c  Grated cheese
1 lb Hamburger
1 ea Medium onion, chopped
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
Brown hamburger and onion together. Drain and add mushroom soup,
chicken soup, enchilada sauce and milk. Bring to a boil, then simmer
20 minutes. Pour 1/2 of meat mixture into a 9x13-inch buttered pan.
Place 1/2 of broken tortillas on top of meat mixture. Place 1/2 of
cheese next. Repeat with remaining meat mixture, broken tortillas and
cheese. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes.
