Title: Sour Cream Prune Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dips/spread, 1941
Yield: 6 servings
2    Eggs, well beaten
1 c  Uncooked pitted prunes
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Nutmeg
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Thick sour cream
1/4 c  Cold water
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Cake flour
Combine eggs, sugar, cream, and water.  Mix well.  Sift flour,
measure 1 1/4 cups, and sift with baking powder, baking soda, salt,
and spices. Add to first mixture.  Beat thoroughly.  Add prunes which
have been cut in small pieces and dredged in 1/4 cup of the sifted
flour. Pour into well-oiled loaf pan.  Bake in moderate oven (375 F)
about 45 minutes. The Household Searchlight
