Title: Peanutty Cheddar Spread
Categories: Question?
Yield: 1 cup
1 c  Cheese, Cheddar; shredded
1/4 c  Milk; TO 1/3 CUP
1/2 c  Peanut butter; smooth or
-crunchy
Combine all ingredients; blend well.  To make a thinner spread,
slowly add more milk.  Cover and stor in refrigerator.  To serve,
spread on creackers, bread, or apple and pear slices; it may also be
used to stuff celery.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, May 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
