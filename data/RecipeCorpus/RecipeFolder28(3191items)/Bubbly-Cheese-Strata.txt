Title: Bubbly Cheese Strata
Categories: Chicken, Casseroles
Yield: 8 servings
4    Eggs
3 1/2 c  Milk
1 t  Hot pepper sauce (optional)
6 oz Stove top stuffing
2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
2 c  Chicken; cooked and cubed
2 T  Flour
Pour stuffing crumbs into greased 9" square pan.  Add cheese and
chicken or turkey; sprinkle with flour.  Mix together eggs, milk, hot
pepper sauce and contents of seasoning packet.  Pour over crumb
mixture. Bake uncovered at 350 for 50 minutes.  Microwave:  Cook
uncovered at HIGH 5 minutes. Stir and cook 10 to 15 minutes longer or
until knife inserted near edge comes out clean.  Let stand 5 minutes.
