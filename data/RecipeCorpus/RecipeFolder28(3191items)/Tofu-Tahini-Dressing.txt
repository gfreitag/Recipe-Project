Title: Tofu-Tahini Dressing
Categories: Ethnic, Dress/stuff
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1    Green onion (with top), chop
-(about 1 tablespoon)
2    Cl Garlic, crushed
4 To 6 dr Red pepper sauce
1/4 c  Tahini (sesame seed paste)
4    Ounces tofu (about 1/2 cup)
1/3 c  Water
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Lemon juice
Tahini is a popular food from the Middle East made from sesame seeds.
Place all ingredients in blender or food processor. Cover and blend
on high speed, or process, until mixture is smooth.  ABOUT 1-1/3 CUPS
DRESSING; 60 CALORIES PER TABLESPOON
