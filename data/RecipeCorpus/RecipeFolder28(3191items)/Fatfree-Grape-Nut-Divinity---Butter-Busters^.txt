Title: Fatfree Grape Nut Divinity - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Snacks
Yield: 48 ----------
3 ea Egg whites
1 t  Vanilla
1 c  Grape Nuts Cereal
4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Light corn syrup
Place sugar, water and corn syrup in a saucepan and cook until it
forms a soft ball. Take a third of this mixture and pour over stuffly
beaten egg whites, beating constantly. Place the rest of the syrup
back on the stove and cook to a firm ball. Pour over the first part
and continue beating. Add vanilla. When mixture starts to thicken,
add Grape Nuts and srop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper sprayed with
Pam. You will never miss the pecans. Per sugar serving: 94 cal., 0g
fat (0%), 0g fiber, 1g pro., 24g carb., 25mg sod. Per S&L serving: Do
not use S&L in this recipe. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN
