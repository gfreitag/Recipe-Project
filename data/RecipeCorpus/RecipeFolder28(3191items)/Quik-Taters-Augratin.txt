Title: Quik Taters Augratin
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
10 1/2 oz Schwan's Shredded Cheddar
-Cheese Spread, softened
1/4 ts Dry Mustard
4 c  Schwan's Quik Taters
Paprika
1 cn Cond. Cream of Celery Soup
1/3 c  Milk
3 oz Cream Cheese, softened (opt)
1/4 c  Onion, diced
1/4 ts Pepper
Combine all ingredients except potatoes in a bowl.  Mix well. Fold in
Quik Taters. Pour into a greased pan or casserole dish.  Sprinkle
with paprika and bake at 350Ã¸F. for 45 minutes.  Let rest 5-10
minutes before serving.

Makes 6 - 8 servings.

Note:  Schwan's Hash Brown Patties may be substituted for the Quik
Taters. Simply line a greased pan with 8 Hash Brown Patties and pour
cheese mixture over.  Sprinkle with paprika and bake as directed
above.
