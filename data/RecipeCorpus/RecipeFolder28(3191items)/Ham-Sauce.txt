Title: Ham Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
3/4 c  Packed light brown sugar
6 tb Cider vinegar
3 tb Dijon mustard
3 c  Apple cider
1 1/2 c  Packed dried apple chunks
-(about 4 oz)
Bring cider and apples to boil in heavy medium saucepan over
medium-high heat. Boil until liquid is reduced to scant 1 cups, about
8 minutes. Whisk sugar, vinegar and mustard in small bowl until
blended. Add to cider mixture. Simmer sauce until reduced to 2 cups,
stirring occasionally, about 6 minutes. Pour sauce over baked ham.
