Title: Creole Style Fish Fillets
Categories: Fish
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Onion, chopped
1/3 c  Green Pepper, chopped
1/2 cl Garlic, minced
1 lb Pollack Fish Fillets, thawed
1 cn (16 oz) Tomatoes & juice
1 tb Lemon Juice
Season fish fillets with salt and pepper to taste. Place in an
ungreased baking dish.  Mix together tomatoes & juice, lemon juice,
onion, green pepper, and garlic.  Pour over fish fillets.  Bake in a
400Ã¸F oven. Spoon tomato mixture over fish several times during
baking. Fish is done when it flakes easily with a fork and is opaque
in the middle.
