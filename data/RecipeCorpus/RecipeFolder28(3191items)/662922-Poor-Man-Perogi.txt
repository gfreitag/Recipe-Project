Title: 662922 Poor Man Perogi
Categories: East europe, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2    Sticks butter or margarine
1 md Onion, chopped
-Salt and pepper to taste
1 lg Can sauerkraut (Bavarian
-style)
1 pk Creamette* shells (tiny)
Cook noodles; drain.  Add one stick butter; set aside.  In large
skillet, cook onion and 1 stick of butter until onion is transparent.
Add drained sauerkraut. Cook 20 minutes.  Add noodles. Mix well.
Simmer 20 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
