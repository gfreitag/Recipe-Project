Title: Sesame Chive Tortilla Triangles
Categories: Nuts/grains, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Chopped chives or green
-onions
3 T  Sesame seeds, toasted
6 ea 10 inch flour tortillas
1 ea Egg white
2 T  Sesame oil
1/4 t  Salt
Put 3 tortillas on work surface.  Mix together the oil, egg white and
salt together and brush onto the three tortillas.  Sprinkle on the
onions and sesame seeds.  Place remaining three tortillas on top and
press together. Bake in a 400F oven for about 10 minutes, or until
lightly browned on top. Cut into wedges and serve immediately.
Origin: Adapted from a recipe in Canadian Living, March/95. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, March/95.
