Title: Rice Pudding (comfort style)
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Raisins or sultanas
pn Nutmeg
pn Cinnamon
2 c  Milk
5 tb Rice flour
4 tb Sugar
Put rice flour in the bottom of the pan you are going to cook it in.
Add a little milk, stirring till you make a paste so that all the
rice flour is wet. Add the rest of the milk stirring all the while.
Put over a low heat.. When it starts to thicken, add the raisins and
the sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg. Stir for maybe 15 minutes till the
right consistancy. Nice with chocolate sauce over it too.
