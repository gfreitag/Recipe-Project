Title: Chocolate-Coated Potato Chips
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 6 servings
1/2    Ruffled potato chips
(6 1/2 oz bag)
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
1 tb Vegetable shortening, solid
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:12 1. In a small shallow glass dish, combine
chocolate chips and shortening. Heat in microwave on High 1 1/2 to 2
mins, or until melted and smooth when stirred.

2. Dip chips, one at a time, into chocolate, coating 1/3 to 1/2 of
each chip. Pull chip against edge of dish to remove excess chocolate.
Place chips on wax paper-lined baking pan. Let stand at room
temperature 15 mins, then refrigerate until set.
