Title: Low Calorie Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Uncooked rice
1 cn Condensed tomato soup with
1 can of water
1 sm Head of cabbage
1 lb Lean hamburger
1 md Onion
Chop cabbage and place in a greased baking dish.  Brown meat and
onion; stir in rice.  Pour on top of cabbage.  Mix soup and water.
Pour over entire casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  Add
more water, if casserole is too dry.  Bake in a 9 x 13 inch pan.
