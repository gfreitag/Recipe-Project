Title: 289163 Pierogi Casserole
Categories: East europe, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
2 T  Dry onions
1 1/2 c  Milk
6    8 potatoes
1 lb Kluski noodles
1 lb Velveeta cheese
1 1/2    Sticks oleo
Boil potatoes.  Mash.  Cook noodles 10 minutes.  In small saucepan,
place oleo, milk, onions and cheese.  Heat until cheese is melted. In
9 x 13 inch baking dish add 1/2 of noodles, spread out evenly. Add
2/3 of cheese sauce, spread over noodles.  Put mashed potatoes on top
of sauce. Add remaining noodles and cheese sauce. Bake at 350 degrees
for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
