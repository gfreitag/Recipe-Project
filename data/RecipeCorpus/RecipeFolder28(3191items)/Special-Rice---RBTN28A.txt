Title: Special Rice   RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
6 ea Green onions. finely chopped
1 x  Soy sauce to taste
2 c  Cooked rice
6 ea Slices crisp cooked bacon
Lightly saute the green onions in the bacon grease. Put rice in
skillet with onions and a bit of the grease. Heat. Add soy sauce in
small amounts to desired flavor (You'll know). Crumble bacon and add.
Stir and serve.
