Title: Curried Sweet Potato Soup with Ginger, Yogurt and Cilantr
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 1 batch
1 1/2 lb Sweet potatoes (or yams)
-peeled and thinly sliced,
-crosswise
1 tb Curry powder (mild)
2 pn Cayenne powder (to taste)
1 qt Chicken stock
2 tb Unsalted butter
4    Leeks, white with a bit of
-green, cut in rings
2    Celery ribs, finely chopped
1 tb Fresh ginger, peeled and
-minced
MMMMM-------------------------GARNISHES------------------------------
-minced
1 sm Container plain yogurt
1 bn Cilantro, stems removed,
-dried in paper toweling and
MMMMM-------------------------SEASONING------------------------------
-black pepper to taste
Salt and freshly ground
YIELD: 1 1/2 quarts

Melt butter in a soup-or stockpot. Add leeks, celery and ginger, cook
over medium heat until wilted.

Add sweet potatoes (or yams), curry powder and stock. cover and simmer
until sweet potatoes are tender, about 30 to 40 minutes.

Puree the soup in batches in blender, or food processor. (A blender
will yield a smoother consistancy.)

Return pureed soup to the stockpot and heat until warm. Add cayenne
pepper, salt and black pepper to taste.

Serve, garnished with a dollop of yogurt and minced cilantro.
