Title: Pineapple Sauce for Ham RBTN28A
Categories: Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 12 servings
3/4 tb Corn starch
1/16 c  Vinegar
3/4 tb Water
7 3/16 oz Crushed pineapple
2 7/8 oz Pineapple juice
1/16 c  Brown sugar
1/16 c  Soy sauce
Mix first 4 items together in sauce pan & heat. While heating mix
last 3 items together & pour into sauce pan with other ingredients.
Pour over baked ham. good good...
