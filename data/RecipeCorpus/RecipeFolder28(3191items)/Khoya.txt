Title: Khoya
Categories: Condiments, Indian
Yield: 3 ounces
5 c  Gold top milk
Place milk in heavy saucepan and bring to a boil. Simmer gently over
low heat for about 2 hours until it solidifies.

Note: I don't know the US equivalent of khoya or gold top milk.
