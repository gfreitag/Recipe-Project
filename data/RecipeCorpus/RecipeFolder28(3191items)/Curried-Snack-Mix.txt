Title: Curried Snack Mix
Categories: Snacks, Healthy
Yield: 3 cups
1 tb Margarine, melted
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Curry powder - or more
3    Plain rice cakes, broken
Into bite size pieces
1 c  Bite size corn chex
3/4 c  Pretzel sticks, halved
In a 13 x 9 x 12 baking pan stir together broken rice cakes, corn
cereal and pretzels.

In a custard cup stir together melted margarine, wochestershire sauce
and curry powder.  Drizzle mixture over cereal mixture.  Toss cereal
mixture until coated.

Bake in a 300 degree oven for 20 minutes, stirring twice.

If desired, store cooled mixture in a tightly covered container.

Makes six 1/2 cup servings

74 cal; 1 g protein, 12 carbo, 2 g fat, 0 mg chol, 176 sodium
