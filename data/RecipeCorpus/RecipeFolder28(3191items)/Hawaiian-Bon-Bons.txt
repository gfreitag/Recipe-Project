Title: Hawaiian Bon Bons
Categories: Chocolate
Yield: 25 servings
1/2 c  Coconut flakes
1 ts Orange zest; grated (optiona
8 oz White chocolate, imported
-- cut-up
1 c  Macadamia nuts, unsalted
Recipe by: 365 Great Chocolate Desserts - ISBN 0-06-016537-5
Preparation Time: 0:14 1. In a 1-qt glass bowl, heat white chocolate
in microwave on Medium 2 mins; stir. Return to microwave and heat on
Medium 1 1/2 to 2 mins longer, or until melted and smooth when
stirred.

2. Stir in nuts, coconut, and orange zest. Carefully sppon into 25 (1
1/2-inch) paper bon bon cups, fill to top. Let stand at room
temperature, or refrigerate 1 hour, until firm. Store, well wrapped,
in refrigerator.
