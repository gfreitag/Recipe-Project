Title: Rice Pudding 4
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 t  Vanilla
1/4 t  Salt
1 x  Cinnamon
1/2 c  Rice
1 qt Milk
1/3 c  Butter
3 ea Eggs, beaten
Mix rice with 2 cups of the milk in top of a double boiler.  Cook
over hot water until tender.  Add butter.  Combine eggs, sugar,
vanilla, salt and remaining milk.  Stir into hot rice mixture.  Pour
into a greased 1-1/2 quart baking dish.  Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Set
in pan and fill half full of warm water.  Bake in a moderately low
oven 325 for 25 minutes or until set.
