Title: Oven Fried Chicken  RBTN28A
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Thyme
1/4 c  Margarine
3 lb Chicken cut up
1/3 c  Flour
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Salt
Melt margarine in baking dish. Mix dry ingredients & roll chicken in
it. Then dredge chicken in margarine. Place chicken on rack in baking
dish skin side down. Bake 425F 30 minutes then turn chicken over &
cook 15 minutes or until done.
