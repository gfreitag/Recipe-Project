Title: Sausage Supreme
Categories: Pasta, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
6 oz Cheddar cheese; grated
2 c  Rigatoni
1/2 lb Italian sausage
2 T  Red pepper; chopped
2 T  Green pepper; chopped
Cook rigatoni according to package directions; drain. Crumble sausage
and cook in large skillet until browned; drain off excess fat. Stir
in red and green peppers, milk, soup and rigatoni. Turn into 2 qt
baking dish. Top with cheddar cheese and bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
Let stand 5 minutes. Source: Wegmans Rigatoni box
