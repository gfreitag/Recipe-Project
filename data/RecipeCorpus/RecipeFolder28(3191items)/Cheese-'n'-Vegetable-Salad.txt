Title: Cheese 'n' Vegetable Salad
Categories: Fruit, Vegetables, Dips/spread, Mom's
Yield: 6 servings

1 c  Coarsely grated process
-sharp cheese
6 cn Or cored fresh, pear halves
6 cn Or fresh apricot halves
3 1/2 c  Hot water
2 pk Lime-flavored gelatin
1 c  Shredded cabbage
1/2 c  Diced celery
Pour hot water over gelatin- stir until dissolved. Chill stirring
occasionally, till consistency of unbeaten egg white. Add cabbage,
celery, and cheese. Pour into 1 1/4-qt. mold. Chill till firm.
Unmold; garnish with crisp lettuce and pear and apricot halves. Serve
with mayonnaise or cooked salad dressing. Makes 6 servings.
