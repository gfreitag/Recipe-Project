Title: Chip Beef Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
3 tb Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Grated American cheese
Pepper to taste
1 pk Macaroni, 8 oz., cook in
Unsalted water
3 tb Butter
1/4 lb Chipped beef
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
Cook and drain macaroni.  In a skillet put butter, beef and green
peppers, stir in flour a little at a time.  Mix until smooth; then
add milk. Cook until thick, add cheese and melt.  Pour sauce over
macaroni. Mix. Put in a buttered casserole dish.  Bake at 350 degrees
for 40 minutes. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   03-11-95
