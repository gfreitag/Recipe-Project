Title: Rich Fudge Brownies - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 ----------
3/4 c  Egg Beaters
1/4 c  Promise Ultra fatfree
-margarine
1/4 c  Light corn syrup
1/4 c  Cocoa
1/4 c  Hershey's Chocolate Syrup
1 1/2 t  Vanilla
1/2 c  Grape Nuts Cereal, optional
1/2 ea Jar Braum's or Smucker's
-fatfree fudge topping
1 c  Packed brown sugar
OR
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
AND
2 t  Sweet 'n Low Brown
3/4 c  Sugar
OR
1/2 c  Sugar
AND
3 ea Pk Sweet 'n Low
1 c  Flour
In a large bowl, combine sugars, flour, Egg Beaters, Promise Ultra,
corn syrup, Hershey's syrup and cocoa and mix until well blended.
Stir in vanilla and Grape Nuts if desired. Spread into a 13 x 9 x 2"
pan that has been sprayed with a nonfat cooking spray. Bake at 350F.
until done. Cool in pan on wire rack. Instead of adding Grape Nuts,
you can also reserve them and sprinkle them on top of the frosted
brownines. Frost with fudge topping. Per sugar serving: 82 cal., 0.2g
fat (2%), 0mg chol., 0g fiber, 0g pro., 20g carb., 28mg sod. Per S&L
serving: 65 cal., 16g carb. Butter Busters by Pam Mycoskie ISBN
