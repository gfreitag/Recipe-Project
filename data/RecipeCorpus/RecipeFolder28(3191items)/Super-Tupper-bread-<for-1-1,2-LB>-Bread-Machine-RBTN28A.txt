Title: Super Tupper bread <for 1 1/2 LB> Bread Machine RBTN28A
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 1 1/2#loaf
1 ts Salt
4 tb Margarine,Corn Oil Etc.
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Water
2 ts Bread machine Yeast
3 c  Better for bread flour
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
Add ingredients in order listed. All ingredients should be room
temperature. Fleishman Bread machine yeast in jar recommended. Gold
star Better for bread flour recommended. Also recomend careful
measurements ie; Spoon the flour in Measure cup & level off with a
knife (avoids packing flour). Dipping the flour with measure cup
packs it in & results in too much flour.
