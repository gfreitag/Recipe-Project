Title: LEOTA'S CASSEROLE  RBTN28A
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Milk
12 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
3 c  Mashed sweet potatoes
3/4 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs
3/4 sl Stick oleo
Mix all ingredients and bake in 400F oven for 25 minutes. Melt 3/4
stick oleo, 1/2 cup sugar, 3/4 cup corn flakes, 1/2 cup nuts. Spread
on top, bake another 10 minutes until bubbly.
