Title: 814742 West African Peanut Chicken
Categories: African, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
-simmering)
1/2 c  Crunchy peanut butter
1    (6 oz.) can tomato paste
-Salt, pepper and red
-cayenne Pepper to taste
-Chopped peanuts
3 lb Chicken breasts or thighs
1/2 c  Cooking oil
2 md Onions, chopped
2    To 3 garlic cloves, chopped
-SAUCE:
2 c  Chicken broth (heated to
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Remove chicken skins; wash and pat dry.
Brown chicken in oil, a few pieces at a time, and remove from heat.
Brown onion and garlic; remove from heat.  Place chicken, onion, and
garlic in casserole dish.  In a medium bowl, blend chicken broth,
peanut butter, and tomato paste.  Add seasonings and chopped peanuts;
pour over chicken. Bake covered, 45 minutes to 1 hour or until
chicken is tender. Serve over hot cooked rice.
