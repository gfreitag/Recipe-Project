Title: Peanutty Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Sugar, brown; firmly packed
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Marshmallows
18 oz Sweet potatoes; drained
1/2 c  Peanut butter; smooth or
-crunchy
1/2 c  Orange juice
Put sweet potatoes into bowl and mash; stir in peanut butter, orange
juice, brown sugar, salt, and pepper.  Spoon mixture into a buttered
9-inch piepan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes.

Remove from oven and place marshmallows on top.  Return to oven for
about 5 minutes or until marshmallows are brown and melting.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, May 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
