Title: PEPPERED SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Beef poaching liquid *
1/4 ts Coarse ground black pepper
2 ts Cornstarch
1 ea Bay leaf
Dissolve cornstarch in one tablespoon poaching liquid. Combine
cornstarch mixture, remaining poaching liquid, bay leaf & pepper.
Cook & stir over medium heat until clear & slightly thickened, about
3 to 5 minutes. Remove bay leaf. (Yield: 3/4 cup) * strained &
divided. ** Sauce for POACHED BEEF CHUCK ROAST.
