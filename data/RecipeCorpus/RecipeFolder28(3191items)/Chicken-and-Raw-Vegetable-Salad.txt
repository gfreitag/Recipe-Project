Title: Chicken and Raw Vegetable Salad
Categories: Vegetables, Condiments, Mom's, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: MOM'S RECIPE CLIPPIN---------------------
ds Freshly ground pepper
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 t  Herb 'N' Spice
Wine Vinegar
2 c  Cooked shredded chicken
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Shredded carrots
2 T  Minced parsley
Remove bones, skin, fat, and gristle from chicken. Combine with
celery, carrots, parsley, seasonings and mayonnaise. Serve on a bed
of crisp lettuce and watercress.
