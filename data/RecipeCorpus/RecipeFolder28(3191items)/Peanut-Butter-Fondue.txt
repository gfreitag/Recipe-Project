Title: Peanut Butter Fondue
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 c  Margarine
1/8 ts Salt
12 oz Peanut butter, crunchy
5 oz Milk, evaporated
1 c  Sugar, brown; firmly packed
Combine all ingredients and keep warm in fondue pot.  Serve with apple
wedges and chunks of French bread.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, May 1974. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
