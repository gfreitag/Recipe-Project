Title: Easy Pralines
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Nuts, chopped
1 pk Graham crackers
2    Sticks of margarine
1/2 c  Sugar
Place small squares of graham crackers on a cookie sheet.  Bring
sugar and margarine to boil, stirring constantly.  Boil 2 minutes,
remove from heat. Add vanilla and nuts.  Spread over graham crackers.
Bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees.  Remove from pan while warm.
