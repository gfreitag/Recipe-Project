Title: Chicken Broccoli Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 ts Curry powder
1 pk Crumbled Cheddar cheese
3    Boxes of frozen broccoli
4    Chicken breasts, cooked and
Diced
2 cn Cream of chicken soup
Layer broccoli and chicken in a 9 x 13 inch casserole dish.  Mix the
remaining ingredients well and pour over chicken.  Bake at 375
degrees for 40 minutes.  Serve with spiced apple rings or spiced
peaches and a hot roll. Randy Rigg
