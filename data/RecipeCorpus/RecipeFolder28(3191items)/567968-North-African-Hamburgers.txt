Title: 567968 North African Hamburgers
Categories: African, Beef
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Chopped parsley
1    Egg
1 T  Dijon mustard
1 c  Water
2 lb Ground sirloin
1 md Chopped onion
1 t  Paprika
1 t  Cumin
2 t  Bread crumbs
Mix all ingredients together.  Form into patties and grill on
moderately hot grill.  Serve on pita bread or hamburger bun.
