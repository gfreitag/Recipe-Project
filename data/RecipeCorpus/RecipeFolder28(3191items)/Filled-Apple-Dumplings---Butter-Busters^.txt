Title: Filled Apple Dumplings - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Desserts
Yield: 6 ----------
1 t  Cinnamon
3/4 c  Liquid Butter Buds
6 ea Apples, cored
3/4 c  Grated nonfat Cheddar cheese
1/4 c  (to 1/2) Grape Nuts
6 lg Flour tortillas
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
OR
1 c  Brown sugar
AND
2 t  Sweet 'n Low Brown
2 c  Water
Boil sugar, water, cinnamon and liquid butter Buds for 5 minutes. Fill
apples with a mixture of cheese and Grape Nuts. Rex\serve 2
tablespoons Grape Nuts for topping. Place apples in tortillas stem
side down. Pull edges to seal with warm water. Place in a 9 1/2 x 11"
casserole dish that has been sprayed with Pam. Pour syrup over apples
Sprinkle with remaining Grape Nuts. Bake at 350F 30-40 minutes. Serve
warm. These can be topped with frozen nonfat yogurt or Dream Whip.
Per sugar serving: 471 cal., 3.6g fat (7%), 0mg chol., 6g fiber, 6g
pro., 110g carb., 298mg sod. Per S&L serving: 410 cal., 94g carb.
