Title: Cheese Grits - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Side dish
Yield: 16 ----------
1 1/2 c  Egg Beaters, beaten
1 ea Tb garlic powder
1 ea Tb Tabasco sauce
2 cn (4oz) chopped green chiles,
-drained, optional
4 c  Grits
4 qt Boiling water
2 lb Nonfat Cheddar cheese,
-grated
1 c  Lliquid Butter Buds
Cook grits in boiling water 15-20 minutes. Add cheese, Butter Buds,
Egg Beaters, garlic powder and Tabasco sauce. Stir in chopped green
chiles if desired. Pour into a large glass casserole that has been
sprayed with a nonstick cooking spray. Bake at 375F. for 30-40
minutes or until bubbly and cheese is melted. Per serving: 67 ca;.,
0.1g fat (2%), 2mg chol., 0g fiber, 7g pro., 9g carb., 213mg sod.
