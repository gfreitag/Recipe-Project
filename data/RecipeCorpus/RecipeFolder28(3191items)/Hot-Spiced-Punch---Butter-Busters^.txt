Title: Hot Spiced Punch - Butter Busters^
Categories: Healthy, Beverages
Yield: 12 ----------
AND
1 t  Sweet 'n Low Brown
1 t  Ground cloves
1/2 t  Ground allspice
1/2 t  Ground cinnamon
1/4 t  Ground nutmeg
1 ea Rum, optional
1 cn (48oz) cranberry juice
-cocktail
1 c  Water
1 cn (46oz) pineapple jiuce

OR

Combine all ingredients in a large pot and mix well. Bring to a boil,
stirring occasionally. Reduce heat and simmer about 10 minutes. One
half ounce rum can be added to each mug before pouring if desired.
