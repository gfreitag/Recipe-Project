Title: MicroWave Peanut Brittle  RBTN28A
Categories: Candies
Yield: 10 servings
1 c  Mason's raw peanuts
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Drops O'Gold vanilla
1/2 c  Griffins white syrup
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Butter
1 ts Baking soda
In 1 1/2 quart casserole stir together syrup, sugar, raw peanuts and
salt. Microwave on high for 4 minutes. Stir and cook 4 minutes more.
Add butter and vanilla to syrup mixture, blending well. Microwave on
high for 1 minute. Add bakin soda and gently stir until light and
foamy. Pour onto sheet of aluminum foil. Let cool 1 hour. When cool,
break into small pieces...
