Title: 505256 Yugoslavian Kumarcna Solata
Categories: East europe, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
2 t  Salt
8    Salad potatoes
4 md Cucumbers
2 md Onions, finely chopped
1    Clove garlic, minced
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/4 t  Pepper
-Add more salt, if needed
1/4 c  Salad oil
2 T  Vinegar
Wash, peel and slice cucumbers thin.  Add onions, garlic and sprinkle
with salt; let stand 1 hour.  Then squeeze the liquid from the
cucumber mixture. Boil potatoes, peel and slice when cool.  Add to
cucumber. Toss lightly with dressing.  Mix lightly and toss with
potatoes and cucumbers. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-05-95
