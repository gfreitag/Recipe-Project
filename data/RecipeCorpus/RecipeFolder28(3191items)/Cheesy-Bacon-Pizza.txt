Title: Cheesy Bacon Pizza
Categories: Italian
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 ts Dried basil, crushed
ds Pepper
3 oz Canadian bacon, chopped
3/4 c  Monterey jack cheese,
Shredded
2    8 inch Italian Bread Shells
(pizza crust) in bread
Section
8    Tomato slices
1/4 ts Dried oregano, crushed
Preheat oven to 450

Place bread shells on cookie sheet.  Top with tomatoes, sprinkle with
spices.  Top with Canadian bacon and sprinkle with cheese.

Bake 8 minutes or until cheese is melted.

May substitute pita bread rounds if necessary.
