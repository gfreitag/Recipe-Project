Yield: 6 servings

1 ea Drained No. 303 can fruit
-cocktail
1 ea Diced small banana
3/4 c  Raspberries or sliced
-strawberries
Fruit cocktail plus.
1 1/4 c  Hot water
1 pk Lemon-flavored gelatin
1/2 c  Syrup drained from fruit
-cocktail
Pour hot water over gelatin- stir until dissolved- then stir in syrup.
Chill, stirring occasionally, till consistency of unbeaten egg white-
fold in fruits. Pour into 6 to 9 individual molds. Chill until firm.
Unmold on greens; serve with Lemon-Cream Mayonnaise. Makes 6 to 9
servings.
