Title: NO BAKE COOKIES  RBTN28A
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Coconut
3 c  Quick oats
2 c  Sugar
1 ea Stick butter
1/2 c  Cocoa
Combine sugar, butter, cocoa and milk in saucepan and boil 2 minutes.
Remove from heat; add coconut and oats. Drop on wax or brown paper
and let cool. Makes about 2 dozen cookies..
