Title: Caraway Brandy (or Vodka)
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 batch
3 oz Sugar
1    Bottle brandy or vodka
1 oz Caraway seeds
Bruise the seeds and put them into the spirit with the sugar.  Cap
firmly, shake well and leave in a warm place for a few days to
dissolve the sugar. Infuse for a month, then strain through double
muslin and rebottle.

from Edible Gifts by Claire Clifton and Martina Nicolls typed by
