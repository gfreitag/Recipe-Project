Title: Brown bag carmelcorn
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Karo syrup
1/4 t  Salt
1/2 t  Baking soda
1 t  Vanilla
1    Brown shopping bag 1/3
-full of popped corn
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Nucoa
Brown paper shopping bag 1/3 full of popped corn (Do not use
commercial bagged popcorn...use plain old fashioned home made...no
butter) Bag should be large size shopping bag make syrup... melt
nucoa, mix in brown sugar, Karo, salt...place in microwave safe bowl
boil hard for 2 min (takes about five minutes total in microwave Add
1/2 t baking soda Add 1 t vanilla stir till it foams. Pour over
popcorn in sack, fold down top of sack microwave sack on high 1/2
minute Shake sack ( or stir) Put back in microwave 1 1/2 min Roll
down side of sack to cool Fast easy recipe requiring very little
clean up and kids love it, plus it is good! Submitted By ELINOR MYREN
