Title: Spinach Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Olive Oil
1 ts Water
1 ts Dijon Mustard
1 ts Honey
2 c  Torn Spinach Leaves
2 c  Torn Red Leaf Lettuce
1/4 c  Thinly Sliced Radishes
1 tb White Wine Vinegar
Combine Lettuce, Spinach Leaves & Radishes in A Large Bowl.  Toss Well.
Combine Vinegar, Oil, Water, Mustard & Honey. Stir Well.  Drizzle Over
Salad.  Toss Well.
