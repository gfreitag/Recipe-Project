Title: Tuxedo Cheesecake
Categories: Cheesecakes
Yield: 6 servings
30    Seconds.
2/3 c  SOUR CREAM
1 ts VANILLA
2/3 c  SEMI-SWEET CHOCOLATE, melted
3 tb ORANGE FLAVORED LIQUEUR
-(Curacao) (Opt)
1 ts ORANGE PEEL, grated
-(optional)
1/4 c  HEAVY CREAM
2 tb CORNSTARCH
1 1/4 lb OREOS, crushed
6 tb BUTTER, melted
Mix and pat into bottom and
-sides of springform pan.
CAKE
32 oz CREAM CHEESE, unwrapped and
-warmed in
Microwave for 2 minutes.
1 c  SUGAR
6    JUMBO EGGS, unshelled and
-warmed in microwave
** CRUST
