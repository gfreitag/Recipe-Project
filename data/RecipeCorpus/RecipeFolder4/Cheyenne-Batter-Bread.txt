Title: Cheyenne Batter Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
3    Eggs, separated
1 qt Sweet milk
1 tb Melted butter
1 pt White cornmeal
Bring milk to a full boil; stir in cornmeal slowly.  Cool.  Add well-beaten
egg yolks, melted butter and salt.  Add stiffly beaten egg whites. Bake in
moderate oven - 375 until done.
