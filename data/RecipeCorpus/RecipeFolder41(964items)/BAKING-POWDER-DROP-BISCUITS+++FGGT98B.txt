Title: BAKING POWDER DROP BISCUITS+++FGGT98B
Categories: Master mix, Biscuits, Jewel
Yield: 9 Servings
1 1/2 c  Bake-it-all mix*
1/2 c  Milk
*Do not pack
Make a well in the Mix.  Add milk and stir with a fork until all
of the Mix is moistened.  Drop dough by spoonfuls onto a cooky sheet.
Bake in a 450 F. oven 10 to 12 minutes. Yield: 9 to 10 biscuits NOTE:
for 6 biscuits, use 1 cup Mix with about 1/3 cup milk.
