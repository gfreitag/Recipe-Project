Title: BAGEL & EGGS
Categories: Eggs/cheese, Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
3    Bagels
1 tb Butter
6    Eggs
1/2 c  Shredded low-fat cheese
(Danbo or cheddar style)
1    Green onion, sliced
Pinch each salt and pepper
Cut bagels in half to make 2 rings.  If necessary, trim to make holes
about 1 inch round.  Spread butter over cut sides; place, cut side
up, on well greased rimmed baking sheet. Broil for 2 minutes or until
golden.

Turn bagels over; break egg into center of each. Sprinkle cheese,
onion, salt and pepper over each egg; bake in 350Ã¸F oven for 12 to 15
minutes or until whites are set and yolks are slightly runny. Makes 6
