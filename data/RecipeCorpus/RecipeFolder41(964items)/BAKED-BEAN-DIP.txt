Title: BAKED BEAN DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Sauces/dips
Yield: 5 Cups
1    Cream cheese; softened
1    Sour cream
1    Refried beans
2 tb Taco spice
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
6 dr Tabasco suace
6 dr Tabasco sauce
2 1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded and
- divided
MMMMM-----------------------ACCOMPANIMENTS----------------------------
1    Large Fritos
Combine all ingredients except 1/2 cup of the shredded cheddar cheese.
Spread into an 8x8x2 pan.  Sprinkle remaining cheese over the top.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.  Serve with large Fritos. Contributor:
Carol Osenga
