Title: BAKE SALE LEMON BARS
Categories: Cookies, Bars
Yield: 4 Dozen
1 1/2 c  Flour
2/3 c  Powdered sugar
3/4 c  Margarine; softened
3    Eggs; lightly beaten
1 1/2 c  Granulated sugar
3 tb Flour
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Additional powdered sugar
Combine 1-1/2 cups flour, sugar and butter; pat into a greased 13x9x2"
baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Meanwhile in a bowl,
whisk eggs, sugar, 3Tbsp. flour and lemon juice until frothy; pour
over the hot crust.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until
light golden brown. Cool on a wire rack. Dust with powdered sugar.
Cut into squares.

Source:  Taste of Home mag, June/July 1996
