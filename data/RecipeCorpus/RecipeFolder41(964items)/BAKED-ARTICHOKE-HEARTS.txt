Title: BAKED ARTICHOKE HEARTS
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
1    13-oz can whole artichoke
-hearts
3 oz Cream cheese -OR- Boursin
-cheese
1/2 c  Garlic butter, melted
3/4 c  Parmesan cheese, grated
3/4 c  Bread crumbs
1/4 c  Pine nuts, toasted
Open artichoke heart centers gently. Stuff with cream cheese. Dip into
butter, then mixture of Parmesan cheese and crumbs. Press pine nuts
into cheese. Place on foil-lined baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees
for 30 minutes. May flavor cream cheese with any favorite herbs.

Approx per serving:  Cal 251; Prot 6 g; Carbo 11 g; Fiber <1 g; Fat
20g;
72% Calories from Fat; Chol 49 mg; Sod 457 mg.
