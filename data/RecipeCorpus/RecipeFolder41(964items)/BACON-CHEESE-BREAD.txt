Title: BACON CHEESE BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Tap water
3 c  Flour
1/2 c  Cheese (grated, sharp)
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 tb Butter
1 1/2 ts Dry yeast
3 tb Bacon bits
Add the bacon bits when beeping sounds during kneading process. Place
ingredients into pan according to manufacturer's directions. Press
Start.
