Title: BALSAMIC FIG SAUCE
Categories: June 1996 i, Marinades, Sauces, Salsas
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Fresh figs -- preferably
Black
Mission, chopped
1/3 c  Red wine
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 pn Salt
1/8 ts Freshly ground pepper
1/2 ts Sugar
1    Sprig fresh thyme
Combine all ingredients with 1/2 cup water in a saucepan. Cover;
bring to boil over high heat. Reduce to a simmer; cook, partially
covered, until fruit has broken down, about 20 to 30 minutes. Let
cool slightly; remove thyme sprig. Press mixture through a
large-holed sieve with a rubber spatula.
