Title: BAKED LASAGNA
Categories: Italian, Beef, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Onion, minced
1    Clove garlic menced
1 lb Ground beef
2 ts Shortening
1 md Can whole tomatoes
1 cn Tomato sauce
1 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 lb Lasagna noodles
2 1/2 qt Boiling water
1/2 lb Sliced Mozzarella cheese
1 c  Cream cottage cheese
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese
Saute onion, garlic, and meat in shortening about 20 minutes. Add
next four ingredients. with 1 tsp salt. Simmer for 40 minutes. Cook
noodles with 1 tbsp salt for 20 minutes; drain. Put 1/3 meat mixture
in baking dish. Add layer of noodles, add 1/2 cottage cheese, 1/3
Mozzarella, and 1/2 Cheddar. Repeat layers, ending with remaining
meat mixture and top with remaining 1/3 Mozzarella cheese.
