Title: BACON & CHIVE WHITE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Microwave, Breakfast, Dairy, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
2 sl Bacon, cooked and crumbled
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 tb All-purpose flour
1 c  Milk
1 ts Chopped chives
1/2 ts Salt
1. Prepare bacon and set aside.

2. Melt butter in a 1-quart glass measuring picher in mirowave oven
(about 30 seconds at High).  Add flour; mix until smooth. Add milk
gradually, stirring constantly.

3. Cook uncovered in microwave oven 4 minutes at High, or until
thickened; stir after 2 minutes, then every
30    seconds.

4. Add crumbled bacon, chives and salt; stir.  Serve over fresh
vegetables.

Yield: About 1 cup.

SOURCE: Adventures in Microwave Cooking
