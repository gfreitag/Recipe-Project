Title: BAKED CARROTS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
18 ea Carrots, small
1/3 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/3 ts Cinnamon
1/3 c  Boiling water
Approx. Cook Time: 1:30 Scrape
Fat grams    per serving:
or pare carrots and place in casserole. Cream butter, sugar, salt and
cinnamon together; add water and blend well.  Pour over carrots,
cover and bake in moderate 350F oven 1 1/2 hours.
