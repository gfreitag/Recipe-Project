Title: BACONY CREAMED SPINACH
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 4 Servings
2 pk Frozen spinach (10oz. ea)
6    Bacon strips
1    Garlic clove,minced
1/2 c  Onion,chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts White pepper
1 pn Cayenne pepper
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1 c  Half-and-half or milk
1. Cook spinach by boiling or in microwave. Drain well and squeeze
out as much moisture as possible. Chop fine, using food processor of
food grinder.
2. Cook bacon in skillet until crisp. Remove and drain. Crumble and
reserve. In about 2 T of the bacon fat, saute garlic and onion over
medium heat. When onion is limp, add spinach. Lower heat. Season with
salt and peppers; stir well as moisture evaporates from spinach,
about 1 minute. Remove from heat.
3. In a separate large skillet, melt butter over medium heat.
Sprinkle in flour, stirring constantly until smooth. Graduallly stir
in half-and-half or milk and cook until smooth and bubbly. Stir in
spinach mixture and heat through. Remove from heat, stir in reserved
bits of bacon, and serve.
