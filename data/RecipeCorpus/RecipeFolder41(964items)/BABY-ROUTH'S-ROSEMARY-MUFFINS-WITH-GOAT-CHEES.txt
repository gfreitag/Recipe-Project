Title: BABY ROUTH'S ROSEMARY MUFFINS WITH GOAT CHEES
Categories: Breads, Herbs
Yield: 12 Servings
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Golden raisins
2 ts Baking powder
1 tb Chopped fresh rosemary
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Unsalted butter
1 lg Egg
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
8 tb Goat cheese
Rosemary, golden rasins, and a creamy goat cheese center make these
muffins

DIRECTIONS: =========== Simmer milk, raisins, and rosemary in a
small sauc Let cool.

Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.  Beat egg into cooked milk
mixture. Add to dry ingredients and mix lightly just until dry
ingredients are moist

(65 min left), (H)elp, More?   Serve muffins hot or cool. If
desired, a 3 * Source: The Herb Garden Cookbook, by Lucinda Hutson *
