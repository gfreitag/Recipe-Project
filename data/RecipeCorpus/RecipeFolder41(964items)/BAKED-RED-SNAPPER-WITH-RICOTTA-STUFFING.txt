Title: BAKED RED SNAPPER WITH RICOTTA STUFFING
Categories: Main dishes, Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lb To 4 lb red snapper, striped
-bass or similar fish
Salt
2 tb Olive oil
MMMMM--------------------------STUFFING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
1/8 c  Olive oil
3/4 c  Ricotta cheese
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Lemon rind -- grated
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/4 c  Lemon -- diced and peeled
1 ts Paprika
Clean, wash and dry fish.  Sprinkle inside and out with salt.

Prepare stuffing: Saute celery and onion in 1/4 cup olive oil until
soft. Remove from heat and combine with rest of stuffing ingredients,
blending well.  Stuff fish loosely. Close opening with small skewers
or toothpicks. Place in well-greased baking pan.  Brush with 2 tbsp.
olive oil and bake in pre-heated 350 degree oven for 40-60 minutes or
until fish flakes easily with a fork.  Baste occassionally with
drippings while fish is baking.

: Gilda Latzky
