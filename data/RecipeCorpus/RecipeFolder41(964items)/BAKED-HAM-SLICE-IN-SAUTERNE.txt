Title: BAKED HAM SLICE IN SAUTERNE
Categories: Meats, Ham
Yield: 8 Servings
-Sue Woodward
MMMMM-----------------AMERICAN HEART ASSOC. CKBK----------------------
2 lb Center cut ham slice
1/2 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
3 tb Cornstarch
1 1/2 c  -Water
1 tb Margarine
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  American sauterne; or other
-dry white wine
Preheat oven to 350~.  Place ham in a shallow baking pan. Set aside.
Combine sugar and cornstarch in a saucepan. Stir in water and
margarine. Place over med-high heat and cook, stirring constantly, 5
mins. Remove pan from heat and stir in raisins and wine. Pour the
mixture over ham. Bake uncovered 45 mins. or until tender.

Per serving - 291 cal, 24 g pro, 31 g carbo, 60 mg chol, 1391 mg sod,
8 g tot fat (2 g sat, 1 g poly, 4 g mono)

Note - one serving of this dish provides most of the recommended
daily amt. of sodium.  Enjoy it and try to select low sodium foods
for the rest of the day's meals
