Title: BAKED CHICKEN BURGERS
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Chicken, cooked -- cut up
1 tb Onion, minced -- instant
1 c  Celery -- chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Cheese, american, processed
1/8 ts Pepper
6 ea Burger bun -- split and
Butter
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
Heat oven to 350.  In bowl, mix all ingredients except buns. Fill
buns with chicken mixture.  Wrap each sandwich in square of aluminum
foil; place on baking sheet. Bake 20 minutes or tilhot.
