Title: BAILEYÕS IRISH CREAM BARS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 32 Servings
For Cake:
1 pk Yellow cake mix, Moist
-Deluxe
3    Eggs
2/3 c  Bailey's Irish Cream
1/3 c  Oil
For Swirl:
1 cn Cream cheese frosting,
-Vanilla Creamy
1 pk White chocolate chips
1/4 c  Bailey's Irish Cream
Preheat oven to 350¡. Prepare a jelly roll pan (about 15" x 10" x1").
Mix cake mix with eggs, Irish Cream and oil. Set aside 1/2 cup of
prepared cake batter for swirl. Pour cake batter into prepared pan.

In a small bowl, blend all swirl ingredients. Drop large spoonfuls of
this mixture over the cake batter. Swirl with a fork to make a marble
effect. Bake 25 - 30 minutes. Cool and cut into squares. From: Sandy
