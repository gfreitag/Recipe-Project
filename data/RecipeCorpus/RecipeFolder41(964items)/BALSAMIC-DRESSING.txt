Title: BALSAMIC DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Marinades, Low-fat, Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
1    Shallot; finely chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Freshly ground pepper
Combine all ingredients in a jar. Close tightly, and shake
vigorously.
Source: Southern Living, November, 1995
