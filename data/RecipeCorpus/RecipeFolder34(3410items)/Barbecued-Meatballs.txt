Title: Barbecued Meatballs
Categories: Bbq/grill, Beef, Country wom
Yield: 80 servings
MMMMM-------------------------MEATBALLS------------------------------
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 ts Chili powder
3 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1 cn Evaporated milk -- 12 oz
1 c  Oatmeal
1 c  Cracker meal -- crumbs
2 ea Eggs
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
To taste
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 c  Ketchup
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Liquid smoke flavoring -- or
To make meatballs, combine all ingredients. (mixture will be soft) and
shape into walnut-size balls. Place meatballs in a single layer on wax
paper-lined cookie sheets; freeze until solid. Store frozen meatballs
in freezer bags until ready to cook. To Make sauce, combine all
ingredients and stir until sugar is dissolved. Place frozen meatballs
in a 13x9x2 inch baking pan; pour on the sauce. Bake at 350 deg. for
1 hour. Yield: 80 meatballs.
