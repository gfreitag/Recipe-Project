Title: Meltaway Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 7 dozen
2 c  (12 oz) chocolate chips
Powdered sugar for dusting
(optional)
2 c  Butter (1 lb.)
1/2 c  Sugar
4 c  Unsifted flour
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cream butter and sugar in large bowl
until light. Gradually add flour, then stir in chips. Roll well
rounded teaspoonfuls of dough into balls and place on ungreased
cookie sheets. Bake 12-14 minutes, cookies will still be pale. Let
stand 2 minutes before removing from cookie sheet, and dust with
powdered sugar if desired. Submitted By JANE KNOX   On   09-13-95
