Title: MANDARIN ALMOND SPICE MUFFINS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Muffins, Breads, Breakfast
Yield: 6 muffins
MMMMM--------------------------MUFFINS-------------------------------
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter, melted
1 lg Egg
1/2 ts Almond extract
1 cn (11-oz) mandarin orange
-segments, drained and
-chopped
3/4 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
3/4 c  Unsifted whole-wheat flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Chopped slivered almonds
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground allspice
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
3 tb Butter, melted
1/4 c  Sugar
1. Prepare Muffins: Heat oven to 400'F. Grease six 3-inch muffin-pan
cups. In large bowl, combine both flours, the sugar, almonds, baking
powder, salt, allspice, and nutmeg.

2. In small bowl, with fork, beat milk, butter, egg, and almond
extract until well mixed. Add milk mixture to flour mixture and stir
lightly just until moistened. With rubber spatula, gently stir
mandarin or- anges into the mixture, or batter.

3. Divide batter among prepared cups. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until
light golden brown. Cool muffins in pan 5 minutes. Remove from cups
to wire rack.

4. Meanwhile, prepare Topping: Place melted butter in small bowl or
cup. In another small bowl, combine sugar and cinnamon. While muffins
are still warm, dip tops in butter and then in sugar mixture. Serve
muffins warm.

Nutrition information per muffin-protein: 8 grams; fat: 21 grams;
carbohydrate: 60 grams, fiber: 5 grams; sodium: 427 milligrams;
cholesterol: 74 milligrams; calories: 447.
