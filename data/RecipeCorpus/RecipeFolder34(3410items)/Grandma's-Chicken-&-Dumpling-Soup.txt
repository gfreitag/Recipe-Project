Title: Grandma's Chicken & Dumpling Soup
Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------------CHLCKEN BR0TH----------------------------
6    Peppercorns
3    Whole cloves
3 lb Chicken -- cut up
6 c  Cold water
3    Chicken
MMMMM-------------------------SOUP BASE------------------------------
1 c  Chopped patatoes
1 sm Bay leaf
1 c  Peas
1    Cooked fryer, cut in
Bite-size pieces
Reserved chicken broth,
Strained
1 ts Seasoned salt
1 cn Chicken broth -- l0-3/4-oz
1 cn Cream chicken soup --
-1 3/4 oz
1 cn Cream mushroom soup --
-10 3/4 oz
1 c  Chopped celery
1 1/2 c  Chopped carrots
1/4 c  Chopped onion
MMMMM---------------------FEATHER DUMPLINGS--------------------------
1    Egg -- well beaten
2 tb Melted butter
2/3 c  Milk
Boulllon cubes
2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Black pepper
Place fryer, water, bouillon, peppercorns and cloves in kettle and
bring to boil.Reduce heat; simmer until chicken is tender(about 1-1/2
hours). Cool chicken just slightly; cut into bite-size pieces and set
aside. Strain and skim chicken broth. Put reserved chicken and broth
in large kettle; add cans of broth, chicken and mushroom soups,
celery, carrots, onion, potatoes, bay leaf, peas and seasoned salt.
Put cover on kettle; simmer soup on low heat for 2-3 hours. About 30
minutes before serving, mix up feather dumplings by sifting dry
ingredients together. Add egg, melted butter and enough milk to make
moist, stiff batter. Drop by teaspoons into boiling liquid. Cook,
covered and without "peeking': for 18-20 minutes or until the
dumplings are done. Yield: 10-12 servings
