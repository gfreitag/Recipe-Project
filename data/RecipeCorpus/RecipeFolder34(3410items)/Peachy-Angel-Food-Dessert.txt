Title: Peachy Angel Food Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Softened
1 c  Confectioners' sugar
1 cn Peach filling
Or cherry pie 21 oz.
1 ea Prepared angel food cake
2    Envelopes whipped topping
-mix 1.3 oz. each
2 pk Cream cheese -- 3 oz. ea
Tear cake into bite-size pieces; set aside. Prepare whipped topping
according to package directions; set aside. In a large mixing bowl,
beat cream cheese and confectioners' sugar; fold in whipped topping.
Add cake pieces; stir gently to coat evenly. Spoon into a 13-in. x
9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Top with pie filling. Chill until ready to
serve. Yleld: 12 servings.
