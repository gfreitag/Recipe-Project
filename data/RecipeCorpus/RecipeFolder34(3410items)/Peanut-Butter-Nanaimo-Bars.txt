Title: Peanut Butter Nanaimo Bars
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 15 squares
MMMMM----------------------------BASE---------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Graham wafer crumbs
1/4 c  Coconut
1/2 c  Unsalted peanuts
1/2 c  Butter
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
MMMMM------------------------MIDDLE LAYER-----------------------------
2 c  Icing sugar
1/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Peanut butter
2 tb Butter
MMMMM-------------------------TOP LAYER------------------------------
1 tb Butter
4 ea Squares semi sweet chocolate
Base:  In a saucepan mix together butter, sugar, and beaten egg.
Cook over low heat for 5 minutes stirring constantly until smooth and
slightly thickened.  In a large bowl mix together graham wafer
crumbs, coconut and chopped peanuts.  Add the butter egg mixture and
mix well. Press evenly into an 8 inch square pan and chill for 15
minutes. Middle Layer: Beat together peanut butter, butter, icing
sugar, and milk until smooth. Spread over the base.  Chill for 30
minutes or until firm. Top Layer: Melt chocolate and butter together
over hot water or in microwave on medium for 2 to 3 minutes.  Spread
over middle layer. Chill until firm and then cut into squares. From
Empress Food Flair Recipe Pamphlet by Lucerne foods. * CROSSPOSTED
