Title: SEAFOOD MANDARIN
Categories: Main dish, Fish/seafoo, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
22 oz Mandarin orange segments;
--from can (reserve syrup)
3 c  Liquid (orange syrup &
Chicken broth)
1 ts Soy sauce
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Salt
3 c  Minute Rice; dry
4 tb Butter or margarine
2 tb Oil
2 sm Green pepper; seeded & cut
--into chunks
1 c  Scallions; diagonally sliced
2 lb Cod or Snapper fillet; cut
--into 1" chunks
Saute rice in butter until golden.  Add oil, green pepper and
scallions. Saute for 2 minutes.  Add fish and chicken broth mixture.
Stir gently. Stir in spices and soy sauce.  Bring to a full boil.
Cook, stirring, until fish is cooked.  (Cooked fish is opaque and any
bones come out easily.) Add orange sections.  Cover and remove from
heat. Let stand 5 minutes. Fluff with fork.  Garnish with scallion
greens.

Preparation Time:  30 minutes.
