Title: Mashkigimin-Onagan (Traditional Cranberry Sauce)
Categories: Desserts, Native amer, Algonquin, Fruit
Yield: 1 recipe
1 1/2 c  Birch sap -=OR=- spring
-- water
1 1/2 lb Cranberries
2 c  Maple sugar
Place all ingredients into a saucepan & bring to a boil.  Reduce heat
& simmer for 25 to 30 minutes.  Cool & serve.

Bernard Assiniwi, "Indian Recipes"
