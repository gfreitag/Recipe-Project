Title: ROYAL FROSTING 02 - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Holiday, Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 cups
1 ts Cream of tartar
2 lb Confectioners' sugar
6 lg Egg whites (see Note)
In medium-size bowl, combine confectioners' sugar, egg whites, and
cream of tartar. With electric mixer, beat until thick. Cover tightly
with a damp towel while using to keep from drying out.

Note: If frosting is to be eaten, use meringue powder and follow
package instructions for Royal Frosting. Meringue powder can be
purchased wherever cake-decorating supplies are sold or by mail from
Wilton Industries, Inc., 2240 W. 75th St., Woodridge, IL. 60517;
(708) 963 -7100 ext. 320.
