Title: Hawaiin Pineapple & Macadamia Loaf
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Oil
3/4 c  Coconut flakes
3/4 c  Pineapple chunks in juice --
-reserve juice
1/3 c  Pineapple juice
1/2 c  Water
1/3 c  Macadamia nuts -- chopped
2 ts Active dry yeast
2 3/4 c  Bread flour
1 tb Bread flour
3/4 c  Cracked wheat
3 tb Cornmeal
3 tb Brown sugar
3 tb Powdered milk
3/4 ts Ground ginger
Add all ingredients except the nuts in the order suggested by your
manual. Bake on raisin bread cycle.  At the beeper add nuts.
