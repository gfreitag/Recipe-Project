Title: Garden Pizza 1
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
Carrots
Broccoil
Celery
Parmesan cheese
1 lg Pack crescent rolls
Hidden valley ranch dresing
8 oz Cream cheese
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Grease 9 x l3 pan with butter.  Open rolls and lay in pan (long) pinch
perforations.  Bake 350 degrees for 8-l0 minutes. Mix 8 oz. cream
cheese, l pack (2 oz) Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing, l/2 cup mayo.
Spread on crescent dough.  Chop carrots, broccoli and celery fine and
spread on top. Sprinkle with grated parmesian cheese. Cut in squares
