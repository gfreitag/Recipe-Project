Title: Miniature Pizzas
Categories: Snacks, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1 cn Refrigerated biscuits -- 10
Pk
1 cn Tomato sauce -- small can
1 l  Ground beef
Preheat toaster oven to 475 degrees.  Roll biscuits out to 1/4"
thickness. Spread half the tomato sauce over top of each biscuit.
Cover with a layer of beef; sprinkle seasoning, onion and cheese over
beef. Top with remaining tomato sauce.  Place foil on rack and pizza
on foil. Bake in toaster oven 20 to 35 minutes or until brown.
