Title: MAPLE PECAN SCONES - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Breads
Yield: 8 scones
2/3 c  Vegetable shortening
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Maple syrup
1/2 ts Maple flavoring
3 1/2 c  Unsifted all purpose flour
1 c  Finely chopped pecans
4 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1. Heat oven to 425'F. Grease large baking sheet. In large bowl,
combine flour, pecans, baking powder, and salt. With pastry blender
or 2 knives, cut the shortening in until mixture resembles coarse
crumbs.

2. Add milk, 1/3 C of maple syrup, and the maple flavoring to dry
ingredients and mix lightly with fork until mixture clings together
and forms a soft dough.

3. Turn dough out onto lightly floured surface and knead gently 5 or 6
times. Divide dough in half. With lightly floured rolling pin, roll
one half of dough into a 7-inch round. Cut into 4 wedges. Repeat with
remaining half of dough.

4. Place scones, 1 inch apart, on greased baking sheet. Pierce tops
with tines of fork, Brush tops with remaining maple syrup.

5. Bake scones 15 to 18 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.
