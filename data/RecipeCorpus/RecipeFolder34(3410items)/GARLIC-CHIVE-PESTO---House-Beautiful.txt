Title: GARLIC CHIVE PESTO - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Herbs/spice
Yield: 3 /4 cup
1 lg Garlic clove, peeled and
-crushed
3 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
2 tb Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese, preferably Reggiano
1/2 c  Packed fresh garlic chives
1 c  Packed fresh flat-leaf
-parsley sprigs
1/2 c  Packed fresh spinach leaves
2 tb Walnuts or pistachios
In a blender or food processor, place the garlic chives, parsley,
spinach, nuts, and garlic. Process until finely minced. Add oil and
cheese and process until blended. Transfer to a small bowl, cover and
refrigerate. Makes about 3/4 C.
