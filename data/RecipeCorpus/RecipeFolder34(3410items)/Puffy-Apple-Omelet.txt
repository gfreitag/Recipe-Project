Title: Puffy Apple Omelet
Categories: Eggs, Country wom
Yield: 2 servings
3 tb Skim milk
3 tb Sugar
1 tb Lemon juice
3 tb All-purpose flour
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/8 ts Salt -- optional
2 ea Eggs -- separated
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING:-------------------------------
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1 lg Baking apples -- thinly
Sliced
In a small bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt if desired; mix
well. Add egg yolks and milk; mix well and set aside. In a small
mixing bowl, beat egg whites until foamy. Gradually add sugar,
beating until stiff peaks form. Fold in yolk mixture and lemon juice.
Pour into a greased 1 1/2 qt. shallow baking dish. Arrange apple
slices on top. Combine sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle over all. Bake
uncovered at 375 deg. for 18 to 20 min. or until a knife inserted
near the center comes out clean. Serve immediately. Yield: 2
servings. Diabetic Exchanges: One serving (prepared with skim milk
and without salt) equals 1 starch, 1 meat, 1/2 fruit; also 193
calories, 148 mg. sodium, 214 mg. cholesterol, 26 gm carbohydrate, 9
gm protein, 6 gm fat.
