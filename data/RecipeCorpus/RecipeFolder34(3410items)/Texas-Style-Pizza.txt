Title: Texas Style Pizza
Categories: Main dish, Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Warm water
2 1/4 c  Biscuit mix
1/2 c  Cornmeal
Shredded cheddar or American
Cheese
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 3/4 oz French's Chilli-O-Seasoning
16 oz Canned tomatoes
15 oz Kidney beans; drained --
Rinsed
1 pk Active dry yeast
Brown ground beef in large skillet, stirring to crumble; pour off
excess fat. Stir in seasoning mix, tomatoes, and beans; simmer,
uncovered, ten minutes. Sprinkle yeast over warm water in large
mixing bowl; stir to dissolve. Add biscuit mix and cornmeal; stir
until smooth. Knead 25 strokes on floured surface. Divide in half,
roll or pat each to a 14-inch circle on greased pizza pans or baking
sheets. Spoon filling on top of crust; sprinkle with cheese. Bake at
425 degrees F. for 15 to 20 minutes, until crust is golden brown.
Serves 6 to 8.
