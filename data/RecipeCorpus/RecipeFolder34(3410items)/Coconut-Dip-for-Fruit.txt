Title: Coconut Dip for Fruit
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 3 servings
2 ts Light sugar (no more)
1/2 ts Vanilla
2    8 oz. cans Coco Lopez cream
Of coconut
2    8 oz. cartons sour cream
Mix well with a wire whisk.  Serve with fresh pineapple, coconut
strips, kiwi, and strawberries. Randy Rigg
