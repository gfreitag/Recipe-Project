Title: Meat Loaf-Stuffed Peppers
Categories: Vegetables, Dishes, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Meat loaf -- cubed leftover
1 c  Cooked rice -- leftover
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Green pepper -- chopped
3/4 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
6 lg Green pepper
1    Jar spaghetti sauce -- 27 oz
Divided
Or 3 cup leftover spaghetti
Sauce -- divided
Remove tops and seeds from peppers. Immerse in boiling water for 3
minutes; drain. Pour 1 cup of spaghetti sauce into the bottom of a
shallow baking dish; set aside. In a saucepan, combine meat loaf,
rice, onion, chopped green pepper and remaining spaghetti sauce. Cook
and stir over medium-heat for 5-10 min. or until heated through.
Stuff each pepper with meat loaf mixture; place on sauce in baking
dish. Bake uncoved at 375 deg. for 15-20 min. or until heated
through. Sprinkle with the cheese and let stand until melted. Yield:
6 servings.
