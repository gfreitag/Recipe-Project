Title: RAISIN-BRAN MUFFINS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Breads
Yield: 9 muffins
3/4 c  Whole-wheat flour
1 tb Baking powder
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Dark seedless raisins
Grape Spread (opt., separate
-recipe)
2 c  Oat-bran cereal flakes
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  (1 %) lowfat milk
1/2 c  Thawed frozen white grape
-juice concentrate
1 lg Egg
3/4 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
1. Heat oven to 400'F. Grease nine 3 inch muffin-pan cups.

2. In large bowl, stir together cereal flakes, oil, milk, undiluted
grape-juice concentrate, and egg until blended.

3. In small bowl, combine the flours, baking powder, cinnamon, and
salt. Stir in raisins. Stir dry ingredients into cereal mixture just
until moistened. Spoon mixture into greased muffin-pan cups.

4. Bake 15 to 18 minutes or until centers spring back when lightly
pressed with fingertip. Meanwhile, prepare Grape Spread, if desired.
Cool muffins in pan on wire rack 5 minutes. Remove muffins from pan
and serve wann with Grape Spread, if desired.
