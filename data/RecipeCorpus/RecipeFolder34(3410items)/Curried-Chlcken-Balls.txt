Title: Curried Chlcken Balls
Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Chicken
Finely minced cooked
3 tb Green onion
3 tb Celery -- minced
1 c  Almonds
Finely chopped toasted
2 pk Cream cheese -- 3 oz ea
Softened
2 tb Orange marmalade
2 ts Curry powder
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
In a mixing bowl, combine first five ingredients. Beat until smooth.
Stir in chicken, onion and celery. Shape into 1-in. balls; roll in
almonds. Cover and chill until firm (can refrigerate up to 2 days).
Yield: about 5 dozen appetizers
