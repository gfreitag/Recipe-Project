Title: Finlandia's Fin-Madras
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 1 serving
4 oz Orange juice freshly
. squeezed
1 1/2 oz Finlandia's cranberry
. flavord vodka
In a tall glass, over ice, pour the vodka and the orange juice.
Garnish with maraschino cherry and an orange slice.
