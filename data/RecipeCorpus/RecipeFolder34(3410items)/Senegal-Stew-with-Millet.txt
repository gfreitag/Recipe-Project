Title: Senegal Stew with Millet
Categories: Soup/stew, Low cal/fat, African
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Vegetable broth ; or water
2 tb Smooth peanut butter
2 c  Sweet potato ; cubed
1 1/2 c  Rutabaga ; cubed
1 c  Carrot ; in 1/2-inch pieces
1 c  Chickpeas ; cooked
2 c  Millet ; cooked
2 tb Oil
2 c  Cabbage ; coarsely chopped
1 c  Onion ; chopped
2    Cl Garlic ; minced
1/2 ts Cayenne
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/4 ts Dried thyme
14 1/2 oz Whole peeled tomatoes
Recipe by: "Wholesome Harvest" by Carol Gelles

In a 3-qt. saucepan, heat the oil over med.-high heat.  Add cabbage,
onion, & garlic, and cook ,stirring , until the cabbage is softened.
Stir in the cayenne, curry pwdr, and thyme; stir in tomatoes, braking
them up with spoon (can also squish them through your hands as you
add them --- jtk). Add the broth and peanut butter, and stir until
completely smooth.

Add potato, rutabaga, carrot, & chickpeas.  Bring to a boil, reduce
heat, & simmer uncovered for 35 mins.  Serve over the millet.
