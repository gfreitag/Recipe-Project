Title: Peachy Ham Sllce
Categories: Pork, Ham, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Cornstarch
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 tb Lemon juice
Hot cooked rice
1 cn Sliced peaches -- l6 ounces
1 ea Ham slice
(about 1-1/2 pounds)
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Sugar
Drain peaches, reserving 1/2 cup syrup; set aside. In a large skillet,
brown ham slice on both sides in butter over medium heat. Remove ham
to a platter and keep warm, reserving drippings in skillet. In a
small bowl, mix sugar, cornstarch and nutmeg. Add orange juice, lemon
juice and reserved peach syrup; stir until smooth. Add to drippings
in skillet. Cook until thick, stirring constantly. Stir in peaches
and heat through. Add ham slice and heat for 2-3 minutes. Cut into
serving-size pieces. Serve over rice. Yield: 4-6 servings
