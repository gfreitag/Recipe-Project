Title: Master Recipe for Oven-Baked Potato
Categories: Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
-(7oz ea)
4    Idaho Russet potatoes
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Scrub potatoes well to remove dirt. Poke
with a fork if desired. Place on middle rack of oven and bake for 75
minutes. Remove from oven and pierce with fork to created dotted "X".
Press in ends of potato to push flesh up and out.

Microwave Directions: Cook a seven ounce potato for four minutes on
high power, changing the position of the potato halfway through
cooking. Increase the cooking time by four minutes for each
additional potato.
