Title: Traditional Bolognese Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Crockpot, Meats, Italian
Yield: 8 cups
1 ts Sugar
1 tb Chopped fresh oregano
1 tb Chopped fresh basil
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Dry red wine
Cooked pasta
Parmesan cheese (optional)
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 lb Italian sausage, no casing
1    Onion, chopped
1    Glove garlic, crushed
1 sm Carrot, grated
2    Stalks celery, chopped
1 cn (28 oz) diced, peeled tomato
2 cn (6 oz) tomato paste
Turn slow cooker on HIGH.  Add ground beef and sausage.  Cook and stir
until meat is broken up into small pieces.  Turn pot on LOW.  Add
remaining ingredients, except pasta and cheese.  Cover and cook on
LOW 8 to 9 hours. Make about 8 cups.  Spoon over your favorite pasta.
Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
