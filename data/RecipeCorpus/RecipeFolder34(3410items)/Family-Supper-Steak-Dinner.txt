Title: Family Supper Steak Dinner
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Bottled Chili Sauce
8 ea Small New Potatoes
8 ea Carrots
2 tb Chopped Parsley
1 lb Round Steak
3 tb Flour
1/4 ts Each Salt and Pepper
2 ts Canola Oil
1 ea Onion
Cut round steak into 4 equal pieces.  Peel carrots.  Cut small ring of
peeling from center of each potato, wash well.  Combine flour, salt
and pepper.  Slice onion. Dredge steak in flour, brown well in oil.
Top steak with onion, add 1/4 cup water, cover and simmer 45 min.
Stir in chili sauce and remaining water, cover again and simmer 45
min. Steam potatoes, cook carrots.
