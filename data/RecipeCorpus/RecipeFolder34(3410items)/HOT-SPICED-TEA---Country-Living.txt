Title: HOT SPICED TEA - Country Living
Categories: Beverages, Country liv
Yield: 1 serving
1    Tea bag
1/2 ts Honey
2    Whole cloves
1    3-inch strip orange peel
Cold water
1. Insert cloves into orange peel. Fill microwave-safe mug or cup
with cold water to within 1/2 inch of top and add milk clove-studded
orange peel. Microwave, uncovered, on high (100 percent) 1 1/2 to 2
minutes or until water is steaming.

2. Add tea bag and honey to water mixture; microwave on high (100
percent) 30 seconds. Allow tea to steep and cool slightly; remove tea
bag and serve.
