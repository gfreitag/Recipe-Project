Title: Korean Beef Patties
Categories: Ground, Beef, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Sesame oil
2 tb Green onion -- chopped
1 tb Garlic -- minced
1 ds Pepper
1 lb Lean ground beef
4 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Toasted sesame seeds --
Crushed
Combine all ingredients. Form into flour balls and flatten into
patties. Broil, grill or pan fry until done. Yield: 4 servings.
