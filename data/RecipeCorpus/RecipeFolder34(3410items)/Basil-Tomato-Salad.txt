Title: Basil Tomato Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
3 c  Cooked rice -- cooled
2 md Tomatoes -- chopped
1 md Cucumber -- seeded/chopped
1 sm Red onion -- chopped
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Cider vinegar
2 ts Fresh basil -- chopped
Or 1 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 ts Salt
In a small bowl, whisk together oil, vinegar, basil, salt and pepper.
Add rice, tomatoes, cucumber and onion; toss. Serve chilled. Yield: 6
servings
