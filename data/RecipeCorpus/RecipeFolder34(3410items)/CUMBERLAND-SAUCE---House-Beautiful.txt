Title: CUMBERLAND SAUCE - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 3 1/2 cups
1/2 c  Finely chopped shallots
1 tb Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Ground ginger
1 1/2 ts Coarsely ground black pepper
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Red currant jelly
1/2 c  Ruby port wine
1 tb Finely slivered orange zest
1 1/2 tb Finely slivered lemon zest
1/4 c  Fresh orange juice
1/4 c  Fresh lemonjuice
In a small saucepan over medium-low heat, cook all ingredients,
stirring constantly, until the jelly has melted. Pour sauce into a
bowl and cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for 2 hours or until
sauce has set to the consistency of jelly. Makes 3 1/2 C.
