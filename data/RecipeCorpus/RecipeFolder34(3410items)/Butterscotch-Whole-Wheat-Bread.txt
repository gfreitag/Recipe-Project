Title: Butterscotch Whole Wheat Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Desserts, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Powdered milk
3/4 ts Salt
3 tb Sugar
1    Egg
3 tb Oil
1 c  Water
3 tb Butter
3/4 c  Brown sugar
2 1/4 ts Active dry yeast
1 3/4 c  Bread flour
2 tb Bread flour
1 c  Whole-wheat flour
Cook the butter and brown sugar together over medium-low heat until
the sugar bubbles and melts.  Let cool.  Add the brown sugar mixture
and all remaining ingredients in the order suggested by your manual.
Bake on the basic bread cycle.
