Title: Creamy Whipped Turnips - Country Living Holidays
Categories: Country liv, Holiday, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2    To 3 T  ha!f-and-hatf
1 ts Butter
1 lb White turnips or rutabagas
1/4 ts Salt
1. Peel turnips; cut into one-inch cubes. Place in a medium saucepan;
cover with water and bring to boiling. Reduce heat and simmer, cov-
ered, until tender-about 15 minutes.

2. Drain turnips very well. Add salt, cream, and butter. Beat with a
hand electric beater until smooth and fluffy. Serve immediately.
