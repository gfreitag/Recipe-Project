Title: CHINESE INGREDIENTS: SZECHWAN PEPPER (FAGARA)
Categories: Chinese, Information
Yield: 1 servings
Pub: time life books-----
From: foods of the world----
SZECHWAN PEPPER (FAGARA):  Speckled brown peppercorns with a mildly
hot flavor and a pleasant scent.  Sold whole, not ground, by weight.
It will keep indefinitely in tightly covered containers. No
substitute.

From:  The Cooking of China by Emily Hahn and the Editors of
Time-Life Books "Foods of the World" series. Copyright: 1968 Time
Inc. ISBN 68-56965.
