Title: Brussels Sprouts Piquant
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 2 servings
1/3 ts Sugar
1 x  Salt
1/16 ts Pepper
2 tb Shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 ea Onion
1/3 tb Canola oil
6 2/3 oz Frozen brussels sprouts
2/3 tb Flour
1/3 c  Tomato juice
Slice onion, saute in oil, remove and put in casserole dish with
brussels sprouts.  To onion drippings, add flour, mix well, add
tomato juice, sugar, and salt and pepper to taste.  Pour thickened
sauce over sprouts. Bake at 350 F for 15 minutes or microwave for 5
minutes. Submitted By SHEILA EXNER   On   09-03-95
