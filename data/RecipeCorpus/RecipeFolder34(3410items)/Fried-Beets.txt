Title: Fried Beets
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
4 tb Butter -=OR=-
4 tb Margarine;
4 c  Cooked beets; sliced
1/8 ts Pepper;
Combine ingredients, cover and fry about 20 minutes.
