Title: Cherry Chewbilees
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 20 servings
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
Butter-flavoring
1 c  Walnuts -- divided chopped
1/2 c  Coconut flakes
1 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Shortening --
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
2 ea Eggs
2 ts Vanilla extract
2 cn Cherry pie filling -- 21 oz
2 pk Cream cheese -- softened 8
Oz
2/3 c  Sugar
In a bowl, combine flour and brown sugar; cut in shortening until fine
crumbs form. stir in 1/2 cup nuts and coconut. Reserve 1/2 cup crumb
mixture for topping. Press remaining mixture into the bottom of a
greased 13x9x2 inch baking powder. Bake at 350 deg. for 12-15 minutes
or until lightly browned. Meanwhile, for filling, beat cream
cheese,sugar, eggs and vanilla in a mixing bowl until smooth. Spread
pie filling on top. Combine remaining nuts and reserved crumbs;
sprinkle over cherries. Bake 15 min. more. Cool. Refrigerate until
servings. Yield: 20 servings.
