Title: Butter Pecan Banana Cake
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
4    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
1/2 c  Banana, Mashed
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 pk Butter pecan instant pudding
Mix
Combine all ingredients in large mixer bowl. Blend on low speed until
all ingredients are just combined.  Then beat on medium speed for
four minutes. Pour into greased and floured 10" tube pan. Bake at
350F. for 50 minutes or until cake tester inserted into center comes
out clean and cake begins to pull away from sides of the pan. Cool in
pan 15 minutes. Remove and glaze with a thin confectioners sugar
icing if desired.
