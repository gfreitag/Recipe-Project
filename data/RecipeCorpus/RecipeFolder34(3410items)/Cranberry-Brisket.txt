Title: Cranberry Brisket
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
14 oz Jellied cranberry sauce
1 c  Water
4 lb Rolled Brisket of Beef
1 1/2 oz Dry onion soup mix
Pre-heat oven to 350 degF (180 degC). Combine all ingredients and
pour over brisket, using enough water to cover. Cover and bake for 3
hours. Slice while warm and return slices to pot to absorb juice.
Re-heat just before serving.
