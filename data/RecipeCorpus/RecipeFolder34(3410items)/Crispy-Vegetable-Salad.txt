Title: Crispy Vegetable Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1 lg Carrot -- shredded
1    Bottle Ranch salad dressing,
-8 oz.
2 tb Parmesan cheese -- grated
1/2 ts Lemon pepper
1 bn Leaf lettuce -- torn
1 ea Cucumber -- sliced
2 c  Broccoli flowerets
1 c  Cauliflower
1 lg Tomato -- cut in wedges
In a large salad bowl, combine lettuce, cucumber, broccoli,
cauliflower, tomato and carrot. In a small bowl, combine dressing.
Parmesan cheese and lemon pepper; mix well. serve with salad. Yield:
4 to 6 servings.
