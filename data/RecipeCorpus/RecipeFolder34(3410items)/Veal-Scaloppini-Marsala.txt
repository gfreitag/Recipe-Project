Title: Veal Scaloppini Marsala
Categories: Meats, Italian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Marsala wine
3/4 c  Cream
Juice of 1/2 lemon
2 tb Butter
2 tb Oil
8    Scaloppini of veal
2 tb Flour
1 ts Salt
Heat the butter and oil in a saute pan.  Combine the flour with the
salt and pepper.  Dust the scaloppini with the seasoned flour,
shaking off the excess.  Saute the meat, a few pieces at a time, for
about 1 minute on each side or until just lightly colored.  Move the
cooked veal to a warm serving dish and keep warm.

Add the wine to the saute pan and reduce to 1/4 cup.  Add the cream
and reduce by half.  Add the lemon juice and season the sauce with
salt and pepper.  Pour the sauce over the veal and serve.

Makes 4 servings.
