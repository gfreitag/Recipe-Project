Title: Fast Holiday Drops
Categories: Diabetic, Chocolate, Candies, Holiday
Yield: 24 drops
1 tb Vanilla extract;
1/2 c  Creamy peanut butter;
1/3 c  Mini chocolate chips;
1    Evnv. unflavored gelatin;
2/3 c  Skim milk;
30    Soda crackers; crumbled
1/2 c  Pecans; chopped fine
Soak gelatin in milk for 5 minutes; heat until gelatin is dissolved
and mixture boils.  Remove from heat and quickly add remaining
ingredients. Beat with wooden spoon until mixture is thick enough to
drop from a teaspoon onto waxed paper.  Makes 24 drops. Refrigerate
until firm.

Food Exchange per serving: 1/2 BREAD EXCHANGE + 1 FAT EXCHANGE; CAL: 1
DROP: 76
