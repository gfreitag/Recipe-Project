Title: Chocolate Shortbread
Categories: Cookies, Country wom
Yield: 48 servings
1 ds Salt
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Butter or margarine
1/3 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
2/3 c  Confectioner's sugar
In a large mixing bowl, cream butter until light and fluffy. Blend in
remaining ingredients. Chill 1 hour. Drop by rounded teaspoonsful 2
inch apart on greased cookie sheets. Bake at 300 deg. for about 20
min. or until the cookies are set. Yield: about 4 dozens.
