Title: Orange Slice Candy Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Orange slice candies, cut up
1/2 c  Nuts, chopped
1 c  Shortening
2 c  Brown sugar
3    Eggs
3 c  Flour
1 ts Cream of tartar
Cream shortening; add sugar and eggs.  Beat well.  Add sifted dry
ingredients.  Add vanilla, nuts and orange pieces.  Drop by
teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees until
lightly browned. Randy Rigg
