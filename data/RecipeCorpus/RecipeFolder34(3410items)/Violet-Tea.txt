Title: Violet Tea
Categories: Beverages, Game
Yield: 1 batch
;Boiling water
2    To 3 tsp. violet leaves
Pour boiling water over violet leaves.  Steep five minutes.

From Kathleen S. Van Horn's "Sweet Violets" article in "The Herb
Companion."  April/May 1993, Vol. 5, No. 4.  Pg. 29.  Posted by Cathy
Harned.
