Title: Creamy curried shrimp
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Brandy, heated until luke
Warm
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Whipping cream
3    Drops tabasco sauce
Fluffy rice
1 lb Large shrimp
1 md Onion chopped fine
1 sm Green pepper, seeded and cut
Into strips
1/4 c  Butter
1    Clove garlic, minced
2 ts Curry powder
1) Shell and devein shrimp.

2) In a large frying pan cook onions and pepper strips in heated
butter until soft but not brown. Mix in garlic and curry powder. cook
about 30 seconds.

3) Add uncooked shrimp in a single layer. Drizzle with brandy and
ignite, and cook, stirring lightly, until flames subsides. Sprinkle
with paprika and salt.

4) Mix in cream. Cook over medium heat for 2-3 minutes, stirring
frequently, until shrimp are pink and firm to the touch and liquids is
reduced slightly.

5) Taste, add salt if needed. Season with tabasco sauce to taste.
Serve curried shrimp over rice.
