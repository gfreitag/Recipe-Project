Title: Zucchini Relish No. 2
Categories: Relishes, Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 ts Mustard seed
1 md Onion, chopped
1 c  Cider vinegar
2 ea Cloves garlic, mashed
1/2 ts Ground turmeric
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 lb Zucchini, chopped
2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Celery seed
In a 4-cup measure, whisk together the vinegar, sugar, garlic, celery
seed, turmeric, dry mustard, and mustard seed.  Cover with vented
plastic wrap and microwave on full power unti; just ready to boil,
about 2 minutes. Combine the zucchini and onion in a large casserole
dish.  Pour the liquid over the zuchini mixture and cover with vented
plastic wrap. Microwave on full power until the vegetables are just
tender, about 2 minutes. Pour into glass jars and refrigerate for up
to 2 weeks.  Serve slightly chilled as an accompaniment to curries,
pate, or as part of an antipasto. Also great with poached chicken.
Per 1/4 cup Portion:29 Calories, 1 g Protein, 9 g Carbohydrate, 0.2 g
Fat, 0 g Saturated Fat, 0 mg Cholesterol, 3 mg Sodium
