Title: Individual Funny Faces Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
4    6" pizza crusts
Zucchini slices
Ripe olive slices
2    Cherry tomatoes -- halve
Cheddar cheese -- slice
1    "wide
1 c  Sm broccoli florets -- cook
Rinse -- drain
1 c  Ricotta cheese
1 tb Parmesan -- grated
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Preheat oven to 450~. Combine ricotta, Parmesan, salt and pepper.
Spread mixture over each pizza crust. Make faces on each with 1/4 c
broccoli florets each (for hair), zucchini (for eyebrows), olives for
eyes, tomatoes (for nose) and Cheddar (for mouth). Bake 8 to 10
minutes. Per Slice: 230 Cal, 9 Gr Fat.
