Title: Bisquick Breakfast Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Bisquick
1/3 c  Very Hot Water
1 c  Shredded Cheddar Cheese 4oz
1 c  Shredded Monterey Jack 4oz
12 oz Bulk Pork Sausage
2 c  Frozen Loose-Pack Hash
Brown
Potatoes
Heat oven to 450~.  Grease 12" pizza pan or a cookie sheet. Cook and
stir pork sausage and potatoes until sausage is fully cooked; drain.
Mix baking mix and water until dough forms; beat 20 strokes. Turn
dough onto surface dusted with baking mix; knead 60 times. Press
dough in pizza pan or into 12" circle on cookie sheet with fingers
dipped in baking mix. Spread sausage mixture over crust; sprinkle
with cheese. Bake on lowest oven rack until crust is golden brown and
cheese is melted and bubbly, 12 to 15 minutes.
