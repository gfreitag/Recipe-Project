Title: Beef Pizza's
Categories: Appetizers, Pizza
Yield: 12 servings
3/4 lb Gd. Beef
1/4 ts Salt -- pepper & oregano
8 oz Can tomato sauce
1/4 lb Cheese -- American Square
5    English Muffins
1    Chopped onion
1 ts Oil
1    Clove garlic
Saute onion and garlic in hot oil, add meat and seasoning. Brown
lightly. On each muffin half put 1 tbls tomato sauce (can use left
over spaghetti sauce) 2 tbls meat, sprinkle with oregano and top with
slice of cheese. Bake in 375 degree oven until cheese bubbles.
