Title: Banana Tapioca Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 ea Banana -- sliced
2 3/4 c  Milk
3 tb Tapioca -- quick-cooking
1/3 c  Sugar
Om a saucepan, combine milk, tapioca, sugar and egg; let stand 5 min.
Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture comes to a full boil;
remove from heat. Stir in vanilla and bananas. Cool for 20 min. Serve
warm or cold. Yield: 6 servings.
