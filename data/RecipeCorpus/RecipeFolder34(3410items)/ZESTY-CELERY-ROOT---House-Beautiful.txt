Title: ZESTY CELERY ROOT - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb To 3 T grainy Dijon-style
-mustard
Salt & freshly ground
-black pepper to taste
1 lg Celery root, about 3/4 lb,
-trimmed and peeled
1/2    Lemon, plus 1/4 c freshly
-squeezed lemon juice
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
Immediately after trimming and peeling celery root, rub with lemon
half to prevent browning. Set aside.

In a large serving bowl add lemon juice. Grate celery root into thin
shreds and quickly transfer to the large bowl.

In a small bowl combine mayonnaise and mustard. If the mixture seems
too thick, add a little lemon juice. Add the mayonnaise mixture to
celery root and toss to blend well. Season with salt and pepper.
Cover bowl and chin until ready to serve. Salad can be made up to 24
hours ahead. Serves 4 to 6.
