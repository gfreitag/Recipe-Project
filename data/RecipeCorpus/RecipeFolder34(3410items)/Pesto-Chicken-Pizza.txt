Title: Pesto Chicken Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Poultry
Yield: 8 servings
1/3 c  Pesto sauce
1    12" pizza crust
1 1/2 c  Mozarella; shred -- divide
1    Plum tomato -- diced
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper
1 tb Olive oiil
1/2 lb Chicken breasts; skin, bone
Cut 1/2" pieces
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat oil and add chicken pieces, oregano and
salt; cook just until no longer pink, about 5 minutes. Spread pesto
over crust; sprinkle with 1 c cheese; Top with tomatoes, chicken, 1/2
c more cheese and red pepper. Bake 10 minutes. Per Slice: 305 Cal, 4
gr Fat.
