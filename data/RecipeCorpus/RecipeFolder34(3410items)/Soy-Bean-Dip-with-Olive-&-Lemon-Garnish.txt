Title: Soy Bean Dip with Olive & Lemon Garnish
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------------------DIP---------------------------------
5 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Liquid sweetener
Salt, to taste
8 oz Soy beans, soaked
6 tb Olive oil
2 lg Onions, chopped
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
3 tb Parsley, chopped
1 ts Paprika
2 tb Olive oil
3 tb Lemon juice
DIP: Rinse the soy beans, cover with fresh water & cook for 2 hours or
until soft.  Drain & set aside.

Heat the oil in a pan & saute the onions until soft, 5 minutes.  Put
the onions & oil in a blender with the cooked beans, lemon juice,
sweetener & salt.  Blend until smooth.  Transfer the dip to a serving
bowl.

GARNISH: Mix all the ingredients together & trickle the mixture over
the dip.  Serve immediately.

VARIATION: Use chick peas instead of the soy beans.

David Scott & Claire Golding, "The Vegan Diet"
