Title: Pie Crust (Matzo Meal)
Categories: Pastry
Yield: 1 pie shell
1/4 c  Pecan nuts, ground
1/3 c  Oil
Cold water
1/4 c  Matzo meal
1/2 c  Cake flour
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Sugar
Combine dry ingredients in a bowl and, with a fork or pastry blender,
mix in oil and just enough water to make a moist dough. Pat into a 9"
(1 L) pie plate and bake at 400 degF (200 degC) for 10 minutes or
until brown.
