Title: Beef And Peppers
Categories: Beef, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 md Green bell pepper
Seeded and cut into strips
1 cn Beef broth -- 10-1/2 oz.
1/4 c  Water
3 tb Cornstarch
Salt and pepper to taste
Hot cooked rice
Clove -- minced
2 tb Cooking oil
1 1/4 lb Beef round
Or sirloin steak, cut into
1    Inch cubes
1    Garlic
1 md Onion -- cut in wedges
1 md Red bell pepper
Seeded and cut into strips
Heat oil in skillet; brown beef on all sides. Add the garlic and
continue to cook for 2 minutes. Add onion, peppers and broth; cover
and simmer 20 minutes. Combine water and cornstarch; stir into broth.
Cook and stir until gravy is thick and shiny. Add seasonings. Serve
over hot cooked rice. Yield: 4 servings
