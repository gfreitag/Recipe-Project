Title: Easy Rice And Peas
Categories: Rice, Dishes, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Instant rice -- uncooked
1 c  Frozen peas
2 c  Water
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 ts Salt
In a medium saucepan, bring water, butter and salt to a boil. Add
rice and peas. Cover and remove from the heat. Let stand for 5 to 7
min. or until all the water is absorbed. Yield: 4 servings.
