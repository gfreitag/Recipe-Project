Title: Barbecued Muffin Meatballs
Categories: Bbq/grill, Beef, Country wom
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/4 ts Nutmeg
2 ts Dry mustard
6 tb Brown sugar
1/2 c  Ketchup
MMMMM-------------------------MEATBALLS------------------------------
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 tb Chopped onion -- finely
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean
1 lb Ground pork
2 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  Seasoned bread crumbs
Combine sauce ingredients in a small bowl; set aside. Combine the
meats, eggs, crumbs, salt, pepper, onion and half of sauce; mix
gently until all ingredients are blended. Shape mixture into 12 balls
and place in 3 inch deep muffin pans. Make a small indentation in
each ball fill with fill with remaining sauce. Bake at 400 deg. for
30 minutes. Yield: 12 meatballs.
