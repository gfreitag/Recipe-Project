Title: Spinach and Onions in Yogurt
Categories: Side dish, Bon appetit
Yield: 4 servings
10 oz Pkg fresh spinach
-stems removed
-leaves coarsely chopped
8 oz Carton plain yogurt
3 tb Olive oil
2 md Onion(s), chopped
-(abt 2 1/2 cups)
2 lg Garlic clove(s), minced
Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onions and
garlic and saut until golden brown, about 10 minutes. Add spinach and
saut just until wilted, about 2 minutes. Transfer mixture to large
bowl. Cool slightly. Add yogurt to spinach mixture and stir to blend.
SEason to taste with salt and pepper. Serve warm or at room
temperature.
