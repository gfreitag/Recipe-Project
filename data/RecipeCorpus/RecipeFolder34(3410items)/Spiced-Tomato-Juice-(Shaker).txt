Title: Spiced Tomato Juice (Shaker)
Categories: Beverages, Vegetables, Tomatoes
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Seasoned salt
1 ts Hot sauce
1/2 c  Whipping cream; whipped
1 ea 48 oz can tomato juice
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 3/4 ts Salt
Combine tomato juice, Worcestershire sauce, and seasonings in a
large saucepan.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes.
Cover and chill completely.
Serve with a dollop of whipped cream.
