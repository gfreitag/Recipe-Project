Title: Fireside Coffee Mix
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 3 servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
2 c  Non-dairy coffee creamer
1 1/2 c  Carnation hot cocoa mix
1 1/2 c  Instant coffee (reg or dec)
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl, stirring well.  Store in an
airtight container.  To make 1 cup; spoon 2 tablespoons plus 1
teaspoon of mix in a coffee mug.  Add 1 cup boiling water, stir until
well blended. Randy Rigg
