Title: Chilled Cantaloupe-Amaretto Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Alcohol, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Heavy cream, milk, OR
-Half-and-half
1/4 c  Amaretto liqueur
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
pn Ground nutmeg OR
Cinnamon
-JUDI M. PHELPS
1    Fresh ripe cantaloupe;
-peeled, seeded and cubed
2    Fresh, ripe peaches; peeled,
-pitted and chopped
1 ts Pure almond extract
In a blender, puree small amounts of the fruit with the liquids

until all the ingredients are used.  The soup should be very

smooth.  Stir in the nutmeg or cinnamon.  Chill several hours

before serving.  Serves 4 to 6.
Serving suggestions:  Serve as first course with an antipasto

platter, fettuccine with pesto sauce, crusty Italian bread and

a light white wine.  Source:  San Jose Mercury News.
