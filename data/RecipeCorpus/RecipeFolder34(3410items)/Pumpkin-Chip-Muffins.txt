Title: Pumpkin Chip Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 24 servings
2 tb Baking powder
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Salt
2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips --
12    Oz
4 ea Eggs
2 c  Sugar
1 cn Pumpkin -- 16 oz
1 1/2 c  Vegetable oil
3 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking soda
In a large mixing bowl, beat eggs, sugar, pumpkin and oil until
smooth. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon and salt;
add to pumpkin mixture and mix well. Fold in chocolate chips. Fill
greased or paper-lined muffin; cups three-fourths full. Bake at 400
deg. for 16-20 min. or until muffins test done. Cool in pan 10 min.
before removing to a wire rack. Yield: about 24 standard-size muffins.
