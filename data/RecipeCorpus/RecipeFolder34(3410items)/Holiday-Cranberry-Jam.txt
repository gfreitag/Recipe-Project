Title: Holiday Cranberry Jam
Categories: Fruit, Preserves, Etc., Country wom
Yield: 30 servings
Sliced, thawed
3 c  Sugar
1 pk Liquid fruit pection -- 3
Oz
2 c  Cranberries -- fresh or
Frozen
1 md Orange -- peeled & sections
1 cn Frozen strawberries --
In a food grinder or food processor, coarsely grind cranberries and
orange sections, Place in a dutch oven or large kettle with
strawberries and sugar. Bring to a full rolling boil over high heat,
stirring constantly. Boil for 1 min. Remove from the heat and stir in
the pectin. Quickly skim off the foam with a large metal spoon.
Immediately pour into hot sterilized jars. Adjust caps. Process 10
min. in a boiling water bath. Yield: 5 half pints.
