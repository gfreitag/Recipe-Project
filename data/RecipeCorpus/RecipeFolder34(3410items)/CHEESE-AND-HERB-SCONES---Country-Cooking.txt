Title: CHEESE AND HERB SCONES - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Breads
Yield: 8 scones
1/4 ts Ground red pepper
2/3 c  Vegetable shortening
1 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 1/3 c  Milk
1 tb Dijon-style prepared mustard
4 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
4 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Dried thyme leaves
1. Heat oven to 425'F. Grease large baking sheet. in large bowl,
combine flour, baking powder, salt, basil, thyme, and red pepper.
With pastry blender or 2 knives, cut the shortening in until mixture
resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in 1/4 C Cheddar cheese,

2. Add milk and mustard to dry ingredients and mix lightly with fork
until mixture clings together and forms a soft dough.

3. Turn dough out onto lightly floured surface and knead gently 5 or 6
times. Divide dough in half. With lightly floured rolling pin, roll
one half of dough into a 7-inch round. Cut into 4 wedges. Repeat with
remaining half of dough.

4. Place scones, 1 inch apart, on greased baking sheet. Pierce tops
with tines of fork. Brush tops with water and sprinkle with remaining
cheese.

5. Bake scones 15 to 18 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.
