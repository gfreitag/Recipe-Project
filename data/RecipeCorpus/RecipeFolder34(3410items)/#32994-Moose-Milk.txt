Title: #32994 Moose Milk
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
120 oz Amber rum
40 oz Canadian club
4    (26 oz.) bottles Tia Maria
4 qt Milk
2 qt Half & half
6 qt Egg nog
2 ga Vanilla ice cream
Mix above ingredients together well.  When serving, sprinkle the top
with nutmeg.  Makes 6 gallons.  Note:  Since this makes so much I
make only 1/4 of the recipe.
