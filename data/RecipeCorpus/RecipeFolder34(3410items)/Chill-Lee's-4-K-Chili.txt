Title: Chill Lee's 4-K Chili
Categories: Soup/stew, Chili
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Beer
2 tb Cumin
4 tb New mex grd red chilies
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Ground black pepper
5 lb Gr chuck
1    Large onion chopped
4    Garlic cloves
; finely choppd
1    Jalapeno(canned) with seeds
enhancer 1 ts Salt 1 pk Chill Lee's chili mix Water Brown the meat.
Brown onions,garlic an jalapeno together. Combine meat and onion with
beer, cumin,chilies, paprika, pepper, flavor enhancer and salt;
marinate for one hour. Add water to cover. Stir well. Cook
chili-grind meat at least 2 hours. Hand cut meat should be cooked 3
to 4 hours. Hamburger meat, after its browned, should be ready in
about 30 min. If using a fresh jalapeno, remove the seeds. Chili Lee
has also been known to add a 15 ounce can of tomato sauce to his
chili and substitute 2 T NW chili powder for the grd red chilies.
