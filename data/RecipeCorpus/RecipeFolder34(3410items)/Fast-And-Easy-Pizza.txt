Title: Fast And Easy Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 8 servings
Peppers
Pepperoni -- olives
Sausage etc.
1    Loaf french bread --
Split horizontally;
1 1/2 c  Contidina pizza squeeze
3 c  Assorted toppings --
Place french bread halves on cookie sheet or broiler pan. Spread each
half with approximately 3/4 cup pizza sauce. Arrange desired toppings
over sauce. Bake in preheated oven (350 F) 10 to 12 minutes or until
topping is heated through.  Slice each half into 4 servings.
