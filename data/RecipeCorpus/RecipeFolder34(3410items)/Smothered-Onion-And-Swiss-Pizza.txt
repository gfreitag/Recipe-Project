Title: Smothered Onion And Swiss Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Thyme
1/2 c  Mozzarella; shred -- divide
1/2 c  Swiss cheese; shred --
Divide
1    12" pizza crust
2 tb Butter
1 ts Vegetable oil
3 c  Leeks; white pt only --
Chop
2 c  Onions -- sliced
1 ts Garlic -- minced
1/2 c  Green onions -- sliced
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat butter and oil and cook leeks, onions and
garlic, stirring, until tender, 12 minutes. Remove from heat and stir
in green onions, salt, pepper and thyme. Sprinkle 1/4 c each
mozzarella and Swiss cheeses over crust. Spoon onion mixture on top.
Sprinkle with additional 1/4 c each mozzarella and Swiss cheeses.
Bake 10 minutes. Per Slice: 270 Cal, 10 gr Fat.
