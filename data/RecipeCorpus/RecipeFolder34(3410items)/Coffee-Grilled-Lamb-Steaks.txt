Title: Coffee Grilled Lamb Steaks
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/4 ts Rosemary
1/4 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Savory
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
6    Sirloin lamb steaks
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Strong black coffee
1 ts Dijon mustard
Have butcher cut 1-inch thick steaks from the sirloin part of the
lamb leg. Mix all remaining ingredients in a large bowl and marinate
the steaks, 4 hours to overnight. Grill quickly over very hot fire,
about 4 to 6 minutes per side.

Serve with rice or grain pilaf, honeyed carrots, and a chilled green
salad. Submitted By RICHARD DOUVILLE   On   08-31-95
