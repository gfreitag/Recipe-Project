Title: ROASTED SHALLOTS AND CHESTNUTS - Country Living Holidays
Categories: Country liv, Holiday, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
-chestnuts
3    Sprigs fresh thyme
6    Bay leaves (opt.)
1/2 lb Shallots, peeled
2 ts Olive oil
1/8 ts Salt
1    8-oz jar roasted whole
1. On rimmed baking sheet, toss shallots with olive oil and salt.
Spread in a single layer and roast in 325'F oven with goose 20
minutes. Add chestnuts and thyme; roast 10 minutes longer.

2. Remove mixture to serving bowl and top with bay leaves, if desired.
