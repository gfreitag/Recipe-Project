Title: Cherries & Ricotta Cheese Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Ground cinnamon
3    Eggs
3/4 c  Ricotta cheese
6 tb Unsalted butter
1/3 c  Water
2/3 c  Dried cherries
2 1/4 ts Active dry yeast
1 3/4 c  Bread flour
2 tb Bread flour
1 1/2 c  Whole-wheat flour
3 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
Add all ingredients except the cherries according to your manual and
bake on raisin bread setting.  At the beep add the dried cherries.
