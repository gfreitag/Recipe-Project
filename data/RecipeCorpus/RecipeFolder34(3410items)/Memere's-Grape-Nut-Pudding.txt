Title: Memere's Grape Nut Pudding
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1/8 ts Salt
1 c  Grape nuts
4 c  Milk scalded
4    Eggs
1 c  White sugar
Mix together all ingredients.  Pour into a 2 or 3 quart oven safe
baking dish.  Set dish in a pan of water so that water comes up half
way on the baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.  Serve
warm or cold.
