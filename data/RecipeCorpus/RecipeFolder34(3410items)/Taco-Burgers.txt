Title: Taco Burgers
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 3 servings
3 oz Cream cheese, cut into 12
1/8 inch strips
Paprika
10 oz Package of corn chips
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 pk Taco seasoning mix
Crunch enough of the corn chips (2/3 cup) to measure 1/3 cup.  Mix
ground beef, crushed chips and seasoning.  Shape mixture into
patties. Broil patties for 5 minutes, turn and broil.  When almost
done, criss cross 2 strips of cheese on each patty; sprinkle with
paprika. Broil until cheese is puffed and golden brown. Randy Rigg
