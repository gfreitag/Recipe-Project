Title: French Style Pizzas
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
Drain
1 sm Red bell pepper -- sliced
8    Ripe olives -- sliced
1 tb Capers -- drained
1/4 c  Goat cheese -- crumbled
1 c  Pizza sauce
1/4 ts Tarragon -- crushed
1/8 ts Thyme -- crushed
4    Pita bread rounds
1 cn Minced clams; rinse --
Preheat broiler.  Mix pizza sauce, tarragon and thyme; spread on pitaa
bread rounds.  Arrange clams, shrimp, red pepper and olives over
sauce. Sprinkle with capers and goat cheese. Broil until cheese is
melted.
