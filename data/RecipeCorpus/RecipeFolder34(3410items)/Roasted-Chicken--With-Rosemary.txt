Title: Roasted Chicken  With Rosemary
Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1    Whole roasted chicken -- 5
To 6 lb
8 sm Red potatoes -- halved
6 ea Carrot -- cut into 2 inch
2 md Onions -- quartered
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
2 tb Dried rosemary
2 tb Chopped parsley -- fresh
3    Cloves garlic -- minced
1 ts Salt
ts Pepper
In a small saucepan, melt butter. Add rosemary, parsley, garlic, salt
and pepper. Place chickin on a rack in a roasting pan; tie drumsticks
together. Spoon half of the butter mixture over chicken. Place the
potatoes, carrots and onions around chicken. Drizzle remaining butter
mixture over vegetables. cover and bake at 350 deg. for 1 1/2 hr.
basting every 30 min. Uncover ; bake 1 hour longer or until juices
run clear basting occasionally. Yield: 8 servings.
