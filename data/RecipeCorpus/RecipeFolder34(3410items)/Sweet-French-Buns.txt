Title: Sweet French Buns
Categories: Pastry
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Butter
1/4 c  Lukewarm water
1 ts Salt
1 ea Egg
1/4 c  Sugar
Mrs. Bena King,
-Quarryville, Pa.
1 tb Yeast
1 c  Milk, scalded
4 c  Sifted flour
1 tb Sugar
Dissolve yeast and 1 tbsp. sugar in lukewarm water.  Add 1-1/2 cups
flour. Beat until smooth.  Set aside to rise for 15 minutes.  Add the
remaining ingredients.  Bake in hot oven for 15 minutes. From:
Lancaster Farming Shared By: Pat Stockett
