Title: Ground Beef in Sour Cream [1-164]
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
Pepper to taste
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c  Chopped green peppers
1 c  Dairy sour cream
1 cn 3oz broiled mushrooms
1 lb Grd beef
1 c  Chopped onions
3 c  Medium noodles
3 c  Tomato juice
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Celery salt
BETTER HOMES & GARDENS  page 164 Lightly brwn grd beef in large
skillet. add chopped onion ; cook until tender but not brown. Place
noodles in layer over meat Combine tomato juice, salt, celery salt,
dash of pepper and worcestershire sauce; pour over noodles. Bring to
boil; cover, and simmer over low heat 20 minutes. Add chopped green
pepper. Cover and continue cooking 10 minutes or till noodles are
tender. Stir in sour cream and broiled mushrooms, drained. Heat just
to boiling. Season to taste. Posted by Ken Strei
