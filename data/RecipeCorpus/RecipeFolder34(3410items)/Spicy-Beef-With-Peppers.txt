Title: Spicy Beef With Peppers
Categories: Meats, Country wom, Wok
Yield: 6 servings
Diagonally
1/2 c  Water
3 tb Cooking oil -- divided
1 md Green pepper -- cut in
Strips
1 md Red pepper -- cut in strips
Cooked rice or chow mein
Noodles
2 tb Cornstarch -- divided
4 tb Dry sherry -- divided
Or beef broth divided
4 tb Soy sauce -- divided
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Crushed red pepper -- or t
Teaspoon
1 lb Top sirloin steak -- thinly
Sliced
In a medium bowl, combine 1 tablespoon of the cornstarch with 2
tablespoon of sherry or broth, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, garlic and
dried red pepper. Add beef and toss to coat. Set aside. In a small
bowl, combine water with remaining cornstarch, sherry and soy sauce.
Set aside. In a wok or skillet, heat 1 tablespoon oil on medium-high.
Add green and red peppers; stir-fry 1 min. Remove peppers to a
platter. Add remaining oil and half the beef; stir-fry until beef is
no longer pink. Remove and stir-fry remaing beef. Return peppers and
beef to pan. Stir cornstarch mixture and add to pan; bring to a boil,
stirring constantly. Cook 1 minute. Serve immediately with rice or
chow mein noodles. Yield: 4-6 servings.
