Title: SHIRLEY GOMES'S BAKED-STUFFED QUAHOGS - Country Living
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish, Country liv
Yield: 24 stuffed sh
1    Sweet green pepper, chopped
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter
Paprika or hot red-pepper
-sauce (opt.)
12    Fresh quahogs or large clams
1    12-oz package round butter
-flavored crackers, crushed
2    Stalks celery, chopped
1    Large onion, chopped
1. Remove quahogs from shells; reserve shells and the liquid. Wash
and dry reserved shells well.

2. In food processor, with chopping blade, process quahogs until
finely chopped. Add crackers, celery, onion, and green pepper.
Process just until combined. If mixture seems too dry, add some of
reserved quahog liquid.

3. In large skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. Add quahog
mixture; cook, stirring occasionally, 10 minutes.

4. Heat oven to 350'F. Place reserved shells on baking sheet. Divide
quahog mixture onto shells. Season with paprika or red-pepper sauce,
if desired. Bake 20 minutes or until golden brown.

Note: Stuffed Quahogs can be frozen in their shells before baking. To
reheat, heat oven to 350'F. Place quahogs in single layer on baking
sheet. Heat in oven 40 minutes or until golden brown.
