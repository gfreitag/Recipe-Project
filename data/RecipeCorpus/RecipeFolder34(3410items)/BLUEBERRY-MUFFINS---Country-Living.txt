Title: BLUEBERRY MUFFINS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Muffins
Yield: 6 servings
No ingredients found
Prepare Buttermilk Jam Muffins but omit jam and grease six 3-inch
crown muffin-pan cups. Fold 1 1/2 C fresh or unthawed frozen
blueberries into batter. Bake 25 to 30 minutes.
