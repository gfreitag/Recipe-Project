Title: Aztecburgers
Categories: Poultry, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Tomato paste, your choice,
-to taste
1 lb Ground turkey
1/4 lb Italian sausage
Recipe by: connorbd@bcvms.bc.edu

Mix together and grill.  Wrap in a corn or flour tortilla or serve on
a roll with salsa and accompaniments of your choice.
