Title: Chavrie & Basil Pizza Bites
Categories: Appetizers, Pizza
Yield: 12 servings
2    Plum tomatoes -- thin
Sliced
Olive oil
1/2 c  Fresh basil leaves --
Shredded
5 1/3 oz Chavrie (goat cheese)
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Dried oregano -- crushed
1/4 ts Black pepper
1 sm Loaf french bread
Preheat oven to 400 F.  Combine Chavrie, garlic, oregano, and pepper.
Mix well.  Cut French bread into 12 slices. Top each slice with a
slice of tomato and brush with olive oil. Place 1 t of cheese mixture
over each tomato slice.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until bread is
toasted. Remove from oven and top with shredded basil.
