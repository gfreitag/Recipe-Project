Title: ORANGE HORSERADISH CREAM - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
2 tb Dijon-style mustard
1/2 c  Heavy cream
Pinch of sugar
Salt & freshly ground black
-pepper to taste
1/2 c  Crime fraiche
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Prepared white horseradish,
-drained
2 tb Finely minced orange zest
In a bowl, mix together creme fraiche, mayonnaise, horseradish,
orange zest and mustard.

In a bowl, whip heavy cream until it forms soft peaks.

Using a rubber spatula, fold the whipped cream into the creme fraiche
mixture. Stir sugar, salt and pepper into the mixture and transfer it
to a serving bowl.
