Title: Cherry Plneapple Cake
Categories: Cakes, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Whlte cake mix
18 1/4 oz (without pudding)
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
Whipped cream, optional
2 cn Tart red cherries -- 16 oz
ea Drained
1 cn Crushed pineapple
20 oz In syrup,undrained
1/3 c  Walnuts -- finely chopped
Spread cherries and pineapple with juice over the bottom of an
ungreased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Top with nuts. Sprinkle
dry cake mix over all. Pour butter evenly over cake mix. Bake at 350
deg. for 1 hour. Cool. Invert onto a serving plate. Cut into squares.
Top with whipped cream if desired. Yield: 12-15 servings
