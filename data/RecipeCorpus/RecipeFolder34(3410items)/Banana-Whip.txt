Title: Banana Whip
Categories: Salads
Yield: 5 servings
1/2 c  Whole milk -- cold
-ice cubes
1 md Banana -- sliced
1    Box gelatin -- 4 serving
-size
3/4 c  Boiling water
Combine gelatin and boiling water in a blender.  Blend until
dissolved, about 30 seconds.  Add milk and ice cubes to make 1 1/4
cups.  Stir in blender until partially melted.  Add banana slices;
blend until smooth. 45 calories per serving.
