Title: Corned Beef and Swiss Cheese Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 3 servings
2 ts Finely chopped onion
1 tb Ketchup
2 ts Spicy brown mustard
1 c  Grated Swiss cheese
Rye crackers
8 oz Cream cheese, cut into bits
& softened
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 c  Drained, chopped sauerkraut
1/2 lb Cooked, lean corned beef,
Chopped fine
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  In a large bowl, combine cream cheese,
sour cream, sauerkraut, corned beef, onion, ketchup, mustard and
Swiss cheese. Transfer the dip to a small ovenproof casserole and
bake, covered, for 30 minutes, or until bubbles form around the
edges. Remove the cover and bake the dip for 5 more minutes, or until
it begins to turn golden. Serve the dip warm with rye crackers. Randy
