Title: Cheesy Fish Fillets With Spinach
Categories: Fish, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
Oz
Thawed and well drained
1 tb Lemon juice
1 lb Fish fillets -- cut in
Chunks
Serving pieces
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Grated parmesan cheese
Paprika
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1 ts Chicken bouillon -- instant
1 ds Nutmeg
1 ds Red pepper -- cayenne
1 ds White pepper
1 c  Milk
2/3 c  Shredded cheddar cheese --
Or shredded swiss
1 pk Frozen spinach -- chopped 10
Heat butter over low heat until melted. Stir in flour, bouillon,
nutmeg, red pepper and white pepper; cook over low heat, stirring
constantly, until mixture is smooth and bubbly. Stir in milk; heat to
boiling and cook, stirring constantly, for 1 min. Add cheese and
cook, stirring constantly, just until cheese melts. Set aside. Place
spinach in ungreased 12x7x2 inch baking dish or 8 inch square baking
pan. Sprinkle with the lemon juice. arrange fish on spinach; sprinkle
with salt. Spread sauce over fish and spinach. Bake, uncovered at 350
deg. until fish flakes easily with a fork about 20 minutes. Sprinkle
with the Parmesan cheese and paprika; return to oven for 5 minutes.
Yield: 4 servings.
