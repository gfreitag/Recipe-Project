Title: Basic Fettuccine Alfredo
Categories: Italian, Side dish, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Parmesan cheese
1/4 ts Pepper
3 c  Fettuccine
3 tb Unsalted butter
1 c  Heavy cream
Cook fettuccine according to directions, drain. In a pan, slowly
cook 2/3 cup cream and butter until thickened.  Add fettuccine,
remaining cream and cheese, pepper to taste. Stir till fettuccine is
coated, sauce thick. Serve immediately.
