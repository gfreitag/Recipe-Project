Title: Round Steak Ranchero
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Medium Onion, sliced
1 ea Garlic clove, minced
1/2 ts Thyme, dried
1/2 ts Pepper
1 1/2 lb Round Steak
2 tb Canola or Olive Oil
1/3 c  Red wine vinegar
2 tb A-1 Sauce
Make a marinade of all ingredients (except round steak).  Remove all
visable fat from the meat, cut into four pieces.  Marinate steak two
hours (or more - refrigerate if for longer period). Broil steak,
basting with marinade.
