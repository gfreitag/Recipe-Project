Title: Cream Of Broccoli And Cheese Soup
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn (10-3/4 ounces) cream of
Chicken soup -- undiluted
1/2 ts Salt -- if desired
1/8 ts White pepper
2 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Finely chopped onion
1 pk (lO-ounce)chopped broccoli
1 c  Cottage cheese
2 c  Whole milk
Cook celery, onion and broccoli in 2-1/2-qt. covered casserole in
microwave on HlGH for 6 minutes, stirring after 3 minutes. Set aside.
Blend cottage cheese in blender or food processor until very smooth;
slowly add milk while continuing to blend. Add chicken soup to
cheese/milk mixture; blend; add mixture to cooked, undrained
vegetables.Microwave on HlGH until heated through (about 3 minutes)
without boiling. Add salt, pepper. Yield: 6 servings
