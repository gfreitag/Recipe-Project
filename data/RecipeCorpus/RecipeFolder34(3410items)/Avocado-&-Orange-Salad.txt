Title: Avocado & Orange Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2    Avocados -- cut in 6 wedges
2    Oranges -- peeled & in
-segments
1/2    Red onion -- thinly sliced
1/2    Head romaine lettuce -- cut
-into thin strips
1/4 c  Curd black olives --
-chopped
6 tb Olive oil
1 ts Garlic -- minced
1 ts Sugar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Salt and pepper
In a small bowl, make the dressing: whisk together the chopped olives,
olive oil, garlic, sugar, lemon juice, salt and pepper.  In a large
bowl, combine the avocados, orange segments and onion.  Pour the
olive dressing over the avocado mixture and toss lightly. Serve on a
bed of the cut romaine.
