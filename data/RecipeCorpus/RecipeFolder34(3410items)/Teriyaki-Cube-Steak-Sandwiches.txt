Title: Teriyaki Cube Steak Sandwiches
Categories: Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
1 ts Canola Oil
1/2 ea Head Green Cabbage
4 ea Hamburger Buns
2 c  Frozen Curley Fries
4 ea Cube (minute) steaks
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
1 tb Brown Sugar
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Ground ginger
Pound cube steak to 1/8" thickness.  Marinate steaks for 3 hours in
mixture of next five ingredients.  Saute in hot oil for about 1 1/2
minutes each side.  Remove, put on buns and keep warm. Slice cabbage
thinly amd saute in drippings with any leftover marinade.
