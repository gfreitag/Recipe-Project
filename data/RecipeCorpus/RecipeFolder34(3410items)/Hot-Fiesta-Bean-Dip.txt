Title: Hot Fiesta Bean Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Hot/spicy
Yield: 3 servings
1 pk Taco seasoning mix
2 pk Tortilla chips
1 c  Sour cream
30 oz Can of refried beans
8 oz Picante sauce, mild - med.
Or hot
In a large saucepan, combine refried beans, picante sauce and taco
seasoning mix.  Stir over medium heat until thoroughly heated.
Garnish with sour cream.  Serve with tortilla chips.  The sour cream
is optional. Randy Rigg
