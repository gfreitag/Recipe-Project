Title: Pineapple-Pear Jam
Categories: Can/preserv, Fruit, Bon appetit
Yield: 5 cups
-- cored, cut into 1" pieces
4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/2 md Pineapple; cored, peeled,
-- cut into 1-inch pieces
4 lg Ripe Anjou pears; peeled,
In food processor, pulse pineapple until evenly chopped.  Strain.
Place pineapple juice in a large non-reactive saucepan with pears.
Cook over medium low heat until tender (about 10 minutes), stirring
occasionally. Add chopped pineapple, sugar and lemon juice to
saucepan. Turn heat down to low and let mixture simmer until thick,
stirring frequently, about 1 hour. Cool.
