Title: Cider Jelly
Categories: Canning, Preserves, Fruit, Country wom
Yield: 30 servings
-3/4 oz
5 c  Sugar
1 qt Apple cider
2/3 c  Red hots candy
1 pk Powdered fruit pectin -- 1
In a large kettle. heat cider, red hots and pectin, stirring often.
bring to a full rolling boil. Add sugar; return to a full rolling
boil, stirring constantly. boil for 1 min. remove from the heat; skim
off any foam. Pour hot into hat jars, leaving 1/4 in. headspace.
Adjust caps. Process for 10 min. in a boiling-water bath. Yield about
6 half-pints.
