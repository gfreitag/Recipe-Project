Title: BASIC HOT CHOCOLATE - Country Living
Categories: Beverages, Chocolate, Country liv
Yield: 1 serving
Miniature marshmallows
-(opt.)
1 c  Skim milk
2 ts Sugar
1 ts Unsweetened cocoa powder
In microwave-safe mug or cup, beat milk, sugar, and cocoa until well
combined. Microwave on high (100 percent) 2 to 2 1/2 minutes or until
mixture is heated through. Stir and serve immediately. Top with
marshmallows, if desired.
