Title: MOCK FONDANT - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 1 fondant
MMMMM-------------------------FOR SYRUP------------------------------
5 tb Water
1 1/4 c  Granulated sugar
3 tb Com syrup
MMMMM------------------------FOR FONDANT-----------------------------
Paste or liquid food
-coloring, available at cake
-supply stores
1/2 c  Syrup
1 ts Egg white
1 ts Butter, room temp.
1 1/2 c  Sifted confectioners' sugar
Prepare syrup: In a small heavy pan over low heat, dissolve sugar,
corn syrup and water, stirring occasionally. Place lid on pan and
boil mixture for 5 minutes; remove lid and boil 5 more minutes and
remove pan from heat, Cool.

Prepare fondant: In a small bowl add syrup, egg white and butter.
Gradually add confectioners' sugar, stirring with a wooden spoon or
hand mixer, until just blended. Choose food coloring and add a small
amount to mixture and stir. If fondant is too stiff to pour as a
coating, add a few drops warm water. Fondant can be made several days
ahead and refrigerated. When ready to use, allow mixture to return to
room temperature. Mixture will cover about 18 large hat cookies.
