Title: Tuna Chili Texas-Style
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Microwave, Casseroles, Chili
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Tuna (6-1/2 oz); drained and
-flaked
2 tb Red wine vinegar
2 tb Chili powder*
1 ts Dried basil; crushed
1 ts Dried oregano; crushed
1/2 ts Ground cumin
Bottled hot pepper sauce
; to taste
1 c  Red or green bell pepper;
-cut in 1" pieces
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1    Clove garlic; minced
2 tb ; water
1 cn Whole tomatoes w/juice-
(28 oz) cut up
1 cn Kidney beans-8 oz; drained
*If you don't like it too hot, cut to 1 tablespoon. In a 3-quart
microwavable bowl or casserole, combine bell pepper, onion, celery,
garlic and water. Cover with waxed paper; micro-cook on High power
for 4-5 minutes or until vegetables are nearly tender. Stir in
remaining ingredients excpet hot pepper sauce. Cover; micro-cook on
High power for 15-17 minutes, to allow flavors to blend, stirring
every 3 minutes. Season to taste with hot pepper sauce.  Makes 4
servings. Preparation time: 10 minutes. Calories: 206 per
