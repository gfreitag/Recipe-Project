Title: Beef Gumbo
Categories: Cajun/creol, Soup/stew, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1 ea Meaty soup bone
1/4 c  Parsley, chopped
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Okra, sliced
2 c  Tomatoes, canned
2 tb Butter or beef fat
1/4 c  Celery, chopped
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Celery, leafy, chopped
In a large pot, brown soup meat in fat. Add 3 qts water, celery,
parsley, onion, salt and paprika. Simmer until meat falls off bone,
2-3 hrs. Strain, cool and skim the broth. In a skillet, saute onion,
okra and celery in butter. In the soup pot, combine, stock, meat,
sauteed vegetables and tomatoes. Simmer a few min for flavors to
blend. Submitted By BOBBI ZEE   On   08-23-95
