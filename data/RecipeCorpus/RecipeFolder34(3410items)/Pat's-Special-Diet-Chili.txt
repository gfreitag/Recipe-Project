Title: Pat's Special Diet Chili
Categories: Meats, Soup/stew, Low cal/fat, Chili
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 cn Chili powder; (3.5oz jar)
1 ts Cumin
Garlic powder; to taste
Salt and pepper; to taste
2 pk Artifical sweetener
3 lb Ground meat
1    Red onion; chopped
2    Bell peppers; chopped
1 cn Tomato juice (46oz)
2 cn French cut green beans
Brown meat and drain fat.  Combine meat with other ingredients into
large pot and cook to chili consistency. Serves 6.
