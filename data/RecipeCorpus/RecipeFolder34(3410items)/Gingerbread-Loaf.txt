Title: Gingerbread Loaf
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 loaf
MMMMM-----------------------1.5 POUND LOAF----------------------------
1 tb Brown sugar
3/4 ts Salt
3/4 ts Ground cinnamon
3/4 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Yeast
3/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Light molasses
1    Egg
3 tb Margarine
3 1/3 c  Bread flour
MMMMM-------------------LEMON ICING (OPTIONAL------------------------
1 ts Lemon juice
3 ts Milk
1/2 c  Powdered sugar, sifted
1/4 ts Vanilla
Add the first 10 ingredients to machine according to manufacturer's
directions. Use light setting. If desired, when bread is cool, drizzle
bread with Lemon Icing. Lemon Icing: In a small mixing bowl stir
together the powdered sugar, vanilla, lemon juice and enough milk to
make an icing of drizzling consistency.
