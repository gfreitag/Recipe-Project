Title: Garden Medley
Categories: Vegetables, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Cut into julienne strips
1 ea Yellow pepper
Cut into julienne strips
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1 ds Pepper
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 md Zucchini squash
Cut into julienne strips
1 ea Sweet red pepper
Cut into julienne strips
1 ea Green pepper
In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Saute vegetables until
crisp-tender. Season with salt and pepper. Yield: 4-6 servings
