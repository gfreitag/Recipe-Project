Title: Persian Rice SL
Categories: Pasta/rice, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Orange juice
1 tb Fresh parsley; chopped
1/4 ts Grated orange rind
1/4 c  Slivered almonds; toasted
2 tb Butter
1 1/3 c  Uncooked rice
1 ts Salt
3/4 c  Raisins
2 c  Chicken broth
Melt butter in a small Dutch oven over medium heat.  Add rice; cook,
stirring constantly, until rice is lightly browned.  Add next 4
ingredients; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer 20
minutes. Remove from heat; let stand 5 minutes.  Stir in remaining
ingredients. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
