Title: Grilled Beef Kabobs
Categories: Beef, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
10 sl Bacon -- cut in half
1 lg Sweet red peppers -- cut ino
Chunks
1 lg Green pepper -- cut into
Chunks
2 sm Zucchini squash -- cut in
Chunks
8 md Fresh mushrooms
1 lg Onion -- quartered
1 lb Sirloin steak -- cut into 1
1/2    Cubes
Boneless
1    Bottle Russian salad
-dressing, or French (8 oz)
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Garlic powder
Place beef in a shallow glass pan. Combine salad dressing,
worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, pepper and garlic powder. Pour
over beef. Cover and refrigerate 8 to 24 hours. Drain and reserve
marinade. Wrap bacon around beef cubes. On metal or soaked wooden
skewers, alternately thread beef and vegetables. Grill over hot coals
for 10 to 15 min. or until the desired doneness is reached. Baste
frequently with marinade. Yield: 4 servings.
