Title: Breakfast Pizza Iii
Categories: Breakfast, Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
4 oz Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 cn Mushrooms; slice --
Drain-4oz
1 cn Refrigerated crescent rolls
6    Eggs -- beaten
1/2 lb Bacon -- cook and crumble
Spread rolls onto a lightly greased 12" pizza pan; firmly press
perforations to seal. Combine eggs, bacon, cheese and mushrooms; pour
over prepared crust. Bake ata 375~ for 12 to 15 minutes.
