Title: Italian Style Macaroni & Cheese
Categories: Main dish, Pasta/rice, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Ham -- coarsely chopped
1/4 c  Basil -- chopped
2 c  Sargento 6 Cheese Italian
-Recipe Blend -- divided
Fresh ground pepper
8 oz Elbow macaroni
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 tb Flour
1 3/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
Cook macaroni according to package directions.  In large saucepan melt
butter over medium heat.  Add flour; stir 1 minute.  Add milk, salt
and cayenne pepper; heat to a boil, stirring constantly.  Drain
macaroni; add to saucepan.  Toss to coat with sauce.  Remove from
heat; add ham and basil; toss again.  Transfer half of macaroni
mixture to greased shallow baking dish.   Top with 1 cup cheese.
Repeat layering with remaining macaroni mixture and remaining 1 cup
cheese.  Bake uncovered at 350 for 25 to 30 minutes.  Serve with
freshly ground pepper.
