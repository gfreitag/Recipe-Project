Title: Maine Scalloped Scallops
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 pt Sea scallops
Onion salt, pepper
1 c  Heavy cream
1 c  Fine cracker crumbs
1/2 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/2 c  Melted butter or margarine
Mix crumbs with butter.  Arrange alternate layers of scallops,
seasoned with onion salt, pepper, and crumbs in baking dish.  Pour
cream over mixture.  Bake in moderate oven (350F) for 30 minutes.
Makes 4 servings.
