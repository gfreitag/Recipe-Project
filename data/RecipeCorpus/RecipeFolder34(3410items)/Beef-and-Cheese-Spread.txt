Title: Beef and Cheese Spread
Categories: Appetizers, Meats, Cheese/eggs, Dips/spread
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Green bell pepper flakes
2 tb Milk
1/4 c  Chopped pecans
Crackers of your choice
2 1/2 oz Jar dried beef
8 oz Philadelphia cream cheese
4 oz Sour cream
2 tb Minced onion
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cut slices of dried beef into small
pieces and put into medium mixing bowl.  Add cream cheese, sour
cream, onion, bell peppers and milk.  Mix thoroughly with a spoon.
Pour mixture into a round glass pie pan and sprinkle chopped pecans
on top. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.  Serve hot on crackers.
