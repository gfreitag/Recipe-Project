Title: Raw Fava Bean Spread
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 1 cup
1/3 c  Extra-virgin olive oil
1 1/2 c  Shelled fava beans
-(abt 1 1/2 lb in the shell)    1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
6    Mint leaves
Pepper
2 tb Romano cheese
-freshly grated
1/2 ts Garlic, finely chopped
1. Slip your fingernail or the tip of a paring knife under the pale
skin of each fava bean and peel the skin away.

2. Put the peeled favas and all the remaining ingredients in a food
processor and puree until creamy. Transfer to a bowl and serve.
