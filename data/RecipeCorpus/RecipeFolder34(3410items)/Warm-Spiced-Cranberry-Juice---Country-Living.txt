Title: Warm Spiced Cranberry Juice - Country Living
Categories: Beverages, Country liv
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground cloves
3 qt Cranberry-juice cocktail
4    4-inch sticks cinnamon
1. In 4-quart saucepan, heat cranberry-juice cocktail, cinnamon,
nutmeg, and cloves to boiling over high heat. Reduce heat to low and
simmer 5 minutes.

2. Serve immediately in small mugs or pour spiced juice into thermos
containers to transport to a picnic.
