Title: Oreo Mud Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Heavy cream, whipped
1/4 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Chocolate fudge topping
1 pt Chocolate ice cream, soft
1    Oreo pie crust
2 pt Coffee ice cream, soft
Evenly spread chocolate ice cream into crust. Scoop coffee ice cream
into balls; arrange over chocolate layer. Freeze 4 hours or until
firm. To serve, top with whipped cream, walnuts and fudge topping.
