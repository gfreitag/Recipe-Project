Title: OLD FASHIONED POT ROAST
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Crockpot
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 cn Condensed beef broth
3 x  Medium potatoes,peeled+cut
3 x  Carrots,pared and cut small
3 x  Onions,peeled and cut small
4 lb Bottom round or rump roast
Place all ingrediants in order given in slow cooker. Cover. Turn
control on LOW. Cook 8-10 hours or until meat is done Serves: 6
