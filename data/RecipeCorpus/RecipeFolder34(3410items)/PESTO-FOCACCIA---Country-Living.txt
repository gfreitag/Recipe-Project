Title: PESTO FOCACCIA - Country Living
Categories: Breads, Country liv
Yield: 1 10-inch fo
1/3 c  Olive oil
2 ts Cornmeal
1 oz Goat cheese, coarsely
-crumbled
1 tb Pine nuts
Basic Pizza Dough
1/2 c  Tightly packed fresh basil
-leaves
2 tb Coarsely chopped walnuts
1    Clove garlic
1/8 ts Salt
1. Prepare Basic Pizza Dough. Set aside to rise.

2. Meanwhile, in food processor, with chopping blade, pro- cess basil,
walnuts, garlic, and salt until finely chopped. With processor blade
whirling, slowly pour in oil until mixture has combined to make pesto.

3. Heat oven to 450'F. Shape pizza dough into 10-inch round. Lightly
dust baking sheet with cornmeal. Place round 14 on baking sheet.
Brush top of round with some pesto. Sprinkle with goat cheese and
pine nuts.

4. Bake focaccia on middle oven rack 10 to 12 minutes or until golden
brown, Serve immediately. Dip focaccia in any remaining pesto, if
desired. Country Living/October/90  Scanned & fixed by DP & GG
