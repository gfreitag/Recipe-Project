Title: Rosemary Chicken Saute Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1    12" pizza crust
1 cn Chunky tomatoes
2 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
1    Green pepper -- sliced
2    Chicken breasts -- cut
Strips
1 md Onion -- sliced
1/2 ts Rosemary -- crushed
1 tb Vegetable oil
Preheat oven to 450F. In large skillet, cook chicken, onion and
rosemary in oil over medium high heat. Drain, Place crust on baking
sheet. Spread tomatoes evenly over crust. Top with 1/2 cheese the
chicken mixture, pepper slices and remaining cheese. Bake 10 minutes
or until hot and bubbly.
