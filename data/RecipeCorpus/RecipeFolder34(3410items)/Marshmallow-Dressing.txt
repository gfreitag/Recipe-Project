Title: Marshmallow Dressing
Categories: Condiments, Salads, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
2/3 c  Light corn syrup
1 tb Grated orange rind
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Hot water
2    Stiff beaten egg whites
1 ds Salt
Combine sugar, corn syrup, and hot water.  Heat slowly, stirring until
sugar dissolves, then boil without sitrring to form ball stage.
Gradually beat hot syrup into the stiff beaten egg whites.  Add salt
and vanilla. Coll and fold in mayonnaise and orange peel.  Mix well.
Serve as a dressing for fruit salad or sliced pineapple or as a
dunking dip for fresh strawberries.
