Title: Corn Pickle or Salad (Mennonite)
Categories: Pickles, Can/preserv, Vegetables, Mennonite
Yield: 1 bunch
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Vinegar
1 c  Water
1 1/2 tb Dry mustard
12 ea Ears corn
1/2 ea Head cabbage
4 ea Peppers, red or green
1 bn Celery
1 1/2 tb Salt
Cook corn on the cob until tender.  Cut off. Chop cabbage, celery,
peppers into small pieces and cook until tender
but not soft. Drain. Mix vegetables together and add sugar, salt,
vinegar, and mustard. Bring to a boil and put in jars. Seal.

Esther Rhodes of Virginia, Mrs Noah Zimmerman of Pennsylvania
_Mennonite Community Cookbook_   Mary Emma Showalter  1950 The
