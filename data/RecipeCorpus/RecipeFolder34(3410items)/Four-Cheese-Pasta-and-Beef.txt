Title: Four Cheese Pasta and Beef
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Pasta/rice, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 5 servings
3 oz Cream cheese
1/2 c  Shredded swiss cheese
1/2 c  Parmesan, shredded
1/2 c  Sharp cheddar, shredded
6 oz Penne or tube pasta
3/4 lb Ground beef
1 x  Can stewed tomatoes(14 oz)
1 c  Sliced green onions
Cook pasta and drain. Brown meat in a skillet; add salt and pepper
to taste, drain. Add tomatoes and cook uncovered until slightly
thickened. Stir in onions and cream cheese. Toss with remaining
ingredients. Cover 2 minutes to melt cheese, serve immediately.
