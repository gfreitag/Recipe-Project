Title: PEACH-BUTTERMILK FROZEN TREAT - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Fruit, Ice cream, Desserts
Yield: 1 quart
2 c  Buttermilk
1 tb Grated peeled fresh ginger
3 c  Sliced, peeled ripe peaches
-(about 1/4 lb)
1/2 c  Sugar
Toss peaches with sugar in large bowl. Let stand until peaches exude
juice and sugar has dissolved, stirring occasionally, about 45
minutes.

Transfer peaches to processor or blender. Add buttermilk and ginger
and puree, stopping to scrape down sides, about 3 minutes.

Transfer mixture to ice cream maker and process according to
manufactur- er's instructions, or, without an ice cream maker, freeze
following directions in Very Berry Ice Milk recipe.

Transfer to plastic container. Cover and freeze several hours or
overnight to mellow flavors. If frozen solid, let soften slightly at
room temperature.

Bon Appetit/August/89  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
