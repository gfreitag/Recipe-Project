Title: Mock Pina Coladas^
Categories: Beverages, Low cal/fat, Healthy
Yield: 6 servings
Juice, undrained
1 c  Crushed ice
1/2 ts To 1 rum extract
1/2 ts Coconut extract
Fast & Healthy 8/93
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
2 c  Frozen nonfat vanilla
Yogurt
1 c  Crushed pineapple in its own
Combine all ingredients in blender. Cover; blend until smooth. Serve
immediately. 6 (1/2 cup) servings.

Per serving: 80 calories, 2 g protein, 19 g carbohydrate, 1 g fiber,
40 mg sodium, 50 mg potassium and 1 starch exchange.
