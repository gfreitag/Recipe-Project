Title: Individual Tuna Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 1 servings
2    Green pepper rings --
Halved
1/4 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
1    8" flour tortilla
1/4 c  Pizza sauce
1 cn Tuna chunks
1/4 c  Sliced mushrooms
1/4 c  Tomato -- sliced
Preheat oven to 375F.  Place tortilla on a small baking sheet. Bake
for 5 minutes, or until tortilla begins to crisp. Spread sauce to 1/2
" of edge. Sprinkle tunan mushrooms, and tomato over tortilla.
Arrange bell pepper half-rings on top.  Sprinkle cheese over pizza.
Bake for 8 to 10 minutes more or until heated through.
