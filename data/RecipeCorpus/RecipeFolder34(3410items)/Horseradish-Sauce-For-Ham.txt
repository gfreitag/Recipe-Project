Title: Horseradish Sauce For Ham
Categories: Pork, Ham, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
4 dr Hot pepper sauce
1 c  Heavy cream -- whipped
1/4 c  Prepared horseradish
1 1/2 ts Vinegar
1 tb Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Salt
In a small bowl, combine first six ingredients. Mix well. Gently fold
in whipped cream. Chill. Serve with sliced ham, ham loaf or ham
balls. Yield: about 2 cups
