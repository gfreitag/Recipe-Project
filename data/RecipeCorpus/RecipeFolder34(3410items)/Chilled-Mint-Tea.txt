Title: Chilled Mint Tea
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 /2 gallon
6 oz (1 cn) Lemonade concentrate;
-- frozen
6 oz (1) Can frozen orange juice;
-- concentrate
2 c  Water;
3    Family-size tea bags;
10    Fresh mint leaves;
1/2 c  Sugar;
Bring water to boil in large, nonaluminun saucepan.  Add tea bags and
mint leaves, reduce heat and let remove tea bags and mint.  Add sugar
and stir until dissolved.  Using 1/2-gallon pitcher, prepare lemonade
and orange juice concentrates, following package directions.  Add
sweetened mint tea. Refrigerate mixture overnight.  Serve over ice.
