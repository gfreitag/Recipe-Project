Title: Chocolate Raspberry Yogurt Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Cool Whip Lite* -- thawed
1 c  Strawberries -- sliced
1    Pie crust, chocolate wafer
2/3 c  Boiling water
1    Box strawberry gelatin
Powder -- 4 serving size
1 c  Frozen vanilla yogurt
Stir boiling water into gelatin in large bowl 2 minutes or until
completed dissolved.  Stir in frozen yogurt until melted.  Stir in
whipped topping and fruit.  Refrigerate until mixture mounds, if
necessary.  Spoon into crust.  Refrigerate 2 hours or until firm.
