Title: Triple Cheese Spread
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Shredded carrots
1/4 c  Unsalted sunflower seeds
(opt)
1 c  Cottage cheese
1/2 c  Shredded Swiss
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan
2 tb Milk
1/8 ts Dill weed
In a blender or food processor, combine cheeses, milk, dill and
pepper. Process until smooth.  Stir in carrots.  Cover and chill.
Just before serving, stir in the sunflower seeds, if desired.

Makes 1 3/4 c
