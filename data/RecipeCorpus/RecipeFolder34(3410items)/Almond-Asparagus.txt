Title: Almond Asparagus
Categories: Vegetables, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 lb Fresh asparagus
Trimmed and cut into 1-inch
Pieces
1 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Butter or margarine --
Divided
3 tb Bread crumbs
1 ea Garlic clove -- minced
1/2 ts Dill weed
1/2 c  Sliced almonds
In a skillet, melt 2 tablespoons butter over medium heat. Stir in
bread crumbs, garlic and dill: saute until crumbs are golden brown.
Remove from heat; stir in the almonds and cheese. Set aside. Cook
asparagus in a small amount of water until crisp-tender. Drain; heat
with remaining butter. Sprinkle with lemon juice. Spoon asparagus
into a serving dish and top with the reserved crumb mixture. Yield:
4-6 servings
