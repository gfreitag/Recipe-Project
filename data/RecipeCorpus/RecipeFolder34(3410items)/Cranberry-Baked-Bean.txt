Title: Cranberry Baked Bean
Categories: Beans, Country wom
Yield: 16 servings
Undrained
1 tb Mustard -- prepared
1/2 c  Chopped onion
6    Strips bacon -- halved
3 tb Brown sugar
1 cn Cranberry sauce -- jellied
16    Oz
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 8 oz
2 cn Pork and beans -- 31 oz.
Each
In a large bowl, combine the cranberry sauce, tomato sauce, beans,
mustard and onion. Place in a greased 13x9x2 in baking dish. Lay
bacon on top. Sprinkle with brown sugar. Bake at 350 deg. for 1 hour.
Yield: 12 to 16 servings.
