Title: Spinach with Basil (Shaker)
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ts Dried whole basil
4 lb Fresh spinach
6 ea Sprigs parsley; chopped
4 ea Green onions; chopped
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
Remove stems from spinach; wash leaves thoroughly, and tear into
pieces. Place all ingredients in a large Dutch oven (do not add
water). Cover, and cook over medium-high heat for 5 minutes or until
spinach is limp.
