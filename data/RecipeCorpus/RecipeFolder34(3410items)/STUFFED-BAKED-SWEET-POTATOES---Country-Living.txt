Title: STUFFED BAKED SWEET POTATOES - Country Living
Categories: Side dish, Holiday, Vegetables, Country liv
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Brown sugar
8 md Sweet potatoes
1/4 c  Orange juice
3 tb Butter or margarine,
-melted
1. Heat oven to 350'F. Rinse and dry sweet potatoes well. Place
potatoes on baking sheet and bake 55 to 60 minutes or until tender.
Do not turn off oven. Cool potatoes until easy to handle.

2. When potatoes are cool, cut in half lengthwise. With spoon, scoop
out pulp from 8 potato halves into large bowl and discard skins.
Scoop out pulp from remaining 8 potato halves, leaving 1/4 inch
potato intact on skin to make shells. Place pulp in same large bowl;
return potato shells to same baking sheet.

3. With electric mixer, beat potato pulp, orange juice, 2 T  butter,
the cinnamon, and salt until smooth. Place potato mixture into large
pastry bag fitted with star tip; pipe into potato shells, spiraling
mixture above shell line.

4. Sprinkle remaining 1 T melted butter and the brown sugar over
stuffed potatoes. Bake 10 to 15 minutes until potatoes are heated
through.
