Title: Light Caramel Sauce^
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 9 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Nonfat vanilla yogurt
Fast & Healthy 12/94
Carolyn Shaw 8-95
3/4 c  Water
In small saucepan, combine all ingredients except yogurt; mix well.
Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Boil 8 minutes without
stirring. Cool to room temperature. Add yogurt; blend well. Makes
about 1 cup.

Per 1/9 of recipe: 90 cal., 0 g protein, 24 g carb., 0 g fiber and
fat, 0 mg chol., 10 mg sodium, 55 mg potassium, 1 1/2 fruit exchanges.
