Title: Pralines - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Desserts, Candies
Yield: 32 pralines
2 c  Pecan halves
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/8 ts Salt
1 tb Vanilla
2 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Firmly packed light-brown
-sugar
1/4 c  Half-and-half
1. In heavy 3-quart saucepan, combine both sugars and half-and-half.
Heat to boiling over medium heat, stirring occasionally.

2. Continue boiling until mixture reaches 228'F on candy thermometer.
Stir in pecans, butter, and salt; cook, stirring, until 236'F is
reached on thermometer. Or, to test, drop some candy into cold water;
if it forms a soft ball, it is ready.

3. Place pan on wire rack; cool candy 10 minutes. Stir in vanilla;
beat until almost thick, then drop by tablespoonfuls onto oiled waxed
paper. Cool completely. In airtight container, pack pralines between
layers of waxed paper and store in cool, dry place.
