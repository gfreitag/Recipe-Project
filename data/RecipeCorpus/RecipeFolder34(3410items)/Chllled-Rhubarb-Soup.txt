Title: Chllled Rhubarb Soup
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sugar
Sliced oranges, kiwifruit
And/or
Additional strawberries,
Optional
1 pt Fresh strawberries --
Sliced
3 c  Rhubarb -- sliced
Fresh or frozen (1/2-inch
Pieces)
1 1/4 c  Orange juice
In a 3-qt. saucepan, bring strawberries, rhubarb and orange juice to a
boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove from heat;
stir in sugar to taste. In a blender or food processor, blend half
the fruit mixture at a time until Smooth. Chill. To serve, spoon into
soup bowls and, if desired, garnish with oranges, kiwi and/or
strawberries. Yield: about 1 qt
