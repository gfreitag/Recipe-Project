Title: Cauliflower-Broccoli Scallop
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Minced onion
1/2 ts Salt
ds Of pepper
1 ea (9 oz.) pkg. frozen broccoli
1 ea (9 oz.) pkg. frozen
-cauliflower
1 ea (10 1/2 oz.) can celery soup
2 ea Beaten eggs
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/4 c  Parsley
1/4 c  Pimiento
Mix together and put in 10 x 6 x 1 1/2 inch baking dish.  Bake at 375
degrees for 45 minutes.
