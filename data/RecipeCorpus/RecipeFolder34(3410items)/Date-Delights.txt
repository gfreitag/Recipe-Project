Title: Date Delights
Categories: Cookies, Country wom
Yield: 48 servings
3/4 c  Pecans -- chopped
2 c  Crispy rice cereal
Confectioners'
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 ea Egg
3/4 c  Chopped dates
Combine sugar, butter, egg, dates and pecans in a heavy saucepan;
bring to a boil, stirring thoroughly. Cook over medium heat until
well blended, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in cereal.
Cool. With buttered hands, roll into 1 inch balls; roll in
confectioners' sugar. Yield: about 4 dozen.
