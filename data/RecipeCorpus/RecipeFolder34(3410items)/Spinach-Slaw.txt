Title: Spinach Slaw
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 16 servings
Dressing
1/4 c  Honey
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
8 c  Iceberg lettuce -- shredded
5 c  Spinach -- shredded
4 c  Shredded red cabbage
3 c  Shredded cabbage
1 c  Mayonnaise -- or salad
In a large bowl, toss lettuce, spinach and cabbage; cover and
refrigerate. In a large bowl, toss lettuce, spinach and cabbage;
cover and refrigerate. In a small bowl, combine remianing
ingredients; cover and refrigerate. Just before serving, pour
dressing over the salad and toss to coat. Yield: 12 to 15 servings.
