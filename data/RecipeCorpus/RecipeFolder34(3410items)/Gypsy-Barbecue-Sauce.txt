Title: Gypsy Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Black Pepper
1/2 tb Hot Red Pepper -- finely
-chopped
1 tb Salt
1 pt (Scant) White Vinegar
1    Bottle Worcestershire Sauce
(10 Oz)
1    Bottle Hot Pepper Sauce (2
-Oz)
1    Stick Butter Or Margarine
In a large saucepan combine all ingredients and bring to a boil. Lower
heat, stir thoroughly, and simmer about 10 mins. Cool and refrigerate
till ready to use.
