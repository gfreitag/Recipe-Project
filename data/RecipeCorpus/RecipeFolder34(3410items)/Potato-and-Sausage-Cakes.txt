Title: Potato and Sausage Cakes
Categories: Sausage, Potatoes, Main dish, Oldies
Yield: 1 main dish
3 c  Potatoes; mashed
Fine dry crumbs;
1 c  Cooked sausage meat; minced
1    Egg;
Combine the sausage meat, egg and potato; form into small flat cakes,
roll in the crumbs and saute in the sausage fat until browned on both
sides.

Source: The Service Cook Book NO. 2 by Mrs. Ida Bailey Allen,
Published exclusively for F. W. Woolworth Co.  Copyright 1935.
