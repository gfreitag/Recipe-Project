Title: Beans And Ham > Dorothy Flatman
Categories: Vegetables, Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Onion, diced
1 c  Carrots, sliced thin
1 tb Season salt
12 oz Dried beans
5 c  Water
2 c  Ham, diced
1 c  Celery, chopped
Combine all ingredients in Slow cooker.  Cook on low 8 hours or until
beans are tender.

Note: Use 4 cups water for Lima beans.
Use 2 T dried vegetables in place of 1 c fresh.
I use 1 T dried carrot tops in place of sliced fresh carrots.
