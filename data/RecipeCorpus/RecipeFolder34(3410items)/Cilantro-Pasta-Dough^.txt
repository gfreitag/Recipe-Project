Title: Cilantro Pasta Dough^
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
4 ts Water
1 ts Olive oil
2 tb Flour
1 c  Flour
1/4 c  Coarsley chopped cilantro
1/4 ts Salt
1    Egg, beaten
Stir together the flour, cilantro and salt in a bowl. Make a well in
the center. combine water, egg and olive oil. Add to flour mixture
and mix well. Sprinkle a flat surface with 2 tablespoons flour. Turn
dough out onto the surface. Knead till the doug is smooth and
elastic, about 10 minutes. Cover and let rest 10 minutes. Divide
dough in half; shape each half into a ball.

This recipes goes with Chicken Ravioli with Walnut Butter Sauce.
