Title: SESAME-SCENTED CORN AND EGGPLANT SALAD - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Vegetable broth or water
2 cn (14 1/2-oz) whole-kernel
- corn, drained
2 tb Chopped pimiento
1 tb Sesame oil
1    1-lb eggplant, cut into 1/2-
-inch cubes
1    Clove garlic, coarsely
-chopped
1. In large, nonstick skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add
eggplant and garlic; cook, stirring, until browned.

2. Add vegetable broth to eggplant. Cover and cook, stirring
occasionally, until eggplant is tender, about 5 minutes.

3. Uncover skillet and cook, stirring, until liquid evaporates. Add
corn and pimiento; stir until combined.
