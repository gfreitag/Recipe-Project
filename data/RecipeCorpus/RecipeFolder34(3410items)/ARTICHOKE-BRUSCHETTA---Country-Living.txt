Title: ARTICHOKE BRUSCHETTA - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Vegetables, Appetizers
Yield: 24 bruschetta
3    Cloves garlic, pressed
2 tb Olive oil
1 1/2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
Fresh-grated Parmesan cheese
-(opt.)
1    14-inch loaf Italian bread
1 c  Drained, canned artichoke
-hearts, chopped
1 c  Chopped, seeded, fresh
-tomato
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1. Heat oven to 350'F. With serrated knife, cut off ends from bread
and reserve for another use. Slice remaining bread crosswise into
twenty-four 1/2-inch-thick pieces. Place bread slices on ungreased
baking sheet; bake 10 minutes or until lightly toasted.

2. In medium-size bowl, combine artichoke hearts, tomato, onion,
garlic, oil, vinegar, and pepper.

3. Divide artichoke mixture among toasted bread and spread to cover
surface. Drizzle any juice remaining in bowl over bruschetta and
sprinkle with cheese, if desired. Arrange bruschetta on platter and
serve.
