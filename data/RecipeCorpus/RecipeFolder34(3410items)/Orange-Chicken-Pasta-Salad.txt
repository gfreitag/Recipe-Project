Title: Orange Chicken Pasta Salad
Categories: Poultry, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
-breast halves, cooked &
-cubed
2 c  Rotini -- cooked & drained
11 oz Mandarin oranges -- drained
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
1 c  Miracle Whip*
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 ts Dried basil
1/4 ts Ground ginger
4    Skinless boneless chicken
Mix dressing, juice and seasonings in large bowl.  Add remaining
ingredients; mix lightly.  Refrigerate.
