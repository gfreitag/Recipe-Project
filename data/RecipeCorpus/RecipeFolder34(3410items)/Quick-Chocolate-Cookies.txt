Title: Quick Chocolate Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Miracle Whip+
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 pk Chocolate cake mix
1 c  Chocolate chips
2    Eggs
Heat oven to 350.  Mix all ingredients in large bowl with electric
mixer on medium speed until blended.  Batter will be thick.  Drop by
rounded tsp onto greased cookie sheets.  Bake 10 to 12 minutes. Makes
4 dozen.
