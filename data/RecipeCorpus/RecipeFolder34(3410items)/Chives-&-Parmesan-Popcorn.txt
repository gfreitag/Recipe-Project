Title: Chives & Parmesan Popcorn
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Snacks
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Finely grated parmesan
-cheese
-salt and pepper
2/3 c  Popcorn
1/3 c  Butter
1/2 c  Fresh chives
Pop the popcorn.  Melt the butter.  Grind the pepper into the butter,
(as much as you want).  Chop the chives up and sprinkle on the top of
the popcorn along with the grated cheese.  Drizzle the butter mixture
over the popcorn and salt.
