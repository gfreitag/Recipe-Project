Title: Aunt Sudie Winkler's Peach Cobbler
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Baking powder
3/4 c  Milk
2 c  Sliced peaches
6 tb Butter
2 c  Sugar; (divided 1 c. & 1 c.)
3/4 c  Flour
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Melt butter (or margarine) in bottom
of 2-quart baking dish. Combine 1 cup sugar, flour, baking powder and
salt. Add milk and stir. Pour batter over butter, but do not stir.
Combine peaches and 1 cup sugar and spoon over batter.  Do Not stir.
Bake for 1 hour.

Per serving: Calories 666, Fat 20g, Cholesterol 56 mg, Sodium 437 mg,
Percent calories from fat 26%

Recipe from Sudie Lightsey, Mexia, Texas
