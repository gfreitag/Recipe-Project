Title: Shrimp Ceviche
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Appetizers
Yield: 50 servings
1/2 c  To 1 c cilantro, chopped
2/3 c  To 1 cup Lime juice
2 1/2 lb Cooked salad shrimp or more
5 lg Red onions, diced
5    Jalepenos, diced
5 lg Tomatoes, peeled and diced
Mix everything but shrimp together. Refrigerate for 4 hours or
overnight. Add shrimp just before serving. Will keep for a week in
the Frig. Submitted By KATHRYN CONE   On   08-20-95
