Title: Quick Fruit Cup
Categories: Fruits, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
2 ea Kiwi fruit -- peeled &
Sliced
1 ct Lemon yogurt, lowfat -- or
Vanilla 8 oz
1 ea Fresh mint -- optional
2 c  Red apples -- unpeeded cut
Chunks
1 lg Orange -- peeled &
Sectioned
1 ea Banana -- peeled & sliced
Combine all fruit in a medium bowl; toss gentlly. Spoon into
individual serving bowls; top with a dollop of yogurt. Garnish with
mint if desired. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
