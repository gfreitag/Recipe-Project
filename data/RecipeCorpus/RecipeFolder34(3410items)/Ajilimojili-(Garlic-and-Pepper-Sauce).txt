Title: Ajilimojili (Garlic and Pepper Sauce)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Caribbean
Yield: 3 cups
2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Lime juice
1/2 c  Oil, olive
3 ea Peppers, red, hot; seeded
3 ea Peppers, bell, red; seeded
4 ea Peppercorns, white
4 lg Garlic cloves; peeled
Pound peppers, peppercorns, garlic and salt in a mortar, or chop in
processor finely. Transfer to a bowl and beat in lime juice and oil.
Served with suckling pig.
