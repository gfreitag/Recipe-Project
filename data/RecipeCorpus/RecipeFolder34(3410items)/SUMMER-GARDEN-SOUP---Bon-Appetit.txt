Title: SUMMER GARDEN SOUP - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Soup/stew, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
-undrained
1 cn (14 1/2-oz) chicken broth
4 oz Ham steak, diced
1 md Tomato, seeded and chopped
1 tb Prepared pesto sauce
Additional pesto sauce
1 tb Olive oil
1 md Onion, chopped
1 sm Zucchini
1 sm Yellow crooknock squash
Freshly ground pepper
1 cn (15-oz) cannellini beans
-(white kidney beans),
Heat oil in heavy medium saucepan over medium-low heat. Add onion and
cook until translucent, stirring occasionally, about 8 minutes. Cut
zucchini and squash lengthwise into fourths, then slice. Add to
saucepan and season with pepper. Cook until crisp-tender, stimng
occasionally, about 6 minutes. Remove from heat. Mix in beans with
their juices, broth, ham and tomato. Mix in 1 T pesto. Cool to room
temperature or bring to simmer. Ladle into bowls. Swirl additional
pesto into each bowl and serve.
