Title: Cherry Cream Scones
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Butter -- room temperature
1    Egg -- separated
1/2 c  Sour cream
3/4 c  Half-and-half cream
1 1/2 ts Almond extract
Additional sugar
3/4 c  Dried cherries
1 c  Boiling water
3 c  All-purpose flour
3 tb Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cream of tartar
Soak cherries in water for 10 minutes. Drain and set aside. In a large
mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and cream
of tartar. With a pastry blender, cut in the butter. Set aside. In a
small bowl, combine egg yolk, sour cream, cream and extract. Add to
flour mixture; stir until a soft dough forms. Turn out onto a lightly
floured surface; knead gently six to eight times. Knead in cherries.
Divide dough in half and shape into balls. Roll each ball into a
6-in. circle. Cut into six wedges. Repeat with remaining ball. Place
on lightly greased baking sheet. Beat the egg white until foamy;
brush tops of scones and sprinkle with sugar. Bake at 400 deg. for
15-20 minutes. Serve warm. Yield: 12 scones
