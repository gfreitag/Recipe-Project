Title: Hot And Spicy Cranberry Dip
Categories: Dips, Country wom
Yield: 20 servings
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
Pineapple chunks
Orange sections
Mini precooked sausages
Warmed
1 cn Cranberry sauce -- jellied
(16 oz)
3 tb Prepared horseradish
2 tb Honey
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Lemon juice
Om a med. saucepan, combine first seven ingredients. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer, covered, 5 min. Serve warm with pineapple,
oranges and sausages. Yield: 2 cups.
