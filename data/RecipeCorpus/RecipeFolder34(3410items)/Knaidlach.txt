Title: Knaidlach
Categories: Soup/stew, Breads
Yield: 10 balls
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Schmaltz
1 1/2 ts Onion, grated
1 1/2 c  Matzo meal
3 ea Eggs, seperated
1/2 c  Cold water
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Beat egg whites until stiff. Beat the yolks and combine with next 6
ingredients. Fold in egg whites. Add enough matzo meal to make a light
dropping consistency. Adjust seasoning. Put in freezer for 25 to 35
minutes. Form into balls. Drop into boiling soup or water and cook,
covered, about 20 minutes. DO NOT LIFT LID. Makes about 10 large
balls.
