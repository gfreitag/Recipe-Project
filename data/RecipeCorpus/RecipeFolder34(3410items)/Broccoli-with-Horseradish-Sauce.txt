Title: Broccoli with Horseradish Sauce
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Dry mustard
Pinch of red pepper
1 lb Bunch of broccoli
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Butter or margarine; melted
1 tb Prepared horseradish
1 tb Grated onion
Combine first 7 ingredients in a bowl; stir well. Chill 3 to 4
hours.
Trim off large leaves of broccoli, and remove tough ends of
stalks. Wash broccoli thoroughly, and separate into spears.  Cook
broccoli, covered, in a small amount of boiling water 8 to 10 minutes
or until crisp- tender. Arrange in a dish.  Top with horseradish
sauce.
