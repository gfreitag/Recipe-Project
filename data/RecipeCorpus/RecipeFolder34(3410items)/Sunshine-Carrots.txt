Title: Sunshine Carrots
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Jellied Cranberry Sauce
2 tb Brown Sugar
8 ea Medium size Carrots
1/4 c  Butter or Margarine
Peel, slice and cook carrots, drain well. Combine remaining
ingredients in saucepan, add carrots and stir until the carrots are
well glazed and warm.
