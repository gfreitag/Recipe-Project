Title: Creamy Sausage Stew
Categories: Sausage, Stews, Country wom, Potatoes
Yield: 12 servings
Smoked sausage polish
1/3 c  Cooking oil
1 tb Dried basil
2 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1 pt Heavy cream
3 tb Cornstarch
3 tb Water
10 md Red potatoes -- cut 1 1/2 in
Pieces
2 lg White onions -- quartered
1 lg Green pepper -- 1 in.
Pieces
1 lg Red pepper -- cut 1 in
Pieces
2 lb Sausage -- cut in 1 inch
Piece
Place potatoes in a 5 qt. roasting pan. Add onions, peppers and
sausage; toss gently. Combine oil, basil salt and pepper. Pour over
the meat and vegetables. toss well. Cover and bake 350 deg. for 45
min; stir. Add the cream; cover and bake 30 to 40 min. longer or
until potatoes are tender. Combine cornstarch and water; stir into
stew. Place on stovetop and bring to a boil, stirring constantly
until thickened. Yield: 10-12 servings.
