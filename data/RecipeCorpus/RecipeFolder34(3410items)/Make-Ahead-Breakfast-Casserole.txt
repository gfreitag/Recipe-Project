Title: Make Ahead Breakfast Casserole
Categories: Breakfast, Casseroles
Yield: 8 servings
3 c  Milk
1 c  Miracle Whip
2 tb Flour
1 tb Dry mustard
2 ts Dried basil
1 ts Salt
1 lb Bulk sausage
10 c  Fresh bread cubes
10 oz Chopped broccoli --
-thawed and drained
4 oz Mushroom -- drained
8 oz Cheddar cheese -- shredded
6    Eggs -- beaten
Brown sausage; drain.  Add bread cubes, broccoli, mushrooms and
cheese; mix lightly.  Spoon into 13 x 9 baking dish.  Pour combined
eggs, milk, dressing, flour and seasonings over bread mixture; cover.
Refrigerate 1 hour or overnight.  Heat oven to 375.  Bake, uncovered,
1 hour.
