Title: Fresh and Dried Fruit Compote
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 1 quart
2 tb Dry sherry
2 ts Chopped candied ginger
1 lb Firm ripe pears
-or apples or combo
-peeled, and cored
-cut in thick wedges
1    2" strip lemon zest
3/4 c  Blueberries, raspberries
-cherries or combo
1    Bottle dry white wine
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sugar
1    4" cinnamon stick
1    Bay leaf
1 1/2 ts Coriander seeds
-lightly crushed
1 ts Black peppercorns
1/2 lb Mixed dried fruit
In a nonreactive pan, simmer the wine, lemon juice, sugar, cinnamon,
bay leaf, coriander seeds, and black peppercorns for 15 minutes,
covered. Strain, return to the pan, and add the dried fruit, sherry,
and ginger. Simmer, covered, another 10 minutes. Add the pears or
apples and lemon zest, simmer until just tender, 3-5 minutes, and
then cool. Carefully stir in the berries. Store, refrigerated, for up
to 10 days.
