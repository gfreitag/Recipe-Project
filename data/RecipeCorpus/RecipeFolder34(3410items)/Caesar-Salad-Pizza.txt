Title: Caesar Salad Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Mayo
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
14 ts Garlic; minced
1/4 ts Pepper
4 c  Romaine lettuce
Leaves -- torn
1/3 c  Marinara sauce
4    Anchovy fillets -- chop
1/4 ts Garlic -- minced
1    12" pizza crust
1/2 c  Parmesan; grated -- divided
1 tb Olive oil
Preheat oven to 450~. Combine sauce, fillets and garlic. Spread
evenly over crust. Sprinkle with 1/4 c Parmesan. Bake 10 minutes.
Meanwhile combine olive oil, mayo and lemon juice until smooth. Stir
in garlic and pepper. Add lettuce and 1/4 c Parmesan cheese; toss to
coat. Spoon on hot pizza. Serve with extra Parmesan, if desired. Per
Slice: 220 Cal, 2 gr Fat.
