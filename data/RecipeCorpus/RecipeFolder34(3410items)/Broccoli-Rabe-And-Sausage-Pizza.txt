Title: Broccoli Rabe And Sausage Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
Coarsely chop, 1-1/4 lbs
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Marinara sauce
1    12" pizza crust
2 c  Mozzarella; shredd --
Divide
1/2 lb Sweet Italian sausage
1/2 c  Water
1 tb Olive oil
3/4 c  Onion -- sliced
1 ts Garlic -- minced
1 bn Broccoli rabe; remove stems
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat sauce and water to boiling in skillet over
high heat. Cover and cook 5 minutes. Uncover and cook until sausage
is golden brown; set aside. Add olive oil and onion to drippings in
skillet; cook until tender, 5 minutes. Stir in garlic, broccoli and
salt; cover and cook until broccoli is tender, 5 minutes. Slice
sausage 1/4" thick. Spoon sauce over crust. Sprinkle with 1 c
mozzarella and layer with broccoli mixture, sausage and 1 c
mozzarella. Bake 10 minutes. Per Slice: 375 Cal 21 Gr Fat.
