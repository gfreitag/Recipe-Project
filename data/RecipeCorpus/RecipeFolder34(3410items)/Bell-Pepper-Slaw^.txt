Title: Bell Pepper Slaw^
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Celery seeds
1/4 ts Pepper
Cooking Light 9-94
Carolyn Shaw 8-95
4 c  Thinly sliced green cabbage
2 c  Julienne cut red bell
Pepper
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and toss well. Let stand 15
minutes before serving. Makes 6 1 cup servings.

Per serving: 32 calories, .9 g protein, .3 g (8%) fat, 7.7 g carb.,
1.7 g fiber, 0 mg cholesterol, .8 mg iron, 205 mg sodium, 28 mg
calcium. Submitted By CAROLYN SHAW   On   09-05-95
