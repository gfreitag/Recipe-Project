Title: My Skyline Chili
Categories: Meats, Cheese/eggs, Pasta/rice, Chili
Yield: 8 servings
4 tb Chili powder
1 ts Ground red pepper
2 1/2 tb Vinegar
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 ts Ground allspice
1 ts Worchestshire sauce
1 ts Salt
1 c  Colby cheese; shredded
Pasta; cooked; hot
2 lb Ground beef
2 lg Onions; minced
35    Whole black peppercorns
6    Bay leaved
4 sm Red peppers
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper
2    Garlic cloves; whole
6 oz Tomato paste
3 c  Water
Brown ground beef in skillet with onions. Drain fat. Place next 4
ingredients in a spice bag. Combine remaining ingredients, except
cheese and pasta, in a large pot. Cook over low heat for 3-4 hours.
Don't forget to remove spice bag and whole garlic cloves. Serve over
spaghetti or some type of pasta, topped with shredded Colby cheese
and additional chopped onions if desired.
