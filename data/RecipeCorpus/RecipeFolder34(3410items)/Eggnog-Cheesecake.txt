Title: Eggnog Cheesecake
Categories: Pie/tart, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
1    Graham cracker pie crust
Whipped topping & ground
Nutmeg for garnish
1    8 oz cream cheese, soft
1 cn 14 oz condensed milk
1    Egg
1/2 ts Brandy or rum extract
In large bowl, with electric mixer, mix cream cheese until fluffy. Add
milk, egg, extract and nutmeg and beat until smooth. Pour into crust.
Bake at 300 degrees for 35-40 minutes or just until set. Cook
completely. Chill for 3-4 hours, and garnish. Serve.
