Title: Freezer Coleslaw
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Celery seed
1/2 ts Mustard seed
1 md Head cabbage -- 3 lbs
Shredded
1/2    Green pepper  chopped
1/2 c  Shredded carrot
1 sm Onion -- chopped
1 ts Salt
DRESSING:
In a large bowl, combine the first five ingredients. Let stand 1 hour.
Drain. Bring all dressing ingredients to a boil; simmer 2-3 minutes.
Cool. Pour over cabbage. Pack in freezer containers; freeze. Yield:
about 4 pints
