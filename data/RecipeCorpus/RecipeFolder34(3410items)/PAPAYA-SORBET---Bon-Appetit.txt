Title: PAPAYA SORBET - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Fruit, Ice cream, Desserts
Yield: 1 quart
3/4 c  Papaya nectar or juice
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Fresh lime juice
3    To 4 C  cubed, seeded and
-peeled ripe papaya (from 2
-large or 4 small)
Puree all ingredients in processor or blender, stopping to scrape down
sides, about 3 minutes. Press mixture through fine strainer into bowl
to eliminate strings, if desired.

Transfer mixture to ice cream maker and process according to
manufactur- er's instructions, or, without an ice cream maker, freeze
following directions in Very Berry Ice Milk recipe.

Transfer sorbet to plastic container. Cover and freeze several hours
or overnight to mellow flavors. If frozen solid, let soften slightly
at room temperature.

Bon Appetit/August/89  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
