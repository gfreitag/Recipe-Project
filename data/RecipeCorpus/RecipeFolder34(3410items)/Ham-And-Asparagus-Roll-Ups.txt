Title: Ham And Asparagus Roll-Ups
Categories: Pork, Ham, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Soup -- 10-3/4oz undiluted
1/4 c  Mllk
3 tb Almonds -- sliced
1 lb Fresh asparagus
10 ea Thin ham slices
1 cn Condensed cream of celery
Steam asparagus until tender; pat dry. Roll a slice of ham around two
to three asparagus stalks. Place ham rolls, seam side down, in a
greased ll-in, x 7-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Combine the soup and
milk: pour over the ham rolls. Top with almonds. Bake, uncovered, at
350 deg. for 30 minutes or until bubbly. (Can also be microwaved on
high for 3-5 minutes.) Yield: 5 servings
