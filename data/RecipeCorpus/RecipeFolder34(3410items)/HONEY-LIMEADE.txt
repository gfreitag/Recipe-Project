Title: HONEY LIMEADE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Honey
Ice cubes
8 ts Reconstituted lime juice
1 c  Water
2 tb Sugar (or substitute)
Equipment:  Measuring cups and spoons, large pitcher, large spoon, 6
tall glasses

Directions: 1. Put the lime juice in the pitcher. Add the water,
sugar, and honey. Stir till the sugar is dissolved.  Put in the
refrigerator till serving time. 2. Put several ice cubes in each
glass. Pour limeade over the ice cubes in the glasses.

Origin:  Better Homes and Gardens New Junior Cookbook
