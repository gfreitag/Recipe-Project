Title: Mini-Melba Pizzas
Categories: Snacks, Low cal/fat, Appetizers, Pizza
Yield: 24 snacks
-sliced
2 ea Green onions; sliced
1/4 c  (1 oz.) Healthy Choice fat-
-free mozzarella shreds
1/4 c  (1 oz.) Healthy Choice fat-
-free cheddar shreds
1/2 c  No-salt added tomato sauce
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/2 ts Dried basil
24 ea Whole grain melba toast
-rounds
6 md Fresh mushrooms; thinly
Combine first 3 ingredients in a small bowl; stir well.  Place melba
toast rounds on a baking sheet.  Spread 1 teaspoon tomato sauce
mixture on each round.  Evenly distribute mushrooms, onions, and
cheeses over tomato sauce. Bake at 400 degrees for 3 minutes or until
cheese softens. Yield: 24 snacks.

PER SNACK:  CALORIES 20; FAT 0.2g; CARB 3.2g; PROTEIN 1.4g;
CHOLESTEROL 0mg; SODIUM 45mg.

From:  Healthy Choice CHOICES FOR LIVING HAPPIER AND HEALTHIER, Fall
1995.
