Title: Sebastian Joe's Campari Pink Grapefruit Sorbet
Categories: Newspaper, Desserts, Fruit, Alcohol
Yield: 1 quart
1 c  Pink grapefruit juice
1/4 c  Campari liqueur
1/8 c  Sugar
2 1/4 c  Water
Whisk the sugar into the water, add the grapefruit juice and then the
campari.  Mix well and then freeze according to ice-cream maker's
instructions.
