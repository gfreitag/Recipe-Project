Title: Oregano Pesto^
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 16 servings
Cheese
2 tb Pistachios
4 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Salt
2 cl Garlic, large
3 tb Extra virgin olive oil
Cooking Light 9-95
Carolyn Shaw 8-95
2 1/2 c  Torn spinach
2 c  Fresh oregano leaves
1 c  Fresh flat leaf parsley
Leaves
2 tb Grated fresh Parmesan
Process all ingredients except oil in food processor until smooth.
With processor or, slowly pour oil through food chute; process until
well blended. Spoon into a ziploc bag, store in refrigerator. Makes 1
cup.

Per tablespoon: 48 cal., 1.3 g protein, 3.8 g (71%) fat, 3.2 g carb.,
1.1 g fiber, 1 mg chol., 1.9 mg iron, 60 mg sodium, 75 mg calcium.
