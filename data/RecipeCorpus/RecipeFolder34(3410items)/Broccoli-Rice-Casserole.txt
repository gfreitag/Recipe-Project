Title: Broccoli Rice Casserole
Categories: Pasta/rice, Side dish, Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
-& drained
8 oz Cheez Whiz
1/2 c  Fresh bread cubes --
-optional
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
2 tb Margarine -- divided
2 c  Cooked rice
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
10 oz Chopped broccoli -- cooked
Heat oven to 350.  Cook and stir onion in 1 tbsp of margarine in large
skillet on medium heat until tender.  Add rice, soup, broccoli and
cheez whiz; mix lightly.  Spoon into 1 1/2 qt casserole.  Top with
combine melted margarine and bread cubes.  Bake 30 to 35 minutes.
