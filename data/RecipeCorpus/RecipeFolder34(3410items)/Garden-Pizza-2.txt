Title: Garden Pizza 2
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Minced vegetables *
2 cn Crescent rolls
16 oz Cream cheese -- softened
1 pk Knorrs vegetable soup mix
Pat out crescent rolls on cookie sheet, bake according to package

directions. Cool. Mix cream cheese, soup mix, and mayo together.
Spread on cooled crust. **Top with your favorite vegetables chopped
finely.
