Title: Strawberry Poundcake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Chopped nuts
4    Eggs
2 tb Flour
Whipped cream, to serve on
Top
1 pk White cake mix
1 sm Package strawberry Jello
3/4 c  Oil
10 oz Frozen strawberries
1/2 c  Sugar
Grease a 10 inch tube or bundt pan.  Combine ingredients; beat 3
minutes. Bake 55 to 65 minutes at 350 degrees.  Cool for 10 minutes,
then remove from the pan. Randy Rigg
