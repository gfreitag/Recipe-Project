Title: HENNY PENNY MUFFINS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Muffins
Yield: 24 muffins
No ingredients found
Prepare Cheddar Muffins, substituting 1/2 C chopped cooked chicken, 2
T finely chopped sweet red pepper, 2 green onions, finely chopped,
and 1/4 t cracked black pepper for the Cheddar and chives. Serve
immediately; store any leftover muffins in the refrigerator.
