Title: Baked Dove Breasts
Categories: Main dish, Game
Yield: 6 servings
1 md Onion; sliced
1 cn (10-3/4 oz) cream of chicken
-soup
Prepared brown rice
12 ea To 24 dove breasts
Salt and pepper
Flour
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 lb Fresh mushrooms; sliced
Salt and pepper dove breasts and lightly flour.  Brown in skillet
with butter.
Arrange in glass baking dish and add pan drippings on top.  Place
sliced mushrooms and onions on top of breasts.  Top with cream of
chicken soup. Cover baking dish with aluminum foil.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 25 minutes.  Uncover and continue baking
for 15 minutes longer.
Serve in baking dish with brown rice.
