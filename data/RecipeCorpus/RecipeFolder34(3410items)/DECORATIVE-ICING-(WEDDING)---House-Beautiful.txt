Title: DECORATIVE ICING (WEDDING) - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 icing
1    Egg white
Water
1 1/4 c  To 1 1/2 C confectioners'
-sugar
1/8 ts Cream of tartar
*It is best to prepare this icing just prior to using it.

In a small mixing bowl combine sugar and cream of tartar. Add the egg
white with a hand mixer on low speed, mix for 3 minutes, or until the
icing is bright white and the consistency of very softly whipped
cream. If the icing is too thick, add a few drops of water. If the
icing is too thin add confectioners' sugar.

Keep unused icing covered with a damp towel when not in use.
