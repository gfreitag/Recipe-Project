Title: Dllled Turkey Breast
Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Dried dill weed
Sliced cooked turkey,
Warmed
1 cn Condensed cream of mushroom
Soup -- 10-3/4 oz undiluted
3/4 c  Chicken broth
3/4 c  Sour cream
In a saucepan, combine soup, chicken broth, sour cream and dill weed.
Cook until heated through but do not boil. Arrange turkey slices on a
platter; pour sauce over and serve immediately. Yield: 6-8 servings
