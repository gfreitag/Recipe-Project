Title: GREEN BEANS WITH HONEY-CASHEW SAUCE - Country Living
Categories: Vegetables, Salsa/sauce, Side dish, Country liv
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Honey
1 lb Green beans, trimmed, cooked
-tender-crisp, drained
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped, salted
-cashews
3 tb Unsalted butter
1. In large skillet, saute cashews in butter over low heat until
lightly browned-about 5 minutes. Add honey and cook, stirring
constantly, 1 minute longer.

2. Pour cashew sauce over beans and toss until coated; spoon into
serving dish and serve immediately.
