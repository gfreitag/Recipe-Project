Title: Ground Beef Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Beef, Country wom
Yield: 16 servings
8 c  Bread cubes
1 tb Poultry seasoning
1 ts Salt -- optional
1/2 ts Pepper
2 c  Chicken broth -- low-sodium
1 lb Lean ground beef -- extra
Lean
1 md Onion -- chopped
1 md Apple -- chopped
2 ea Celery ribs -- chopped
In a skillet, brown beef; drain. Place in a large bowl; add remaining
ingredients and mix well. Transfer to 3 qt. baking dish that has been
coated with nonstick cooking spray. Cover and bake at 325 deg. for 1
1/2 hrs. Uncover and bake 10 min. more until dressing is lightly
browned. Yield: 16 servings.
