Title: Small Crusty Bran Bread
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
2 c  Flour
2/3 c  Natural bran
1 ts Quick rising yeast or
3/4 ts Bread machine yeast
3/4 c  Water
1 tb Packed Brown Sugar
1 tb Molasses
1 tb Butter melted
1 ts Salt
Follow instructions for large loaf. From Canadian Living October 1995
