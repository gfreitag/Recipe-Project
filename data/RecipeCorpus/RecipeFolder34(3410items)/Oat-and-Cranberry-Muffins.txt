Title: Oat and Cranberry Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 c  Fresh or frozen cranberries
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter
1 c  Milk
1 lg Egg
Heat oven to 425. Grease 12
-muffin cups.
3/4 c  Unsifted all purpose flour
3/4 c  Unsifted all purpose flour
1 c  Oatmeal (do not use quick
-or instant oatmeal)
1/2 c  Light brown sugar
1 tb Baking powder
1 ts Salt
In a bowl, combine dry ingredients and blend well. Toss the
cranberries with 1 tbsp. of dry ingredients and set aside. Melt
abutter. Remove from Heat: Stir in milk and egg Stir butter mixture
into dry ingredients and, when well mixed, stir in cranberries. Spoon
into muffin cups and bake until lightly browned++ 15 to 20 minutes.
Let stand 5 minutes before removing from tin. Shared by: Pat Stockett
