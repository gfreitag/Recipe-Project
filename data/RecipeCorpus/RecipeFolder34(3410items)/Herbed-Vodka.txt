Title: Herbed Vodka
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Vodka
2    Rosemary sprigs; 3" long
. *or* use basil or mint
Herbed vodka: Place the herbs in a glass container with the vodka. Set
aside eight hours or overnight.  Strain out the herbs and chill in the
freezer.  Use in vodka and tonic.
