Title: Grilled Chicken with Passion Fruit Vinegar
Categories: Poultry, Bon appetit
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Consorzio Vignette Passion
-Fruit Flavored Vinegar
Salt and pepper to taste
4    Chicken breasts
-skinned and boned
2 ts Tarragon, chopped or
1 ts Dried tarragon
Prepare grill or preheat broiler. Put chicken in a bowl and pour
Passion Fruit Vinegar over chicken. Add tarragon and turn breasts
until well coated.

Oil grill or broiler rack and cook chicken over medium heat with no
flame. (If the fire is too hot, the sugar in the vinegar will burn
instead of caramelize.) Grill on one side until brown, about 5
minutes. Turn and baste with Passion Fruit Vinegar. continue to cook
until done, about 5 minutes more. Pass more Passion Fruit Vinegar as
a sauce.

Bon Apptit September 1995
