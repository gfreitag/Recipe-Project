Title: GRAND MARNIER MIMOSAS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 tb Grand Marnier
1 sm Seedless orange
1 1/4 c  Champagne
1. Thinly slice orange into wedges. In chilled 3- or 4-C  pitcher,
combine orange slices, champagne, orange juice. and Grand Marnier.

2. Pour champagne mixture into 2 chilled wineglasses; spoon several
orange slices into each.

Nutritional information per serving-protein: .7 gram; fat: . 1 gram;
carbohydrate: 15 grams@ fiber: .25 gram; sodium: to milligrams
cholesterol: 0 milligram; calories: 176.
