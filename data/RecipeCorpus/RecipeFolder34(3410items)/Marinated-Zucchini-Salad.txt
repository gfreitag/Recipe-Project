Title: Marinated Zucchini Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb White wine vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
6 sm Zucchini -- thinly sliced
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1/2 c  Diced celery
1/2 c  Diced onion
1    Jar pimiento -- drained
2/3 c  Vinegar
Combine zucchini, green pepper, celery, onion and pimiento in a medium
bowl; set aside. Combine remaining ingredients in a jar; cover
tightly and shake vigorously. Pour marinade over vegetables; toss
gently. Cover and chill 8 hours or overnight. Serve with a slotted
spoon. Yield: 8 servings
