Title: Maple Butterscotch Brownies
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 16 servings
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Walnuts -- chopped
Confectioners'sugar --
Optional
1 1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 1/2 ts Flavoring maple --
Imitation
2 ea Eggs
In a bowl, combine brown sugar, butter and maple flavoring. Beat in
the eggs, one at a time. Combine flour and baking powder; add to egg
mixture. Stir in walnuts. Pour into a greased 9 inch square baking
pan. Bake at 350 deg. for 30 min. or until brownies test done. cool.
Dust with confectioners' sugar if desired. Yield: 16 brownies.
