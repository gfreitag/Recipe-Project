Title: Mini Pita Pizzas
Categories: Snacks, Pizza
Yield: 2 servings

1    Pita -- 4"
2 tb Pizza -- or spaghetti sauce

Split pizza in half into 2 round circles and place each one onto a
paper towel, cut side up.  Spread 1 T sauce over each pita half. Top
with 1/4 c mozzarella cheese and 1 t Parmesan; sprinkle on some
oregano if you like. Cook on High for 30-45 seconds.
