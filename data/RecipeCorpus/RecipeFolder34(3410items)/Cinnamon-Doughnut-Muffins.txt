Title: Cinnamon Doughnut Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 10 servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
3/4 c  Milk
Jam
1 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, nutmeg and
cinnamon. Combine sugar, oil, egg and milk; stir into dry ingredients
just until moistened. Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups half
full; place l teaspoon jam on top. Cover jam with enough batter to
fill muffin cups three-fourths full. Bake at 350 deg. for 20-25 min.
or until muffins test done. Place melted butter in a small bowl;
combine sugar and cinnamon in another bowl. Immediately after
removing muffins from the oven, dip tops in butter, then in
cinnamon-sugar. Serve warm. Yield; 10 standard-size muffins.
