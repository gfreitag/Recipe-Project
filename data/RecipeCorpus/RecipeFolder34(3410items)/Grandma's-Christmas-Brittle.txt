Title: Grandma's Christmas Brittle
Categories: Candy, Country wom
Yield: 30 servings
1    Jar peanuts, dry-roasted
Unsalted -- 16 oz
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Water
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Butter -- cut into pieces
1 ts Salt
Grease two cookie sheets and keep warm in a 200 deg. oven. In a large
saucepan, combine the sugar, corn syrup and 1 cup water. Cook over
medium heat, stirring constantly, until a candy thermometer reaches
240 deg..(soft ball stage). Stir in butter, salt and peanuts.
Continue heating, stirring constantly, until the mixture reaches 300.
Meanwhile, combine baking soda, 1 teaspoon water and vanilla. remove
saucepan from the heat. Stir in baking soda mixture. Quickly pour
half the mixture over each cookie sheet. Spread with a buttered metal
spatula to a 1/4 in. thickness. Cool. Bread into pieces. Yield: about
2 1/2 pounds.
