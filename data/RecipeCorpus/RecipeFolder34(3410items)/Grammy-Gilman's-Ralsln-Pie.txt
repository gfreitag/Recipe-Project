Title: Grammy Gilman's Ralsln Pie
Categories: Pies, Pastry, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Vinegar
1 1/2 c  Cold water
Pastry for 8-inch
Double-crust pie
1 c  Sugar
3 tb All-purpose flour
1 c  Raisins
1 tb Butter or margarine
In a saucepan, combine sugar and flour. Add all remaining ingredients
except crust; cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until
thickened. Pour into a prepared shell. Cut several slits in top crust
and position over filling. Seal. Bake at 400 deg. for about 45
minutes or until golden. Yield: 6 servings
