Title: Graham Kerr Pizza Dough
Categories: Pizza, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
Coffee mill
2 tb Fresh oregano
1 tb Fresh rosemary
1    Garlic clove
Plastic wrap
1 1/2 c  Sifted flour
3 oz Yeast cube
1/4 c  Warm water
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
Dissolve yeast in small amount of the water.  Grind fresh oregano,
rosemary and garlic in the coffee mill. Combine ingredients as you
would for making a dough, let rise in a bowl covered with plastic
wrap. Punch down and work more flour in for about 2 minutes. Tease
out into pizza round. Let rest 8 to ten minutes and then bake at 500~
for 2 to 3 minutes. Remove from the oven and flip over.  Top your
pizza.
