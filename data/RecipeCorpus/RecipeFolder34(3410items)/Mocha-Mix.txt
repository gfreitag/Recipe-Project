Title: Mocha Mix
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
2 cups dry coffee creamer
Vanilla bean cut in
- quarters
1 cup cocoa (unsweetened)
2 cups non-fat dry milk
1/2 cup instant coffee
2 1/2 cups sugar
Mix well and divide into jars with a piece of vanilla bean in each
jar. Store at least a week so flavor of vanilla bean is absorbed. Use
3 tablespoons per 3/4 cup of hot water. Great topped with whipped
cream or Cool Whip.
