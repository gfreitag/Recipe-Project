Title: Relish Bowl (Shaker)
Categories: Appetizers, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Sweet pickle slices
1/3 c  Dill pickle slices
1 sm Head cauliflower; broken
-into flowerets
2 lg Cucumbers; unpeeled and
-sliced
1 ea 6-oz package radishes
6 ea Stalks celery hearts; cut in
-half
1/2 c  Small ripe pitted olives
1/2 c  Pimiento-stuffed olives
1 ea 14-oz jar crisp okra pickles
2 ea 3-1/2 oz jars miniature
-pickled corn
Arrange vegetables in a large [shallow] bowl or plate.

"Shaker Daily Fare" (Pleasant Hill, Kentucky) _Company's Coming!_
