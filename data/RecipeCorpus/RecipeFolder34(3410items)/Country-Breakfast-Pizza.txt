Title: Country Breakfast Pizza
Categories: Breakfast, Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
1 sm Green pepper
1 c  Fresh sliced mushrooms
4    Eggs
1/4 c  Milk
1 pk Turkey sausage
1 cn Refrigerated crescent rolls
1/4 c  Creamy-style Italian style
-dressing
1 sm Onion -- minced
Cook sausage and break up to crumble; drain. Spread dough over bottom
to form crust on 12-inch pizza pan; spread crust with dressing (note
I use the prepared Pillsbury Pizza dough) Make sure you build up
edges to keep eggs on pan) Top with onion green pepper, mushrooms and
cooked sausage. Beat eggs with milk; pour carefully over top of
pizza. Bake in preheated 400F oven for 20 minutes.
