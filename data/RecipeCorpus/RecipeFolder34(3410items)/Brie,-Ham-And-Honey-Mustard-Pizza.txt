Title: Brie, Ham And Honey Mustard Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Ham -- julienned
Fresh parsley -- garnish
3 tb Honey mustard
1    12" pizza crust
1/2 lb Brie cheese -- sliced thin
Preheat oven to 450~. Spread mustard over crust. Arrange cheese spoke-
fashion over mustard. Sprinkle with ham. Bake 10 minutes. Garnish with
parsley. Serve at room temperature. Per Slice: 275 Cal, 12 gr Fat.
