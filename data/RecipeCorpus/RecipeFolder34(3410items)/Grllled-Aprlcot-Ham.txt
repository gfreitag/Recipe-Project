Title: Grllled Aprlcot Ham
Categories: Pork, Ham, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Cider vinegar
1 ea Ham steak -- about 1 pound
1/4 c  All-fruit apricot spread
2 ts Dijon mustard
In a small bowl, combine apricot spread, mustard and vinegar. Grill
ham steak over hot coals, brushing occasionally with sauce, until
browned on both sides, about 8-10 minutes. Yield: 4 servings.
Diabetic Exchanges: One serving equals 1 fruit, 3 lean meat; also,
205 calories, 1,654 mg sodium, 53 mg cholesterol, 15 gm carbohydrate,
22 gmprotein, 5 gm fat
