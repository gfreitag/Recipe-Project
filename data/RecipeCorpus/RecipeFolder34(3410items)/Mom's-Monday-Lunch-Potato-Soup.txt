Title: Mom's Monday Lunch Potato Soup
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Soup -- 10-3/4 oz undiluted
2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt -- optional
1/8 ts Pepper
2 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
8 ea Bacon strips -- diced
1 sm Onion -- chopped
1 1/2 c  Potatoes
Left over mashed
1 cn Condensed cream of chicken
In a 3-qt. saucepan, cook bacon until crisp: remove to paper towels to
drain. Saute onion in the drippings until tender. Add the potatoes
and soup and stir until smooth. Gradually stir in milk. Cook over
medium heat, stirring constantly. Stir in bacon, salt if desired and
pepper. Cook until heated through. Garnish with parsley. Yield: 3-4
servings
