Title: Picadilly Pizzas
Categories: Main dish, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
Toasted
8    Tomato slices
8    Fat-free pasteurized cheese
Singles, Healthy Cho
1/2 c  Zucchini -- finely chopped
2 tb Onion -- finely chopped
1/2 ts Dried oregano -- crushed
8    English muffin halves --
Mix together zucchini, onion and oregano.  for each pizza, top muffin
half with tomato slice and 1 tb zucchini mixture; cover with cheese
slice. Broil until cheese melts. 8 servings, each 102 calories, 7 g
protein, 16 g carbo., 5 g fat, 3 mg cholesterol, 451 mg sodium,
calories from fat = 6%.
