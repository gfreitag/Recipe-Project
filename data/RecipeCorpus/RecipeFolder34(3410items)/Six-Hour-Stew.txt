Title: Six-Hour Stew
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1    Onion soup mix
3    Onions ; sliced
1 cn Tomatoes ; whole
1 ts Basil
4 tb Tapioca
3 lb Chuck roast ; cubed
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Water
3    Celery
8    Carrots ; thickly sliced
4 lg Potatoes ; quartered
MMMMM----------------I USE ABOUT 5 ONIONS AND 2 Q---------------------
Recipe by: cjpope@ingr.com (Candy Pope) Place all ingredients in a
heavy roaster with a tight fitting lid and bake

at 275 for 6 hours. DO NOT OPEN THE OVEN DOOR DURING COOKING TIME!
Notes: have also made this stew using chicken (deboned thighs) and it
was great.

It is good without meat as well.
