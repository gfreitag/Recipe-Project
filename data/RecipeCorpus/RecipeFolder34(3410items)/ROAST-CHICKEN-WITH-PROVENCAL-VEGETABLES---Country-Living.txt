Title: ROAST CHICKEN WITH PROVENCAL VEGETABLES - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Main dish, Poultry, Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Chopped tomatoes (about 3
-plum, or Roma, tomatoes)
1/4 ts Dried marjoram leaves,
-crushed
1/8 ts Dried thyme leaves, crushed
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
1/2    1-lb roasted chicken
1 sm (4 to 5 oz) zucchini, cut
-into 1/2-inch pieces
-(1 1/2 C)
4 oz Eggplant (with skin), cut
-into 1/2-inch pieces
-(1 1/2 C)
1. Cut chicken into two pieces. In medium-size bowl, combine remaining
ingredients.

2. Place one piece of chicken on the side of large microwave-safe
dinner plate. Place half the vegetable mixture next to chicken.
Repeat with remaining chicken and vegetables on another plate.

3. Cover plates with plastic wrap. Microwave each plate on high (100
percent) 6 minutes. Puncture the plastic wrap or uncover immediately
to prevent wrap from shrinking onto food.
