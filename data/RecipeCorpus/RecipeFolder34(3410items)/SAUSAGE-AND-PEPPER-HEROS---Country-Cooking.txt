Title: SAUSAGE-AND-PEPPER HEROS - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
-chopped
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Dried oregano leaves
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
4    6-inch French rolls or
-6-inch pieces of French
-or Italian bread
2 tb Butter or margarine
2    Hot and 2 sweet Italian
-sausages, sliced into 1/2
-inch pieces
2 ea Medium-size sweet red and
-green peppers
1 md Onion
3    Cloves garlic, finely
1. In large skillet, melt butter over low heat; saute sausage pieces
until well browned and cooked through. Push to side of skillet.

2. Quarter, seed, and thinly slice peppers. Cut slices in half.
Coarsely chop onion. In butter remaining in skillet, saute peppers,
onion, and garlic until just tender. Add basil, salt, oregano, and
black pepper. Stir in sausage pieces.

3. Split rolls; top with sausage-and-pepper filling. Serve
immediately.
