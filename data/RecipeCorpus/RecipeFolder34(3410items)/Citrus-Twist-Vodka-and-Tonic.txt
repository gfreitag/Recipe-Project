Title: Citrus Twist Vodka and Tonic
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 1 serving
Tonic water
2 oz Smirnoff Citrus Twist vodka
Pour the vodka into a tall glass over ice.  Fill with tonic water and
garnish with lemon or lime slices.
