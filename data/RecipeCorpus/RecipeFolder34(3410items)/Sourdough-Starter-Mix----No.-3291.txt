Title: Sourdough Starter Mix    No. 3291
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Sugar
4 c  Flour
1 qt Lukewarm Water
1 pk Active Dry Yeast
Combine the ingredients in a large crock. Cover with a clean cloth.
Let the mixture stand until it is light and slightly aged (about 2
days). Note: The mixture will thin as it stands   add flour as
needed. Always replace any of the starter you use with an equal
amount of flour and water.
