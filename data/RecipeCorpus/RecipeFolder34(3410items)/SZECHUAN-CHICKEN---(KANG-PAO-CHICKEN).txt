Title: SZECHUAN CHICKEN   (KANG PAO CHICKEN)
Categories: Chinese, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2    Scallions; sliced
2 tb Dry sherry
2 tb Hoisin sauce
4 tb Black bean sauce
1/4 ts Chili paste
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Sugar
4    Chicken-breast halves
-- skinned, boned, and cut
-- in 3/4 in cubes
1    Egg white
1 tb Corn starch
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Unsalted peanuts or cashews
Combine the cubed chicken with the egg white and cornstarch.
Refrigerate for 1/2 hour. Heat oil in wok and stir-fry chicken 3 to 4
minutes, until done. Add nuts, scallions, and remaining ingredients.
Heat thoroughly and serve at once with rice.
