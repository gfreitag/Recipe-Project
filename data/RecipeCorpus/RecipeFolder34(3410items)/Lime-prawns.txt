Title: Lime prawns
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Olive oil
Fresh ground pepper
4    Limes cut into quarters
16    Prawns in shell
Juice and zest of 3 limes
4    Cloves of garlic minced
1) In a shallow glass dish, place the prawns, juice, zest, garlic,
oil, and pepper. Bast the prawns and marinade for 2 hours.

2) Prepare the grill.

3) Thread the prawns on skewers. Place the skewers on the grill for 10
min., turning and basting once, or until pink.
