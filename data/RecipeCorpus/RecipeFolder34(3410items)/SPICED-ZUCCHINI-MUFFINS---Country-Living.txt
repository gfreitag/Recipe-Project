Title: SPICED ZUCCHINI MUFFINS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Breads, Muffins
Yield: 6 3-inch muf
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Low-fat (I%) milk
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 lg Egg
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 1/2 c  Shredded zucchini
1 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
1/2 c  Whole-wheat flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Ground cardamom
1/4 ts Salt
1. Heat oven to 400'F; grease six 3-inch muffin-pan cups. In
medium-size bowl, combine both flours, the baking powder, cinnamon,
baking soda, cardamom, and salt; set aside.

2. In large bowl, with wire whisk, beat sugar, milk, oil, egg, and
vanilla until blended. Stir flour mixture into milk mixlure just
until flour mixture is moistened. Stir in zucchini and divide batter
among prepared cups.

3. Bake muffins 18 to 20 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center
comes out clean. Cool muffins in pan on wire rack 5 minutes. Remove
muffins from cups and serve warm.
