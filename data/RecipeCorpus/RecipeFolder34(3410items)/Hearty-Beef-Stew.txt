Title: Hearty Beef Stew
Categories: Crockpot, Meats, Soup/stew, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
3    Stalks celery, sliced
1    28-ounce can tomatoes
1/2 c  Quick-cooking tapioca
1    Whole clove
2    Bay leaves
Salt and pepper to taste
(Old Fashioned flavor Iike
Grandma's
Thanks to the tapioca!)
2 lb Stew beef,
-cut in 1 -inch cubes
5    Carrots, sliced
1 lg Onion, diced
Trim all fat from meat. Put all ingredients in CROCK-POT. Mix
thoroughly. Cover and cook on Low 12 hours. (High: 5 to 6 hours).
From Rival Crock-pot cook book, date unknown
