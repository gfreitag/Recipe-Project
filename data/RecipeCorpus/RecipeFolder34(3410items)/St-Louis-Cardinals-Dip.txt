Title: St Louis Cardinals Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Oregano -- crushed
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 c  Cocktail sauce
1/2 c  Chili sauce
Combine all ingredients.  Chill 30 minutes before serving.  Serve with
chicken nuggets.
