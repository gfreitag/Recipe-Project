Title: Roasted Vegetable Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Oregano
1    12" pizza crust
1 c  Fresh mozzarella -- diced
4    Plum tomatoes -- quartered
1/4 lb Asparagus -- cut 1-1/2" pcs
1 sm Zucchini -- slice
1 sm Red onion -- sliced
4 ts Olive oil
1 ts Garlic -- minced
Preheat oven to 450~. Combine tomatoes, asparagus, zucchini, onion,
olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper and oregano; toss. Arrange in single
layer in jellyroll pan. Roast 15 minutes. Spoon veggies on crust. Top
with cheese. Bake 10 minutes. Per Slice 270 Cal, 12 Gr Fat.
