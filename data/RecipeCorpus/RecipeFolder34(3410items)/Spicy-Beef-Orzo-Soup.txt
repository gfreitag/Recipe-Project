Title: Spicy Beef Orzo Soup
Categories: Meats, Soup/stew
Yield: 10 servings
1 c  Onions -- finely chopped
2 c  Fresh Green Beans --
-chopped
1/2 c  Orzo
1 ts Salt
1 ts White Pepper -- ground
12 c  Water
1 lb Beef Round Steak, Boneless
-cut in bite-size pcs
1 cn (16 Oz) Tomatoes, Whole,
-Salt-Free -- cut in
-bite-size pcs
For a less spicy version, use less pepper.

In a 5-qt Dutch oven over high heat, bring the water to a boil. Add
the meat, tomatoes and onions and cook for 30 mins, stirring often.
Add the green beans, orzo, salt and pepper; continue cooking for 20
mins, or until the meat is tender. Serve hot. Makes 10 cups.
