Title: Hot and Garlicky Chicken Wings
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 15 wings
10    To 15 drops Tabasco
1 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
1 c  Italian-style bread crumbs
1 ts Black pepper
-JUDI M. PHELPS
2 lb Chicken wings; about 15
-wings
3    Heads fresh garlic
1 c  Olive oil plus 1 tabl.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Disjoint chicken wings, discarding

tips, rinse and pat dry.  Separate garlic cloves and peel.  Place

garlic, olive oil and Tabasco in blender or food processor; puree.

Combine Parmesan, bread crumbs and pepper in a plastic bag.  Dip

wings, one at a time, in the garlic puree and roll in bread crumb

mixture coating thoroughly.  Coat a shallow nonstick baking pan

with oil and add wings in a single layer. Drizzle with remaining

garlic in a single layer.  Drizzle with remaining garlic puree and

sprinkle with any remaining bread crumb mixture.  Bake for 45 to 60

minutes until brown and crisp.  About 15 wings.
