Title: Grandaddy's Green Tomato Pickle (Mennonite)
Categories: Pickles, Can/preserv, Tomatoes, Mennonite
Yield: 1 gallon +/-
2 pt Vinegar
1/4 c  Salt
1 c  Sugar
1 ga Green tomatoes; sliced
10 md Onions
1 tb Mustard seed
Slice tomatoes in thin slices; do not peel.
Slice onions in thin rings.
Place alternate layers of sliced tomato and onion in a crock,
sprinkling each layer with salt.  Cover with a plate and weight.
Let stand overnight. Drain off liquid in the morning and cover
mixture with the syrup made by combining sugar, vinegar and
mustard seed.
Cover and let stand 24 hours before using.

Mrs. H.D.H. Showalter of Virginia. _Mennonite Community Cookbook_
