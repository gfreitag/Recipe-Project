Title: Chilled Orange-Cantaloupe Soup
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
-orange juice
1/2 ts Orange zest
-finely grated
2 tb Slivered mint
Sugar cookies (opt)
-for serving
2 md Ripe cantaloupes
-peeled, seeded
-cut into large chunks
1/4 c  Plus 2 tbs
-superfine sugar
1/4 c  Plus 2 tbs
In a blender, working in batches, combine the cantaloupes, sugar,
orange juice and orange zest and blend until smooth. Transfer to a
large bowl and refrigerate until chilled for at least 45 minutes.
Serve in shallow bowls or large cups, garnished with the mint and
accompanied with the cookies.
