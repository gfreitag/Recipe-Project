Title: Sour Crean 'n' Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz
1 ts Dill weed -- or 1 T. fresh
Dill
1 cn Mushrooms -- drained sliced
4    Oz
1 ds Paprika
Cook wide egg noodles --
Optional
10 ea Chicken breast halves
Without skin -- or 10
Chicken pieces
1 ds Pepper
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup --
Undiluted
1 ea Onion soup mix -- dry
1 tb Lemon juice
Place chicken in a single layer in a 13x9x2 inch baking pan. Sprinkle
with pepper. combine soup, soup mix, sour cream, lemon juice, dill and
mushrooms; pour over chicken. Sprinkle with paprika. Bake, uncovered
at 350 deg. for 1 hour or until chicken is tender. Serve over egg
noodles if desired. Yield: * servings.
