Title: Lincoln Logs
Categories: Candies
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Eagle Brand milk
12 oz Chocolate chips
4 c  Graham cracker crumbs
Mix all ingredients.  Put into an 8 x 8 inch greased pan.  Bake at 350
degrees for 30 minutes.  Partly cool.  Cut into 1 x 2 inch pieces,
and roll in powdered sugar. Randy Rigg
