Title: Chile 'n Cheese Roll-ups
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 48 appetizers
1/2 c  Sliced green onions
1/2 c  Pitted ripe olives; chopped
4 ea (6-in.) Flour tortillas
x  Ortega garden style salsa,
-optional
4 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
-(4 oz.)
1 cn (4-oz.) Ortega diced green
-chiles
In bowl, blend cheese, chiles, scallions and olives.  Spread 1/2 cup
cheese mixture on each tortilla.  Roll up each tortilla jelly-roll
fashion. Wrap each roll in plastic wrap and chill at least 1 hour.

To serve, cut each roll into 12 (1/2-inch thick) slices.  Serve with
salsa, if desired.
