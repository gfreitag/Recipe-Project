Title: Texas Turkey Pasta Sauce
Categories: Pasta/rice, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Monterey Jack cheese;
-cut in cubes (1 cup)
12 oz Ground turkey
14 1/2 oz Can salsa with cilantro
8 oz Cn whole-kernel corn;drained
Prepare 12 ounces pasta according to package directions.
Meanwhile... Cook ground turkey in a large nonstick skillet over
medium-high heat, breaking up clumps, 3 to 4 minutes until no longer
pink. Reduce heat to medium-low.
Stir in salsa and corn.  Cover and simmer 4 to 5 minutes to blend
flavors.
Before draining pasta, scoop off about 1/4 cup of the pasta cooking
water.  After draining, return pasta to pot along with reserved
cooking water.
Toss sauce and cheese with hot pasta. Serves 4.

Per serving: 614 cal, 36 g pro, 81 g car, 17 g fat, 92 mg chol, 678
mg sodium.
Prep:  5 min   Cook:  9 min   Cost per serving:  99 cents
