Title: Cheap and Easy Bean and Hominy Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Low cal/fat, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Kidney beans
6 oz Tomato sauce
1 pk Chili/taco/mexican seasoning
1 cn Hominy, canned
1 cn Pinto beans
1 cn Garbanzo beans
Recipe by: gypsy@nmt.edu (Helga or Olga)

Mix and heat. Occasionally I'll add half a package of tempei (sp?) if
I'm feeling a bit rich.  And yes, you can live off of the stew for
quite a while.  :)
