Title: Poppy Seed Rolls
Categories: Breads, Country wom
Yield: 15 servings
Each
6 c  All-purpose flour --
Divided
1 cn Poppy seeds -- 12 oz
Filling
1 1/3 c  Walnuts -- chopped divided
1 ts Water
3/4 c  Milk
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
3 ea Eggs -- divided
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Warm water
1 pk Active dry yeast -- 1/4 oz
In a saucepan, heat milk, butter, sugar and salt. Cool to lukewarm.
Add 2 beaten eggs and the vanilla extract. Dissolve yeast in warm
water. In a large mixing bowl, combine milk mixture, yeast mixture
add enough flour to form a soft dough. Turn out onto a floured
surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 6 to 8 min. Place
dough in a greased bowl, turning once to grease top. Cover and let
rise in warm place until doubled, about 1 hr. Punch dough down and
let rest 5 min. Roll dough in a 12 in x 18 in. rectangle. Spread with
poppy seed filling and 1 cup nuts. Starting with the long side, roll
up dough and pinch the edges to seal. Place on a large greased baking
sheet, seam side down. Curve slightly to form a crescent shape. Cover
and let rise in a warm place until doubled, about 30 min. Beat
remaining egg with 1 teaspoon water; brush over top. Bake at 350 deg.
for about 30 m9in. Cool on wire rack. Glaze with confectioners sugar
icing and top with remaining nuts. Yield: 1 loaf.
