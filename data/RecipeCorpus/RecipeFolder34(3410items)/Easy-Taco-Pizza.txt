Title: Easy Taco Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
1 1/2 c  Shredded lettuce
1/2 c  Sour cream
6    7" flour tortillas
1 cn Refried beans
3/4 ts Ground cumin
1 cn Pizza style chunk tomatoes
Preheat oven to 450 F.  Place tortillas on baking sheet. Spread beaans
evenly over tortillas.  Stir cumin into tomatoes. Spread over beans.
Top with cheese. Bake 6 to 8 minutes or until hot and bubbly. Serve
with lettuce and sour cream.
