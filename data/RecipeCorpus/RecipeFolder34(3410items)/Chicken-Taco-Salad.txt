Title: Chicken Taco Salad
Categories: Newspaper, Poultry, Salads, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
.  1/2-inch dice
1    Cucumber; cut into a
.  1/2-inch dice
1    Ear of cooked corn; kernals
.  cut off
1/2 sm Yellow onion; minced
1 c  Salsa; your favorite
1/2 lb Cheddar cheese; grated
1/2 c  Cilantro leaves
Jalapeno peppers; pickled
.  or fresh, seeded & minced
4    Chicken breasts; cut into a
.  1/2-inch dice
2 tb Lime juice
1 cn Mild green chiles; 4oz
.  chopped
4    Tortilla salad bowls
1    Head romaine or curly leaf
. lettuce; sliced 1/2-inch
.  thick; then chopped
1 cl Garlic; minced
6    Roma tomatoes; cut into a
Toss the chicken breasts with the lime juice and the chilies; divide
among the four tortilla bowls.  Toss the lettuce, garlic, tomatoes,
cucumber, corn, onion and salsa; divide among the four bowls.
Sprinkle the cheese and cilantro over each equally.  Serve cold or at
room temperature with the jalapenos.

Nutritional Information per serving:  370 calories, 17g fat, 63mg
cholesterol, 518mg sodium
