Title: Pepperoni, Onion And Peppers Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1    12" pizza crust
1 1/2 c  Mozzarella; shred -- divide
1/4 lb Pepperoni -- slice thin
2 tb Parsley -- chopped
1/2 ts Pepper
2 tb Olive oil
1    Red pepper -- slice rings
1    Yellow pepper -- slice
Rings
1 md Onion -- slice thin
1/3 c  Marinara sauce
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat oil and add peppers and onion; cook,
stirring occasionally, until tender 10 to 12 minutes. Spread sauce
over crust. Top with 1 cheese, veggies and pepperoni. Sprinkle 1/2 c
more cheese, parsley and pepper. Per Slice: 330 Cal, 18 Gr Fat.
