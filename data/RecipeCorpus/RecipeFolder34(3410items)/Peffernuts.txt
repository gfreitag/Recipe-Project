Title: Peffernuts
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Cloves
1 tb Cinnamon
2 ts Baking powder, dissolved in
1 tb. of milk
1 tb Butter, heaping
1 lb Flour
1 lb Powdered sugar
4    Eggs
3 oz Citron, chopped
1/2 c  Nuts
Beat eggs till light, add sugar, beat 15 minutes.  Add flour with
spices. Makes very stiff dough.  Shape in little balls.  Let set
overnight. Bake at 350 degrees until light golden brown. Randy Rigg
