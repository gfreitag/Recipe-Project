Title: Honey Mustard Dressin #1
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Vinegar
3 pk Oil
1/2 ts Dry mustard
2 tb Honey
Mix 1/2 tsp. of dry mustard powder with just enough water to make it
smooth.  Let it sit for a few minutes until it opens your sinuses
when you

sniff it! Add 2-3 tbs. of honey to vinegar and oil and shake until
well mixed.  Vinegar and oil are in about the same proportions as a
plain dressing, 1 to 3.
