Title: Speedy Bean Soup
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Jalapeno pinta beans -- 15
oz Undrained
1 md Onion -- finely chopped
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Pepper
2 cn Bean and bacon soup
11 1/2 oz Each undiluted
1    Soup can water
3 cn Great northern beans
Or navy beans,undrained(15.8
Ouncec each)
In a large Dutch oven or soup kettle, combine all ingredients. Simmer
about 20 minutes. Yield: 3 qts.
