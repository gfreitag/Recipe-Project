Title: Dried Tomato Pita Pizzas
Categories: Pizza, Can/preserv
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Artichoke hearts; drain --
Slice, 7oz
1/3 c  Sliced ripe olives
2 ts Dried basil -- savory
Or 3 ts
Garlic -- pressed
8 oz Oil-pk sun-dried tomatoes
10    Milliliters
1/8 ts Hot pepper sauce
4    Pita breads -- 5-7"
1 1/2 c  Fontina cheese; shred --
6    Oz
Preheat baking sheet in 450~ oven. Drain oil from marinated tomatoes
into small bowl; set aside tomatoes. Mix garlic and hot pepper sauce
with oil. Brush onto both sides of breads. Cover breads with half of
the cheese. Arrange tomatoes, artichokes, olives and herbs over
cheese, dividing equally. Top with remaining cheese; place on baking
sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes just until breads are crisp.
