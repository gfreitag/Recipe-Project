Title: Tumbled Summer Greens With Dil1 Dressing - Country Living
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Country liv
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground white pepper
2 1/4 lb Mixed salad greens,
-rinsed and crisped
3/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Chopped fresh dill
2 tb Dijon-style prepared mustard
1. Several days before serving, in jar with tight fitting lid,
combine oil, lemon juice, dill, mustard, salt, and pepper. Shake to
combine dressing and refrigerate until ready to serve.

2. Just before serving, place greens into serving bowl. Shake
dressing well and toss with greens.
