Title: CHERRY PRESERVES - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Dips/spread, Fruit
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
3    12-oz packages frozen,
-unsweetened, pitted dark
-cherries
1. In large microwave-safe bowl, combine cherries and sugar. Cover
loosely and microwave on high (100 percent) 12 minutes.

2. Stir mixture to dissolve sugar. Microwave on high (100 percent),
uncovered, 45 minutes.

3. In food processor or blender, with chopping blade, process cherry
mixture with lemon juice until coarsely pureed. Check mixture for
doneness (see Testing for Doneness).

4. Spoon cherry preserves into jars and refrigerate or cool to room
temperature and spoon into plastic bags and freeze.
