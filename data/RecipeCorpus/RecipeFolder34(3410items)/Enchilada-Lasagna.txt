Title: Enchilada Lasagna
Categories: Tex/mexican, Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Each garlic, chili powder
-and cilantro (coriander)
1 ts Salt
1 lb Ground beef (or can use
-canned chicken)
1 cn (16 oz.) refried beans
12 md Flour tortillas
1 lb Cheddar, Jack, or Mexican
-cheese, grated
1 lg Can enchilada sauce, red or
-green
1    Onion, chopped
In a 2-quart saucepan, Brown beef with chopped onion, garlic,
cilantro, salt, chili powder and about 1/4 of the enchilada sauce.

Grease a lasagna pan or a large baking dish.  Line bottom of pan with
tortillas.  Sprinkle just enough sauce over tortillas to dampen. Layer
remaining ingredients in the following order: cheese, tortillas, meat
mixture, tortillas, sauce, refried beans, cheese, tortillas, meat
mixture, cheese, tortillas, and top with remaining sauce. Sprinkle
with remaining cheese.

Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes or until bubblly.

Serve with salad or spanish rice.
