Title: Splcy Peach Crlsp
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Ground cinnsmon
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground allspice
1/4 c  Buttter or margarine
Cream or ice cream
1 cn Sliced peaches -- 29 oz
Drained
1/2 c  Rolled oats
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
Arrange peaches in an 8-in. x 8-in. pan. In a small bowl, combine
oats, sugar, flour and spices. Cut in butter until coarse crumbs
form; sprinkle over peaches. Bake at 400 deg. for 15-20 minutes.
Serve warm with cream or ice cream. Yield: 6-8 servings
