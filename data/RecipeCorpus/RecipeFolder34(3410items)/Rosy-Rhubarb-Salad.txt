Title: Rosy Rhubarb Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
Unsweetened
1 ts Lemon juice
1 c  Apple -- peeled diced
1 c  Celery -- diced
1/4 c  Pecans -- chopped
3 c  Rhubarb, frozen -- or fresh
Sliced 1 in
1 tb Sugar
1 pk Raspberry gelatin powder --
3    Oz
1 c  Pineapple juice --
In a med. saucepan, cook and stir rhubarb and sugar over medium-low
heat until rhubarb is soft and tender. Remove from the heat; add
gelatin and stir until dissolved. Stir in pineapple and lemon juices.
Chill until partially set. Stir in apples, cedery and pecans. Pour
into a 4 1/2 cup mold or glass bowl. Chill several hours or
overnight. Yield: 8 servings.
