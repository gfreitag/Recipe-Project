Title: SWEET POTATO HASH BROWNS - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Side dish, Potatoes, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
Marjoram leaves
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
1 tb Butter
1 ts Brown sugar
4 md (about 2 lb) sweet potatoes,
-peeled and coarsely chopped
2 ts Vegetable oil
1 sm Onion, chopped
2 ts Chopped fresh or 1/2 t dried
1. In 3-quart saucepan, place sweet potatoes and enough water to
cover. Heat to boiling and cook 5 minutes; drain and set aside.

2. Meanwhile, in large nonstick skillet, heat oil over medium-low
heat; add onion and saute 3 minutes. Add drained sweet potatoes, the
marjoram, salt, and pepper. Cover and cook 15 minutes, stirring
occasionally.

3. Increase heat to medium-high. Add butter and brown sugar to
sweet-potato mixture. Cook, uncovered, 5 to 10 minutes longer or
until potatoes are tender and begin to brown, turning occasionally
with spatula. Serve warm.
