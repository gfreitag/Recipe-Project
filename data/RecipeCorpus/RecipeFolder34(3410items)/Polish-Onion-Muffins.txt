Title: Polish Onion Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Breads, Polish
Yield: 10 servings
1    Egg;
2 tb Butter; melted
1 c  Buttermilk;
1/4 c  Grated onion;
1 tb Butter; melted
1 tb Poppy seeds;
1 1/2 c  Flour;
1 ts Sugar;
1 1/2 ts Baking powder;
1 ts Soda;
1/2 ts Salt;
1/2 ts Dill;
Mix together flour, baking powder, soda, salt and dill.  Whisk egg
with buttermilk, 2 tablespoons melted butter and grated onion.  Blend
ingredients, stirring no more then 10 seconds.  Spoon into greased
muffin tins.  Brush tops with 1 tablespoon melted butter and sprinkle
with poppy seeds.  Bake until golden brown.  Bake at 400 degrees 15 -
20 minutes. Makes 10 muffins

NOTE: Inspired by Polish onion biscuits, these savory muffins are
great with a hearty bowl of homemade soup.  Serve them with krupnik
(barley-potato soup) or grochowka (split pea soup) for a real Polish
flavor.

Source: The Joy of Muffins, The International Muffin Cook Book
