Title: Garlic Garbanzos
Categories: Appetizers, Low cal/fat
Yield: 12 servings
2 tb Garlic -- finely minced
12    Sprigs fresh thyme
Salt and pepper
2 cn Garbanzo beans (16 ounce
Each)
2 tb Olive oil
Preheat the oven to 450.  Drain the beans and place them in a glass
baking dish in a single layer.  Drizzle with the oil, and toss with
your fingers to coat each bean.  Sprinkle evenly with the garlic, and
place the thyme evenly over.  Roast for 15 minutes.  Season with salt
and pepper to taste. Serve with toothpicks to spear the beans. 91
calories and 3 grams fat.
