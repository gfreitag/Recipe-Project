Title: Mom's Punch
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 3 servings
2 qt Ginger ale
1 qt Sherbet (any flavor)
1 1/2 qt Cans of cranberry juice
4    6 oz. cans frozen lemonade
Mold with any fruit juice
Mix cranberry juice and lemonade in punch bowl, place frozen mold in
center and add 1 bottle of ginger ale, then add sherbet.  Add the
rest of ginger ale.  Lemon or lime sherbet makes a tart punch. Randy
