Title: Layered Lettuce Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 pk Whipped topping -- frozen,
Thawed
1 ds Yellow food coloring --
Optional
1 ea Graham cracker pie crust, 9
Inch
1 c  Mayonnaise
3 tb Grated parmesan cheese
2 tb Sugar
1 md Lettuce -- head
Torn in bite size piece
1 md Cauliflower flowerets
1 md Red onion -- sliced thinly
1/2 c  Bacon bits
In a small bowl, stir together first three ingredients for dressing;
set aside. Layer remaining ingredients in a large salad bowl. Pour on
dressing; refrigerate. Toss just before serving. Yield: 6 to 8
servings.
