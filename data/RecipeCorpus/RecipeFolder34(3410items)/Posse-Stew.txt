Title: Posse Stew
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Hominy, yellow -- drained
1 cn Corn, whole kernel, canned
Drained 16 oz
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 tb Cornstarch -- optional
1/4 c  Water -- optional
1 lb Ground beef, extra lean --
Or turkey
1 md Onion -- diced
1 cn Tomatoes -- undrained 16 oz
1 cn Beans -- undrained 15 oz
Chili
1 cn Green chili pepper --
Undrained 4 oz
In a skillet, brown ground meat and onion; drain. Stir in tomatoes,
bean, chilies, hominy and corn; season with salt and pepper. For a
thicken stewk combine cornstarch and water, then stir into stew. Cook
and stir until thickened. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
