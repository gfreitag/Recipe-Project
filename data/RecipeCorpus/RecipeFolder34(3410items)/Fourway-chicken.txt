Title: Fourway-chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
4 tb Soy Sauce
4 tb Lemon Juice
1    Chicken, cut up
4 tb Margarine
4 tb Honey
Place chicken in baking dish, skin side down [if you leave it on].
Combine remaining ingredients in a saucepan and cook over low heat
until dissolved. Pour over chicken. Bake uncovered at 350 deg F for 1
hour, turning once and basting frequently until covered with a rich
brown sauce.
