Title: Couscous With Fruit
Categories: Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Golden raisins
1/4 c  Diced dried apricots
1 c  Couscous
1 1/2 c  Boiling, salted water
Stir couscous into boiling salted water.  Add raisins and apricots.
Remove from heat, cover and let stand 5 minutes. Source: Woman's Day
