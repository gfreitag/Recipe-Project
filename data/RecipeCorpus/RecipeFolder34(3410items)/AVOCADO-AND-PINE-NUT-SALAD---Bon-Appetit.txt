Title: AVOCADO AND PINE NUT SALAD - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Minced red onion
1/4 c  Grated mozzarella
2 tb White wine vinegar
1 1/2 tb Dijon mustard
6 tb Olive oil
Salt and pepper
1    Head lettuce, torn into
-bite-size pieces
1    Avocado, peeled, pitted
-and chopped
1    Tomato, chopped
1/3 c  Pine nuts, toasted
1/4 c  Alfalfa sprouts
Place lettuce in large bowl. Add avocado and next 5 ingredients.

Whisk vinegar and mustard in small bowl. Gradually whisk in oil.
Season with salt and pepper. Pour 1/3 C dressing over salad and toss.
Serve, passing remaining dressing.
