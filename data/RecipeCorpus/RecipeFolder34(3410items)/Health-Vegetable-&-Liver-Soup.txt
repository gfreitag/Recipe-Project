Title: Health Vegetable & Liver Soup
Categories: Microwave, Soup/stew, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Sugar
1 c  Fresh or frozen spinach;
-chopped
1 cn Tomatoes (20 oz)
Salt & pepper to taste
1/2 lb Liver; chopped
2 tb Butter
4 sm Carrots; diced
1/2 c  Celery; diced
1 lg Onion; thinly sliced
2 c  Hot water
Stir cook the liver in butter.  Add remaining ingredients.  Stir
well. Cook covered (25 min in microwave).  This soup freezes and
reheats very well.
