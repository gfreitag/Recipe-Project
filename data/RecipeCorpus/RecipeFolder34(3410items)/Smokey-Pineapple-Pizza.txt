Title: Smokey Pineapple Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
Shred
1 cn Pineapple tidbits --
Drained
3    Green onions -- sliced
1    12" prepared crust
1/2 c  Bottled pizza sauce
1/4 lb Cooked ham -- diced
1/2    Green pepper -- diced
1 1/2 c  Smoked cheddar cheese --
Spread crust with pizza sauce.  Top with ham, green peppers,
onions,cheese and pineapple. Bake in 425 F oven 20 minutes or until
cheese is melted and pizza is heated through.
