Title: ATHOLL BROSE - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Honey
1 pt Scotch whisky
1 c  Old-fashioned rolled oats
3 c  Boiling water
1. In large bowl, combine rolled oats and boiling water. Stir with a
spoon breaking up oats as much as possible. Set aside 1/2 hour.

2. Strain oat mixture into a 1-quart pitcher, pressing to get 2 C
liquid. you do not get enough liquid, pour more boiling water through
oats in strainer until liquid measures 2 C. Discard oats; add honey
and whisky to the oat liquid; stir until well combined. Serve immedi-
ately; shake or stir before pouring into each mug.
