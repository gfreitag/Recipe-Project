Title: Vegetarian Padadise Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Basil
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Marinara sauce
2 c  Fresh mozzarella; diced --
Divided
1    Plum tomato -- diced
1 c  Small broccoli florets
2 tb Olive oil
1 c  Eggplant -- diced
1 tb Olive oil
1 1/2 c  Yellow squash -- diced
1 c  Snall zucchini -- diced
1/2 c  Red onion -- chopped
Preheat oven to 450~. Cook broccoli in boiling salted water 2 minutes;
drain. Heat olive oil and add eggplant and cook 10 to 12 minutes until
tender. Add 1 tb more oil, squash, zucchini, onion, salt, basil and
pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, until veggies are tender and
golden, about 8 minutes. Spread sauce over crust. Top with 1 c
cheese, then veggies. Sprinkle 1 more c cheese and tomatoes. Bake 10
minutes. Per Slice: 380 Cal, 21 Gr Fat.
