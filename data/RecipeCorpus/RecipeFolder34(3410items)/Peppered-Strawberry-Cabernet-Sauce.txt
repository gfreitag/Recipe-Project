Title: Peppered Strawberry Cabernet Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Butter
2 ts Cracked black pepper
Salt
1 c  Cabernet sauvignon
1 lb Strawberries, hulled
-sliced 1/4" thick
2 tb Flat-leaf parsley
Put the wine in a medium saucepan, bring to a boil and reduce over
high heat until only about  cup remains, about 6-8 minutes. When the
wine is reduce, add the strawberries and reduce the heat to very low.
Stir in the parsley, butter, cracked pepper and salt. The
strawberries should be softened, but still hold their shape. Serve
with broiled salmon.
