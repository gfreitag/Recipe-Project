Title: Corn Wheels Wlth Llme Butter
Categories: Vegetables, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Fresh lime juice
1/2 ts Pepper -- if desired
4 md Ears of corn
2 tb Butter
Using cleaver or large knife. cut corn into 1-in. lengths. In a large
pot, bring water to boil over high heat. Add corn wheels: cover and
boil 2 minutes. Melt butter; mix with the lime juice. Transfer corn
to serving platter and drizzle with lime-butter. Sprinkle with
pepper, if desired. Yield: 4 servings
