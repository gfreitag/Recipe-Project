Title: Suess-Sauer Rotkohl (Sweet-Sour Red Cabbage)
Categories: Aust/german, Vegetables
Yield: 1 recipe
1/2 c  Water
1/3 c  Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 sm Onion
5 c  Shredded red cabbage
2 c  Salted water
4    Slices diced bacon
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Flour
Cook cabbage in 2 cups salted water, in covered pan 5 to 8 minutes,
until crisp-tender.  Drainandreservewater.  Fry bacon, remove bacon
and half of bacon fat.  Reserve.  Add sugar and flour to remaining
fat, blend.
Add reserved water, vinegar, seasoning and onions.  Cook until thick,
about 5 minutes.  Add bacon and cabbage, heat thoroughly.  Garnish
with diced bacon.
