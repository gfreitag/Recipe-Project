Title: Easy Horseradish Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 cup
8 oz Carton commercial sour cream    1 1/2 tb Prepared horseradish
2 tb Milk
Combine all ingredients; stir well.
