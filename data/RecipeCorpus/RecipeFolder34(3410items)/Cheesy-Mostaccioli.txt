Title: Cheesy Mostaccioli
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Black pepper
1 ts Italian seasoning
3 c  Shredded Mozzarella cheese
1 lb Mostaccioli, cooked
1 1/2 lb Ground beef, brown & drain
11 cn Cheddar cheese soup
30 oz Spaghetti sauce
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Mix all ingredients, except 1 1/2 cups
Mozzarella cheese, in a large bowl.  Place mixture in a 9 x 13 inch
baking dish.  Sprinkle remaining 1 1/2 cups of Mozzarella cheese on
top of mixture.  Bake for 25 minutes. Randy Rigg
