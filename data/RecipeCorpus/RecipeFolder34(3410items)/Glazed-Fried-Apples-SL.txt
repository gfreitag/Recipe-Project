Title: Glazed Fried Apples SL
Categories: Side dish, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
4 md Apples; unpeeled and cored
1/4 c  Plus 2 tbsp butter or
-margarine
Slice apples into 1/8-inch rings.  Place apples and butter in a
large skillet; cover and cook over medium heat 10 minutes or until
apples arre tender.  Add sugar, and cook uncovered 10 minutes or
until golden brown.
