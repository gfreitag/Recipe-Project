Title: Picante Chicken 'n Rice
Categories: Poultry, Pasta/rice, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
-sauce
1 c  Uncle Ben's Converted Brand
-rice
1 ea Ripe avocado; peeled and
-sliced
1 md Tomato; coarsely chopped
1/2 c  Cheddar or Monterey Jack
-cheese; shredded (2 oz.)
6 ea Boneless, skinless chicken
-breast halves
x  Salt; to taste (optional)
3/4 c  Onion; coarsely chopped
3 lg Garlic cloves; minced
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 1/2 c  Chicken broth
1 c  Medium red salsa or picante
Sprinkle chicken breasts with salt; set aside.  Cook onion and garlic
in oil in 10-inch skillet 2 minutes. Add chicken broth and salsa.
Bring to a boil.  Stir in rice.  Arrange chicken breasts over rice.
Cover tightly and simmer 20 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Let stand
covered until all liquid is absorbed, about 5 minutes.  Garnish with
avocado, tomato and cheese. Serves 6.
