Title: Blueberry Grunt
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Shortening
3/4 c  Milk
2 c  Blueberries
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Lemon juice
2 c  Flour
4 ts Baking powder
Put the blueberries, 1/2 cup of sugar, lemon juice and 1 cup of water
into a pan and bring to boil, stirring continuously. Boil for 3
minutes. Sift together the flour, baking powder and salt.  Add the 2
tbsp sugar. Cut in the fat and mix with milk to make a soft dough.
Drop the dough over the boiling fruit, cover and simmer for 10
minutes without raising the lid. Submitted By DOROTHY FLATMAN   On
08-29-95
