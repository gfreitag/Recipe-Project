Title: Tropical Pops
Categories: Diabetic, Fruit, Snacks
Yield: 3 - 6 pops
-- (canned or fresh)
1/4 c  Orange juice;
1 ct (8-oz) low-fat or non-fat
-- pina-colada yogurt
1/4 c  Crushed pineapple; drained
Stir together all ingredients.  Freeze until firm in a mold if you
have one.  If NOT, freeze mixture in small paper cups.  Add a stick
or plastic spoon when pops are half frozen.  To eat, peel away the
paper. (Eat right away or transfer to a plastic container or
heavy-duty plastic bags for long-term freezing).
