Title: Pronto Chili
Categories: Chili, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Black pepper
2 tb Brown sugar
1 cn Kidney beans -- 16 oz
Drained
And rinsed
Shredded cheddar cheese
1 lb Ground beef, lean
1 sm Chopped onion
1 cn Tomato paste -- 6 oz
2 cn Water
2 ts Chili powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cumin -- optional
In a saucepan, brown beef and onion. Drain fat. Stir in all remaining
ingredients except cheese; cover and simmer 20 min. Top each serving
with cheese, if desired. Yield: 4 servings.
