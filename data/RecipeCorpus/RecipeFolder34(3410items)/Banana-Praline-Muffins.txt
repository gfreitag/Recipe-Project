Title: Banana Praline Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 9 servings
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
1/2 c  Sugar -- sugar
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 1/2 c  Buttermilk baking mix
1/3 c  Pecans -- chopped
3 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Sour cream
3 sm Bananas -- mashed 1 cup
In a small bowl, combine pecans, brown sugar and sour cream; set
aside. In a large bowl, combine bananas, egg, sugar and oil; mix
well. Add baking mix and stir just until moistened. Fill greased or
paper-lined muffin cups two thirds full. Place 1 teaspoonful of pecan
mixture in center of each muffin. Bake at 400 deg. for 15 to 18 min.
or until muffins test done. Cool in pan 10 min. before removing.
Yield: about 9 muffins.
