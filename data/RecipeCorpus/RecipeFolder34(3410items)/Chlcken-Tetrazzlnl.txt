Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Pimiento -- chopped
2 c  Chicken -- cubed cooked
4 oz Spaghetti -- cooked/drained
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz.
Grated Parmesan cheese
Paprika
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup --
10 3/4 oz Undiluted
1 cn Cream of chicken soup --
10 3/4 oz Undiluted
1    Jar mushrooms
4 1/2 oz Sliced, drained
In a saucepan, melt butter over low heat. Add soups and mushrooms.
Stir until well blended. Remove from the heat; add pimiento, chicken,
spaghetti and sour cream. Pour mixture into greased 13-in. x 9-in. x
2-in. baking pan. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and paprika. Bake,
uncovered, at 350 deg. for 30-35 minutes. Yield: 4-6 servings
