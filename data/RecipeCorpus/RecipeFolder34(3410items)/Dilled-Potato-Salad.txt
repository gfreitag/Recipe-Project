Title: Dilled Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetarian
1 c  Light or Cholesterol Free
Reduced Calorie Mayonnaise
Yield: 4 cups
:
1 Tb Prepared Mustard
2 Tb Chopped fresh Dill or 1 1/2 ts Dried Dill
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper 1 1/2 lb Small Red Potatoes, Cooked,
-and Quartered
:
1/2 c  Sliced Green Onions

Combine Mayonnaise, Mustard, Dill, Salt and Pepper. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Cover, Chill. Makes about 4 cups end product.
