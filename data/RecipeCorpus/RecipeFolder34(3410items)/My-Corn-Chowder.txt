Title: My Corn Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
-and cubed
1 1/2 tb Oil
1 ts Garlic
2 tb Parsley
8-12 cups milk or cream
4-5 large ears fresh corn,
-cut off cob
1 lb Bacon, diced
1 lg Onion, diced
3-4 stalks celery, diced
3-4 med potatoes, peeled
Dice bacon and saute in dutch oven with the oil.  Add the onions,
celery and potatoes, and cook until onion is translucent.  Add
kernelled corn, and continue to cook until corn starts to take on a
bright yellow colour. Add the milk or cream, garlic, parsley, and
salt and pepper to taste. Low heat and simmer until the potatoes are
tender. Origin: Sharon Stevens Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Sept/94.
