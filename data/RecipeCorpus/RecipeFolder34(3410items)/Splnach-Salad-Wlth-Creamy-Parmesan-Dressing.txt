Title: Splnach Salad Wlth Creamy Parmesan Dressing
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Bite-size piec
4 c  Fresh spinach
Washed, drained and torn in
MMMMM-------------------------DRESSING:------------------------------
1 1/2 ts Dill weed
1 1/2 ts Onion flakes
1 1/2 ts Lemon pepper
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
2 tb Evaporated milk
Combine dressing ingredients (thinning with additional milk. if
necessary) and serve over spinach. Coverand refrigerate any leftovers
