Title: Snappy Ginger Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 20 servings
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1 c  Molasses
1 ea Egg
3 c  All-purpose flour
In a mixing bowl, beat the oil and sugars. Beat in molasses and egg.
Combine the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, ginger and salt; stir into
molasses mixture alternately with water. Fill greased or paper-lined
muffin cups two-thirds full. Bake at 350 deg. for 20 to 25 min. or
until muffins test done. Cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing
to a wire rack. Yield: about 20 standard size muffins.
