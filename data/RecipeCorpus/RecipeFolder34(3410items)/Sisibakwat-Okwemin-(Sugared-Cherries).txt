Title: Sisibakwat-Okwemin (Sugared Cherries)
Categories: Desserts, Native amer, Okanagan, Fruit
Yield: 5 servings
1 c  Water
2 1/2 lb Giant B.C. cherries
1 1/2 c  Maple sugar
Put maple sugar & water in a pot & boil for 15 minutes.  Add the
cherries & simmer for 10 minutes.  Turn off the heat, cool & eat
fresh with bannock.

Bernard Assiniwi, "Indian Recipes"
