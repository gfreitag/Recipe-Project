Title: Qwik Stew
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 md Onion, chopped
1    Beef bullion cube
Celery salt to taste
Garlic salt to taste
2 md Potatoes, cooked, sliced
2    Carrots, cooked, chopped
1 cn Corned beef
1 cn Stewed tomatoes
Combine all ingredients, mix well and simmer until flavor is blended.
