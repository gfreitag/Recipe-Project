Title: Chicken Parmesan Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Mozzarella -- shredded
8    Black olives -- halved
1 tb Parmesan cheese -- grated
1/2 ts Oregano -- crushed
10 oz Chicken breasts
1/4 c  Flour
Vegetable oil
2 c  Tomato sauce
1    12" Boboli
Dredge chicken in flour.  Pour oil into large skillet. Heat over
medium heat until hot.  Add chicken,  fry until chicken is tender and
golden brown.  Remove to paper towel with slotted spoon. Spread 1 cup
sauce over bread shell.  Sprinkle with 1/2 c Mozzarella cheese. Top
with chicken, remaining tomato sauce and olives. Sprinkle with
remaining mozzarella cheese, parmesan and oregano. Bake onm ungreased
baking sheet in lower 1/3 of ovn at 450F about 8 to 10 minutes or
until puffed and lightly browned.
