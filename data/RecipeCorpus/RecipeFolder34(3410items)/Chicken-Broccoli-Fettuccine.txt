Title: Chicken Broccoli Fettuccine
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Pasta/rice
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 1/2 c  Cooked chicken,cut in strips
3 c  Hot, cooked fettuccome
1 tb Margarine
1 cl Garlic, minced
10 3/4 oz Can broccoli cheese soup
1 c  Milk
In a skillet, in hot margarine, cook garlic 2 minutes stirring
constantly. Stir in soup, milk, and cheese.  Heat to boiling.  Add
chicken. Cook over low heat 5 minutes, stirring often.  Toss with
fettucine. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   08-26-95
