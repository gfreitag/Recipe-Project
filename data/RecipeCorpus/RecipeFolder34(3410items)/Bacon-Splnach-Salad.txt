Title: Bacon Splnach Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Vinegar
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Dry mustard
6 ea Bacon strips
6 c  Spinach leaves -- torn
1/2    Head iceberg lettuce --
Torn
1 bn Green onions -- thinly
Sliced
In a skillet, cook bacon until crisp. Drain, crumble and set aside.
Place greens and onions in a salad bowl; refrigerate until serving.
Combine all remaining ingredients in a jar and shake until well
mixed. Just before serving, pour dressing over greens. Toss well to
coat. Add bacon and toss. Yield: 6-8 servings
