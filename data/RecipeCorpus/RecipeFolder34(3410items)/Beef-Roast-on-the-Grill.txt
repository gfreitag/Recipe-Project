Title: Beef Roast on the Grill
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Ginger
3 tb Lemon juice
1 ea Lean roast, 3 to 4 pounds
1 c  Soy sauce
6 tb Sugar
3 cl Garlic; minced -OR-
1 ts Garlic powder
Mix soy sauce, sugar, garlic, ginger, and lemon juice.
Cover roast with mixture; marinate in refrigerator for hours,
occasionally turning roast.
Cook on grill for approximately 20 minutes on each side, or until
done.
