Title: Skillet Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Green pepper
1/4 c  Black olives -- sliced
2 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
1 pk Pizza crust -- mix
1 cn Tomato sauce -- 8 oz
1 ts Italian seasoning
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 c  Pepperoni slices
Grease a 12 in electric or stove-top skillet. Prepare pizza crust
according to package directions. Line bottom and 1/2 in. up the sides
of the skillet with dough. Combine tomato sauce, italian seasoning
and oregano; spread over dough. Layer pepperoni, onion, green pepper
and olives over sauce; sprinkle with cheese. Cover and cook over
medium heat (set electric skillet to 375deg.) for 15 min or until
crust is brown on bottom and cheese is melted. Slide out onto a
cutting board and cut into wedges or squares. Serve immediately.
Yield: 1 pizza (12 inches).
