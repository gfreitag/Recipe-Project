Title: MUSHROOM-BARLEY SOUP - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  White wine
3 tb Snipped fresh dill
2 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Dried marjoram leaves
1 ts Dried thyme leaves
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
1 tb Vegetable oil
3 lg Onions, coarsely chopped
1    Clove garlic, finely chopped
1 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1 c  Quick-cooking barley
3 md Carrots, thinly sliced
6 c  Vegetable broth
1. In 5-quart saucepot, heat oil over medium heat. Add onions,
garlic, and mushrooms; cover and cook about 5 minutes, or until
mushrooms collapse and give up their liquid. Uncover and saute until
onions are lightly browned.

2. Stir barley into mushroom mixture; saute 2 minutes or until lightly
toasted.

3. Add remaining ingredients. Heat to boiling over high heat, then
reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer about 15 minutes or until
barley is tender.
