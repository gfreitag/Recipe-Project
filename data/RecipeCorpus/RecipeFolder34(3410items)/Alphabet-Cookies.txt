Title: Alphabet Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 dozen
2 1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Margerine
1 ea Egg
1 ts Vanilla
Decorations chocolate chips, sprinkles, raisins nuts etc. 1. Mix
together sugar margerine egg and vanilla in large bowl. 2. Mix
together flour baking powder and salt.  Add mixture to first bowl.
Stir until it feels like soft clay. 3. Put dough in freezer for 20
minutes. 4. Roll dough into snakes and shape into letters.  Place
letters on baking sheet and decorate. Bake cookies for 10 to 12
minutes. From chickadee magazine * CROSSPOSTED Submitted By
