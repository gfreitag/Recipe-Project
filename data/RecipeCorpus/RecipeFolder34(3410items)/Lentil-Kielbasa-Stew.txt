Title: Lentil Kielbasa Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Sausage, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Carrots ; chopped
1    Bay leaf
1 pn Thyme
2 lb Kielbasa ; sliced thinly,
2    Bacon slices ; chopped
3 md Onions ; chopped
1 1/2 qt Beef stock ; boullion
2 c  Dried lentils
Recipe by: lzsndt@ritvax.isc.rit.edu (Lynn Z. Schaeffer) Fry bacon and
onion in skillet.  Discard drippings (or store in your cherished
drippings jar).  Place all ingredients except for 1/2 pound of the
kielbasa into a large stockpot.  Bring to a boil and simmer for about
1 hour. Add remaining keilbasa and cook 10 minutes more.  Season to
taste.
