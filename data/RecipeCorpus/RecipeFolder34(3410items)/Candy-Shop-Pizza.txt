Title: Candy Shop Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg
12 oz Nestles semi-sweet morsels
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1 c  Candy bars -- chopped
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
10 tb Butter -- softened
1/2 c  Sugar
Preheat oven to 375F.  Lightly grease 14 inch pizza pan or 15x10 x1
inch baking pan.  In small bowl combine flour, baking soda and salt.
In large mixer bowl beat butter, sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
Beat in egg and vanilla extract.  Gradually beat in flour mixture.
Stir in 1 cup chocolate morsels. Spread in prepared pan. Bake 20 to
24 minutes or until lightly browned. Immediately sprinkle remaining 1
cup semi-sweet chocolate morsels over crust; drop peanut butter by
spoonfulls on morsels. Let stand 5 minutes or until morsels become
soft and shiny. Gently spread chocolate and peanut butter over crust.
Decorate pizza with candy bars, broken up. Cut into wedges, serve
warm.
