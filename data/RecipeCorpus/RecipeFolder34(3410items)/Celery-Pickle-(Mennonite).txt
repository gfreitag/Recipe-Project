Title: Celery Pickle (Mennonite)
Categories: Pickles, Can/preserv, Vegetables, Mennonite
Yield: 3 quarts
2 tb Salt
1 tb Dry mustard
1 ts Turmeric
3 qt Celery; chopped
3 c  Vinegar
1 c  Water
4 c  Sugar
Cut celery stems into 1 inch lengths. Cook in salt water until tender
but not soft.  Drain. Combine vinegar, sugar, water, and spices.
Bring to a boil. Add celery and bring to boiling point. Pack in
jarsand seal.

Helen Ernst, Ontario _Mennonite Community Cookbook_ Mary Emma
