Title: Extra-Quick Yeast Rolls
Categories: Breads, Country wom
Yield: 24 servings
3/4 c  Warm water
2 1/2 c  Buttermilk baking mix
1 pk Yeast -- quick-rise 1/4 oz
1 tb Sugar
Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Stir in biscuit mix and turn
out onto a lightly floured surface. Knead until smooth and elastic,
about 5 minutes. Shape into dinner-size or cut with 2 1/2 inch round
biscuit cutter. Place on greased baking sheet. Cover and let rise in
a warm place until doubled, about 30 min. or until golden brown.
Yield: 24 rolls.
