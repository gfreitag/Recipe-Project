Title: Cabbage Fruit Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise -- or salad
Dressing
1/4 c  Milk
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped & toasted
4 c  Shredded cabbage
2 ea Oranges -- peeled
Cut in bite-size pieces
2 md Apple -- choped
1 c  Seedless grapes -- red
Halves
1/4 c  Raisins -- or currants
In a large bowl, toss cabbage, oranges, apples, grapes and currants;
cover refrigerate. In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, milk,
lemon juice, sugar and salt; cover and refrigerate. Just before
serving, stir dressing and pecans into salad. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
