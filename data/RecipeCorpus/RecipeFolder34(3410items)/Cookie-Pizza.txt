Title: Cookie Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 8 servings
Snacks
Decorator icing
Cake decorations
1 pk Refrigerated sugar cookie
Dh
1 pk Bite size chewy fruit
Preheat oven to 350 F.  line izza pan with aluminum foil. spray with
cooking spray.  press in cookie dough. Press fruit snacks into dough.
Bake 20 minutes. Cool on wire rack. loosen from pan and remove foil.
Decorate with icing.
