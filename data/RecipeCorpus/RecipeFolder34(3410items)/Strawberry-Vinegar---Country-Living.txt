Title: Strawberry Vinegar - Country Living
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Dress/stuff, Salads, Country liv
Yield: 1 2/3 cups
1 1/2 c  White-wine vinegar
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Small ripe fresh
-strawberries, hulled
1. In medium-size jar, place berries and vinegar. Seal jar and let
stand in a sunny window for 3 or 4 days or until vinegar takes on a
bright red color. The berries will soften and lose their color.

2. Strain vinegar mixture into a 1-quart stainless-steel or enamel
saucepan and discard the berries. Stir sugar into vinegar and heat to
boiling. Simmer over low heat 10 minutes. Cool vinegar and pour into
1-pint bottle. Seal and store in a cool, dry place or in the
refrigerator.
