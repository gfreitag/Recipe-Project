Title: Eggless, No Cholesterol Corn Muffins
Categories: Vegetarian, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 tin of muf
2 ts Baking Powder
1 c  Skim Milk
2 tb Oil
1 c  Cornmeal
1/2 c  Flour
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
Sift and mix together the dry ingredients. Add the milk and
shortening. Pour into paper lined muffin cups until they are 2/3
filled. Bake in a pre-heated 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.
