Title: Carrot Raisin Slaw
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Cumin seed
1/2 c  White vinegar
1/4 c  Honey
Salt and pepper
2 c  Carrot -- shredded
1/4 c  Raisins
1    Red bell pepper -- thinly
-sliced
1/4 c  Parsley -- chopped
In large bowl, combine the carrots, raisins and red pepper and toss.
Add the parsley and cumin and toss again.  Add the vinegar, honey,
salt and pepper.  Toss one more time and serve.
