Title: Spicy Cranberry Warmer
Categories: Beverages, Country wom
Yield: 10 servings
1/3 c  Packed brown sugar
4 c  Cranberry juice
Additional cinnamon sticks
Optional
3    Whole cloves
2    Sticks cinnamon
2    Whole allspice
4 c  Apple cider
Place first three ingredients in a double thickness of cheesecloth.
Bring up corners of cloth and tie with string. Place with cider in a
large saucepan. (Or, if desired, place loose spices in saucepan and
strain before serving.) simmer, covered, for 5 min. Stir in sugar and
simmer for 5 min. Add cranberry juice and heat to simmering
temperature. Serve hot in mugs. Garnish with cinnamon sticks if
desired. Yield: 8 to 10 servings.
