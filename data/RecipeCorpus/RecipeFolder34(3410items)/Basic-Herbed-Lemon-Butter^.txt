Title: Basic Herbed Lemon Butter^
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 12 servings
Minced onion, tarragon, or
Paprika
OR
1 ts Dill weed or basil
1/4 c  Butter
1 ts Freshly grated lemon peel
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
1/2 ts Parsley, oregano, marjoram,
Melt butter, add lemon juice and peel, blending well. add any one or a
combination of seasonings, if desired. Keep warm. Serve with
vegetables. Makes about 12 1 teaspoon servings with 33 calories, 4 g
fat and 33 mg cholesterol.
