Title: Razz-A-Dazzical Pear Shake
Categories: Fruit, Beverages, Kids
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Fresh raspberries
1 c  Raspberries sherbet;
1 c  Vanilla ice cream;
1 1/2 c  Canned pears; drained
-- chopped
1 c  Milk
1 c  Frozen raspberries; -=OR=-
In the container of electric blender place all ingredients.  Blend
until smooth, about 30 seconds.  Pour into four tall glasses.

Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Food Section, 8/31/95 + Winner
prize winner of "Great Shakes" contest for kids.
