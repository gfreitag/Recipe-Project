Title: Baked Shrimp
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 3 servings
Juice of 4 lemons
1 lg Bottle of Wish-Bone Italian
Dressing
5 lb Fresh shrimp
1 lb Butter, melted
2 tb Black pepper
Clean shrimp and place in a large casserole dish.  Combine remaining
ingredients; pour over shrimp.  Cover, and bake 45 minutes at 350
degrees. Serve with salad and French bread. Randy Rigg
