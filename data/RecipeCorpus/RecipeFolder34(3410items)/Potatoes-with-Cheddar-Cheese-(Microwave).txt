Title: Potatoes with Cheddar Cheese (Microwave)
Categories: Side dish, Microwave, Potatoes, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/4 c  White wine
1 ea Red sweet pimiento
2 c  Cheddar cheese; grated
2 tb Butter
Salt and pepper
2 lb Grated potatoes
Melt the butter in a bowl in the microwave for 1 minute on High.
Season generously with salt and pepper.
Sprinkle salt and pepper in the base of a round dish 9 inches in
diameter.  Add the potatoes and pour the white wine over.  Cover and
microwave on high for 14 minutes.
Dice the sweet pimiento very finely.  Mix the Cheddar cheese with
the diced pimiento.  Sprinkle on top of the potatoes and pour the
melted butter over.  Let the cheese melt for 2 to 3 minutes, then
serve.
