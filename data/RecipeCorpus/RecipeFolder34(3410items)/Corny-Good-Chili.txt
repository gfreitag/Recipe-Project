Title: Corny Good Chili
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Condensed tomato soup --
L0-3/4 oz. undiluted
1 cn Condensed vegetable soup --
10 3/4 oz Undiluted
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar --
Optional
1 tb Vinegar
3 tb Chili powder
1 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion -- chopped
1/4 c  Celery -- chopped
1 cn Pork and beans -- 16 oz.
Undrained
1 cn Kidney beans
15 1/2 oz Rinsed and drained
1 cn Whole kernel corn -- 12 oz.
Undrained
In a Dutch oven, brown ground beef, onion and celery; cook until
tender. Drain. Add remaining ingredients; simmer until heated
through. Yield: about 6-8 servings
