Title: Almond Balls for Soup
Categories: Nuts/grains, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Lemon rind, grated
Salt
1 ea Egg, seperated
1/4 c  Almonds, chopped
Beat egg yolk until light; add almonds, lemon rind and salt. Whip egg
white until stiff.  Fold into mixture. Drop from teaspoon into
boiling fat and deep fry until brown. Drain on brown paper. Add to
soup just before serving.
