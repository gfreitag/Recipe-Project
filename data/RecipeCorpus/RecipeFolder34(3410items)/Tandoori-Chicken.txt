Title: Tandoori Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Indian
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Paprika
1 ts Curry powder
8    Chicken breast halves
-without skin -- boned
16 oz Nonfat yogurt
1 tb Ground cumin
2 ts Pepper
Place chicken in baking dish.  Pierce in several places.  Set aside.

Mix remaining ingredients in a small bowl.  Pour over chicken, coating
well.  Cover and chill 2 hours to overnight.

Preheat broiler.  Broil chicken pieces 5-6 inches from heat, turning
occasionally, approximately 6 minutes per side, until cooked through

Note:  The marinade freezes well.  For a smaller number of servings,
use only the amount needed and put the rest in the freezer in similar
portions to what you will use (ex., enough for 2 chicken pieces.)
Thaw chicken and marinade in the same container!
