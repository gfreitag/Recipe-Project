Title: Zesty Sausage and Beans
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
16 oz Thick & chunky salsa
3 c  Hot cooked rice
1 lb Smoked sausage, sliced
15 1/2 oz Can dark red kidney beans,
Drained
Combine smoked sausage, beans, and salsa in a large skillet.  Bring
to a boil over medium heat.  Cover and simmer 10 minutes, or until
hot, stirring occasionally.  Serve sausage mixture over rice. Randy
