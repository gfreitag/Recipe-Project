Title: The Real Mcpizza Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Cheddar -- shredded
2 tb Mayo or salad dressing
2 ts Ketchup
1 c  Iceberg lettuce -- shreded
1 sm Red onion -- slice thin
1    Dill pickle -- slice thin
1/4 c  Ketchup
1    12" pizza crust
1 ts Oil
1 ts Sesame seeds
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Preheat broiler. Spread ketchup evenly over crust. Brush border with
oil and sprinkle with sesame seed. Flatten beef into 9" patty on
foil-lined cookie sheet. Season with salt and pepper. Broil 4" from
heat source 4 minutes. Slide patty onto crust and sprinkle with
Cheddar. Reduce heat to 450~ and bake 10 minutes. Meanwhile, combine
mayo and ketchup. Spread on pizza and layer with lettuce, onion and
pickle. Per Slice: 410 Cal; 8 gr Fat.
