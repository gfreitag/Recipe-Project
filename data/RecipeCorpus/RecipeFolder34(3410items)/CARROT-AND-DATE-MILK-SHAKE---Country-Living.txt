Title: CARROT AND DATE MILK SHAKE - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Beverages
Yield: 5 servings
-vanilla yogurt, softened
-slightly
Carrots and their green tops
-(opt.)
2 c  Skim milk
1 c  Coarsely shredded carrots
1/2 c  Pitted dates, cut up
1    1-pint container frozen
1. In electric blender on low speed, blend milk, carrots, and dates
just until carrots anct dates are very finely chopped. Increase speed
to high and blend mixture until smooth. Turn off blender.

2. Add yogurt to carrot mixture. Cover and blend just until frothy
and well mixed. Pour into glasses; garnish with carrots and some
green tops, if desired.
