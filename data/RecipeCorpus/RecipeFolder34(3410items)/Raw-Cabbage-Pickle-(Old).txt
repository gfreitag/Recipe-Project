Title: Raw Cabbage Pickle (Old)
Categories: Pickles, Can/preserv, Mennonite, Vegetables
Yield: 2 quarts?
1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Vinegar
1 lg Head cabbage
1 1/2 tb Salt
1 ts Celery or mustard seed
Shred cabbage coarsely. Add salt and sugar and work it well into the
cabbage. Add vinegar and spice and let stand 12 hours before serving.

Mrs. Amos K. Mast of Pennsylvania. _Mennonite Community Cookbook_
