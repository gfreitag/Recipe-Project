Title: SPICED PECANS - Country Living
Categories: Appetizers, Nuts/grains, Country liv
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Ground allspice
1 1/2 ts Hot red-pepper sauce
3 c  Pecan halves
2 tb Butter, melted
1    Large egg white
2 tb Sugar
1. Heat oven to 350'F. On large baking sheet, stir together pecans and
butter. Bake pecans 5 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned, Cool
pecans on baking sheet on wire rack 10 minutes.

2. In small bowl, combine egg white, sugar, cinnamon, cloves,
allspice, and pepper sauce; stir into browned pecans. Bake pecans 5
to 8 minutes longer, stirring occasionally, until spice mixture
hardens.

3. Cool pecans on sheet on wire rack to room temperature. Stir to
break up nuts, if necessary. Transfer to serving bowl.
