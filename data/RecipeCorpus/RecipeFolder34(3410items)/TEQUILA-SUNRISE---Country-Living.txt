Title: TEQUILA SUNRISE - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 serving
Ice cubes
1 tb (1/2 oz) grenadine
-syrup
1/2 c  Orange juice
1    Jigger (1 1/2 oz or 3 T)
-tequila
1. In tall 8-oz glass, pour in orange juice and tequila. Stir until
mixed. Fill with ice cubes.

2. Pour in grenadine and let settle on bottom. Serve immediately.
