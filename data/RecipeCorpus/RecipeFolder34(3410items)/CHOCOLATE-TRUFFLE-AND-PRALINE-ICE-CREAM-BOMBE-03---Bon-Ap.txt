Title: CHOCOLATE TRUFFLE AND PRALINE ICE CREAM BOMBE 03 - Bon Ap
Categories: Bon appetit, Desserts, Chocolate, Ice cream
Yield: 10 servings
MMMMM------------------------COOKIE CRUST-----------------------------
1 tb Unsalted butter, cut
-into pieces, room temp.
1 lg Egg yolk
1 1/2    6-oz packages Pepperidge
-Farm Cappuccino cookies,
-broken
Finely grind cookies in processor. Add butter and yolk and blend well.
Press crumbs evenly and firmly over ice cream and truffle filling (do
not cover bowl rim). Cover and freeze overnight. (Can be prepared 4
days ahead.)

Bon Appetit/August/89  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
