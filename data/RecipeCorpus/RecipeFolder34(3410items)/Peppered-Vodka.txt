Title: Peppered Vodka
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Vodka
2 tb Black peppercorns
Peppered vodka: Crush the black peppercornms and put in a glass
container with the vodka. Set aside about eight hours or overnight,
strain out the peppercorns and chill in freezer.  Drink straight and
neat from a stemmed glass, or use in a Bloody Mary.
