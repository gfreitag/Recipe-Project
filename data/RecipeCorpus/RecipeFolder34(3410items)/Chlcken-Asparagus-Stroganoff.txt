Title: Chlcken Asparagus Stroganoff
Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chicken -- diced cooked
1/4 ts Rosemary
1/4 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
Cooked rice or noodles
1 cn Cream of chicken soup --
10 3/4 oz Undiluted
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Sour cream
2 c  Asparagus -- cooked sliced
Grease a 1-qt. casserole. Combine soup, milk and sour cream. Pour
half the soup mixture into the casserole; top with asparagus, chicken
and rosemary. Pour remaining soup over chicken. Sprinkle with cheese.
Bake at 350 deg. for 30 minutes. Serve with rice or noodles. Yield: 4
servings
