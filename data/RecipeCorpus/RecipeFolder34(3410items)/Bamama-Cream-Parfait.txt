Title: Bamama Cream Parfait
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
2 md Bananas -- sliced
Whipped cream
4 ea Maraschino cherries --
Optional
1 pk Instant pudding mix -- 3 1/2
oz Banana
2 c  Milk -- cold
1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
Prepare pudding according to package directions, using the 2 cups cold
milk.Sprinkle 1 tablespoon graham cracker crumbs into each of four
dessert glasses. Top crumbs with 1/4 cup prepared pudding and half of
the banana slices. Repeat layers of crumbs, pudding and banana
slices. Top each dessert with a dollop of whipped cream and garnish
with a cherry, if desired. Yield: 4 servings.
