Title: Mozzarella & Sun-Dried Tomato Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sun-dried tomatoes --
-chopped
3/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Mozzarella cheese -- cubed
2 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
2 3/4 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Olive oil
Add all the ingredients except the cheese in the order suggested by
your manual.  Bake on the raisin bread cycle and add the cheese when
the beeper sounds.
