Title: Fried Rice (Minute Rice)
Categories: Asian, Pasta/rice, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Butter
3    Green onion, chopped
3 tb Soy sauce
1 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Minute Rice
1    Egg
Boil water, add rice and cover for 5 min.  Beat egg lightly just until
swirled.  fry in butter until just set.  add onion and rice.  Stir
until light brown.  Add soy sauce and serve. From: Joan Mershon Date:
