Title: Chicken-Flavored Rlce Mix
Categories: Poultry, Country wom, Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Dried minced onion
1 ts Sugar
Or equivalent substitute
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Uncooked long-grain rice
1 tb Instant chicken bouillon
1 tb Parsley flakes
1 ts Celery flakes
Combine all ingredients. Store in small plastic bag or container. For
gift-giving, package in a pretty container and decorate with colorful

ribbons or bows. Be sure to attach preparation instructions. TO
PREPARE: In a saucepan, combine rice mix with 2 cups water and 2
tablespoons butter. Cover and bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer
15 minutes or until rice is tender. Yield: 3 cups
