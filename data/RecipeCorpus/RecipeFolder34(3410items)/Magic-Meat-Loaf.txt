Title: Magic Meat Loaf
Categories: Crockpot, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 c  MiIk
1 c  Cracker crumbs
6    To 8 smalI potatoes, cut up
2 lb Ground chuck
1    Egg
1/2 c  Green pepper, chopped
1    Packet (3/4 to 1 ounce)
Brown gravy mix
Mix alI ingredients, except potatoes,  in  large bowl. Shape into a
loaf. Place in CROCK-POT. Place potatoes at the sides of loaf. (If
potatoes are peeled, wrap in foil to keep from darkening). Cover and
cook on Low 10 hours.

VARIATION: Omit gravy mix and milk-add one 8-ounce can tomato sauce.
Stir half into remaining  ingredients. Shape into loaf and place in
CROCK-POT. Pour remaining sauce over top of loaf and bake as directed
above. From Rival Crock-pot cook book, date unknown
