Title: Pan-Barbecued Steak
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1 cl Garlic; chopped
4 tb Brown sugar
4 tb Prepared mustard
3 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Lemon juice
1 cn (10-3/4 oz) tomato soup
1 ea Round steak, about 2 pounds
1/2 ts Salt (or garlic salt)
1/2 ts Pepper
3 tb Shortening
1 ea Onion; chopped
1 tb Celery seed
Sprinkle steak with salt and pepper. Melt shortening in heavy skillet;
brown steak with onion, celery
seed and garlic. Add remaining ingredients; simmer, covered, for
1-1/2 hours. Check after 45 minutes; add a little water if necessary,
and stir.
