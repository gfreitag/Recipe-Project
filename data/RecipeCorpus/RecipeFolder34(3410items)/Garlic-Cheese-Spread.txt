Title: Garlic Cheese Spread
Categories: Dips/spread, Appetizers, Low cal/fat, Snacks
Yield: 1 cup
3 tb Chopped fresh chives
2 ea Cloves garlic; minced
1 pk (8-oz.) Healthy Choice fat-
-free cream cheese; softened
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl; stirring until well blended.
Cover and chill thoroughly.

Serve spread with unsalted crackers or fresh raw vegetables.  Yield 1
cup.

PER TABLESPOON:  CALORIES 16; FAT 0.2g; CARB 0.7g; PROTEIN 2.3g;
CHOLESTEROL 3mg; SODIUM 97mg.

From:  Healthy Choice CHOICES FOR LIVING HAPPIER AND HEALTHIER, Fall
1995.
