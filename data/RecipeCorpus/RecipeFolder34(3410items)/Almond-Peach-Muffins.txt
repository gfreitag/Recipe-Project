Title: Almond Peach Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/8 ts Almond extract
1 1/4 c  Fresh peaches -- peeled &
Chopped
1/2 c  Almonds -- chopped
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt and baking soda. In
another bowl, beat eggs, oil and extracts; stir into dry ingredients
just until moistened. Fold in peaches and almonds. Fill greased or
paper-lined muffin cups three-fourths full. Bake at 375 deg. for
20-25 min. or until muffins test done. Cool in pan for 10 min. before
removing to a wire rack. Yield: about 12 standard size muffins.
Editor's Note: A 16 oz. can of peaches, drained and chopped can be
substituted for the fresh peaches.
