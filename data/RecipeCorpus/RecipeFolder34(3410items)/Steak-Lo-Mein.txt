Title: Steak Lo Mein
Categories: Beef, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
Partially cooked
1 md Onion -- sliced in ring
1/2 c  Fresh mushrooms -- sliced
1/2 c  Celery -- diagonally sliced
1/3 c  Green onion -- sliced
15 ea Snow peas -- pods trimmed
1 cn Waterchestnuts -- 8 oz
Drained
4 oz Spaghetti -- thin
Cooked and drained
1 lb Round steak -- trimmed
1 ts Beef bouillon -- granules
3/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Cornstarch
2 tb Cooking oil
1    Clove garlic -- minced
2 c  Shredded cabbage
1 c  Carrots -- diagonally
Sliced
Freeze steak just until firm; slice diagonally across grain into 1/4
in. strips. Combine bouillon, water, soy sauce, and cornstarch. Set
aside. In a large skillet, heat oil on med.high. Add meat and garlic;
stir-fry until the meat is no longer pink, about 5 min. Remove meat
to a platter. Add cabbage, carrots, onion, mushrooms, celery and
green onions; stir-fry for about 3 min. Add pea pods and water
chestnuts; stir-fry 2 min. Add meat. Stir bouillon mixture and pour
into skillet; cook and stir until thickened. Gently toss in spaghetti
and heat through for l min. Yield: 6 servings.
