Title: Summer Frozen Fruit
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Blueberries
3 c  Orange juice
3 ea Peaches, peeled and sliced
1 c  Strawberries, halved
Place fruits in baking pan 8 x 8 x 2".    Pour Orange Juice over
fruit. Cover with aluminum foil and freeze.   To Serve, cut into
squares. From: Lancaster Farming Shared by: Pat Stockett
