Title: Beet Puree
Categories: Diabetic, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2/3 c  Unsweetened orange juice
1 1/2 lb Small beets, trimmed
1 ts Grated orange juice;
Place beets in a medium non-aluminum saucepan with water to cover.
Bring to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 25 minutes. Drain
and let cool. Rub off skins.  Position knife blade in food processor
bowl. Add beets and remaining ingredients to processor bowl. Process
2 minutes or until smooth, scraping sides of bowl occasionally.
Return mixture to saucepan, cook over low heat 5 minutes or until
mixture is thoroughly heated, stirring occasionally. Yield: 6
servings (62 Calories pre serving) pro 1.9; fat 0.2; crab 14.4; chol
0; sod 82; pot 420; NOTE. May want to try the pressure cooker to cook
beets.
