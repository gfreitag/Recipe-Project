Title: BRAISED RED CABBAGE - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Holiday, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 7 cups
-cored, and sliced
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Red-wine vinegar
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 md Onion, sliced
1    2 1/2-lb head red cabbage,
-quartered, cored, & cut
-into 1/2-inch-thick slices
1 lg Cooking apple, peeled,
1. In 5-quart Dutch oven or kettle, melt butter over medium heat. Add
onion and saute 5 minutes. Stir in cabbage, apple, water, vinegar,
sugar, salt, and pepper. Cover, increase heat to high, and heat
cabbage mixture to boiling, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to low
and simmer mixture 45 minutes or until cabbage is tender, stirring
occasionally.

2. Spoon cabbage mixture into serving dish. Or when made ahead, cool
cabbage mixture and transfer to a microwave-safe container. Cover and
refrigerate until ready to reheat.
