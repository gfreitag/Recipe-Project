Title: Persimmon Dream Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Persimmon pulp
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding
1    Baked pie shell
1 pk Dream Whip
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
2/3 c  Milk
Prepare Dream Whip with 1/2 cup of milk and vanilla as directed on the
package.  Combine 1 cup of the prepared Dream Whip with the instant
pudding, 2/3 cup of milk, persimmon pulp and cinnamon.  Beat slowly
with mixer just until well mixed, about 1 minute.  Pour into the pie
shell. Chill in refrigerator until set, at least 2 hours.  Top with
remaining Dream Whip. Randy Rigg
