Title: Epangishimog Pakwejigan (Shuswap Bannock)
Categories: Fruit, Native amer, Shuswap, Breads
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Blueberries
3 c  All-purposes flour
1 tb Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Salt
Mix the dry ingredients together, the add the water quickly &
continue to stir.

Spread batter on a pie plate & put in a preheated oven heated to
425F. Bake for 20 minutes.  Cut in pieces & serve hot or cold.
Excellent with mint tea.

**MARKS NOTE: The recipe doesn't say when the blueberries should be
added, I would assume that it would be before mixing in the water.

This recipe comes from the Cappilano Reserve, Chilliwack, BC &
belongs to the Shuswap tribe.

Bernard Assiniwi, "Indian Recipes"
