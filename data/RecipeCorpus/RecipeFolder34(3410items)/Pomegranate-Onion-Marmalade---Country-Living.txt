Title: Pomegranate-Onion Marmalade - Country Living
Categories: Dips/spread, Country liv, Vegetables
Yield: 2 cups
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Red-wine vinegar
2 tb Grenadine syrup
1/4 c  Pomegranate seeds
2 tb Olive oil
2 lb Sweet Spanish onions, sliced
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground white pepper
3/4 c  White wine
1. In large skillet, heat oil. Add onions, salt, and pepper; cook,
covered, 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

2. Stir white wine honey, vinegar, and grenadine syrup into, onions;
cook, uncovered, over low heat until onions are tender and all liquid
evaporates-about 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.

3. Remove onion mixture from heat; stir in pomegranate seeds. Spoon
relish into jars. Seal; store in the refrigerator.
