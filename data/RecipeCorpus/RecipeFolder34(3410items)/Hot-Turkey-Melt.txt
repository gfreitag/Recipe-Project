Title: Hot Turkey Melt
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 2 servings
4    Turkey breast slices
2 sl Tomato
2 sl Havarti cheese
2 tb Mayonnaise
1    English muffins -- split
-& toasted
Spread each muffin half with 1 tbsp mayonaise.  Divide turkey evenly
between each muffin half; top with tomato and cheese.  On broiler pan
or baking sheet, arrange muffins.  Broil 5 inches from heat for 3 to
4 minutes or until cheese melts.
