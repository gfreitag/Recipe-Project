Title: CUCUMBER-FETA CHEESE SALAD - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Salads, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 5 servings
-or basil leaves
3 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
4 ts Lemon juice
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Ground black pepper
1    Cucumber, sliced
6    Red radishes, sliced
1/4 c  Pitted ripe olives,
-quartered
1/2 c  Crumbled feta cheese
1 tb Chopped fresh chives
1. On a serving plate, arrange cucumber slices around rim of plate.
Place radishes and olives in center of plate. Top salad with feta and
sprinkle with chives. Cover and refrigerate until well chilled.

2. Just before serving, in a jar with a tight-fitting lid, place oil,
lemon juice, salt, and pepper; cover and shake until well mixed.
Drizzle oil mixture over the salad. Serve immediately.
