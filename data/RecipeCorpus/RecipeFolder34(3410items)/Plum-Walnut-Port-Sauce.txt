Title: Plum-Walnut Port Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
4    Pitted prunes
-thinly sliced
2 tb Cold butter
4    Sweet red plums
-halved and pitted
1 c  Port wine
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
Cut each plum half into 4 wedges, as you would a lemon. Set aside.

Put the port in a small saucepan, bring to a boil and reduce by half.
Add the plums, walnuts, prunes and butter, mixing together. Remove
from the heat. Serve with seafood.
