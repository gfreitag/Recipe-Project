Title: Deviled Almonds^
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Ground red pepper
2 c  Blanched almonds
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 ts Celery salt
3/4 ts Chili powder
Melt margarine or butter in a medium saucepan. Stir in celery salt,
chili powder and ground red pepper. Add almonds, stirring until nuts
are evenly coated. Transfer nut mixture to a 13x9x2" baking pan.

Bake in a 325 degree oven 20-25 minutes or till nuts are lightly
toasted, stirring once. Cool in pan for 15 minutes. Turn out onto
paper towels to finish cooling. Store tightly covered. Makes 2 cups.
