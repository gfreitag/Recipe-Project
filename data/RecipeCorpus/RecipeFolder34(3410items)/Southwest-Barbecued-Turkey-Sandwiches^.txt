Title: Southwest Barbecued Turkey Sandwiches^
Categories: Poultry, Sandwich, Low cal/fat
Yield: 5 servings
2 tb Sliced green onions
5    Whole grain buns, split and
Toasted if desired
1 1/2 c  Shredded cooked turkey
Breast
1/2 c  Chunky salsa
1/2 c  Barbecue sauce
In medium saucepan, combine all ingredients except buns, mix well.
Cook over low heat for 10 minutes to blend flavors, stirring
occasionally. Serve on buns. 5 sandwiches.

Per Sandwich: 180 cal., 18 g protein, 21 g carb., 4 g fiber, 2 g
(10%) fat, 36 mg chol., 720 mg sodium, 360 mg potassium.
