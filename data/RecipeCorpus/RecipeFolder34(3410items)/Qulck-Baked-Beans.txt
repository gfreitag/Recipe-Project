Title: Qulck Baked Beans
Categories: Beans, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/3 c  Prepared mustard
1 c  Barbecue sauce
1 lb Bacon -- diced
1 lg Onion -- chopped
3 cn Pork and beans -- 28 oz ea
Undrained
In a large saucepan, cook bacon and onion until bacon is crisp and
onion is tender. Drain drippings. Add all remaining ingredients;
simmer 10 minutes. Yield: 10-12 servings
