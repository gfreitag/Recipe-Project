Title: Honey Raisin Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 10 servings
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Honey
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 tb Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Raisin bran -- cereal
Combine flour, baking powder and salt; set aside. In a large bowl,
combine cereal, milk and honey; let stand until softened, about 2
min. Stir in egg and oil; mix well. Add dry ingredients; stir just
until moistened. Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds
full. Bake at 400 deg. for 18 to 20 min. or until muffins test donel.
Cool in pan 10 min. before removing to a wire rack. Yield: about 10
muffins.
