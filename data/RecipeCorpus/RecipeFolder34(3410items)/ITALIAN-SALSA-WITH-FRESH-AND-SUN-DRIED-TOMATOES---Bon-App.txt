Title: ITALIAN SALSA WITH FRESH AND SUN-DRIED TOMATOES - Bon App
Categories: Bon appetit, Salsa/sauce, Vegetables
Yield: 3 1/2 cups
1 tb Balsamic  vinegar* OR
2 tb Red wine vinegar and pinch
-of sugar
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1/2 tb Salt
2 lg Tomatoes, halved, seeded
-and cut into 1/2-inch dice
10    Kalamata olives, * pitted
Fresh basil leaves
1 lg Red bell pepper
12 lg Basil leaves
1 lg Garlic clove
1    Jalapeno chili, halved and
-seeded
4    Oil-packed sun-dried
-tomatoes
1/4 lg Red onion
1/4 c  Olive oil
Preheat broiler. Cut bell pepper lengthwise into 4 pieces, discarding
core and seeds. Arrange in single layer on foil-lined baking sheet,
skin side up. Broil 6 inches from heat source until skin is
blackened. Remove from oven and wrap tightly in foil. Let stand at
least 10 minutes. Remove skin; cut pepper into 1/2-inch dice.

Steel Knife: Place 12 basil leaves in dry work bowl. With machine
running, drop garlic and chili through feed tube and process until
minced. Add sundried tomatoes and onion and coarsely chop using
several on/off turns. Add olive oil, both vinegars and salt and
process until combined, about 5 seconds. Transfer contents of work
bowl to large mixing bowl. Add bell pepper, tomatoes and olives and
toss gently. (Can be prepared 6 hours ahead. Cover and refrigerate.)
Garnish with basil.

*Available at specialty foods stores, Italian markets and some
supermarkets.
