Title: MOZZARELLA TOMATO AND BASIL SANDWICH - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Sandwich, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 lg Ripe yellow tomato, sliced
1    8-oz ball fresh mozzarella
-cheese, sliced
Fresh basil leaves
Pesto Spread (recipe
-follows)
1    15-inch loaf Italian bread
1 lg Ripe red tomato, sliced
1. Prepare Pesto Spread.

2. With serrated knife, from a long edge, cut bread horizontally in
half to within a half inch of opposite side. Open loaf; spread cut
surfaces of bread with Pesto Spread. On bottom half of bread, arrange
overlapping slices of red tomato, yellow tomato, and mozzarella; tuck
basil leaves between slices. Replace top of bread. To serve, cut
sandwich crosswise into 4 pieces.

Pesto Spread: In food processor fitted with chopping blade, process 2
C fresh basil leaves, 1/2 C pine nuts, 1/4 C grated Parmesan cheese, 1
teaspoon lemon juice, 1 clove garlic, 1/8 teaspoon salt, and 1/8 t
ground black pepper until smooth. With motor running, slowly add 1/4
C olive oil.
