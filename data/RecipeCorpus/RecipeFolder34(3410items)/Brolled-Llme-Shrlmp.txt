Categories: Fish, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
36 ea Shrimp
Extra-large uncooked, peeled
And deveined
Shredded lettuce or sliced
Cucumbers, optional
2 tb Sweet red pepper -- minced
Optional
Chili
6 tb Lime juice
2 ea Green onions -- thinly
Sliced
1    Serrano
Stemmed, seeded and minced,
Optional
2 ts Olive oil
1 ts Garlic -- minced
1/4 ts Salt
Combine all marinade ingredients. Thread shrimp on metal or wooden
skewers that have been soaked in water. Place in a large glass baking
dish and pour marinade over shrimp. Cover and refrigerate several
hours. Preheat broiler or grill. Cook shrimp, turning once, until
pink, about 2-3 minutes per side. Brush frequently with the marinade.
Do not overcook. Serve shrimp hot or at room temperature over a bed
of shredded lettuce or sliced cucumbers. Sprinkle with red pepper, if
desired. Yield: 6 servings
