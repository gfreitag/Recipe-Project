Title: Gingerbread Boys
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 2 dozen
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 ts Ginger, ground
1/2 ts Cinnamon, ground
Chocolate, semisweet morsels
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Molasses, light
1/2 tb Vinegar
1    Egg; beaten
3 c  Flour, all-purpose
Bring shortening,, sugar, molasses, and vinegar to a boil; cool.  Add
beaten eggs.  Combine flour, salt, soda, ginger, and cinnamon; add to
liquid mixture.  Mix well and chill.  Roll to 1/8" thickness on
lightly floured board.  Follow a pattern or use a cookie cutter ot
shape gingerbread boys.  Use chocolate morsels for eyes, nose, mouth,
and buttons.  Place on greased cookie sheet and bake at 375 degrees
for 12 to 15 minutes.
