Title: Hearty Chlcken Broccoll Soup
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Milk
2 ts Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  Chicken -- diced cooked
2 pk Cut broccoli in cheese
Sauce
10 oz Ea. thawed/chopped
Seasoned croutons, optional
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
3 tb All-purpose flour
1 tb Dry mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Saute onion and
celery until crisp-tender. Stir in flour, mustard, salt and pepper.
Cook until mixture is smooth and bubbly. Gradually add milk; cook and
stir until mixture boils and thickens. Stir in the lemon juice,
chicken and broccoli. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until heated
through. Top with croutons, if desired. Yield: 6-8 servings
