Title: SZECHUAN BEEF OR PORK
Categories: Chinese, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Beef(round or chuck or pork
-- loin) cut into fine
-- strips; 1/8 inch by 2 to
-- 3 inches long
2 tb Dry sherry
2 tb Hoisin sauce
1 tb Black bean sauce
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Sugar
1/4    To 1/2 ts chili paste
2 tb Peanut oil
1    Or 2 green peppers; cut in
1/8    Inch strips
1    Or 2 carrots; finely shred
-- 1/8 inch match-stick
-- size strips (sliced
-- stack slices; slice
-- through stack)
1    Scallion; quartered length-
-- then into 3 inch long
-- strips
Heat oil in wok and stir-fry the green peppers, carrots, and scallion
for 1-2 minutes. Push aside. Stir-fry the slivers of meat for 1 to 2
minutes and recombine with the vegetables. Add the remaining
ingredients. Stir and heat throughly. Serve at once with boiled rice.
Serves 4 I usually double the sauce ingredients because it doesn't
seem to be quite enough.
