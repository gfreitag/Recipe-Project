Title: Goldie Lox
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Bon appetit
Yield: 6 servings
-cut into 1/2" cubes
6 oz Nova Scotia smoked salmon
-or lox, thinly sliced
-cut into 1/2" wide strips
Chopped chives (opt)
12 lg Eggs
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
3 tb Butter
8 oz Pkg cream cheese
-well chilled
Whisk eggs, salt and pepper in large bowl to blend. Melt butter in
large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Add eggs. Using wooden
spoon, stir until eggs are almost set, about 5 minutes. Gently fold
in cheese and salmon and stir until eggs are set, about 1 minute.
Transfer eggs to platter. Sprinkle with chives, if desired, and serve.

Bon Apptit September 1995
