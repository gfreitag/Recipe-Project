Title: INDIAN FRY BREAD
Categories: Breads, Native amer, Oneida
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
-Powder
Shortening
8 c  Plain Flour
2 ts Salt
8    Heaping Teaspoons Baking
Sift flour and baking powder together, add just enough water to make a
dough. Knead about 3 minuets. Pinch off enough dough to make a round
patty, flatten with hand and punch a hole in center. Drop in skillet
of hot shortening and fry until light brown, turning once. Remove,
drain on paper towels. (Can be served with honey or jelly).
