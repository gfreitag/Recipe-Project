Title: Pineapple Ham Balls
Categories: Pork, Ham, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Sliced pineapple -- 20
Ounces
3/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 ts Dry mustard
3 c  Ham
Ground fully cooked
1/2 lb Ground pork
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
2 ea Eggs -- beaten
1/4 ts Pepper
In a bowl, combine ham, pork, bread crumbs, eggs and pepper and mix
well. Chill. Form into 10 balls; set aside. Drain pineapple,
reserving 1/2 cup of juice. Place juice in a saucepan with sugar,
vinegar and dry mustard; cook and stir over low heat until sugar is
dissolved. Arrange pineapple slices in the bottom of an ungreased
13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Place a ham ball on each
pineapple slice. Pour sugar mixture over all. Cover and bake at 350
deg. for 1 hour or until ham balls are thoroughly cooked. Yield: 4-5
servings
