Title: Quick Italian Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Red onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Mozzarella cheese -- cubed
1/4 c  Ripe olives -- sliced
Bottled ltalian dressing
1    Head romaine lettuce --
Torn
1 md Tomato -- cut in wedges
1/2 md Cucumber -- sliced
Toss the first six ingredients in a large salad bowl. Pour dressing
over all and toss lightly to coat. Serve immediately. Yield: 4
servings
