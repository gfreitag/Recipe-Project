Title: Star Of The North Pasties
Categories: Ground, Beef, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Cubed -- raw rutabagas
1/2 c  Cubed raw carrots
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/2 lb Hamburger
Or diced sirloin steak,
Browned
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Shortening or lard
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Cubed raw potatoes
Mix together first three ingredients; cut in shortening to form
particles the size of large peas. Gradually add milk and mix just
enough to make mixture stick together. Lightly shape dough into four
balls; let stand 5 minutes. Meanwhile, combine vegetables and meat.
On a lightly floured board, roll each ball into a circle. Place 1/4
of the vegetable/meat mixture on one side of each circle; brush edge
of crust with water and fold half the dough over filling. Press edges
together to seal. Place on an ungreased baking sheet and prick tops
with a fork. Bake for 1 hour at 350 deg. Yield: 4 servings
