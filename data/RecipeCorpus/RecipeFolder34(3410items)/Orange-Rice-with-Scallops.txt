Title: Orange Rice with Scallops
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Pasta/rice, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
Add rice and tomatoes.
2 ea Cover and simmer 5 minutes.
-Stir in mushrooms (if
-fresh).
3 ea Cover and simmer 10 more
-minutes. Stir in scallops
-(and
Mushrooms, if canned).
4 ea Cover and simmer on low,
-stirring occasionally until
Rice is cooked and all
-liquid is absorbed.
fl With fork before serving.
Fat free & ever so yummy!
1 1/2 c  Orange juice (fresh
-squeezed is best)
1 1/4 c  Water
1 1/4 c  Uncle Ben's special Blend
-Rice Trio
1 3/16 oz Julienned sun-dried
-tomatoes (NOT packed in
-oil!)
1 1/4 lb Sliced mushrooms (fresh is
-best, canned is ok)
1/2 lb Fresh calico scallops,
-rinsed & drained
1 ea Mix orange juice and water,
-bring to boil in stir-fry
-pan or saucepan.
