Title: Persimmon Supreme
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
Pan, bake 20 to 25 minutes
At 350 degrees.  Let cool.
Prepare 1 box of Dream Whip
(2 pkgs.)
1 c  Sugar
16 oz Philadelphia cream cheese
6    Egg whites beaten, stiff
2 c  Sugar folded in
1 c  Chopped pecans
Add 2 cups Ritz cracker
Crumbs
Mix and spread on greased
Whip cheese and sugar together and add to prepared Dream Whip. Spread
on top of crust, then add persimmon topping of: 2 cups persimmon pulp
1/4 cup sugar 2 cups miniature marshmallows

Sprinkle with additional chopped nuts.  Chill in refrigerator for 3
hours. Cut in squares to serve. Randy Rigg
