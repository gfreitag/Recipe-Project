Title: Oven-Roasted Vegetables with Garlic
Categories: Vegetables, Bon appetit
Yield: 6 servings
-cut into 8 wedges
1 lg Head garlic, peeled
-separated into cloves
3 tb Rosemary, chopped or
1 tb Dried rosemary
3 tb Thyme, chopped or
1 tb Dried thyme
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Butter (1/4 stick), melted
6    Parsnips, peeled
-halved crosswise
-then lengthwise
6    Carrots
-halved crosswise
-then lengthwise
6    Shallots
-peeled and halved
2 md Red onions, peeled
Preheat oven to 400F. Mix first 7 ingredients in large roasting pan.
Drizzle with oil and butter and toss to coat. Roast vegetables until
golden and tender, stirring occasionally, about 1 hour 20 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to platter and serve.
