Title: Louisiana Barbecue Mix
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 16 servings
6 c  Crispix cereal
2 c  Cornuts
3 tb Oil
2 tb Dry barbecue sauce mix
1 ts Brown sugar
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
Combine dry barbecue sauce mix, sugar and cayenne pepper.  Set aside.
In a 2 gallon storage bag, combine cereal and cornuts.  Pour oil over
cereal mixture.  Close bag and gently toss cereal mixture until well
coated.  Add barbecue mixture and close bag.  Gently toss cereal
mixture until well coated.  Store in airtight container.  Per 1/2 cup
serving: 100 calories and 3 grams fat.
