Title: Apple Butter Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Country wom
Yield: 12 servings
1/8 ts Ground allspice
1/8 ts Ground ginger
1 ea Egg -- lightly beaten
3/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Apple butter -- thick
1 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/3 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
3 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
In a medium bowl combine the first eight ingredients. Combine egg,
milk and oil; stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Fill
greased or paper-lined muffin cups with a rounded tablespoon of
batter. Top each with a rounded teaspoon of apple butter and
remaining batter. Combine topping ingredients; sprinkle over muffins.
Bake at 400 deg. for 15 to 18 minutes. Cool in pan 10 min. before
removing. Yield: about 12 standard-size muffins. Editor's Note: This
recipe was tested with commercially prepared apple butter.
