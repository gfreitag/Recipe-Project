Title: Spiced Pineapple
Categories: Desserts, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground coriander or nutmeg
3 c  Pineapple chunks; fresh or
-canned
2 tb Sugar
Mix all ingredients in a serving bowl. Serves 4. Per serving: 82
cal; 0g pro; 21g car, 1g fat, 0mg chol; 1mg sod. Total time: 5
minutes. Cost per serving:  51 cents. Source: Woman's Day 9/19/95.
