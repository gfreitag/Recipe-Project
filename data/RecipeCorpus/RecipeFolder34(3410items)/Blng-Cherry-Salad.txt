Title: Blng Cherry Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Drained
1 ct Frozen whipped topping -- 8
oz Thawed
2 c  Miniature marshmallows
1 cn Dark sweet cherries
16 oz Pitted, juice reserved
1 pk Cream cheese -- 8 oz.
Softened
1 c  Crushed pineapple --
Drain cherries and reserve 1/4 cup juice. In a mixing bowl, whip
juice with cream cheese. Stir in cherries, pineapple, topping and
marshmallows. Chill until ready to serve. Yield: 6-8 servings
