Title: All-Purpose Asian Salad Dressing
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Ginger, grated
1 tb Cilantro, chopped
A few drops
-of hot chile oil
2 tb White vinegar
1 1/2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Plus 1 ts sugar
1 1/2 ts Asian sesame oil
This all-purpose dressing is ideal for leafy greens, steamed
vegetables, and cold meats.

In a small bowl, whisk together the vinegar, soy sauce, sugar, sesame
oil, and ginger. Just before serving, stir in the cilantro and chile
oil.
