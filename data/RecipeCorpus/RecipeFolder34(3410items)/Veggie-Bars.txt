Title: Veggie Bars
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
(dry)
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 c  Carrots, shredded
1 c  Broccoli, chopped
1 c  Cauliflower, chopped
8    Crescent rolls
2    8 oz. pkg. cream cheese,
Softened
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 pk Hidden Valley Ranch dressing
Spread rolls in the bottom and up sides of greased jellyroll pan or
cookie sheet with sides.  Smooth seams well.  Bake in a 350 degree
oven for 10 minutes or until golden brown.  Mix cream cheese with
mayonnaise and salad dressing.  Add Cheddar cheese and mix well.
Spread mixture on cooled crust. Sprinkle on vegetables.  Press
lightly into cream cheese. Cover and refrigerate until served.  Cut
into bars. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   08-18-95
