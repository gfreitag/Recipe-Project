Title: Walking Tostada
Categories: Tomatoes, Dips/spread, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1    Jar Mild taco sauce
4 oz To 8 oz Hot picante sauce
1 c  Coarsely grated Colby cheese
1 c  Coarsely grated Monterey
-Jack cheese
1 cn Refried beans (31 oz.)
2    Ripe avocados, chopped
2    To 3 Ripe tomatoes, chopped
2 cn Pitted ripe olives, drained
-and sliced
5    Scallions, chopped
Spread the beans evenly on a large round platter. Layer with the
remaining items in the order given. Serve with tostadas or nacho
chips.

SOURCE:* Yankee Magazine, January 1944 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 1/94
