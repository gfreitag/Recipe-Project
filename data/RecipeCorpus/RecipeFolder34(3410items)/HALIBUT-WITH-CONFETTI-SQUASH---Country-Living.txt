Title: HALIBUT WITH CONFETTI SQUASH - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Main dish, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
-red pepper or pimiento
1/4 ts Dried tarragon leaves,
-crushed
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
2    Lemon or lime wedges
1    8-oz skinless halibut fillet
1 sm (4 to 5 oz) yellow squash,
-cut into 1/2-inch pieces
1 sm (4 to 5 oz) zucchini, cut
-into 1/2-inch pieces
3 tb Coarsely chopped roasted
1. Cut halibut in half. In small bowl, combine remaining ingredients
except lemon wedges

2. Place one piece of halibut on the side of large microwave-safe
dinner plate. Place half of vegetable mixture next to halibut. Repeat
with remaining fish and vegetables on another plate.

3. Cover plates with plastic wrap. Microwave each plate on high (100
percent) 3 minutes and 30 seconds. Puncture plastic wrap or uncover
immediately to prevent wrap from shrinking onto food. Serve fish with
lemon or lime wedges.
