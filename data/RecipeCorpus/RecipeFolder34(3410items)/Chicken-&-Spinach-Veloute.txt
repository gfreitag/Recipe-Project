Title: Chicken & Spinach Veloute
Categories: Poultry, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
-ground
6 tb Instant Cream Of Wheat
6 oz Spinach, Washed -- stems
-removed
1/3 c  Heavy Cream
4 c  Chicken Stock, Unsalted --
-prefer homemade
1 c  Water
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black Pepper -- freshly
1. Bring the stock, water, salt and pepper to a boil in a stainless
steel saucepan. Add the Cream of Wheat, (you can use cornmeal,
tapioca or semolina), mix well, bring back to the boil, and cook
gently for 3 mins.

2. Cut the washed spinach very coarsely and add it to the pan. Bring
back to the boil and boil for 2 mins. Stir in the cream and serve
immediately. Makes 4 servings.
