Title: Savory Oven Fried Chicken^
Categories: Poultry, Healthy
Yield: 8 servings
Breast halves
1/3 c  Dijon mustard
Fast & Healthy 8/92
Carolyn Shaw 8-95
1 1/4 c  Seasoned croutons, crushed
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
2 tb Chopped fresh parsley
8    Boneless, skinless chicken
Line a 15x10x1" baking pan with foil and spray with Pam. Combine
croutons, cheese and parsley. Brush with chicken with mustard and
roll in crouton mixture. Place chicken on pan. Bake at 450F. 15-20
minutes or until chicken is tender and juices run clear.

Per serving: 200 cal., 29 g protein, 4 g carb., 0 g fiber, 5 g fat,
76 mg cholesterol, 480 mg sodium, 240 mg potassium, 1/2 starch and 3
lean meat exchanges.
