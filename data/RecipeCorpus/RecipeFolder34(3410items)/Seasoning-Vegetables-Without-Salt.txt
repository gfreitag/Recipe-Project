Try these herbs alone or in combination with vegetables as listed
below:

þ Beets: lemon juice, vinegar, dill, cloves, allspice, ginger, savory,
thyme, pepper

þ Broccoli: lemon juice, pepper, vinegar, caraway seed, dry mustard,
nutmeg,  basil, curry, oregano, garlic

þ Cabbage and Cauliflower: lemon juice, vinegar, caraway, dill seed,
cumin, allspice, celery seed, mace, mint, dry mustard, savory,
tarragon, oregano, parsley, rosemary, pepper

þ Carrots: parsley, cinnamon, lemon juice, allspice, nutmeg, mint, bay
leaf, carawya seed, dill seed, ginger, mace, thyme, marjoram, mint
jelly, honey, pepper

þ Corn: green pepper onion, paprika, pepper, curry, pimento, fresh
tomato, lima beans for succotash

þ Cucumbers: chives, dill, garlic, vinegar

þ Greens: lemon juice, vinegar, onion, allspice, pepper

þ Lima Beans: sage, lemon juice. chives, pepper, onion

þ Okra: lemon juice, pepper, vinegar, onion

þ Onion: caraway seed, nutmeg, oregano, sage, thyme, pepper, basil,
marjoram

þ Peas, green: onion, mint, sage, mint jelly, rosemary, parsley,
savory, green pepper, chives, cedery seed, oregano, poppy seed,
lettuce leaf, pepper, garlic

þ Potatoes: onion, basil, mace, parsley, paprika, bay leaf, green
pepper, chives, celery seed, oregano, poppy seed, rosemary, thyme,
pepper, garlic, mint, nutmeg

þ String Beans: marjoram, lemon juice, nutmeg, savory, dill seed,
thyme, vinegar, dry mustard, oregano, onion, honey, carawat seed,
sage, garlic, pepper, mint jelly, pimento

þ Spinach: lemon juice, vinegar, onion, allspice, basil, mace,
oregano, pepper

þ Squash: ginger, mace, allspice, onion, basil, cinnamon, cloves,
nutmeg, fennel, rosemary, honey, pepper

þ Tomatoes: basil, marjoram, thyme, onion, lemon juice, vinegar,
oregano, green pepper, caraway seed, sage, sesame seed

The Vegetable Lovers Guide to New Orleans Restaurants by Gay Sperling
