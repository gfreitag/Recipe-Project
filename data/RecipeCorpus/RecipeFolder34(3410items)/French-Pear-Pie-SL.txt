Title: French Pear Pie SL
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 9 inch pie
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
1 ea Unbaked 9-inch pastry shell
1/3 c  All-purpose flour
1/3 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
2 tb Butter or margarine
6 c  Peeled and sliced pears
2 tb Lemon juice
1/3 c  Sugar
1/3 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Ground ginger
Combine pears and lemon juice; toss gently.  Combine sugar, 1/3 cup
flour, and ginger; add sugar mixture and corn syrup to pears.  Spoon
mixture into pastry shell.
Combine 1/3 cup flour and brown sugar in a small bowl; blend well.
Add butter; mix until crumbly.  Sprinkle on pear mixture.  Bake at
450 degrees F for 15 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350 degrees F, and bake
30 minutes longer.  Yield: One 9-inch pie.

From Ethel C. Jernegan of Georgia in October, 1987 "Southern Living"
