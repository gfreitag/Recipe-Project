Title: Buttermilk Salad Dressing
Categories: Salad, Dressings, Country wom
Yield: 20 servings
1 ts Dried parsley -- flakes
1/2 ts Dried onions -- minced
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1    Clove garlic clove --
Minced
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Buttermilk
Combine all ingredients in a jar with a tight-fitting lid; shake until
smooth. Chill for 30 min. Yield: 1 1/4 cup.
