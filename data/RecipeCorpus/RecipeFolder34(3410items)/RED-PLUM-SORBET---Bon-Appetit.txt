Title: RED PLUM SORBET - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Ice cream, Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 3 cups
1/4    To 1/2 C water
1    To 3 T (about) fresh
-lime juice
2 lb Ripe sweet red plums,
-unpeeled, pitted and
-chopped
1/4    To 1/2 C sugar
The amounts of sugar, water and lime juice used will vary according
to the sweetness and juiciness of the plums. Make sure to taste a
piece of fruit before you begin to prepare the recipe.

Cook plums, sugar to taste and 1/4 C water in heavy medium saucepan
over low heat until medium-thin puree forms, stirring occasionally,
about 12 minutes. (If plums do not form puree, add up to 1/4 C
water.) Cool.

Puree mixture in processor or blender, stopping to scrape down sides,
3 minutes. Blend in lime juice to taste.

Transfer plum mixture to ice cream maker and process according to
manu- facturer's instructions, or, without an ice cream maker, freeze
following directions in Very Berry Ice Milk recipe.

Transfer sorbet to plastic container. Cover and freeze several hours
or overnight to mellow flavors. If frozen solid, let soften slightly
at room temperature.
