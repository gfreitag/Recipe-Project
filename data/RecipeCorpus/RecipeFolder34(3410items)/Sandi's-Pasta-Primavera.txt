Title: Sandi's Pasta Primavera
Categories: Main dish, Pasta/rice, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
4 c  Broccoli Flowerets
2 lg Carrot; coarsely shredded
340 1/4 g  Spaghetti; uncooked
20 oz Canned Cream of Chicken &
Broccoli Soup (Campbell's)
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Parmesan Cheese; grated
1/4 ts Garlic Powder
Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
:
In 4 qt. saucepan, combine soup, milk, cheese, garlic powder and
pepper; add broccoli and carrot.  Heat to boiling, stirring
occasionally. Cover; cook over low heat 5 min. or until vegetables
are tender, stirring occasionally.
Add spaghetti.  Heat through, stirring occasionally to keep from
sticking. Posted by: Sandi Cutright
