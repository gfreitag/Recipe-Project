Title: Ginger Glazed Beets
Categories: Vegetables, Herbs/spice, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
4 ea 16 oz cans or jars whole
-beets, drained
3 lg Oranges
1/4 c  Packed light brown sugar
2 tb Chopped crystallized ginger
From 2 oranges, grate enough rind to equal 1 tablespoon and equeze
enough juice to equal 1 cup.  In 2-qt. saucepan, mix orange juice,
brown sugar, ginger and salt; over high heat, heat to boiling.
Reduce heat to medium; cook, stiring occasionally, until mixture
thickens, about 10 minutes. Gently stir in beets; heat through.  Cut
remaining orange into thin slices; cut each slice in half.  Spoon
beets onto platter. Arrange orange slices aroud beets and sprinkle
beets with orange peel. Makes 6 accompaniment servings. Shared by:
Marie Culver
