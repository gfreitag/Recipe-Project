Title: Parmesan Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Dijon mustard
Regular or country-style
6 ea Chicken breast halves
Boneless skinless
1 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
2 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/3 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
Combine cheese, bread crumbs and butter. Coat chicken breasts with
mustard, then dip into crumb mixture. Place breaded chicken in a
9-in. x 13-in. baking pan. Bake at 425 deg. for 15 minutes or until
chicken is done. Yield: 6 servings
