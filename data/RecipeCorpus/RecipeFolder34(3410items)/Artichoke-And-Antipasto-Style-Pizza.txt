Title: Artichoke And Antipasto-Style Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 9 servings
Drain -- quarter
1/3 c  Roasted red peppers --
-slice, strips
1 c  Mozzarella -- shredded
1 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
1/2 c  Mayo
1/4 c  Parmesan -- grated
1/2 ts Pepper
1    12" pizza crust
15 oz Can artichoke hearts;
Preheat oven to 450~. Combine mayo, cheese and pepper; spread over
crust. Top with artichokes, red peppers and cheese. Bake 10 minutes.
Sprinkle with parsley. Per Slice 320 Cal, 4 Gr Fat.
