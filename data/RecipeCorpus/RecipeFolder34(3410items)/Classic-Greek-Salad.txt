Title: Classic Greek Salad
Categories: Salads, Greek, Tomatoes, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Salad oil
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Oregano
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ea Head lettuce, torn into
-bite sized pieces
1 ea Romaine lettuce
2 ea Tomatoes, cut bite size
1 ea Cucumber, cut bite size
1/4 c  Sliced green onions
1/4 c  Sliced ripe olives
3/4 c  Feta cheese, cubed
Layer lettuce in large salad bowl or on large platter.  Spread with
tomatoes, cucumber, onions, olives and cheese.  Beat dressing
ingredients together well. Shake before serving.  Serve with salad.
NOTE** A few sardines or anchovies may be arranged over top of salad.
Feta cheese may be exchanged with 8 oz cream cheese, cubed.

Origin: Jean Pare's Salads Shared by: Sharon Stevens
