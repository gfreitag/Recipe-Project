Title: ROYAL ICING - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
3    Egg whites
1 lb Confectioners'sugar
1/2 ts Cream of tartar
In the bowl of an electric mixer mix sugar, cream of tartar and egg
whites until combined. Set speed on high and mix at least 7 minutes
or until smooth, stiff and glossy. Store in an airtight container
until ready to use. Makes 2 1/2 to 3 cups. Do not double recipe.
