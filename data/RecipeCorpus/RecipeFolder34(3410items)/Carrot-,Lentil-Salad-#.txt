Title: Carrot /Lentil Salad #
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
Chopped
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Lentils -- dry
1 c  Carrots -- diced
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1 ea Bay leaf
Dressing
1/2 c  Celery -- finely chopped
1/4 c  Fresh parsley -- finely
In a Dutch oven, combine lentils, carrots, garlic and bay leaf. cover
with 1 inch of water. Bring to a boil, then simmer 15 to 20 min. or
until lentils are tender. Remove bay leaf; drain and cool. Meanwhile,
combine all ingredients for dressing. Pour over lentil mixture. cover
and refrigerate several hours. Yield: 6 servings.
