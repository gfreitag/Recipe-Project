Title: Blueberry And Peach Cobbler
Categories: Desserts, Country wom, Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
TOPPING:
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Water
1 tb Lemon juice
2 c  Fresh peaches -- peeled
Sliced
1 c  Blueberries
In a saucepan, combine first five ingredients. Bring to a boil,
stirring until thick. Add fruit and pour into a 2-qt. baking dish.
For topping, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a mixing
bowl. Sfir in milk and butter. Spread over fruit mixture and bake at
375 deg. for 50 minutes or until topping is golden brown and tests
done. Serve warm. Yield: about 6 servings
