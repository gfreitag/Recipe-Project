Title: NUT AND CHEESE LOAF
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 5 servings
3/4 c  Boiling water
1 ea Egg, beaten
Salt
Pepper
1 tb Onion, chopped
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 c  Cheese, grated
1 c  Walnuts, chopped
1 c  Breadcrumbs, dry
Cook onion in butter for few minutes.  mix cheese, nuts, Breadcrumbs,
and water.  Add melted butter, onion, egg, salt, and pepper.
Bake in a small pan in 350 F. oven about 30 minutes.  Serve with
tomato sauce.
