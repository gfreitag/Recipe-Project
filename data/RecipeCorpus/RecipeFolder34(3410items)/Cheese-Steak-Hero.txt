Title: Cheese Steak Hero
Categories: Main dish, Sandwich, Meats, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
7 oz Jar of roasted red peppers,
Drained, sliced
1 sm Red onion, sliced thin
8    Velveeta slices, cut in
Half diagonally
1    Italian bread, cut in half
Lengthwise
8 oz Philadelphia soft cream
Cheese with herb & garlic
1 1/2 lb Roast beef, slice thin
Preheat broiler.  Spread bread with cream cheese.  Cover bottom half
of bread with peppers, roast beef, cheese, and onions.  Broil 5
minutes, or until process cheese begins to melt.  Cover with top half
of bread. Randy Rigg
