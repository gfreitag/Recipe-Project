Title: Italian Pita Crisps
Categories: Breads, Low cal/fat
Yield: 32 crisps
2 tb Reduced-calorie margarine;
-melted
2 ea (6-in.) Whole-wheat pita
-bread rounds; split
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1 ts Dried Italian seasoning
1/2 ts Paprika
1/8 ts Salt
pita half; sprinkle evenly with cheese mixture.

Cut each pita half into 8 wedges, and place on a baking sheet.  Bake
at 350 degrees F. for 8 minutes or until crisp; let cool.  Store in
an airtight container.  Yield:  32 crisps (serving size: 1 crisp).

CALORIES 12 (45% from fat); PROTEIN 0.4g; FAT 0.6g (sat 0.1g, mono
0.2g, poly 0.2g); CARB 1.3g; FIBER 0.1g; CHOL 0mg; IRON 0.1mg; SODIUM
24mg; CALC 6mg.

Source:  COOKING LIGHT magazine, 9/95.
