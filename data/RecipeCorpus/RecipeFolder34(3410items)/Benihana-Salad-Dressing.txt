Title: Benihana Salad Dressing
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Soy sauce
1 1/2 ts Tomato paste
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ds Salt and pepper
1/4 c  Onion ; chopped
1/4 c  Peanut oil
2 ts Rice wine vinegar
2 ts Water
1 tb Ginger root ; chopped
1 tb Celery ; chopped
Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor and process
until smooth.
