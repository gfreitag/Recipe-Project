Title: Sausage Bean Stew
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1 lb Sausage links -- smoked cut
1/2    In
1 lg Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Pearled barley
1/2 c  Dried lentils
1 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 c  Fresh spinach -- chopped
4 c  Chicken broth
3 cn Beans -- 16 oz each
(kidney, butter, pinto or
Black eye peas -- rinsed and
Drained
3 md Carrot -- diced
3 c  Celery ribs -- diced
In a 4 qt. dutch oven, place all ingredients except spinach. Cover
and cook over med. heat for 45 to 60 min. or until the barley and
lentils are tender. Stir in spinach and heat through. Yield: 6 to 8
servings.
