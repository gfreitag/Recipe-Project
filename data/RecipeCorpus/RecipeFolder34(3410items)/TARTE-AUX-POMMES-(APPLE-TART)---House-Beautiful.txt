Title: TARTE AUX POMMES (APPLE TART) - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Pie/tart, Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2 lg Granny Smith apples, peeled,
-cored and cut into thin
-slices
Kanten coulis, seperate
-recipe
1 c  Flour
Pinch of salt
4 tb Cold butter
2 tb Cold solid shortening
2 ts Honey
About 2 T ice water
In a food processor fitted with a steel blade, combine flour, salt,
butter, shortening and honey. Process until mixture resembles corn-
meal. With motor running, slowly add water and process until mixture
forms a ball. Remove dough, wrap in plastic wrap and chill at least 1
hour.

Roll out chilled dough on a floured surface and line a 8-inch tart
ring. Chill for 30 minutes. Prick bottom of dough with a fork, cover
bottom with parchment paper and fill with dried beans. Bake in a
preheated 375' oven for 10 minutes. Remove weighted paper and bake 5
minutes longer. Cool. Leave oven on.

Place sliced apples on pastry in an overlapping circular design,
brush top with Kanten coulis and bake for 15 minutes or until apples
begin to color. Cool. Unmold and serve at room temperature. Serves 4
to 6.
