Title: Very-Berry Cobbler
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Pie/tart
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 c  Flour
2 tb Vanilla
1 ea Recipe biscuits from a mix
1 tb Cinnamon-sugar
3 pt Strawberries; washed, hulled
1 pt Raspberries
1 pt Blackberries
1 pt Blueberries
1 2/3 c  Sugar; divided
Reserve 1 pint of strawberries.  Put the rest and the other berries
in a heavy pot and sprinkle with two-thirds cup of sugar and the
flour. In a food processor puree the reserved strawberries with 1 cup
of sugar and the vanilla.  Pour the puree over the berry mixture and
bring to a boil, stirring.  Pour the fruit mixture into a 9- x 13- x
2-inch baking dish. Drop the prepared biscuit dough by spoonfuls on
top of the hot fruit until it is almost covered. Sprinkle the
cinnamon-sugar over the dough. Bake in a preheated 425-degree oven
for 35 to 40 minutes until the top is lightly browned.  Cool slightly
and serve.

Makes 12 servings.

Each serving has:  140 calories; 4g fat; 0 cholesterol.
