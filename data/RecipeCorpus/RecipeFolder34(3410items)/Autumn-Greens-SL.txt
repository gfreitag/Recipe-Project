Title: Autumn Greens SL
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Pepper
4 1/2 lb Turnip greens
1 lb Salt pork; washed and diced
1 1/2 c  Water
Cut off and discard tough stems and discolored leaves from greens.
Wash greens thoroughly and drain.
Cook salt pork in a large Dutch oven over medium heat, stirring
frequently, until crisp and brown.
Add greens, water, onion, sugara, and pepper; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 40 to 45 minutes or until greens are
tender.
