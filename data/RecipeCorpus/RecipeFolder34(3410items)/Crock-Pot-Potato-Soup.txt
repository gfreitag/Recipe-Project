Title: Crock Pot Potato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Crockpot, Potatoes
Yield: 3 servings
ds Pepper
4 tb Margarine
1 qt Milk
6    Potatoes
Onion, to taste
Celery, any amount you like
2 ts Salt
Chop potatoes, onion and celery.  Put all ingredients in crock pot.
Cover and cook on low for 5 to 6 hours. Randy Rigg
