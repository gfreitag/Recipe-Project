Title: Spicy Red Beans And Rice
Categories: Beans, Country wom, Pasta/rice
Yield: 8 servings
1 lg Smoked ham hocks
2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
1 1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
3 tb Fresh parsley -- minced
3 c  Cooked rice
1 lb Red kidney beans
2 ts Paprika
1 ts Cayenne pepper -- or to
Taste
1 ts Black pepper
2 ea Bay leaves
1 ts Ground cumin
1 qt Water
Sort and rinse beans. In a large dutch oven or kettle, place all
ingredients except parsley and rice. Bring to a boil, then simmer,
covered 3 to 4 hr. or until beans are tender. Stir occasionally
adding water as needed to make a thick gravy. Just before serving,
remove bay leaves and stir in parsley. Serve over rice. Yield: 8
servings.
