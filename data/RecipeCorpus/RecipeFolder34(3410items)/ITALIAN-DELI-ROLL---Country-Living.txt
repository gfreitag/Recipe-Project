Title: ITALIAN DELI ROLL - Country Living
Categories: Snacks, Sandwich, Pizza, Country liv
Yield: 8 slices
-chopped
1    7-oz jar roasted red
-peppers, drained and
-coarsely chopped
2 ts Olive oil
1/4 ts Dried oregano leaves
Basic Pizza Dough
1 tb Cornmeal
1/4 lb Sliced provolone cheese,
-coarsely chopped
1/8 lb Sliced pepperoni, coarsely
-chopped
1/8 lb Sliced salami, coarsely
1. Prepare Basic Pizza Dough. Set aside to rise.

2. Heat oven to 400'F. Lightly dust jel ly-roll pan with cornmeal.
With rolling pin, roll out pizza dough to 18- by 12- inch rectangle.
Spread provolone, pepperoni, salami, and red peppers over rectangle,
leaving 1/2-inch border around edges. Starting at one short edge,
roll up dough, jelly-roll fashion. Secure ends and remaining short
edge by squeezing dough together between fingers. Place roll, seam
side down, on jelly-roll pan. Brush with olive oil and sprinkle with
oregano.

3. Bake roll 18 to 20 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on wire
rack 5 minutes. Slice and serve immediately.
