Title: Savory Watermelon & Pineapple Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Fresh oregano -- chopped
6 tb Lime juice
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1 1/2 c  Watermelon -- chunked &
-seeded
1 1/2 c  Pineapple -- chunked
1    Red onion -- thinly sliced
Mix and serve.
