Title: Easy Baked Corn
Categories: Vegetables, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
2 cn Whole kernel corn -- 16 oz.
ea Drained
1 ea Egg beaten
1 c  Sour cream -- 8 oz.
1 pk Corn bread mix -- 8-1/2 oz.
1/2 ts Salt
In a mixing bowl, combine egg and sour cream; stir in corn bread mix,
salt and pepper. Add corn; mix well. Pour into a greased 11-in. x
7-in. x 1-1/2-in. baking pan. Bake, uncovered, at 400 deg. for 25-30
minutes or till golden. Yield: 8 servings
