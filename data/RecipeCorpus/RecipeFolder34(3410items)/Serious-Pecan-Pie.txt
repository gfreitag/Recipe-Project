Title: Serious Pecan Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Dark Corn Syrup
1 1/4 c  Pecans, Broken Or Slightly
-Chopped
1 tb Rum
1/2 ts Salt
1    9 Inch Pie Shell --
-partially baked
1/3 c  Butter Or Margarine --
-melted
2/3 c  Brown Sugar -- packed
3 lg Eggs
1. Preheat oven to 450 F.

2. Bake the empty pie shell about 8 mins. Allow to cool while
preparing filling. Reduce oven temperature to 375 F.

3. In a mixing bowl, cream the butter and brown sugar. Beat in the
eggs, one at a time, until smooth. Stir in the corn syrup, pecans,
rum, and salt, blending thorouhgly.

4. Pour the mixture into the pie shell. Bake for 40 to 45 mins, or
until a knife inserted into the filling comes out clean. Serve warm
with vanilla ice cream. Also good served cold, though much chewier.
Makes 1 9-inch pie.
