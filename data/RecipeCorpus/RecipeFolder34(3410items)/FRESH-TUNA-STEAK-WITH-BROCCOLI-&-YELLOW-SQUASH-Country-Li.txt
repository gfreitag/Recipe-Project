Title: FRESH TUNA STEAK WITH BROCCOLI & YELLOW SQUASH-Country Li
Categories: Country liv, Main dish, Fish/seafoo, Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1/8 ts Dried rosemary leaves,
-crushed
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
2    4-oz tuna steaks
2    Lemon wedges
1 1/2 c  Small broccoli flowerets
1 sm (4 or 5 oz) yellow squash,
-cut into 1/2-inch pieces
2 tb Thinly sliced green onion
1/8 ts Dried oregano leaves,
-crushed
1. In small bowl, combine broccoli, squash, green onion, oregano,
rose- mary, and pepper.

2. Place one tuna steak near the side of a large microwave-safe dinner
plate. Place half of vegetable mixture next to tuna. Repeat with
remaining tuna steak and vegetables on another plate.

3. Cover plates with plastic wrap. Microwave each plate on high (100
percent) 3 to 4 minutes or until fish is opaque all the way through.
Puncture plastic wrap or uncover immediately to prevent wrap from
shrinking onto food. Serve fish with lemon wedges.
