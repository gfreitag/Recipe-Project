Title: Cranberry Chutney-1
Categories: Relishes, Preserves, Etc., Country wom
Yield: 20 servings
3/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1 tb Orange peel -- grated
3/4 c  Orange juice
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground ginger
3 c  Cranberries -- fresh or
Frozen
1 c  Dried apricots
1/2 c  Chopped dates
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
In large heavy saucepan, combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil.
reduce heat and simmer, uncovered for 15 to 20 min. or until
thickened and cranberries have popped. Chill. Serve as an
accompaniment to turkey or pork. Yield: about 3 1/2 cups.
