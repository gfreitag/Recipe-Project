Title: Creole Liver and Rice
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Pasta/rice, Cajun/creol, Bbq/grill
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1 pn Black pepper
2 tb Sherry
1 c  Rice,hot cooked
1/2 lb Beef liver
1 tb Vegetable oil
1/3 c  Green bell pepper,chopped
1 cn Stewed tomatoes (8oz)
1/2 ts Basil
1. Cut liver into serving pieces. 2. In skillet, brown liver quickly
in oil on both sides. 3. Add remaining ingredients except rice; cover
and simmer 45 minutes, or
until liver is tender. 4. Uncover and allow sauce to thicken, if
necessary. 5. Serve over mounds of hot rice.
