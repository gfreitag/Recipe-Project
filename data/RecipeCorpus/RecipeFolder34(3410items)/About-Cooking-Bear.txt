Title: About Cooking Bear
Categories: Information, Game
Yield: 1 text file  Remove all fat from bear meat at once, as it turns rancid very
quickly. Marinate at least 24 hours in an oil based marinade. Cook as
for any recipe for beef pot roast or beef stew. Cub will need at
least 2 1/2 hours cooking and mature bear 3 1/2 to 4 hours. Always
cook to well done at avoid trichinosis as for pork.

Adapted from the Joy of Cooking by Jim Weller
Submitted By JIM WELLER   On   08-24-95
