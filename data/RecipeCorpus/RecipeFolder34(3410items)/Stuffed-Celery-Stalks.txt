Title: Stuffed Celery Stalks
Categories: Appetizers, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Curry powder
4 ea Celery stalks
Cut into 9-inch pieces
1/4 c  Salted peanuts -- chopped
1 pk Cream cheese -- 3 ounces
Softened
2 tb Creamy peanut butter
1 tb Light cream
1 1/2 ts Onion -- minced
In a small bowl, blend first five ingredients. Stuff into celery
pieces; sprinkle with peanuts. Chill until you serve. Yield: 6-8
servings
