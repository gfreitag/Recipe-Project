Title: Easy Hot Chili
Categories: Chili, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn (10-oz.) Tomatoes and green
-chiles; drained
2 tb All-purpose flour
2 tb Chili powder
1/4 ts Ground cumin (optional)
x  Vegetable cooking spray
1 lb Ground chuck
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 cn (8-oz.) No-salt-added tomato
-sauce
1 c  Water
Coat a large skillet with cooking spray.  Add meat and onion; cook
until browned, stirring to crumble meat.  Drain well; pat meat
mixture with paper towels.  Wipe drippings from skillet with a paper
towel. Return meat mixture to skillet; add tomato sauce, water, and
tomatoes and chiles.

Combine flour, chili powder (and cumin, if desired).  Stir into beef
mixture.  Cover and simmer 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Yield:
4 (1-cup) servings (30% calories from fat).

PER SERVING:  CALORIES 234; FAT 7.7g; CARB 13.0g; PROTEIN 25.8g;
CHOLESTEROL 70mg; SODIUM 403mg.

From:  Healthy Choice CHOICES FOR LIVING HAPPIER AND HEALTHIER, Fall
1995.
