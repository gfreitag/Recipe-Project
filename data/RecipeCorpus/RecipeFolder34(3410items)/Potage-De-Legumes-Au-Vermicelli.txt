Title: Potage De Legumes Au Vermicelli
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
4    About 4 Oz Mushrooms --
-coarsely chopped
1    About 6 Oz Yam, Peeled --
-cut in 1/2" pieces
7 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black Pepper -- freshly
-ground
1 c  Vermicelli, Angel Hair/Very
-Thin Noodles
1 tb Virgin Olive Oil
1/2 ts Unsalted Butter
1    About 3 Oz Leek, Trim Of
-Damaged Leaves, Clean --
-cut in 1/2" pieces
1 md Onion, (About 4 Oz) Peeled
-coarsely chopped
2    About 4 Oz Carrots, Peeled
-cut in 1/2" pieces
1    About 4 Oz Zucchini, Trimmed
-cut in 3/4" pieces
1. Heat the oil and butter in a saucepan. When hot, add the leeks and
onions and saute for 2 mins. Then add the carrots, zucchini,
mushrooms and yams, and mix well. Add the water, salt and pepper.

2. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat and boil gently for about 12 mins.

3. Add the pasta, bring back to the boil and cook, covered, for about
4 mins. Serve. Makes 6 servings (8 cups).
