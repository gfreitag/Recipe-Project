Title: Frosted Chicken Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
12 c  Seedless grapes -- green
Sliced
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
2 c  Red apples -- unpeeled &
Diced
1 tb Lemon juice
4 c  Cooked chicken -- diced
3/4 c  Miracle Whip® light
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
Lettuce leaves
Sliced apple and grapes for
Garnish
1 pk Cream cheese -- softened 8
Oz
1/4 c  Miracle Whip® light
Line a 1 1/2 qt. bowl with plastic wrap. Combine all salad
ingredients and gently press into the bowl. Cover; chill several
hours. Carefully unmold onto a plate lined with a bed of lettuce
leaves. Combine the cream cheese and salad dressing; frost salad.
Garnish with apples and grapes. Chill for several hours. Yield: 6
servings.
