Title: CHOPPED-PEPPER SALAD - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1    Stalk celery, coarsely
-chopped
1 md Cucumber, peeled and
-coarsely chopped
2 ea Large sweet red and green
-peppers, seeded and
-coarsely chopped
1 lg Banana pepper or sweet
-yellow pepper, seeded
-and coarsely chopped
1/3 c  Olive oil
3 tb Cider vinegar
2 sm Cloves garlic, finely
-chopped
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley leaves
1 tb Chopped fresh or 1/2 t dried
-basil leaves
1 tb Chopped green onion
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Dry mustard
1/8 ts Ground black pepper
1. In large bowl, whisk together oil, vinegar, garlic, parsley, basil,
green onion, salt, mustard, and black pepper.

2. Add celery, cucumber, and peppers; toss until coated with dressing.
Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
