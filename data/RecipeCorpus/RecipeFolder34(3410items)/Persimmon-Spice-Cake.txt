Title: Persimmon Spice Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
2 ts Baking powder
2 ts Soda
1 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Allspice
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Corn oil
4    Eggs, well beaten
2 c  Persimmon pulp
2 c  Flour
Cream sugar, oil, eggs and persimmon.  Add dry ingredients gradually.
Pour into 2 well oiled 9 inch pans.  Bake for 25 to 30 minutes at 350
degrees. Ice with carmel icing. Randy Rigg
