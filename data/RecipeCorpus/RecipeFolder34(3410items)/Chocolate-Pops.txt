Title: Chocolate Pops
Categories: Diabetic, Snacks
Yield: 3 - 6 pops
3/4 c  Club soda; -=OR=-
3/4 c  Sparkling water
1 ct (8-oz) chocolate-flavored
-- low-fat or non-fat yogurt
1    Very ripe banana;
Combine yogurt and banana in blender or food processor.  Whirl until
smooth.  Stir in club soda.  Freeze until firm in a plastic mold if
you have one.  IF NOT, freeze mixture in small paper cups.  Add a
stick or plastic spoon when pops are half frozen.  To eat, peel away
the paper. Eat right away or transfer to a plastic bags for long-term
freezing. NOTE: If you can fine chocolate, use another flavor of your
choice.
