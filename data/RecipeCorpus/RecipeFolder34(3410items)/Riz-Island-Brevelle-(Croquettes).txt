Title: Riz Island Brevelle (Croquettes)
Categories: Side dish, Cajun/creol
Yield: 9 croquettes
1 1/2 ts Onion juice
5 ea Egg yolks; beaten
3 tb Parmesan cheese; grated
2 ea Egg yolks
1 1/2 tb Milk
1 1/2 c  Cracker crumbs
Vegetable oil
2 1/2 c  Water
3/4 c  Uncooked regular rice
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Tomato juice
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 1/2 tb Butter or margarine
Bring water to a boil in a large saucepan; add rice and salt.  Boil
10 minutes; drain.
Combine next 4 ingredients; stir into rice.  Cook over low heat 10
minutes.  Remove from heat; add butter and onion juice.  Stir in 5 egg
yolks and cheese, mixing well.  Coook over low heat 3 minutes. Remove
from heat; cool.  Shape into croquettes.
Combine 2 egg yolks and milk, beating well.  Dip croquettes into egg
mixture; dredge in cracker crumbs, coating well.
Chill croquettes 1 hour.  Fry in 1 inch of hot oil (375 F) until
golden brown.  Drain on paper towels.
