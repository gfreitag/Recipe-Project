Title: Cappucino Royale Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1/3 c  Brandy
1/2 oz Baking chocolate -- melted
3    Eggs
1 pk Chocolate cake mix
1/2 c  Whipping cream
1/2 c  Hot water
3 tb Instant coffee
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

Blend cake mix, whipping cream, coffee dissolved in water, brandy,
melted chocolate, and eggs in a large mixer bowl, then beat 2 minutes
at medium speed.

Pour batter into 2 greased and floured 9-inch round cake pans.

Bake for 35-45 minutes, or until center springs back when lightly
touched.
