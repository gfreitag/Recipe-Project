Title: SPICED PEACH CHUTNEY - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Condiments, Relishes
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
1/8 ts Ground cloves
1/8 ts Salt
1 pn Ground red pepper (opt.)
1 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
-leaves
1 ts Cider vinegar
1 sm Onion, chopped
2 ts Olive oil
1 1/2 c  Chopped peeled peaches
1/3 c  Dark seedless raisins
2 tb Light-brown sugar
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1. In 1-quart microwave-safe dish, combine onion and oil; cover and
microwave on high (100 percent) 2 minutes.

2. Add peaches, raisins, sugar, cinnamon, cloves, salt, and red
pepper, if desired, to onion. Cover and microwave on medium (50
percent) 5 minutes. If microwave does not have carousel, stir mixture
after 2 minutes. Uncover chutney mixture and microwave on medium 5
minutes.

3. Add cilantro and vinegar to mixture; set aside until cool. Serve or
cover and refrigerate until serving.

Nutritional information per T -protein: .2 gram; fat: .4 gram;
carbohydrate: 4 grams; fiber: .4 gram; sodium: 11 milligrams;
cholesterol: 0; calories: 19.
