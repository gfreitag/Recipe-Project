Title: CROCKPOT PORK ROAST
Categories: Meats, Crockpot, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
-Soup Mix"
1 ea Env. water
lg Pinch garlic powder
3-4 lb. pork roast (not
-chops)
1 ea Env. Lipton "Golden Onion
Place the roast in your crockpot.  Pour in soup, water and garlic
powder. Cook all day on low.  Thicken juice to make gravy and serve
over potatoes, rice or noodles. From: Barbara O'Connor (JCGV17A) -
