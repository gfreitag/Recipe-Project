Title: Light Hearted Lasagna
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Casseroles
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
2 ea Eggs beaten
1 pk Frozen spinach thawed and
Squeezed dry
8 oz Mozzerella thinly sliced
3/4 lb Lean ground beef
1 cn Meatless spaghetti sauce
2    Cloves garlic crushed
1 ts Basil
1 ts Oregano
2 c  Converted rice
Cook beef until no longer pink.  Drain off fat and stir in sauce
garlic and half of the oregano and basil.  Bring to a boil. Simmer
for 15 minutes stirring occaisonally.  Cook rice according to package
directions. Stir in parmesan cheese, eggs, and remaining oregano and
basil. In a 13 x 9 inch pan layer one third of the rice mixture. Top
with half of the meat mixture half of the spinach and half of the
mozzerella. Repeat layers ending with a layer of rice.  Cover and
bake at 350 for 15 minutes. Uncover and bake 15 to 20 minutes longer.
Place under broiler for 1 min until lightly golden. From Uncle Bens
