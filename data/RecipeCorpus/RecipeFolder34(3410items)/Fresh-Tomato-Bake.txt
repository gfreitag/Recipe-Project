Title: Fresh Tomato Bake
Categories: Vegetables, Tomatoes
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Margarine
2 lg Onions, sliced
2 1/2 c  Fresh bread crumbs
6 tb Margarine, melted
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
8 md Tomatoes, peel & quarter
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Dry mustard
Mix tomatoes, salt, pepper, sugar and dry mustard until well blended.
Saute onions in margarine for 5 minutes.  Mix bread crumbs and
margarine until blended.  In a buttered 1 1/2 quart casserole, layer
the tomato mixture, the onions, then crumbs.  Bake in a 350 degree
oven for 25 minutes. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake 5 minutes
more. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   08-22-95
