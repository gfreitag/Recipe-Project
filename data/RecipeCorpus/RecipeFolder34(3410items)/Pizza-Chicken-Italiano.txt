Title: Pizza Chicken Italiano
Categories: Poultry, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Sugar
4 sl Mozzarella cheese
2    Whole chicken breasts
Split, boned -- skinned
15 oz Can tomato sauce
1 1/2 ts Oregano
1 ts Parsley -- chopped
Place chicken in 2 qt. baking dish. Combine tomato sauce, oregano,
chopped parsley, garlic salt, onion powder, and sugar. Pour over
chicken. Cover and bake at 425 for 45 minutes. Top with cheese and
continue to bake, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
