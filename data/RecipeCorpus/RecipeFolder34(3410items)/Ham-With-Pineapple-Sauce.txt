Title: Ham With Pineapple Sauce
Categories: Pork, Ham, Country wom
Yield: 4 servings
Undrained
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
2 tb Mustard -- prepared
1 ds Cloves
4 sl Cooked ham -- 1/2 inch
Thick
2 tb Butter or margarine --
Melted
1 cn Crushed pineapple --
In a skillet, saute ham slices in butter until warmed. Meanwhile, in a
saucepan, combine all of the remaining ingredients; simmer for 3 min.
Serve over ham. Yield: 4 servings.
