Title: Scone Cream
Categories: Newspaper, Scot/irish, Breads
Yield: 1 cup
3 tb Powdered sugar
2 c  Yogurt; plain
1/4 c  Whipping cream
Put the yogurt in a colander lined with a double layer of cheesecloth.
Place over a deep bowl, cover and refrigerate overnight.  In a small
bowl, whip the cream.  Fold in the yogurt cheese and powdered sugar.

Nutritional Information per serving:  serving equals 1 tbsp 30
calories, 1 g fat, 2g protein, 4g carbohydrates, 0mg cholesterol,
23mg sodium
