Title: Turkey Smoked Sausage and Rice
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Pasta/rice
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Butter
8 oz Stewed tomatoes, undrained
1/4 ts Basil leaves
1 lb Turkey smoked sausage
1/2 c  Sliced carrots
1 1/4 c  Zucchini, sliced
Cut sausage into serving size pieces, set aside.  Saute carrots and
zucchini in butter in skillet over medium heat for 3 minutes.  Add
tomatoes with juice, basil, and sausage.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce
heat to low; cover and simmer 5 minutes.  Serve with rice. Randy Rigg
