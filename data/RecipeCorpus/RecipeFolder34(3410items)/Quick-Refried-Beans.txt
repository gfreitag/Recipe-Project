Title: Quick Refried Beans
Categories: Vegetables, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------FROM: PAULETTE GOLDSTEIN-----------------------
Salt to taste
Using a non-stick pan (if
-possible), saute onion
-until
Soft. Add beans and liquid
-and mash and stir
-constantly
Until creamy. Season to
-taste.
This is very good served
-with tortilla chips!
No need to soak beans and
-hassle.. whenever you have
-an urge
For some beans... you can
-have them 1-2-3!
1 ea 16 oz can of black beans
-(do not drain)
1/2 sm Onion finely chopped
1 ts Oil
A few dashes tabasco
-(OPTIONAL)
