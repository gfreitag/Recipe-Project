Title: Greek Salad with Tomatoes & Feta
Categories: Salads, Greek, Tomatoes
Yield: 4 servings
1    Tomato; cut into wedges
1/2 c  Black olives
4 oz Feta cheese; crumbled
1    Leaf lettuce head; torn
1/3    Cucumber; seedless; thinly
-sliced
1/3 c  Sweet onion; Finely chopped
MMMMM-------------------OIL AND LEMON DRESSING------------------------
1 tb Oregano; fresh and chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Lemon juice
Mix all ingredients in a small bowl and pour over salad.  183
calories and 16 grams fat.  Source:  Store ad Cooking Echo 03/24/95
