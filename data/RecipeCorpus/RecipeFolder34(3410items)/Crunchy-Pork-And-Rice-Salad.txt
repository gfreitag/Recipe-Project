Title: Crunchy Pork And Rice Salad
Categories: Salads, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Rice -- leftover cooked
1 pk Frozen peas -- l0 oz.
Thawed
1 cn Water chestnuts -- 8 oz.
Sliced/drained
1    Head Chinese cabbage
Or green cabbage
Shredded(about 6 cups)
2 c  Pork roast
Cubed leftover cooked
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1 ts Celery seed
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
In a large bowl, toss together cabbage, pork, rice, peas and water
chestnuts. In a small bowl, combine dressing ingredients; mix well.
Pour over the salad and stir gently to mix. Chill for several hours.
Yield: 8-10 servings
