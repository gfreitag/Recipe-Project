Title: Pizza Via Veneto
Categories: Appetizers, Pizza
Yield: 36 servings
1 ts Celery seed
4 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
3 c  Salted peanuts -- chopped
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Box hot roll mix
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
1/3 c  Peanut butter
1/4 c  Chopped pimento
prepare hot roll mix and let rise as package directs. Divide dough
into two equal parts.  Pat each part, using oiled hands, into
well-oiled jelly roll pan. Beat eggs, milk and peanut butter until
smooth. Add pimento and celery seed.  Stir in cheese; spread evenly
over dough, almost to edge. Sprinkle with peanuts and pepper. Cover;
let rise for 30 minutes. Preheat oven to 425 F.  Bake 12 to 15
minutes or until brown and bubbly. Cut into 4x2 inch rectangles.
Serve hot.
