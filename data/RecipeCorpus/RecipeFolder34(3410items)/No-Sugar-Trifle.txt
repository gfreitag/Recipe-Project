Title: No Sugar Trifle
Categories: Desserts, Diabetic
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM--------------------------CUSTARD-------------------------------
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Grated orange rind
2/3 c  Splenda (artificial sweetner
1/4 c  Cornstarch
1/4 ts Salt
1 3/4 c  Skim milk
2    Eggs,beaten
MMMMM---------------------------TRIFLE--------------------------------
3 c  Assorted fruits or canned
Fruits
1/2 pk Lowfat pound cake,cubed
2 tb Unsweetened strawberry jam
1 tb Orange liqueur or orangejuic
Directions Mix sweetener cornstarch ,salt in heavy saucepan Stir in
milk. Stir over medium heat until boiling Simmer,stirring ,over low
heat 2 minutes Gradually stir into eggs Return to saucepan; cook and
stir 1 minute Remove from heat, stir in vanilla and orange rind CHILL
Cut pound cake in half lengthwise Spread with jam , put layers
together ,cut into 1/2" cubes Place in glass bowl Drizzle with
liqueur or orangejuice Arrange fruit on top Spread custard over fruit
Refrigerate for at least 1 hour
