Title: Salsa Picante with Shrimp
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 11 cups
3 ea Jalapeno peppers; seeded &
-chopped
1 tb Ground cumin
2 ts Paprika
1 ts Salt
Lettuce leaves
Tortilla chips
4 c  Water
1 1/2 lb Unpeeled, large fresh shrimp
2 ea Avocados; peeled and cubed
1/4 c  Lime juice
6 c  Cubed tomatoes (about 3 lbs)
3 lg Onions; chopped (ab 4 cups)
8 cl Garlic; minced
1/4 c  Fresh cilantro; chopped
Bring water to a boil, add shrimp, and cook 3 to 5 minutes or until
shrimp turns pink.  Drain well; rinse with cold water.  Chill; peel
and devein shrimp.  Cut shrimp into quarters, and set aside.
Combine avocados and lime juice in a small bowl; set aside. Combine
shrimp, tomatoes, and next 7 ingredients in a large bowl; gently stir
in avocado-lime mixture.  Chill at least one hour.  Serve in a
lettuce-lined bowl with tortilla chips.  Yield:  11-1/2 cups.

Recipe from Jade and Charlie McCulloch of Texas in September, 1992
