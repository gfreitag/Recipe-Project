Title: Frothy Mocha Cocoa
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 8 servings
3 c  Milk
2 c  Light Cream Or Half & Half
2 ts Vanilla Extract
4 oz Unsweetened Baking Chocolate
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Instant Coffee Powder
1 c  ;Water
Combine the chocolate, sugar, coffee powder and water in a 3-quart
saucepan.  Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the
chocolate is melted.  Increase the heat to medium and continue to
cook until the mixture comes to a boil.  Reduce the heat to low and
simmer for 4 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Stir in the milk,
cream, and vanilla extract. Heat for 5 minutes or until hot.  Remove
from the heat and beat, with a rotary beater, until foamy.  Pour into
a thermos jug or serve at once.

Makes eight 6-ounce servings.
