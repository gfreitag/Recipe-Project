Title: Baked Haddock
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 3 servings
4 tb Tomato juice
2 c  Crushed Ritz crackers
1    Stick of margarine, melted
Fish fillets, haddock
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Place fish fillets in a shallow baking
dish. Add a little water and butter to the bottom of dish.  Set aside
and mix cheese, celery, garlic powder and tomato juice; blend well.
Sprinkle the mixture generously over the fish.  Top with crushed Ritz
crackers; pour melted margarine over crackers.  Bake for 20 minutes.
