Title: Barbecue Beef Sandwiches
Categories: Bbq/grill, Beef, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Sugar
2 tb Prepared mustard
1    Ablespoon
Salt and pepper to taste
Hamburger buns
Vinegar
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1 md Green pepper -- chopped
3/4 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Water
In a skillet, melt butter over medium high heat. Brown the beef,
onion and green pepper. Add all remaining ingredients except buns.
Simmer, uncovered, 15 minutes. Serve on buns. Yield: about 6 servings
