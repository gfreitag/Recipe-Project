Title: Raspberry Peach Jam
Categories: Fruit, Preserves, Etc., Country wom
Yield: 100 servings
Frozen crushed
3 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
2 2/3 c  Peaches -- peeled
Finely chopped
1 1/2 c  Fresh raspberries -- or
In a large kettle, combine all ingrdients. cook over low heat,
stirring occasional, until sugar is dissolved and mixture is bubbly,
about 10 min. Bring to a full rolling boil; boil for 15 min. stirring
constantly. Remove from the heat; skim off foam. Pour hot into hot
jars leaving 1/4 inch headspace. Adjust caps. Process for 15 min. in
a boiling water bath. Yield: 5 half-pint.
