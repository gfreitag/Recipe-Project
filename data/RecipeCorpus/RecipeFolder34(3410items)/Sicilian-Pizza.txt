Title: Sicilian Pizza
Categories: Appetizers, Pizza
Yield: 1 servings
Black pepper -- to taste
Black or green olives -- or
Pitted and chopped
1 lb Bread dough
1 c  Onions -- thinly sliced
10    Garlic cloves -- finely chpd
1/4 c  Olive oil
Parmesan cheese -- to taste
Form bread dough to pan.  Brush dough with olive oil and set aside.
Lightly saute onions.  Sprinkle the dough liberally, with black
pepper, add sauteed onions,"raw" chopped garlic, parmesan, and
olives. Bake at 350 until bottom is lightly browned, about 30 to 40
minutes.
