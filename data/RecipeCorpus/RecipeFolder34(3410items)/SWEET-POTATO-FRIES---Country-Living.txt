Title: SWEET-POTATO FRIES - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
Coarse salt or confectioners
-sugar (opt.)
4 md (about 2 lb) sweet potatoes
Vegetable oil for frying
1. Peel potatoes and cut into 3 by 3/4inch wedges. Place in large
bowl, cover with cold water, and set aside to soak for 20 minutes.

2. In large skillet, heat 1/4 inch oil until deep-fat thermometer
registers 300'F. Drain potato wedges and pat dry. In batches, fry
potatoes in oil, turning frequently-8 to 10 minutes or until lightly
browned. Add more oil, if necessary. Drain on paper towels; sprinkle
with salt or sugar, if desired, and serve.
