Title: DILL PESTO - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Herbs/spice
Yield: 3 /4 cup
2 lg Garlic cloves, peeled
-and minced
3 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
2 tb Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese, preferably Reggiano
1 1/2 c  Packed fresh dill leaves
1/2 c  Packed fresh flat-leaf
-parsley sprigs
2 tb Walnuts, pistachios,
-or pine nuts
In a blender or food processor, place the dill, parsley, nuts, and
garlic. Process until finelv minced. Add oil and cheese and process
untif blended. Transfer to a small bowl, cover and refrigerate.

House Beautiful/July/94  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
