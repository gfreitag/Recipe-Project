Title: Tofu Curry Cream
Categories: Dips/spread, Dress/stuff, Curries, Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
================
TOFU CURRY CREAM
Serve this creamy spread from Vegetarian teacher Eta Brand as a dip
for fruit or vegetable platters or as a dressing for baked potatoes.

INGREDIENTS:

8 oz Tofu, silken or firm
2 tb Canola Oil
1/3 c Lemon Juice
1/4 c  Honey
1/2 ts Dried Ginger
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Curry Powder

DIRECTIONS:

In food processor, crumble tofu and blend with oil and lemon juice.
Add honey and spices. Blend until creamy yellow. If using firm tofu,
add a little water for a creamier consistency. Makes 1 1/2 cups.
