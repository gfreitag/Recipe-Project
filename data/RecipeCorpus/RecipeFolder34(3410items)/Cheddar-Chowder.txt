Title: Cheddar Chowder
Categories: Soups, Stews, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Water
2 c  Diced potataes
1/2 c  Diced carrots
1/2 c  Diced celery
MMMMM------------------------WHLTE SAUCE-----------------------------
2 c  Cheddar cheese -- grated
1 c  Cubed ham
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Flour
2 c  Milk
Combine water, potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, salt and pepper in
large kettle. Boil 10-12 minutes. Meanwhile, in small saucepan, make
white sauce by melting the butter. Add flour and stir until smooth
(about 1 minute). Slowly add milk; cook until thickened. Add grated
cheese to white sauce; stir until melted. Add white sauce and cubed
ham to vegetables that have not been drained. Heat through. Yield: 6
servings
