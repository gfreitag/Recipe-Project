Title: Fat Rascals (Potato Cheese Puffs)
Categories: Appetizers, Country wom
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Baking powder
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
Salad oil for frying
1 c  Mashed potatoes
2 ea Eggs -- beaten
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  American cheese -- 8 oz
Or cheddar cheese
Combine ingredients except oil; mix well. 'Pour about 2 inch of salad
oil into a saucepan or frying pan and heat to  375 deg. Drop batter by
tablespoons, 4 or 5 at a time, into the hot oil. Fry 3-4 min. or until
golden brown. Serve immediately. Yield: about 24 puffs.
