Title: Lentils With Garlic And Rosemary^
Categories: Vegetables, Crockpot, Low cal/fat, Healthy
Yield: 11 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Dried lentils
1 cn (14.5 oz) can fatfree beef
Broth
2 cl Garlic, chopped
1    Bay leaf
Chopped fresh parsley,
Optional
Cooking Light 9-95
Carolyn Shaw 8-95
5 c  Water
3 c  Chopped onions
2 c  Diced cooked ham
1 c  Diced carrot
1 ts Dried rosemary, crushed
3/4 ts Rubbed sage
Combine all ingredients except fresh parsley in slow cooker. Cover
and cook on high for 3 hours or until lentils are tender. Discard bay
leaf. Garnish with parsley, if desired. Makes 11 1 cup servings.

Per serving: 196 cal., 16.8 g protein, 2.2 g (10%) fat, 28.9 g carb.,
6 g fiber, 13 mg chol., 4.1 mg iron, 248 mg sodium, 38 mg calcium.
