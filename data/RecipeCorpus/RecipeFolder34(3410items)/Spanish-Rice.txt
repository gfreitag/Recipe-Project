Title: Spanish Rice
Categories: Crockpot, Tex/mexican, Casseroles, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
2    Green peppers, chopped
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
28 oz Can tomatoes
8 oz Can tomato sauce
1 c  Raw rice (converted)
2 lb Ground chuck
Or beef
1 c  Water
2 1/2 ts Chili powder
2 md Onions, chopped
2 1/2 ts Salt
Brown beef in skillet and drain off fat. Put all ingredients in
CROCK-POT. Stir thoroughly. Cover and cook on Low 6 to 8 hours.
(High: 3 hours). From Rival Crock-pot cook book, date unknown
