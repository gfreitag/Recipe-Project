Title: MARY FRENCH'S CREME DE MENTHE BROWNIES - Country Living
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate, Country liv
Yield: 18 bars
MMMMM-----------------------CHOCOLATE BASE----------------------------
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn 16-oz chocolate-flavored
-syrup
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  (1 stick) butter or
-margarine, softened
1 c  Granulated sugar
4    Large eggs
1 c  Unsifted all-purpose flour
MMMMM-------------------------MINT ICING------------------------------
2 c  Confectioners' sugar
2 tb Mint-flavored liqueur
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter or
-margarine, softened
MMMMM---------------------CHOCOLATE TOPPING--------------------------
1/2 c  (1 stick) butter or
-margarine
1 pk (12-oz) semisweet
-chocolate chips
1. Prepare Chocolate Base: Heat oven to 350'F. Grease and lightly
flour a 13- by 9-inch baking pan. In large bowl, beat butter until
light and fluffy. Gradually beat in granulated sugar until well
combined.  Add eggs, one at a time beating well after each addition.

2. In small bowl or on sheet of waxed paper, combine flour and salt.
Beat flour mixture, chocolate syrup, and vanilla into butter mixture
until well combined. Pour chocolate-base batter into prepared baking
pan. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until cake tester when inserted in
center comes out clean. Cool base completely in pan on wire rack.

3. Prepare Mint Icing: In medium-size bowl, beat butter until light
and fluffy. Gradually beat in confectioners' sugar and liqueur until
smooth. Spread icing evenly over top of cooled chocolate base in
baking pan. Refrigerate at least 1 hour or until well chilled.

4. Prepare Chocolate Topping: In top of double boiler or heatproof
bowl, over simmering water, stir chocolate chips and butter until
melted and smooth. Spread topping evenly over icing in baking pan.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour to set. Cut brownies into bars to serve.
Refrigerate any leftovers.
