Title: Panamanian Hot Tomato Beef
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Vegetables, So. america, Tomatoes
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 sl Tomatoes
1 lg Onion; sliced
1/4 ts Oregano
1 lb Sirloin steak
1 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Seasoning salt
1 ts Habanero hot sauce
In Spanish: Biftec Picante con Tomate al Estilo Panameno

Pound the sirloin and then rinse it under running water.  Pat the
sirloin dry and place it in a large ceramic bowl.
Pour the soy sauce, seasoning salt, and pepper sauce over the steak
and marinate for 20 minutes.
In a medium skillet, heat the oil and brown the steak on both
sides. Add the tomato slices, onion, and oregano and simmer for 15
minutes.

Authors' heat scale: Medium
