Title: Beef And Bean Burritos
Categories: Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/4 oz Pkg taco seasoning mix
8 ea Flour tortillas (7" - 8")
3/4 c  Lettuce; shredded
3/4 c  Cheese; shredded (3 oz)
16 oz Jar Chunky Salsa
1 lb Ground beef
16 oz Can refried beans
4 oz Can green chilies; drained
Drain salsa, reserving 1/2 cup liquid. In a large skillet, brown
ground beef; drain fat. Add beans, chopped green chilies, taco
seasoning mix and reserved salsa liquid. Bring to boiling; reduce
heat. Simmer, uncovered, 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Heat tortillas according to package directions.

To assemble burritos, spoon about 1/3 cup beef mixture just below
center of each tortilla. Add 1 heaping tablespoon each of drained
salsa, lettuce and cheese to each tortilla. Fold bottom edge of each
tortilla up and over just until filling is covered. Fold opposite
sides of tortilla in, just until they meet. Roll up from the bottom.

MICROWAVE DIRECTIONS Drain salsa, reserving 1/2 cup liquid. Crumble
ground beef into large microwave safe bowl. Microwave on high (100
percent) 4 to 6 minutes or until no longer pink, stirring once.
Drain; return meat to bowl. Stir in beans, chilies, taco seasoning
and reserved salsa liquid. Microwave, uncovered, on high 7 minutes,
stirring twice. Assemble burritos as directed above.

Makes 8 servings

Per serving: Calories: 345 (25% from protein, 43% from carbohydrate,
33% from fat) Protein: 21 grams Fat: 13 grams Cholesterol: 49 mg
Carbohydrate: 37 grams Sodium: 1,348 mg Exchanges: 2 vegetable, 1-1/2
bread, 2 meat, 1 fat.
