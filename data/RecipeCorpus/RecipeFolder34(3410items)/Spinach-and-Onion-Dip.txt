Title: Spinach and Onion Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Vegetables
Yield: 1 batch
1/4 ts Ground white pepper
1/4 ts Ground red
-chile pepper powder
1/4 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Seasoned salt
3 tb Olive oil
1 lg Onion(s), chopped
2 lg Garlic clove(s), minced
10 oz Pkg frozen spinach, thawed
3/4    Carton fat-free sour cream
-(about 6 oz)
Heat oil in heavy skillet over medium heat. Add onions and garlic and
saut until golden brown, about 10 minutes. Add spinach and saut for
about 2 minutes more. Let cool. Pure in blender or food processor
along with the sour cream and all the spices. Adjust seasoning; add
more chile pepper powder if you like it very hot. Serve with crackers
or raw vegetables.

Makes about 2 cups.
