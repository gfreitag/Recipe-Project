Title: PICKLED OKRA - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Cumin seeds
1/2 ts Celery seeds
1/2 ts Crushed red pepper
1/2 lb Fresh okra
2 c  Water
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/3 c  Sugar
6    Whole cloves
1 ts Salt
1. In 2-quart saucepan, heat water, vinegar, sugar, cloves, salt,
cumin seeds, celery seeds, and red pepper to boiling.

2. Add okra; boil 3 minutes. Remove from heat and cool to room
temperature. Transfer okra and pickling liquid to jar or container;
seal. Refrigerate several hours or overnight before serving.
