Title: Shrimp Vegetable Stir Fry With Linguine^
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish, Pasta/rice, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1    Green bell pepper, cut in
1/4    " strips
1 cn (15.5oz) red beans or light
Kidney beans, drained
1 tb To 2 chopped fresh basil
**OR**
1 1/2 ts Dried basil leaves
1 cn (8oz) tomato sauce
1/4 ts To 1/2 hot pepper sauce
Fast & Healthy 8/92
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
6 oz Uncooked linguine
1 tb Olive oil
3/4 lb Shelled raw shrimp
6    Green onions, cut in 1"
Pieces
2 cl Garlic, minced
1    Red bell pepper, cut in 1/4"
Strips
Cook linguine to desired doneness as directed on package. Drain;
rinse with hot water. Meanwhile, heat large skillet over medium-high
heat until hot. Add oil; heat until it ripples. Add shrimp, green
onions, garlic and bell peppers; stir fry about 4 minutes or until
shrimp turn pink. Stir in beans, basil, tomato sauce and hot pepper
sauce. Cook until thoroughly heated. Serve over cooked linguine. 6 (1
1/2 cup) servings.

Per serving: 230 calories, 17 g protein, 36 g carbohydrate, 5 g
fiber, 4 g fat, 81 mg cholesterol, 410 mg sodium, 460 mg potassium, 2
starch, 1 lean meat and 1 vegetable exchange.
