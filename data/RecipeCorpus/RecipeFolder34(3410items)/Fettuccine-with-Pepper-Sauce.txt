Title: Fettuccine with Pepper Sauce
Categories: Main dish, Pasta/rice
Yield: 3 servings
10 3/4 oz Can cream of chicken soup
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Basil
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
8 oz Spinach fettuccine, cooked
And drained
1 1/2 c  Broccoli flowerets
1 md Red pepper
1 md Onion, chopped
1 tb Oil
12 oz Skinless, boneless chicken
Cut into strips
Stir fry broccoli, pepper and onion for 3 to 4 minutes; remove from
skillet.  Add oil to skillet.  Add poultry; stir fry 3 to 4 minutes.
Add soup, water and basil to skillet.  Mix thoroughly.  Stir in sweet
pepper mixture.  Bring to boiling, reduce heat.  Add cheese, cook and
stir until cheese is almost melted.  Serve over cooked pasta. Randy
