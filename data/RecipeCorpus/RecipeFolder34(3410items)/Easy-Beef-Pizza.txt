Title: Easy Beef Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
1 md Green pepper -- cut in
Rings
1    Green onion -- sliced
3/4 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1/2 lb Lean ground beef
Quick Pizza Crust
1 ts Dried italian seasoning
1/4 ts Fennel seeds -- crushed
1/4 ts Dried red pepper -- crushed
1 cn Pizza sauce
Prepare quick pizza crust (recipe follows). Preheat oven to 450 F.
Heat large skillet over medium heat until hot. Crumble beef into
skillet. Cook, stiring to separate meat, until no pink remains. Pour
off drippings. Sprinkle italian seasoning, fennel seeds and crushed
red pepper over beef. Spread pizza sauce over baked pizza crust.
Place green pepper and onion over sauce; top with beef mixture.
Sprinkle with mozzarella and parmesan cheeses.  Bake 7 to 10 minutes
or until cheese is melted.
