Title: Glazled Baby Carrots
Categories: Vegetables, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Water
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 lb Fresh baby carrots
Frozen or canned whole baby
Carrots
Cook carrots in a small amount of water until tender. Drain. In a
saucepan, combine butter and brown sugar; heat until sugar dissolves.
Add carrots and toss to coat. Heat through. Yield: 4 servings
