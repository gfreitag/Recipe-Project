Title: Pot Roast of Beef
Categories: Crockpot, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
Thinly sliced
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 c  Water or beef consomme
2    To 3 carrots, pared and
3    To 4 pound brisket, rump
Roast, or pot roast
1    To 2 onions, peeled and
Sliced
2    To 3 potatoes, pared and
(Melt in your mouth tender, and s-u-c-h flavor!) thinly sliced

Put vegetables in bottom of CROCK-POT. Salt and pepper meat, then put
in pot. Add liquid. Cover and cook on Low 10 to 12 hours. (High: 4 to
5 hours).  Remove meat and vegetables with spatula. From Rival
Crock-pot cookbook, date unknown
