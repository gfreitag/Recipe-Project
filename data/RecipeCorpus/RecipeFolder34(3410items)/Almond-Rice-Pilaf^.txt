Title: Almond Rice Pilaf^
Categories: Pasta/rice, Low cal/fat, Healthy
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Low sodium soy sauce
1/4 ts Salt
4 c  Hot cooked rice
1/4 c  Lsliced green onions
2 tb Sliced onions, toasted
Combine all ingredients and toss well.

Per serving: 222 cal., 4.8 g protein, 2.3 g (9%) fat, 44.1 g carb.,
1.4 g fiber., 0 mg chol., 2.2 mg iron, 185 mg sodium, 47 mg calcium.
