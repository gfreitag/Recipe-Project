Title: DILL MUSTARD SAUCE - Country Living
Categories: Country liv, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
-prepared mustard
1/3 c  Chopped fresh dill
1/2 c  Light sour cream
1/2 c  Spicy brown or Dijon-style
In small bowl, whisk together sour cream and mustard until well
mixed. Stir in dill. Transfer to small serving bowl. Cover and
refrigerate until ready to serve.

Nutrition information per T-protein: .9 grain; fat. 1 gram;
carbohydrate: 1 gram; fiber: 0; sodium: 103 milligrams; cholesterol:
3 milligrams; calories: 17.
