Title: Mushrooms With Garlic And Lime
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
6    Garlic cloves, mashed
1/2 c  Fresh basil; chop coarsly
Salt and pepper
2 lb Mushrooms; fresh small ones
2 oz Butter
1/2 c  Olive oil; divided use
1/3 c  Fresh lime juice
Heat the butter and 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet. Add
mushrooms and saute 5 minutes. Transfer to a bowl. Add the remaining
oil and all the other ingredients, mix. Cool 6 hours in the fridge.
Serve cold.

Source: Hanoch Bar-Shalom, Yediot Acharonot 31-May-94. Translated by
Gabi Shahar, May 1994.
