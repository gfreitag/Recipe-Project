Title: Ham Special
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 2 servings
4 sl Pumpernickel bread
4 sl Ham
1 1/2 c  Coleslaw mix
1/3 c  Marie's thousand island
-dressing
In small bowl, combine coleslaw mix and dressing.  Divide mixture
evenly between 2 bread slices.  Top each with 2 slices of ham and
bread slice.
