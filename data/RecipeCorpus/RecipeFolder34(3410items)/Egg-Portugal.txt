Title: Egg Portugal
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
Squirt of mustard
6    Eggs, beaten
8 sl American cheese
1 lb Sausage
8 sl Bread, pinch up
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1    Soup can of milk
Fry sausage.  Drain well.  Crumble.  Put into a 9 x 13 inch baking
pan. Mix all remaining ingredients, except cheese, together.  Pour
over sausage. Put Saran Wrap on top, and put in refrigerator
overnight. Next day, pull off wrap and put on American cheese.  Bake
at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until golden brown. Randy Rigg
