Title: Saucy Beef Over Rice
Categories: Pasta/rice, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Water
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Beef sirloin steak (strips)
2 c  Hot cooked rice
1 lg Oven bag
2 tb Flour
1 cn 14.5 oz stewed tomatoes
1 pk Onion soup mix
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Shake flour in oven bag, place in 13x9
inch baking pan. Add drained stewed tomatoes, onion soup mix, water
and pepper to bag. Squeeze bag to blend in flour. Add beef strips to
bag. Turn bag to coat beef with sauce. Arrange ingredients in an even
layer. Close bag with nylon tie (cut six 1/2 inch slits in top of
bag). Bake until beef is tender, 40-45 minutes. Serve over rice.
