Title: Orange and Lentil-Rice Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Pasta/rice, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Soy sauce
1/2 c  Canola oil
2 tb Cider vinegar
MMMMM---------------------SALAD INGREDIENTS--------------------------
10 oz Canned Mandarin oranges
2 tb Green onions; chopped
1/2 c  Almonds; slivered
2 c  Cooked lentils
2 c  Cooked rice
10 oz Mushrooms; canned
1 c  Celery; chopped
In a container with a tight fitting lid combine oil, vinegar, lemon
juice and soy sauce. Shake well.

Combine lentils, rice and mushrooms. Pour dressing over the mixture,
cover and chill at least one hour. Just before serving, add celery,
oranges, onion and almonds.

From: "Discover The Pulse Potential" from the Saskatchewan Pulse Crop
