Title: SPINACH QUICHE WITH KASHA CRUST - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
Salt and pepper to taste
Pinch of freshly grated
-nutmeg
1 c  Grated Gruyere or cheddar
-cheese
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Kasha
4 c  Chopped fresh spinach
3    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Heavy cream, not
-ultra-pasteurized
Butter an 8 inch pie pan with 1 T butter and liberally coat the
bottom and sides with kasha, leaving leftover kasha on the bottom of
the pan.

In a skillet over medium heat, cook spinach in remaining T of butter
until just wilted and set aside.

In a bowl beat together eggs, cream, salt, pepper and nutmeg. Stir
spinach into egg mixture, pour into pie pan and top with cheese. Bake
quiche in a preheated 375'F oven for 25 to 30 minutes or until set.
If a very crisp crust is desired, set pan on top of stove over a low
burner for a few minutes. Serves 4 to 6.
