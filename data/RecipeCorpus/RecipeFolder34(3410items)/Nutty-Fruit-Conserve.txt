Title: Nutty Fruit Conserve
Categories: Fruit, Preserves, Etc., Country wom
Yield: 30 servings
1/2 ts Butter or margarine
8 oz Slivered almonds -- coarsely
Chopped
1    Pouch
Liquid fruit pectin -- 3 oz
5 c  Pear -- coarsely chopped
18 oz Dried apricots -- coarsely
Ground
15 oz Golden raisins
3 1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Almond extract
In a large kettle, combine the first six ingredients. cook and stir
over medium heat until mixture thickens, bout 20 min. adding the
almonds during the last 5 min. quickly stir in pectin. Bring to a
full rolling boil and boil for 1 min. stirring constantly. Remove
from the heat; skim off any foam. Pour hot into hot jars, leaving 1/4
inch headspace. Remove air bubbles. Adjust caps. Process for 15 min.
in a boiling-water batj/ Yield: 5 pints.
