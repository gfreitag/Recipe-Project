Title: Sunday Brunch Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Creme fraiche or sour cream
1/4 lb Smoked salmon; slice thin --
Cut-up
2 tb Fresh chives or green onion
Chopped
1 sm Red onion; slice thin --
Save
12    Rings for garnish
1 tb Olive oil
1/8 ts Salt
1    12" pizza crust
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat olive oil and add onion rings and salt;
cook until tender. Spread over pizza crust. Bake 5 minutes. Spread
creme fraiche or sour cream on hot pizza. Top with salmon. Sprinkle
chives or green onion over top and reserved onion rings around edge.
Per Slice: Cal 220, Fat 9gr.
