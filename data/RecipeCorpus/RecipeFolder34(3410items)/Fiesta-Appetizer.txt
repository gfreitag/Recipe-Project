Title: Fiesta Appetizer
Categories: Appetizers, Country wom
Yield: 10 servings
1 cn Green chiles -- chopped 4
Oz
2 md Tomatoes -- chopped
6 ea Green onion -- sliced
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese -- 4
Oz
Tortilla chips
1 cn Refried beans -- 16 oz
1 pk Taco seasoning mix -- 1 1/4
Oz
3 ea Avocados -- ripe
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Sour cream
1 cn Olives -- sliced 2 1/4 oz
Drained ripe
Combine beans and taco seasoning. spread mixture on a round 12 in.
serving placer. Mash avocados with lemon juice. Spread over beans.
Spread sour cream over avocado. Sprinkle olives, chilies, tomatoes,
onions and cheese over sour cream. Serve with tortilla chips. Yield:
8 to 10 servings.
