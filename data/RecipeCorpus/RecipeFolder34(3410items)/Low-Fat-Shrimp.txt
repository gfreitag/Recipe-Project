Title: Low Fat Shrimp
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 ts Paprika
ds Cayenne pepper
2 ts Coconut extract
2 lb Raw shrimp
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  Bread crumbs
3 tb Parsley, chopped
1 tb Minced garlic
3/4 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients, except shrimp, then toss with raw, peeled
shrimp, until coated.  Spread in a shallow 9 x 13 inch pan, sprinkle
extra crumbs on tops.  Bake, uncovered at 375 degrees for about 30 to
40 minutes, until tender. Randy Rigg
