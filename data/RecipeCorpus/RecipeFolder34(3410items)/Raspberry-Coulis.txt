Title: Raspberry Coulis
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Bon appetit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 c  Plus 2 T powdered sugar
2    (1/2 pint) baskets
-raspberries
Puree raspberries and powdered sugar in blender or processor until
smooth. Strain.  (Can be prepared 1 day ahead.  Cover and refrigerate.

Goes with Hazelnut Cake With Chocolate And Raspberries

Bon Appetit/May 94
