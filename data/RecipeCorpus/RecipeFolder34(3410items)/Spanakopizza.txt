Title: Spanakopizza
Categories: Pizza, Greek
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Marinara sauce
1    12" pizza crust
1 c  Feta cheese -- crumbled
1 tb Olive oil
1 c  Red onion -- sliced
10 oz Fresh spinach
2 ts Fresh mint -- or
1 tb Parsley -- minced and
1/2 ts Dried mint
Preheat oven to 450~. Heat oil and add onion and cook until just
tender, about 4 minutes. Add spinach; cover and cook until liquid is
evaporated, 2 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in mint (or parsley
combined with mint), salt and pepper. Spread sauce over crust. Spread
evenly with spinach mixture. Sprinkle with feta. Bake 10 minutes. Per
Slice: 230 Cal, 9 gr Fat.
