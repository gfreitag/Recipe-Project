Title: Reuben Baked Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1 tb Horseradish sauce --
Prepared
1 ts Caraway seed
1 pk Cream cheese -- softened
3 tb Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 ds Paprika
4 lg Baking potatoes
2 c  Corned beef brisket --
Cooked diced finely
1 cn Sauerkraut -- 14 oz drained
Chopped
1/2 c  Swiss cheese -- shredded
3 tb Green onion -- sliced
Bake the potatoes at 425 deg. for 45 min. or until tender. Cool. In a
bowl, combine the corned beef, sauerkraut, swiss cheese, onions,
garlic, horeradish and caraway. Cut potatoes in half lengthwise.
Carefully scoop out potatoes, leaving shells intact. Mash potatoes
with cream cheese; stir into the corned beef mixture. Mound potato
mixture into the shells. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and paprika.
Return to the oven for 25 min. or until heated through. Yield: 8
servings.
