Title: Hot Cheese Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Green chilies -- 4 oz.
Drained choppe
1 1/2 oz Sliced pepperoni
1/2 c  Sliced ripe olives
8 oz Shredded mozzrella cheese
8 oz Shredded sharp cheddar
Cheese
2 c  Mayonnaise
1 md Onion -- minced
In a shallow baking dish or pie plate, combine all ingredients except
pepperoni and olives. Top mixture with pepperoni and olives. Bake at
325 deg. about 25 minutes or until bubbly. Serve with rye chips,
crackers of choice or assorted fresh vegetables. Yield: 36 appetizer
servings
