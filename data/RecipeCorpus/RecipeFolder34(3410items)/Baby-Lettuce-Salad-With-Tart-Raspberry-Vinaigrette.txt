Title: Baby Lettuce Salad With Tart Raspberry Vinaigrette
Categories: Salads, Healthy
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Oregano -- chopped
1 ts Chives -- chopped
4 c  Baby lettuce leaves
2 tb Olive oil
4 tb Raspberry vinegar
2 tb Rich lamb stock
1 ds Black pepper -- to taste
Stir together oil, vinegar, and lamb stock in a small bowl. Add herbs
as close to serving time as possible. Toss with baby lettuce and
serve.
