Title: Peanut Butter and Fudge Swirl
Categories: Pie/tart, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Heavy cream, whipped
1/4 c  Chocolate fudge sauce
1    Oreo pie crust
1 pk Cream cheese (8 oz) soft
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Peanut butter
In bowl, with electric mixer, beat cream cheese, sugar and peanut
butter until smooth. Gradually fold in whipped cream, Spoon into
prepared crust. Drizzel fudge sauce over filling; draw a knife
through the sauce to swirl. Chill until firm, about four hours.
