Title: Persimmon Nut Dessert
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Half and half cream
1 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Soda
1/3 c  Melted butter
1 c  Chopped pecans
2 c  Persimmon pulp
2 c  Sugar
1 3/4 c  Flour
2    Eggs beaten
2 ts Baking powder
Combine pulp, sugar and eggs.  Add flour and baking powder
alternately with cream, buttermilk and soda.  Add butter, cinnamon
and nuts.  Stir well and pour into 13 x 9 x 3 inch pan which has been
buttered.  Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour. Randy Rigg
