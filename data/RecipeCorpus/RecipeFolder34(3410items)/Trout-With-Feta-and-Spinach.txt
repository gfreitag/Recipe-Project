Title: Trout With Feta and Spinach
Categories: Israeli, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 1 serving
1/2 c  Fresh spinach leaves
1    Trout; about 14 oz.
2 oz Feta cheese
Preheat oven to 180 C.

Cook spinach in salted boiling water for 2 to 3 minutes.  Drain and
run under cool water to stop cooking.  Squeeze all water out and chop
coarsely. Add feta cheese, mix well.

Clean the fish, and fill the cavity with the mixture.

Bake 15-20 minutes.

Source: Avi Goldman, chef in "Beetzem Dag", a fish restaurant in
Hertzliya, Israel.  Featured in the "Yediot Aharonot" newspaper, Food
section, May 1994.

Translated by Gabi Shahar, June 1994.
