Title: Cantaloupe And Raspberry Melba
Categories: Desserts, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Cantaloupe cubes -- or
Balls
1 c  Raspberries
Mint leaves, optional
1/2 c  Cranberry juice cocktall
1 tb Sugar
2 ts Cornstarch
1/4 ts Almond extract
In a saucepan, blend juice, sugar and cornstarch. Cook and stir over
medium heat until mixture is thickened. Stir in extract. Cool. When
ready to serve, combine cantaloupe and raspberries in individual
bowls. Top with cranbeny sauce and a mint garnish, if desired. Yield:
4-6 servings
