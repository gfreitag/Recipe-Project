Title: Seafood Chop Salad
Categories: Salads, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
Trout
Choose 3-4 of the following:
2    Eggs; sliced
1 cn Black olives; sliced
8    Mushrooms; sliced
1 md Tomato; core, dice
2    Green onions; thinly sliced
1/4 c  Walnuts; chopped
1 lg Head romaine lettuce
-- wash and tear
1/3 c  Salad dressing; your choice
1 lb Total
Shrimp
Crab meat
Lobster meat
Smoked salmon
Recipe by: Simply Seafood Preparation Time: 0:30 Stack the lettuce
leave, a few at a time, and slice crosswise very thin. Put them in a
large bowl and toss with enough dressing to lightly coat. Divide the
lettuce among individual large plates or shallow bowls. Equally
portion the seafood and garnished among the salads, arranging them in
a wagon-wheel pattern.

FALL 1994
Simply Seafood magazine, Vol. 4 No. 4
