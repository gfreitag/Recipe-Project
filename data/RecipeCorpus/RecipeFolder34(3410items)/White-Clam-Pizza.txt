Title: White Clam Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Romano cheese
1 tb Oregano -- crushed
1/2 ts Tabasco sauce
Olive oil
1 cn Refrigerated pizza dough
2 tb Cornmeal
1 cn Clams -- drained
3    Garlic cloves -- minced
1    Strips bacon -- cooked
Preheat oven to 450 F.  Prepare 12 inch round pizza crust according to
package directions.  Dust crust with cornmeal. Divide clams evenly
over dough.  Top evenly with garlic, bacon, Romano cheese, and
oregano. Drizzle with tobasco sauce and olive oil.  Bake 15 minutes
or until crust is golden brown.
