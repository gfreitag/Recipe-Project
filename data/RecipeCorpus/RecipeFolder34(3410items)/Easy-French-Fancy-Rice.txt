Title: Easy French-Fancy Rice
Categories: Kids, Side dish, Pasta/rice, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Milk
1 c  Swiss cheese; shredded (4oz)
1/4 ts Salt
Pepper to taste
1 lg Onion; diced
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 c  Cooked rice
2 ea Eggs; beaten
Preheat oven to 375F.  In a large skillet, saute the onion in the
butter until golden.  In a large bowl, mix the sauteed onion with the
remaining ingredients.  Pour the mixture into a greased 1 1/2-quart
baking dish and bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until lightly golden and
heated through. Note: Garnish with chopped parsley and paprika.
