Title: Raisin Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie, Snacks, Fruit
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 ts Each; baking powder, salt,
-and vanilla
1/2 c  Chocolate chips ( I leave
-them out)
1 c  Raisins (rinsed and drained)
1/3 c  Oil
1 ea Egg
1 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Flour
Beat egg lightly, stir in sugar and oil.  Blend in flour, baking
powder and salt.  Stir in vanilla, raisins, and chocolate chips.
Spread in a greased 8 inch square pan, and bake in 350F oven for
about 35 minutes. Cut while still warm.  Makes 16 squares Origin: Old
family friend Shared by: Sharon Stevens
