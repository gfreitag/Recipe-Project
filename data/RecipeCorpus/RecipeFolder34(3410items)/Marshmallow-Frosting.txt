Title: Marshmallow Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Light corn syrup
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
2    Egg whites -- at room temp.
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Sugar
Put the egg whites, sugar and corn syrup in the top of a double boiler
placed over rapidly boiling water and beat with a hand-held mixer
(electric or rotary) until soft peaks form, about 4 minutes.
Transfer the mixture to a medium size mixing bowl.  Add the vanilla
and beat with an electric mixer on medium high speed until soft peaks
form, about 30 seconds. Use immediately.
