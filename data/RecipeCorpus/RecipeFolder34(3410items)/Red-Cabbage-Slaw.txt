Title: Red Cabbage Slaw
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Pineapple juice
1/4 c  Golden raisins
3 ds Tabasco sauce
Salt and pepper
1 sm Red cabbage -- thinly
-sliced
1/2    Red bell pepper -- thinly
-sliced
1/4 c  Olive oil
Combine the cabbage and red peppers in a large bowl.  In a small bowl,
combine all the remaining ingredients and whisk together well. Pour
the dressing over the cabbage and pepper and toss well.
