Title: Salsa Romano
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetables
Yield: 3 cups
1 ts Garlic, minced
3 tb Olive oil
1 ts Salt
8 ea Roma tomatoes, diced
3 tb Red onion, diced
3/4 c  Basil leaves, julienned
Thoroughly combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl.  Allow to rest
at room temperature for about an hour before serving.

This salsa has no heat at all but is excellent with pasta or on
grilled bruschetta or pizza.

Mark Miller, "The Great Salsa Book" reprinted in "Vegetarian Gourmet"
Summer 1995
