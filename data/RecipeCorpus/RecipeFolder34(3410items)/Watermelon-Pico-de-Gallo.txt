Title: Watermelon Pico de Gallo
Categories: Fruit, Vegetables, Tex/mexican, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Diced red oino
2 ea Tbps freshly squeezed lime
-juice
1 ea Fresh jalapeno pepper,
-seeded and finely chopped
1/2 ts Salt, or to taste
1 1/2 c  Diced watermelon
1/2 c  Diced jicama
1/2 c  Chopped fresh cilantro
-leaves
1/4 c  Diced honeydew melon
1/4 c  Diced cantaloupe
In large bowl, toss together all ingredients and allow to chill
thoroughly before serving.  Can be served with grilled or barbecue
chicken or pork. Origin: Cooking with Regis and Kathie Lee. Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Aug/95.
