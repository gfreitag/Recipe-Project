Title: No-Crust Pizza
Categories: Low cal/fat, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
ds Pepper
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 lb Ground beef
2 oz Shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 c  Pizza sauce
Sliced mushrooms (optional)
1    Egg -- slightly beaten
1/4 c  Water
2 sl Diet bread -- in soft
Crumbs
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Salt
Combine first 7 ingredients in large bowl, and let stand 5 minutes.
Add beef; mix well.  Pat into 9" pie plate to form crust. Top with
1/2 of the cheese, pizza sauce and mushrooms. Bake in 350^ oven 45
minutes. Top with remaining cheese; bake 5 minutes. Remove to serving
platter, using two spatulas. Weight Watchers: Each Serving = 2 oz.
meat, 1/4 egg, 1/2 oz. cheese,1/4 bread, 1/4 bonus
