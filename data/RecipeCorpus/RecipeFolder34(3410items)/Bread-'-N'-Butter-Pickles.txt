Title: Bread ' N' Butter Pickles
Categories: Pickles, Relishes, Country wom, Can/preserv
Yield: 100 servings
6 c  Vinegar
6 c  Sugar
2 ts Celery seed
2 ts Mustard seed
1 ts Ground turmeric
8 lb Cucumbers -- about 12 large
Cut into 1/4 inch slices
8 lg Onions -- cut 1/8 inch
Sliced
4 lg Green peppers -- sliced
2/3 c  Salt -- canning
In a large container, combine cucumbers, onions, green peppers and
salt. Add enough cold water to cover. Chill for 2 hours. Drain and
rinse. In a large kettle, combine remaining ingredients; bring to
boil. Add cucumber mixture; return to a boil. Ladle hot into hot
jars, leaving 1/4 in. headspace. Adjust lids. Process for 10 min. in
a boiling-water bath. Yield: 10 pints.
