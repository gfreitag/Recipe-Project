Title: Pasta With Asparagus (2)
Categories: Vegetables, Pasta/rice, Country wom
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Softened
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated or
2 lb Asparagus -- fresh slice 1
In
1 lb Spaghetti -- very thin
8 sl Bacon -- cut 1 in. pieces
1/2 c  Green onion -- sliced
3/4

Cooked asparagus in boiling salted water 3 min. Drain and set aside.
Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Drain and return to
kettle to keep warm. Meanwhile, in a skillet, cook bacon until crisp.
Remove to a paper towed. In bacon drippings, saute onion until soft.
Add asparagus and pepper; heat through. Quicky toss together
spaghetti, asparagus mixture, bacon, butter, milk and cheese. Serve
immediately. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
