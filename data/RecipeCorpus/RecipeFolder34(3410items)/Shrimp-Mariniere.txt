Title: Shrimp Mariniere
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
Boiling water
1 c  Ketchup
Salt and pepper to taste
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Dash of tabasco sauce
2    Slices of bacon
1 1/2 c  Diced onions
1    Green pepper, seeded and
Diced
2    Stalks celery, chopped
1 lb Shrimp, peeled and deveined
1) Cook bacon in frying pan until crisp; drain on paper towels and set
aside.

2) Saute onion until golden in remaining bacon fat. Add green pepper
and celery; cover and simmer for 5 minutes.

3) Add shrimp and just enough boiling water to cover. Add ketchup,
salt, pepper, worcestershire sauce, and tabasco sauce; simmer for 30
minutes, or until thicken.

4) Sprinkle with crumbled bacon, and serve hot.
