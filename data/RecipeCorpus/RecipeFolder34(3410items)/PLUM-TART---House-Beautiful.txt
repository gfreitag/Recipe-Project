Title: PLUM TART - House Beautiful
Categories: House beaut, Fruit, Pie/tart, Desserts
Yield: 1 tart
-vanilla extract
7 tb Sugar
2 tb All-purpose flour
1 ts Unsalted butter
1    Recipe Flaky Butter Pastry
Flour for rolling
1 lb Ripe plums
1 ts Kirsch, framboise, or
Roll out the dough as directed for the desired tart size. Transfer the
dough to a baking sheet and refrigerate while you prepare the plums.

Slice the plums in half through the stem and remove the pits. Slice
each half into 1/4-inch slices. You should have about 3 C of fruit.
Place the plums in a bowl and toss with the kirsch and 6 T of sugar.
Set mixture aside.

In a small bowl, combine the 2 tablespoons of flour with the remaining
sugar.

Remove the dough from the refrigerator and sprinkle the flour-sugar
mixture over the dough, leaving a border uncovered as directed above.
Arrange the plum slices evenly over the flour-sugar mixture. Fold the
rim of dough over the plums, crimping the seams lightly with
moistened fingers. Shave the butter on top of the plums.

Bake the tart in a preheated 400'F oven as directed above.
