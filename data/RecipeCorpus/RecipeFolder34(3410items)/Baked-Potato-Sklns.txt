Title: Baked Potato Sklns
Categories: Appetizers, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Choppe
1/2 c  Ham -- cooked
Or shrimp (optlonal)
1/2 c  Green peppers -- chopped
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- 4 oz.
Shredded
1 c  Sour cream
8 ea Baking potatoes
1/2 c  Butter melted
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 c  Green onions -- finely
Chopped
1/2 c  Bacon -- cooked finely
Bake potatoes at 400 deg. for 1 hour or until tender. Cool slightly;
cut in half lengthwise and scoop out pulp, leaving 1/4 in. of pulp
still attached to skin. (The potato pulp you remove can be
refrigerated for use later.) Cut skins into strips or halves; brush
skin sides with melted butter and place on a baking sheet. Sprinkle
pulp sides with salt and paprika; cover with the green onions, bacon,
ham or shrimp, and chopped peppers. Top with the cheese. Bake at 450
deg. until cheese is melted and skins are crisp, about 10-15 minutes.
Garnish each skin with a dollop of sour cream. Serve immediately.
Yield: 6-10 servings
