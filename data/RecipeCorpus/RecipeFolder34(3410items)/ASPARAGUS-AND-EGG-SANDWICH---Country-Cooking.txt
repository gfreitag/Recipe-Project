Title: ASPARAGUS AND EGG SANDWICH - Country Cooking
Categories: Country coo, Appetizers, Sandwich
Yield: 4 sandwiches
-salad dressing
2    Slices white bread, crusts
-removed, cut in half
1    Hard-cooked egg, finely
-shredded
8    To 12 stalks fresh
-asparagus, trimmed
-(about 1/2 lb)
1 tb Water
2 tb Prepared Dijon-vinaigrette
1. To microwave asparagus, arrange tips toward the center of a
microwave safe shallow casserole. Add water; cover and cook on high
(100 percent), 2 to 4 minutes until crisp-tender. Let stand 2
minutes. Drain.

2. Pour dressing over asparagus and marinate for 20 minutes. Lightly
drain asparagus and trim to fit bread. on serving platter or 4
sandwich plates, place bread, Divide asparagus among pieces of bread;
sprinkle with egg.

Country Cooking/Summer/94  Scanned & fixed by Di and Gary
