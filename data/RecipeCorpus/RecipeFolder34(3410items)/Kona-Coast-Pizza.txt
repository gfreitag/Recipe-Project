Title: Kona Coast Pizza
Categories: Appetizers, Pizza
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Ham; thinly sliced --
Strips
1 tb Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
1 tb Green onion -- thinly
Sliced
2 tb Pizza sauce
1/4 ts Hoisin sauce
1    English muffin; split --
Toast
1 tb Water chestnuts -- chopped
1 tb Red bell pepper -- chopped
2 tb Pineapple -- crushed
In small bowl, combine pizza sauce and hoisin asuce; spread on English
muffin halves.  Top with water chestnuts, bell pepper, pineapple, ham,
cheese, and green onion. Bake in preheated 450 F oven for 5 minutes.
