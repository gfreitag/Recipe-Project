Title: Hearty Cheese 'n' Toast
Categories: Luncheon, Country wom
Yield: 1 servings
Shredded
6 sl White bread
Or whole wheat , toasted
6 sl Cooked turkey
Chicken, beef or ham,
Optional
Chopped fresh parsley
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb All-purpose flour
2/3 c  Mllk
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Whole caraway seeds
Pepper to taste
2 c  Sharp cheddar cheese -- 8 oz
In a saucepan, melt butter over medium high heat. Stir in flour to
form a smooth paste. Gradually stir in milk. Cook and stir until the
mixture is smooth and thickened. Add the Worcestershire sauce,
mustard, caraway and pepper. Stir until well blended. Add the cheese;
cook and stir over low heat until melted. Cut toast diagonally and
place on individual plates. If desired, top each with a slice of
meat. Spoon cheese sauce over meat and toast. Sprinkle with parsley.
Serve immediately. Yield: 4 servings
