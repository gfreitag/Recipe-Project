Title: Party Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 recipe
1 lg Pkg. dried beef
4    Beaten eggs
1 lb Velveeta or American cheese
2 1/3 c  Tomatoes (1 #2 can)
2 ts Heaping - chili powder
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
MMMMM-------------------------SERVE WITH------------------------------
Potato puffs
Crackers
Chips
Squeeze tomatoes through sieve.  Simmer 10 minutes.  Add chili
powder and Worcestershire sauce.  Cut pkg dried beef fine.  Add.
Heat thoroughly. Remove from heat.  Add eggs.  Mix thoroughly; return
to heat; cook until thick.  Add cheese (cut up fine).  Put all in
double boiler and cook until thick.
