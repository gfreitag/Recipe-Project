Title: Kijiga
Categories: Beverages, Native amer, Cree
Yield: 1 recipe
10 c  Water
1 c  Maple sugar
Pit all ingredients into a deep saucepan & simmer for 20 to 25
minutes, stirring gently.  Serve either hot or ice cold.

Apparently this drink can be served either with cognac or p'tit
caribou, a liqueur known to many tribes & nations.

Bernard Assiniwi, "Indian Recipes"
