Title: Sweet Potatoes w/ Honey Pepper Butter
Categories: Potatoes, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Honey
1/4 ts Ground Cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground Pepper
6 md Sweet potatoes,scrubbed
1 tb Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Butter, at room temperature
Rub sweet potatoes with oil.

To Grill: Put potatoes 4 to 6 inches above hot coals. Cover with foil
tent. Grill 45 minutes to 1 hour, turning several times, until soft.

To Bake: Heat oven to 425 degrees. Bake potatoes 45 to 55 minutes.

Meanwhile mix butter with honey, cinnamon and pepper.

To serve, cut slits in tops and spoon some seasoned butter into each.
