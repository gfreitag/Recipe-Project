Title: Orange Cinnamon Oatmeal Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Desserts, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 1/2 tb Orange zest -- grated
2 tb Oil
2 tb Honey
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/3 c  Water
2 1/4 ts Active dry yeast
1 2/3 c  Bread flour
3/4 c  Whole-wheat flour
3/4 c  Rolled oats
3 tb Wheat germ
3 tb Powdered milk
Add all the ingredients in the order suggested by your manual and
bake on the basic bread cycle.
