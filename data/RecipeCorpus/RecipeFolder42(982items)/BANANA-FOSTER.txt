Title: BANANA FOSTER
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
6    To 12 scoops vanilla
-ice cream
3 lg Bananas
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1 pn Clove
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 oz Rum
1 oz Brandy
1 oz Creme de banana
Split and slice bananas into small pieces.  Melt butter over low
heat, add sugar, spices and creme de banana (if used) and bring to a
boil. Heat bananas in sauce and pour brandy and rum over mixture,
then ignite. Serve over vanilla ice cream after flames subside.
Serves 6 (use 6 scoops ice cream for one scoop person, 12 scoops for
2 per person).

>From _La Bonne Cuisine, Cooking New Orleans Style_
