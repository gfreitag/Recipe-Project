Title: BARLEY CHEESE SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Broccoli pieces
1 ts Salt
3 1/2 c  Water
1    Clove garlic, minced
1 tb Chicken soup mix
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Carrots, sliced
1 1/2 c  Skim milk
1    Onion, chopped
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Barley
4 oz Cheddar cheese, diced
Combine all but last 3 ingredients in 3 qt pot.  Bring to boil, then
simmer 15 minutes till barley is tender. Mix glour into 1/2 c milk
and add. Add rest of milk. Cook about five minutes till thickened.
Remove from heat and stir in cheese.  Cover and let stand 3-5
