Title: BASIL VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Salads, Sauces, Seafood
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cl Garlic
1/2 ts Dijon-style mustard
1/4 c  Basil or tarragon vinegar
2/3 c  Basil olive oil OR
-olive oil
1 c  Loosely packed fresh basil,
Rinsed and spun dry
In a blender blend the garlic, the mustard, the vinegar, and salt to
taste; add the oil and basil, and blend the mixture until it is
emulsified. Serve the vinaigrette over sliced tomatoes or with
grilled scallops or other seafood.  Makes about 1 cup.
