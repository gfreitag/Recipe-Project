Title: BANANA CREAM CHEESE PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Vanilla wafer crumbs
1/4 c  Sugar
6 tb Butter, melted
16 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
1/2 c  Bananas, mashed
Banana, sliced
Beat the softened cream cheese with an electric mixer until light and
fluffy. Gradually add the sugar, mixing well after each addition. Add
the eggs one at a time, beating after each. Stir in the mashed
bananas. Pour filling into a cooled pie shell and place sliced banana
on top. Bake for 50 minutes in a preheated 350 degree oven. Cool on a
wire rack. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
