Title: BANANA-PEACH SHAKE
Categories: Beverages, Vegetables
Yield: 3 Servings
3/4 c  Skim milk
2/3 c  Sliced ripe banana
1/2 c  Peeled sliced fresh peach
1 1/2 c  Vanilla nonfat frozen yogurt
Combine first 3 ingredients in container of an electric blender;
cover and process until smooth. Add frozen yogurt; process until
smooth. Yield: 3 cups (serving size: 1 cup). - - - - - - - - - - - -
~ - - - ~ - Serving Ideas : Serve immediately. Nutr. Assoc.
