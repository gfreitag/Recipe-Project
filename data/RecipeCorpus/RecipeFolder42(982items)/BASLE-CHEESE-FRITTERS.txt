Title: BASLE CHEESE FRITTERS
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Slices bread
1 oz Butter
3    Onions
4    Slices Gruyere or Emmenthal
-cheese
Paprika
Fry bread lightly on both sides in butter and arrange on a baking
sheet. Pour boiling water over finely chopped onions and leave for a
moment. Pour off water, and fry onions in the remains of the butter
until tender. Spread onions thinly on the bread and cover each slice
with a slice of cheese. Sprinkle with paprika and bake in a very hot
oven (445 degrees F/Gas mark 8) until cheese melts. Serve at once.
