Title: BASIC FRENCH DRESSING
Categories: Sauces, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Oil
1/4 c  Wine vinegar
3 tb Water
2 1/2 ts Sugar
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts MSG (optional)
1 ts Black pepper
2 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 1/4 ts Hungarian paprika
1 ts Grated onion
2 oz Blue cheese (optional)
Combine oil, vinegar and water and beat until blended. Add sugar,
mustard, MSG, pepper, salt, Worcestershire and paprika and beat until
blended. Add onion and beat well. If desired, stir in cheese.

Makes 1 3/4 cups

This dressing takes kindly to the addition of crumbled blue cheese if
you feel the need.
