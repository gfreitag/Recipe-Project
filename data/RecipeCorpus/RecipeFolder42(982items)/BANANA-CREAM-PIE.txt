Title: BANANA CREAM PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
1 ea Banana
1 ea Baked 9" pie shell,cooked
2 pk Jello Vanilla or Banana Crea
1 x  Instant Pudding and Pie Fill
2 1/2 - cups cold milk
2 - cups thawed Kool Whip topping Slice banana into pie
shell.Prepare pie filling as directed on package for pie,using 2 1/2
cups milk.Fold in 1/2 cup of the Whipped topping and pour over banana
in shell.Chill at least 3 hours.Top with remaining whipped
topping;garnish with banana slices and mint,if desired
