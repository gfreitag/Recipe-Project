Title: BASIC FORMULA FOR A ONE POUND LOAF
Categories: Abm
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 c  Liquid
1 tb Sweetener
1/2 ts Salt
3 c  Flour (combination of any 2
-or more white, oat, rye, or
-whole wheat bread flour)
1 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
Follow manufacturers instructions for macking bread.

Total calories per serving: 107  Fat <1 gram
