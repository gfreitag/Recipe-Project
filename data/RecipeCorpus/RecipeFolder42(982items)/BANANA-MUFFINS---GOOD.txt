Title: BANANA MUFFINS - GOOD
Categories: Bananas, Breads, Desserts, Fat free, Muffins
3/4 c  Sugar
Yield: 12 Servings
1 1/4 c  Flour
3/4 ts Baking Soda
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Egg Beaters - Fat Free
3    Banana -- mashed

Mix dry ingred.  Add wet ingred. Bake at 350 for 20 min.

NOTES : Per Muffin - Calories - 115.3, Total Fat - .2g, 1.7% CFF
