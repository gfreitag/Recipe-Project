Title: BANANA MILK
Categories: Beverages, Fruits, Mcdougall
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Banana; peeled and sliced
1 c  Water
1/2 ts Vanilla extract; optional
Recipe by: The New McDougall Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:05 Place
all of the ingredients in a blender and process until smooth. From
the collection of Sue Smith, S.Smith34, Uploaded June 16, 1994
