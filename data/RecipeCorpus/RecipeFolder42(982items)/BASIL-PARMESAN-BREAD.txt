Title: BASIL PARMESAN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/8 c  Water
1 1/2 tb Olive oil
1/3 c  Grated swiss cheese
1/3 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1/3 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Basil
1 1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
Add ingredients in order suggested by manufacturer and process on
whole wheat cycle if machine has that option. 1-1/2 lb. loaf.
