Title: BANANA WHEAT BREAD - SANDY
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 15 Servings
-JNPM71A
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Water
1    Egg
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 ts Vanilla
2    Ripe bananas; sliced
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Poppy seeds (optional)
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 c  Wheat flour
1 tb Yeast
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
I add the walnuts at the beginning with other ingred. It seems to mix
better. You can also add 1/2 tsp of cinnamon if you like. We like it
better without. Sandy in Arroyo Grande, CA 8:49am PST
