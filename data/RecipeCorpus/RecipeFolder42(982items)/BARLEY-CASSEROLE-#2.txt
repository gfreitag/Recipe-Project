Title: BARLEY CASSEROLE #2
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms
1 lg Onion, finely chopped
4 tb Butter
1 c  Barley
Salt and pepper
3 c  Beef broth
Slivered almonds, toasted
Saute mushrooms and onions in butter. Don't let onions brown. Mix in
barley, salt and pepper. Pour into buttered 1 1/2 qt casserole. Cover
with broth to 1/2 inch above barley. Cover and bake at 350 degrees
for 45 minutes, until liquid is absorbed and barley is tender.
Garnish with almonds.

SOURCE: National Cooking Echo 04/19/90 Contributed to the echo by:
