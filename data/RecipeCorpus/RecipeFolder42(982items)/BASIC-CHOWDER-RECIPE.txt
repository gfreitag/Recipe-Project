Title: BASIC CHOWDER RECIPE
Categories: Camping, Resort
Yield: 1 Servings
1 sl Bacon
1/8 sm Onion
1/2 md Potatoe
1/4 cn Corn
1/4 lb Fish
1 c  Water or milk
Cut bacon and onion small.  Fry in pot until brown. Add corn, fish or
meat with a little water.   Cook slowly until cooked. Add potatoes
about 1/2 hr before serving and cook until done. Season and if using
milk add just before serving.  Heat but do not boil.
