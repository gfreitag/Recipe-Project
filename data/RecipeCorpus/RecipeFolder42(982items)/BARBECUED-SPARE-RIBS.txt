Title: BARBECUED SPARE RIBS
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Spare ribs
1 ea Onion
2 tb Fat or salad oil
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Vinegar
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Brown Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Chili sauce
Place ribs in baking dish and bake in 350 oven 30 min.Chop
onion;brown in fat or salad oil. Add juice, vinegar, worcestershire
sauce, brown sugar,water, chili sauce, salt and pepper if you wish.
Cook slowly 20 min.Pour over ribs; continue baking 1 hour.
