Title: BARLEY-AMARANTH WAFFLES
Categories: Breads, Quick, Breakfast, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Waffles
3/4 c  AM Amaranth Flour
1 c  AM Barley Flour
1 tb Non-alum baking powder
1/4 ts Sea salt (optional)
1 1/2 c  Milk, soymilk, or water
3 tb AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil
Mix liquids and dry ingredients separately, then beat together with a
hand mixer.  Pour onto hot, non-stick surface griddle. Thin batter
with additional water if needed. Makes four waffles. May be used for
pancakes also.
