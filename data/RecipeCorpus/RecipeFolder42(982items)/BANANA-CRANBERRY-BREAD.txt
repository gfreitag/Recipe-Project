Title: BANANA-CRANBERRY BREAD
Categories: Breads, Abm, Fat-free, Quickbreads, Fruits
Yield: 1 Loaf
MMMMM------------------1-POUND RECIPE (8 SLICES-----------------------
1 1/2 ts Yeast
3/4 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Multi-grain oatmeal
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
3 tb Molasses
2 tb Lite applesauce
1/4 c  Egg substitute
3/4 c  Mashed bananas
3/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Dried cranberries
MMMMM-----------------1-1/2-POUND RECIPE (12 SL----------------------
2 1/2 ts Yeast
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Multi-grain oatmeal
3 c  Bread flour
1/4 c  Molasses
3 tb Lite applesauce
1/4 c  Egg substitute
1    Egg white
1 c  Mashed bananas
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Dried cranberries
Add ingredients in the order suggested by the bread machine
manufacturer and follow instructions provided in the manual.

Source: "Recipes for Fat-free Living: Breads for Bread Machines" by
Jyl Steinback; 1996. MM format by Mary Ann Young
