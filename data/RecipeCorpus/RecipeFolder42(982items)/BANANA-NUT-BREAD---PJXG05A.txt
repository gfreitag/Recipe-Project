Title: BANANA NUT BREAD - PJXG05A
Categories: Breads, Favorite, Gifts, Nuts, Quickbreads
Yield: 8 Servings
3/4 c  Margarine; or butter
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs; separated
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
3    Over-ripe bananas
1 c  Pecans; chopped
2 c  All-purpose flour
Cream margarine and sugar; add egg yolks (only) and salt. Add flour
and blend. Combine soda with the mashed bananas. Fold in well-beaten
egg whites. Add nuts. Pour into 2 small or 1 large greased loaf pans.
Bake at 350 degreees for 30 to 40 minutes or until toothpick inserted
in center comes out clean. Source: Greenville, SC Jr. League Cookbook
Judy Garnett
