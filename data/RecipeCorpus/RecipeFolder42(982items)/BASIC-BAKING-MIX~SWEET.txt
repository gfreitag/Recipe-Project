Title: BASIC BAKING MIX~SWEET
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
3 1/4 c  All-purpose flour --
Unsifted
2 1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 c  Cornstarch
1 tb Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 ts Salt
Sift together all ingredients. Store in tightly covered container at
cool room temperature. Stir before using.Makes 7 cups mix.
