Title: BAPTIST POUND CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Pound
Yield: 10 Servings
3 c  Sugar
1    Stick margarine
1/2 c  Crisco
5    Eggs, rm temp
Pinch of salt
1 c  Milk
3 c  Plain flour
1 ts Canilla
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 tb Butternut flavoring
Cream together sugar, butter, and shortening; add eggs, one at a
time. Mix well after each. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt; add
alternately with milk and flour to first mixture. Add flavorings.
Grease and flour pan. Place in cold over. Turn heat to 350F, and bake
1/2 hour. then turn heat to 325F and bake 40 minutes more. Let stand
5 minutes after taking from oven.
