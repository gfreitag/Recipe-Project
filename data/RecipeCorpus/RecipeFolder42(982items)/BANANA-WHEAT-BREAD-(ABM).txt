Title: BANANA WHEAT BREAD (ABM)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 pk Yeast
1 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 c  Wheat flour
1/2 ts Salt
2 md Ripe bananas, sliced
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Warm water
1    Egg
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 ts Poppy seeds (optional)
Add ingredients into the pan in the order listed, select white bread,
and push start.
