Title: BASIC HARDTACK
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Flour
1/2 tb Salt (optional)
1/2 tb Sugar (optional)
1/2 c  Water
Basic HardTack

Mix flour, salt (optional), sugar (optional), and water. Using hands
or rolling pin, flatten dough on floured cloth until 1/4-inch thick.
Score with a knife if desired. Bake on cookie sheet in 350-degree
oven for 30 minutes. Break into pieces as needed.
