Title: BANANA ICE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
1    Banana mashed
3/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Pineapple juice
2 tb Lemon juice
Put sugar and water in glass bowl and microwave on high 3 to 4 minutes
until boiling.  Stir in remaining ingredients and pour into ice trays.
Freeze. From 365 microwave recipe calendar 1990
