Title: BASIC STRAINED YOGURT
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
Strainer
Bowl
ORange
1 sm Sieve lined with
100    %-cotton cheese cloth
Place  2 cups plain nonfat yogurt in a yogurt strainer over a bowl.
(Or, use a small sieve lined with a piece of 100-percent-cotton
cheesecloth.) Cover and let drain in the refrigerator for 5 hours or
overnight. Makes about 1 cup. Eating Right, Living Well 1993 This can
be used in place of cream cheese for many recipes with the result
being much less fat and cholesterol.
