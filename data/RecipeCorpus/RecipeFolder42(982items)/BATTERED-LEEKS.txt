Title: BATTERED LEEKS
Categories: Low-cal, Dairy
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Leeks; washed & diced + grn.
4    Eggs
2/3 c  Dry milk pwd; instant
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4    Tomatoes; cherry
1    Parsley sprig
Spray nonstick skillet with cooking spray and set over medium heat;
add 2 cups leeks, cover skillet and cook, stirring occasionally,
until leeks are softened, 3-4 min. In small mixing bowl beat together
2 eggs, 1/3 c milk powder, half of the salt and pepper.  Pour into
skillet, making sure egg mixture thoroughly coats leeks. Cook until
bottom of omelet is golden, 1 to 2 minutes; using pancake turner,
carefully turn omelet over. Cook other side just until set, about 1
minute; transfer to serving platter and keep warm.  Repeat procedure;
garnish omelets with tomatoes and parsley.
