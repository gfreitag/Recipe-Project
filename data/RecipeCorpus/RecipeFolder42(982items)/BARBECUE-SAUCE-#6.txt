Title: BARBECUE SAUCE #6
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
2 tb Paprika
2 ts Salt
2 ts Dry mustard
2    Bay leaves
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
2 c  Tomato juice
1/2 c  Catsup
1 c  Water
Mix together brown sugar, paprika, salt, dry mustard, bay leaves,
chili powder, cayenne pepper, Worcestershire, vinegar, tomato juice,
catsup and water in large saucepan.
Place over high heat and bring to boil.  Reduce heat to simmer and
cook until slightly thickened, about 30 minutes. Discard bay leaves.
Makes about 4 cups.
