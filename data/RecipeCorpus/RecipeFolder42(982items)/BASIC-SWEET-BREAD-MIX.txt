Title: BASIC SWEET BREAD MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
10 c  All-purpose flour
6 1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Cornstarch
5 tb Baking powder
1 tb Salt
2 1/2 c  Vegetable shortening
In a large sifter, combine flour, sugar, cornstarch ,baking powder and
salt. Sift, in batches, into a large bowl. Use a pastry blender to a
heavy-duty mixer to blend in shortening until mixture resembles
cornmeal in texture. Spoon into a 20-cup container with a
tight-fitting lid. Seal container. Label with date and contents.
Store in a cool, dry place. Use within 10 to 12 weeks. Makes about 17
cups of mix.
