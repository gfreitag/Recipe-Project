Title: BANANA-PINEAPPLE BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 2 Loaves
1 c  Chopped nuts
2 1/2 c  Sugar
5 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 c  Vegetable oil
3    Eggs
3    Bananas, mashed
1 ts Lemon juice
1 cn Crushed pineapple, drained
Mix together dry ingredients.  Mix together liquid ingredients;
combine. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
