Title: BARBEQUE SAUCE #28
Categories: Barbeque, Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Oil (Canola)
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Onion
1/4 ts Oregeno
1/3 c  Vinegar (Apple Cider)
1/2 c  Fruit juices
1 ea Hot pepper
2 ea Garlic cloves
Combine all ingredients except oil in blender.  Blend until smooth.
Place blended ingredients and oil in saucepan. Boil for 7 to eight
minutes.

Source:  The Home Book of Barbecue Cooking.  Fawcett Publications,
1963.
