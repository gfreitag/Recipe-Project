Title: BASIC QUICK BREAD MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
10 c  All-purpose flour
1/3 c  Baking powder
1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Salt
1 ts Salt
2 c  Shortening
Combine dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Divide flour mixture
and shortening in half.
Cut 1 cup shortening into half of flour mixture
pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse meal.
with a
Repeat with remaining shortening and flour mixture. Combine mixtures.
Store in an airtight container at room temperature up to 6 weeks.
Refrigerate or freeze for
longer storage. Yield: 13 cups
