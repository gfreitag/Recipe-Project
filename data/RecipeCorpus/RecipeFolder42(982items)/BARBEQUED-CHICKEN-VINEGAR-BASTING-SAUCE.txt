Title: BARBEQUED CHICKEN VINEGAR BASTING SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/4 c  Cider vinegar
4 ts Chili powder
2 1/2 ts Sugar
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Cumin, ground
Whisk all ingredients in small bowl to blend.  Arrange chicken in
shallow glass dis.  Spoon 1/4 cup sauce over; turn to coat evenly.
Let stand at least 10 minues and up to 20 minutes, turning chicken
occasionally.

Grill chicken until just cooked through, basting occasionally with
another
1/4    cup sauce.

Serve with remaining sauce.
