Title: BANANA-STRAWBERRY SHAKE
Categories: New, Text, Import
1 c  ice cubes
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  skim milk
1/2    very ripe banana
1/2 c  whole strawberries
3 TB instant nonfat dry milk
few drops vanilla extract
:

In blender, mix together ice cubes, skim milk, banana, strawberries,
nonfat dry milk and extract. Blend on high speed until ice is crushed
and mixture is slushy and serve immediately.
