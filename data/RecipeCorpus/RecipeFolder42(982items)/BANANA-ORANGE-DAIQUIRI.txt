Title: BANANA-ORANGE DAIQUIRI
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 5 Servings
1 1/2 c  Sliced banana
1/2 c  Light rum
1/2 c  Bottled sweet-and-sour
-cocktail mix
1/4 c  Thawed orange juice
-concentrate
30    Ice cubes
Orange slices; (optional)
Place first 4 ingredients in blender, and process until smooth. With
blender on, add ice cubes, 1 at a time, and process until smooth.
Yield: 5 cups (serving size: 1 cup). - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
~ - Serving Ideas : Garnish with an orange slice, if desired.

NOTES : Serve immediately.
