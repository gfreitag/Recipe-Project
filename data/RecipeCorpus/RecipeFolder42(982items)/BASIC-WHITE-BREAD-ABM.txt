Title: BASIC WHITE BREAD ABM
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
2 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
1 tb Granulated sugar
1 tb Nonfat dry milk
1 tb Butter/margarine
3/4 c  Plus 1T water
Automatic Bread Program:

Press SELECT for Normal Press START. Bread will be ready in 3 hours.
After baking, press STOP/RESET. Remove bread from machine. Cool.
