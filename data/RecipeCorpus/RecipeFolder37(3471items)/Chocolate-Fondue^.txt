Title: Chocolate Fondue^
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread, Holiday
Yield: 8 servings
Milk
1/3 c  Milk
8 oz Chopped semisweet chocolate
1 cn (14oz) sweetened condensed
MMMMM--------------------------DIPPERS-------------------------------
Dried apricots
Whole strawberries
Banana slices
Pineapple chunks
Angel cake
lb Cake
Brownie squares
Marshmallows
Melt chocolate. Stir in condensed milk and milk; heat through.
Transfer to fondue pot; keep warm.
Serve immediately. Stir in additional warm milk as needed to thin.
