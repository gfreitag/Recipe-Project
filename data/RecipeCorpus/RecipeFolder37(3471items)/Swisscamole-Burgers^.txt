Title: Swisscamole Burgers^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
4 sl Bacon, cooked and crushed
4 sl Swiss cheese
4    Hamburger buns
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 1/2 ts Salt, divided
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Ripe avocados
1 tb Grated onion
Combine meat, salt and pepper. Make 8 small patties. Mix together
avocados, salt, onion, lemon juice, mayonnaise and bacon. Pplace a
slice of cheese on 4 of the patties and top with avocado mixture. Top
with plain patties pressing edges to seal. Cook as desired. Serve on
buns.
