Title: CAP'N'S COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt (opt)
2 c  CAP'N CRUNCH Cereal, any
-flavor, coarsely crushed
1 c  Raisins or semi-sweet
-chocolate pieces
1 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Margarine or butter,
-softened
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
Heat oven to 375'F. Lightly grease cookie sheet. Beat sugar and
margarine until fluffy. Blend in eggs and vanilla. Add combined
flour, baking powder and salt; mix well. Stir in cereal and raisins.
Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto prepared cookie sheet. Bake 10-12
minutes or until light golden brown. Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet;
remove to wire rack. Cool completely. Store tightly covered.

Makes about 3 dozen cookies.
