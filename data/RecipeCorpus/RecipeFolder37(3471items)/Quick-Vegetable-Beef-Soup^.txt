Title: Quick Vegetable Beef Soup^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Soup/stew, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 10 servings
1 pk (20oz) frozen mixed
Vegetables, thawed
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Pepper
1 1/2 lb Ground beef, browned with
1/3 c  Instant minced onion and
Drained
1 cn (46oz) tomato juice
2    Beef bouillon cubes
Bring all ingredients to a boil. Simmer a half hour or so until
vegetables are tender.
