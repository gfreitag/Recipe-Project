Title: Pizza Bread (A B M)
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese, shredded
1    Egg
1 c  Bottled pizza sauce
1 ts Whole fennel seed
1/8 ts Ground black pepper
1 ts Oregano, dry
3 tb Hot water
2 1/2 ts Active yeast
1 ts Sugar
3 c  Bread flour
1/2 c  Thin sliced pepperoni
1/2 c  Cooked sweet Italian
Sausage
1/2 c  Whole black olives
1/4 c  Mozzarella cheese, shredded
All ingredients must be at room temperature to start. Water hot from
tap. Add all ingredients in the order listed and select white bread.

Believe me it really works. Note: when I made it I left out the egg
with no problems.

Source: Dak Gourmet Gazette 1991
