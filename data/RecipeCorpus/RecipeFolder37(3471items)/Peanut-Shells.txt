Title: Peanut Shells
Categories: Side dish, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Of the oil
1/2 c  Dry-roasted salted peanuts
3 tb Cilantro, chopped
Salt and pepper
1 c  Small pasta shells
2    Green onion(s)
1/2 c  Oil-packed
Sun-dried tomatoes plus
1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling, salted water until
tender but still firm, 7-9 min. Drain well.

2. Meanwhile, cut the green onions crosswise in to very thin slices.
Cut the sun-dried tomatoes into long, thin strips.

3. In a large serving bowl, combine the pasta with the green onions,
sun-dried tomato strips, and 1 tbs of oil from the tomatoes. Add the
peanuts and cilantro and toss. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve warm or at room temperature.
