Title: PUMPKIN SQUARES
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 35 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Butter;melted
1/2 c  Nuts;chopped
1 1/2 c  Mincemeat
1 lb Pumpkin
2 1/4 c  Flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Allspice
4    Eggs;
Sift together first 5 engredients. Set aside. Beat eggs and sugars
together will; add remaining ingredients. Add dry ingredients and mix
just enough to combine ingredients. Pour into a greased 13x9x2 55 to
60 minutes. Cool, and then frost with Cream Cheese Frosting. Cut into
35 squares.
