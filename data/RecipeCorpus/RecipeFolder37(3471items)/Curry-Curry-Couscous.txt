Title: Curry-Curry Couscous
Categories: Nuts/grains, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Cashews
2 tb Cilantro, chopped
Fresh ground pepper
1 c  Couscous
Salt
3    Plum tomatoes
1 tb Indian-style curry paste
1. In a covered saucepan, bring 1 cup of water to a rolling boil over
high heat. Add the couscous and  1/2 tsp salt, stir well and remove
from the heat. Cover and let stand for at least 5 min.

2. Meanwhile, chop the tomatoes.

3. Fluff the cooked couscous with a fork. Add the tomatoes and curry
paste and toss. Add the cashews, cilantro, and salt and pepper to
taste; toss well and serve.
