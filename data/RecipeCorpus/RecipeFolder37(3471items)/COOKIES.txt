Title: COOKIES: M&M SITTING-PRETTIES
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
Finely chopped nuts
1 cn Vanilla frosting
M&M peanut candies
1/2 c  Butter
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1    Egg, separated
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Flour
Blend butter and sugar; stir in egg yolk and vanilla. Sift flour and
salt into bowl; mix well.  Chill 1 hour. Roll into 1 inch balls.

Dip into slightly beaten egg white.  Roll in nuts. Place 1 inch apart
on Cookie sheet.

Bake 350-degrees F. 5 minutes.  Press thumb gently in center of each;
bake 5 minutes longer.  Cool.  Fill with frosting. Garnish with one
peanut M&M on each cookie.
