Title: Tex Mex Casserole^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Tex/mexican
Yield: 10 servings
2 cn (8oz) mild enchilada sauce
3/4 c  Milk
30    Crisp corn tortillas,
Broken
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
2 lb Ground beef
1    Chopped onion
1 ts Garlic powder
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
Brown meat, onion and garlic until onion is tender; drain. Stir in
soup, sauce and milk.

Layer as follows:

Half the shells, meaat anad cheese. Repeat layers. Bake 45 minutes or
until heated through at 350F. Let stand a few minutes before serving.
