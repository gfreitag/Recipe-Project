Title: MARITIME MADNESS
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Sage
3 tb Ketchup
1 tb Barbaque sauce
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 c  Oatmeal
1 lb Hamburg
1 cn Mushroom soup
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 tb Parsley flakes
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Combine all ingredients in bowl and mix well. Pat down in casserole
and bake (covered) 50 min. at 350, remove cover and continue to bake
15 min. to brown.
