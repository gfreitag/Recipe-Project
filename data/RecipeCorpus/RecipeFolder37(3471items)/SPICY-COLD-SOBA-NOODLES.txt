Title: SPICY COLD SOBA NOODLES
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Balsamic or red wine vinegar
1/2 bn Sliced green onions
Salt
1/2 lb Soba (Japanese -
Buckwheat noodles)
1/3 c  Soy sauce
1/2 ts Molasses
1/4 c  Sesame oil
1/4 c  Tahini
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Chili oil
Heat soy sauce until reduced by half.  Turn to low and add molasses.
Warm briefly and transfer to bowl.  Add remaining ingredients except
noodles. Add
salt to taste.  Boil noodles about 3 minutes.  Drain and plunge in ice
water.
Drain and rinse.  Combine with sauce and chill.  Makes 4 smaller
servings at
467 calories each.
