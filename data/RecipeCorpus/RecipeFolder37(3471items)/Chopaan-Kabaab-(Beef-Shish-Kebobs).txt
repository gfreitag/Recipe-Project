Title: Chopaan Kabaab (Beef Shish Kebobs)
Categories: Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Coriander (ground)
1 ts Cayenne Pepper
4 ts Vinegar
2 lb Sirloin (cut in thin strips)
1 ts Salt
3 md Onions (chopped)
1 ts Cumin (ground)
Makes 4 servings

Pita Bread
Sliced onions, cucumbers or tomatoes for garnish

Mix all ingredients, except garnish and Pita bread, then place strips
of meat in with the marinade. Marinate for 4 to 24 hours (the longer
the better). Arrange meat on 8 skewers and grill or broil until done,
about 10 minutes. Serve 2 skewers on a piece of Pita bread. Garnish
with sliced onions, cucumbers and/or tomatoes.

Each serving contains about: 343 calories, 842 mg sodium, 100 mg
cholesterol, 11 grams fat, 21 grams carbohydrates, 40 grams protein,
0.67 gram fiber.
