Title: Blueberry Mini Muffins^
Categories: Muffins, Fruit
Yield: 36 servings
1    Egg
3/4 c  Milk
1/3 c  Oil
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Fresh or frozen blueberries,
Do not thaw
Fast & Healthy Nov/Dec 92
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 400F.

Combine dry ingredients. Combine wet ingredients. Mix just until wet.
Fold in
berries. Put into 36 sprayed mini muffin cups.

Bake 10-15 minutes until light golden brown and test clean. Remove
immediately

from pan.

Per muffin:
60 cal., 1g pro., 9g carb., 0g fiber, 2g fat, 35mg sod., 20mg pot.,
1/2 starch

and 1/2 fat exchanges.
