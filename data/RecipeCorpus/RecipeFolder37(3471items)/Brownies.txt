Title: Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday, Diabetic
Yield: 16 servings
2    Eggs, beaten, or substitute
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 c  Nuts -- chopped
1/4 c  Cocoa
1/4 c  Butter or shortening
1/4 c  Applesauce
2 tb Sweet*10 liquid
2 tb Vanilla
Sift cocoa, flour, salt and baking soda and mix well.  Add vanilla,
sweetener, eggs.  Stir until well-blended and add the dry ingredients.
Mix until well-blended, stir in chopped nuts, pour into a greased 8"
square pan.  Level batter smoothly.  Bake at 325 degrees for 20
minutes. Cut into 16 squares.

Calories:  82
Exchanges:  1/2 bread, 1 fat
Nutritive Values:  2 g prot; 7 g carb; 5 g fat
