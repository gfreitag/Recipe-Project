Title: COCONUT SQUARES
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Melted butter
2 1/2 c  Graham wafer crumbs
1 ts Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
1 cn Eagle bran milk
2 c  Coconut
Combine crumbs,sugar and melted butter & press into pan, cook 10
minutes in 350 oven. Cool.
Combine milk, coconut, vanilla and salt, cover first mixture & bake 15
min in 350 oven, When cool ice with chocolate icing.
