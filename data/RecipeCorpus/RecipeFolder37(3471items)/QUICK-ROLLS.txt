Title: QUICK ROLLS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Sugar
4 tb Warm water
1 pk Yeast
4 c  Self rising flour
2 c  Buttermilk
3/4 c  Oil
Mix water, sugar and yeast.  Add to first three ingredients.  Beat
with spoon.  Drop into greased muffin pans.  Bake at 400 degrees for
15 minutes. Randy Rigg
