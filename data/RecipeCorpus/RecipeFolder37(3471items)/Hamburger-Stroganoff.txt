Title: Hamburger Stroganoff
Categories: Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
8 oz Sour cream
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
Salt and pepper to taste
2 lb Ground beef
1 lg Onion, chopped
1 cn Mushrooms
1 cn Tomato soup
Brown ground beef, drain off grease, add onion and mushrooms.  In a
bowl mix together tomato soup, mushroom soup, sour cream.
Worcestershire sauce and salt and pepper.  Pour this mixture in with
hamburger and mix.  Let simmer a half hour or longer.  Serve over
noodles or rice.
