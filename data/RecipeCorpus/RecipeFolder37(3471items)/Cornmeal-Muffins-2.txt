Title: Cornmeal Muffins 2
Categories: Breads, Muffins, Tasteofhome
Yield: 18 muffins
3/4 ts Salt
1    Egg; beaten
1 1/4 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Shortening; melted
1 c  Cornmeal, yellow
1 c  Flour, all-purpose
1/3 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Soda
Combine first 6 ingredients.  Stir together egg, sour cream, and
shortening; add to dry ingrdients, mixing well.

Fill lightly greased muffin tins two-thirds full.  Bake at 425
degrees for 15 minutes or until golden brown.
