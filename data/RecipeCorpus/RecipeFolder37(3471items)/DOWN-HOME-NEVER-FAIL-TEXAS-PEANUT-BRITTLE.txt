Title: DOWN HOME-NEVER FAIL TEXAS PEANUT BRITTLE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
3 ts Butter
1 ts Salt
2 ts Baking soda
2 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Water
3 c  Raw Spanish peanuts
Boil sugar, corn syrup and water until thread spins.  Add peanuts,
stir continuously after peanuts are added.  Cook until mixture turns a
brownish-gold color.  Remove from heat, add butter, salt and baking
soda. Pour onto buttered board, spreading to 1/4-inch thickness.
When cool break into pieces.  Store in an airtight container.
