Title: Ham Strata^
Categories: Meats, Poultry, Cheese/eggs, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
12 sl White bread, crusts removed
1 pk (6oz) sharp cheddar cheese
Slices
4 lg Eggs
3 c  Milk
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Butter
1 c  Chopped celery
1/3 c  Chopped green pepper
2 tb Chopped onion
2 c  Chopped cooked ham, chicken
Or turkey
1/4 c  Mayonnaise or that fake
Stuff, divided
MMMMM-------------------------GARNISHES------------------------------
Fresh parsley sprigs
Sour cream
Cook celery, onion and pepper in butter unti tender. Take off heat,
stir in ham and let cool.

Spread each slice bread with 1 teaspoon mayonnaise and put mayonnaise
side up in a 13X9X2" baking dish. Top with ham, cheese and rest of
bread, mayonnaise side down.

Combine next 3 ingredients and eggs, stir well;pour over bread slices.
Refrigerate 8 hours or overnight covered. Before baking remove from
refrigerator for 30 mins.

Bake at 300F. for 1 1/2 hours. If desired garnish. Serve immediately.
