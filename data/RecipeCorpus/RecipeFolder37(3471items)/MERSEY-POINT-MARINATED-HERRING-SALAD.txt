Title: MERSEY POINT MARINATED HERRING SALAD
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Vegetables, Salads, Canadian
Yield: 1 batch
2    Apples; chopped
1/2 c  Dill pickles; chopped
1/2 sm Spanish onion; sliced
250 g  Marinated herring; drained
1 c  Potatoes; cooked, diced
1 c  Beets; cooked, diced
MMMMM-----------------------CREAM DRESSING----------------------------
1/2 ts Dillweed; dried
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Whipping cream
1 tb White vinegar
1 ts Prepared mustard
Combine salad ingredients in large bowl. Divide into individual
servings and allow each person to apply dressing as desired.
