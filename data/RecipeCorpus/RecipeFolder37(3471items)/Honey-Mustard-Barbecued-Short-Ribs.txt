Title: Honey-Mustard Barbecued Short Ribs
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Crockpot
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
1 c  Bottled hickory smoke
-Barbecue sauce
2 tb Cornstarch
2 tb Cold water
3 lb Beef short ribs
1 tb Dijon mustard
1    Garlic clove, crushed
2 tb Honey
1/2 ts Salt
Place short ribs in slow-cooker.  In medium bowl, combine mustard,
garlic, honey, salt, pepper, and barbecue sauce.  Pour over ribs.
Cover and cook on LOW 6-7 hours or until tender.  Refridgerate
several hours or overnight.  Skim any solidified fat from top.
Remove ribs; heat in microwave or conventional oven.  Dissolve
cornstarch in water.  Add to sauce.  Cook and stir in microwave or
stovetoo until hot and slightly thickened.  Pour hot sauce over warm
ribs.

Crockery Favorites, Mable Hoffman
