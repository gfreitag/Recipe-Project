Title: SWEET HEAVEN DELIGHT
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Pecans; chopped
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
2    Margarine
2 c  Flour
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING:-------------------------------
Topping:
Whipped topping
Pecans; chopped
2    8 oz pkgs cream cheese
2 c  Whipped topping
2 c  Powdered sugar
2 c  Cooked sweet potato puree
Recipe by: Sweet Potato Festival, Vardamon, Mississippi, 1994
Melt margarine, add flour, nuts and sugar.  Press into 9 X 13 inch
baking p

Bake 15 minutes at 375 degrees or until lightly browned. Combine
filling ingredients, mix well and spread over cooled first layer. Top
with a layer whipped topping and sprinkle with chopped pecans.
