Title: APPLE SNAPS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Cinnamon
Mint
Apple;sliced thin
Cinnamon sticks
1 pk Sugar-free gelatin; any
-flavor (4 serving size box)
3/4 c  -water; boiling
1 3/4 c  Applesauce; unsweetened but
-well chilled
Prepare gelatin as usual using the applesauce instead of cold water.
Chill. Garnish with a mint sprig, thin slices of apple and a small
cinnamon stick.
