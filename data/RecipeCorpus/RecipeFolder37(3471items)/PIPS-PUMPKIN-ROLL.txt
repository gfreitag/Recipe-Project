Title: PIPS PUMPKIN ROLL
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Powdered sugar
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
3 tb Butter
1/2 ts Vanilla
3    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Pumpkin
1 ts Lemon juice
3/4 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
2 ts Ginger
Beat for 5 minutes the 3 eggs. Slowly add sugar,pumpkin,lemon juice,
flour, baking powder,ginger,cinnamon and salt. Grease and flour a
small cookie sheet (jelly roll pan) Put in the above mixture and bake
at 350 for 20 minutes. SPRINKLE "cake" with powdered sugar and tip
onto a linen towel. ( Sprinkle towel with P. sugar also).. Roll and
refrigerate for 30 minutes. Filling: Mix together -1 c Powdered
sugar, softened cream cheese, butter and vanilla Spread over "cake".
Again "roll" the cake and refrigerate. Slice & Enjoy. This recipe can
easily be doubled.
