Title: Spaghetti With Turkey Meatballs~
Categories: Poultry, Pasta/rice, Low cal/fat, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Green bell pepper --
- chopped
1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper
10 oz Spaghetti; cooked --
- drained
1 pk Premium fresh lean ground
- turkey -- about 1 1/4 lb
1/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1 ea Egg
1 tb Oil
16 oz Can Whole tomatoes
- undrained, cut up
6 oz Tomato paste
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
In medium bowl, combine ground turkey, bread crumbs, parmesan cheese,
and egg; mix well.
Shape into 15 <1 1/2 inch> meatballs.
Heat oil in large skillet until oil sizzles, about 1 minute.
Add turkey meatballs.
Cook 5 mins or until well browned and no longer pink in center,
turning frequently.
Add all remaining ingredients except spaghetti; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 10 mins.
Remove cover; simmer an additional 5-10 minutes or until sauce is of
desired consistency, stirring occasionally.
Serve turkey meatballs and sauce on spaghetti.

Per serving:
415 cals, 25% cals from fat, 29g prot, 50g carb, 12g fat, 111mg chol,
481mg sodium
