Title: Welsh Rarebit^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Cheese/eggs, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
1/8 ts Lemon juice
1/8 ts Ground red pepper
16 sl (1/2") French baguette,
Toasted
3/4 c  Evaporated skimmed milk
1 c  Shredded lowfat cheddar
Cheese
1 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 ts Low sodium Worcestershire
Heat milk without boiling. Add cheese, mustard, Worcestershire, lemon
juice, and red pepper. Cook, stirring, until cheese melts and is
smooth. Reduce heat and cook another 2 minutes.

Serve 2 baguette slices topped with 1 tablespoon cheese.

Per serving:
182 cal., 14.2g pro., 6.3g (31%) fat, 16.7g carb., 23mg chol., 416mg
sod.
