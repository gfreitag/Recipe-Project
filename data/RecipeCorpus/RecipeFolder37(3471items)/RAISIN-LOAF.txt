Title: RAISIN LOAF
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Shortening
2    Eggs beaten
1/2 ts Salt
1 3/4 c  Flour
1 lb Raisins
1 ts Soda
1 c  Hot water
1 c  Sugar
Put the raisins and soda in a bowl and pour the hot water over and
let sit till cool. Cream the sugar and shortening and add the beaten
eggs. Add the raisin mixture and then the flour & salt. Bake in loaf
pan 1 hour in 350 oven.
