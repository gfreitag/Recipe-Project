Title: BROCCOLI SALAD WITH BACON
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
- cheese
1/2 lb Bacon, crisp fried and
- crumbled
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Vinegar
2 1/2 lb Broccoli (or 2 big bunches)
Whisk together mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar and set aside.  Cut
broccoli into florets and combine in large bowl with cheese and
bacon.  Toss with mayonnaise dressing.  Cover and chill overnight
before serving.
