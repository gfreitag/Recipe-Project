Title: Salisbury Steak with Mushroom Sauce^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-----------------------MUSHROOM SAUCE----------------------------
Reserved
1    Beef bouillon cube
Water
1/4 ts Gravy master
1 tb Dried minced onion
3 tb Butter
3 tb Flour
1/8 ts Pepper
1 cn (4oz) mushrooms, drained and
MMMMM------------------SALLISBURY STEAK MIXTURE-----------------------
2 tb Minced chives or green onion
Tops
1/2 ts Dry mustard
ds Paprika
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Minced onion
1/3 c  Minced green pepper
1/4 c  Chopped celery
1 cl Garlic, minced
2 tb Butter
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
Sauce:

Melt butter; stir in flour and pepper. Add mushrooms, bouillon and
enough combined mushroom liquid and water to measure 1 1/4 cups.
Cook, stirring until thickened. Add gravy master. Keep warm.

Salisbury Steaks:

Soften onion, pepper, celery and garlic in butter. Remove from heat.
Add remaining ingredients and shape into 6 patties about 3/4" thick.
Broil; turning after 7 minutes until cooked through. Serve with sauce.
