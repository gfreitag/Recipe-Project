Title: Dinosaur Punch
Categories: Kids, Beverages
Yield: 6 servings
2 dr Green food color (if
- Desired)
1 l  Bottle (4 1/2 Cup)
Lemon-Lime carbonated Soda
- Chilled
6 oz Frozen lemonade concentrate
- Thawed
1/2 c  Frozen limeade concentrate
- Thawed
1 c  Water
In large non metal pitcher, combine concentrates and water.  Add food
color, blend well.

Just before serving, add lemon lime soda, stir gently to blend.  Serve
over ice.

Kids love this!! == Courtesy of Dale & Gail Shipp, Columbia Md. ==
