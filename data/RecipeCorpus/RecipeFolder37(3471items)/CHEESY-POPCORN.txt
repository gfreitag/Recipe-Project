Title: CHEESY POPCORN
Categories: Appetizers, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Oregano leaves
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/8 ts Black pepper
1/4 c  Uncooked popcorn kernels
1 tb Corn oil
1 pk Butter Buds Mix, dry
1 ts Basil leaves
To prepare popcorn, place oil and unpopped corn kernels in popcorn
popper.  Cover and pop according to manufacturer's directions.  Remove
popped corn from heat.  If using a heavy saucepan or skillet, heat,
covered, over medium heat.  Shake while corn is popping.  If using
hot-air popcorn popper, spray popped corn lightly with non-stick
cooking spray.
Combine warm popcorn with the remaining ingredients in large bowl;
Makes four 1-cup servings.
toss to mix.
