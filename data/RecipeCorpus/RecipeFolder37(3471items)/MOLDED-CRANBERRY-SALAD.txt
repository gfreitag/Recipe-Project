Title: MOLDED CRANBERRY SALAD
Categories: Salads, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Grated orange peel
2 1/2 ts Sweetener or sugar
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
1/4    Medium apple, chopped
1/2 tb Unflavored gelatin
1 c  Diet ginger ale
1 c  Cranberries,coarsely chopped
1/2 tb Fresh lemon juice
Sprinkle gelatin over 2 tb ginger ale; let stand 5 min to soften.
Combine remaining ginger ale, cranberries, lemon juice and orange
peel in a saucepan.  Bring quickly to a boil. Remove from heat. Add
softened gelatin and sweetener; stir until gelatin dissolves. Chill
until partially set. Stir in celery and apple. Spoon into a rinsed
mold. Refrigerate at least 4 hours until set.

each serving about 1/2 cup 1/2 Fruit & Veg. Choice 6 g carbohydrate,
1 g protein (28 calories) Source: Choice Cooking, Canadian Diabetes
Association 1986
