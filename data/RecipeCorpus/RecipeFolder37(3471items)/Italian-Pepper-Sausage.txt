Title: Italian Pepper Sausage
Categories: Meats, Sausage, Italian
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Crushed dried red peppers
2 ts Fennel seeds
1/2 ts Crushed bay leaf
1/4 ts Thyme
1/3 c  Red wine or water
Pinch of coriander
2.5 yards sausage casing
4 1/2 lb Fresh lean pork
1 1/2 lb Fresh pork fat (fresh pork s
1    Med. onion, chopped
1    Large clove garlic, minced
3 tb Salt
1 1/2 tb Freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 ts Paprika
Grind the lean and fat pork, onion and garlic. Add the seasonings
and mix thoroughly. Add the wine and mix well.
Force through a sausage stuffer into casing.
Makes 6.5 lb.  Taken from: The New York Times Cook Book
