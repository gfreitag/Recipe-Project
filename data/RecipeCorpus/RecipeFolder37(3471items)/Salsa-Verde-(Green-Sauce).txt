Title: Salsa Verde (Green Sauce)
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Black pepper
3/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
2 tb Chopped bell pepper
2 qt Beef broth
2 md Onions -- chopped
1 c  Canned diced green chiles
2 1/2    Yellow hot chiles -- diced
1 c  Canned whole tomatoes --
-crushed
1 tb Oregano
Combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil, let simmer 1 hour. If
needed, add roux to thicken.

Roux: Melt 1 stick of butter. Blend in 3/4 cup sifted flour. Can be
refrigerated until needed.
