Title: CHOPS MILANAISE
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Bread crumbs
1 c  Parmesan cheese
3 tb Butter
4 ea Pork chops
1/2 c  Flour
2 ea Eggs, beaten
Mix bread crumbs and parmesan cheese in a shallow bowl.  Dip chops in
flour, then in egg, then dredge in bread crumb mixture.
Melt butter in a large skillet and brown chops.  Reduce heat, cover
and cook for about 3 minutes.  Remove cover and continue cooking till
chops are completely cooked.
Season with salt and pepper; serve with lemon and butter noodles.
