Title: Peach Blossom^
Categories: Holiday, Appetizers, Fruit, Beverages
Yield: 5 servings
Concentrate, thawed
2 ts Lime juice
Fresh mint (optional)
2 c  Ice water
1/3 c  Frozen peach juice blend
Concentrate, thawed
1/3 c  Frozen orange juice
In a blender or food processor,place ice water, peach and orange juice
concentrates. Till foamy  cover and blend. pour into chilled stemmed
glasses.
Granish with mints, if desired.
