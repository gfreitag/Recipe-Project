Title: CHEWY ROLO COOKIE BAR
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Butter, melted
36    Rolo candy pieces, halved
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1/2 c  Nuts, chopped
2/3 c  Evaporated milk
Combine cake and nut meats in a medium bowl.  Stir in evaporated milk
and melted butter or margarine.  Blend well.  Spread half of cake
mixture into a greased 9x13 inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15
minutes.  Remove from oven.  Immediately place Rolo pieces, cut side
down, over hot crust.  Drop remaining cake mixture by teaspoonfuls
over Rolo pieces.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes or until
lightly browned.  Cut in squares. Randy Rigg
