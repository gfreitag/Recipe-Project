Title: Stuffed Eggs
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
4 tb Butter
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Onion juice
6    Hard-cooked eggs
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Tarragon vinegar
Peel and cut eggs lengthwise. Remove yolks, being careful not to
break whites. Mash yolks through sieve. Add remaining ingredients and
whip until creamy. Refill whites and sprinkle with paprika.
