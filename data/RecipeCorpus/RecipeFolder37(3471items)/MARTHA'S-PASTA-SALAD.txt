Title: MARTHA'S PASTA SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Main dish, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
2    Whole tomatoes, diced
18 oz Can tomato sauce
8 oz Italian dressing
1 lb Spaghetti
1 ts Olive oil
1    Cucumber, diced
1    Bell pepper, chopped
Boil pasta for 8 minutes, then drain and add olive oil. REfrigerate
until cool.  Add rest of ingredients. Add onion and red wine vinegar
to taste.
