Title: Gyoza Potstickers with Gingered Dipping Sauce
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Minced pickled ginger
Or peeled and minced
Fresh ginger
16    Gyoza potstickers
2 1/2 tb Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Seasoned rice vinegar
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1. In a large frying pan, cook the potsticker in the oil over
medium-high heat until they begin to brown on one side.

2. Reduce heat to medium and add  1/4 cup of water to the pan. Cover
pan and steam until the bottoms of the potstickers are brown and they
are tender, 6-8 min.

3. In a small bowl, stir together the rice vinegar, soy sauce, and
pickled ginger. Serve this dipping sauce with the warm potstickers.
