Title: Milk Flitcher
Categories: Pie/tart, Penndutch, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
Butter
Cinnamon
1    Pastry lined pie dish
1 c  Brown sugar
1 1/4 c  Milk
Line dish with pastry.  Sprinkle bottom with flour.  Then place brown
sugar and milk in the pastry lined dish.  Dot mixture with butter and
sprinkle with cinnamon.  Place in pre-heated oven (375 - 400 ) and
bake until filling is firm and pie dough is a nice golden brown.

From: Lancaster Farming
