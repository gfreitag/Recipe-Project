Title: Herbed Zucchini
Categories: Vegetables, P-cooker, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Oregano, dried, crushed
1 tb Butter
4 md Zucchini, cut into 1/2" pcs
2 md Tomatoes, cut into wedges
2 ts Instant minced onions
