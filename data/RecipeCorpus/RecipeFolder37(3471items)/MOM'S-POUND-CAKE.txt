Title: MOM'S POUND CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Vanilla and lemon extrac
1 c  Butter
5    Eggs
3 c  Sugar
3 c  Flour
1 c  Milk
Butter and flour pan well.  Cream butter and sugar.  Add extract then
add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.  Add milk
and flour alternately.  Bake at 300 degrees in cold oven for 1 1/4
hours.  Let pan cool well before taking cake out. Randy Rigg
