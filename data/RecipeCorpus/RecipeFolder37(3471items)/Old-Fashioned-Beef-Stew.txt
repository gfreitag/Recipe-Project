Title: Old Fashioned Beef Stew
Categories: Meats, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ds Allspice or cloves
6    Carrots -- sliced
4    Potatoes -- diced
3 tb All-purpose flour
2 lb Beef stew meat
2 tb Shortening
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1    Clove Garlic
1 md Onion -- chopped
1    Or 2 Bay leaves
1 tb Salt
Brown meat in hot shortening, add 2 cups of hot water and the next
nine ingredients.  Cover, simmer 1-1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
Remove bay leaves and garlic clove.  Add vegetables, cover and cook
30 to 40 minutes or until veggies are tender.  Slowly blend flour
into 1/3 cup cold water. Stir flour mixture slowly into hot stew.
Cook and stir until bubbly. Cook and stir 3 minutes longer.  Serve.
