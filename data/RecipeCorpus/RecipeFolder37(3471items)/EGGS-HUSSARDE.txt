Title: EGGS HUSSARDE
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
2    Eggs, soft poached
3/4 c  Hollandaise sauce
Paprika
2 lg Thin slices ham, grilled
2    Holland rusks
1/4 c  Marchand de Vin sauce
2    Slices tomatoes, grilled
Lay a large slice of grilled ham across each rusk and cover with
Marchand de Vin sauce.  Add a slice of grilled tomato, and then the
egg. Top with Hollandaise Sauce, and sprinkle with paprika.

Southern Living ... August, 1971
