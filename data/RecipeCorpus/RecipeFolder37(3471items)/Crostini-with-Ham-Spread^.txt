Title: Crostini with Ham Spread^
Categories: Breads, Meats, Dips/spread, Appetizers
Yield: 40 servings
1 ts Spicy brown mustard
1 ts Snipped fresh thyme or 1/4
ts Dried thyme, crushed
1/4 c  Finely chopped water
Chestnuts or celery
1/4 c  Finely chopped, seeded
Cucumber
1 lb Loaf unsliced french or
Italian bread (baguette)
2 tb Olive oil
2 c  Ground fully cooked ham (10
Oz)
1/2 c  Mayonnaise or salad
Dressing
For crostini, cut (about 40 sl) of bread into 1/4" thick slices. Lay
slices in a single layer on baking sheets. On one side of each slices
brush olive oil. Bake for 10 mins. at 400F. or until till crisp. Cool
and store in a plastic bag.

For ham spread, in med. mixing bowl, combine ham, mayonnasie or salad
dressing, mustard, and thyme. Stir in water chestnuts or celery and
cucumber. Cover and chill for 4 to 24 hours. Serve ham spread with
crostini.
