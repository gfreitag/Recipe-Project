Title: Turtle Dove Bars^
Categories: Holiday, Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 servings
2/3 c  Butter
1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Butterscotch chips
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
2 c  Flour
1 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Softened butter
1 c  Pecan havles
Preheat oven to 350F.

Beat flour, brown sugar and butter. Press into an ungreased 13X9" pan;
sprinkle with pecans.

Combine butter and brown sugar and boil unti firm ball stage is
reaches (242F.). Pour over dough. Bake 18-20 minutes or until caramel
is bubbly. Put chips on top, allow to melt slightly then swirl with a
knife to marble. Cool; cut into bars. Makes 36
