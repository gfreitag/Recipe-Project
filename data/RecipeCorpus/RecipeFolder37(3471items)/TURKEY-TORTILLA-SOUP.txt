Title: TURKEY TORTILLA SOUP
Categories: Low cal/fat, Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
4 cn Chicken broth (14.5 oz)
2 c  Chopped turkey
1/2 c  Fresh cilantro
Tortilla chips
Grated cheese
1 sm Chopped onion
3    Garlic cloves
1 ts Olive oil
1 sm Can chopped green chiles
1/4 c  Taco seasoning (packet)
1 cn Chopped tomatoes (14.5oz)
Saute first three ingredients in a 4 quart sauce pan until the onions
are tender.

Add next six ingredients and allow to simmer for 15 minutes.

Put 1/2 oz. cheese and 1/2 oz. chips at the bottom of the bowl and
spoon soup over cheese and chips.

Makes eight 1.5 cup servings.

1/2 B, 1P, 1V, and 20 optional calories.
