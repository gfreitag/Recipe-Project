Title: TERIYAKI FISH
Categories: Main dish, Fish/seafoo, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 ts Monosodium glutamate (opt)
3 ts Sake (Japanese rice wine) or
Cooking sherry
1 1/2 lb Fish fillets
3/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Grated fresh ginger root
1    Clove garlic,
-peeled and crushed
1. In a shallow, 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic baking dish
combine soy sauce, sugar, ginger root, garlic, monosodium glutamate
and sake. Stir to
combine well.
2. Place fish fillets in sauce mixture and marinate for 1/2 hour.
3. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven for 9 minutes basting
occasionally with
sauce.
