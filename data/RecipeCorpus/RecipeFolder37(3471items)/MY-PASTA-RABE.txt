Title: MY PASTA RABE
Categories: Pasta/rice, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Broccoli rabe (rappini)
9 oz Fresh pasta (linguini),
-cooked
3/4 c  Parmesan or romano cheese
3 tb Olive oil
4 lg Garlic cloves, minced
1 md Onion, chopped
1 c  Mushrooms, sliced
Saute' garlic, onion and mushrooms in olive oil, until golden.  Wash
and chop the broccoli rabe (rappini). Steam over boiling water for 5
minutes. Toss with cooked pasta, saute'ed garlic mixture and grated
cheese. Serve hot. 6-8 servings.
