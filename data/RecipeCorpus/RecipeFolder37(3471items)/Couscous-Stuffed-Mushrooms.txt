Title: Couscous-Stuffed Mushrooms
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Red bell pepper
Finely diced
Fresh ground pepper
1/3 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/3 c  Couscous
1/2 ts Salt
16 lg Mushrooms
1/4 c  Pesto sauce
1. Preheat oven to 400 F. In a small saucepan, bring  1/2 cup water
to a boil over high heat. Stir in the couscous and salt, remove from
the heat, cover and set aside for at least 5 min.

2. Remove stems from the mushrooms; reserve stems for another use.
Brush caps all over with 2 tbs pesto.

3. Fluff couscous with a fork and stir in remaining pesto and the red
pepper. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Stuff each mushroom
with 1 heaping tbs of couscous. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese
on top.

4. Bake the mushrooms for 8-10 min, until hot.
