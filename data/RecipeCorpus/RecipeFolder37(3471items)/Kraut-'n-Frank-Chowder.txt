Title: Kraut 'n Frank Chowder
Categories: P-cooker, Meats, Main dish, Soup/stew, Kids
Yield: 4 - 6 folks
1 cn (16-oz) sauerkraut; drained
1    Bay leaf;
1/4 ts Pepper;
1/4 ts Thyme;
1 tb Instant beef bouillon;
3 c  Water;
1/4 c  Butter -=OR=-
1/4 c  Margarine;
1 lb Wieners; cut into 1/2"
-chunks
2 lg Onions; chopped
1    Stalk celery; chopped
Heat pressure cooker.  Add butter, brown wierners.  Remove wierners.
Saute onions and celery until tender.  Stir wieners, sauerkraut, bay
leaf, pepper, thyme, bouillon, and water.  Close cover securely.
Place pressure regulator on vent pipe.  COOK 5 MINUTES.  Let pressure
drop of its own accord. 4 to 6 servings.

Source: Pressure Cooking is Pleasure Cooking by Patricia Phillps.
