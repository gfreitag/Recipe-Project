Title: BAKED LEMON PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Lemon rind; grated
2    Eggs; separated
1 tb Butter; melted
1 c  Milk
1 c  Sugar;granulated
1/4 c  Flour; all purpose
1/4 ts -Salt
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Preheat oven to 350F. In mixing bowl, combine sugar, flour and salt.
Stir in lemon juice rind, beaten eggs yolks, butter and milk. Beat
egg whites till stiff but not dry; fold into lemon mixture. Pour into
buttered 6 cup casserole. Place in larger pan, pour in hot water to
about 1" depth. Bake in 350F oven for 40 minutes or till topping is
golden brown. Serve warm. Makes about 4 servings
