Title: Dijon Roasted Vegetable Soup
Categories: Diabetic, Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
5 c  Lower sodium chicken broth;
1/4 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Crushed red peppers flakes;
2 c  Chicken; cooked diced
-- (about 10-oz)
1/2 c  Grey Poupon Dijon Mustard;
1/2 c  Parsley; chopped
2    Plum tomatoes; halved
1 md Zucchini; split lengthwise
-- and halved
1 lg Onion; quartered
1    Red bell pepper; sliced
1 c  Carrots; sliced
2 cl - 3 cl garlic;
5 c  Chicken broth -=OR=-
VEGETABLE: On large baking sheet, arrange tomatoes, zucchini, onion,
bell pepper, carrots and garlic.  Bake at 325 for 30 to 45 minutes or
until golden brown and tender.

In a large 3-qt pot, over high heat, heat chicken broth, chopped
vegetables, cumin and red pepper flakes to a boil; reduce heat.
Simmer for 5 minutes.  Stir in chicken broth and mustard; cook
for 5 minutes more. Stir in parsley and serve warm.
