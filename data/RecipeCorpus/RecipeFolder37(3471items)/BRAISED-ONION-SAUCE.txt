Title: BRAISED ONION SAUCE
Categories: Pasta/rice, Salsa/sauce, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Madeira
Parmesan cheese
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/2 lb Yellow onions; sliced 3/8" t
1 tb Sugar
Recipe by: "The New James Beard"
Melt the butter in a skillet, add the onion, and cook, covered, over
medium heat until soft and transparent.  Uncover, stir in the sugar,
reduce the he

and cook gently until brown, about 1 hour.  Stir in the Madeira and
cook 2 minutes. Toss the sauce with drained, cooked pasta, preferably
tomato pasta spaghetti, or macaroni.  Serve at once with grated
Parmesan cheese.
