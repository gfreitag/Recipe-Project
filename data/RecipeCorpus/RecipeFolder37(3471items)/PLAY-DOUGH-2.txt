Title: PLAY DOUGH 2
Categories: Information
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Oil
Food coloring
-Jo Ferry cmsj69b
1 c  Salt
1 1/2 c  Flour
Mix the oil and food coloring into the water. Add the salt and
flour. Mix all together, then knead to get out the lumps. Store
tightly covered in the refrigerator.
