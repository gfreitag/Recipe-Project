Title: SWEET-SOUR KRAUT AND CHOPS
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
- (27-oz.)
1 cn Crushed pineapple (20-oz)
2 tb Brown sugar
1/2 ts Salt
ds Pepper
4 md Baking potatoes, peeled and
-thinly sliced (4-cups)
1/2 c  Chopped onion
6 ea Pork chops, 3/4" thick
2 tb Cooking oil
1 cn Sauerkraut, rinsed & drained
In a 12 x 7 1/2 x 2-inch baking dish combine potato slices and onion.
Add 1/4 cup water. Bake covered, at 350 till nearly tender,
45-minutes. Meanwhile, in a skillet brown pork chops on both sides in
hot oil. In a bowl combine sauerkraut, undrained pineapple, and brown
sugar. Spoon over potato mixture. Place chops atop. Season with salt
and pepper. Bake, covered, 45 minutes longer.
