Title: DILL PICKLES #2
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Salt
Dill
2 qt Water
1 qt Vinegar
1 c  Sugar
Bring to a boil and let stand until cool the above ingreds.

Use 5-6 quart jars and pack with large cucumbers, then add one large
bunch of dill to each quart. Fill each jar with the vinegar mixture.
Do not seal tight for 2-3 days.

From "Ma's Cookin' Mountain recipes"
