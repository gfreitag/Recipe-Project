Title: Garlic Soup^
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Chopped carrots
Salt and pepper to taste
Whipping cream, optinal
2 c  Water
1 c  Peeled garlic cloves
1 c  Chopped peeled potatoes
Boil water, garlic, potatoes and carrots. Cover, reduce heat and
simmer until vegetables, about 20-25 minutes. Puree. Season. Reheat,
if needed. Drizzle with cream, if desired.
