Title: Green Bean and Ham Soup
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Potatoes, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Fresh dill snipped -=OR=-
1 ts Dried dill; crushed
1/4 ts Salt & black pepper;
-- TO TASTE
1 c  Milk half & half -=OR=-
1 c  Light cream
-- (I used just plain old
-- milk.)
4 c  Fresh green beans; cut into
-- 1" pieces
3 c  Potatoes; peeled cut into
-- 1" pieces
2 c  Ham; cooked cubed
1 c  Onion; chopped
4 c  Water;
1 tb Cooking oil;
1/4 c  All-purpose flour;
In a 4 or 6 quart pressure cooker combine green beans, potatoes, ham
onion, water, and oil.  Lock lid in place.  Place regulator on vent
pipe (if you have first-generation cooker).  Over high heat, bring
cooker to pressure.  Reduce heat just enough to maintain and pressure
regulator rocks gently; cook for 3 minutes.  Quick-Release the
pressure.  Carefully remove lid.  Stir together flour, dill, salt,
pepper, and milk, half and half, or light cream till smooth.  Add to
cooker.  Cook over medium heat till thickened and bubbly.  Cook and
stir for 1 minute more.  Makes 6 main dish servings. (10 cups)

NOTE: I substituted Spam for Ham, low-fat milk, and canned green
beans.  Served with sourdough bread for dunking!!!:-)))))
