Title: Sausage & Cheese Strata^
Categories: Sausage, Appetizers, Holiday, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
6    Eggs
2 c  Milk
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Dry mustard
12 sl White bread, cubed
8 oz Cheddar cheese, cubed
4 oz Mozarella cheese, cubed
8 oz Ham or bacon, cubed cooked
And crumbled
1 cn (4oz) mushrooms, drained
Grease 13X9" pan. Layer half the bread cubes, cheese, ham or bacon,
mushrooms and remaining bread. Beat together eggs, milk and
seasonings. Cover and chill at least four hours.

Preheat oven to 350F., then bake 1 hour until a knife tests clean.
