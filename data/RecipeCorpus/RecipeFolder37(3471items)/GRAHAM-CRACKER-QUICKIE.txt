Title: GRAHAM CRACKER QUICKIE
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Graham cracker crumbs
ds Salt
3    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Chopped walnuts
Beat eggs thorougly and add sugar.  Fold in walnuts and crumbs, also
salt. Spread in a 9 inch greased pie pan.  Bake fo 25 minutes at 325
degrees. Serve warm with whipped cream on top.  Chewy and delicious.
