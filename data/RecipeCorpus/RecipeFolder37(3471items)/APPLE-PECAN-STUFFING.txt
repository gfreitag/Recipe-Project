Title: APPLE-PECAN STUFFING
Categories: Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Ground sage
1/8 ts Ground marjoram
1/8 ts Ground thyme
1/2 ts Dried parsley flakes
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1/2 c  Water
Butter or margarine
2/3 c  Diced onion
2/3 c  Diced celery
3 c  Diced day-old bread
3 c  Diced apples
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
Melt 1/4 cup butter in skillet. Add onion and celery and cook until
tender. Add to bread. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in skillet. Stir in
apples. Cook until golden. Add apples to bread mixture. Mix in salt,
pepper, sage, marjoram, thyme, parsley and pecans. Add water and mix
thoroughly. Makes enough for 10-pound turkey
