Title: CRESCENT SAND TARTS II
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Pecans chopped
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 lb Butter
1/2 c  Confectioner's sugar sifted
2 c  Cake flour sifted
Cream butter; add sugar.  Stir well and add flour, nuts and vanilla.
Shape into balls or crescent (just drop them from the spoon - the
more you handle them the harder they will get) and bake on ungreased
cookie sheet at 325 deg.  for 20 minutes or until a light brown.
Roll in powdered sugar while warm.  Makes 4 dozen.

Source: DALLAS JR. LEAGUE COOKBOOK page 356 Posted by Ken Strei
