Title: CHICKEN LIN
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
1 pt Spaghetti sauce of your
Choice
1 ts Lemon-pepper seasoning
1 ts Dill
Salt and pepper as needed
1    Whole chicken, cut up
1 cn Ortega whole chiles
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1 md Onion, sliced
1    Clove garlic, minced
Put everything in a chicken skillet. Microwave 22 minutes, or until
chicken is tender. Serve with rice, noodles, or pasta.
