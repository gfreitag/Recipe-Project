Title: Potato Burgers^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Potatoes, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Water
1 lb Ground beef
2 c  Grated peeled potatoes
1/2 c  Chopped onion
Combine ingredients except water. Brown. Add water and simmer until
cooked through, about 15 minutes.
