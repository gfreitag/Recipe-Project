Title: BEEF CASSEROLE
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 cn Water
3 lb Beef stew meat (cut in small
Pieces)
1 cn Golden mushroom soup
1 cn Onion soup (not creamed)
1 cn Cream of celery soup
Put all 3 soups, can of water and stew meat in covered casserole.
You do not have to brown meat before baking.  Cook 4 to 5 hours at
325 degrees. This makes a delicious gravy without adding anything
else.
