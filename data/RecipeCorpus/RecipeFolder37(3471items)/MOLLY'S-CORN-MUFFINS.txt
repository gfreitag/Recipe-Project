Title: MOLLY'S CORN MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Corn Meal
1 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking Powder
1 c  Sour Cream or Yogurt
1 ts Baking Soda
4 oz Butter, melted
2 lg Eggs, beaten
Mix sour cream or yogurt with the baking soda and set aside. In a
large bowl place the butter, sugar, corn meal and eggs. Stir well.
Add the flour mixture alternating with the sour cream mixture,
beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Grease 12 regular
muffin tins and bake 25-30 minutes in a preheated 350oF oven.

Source: "The Yankee Kitchen" 04-01-93 (#4) [Pauline]
