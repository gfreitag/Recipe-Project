Title: MERSEY POINT SALMON SAUCE
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Salsa/sauce, Canadian
Yield: 1 batch
1 ts Dill
1 tb Capers
Pepper
Lemon juice
1/4 lb Salmon bits
3 tb Butter or margarine
3 tb Flour
1 1/3 c  Fish or chicken stock
1 tb Milk or cream
Melt butter, stir in flour and blend. Slowly add the stock and milk.
When sauce is smooth and boiling, add the salmon bits, dill, and
capers. Add pepper and lemon to taste.
This sauce is great served over rice, fettucini, or pastry. Serve it
as a hors d'oeuvre, appetizer, or main course.
