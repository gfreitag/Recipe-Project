Title: Wisconsin Cheddar Bacon Puffs^
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 20 servings
1/2 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
4 sl Bacon, cooked and crumbled
2 tb Grated Parmesan
1/3 c  Butter
1 c  Water
1 c  Flour
4    Eggs
Boil butter and water. Add flour all at once, stirring vigourously.
Cook, stirring until the mixture forms a ball that does not seperate.
Remove from heat and let cool about 10 minutes.

Preheat oven to 400F.

Beat in eggs, one at a time. Beat in cheese and bacon.

Drop dough by well rounded tablespoons into a 6" ring on greased
cookie sheets, leaving about 1/4" between scoops of dough. Sprinkle
with Parmesan.

Bake about 30 minutes, until golden brown and puffed. Serve warm.
