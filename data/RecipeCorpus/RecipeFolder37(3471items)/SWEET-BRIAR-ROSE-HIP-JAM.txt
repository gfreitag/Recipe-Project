Title: SWEET BRIAR ROSE HIP JAM
Categories: Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Water
2 tb Lemon juice
2 c  Hips; before seeding
1 1/2 c  Sugar
Prepare hips and measure 1 cup.  Boil sugar and water 4 minutes, add
hips and lemon juice and boil, covered, 15 minutes, uncover and boil
5 minutes more.  The berries should be clear and transparent and
syrup thick.  When done, pour into hot sterilized jars and seal.
