Title: HERB CHEESE
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------BACKDOOR HERB GARDEN RECIPES---------------------
1/2 ts Thyme leaves
1/8 ts Black pepper
2 ds Garlic powder
4 tb Butter
8 oz Cream cheese
2 ts Parsley
1/2 ts Chives
1/2 ts Marjoram leaves
Crumble herbs with fingers in small bowl until fine.  Add remaining
ingredients and mix well.  Refrigerate for several hours.

Use with crackers or spread on bread for a veggie sandwich with
tomatoes, cukes, mushrooms and sprouts--DELICIOUS..
