Title: Zucchini Tomato Bake^
Categories: Tomatoes, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
Chopped
1 c  Bread crumbs
1 tb Chopped fresh herbs
2 tb Grated Parmesan
1 c  Thinly sliced onion
1 tb Olive oil
2 cl Chopped garlic
1 1/4 lb Thinly sliced zucchini
1 lb Tomatoes, seeded and
Preheat oven to 400F.

Saute onion in oil until clear; add zucchini and garlic, saute until
zucchinni is crisp tender, about 5 minutes.

Combine remaining ingredients.

Put 1/3 zucchini mixture in a sprayed casserole, top with 1/3 crumb
mixture. Repeat twice, ending with bread crumbs. Bake 309 minutes
until golden.

Per serving:
192cal., 6g pro., 5g (24%) fat, 27g carb., 244mg sod., 3.2mg chol., 4g
fiber.
