Title: KING DIABLO FOR 3
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb SHERRY
1/4 c  LEMON JUICE
1/4 c  DIJON MUSTARD
BSNX01A -PRODIGY
S.TALKOFSKY -GENIE
FIDO
1 lb KING MACKEREL
Drizzle sherry, lemon and mustard over fish and allow to marinate 20
minutes. Grill 10 minutes, turning over once and basting often. Serve
on spinach leaves with lemon twist.

Preparation Time:  20 min. Cooking Time: 12 min.

Nutritional Analysis Per Serving: Calories-228 Protein-21g, Iron-
1-4mg, Sodium-114mg, Cholesterol 64mg, Fat-15g, (59% of the calories
from fat)
