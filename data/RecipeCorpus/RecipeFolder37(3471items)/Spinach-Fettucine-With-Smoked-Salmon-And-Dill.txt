Title: Spinach Fettucine With Smoked Salmon And Dill
Categories: Pasta/rice, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Light cream
2    To 3 tbsp chopped fresh dill
1/2 lb Spinach fettucine, cooked as
-package directs
6    To 8 scallions, cut into
-1/4-inch pieces
1 tb Butter
1/3 lb Smoked salmon, cut into
-1/2-inch pieces
Saute scallions in butter for 2 to 3 minutes. Lower heat and add
smoked salmon and cream. Stir and heat gently (do not boil) for about
5 minutes. Add dill. Serve immediately over hot pasta; garnish with
dill sprigs.

Makes 3 to 4 servings.

From: Steve Herrick
Source: [Yankee Magazine's Second Great Annual New England Cook-Off
Cookbook]
