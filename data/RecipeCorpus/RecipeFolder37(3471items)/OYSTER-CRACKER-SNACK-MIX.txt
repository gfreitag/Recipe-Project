Title: OYSTER CRACKER SNACK MIX
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Dill weed
1 ts Garlic powder
1 pk Oyster crackers
1 c  Wesson oil
1 pk Hidden Valley Ranch dressing
Warm oil; add dressing mix, dill weed and garlic powder.  Mix
together in a large bowl; toss until evenly distributed. Randy Rigg
