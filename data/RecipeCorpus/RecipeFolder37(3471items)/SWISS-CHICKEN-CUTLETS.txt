Title: SWISS CHICKEN CUTLETS
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Chicken broth, reduced sodim
1/4 c  White wine, dry *3
1/4 ts Oregano, dried
Parsley, chopped, fresh
Oregano sprigs, fresh
2 sl Swiss cheese, reduced fat *1
4 ea Chicken cutlets *2
2 tb Flour, all purpose
1/2 ts Pepper, black
1 tb Unsalted butter or margarine
*    Sliced Thin.  (2 ounces)

*2   4 ounces each, 1/4 inch thick

*3   or 1/4 c chicken broth, reduced sodium.
Prep time: 10 minutes.
Cook time: 15 minutes

Suggested accompanying dish: Steamed rice.
Cut each cheese slice in half; placing one half on top of each cutlet.
Starting with short end, tightly roll up cutlets, jelly roll style.
Tie securely with string.

On waxed paper, combine flour and pepper.  Mix well.  Add cutlets;
toss gently to coat.

In a large non-stick skillet, melt butter over medium heat.  Add
cutlets; cook, turning frequently until golden, about 3 minutes.

Add broth, wine, and dried oregano to skillet.  Increasw heat; bring
to a boil then reduce to  medium low.  Simmer until chicken is cooked
through and sauce is slightly thickened, about 10 - 12 minutes.
Place on serving plate; remove string.  Garnish with parsley and
oregano sprigs.

If cutlets are unavailable aat your market simply pound skinless,
boneless chicken breast halves between 2 sheets of waxed paper to a
1/4 inch thickness.
