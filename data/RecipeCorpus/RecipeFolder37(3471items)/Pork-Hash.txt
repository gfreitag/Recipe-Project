Title: Pork Hash
Categories: Chinese, Appetizers, Hawaii, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Sherry
1 ts Cornstarch
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 lb Ground Pork
3    Water Chestnuts
2 tb Green Onion; sliced 1/4"
2 ts Soy sauce
Peel chestnuts and crush. Combine all ingredients and mix well.
Put in deep bowl and steam 25 minutes. ** 104. PUDCAKE.TXT Pineapple
Upside Down Cake   Stargazer **
