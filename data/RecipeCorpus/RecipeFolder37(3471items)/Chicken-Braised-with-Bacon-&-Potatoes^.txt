Title: Chicken Braised with Bacon & Potatoes^
Categories: Foodmagazin, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Frozen pearl onions, thawed
And drained
1/3 c  Low salt chicken broth
1/2 ts Thyme
Chopped parsley
2 lg Red potatoes, cut in 3/4"
Dice
2 sl Low salt bacon, cut in 1/2"
Pieces
4    Skinned, boned chicken
Thighs
Boil potatoes 4 minutes; drain.

Crisp bacon. Transfer bacon to paper towels. Fry chicken, seasoned
with peppre, in bacon fat until brown. Turn heat to low; add
potatoes, onions, broth and thyme. Cover and simmer until potatoes
are tender and chicken is done, about 18 minutes. Remove cover,
sprinkle with bacon and cook until sauce is slightly thickened.
Garnish with parsley.
