Title: Crispy Date Balls^
Categories: Fruit, Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 60 servings
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Rice crispies
2 c  Finely chopped pecans
Coconut
1/2 c  Butter
1 pk (8oz) chopped dates
1 c  Sugar
1 lg Egg
In a large saucepan, cook the first 4 ingredients on low heat,
stirring constaantly, 10 mins. Remove from heat. Stir in vanilla,
cereal and pecans. Cool to touch.

Make mixture into 1" balls roll in cocnut. Store in refrigerator in a
airtight container.
