Title: Sweet & Sour Sauce^
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetarian, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Arrowroot powder or
Cornstarch
1 c  Water
2 tb To 3 soy sauce or tamari
2 tb Brown rice syrup
1 tb Brown rice vinegar
pn Cayenne
Bring all ingredients to a boil and cook until a thick sauce forms,
about 1 minute. Makes 1 1/4 cups.

Per 1/4 cup:
31cal., 0.4g pro., 413mg sod., 7g carb., 0g fat, omg chol., 4mg
calcium.
