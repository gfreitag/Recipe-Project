Title: Far East Fettuccine
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Cooked bay shrimp
2/3 c  Bottled plum sauce
Salt
9 oz Fresh fettuccine
1/2 lb Frozen Oriental
Vegetable mix
2    Green onion(s)
1. Cook the fettuccine and the frozen vegetables together in a large
pot of boiling, salted water until the pasta is tender but still
firm, 3-4 min. Drain well.

2. Meanwhile, cut the green onions crosswise into very thin slices.

3. In a large serving bowl, combine the pasta and vegetables, the
shrimp, green onions, and plum sauce. Toss to mix. Add salt to taste
and toss well.
