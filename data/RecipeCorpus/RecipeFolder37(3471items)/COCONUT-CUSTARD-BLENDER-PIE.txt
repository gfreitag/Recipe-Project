Title: COCONUT CUSTARD BLENDER PIE
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
4    Eggs
1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Shredded coconut
1/2 ts Nutmeg
2 c  Milk
1/3 c  Butter or Margarine
3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
Combine all ingredients in blender container. Blend on high for 1
minute. Pour into greased and floured 10" pie dish. Bake at 350^ for
45 minutes or until custard is set. Source: Connie Putnam, Warren, MI
