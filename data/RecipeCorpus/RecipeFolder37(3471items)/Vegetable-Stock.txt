Title: Vegetable Stock
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 9 cups
MMMMM--------------------FOOD AND WINE-JAN 95-------------------------
Coarsely chopped
2 lg Garlic clove(s)
Coarsely chopped
2    Thyme sprigs or
1 pn Dried thyme
2    Parsley sprigs
1    Bay leaf
6    Black peppercorns
1 ts Salt
1 tb Olive oil
1/2 lb Mushrooms
Coarsely chopped
2 lg Celery ribs
Coarsely chopped
1 lg Onion(s)
Coarsely chopped
1 md Leek
Coarsely chopped
1 md Tomato
Heat the olive oil in a stockpot. Add the mushrooms, celery, onion,
leek, tomato, and garlic and cook over high heat, stirring, until the
vegetables begin to wilt, about 5 min. Add the thyme, parsley, bay
leaf, black peppercorns, slat and 3 quarts of water and bring to a
boil. Lower the heat and simmer for 1 1/4 hours. Strain the stock.

Can be refrigerated for 3 days or frozen for 1 month.
