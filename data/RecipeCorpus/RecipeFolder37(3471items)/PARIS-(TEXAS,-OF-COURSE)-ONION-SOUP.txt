Title: PARIS (TEXAS, OF COURSE) ONION SOUP
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1 qt Milk
1 pt Half and half
2 cn Stock, chicken
2 qt Onions, sliced
1 1/2 c  Butter
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
Simmer onions in butter 45 minutes. Season to taste. Add milk, cream
and chicken broth.

Place toast in bottom of soup bowl.  Sprinkle with Parmesan. Pour
soup over toast.

Source: _A Taste of Texas_
