Title: Apricot and Rosemary Chutney
Categories: Condiments, Relishes, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 lg Garlic clove(s)
Finely chopped
2 ts Grated lemon peel
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 c  Blanched slivered
Almonds, toasted
12 oz Dried apricots, chopped
1 lg Red onion(s), chopped
2/3 c  Cider vinegar
2/3 c  (packed)
Golden brown sugar
3 oz Dried tart cherries
(about 3/4 cup)
1 1/2 tb Rosemary, chopped
Combine all ingredients except almonds in heavy large saucepan. Bring
to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring until sugar dissolves.
Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer until most liquid has evaporated
and chutney is thick, stirring occasionally, about 25 min. Mix in
almonds. Transfer chutney to bowl. chill until cold, about 3 hours.
