ZUCCHINI PRITTI

Zucchini was mentioned in the Bible (Book of Leviticus), and, until
this day
has remained part of the fare in Israel and neighboring lands.

Cut zucchini into rings and fry in oil.  Set zucchini aside.  Fry
chopped onion until transparent.  Add tomatoes, black pepper, salt
and green and/or red pepper.  Saute until soft.  Pour over zucchini
rings.
