Title: Zucchini Pizza^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Pizza, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1 1/2 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
1 c  Grated Parmesan
2    Eggs
4 c  Shredded peeled zucchini,
Drained and pressed dry
2 c  Cooked rice
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1 lb Ground beef, browned with
1 md Chopped onion
1 1/2 c  Spaghetti sauce
Combine crust ingredients and press into a greased 15X11X1" jelly roll
pan. Bake at 400F. 20-25 minutes until set and lightly browned.

Combine filling ingredients except for cheddar. Pour over crust,
sprinkle with cheese. Bake at 400F. 15 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes
before cutting and serving.
