Title: MERSEY POINT SALMON PATE
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Meats, Canadian
Yield: 1 batch
2 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Horseradish
Fresh ground pepper
1/4 lb Smoked salmon
1/2 lb Smoked mackerel fillets
8 oz Light cream cheese
Remove skin and bones from smoked mackerel. Puree all ingredients in a
food processor. Package for refrigerator.
