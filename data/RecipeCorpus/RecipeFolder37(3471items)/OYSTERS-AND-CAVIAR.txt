Title: OYSTERS AND CAVIAR
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
2 oz Black caviar
2    Lemons
2 lb Seaweed
18    Oysters, on half shell
2    Scallions
Spread seaweed in a flat basket. Arrange chilled oysters in their
shells, on the seaweed.
Thinly slice scallions into rings. Sprinkle 2 or 3 pieces on each
oyster. Top each one with a dab of caviar. Serve very cold,
accompanied by fresh, thin sliced lemon wedges. Pass well chilled
Champagne.
