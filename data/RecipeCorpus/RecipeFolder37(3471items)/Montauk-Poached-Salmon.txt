Title: Montauk Poached Salmon
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Dried whole basil
1/8 ts Pepper
4    (4-ounce)salmon fillets**
1 sm Onion, sliced
1 md Tomato, peeled and sliced
1/2 c  Fresh Mushrooms, sliced
1 1/2 c  Chablis or other dry white
-wine
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Lemon juice
1    Bay Leaf
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dried whole thyme
Instructions:

Combine first 8 ingredients in a 10-inch skillet, stirring well; bring
mixture to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat, and simmed 10 minutes.  Layer
fillets, onion, tomato, and mushrooms in wine mixture; cover and
simmer 8 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.
Transfer fish and vegetables to a serving platter.
