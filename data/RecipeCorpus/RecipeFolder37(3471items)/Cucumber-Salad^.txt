Title: Cucumber Salad^
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 sm Onions, thinly sliced
1 c  White or cider vinegar
5 md Kirby cucumbers
Salt
Peel cukes and slice very thinly. Salt generously in a loarge
non--metallic bowl. Weight down and let stand an hour.

Drain, mix with onion and pour vinegar over top. Let stand 10 minutes
before
serving.

Per serving:
53cal., 2g pro., 1g fat, 10g carb., 539mg sod., 0mg chol., 1 1/2
vegetable exchanges.
