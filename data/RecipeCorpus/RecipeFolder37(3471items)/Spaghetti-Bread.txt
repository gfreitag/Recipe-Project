Title: Spaghetti Bread
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1/3 c  Parmesan cheese, grated
1 ts Italian seasoning, dry
1 tb Olive oil
1 1/2 c  Warm water, 115 deg
2 1/2 ts Active yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Garlic salt
All ingredients must be at room temperature except the water.  Add the
ingredients into the ABM in the order listed. Select white bread and
push start.  A great 1 1/2 pound loaf to have with spaghetti or great
toast in the morning

Source: DAK 1989 Guide to Automatic Bread Making
