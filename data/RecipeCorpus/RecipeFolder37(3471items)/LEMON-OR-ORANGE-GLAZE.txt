Title: LEMON OR ORANGE GLAZE
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Lemon or Orange Juice
1 c  Confectioners' Sugar
Mix the sugar with the lemon or orange juice.  Beat well.  Then
spread thin on top of cake.  Let it drizzle down the sides, but do
not ice the cake sides.

Source:  The First Ladies Cookbook - 1969 . Chapter on Abraham & Mary
Lincoln

Posted by:  Sandy May 11/93
