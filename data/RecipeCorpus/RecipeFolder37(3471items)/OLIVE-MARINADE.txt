Title: OLIVE MARINADE
Categories: Greek, Salsa/sauce, Ethnic
Yield: 1 servings
1    Garlic clove
1 ts Oregano
1 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Vinegar
1    Bay leaf
Cut a slit in each olive.  Fill a jar with olives.  Cover with
marinade. Olives will keep many months and improve with marinating.

Source: Cooking in Modern Greece - by Theresa Karas Yianilos Typed
