Title: Peanut Butter-Chocolate Balls
Categories: Candies, Holiday, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 11 servings
1 1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1 1/2 c  Chopped nuts
4    (2 oz) squares chocolate
Flavored candy coating,
Melted
1 pk (16 oz) powered sugar
1    (12 oz) jar crunchy peanut
Butter
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Butter or margarine, melted
1 1/2 c  Flaked coconut
Mix the first 4 ingredients in a large bowl, stir well. Add crumbs,
coconut and nuts; shape into 1" balls. Roll in coating; covering
well. Remove and place on wax paper until set. Store in an airtight
container; can be frozen up to 2 months.
