Title: Mini Ruebens^
Categories: Appetizers, Holiday, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 30 servings
1 cn (8oz) sauerkraut, rinsed,
Drained and snipped
1/2 ts Caraway seed
1 c  Finely shredded Swiss
Cheese
30 sl Party rye bread
1/4 c  Creamy Dijon style mustard
Blend
4 oz Thinly sliced cooked corn
Beef
Lightly toast bread slices under broiler on a cookie sheet, about 1
minute each side.

Spread lightly with mustard, top with a slice of meat, combined
sauerkraut and caraway seed and cheese. Broil 4-5" from heat 1-2
minutes until heated through and cheese is melted. Serve immediately.

Better Homes and Gardens Holiday Appetizers 1995
