Title: Wild Willy's Number One-Derful Rub Austin American 7/94
Categories: Jerky/rubs, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Chili powder
2 tb Garlic powder
2 tb Onion powder
2 ts Cayenne powder
3/4 c  Paprika
1/4 c  Ground black pepper
1/4 c  Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
Mix the spices thoroughly in a large jar.  Store covered.  Makes
about 2 cups, enough for several briskets or slabs of ribs.  A good
all-purpose rub.
Use on ribs, brisket, chicken and more.
~---Smoke & Spice
