Title: CONEY ISLAND HOT DOGS
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 10 servings
2 tb Shortening
1 ts Chili powder
3/4 c  Catsup
1 pk Onion soup mix
1 c  Water, hot
1/2 lb Ground beef
Soak onion soup mix in hot water for about 15 minutes. Strain out
onion pieces and ground beef in shortening in large skillet. Add
seasonings, catsup and liquid drained from onions. Simmer together
about 30 minutes.
