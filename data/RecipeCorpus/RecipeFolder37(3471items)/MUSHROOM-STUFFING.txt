Title: MUSHROOM STUFFING
Categories: Breads, Poultry
Yield: 10 servings
1/4 ts Marjoram leaves
20    Wheat bread slices
2    Eggs
2 tb Oil
1 lb Mushrooms, fresh
1 c  Onions, chopped
1/2 ts Sage leaves
Cut mushrooms in quarters. Heat oil in skillet. Add mushrooms, onion,
sage, marjoram, and salt and pepper to taste; cook 10 minutes,
stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender. Remove from heat.
Cube bread and add; toss well to mix. Pour egg over stuffing and
toss. Makes 12-14 cups, enough to stuff 14-lb turkey. (Allow 1 cup
stuffing per lb of turkey.)
