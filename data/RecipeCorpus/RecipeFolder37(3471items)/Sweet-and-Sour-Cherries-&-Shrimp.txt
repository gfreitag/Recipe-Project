Title: Sweet and Sour Cherries & Shrimp
Categories: Fruit, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 9 servings
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
3 ts Ground ginger
3/4 ts Salt
3 tb Cornstarch
4 tb Sugar, using sweet cherries,
-OR-
1/2 c  And 1 tsp. sugar, using tart
-cherries
1 1/2 c  Green pepper, cut in
-strips
1 1/2 lb Red tart or dark sweet
-cherries, pitted
1 cn Water chestnuts
1 1/2 lb To 3 lbs. shrimp, cooked
2 1/4 c  Cherry juice & water
3 1/2 c  Rice, cooked
Penn. Cherry Growers

Sweet and Sour Sauce:
Drain canned cherries.  Add cherry juice and water and blend well
with rest of the sauce ingredients.  Cook, stirring constantly, until
sauce thickens. Add green pepper and cook a few minutes, then add
water chestnuts, cherries, and cooked shrimp.  Heat until warm.  Cook
rice according to package direction.  Serve over rice.  Yields: nine
servings.
