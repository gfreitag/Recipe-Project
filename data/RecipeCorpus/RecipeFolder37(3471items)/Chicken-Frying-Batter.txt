Title: Chicken Frying Batter
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Salt
2 tb Pepper
1/2 ts Parsley flakes
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Corn meal, yellow
1 1/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Baking powder
Mix all ingredients together.  Cut up chicken and wash.  Dip chicken
in batter and deep fry in hot grease.
