Title: HAM HIDEAWAYS
Categories: Appetizers, Meats, Pickles
Yield: 8 pieces
Cream cheese
Sweet pickled gherkins
2 oz (60g) thinly sliced ham
2 tb Chive-and-onion-flavored
Lay ham on waxed paper to form a 7x8-inch rectangle. Spread with
chive-and-onion flavored cream cheese. Lay a row of small, sweet
pickled gherkins along short side. Roll ham tightly around pickles.
Cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate for 30 minutes or overnight.
Slice diagonally into 1-inch pieces. Each roll makes 8 pieces.
