Title: OLD-FASHIONED POLE BEANS
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 10 servings
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 lb Pole beans; fresh
5 c  Water
1    Ham hock; 1/2 lb.
Remove strings from beans, cutting beans into 2 inch pieces.  Wash
beans.

Place water and ham hock in a Dutch oven; bring to a boil.  Reduce
heat; cover and simmer 1 hour.  Add beans, salt, and pepper; cook 30
minutes or until tender.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in 1980. Typed for you by
Nancy Coleman.
