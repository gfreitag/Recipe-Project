Title: Hollandaise Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 cup
4 tb Boiling water
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cayenne
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
3    Egg yolks
Melt the butter slowly and keep warm. Warm the lemon juice. Have water
boiling. In a double boiler, whisk egg yolks continuously until they
start to thicken. Add the water 1 T at a time, whisking until
thickened between each addition. Then beat in the lemon juice. Add
the salt and cayenne then beat in the butter and continue whisking
until thick. Serve immediately.
