Title: White Chocolate Mousse^
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Light rum
1 tb White creme de caacao
1 ts Unflavored gelatin
1 c  Whipping cream
1 lg Egg
1    Egg yolk
6 oz Coarsley chopped white
Chocolate
MMMMM-------------------------GARNISHES------------------------------
Chocolate curls
Fresh mint leaves
At high speed, beat egg and egg yolk until pale; set aside.

Melt chocolate in double boiler, stirring constantly.

Add a aquarter of the chocolate to the eggs; stir back into hot
chocolate and
cook, stirring constantly, until a candy thermometer reaches 160
degrees. Remove from heat. Pour into a large mixing bowl and let cool.

Combine liqueurs in saucepan and sprinkle gelatin over. Let soften 1
minute.
Cook over low heat, stirring, until gelatin dissolves, about 2
minutes.

Beat gelatin mixture into chocolate mixture until smooth; set aside.

Beat whipping cream until soft peaks form. Fold in half of chocolate
mixture,
then fold into remaining chocolate mixture.

Spoon into dessert diahes; chill at least 3 hours. Garnish.
