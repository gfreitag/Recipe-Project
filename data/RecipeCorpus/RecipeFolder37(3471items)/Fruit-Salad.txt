FRUIT SALAD

The abundance of fresh fruit in Israel, ripe from the fields and
orchards, makes fruit salads a natural choice.  After a good meal,
nothing is more refreshing than this cool dessert.

grapes, melon, pears, or 1 can fruit cocktail

Cut the apples and oranges into small pieces.  Add lemon juice and
sugar. Add the rest of the ingredients a short time before serving.
