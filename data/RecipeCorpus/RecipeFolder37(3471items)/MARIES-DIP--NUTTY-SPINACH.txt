Title: MARIES DIP -NUTTY SPINACH
Categories: Dips/spread, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
Thawed and well drained
2 tb Dry vegetable soup mixture
1/4 c  Chopped walnuts
1    12 oz jar Maries Ranch Salad
Dressing
1 pk 10oz frozen chopped spinach
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients.  Cover, refrigerate until
serving time, at least 2 hours.

Garnish with additional chopped walnuts.

Serve with fresh vegetables for dipping or use as a filling for fresh
mushroom caps.

Makes 1 1/2 cups.
