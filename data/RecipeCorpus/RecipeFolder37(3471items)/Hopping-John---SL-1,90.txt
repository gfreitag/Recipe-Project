Title: Hopping John - SL 1/90
Categories: Side dish, Holiday, Pasta/rice, Vegetables
Yield: 12 side servs
-chicken broth; undiluted
1 ea 16-oz package frozen black-
-eye peas
1/2 lb Cooked ham; cut into cubes
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper
1 ea Bay leaf
3 c  Cooked long-grain rice
1 c  Celery; sliced
2/3 c  Onion; chopped
1 cl Garlic; minced
1 tb Vegetable oil or bacon
-drippings
4 c  ;Water
2 ea 10-3/4 oz cans condensed
Saute celery, onion, and garlic in vegetable oil or bacon
drippings in a large Dutch oven until tender.  Add water and
remaining ingredients except rice.  Bring to a boil; cover, reduce
heat, and simmer 40 minutes or until peas are tender.  Remove and
discard bay leaf, and serve over rice. Yield: 12 side dish servings
or 6 main dish servings.

Note: To cook rice with black-eyed peas, stir 1 cup uncooked
ong-grain rice into peas after 40 minutes, and simmer an additional
35 minutes.
