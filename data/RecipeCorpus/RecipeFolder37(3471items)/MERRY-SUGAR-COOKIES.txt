Title: MERRY SUGAR COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 54 servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 pk NESTLE Toll House semi-sweet
-chocolate Rainbow Morsels,
-divided (9 oz)
2 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Butter or margarine,
-softened
Preheat oven to 375'F. In small bowl, combine flour, baking soda,
baking powder and salt; set aside.

In large mixer bowl, beat butter and sugar until creamy. Blend in egg
and vanilla extract. Gradually beat in flour mixture. (Batter will be
stiff.) Stir in 1 cup Nestle Toll House semi-sweet chocolate Rainbow
Morsels. Shape rounded teaspoonfuls dough into balls; place on
ungreased cookie sheets. Gently press 3 of 4 remaining Rainbow
Morsels into each balls.

Bake 8-10 minutes until edges are lightly browned. Let stand 2
minutes. Remove from cookie sheets; cool.

Makes about 4 1/2 dozen cookies.
