Title: CREAMY SMOOTH PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
2 ts Vanilla Extract
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking Soda
2 c  Unbleached All-Purpose Flour
1 c  Butter Or Margarine
1 1/2 c  Creamy Peanut Butter
1 c  Granulated Sugar
1 1/2 c  Brown Sugar; Packed
2 lg Eggs
Cream the butter and peanut butter.  Beat in the sugars, blending
well. Add the eggs, vanilla, salt, baking soda and flour and mix
well. Drop by spoonfuls onto a greased cookie sheet. Bake in a
preheated 350 Degree oven for 10 to 12 minutes

Yield: About 5 dozen cookies
