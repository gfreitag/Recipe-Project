Title: Baked Salmon
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1    TOMATO ; THINLY SLICED
2 tb FRESH PARSELY ;CHOPPED
1/4 c  DRIED BREAD CRUMBS
1 tb VEGETABLE OIL
1 lb SALMON FILLETS OR STEAKS;
;SKINLESS
1 ts DRIED OREGANO
2    GARLIC CLOVES
PEPPER ; TO TASTE
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  Spray shallow baking pan with non stick
vegetable oil cooking spray.  Place fish in baking pan and sprinkle
with oregano, garlic and pepper. Layer with tomato, onion and
parsley.  Mix bread crumbs with oil and sprinkle on top.  Bake 8 to
10 minutes, or just until fish flakes easily. Makes 4 servings.

Nutritional analysis per serving:
Calories-225, Cholesterol-41mg, Sodium-74mg.

From Great Atlantic and Pacific Seafood
