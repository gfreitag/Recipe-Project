Title: Meat Ball Stroganoff
Categories: Meats
Yield: 3 servings
Dash of pepper
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Water
Noodles, cooked
1 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Dry bread crumbs, fine
1/4 c  Onions, chopped fine
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1/4 ts Salt
Mix thoroughly beef, bread crumbs, onion, egg and seasonings.  Shape
into 16 meat balls.  In a skillet, brown meat balls; pour off fat.
Stir in soup, sour cream and water.  Cover; cook over low heat for 20
minutes. Stir now and then.  Serve with noodles.
