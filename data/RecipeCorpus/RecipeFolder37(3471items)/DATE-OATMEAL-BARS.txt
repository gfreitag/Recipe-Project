Title: DATE-OATMEAL BARS
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
1/3 c  Margarine
1 c  Uncooked quick-cooking
-oatmeal
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Firmly packed light brown
-sugar
MMMMM------------------------DATE FILLING-----------------------------
2 tb Sugar
2 c  Chopped pitted dates
1 c  Water
1. Preheat oven to 400Ã¸F. Grease 9x9x2 inch square pans.
2. Simmer uncovered, dates, water and sugar 7-10 minutes until thick.
Cool.
3. Sift flour and salt together.  Add Brown Sugar.  Using a pastry
blender, cut in margarine until the size of small peas. Mix in
oatmeal. Pack half the mixture in to the bottom of the pan; top with
the date mixture. Add remaining oatmeal mixture to form top layer.
4. Bake 45-50 minutes until tan on top. Cool upright in pan on a wire
rack and cut into bars.
