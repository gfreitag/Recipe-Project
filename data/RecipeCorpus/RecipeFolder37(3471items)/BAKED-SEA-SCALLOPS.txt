Title: BAKED SEA SCALLOPS
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Fresh bread crumbs
Salt and pepper to taste
12 oz Sea scallops
4 oz Butter, melted
1 oz Sherry
Slice scallops in half and place in a buttered casserole dish. Season
with salt and pepper. Combine butter, sherry and crumbs. Pour over
scallops. Bake at 350 degs for 10-15 mins.
