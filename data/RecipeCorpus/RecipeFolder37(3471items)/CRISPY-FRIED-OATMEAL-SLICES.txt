Title: CRISPY FRIED OATMEAL SLICES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Finely-chopped cooked ham
-OR- crumbled cooked bacon
-- drained
2 c  Quaker Oats, uncooked
-- (quick or old-fashioned)
1 ts Salt
3 1/2 c  Water
Stir oats into briskly boiling salted water.  Cook 1 minute for quick
oats, stirring occasionally.  Cook 5 minutes or longer for old
fashioned oats.  Cover pan, remove from heat and let stand 5 minutes.
Stir in ham or bacon.

Pour into ungreased 8-1/2" x 4-1/2" x 2-1/2" loaf pan. Cool slightly.
Cover and refrigerate several hours or overnight.  Cut into 16 slices.
Pan-fry in small amount of butter until golden brown, about 10
minutes per side.  Serve hot with butter and syrup.
