Title: FRESH GREEN AND WAX BEANS
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables, Salads, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Water
1 lb Fresh beans (green or wax
-or a combination of both)
1. Wash and cut beans into 1 1/2 inch pieces.
2. Place in a deep 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole
with the water.
3. Heat, covered, in Microwave Oven 12 minutes or until tender. Stir
occasionally during cooking.
4. Drain water and season to taste.
