Title: Creamy Dilled Salmon
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------COLORADO HIGH COUNTRY COOKIN---------------------
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Yogurt
1/4 ts Dill weed
1    Eggs
1/2 c  Yogurt
3 tb Water
1 tb Oil
1/4 ts Dill weed
4    Salmon steaks
Salmon steaks should be 1" thick.  In 12"x7" baking dish, combine
yogurt, water, oil, and dill weed.  Add salmon; cover and refrigerate
one hour. Remove salmon from sauce; grill or broil until fish flakes
with a fork.

Meanwhile, melt butter in small saucepan; stir in yogurt, dill weed
and beaten egg.  Over low heat, cook and stir until thickened, being
careful not to boil.  Serve warm with salmon.
