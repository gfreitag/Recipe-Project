Title: Jerky - Home Style
Categories: Meats, Jerky/rubs
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 ts Hickory salt
1/2 ts Garlic powder
ds Hot sauce
1 lb Beef, cut in strips
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Onion salt
1 ts Black pepper
Directions: Combine all the ingredients and marinate in the fridge for
at least 8 hours, mixing occasionally. Then, skewer the marinated
strips on bamboo skewers (or toothpicks) and hand the strips in the
oven with a pan below to catch the drippings. Set the oven on it's
lowest setting (100-150 Deg F) and use something to keep the door
ajar. The jerky should be dry after 8-10 hours.
