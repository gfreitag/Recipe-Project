Title: TOMATO PATTIES
Categories: Pcake/waffl, Breakfast
1/2 tsp. baking soda 2 eggs 2-1/2
Yield: 1 servings  8 Medium Tomatoes
cups cracker crumbs

Peel and mash tomatoes.  Add soda, eggs and crumbs.  Fry like
pancakes in a hot skillet.  Serve with pancake syrup if desired.

From: Lancaster Farming Shared By: Pat Stockett
