Title: TURKEY PATE
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 24 servings
1 tb Pimento, chopped
2 tb Parsley, minced
Paprika
Assorted crackers
1 pk Onion soup mix
1 c  Sour cream
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
2 c  Minced cooked turkey
1/2 c  Pecans, finely chopped
Combine soup mix with sour cream and mayonnaise.  Stir in turkey,
nuts and pimento.  Refrigerate for several hours for flavors to meld
and mixture to thicken. Mound on a platter, sprinkle with parsley and
paprika. Serve chilled with crackers.  Makes about 2 cups.
