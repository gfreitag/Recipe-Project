Title: MARYANNE'S CHEESECAKE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 cake
1    Stick butter (softened)
1 tb Vanilla
1 pt Sour cream
-lemon rind
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb Flour
3 tb Cornstarch
32 oz Cream cheese (softened)
Crust

2 C  graham
1/2 C sugar
2/3 C melted salt butter

mix crust ingredients and fill a large spring pan.
Beat filling together until smooth, Fill crust,
Bake 90 min @ 225 degrees
Open door & let cool slowly, Best made 2-3 days in
advance
