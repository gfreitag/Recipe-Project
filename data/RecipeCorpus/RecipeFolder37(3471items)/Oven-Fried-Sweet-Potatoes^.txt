Title: Oven Fried Sweet Potatoes^
Categories: Potatoes, Low cal/fat
Yield: 7 servings
Vegetable cooking spray
1 tb Finely chopped fresh
Parsley
1 ts Grataed orange rind
1 cl Garlic, minced
4 md Sweet potatoes, peeled and
Sliced 1/4" thick
1 tb Olive oil
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Combine potatoes, oil, salt and pepper; coating well. Bake on a large
sprayed cookie sheet at 400F for 30 minutes, until tender, turning
halfway through.

Combine remaining ingredients; stir well and sprinkle over potatoes.

Per 1/2 cup serving:
176 cal., 2.5g pro., 2.5g (13%) fat, 36.6g carb., 4.5g fiber, 0mg
chol., 0.9mg iron, 104mg sodium, 35mg calcium.
