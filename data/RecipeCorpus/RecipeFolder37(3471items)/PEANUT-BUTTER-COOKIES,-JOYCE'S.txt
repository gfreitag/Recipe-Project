Title: PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES, JOYCE'S
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
2 3/4 c  Flour
2 ts Soda
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Peanut butter
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown sugar
2    Eggs
Mix all to - gether and form into small balls and flatten with fork.
Bake in 350 oven 12 - 15 minutes
