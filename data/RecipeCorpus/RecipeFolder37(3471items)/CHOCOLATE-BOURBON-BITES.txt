Title: CHOCOLATE-BOURBON BITES
Categories: Candies, Alcohol, Holiday
Yield: 30 servings
1/2 c  Miniature semisweet
Chocolate pieces
1    Slightly beaten egg white
1 c  Finely chopped pecans
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/4 c  Bourbon
1 c  All-purpose flour
3 tb Unsweetened cocoa powder
In a large mixer bowl beat margarine or butter and sugar with electric
mixer on medium speed till fluffy.  Add bourbon; beat well.
Gradually add flour and cocoa powder, beating till well mixed. Stir
in chocolate pieces.
Chill 2 hours or till firm enough to handle.

Shape dough into 1-inch balls.  Roll in egg white, then in nuts.
Place on a lightly greased baking sheed.

Bake in a 350 F oven about 12 minutes or till edges are firm (The
center will still be soft).  Cool on bakin sheet for 1 minute.
Finish cooling on wire racks.  Makes about 30
