Title: NOT MOM'S MEATLOAF
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 lg Egg, beaten
1 tb Tomato paste
1/2 lb Lean, ground beef
1/2 c  Shredded mozzarella
1    Clove garlic
1    Shallot or onion, minced
2 ts Butter
1/2 c  Fine bread crumbs
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Black pepper
Mince together garlic and shallot.  Melt butter in medium skillet.
Add garlic and shallot, and saute until shallot is trnaslucent, about
5 minutes.  Stir in bread crumbs.  Remove from heat.  Mix in oregano,
thyme, pepper, salt, hot pepper sauce, egg, and tomato paste.  Add
bread crubm mixture to ground beef and gently mix in.

Spread half the meat mixture in loaf pan.  Sprinkle with cheese, and
gently spread remaining meat mixture over the top.  Bake 350 45
mins--let stand 5 mins before serving.
