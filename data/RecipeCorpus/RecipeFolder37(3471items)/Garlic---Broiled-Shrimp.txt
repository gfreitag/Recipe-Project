Title: Garlic - Broiled Shrimp
Categories: Main dish, Appetizers, Fish/seafoo, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Green onion; minced
3 cl Garlic; minced
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
Coarsely ground black pepper
2 lb Unpeeled large shrimp
1/2 c  Butter or margarine; melted
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Fresh parsley; minced
Peel shrimp, leaving tails on; devein and butterfly.  Set shrimp
aside.
Combine next 6 ingredients in a large shallow dish.  Add shrimp,
tossing well to coat.  Cover and marinate at least 30 minutes,
stirring occasionally.
Broil shrimp 4 inches from heat for 3 to 4 minutes; turn and broil
an additional 3 to 4 minutes or until done.  Sprinkle with pepper;
serve with pan drippings.  Yield: 4 to 6 servings.

From Lilly B. Smith of Virginia  in August, 1983 "Southern Living"
