Title: Vegetable Eggplant soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 10 servings
Combination
1    14 1/2 ounce can tomatoes
Coarsely chopped, including
Juice
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts White pepper
Salt to taste
1 ts Garlic, finely chopped
1    Medium onion, finely chopped
3    Medium carrots,
Finely chopped
2    Ribs celery, finely chopped
1    Medium eggplant, peeled
And finely chopped
1    Medium turnip, finely
Finely chopped
8 c  Chicken stock, water or a
In a heavy large pot over low heat, cook the garlic, onion, carrots,
celery eggplant and turnips until they begin to soften, about 5
minutes. Do not brown.

Add stock or other liquid.  Over high heat, bring liquid to a boil and
skim off any gray foam that rises to the surface.  Reduce heat and
simmer until vegetables are crisp-tender. 7 to 10 minutes.

Add tomatoes and simmer 5 minutes longer. Add herbs, pepper and salt.
