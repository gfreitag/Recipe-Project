Title: CUCUMBER ROUNDS
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Seasoned salt
1    Loaf multi grain bread
Parsley sprigs
1 lb Cucumber
1 pk Cream cheese, softened 8 oz.
1 1/2 ts Mayonnaise
Cut cucumber in half horizontally.  Peel, seed and shred half of
cucumber. Measure 1/3 cup shredded cucumber.  Slice remaining
cucumber for garnish. Cut slices in half; set aside.  Combine cream
cheese, mayonnaise and seasoned salt, mixing until blended.  Stir in
shredded cucumber. Cut two 2 inch rounds from each bread slice.
Spread each round with 1 teaspoon cucumber spread.  Garnish each
sandwich with a thin slice of cucumber and a sprig of parsley.  Makes
48 sandwiches. Randy Rigg
