Title: MOM'S COCONUT PIE
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Milk
1 pk Coconut (7 oz.)
4    Eggs, well beaten
1/2 c  Self rising flour
1 3/4 c  Sugar
1/2    Stick of butter, melted
Combine all ingredients.  Mix well and pour into 2 pie pans. Bake at
325 degrees for 30 minutes or until brown.  This recipe makes its own
crust.
