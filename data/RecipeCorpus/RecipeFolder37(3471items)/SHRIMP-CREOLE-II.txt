Title: SHRIMP CREOLE II
Categories: Main dish, Fish/seafoo, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
15 oz Can tomatoes
1 ts Sugar
1/8 ts To 1/4 t Tabasco sauce
Few leaves dried thyme
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
3 tb Butter or margarine
3/4 c  Finely chopped green pepper
1 c  Finely chopped onion
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
2 tb Flour
2 tb Dried parsley flakes
1. Melt butter in a deep, 3-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic
casserole 30
seconds in Microwave Oven.
2. Add green pepper, onion and celery and heat, uncovered, in
Microwave Oven
4 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender. Stir occasionally.
3. Add flour and parsley flakes and stir until well blended.
4. Stir in tomatoes and heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 3 minutes.
5. Add remaining ingredients and stir to blend. Heat, covered, in
Microwave Oven 7 minutes or until shrimp are pink and tender. Do not
overcook as shrimp
will become tough.
6. Let stand 3 to 5 minutes before serving. Serve over hot rice.
