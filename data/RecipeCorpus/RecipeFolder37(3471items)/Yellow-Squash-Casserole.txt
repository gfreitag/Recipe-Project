Title: Yellow Squash Casserole
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
-divided
1 1/2 c  Cheddar -- shredded
2 oz Pimiento -- diced
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 lb Sliced squash
1 c  Water
2 sm Onions -- minced
2 tb Butter -- melted
2    Eggs -- beaten
1 c  Ritz cracker crumbs --
Simmer squash in water 10 min till tender, drain and mash. Saute onion
in butter till tender, combine with everything, reserving 1/4 cup Ritz
cracker crumbs. Spoon into greased 2 qt, top with crumbs. Bake 350F
45 min.

Diana Hamilton  10 Nov 1994
