Title: RICH CUSTARD
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1 pn Salt
6    Eggs, whole
Almond Extract
1 qt Milk, scalded
1/2 c  Cold Milk
1/2 c  Sugar
Scald 1 quart milk; add sugar, and salt.  Beat eggs and add cold
milk to them.  Stir, and gradually add to the hot milk mixture.  Cook
in top of double boiler until custard coats the spoon.  When cold,
add the flavoring.

Source:  The First Ladies Cookbook - 1969 . Chapter on George & Marsha
Washington
