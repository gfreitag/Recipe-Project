Title: SPECIALITY CASSEROLE
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Casseroles, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
-drained
2 oz Jar pimiento, drained &
-chopped
1 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
8 oz Cream cheese, cubed
10 1/2 oz Cream of mushroom soup, can
-ned, condensed
12 oz Whole kernel corn, can,
1. In a large, heat-resistant, non-metallic bowl, brown meat,
uncovered, in Microwave Oven 4 minutes. Stir occasionally to break up
meat chunks. 2. Drain excess fat.
3. Add onions and heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 3 minutes or
until onion
is tender.
4. Add remaining ingredients to meat mixture. Pour into a shallow,
2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic baking dish.
5. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 9 minutes or until sauce is
bubbly.
