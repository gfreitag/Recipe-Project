Title: NEW WAVE CHOCOLATE SPRITZ COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 90 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract
2    Eggs
2 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 cn Cinnamon candies (4 oz)
1 pk NESTLE Toll House semi-sweet
-chocolate morsels (6 oz)
1 c  Butter, softened
2/3 c  Sugar
Over hot (not boiling) water, melt Nestle Toll House semi-sweet
chocolate morsels, stirring until smooth; set aside.

In large mixer bowl, beat butter, sugar and vanilla extract until
creamy. Beat in eggs. Stir in melted morsels. Gradually beat in
flour. Cover dough; refrigerate 30-45 minutes.

Preheat oven to 400'F. Place dough in cookie press fitted with star
plate. Press dough into 2" circles on ungreased cookie sheets;
decorate with cinnamon candies.

Bake 5 minutes or just until set. Let stand on cookie sheets 2
minutes. Remove from cookie sheets; cool completely.

Makes about 7 1/2 dozen cookies.
