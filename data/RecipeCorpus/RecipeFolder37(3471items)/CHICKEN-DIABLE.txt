Title: CHICKEN DIABLE
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Prepared mustard
1 ts Salt
1 ts Curry powder
1    Broiler/fryer cut up
4 tb Butter  (1/2 stick)
1/2 c  Honey
Wash chicken, pat dry.
Melt butter in shallow baking pan.  Stir in rest of ingredients.
Roll chicken pieces in baking mixture to coat both sides.  Arrange
meaty side up in dish.
Bake 375 for 1 hour or until tender and glazed.  Turn once and baste
frequently.
