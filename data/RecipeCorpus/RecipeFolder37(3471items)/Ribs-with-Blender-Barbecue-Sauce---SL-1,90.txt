Title: Ribs with Blender Barbecue Sauce - SL 1/90
Categories: Meats, Holiday, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Chili powder
1/2 c  Catsup
1/2 c  White vinegar
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
5 lb Country-style pork ribs or
-loin spare ribs
16 oz Can apricots; drained
1/4 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
Place ribs in a large Dutch oven; cover with water.  Bring water
to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer 30 minutes.  Drain ribs,
and arrange in a large, shallow dish.
Combine apricots and remaining ingredients in container of an
electric blender; blend until smooth.  Pour sauce over ribs, turning
to coat; cover and marinate in refrigerator 8 hours, turning
occasionally.
Remove ribs from marinade, and place on a lightly greased rack in
broiler pan, reserving marinade for basting.  Bake at 425 degrees F
for 40 minutes, basting twice.  Yield: 6 servings.

From Marjorie Henson of Kentucky, in January, 1990 "Southern Living"
