Title: SESAME SEED DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Low cal/fat
Yield: 99 servings
1 ts Soy sauce
2 ts Dry pectin (eg.Certo)
1/4 ts Salt
Paprika
1 ts Sesame seeds
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Rice Vinegar
In a small heavy skillet, stir sesame seeds over medium-high heat
until golden.  Watch carefully so seeds don't burn.  Set toasted
seeds aside. In a small container with a tight-fitting lid, combine
oil, water, vinegar, soy sauce, pectin and salt. Cover tightly; shake
vigorously 15 seconds. Add toasted sesame seeds and a dash of
paprika; shake again.Refrigerate until served, up to 24 hours. Shake
before using. Makes about 1/2 Cup.
