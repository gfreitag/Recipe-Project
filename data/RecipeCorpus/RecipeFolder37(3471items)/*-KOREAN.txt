Title: * KOREAN: ORANGE-FLAVORED BEAN SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Korean, Ethnic
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Sugar
Freshly graged peel 1 small
- orange
1/2 c  Korean-style bean paste
- (kochu jang)
1 tb Red wine vinegar
2 tb Fresh orange juice
In a small bowl, combine all ingredients until smooth. Serve at once
or cover and refrigerate until needed.

Variation: Use red peppers, eat over rice.

Makes 3/4 cup
