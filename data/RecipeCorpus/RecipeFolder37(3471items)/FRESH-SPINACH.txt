Title: FRESH SPINACH
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 2 servings
10 oz Pk fresh spinach
1. Wash spinach and remove any bruised leaves or thick stems. Shake
excess water off leaves.
2. Place spinach in a 2-quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole.
3. Heat, tightly covered, in Microwave Oven 5 minutes. Stir after 2
minutes.
4. Drain excess water and season to taste.
