Title: NECTARINE SALAD BOWL
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1 sm Head lettuce
2 c  Fresh raspberries; or halved
- strawberries
1/2 c  Seedless green grapes; or
- blueberries
4 md Nectarines; sliced (2 cups)
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Ginger
1 ts Lemon juice
3 oz Cream cheese; softened
MMMMM----------------NUTRITIONAL DATA PER SERVING---------------------
4 x  *Gm Fat
35 x  *Mg Sodium
200 x  *Mg Potassium
100 x  *Calories
2 x  *Gm Protein
16 x  *Gm Carbohydrate
In medium bowl, combine nectarines, sugar, ginger and lemon juice;
toss gently.  Cover; refrigerate 1 hour. Drain nectarines, reserving
liquid. In small bowl, blend cream cheese and reserved liquid. To
serve, line serving bowl with outer lettuce leaves; shred remaining
lettuce and place in bowl. Arrange nectarines, raspberries and grapes
on shredded lettuce. Serve with cream cheese dressing.
