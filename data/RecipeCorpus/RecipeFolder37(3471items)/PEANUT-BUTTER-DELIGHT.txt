Title: PEANUT BUTTER DELIGHT
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Graham wafers;crushed finely
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Margarine
1 c  Peanut butter
2 c  Icing sugar; confectioner's
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
2 tb Margarine
2 oz Chocolate; semisweet
NB. Use the finest grain powdered sugar for this recipe and graham
cracker crumbs should be ground to a fine powder

Cream margarine, peanut butter & sugar until smooth.  Blend in graham
wafer crumbs & vanilla, mix well. Spread evenly in 9" square pan and
chill. Melt the chocolate and margarine and pour over. Chill until it
looks dull (about 30 minutes). Score and cut with sharp knife.

Source: Gail Teichmann posted by Anne MacLellan
