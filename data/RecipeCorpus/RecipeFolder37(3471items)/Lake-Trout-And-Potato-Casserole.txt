Title: Lake Trout And Potato Casserole
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
1 cl Garlic; minced
4    Sprigs parsley, finely
-chopped or
1/2 ts Dried tarragon
1    Fresh hot chili, stemmed,
-seeded and finely chopped
4    Lake trout fillet portions
Salt and pepper
1/4 c  Vegetable oil or
1/4 c  Butter; melted
4 md Potatoes; thinly sliced
1 lg Onion; thinly sliced
1 bn Spinach; chopped, optional
Sprinkle the fish with salt and pepper on both sides. Brush a
baking dish with some of the oil or butter and cover the bottom
with half of the potato and onion slices. Add the optional layer
of spinach. Add the fish. Sprinkle them with more oil, the garlic,
parsley/ tarragon and the chopped chili. Cover with the remaining
potato and onion slices and pour on the rest of the oil. Bake in a
preheated 350 F oven for 1 hour, or until the potatoes are soft.
