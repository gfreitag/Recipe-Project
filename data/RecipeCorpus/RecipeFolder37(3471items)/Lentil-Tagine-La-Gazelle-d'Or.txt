Title: Lentil Tagine La Gazelle d'Or
Categories: Vegetables, Foodmagazin
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Ground white pepper
1 ts Salt
3 1/2 c  Water
Plus more if needed
1 c  Flat-leaf parsley
Washed, dried, chopped
1 c  Packed cilantro sprigs
Washed, dried, chopped
1 lb Lentils (abt 2 1/2 cups)
Picked over
4 lg Vine-ripened tomatoes
Coarsely chopped
1 lg Onion(s)
Finely chopped
1/4 c  Olive oil
2    Garlic clove(s), chopped
1 ts Paprika
In a 3-quart saucepan combine lentils, tomatoes, onion, oil, garlic,
paprika, salt and pepper, and water, making sure water cover lentils
and adding additional water if needed. Simmer lentil mixture,
covered, over moderately low heat 45 min, or until lentils are
tender. Stir in parsley and cilantro and cook 1 min more.
