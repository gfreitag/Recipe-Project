Title: Spinach Dip In Hollowed-Out Sourdough Bread
Categories: Vegetables, Dips/spread, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
- favorite *
1/2 cn Water chestnuts -- chopped
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1    Loaf Sourdough bread
1    10 oz box Frozen spinach,
- thawed, squeezed out
1    Tub Sour cream
1 pk Onion soup mix -- your
* I prefer Knorr's and I have also tried the onion/mushroom and other
onion varieties.

Mix spinach, sour cream, soup mix, water chestnuts and mayonnaise and
leave in the refrigerator for a few hours.  Put in a hollowed-out
loaf of very sour sourdough bread and serve with more bread
pieces...yummy!
