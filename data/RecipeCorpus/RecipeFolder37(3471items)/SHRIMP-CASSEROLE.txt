Title: SHRIMP CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
1    Stick of butter
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Ro-Tel tomatoes
3 lb Shrimp
1    Box of yellow rice
1 lg Onion
Cook rice as directed on the package.  Saute the large onion and
shrimp in butter until the shrimp turn pink.  Add the cream of
mushroom soup, Ro-Tel tomatoes and cooked rice to shrimp and onion.
Mix all ingredients together and pour into the pan.  Bake at 350
degrees for 1 hour. Randy Rigg
