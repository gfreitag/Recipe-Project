Title: APPLE BROWNIES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
3/4 ts Soda
3/4 ts Baking powder
3/4 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2    Stick margarine
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3    Apples, chopped
3/4 c  Nuts
2    Eggs
Cream margarine, sugar and eggs.  Add flour, soda, baking powder and
cinnamon; mix well.  Stir in apples and nuts.  Bake at 375 degrees
for 40 minutes. Randy Rigg
