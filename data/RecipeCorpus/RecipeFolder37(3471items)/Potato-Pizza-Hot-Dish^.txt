Title: Potato Pizza Hot Dish^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Potatoes, Pizza, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 md Chopped onion
1 cn (14oz) pizza sauace
2 c  (8oz) shredded mozzarella
3 c  To 4 peeled sliced potatoes
1 cn Cheddar cheese soup
1/2 c  Milk
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
Put potatoes in a greased 13X9" baking dish. Cover with combined soup
and milk. Spread browned beef and onion over soup. Pour pizza sauce
over. Bake at 350F about an hour and a half until the potatoes are
tender. Sprinkle with cheese and bake until melted.
