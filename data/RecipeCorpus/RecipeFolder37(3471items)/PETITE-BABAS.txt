Title: PETITE BABAS
Categories: Breads, Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 12 servings
3 md Eggs (room temperature)
Rum syrup
Sweetened Whipped Cream
Sliced Almonds
2 c  Unsifted Flour (about)
1/4 c  Sugar
1 pk Active Dry Yeast
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Margarine
MMMMM-------------------------RUM SYRUP------------------------------
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Rum
Combine 2/3 cup flour, sugar and yeast.
Heat milk and margarine until very warm (120 - 130 F). Add to dry
ingredients and beat 2 minutes.
Stir in more flour to make a stiff batter.
Cover; Let rise until doubled - about 1 hour.
Stir down. Spoon evenly into 12 greased 3x1 1/2 inch muffin pans. Let
rise, just until batter reaches top of cups, about 25 minutes.
Bake at 375F about 15 minutes or until done.
Before removing from pans, immediately prick cakes with fork. Slowly
spoon syrup over cakes.
Turn out onto wire rack after syrup is absorbed. Serve topped with
whipped cream and almonds.
Rum Syrup: Bring 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup rum and 1/2 cup water to a full
boil, stirring. Remove from heat; let cool.
