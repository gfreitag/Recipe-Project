Title: CHOCOLATE CHIP BITES
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Low cal/fat
Yield: 200 cookies
2 1/2 c  All purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 c  Canola oil
1 c  Light brown sugar
3/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg
2    Egg whites
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Lightly spray or wipe two baking sheets
with vegetable oil.  In a large mixing bowl, cream the oil, sugar,egg
and egg whites until smooth.  Add flour, soda and salt.  Mix well.
Add the chocolate chips and mix gently.  Place dough in 1/2 teaspoon
mounds on sheet.  Bake for 7 minutes or until brown.
