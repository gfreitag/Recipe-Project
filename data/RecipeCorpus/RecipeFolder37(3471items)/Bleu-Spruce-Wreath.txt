Title: Bleu Spruce Wreath
Categories: Foodmagazin, Cheese/eggs, Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 20 servings
1 cn Crushed pineapple; drained
.  (8oz)
2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
Garnishes: fresh parsley,
.  chopped red bell pepper,
8 oz Cream cheese
4 oz Bleu cheese; crumbled
2 tb Brandy or pineapple juice
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Instant onion; minced
Place unwrapped cream cheese in a 2-quart glass measure, Microwave on
medium (50 percent) 1@/2 minutes to soften. Blend in bleu cheese. Add
brandy, Worcestershire. onion and drained pineapple, blend thoroughly.
Fold in Cheddar; mix well.

Place drinking glass in center of serving plate; spoon cheese mixture
to form a wreath around glass. Smooth with a spatula; remove glass.
Garnish as desired with parsley and bell pepper.

Optional: Form cheese mixture into shape of a Christmas tree. In
addition to chopped parsley and bell pepper, add a few pecan halves
for the tree trunk and a star (cut from lemon rind or yellow bell
pepper) at top of tree.

Serve with crackers.

Nutritional analysis per serving: 121 calories, 9 grams fat, 4 grams
carbohydrates, 29 milligrams cholesterol, 187 milligrams sodium, 66
percent of calories from fat.

** Fort Worth Star Telegram -- Food section -- 29 November 1995 **
