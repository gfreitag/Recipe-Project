Title: DEVONSHIRE CREAM
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Powdered sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
From:  Gaye Levy ** DTXT63A
2 c  Sour cream
Mix all ingredients in a bowl and chill. Serve with scones and big
bowls of fresh berries OR served with as a dip with a fruit platter.
This is a great appetizer for a brunch. This can be prepared 2 days
ahead amd refrigerate.
