Title: Southwestern Chicken Salad Sandwiches
Categories: Poultry, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-------------------SHARED BY IRIS GRAYSON------------------------
1/3 c  Finely chopped sweet red
-pepper
1/3 c  Minced green onions
8 ea (1/2 oz./ea) slices reduced
-calories whole wheat bread;
-toasted
16 md Fresh spinach leaves
1 c  Alfalfa sprouts
4 oz HEALTHY CHOICE Fat Free
-Cream Cheese; softened
1/3 c  Nonfat sour cream
2 tb No-salt-added mild salsa
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground red pepper
1 1/2 c  Shredded cooked chicken
Combine first 6 ingredients in a small bowl.  Stir in chicken,
chopped pepper, and green onions.

Spread mixture over 4 slices of bread; top with spinach leaves.  Place
alfalfa sprouts over spinach; top with remaining 4 bread slices.
Spray both sides of each sandwich with cooking spray; place on a hot
griddle or skillet coated with cooking spray.  Cook 2 minutes on each
side or until bread is lightly browned and cheese melts.  Serve
immediately.  Yield:  4 servings (23% calories from fat).

PER SANDWICH:  calories 225, fat 6.0g; carb 17.6g; protein 25.3g; chol
52mg; sodium 595mg.
