Title: GARLICKY GREEN BEANS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
Salt
Ground pepper, fresh
1 lb Green beans, topped/tailed
2 tb Butter
1    Garlic clove, fine chop
Boil beans ahead of time then reheat in the garlic/butter mixture.
Always run
cold water over green vegetables to stop the cooking. This also helps
them retain their colour.
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil on high heat. Add beans
and boil
until crisp-tender, about 2 to 4 minutes. Alternately, steam beans for
about
5 minutes, until crisp-tender.
2. Drain beans and refresh under cold running water.
3. Heat butter and garlic together in skillet on medium heat. Toss in
beans and saute until beans are hot and coated with mixture. Season
with salt and pepper.
