Title: Ramen Pasta Salad
Categories: Pasta/rice, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb White vinegar
2 ts Sugar
2 ts Seasoned salt
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts White pepper
Paprika
3 pk Ramen noodles -- uncooked
3 c  Finely shredded cabbage
1/2 c  Thinly sliced celery
1/2 c  Finely shredded Cheddar
Cheese
1/2 c  Diced carrot
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
Prepare Ramen noodles according to package directions but do not add
spice packets; drain.
In large bowl combine noodles, cabbage, celery, cheese, and
carrot.
In small bowl blend oil, vinegar, sugar, seasoned salt, dry mustard,
and pepper.  Add to noodle mixture; toss to coat.  Chill thoroughly.
Toss gently and sprinkle with paprika before serving.

8 servings.
