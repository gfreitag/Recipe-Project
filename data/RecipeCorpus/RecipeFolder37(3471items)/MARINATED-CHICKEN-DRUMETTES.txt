Title: MARINATED CHICKEN DRUMETTES
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Plum jelly
18    Green onions
-minced
6    Garlic cloves
3/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 c  Honey
10 lb Chicken drumettes
-(little drumsticks
-from wings)
1 1/2 c  Soy sauce
3/4 c  Dry sherry
1 1/8 c  Hoysin Sauce
Combine all ingredients except chicken in saucepan.  Bring to boil and
simmer 5 minutes.  Cool.  Pour sauce over chicken, cover and
refrigerate overnight.
Preheat oven to 375.  Place chicken on greased cookie sheets and bake
uncovered for 1 to 1-1/2 hours, basting frequently.  Switch pans while
cooking so that they cook evenly.
