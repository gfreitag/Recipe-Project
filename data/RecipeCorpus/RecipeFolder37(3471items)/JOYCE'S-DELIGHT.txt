Title: JOYCE'S DELIGHT
Categories: Holiday
Yield: 8 servings
1 pk Minature marshmallows
1 lg Container sour cream
2 sm Cans manderine oranges
1 cn Pineapple chunks
1 sm Pkg. coconut(long)
Drain fruit and mix all ingredients to-gether. You may want to use 2
pkg marshmallows, the recipe says one or two pkgs. Chill and before
serving add cut up red and green cherries for color..
