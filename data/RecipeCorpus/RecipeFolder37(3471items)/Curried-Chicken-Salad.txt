Title: Curried Chicken Salad
Categories: Salads, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Curry powder
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Salt and pepper
1/2 lb Wagon wheel pasta
2 c  Cooked chicken
1 c  Red seedless grapes
1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling, salted water until
tender but still firm, 8-9 min. Drain and rinse well in cold water.

2. While the pasta is cooking, cut up the chicken into 1-inch chunks.
Cut the grapes in half lengthwise. Combine the curry powder and
mayonnaise and stir well.

3. In a medium bowl, combine the pasta, chicken, grapes, and curry
dressing and toss. Season to taste with salt and pepper and toss well.
