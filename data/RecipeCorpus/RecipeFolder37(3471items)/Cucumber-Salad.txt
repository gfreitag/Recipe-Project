Title: Cucumber Salad
Categories: Salads, Thai
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Brown rice syrup
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 c  Fresh mint, chopped
1 lg Cucumber, peeled & diced
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  White vinegar
Mix cucumber with salt & set aside for 30 minutes.  Drain well &
place in a serving bowl.  In a small saucepan, make the dressing by
heating the vinegar, syrup & pepper flakes.  Remove from heat & let
cool. Pour dressing over the cucumber & garnish with the mint. Serve
chilled or at room temperature.
