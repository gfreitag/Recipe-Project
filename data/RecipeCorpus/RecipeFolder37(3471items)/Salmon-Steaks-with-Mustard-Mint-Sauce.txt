Title: Salmon Steaks with Mustard-Mint Sauce
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
-or 2 teaspoons dried
4    Salmon steaks, 6 to 8 ounces
-each
Salt and pepper
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
4 ts Coarse-grained Dijon-style
-mustard
1/4 c  Shredded fresh mint leaves,

1. Whisk the mayonnaise, lemon juice, mustard, mint and 2
Tablespoons water together in a bowl and let stand at room
temperature for 15 minutes. Preheat the broiler.

2. Season the salmon steaks with salt and pepper to taste, then
broil for about 5 to 6 minutes on each side.

3. Place a broiled salmon steak on each of 4 warm plates and top
with mustard-mint sauce. Serve hot.

Source: Fabulous Fish
Recipe from Fabulous Fish, 150 Healthful Recipes For Cooking
Seafood, sent to me by Apple for the August Cookbook swap.
