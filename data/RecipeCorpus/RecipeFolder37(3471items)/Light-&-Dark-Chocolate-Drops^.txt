Title: Light & Dark Chocolate Drops^
Categories: Candies, Holiday, Chocolate
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Chopped pecans
1/2 c  Flaked coconut
6    (2 oz) squares chocolate or
Vanilla flavored candy
Coating, melted
1 pk (16 oz) powdered sugar
2/3 c  Sweetened condensed milk
1/4 c  Butter or margarine, melted
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Lemon juice
Stir first 5 ingredients until smooth. Stir in pecans and coconut.

Roll mixture into 1" balls; cover them and refrigerate at least 1 hr.

Put balls in melted candy coating, coat completely and evenly. Allow
excess coating to drain off. Place on wax paper to set. Store in
refrigerator in a airtight containter.
