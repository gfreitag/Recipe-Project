Title: PITA PIZZA BITES
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs, Vegetables
Yield: 32 servings
2 tb Chopped fresh basil leaves
- OR -
2 ts Dried basil leaves
1 c  Finely shredded Mozzarella
- cheese (4 ounces)
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
2    Pita breads (6 inch)
2 c  Sliced fresh mushrooms
- OR -
4 oz Mushroom stems and pieces,
- drained (1 can)
1 sm Red onion, thinly sliced
1/4 c  Chopped green bell pepper
Heat oven to 425 degrees.  Split each pita bread in half around edge
to make 4 rounds.  Place rounds, cut-sides up on ungreased cookie
sheet. Place mushrooms on bread rounds.  Top with onion and bell
pepper. Sprinkle with basil and cheeses.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes or
until cheese is melted.  Cut each round into 8 pieces.  Makes 32
Baking time: 8 to
appetizers. Preparation time: 10 minutes
10 minutes
