Title: Lady Fingers รก
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Almond extract
pn Salt
2 c  Sifted flour
1 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
Beat sugar and eggs until thoroughly blended and cream in color.
Mix flour, salt and baking powder; sift slowly into egg mixture.
keep beating until smooth creamy consistency is obtained.  Butter
cookie pans generously and sprinkle with light flour.  Drop batter
by spoonfuls, shaping about 3 inch long by 1 inch wide.  Place 2
inches apart and bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
