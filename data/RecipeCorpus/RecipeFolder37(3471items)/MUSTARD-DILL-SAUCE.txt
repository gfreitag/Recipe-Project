Title: MUSTARD DILL SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Dried dill weed
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Honey
3/4 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
1/4 c  Prepared mustard, spicy or
-dijon
Mix ingredients thoroughly in a small bowl.  Serve on vegetables or
fish.

Store any remaining sauce in a covered container in the refrigerator.

Yield: 1 cup

I mixed my mustards in this recipe.  I used about 1/2 and 1/2 plain
sandwich mustard and dijon mustard and it still had quite a bite to
it. It's a very flavorful sauce that is very good with fish!!

Nutritional Information per serving:  12 Calories, .08 grams Fat, 6%
Calories from fat, .2 mg Cholesterol, 57 mg Sodium, 0 g Dietary fiber.

From "Cooking Without Fat" by George Mateljan.
