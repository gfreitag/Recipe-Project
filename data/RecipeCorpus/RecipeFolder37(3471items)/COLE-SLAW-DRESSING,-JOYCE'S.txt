Title: COLE SLAW DRESSING, JOYCE'S
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Celery seed or celery salt
1 tb Dijon mustard
Salt & pepper
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1/3 c  Sugar
In saucepan put vinegar, oil, sugar, celery seed or celery salt and
bring just to a boil on medium heat. Remove from heat and wisk in the
dijon mustard and salt and pepper. Refrigerate. Very Good. Can also
be made in the microwave.
