Title: BITTERSWEET FUDGE SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Unsweetened cocoa powder
2/3 c  Heavy cream
1/8 ts Salt
6 tb Butter; unsalted
2/3 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Brown sugar; dark
Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan. cook over a medium heat,
stirring constantly, until butter melts. Reduce heat to low. Cook,
stirring constantly until sugar melts and sauce is smooth. Serve
warm. Store in a tightly covered container in the refrigerator for up
to 1 month. Reheat over hot water.
