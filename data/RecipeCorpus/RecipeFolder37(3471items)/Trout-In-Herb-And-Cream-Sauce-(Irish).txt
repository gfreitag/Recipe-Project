Title: Trout In Herb And Cream Sauce (Irish)
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Scot/irish
Yield: 4 servings
85 g  Butter
Mixture of herbs*
4 x  Rainbow trout, gutted
300 ml Cream
* Finely chopped parsley, chives, basil, watercress, etc etc.

Poach the fish in court-bouillon for 10-12 minutes.  Lift out
carefully: remove skin and eyes.  Keep warm.  Boil the cream until it
reduces by half.  Whisk in knobs of butter gradually.  Finally, add
in finely chopped herbs.  Pour the sauce into a serving dish and
arrange the fish on top. Serve at once, garnished with lemon slices.
