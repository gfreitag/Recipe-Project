Title: Grilled Tomato Sauce^
Categories: Tomatoes, Salsa/sauce, Low cal/fat, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
Salt and pepper to taste
20    Roma tomatoes, halved
Broil tomatoes, skin side up on a lightly oiled baking sheet until
slightly charred and soft. Puree in food processor. Season. Makes
about 8 cups.

Per cup:
33 cal., 1g pro., 81mg sod., 7g carb., 1g fat, 0mg chol., 8mg calcium.
