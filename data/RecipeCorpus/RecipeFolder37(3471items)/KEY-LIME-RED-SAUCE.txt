Title: KEY LIME RED SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Tabasco
1 tb Key lime juice
1/2 c  Catsup
Mix all ingredients, refrigerate.  Add more hot sauce for hot
pepper fans.  Makes about 2/3 cups.

Source: _Key Lime Cookbook_
