Title: PORK CHORIZO
Categories: Breakfast, Tex/mexican
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Cumin, ground
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Oregano leaves
1/2 c  Vinegar
2 lb Pork, ground
1    Garlic cloves, crushed
1/2 c  Chile, red, ground
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Cloves, ground
1/4 ts Cinnamon, ground
1/4 ts Oregano, ground
Note: This recipe requires advance preparation.
Combine all the ingredients except the pork in a blender and puree.
Knead this mixture into the pork until it is throughly mixed
together. Cover and refrigerate for 24 hrs. At this point, chorizo
may be frozen. To cook, crumble the chorizo in a skillet and fry.  If
desired, add eggs and/or potatoes.  Drain before serving.
