Title: Spiced Cherries & Pecans^
Categories: Holiday, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1/4    Dried oregano, crushed
3 c  Pecan halves
1 1/2 c  Dried tart cherries
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Margarine or butter melted
1/2 ts Ground red pepper
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Ground cumin
Stir together the first 7 ingredients. Add pecans, tossing to coat.

Put mixture in a 13X9X2" baking pan. Bake at 350F. for 15 mins.; stir
every 5 mins. Stir in cherries. Bake for another 5 mins. or till
pecans are toasted. Spread on paper towels to cool. Store in airtight
container.
