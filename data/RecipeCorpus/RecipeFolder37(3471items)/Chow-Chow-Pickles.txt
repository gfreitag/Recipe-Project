Title: Chow Chow Pickles
Categories: Tomatoes, Can/preserv, Pickles
Yield: 1 servings
6 c  White Vinegar
6 c  White Sugar
1/2 c  Pickle spice in a bag
6 qt Green Tomatoes
2 lb Onions
1/2 c  Coarse Salt
Slice tomatoes and onions, add salt, let stand overnite in frig. Drain
and add white vinegar, white sugar and pickle spice in a bag.
Cook 25 minutes and pack in sterilized jars.
