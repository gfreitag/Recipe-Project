Title: SWEET CORN NOODLE SOUP
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1/2 ts Tumeric
Salt & pepper to taste
1 qt Vegetable stock
1/2 c  Ribbon noodles, broken
2 c  Sweet corn kernals
Bring stock to a boil. Add noodles and sweet corn and boil 5 mins. Add
celery and tumeric. Stir well, reduce heat, and simmer another 10
mins.
