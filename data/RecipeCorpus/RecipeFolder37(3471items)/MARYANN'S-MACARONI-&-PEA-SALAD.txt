Title: MARYANN'S MACARONI & PEA SALAD
Categories: Pasta/rice, Salads
Yield: 999 servings
2    Bell pepper
1 cn Pimento
1 pk Petite Peas (green) frozen
Salt & pepper to taste
1 pk Shell Macaroni
Hellman's mayo
2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Onion powder
1    Dill pickles
Cook & drain macaroni.  Chop pepper, pickle, and pimento. Stir in with
mayo and seasonings. Stir in peas. Chill several hours.
