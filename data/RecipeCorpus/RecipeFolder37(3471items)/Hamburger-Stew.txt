Title: Hamburger Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Meats, Potatoes, Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Tomatoes, 19 oz.
3 md Potatoes, sliced
3 md Carrots, sliced
2    Stalks of celery, diced
Split "hot" biscuits
1 lb Ground beef
1 tb Butter
1 lg Onion, sliced
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 tb A-1 Steak sauce
Brown beef lightly in butter, stirring to break up meat.  Add onion,
cook for a few minutes.  Add remaining ingredients except biscuits.
Bring to a boil, simmer covered for 30 minutes or until vegetables
are tender.  Serve over biscuits.
