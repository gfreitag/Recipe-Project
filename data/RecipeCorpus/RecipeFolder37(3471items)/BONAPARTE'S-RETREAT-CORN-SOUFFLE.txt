Title: BONAPARTE'S RETREAT CORN SOUFFLE
Categories: Appetizers, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
2 tb Sugar
2 c  Whole-kernel corn
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs, beaten
1 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 350.  Combine all the ingredients in an ovenproof
dish, mixing well. Bake at 350 degs for 30 mins. Stir occasionally.
