Title: Old-Fashioned Sweet Potato Pie
Categories: Potatoes, Pie/tart, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/8 ts Ground cloves
2    Eggs, beaten
2    (5 1/3-oz) canned evaporated
-milk
2/3 c  Milk
9    Inch unbaked pastry shells
2 1/4 c  Cooked sweet potatoes,
-mashed
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1/2 ts Ground ginger
Combine sweet potatoes and butter, mixing until blended.  Add combined
sugar, flour and spices:  mix well.  Blend in eggs and milk.  Pour
into crusts. Bake 10 minutes (I think at 400 degrees F, I lost my
original recipe), reduce oven to 350 degrees F., continue baking 50
to 60 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.

Recipe from:  Essence Magazine
LASTED POSTED DATE: 4/92
