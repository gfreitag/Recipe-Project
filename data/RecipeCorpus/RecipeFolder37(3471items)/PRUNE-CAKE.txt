Title: PRUNE CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Salt
3 ts Baking powder
2 c  Flour
3    Eggs
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Salad oil
1    Jar Jr. baby food prunes
1 ts Nutmeg
Beat all ingredients together for 3 minutes.  Bake at 325 degrees for
1 1/2 hours.  Can use 1 jar of plums in place of prunes. Randy Rigg
