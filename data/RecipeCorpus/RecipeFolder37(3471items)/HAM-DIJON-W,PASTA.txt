Title: HAM DIJON W/PASTA
Categories: Main dish, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Mushrooms, scliced,4 oz size
1/4 ts White pepper
6 c  Hot cooked pasta noodles,
-ribbons, or vermicelli
1 1/2 c  Whipping cream
1/3 c  Dijon style mustard
1 lb Diced Smoked Ham
3 c  Chopped broccoli, cooked
In an large skillet, combine cream and mustard. Bring to a boil.
Reduce to medium heat and cook for 8 to 10 minutes, until slightly
thickened, stirring occasionally.  Add remaining ingredients except
pasta.  Heat throughly. Serve over hot pasta.,  Serves 4-6.
