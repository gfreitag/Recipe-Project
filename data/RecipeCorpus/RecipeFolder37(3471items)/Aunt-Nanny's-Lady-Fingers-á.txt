Title: Aunt Nanny's Lady Fingers รก
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Chopped black walnuts
Confectioners' sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Shortening
1/3 c  Sugar
2 ts Water
Cream butter and shortening.  Add together rest of ingredients.
Mix well.  Chill 3 to 4 hours.  Form into small finger shaped
cookies.  Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 325 degrees for about 15
minutes.  Remove from sheet and cool slightly.  Dip cookies in
confectioners' sugar.
