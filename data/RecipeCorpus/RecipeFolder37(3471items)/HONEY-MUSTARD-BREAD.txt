Title: HONEY MUSTARD BREAD
Categories: Breads, Pickles
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Chicken broth; low salt/can
1 2/3 tb Gourmet mustard (dijon etc)
1 ts Chives; (dried)
2 ts Yeast
1/2 c  Water
1 1/2 c  White bread flour
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
2 ts Dry milk
2 1/2 tb Honey
The chives give this bread a sweet taste and it is great for
sandwiches and even just toasted.
