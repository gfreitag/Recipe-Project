Title: Campbell's Pasta Primavera
Categories: Pasta/rice, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
Crushed
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Pepper
16 oz Bag frozen vegetable
-conbination *
4 c  Hot cooked spaghetti
Cooking time:  20 min.
10 3/4 oz Can reduced fat cream of
-chicken soup
1/2 c  Water
3 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Dried basil leaves --
Prep time:  10 min.

* Broccoli, cauliflower, carrots.

In a skillet mix soup, water, cheese, lemon juice, basil, garlic
powder, pepper and vegetables. Over medium heat, heat to a boil.
Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or until vegetables are
tender, stirring occasionally. Toss with spaghetti.
Serves 4.
