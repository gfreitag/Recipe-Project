Title: Spring Rolls
Categories: Chinese, Appetizers, Hawaii, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Sherry
1 ts Ajinomoto or Accent
1 ts Salt
2 ts Cornstarch
4 tb Water
20    Spring roll or Lumpia skins
1/2 lb Lean Pork; shredded
1/2 lb Bean sprouts
8    Large dried mushrooms
3 1/4 c  Oil
2    Celery stalk; sliced thin
3 tb Soy sauce
Mix soy sauce, sherry, Ajinomoto, and salt. Marinate pork in this
mixture. Drop sprouts in boiling water for 1 second then remove.
Clean and soak mushrooms. Squeeze dry and slice. Heat pan with 2
Tbsp. oil. Saute celery and sprouts 1 minute. Remove, reheat pan with
2 tbsp oil, saute pork and mushrooms 1 minute. Add vegetables and
cook 2 minutes. Cool and spread 1 1/2 Tbsp. on each skin. Fold edges.
Moisten with mixture of cornstarch and water and roll. Deep fry to
golden brown.
