Title: Western Mac
Categories: Pasta/rice, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
- medium)
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1 cn Corn -- (or can use frozen)
1 sm Can tomato paste
1    Box macaroni and cheese mix
- prepared as directed
1 lb Hamburger
1/2 c  Onion, chopped (or 1
Prepare macaroni mix according to directions.  Brown hamburger,
onions, and green peppers.  Combine macaroni, hamburger mixture, corn
and tomato paste.  Simmer for 1/2 hour or so.  Salt and pepper to
taste.

Note:  When you add the tomato paste, you will swear that you need
more, but just keep stirring, and pretty soon it looks OK.
