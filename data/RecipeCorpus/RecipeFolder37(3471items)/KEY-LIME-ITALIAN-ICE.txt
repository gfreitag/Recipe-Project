Title: KEY LIME ITALIAN ICE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
1 ds Salt
1/2 c  Key Lime juice
1    Key Lime
1 c  Sugar
4 c  Water
Remove zest, the colored part of the rind, from 1 Key Lime, and cut
into small
pieces.  Place zest and sugar in a blender or food processor, and
process until zest is finely chopped.  Pour mixture into a 3 qt
saucepan. Add water
and salt and heat until sugar dissolves.  Cool.

Add enough water to Key Lime juice to make 2/3 cup liquid. Stir into
cooled
sugar mixture.  Pour into divided ice cube trays and freeze. Place
frozen cubes in container of blender.  Cover and process until cubes
become a velvety
slush.

Scoop into individual goblets or store, covered in the freezer.

Makes about 4 1/2 cups.

This recipe is from The Key Lime Cookbook that my Mumsie sent me.
