Title: SAUSAGE CROQUETTES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Milk
Cracker or bread crumbs
2 lb Sausage meat
4    Eggs
1 c  Butter
Add powdered cracker or stale bread crumbs sufficient to thicken,
while on the fire.  Roll in oblong shapes and fry in lard. Roll the
balls in cracker dust before frying.

Mrs. R. K. M. From Housekeeping in Old Virginia, 1879
