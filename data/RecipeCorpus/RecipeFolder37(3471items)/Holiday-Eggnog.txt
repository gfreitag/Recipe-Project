Title: Holiday Eggnog
Categories: Holiday, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
- milk
1/2 c  Dark rum
1/4 c  Brandy
1/4 c  Praline liqueur (optional)
Sweetened whipped cream
Freshly grated nutmeg
6    Egg yolks
1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Vanilla extract
1/4 ts Freshly grated nutmeg
4 c  Milk
1 cn 12 oz evaporated milk
1 cn 14 oz sweetened condensed
Beat egg yolks at medium speed of an electric mixer until thick and
lemon colored; gradually add sugar, vanilla, and 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg,
beating well.

Place 4 cups milk in a large saucepan over medium-low heat.
Gradually add egg yolk mixture, and cook, stirring constantly with a
wire whisk, until mixture reaches 165 degrees.  Remove from heat, and
let cool,  Stir in evaporated milk and sweetened condensed milk.
Gradually add rum.  Cover and chill mixture thoroughly.

Add brandy and praline liquer to eggnog just before serving.  Top each
serving with whipped cream, and sprinkle with freshly grated nutmeg.
Yield: 9 cups.
