Title: BOMMA'S DATE NUT COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 60 servings
1 lb Chopped dates
2 c  Chopped pecans
1/4 ts Vanilla
3    Egg whites, stiffly beaten
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
Combine dry ingredients and mix with dates, pecans, and vanilla in a
large bowl. Mix in egg whites.  Drop by teasponnful onto a greased
cookie sheet.
Bake at 325F for 15 minutes.
