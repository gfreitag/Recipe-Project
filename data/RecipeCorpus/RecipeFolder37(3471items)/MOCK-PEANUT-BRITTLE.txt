Title: MOCK PEANUT BRITTLE
Categories: Snacks, Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  White karo
1 c  Sugar
8 c  Corn flakes
1 c  Peanut butter
Spread corn flakes over large toast pan.  Mix karo and sugar in
saucepan and bring to a boil.  Remove from stove.  Add peanut butter
and stir till it melts.  Pour over cornflakes until well covered and
spread thin on wax paper.  Let cool.

Minnie Lee, LPN    (another recipe from my dentist's mother!) OB - KDH

NOTE: These were a favorite after school snack when I was growing up
(back in the good old days when snacks were allowed to be "bad for
