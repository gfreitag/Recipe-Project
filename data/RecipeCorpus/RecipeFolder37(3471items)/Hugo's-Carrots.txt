Title: Hugo's Carrots
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
4 tb Wine -- white
1    Orange -- juice of
Salt -- to taste
10    Carrots -- baby
1 tb Butter
2 ts Sugar
Cook carrots in boiling water about 40 minutes or until tender; drain.
melt butter in skillet; add sugar and let brown slightly.  Add white
wine, orange juice, and salt to taste.  Cook carrots in this mixture
until they are glazed.
