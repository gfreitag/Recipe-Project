Title: Creamy Low-fat Turkey Ham Zita With Spinach
Categories: Low cal/fat, Pasta/rice, Poultry, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Dijon mustard
1/4 c  Fresh parsley; chopped
2 tb Fresh dill; minced
1 tb Lemon juice
3/4 ts Hot pepper sauce
1/2 ts Salt [optional]
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Zita or Penne pasta
1/2 ts Oil
12 oz Lean turkey "ham"; cubed
1    Red or green pepper; diced
10 oz Pkg frozen spinach ; thawed
-and squeezed dry
1 c  Non-fat sour cream
3/4 c  Skim milk
Prepare pasta. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add
turkey ham and pepper; cook until pepper softens and ham browns,
about 5 min.

In a blender puree the spinach and the remaining ingredients and add
to the ham and peppers. Continue cooking until the sauce starts to
simmer and toss the pasta with the sauce.

319 cal.,19 g protein, 4 g fat, 24 mg chol., 50 g carbs, 833 mg sodium
using the optional salt. *Only* 12% of the calories come from the fat!

From Women's World magazine, Sept.19 '95.
