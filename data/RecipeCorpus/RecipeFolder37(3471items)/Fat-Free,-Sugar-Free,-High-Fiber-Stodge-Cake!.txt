Title: Fat Free, Sugar Free, High Fiber Stodge Cake!
Categories: Cake/cookie, Low cal/fat, Diabetic
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Figs
2 ts Mixed spice (cinnamon, clove
-ginger, nutmeg, etc.)
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Water
1 sm Apple
1    Banana
1    Carrot
3/4 c  Prunes
1/2 c  Dates
You can vary the quantities to taste. Try to experiment.

Grate apple and carrot into bowl, add mashed banana.
Remove stones from fruit and mash in with fork. Add spices, flour
and water. Mix well. Spoon mixture into two 7" x 3" cake tins
(pre-lined with silicone paper). Bake at 350 deg F until firm - about
3/4 hour. The clean knife test is not suitable for this cake!!
