Title: Shrimp and Shells in Black Bean Cream Sauce
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Pasta/rice
Yield: 2 servings
3/4 c  Heavy cream
1/2 lb Large shrimp
Shelled and deveined
1/2 lb Medium pasta shells
4    Green onion(s)
1 tb Bottled
Black bean-garlic-sauce
1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling, salted water until
tender but still firm, 7-9 min. Drain well.

2. Meanwhile, chop the green onions. In a large frying pan, combine
the black bean-garlic sauce and cream and bring to a gentle boil over
high heat. Cook 1 min. Reduce the heat to medium and stir in the
shrimp. Cook, stirring often, until the sauce thickens somewhat and
the shrimp are pink and curled, 2-3 min.

3. Add the pasta and toss. Add the green onions and toss well.
