Title: Sweet Breakfast Bread^
Categories: Breakfast, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Salt
1 c  Sugar
2 lg Eggs
3 tb Softened unsalted butter
1 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 350F.

Beat sugar eggs and butter. Beat in milk. Mix in, just until blended,
combined remaining ingredients.

Bake in a greased and floured loaf pan 1 hour, until loaf tests done
with a toothpick. Cool 10 minutes on rack before removing from pan.
Run knife around edges of pan to remove. Return to rack to cool
completely.
