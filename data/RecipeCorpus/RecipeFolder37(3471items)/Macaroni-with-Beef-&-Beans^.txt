Title: Macaroni with Beef & Beans^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Meats, Pasta/rice, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
Undrained
2 ts To 3 chili powder
1 ts Salt
3/4 c  Uncooked elbow macaroni
1 lb Ground beef
1/3 c  Chopped onion
1 cn (16 oz) tomatoes with liquid
Cut up
1 cn (15 oz) kidney beans,
Brown beef and onion until onion is tender. Stir in rest of
ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer until
macaroni is tender; about 20 minutes, adding water ifneeded.
