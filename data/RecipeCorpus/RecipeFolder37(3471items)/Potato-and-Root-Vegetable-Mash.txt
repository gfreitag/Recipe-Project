Title: Potato and Root Vegetable Mash
Categories: Potatoes, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
Thinly sliced
6 sm Parsnips (14oz)
Peeled, cut in 1" pieces
3 tb Olive oil
3 lg Baking potatoes (2 1/2 lb)
Peeled, cut in 2" pieces
3    Rutabagas (1 3/4 lb)
Peeled, halved,
Bring large pot of salted water to boil. Add potatoes, rutabagas, and
parsnips. Boil until vegetables are tender, about 30 min. Drain well.

Return vegetables to same pot. Mash until coarse puree forms. Mix in
3 tbs olive oil. Season vegetables to taste with salt and pepper.

Can be prepared 2 hours ahead. Let stand at room temperature. Rewarm
over low heat, stirring frequently.

Transfer vegetables to bowl and serve.
