Title: PIGEON PEAS AND RICE
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Chopped fresh parsley
2 c  Water
1 tb Fresh lime juice
Salt; to taste
Freshly ground black pepepr
-- to taste
1 tb Oil
1 sm Onion; chopped
2    Garlic cloves; crushed
6 tb Tomato sauce
16 oz Canned pigeon peas
1 c  Uncooked rice
Heat the oil in a saucepan and add the onion; simmer for 5 minutes.
Add the garlic and tomato sauce and cook 1 minute.  Add the peas,
rice, and parsley and saute 1 minute.  Add the water and lime juice
and simmer, covered, for 15 minutes until rice is cooked.  Add salt
and pepper to taste.
