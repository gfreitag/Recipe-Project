Title: Caramel Nut Logs^
Categories: Candies, Holiday
Yield: 48 servings
1 c  Dry roasted peanuts
2 c  Mini marshmallows
1 cn (3 1/2oz) flaked coconut
1/2 lb Caramels
1/4 c  Butter
1 tb Milk
1 3/4 c  Sifted powdered sugar
Microwave caramels, butter and milk at 50% power 4-6 minutes, or until
melted,

stirring after 2 minutes, then every minute.

Stir in sugar, peanuts and marshmallows, just until blended.

Shape into 2-12" logs on waxed paper coated with coconut, taking care
to coat each log well with coconut. cover and refrigerate until firm.
Cut into 1/2" slices.
