Title: Easy Cassoulet
Categories: Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Bay leaf
2 cn Navy beans (15-oz. each)
1/4 c  Dry white wine
ds Ground cloves
1/2 lb Bulk pork sausage
1 sm Onion, sliced (1/2 cup)
1 cl Garlic, minced
1 1/2 c  Cubed, fully cooked ham
2 tb Snipped parseley
In skillet, cook sausage, onion and garlic till meat is slightly
browned and onion is tender; drain off fat.
Add ham, parsley, and bay leaf; mix well. Stir in undrained beans,
wine and cloves. Turn into a 1 1/2-quart casserole.
Bake, covered, at 325 for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake 40 to 45
minutes longer; stirring occasionally. Remove Bay leaf.
Serve in bowls with hot corn bread, if desired.
