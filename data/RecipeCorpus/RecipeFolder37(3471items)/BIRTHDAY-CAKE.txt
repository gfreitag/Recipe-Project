Title: BIRTHDAY CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 10 servings
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Cake flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
3/4 ts Salt
1 3/4 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Shortening
2    Eggs
1 1/4 c  Milk
Cream sugar & shortening & add the eggs, beat 2 min. Add flour baking
powder & salt which have been blended to - gether alternately with
the milk and vanilla. Bake in greased layer cake pans in 350 oven 30
min.
