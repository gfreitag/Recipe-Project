Title: MASHED POTATO CASSEROLE/GRANDPA'S
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
3 lb Potatoes; bakers
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 x  Paprika
1 x  Parsley
1 pk Cream cheese (3 oz)
1 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Sour cream
In a bowl, stir together cream cheese, garlic salt, pepper, and sour
cream; set aside.  Prepare and boil quartered potatoes; drain. Using
an electric mixer or a potato masher, beat hot potatoes until they're
in fine lumps. Add sour cream mixture and continue beating until
smooth. Spoon potatoes into a well buttered 2-quart casserole; dot
with butter and sprinkle with paprika.  If made ahead, cover and
refrigerate until the next day. Bake, covered, in a 400 deg F oven
for 25 minutes (50 minutes if refrigerated); uncover and bake for 10
minutes more or until top is golden brown. Garnish with parsley
sprigs, if desired. Makes 6 servings.
