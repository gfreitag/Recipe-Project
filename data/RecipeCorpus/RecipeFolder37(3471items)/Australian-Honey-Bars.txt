Title: Australian Honey Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 36 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Dried raisins
3/4 c  Minced walnuts
3/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Honey
3    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour
Beat butter and honey into cream.  Add the eggs, one at a time,
beating well after each.  Add flour, baking soda, raisins and walnuts.
Pour in a 9x9-inch greased mold. Bake at 325 F (or 180 C) for 30
minutes until dough is firm.
Before serving, sprinkle with icing sugar.
Makes 36 "squares".
(Jean Pare, 150 squares)

Eleonore Beaudoin   3 Nov 1994
