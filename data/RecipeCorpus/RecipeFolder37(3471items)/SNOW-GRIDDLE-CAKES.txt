Title: SNOW GRIDDLE CAKES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------LISA CRAWLEY  TSPN00B------------------------
Snow is leven!  To 6 tb. flour, add a little salt and 6 tb. of light,
fresh-fallen snow. Stir flour and snow well together and add sweet
milk. Bake batter in sm. cakes on griddle, using very little butter.
Cakes may be eaten w/ butter and sugar, or w/ syrup, and are very
delicate. Colonial Cookbook, updated as researched from Old Farmer's
Almanac, 1792.
