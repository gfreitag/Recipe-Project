Title: Hot Cocoa Mix
Categories: Mixes, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 lb Hershey's Cocoa Mix
1 ds Salt
5 lb Powdered milk (Carnation)
11 oz Coffee-mate
1 1/2 c  Powdered sugar
Mix and store in airtight containers.

Makes about 10 quarts.

(OPTIONAL)
Adding more Coffee Mate, Cocoa Mix, and Powdered Sugar than the recipe
calls for makes the cocoa creamier and more chocolaty.
