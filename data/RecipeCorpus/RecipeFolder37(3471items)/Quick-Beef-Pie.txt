Title: Quick Beef Pie
Categories: Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Cut green beans, drained
16 oz.
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Potatoes, seasoned & mashed
1/2 c  Chedder cheese, shredded
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion, chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Condensed tomato soup
10 1/2 oz. size
Crumble beef into large fry pan.  Add onion and salt, and cook until
browned.  Drain off excess fat.  Add soup, green beans and pepper.
Simmer for 5 minutes.  Pour into a greased 2 quart casserole.  Spread
mashed potatoes over the top.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake at 350
degrees for 20 minutes.
