Title: CREAM CHEESE CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Crockpot
Yield: 4 servings
6 oz Cream cheese; cut into cubes
1/2 c  Sauterne wine or sherry
1 tb Chopped onion
Mix
1 ea Frying chicken; cut up
2 tb Melted butter or margarine
1 x  Salt and pepper
2 tb Dry italian salad dressing
1 cn Condensed mushroom soup
Brush chicken with butter and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Place in
a crockpot and sprinkle dry mix over all. Cover and cook on low for 6
to 7 hours. About 45 minutes before done, mix soup, cream cheese,
wine, and onion in a small saucepan. Cook unti smooth. Pour over the
chicken and cover and cook another 45 minutes. Serve with sauce.
