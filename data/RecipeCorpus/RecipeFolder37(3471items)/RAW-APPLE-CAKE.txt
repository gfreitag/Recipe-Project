Title: RAW APPLE CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Self rising flour
Topping
1 c  Sugar
1    Stick of margarine
1 cn Condensed milk
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Oil
2 c  Sugar
4    Raw apples, chopped
3    Eggs
1 c  Pecans
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Vanilla
Mix all ingredients together until well blended.  Pour into a greased
9x13 inch pan and bake at 300 degrees fro 1 hour. Topping: Mix in a
saucepan and cook until thick.  Remove cake from oven and cut into
squares while still hot.  Pour topping over hot cake and let cool.
Randy Rigg
