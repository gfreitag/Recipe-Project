Title: FRUIT AND CHOCOLATE DREAM SQUARES
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 16 servings
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/3 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
6 tb Butter, softened
2/3 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1/2 c  Raspberry or strawberry jam
1 pk NESTLE Toll House milk
-chocolate morsels (11.5 oz)
1 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Butter
TOPPING: In small bowl, combine 2/3 cup flour, pecans and brown
sugar. With pastry blender or 2 knives, cut in 6 tablespoons butter
until mixture resembles coarse crumbs; set aside.

CRUST: Preheat oven to 375'F. Grease 9" square baking pan. In small
bowl, combine 1 1/4 cup flour and granulated sugar. With pastry
blender or 2 knives, cut in 1/2 cup butter until mixture resembles
fine crumbs. Press into prepared pan.

Bake 20-25 minutes until set but not brown. Spread with jam. Top with
Nestle Toll House milk chocolate morsels and Topping.

Bake 15-20 minutes longer until top is lightly browned. Cool
completely; cut into 2 1/4" squares.

Makes 16 squares.
