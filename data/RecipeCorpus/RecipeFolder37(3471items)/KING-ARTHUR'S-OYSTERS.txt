Title: KING ARTHUR'S OYSTERS
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Flour
3 tb Water
24    Oysters
2 tb Butter, melted
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Dry sherry
1 c  A-1 Steak sauce
Salt & Pepper

In medium saucepan, combine butter, lemon juice, sherry, and steak
sauce. Cook over low heat.  Blend flour and water together. Slowly
stir into sauce being careful not to let boil. Add oysters to sauce
and heat for 1 minute. Adjust seasonings to taste with salt and
pepper. Transfer to chafing dish to keep warm.  If oysters are to be
eaten immediately they can be placed in a shallow baking or serving
dish.
