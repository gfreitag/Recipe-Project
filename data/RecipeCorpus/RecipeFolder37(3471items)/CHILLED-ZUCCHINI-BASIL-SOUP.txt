Title: CHILLED ZUCCHINI-BASIL SOUP
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 cups
1/2 c  HELLMANN'S or BEST FOODS
-Real, Light or Cholesterol
-Free Reduced Calorie
-Mayonnaise
2 tb Lemon juice
1/8 ts Hot pepper sauce
2 c  Chicken broth
3 md Zucchini, sliced
2 md Onions, chopped
1 tb Minced fresh basil
-OR
1    Dried basil
1    Garlic clove, sliced
In 3-quart saucepan combine chicken broth, zucchini, onions, basil and
garlic. Bring to boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low; cover and
simmer 10 minutes or until zucchini is tender. Cool. In blender or
food processor container place zucchini mixture, half at a time.
Process until smooth. Pour into large bowl. Stir in mayonnaise, lemon
juice and hot pepper sauce until well blended. Cover; chill several
hours or overnight.

Makes about 4 cups.
