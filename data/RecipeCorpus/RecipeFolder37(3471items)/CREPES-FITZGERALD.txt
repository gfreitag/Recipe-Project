Title: CREPES FITZGERALD
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
Sugar
Butter
Strawberry liqueur
Kirsch
2    Crepes
2 ts Cream cheese (heaping ts)
2 tb Sour cream
1/2 c  Stawberries
Make crepes by your favorite recipe.  Roll cream cheese and sour
cream in crepes and put on a plate. Cook stawberries in sugar and a
small amount of butter.  Flame in strawberry liqueur and kirsch and
pour over crepes.

Southern Living ... August, 1971
