Title: BROWNIES A LA MODE
Categories: Candies, Desserts, Microwave
Yield: 8 servings
2/3 c  Butter or margarine, softend
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 tb Corn syrup
Vanilla ice cream
3/4 c  Unsifted flour
1 c  Sugar
7 tb Unsweetened cocoa
1/2 ts Baking powder
3/4 ts Salt
1. Grease an 8-inch square, heat-resistant, non-metallic, baking dish
and set aside.
2. Sift together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder and salt into the
large bowl of an electric mixer. Add remaining ingredients and beat
until smooth. 3. Pour batter into the prepared baking dish. Heat,
uncovered, in Microwave Oven 6 minutes.
4. Test for doneness with a toothpick; if not done return to
Microwave Oven for an additional 1 minute.
5. Allow to cool slightly and cut into squares for serving.
6. Place a scoop of vanilla ice cream on each serving.
