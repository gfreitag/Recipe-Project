Title: Fruited Tea Cooler
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
- juice concentrated
1 qt Water
1    Lemon -- sliced
3 c  Boiling water
6    Celestial seasoning red
Zinger tea bags
1 cn 12 oz Frozen pineapple
Pour boiling water over tea bags cover and steep for 15 mins.  (if you
like it strong 30 mins)
Remove tea bags from water squeezing gently.
Stir in pineapple juice & remaining ingredients.  Chill.  Serve over
ice. Yield about 2 qrts.
