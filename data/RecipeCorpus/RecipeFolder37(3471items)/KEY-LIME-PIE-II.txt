Title: KEY LIME PIE II
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
28 oz Condensed milk (Sweetened)
8 oz Fresh squeezed lime juice
1 ea 9" Graham cracker crumb crus
6 ea Egg yolks
Pre heat oven to 350 degrees. In a large bowl beat the egg yolks and
condensed milk. Stir in lime juice. Pour into shell and bake for 12
minutes. Cool. Chill. Serve with whipped cream topping.
NOTE..you can make a meringue topping for the pie using leftover egg
whites. Beat 4 egg whites until stiff, gradually add 1/2 cup sugar.
Top off UNCOOKED pie with meringue mixture and bake for 15 minutes
instead. (recipe from Bordens Easy Entertaining Cookbook)
