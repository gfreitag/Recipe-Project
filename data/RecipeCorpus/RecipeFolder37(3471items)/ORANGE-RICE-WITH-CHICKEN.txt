Title: ORANGE RICE WITH CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Salad oil
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
2 c  Rice, cooked
1 pk Chicken tenderloins
2 tb Margarine
1 c  Mandarin oranges, drained
1/4 c  Almonds, sliced (optional)
Heat large skillet. Add oil and chicken, saute until tender. Remove
chicken, metl margarine in same skillet and saute celery. Stir in
orange slices, and other ingredients. Toss until heated.
