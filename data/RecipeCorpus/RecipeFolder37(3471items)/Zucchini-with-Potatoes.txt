Title: Zucchini with Potatoes
Categories: Diabetic, Vegetables, Potatoes, P-cooker
Yield: 6 servings
1 lg Tomato; diced
1/3 c  Water or chicken broth;
2 ts Oregano; chopped
Salt & pepper;
2    Zucchini;
3    Potatoes;
2 ts Oil;
1    Onion; chopped
Cut unpeeled potatoes and zucchini into 1/2" pieces.  Heat oil in
skillet. Add potatoes and stir fry for 1 minute.  Pour in water or
broth, cover and cook over low heat 4 to 5 minutes.  Add onion,
zucchini, tomato, oregano and season to taste.  Stir and cook until
vegetables are tender. Per serving:  140 calories and 2 grams fat.
