Title: Baked Artichoke Hearts+
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese/eggs, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
ds Tabasco
1 tb Lemon juice
Garlic salt, to taste
Paprika, to taste
1 cn Artichoke hearts
-drained & quartered
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
Combine all ingredients except paprika. Pour into buttered 1 qt.
casserole dish. sprinkle with paprika. Bake for 20 to 30 minutes at
350 degrees.
