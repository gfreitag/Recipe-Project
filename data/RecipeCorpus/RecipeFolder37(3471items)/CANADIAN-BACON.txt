Title: CANADIAN BACON
Categories: Meats, Appetizers, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
-cut 1/4 inch thick
8 sl Canadian bacon,
1. Arrange bacon slices in a single layer on a paper-towel-lined
double paper plate.
2. Heat, covered loosely with wax paper or paper toweling in
Microwave Oven 6 minutes or until the edge of the meat begins to
sizzle and bacon is well heated.
3. Pork products should always be heated to well-done.
