Title: WAGON WHEEL PASTA SALAD
Categories: Pasta/rice, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2    Garlic cloves; crushed
1    Lemon (juice only)
Salt, pepper
1 c  Oil
1/3 c  Red wine vinegar
1 tb Dijon mustard
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
1    Red bell pepper; sliced
1/2 c  Chopped fresh dill
- or more if desired
1 lb Wagon wheel pasta
2 tb Olive oil
1    Green bell pepper; sliced
To make dressing, combine oil, vinegar, mustard, garlic and lemon
juice and season to taste with salt and pepper. To make salad, cook
pasta in boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and cool. Place
in large bowl. Heat olive oil in skillet. Add green and red peppers
and saute until barely tender. Add to pasta along with dill. Add
dressing. Toss to coat pasta well. Chill, covered, until serving time.
