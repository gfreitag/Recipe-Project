Title: Broiled Salmon with Fines Herb Sauce
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1 ts Salt
1 pn White pepper
1/3 c  Oil, olive
2    Bell peppers, red
1 tb Basil leaves
1 ts Thyme leaves
1 ts Rosemary leaves
MMMMM---------------------------SALMON--------------------------------
1/4 c  Oil, olive
1 1/2 lb Salmon fillets
Roast red peppers and let cool.

Make fines herb sauce by blending red pepper, basil, thyme,
rosemary, salt, pepper, and olive oil in a food processor or
blender for about 30 seconds.  Do not puree.  Blend only long
enough to incorporate ingredients.  Heat sauce until just warm.

Brush salmon fillets with olive oil and broil until flesh is
firm and pink (about 10 minutes).

Serve on warm platter with warmed fines herb sauce.

Source: _Cooking with Mickey, Volume II_
