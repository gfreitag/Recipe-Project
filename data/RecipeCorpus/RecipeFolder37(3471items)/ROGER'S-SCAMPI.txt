Title: ROGER'S SCAMPI
Categories: Herbs/spice, Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings
1/8 ts Each sweet basil, oregano
Juice of 1 lemon
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 c  Triple Sec or Cointreau
1 lb Large prawns
4 tb Sweet butter
1/4 c  Olive oil
5    Cloves fresh garlic, minced
Shell and devein prawns; rinse and drain.  Melt butter in medium
skillet; add olive oil, garlic, basil and oregano and lemon juice.
Saute for 1 minute.  Add prawns and cook until pink.  Add salt and
pepper and Triple Sec and cook on high until liquid is reduced by
3/4.  Serve over pasta or rice.
