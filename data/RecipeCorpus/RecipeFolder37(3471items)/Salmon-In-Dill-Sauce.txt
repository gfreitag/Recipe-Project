Title: Salmon In Dill Sauce
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Chablis or other dry white
-wine
2 tb Chopped fresh dill weed
1 ts Dried dill weed, crushed
4    Salmon steaks, 3/4 inch
-thick (about 6 oz. each
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Chopped green onions
1 cn (10-3/4 oz.) Campbell's
-Condensed Cream of Celery
-Soup
1/2 c  Half-and-half
In 12- by 8-inch microwave-safe baking dish, combine butter and
onions. Cover with vented plastic wrap; microwave on HIGH 2 min. o
until onions are tender, stirring once during cooking.

Stir in soup until smooth.  Stir in half-and-half, wine and dill;
blend well.

Arrange salmon steaks in sauce with thicker portions toward edges of
dish.  Cover; microwave on HIGH 9 min. or until fish is nearly done,
rotating dish twice during cooking.  Let stand, covered, 5 min. or
until fish flakes easily with fork.  Garnish with additional dill
weed.
