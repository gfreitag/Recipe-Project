Title: Pork Tenderloin with Mustard Sauce^
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 8 servings
2    (1-1 1/2lb) pork
Tenderloins
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Bourbon
2 tb Brown sugar
MMMMM----------------MUSTARD SAUCE-COMBINE ALL AN---------------------
3    To 4 green onions, finely
Chopped
2/3 c  Sour cream
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Dry mustard
Combine soy sauce, bourbon and brown sugar in a ziploc bag. Marinate
tenderloins, refrigerated, at least 2 hours, turning bag occasionally.

Remove from bag and discard marinade.

Bake for 45 minutes on a rack in roasting pan at 325F. until meat
thermometer registers 160 degrees.
