Title: NUT RAISIN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Milk
1    Egg
1 c  Walnuts chopped
1 c  Raisins
3 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
4 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
Mix dry ingredients and add milk and beaten egg then nuts and
raisins. Put in greased and floured loaf pan and let set 20 minutes.
Bake 1 hour in 350 oven.
