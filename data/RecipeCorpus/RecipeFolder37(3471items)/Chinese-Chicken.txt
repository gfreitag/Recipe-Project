Title: Chinese Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Chinese
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Bean sprouts
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Corn starch
2 tb Water
1 c  Chicken, diced & cooked
1 c  Celery, sliced
2 tb Margarine
1/3 c  Green onion, sliced
1 cn Chunky chicken & rice soup
In saucepan, brown meat and cook celery and onion until tender (use
margarine).  Add soup, bean sprouts and soy sauce.  Blend corn starch
and water.  Add to soup mixture.  Cook until thick.  Serve with
Chinese noodles and egg rolls.
