Title: Alfredo Sauce
Categories: Italian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
-Granules
1/2 ts Dried Basil
1/2 ts Ground Black Pepper
1 ds Ground Red Pepper
2 c  Nonfat Cottage Cheese
3 tb Grated Parmesan Cheese
2 tb * Butter-flavored Granules
1/2 c  Evaporated Skimmed Milk
1/2 ts Chicken-flavored Bouillon
Serving Size : 6-8

Combine all ingredients in container of an electric blender; process
until smooth, stopping once to scrape down sides.

Pour into a small saucepan; cook sauce over low heat, stirring
constantly, until thoroughly heated. Makes 2 3/4 cups.

Per serving (3 1/2 ounces):  Calories 86 (8% from fat), Fat 0.8g (0.5g
saturated), Cholesterol 6mg, Sodium 495mg, Carbohydrate 6.8g, Fiber
0g, Protein 13.1g.

* I used 2 Tbsp margarine instead since we don't have butter-flavored
granules.
