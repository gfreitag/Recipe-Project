Title: Morning Glory Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Pecans or walnuts
1 cn 8 oz Crushed pineapple --
- drained
3    Eggs
1 c  Vegetable oil
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 1/4 c  Flour
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Ground cinnamon
2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Grated carrots
1    Grated apple
1/2 c  Shredded coconut
Into large bowl, sift together flour, sugar, cinnamon, baking soda
and salt.  Stir in carrots, apple, coconut, raisins, nuts and
pineapple.    In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, oil and
vanilla.  Pour into bowl with dry ingredients; blend well.  Spoon
batter into 16 muffin cups.  Fill about two-thirds full.  Bake in a
pre-heated 350F degree oven about 35 minutes.

Stephanie da Silva  24 Oct 1994
