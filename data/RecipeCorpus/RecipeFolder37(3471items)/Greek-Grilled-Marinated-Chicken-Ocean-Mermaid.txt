Title: Greek Grilled Marinated Chicken Ocean Mermaid
Categories: Bbq/grill, Poultry, Greek
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Dried Oregano -- crumbled
2    2 1/2 To 3 Pound Chickens --
-cut in serving pcs
1 c  Olive Oil
1/4 c  Fresh Lemon Juice
4    Garlic Cloves -- mashed to a
-paste
Rinse and pat dry chicken pieces. In a large shallow dish,whisk
together the oil,lemon juice,garlic & oregano with salt & pepper to
taste.

Add chicken and turn to coat. Let marinate for 2 days,covered and
chilled. Turn chicken pieces twice each day. Drain chicken
pieces,reserving marinade. Grill skin side down over glowing coals
,basting with marinade for 30-35 minutes,turning once (chicken breast
should be just springy to the touch). Transfer the chicken breasts to
a platter to keep warm.Continue grilling remaining chicken for about
10 more minutes or until thigh meat is tender.
