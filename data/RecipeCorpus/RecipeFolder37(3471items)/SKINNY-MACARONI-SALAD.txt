Title: SKINNY MACARONI SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Minced fresh basil
1/8 ts Grnd blk pepper
2    Cloves garlic minced
2 c  Cooked penne, elbows or etc.
1 c  Peeled & chopped Tomatoes
2 ts Balsamic vinegar
1/4 c  Minced red onions
2 ts Olive oil
combine all ingred excpt pasta in glass bowl.  Cover & let stand at
room temp. for 1 hour.  Just before serving fold in the pasta.
