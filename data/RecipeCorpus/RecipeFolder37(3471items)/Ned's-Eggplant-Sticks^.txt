Title: Ned's Eggplant Sticks^
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 10 servings
1 ts Pepper
3 lg Eggs, beaten
1/4 c  Milk
Oil for frying
3 lb Eggplant
1 c  Italian seasoned
Breadcrumbs
1 ts Salt
Peel and cut eggplant into 3X1/2" sticks. Soak in ice water a half
hour.

Mix breadcrumbs, salt and pepper.

dip eggplant into combined egg and milk, then in crumbs; chill 30
minutes.

Heat oil to 350 degrees at a depth of 1" in large fry pan and cook
sticks 10-12 at a time until golden brown, turning once, about 2
minutes. Drain on paper towels.
