Title: Priazzo^
Categories: Tasteofhome, Main dish, Meats, Pizza
Yield: 12 servings
Divided
18 sl Pepperoni
1 cn (4oz) sliced mushrooms,
Drained
1 cn (14oz) pizza sauce
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 sm Chopped onion
1 sm Chopped green pepper
2    (1lb) frozen bread loaves,
Thawed
4 c  Shredded mozzarella,
Brown beef, pepper and onion; drain.

Divide one loaf dough in half and press into bottom and up sides of
2-9" greased pie plates. Sprinkle each with 1 1/2 cups mozzarella
cheese. top each with half of the meat mixture. Top with pepperoni
and mushrooms. Divide remaining bread dough and roll into 2 10"
circles. cover each pie; crimp edges. Spread each with sauce and
sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake at 400F. 25-30 minutes. 12-16
servings.
