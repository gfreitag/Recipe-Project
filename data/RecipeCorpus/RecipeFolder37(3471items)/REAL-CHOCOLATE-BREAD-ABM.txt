Title: REAL CHOCOLATE BREAD ABM
Categories: Breads
Yield: 24 servings
1    Egg; unbeaten
1/4 c  Soft butter, or margerine
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Warm milk
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Flour, bread
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order. Set "baking control" at 10 o'clock.  Select "white bread" and
push Start.

In hot & humid weather, use 1/8 c less water.
