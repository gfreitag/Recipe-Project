Title: TROUT & VEGETABLE STEW
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Soup/stew
Yield: 3 servings.
1 tb Fresh tarragon leaves or
-1/4 tsp dry tarragon
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
3    Cleaned whole trout (8 to
-10 oz. each)
1 1/2 lb Thin red-skinned potatoes,
-cooked
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 sm Onion, thinly sliced
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 sm Red Bell Pepper, seeded,
-cut into 1/2-inch wide
-bite-size strips
1 lb Zucchini, sliced
-1/8-inch thick
Instructions:

Melt butter in wide nonstick frying pan over medium heat. Add onion
and garlic; cook, stirring often, until onion is limp. Add bell
pepper, zucchini, tarragon and pepper; cover and cook, stirring
occasionally, until squash is tender-crisp to bite (6 to 8 minutes).
Rinse fish; pat dry. Shake pan so vegetables are level; lay fish on
top. Cover and cook until fish are just slightly opaque or wet
inside; cut in thickest part to test (6 to 8 minutes). Transfer fish
and vegetables to warm platter. Serve with hot cooked potatoes.
