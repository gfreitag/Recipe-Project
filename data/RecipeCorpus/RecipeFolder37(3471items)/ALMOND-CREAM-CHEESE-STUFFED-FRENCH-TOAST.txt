Title: ALMOND CREAM CHEESE STUFFED FRENCH TOAST
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 6 servings
3 lb French bread (wide loaf); un    1 1/2 qt Milk
1 ts Almond extract
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 ts Cinnamon
Butter, maple syrup, powdere
4 oz Almonds, dry-roasted; diced
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/2 c  Raspberry preserves
24    Eggs
Recipe by: Blue Diamond Growers
Cut bread into 1-inch slices and cut a pocket into each side. Combine
almonds, cream cheese and preserves and stuff into bread pockets. Beat
together eggs, milk and flavorings. Soak bread and saute in butter
until browned on both sides. Serve 2-4 slices per person.
