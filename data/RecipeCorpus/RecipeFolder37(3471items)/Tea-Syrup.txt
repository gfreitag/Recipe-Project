Title: Tea Syrup
Categories: Beverages, Mixes
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Lipton tea leaves
6 c  Water
4 c  Sugar
Bring water to a hard boil; add tea leaves.  Let set for 15 minutes.
Drain hot tea into sugar and stir until dissolved.  Add 1 cup of this
syrup to 1 pitcher of cold water.  (This syrup will keep as long as 2
weeks in the refrigerator.)
