Title: Easy Fruit Punch
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
2 qt Any fruit punch
2 l  Ginger ale
1/2 ga Raspberry or orange
- sherbert
Soften up the half gallon of raspberry or orange sherbet and press it
into a jello mold that has a tube center, then freeze until hard.
Mix up 2 quarts of any fruit punch with two liters of ginger ale,
then float the sherbet ring on top.
