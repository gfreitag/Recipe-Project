Title: Grilled Salmon with Creamy Pesto^
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Soy sauce, divided
1 tb Shredded Parmesan
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 lb Salmon fillets, cut in 6
Pieces
2 cl Garlic, sliced
1/2 c  Fresh parsley, packed
1/2 c  Fresh basil leaves, packed
1/2 c  Low or nonfat sour cream
3 tb Skim milk
Blend garlic, parsley and basil; add sour cream, milk, 1 tablespoon
soy sauce,

and cheese. cover and process until smooth.

Mix remaining soy sauce and oil, brush on fish. Grill until fish
flakes easily

with a fork.

Warm pesto; spoon on salmon.

Salmon may be broiled.
