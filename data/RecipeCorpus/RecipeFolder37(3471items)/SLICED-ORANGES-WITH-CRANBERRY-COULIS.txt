Title: SLICED ORANGES WITH CRANBERRY COULIS
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Orange liqueur
6    Oranges, peels and white
Membranes removed, cut into
1/2 inch slices
1 1/2 lb Fresh cranberries
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Fresh orange juice
Grated zest of one orange
1/2 c  Water
1.  Simmer the cranberries, sugar, and orange juice uncovered in a
medium
size saucepan for 15 minutes. 2. Add the orange zest and water.
Simmer uncovered another 15 minutes,
stirring occasionally. 3. Strain the cranberry mixture through a
fine mesh sieve.  Stir in the
liqueur.  Let cool to room temperature. 4. On each of 6 dessert
plates, make a pool of the cranberry coulis.
Fan a sliced orange on each cranberry pool; spoon a little more
cranberry coulis over the oranges. ---
