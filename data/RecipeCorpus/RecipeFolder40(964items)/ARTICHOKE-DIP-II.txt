Title: ARTICHOKE DIP II
Categories: Dips, Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Artichoke heats unmarinated
4 oz Green chiles
1 c  Mayonnaise(no salad dressing
1 c  Parmeasan cheese
Drain artichoke  hearts and break apart.  Cut up green chiles. Mix
all ingredients together.  Bake at 350F for 30 minutes. Serve wit
tostada chips.
