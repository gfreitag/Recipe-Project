Title: APPLE-NUT FILLING
Categories: Crocker, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Chopped apples
1/3 c  Packed brown sugar
1 tb All-purpose flour
2 tb Margarine or butter
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 c  Finely chopped nuts
Cook all ingredients except nuts over medium heat, stirring
constantly, until apples are tender, 3 to 4 minutes; stir in nuts.

Source: Betty Crocker's Cookbook, 6th Edition
