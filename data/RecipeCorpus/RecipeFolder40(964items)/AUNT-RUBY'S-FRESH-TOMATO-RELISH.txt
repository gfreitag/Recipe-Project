Title: AUNT RUBY'S FRESH TOMATO RELISH
Categories: Condiment
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Tomatoes, peeled, seeded,
-and chopped
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper, chopped
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 ts Celery seed
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1.  In a medium bowl, combine tomatoes, onion, and green pepper.

2.  In a small bowl, combine sugar, vinegar, celery seed, salt, and
pepper.  Stir to dissolve the sugar.  Pour over tomato mixture;
toss.
Cover and chill till serving time.

Makes 4 cups.
