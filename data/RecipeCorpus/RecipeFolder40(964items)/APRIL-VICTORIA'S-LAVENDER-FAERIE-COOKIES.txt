Title: APRIL VICTORIA'S LAVENDER FAERIE COOKIES
Categories: Biscuits, Desserts
Yield: 1 Batch
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 tb Finely chopped lavender
-- flowers
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
Preheat oven to 375 F.

Cream butter and sugar.  Beat in eggs and vanilla; stir in the dry
ingredients until well blended.

Drop by half-teaspoons on ungreased baking sheet. Bake 8 to 10
minutes, and cool slightly on baking sheet before transferring to
plates.

From "Where the Wild Thyme Grows" article in "The Herb Companion."
April/May 1993, Vol. 5, No. 4.  Pg. 57. Posted by Cathy Harned.
