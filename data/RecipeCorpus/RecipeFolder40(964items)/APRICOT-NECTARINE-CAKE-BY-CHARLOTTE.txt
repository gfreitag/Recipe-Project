Title: APRICOT NECTARINE CAKE BY CHARLOTTE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk D.Hines Lemon Sup. cake mix
1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Apricot Nectar
4    Eggs (or 1 cup Egg Beaters)
1 c  Wessin Oil
Combine the above ingredients. Pour the mixture into BUNDT pan. Bake
for 55 minutes at 375 degrees.
