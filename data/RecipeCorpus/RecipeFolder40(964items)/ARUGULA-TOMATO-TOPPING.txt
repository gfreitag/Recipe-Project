Title: ARUGULA-TOMATO TOPPING
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 Servings
4 c  Arugula -- coarsely chopped
8    Plum tomatoes -- cored and
Diced
4 ts Lemon juice, fresh
2 ts Olive oil
1 ts Kosher salt
Freshly ground black pepper
To taste
Toss the arugula, tomatoes, lemon juice and olive oil together.
Season with salt and pepper.
