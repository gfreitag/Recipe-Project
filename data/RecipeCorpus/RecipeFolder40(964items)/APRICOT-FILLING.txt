Title: APRICOT FILLING
Categories: Preserves, Condiments
Yield: 10 Servings
4 c  Dried apricots
Orange peel
pn Nutmeg or cinnamon -- very
Sparingly
Combine all ingredients in a heavy pot. Add water to barely cover.
Bring to a bo il, then cook over low heat until sauced.
