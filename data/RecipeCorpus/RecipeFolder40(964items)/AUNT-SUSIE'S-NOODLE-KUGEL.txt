Title: AUNT SUSIE'S NOODLE KUGEL
Categories: Breakfast, Brunch, Cheese/eggs, Desserts, Pasta
Yield: 8 - 10
8 oz Spaghetti noodles; cooked &
-drained
1/4 c  Butter; melted
1 lb Cottage cheese with
-pineapple
4    Egg
1 pt Sour cream or yogurt
1/2 c  Sugar (white)
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/4 c  Golden raisins
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
4 sl Pineapple; cut into quarters
3 tb Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
Mix all ingredients (except topping) together and pour into greased
13x9 pan.  Lay pineapple quarters decoratively on the top. Bake at
350 for 30 minutes. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar mixture on top and
bake for 30 minutes longer. Serve warm or cold. Refrigerate leftovers.
