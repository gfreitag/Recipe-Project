Title: ARBY'S SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Catsup
2 ts Water
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Onion powder
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Tabasco sauce
Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan and cook over medium heat,
stirring constantly, until the sauce begins to boil, 5 to 10 minutes.
Remove the sauce from the heat. Cover and allow to cool. Pour into a
covered container for storage in your refrigerator. Keeps for a month
or two.  Makes 1 cup.
