Title: AVOCADO & BANANA
Categories: Baby food
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Dead ripe, almost black
-avocado
1 tb Dead ripe, almost black
-banana
What can I say except mush together with a fork.  Most babies adore
this combination, but avocados are high in unsaturated fat, so go
easy on this dish.
