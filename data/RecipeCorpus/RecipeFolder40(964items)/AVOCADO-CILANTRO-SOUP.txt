Title: AVOCADO CILANTRO SOUP
Categories: Soups, Low-cal
Yield: 4 Servings
8 md Scallions
1/2 c  Fresh Cilantro Leaves
2    Cloves Garlic
4 oz Pared Avocado
1 1/2 c  Plain Nonfat Yogurt
1/2 c  Skim Buttermilk
2 tb Lime Juice
1/2 ts Curry Powder
1/8 ts Ground Red Pepper
In a food processor, combine scallions, cilantro and garlic; process
until finely chopped.  Add avocado; process until smooth. Add yogurt,
buttermilk. 1/4 cup water, lime juice, curry powder and red pepper to
food processor; process until smooth.  Cover and refrigerate at least
1 hour, until well chilled.  Each 1/2 cup serving contains 115
calories, 5 gm fat,
104 mg sodium and 3 mg cholesterol.
