Title: AVOCADO STUFFED TOMATOES
Categories: Salads, Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 4 Servings
4 ea Fresh tomatoes
1 ea Ripe avocado
2 dr Lemon juice
1 pn Chili powder
1 sm Handful of alfalfa sprouts
2 tb Chopped celery or green bell
-- pepper
1 ts Chopped parsley
1 pn Coriander
Salt
Cut tops off tomatoes & scoop out insides.  Save insides for another
dish. Mash avocado & mix with the rest of the ingredients. Stuff into
the tomato shells.  Particularly good as an appetizer to a Mexican
meal.
