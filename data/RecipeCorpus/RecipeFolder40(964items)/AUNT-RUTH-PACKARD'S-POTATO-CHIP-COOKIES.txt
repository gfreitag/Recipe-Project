Title: AUNT RUTH PACKARD'S POTATO CHIP COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 5 Dozen
2    Sticks of butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Crushed potato chips
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
2 c  Flour
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix margarine and sugar and vanilla. Add
crushed potato chips and nuts mixed with flour. Roll into balls about
the size of a walnut and place on ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten
down with a glass that has been dipped into sugar.  Bake for 14-16
minutes. Makes approximately 5 dozen cookies
