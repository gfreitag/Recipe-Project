Title: APRICOT FILLING 2
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Dried apricots
2 c  Water
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
3 tb Ground blanched almonds
:    Boil the apricots in the water with the sugar for 10 minutes,
then cover and simmer 15-20 minutes. Remove from the fire and sieve.
Add the almonds and cool.
When using, heat the amount desired and spread on toast. Makes 1 c.
