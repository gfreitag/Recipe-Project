Title: APPLE-NUT PANCAKES
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 10 Pancakes
1    Egg
1 c  Milk
2 tb Oil or melted margarine
1 1/4 c  Flour
1 tb Baking powder
1 tb Sugar
1/2 tb Salt
1/2 c  Walnuts
1 c  Peeled apple/cubes
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Cloves
Preheat griddle, 350F (180C) or medium high. Put all ingredientes in
the process at MIX until batter is almost smooth.

Bake on lightly greased griddle until nicely browned. Turn only once.
Yield: 10-12 pancakes.
