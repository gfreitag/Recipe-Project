Title: APPLESAUCE MUFFINS (NO GUILT BAKING)
Categories: Muffins, Desserts, Breakfast
Yield: 12 Servings
1    Large egg
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 1/2 c  Unsweetened applesauce
2 c  Flour
3/4 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Cinnamon
3/4 c  Raisins (optional)
Combine egg, oil and applesauce. Blend in remaining ingredients. Bake
in no-stick pan, at 425 deg f. for 25 minutes or until lightly
browned on top. Makes 12 servings.
