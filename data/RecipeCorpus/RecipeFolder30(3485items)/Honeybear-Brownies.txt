Title: Honeybear Brownies
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 c  All-Purpose Flour --
Unsifted
1/3 c  Hershey's Cocoa
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Chopped Nuts
1/3 c  Butter Or Margarine
3/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Honey
2 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
Grease one 9-inch pan. Cream butter and sugar in a small mixer bowl;
blend in honey and vanilla. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well
after each addition. Combine flour, cocoa and salt; gradually add to
creamed mixture. Stir in nuts. Pour into pan. Bake at 350 F for 35-30
mins or until brownies begin to pull away from edge of pan. Cool in
pan. Makes 16 brownies.
