Title: SALMON & CREAM CHEESE BALL
Categories: Appetizers, Fish
Yield: 16 servings
MMMMM---------------------THE FRUGAL GOURMET--------------------------
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 lb Cream cheese; mashed
2 tb Mayonnaise
1 tb Dried parsley flakes
-posted by Gaye Levy DTXT63A
1 lb Canned Salmon
1/2 ts Liquid smoke
1 tb Horseradish
Remove any bones that are in the salmon.  Add all ingredients and mix
well. According to Jeff SMith, this recipe serves only 1 if he is
present <g>. NOTES from Gaye: This recipe is a real keeper. I eat it
piggy-style, with a spoon! From: Lorna Price Date: 05-30-94
