Title: Andersen's Split Pea Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Restaurants
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM------------------------THE REGISTER-----------------------------
1/4 ts Ground dried thyme
ds Cayenne
1    Bay leaf
Salt and pepper
2 qt Water
2 c  Green split peas
1    Rib celery, coarsely chpd
1 lg Carrot, chpd
1 sm Onion, chpd
In lg pot, combine all ingreds and bring to hard boil, 20 mins. Reduce
heat, simmer 45 mins oir until peas are tender. Strain soup through
fine sieve and reheat. Taste, correct seasonings.
