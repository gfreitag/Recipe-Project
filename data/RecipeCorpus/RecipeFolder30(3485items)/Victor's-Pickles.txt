Title: Victor's Pickles
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Hot Pepper
1 ea Celery Stalk; cut up
2 ea Whole Garlic Cloves
1 ts Pickling Spice (rounded)
1 tb Coarse Salt (rounded)
12 sm Cucumbers
VICTOR'S PICKLES

These pickles are easy to prepare. The hot pepper and garlic give
them a wonderful taste.

Place al the ingredients into a freshly rinsed quart jar. Add water to
cover and seal. Let stand at room temperature for 5 days. Refrigerate.

From: Classic Kosher Cooking by Sara Finkel
