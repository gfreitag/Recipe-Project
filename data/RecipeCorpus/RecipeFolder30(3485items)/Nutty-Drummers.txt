Title: Nutty Drummers
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Chunky Peanut Butter
2    Scallions, sliced
1/4 ts Garlic Powder
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/4 c  Chopped peanuts
3 tb Vegetable oil
8    Chicken Drumsticks
3/4 c  Chicken broth
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Worchestershire sauce
Preheat oven to 375 deg.  In a large skillet heat oil to medium hot.
Add drumsticks and cook, turning frequently, until lightly brown on
all sides (about 10 min.).  REmove dumsticks to a &' x 11" baking
dish and drain oil from the skillet.  In Same skillet, place
remaining ingredients, except peanuts; stir to mix well.  Cook over
medium heat, stirring constantly, for about 3 min. or until slightly
thickened.  Pour sauce over drumsticks.
Bake, covered for about 30 -35 min. or until fork can be inserted
into chicken with ease.  REmove drumsticks to serving dish and pour
sauce over all.  Sprinkle with Peanuts.
