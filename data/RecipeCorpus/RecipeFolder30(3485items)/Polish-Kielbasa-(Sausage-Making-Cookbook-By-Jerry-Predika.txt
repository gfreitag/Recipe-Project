Title: Polish Kielbasa (Sausage-Making Cookbook By Jerry Predika
Categories: Meats, Homemade, Polish
Yield: 6 servings
8    Cloves pressed garlic
1/4 ts Ground Allspice
3 1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Cold Water
4 lb Coarse Ground Pork Butt
1 lb Coarse Ground Beef Stew Meat
2 tb Coarsely Ground Pepper
1 tb Marjoram Leaves
FROM :    Clarence Fontish

Combine all ingredients, mix well and stuff into hog casing or make
patties.

Source: The Sausage Making Cookbook, by Jerry Predika
