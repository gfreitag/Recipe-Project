Title: Banana Fudge Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 32 servings
3/4 c  Cocoa
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Cream cheese
2 c  Granulated sugar
4    Eggs -- beaten
3    Bananas -- very ripe,
Mashed
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a 9x13 pan.

Cream together the cream cheese and sugar, then add eggs one at a
time and mix until well blended.  Mix in bananas.

Sift dry ingredients together and add to creamed mixture, and stir
just until moist.  Stir in vanilla.

Pour into prepared pan and bake 45 minutes, until a toothpick
inserted in the center comes out clean.  Let cool at least 15 minutes
before slicing.
