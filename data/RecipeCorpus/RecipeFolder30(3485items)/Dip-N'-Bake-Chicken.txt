Title: Dip N' Bake Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Milk
1    Egg
1/4 c  Butter or margarine, melted
1 1/2 c  Ground Oat Flour
3/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Paprika or sage
1/2 ts Salt
One 2-1/2 to 3 lb. broiler-fryer, cut up

Coat chicken with combined dry ingredients; dip into combined milk
and egg. Coat again with dry ingredients.  Place in foil-lined large
shallow baking pan; drizzle butter over chicken.  Bake in preheated
hot oven (400) 45 to 50 minutes.  makes 4 servings
