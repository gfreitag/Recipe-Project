Title: Simple Chicken Italiano
Categories: Poultry, Italian
Yield: 5 servings
-Mixtures California Style
-Frozen Vegetables (16 oz)
Hot cooked pasta
Grated parmesan cheese (opt)
3/4 lb Thin strips chicken breast
3/4 c  Reduced calorie Italian
-dressing
1 pk Green Giant American
1. In large skillet, combine chicken and about 1/3 of the Italian
dressing.

2. Saute until chicken begins to brown.

3. Add vegetables and remaining dressing. Cover; simmer 7-9 minutes
until vegetables are crisp-tender, stirring frequently. Serve over
pasta; sprinkle with cheese.
