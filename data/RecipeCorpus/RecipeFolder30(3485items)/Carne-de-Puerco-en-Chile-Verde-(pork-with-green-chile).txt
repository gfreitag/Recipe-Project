Title: Carne de Puerco en Chile Verde (pork with green chile)
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 x  Oil or lard
2 ea Cloves garlic, crushed
1 ea Tomato, med, chopped
1 x  Salt, pepper
1 lb Pork or beef
4 ea Grn chiles, toasted/chopped
1 ea Onion, med, chopped
1 c  Boiling water
Cut pork or beef into small cubes. Saute in oil until well
browned. Drain off all but 1 tablespoon fat. Add chopped chiles,
garlic, onion, tomato and water and season to taste with salt and
pepper. Cover tightly and simmer 1 hour.
