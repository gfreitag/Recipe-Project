Title: Lemon Bars Deluxe
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 dozen
2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1/2 ts Baking powder
Sugar, powdered
2 1/4 c  Flour, all-purpose; divided
1/2 c  Sugar, powdered
1 c  Margarine; softened
4    Eggs; beaten
Sift together 2 cups flour and 1/2 cup powdered sugar.  Cut in
margarine until mixture clings together; press into a greased 13x9x2"
pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes or unitl lightly
browned.

Combine eggs, sugar, and lemon juice; beat well.  Sift together 1/4
cup flour and baking powder; stir into egg mixture.  Pour over baked
crust. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes or until lightly
browned. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.  Cool; cut into bars.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in the 1970s. Typos by
