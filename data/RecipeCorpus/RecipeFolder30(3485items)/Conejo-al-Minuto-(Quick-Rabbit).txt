Title: Conejo al Minuto (Quick Rabbit)
Categories: Game, Main dish
Yield: 1 serving
1 tb Parsley, chopped
4 oz Onion, chopped
2 tb Brandy
1/2 c  Water
1    Young, jointed rabbit
Salt
Flour
4 oz Lard
Season the rabbit with salt, coat with flour and fry in lard. Remove,
and in the same fat fry parsley and onions.  When they begin to turn
golden, return the rabbit to pan, sprinkle with brandy, cook a few
minutes, add water, season with salt, and cook on a lively heat for
30 minutes, stirring from time to time to prevent sticking.
