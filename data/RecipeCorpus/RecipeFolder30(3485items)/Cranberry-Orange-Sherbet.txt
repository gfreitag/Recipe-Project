Title: Cranberry-Orange Sherbet
Categories: Homestyle, Ice cream
Yield: 6 servings
Concentrate
1/4 c  Sugar -- optional
ds Ground Cinnamon
ds Salt
1 c  Heavy Cream
16 oz Can Cranberry Sauce,
Jellied
6 oz Can Orange Juice, Frozen
Place heavy cream in a mixing bowl and beat with an egg beater until
stiff peaks form. Gradually beat in cranberry sauce and frozen juice.
Add sugar if desired, cinnamon, ans salt. Beat until well blended.
Pour into 2 ice trays, put into refrigerator. Freeze until solid.

Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
