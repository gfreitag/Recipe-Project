Title: Party Bake Apples
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
4    Baking apples
1    Egg white
2 tb Sugar
14    Blanched almonds, shredded
4    Maraschino cherries, chopped
1/2 c  Dark corn syrup
1/2 c  Water
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Grated lemon rind
1 tb Butter
2 tb Sugar
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine first 6 ingredients. Bring to
boil. Remove from heat. Core apples; pare upper halves. Place in
shallow baking pan. Pour syrup mixture over apples. Bake for about 1
hour, or until tender, basting frequently. Remove from oven.

Beat egg white until foamy; gradually beat in 2 tblsp. sugar. Top
apples with meringue; insert almonds and cherries. Return to oven;
bake 15 minutes, or until delicately browned. Serves 4.
