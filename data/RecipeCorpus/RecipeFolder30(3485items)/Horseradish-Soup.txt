Title: Horseradish Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1    Pc horseradish root (freshly
-dug if possible)
1 qt Beef stock
1 c  Shredded beef
Grate horseradish directly into simmering beef stock. (stand back, the
fumes will knock your socks off). Float a piece of french bread on
top of each serving.
