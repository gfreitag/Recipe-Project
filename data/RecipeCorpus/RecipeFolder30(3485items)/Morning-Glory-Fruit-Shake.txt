Title: Morning Glory Fruit Shake
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Chopped papayas
1 c  Chopped peeled apples
1/2 c  Ice cubes
1 ts Honey
2 c  Sliced bananas
2 c  Sliced strawberries
2 c  Chopped pineapple
-OR
In a blender container, combine the bananas, strawberries, pineapples
or papayas, apples, ice cubes and honey. Puree on high speed until
smooth. Serve immediately.
