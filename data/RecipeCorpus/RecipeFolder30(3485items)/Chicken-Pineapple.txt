Title: Chicken Pineapple
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
4 tb Brown sugar
1/2    Onion, finely chopped
1/3 c  Chili sauce
Ingredients:
2    Chicken brest, bone and
-halved
3/4 c  Pineapple juice
Directions: Place chicken in casserole. Mix remaining ingredients and
pour over chicken. Baked covered for one hour at 350 degres. Bake
uncovered for half an hour. If sauce is thin add little flour to
thicken during final moments of cooking.
