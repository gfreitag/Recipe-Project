Title: Grape Nuts Hot Recipe
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 bowl
Fresh fruit (opt)
Honey (opt)
1/4 c  Grape Nuts cereal
1/4 c  Skim milk
Place cereal in microwaveable bowl; add skim milk. Microwave on high
to taste; 30 seconds for a BIG crunch, 60 seconds for a little
crunch. Add fresh fruit or honey, if desired.
