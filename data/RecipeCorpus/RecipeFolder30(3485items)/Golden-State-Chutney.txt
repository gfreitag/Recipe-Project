Title: Golden State Chutney
Categories: ', Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 4 cups
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
2 c  Chopped Calif. dried figs
1    Apple; peeled & diced
1    Lemon; sliced paper-thin
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Molasses
In large sauce pan, combine dried figs and apple.  Add remaining
ingredients.  Bring to a boil; simmer, uncovered 30 minutes.  Makes
about 4 cups.  Serve as an accompaniment to pork, lamb, or chicken.
