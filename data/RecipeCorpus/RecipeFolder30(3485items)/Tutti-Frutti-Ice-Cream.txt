Title: Tutti-Frutti Ice Cream
Categories: Homemade
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Pineapple Sundae Topping
1/2 c  Maraschino Cherries --
Chopped
1 c  Pecan Or Walnut Sundae
Topping -- drained
3 c  Light Cream
3 c  Heavy Cream
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Vanilla Extract
1/2 c  Strawberry Sundae Topping
Combine light and heavy creams and stir in sugar and salt until
dissolved. Add vanilla. Freeze according to ice-cream maker's
directions. When ice cream begins to form a mush, add fruit toppings,
cherries, and nuts. Finish freezing. Yield: 2 quarts
