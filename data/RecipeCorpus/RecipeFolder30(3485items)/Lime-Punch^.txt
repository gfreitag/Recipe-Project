Title: Lime Punch^
Categories: Homestyle
Yield: 1 servings
Cracked ice
1 sl Pineapple
2    Crystallized cherries
8    Cubes sugar
2    Oranges, juice of
2    Limes, juice of
1 1/2 c  Water
Rub the cubes of sugar over the rind of the oranges and limes. Then
put the sugar in a bowl and pour the lime and orange juices over it.
Add the water and serve when the sugar is melted; chilling with
plenty of cracked ice. Put the pineapple and cherries in at the
moment of serving. Makes 2 servings. Many of the recipes in this
collection did not contain amounts or oven temperatures. I have typed
them in as they appear in the book, typos and all.
