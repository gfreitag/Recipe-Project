Title: Raisin Spice Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Sugar
Stir until shortening melts,
Then add 1 teaspoon vanilla.
Add raisins and water.
Add 1/2 box of rasins to 2
Cups boiling water and cook
For 15 minutes.  Then add 1
Cup cold water and set aside
1/2 c  Shortening
MMMMM-----------------------SIFT TOGETHER----------------------------
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Allspice
4 c  Flour
1 tb Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
Stir altogether; mixture will be thick.  Bake approximately 40
minutes in two 9 inch pans or in three loaf pans at 350 degrees until
toothpick comes out clean.  May ice if desired. Randy Rigg
