Title: Italian-Style Oven Dinner
Categories: Main dish, Italian
Yield: 8 servings
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1    Chicken, 3 pound, cut up
1 tb Olive oil
Salt
Pepper
1/2 ts Black pepper
1    Red bell pepper, sliced
1    Green bell pepper, sliced
1 cn Italian peeled toamtoes,
-drained and cut up (28 oz)
1 lb Italian sausage, cut in 2"
-chunks
Put pepper, tomatoes, and sausage chunks in a baking pan and sprinkle
with garlic. Arrange chicken on top. Brush chicken with olive oil.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake, uncovered, 1 hour and 15
minutes, until tender, basting frequently.

Makes 6-8 servings.
