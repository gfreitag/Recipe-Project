Title: Woodruff Orange Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 sm Can of mandarian oranges
With juice
1 sm Can of frozen orange juice
2 sm Boxes of orange Jello
2 c  Hot water
1 sm Cans of crushed pineapple
With juice
Mix together orange Jello and hot water.  Let cool, then add rest of
ingredients.  Mix and let set in a 9x9 dish.

Topping for Salad 1 envelope of Dream Whip 1/2 cup of milk 1 box of
instant lemon pudding

Mix dream whip and milk and whip.  Make lemon pudding as directed on
the box.  Fold Dream Whip into pudding and spread on top of congealed
Jello mixture. Randy Rigg
