Title: Goat Cheese Stuffed Plum Tomatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese/eggs, Herbs/spice, Salads
Yield: 18 stuffers
1/4 c  Our Thyme Garden Italian
. Blend Oil
9 lg Plum tomatoes
1/2 lb Goat cheese
1/2 c  Fresh chives, chopped
Cut the tomatoes in half lengthwise and scoop out all the seeds and
inner membranes.  Place them upside down in a colander and allow to
drain. Place the cheese, chives and oil in a bowl and mash together
to form a smooth creamy mixture.  Divide the cheese mixture evenly
among the tomato halves.

Serve as an appetizer or for the salad course.
