Title: Honey Chicken Wings
Categories: Poultry, Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Ketchup
1    Garlic clove, crushed
2 lb Chicken wings, disjointed &
-tips discarded
Freshly ground pepper
1 c  Honey
1. Preheat oven to 350Ã¸. Season chicken liberally w/ pepper. Place
wings in a large baking dish.

2. In a small bowl, mix together honey, soy sauce, oil, ketchup &
garlic until well blended. Pour over chicken. Bake for 45 minutes,
until chicken is well done & sauce is thick.
