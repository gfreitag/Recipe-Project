Title: Stuffed Tomatoes~
Categories: Low cal/fat, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Grated Romano Cheese
1/8 ts Pepper
md Tomatoes
2 c  Loose pack frozen veggies *
* zucchini, carrots, cauliflower, lima beans, and Italian beans

Cut tomatoes in half. Spoon out pulp, leaving a 1/4" thick shell.
Reserve tomato pulp; discard seeds. Chop tomato pulp. Set shells and
pulp aside. In a small saucepan cook frozen veggies according to
package

directions.   Drain. Stir in chopped tomato pulp. Spoon 1/8 of the
veggie mixture into   each tomato shell. Place tomatoes in a 10x6x2"
baking dish. Sprinkle with   grated cheese and pepper. Bake in 350
deg F. oven for 15-20 minutes or till heated through.

Per serving: 53 calories, 3 g protein, 9 g carbohydrates, 1 g fat,
4 mg cholesterol, 63 mg sodium, 255 mg potassium.
