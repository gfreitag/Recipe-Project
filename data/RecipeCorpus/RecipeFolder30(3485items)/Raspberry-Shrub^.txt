Title: Raspberry Shrub^
Categories: Homestyle
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Sugar
2    Lemons, juice of
1 qt Red raspberries
1 qt Water
Put berries in a large bowl and mash to a pulp. Boil water, add sugar
and lemon juice and pour over berries. When cool, pres through a
colander. Chill and serve over ice.
