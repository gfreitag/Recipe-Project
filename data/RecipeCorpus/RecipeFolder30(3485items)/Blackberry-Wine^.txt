Title: Blackberry Wine^
Categories: Homestyle
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Sugar
1 ga Blackberries
1 qt Boiling water
Bruise berries, add boiling water. Let stand 24 hours, stirring
occasionally. Strain off the liquor and put into a cask. Add sugar;
cork tightly and let stand till next October, when it will be ready
for use. It may be bottled. Many of the recipes in this collection
did not contain amounts or oven temperatures. I have typed them in as
they appear in the book, typos and all.
