Title: Sesame Honey Crisps
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Oil
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Each mild honey and brown
-sugar
1 ts Vanilla essence
2 c  Sesame seeds, toasted
1/2 c  Coconut, toasted
2 1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Each of baking soda and salt
Mix all the ingredients together. Form into small balls and place on a
greased baking tray. Flatten with a fork. Bake at 170Ã¸C for 8-10
minutes until golden brown. Allow to cool then store in an airtight
tin. Makes three dozen crisp, delicious biscuits.
