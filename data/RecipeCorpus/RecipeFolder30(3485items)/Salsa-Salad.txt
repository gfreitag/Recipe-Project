Title: Salsa Salad
Categories: Salads, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Season salt
1/2 ts Garlic flakes
1/4 ts Oregano
1/16 ts Cayenne pepper, or red
-pepper flakes to taste
2 c  Finely chopped fresh
-tomatoes
1/3 c  Chopped onions
1/2 c  Chopped peppers (green)
1 tb Parsley
salt to taste shredded lettuce

Combine the tomato, onion and peppers together with the spices.
Chill to allow the flavours to combine.  Spoon Salsa over shredded
lettuce. Salsa can be made the night before.

Origin: Adapted from Watkins Jiffy Dinner Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, May/95.
