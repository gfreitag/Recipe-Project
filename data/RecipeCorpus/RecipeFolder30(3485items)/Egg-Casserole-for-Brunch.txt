Title: Egg Casserole for Brunch
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
6    Eggs, lightly beaten
1 qt Milk
12 sl Bread, buttered and cubed
1/2 lb Velveeta cheese cubed.
1 ts Salt
Mix eggs and milk and pour over other ingredients in a buttered 9 X
13 pan. Refrigerate overnight and set out 1/2 hour before baking.
Sometime add a cup of cubed ham before baking.  Bake 1 hour at 350
degrees. Submitted By DOROTHY THOMPSON   On   04-26-95
