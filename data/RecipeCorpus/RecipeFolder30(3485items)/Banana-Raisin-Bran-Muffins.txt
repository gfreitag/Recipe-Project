Title: Banana-Raisin Bran Muffins
Categories: Fruit, Breads, Muffins
Yield: 18 servings
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 md Banana -- mashed
2 c  Raisin bran
1 c  Flour
3/4 c  Whole wheat flour
1/3 c  Sugar
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 350.  In a medium bowl, combine flours, sugar, baking
powder, baking soda and salt.  In a small bowl, whisk eggs,
buttermilk, oil and bananas.  Add buttermilk mixture and raisin bran
flakes to dry ingredients; stir until ingredients are moistened.
Fill paper-lined muffin tins two-thirds full.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes
or until golden brown.

Source:  The Spirit of Christmas Book 8.
