Title: Quick Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
4 tb Butter
1 ts Vanilla
2    Egg whites
Powdered sugar
Beat egg whites until light.  Add enough powdered sugar to make
stiff, then add melted butter and vanilla.  Beat and spread on your
favorite cake. Randy Rigg
