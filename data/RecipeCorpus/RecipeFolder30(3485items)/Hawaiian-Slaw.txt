Title: Hawaiian Slaw
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
2    Eggs, well beaten
5 tb Salad oil
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/3 c  Sugar
1/2 cn Pimentoes, cut fine
1/4 ts Powdered garlic
1 lg Head of cabbage
6    Ribs of celery
2    Mangoes, 1 red 1 green
8    Marshmallows, cut up
6 sl Pineapple, cut up
Salt & pepper
Dressing for Hawaiian Slaw
Combine all ingredients and cook to a boil.  Cool.  Pour over cabbage.
(Before mixing the cabbage and dressing, take a tablespoon of the
cabbage mixture and pour a little of the dressing on it to taste.  If
it is too sweet add a little vinegar; if too sour add a little sugar,
and if it tastes just right go ahead and eat it.) Randy Rigg
