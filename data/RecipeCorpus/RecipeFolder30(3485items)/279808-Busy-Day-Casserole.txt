Title: 279808 Busy Day Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
About 5 tbsp. brown sugar
ds Worcestershire sauce
ds Prepared mustard
Bacon strips to cover top
Of casserole (about 10)
2 lg Cans baked beans (at least
3 lbs total)
3 lg Onions, chopped
2    Bell peppers, chopped
1 1/2 tb Garlic pods (leave whole)
Cook onions and bell peppers until soft.  Combine all ingredients in
baking pan with bacon strips on top.  Bake at 400 degree oven for 45
minutes to 1 hour.  (Some ovens may need to be set a little higher,
ovens vary.) Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-25-95
