Title: 279853 Layered Supper
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables, Potato, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2    To 2 lb. ground beef or
Ground chuck
15 oz Can tomato sauce
1 c  Shredded cheese
6 c  Potatoes, thinly sliced
2 cn Whole kernel corn, drained
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1 c  Chopped onion
2 c  Sliced carrots
Place potatoes in buttered casserole dish season with 1 teaspoon salt
and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, sprinkled on top.  Mix corn, pepper
and pour on top potatoes.  Put onions and then carrots, crumble
ground beef over vegetables.  Sprinkle 1 teaspoon salt and 1/4
teaspoon black pepper on top. Top with tomato sauce.  Bake in covered
casserole dish at 350 degrees for 2 hours.  When done uncover and
sprinkle with cheese. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-25-95
