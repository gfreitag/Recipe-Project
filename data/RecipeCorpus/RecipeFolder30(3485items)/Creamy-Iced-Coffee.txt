Title: Creamy Iced Coffee
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
-(rounded tablespoons)
3 c  Chopped ice
1 c  Chilled brewed coffee,
-made double-strength
2 tb Confectioners' sugar
Combine the coffee, sugar, and ice, and blend until creamy.

Nutiritional analysis per serving: 122 calories; 0.011 grams total
fat (0 percent of calories from fat); 0 milligrams cholesterol; 6.20
milligrams sodium.
