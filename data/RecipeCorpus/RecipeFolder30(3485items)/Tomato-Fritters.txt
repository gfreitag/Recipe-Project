Title: Tomato Fritters
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 16 servings
3/4 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
pn Cinnamon
Olive oil for frying
1 1/3 c  Plum tomatoes, seeded, diced
2/3 c  Zucchini, finely diced
1/2 c  Onion, finely chopped
2 tb Mint leaves, chopped
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
Combined diced tomatoes, zucchini, onion and mint in a small bowl.
Combine flour, baking powder, salt & pepper and cinnamon in a medium
bowl. Stir vegetables into dry ingredients.

Heat olive oil in a lage non-stick skillet over med-high heat. Work in
batches and add more oil as needed, drop batter by rounded
tablespoonfuls into oil. Cook until golden brown, about 2 minutes per
side. Drain on paper towels, serve hot.
