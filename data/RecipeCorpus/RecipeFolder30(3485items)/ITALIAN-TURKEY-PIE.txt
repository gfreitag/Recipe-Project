Title: ITALIAN TURKEY PIE
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 1 pie
2 1/2 oz Jar sliced mushrooms,
- drained
1/4 c  Grated parmesan
1 c  Shredded mozzarella (4 oz)
2 tb Chopped green pepper
2 tb Sliced olives
1 lb Ground turkey
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Seasoned bread crumbs
1 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Crushed oregano leaves
4 tb Tomato paste
Combine turkey, milk, bread crumbs, garlic salt, pepper and 1/2 tsp
crushed oregano.  Press mix evenly over bottom and sides of 9 inch
pie plate. Spread paste over meat "crust."  Top with oregano,
mushrooms and parmesan. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes. Sprinkle with
mozzarella, green pepper, olives. Bake 15 minutes longer. Cool 5
minutes, cut into wedges. Makes 6 servings.
