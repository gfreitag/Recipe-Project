Title: Baked Squash Casserole  --  Progressive Farmer
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
2 lg Eggs,  lightly beaten
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Soft breadcrumbs
2 tb Butter or margarine, melted
2 lb Yellow squash, small, sliced
1 lg Onion, chopped
2    Stalks celery, chopped
3/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Milk
Combine the first four ingredients in a saucepan; bring to a boil over
medium-high heat.  Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer 10 minutes or
until squash is tender; drain.  Mash the squash; stir in the milk,
eggs, salt and pepper.  Spoon the squash mixture into a greased
2-quart casserole dish.

Combine the breadcrumbs and butter, sprinkle over the top of the
squash mixture.

Bake at 350Ã¸F for about 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
