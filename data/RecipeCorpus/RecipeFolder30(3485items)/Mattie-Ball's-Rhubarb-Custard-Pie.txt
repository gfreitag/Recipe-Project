Title: Mattie Ball's Rhubarb Custard Pie
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Flour
1 ts Lemon Rind -- grated
pn Salt
2 tb Unsalted Butter
2 c  Rhubarb -- diced
1    9 Inch Pie Shell -- unbaked
2    Eggs
2 tb Water
3/4 c  Sugar
Place the rhubarb in the pie shell. Combine the eggs, water, sugar,
flour, lemon rind, and salt. Pour over the rhubarb and dot with
butter. Bake in a 350 F oven for 35-45 mins.
