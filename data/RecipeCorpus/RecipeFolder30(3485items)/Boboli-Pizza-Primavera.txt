Title: Boboli Pizza Primavera
Categories: Italian, Pizza
Yield: 1 servings
6    Asparagus spears; cooked
Basil; fresh
Oregano; fresh
Garlic; crushed
Salt and pepper
Olive Oil
3/4 c  Mozzarella; grated
3    Cherry tomatoes; thinly
-sliced or halved
1/2 c  Broccoil; lightly cooked
Brush bread with olive oil.  Cover with mixture of veggies. Sprinkle
with herbs, garlic, salt and pepper. Top with cheese. Heat to taste
or until cheese has melted.
