Title: Marinated Broccoli
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 large bowl
1 tb Accent
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1 ts Garlic salt
3 bn Fresh broccoli
1 c  Vinegar
1 1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Dill seed
Divide the broccoli into flowerets.

Combine the remaining ingredients and marinate the broccoli in
refrigerator over night.

Drain before serving.
