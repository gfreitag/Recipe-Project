Title: Cheesy Chili for a Crowd
Categories: Main dish, Tex/mexican
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Water
3 tb Chili powder
2 ts Cumin
16 oz Cheez whiz
4 c  Cooked pasta; such as rotini
--macaroni or wagon wheels
8 oz Sour cream
2 lb Ground beef
1 c  Onion; chopped
14 1/2 oz Tomatoes, whole; undrained
-and cut up
15 oz Kidney beans; rinsed and
-drained
6 oz Tomato paste
Brown meat with onion on 4 qt dutch oven.  Drain. Reduce heat to
medium-low.  Stir in tomatoes, beans, tomato paste, water, chili
powder and cumin.  Cover; simmer 10 minutes or until thoroughly
heated. Stir in cheez whiz until melted.  Spoon over macaroni.  Top
with sour cream. Source: Kraft ad
