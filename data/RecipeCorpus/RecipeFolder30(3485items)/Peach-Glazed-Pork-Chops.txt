Title: Peach Glazed Pork Chops
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
6    Pork chops; 1/2" thick
1/3 c  Peach or apricot preserves
1 tb Mustard, dijon
8 1/2 oz Peach slices; undrained
1 c  Hot water
6 oz Stove Top Stuffing for Pork
1/4 c  Margarine; in pieces
Heat oven to 375.  Drain peaches, reserving liquid. Add water to
syrup to measure 1 1/2 cups.  Mix syrup mixture, contents of
seasoning packet and margarine in large bowl until margarine is
melted. Stir in stuffing crumbs and peaches.   Let stand 5 minutes.
Spoon stuffing into 13 x 9 baking pan. Arrange chops over stuffing.
Mix preserves with mustard. Spoon over chops. Bake 40 minutes or
until chops are cooked through. Source: Stove Top ad Submitted By MEG
