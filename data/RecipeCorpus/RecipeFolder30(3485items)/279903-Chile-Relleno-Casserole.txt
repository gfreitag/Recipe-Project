Title: 279903 Chile Relleno Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
1    Doz  eggs
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt & pepper
1 ts Cumin
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1    (18 oz.) can Ortega chilies,
Whole
6    To 8 corn tortillas, cut
Into wide strips
1 1/2 lb Jack cheese
2    Tomatoes, sliced
Grease a 9 x 13 inch casserole.  Lay 1/2 chiles in pan.  Top with 1/2
tortilla strips and 1/2 cheese.  Cover with tomato slices. Repeat
layering with remaining chiles, tortillas and cheese. Combine eggs,
milk and spices. Pour over casserole.  Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees
for 30 minutes until puffy hot.  Let stand 5 to 10 minutes before
cutting. Serve with salsa. Makes 6 to 8 servings.
