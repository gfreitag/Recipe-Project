Title: Chicken with Green Pepper and Cashews
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Soy sauce (light works well)
1 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Cold chicken broth
2 tb Dry white wine (or any
-liquid)
1/2 c  Cashews
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 lg Green pepper cut into 1/4
-inch strips
4    Chicken-breast halves,
-skinned, bones, cut into
-1/2 inch strips
Source: Creative Wok Cooking

Yield: 4 servings

Heat oil in the wok.  Add the vegetable and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
Push aside or remove to serving dish.  Stir-fry the chicken 3-4
minutes, until done.  Return the vegetable to the wok and combine
with chicken. Stir in the soysauce mixed with the cornstarch, chicken
broth, and wine. Heat and stir gently until the sauce is thickened
and clear.  Add the nuts and serve at once with white rice.
