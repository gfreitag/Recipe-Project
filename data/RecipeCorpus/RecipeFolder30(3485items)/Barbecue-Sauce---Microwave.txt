Title: Barbecue Sauce - Microwave
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Cider Vinegar
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1    Slice Lime Cut In Half
1/2 ts Ground Cumin
1/4 ts Chili Powder
3 ds Tabasco Sauce
Salt To Taste
1/4 c  Chopped Fresh Coriander
4 tb Unsalted Butter
1 md Onion, Chopped
1    Clove Garlic, Minced
1/2 c  Diced Celery
1 c  Water
1 c  Catsup
1    Diced Red Pepper
1/4 c  Fresh Lime Juice
Microwave butter, onion and garlic in a medium microwave safe bowl,
covered with plastic wrap, on high power for 2 minutes.  Stir in
celery and microwave covered for 2 minutes.  Stir in remaining
ingredients except coriander until blended.  Microwave covered for 7
minutes, stirring halfway through cooking.  Puree sauce in a blender
bowl or food processor. Transfer to bowl and stir in all but 1 tb
coriander.  Garnish with remaining coriander.  Serve warm or at room
temperature.  Makes 2-1/2 cups. 81 calories per 1/4 cup.  From: Syd's
Cookbook.
