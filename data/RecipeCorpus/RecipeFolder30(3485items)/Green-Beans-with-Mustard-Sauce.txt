Title: Green Beans with Mustard Sauce
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Lemon juice
1    Can French cut green beans
1/3 c  Butter
2 ts Prepared mustard
Cream butter; add remaining ingredients and blend. Stir into hot green
beans.
