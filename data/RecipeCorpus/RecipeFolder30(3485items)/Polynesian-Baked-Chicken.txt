Title: Polynesian Baked Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Sliced papaya
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Flour
1 ts Seasoned salt
4 lb Chicken pieces
1 c  Orange juice
2 tb Lemon juice
Melt butter in a large baking pan.  Combine flour and seasoned salt.
Dredge chicken pieces in flour mixture; arrange in baking pan, skin
side down. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes; turn and bake 20
minutes longer.

In a saucepan combine orange juice, lemon juice, sugar, cornstarch,
soy sauce and salt; cook until mixture thickens.  Gently stir in
papaya and pour mixture over chicken.  Bake 10 minutes longer.
