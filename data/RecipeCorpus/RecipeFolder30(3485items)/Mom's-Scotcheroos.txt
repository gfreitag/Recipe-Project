Title: Mom's Scotcheroos
Categories: Desserts, Snacks, Mom's
Yield: 8 servings
6 c  Rice krispies
12 oz Chocolate chips
12 oz Butterscotch chips
1 c  White sugar
1 c  White corn syrup
1 c  Peanut butter
Cook sugar and corn syrup just until bubbly.  Add peanut butter.

Remove from heat and add cereal.  Press in greased 9 x 13 pan.

Melt chips and spread on top.
