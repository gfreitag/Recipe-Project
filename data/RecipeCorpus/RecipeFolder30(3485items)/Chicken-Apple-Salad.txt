Title: Chicken Apple Salad
Categories: Poultry, Salads, 1941
Yield: 2 servings
Mayonnaise or boiled salad
-dressing
1 c  Chopped nuts
4 c  Cold cooked diced chicken
2 c  Diced apples
1 c  Diced celery
Combine chicken, apples, celery, and nuts.  Moisten with mayonnaise or
boiled dressing.  Mix lightly with 2 forks.  Serve on crisp lettuce.
12 servings.

Mrs. Ione Shields. Brooksmith, TX.
