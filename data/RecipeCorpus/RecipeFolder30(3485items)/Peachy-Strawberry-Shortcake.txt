Title: Peachy Strawberry Shortcake
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 pt Whipping cream
1    Yellow cake layer; 9"
-yellow cake mix
1 pk Strawberries; 10 oz, frozen
-thawed
1 pk Peaches; 10 oz, slices,
-frozen, thawed
Drain strawberries, reserving juice. Combine berries and peaches;
cover. Refrigerate. Beat cream until stiff. When ready to serve, cut
cake into wedges; drizzle with small amount of reserved juice. Top
with fruit mixture and whipped cream. Refrigerate leftovers.
