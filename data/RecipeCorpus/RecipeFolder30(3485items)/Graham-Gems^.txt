Title: Graham Gems^
Categories: Desserts, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
1    Egg
1 1/4 c  Milk
2 tb Melted shortening
2 c  Graham flour*
1/2 ts Salt
4 ts Baking powder
*Graham flour is whole wheat flour. Combine dry ingredients. Combine
egg, milk and shortening. combine together. Bake in hot greased
muffin tins in a moderately hot oven 20 minutes or so.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
