Title: Country Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Sandwich, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Vegetable Oil
3/4 c  Sourdough Starter
1 c  Water

2 c  Bread Flour
1 c  Whole-Wheat Flour
