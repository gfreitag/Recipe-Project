Title: Apple Butter Pie
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
2    Eggs, well beaten
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Light cream
1/2 c  Ground nutmeg
1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb All purpose flour
1/2 c  Apple butter
2 tb Melted butter
MMMMM---------------------------PASTRY--------------------------------
Unbaked
1    Pastry crust for 9 inch pie
Use pastry to line a 9 inch pie pan, fluting a HIGH edge.

In a large mixing bowl- mix sugar with flour.  Gradually stir in the
apple butter and melted butter. In separate smaller bowl -  beat eggs
with milk and cream, then turn into the apple butter mixture.

After well mixed, pour into the pastry lined pie pan.  Sprinkle top
with nutmeg.

Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 50 - 60 minutes or until a knife
inserted comes out clean.

Cool on rack.
