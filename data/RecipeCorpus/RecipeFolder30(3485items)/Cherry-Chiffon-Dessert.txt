Title: Cherry Chiffon Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Salads
Yield: 12 servings
8 oz Cool whip; reg or lite
14 oz Chunk pineapple; drained
1 c  Marshmallows, minature
21 oz Cherry pie filling; regular
-or light
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk; or
-8 oz plain yogurt
Combine ingredients in large bowl, folding together until blended.
Spoon into serving bowl.  Chill. (Works well with peach or strawberry
also) Source: Comstock label
