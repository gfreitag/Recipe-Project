Title: Frosted Date Balls
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 dozen
1 ts Vanilla extract
2/3 c  Dates; chopped
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
Sugar, powdered
1 1/4 c  Flour, all-purpose
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter (or marg.); softened
1/3 c  Sugar, powdered; sifted
1 tb Milk
Combine flour and salt.  Cream butter until smooth; add 1/3 cup
powdered sugar gradually, beating well.  Add milk and vanilla, and
stir in flour mixture.  Add dates and nuts; mix well.

Roll into 1-inch balls and place about 3 inches apart on ungreased
cookie sheets.  Bake at 300 degrees about 20 minutes or until lightly
browned. Roll date balls in powdered sugar while warm.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in the 1970s. Typos by
