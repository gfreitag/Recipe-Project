Title: BLACKBERRY BAKE
Categories: Fruit, Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Butter
5 c  (to 6 cups) fruit
3/4 c  Oatmeal
3/4 c  Brown sugar
NOTE:  I use this recipe with a lot of different fruits, but
blackberries are my favorite.

Arrange berries in greased pan.  Combine oats, sugar, flour and
cinnamon. Cut into butter, sprinkle mixture over fruit. Bake at 350
degrees for 35-40 minutes.
