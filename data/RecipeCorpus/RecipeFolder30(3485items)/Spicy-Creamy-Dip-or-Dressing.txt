Title: Spicy Creamy Dip or Dressing
Categories: Low cal/fat, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
3 ds Tabasco
1 c  Cottage cheese
2 tb Milk (or cream)
1 tb White horseradish
Combine the ingredients in a food processor, blender, or mixer and
beat until smooth.  Cover the mixture and chill.

use as a dip with steamed vegetables, such as green beans or
asparagus, or raw vegetables, such as mushroom caps, green or red
pepper strips, sliced summer squash or zucchini, cucumber or fennel.
