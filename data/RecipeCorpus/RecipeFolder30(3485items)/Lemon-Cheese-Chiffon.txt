Title: Lemon Cheese Chiffon
Categories: Desserts, Salads
Yield: 5 servings
1 c  Ice cubes
3 oz Cream cheese; softened
1 pk Jello, Lemon; 4 serv size
1 tb Sugar
3/4 c  Boiling water
Combine gelatin, sugar and boiling water in blender container. Cover
and blend at low speed until gelatin is dissolved, about 1 minute.
Add ice and stir until partially melted. Add cheese; cover and blend
at high speed until smooth, about 1 minute.  Pour into individual
glasses or a serving bowl.  Chill until set, about 30 minutes. Top
with canned pie filling or drained canned fruit, if desired.  Source:
