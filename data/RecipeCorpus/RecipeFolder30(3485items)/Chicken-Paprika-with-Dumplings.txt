Title: Chicken Paprika with Dumplings
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1    Bay leaf
1 c  Packaged biscuit mix
1/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Thyme
2 tb Chopped parsley
3/4 c  Sour cream
2 ts Paprika
2 tb Vegetable oil
4    Chicken breast halves,
-boned & skinned
Seasoned flour
1 cn VEG-ALL Mixed Vegetables
-(16 oz)
1 c  Chopped onion
2 c  Chicken broth
1. Coat chicken with seasoned flour and saute in oil in heavy skillet
over medium heat.

2. Add onion; cover and cook five minutes.

3. Drain VEG-ALL and reserve vegetables. Add liquid to chicken broth.
Boil, then simmer

4. Add bay leaf to chicken.

5. Combine biscuit mix, milk, thyme, parsley and reserved vegetables.

6. Drop batter to make 8 dumplings around edge of skillet. Cook 10
minutes; then cover and cook 10 minutes longer.

7. Place chicken and dumplings on plate.

8. Add sour cream and paprika to sauce; serve over chicken and
dumplings.
