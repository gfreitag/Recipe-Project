Title: O Henry Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1 ts Vanilla
4 c  Quick oats
1 c  Brown sugar
2/3 c  Margarine
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
3/4 c  Peanut butter
1 c  Chocolate chips (6 oz)
Mix oats, brown sugar, margarine, corn syrup, and vanilla well.
Grease a 9 by 13 pan.  Pack mixture into bottom of pan and bake at
375 for 10- 15 minutes. Melt chips and peanut butter together, spread
over baked mixture, cool, cut into squares.
