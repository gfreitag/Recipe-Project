Title: Sweet and Sour Spinach Salad
Categories: Salads, Restaurants
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM----------------FAVORITE RESTAURANTS, BON A---------------------
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1 bn Spinach, lg, stems discard
-torn into pieces
12    White mushrooms, thin sliced
3/4 c  Bacon, cooked, crumbled,
-about 1/2 lb
4    Egg yolks
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts White pepper
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Sugar
3 tb Red wine vinegar
D'Amicos, Medina, OH Beat yolks in sm bowl until light and lemon
colored. Add mustard, salt and pepper and blend well. Whisking
constantly, add oil drop by drop, then in slow steady stream, until
thickened. Stir in sugar, vinegar, and lemon juice, and mi well.
Place spinach in lg salad bowl. Add mushrooms and bacon and dressing.
Toss well. Source: Favorite Restaurant Recipes, Bon Appetit
