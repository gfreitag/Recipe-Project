Title: Mexican-Style Creamed Corn~
Categories: Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Cream Cheese (reduced
Calorie)
1/4 c  Diced green Chili Peppers
1 tb Skim Milk
10 oz Pkg Frozen Whole Kernel
-Corn
1/4 c  Chopped Green Pepper
1/4 c  Chopped sweet Red Pepper
1/4 c  Chopped Celery
In a medium saucepan combine corn, green pepper, red pepper, celery,
and 1/2 cup water. Cook about 5 minutes or till corn is tender.
Drain. Stir in cream cheese, chili peppers, milk, 1/4 teaspoon salt,
and dash pepper. Heat through.

Per serving: 94 calories, 4 g protein, 17 g carbohydrates, 3 g fat, 0
mg cholesterol, 215 mg sodium, 144 mg potassium.
