Title: Boboli Greek Pizza
Categories: Italian, Pizza
Yield: 1 servings
1    Tomato; sliced
1/2 c  Greek olives; sliced
1 ts Basil
Olive Oil
3/4 c  Tomato or Spaghetti sauce;
-yours or commercial
1/2 c  Feta cheese
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
Lightly brush bread with olive oil.  Spread a thin layer of sauce. Add
grated cheese to cover sauce. Tjop with fresh sliced tomatoes and
Greek olives. Sprinkle with Basil. Bake at 350-375 degrees for 10-15
minutes or until cheese melts.  Serve with green salad.
