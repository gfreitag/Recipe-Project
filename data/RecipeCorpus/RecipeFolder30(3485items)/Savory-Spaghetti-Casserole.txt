Title: Savory Spaghetti Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
1    Soup Can Of Water
1    Clove Garlic -- minced
1 c  Sharp Pasteurized Process
Cheese -- shredded
1/2 lb Spaghetti -- cooked and
Drained
1 lb Ground Beef
1/2 c  Chopped Onion
1/4 c  Green Pepper -- chopped
2 tb Margarine
1 cn Cream Of Mushroom Soup,
Condensed
1 cn Tomato Soup, Condensed
Cook beef, onion, and green pepper in margarine in large skillet
until meat is lightly browned and vegetables are tender; stir often
to separate meat. Add soups, water, and garlic. Heat. Blend with 1/2
cup cheese and cooked spaghetti in a 3-qt casserole dish; top with
remaining cheese. Bake at 350 F for about 30 mins, or until bubbling
and hot. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
