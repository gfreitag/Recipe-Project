Title: Oat Date Nut Bread (Abm)
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
1/4    Walnuts -- toasted
1 1/2    Instant dry milk powder
1 1/2    Sugar
3/4    Cinnamon
1 1/2    Rapid rise yeast
3/4    Salt
1    Applesauce or margarine
2    Bread flour
1/3    Rolled oats
1/3    Dates -- chopped
Combine all ingredients according to manufacturer's directions.
Process in regular, delayed, or rapid bake cycle.
