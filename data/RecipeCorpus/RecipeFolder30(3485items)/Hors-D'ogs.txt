Title: Hors D'ogs
Categories: Pets
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 ts Brewers yeast
1/4 c  Grated Swiss cheese
1/4 c  Cheddar cheese, grated
2 tb Hydrogen. Vegetable shorten.
1/2 c  Oatmeal, toasted
Combine cheeses, yeast and shortening.  Using plastic wrap, shape
mixture into a log about 1 inch thick and 8 inches long.  Roll log in
toasted oatmeal.  Refrigerate.  Slice into 1/2 pieces and serve.
