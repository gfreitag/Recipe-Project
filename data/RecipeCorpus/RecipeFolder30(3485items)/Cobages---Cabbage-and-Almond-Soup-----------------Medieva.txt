Title: Cobages - Cabbage and Almond Soup
Categories: Medieval, West europe, Soup/stew, Vegetables, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dried sweet basil
2 c  Fresh peas
1 ea Head of cabbage; shredded
1 c  Almonds; coarsley chopped
6 c  Beef broth
4 tb Honey
