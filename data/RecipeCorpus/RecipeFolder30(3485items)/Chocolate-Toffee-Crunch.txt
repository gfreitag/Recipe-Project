Title: Chocolate Toffee Crunch
Categories: Candies, Snacks
Yield: 5 cups
1 c  Brown sugar
6 oz Chocolate chips
3 c  Muesli cereal
1 c  Margarine
Preheat oven to 400.  Spread cereal in even layer on large foil lined
baking sheet.  In medium saucepan over medium high heat combine
margarine and brown sugar. Cook until mixture comes to a boil; boil 3
minutes, stirring constantly.  Pour margarine mixture over cereal in
thin stream; spread evenly to cover cereal. Bake 5 minutes. Sprinkle
chocolate pieces evenly over cereal mixture; spread evenly when
chocolate pieces begin to melt.  Chill until set; break into pieces
and serve. Store in airtight container in the fridge.  Source: Muesli
