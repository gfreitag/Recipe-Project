Title: Asparagus with Lemon Crumbs
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Bread crumbs
Twisted lemon rind
Salt & pepper
1 1/2 lb Asparagus
3 tb Butter
Juice of 1 lemon
1.  Place asparagus and butter in a large skillet.  Cover and cook
over medium high heat 10 minutes, stirring frequently.

2.  Transfer asparagus to a serving platter.

3.  Add lemon juice to skillet, then bread crumbs.

4.  Cook, stirring constantly, approx. 3 minutes, until bread crumbs
are crisp.

5.  Sprinkle crumbs over asparagus.  Garnish with twisted lemon rinds
and serve.

...from "Necessities and Temptations"
