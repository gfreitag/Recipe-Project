Title: Tandoori Chutney
Categories: Herbs/spice, Relishes, Indian
Yield: 4 servings
1 pn Green food colour (optional)
1 pn Salt
1 pn Sugar
4 fl Plain yoghurt
1 ts Fresh mint; chopped
1 ts Lemon juice (optional)
1/4 ts Garam masala
This is the chutney that you will find in every tandoori restaurant.
It is easy to make and goes well with barbecue dishes.

Mix everything together well and serve.

Recipe from Indian Restaurant Cookbook - Pat Chapman ISBN 0 86188 3780
