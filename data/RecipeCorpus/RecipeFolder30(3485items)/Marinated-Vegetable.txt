Title: Marinated Vegetable
Categories: Side dish, Salads, Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 ea - 1 Tbsp salt
1 tb MSG
(optional)
3 lb Fresh vegetables, any combo
1 1/4 c  Oil
1 tb Sugar
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 tb Garlic salt
1/2 cn Pitted ripe olives
1 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 ea - 1 Tbsp pepper
1 tb Dried dillweed
Clean and cut fresh vegetables.  Separate the broccoli and
cauliflower into florets.  Slice zucchini, mushrooms and green
pepper.  cut carrots on a diagonal and halve the cherry tomatoes.
Combine vinegar, oil, pepper, sugar, dillweed, salt, garlic salt and
MSG. Pour over vegetables. Add olives, if desired. Marinate 12 hours,
stirring occasionally. Keep in refrigerator and serve cold.
