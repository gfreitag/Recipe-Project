Title: Sonny's bread and butter pickles
Categories: Pickles, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 batch
4 1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Turmeric
1 1/2 ts Celery seed
2 tb Mustard seed
3 c  White vinegar
6 lb Cukes
1 1/2 c  Sliced onions
2 lg Garlic cloves
1/3 c  Salt
2    Trays of ice cubes OR
-crushed ice
Wash cukes thoroughly; drain.  Cut unpeeled cukes into 1/4 inch
slices. In a large bowl, combine the cuke slices, onions, garlic and
salt and let stand for 3 hours.  Drain off the liquid and remove the
garlic.

Combine the sugar, spices and vinegar and heat just to a boil.  Add
the cukes and onion slices; simmer together 10 minutes.  Pack
looseley in clean, hot pint jars, leaving 1/2 in. of headroom; remove
air bubbles. Adjust lids; process in a boiling water bath for 10
minutes.
