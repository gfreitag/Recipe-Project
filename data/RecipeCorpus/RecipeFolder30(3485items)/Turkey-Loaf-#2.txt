Title: Turkey Loaf #2
Categories: Poultry, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Green pepper, finely chopped
1 cn Tomato sauce
2 cl Garlic
2 ts Thyme
1 ts Sage
ds Cayenne pepper
1 lb Ground turkey breast
1 lg Onion, chopped
2 c  Bread crumbs
2    Egg whites, beaten
1/2 c  Skim milk
1 c  Carrots, finely chopped
Mix all together and press into a loaf pan. Bake at 350* for 60-75
minutes. Serves 8

Calories: 200 Fat Grams: 2.4 Fat: 11%
