Title: Brown Sugar Oatmeal Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Breakfast
Yield: 12 muffins
3/4 c  Packed dark brown sugar
3/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter; melted
-OR vegetable oil
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Old-fashioned oats
1 c  Whole-wheat flour
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 lg Eggs
Heat oven to 400F.  Grease muffin cups, or use foil or paper baking
cups.

Thoroughly mix oats, flours, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.

Whisk eggs and brown sugar in another bowl.  When smooth, whisk in
milk, melted butter, and vanilla.  Pour over dry ingredients.  Fold
in with rubber spatula, just until dry ingredients are moistened.

Scoop the batter into muffin cups.  Bake 15 to 25 minutes or until
springy to the touch in the center.  Turn out onto a rack to cool.

Serve warm.

from Muffins by Elizabeth Alston
