Title: Selyodska V Smetane
Categories: Russian, Seafood, Appetizers
Yield: 10 servings
1 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Onion sliced thinnly
2 lb Herring, boned and skinned,
-cut in 1/2" diagonal pieces
1/4 c  Marinade from the herring
Mix the pickling liquid and the sour cream. Stir in the herring and
serve garnmished with the onion slices. Origin: Misha Lavazhurev, St.
Petersburg-CIS, circa, 1994
