Title: Pear-Honey Bake
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
-stick), softened
1/2 ts Vanilla
1    Egg
1 c  Chopped walnuts
Sliced pears
1    16 oz can
1 c  Buttermilk baking mix
1/3 c  Milk
3 tb Honey
2 tb Butter or margarine (1/4
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9-inch pie plate; set aside.
Drain pears well; dice pears; set aside. In a bowl, measure baking
mix, milk, honey, butter, vanilla and egg. Beat mixture just until
blended. Fold diced pears and walnuts into batter. Spread batter in
pie plate. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until inserted toothpick comes
out clean. Serves 6.
