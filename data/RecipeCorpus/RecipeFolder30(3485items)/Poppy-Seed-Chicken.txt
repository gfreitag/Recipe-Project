Title: Poppy Seed Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
-diced (2 Oz.)
TOPPING:
1/2 c  Butter or Margarine
1 1/3 c  Butter flavored Crackers,
-finely crushed
2 ts Poppy Seeds
1 tb Butter or Margarine
8 oz Fresh Mushrooms, sliced
5 c  Chicken, cooked, cubed
1 cn Cream of Chicken Soup,
-undiluted (10 3/4 Oz.)
1 c  Sour Cream (8 Oz.)
1    Jar Pimiento, drained and
In a skillet, melt butter. Saute mushrooms until tender. Stir in
chicken, soup, sour cream, and pimiento; mix well. Spoon mixture into
a greased 2 quart casserole. In a small bowl, combine all topping
ingredients. Sprinkle over the chicken. Bake at 350 degrees for 20
minutes. Yield: 6 Servings

SOURCE: Ernestin Plaser, Houston, TX, Reminisce Magazine Sept/Oct 92
