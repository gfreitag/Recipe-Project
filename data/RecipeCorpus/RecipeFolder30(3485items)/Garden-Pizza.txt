Title: Garden Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetables
Yield: 1 pizza
Sprinkle of parsley
Misc. Vegetables
Shredded chedar or
-mozzarella cheese
2 pk Cresent Rolls
8 oz Cream cheese
1 c  Mayonaise
1 pk Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing
-Original flavor
Flatten rolls onto a large cookie sheet, making sure slits are
pressed together. Bake as directed on package.
Mix cream cheese, mayo and ranch dressing and spread on cooled
dough. Top with finely chopped vegetables of your choice, and cheddar
or mozzarella cheese, shredded. Refrigerate for a few hours to blend
flavor.
Vegetables: broccoli, cauliflower, onions, radishes, carrots,
peppers, tomatoes, olives.
