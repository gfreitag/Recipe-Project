Title: Myrtle's Melon Balls
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM----------------AUNT BEE'S MAYBERRY COOKBOOK---------------------
1/2 c  Sour cream or Yogurt
1/2 c  Conf. sugar or honey
1/2 c  Orange juice
2/3 c  Pecans, chopped
1    Cantaloupe
1/4    Watermelon
1    Honeydew melon
2 c  Strawberries,washed & halved
2 c  Blueberries, washed
Scoop the cantaloupe, watermelon and honeydew melon into balls. In a
large bowl, combine the fruits.  In a separate bowl combine the
remaining ingredients. Pour the dressing over the fruit, and
refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
