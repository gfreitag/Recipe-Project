Title: Quick Glazed Meat Loaf
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Microwave
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 lb Ground pork
1 sm Onion; chopped
1 tb Chili powder
2 tb Teriyaki Baste & Glaze
-- (Kikkoman)
2    Eggs; beaten
2/3 c  Teriyaki Baste & Glaze
-- (Kikkoman)
4 sl Bread; cubed
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
Combine eggs and 2/3 cup teriyaki baste & glaze in large bowl; stir in
bread cubes.  Let stand 5 minutes, or until cubes are soft.  Mix in
ground beef and pork, onion and chili powder until well blended.
Press firmly into 10-inch microwave-safe pie pan.  Microwave on High
20 minutes; rotate pan once. Brush top of meat loaf with remaining
teriyaki baste & glaze; microwave on High 5 minutes longer.  Let
stand 5 minutes before serving.
