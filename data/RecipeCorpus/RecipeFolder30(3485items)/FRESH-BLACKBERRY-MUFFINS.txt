Title: FRESH BLACKBERRY MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins, Breads, Fruit
Yield: 18 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Blackberries; *
4 ts Sugar
1/2 c  Butter; room temperature
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2 lg Eggs; room temp.
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
*Fresh or frozen thawed and drained Preheat oven to 375. Grease 18
1/2-cup muffin cups. Using electric mixer, cream 1/2 C. butter and 1
1/4 C sugar in large bowl. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after
each addition. Sift flour, baking powder and salt into small bowl.
Mix dry ingredients into butter mixture alternately with milk. Fold
in berries. Divide batter among prepared cups. Sprinkle the 4 tsp.
sugar over. Bake until tester inserted in centers comes out clean.
About 30 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Makes 18
