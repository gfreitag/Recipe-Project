Title: Orange Drop Doughnuts
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Cooking oil
2    Beaten eggs
Grated rind of 1 orange
2 1/2 c  Flour, pre-sifted
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Sugar
Sift dry ingredients, then add other ingredients.  Beat well.  Drop
from teaspoon in shortening and fry at 370 degrees.  Drain on
absorbent paper. Place a few doughnuts at a time into a bag with
sugar and shake lightly. Randy Rigg
