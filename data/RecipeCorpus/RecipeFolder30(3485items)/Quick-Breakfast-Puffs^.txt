Title: Quick Breakfast Puffs^
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Milk, scant
1 tb Melted shortening
Combine milk, eggs and shortening. Sift flour, powder and salt twice.
Add milk and beat 2 minutes. Pour into hot muffin tins and bake 20
minutes in a hot oven. Serve hot with butter. Many of the recipes in
this collection did not contain amounts or oven temperatures. I have
typed them in as they appear in the book, typos and all.
