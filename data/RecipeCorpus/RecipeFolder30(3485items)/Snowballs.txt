Title: Snowballs
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 lg Bag of vanilla wafers
2 pk Dream Whip
1 lg Package of coconut
1 c  Sugar
1    Stick of butter
2    Egg yolks
1 md Can crushed pineapple, drain
Mix together sugar, butter, egg yolks, pecans and pineapple.  Put
approximately 1 large teaspoon of mixture between two vanilla wafers.
Place these on a cookie sheet and freeze overnight.  Mix Dream Whip
and roll each cookie in the whipped mixture and roll in coconut.  Lay
on cookie sheet until forzen.  Keep in freezer in a plastic bag or
Tupperware for 2 to 4 weeks if you can keep from eating them all in
the first hour. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   05-09-95
