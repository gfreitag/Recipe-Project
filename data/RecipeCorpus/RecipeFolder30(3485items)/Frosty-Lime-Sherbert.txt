Title: Frosty Lime Sherbert
Categories: Ice cream
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
2 tb Grated lime peel
2 dr Or 3 drops lime-flavored
-gelatin
1 pk 3 oz green food coloring
1 c  Hot water
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 cn 13 oz evaporated milk
In a medium bowl, dissovle gelatin in hot water. Add sugar and stir
until dissolved. Stir in milk, evaporated milk, lime juice, peel and
food coloring. Pour into an 8-inch square dish or 2 refrigerator
trays. Freeze 2 to 3 hours, or until firm.

When completely frozen, break into chunks with wooden spoon and turn
into a chilled bowl. Beat with an electric mixer, on high, until
fluffy and smooth. Return quickly to cold dish or trays and freeze
firm. Cover with foil when frozen. This will keep several weeks.
Serve plain or with fruit. Serves 6.
