Title: Cheesy Chicken Meatballs
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Ketchup
1 ts Chopped fresh basil
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1    Beaten egg
2 tb Seasoned breadcrumbs
1 lb Ground chicken
2 tb Minced onion
2    Cloves garlic minced
2 tb Ricotta cheese
2 tb Grated Parmesan
1 oz Grated Fontina cheese
Mix all ingredients and form into 24 meatballs.  Roll in an
additional 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan, 1 ts ground fennel and 1/2 cup
seasoned bread crumbs.  Pan-fry until browned and firm. Posted by
Linda Davis
