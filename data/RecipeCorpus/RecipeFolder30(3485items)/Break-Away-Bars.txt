Title: Break Away Bars
Categories: Desserts, Snacks, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  100% Natural Cereal
3/4 c  Raisins
2 c  Miniature Marshmallows
1/2 c  Chunk Style Peanut Butter
1/4 c  Butter
Melt together first three ingredients over low heat with occasional
stirring until smooth.  Add cereal and raisins.  Mix well.  Press
mixture firmly into a greased 8 inch square pan.  Chill.  Cut into
bars.  From: Syd's Cookbook.
