Title: Pasta Primavera (#16)
Categories: Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Chicken broth
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese, plus addi-
. tional at table
x  Salt and Freshly ground
. black pepper
8 oz Large bow ties or other
. pasta of your choice
1 x  Recipe STEAMED FRESH VEGE-
. TABLES
1/2 c  Heavy Cream
1.  Cook the pasta according to the package directions.  Drain.
Return to the pot.

2. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the STEAMED FRESH VEGETABLES.
Add to the pasta.

3.  In a deep skillet, simmer the cream, broth, and cheese until
slightly thickened.  Pour over the pasta and vegetables.  Season with
salt and freshly ground black pepper.

4.  Toss over low heat until the pasta is heated through and coated in
sauce.  Serve with additionl cheese.

From "Woman's Day:  The Only 25 Recipes You'll Ever Need," by Sidney
Burstein, ISBN 0-385-41179-0, 1990.
