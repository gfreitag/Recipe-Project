Title: Spruce Beer^
Categories: Homestyle
Yield: 1 servings
4 oz Essence of spruce
1 pt Water
1/4 pt Yeast
Molasses
2 oz Hops
1/2 ga Water
8 ga Warm water
1 ga Molasses
Put hops and water in a kettle and boil for 30 minutes. Strain; add 8
gallons warm water, molasses and spruce essence. Put in a clean cask,
shake all well together and add yeast. Let stand and work for 6-7
days, or less if the weather is warm. When drawn off, add 1 teaspoon
molasses to each bottle. Many of the recipes in this collection did
not contain amounts or oven temperatures. I have typed them in as
they appear in the book, typos and all.
