Title: CHOP SUEY COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 20 servings
3/4 c  Shortning
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Milk
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
6 tb Milk
1 c  Coconut
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Candied green cherries, chop
1/2 c  Candied red cherries, choppd
Cream sugar and shortning; add eggs. Dissolve baking soda in milk. Add
flour and milk alternately to mixture and blend well. Add other
ingredients, blending thoroughly. Drop by tspfull onto greased cookie
sheets. Bake at 350 degrees until slightly brown. Makes 4 to 5 dozen
cookies.
