Title: Swedish Applesauce Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Cocoa
1 c  Nuts
1/2 c  Margarine
1 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Applesauce
1 ts Soda
Cream margarine, sugar, and flour.  With other dry ingredients, add
applesauce and nuts.  Bake at 350 degrees in a 8 x 12 inch glass cake
dish for 35 minutes. Randy Rigg
