Title: American Farm Style Sausage (Predika - 1)
Categories: Breakfast, Homemade, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Ground mace
1 ts Ground sage
1/2 c  Fine, dry bread crumbs
1 c  Water
3 lb Medium ground pork butt
2 lb Medium ground veal
1 tb Salt
1 tb Pepper
1 ts Ground Nutmeg
Veal

FROM :    Clarence Fontish

Mix all ingredients together and stuff into sheep casing or make into
patties. Source: The Sausage Making Cookbook, by Jerry Predika
