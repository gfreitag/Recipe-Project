Title: Cheddar Apple Breakfast Lasagne
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 6 servings
2 1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded &
-divided
20 oz Apple pie filling
1 c  Granola with raisins
1 c  Sour cream
1/3 c  Brown sugar
2 pk French toast; 9 oz frozen
1/2 lb Ham; sliced
In small bowl blend sour cream and brown sugar; chill. Place 6 french
toast slices in bottom of greased 9 x 13 baking pan. Layer ham, 2
cups of the cheese and remaining slices of french toast. Spread apple
pie filling over top; sprinkle with granola.  Bake in 350 oven for 25
minutes. Top with remaining cheddar cheese and bake about 5 minutes
more. Serve with sour cream mixture.  Source: Sargento label.
