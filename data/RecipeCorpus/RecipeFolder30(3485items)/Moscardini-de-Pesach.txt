Title: Moscardini de Pesach
Categories: Italian, Cake/cookie
Yield: 30 servings
Matzo meal
1    Grated rind of orange
1/2 ts Almond extract
1    Egg, lightly beaten
1    Egg yolk, lightly beaten
1 1/2 c  Coarsely ground, roasted
-almonds
1 c  Sugar
pn Salt
1/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa
Preheat oven to 350'F. Combine almonds, sugar, salt, cocoa, 1 cup
matzo meal, orange rind, almonds extract, egg and egg yolk in a small
bowl.

Drop dough by rounded teaspoonfuls on oiled baking sheet dusted with
matzo meal. Shape each cookie into a oval, flatten with bakc of fork.

Bake for 10 minutes. Cool on rack.

Makes 2-1/2 dozen 3" cookies.
