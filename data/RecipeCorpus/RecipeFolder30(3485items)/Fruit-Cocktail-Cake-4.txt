Title: Fruit Cocktail Cake 4
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Flour
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
2 c  Bisquick
2/3 c  Sugar
1    Egg; well beaten
16 oz Fruit cocktail; undrained
Stir bisquick and sugar together.  Combine egg and fruit cocktail.
Add to dry mixture and stir to make soft dough. Turn into greased 9
x 9 pan. Blend remaining ingredients and sprinkle over the top. Bake
at 325 for 50 to 55 minutes.  Serve with whipped topping. Source:
Times Union newspaper Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   05-17-95
