Title: Souperior Italian Meat Loaf
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 c  Fresh bread crumbs
2    Eggs
3/4 c  Water
1/3 c  Ketchup
1 pk Lipton Recipe Secrets
-Italian Herb with Tomato
-Recipe Soup Mix
1 lb Ground beef
Preheat oven to 350'F.

In large bowl, combine all ingredients. In large baking pan, shape
into loaf. Bake 1 hour or until done. Makes about 8 servings.

Also terrific with Lipton Recipe Secrets Onion, Beefy Onion,
Onion-Mushroom or Beefy Mushroom Recipe Soup Mix
