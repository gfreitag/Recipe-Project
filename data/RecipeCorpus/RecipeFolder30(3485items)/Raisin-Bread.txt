Title: Raisin Bread
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Ground Cinnamon
1 ts Nutmeg -- grated
3/4 c  Seedless Raisins
3/4 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 c  Applesauce
1/4 c  Butter -- melted
1    Egg -- beaten
1/2 c  Granulated Sugar
1/4 c  Brown Sugar -- packed
2 c  All-Purpose Flour
2 ts Baking Powder
Preheat oven to 350 F. Combine applesauce, butter, egg, and sugars in
a bowl. Blend well. Sift together other dry ingredients (including
cinnamon and nutmeg) and stir into applesauce mixture until smooth.
Add raisins and chopped nuts. Turn into a well-greased 9 x 5 x 3-inch
loaf pan. Bake for 1 hr, or until the edges shrink from the sides of
the pan and the bread is nicely browned. Serve plain or toasted, with
butter or cream cheese. Yield: 1 loaf.
