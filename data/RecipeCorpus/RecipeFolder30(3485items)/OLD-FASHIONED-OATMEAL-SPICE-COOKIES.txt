Title: OLD FASHIONED OATMEAL SPICE COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 batch  1 pk spice cake mix 2 c uncooked rolled oats 2 eggs 3/4 c cooking oil
1/2 c milk 2 c raisins 1 c chopped nuts 1/4 c brown sugar

Combine all ingredients.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie
sheets.  Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes.
*****************************************************************

I've seen other recipes that are similar and these recipes said that
you could switch the type of cake mix.  I've only made these using
the spice cake mix and I think the cookies are awesome. Even my
husband, who claims not to like oatmeal cookies, scarfed a bunch of
these.
