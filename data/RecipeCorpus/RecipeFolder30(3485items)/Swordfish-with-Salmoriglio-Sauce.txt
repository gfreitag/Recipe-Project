Title: Swordfish with Salmoriglio Sauce
Categories: Fish, Bbq/grill, Hot/spicy
Yield: 6 servings
2 lg Garlic cloves, fine chopped
1 tb Oregano, dried
6    Swordfish fillets (6 oz ea.)
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Lemon juice, fresh
2 tb Hot water
6 tb Parsley, fresh, chopped
Preheat broiler or BBQ. Whisk olive oil in top of double boiler over
simmering water until heated through. Gradually add the lemon juice,
then 2 Tbsp hot water.  Add chopped parsley, garlic and oregano; cook
sauce 5 minutes to allow flavors to blend, whisk frequently. Season
sauce with salt & pepper to taste, remove from heat.

Lighty brush fish on both sides with sauce. Season with salt & pepper.
Grill or broil until just cooked through, about 5 minutes per side.
Arrainge fish on platter and spoon remaining sauce over.
