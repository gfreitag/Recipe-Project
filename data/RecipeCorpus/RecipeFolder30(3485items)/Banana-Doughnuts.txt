Title: Banana Doughnuts
Categories: Homemade, Pastry
Yield: 24 servings
1 md Banana, Ripe, mashed
1/8 ts Ground Nutmeg
1/8 ts Ground Cinnamon (Or Mace
-And Cloves)
1/2 ts Vanilla Extract
Vegetable Shortening, for
Frying
Confectioner's Sugar
2 c  All-Purpose Flour
2 ts Baking Powder
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Vegetable Shortening
(Crisco)
1/3 c  Granulated Sugar
1    Egg
1/4 c  Buttermilk
Sift together dry ingredients. Beat shortening until creamy, then add
sugar and beat until light. Add egg and beat in well. Then add
buttermilk, banana, spices, and vanilla, and blend. Put onto a
floured board, knead lightly, and roll to 1/2-inch thickness. Cut
dough with a doughnut cutter. Fry in hot deep oil until nicely
browned, drain on paper towels, and dust with confectioners' sugar.

Yield: 2 dozen
