Title: Brown Rice Vegetarian Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1 c  Mozzarella cheese, shredded,
-divided
Vegetable cooking spray
3 c  Cooked Mahatma Brown Rice,
-cooled
2    Egg whites, beaten
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1/4 c  Ripe olives
1/4 c  Chopped red peppers
2/3 c  Pizza sauce
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Black pepper
1 tb Parmesan cheese
Combine cooled rice, egg whites, and 1/3 cup mozzerella cheese in a
large bowl. Press into 12-inch pizza pan sprayed with vegetable
cooking spray. Bake at 400~ for 5 minutes.

Combine pizza sauce, garlic powder, and black pepper in a small bowl;
spread over prepared rice crust. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Layer
1/3 cup mozzerella cheese, mushrooms, olives, and red pepper   . Top
with remaining cheese. Bake at 400~ for 10 minutes. Garnish with
green peppers.
