Title: Fruited Honey Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Homemade
Yield: 4 servings
1    Fresh Apple, Unpeeled,
-sliced OR a handful of
-dried apples
Handful Of Seedless Raisins
Handful Of Dried Apricots
Handful Of Pitted Prunes
2 tb Honey
1    3-Lb Chicken, cut up for
-frying
Salt And Pepper
3/4 c  Apple Cider Or Juice
1/2 c  Orange Juice
1/4 c  Lemon Juice
1    Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
Salt and pepper the chicken pieces, then marinate chicken in fruit
juices and onion for 1 hour or so (reserve marinade). Preheat oven to
350 F. Brown both sides of all chicken pieces in oil. Place chicken
in a covered casserole and cover with marinating juices plus the
remaining ingredients. Bake for 45 mins, basting occasionally with
marinade and pan juices.

Yield: 4 to 5 servings.
