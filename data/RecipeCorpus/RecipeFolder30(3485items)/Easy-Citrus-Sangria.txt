Title: Easy Citrus Sangria
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 3 1/2 quarts
4    Lemons; grated rind of
-and juice of
3/4 c  Sugar; to 1 cup
33 3/4 oz Club soda; chilled
50 2/3 oz Burgundy; or other dry red
-wine, chilled
1 1/2 c  Brandy; or bourbon
6 oz Orange juice; thawed and
-undiluted
Combine first 6 ingredients in a large pitcher, stirring to dissolve
sugar. Add club soda before serving.  Serve over ice.
