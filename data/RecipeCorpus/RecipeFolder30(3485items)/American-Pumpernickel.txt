Title: American Pumpernickel
Categories: Breadmaker, Sandwich, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Honey
1 1/2 ts Caraway Seed
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Caramel Coloring
1 1/4 c  Water
2 1/2 ts Active Dry Yeast
2 1/4 c  Bread Flour
1 c  Rye Flour
1/4 c  Cornmeal
3 tb Powdered Milk
1 1/2 tb Vegetable Oil
Add all ingredients in the order suggested by your ABM manual and
process on the basic bread cycle according to the manufacturer's
directions.
