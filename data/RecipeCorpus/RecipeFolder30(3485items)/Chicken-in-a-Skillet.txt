Title: Chicken in a Skillet
Categories: Poultry, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Whole Peeled Tomatoes *
1/3 c  Water
3 lb Cut Up Chicken
1    Env Onion Soup Mix
* 14-1/2 oz can, drained and chopped (RESERVE LIQUID) In 3 quart
casserole heat chicken, uncovered, on HIGH 12 mins, re- arranging
once; drain. Add tomatoes, then onion soup mix blended with water and
reserved liquid. Heat, covered, 14 mins or until chicken is tender,
rearranging chicken once. Let stand covered 5 mins.
