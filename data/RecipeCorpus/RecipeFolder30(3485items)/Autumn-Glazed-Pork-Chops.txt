Title: Autumn Glazed Pork Chops
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Whole cranberry sauce
2 tb Honey
2 tb Frozen orange juice conc.
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
Posted by Stu Hopper
----------------------------
4 ea Boneless chops, 3/4" thick
1/4 ts Freshly ground pepper
1/4 c  Apple juice or cider
Heat non stick skillet over medium-high heat.  Sprinkle both sides of
chop with pepper.  Brown each side.  Add apple juice or cider.  Cover
tightly and cook over low heat 5-6 minutes until chops are just done.
Drain. Combine remaining ingredients in small bowl.  Pour over chops.
Cook 1-2 minutes until heated through.
