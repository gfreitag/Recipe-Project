Title: Cracker'n Vegetable Lasagna
Categories: Snacks, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
32    Saltine crackers
4 oz Cheddar cheese; lo-fat, lo-
-cal, shredded.
2 c  Skim milk
1 c  Egg beaters
1 md Red pepper; cut into strips
1 sm Onion; sliced
1 sm Zuchini; sliced
1/2 ts Oregano; dried, leaves
1 tb Margarine
In lge. skillet, over med. heat, cook and stir red pepper, onion,
zuchini and oregano in margarine until tender. In lightly greased
9x9x2 inch baking pan, layer half each of the crackers, vegetable
mixture and cheese.  Repeat layers, ending with cheese.

In bowl, beat milk and egg product; evenly pour over cheese layer.
Bake at 350 F. for 30 to 35 minutes or until knife inserted in center
comes out clean.

Nutrition info. per serving:  195 calories, 418mg sodium, 15mg
cholesterol, 7g total fat, 2g saturated fat.
