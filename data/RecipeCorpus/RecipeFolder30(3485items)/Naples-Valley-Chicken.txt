Title: Naples Valley Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
Garlic Powder
1 1/2 ts Oregano
1 ts Salt
Pepper
4 tb Flour
1/2 c  Water
4 tb Margarine
6    Boneless Chicken Breasts
1/2 lb Fresh Mushrooms, Sliced
1/2 c  Onion, Chopped
1 c  Dry White Wine
1 c  Chicken Bouillon
1 tb Parsley, Chopped
Brown chicken breasts in melted margarine, remove and set aside. Saute
mushrooms & onions in the same pan until tender. Return chicken to
pan. Add wine, bouillon, parsley, garlic powder, oregano, salt &
pepper. cover chicken and simmer 40 minutes.  Remove chicken. Blend
flour & water into pan juices.  Stir until thickened. Return chicken
to pan. Heat through or refrigerate until serving time, then reheat.
From "Applehood & Mother Pie"
