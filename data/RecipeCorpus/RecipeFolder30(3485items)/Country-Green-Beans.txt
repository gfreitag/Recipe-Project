Title: Country Green Beans
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Water
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Fresh green beans --
Trimmed
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
1/4 c  Fully cooked ham -- chopped
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
This deliciously different way to dress up green beans is sure to
become a family favorite at your house, too. The garlic, chopped ham
and onion blend so well with the beans. It's a beautiful and tasty
side dish that has real country appeal.

In a saucepan, combine all ingredients. Cover and simmer for 15-20
minutes or until beans are tender.

Yield:  4 servings
