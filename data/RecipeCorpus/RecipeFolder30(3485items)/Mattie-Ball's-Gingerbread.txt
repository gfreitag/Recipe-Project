Title: Mattie Ball's Gingerbread
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1    Egg
1/2 c  Molasses
1 1/4 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Baking Soda
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Crisco Or Lard
2 c  Flour
2 ts Ginger
1/3 ts Ground Cloves
Cream together the sugar and shortening. Add the flour and spices, and
crumble together. Reserve 1/2 cup for the topping.

Combine the egg, molasses, buttermilk, and baking soda. Add to the
flour mixture and pour into a greased and floured 9 X 13-inch pan.
Sprinkle the 1/2 cup of dry mixture on top of the batter.

Bake for 30-40 mins in a 350 F oven.

Serve with whipped cream or applesauce.
