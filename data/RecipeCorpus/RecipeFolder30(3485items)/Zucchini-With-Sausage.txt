Title: Zucchini With Sausage
Categories: Sausage, Vegetables, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
Salt And Freshly Ground
-Pepper to taste
1 c  Parmesan Cheese, freshly
-grated
1/4 c  Bread Crumbs
2 tb Butter -- softened
2 1/2 lb Zucchini
1 lb Sausage Meat
1 lg Onion -- diced
1    Green Pepper -- diced
6    To 7 Tomatoes, peeled and
-sliced 1/4"
In a saucepan, blanch the zucchini in boiling salted water to cover
for 10 mins. Drain. Cut the zucchini into 1/2" slices.

In a skillet, saute the sausage meat (breaking it into pieces wuth a
fork) with the onion and green pepper until the onion is golden.
Drain.

Layer the zucchini, sausage mixture tomatoes sprinkled with salt and
pepper, and Parmesan cheese in a lightly oiled 2-qt baking dish,
ending with a layer of tomatoes. Sprinkle the tomatoes with the bread
crumbs and more cheese. Add salt and pepper to taste, dot the crumbs
with butter, and bake the dish in a preheated 350 F. oven for 1 hr,
or until top is golden.

Serves 6-8.
