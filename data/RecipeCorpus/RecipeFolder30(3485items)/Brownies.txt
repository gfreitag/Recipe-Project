Title: Brownies
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts
Yield: 16 servings
1/8 ts Salt
2    Eggs -- beaten
2/3 c  Walnut Meats -- chopped
1 ts Vanilla Extract
1/2 ts Baking Soda
2    1-Oz Unsweetened Chocolate
Squares
2/3 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flour -- sifted
Preheat oven to 375 F. Melt chocolate and butter in top of a double
boiler and cool slightly. Then add all other ingredients in the given
order. Mix well and pour into a greased 8-inch- square pan and bake
for 25 mins or until firm whentouched in the cneter.

Yield: 16 brownies.
