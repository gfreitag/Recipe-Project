Title: Mainated Chicken Italiano
Categories: Poultry, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
3 lb Chicken pieces
1/2 c  Wish-Bone Italian Dressing
In shallow dish, pour dressing over chicken. Cover and marinate in
refrigerator, turning occasionally, 3 hours or overnight. Remove
chicken, reserving marinade. Grill or broil, turning and basting
frequently with reserved marinade, until done. Also terrific with
Wish-Bone Robusto Italian or Lite Italian Dressing.
