Title: Chocolate Punch
Categories: Beverages, Homestyle
Yield: 12 servings
1 qt Vanilla Ice Cream
1 qt Club Soda
1/2 pt Heavy Cream, whipped
Ground Cinnamon
4    1-Oz Sq Semisweet Chocolate
1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  Hot Water
2 qt Milk
1 1/2 ts Vanilla Extract
In a large saucepan combine the chocolate and sugar with the hot
water. Bring to a boil, stirring for 2 mins. Add milk, and continue
heating. When hot, beat in vanilla with a rotary egg beater or whisk.
Remove from heat. Chill, then pour into a punch bowl over ice cream.
For sparkle, add club soda. Top with whipped cream and dust with
cinnamon.

Yield: 12 servings.
