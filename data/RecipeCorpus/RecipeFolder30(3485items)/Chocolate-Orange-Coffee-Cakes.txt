Title: Chocolate Orange Coffee Cakes
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
3    Eggs
1 ts Orange extract; or 2 tsp
-grated orange peel
1 pk Fudge marble cake mix
1 c  Water
1/3 c  Oil
MMMMM----------------------STREUSEL TOPPING---------------------------
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Nuts; finely chopped
Heat oven to 350.  Grease and flour 2 round 9" pans. Beat cake mix,
water, oil and eggs in large bowl on low speed 30 seconds. Beat on
medium speed 2 minutes. Pour 1 1/2 cups batter into small bowl; blend
in marbling mixture on low speed.  Stir orange extract into yellow
batter. Divide yellow batter between pans. Drop chocolate batter
randomly in 3 or 4 mounds onto yellow batter in each pan.  Cut
through batter with knife in S shaped curves in one continuous
motion. Turn pan 1/4 turn and repeat. Sprinkle with topping. Bake 28
to 32 minutes.  Serve warm.  Source: Betty Crocker box Submitted By
