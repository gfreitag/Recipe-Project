Title: Casserole Chicken and Dressing
Categories: Poultry, Casseroles
Yield: 8 servings
1    Can evaporated milk
1    Pepperidge Farm dressing
1 c  Chicken broth
1    Cut up chicken, boiled
1    Can golden mushroom soup
1    Stick of butter
1    Can cream of chicken soup
Debone chicken.  Place chicken in casserole dish.  Mix soups and
milk. Pour over chicken.  Mix dressing, butter, and broth.  Pour over
top. Brown in oven at 350F for 30 minutes.

From: Lillian Chavis, North, S.C.
