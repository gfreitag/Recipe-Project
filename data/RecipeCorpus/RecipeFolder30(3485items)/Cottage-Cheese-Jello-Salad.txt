Title: Cottage Cheese Jello Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1 ct Small curd cottage cheese
1 lg Carton of Cool Whip
1 pk Jello
2 tb Sugar
1 sm Can of crushed pineapple
Mix Jello, sugar and pineapple and cook until it comes to a boil.
Cool. Add cottage cheese and fold in Cool Whip.  Refrigerate. Randy
