Title: Ham and Potato Patties^
Categories: Meats, Potato, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
Little pepper
Flour
Bacon fat or lard
1 1/2 c  Leftover mashed potatoes
1    Egg
1 c  Cooked ham, chopped
1 tb Onion
Mix together potatoes and egg with a fork. Add ham, onion and pepper.
Shape into flat patties. Dip in a little four and fry in bacon fat or
lard. Serve hot. Many of the recipes in this collection did not
contain amounts or oven temperatures. I have typed them in as they
appear in the book, typos and all.
