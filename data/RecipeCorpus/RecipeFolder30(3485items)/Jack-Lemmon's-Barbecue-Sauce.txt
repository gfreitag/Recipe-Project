Title: Jack Lemmon's Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 serving
4 tb Lemon Juice
1    Bay Leaf
4 tb Sharp salad dressing
3/4 c  MAPLE SYRUP
1 c  Butter
Chili Powder
3/4 qt Catsup
1 ts English mustard
1 ts Freshly ground black
-pepper
1 tb Worcesteshire sauce
1    Clv  Garlic
1/2 md Onion
Cooking Time:
Preparation Time: 0:00

Instructions:

Mix, bring to a boil and simmer one hour. Remove onion and bay leaf.
