Title: Hide 'N' Seek Coffee Cake
Categories: Breads, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 8" cake
MMMMM---------------------------BATTER--------------------------------
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
1    Egg
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Butter; melted
1 tb Cinnamon
1/2 c  California dried figs
-- (chopped fine)
1/4 c  Chopped walnuts
BATTER: Cream shortening, add sugar and vanilla and cream thoroughly.
Add beaten egg and mix thoroughly.  Sift dry ingredients, and add
alternately with milk.  Spread half the batter in greased 8-inch cake
pan.  Cover with fig filling and add remaining batter.  BAke 45
minutes at 350 F.

FILLING: Rinse figs with hot water, then chop fine.  Blend with
remaining ingredients for coffee-cake filling.

Source: 48 Family Favorites with California Figs Reprinted with the
permission of The California Fig Advisory Board Electronic format
courtesy of Karen Mintzias
