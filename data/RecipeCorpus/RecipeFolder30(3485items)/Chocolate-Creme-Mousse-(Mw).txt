Title: Chocolate Creme Mousse (Mw)
Categories: Desserts, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1    7 oz jar marshmallow creme
2 c  Cool whip -- thawed
3    Squares semisweet chocolate
1 pk 3 oz pkg philadelphia cream
Cheese -- softened
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Shaved chocolate
Fresh raspberries
Microwave chocolate in large micro-safe bowl on HIGH 45-60 secs or
until almost melted, stirring halfway thru heating time. Remove from
oven; stir until completely melted. Add cream cheese and vanilla; mix
at medium speed with electric mixer until well blended. Blend in
marshmallow creme; fold in whipped topping. Pour into 1-quart serving
bowl or individual dishes. Refrigerate 2 hrs. Garnish with fresh
raspberries and shaved chocolate. Makes 4-6 servings.

Variation: Substitute 1 cup whipping cream, whipped, for cool whip.

Source: minature marshmallow bag
