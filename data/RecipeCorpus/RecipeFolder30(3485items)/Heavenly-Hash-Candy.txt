Title: Heavenly Hash Candy
Categories: Candies
Yield: 3 servings
1    12 oz. Hershey bar
1 cn Evaporated milk
1    Bag miniature marshmallows
2 c  Chopped nuts
Dissolve milk and chocolate in top of a double boiler.  Put
marshmallows and nuts in large bowl that is lightly buttered.  Pour
chocolate syrup over the nuts and marshmallows; toss together. Spread
in 13 x 9 inch buttered pan.  Let set overnight before cutting into
squares. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   05-13-95
