Title: SEAFOOD PASTA
Categories: Pasta, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 2 servings
2    Cloves garlic, pressed
1/4 ts Freshly ground pepper
1 ds Salt
2 tb Fresh dill or 1 t. dillweed
1    8 oz package thin spaghetti
1/2 lb Bay scallops
1 oz Smoked salmon, cut into 1/2
-inch pieces
3/4 c  Heavy or whipping cream
Cook spaghetti.

In a 1 1/2 quart saucepan, combine all other ingredients except dill.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until slightly thickened.
(about 6 minutes)  Drain spaghetti, toss with sauce and add dill.
