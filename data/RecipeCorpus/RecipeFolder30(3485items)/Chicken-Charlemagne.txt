Title: Chicken Charlemagne
Categories: Poultry, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
8    Green onions, sliced
1    Bay leaf
1/2 c  Sliced celery
2 tb Flour
1    Carrot, diagonal sliced
1/4 c  Water
1    Broiler, cut up & skinned
1 c  Light beer
Salt & pepper
14 oz Can tomatoes
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 ts Thyme
Sprinkle chicken with salt & pepper, brown in butter.  Remove
chicken, add onions, celery and carrots to skillet; saute.  Add beer
and next 3 ingredients, bring to boil. Return chicken to skillet,
cover and simmer 45 minutes. Place chicken on serving platter.
Dissolve flour in water, thicken skillet liquid over medium heat.
Pour sauce over chicken, serve.
