Title: Vegetable Salad
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Celery -- diced
1 sm Can water chestnuts
1    8 oz bottle Caesar dressing
1 pk 10 oz frozen broccoli
1 pk 10 oz frozen peas
1 sm Head cauliflower
1 bn Green onions
Cook peas and broccoli 2 or 3 minutes in salted water; drain. Cool;
add diced cauliflower, diced onions, celery and water chestnuts. Toss
with an 8-ounce bottle creamy Caesar dressing. This may be prepared
ahead and put dressing on as needed.
