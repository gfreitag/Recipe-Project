Title: Blue Cheese Garlic Bread
Categories: Breads, Side dish
Yield: 1 loaf
1/2    Garlic clove, minced
1    Loaf French bread
1/4 c  Blue cheese
1/4 c  Margarine
Heat oven to 400 F.  Mix together cheese and margarine; blend well.  A
garlic.  Slice bread diagonally, not quite through bottom crust.
Spre one side with mixture.  Heat in oven 10 to 15 minutes or until
hot and crusty. Serve with cottage cheese and a tossed green salad
for a quic and economical meal.

Recipe from "The Smart Shopper's Cookbook"
