Title: Chicken in the Skillet
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Green Beans
1 sm Onion
1    Bay Leaf
1 cn Cream Of Mushroom Soup
1 sm Chicken Or 4 Chicken Breasts
1 c  Rice
Drain juice from can of green beans (if not enough to make 1 cup add
water and d mix it with cream of mushrooom soup. Brown Chicken in in
a little butter. After chicken is brown, add green bean juice and
mushroom soup. Simmer about 25 min. or until chicken is tender.  Add
rice.  Cook 15 min. Add Green beans abd bay leaf.  Cook until beans
are done (total - about 45 min. to 1 hr.) from Bumgarner Family
Recipes Submitted by Karen Watts Groat
