Title: Baked Poppy Seed Chicken
Categories: Jewish, Poultry, Herbs/spice, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 cl Garlic, pressed
1 ts Fresh ginger, minced
1 ts Vinegar
3 lb Frying chicken pieces
1/4 c  Teriyaki marinade & sauce
1 tb Poppy seed
Rinse the chicken under cold water; pat dry with paper towels.  Place
the chicken, skin side up, in a shallow baking dish.  Combine the
remaining ingredients and pour over the chicken.  Bake in a 350Ã¸F
oven for 50 minutes or until the chicken is no longer pink in the
center, basting occasionally with the pan drippings.
