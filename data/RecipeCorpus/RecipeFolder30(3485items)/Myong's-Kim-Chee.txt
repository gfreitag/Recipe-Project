Title: Myong's Kim Chee
Categories: Can/preserv, Side dish, Vegetables, Pickles, Korean
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Ginger root -- sliced
1 c  Scallion -- chopped
1    Korean horseradish --
Shredded
2 c  Water -- boiled
2 c  Rice wine vinegar
3 tb Sesame seeds
1    Head bok choy -- chopped
2    Daikon -- thinly sliced
2    Carrots -- shredded
12    Cloves garlic -- thinly
Sliced
1 1/2 c  Sea salt
3/4 c  Dried crushed red pepper
Mix all dry ingredients together.  Place in a large crockery or glass
container that can be sealed airtight.  Pour liquids over them,
vinegar first.  If the liquid does not cover, add more vinegar, not
water.  Seal jar and place in refrigerator for 2-12 weeks.
