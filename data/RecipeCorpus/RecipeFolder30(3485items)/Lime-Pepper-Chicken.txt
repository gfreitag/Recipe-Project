Title: Lime Pepper Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Garlic salt
2    To 2 1/2 lbs. meaty chicken
-pieces(breasts,wings,thighs
-,legs)
1/2 ts Finely shredded lime peel
1/3 c  Lime juice
2 tb Cooking oil
1 ts Dried thyme,crushed
1 ts Cracked black pepper
Preheat the broiler. Meanwhile, for sauce, in a small bowl, stir
together lime peel, lime juice, oil, thyme, pepper and garlic salt.

Set mixture aside. Place chicken, skin side down, on a unheated rack
in a broiler pan. Brush with sauce. Broil 4 to 5" from the heat for
15 to 18 minutes or until lightly browned, brushing often with sauce.
Turn chicken. Brush with sauce. Broil 10 to 15 minutes more or until
no longer pink, brushing often with sauce.

Makes 4 servings.
