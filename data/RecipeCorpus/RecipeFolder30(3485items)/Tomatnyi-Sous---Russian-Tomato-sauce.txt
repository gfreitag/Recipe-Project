Title: Tomatnyi Sous - Russian Tomato sauce
Categories: Russian, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 2 cups
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Basil dried
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 tb Flour
1 tb Butter
1/2 ts Sugar or honey
1 tb Tomato paste
1 1/2 lb Tomato, fresh, chopped
1 ea Garlic clove crushed
1 ea Green pepper, sm, chopped
1 ea Onion lg, chopped
1 ts Salt
Saute the garlic, onion, and the green pepper in the olive oil until
soft but not browned. Stir in the tomatos, salt, pepper, basil, & the
hot sauce. Cover and simmer for 25 minutes. Dump mixture into blender
and puree for 1 minute. Melt butter in a saucepan then stir in the
flour and cook for 3-5 minutes. Stir in the puree and add the tomato
paste & sugar. Simmer for 10 more minutes. This may be refrigerated
for 4 days or frozen. It is used in many Russian recipes. Origin:
Chef Petra Ekanobanov, Arbot Gate Pub, Moscow-CIS, circa 1994
