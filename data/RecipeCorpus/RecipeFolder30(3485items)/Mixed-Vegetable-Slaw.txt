Title: Mixed Vegetable Slaw
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Grated Lemon Peel
1/4 ts Tabasco Sauce
4 c  Shredded Cabbage
1 c  Shredded Carrots
1    Red Or Yellow Bell Pepper *
1/3 c  Thinly Sliced Radishes
1 c  Low Fat Yogurt
2 tb Miracle Whip
2 tb Grated Onion
1 tb Dijon Mustard
1/2 ts Dried Basil, Crumbled
1/2 ts Salt
* Red or yellow bell pepper should be seeded and julienned.
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Mix yogurt, Miracle Whip, onion, mustard, basil, salt, lemon peel
and Tabasco sauce in a large bowl.  Add cabbage, carrots, bell
peppers and radishes; toss to coat.  Refrigerate covered to allow
flavors to blend, at least 1 hour.  93 calories per serving.  From
Syd's Cookbook.
