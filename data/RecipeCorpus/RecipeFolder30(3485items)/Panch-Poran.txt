Title: Panch Poran
Categories: Herbs/spice, ', Indian, Balti
Yield: 1 recipe
Mustard Seeds
Wild onion Seeds
White Cummin seeds
Fennel seeds
Fenugreek Seeds
This is a Bengali mixture of 5 spices. There are several possible
combinations, but this is my favourite.

Simply mix together equal parts (a teaspoon of each is plenty).

Taken from The Balti Curry Cookbook - Pat Chapman ISBN 0 7499 1342 8
