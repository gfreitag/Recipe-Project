Title: Eggless Applesauce Cake
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 9 x 13
2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Nutmeats
1 c  Raisins
1 1/2 c  Applesauce
1/2 c  Salad oil
2 c  Flour
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
3 tb Cocoa
1 ts Cornstarch
1 c  Sugar
Sift dry ingredients, add raisins and nuts, then applesauce and oil.
Beat well.  Bake in greased 9 x 13 pan in 325 degree F. oven.  Good
without nuts, with a few extra raisins, or try a cup of Toasted
Oatmeal.

From "This For That:  A Treasury of Savvy Substitutions for the
Creative Cook," by Meryl Nelson, ISBN 0-88247-847-6.
