Title: Orange Cream Sherbet
Categories: Desserts, Homestyle
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Heavy Cream
1    Lemon, Juice Of
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Gelatin, Unflavored
2 tb Cold Water
1 c  Orange Juice
1/2 c  Milk
In a small saucepan soak gelatin in cold water. Stir over medium heat
until completely dissolved. Let cool. Combine all other ingredients
and add gelatin. Pour into an ice tray, cover with wax paper, and put
in freezer. When frozen, put in a mixing bowl and beat until light.
Return to ice tray and refreeze.

Yield: 3 to 4 servings.
