Title: Horn and Hardart's Baked Macaroni and Cheese
Categories: Pasta, Casseroles, Restaurants
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM--------------------------NY CKBK-------------------------------
1 ds Cayenne
2 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2 lb Elbow macaroni, cooked
1/2 c  Canned tomatoes, chpd, drain
1 ts Sugar
1 tb Butter
1 tb All purp flour
3 c  Milk
1 ts Salt
1 ds White pepper
Automat favorite. Reheats well. Preheat oven 350F. Grease 1 1/2qt
baking dish. Melt butter in saucepan; whisk in flour; add milk, slat,
peppers. Stir constantly until mixture thickens and is smooth, 8-10
mins. Add hceese; cook, stirring until melted. Remove from heat. In
mixing bowl, combine macaroni and sauce. Stir in tomatoes and sugar.
Transfer to baking dish. Bake until surface browns, 30-40 mins.
