Title: Hearty Burgundy Shortribs
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Italian Herbs
1 c  Burgundy
6 lg Short Ribs Of Beef
1 tb Seasoned Salt
1 ts Garlic Powder
Place ribs, fat side up, in a shallow roasting pan. Combine remaining
ingredients. Pour over ribs. Roast uncovered in a 325 F oven 2 1/2
hours. Makes 6 servings.
