Title: Breakfast in the Car
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Fresh fruit of choice
1 tb Chopped walnuts
1    Pita pocket
1 tb Reduced fat cream cheese
Stuff pita pocket with cream cheese, fruits and nuts. Place on a paper
napkin and microwave on high power for 20 seconds. Pick up and eat on
the go. per serving 187 cal 7 g fat
