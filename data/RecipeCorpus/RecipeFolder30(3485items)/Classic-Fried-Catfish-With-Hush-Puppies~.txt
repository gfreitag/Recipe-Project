Title: Classic Fried Catfish With Hush Puppies~
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Breads
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Garlic powder
4    Catfish fillets
Oil
3/4 c  Yellow cornmeal
1/4 c  Flour
Salt -- to taste
1 ts Cayenne pepper
MMMMM------------------------HUSH PUPPIES-----------------------------
1/2    Green pepper -- chopped OR
1    Jalapeno pepper -- chopped
1 c  Milk
Vegetable oil
1 c  Self-rising cornmeal
1/2 c  Self-rising flour
1 tb Sugar
1    Egg
1 md Onion -- chopped
Combine first 5 ingredients. Coat catfish with mixture, shaking off
excess. Fill deep pot half full with oil. Heat to 350 degrees. Add
catfish in single layer, and fry until golden brown, about 5 - 6
minutes. Remove and drain on paper towels. Serve with Hush Puppies.
Serves 4.

HUSH PUPPIES: Combine first 7 ingredients. Drop by tablespoons into
hot oil (350 defrees). Fry until golden brown on all sides. Drain on
paper towels.

Per serving:  Calories 515.6; fat 9.6g; cholesterol 154mg;
carbohydrates 68.7g; dietary fiber 3.4g; protein 37.4g; sodium 832mg.
Good source vitamin A.
