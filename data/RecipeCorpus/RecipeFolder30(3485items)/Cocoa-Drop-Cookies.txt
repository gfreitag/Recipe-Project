Title: Cocoa Drop Cookies
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 5 dozen
1 3/4 c  Flour, all-purpose
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Cocoa
1 c  Pecans; chopped (or walnuts)
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg
3/4 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Vanilla extract
Cream shortening; gradually add sugar, beating until light and
fluffy. Add egg, beating well.  Stir in buttermilk and vanilla
extract.

Combine flour, soda, salt, and cocoa; add to creamed mixture, beating
well. Stir in pecans.  Chill dough 1 hour.

Drop dough by teaspoonfuls, 2 inches apart, on greased cookie sheets.
Bake at 400 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes.
