Title: Sweet and Sour Chicken #2
Categories: Mixes, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  White vinegar
2 c  Chicken Broth
2 c  Water
2 c  Rice
1/2 c  Thinly sliced onion
3/4 c  Thinly sliced green peppe
2    Tomatoes, cut in wedges
1/2 c  Water
5 tb Corn Starch
3/4 c  Brown Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 c  Pineapple chunks, drained
2 c  Chicken Mix
2 tb Soy sauce
Combine 1/2 c water, cornstarch, brown sugar and salt.  Add pineapple
juice and cook for 5 to 7 min.  Add chicken mix, soy sauce and
vinegar. Cook for 15 more min.  Cook rice in 2 c water and broth. Add
pineapple chunks, onion slices, green peppers cooking till veg are
tender. Add tomatoes just before serving.  Serve on rice.
