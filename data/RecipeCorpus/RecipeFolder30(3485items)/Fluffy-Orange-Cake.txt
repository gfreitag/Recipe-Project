Title: Fluffy Orange Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
3    Eggs
1/4 c  Poppy seeds
Powdered sugar
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 c  Orange juice
3/4 c  Miracle whip
Heat oven to 350.  Mix all ingredients except powdered sugar with
electric mixer on medium speed for 2 minutes. Pour into greased and
floured bundt pan. Bake 35 to 40 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes;
remove from pan. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.  Source: Kraft ad
