Title: Smartie Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts, Candies
Yield: 24 servings
2 1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
2 pk Smarties
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  White sugar
2 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
Mix all ingredients together. Drop 1 teaspoon of dough on a greased
cookie sheet. Flatten and put 2 Smarties per cookie. Bake at 350 F
for 10 min. Submitted By TERRY JACKSON   On   05-13-95
