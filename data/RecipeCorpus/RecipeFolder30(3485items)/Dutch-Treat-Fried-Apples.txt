Title: Dutch Treat Fried Apples
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sour cream
1 ds Cinnamon (optional)
6 lg Tart green apples, such as
-Granny Smith
1    Stick Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Pure maple syrup or honey
This is the most delicious, nutritious, accompaniment to any main
dish that anyone could possible serve.

Peel, core and slice apples into 1-inch slices. Melt butter in 10-inch
skillet; fry apple slices 3 minutes on each side. Add remaining
ingredients; stir well; cover and continue frying until crisp-tender
or very tender according to preference. Serve warm or cold.
