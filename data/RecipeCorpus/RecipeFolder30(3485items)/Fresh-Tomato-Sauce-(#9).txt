Title: Fresh Tomato Sauce (#9)
Categories: Vegetables, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 3 cups
2 tb Olive oil
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
1/2 ts Salt
x  Freshly ground black pepper
2 lg Ripe tomatoes, about 8 oz.
. each
1/2 c  Tomato or vegetable juice
1/2 md Red onion, minced
1/4 c  Slivered fresh basil leaves
1.  Core the tomatoes; cut into 1/2-inch chunks.  Transfer to a bowl.

2.  Toss with the remaining ingredients. Let stand at least 30
minutes, tossing occasionally to blend flavors.

From "Woman's Day:  The Only 25 Recipes You'll Ever Need," by Sidney
Burstein, ISBN 0-385-41179-0.
