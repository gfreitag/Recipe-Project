Title: Dilled Zucchini - Microwave
Categories: Microwave
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Snipped Fresh Dill
Salt And Pepper To Taste
5 md Zucchini, Sliced 1/4" Thick
1 1/2 c  Chicken Broth
4 tb Unsalted Butter
Combine zucchini, chicken broth and butter in a 3 quart microwave safe
dish.  Cover dish with plastic wrap, leaving one corner open to vent.
Cook on high power, stirring once, for 10 minutes.  Using a slotted
spoon, transfer zucchini to a serving bowl.  Sprinkle with dill,
season with salt and pepper, toss to combine.  Serve hot.  From:
Syd's Cookbook.
