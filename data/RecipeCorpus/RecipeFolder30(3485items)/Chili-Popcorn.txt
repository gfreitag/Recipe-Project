Title: Chili Popcorn
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Paprika
6 c  Popped corn
1 tb Margarine, melted
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/8 ts Salt
(From Helen Anne Dorrough, registered dietitian with Southern Living
magazine)

Combine first five ingredients and drizzle over warm popcorn.

41 calories per 1 cup serving.
