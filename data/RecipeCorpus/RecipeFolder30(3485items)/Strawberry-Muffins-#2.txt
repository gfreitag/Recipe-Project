Title: Strawberry Muffins #2
Categories: Muffins, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Milk
1/3 c  Butter, melted
Egg, beaten
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Strawberries, chpd
1 1/4 c  Flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Rolled oats
1/2 c  Sugar
Preheat oven 425 deg.  Sift flour, baking powder, and salt into a
large bowl.  Stir in oats and sugar.  Set aside.  Combine milk,
butter, egg, and vanilla in a small bowl.  Stir milk mixture into dry
ingredients until just moistened.  Stir in strawberries.  Spoon
batter into muffin cups, filling each 2/3 full.  Bake 15-18 mins or
until lightly browned and toothpick inserted comes out clean.
