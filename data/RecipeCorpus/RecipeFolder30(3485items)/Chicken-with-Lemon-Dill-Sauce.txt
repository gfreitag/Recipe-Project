Title: Chicken with Lemon Dill Sauce
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Chopped dill
3    Or 4 - cloves garlic, minced
Juice of 1 lemon
2    Chicken breasts
1    Stick (8 tbsp.) unsalted
-butter, softened
Preheat broiler. Put all ingredients, except chicken, in a blender and
puree. Spread sauce over chicken breasts and broil, turning once or
twice and brushing again with the sauce, until chicken is done, about
13 - 17 minutes, and skin is crisp.

Serves 2.
