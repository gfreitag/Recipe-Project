Title: Ethiopian Ginger Vegetables
Categories: Vegetables, African, Homemade
Yield: 6 servings
Water
2 md Onions; quartered, separated
2 tb Olive oil
2 cl Garlic
Salt and pepper to taste
5 ea Green chiles;skin,seed,chop
1 ts Fresh ginger; grated
6 ea Small potatoes; cubed
1/2 lb Green beans
4 ea Carrots; cut in strips
Place potatoes, green beans, and carrots into boiling salted water,
cover, and cook 5 mins. Remove veggies and rinse.

Saute the chile and onion in oil until soft but not brown. Add the
ginger, garlic, salt, and pepper and sautee 5 mins. Add the rest of
ingredients, stir well, and cook over medium heat until veggies are
tender.
