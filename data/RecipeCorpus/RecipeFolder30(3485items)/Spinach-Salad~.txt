Title: Spinach Salad~
Categories: Low cal/fat, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Orange Juice
1 tb Olive Oil or cooking oil
1 tb Honey
1 ts Toasted Sesame seeds
1 ts Prepared Mustard
6 c  Torn fresh Spinach
md Oranges, peeled --
Sectioned
sm Red Onion *
1/2 c  Sliced water chestnuts --
Drained
* sliced and separated into rings

For salad, in a large salad bowl place spinach, orange sections, onion
slices, and water chestnuts (use 1/2 of 8 oz can). Sprinkle with 1/4
teaspoon pepper.

For dressing, in a screw-top jar combine orange juice, oil, honey,
sesame seed, and mustard. Cover and shake well. Pour dressing over
salad. Toss lightly to coat. Serve immediately.

Per serving: 141 calories, 4 g protein, 24 g carbohydrates, 4 g fat,
0 mg cholesterol, 87 mg sodium, 807 mg potassium.
