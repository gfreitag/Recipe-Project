Title: Extra-Large Bran Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Golden raisins
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1 c  Buttermilk
1    Egg -- beaten
3 tb Molasses
4 tb Unsalted butter -- melted
1 c  Wheat bran
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

Combine dry ingredients, raisins, and walnuts in a bowl.  Mix well.
In a separate bowl, combine buttermilk. beaten egg, molasses, and
butter.  Stir liquid ingredients into dry ingredients only enough to
mix then quickly spoon into muffin tins.  Bake 20-25 minutes until
golden brown.
