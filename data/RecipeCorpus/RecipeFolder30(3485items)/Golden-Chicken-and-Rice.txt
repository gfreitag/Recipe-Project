Title: Golden Chicken and Rice
Categories: Poultry, Rice
Yield: 4 servings
3 lb Broiler fryer chicken,cut up    2 1/4 c  Dry instant rice
1/2 c  Cold water
1/3 c  Chopped onion
2 oz Jar chopped pimento,drained
Paprika
10 3/4 oz Can condensed cream of
-mushroom soup
3/4 lb Velveeta Pasteurized Process
-Cheese Spread,cubed
Remove skin and excess fat from chicken. In a 2 quart saucepan,
combine soup and cheese;stir over low heat until cheese is melted.
Add rice, water, onions and pimento; mix well. Spoon into a 13 x 9"
baking pan. Place chicken on top of the rice mixture; cover. Bake @
350 degrees for 45 minutes. Uncover; sprinkle with paprika. Continue
baking 15 minutes or until chicken is tender.

Makes 4 to 6 servings.
