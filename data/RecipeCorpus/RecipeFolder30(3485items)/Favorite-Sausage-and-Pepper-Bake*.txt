Title: Favorite Sausage and Pepper Bake*
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
16 oz MUELLER'S ridged mostacciol
Cooked and drained
2 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 lb Sweet Italian sausage
Casing removed
1 ea Green pepper, sliced thin
4 c  Prepared spaghetti sauce
1 c  Water
In large skillet over medium high heat cook sausage, pepper and onion
6-8 minutes, stirring occasionally to break up sausage. Stir in
spaghetti sauce and water; bring to boil. Reduce heat; simmer 10
minutes. In large bowl combine mostaccioli, 1 3/4 cups mozzarella and
Parmesan; stir in sauce. Turn into 13x9 inch baking dish. Sprinkle
with remaining 1/4 cup mozzarella. Bake in 350 degrees oven 30
minutes or until heated through.

Source: Back of a Mueller's mostaccioli box
