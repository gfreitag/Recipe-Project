Title: Pineapple Cream Candy
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Butter
12 ea Marshmallows
1 c  Nut meats
1 c  Sugar; white
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Pineapple; pulp & juice
1 ts Lemon extract
Cook sugar and pineapple to soft ball stage. Add butter, remove from
fire and beat in marshmallows, nuts and extract. Beat until creamy.

Source: Florence E. Harper, Plymouth Grange, Ashtabula County, OH
