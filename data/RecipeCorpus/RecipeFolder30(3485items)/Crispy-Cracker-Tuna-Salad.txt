Title: Crispy Cracker Tuna Salad
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 cn 6 1/2 oz Tuna, drained
3    Green onions -- cut into
-rounds
1    Cucumber, peeled, seeded,
-diced
6    Radishes, sliced
2 c  Cheese crackers, crushed
Salad dressing of your
-choice
1    Head romaine, Boston or Bibb
-lettuce, torn into
-bite-sized pieces
4    Tomatoes, peeled and diced,
-divided
1    Clove garlic, mashed
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Sour cream
1 tb Red wine vinegar
Note:  Chicken or salmon may be used in place of the tuna.

In a chilled salad bowl place lettuce; place 2 diced tomatoes on
lettuce. Mash garlic with salt; combine with mayonnaise, sour cream,
vinegar and Worcestershire sauce; set aside. Break tuna into chunks
over greens; scatter on chopped green onion rings, diced cucumber and
sliced radishes. Pour on dressing; toss to coat well. Garnish with
remaining diced tomatoes and crushed cheese crackers. Serves 4.
