Title: Eggless Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
3 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Water
3 tb Lard
1 lb Raisins
Boil these ingredients in a
Saucepan, then add:
Mix well and place in a well greased 9 x 9 1/2 inch pan.  Bake 40
minutes at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until done. Randy Rigg
