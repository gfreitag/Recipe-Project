Title: Dixie Fried Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1    Egg (slightly beaten)
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Red pepper
Ingredients: 1 (2 to 3 lb.) broiler-fryer chicken (cut up) salt and
pepper (to taste) hot oil

Season chicken with salt and pepper. Combine flour and red pepper; set
aside. Combine egg and milk; dip chicken in egg mixture;then dredge in
flour mixture, coating well. Heat 1 inch of oil in a skillet; place
chicken in skillet. Cover and cook over med. heat about 30 mins. or
until golden brown; turn occasionally. Drain on paper towels. makes 4
servings shared by: Perry Parsons 5/24/93
