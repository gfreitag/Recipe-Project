Title: Prunes in Blankets
Categories: Snacks, Fruit
Yield: 20 servings
1 tb Melted margarine
ds Cinnamon sugar
1 pk (8 oz) refrigerator biscuits
20    Prunes-pitted
Cut each biscuit in half; flatten.  Roll a prune in each half; pinch
tops together and leave ends open.

Bake on ungreased baking sheet in preheated 450 degree oven for about
8 minutes.

Brush while hot with melted margarine and sprinkle with cinnamon
sugar.
