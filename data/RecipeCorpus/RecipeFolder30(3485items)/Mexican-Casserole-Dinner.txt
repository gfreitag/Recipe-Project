Title: Mexican Casserole Dinner
Categories: Meats, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
1    Taco seasoning mix
3 md Tomatoes; chopped
2 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 c  Corn chips; crushed
1 lb Ground beef
2 c  Long-grain rice; cooked
1 cn Tomato soup
1 cn Green chiles; chopped, drain
Recipe by: Sue Klapper Preparation Time: 0:30 Heat oven to 350. In 10
inch skillet brown ground beef over medium heat (5 to 8 minutes).
Drain off fat. Stir in rice, soup, chilies and taco seasoning mix.
Spoon into 9 x 13 baking pan.  Top with tomatoes, cheese and corn
chips. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until heated through. Source:
Land o Lakes One Dish Meals. Submitted By BILL SPALDING   On
04-26-95
