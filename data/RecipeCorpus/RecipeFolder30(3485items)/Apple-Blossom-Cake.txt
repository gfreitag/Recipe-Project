Title: Apple Blossom Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Almond
1/2 ts Apple pie spice
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Oil
3 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
3 lg Apples, chop with peelings
3    Eggs
1/2 c  Orange juice
Mix well.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until done. Randy Rigg
