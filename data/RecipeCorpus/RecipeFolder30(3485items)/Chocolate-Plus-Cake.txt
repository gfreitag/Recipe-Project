Title: Chocolate Plus Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 10 servings
1 3/4 c  Chocolate milk or milk
2    Eggs
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 tb Vanilla
1 pk 4 oz Chocolate instant
Pudding
1    Box Devil's food cake mix
1 pk 12 oz Semisweet chocolate
Chips
This rich, moist cake doesn't need frosting.

Preheat oven to 350~. Combine pudding, cake mix, chocolate chips,
milk and eggs in a large bowl. Mix by hand until well blended. Fold
in nuts. Bake in greased fluted tube pan for 50 - 55 minutes or until
cake springs back when lightly pressed with finger. Cool 30 minutes.
Remove from pan. Serves 10 - 14.
