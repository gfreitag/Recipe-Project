Title: Honey Bars
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Honey
2 c  Raisins
1 c  Mixed nuts
Grind raisins and nuts.  Mix with honey and press into sheet 1/2 inch
thick.  Cover, and place weight on top for 24 hours.  Cut in bars.
Roll in white or colored coconut. Mrs. Edna B. Finley, Oroville, WA.
