Title: Clouds Of Pineapple~
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 9 servings
1 c  Whipping cream -- beaten
Until stiff
1 c  Crushed graham crackers
3 c  Miniature marshmallows
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Crushed pineapple
1 ts Vanilla
In the top of a double boiler, melt marshmallows; stir in milk,
pineapple and vanilla. Allow to cool and fold in whipping cream. Pack
an 8 x 8-inch baking pan with crushed graham crackers and pour
mixture on top. Chill and serve. Serves 9-12.

Per serving:  Calories 204.9; fat 11.2g; cholesterol 38mg;
carbohydrates 25.4; dietary fiber 0.5g; protein 2.0g; sodium 81.
