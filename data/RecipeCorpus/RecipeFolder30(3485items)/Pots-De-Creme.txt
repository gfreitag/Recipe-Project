Title: Pots De Creme
Categories: Chocolate, Cheese/eggs, Alcohol, Desserts
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 6 SERVINGS--------------------------
2 ea Egg yolks
3 ea Tb Brandy
1 c  Semi sweet chocolate bits
1 1/4 c  Light cream
Lightly scald cream.  Put all ingreadients into blender, cover and
process at BLEND until smooth.  Put in six small sherbets.  Cover and
chill at least 3 hours Source: Oster Kitchen Center Cookbook Shared
by: Janet McCoy
