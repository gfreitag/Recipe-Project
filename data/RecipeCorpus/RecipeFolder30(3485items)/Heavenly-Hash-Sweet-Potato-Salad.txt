Title: Heavenly Hash Sweet Potato Salad
Categories: Tasteofhome, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Pecans -- chopped
1/4 c  Celery -- diced
2 tb Fresh Orange Juice
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Lettuce Of Choice -- as
Needed
Red Cherries -- for garnish
4 md Sweet Potatoes -- cooked and
Chilled
3/4 c  Crushed Pineapple --
Drained
3/4 c  Marshmallows, (Or Use
Miniatures) -- cut in small
Pieces
Peel chilled sweet potatoes and cut into small cubes. Add pineapple,
marshmallows, pecans, and celery. Mix orange juice with mayonnaise,
add to salad, and toss gently. Serve on bed of lettuce and top with
red cherries. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
