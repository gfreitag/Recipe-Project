Title: Fudgy Casserole A La Mode
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
3    Eggs
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  All-Purpose Flour -- sifted
3/4 c  Pecans -- chopped
Vanilla Ice Cream
3    Blocks Hershey's Baking
- Chocolate
1/2 c  (+ 1 Tbs) Butter Or
- Margarine, softened
1 1/2 c  Sugar
Melt baking chocolate in top of double boiler over simmering water.
Remove from heat. Beat in butter or margarine, sugar, eggs and
vanilla. Blend in flour; add chopped pecans. Pour into buttered 1- or
1 1/2-qt casserole. Bake at 350 F for 55 mins. Spoon into individual
dishes and serve warm with ice cream. Makes 6 to 8 servings.
