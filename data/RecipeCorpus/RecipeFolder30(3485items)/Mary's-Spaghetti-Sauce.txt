Title: Mary's Spaghetti Sauce
Categories: Italian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Water
2 ts Paprika
1 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Granulated sugar
Salt, to taste
Ground black pepper to taste
1 lb Ground chuck
1    Onion, chopped
1    Green bell pepper, chopped
1 c  Chopped celery (opt)
1/4 ts Ground oregano
12 oz Tomato paste
Brown meat in large heavy pan.  Add onion, green pepper and celery;
saute until onion is translucent.  Add oregano, tomato paste, 3 cups
water, paprika, garlic powder, sugar, salt and pepper.  Cook over low
heat at least 2 hours; the longer the better.  Skim fat from surface.
Serve over cooked pasta or use in Spanish rice, as a sauce for pizza
or in any dish that requires tomato sauce. Mary Gourley, Florissant
