Title: Creamy Vegetables and Fettuccine
Categories: Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 cl Garlic; crushed
1/3 c  Parmesan; grated
6 c  Water
1 ts Salt
9 oz Fettuccine
1 1/2 c  Broccoli & cauliflower
-florets
1 1/2 c  Milk
3 tb Wondra flour
3 tb Margarine
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1/4 c  Red bell pepper; chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Mix milk, flour, margarine, onion, bell pepper, 1/4 tsp salt, pepper
and garlic in 1 quart saucepan.  Heat to boiling over medium heat,
stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute; remove from heat. Stir
in cheese. Keep warm over low heat.  Heat water and 1 tsp salt to
boiling in 3 quart saucepan.  Cook fettuccine as directed on package.
Add broccoli and cauliflower during last 2 minutes of cooking; drain.
Place fettuccine and vegetables on platter; top with cheese sauce.
Sprinkle with additional grated cheese and pepper if desired. Source:
