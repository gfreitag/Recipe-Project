Title: Slow-Cooked Green Beans
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Oregano
1 1/2 ts Savory
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
ds Cayenne Pepper
2 lb String Beans
2    Slabs Bacon cut through
-rind at 1/2" intervals
4 tb Bacon Fat Or Vegetable Oil
1 tb Red Wine Vinegar
1    Clove Garlic -- minced
Wash the beans and place on top of the bacon in a saucepan. Barely
cover with water. Add the other ingredients, except the salt and
cayenne pepper. Simmer, partially covered, at least 1 hr, until the
liquid has almost evaporated. Add salt and cayenne pepper to tatse,
stir, cover again, and simmer a few mins, until the water has
completely evaporated. Serve the same or the following day.

Serves 6-8.

NOTES:  From Mattie Hall and The Inn at Little Washington, VA.
Mattie is a direct descendant of George Washington's brother Charles.
