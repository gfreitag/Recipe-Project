Title: Noodles With Tahini Sauce
Categories: Pasta, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Tahini
1 1/4 c  Water
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Chopped parsley
8 oz Spiral vegetable pasta
1    Onion, sliced
2    Cloves garlic, chopped
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1 tb Oil
Heat a large pot of water until boiling.  Add the pasta, stir,
and return to a boil.
Saute the onions, garlic, and mushrooms in oil in a heavy skillet
until all of the vegetables are tender.  Add the tahini, water, soy
sauce, and the parsley.  Stir until the ingredients are well-blended
and heated all the way through.
When the pasta is cooked, drain it and cover with the sauce.
Serve right away.
