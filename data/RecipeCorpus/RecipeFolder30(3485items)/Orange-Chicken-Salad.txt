Title: Orange Chicken Salad
Categories: Poultry, Salads, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Chopped Onion
1/4 ts Salt
11 oz Can, Mandarin
Orange slices
Drained
Lettuce
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
3 c  Chopped Cooked
Chicken
8 oz Edam Cheese,
Cubed
1 c  Sliced Celery
Combine mayonnaise, chicken, cheese, celery, onions    and salt; mix
lightly.  Fold in orange segments; chill.    Spoon into lettuce-lined
bowl.

Serves: 4 to 6
