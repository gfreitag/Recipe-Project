Title: Party Dream Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Diced orange sections
1/2 c  Halved maraschino cherries
1/2 c  Pecans, crushed
2 c  Diced bananas
1 c  Whipping cream
8 oz Cream Cheese
2 tb Powdered sugar
1/4 c  Dairy sour cream
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
In a small mixing bowl beat together cream cheese (softened to room
temperature), sour cream, powdered sugar, lemon juice and salt until
light and fluffy.  Fold in oranges, cherries, pecans, bananas, and
whipped cream. Turn into a mold.  Chill until firm.  Unmold onto
serving plate. Garnish with cherries. Randy Rigg
