Title: Easy Skillet Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
-vegetables
1/2 c  Lowcal Italian salad
-dressing
2 tb Cooking wine, Optional
-no-salt seasoning mix
2 c  Pineapples, chunks
1 tb Low sodium soy sauce
2    Whole skinless boneless
-chicken breasts, or thighs
2    Onions, cut in strips
-nonstick cooking spray
1 ts Vegetable oil
1 ts Diet margarine
1    16 oz. package frozen
Spray heated skillet with cooking spray.  Add oil and margarine.
Prepare chicken by cutting into 1" by 3" chunks.  Brown chicken and
onion in oil and margarine.  Toss vegetables (broccoli, carrots and
cauliflower are a good choice) with salad dressing. Add to hot
chicken. Sprinkle with seasoning mix.  Stir fry until done, about 5
to 8 minutes. Mix pineapple chunks and soy sauce, stirring to coat
pineapple. Garnish with pineapple chunks.
