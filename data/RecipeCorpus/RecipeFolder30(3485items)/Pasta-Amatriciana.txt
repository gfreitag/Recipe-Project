Title: Pasta Amatriciana
Categories: Pasta, Vegetables, Kids, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
-OR about 6 lg tomatoes
Salt
Pepper
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1 lb Fusili pasta
6 sl Bacon
1/2 c  Minced onion
1 tb Garlic; minced
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes
2 tb Tomato paste
28 oz Canned tomatoes
Cook bacon in large skillet till crisp, about 4 min. Remove and
crumble. Discard all but 1 tb drippings from pan.  Add onion; cook 2
min.  Add garlic and red pepper flakes; cook 15 sec. Return bacon to
pan and add tomatoes and paste. Bring to boil, reduce heat, and
simmer 30 min, stirring occasionally to break up tomatoes.  Add salt
and pepper to taste. Toss with pasta and cheese.
