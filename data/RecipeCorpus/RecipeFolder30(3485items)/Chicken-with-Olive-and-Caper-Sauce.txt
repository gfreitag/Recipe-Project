Title: Chicken with Olive and Caper Sauce
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 c  Sliced stuffed green olives
2 tb Capers
1 tb Caper liquid
2 tb Water
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1    Frying chicken (about 2 1/2
-lbs), cut up
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 tb Butter or margarine
Roll chicken in flour seasoned with salt and pepper. Brown in butter.
Cover and cook slowly for 40 minutes. Add remaining ingredients; heat.

Makes 4 servings.

From: Steve Herrick Source: [Woman's Day Encyclopedia of Cookery -
Vol. 5]
