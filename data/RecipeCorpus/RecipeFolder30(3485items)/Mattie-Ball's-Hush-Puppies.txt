Title: Mattie Ball's Hush Puppies
Categories: Breads
Yield: 36 servings
2    Egg Yolks
2 tb Butter Or Shortening --
Melted
1/2 c  Milk
Vegetable Oil -- for
Deep-frying
2 c  White Cornmeal
(Stone-Ground)
1/2 c  Flour
3 ts Baking Powder
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
1/2 md Onion -- grated
Combine the dry ingredients with the onion. Mix in the eggs,
shortening, and enough milk to make a spongy dough. Mix only briefly.
Drop by teaspoons into deep hot fat, as for French fries.

Serve with fish, or with toothpicks as an hors d'oeuvre at your next
cocktail party.
