Title: Pink Potato Salad
Categories: Russian, Potato, Salads, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
3 tb Cider vinegar
3 ea Scallions, chopped
4 tb Dillpickle relish
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/2 c  Sour cream or plain yogurt
3/4 ts Dill, dried
3 ea Eggs hard-boiled, chopped
2 lb Potatos, peeled, cooked
- cubed 1/2"
1 ea Beet Lg., peeled, cooked,
- 1/2" cubed
1 ea Cumcuber, peeled & diced
1 tb Capers
2 tb Olive oil
Put beet cubes and potato cubes into  large bowl. Add the sour
cream/yogurt, salt, pepper, and capers. Mix gently then add the
remeaining ingredients. Mix gently but well. Cover and refrigerate
8-12 hours. Origin: Tatiana Golubskaya, Rostov-CIS, circa 1994
