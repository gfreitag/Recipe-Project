Title: Pearl Tapioca Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 8 servings
Grated rind of a lemon
Juice of 1/2 lemon
2/3 c  Sugar
5    Egg whites
Cinnamon, opt'l
1 c  Pearl Tapioca
1 c  Milk
3 c  Milk
1 ds Salt
5    Egg yolks
Soak overnight, refrigerated the tapioca in one cup of milk. In a
double boiler add the tapioca mixture, 3 more cups of milk and a dash
of salt and cook 3 hours. Cool. Preheat oven to 325. Beat and add in
the yolks, lemon rind and juice and sugar. Increase sugar to 3/4 cup
or more if desired. Beat until stiff the egg whites and fold in. Put
mixture in a baking dish and bake for 20 min. Serve hot or cold with
a sprinkling of cinnamon or a fruit sauce, stewed fruit and/or
whipped cream. Submitted By JIM WELLER   On   05-07-95
