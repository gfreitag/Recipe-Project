Title: Stuart Anderson's Teriyaki Sauce
Categories: Restaurants, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------THE REGISTER-----------------------------
1 tb Ginger, minced
1/4 c  Asian sesame oil
1 tb Cornstarch
1/4 c  Water
1 tb Brown sugar
1/4 c  Sesame seeds
2 c  Soy sauce
3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 tb Hot pepper sauce
3/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Garlic, minced
Toast sesame seeds: Place in sm skillet on med heat. Shake to keep
seeds moving and toast until lightly. Browned. Combine with soy
sauce, sugar, hot sauce, pepper, garlic, and ginger. Stir in sesame
oil. Bring to simmer. Mix together cornstarch, water, and brown sugar
until smooth. Stir into hot glaze. Simmer until sauce is slightly
thickened. Makes 2 3/4c. Serve as basting sauce or marinade for
baked, broiled, or grilled chicken, beef, steak, pork chops, or fish.
