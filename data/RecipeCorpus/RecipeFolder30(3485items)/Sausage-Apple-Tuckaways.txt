Title: Sausage Apple Tuckaways
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Chopped apple (1 small)
Posted by Stu Hopper
----------------------------    1 1/2 tb Finely chopped celery
1 lb Roll pork sausage
Cut sausage into 8 patties.  Combine apple and celery. Place 2 TB of
apple mixture on 4 of the patties.  Place the other 4 patties on top
and pinch edges together.  Cook slowly 25 minutes until brown on both
sides and thoroughly cooked.  Serve very hot.
