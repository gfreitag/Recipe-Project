Title: Sonny's tongue pickles
Categories: Pickles, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 batch
4 c  Sugar
2    Stick cinnamon
1 tb Whole cloves
9 lg Ripe cucumbers
1/3 c  Salt
4 c  Vinegar
Pare cukes and cut in half length-wise.  Scrape out seeds and pulp,
then cut in pieces about 1x2 inches.  Sprinkle with salt and cover
with cold water.  Let stand overnight.  Drain; rinse with fresh water
and drain again.

Combine vinegar and sugar in a large enamelware kettle.  Tie spices
in a small cloth bag and add.  Bring to a boiling and cook 5 minutes.
Add drained cukes and bring again to boiling.  Reduce heat and simmer
until cukes are tender and appear translucent.  Discard the spice
bag; ladle into hot pint jars, leaving 1/2 inch of headroom and
process for 10 min. in a boiling water bath.
