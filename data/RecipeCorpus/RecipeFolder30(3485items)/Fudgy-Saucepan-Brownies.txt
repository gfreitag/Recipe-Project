Title: Fudgy Saucepan Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 16 servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1 3/4 c  Pecans -- chopped
3/4 c  Butter
4 oz Unsweetened chocolate
2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Melt butter and chocolate in a large,
heavy saucepan over lowest heat.  Off heat, mix in remaining
ingredients in order listed, beating well after each addition.  Pour
batter into a greased 9x9 pan and bake 40 minutes until brownies just
begin to pull away from sides of pan. Cool in pan on a rack before
cutting.
