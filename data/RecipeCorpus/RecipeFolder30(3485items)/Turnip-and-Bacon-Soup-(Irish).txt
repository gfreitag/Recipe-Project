Title: Turnip and Bacon Soup (Irish)
Categories: Soup/stew, Irish
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 lb Chopped turnips
2 pt Stock
1 x  Fat for frying
1/4 lb Streaky bacon, rinds off
1/4 lb Chopped onions
1/4 lb Chopped potatoes
Chop and fry bacon and onions.  Add potatoes, turnips and stock.  Cook
gently until vegetables are soft.  Adjust seasoning and serve.
