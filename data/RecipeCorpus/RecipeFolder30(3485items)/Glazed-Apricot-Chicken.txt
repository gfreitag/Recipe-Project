Title: Glazed Apricot Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
4    Chicken breasts; boneless
-and skinless halves
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
1/2 c  Apricot preserves
1 1/4 oz Onion soup mix
Heat oven to 350.  Mix dressing, preserves and soup mix. Place
chicken in 13 x 9 pan; spread dressing mixture over chicken. Bake 25
to 30 minutes or until cooked through. Source: Kraft ad
