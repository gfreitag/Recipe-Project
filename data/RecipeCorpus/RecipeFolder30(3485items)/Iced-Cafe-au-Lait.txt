Title: Iced Cafe au Lait
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Crushed ice
Sugar (opt)
2 1/4 c  Cold freshly brewed coffee
2 c  Milk
Blend ingredients. Add sugar and continue blending until frothy. Pour
over ice.

Nutritional analysis per serving: 76.1 calories (no sugar added),
92.2 with sugar; 4.08 grams total fat (47 percent of calories from
fat); 16.5 milligrams cholesterol; 63 milligrams sodium.
