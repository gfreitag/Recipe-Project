Title: Broccoli-Almond Pilaf
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------VEGETARIAN JOUR; SEP-OCT 94---------------------
1 lg Bunch broccoli; cut into
-florets and stems peeled
-and sliced
1/3 c  Dried apricots; chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 ts Almond oil
1/4 c  Green onions; chopped
3 tb Almonds; slivered
3 c  Vegetable broth
1 c  Bulgur
In large saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Add onions and almnds
and saute until onions are bright green and almonds are toasted.

Add remaining ingredients. Heat to boiling over medium het. Reduce
heat to low, cover and simmer about 20 minutes or until bulgur is
tender and liquid is absorbed.

Total calories per serving: 229 Fat: 4 grams
