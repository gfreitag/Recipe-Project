Title: Potato Corn Chowder #2
Categories: Soup/stew, Potato, Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
-divided
1 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Salt
3 c  Corn, fresh or canned
1/3 c  Diced green pepper
1/3 c  Diced sweet red pepper
2 tb Diced green onion
2 tb Chopped fresh parsley
2 oz Thick sliced bacon, diced
1 lb White potatoes, peeled and
-cubed
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2    Cloves Garlic, minced
1 c  Chicken broth
1/2 ts Crushed red pepper,
-optional
4 c  Milk, room temperature,
Microwave Directions: In a 4 quart microwave safe bowl, cook bacon at
full power until crisp, about 2-3 minutes. Add potatoes, onion and
garlic; stir to coat with bacon drippings. Cover bowl with plastic
wrap, microwave at full power 5 minutes. Add broth and red pepper;
cover, return to microwave and cook until potatoes are tender, about
8-10 minutes. In a separate bowl, stir 2 tablespoons milk into
cornstarch; add to potatoes with remaining milk, salt, corn and
peppers. Stir well. Cover and cook, stirring twice, until vegetables
are tender, about 6-8 minutes. Just before serving, stir in green
onion and parsley. Yield: 8 servings.
