Title: Chocolate Chip Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Nuts -- chopped
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/3 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
3 tb Cocoa
3/4 c  Flour
Cream together shortening, sugar, and eggs.  Sift dry ingredients
together and add.  Stir in nuts, chips, and vanilla.  Spread in a
greased 9x9 pan and bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes. Cool
completely in pan before cutting.
