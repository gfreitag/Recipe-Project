Title: Oven-Fried Buttermilk Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Freshly ground pepper
8    Chicken thighs, wiped with
-damp cloth
3/4 c  Buttermilk
4    Servings
1/2 c  Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese
1/2 c  Raw wheat germ
1/2 ts Dried rosemary
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 325 F. lightly grease baking sheet.  Combine all
ingredients except chicken and buttermilk in pie plate or shallow
dish. Dip chicken in buttermilk, then roll in dry mixture and place
on baking sheet. Bake until chicken test done, about 50 minutes.

Bon Appetite
