Title: Carrot Salad with Apples and Cinnamon
Categories: Salads, Herbs/spice, Fruit, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
-concentrate
3 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 ts Parsley
1/2 ts Cinnamon
4 ea Carrots, thinly sliced (2
-cups)
2 ea Apples, peeled and coarsely
-chopped
1 tb Frozen apple juice
Toss all ingredients together and chill.  Serve on lettuce leaves.
Origin: Adapted from Watkins Jiffy Dinner Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, May/95.
