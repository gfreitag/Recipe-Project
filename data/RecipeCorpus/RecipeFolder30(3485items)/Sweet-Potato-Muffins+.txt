Title: Sweet Potato Muffins+
Categories: Muffins, Potato
Yield: 18 muffins
1 1/2 c  Flour, unbleached, all
-purpose
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt, optional
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Allspice
1/2 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Chopped pecans (optional)
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
-(1 stick) softened
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2    Eggs
1 1/4 c  Sweet potatoes, cooked and
-mashed
1/4 c  Molasses
2 tb Milk
In large bowl, cream butter and sugars until light and smooth. Add
eggs, one at a time, beating thoroughly after each addition. Stir in
sweet potatoes, molasses, and milk, blending well. Sift together
flour, baking powder, salt (if using), and spices, and add to
sweet-potato mixture, stirring until just moistened. Fold in raisns
and nuts, if using.

Grease bottoms only of muffin cups. Spoon batter into cups, filling
3/4 full, and bake in preheated 400 degree F. oven for 20 to 25
minutes.
