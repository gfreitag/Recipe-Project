Title: Onion Chicken Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1    Soup can water
3/4 c  Converted rice, uncooked
1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms
6    Chicken breasts (boneless,
-if desired)
1 pk (10 ounces) frozen broccoli
-cuts
1/2 c  Chopped sweet onions
1 c  Grated cheddar cheese
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
paprika

Cook onions with broccoli according to package directions; drain. Mix
together the next 7 ingredients.  Add broccoli and onions and stir.
Pour into  a 9 x 13 inch baking pan and place chicken on top.
Sprinkle with paprika.  Bake at 350 degrees for one hour.

Source: Rock Springs Sweet Onion Festival Cook Book 1992
