Title: 100% Whole Wheat Bread for Welbilt ABM
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 loaf
1 tb Butter/margarine
1/4 c  Honey
1 1/4 c  Warm water (105F-115F)
2 1/2 ts Active dry yeast
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Salt
2 tb Nonfat skim milk
Press SELECT for Whole Grain

Press BAKING CONTROL for Light.

Press START.  Tent a piece of aluminum foil, shiny side down, over
glass dome at start of Baking.  Remove when Cooling begins.  Bread
will be ready in 4 hours and 10 minutes.

from Easy Baking by Claudia Burns and Tom Lacalamita (came with the
Welbilt) typed by Tiffany Hall-Graham
