Title: Chili Bean Machine Bread~
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Chili powder
1 cn (10oz) fat-free refried
-beans *
3 tb Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Egg substitute
1 tb Honey
2/3 c  Water
1 tb Active dry yeast
1/2 c  Yellow cornmeal
3 tb Nonfat dry milk
2 c  Unbleached all-purpose
-flour
1 c  Whole-wheat flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
* canned or reconstituted from dry mix

Place all ingredients in the order listed in the bread machine.
Program machine for basic bread setting, and press start.

Yields: 1 loaf/10 slices

(All flours are approximate measures. You may use more or less,
depending on the weight and absorbency of your flour.)

Per serving: 246 calories, 4.9g fat (18% of calories), 4.1g dietary
fiber, 8.3g protein, 43g carbohydrates, no cholesterol, 342 mg sodium.
