Title: Golden Parmesan Potatoes
Categories: Potato
Yield: 6 servings
1/8 ts Pepper
Milk or water
1/3 c  Butter
Sprinkle of parsley
6    Potatoes
1/4 c  Flour
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
3/4 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Cut potatoes in quarters. Combine flour,
cheese, salt and pepper in a bag. Moisten potatoes with milk or water
and shake in bag until evenly coated. Melt butter in 13 x 9-inch pan.
Place potatoes in pan; sprinkle with parsley and bake for about 1
hour, turning once. Serves 6-8.
