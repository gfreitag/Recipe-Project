Title: Apple Dabble Cake with Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Soda
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
3 c  Plain flour
1 1/4 c  Oil
2 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
3 c  Raw apples, chopped fine
1 c  Chopped nuts
Mix well.  Bake in a tube pan for 1 hour at 350 degrees.

Frosting: 1 stick of butter 1 cup light brown sugar 1/4 cup milk

Mix and let boil 5 to 10 minutes.  Pour over cake while hot and let
stand for 2 hours. Randy Rigg
