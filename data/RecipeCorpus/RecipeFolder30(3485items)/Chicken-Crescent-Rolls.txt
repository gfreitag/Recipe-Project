Title: Chicken Crescent Rolls
Categories: Kids, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Milk
1    8 oz can crescent rolls
2 c  Cooked cubed chicken
4 oz Cream cheese, softened
Mix cream cheese and milk. Add chicken. Separate crescent rolls into 4
rectangles and seal perforations. Put 1/2 cup chicken mixture in
center of each rectangle. Bring 4 corners of dough to center and
seal. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet 20-25 minutes until brown.
