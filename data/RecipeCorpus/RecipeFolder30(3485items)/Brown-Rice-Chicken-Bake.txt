Title: Brown Rice Chicken Bake
Categories: Poultry, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Soy sauce
1/4 ts Ground black pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Dried tarragon leaves
1    Vegetable spray
3 c  Cooked brown rice
10 oz Frozen green peas
2 c  Cooked chicken breast*
1/2 c  Low-cal mayonnaise
1/3 c  Slivered almonds (opt)
*Cut chicken breast into cubes.

Combine rice, peas, chicken, mayonnaise, almonds, soy sauce, pepper,
garlic powder and tarragon.  Put in 3 quart baking dish coated with
vegetable spray.  Cover and bake at 350 F 15-20 minutes. 6 servings.

Microwave directions: Place mixture in 3 quart dish.  Cover with waxed
paper and cook on Medium-high (70%) 5-7 minutes.  Rotate dish after 2
1/2 minutes.

Per serving: 270 calories, 19.6 g protein, 6.5 g fat, 32.6 g
carbohyd., 3.8 g dietary fiber, 44 mg cholesterol, 272 mg sodium.

From: USA Rice Council - Texas State Fair Posted by: Sheila Exner -
