Title: Russian Beet & Potato Salad
Categories: Russian, Potato, Salads, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Yogurt, plain
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Cider vinegar
6 ea Scallions chopped
1/2 c  Egg, hardboiled & chopped
1 c  Potato, cooked & diced
1 c  Beets, cooked & diced
1 c  Green peas, cooked
1/4 c  Carrots, shredded
Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl. Cover, refrigerate to
chill, and serve on a bed of lettuce or cabbage leaves. Origin: Chef
Igor Polvotshev, Hotel Bering, Yalta-Ukriane, circa-1994
