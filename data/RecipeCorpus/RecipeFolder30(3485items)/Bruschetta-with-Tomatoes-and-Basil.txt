Title: Bruschetta with Tomatoes and Basil
Categories: Appetizers, Italian, Breads
Yield: 10 pieces
1 cl Garlic; finely chopped
Salt and pepper
1    Loaf sourdough or baguette
8    Plum tomatoes; chopped
3/4 c  Basil; fresh and chopped
2 tb Olive oil
Combine tomatoes, basil, garlic and olive oil in a bowl; season to
taste. Slice bread 1/2 to 3/4" thick; broil or grill on both sides
until lightly toasted.  Spoon tomato mixture, along with juices, on
top of bread. 110 calories and 3 grams fat.  Source: Store ad
