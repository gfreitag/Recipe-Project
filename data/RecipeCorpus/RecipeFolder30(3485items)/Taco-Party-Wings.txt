Title: Taco Party Wings
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry, Main dish, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 ts Salt
2 ts Parsley Flakes
1    Plastic bag
2 lb Chicken wing drummettes
1    Pkg Taco Seasoning
1/4 c  Cornmeal
Shake wings in bag, a few at a time, until coated. Place in single
layer on greased shallow baking pans.  Bake in 350F oven 25 min. Turn
wings over and bake 10 min or until tender.

From McCormick & Company, Inc. 1991 advertisement.
