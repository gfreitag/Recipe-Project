Title: Salad Dressing (#11)
Categories: Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 ts (to 1/2 tsp) Salt
1/4 ts Sugar
x  Freshly ground black pepper
x  Fresh parsley or chives,
. chopped (optional)
1/3 c  Olive oil
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
3 tb Vinegar of your choice
2 tb Mayonnaise
1 tb Water
2 ts Dijon mustard
1.  In a small covered jar, shake all the ingredients until the
mixture is smooth.

2.  Let stand at least 30 minutes for the flavors to develop.

From "Woman's Day:  The Only 25 Recipes You'll Ever Need," by Sidney
Burstein.
