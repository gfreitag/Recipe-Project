Title: Fried Strawberries
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 1 SERVINGS--------------------------
1/2 c  White wine dry
2 ea Tb Oil
1/2 c  Sugar
1 x  Honey cream (see recipe)
4 c  Strawberries; fresh
1 c  Flour
1 ea Tb Sugar
2 ea Eggs; beaten
Wash strawberries, remove stems. Pat dry with paper towels. Set
aside. In a large deep saucepan or a deep fryer, heat oil to 375~.
Meanwhile, combine the flour and 1 tbsp sugar. Make a well in the
center. Combine eggs, wine and the 2 tbsp cookin oil; add to the
flour mix. Beat with a rotary beater or wire whisk until smooth. Dip
berries, a few at a time, into batter. Fry strawberries, a few at a
time, in hot oil for 1 -2 minutes or until golden. Remove with a
slotted spoon; drain on paper towels. While warm, roll in the
remaining 1/2 cup sugar, if desired. Cool slightly before eating.
Serve with warm Honey Cream (see recipe).
