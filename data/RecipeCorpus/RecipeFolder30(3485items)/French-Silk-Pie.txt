Title: French Silk Pie
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
2 lg Eggs
1    Prepared 9" Pie Shell
Whipped Cream
Slivered Almonds
1/4 lb Butter
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1 oz Melted Chocolate
Combine the butter and sugar and mix until creamy and fluffy.  Add the
vanilla and chocolate.  Mix well.  Add one egg and beat with a mixer
for 5 minutes.  Add another egg and beat for an additional 5 minutes.
Pour into a baked pie shell.  Refrigerate overnight.  Top with
whipped cream and slivered almonds.  From: Syd's Cookbook.
