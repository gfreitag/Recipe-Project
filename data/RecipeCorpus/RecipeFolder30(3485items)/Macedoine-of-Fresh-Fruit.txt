Title: Macedoine of Fresh Fruit
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Frozen Raspberries
1 lg Lemon
4 tb Rum
1/4 c  Fresh Orange Juice
4 lg Thinly Sliced Oranges
2 lg Diced Apples
2 lg Diced Pears
1/3 c  Walnuts
2 lg Cubed Bananas
Mix the orange juice and rum in a mixing bowl.  Prick fruit to allow
the marinade to soak for several hours.  Stir the fruit marinade
frequently. Add raspberries and walnuts just before serving.
Sprinkle each layer with powdered sugar and serve very cold over ice
cream or cake.  From: Syd's Cookbook.
