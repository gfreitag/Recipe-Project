Title: BONNIE'S BLUEBERRY MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins, Fruit, Breakfast, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Milk
1/3 c  Butter or margarine, melted
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Fresh or frozen blueberries*
Additional butter, melted
Additional sugar
"Heritage House in
-Chesaning, Michigan serves
-delicious blueberry muffins
With all their meals.
-Those muffins are tops..."
-- Carole Mulder  (This is
It!)
2 c  Flour
2/3 c  Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.  In
another bowl, beat eggs.  Blend in milk, butter, nutmeg and vanilla;
pour into dry ingredients and mix just until moistened.  Fold in
blueberries.  Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds
full.  Bake at 375 degrees for 20-25 minutes. Brush tops with melted
butter and sprinkle with sugar. * = If using frozen blueberries,
rinse and pat dry before adding to batter. Yields:  1 dozen From:
"Taste of Home" Magazine
