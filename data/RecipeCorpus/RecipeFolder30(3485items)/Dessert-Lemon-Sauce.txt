Title: Dessert Lemon Sauce
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
5 tb Butter
3 tb Lemon juice
ds Salt
2 ds Angostura bitters, opt'l
3/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1 1/2 ts Grated lemon rind
2 c  Water
In a double boiler or a heavy sauce pan, combine and stir until
thickened the sugar, starch, rind and water. Remove from the heat and
add the butter, juice, salt and bitters. Mix and serve hot over
puddings or cakes. Submitted By JIM WELLER   On   05-04-95
