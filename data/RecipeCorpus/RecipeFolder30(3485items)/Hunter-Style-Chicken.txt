Title: Hunter Style Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1    15 oz can tomato sauce
1/2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Thyme
1 cn Mushroom stems and pieces
1/4 c  Salad oil
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
3 lb Cut up chicken
1    Green bell pepper,cut into
-strips
2 md Onions,quartered
1    Clove garlic,finely chopped
Heat salad oil in large skillet over medium heat. Mix flour and 1
teaspoon salt; coat chicken pieces. Cook chicken in oil 15 to 20
minutes or until brown. Drain fat from skillet. Add green bell pepper
and onions. Mix garlic, tomato sauce, water, 1 tsp salt, oregano,
pepper, thyme and mushrooms with liquid; pour over chicken and heat
to boiling. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 45 minutes or until chicken
is tender.

Serves 4 to 5.
