Title: Quick Caramel Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Milk
1 c  Confectioners sugar
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
Melt butter; add brown sugar and cook over low heat for 2 minutes,
stirring constantly.  Add milk and continue, stirring until mixture
comes to a boil. Remove form heat and cool.  Add confectioners sugar
until right consistency is achieved. Randy Rigg
