Title: Chicken Pita, Cucumber Salad, Ice Tea
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Honey
1 tb Dijon mustard
1/2 c  Sliced cucumber
2 tb Low-cal vinaigrette
2 oz Cooked chicken breast meat
1    Small pita
1    Lettuce
2    Tomato slices
Servings:  1

Combine sliced chicken, lettuce, tomato, honey and mustard.  Pack
into pita bread.  Serve with cucumber salad and ice tea.
