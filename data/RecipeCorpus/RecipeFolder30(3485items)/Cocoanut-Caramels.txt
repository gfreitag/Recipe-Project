Title: Cocoanut Caramels
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Butter
1 c  Cocoanut
3 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Milk
Melt 1 c. brown sugar. Add milk, remaining brown sugar and butter.
Boil to soft ball. Add cocoanut. Beat and drop from teaspoon on wax
paper. Cool.

Source: Ruth Leimbach, Huron Grange, Erie County, OH
