Title: Cherry Yogurt Frosting
Categories: Diabetic, Cake/cookie
Yield: 9 sweet ones
2 tb Cheery yogurt;
1    Egg white;
Beat egg white in soft peaks; gradually add the yogurt.  Beat to stiff
peaks.  Spread on 8 X 9" cake. Serve immediately. Yield: Frost sided
and tops of any size cake. Food Exchange per serving: 1 serving
NEGLIGIBLE; CAL: 1 serving NEGLIGIBLE Source: The Diabetic Chocolate
