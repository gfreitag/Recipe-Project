Title: Strawberry Muffins w/Strawberry-Cream Cheese Spread
Categories: Muffins, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
MMMMM--------------------------LA TIMES-------------------------------
3    Eggs
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 c  Milk
1 ts Grated orange peel
12    Strawberries, cut fan-shape
1 c  Strawberries, chpd
3/4 c  + 1T sugar
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
MMMMM----------------STRAWBERRY CREAM CHEESE SPRD---------------------
1/4 c  Strawberries, crush OR chp
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
Sprinkle the chpd strawberries with 1T sugar and set aside. Sift
together flour, remaining 3/4c sugar, baking powder, and salt in lg
bowl. Beat eggs until light in a sm bowl. Add oil, milk, and orange
peel, and blend well. Add egg mixture to flour mixture. Stir until
mixed, 10-15 strokes. Drain chpd berries and fold into batter. Fill
paperlined cups 2/3 full. Bake 400 F, 15 mins, or until a toothpick
incerted in center comes out clean. Serve warm or cooled with
Strawberry-Cream Cheese Spread. Garnish each muffin with a fan-shaped
berry.

STRAWBERRY-CREAM CHEESE SPREAD: Mix until well blended. S: LA Times
