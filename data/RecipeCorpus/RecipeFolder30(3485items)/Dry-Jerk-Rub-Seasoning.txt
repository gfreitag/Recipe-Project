Title: Dry Jerk Rub Seasoning
Categories: Chilies, Salsa/sauce, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 cup
2 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Ground black pepper
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Ground dried Habanero
2 tb Onion powder
1 tb Ground allspice
1 tb Ground thyme
2 ts Ground cinnamon
Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix well. It is also a good
sprinked on cooked food instead of salt.

Dave DeWitt and Nancy Gerlach. "The Habanero Cookbook".
