Title: Boudin Blanc (Ellen Cleary 2)
Categories: Sausage, Cajun/creol, Homemade, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Parsley, chopped
1 tb Garlic, minced
2 c  Cooked rice
1 ts Cayenne
1 ts Sage
1 ts Pepper
1 lb Lean pork
3 lg Onions, chopped
2    Bay leaves, crushed
5    Peppercorns
1 1/2 ts Salt
1    Green pepper, chopped
1/3 c  Green onions, chopped
FROM :    Ellen Cleary, Apr-15-91 5:27am

Place pork in casserole, add water to cover.  Bring to a boil. Add
half the onions, all the bay leaves, peppercorns, and salt. Simmer 1
hr until meat is well cooked.  Drain.

In a meat grinder, grind pork, remaining onion, green pepper, green
onion, parsley, and garlic.  Add rice, cayenne, sage, and pepper. Mix
well. Check for seasonings by frying a small patty.
