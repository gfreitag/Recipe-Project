Title: Artelia's Plain Biscuits
Categories: Breads, Breakfast
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 c  Butter
3/4 c  (Approx)
1    Egg -- well beaten
Milk
3 c  All-Purpose Flour -- sifted
3 ts Baking Powder
1 tb Sugar -- optional
3/4 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 400 F. Sift dry ingredients together. Then; using a
pastry blender or 2 knives, cut in the butter. Combine milk and egg
and slowly add to flour until mixture is wet enough to hold together.
Turn onto a floured board. Knead, then roll to 1/2-inch thickness and
cut with a biscuit cutter. Bake for 10 to 12 mins or until lightly
golden. Yield: about 2 dozen biscuits.
