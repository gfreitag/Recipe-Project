Title: Meat Loaf, Barbecue Style
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
16 oz Tomato sauce; divided
1/2 c  Water
3 ts Vinegar
3 ts Sugar, brown
2 ts Mustard, prepared
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 lb Beef, ground
1 c  Breadcrumbs
1    Onion; finely chopped
1    Egg; beaten
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Combine beef, breadcrumbs, onion, egg, salt, pepper, and 1/2 can
tomato sauce.  Form into a loaf, and put in shallow baking dish.
Combine remaining ingredients; pour over meat loaf; bake at 350
degrees for 1 hour and 15 minutes, basting occasionally.
