Title: Dropped Eggs^
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
2    Eggs
Buttered toast
Boiling water
Salt
Boil water in a shallow fry pan. Salt lightly and drop in eggs, one
at a time. Cook til the whites are just set, then lift from water
with a skimmer and place on hot buttered toast.
