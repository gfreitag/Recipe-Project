Title: Cabbage Cheese Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
Salt and pepper to taste
1 c  Cubed Velveeta cheese
6 sl Bread, cubed
1    Stick of butter
1 md Head cabbage
3 tb Butter
3 tb Flour
1 cn Milk
Cut up cabbage in small pieces.  Cook in slightly salted water for 3
minutes.  Put in colander and let sit for a while to drain well. Melt
3 tablespoons of butter, stir in flour, add milk, combine cabbage
with sauce. Stir in cheese and keep stirring until cheese melts.
Taste and add salt pepper to taste.  Put in buttered casserole. Brown
bread 1 stick butter. Put on top of cabbage and bake in 350 oven for
about 20 minutes. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   05-01-95
