Title: Delicious Microwavable Rice
Categories: Microwave, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Instant chicken bouillon
1/4 ts Dried basil leaves
1 cn 4 oz Mushrooms
2 tb Snipped parsley
Grated Parmesan cheese
1 1/2 c  Uncooked instant rice
1 1/2 c  Hot water
1/4 c  Margarine or butter
1 c  Finely chopped onion
1 sm Clove garlic -- finely
Chopped
Mix all ingredients, except mushrooms, parsley and Parmesan cheese,
in 1 1/2-quart microwavable casserole. Cover tightly and microwave on
HIGH for 7 minutes. Stir in mushrooms. Cover tightly and microwave
until rice is tender and water is absorbed, 4-6 minutes. Stir in
parsley and sprinkle with cheese.
