Title: Fresh Garden Loaf
Categories: Vegetables, Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
3    Eggs, slightly beate
1 tb Vegetable oil
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Crushed potato chips
1/2 c  Water
1 lb Spinach
1/2 lb Green beans
1/2 lb Walnuts
1 lg Onion, quartered
2    Carrots, cut into pieces
1    Stalk celery, cut in pieces
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
1 c  Baked beans ( 15 1/2 oz )
Heat oven to 350 F.  Grease a shallow baking pan.  Wash green
vegetabl put through food grinder or chop together with nuts, onion,
carrots, celery and crumbs; mash beans and blend into vegetable
mixture.  Add e oil and seasonings; blend well.  Shape into and 8"
loaf; place in baki pan. Sprinkle with potato chip crumbs.  Bake 1
hour or until loaf is firm, adding 1/2 cup water by tablespoon while
loaf is cooking.
