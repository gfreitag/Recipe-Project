Title: SUPER BOWL COOKIE BARS
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 bars
2 c  Hershey's Semi-Sweet
-Choclate Chips (12 oz)
1 c  Hershey's Butterscotch
-Chips or Reese's Peanut
-Butter Cups
1 cn Flaked coconut (3 1/2 oz)
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/4 c  Hershey's Cocoa
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Margarine or butter, melted
1 cn Eagle Brand Sweetened
-Condensed Milk (14 oz; NOT
-evaporated milk)
Preheat oven to 350'F. (325'F. for glass dish). Combine crumbs,
Hershey's Cocoa, sugar and margarine. Press firmly on bottom of 13x9"
baking pan; pour Eagle Brand evenly over crust. Top evenly with
remaining ingredients in order listed; press down firmly. Bake 25-30
minutes or until lightly browned. Cool. Chill thoroughly if desired.
Cut into bars. Store loosely covered at room temperature.

Prep time: 10 minutes.
