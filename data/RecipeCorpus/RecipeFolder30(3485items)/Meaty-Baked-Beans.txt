Title: Meaty Baked Beans
Categories: Side dish, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Hamburger
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Vinegar
1/2 c  Onion
1    Can french fried onions
3 lb Great north beans; jarrd
1    Kidney; can or red beans
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 lb Bacon; fried and drained
Brown burger and add bacon. Mix rest of ingredients. Add meat. Put in
casserole dish.  Sprinkle 1 can french friend onions on top.

Bake uncovered 2 hours at 350
