Title: Honey and Bourbon Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Tb Garlic Powder
1 ea Tb Cayenne Pepper
1 ea Tb Mrs. Dash
1/4 ts Tabaco Sauce
1/3 c  Jamaican jerk seasoning
1 cn 6 oz Tomato Paste
2 oz Wild Turkey Bourbon
1/2 c  Dark Brown Sugar
2 c  Orange Blossom Honey
1 ea Tb Dijon Mustard
Mix all the ingredients in the order listed in a medium sized bowl
using a wire whisk. Let the mixture stand at room temperature for 1
hour. Makes 3 cups. Note: Jamaican jerk is a seasioning that can be
found in gourmet shops, Indian grocery stores, and some super
markets. It gives the sauce a delicious peppery kick.
