Title: Sausage Egg Brunch
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 3 servings
12 sl Sandwich bread, cubed
Without crust
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 lb Minced sausage, brown and
Drain
9    Eggs, beaten
1 1/2 c  Grated sharp cheddar cheese
3 c  Milk
Beat eggs.  Add milk and dry ingredients.  Stir in cubed bread.  Pour
into large greased baking dish.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Allow to set at room temperature 1/2 hour before baking.  Bake at 350
degrees for 45 minutes.  Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting to
serve. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   04-27-95
