Title: Cincinnati Chili 3
Categories: Chili, Soup/stew, Meats
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Red pepper or chili pepper
1 tb Salt
1 1/2 ts Ground allspice
1 cn (6 oz) tomato paste
1 1/2 tb Cider vinegar
3 lg Whole bay leaves
1 qt Water
2 lb Lean hamburger
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Cumin seed or ground cumin
1 lg Chopped onions
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1    Garlic toe
2 tb Chili powder
Crumble raw (do not brown) hamburger into water; add all ingredients.
Bring to boil, then simmer 3 hours.  Remove garlic toe and bay leaves.
Add whole red peppers (if desired) for spicier taste.
