Title: Chicken/turkey and Mushrooms Parmesan
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Dry sherry wine
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1/2 c  Sliced red or green bell
-pepper
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1    Bag Minute Rice Boil in Bag
-rice,cooked
1 lb Boned chicken or turkey
-breasts,cut into strips
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Oil
10 3/4 oz Can condensed cream of
-mushroom soup
1/4 c  Water
Cook and stir chicken and onion in hot oil in a skillet until chicken
is lightly browned and onion is tender. Stir in soup, water and wine.
Add mushrooms, peppers and cheese. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat;
cover and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve over rice.
Top with additional cheese, if desired.

Makes 4 servings.
