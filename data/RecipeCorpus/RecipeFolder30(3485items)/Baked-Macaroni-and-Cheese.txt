Title: Baked Macaroni and Cheese
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 10 servings
1 lb Shredded cheddar cheese
1 cn RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and
-Green Chilies (10 oz)
3 c  Uncooked elbow macaroni
1/2 c  Margarine
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
2 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 375'F. Cook macaroni as directed on package; drain
and set aside. Melt margarine over low heat. Stir in flour; cook 1
minute, stirring constantly until mixture is smooth and bubbly. Stir
in milk; heat to a boil, stirring constantly. Reduce heat and simmer
until thickened, about 1 minute. Add cheese and RO*TEL. Stir until
cheese is melted, remove from heat. Stir macaroni into cheese sauce.
Transfer to an ungreased 9x12" baking dish. Bake uncovered 30 minutes
or until heated through.
