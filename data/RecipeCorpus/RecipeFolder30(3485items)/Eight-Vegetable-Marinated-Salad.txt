Title: Eight-Vegetable Marinated Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 12 servings
3 md Onions; chopped
2 md Green peppers; chopped
1 c  Vinegar
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Oil, vegetable
16 oz Green beans, cut; drained
16 oz Wax beans, cut; drained
15 1/2 oz Kidney beans; drained
1 c  Lima beans; cooked & drained
1 c  Corn, whole kernel; drained
1 c  Carrots; sliced
Combine vegetables in a large bowl, and toss lightly.

Combine vinegar, sugar, and oil in a small saucepan; bring to a boil.
Pour hot marinade over vegetables; stir gently.  Cover and chill salad
overnight.
