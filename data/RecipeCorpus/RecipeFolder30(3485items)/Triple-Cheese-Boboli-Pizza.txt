Title: Triple Cheese Boboli Pizza
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM-------------------BON APPETIT, 8/92 TBTC------------------------
2 ts Oregano, crumbles
1/2 c  Mozzarella, agrated, 2 oz
1/4 c  Parmesan
Fresh basil leaves
16 oz Boboli
6 tb Purchased pesto sauce
1 c  Fontina, grated, 4 oz
5    Plum tomatoes, seeded, thin
-sliced
Preheat oven 500 F. Place pizza crust on lg baking sheet. Spread pesto
over. Sprinkle evenly with Fontina. Arrange tomato over. Season with
pepper. Sprinkle oregano over, then mozzarella, and Parmesan. Bake
until crust is golden and topping bubbles, 10 mins. Garnish with
basil leaves. S: TBTC
