Title: Prune-Walnut Biscuits
Categories: Snacks, Fruit, Nuts/grains, Breads
Yield: 1 pan full
1/4 c  Chopped walnuts
1 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1 pk (8 oz) refrigerator biscuits
2 tb Melted margarine
10    Prunes, pitted and cooked
Place biscuits in greased 8 inch layer cake pan.  Brush with half of
the margarine.  Press a prune in center of each biscuit. Sprinkle
with walnuts, sugar and cinnamon.  Drizzle with remaining margarine.

Bake in preheated 425 degree oven 15 to 20 minutes.  Serve Warm ==
