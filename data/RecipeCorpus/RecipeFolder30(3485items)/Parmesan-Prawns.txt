Title: Parmesan Prawns
Categories: Seafood, Italian, Restaurants
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------TRADER VIC'S-----------------------------
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
2 c  Fine bread crumbs
1 c  Parmesan cheese
24 md Prawns, uncooked
1 tb Salt
1 1/2 tb Worcestershire sauce
MMMMM-----------------------DIPPING BATTER----------------------------
1/2 c  Water
1    Egg
1 pn Salt
2 tb Flour
1 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Oil
Shell the prawns and butterfly them. Season with salt, Worcestershire
sauce and lemon juice.  In a separate bowl, mix the breadcrumbs and
Parmesan. Dip the seasoned prawns into the DIPPING BATTER and roll
them in the crumb/cheese mixture. Deep fat fry them at 350 F, 2 mins,
until golden brown. DIPPING BATTER: Mix all ingredients together.
