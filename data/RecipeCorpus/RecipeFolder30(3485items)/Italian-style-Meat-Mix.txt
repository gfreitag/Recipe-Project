Title: Italian-style Meat Mix
Categories: Meats, Italian, Mixes, Sausage
Yield: 16 cups
3 lb Sweet Italian Turkey Sausage    1 1/4 ts Parsley
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
5 ts Romano, Grated
6 c  Water
-cut Into 2' Lengths
56 oz Tomato Puree
28 oz Canned Peeled Tomatoes,
-slightly Mashed
1 1/2 ts Basil
MMMMM-------------------------MEATBALLS------------------------------
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Soft Bread Crumbs
1 ea Egg, Beaten
1 1/2 lb Ground Meat, Lean
1/2 ts Basil
1/4 ts Onion Powder
1/2 ts Parsley
In a large dutch oven, brown sausage over medium high heat, stirring
occasionally. Simmer over low heat 20-25 min until meat is no longer
pink. Drain, reserving 2 tb drippings in skillet. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Cover and simmer 30 minutes over medium heat. Prepare
meatballs <or use 2 quarts Meatball Mix>. Spoon meatballs into tomato
mixture. Bring to a boil over medium high heat, cover, simmer over
low heat 5-6 hours until thickened. Cool. Store in freezer in 1 cup
containers. Use within 6 months. Meatballs: Preheat oven to 400Ã¸.
combine all ingredients, shape into 1' balls. Place on ungreased
baking sheet, bake 10-15 min until browned, discard drippings.
