Title: Rigatoni with Creamy Tomato Sauce
Categories: Pasta, Italian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
14 1/2 oz Can (1 3/4 c) chunky tomato
-sauce
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1 c  Ripe olives, halved
1/2 c  Parmesan, grated
1/4 c  Green onions, sliced
8 oz Dry rigatoni or penne,
-cooked, drained, and kept
-warm
1 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Diced onion
2 tb Dry vermouth or white wine
In large skillet, heat oil; saute onion for 4-5 minutes.  Add
vermouth; cook for 1 minute.  Stir in tomatoes and juice, cream,
pasta, ripe olives, and Parmesan cheese; toss well.  Sprinkle with
green onions.

Suggested wine: Chenin blanc or white zinfandel From Bryan/College
Station Eagle, 4/95
