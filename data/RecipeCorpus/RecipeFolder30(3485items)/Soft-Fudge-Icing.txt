Title: Soft Fudge Icing
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Butter
8 tb Cream
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa
2    Eggs, well beaten
Mix and cook until it forms a soft ball in cold water and until thick
and creamy.  Add 1 1/2 teaspoons of vanilla. Randy Rigg
