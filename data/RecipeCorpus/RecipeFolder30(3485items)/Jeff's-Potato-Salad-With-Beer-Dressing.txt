Title: Jeff's Potato Salad With Beer Dressing
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Potato
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Margarine
2 ts Flour
1 tb Sugar
1 c  Beer
1/2 ts Tabasco sauce
2 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
6    Potatoes
1 ts Mustard -- or brown
1    Bacon slices
1 tb Chopped onions
1    Celery stalks -- chopped
2 ts Salt
Boil potatoes until just tender. Peel and slice. Fry bacon until
crisp. Break into small pieces and mix with onion, celery and salt;
set aside. Stir melted butter and flour in a small saucepan until
blended. Add mustard and sugar. Slowly stir in beer and Tabasco
sauce. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Pour over potatoes.
Sprinkle with parsley. Toss lightly and let stand 1 hour. Add bacon
mixture; toss gently and serve.

: Jeff Merrill of Redding, CA.
