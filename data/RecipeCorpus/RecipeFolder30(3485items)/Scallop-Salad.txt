Title: Scallop Salad
Categories: Seafood, Salads, Restaurants
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------TASTE OF SF-----------------------------
4 cl Garlic, minced
2 md Ripe tomatoes, peeled,
-seeded, diced
Salt and pepper
3 c  Assorted baby greens
1    Head radicchio, chpd
3/4 c  Virgin olive oil
1 lb Fresh bay scallops
MMMMM-----------------DIJON MUSTARD VINAIGRETTE----------------------
1 c  Virgin olive oil
Salt and pepper
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
2 ts Dijon
CA Culinary Academy Salad: Arrange greens on 6 chilled plates.
Distribute radicchio equally. Sprinkle approx 2T vinaigrette over
each salad. Heat oil in skillet and saute scallops until opaque, 1
min. Reduce heat and add garlic and tomatoes. Season with salt and
pepper. Heat 1 min. Place scallop mixture on top of radicchio. Dijon
Mustard Vinaigrette: Combine with a whisk. Season with salt and
pepper. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   04-28-95
