Title: Bangers
Categories: British isl, Homemade, Sausage
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Grated nutmeg
6    Fresh sage leaves chopped
1/2 ts Chopped winter savory
1/2 ts Dried marjoram
3 1/4 lb Pork, minced
1 lb Fresh white bread crumbs
1 ts Fresh ground pepper
Grated rind of 1/2 lemon
From : Lynda Weaver, 04-11-94  23:41, Area: COOKING

Mix the pork with the other ingredients in a large bowl. Add enough
water to make a mixture with a soft piping constistency. Fill sausage
skins. Allow sausages to mature overnight..
