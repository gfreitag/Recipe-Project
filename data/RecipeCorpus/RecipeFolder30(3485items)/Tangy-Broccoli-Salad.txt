Title: Tangy Broccoli Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 servings
4 c  Spinach; loosely packed
8 oz Bacon; cooked crisp and
-crumbled
1/2 c  Onion, red; cut into strips
1/4 c  Raisins
1 c  Miracle whip
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Vinegar
1 bn Broccoli; cut into flowerets
-about  6 cups
Mix dressing, sugar and vinegar in large bowl.  Add remaining
ingredients; mix lightly.  Refrigerate. Source: Kraft ad
