Title: Easy Jello Salad
Categories: Salads, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 sm Can crushed pineapple with
-juice
6 oz Package of jello
2 c  Hot water
1 pt Vanilla ice cream
Dissolve jello in hot water. Add ice cream and stir until completely
melted. Add pineapple and juice. Pour into mold. Let stand several
hours. From Roger's Department Store Cookbook
