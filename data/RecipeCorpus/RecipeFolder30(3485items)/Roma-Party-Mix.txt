Title: Roma Party Mix
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 10 cups
1/8 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 ts Italian seasoning
1/2 ts Fennel seeds; crushed
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
7 c  Crispix
2 c  Cheez-It
1 c  Pretzel sticks
3 tb Olive oil
In 2 gallon ziploc bag combine crispix, crackers and pretzels. Set
aside. Combine oil, hot pepper sauce, italian seasoning and fennel
seeds. Pour oil and spices over cereal mixture. Close bag and gently
toss cereal until well coated.  Sprinkle cheese over cereal. Close
bag and gently toss cereal mixture until well coated. Store in
airtight container. 1/2 cup serving contains 105 calories and 4 grams
fat. Source: Crispix box Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   05-02-95
