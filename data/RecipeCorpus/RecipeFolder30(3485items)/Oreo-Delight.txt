Title: Oreo Delight
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
10 oz Cool Whip
1 1/2 ga Vanilla ice cream, softened
1 lg Package or Oreo cookies
Crumb cookies in food processor.  Add to ice cream and Cool Whip.  Mix
thoroughly and pour into 13x9 inch pan, or Tupperware.  Freeze
overnight. Cut in squares and serve. Randy Rigg
