Title: Poulet Cordon Bleu (Chicken Cordon Bleu)
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
6    Thin slices Gruyere cheese
6 tb Butter
3/4 c  Fine, dry breadcrumbs
1/8 ts Paprika
3    Whole large, boned and
-skinned chicken breasts
Cut in half
6    Thin slices of Prosciutto
-ham
Pull chicken breast open in middle to make a pocket.  Place 1 hame
slice and 1 cheese slice in each.  Melt butter.  Mix crumbs and
paprika. Roll stuffed chicken in butter, then in crumbs. Place in
preheated oven at 400 F for 30-40 minutes until golden brown. Serves
4.

SILVER QUEEN RESTAURANT - Georgetown, CO.
