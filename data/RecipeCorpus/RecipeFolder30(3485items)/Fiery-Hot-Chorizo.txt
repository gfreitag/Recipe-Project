Title: Fiery Hot Chorizo
Categories: Homemade, Sausage
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 ts Coarsely black pepper
2 ts Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Dried oregano
1 ts Ground cumin
1 ts Paprika, optional (for
-color)
2 ts Crushed red chile
1 lb Pork butt, ground
1 lb Beef chuck, ground
1/4 ts Liquid smoke
3 tb Wine vinegar
2    Garlic cloves, crushed
1 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients together, using your hands to mix well.
Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.  Fill the casings and twist into
8 inch links or make patties.  To cook, boil in a skillet in a small
amount of water for 5 seconds and then fry until brown in a skillet,
adding a little oil if necessary.
