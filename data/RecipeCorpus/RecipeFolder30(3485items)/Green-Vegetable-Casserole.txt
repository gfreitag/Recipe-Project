Title: Green Vegetable Casserole
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
Chopped
8 oz Cream cheese
Topping:
1    Roll Ritz crackers,
- crushed
3/4    Stick Margarine -- melted
1 sm Pkg Sliced almonds
1 cn French-style green beans
1 cn White corn (shoe peg)
1 cn Sliced water chestnuts
1 cn Cream of celery soup
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper (optional) --
Mix vegetable ingredients together and place in casserole dish.

Topping:

Mix crushed crackers, melted margarine and sliced almonds together and
spread on top of casserole.

Bake in 350 oven for 40-45 minutes.
