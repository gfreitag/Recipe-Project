Title: Parmesan Broccoli Bake
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea 10 oz packages frozen
Broccoli spears, cooked and
Drained
2 tb Butter
Sesame seeds
Paprika
1 ea Egg white
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
3 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
2 tb Chopped parsley
Grated peel of 1/2 lemon
2 lb Fresh OR
1.  Beat egg white until soft peaks form.

2.  Fold in mayonnaise.  Stir in cheese, parsley, lemon peel.

3.  Arrange cooked broccoli in an oven-proof serving dish.  Pour
melted butter over broccoli.  Top with egg white mixture.

4.  Sprinkle with sesame seeds and paprika.

5.  Bake 5 minutes at 450 F or until puffy and lightly browned.
