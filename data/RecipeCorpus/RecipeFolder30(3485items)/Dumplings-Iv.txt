Title: Dumplings Iv
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
Milk
2 c  Flour
4 ts Baking-powder
2 tb Shortening
Sift flour, measure, and sift with salt and baking-powder.  Cut in
shortening with 2 spatulas.  Add milk until a thick drop batter is
obtained.  Drop by teaspoonfuls into boiling broth. Cover. Boil 12
minutes. Serve at once.
