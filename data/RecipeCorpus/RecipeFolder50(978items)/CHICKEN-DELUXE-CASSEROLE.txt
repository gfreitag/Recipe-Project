Title: CHICKEN DELUXE CASSEROLE
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  Stuffing mix -- + extra for
Topping
1 c  Melted butter
2 cn Cream of celery soup
1 cn Milk
3 c  Cooked cubed chicken or
Turkey
10 oz Pkg.
Drained
1 tb Minced onion
Frozen peas -- cooked and
Mix stuffing mix with butter and press in bottom of a 3 quart baking
idsh. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over stuffing mixture.
Top with some extra stuffing mix. Bake at 375 F. for 30 minutes.
