Title: CHICKEN BARBEQUE SAUCE III
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Olive oil
1 c  Water
3 tb Onion, grated
1/2 ts Garlic puree
2 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
2 ts Paprika
1 ts Fresh ground black pepper
1/3 ts Dry mustard
1 1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 1/4 ts Tabasco
2 tb Tarragon vinegar
Combine all ingredients and simmer about 25 minutes. Use as a basting
sauce when grilling or spit barbequing chicken.

Source:  Modern Barbecue Cooking by Ed Bell
