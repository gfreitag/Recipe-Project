Title: CHICKEN SAVOY
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Whole chicken cut into
-small piece
-oil
-garlic
-oregano
-parmesan cheese
-salt /pepper
-red wine vinegar
Instructions: Pour oil and garlic all over the chicken pieces. Season
with salt, pepper, oregano and parmesan cheese. Bake in pre-heated
oven at 500! until cooked. Immediately after removing from heat ,
pour approx. two tablespoons of red wine vinegar over chicken and
serve immediately.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
