Title: CHICKEN GRAVY
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Chicken bouillon cubes
1    Beef bouillon cube
2 c  Water
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/2 ts Onion powder
2 tb Corn starch
1/2 c  Water
Mix first six ingredients to - gether, mix the cornstarch and 1/2 cup
of water and add to first mixture when hot, stir until thickened.
