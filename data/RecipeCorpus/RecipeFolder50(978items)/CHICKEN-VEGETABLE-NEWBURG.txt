Title: CHICKEN VEGETABLE NEWBURG
Categories: Poultry, Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Boneless chicken breast
-cut into 1" cubes
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 1/2 c  Broccoli florets
1 1/2 c  Cauliflower florets
1    Jar Chicken Tonight Cooking
-Sauce for Chicken
-Creamy Mushroom
1/3 c  Sherry
Hot,cooked rice
In a large skillet, thoroughly brown chicken in vegetable oil; drain
fat. In a medium saucepan, lightly steam broccoli and cauliflower
about 3 minutes; drain thoroughly. Add steamed vegetables, sauce and
sherry to skillet. Cover and simmer 20 minutes or until chicken is
thoroughly cooked. Serve over hot, cooked rice.
