Title: CHICKEN CRUNCH SANDWICH SPREAD
Categories: Sandwiches, Poultry
Yield: 2 Cups
9 1/2 oz Canned chicken spread
3 oz Cream cheese
1/2 c  Chopped Calif. Dried Figs
1/4 c  Finely chopped nuts
1 ts Minced candied ginger
Combine chicken spread with cream cheese, California dried figs, nuts
and ginger.  Use as a sandwich filling.
