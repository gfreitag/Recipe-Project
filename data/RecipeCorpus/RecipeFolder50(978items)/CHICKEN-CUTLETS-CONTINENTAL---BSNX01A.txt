Title: CHICKEN CUTLETS CONTINENTAL - BSNX01A
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
Talkofsky-BSNX01A
1 sm Garlic clove -- crushed
4    Chicken Breast Cutlets
1 pn Italian herbs
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1 tb Chopped parsely
1/2 c  Dry white wine
Pepper
1 ts Lemon juice
Salt
Margerine
Brown cutlet briefly on both sises over medium-high heat. Remove. Add
garlic and mushrooms, saute until mushrooms are golden and pan is
virtually dry.  Add remaining ingredients and cutlets. Simmer over
low heat for about 5 minutes per side or until cooked thorough.

From bag of Empire Individualy Quick Frozen Chicken Breast Cutlets.
