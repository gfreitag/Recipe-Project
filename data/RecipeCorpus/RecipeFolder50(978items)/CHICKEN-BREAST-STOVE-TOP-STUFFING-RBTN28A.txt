Title: CHICKEN BREAST STOVE TOP STUFFING RBTN28A
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 8 Servings
1 ea Box stove top stuffing
8 ea Chicken breast
Butter
1 c  Cream of chicken soup
1/2 c  White cooking wine
Make stove top (preferably mushroom/onion flavor) according to
directions Grease a 13x9 pan with butter, put chicken in pan. Pour
can of soup in bowl & mix with wine real well, pour over chicken. Put
stove top stuffing on top of chicken and bake 350F 1 hour or until
done.
