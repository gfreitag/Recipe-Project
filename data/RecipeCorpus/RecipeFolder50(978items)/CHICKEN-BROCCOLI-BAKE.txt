Title: CHICKEN BROCCOLI BAKE
Categories: Poultry, Sauces
Yield: 6 -8 serving
1 1/2 lb Boneless chicken breast,
-cut into 1" cubes
1 tb Vegetable oil
3 c  Fresh broccoli florets
1    Jar Chicken Tonight Cooking
-Sauce for Chicken
-Country French
2 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1 1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 1/2 c  Fresh bread crumbs
2 tb Melted margarine
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. In a large skillet, thoroughly brown
chicken in vegetable oil; drain fat. In a medium saucepan, lightly
steam broccoli about 3 minutes; drain thoroughly and set aside. Add
sauce and parsley to chicken in skillet. Spoon chicken mixture evenly
in an 8 x 12-inch baking dish. Top with broccoli and Cheddar cheese.
Sprinkle with bread crumbs. Drizzle evenly with melted margarine.
Bake, uncovered, 35 to 40 minutes or until crumbs are golden brown.
