Title: CHICKEN AU PAPILLOTE
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 2 Servings
1 cn Evaporated milk
1 pk Onion soup mix
4    Pcs. chicken, or more
1 c  Rice
2 c  Water
1 tb Paprika
Cook rice as usual.  Place in double layer of foil. Pour
evaporated milk over it and sprinkle dried onion soup over that.
Place chicken on top. Prepeat with rice, milk and soup. Season. Wrap
tightly and bake at 350 F. for abt. 1 hr.  Eat!!  Enjoy!! Courtesy
