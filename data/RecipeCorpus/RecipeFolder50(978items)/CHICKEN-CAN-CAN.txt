Title: CHICKEN CAN-CAN
Categories: Poultry, Easy
Yield: 5 Servings
1 cn (12 oz.) evap. milk
1 cn (10 3/4 oz.) cond. cream of
-celery soup, undiluted
1 cn (10 3/4 oz.) cond. cream of
-chicken soup, undiluted
1 cn (10 3/4 oz.) cond. chicken
-noodle soup, undiluted
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 cn (10 oz.) chunk white chicken
-drained
1 cn (5 oz.) chow mein noodles
In a large skillet, combine the first five ingredients. Bring to a
boil. Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Add the
chicken; heat through.  Serve over chow mein noodles.  Yield: 5
servings. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Quick Cooking
