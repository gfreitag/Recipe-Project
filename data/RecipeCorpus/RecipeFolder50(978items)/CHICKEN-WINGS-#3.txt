Title: CHICKEN WINGS #3
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
12    Chicken wings
1 ts Dark soy sauce
2 tb Lyle's Golden Syrup
2 tb Tomato puree'
1 tb Wine vinegar
-Sesame seeds
Wash and dry the chicken wings, then place them in an ovenproof dish.

Mix the soy sauce, syrup, tomato puree' and vinegar, then pour over
the chicken.  Sprinkle sesame seeds on top.

Bake in the oven at 375^F for 45 minutes.  Serve with rice and prawn
crackers.

Serves 4.

SOURCE:  Lyle's Golden Syrup Recipe Booklet
