Title: CHICKEN BREASTS DIJON
Categories: Entree, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1/3 c  Fine dry breadcrumbs
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Dried whole thyme
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Creamy mustard-mayonnaise
-blend
4    Boneless skinless chicken
-breast ha; (4-ounce)
Vegetable cooking spray
Combine first 4 ingredients in a shallow dish; stir well, and set
aside.

Brush mustard blend evenly over both sides of chicken. Dredge chicken
with breadcrumb mixture.

Place chicken on a rack coated with cooking spray; place rack in
shallow roasting pan. Bake at 375 deg for 45 minutes or until done.
Yield: 4 servings (serving size: 1 chicken breast half).
