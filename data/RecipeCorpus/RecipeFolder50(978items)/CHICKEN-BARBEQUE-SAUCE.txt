Title: CHICKEN BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Ketchup
1 c  Minced onion
1 c  Dry red wine
3 lg Garlic cloves -- minced
2 tb Ginger -- grated
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Dijon mustard
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Oyster sauce
1 ts Tabasco sauce
1 ts Ground coriander
Black pepper to taste
Combine all ingredients and simmer for 20 minutes. Cool and serve on
the side. This freezes well.
