Title: CHICKEN PATTY WITH ROSEMARY FOR ONE
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Serving
1/4 lb Ground chicken
1 tb Grated peeled apple
1 tb Grated onion
1 tb Dry bread crumbs or cracker
-crumbs
1/2 ts Chopped fresh rosemary or
-pinch dried
1/2 ts Prepared horseradish
Pinch each salt and pepper
In bowl, mix together chicken, apple, onion, bread crumbs, 1 tbsp.
water, rosemary, horseradish, salt and pepper.

Shape into 1/2 inch thick patty.  Broil for about 4 minutes per side
or until cooked through. Makes 1 serving. Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Canadian Living 20th Anniversary Cookbook.
