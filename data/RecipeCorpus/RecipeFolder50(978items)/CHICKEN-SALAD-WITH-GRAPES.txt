Title: CHICKEN SALAD WITH GRAPES
Categories: New, Text, Import
1    whole chicken, cooked,
skinned, -- boned, meat
shredded
Yield: 4 Servings
:
:
4 TB orange juice
1 c  mayonnaise
1/2 c  heavy cream, -- whipped
1 1/2 c  halved seedless grapes
1/4 c  sugar
Salt & freshly ground black
pepper
:
:
1/4    head Boston or Bibb lettuce
2 TB julienned orange peel

In a bowl, combine the chicken, orange juice, mayonnaise, whipped
cream, and grapes. Season to taste with sugar, salt, and pepper.
Chill, covered. Arrange on lettuce leaves if desired or on salad
plates. Top with the slivered orange peel.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings
