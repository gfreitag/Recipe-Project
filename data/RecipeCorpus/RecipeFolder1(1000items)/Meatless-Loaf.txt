Title: Meatless Loaf
Categories: Rice/grains, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Crushed peanuts
2 c  Cottage cheese
2 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Cooked brown rice
1 ea Egg
2 tb Chopped onions
1 ts Salt
Combine ingredients and bake in loaf @ 350 degrees for about 30 minutes.
From: Theresa Bryant
