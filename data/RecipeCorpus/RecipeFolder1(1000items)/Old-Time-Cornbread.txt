Title: Old Time Cornbread
Categories: Breads, Londontowne
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Cornmeal
3 tb Lard, melted
1 c  Flour
2 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Salt
3/4 ts Soda
Beat eggs, milk, and lard together. Add remaining ingredients and mix
well. Pour into greased 11" X 7" X 2" and bake in hot (400) oven about 30
minutes.
