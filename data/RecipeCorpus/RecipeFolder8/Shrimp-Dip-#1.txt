Title: Shrimp Dip #1
Categories: Dips, Fish/sea
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Garlic Salt
1    Onion (Chopped)
8 oz Cheddar Cheese (Cubed)
5 oz Shrimp (Drained)
1/4 c  Milk
1 c  Mayonnaise
3 dr Tabasco Sauce
1 tb Worcestershire Sauce
Place all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth.  Served chilled.
