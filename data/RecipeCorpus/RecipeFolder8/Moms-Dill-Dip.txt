Title: Moms Dill Dip
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Hellmanns mayonnaise
1 tb Onion flakes
2 dr Tabasco sauce
1 ts Seasoned salt
1/3 c  Sour cream
1 tb Dry parsley
1/2 ts Accent
1 ts Dill weed
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Mix all ingredients together.  Delicious dip with carrot sticks or celery!
