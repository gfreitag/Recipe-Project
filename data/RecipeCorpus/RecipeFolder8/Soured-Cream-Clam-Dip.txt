Title: Soured Cream Clam Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Fish/sea
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Lemon juice
2 tb Minced onion
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Gorton's clams, drained
1/8 ts Pepper
1 c  Soured cream
1/2 ts Celery salt
Gently fold clams into sour cream.  Add other ingredients and stir gently
until blended.  Serve with chips.
