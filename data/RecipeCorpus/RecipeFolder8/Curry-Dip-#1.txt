Title: Curry Dip #1
Categories: Dips
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Fresh grated onion
1 ts Horseradish
1 ts Tarragon vinegar
1 c  Hellman's Mayo
1 ts Curry powder
1 ts Garlic salt
Mix in order given.  Chill several hours or overnight.  The flavor will get
better.

Good with cauliflower, carrots, celery, etc.
