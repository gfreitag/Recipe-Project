Title: Low Calorie Clam Dip for Vegetables
Categories: Appetizers, Fish/sea
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Thyme
1 cn Gorton's Clams, drained
1 ts Chopped chives
1 c  Cottage cheese
Combine all ingredients; mix well.  Serve as a dip for raw vegetables.
Makes approximately 1-1/3 cups.
