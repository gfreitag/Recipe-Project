Title: Crab Claw Marinade
Categories: Appetizers, Marinade
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 ts Black Pepper
3/4 ts Salt
3/4 ts Sugar
1 c  Olive Oil
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Lemon Juice
1 ts Tarragon
-------------------------------FOOD PROCESSOR-------------------------------
1 c  Scallions
4    Cloves Of Garlic (4-10)
1 c  Parsley
1 c  Celery
Mix all together, pour over claws and serve.
