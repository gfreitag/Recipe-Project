Title: Cheese-Stuffed Mushrooms - NSVhol
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Italian, Nsv
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Large mushrooms (about 40)
1/2 c  Grated Romano cheese
3/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/2 c  Grated onion
2    Cloves garlic, minced
3 tb Minced parsley
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1/2 ts Oregano
3/4 c  Olive oil
Wash but do not peel mushrooms. Remove stems and chop. Mix all
ingredients except mushrooms and oil. Stuff mushroom caps. Pour a
little oil into baking pan; arrange mushrooms in pan. Pour
remaining  oil over each one. Bake at 350F for 25 minutes. Serve.
