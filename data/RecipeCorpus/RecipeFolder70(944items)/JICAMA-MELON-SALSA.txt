Title: JICAMA-MELON SALSA
Categories: Salsa, Sauces
Yield: 8 Servings
1 sm Mango, peeled, pitted
1    Chili, serano, seeded and
-deribbed
Juice of one lime
1 1/2 tb Bell pepper, red, diced
1/2 c  Cantalope, diced
1/2 c  Honeydew melon, diced
2 tb Cucumber, peeled, diced and
-seeded
1/2 c  Jicama, peeled, diced
2 ts Cilantro, chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper, black
2 tb Sour cream
In a food processor or blender, puree lime juice, mango and chile.
Place diced vegetables and fruit in a mixing bowl, add puree and
combine. Mix in cilantro, salt and pepper. Adjust seasonings to
taste. Gently fold in sour cream. Serve with grilled fish or chicken.
Per serving = 28 calories, 1 g fat, 1 mg chol, 80 mg sodium. Calories
from fat = 32%
