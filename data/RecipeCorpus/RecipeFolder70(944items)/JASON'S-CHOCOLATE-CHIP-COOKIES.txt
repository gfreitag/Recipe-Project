Title: JASON'S CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Chocolate
Yield: 6 Dozen
1 c  Butter,softened
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Corn starch
3/4 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Chocolate chips
Cream together butter and brown sugar. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix until
light. Add flour, corn starch, salt and baking soda and mix until
combined well. Mix in chocolate chips. Drop by heaping teaspoonfuls
onto cookie sheet, 2 inches apart. Bake at 350F for 10-12 minutes.
Cool 2 minutes before removing from cookie sheet. Submitted By
