Title: JASON'S CHICKEN PARMESAN
Categories: Chicken
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Chicken breast halves
2 cl Garlic, mashed
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Combine parmesan, salt and pepper. Flatten chicken breasts (if
desired). Heat garlic in butter to melt. Cool slightly. Mix half of
the garlic butter with the sour cream. Lather each breast with the
sour cream mixture. Roll in parmesan mixture. Place breasts in
foil-lined baking dish.Bake, uncovered, at 375F for 25 to 30 minutes.
Baste often with remaining garlic butter. During last 5 minutes, run
under the broiler to brown the cheese. Serve with linguine. Cover
with sauce in pan and additional parmesan if desired. Submitted By
