Title: JOAN COOK'S TUNA FRUIT & PASTA SALAD
Categories: Pasta, Fish, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn (6-3/4 oz.) tuna with canola
Oil
2 c  Cooked shell pasta
2 c  Fresh fruit and/or sweet --
Peppers; cut into bi
Pieces (Jean used -- melon,
Grapes, jicam
Bell peppers)
1 tb Sweet onion -- chopped
DRESSING:
1    (6 oz.) vanilla yogurt
1/2 ts Curry powder
1 tb Lime juice
1 tb Ginger root -- peeled and
Poppy seeds for garnish
Drain tuna and separate into bite-size pieces.  Mix fruit, pasta,
onion and tuna.  Mix dressing ingredients well. Gently mix dressing
with fruit, pasta and tuna.  Sprinkle with poppy seeds, if desired.
Serve chilled.
