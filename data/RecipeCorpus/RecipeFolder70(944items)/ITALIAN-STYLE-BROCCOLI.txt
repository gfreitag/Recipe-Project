Title: ITALIAN-STYLE BROCCOLI
Categories: Italian, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Broccoli, broken into
-florets
1 tb Olive oil
1    Clove garlic, minced or put
-through press
1 sm Dried hot red pepper,
-crumbled
2 tb Dry white wine
Steam broccoli 10 minutes, or until tender and still bright green.
Refresh under cold water. Heat olive oil over medium heat and saute
garlic and res pepper 2 minutes. Add broccoli and wine and saute,
stirring, another 3 to 5 minutes.  Serve.

Serves 4 to 6.
