Title: JALAPENO JELLY #1
Categories: Jams
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Apple cider vinegar
10 c  Sugar
20    Jalapenos, stemmed, seeded,
-finely diced
3 lg Anaheim chilies,
-seeded and finely diced
12 oz Liquid pectin
Paraffin
In a large saucepan over high heat, bring the vinegar and sugar to a
boil. Stir mixture until sugar is dissoved, and lower heat to simmer.
Add the jalepenos and Anaheims, and simmer for 10 minutes, all the
while skimming foam from the top.  Stir in the pectin and bring to a
boil. Boil for one minute, remove from heat, and let stand for 20
minutes. Pour into hot steralized jars and seal with paraffin.
