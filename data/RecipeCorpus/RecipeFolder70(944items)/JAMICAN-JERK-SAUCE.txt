Title: JAMICAN JERK SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Green onions -- chopped
1 sm Onion -- chopped
1 ts Cayenne pepper -- or more to
Taste
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Ground allspice
1/4 ts Ground cloves
Combine all ingredients in a blender containe and process for 15
seconds at High speed.
