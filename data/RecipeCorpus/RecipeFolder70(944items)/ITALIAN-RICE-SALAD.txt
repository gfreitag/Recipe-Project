Title: ITALIAN RICE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Rice
Yield: 48 Servings
1 pk Uncle Ben's Original Rice
-Pilaf
2 1/2 c  Italian Salad Dressing
1 1/2 c  Basil leaves, crushed
3 c  Frozen peas, slightly cooked
1 c  Green onions, sliced
1 c  Ripe olives, sliced
1/2 c  Pimento strips
1.  Cook rice according to package directions. 2. Transfer rice and
reaining liquid to shallow pan.  Let stand until all
liquid is absorbed. 3. Combine salad dressing and basil. 4. Stir
into cooked rice. 5. Cover and chill. 6. Fold in peas, onion, olives
and pimentos before serving. Measurements and timing are designed
only for Uncle Ben's Fast Cooking Rice Pilaf, Original Rice Pilaf,
Fast Cooking Spanish Rice, Long Grain & Wild Rice Blend, and
CONVERTED Brand Rice
