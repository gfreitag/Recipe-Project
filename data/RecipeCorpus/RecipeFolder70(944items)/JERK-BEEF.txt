Title: JERK BEEF
Categories: Meats
Yield: 10 Servings
2 3/8 lb Beef; diced 1" (Raw)
1 tb Soy sauce
2/3 c  Canned crushed tomatoes
5 oz Onions;chopped
1 1/2 oz Green onions; chopped
1 oz Banana peppers; chopped
1 1/2 ts Garlic; minced
2 1/2 ts Black pepper
1 ts Tabasco sauce
2 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Thyme
Trim fat from meat.  Mix all other ingredients. Marinate meat in
mixture 24 hours in fridge.  Drain most of marinade.

Saute meat with a little of marinate until brown and cooked.

Serve with fluffy white rice.
