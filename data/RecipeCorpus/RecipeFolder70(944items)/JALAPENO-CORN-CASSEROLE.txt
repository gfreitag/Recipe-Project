Title: JALAPENO CORN CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Cheese/eggs, Kooknet
Yield: 4 Servings
17 oz Corn, Canned
17 oz Creamed Corn, Canned
1/2 c  Corn Meal
2 ea Eggs
6 tb Wesson Oil
1 ts Garlic Salt
2 ea Jalapenos, Chopped
1/2 ea Onion, Chopped
8 oz Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
