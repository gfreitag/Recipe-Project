Title: JADI'S GARLIC BREAD
Categories: Breads &, Muffins
*****  NONE  *****
Yield: 4 Servings  :

Preheat oven to 375-degrees. Mix together 1 cup softened butter, 1 cup
grated Parmesan cheese, 1/2 cup mayonaise, 5 garlic cloves (minced or
pressed), 3 T. fresh chopped parsley and 1/2 tsp. oregano. Spread the
mixture onto 1 loaf of French or Italian bread (cut lengthwise). Wrap
bread in aluminum foil. Bake for 20 minutes. Unwrap and place under
broiler to brown. (You may skip the broiler if you choose).
