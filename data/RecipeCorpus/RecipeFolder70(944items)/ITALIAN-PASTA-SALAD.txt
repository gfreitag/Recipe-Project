Title: ITALIAN PASTA SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
5 oz SPIRAL MACARONI
2 c  COOKED CUTUP CHICKEN
1 c  CHERRY TOMATOES, HALVED
1 c  BROCCOLI FLOWERETS
1/2 c  CHOPPED CUCUMBERS
1/2 c  THIN SLICED CARROTS
1/2 c  REDUCED CAL.ITALIAN DRESSING
COOK MACARONI ACCORDING TO PACKAGE DIRECTIONS. DRAIN. RINSE WITH COLD
WATER. MIX MACARONI WITH REMAINING INGREDIENTS. SERVE ON SALAD GREENS
IF DESIRED.
