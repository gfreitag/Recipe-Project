Title: JOSHUA TREE SUMMER COOLER   (PRICKLY PEAR SHAKE)
Categories: Beverages, Native amer
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Cactus fruits; peeled
1    Apple; diced
1    Pear; diced
1    Banana
1 c  Walnuts & almonds; chopped
1/4 c  Chia seeds
2 c  Milk
5 tb Honey
Add all the ingredients into a blender or food processor and blend
thoroughly.
