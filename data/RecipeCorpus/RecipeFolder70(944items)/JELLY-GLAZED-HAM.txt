Title: JELLY-GLAZED HAM
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 20 Servings
1    (8-lb) cooked boneless ham
1 1/2 c  Grape jelly
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 tb Prepared horseradish
Heat ham as directed on label or at 325F 1 1/2 hours. Remove from
oven and score, cutting diagonal lines 1/2-inch deep and 1-inch
apart. Cut across lines to form diamond shapes. Combine jelly,
mustard and horseradish. Spoon mixture, small amounts at a time, at
intervals, over ham throughout remainder of baking. Increase
temperature to 425F and bake until ham is browned and glazed, about
30 to 45 minutes.
