Title: ITALIAN SPAGHETTI SAUCE
Categories: Pasta, Italian, Sauces
Yield: 1 Gallon
2 lb Italian Sausage
1/2 c  Water
4 ea 28 oz. canned tomatoes
1 ea 8 oz. tomatoe paste
1/3 c  Wine, dry red
1 1/2 tb Parmesan
1 1/4 ts Sugar
2 tb Minced Onion
4 ea Garlic cloves
2 tb Oregano
2 tb Rosemary
1 tb Thyme
2 tb Basil
1 tb Parsley
2 ea Bay Leaf
Salt
Pepper
(Use fresh herbs, or cut the amount by 1/3) Pierce sausage link with
fork. Place in baking pan with 1/2 cup water and broil, turning once,
until browned.  Reserve pan drippings. Mash or cut tomatoes and add
remaining ingredients.  Mix in sausage links and pan drippings.
Simmer 4-6 hours or until mixture reaches desired consistency. Remove
bay leaves before serving. Freezes well.
