Title: JALEPENO CORN BREAD
Categories: Side dishes, Cheese
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Corn bread mix
2    Eggs
1 cn Creamed corn
2/3 c  Buttermilk
1    Onion; chopped
1    Margerine
2    Jalepeno peppers; chopped
16 oz Cheddar cheese; shredded
1 lb Ground beef; browned
Recipe by: Wayne Preston Allen Saute onion with stick of butter in
microwave. In large bowl, mix remaining ingredients. pour into
casserole and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
