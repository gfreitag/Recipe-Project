Title: ITALIAN MOCHA ESPRESSO
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 7 Cups
1    Cup instant coffee
1    Cup sugar
4 1/2    Cup nonfat dry milk
1/2    Cup cocoa
Stir ingredients together.  Process in a blender until powdered. Use 2
Tablespoons to one small cup of hot water. Serve in demitasse cups.
Makes about 7 cups of mix.
