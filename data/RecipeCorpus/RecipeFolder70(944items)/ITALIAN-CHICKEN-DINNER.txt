Title: ITALIAN CHICKEN DINNER
Categories: Pot roast, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1 sm Cabbage; cut into wedges
1 md Onion; sliced, seperated
-into rings
4 oz Mushrooms; canned, drained
2 tb Tapioca; quick-cooking
3 lb Chicken; cut-up, skinned
-and frozen
15 oz Spagetti sauce, bottled
-extra thick & meatless
Parmesan cheese; grated
In crockpot, place cabbage wedges, onion, and mushrooms. Sprinkle
tapioca over vegetables.  Place frozen chicken pieces atop
vegetables, pour spagetti sauce over chicken. Cover; cook on low-heat
setting for 10-12 hours or on high-heat setting for 4 1/2-5 1/2
hours. Transfer to a serving platter.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
