Title: JICAMA WALDORF SALAD
Categories: Wasnone
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lg Jicama
6    Celery stalks -- sliced
2 c  Pecan halves -- toasted
2 c  Green seedless grapes --
Halved
6    Oranges -- peeled and diced
16 oz Plain yogurt
Honey
Lime juice
You have a choice of grating the jicama with the grater on your food
processor or chopping it up, apple style. Combine all the ingredients;
season the yogurt with honey and lime juice to taste; toss it all
together.
