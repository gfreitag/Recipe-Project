Title: ITALIAN HERB DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dressings, Italian
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Vegetable Oil
1/2 c  Wine or White Vinegar
1    Clove garlic
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dried basil leaves
1/2 ts Dried oragano leaves
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper
Combine ingredients in jar.  Cover and shake well. Chill to blend
flavors. Remove garlic.  Shake again before serving. Makes 1 Cup.

From: Lancaster Farming Shared By: Pat Stockett
