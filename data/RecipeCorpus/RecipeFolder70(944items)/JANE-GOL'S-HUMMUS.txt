Title: JANE GOL'S HUMMUS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 2 Servings
2 c  16 oz Canned chick peas
3 tb Tahini paste
1 cl Garlic, minced
2    Lemons, juiced
Salt to taste
Olive oil
Combine all of the ingredients except the olive oil in a food
processor or blender. Add 1/2 cup water. Process to a thick puree.
Transfer to a bowl and cover with a thin film of olive oil to keep
the hummus from crusting. Refrigerate until ready to use. Makes: 2
cups
