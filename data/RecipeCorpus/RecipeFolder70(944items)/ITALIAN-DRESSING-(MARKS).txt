Title: ITALIAN DRESSING (MARKS)
Categories: Diabetic, Salads, Sauces
Yield: 8 Servings
1/4 c  Chicken broth
1 tb Virgin olive oil;
2 tb Red wine vinegar;
2 ts Dijon-style mustard;
1 cl Garlic; minced
1/2 ts Dried leaf basil;
pn Dried leaf thyme
Salt and pepper to taste;
Mix all ingredients together in a small jar and shake well. Chill
before serving.  Makes 1/2 cup = 8 serving. Food Exchange per
serving: FREE; CAL: 17; CHO: 0mg; CAR: 0g; PRO: Og; SOD: 43mg; FAT:
2g;

Source: Light & Easy Diabetes Cuisine by Betty Marks Brought to you
and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
