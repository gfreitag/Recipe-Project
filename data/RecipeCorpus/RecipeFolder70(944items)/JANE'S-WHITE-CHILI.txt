Title: JANE'S WHITE CHILI
Categories: Jane, Spicy, Chicken
Yield: 10 Servings
2 tb Butter
2 lb Chicken, boneless, chopped
2 md Onions, chopped
1 c  Chicken broth
8 oz Green chilies
2 cl Garlic, chopped
2 ts Cumin
1 ts Oregano, dried
1/4 ts Cloves, ground
1/4 ts Cayenne (or more)
48 oz Jar of Great Northern Beans
3 c  Monterey Jack, shredded
Saute chicken, onions and garlic in butter until chicken is done. Mix
in seasonings and chilies and saute for a few more minutes. Add this
mixture with chicken broth and beans to crock pot. Cook on low for
4-8 hours. Add cheese just before serving. personal recipe of jane s
herr, MJPU13B@prodigy.com
