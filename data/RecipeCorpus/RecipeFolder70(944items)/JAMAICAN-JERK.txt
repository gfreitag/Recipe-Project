Title: JAMAICAN JERK
Categories: Jamaican, Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Scallion; chopped
1 ts Salt
4 ts Jamaican pimento (allspice)
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
4    Habañero peppers; preferred,
-else Jalapeño
1 ts Black pepper
4    Garlic cloves
2 tb Grated ginger
2 tb Lime juice
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
Place all ingredients in a food processor fitted with a metal blade.
Process at high speed for 10 to 15 seconds.  Store in a non-reactive
container; this is very corrosive to metals!
