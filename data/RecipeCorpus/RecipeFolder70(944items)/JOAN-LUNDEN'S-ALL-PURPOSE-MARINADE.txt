Title: JOAN LUNDEN'S ALL-PURPOSE MARINADE
Categories: Marinades, Dkuhnen msn
Yield: 3 Cups
2 c  Dijon mustard
9 cl Garlic, crushed
1 tb Lemon pepper
2 ts Fine herb seasoning
1/2 c  Wine vinegar
1/2 c  Low-fat or non-fat italian
Dressing
1/3 c  Reduced sodium soy sauce
MMMMM---------------------OPTIONAL FLAVORING--------------------------
2 tb Liquid smoke
4    Shakes hot pepper sauce
Red pepper flakes to taste
In a bowl, whisk together the mustard, garlic, lemon pepper and
herbs. Add the vinegar, italian dressing, soy sauce and any optional
flavoring. Source:  TV Guide, May 25, 1996.
