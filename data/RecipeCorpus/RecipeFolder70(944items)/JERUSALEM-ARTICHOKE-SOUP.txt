Title: JERUSALEM ARTICHOKE SOUP
Categories: Soups, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Jerusalem artichokes
6 c  Chicken broth
1 c  Thinly sliced green onions
Salt/pepper to taste
2 tb Minced fresh dill
Scrub Jerusalem artichokes and cook in simmering water 30-40
minutes, until tender. Drain and discard cooking liquid. Peel and
mash artichokes and place in a large saucepan. Stir in chicken broth
and green onions.
Simmer for about 15 minutes; season to taste with salt/pepper and
serve sprinkled with dill.

Source: Spirit of the Harvest
