Title: JADE TREE SALAD
Categories: Greensalads, Restaurants, Hawaiian
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Safflower oil
1/4 c  Tarragon vinegar
2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Parmesan
8 oz Sliced water chestnuts
1/4 c  Sesame seeds; toasted
1 lg Head iceberg lettuce
Fresh ground black pepper
Lightly toast sesame seeds in skillet over low heat. Combine all
ingredients in a large jar; shake to blend. Chill at least 3 hours.
Serve over crisp, chilled lettuce torn (not cut) in bite-size piece
Toss with pepper. Per serving 241 calories. Waikiki Beach, Hawaii
