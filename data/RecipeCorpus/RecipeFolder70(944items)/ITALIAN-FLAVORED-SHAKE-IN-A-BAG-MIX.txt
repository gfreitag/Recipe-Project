Title: ITALIAN FLAVORED SHAKE IN-A-BAG MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Chicken Pieces -- OR
1 lb Pork Chops -- OR
1 lb Beef Cubes
1 tb Olive Oil -- (optional)
1 c  Healthy Bread Crumbs
(index)
1/2 c  Grated Romano OR Parmesan
Cheese
1/4 c  Parsley -- chopped
2 tb Oregano
1 tb Garlic Powder
1 tb Basil
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1.  Preheat oven to 400^ degrees. 2.  Coat meat with milk and egg, as
described in the recipe for "Shake-In-A-Bag Mix for Chicken (index),
OR brush the meat with olive oil before coating. Combine crumbs,
cheese, and spices in a paper bag, drop meat into the bag. Arrange
meat in a shallow baking pan.  Bake 1 hour. YIELD: 18 Ounces Source:
Cheaper and Better
