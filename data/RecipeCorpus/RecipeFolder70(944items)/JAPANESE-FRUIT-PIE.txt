Title: JAPANESE FRUIT PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
1 ea Cube margarine,melted,cooled
1 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs,beaten
1/2 ts Vinegar
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Chopped dates
1/2 c  Coconut
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 ea Unbaked pie shell
Combine margarine,sugar,eggs,vinegar and vanilla in bowl.Mix well.
Stir in dates,coconut and nuts.Pour into pie shell.Bake in 300
degree oven for 45 to 50 minutes or until brown.Makes 6 to 8 servings.
