Title: JELL-O POKE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Snacks
Yield: 6 Servings
1    White or yellow cake mix
-(two layer)
(baked and cooled)
1 c  Boiling water
1 pk Jell-O (any flavor - 4
-serving size)
1/2 c  Cold water
FROM THE KITCHEN OF: Gary & Margie Hartford [Eugene, Oregon]

Thawed whipped topping or prepared frosting

Place cake layers, top sides up, in two clean 8" or 9" layer pans;
prick each cake wht large two pronged fork at 1/2 inch intervals.
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Add cold water and spoon over cake
layers. Chill 4 hours. Dip one pan in warm water for 10 seconds;
unmold onto serving plate. Frost. Unmold second layer; place on first
layer. Frost. Refrigerate. Garnish as desired.

From: General Foods Corp. Shared 7/93
