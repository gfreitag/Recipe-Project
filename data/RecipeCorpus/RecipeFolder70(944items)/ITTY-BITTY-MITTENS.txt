Title: ITTY BITTY MITTENS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
Brown Sugar Sweeties Recipe
Roll dough out; cut into 3" mitten shapes. Bake 8-10 minutes. Paint
cooled cookies with red & green Royal Icing diluted with a few drops
of water to proper consistency. Submitted By THESERVS@GATE.NET
