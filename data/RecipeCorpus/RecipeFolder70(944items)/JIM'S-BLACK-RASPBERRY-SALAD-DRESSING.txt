Title: JIM'S BLACK RASPBERRY SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 1 Cup
1/2 c  Olive Oil
5 ds Tabasco sauce
1 tb Black Raspberry Preserves
1/4 c  Balsamic Vinegar
In a small bowl, whisk all ingredients together and drizzle over the
top of the salad. That's it! Enjoy

Source: "Garlic" class at Omalia Cooking School, Steve Dillback,
instructor
