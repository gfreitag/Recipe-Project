Title: JAMAICAN COLE SLAW
Categories: Miamiherald, Salads, Ethnic, Jamaican
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM-----------------FORMATTED BY LISA CRAWFORD----------------------
4 c  Shredded cabbage
1/4 c  Shredded carrots
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Mayonaise
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Cider vinegar
1 tb Dry jerk seasoning
Combine the cabbage, carrots and nuts in a large bowl; set aside. Mix
togethre the mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, and seasoning. Spoon over
cabbage mixture and toss well. Cover and chill before serving.

Nutritional info per serving: 177 cal; 3g pro, 8.5g carb, 16g
fat(76%), 3.5g fiber, 8mg chol, 197g sodium

Source:  Jerk: Barbecue from Jamaica by Helen Willinsky
