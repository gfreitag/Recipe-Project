Title: ITALIAN-FLAVORED SHRIMP DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sour cream
2 ts Lemon juice
1 tb Italian salad dressing mix
4 1/2 oz Tiny shrimp (1 can) rinsed
And drained
1/3 c  Finely chopped green pepper
3 oz Cream cheese softened
Combine sour cream and cream cheese in medium bowl; beat until
fluffy. Beat in salad dressing mix and lemon juice. Stir in shrimp
and bell pepper. Chill, covered at least one hour.

Makes about 2 1/2 cups.
