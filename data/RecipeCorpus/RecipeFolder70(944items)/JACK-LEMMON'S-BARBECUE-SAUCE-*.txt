Title: JACK LEMMON'S BARBECUE SAUCE *
Categories: Sauces, Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
3/4 qt Catsup
1 ts English mustard
1 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1 tb Worcesteshire sauce
1    Clove garlic
1/2 md Onion
4 tb Lemon juice
1    Bay leaf
4 tb Sharp salad dressing
3/4 c  Maple syrup
1 c  Butter
Chili powder
Mix, bring to a boil and simmer one hour. Remove onion and bay leaf.
