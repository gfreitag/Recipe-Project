Title: ITALIAN SAUSAGE WITH GRILLED ONIONS~ PEPPERS~
Categories: Sausage
4 lg Italian sausage links --
sliced in half
lengthwise
Yield: 1 Servings
:
:
1 lg green bell pepper -- cut
into 1 1/2 "
wide strips
:
:
1 lg red bell pepper -- cut into
1 1/2    red bell pepper -- cut into
wide strips
:
1 lg vidalia onion -- cut in
center and
then in 1" slices
:
:
2    jalapenos -- quartered
1 c  fresh pineapple -- cut in
lg chunks
:
1/4 c  fresh pineapple juice
1 TB fresh picked rosemary
salt
freshly ground pepper
:
:

Prepare a wood or charcoal grill and allow it to burn down to embers.

Toss vegetables and pineapple in olive oil and season to taste with
salt and pepper. Grill with sausage for 5 minutes or until vegetables
are tender and sausage is brown and heated through. Place in a small
mixing bowl and add pineapple juice and rosemary. Toss and season to
taste with salt and pepper. Serve over mashed potatoes.
