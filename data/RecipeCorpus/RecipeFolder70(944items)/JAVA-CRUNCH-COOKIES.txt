Title: JAVA CRUNCH COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 38 Cookies
1 1/2 c  Flour; all-purpose
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
1 tb Instant coffee
3/4 c  Butter; softened
1 ea Egg
1 1/2 c  Flaked coconut
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.  In large bowl combine all
ingredients except coconut.  Blend well with mixer. Stir in coconut;
mix thoroughly.
Drop by rounded teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake at 350
degrees for 10 to 12 minutes. Cool. Makes 36 to 42 cookies.
