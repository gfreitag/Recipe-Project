Title: JAMES BEARD'S BROWNIES *
Categories: Brownies, Celebrity
Yield: 16 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
4 oz Semi sweet chocolate
2    Eggs
1 c  Maple syrup -OR-
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Flour; sifted
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
Combine all ingredients, except walnuts and blend until smooth. Add
walnuts and pour batter into a greased and floured 9x11" pan. Bake at
325~ for 30 minutes. DO NOT OVERBAKE.
