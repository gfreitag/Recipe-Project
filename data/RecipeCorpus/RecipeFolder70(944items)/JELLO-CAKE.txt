Title: JELLO CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 24 Servings
1 pk Strawberry jello <4 serv>
1 pk Cake mix-white <NOT yellow>
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1    Envelope dream whip mix
1 sm Package ins.van.pudding mix
1 1/2 c  Cold milk
1 ts Vanilla
Dissolve jello in 3/4 c boiling water. Add 1/2c cold water and set
aside at room temp.

Mix and bake cake as directed in 9 x 13 pan. Cool 20-25 mins. Poke
holes in cake with end of wooden spoon, about 1 1/2 inches apart.
With cup, slowly pour jello mixture into holes.

Refrigerate cake and prepare topping. In chilled bowl, blend whipped
topping mix, pudding, cold milk and vanilla til stiff, 3-8 minutes.

Immediately frost cake.  Store in frig and serve chilled.
