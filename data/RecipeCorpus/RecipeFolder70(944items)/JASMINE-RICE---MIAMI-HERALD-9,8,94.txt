Title: JASMINE RICE - MIAMI HERALD 9/8/94
Categories: Grains
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Jasmine rice
3 1/2 c  Water
Rinse rice throughly in several changes of water.  In a large
saucepan, add the rice and water, cover and bring to a high boil.
Turn down the heat to the lowest setting and let the rice cook for 20
minutes. Fluff rice, cover and let stand 5 min
