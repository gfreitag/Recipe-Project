Title: JULIE'S WHOOPSIE PIES
Categories: Desserts, Cakes, Chocolate
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------WHOOPSIE PIES----------------------------
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
2    Egg; yolks, beaten
5 tb Cocoa
2 c  Flour; sifted all-purpose
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Soda
1 ts Salt
1 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
3/4 c  Shortening
3/4 c  Confectioners sugar
1 c  Marshmallow Fluff
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
FOR THE FILLING:
Cream shortening and sugar with the Marshmallow Fluff; stir in salt
and vanilla. FOR THE PIES:
Cream the shortening and sugar; beat egg yolks into creamed
mixture. Mix together and sift flour, cocoa, baking powder, soda and
salt; add to the creamed mixture alternately with milk. Stir in
vanilla. Drop
