Title: JELLO WITH SWIMMING FISH
Categories: Desserts, Misc
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk (4 serving size) blue Jello
3/4 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Cold water
Ice cubes
Gummy fish
Dissolve Jello in boiling water.  Combine cold water and ice cubes to
make 1 1/4 cups ice and water.  Add to Jello, stir until slightly
thick. Remove any unmelted ice. If it is still too thin, refrigerate
for a few minutes. Pour Jello into clear glasses. Drop in gummy fish.
Refrigerate until set, about 1 hour. Makes
4    servings.
