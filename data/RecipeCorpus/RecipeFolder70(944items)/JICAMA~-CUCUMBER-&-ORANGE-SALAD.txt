Title: JICAMA~ CUCUMBER & ORANGE SALAD
Categories: Salad
Yield: 8 Servings
3/4 lb Jicama, peeled and cubed
1    Cucumber, seeded and thickly
Sliced
2    Oranges, peeled and cubed
3    Green onions, sliced
1/4 c  Lime juice
3 tb Orange juice
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Chopped mint
1. Combine the jicama, cucumber, oranges and green onions in a glass
bowl. 2. Combine the lime juice, orange juice, chili powder and salt;
mix and pour over the salad ingredients. 3. Sprinkle the mint over
the salad, toss and chill for at least 2 hours.
