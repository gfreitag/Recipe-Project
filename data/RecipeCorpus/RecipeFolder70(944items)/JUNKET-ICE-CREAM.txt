Title: JUNKET ICE CREAM
Categories: Desserts, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
4 qt Milk; whole
3 c  Sugar
1 tb Vanilla
3 ea Junket tablets
2 tb Water; cold
1 pt Cream
Dissolve tablets in cold water. Have milke lukewarm. Add sugar and
dissolve tablets and vanilla. Put in freezer until set. When thick,
add 1 pt cream and freeze. Makes 1 1/2 G.

Source: Doris A. Peters, Biglick Grange, Hancock County, OH
