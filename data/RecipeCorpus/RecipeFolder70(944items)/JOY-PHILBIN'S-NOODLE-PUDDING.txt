Title: JOY PHILBIN'S NOODLE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Pasta, Celebrity
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Broad wide noodles -- cook
1/4 c  Sugar
1 lb Cottage cheese -- soft
2 ts Vanilla
1 c  White raisins
7    Eggs -- beaten
16 oz Sour cream
1/4 lb Butter -- melt
Bread crumbs
Mix all except crumbs and put in 9x12 pyrex baking dish. Top with
crumbs and bake at 350~ for 45 minutes. Source: Live with Regis and
Kathie.
