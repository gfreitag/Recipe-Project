Title: JOAN'S FAVORITE TERIYAKI SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Misc
Yield: 2 Cups
1 1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Soy sauce
1 ts Fresh grated ginger
1 cl Garlic crushed
1 tb Seseme seed oil
Mix all in screw top jar and shake well to dissolve sugar. Marinade
steak or chicken overnight or use as a glaze or basting sauce when
baking or barbecuing. Most delicious.
