Title: JELLO PUDDING FUDGE
Categories: Candies, Fudge
Yield: 1 Servings
-Patricia Dwigans fwds07a-
1 pk Jello chocolate pudding and
- pie filling; not instant
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Confectioners' sugar; sifted
1/4 c  Nuts; chopped
Combine pudding, butter and milk.  Bring to a full boil. Boil
gently for 1 minute; stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Quickly
blend in sugar; add nuts. Pour into greased 8x4-inch pan. Cool; cut
into squares. Makes 1 lb.

Note.  Any flavor of pudding will do.
