Title: JOANNE WOODWARD LOBSTER TAILS IN PATTY SHELLS
Categories: Shellfish, Sauces, Celebrity
Yield: 6 Servings
PATTI - VDRJ67A-----
6    Frozen lobster tails -- or
2 c  Lobster -- cook
2 qt Salt water -- boiling
WHITE SAUCE-----
4 tb Butter
4 tb Flour
1 c  Milk
1 c  Cream
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Ripe olives -- chop
2 ds Pepper
1 tb Red pimento -- dice
1/2 c  Sliced canned mushrooms
1/2 c  Sauterne
1 ds Parsely
6    Patty shells
