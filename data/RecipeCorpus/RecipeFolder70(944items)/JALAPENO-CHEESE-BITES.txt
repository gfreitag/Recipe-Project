Title: JALAPENO CHEESE BITES
Categories: Appetizers, Silver p, Tvg
Yield: 14 Servings
-The New Basics
2    Jalapeno peppers, cored
-seeded, minced
1 1/2 c  Monterey Jack, grated (6 oz)
1 1/2 c  Sharp cheddar, grated (6 oz)
2 tb Cilantro, minced
Combine jalapenos, and cheeses in mixing bowl. Bland well. Shape into
3/4" balls, roll in cilantro. Chill loosely covered, 2-3 hours before
serving. Formatted by T. Grant, HWWK11b, from The New Basics.
