Title: ITALIAN-STYLE RED SAUCE
Categories: Italian, Pasta, Sauces and
Yield: 1 Servings
14 oz Can Chopped Plum Tomatoes
6 oz Can Tomato Paste
2 tb Red Wine Vinegar
1 tb Olive Oil
1 ts Dried Oregano
1 ts Dried Basil
1 ts Dried Parsley
2 md Cloves Garlic
1    Cube Beef Bullion
1/4 ts Ground Ginger
1/8 ts Ground Black Pepper
Salt -- to taste
Mix everything together in a small saucepan on medium heat until it
starts bubbling.  Reduce to simmer for 30 minutes to 1 hour, stirring
occassionally.
