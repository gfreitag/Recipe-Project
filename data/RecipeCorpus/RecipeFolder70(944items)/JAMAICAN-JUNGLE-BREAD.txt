Title: JAMAICAN JUNGLE BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Of all-purpose flour
1 c  Of 3 minute brand quick oats
1/2 c  Of sugar
1 ts Of baking powder
1 ts Of baking soda
1 ts Of cinnamon
1/2 ts Of salt
1/2 c  Of flaked coconut
1/2 c  Of chopped walnuts or
-almonds or pecans (toasted)
3    Mashed bananas
1 cn Of pineapple (8 ounces)
1/4 c  Of vegetable oil
2    Eggs
Mix flour, oats, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, salt,
coconut and nuts in a large bowl.

Mix bananas, pineapple, oil and eggs in a medium bowl

Add all to large bowl and stir, but not too much

Bake on a greased pan at 350 degrees for 50 minutes

For the glaze, mix 1/2 cup of confectioners' sugar with 1 T of butter
and add either 1/2 t coconut or vanilla extract.

Add 2 t of milk

Pour this glaze over the bread.

Makes one loaf.
