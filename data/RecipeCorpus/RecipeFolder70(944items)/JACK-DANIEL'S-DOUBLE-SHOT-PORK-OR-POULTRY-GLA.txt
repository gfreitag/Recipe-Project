Title: JACK DANIEL'S DOUBLE SHOT PORK OR POULTRY GLA
Categories: Meats, Poultry, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  JACK DANIEL'S WHISKEY
1 1/3 c  HONEY
3 tb LEMON JUICE
1/2 ts BLACK PEPPER, freshly ground
Combine all ingredients; mix well. Serve as a sauce or glaze for
grilled chicken or pork. Makes 2 cups.
