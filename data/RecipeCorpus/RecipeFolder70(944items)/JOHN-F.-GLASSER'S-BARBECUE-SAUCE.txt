Title: JOHN F. GLASSER'S BARBECUE SAUCE
Categories: Bar-b-q, Sauces
Yield: 3 Servings
1/4 c  Ketchup
1 c  Tomato juice
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Water
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Paprika
1/8 ts Ground cayenne pepper
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Salt
2 ts Brown sugar
1/2 c  Chopped onion
Combine all ingredients and simmer 15 minutes.

Sufficient for 3 lbs. of ribs.

Sauce was the creation of my late father-in-law.

Enjoy! - Jeff Duke
