Title: JOYCE'S CASSEROLE
Categories: Beef, Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 tb Oil
1 cn Cream mushroom soup
10 oz Frozen mixed vegetables
2 tb Soy sauce
Pepper to taste
1 c  Onion, chopped
2 c  Celery, sliced
3/4 c  Water
2 c  Cooked rice
1 ts Salt
3    Ox chow mein noodles
Brown beef and onions in oil. Cook mixed vegetables in 3/4 cup water
for 10 minutes. Mix meat, onions, mixed vegetables, liquid, soup,
rice, seasonings and noodles in 2-quart casserole. Cover and bake 25
minutes at 350 degrees or microwave 10 minutes on reheat.
