Title: JACKSON'S VANILLA WAFER BANANA PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Box vanilla wafers
4 tb Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1 lb Bananas (3-4) large
2 c  Scalded milk
2    Eggs (separated)
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Sugar for meringue
Mix flour, 1/2 cup sugar and salt in top of double boiler; slowly
stir hot milk and cook until mixture thickens, stirring constantly.
Stir a little bit of hot mixture into beaten egg yolks, pour back
into double boiler, cook about 2 minutes.  Remove from heat, add
vanilla and cool until just warm. Alternate layers of vanilla wafers,
sliced bananas an pudding in a baking dish.  Beat 2 egg whites until
stiff.  Pile lightl pudding. Bake in hot (400F) oven about 8 minutes
or until golden brow
