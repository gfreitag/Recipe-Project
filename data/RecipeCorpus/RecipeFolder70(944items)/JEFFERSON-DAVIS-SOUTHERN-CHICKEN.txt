Title: JEFFERSON DAVIS SOUTHERN CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 2 Servings
1    Broiler-fryer chicken --
Cleaned --  cut up
1 c  Cracker crumbs -- finely
Ground
5 ts Butter
Place the chicken pieces in a pressurer cooker and cook for 20
minutes. Remove the chicken and lightly bread with cracker crumbs.
Melt the butter in a frying pan. Cook thechicken until lightly brown
(about 5 minutes). Remove and serve at once. Walt
