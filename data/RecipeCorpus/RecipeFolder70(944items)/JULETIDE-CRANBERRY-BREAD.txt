Title: JULETIDE CRANBERRY BREAD
Categories: Breads, Fruits, Christmas, Midwestern, Norwegian
Yield: 1 Loaf
2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 c  Cranberries, sliced in half
1 c  Chopped nuts
Juice and grated rind of 2
-oranges
2 tb Shortening
1/2 c  Boiling water
1    Egg, beaten
Preheat the oven to 350F. Sift the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking
soda, and salt together. Add the cranberries and the nuts.

Combine the juice, rind, shortening, and water.  Add the egg to the
juice and blend into the cranberry mixture.

Pour into a buttered loaf pan, and bake 1 hour, until a toothpick
comes out dry.
