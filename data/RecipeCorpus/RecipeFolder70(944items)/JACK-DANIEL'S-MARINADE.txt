Title: JACK DANIEL'S MARINADE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  JACK DANIEL'S WHISKEY
1/4 c  SOY SAUCE
1/4 c  DIJON-STYLE MUSTARD
1/4 c  GREEN ONIONS; minced
1/4 c  LT. BROWN SUGAR, packed
1 ts SALT
1 ds WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE
BLACK PEPPER
Combine all ingredients. Blend well. Use to marinate shrimp or
scallops for 1 hour or beef, chicken or pork in the refrigerator
overnight. Use to baste the shellfish or meat as it is grilled or
broiled. Makes about 1-1/4 cups.
