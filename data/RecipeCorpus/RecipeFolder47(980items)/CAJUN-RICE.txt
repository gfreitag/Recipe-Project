Title: CAJUN RICE
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb MAZOLA corn oil
1 c  Uncooked rice
1 lg Onion, chopped
1 lg Green pepper, chopped
1 tb Minced garlic
1 cn (14 1/2 to 16 oz) stewed
-tomatoes, undrained
1 1/4 c  Water
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
1.  In medium skillet heat corn oil over medium-high heat. Add rice,
onion, pepper and garlic; cook, stirring, 3 minutes. Stir in
undrained tomatoes, water and hot pepper sauce, breaking tomatoes
with a spoon. Bring to a boil.

2.  Reduce heat.  Cover and simmer, stirring occasionally, 20 minutes
or until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.

Makes 8 servings
