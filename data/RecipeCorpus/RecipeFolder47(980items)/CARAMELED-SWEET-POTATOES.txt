Title: CARAMELED SWEET POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Favorite
Yield: 8 Servings
5 md Sweet potatoes, cooked and
-peeled
1 ts Salt
1 c  Brown sugar
3 tb Flour
2 tb Butter
8    Marshmallows
1/2 c  Nuts
1 c  Half-and-half
Cut sweet potatoes in half lengthwise and place in a 9x13-inch pan.
Mix together salt, brown sugar, flour and butter until crumbly and
sprinkle over sweet potatoes. Arrange marshmallows on top and
sprinkle with nuts. Pour half-and-half over top.

Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
