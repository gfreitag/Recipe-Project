Title: BUTTERSCOTCH COOKIES #2
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Shortening
4 ea Eggs
5 c  Flour
3/4 ts Baking soda
3/4 ts Cream tartar
1 ts Vanilla
Filling; cook together:
1 c  Dates
1/2 c  Nuts
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
Make into rolls and place in refrigerator for a few hours. Cut into
thin slices and place cooled filling between slices. Bake in a slow
oven.

Note: Slow oven is 300 - 350 F.

Source: Mrs. C. W. Roberts, Morgan Grange, Butler County, OH
