Title: CANTALOUPE SOUP (SOPA DE MELON ESCRIBE)
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Half and half
1 c  Cooked, peeled and diced
-potato
3 c  Peeled and diced cantaloupe
1/4 c  Dry sherry
1 pn Of salt
Keywords: soup

Place the half and half, potato, and cantaloupe in the blender. Blend
to a smooth puree.  Stir in sherry. Season to taste. Serve chilled.
If desired, garnish with a sprinkle of nutmeg or lime slice.

Serves 6.
