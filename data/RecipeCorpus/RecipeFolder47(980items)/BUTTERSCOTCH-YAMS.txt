Title: BUTTERSCOTCH YAMS
Categories: Low-fat/low, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
6 md Yams -- cook,peel, quarter
1/2 c  Light corn syrup -- or dark
1/2 c  Firmly packed light brown
Sugar
1/4 c  Evaporated skim milk
1 ts Butter
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Preheat oven to 325F.  Arrange yams in single layer in 13x9x2 inch
baking dish.  Bake 15 min.  Meanwhile, combine remaining ingredients
in a med. saucepan. Stirring constantly, bring to boil over med.
heat, boilf for 5 min.  Por over yams.  Return dish to oven and bake,
basting frequently, for at least 15 min.
