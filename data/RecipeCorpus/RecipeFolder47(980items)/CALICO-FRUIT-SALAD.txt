Title: CALICO FRUIT SALAD
Categories: Salads, Fruits, Dressings
Yield: 6 Servings
1 qt Ice Berg Lettuce Chunks;
-(Otherwise Known As Head
-Lettuce)
2 c  Tart Apples; Chopped
2 lg Bananas; Sliced
1/2 c  Raisins
MMMMM-------------------PEANUT BUTTER DRESSING------------------------
1/4 c  Peanut Butter
3 tb Honey
1/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
FOR THE SALAD:

Combine the lettuce and fruit, tossing lightly to mix.

FOR THE DRESSING:

Combine the peanut butter and honey, then gradually add the milk. Add
the dressing and mix well until well blended. Chill until serving
time.

VARIATION:

The dressing may also be served as a dip with apple and pear slices or
banana chunks.
