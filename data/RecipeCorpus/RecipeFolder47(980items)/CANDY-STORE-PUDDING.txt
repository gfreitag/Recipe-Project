Title: CANDY STORE PUDDING
Categories: Easy, Dessert
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Cold milk
1 pk (3.9 oz.) instant chocolate
-pudding mix
1 c  Whipping cream, whipped
1/2    To 1 cup miniature
-marshmallows
1/4    To 1/2 cup chopped salted
-peanuts
In a bowl, whisk milk and pudding mix for 2 minutes. Fold in whipped
cream, marshmallows and peanuts. Spoon into individual dessert
dishes. Refrigerate until serving.  Yield: 4 servings.  Typed in
