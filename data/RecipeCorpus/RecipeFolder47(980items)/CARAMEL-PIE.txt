Title: CARAMEL PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Butter
1 c  Hot water
1 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Vanilla
2 ea Egg yolks
bring brown sugar and butter to a boil in saucepan. Thin out with the
hot water.Mix the cornstarch,vanilla and yolks of 2 eggs with a
little water and add to hot mixture, stir until thickened. Cool and
put in baked pie shell.Make meringue and put on top.Brown in 350 oven
10 min.
