Title: BUTTERY PEAS & ZUCCHINI
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
10 oz Frozen peas in butter
-sauce
2 tb Water
1 md Zucchini, cut into thin
-strips
1 tb Diced pimiento
Cook peas according to package directions. Pour into a serving bowl;
cover with plastic wrap and set aside. Meanwhile, in a skillet,
simmer water and zucchini over medium heat until water is evaporated
and zucchini is tender. Stir in peas and pimiento.

Yield: 2 servings.

Meals for two

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
