Title: CAFE' MEXICANO
Categories: Mexican, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Coffee liqueur
1/2 oz Brandy
1 ts Chocolate syrup
1 ea Dash ground cinnamon
1 ea Hot coffee
1 ea Sweetened whipped cream
Combine coffee liqueur, brandy, chocolate syrup and cinnamon in a
coffee cup or mug. Fill to the top with hot coffee. Top with whipped
cream.
