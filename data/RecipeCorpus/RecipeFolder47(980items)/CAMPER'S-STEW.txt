Title: CAMPER'S STEW
Categories: Camping, Soups
Yield: 2 Servings
1 cn Whole new potatoes
1 cn Green beans
1 cn Diced carrots
1 cn Meatballs with gravy
8 oz Can tomato sauce
2 tb Dehydrated onion
Drain liquid from vegetables, save 1 cup.  Combine all ingredients,
bring to a boil and serve. Delicious also with "biscuit mix"
dumplings. Follow directions on package.  Serves 2 to 3.  Easily
doubled.
