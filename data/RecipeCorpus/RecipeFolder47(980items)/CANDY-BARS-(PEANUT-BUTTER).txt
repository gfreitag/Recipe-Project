Title: CANDY BARS (PEANUT BUTTER)
Categories: Candies
Yield: 16 Servings
2 c  PEANUT BUTTER
1 c  HONEY
2 c  NON-FAT DRY MILK
1/2 c  CHOCOLATE CHIPS
IN A LARGE BOWL BEAT TOGETHER THE PEANUT BUTTER AND HONEY TILL
SMOOTH. STIR IN DRY MILK. PAT MIXTURE INTO 8 X 8 INCH PAN. DRIZZLE
WITH MELTED CHOCOLATE CHIPS. CHILL FOR 2 HOURS MINIMUM. CUT INTO 1" X
4" BARS
