Title: CAPER-SAFFRON MAYONAISE - BON APPETIT
Categories: Bon appetit, Condiment
1 T  Water
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 t  Packed saferon threads
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Drained capers plus
1 t  Caper liquid
2 T  Mixed chopped fresh herbs
(such as thyme, oregano
And parsley) or 2 t dried
:
:
2 t  Fresh lemon juice
1 1/2 t  Minced garlic

Bring water and saffron to simmer in small saucepan. Remove from
heat; let stand 10 minutes. Place mayonnaise in small bowl; whisk in
saffron mixture. Add remaining ingredients; stir until blended.
Season with salt and pepper. Cover and chill at least 2 hours. (Can
be made 3 days ahead. Keep chilled.)
