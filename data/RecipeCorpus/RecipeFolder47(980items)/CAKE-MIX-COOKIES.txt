Title: CAKE MIX COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Cakes, Round robin
Yield: 48 Servings
1 pk Cake mix (any flavor)
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
MMMMM------------------ANY 1 OR 2 OF FOLLOWING-----------------------
6 oz Mini chocolate chips
6 oz Butterscotch chips
6 oz Peanut butter chips
1/2 c  Coconut
1/2 c  Raisins
1 c  Toffee chips
1 c  Nuts chopped
Preheat oven to 350~F. Combine all ingredients and mix well. Drop by
rounded teaspoons onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 9 - 12 minutes.
Makes about 4 dozen cookies.
