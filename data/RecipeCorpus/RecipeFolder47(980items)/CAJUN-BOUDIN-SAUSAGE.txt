Title: CAJUN BOUDIN SAUSAGE
Categories: Cajun, Sausage
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Ground beef
2 lb Ground pork
4 c  Cooked rice
4 lg Onions, minced
10    Cloves garlic, minced (10 to
-25)
6 sm Hot Chile Peppers, (Serrano,
-Etc), minced
2    Stalks celery, minced
1 lg Sweet red pepper, minced
1 lg Sweet green pepper, minced
2 md Leeks, minced
6    Green onions, minced
1 c  Minced parsley
1/3 c  Minced cilantro
1 ts Red pepper flakes
1 ts Black pepper
2 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Sage
1/4 ts Rosemary
1/4 ts Savory
Mix thoroughly. Stuff into casings. Posted to MasterCook Mailing List
