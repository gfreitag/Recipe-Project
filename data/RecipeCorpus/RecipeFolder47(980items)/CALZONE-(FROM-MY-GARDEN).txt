Title: CALZONE (FROM MY GARDEN)
Categories: Wasnone
1    recipe pizza dough
Yield: 1 Servings
24 oz Ricotta cheese
4 TB chopped herbs: rosemary --
parley, sage
:
2 ts chopped garlic
Pepper
Salt
:
:
1    egg and 1 yolk
2 ts water

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Roll out pizza dough and cut 7-inch
circles from the dough. In large bowl combine Ricotta cheese, herbs
and garlic. Mix well and season with salt and pepper. In small bowl
place eggs and water .. Beat together well. Place 3/4 cup of cheese
mixture onto dough. Brush edges of dough with egg wash then fold over
to form calzone. Press edges together to seal. Brush top with egg
wash and bake at for 25 minutes, or until golden.
