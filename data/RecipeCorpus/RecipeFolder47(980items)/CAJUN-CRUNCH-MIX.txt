Title: CAJUN CRUNCH MIX
Categories: Snacks, Desserts, Famfavorite
Yield: 8 Servings
3 tb BUTTER
1/4 c  HOT CAYENNE PEPPER SAUCE
1 tb WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE
1/4 c  GRATED PARMESAN CHEESE
2 cn DURKEE FRENCH FRIED ONIONS
2 cn DURKEE POTATO STICKS
2 c  CORN CHEX CEREAL
1 c  MINI TWIST PRETZELS
IN THE OVEN, AT 250 DEG. F. MELT BUTTER IN A 10 X 15 INCH PAN. STIR IN
PEPPER SAUCE, WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE AND THE PARMESAN CHEESE. ADD
REMAINING INGREDIENTS, ONE AT A TIME, STIR AFTER EACH ADDITION. NOTE:
STIR EVERY 15 MINUTES.
