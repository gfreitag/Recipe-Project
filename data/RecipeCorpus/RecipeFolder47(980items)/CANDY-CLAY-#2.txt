Title: CANDY CLAY #2
Categories: Kids, Crafts, Play dough
Yield: 1 Batch
-Robbie Shelton
1/3 c  Margarine
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla or Peppermint
1 lb Powdered Sugar
Mix the first 4 ingredients (margarine, corn syrup, salt and
flavoring). Add sugar, knead. This recipe is from ACC - Child
Development Class.
