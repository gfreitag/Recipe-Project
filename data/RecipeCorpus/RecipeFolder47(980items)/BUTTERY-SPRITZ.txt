Title: BUTTERY SPRITZ
Categories: None
Yield: 96 Cookies
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter flavored Crisco
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Light corn syrup or regular
-pancake syrup
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Almond extract
3 c  Flour
3/4 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
Heat oven to 375 F.  Beat sugar and shortening until light and
fluffy. Add eggs, syrup, vanilla and almond extracts. Beat until well
blended and fluffy.
Combine flour, with remaining dry ingredients and gradually add to
first mixture.  Mix until well blended. Fill press with dough and
press out 2 inches apart onto cold ungreased baking sheets. Decorate
as desired. Bake for 7 - 9 minutes or until bottoms are golden. Cool
2 minutes on baking sheet before removing to wire rack to cool
completely. 					Yield, 7 ~ 8 doz. cookies
