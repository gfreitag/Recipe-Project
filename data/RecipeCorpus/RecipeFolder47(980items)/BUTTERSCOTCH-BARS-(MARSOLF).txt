Title: BUTTERSCOTCH BARS (MARSOLF)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
2 c  Brown sugar
2 ts Vanilla
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Margarine
2    Eggs
1 c  Chopped nuts
Combine all ingredients and spread into a greased 9X13 pan. Bake at
350 degrees for 45 minutes.
