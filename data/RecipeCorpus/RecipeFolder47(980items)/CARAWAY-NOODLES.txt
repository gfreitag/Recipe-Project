Title: CARAWAY NOODLES
Categories: Crs, Totest, Post, Lowerfat
Yield: 2 Servings
2 c  Uncooked wide egg noodles
2 tb Redueced calorie margaraine,
Melted
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Caraway seed
1/8 ts Salt
Cook egg noodles using no salt or fat. Stir in remaining ingredients
until well combined.

Per 1 cup serving: 246 cal., 7.3g pro., 7.9g (29%) fat, 36.8g carab.,
47 mg chol., 296 mg sodium.
