Title: CAJUN SPICE
Categories: Vegetables, Cajun, Condiments, Formatted
Yield: 2 Cup
9 ts Pepper, cayenne
4 1/2 ts Pepper, black
4 1/2 ts Salt, sea
6 ts Oregano, dried, ground
6 ts Thyme, dried
6 ts Fennel, dried
6 ts Cumin, ground
6 ts Cardamon, ground
6 ts Garlic powder
6 ts Chile powder
6 ts Coriander, dried
Whirl in blender or mix all together by hand and fill up jar to
store.
