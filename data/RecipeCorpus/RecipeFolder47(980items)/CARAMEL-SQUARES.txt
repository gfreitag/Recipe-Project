Title: CARAMEL SQUARES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Margarine
2 ea Egg yolks beaten
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
Mix well and press in pan. Sprinkle with walnuts. Beat egg whites
stiff and add 1 cup of brown sugar while beating. Spread mixture on
walnuts. Bake in slow oven 325 F or 350 F till nicely browned.
