Title: CAJUN STYLE CORN ON THE COB
Categories: Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
6 ea EARS OF CORN
1/2 c  SOFTENED BUTTER
1/2 ts GROUND RED PEPPER
1/4 ts OREGANO
1/4 ts ONION POWDER
MIX BUTTER WITH PEPPER, OREGANO, ONION AND A PINCH OF SALT. SPREAD ON
EARS OF CORN, RIGHT OFF THE GRILL OR FROM BOILING WATER.
