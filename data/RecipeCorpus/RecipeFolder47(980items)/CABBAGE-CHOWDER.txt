Title: CABBAGE CHOWDER
Categories: Soups
Yield: 8 Servings
4 c  Cabbage, shredded
2 c  Carrots, sliced
3 c  Potatoes, diced
1 tb Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Sugar
2 c  Water
2 tb Butter
4 c  Milk
Put all ingredients, except milk and butter, in a pot and cook over
low heat until the vegetables are done. Add the butter and milk and
heat through.
