Title: Strawberry Flip
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
3 oz Unsweetened pineapple juice
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Wheat germ, if desired
5 oz Sweetened, frozen
-strawberries, thawed
4 oz Plain yogurt
A delicious energy booster combination.

Blend all ingredients at high speed until smooth. Makes 1 serving, 417
calories, 8.5 g. protein.
