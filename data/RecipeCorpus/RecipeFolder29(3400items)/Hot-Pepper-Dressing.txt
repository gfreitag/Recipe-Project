Title: Hot Pepper Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Hot/spicy
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Hot sauce
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
2 tb Olive oil
1 ts Hot pepper flakes
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl or cruet. Mix well. Yield: 1
cup.

Source: More Cajun Cooking, Modern Publishing, New York, NY Copyright
1990 SHARED BY: Jim Bodle 1/93
