Title: Rose Hip Jam
Categories: Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
4 1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Margarine
3 1/2    Rose Hip Puree*
1    Box Powdered Pectin
* To make Rose Hip puree use the following method: Select the best
Rose Hips (either red or orange) and wash in clean water. Remove the
withered flower ends, and place in a saucepan with the water.  Boil
till tender (about 20 minutes) and rub through a sieve.

Put rose hip puree and pectin in an 8 quart saucepan.  Mix well.
Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Add sugar and margarine, all
at one time, to boiling mixture.   Return to boil and let boil for 4
more minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat, skim foam. Fill
sterilized jars, seal and turn upside down for 5 minutes to seal.
