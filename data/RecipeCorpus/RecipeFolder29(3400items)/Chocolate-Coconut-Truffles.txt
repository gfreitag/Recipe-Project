Title: Chocolate Coconut Truffles
Categories: Candies
Yield: 2 dozen
-chocolate; broken in chunks
1 tb Orange, almond, coffee or
-other liqueur OR use
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Heavy cream
3 tb Butter or margarine; cut in
-chunks
2 tb Sugar
2    4-oz packages sweet cooking
Microwave cream, butter and sugar in large microwaveable bowl on high
1 1/2 minutes; stir.  Microwave 1 1/2 minutes or until mixture comes
to a full boil.  Add chocolate and liqueur; stir until chocolate is
melted.

Refrigerate about 2 hours or until firm enough to handle.  Shape into
1-inch balls.  Roll in coconut or nuts.  Store in refrigerator.
