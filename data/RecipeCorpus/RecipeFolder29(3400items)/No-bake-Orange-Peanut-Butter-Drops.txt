Title: No-bake Orange-Peanut Butter Drops
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 30 each
1/4 c  Chunky peanut butter
1 1/2 c  Quick-cooking oats
1/2 c  Raisins
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Thawed frozen orange juice
Concentrate
1/4 c  Butter
In medium size saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly, bring
to boil sugar, orange juice and butter.  Remove from heat; stir in
peanut butter until blended, then oats and raisins.  Drop by
teaspoonfuls on waxed-paper lined trays.  Chill until firm.
