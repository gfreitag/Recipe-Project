Title: Salat Znonit Beshamenet (Radish & Sour Cream Salad)
Categories: Middle east, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
;crosswise into 1/8 inch
;thick slices
1 sm Onion, red; peeled, cut into
;1/8 inch thick slices and
;separated into rings
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
Black pepper; freshly ground
1 c  Sour cream
24 md Radish; trimmed and cut
Combine the vinegar, sugar, salt and a few grindings of pepper in a
salad or serving bowl and stir thoroughly.  Beat in the cream a few
tablespoons at a time, then fold in the radishes and onion rings.
Taste for seasoning. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour
before serving.
