Title: My Canadian Pea Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Canadian, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1    Onion, chopped
Celery leaves
1 ts Salt
2    Carrots, minced
1 c  Split peas
3 c  Water (cold)
2 lb Smoked ham, shank or bone
3 qt Water, boiling
Wash and sort peas; soak in cold water overnight. Drain and place in a
large pot; add water, ham bone, onion, celery leaves, carrots, salt.
Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer until peas are very tender,
about 2 hours, adding more water if needed. Remove ham; chop and
return to soup. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Also freezes
well.

Enjoy.
