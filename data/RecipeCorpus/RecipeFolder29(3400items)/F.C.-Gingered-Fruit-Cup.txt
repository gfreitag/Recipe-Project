Title: F.C. Gingered Fruit Cup
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Diabetic
Yield: 12 servings
1 pt Strawberries, hulled and
Halved if large
1 lb Seedless Green Grapes
1    Cantelope
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
3 sl Fresh Ginger (1/8" thick)
1 sm Cinnamon Stick
Bring sugar, water, ginger and cinnamon stick to boiling in small
sauce pan, stirring constantly to dissolve sugar. Lower heat, simmer,
uncovered, for 10 minutes. Strain through sieve into a bowl. Cool to
room temperature. Add strawberries and grapes to syrup. Quarter
cantelope. Remove seeds. Cut into wedges, then crosswise into bite
size pieces. Add to bowl. Refrigerate, covered, until cold & serve.

Nutrient Value Per Serving: 83 Calories, 1 g Protein, .42 g Fat, 21 g
Carbohydrate, 5 mg Sodium, and 0 mg Cholesterol.

Exchanges: 1 1/3 Fruit.

Recipe From "Family Circle" magazine 6/25/91. Posted by Anne Marie
