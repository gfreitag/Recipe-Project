Title: Creamy Cucumber Artichoke Dressing^
Categories: Salads, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 16 servings
Only
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Low sodium soy sauce
1/2 c  Lowfat buttermilk
1/4 ts Dillweed
1/2 md Cucumber, peeled, seeded and
Chopped
3    Water packed artichokes,
Rinsed and chopped
1 cl Garlic, minced
2    Green onions, white part
Blend all ingredients in blender until smooth. store in refrigerator.

Per tablespoon serving: Calories: 16 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 3g
Fat: trace Sodium: 31mg Cholesterol: trace
