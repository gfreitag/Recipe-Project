Title: Sweet and Buttery Noodle Pudding
Categories: Puddings, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Salt
3 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Raisins
3 3/4 c  Light 'n Fluffy medium egg
-noodles, uncooked
1/4 c  Butter, softened
1 1/2 c  Ricotta cheese
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream
1/2 c  Sugar
Heat oven to 350.  Grease 8x8-inch glass baking dish.  Coo noodles
according to package directions; drain.  In a large bowl, stir
together butter, ricotta cheese, sour cream, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon
and salt. Add eggs, beating after each addition.  Stir in cooked
noodles and raisins. Pour noodles mixture into prepare dish; sprinkle
with additional cinnamon, if desired.  Bake 40 to 45 minutes or until
golden brown. Let stand 10 minutes before cutting into squares.  4 to
6 servings.
