Title: Diabetic Mushhroom Sauce
Categories: Diabetic, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Cornstarch
2 tb Water
Vegetable Oil or butter
1 c  Mushrooms (stem/pieces)
1 c  Beef broth
pn Pepper
2 ts All purpose flour
Coat a frypan lightly with oil or butter. Add mushrooms; stir-cook 2-3
minutes until mushrooms look a bit dry. Add broth and pepper. Bring
to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes. Stir flour and
cornstarch into water to form a smooth paste or shake together in a
small screw-top jar. Add to mushroom mixture and stir-cook about 2
minutes until sauce thickens.

Makes 1 cup sauce 1 Serving = 1/4 cup
1 extra choice
3 g carbohydrate
2 g protein
90 kilojoules
20 calories
