Title: Mediterranean Vegetable Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1 cl Garlic; finely chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Lemon juice
4    Green Onions; chopped
8    Radishes; sliced
12    Plum tomatoes; diced
1    Seedless cucumber; diced
1/2 c  Parsley; chopped
Toss the vegetables with oil and lemon juice to coat.  The salad can
be made a day in advance and kept covered and chilled.
