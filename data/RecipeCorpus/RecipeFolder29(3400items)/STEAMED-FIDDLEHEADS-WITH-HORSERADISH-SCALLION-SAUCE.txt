Title: STEAMED FIDDLEHEADS WITH HORSERADISH SCALLION SAUCE
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 -6
2 ts Dijon style mustard
1 tb Drained bottled horseradish
-or to taste
3 tb Finely chopped scallion
-green
1 lb Fiddleheads cleaned
1/2 c  Plain yoghurt
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Fresh lemon juice or to
-taste
In a steamer set over boiling water steam the fiddleheads for 5 mins,
or until they are crisp tender, transfer them with a slotted spoon to
a bowl of ice and cold water to stop the cooking, and transfer them
to paper towels to drain.  In a small bowl whisk together the
yoghurt, the mayonnaise, lemon juice, mustard, horseradish, scallion
and salt and pepper to taste, whisking until the sauce is smooth, and
serve the fiddleheads topped with the sauce.
