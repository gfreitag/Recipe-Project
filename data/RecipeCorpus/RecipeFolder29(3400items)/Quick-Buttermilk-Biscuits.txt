Title: Quick Buttermilk Biscuits
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 biscuits
5 tb Sacco Buttermilk Blend
1/2 c  Oil
1 c  Water
3 c  Sifted flour
1 ts Salt
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
Sift dry ingredients.  Make a well in the center & add oil/water all
at once.  Stir well.  Pat into a solid mass & divide into 8 pieces.
Roll each into a ball and place on ungreased baking sheet.  Bake at
450 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.  To freeze for later: Bake for 7-8
minutes, until risen. Freeze.  To thaw & serve - Bake at 450 degrees
for 8 to 10 minutes in the top half of the oven.
