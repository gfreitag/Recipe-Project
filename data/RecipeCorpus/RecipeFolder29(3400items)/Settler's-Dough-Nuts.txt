Title: Settler's Dough Nuts
Categories: Breads, Pastry, Settler's
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
Three pounds of flour; one pound sugar; 3/4 lb butter; four eggs;
1-1/2 pints of milk; nutmeg and cinnamon, one teaspoonful; two large
tablespoonfuls of barm (yeast):  Knead lightly; cut in strips, and
twist and throw into boiling lard; when they are of a fine light
brown, take the dough-nuts out; sift sugar over them while hot.
Origin: The Canadian Settler's Guide, written in 1855 Shared by:
Sharon Stevens. Submitted By SHARON STEVENS   On   03-28-95
