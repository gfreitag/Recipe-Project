Title: Chicken Lentil Casserole^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Cornstarch
3 tb Water
1/4 c  Tomato puree, no salt added
1 c  Chicken, cooked and diced
1/8 ts Black pepper
1 c  Lentils
4 c  Cold water
2/3 c  Green onions, minced
1 tb Parsley, minced
2 cl Garlic, minced
1/2    Sweet red pepper, diced
Wash lentils and soak overnight in cold water. Drain, saving 2 cups.
Simmer lentils and vegetables until tender. Combine cornstarch and
water and add to pan along with tomato puree, cooked chicken and
black pepper. Cook, stirring, until it boils and is thick.

Per serving: Calories: 286 Protein: 28g Carbohydrates: 38g Fat: 2g
Sodium: 58mg Cholesterol: 30mg
