Title: Banana Flip
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Whole milk
4 oz Orange juice
2    Peeled bananas
1 pk Vanilla instant breakfast
-drink
Traditional breakfast foods in a new combination.

Mix all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth.

Makes 1 serving, 545 calories, 17 g. protein.
