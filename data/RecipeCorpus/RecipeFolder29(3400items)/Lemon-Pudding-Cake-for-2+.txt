Title: Lemon Pudding Cake for 2+
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 2 servings
1/3 c  Milk
2 tb Flour
1/8 ts Salt
1    Egg, separated
1 ts Grated lemon zest
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
Heat oven to 325 degrees F. Beat egg white till stiff peaks form.
Beat egg yolk; add remaining ingredients and beat till smooth. Fold
in egg white. Divide between ungreased 1 cup baking dishes or 6 oz.
custard cups. Place in pan of hot water (1 inch deep). Bake 45 to 50
minutes. Serve warm. Two generous servings.
