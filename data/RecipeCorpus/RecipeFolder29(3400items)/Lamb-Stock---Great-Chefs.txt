Title: Lamb Stock - Great Chefs
Categories: Basics, Masterchefs, New orleans
Yield: 1 batch
1 tb Parsley, minced
4 md Tomatoes, peeled, seeded
-- chopped
1/2 c  Wine, white
1/2 c  Stock, veal
Bones, lamb
3 tb Oil, olive
1 md Carrot, chopped
2 md Shallots, minced
1 ea Celery, stalk, chopped
Brown the lamb bones in the oil and add the rest of the
ingredients. Bring to a boil and reduce the heat to simmer for 30
minutes.

Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions
:    Box 71112, New Orleans, Louisiana - 1983
:    Chef Chris Kerageorgiou, La Provence Restaurant, New Orleans
