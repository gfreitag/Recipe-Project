Title: Shrimp-Topped Bagel
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 20 servings
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
3/4 c  Shredded cheddar
10    Mini bagels, split & toasted
2    Green onions
4 oz Can tiny shrimp
1/4 c  Sour cream
2 ts Lemon juice
Slice onions, reserving sliced green tops. Combine shrimp, sour cream,
white onion slices, lemon juice, Worcestershire, and 1/2 c. cheese.
Spread a rounded tsp. shrimp mixture on cut side of each bagel.
Sprinkle remaining cheese atop. Arrange bagels on lightly greased
baking sheet. Bake , uncovered, in a 400 degree oven 5-10 mins., or
until heated through. Top with green onion.
