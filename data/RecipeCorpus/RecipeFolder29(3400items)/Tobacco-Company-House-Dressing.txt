Title: Tobacco Company House Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
1 1/2 tb Sugar
3 tb White vinegar
1 1/8 ts Prepared mustard
5 tb Vegetable oil
2 c  Mayonnaise
1 1/8 ts Lemon juice
1 1/8 ts Minced onion
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Salt
Put mayonnaise in blender and blend on low speeds; add lemon juice,
onion, garlic, salt and pepper.  Blend.  Add sugar and blend.  Add
vinegar and mustard and blend.  Add oil, one TB at a time blending
well at each addition.  Makes one pint.  Keeps in refrigerator one
week or just slightly longer.  The Tobacco Co. is/was a famous
restaurant in Richmond, VA. Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   04-11-95
