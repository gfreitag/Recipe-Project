Title: Vermont Crackles
Categories: Syrups, Cake/cookie, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
-maple-flavored syrup
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Ground mace
Course sugar
Perfect with a dish of
-vanilla ice cream
1/2 c  Margarine or butter
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
3 tb Maple syrup or
Beat margarine in large mixing bowl at medium to high speed 30
seconds or till softened.  Add half of the flour, sugar, egg, maple
syrup, baking powder, cinnamon, soda, and mace.  Beat till well
combined.  Beat or stir in remaining flour. Form dough into 1-inch
balls. Roll balls in coarse sugar.  Place on greased baking sheets,
about 2-1/2 apart. Bake in a 350 oven for 10 to 12 minutes or till
set. Transfer to a wire rack to cool. Makes about 48 From: Better
Homes and Gardens, Christmas Cookies Shared By: Pat Stockett
