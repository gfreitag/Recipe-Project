Title: White Trash Orange Marmalade
Categories: Can/preserv, Fruit, Whitetrash
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn Crushed pineapple
1    Bottle maraschino cherries
Chopped walnuts
8    Oranges
Water
Sugar
Cut oranges into wedges.  Remove any seeds.  Process oranges, rind
and pulp until thoroughly chopped.  Into pot measure the orange
mixture, and add 1 cup water for each cup orange mixture.  Boil for
10 minutes.  Add 1 large can of crushed pineapple, and bring back to
boil.  Continue to boil an additional 10 minutes.  Remove from heat.
Let stand overnight or for at least 12 hours.  Using 1-1/2 cups sugar
to 1 cup orange/pineapple mixture, return to boil.  Continue cooking
until mixture is thick. Before removing from heat last time, add 1
bottle maraschino cherries and some chopped walnuts if desired.
Place in sterilized jars and seal.

Origin: Adapted from White Trash. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, April/95.
