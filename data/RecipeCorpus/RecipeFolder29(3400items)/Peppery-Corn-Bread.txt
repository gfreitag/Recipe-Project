Title: Peppery Corn Bread
Categories: Breads, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 servings
3 tb Nutritional yeast powder
2 ts Baking powder
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper (or more!)
1/3 c  Mashed tofu
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  Water
1 c  Yellow corn meal
1 c  All-purpose flour
Using electric mixer or food processor, beat tofu with oil and water
until smooth.

Mix cornmeal, flour, nutritional yeast, sugar, baking powder, salt and
pepper in a bowl, then add to tofu mixture.  Mix well to combine. Pour
batter into a greased 8" (round or square) casserole dish or baking
pan. Bake at 350 F. for about half an hour.  Cut into wedges or
squares and serve hot.
