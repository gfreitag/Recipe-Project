Title: Banana Crisp
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Brown sugar; packed
1 ts Ground cinnamon
Whipped cream or cool whip
6 md Bananas; peeled
1/4 ts Salt
3/4 c  Vanilla wafer crumbs
1/4 c  Unsalted butter; melted
Recipe by: Produce Pete's "Farmacopeia" - ISBN 0-688-12847-5

Preheat oven to 350 F.

Slice the bananas into 1/2-inch rounds. Grease a large casserole and
arrange the banana slices on the bottom. Sprinkle with salt.

In a bowl, combine the vanilla wafer crumbs with the melted butter.
Add the brown sugar and cinnamon and mix well. Sprinkle over the
bananas. Bake for approx. 20 mins, or until browned. Serve warm or
cold and top with whipped cream or Cool Whip.
