Title: Wheat Germ Dog Biscuits
Categories: Homemade, Snacks
Yield: 1 batch
1/4 c  Molasses
1 c  Milk, evaporated
1 c  -water
3 c  Flour, whole wheat or
Flour, rye
1/2 c  Wheat germ, plain
6 tb Margarine
Combine first three ingredients. Then mix in the rest. Dough will be
stiff. Chill for a half hour. Roll rounded teaspoonfuls into balls.
Flatten, place on greased cookie sheet, bake one hour at 300 degrees.
