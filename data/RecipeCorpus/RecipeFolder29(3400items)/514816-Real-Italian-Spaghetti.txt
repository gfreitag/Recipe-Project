Title: 514816 Real Italian Spaghetti
Categories: Italian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Dried whole thyme
1 ts Cumin seeds
1 ts Dried whole basil
3/4 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Ground turmeric
2 tb Garlic powder, divided
1 c  Shredded Mozzarella cheese
1 pk Spaghetti
-Grated Parmesan cheese
1 lb Lean ground beef
1 lb Italian sausage
1    (15 oz.) can tomato sauce
1    (6 oz.) can tomato paste
1    (16 oz.) can tomatoes,
-chopped
1 lg Onion, chopped
2 lg Bell peppers, chopped
1    (4 oz.) jar whole mushrooms,
-drained
1 1/2 ts Chopped fresh parsley
Brown ground beef and sausage.  Drain.  Stir in next 13 ingredients.
Add 1 tablespoon garlic powder, stirring well.  Cover and simmer 20
minutes. Stir in an additional tablespoon of garlic powder; cover and
simmer 15 minutes. Add Mozzarella cheese and stir well. Cover and
simmer an additional 15 minutes.

Prepare spaghetti according to package directions.  Serve sauce over
spaghetti. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
