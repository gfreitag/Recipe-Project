Title: Chicken-Flavored Rice
Categories: Side dish, Mixes, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Margarine
1 1/3 c  Chicken-Flavored Rice Mix
2 c  ;Cold Water
Combine ingredients in medium saucepan.  Bring to a boil, cover,
reduce heat and cook 15-25 min.
