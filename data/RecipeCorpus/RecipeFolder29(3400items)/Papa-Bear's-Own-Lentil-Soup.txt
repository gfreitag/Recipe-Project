Title: Papa Bear's Own Lentil Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
-seeded and quartered
2 lg Carrots
-peeled and diced
3    Garlic clove(s), minced
1 ts Dried oregano
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Olive oil
Salt to taste
1 qt Chicken stock
1 qt Water
1 lb Dried lentils
-washed and picked over
2 md Potatoes
-peeled and diced
2 md Onion(s), diced
2 lg Tomatoes, peeled
In a large pot, bring chicken stock and water to a boil. Add all the
other ingredients at once.

Bring to a boil; continue boiling for 15 minutes. Then simmer over
low heat for  hour and it's done.
