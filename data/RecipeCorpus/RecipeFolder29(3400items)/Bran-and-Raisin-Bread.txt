Title: Bran and Raisin Bread
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Grated orange zest
1 1/2 tb Applesauce
1 1/2 tb Honey
2 1/4 ts Yeast
1/2 c  Raisins
1 1/4 c  Water
2 3/4 c  Bread flour plus 1 Tbl
1 c  Wheat bran
3 tb Cracked wheat
3/4 ts Salt
This is the best new recipe I have tried in months.  It makes a raisin
bread "to die for".  I adapted this recipe from "The Best Bread
Machine Cookbook Ever" by Madge Rosenberg.
