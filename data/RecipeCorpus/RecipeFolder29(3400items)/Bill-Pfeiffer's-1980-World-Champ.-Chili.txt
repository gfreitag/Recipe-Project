Title: Bill Pfeiffer's 1980 World Champ. Chili
Categories: Chili, Hot/spicy, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 pot
MMMMM-----------------"CAPITOL PUNISHMENT" CHILI----------------------
2 lg Onions, finely chopped
10 cl Garlic, finely chopped
1/2 c  Wesson oil or kidney suet
1 ts Mole. powdered, also called
-mole poblano
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Coriander seed (from Chinese
-parsley, cilantro)
1 ts Durkee's Louisiana Red Hot
-Sauce
8 oz Tomato sauce
1 tb Masa Harina flour
Salt to taste
1 tb Oregano
2 tb Paprika
2 tb MSG (monosodium glutamate)
9 tb Chili powder, light
4 tb Cumin
4 tb Beef bouillon (instant,
-chrushed)
24 oz Old Milwaukee beer
2 c  Water
4 lb Extra lean chuck, chili
-grind
2 lb Extra lean pork, chili grind
1 lb Extra lean chuck, cut into
-1/4" cubes
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
In a large pot, add paprika, oregano, MSG, chili powder, cumin, beef
bouillon, beer and 2 cups water. Let simmer.

In a separate skillet, brown meat in 1 lb. or 1 1/2 lb. batches with
Wesson oil or suet.  Drain and add to simmering spices. Continue
until all meat is done.

Saute chopped onion and garlic in 1 T. oil or suet. Add to spices and
meat mixture.  Add water as needed. Simmer 2 hours. Add mole, sugar,
coriander seed, hot sauce and tomato sauce. Simmer 45 min.

Dissolve masa harina flour in warm water to form a paste. Add to
chili. Add salt to taste.  Simmer for 30 minutes. Add additional
Louisiana Hot Sauce for hotter taste.

Makes 1 pot.
