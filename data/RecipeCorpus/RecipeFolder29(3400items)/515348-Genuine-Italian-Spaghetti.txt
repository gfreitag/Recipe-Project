Title: 515348 Genuine Italian Spaghetti
Categories: Italian, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Ground beef and 1/4 lb.
-pork sausage
1    Kernel garlic and 1 onion
4    Eggs
2 sl Bread, cut up
1/4 cn Parmesan cheese
-Salt and pepper to taste
1 lg Can tomato juice
2 cn Tomato paste
1 lg Onion, grated
1    Stock celery, grated
-Parsley leaves, grated
1/2 cn Grated Parmesan cheese
-Vegetable oil
Sauce:  Saute - oil, grated onion, celery and parsley until done, but
not too brown.  Add tomato paste and simmer, then add tomato juice.

Meatballs:  Mix ground beef, pork sausage, garlic, onion, eggs, bread
and Parmesan cheese together and form balls.  Fry in oil. Add to the
prepared sauce and simmer for 1 1/2 hours.

Serve over spaghetti.
