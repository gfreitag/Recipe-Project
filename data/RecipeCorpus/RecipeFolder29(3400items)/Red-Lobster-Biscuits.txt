Title: Red Lobster Biscuits
Categories: Breads, Pastry
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Butter, melted
1/4 ts Garlic salt
ds Italian Seasoning
(generous)
2 c  Bisquick
2/3 c  Milk
1/2 c  Finely grated Cheddar
Cheese
Combine first 3 ingredients. (It will be sticky) Spoon into 12
well-greased muffin tins. Do not use paper liners.

Combine last 3 ingredients, and spread evenly over the raw batter.
Bake for about 10-15 minutes at 450 degrees until nicely browned.
