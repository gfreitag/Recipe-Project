Title: Noah's Squash
Categories: Vegetables, Vegetarian, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
2 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Olives, green or black
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 md Zucchini; sliced
2 md Summer squash; sliced
2 md Onions; peeled and sliced
2 md Tomatoes; quartered
2 ea Garlic cloves; minced
In a large skillet or wok, combine all ingredients.  Cook over
medium heat, stirring occasionally, for about 20 minutes or until
zucchini and summer squash are tender. Source: Mr. Food Cooks Like
Mama. Typos by Elizabeth Wood.
