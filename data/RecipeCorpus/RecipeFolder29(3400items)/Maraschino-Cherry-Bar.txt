Title: Maraschino Cherry Bar
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Nuts
1 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Butter
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Confectioners sugar
2 1/4 c  All purpose flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Butter
4 oz Maraschino cherries
Mix flour, sugar and butter until crumbly. Pat into 9 x 13 inch pan
evenly. Bake for 20 minutes until lightly brown. Combine eggs, brown
sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla. Drain cherries. Save the
juice. Chop the cherries, add cherries and nuts to egg mixture. Pout
over crust and return to oven for 25 minutes. Cover the last 10-15
minutes to prevent burning. Combine butter and confectioners sugar.
Add maraschino juice, enough to make frosting thin enough to pour.
Pour over pan after cooked.
