Title: 498811 Biscotta
Categories: Cake/cookie, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Anise flavoring
1 1/3    Sticks butter (softened)
4    Eggs, slightly beaten
1 tb Baking powder
3 1/3 c  Flour
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Add
eggs, blend well.  Add anise flavoring.  Mix flour and baking powder
together.  Mix well.  Form dough on lightly greased cookie sheet
(into long cylinder shape) and bake 20 to 25 minutes.  Take out of
oven. Slice diagonally and put back in oven for 5 minutes; cool.
Drizzle cookies with thin powdered sugar glaze mixed with water and
sprinkle with small candies. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
03-27-95
