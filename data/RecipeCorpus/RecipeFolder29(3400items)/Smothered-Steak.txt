Title: Smothered Steak
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Hot sauce
2 tb Vinegar, red wine
2 c  Water
PLUS-
2 tb Water
4    Green pepper rings
2 1/2 lb Round steak, boneless
-cut in serving pieces
Seasoned salt, to taste
Pepper, to taste
Flour, all-purpose
1/4 c  Oil, vegetable
1 ts Garlic salt
Sprinkle steak with seasoned salt and pepper; dredge in flour.  Heat
oil in a large skillet; brown meat on both sides.  Combine 3
tablespoons flour, garlic salt, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, wine
vinegar, and 2 tablespoons water; stir until smooth.  Add remaining
water; stir until well blended.  Pour mixture over steak, and top
with green pepper rings. Cover and cook over low heat 50 to 60
minutes or until tender.
