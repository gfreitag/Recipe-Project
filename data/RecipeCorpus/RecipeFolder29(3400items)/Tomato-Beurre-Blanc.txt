Title: Tomato Beurre Blanc
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Seafood
Yield: 2 cups
2 tb Unsalted butter, cut into
Small pieces
Salt & freshly ground black
Pepper to taste
2 ts Fresh lemon juice
1 tb Snipped fresh chives
1 c  Homemade fish stock
1 c  Dry white wine
1    Ripe medium-size tomato,
Seeded & finely chopped
2 tb Finely chopped fresh basil
3/4 c  PLUS ...
Heat the stock, wine, tomato and basil to boiling in a small saucepan
over medium-high heat.  Reduce heat to medium-low and cook, stirring
occasionally, until the mixture is reduced to 1 cup.  Reduce heat to
low and whisk in the butter, 1 piece at a time.  Season to taste with
salt and pepper and stir in the lemon juice.  Just before serving,
stir in the chives.  Serve hot.  From The Silver Palate Good Times
Cookbook by Julee Russo & Sheila Lukins.
