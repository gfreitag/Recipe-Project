Title: Bleu Cheese Salad Dressing
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Lemon juice
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
4 oz Wedge of Bleu cheese,crumble
2 c  Sour cream
6 dr Tabasco sauce
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Crumble Bleu cheese.  Add sour cream and mix well.  Add remaining
ingredients.  Mix well.  This is quite thick but it may be thinned
with a tablespon of Half and Half or cream.  After it is mixed for a
while it will thin out some. Randy Rigg
