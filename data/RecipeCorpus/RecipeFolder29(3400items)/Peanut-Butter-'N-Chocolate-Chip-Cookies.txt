Title: Peanut Butter 'N Chocolate Chip Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 dozen
2 ts Vanilla
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 pk Baker's Milk Chocolate Chips
-(11.5 ounces)
3/4 c  Parkay Spread sticks ( 1 1/2
-sticks) (Margarine)
1 c  Granulated Sugar
1 c  Firmly Packed Brown Sugar
1/2 c  Peanut Butter
2    Eggs
Heat oven to 350Ã¸F.

Beat Parkay Spread Sticks, sugars and peanut butter in large bowl with
mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy.  Blend in eggs and
vanilla. Mix flour, baking soda and salt. Stir in chips.

Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.

Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned.  Cool 2 minutes;
remove from cookie sheets onto wire racks.

Makes about 4 dozen.
