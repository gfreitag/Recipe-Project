Title: Serundeng
Categories: Cake/cookie, Side dish, Indian
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Curry powder
2 pn Nutmeg
2 tb Brown sugar
10 oz Dried coconut flakes
10 oz Skinless peanuts
2 tb Peanut oil
Mix all the ingerdients. Put on a cookie sheet in a 300 degree oven
for 30 to 40 minutes, stirring frequently, until brown.
