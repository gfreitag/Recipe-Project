Title: Peanutty Play Dough
Categories: Kids
Yield: 6 servings
3 1/2 c  Light corn syrup
4 c  Dried milk powder
3 1/2    Cips creamy peanut butter
4 c  Powdered sugar
assorted cake sprinkles or small candies

Note: This mixutre is too thick for most electric mixers, so mix by
hand, kneading works best.

Mix first four ingredients together until smooth. Divide into 10 15
portions. Children can mold and shape into animals, flours, cars,
etc., then decorate and eat!
