Title: Apricot Phrynia
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Orange juice
1 c  Pineapple, diced finely
12 md Ripe apricots, pitted
1 md Orange, juiced -=OR=-
Cut apricots into quarters.  Put in a blender with the orange juice.
Blend thoroughly at medium speed.  Pour mixture into a bowl.  Stir in
the pineapple & serve immediately for the best colour, taste &
nutritional value.

If not serving immediately, cover the bowl & store in the fridge.  It
can be kept up to 5 days, but the sooner used the better the flavour.
Stir gently before dishing into glass dessert bowls.

Frances Kendall, "Sweet Temptations"
