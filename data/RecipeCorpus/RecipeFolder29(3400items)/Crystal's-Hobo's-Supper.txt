Title: Crystal's Hobo's Supper
Categories: Countyfairs, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
Thick
1    Carrot, Cut Into Slices
1/3    -Inch Thick, optional
1 lg Hamburger Patty
Salt And Pepper, to taste
3 lg Onion Slices, 1/3 Inch
Thick
3 lg Potato Slices, 1/3 Inch
NOTE: This serves 1 person. Multiply the ingredients by the number of
people you are feeding.

* Heat oven to 325 F.

1. For each serving, oil a 1-foot-square sheet of aluminum foil. Salt
and pepper the onion and potato slices, and lay them in the center of
the foil. Add the carrot slices, if using. Salt and pepper the meat,
and lay it on top of the vegetables. Enclose tightly, and bake for 1
1/2 to 2 hours. Do not open until ready to eat. Open carefully.

Submitted by Crystal Sultemeier to section on the BLANCO COUNTY FAIR &
RODEO of Johnson City, TX. Fair dates: 3rd week in August, for 3 days,
Thurs. through Sat.
