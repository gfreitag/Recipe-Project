Title: Penne Pasta & Cheese
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 8 servings
28 oz Tomatoes; drained & chopped
1/2 c  Roasted red peppers; chopped
1 1/2 lb Cheddar cheese; chopped
1 qt Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Parmesan; grated
1    Onion; finely chopped
Olive oil
3 cl Garlic; finely chopped
1/2 lb Ham; chopped
1/2 lb Escarole; coarsely chopped
1 c  Parsley; chopped
1 lb Penne; cooked al dente
In frying pan, saute onion in olive oil.  Add garlic, ham, escarole
and parsley and saute until slightly limp.  In large casserole dish,
toss onion mixture with next four ingredients.  In large pan, heat
milk, salt and pepper to boiling.  Pour milk over pasta mixture and
sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Bake at 350 for 30 to 45 minutes.
