Title: 498817 Strufoli
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Honey
1 tb Sugar
2 c  Flour, sifted
1/4 ts Salt
3    Eggs
MMMMM-----------------------HONEY  MIXTURE----------------------------
1 tb Sugar
1 c  Honey
Put flour and salt in large bowl.  Make a well in center of flour.
Add one egg at a time, mixing slightly after each egg.  Add vanilla.
Mix well to make a soft dough.  Turn dough onto a lightly floured
surface and knead. Divide dough into halves.  Lightly roll each half
1/4 inch thick to form a rectangle.  Cut dough with knife or pastry
cutter into strips 1/4 inch wide.  Use palm of hand to roll strips to
pencil thickness. Cut into pieces about 1/4 to 1/2 inch long. Deep
fry (one layer deep in fat) 3 to 5 minutes, turning occasionally.
Drain over fat. Cook honey mixture over low heat about 5 minutes.
Remove from heat and add deep-fried pieces. Stir constantly until all
pieces are coated with honey-sugar mixture. Remove strufoli with
slotted spoon; set in refrigerator to chill slightly. Remove to a
large serving platter and arrange in cone-shape mound. Sprinkle with
multi-colored candies.  Chill in refrigerator. Submitted By BARRY
