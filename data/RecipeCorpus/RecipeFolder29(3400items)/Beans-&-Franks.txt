Title: Beans & Franks
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Mustard,prepared
1 ts Celery salt
3 tb Onion,chopped
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 cn Pork and beans(31oz)
6    Wieners;sliced penny style
1. Saute onion in butter until tender. 2. Combine onion with remaining
ingredients in a 2-quart casserole. 3. Bake, uncovered, in preheated
350'F. oven 40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
