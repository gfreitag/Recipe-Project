Title: Pasta, Roman Style^
Categories: Pasta, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Chicken stock
3 lg Tomatoes, chopped
3 c  Shredded cabbage
2 md Zucchini, chopped
2 c  Cooked cannellini beans
1/2 lb (to 1lb) cooked rigatoni
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan
1 c  Parsley sprigs, minced
2 cl Garlic, minced
1 lg Onion, minced
3    Green onions, minced
2    Radishes, minced
2 md Carrots, minced
3 tb Fresh basil leaves, minced
Cook minced vegetables in 1/4 cup of the stock in soup pot until
soft. Add tomatoes, stock, cabbage, zucchini and beans. Simmer 5
minutes. Toss pasta with cheese. Add to vegetables, mix and serve.

Per serving: Calories: 262 Protein: 13g Carbohydrates: 51g Fat: 2g
Sodium: 83mg Cholesterol: 2mg
