Title: Celery in White Wine
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1/2    Chicken bouillon cube, crush
1/4 ts Sugar
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Ground ginger
3 c  Sliced celery
2 tb Butter
2 tb White wine (dry)
1/3 c  Slivered almonds
1 tb Minced onions
Saute almonds in butter; add remaining ingredients.  Cook, covered, 5
to 10 minutes until celery is tender. Randy Rigg
