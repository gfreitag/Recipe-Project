Title: Chili Krieghauser
Categories: Meats, Chili
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Black pepper; ground
2 cn Tomato sauce (8 oz)
1 cn Kidney beans (21 oz)
2 ts Cocoa
8 oz Coca-cola
2 lb Beef; ground
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Soup; condensed onion
1 tb Chili powder
(more to taste)
2 ts Cumin
Brown ground beef for 20 minutes, or until dark and crumbly. Pour off
excess fat. Add salt, onion soup, chili powder, cumin, pepper and
tomato sauce. Simmer for awhile, then add undrained kidney beans,
cocoa and Coke. Simmer until ready.
