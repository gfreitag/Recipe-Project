Title: Cheesy Chili Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Appetizers
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
2 tb Brown sugar; dark
1 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Ground red pepper
16 oz Cheez whiz
8 oz Tomato sauce
2 tb Chili powder
Cook cheez whiz, tomato sauce, chili powder, sugar, cumin, and red
pepper over low heat until cheese melts and is thoroughly heated,
stirring frequently.  Source:  Kraft ad
