Title: Corn Cakes^
Categories: Breads, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 15 servings
1 c  Corn
2    Egg whites, beaten until
Stiff
1/2 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 c  Boiling water
3 tb Apple juice concentrate
Combine cornmeal, water and apple juice concentrate, mixing well. Add
corn. Fold in egg whites. Spoon 1 tablespoon cakes onto a griddle
sprayed with pam. Cook on each side until golden brown.

Per serving: Calories: 34 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 7g Fat: 0.5g
Sodium: 33mg Cholesterol: 0mg
