Title: Some More Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Soy sauce
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1    Box of frozen peas
1 sm Can water chestnuts, sliced
1 cn Chinese noodles
1 lb Hamburger
2 sm Onions
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1/2 c  Minute Rice
Brown meat, onion and celery.  Then add soups, rice, soy sauce,
Worcestershire sauce.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.  Cover
lightly with foil, first 30 minutes, then uncover and add 1 can of
Chinese noodles on top.  Bake an additional 15 minutes. Randy Rigg
