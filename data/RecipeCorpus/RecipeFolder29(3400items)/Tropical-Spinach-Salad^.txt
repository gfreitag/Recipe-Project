Title: Tropical Spinach Salad^
Categories: Vegetables, Healthy, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
BUTTERMILK DRESSING
1 c  Lowfat buttermilk
1/4 c  Orange juice
1 cl Garlic, minced
1    Or 2 navel oranges, in
Chunks
1/2 md Sliced red onion
1 lg Bunch spinach, torn
Combine salad ingredients. Combine dressing ingredients. Combine all
ingredients. Eat.

Per serving: Calories: 71 Protein: 4g Carbohydrates: 17g Fat: 0g
Sodium: 101mg Cholesterol: 2mg
