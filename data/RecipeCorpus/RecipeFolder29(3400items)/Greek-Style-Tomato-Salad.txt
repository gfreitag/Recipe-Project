Title: Greek Style Tomato Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Black olives; sliced
2/3 c  Feta cheese; crumbled
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar salad dress
Lettuce leaves
2    Tomatoes; thinly sliced
1/2 c  Red onion; sliced
On a salad platter, arrange lettuce leaves.  Layer tomatoes over
lettuce. Sprinkle with onions, olives and feta.  Drizzle with salad
dressing. Source: Store ad
