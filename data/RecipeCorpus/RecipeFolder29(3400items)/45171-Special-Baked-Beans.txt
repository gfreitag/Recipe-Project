Title: 45171 Special Baked Beans
Categories: Casseroles, Velveeta
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  White sugar
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1/4 lb Velveeta cheese, cubed
-(don't substitute)
1    Onion
1/2 lb Bacon
1 cn Butter beans, drained
1 cn Kidney beans, drained
1 cn B & M beans, undrained
1 cn Pork & beans, undrained
Cut onion and bacon in small pieces and brown.  Drain off all grease.
Combine all ingredients in casserole dish and bake 1 hour in 350
degree oven.  This casserole may also be made in microwave on medium
until cheese melts and ingredients have begun to cook.  I also make
it in a crockpot, cooking on high about 2 hours.
