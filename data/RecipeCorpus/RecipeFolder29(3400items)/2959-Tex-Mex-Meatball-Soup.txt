Title: 2959 Tex-Mex Meatball Soup
Categories: Tex/mexican, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Oregano
1 lb Canned tomatoes
1 1/3 c  Beef stock or broth
1/8 ts Hot pepper sauce
1    Onion
1    Zucchini
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 ts Chili powder
-Salt and pepper
1 tb Oil
Chop onion.  Cut zucchini into 1 inch pieces.  Combine beef, chili
powder, salt and pepper into 1 inch balls.  In Dutch oven, heat oil
over medium heat.  Add meatballs and brown well - remove meatballs
and lower heat. Add onion and zucchini.  Cover and cook 5 minutes.
Stir in cumin, oregano, tomatoes with their juice stock, hot pepper
sauce, salt and pepper. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes.  Add
meatballs, heat thoroughly. * use 1 pound ground beef if you like a
lot of meat in your soup. Debbie Blakeslee Submitted By BARRY
