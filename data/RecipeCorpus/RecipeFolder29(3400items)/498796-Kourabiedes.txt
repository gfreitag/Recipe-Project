Title: 498796 Kourabiedes
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Ice water
1 lb Sweet butter
8 tb Confectioners' sugar
2    Eggs
3 1/2 c  Flour
Have butter at room temperature.  Combine butter and sugar, cream
together. Add eggs and cream together.  Add vanilla, flour and baking
powder, blend together with mixer.  Flour table and roll cookies into
logs. Cut into 2 inch logs.  Bake at 350 degrees, 20 minutes until
slightly brown. (Be careful not to burn cookies). Let cool. Cover
each cookie completely with confectioners' sugar.
