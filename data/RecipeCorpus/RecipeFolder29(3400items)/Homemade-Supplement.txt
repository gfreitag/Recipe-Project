Title: Homemade Supplement
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake
Yield: 1 servings
-breakfast mix
1 c  Double strength milk
1    Envelope powdered instant
One of several tasty ideas to make getting enough calories and protein
easy.  They are a great way to get the nutrition you need even when
you're not feeling well.

Mix together with spoon or in blender.  Approximately 330 calories,
24 gm. protein per cup.
