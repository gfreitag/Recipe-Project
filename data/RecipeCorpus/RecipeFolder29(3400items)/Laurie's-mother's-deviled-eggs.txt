Title: Laurie's mother's deviled eggs
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 ds Salt
Paprika for garnish
6 ea Eggs, hard-cooked
2 tb Butter, softened
2 ts Mustard, Dijon
Remove warm egg yolks from whites. Set whites aside on a serving tray.
Break apart and mash warm egg yolks with a fork and then mash in
butter. When butter is completely incorporated, mix in mustard and
salt. Fill white shells with mixture and dust with paprika.
