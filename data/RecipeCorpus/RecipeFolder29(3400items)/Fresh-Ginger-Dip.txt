Title: Fresh Ginger Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
1/3 c  Chives; fresh
1 cl Garlic; finely chopped
2 tb Onion; finely chopped
1 c  Lite sour cream
1 1/2 ts Ginger root; freshly ground
1 1/2 tb Soy sauce
Combine ingredients.  Chill and serve.  2 Tbsp serving contains 35
calories and 3 grams fat.  Source:  Store ad
