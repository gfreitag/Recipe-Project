Title: Turnip Whip
Categories: Crockpot, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Light cream
2 tb Soft butter or margarine
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
4    Turnips, peeled & quartered
4    Potatoes, peeled & quartered
2 tb Minced onion
1 ts Salt
Water
In slow-cooking pot, combine turnips, potatoes, onion and 1 tsp. salt.
Cover with water.  Cover pot and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours or until
vegetables are tender.  Drain well.  Mash; add cream, butter, 1/2 tsp
salt

From "Crockery Cookery" by Mable Hoffman.  (C) 1975, Bantam Books
