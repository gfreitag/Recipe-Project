Title: Cottage Cheese Pineapple Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1 lb Carton of cottage cheese
9 oz Cool Whip
20 oz Can crushed pineapple
2 tb Sugar
3 oz Package of Cherry Jello
Let pineapple and sugar come to a boil.  Set off and add Jello and
let set until it thickens.  Add 1 lb. carton of cottage cheese and
Cool Whip. Chill. Randy Rigg
