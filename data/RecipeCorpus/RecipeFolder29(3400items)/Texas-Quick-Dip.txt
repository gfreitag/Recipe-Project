Title: Texas Quick Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Medium salsa
1 ts Salt, seasoned
16 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/2 c  Sour cream
In a medium bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.  Add sour
cream. Fold in salsa and salt.  Store in refrigerator, but serve at
room temperature. Serve with veggies or chips. Source: Mr. Food Cooks
Like Mama Typos by Elizabeth Wood.
