Title: Corn Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Breads
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Salt
1    Egg
2/3 c  Milk, 1% lowfat
1/3 c  Butter
1 c  Flour
3/4 c  Cornmeal
4 tb Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
Preheat overn to 425.  In bowl, with fork, mix together floour,
cornmeal, sugar, baking powder and salt.  In other bowl, with fork,
beat together egg, milk and butter and pour egg mixture all at once
into flour mixture. Stir just until flour is moisteened and quicly
pour batter in lined muffin tins. Bake 20 minutes.

for bread, spread batter evely and bake 25 minutes into greased 8 by
8 pan. but into squares and serve
