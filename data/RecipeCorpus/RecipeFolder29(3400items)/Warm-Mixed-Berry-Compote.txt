Title: Warm Mixed Berry Compote
Categories: Desserts, Ice cream, Masterchefs
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Blueberries; fresh
Ice cream; vanilla
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Strawberries; fresh
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Raspberries; fresh
2 oz Butter
In a large saucepan, bring the sugar and water to a boil.  Simmer for
10 minutes.  Add the strawberries and blueberries to the pan and cook
for 2 minutes.  At the last minute, add the mixed raspberries and
butter to the saucepan with the other ingredients.

Heat just until butter melts.  Spoon the mixture onto a flat plate
and serve with a scoop or ice cream in the center.

Source: Great Chefs of San Francisco, Avon Books, 1984 Chef: Jeremiah
Tower, Santa Fe Bar and Grill, Berkeley, CA
