Title: Blueberry Torte
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Flour
1 1/4 ts Baking powder
2 c  Fresh blueberries
1 tb Ground cinnamon
1/2 c  Unsalted butter
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  Sugar (plus 2 tbs)
3    Egg yolks
1/2 c  Cornstarch
Recipe by: Produce Pete's "Farmacopeia" - ISBN 0-688-12847-5

Preheat oven to 350 F.

Using an electric mixer, combine the butter vanilla, 1/2 cup sugar,
egg yolks, cornstarch, flour, and baking powder. When well blended,
press the mixture into the bottom and sides of a greased 8-inch
square or 9-inch round baking pan.

In a large bowl, mix the blueberries with the remaining 2 Tbs sugar
and the cinnamon until the fruit is well coated. Pour into the
prepared pan and bake for 1 hour. Serve hot or cold.
