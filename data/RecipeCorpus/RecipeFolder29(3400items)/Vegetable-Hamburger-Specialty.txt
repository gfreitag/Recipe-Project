Title: Vegetable Hamburger Specialty
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Vegetables, Potato
Yield: 3 servings
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 cn Tomato soup
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
2 lb Ground beef
1 lg Onion
1 c  Diced celery
5 c  Raw diced potatoes
Brown beef and onion; alternate layers of celery, potatoes and
seasonings in buttered casserole.  Mix tomato and mushroom soups;
pour over ingredients in casserole.  Cover; bake at 350 degrees for 1
1/2 hours, removing cover the last 15 minutes of baking. Randy Rigg
