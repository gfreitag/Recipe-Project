Title: Lemon Asparagus
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Chives, chopped
2 lb Asparagus
1 tb Oil
Trim asparagus & steam spears until barely tender.  Arrange on a
serving platter.

Combine remaining ingredients & pour over hot asparagus.  Serve
immediately.
