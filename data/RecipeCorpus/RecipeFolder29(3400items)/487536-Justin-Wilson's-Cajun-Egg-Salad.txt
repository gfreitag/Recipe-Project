Title: 487536 Justin Wilson's Cajun Egg Salad
Categories: Cajun/creol, Salads
Yield: 3 servings
2    Heaping tbsp. mustard
1/4    Box crackers, crushed
-Dash of soy sauce
-Dash of hot sauce
-Salt and pepper to taste
6    Hard boiled eggs, peeled and
-mashed
2 tb Sweet pickles, chopped
2 tb Dill pickles, chopped
2    Heaping tbsp. mayonnaise
Mix all ingredients together.
