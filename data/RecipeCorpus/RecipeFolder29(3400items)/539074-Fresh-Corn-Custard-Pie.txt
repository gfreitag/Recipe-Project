Title: 539074 Fresh Corn Custard Pie
Categories: Vegetables, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar
-Pinch of pepper
9    Inch unbaked pastry shell
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Milk, scalded
2 c  Cooked fresh corn kernels
2 tb Melted butter
Beat eggs.  Mix in milk, corn, butter, salt, sugar and pepper. Pour
into pastry shell.  Bake in 400 degree oven 30 minutes, or until
silver knife inserted in center comes out clean.  Garnish with bacon
slices. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-19-95
