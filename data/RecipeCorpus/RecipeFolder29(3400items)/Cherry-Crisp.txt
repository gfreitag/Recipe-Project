Title: Cherry Crisp
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Sugar
1 ds Salt
2    Eggs
1    Stick Margarine, melted
4 c  Fresh or frozen Cherries +
1/4 c  Cornstarch, 1 t. Vanilla
OR
2 cn Cherry Pie Filling
2 c  Flour
Place both cans of pie filling (or 1st cherry mixture) in 9x13"
oblong pan. Blend together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.
Drop eggs into this mixture and mix until crumbs form.  Pour over
cherries. Pour melted margarine evenly over cherries.  Bake at 350
degrees for 35 minutes.
