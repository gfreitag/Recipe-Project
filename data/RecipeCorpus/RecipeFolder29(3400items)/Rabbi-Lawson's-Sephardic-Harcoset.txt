Title: Rabbi Lawson's Sephardic Harcoset
Categories: Passover, Jewish
Yield: 16 servings
2 c  Almonds; coarsely ground
1/4 c  Apple cider vinegar;
1/2 c  Sweet kosher red wine;
5    Apples;
16 oz Pkg pitted dates;
2 c  Pecans -=OR=-
Peel, core and slice apples.  Cook with dates in water to cover until
the apples are tender, approximately 45 minutes.  Water should be
almost evaporated; if not, strain mixture to remove excess water.
Cool the mixture.  Blend apples and dates in processor until smooth.
Place mixture in a large bowl.  Stir in nuts, vinegar and wine.  If
to thick, add more wine or vinegar.  Chill and serve. Source: The San
Diego Union-Tribune, Food Section, Apr. 6, 1995 Brought to you and
yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
