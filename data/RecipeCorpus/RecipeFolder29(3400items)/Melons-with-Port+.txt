Be sure the melons are fully ripe and sweet. Cut them into halves or
sliced, depending on the type and size, and make 2 or 3 gashes in the
flesh of each piece with a spoon. Add 2 oz. of port to each piece and
let it stand for a half and hour to mellow. Serve half a cantalope or
a slice of large melon per person.
