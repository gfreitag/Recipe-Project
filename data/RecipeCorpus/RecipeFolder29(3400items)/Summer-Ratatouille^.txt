Title: Summer Ratatouille^
Categories: Healthy, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
2 md Zucchini, diced
1    Green pepper, in strips
1    Red pepper, in strips
ds Tabasco
2 ts Cornstarch, optional
MIXED WITH
2 tb Water, optional
2 tb Salsa, optional
1 lg Onion, thinly sliced
2 cl Garlic, minced
3 tb Chicken stock
1 cn (16oz) no salt added tomato
Sauce, quartered, drained
1 1/2 ts Dried thyme
1    Bay leaf, halved
1 md Eggplant, diced, unpeeled
Soften onions and garlic in stock. Add tomatoes, bay leaf and thyme.
Simmer, covered,  10-20 minutes. Add remaining ingredeients, removing
bay leaf and simmer another 20 minutes. Add cornstarch and cook until
thick and bubbly if desired.

Per serving: Calories: 50 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 11g Fat: trace
Sodium: 13mg Cholesterol: 0mg
