Title: Buckingham Palace Scones
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Raisins
3 1/2 c  Flour
1 pn Salt
3/4 c  Butter
1 tb Baking powder
Cut together butter, flour, salt and baking powder.  Add sugar and
raisins. Make well in center of the mixture; add egg and small amount
of milk. If it appears dry, add milk. Blend to a nice smooth dough.

Roll out on floured board to 1/2" thick. Use cutter with 2" fluted
edge. Place on lightly greased baking sheet, brush with beaten egg.

Bake at 350 degrees for 10-15 minutes. Center of bottom should be
lightly colored.

Serve hot.
