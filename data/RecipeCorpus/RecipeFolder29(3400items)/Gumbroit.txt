Title: Gumbroit
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Fresh string beans
-- cut into pieces
2    Fresh tomatoes
1 tb Oregano
ds Salt
3 tb Olive oil
2    Onions; diced
2    Cl Fresh garlic
2    Zucchini; cubed
1    Eggplant; cubed
2    Peppers; diced
Recipe by: Produce Pete's "Farmacopeia" - ISBN 0-688-12847-5

In a large skillet, pour in the oil and add the diced onions and
garlic. Saute until golden brown. Add the zucchini, eggplant,
peppers, and string beans and saute until soft. Add the fresh
tomatoes, oregano, and salt. Stir and saute for 5 mins longer.
