Title:  CURLY MAPLE'S APPLESAUCE MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins, Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Applesauce
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter, softened
2    Eggs
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
In a mixing bowl, cream sugar and butter.  Add eggs, one at a time,
beating well after each addition.  Combine dry ingredients; add to
the creamed mixture alternately with applesauce.  Mix just until
combined.  Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes. Cool on a wire rack.

Yields:  1 1/2 dozen Originated: The Curly Maple Restaurant, New York
From: "Taste of Home" Magazine, April/May 1995
