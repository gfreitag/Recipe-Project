Title: Boiled Fresh Beef Tongue
Categories: Meats
Yield: 9 servings
6    Sprigs of parsley
2    Bay leaves
10    Peppercorns, cracked
1    Dried chile, optional
1    3 lb large beef tongue
1    Onion, quartered
1    Carrot, sliced
3    Ribs of leafy celery
See about tongue. Simmer the tongue 3 hours with the seasonings.
