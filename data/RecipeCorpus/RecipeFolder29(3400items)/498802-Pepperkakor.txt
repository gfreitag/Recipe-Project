Title: 498802 Pepperkakor
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Ginger
3    To 4 c. flour
1 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Soda in 2 tbsp. hot water
-Rind of 1 orange
Cream shortening and sugar; add remaining sifted dry ingredients,
beaten egg syrup and soda.    Let stand in refrigerator overnight.
Roll out and cut with fancy cutters.  Bake in moderate oven 8 to 10
minutes. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-20-95
