Title: Malted Rye Bread
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------1 POUND LOAF-----------------------------
1/2 ts Ground cardamom
3 tb Vegetable oil
2 ts Barley malt syrup*
1/4 c  Honey
2    Eggs
1/4 c  (plus 2 tb) Milk
1 ts Active dry yeast
1 2/3 c  Bread flour
2/3 c  Rye flour
1 ts Salt
1.  Scald the milk and let cool to room temperature.

2.  Add the milk and all remaining ingredients in the order suggested
by
your bread machine manual and process on the basic bread cycle.

* Available in health food stores

Source:  The Best Bread Machine Cookbook Ever by Madge Rosenberg ---
