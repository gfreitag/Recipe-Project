Title: 442nd Veterans Chicken
Categories: Countyfairs, Bbq/grill
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Shoyu (Soy Sauce)
4 lb Chicken Thighs, trimmed
1 c  Packed Brown Sugar
1/4 c  Fresh Garlic, pulverized
1/4 c  Fresh Ginger, pulverized
1. Combine the sugar, garlic, ginger and shoyu in a large bowl.
Marinate the thighs for 1 to 2 hrs. Pat dry.

2. Cook over white- hot coals for about 30 mins or until tender,
turning often.

From the MAUI COUNTY FAIR of Kahului, Maui, Hawaii. Fair dates:
October, the weekend before Columbus Day, for 4 days.
