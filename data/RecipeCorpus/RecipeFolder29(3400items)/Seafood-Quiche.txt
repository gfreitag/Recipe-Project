Title: Seafood Quiche
Categories: Pastry, Seafood, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Combination Of Chopped:
Green Bell Peppers
Red Bell Peppers
Onions
Mushrooms
Celery
1    10/" Pie Shell
1/2 lb Shrimp Or Crabmeat
3 lg Eggs
1/2 c  Bisquick
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Grated Cheese
1 ds Tabasco Sauce
1/2 c  Melted Butter
Sautee peppers, onions, mushrooms and celery in the melted butter.
Lightly beat eggs and combine with Bisquick, milk, salt, Tabasco
sauce and cheese. Add shrimp or crabmeat to mixture along with
vegetables. Pour into a prepared pie shell and bake in a preheated
350oF oven for 45 minutes. Add an additional 15 minutes if prepared
ahead of time. Submitted By FRED NUNNAMAKER   On   03-22-95
