Title: Mexican Hot Chocolate
Categories: Tex/mexican, Beverages, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Heavy cream
2 tb Sugar
6    Cinnamon sticks
6 c  Milk
3/4 ts Cinnamon
6 oz Sweet cooking chocolate --
Chopped
Heat milk with cinnamon until steaming. Pour into blender. Add
chocolate and blend until frothy. Whip cream with sugar until stiff.
Pour hot chocolate into cups. Top with whipped cream. Garnish each
cup with a cinnamon stick. Serves 6.
