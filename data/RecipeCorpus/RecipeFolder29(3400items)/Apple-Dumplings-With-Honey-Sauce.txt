Title: Apple Dumplings With Honey Sauce
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
-
3/4 c  Honey
2 tb Shortening
1/4 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Butter
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Honey Sauce-----
6 md Apples
1 1/2 ts Baking Powder
1/2 c  Shortening
1 tb Butter
1 tb Lemon Juice
2 c  Sifted Flour
3/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Milk
6 tb Honey
Dumplings:

Pare and core apples. Sift flour with baking powder and 1/2 tsp salt;
cut in shortening. Add milk, mixing until soft dough is formed. Roll
dough into 12 x 8 inch rectangle; cut into 6 squares. Place an apple
in each square. Combine butter, honey, lemon juice and 1/4 tsp salt;
blend well. Fill apple centers with mixture. Moisten edges of dough
with cold water. Bring 4 corners of dough over top of apple; press
edges together firmly. Place in greased 10 x 10 x 2 inch pan.

Honey Sauce:

Combine honey, butter, shortening, and salt with 1 1/2 cups of water.
Bring to a boil; boil for 5 mins. Pour over dumplings. Sprinkle with
cinnamon and nutmeg. Bake at 400 F for 30 to 35 mins.

Yield: 6 servings.
