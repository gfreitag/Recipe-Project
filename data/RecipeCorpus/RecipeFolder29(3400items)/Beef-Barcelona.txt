Title: Beef Barcelona
Categories: Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Sliced stuffed olives
8 sm Onions
8 sm Whole potatoes
4 lb Pot roast
8 oz Bottle of Catalina French
Dressing
1/2 c  Water
Brown the roast in 1/4 cup of French dressing.  Add the remaining
dressing and 1/2 cup of water.  Cover and cook slowly for 2 to 2 1/2
hours. Add olives, onions and potatoes.  Cook 45 minutes more or
until tender. Randy Rigg
