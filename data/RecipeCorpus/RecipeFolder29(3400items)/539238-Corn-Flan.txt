Title: 539238 Corn Flan
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
-Few grains cayenne pepper
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 lb Vermont Cheddar cheese,
-thinly sliced
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
3    Eggs
1 c  Rich milk or half and half
4 tb Melted butter
1 pt Frozen corn niblets, thawed
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Beat together eggs, milk and butter.  Stir in corn and seasonings.
Place half of mixture in buttered pie pan or 1 1/2 quart baking dish.
Spread with Cheddar cheese, top with remaining corn mixture and
sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake at 325 degrees for approximately
45 minutes or until set. Let stand a few minutes before cutting.
