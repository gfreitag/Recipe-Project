Title: 4702 Cajun Wings
Categories: Cajun/creol, Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Sweet basil
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Onion powder
1 tb Dijon mustard
-Salt and pepper to taste
-Vegetable oil for frying
5 lb Chicken wings, split at
-joint
1    Stick butter
2 tb Louisiana Red Sauce
1/2 ts Thyme
Add ample oil for frying wings in a large skillet.  Heat to
approximately 375 degrees.  Add wings without any coating of flour,
cook until browned, remove and drain on paper towel.  Remove oil from
frying pan; add butter and return to low heat.  Add dried wings and
remaining ingredients, stir until well coated.  Let stand for 30
minutes, then serve with your favorite blue cheese dressing.
