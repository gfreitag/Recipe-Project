Title: Almond Ratatouille
Categories: Casseroles, Microwave
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Fresh cilantro
2 tb Lime juice
2 tb Balsemic or red wine vinegar
2    Cloves garlic, minced
1 tb Chopped fresh basil*
1 1/2 ts Chopped fresh dill*
2/3 c  Chopped natural almonds
3/4 lb Small new potatoes
2    Tomatoes, chopped
1 md Eggplant, cubed (4 cups)
2 md Zucchini, sliced (2 cups)
1    Red pepper, sliced
1    Onion, thinly sliced
1/2 c  Vegetable cocktail juice
*Or use 1 teaspoon dried basil and 1/2 teaspoon dried dill

Cut potatoes in half to form bite-sized pieces.  Place in a glass
8x12-inch baking dish.  Cover; microwave on Hi power 2 minutes.  Stir
in remaining ingredients except almonds.  Cover; microwave on HI
power 15 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes until vegetables are
tender-crisp and potatoes are cooked through.  Remove from oven, stir
in almonds. Serve warm or chilled.

Nutrients per serving:  Calories 190, Fat-Total 8.33 g, Fat-Mono 5.16
g, Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 86.4 mg

Source:  Good Health with California Almonds Typos by Bobbi Zee
