Title: White Trash Limpin' Susie
Categories: Whitetrash, Vegetables, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
OR
1/2 c  Ham, chopped
1 c  Rice
1 md Onion, chopped
3 c  Okra, sliced
1/2 c  Bacon, diced
In a medium sized saucepan, start the rice cooking.  In fry pan, fry
the bacon or ham, onion, and okra.  When rice is 1/2 done, add the
okra/meat mixture, and continue to cook until water is completely
absorbed.

Origin: Adapted from White Trash Cooking. Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
