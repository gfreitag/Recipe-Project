Title: 45835 Corn And Bean Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Shredder Cheddar cheese (4
-oz.)
1    Stack Ritz crackers
1    Stick butter or oleo
2 cn White Shoe Peg corn
1    French style green beans
1    Cream of celery soup
1    (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese
Drain corn and beans, mix together cream cheese and cream of celery
soup. Then add corn and beans and Cheddar cheese.  mix well, spread
in 9 x 13 dish.  Melt butter or oleo.  Add 9 stacks of Ritz crackers
(crushed). Sprinkle over casserole and bake, 45 minutes at 350
degrees. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-17-95
