Title: Spicy Salsa Chops
Categories: Diabetic, Main dish, Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
2 cl Garlic; minced
1/2    Jalapeno chile pepper,finley
-chopped
4    (1/2" thick;pork loin chops;
-(about 1 lb.)
-trimmed of fat;
2 md Tomatoes; chopped
-(about 2 cups)
1/4 c  Red onion;
1 tb Brown sugar; (substitute)
1/2 ts Salt;
2 tb Lime juice;
In large nonstick skillet, combine tomatoes, onion, brown sugar,
salt, lime juice, garlic and chile pepper; mix well.  Cook over
medium heat until mixture comes to a boil.  Add pork chops spoon
chops.  Cook over medium-high heat for 5 minutes.  Cook over
medium-heat for 5 minutes. Turn pork chops, cook an additional 5
minutes to 10 minutes or until pork is no longer pink. FOOD EXCHANGE
PRE SERVINGS: 2 LEAN MEAT EXCHANGES + 1 VEGETABLE; FAT: 8g; CHOL:
65mg; SOD: 320mg; SUGARS; 5g; PRO: 23g; Source: Fast and Healthy
