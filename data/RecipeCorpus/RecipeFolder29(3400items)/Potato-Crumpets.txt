Title: Potato Crumpets
Categories: Breads, Potato
Yield: 16 crumpets
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Warm water
1 pk Yeast
1/2 c  Mashed potatoes
Add yeast to the water and let it soften 10 min. Add the potatoes,
salt and flour. Mix  and beat 3 min. The batter will be thin. Cover
and let stand in a warm place for 1/2 hr. Beat and let stand two more
times. Place muffin rings on a med hot greased griddle and fill rings
1/3 full. Brown and turn. Cool on a rack. Split and toast to re-heat.
