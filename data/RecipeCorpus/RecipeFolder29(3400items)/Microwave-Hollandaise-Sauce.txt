Title: Microwave Hollandaise Sauce
Categories: Microwave, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 cups
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 lb Butter
3    Egg yolks
Microwave butter till soft but not completely melted. It's OK if some
melts. Takes 20 seconds on high in my 900 watt machine. Stir in yolks
and lemon juice with a whisk. Cook on high stirring every 15 seconds.
