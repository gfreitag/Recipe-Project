Title: Pineapple Turkey Salad^
Categories: Poultry, Healthy, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
PINEAPPLE DRESSING
3 tb Pineapple juice
1 ts Dried tarragon
1 tb Orange marmalade,
Unsweetened
1/2 c  Lowfat yogurt
3 c  Diced cooked turkey
1 c  Celery, sliced
1 c  Crushed unsweetened
Pineapple with juice
1/2 c  Diced green onions
1/2 c  Sliced water chestnuts
Lettuce
Combine dressing ingredients. Combine salad inredients except lettuce.
Combine dressing and salad. Chill. Serve over lettuce.

Per serving: Calories: 125 Protein: 13g Carbohydrates: 10g Fat: 2g
Sodium: 87mg Cholesterol: 33mg
