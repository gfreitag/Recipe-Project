Title: Easy coconut Custard Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Nuts/grains, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Nutmeg
4    Eggs
2 1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Shredded coconut
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
(optional)
Fresh fruit, sliced,
Mix together all ingredients, except fresh fruit for garnish, in a
blender or mixer.  Pour coconut mixture into a greased 10 inch pie
plate. Bake at 350 F for 1 hour.  For a special presentation, garnish
with fresh seasonal fruit, kiwi, strawberries, pineapple, etc.

Origin: Homestyles, Canadian Classics Shared by: Sharon Stevens
