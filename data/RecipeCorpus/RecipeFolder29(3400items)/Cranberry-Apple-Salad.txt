Title: Cranberry Apple Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 c  Diced apples
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
2 cn Whole cranberry sauce, 1 lb.
Each
2 c  Boiling water
2    3 oz. strawberry gelatin
2 tb Lemon juice
Melt cranberry sauce over medium heat.  Drain, reserving liquid and
berries.  Mix together cranberry liquid, boiling water and gelatin;
stir until gelatin is dissolved.  Add lemon juice and salt.  Chill
until mixture mounds slightly on a spoon.  Add mayonnaise; beat until
smooth. Fold in cranberries, apples, and nuts.  Pour into 2 quart
mold. Chill overnight. Randy Rigg
