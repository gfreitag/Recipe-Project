Title: Speedy Taco Bake
Categories: Tex/mexican
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
2 c  Bisquick
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1 1/4 oz Taco seasoning mix
15 oz Tomato sauce
15 1/4 oz Corn; whole kernal, drained
Heat oven to 350.  Cook ground beef and onion until beef is brown;
drain. Spoon into 13 x 9 baking dish.  Stir in taco mix, tomato
sauce, and corn. Sprinkle with cheese.  Stir remaining ingredients
until blended. Pour over beef mixture.  Bake 35 minutes or until
light brown. Serve with sour cream, chopped tomato and shredded
lettuce if desired. Source: Ad Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On
