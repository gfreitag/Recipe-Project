Title: Chicken In Cider And Mustard ^
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Apple cider
1 tb Dijon mustard
2    Sprigs fresh thyme
-OR-
1/2 ts Dried thyme
2/3 c  Light cream
1 tb Butter
3 lb Chicken, cut in serving
-pieces
1 lg Onion, thinly sliced
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Melt butter in deep skillet. Add chicken and brown on all sides. Add
onion, salt, pepper and cider. Mix mustard, thyme, and cream
together; stir into chicken. Simmer chicken 30-35 minutes. Serve with
boiled potatoes and carrots. 4 servings.
