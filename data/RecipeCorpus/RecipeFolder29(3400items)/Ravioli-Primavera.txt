Title: Ravioli Primavera
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
-broccoli, red peppers
-onion, mushrooms
1/3 c  Chopped fresh basil;
-optional
2 c  Bottled marinara sauce
2 c  Water
16 oz Box frozen ravioli
16 oz Bag frozen mixed vegetables;
Bring marinara sauce and water to a boil in a large nonstick skillet.
Stir in frozen ravioli.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer, stirring a
few times with a rubber spatula, for 15 minutes.  Add frozen mixed
vegetables.
Return to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 8 to 10 minutes,
stirring gently 2 or 3 times, until vegetables are tender.  If
desired, stir in fresh basil.
