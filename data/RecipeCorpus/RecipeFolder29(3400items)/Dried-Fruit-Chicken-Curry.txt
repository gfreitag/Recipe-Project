Title: Dried Fruit Chicken Curry
Categories: Poultry, Curries, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Snipped dried apricots
1/4 c  Raisins
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Instant chicken bouillon
Granules
1 ts Lemon juice
1 tb Butter or margarine
1    Banana, sliced
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped peanuts
Hot cooked rice
1    Whole large chicken breast,
Split
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 sm Bay leaf
1 1/2    To 2 tsp curry powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Crushed red pepper
3/4 c  Hot water
1/2 c  Snipped dried apples
In medium skillet brown chicken slowly in the 2 tbsp butter about 10
minutes, turning once.  Remove chicken from pan, reserving drippings.
Cook onion, bay leaf, curry powder, salt, and red pepper in ther
reserved drippings till onion is tender.  Remove from heat.  Stir hot
water, dried apples, apricots, raisins, sugar, bouillon granules, and
lemon juice into onion mixture in skillet.  Return chicken to
skillet. Bring to boiling, reduce heat.  Cover and simmer 30-35
minutes or till chicken is tender. In small skillet melt the 1 tbsp
butter, stir in sliced banana and heat through.  Serve chicken with
banana, peanuts, and rice. Makes 2 servings.
