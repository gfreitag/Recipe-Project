Title: Spaghetti with Walnut Sauce
Categories: Vegetarian, Pasta, Italian
Yield: 2 servings
2    Tomatoes
1/3 tb Tomato puree
1/3 ts Lemon juice
1/3 ts Brown sugar
1/3 tb Olive oil
2/3 sl Wholemeal bread
1/3 ts Ground cinnamon
166 2/3 g  Wholemeal spaghetti
1/3 ts Olive oil
41 2/3 g  Walnuts
1/3    Onion
1/3    Garlic clove
1/3 ts Ground cinnamon
1/3 ts Ground allspice
83 1/3 g  Button mushrooms
Preparation: roughly chop the walnuts
chop the onion
crush the garlic cloves
slice the mushrooms
skin and seed the tomatoes and chop
cut the wholegrain bread into 1 cm squares
:
:
:
:
:

1. Cook the pasta in a large quantity of boiling water until just
tender.

2. While the pasta is cooking heat the oil in a pan. Add the walnuts
and cook for 3-4 minutes until lightly browned.  Remove and set
aside.

3. Add onion , garlic, and spices to the pan. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
Add the tomatoes and tomato paste, lemon juice and brown sugar.
Simmer for 10 minutes.

4. Heat the extra oil in another pan. Stir in the cinnamon, then add
the bread squares and cook until golden brown.  Stir the walnuts
into the sauce.

5. Combine the spaghetti and the sauce. Serve on a large plate,
sprinkled with croutons.
