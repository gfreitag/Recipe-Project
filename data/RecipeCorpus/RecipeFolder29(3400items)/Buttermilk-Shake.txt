Title: Buttermilk Shake
Categories: Beverages, Tasteofhome
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 dr Lemon extract
1 pt Vanilla ice cream
1 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Grated lemon peel
This shake tastes like liquid cheescake!

Place all ingredients in a blender. Cover and process on high until
smooth. Pour into glasses. Refrigerate any leftovers. Yield: 2
servings.
