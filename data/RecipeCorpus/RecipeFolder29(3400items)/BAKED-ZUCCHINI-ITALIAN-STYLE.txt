Title: BAKED ZUCCHINI ITALIAN-STYLE
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Garlic powder
4    Eggs
1 c  Light cream or half and half
1 c  Shredded Provolone cheese
2 tb Flour
1 tb Grated Romano cheese
2 tb Butter
6    Small zucchini, cut into
-1/4" slices and quartered
1/3 c  Chopped red pepper
1 ts Italian seasoning
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Preheat oven to 350 F.  Heat butter in large skillet over medium heat
until melted.  Add zucchini, red pepper and seasonings.  Saute about
5 minutes or until vegetables are tender-crisp; set aside.  Beat eggs
until foamy. Stir in cream, Provolone cheese and flour.  Add
vegetable mixture.
Pour into buttered 1 1/2-quart baking dish.  Sprinkle Romano cheese
over top.  Bake about 40 minutes or until a knife inserted near
center comes out clean.  Let stand about 5 minutes before serving.
