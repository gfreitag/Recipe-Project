Title: Rowe's Fresh Strawberry Layer Pudding
Categories: Fruit, Puddings
Yield: 12 servings
Or
8 oz Cool Whip
1 qt Strawberries
1 lb Vanilla wafers
1/2 c  Butter
1 lb Confectioners' sugar
2    Eggs
1/2 pt Whipping cream -- whipped
Cream together first 3 ingredients.  Roll vanilla wafers into fine
crumbs. Place 1/2 in bottom of pan (9x13).  Cover with layer of
creamed butter, sugar and eggs.  Then cover with strawberries whole
or sliced. Cover with whipped cream - then top with rest of vanilla
crumbs.  Garnish with whole berries and chill.  May be chilled
overnight.
