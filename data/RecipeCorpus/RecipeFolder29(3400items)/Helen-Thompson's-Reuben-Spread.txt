Title: Helen Thompson's Reuben Spread
Categories: Countyfairs, Dips/spread
Yield: 10 servings
1 pk (4 Oz) Corned Beef
Torn in small pieces
3/4 c  Mayonnaise, Hellmann's
Or Best Foods
8 oz Swiss Cheese, grated
8 oz Cheddar Cheese, Sharp,
Grated
1 cn (14 Oz) Sauerkraut, drained
* Preheat oven to 350 F.

Mix all the ingredients well. Put into a small casserole or an
8-inch-square Pyrex dish. Bake, covered, 30 mins. Serve on cocktail
rye bread as hors d'oeuvre or full size slices of your favorite bread
as a lunch treat.

From the section on the DELAWARE COUNTY FAIR of Delaware, OH. Fair
dates: 3rd week in Sept., 7 days, Sat through Fri.
