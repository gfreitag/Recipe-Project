Title: Bell Pepper Burritos with Guacamole
Categories: Tex/mexican, Main dish
Yield: 2 people
1/2 c  Tomatoes; chopped
1/4 c  Avocado;
2 tb Red Onions; grated
1 ts Lime peel; grated
1 ts Lime juice;
1 ts Olive oil;
6    Flour tortillas; warmed
1 1/2 c  Torn romaine lettuce;
1/2 c  Jicama; shredded
1    Sweet green pepper; cut
-into strips
1    Sweet red pepper; cut into
-strips
1/4 c  Lemon juice;
2 tb Fresh cilantro; snipped
1/2 ts Ground cumin;
1/2 ts Chili powder;
1/4 ts Black pepper; freshly ground
In a medium bowl, mix the green and red peppers, lemon juice,
cilantro, cumin, chili powder and black pepper.  Let stand for 30
minutes. In another medium bowl, mix the tomatoes, avocado, onions,
lime peel and juice. Cover and refrigerate until needed.  In a large
nonstick skillet over medium heat, warm the oil.  Add the pepper
mixture. Cook, stirring frequently, until the peppers are tender and
the liquid has evaporated, 4 to 5 minutes. Divide the pepper mixture
among the tortillas. Sprinkle with the lettuce and jicama.  Roll to
enclose the filling. Serve with the avocado mixture. Servings: 2 Pre
