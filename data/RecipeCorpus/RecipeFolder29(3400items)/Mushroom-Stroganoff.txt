Title: Mushroom Stroganoff
Categories: Fatfree, Mushroom
Yield: 1 servings
Ones)
10 ml Or sauteeing substitute
1    Or 2 Tbs LF sour cream or
Substitute
5    To 10 ml balsamic vinegar.
150    Grs Mushrooms (cut in
Strips)
1    Onion (medium, preferably
Brown)
1    Garlic clove (or 2 small
Sautee chopped onion and garlic over  moderate heat till soft. Add
mushrooms. When soft (usually 2 min), add seasoning, LF sour cream  or
substitute and vinegar. Stir gently. When heated serve on rice or
noodles or baked potatoes.

Tip: When sauteeing mushrooms use little sauteeing agent and cook
lightly. Never overcook mushrooms.!!!
