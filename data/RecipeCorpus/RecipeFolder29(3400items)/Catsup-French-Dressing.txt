Title: Catsup French Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Condiments
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Salt
1 ts Paprika
1 sm Onion, grated
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1/3 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Salad oil
Place ingredients in a jar and shake like or jump up and down.  I do
my mixing in a blender. Randy Rigg
