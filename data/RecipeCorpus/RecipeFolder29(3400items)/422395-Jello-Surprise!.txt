Title: 422395 Jello Surprise!
Categories: Salads, Velveeta
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Crushed pineapple
1 sm Box Velveeta cheese
1 lg Box lime Jello
1 c  Cool Whip
1 c  Nuts
Jello - use (fast jell method).  Refrigerate 20 minutes (Jello to
set). Beat with a spoon.  Grate the small box of cheese and mix
everything from above into Jello.  Refrigerate.
