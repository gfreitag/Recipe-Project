Title: Watergate Salad 3
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 3/4 c  Cool Whip
Strawberries (optional)
1 pk Small Pistachio Instant
Pudding
20 oz Crushed pineapple in syrup
1 c  Miniature Marshmallows
Stir pudding mix, pineapple, marshmallows and nuts in large bowl
until well blended.  Gently stir in whipped topping

Refrigerate one hour or until read to serve.

Garnish with additional whipped topping and sliced strawberries. ...
