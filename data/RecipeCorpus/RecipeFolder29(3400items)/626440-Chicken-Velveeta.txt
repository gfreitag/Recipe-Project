Title: 626440 Chicken Velveeta
Categories: Poultry, Casseroles, Velveeta
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Velveeta cheese
2    Boxes chicken Stove Top
-dressing
4    Whole chicken breasts
-(cooked & boned)
2 cn Cream of chicken soup
After cooking chicken, break into small pieces and line bottom of a
9z13 inch pan.  Mix soup and Velveeta cheese and place over low heat
until cheese is thoroughly melted.  Pour cheese sauce over chicken.
Prepare Stove Top dressing as directed on package.  Place dressing on
top of chicken and cheese sauce.  Bake in a 375 degree oven,
uncovered, about 45 minutes. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
04-12-95
