Title: Peach Cobbler #1
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Melted butter
2 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1 tb Cornstarch
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Water or peach juice
3 c  Sugared sliced peaches
1 tb Butter
1 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Flour
Mix cornstarch, brown sugar, and water or peach juice if using canned
peaches. Add fruit. Cook and stir until thick. Add butter and lemon
juice and pour into baking dish. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and
salt. Add milk and butter and beat until smooth. Pour over fruit.
Combine sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle over batter. Bake 30 minutes at
350 degrees.
