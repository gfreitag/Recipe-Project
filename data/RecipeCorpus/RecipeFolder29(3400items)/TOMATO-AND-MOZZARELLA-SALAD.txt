Title: TOMATO AND MOZZARELLA SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
1    Purple onion, sliced (OPT)
12    Whole fresh basil leaves
6 lg Tomatoes, sliced
8 oz Mozzarella cheese
MMMMM-----------------------***DRESSING***----------------------------
Chopped
1/4 ts Oregano
Pepper
3 tb Olive oil
3 tb Red wine vinegar
1    Garlic clove, minced
3    Fresh basil leaves, coarsely
On a bed of lettuce, arrange tomatoes, cheese, onion, if using, and
basil. Mix all dressing ingredients together and pour on top. Garnish
with sprigs of parsley, if de- sired.
