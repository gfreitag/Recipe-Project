Title: POKE CAKE
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
3 oz JELLO
1/2 c  COLD WATER
18 oz CAKE MIX
1 c  BOILING WATER
_______ ea JELLO ICING

PREHEAT OVEN TO 350.  PREPARE CAKE AS DIRECTED ON PACKAGE.  POUR INTO
WELL GREASED AND FLOURED 13 X 9 PAN.  BAKE 30 TO 35 MINUTES OR UNTIL
DONE. COOL CAKE IN PAN 15 MINUTES, THEN PRICK WITH FORK AT 1/2"
INTERVALS. MEANWHILE, DISSOLVE GELATIN IN BOILING WATER.  ADD COLD
WATER AND ALLOW TO COOL TO ROOM TEMP. CAREFULLY POUR OVER CAKE. CHILL
3 TO 4 HOURS. GARNISH WITH JELLO ICING OR COOL WHIP. (SQUARE MEALS)
