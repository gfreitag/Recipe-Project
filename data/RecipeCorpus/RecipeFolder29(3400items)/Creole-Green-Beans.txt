Title: Creole Green Beans
Categories: Vegetables, Cajun/creol
Yield: 3 servings
1/8 ts Salt
1 lb Can of green beans, drained
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 tb Butter
1/4 c  Chili sauce
Cook onion in butter until tender but not brown.  Add chili sauce,
salt and drained green beans.  Heat through; stir often. Randy Rigg
