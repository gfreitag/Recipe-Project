Title: Camper's Special
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Pasta
Yield: 1 servings
;salt and pepper
1    Can vegetable soup
19 1/2 oz Can franco amer. spaghetti
1 lb Ground beef
1 sm Onion; chopped
2 tb Shortening
Brown ground beef and onion together in hot shortening. Add
seasoning, soup and spaghetti. Simmer until heated thru.
