Title: 539106 Aunt Janice's Corn Mac Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Creamed corn, do not drain
1 c  Uncooked macaroni
2 tb Diced onion
1/2 c  Margarine
1 c  Diced Velveeta
1 cn Corn, do not drain
Mix all the ingredients in a 9 x 13 pan.  You may want to place
Velveeta cubes on top for even melting.  Bake at 375 degrees for 1
hour, covered for last 30 minutes.
