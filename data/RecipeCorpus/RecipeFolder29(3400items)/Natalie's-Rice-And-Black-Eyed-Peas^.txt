Title: Natalie's Rice And Black Eyed Peas^
Categories: Rice, Healthy, Low cal/fat, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Water
1 cn (16oz) black eyed peas,
Rinsed and drained
2 tb Chicken stock
1 md Onion, diced
1 lg Tomato, diced
1 c  Uncooked brown rice
Saute onion in stock in a fry pan sprayed with pam. Add tomato and
cook another 5 minutes. Stir in rice and water. Cook until rice is
done. Add beans and heat through.

Per serving: Calories: 165 Protein: 8g Carbohydrates: 32g Fat: 1g
Sodium: 125mg Cholesterol: 0mg
