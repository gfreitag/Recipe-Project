Title: Classic Brooklyn Egg Cream+
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Chocolate syrup
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Seltzer
Pour the milk into a 12 oz. chilled glass. Spritz in the seltzer
until a white head reaches the top of the glass. Spoon in the syrup
with a twist of the wrist!

Surprise! Egg Cream has no egg and no cream!
