Title: Straw And Hay
Categories: Main dish, Healthy
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Ham, deli-style,
-- reduced fat
-- diagonally sliced
2 tb Chives; chopped
1 c  Frozen peas
-- thawed and drained
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
1/8 ts Paprika
1 c  Skim milk
1/2 c  Nonfat cottage cheese
2 ts Cornstarch
1/4 ts Ground mace
1/8 ts Ground black pepper
4 oz Fettucine noodles,
-- uncooked
4 oz Spinach fettucine
-- noodles, uncooked
Recipe by: Cooking Healthy Series Vol 1. # 5

1. Combine milk, cottage
cheese, cornstarch, mace and black pepper in blender or food
processor; process until smooth. Set aside.

2. Cook noodles according to package directions, omitting salt.
Meanwhile, spray large nonstick skillet with nonstick spray. Cook and
stir ham and chives over medium heat until ham is lightly browned.
Stir in milk mixture and peas; cook over medium heat until thickened.

3. Remove from heat. Drain noodles; combine with milk mixture. Add
Parmesan cheese. Toss to coat evenly. Sprinkle with paprika; serve
immediately.

Makes 4 (1 1/2-cup) servings
