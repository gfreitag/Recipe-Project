Title: TWO-POTATO OVEN FRIES
Categories: Potato, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 tb Chili powder
1 ts Ground red pepper (optional)
2 lg Baking potatoes
1 lg Sweet potato
1    Egg white
MMMMM------------------------INSTRUCTIONS-----------------------------
Ruth Nicolosi of Novato adapted this recipe from Prevention magazine.

Cut unpeeled baking and sweet potatoes into thin fries. Beat the egg
white in a large bowl until foamy. Stir in chili powder and pepper.
Add potatoes and toss to coat well. Spread potatoes in a single layer
on a nonstick cookie sheet  or a regular cookie sheet coated with
no-stick spray. Bake at 450 degrees F. for 30 to 35 minutes, or until
po- tatoes are crisp and brown. Serves 4. PER SERVING: 230 calories,
6 g protein, 52 g carbohydrate, 1 g fat, 0 mg cholesterol, 51 mg
sodium, 6 g fiber. Karola Saekel writing in the San Francisco
Chronicle, 3/4/92.
