Title: Apple Streusel cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
5 c  Apples, coarsely diced
1/2 c  Chopped nuts, optional
THE STREUSEL:
2 tb Flour
2 tb Butter, softened
5 tb Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1 1/3 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Oil
1/4 c  Honey
Pre-heat the oven to 350Ã¸F. Grease and flour a 13x9-inch pan.

In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients except nuts,apples and
the streusel ingredients and mix, at low speed  using an electric
mixer, until moistened; beat 2 minutes at medium speed.

Stir in the nuts and apples by hand. Spread the batter in the
prepared pan. In a small bowl mix the streusel ingredients to form a
crumb mixture and top the batter with it. Bake at 350 deg F for 40 to
50 min or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

Serve warm or cool with whipped cream or ice cream.
