Title: Baked Corn in Sour Cream
Categories: Side dish, Casseroles, Vegetables, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Sour cream
24 oz Corn, whole kernel; drained
1 tb Parsley; chopped
6 sl Bacon
2 tb Onion; chopped
2 tb Butter (or marg.)
2 tb Flour, all-purpose
Fry bacon; drain and crumble. Set aside.

Saute onion in butter; blend in flour and salt.  Gradually add sour
cream, stirring until mixture is smooth.  Heat just to boiling; add
corn, and heat thoroughly.  Fold in half of bacon.

Spoon into a greased 2-quart casserole; top with parsley and remaining
bacon.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 45 minutes.
