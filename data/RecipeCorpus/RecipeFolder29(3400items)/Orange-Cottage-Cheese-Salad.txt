Title: Orange Cottage Cheese Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1 lg Can crushed pineapple
1 sm Carton cottage cheese
1 lg Carton of Cool Whip
1    Box of orange Jello, family
Size
4 tb Sugar
Mix first three ingredients together and cook to full boil.  Cool.
After cooled add cottage cheese.  Then fold in Cool Whip.
Refrigerate. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   03-27-95
