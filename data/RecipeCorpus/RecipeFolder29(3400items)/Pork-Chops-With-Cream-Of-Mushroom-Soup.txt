Title: Pork Chops With Cream Of Mushroom Soup
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 3 servings
1 sm Can Mushroom stems
And pieces, drained
Few drops Kitchen Bouquet
Hot cooked noodles
6    Pork chops
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
3/4 cn Milk
1 tb Dried minced onion
Trim fat from chops. Rub the fat from the chops over bottom of
electric frypan; then discard. Brown chops in frypan, salt and
peppering to taste.

In a bowl combine soup, milk, onion, mushrooms, and Kitchen Bouquet.
Stir until creamy. Pour over chops, lifting chops so soup mixture
coats both sides. Simmer covered 45 minutes or until chops are
tender. Remove chops. Add hot cooked noodles to soup mixture and stir
to coat.

Serves 3-6.
