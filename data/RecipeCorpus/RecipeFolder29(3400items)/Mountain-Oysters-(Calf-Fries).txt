Title: Mountain Oysters (Calf Fries)
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
-Sprinkle with salt and
-pepper and serve with
Scrambled eggs and
-buttermilk biscuits.
Clean the oysters, soak in
-ice water - dredge with
-flour and fry in hot
Fat until well browned.
