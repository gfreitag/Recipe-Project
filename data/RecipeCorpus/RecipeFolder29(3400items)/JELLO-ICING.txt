Title: JELLO ICING
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  POWDERED SUGAR
1/2 c  BOILING WATER
1/2 lb BUTTER
3 oz JELLO
CREAM BUTTER AND SUGAR.  DISSOLVE JELLO IN BOILING WATER; COOL TO ROOM
TEMP.  SLOWLY MIX COOLED JELLO INTO BUTTER-SUGAR MIXTURE. SPREAD ON
POKE CAKE. (SQUARE MEALS)
