Title: 279768 Asparagus Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
-Pepper to taste
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Lawry's seasoned salt
1 1/2 c  Buttered bread crumbs
1 sm Jar pimentos, sliced and
-drained
-Slivered almonds
2 pk Frozen asparagus, cut up
5    Hard-boiled eggs, sliced
1 3/4 c  Grated Colby cheese
3 tb Butter
3 tb Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Asparagus liquid
Cook asparagus.  Make white sauce with butter, flour, milk,
seasonings. Add cheese and stir until melted.  Add pimento.  If too
thick, add a little more milk.  In 9x13 inch baking dish, put 1 1/2
cups buttered bread crumbs. Layer asparagus, eggs, almonds, sauce.
Repeat. Top with 1 cup buttered bread crumbs.  Bake at 325 degrees
for 25 minutes, uncovered. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
04-17-95
