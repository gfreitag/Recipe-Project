Title: Gorp
Categories: Candies
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1/2 c  Wheat Germ
1/2 c  Crushed peanuts
1/2 c  Raisins
1 c  Corn syrup or molasses or
Honey
3/4 c  Milk powder
1 c  Oatmeal
1/2 c  Peanut butter
Mix all the ingredient thoroughly.  Roll into balls and each in a
small piece of wax paper, twisting ends and chill.
