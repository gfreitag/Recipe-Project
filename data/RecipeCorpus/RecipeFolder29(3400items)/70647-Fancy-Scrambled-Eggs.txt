Title: 70647 Fancy Scrambled Eggs
Categories: Casseroles, Cheese/eggs, Velveeta
Yield: 1 servings
3 oz Sauteed mushrooms
1 c  Ham
12    Eggs, beaten
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1 c  (4 oz.) Velveeta cheese
-topping
2 1/4 c  Bread crumbs
1/8 ts Paprika
3 tb Butter
1 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 c  Milk
Fry 1 cup ham.  Add 12 eggs, beaten.  Scramble until just set. Fold
sauteed mushrooms and eggs into cheese sauce.  Sauce:  Melt butter.
Blend in flour, salt and pepper.  Add milk.  Cook and stir. Stir in
Velveeta cheese until melted.  Place in 9 x 13 inch dish. (Can be
refrigerate overnight.) Combine bread crumbs, paprika and butter;
saute. Sprinkle on top. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes (more if
cold). Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-12-95
