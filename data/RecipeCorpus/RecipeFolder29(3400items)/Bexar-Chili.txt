Title: Bexar Chili
Categories: Main dish, Chili
Yield: 1 servings
15 oz Can tomato sauce
1 qt Water
1/4 ts Salt
2 lb Ground meat
1 tb Oil
3 tb Chili powder
Brown the meat slowly in the oil.  Add the tomato sauce and water, and
simmer for one hour.  Add the chili powder and salt and simmer for a
half hour. Serve in bowls.

This is based on the Bolner Family San Antonio Chili recipe.
