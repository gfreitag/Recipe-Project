Title: 881047 Excellent Corn Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 bn Green onion
1 sm Bottle of favorite Italian
-dressing
2 cn Yellow corn, drained
1 cn White corn
2 c  Green olives
1 c  Green peppers, diced
Mix all ingredients together; cover.  Refrigerate overnight. Serve
chilled. Keep up to 2 weeks in refrigerator.
