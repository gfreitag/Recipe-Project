Title: Creamy Basil Yogurt Vinaigrette
Categories: Dress/stuff, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 cup
1 ts Dijon style mustard
3/4 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
Ground pepper
3 tb Basil, fresh chopped
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb White wine Vinegar
1 tb Balsamic Vinegar
1 x  Clove garlic, minced
Mix together the lemon juice, vinegar, garlic, mustard, and pepper.
Add chopped basil. Whip in the yogurt with a hand mixer. Modified
from a recipe for vinaigrette in: Reversing Heart Disease, Dean
Ornish, Random House, 1990
