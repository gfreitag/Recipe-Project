Title: Sugar On Snow
Categories: Kids, Countyfairs
Yield: 8 servings
Crushed Ice
1 c  Maple Syrup
1. Bring the syrup to a boil and simmer until it reaches 236 F on a
candy thermometer. Divide over 8 pie plates filled with crushed ice.

NOTE: Chewy treat, by tradition, eaten with sourpickles and doughnuts.

From section on the CHAMPLAIN VALLEY EXPOSITION of Essex Junction,
VT. Fair dates: Late August, early September, 8 days.
