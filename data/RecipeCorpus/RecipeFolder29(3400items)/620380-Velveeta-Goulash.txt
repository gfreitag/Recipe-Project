Title: 620380 Velveeta Goulash
Categories: Casseroles, Velveeta
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Elbow macaroni
2 lb Velveeta cheese
2 lb Hamburger
1 lg Can tomatoes
Brown ground beef; drain and rinse with hot water to remove grease.
Return to heat and add tomatoes.  Simmer 1/2 hour.  Cook macaroni and
drain. Put into large casserole and stir in ground beef. Slice cheese
and cover top of mixture. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven until
cheese is completely melted.
