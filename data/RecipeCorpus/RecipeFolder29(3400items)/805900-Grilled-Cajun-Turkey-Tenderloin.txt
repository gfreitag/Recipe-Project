Title: 805900 Grilled Cajun Turkey Tenderloin
Categories: Cajun/creol, Poultry
Yield: 5 servings
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/4    - 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/8    - 1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 pk Boneless fresh turkey
-breast tenderloins
1 1/2 ts Paprika
In small bowl combine all ingredients except turkey breast
tenderloins. Spray turkey lightly with Pam.  Coat evenly with spice
mixture. Prepare grill.  Place aluminum foil drip pan in center of
grill with hot coals around it, fill with 1/2" water.  Place turkey
on grill 4 to 6 inches from coals, directly over drip pan.  Cover
grill with lid. Grill 20 minutes. Turn and cover.  Grill 15 to 25
minutes more or until turkey is no longer pink in center.
