Title: Mexican Dip Tray
Categories: Tex/mexican, Dips/spread, Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/4 c  Grated Jack cheese
2    Chopped tomatoes
4 oz Chopped jalapenoes
1 cn Chopped black olives
3    Sliced green onions
32 oz Can refried beans
1 pk Taco seasoning, (or 2 tbsp
-taco seasoning)
1 pt Sour cream
2    Avocadoes, mashed
1 1/2 c  Grated cheddar cheese
Mix taco mix with beans and spread in pan (1 9x13 or 2 pie plates) Mix
avocadoes with sour cream.  Spread on top of bean mixture.  Mix the
two cheeses together and sprinkle half the mixture over the sour
cream mix. Place the chopped tomatoes, jalapeno's and olives over
cheese.  Spread rest of cheese over top and garnish with sliced green
onions.  Serve with taco, natcho, or tortilla chips.

Origin: Friend of mine Shared by: Sharon Stevens
