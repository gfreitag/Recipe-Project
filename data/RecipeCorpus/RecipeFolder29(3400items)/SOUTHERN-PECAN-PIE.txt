Title: SOUTHERN PECAN PIE
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 pie
2 c  Dark corn syrup; (1 bottle
-of Karo)
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Pecan halves
Pastry for 1 crust pie
2 tb Flour
1 tb Granulated sugar
3    Eggs; lightly beaten
1.  Preheat the oven to 425F.

2.  Line a 9" pie plate with the pastry.

3.  Combine the flour and sugar and add to the beaten eggs, beating
well. Add the corn syrup, vanilla and pecans, stirring well to blend.

4.  Pour the pecan mixture into the pie shell and bake 10 minutes.
Reduce the heat to 325F and continue baking about 45 minutes.
