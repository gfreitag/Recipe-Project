Title: Wine and Tarragon Sauce - Great Chefs
Categories: Basics, Salsa/sauce, Masterchefs, New orleans
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Wine, white
1/2 lb Butter
Salt (to taste)
Pepper, white (to taste)
2 lg Egg yolks
1 ds Salt
1 ts Juice, lemon
1 ts Tarragon
Carefully cook the egg yolks with a dash of salt and lemon juice,
stirring all the while.

Reduce the wine and tarragon to a paste and add.

Drizzle in butter until the sauce is thick and emulsified.
Taste and correct seasoning with salt and white pepper.

Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions
:    Box 71112, New Orleans, Louisiana - 1983
:    Chef Gunter Preuss, Versailles Restaurant, New Orleans
