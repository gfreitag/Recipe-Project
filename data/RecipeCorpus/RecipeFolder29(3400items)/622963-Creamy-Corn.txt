Title: 622963 Creamy Corn
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Onion salt
2 c  Corn
1    (3 oz.) pkg. cream cheese
1/4 c  Milk
1 tb Butter
Combine all ingredients in saucepan.  Place on low heat, stir until
hot. Garnish with paprika or parsley.
