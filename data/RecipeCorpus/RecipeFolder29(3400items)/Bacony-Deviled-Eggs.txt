Title: Bacony Deviled Eggs
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 24 servings
1 tb Prepared white horseradish
-or to taste
8 oz Bacon, cooked crisp
-drained and crumbled fine
4    Scallions, finely chopped
12 lg Hard-boiled eggs
-peeled and halved
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Sour cream
1 tb Dijon mustard
Put the egg yolks in a small bowl and mash them with the back of a
spoon until smooth. Stir in the mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard, and
horseradish. When the yolk mixture is smooth, stir in the bacon and
scallions. Taste for seasoning.

Using two spoons, stuff the egg whites with the yolk mixture. Cover
with plastic wrap and chill until ready to serve.
