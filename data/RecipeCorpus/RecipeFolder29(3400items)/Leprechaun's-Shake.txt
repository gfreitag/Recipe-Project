Title: Leprechaun's Shake
Categories: Kids, Beverages, Holiday
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Peppermint extract
3 dr Green food coloring
1 c  Milk; skim
2    Scoops frozen vanilla yogurt
Pour all ingredients into a blender and whirl until smooth and green.
Serve with a shamrock.  Source: Healthy Treats and Super Snaks for
