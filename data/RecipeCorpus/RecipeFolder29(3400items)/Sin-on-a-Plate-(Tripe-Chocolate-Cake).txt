Title: Sin on a Plate (Tripe Chocolate Cake)
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 1 cake
MMMMM----------------------------CAKE---------------------------------
1/2 c  Toasted almonds; chopped
1/4 c  Mayonnaise;
4    Eggs;
3 tb Almond liqueur (amaretto)
1 ts Almond extract;
1 c  Chocolate chips;
18 1/2 oz Box deep chocolate cake mix;
-(1 box) -=OR=-
18 1/2 oz Devil's food cake mix;
4 oz Box instant choc. pudding;
3/4 c  Sour cream;
1/2 c  Vegetable oil;
1/2 c  Water;
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
1 ts Almond extract;
1 c  Powdered sugar;
3 tb Milk;
CAKE: Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a 10" bundt pan and dust
with cocoa. Place all ingredients except chocolate chips in a large
bowl and beat 2 minutes with electric mixer on medium speed.  Mix in
chocolate chips. Pour into rack 10 minutes before removing from pan.
Place warm cake on serving dish and drizzle with glaze. GLAZE: Mix
all ingredients thoroughly in small bowl.  Let stand at room
temperature until ready to glaze.  Serves 10-12. Source: Carleen
