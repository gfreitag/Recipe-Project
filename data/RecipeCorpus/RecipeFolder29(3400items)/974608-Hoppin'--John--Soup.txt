Title: 974608 Hoppin'  John  Soup
Categories: Rice, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
2 md Onions, chopped
1    Bell pepper, chopped
1    Bay leaf
1/4 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Stalks celery, finely
-chopped
1/2 ts Seasoning salt
1 c  Dried black eyed peas
8 c  Water
1/2 ts Finely chopped jalapeno or
-other hot chili pepper
1    Clove garlic, finely
-chopped
1    Smoked turkey wing or leg
1/2 c  Uncooked long grain rice
Heat peas and water to boiling in 4 quart Dutch oven.  Boil,
uncovered, 2 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Cover and let stand 1 hour.
Stir in jalapeno, garlic, bay leaf, and turkey.  Heat to boiling;
reduce heat. Cover and simmer until peas are tender (1 to 1 1/2
hours, do not boil). Place bell pepper, onions, and celery in skillet
with 1/4 stick of margarine until tender.  Stir onion mixture along
with rice and other seasonings into Dutch oven.  Cover and simmer;
stir occasionally until rice is tender, about 25 minutes.  Remove bay
leaf before serving. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-16-95
