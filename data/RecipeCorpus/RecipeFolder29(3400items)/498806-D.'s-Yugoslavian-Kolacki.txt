Title: 498806 D.'s Yugoslavian Kolacki
Categories: Cake/cookie, East europe
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Baking powder
1 tb Sugar
1    Egg yolk
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Butter
1 tb Milk
1    (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese
Good Holiday cookies.  Sift flour, baking powder, and salt.  Cream
butter, cream cheese, milk and sugar.  Add egg yolk, vanilla and
flour. Mix and knead into a smooth dough.  Roll out 1/8 inch thick
and cut in squares. Place on ungreased cookie sheet and fill with
preserves or almonds or poppy seed is also good.  Pick up opposite
corners and press together in the center.  Bake in 400 degree oven
for 10 minutes or until slightly brown. Sprinkle with confectioners
sugar. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-24-95
