Title: Layered Meat And Polenta
Categories: Italian
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 c  Cold Water
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Parmesan Cheese -- grated
2 1/2 c  Bolognese Meat Or Meat And
Mushroom Sauce
2 c  Water
3/4 c  Yellow Cornmeal
Heat desired meat sauce; cover and keep warm. In saucepan bring 2 cups
water to a rolling boil. Meanwhile, combine cornmeal, 3/4 cup cold
water, and salt. Slowly pour cornmeal mixture into boiling water,
stirring constantly. Return just to boiling; reduce heat to low.
Cook, uncovered, about 5 mins or till thick, stirring frequently.
Pour about 2 Tbs of cornmeal mixture into each of eight 6-oz custard
cups. Spread each with about 2 Tbs of the meat sauce and sprinkle
with 1 Tbs of the cheese. Repeat the layers of cornmeal, meat and
cheese. Bake, uncovered, in 350 F oven about 15 mins. Let stand 5
mins before serving. Makes 8 servings.
