Title: Wilted Cabbage Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Dried mustard
1 ts Celery seed
1 ts Sugar
1 lg Head of cabbage, shredded
1 md Onion, sliced
3/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Salad oil
1 c  Vinegar
Layer cabbage and onion in a bowl.  Pour sugar on top but DO NOT MIX.
Bring other ingredients to a boil.  Pour hot dressing over cabbage,
onion and sugar.  Let stand for at least 3 hours but better
overnight. Mix before serving. Randy Rigg
