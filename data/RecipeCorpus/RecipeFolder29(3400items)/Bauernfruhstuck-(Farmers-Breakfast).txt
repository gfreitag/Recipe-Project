Title: Bauernfruhstuck (Farmers Breakfast)
Categories: Aust/german, Main dish, Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
3 ea Eggs; Large
2 ea Tomatoes; Medium, Peeled
3 tb Milk
1 tb Chives; Chopped
4 ea Potatoes; Medium
1/2 ts Salt
4 ea Bacon; Strips, Cubed
1 c  Ham; Cooked, Small Cubes
Boil unpeeled potatoes 30 minutes.  Rinse under cold water, peel and
set aside to cool.  Slice potatoes.  In a large fry pan cook bacon
until transparent.  Add the potato slices; cook until lightly
browned. Meanwhile blend eggs with milk and salt.  Stir in the cubed
ham.  Cut the tomatoes into thin wedges; add to the egg mixture.
Pour the egg mixture over the potatoes in the fry pan.  Cook until
the eggs are set. Sprinkle with chopped chives and serve at once.
