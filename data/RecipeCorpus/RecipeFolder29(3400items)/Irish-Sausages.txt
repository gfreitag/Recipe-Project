Title: Irish Sausages
Categories: Irish, Meats, Homemade, Sausage
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Fresh ground pepper
1/4 ts Dried sage and marjoram
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg and/or cloves
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1 1/2 lb Lean pork
1/2 lb Pork fat, no gristle
1/2 ts Mace
1 ts Salt
Mince the meat and fat twice, then mix very well and season. Fry a
teaspoon or so to check the flavor until you get it the way  you like
it. Add more herbs and spices as desired. Fill the skins.
