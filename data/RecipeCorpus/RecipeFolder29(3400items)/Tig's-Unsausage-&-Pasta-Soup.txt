Title: Tig's Unsausage & Pasta Soup
Categories: Fatfree, Soup/stew, Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Lemon pepper (or, some black
Pepper & a dash of lemon
Juice, I suppose)
1    Thinly sliced serrano chilis
(or more, or another
Variety, if you
Like)
1 c  Small shell pasta
1 c  Frozen peas
5 c  Vegetable broth
1 pk Morninstar links, sliced
(vegetarian sausage
Substitute)
1 md Onion, chopped
3    Green onions, thinly sliced
1    Bell pepper (your choice of
Color), diced
2    Bay leaves
1. In a large pot, combine veg broth, links, onion, green onion, bell
pepper, bay leaves, lemon pepper, and serranos. Simmer for 20 minutes.

2. Add pasta shells and simmer another 7 to 9 minutes, until cooked
tender.

3. Add peas and heat to warm. Ladle into bowls and serve.
