Title: KIDS' STUFF
Categories: Main dish, Kids
Yield: 1 meal  For those who have kids who don't like to sit down  to fancy meals...
take a cupcake  pan  and  place diced onion,carrots and potatoes in
each section. Then place a ball of raw meatloaf mix (or just plain
hamburger) in each. place in oven at 350 degrees til meat is just
done. put a quarter slice of cheese on  each and return to oven long
enough to melt cheese. can be stored in freezer and heated in
microwave as needed. Submitted By TIFFANY HALL-GRAHAM   On   04-18-95
