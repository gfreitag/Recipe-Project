Title: Tuna Fruit Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
1 ct Sour cream
1 c  Pecans
20 oz Can of Dole pineapple chunks
Drained
1 c  Mandarin oranges
1 c  Angel Flake coconut
Drain liquid from the pineapple and oranges.  Mix with remaining
ingredients.  Prepare salad at least two hours before serving. Randy
