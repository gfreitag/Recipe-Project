Title: Rye Bread *M*
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Ground allspice (optional)
2 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
1 tb Caraway seeds (optional)
1 tb Sugar
1 1/2 c  Warm water
1 ts Salt
===FOR 11/2 LB. LOAF===
1 pk Yeast
1 tb Honey
2 c  Rye flour
1 tb Margerine
2 c  Bread flour
Add all ingredients listed above, select white bread and push

start.
NOTE: Caraway seeds and allspice may be added to make an unusual

spicy rye, or omit them for a good conventional rye.
