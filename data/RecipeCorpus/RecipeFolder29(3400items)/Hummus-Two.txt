Title: Hummus Two
Categories: Nuts/grains, Vegetables, Chilies
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Coriander
1/2 ts Toasted Sesame Oil
3/4 c  Water
1/2 ts Salt
1 lb Chick peas
1 ea Habanero pepper
1 ea Garlic
1/4 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Cumine
Pack all into blender and process it until it is soft.
