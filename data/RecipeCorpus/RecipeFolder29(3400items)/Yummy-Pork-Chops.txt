Title: Yummy Pork Chops
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2/3 c  Lemon or lemon/lime
-sparkling beverage
1/4 ts Allspice
Salt and pepper to taste
Cooking oil
Posted by Stu Hopper
----------------------------
6 ea Pork chops
1 ea #303 can whole kernel corn
2 cn Mushroom soup
6 ea Green onions
Season chops with salt, pepper and allspice.  Braise in very small
amount of oil.  Add corn, green onions and heat slowly.  Add mushroom
soup blended with lemon or lemon/lime beverage.  Cover and let simmer
for about 15 to 20 minutes or until meat is done.
