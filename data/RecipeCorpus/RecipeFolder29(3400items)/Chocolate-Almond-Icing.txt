Title: Chocolate Almond Icing
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 recipe
1 c  Chocolate chips
1 c  Almond butter
1/2 c  Soymilk or water
1 c  Brown sugar
Combine soymilk, sugar & chocolate chips in a large saucepan.  Over a
medium heat, stir constantly, until the chocolate chips are melted &
the mixture comes to a boil.  Add amond butter & mix well.  Transfer
icing to a bowl & cool to room temperature.  The icing will thicken
as it cools.
