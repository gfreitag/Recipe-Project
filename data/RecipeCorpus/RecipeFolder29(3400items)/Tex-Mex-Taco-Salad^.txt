Title: Tex-Mex Taco Salad^
Categories: Tex/mexican, Healthy, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
4 c  Can Opener chili, (separate
Recipe)
1    Head lettuce, shredded
2 lg Chopped tomatoes
1 sm Onion, diced
Line plates with vegetables and top with chili.

Variation: Serve with baked corn chips.

Per serving: Calories: 275 Protein: 15g Carbohydrates: 40g Fat: 6g
Sodium: 443mg Cholesterol: trace
