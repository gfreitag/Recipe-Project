Title: 279710 Yellow Squash Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Margarine
1 c  Bread crumbs or Pepperidge
-Farm dressing
-Salt & pepper to taste
2 c  Yellow cooked squash
2    Eggs, beaten
1 c  Cheese, grated
2    Green peppers, chopped
Mix squash, eggs, cheese and peppers.  Melt oleo and mix with bread
crumbs. Add half of bread crumbs to squash mixture.  Put into greased
baking dish. Add remaining crumbs to top.  Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees.
