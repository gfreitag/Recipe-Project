Title: Fried Squash Bread
Categories: Breads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1    Egg
1/4 c  Buttermilk
2    Summer Squash -- diced
1 c  Cornmeal
Wash clean and dice 2 summer squash can cook until soft (do not cook
dry, leave about 3/4 cup of water in pot). Combine: 1 cup of cornmeal
(or cornmeal mix) 1 egg 1/4 cup of buttermilk Add squash and water
and mix together. Fry in hot oil until golden brown.
