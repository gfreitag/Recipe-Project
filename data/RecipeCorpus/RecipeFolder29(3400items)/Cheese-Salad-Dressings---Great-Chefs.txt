Title: Cheese Salad Dressings - Great Chefs
Categories: Basics, Dress/stuff, Masterchefs, New orleans
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2 tb Butter
1 tb Wine, Port
3 tb Cheese, Roquefort OR
3 tb Cheese, Bleu
Dressing: =========

Warm the ingredients gently until softened then mix the
ingredients with a whisk.

Makes enough for two salads.

Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions
:    Box 71112, New Orleans, Louisiana - 1983
:    Chef Michel Marcais, Begue's Restaurant, New Orleans
