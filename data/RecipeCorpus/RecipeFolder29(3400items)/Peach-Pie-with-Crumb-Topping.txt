Title: Peach Pie with Crumb Topping
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
9    Inch unbaked pie shell
8    Peaches; peeled & sliced
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Vanilla
3 tb Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/3 c  Margarine; softened
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
In a large bowl, combine filling ingredients; toss well to distribute
evenly.  In another bowl, mix together topping ingredients until
crumbly. Turn filling into shell and sprinkle with topping.  Bake in
a preheated 375 oven for 1 to 1 1/4 hours or until brown and bubbly.
Each serving contains 351 calories and 12 grams fat.  Source:  Store
