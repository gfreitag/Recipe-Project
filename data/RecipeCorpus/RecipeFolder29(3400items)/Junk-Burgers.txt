Title: Junk Burgers
Categories: Sandwich, Meats
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Catsup
1 tb Mustard
2 lb Ground beef
1 cn Chicken gumbo soup
2 tb Minced onion
Brown the ground beef and onion in a skillet.  Add soup, do not add
water to soup; add catsup and mustard and simmer on low heat for 10
minutes. Stir often.  Serve on toasted or at least warm buns. Randy
