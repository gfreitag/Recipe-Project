Title: Wine Country Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Green peppercorns OR
2 ts Freshly ground pepper
1 ts Salt
4 tb Brandy
3/4 c  Heavy cream
5    Cloves garlic wrapped in
-cheesecloth
3/4 ts Thyme
1/2 c  Raisins soaked in hot water
-for 5 minutes, and drained
Flour and water
2    To 3 lbs Top Sirloin, cut
-into 2-inch cubes
2 tb Bacon fat
1/2 lb Westphalian ham, cut in 1
-inch cubes
2 lg Onions, chopped
25    Stuffed green olives,
-halved
1    Bottle (but no more than
-3 cups) full bodied
Red wine (petite Sirah,
Zinfandel, etc.)
Brown beef in 12" skillet with bacon fat over moderately high heat,
for 3-5 minutes.  Add ham and onions, and saute' 3-4 minutes until
onions are golden brown.  Place beef, onions, and ham in large Dutch
oven.  Add olives, garlic, raisins, and wine.  Cover and cook in
preheated oven at 350 F for 40 minutes.  Add peppercorns, salt,
brandy, and cream. Continue cooking for 30 minutes longer.  Strain
the juices and arrange meat, keeping warm.  Return juices and add
1&1/2 tbs. flour, and 3 tbls. water per cup of juice.  Heat with low
heat for about 5 minutes, stirring. Pour over the meat and serve with
rice dish and a buttered vegetable.
