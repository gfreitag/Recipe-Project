Title: Mrs. Miller's Blueberry Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Sugar
3 tb Cornstarch
Pie shell, 9"; baked
5 c  Blueberries, fresh; washed
-and well drained, divided
3/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Water
1 ts Lemon rind; grated
In saucepan, combine 1 cup blueberries, 3/4 cup sugar, 3/4 cup water,
and 1 teaspoon lemon rind.  Bring mixture to a boil. Mix 1/4 cup
water, 1/4 cup sugar, and cornstarch and add to hot mixture. Cook
until clear and thick. Cool.  Pour thickened mixture over 4 cups
blueberries in baked pie shell. Chill until ready to serve.
