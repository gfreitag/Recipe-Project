Title: Pepper Spice Bread *M*
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Whole anise seed
1/4 c  Nonfat dry milk powder
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 c  Soft butter
1/4 ts Allspice
1    Egg
3/4 c  Very warm water
2 tb Honey
===FOR 11/2 LB. LOAF===
1 pk Yeast
2 tb Corn syrup
3 c  Bread flour
1/4 c  Pecans (or other nuts)
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground black
Pepper
1/4 c  Sugar
Put in all ingredients in the order listed, select white bread and

push start.
NOTE: This recipe produces a light brown loaf with a unique spicy

taste.
