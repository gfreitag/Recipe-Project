Title: Tallerine
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1    Green pepper
2 tb Chili powder
Salt and pepper to taste
2 lb Ground beef
2 lg Chopped onions
2    Garlic buttons
Combine and cook all of the above in a skillet. Boil one large
package of egg noodles and drain.

1 lb. American cheese, grated 1 can Ro-Tel tomatoes 1 can tomato soup
1 soup can of water 1 can whole kernal corn

Place one-half of the noodles in bottom of baking dish; place meat on
noodles; place corn on meat.  Put one half of the cheese on the corn.
Put the rest of the noodles on top and cover with tomatoes, tomato
soup and water.  Cover with remaining cheese and bake at 350 degrees
for 1 1/2 to 2 hours. Randy Rigg
