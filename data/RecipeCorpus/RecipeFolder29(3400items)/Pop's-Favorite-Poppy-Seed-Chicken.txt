Title: Pop's Favorite Poppy Seed Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Water
1 c  Sour Cream
1 pk Almond slivers (optional)
3 lb Frying chicken, cut up
1    Envelope Mushroom Soup Mix
1 cn Celery soup
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
3 tb Poppy Seeds
1    Stick Margarine
1    Long pkg Ritz Crackers
Boil chicken until tender; debone and shred.  Add mushroom soup mix,
celery soup, water, sour cream, and almonds.  Place in a baking dish.
Cook at 325 degrees for 25 minutes.

TOPPING: Mix all together, sprinkle on top of casserole and broil 3
minutes.
