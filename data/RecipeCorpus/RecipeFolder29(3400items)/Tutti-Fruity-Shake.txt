Title: Tutti-Fruity Shake
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Canned in syrup fruit, or
1/2 c  Fruit cocktail.
1/2 c  Half & half cream
1/2 c  Vanilla ice cream
1 pk Vanilla instant breakfast
-drink
A fun shake to make with a different fruity taste every time.

Drain canned fruit.  Mix and blend ingredients until smooth.

Makes 1 serving, 518 calories, 13 g protein.
