Title: Orange, Cucumber and Peanut Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
-sliced
1 tb Rice vinegar
1 tb Oriental sesame oil
Salt and pepper
1    Cucumber; scrubbed and
-thinly sliced
1 tb Peanuts; chopped
1    Orange; peeled and thinly
In plastic container, mix all of the ingredients together.  Season
with salt and pepper.  Cover tightly.  Source:  Penny Whistle Lunch
