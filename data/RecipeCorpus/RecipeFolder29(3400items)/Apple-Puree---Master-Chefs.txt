For Apple Puree: ================

Place the apples in a heavy saucepan over medium-low heat,
adding a few drops of water if they're "dry." Cover the pan and cook,
stirring occasionally, until slightly softened, 10 to 15 minutes.

Break the apples up into 1-inch pieces.  Refrigerate, covered,
until cold.
