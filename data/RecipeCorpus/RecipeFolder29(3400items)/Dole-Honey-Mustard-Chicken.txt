Title: Dole Honey Mustard Chicken
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Thyme, crumbled
2 ts Vegetable oil
1 tb Cornstarch
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Dijon mustard
1 cn Dole pinapple slices
(20 oz)
4 ea Boneless, skinless chicken
Breast halves
Salt and pepper to taste
2 lg Cloves garlic, pressed
Drain pineapple, reserve juice. Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper
to taste. Rub with garlic and thyme. Brown in hot oil in nonstick
skillet. Combine 2 tablespoons reserved juice with cornstarch.
Combine honey and mustard; stir in to skillet with remaining juice.
Spoon sauce over chicken. Cover, simmer 15 minutes. Stir cornstarch
mixture into pan juices. Add pineapple. Cook, stirring, until sauce
boils and thickens.

Source: A promotional booklet by Dole
