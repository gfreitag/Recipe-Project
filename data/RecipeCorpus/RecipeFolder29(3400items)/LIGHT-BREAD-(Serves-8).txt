Title: LIGHT BREAD (Serves 8)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Salt
1/3 c  Sugar
-flour to make soft batter
1 1/2    Pints potato water
-when lukewarm, soak:
1    Yeast cake
Set in warm place overnight.  Take 1 pint warm water and more flour
to mix stiff.  Let rise.  Knead and let rise again.  Mold into
greased loaf pans. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 45 minutes.
