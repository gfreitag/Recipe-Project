Title: 621937 Corn Pone Pie
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 12 servings
1 cn Cream style corn
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1    Mixing favorite cornbread
-recipe
1 lb Ground beef
1    (15 oz.) can Ranch style
-beans
1 lg Can tomato sauce with bits
1 cn Tomatoes
Cook ground beef and onions until done.  Season as desired.  Add
beans, corn, tomatoes, and tomato sauce.  Heat until boiling. Remove
from stove. Mix your favorite cornbread recipe and spoon over meat
mixture and bake in 9 x 12 inch casserole dish at 400 degrees until
cornbread is a golden brown.  (Pans should be no deeper than 3
inches.) May be baked in two smaller pans, if desired.)
