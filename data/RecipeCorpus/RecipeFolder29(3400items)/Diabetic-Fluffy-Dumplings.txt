Title: Diabetic Fluffy Dumplings
Categories: Diabetic, Main dish, Soup/stew, Breads
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Margerine or butter
1/3 c  Milk
1 c  All purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
Combine flour, baking powder and salt. Cut in butter until mixture
resembles fine crumbs. Add milk, stir until just moistened. Divide
into 6 portions and spoon onto bubbling stew, soup or meat mixture.
Cover tightly and simmer 15 minutes without lifting lid.

Makes 6 small dumplings 1 serving = 1 dumpling
1 starchy choice
14 g carbohydrate
3 g protein
2 g fat
360 kilojoules
86 calories
