Title: Black Cherry Coke Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
2 pk Black cherry Jello
2 c  Coke
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1 cn Black cherries, drained
1 lg Can crushed pineapple, drain
3 oz Philadelphia cream cheese
Heat coke and dissolve Jello; drain cherries and pineapple.  If
juices do not make 2 cups of liquid, add water.  Chill until syrupy.
Beat cheese until fluffy and add pineapple, cherries and nuts.  Fold
into Jello and chill. Randy Rigg
