Title: 498787 Empanadas
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Flour
-Jam
2    Sticks (1/2 lb.) butter
1    (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese
Cream butter and cream cheese.  Add flour and beat well.  Chill
overnight. Roll out (1/3 at a time) fairly thin.  Cut into circles
about 2 1/2 to 3 inches across.  Put a dab of jam in each circle,
fold in half and seal edges with a fork.  Prick tops and bake in 350
degree oven for 15 to 18 minutes.  Roll in powdered sugar after
slightly cooled. Roll in sugar again after completely cooled.
