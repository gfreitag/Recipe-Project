Title:  Biscuits For Two
Categories: Breads, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 ts Cream of tartar
1/4 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Flour
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
In a bowl, combine dry ingredients.  Cut in shortening until mixture
resembles coarse crumbs.  Stir in milk.  Turn onto a floured board;
knead 1 minute.  Roll or pat dough to 1/2" thickness.  Cut with a
floured 2 1/2" biscuit cutter. Place on a greased baking sheet.  Bake
at 450 degrees for 10-12 minutes.

Yields:  4 biscuits From: "Taste of Home" Magazine, Feb/Mar 1995
