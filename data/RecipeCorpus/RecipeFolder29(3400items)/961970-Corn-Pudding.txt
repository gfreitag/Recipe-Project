Title: 961970 Corn Pudding
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
1    (17 oz.) can whole kernel
-corn
1    (8 1/2 oz.) pkg. cornbread
-mix
-Pimientos & parsley for
-garnish
3    Eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 c  Butter or margarine, melted
1 sm Onion, chopped
1    Green pepper, chopped
1    (17 oz.) can cream style
-corn
Combine first 7 ingredients.  Stir well.  Spoon into 6 well greased
custard or muffin cups . Place on baking sheet and bake at 350
degrees for 40 to 45 minutes or until set.  May also be baked in
shallow 2 quart casserole at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until set.
Serve out of casserole on table. For custard cups or muffin, loosen
edges with spatula, invert onto plate and garnish.  This is delicious.
