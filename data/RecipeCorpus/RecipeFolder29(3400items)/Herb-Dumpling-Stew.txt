Title: Herb Dumpling Stew
Categories: Irish, Soup/stew, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
2    Tomatoes, peeled and
-chopped
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 lg Bunch fresh mixed herbs,
-tied with a string
2 1/2 c  Beef broth
2 lb Stew meat, lamb or beef
1/2 c  Flour
3 tb Vegetable oil
2    Onions
2    Carrots, chopped in large
-pieces
4    Potatoes, chopped
Dumplings (see below) Salt and pepper to taste

Coat meat in flour,  then brown in oil in a skillet.  Add onions and
saute. Place browned meat,  onions and vegetables in large cooking
pot. Add garlic powder.  Place herbs  in  middle  of mixture. Cover
with broth, cook 2 hours over low heat.  While stew is cooking, make
dumplings. During last 20 minutes  of cooking, add dumplings. Salt
and pepper to taste.

Makes 6 to 8 servings
