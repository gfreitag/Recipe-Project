Title: Rodney Darling's Blackened Pig
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
Sauce (optional)
1 md To large pig
1 md Sized house
Run the pig into the house.  Shut all of the doors.  Set the house
afire. Return in 3 days with oven mitts.  Search and serve.  Serve
with sauce if desired.  Serves the entire Darling family. Submitted
by: Rodney Dillard - Cast member, Mayberry. Origin: Aunt Bee's
Mayberry Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Nov/94.
