Title: 249452 Corn Souffle
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
2    Eggs, beaten with fork
1 sm Can Ortega green chilies,
-diced
-Lots of grated Tillamook
-cheese
1 c  Yellow cornmeal
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 pt Sour cream
1 cn (15-16 oz.) cream style corn
3/4 c  Wesson oil (may use less)
Mix ingredients in a three inch deep casserole.  Top with grated
cheese. Bake uncovered in preheated 375 degree oven for 45 minutes.
Serves 6. Easy to double and freezes well.
