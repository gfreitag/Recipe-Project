Title: Smoked Pork Chops With Polenta
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 servings
4    (1 1/4 Lb) Smoked Pork Chops
2 tb Parmesan Cheese -- grated
2 tb Parsley -- chopped
3 1/2 c  Chicken Broth,
Regular-Strength -- boiling
1 c  Instant Polenta
1 cn Creamed Corn -- (8 1/2 oz)
Pour broth into a shallow 2 1/2- to 3-qt baking dish. Gradually and
smoothly stir in polenta; mix in corn. Lay pork chops on top of
polenta mixture. Bake in a 350 F oven until pork chops are hot in
thickest part, 25 to 30 mins. Sprinkle meat with grated Parmesan
cheese and chopped parsley. Serves 4.
