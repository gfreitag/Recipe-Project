Title: Jenny's Peanut Butter Noodles
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts White wine vinegar
2 cl Garlic; crushed
1 ts Onion; grated
8 oz Linguine
3 tb Peanut butter
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Oriental sesame oil
Cook the linguine according to the directions on the package.  Drain.
Meanwhile in a large bowl, mix all of the remaining ingredients
together. Add the linguine to the sauce and toss to coat well.
Source: Penny Whistle Lunch Box Book by Meridith (Mrs. Tom) Brokaw
and Annie Gilbar. Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   03-24-95
