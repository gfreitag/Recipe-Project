Title: Mama's Pickled Beets
Categories: Can/preserv, Pickles
Yield: 3 pints
1 pt Vinegar
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar
3 lb Beets
1    Stick Cinnamon
1 ts Whole allspice
6    Whole cloves
Cook beets in boiling water until tender, remove skins, roots and
tops. Tie spices in cheesecloth bag. Heat vinegar, water, sugar and
spices to boiling. Add beets, whole or sliced and boil 5 minutes.
Remove spice bag, pack beets in sterile jars and fill jars with hot
liquid. Seal. Submitted By KATHRYN CONE   On   03-30-95
