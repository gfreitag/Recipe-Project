Title: Ginger Beef
Categories: Meats, Chinese
Yield: 4 servings
-pressed
2 tb Ginger, fresh; chopped
3    Scallion; cut in 1/2" wide
-slanting slices
1 tb Cornstarch
1 tb ;Water
1/4 c  Regular strength beef broth
1 1/2 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Shao xing
1 ts Sugar
1 1/2 lb Boneless beef sirloin
2 tb Salad oil
1 sm Garlic clove; minced or
Combine broth, soy, shao xing and sugar; set aside.

Cut beef with the grain into 1 1/2 in wide strips. Cut each strip
across the grain in 1/8 in thick slanting slices.

Place wok over med-high heat. When wok is hot, add 1 tbsp oil. When
oil is hot, add garlic and ginger. Stir fry for about 30 seconds.
Turn heat to high. Add half the beef and onion and stir-fry until
meat is lightly browned (about 2 minutes); remove from wok. Repeat,
using remaining 1 tbsp oil, beef and onion. Return cooked meat to
wok. Pour in broth mixture; cover and simmer for 3 minutes. Mix
cornstarch and water and gradually stir into wok. Cook until slightly
thickened, stirring to coat meat (about 1 minute).
