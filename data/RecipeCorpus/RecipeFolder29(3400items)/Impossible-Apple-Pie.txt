Title: Impossible Apple Pie
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
2    Eggs
3/4 c  Milk
2 tb Butter or margerine
1 ts Vanilla
5 c  Cooking apples peeled and
Cored
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Biscuit mix
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/4 ts Salt
3 tb Butter or margerine
2/3 c  Flour
1/3 c  Brown sugar packed
Place apples in greased 10 inch pie plate.  Pour sugar over top and
sprinkle cinnamon over sugar.  Mix the next 5 ingredient in a blender
and blend until smooth.  Pour over pie.  Mix flour, sugar, salt, and
butter until crumbly.  Scatter over apples.  Bake at 350 for about 50
minutes until apples are cooked.
