Title: Chicken Ravioli Filling
Categories: Pasta, Italian, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Parsley, chopped
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb White wine
1/4 c  Parmesan, grated
1 c  Chicken, cooked, minced
2 tb Shallots, finely chopped
2    Plum tomatoes, chopped
1 ts Dried sage
1/4 ts Basil
Place all ingredients in a large mixing bowl and combine thoroughly.
Fill ravioli with mixture.
