Title: 1029349 Velveeta Cheese Potatoes
Categories: Casseroles, Potato, Velveeta
Yield: 1 servings
-Salt and pepper
2    Sticks margarine or butter
3 sl Bread, torn in pieces
10 md Potatoes, cooked and diced
1    Onion, sliced or diced
1/2 lb Velveeta cheese
Combine potatoes, onions and bread pieces into large casserole dish.
Salt and pepper to taste.  Melt margarine and cheese together. Pour
over mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees, 45 minutes or until bubbly, hot,
and brown. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-12-95
