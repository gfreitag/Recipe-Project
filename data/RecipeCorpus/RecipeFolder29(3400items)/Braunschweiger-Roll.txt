Title: Braunschweiger Roll
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
1 tb Horseradish
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1 lb Braunschweiger
8 oz Philadelphia cream cheese
1/4 c  Catsup
Mix together everything except about on-fourth of the cream cheese and
reserve for later.  Form into a ball and chill in freezer for about 1
hour. Remove and ice with remaining cream cheese and garnish with
sliced green olives.  Serve with your favorite crackers; personally I
skip the olives but my neice loves them. Randy Rigg
