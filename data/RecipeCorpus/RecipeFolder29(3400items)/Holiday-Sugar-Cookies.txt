Title: Holiday Sugar Cookies
Categories: Holiday, Cake/cookie
Yield: 42 servings
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 ts Almond extract
2 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
Decorators' sugar, optional
1 c  Butter or margarine,
-softened
1 1/2 c  Confectioners' sugar
1    Egg, lightly beaten
You can use heart cutter and red decorators' sugar for Valentines,
shamrock cutter and green decorators' sugar for St. Patrick's, etc.

Cream butter and sugar in a mixing bowl. Add egg and extracts. Stir in
flour; mix well. Chill several hours. Roll dough to 1/4-inch
thickness on a lightly floured surface. Cut with a 2 1/2 or 3-inch
cookie cutter. Place on ungreased baking sheets; sprinkle with sugar,
if desired. Bake at 375 for 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned.

Yield:  3 1/2 dozen cookies.
