Title: Apple Loaf
Categories: Breads, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  Shortening
2/3 c  Brown sugar
2    Eggs
1 c  Thick applesauce
2 c  Sifted flour
Mix shortening, sugar and eggs. Stir in applesauce. Sift together and
add flour, baking powder, soda and salt. Fold in nuts.

Pour into greased loaf pan.  Bake 50-55 mins in 350 oven.
