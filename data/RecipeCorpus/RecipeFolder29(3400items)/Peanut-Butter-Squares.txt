Title: Peanut Butter Squares
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/4 c  Peanut butter
12 oz Chocolate chips
1/3 lb Graham cracker crumbs
1/3 lb Melted oleo
1 lb Powdered sugar
Mix all but chips until smooth.  Pat down firmly in 9x13 pan.  Melt
choc chips, and spread over top.  Chill until chocolate is firm.  Cut
into squares.
