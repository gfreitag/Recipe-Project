Title: Beef Paprika
Categories: Meats, Herbs/spice, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Brown Sugar
2 ts Salt
2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Dry Mustard
ds Cayenne Pepper
1 1/2 c  Water
2 tb Flour
3 c  Hot Cooked Noodles
1/4 c  Shortening
2 lb Beef Chuck or Round
Cut into 1" Cubes
1 c  Sliced Onion
1 sm Clove Garlic, Minced
3/4 c  Ketchup
2 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 c  Water
Melt shortening in skillet. Add meat, onion and garlic. Cook and stir
till meat is brown; stir in ketchup, worcestershire sauce, sugar,
salt paprika, mustard, cayenne and 1 1/2 cups water. Cover. Simmer 2-
2 1/2 hours. Blend flour and 1/4 cup water. Stir gradually into meat.
Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute. Serve
over noodles. Makes 6-8 servings.
