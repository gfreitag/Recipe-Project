Title: Rich Pound Cake^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 14 servings
OR
6    Egg whites
1 ts Butter flavoring, optional
1/4 c  (to1/2) buttermilk
4 ts Canola oil
1 c  Nonfat yogurt
2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Coriander
1/2 c  Brown rice syrup
3/4 c  Egg Beaters
Preheat oven to 350F. Spray a loaf pan with pam and set aside.
Combine dry ingredients in one bowl and dry ingredients in another.
Combine the two, a bit at a time, beating well after each. Pour into
pan and bake 50-60 minutes until golden and a toothpick inserted in
