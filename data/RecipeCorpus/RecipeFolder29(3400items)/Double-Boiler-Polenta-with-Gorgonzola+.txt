Title: Double-Boiler Polenta with Gorgonzola+
Categories: Italian, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Gorgonzola cheese in 4
-1 oz. slices
1    Recipe Double-Boiler Polenta
2 tb Unsalted butter, softened
Prepare Double Boiler Polenta. Stir in butter. Divide polenta among
four bowls. Top each with a slice of cheese. Serve immediately.
