Title: 566254 Cajun Meatloaf
Categories: Cajun/creol, Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Ground pork
1    Egg (beaten)
1 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Salt
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Ketchup
1 tb Butter
1 md Onion (chopped)
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Green bell pepper
2    Cloves garlic (minced)
3 sl White bread
1/3 c  Milk
1 1/2 lb Ground chuck
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  In frying pan melt butter, add onion,
celery, green pepper and garlic.  Cook until wilted. In a bowl, soak
bread in milk. Mix meat and other mixtures, also the remaining other
ingredients. Shape into loaf.  Bake one hour.
