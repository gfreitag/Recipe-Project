Title: Weeknight Fajitas
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Microwave
Yield: 8 fajitas
1 md Onion, cut in thin wedges
1 cl Garlic, minced (optional)
1/2 ts Dried oregano leaves,crushed
1 ea Tomato, cut in thin wedges
8 ea Flour tortillas (6-inch)
1/3 c  7 Seas Viva Italian Dressing
1 1/4 lb Boneless Chicken breasts-
Halved, skinned & cut-
Into strips
1 ea Green bell pepper-
Cut into strips
In large bowl, pour dressing over chicken, green pepper, onion,
garlic and oregano.  Toss lightly.  Refrigerate at least 10 minutes.
Drain.
Stir-fry chicken mixture until chicken is browned and no longer
pink. Stir in tomatoes.
Wrap tortillas in dampened paper towels.  Microwave on high 45
seconds to 1 1/2 minutes or until softened.
Fill tortillas with chicken mixture.  Serve with salsa, sour
cream, shredded cheese, refried beans or guacamole.

VARIATION:  Substitute 1 pound flank steak or pork cutlets for
chicken.
