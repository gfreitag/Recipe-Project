Title: Bay Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM--------------------------CHICKEN:-------------------------------
-sliced
2 cn Condensed golden mushroom
-soup
1/2 c  Dry white wine
2 c  Water
2    Pimientos, diced
2    Broiler-fryers, cut up
-(2 1/2 lbs each)
3 tb Butter
1/2 c  Sliced green onion
1 sm Green pepper, quartered,
1 lg Carrot, pared and thinly
MMMMM-------------------------BISCUITS:------------------------------
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Vegetable shortening
1 c  Milk
1 3/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Cornmeal
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
Brown chicken pieces in butter in a large skillet; place in a 10-cup
shallow casserole.  Stir green onion, green pepper and carrot into pan
drippings; saute 3 minutes.  Stir in soup, wine and water.  Cook,
stirring constantly; until mixture bubbles.  Stir in pimiento; pour
over chicken; cover.  Bake in moderate oven (375 degrees) one hour.
While the chicken is baking, combine dry biscuit ingredients and mix
well. Cut in shortening with a pastry blender until a coarse meal is
formed, then mix in milk.
Increase oven temperature to 425 degrees, drop biscuits in a ring
around the edge of the casserole,sprinkle with a little additional
cornmeal and return to the oven to bake, uncovered for 15 minutes
(biscuits are golden); sprinkle with parsley just before serving.
