Title: Broccoli Soup #1
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Cornstarch
1/4 ts Salt
ds Black Pepper
ds Ground Thyme
1/4 c  Swiss Cheese, grated
1 1/2 c  Fresh Broccoli, chopped or
10 oz frozen broccoli
1/4 c  Celery, diced
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
1 c  Low Sodium Chicken Broth
2 c  Nonfat Milk
Place vegetables and broth in saucepan. Bring to boiling, reduce heat,
cover and cook until vegetables are tender about 8 minutes. Mix milk,
corn starch, salt, pepper & thyme; add to cooked vegetables. Cook
stirring constantly, until soup is lightly thickened and mixture just
begins to boil. Add cheese and stir until melted. Serve.

Per serving (1 cup): 115 calories, 10 mg cholesterol, 225 mg sodium
