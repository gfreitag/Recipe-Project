Title: Chile Sausage Quiche
Categories: Main dish, Pie/tart, Tex/mexican, Sausage
Yield: 6 servings
4    Eggs -- lightly beaten
2 c  Light cream
1/2 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1/2 c  Grated swiss cheese
Salt and pepper to taste
1    10-inch uncooked pie shell
1 cn (7oz) Ortega Whole Green
Chiles
1 lb Sausage meat --
Cooked/crumbled
Line bottom of pie shell with split and seeded whole chiles. Sprinkle
sausage meat over chiles.  Combine eggs, cream, cheeses and
seasonings; pour over sausage.  Bake in pre-heated oven (350 degree)
for 30-40 minutes, or until top is golden brown and quiche is set.
Remove from oven and allow to set for 5 minutes before serving.
