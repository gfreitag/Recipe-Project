Title: Applesauce Cinnamon Bread
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker, Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
2 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Butter
1 1/2 ts Yeast
1/3 c  Applesauce
3/4 c  Buttermilk
2 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 tb Cinnamon
Place all ingredients in pan in order listed (Hitachi machine).
Adjust as needed for your machine.
