Title: Winter Turkey Salad ^
Categories: Poultry, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper to taste
3 dr Tabasco sauce
1/2    Lemon, juice of
Lettuce leaves
2 c  Chopped cooked turkey
1/4 c  Sliced celery
2 tb Finely chopped pimentos
2 tb Capers
1 tb Finely chopped scallions
1 tb Finely chopped parsley
Toss turkey, celery, pimento, capers, scallion and parsley together.
Combine mayonnaise, mustard, salt, pepper and Tabasco; stir lightly
into turkey mixture. Sprinkle with lemon juice and toss lightly. To
serve, arrange lettuce leaves in slald bowl and place turkey salad in
center.
