Title: 961971 El Paso Corn Pudding Bake
Categories: Tex/mexican, Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
-Pepper & savory salt to
-taste
-Grated cheese
10 oz Can whole green chilies
1 lb Can cream style corn
1 tb Flour
2    Eggs, well beaten
1/4 c  Butter, melted
Cut chile in strips and line greased glass baking dish.  Mix rest of
ingredients and pour and cover chilies.  Cover with plenty of grated
cheese.  Bake 30 minutes or until set in 350 degree oven. Serves 4.
