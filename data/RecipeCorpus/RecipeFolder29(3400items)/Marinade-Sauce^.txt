Title: Marinade Sauce^
Categories: Marinades, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 12 servings
1 tb Dry sherry, or to taste
2 tb Apple juice concentrate
2 tb Tamari or low sodium soy
Sauce
5 tb Water
Combine and use as a marinade.

Variations: Add: 1 ts freshly grated gingerroot 1/2 to 1 ts minced
fresh garlic 1/2 to 1 ts grated orange rind 1/2 ts no salt added
Dijon mustard 1/4 ts sesame or hot chili oil

Combine 1 tb cornstarch and 2 tb cold water and use to thicken
marinade for use as gravy.

Per tablespoon serving: Calories: 115 Protein: 3g Carbohydrates: 25g
Fat: 0g Sodium: 858mg Cholesterol: 0mg
