Title: Carolina Classic Chicken Risotto
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/2 c  Water
2    Chicken Flavor Bouillan
Cubes
4 tb Parmesan Cheese, freshly
Grated
1 tb Parsley, finely chopped
2 tb Butter
1 tb Olive Oil
1 md Onion, finely chopped
2 c  Raw Chicken,cubed
1 1/2 c  Carolina Rice
2 ts Italian Seasoning
1    Bay Leaf
Heat butter and oil in a large saucepan. Saute the onion and chicken
for three minutes. Add rice and italian seasoning, cook for 2
minutes. Add the bouillan cubes, water and bay leaf. Bring to a boil.
Stir once and lower heat to simmer. Cover and cook for 18 minutes or
until rice is creamy and water is absorbed. Remove bay leaf. Lightly
toss with parmesan cheese and parsley. Serve.
