Title: Honey Dew Biscuits
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sour Cream
1 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg
1 c  Butter -- whipped
1 c  Honey -- strained
2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
Mix well all ingredients and bake in greased and floured muffin pans.
Bake @ 375 F for 10-12 mins.
