Title: Butter Cream Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Lemon extract
3 tb Half and half
1 lb 10x confectioners' sugar
1/2 c  Unsalted butter, room
-temperature
1.  Beat together 10x sugar, butter, vanilla, lemon extract, and
half-and-half in a large bowl with electric mixer at medium-low speed
until smooth and creamy.  (The frosting can be made ahead and
refrigerated, covered, for up to 2 weeks.  Before using, let stand at
room temperature until soft enough to spread.)

2. Spread the frosting evenly over tops and sides of some of the
cakes.
