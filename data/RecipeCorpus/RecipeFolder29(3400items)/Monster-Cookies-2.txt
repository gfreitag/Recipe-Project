Title: Monster Cookies 2
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Butter
4 ts Baking soda
9 c  Oatmeal
1 c  Chocolate chips
1 c  M&M's
6    Eggs
2 c  White sugar
2 1/3 c  Brown sugar
2 1/3 c  Peanut butter
1 ts Vanilla
1 tb Corn syrup
350 degree oven for 11 minutes

(I usually throw in the whole bag of chips and M&M's if it's the
large bag)

In LARGE bowl or pot cream eggs, sugars, add peanut butter, vanilla,
corn syrup. Mix in butter and baking soda. By hand stir in oatmeal,
chocolate chips and M&M's. Drop by teaspoon, tablespoon, or large
stirring spoon depending on size you want (I usually use a tblsp)
