Title: 879750 Corn Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1    Purple onion, chopped
1    Cucumber, chopped
2 cn Corn, drained
2    Tomatoes, chopped
1    Bell pepper, chopped
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE:--------------------------------
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sour cream
4 tb Mayonnaise
2 tb Wine vinegar
1/2 ts Celery seed
Mix all ingredients and chill overnight.
