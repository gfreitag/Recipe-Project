Title: Cardamon Cookies - Refrigerated
Categories: Cake/cookie, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Sifted all purpose flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Baking soda
1 c  Soft butter or margarine
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Ground cardamon
1/2 c  Sour cream
Cream butter and sugar until light.  Add remaining ingredients and mix
well.

Shape into 2 long rolls 2 inches in diameter.  Roll up in wax paper
and chill overnight.  Cut into 1/8 inch slices and arrange on
ungreased cookie sheet.

Bake in preheated moderate oven (375 degrees) for 8 - 10 mins.

Makes 10 doz.
