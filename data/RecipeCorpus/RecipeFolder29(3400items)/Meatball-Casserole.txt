Title: Meatball Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1    Egg
1 c  Milk
1 cn Mushroom soup
1/3 c  Flour
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Salt
1 c  Bread crumbs, fine
Combine the first 7 ingredients; mix and blend well.  Add milk to
mushroom soup and stir until blended.  Add 1/4 of the soup mixture to
the first ingredients.  Mix well and form into medium size balls. Dip
the balls in flour and brown on all sides in a hot deep fryer or pan
of oil. Place in a casserole.  Combine chili sauce and remaining soup
mixture. Pour the mixture over the balls.  Bake in a 350 degree oven
for 30 minutes. Randy Rigg
