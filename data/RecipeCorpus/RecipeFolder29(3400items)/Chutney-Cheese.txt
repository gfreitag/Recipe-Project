Title: Chutney Cheese
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
2    Scallions, minced
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Grated cheddar cheese
3 oz Cream cheese
1/4 c  Major Grey chutney
Process/mince ingredients individually in food processor.

Re-combine all in processor and pulse until mixed to consistency of
your desire.

This recipe can be doubled/tripled in larger food processors, and is
calculated to make a moderate amount of appetizer servings.

Serve with sesame crackers.
