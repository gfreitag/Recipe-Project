Title: AM's Honey Garlic Spareribs
Categories: Appetizers, Meats
Yield: 22 spareribs
3 tb Cider Vinegar
5    Garlic cloves, minced
1/4 ts Black Pepper
3 lb Pork Spareribs
Garlic Salt
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
Season sprareribs with garlic salt; arrange in a 13"x9"x2" baking
dish. Cover with foil & bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour until tender;
drain all liquid. Combine remaining ingredients into a saucepan and
simmer 5 minutes. Drizzle honey mixture over spareribs & bake
uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 minutes; baste every 10 minutes.
Makes about 20 to 24 spareribs.
