Title: Maple Parfait
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 amply
1 c  Milk;
1 pt Heavy cream; whipped
1 c  Nuts; chopped
6    Egg yolks;
7/8 c  Maple syrup;
1 1/2 ts Gelatin;
Beat yolks until they are light, then stir in the maple syrup.  Cook
the mixture over hot water, stirring, until thick.  Remove from fire
and until cool.  Add the gelatin which has been thoroughly dissolved
in the milk and lightly mix in the whipping cream.  Pack into a
freezer dish and allow to freeze for 1 hour.  Remove from freezer,
beat until light and smooth, and stir in the chopped nuts.  Spoon
into a serving dish and keep very cold. Serves 8 amply.

Source: Annie Kate Gilbert, '15, New York City, The Randolph-Macon
Cookbook, by the Randolph-Macon Woman's College Alumnae Asso.
