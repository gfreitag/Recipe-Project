Title: Pineapple Chicken Salad^
Categories: Poultry, Healthy, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Sliced green onions
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Curry, or to taste
Lettuce
1/2    Diced poached chicken
1 cn (8oz) unsweetened crushed
Pineapple
1/4 c  Lowfat yogurt
1/4 c  Sliced celery
Lightly drain pineapple and combine with remaining ingredients. Serve
on lettuce.

Per serving: Calories: 197 Protein: 24g Carbohydrates: 15g Fat: 3g
Sodium: 105mg Cholesterol: 28mg
