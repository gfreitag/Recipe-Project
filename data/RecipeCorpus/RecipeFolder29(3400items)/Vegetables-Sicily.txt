Title: Vegetables Sicily
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
1/3 c  Parkay Margarine
1 md Tomato, cut into wedges
3 c  Zucchini slices
1 md Onion, sliced
1 ts Oregano Leaves, crushed
1/2 ts Salt
Saute zucchini, onion, and seasoning in margarine.  Add tomato; cook 5
minutes or until vegetables are tender.

Makes 4-6 servings.
