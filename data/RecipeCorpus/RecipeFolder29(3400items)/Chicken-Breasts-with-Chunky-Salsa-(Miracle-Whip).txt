Title: Chicken Breasts with Chunky Salsa (Miracle Whip)
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk Good Seasons Ranch-
Salad Dressing Mix
6 ea Chicken breast halves-
Boneless and skinless
1/2 c  Miracle Whip salad dressing
1/3 c  Pineapple juice reserved-
From canned pineapple-
(chunky salsa recipe)
MMMMM------------------------CHUNKY SALSA-----------------------------
1/2 c  Tomato, finely chopped
1/4 c  Green Pepper, finely chopped
1 tb Cilantro, chopped (optional)
1 cn 8 oz crushed pineapple-
Drained (reserve 1/3 cup-
Juice for chicken marinade
1 c  Prepared Salsa
SALSA:  Mix all salsa ingredients together.  Store covered to
serve with chicken.   Makes 2 cups.

CHICKEN:  Mix salad dressing, juice and dressing mix.  Spread
mixture over chicken; cover.  Refrigerate at least 20 minutes to
marinate. Heat grill.  Remove chicken from marinade; place on grill
over medium coals. Grill, covered, 8-10 minutes on each side or until
cooked through. Serve with Chunky Salsa.
