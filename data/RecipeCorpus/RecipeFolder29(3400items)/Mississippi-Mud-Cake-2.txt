Title: Mississippi Mud Cake 2
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 cake
4    Eggs
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Nuts (chopped)
10 oz Marshmallows (miniature)
1 c  Margarine
4 tb Cocoa
1 1/2 c  Flour (self-rising)
1/4 ts Salt
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Cocoa
1 lb Confectioners sugar
4 tb Butter
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
Melt margarine and cocoa together; add to eggs and sugar.  Add salt,
flour and nuts.  Bake at 375 degrees.  Spread a 10 ounce package of
miniature marshmallows on cake while hot.

Frosting: Melt together butter, evaporated milk, and cocoa.  Pour over
confectioners sugar; spread over cake.
