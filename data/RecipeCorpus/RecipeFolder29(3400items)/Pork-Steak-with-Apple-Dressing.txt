Title: Pork Steak with Apple Dressing
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Chopped apples
1/4 c  Raisins
2 tb Apple jelly
2 tb Water
5    Pork steaks
1 tb Lard (yes Lard!)
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1/4 c  Chopped onion
5 c  Dry bread
Brown steaks in lard.  Remove meat and add celery and onions.  Brown a
little; remove from heat and add bread, apples, raisins, apple jelly
and water.  Stir in mix.  Place dressing in roasting pan or baking
dish. Place meat on top, cover lightly with aluminum foil and bake at
325 degrees for 1 hour.  Uncover and continue baking for 5 minutes.
