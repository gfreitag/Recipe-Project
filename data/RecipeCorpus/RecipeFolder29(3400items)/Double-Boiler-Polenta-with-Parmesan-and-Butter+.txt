Title: Double Boiler Polenta with Parmesan and Butter+
Categories: Main dish, Italian, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
4    Pats butter for garnish
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese, plus
-more for passing
1    Recipe double boiler Polenta
-see preceeding recipe
4 tb Unsalted butter, softened +
Prepare Double-Boiler Polenta. Stir in butter and 1/2 cup Parmesan
cheese. Divide polenta among 4 bowls. Top each with a pat of butter
and sprinkle generousely with Parmesan cheese to taste.
