Title: 464455 Green Goddess Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Boneless, skinless
Anchovies
1/3 c  Parsley (no stems)
3 tb Tarragon wine vinegar
Salt & pepper to taste
1 sm Clove garlic
3 tb Chopped green onions, tops
And all
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 c  Mayonnaise
Put all ingredients except mayonnaise and sour cream in blender and
blend until well mixed.  Then combine mayonnaise and sour cream with
other ingredients and mix well.  Add salt and pepper to taste (easy
on the salt). Makes 1 pint.
