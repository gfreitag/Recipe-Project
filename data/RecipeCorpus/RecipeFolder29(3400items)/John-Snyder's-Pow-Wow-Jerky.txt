Title: John Snyder's Pow Wow Jerky
Categories: Countyfairs, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Brown Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1 ts Pureed Garlic
2 lb Very Lean Ground Beef
Or Other Meat
1/3 c  Soy Sauce
1 ts Liquid Smoke
1. Mix all ingredients together well. Refrigerate, covered, overnight
to blend flavors.

2. Roll out the mixture between sheets of wax paper or plastic wrap
to a 1/4-inch thickness. Place the layers of meat in a 150 F
dehydrator, in convenient size pcs or place on metal mesh over cake
racks in a 150 F oven. Dry meat for 7 to 8 hrs. The jerky will be
chewy but not completely dry. Cut into 3/4- to 1-inch strips with
kitchen shears. Makes 3/4 lb jerky.

From the CALAVERAS COUNTY FAIR & JUMPING FROG JUBILEE of Angels Camp,
CA. Fair Date: 3rd weekend in May, for 4 days.
