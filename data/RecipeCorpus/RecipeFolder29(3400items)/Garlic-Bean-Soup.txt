Title: Garlic Bean Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Low cal/fat, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
4 c  Cooked white beans, **OR**
2 cn (19-oz.) white kidney (can-
; nellini beans), drained
2 c  Vegetable or chicken broth
8 lg (very) garlic cloves, peeled
1 md Onion, coarsely chopped
Put the broth, garlic and onion in a large bowl; cover.  Microwave
for 20 minutes on high, or until vegetables are soft.  Transfer to a
blender or food processor, add half the beans and puree.  Return soup
to bowl, add remaining beans and microwave 5 minutes, or until beans
are heated throughout.  Serves 6.

Per serving:  195 CALORIES, 13.9g PROTEIN, 33.9g CARBOHYDRATES, 8g
FIBER, 0.9g FAT (0.25g SATURATED), 267mg SODIUM

From USA WEEKEND, March 31-April 2, 1995.
