Title: Onion Rice Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
2 cn Beef consomme soup
1 c  Long grain rice
1    Stick butter
1 lg Onion
Saute onion in butter.  Add soup and rice.  Place in casserole and
bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes. Randy Rigg
