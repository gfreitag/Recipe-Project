Title: Apollo Float
Categories: Beverages, Desserts
Yield: 2 servings
2    Sugar Cones
12 oz Ginger Ale
2 x  Vanilla Ice Cream scoops
Fill 2 glasses with equal amounts of ginger ale.  Securely place ice
cream on cones.  Turn ice cream cones upside down and place one in
each glass. Cones will stay afloat as ice cream melts.  Drink with
straws.

SOURCE: Girl Scouts - Mile Hi Council - Denver, Colorado 1995
