Title: 3721 Sauerkraut Balls
Categories: Spam
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Flour
1/2 c  Beef stock or bouillon
-cube, dissolved in 1/2 cup
-of water
2    Eggs, well beaten
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1/8 ts Pepper
1 md Onion, minced
2 tb Butter
1 cn Spam (ground)
1 c  Ground corned beef
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1 tb Mustard
3 tb Minced parsley
2 c  Sauerkraut
Saute onions in butter, add spam, corned beef.  Cook 5 minutes and
stir often.  Add garlic salt, mustard, parsley, pepper, sauerkraut,
1/2 cup flour, and beef stock.  Mix well.  Cook for 10 minutes.
Spread out on platter to cool.  Shape into small balls.  Roll in
flour, dip into eggs and roll in crumbs.  Deep fry in hot fat at 375
degrees until golden brown. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
04-01-95
