Title: Fricassee With Walnuts ^
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Thinly sliced celery
1 md Onion, cut in juilienned
-strips
1    Red pepper, seeded and cut
-in julienned strips
1/2 c  Chicken broth
1 c  Walnut halves
3 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Dry sherry
1/2 ts Sugar
1/8 ts Salt
2 tb Cornstarch
1 1/4 lb Chicken breast fillets,
-cubed
5 tb Oil
Mix soy sauce, sherry, sugar, salt and cornstarch together and
combine with chicken cubes. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in fry pan and
saute celery 1 minutes. Add onion, saute over high heat 1 minute. Add
red pepper; saute 1 minute more. Remove vegetables from pan. Heat
remaining oil in pan and cook chicken cubes 3 minutes, stirring
frequently. Reduce heat and stir in chicken broth. Return vegetables
and walnuts to pan and heat briefly. Serve with rice.
