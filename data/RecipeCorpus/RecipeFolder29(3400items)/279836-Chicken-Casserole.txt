Title: 279836 Chicken Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1 md Onion, chopped
1 pk Pepperidge Farm stuffing
Mix(or pan cornbread,cooked)
1    Stick butter
1 tb Poultry seasoning
6    Pieces stewed chicken breast
(save broth)
1 cn Cream of chicken soup (mix
With  1 can broth)
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup (mix
With 1 can broth)
Grease pan 13 x 9 inch with butter.  Remove bones from chicken. Pour
melted butter over stuffing mix.  Sprinkle poultry seasoning on and
stir well. Then, place layer of stuffing mix in bottom of pan, layer
of chicken in small pieces, half of chopped onion, cream of chicken
mixed with 1 can chicken broth.  Place another layer of stuffing,
chicken, rest of onion, cream of mushroom soup mixed with 1 can
chicken broth. Place remaining stuffing mix on top.  Bake at 350
degrees for 30 minutes. Submitted By SY KOTTIK   On   04-22-95
