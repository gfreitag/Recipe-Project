Title: Turkey Salad with Yogurt ^
Categories: Poultry, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1 3/4 c  Seedless red grapes, halved
1 3/4 c  Heavy whipping cream
2 1/2 c  Plain full cream yogurt
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 tb Brandy
6    Sprigs fresh mint for
-garnish
4 c  Water
1 ts Salt
2 1/4 lb Turkey drumstick
2    Bananas, peeled and sliced
2    Red apples, peeled, cored
-and diced
1/2    Lemon, juice of
1 sm Pineapple, in small cubes
Cover drumstick with water, bring to a boil. Cover and simmer 1 hour.
Combine banana slices and apples; sprinkle with lemon juice. Add
pineapple and grapes. Remove drumstick from broth and let cool.
Remove meat and cut into small cubes. Add to fruit. Whip cream until
stiff. Mix with yogurt, saot, pepper and brandy. Pour over fruit and
turkey; mix well. Garnish with mint and serve.
