Title: Beef and Noodle Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
2 ts Chili powder
2 ts Salt
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
8 oz Noodles
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Chopped onion
2 cn Stewed tomatoes, 16 oz size
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Cook noodles according to package
directions; drain.  In a large skillet, brown meat and onion, stir in
tomatoes, chili powder and salt; heat thoroughly.  Combine with
noodles; turn into a 3 quart casserole, sprinkle with cheese.  Bake
for 30 minutes. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   04-12-95
