Title: Green Goddess Dressing 2
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Mustard -- Dry
1/8 ts Garlic Powder
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Yogurt -- Plain
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Parsley Sprigs
2 ea Green Onions/Tops -- Cut Up
2 tb Tarragon Vinegar
2 ts Sugar
Place the first 9 ingredients in a blender container, cover, and blend
until smooth.  Fold in the yogurt.  Cover and chill.  Serve on cold
seafood as well as green salads.

Makes 1 1/2 cups of dressing.
