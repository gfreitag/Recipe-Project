Title: 279758 Jan's Hot Fruit Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Fruit
Yield: 10 servings
2 tb Flour
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1    Stick butter
1 c  Sherry
1 md Can pineapple chunks
1 md Can peach halves
1 md Can pear halves
1    Jar spiced apple rings
Drain all fruit.  Let sit.  Combine butter, flour, sugar and sherry.
Cook over hot water (double boiler) until thick, stirring constantly.
Arrange fruit in oven proof dish.  (I usually place apple rings on
top for color.) Pour sauce over fruit.  Let stand overnight in
refrigerator. Bake uncovered for about 20 minutes, until hot and
bubbly. Delicious with beef, pork or chicken.
