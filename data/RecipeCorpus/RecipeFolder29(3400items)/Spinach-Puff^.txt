Title: Spinach Puff^
Categories: Healthy, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1 cl Garlic, minced
1 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Low sodium soy sauce
3/4 c  Egg Beaters
2 tb Toastd bread crumbs
1 pk (10oz) spinach, cooked and
Squeezed dry
1 c  Lowfat cottage cheese
2 tb Parmesan cheese
1 sm Onion, minced
Combine all ingredients except bread crumbs and 1 tablespoon Parmesan
and pour into a pie plate sprayed with pam. Top with bread crumbs and
cheese. Bake 20-25 minutes at 350F.

Per serving: Calories: 110 Protein: 10g Carbohydrates: 6g Fat: 5g
Sodium: 307mg Cholesterol: 1.5mg
