Title: CHEESE & PEPPERONI BREAD *M*
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker, Cheese/eggs, Sausage
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Olive oil
1/4 c  Pepperoni (1 oz); chopped
3/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Provolone cheese (1 1/5 oz)
1 ts Dry yeast
Chopped
2 ts Parmesan cheese, grated
2 1/4 c  Bread flour
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Onion powder
1 tb Dry milk
1/4 ts Oregano
Place all ingredients (except liquids and yeast) inside the bread

pan. Add liquid ingredients. Close cover and place dry yeast into

the yeast holder. Press start. Don Fifield
