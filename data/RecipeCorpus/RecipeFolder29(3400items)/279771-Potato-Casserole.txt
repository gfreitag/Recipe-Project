Title: 279771 Potato Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
1    (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese
Salt and pepper to taste
4 c  Instant potato flakes
1 ct Sour cream
Prepare potatoes as directed on package. Add sour cream and softened
cream cheese.  Stir until well blended.  Spread in greased 9 x 13
inch baking dish.  Dot with butter.  Sprinkle with paprika. Bake at
350 degrees until it browns nicely - 30 minutes.
