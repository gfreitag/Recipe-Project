Title: Freckle Faced Bears
Categories: Kids, Snacks
Yield: 6 servings
-seeds
36    Raisins
Pam
10 oz Buttermilk biscuits
1/4 c  Sesame seeds; or sunflower
Heat oven to 400.  Spray a cookie sheet with pam.  Separate biscuits.
Cut three biscuits into sixths for ears and noses and leave six
biscuits whole for heads.  Rol larger dough pieces into balls, roll
in seeds, then flatten slightly on cookie sheets to form heads. Round
three small pieces to form two ears and one nose for each head and
roll in seeds. Press an ear on either side of each head near the top.
Press a nose into the center of the head.  Press a raisin into the
center of both ears and two into the center of both ears and two into
the center of the nose. Add two more above the nose to make eyes.
Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, until golden brown. Source: Healthy Treats
and Super Snacks for Kids Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   04-22-95
