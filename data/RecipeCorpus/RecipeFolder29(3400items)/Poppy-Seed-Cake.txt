Title: Poppy Seed Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/3 c  Poppy seeds
1/2 c  Sherry
Icing sugar
1    Yellow cake mix
4    Serving size box of lemon
-instant pudding mix
4 lg Eggs
1 c  Light plain yogurt
Mix all ingredients except for icing sugar.  Pour batter into greased
and floured bundt pan.  Bake for 60-65 minutes at 350F, or until
tested clean. Invert onto platter, cool, and sprinkle with icing
sugar.

This is an excellent cake for parties or picnics, as it is moist,
cuts well and doesn't need icing.

Origin: Adapted from a recipe in Appeal, Spring 1995 issue. Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, Mar/95.
