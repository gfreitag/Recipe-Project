Title: One Bowl Macaroons
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 dozen
1 ts Vanilla
2 2/3 c  Flaked coconut
2/3 c  Sweetened condensed milk
Heat oven to 350F.

Mix coconut, milk and vanilla in large bowl.  Drop by teaspoonfuls,
about 1 inch apart, onto well-greased cookie sheets, pressing down
ends of coconut with back of spoon.

Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown.  Immediately remove from
cookie sheets.  Cool on wire racks.

ONE BOWL CHOCOLATE MACAROONS:  Prepare Macaroons as directed, adding 2
squares unsweetened chocolate, melted, before baking.
