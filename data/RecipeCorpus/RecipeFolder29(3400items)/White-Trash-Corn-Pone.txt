Title: White Trash Corn Pone
Categories: Breads, Vegetables, Whitetrash
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Molasses (cane)
1 ts Salt
1 tb Bacon drippings
2 c  Cornmeal
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ea Egg
Add enough warm water to the cornmeal to make a thin dough.  Allow it
to rise overnight in a warm place (or in the oven).  Add all other
ingredients to the sponge, mix well, and then bake in a skillet at
400F until a nice golden brown in color.  Slice and eat.  You can
make one large pone per skillet, or smaller individual ones. Origin:
Adapted from White Trash. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, April/95.
