Title: 575270 Tex-Mex Eggs
Categories: Tex/mexican, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1    (4 oz.) can chopped green
-chilies, drained
4 oz Cheddar cheese, shredded
(1 c.)
6 lg Eggs
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Fresh ground black pepper
Beat eggs, cream, salt and pepper in medium-size bowl until smooth.
Stir in chopped chilies.  Sprinkle cheese over bottom of lightly
greased shallow baking dish; pour egg mixture over cheese. Bake in
preheated 325 degree oven 25 minutes or until set.  Broil briefly to
brown top if desired. Other vegetables may be added (onion,
mushrooms, etc.). Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   03-14-95
