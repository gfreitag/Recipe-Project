Title: 618482 Bumstead's (With  Spam)
Categories: Spam, Sandwich
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Chopped onion
2 tb Chopped green pepper,
-optional
1 c  Mayonnaise
1    (12 oz.) can Spam, cubed
1 c  Velveeta cheese, cubed
4 c  Hard cooked eggs, chopped
1 tb Chopped sweet pickle
Mix meat, cheese, eggs, pickles, onion, green pepper with mayonnaise
together. Spoon on hamburger buns.  Wrap in foil and bake at 250
degrees for 30 minutes. This was World War II special at the Bumstead
house. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-01-95
