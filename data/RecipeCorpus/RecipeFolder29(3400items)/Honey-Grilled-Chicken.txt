Title: Honey Grilled Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Salt or to taste
1 ts Paprika
2 tb Honey
2 ds Tabasco sauce (to 3 drops)
6    Chicken breasts
-skinned and boned
-(6 oz ea)
Juice of 2 limes
Place chicken in a shallow pan. Combine remaining ingredients, except
tomato and cilantro, pour over chicken and marinate for 15 minutes per
side. Lift chicken out of marinade, broil of charcoal grill for about
5 minutes per side or until just cooked through. Discard the marinade.
