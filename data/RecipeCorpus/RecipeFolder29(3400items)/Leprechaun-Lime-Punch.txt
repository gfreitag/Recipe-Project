Title: Leprechaun Lime Punch
Categories: Holiday, Beverages, Tasteofhome
Yield: 20 servings
1    2 liter
Lime slices
Lime citrus drink
Frozen limeade concentrate
Thawed
Bottle white soda
1    46 oz can
2    12 oz cans
-
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Lime juice
1 ct (1 quart) Lime sherbert --
Softened
In a punch bowl, combine citrus drink, limeade, sugar, lime juice and
sherbert; stir until smooth and sugar is dissolved. Add soda; stir to
mix. Float lime slices on top. Serve immediately. Yield: 1 gallon,
20-24 servings.
