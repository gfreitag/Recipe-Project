Title: Cheddar Cornmeal Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Nuts/grains
Yield: 12 muffins  1-1/2 cups unsifted all purpose flour 1/2 cup yellow cornmeal 1 tbsp.
baking powder 1/2 tsp. salt Pinch cayenne pepper 1 large egg 1 cup
milk 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter or margarine, melted 1-1/4 cups
coarsely grated sharp cheddar cheese.

Heat oven to 425.  Grease 12 muffin cups.

In a bowl, blend flour, cornmeal, baking powder, salt and cayenne.

Beat egg with milk and butter and add to dry ingredients.  Stir until
thoroughly moistened.

Stir in 1 cup cheese and spoon batter into muffin cups.  Sprinkle
about 1 tsp. of the remaining cheese over each muffin and bake 15 to
20 minutes.
