Title: Peanut Butter Popcorn
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1 c  Peanut butter
1 ts Vanilla
3 qt Popcorn, cooked in oil
1 1/2 c  Peanuts, or use crunchy
Peanut butter
1 c  Sugar
Keep popcorn and nuts warm.  Mix sugar, honey and syrup together.
Hard boil for 2 minutes.  Mix in peanut butter and vanilla.  Mix with
corn and nuts. Randy Rigg
