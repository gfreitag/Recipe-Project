Title: Flank Steak Teriyaki
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 servings
4-6 pineapple slices
1/2 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Sherry (optional)
1 ea Clove garlic, crushed
4-6 flank steaks
1 tb Salad oil
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Ginger
1/2 ts MSG
To form marinade, combine all except steaks and pineapple.  Mix well
and pour over steaks.  Let marinate 1 to 1-1/2 hours.  Fry steaks in
very hot oven or skillet brushing once with marinade. Add pineapple
during last few minutes, brush with marinade and cover.  Cook 3-5
min.  Serve over rice.
