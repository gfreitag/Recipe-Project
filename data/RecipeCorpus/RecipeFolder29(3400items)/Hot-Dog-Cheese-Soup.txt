Title: Hot Dog-Cheese Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Kids
Yield: 5 servings
-drained
15 oz Can red kidney beans,
-drained
Green onion, chopped
1 c  Milk
8 oz Velveeta
1/2 ts Dry mustard
5    Hot dogs, sliced
15 oz Can whole kernel corn
Heat milk, cheese & mustard in a 2-quart saucepan over medium heat,
stirring occasionally, until hot.  Stir in remaining ingredients; heat
through.  Garnish with green onion. Per serving: 505 calories, 25 g
protein, 43 g carbohydrate, 26 g fat, 55 g cholesterol, 1,740 mg
sodium. Submitted By TERRI WOLTMON   On   04-07-95
