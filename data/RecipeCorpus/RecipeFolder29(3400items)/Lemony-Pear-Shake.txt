Title: Lemony Pear Shake
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Chopped pear
1/4 c  Egg substitute (no raw
-eggs)
1/2 c  Pear nectar
1/2 ct Lemon yogurt
1/2 c  Lemon sherbet
One of several tasty ideas to make getting enough calories and protein
easy.  They are a great way to get the nutrition you need even when
you're not feeling well.

Combine in blender.  Approximately 240 calories, 6 gm. protein per
cup.
