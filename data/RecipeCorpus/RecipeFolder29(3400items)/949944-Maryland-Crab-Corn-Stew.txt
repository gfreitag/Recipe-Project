Title: 949944 Maryland Crab-Corn Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables, Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
12 oz Reg. crab meat
1 c  Whole kernel corn
1 lg Tomato, peeled and chopped
1 md Size green pepper, chopped
1 1/2 c  Half and half
1 md Onion, chopped
2 c  Potatoes, diced and peeled
2 c  Water
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts White pepper
Put onions, potatoes, water, oregano, salt and pepper in a large
kettle. Bring to boil, lower heat and cook slowly covered for 25
minutes or until potatoes are tender.  Mix in crab meat, corn,
tomatoes, green peppers. Continue cooking 5 minutes.  Add half and
half. Leave on low heat long enough to heat through.  Serve at once
or refrigerate one to two days, reheat over low heat. Do not boil.
Serve with warm rolls or garlic bread. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN
