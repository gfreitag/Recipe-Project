Title: Monkey Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Kids
Yield: 3 dozen
2/3 c  Margarine; softened
2    Eggs;
2    Bananas; mashed
1 ts Vanilla;
2 1/4 c  All-purpose flour;
2 ts Baking powder;
1/4 ts Salt;
1 c  Sugar;
2/3 c  Butter -=OR=-
(1).  Combine flour, baking powder, salt and sugar in a large bowl.
Mix well.  (2). Add butter or margarine.  Mix well.  (3).  Add
remaining ingredients.  Mix well.  (4).  Drop by teaspoonfuls on
cookie sheet. (5). Bake in a preheat, 400-degree oven 12 minutes.
Makes about 3 dozen. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Apr. 20,
1995, Rookie Cookie's Recipe in The Mini Page for Kids. Brought to
you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
