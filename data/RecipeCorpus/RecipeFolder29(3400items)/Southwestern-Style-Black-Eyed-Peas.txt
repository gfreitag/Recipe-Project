Title: Southwestern Style Black Eyed Peas
Categories: Fatfree, Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 2 servings
Pressed
1/2 ts Salt, or to taste
1/4 ts Pepper, or to taste
1/4 ts Tobasco or other hot sauce,
Or to taste
1 cn Black-eyed peas
1    Carrot, diced
1/2    Onion, diced
1/4    Green pepper, diced
1    Stalk celery, diced
3    Cloves garlic, minced or
Mix all ingredients together in a saucepan, and bring to boil. Simmer
for at least 10 minutes, but 20 - 25 is better.
