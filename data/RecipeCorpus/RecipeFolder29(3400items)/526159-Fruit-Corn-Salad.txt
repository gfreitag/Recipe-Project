Title: 526159 Fruit Corn Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2    (17 oz.) cans corn, whole
-kernel
2    Young coconut (makapuno),
-unsweetened
1    Bottle kaong
10    Pieces of cherries, pitted
1 cn Condensed milk
1    (8 oz.) pkg. softened cream
-cheese
1    (4 oz.) whipped topping
2    (40 oz.) cans fruit
-cocktail
Beat cheese until smooth.  Blend in condensed milk.  Fold in whipped
topping. Drain well fruit cocktail, corn, coconut and kaong. Mix all
ingredients. Garnish with cherries.  Chill before serving.
