Title: Low Fat Banana Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Breads
Yield: 12 servings
3 tb 1% milk
2 tb Vanilla extract
2 ts Baking soda
2 c  Flour, all-purpose
3 md Bananas -- very ripe
1/2 c  Applesauce, unsweetened
2    Egg whites
1/4 c  Canola oil
3/4 c  Sugar
In food processor, process bananas until well mashed.  Add add
ingredients except  baking soda and flour. Process for 20 seconds, or
until well blended.

Add flour and baking soda. Process with 4 quick on off turns until
flour is moistened.

Fill paper lined muffin tins 2/3 full.  Bake 300F for 20-25 minutes.
