Title: Catalina Salmon Quiche
Categories: Pie/tart, Seafood, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
-frozen -=OR=-
1    Pastry for single crust pie
1 pk (10-oz) frozen broccoli;
-thawed drained
1/4 c  Ripe olives; pitted chopped
1/4 c  Swiss cheese; shredded-=OR=-
1/4 c  Mozzarella cheese; shredded
2 pk (3-oz ea.) cream cheese;
-softened
1/2 c  Dairy sour cream;
2    Eggs;
2 tb Prepared yellow mustard;
1 cn (7-3/4 oz) salmon; drained*
1    9" deep dish pie shell;
* If Preferred, use tuna. Using mixer or blender, combine cream
cheese, sour cream, eggs and mustard until well blended. Break salmon
into small chunks and arrange in bottom of unbaked pie shell. Top
with broccoli. Pour cream-cheese mixture over salmon and broccoli.
Sprinkle with olives and cheese.  Bake in 375 degrees oven for 40 to
45 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes clean.   Cut in
wedges and serve warm or cold. Makes 6 servings. Source: 1001 Recipes
