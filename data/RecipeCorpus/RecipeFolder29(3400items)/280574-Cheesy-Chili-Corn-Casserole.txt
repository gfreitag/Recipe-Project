Title: 280574 Cheesy Chili Corn Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 10 servings
1 1/4 c  Coarsely chopped corn
-chips, divided
1 c  Chopped red bell pepper
1    (4 oz.) can chopped green
-chilies, drained
2    (16 oz.) pkgs. Birds Eye
-sweet corn, thawed, drained
2    (8 oz.) pkgs. Velveeta,
-shredded pasteurized
-process cheese food,divided
1 1/4 c  Milk
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix corn, 3 1/2 c. Velveeta process cheese
food, milk, 1/2 cup corn chips, peppers and chilies until well
blended. Spoon into greased 12" x 7 1/2" baking dish. Sprinkle with
remaining 3/4 cup corn chips.  Bake 50 minutes or until thoroughly
heated. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Velveeta cheese food;
continue baking 5 minutes or until Velveeta process cheese food
begins to melt. Let stand 10 minutes before serving. Submitted By
