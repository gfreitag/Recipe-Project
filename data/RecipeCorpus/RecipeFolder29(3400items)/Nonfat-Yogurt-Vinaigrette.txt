Title: Nonfat Yogurt Vinaigrette
Categories: Dress/stuff, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 cups
1 ts Dijon style mustard
3/4 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
Salt and ground pepper
1 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Vinegar
1 x  Clove garlic, minced
Mix together the lemon juice, vinegar, garlic, mustard, salt and
pepper. Whisk in the yogurt. From: Reversing Heart Disease, Dean
Ornish, Random House, 1990 Typos by Joell Abbott 3/95
