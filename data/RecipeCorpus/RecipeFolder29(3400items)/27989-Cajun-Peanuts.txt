Title: 27989 Cajun Peanuts
Categories: Cajun/creol, Snacks, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
2    Or 3 drops Tabasco sauce
-Dash of cayenne pepper
1 tb Salad oil
1    Jar Planter's unsalted
-peanuts
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Chili powder
Mix salt, chili powder, cayenne pepper, peanuts and oil together.
Place on cookie sheet.  Roast in 350 degree oven until golden brown.
