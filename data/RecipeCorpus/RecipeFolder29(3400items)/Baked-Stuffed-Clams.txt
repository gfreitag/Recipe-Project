Title: Baked Stuffed Clams
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  Ritz cracker crumbs
1 tb Sherry
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 cn Minced clams
1    Stick of melted margarine
4 tb Clam broth
Pinch of garlic salt
Drain clams, reserving 4 tb liquid.  Mix all ingredients together and
stuff shells.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.  If you do not
have shells, bake in a small baking dish for 20 to 25 minutes and
serve on crackers. Randy Rigg
