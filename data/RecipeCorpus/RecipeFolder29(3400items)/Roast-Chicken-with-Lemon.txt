Title: Roast Chicken with Lemon
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 1 chicken
2    Lemons;
1    Chicken;
Use a toothpick to poke a bunch of holes in two fresh lemons.  Place
lemons inside the chicken, and roast at 375 degrees until done.
Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Food Section, Mar. 23, 1995
