Title: Pina Colada Muffins^
Categories: Muffins, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
2    Egg whites, lightly beaten
1/2 ts Coconut extract
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Lowfat yogurt
1/2 c  Crushed pineapple,
Unsweetened, drained
3 1/2 tb Pineapple juice concentrate
Preheat oven to 375F. Spray 6 muffin cups with pam and set aside.
Combine wet ingredients. Combine flour and baking soda. Mix the two
mixtures together, spoon into muffin cups and bake until golden brown
and a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, about 25 minutes.

Per serving: Calories: 94 Protein: 4g Carbohydrates: 18g Fat: 1g
Sodium: 102mg Cholesterol: 1mg
