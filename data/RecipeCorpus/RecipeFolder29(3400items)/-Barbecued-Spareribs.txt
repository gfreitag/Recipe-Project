Title:  Barbecued Spareribs
Categories: Meats, Tasteofhome, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 lb Pork spareribs, cut into
-serving-size pieces
1 md Onion, quartered
SAUCE:

cup
cup
1/2
ketchup 1/4
Tbsp. chopped onion 1 Tbsp. lemon juice 1/2 tsp. dry mustard 1 clove
garlic, minced dash cayenne pepper

In a large kettle or Dutch oven, place ribs and onion; sprinkle with
salt and pepper.  Add enough water to cover ribs; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 1 1/2 hours or until tender; drain.
Combine all sauce ingredients in a saucepan.  Simmer, uncovered, for
1 hour or until slightly thickened, stirring occasionally. Arrange
ribs on a rack in a broiler pan. Brush with sauce. Broil 5" from the
heat for 5 minutes on each side, brushing frequently with sauce.
