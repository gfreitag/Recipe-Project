Title: Fresh Cilantro and Mint Chutney
Categories: Condiments, Indian, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 batch
1 ea 1-inch piece fresh ginger
-- peeled and chopped
1 ts Ground cumin
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
3 c  Cilantro leaves
-- (firmly packed)
1/2 c  Fresh mint leaves
1/3 c  Fresh lemon or lime juice
3    Serrano chilies; chopped
6 md Garlic cloves; chopped
In a food processor or blender, mix all ingredients to a smooth paste,
scraping down sides of bowl frequently.  Refrigerate in an airtight
container up to 10 days.

Adapted from: Suneeta Vaswani's recipe in Bon Appetit (February 1987)
