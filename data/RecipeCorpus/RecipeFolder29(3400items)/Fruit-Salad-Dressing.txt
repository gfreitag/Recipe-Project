Title: Fruit Salad Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Sugar
3 oz Cream Cheese, Softened --
Cut Into Cubes
1 ds Cayenne Pepper
1/2 c  Orange Juice
1/2 ts Orange Peel -- Grated
1/4 c  Mayonnaise Or Salad
Dressing
Put all of the ingredients into a blender container and blend until
smooth.

Makes 1 cup.
