Title: Tomato Beef Bake
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles, Asian, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
3 md Tomatoes; chunked
1 md Green pepper; chunked
1/4 c  Kikkoman Soy Sauce
6 oz Medium-size egg noodles
1 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion; chunked
2    Garlic cloves; minced
Cook noodles in boiling water according to package directions,
omitting salt; drain.  Brown beef with onion and garlic; cook until
onion is translucent.  Add remaining 3 ingredients; stir to combine.
Bring to boil and cook only until tomatoes are heated through.  Stir
in noodles; turn into 2-quart baking dish.  Bake, uncovered, in
preheated 350 F. oven 20 minutes.
