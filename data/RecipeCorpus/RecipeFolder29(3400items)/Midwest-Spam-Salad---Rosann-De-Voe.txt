Title: Midwest Spam Salad - Rosann De Voe
Categories: Countyfairs, Main dish
Yield: 12 servings
-diced
1/2 c  Bread And Butter Pickles,
-chopped
1/2 c  Onion, finely chopped
3    Hard-Boiled Eggs, chopped
1 cn Low-Salt Spam, diced
Lettuce Leaves. if
-desired
1 c  Miracle Whip Or Similar
Dressing
1 tb Prepared Mustard
3 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
2 1/2 lb Green Peas, Frozen, thawed
And drained
1 c  Cheddar Cheese, Mild,
In a large bowl, combine the dressing, mustard, sugar and pepper. Add
the peas, cheese, pickles and onion. Toss gently to mix. Add the eggs
and SPAM and stir to combine. Cover and refrigerate until chilled, at
least 2 hours. Serve on lettuce leaves if desired.

From section on the MINNESOTA STATE FAIR of St. Paul, Minn. Fair
dates: Late August, 12 days, through Labor Day.
