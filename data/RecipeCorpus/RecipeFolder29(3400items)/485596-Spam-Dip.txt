Title: 485596 Spam Dip
Categories: Spam, Dips/spread, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1    2 tsp. creamy horseradish
3 oz Spam spread
1/2 c  Sour cream
Blend ingredients, adding horseradish to taste.  Serve as a dip with
choice of crackers and chips.  Use hollowed-out red or green peppers
to serve dip in.
