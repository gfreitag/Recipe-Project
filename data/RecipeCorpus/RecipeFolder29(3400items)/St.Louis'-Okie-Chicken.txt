Title: St.Louis' Okie Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c  Grated Romano cheese
1 1/2 lb Chicken half-breasts (3 ea)
3 tb Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Durkee french-fried onions
Fresh ground black pepper
2 cn Cut green beans, drained
1 cn Mushroom soup
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/4 c  Minced red onion
Place green beans in the bottom of an 11 x 7 inch baking pan.
Combine soup, sour cream, milk, garlic powder, minced onion and
worcestershire sauce.  Blend well & pour over green beans.  Sprinkle
with Romano cheese. Set chicken pieces atop casserole, skin-side up.
Brush chicken pieces with mayonnaise.  Sprinkle french-fried onions
over all & douse with fresh ground black pepper.  Cover and bake in a
350 degree oven for 1 hour. Uncover and bake an additional 30 minutes
until top is brown. Serve chicken pieces with some of the mushroom
sauce poured over & green beans on the side.
