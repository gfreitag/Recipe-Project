Title: 498815 Norwegian Kringla
Categories: Cake/cookie, West europe
Yield: 60 servings
3 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Lemon extract
1 ts Orange extract
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
2    Eggs, well beaten
1 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Soda
Mix ingredients in order given.  Dissolve soda in buttermilk. Chill
dough in refrigerator overnight.  Roll out small amounts of dough at
a time. Roll to thickness of a pencil and about ten inches long.
Shape into figure 8 on lightly greased cookie sheet.    Bake 10
minutes at 425 degrees or until light brown.  Remove from oven. Dip
top of kringle in cold water. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
03-27-95
