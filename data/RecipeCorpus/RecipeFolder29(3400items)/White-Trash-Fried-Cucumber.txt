Title: White Trash Fried Cucumber
Categories: Vegetables, Whitetrash
Yield: 4 servings
Salt
Pepper
2 lg Cucumbers, cut length wise
1 1/2 c  Corn meal
Season sliced cucumbers with salt and pepper and then using a brown
paper bag, coat with corn meal.  Fry in hot skillet with a little oil
until golden brown.  Allow to drain before serving.  Serves 4-5.
Origin: Adapted from White Trash. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, April/95.
