Title:  Pigs In A Blanket
Categories: Sausage, Meats, Appetizers, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
12 oz Pkg. smoked sausage links
8 oz Tube refrigerated crescent
Rolls
Separate the dough into 8 triangles.  Place a sausage on wide end of
each triangle and roll up.  Place, tip down, on an ungreased baking
sheet.  Bake at 400 degrees for 10-15 minutes or until golden brown.
