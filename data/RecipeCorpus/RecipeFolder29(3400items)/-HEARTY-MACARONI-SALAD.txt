Title:  HEARTY MACARONI SALAD
Categories: Salads, Pasta, Side dish, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
DRESSING:
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 tb Vinegar
1/2 ts Sugar
2 ts Minced fresh dill or 1 tsp.
-dried
2 c  Cubed cooked chicken
2 c  Cubed fully cooked ham
2 c  Cooked salad shrimp
7 oz Macaroni shells, cooked and
-drained
1    Rib celery, sliced
1/4 c  Diced green pepper
1/4 c  Diced sweet red pepper
1/4 c  Diced onion
"With chicken, ham and tiny shrimp, this recipe makes a hearty
warm-weather salad.  The dill adds a nice twist. It's great to serve
for a luncheon." - Mary Selner

In a large bowl, toss the chicken, ham, shrimp, macaroni, celery,
peppers, onion, salt and pepper.  In a small bowl, combine all
dressing ingredients; mix well.  Pour over salad and toss.  Cover and
chill for 3-4 hours.

From:  "Taste of Home" Magazine, April/May 1995
