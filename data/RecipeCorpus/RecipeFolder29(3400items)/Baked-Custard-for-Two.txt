Title: Baked Custard for Two
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 2 servings
3/4 ts Vanilla extract
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ea Egg
1 c  Milk
3 tb Sugar
In a bowl, lightly beat the egg.  Add milk, sugar, vanilla and salt.
Pour into two ungreased 6-oz. custard cups.  Sprinkle with nutmeg.
Set in a pan containing 1/2 to 1 inch of hot water.  Bake at 350 deg.
F for 35 minutes, or until set.
