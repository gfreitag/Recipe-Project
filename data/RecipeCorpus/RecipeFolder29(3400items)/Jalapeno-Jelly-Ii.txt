Title: Jalapeno Jelly Ii
Categories: Can/preserv, Chilies, Hot/spicy
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Cider vinegar
5 1/2 c  Sugar
1    Bottle Certo (6 oz)
-Green Food coloring
1/3 c  Jalapeno; chopped/seeded
3/4 c  Bell pepper;
-chopped/seeded
Cook peppers, vinegar and sugar 10 minutes.  Add liquid pectin, boil 1
minute longer.  Add green food coloring.  Set 15 minutes before
putting in jars.  Makes 6 (1/2 pint) jars.  (Peppers may be strained
out if you wish)
