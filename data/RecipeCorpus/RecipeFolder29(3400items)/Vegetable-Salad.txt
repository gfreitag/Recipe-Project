Title: VEGETABLE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 md Green or sweet red
-pepper, diced
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Poppy seeds
"A combination of crunchy
-fresh vegetables coated
-with a tangy marinade." -
Pat Scott
2 c  Broccoli florets
2 c  Cauliflowerets
4 lg Mushrooms, sliced
1 ea Rib celery, sliced
In a large bowl, combine all the vegetables.  Combine remaining
ingredients in a jar with a tight-fitting lid; shake well.  Pour over
vegetables and toss.  Cover and chill 6-8 hours. Serves: 4 From:
"Taste of Home" Magazine, April/May 1995
