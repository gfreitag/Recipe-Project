Title: Low Fat Apple Cinnamon Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 1 dozen
1/3 c  Dark Brown Sugar, firmly
Packed
1 1/2 ts Ground Cinnamon
1 1/2 ts Baking Soda
3/4 ts Baking Powder
1 md Apple, peeled, cored &
Chopped
Pam Cooking Spray
3/4 c  Skim Milk
1/3 c  Not Fat Plain Yogurt
2    Egg Whites
1    Egg
2 ts Vanilla
Zest of 1 Lemon
1 1/2 c  All Purpose Flour
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Spray only the bottoms of 12 medium muffin
cups with PAM. In a large bowl beat milk, yogurt, egg whites, egg,
vanilla and lemon zest. In a seperate bowl combine flour, sugar,
cinnamon, baking soda & baking powder. Fold dry ingredients into wet
until just moist. Stir in apple. Divide batter among muffin cups.
Bake 18 to 20 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out
clean. Immediately remove from pan and cool on wire rack.

102 calories & 1 gram fat per muffin
