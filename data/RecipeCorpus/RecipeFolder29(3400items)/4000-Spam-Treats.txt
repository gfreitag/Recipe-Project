Title: 4000 Spam Treats
Categories: Spam, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
-sm.
2    Hard boiled eggs, chopped
1 pk Party rye (sm. rye)
1 cn Spam, grated
1    (8 oz.) white sharp cheese,
-grated
1    Onion, grated or diced very
Mix all ingredients together and add mayonnaise or Miracle Whip until
moistened.  Spread on rye breads and bake in oven at 350 degree oven
for 20 minutes.
