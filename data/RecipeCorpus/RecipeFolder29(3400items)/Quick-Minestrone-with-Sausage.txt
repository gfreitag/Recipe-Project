Title: Quick Minestrone with Sausage
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
46 oz V-8
15 oz Garbanzo beans; drained
1 tb Oregano; fresh and chopped
6 tb Romano cheese
2    Italian sausage link;turkey
1    Potato; diced
1    Zucchini; diced
1    Onion; chopped
1 c  Carrots; shredded
Remove sausage from casing and cook in large pot until no longer
pink. Add the rest of the ingredients except the cheese.  Bring to a
boil, reduce heat and simmer 10 to 15 minutes.  Top each serving with
cheese. 234 calories and 4 grams fat.  Source:  Store ad
