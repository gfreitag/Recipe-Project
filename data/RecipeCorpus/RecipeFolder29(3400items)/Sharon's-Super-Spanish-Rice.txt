Title: Sharon's Super Spanish Rice
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
6 oz Water,(use sauce can)
1 tb Chili powder
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 ts Salt
1 ts Garlic powder
1 lb Lean ground beef
6 cn Tomato sauce (6oz)
1 c  Tomato paste (small
1 ea Green pepper, chopped
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 cn Rotel diced tomatoes
Brown ground beef, onion, and green pepper together. Drain.  Put into
large heavy pan. Add rest of ingredients.  Cover and simmer about 1
1/2 hour or until rice is done. Stir often to keep from sticking.
