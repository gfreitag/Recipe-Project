Title: Mama's Maple Custard Parfait
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 quarts
1/8 ts Salt
1 pt Heavy cream, whipped
1 c  Hot maple ssirup
4    Eggs, seperated
Pour maple sirup slowly over slightly beaten egg yolks. Add salt, cook
until thickened and chill. Whip cream until smooth enough to hold a
soft peak and fold into chilled micture. Fold in stiffly beaten egg
whites and freeze until firm.
