Title: Apple Spinach Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1    Stalk celery; sliced
1/3 c  Onion; chopped
1/3 c  Marzetti poppyseed dressing
4 c  Spinach; washed and torn
2    Apples; cubes
1 c  Shredded carrots
Place all ingredients in a large bowl. Toss and serve.  174 calories
and 11 grams fat.  Source:  Store ad
