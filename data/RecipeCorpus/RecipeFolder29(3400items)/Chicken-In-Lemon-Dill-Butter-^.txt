Title: Chicken In Lemon Dill Butter ^
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1 tb Dill weed
3 lb Chicken, cut in serving
-pieces
1/2 c  Butter
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Salt
1 cl Garlic, minced
1/2 ts Paprika
Melt butter in large skillet with lid. Add all ingredients except
chicken and bring to a boil. Add chicken and bring to boiling point,
but do not actually boil. Cover and simmer 30 minutes or until
chicken is tender. Transfer chicken to a serving platter. Serve with
rice or noodles covered with remaining cooking liquid. 4 servings.
