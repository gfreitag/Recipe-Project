Title: Mushroom-Tomato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Sherry
2 tb Tomato Powder
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 ts Parsley Flakes
1    Onion, chunked sliced
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
1 tb Margarine
1 tb Avacado Oil
4 c  Fresh Mushrooms, sliced
2 tb Chicken Bouillon
In a 2-quart saucepan mix onion and garlic powder, margarine, avacado
oil, mushrooms, bouillon, water, sherry, tomato powder, and pepper.
Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Bring to
a boil; reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.  Sprinkle each serving
with Parmesan cheese and parsley flakes.

Makes 4 servings.
