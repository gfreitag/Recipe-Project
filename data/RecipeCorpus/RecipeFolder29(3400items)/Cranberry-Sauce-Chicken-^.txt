Title: Cranberry Sauce Chicken ^
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Cornstarch
3/4 c  Cranberry juice cocktail
16 oz Can cranberry sauce
1 ts Ground nutmeg
1 ts Ground marjoram
1 tb Minced onion
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
6    Chicken pieces
Oil
2 tb Brown sugar
Mix flour, salt and pepper together in paper bag. Shake chicken until
well coated. Pour oil to a depth of 1/4" in bottom of heavy skillet.
Heat oil and brown chicken on all sides. Cover tightly. Reduce heat
and simmer 20-30 minutes, turning occasionally, until chicken is
tender. Drain on paper towels. Mix brown sugar and cornstarch
together in saucepan. Gradually stir in cranberry juice until smooth.
Add cranberry sauce, spices and onions. Cook lover medium heat,
stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a boil. To serve, place
