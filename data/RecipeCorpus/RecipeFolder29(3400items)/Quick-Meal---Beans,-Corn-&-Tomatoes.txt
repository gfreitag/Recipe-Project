Title: Quick Meal - Beans, Corn & Tomatoes
Categories: Fatfree, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Tomatoes (I like stewed)
1 ea Garlic, cayenne pepper, and
Other spices, to taste
1 ea Beans (cooked Great Northern
Beans, garbonzo, what-
Ever you like)
1 ea Corn
Just mix all of these ingredients together, adjusting the amounts
(using more beans, corn, spices, etc., to taste). Heat & eat!
:) ~Enjoy~

Source: improvised
