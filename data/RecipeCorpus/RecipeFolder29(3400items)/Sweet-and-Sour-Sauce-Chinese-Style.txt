Title: Sweet and Sour Sauce Chinese Style
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chinese
Yield: 1 cup
1 1/2 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Tomato paste or ketchup
2 tb Sherry wine
1 1/2 tb Cornstarch
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
1 ts Chinese chili sauce (opt'l)
1 cl Garlic; crushed or finely
-chopped
1 ea Onion; finely chopped
1 tb Oil
6 tb Chicken stock
1 1/2 tb Wine vinegar
1 1/2 tb Sugar
Fry the garlic in the oil until soft but not colored. Add the
stock and the next five ingredients. Blend the cornstarch to a paste
with 1/4 cup water and stir in. Cook for 1 minute. Taste and season,
adding the chili sauce if wished.
