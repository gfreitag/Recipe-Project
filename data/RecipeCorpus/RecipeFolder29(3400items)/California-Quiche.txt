Title: California Quiche
Categories: Main dish, Tex/mexican, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
1 cn (4 oz) Ortega Diced Green
Chiles
1 1/2 c  Light cream
4    Eggs
1 md Onion -- quartered
1    10" uncooked pie shell
6 sl Bacon, crisply cooked --
Crumbled
1 pk (8 oz) Jack cheese --
Grated
Sprinkle bacon, cheese and chiles onto bottom of pie crust. Put
cream, eggs and onion into blender; blend for about 10 seconds (or
mince onion and mix with beaten eggs and cream).  Pour cream mixture
into pie shell. Bake in preheated oven (350 degree) for 30-40
minutes, or until top is golden brown and quiche is set.  Remove from
oven and allow to set for 5 minutes before serving.

Serve with hot garlic bread, crisp green salad, and don't forget the
wine. Perfect for a brunch.  Quiche can also be serve cold in small
slices as an appetizer.  Garnish with strips of Ortega Pimientos for
extra flavor and color.
