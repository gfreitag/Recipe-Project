Title: NANCY REAGAN'S ONION WINE SOUP
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Vinegar
2 ts Sugar
1 c  Light cream
1 ts Minced parsley
Pepper
4 tb Butter
5 lg Onions, chopped
5 c  Beef broth
1    Celery stalk,chopped
1 lg Potato, peeled and chopped
1 c  Dry white wine
Melt butter in large saucepan. Add chopped onions and gently saute
(about 10 minutes). Add beef broth, celery and potato. Bring to a
boil. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes. Puree mixture in a blender.
Return to saucepan and add wine, vinegar and sugar. Bring to a boil
and simmer 5 minutes. Stir in cream, parsley and pepper to taste.
Heat, being sure NOT to boil, as the cream will curdle.
