Title: 652943 Spam Pockets
Categories: Spam
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1    Loaf frozen bread dough,
-thawed
1 cn Spam luncheon meat, cubed
1 pk Frozen broccoli, cooked
1 tb Minced onion
Mix Spam, broccoli, onion and cheese together.  Season to taste. Roll
dough to 18 x 12 inch rectangle, on floured surface; cut into 6 six
inch squares. Divide mixture evenly and place on center of each
square. Bring points together over filling, pinch and twist to seal
points, then pinch corners. Place on greased baking sheet or sprinkle
baking sheet with cornmeal. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes or
until done. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-01-95
