Title: Tuna Burgers
Categories: Sandwich, Fish
Yield: 3 servings
2    Hard boiled eggs, sliced
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/3 c  Milk
4 sl Buttered toast
1 cn Tuna, drained
4 sl Onion
Place toast on a cookie sheet, spread with tuna, top with onion and
egg. Combine soup and milk and pour over open face sandwich.  Broil
until hot. Randy Rigg
