Title: Zesty Macaroni
Categories: Pasta, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
10 oz Spinach; thaw & drain
4 c  Macaroni; cooked
Pam
1/2 lb Ground turkey
26 1/2 oz Spaghetti sauce
1/4 c  Parmesan; grated
Spray 10" skillet with pam.  Heat pan 1 minute.  Cook turkey until no
longer pink, stirring to separate meat.  Stir in spaghetti sauce,
parmesan cheese and pepper. Heat to boiling. Reduce heat to low.
Cover; cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Stir in spinach.  Heat
through. Toss with macaroni.  250 calories and 6 grams fat.  Source:
