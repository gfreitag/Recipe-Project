Title: Passover Cookies (Egg-reduced)
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Jewish, Passover
Yield: 1 servings
-1/4 c fruit juice
1/8 ts Cinnamon (omit if using
-orange juice)
3/4 ts Passover Baking Soda
1 c  Cake Meal
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped (less if they
-are very finely chopped)
1/4 c  Raisins
PASSOVER COOKIES
-(REDUCED-EGGS)
============================
-===
(Makes about 1 1/2 dozen)
1 ea Egg
1/3 c  Oil
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ea Pear or Apple; grated <OR>
MMMMM------------------------INSTRUCTIONS-----------------------------
In a large bowl, beat egg slightly. Add oil, sugar, cinnamon, grated
fruit <OR> juice. Mix well. Add baking soda, cake meal, nuts and
raisins one at a time, mixing after each addition. Drop by
teaspoonfuls on cookie sheet> Bake at 350F for 10 to 12 minutes.
From: Passover Foods: How To Survive Pesach Without Using Eggs. Pub:
Canadian Jewish News, 6 April, 1995
