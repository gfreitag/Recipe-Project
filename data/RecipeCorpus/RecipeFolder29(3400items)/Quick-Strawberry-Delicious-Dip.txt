Title: Quick Strawberry Delicious Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Fruit
Yield: 2 cups
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar;
1 tb To 2 tb maple syrup
1 pk Cream cheese; softened
1/2 c  Sour cream;
In a small mixing bowl, combine all ingredients, beat until all
ingredients, beat until smooth.  For a light pink color, add 4 mashed
strawberries.  Chill.  Serve with fresh strawberries.  Yield 2 cups.
