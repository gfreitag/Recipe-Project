Title: Apple-Nut Cake
Categories: Mixes, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 cake
1/4 c  Milk
2 c  Apples; pared and grated
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
2 1/3 c  Quick mix
1 c  Sugar brown
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Cloves
2    Eggs; slightly beaten
Mix dry ingredients then add milk and eggs.  Add apples and stir in
raisins and nuts.  Pour in 8" greased pan.  Cook for 40-50 minutes at
375 degrees. Cool on wire rack.

Banana-Nut Cake: omit raisins.  Substitute 1 C mashed bananas for
apples.
