Title: Apple Granola Bars
Categories: Desserts, Candies, Snacks, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
-sugar
1/2 c  Dried Apricots
6 c  Oatmeal, quick cooking
3/4 c  Unsalted Butter
1/2 c  Honey
1 1/2 c  Walnuts, shelled
1/2 c  Dry Apples
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Firmly packed LightBrown
Place nuts and dried fruit in a food processor and chop finely. Place
in a large bowl and add the oatmeal. In a sauce pan, combine honey,
sugar, butter and cinnamon, bring to a boil for 1 minute. Stir into
the oatmeal mixture with the vanilla and salt. Press evenly and
firmly in a 10x15 jelly roll pan. Bake 15 minutes in a preheated
350oF oven until golden. DO NOT OVERBAKE Cool and cut into bars.
Store in an airtight container or refrigerate.
