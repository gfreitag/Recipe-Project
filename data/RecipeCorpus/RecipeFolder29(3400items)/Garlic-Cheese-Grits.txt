Title: Garlic Cheese Grits
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
2    Eggs
Milk
Paprika
1 c  Cooked grits
1    Roll Kraft garlic cheese
1/4 lb Butter
Cook grits according to directions on the box.  Immediately add garlic
cheese and butter.  Stir until melted and mixed.  Beat eggs slightly
and add enough milk to make 1 cup.  Add to grits and mix well.  Bake
in a buttered casserole dish for 50 to 60 minutes at 300 degrees.
Sprinkle with paprika before baking. Randy Rigg
