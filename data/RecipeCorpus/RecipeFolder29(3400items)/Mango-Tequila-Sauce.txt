Title: Mango-Tequila Sauce
Categories: Desserts, Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 1 cup
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
3    Ripe mangos
3 tb Tequila; preferably gold
1.  Peel the mangos; cut the pulp off the pits.  In a food processor,
blender, or food mill, puree the mango pulp.

2.  Place the mango pulp and tequila in a small nonreactive saucepan.
Bring to a boil.  Stir and remove from the heat.  Stir in the nutmeg.
Chill several hours before serving.

from The Well-Filled Tortilla by Victoria Wise and Susanna Hoffman
