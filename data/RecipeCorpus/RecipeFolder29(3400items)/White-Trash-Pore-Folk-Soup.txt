Title: White Trash Pore Folk Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Crackers, Whitetrash
Yield: 4 servings
Salt and pepper
Milk
Crackers
Warm milk, break crackers into milk and season with salt and pepper.
Hooka tooka my soda crackers? Does yer Mammy chaw tobaccer? If yer
Mammy chaw tobaccer then Then Hooka tooka my soda cracker? Origin:
Adapted from White Trash. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, April/95.
