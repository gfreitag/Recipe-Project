Title: Cranberry Chicken^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
Eight pieces
1/2 lb Cranberries
2 tb Rice vinegar
1/4 c  Apple juice concentrate
1 ts Cornstarch
4 c  Cranberry juice,
Unsweetened
1 1/2 tb Dried basil
1    Onion, thinly sliced
3 lb (to 3 1/2) chicken, cut in
MMMMM-------------------------MIXED WITH------------------------------
2 tb Cold water
Simmer 3 cups of the cranberry juice with the basil and onion slices
10 minutes. Cool; pour over chicken and marinate in the refrigerator
overninght. Poach chicken in marinade until tender; keep warm. Simmer
remianing ingredients except cornstarch and water mixture about 20
minutes or until berries are tender. Stir in cornstarch mixture and
cook until thickened. Serve poured over chicken.

Per serving: Calories: 330 Protein: 30g Carbohydrates: 43g Fat: 4g
Sodium: 125mg Cholesterol: 77mg
