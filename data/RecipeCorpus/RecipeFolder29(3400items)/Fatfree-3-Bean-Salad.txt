Title: Fatfree 3 Bean Salad
Categories: Fatfree, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1    15-16oz can kidney beans
1 lg Red onion, coarsley chopped
1/2 c  Seasoned rice vinegar
1 lb Fresh green beans, 1"
Pieces, steamed until tender
& cooled
1    15-16oz can garbanzos
Combine all ingredients in a sealed plastic container and shake well.
Let sit in fridge overnight, gets better with age, up to 10 days in
the fridge.
