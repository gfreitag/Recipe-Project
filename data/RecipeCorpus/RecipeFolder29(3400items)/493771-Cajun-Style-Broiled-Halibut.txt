Title: 493771 Cajun Style Broiled Halibut
Categories: Cajun/creol, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper to taste
4    (4-6 oz.) halibut steaks,
-thawed if necessary
2 tb Paprika (Hungarian is best)
1 1/2 ts Dried oregano
1 1/2 ts Dried thyme
1 ts Onion powder
1 ts Black pepper
Mix together all ingredients until well combined.  Sprinkle
approximately 1/2 teaspoon spice mixture over the surface of each
halibut steak before broiling.  Broil 10 minutes per inch of
thickness, measured at thickest part, or until fish flakes when
tested with a fork. Save remaining spice mixture in airtight
container for future uses. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On
03-15-95
