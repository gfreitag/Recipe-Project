Title: Beef with Cauliflower and Snow Peas
Categories: Chinese, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Water
3 tb Soy Sauce
1 ts Cornstarch
1 ts Instant Beef Bouillon
1/2 ts Sugar
3 c  Hot Cooked Rice
1    Small Head Cauliflower
1    Green Pepper, in strips
1 lb Steak, cut in thin strips
1    Med Onion, Chopped
1 c  Snow Pea Pods
2 tb Veg oil
1    Clove Garlic, Minced
Break cauliflower into flowerets and cut into 1/4" slices. Prepare
green pepper, onion and steak. Set aside. In a bowl whisk together
water, soy, cornstarch, bouillon powder and sugar. Set aside. In a
wok, heat oil and fry garlic until fragrant. Add cauliflower, green
pepper, and onion and stir fry 2-3 min. Add steak and pea pods. Stir
fry another minute. Pour in the liquid, stir as the sauce thickens,
cover, reduce heat and let simmer 1 min. Serve over hot rice.
