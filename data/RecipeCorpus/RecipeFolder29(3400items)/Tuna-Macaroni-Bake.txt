Title: Tuna Macaroni Bake
Categories: Main dish, Fish, Pasta, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
3/4 ts Salt
3 c  Cooked macaroni
1 1/2 c  Grated American cheese
2    6 1/2 oz. cans of tuna
2    Eggs, well beaten
3/4 c  Evaporated milk (Milnot)
1/2 c  Water
3/4 ts Dry mustard
1 tb Grated onion
Mix all ingredients together and turn into a greased baking dish, 6 x
10 inches.  Bake in a 375 degree oven until firm and light brown,
about 1 hour.  Cut in squares and top each with catsup or chili
sauce. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   04-15-95
