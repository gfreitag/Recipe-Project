Title: Snow Ice Cream
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1 cn Milnot or Eagle brand
1    Egg
3/4 c  Sugar
pn Salt
Mix together the egg, sugar, salt and vanilla.  Then pour in milk.
Fold in clean snow into the mixture until it becomes stiff.  Serve
PDQ. (Hopefully, we won't try this again until the winter of 1995.
Also finding that clean snow can be a chore) Randy Rigg
