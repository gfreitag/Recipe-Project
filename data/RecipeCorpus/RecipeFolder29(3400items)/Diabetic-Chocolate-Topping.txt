Title: Diabetic Chocolate Topping
Categories: Diabetic, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 3 cups
1 ts Salt;
2 tb Butter;
2 ts Vanilla extract;
3 c  Skim milk;
2 oz Baking chocolate;
3 tb Cornstarch;
1/2 c  Granulated sugar replacement
Combine milk, chocolate, cornstarch, sugar replacement and salt in
saucepan. Bring to a full boil, and boil for a 2 to 3 minutes; remove
from heat.  Stir in the butter and vanilla. Food Exchange per
serving: 2 tb. 1 FAT EXCHANGE; CAL: 2 TB: 35; Source: The Diabetic
Chocolate Cookbook by Mary Jane Finsand. Brought to you and yours via
Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
