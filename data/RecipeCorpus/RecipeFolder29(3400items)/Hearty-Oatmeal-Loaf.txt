Title: Hearty Oatmeal Loaf
Categories: Fatfree, Breads, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/2 c  - 1 T. whole-wheat flour
1/4 c  Wheat gluten
3/4 c  Quick oats
3 ts Yeast
1/2 c  Apple juice
3/4 c  Water
2 tb Honey
3 tb Orange juice concentrate
1 1/2 ts Salt
Mix in the order recommended by your breadmaker.  This uses the
fresh-milk white/wheat setting on the Black & Decker.

Source: my adaptations from the cookbook that came with the machine.

Posted by Lois Patterson <lois@helix.net> to the Fatfree Digest
[Volume 16 Issue 2] Mar. 6, 1995.
