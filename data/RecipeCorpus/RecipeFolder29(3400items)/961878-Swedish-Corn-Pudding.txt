Title: 961878 Swedish Corn Pudding
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
-Pinch of salt
2 sl Bread crumbs
-Bread crumbs also for
-topping
2 cn Cream corn
4    Eggs
1 c  Milk
1 tb Sugar
Mix all ingredients together.  Then put bread crumbs with lots of
butter on top and bake for 20-25 minutes, oven temperature, 350
degrees. If you are serving a large group use 3 cans of cream corn,
add 2 more eggs, 1 1/2 milk.
