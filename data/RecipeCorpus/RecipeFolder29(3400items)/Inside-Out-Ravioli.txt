Title: Inside-Out Ravioli
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 servings
ds Pepper
2 c  Shell or elbow macaroni,
Cooked and drained
4 oz Sharp shredded cheese
1/2 c  Soft bread crumbs
2    Eggs, well beaten
1/4 c  Salad oil
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1 cl Garlic
1 pk Frozen chopped spinach 10oz
1    Jar spaghetti sauce with
Mushrooms 32 oz.
1/2 ts Salt
Brown first 3 ingredients in a large skillet.  Cook spinach as
directed, drain, reserving liquid; add water to make 1 cup.  Add
spinach liquid and the next 5 ingredients to meat mixture.  Simmer
for 10 minutes. Combine spinach with macaroni and remaining
ingredients; spread in a 13x9x2 inch baking dish.  Top with meat
sauce. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Let stand for 10 minutes.
