Title: The Best Home-Made Bubble Stuff
Categories: Kids, Information
Yield: 1 batch
-dishwashing liquid
3/4 c  Light corn syrup
6 c  Water
2 c  Crystal Octagon
Mix all the ingredients together in a dishpan. Put in a jar and give
it to your kids. It should be used right away.

Beat This! by Ann Hodgman ISBN 1-881527-21-2 pg 41
