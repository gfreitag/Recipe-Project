Title: Cheese-Peanut Sandwich Filling
Categories: Sandwich, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 sandwiches
2 tb Salad dressing;
1/4 lb Cream cheese;
1/4 c  Peanuts;
Grind cheese and nuts through food chopper using coarse knife.  Add
salad dressing and enough cream to make right consistancy for
spreading. Source: Food for The Body, For the Soul, The Moody Bible
Institute of Chicgo. Mrs. Frances Youngren, Director of WMBI Home
Hour, 1943
