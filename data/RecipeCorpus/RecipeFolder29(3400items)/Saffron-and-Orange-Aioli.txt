Title: Saffron and Orange Aioli
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Question?
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
2    Garlic clove(s)
-finely minced
1 c  Plus 2 tb olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 ts Saffron threads
2 tb Dry white wine
2 tb Orange juice
2 ts Finely grated orange zest
2 lg Egg yolks, at room temp
1. In a small saucepan, crumble the saffron into the white wine and
orange juice and warm over low heat, stirring, for 2 minutes. Remove
from the heat and stir in the orange zest. Steep for 15 minutes.

2. In a food processor, combine the egg yolks, garlic, and lemon
juice. With the machine on, add the oil in a thin steady stream and
process until thickened and emulsified. Add the saffron-orange
infusion and process until blended. Season with salt and pepper and
serve.
