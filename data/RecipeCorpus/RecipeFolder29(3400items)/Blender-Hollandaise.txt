Title: Blender Hollandaise
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 cup
1/8 ts Cayenne
1/2 c  Butter
3    Egg yolks
2 tb Lemon juice
1/8 ts Salt
Have the egg yolks, lemon juice, salt and cayenne ready in the
blender. Heat the butter to bubbling but not browned. Cover the
blender, turn on high for 3 seconds. Remove the lid and with the
blender running pour in the butter in a slow, steady stream. The
sauce should be finished seconds after the butter is added.
