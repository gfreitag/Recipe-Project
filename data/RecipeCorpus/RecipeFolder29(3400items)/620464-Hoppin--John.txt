Title: 620464 Hoppin  John
Categories: Rice, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Chopped jalapeno peppers
2 c  Cooked rice
1/2 c  Grated cheese
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 lb Ground sausage
1 cn Stewed tomatoes
1 cn Black-eye peas
Brown ground beef and sausage, then drain.  Add tomatoes, peas,
peppers. Let simmer for 15 minutes.  Add cooked rice and let simmer 5
more minutes. Pour into casserole dish.  Top with cheese and bake for
15 minutes or until cheese is melted.
