Title: Easy Dessert
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1    Stick oleo; melted
1 c  Nutmeats
1    Can pie filling
1    Small pkg cake mix
Spread one can of pie filling over bottom of greased square pan.
Spread over this one, small cake mix dry, as it comes from box. Pour
over this one stick melted oleo.  Sprinkle one cup of nutmeats over
mixture and bake from 45-60 mins in 350 oven. Cut into squares when
cool and serve with whipped cream or ice cream.
