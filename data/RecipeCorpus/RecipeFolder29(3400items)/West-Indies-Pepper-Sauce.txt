Title: West Indies Pepper Sauce
Categories: Chilies, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
2 tb Fresh lime juice
1 tb Chopped fresh ginger
2 tb Coriander
1 ts Dijon-style mustard
1 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Ground turmeric
3 ea Scotch bonnets, seeds and
; stems removed, chopped
2 c  White vinegar
1 c  Chopped onion
1 cn (15 ounces) diced papaya,
; incuding the juice
1/2 c  Golden rainsins
1/4 c  Malt vinegar
In a saucepan, combine all ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce
the heat and simmer for about 10 minutes. Allow the sauce to sit for
a least 2 hours to blend the flavors.  The sauce will keep for months
covered in the refrigerator.

Dave DeWitt and Nancy Gerlach.  "The Habanero Cookbook".
