Title: Creamy Artichoke Dressing^
Categories: Salads, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 24 servings
1 tb Apple juice concentrate
1 tb Chopped green chilies
1 tb Chopped fresh dill
1/2    Onion, finely chopped
1 ts Basil
1/2 c  Lowfat cottage cheese
1/2 c  Lowfat buttermilk or skim
Milk
1/2 cn (14oz)water packed
Artichokes, rinsed and
Chopped
Blend cottage cheese and buttermilk until smooth in blender. Add
artichokes and apple juice concentrate, blend until smooth. Add
remaining ingredients and blend just long enough to combine. Store in
refrigerator.

Per tablespoon serving: Calories: 12 Protein: 1.5g Carbohydrates: 2g
Fat: trace Sodium: 34mg Cholesterol: trace
