Title: Stardust Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 3 servings
1 lg Can of crushed pineapple
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Cornstarch
1    Egg
1 pk Dream Whip
3 oz Cream cheese
2 lg Packages of strawberry
Banana Jello
3 c  Hot water
2 c  Marshmallows
1/2 c  Cold water
2 sl Bananas
Dissolve Jello in hot water; add marshmallows and stir until
dissolved; add cold water and pour into 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan.  When
cool enough; add bananas and chill.  Meanwhile cook until thick
pineapple, sugar, cornstarch, egg.  When cool spread on chilled Jello
mixture. Add cream cheese and beat until smooth.  Spread on top of
pineapple filling. Randy Rigg
