Title: 279809 Chicken Elegant
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Curry powder (optional)
1    Stalk celery, chopped
1 md Onion, chopped
1 c  Grated cheese
Buttered cracker crumbs or
Italian crumbs
2 pk Frozen broccoli spears or a
Bunch of broccoli
1    Whole chicken or 3 full
Chicken breasts
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 tb Lemon juice
Cook broccoli and drain.  Boil chicken and debone.  Saute celery and
onion in butter and set aside.  Mix mayonnaise, soup, lemon juice,
chicken and curry powder.  Grease a 9 x 13 inch casserole dish. Layer
broccoli first, then chicken mixture, then cheese.  Top with crumbs.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes at 350 degrees until bubbly.
