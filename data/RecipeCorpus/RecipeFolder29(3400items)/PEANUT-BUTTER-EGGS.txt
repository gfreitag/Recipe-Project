Title: PEANUT BUTTER EGGS
Categories: Candies, Holiday
Yield: 24 servings
4 tb Honey;can use 2 to 4 tbls
1 lb Milk choc-coating
1 c  Peanut Butter
2/3 c  Powder Milk
Mix well together, and chill make into egg shape or can be put in
peanut butter cup mold or papers. Dip in Melted Chocolate coating.
Put in Freezer for a few minutes. Good Luck and Enjoy.
