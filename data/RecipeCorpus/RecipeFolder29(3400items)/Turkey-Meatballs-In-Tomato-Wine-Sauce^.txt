Title: Turkey Meatballs In Tomato Wine Sauce^
Categories: Poultry, Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1    Bay leaf
1 ts Low sodium soy sauce
1 1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/4 c  Dry sherry
2 tb Tomato paste, no salt added
1 md Onion, sliced
2 cl Garlic, minced
2 c  Chicken stock
1 1/2 tb Cornstarch
1 cn (8oz) chopped tomatoes,
Drained, no salt added
MMMMM-------------------------MEATBALLS------------------------------
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/4 c  Chicken stock
1/4 c  Tomato sauce
ds Pepper
1/2 md Onion, minced
1 lb Ground turkey
2 sl Whole wheat bread, torn
1/4 c  Whole wheat bread crumbs
1 ts Onion powder
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Low sodium soy sauce
1    Egg white
Saute onion and garlic until soft in a bit of stock. Combine
cornstarch and 2 tablespoons stock and set aside until needed.
Combine garlic and onion with the rest of the sauce ingredients and
bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer over low heat 5-7 minutes.
Stir in the cornstarch and cook until thickened, stirring. Combine
meatball ingredients and cook in sauce 30-45 minutes until done.

Per serving : Calories: 137 Protein: 18g Carbohydrates: 12g Fat: 2g
Sodium: 246mg Cholesterol: 45mg
