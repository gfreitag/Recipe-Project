Title: Hasoret
Categories: Appetizers, Holiday
Yield: 2 cups
1 ts Honey
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ds Nutmeg
1 ds Allspice
2 lg Sweet apples, peeled, cored
-- & diced
1 c  Walnuts
1/4 c  Kosher wine
Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor.  Process until
coarsely pureed.  Store in an air-tight container until needed.
