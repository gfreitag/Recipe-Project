Title: Sweet And Sour Pickled Beets
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
6    Peppercorns, lightly
-bruised
1    Stick Cinnamon
4 tb Sugar
Water to cover completely
2 c  Cooked (can be canned)
-beets sliced or cubed
Vinegar to half cover
6    Allspice Berries
(or 1/8 t. powder)
5    Whole Cloves
Let marinate for a couple of days in the refrigerator, then serve.
