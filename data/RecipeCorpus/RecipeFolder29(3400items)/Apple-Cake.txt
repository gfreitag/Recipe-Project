Title: Apple Cake
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
And chopped
1 c  Shredded Coconut
1 c  Chopped Nuts
-
6 tb Butter
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/2 c  Buttermilk
Sauce-----
2 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
1 1/4 c  Salad Oil
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Orange Juice
3 c  Flour -- sifted
1 ts Baking Soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Vanilla Extract
1 c  Apples, Raw -- peeled
Mix together all the ingredients up to the apples, in the order
given. Then mix in, with a spoon, the apples, coconut, and nuts. Pour
the batter into a greased and floured tube pan. Bake in a preheated
325 F oven for 1 1/2 hrs.

When the cake is almost done, prepare the sauce. Melt the butter and
stir in all the other ingredients. Bring the mixture to a rolling
boil. Pour the sauce over the hot cake while it is still in the pan.
Let it stand for at least 1 hr before turning it out.

from A Moveable Feast, Philadelphia, Penn.
