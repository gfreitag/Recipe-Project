Title: 661652 Chicken Corn Soup (Singapore)
Categories: Soup/stew, Poultry, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
2 sm Green onions, sliced very
-thin
1 cn Creamed corn
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
3 c  Water
1 tb Soy sauce
Bring water to a boil and add can of corn, can of soup, soy sauce, and
pepper. Stir well and let cook over very low heat until ready to
serve. Stir occasionally while you prepare the other dishes. When you
are ready to eat, pour the soup into a big bowl to put on the table.
Sprinkle the onions on top.
