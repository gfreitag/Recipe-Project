Title: Chinese Pot Roast
Categories: Asian, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Burgundy wine
1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms; sliced
1 tb Cornstarch
1/4 c  Water
3 lb Chuck roast
-- 1 1/2 in thick
1 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Bottled stir-fry sauce
Preparation Time: 2:30 Heat Oil in dutch oven. Add meat and brown on
both sides.  Combine stir-fry Sauce with Wine and pour over meat.
Cover, reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour 40 minutes. Add mushrooms
and simmer, covered, another 15 minutes or until Beef is tender.
Combine cornstarch and 1/4 cup Water.

Remove meat to platter and keep warm.  Add cornstarch mixture to pan
juices; cook and stir until mixture boils and thickens slightly.  To
serve, cut meat across grain into thin slices and serve with the
mushroom gravy.
