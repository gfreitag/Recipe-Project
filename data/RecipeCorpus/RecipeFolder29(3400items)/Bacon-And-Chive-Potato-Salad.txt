Title: Bacon And Chive Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
-cooked and crumbled
1/4 c  Chopped chives; or
Sliced green onions
6 c  Unpeeled small red potatoes
1/4 c  Kraft Real Mayonnaise
2 tb Stone ground mustard
8 sl Oscar mayer bacon; crispy
Recipe by: Kraft ad, 92/04 Preparation Time: 2:00

Add potatoes to boiling water; cook 14 minutes or until tender. Drain.

Mix mayonaise and mustard in large bowl.

Add potatoes, bacon and chives; mix lightly. Refrigerate. Makes 6
