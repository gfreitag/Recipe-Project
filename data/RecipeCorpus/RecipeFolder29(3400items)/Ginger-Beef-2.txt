Title: Ginger Beef 2
Categories: Meats, Stir fry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
-Sue Woodward
MMMMM-------------------ROCKY MOUNTAIN CUISINE------------------------
3 tb Peanut oil; divided use
1 lg Yellow onion; julienned
1    Red bell pepper; julienned
1    Green bell pepper; julienned
Salt; to taste
Pepper; to taste
White rice; steamed
1 lb Flank steak; sliced thinly
-across the grain
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Dry sherry
1 tb Fresh ginger; minced
1 ts Garlic; minced
1 ts Sesame oil
Mix soy sauce, sherry, ginger, garlic, and sesame oil for marinade.
Add the steak and marinate for 15 mins. Heat a lg. wok or skillet,
and add 2 tbsp peanut oil.

Drain beef, reserving the marinade, and stir fry the beef for 1 min.
in peanut oil.  Remove beef from the wok, and add 1 tbsp peanut oil
to the wok and stir fry the vegetables for 1 minute. Add the beef and
marinade, season with salt and pepper, and serve over steamed white
rice.

Source:  The Boulderado Hotel in Boulder, CO
