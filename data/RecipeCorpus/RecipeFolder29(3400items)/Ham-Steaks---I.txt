Title: Ham Steaks - I
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Currant Jelly
2 tb Prepared Horseradish
2    15 oz. Bone-in Ham Steaks
-(pre-cooked)
Gash the sides of the ham, then place in a shallow baking dish.  Mix
currant jelly and horseradish.  Spread on top of the ham and bake for
30 minutes at 325 degrees.
