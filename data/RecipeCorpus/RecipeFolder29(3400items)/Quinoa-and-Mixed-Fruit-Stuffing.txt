Title: Quinoa and Mixed Fruit Stuffing
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 5 cups
2/3 c  Dried currants
2/3 c  Toasted walnuts
-coarsely broken
1 tb Thyme leaves or
1 ts Dried thyme
1 ts Ground coriander seeds
3 c  Cooked quinoa (use Method 2)
2 tb Flat leaf parsley, minced
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 lb Fennel pork sausage, removed
-from casings and crumbled
1 lg Onion(s), finely chopped
1 lg Garlic clove(s), minced
1 lg Tart green apple
-peeled and diced
1 md Ripe pear
-peeled and diced
1 lg Navel orange
In a large skillet, saut the crumbled sausage over medium heat until
it loses its color. Lift out with slotted spoon and set aside. To the
same skillet, add onions and garlic and cook, stirring occasionally,
for about 5 minutes or until the onions are translucent. Lower the
heat, stir in the apples and pears and continue cooking, stirring
occasionally.

Remove the peel from the orange with a vegetable peeler and mince it
finely. Then peel and discard the white pith from the orange. Cut the
orange into pieces and add to the skillet with the remaining
ingredients, including the reserved sausage. Stir to combine, then
cook for 2 minutes more. Set aside to cool. Can be prepared in
advance and refrigerator. Leftover stuffing can be frozen. makes
enough for 2 chickens or 1 small turkey.
