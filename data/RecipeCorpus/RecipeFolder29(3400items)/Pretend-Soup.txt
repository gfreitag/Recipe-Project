Title: Pretend Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Kids, Fruit, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 4 kids
1    Banana;
1 c  Berries; fresh or frozen
-and defrosted
2 c  Orange juice;
1/2 c  Plain yogurt;
1 tb Honey;
1 ts Lemon juice;
Place orange juice in a bowl.  Add yogurt, honey, and lemon juice.
Whish until blended.  Place 5 banana slices and 2 tablespoon berries
in each bowl. Ladie the soup over the berries and bananas. Source:
The San Diego Union-Tribune, Food Section, Mar.23, 1995. + "Pretend
Soup" by Mollie Katzen and Ann Henderson Brought to you and yours via
Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
