Title: Tropical Chicken Salad ^
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Lemon juice
Salad greens
2 c  Diced cooked chicken
4 sl Pineapple, diced
2    Bananas, diced
1 c  Chopped celery
Toss all ingredients except salad greens. Refrigerate 1 hour until
well chilled. Line bowl with greens. Spoon chicken salad on top and
serve.
