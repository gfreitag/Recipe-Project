Title: Potatoes with Kale or Onions, Irish Style
Categories: Potato, Irish, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Onion, sliced
1/4 c  Butter, melted
2    Potatoes
1/2 c  Kale, chopped or
Peel, quarter, boil and mash the potatoes. While they are boiling
either steam the kale or fry the onion in a little butter. Mix the
vegetable into the mashed potato. Press the mixture into a soup bowl,
invert and tap out onto a plate. Press a well in the top of the
inverted mound to hold the melted butter.

Variations: Fill the well with gravy, meatballs, stew, a sausage or a
poached egg.

Garnish the dish with crumbled fried bacon, grated cheese or diced ham

Substitute 1 or 2 Tbl minced parsley for the kale.
