Title: Senator Joseph Montoya's New Mexican Chili
Categories: Chili, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 ts Flour
3 tb Unspiced chili powder
2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 lb Round steak, cut in 1/4"
-cubes
2    Cloves garlic, minced
2 tb Oil
Heat oil in saucepan of Dutch oven, add meat and cook and stir 10
min. add garlic when meat is partially browned. Sprinkle with flour
and stir 1 min longer. Add chili powder, water and salt. Cover and
simmer 45 min.

Serves 4 Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.20
