Title: 42391 Spam Pizza
Categories: Spam
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Shredded cheese
3 tb Ketchup
1 tb Prepared mustard
1    (12 oz.) can Spam, finely
-chopped
1 sm Onion, chopped
1 sm Green pepper, chopped
Mix all ingredients well and put on an unbaked pizza shell.  Bake at
425 degrees for about 20 to 30 minutes until crust is nicely browned
underneath or according to pizza crust directions.
