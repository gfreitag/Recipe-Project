Title: Oatmeal-Chocolate Morsel Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 72 servings
1 c  Semisweet chocolate morsels
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
3 lg Eggs, lightly beaten
1 ea Pk yellow cake mix with
-pudding
1 c  Regular oats, uncooked
1 c  Raisins
Combine all ingredients; stir well. Drop dough by rounded
teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto a lightly greased baking sheet. Bake
at 350 for 8 to 10 minutes.  Remove to wire racks to cool.
