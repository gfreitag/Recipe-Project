Title: Sambal Ulek
Categories: Relishes, Indian
Yield: 1 servings
Wine or rice vinegar
30    Chili peppers
2 ts Salt
Put peppers and salt in a food processor. Pulse until everything is
mixed. Add enough vinegar to make a sauce. BE SURE TO WEAR GLOVES
