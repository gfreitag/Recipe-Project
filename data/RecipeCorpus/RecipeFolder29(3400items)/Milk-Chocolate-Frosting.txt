Title: Milk Chocolate Frosting
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1    Egg
1/4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
2    Squares unsweetened
-chocolate
2 tb Soft butter
1 c  Sifted confectioners sugar
Melt chocolate over hot water; let cool. In small bowl, with portable
electric mixer, at medium speed, beat butter, egg, sugar, milk and
vanilla until smooth. Stir in chocolate. Set bowl of frosting in ice
water. Beat until frosting is smooth and barely holds its shape.

Makes enough to frost a 9-inch square cake; or a 13x9x2  inch oblong
pan; or a loaf cake.
