Title: Tabboulleh^
Categories: Salads, Healthy, Low cal/fat, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Fresh parsley, minced
2    (or 3) chopped tomatoes
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 ts Low sodium soy sauce
Sprouts or lettuce
4 c  Boiling water or chicken
Stock
1 1/4 c  Bulgur
3/4 c  Cooked chick peas
1/2 c  Fresh mint, minced
Combine water and bulgur. cover and let stand bulgur is fluffy, 1-2
hours. Strain and squeeze out excess water. Combine with remaining
ingredients except sprouts or lettuce, chill and serve over sprouts.

Per serving: Calories: 157 Protein: 5g Carbohydrates: 34g Fat: trace
Sodium: 100mg Cholesterol: 0mg
