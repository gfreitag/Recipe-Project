Title: EAST-WEST ASPARAGUS SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Brown sugar
1 tb Olive oil
1 lb Fresh asparagus
2 tb Soy sauce
Cut or break off the tough bottoms of the spears. Cut spears into
2-inch pieces. Drop into a pot of boiling water and simmer 2 minutes,
or just until tender. DO NOT OVERCOOK. Drain and rinse immediately
under cold running water until the asparagus are cool. Drain well.
Place as- paragus in a bowl and add the soy sauce, sugar and oil. Mix
well. Serve on lettuce bed.
