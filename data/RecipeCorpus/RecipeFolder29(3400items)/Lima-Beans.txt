Title: Lima Beans
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
2    Stems celery, chopped
1/2 pt Coffee Rich
1 tb Flour
2    Boxes of frozen limas
1/2    Stick butter
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
Saute celery and onion in butter until tender and stir in flour. Cook
and drain limas.  Stir in onions and celery.  Fold in 1/2 pint of
Coffee Rich. Randy Rigg
