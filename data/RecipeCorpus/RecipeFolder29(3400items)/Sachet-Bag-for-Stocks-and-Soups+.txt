Title: Sachet Bag for Stocks and Soups+
Categories: Herbs/spice, Mixes
Yield: 1 recipe
1/2 ts Cracked black pepper
3    Cloves garlic, crushed
Cheesecloth or teaball
1 tb Chopped parsley with stems
1 ts Thyme
2 sm Bay leaves
Combine all ingredients and tie in a piece of cheesecloth or place in
tealball. Place in stew, stock or soup as you are cooking.
