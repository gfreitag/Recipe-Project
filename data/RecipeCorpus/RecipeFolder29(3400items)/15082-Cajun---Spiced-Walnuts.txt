Title: 15082 Cajun - Spiced Walnuts
Categories: Cajun/creol, Snacks, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Garlic salt
2 ts Paprika
4 c  (1 lb.) walnut halves
2    Egg whites, slightly beaten
2 ts Ground cayenne pepper
2 ts Mixed dried herbs
Coat a large, shallow baking pan with non-stick vegetable spray. Mix
egg whites with spices.  Stir in walnuts; coat thoroughly. Spread in
prepared pan.  Bake in a 350 degree oven 15 to 18 minutes, or until
dry and crisp. Cool completely.  Makes 4 cups.
