Title: 46043 Enchilada Bean Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Velveeta
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Box Velveeta cheese
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 cn Rotel tomatoes
1 lb Ground beef
1    Dozen corn tortillas
1 cn Ranch style beans
1    Chopped onion
Put 6 tortillas in 10x13 inch baking pan.  Brown meat; add salt,
garlic salt and chili powder to taste in meat.  Spread meat over the
tortillas; add chopped onions.  Spread on beans.  Slice entire box of
cheese and layer over beans.  Add 6 remaining tortillas and add the
cream of chicken soup (full strength).  Then add tomatoes. Cover
tightly with foil and bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees.
