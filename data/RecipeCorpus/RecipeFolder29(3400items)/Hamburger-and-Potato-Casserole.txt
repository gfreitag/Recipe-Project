Title: Hamburger and Potato Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Potato
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Onions
1 tb Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 md Potatoes
Sliced cheese
1 lb Ground beef
3/4 c  Canned milk
1/2 c  Soda crackers (14)
Slice potatoes in the bottom of a 9 x 13 inch greased dish.  Sprinkle
with salt and pepper.  Cover potatoes with cheese.  Mix the remaining
ingredients together and place over the cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees
for 1 1/2 hours. Randy Rigg
