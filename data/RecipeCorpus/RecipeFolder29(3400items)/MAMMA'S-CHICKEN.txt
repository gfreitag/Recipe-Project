Title: MAMMA'S CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
Mixed with
2 tb Grated cheese
1    Onion, sliced
Olive oil
2    Chickens, cut in serving
Pieces
1 c  Lemon juice
2 c  Seasoned bread crumbs
Dip chicken in lemon juice and then roll in seasoned bread crumb and
cheese mixture. In a baking dish, place onion in and around chicken.
Sprinkle with olive oil and bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 1
hour, uncovered. Serve immediately. You can also prepare potatoes
this way. Use 4 potatoes, peeled and cut in sixths lenghtwise. Rub a
little olive oil on each piece, dip in seasoned bread crumbs and
place in between the chicken pieces. Or you may cook the potatoes in
their own dish at 350 for 1 hour.
