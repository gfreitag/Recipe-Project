Title: Sausage & Squash Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Chopped parsley
1/2 ts Whole oregano
1/2 ts Salt
2    Eggs, beaten
1 lb Bulk pork sausage
1 cl Garlic, crushed
4 c  Sliced yellow squash
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
Saute sausage, squash and remaining ingredients; blend well.  Spoon
mixture into a lightly greased 10 x 6 x 1 1/2 inch baking dish.  Bake
at 325 degrees for 20 minutes. Randy Rigg
