Title: Plum Pudding - Grandma Moses
Categories: Countyfairs, Puddings
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Flour
1/2 pt Milk
4    Eggs
1/2 lb Raisins
1/2 lb Suet
1/2 lb Currants
Her recipe quoted verbatim:

"1/2 pound of raisins, the same of suet, currants & flour, 1/2 pint
of milk & 4 eggs. To be boiled four hours."

From the section about the DELAWARE COUNTY FAIR of Walton, N.Y. Fair
dates: Mon through Sat, 3rd week in August.
