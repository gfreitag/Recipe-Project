Title: HARVEST POTATOES AND HERBS
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Potato
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Dried thyme leaves
2 lb Potatoes, peeled, very
-thinly sliced, divided
1/4 ts Ground pepper
6 tb Butter or margarine, melted
-and divided
1/2 ts Salt
6 tb Finely chopped onions,divide
1/4 ts Dried basil leaves
Preheat oven to 425F.  In small bowl combine seasonings.  Place 2
tbsp. melted butter in a 10" non-stick skillet.  (If handle is not
ovenproof, wrap with foil.)  Arrange a third of the potato slices in
a circular pattern in skillet.  Sprinkle with about 1/4 tsp.
seasonings, 4 tsp. melted butter and 2 tbsp. onion.  Repeat layers
twice. Cover lightly with foil; press layers down.  Bake 20 minutes.
Uncover; bake 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.  Remove from
oven; press layers down with a spatula. Let stand 10 minutes. Invert
onto serving platter to unmold potatoes. Remove from skillet and
serve. Submitted By FRANK HEMPHILL   On   03-14-95
