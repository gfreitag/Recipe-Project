Title: 778719 Scalloped Potato And Spam Dinner
Categories: Spam, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
-thin)
-Toast, crumbled
1 cn Spam
1 cn Cream of celery soup
-Potatoes (raw, sliced
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Chop meat and stir together with soup. Put
1/2 mixture in an ungreased casserole dish.  Top with 1/2 of the
potatoes. Put meat and soup mixture next and top with potatoes until
dish is almost full. Cover; bake about 1 1/2 hours or until potatoes
are fork tender. Toss crumbs with melted butter, remove cover from
dish and sprinkle top with crumbs.  Bake 15 minutes longer.
