Title: Potato Fan Club Sandwich
Categories: Poultry, Sandwich, Meats, Vegetables, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 lb Tomatoes, sliced 1/3 inch
-slices
1 1/2 lb Cooked crisp bacon
72    Lettuce leaves
24    Russet potatoes, scrubbed
-(about 15 lbs.)
-Kikkoman soy sauce
1 1/2 lb Smoked turkey, thinly
-sliced
MMMMM---------------------GARLIC MAYONNAISE--------------------------
1 1/4 tb Dijon-style mustard
1 1/4 ts White wine vinegar
1 1/4 c  Mayonnaise
3/4 oz Green onions, minced
1/2 oz Garlic, minced
Pierce potatoes with tines of fork; rub generously with soy sauce.
Bake in 400 degree oven 1 hour until fork tender. Remove from oven;
keep hot. Slicing crosswise, without cutting completely through, cut
5 slits into each potato. Fill each slit with 1/2 teaspoon garlic
mayonnaise. Fill two of slits with 1/2 ounce turkey and tomato. Fill
remaining 3 slits with 1/3 ounce bacon and 1 lettuce leaf. Serves 21.

For Garlic Mayonaise: Mix ingredients; cover and refrigerate.
