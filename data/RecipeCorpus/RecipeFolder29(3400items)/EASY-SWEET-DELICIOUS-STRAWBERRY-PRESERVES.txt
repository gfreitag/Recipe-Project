Title: EASY SWEET DELICIOUS STRAWBERRY PRESERVES
Categories: Fruit, Can/preserv
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  Sugar
2 pt Strawberries, hulled
"Strawberry preserves on hot biscuits....oh my..."

Combine berries and 1 1/2 cups sugar in a heavy saucepan; bring to a
boil, stirring constantly.  Boil, uncovered 10 minutes.  Add
remaining sugar and boil 15 minutes, stirring frequently, until
mixture reaches 221 degrees on a candy thermometer.  Skim foam.
Cool, cover, and let stand at room temperature for 24 hours, stirring
occasionally. Spoon into clean jars and refrigerate.

Yields:  3 half-pints From: "Original Whistle Stop Cafe Cookbook"
