Title: Amazing Coconut Pie
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Coconut
2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Bisquick
4    Eggs
Combine everything but coconut in blender on low for 3 minutes.  Pour
into pie plate, let sit for 5 min.  Top with coconut.  Bake at 350
for 40 minutes.
