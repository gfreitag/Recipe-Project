Title: Sausage-Potato Bake
Categories: Sausage, Potato, Casseroles, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Milk
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb Flour
1/2 ts Thyme
3 md Potatoes,peel, thinly sliced
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1 lb Fullcook smoke sausage,slice
3 oz Pkg cream cheese
Cook onion, potatoes and green pepper in boiling water till tender;
drain. Turn into a 1 1/2 qt. casserole. Add sliced sausage;toss
lightly. Cut up cream cheese. Cook and stir cream cheese and milk
till cream cheese melts. Remove from heat. Stir in sour cream, flour,
thyme, and 1/8 tsp. pepper. Pour over potato mixture; stir. Cover and
refrigerate for 3-24 hours. Before serving. Bake in 350 degree oven
about 50 minutes. Submitted By CAROL VERGE   On   04-13-95
