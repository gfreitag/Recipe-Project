Title: Peanut Butter Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Peanut Butter -- Chunky
1/2 c  Dark Corn Syrup -- OR
Stir the peanut butter and syrup together in a small bowl until well
blended.  Add the milk and then cover.  Refrigerate until serving
time. Serve over fresh fruit and fruit salads.

Makes about 1 1/3 cups.
