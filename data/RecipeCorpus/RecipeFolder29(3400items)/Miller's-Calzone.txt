Title: Miller's Calzone
Categories: Main dish, Italian
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM---------------------------PASTRY--------------------------------
1/3 c  Margarine
1/3 c  Shortening
1 x  Buttermilk, as needed
3 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 1/4 ts Salt
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/4 ts Thyme
1 ea Egg, beaten
1 c  Ham, diced
1 1/2 c  Ricotta cheese
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1 x  Salt & Pepper to taste
PASTRY:  In mixing bowl, measure flour and salt together.  Quarter
margarine and shortening into cubes and add to bowl.  Work the dough
by hand, adding buttermilk a tablespoon at a time until kneaded to a
pliable dough consistency.  Cover and refrigerate.

FILLING:  In mixing bowl, combine all ingredients.  Take dough and
divide it into 6 equal balls.  On floured surface, roll out dough to
1/4" thickness.  Place 1/6 of the filling mixture on dough and fold
dough in half, forming a half-moon shape.  Pinch edges together and
prick dough with a fork.  Repeat until all are filled.  Placed on
greased baking sheet and bake in a 350-degree oven approximately 20
minutes.

Miller's restaurant is located in Charlottesville, VA.
