Title: 106139 Cajun Hush Puppies
Categories: Cajun/creol, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 c  Green onion
1/2 c  Corn
1/2 c  Buttermilk
2    Beaten eggs
2 tb Hot oil
1 c  Corn meal
1/2 c  Plain flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Garlic powder
Heat enough peanut oil for deep frying in skillet to 375 degrees. Mix
all ingredients well and drop by spoonful into hot oil.  Fry until
golden. Remove with slotted spoon to paper towel.  Serve while warm.
Wonderful with fried fish, and coleslaw.
