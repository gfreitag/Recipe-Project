Title: Dole Ambrosia
Categories: Side dish, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
1/2 c  Flaked coconut
1/4 c  Dole chopped almonds toasted
1 ct Vanilla yogurt
(8 oz)
1 cn Dole pineapple chunks
(20 oz)
1 cn Dole mandarin orange segment
(11 oz)
1 ea Dole banana, peeled, sliced
1 1/2 c  Dole seedless grapes
Drain pineapple and mandarin oranges. Combine pineapple, oranges,
banana, grapes, marshmallows, coconut and almonds. fold in yogurt.
Chill

Source: A Dole promotional booklet.
