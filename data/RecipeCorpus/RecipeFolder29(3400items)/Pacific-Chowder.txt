Title: Pacific Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Fish, Potato
Yield: 3 servings
Potato soup
2 c  Milk
6 1/2 oz Tuna
4 sl Bacon
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Chopped green pepper
10 1/2 oz Can condensed cream of
Cook bacon; drain, reserving grease and crumble bacon.  Cook onion and
green pepper in 2 tablespoons of bacon grease just until tender.  Add
soup, milk, and a dash of salt; heat to boiling.  Drain tuna and
break in chunks; stir into soup mixture with half the crumbled bacon.
Heat through. Dash with paprika.  Garnish with remaining bacon.  Heat
through. Dash with paprika.  Garnish with remaining bacon. Randy Rigg
