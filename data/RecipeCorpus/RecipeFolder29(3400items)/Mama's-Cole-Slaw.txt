Title: Mama's Cole Slaw
Categories: Vegetables, Salads, Cajun/creol
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Paprika
ds Cayenne
1 tb Oil
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Honey
5 c  Green cabbage, thinly sliced
1 c  Red cabbage, thinly sliced
2 ea Carrots, shredded
1/2 ea Small onion, minced
1 ea Garlic clove, finely
. shredded
1/3 c  Soy (eggless) mayonnaise
Mix cabbage and carrots, and set aside.  In a separate bowl, mix the
remaining ingredients, add to the cabbage and carrot and mix well.

Per serving:  99 CALORIES, 1g PROTEIN, 12g CARBOHYDRATES, 4g FAT

From "Good Time Eatin' in Cajun Country:  Cajun Vegetarian Cooking" by
Donna Simon (Book Publishing Co., 1995, $9.95), as seen in Cory-Don
Ireland column in THE OLYMPIAN, 4/5/95.
