Title: Berries' Favorite Shortcake
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Butter -- melted
1    Egg
1/2 c  Cream
2 c  All-Purpose Flour
4 ts Baking Powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Sugar
Mix all ingredients together and spread the batter into a greased
8-inch pan. Bake in a preheated 450 F oven for 10 to 15 mins, until
golden brown. This exceptionally rich shortcake is the best base for
your favorite berries.

from Goodies To Go, Lexington, Mass.
