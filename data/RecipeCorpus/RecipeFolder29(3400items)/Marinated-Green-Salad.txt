Title: Marinated Green Salad
Categories: Salads, Marinades, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Oil
Sprinkle of paprika
1 sm Can of peas
1 cn French style green beans
1    Onion, sliced in rings
1 c  Green pepper, slice in rings
1    Jar of pimentos
1 c  Celery
Drain vegetables thoroughly; set aside.  Combine sugar, salt,
vinegar, oil and paprika.  Pour mixture over vegetables and let set
overnight. Drain mixture before serving.  Keep this in the fridge to
keep the veggies crisp. Randy Rigg
