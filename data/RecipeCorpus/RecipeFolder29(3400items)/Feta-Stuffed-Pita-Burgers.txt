Title: Feta Stuffed Pita Burgers
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1/3 c  Finely crumbled feta cheese
1 ts Ouzo liqueur (optional)
1/4 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Ground red pepper
2 lg Pita bread rounds; split
2 c  Shredded spinach
3 tb Milk
2 tb Cornmeal
1 tb Onion; finely chopped
1    Clove garlic; minced
1/4 ts Dried oregano; crushed
1/8 ts Lemon pepper seasoning
1/2 lb Lean ground lamb
In a bowl, combine 2 tablespoons of the milk, cornmeal, onion, garlic,
oregano, lemon pepper, and 1/4 teaspoon salt.  Add meats; mix well.
Shape into eight 1/4" thick patties.  Combine feta, remaining milk,
and ouzo. Place 1 tablespoon feta mixture in centers of 4 patties.
Top with remaining patties; press edges to seal.  Combine cumin and
red pepper; sprinkle onto patties.  Grill burgers, on an uncovered
grill, directly over medium-hot coals for 7 minutes.  Turn and grill
for 6 to 8 minutes more for medium doneness; serve in pitas with
spinach.

Nutritional information per serving:  334 cal., 31 g pro., 22 g
carbo., 13 g fat, 93 mg chol., 384 mg sodium, 500 mg potassium, 2 g
dietary fiber. US RDA: 31% vit. A
