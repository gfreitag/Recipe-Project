Title: 689358 Meatless Lentil Rice Patties
Categories: Vegetarian, Rice
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Snipped parsley
1/4 c  Toasted wheat germ
-Pam non-stick spray
1/3 c  Chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Chopped carrot
3/4 c  Dry lentils
1/4 c  Brown rice
1    Slightly beaten egg
1/2 c  Shredded Swiss cheese
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Fine dry bread crumbs
Cook lentils and rice in 3 1/2 cup water, simmer, covered 45-50
minutes. Drain and mash slightly.  Cool 15 minutes.  Stir in egg,
cheese, onion, crumbs, parsley and 1/2 teaspoon salt.  Shape into 4
patties, 1 inch thick. Coat with wheat germ.  Spray skillet with Pam,
cook patties over medium heat 3 minutes on each side (or bake in 450
degree oven 10 minutes). Cook carrot and pepper in boiling water just
until tender. Serve each pattie topped with cooked vegetables.
