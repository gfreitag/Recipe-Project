Title: Winter Spinach Salad+
Categories: Salads, Low cal/fat
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2 tb Maple syrup
3 tb Balsamic vinegar
3 tb Fresh orange juice
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
-red cabbage
3    Seedless oranges, peeled and
-sectioned
6 c  Trimmed, washed and torn
-fresh spinach leaves
2 c  Thinly shredded radicchio or
To make dressing; Whisk balsamic vinegar, orange juice and maple syrup
together. Set aside.

To make salad; Just before serving, prepare individual serving plates
with 3/4 cup spinach leaves, 1/4 cup shredded radicchio or red
cabbage and 4 to 5 orange sections. Drizzle each salad with a
tablespoon of dressing Submitted By MARY RIEMERMAN   On   04-06-95
