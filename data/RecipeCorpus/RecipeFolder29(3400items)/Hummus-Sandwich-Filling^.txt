Title: Hummus Sandwich Filling^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Low sodium soy sauce
MIXED WITH
1/2 ts Water
1 ds Cayenne
1/4 ts Sesame oil, optional
1/4 c  Sesame seeds, toasted
2 c  Cooked chick peas
3 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Ground cumin
3 cl Garlic, minced
Combine ingredients in food processor and pulse until well chopped.
Process until light and fluffy adding water, 1 tablespoon at a time
if too thick.

Per serving: Calories: 211 Protein: 11g Carbohydrates: 32g Fat: 5g
Sodium: 80mg Cholesterol: 0mg
