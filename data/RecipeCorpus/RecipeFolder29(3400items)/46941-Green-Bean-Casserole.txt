Title: 46941 Green Bean Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Velveeta
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Velveeta cheese
24    Crackers, crushed
1    Bag almonds
3 cn String beans, drained
-Garlic salt
1 ct Sour cream
1 cn Mushroom soup
Put beans in bottom of casserole dish.  Sprinkle with garlic salt.
Layer almonds over beans.  Slice cheese over almonds.  Mix soup and
sour cream. Cover beans with soup mixture and sprinkle with cracker
crumbs moistened in butter. Bake 30 minutes at 375 degrees.
