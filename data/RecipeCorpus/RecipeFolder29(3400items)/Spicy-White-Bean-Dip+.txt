Title: Spicy White Bean Dip+
Categories: Dips/spread, Vegetables, Appetizers, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Cloves garlic
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 tb Plain non-fat yogurt
1 cn (15 oz.) white beans
-drained
1/2 c  Fresh parsley leaves
1/3 c  Fresh cilantro leaves
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground crushed coriander
Place all ingredients in food processor, and process until smooth.
Serve with pretzels or low-fat chips.
