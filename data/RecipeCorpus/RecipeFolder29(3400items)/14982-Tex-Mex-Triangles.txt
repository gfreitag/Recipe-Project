Title: 14982 Tex-Mex Triangles
Categories: Tex/mexican, Snacks, Appetizers
Yield: 30 servings
1 c  (2 1/4 oz.) sliced ripe
-olives, drained
2    Green onions, sliced
1/3 c  Shredded Monterey Jack
-cheese (1 1/3 oz.)
1/3 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
-(1 1/3 oz.)
1 1/2 c  Bisquick baking mix
1/4 c  Cornmeal
1/3 c  Milk
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 ts Chili powder
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 c  Chopped tomatoes
Mix baking mix, cornmeal, milk, oil, chili powder and garlic powder
until moistened; beat vigorously 30 seconds.  Pat dough evenly in
ungreased rectangular pan, 13 x 9 x 2 inches.  Bake 15 minutes or
until edges begin to pull away from sides of pan.  Spread sour cream
over hot crust, distribute tomatoes, ripe olives and onions evenly
over sour cream; sprinkle with cheeses.  Bake 5 minutes. Cut into
about 4 inch squares. Cut squares diagonally into halves. 6 servings.
Appetizer Tex-Mex squares: Cut into about 2 inches squares.
