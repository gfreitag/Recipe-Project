Title: Swiss Porridge
Categories: Breakfast, Mixes
Yield: 8 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
-grated peel of 1 lemon
1 c  Whipping cream
8 oz Yogurt, plain
1 pt Strawberries; fresh
1 cn Pineapple, crushed; 20 oz
1 c  Spiced Oatmeal Mix
2    Bananas; sliced
1/3 c  Nuts; finely chopped
Set aside 8 whole strawberries.  Slice remaining strawberries and set
aside.  In a small bowl, combine Spiced Oatmeal Mix and the juice
from the drained pineapple; set aside. In a medium bowl, combine
pineapple, sliced strawberries, bananas and nuts. Sprinkle with lemon
juice and peel. Toss lightly with a couple of forks.  In a large
bowl, whip cream until stiff peaks form.  Reserve 1 cup of the
whipped cream. Fold yogurt into the remaining whipped cream and then
fold fruit mixture in. Gently stir in oatmeal mixture.  Refrigerate
at least 1 hour. Spoon into 8 parfait glasses or custard cups and
garnish each with 2 tbl of reserved whipped cream and a strawberry.
