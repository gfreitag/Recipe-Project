Title: Sherbet Shake
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Whole milk
1 c  Any flavor sherbet
For a low lactose version, make with lact-aid milk

Blend well.   1 serving, 345 calories, 6 g protein.
