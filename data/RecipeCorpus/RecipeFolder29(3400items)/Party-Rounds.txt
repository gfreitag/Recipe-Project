Title: Party Rounds
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
1 ts Italian seasoning
2/3    Box of the 2 lb. Velveeta
Cheese
2    Loaves party rye bread
1 lb Hot sausage
1 lb Mild sausage
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
Fry sausage; drain off excess fat; add onion and Italian seasoning.
Stir in cut up cheese and let melt.  Mixture will appear very gooey.
Spread mixture on party rye and place on cookie sheet.  Bake at 400
degrees for 10 minutes. Randy Rigg
