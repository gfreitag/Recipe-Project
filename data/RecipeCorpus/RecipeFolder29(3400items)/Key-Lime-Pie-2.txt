Title: Key Lime Pie 2
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
Yellow food coloring (opt)
Green food coloring (opt)
Whipped cream OR
Whipped topping
1    9" baked pastry shell
1    9" graham cracker crust
6    Egg yolks
28 oz Sweetened condensed milk
8 oz Lime juice from concentrate
Preheat oven to 325'F. In large mixer bowl, beat egg yolks with
sweetened condensed milk, lime juice and food coloring if desired.
Pour into prepared pastry shell; bake 40 minutes. Cool. Chill. Top
with whipped cream. Garnish as desired. Refrigerate leftovers.
