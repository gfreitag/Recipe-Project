Title: Quaker's Chocolate Cherry Thumbprints
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 dozen
Uncooked
3/4 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Margarine or butter,softened    1 1/2 c  All-purpose Flour
1 ts Baking Powder
1/4 ts Salt (optional)
2 c  Maraschino Cherries, drained
& patted dry
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Semi-sweet chocolate morsels
(divided)
2 c  Quaker Old Fashioned Oats,
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Beat margarine, sugar, eggs and vanilla
until smooth. Add 1 cup of morsels, melted*; mix well. Stir in oats,
flour, baking powder & salt; mix well. Cover & chill dough for 1
hour. Shape dough into 1 inch balls. Place 2 inches apart on
ungreased cookie sheet. Press deep centers with thumb. Place
maraschino cherry into each center. Bake 10 to 12 minutes until set.
Remove to wire rack; cool ccompletely. Drizzle cookies with remaining
1 cup chocolate morsels, melted.

To melt 1 cup chocolate morsels: Microwave at HIGH 1 to 2 minutes,
stirring every 30 seconds until smooth. Or heat in heavy sauepan over
low heat; stirring until smooth.
