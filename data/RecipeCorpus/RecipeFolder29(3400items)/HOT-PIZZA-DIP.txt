Title: HOT PIZZA DIP
Categories: Dips/spread, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  (8 oz.) Mozzarella cheese
1 c  (4 oz.) Cheddar cheese,
-shredded
1/2 c  Pizza sauce
1/2 c  Green pepper, finely chopped
1/2 c  Sweed red pepper, finely
-chopped
Tortilla chips or
-breadsticks
"I love this recipe because
-it's easy to prepare in
-advance and keep
Refrigerated. Put it in the
-oven when guests arrive.
-It gets gobbled up
Quickly!" - Karen Riordan
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 ts Italian dressing
1/4 ts Garlic powder
In a bowl, combine cream cheese, Italian seasoning and garlic powder;
spread on the bottom of a greased 9" pie plate. Combine cheeses;
sprinkle half over the cream cheese layer. Top with the pizza sauce
and peppers. Sprinkle with the remaining cheeses. Bake at 350 degrees
for 20 minutes. Serve warm with tortilla chips or breadsticks.
Yields: 3 1/2 cups From: "Taste of Home" Magazine, April/May 1995
