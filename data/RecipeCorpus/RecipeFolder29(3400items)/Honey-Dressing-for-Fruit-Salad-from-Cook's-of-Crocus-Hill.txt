Title: Honey Dressing for Fruit Salad from Cook's of Crocus Hill
Categories: Dress/stuff, Fruit
Yield: 2 cups
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Grated onion
3/4 c  Salad oil
1 tb Fresh chopped herbs i.e.
-lemon verbena, lemon balm,
-or mint
1/4 c  To 1/3 cup sugar
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Celery seed
1/3 c  Honey
1/3 c  Vinegar
Measure everything into a blender and blend. Toss over your choice of
fruit. Anice combination would be blueberries, melon balls and
strawberries.

NOTE: Dressing keeps refrigerated but may need to be warmed in bottle
in hot water before using
