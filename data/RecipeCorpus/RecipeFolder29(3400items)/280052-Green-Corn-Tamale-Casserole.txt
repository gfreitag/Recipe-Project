Title: 280052 Green Corn Tamale Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Melted butter
2 ts Sugar
1/2    -1 tsp. salt
1 c  Cheese, grated
1    (4 oz.) can whole green
-chilies
3 c  Fresh corn or frozen
1/3 c  Yellow cornmeal
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Butter baking dish.  Cut chilies in wide
strips.  Combine corn, cornmeal, butter, sugar and salt in blender.
Layer 1/2 cornmeal mixture, chili strips and cheese.  Top with rest
of cornmeal mixture and put cheese on top.  Cover with foil and bake
1 hour at 350 degrees.
