Title: Quaker's Choc-Oat-Chip Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 5 dozen
1 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Salt, (optional)
2 1/2 c  Quaker Old Fashioned Oats,
Uncooked
2 c  Semi-sweet choolate morsels
1 c  Nuts, coarsely chopped -
(optional)
1 c  Margarine/butter softened
1 1/4 c  Brown Sugar, firmly packed
1/2 c  Granulated Sugar
2    Eggs
2 tb Milk
2 ts Vanilla
1 3/4 c  All-purpose Flour
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Beat margarine & sugars until creamy. Add
eggs, milk and vanilla; beat well. Add flour, baking soda 7 & salt;
mix well. Stir in oats, chocolate morsels & nuts; mix well. Drop by
rounded measuring Tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 9
to 10 minutes for a cchewy cookie or 12 to 13 minutes for a ccrispy
cookie. Cool 1 minute on cookie sheet; remove to wire rack. Cool
completely.
