Title: Swiss Steak (Crock Pot)
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Crockpot
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 cn (16oz) whole tomatoes
1/2 c  Grated American cheese
2 lb Round steak 1" thick
1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1    Stalk celery, chopped
2    Carrots, chopped
Cut steak into 4 servings, dredge in flour with salt.  Place in pot.
Add chopped vegetables and Worcestershire sauce.  Pour tomatoes over
meat and vegetables.  Cover and cook on low for 7 to 10 hours.  Just
before serving sprinkle with grated cheese.
