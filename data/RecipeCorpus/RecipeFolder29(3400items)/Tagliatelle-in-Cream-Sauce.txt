Title: Tagliatelle in Cream Sauce
Categories: Vegetarian, Italian, Pasta
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 c  Grated parmesan cheese
Freshly ground pepper
1/8 c  Sliced green pimento
Stuffed olives
250 g  Wholemeal tagliatelle
15 g  Butter
1    Onions
125 g  Creamed cottage cheese
1 tb Cream
Preparation: finely chop the onions

1. Cook the tagliatelle in a large quantity of boiling water until
just cooked.

2. While the pasta is cooking melt the butter over a low heat. Add the
onion and cook for 3 to 4 minutes until the onion is soft. Do not
allow the onion to brown.

3. Stir in the cottage cheese and cream. Cook over a low heat until
the cheese has melted and is heated through.  Add the parmesan
cheese and the pepper.  Stir well to combine the ingredients.

4. Drain the tagliatelle then place in a large bowl and add the sauce.
Toss to combine.

Top with sliced olives and serve.
