Title:  Dutch Apple Bread
Categories: Breads, Fruit, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Buttermilk
1 c  Chopped peeled apple
1/3 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Butter, softened
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 c  Flour
TOPPING:

cup
1/3
ground cinnamon 3 Tbsp. butter

In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar.  Beat in eggs and vanilla.
Combine flour, baking soda and salt; stir into the creamed mixture
alternately with buttermilk.  Fold in apple and nuts.  Pour into a
greased 9x5x3" loaf pan.  For topping, combine the first 4
ingredients; cut in butter until crumbly.  Sprinkle over batter.
Bake at 350 degrees for 55-60 minutes or until bread tests done.
Cool in pan 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack.
