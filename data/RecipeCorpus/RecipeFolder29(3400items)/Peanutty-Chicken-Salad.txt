Title: Peanutty Chicken Salad
Categories: Side dish, Salads, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Curry powder
2 c  Cooked chicken breast, cubed
1/4 c  Spanish peanuts
1 pk Birds Eye frozen green peas
(16 oz)
2/3 c  Plain lowfat yogurt
1/3 c  Nonfat mayonnaise dressing
Run cold water over peas in strainer to thaw completely; drain. Mix
yogurt, mayonnaise, mustard and curry powder in large bowl. Stir in
peas and chicken. Refrigerate at least 1 hour. Stir in peanuts just
before serving.

Source: Back of a Bird Eye's frozen peas package
