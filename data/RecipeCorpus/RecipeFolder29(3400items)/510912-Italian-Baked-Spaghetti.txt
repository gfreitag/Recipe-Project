Title: 510912 Italian Baked Spaghetti
Categories: Italian, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Gunnoes mild Italian
-sausage
1 lg Pkg. shredded Mozzarella
-cheese
8 oz Box Mueller's thin
-spaghetti
1 cn Hunts "traditional"
-spaghetti sauce (tall can)
Peel skin off of sausage and break up into pieces and brown. Drain off
grease.  Mix sausage and spaghetti sauce and set aside.

Cook spaghetti noodles 2 minutes less than package directions say then
drain water off of spaghetti.  Pour noodles into sauce/sausage
mixture and add 1 cup of the Mozzarella cheese.  Stir well and pour
into a buttered 9"x13" baking dish.  Spread rest of Mozzarella cheese
on top and bake for 20 to 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
