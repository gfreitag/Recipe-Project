Title: Shortbread Triangles
Categories: Kids, Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 wedges
2 c  All-purpose flour;
1 c  Butter; softened
1/2 c  Powdered sugar;
(1).  Mix butter and powdered sugar in a larger bowl until smooth and
creamy.  (2).  Add flour.  Stir until well-mixed.  (3).  Spread dough
evenly in an ungreased pie pan. (4).  Bake in preheated, 350-degree
oven for 40-45 minutes.  (5).  When cool, cut into wedges like a pie.
Makes about 8 Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Food Section, Apr.
