Title: Chicken Orange Salad^
Categories: Healthy, Salads
Yield: 2 servings
3 cl Garlic, minced
1 ts Red pepper flakes
6 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/2 lb Cooked chicken
1 oz Dried shittake mushrooms,
Soaked 20 minutes
4    Navel oranges, sectioned
4    Green onions, minced
Slice chicken into strips and combine with remaining ingredients.

Per serving: Calories: 379 Protein: 40g Carbohydrates: 50g Fat: 4.5g
Sodium: 81mg Cholesterol: 70mg
