Title: Lemonade Grilled Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Bbq/grill
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/4 ts Garlic salt
4 lb Chicken portions
6 oz Frozen lemonade concentrate
1/3 c  Soy sauce
1 ts Salt
Thaw lemonade; add soy sauce and seasonings.  Blend well.  Dip
chicken in mixture.  Grill about 5 inches from hot coals; turn often;
basting with mixture until chicken is tender and glazed;
approximately 45 to 50 minutes.
