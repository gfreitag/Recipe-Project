Title: Cucumber Herb Dressing^
Categories: Salads, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 12 servings
2    Green onions, cut in 1"
Pieces
1 1/2 ts White wine vinegar
1/2 c  Lowfat yogurt
1/4 ts Dijon mustard, no salt
Added
1/2 c  Parsley
1 tb Fresh dill, minced
1 ts Fresh tarragon, minced
2 tb Apple juice concentrate
1 md Cucumber, peeled, seeded and
Chopped
1 cl Garlic, minced
Combine all ingredients except yogurt and mustard in blender. Blend
until smooth, stir in yogurt and mustard. Store in refrigerator.

Per tablespoon serving: Calories: 11 Protein: 0.5g Carbohydrates: 1g
Fat: trace Sodium: 6mg Cholesterol: trace
