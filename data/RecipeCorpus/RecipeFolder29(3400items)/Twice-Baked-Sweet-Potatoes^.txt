Title: Twice Baked Sweet Potatoes^
Categories: Healthy, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  (to 1/2) c skim milk
Paprika
Parsley
2 lg Sweet potatoes
1    Egg white
1/2 c  Drained crushed pineapple,
Juice reserved
Scrub potatoes. Score 1/4" deep all the way around and prick with a
fork. Bake until tender, about 45-60 minutes. Cool slightly then cut
in half at score marks. Combine with egg white, 1/4 c reserved
pineapple juice and enough milk to make a good stuffing consistency.
Stir in pineapple; stuff into shells nad sprinkle with paprika. Bake
at 400F until hot, about 15 minutes. Sprinkle with parsley.

Per serving: Calories: 100 Protein: 3g Carbohydrates: 22g Fat: 0g
Sodium: 44mg Cholesterol: 1mg
