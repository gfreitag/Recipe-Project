Title: Chocolate Mint Shake
Categories: Low cal/fat, Shake
Yield: 1 servings
4 oz Whole milk
1/2 ts Peppermint extract
1 c  Chocolate ice cream
1 pk Chocolate instant breakfast
-drink
A cool and minty combination.

Mix in blender until smooth. Makes 1 serving, 500 calories, 16 g
protein.
