Title: Broiled Icing
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 8x8 cake
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 c  Shredded coconut or nutmeats
3 tb Melted butter
2/3 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Cream
Combine the ingredients and spread over a warm, freshly baked cake.
Place the cake under the broiler until the icing bubbles but watch it
doesn't burn.
