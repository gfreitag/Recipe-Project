Title: Summer Casserole
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 10 servings
4 md Yellow squash;or zucchini,sl
1/2 lb Mozzarella cheese; thinly
-sliced
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1 lb Ground beef
1 lb Italian sausage; mild
1    Onion; chopped
1    Green pepper; in strips
1 qt Spaghetti sauce
Brown meats in heavy skillet.  Add onion and pepper and saute until
tender. Drain off and discard excess liquid.  Add spaghetti sauce and
simmer for 1 1/2 hours.  Put half of the squash or zucchini into 13 x
9 pan. Cover with half of the sauce and meat mixture, then with half
of the mozzarella cheese.  Make second layer, using remaining
ingredients. Top with parmesan cheese and bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
