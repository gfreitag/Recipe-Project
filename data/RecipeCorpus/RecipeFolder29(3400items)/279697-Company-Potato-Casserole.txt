Title: 279697 Company Potato Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1/2    Bunch green onions with tops
1 1/2 c  Shredded American cheese
-Corn flakes
6    To 9 potatoes, boiled &
-diced
1 pt Sour cream
1/4 c  Butter
Heat butter and soup.  Add sour cream, onion and cheese.  Combine with
potatoes. Bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees.  Cover with 2 tablespoons
melted butter and corn flakes.
