Title: Pork Chop Potato Bake
Categories: Meats
Yield: 3 servings
Mix
1 c  Hot water
1 cn Mushroom stems and
-pieces, drained
Margarine
6 ea Pork chops, fat removed
Oil for browning chops
4 md (to 6) potatoes, peeled,
-cut in 1/4" slices
1    Envelope Lipton onion soup
Preheat oven to 350~.

Grease a glass roasting dish with butter; set aside. Dissolve onion
soup in the hot water; set aside. Brown chops in a little oil in
skillet. Place potato slices one at a time in the greased dish,
layering as needed. Place browned chops on top of potato slices. Pour
dissolved soup over chops.

Spread mushrooms over top. Turn chops over. Cover and bake for 30 to
40 minutes or until chops are done and potatoes tender.

Note:  You can adjust number of chops and potatoes for the size of
your family. Just make sure to occasionally check to make sure all
the liquid is not cooked away.
