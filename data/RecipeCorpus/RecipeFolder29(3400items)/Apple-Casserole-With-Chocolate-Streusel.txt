Title: Apple Casserole With Chocolate Streusel
Categories: Chocolate, Healthy
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM---------------------CHOCOLATE STREUSEL--------------------------
-- powder
1 ts Cinnamon
3 tb Margarine, diet
Non-stick cooking spray
6 tb Fructose
6 tb Flour
1 tb Unsweetened dutch cocoa
MMMMM------------------------APPLE LAYER-----------------------------
6 tb Fructose
Cinnamon, several large pinc
6    Apples, sweet, peeled, cored
-- cut in thick slices
Recipe by: Skinny Chocolate - ISBN 0-940625-80-6

Heat oven to 350 F and place rack in center. Spray a 10 x 8-inch
shallow ovenproof casserole or a 9 x 9-inch baking pan with non-stick
spray.

STREUSEL: Combine fructose, flour, cocoa, and cinnamon in bowl. Add
margarine and use fingertips to lightly knead all ingredients together
until mixture forms large clumps. Refrigerate streusel.

APPLE LAYER: Place half of apple slices on bottom of casserole.
Sprinkle with 1 Tbs fructose. Sprinkle several large pinches of
cinnamon over apples. Add remaining apple slices to casserole and
sprinkle with remaining fructose and cinnamon. Bake 30 mins and
remove from oven.

Strew streusel over apple casserole. Return to oven for 10 mins. Serve
immediately.
