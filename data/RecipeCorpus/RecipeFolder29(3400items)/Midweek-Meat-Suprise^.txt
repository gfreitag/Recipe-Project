Title: Midweek Meat Suprise^
Categories: Healthy, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
ds Pepper
1/4 ts Ground thyme
1 cn (8oz) no salt added tomato
Sauce
1 c  Water
2/3 c  Uncooked rice
1 lb Lean flank steak, ground or
Very lean ground beef
1/2    Onion, chopped
2 tb Beef stock
1/3 c  Chopped red bell pepper
1/3 c  Chopped green bell pepper
Saute meat in a nonstick skillet until browned, drain off fat and
place meat in a bowl. Saute onion in stock or water until soft. Add
remaining ingredients except meat. Cover and simmer until rice is
tender, about 35 minutes.

Per serving: Calories: 171 Protein: 18g Carbohydrates: 18g Fat: 3g
Sodium: 58mg Cholesterol: 38mg
