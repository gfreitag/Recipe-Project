Title: Play Clay #1
Categories: Kids
Yield: 1 batch
Poster Paints
Shellac or clear nail polish
Saucepan
Board
1 c  Baking soda
1/2 c  Corn Starch
2/3 c  Warm Water
Food coloring or
Mix baking soda and cornstarch in saucepan. Add water and stir until
smooth. Over medium heat, boil and stir until like mashed potatoes.
Pour onto board to cool. Knead when cool. For color, knead food
coloring into clay until blended or paint with poster paints when
dry. For a glossy finish when dry brush with shellac or clear nail
polish. (This process should be done only by much older children!)

Typed & tested by Anne Marie Chiappetta. The Chiappetta Family
Learning Center

Note this clay (when not colored) dries pure white. It also hardens
very quickly! This recipe make 1 1/2 cups of clay and doubles well.
It ccan also be stored in an airtight container for several weeks.
