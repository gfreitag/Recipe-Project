Title: Corn & Cabbage Slaw
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 ts Dried mustard (2ts max)
1/2 ts Sugar
1 tb Chopped chives
1/4 c  Italian minced parsley
2 c  Shredded cabbage
1 1/2 c  Corn
1/4 c  Vegatable oil
3 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Salt
In a large bowl, combine the cabbage and corn. ( Frozen or drained
canned corn are good choices). In a smaller bowl, whisk together the
oil, lemon juice, salt, mustard and sugar. Correct the seasoning. Add
to the shredded cabbage, mix well and let sit in the refrigerator for
at least 1 hour. Add the chives and parsley just before serving.

Source: The Times-Colonist May 4, 1994
