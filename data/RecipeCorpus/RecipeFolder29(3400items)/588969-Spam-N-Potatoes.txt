Title: 588969 Spam-N-Potatoes
Categories: Spam, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk
1    Brick cheddar cheese
3 lb Potatoes
2 cn Spam
6 tb Butter
Boil and cube potatoes.  Cube Spam.  Layer in a greased 2 quart
casserole dish.  Melt butter in saucepan, stir in flour until brown
and frothy. Add milk, stirring constantly until mixture thickens.
Blend in cheese until melted.  Pour over Spam-n-potatoes and warm in
microwave or oven. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   04-01-95
