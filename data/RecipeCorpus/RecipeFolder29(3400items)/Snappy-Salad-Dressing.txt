Title: Snappy Salad Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
Pieces
1 ds Chili Powder
1 c  French Salad Dressing
1/2 c  Dairy Sour Cream
1 md Green Pepper -- Cut Into
Put all of the ingredients into a blender container and blend until
the green pepper is chopped.  Chill before serving.

Makes 1 3/4 cups.
