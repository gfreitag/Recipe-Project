Title: Xnipec
Categories: Chilies, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
1 ea Purple onion, chopped
1/3 c  Lime juice
3 tb Fresh cilantro, chopped
2 ea Habeneros, seeds, and stems
; removed and chopped
2 md Tomatoes chopped
Combine he chiles, tomatoes, onions, and lime juice in a bowl and mix
well. Allow to sit for a couple of hours to blend the flavors. Just
before serving, stir in the cilantro.

Dave DeWitt and Nancy Gerlach. "The Habanero Cookbook".
