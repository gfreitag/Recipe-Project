Title: Kool-Aid Playdough
Categories: Kids, Question?
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Boiling water
3 tb Vegetable oil
2 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Salt
2 pk Unsweetened Kool-aid
Mix dry ingredients.  Add water and oil, stir.  Mix or knead with
hands when cool.  Store in Ziploc bag or container with lid.  Lasts
for several months.
