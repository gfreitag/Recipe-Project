Title: Squaw Bread *M*
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Salt
===LIQUIFY IN BLENDER===
1 1/4 c  Warm water
2 tb Raisins
2 3/4 tb Oil
2 tb Brown sugar
2 tb Honey
===FOR 11/2 LB. LOAF===
1 pk Yeast
1 c  Rye flour
1 tb Brown sugar
1/4 c  Instant nonfat dry milk
2 c  Bread flour
Powder
1 c  Whole wheat flour
Combine water, oil, honey, raisins, and brown sugar and blend till

liquid. Add dry ingredients in the order listed, pour in liquid,

select white bread and push start.
From Loafing It by DAK
