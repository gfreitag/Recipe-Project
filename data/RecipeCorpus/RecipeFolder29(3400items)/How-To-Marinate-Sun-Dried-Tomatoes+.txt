Title: How To Marinate Sun-Dried Tomatoes+
Categories: Vegetables, Marinades
Yield: 1 recipe
2 lg Garlic cloves, sliced
4 lg Leaves basil or
-fresh thyme sprigs
15    Sun-Dried tomatoes
1/4 c  Extra Virgin Olive Oil
-or enough to cover tomatoes
1. Pour boiling water to cover over the sun-dried tomatoes and cook
for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat, cover and let stand for 2 to 3
minutes; drain.

2. Place rehydrated tomatoes in a bowl and layer with remaining
ingredients, then pour olive oil over all.

3. cover and let marinate at room temperature for at least an hour, or
refrigerate for up to 5 days.

One suggestion for serving: Spread thinly sliced baguettes, liberally
with either chevre or cream cheese. Top with a julienne of marinated
sun-dried tomatoes,(above), and a bit of chopped basil.
