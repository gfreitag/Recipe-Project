Title: Texas Pork Roast
Categories: Meats, Tex/mexican
Yield: 14 servings
2 tb Lemon juice
ds Of tabasco sauce
1 c  Melted cinnamon-flavored
Or plain apple jelly
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 sm Leg of pork
1 ts Salt
Pepper to taste
1/8 ts Allspice
1 ts Chili powder
1 1/4 c  Chili sauce
Place pork in oven and sprinkle with mixture of salt, pepper,
allspice and chili powder.  Combine remaining ingredients, and spread
evenly on pork. Roast at 350 for 30 min per pound.  Baste frequently
with well seasoned drippings in the pan.  Serves 14-16.
