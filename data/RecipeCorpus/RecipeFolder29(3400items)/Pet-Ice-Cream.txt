Title: Pet Ice Cream
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 batch
1    Envelope Kool-aid
1 2/3 c  PET evaporated milk
1/3 c  Sugar
Chill evaporated milk in ice tray until almost frozen at edges.  Put
ice-cold milk into col small bowl of electric mixer.  Using cold
beaters, whip with electric mixer at high speed until fluffy.  Add
sugar and Kool-aid.  Whip until stiff.  Freeze in a 1 quart ice tray
until firm, 3 to 4 hours.  Serve.  From Family Circle Magazine, Aug
'63. Submitted By TERRI WOLTMON   On   04-22-95
