Title: Chock-Full-of-Chicken Soup
Categories: Kids, Poultry, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 grandkids*
1 c  Cooked chicken, cut up
1    Carrot; thinly sliced
2 cn (10-oz) chicken broth;
2 c  Uncooked noodles;
*Or two granddaughters and two grandparents. WHAT TO DO: (1). Pour
chicken broth in a large pot.  Bring to a boil over medium high heat.
(2). Add remaining ingredients.  (3).  Cook 15 minutes or until
noodles are soft. Serves 4. Source: The San Diego Union-Tribune, Food
