Title: Sweet & Sour Chicken Wings ^
Categories: Poultry, Appetizers
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
24    Whole chicken wings
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Preheat oven to 350. Season wings with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
Place in shallow baking pan and bake for 15 minutes. In small
saucepan, combine ketchup, vinegar, sugar and water. Heat, stirring,
until well blended. Brush sauce over wings and continue to bake
andother 20-30 minutes, or until wings are tender. Serve.
