Title: Calgary Stampede Ribs And Steak Tails - Don Copthorne
Categories: Countyfairs, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Chili Powder
Freshly Ground Pepper
To taste
2 1/2 lb Short Ribs, Boneless
Or Steak Tails
1 tb Vegetable Oil
1 lg Onion, chopped
1 cn (10 Oz) Tomato Soup
1/3 c  Cider Vinegar
*Heat oven to 325 F.

1. Brown the meat in the oil over moderately high heat, turning often
with tongs. Arrange in a single layer in a baking pan.

2. Mix remaining ingredients together and pour over the meat. Cover
the pan with a lid or foil. Bake for 3 hrs, or until very tender.

From the CALGARY EXHIBITION & STAMPEDE of Calgary, Alberta, Canada.
Fair Dates: Early July, for 10 days.
