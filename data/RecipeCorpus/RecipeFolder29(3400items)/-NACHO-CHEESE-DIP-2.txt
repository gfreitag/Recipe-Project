Title:  NACHO CHEESE DIP 2
Categories: Tex/mexican, Dips/spread, Tasteofhome, Sausage
Yield: 3 cups
1 lb Process American cheese,
-cubed
3/4 c  Salsa
1/4 lb Bulk spicy pork or Mexican-
Style sausage
2 tb Chopped green pepper
2 tb Chopped onion
Tortilla chips or raw vegetables

In a 1 1/2 qt. microwave-safe container, cook sausage, green pepper
and onion on high for 2-3 minutes or until sausage is fully cooked;
drain.  Add the cheese and salsa. Cover and microwave on high for 2-3
minutes, stirring frequently until cheese is melted and mixture is
smooth. Serve with tortilla chips or vegetables.

Yields:  3 cups From: "Taste of Home" Magazine, April/May 1995
