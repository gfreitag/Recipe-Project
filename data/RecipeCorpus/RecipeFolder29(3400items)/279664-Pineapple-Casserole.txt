Title: 279664 Pineapple Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Flour (sifted with sugar)
1    Stick butter or margarine
1 pk Ritz crackers
2 lg Cans pineapple tidbits,
-drained
1 c  Sugar
Mix with pineapple in casserole dish.  Melt butter.  Mix with Ritz
crackers.  Spread on top of pineapple mixture and bake at 350 degrees
for 30 minutes.
