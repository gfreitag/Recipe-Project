Title: Snap Beans and Fresh Herbs
Categories: Vegetables, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Olive oil
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/2    Lemon
Salt and pepper to taste
1 lb Fresh yellow/green beans
2    Scallions; finely chopped
2 tb Fresh mixed herbs:
-sage, tarragon, thyme,
-marjoram or basil
Trim beans and cut into 2" pieces. Steam them with a little water in a
covered saucepan about 5 min until cooked but still a little crisp.
Drain and toss with the seasonings. Serve hot or cold as a salad.
