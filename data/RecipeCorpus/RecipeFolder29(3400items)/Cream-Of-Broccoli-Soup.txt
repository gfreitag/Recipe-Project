Title: Cream Of Broccoli Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Half and half
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
1    Head broccoli
-- trimmed & chopped
1/4 c  Unsalted butter
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
2 tb Flour
2    Cans chicken broth
-- (10 3/4-oz cans)
1/2 c  Heavy cream
Recipe by: Produce Pete's "Farmacopeia" - ISBN 0-688-12847-5

In a large soup pot, melt the butter over moderate heat. Add the
onions and saute until translucent. Sprinkle in the flour and stir
until the mixture has thickened. Add the chicken broth, heavy cream,
half & half, salt, pepper, and broccoli. Gently stir together. Cover
and bring to a boil, stirring occasionally. Turn heat to low and
simmer for an additional 15 mins. Serve.
