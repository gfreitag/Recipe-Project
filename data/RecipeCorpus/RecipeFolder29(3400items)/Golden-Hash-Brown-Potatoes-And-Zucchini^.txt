Title: Golden Hash Brown Potatoes And Zucchini^
Categories: Healthy, Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
OR
1/3 c  Egg Beaters
1/3 c  Matzo meal
4 ts Low sod. soy sauce**
2 lg Potatoes, peeled and
Shredded
1 md Zucchini, shredded
1 md Onion, grated
2    Egg whites
Drain vegetables very well. Combine with remaining ingredients. Cook
in a skillet sprayed with Pam until brown and crusty.

Per serving: Calories: 66 Protein: 4g Carbohydrates: 9g Fat: trace
Sodium: 217mg** Cholesterol: 0mg
