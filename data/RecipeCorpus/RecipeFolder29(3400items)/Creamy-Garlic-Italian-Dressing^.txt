Title: Creamy Garlic Italian Dressing^
Categories: Salads, Healthy, Low cal/fat
Yield: 16 servings
1 ts Tamari
1 ts Cider or wine vinegar
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Grated Parmesan
1 ts Minced fresh parsley,
Generous
1/2 c  Lowfat buttermilk
1/2 c  Lowfat cottage cheese
1 cl Garlic
2    Water packed artichoke
Hearts, rinsed
1/4 sm Onion
2 ts Lemon juice
Blend all ingredients except parsley in blender until smooth. Add
parsley. Store in refrigerator up to three days.

Per tablespoon serving: Calories: 8 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 1g Fat:
trace Sodium: 32mg Cholesterol: trace
