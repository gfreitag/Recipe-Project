Title:  STUFFED PEPPERS
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Fine dry breadcrumbs or
-grated
Cheddar cheese
6 lg To 8 lg green peppers
Salt and pepper
3/4 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion, peeled and chopped
1 1/2 c  Cooked rice
14 1/2 oz Can stewed tomatoes, well
-drained
Slice off tops of green peppers; scrape out membranes and seeds.  Cook
peppers in boiling salted water to cover for 3 to 5 minutes.  Drain
well and rinse under cold water to stop cooking. Pat dry inside and
out with dry towels; sprinkle inside with salt and pepper.

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cook ground beef and onion in a skillet
until browned, stirring to crumble meat.  Drain in a colander.
Transfer to a bowl and stir in next 5 ingredients. Stuff pepper cases
with mixture and place in a baking dish. Sprinkle tops with
breadcrumbs or cheese; bake for 25 minutes.
