Title: 879751 Mom's Spicy Corn Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
-Diced jalapeno peppers
2 cn White shoe peg corn
8 oz Cream cheese
This recipe sounds and looks awful, but tastes wonderful.  Try it
when you don't have time to make the dish you had planned.  It's
quick, but is best when refrigerated overnight.  Drain corn.  Mix
with softened cream cheese. Add jalapenos to taste.
