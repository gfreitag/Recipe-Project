Title: Chocolate 7 Minute Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
2 oz Unsweetened chocolate,
-melted and cooled
1 ts Vanilla
2    Egg whites
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Water
1/4 ts Cream of tartar
Combine all ingredients except for the chocolate in top of double
boiler. Beat with electric mixer at high speed for 1 minutes.  Place
over simmering water.  Cook 7 minutes, beating constantly at high
speed, until soft glossy peaks form.  Remove from hot water.  Beat in
1 tsp vanilla. Fold in 2 oz unsweetened chocolate.

Origin: Farm Journal's Choice Chocolate Recipes. Shared by: Sharon
