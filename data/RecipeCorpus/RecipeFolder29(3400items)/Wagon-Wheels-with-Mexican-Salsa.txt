Title: Wagon Wheels with Mexican Salsa
Categories: Rice, Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 2 people
1 tb Chili peppers;canned chopped
1    Lime; juiced
1/2 ts Cumin; ground
4 dr Hot-pepper sauce;
6 oz Wagon wheels pasta; cooked
1/2 md Tomato; chopped
1/2 md Avocado; diced
1/2 cn (19-oz) black beans; rinsed
-drained
1/4 c  Corn; cooked
1 tb Cilantro; chopped
In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, avocados, beans, corn,
cilantro and peppers.  Stir in the lime juice, cumin and hot-pepper
sauce; set aside for 1 hour.  Place the pasta in a serving bowl.  Add
the salsa mixture; toss to mix well.  Servings: 2 Per serving: cal:
246; fat: 4.7g; chol: 0mg; sod: 4.8g; Source: Prevention's Guide
