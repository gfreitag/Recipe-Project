Title: Pilaf
Categories: Nuts/grains
Yield: 2 + cups
1 cn Campbell's Beef Broth
1/3 c  Water
1/2 c  Raw long grain Rice
1/2 c  Fine Noodles, broken in
-pieces
2 tb Butter or Margarine
In saucepan, brown noodles in butter; stir often.  Add remaining
ingredients.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat.  Cover; simmer 20-25
minutes, or until liquid is absorbed.

Makes about 2 1/2 cups.
