Title: Honeyed Nahit
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Chickpeas
2 ts Salt
2 tb Melted fat or butter
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Honey
Wash the chickpeas and soak overnight in water to cover. Drain.
Cover with fresh water; bring to a boil and cook over medium heat 1
1/2 hours. Drain Mix the chickpeas, salt, fat or butter, 1/2 cup
water and the honey in a baking dish.    Bake in a 350 degree oven 30
minutes. Serves 6. Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by
Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc. (c) 1958   Recipe
