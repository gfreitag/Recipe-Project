Title: Cream Of Sweet Potato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
3 md Sweet potatoes
2 c  Vegetable broth
pn Nutmeg
pn Ground cloves
1 1/2 c  Soy milk -- or 1% milk
Salt -- to taste
Peel and slice sweet potatoes. Place potatoes in dutch oven with
broth. Bring to a boil, cover and reduce heat.  simmer until potatoes
are tender, about 20 minutes.

Place half of potatoe slices and half of broth in a
blender/processor. add nutmeg, cloves, milk and salt.  Puree. Repeat
with remaining potatoes and broth.

Return to dutch oven, stir to blend. and reheat over low heat. Serve
hot or chill and serve cold.  Superb!

Per serving:  84 calories;  3 g protein;  154 mg sod;  15 g carbo; 0
mg chol;  46 mg calcium
