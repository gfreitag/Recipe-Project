Title: Blue Cheese Butter
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats, Potatoes, Cheese/eggs, Dips/spread
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Butter or margarine;softened
1/2 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Crumbled Blue cheese
To softened butter or margarine add lemon juice, worcestershire, and
pepper.  Mix thoroughly.  Fold in blue cheese and mix just till
combined. Serve with meat or baked potatoes.  Makes 1/4 cup. Source:
BH&G Summertime Cooking. Typos by Elizabeth Wood.
