Title: Baked Ziti With Three Cheeses
Categories: Main dish, Pasta
Yield: 3 servings
6 oz Ziti
1    Clove garlic -- minced
15 oz Ricotta cheese
1/4 ts Black pepper
8 oz Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
pn Ground nutmeg
1    Egg -- lightly beaten
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
Cook pasta following package directions; drain. Preheat oven to 350F.
Grease 2-quart casserole.  In lg bowl, blend ricotta, 1 cup
mozzarella, egg, parsley, garlic, pepper & nutmeg; stir in ziti.
Spoon into prepared casserole & sprinkle with remaining mozzarella &
Parmesan. Bake until top is golden, about 30 min. Makes 8 ser
