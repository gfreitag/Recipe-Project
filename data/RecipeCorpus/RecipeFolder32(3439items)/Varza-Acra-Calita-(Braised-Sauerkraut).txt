Title: Varza Acra Calita (Braised Sauerkraut)
Categories: Vegetables, Romanian
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Bacon, cut in small pieces
1 c  Onion, minced
1 cn No.3 can sauerkraut
1 c  Water
Fry the bacon, add onion and brown slightly. Squeeze excess water
from sauerkraut and add to bacon & onion. Add water and let simmer
until all water has evaporated. Be sure not to let it scorch pan.
Serve hot. Submitted By MARK MIKLAS   On   07-14-95
