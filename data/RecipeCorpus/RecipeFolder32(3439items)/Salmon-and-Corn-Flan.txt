Title: Salmon and Corn Flan
Categories: Fish, Vegetables, Cake/cookie, Pie/tart
Yield: 2 servings
Pie crust
2 tb Margarine
1/4 c  Flour
1 1/4 c  Milk
1 md Sized can kernel corn
1 md Can salmon
Finely grated rind of 1/2
-lemon
Salt and pepper
1/2 c  Grated cheddar cheese
Melt margarine, stir in flour, cook gently for 2 minutes, remove from
heat. Add milk gradually, beating well.  Bring to boil, cook until
thick, stirring continuously. Remove from the heat, stir in drained
corn, flaked salmon, lemon rind, and seasonings, and pour into flan
case. Sprinkle with cheese, return to oven or place under hot broiler
to brown top. Origin: Marguerite Patten's recipe cards Shared by:
Sharon Stevens
