Title: Cheesy Chili Dip 2
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 servings
15 oz Chili no beans
1 lb Velveeta -- cubed
Green onion -- chopped
Combine chili and cheese in saucepan.  Heat until cheese melts,
stirring frequently.  Serve hot with chips or vegetable dippers.
