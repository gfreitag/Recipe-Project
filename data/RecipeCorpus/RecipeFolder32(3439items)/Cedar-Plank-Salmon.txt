Title: Cedar Plank Salmon
Categories: Bbq/grill, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
1    Salmon fillet about 1 1/2
-lbs
1/4 c  Olive Oil
1    Lemon or orange, juice and
-zest
1 tb Basil, chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Black pepper, freshly
-ground
Description:

Baked on a cedar plank, this original recipe is steeped in flavour and
aroma.  The plank should be a little longer and wider than the fillet.

Cooking Time:
Preparation Time: 2 hrs.

Instructions:

Barbecue Method:

Marinate the salmon in the remaining ingredients.  Meanwhile soak a
piece of untreated cedar plank in cold water for about 2 hours
(weighting it with something heavy).  Place salmon on plank.  Put the
plank directly on the barbecue grill.  Close the lid and cook over
medium heat for 20 minutes.

Oven Method:

Soak the plank as above, the place in a 450F heated oven for 5-10
minutes. Remove the slamon from the mainade and bake on the plank
until cooked, allowing about 10 minutes per inch thickness of fish.
