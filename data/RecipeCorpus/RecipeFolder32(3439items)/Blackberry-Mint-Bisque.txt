Title: Blackberry Mint Bisque
Categories: Beverages, Fruit, Healthy
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Blackberries
1/2 c  Raspberry lemonade
1 1/2 c  Plain nonfat or soy yogurt
x  Fresh mint; chopped
PREP TIME:  5 minutes

1.  Puree blackberries and lemonade in a food processor or blender.

2.  Pour fruit mixture into a medium saucepan and heat on low for
about 5 minutes to blend flavors.  Remove soup from burner and whisk
in yogurt. Remove soup from burner and whisk in yogurt.  Chill if
desired. Serve topped with fresh mint.

PER SERVING:  172 calories; 1g fat; 5% calories from fat; 0mg
cholesterol; 6.6g fiber.

Source:  Karen Greene "Fast!  Fresh!  Fruity!" (New Hope Press) as
seen in "Delicious!  Your Magazine of Natural Living," July 1995.
