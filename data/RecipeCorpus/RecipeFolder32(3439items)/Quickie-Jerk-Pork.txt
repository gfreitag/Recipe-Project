Title: Quickie Jerk Pork
Categories: Meats, Guestchefs
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Vinegar
Chopped hot pepper -- to
Taste
Chopped onion -- to taste
Chopped garlic -- to taste
Red pepper flakes -- to
Taste
Salt -- to taste
1    Boned leg of pork -- cut
Into bite-size p
COMBINE ALL THE INGREDIENTS except the pork in a non-reactive pan.
Add the pork and marinate for 4 days, turning frequently. Keep
covered in the refrigerator. Take out and wipe dry. Cook pork on a
grid over burning coals to get a smokey flavor.
