Title: Tzibbele Kugel
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
6    Egg yolks
3 c  Minced onions
1/3 c  Cracker or matzo meal
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4 tb Melted fat or butter
6    Egg whites -- stiffly
Beaten
Beat the egg yolks until thick.  Stir in the onions, meal, salt,
pepper and fat or butter.  Fold in the egg whites.  Turn into a
greased 2-quart casserole.    Bake in a 350 degree oven for 40
minutes or until set. Serves 6 to 8.    Recipe Source: THE ART
