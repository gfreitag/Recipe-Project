Title: Pickled Garlic
Categories: Pickles, Can/preserv, Herbs/spice
Yield: 2 half pints
1/2 lb Garlic cloves
1 lg Red pepper, seeded and
Slivered
2 c  White vinegar
2/3 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 ts Mustard seed
1/2 ts Celery seed
Peel garlic. Leave small cloves whole. Cut large cloves in half.
Combine with red pepper.
Measure vinegar and sugar into saucepan. PLace mustard seed and
celery seed into cotton bag. Tie. Add to saucepan. Heat on
medium-high, stirring frequently, until it boils. Boil 5 minutes. Add
garlic and red pepper. return to a boil. Boil 5 minutes more. Discard
spice bag. Fill hot sterilized jars with garlic and red peppers to
within 1 inch of top. Fill with hot brine to 1/4-inch of top. Seal.
Let stand several weeks before serving.

Make 2 half pints.
Source: Company's Coming by Jean Pare
