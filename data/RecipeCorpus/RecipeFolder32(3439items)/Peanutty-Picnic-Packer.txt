Title: Peanutty Picnic Packer
Categories: Kids, Snacks
Yield: 24 servings
1    Recipe
Rice Krispies* Treats
Prepare Rice Krispies Treats recipe, adding 1/4 cup peanut butter to
melted marshmallows just before adding cereal.  Using, buttered
spatula, press mixture into pan.  190 calories and 6 grams fat.
