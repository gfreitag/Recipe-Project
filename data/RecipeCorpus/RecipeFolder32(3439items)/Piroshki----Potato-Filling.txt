Title: Piroshki -  Potato Filling
Categories: Jewish, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Minced onions
4 tb Chicken fat or butter
2 c  Mashed potatoes
1    Egg yolk
1 ts Salt
1 ds Cayenne pepper
Lightly brown the onions in the fat or butter.  Stir in the potatoes,
egg yolk, salt and cayenne pepper.  Cool 10 minutes.  Place filling
on dough.
