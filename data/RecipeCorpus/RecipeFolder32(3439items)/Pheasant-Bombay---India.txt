Title: Pheasant Bombay - India
Categories: Nuts/grains, Curries, Game, Indian, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1    Pheasant-disjointed
1/4 c  Flour
4 tb Butter
1/3 c  Onions-chopped
1/3 c  Green peppers-diced
Toasted almonds
1    Clove garlic-minced
1 1/2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Thyme
2 c  Stewed tomatoes
From : Sallie Krebs, Thu 06 Jul 95 04:00, Area: COOKING

Dredge pheasant in seasoned flour, heat butter and brown pheasant on
all sides. Remove pheasant; add the onions, pepper, garlic, curry
powder and thyme. Add tomatoes and liquid from can. Return the
pheasant, cover and cook until tender. Serve with the almonds.
