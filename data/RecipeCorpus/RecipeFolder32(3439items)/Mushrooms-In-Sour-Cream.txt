Title: Mushrooms In Sour Cream
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
3/4 c  Diced mushrooms
4 tb Butter
1 1/2 lb Mushrooms -- sliced
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Paprika
1 c  Sour cream
Cook the onions in half the butter for 10 minutes. Remove onions and
reserve.  Add the remaining butter and the mushrooms to the same pan
and cook over low heat 10 minutes. Return onions to the pan and add
the salt, pepper and paprika.  Cook 5 minutes. Stir in the sour
cream. Serve on toast or in patty shells.  Serves 3 to 4.    Recipe
Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by
Bantam Books, Inc.
