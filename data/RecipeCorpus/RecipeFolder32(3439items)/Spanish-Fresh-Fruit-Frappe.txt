Title: Spanish Fresh Fruit Frappe
Categories: Beverages, Ethnic
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Watermelon -- diced
1 c  Cantaloupe -- cubed
1 c  Pineapple -- diced
1 c  Mangos -- sliced
1 c  Strawberries -- halved
1 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Sugar
Mix all the ingredients. Fill blender 1/2 full of mixture and fill to
the top with crushed ice. Cover and blend on high speed until a
uniform consistency is achieved. Repeat with remaining mixture. Serve
immdiately, garnishing with fresh fruit if desired. Yield: about 7
cups. This can be doubled or tripled.
