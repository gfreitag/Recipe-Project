Title: Chickpea "Hot Dogs"
Categories: Jewish, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Dried chickpeas
3 sl White bread -- trimmed
2    Eggs
1/2 ts Minced garlic
1/4 ts Diced ground red peppers
1 1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Fat for deep frying
Wash the chickpeas and soak overnight in cold water. Drain.    Grind
the peas and bread together.  Mix in the eggs, garlic, red peppers,
salt and pepper.  Shape into finger-length rolls.  Chill for 1 hour.
