Title: Fresh Tomato, Basil, and Cheese Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Cheese/eggs, Tomatoes
Yield: 5 servings
1    (12-inch) Basic Pizza Crust
-(see previous post for
-recipe)
2 ts Olive oil
1/2 c  Grated fresh Parmesan
-cheese, divided
3 lg Ripe unpeeled tomatoes, cut
-into 1/4-inch slices (about
-1 1/2 pounds)
6    Cloves garlic, thinly sliced
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Chopped fresh basil
Fresh Tomato, Basil, and Cheese Pizza

Brush crust with olive oil.  Sprinkle with 1/4 cup cheese, leaving a
1/2 inch border.  Arrange tomatoes over cheese, over-lapping.  Top
with garlic, remaining 1/4 cup cheese, salt, and pepper.  Bake at 500
deg.F for 12 minutes on bottom rack of oven.  Remove pizza to a
cutting board; to with basil.  Let stand 5 minutes.  Yield: 6
servings (about 189 calories per wedge).

Protein 7.2, Fat 4.6, Carbohydrate 30.1, Cholesterol 5.3, Iron 2.1,
Sodium 280, Calcium 111

From: Cooking Light Magazine, Sept./Oct. 1991
