Title: Lychee Slap (Trai Vai Dap)
Categories: Desserts, Vietnamese
Yield: 4 servings
1 md Jar Chinese stem ginger --
Drained
4 oz Canned lychees
1 tb Ginger wine
1.  Insert a piece of the drained stem ginger into each lychee. 2.
Mix the liquid from the stem ginger and the lychee syrup and add the
ginger wine. Pour over the stuffed lychees.

Recipe Source: VIETNAMESE COOKING - EXOTIC DELIGHTS FROM INDO-CHINA by
