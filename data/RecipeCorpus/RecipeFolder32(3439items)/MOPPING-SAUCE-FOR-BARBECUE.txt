Title: MOPPING SAUCE FOR BARBECUE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill, Tex/mexican, Beverages
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Strong black coffee
1/2 c  Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Tomato catsup
1/4 c  Butter
1 tb Freshly ground black pepper
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Salt
Combine all ingredients and simmer 30 minutes.  Stir frequently.
Store in a tightly covered jar in refrigerator until needed. Heat
gently before using*.  This is wonderful, too, for oven roasts of
pork, etc.

[*Note:  We add about half a can of beer at this point.  K.B.]

From:  THE WIDE, WIDE WORLD OF TEXAS COOKING by Morton G. Clark,
Bonanza Books, New York. 1970.  LCC# 70-99574 Posted by: Karin
Brewer, Cooking Echo, 12/92
