Title: Kalbsschnitzel Mit Feinen Gemusen (Veal Rounds / Vegetabl
Categories: Aust/german, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Salt -- Or To Taste
1/4 ts Pepper -- Or To Taste
1/4 ts Paprika -- Or To Taste
4 ea Veal Fillets -- Cut 1/4"
Thick
4 tb Butter
4 ea Stewed Tomatoes -- Whole
12 ea White Asparagus Spears -- *
1/4 lb Mushrooms; Fresh -- Sliced
*  Asparagus Spears should be canned.
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
+++ Sprinkle salt, pepper, and paprika over the veal slices.  Saute
in butter until browned.  On each filletplace 1 stewed tomato, 3
spears asparagus and a heaping T of mushrooms.  Coo Serve with pureed
potatoes and a salad.
