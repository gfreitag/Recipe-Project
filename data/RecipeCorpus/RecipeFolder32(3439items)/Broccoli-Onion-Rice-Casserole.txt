Title: Broccoli Onion Rice Casserole
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Fresh broccoli, cooked
1 c  Rice, cooked
1 lg Sweet onion, chopped
2 tb Butter
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Milk
1/4 lb Velveeta cheese
Saute onions in butter.   Add soup, milk, broccoli, and cheese. Cook
until cheese melts.  Mix with rice.  Pour into casserole dish. Bake
at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Source: Rock Springs Sweet Onion
Festival Cook Book 1992
