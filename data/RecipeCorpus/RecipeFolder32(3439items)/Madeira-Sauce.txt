Title: Madeira Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Guestchefs
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Madeira
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 c  Chicken stock -- OR
Low-sodium chicke
1/4 c  Cream
2 tb Butter
Salt and pepper -- to taste
Here's a simplified recipe for Madeira Sauce. Serve it with roast
meat, such as Beef Wellington.

COMBINE MADEIRA, THYME AND STOCK in a saucepan over medium heat and
cook until reduced by about half. Add the cream and continue to cook
until the liquid becomes saucelike. Remove from the heat, beat in the
butter and add salt and pepper. Makes 1/2 Cup MICHAEL ROBERTS -
