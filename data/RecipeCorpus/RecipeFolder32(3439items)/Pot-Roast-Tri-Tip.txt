Title: Pot Roast Tri-Tip
Categories: Meats, Guestchefs
Yield: 8 servings
2 lb Sirloin tri-tip
Salt and pepper -- as
Desired
2 tb Oil
1 md Onion -- peeled and
Quartered
1    Carrot -- cut up
1    Celery stalk -- chopped
2 tb Flour
1 c  Red wine
3 c  Chicken broth
1 ts Thyme
PAT THE MEAT DRY, SPRINKLE with salt and pepper. Heat the oil in a
large flame-proof casserole, add the meat and brown on both sides
over high heat. Remove the meat from the pot, reduce heat to medium
and add onions, carrots and celery and cook, stirring, Serve the
sauce separately.
