Title: LEMON SWEET AND SOUR SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chinese
Yield: 1 servings
JANE HARRIS  DXDG05A
1 ts Lemon peel;grated
1 tb Oil
2 tb Sugar
2    Lemon juice;from 2 lemons
6 tb Water
1 ts Cornstarch
saute peel in oil add sugar juice of 2 lemons mix cornstarch with
water and add to mixture cook over medium heat till smooth and thick
this is excellent with chicken.
