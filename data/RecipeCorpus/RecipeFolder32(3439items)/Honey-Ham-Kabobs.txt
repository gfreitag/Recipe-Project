Title: Honey Ham Kabobs
Categories: Main dish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sugar, brown; firmly packed
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Orange juice
1 1/2 lb Ham steak; fully cooked, cut
-into 1" cubes
1 sm Pineapple; peeled, cored and
-cut into 1" cubes
16 lg Olives, pimiento-stuffed
Combine brown sugar, honey, and orange juice; mix well, and set aside.

Alternate ham, pineapple, and olives on skewers.  Grill over medium
heat 10 to 12 minutes, turning often.  Baste liberally with honey
mixture after each turn.
