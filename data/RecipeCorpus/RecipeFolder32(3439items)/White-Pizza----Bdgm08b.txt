Title: White Pizza    Bdgm08b
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Ethnic
Yield: 6 servings
Denise Bradshaw
2 oz Feta cheese -- crumbled
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1 oz Parmesan/Romano cheese --
Grtd
1/4 c  Butter -- melted
1 ts Olive oil
Dough or pizza shell
1/2 ts Oregano
4 oz Cheddar or swiss cheese --
Grtd
Red hot pepper -- to taste
4 oz Mozzerella cheese -- grtd
Ssaute garlic in butter until light brown.  Spray bottom of pizza pan
or cookie sheet w/non-stick oil; knead dough to fit, or place shell...
Sprinkle cheese on top; drizzle w/olive oil, sprinkle oregano & red
pepper (if desired) on top.  Bake @425 until golden brown.
