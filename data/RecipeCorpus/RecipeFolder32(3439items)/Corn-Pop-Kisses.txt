Title: Corn Pop Kisses
Categories: Snacks, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
2    Egg whites
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Kellogg's sugar pops
1/2 c  Chopped nutmeats
1 c  Shredded coconut
Beat egg whites until stiff but no dry.Beat in sugar,a tablespoonful
at a time.Fold in remaining ingredients.Drop by teaspoonfuls onto
well greased baking sheets. Bake in moderate oven 350 about 10
minutes. Remove at once from baking sheets.

From back of box Kellogg's Cereal
