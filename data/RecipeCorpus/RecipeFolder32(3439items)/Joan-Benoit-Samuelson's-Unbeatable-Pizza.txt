Title: Joan Benoit Samuelson's Unbeatable Pizza
Categories: Pizza, Pizza
Yield: 4 servings
1    BOBOLI crust (12")
1    Tomato,cut into eighths
1 c  Broccoli flowerets
1/2 c  Red onion rings
Dried basil leaves
2 c  KRAFT Mozzarella Cheese
1. Heat oven to 350'F.~ 2. Place crust on cookie sheet.~ 3. Top with
vegetables.~ 4. Bake 10-15 minutes or until cheese is melted and
topping is thoroughly heated.
