Title: Caltex Chili Powder
Categories: Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Toasted cumin seed --
Ground
2 tb Toasted coriander seed --
Ground
2 tb Paprika
1 tb Toasted teicherry black
Peppercorns -- ground
2 1/4 ts Chipotle chilies -- ground
1 1/2 ts Pequin chiles with seeds --
Ground
3/4 ts Dried cascabels with seeds
Ground
1/2 ts Ginger -- grond
1 ts Cloves -- ground
Sift all the ingredients together and store in a tightly sealed jar.
