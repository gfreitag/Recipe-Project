Title: Lyons Spaghetti
Categories: Italian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
DICK KATHRIEN BVXV78A
1 cn #2 tomatoes Italian style
1/2 c  Cooking oil
1 lb Fresh mushrooms sliced or
1 lb Ground beef lean
1 cn Condensed mushroom
1    Box 7 oz macaroni
Soup
2 md Green peppers minced
1 lb American cheese
1 lg Onion minced
Brown meat, onions, green peppers and cooking oil. Cook macaroni. Then
combine all the rest of ingredients and pour in 9 X 13 pan. Bake at
350 for about 1 hour. From the cookbook ASK YOUR NEIGHBOR 1970
