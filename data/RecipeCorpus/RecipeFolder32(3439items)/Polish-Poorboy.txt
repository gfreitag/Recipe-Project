Title: Polish Poorboy
Categories: Sausage, Sandwich, Polish, Potatoes
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ea - cup chopped onion
1 ea - Polish sausage
5 ea - potatoes, peeled and diced
1 ea - tsp garlic powder
1 ea - 8 oz can mushrooms
Mix all ingredients together in a greased skillet, cook, covered, for
5 minutes. Stir occasionally. Cook over medium heat until potatoes
are soft. When potatoes are soft, increase heat. Brown and serve.
Best with buttered bread and milk!
