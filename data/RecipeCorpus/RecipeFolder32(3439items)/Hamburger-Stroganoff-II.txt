Title: Hamburger Stroganoff II
Categories: Meats, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1    Onion; chopped
2 tb Flour
1 ts Salt
1 tb Garlic salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 c  Sour cream
2 c  Noodles; cooked, hot
4 oz Mushrooms; sliced
In a large skillet, cook ground beef and onions. Stir in flour, salt,
garlic salt and pepper. Add mushrooms. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring
constantly. Remove from heat. Stir in soup, simmer uncovered for 10
minutes. Stir in sour cream and heat through. Serve over noodles.
