Title: PETA Tofu Scrambles
Categories: Breakfast, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Firm tofu; patted dry
-and mashed
1/8 ts Tumeric
1 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Finely chopped veggies;
-bell pepper, fresh
-mushrooms, onions,
-tomatoes, etc
Place the tofu in a lightly oiled saute pan and cook over med heat
for 3 mins.  Add the remaining ingredients, stir well, and cook for
5-8 mins, until the veggies are thoroughly cooked and the tofu is
heated thru.
