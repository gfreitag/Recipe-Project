Title: Piroshki -  Cheese Filling
Categories: Jewish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Pot cheese
1/4 lb Cream cheese
2 tb Sour cream
1    Egg yolk
1 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar -- (optional)
Beat all the ingreients together until smooth.  Use the sugar or not,
depending on whether you like a sweet filling. Place filling on dough.
Serve finished piroshki with sour cream. Recipe Source: THE ART OF
JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc.
