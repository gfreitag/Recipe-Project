Title: SPECIAL SMOKED RIBS FOR SMOKER
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
4 lb To 6 lb pork spareribs
16 oz Tomato sauce
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Chili powder
2 ts Paprika
2 ts Salt
1 ts Celery seed
Cut ribs in 5-6 rib sections and set aside.  Combine remaining
ingredients in a saucepan and heat until sugar melts and mixture is
well-blended. Pour mixture over ribs and marinate 5-7 hours.  Remove
ribs from mixture and place on smoker grid or in a rib rack on the
grid. Brush with mixture once again before smoking.

If you are using a double grid smoker, the ribs on the top grid will
cook faster.

CHARCOAL:  Use 7-8 lbs. of charcoal, 4 quarts hot water, 3 wood
sticks and smoke 2 1/2 to 3 1/2 hours.

ELECTRIC:  Use 3-4 quarts hot water, 3 wood sticks, and smoke 2 1/2
to 3 1/2 hours.
