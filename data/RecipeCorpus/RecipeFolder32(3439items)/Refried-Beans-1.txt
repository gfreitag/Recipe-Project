Title: Refried Beans 1
Categories: Vegetables, Tex/mexican, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Lard Or Vegetable Oil
2 c  Cooked Pinto Beans
2 tb Chile Powder
1 tb Cumin; Ground
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
~---------------------------------------------------------------------
~-- Heat the lard in a 10-inch skillet over medium heat until hot.
Add the pinto beans and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Mash the beans then stir in the remaining ingredients.  Add oil to
the skillet if necessary then cook and stir until a smooth paste
forms, about 4 minutes. Garnish with shredded cheese, if desired and
serve, Submitted By RICH HARPER   On   07-03-95
