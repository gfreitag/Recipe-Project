Title: Home-Style Hamburgers
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
4 tb Grated onion
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Grated potato -- drained
1    Egg
1/4 c  Water
3 tb Fat
2    Onions -- sliced
Mix the beef, grated onion, garlic, salt, pepper, potato, egg and
water together.  Form into 8 hamburgers.    Melt the fat in a skillet
and brown the hamburgers and sliced onion for 15 minutes, turning the
hamburgers after 10 minutes. Serves 4 to 8. Recipe Source: THE ART OF
JEWISH COOKING by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc.
