Title: Pigs In A Poke
Categories: Meats, Cheese/eggs, Pastry
Yield: 8 servings
8    Hot dogs, partially split
8 sl Cheddar cheese that are the
-size of 1" strips
8 oz Refrigerator Crescent Rolls
Fill each hot dog with a cheese strip.  Separate crescent dough into 8
triangles.  Place hot dog on wide end of triagle, and roll up.  Place
on greased cookie sheet, cheese side up.  Bake at 375 degrees for 15
minutes or until rolls are golden brown.  Makes 8 servings.

Source:  S.H.A.R.E. Colorado June/July 1995
