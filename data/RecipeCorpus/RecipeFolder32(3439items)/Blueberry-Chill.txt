Title: Blueberry Chill
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
24    Crushed vanilla wafers
8 oz Cream cheese
1 c  Whipping cream
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1 cn Blueberry filling
Spread crushed wafers in square 8x8 pan. Beat until smooth cream
cheese, cream and sugar. Pour over crumbs, top with filling, freeze
at least 2 hours. From: Agnes Colgan Date: 05 May 94
