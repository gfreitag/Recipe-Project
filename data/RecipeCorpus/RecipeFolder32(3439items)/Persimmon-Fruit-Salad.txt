Title: Persimmon Fruit Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
11 oz Mandarin oranges; drained
2 c  Apple; diced
2    Bananas; sliced
1/2 c  Persimmons; peeled & sliced
1/4 c  Walnuts, black; chopped
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Cream, whipped
1 ts Sugar
Combine fruits and nuts, stirring gently.  Combine remaining
ingredients; mix well, and pour over fruit mixture.  Toss lightly.
Chill 1 to 2 hours
