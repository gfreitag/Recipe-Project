Title: Hot Pasta Primavera    (Pstt79c)
Categories: Main dish, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
Heavy cream
Reggiano Parmesan -- grated
Butter
Salt black pepper to taste.
Mix all the ingredients together in a saucepan until melted and
smooth. Taste, adjust seasonings pour over hot angel hair or linguine
that has been tossed with steamed-----> vegetables (sliced carrots,
broccoli, cauliflower, zucchini, yellow squash (seeds removed), snap
peas, snow peas, red bell pepper--you name the vegetable It makes a
stunning presentation. I serve this as an appetizer in a large
scallop shell.
