Title: Cream Cheese Pound Cake - 2
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 cake
1 1/2 c  Butter
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
3 c  Sugar
6    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Almond flavoring
3 c  Cake flour
Cream butter and cream cheese together, blend in sugar.  Add eggs,
one at a time and continue to blend.  Add vanilla, almond flavoring
and flour, blend until smooth but DO NOT over beat.

Pour batter into a greased and floured tube pan.

Bake at 325 degree oven for one hour and 15 minutes or until cake
tests done.
