Title: Vinete (Eggplant)
Categories: Romanian, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 x  Eggplant
1 x  Onion, medium, grated
1 ts Lemon juice
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 x  Salt (to taste)
Broil eggplant on all sides until well cooked. Scrape off skin and
mash well with a wooden spoon. Add salt to taste. Mix grated onion
with 1 teaspoon of the oil and add to eggplant. Add remaining oil to
eggplant slowly, stirring constantly. Add lemon juice and serve as an
appetizer on crackers or bread.
