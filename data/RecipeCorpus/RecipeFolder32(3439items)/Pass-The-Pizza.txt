Title: Pass The Pizza
Categories: Italian, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Olive or vegetable oil
20 ml Garlic -- minced
1 md Eggplant -- peeled & cubed
1 c  Spaghetti or pizza sauce
1 lg Green pepper -- cut in
Strips
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 md Onion -- sliced
1 pk Refrigerated pizza crust
1 sm Zucchini -- sliced
1 c  Mozzarella cheese --
Shredded
1. Preheat oven to 425. 2. In a 10" skillet over medium heat, in hot
oil, cook the eggplant, pepper, onion, zucchini and garlic under
pepper is tender-crisp, stirring occasionally.  Stir in spaghetti
asuce and Parmesan cheese. 3. Unroll and gently strech 4. to serve:
Carefully invert onto serving platter. Sprinkle with Mozzarella
cheese and let stand for 5 minutes. FOOD AND WINE BB TOPIC: FOOD
