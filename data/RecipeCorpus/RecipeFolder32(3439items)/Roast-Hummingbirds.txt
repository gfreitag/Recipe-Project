Title: Roast Hummingbirds
Categories: Game, Poultry, Tex/mexican
Yield: 2 servings
4    Hummingbirds*
8 sl Bacon or salt pork
1 tb Butter
Small batch of rice or
-bread stuffing; your
-favorite recipe
Draw and dry pluck the birds. Cover high exposed surfaces with bacon
or salt pork around bird. Melt butter in skillet, put in birds and
bake in a hot oven for about 5 minutes. Reduce heat to about 350F.
and bake another 15 minutes. If birds are stuffed, bake 20 minutes.

*If large Texas hummingbirds aren't available, you can substitute
quail with good effect.
