Title: Cream Cheese Pound Cake 1
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 cake
1/2 lb Butter
6    Eggs
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Sour cream
4 c  Sifted all purpose flour
1 ts Baking Soda
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
Sift together last three items.   Cream butter and sugar until light
and fluffy.  Add cream cheese and beat well.  Add eggs one at a time,
beating well after each.  Add dry ingredients alternately with sour
cream. Beat well.  Add vanilla.

Bake in greased angel food pan for one hour and five minutes or until
done.
