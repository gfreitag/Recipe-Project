Title: Basic Hard Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Desserts, Guestchefs
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 lb Unsalted butter -- softened
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla -- =OR=- other
Flavorin
Rum or Orange liqueur
CREAM BUTTER UNTIL VERY SOFT. Gradually add the sugar until well
whipped. Add the flavoring; chill before serving.

Makes 3/4 Cup PETER KUMP - PRODIGY GUEST CHEFS COOKBOOK
