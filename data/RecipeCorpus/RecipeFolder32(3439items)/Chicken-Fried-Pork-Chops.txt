Title: Chicken Fried Pork Chops
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 servings
4    Pork chops, trimmed of fat
2    Eggs, beaten
3 tb Water
1/4 c  Flour
3/4 c  Cornmeal
Whip eggs with water.  Mix remaining ingredients and place on plate.
Dip chops into egg wash, then into cornmeal coating.  Pan-fry in a
bit of oil over medium heat until golden brown. From:
