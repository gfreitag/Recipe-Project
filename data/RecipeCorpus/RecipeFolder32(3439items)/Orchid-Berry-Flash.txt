Title: Orchid Berry Flash
Categories: Beverages, Fruit, Healthy
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Peach juice
1 c  Vanilla frozen yogurt
1 c  Blueberries; fresh or frozen
1 tb Protein powder
2 tb Plain nonfat yogurt
PREP TIME:  5 minutes

Combine all ingredients in a blender and puree at high speed until
smooth.

PER SERVING:  229 calories; 2g fat; 8% calories from fat; 15mg
cholesterol; 1.7g fiber.

Source:  Karen Green in "Fast!  Fresh!  Fruity!" (New Hope Press) as
seen in "Delicious!  Your Magazine of Natural Living," July 1995.
