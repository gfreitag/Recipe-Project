Title: Pheasant Mulligan With Dumplings - United States
Categories: Vegetables, Breads, Game
Yield: 1 servings
2    Pheasants-disjointed
2 c  Carrots-diced
1 c  Onions-diced
1 c  Cabbage-shredded
2 c  Potatoes-diced
2 tb Fat
Salt & pepper to taste
Clean and wash pheasants and cover with water. Add carrots, onions and
cabbage, cook slowly until tender. Combine potatoes, fat, salt and
pepper, continue cooking meat and vegetables until an are tender. Add
dumplings and cook for fifteen minutes without lifting the cover.
