Title: Knishes -  Chicken Filling
Categories: Jewish, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Ground cooked chicken
3/4 c  Mashed potatoes
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Mix all the ingredients until smooth.  Place on dough.    Recipe
