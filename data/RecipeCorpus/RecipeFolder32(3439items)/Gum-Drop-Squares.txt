Title: Gum Drop Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 35 squares
1 pk McCormick's baking gums,slic
2 c  Flour
4    Eggs
1 tb Cold water
2 c  Light brown sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Chopped pecan meats
Mix baking gums with 1/4 c flour.Beat eggs with water.Beat in sugar
until fluffy.Add remaining flour,salt and cinnamon sifted
together.Add nuts and baking gums.Spread in buttered pan .Bake
30minutes at 325 degrees.Cut into squares.Roll in powdered sugar.
