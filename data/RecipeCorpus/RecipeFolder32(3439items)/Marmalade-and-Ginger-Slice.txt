Title: Marmalade and Ginger Slice
Categories: Scot/irish, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 cake
8 oz Flour
8 oz Golden syrup
4 oz Butter
8 oz Orange marmalade
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Ground ginger
1    Egg; beaten
Set oven to 350Ã¸F or Mark 4.  Grease an 8 inch square cake tin. Melt
the syrup and butter in a pan over a very gentle heat. Stir in the
marmalade. Leave to cool.  Sift the dry ingredients into a bowl. Make
a well in the centre and pour in the syrup mixture and the beaten
egg. Beat well until thoroughly blended. Turn into the tin and cook
for about 45 minutes until firm and golden in colour.  Cool on a wire
rack and cut into slices.
