Title: Souvlakia
Categories: Bbq/grill, Greek, Meats
Yield: 12 skewers
1 1/2 lb Boneless lamb shoulder
1/4 c  Dry red wine
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Olive Oil
2    Clv  Garlic, minced
1 tb Oregano, dried
1 tb Rosemary
2 ts Lime rind, grated
1    Bay Leaf
1/4 ts Pepper
Description:

Sizzling, skewered lamb kebabs are delicious served with generous
dollops of Tzatziki.

Cooking Time: 6 to 8 min
Preparation Time: 20 min

Instructions:

Trim excess fat from lamb; cut lamb into 4 by 1/2-in strips; set
aside.

In a shallow nonmetallic dish, whisk together remaining ingredients.
Add lamb to marinade, turn and coat thoroughly with marinade.

Refrigerate covered, at least 4 hours and up to 24 hours, stirring
occasionally.

Just before serving, remove lamb from the marinade.  Thread lamb,
accordian style onto 12 metal skewers.

Place skewers on oiled barbecue grill; grill over medium heat 6-8
minutes, turning often and basting with marinade halfway through
cooking time, until meat is tender but still pink inside.
