Title: Apricot Champagne
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Apricot liqueur
1 ts Apricot brandy
Champagne or sparkling wine
For each serving, pour the apricot liqueur and apricot brandy into a
champagne flute. Pour iced champagne on top and serve immediately.
