Title: Fresh Raspberry Sauce
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Fresh raspberries
1/3    To 1/2 cup sugar
1 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 tb Brandy (Optional)
1.  Combine the raspberries and sugar in a saucepan, crushing the
berries with a spoon.  Bring to a boil, stirring frequently.  Strain .

2.  Combine the cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of cooled raspberry
juice. Bring the remaining juice to a boil.  Stir in the cornstarch
mixture; bring to a boil, stirring frequently.  Boil for 1 minute.

3.  Cool the sauce.  Add the lemon juice and brandy (if used).  Serve
the sauce over vanilla ice cream or lemon sherbet.

[Raspberry Recipes from Larriland Farms; Lisbon, MD]
