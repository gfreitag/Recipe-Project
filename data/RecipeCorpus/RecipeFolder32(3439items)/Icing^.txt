Title: Icing^
Categories: Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Cream
1/2 c  Maple syrup
Butter the size of a walnut
Mix all together in a saucepan. Let boil until it forms a soft ball
in ice cold water. Beat till creamy. Spread on cake.
