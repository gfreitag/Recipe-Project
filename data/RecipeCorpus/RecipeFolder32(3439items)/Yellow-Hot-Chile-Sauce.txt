Title: Yellow Hot Chile Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chilies
Yield: 1 servings
2    Shallots
-peeled and chopped
4 md Yellow hot chile peppers
-roasted, peeled,
-seeded and quartered
1 c  Dry white wine
1/3 c  Heavy cream
1 1/4 c  Butter (3 sticks)
-cut in 1/2" cubes
1 ts Chopped basil
1/4    Lemon
Salt and pepper to taste
Combine the shallots, chiles and wine. Bring the mixture to a boil and
reduce it until there is no liquid left; about 5 minutes. Add the
cream and simmer for about 2 minutes. Remove the pan from the heat
and whisk is the butter, bit by bit. Strain the sauce, add the basil
and the juice from the lemon. Add salt and pepper to taste.

Serve with steamed seafood. Garnish with additional yellow chiles if
desired.
