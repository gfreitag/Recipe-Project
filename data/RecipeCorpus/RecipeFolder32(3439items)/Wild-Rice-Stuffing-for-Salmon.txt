Title: Wild Rice Stuffing for Salmon
Categories: Seafood, Dress/stuff, Fish
Yield: 12 servings
3/4 c  Wild rice
3 tb Butter
1 lg Onion, chopped
4 ea Stalks celery, chopped
1/2 c  Red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1 1/2 c  Croutons
1/2 c  Pecans, toasted
3 ea Tbps sherry
3 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Fresh (or 1 tsp dried) thyme
1 tb Chopped fresh (or 1 tsp
-dried) basil
Salt and pepper to taste
Put rice in 2 1/2 cups boiling water, simmer 30 - 40 minutes.

Melt butter in saucepan, Saute onion, mushrooms, celery, red pepper
until tender.  Add croutons, and wild rice.

Combine the rest of ingredients and add to the above.

Fill salmon cavity and bake as directed. Source: Official Expo 86
Cookbook
