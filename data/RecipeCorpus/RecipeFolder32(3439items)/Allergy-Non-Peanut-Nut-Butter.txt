Title: Allergy Non Peanut Nut Butter
Categories: Allergy, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
6 tb A tolerated oil (or water)
1 c  Any tolerated seeds or nuts
Salt to taste
Blend in processor until smooth consistency.
