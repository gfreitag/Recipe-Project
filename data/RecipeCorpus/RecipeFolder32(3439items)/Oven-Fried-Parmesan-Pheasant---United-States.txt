Title: Oven Fried Parmesan Pheasant - United States
Categories: Game, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1    Pheasant-disjointed
Salt & pepper to taste
1 c  Stuffing-seasoned
1/2 c  Butter-melted
2/3 c  Parmesan
Cheese
1/4 c  Parsley
From : Sallie Krebs, Thu 06 Jul 95 04:00, Area: COOKING

Combine fresh crushed stuffing, cheese and parsley. Dip pheasant
pieces in butter and roll in stuffing mixture. Arrange pieces, skin
side up, in a shallow pan. Sprinkle with remaining crumbs and butter.
Bake in a 375 oven for one hour or until tender.
