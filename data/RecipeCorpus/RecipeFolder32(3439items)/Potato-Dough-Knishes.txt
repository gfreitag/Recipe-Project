Title: Potato-Dough Knishes
Categories: Jewish, Potatoes
Yield: 20 servings
3/4 c  Minced onions
6 tb Chicken fat or butter
4 c  Mashed potatoes
1/2 c  Potato flour
3    Eggs
1 ts Salt
1/4    Teadsoon
Pepper
Brown the onions in 4 tablespoons chicken fat or butter. Cool.
Knead together the remaining fat or butter, the potatoes, potato
flour, eggs, salt and pepper.  Break off pieces (about 2-inches long)
and flatten slightly.  Place a teaspoon of browned onions on each and
cover by pinching the edges together. Place on a greased baking
sheet. Bake in a 375 degree oven 25 minutes.  Makes about 20.
