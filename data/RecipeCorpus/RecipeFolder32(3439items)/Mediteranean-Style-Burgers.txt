Title: Mediteranean-Style Burgers
Categories: Meats
Yield: 8 burgers
2 lb Lean ground beef
10 oz Spinach, frozen chopped-
-thawed and drained well
2 cl Garlic; finely chopped
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 c  Prepared spaghetti sauce
1/2 c  Packaged Italian-seasoned
-bread crumbs
Preheat the broiler or prepare the grill so the coals are hot.
Combine the beef, spinach, garlic, Parmesan cheese, spaghetti sauce
and bread crumbs in a large bowl until well mixed.  Divide the
mixture into 8 equal portions, and shape into 8 burger patties.
Broil or grill the burgers 4 inches from heat for 6 minutes on each
side or until cooked through.
Makes 8 burgers at 78 cents each.  Prep:  15 minutes Broil or
grill: 12 minutes.
Per burger:  333 calories, 30 g protein, 19 g fat, 10 g
carbohydrate, 510 mg sodium, 90 mg cholesterol.
