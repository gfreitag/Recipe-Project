Title: Mexican Wings
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry
Yield: 80 servings
10 oz Hot Sauce -
1/2 c  Margarine -- melted
1/4 c  Catsup -
1 ts Chili Powder -
1 ts Ground Cumin seed -
1/2 ts Garlic Powder -
5 lb Large chicken wings -- split
Or not, tips cut off
1.Combine hot sauce margarine,catsup & spices and heat, stirring
frequently.

2. Fry wings at 365 until crispy, about 6-9 minutes depending on size
and equipment used.( Check under ANCHOR BAR HOT WINGS for good
handling procedure of Wings) 3. Drain well 4.Toss the wings in hot
sauce mixture
