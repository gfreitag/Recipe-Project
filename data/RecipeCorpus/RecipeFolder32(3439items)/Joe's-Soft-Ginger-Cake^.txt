Title: Joe's Soft Ginger Cake^
Categories: Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Shortening
2/3 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Molasses
1 1/2 ts Baking soda in
1 c  Boiling water
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Ground cloves
2    Eggs, beaten
Mix together shortening, sugar, salt and molasses. Add the
soda-in-water while hot. Mix and add the flour. Mix in spices.
Finally mix in beaten eggs. Pour into greased pan and bake in a
moderate (350 degree) oven for 30 minutes.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
