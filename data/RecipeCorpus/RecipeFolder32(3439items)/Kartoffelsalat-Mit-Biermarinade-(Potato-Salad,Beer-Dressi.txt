Title: Kartoffelsalat Mit Biermarinade (Potato Salad/Beer Dressi
Categories: Aust/german, Salads
Yield: 4 servings

4 ea Bacon -- Slices
1 tb Onion -- Chopped
1 ea Celery; Stalk -- Chopped
1 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
2 tb Unbleached Flour
1/2 ts Mustard -- Dry
1 tb Sugar
1 c  Beer -- Any Brand
1/2 ts Tobasco Sauce
2 tb Parsley -- Chopped Fresh
