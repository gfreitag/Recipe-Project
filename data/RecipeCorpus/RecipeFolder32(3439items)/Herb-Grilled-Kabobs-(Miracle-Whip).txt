Title: Herb Grilled Kabobs (Miracle Whip)
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Vegetables, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 lb Boneless Beef Sirloin Steak
- cut into 1 1/2" pieces
1 md Zucchini, cut in 3/4" pieces
12 md Mushrooms
1 sm Red onion, quartered
1 sm Yellow onion, quartered
1/2 ea Red and Yellow Pepper, cut
- into pieces
12 ea Cherry Tomatoes
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
1/2 c  Kraft House Italian Dressing
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Ground red pepper
Arrange meat and vegetables on skewers.  Mix remaining
ingredients. Place kabobs on grill over medium-high coals; brush with
dressing mixture. Grill 10 to 15 minutes or to desired doneness,
turning once.
OVEN DIRECTIONS:  Heat broiler.  Place kabobs on rack of broiler
pan; brush with dressing mixture.  Broil 5 to 7 inches from heat 10
to 15 minutes or to desired doneness, turning once.
Prep Time: 45 minutes
