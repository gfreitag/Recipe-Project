Title: Frankfurter Hash
Categories: Jewish, Meats, Potatoes, Tomatoes
Yield: 6 servings
12    Frankfurters
4    Boiled potatoes -- peeled
And diced
3    Onions -- sliced
2 tb Fat
3    Green peppers -- sliced
3    Tomatoes -- diced
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Cut the frankfurters into 1-inch slices.    Lightly brown the
potatoes and onion in the fat.  Add the green peppers and
frankfurters. Cook over low heat 5 minutes.  Add the tomatoes, salt
and pepper. Cover and cook 10 minutes.  Serves 6.    Recipe Source:
THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam
