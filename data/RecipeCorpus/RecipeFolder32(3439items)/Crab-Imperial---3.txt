Title: Crab Imperial - 3
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 8 servings
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
2 tb Chopped pimento
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
8 dr Hot pepper sauce
1 ts Salt
2 lb Backfin crabmeat
1 ts Old Bay or to taste
1 ts Mayonnaise per shell
ds Paprika for sprinkling
Mix fist six ingredients well with fork until blended.  Put crabmeat
in a large bowl and sprinkle with seafood seasoning.  Add egg mixture
and mix gently.

Spoon mixture into 8 individual shells.  Spread mayonnaise over top
of each shell and sprinkle with paprika.

Bake in 400 oven until hot and bubbly - about 20 min.  Serve
immediately

The Crab Claw, St. Michaels Md
