Title: Lentil And Rice Stew
Categories: Jewish, Meats, Vegetables, Rice, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
3 lb Chuck
3    Onions -- diced
2 tb Fat
1 c  Lentils
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
4 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Uncooked rice
Cut the beef in 2-inch cubes and brown with the onions in the fat.
Soak lentils in cold water for 2 hours.  Drain. Add the lentils,
salt, pepper and boiling water.  Cover and cook over low heat 1 1/2
hours.
Stir in the rice; recover and cook additional 25 minutes.  Serves 6
to 8.
