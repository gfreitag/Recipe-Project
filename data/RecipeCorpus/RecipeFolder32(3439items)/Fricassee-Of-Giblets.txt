Title: Fricassee Of Giblets
Categories: Jewish, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Mixed giblets -- * see note
4 tb Chicken fat
1 c  Diced onions
2 tb Flour
4 c  Boiling water
3 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Garlic powder
3/4 lb Ground beef
2 tb Cold water
3/4 c  Raw rice
* Note:  Buy necks, gizzards, livers, wings and feet. Wash them
thoroughly and remove the heavy skin from the feet.    Heat the
chicken fat in a saucepan and brown the onions init.  Add the giblets
and let them brown for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with the flour
