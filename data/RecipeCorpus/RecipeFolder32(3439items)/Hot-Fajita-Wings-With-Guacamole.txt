Title: Hot Fajita Wings With Guacamole
Categories: Appetizers, Tex/mexican
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Honey
2 tb Lime juice
2 tb Chili powder
2 tb Soy sauce
2 1/2 lb Chicken wings -- shoulder
Midsection only -- cut
Apart
1 ct (6 oz) frozen -- avocado dip
Thawed;
1 c  Homemade guacamole
In a large plastic bag, mix honey, lime juice, chili powder, and soy
sauce. Add wings; seal bag and mix to coat. Chill, turning
occasionally, at least 1 hour or up to a day. Lift out wings and
place in a single layer on racks in 2 broiler pans, each about Makes
8 to 10 appetizer servings. Per serving: 170 calories (53% from fat),
10 g fat (2.4 g saturated), 363 mg sodium, 29 mg cholesterol.
