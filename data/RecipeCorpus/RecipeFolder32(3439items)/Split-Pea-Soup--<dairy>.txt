Title: Split Pea Soup  <dairy>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Split peas
2 1/2 qt Water
2    Onions -- diced
1    Carrot -- grated
2    Celery stalks -- sliced
2    Sprigs Parsley
2    Sprigs Dill
3    Potatoes -- peeled and
Diced
2 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Milk
1 tb Butter
Wash the peas and combine with the water, onions, carrot, celery,
parsley and dill.  Cover; bring to a boil and cook over low heat 2
1/2 hours. Add the potatoes, salt and pepper.  Cook 20 minutes.  Stir
in the milk and butter, bring to boiling point and serve.  Serves 8.
Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOK
