Title: Hopping John Vinaigrette
Categories: Salads, Guestchefs
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Dried black-eyed peas
1    Piece of fatback -- or salt
Pork
1    Hot red pepper
2 md Onions -- chopped
Salt
Freshly ground pepper
1 c  Uncooked rice
4 tb Drippings -- preferably
Bacon
MMMMM------------------------VINAIGRETTE-----------------------------
1/3 c  Red wine vinegar
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 c  Peanut oil
Salt and pepper
2 tb Chopped fresh basil
4 sl Bacon -- crisply fried &
Crumbled
Hopping John is a traditional Southern New Year's dish. This is a
modern salad version.

PLACE BLACK-EYED PEAS in a large pot, cover with water and add 3 cups
more water. Add fatback or salt pork, slashed in several places, hot
red pepper, half the onion, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, cover,
reduce the heat and simmer until the peas are t Remove the fatback
and hot pepper. Add rest of chopped onion. Make a vinaigrette of red
wine vinegar, dijon mustard and peanut oil. Add salt and pepper.
Combine with the Hopping John, add chopped fresh basil and bacon,
crisply fried and crumbled.
