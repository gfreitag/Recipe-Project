Title: Rice Kugel
Categories: Jewish, Rice, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
4 c  Boiling water
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Rice
6    Eggs
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Seedless raisins
1/3 c  Melted butter of fat
Boil the water, salt and rice in a covered saucepan for 10 minutes.
Drain. Beat the eggs and brown sugar together until thick.  Stir in
the rice, raisins and butter or fat.  Turn into a greased baking dish
or casserole. Bake in a 350 degree oven 40 minutes or until browned.
Serves 6. Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie
