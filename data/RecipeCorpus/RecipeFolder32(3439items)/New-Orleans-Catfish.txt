Title: New Orleans Catfish
Categories: Cajun/creol, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Catfish fillets
1 pn Black pepper
2 tb Onion,grated
6 ea Lemon slices,thin,seeded
1 x  Parsley chopped
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Cooked rice
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1. Thaw fish according to package directions.
2. Cut fish into serving-size portions; place in a well-greased
13x9x2-inch baking dish and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Mix rice, onion and curry powder; spread over fish.
4. Top with lemon slices and dot with butter.
5. Cover and bake in preheated 350'F. oven 25 to 35 minutes or until
fish flakes easily.
6. Uncover last few minutes of cooking to allow for slight browning.
7. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
