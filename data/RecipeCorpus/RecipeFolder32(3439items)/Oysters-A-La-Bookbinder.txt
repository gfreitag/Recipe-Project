Title: Oysters A La Bookbinder
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
12 ea Oysters
1 ea Mushroom-large -- finely
Chopp
1 ts Onion-minced
3 tb Butter
1 ts Flour
1/2 c  Milk-warm
1/4 c  Oatmeal-cooked
Salt & pepper
1 tb Parsley-minced
3 tb Parmesan cheese-grated
Wash & shuck oysters,putting them on the half shell. Saute the
mushroom & onion in 1 tbs. butter until soft but not browned. Add
flour & stir to blend. Add milk & stir until thickened. Add oatmeal &
stir well. Season to taste w/ salt & pepper. Stir in parsley. Place
oysters under broiler for 4 min. Remove & Melt 2 tbs. butter &
drizzle a few drops over oysters. Return to broiler & cook for 5 min.
or until browned.
