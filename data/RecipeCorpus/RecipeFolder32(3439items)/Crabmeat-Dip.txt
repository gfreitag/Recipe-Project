Title: Crabmeat Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Ethnic
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Coconut cream
10 5/8 oz Crab meat -- fresh, frozen
Or can
1/4 ts Salt
2    Spring onions -- very finely
Chopped
2 tb Coriander -- * see note
1 tb Lemon juice
1    Green chile -- seeded and
Chopped
White pepper -- to taste
1. In a small saucepan simmer the coconut, crabmeatr and salt for 5
min.

2. Stire rest of ingredients and leave to cool.

Place dip in bowl and serve with vegetable sticks: carrots, peppers,
celery, cucumber, spring onions, etc.
