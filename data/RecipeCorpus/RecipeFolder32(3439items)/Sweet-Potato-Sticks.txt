Title: Sweet Potato Sticks
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
*****  NONE  *****
Wash and peel sweet potatoes.  Cut into matchstick strips as for
carrot sticks.  Place in a bowl of ice water to crisp until ready to
serve. Serve them for snacks or as crudites on a relish tray or in a
lunch.
