Title: Smothered Pheasant - United States
Categories: Game, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1    Pheasant-disjointed
Flour
Salt & pepper to taste
1 ds Of garlic salt
1/2 c  Oil
1 cn Mushroom soup
From : Sallie Krebs, Thu 06 Jul 95 04:00, Area: COOKING

Dredge pheasant pieces with flour, seasoned with salt, pepper and
garlic. Brown all pieces turning once. Place in casse- role, pour
mushroom soup over top, cover and bake in a preheated 325 oven for
thirty or forty minutes or until tender. Serve with gravy made from
the pan drippings.
