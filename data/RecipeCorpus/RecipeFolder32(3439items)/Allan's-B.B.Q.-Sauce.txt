Title: Allan's B.B.Q. Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 ketchup, 1
1    Chili sauce
1/3 c  Prepared Mustard
1 tb Dry mustard
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Wine vinegar
1 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Thick steak sauce
1/2 c  Worchestershire sauce
1 cn Beer
1 tb Pepper
2 tb Oil
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Garlic Powder
Instructions:

Mix together well and put into jars.

Source/Author: Family Secret / typo Bob Shiells

Notes:
