Title: TEQUILA BEEF STEAKS
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
-Dorothy Cross TMPJ72B
4    New York strip steaks; (each
-8 to 10 oz., cut 1 to 1-1/2
-inch thick), fat trimmed
1/2 c  Tequila
2 tb Olive oil
1 tb Pepper
2 ts Grated lemon peel
1    Clove garlic; pressed or min
-Salt
With a damp paper towel, wipe steaks; put meat in a 1- gallon plastic
food bag. Add tequila, oil, pepper, lemon peel, and garlic; seal bag,
and turn to mix seasonings. Set bag in a bowl; chill at least 1 hour
or up to 1 day; turn bag over occasionally. Drain steaks and place on
a grill 4 to 6 inches above a solid bed of hot coals (you can hold
your hand at grill level only 2 to 3 seconds, or set gas barbecue at
this heat). Turn steaks to brown evenly; for medium-rare (cut to
test), cook 12 to 14 minutes. Transfer meat to plates; season to
taste with salt. Serves 4. Per serving: 385 calories (44 % from fat),
19 g fat, 116 mg sodium, and 291 mg cholesterol. Source: Sunset
Magazine - February, 1993 Reformatted for MealMaster by: CYGNUS,
