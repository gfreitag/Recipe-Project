Title: Allergy Cottage Cheese Pancakes
Categories: Allergy, Pcake/waffl, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Cottage cheese
2    Eggs, lightly beaten
2 tb Potato flour
1/4 c  Rice flour
1/4 ts Baking powder
1 tb Melted butter
Mix together the cottage cheese, eggs, flours, baking powder, and
butter. Fry on a hot, oiled griddle.  Serve with apple sauce.
