Title: Egg Punch
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 20 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
2    16 oz cans evaporated milk
4 c  Water
Sugar to taste
3 sm Cinnamon sticks
1 1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
4    Egg yolks -- well beaten
2 c  Vodka
In a saucepan, cook the first 5 ingredients together until the mixture
comes to the  boil. Lower the heat and cook 5 minutes more, stirring
constantly. Remove the mixture form theheat, cool it and put it
through a sieve. beat in the egg yolks and put the Stir in the vodka,
More can be used if desired. Serve well chilled in punch cups.
