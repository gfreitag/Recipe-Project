Title: Spinach Soup  <dairy>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
2 pk Frozen spinach
1/2 c  Water
3 tb Butter
3 tb Minced onion
3 tb Flour
6 c  Milk
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ds Nutmeg
Cook the spinach in the water for 5 minutes.  Force it through a food
mill. Melt the butter in a saucepan and cook the onion in it for 5
minutes. Blend in the flour and gradually add the milk.  Stir in the
spinach, salt, pepper and nutmeg.  Cook over
