Title: Dairy Noodle Ring
Categories: Jewish, Pasta, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 lb Cream cheese
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
3 c  Cooked medium noodles --
Drained
2 tb Dry bread crumbs
3 tb Melted butter
Cream the cheese with the eggs, sugar and salt.  Stir in the noodles.
Turn into a buttered 9-inch ring mold. Sprinkle with the bread crumbs
and butter.    Bake in a 375 degreeoven 40 minutes or until browned.
Unmold carefully.  Serves 6.    Recipe Source
