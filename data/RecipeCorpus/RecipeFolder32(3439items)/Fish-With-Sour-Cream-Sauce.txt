Title: Fish With Sour-Cream Sauce
Categories: Jewish, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Butter
1 c  Chopped onions
4 sl Pike or whitefish
1 ts Paprika
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Sour cream
Melt the butter in a skillet.  Brown the onions, then arrange the
fish over them.  Sprinkle with the paprika, salt and pepper. Add the
water. Cook over low heat 45 minutes. Stir the sour cream into the
sauce. Taste to correct seasoning and serve hot.  Serves 4. Recipe
Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by
Bantam Books, Inc.
