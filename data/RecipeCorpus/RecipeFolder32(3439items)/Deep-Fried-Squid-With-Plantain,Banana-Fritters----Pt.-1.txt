Title: Deep Fried Squid With Plantain/Banana Fritters  - Pt. 1
Categories: Seafood, Vietnamese
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Fresh squid
2 lg Garlic cloves -- minced
2 ts Nuoc Mam sauce -- optional
2 tb Chopped fresh dill
Black pepper
Cornstarch
Vegetable oil -- for deep
Frying
MMMMM----------------------NUOC CHAM SAUCE---------------------------
2 sm Garlic cloves -- crushed
1 sm Fresh red chili pepper --
Seeded and minced
1 oz Sugar
2 tb Fresh lime or lemon juice
1/4 c  Cider vinegar or wine
Vinegar
1/4 c  Nuoc Mam sauce
Maggi liquid seasoning
1 tb Water
MMMMM---------------------PLANTAIN FRITTERS--------------------------
1    Egg
2 tb Superfine sugar
1    Whole ripe plantain --
Peeled and mashed
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
Vegetable oil -- for frying
MMMMM---------------------------BATTER--------------------------------
1 c  All-purpose flour
pn Salt
pn Groung cinnamon
1    Egg -- beaten
2/3 c  Milk
* (Muc Chien Voi Chuoi Chien).

See part 2 for directions.
