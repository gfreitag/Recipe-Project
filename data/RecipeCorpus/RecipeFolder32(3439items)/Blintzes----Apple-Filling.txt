Title: Blintzes -  Apple Filling
Categories: Jewish
Yield: 1 servings
1    Egg white
1 1/2 c  Finely chopped apples
4 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
3 tb Brown sugar
3 tb Melted butter
Beat the egg white until it begins to stiffen.  Fold in the apples,
sugar and cinnamon.  Fill the pancakes and arrange in a buttered
baking pan. Sprinkle with the brown sugar and butter.    Bake in a
400 degree oven 20 minutes.  Makes about 18.    Recipe
