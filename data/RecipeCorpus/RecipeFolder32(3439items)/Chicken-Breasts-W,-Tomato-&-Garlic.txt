Title: Chicken Breasts W/ Tomato & Garlic
Categories: Poultry, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM----------------------CHICKEN BREASTS---------------------------
2 tb Olive Oil
3 c  Tomatoes -- Peel/ Seed
2    Whole Boneless Breasts --
Half
1    Bay Leaf
4    Cloves Garlic -- Minced
1 ts Thyme
1/2 c  Onions -- Finely Chopped
Salt and Pepper
1/2 c  Dry White Wine
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
Salt and pepper chicken.  Saute in oil for 3 to 4 minutes on each
side. Add garlic, onions, wine, tomatoes, bay leaf and thyme (if you
don't like thyme, use rosemary or basil). Bring to a boil, reduce
heat and simmer for 15 minutes, un- til chicken is don Discard bay
leaf before serving.
