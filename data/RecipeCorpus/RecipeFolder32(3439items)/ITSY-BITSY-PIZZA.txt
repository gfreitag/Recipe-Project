Title: ITSY BITSY PIZZA
Categories: Snacks, Kids
Yield: 1 servings
-BSC Custom Choice
-Measuring spoons
-A cheese grater
-A small bowl
1/2    English muffin; or
-whole pita bread
Tomato sauce
Cheese
* Spread 1 to 2 tablespoons tomato sauce on the muffin or pita. (You
may need a bit more for a pita.) * Grate 2 tablespoons cheese into
the small bowl. * Sprinkle the cheese onto the muffin or pita. Add
more
cheese if you like. * Heat in a toaster oven until the cheese melts.
Watch
carefully. Let cool before serving.
COMMENTS: "I want pizza!" Here's a kid-sized pizza he'll love!
