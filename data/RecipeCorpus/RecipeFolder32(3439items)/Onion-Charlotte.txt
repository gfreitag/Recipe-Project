Title: Onion Charlotte
Categories: Jewish, Pie/tart, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
4 c  Diced onions
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Milk
3    Egg yolks
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 c  Light cream
1/3 c  Bread crumbs
Cook the onions, butter and milk over low heat for 10 minutes.  Drain
and turn into a buttered 9-inch pie plate.    Beat the egg yolks,
salt, pepper and cream together. Pour over the onions and sprinkle
with the bread crumbs.    Bake in a 350 degree oven
