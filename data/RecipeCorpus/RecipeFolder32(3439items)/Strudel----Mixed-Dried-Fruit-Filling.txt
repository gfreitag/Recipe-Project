Title: Strudel -  Mixed Dried Fruit Filling
Categories: Jewish, Pastry, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Prunes -- pitted
1 lb Dates -- pitted
1 c  Candied mixed fruit
1    Whole Lemon
2 c  Walnuts
4 tb Sugar
2 ts Cinnamon
Grind the prunes, dates, candied fruit, lemon and walnuts in a food
chopper.  Divide the strudel dough (two recipes for strudel dough are
included in this database) in two. Spread the rolled dough with oil
and use half the fruit mixture for each.  Roll up, brush with oil and
sprinkle with a mixture of sugar and cinnamon.    Recipe Source: THE
ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam
