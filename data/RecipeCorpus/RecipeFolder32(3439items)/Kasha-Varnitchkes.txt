Title: Kasha Varnitchkes
Categories: Jewish, Pasta, Nuts/grains
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Minced onions
1/3 c  Chicken fat
2 c  Cooked kasha -- * see note
3 c  Cooked broad noodles --
Drained
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
* Note:  The recipe for "Kasha" is included in this database.
Brown the onions in the fat.  Combine with the kasha, noodles, salt
and pepper.  If you have any 'grebenes' (therecipe for "Rendered Fat
And Grebenes" is included in this database), add some
