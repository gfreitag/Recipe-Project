Title: Lemon Broccoli Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------LIZ JONES VXRF36B--------------------------
1    Lemon
1 tb Vegetable oil
4    Skinned/boned chicken breast
-halves
10 oz Cream of broccoli soup
1/4 c  Milk
1/8 ts Pepper
1. Cut 4 thin lemon slices; squeeze 2 teaspoons juice from remaining
lemon. Set aside. 2. In skillet, in hot oil, cook chicken 10 minutes
or until browned.  Remove; set aside. Spoon off fat. 3. In skillet,
combine soup, milk, reserved lemon juice and pepper. Heat to boiling.
Return chicken to skillet; top with lemon slices. 4. Cover; cook over
low heat 5 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink, stirring often.
Garnish with fresh thyme,  if desired.
