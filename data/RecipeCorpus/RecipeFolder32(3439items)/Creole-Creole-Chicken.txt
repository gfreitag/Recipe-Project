Title: Creole Creole Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Ethnic
Yield: 4 servings
JEAN ALLEN
1/4 lb Cooked ham
1 ea Fryer chicken cut up
1 ts Salt
1 ea Green pepper -- chopped
1/2 lb Polish sausage -- sliced
6 ea Green onions -- chopped
3 c  Cooked rice
16 oz Can tomato paste
Several drops of bottled
Hot
(GRDG72B)
In crockpot combine chicken, pepper, onions, tomatoes, tomato paste ,
ham, salt and hot pepper sauce. Cover and cook on low 4-5 hours. Turn
control to high. Add sausage and cookedrice. Cover and cook on high
20 minutes. Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.00
