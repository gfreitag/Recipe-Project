Title: Double Chocolate Treasures
Categories: Tasteofhome, Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Flour
3/4 c  Cocoa
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Butter, softened
2/3 c  White sugar
2/3 c  Light brown sugar, packed
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
1 pk 10 oz large semisweet
Chocolate pieces or 2 - 12
Oz packages small pieces
3/4 c  Chopped pecans or other nuts
Preheat oven to 350F. In a small bowl, combine flour, cocoa, soda,
and salt and set aside. In a large mixing bowl, cream butter with
sugars, vanilla, and then add eggs. Mix flour mixture with butter
mixture. Add chocolate and nuts.

Bake on an ungreased cookie sheet for 9-12 minutes.

Very rich and very good.
