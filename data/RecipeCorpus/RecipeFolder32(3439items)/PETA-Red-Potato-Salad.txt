Title: PETA Red Potato Salad
Categories: Salads, Potatoes, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
12    To 15 unpeeled red potatoes
-cut into bite size wedges
4    Stalks celery; sliced
1 lg Green pepper; chopped
6    Green onions; chopped
2 c  Italian dressing
;garlic salt and pepper
;to taste
Boil the potatoes for about 15 mins or until tender.  In a large
bowl, mix all the ingredients while the potatoes are still hot. Chill
for 2-3 hours. From The Compassionate Cook by PETA and Ingrid Newkirk
