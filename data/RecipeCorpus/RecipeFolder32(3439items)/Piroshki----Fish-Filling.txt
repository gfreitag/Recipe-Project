Title: Piroshki -  Fish Filling
Categories: Jewish, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Cooked flaked fish
4 tb Grated onion
2    Hard-boiled egg yolks
4 tb Sour cream
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Mix all of the ingredients together.  Place filling on dough.
