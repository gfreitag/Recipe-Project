Title: Madras (hot) Curry Mixture
Categories: Herbs/spice, Curries, Indian
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Ground chilies (not chili
-powder)
8 1/2 ts Ground coriander seeds
1/8 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Ground black mustard seeds
1 ts Ground fenugreed seeds
1 ts Ground black pepper
1 2/3 ts Ground tumeric
4 ea Who curry leaves
Mix together all ground dry spices; then proceed according to recipe
instructions; adding the curry leaves directly to the cooking pot.
