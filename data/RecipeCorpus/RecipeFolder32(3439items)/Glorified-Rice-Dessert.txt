Title: Glorified Rice Dessert
Categories: Desserts, Rice, Fruit, Tasteofhome, Puddings
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Rice, regular; cooked, cold
1 c  Pineapple, cubed
1 c  Marshmallows, miniature
1 c  Apple, chopped
1 ts Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Cream, whipping; whipped
Strawberries, fresh; opt.
Combine all ingredients except whipped cream and strawberries.  Mix
well; chill thoroughly.  Fold in whipped cream before serving.
Garnish with strawberries, if desired.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, April, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy
Coleman.
