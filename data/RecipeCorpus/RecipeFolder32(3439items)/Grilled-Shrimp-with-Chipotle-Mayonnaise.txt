Title: Grilled Shrimp with Chipotle Mayonnaise
Categories: Seafood, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
8    Bamboo skewers
-soaked in water
32    Shrimp (small-to-med-sized)
-peeled and deveined
1 tb Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2    Recipe Chipotle Mayonnaise
1 tb Parsley, chopped
After preparing the shrimp (heads-on if you like), thread 4 shrimp pn
each skewer about 1-inch apart, making sure the shrimp stay flat.
Brush shrimp with black peppercorns and add salt and pepper to taste.
Grill shrimp over a hot fire about 1 minute on each side. Serve
topped with Chipotle Mayonnaise and chopped parsley.
