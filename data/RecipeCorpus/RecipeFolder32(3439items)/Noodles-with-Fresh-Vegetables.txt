Title: Noodles with Fresh Vegetables
Categories: Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Whole wheat or buckwheat
-noodles
4 c  To 6 c vegetables: peppers,
-asparagus, broccoli,
-zucchini, snow peas,
Cauliflower, carrot
-(whatever you are allowed)
2 tb Olive oil
Steam vegetables briefly until tender-crisp.  Combine with hot
noodles and olive oil.

Origin: Yeast Free Feast. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Oct/94.
