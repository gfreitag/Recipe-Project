Title: Lemon Herb Barbecued Shrimp HBWK07A
Categories: Seafood, Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Catsup
1/2 c  White vinegar
1/4    Lge lemon - seeded and
Finely minced, incldg rind
1 tb Gravy Mstr or Ktchn Bouquet
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Ground coriander
1/2 ts Ground cumin seed
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/8 ts Paprika
Cayenne pepper to taste
2 lb Lge shrimp (20 -25 count) -
Shelled and deveined
Bamboo skewers - soaked in
Water for 30 minutes
Combine all ingredients excep  shrimp in a small saucepan; simmer 15
minutes,  stirring  frequently; thread shrimp, interlapping, on
skewers; grill  4 to 6 inches from hot glowing coals, 3 to 5 minutes,
turning and brushing frequently with lemon mixture; DO NOT OVERCOOK!
Makes 6 to 8 servings. Posted by Chuck Ozburn. MM:MK VMXV03A.
