Title: Fettuccine W/Cream, Basil, And Romano  Sss
Categories: Italian
Yield: 4 servings
Sherilyn Schamber
2/3 c  Fresh basil -- chpd
sl Bacon
1 lb Fettuccine
Green onions -- chpd
Salt and pepper
1 c  Whipping cream
Additional cheese
1 c  Romano or parmesan --
Grated
Cook bacon, remove and crumble.  Add green onions to skillet, and stir
until softened, about 1 min.  Add cream and simmer until beginning to
thicken.  Mix in 1 cup cheese and basil. Meanwhile, cook fettuccine;
drain. Add sauce and stir to coat.  Season with salt and pepper. Pass
additional cheese when serving. Source: Bon Appetit Converted by
MMCONV vers. 1.00
