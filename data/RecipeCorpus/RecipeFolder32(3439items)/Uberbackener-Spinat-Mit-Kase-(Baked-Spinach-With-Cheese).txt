Title: Uberbackener Spinat Mit Kase (Baked Spinach With Cheese)
Categories: Aust/german, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Spinach -- Fresh *
1/2 lb Emmenthaler Cheese --
Grated
1/4 lb Butter
1 ts Paprika
1    Onion; Large -- Diced
1/8 ts Nutmeg
2    Garlic; Cloves -- Minced
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Salt
*  Wash and clean spinach of sand.  Dry.
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++
Cut spinach into strips.  In a large Dutch oven, heat butter until
bubbly. Add onion and garlic, saute for 2 to 3 minutes.  Add spinach.
Sprinkle with salt.  Cover and steam for 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
Grease an ovenproof casserole. Sprinkle half the cheese over the
bottom of the casserole. Add the spinach.  Sprinkle with Top with
remaining cheese. Bake at 360 degrees F about 20 minutes or until
cheese bubbles.
