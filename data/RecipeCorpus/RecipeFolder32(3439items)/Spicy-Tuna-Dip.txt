Title: Spicy Tuna Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Tuna (13-oz)
6 1/2 oz Jar or can jalapeno chiles
Chopped
1    Onion -- minced
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Chopped cilantro
Tortilla chips (optional)
Mix tuna with chopped jalapeno chiles and jalapeno liquid. Add onion
to tuna mixture. Stir in mayonnaise until consistency is mushy.
Sprinkle cilantro on top and serve with tortilla chips, if desired.
Makes 1 1/2 cups
