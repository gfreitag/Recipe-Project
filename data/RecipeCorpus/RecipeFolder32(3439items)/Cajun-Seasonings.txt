Cajun Seasoning for Fish

I like the fish one best - it is hot!  Beware the fumes, they can
clear your sinuses instantly! Try with swordfish - outstanding!
Butter the pan, Rub lots of spice on the fish and smoke it - about
2-3 min each side til black. WOW!! Enjoy!
