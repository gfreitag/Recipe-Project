Title: Spinach Bake
Categories: Rice, Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 casserole
3/4 c  Uncooked brown rice
1 1/2 c  Water
Sea salt
1 ea Onion, peeled and chopped
2 tb Oil
16 oz Frozen spinach, thawed (or
-two pounds fresh, cooked
-and drained)
1/2 lb Dairy-free cheese, grated
-(available in natural foods
-stores)
x  Freshly ground black pepper
1/4 c  Whole wheat bread crumbs
Put the rice in a medium saucepan with water and salt.  Bring to a
boil. Cover, reduce the heat and cook 45 minutes.

While the rice is cooking, fry onion in oil for 10 minutes over low
heat. Then add spinach and remove from heat.  Add this mixture to the
cooked rice, together with three-quarters of the cheese.  Add pepper
to taste.

Turn the mixture into a shallow-oven-proof dish.  Sprinkle the bread
crumbs and remaining cheeese on top.  Cook for 30 minutes at 350
degrees.

Recipe by Jody Mykins, Sojourner House, Rochester, NY, as seen in the
Corydon Ireland column in THE OLYMPIAN, 6/28/95.
