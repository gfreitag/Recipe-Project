Title: Creole Crab Fingers
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Jumbo crab claws
3    Eggs
1 pt Milk
1 1/2 c  Corn flour
1 c  Flour
1 ts Red pepper
2 ts Salt
1 tb Onion powder
1 1/2 tb Garlic powder
3 tb Paprika
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1/4 c  Green onions -- chopped
Fine
Make egg wash with eggs and milk. Mix remaining ingredients except
crab fingers. Put fingers in dry, then egg wash and then again in
dry. Let rest for about 30 minutes or so. Fry to brown in hot oil.
Serve with a horseradish sauce.
