Title: Anti-Cancer, Anti-Aging Hamburger
Categories: Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 4 patties
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 c  Textured vegetable protein
-granules*
2 tb Cold water
x  Salt and pepper, to taste
*  A soy protein sold in health food stores and some supermarkets.

Put all in a large bowl.  Knead until combined.  Form into four
patties, grill, broil or fry.  Serve on whole-grain buns with sliced
onion for extra antioxidant protection.

Per patty:  241 calories, 24g protein, 2.5g carbohydrates, 2g fiber,
14.9g fat (5.9g saturated), 63mg sodium.

Recipe from:  John Weisburger, as adapted from Jean Carper's new book,
"Stop Aging Now."
