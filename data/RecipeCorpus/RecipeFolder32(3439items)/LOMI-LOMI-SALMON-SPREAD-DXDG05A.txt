Title: LOMI LOMI SALMON SPREAD DXDG05A
Categories: Asian, Appetizers, Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
1    8 oz pkg cream cheese
1    3 oz pkg smoked salmon,
-cut into small pieces
1 ts Lemon juice
1 tb Chopped onion
1 tb Green onion top thinly slcd
1. Combine cream cheese salmon, lemon juice, and onion 2. Mix well,
sprinkle with green onions 3. Serve with crackers or melba toast
