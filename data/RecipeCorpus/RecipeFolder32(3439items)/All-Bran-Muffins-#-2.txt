Title: All-Bran Muffins # 2
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 12 muffins
2 tb Shortening
1/4 c  Sugar
1    Egg,well beaten
1 c  Sour milk
1 c  Kellogg's all-bran
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
Cream the shortening and sugar,add egg and sour milk.Add all-bran and
let soak until most of the moistkure is taken up.Sift floour with
baking powder,soda and salt and add to first mixture,stisrring only
until flour disappears.Fill greased muffin tins 2/3 full and bake in
a moderate oven 400 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes.
