Title: Cold Fruit Soup  <dairy>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Pitted plums
1 c  Pitted sour red cherries
1 c  Sliced peaches
6 qt Water
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Cornstarch
6 tb Sour cream
Combine the fruit, water, sugar, salt and cinnamon in a saucepan.
Bring to a boil and cook over low heat 15 minutes.  Force through a
food mill.
