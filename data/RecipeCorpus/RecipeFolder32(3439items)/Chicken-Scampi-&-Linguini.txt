Title: Chicken Scampi & Linguini
Categories: Poultry, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Linguini
2    Whole chicken breasts;
- boneless, cut 1" pieces
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Oil
2    Cloves garlic; minced
2 bn Broccoli; chopped
1 lg Red pepper; cut into 1"
-chunks
1 ts Salt
1    Lemon; juice of
Parmesan cheese
Put on water for linguini. Heat butter and oil in large skillet, add
garlic and saute 2 minutes. Add chicken and cook, stirring
constantly, until golden. Push to one side, add broccoli and red
pepper. Cook 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, sprinkle with
lemon juice and cook 2 minutes. When water comes to boil, cook
linguini, then drain pasta. Add chicken and vegetable sauce to pasta
and toss to coat evenly. Serve with grated parmesan cheese
