Title: Broccoli & Rice
Categories: Casseroles, Meats, Rice, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Minute rice
1 1/2 c  Fresh broccoli
OR
10 oz Frozen broccoli
1 cn 16 oz. cream of mushroom
-soup
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Velveeta cheese, diced
1/2 c  Butter
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped celery, optional
Dice velveeta cheese so it will melt easier. Chop the broccoli into
bit size pieces or if frozen, pour boiling water over to thaw. Chop
onion. Chop celery if desired. Cook rice according to directions. Add
cheese and soup to hot rice. Prepare broccoli. Brown ground beef.
Saute onion in the butter. Combine ingredients and pour into greased
casserole. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes. Use sausage instead of
ground beef, if desired. Regular rice may be used.
