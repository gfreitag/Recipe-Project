Title: Baked Acorn Squash
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2    Acorn squash
1/4 c  Melted butter or fat
1/4 c  Corn syrup
1/2 ts Salt
Cut the squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds and fibers.
Prick the flesh in several places with a fork. Place in a baking pan
cut side down.  Bake in a 375 degree oven 30 minutes.    Mix together
the butter or fat, corn syrup and salt. Turn the squash over and
divide the mixture among the four halv 4. Recipe Source: THE ART OF
JEWISH COOKING by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc.
