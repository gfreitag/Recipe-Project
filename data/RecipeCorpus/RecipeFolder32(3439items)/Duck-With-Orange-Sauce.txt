Title: Duck With Orange Sauce
Categories: Jewish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
6 lb Duck
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3 tb Flour
2 c  Orange juice
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Beef broth
2 tb Currant jelly
2    Oranges -- segmented
Singe the duck to remove pin feathers; wash and dry. Season with the
salt and pepper.  Place on a rack in a roasting pan.  Roast in 350
degree oven 3 hours, or until tender andbrowned.    Measure 2
tablespoons of the fat from the roasting pan and pour it
