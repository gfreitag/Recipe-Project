Title: Biersuppe (Beer Soup) & Buttermilchsuppe (Buttermilk Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Aust/german
Yield: 1 recipe
2 c  Sweet milk
2 ts Cornstarch
1/2 c  Sugar
3    Egg yolks
3    Egg whites
2 c  Beer
Scald milk.  Mix cornstarch and sugar, add beaten egg yolds and blend
thoroughly before stirring slowly into the milk.  In a separate pan
scald the beer.  Combine with milk mixture.  To the beaten whites add
1 tablespoon sugar and heap by tablespoons on top of soup.

BUTTERMILK SOUP - Follow recipe using 4 cups buttermilk in place of
sweet milk and beer.
