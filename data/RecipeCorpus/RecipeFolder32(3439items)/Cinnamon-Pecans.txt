Title: Cinnamon Pecans
Categories: Appetizers, Nuts/grains
Yield: 20 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg White
4 c  Pecan Halves
Preheat oven to 325 Combine sugar, cinnamon and salt in small bowl.
Whisk egg white in large bowl until frothy.  Add pecans to egg white
and toss to coat.  Add sugar mixture and toss thoroughly. Spread
pecans in a single layer on heavy large cookie sheet Cool. (Can be
prepared 2 weeks ahead. Store in airtight container.)
