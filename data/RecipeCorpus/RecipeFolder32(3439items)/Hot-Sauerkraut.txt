Title: Hot Sauerkraut
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Fat or butter
3/4 c  Diced onions
1 1/2 lb Sauerkraut -- drained
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ts Paprika
1 ts Sugar
1/2 c  Water
Melt the fat in a saucepan and brown the onions lightly. Add the
sauerkraut, pepper, paprika, sugar and water.  Cover loosely and cook
over low heat 1 hour.  Serves 4 to 6.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF
JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, Inc.
