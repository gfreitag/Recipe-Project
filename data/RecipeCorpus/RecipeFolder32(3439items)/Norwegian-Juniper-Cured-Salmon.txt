Title: Norwegian Juniper-Cured Salmon
Categories: West europe, Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Salmon fillet
1/2 c  Juniper berries
2 tb Salt
4 tb Sugar
MMMMM-----------------------MUSTARD SAUCE----------------------------
1/4 c  Dijon-style mustard
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 tb Olive oil
1/2 tb Dill, finely chopped
Wash the salmon, pat dry and pull out any bones.  Crush juniper
berries in a food processor or blender.  Mix salt and sugar together.
Rub the salt and sugar mix into both sides of the salmon and place
flat, skin side down, in a pan.

Spread berries over the top side, cover with foil and place weights
(several cans of food or a small board with a can or two) on top.
Refrigerate for 48 hours, turning several times.  Keep weight over
salmon. Scrape berries off, cut in thin slices and serve with mustard
sauce.

For the sauce;  Blend mustard, sugar, oil and dill together.

Recipe:  Olympic Pantry in Lillehammer, Norway
