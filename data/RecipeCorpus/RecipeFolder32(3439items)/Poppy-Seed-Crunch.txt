Title: Poppy-Seed Crunch
Categories: Jewish, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
2    Egg yolks
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 1/2 c  Flour -- sifted
1/8 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
2    Egg whites
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Ground poppy seeds
