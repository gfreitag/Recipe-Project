Title: MURRAY'S VOO DOO RIB SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Ketchup
1 c  Chili Sauce
1 ea Whole Spanish Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Molasses
1 ts Corriander
1 ts Cummin
1 ts Chili Peppers
1 ts Tobasco
1/2 c  Honey
Pepper Garlic

Combine all ingredients. Simmer the ribs for 2 or 3 hours, then soak
overnight in the sauce. Bake or barbecue the ribs.
