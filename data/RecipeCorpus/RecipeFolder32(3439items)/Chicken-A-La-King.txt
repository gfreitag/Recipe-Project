Title: Chicken A La King
Categories: Jewish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Chicken fat
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
4 tb Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 3/4 c  Chicken stock
2 c  Diced cooked chicken
1    Pimento -- chopped
2 tb Sherry
Heat the fat in a saucepan; cook the mushrooms in it for 5 minutes.
Stir in the flour, salt and pepper.  Gradually add the stock,
stirring steadily until mixture reaches the boiling point.  Cook over
low heat 5 minutes. Add the chicken, pimento and sher
