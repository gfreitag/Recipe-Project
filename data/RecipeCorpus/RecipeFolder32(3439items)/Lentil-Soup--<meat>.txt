Title: Lentil Soup  <meat>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Lentils
2 1/2 qt Water
2    Onions -- diced
2 tb Fat
2    Carrots -- diced
1 tb Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Bay leaf
4    Frankfurters -- sliced
Wash and drain the lentils.  Combine with water; bring to a boil and
cook over medium heat 1 hour.    Brown the onions in the fat and add
to the lentils with the carrots, salt, pepper and bay leaf.  Cook
over low heat 2 hours.  Discard the bay leaf and rub mixture through
a food mill. Return to the saucepan.  A
