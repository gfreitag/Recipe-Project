Title: Candied Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
6    Sweet potatoes
1/4 c  Butter or fat
1/2 c  Dark corn syrup
2 tb Orange juice
1/4 c  Brown sugar
Cook the unpeeled potatoes in boiling water for 15 minutes.  Peel and
cut in half lengthwise.  Heat the butter or fat in a baking dish and
add the corn syrup, orange juice, sugar and sweet potatoes.    Bake
in a 350 degree oven 45 minutes or until tender,
