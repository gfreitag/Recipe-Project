Title: Steamer Polish Sausage Supper
Categories: Potatoes, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2    Potatoes; peeled and cut
-into 1/2-inch pieces
1 lb Smoked polish sausage
16 oz Can sauerkraut
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Caraway seed
Place potatoes in steamer basket. Add 1/2 cup water to reservoir,
cover and steam from 10 to 12 minutes.

Slice sausage into 1/2-inch pieces. Place sausage in rice bowl. top
with steamed potatoes. Cover with sauerkrat and sprinkle seasonings
on top. Add 2 cups water to reservoir, cover and steam from 38 to 39
minutes.

Serves 4

Total steaming time: 48 to 52 minutes.

Source: Rival Automatic Steamer And Rice Cooker Booklet Typos by
Dorothy Flatman 1995
