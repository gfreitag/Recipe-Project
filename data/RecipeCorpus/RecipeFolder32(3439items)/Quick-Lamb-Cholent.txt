Title: Quick Lamb Cholent
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Dried lima beans
2    Lamb shanks
2    Onions -- sliced
3 tb Fat
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Ginger
3 c  Water
Soak the beans overnight in water to cover.  Bring to a boil and cook
30 minutes.  Drain.    Brown the lamb and onions in the fat.  Add the
garlic, salt, pepper, ginger and water.  Cover and cook over low heat
2 1/2 hours or until lamb and beans are tende
