Title: Apple Fritters
Categories: Jewish, Desserts, Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
12 sl Peeled apples -- thin
Slices
5 tb Sugar
2 tb Brandy
1 1/2 c  Flour -- sifted
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1    Egg
1 c  Milk
2 tb Melted butter
Butter -- for frying
Sprinkle the apple slices with 4 tablespoons of the sugar and the
brandy. Set aside.    Sift the flour, salt and baking powder into a
bowl. Beat the egg, milk and melted butter together and add to the
flour mixture gradually, beating until smooth.    Heat a little
butter in a large skillet.  The next operation must be donequickly.
Pour about 1 tablespoon of the batter into the pan, place an apple
