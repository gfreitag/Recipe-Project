Title: GOOSEBERRY PIE
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
3 1/2 c  Gooseberries
2 tb Tapioca - minute
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Orange - grated rind of
2 tb Butter - melted
Mix together and let stand 15 minutes. Line 9-inch pastry tin with
pastry, add fruit mixture, cover, slit in several spots to allow
steam to escape. Bake in hot oven, 450 F. 15 minutes and decrease to
moderate, 350 F. and bake 30 minutes longer.

Source: Mrs. Pearl Tidrice, Tri-County Grange, Tuscarawas County, OH
