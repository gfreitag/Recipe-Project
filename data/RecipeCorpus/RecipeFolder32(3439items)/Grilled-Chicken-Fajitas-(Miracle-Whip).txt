Title: Grilled Chicken Fajitas (Miracle Whip)
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
1/4 c  Lime juice
3 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1 ts Dried Oregano leaves,crushed
1 ts Ground Cumin
1 1/2 lb Chicken Breasts, boned,
- and skinned
1 ea Green, red, & yellow pepper,
- quartered
1 lg Onion, cut in 6 wedges
4 ea Flour tortillas (8"),warmed
Sour Cream
Salsa
Mix salad dressing, juice, garlic and seasonings; reserve 1/4
cup. Pour remaining mixture over chicken.  Refrigerate at least 20
minutes to marinate.
Remove chicken from marinade; place on grill over medium coals.
Grill, covered, 8 minutes.
Toss vegetables with reserved 1/4 cup salad dressing mixture;
place on grill.  Continue cooking chicken and vegetables 8 - 10
minutes longer or until chicken is cooked through, turning chicken
and vegetables occasionally.
Slice chicken and peppers into strips.  Fill each tortilla with
chicken and vegetables.  Top with sour cream and salsa: roll up.
Prep Time: 15 minutes plus refrigeration
Grilling Time: 20 minutes
