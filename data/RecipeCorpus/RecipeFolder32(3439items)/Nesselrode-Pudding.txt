Title: Nesselrode Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Guestchefs
Yield: 8 servings
5    Eggs
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 tb Gelatin
1/4 c  Water
2 c  Whipping cream
2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Sweetened chestnut puree
5 oz Chopped candied chestnuts
1    Pie shell
WARM THE EGGS IN A DOUBLE BOILER. Whip them with an electric mixer,
and gradually beat in the sugar. Beat until the mixture is thickened
and forms soft peaks. Soften the gelatin in the water and then
dissolve over hot water. Beat the cream to soft peaks. Fold the
gelatin and vanilla into the eggs and then fold in the whipped cream.
