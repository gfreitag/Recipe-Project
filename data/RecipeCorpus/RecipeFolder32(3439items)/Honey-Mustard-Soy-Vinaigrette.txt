Title: Honey Mustard-Soy Vinaigrette
Categories: Salads, Salsa/sauce, Guestchefs
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Honey mustard
2 tb Soy sauce
6 tb White wine vinegar
3/4 c  Vegetable oil
3 tb Chopped fresh dill --
Optional
Serve this with shredded Savoy cabbage, thinly sliced zucchini and
diced tomatoes, or with any other salad you think it will work in.

IN A MEDIUM-SIZE BOWL, blend the honey mustard, soy sauce and vinegar
until smooth. Whisk in the oil in a slow, steady stream. Add the
dill, if desired.

Makes 1 1/2 Cups JOANNA PRUESS - PRODIGY GUEST CHEFS COOKBOOK
