Title: Fruit Roasted Pheasant - United States
Categories: Fruit, Game, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
1    Pheasant-whole
Salt & pepper to taste
1 c  Wild rice
1 c  Apricot juice
From : Sallie Krebs, Thu 06 Jul 95 04:00, Area: COOKING

Place pheasant in foil in a baking pan. Rub inside and out with salt
and pepper. Cover with the uncooked rice. Pour juice over all and
wrap tightly in the foil. Bake in a preheated 325 oven until pheasant
is done and tender.
