Title: Steamed Open Dumplings
Categories: Breakfast, Chinese, Guestchefs
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk Wonton skins -- (about 30-35
Skins
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
3/4 lb Ground pork
2 tb Minced Smithfield ham --
=OR=- Prosciutto
1 tb Light soy sauce
2 ts Rice wine or dry sherry
1 1/2 tb Finely chopped scallions
1 ts Finely chopped ginger root
1 ts Sesame oil
1    Egg -- beaten
1 ts Granulated sugar
COMBINE THE FILLING ingredients and mix them together well. Spoon a
generous portion of filling onto each wonton skin. Bring up the sides
and press them around the filling mixture. Tap the dumpling on the
bottom to make a flat base. The top should be wide KEN HOM - PRODIGY
