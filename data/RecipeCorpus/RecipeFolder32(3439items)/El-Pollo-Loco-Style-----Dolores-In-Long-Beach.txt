Dolores In Long Beach
Title: El Pollo Loco-Style
Categories: Main dish, Ethnic
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Water
1/3 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  White wine vinegar
1 c  Garlic -- crushed
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Tabasco
1 tb Lemon juice
Cilantro -- chopped
Salt -- to taste
Combine all ingredients in a glass dish and marinate chicken,
covered, 8 hours or overnight.   FROM: DOLORES GILLETTE (SRDS42A)
