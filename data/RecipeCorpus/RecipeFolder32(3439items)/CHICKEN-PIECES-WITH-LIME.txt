Title: CHICKEN PIECES WITH LIME
Categories: Poultry, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
Chicken, cut up
2 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Thyme
2 tb Lemon juice
Pepper
2    Limes,cut into wedges
Place chicken pieces in an oiled baking dish, and mix with olive
oil,thyme, juice and pepper. Bake at 375 F for 45 minutes to and
hour, or until chicken is golden and tender. Garnish with wedges of
lime so guests can soak his own chicken.
