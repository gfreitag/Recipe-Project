Title: Aunt Dodie's Butter Cream Cake^
Categories: Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Milk
1 ts Lemon extract
2 1/4 c  Flour, sifted
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
3    Egg whites, well beaten
Cream together butter and sugar. Add milk a little at a time. Add
lemon. Gradually beat in flour and baking soda. Carefully fold in
beaten egg whites. Turn into greased and floured pan and bake in a
moderate oven until done.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
