Title: PETA Marinated Pea Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Distilled white vinegar
3/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Water
1    Can (15-17oz) can petite
-peas, drained
16 oz Can corn kernals; drained
1 c  Celery; chopped
1 c  Onions; chopped
Combine the vinegar, sugar and water in a small saucepan.  Bring to a
boil over med heat.  Boil for 5 mins, then remove from the heat and
cool completely.

In a med bowl, combine the remaining ingredients.  Pour the cooled
vinegar mixture over the vegetables and chill 4 hours or overnight.
