Title: Mother's Port Wine Sauce
Categories: Aust/german, Desserts, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 recipe
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Corn startch
ds Salt
1 c  Boiling water
1    Stick cinnamon (opt)
1/2 c  Port wine
1 tb Butter
Juice of 1/2 lemon
2    Or 3 lemonslices
Several dashes nutmeg
Few drops red cake coloring
Mix sugar, starch, salt and water.  Cook and stir well.  When
mixture thickens add butter, wine, lemon juice, nutmeg, lemon slices
and cinnamon stick.  Stir well.

This sauce may be served over custards, puddings, or plain cakes.
It is better if you let it set for several hours.

~ Mrs. D. J. Hoester, Sr.
