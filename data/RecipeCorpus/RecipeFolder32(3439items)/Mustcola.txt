Title: Mustcola
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Sausage, Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
2 tb Bacon drippings
2 c  Onion, chopped
2 md Sized green peppers, seeded
And chopped
1/4 lb Mushrooms, thinly sliced
3/4 lb Ground round
1 ts Bulk sausage spice
1 ts Chili powder
3/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 lb Can of tomatoes
1/4 lb Elbow macaroni, cook & drain
1/4 lb Bulk sausage
Heat bacon drippings in a large kettle or Dutch oven.  Add onions and
green peppers, stir fry until tender.  Add mushrooms and stir fry 5
minutes; add beef and sausage; stir fry, breaking up clumps with a
spoon, until meat is no longer pink, stir in chili powder, salt and
pepper. Add tomatoes; simmer, uncovered, about 30 minutes.  Stir in
macaroni, heat just until bubbly hot. Randy Rigg
