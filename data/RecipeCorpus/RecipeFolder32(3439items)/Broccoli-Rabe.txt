Title: Broccoli Rabe
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Guestchefs
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Broccoli rabe; washed --
lg Stems removed
Finely chopped
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
1 sm Onion -- finely chopped
1/4 c  Olive oil
Broccoli rabe goes under many names, such as broccoli rape (pronounced
"rap-ay"), rape and cima di rape. It is a peppery-tasting broccoli
that works well with roasted or grilled meats.

SAUTE THE GARLIC AND ONION in oil until softened. Add the broccoli
rabe, saute 2-to-3 minutes, cover and steam 4-to-5 minutes.
