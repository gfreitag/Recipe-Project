Title: Borscht (Beet Soup)
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
1 lg Onion -- chopped coarsley
10    Milliliters
Oil for cooking
2 lb Beets
1/4 c  Wine vinegar
1 c  Tomato juice
2 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
Juice of 1 lemon
1/2 c  Fresh dill
Sour cream
Garlic
Saute the onion and garlic in oil until golden. Meanwhile, peel and
slice the beets and then place them in a saucepan with the onion and
garlic. Cover them with water and simmer gently, covered, for about
40 minutes until the beets are tender. Place the mixture in a blender
and puree, add the wine vine
