Title: Quick Cake^
Categories: Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Soft butter
1 1/3 c  Brown sugar
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
1 3/4 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 lb Dates
Beat well butter and sugar. Add eggs and milk. Add remaining
ingredients and beat everything together. Beat for 3 minutes. Bake
45-50 minutes in a moderate oven. From Mrs. Louie Johnson

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
