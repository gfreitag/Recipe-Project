Title: Ginger Orange Squash
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Frozen cubed squash
2 tb Orange juice
1/4 ts Each salt and ginger
2 tb Butter
1 ts Grated orange rind
pn Each nutmeg and pepper.
In suacepan, bring small amount of water to boil; add squash.  Reduce
heat to simmer and cook for 6 minutes or until tender; drain. Mash
squash; beat in butter, orange juice and rind, salt, ginger, nutmeg
and pepper. Makes 4 servings.
