Title: Baked Fish (Psito Psari)
Categories: Seafood, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
MARY SUE DAY
1    Whole grouper -- about 3 lbs
3/4 c  Olive oil
Juice of 2 lemons
1    Lemon -- sliced
3 lg Onions -- finely chopped
20 ml Garlic -- finely chopped
1/2 c  -water
Salt & pepper to taste
MMMMM--------------------------STUFFING-------------------------------
1/2 ts Parsley -- chopped
1/2 ts Oregano.
(RBCK34E)

pepper and let stand 20 min. Drain & place in baking dish & stuff
