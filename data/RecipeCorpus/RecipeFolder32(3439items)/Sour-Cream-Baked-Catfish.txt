Title: Sour Cream Baked Catfish
Categories: Game, Fish
Yield: 1 servings
6 ea Dressed catfish
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 1/2 tb Flour
1 pt Sour cream
3 tb Olives, stuffed, chopped
1 ts Celery salt
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Thyme
1 tb Dillweed, fresh
1 x  Lemon wedges
1 x  Parsley
A Finalist in the 5th National Farm-Raised Catfish Cooking Contest
Place cleaned, washed, dried fish in a greased baking dish. Combine
mayonnaise and flour; add sour cream, olives, celery salt, paprika,
pepper and thyme. Spread sauce over fish. Sprinkle with dillweed.
Bake in 350 degree oven about 25 minutes, or until fish flakes when
touched with a fork. Apply garnish. Also for: Catfish, Any Fish .....
Recipe date: 12/11/87
