Title: PIZZA SAUCE #5
Categories: Pizza, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Tomato puree
1/4 pt Basil
3 c  Water
1 1/4 pt Black pepper
3 pt Cooking oil
1/4 pt Oregano
1    Glove garlic (chopped)
1 pt Grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Raw onion (chopped)
1 pt Brown sugar
Combine ingredients in a 2 qt sauce pan and cook for 30-40 minutes.
Makes enough sauce for 5 12" Pizzas.
