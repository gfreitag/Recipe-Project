Place the rice in a bowl and cover it with water, not the 2 1/4 cups
to soak.  Chop the onion coarsely, then clean the carrots and chop
them into small pieces. Peel and slice the potato into thick
crosswise circles. Drain the rice in a colander and rinse it with
cold water. Place the rice and 2 1/4 cup of water in a pot, sprinkle
