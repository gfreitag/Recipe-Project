Title: Shrimp Creole - Gwhp32a
Categories: Seafood, Main dish
Yield: 7 servings
3 c  Rice -- hot/cooked
2 tb Shortening
2    Onions -- sliced
4    Celery -- stalks/chopped
1 tb Flour
1 ts Salt
2 c  Tomatoes; cooked -- chopped
2 c  Peas
1 tb Vinegar
1 1/2 c  Shrimp -- cooked
2 ts Chili powder
1 c  Water
Cook the onions and celery until brown in the shortening.  Add flour,
seasonings and slowly add the water. Cook 15 minutes, then add the
tomatoes, peas, vinegar, sugar and shrimp.Continue cooking about 10
minutes more, or until shrimp are heated through. Serve over the
cooked rice.
