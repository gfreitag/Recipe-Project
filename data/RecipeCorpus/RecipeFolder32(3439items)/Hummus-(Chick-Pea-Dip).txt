Title: Hummus (Chick Pea Dip)
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread, Jerusalem
Yield: 4 servings
7    Pat dwigans -- nathan and
Goldman
2 c  Canned chick peas --
Drained
2/3 c  Tahina paste ( Ground
Sesame seeds)
3/4 c  Lemon juice
20    Milliliters
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cumin
Parsley for garnish
Garlic -- mashed
Place all the ingredients in a blender and mix with the chickpeas
until smooth. Store the  hummus in a covered container n the
refrigerator. Serve well chilled, and just before serving sprinkle
chopped parsley on top.

if a thinner consistency is required, thin with lemon juice.

: "The Flavor of Jerusalem" by Nathan and Goldman
