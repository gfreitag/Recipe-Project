Title: Brandy Wafers
Categories: Scot/irish, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 batch
2 oz Golden syrup
2 oz Butter
2 oz Flour
2 oz Caster sugar
1/2 ts Brandy
1/2 ts Ground ginger
Set oven to 450Ã¸F or Mark 8. Grease baking sheets. Melt the butter,
syrup and sugar together in a saucepan over a very gentle heat. Stir
in the flour, ground ginger and the brandy. Beat tagether for about 5
minutes. Drop small teaspoonsful on to the baking sheet, spaced well
apart. Bake For 5 minutes until pale golden brown in colour. Roll
immediately, while still hot, around the greased handle of a large
wooden spoon. Set aside to cool. These are delicious filled with
freshly whipped cream.
