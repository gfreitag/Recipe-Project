grain. Put the beef into a bowl together with the soy sauce, rice
wine or sherry, ginger, cornstarch and 1 teaspoon of sesame oil. Mix
well, then let the mixture marinate for about 20 minute Heat the oil
in a wok or large skillet until it is very hot. Remove the beef from
