Title: Bran Muffins  # 4
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 16 muffins
1/4 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Brown sugar (firmly packed)
1/4 c  Molasses
2    Eggs,unbeaten
1 c  Sweet milk
1 c  Quaker flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Quaker bran
1/2 c  Raisins, optional
Cream shortening and sugar together.Add molasses and eggs and beat
together well. Add milk and then the bran.Sift flourl,baking
powder,soda and salt together.Then add raisins if desired,mixing
well.Place in greased muffin tins.Bake in hot oven at 400-425 degrees
for 15 -20 minutes.
