Title: C/P Chicken Cacciatore
Categories: Poultry, Ethnic
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea Med Onions -- sliced thin
1 1/2 ts Dried oregano -- crushed
3 lb Chicken -- cut up
1/2 ts Dried basil -- crushed
2 c  Garlic -- minced
1 ea Bay leaf
1 c  Tomatoes -- 16 oz
1/4 c  Dry white wine
1 ts Salt
Cooked spaghetti
1/4 ts Pepper
Place onions in slow cooker. On top of onions place chicken pieces,
garlic, tomatoes, tomato sauce, salt, pepper, herbs and wine. Cover
pot. Cook on low 6 to 8 hours, or high 3 to4 hours. Serve chicken
with its sauce on spaghetti which you have prepared. Judy/NC PJXG05A
