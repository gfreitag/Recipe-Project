Title: Sesame Seed Milk
Categories: Allergy, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Water
1/4 c  Sesame seeds
Whiz the water and sesame seeds in a blender for about 2 minutes.
Strain if desired.  Drink can be flavored with molasses, banana,
carob powder, honey, fruit concentrate, dates or raisins. This drink
can also be prepared with 2 cups water and 4 tbsp tahini (Tahini is a
sesame paste that is available in most health food stores) Origin:
The Allergy Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, July/95.
