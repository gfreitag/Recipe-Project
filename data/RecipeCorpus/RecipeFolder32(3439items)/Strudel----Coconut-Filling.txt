Title: Strudel -  Coconut Filling
Categories: Jewish, Pastry, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Shredded coconut
2 c  Ground nuts
2 c  Fruit preserves
For this recipe (because coconut is very rich), it is advisable to
make 4 small strudels.  Mix the coconut, nuts and preserves together
and spread 1/4 amount on each piece of oiled dough (two recipes for
strudel dough are included in this database).  Roll
