Title: Our Crockpot Spaghetti Sauce
Categories: Crockpot, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
Judy Garnett pjxg05a
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
2 lb Ground beef
3 tb Quick tapioca (Heaping)
1 lg Onion -- chopped
2 ts Oregano -- crushed
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
1 ts Basil
1 cn Mushrooms -- drained
1 ts Salt
1    Green pepper -- chopped*
30 oz Tomato sauce
28 oz Can crushed tomatoes
* The green pepper is optional. Brown meat. Drain all grease and then
blot with paper towels. Spray Pam in crockpot. Add onion, garlic,
crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, oregano, basil & mushrooms.
Cover crockpot and turn heat to LOW.  Cook for approx Converted by
MMCONV vers. 1.00
