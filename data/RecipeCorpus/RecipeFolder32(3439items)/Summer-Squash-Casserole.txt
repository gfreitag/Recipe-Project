Title: Summer Squash Casserole
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 7 servings
6 c  Summer squash -- sliced
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Carrots -- shredded
1 pk 8-oz herb-seasoned stuffing
1/2 c  Margarine -- melted
Cook summer squash and onion in boiling water for 5 minutes.  Drain
and set aside. Combine chicken soup and sour cream.  Stir in carrots.
Fold in drained squash and onion.  In a separate bowl combine
stuffing mix and margarine. Spread 1/2 stuffing mix in bottom of 8" x
12" baking dish. Spoon vegetable mixture on top.  Sprinkle with
remaining stuffing mix. Bake at 350Â°F for 25-30 minutes or until
heated.
