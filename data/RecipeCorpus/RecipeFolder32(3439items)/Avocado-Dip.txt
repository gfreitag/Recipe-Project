Title: Avocado Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
7    Pat dwigans -- nathan and
Goldman
1    Avocado
1    Crushed garlic clove
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tb Mayonnaise Lurie
Mash the avocado, then blend in garlic, salt and pepper to taste.
Then stir in mayo until the mixture is of the desired consistency.
Chill to serve.
