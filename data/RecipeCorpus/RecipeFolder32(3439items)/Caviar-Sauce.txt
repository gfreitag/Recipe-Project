Title: Caviar Sauce
Categories: Fish, Salsa/sauce, Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 1/3 cups
1 c  Sour cream
1 ts Onion juice
2 ts Capers
1/4 c  Caviar; lumpfish or other
-economical red type
Mix all the ingredients and serve on baked potatoes, salmon steaks and
other fish dishes, in lieu of Tartar sauce for batter fried fish or
as a dip for assorted raw vegetables.
