Title: Icing for White Cake^
Categories: Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
2    Egg whites
1/2 c  Pineapple, chopped
Boil sugar and water until it threads well; pour over egg whites well
beaten, beating all the time. when partly cool, add pineapple.
