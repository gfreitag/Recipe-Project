Title: Potato Muffins
Categories: Jewish, Muffins, Potatoes
Yield: 12 servings
2    Egg yolks
3 c  Grated, drained potatoes
4 tb Grated onion
1/2 c  Flour -- sifted
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
3 tb Melted butter or chicken
Fat
2    Egg whites -- stiffly
Beaten
Beat the egg yolks, then stir in the potatoes, onion, flour, salt,
baking powder and melted butter or fat.  Fold in the egg whites.
This quantity makes twelve 2-inch muffins. Grease muffin tin and fill
2/3 full with the mixture. Bake in a 400 degree oven
