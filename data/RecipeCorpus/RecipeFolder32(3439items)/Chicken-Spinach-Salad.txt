Title: Chicken Spinach Salad
Categories: Poultry, Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
3/4 lb Chicken Breast Planks
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Soy sauce
1 bn Fresh spinach
Boiling water
16 oz Canned mandarin oranges
Salad Dressing (your choice)
Combine Chicken breast planks with Worcestershire sauce and soy sauce.
Saute in small amount of butter or margarine until chicken is tender
and done. Set aside.

Place spinach leaves in a colander.  Pour boiling water over leaves.
Drain and set aside to cool.

Place a piece of chicken and a mandarin orange slice at the end of a
spinach leaf.  Roll up and secure with a toothpick.  Refrigerate at
least one hour.  Serve with salad dressing of your choice.
