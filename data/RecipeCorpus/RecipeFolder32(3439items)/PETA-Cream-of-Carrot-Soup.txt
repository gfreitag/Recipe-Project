Title: PETA Cream of Carrot Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Margerine; or water
1 md Onion; chopped
12 md Carrots; slices
1 md Potato; peeled and diced
4 c  Vegetable broth
1/2 c  Soy milk
;salt and pepper to taste
;dash of grated nutmeg
1/4 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Rosemary
1 tb Orange juice
Heat the margerine or water in a large saucepan.  Cook the onion for
5 mins over low heat, or until soft.  Add the carrots and potato,
cover, and simmer for 5-10 mins.  Add the broth, cover and simmer
about 20 mins, or until the veggies are tender.

Pour the soup thru a sieve or colander, reserving the stock for later.
Puree the vegetables in a blender or food processor and return them
to the saucepan.  Add the soy milk, then gradually add the reserved
stock until the soup reaches desired consistency.

Flavor with the remaining ingredients.  Reheat slowly and serve.
