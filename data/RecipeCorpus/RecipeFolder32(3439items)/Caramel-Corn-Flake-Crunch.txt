Title: Caramel Corn Flake Crunch
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 servings
24    Kraft caramels
2 tb Water
1/2 c  Salted peanuts
7 c  Corn flakes
Place the caramels and water in the top ofa double
boiler.Heat,stirring frequently until the caramels are melted and the
sauce is smooth. Add the peanuts and mix well.Pour over the corn
flakes placed in a large bowl and toss until well coated. Spread on a
lightly greased cookie sheet.Let stand until firm.Break apart and
serve.
