Title: Flavored Marshmallow Snacks
Categories: Kids, Snacks
Yield: 24 servings
1    Recipe
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
(colored)
Rice Krispies* Treats
Prepare Rice Krispies Treats recipe.  Gently add colored marshmallows
and stir until well coated.  Using buttered spatuala press mixture
into 13 x 9 pan.  Cut into 24 squares.  160 calories and 3 grams fat.
