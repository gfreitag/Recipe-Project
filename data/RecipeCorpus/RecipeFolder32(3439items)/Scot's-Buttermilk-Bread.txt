Title: Scot's Buttermilk Bread
Categories: Scot/irish, Breads
Yield: 1 batch
1 lb Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
1 oz Butter
2 c  Buttermilk
Set oven to 425 Ã¸F or Mark 7.  Grease and flour a baking sheet. Mix
all the dry ingredients together in a bowl. Lightly rub in the
butter. Add the buttermilk and mix into a light, soft dough. Divide
into two and shape into rounds on a lightly floured surface. Place
the rounds on the baking sheet and bake for about 20 minutes. Serve
sliced with butter.
