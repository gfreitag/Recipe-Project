Title: Wiener Zollen (W/citrus Flavor)
Categories: Cake/cookie, Aust/german
Yield: 1 cervings
1 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
Grated rind of 1 lemon
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground cardamon
1/2 c  Finely chopped citron (opt)
1 c  Chopped pecans
2 1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
Beat eggs thoroughly; add sugar gradually and continue beating.
Grind or pulverize cardamon seed; sift flour, baking powder and
spices several times and add to mixture.  Blend and drop from
teaspoon into sugar; roll in sugar and place on greased cookie sheets.

Bake at 325Ã¸ about 15 minutes.
