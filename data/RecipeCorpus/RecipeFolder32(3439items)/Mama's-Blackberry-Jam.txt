Title: Mama's Blackberry Jam
Categories: Can/preserv, Dips/spread
Yield: 2 pints
2 1/2 lb Blackberries
1 1/2 lb Sugar
Wash berries thorughly, remoce caps and crush berries. Heat slowly to
boiling, stirring constantly. Add sugar and continue cooking rapidly
until mixture gives test for jelly. Pour into sterile jars and seal.
If seedless jam is desired rub cooked berries thru seive before
adding sugar. Submitted By KATHRYN CONE   On   07-03-95
