Title: Marinata de Ciuperci (Marinated Mushrooms)
Categories: Romanian, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Button mushrooms
1 c  Oil
1 x  Salt, to taste
2 c  Vinegar(approx)
2 x  Bay leaves
1 x  Peppercorns
Wash mushrooms well and cut off tips of stems. Place on a cloth to
dry. Heat oil to boiling, add mushrooms and saute for about 15 min or
until tender. Place mushrooms in half quart or quart jars. Add salt,
about 1 ts to a quart jar. Add bay leaves and peppercorns to vinegar
and bring to a boil. Simmer for about ten more minutes. Pour hot
vinegar over mushrooms and seal jars.
