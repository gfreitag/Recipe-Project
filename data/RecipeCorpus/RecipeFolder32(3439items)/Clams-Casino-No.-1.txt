Title: Clams Casino No. 1
Categories: Seafood, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
24 ea Cherrystone clams
4 tb Green pepper, chopped
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ea Lg Garlic clove, minced
8 tb Butter
6 tb Seasoned bread crumbs
Preheat oven to 450f.  Open clams and discard top shell.  Place clams
in a shallow baking pan. In a saucepan, melt 4 Tbsp. butter and saute
the garlic and green pepper for 2 - 3 minutes. Add remaining butter,
lemon juice, and bread crumbs, mixing thoroughly. Place 1 teas. of
mixture on top of each clam and bake for 10 minutes. Shellfish
