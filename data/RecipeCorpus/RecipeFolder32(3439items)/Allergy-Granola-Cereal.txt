Title: Allergy Granola Cereal
Categories: Allergy, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
8 c  Rolled oats
1 c  Unsalted raw sunflower
-seeds
1 c  Sesame seeds or flax seeds
1 c  Sliced almonds or other
-nuts
1 c  Raw wheat germ or corn
-grits
1 c  Dessicated coconut OR 1 cup
-lecithin granules
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Powdered skim milk
1 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  Liquid honey, or corn syrup
2 c  Raisins or currants
Mix dry ingredients (do not add raisins)  Blend in oil and honey.
Bake on broiler tray at 250F for 1-1/2 hours, stirring every half
hour.  Add raisins after cooking, while still warm. If you use salted
sunflower seeds, do not add extra salt.
