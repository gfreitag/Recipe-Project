Title: Roast Turkey
Categories: Jewish, Poultry
Yield: 10 servings
12 lb Turkey
1 tb Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 lb Unswetened pitted prunes
1 c  Water
4 c  Sliced apples
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
2 ts Lemon juice
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
Season the turkey with salt and pepper.  Cook the prunes in the water
for 10 minutes.  Drain.  Add the apples, bread crumbs, lemon juice,
sugar and cinnamon.  Mix lightly and stuff the turkey.  Sew the
opening or fasten with skewers. Place in a rosting pa
