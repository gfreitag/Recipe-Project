Title: Bananas In A Rich Coconut Sauce
Categories: Desserts, Vietnamese
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Coconut milk
1 1/2 tb Sugar
Salt
6 lg Peeled bananas
And sliced diagonally about
3/4    " thick
Ice cream or whipped cream
To serve
*(Chuoi Voi Nuoc Cot Dua)

1.  Heat the coconut milk in a small saucepan and add the sugar and
salt. Bring to a boil.  Lower the heat and simmer for 2 minutes. 2.
Remove from the heat and stir in the banana slices.  Bring back to a
boil for a few seconds to coat the bananas.  Serve with ice cream or
whipped cream.

Recipe Source: VIETNAMESE COOKING - EXOTIC DELIGHTS FROM INDO-CHINA by
