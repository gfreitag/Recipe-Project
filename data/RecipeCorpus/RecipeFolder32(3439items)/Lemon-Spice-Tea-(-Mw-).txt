Title: Lemon Spice Tea ( Mw )
Categories: Microwave, Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
2 c  Warm Water
3 ts Honey
2    Slice Lemon
2    1" Stick Cinnamon, Broken
2    Tea Bag
Lemon Slice (Opt.)
In a nonmetal mug combine water, honey, lemon slice, and cinnamon.
Micro-cook mixture, uncovered, on 100% power about 1 1/2 minutes or
just till steaming hot.  Add tea bag.  Cover and steep for 4 minutes.
Remove tea bag, lemon slice, and cinnamon.  Garnish with an
additional lemon slice, if desired.
