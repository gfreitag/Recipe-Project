Title: Knaidlach  <passover>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Passover
Yield: 1 servings
2    Eggs
4 tb Chicken fat -- melted
1/3 c  Cold water
1 ts Salt
1 c  Matzo meal
Beat the eggs, fat, water and salt together.  Stir in the matzo meal,
adding just enough to make a stiff batter. Chill one hour. Form into
balls and cook for 30 minutes in boiling soup or salted water. Makes
about 18. Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie
Grossinger Published by Bantam Book
