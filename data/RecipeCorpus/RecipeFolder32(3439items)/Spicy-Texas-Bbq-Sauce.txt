Title: Spicy Texas Bbq Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Lime juice
1 ts Veg oil
1 1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
2    Cloves garlic -- fine chop
12 oz Beer
1/2 c  Brown sugar -- firm packed
2 ts Red chiles -- to taste
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
2    Jalapeno -- seeded and
Chopped
12 oz Tomato paste
Heat all ingredients to boiling in a 2 quart pan, reduce heat to low.
cover and simmer 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Makes 5 cups sauce.
