Title: Spinach Salad With Lemon Dressing
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Fresh spinach
1    Clove(s) garlic
1 ts Sugar
1    Lemon rind, grated
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
3 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Sour cream
1/2 c  Oil
Blend all ingredients except spinach for 15 seconds.  Toss with
spinach and serve.
