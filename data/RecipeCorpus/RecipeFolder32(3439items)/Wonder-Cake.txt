Title: Wonder Cake
Categories: Jewish, Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
5    Egg yolks
2 1/4 c  Cake flour -- sifted
3/4 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
3/4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
Confectioners' sugar
Cream the shortening and beat in the sugar.  Add one yolk at a time,
beating after each addition.  Sift together the flour, salt and baking
powder and add to batter, alternating with the milk. Beat in the
vanilla. Grease a 10-inch loaf pan and dust it lightly with flour.
Pour the batter into it.    Bake in a 350 degree oven 1 hour or until
a cake tester comes out clean.  Cool on a cake rack.  Sprinkle with
confectioners' sugar. Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by
Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam Books, I
