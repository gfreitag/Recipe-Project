Title: Broccoli Sesame Salad
Categories: Salads, Ethnic
Yield: 8 servings
1    Head broccoli
1/4 c  Rice wine vinegar
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Sesame oil
2 tb Olive oil
4 tb Sesame seeds -- toasted
Wash broccoli, discarding leaves and toughest part of stem.  Blanch
entire head in boiling water for one minute.  Rinse under cold water.
Break off florets and cut remainingstem (peeled, if desired) into 2"
pieces. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  Spread broccoli pieces in one
layer on a greased baking sheet.  Roast at 450 degrees for 5 minutes,
turn broccoli pieces over, and continue roasting until broccoli
begins to brown, about 5 minute more. Whisk together soy sauce,
vinegar, and oils. Stir in 3 tablespoons sesame seeds. When broccoli
is done, transfer to a bowl and pour dressing over it, stirring
gently to coat. Sprinkle with remaining tablespoon sesame seeds.
Serve warm or at room tempe
