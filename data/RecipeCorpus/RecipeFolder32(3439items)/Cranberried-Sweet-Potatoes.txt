Title: Cranberried Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 servings
3 md Sweet potatoes
1 cn Cranberry sauce -- 15 ounce
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Grated orange peel
1/2 c  Chopped toasted almonds
Preheat oven to 350. Lightly oil a baking dish.

Peel sweet potates and cut into 1 inch cubes. Steam until tender,
about 25 minutes.  Place potatoes in baking dish.

combine cranberry sauce, cinnamon and orange peel . Spoon mixture over
potatoes and sprinkle with toasted almonds.

Bake for 20 minutes.  Serve warm or at room temperature.

Per serving:

197 calories;  3 g protein;  87 mg sodium;  35 g carbo;  6 g fat;  47
mg calcium
