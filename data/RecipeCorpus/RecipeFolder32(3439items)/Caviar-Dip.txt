Title: Caviar Dip
Categories: Fish, Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 cup
1/2 c  Whipping cream
3 tb Caviar
2 tb Onion; minced
Hard cooked eggs; sliced
Toast rounds or Melbas
Fold the caviar and onion into the heavy cream. Serve with sliced egg
on Melba toast or similar crackers.

To reduce fat try low-fat sour cream and yogurt combined with just a
dash of sugar and thinned with a bit of milk in place of the cream.
