Title: Pasta Sauce With Lemon And Garlic
Categories: Vegetarian, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Olive oil
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1/2 c  Dry white wine
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 c  Tomatoes -- stewed
1/4 c  Basil
2 tb Parmesan cheese
1/2 ts Black pepper
In a skillet, heat oil over medium heat and add garlic until tender.
Remove from heat and pour wine. Return it to the heat. Cook another 1
to 2 minutes, until wine has been reduced by half. Stir in lemon
juice and tomatoes, basil, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.  Serve
over cooked pasta.
