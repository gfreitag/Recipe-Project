Title: Center Club Chicken Wings
Categories: Asian, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
4 lb Chicken wings
1 1/4 c  Hoisin sauce
3/4 c  Plum sauce
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Dry sherry
1/4 c  Honey
6    Green onions -- minced
6    Cloves garlic -- minced
Cut off and discard wing tips.  Separate wing at joint. At the
drumstick joint, separate bones with small knife and push meat to
tip. Remove smaller bone and discard. Mix all other ingred in large
bowl. Add chicken and coat well. After refrigerating coate Drain
chicken, reserving liquid. Place chicken on rack and roast for 30
min. Baste, turn wings and return to oven for an additional 30 min.
