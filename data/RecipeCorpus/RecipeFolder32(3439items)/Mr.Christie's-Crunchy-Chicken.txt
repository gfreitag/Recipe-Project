Title: Mr.Christie's Crunchy Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
8 lb Frying chicken,cut in pieces
2/3 c  Flour
2    Eggs,beaten
1 tb Water
1    Stack premium crackers,
Crushed
Salt and pepper to taste
1/3 c  Melted butter
Wash chicken pieces and pat dry.Season with salt and pepper.Dredge
with flour.Dip each piece into egg beaten with the water. Roll in
premium cracker crumbs.completely coat each piece with crumbs.Place
in shallow buttered baking dish or pan.Drizzle melted butter on each
piece.Bake in pre-heated 350 degree oven for 1 hour.

For flavour variations mix crackers with any one of the following;
1/4 c grated parmesan cheese 1 ts grated lemon rind 1/4 ts garlic
powder 1 ts mixed herbs; thyme,marjoram and sweet basil.
