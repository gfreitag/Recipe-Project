Title: Falafel Chick Pea Patties
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 24 servings
1 lb Can chick-peas drained
1 lg Onion chopped
Parsley
1 lg Egg
1 ts Salt
1 ts Dried hot red peppers
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Ground coriander or cumin
1/2    To 1 cup bread crumbs or
Fine burghul (crushed
Wheat)
Vegetable oil
Mix chick peas and the onion, add the parsley, lightly beaten egg and
spices. Whirl in a blender. Add bread crumbs until the mixture will
form a small ball without sticking to your hands. Form the chick pea
mixture into small balls about the size of a qua Drain the falafel on
paper towels. Serve individually with toothpicks or as a sandwich
flling with chopped tomato, cucumber, radish, lettuce, onion and /or
tahina inside pita.
