Title: Soup Nockerl
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Flour -- sifted
1/2 ts Baking powder
Beat the eggs, water and salt together.  Mix in the flour and baking
powder.    Drop by the teaspoon into boiling salted water. Cook until
they rise to the surface, about 5 minutes.  Drain. Serve to your
taste, according to your preference for thick or thin soup.    Recipe
Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by
