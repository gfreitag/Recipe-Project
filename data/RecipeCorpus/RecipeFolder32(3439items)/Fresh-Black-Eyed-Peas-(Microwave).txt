Title: Fresh Black-Eyed Peas (Microwave)
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Shelled fresh black-eyed
-peas
3 sl Bacon; chopped
1 c  Water
1 md Onion; chopped
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground red pepper
1 ea Bay leaf
Combine all ingredients in a 2-quart casserole; mixing well. Cover
with heavy-duty plastic wrap.  Microwave at HIGH for 4 minutes; stir
well. Cover and microwave at MEDIUM for 20 to 25 minutes, stirring
every 5 minutes. Let stand, covered, for 5 minutes. Discard bay leaf.
Yield: 4 servings
