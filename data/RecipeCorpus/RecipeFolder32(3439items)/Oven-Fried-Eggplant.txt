Title: Oven-Fried Eggplant
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Fine dry breadcrumbs
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
3/4 lb Eggplant
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
Garnish: fresh basil
Combine first 4 ingredients in a shallow dish.
Peel eggplant, and cut into 1/4-inch slices. Spread both sides of
eggplant slices with mayonnaise; dredge in breadcrumb mixture. Place
o a lightly greased baking sheet. Bake at 400 degrees, uncovered, for
10 to 12 minutes or until browned. Garnish, if desired.

Recipe from Josephine Jones in 8/91 "Southern Living". Typed by Jeff
