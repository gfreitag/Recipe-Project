Title: Crockpot Peppers & Steak
Categories: Crockpot, Chinese, Meats, Asian
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Lean round steak;1-in thick
2    Green peppers;*
1 c  Beef bouillon
1/4 c  Low-sodium soy sauce
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Garlic powder
*Seeded and cut into 1/2 inch strips. Cut steak into serving portions.
Place half the steak in crock-pot. Arrange green pepper strips around
steak.  Place remaining steak on top. Mix remaining ingredients and
pour over meat.  Cover and cook on LOW setting for 8 to 10 hours (on
HIGH setting for 3 to 4 hours).
