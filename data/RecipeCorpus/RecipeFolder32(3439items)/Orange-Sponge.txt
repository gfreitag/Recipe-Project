Title: Orange Sponge
Categories: Cake/cookie, Oldies
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Gelatin
1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Orange juice
3    Egg whites
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Boiling water
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/8 ts Salt
Soften gelatin in cold water.  Dissolve in boiling water.  Add sugar
and salt.  Stir until dissolved.  Cool.  Add strained fruit juices.
Mix thoroughly.  Cool until partially set.  Beat until gelatin will
hold its shape.  Pile lightly in serving dish. 10 servings.

Mrs. F.D.W., Billings, MT.
