Title: Noodles And Cheese
Categories: Jewish, Pasta, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
4 tb Butter
1/2 lb Noodles -- cooked and
Drained
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Pot cheese
Melt the butter in a skillet.  Stir in the noodles and cook over
medium heat, stirring constantly until noodles brown.  Remove from
heat and add the salt and pot cheese. Mix well.  Serves 3 to 4.
Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Gro
