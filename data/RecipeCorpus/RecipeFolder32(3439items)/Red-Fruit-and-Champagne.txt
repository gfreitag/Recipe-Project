Title: Red Fruit and Champagne
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Strawberries
-washed and hulled
1/2 c  Raspberries, cleaned
1/4 c  Red currants, cleaned
3/4 c  Sugar
750 ml Bottle chilled champagne
-or sparkling wine
1/4 c  Raspberry liqueur
Place the fruit in a large pitcher and cover with the sugar in a
layer. Allow the fruit to macerate in the refrigerator for 2-3 hours.
Just before serving, pour a bottle of champagne over the fruit and
add the raspberry liqueur. Serve chilled.
