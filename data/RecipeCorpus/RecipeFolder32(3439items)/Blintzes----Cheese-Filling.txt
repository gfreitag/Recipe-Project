Title: Blintzes -  Cheese Filling
Categories: Jewish
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Drained cottage cheese
1    Egg yolk
3/4 ts Salt
1 tb Melted butter
2 tb Sugar -- (optional)
1 ts Lemon juice -- (optional)
Beat the cheese, egg yolk, salt and butter together.  Add the sugar or
lemon juice if you like--some people like them sweet, some don't. Fill
pancakes.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie
