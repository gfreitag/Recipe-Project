Title: Shrimp /Garlic Sauce
Categories: Seafood, Main dish, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
4 c  Garlic ; thinly sliced
1    Red bell pepper ; seeded an
1 1/4 lb Shrimp ; med
3 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Dry sherry
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes
3 tb Parsley ; finely chopped fr
Approx. Cook Time:
Fat grams    per serving:
:05 Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the
garlic, pepper and shrimp. Cook for about 2 minutes, stirring
constantly, just until the shrimp turn pink. Increase the heat to
high. Add the lemon juice, sherry, hot red pepper, and salt to taste
and bring to a boil. Serve immediately, sprinkled with parsley. The
total cooking time shouldn't be more than 5 minutes. Serve over rice
with a salad for a delicious, quick meal.
