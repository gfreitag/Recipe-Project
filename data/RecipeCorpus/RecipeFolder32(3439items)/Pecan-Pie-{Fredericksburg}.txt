Title: Pecan Pie {Fredericksburg}
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 pie
5    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Corn syrup
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Chopped pecans
6 tb Melted butter
Beat eggs and sugar.  Add corn syrup, salt, vanilla, chopped pecans
and butter.  Mix well.  Pour into unbaked 9-inch pie shell and bake
until filling is set, about 1 hour and 15 minutes at 325Ã¸ F.
