Title: Cottage Cheese Soup  <dairy>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
1    Onion -- chopped
1    Celery stalk -- chopped
2    Green peppers -- chopped
3 tb Butter
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Paprika
3 c  Water
3 c  Milk
1/2 c  Light cream
1 c  Cottage cheese
Cook the onion, celery and green peppers in the butter for 15
minutes. Add the salt, pepper, paprika, water and milk.  Cover and
cook over low heat 1 hour.  Just before serving, add the cream and
cheese.  Heat but don't boil. Serves 8.    Recipe Source:
