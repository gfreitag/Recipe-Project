Title: Beer Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
8    To 10 chicken breasts
(or your favorite parts)
1/3 c  Butter, melted
Juice from 8 medium lemons
(or about 1 1/2 cups juice)
1 cn Of beer
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Lemon pepper
Combine butter, beer, lemon juice, garlic, lemon pepper.  Pour over
chicken and refrigerate 4 to 6 hours.  Barbecue chicken, brushing
sauce over pieces as you turn them.  Grill about 15 to 20 minutes per
side.

Good and simple recipe for grilled chicken.  Serve with broccoli-rice
casserole and grilled Vidalia onions and you have a great weekend
barbeque menu.  Add a salad and garlic bread and it's a fantastic
weekend barbecue!
