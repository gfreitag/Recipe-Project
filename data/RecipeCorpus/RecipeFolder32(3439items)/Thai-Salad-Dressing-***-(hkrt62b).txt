Title: Thai Salad Dressing *** (hkrt62b)
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Ethnic
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 ts Chopped garlic
2 tb Fish sauce
3 tb Ground roasted peanuts
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Water-
Dash cayenne pepper
1 ts Chopped coriander leaves
1    To 2 red chilis, sliced
2 tb To 3 tb white onions,
-chopped
*In a small sauce pan, cook sugar in water over med heat until it
turns into a light syrup. Add all remaining ingredients, stir well.
Let cool. Serve over salad. FROM:    SOPIT MERRELL   (HKRT62B)
