Title: Peach Alexander
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
4 ea Peaches; peeled diced*
2 ts Vanilla OR
1 ts Almond extract
2 lg Scoops vanilla ice cream
-(2/3 cup)
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Fresh mint
Fresh peach slices
Combine diced peaches and vanilla (or almond extract) in blender;
whirl until smooth. Add ice cream and whirl just until smooth. Pour
into glasses. Garnish with fresh mint and peach slice.

*Note: To peel peaches, plunge into boiling water for 30 seconds.
Remove immediately and plunge into ice water. Skin should slip off
easily.

Makes 4 servings

Per serving: Calories: 75 (8% from protein, 83% from carbohydrate, 8%
from fat) Protein: 2 grams Fat: 1 gram Cholesterol: 3 mg
Carbohydrate: 16 grams Sodium: 20 mg Exchanges: 1 fruit
