Title: Kneidlach (Matzah Balls)
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew
Yield: 20 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
3/4 c  Matzah flour
2 lg Eggs slightly beaten
1/2 ts Salt
Pepper to taste
2    Sprigs parsley -- finely
Chopped
1 sm Onion -- chopped and fried
Until crisp in
2 tb Oil
1 tb Cold water
Mix the ingredients in the order given. Form the mixture into small
balls and drop into the boiling soup; cook over medium heat,
uncovered, about 20 minutes.

: "The Flavors of Jerusalem" by Nathan and Goldman
