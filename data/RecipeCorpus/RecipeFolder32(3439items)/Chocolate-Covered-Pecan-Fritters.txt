Title: Chocolate-Covered Pecan Fritters
Categories: Desserts, Candies
Yield: 4 dozen
2 pk Vanilla caramels; 6 oz. ea.
2 tb Milk, evaporated
2 c  Pecan halves
8 oz Milk choc. bar; broken into
-squares
1/3    Parafin bar; broken into
-pieces
Combine caramels and milk in top of double boiler; heat until caramels
melt, stirring constantly.  Beat with wooden spoon until creamy; stir
in pecans.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto buttered waxed paper; let stand
15 minutes.

Combine chocolate and paraffin in top of double boiler; heat until
melted and smooth, stirring occasionally.  Using a toothpick, dip
each fritter into chocolate mixture.  Place on waxed paper to cool.
