Title: Oatmeal Raspberry Bar Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 servings
1/2 c  Butter; room temperature
1/2 c  Light brown sugar
1 c  Flour
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/8 ts Salt
1 c  Rolled oats
3/4 c  Seedless raspberry jam
Heat the oven to 350 degrees.  Butter an 8" square pan, set aside.
Mix all ingredients together except the jam.  Press 2 cups of the
mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.  Spread the jam to
within 1/4" of the edge. Sprinkle the remaining crumb mixture over
the top and lightly press it into the jam.  Bake 35 to 40 minutes and
allow to cool on a wire rack before cutting into 2 x 1-1/2" bars.
