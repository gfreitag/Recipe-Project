Title: Satay-Style Beef With Broccoli
Categories: Meats, Chinese, Guestchefs
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Market steak or beef fillet
=OR=- New York steak
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
2 ts Dark soy sauce
1 ts Shrimp paste
2 ts Cornstarch
2 ts Rice wine
1 lb Chinese broccoli
1 c  Peanut oil -- for
Deep-frying
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/2 ts Shrimp paste
1 c  Chicken stock
1/2 ts Satay sauce -- -=OR=-
2 ts -chili bean sauce
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Dark soy sauce
2 ts Rice wine
1/2 ts Cornstarch -- mixed with
1/2 ts Water
PLACE THE STEAK IN THE FREEZER for about 20 minutes or until it is
firm to the touch. Cut it, against the grain, into thin slices. Place
the meat in a medium-sized bowl with all the marinade ingredients and
refrigerate for 20 minutes. Separate the Chinese When the sauce has
thickened, return the meat to the wok and stir to mix well. Serve
with the warm broccoli.
