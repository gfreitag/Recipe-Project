Title: Teriaki Grilled Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
ELAINE RADIS BGMB90B
2 lg Chicken breasts -- boneless
And skinless -- cut in half
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
1/2 c  Teriaki sauce
1/4 c  Dry Sherry -- Taylors is
Good
sl Fresh ginger -- crushed
ml Garlic -- crushed
Marinate for about 3 or 4 hours...turn every once in awhile.  Grill or
broil...  Easy, moist and delicious..
