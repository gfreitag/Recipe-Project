Title: Pareve Knaidlach  <passover>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Passover
Yield: 1 servings
2    Egg yolks
1/2 ts Salt
2    Egg whites -- stiffly
Beaten
1/2 c  Matzo meal
Beat the egg yolks and salt until thick.  Fold into the egg whites,
then gradually fold into the matzo meal.  Chill 1 hour.  Moisten
hands; shape mixture into 1/2-inch balls. Cook in boiling salted
water 20 minutes. Serve in dairy or meat soups.  Makes a
