Title: Boboli Topping 17
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Ethnic
Yield: 1 servings
Pizza Marietta
4    Italian sausages; cooked
1/2 c  Pizza or Tomato Sauce
Sliced
1 lg Boboli or 4 small
8    Red onion thin slices
1 c  Green pepper -- thin strips
Separated
1 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
Spread sauce on Boboli.  Sprinkle with cheese. Arrange remaining
ingredients on top.  Bake at 450 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes. (wrv)
