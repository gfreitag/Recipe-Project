Title: Updated Mincemeat
Categories: Pie/tart, Guestchefs
Yield: 2 servings
3/4 lb Unsalted butter or margarine
OR beef suet
3/4 lb Raisins
3/4 lb Currants
3/4 lb Sultanas
3/4 lb Chopped apples
3 oz Citron
3 oz Candied lemon peel
3 oz Candied orange peel
1/2 lb Almonds
1/8 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Mace
1/8 ts Allspice
1/8 ts White pepper
3/4 c  Brandy
PREHEAT OVEN TO 325F. Combine all ingredients and grind twice through
the finest blade of your meat grinder. Place in a lidded oven-proof
pot and place in the oven. Bake for 40 minutes. Remove from oven and
stir in brandy. Store the mincemeat in a cool pl
