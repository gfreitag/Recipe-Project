Title: Asian Noodles+
Categories: Asian, Pasta
Yield: 1 recipe
MMMMM------------------------BASIC RECIPE-----------------------------
Noodles cooked al dente
Soy sauce
Toasted sesame oil
MMMMM-------------------------VARIATION------------------------------
Peanuts, coarsely chopped
Fresh chives, chopped
This recipe is so simple that one remembers that less can be more
sometimes.

Vary the pasta any way you wish, just make sure that it is al dente.

To prepare: Toss the noodles with soy sauce and Asian (toasted)
sesame oil to taste.

Variation: Asian peanut and chive pasta

Serve Asian pasta, as above, garnished with coarsely chopped peanuts
and chopped fresh chives.
