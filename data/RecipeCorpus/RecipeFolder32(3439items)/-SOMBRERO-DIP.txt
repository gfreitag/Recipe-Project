Title:  SOMBRERO DIP
Categories: Dips/spread, Tex/mexican, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 lb Spicy refried beans
7 oz Salsa
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1    Clove minced garlic
1/2 c  Shredded Jack w/ Jalapenos
-cheese
1/4 c  Chpped green onions
Shredded cheddar cheese
Brown beef until crumbly; drain.  Add onion and garlic. Cook until
onion is soft.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cook until hot and
bubbly.  Spoon into chafing or fondue dish. Garnish with cheese and
green onions.  Serve hot with tortilla chips.
