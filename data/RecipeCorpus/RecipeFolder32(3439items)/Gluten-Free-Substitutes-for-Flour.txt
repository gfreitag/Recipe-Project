Title: Gluten Free Substitutes for Flour
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Corn flour
3/4 c  Coarse corn meal
1 ea Scant cup of fine corn meal
5/8 c  Potato flour (often called
-potato starch)
1 ea Scant cup wheat starch
7/8 c  Rice flour and 1/3 cup
-potato flour
1 c  Soy flour and 3/4 cup
-potato flour
MMMMM----------------ORIGIN: THE ALLERGY COOKBOOK---------------------
MMMMM----------------SHARED BY: SHARON STEVENS, J---------------------
The above mixtures may be substituted for one cup of wheat flour.
