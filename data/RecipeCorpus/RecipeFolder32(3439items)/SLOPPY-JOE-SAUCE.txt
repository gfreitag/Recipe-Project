Title: SLOPPY JOE SAUCE
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Ketchup
1 tb White vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 ts Paprika
1 tb Dark brown sugar
4    Buns
Brown meat and onions until no longer pink. Be sure to break up the
meat. (I add garlic, too. One clove.) Combine all of the liquids and
add to pan. Cook, uncovered, for about thirty minutes on low heat. It
should be thick. Serve on the buns. Enjoy!
