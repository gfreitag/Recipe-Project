Title: Macaroons
Categories: Jewish, Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 72 servings
5    Egg whites
1 c  Sugar
1/2 lb Almond paste
1/4 c  White corn meal
Beat the egg whites until stiff but not dry; beat in the sugar.  Mix
together the almond paste and corn meal and fold in the egg whites.
Drop by the teaspoon onto a lightly greased cooky sheet; flatten
slightly with a wet knife.    Bake in a 350 degree oven 20 minutes or
until delicately browned.  Makes about 6 dozen.    Recipe Source: THE
ART OF JEWISH COOKING by Jen
