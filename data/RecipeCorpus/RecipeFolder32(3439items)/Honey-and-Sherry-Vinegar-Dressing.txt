Title: Honey and Sherry Vinegar Dressing
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 /4 cup
2 ts Honey
2 ts Sherry vinegar
2 tb Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Combine the honey, vinegar, and oil, mixing well. Add salt and pepper
to taste.
