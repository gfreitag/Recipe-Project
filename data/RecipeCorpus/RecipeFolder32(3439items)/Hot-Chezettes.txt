Title: Hot Chezettes
Categories: Snacks, Cheese/eggs, Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 lb Grated American cheese
1 cn Deviled ham
1 ts Grated onion
1/4 ts Salt
ds Worcestershire sauce
Combine all ingredients.  Spread between 2 slices of bread from which
crusts have been removed.  Spread outside of bread with softened
butter. Wrap in foil and store in refrigerator or freezer until ready
to use. Place package on charcoal grill and toast 8 minutes per side,
or remove from package and grill in a skillet. Randy Rigg
