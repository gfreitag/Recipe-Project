Title: Blueberry Pineapple Cake
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 servings
1 pt Blueberries
18 oz Yellow cake mix
8 oz Can Crushed pineapple
1 c  Nuts
3/4 c  Melted margarine/butter
Pour berries in a greased 9 X 13 inch pan.  Cover with pineapple and
all its juice.  Sprinkle with dry cake mix.  Scatter nuts on top.
Drizzle butter over all.  Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
