Title: Jamaican Yam Casserole
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes
Yield: 2 servings
1 cn 1-lb yams -- drained
1/2 md Banana -- thickly sliced
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 tb Pecans -- coarsely chopped
2 tb Toasted flake coconut
Start heating oven to 350Â°F.  In a buttered 1-at casserole, arrange
yams, banana.  Pour juice over all.  Sprinkle with salt, pepper.  Top
with pecans and coconut.  Bake, covered, 30 minutes.
