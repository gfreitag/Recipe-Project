Title: Red Onion Compote
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
2    Red onions, peeled
-cut into 1/2" dice
1 tb Butter
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Sherry vinegar
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Saut the onions in the butter over medium-high heat for 5 minutes.
Add the sugar and cook for 2 minutes, until the onions are lightly
caramelized. Add the vinegar, salt, and pepper and cook for 5 minutes
more over low heat. Serve warm.
