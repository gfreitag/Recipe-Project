Title: PETA Curried Split Pea and Potato Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Potatoes, Vegetables
Yield: 10 servings
16 oz Green split peas; dried
10 c  Water
1 lg Onion; chopped
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1    Bay leaf
2 lg Carrots; peeled and chopped
3 md Potatoes; peeled and chopped
2    Stalks celery; chopped
In a large saucepan, combine the split peas, water, onion, and spices.
Simmer, uncovered for 1 hour. Stir in the carrots, potatoes and
celery. Simmer covered, stirring occasionally, for an additional 45
mins or until soup reaches desired thickness. Remove bay leaf. Puree
in a blender or food processor. Reheat before serving.
