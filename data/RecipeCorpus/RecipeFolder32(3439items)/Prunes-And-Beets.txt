Title: Prunes And Beets
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Unsweetened prunes
3 c  Julienne beets -- undrained
1/2 ts Salt
4 tb Sugar
2 ts Vinegar
Combine all the ingreients in a sauce pan.  Cover and cook over low
heat 25 minutes or until prunes are tender.    Delicious when served
as an accompaniment to roast meat or poultry.  Serves 4 to 6.
