Title: Citrus Wine
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
3    Blood oranges
1    Pink grapefruit
1    Vanilla bean
1    Stick cinnamon
1 ts Tequila
750 ml Bottle ros wine
1 c  Sugar
Wash but do not peel the oranges and grapefruit and cut them into
quarters. In a large bowl, combine the citrus fruit, vanilla bean,
cinnamon, tequila, and wine. cover and let the mixture steep,
unrefrigerated, for 20 days. Keep the mixture in a cool, dark place
such as a pantry or cellar. After 20 days, add 1 cup sugar and let
the mixture stand for another 10 days. Strain the mixture, bottle it,
and serve the wine at room temperature.
