Title: PETA Pancakes
Categories: Breakfast, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Unbleached all purpose
-flour
1 tb Sugar
2 tb Baking powder
1/8 ts Salt
1 c  Soy milk
2 tb Veg oil
Combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl and mix
thoroughly.  Mix in the soy milk and oil, and with an electric mixer
or wire whisk, beat just until batter is smooth.

Measure 1/3c batter onto a hot, oiled griddle.  When bubbled appear
on the upper surface of the pancake, about 2 mins, flip.  Cook the
pancake for another 2 mins.  Remove from pan and keep warm while you
make remaining pancakes.  Serve warm with maply syrup or fruit syrup.

Makes 6-8 pancakes.
