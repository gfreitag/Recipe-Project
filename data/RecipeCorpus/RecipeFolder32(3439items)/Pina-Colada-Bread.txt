Title: Pina Colada Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Desserts, Breads
Yield: 8 servings
1    Egg
1 ts Coconut extract
1/4    Stick unsalted butter --
Softened
3/4 c  Coconut milk
2 tb Coconut milk (if needed)
3 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ascorbic acid
2 1/2 c  Bread flour
1 tb Yeast
1 c  Coconut, flaked &
Unsweetened
1/2 c  Dried pineapple -- chopped
Place all ingredients except coconut and pineapple in the machine.
Program for sweet or raisin bread.  Add the coconut and pineapple
when the Hitachi timer reads 3:44.
