Title: Cereal Fun Bars
Categories: Kids, Snacks
Yield: 12 servings
1    Recipe
Rice Krispies* treats
Prepare recipe.  Press into 9 x 9 pan sprayed with Pam.  Cut into 2
3/4 by 2" pieces when cool.  Place wooden stick into the end of each
piece to make fun bars.  Decorate as desired with frosting and
candies.  160 calories and 3 grams fat.
