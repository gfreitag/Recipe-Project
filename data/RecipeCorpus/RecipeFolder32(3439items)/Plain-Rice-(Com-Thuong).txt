Title: Plain Rice (Com Thuong)
Categories: Rice, Vietnamese
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Long-grain white rice
2 1/2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1.  Wash the rice several times until the water runs clear. Drain
properly and add the water and salt.  Bring to a boil.  Turn down the
heat until it is just bubbling gently.  cover with a lid and leave to
cook for about 20 minutes.  The Vietnamese believ

2.  Turn off the heat, cover the saucepan once more and leave for
about 10 minutes to give the rice time to absorb all moisture.
