Title: Grilled Teriyaki Lamb Chops
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Lamb chops (rib, loin or
-shoulder)
1/2 c  Kikkoman teriyaki marinade
-and sauce
3 tb Minced fresh parsley
2 tb Dry white wine
2 ea Garlic cloves, pressed
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 tb Sesame seeds (optional)
Mix together ingredients, marinate chops 45 minutes.  Turn chops
occasionally.  Reserving marinade, remove chops; place on grill 4 to 5
inches from hot coals.  Cook 5 minutes on each side (for medium
rare), or to desired doneness.  Turn over and brush occasionally with
reserved marinade.  Sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired.
