Title: Banana Berry Nog
Categories: Beverages, Fruit, Healthy
Yield: 2 servings
1 ea Frozen banana; cut up
1/2 c  Plain nonfat or soy yogurt
3/4 c  Fresh strawberries
x  Grated nutmeg
PREP TIME:  5 minutes

Combine all ingredients in a blender and puree until smooth and
creamy. Pour drink into a glass and dust with nutmeg.

PER SERVING:  133 calories; 1g fat; 7% calories from fat; 0mg
cholesterol; 2.4g fiber.

Source:  Karen Greene in "Fast!  Fresh!  Fruity!" (New Hope Press) as
seen in "Delicious!  Your Magazine of Natural Living," July 1995.
