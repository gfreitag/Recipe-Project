Title: Fresh Apple Coffee Cake
Categories: Desserts, Breakfast, Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine,
-softened
2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
5 c  Peeled and chopped apple
-(about 4 large Granny
-Smith or Jonathan apples)
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
Beat the butter at medium speed with an electric mixer.  Gradually
add 2 cups of sugar; beat well.  Add the eggs, one at a time, beating
after each addition.

Combine the flour, baking powder and salt; add to the creamed
mixture. Stir in the apple and vanilla.  Spoon the batter into a
greased and floured 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan.

Combine the 1 1/2 tablespoons of sugar and the cinnamon; sprinkle
over the cake batter.  Bake at 350F for 45 minutes or until a wooden
pick inserted into the center comes out clean.  Serve warm or cold.

Makes 15 servings.
