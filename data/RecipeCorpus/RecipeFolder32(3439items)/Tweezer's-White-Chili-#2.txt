Title: Tweezer's White Chili #2
Categories: Poultry, Chili
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Large White Beans
6 c  Chicken Broth
1 lb Turkey Ham
2 ts Wright's or Colgin smoke
;grilled, taste, BBQ
2 ea Cloves Garlic, minced
2 ea Medium Onions, chopped
1 tb Olive Oil
1 ea 4 oz. medium chilis, chooped
2 ea Habanero's
1 tb Cumin
1 1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Ground Cloves
1 ts Cayenne Pepper
1 ts Chili Powder (or more)
3 c  Monterey Jack
Salsa
Sour Cream
Combine beans, broth, garlic and half the onions in a large soup
pot. Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, simmer until beans are soft (3
hours or more) adding more broth if it's necessary.
In a skillet, saute remaining onions in oil until tender.  Add
chiles and seasonings and mix thoroughly.  Add the bean mixture.
Simmer 1 hour or until it's just the way you like it.
Salsa: You can use store-bought chunky Mexican tomato or make you
own with your own peeled tomatoes, green chiles, onion, garlic, green
onion, and fresh cilantro.
