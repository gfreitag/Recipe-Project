Title: Pennsylvania Dutch kidney Bean Salad+
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Penndutch
Yield: 1 recipe
1/2 c  White vinegar
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Celery seed
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
3 cn Kidney beans (16-oz.)
1 c  Celery, diced
2    Eggs, hard boiled, chopped
Combine white vinegar, sugar, celery seed, salt and cornstarch in
saucepan. Place on heat; bring to boil, stirring continuously until
smooth. Take off heat and let cool to room temperature. Add
mayonnaise. Carefully rinse and drain kidney beans (drain 5 minutes.)
Combine sauce, kidney beans, celery, and chopped eggs. Marinate
several hours before serving.

Makes 1 1/2 quarts.
