Title: Linguine W/Bacon, Sun-Dried Tomatoes, Cream  S
Categories: Italian
Yield: 4 servings
Sherilyn Schamber
1 lb Linguine
Bacon slices
1/2 c  Romano or parmesan --
Grated
Green onions -- chpd
Salt and pepper
1 c  Whipping cream
Additional cheese
1/2 c  Sun-drd toms,oil pkd --
Sliced
Cook bacon, drain fat.  Add gr onion and stir 1 min. Add cream, and
bring to boil.  Turn off heat and add tomatoes. Meanwhile, cook
linguine; drain. Add sauce and 1/2 cup cheese and stir to coat.
Season. Pass additional cheese when serving.  Source: Bon Appetit
