Title: Lokshen Kugel (Sweet Noodle Pudding)
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 6 servings
Dwigans fwds07a
8 oz Broad noodles
1 c  Pot cheese
1/2 c  Raisins
1 lg Egg beaten slightly
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Sugar
3/4 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Margarine or butter melted
preheat the oven to 350. Cook the noodles as directed on the package.
Drain well. Stir in the remaiing ingredients and half the melted
margarine. Place in a greased casserole and pour over the remaining
melted margarine. bake uncovered for 1 hour.

: "The Flavors of Jerusalem" by Nathan and Goldman
