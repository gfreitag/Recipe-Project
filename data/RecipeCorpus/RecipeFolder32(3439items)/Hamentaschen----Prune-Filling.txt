Title: Hamentaschen -  Prune Filling
Categories: Jewish, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Lackwa (prune butter)
1/2 c  Ground almonds
1 tb Grated orange rind
Mix the ingredients together and fill the dough.    Recipe Source:
