Title: Nutty Wild Rice Salad
Categories: Salads, Rice, Game, Nuts/grains
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Salt
1 c  Wild rice
1/3 c  Minced red bell pepper
2 tb Minced scallions
1/4 c  Minced parsley
3/4 c  Seedless grapes, cut in half
3/4 c  Pecans, toasted
Makes 4-1/2 cups

Bring 4 cups of wter and 1 tsp salt to a boil.  Add rice and lower
heat. cover and simmer for 50 minutes or until rice is tender.  Drain
well and let cool.  In medium sized bowl, put cooked rice and
remaining ingredients. Add dressing and mix gently.  Use Orange
Drambuie dressing.

Origin: Journal American Shared by: Sharon Stevens
