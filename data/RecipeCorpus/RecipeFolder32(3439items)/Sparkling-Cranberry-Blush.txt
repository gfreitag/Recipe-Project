Title: Sparkling Cranberry Blush
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 quarts
3 c  Cold water
2 ea Bottles cranberry JUICE
-cocktail, chilled
2 ea (6-oz.) cans thawed lemonade
-concentrate, undiluted
2 ea (705-milliliter) bottles
-brut champagne, chilled
Combine the first 3 ingredients in a punch bowl and stir well.  Add
chilled champagne and stir gently.  Serve immediately.  (Serving
size: 3/4 cup).

Recipe source:  COOKING LIGHT magazine, April 1995.
