Title: Lemon Pheasant - United States
Categories: Game, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
2    Pheasants-disjointed
6 tb Flour
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
6 tb Oil
1/4 c  Lemon juice
15 1/2 oz Consomme
1    Clove garlic-minced
1 1/2 c  Water
From : Sallie Krebs, Thu 06 Jul 95 04:00, Area: COOKING

Dredge pheasant pieces in well seasoned flour. Brown well in hot oil
in a large skillet. Add lemon juice, consomme, garlic and water.
Cover and simmer about one hour or until tender. Thicken liquid for
gravy.
