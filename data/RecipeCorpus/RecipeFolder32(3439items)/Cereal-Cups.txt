Title: Cereal Cups
Categories: Kids, Snacks
Yield: 16 servings
1    Recipe
Rice Krispies* Treats
Prepare recipe.  Divide and shape warm mixture evenly into 16- 2 1/2"
muffin pan cups sprayed with Pam.  Let cereal cups cool until firm.
Remove from pan and fill with your favorite candies, rainbow sherbet
or non-fat frozen yogurt.
