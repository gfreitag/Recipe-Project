Title: PEPPY CHEESE FILLED HAMBURGERS
Categories: Sandwich, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Dry bread crumbs
1    Small onion,finely chopped
1    Egg
1 ts Salt
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Pepper
MMMMM--------------------PEPPY CHEESE FILLING-------------------------
1/4 c  Shredded American cheese
1/4 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
2 tb Mayonnaise
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Prepared mustard
1/4 ts Pepper
Mix all ingredients except fillings. Shape mixture into 12 thin
patties, about 3 1/2 inches in diameter. Top each of 6 patties with a
filling, spreading to within 1/2 inch of edge. Cover with a remaining
patty, seating edges firmly. Broil or grill patties 4 inches from
heat, turning once, to desired doneness, about 10 minutes.
