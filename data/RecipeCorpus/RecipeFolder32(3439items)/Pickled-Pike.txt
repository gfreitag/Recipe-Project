Title: Pickled Pike
Categories: Jewish, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
4    Onions -- sliced
6 sl Pike
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Water
1/2 c  White vinegar
1 1/2 tb Sugar
2 ts Pickling spice
2    Bay leaves
Combine 2 sliced onions, the pike, salt, pepper and water in a
saucepan. Bring to a boil and cook over low heat 25 minutes.
Carefully remove the fish and place in a bowl or jar alternating in
layers with the remaining 2 sliced onions.    Mix the vinegar, sugar,
