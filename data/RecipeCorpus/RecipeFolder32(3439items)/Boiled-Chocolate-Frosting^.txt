Title: Boiled Chocolate Frosting^
Categories: Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 oz Chocolate
1/2 c  Milk
2    Egg whites
Vanilla
Powdered sugar
In double boiler, boil chocolate and cream; cool, add vanilla. Beat
egg whites to a stiff froth; add the sugar until stiff enough to cut.
Combine the two mixtures, beat and spread.
