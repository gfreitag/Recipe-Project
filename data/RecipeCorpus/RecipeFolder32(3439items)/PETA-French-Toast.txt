Title: PETA French Toast
Categories: Breakfast, Pcake/waffl
Yield: 2 servings
1 1/2 c  Soy milk
2 tb Flour; unbleached
1 tb Nutritional yeast
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1 tb Veg oil
4 sl Bread; to 6 slices, thick
Mix the soy milk, flour, yeast, sugar and cinnamon vigorously with a
wire whisk or beater.  Pour the mixture into a wide, shallow or pie
pan.

Heat the oil in a frying pan or skillet.  Dip a bread slice into the
mixture and then place in skillet.  Fry each side until golden brown
and crispy, about 5-7 mins total.  Remove from pan and keep warm
while cooking remaining slices.

Serve hot with maple syrup.
