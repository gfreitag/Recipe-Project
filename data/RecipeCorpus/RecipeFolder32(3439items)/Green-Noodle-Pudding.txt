Title: Green Noodle Pudding
Categories: Jewish, Pasta, Vegetables, Puddings
Yield: 4 servings
4    Egg yolks
4 tb Chopped scallions
1 1/2 c  Cooked chopped spinach
1/2 lb Fine noodles -- cooked and
Drained
1 ts Salt
4    Egg whites -- stiffly
Beaten
Beat the egg yolks, then stir in the scallions, spinach, noodles and
salt.
Fold in the egg whites.  Pour into a 1 1/2-quart greased baking
dish. Bake in a 350 degree oven 30 minutes.  Serve hot with meat or
dairy dishes. Serves 4 to 6.    Recipe Sour
