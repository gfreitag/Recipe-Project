Title: Quick Chili Dip
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
15 oz Chili no beans
8 oz Cream cheese -- cubed
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
4 oz Green chiles -- chopped
4 dr Hot pepper sauce
Combine chili, cream cheese, onion, chilies and hot pepper sauce.
Heat until cheese melts, stirring frequently.  Serve hot with chips
or vegetable dippers.
