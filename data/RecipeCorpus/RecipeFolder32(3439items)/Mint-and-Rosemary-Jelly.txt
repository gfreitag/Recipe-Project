Title: Mint and Rosemary Jelly
Categories: Dips/spread, Herbs/spice
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Packed mint leaves
1/4 c  Rosemary leaves
1 c  Water
3/4 c  Orange juice
3 c  Sugar
Green food colouring
-(optional)
1/2 bottle liquid pectin
Rosemary sprigs for garnish
chop mint and rosemary.  Place in large saucepan, add water, and
orange juice.  Bring to boil.  Remove from heat.  Let stand 10
minutes to infuse.

Strain mixture and return mint infusion to saucepan; add sugar and
food colouring (if using) bring to boil.  Stir in pectin and bring
back to full rolling boil.  boil vigourously for 1 minute.

Remove from heat, skim foam off and stir for one minute.  Place in
jars, with rosemary sprig and seal. Source: Canadian Living
