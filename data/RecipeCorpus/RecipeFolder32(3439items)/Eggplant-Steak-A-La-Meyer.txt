Title: Eggplant Steak A La Meyer
Categories: Jewish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
1 md Eggplant
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Egg yolks -- beaten
1 c  Grated American cheese
Eggs -- fried as needed
Peel the eggplant and cut lengthwise in 1-inch slices. Soak in cold
salted water for 1 hour.  Drain and dry.    Place the eggplant slices
side by side in a buttered baking pan.Sprinkle with the salt and
pepper and brush with the egg yolks.  Sprinkle with
