Title: Quick Kasha Cholent
Categories: Jewish, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Dried lima beans
4 lb Chuck or brisket
2    Onions -- sliced
2 tb Fat
1 tb Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Garlic powder
5 c  Boiling water
1 c  Kasha -- * see note
* Note:  The recipe for "Kasha" is included in this database.    Soak
the beans in water to cover for 12 hours.  Drain.    Brown the meat
and onions in the fat.  Add the salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder,
water and beans. Cover loosely and cook over l little more water if
necessary. Cook 1/2 hour longer or until meat and beans are tender.
Slice the meat and serve with the beans and kasha. Serves 8 to 10.
