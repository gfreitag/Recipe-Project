Title: Cholent With Knaidel
Categories: Jewish
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Dried lima beans
3 lb Flanken
3    Onions -- sliced
3/4 c  Fat
3    Potatoes -- peeled and
Halved
3 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2    Eggs
2 ts Sugar
1/4 c  Water
3/4 c  Matzo or cracker meal
Soak the beans overnight in water to cover.  Drain.    Use a Dutch
oven or baking dish and brown the meat and onions in 1/4 cup fat.
Add the potatoes and 2 teaspoons salt and the pepper.    Beat the
eggs, sugar, remaining fat and remaining salt together.
