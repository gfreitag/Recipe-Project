Title: Roast Pheasant With Purple Plum Sauce - United States
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce, Game, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
2    Young pheasants-whole
1    Onion-halved
2    Stalks celery-diced
2    Apples-diced
4    Bacon slices
Salt & pepper to taste
Place onions, celery and apple slices inside birds. Two strips of
bacon over each breast. Season with salt and pepper. Roast in a
preheated 350 oven for two hours basting with the plum sauce during
the last half hour of roasting. Serve the remaining sauce in a sauce
boat.

PLUM SAUCE

1 lb can purple plums 1/4 cup butter 1/4 cup onions-chopped 3 tbs
lemon juice 1/4 cup brown sugar 2 tsp chili sauce 1 tsp
worcestershire 1/2 tsp ginger

Drain plums and reserve liquid. Remove pit from plums and puree in
blender. Melt butter in a small sauce pan, add onions and saute until
golden brown. Stir in remaining ingredients including plums and
liquid, simmer for thirty minutes.
