Title: Tomato Mint Relish
Categories: Relishes, Tomatoes
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Whole Tomatoes; peeled
1/2 c  Red Onion; minced
1 c  Red Wine Vinegar
1/2 c  Light brown Sugar
2 ts Fresh Mint; chopped
1 ts Cumin; ground
1 ts Coriander; ground
1 ts Black Mustard Seeds
1 ts Gingerroot, fresh; peeled;
-minced
2 ea Garlic Cloves; minced
1/2 ts Cayenne
1/2 ts Sea Salt
TOMATO MINT RELISH

[By: Tracy Pikhart Ritter] [Makes 1 1/2 cups]

Place all ingredients in a nonreactive saucepan. Bring to a boil,
reduce to a simmer, and cook for 30 minutes over low heat. Cool
before serving.

SERVING SIZE: 2 tablespoons

18 calories 0.2 gram fat 0 milligrams cholesterol 40 milligrams sodium
