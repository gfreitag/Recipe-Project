Title: White Goddess Salad Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb Mayonaise
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Horseradish
Generous pinch sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Chopped fresh dill
1 ts Freshly ground pepper
Worcestershire sauce
Blend together all ingredients, with a couple of dashes of
worcestershire sauce.  Serve over a crisp mixture of lettuce and
spinach.
