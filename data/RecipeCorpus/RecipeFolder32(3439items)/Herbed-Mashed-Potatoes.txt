Title: Herbed Mashed Potatoes
Categories: Potatoes, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
6 1/2 c  Cubed peeled baking potato
-(about 2 3/4 pounds)
2 ea Garlic cloves, halved
1/2 c  1% low-fat milk
1/2 c  Low-fat sour cream
2 tb Minced fresh parsley
2 tb Minced fresh oregano
1 tb Minced fresh thyme
1 tb Margarine
3/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Place potato and garlic in a large saucepan; cover with water and
bring to a boil.  Cook 20 minutes or until very tender; drain.

Return potato mixture to pan.  Add milk and remaining ingredients;
beat at medium speed of a mixer until smooth.  Yield:  6 servings
(serving size: 1 cup).

CALORIES 184 (23% from fat); PROTEIN 4.8g; FAT 4.7g (sat 2.1g, mono
1.6g, poly 0.8g); CARB 31.8g; FIBER 2.8g; CHOL 8mg; IRON 1.6mg;
SODIUM 344mg; CALC 71mg
