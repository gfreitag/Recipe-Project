Title: White Wine-Marinated Squid
Categories: Seafood, Guestchefs
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  White wine
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Minced garlic
1/4 c  Minced onions
1 ts White pepper
1/2 ts Ground coriander
2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Squid; body cut into rounds
Tentacles left whole
PLACE ALL INGREDIENTS except squid in a 1-quart pot and bring to a
boil over high heat on the stove. Add the squid, cook 30 seconds and
immediately remove from the heat. Pour the contents of the pot into a
glass bowl, cover with a plate and let cool to ro Serve over lettuce
or chilled pasta.
