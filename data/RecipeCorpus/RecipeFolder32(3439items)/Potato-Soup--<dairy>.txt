Title: Potato Soup  <dairy>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
3 tb Butter
1 c  Diced onions
3 c  Cubed potatoes
1    Carrot -- grated
3 c  Water
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ts Caraway seed -- optional
2 tb Farina
3 c  Milk
3 tb Minced parsley
1/2 c  Sour cream
Melt the butter in a saucepan and brown the onions.  Add the potatoes,
carrot, water, salt, pepper and caraway seed. Bring to a boil and
stir in the farina.  Cook over low heat20 minutes.  Stir in the milk
and parsley and bring to boiling point.  Garnish Recipe Source: THE
ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by Bantam
