Title: Creamed Beets
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Grated beets
1 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Water
1 1/2 tb Potato flour
1 c  Light cream
1 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
Cook the beets, lemon juice and water for 15 minutes or until beets
are tender.  Drain.    Mix the potato flour and cream in a saucepan.
Cook over low heat for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Stir in the
beets, salt and butter.  Cook 5 minutes.  Serves
