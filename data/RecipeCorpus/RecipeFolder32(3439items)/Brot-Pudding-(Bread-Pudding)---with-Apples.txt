Title: Brot Pudding (Bread Pudding) - with Apples
Categories: Aust/german, Desserts
Yield: 7 servings
4    OR 5 slices dry bread
2    Apples
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
2 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
Break the bread in small pieces and layer it with apples.  Beat 2
eggs and add sugar,milk, vanilla, and salt.  Pour this mixture over
apples and bread.  Sprinkle top with sugar and cinnamon.  Bake at
325Ã¸ for 1 hour.

Serve hot with wine sauce (see recipe) or cold with cream.
