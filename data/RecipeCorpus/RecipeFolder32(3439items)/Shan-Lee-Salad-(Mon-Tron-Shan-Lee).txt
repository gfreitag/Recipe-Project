Title: Shan Lee Salad (Mon Tron Shan Lee)
Categories: Vegetables, Vietnamese
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Sesame seed oil
3 tb Freshly squeezed orange
Juice
1    Butterhead lettuce
Leaves washed and separated
4 tb Bamboo shoots
1.  Mix othe sesame oil and orange juice together, and toss into the
salad leaves and bamboo shoots.  Serves as a simple accompaniment to
the sate dishes or Stuffed Chicken Wings. Recipe Source: VIETNAMESE
COOKING - EXOTIC DELIGHTS FROM INDO-CHINA by Paulette Do Van.

formatted by Olivia Liebermann RDSE68B on May, 1995.
