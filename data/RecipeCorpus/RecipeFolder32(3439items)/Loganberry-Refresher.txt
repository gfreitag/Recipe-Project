Title: Loganberry Refresher
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Water
7 c  Loganberries
2 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  Cold water
Combine sugar and water in a small saucepan and cook over medium high
heat until sugar is dissolved, stirring contantly; remove from heat
and chill.

Mash berries, then press through a strainer to remove seeds. Combine
berries with orange juice and lemon juice, and add sugar syrup. Chill
until very cold. Bevore serving, add cold water.

Makes 8 servings

Per serving: Calories: 150 (6% from protein, 92% from carbohydrate,
2% from fat) Protein: 2 grams Fat: 0-1 gram Cholesterol: 0
Carbohydrate: 37 grams Sodium: 4 mg Exchanges: 2 fruit

Source: The Berry Cookbook by Kyle D Fulwiler as published in the
