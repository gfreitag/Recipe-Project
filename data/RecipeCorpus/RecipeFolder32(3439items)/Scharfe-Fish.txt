Title: Scharfe Fish
Categories: Jewish, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
3    Onions -- sliced
3    Carrots -- sliced
2    Celery stalks -- sliced
3 c  Water
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
6 sl Pike
2    Egg yolks
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Chopped parsley
Combine the onions, carrots, celery, water, salt and pepper in a
saucepan.
Bring to a boil and add the fish. Cover and cook over low heat 30
minutes. Transfer fish to a platter and strain the stock. Beat the
egg yolks and lemon juice in a small saucepan. Gradually add the
stock, mixing steadily. Cook over low heat, stirring steadily until
it thickens, but do not let boil.  Pour over the fish. Sprinkle with
the parsl
