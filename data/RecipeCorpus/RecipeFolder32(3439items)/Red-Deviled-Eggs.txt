Title: Red Deviled Eggs
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
6    Egg yolks, hard cooked
2 tb Mayonnaise or salad
-dressing
1 ts Grated onion
1 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 cn Deviled ham (2 1/2 oz)
Salt and pepper to taste
Mix all ingredients together - adjust mayo for desired creaminess.
Pile back into egg white halves. Garnish with a sprinkle of paprika
