Title: Beef Keleguan
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
2 lb Flank steak
1 ts Salt
4    Lemon
1 sm Onion -- chopped
6    Jalapeno peppers (or the
Hot
Slice meat into thin bite-sized strips,  squeezing as much of the
juice out that you can. Place in a bowl and mix in the salt and the
juice of the lemon. Let sit until the meat doesn't look as raw (about
1 hour). Add the onion and chopped red pepper. If it tastes too
lemony for you, add a splash of vinegar.    STEPHANIE WARTHEN
