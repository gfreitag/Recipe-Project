Title: Sweet-and-Sour Hot Slaw
Categories: Salads, Snacks
Yield: 8 servings
1 md Cabbage, shredded (about
2-1/2 lbs)
3/4 c  Chopped onion
3/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1 c  Sugar
1 c  White vinegar
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Celery seeds
1 ts Paprika
3/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Red pepper
Cabbage leaves
Combine first 3 ingredients in a large bowl; set aside. Combine
sugar and remaining ingredients except cabbage leaves; stir until
blended. Pour over cabbage mixture; cover and chill. Drain mixture
and spoon onto cabbage-lined serving bowl.

Recipe from Barbara McGrath in 3/92 "Southern Living". Computerized
by Jeff Pruett.
