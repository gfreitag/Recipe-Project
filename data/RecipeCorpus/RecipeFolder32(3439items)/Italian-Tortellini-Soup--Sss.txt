Title: Italian Tortellini Soup  Sss
Categories: Soup/stew, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
Sherilyn Schamber
1 sm Green pepper -- diced
4 cn 14 1/2 oz beef broth
1 md Zucchini -- sliced
7 c  Water
1 sm Red onion -- chpd
1 lb Sweet Italian sausage --
1/2    "
1 md Tomato -- diced
Pieces
1 tb Fresh basil -- chpd
9 oz Box tortellini
Salt and pepper
9 oz Box spinach tortellini
Parmesan -- opt
1/2 lb Cabbage -- shredded
Combine first 11 ingreds in lg pot. Season with salt and pepper.
Bring to slow boil. Simmer until vegs are tender, about 15 mins.
Ladle soup into bowls. Pass Parmesan if desired. Makes 5 qts,
