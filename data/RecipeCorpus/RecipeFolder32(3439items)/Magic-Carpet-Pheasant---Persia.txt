Title: Magic Carpet Pheasant - Persia
Categories: Curries, Game, Middle east, Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
2    Pheasants-disjointed
2 lg Onions-chopped
4 oz Oil
4    Oranges-peeled
2    Lemons-peeled
4 oz Olives
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Salt
3 oz Soy sauce
1 ts Curry powder
From : Sallie Krebs, Thu 06 Jul 95 04:00, Area: COOKING

Saute the pheasant pieces and onions in the oil until browned. Add the
remaining ingredients, cover and simmer thirty minutes or until
tender.
