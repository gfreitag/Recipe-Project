Title: Streusel Kaffeekuchen (Coffee Cake)
Categories: Cake/cookie, Aust/german
Yield: 1 coffeecake
MMMMM--------------------------STREUSEL-------------------------------
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Melted butter
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
MMMMM------------------------CAKE BATTER-----------------------------
1/4 c  Shortening
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Salt
1 1/2 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
1    Egg
2/3 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
STREUSEL:  Mix ingredients.

CAKE:  Sift flour, measure and sift 3 times with sugar, salt and
baking powder.  Cut the shortening into the flour mixture with a
pastry blender or two knives.  To the well beaten egg, add the milk
and vanilla and stir this into flour mixture.

Pour 1/2 of the batter into a greased pan 6x10-inches.  Sprinkle with
1/2 of the streusel mixture; add the remaining batter, and top with
the other half of the streusel.  Bake at 350Ã¸ F. 25 minutes.
