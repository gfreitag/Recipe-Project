Title: PETA Tofu Fruit Spread
Categories: Dips/spread, Condiments, Breakfast, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2    Sweet apples; peeled, cored
-and chopped
2    Ripe bananas; peeled and
-sliced
1/2 lb Soft tofu; drained and
-crumbled
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1    Whole clove
1/8 ts Nutmeg; grated
Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor and blend until
smooth.  Chill.  Spread on top of your morning bagel or toast.
