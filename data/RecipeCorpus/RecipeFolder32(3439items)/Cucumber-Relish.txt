Title: Cucumber Relish
Categories: Jewish, Relishes, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
5    Cucumbers -- peeled and
Chopped
1 1/2 lb Tomatoes -- diced
2 c  Finely shredded cabbage
2    Green peppers -- minced
2    Onions -- minced
1 1/4 c  Vinegar
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Salt
1 tb Mustard seed
1/2 tb Celery seed
Mix all the ingredients together very thoroughly.  Pack into three
1-quart sterile jars.  Chill for 36 hours before using.    Recipe
Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Published by
Bantam Books, Inc.
