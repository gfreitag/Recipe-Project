Title: Cinnamon Apple Jello Mold *
Categories: Desserts, Salads, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
1 c  Applesauce; hot
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  7-Up,
- OR Red-pop
- OR gingerale
1 sm Red jello ( any flavor)
1 ts Lemon juice
1/3 c  Nuts; chopped
1/3 c  Raisins
1/3 c  Apples; chopped
Mix all except nuts, raisins and apples together. Chill until barely
set. Add nuts, raisins and apples. Chill.
