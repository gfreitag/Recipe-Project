Title: PETA Watercress Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Magerine; or water
2 md Onions; chopped
1 cl Garlic; minced
2 md Potatoes; quartered, peeled
2 lg Bunches watercress; chopped
3 c  Vegetable broth
2 c  Soy milk
;salt and pepper to taste
Heat the margerine or water in a large saucepan over med heat and
lightly cook the onions and garlic for 3 mins.  Add the potatoes,
watercress, and broth and simmer, covered, for 15 mins or until the
potatoes are tender.

Transfer the soup to a blender or food proc and puree the soup until
smooth.  Pour the mixture back into the saucepan and add the soy
milk, salt and pepper.  Cook over low heat until the soup is heated
thru, then serve. From The Compassionate Cook by PETA and Ingrid
