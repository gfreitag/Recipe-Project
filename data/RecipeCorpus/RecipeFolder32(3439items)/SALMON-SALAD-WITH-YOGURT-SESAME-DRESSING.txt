Title: SALMON SALAD WITH YOGURT-SESAME DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Fish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
10 oz Canned pink salmon; drained
1 1/3    Green onion; chopped
1 1/3    Celery stalk; diced
1 1/3 c  Bean sprouts
1 tb Fresh cilantro; chopped
1/3 c  Fresh mint leaves; chopped
1/3 c  Or fresh parsley
4    Tomatoes
4    Leaf lettuce leaves
1 1/3 tb Sesame seeds; toasted*
MMMMM-------------------YOGURT SESAME DRESSING------------------------
2 2/3 tb Yogurt
1 1/3 tb Mayonnaise
3 ts Sesame oil
3 ts Lemon juice
*Toast sesame seeds in small skillet over high heat, stirring for 2
minutes or until golden.

Combine salmon, green onion, celery, bean sprouts, cilantro and mint
or parsley.  In small bowl, combine yogurt, mayonnaise, sesame oil
and lemon juice; pour over salmon mixture and mix lightly.

Quarter tomatoes, cutting almost through; place on lettuce; lined
plates; spoon salmon mixture in center.  Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
