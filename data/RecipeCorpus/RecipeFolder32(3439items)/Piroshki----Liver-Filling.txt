Title: Piroshki -  Liver Filling
Categories: Jewish, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Minced onions
1/4 lb Mushrooms -- chopped
6 tb Chicken fat
1/2 lb Chicken livers
2 tb Minced parsley
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Brown the onions and mushrooms in half the fat.  Remove onions and
mushrooms and reserve.  Melt remaining fat in the same pan and cook
the livers in it 10 minutes.  Chop together, until very smooth, the
onions, mushrooms, liver, parsley, salt and pepper.  Cool. Place
filling on dough. RecipeSource: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie
Grossinger Published by Bantam
