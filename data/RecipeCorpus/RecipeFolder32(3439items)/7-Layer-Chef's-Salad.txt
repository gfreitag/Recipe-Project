Title: 7 Layer Chef's Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Lettuce -- bite size
3 c  Spinach -- bite size
2 c  Smoked ham -- chopped
2 c  Cauliflower flowerets
1 md Zucchini -- thinly sliced
2    Eggs, hard-boiled -- Peeled
& Sliced
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Creamy italian dressing
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
1/4 c  Parsley -- chopped
Mix lettuce and spinach in large glass bowl.  Layer ham and
cauliflower on lettuce mixture.  Place zucchini in center of bowl.
Arrange egg slices around edge of bowl.  Mix mayonaise and italian
dressing; carefully spread over top of salad to edge of bowl.
Sprinkle with cheese and parsley. Cover tightly and refrigerate no
longer than 24 hours.  Toss before serving if desired.  780 calories
and 70 grams fat.  (Yikes!!! Wish I'd seen that before I went to the
trouble to type this in.  Oh well...)
