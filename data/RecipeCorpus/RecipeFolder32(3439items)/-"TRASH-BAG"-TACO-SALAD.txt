Title:  "TRASH BAG" TACO SALAD
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salads, Meats, Tasteofhome
Yield: 6 servings
3 lb Ground beef
3    Env. taco seasoning mix
3    Heads lettuce, shredded
3 c  (12 oz.) shredded cheddar
-cheese
3 c  Chopped tomatoes
2 c  Chopped onion
3 cn (4 1/4 oz. ea.) chopped or
-sliced
Ripe olives, drained
2 cn (15 oz. ea.) ranch or chili
-beans,
Drained
16 oz Bag corn chips
16 oz Bottle Catalina salad
-dressing
12 oz Jar salsa
"This is the simplest and most economical crowd-pleaser we've ever run
across." - Margie Dodd

Brown beef; drain.  Add taco seasoning and prepare according to
package directions.  Cool.  Toss with remaining ingredients in a
large plastic bag or container.

From:  "Taste of Home" Magazine, April/May 1995
