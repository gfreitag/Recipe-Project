Title: Special Chicken Salad
Categories: Salads, Poultry
Yield: 12 servings
5 c  Chicken, cooked, diced
2 tb Oil
2 tb Orange Juice
2 tb Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Green Grapes
1 1/2 c  Celery, sliced
1 c  Pineapple, canned tidbits,
- drained
1 c  Mandarin Oranges, canned,
- drained
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 pk Macaroni Rings, cooked
Combine oil, orange juice, vinegar, and salt.  Stir in chicken, cover
and refrigerate overnight.

Add remaining ingredients to above mixture and toss gently.
