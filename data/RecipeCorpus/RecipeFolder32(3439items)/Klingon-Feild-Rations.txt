Title: Klingon Feild Rations
Categories: Snacks, Nuts/grains, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
Mix 1.5 cups popped popcorn with 1 cup of mixed nuts. Whip the whites
of 2 eggs until stiff, gradually blending in 1 3/8 cups of
confectioner's sugar. Fold this mixture into popcorn and nuts. Drop
by teaspoon onto oiled cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 15
minutes, or until brown. Submitted By SHARON STEVENS   On    7-09-95
