Title: Sun-Dried Tomato Rounds
Categories: Appetizers, Tomatoes
Yield: 4 servings
1    Baguette
Olive oil
6 oz (2 jars) sun-dried tomatoes
2    Garlic clove(s), minced
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
Fresh basil
Cut the baguette into -inch-thick slices. Brush one side with olive
oil and broil until the bread is golden and crisp.

Pure the sun-dried tomatoes with their oil in a food processor or
blender. Add the minced garlic and season to taste with freshly
grated Parmesan cheese. Spread the pure on the baguette slices and
garnish with fresh basil.
