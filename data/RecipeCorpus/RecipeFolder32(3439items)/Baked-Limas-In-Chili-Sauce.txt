Title: Baked Limas In Chili Sauce
Categories: Jewish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Dried lima beans
2 ts Salt
2 ts Sugar
1 c  Chili sauce
2 tb Melted butter or fat
1    Bay leaf
Soak the beans overnight in water to cover.  Drain.  Add fresh water
to cover, and bring to a boil.  Add the salt and sugar. Cook over low
heat 1 1/2 hours.  Drain.    Combine the beans, chili sauce, butter
or fat and bay leaf in a baking dish.  Bake in a 350 degree oven 1
hour. Remove bay leaf.Serves 6.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH
COOKING by Jennie Grossinger
