Title: Ginger Snaps {Fredericksburg}
Categories: Cake/cookie, Aust/german
Yield: 1 recipe
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Molasses
1    Egg
1/4 ts Salt
2 ts Soda
3 ts Ginger
4 c  Flour
Cream shortening, sugar and ginger.  Add unbeaten egg and beat
well. Add molasses and mix thoroughly.  Sift flour, soda and salt
five times. Add to creamed mixture.  Form into little balls and bake
in slow oven, 325ø - 350ø on greased cookie sheet, 12 to 15 minutes.

~ Mrs. Kurt Eckhardt, Fredericksburg, Tx typed & posted by teri
