Title: Corn on the Cob Cocoons (Miracle Whip)
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Miracle Whip
1 tb Lime juice
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Ground red pepper
Corn, 4-6 ears, husked
Heat grill.  Mix dressing juice and seasonings.  Place each ear
of corn on 12-inch square piece of foil.  Brush dressing mixture on
all sides of corn; roll foil around corn, sealing ends.
Place corn on grill over medium-hot coals.  Grill 20 to 30
minutes or until tender, turning 1/4 turn every 5 minutes.

Prep Time: 5 minutes plus refrigerating**
Grilling Time: 30 minutes
:
:

** NOTE:  The Prep Time says 5 minutes plus "refrigerating". However,
the directions say nothing about it.  I suppose you could refrigerate
the corn after the mixture is brushed on and it's been rolled in
foil. I don't think it's necessary, though.
