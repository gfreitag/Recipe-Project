Title: French Toast
Categories: Jewish, Breakfast
Yield: 2 servings
2    Eggs
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Light cream
1 tb Sugar
4 sl White bread
4 tb Butter
Beat together the eggs, salt, cream and sugar.  Dip the bread in the
mixture.    Fry in the butter until browned on both sides.  Serve with
jelly, syrup or cinnamon-sugar.  Serves 2.    Recipe Source: THE ART
OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger Publi
