Title: Noodle-Apple Pudding
Categories: Jewish, Pasta, Fruit, Puddings
Yield: 8 servings
2    Eggs
4 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Grated apples
1/2 c  Seedless raisins
4 c  Cooked fine noodles --
Drained
3 tb Melted butter or fat
Beat the eggs, sugar, salt and cinnamon together.  Stir in the apples,
raisins, noodles and butter or fat.  Turn into a greased baking dish.
Bake in a 400 degree oven 40 minutes or until browned. Serves 6 to 8.
Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKI
