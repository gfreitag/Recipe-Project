Title: Bourbon Marinade
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Bourbon, 1/4 cup soy sauce,
1/4 c  Firmly packed light brown
Sugar, 1 large onion,
Chopped, 3 tablespoons
Dijon-style mustard, 1
ts Worcestershire sauce
In a ceramic or glass dish combine the bourbon, the soy sauce, the
sugar, the onion, the mustard, and the Worcestershire sauce. Use the
marinade to marinate pork, beef, or chicken in the refrigerator
overnight and to baste it as it is grilled. Source: JULY
1983/GOURMET, p.136
