Title: Fried Fish
Categories: Jewish, Fish
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  White corn meal
1/4 c  Flour
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
6    Fish fillets or slices
4 tb Salad oil
2    Onions -- diced
Mix the corn meal, flour, salt and pepper together.  Dip the fish in
this mixture, coating both sides well.    Heat half the oil in a
skillet. Brown the onions in it. Remove onions and reserve.  Add
remaining oil to the skillet and brown the fish on both sides. Cover
with the onions and cook 5 minutes.  Serve hot or cold.  Serves 6.
Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH COOKING   by Jennie Grossinger
