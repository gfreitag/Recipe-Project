Title: Baked Herring
Categories: Jewish, Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
4    Salt herring fillets
1/2 c  Milk
1/3 c  Flour
4 tb Unsalted butter
3    Onions -- sliced
Soak the herring in water to cover overnight.  Change the water once.
Drain.    Dip the herring first in the milk and then in the flour
(reserving the milk).  Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a baking dish and
arrange the herring in it.  Cover with the sliced onions.  Dot with
remaining butter and add reserved milk.    Bake in a 375 degree oven
30 minutes.  Serves 4 to 6.    Recipe Source: THE ART OF JEWISH
COOKING by Jennie Grossinger Published by B
