Title: Favorite Tuna Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
3/4 c  Milk
2 cn Tuna,drained and flaked
2 c  Cooked noodles or macaroni
2 tb Chopped parsley
2 tb Pimiento
2 tb Buttered bread crumbs
In a 1.5 casserole,blend soup and milk.Add tuma,noodlesl,parsley and
pimiento.Bake in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes. Top with crumbs,bake
5 minutes more.
