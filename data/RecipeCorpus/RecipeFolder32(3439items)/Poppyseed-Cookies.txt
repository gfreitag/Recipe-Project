Title: Poppyseed Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Guestchefs
Yield: 96 servings
1/2 lb Unsalted butter -- softened
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Prepared poppyseed paste
2 3/4 c  Flour
MMMMM--------------------TO DUST THE COOKIES-------------------------
1/4 c  Powdered sugar
