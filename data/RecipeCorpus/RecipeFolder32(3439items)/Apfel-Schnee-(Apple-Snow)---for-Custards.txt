Title: Apfel Schnee (Apple Snow) - for Custards
Categories: Aust/german, Desserts, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 recipe
1 qt Milk
6    Eggs
4 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
6    Apples
Let milk come to a boil.  Beat sugar and yolks of eggs well and stir
into milk.  Add a pinch of salt and continue stirring to prevent
curdling.

When cool, add vanilla.  Before serving, beat the egg whites to a
stiff froth with 1/2 cup sugar.  Fold in the grated apples.  Drop by
spoonful on custard.
