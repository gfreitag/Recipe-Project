Title: Cream Of Corn Soup  <dairy>
Categories: Jewish, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 c  Minced onion
3 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
3 c  Milk
1 cn (#2) Cream-style corn
2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 ts Minced pimento
Cook the onion in the butter for 10 minutes.  Sprinkle with the flour
and gradually add the milk, stirring constantly until mixture reaches
the boiling point.  Add the corn, salt, pepper and pimento. Cook over
low heat 10 minutes.  Serves 6.
