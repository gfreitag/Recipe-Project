Title: Kartoffelpuree Meerrettich (Mashed Potatoes/Horseradish)
Categories: Aust/german, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
5 ea Potatoes -- Medium
-quartered
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
Pepper -- Freshly Ground
1/2 c  Sour Cream
1 tb Horseradish
2 ts Parsley -- Minced
Boiling Water
Peel and quarter potatoes.  Cook in boiling salted water in
medium-size saucepan until tender, drain.  Mash, adding 1 T butter
and the pepper. Add sour cream, horseradish, and minced parsley.
Whip as for mashed potatoes. Place in serving bowl; top with 1 T
melted butter and serve.
