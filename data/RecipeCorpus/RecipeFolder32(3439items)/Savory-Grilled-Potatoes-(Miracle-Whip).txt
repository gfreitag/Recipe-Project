Title: Savory Grilled Potatoes (Miracle Whip)
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Miracle Whip Dressing
3 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lg Onion, sliced
3 lg Potatoes, 1/4" slices
Mix dressing, garlic and seasonings in large bowl.  Add potatoes
and onion; toss to coat.
Divide potato mixture evenly among 6 (12-inch) square pieces of
foil. Seal each to form packet.  Place on grill over medium-hot coals.
Grill, covered, 25 to 30 minutes or until potatoes are tender.

OVEN DIRECTIONS:  Heat oven to 425.  Place foil packets on center
rack in oven.  Bake 30 - 35 minutes, or until tender.
