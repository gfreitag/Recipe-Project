Title: CROCKPOT STUFFING
Categories: Breads, Crockpot
Yield: 7 Servings
1/3 c  Margerine
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped celery
1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
2 tb Chopped parsley
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sage
1/4 ts Thyme or marjarom
8 oz Canned mushrooms
6 1/2 c  Bread cubes, dry
10 1/2 oz Can cream of chicken soup
MMMMM-----------------WITH WATER ADDED TO EQUAL----------------------
MMMMM---------------------------2 CUPS--------------------------------
1    Egg, well beaten
Saute onions, celery and mushrooms in margerine until tender,
approximately 7 minutes.  Combine all ingredients. Add egg last. Pour
mixture into crockpot, cover, cook on high 45 minutes. Turn heat to
low for 5 to 8 hours.  Determine if you should use full amount of
liquid by the moistness of the cubes. For large recipe 13 to 14 cups,
double all ingredients except soup.
