Title: CREAMY CHICKEN PRIMAVERA 2
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Butter or margarine
3/4 lb Boneless skinless chicken
-breasts, cut into strips
1 pk Green Giant Pasta Accents
-Primavera (or any flavor)
-Pasta and Vegetables
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Dairy sour cream
In large skillet, melt butter. Add chicken; cook until no longer
pink. Add pasta and vegetables and water. Cover; cook over medium
heat 7-9 minutes until vegetables are crisp-tender, stirring
occasionally. Stir in sour cream; heat throughly. 4 servings.
