Title: CROCKPOT FAVORITE BRISKET
Categories: Crockpot, Vegetables, Poultry
Yield: 7 Servings
4 lb Fresh beef brisket
2 ts Salt
2 ts Dry mustard
2 ts Paprika
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Garlic powder
Trim all excess fat from brisket. Combine seasonings unti well
blended; rub into brisket.  Place meat in crockpot with fat side up,
cutting to fit if necessary.  Cover and cook on low setting for 8 to
12 hours. Remove brisket from liquid and cut across  the grain into
thin slices. Serve au jus.
