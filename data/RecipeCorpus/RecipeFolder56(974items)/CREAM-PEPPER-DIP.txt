Title: CREAM PEPPER DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk (8-oz) soften cream cheese
1/3 c  Fine chopped hot pepper
Rings
1 tb Liquid from hot pepper
Rings
2 tb Fine chopped onion
2 tb Apple jelly or sweet
Orange marmalade
2 tb Fine chopped celery
2 tb Fine chopped sweet red
Pepper
Hot pepper rings for
Garnish
In medium bowl, stir cream cheese until smooth. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Cover; refrigerate until serving time, at least 2 hours.
Garnish with red pepper rings. Serve with fresh vegetables or
crackers for dipping.
