Title: CREOLE SEAFOOD SEASONING - GREAT CHEFS
Categories: Spices
Yield: 2 Cups
2 tb Oregano
1/3 c  Salt
1 tb Salt
1/4 c  Garlic, granulated
1/4 c  Pepper, black
2 tb Thyme
1/3 c  Paprika
1 tb Paprika
3 tb Onion, granulated
Combine all of the ingredients and mix thoroughly. Pour the
mixture into a large glass jar for storage.

This seasoning can be kept indefinitely.

Source: Great Chefs of New Orleans, Tele-record Productions
:    Box 71112, New Orleans, Louisiana - 1983
:    Chef Gerhard Brill, Commander's Palace Restaurant, New
Orleans
