Title: CRISPY CHICKEN WINGS DGSV43A
Categories: Poultry, Appetizers
Yield: 20 Servings
2 lb Chicken wings
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Margarine; melted
2 tb Parsley; chopped
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
1/4 ts Pepper
Cut chicken wings in two pieces, discarding the tips.
In a small shallow bowl, combine butter and garlic posder. In another
bowl, combine remaining ingredients. Dip chicken into butter mixture,
then into crumb mixture.  Place on greased cookie sheet; bake at 350
degrees for 50 to 60 minutes or until done. A recipe of Nancy Lesky
of La Crosse, Wisconsin reformated and brought to you by Judy Lausch
DGSV43A.
