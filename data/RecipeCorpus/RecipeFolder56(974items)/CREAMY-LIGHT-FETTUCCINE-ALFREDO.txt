Title: CREAMY LIGHT FETTUCCINE ALFREDO
Categories: Digest, Nov.
Yield: 1 Servings
12 oz Dry fettuccine
2 c  Soy milk
4    Cloves garlic, minced
6 tb Grated soy cheese
1/4    Chopped fresh parsley
x  Freshly ground black pepper
2 c  Chopped and steamed
x  Veg. of choice such as
Carrots,
Broccoli, peas, etc.
Cook pasta, drain.  Place pasta in a large nonstick saute pan along
with milk and garlic.  Bring to a simmer, stirring frequently. Add
cheese. Continue cooking until cheese melts and sauce thickens. Stir
in parsley and black pepper.  Add steamed veg. toss gently and serve.
Makes 4 servings.

Per serving: 416 cal.; 25g protein; 4g fat; 68g carbo.; 150mg
cholest.; 343mg sodium, 4g fiber.  This should cure that "heart
attack on a plate"!
