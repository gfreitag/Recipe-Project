Title: CREOLE STEAK STRIPS (CROCKPOT)
Categories: Beef, Crockpot, Mushrooms, Vegetables, Main dishes
Yield: 5 Servings
1 1/2 lb Boneless round steak
Salt and pepper
1    Onion -- chopped
1 c  Sliced celery
1 c  Seasoned tomato juice
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1 md Green pepper -- chopped
1 pk Frozen okra (or 1 1/2 c.
Fresh( -- (10 ounce)
1 cn Sliced mushrooms -- (2 1/2
Ounce)
Drained
Carrot curls (opt.)
Cut steak into strips, about 1/2" wide and 2" long. Sprinkle with
salt and= pepper. Place in crockpot with onion, celery, tomato juice,
Worcestershire= sauce and garlic powder. Cover and cook on Low for
6-8 hours. Turn control= to High. Add green pepper and partially
thawed pkra and mushrooms. Cover= and cook on High for 30 minutes
until okra is done. Garnish with carrot= curls, if desired.
