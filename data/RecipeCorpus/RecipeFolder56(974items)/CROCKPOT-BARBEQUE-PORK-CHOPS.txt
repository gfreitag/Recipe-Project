Title: CROCKPOT BARBEQUE PORK CHOPS
Categories: Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
Pork chops
Barbeque sauce -- (can be
Bottled)
cover both sides of pork chops with barbeque sauce. Pour a little
barbeque sauce in the bottom of the crock pot. Add pork chops---try
not to stack them but spread out as much as possible. Pour rest of
barbeque sauce on the chops. Low heat all day.
