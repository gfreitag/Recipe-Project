Title: CROCKPOT CHICKEN MARENGO
Categories: Crockpot, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM--------------------------GRDG72B-------------------------------
2 1/2 lb Chicken; cut up.
1/2 c  White wine; dry
1/4 lb Mushrooms; fresh
1 pk Spaghetti sauce mix 1 1/20 o
2    Tomatoes; fresh, quartered
Place chicken parts in crockpot. Combine spaghetti sauce mix with
wine;pour over chicken.Cover and cook on low 6-7 hours. Turn control
to high. Add tomatoes and mushrooms. Cover and cook on high 30-40
minutes or until tomatoes are done.
