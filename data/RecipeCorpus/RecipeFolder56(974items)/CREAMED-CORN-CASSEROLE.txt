Title: CREAMED CORN CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Creamed corn 15 0z.
1/4 c  Melted butter
2    Beaten eggs
1/2 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 ts Salt
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Diced cheddar cheese
combine all ingredients and pour into a well greased casserole dish.
Bake in 350 F oven 1 hour.
