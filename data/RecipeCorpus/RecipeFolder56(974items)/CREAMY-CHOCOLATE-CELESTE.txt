Title: CREAMY CHOCOLATE CELESTE
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate
Yield: 8 Servings
-JUDI M. PHELPS
1 pk Semisweet chocolate; 8
-squares, 1 oz each
1    Square unsweetened chocolate
-1 oz
2 tb Butter or margarine
2 tb Strong coffee
Few grains salt
2    Eggs; separated
1 tb Brandy
2 c  Whipping cream
*********GARNISH***********
Whipped cream (optional)
Chocolate; shaved (optional)
Walnuts; chopped (optional)
Melt chocolates with the butter over hot, not boiling, water,
stirring to blend until smooth.  Remove from heat; stir in coffee,
salt, egg yolks, and brandy. Cool slightly. Beat egg whites stiff;
fold into chocolate mixture slowly but thoroughly.  Whip cream until
it mounds softly but is not stiff. Fold in until a marbled effect is
obtained. Spoon into 8 dessert dishes, mounding high.  Chill several
hours. If desired, garnish with small swirls of additional whipped
cream, shaved chocolate, and a sprinkle of finely chopped walnuts.
Makes 8 servings. Source: Parade Cookbook.
