Title: CROCKPOT BBQ BEEF SANDWICHES
Categories: Crockpot, Barbeque, Beef, Sandwiches
Yield: 6 Servings
3 lb Lean chuck or arm roast
1 c  Bottled BBQ sauce
3/4 c  -Water
1 tb Dried onion flakes
1 pk Hamburger or sandwich buns
Sliced red onion
Sliced dill pickles
Trim roast of all visible fat.  Place in crock pot and add remaining
ingredients.  Cover and cook on LOW for 6 to 8 hours, until meat is
very tender.  Using two forks, shred the meat into bite-size pieces.
Turn the heat to HIGH and cook for about 30 minutes, until sauce is
reduced. Serve on hamburger or sandwich buns, with sliced onions and
sliced dill pickles.
