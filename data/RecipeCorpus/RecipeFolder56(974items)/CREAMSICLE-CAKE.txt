Title: CREAMSICLE CAKE
Categories: Box cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Orange supreme cake mix
1 pk Orange jello
1/2 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Water; cold
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 sm Cool whip
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 sm Instant vanilla pudding
Orange flavoring
Mix cake as directed on box for 9x13" pan. Cool. Punch holes with meat
fork. Mix jello with boiling water and cold water and pour over cake.
Refrigerate cake for 1 hour to set.
TOPPING: Mix pudding with milk. Add a few drops of orange flavoring
to taste. Then fold in cool whip. Frost as thick as you like. Keep
refrigrated.
