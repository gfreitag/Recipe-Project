Title: CROCKPOT BARBRQUE BEEF
Categories: Crockpot, Beef, Barbeque, Sandwiches
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM------------------LORI MCDANIELS (MTMN60B-----------------------
4 lb Crossrib roast
2    Bottles BBQ sauce
-your choice
Barbeque buns
Put roast in crockpot with only a small amount of water, maybe 1/2
cup, cook on LOW 8 to 10 hours, depending on the size of the roast.
You should be able to shred it apart with a fork. Then add the bbq
sauce, heat thoroughly.  Serve on large buns.
