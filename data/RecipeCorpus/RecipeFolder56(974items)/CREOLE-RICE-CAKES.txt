Title: CREOLE RICE CAKES
Categories: Rice
Yield: 4 Servings
4 sl Bacon
3 tb Onion -- chopped
3 tb Green pepper -- chopped
1 c  Cooked rice
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Canned tomatoes
2    Eggs -- beaten
Fry bacon crisp, leaving bacon fat in pan.  Chop bacon and combine
with all other ingredients in the order given to make a batter. Cook
as fritters in the bacon fat.
