Title: CREAMY BUTTERMILK DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 1 Cup
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Dried parsley flakes
1/2 ts Seasoned salt
1 ts Chopped fresh/dried chives
1    Clove garlic, crushed
In small bowl, combine all ingredients; blend with wire whisk. Cover;
refrigerate several hours to blend flavors. Store in refrigerator.

From Pillsbury Classic Cookbooks  #137, July 1992.
