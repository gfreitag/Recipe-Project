Title: CRETONS
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Minced Pork
1 c  Dry bread crumbs
1 c  Milk
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Grated onion
Place in sauce pan mix well, cover and cook over low heat 1 hour.Stir
once or twice during cooking time. Empty into bowl and refrigerate.
