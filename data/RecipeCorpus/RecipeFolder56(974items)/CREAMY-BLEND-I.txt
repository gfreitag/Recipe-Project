Title: CREAMY BLEND I
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 32 Servings
1 c  Light sour cream
1 c  Cottage cheese --  1% fat
1 ts Vanilla extract
Place all ingredients in a 5-cup blender container. Cover; blend at
high speed for 30 seconds.  Scrape down sides of container; blend for
1 minute longer, or until smooth. Cover; refrigerate for up to one
week. Makes 2 cups.    Nutrition Analysis (per tablespoon): 15
calories, 1g protein, 1g carbohydrate, 0.5g fat, 0mg cholesterol,
35mg sodium. Note: Use in dips, spreads, salad dressings and baked
goods to reduce the fat in your recipes. Use Creamy Blend I when a
smoother, more fluid mixture is preferred; refer to the recipe for
Creamy Blend II when cutting fat is most important.
