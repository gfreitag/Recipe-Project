Title: CREOLE BAKED COD
Categories: Creole, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Corn oil
1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms
1/4 c  Onion,coarsely chopped
1/4 c  Grn bell pepper,coarse chop
2 c  Tomatoes,chopped
1/2 ts Basil,dried
1/4 ts Black pepper
1 lb Cod,cut into 1" cubes
1. In medium skillet, heat corn oil over medium heat.
2. Add mushrooms, onion and bell pepper; stirring frequently, cook 2
to 3 minutes or until tender.
3. Add tomatoes, basil and black pepper; stirring occasionally, cook
10 minutes or until sauce is thick.
4. Place cod in 10x6x2-inch baking dish; spoon sauce over fish.
5. Bake in 350'F. oven 15 to 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily.
