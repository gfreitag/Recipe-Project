Title: CREAMSICLE FRAPPE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 Drinks
2 c  Vanilla ice cream or
Non-dairy frozen dessert
1 c  Orange juice
1 c  Chilled plain or carbonated
Water
1/4 c  Orange liqueur or undiluted
Orange juice concentrate
In a blender or food processor, combine the ice cream or frozen
dessert, juice, water, and liqueur or juice concentrate. Blend until
smooth. Divide into two glasses. My wife likes this with Cointreau.
