Title: CREAMY COCOA CHEESECAKE
Categories: Cheesecakes
Yield: 8 Servings
-DGSV43A
1 c  Graham cracker crumbs
3 tb Sugar
3 tb Margarine
16 oz Cream cheese
3/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Cocoa
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
1 c  Sour cream
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Combine crumbs; sugar, and margarine; press onto bottom and sides of
9" springform pan.Combine softened cream cheese, sugar, cocoa and
vanilla, mixing on electric mixer until well blended. Blend in eggs;
pour into crust.  Bake at 375* for 30 minutes. Combine sour cream,
sugar and vanilla; carefully spread over cheesecake. Bake at 425*, 10
minutes. Loosen cake from rim of pan. Cool; remove rim. Chill.

From Philadelphia Brand Cream Cheese Cookbook.
