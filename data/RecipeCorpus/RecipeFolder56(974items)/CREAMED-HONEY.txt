Title: CREAMED HONEY
Categories: Cookbook, Dehydrator, Honey
Yield: 9 Cups
10 oz Prepared creamed honey
8 c  Decrystallized honey
Combine the prepared honey and decrystallized honey in a large bowl.
Beat with electric mixer 10 to 15 minutes until thoroughly blended.
Cover the honey and let it stand at room temperature for 2 weeks.
After the 2-week period, the honey is ready to use and may be stored
at room temperature up to 1 year.

Makes about 9 cups. ** How To Dry Foods by Deanna DeLong HPBooks,
California 1992 ISBN = 1-55788-050-6
