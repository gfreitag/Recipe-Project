Title: CREAM OF TOMATO SOUP (4B'S RECIPE)
Categories: Soups
Yield: 4 Servings

12 oz Chicken broth
2 tb Butter
2 tb Sugar
1 sm Onion chopped
1 pn Baking soda
2 c  Cream
Mix tomatoes, chicken broth, butter, sugar, onions and baking soda in

boiler. (Do not boil!) Add heated cream to tomato mixture.
