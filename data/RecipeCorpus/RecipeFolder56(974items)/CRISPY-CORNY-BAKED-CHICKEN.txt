Title: CRISPY CORNY BAKED CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Chicken Fryer (2-3 Lbs.)
Cut Up, Skin & Fat Removed
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 c  Skim Milk
1 c  Cornflake Crumbs
2 cl Garlic Minced
Preheat Oven To 400 F. Season Chicken With Salt, Pepper & Garlic. Dip
Into Milk & Roll in Cornflake Crumbs. Place in Lightly Pam Sprayed
Baking Dish Being Careful That The Pieces Do Not Touch. Bake 45 Min.
OR Until Tender. (Fat 5.6.  Chol. 79.)
