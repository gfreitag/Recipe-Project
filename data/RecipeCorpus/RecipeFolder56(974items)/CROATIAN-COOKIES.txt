Title: CROATIAN COOKIES
Categories: Biscuits, Desserts
Yield: 1 Panful :-)
1/2 lb Sweet butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg yolk
1/2 ts Salt
2 1/2 c  Flour
4    Egg whites
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Finely ground walnuts
1 ts Lemon extract
Blackberry jelly
Cream butter and sugar; add egg yolk and salt.  Add and salt. Add
flour gradually and beat.  Pat dough into thin layer in 12 x 12 inch
pan. Beat egg whites; add sugar until stands in stiff peaks. Fold in
ground nuts and flavoring.  Spread jelly over and swirl meringue on
top. Sprinkle with chopped nuts; bake at 350 F about 45 minutes. When
warm, cut in squares.
