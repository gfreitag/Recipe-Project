Title: CREAMY TOMATO & BASIL SAUCE FOR PASTA
Categories: Try it, Starch, Side dish, Sauce
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM----------------POPIEL PASTA MACHINE RECIPE---------------------
6    Roma tomatoes
4 tb Fresh basil; chopped
1/2 ts Garlic; minced
3 tb Cream
Salt Pepper
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese Parsley; minced, for garnish Roma tomatoes;
chopped, for ~ garnish

Puree tomatoes and basil leaves in a blender.  Heat in a saucepan with
garlic, cream and salt and pepper to taste. Pour over freshly-cooked
fresh pasta and sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Garnish with parsley
and chopped tomatoes.
