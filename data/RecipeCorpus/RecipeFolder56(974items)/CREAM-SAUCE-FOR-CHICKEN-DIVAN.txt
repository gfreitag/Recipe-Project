Title: CREAM SAUCE FOR CHICKEN DIVAN
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1 c  Half & half
Salt & pepper to taste
2    Egg yolks, slightly beaten
1 tb Whipped  cream
Melt butter in heavy pan.   Stir in the flour.   Blend well over low
heat.
Slowly add half & half, stirring constantly, bringing slowly to the
boiling point.  Cook 2 min.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Stir in slightly beaten egg yolks. Fold in whipped cream.
