Title: CROCKPOT SMOTHERED STEAK
Categories: Crockpot, Beef
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Chuck steak, strips
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lg Onion, sliced
3 tb Soy sauce
4 oz Mushrooms, drained
1 cn Tomatoes, (16 ounces)
Put meat, flour, salt and pepper in crockpot and stir well to cook
steak.Add remaining ingredients. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours,
or high for four hours. Serve on noodles or potatoes.
