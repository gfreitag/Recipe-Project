Title: CREOLE GINGER CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Boiling water
1 ts Baking soda
2/3 c  Molasses
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 tb Ginger
Combine the water and soda in a bowl.  Add molasses and vegetable
oil. Beat in the flour and ginger. Spoon into a greased 8 x 8 inch
pan and bake at 350 F for 20-30 minutes. Good with vanilla or praline
ice cream.
