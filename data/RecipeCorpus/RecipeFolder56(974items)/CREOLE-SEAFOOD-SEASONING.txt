Title: CREOLE SEAFOOD SEASONING
Categories: Spices
Yield: 1 Cup
2 tb + 1/2 tsp salt
2 tb Garlic powder
2 tb Black pepper
1 tb Cayenne
2 tb + 1/2 tsp paprika
1 1/2 tb Onion powder
1 tb Dried thyme
In a bowl stir together the salt, the garlic powder, the black
pepper, the cayenne, the paprika, the onion powder, and the thyme.
The Creole seafood seasoning keeps in an airtight container for 6
months. Source: Gourmet Magazine
April 1993
