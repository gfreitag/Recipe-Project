Title: CREAMY HORSERADISH DIP #2
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Cubed Velveeta cheese
Spread
1/3 c  Horseradish
1/4 c  Milk
Microwave process cheese spread, sauce and milk in 1 quart bowl on
high 4-5 minutes or until process cheese spread is melted; stirring
every 2 minutes. Serve with hot cooked chicken nuggets. Conventional:
Combine process cheese spread, sauce and milk in sauce pan; stir over
low heat until process cheese spread is melted. Serve as directed.
