Title: CROCKPOT SWISS STEAK
Categories: None, Crockpot
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Round steak; cut serv. size
1 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes
1/4 ts Pepper
15 oz Tomato sauce
1    Onion; sliced
Flour meat, blend remaining ingredients, except onion. Put meat in
crockpot, top with sliced onion, pour on sauce. Cover, cook on low 6-8
hours.
