Title: CROCKPOT STROGANOFF
Categories: Crockpot, Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 1/2    Onions; sliced
1    Cloves garlic; minced
2 tb Flour
Salt and pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
1 cn Cream of celery or mushroom
1 c  Sour cream
Brown ground beef; drain fat. Add onions and garlic. Stir until
wilted. Add flour, salt, pepper and paprika. Mixing well. Stir in
undiluted soup; mix well. Put all in crockpot on low for 6 hours.
When you get ready to serve add sliced mushrooms and sour cream.
Serve over noodles rice or potatoes.
