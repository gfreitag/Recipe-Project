Title: CREAMT CHICKEN PRIMAVERA
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
2 ts Butter or margarine
3/4 lb Boneless skinless chicken
Breasts -- cut into strips
1 ea 16 oz. pkg green giant
Primavera frozen
Pasta and vegetables
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Light sour cream
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown chicken in butter. Add
pasta accents and water, stir to combine. Cover; reduce heat to
medium. Simmer 7 to 9 minutes, or until vegetables are crisp-tender,
Stir in sour cream; heat thoroughly.
stirring occassionally.
