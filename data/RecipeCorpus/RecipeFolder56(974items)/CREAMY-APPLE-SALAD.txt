Title: CREAMY APPLE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Fruits, Low fat
Yield: 8 Sm. serv.
2 c  Cubed goledn or green apples
1 c  Cubed red apple   chilled
1/2 c  Raisins
1/3 c  Sliced celery
2 tb Orange or apple juice (chill
Dressing
1 c  (8 oz.) vanilla yogurt
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1 tb Chopped nuts
In medium bowl, combine all salad ingredients; toss gently. In small
bowl blend dressing ingredients, Spoon over salad. Stir gently to
coat fruit. If desired spoon into serving bowls.  Sprinkle with
additional nuts if desired.  Serve immediately Source Pillsbury
cookbooks
