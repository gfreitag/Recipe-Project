Title: CREAMY POTATO SALAD
Categories: Digest
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Potatoes, cubed and cooked
1/2 lb Broccoli, cut into  bit
-sized pieces,
1/2    - 3/4 cup frozen corn
1/2    - 3/4 cup frozen peas
1/2    Onion, sliced thin
-dried dill to taste, 1 - 2
-teaspoons
-salt and pepper to taste
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
2    Cups nonfat yogurt
1/4 cup of mustard if you like it rice vinegar to thin

Add dressing, as much as you like. Mix and chill. If you don't like
broccoli, then use green beans or carrots or whatever you prefer.
