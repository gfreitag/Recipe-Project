Title: CREAMY BROCCOLI BAKE
Categories: Vegetables, Bisquick
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Fresh broccoli or
20 oz Frozen broccoli spears
1    Can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
1 c  Bisquick baking mix
1/2 c  Firm margarine or butter
In saucepan,pour in cold water until about 1 inch deep. Add 1/2 to 1 t
salt.  Heat to boiling.  Add broccoli. Cover and heat to boiling. Cool
until stems are almost tender, 10 to 12 minutes. Drain. Place
broccoli in ungreased 1 1/2 qt round casserole.  Heat oven to 400 Ã¸.
Beat soup and milk with hand beater until smooth; pour over broccoli.
Sprinkle with cheese. Mix baking mix and margarine until crumbly;
sprinkle over cheese. Bake until crumbs are light brown, about 20
minutes. Makes six to eight servings.
