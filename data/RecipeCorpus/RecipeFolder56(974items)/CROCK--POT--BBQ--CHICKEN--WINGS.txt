Title: CROCK  POT  BBQ  CHICKEN  WINGS
Categories: Crock pot, Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
5 lb Chicken wings -- with tips
Cut off
1    (12 oz.) bottle chili sauce
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Molasses
2 ts Salt
2 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Hot pepper sauce
ds Garlic salt
Place wings in crock pot.  Combine all remaining ingredients and pour
over chicken.  Cook on low 6 to 8 hours or on high for 2 to 3 hours.
