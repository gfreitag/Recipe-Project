Title: CRUMB TOPPING FOR FRUIT PIE
Categories: Sharon, Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  FLOUR
1 c  OATMEAL
1/2 c  BROWN SUGAR
1 ts CINNAMON
1/2 c  MARGARINE
*** PLACE PIE FILLING IN PREPARED SUGAR CRUST. MICROWAVE ON HIGH FOR
10 MINUTES. ***   COMBINE DRY INGREDIENTS AND CUT IN MARGARINE UNTIL
CRUMBLY. SPRINKLE OVER PIE FILLING.
