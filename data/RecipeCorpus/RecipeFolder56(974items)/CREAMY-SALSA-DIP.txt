Title: CREAMY SALSA DIP
Categories: Dips, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
-Karen Thackeray
1 c  Mayonnaise *
1 c  Sour cream
1 pk Vegetable soup & recipe mix
12 oz Chunky salsa; mild or medium
*You may use fat free, reduced calorie or regular mayo.
Stir all ingredients until well mixed. Cover; chill 2 hrs. Serve with
tortilla chips. Makes 3 cups.
