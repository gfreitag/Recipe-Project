Title: CREAMY SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Low fat, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Ripe mango
1/2 c  Orange juice
1    Clove garlic
1 ts Soy sauce (Bragg's Aminos
Are also good)
1/4 ts Curry powder
Peel the mango and take out the stone, cut in pieces.

Put everything in a blender and mix. Converted to MM by Donna Webster
