Title: CREME DE MENTHE PIE CRUST
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 2/3 c  Graham cracker crumbs
3/4 c  Pecans, crushed
3 tb Maple syrup
1/3 c  Creme de menthe
Combine graham cracker crumbs with crushed pecans. Stir in maple
syrup and creme de menthe.  Press into a lightly oiled 9-inch pie
plate. Bake at 350 degrees 10-12 minutes; do not overbake. Makes 1
pie shell
