Title: CREAMY CUCUMBER SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Cup
1/2 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/2 c  Coarsely grated peeled
Cucumber
1 ts Fresh grated lemon peel
2 ts Fresh squeezed lemon juice
1/4 ts Dried dill weed
Salt and pepper
In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise or sald dressing, cucmber, lemon
peel, lemon juice and dill weed. Seson to taste with salt and pepper.
Serve over broiled, poached or fried fish. Source: Creative Home
