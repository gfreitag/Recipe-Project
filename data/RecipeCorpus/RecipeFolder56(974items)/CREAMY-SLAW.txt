Title: CREAMY SLAW
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 sm Can evaporated milk
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
4 c  Shredded cabbage
1    Stalk celery, diced
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
2 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Celery seed
1/2 c  Mayonnaise or Salad dressing
1    Carrot, shredded
Stir together milk, vinegar, sugar, salt, celery seed, pepper and
mayonnaise.  Chill until ready to serve. Combine cabbage, carrot and
celery.  Chill. To serve: pour dressing mixture over cabbage mixture.
Toss to coat well.  Serve immediately.
