Title: CRISPY CHICKEN WITH ASPARAGUS SAUCE
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Skinless boneless breast
-halves, or eight boneless
-chicken thighs
1    Egg
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 cn Cream of Asparagus Soup
1/3 c  Milk
1/3 c  Water
-Hot cooked rice w/ parsley
1.  In pie plate, dip chicken into egg; coat with bread crumbs. 2. In
skillet over medium low heat, in hot oil cook chicken 15 minutes,
or until browned on both sides and no longer pink. Remove; keep
warm. 3.  In same skillet over low heat, combine soup, milk and
water. Heat
through, stirring occasionally. Spoon soup mixture over chicken.
Serve
with hot rice.
