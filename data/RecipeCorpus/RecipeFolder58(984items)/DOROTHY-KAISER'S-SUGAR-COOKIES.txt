Title: DOROTHY KAISER'S SUGAR COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 15 Servings
3 c  FLOUR
2 ts CREAM OF TARTAR
1 ts BAKING SODA
1/2 ts SALT
1 c  SHORTENING
2 x  EGGS (BEATEN)
1 c  SUGAR
4 tb MILK
1 ts VANILLA
MIX ALL INGREDIENTS WELL AND CHILL FOR 1 HOUR. ROLL TO A THICKNESS OF
ABOUT 1/4" AND CUT WITH YOUR FAVORITE COOKIE CUTTER. PLACE ON COOKIE
SHEET AND BAKE UNTIL COOKIE JUST STARTS TO TURN GOLDEN. BAKE AT 350
DEG F. FOR 8 TO 10 MINUTES.
