Title: DRUNKEN SPARERIBS
Categories: Misc
Yield: 1 Servings
4 lb Spareribs
1/4 c  Bourbon
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Dijon mustard
Place ribs on rack in roasting pan.  Mix remaining ingredients and
spread thickly over both sides of ribs. Roast in 350Ã¸F oven until
brown and crisp (1-1/4--1-1/2 hours), turning once and basting
frequently with sauce. Serves 4.

Source:  "Cooking With Style," by Charlotte Adams, Doubleday &
Company, Inc., 1967.
