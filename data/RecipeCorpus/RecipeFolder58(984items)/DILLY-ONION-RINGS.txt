Title: DILLY ONION RINGS
Categories: Appetizers, Relishes, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  White vinegar
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Water
2 tb Salt
1 ts Dried whole dillweed
1 lg Onion, thinly sliced and
-separated into rings
Combine first 5 ingredients in a small bowl; stir well. Add sliced
onion and toss gently to combine. Cover and chill for at least 5
hours. Serve with cooked meats. Yield: 2 cups.
