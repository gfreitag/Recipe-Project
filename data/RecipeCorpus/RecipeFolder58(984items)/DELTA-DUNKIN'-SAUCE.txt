Title: DELTA DUNKIN' SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 2 Cups
1 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
3/4 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Chili sauce
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Pepper
1 ts Prepared mustard
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Hot sauce
2    Garlic cloves, minced
1 sm Onion, grated
Combine all ingredients; cover and chill. Yield: 2 1/4 cups.
