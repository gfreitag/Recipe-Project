Title: DOUGH-RE-MI
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 ts Yeast
3 c  Whole wheat flour
2 tb Vital gluten
3/4 c  Scallions; diced very fine
1/4 c  Sun-dried tomatoes; (packed
-in air), diced
1 1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 tb Applesauce
1/2 ts Dried basil
3    Cloves garlic; minced
1/2 c  Low-fat cheese
1 tb Caraway seeds
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds
Place ingredients in ABM in order recommended by your manufacturer.
Turn on. When breadmachine is finished, slice and eat.
