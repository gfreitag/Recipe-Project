Title: DROP BISCUITS
Categories: Breads, Biscuits
Yield: 1 Servings
2 3/4 c  Flour
4 ts Baking powder
6 tb Shortening
1 1/4 c  Milk
1 ts Salt
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder and salt. Cut in
shortening.  Add milk, stirring sufficiently to blend well. Drop by
teaspoonfuls onto well-oiled baking sheet. Bake in hot oven (450Ã¸ F)
about 12 minutes.  These biscuits may be split, buttered, and used
with various fruits as the basis for an emergency shortcake. 14
servings. The Household Searchlight - 1941
