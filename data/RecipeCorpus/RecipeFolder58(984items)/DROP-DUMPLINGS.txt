Title: DROP DUMPLINGS
Categories: Breads, Misc
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Shortening
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder and salt. Cut in
shortening.  Add sufficient milk to make a drop batter. Drop by
spoonfuls into boiling liquid. Cover closely and cook about 10
minutes. The liquid must be kept boiling during the entire 10
minutes. Serve at once. 8 servings. The Household Searchlight - 1941
