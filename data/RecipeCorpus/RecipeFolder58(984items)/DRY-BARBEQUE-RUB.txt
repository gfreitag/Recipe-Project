Title: DRY BARBEQUE RUB
Categories: Rubs
Yield: 16 Servings
4 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Hickory Salt
1 tb Oregano
1 tb Cumin
2 tb Garlic Powder
2 tb Chili Powder
2 tb Paprika
1 tb Onion Powder
2 tb Mustard, Dry
1/4 ts Cayenne
Mix dry ingredients thoroughly.  Rub generously into skinless
chicken, pork chops, or whatever.  Let the meat 'marinate' for an
hour, then toss on the grill for a slow cooking.
