Title: DON'S PIQUANT SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Sauces, Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/3 c  Basil vinegar
1 tb Black pepper fresh ground
2 tb Romano cheese, grated
1 ea Garlic clove minced
1 tb Chopped chives
2 tb Plain yogurt
1 ts Tabasco sauce
Mix all ingredients together. Chill for 1-3 hours. Shake well before
pouring over salad.
