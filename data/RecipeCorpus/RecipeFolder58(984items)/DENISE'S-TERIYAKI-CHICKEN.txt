Title: DENISE'S TERIYAKI CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
8    Chicken breasts; boned,
-skinned
1    Bottle teriyaki sauce
1 tb Minced fresh ginger
1/2 c  Vidalia onions
Juice and pulp of one orange
1/4 c  Grated orange zest
Mix all ingredients; heat marinade to a boil and then let it cool.
Pour over chicken breasts at least two hours before grilling or even
overnight.

Source: Ladue News 5/23/97
