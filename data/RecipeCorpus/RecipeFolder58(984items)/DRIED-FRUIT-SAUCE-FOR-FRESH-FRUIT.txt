Title: DRIED FRUIT SAUCE FOR FRESH FRUIT
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Water and apple juice
1/2 c  Dried cherries
Confectioner's sugar or
Honey to taste
Bring water and apple juice to a boil; pour over cherries and steep
for as long as possible. Sweeten to taste if need be with sugar or
honey. Serve hot over ice cream, fresh fruit or cake.

Yield: 1 cup
