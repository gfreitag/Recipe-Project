Title: DI SARONNO SOUR
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, New (to be
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 oz Di Saronno Amaretto
1 oz Lemon Juice
1 ts Sugar
Ice
1    Orange Slice
Pour ingredients into cocktail shaker with cracked ice. Shake well,
strain into sour glass. Garnish with orange slice.
