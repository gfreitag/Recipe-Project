Title: DOTT'S COFFEE CAKE
Categories: Breakfast, Desserts
Yield: 18 Servings
2 c  FLOUR
2 c  BROWN SUGAR
1/2 c  BUTTER
1 c  BUTTERMILK
1 ts SODA
1 x  EGG
1 x  SPECK OF SALT
CRUMB TOGETHER THE FLOUR, SUGAR AND BUTTER. TAKE OUT 1/2 CUP FOR
TOPPING. ADD MILK TO REMAINING FLOUR MIX ADD OTHER INGREDIENTS AND
MIX WELL. PLACE IN GREASED 9 X 13 PAN. SPRINKLE TOPPING OVER THE TOP.
BAKE AT 350 DEG F FOR 40 MINUTES. SERVE FROM BAKING PAN, HOT OR COLD.
