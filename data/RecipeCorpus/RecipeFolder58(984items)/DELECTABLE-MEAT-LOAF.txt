Title: DELECTABLE MEAT LOAF
Categories: Beef, Meat loaf
Yield: 6 Servings
2 1/2 lb Ground round (or 3 lbs)
2 pk Onion soup mix
3 lg Eggs
1 cn Evap milk -- (5 ounces)
1/2 c  Quick Cook Dry Cream Wheat
12 oz V-8 juice
1/4 c  Ketchup
Mix all but one can of V-8 together. Sape into loaf. bak 350 in Pam
sprayed loaf pan 1 hour. Half way through baking smear loaf with
ketchup. When loaf is cooked and drained, pierce top with fork and
pour second can of V-8 over top allowing to run down and around meat.
Let stand 10 minutes before slicing. The Copycat Cookbook pg46.
