Title: DIJON DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Dijon Mustard
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
1/2 c  White Wine Vinegar
2 tb Parmesan Cheese
2 tb Light Karo Syrup
OR
1 tb Fresh Oregano
1 ts Dried Oregano
1 ts Garlic Powder
1/2 ts Celery Seeds
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Combine all of the ingredients and stir with a wire whish until
blended. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour. Serve over salad greens.
