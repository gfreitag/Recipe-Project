Title: DIRT CUPS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Milk
1 pk Chocolate Pudding, instant
3 1/2 c  Cool Whip
16 oz Oreos
8    Gummy Worms (opt)
8    Plastic flowers (opt)
Pour milk into medium bowl, add pudding mix. Beat until well blended,
1 -2 minutes. Crush Oreos and set aside. Let stand 5 minutes. Stir in
cool whip and half of cookies. To assemble: place 1 Tb crushed
cookies in bottom of 8oz cup. Fill cups about 3/4 full with pudding
mixture. Top with remaining crumbs. Optional garnish: place flower in
middle and put gummy worm halfway out of "dirt".
