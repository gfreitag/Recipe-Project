Title: DILL PARSLEY BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 10 Servings
-Heather Smith NPFN03A
2 c  Flour
3 tb Nonfat dry milk
1 tb Dry dill weed
1 tb Dried parsley
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 1/4 c  Water
2 ts Yeast
Put in bread maker in order given. Really nice toasted with lots of
butter. near L.A.
