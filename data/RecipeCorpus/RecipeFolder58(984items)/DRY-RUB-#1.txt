Title: DRY RUB #1
Categories: New, Text, Import
1 TB paprika, Hungarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 ts celery salt
1/2 ts sugar
1/2 ts sage
1/2 ts mustard
1/2 ts Chipotle powder

Mix all ingredients well and reserve in refrigerator, covered
tightly. Will keep for up to 2 weeks.
