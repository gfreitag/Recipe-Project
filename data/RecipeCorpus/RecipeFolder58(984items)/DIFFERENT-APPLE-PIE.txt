Title: DIFFERENT APPLE PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/2    Stick margarine
4    Delicious apples
1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb Rounded flour
Unbaked pie shell
Combine 1st 4 ingredients in saucepan & cook, then add sugar & flour.
Mix well & pour into unbaked pie shell. Bake 30 min. at 425 degrees.
