Title: DIET SPICED TEA MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 3 Servings
2 pk Orange Kool-Aid
2 pk Lemon Kool-Aid
1 c  Instant tea
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Ground cloves
2 c  Sugar Twin
Combine all ingredients and store in an air-tight container. to
serve, add 2 t. mix to 1 cup boiling water. Source: The Best Little
Cookbook in Texas Junior League of Abilene
