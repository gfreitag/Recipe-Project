Title: DRIED CHERRY COUSCOUS
Categories: Pasta, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Couscous (10 oz size)*
1/3 c  Olive oil
1 lg Carrot, diced fine
1 ea Red Bell Pepper/Dice Fine
1 ea Yellow Bell Pepper/Dice Fine
1 ea Green Bell Pepper/Dice Fine
1 bn Fresh chives, or
1 c  Chopped green onions, for
-garnish
1/2 lb Dried cherries**
Prepare the couscous according to package directions, substituting
chicken broth or stock for the water.
In the olive oil, saute the carrots, peppers and chives for about 3
minutes.  Toss with the cooked couscous, dried cherries, salt and
pepper. Be careful not to over mix. Keep warm and arrange on the same
platter as the cooked turkey. ** If dried cherries are not available,
raisins can be substituted.
