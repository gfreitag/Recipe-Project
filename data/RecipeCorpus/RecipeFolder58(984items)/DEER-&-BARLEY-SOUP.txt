Title: DEER & BARLEY SOUP
Categories: Wildgame, Venison, Soups
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Deer shanks (or equal)
1 c  Barley, pearl
1 c  Peas, green split
2 ea Onions, chopped
2 ea Garlic clove, finely chopped
1 ea Bell pepper, seeded, chopped
14 c  Beef or chicken stock
4 tb Butter
1 tb Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Brown garlic, onion and pepper in butter. Add deer, cut into 1"
pieces, and brown lightly. Add stock and remaining ingredients and
bring to a boil. Cover and simmer for 2-3 hours, until meat is
tender. Season according to taste.  Shank took 3 hours to become
tender.
