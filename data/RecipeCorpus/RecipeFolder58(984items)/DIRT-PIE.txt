Title: DIRT PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Jello Chocolate Instant
1 x  Pudding (4 serving size)
1 c  Milk, cold
1 ea COOL WHIP, thawed (1L)
20 ea Chocolate Sandwich Cookies,
1 x  Chopped
1 1/2 c  KRAFT minature Marshmallows
1 ea Prepared Graham Crumb Crust
1 x  9-inch
1 x  Gummy worms for decoration
Prepare pudding according to package directions reducing milk to 1
cup. Fold in whipped topping. Stir in 2/3 of the cookies and all the
marshmallows. Spoon into the crust. Sprinkle top with the remaining
cookies. Decorate with gummy worms. Freeze until firm, about 4 hours.
Remove from freezer 10 minutes before serving for easy slicing.
