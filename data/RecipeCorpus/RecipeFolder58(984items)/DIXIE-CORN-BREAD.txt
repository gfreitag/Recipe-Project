Title: DIXIE CORN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  White cornmeal
3 tb All-purpose flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Buttermilk
1    Egg
2 tb Bacon drippings (or melted
-butter)
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. In a large mixing bowl sift together
cornmeal, with flour salt and baking soda. Add buttermilk, egg and
drippings, mixing just until dry ingredients are moistened. Pour into
greased, hot 10-inch oven proof skillet.  Bake 20 to 25 minutes.
Serve warm, with butter.
