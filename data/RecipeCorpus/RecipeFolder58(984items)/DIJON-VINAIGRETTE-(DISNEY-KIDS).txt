Title: DIJON VINAIGRETTE (DISNEY KIDS)
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 24 Servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/3 c  Red wine vinegar
1/8 ts Coarse black pepper
1    Clove garlic -- crushed
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Salt
Combine all the ingredients in a clean jar with a tight-fitting lid.
Shake well. Makes 1.5 cups
