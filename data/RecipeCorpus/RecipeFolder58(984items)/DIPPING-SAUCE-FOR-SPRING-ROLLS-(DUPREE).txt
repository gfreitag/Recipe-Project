Title: DIPPING SAUCE FOR SPRING ROLLS (DUPREE)
Categories: Dupree
2 c  rice vinegar
Yield: 12 Servings
3    cupa soy sauce
12 ts sesame oil
12 ts hot chili oil

In a small bowl combine vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, and hot chili
oil. Mix thoroughly and store, tightly covered and refrigerated.
Serve with spring rolls.
