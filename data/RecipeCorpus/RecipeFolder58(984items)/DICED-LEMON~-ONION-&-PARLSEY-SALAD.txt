Title: DICED LEMON~ ONION & PARLSEY SALAD
Categories: Appetizers, Moroccan, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
4 lg Lemons, washed
1 lg Spanish onion
1 1/2 bn Italian parsley, washed
Salt
Peel & dice the lemons.  Cut the onion into small dice & coarsely
chop the parsley.  Toss the ingredients together & add salt to taste.
