Title: DILL SALT
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Dill seed
(rounded tb)
2 tb Unrefined coarse salt
(rounded tb)
Grind the dill seed fine at highest speed; add the salt and repeat.
Store in a small jar with a tight lid. This salt is especially great
over cucumber spears. SOURCE: Blend It Splendid (pg. 135)
