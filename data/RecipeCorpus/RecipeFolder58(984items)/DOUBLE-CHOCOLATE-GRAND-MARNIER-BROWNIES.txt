Title: DOUBLE CHOCOLATE GRAND MARNIER BROWNIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Recipe
2    Squares unsweetened
-chocolate
2    Squares semisweet
-chocolate
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  White sugar
2/3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chopped pecans (opt)
1 ts Vanilla
2 tb Grand Marnier
ICING
2    Squares semisweet chocolate
2 tb Light cream (or 2% milk)
1 tb Grand Marnier
2 tb Butter
1 c  Icing sugar
Brownie: Melt chocolate. Add sugar, eggs, and butter. Mix dry
ingredients. Add to chocolate mixture.  Add vanilla. Place in greased
8 X 8 pan. Bake 25 minutes at 350. Immediately after baking, brush on
Grand Marnier. Cool 1 hour.

Icing: Melt chocolate and butter together. Stir in cream and Grand
Marnier. Add icing sugar.  Spread on cooled brownies.

These are supposed to sit for 24 hours before eating. (but I have
never actually managed this!) Submitted By RICHARD HOMMEL
