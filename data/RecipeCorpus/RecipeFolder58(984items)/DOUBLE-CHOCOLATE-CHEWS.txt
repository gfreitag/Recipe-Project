Title: DOUBLE CHOCOLATE CHEWS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts, Easy
Yield: 32 Cookies
4 tb Butter, softened
4 tb Cream cheese, nonfat
1/3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Mini chocolate chips
Preheat oven to 350F.  Spray 2 sheetpans with nonstick spray. In a
medium mixing bowl, cream together the butter, cream cheese, sugar
and brown sugar.  Beat in the vanilla. Into a small bowl, sift the
flour, cocoa and salt through a mesh strainer and stir to combine.
Blend the dry ingredients into the butter mixture. Stir in the
chcoclate chips. Divide the dough into quarters and taking one-eight
of a quarter roll it between the palms of two hands to form a ball
and place it on a sheet pan. Repeat to form 32 dough nuggets.  Using
the bottom of a glass, press the nuggets into 2 inch rounds.  Bake
for 8 minutes until slightly puffed and very soft to the touch.  Let
cool on the pans for 1 minutes before transferring to racks to cool
completely.
