Title: DERBY PIE -1
Categories: Pies & past, Southern, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1    9 Inch Pie Shell
4    Whole Eggs
3/4 c  White Sugar
1/4 c  Brown Sugar
1 tb Vanilla
2 tb Bourbon
3/4 c  Light Corn Syrup
1    Stick Butter -- melted
1 tb Flour
1 c  Pecans -- chopped
1 c  Chocolate Chips
Beat eggs.  Add next 7 ingredients to eggs.   Place pecans and ch.
chips in a 9 inch pie shell.  pour filling over. Bake at 350 degrees
for 40 to 45 mins.
