Title: DELICIOUS SNACK MIX
Categories: Holiday, Munchie
Yield: 16 1/2 cup
4 c  Salted peanuts
1 c  Candy-coated chocolate
Pieces, mm's etc. or semi
Sweet chocolate chips
1 c  Whole almonds
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Chopped dates
1 c  Mini pretzels  (optional)
1/4 c  Sunflower seeds
directions In large bowl, combine all ingredients. Store in covered
containers of choice for gift giving. Will make 16 or more 1/2 cup
servings.  If using pretzels serving sizes will vary, omit dates when
using pretzels.

source pillsbury holiday & gift monthly mag. submitted by marina
