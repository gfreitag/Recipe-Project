Title: DRINK YOUR BREAKFAST (LACTO)
Categories: Digest, Feb95, Breakfast
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Skim milk
1    Ripe banana
1/2 c  Fat free yogourt (fresh or
Frozen)
MMMMM--------------------------OPTIONAL-------------------------------
Flavorings (vanilla,
Chocolate syrup)
Protein powder or
Nutritional yeast for extra
Pep
Blend ten seconds in a blender and carry around the house while you
get dressed! Delicious and easy.
