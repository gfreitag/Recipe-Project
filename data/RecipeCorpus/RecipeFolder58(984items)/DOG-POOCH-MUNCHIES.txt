Title: DOG POOCH MUNCHIES
Categories: Dog biscuit
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1 ts Garlic salt
1/2 c  Soft bacon fat
1 c  Shredded cheese
1    Egg, beaten slightly
1 c  Milk
1. Preheat oven to 400 F. degrees. 2. Place flour and garlic salt in a
large bowl.  Stir in bacon fat. Add cheese and egg. Gradually add
enough milk to form a dough. Knead dough and roll out to about 1 inch
thick. 3. Use dog bone cookie cutter to cut out dough.  Place on
greased cookie sheet.  Bake about 12 minutes, until they start to
brown. Cool and serve.
