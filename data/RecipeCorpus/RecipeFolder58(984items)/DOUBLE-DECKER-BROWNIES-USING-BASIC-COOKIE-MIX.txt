Title: DOUBLE DECKER BROWNIES USING BASIC COOKIE MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 16 Servings
1    Rcp Brownies from Cookie
Mix
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
1 c  Peanut butter chips --
Reese's
1 tb Shortening
1 1/2 c  Crisp rice cereal
Make one recipe brownies from basic cookie mix. Bake for 25 minutes.
Remove from oven.  Evenly sprinkle
miniature marshmallows over brownies. Return to oven. Bake 5
additional minutes.  Remove from oven; cool in pan. Combine peanut
butter chips and shortening in the top of a double boiler over hot,
melted.  Add the rice cereal; stir
NOT boiling water.  Stir until
until thoroughly coated.   Spread over top of marshmallows. Cool
until set. Cut into squares.   Makes 16
