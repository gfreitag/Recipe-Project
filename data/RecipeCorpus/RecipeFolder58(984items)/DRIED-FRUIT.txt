Title: DRIED FRUIT
Categories: Christmas, Fruits, Ethnic, Holiday, Polish
Yield: 2 Servings
-Cookie Lady
Dried apples, pears and prunes are cooked in water until they are
soft. Add lemon juice.

Place a dish of this fruit and a dish of nuts on the table to be
eaten after the meal.

"If you eat the fruit, you won't get sick from eating too much
food", Mrs Jachimowicz says.
