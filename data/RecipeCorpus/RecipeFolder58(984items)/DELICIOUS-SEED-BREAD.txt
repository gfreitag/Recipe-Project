Title: DELICIOUS SEED BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 11 Servings
-NPFN03A
2 c  Bread flour
3 tb Nonfat dry milk
1 tb Poppy seeds
1 1/2 tb Sesame seeds
3 tb Sunflower seeds
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Honey
1 1/2 tb Oil
1 c  Ater
2 ts Yeast
Cook on either "regular" or "quick". Nice crunchy crust, lovely
flavor. Reformatted By Elaine Radis
