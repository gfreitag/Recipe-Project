Title: DIRT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
1 ea Pkg of chocolate pudding
1 ea Container cool whip
1 ea Pkg Oreo cookies
1 x  Gummy worms (optional)
Crush Oreo cookies. Layer cookies, prepared pudding and cool whip
in a plastic flower pot, in several layers, ending with crumbled
cookies on top. Add in gummy worms if desired, add silk flowers as a
finishing touch.
Freak out your guests by dipping their "desert" out of the
"center-piece" flower pot.
