Title: DEVONSHIRE CREAM (NON-TRADITIONAL)
Categories: Sauces, Desserts
Yield: 1 Cup
1/2 c  Heavy cream or 8 oz
Softened cream cheese
2 tb Confectioner's sugar
1/2 c  Sour cream
In a chilled bowl, beat cream until soft peaks form (if using cream
cheese, just beat together with sugar. Fold in sour cream, and blend.

Serve with scones and preserves or over fresh berries.
