Title: DOUGHBOYS AKA MULESADAS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Water, lukewarm
1 pk Yeast
1 c  Milk
3/4 c  Sugar
2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Shortening
2    Eggs
5 c  Flour
2 c  Shortening for fryer
Soften yeast in lukewarm water with a pinch of sugar. Heat milk,
sugar, salt and shortening until melted. Pour into a bowl until
lukewarm, add yeast and eggs, mix well. Add flour and knead. Place in
greased bowl and let rise 1 1/2 hrs.

When light, move bowl to a ready place near the stove or deep-fat
fryer. Heat fryer 400 degrees.  Add second batch of shortening, about
3-4 inches deep.  Take a handful of dough. Stretch it out as much as
you can between your hands. Drop slowly into hot shortening. Cook
'til brown. Turn over, cook other side till done. Drain on paper
towels. Sprinkle sugar on top while still warm.
