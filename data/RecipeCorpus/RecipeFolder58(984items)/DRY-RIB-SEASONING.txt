Title: DRY RIB SEASONING
Categories: Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
6 tb Salt
6 tb Sugar
1 tb Dry lemon powder
2 tb MSG
2 1/2 tb Black pepper
1 tb Paprika
Mix thoroughly.  This recipe is for sprinkling on spareribs before
barbequing.  Use heaping measures where mixing ingredients, and do not
skimp when sprinkling on ribs.
