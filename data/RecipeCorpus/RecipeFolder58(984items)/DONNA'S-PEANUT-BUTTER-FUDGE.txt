Title: DONNA'S PEANUT BUTTER FUDGE
Categories: Candy
Yield: 24 Squares
2 c  Sugar
3 tb Butter
1 c  Evaporated milk
1 c  Minature marshmellows
1    12oz Jar Jiffy Peanut Butter
1 tb Vanilla
This is a recipe made in a electric skillet.

Combine sugar, butter  and milk.  Set temp at 280~. Stor constantly
and boil 5 - 7 minutes.  Turn heat off and add marshmellows, Peanut
Butter, and vanilla. Blend together and then pour into a 8in square
pan which has been buttered.  Let cool before cutting.

Recipe by Donna Tomlinson
