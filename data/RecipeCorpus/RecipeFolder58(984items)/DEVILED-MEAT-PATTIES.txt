Title: DEVILED MEAT PATTIES
Categories: Beef, Sandwiches, Barbeque
Yield: 4 Servings
-Pam Coombes
1 lb Ground chuck
1/4 c  Chili sauce
1 tb Minced onion
1 ts Prepared mustard
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/8 ts Pepper
Vegetable cooking spray
Combine ground chuck, chili sauce,onion, mustard, horseradish,
Worcestershire sauce,and pepper; stir well. Shape into 4 patties.
Place patties on broiler pan coated with cooking spray. Broil 5
inches from heat 4 minutes on each side. Southern Living Annual
Recipes 1987
