Title: DILL RANCH DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 2 Cups
1 c  Buttermilk
1 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
1/4 c  Reduced-calorie mayonnaise
2 ts Sugar
1 1/2 ts Dried dill weed
3/4 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Dried basil leaves
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Salt
In small bowl using wire whisk, combine buttermilk, yogurt, and
mayonnaise; blend well.  Add remaining dressing ingredients; blend
well. Cover; refrigerate 1 to 2 hours to blend flavors.
