Title: DOUBLE PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Pork
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  White sugar
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Butter flavored crisco
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Peanut butter chips
Cream peanut butter, crisco and sugar Beat in eggs Combine dry
ingredients Gradually add to creamed mixture Stir in chips Shape into
1 inch balls Flatten Bake 8 to 10 minutes at 350* Posted to MC-Recipe
