Title: DIABETIC SMUNCHIES
Categories: Diabetic, Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Graham crackers
Sugar-free chocolate pudding
Sliced bananas
SPREAD ONE GRAHAM CRACKER WITH SUGAR-FREE CHOCOLATE PUDDING THAT HAS
BEEN MADE UP, THEN TOP WITH THINLY SLICE BANANA. PUT OTHER GRAHAM
CRACKER ON TOP.  WRAP AND FREEZE. WHEN THAWED IT IS GOOEY. Exchanges:
1 starch. When making - be sure to break each long graham cracker in
half. This was given to me by a dietician at Riverside Hosptial in
Newport News, Va. Pom Pom Mom
