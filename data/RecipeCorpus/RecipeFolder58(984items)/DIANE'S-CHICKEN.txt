Title: DIANE'S CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Chicken breasts, skinless
1 cn Cream of mushroom
1 cn Cream of celery
1 1/3 c  Water
1 c  Rice (convertors)
1/2 pk Lipton onion soup mix
Combine everything but chicken in casserole dish, then place chichen
on top.  Cover, bake at 350 degrees 1 1/2 hous. Uncover chicken last
20 minutes for browning.
