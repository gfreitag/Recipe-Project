Title: Date Pudding
Categories: Penndutch, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
-
-
-
-
-
-
2    Egg, well beaten
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Dates, chopped
1 ts Baking powder
2 tb Flour
1 c  Nuts, chopped
DIRECTIONS  ------------------------------------------------------------
Mix in the order given. Bake at 325-F for 30 minutes. Serve with
cream.
Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary
Arts Press, 1936.
