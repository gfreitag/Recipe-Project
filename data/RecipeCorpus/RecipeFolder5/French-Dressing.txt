Title: French Dressing
Categories: Dressings, French
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Vinegar, red wine
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Paprika
1 ts Garlic salt
1 c  Ketchup
1 c  Sugar
1 1/4 c  Oil
1/3 c  Onion, grated
DIRECTIONS  ------------------------------------------------------------
Put in blender and mix till thick.
Makes 1 quart.
Source: Gwen Pitts
