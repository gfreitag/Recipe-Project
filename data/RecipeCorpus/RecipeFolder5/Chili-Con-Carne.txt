Title: Chili Con Carne
Categories: Soups/stews, Chili, Beans
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Onion; large
Salt
Pepper
1 lb Beef; ground
1 qt Tomatoes
1 pt Kidney beans
Brown meat and onions in skillet. Add tomatoes, beans and seasoning. Heat
thoroughly and serve.

Source: Mary Brown, Olive Grange, Noble County, OH
