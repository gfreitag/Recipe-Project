Title: Peanut Butter Crunches
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Corn flakes
1 c  Butterscotch chips, melted
1/4 c  Peanut butter
Blend butterscotch chips and peanut butter and stir in cereal. Drop by
small teaspoonfuls on wax paper and let set until firm
