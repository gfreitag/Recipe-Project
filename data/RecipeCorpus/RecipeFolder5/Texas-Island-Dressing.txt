Title: Texas Island Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dressings
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Tomato paste
2 tb Sweet pickle relish
1 tb Sugar
1 c  Mayonnaise
-OR
1 c  Light mayonnaise
1/2 c  Pace Picante Sauce
Combine ingredients; mix well. Chill. Toss with green salads or seafood
salads.

Makes 1-3/4 cups.
