Title: Five-Spice Walnuts
Categories: Snack, Nuts/grains, Herbs/spice
Yield: 3 cups
1/2 ts Salt
3 c  Broken walnuts
3 tb Butter or margarine
1 ts Five-spice powder
In a medium saucepan melt butter or margarine.  Stir in five-spice
powder and salt.  Add broken walnuts, stirring till nuts are evenly
coated.

Transfer nut mixture to a 13x9x2-inch baking pan.  Bake in a 300F
oven for 20 minutes, stirring once or twice.  Cool in pan for 15
minutes. Turn out onto paper towels to finish cooling.  Store tightly
covered. Makes 3 cups.
