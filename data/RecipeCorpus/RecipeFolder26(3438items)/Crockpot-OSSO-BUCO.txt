Title: Crockpot OSSO BUCO
Categories: Crockpot, Italian, Veal, Lamb, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
3 sm Carrots; finely diced
1/4 t  Dried marjoram; crushed
1/8 t  Dried sage; crushed
4 ea Veal or lamb shanks
1 ea Clove garlic; minced
1 c  Canned consomme
2 T  Tomato paste
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
-Strips
1 T  Parsley; chopped
1/4 t  Dried oregano; crushed
Well seasoned flour
3 T  Olive oil
1/4 c  Dry white wine
Rind of 1 lemon, cut into
Roll shanks in flour; brown with garlic in hot oil in skillet.  Add
remaining ingredients to crockpot.  Stir to mix.  Add shanks.  Cover
pot and cook on LOW for 10 to 12 hours until shanks are tender.
Garnish with parsley.  Serve with pasta.
