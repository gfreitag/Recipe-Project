Title: Peach Raisin Cobbler
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine, melted
Frozen or canned
1 c  Raisins
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Allspice
1 1/4 c  Sugar, divided
2 c  Peeled peach slices,
-fresh
1 c  All purpose flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Milk
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a 9-inch square pan.  IN large bowl,
combine 1 cup sugar, flour, baking powder and salt; mix well.  Blend
in milk and butter.  Pour batter into greased pan.  Combine peaces,
raisins, remaining 1/4 cup sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and allspice; toss
to coat evenly.  Spoon peach mixture evenly over batter.  Bake at 350
degrees for 45 minutes or until lightly browned.  Serve warm or at
room temperature if desired.
