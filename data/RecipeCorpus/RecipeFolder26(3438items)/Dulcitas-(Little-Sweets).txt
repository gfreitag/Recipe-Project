Title: Dulcitas (Little Sweets)
Categories: Snack, Tex/mexican
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM-------------------FROM: ARIZONA COOKBOOK------------------------
2 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Cocoa
1 ea Dozen corn tortillas
1/2 c  Powdered sugar or 1/3 cup
-sugar
Soften corn tortillas in heated oil, stack, and cut into 8ths. Mix
sugar, cinnamon, and cocoa in a small paper sack. Cook small pieces
of tortilla in hot oil til crisp but not brown. Drain and dump into
bag and shake in sugar mixture to coat.
