Title: Simple Shrimp Appetizer
Categories: Crab/shrimp, Appetizer
Yield: 1 batch
2 ts Red curry paste
2 tb Japanese soy sauce
1 lb Large shrimp in shells
2    Garlic cloves; minced
Marinate shrimp with garlic cloves, curry paste and soy sauce. Mix
well, then cover and refrigerate for 1 hour. Skewer the shrimp, then
cook them over a grill.
