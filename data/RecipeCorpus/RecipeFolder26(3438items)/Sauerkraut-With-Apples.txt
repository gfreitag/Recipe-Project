Title: Sauerkraut With Apples
Categories: German, Vegetable, Apples, Side dish, European
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Caraway seed
1 md Potato, raw; peeled
& grated
Salt and pepper; to taste
1 md Onion; peeled and chopped
2 tb Lard or bacon fat
1 lb Sauerkraut; drained
1/2 c  Beef bouillon or consomme
2    Tart apples, cored, pared
& chopped
Recipe by: The Eastern European Cookbook-ISBN 0-486-23562-9

In a saucepan, saute onion in fat until tender. Add sauerkraut; saute
1 min. Add remaining ingredients. Cook slowly, covered, for 30 mins.
Serves 4.

NOTES : This is usually served with pork or game.
