Title: Stir Fry Ginger Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Stir fry, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Chicken broth
2 tb Cornstarch
1/8 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Garlic, minced
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Butter
6    Chicken breast filets
2 c  Chopped broccoli spears
1 1/2 c  Show peas
1/2 c  Sliced water chestnuts
In wok or skillet, fry filets in butter for 3 to 4 minutes on each
side using med-high heat.  Add broccoli, snow peas and water
chestnuts and cook 2 to 3 minutes.  Mix remaining ingredients in a
bowl until cornstarch is fully dissolved.  Pour over filets and
veggies and bring to a boil. Hold for 2 to 3 minutes.  Makes 6 to 8
servings.
