Title: Best Breakfast Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Sausage
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Bulk pork sausage
8    Eggs, slightly beaten
6 sl Bread, cubed
2 c  Whole milk
Brown sausage and drain well. Set aside. In a large bowl, mix other
ingredients well. Add sausage and stir. Place in a well greased 9x13
pan, cover with foil and refrigerate overnight. Bake at 350Ã¸ for 45 -
50 minutes until set and brown. Serve hot. Serves 6.

This is great because you can make it the night before and take it
from fridge to oven the next morning and have a great breakfast. This
has become a tradition at Christmas around here! Enjoy!
