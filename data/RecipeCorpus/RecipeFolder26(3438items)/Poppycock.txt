Title: Poppycock
Categories: Snack, Popcorn
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Baking soda
7 qt Popped corn, slightly less
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Dark corn syrup
1/2 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 200. Cook sugar, butter, syrup, salt and vanilla.
Boil 5 min. Remove from heat and add baking soda. Pour over popcorn.
Spread in shallow pan and bake for 1 hour. Stir every 10-15 min. Nuts
may be added. Source: Calgary Herald
