Title: Drunk Dogs
Categories: Appetizer, Sausage, Alcohol
Yield: 15 servings
1/2 c  Bourbon
4 ds Soy sauce
2 pk Brown and serve sausages
1/2 lb Dark brown sugar
Cut sausages into thirds and cook till done.  In another pan add rest
of ingredients.  Melt over low heat and boil sauce until slightly
thick. Drain sausage and add to sauce.  Serve hot in chafing dish
with cocktail picks. Submitted By JANIE BOURKE   On   12-26-94
