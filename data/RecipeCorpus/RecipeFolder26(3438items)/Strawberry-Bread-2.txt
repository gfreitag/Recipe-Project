Title: Strawberry Bread 2
Categories: Bread, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
4 ea Eggs, well beaten
1 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  Chopped pecans
20 oz Frozen strawberries, thawed
3 c  Flour
1 t  Baking soda
1 t  Salt
2 t  Cinnamon
2 c  Sugar
Preheat oven to 350.  In a bowl,  combine  flour, soda, salt,
cinnamon and sugar; mix well. In a separate bowl, mix eggs and oil;
add to dry ingredients.   Stir in pecans. Fold in strawberries until
moistened. Pour into 2 greased 9x5x3" loaf pans. Bake at 350 for 50
to 60 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Cool on wire rack.
