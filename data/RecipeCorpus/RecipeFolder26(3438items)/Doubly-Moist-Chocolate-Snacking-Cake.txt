Title: Doubly Moist Chocolate Snacking Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Snack
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Water
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 t  Vinegar
3/4 t  Vanilla
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1 2/3 c  Flour
1 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Powdered cocoa
1 t  Baking soda
1/4 t  Salt
Preheat oven to 350F.  Combine the dry ingredients.  Make well.  Add
all of the liquid ingredients and beat until thoroughly blended.
Pour into 8x8 greased and floured pan. Sprinkle chocolate chips over
top and bake 35-40 minutes or until cake tests clean.  Cool and cut
into squares. Origin: Sharon's Recipe files. Shared by: Sharon
Stevens, Jan/95.
