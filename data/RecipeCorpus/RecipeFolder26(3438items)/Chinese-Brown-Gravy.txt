Title: Chinese Brown Gravy
Categories: Chinese, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 c  White wine
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1 T  Sugar
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 T  Cornstarch
1 T  Sesame oil
Blend all ingredients and
-cook until slightly
-thickened.
