Title: Cheese Swizzles
Categories: Appetizer, Sourdough, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 60 servings
Rectangular loaf of day-
-cheese (about 2 ozs)
-old sourdough bread
-paprika; for sprinkling
-dorothy cross tmpj72b
6 tb Unsalted butter; melted
1    1-1/4-lb unsliced
1/2 c  Freshly grated parmesan
1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees. Trim the crust from the bread.
Slice the loaf crosswise into 1/2-inch strips.

2. On a large baking sheet, toss the bread strips with the melted
butter. Spread the strips in a single layer and bake for 15 to 20
minutes, or until golden brown.

3. Spread the cheese on a large, flat plate. Roll the hot bread
strips in the cheese until lightly coated. Sprinkle lightly with the
paprika. Serve the warm swizzles in a basket lined with a cloth
napkin.

Source: Food and Wine Magazine - March, 1993 These make great little
accompaniments for any occasion.
