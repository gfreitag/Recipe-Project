Title: One Bean & Corn Salad
Categories: Main dish, Side dish, Fatfree
Yield: 4 servings
-(heat is up to you, I like
-mine mild)
1 bn Cilantro (or as much as you
-like for taste) chopped
-a little lemon juice
-some balsamic vinegar
-two cans of red kidney
-beans, drained and rinse.
1 pk Yellow corn, barely thawed
-one red onion, finely
-chopped
1/4    To 1/2 can of S & W salsa
toss it all together.  This is one recipe that takes on flavor the
longer it sits.  You can adjust the tastes (add sugar if you like it
sweet).
