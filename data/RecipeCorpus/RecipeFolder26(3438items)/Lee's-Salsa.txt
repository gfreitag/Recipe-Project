Title: Lee's Salsa
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Garlic chopped
1 t  Salt
1 T  Cilantro chopped (a must)
1 cn Tomatos
1 cn Tomato paste
1 md Onion chopped
1 ea Pickled pepper chopped(hot)
Don't worry about exact proportions chop or throw in food procesor
until chopped fine. cook until thickened about 20 min. cool before
serving. YOU MUST USE FRESH CILANTRO!!!
