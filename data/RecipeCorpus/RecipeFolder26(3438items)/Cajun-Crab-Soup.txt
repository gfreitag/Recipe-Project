Title: Cajun Crab Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Cajun/creol, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
8 sl Lemon, slices
1 ea Egg, hard boiled, chopped
-fine   6 tbs sherry
Salt and pepper to taste
12 ea Crabs
1/4 lb Butter
2 ea Ribs celery , chopped
1 lg Onion, minced
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
4 ea Sprig parsley, minced
1 1/2 t  Paprika
1 1/2 T  Flour
1 cn Tomatoes (#2 can)
3 ea Sprig thyme
Clean and halve crabs. Melt butter and add flour, mixing constantly
until golden brown. Add onions and let simmer for 5 minutes. Add
crabs and then pour in 3 cups of HOT water. Simmer for 5 minutes. Mix
in tomatoes, celery, thyme, parsley, and 6 cups HOT water. Let boil
vigorously about 1/2 hour. Add sherry, lemon, hard boiled egg,
paprika, salt and pepper. (if soup becomes to thick, pour in Hot
water until desired consistency is reached) Let simmer for 1/2 hour.
Serves six
