Title: Chocolate Covered Cherry Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Fruit
Yield: 40 servings
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
6 oz Semi sweet chocolate chips
1/2 c  Sweetened condensed milk
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Cocoa powder (unsweetened)
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
10 oz Marachino cherries w/liquid
Combine flour, cocoa, salt, baking powder & soda. Set aside. Drain the
cherries, reserving juice, set aside. Beat butter in large bowl with
electic mixer at medium speed for 30 seconds. Add sugar and beat until
fluffy. Add egg and vanilla, beating well. Gradually add the dry
ingredients, beating until well combined. Shape dough into 1" balls.
Place on an ungreased cookie sheet. Press down on the center of each
cookie with thumb. Fill with a drained cherry. In a small saucepan,
combine chocolate chips and condensed milk. Stir over low heat until
melted. Stir in 4 t. of reserved cherry juice. Spoon 1 tsp of
frosting onto each cookie, spreading to cover cherry. Thin with more
juice if necessary. Bake at 350Ã¸ for 10 minutes. Cool. *P Debbie
