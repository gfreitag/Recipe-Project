Title: Meatless Taco Filling
Categories: Vegetarian, Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Stewed tomatoes
1 cn Chopped green chiles
1 pk Taco seasoning
1 c  Rice (white or brown)
6    Cloves garlic, minced
2 sm Onions, minced
Prepare rice as usual.  While rice is cooking, saute garlic and
onions in a little water and cooking spray.  When onions are slightly
translucent, at chiles, and tomatoes.  Cook for 30 seconds, then add
1/4 c. water and taco seasoning.  Cook for a few minutes, then add to
rice.  Serve with ff tortillas and your favorite taco toppings!
