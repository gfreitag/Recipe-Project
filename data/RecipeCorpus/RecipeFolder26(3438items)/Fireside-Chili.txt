Title: Fireside Chili
Categories: Chili, Tex/mexican, Ground beef, Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
2 ea Cloves garlic, finely
-chopped
3 T  Chili powder (heaping)
1 T  Oregano
1 T  Cumin
1 t  Paprika
1 t  Ground mustard
1/4 t  Cayenne pepper
1 t  Salt
2 lb Chili meat
1 lb Ground beef
2 T  Bacon drippings
1 ea Pt hot water
1 c  Tomato puree (10 1/2 oz)
1 t  Tabasco
1 ea Chili pepper pod or more to
-taste
2 ea Large onions, finely chopped
Brown meats; add bacon drippings and cook 5 minutes. Add hot water,
tomato puree, Tabasco, pepper, onion and garlic; simmer 30 minutes.
Add rest of the ingredients and simmer slowly for 1 to 2 hours. Serve
with crackers, raw onion, grated sharp Cheddar cheese and plenty of
cold beer.
