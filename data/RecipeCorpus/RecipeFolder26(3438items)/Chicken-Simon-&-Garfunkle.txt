Title: Chicken Simon & Garfunkle
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Sage
1/2 ts Rosemary
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 c  White wine, dry
Flour
Mozzarella cheese, shredded
2 ea Whole chicken breasts
-split in 4 pieces
1/2 c  Butter
1 ea Egg, beaten
1 c  Bread crumbs, unflavored
1 1/2 ts Parsley
Pound out chicken breasts. Melt butter and add seasonings and salt and
pepper to taste. Set aside. Sprinkle Mozzarella cheese on chicken,
roll up and secure with toothpicks. Dip in flour, then in egg, then
in bread crumbs. Arrange chicken in pan. Pour sauce over the chicken.
Bake at 350 degF for 30 minutes. Pour white wine over chicken. Bake
for 30 minutes more.
