Title: Date Waffles
Categories: Waffles, Fruit
Yield: 6 waffles
2 c  Milk;
2    Eggs;
1 c  CALIFORNIA DATES; chopped
2 c  Flour;
1/2 ts Salt;
2 ts Baking powder;
1/4 c  Butter;
Sift together flour; salt and baking powder, add yolks of eggs,
butter and milk gradually and beat till perfectly smooth.  Add dates
and whites, beaten stiff, and bake as ordinary waffles.  Serve with
butter and honey. Source: California Favorites Cookbook by WHOM!
