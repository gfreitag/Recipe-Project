Title: Mustard Beans
Categories: Beans, Penndutch, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 T  Sugar
1/2 t  Salt
1 T  Flour
pn Cayenne pepper
1 ea Egg, well beaten
3/4 c  Vinegar.
1 T  Mustard
Esther Hutter  Lebanon, Pa.
2 qt Green or yellow wax beans
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Vinegar
1 c  Water
3 T  Prepared mustard
1 1/2 t  Salt
MUSTARD DRESSING
MUSTARD BEANS  Cook beans in salt water until tender.  Water should be
absorbed until cooking period is over.  Add sugar, mustard, vinegar,
and water. Bring to boil.  These may be canned or served cold as a
relish or pickle. MUSTARD DRESSING Mix all ingredients together and
bring to a boil, stirring all the time; then add 2 tbsp. butter; beat
well.  Thin with sweet cream.  Pour sauce over cooked beans and bring
to a boil before serving. From: Lancaster Farming Shared By: Pat
