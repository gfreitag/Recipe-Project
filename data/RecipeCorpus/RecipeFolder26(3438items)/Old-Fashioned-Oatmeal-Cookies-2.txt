Title: Old Fashioned Oatmeal Cookies 2
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 78 servings
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 t  Baking soda
1 t  Salt
1 t  Ground cinnamon
1/2 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Ground cloves
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Shortening
2 ea Eggs
2 1/2 c  Whole wheat blend flour
2 c  Quick-cooking oats
Heat raisins and water to boiling; reduce heat to medium.  Simmer
uncover until raisins are plump, about 15 minutes.  Drain raisins,
reserving liquid.  Add enough water to reserve liquid to measure 1/2
cup. Heat oven to 400.  Mix sugar, shortening, vanilla and eggs in
large bowl. Mix in raisin liquid; stir in remaining ingredients.
Drop dough by round teaspoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto ungreased
cookie sheet. Bake until light brown, 8 to 10 minutes.  immediately
remove from cookie sheet.
