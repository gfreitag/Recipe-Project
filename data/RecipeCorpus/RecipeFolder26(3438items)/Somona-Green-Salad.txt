Title: Somona Green Salad
Categories: Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1    RASPBERRY VINAIGRETTE;recipe
1    Butter Lettuce;head
1 1/2 c  Walnuts
Wash lettuce, dry, and tear into bite-sized pieces. Place in salad
bowl, toss in walnuts and drizzle RASPBERRY VINAIGRETTE over salad to
taste. Optional: For a beautiful garnish, use clean unsprayed edible
flowers, such as nasturtium, borage, pineapple sage, chive or violets.
