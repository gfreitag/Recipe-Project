Title: Apple Pie with New Mexico Chili
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit, Chili
Yield: 8 slices
1 ts To 2 ts ground RED NEW
-MEXICO CHILI POWDER
1 1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg;
1/4 ts Salt; optional
Double 9" pie crust
1 1/2 lb Baking apples; peeled sliced
-(about 6 cups)
3/4 c  Water;
3/4 c  Granulated sugar;
2 tb Margarine; -=OR=-
2 tb Butter
In a large pot combine apples, water, sugar, margarine, chili powder,
cinnamon, nutmeg and salt.  Cook over medium heat, stirring
occasionally, 20-25 minutes or until apples are slightly tender and
juice thickens. Pour into bowl and cool 5 minutes.  Pour into unbaked
pie crust; cover with top crust and cut vents, or make lettice top
with strips of crust. Sprinkle with sugar.  Bake in preheated 375
degrees oven 30-40 minutes or until golden brown.  Cool and serve.
