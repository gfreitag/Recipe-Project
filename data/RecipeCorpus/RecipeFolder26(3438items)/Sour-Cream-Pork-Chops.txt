Title: Sour Cream Pork Chops
Categories: Pork, Salsa/sauce, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Chicken broth
1/4 c  White wine
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 ts Flour
6    Pork chops
Sage
Salt and pepper
1 tb Oil
1    Onion; sliced
Recipe by: Bob Schaefer

Sprinkle chops with sage, salt, and pepper. Brown in oil.  Add onion
and cook 1 minute. Add broth and wine. Cover and simmer 20 minutes.
Remove chops from skillet. Combine sour cream with flour; add to pan
and stir until smooth. Return chops to pan and heat through.
