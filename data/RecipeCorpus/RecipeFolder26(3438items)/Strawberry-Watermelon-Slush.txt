Title: Strawberry Watermelon Slush
Categories: Beverage, Low-cal/fat, Fruit
Yield: 1 quart
MMMMM-----------------COOLERS FROM CHEF FREDDY'S----------------------
1/3 c  Vodka
1/4 c  REALEMON lemon juice
2 c  Ice cubes
1 pt Fresh strawberrys [cleaned
& hulled]
2 c  Watermelon [seeded & cubed]
1/3 c  Sugar
In a blender container, combine all of the ingredients except
1)
the ice, and blend well...

While blending, gradually add the ice blending `til smooth...
2)

Serve immediately... garnished as desired...
3)

Calories... 110    Cholesterol... 0mg    Fat... 1g    Sodium... 150mg
