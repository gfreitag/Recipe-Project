Title: Goldon Dijon Barbeque Ribs
Categories: Bbq/grill, Pork, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 T  Soya sauce
1/4 t  Garlic powder
4 lb Spareribs
3/4 c  Dijon mustard
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
2 t  Onion powder
1 c  Liquid honey
2 ea - 3 tbsp red hot sauce
TO PREPARE RIBS;  Cut ribs into serving size pieces.  Place in large
saucepan and cover with water.  Bring to boil  Reduce heat.  Cover and
simmer about 1 hour or until meat is tender.  Drain ribs. Brush Golden
dijon barbecue sauce generously over ribs.  Barbecue or broil 4
inches from heat about 5 minutes per side.  Baste ribs again with
sauce just before serving.  Makes 6 servings. GOLDEN DIJON BBQ SAUCE:
In medium saucepan, combine honey, dijon mustard, hot sauce, soya
sauce, onion and garlic powders.  Bring mixture to boil Reduce heat,
simmer, uncovered, 15 - 20 minutes.  Stir occasionally. Refrigerate
any leftover sauce. (Great for barbecued chicken too)
