Title: Viennese Spiced Coffee
Categories: Mom's, Beverage, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Water
Whipped cream
Ground cinnamon
1/3 c  Instant coffee
3 T  Sugar
8 ea Whole cloves
3 ea Inches stick cinnamon
Combine 1/3 cup instant coffee, 3 tablespoons sugar, cloves, stick
cinnamon, and water. Cover, bring to boiling. Remove from heat and let
stand, covered, about 5 minutes to steep. Strain. Pour into cups and
top each with spoonful of whipped cream; dash lightly with cinnamon.
Fun to serve with cinnamon sticks as muddlers. Makes 4 to 6 servings.
