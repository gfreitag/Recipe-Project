Title: Salmon Spread
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Seafood, Spread
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM-----------------------MAKES: 2 CUPS----------------------------
3 ea Tb finely shredded carrot
Assorted crackers or party
-rye bread
Combine creamcheese,
-yogurt, sherry, dillweed,
-and lemon pepper
8 oz Package light cream cheese
1/3 c  Plain lowfat yogurt
2 ea Tb dry sherry or milk
1/2 t  Dried dillweed
1/4 t  Lemon pepper seasoning
6 1/8 oz Can skinnless, boneless
-salmon, drained and flaked
seasoning; beat until smooth. Stir in salmon and carrot. Cover and
chill at least three hours or overnight. Serve with party crackers or
rye bread. Source: BH&G Holiday Appetizers 1994 Carolyn Shaw's
Collection 11-94
