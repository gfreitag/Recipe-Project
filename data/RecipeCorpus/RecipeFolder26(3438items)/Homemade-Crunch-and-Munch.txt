Title: Homemade Crunch and Munch
Categories: Popcorn, Nuts/grains, Snack
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  Mixed nuts (your choice)
10 c  Popped popcorn
1 c  Real butter
1 c  White sugar
2 T  Clear corn syrup
In cast iron skillet, melt butter, sugar and syrup.  Bring to boil,
add nuts, and continue cooking for about 5 minutes or until mixture
turns a nice golden brown.  Pour over top of popped popcorn and mix
with a spoon until well blended. Be careful as this mixture stays HOT
a long time. Origin: Adapted from the Almond Butter Crunch Recipe.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
