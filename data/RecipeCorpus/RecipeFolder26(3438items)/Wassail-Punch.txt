Title: Wassail Punch
Categories: Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 10 servings
1 ga Sweet cider
2 ea Qts water
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Lemon juice concentrate
6 oz Orange juice, frozen
1 T  Ground allspice
4 ea Cinnamon sticks
Combine all ingredients and boil 10 minutes.  Remove cinnamon sticks
before serving.  Garnish with orange and lemon slices. Source:
Children's Party Book
