Title: Hearty Chicken & Rice Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Low-cal/fat, Chicken, Rice
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 ts Thyme leaves [dried]
1    Bay leaf
1 1/2 c  Chicken [cubed]
2 tb Lime juice
Lime slices for garnish
10 c  Chicken broth
1 md Onion
1 c  Celery [sliced]
1 c  Carrots [sliced]
1/4 c  Parsley [snipped]
1/2 ts Black pepper
Combine the broth, onion, celery, carrots, parsley, pepper,
1)
thyme, and bay leaf in a dutch oven, and bring to a boil over high
heat, stirring once or twice... 2) Reduce heat to low and simmer,
uncovered, 10 to 15 min., then add the chicken and simmer for 5 to 10
min. more or `til the chicken is done... 3) Remove and discard bay
leaf, stir in the rice and lime juice just before serving... garnish
with lime slices...

Calories... 184    Cholesterol... 23mg    Fat... 4g    Sodium...
1209mg
