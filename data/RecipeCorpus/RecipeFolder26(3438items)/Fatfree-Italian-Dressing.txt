Title: Fatfree Italian Dressing
Categories: Fatfree, Italian, Dressing
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Crushed dried basil
1/2 t  Crushed dried oregano
1 pn White pepper
2    Canned artichoke hearts
(packed in water),
Rinsed and drained
(about 1/2 cup)
2 T  Apple cider vinegar
1 T  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Water
2 T  Frozen apple juice
Concentrate
1    Clove garlic, cut in half
1 t  Honey
Combine all ingredients in blender or food processor and puree until
smooth.

Chill at least 15 minutes before serving.

Yields:  1 cup
