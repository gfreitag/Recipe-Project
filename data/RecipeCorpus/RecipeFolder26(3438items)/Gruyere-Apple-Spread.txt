Title: Gruyere Apple Spread
Categories: Condiment, Cheese/eggs, Apples, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 c  Shredded peeled apple
1 1/2 ts Chopped chives
6 oz Pkg cream cheese
1 c  Shredded Gruyere cheese
1 tb Milk
Beat cream cheese, blend in gruyere, milk and mustard. Stir in
remaining ingredients.
