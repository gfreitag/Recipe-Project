Title: Spiced Jellied Apricots
Categories: Mom's, Fruit, Dessert
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Stick cinnamon
1 pk Orange-flavored
Gelatin dessert
1 ea No. 2 1/2 can apricot halves
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
12 ea Cloves
Day before: Into saucepan, drain juice from apricots. add vinegar,
sugar, cloves, and cinnamon. Bring to boil add apricots; simmer 10
min. Remove apricots to 6 custard cups or individual molds. Strain
syrup; add hot water to make 2 cups. Pour over gelatin dessert- stir
until dissolved- pour over apricots. Refrigerate. To serve, unmold
onto relish tray. Makes 6 servings. Jellied Apricot Salad: Unmold
onto water cress or curly chicory. Place spoonful cottage cheese
beside each mold.
