Title: Pineapple Ginger Sauce <t>
Categories: Fatfree, Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Low sodium tamari or soy
Sauce
1 T  Cornstarch dissolved in 1
T  Cold water
1 t  Minced garlic
1 t  Minced ginger
1 c  No-sugar-added pineapple
Juice
Saute garlic and ginger in a little water in a medium non-stick
skillet. After about 3 minutes add juice and soysauce.  Heat on
medium high until it boils, then stir in cornstarch.  Heat until it
gets to your desired consistency.
