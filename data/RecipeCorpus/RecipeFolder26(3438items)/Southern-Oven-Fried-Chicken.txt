Title: Southern Oven Fried Chicken
Categories: Chicken, Sthrn/livng, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Fryer chicken (2-1/2 to 3
-lbs) cut up
1/3 c  Melted marg
1 pk (4oz) potato chips, crushed
1/4 t  Garlic salt
ds Pepper
combine crushed potato chips, garlic salt and peper.  Dip chicken into
melted margarine and roll in dip mixture.  Place on baking pan so they
don't touch.  Bake at 350F for 45 minutes. DO NOT TURN Origin: My mom,
Shirley Whittaker Shared by: Sharon Stevens
