Title: Hard Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Vanilla or
2 tb Cognac or rum
1/3 c  Butter
1 c  Confectioner's sugar
Recipe by: The American Heritage Cookbook - ISBN 0-8281-04-03-4 Cream
1/3 cup of butter until soft. Add, a little at a time, 1 cup
confectioners' sugar and either 1 tsp of vanilla or 2 Tbs cognac or
rum, stirring until smooth. Serve cold with steamed puddings or on
apple pie.

From:  Dan Klepach
