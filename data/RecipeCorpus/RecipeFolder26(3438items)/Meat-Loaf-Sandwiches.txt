Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise on one or two slices of white or
whole wheat bread.  Top with 1 or 2 slices of left over meatloaf. Top
with second slice of bread if desired.  Serve with Potato Chips and
Pickle if served cold.  Gravy if served hot.  Catsup maybe used in
place of the mayonnaise if desired.

From The Redbook Magazine January 1986
