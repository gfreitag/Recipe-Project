Title: Peppermint Chocolate Bonbons
Categories: Candy, Chocolate
Yield: 2 dozens
1 c  Unsifted powdered sugar
1/4 t  Peppermint extract
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Whipping cream
1.  Mix chocolate chips, butter and cream in a heavy saucepan.
2.  Place over very low heat and stir until chips and butter are
melted and mixture is smooth and thick.  Remove from heat.
:
3.  Beat in the powdered sugar until mixture is smooth again.  Stir
in the peppermint extract.
4.  Cover and chill 1 to 2 hours until mixture is thick enough to
shape or drop into small candy cups.
:
5.  Divide the mixture into 24 portions.
6.  Drop into paper bonbon cups or first roll into small balls
before placing into cups.
