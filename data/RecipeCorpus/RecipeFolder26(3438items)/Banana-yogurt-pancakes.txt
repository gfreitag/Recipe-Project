Title: Banana yogurt pancakes
Categories: Breakfast, Low-cal/fat, Fruit, Pancakes
Yield: 5 servings
1    Egg, separated
1 c  Plain, non-fat yogurt
1/4 c  Skim milk
2    Bananas, mashed
1 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Soda
1/4 ts Salt
Sift together dry ingredients.  Set aside.  Mix together beaten egg
yolk, yogurt, milk, and bannana.  Add to four mixture, mixing well.
Fold in whipped egg white, and pour into oiled pan or waffle iron.
Cook each side until brown.
