Title: Blueberry Dessert
Categories: Dessert, Cheese/eggs, Fruit
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1    Can blueberry pie filling
Whipped Cream or Cool Whip
1 3/4 c  Graham wafer crumbs
1/2 c  White sugar
1/2 c  Melted butter
8 oz Cream cheese
2    Eggs
Mix graham crumbs, sugar and melted butter.  Press into a 9 x 13
baking pan.

Beat together cream cheese, eggs, sugar and vanilla.  Spread on crumb
base Bake for 20 min at 375 F Let cool. Spread with blueberry pie
filling and top with whipped cream or Cool Whip.

Note:  These squares freeze well.  I cut them into portions, freeze on
cookie sheets, then store in plastic containers. Then you can just
take our as many as you want for desserts.
