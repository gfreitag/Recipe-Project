Title: California Gold Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 32 1x2" bars
1 ts Vanilla
1 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Apricot jam
1 c  C and H Golden Brown Sugar
-- firmly packed
1 c  Butter or margarine
1    Egg
Beat together sugar, butter, egg and vanilla until smooth and creamy.
Stir in flour and nuts.  Spoon half the batter in a greased 9-inch
square pan; spread evenly.  Spread batter evenly with jam.  Cover jam
with remaining batter.  Bake in 325 degree oven 50 minutes.  Cool 10
minutes. Cut into 32 bars (1 x 2 inches).
