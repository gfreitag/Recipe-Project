Title: Zippy Blue Cheese Spread
Categories: Condiment, Cheese/eggs, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Oregano leaves
1 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts White pepper
3/4 c  Crumbled blue cheese
1 c  Creamed cottage cheese
1/2 c  Butter
1 ts Fresh basil
Blend together and store in refrigerator.
