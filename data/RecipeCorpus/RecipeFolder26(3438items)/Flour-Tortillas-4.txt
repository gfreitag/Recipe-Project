Title: Flour Tortillas 4
Categories: Bread, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Warm Milk
1 tb Baking powder
1 ts Salt
4 c  All-purpose flour
2 tb Lard (yes, lard. Shortening
-doesn't work as well)
Mix all the dry ingredients and sift. Mix in lard. Add warm (not hot)
milk gradually, knead til smooth. Cut into pieces. Roll out paper
thin into round shape. Cook on hot griddle until lightly browned and
"spotted". Submitted By BOBBI ZEE   On   12-25-94
