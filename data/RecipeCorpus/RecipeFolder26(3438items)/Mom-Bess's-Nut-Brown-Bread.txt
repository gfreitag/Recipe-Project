Title: Mom Bess's Nut Brown Bread
Categories: Bread, Mom's, Fruit, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 loaf
MMMMM------------------------MIX AS GIVEN-----------------------------
2    Eggs; well beaten
1/2 ts Salt
4 c  Flour
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 ts Vanilla
1 pk Dates
2 ts Soda
2 c  Boiling water
2 c  Sugar
2 tb Crisco
Pour boiling water over dates and soda and let cool.

(although she didn't say, I assume you add the rest of the ingredients
after the above cools)

Bake slowly for 1 hour until brown all over.  Grease and flour pans
well and fill only one half full.

From:  Becci McClain's paternal grandmother's favorite recipes
collection. 1/95.
