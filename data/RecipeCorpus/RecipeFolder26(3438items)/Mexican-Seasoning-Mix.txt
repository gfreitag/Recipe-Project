Title: Mexican Seasoning Mix
Categories: Herbs/spice, Mixes, Vegetable, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
2 T  Crushed red pepper
1 t  Dried oregano
1/4 t  Garlic powder
1/3 c  Minced dried onion
1/4 c  Dried parsley flakes
1 T  Instant chicken bouillon
-granules
Combine onion, parsley flakes, bouillon granules, chile powder, red
pepper, oregano and garlic powder.  Stor in an airtight container.
Shake or stir thouroughly to mix ingredients before measuring.  Makes
3/4 c. mix. Source: Better Homes and Gardens, Fix it fast cookbook
