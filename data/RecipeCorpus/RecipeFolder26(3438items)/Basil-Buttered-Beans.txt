Title: Basil Buttered Beans
Categories: Fatfree, Beans
Yield: 2 servings
Melted
1 1/2 t  Fresh Basil -- minced, or
1/2 t  Dry
1/4 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
Pieces
2 c  Green Beans -- fresh, cut
Into 2" pieces
2 T  Chopped Onion
2 T  Chopped Celery
1/4 c  Water
1 t  Butter Buds. -- or butter,
REVISED: the original recipe called for 2 T butter or margarine melted

In a saucepan, combine beans, onion, celery and water.  Cover and
cook for 5 min or until beans are tender.  Drain.  Add the butter,
basil, salt and pepper; stir to coat.  Serve immediately.

: Taste of Home, June/July 1994, pg16
