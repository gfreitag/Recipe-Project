Title: Mirabour's Bean Soup
Categories: Fatfree, Beans, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
None
1 grated carrot 1/2 cup chopped bell pepper (any color) 1/2 cup
chopped onion

Place these in a small bowl and top with 4 oz. beer (or water or
vegetable stock).  Cook in microwave ~4 minutes, until they begin to
soften.

Meanwhile, in a 4-qt. pot, place:

1 can fatfree (vegetarian) refried beans 8 oz. beer (can substitute
water or vegetable stock) 1 1/2 cups water or vegetable stock 1
teaspoon ground cumin 1/2 teaspoon chili powder 1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/4 - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro 1/4
teaspoon black pepper

Start over high heat.  Stir until beans "dissolve." Then add 1/2 can
pinto beans.  Mash the remaining beans and add.

Then add:

3/4 cup frozen spinach 3/4 cup frozen corn 1 lime, peel removed, cut
into small chunks 1/2 cup prepared salsa (picante sauce) (I used
Arriba brand) the microwaved vegetables, above

Reduce to medium heat and simmer 20 - 30 minutes until thickened. (Add
water if necessary to thin.)

Top with any combination of the following:

Fatfree cheese Fatfree yogurt Crushed baked tortilla chips Chopped
cilantro Chopped green onions
