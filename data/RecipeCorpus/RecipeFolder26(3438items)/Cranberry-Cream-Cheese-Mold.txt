Title: Cranberry Cream Cheese Mold
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Whole Berry Cranberry
-sauce
1 1/2 c  Cold Water
1 pk Philly Brand Cream Cheese
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
1 pk Cranberry Flavored Jello
(8 serving size)
1/2 ts Ground Cinnamon
1 md Apple, chopped
Stir boiling water into gelatin in large bowl 2 minutes or until
completely dissolved.  Stir in cold water and cinnamon.  Pour 2 cups
of the gelatin into medium bowl.  Refridgerate about 1 1/2 hours or
until thickened (spoon drawn through leaves a definite impression ).
Reserve remaining 1 cup gelatin at room temperature.

Stir apple and cranberry sauce into thickened gelatin.  Spoon into 6
cup mold.  Refigerate about 30 minutes or until set but not firm
(should stick to finger when touched and should mound)

Stir reserved 1 cup gelatin gradually into cream cheese in small bowl
with wire whisk until smooth.  Put over gelatin layer in mold.

Refrigerate 4 hours or until firm, unmold.
