Title: Cuban Pork Roast
Categories: Pork, Main dish, Caribbean
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Oregano
3 ea Garlic cloves
3 lb Pork shoulder - seasoned
1/3 c  Lime or lemon juice
1 ea Onion, sliced
Season meat with salt and pepper.  Pour lime or lemon juice over
roast. Place onion slices over roast.  Let stand in refrigerator 1-2
hours. Split meat in places and add garlic plugs in slashes.  Roast
at 325 degrees for 1 1/2 - 2 hours or 35-40 minutes per pound.
