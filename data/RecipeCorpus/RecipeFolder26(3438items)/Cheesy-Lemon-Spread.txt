Title: Cheesy Lemon Spread
Categories: Condiment, Cheese/eggs, Fruit, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Grated lemon peel
1 ds Salt
3 oz Pkg cream cheese
2    Eggs; slightly beaten
1/4 c  Lemon juice
3 tb Butter
3/4 c  Sugar
In heavy pan, mix eggs, lemon juice, butter, sugar and salt. Bring to
a boil, stirring, over low heat, then cook 3 minutes longer. Beat
cream cheese and lemon peel. When egg mixture is cool, blend into
cheese mixture until smooth. Store in refrigerator.
