Title: Nutty Chocolate Stars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 40 servings
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
2/3 c  Nuts, finely chopped
1 pk Milk Chocolate Stars or
Kisses
3/4 c  Butter,soft
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Oats
Preheat oven to 350 F. Grease cookies sheets. In Lareg bowl, cream
butter and sugar together. Blend in egg and vanilla. Add oats, flour
and baking soda, mix well. Shape into 1 inch balls, roll in nuts.
Place on cookie sheets. Bake 10-12 minutes. Remove from oven, gently
press candy into center of each cookie. Cool 1 minutes on sheet, then
remove to racks. Store tightly covered.
