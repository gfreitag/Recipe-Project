Title: Homemade Heath Bars...
Categories: Candy, Nuts/grains, Chocolate
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Butter; (1 stick)
2/3 c  Brown sugar; dark
Butter
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped, any kind
4    Hershey chocolate bars
Butter the bottom of a loaf pan.  Sprinkle nuts into the pan. Unwrap
the 4 hershey bars and reserve.  Stir butter and brown sugar in
saucepan over medium heat. Stir CONSTANTLY. When it begins actually
boiling, start timing exactly 6 minutes while continuing to stir
constantly. After 6 minutes, immediately pour the mixture over the
nuts, spreading evenly. Then cover the hot mixture with the chocolate
bars, smoothing with a spatular as the chocolate melts.  Chill.
Break into uneven pieces by poking a dull knife through the candy.
This recipe is easily tripled or quadrupled.
