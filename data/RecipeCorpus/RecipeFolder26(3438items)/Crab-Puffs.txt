Title: Crab Puffs
Categories: Appetizer, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Butter or Margarine
1/4 t  Salt
1 c  Flour
4 ea Eggs.
6 oz Crabmeat
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar
3 ea Green onions, chopped
1 t  Dry mustard
1 t  Worchestershire sauce
Combine first five and stir well.  Set Aside.  Combine water, butter
and salt in a medium saucepan, bring to a boil and reduce heat to
low.  Add flour and stir until it leaves the sides and forms a smooth
ball.  Allow to cool and add eggs, 1 at a time beating well after
each addition.  Add Crab. Drop by teaspoon sized balls onto ungreased
cookie sheet.  Bake 400 for 14 min.   350  10 min.  They can be
frozen. Shared By: Pat Stockett
