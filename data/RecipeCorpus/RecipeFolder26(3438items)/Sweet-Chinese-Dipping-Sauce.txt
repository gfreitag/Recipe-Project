Title: Sweet Chinese Dipping Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Dip
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 T  Sherry
1/2 ea Clove garlic in one piece
2 T  Soy sauce
2 T  Honey
Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan and bring to a boil.
Remove from heat and cool to room temperature.  Serves 4 to 6.
Source: American WholeFood Cuisine
