Title: Scalloped Oysters
Categories: Seafood, Clam/oyster, Mushroom
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  Butter
1 c  Milk
Paprika
1 c  Chopped Mushrooms
1 c  Cracker Crumbs
1 1/2 pt Oysters
1/2 c  Light Cream
Saute mushrooms in butter. Line bottom of greased casserole with 1/3
cup of crumbs. Add layer of mushrooms and dot with butter. Add layer
of crumbs, then oysters, mushrooms and crumbs. Pour cream, milk and
melted butter over top. Set in a pan of hot water and bake for 25
minutes at 375 degrees. SOURCE:*Lillian Lacey SHARED BY: Jim Bodle
