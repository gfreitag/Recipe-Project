Title: Ginger Pork Ribs
Categories: Pork, Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 8 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2 T  Fresh ginger,grated
2 T  Orange rind,grated
8 ea To 10 lb baby back pork ribs
2 T  Unsalted butter
1/3 c  Honey
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
16 oz Jar ginger preserve or
-marmalade
2 md White onions,finely chopped
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/3 c  Dry sherry
Place ribs in a bowl or container large enough to accommodate them.
Set aside. In a tall sided pan,saute the chopped onions in the butter
for 5 minutes.Stir in remaining ingredients and heat through;do not
boil. Remove from flame and allow to cool. When cool,pour over ribs
to coat evenly.Cover container and refrigerate.Let marinate for 4
hours,turning occasionally. Grill over hot coals for about 30
minutes,turning several times and basting frequently with the
remaining marinade.Serves 8.
