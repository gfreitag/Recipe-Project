Title: Chicken and Tarragon with Lime
Categories: Chicken, Fruit, Herbs/spice, Marinade
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Onion salt
1/4 t  Black pepper
1/2 c  Lime juice
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 t  Tarragon
Combine ingredients and brush over two chickens, cut into pieces.
Marinate 2-3 hours.  Cook on grill or under broiler, using additional
marinade as a basting sauce. Origin: Adapted from a Tupperware
Marinade recipe insert. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
