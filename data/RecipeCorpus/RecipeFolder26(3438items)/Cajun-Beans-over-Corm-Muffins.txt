Title: Cajun Beans over Corm Muffins
Categories: Beans, Cajun/creol, Main dish, Muffin
Yield: 13 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
-granules   1 tsp minced
-onion
1/8 t  Garlic powder
1/2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 ea Pk frozen corn muffins (3
-muffins)
1 ea - 8 oz can stewed tomatoes
1 t  Instant chicken bouillon
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 T  Diced dried green pepper
1 ea Or 2 drops tabasco sauce
1 ea - 15 oz can great northern
-beans
Bake muffins per package.  Combine undrained beans, undrained
tomatoes, and all but cheese in medium sauce pan.  Bring to a boil,
reduce heat, cover and simmer 10 minutes.  Split muffins.  Spoon
beans over muffins and sprinkle with cheese.
