Title: Oysters Flambeed
Categories: Seafood, Main dish, Mushroom, Clam/oyster
Yield: 2 servings
4    Fresh mushrooms, sliced and
-sauted in butter
1 c  Sour cream
1/2 t  Worcestershire sauce
1/2 t  Dry mustard
3 T  Cognac, warm over hot water
12    Oysters, scrubbed, shucked
-lightly rolled in cracker
-crumbs
2 T  Butter
1 t  Fresh parsley, chop fine
1 t  Finely chopped scallion
Saute the oysters lightly in butter in a chafing dish or saucepan.
Add all other ingredients except cognac, and blend well. Pour in the
cognac and light it. When the flames die, mix the sauce again and
serve over fresh toats triangles.
