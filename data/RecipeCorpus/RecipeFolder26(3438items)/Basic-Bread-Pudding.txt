Title: Basic Bread Pudding
Categories: Dessert, Bread, Pudding
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Buttered bread, cubed
1 ts Cinnamon or nutmeg
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
2 c  Hot milk
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Butter
1/4 c  Raisins
Beat egg well, add sugar, salt, spice and milk.  Beat til very
smooth. Add butter, bread cubes raisins and vanilla.

Pour into buttered baking dish; oven poach until firm, about 45
minutes, at 325 F.
