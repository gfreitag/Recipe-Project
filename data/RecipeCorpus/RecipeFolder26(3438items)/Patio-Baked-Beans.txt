Title: Patio Baked Beans
Categories: Beans, Vegetable, Pork
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Worchestershire sauce
1 t  Garlic powder
ds Of tabasco
3-4 bacon slices, diced
2 ea 28oz cans pork and beans
1/4 c  Chopped onions
1 c  Ketchup or chili sauce
1/2 c  Brown sugar or molasses
1 T  Parsley flakes
Combine all ingredients, spoon into 2 quart casserole or bean pot.
Cover and bake at 350F for 1/2 hour.  Reduce heat to 275F and bake
for an additional 2-1/2 hours. Origin: My Mom, Shirley Whittaker
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
