Title: Tempting Almond Tea
Categories: Fatfree, Beverage
Yield: 6 servings
2 T  Lemon Juice
1 t  Almond Extract
1/4 t  Vanilla
4 t  Bags -- regular strength
1/2 t  Lemon Rind -- finely grated
4 c  Boiling Water
1/2 c  Sugar -- or substitute
Refreshing taste treat.

Place tea bags and lemon rind in lg scalded teapot or pan.  Pour in
boiling water.  Cover and steep 5 min.

Stir in sugar, lemon juice, almond extract and vanilla. Serve at once.

Serves 4 - 6.

NOTE:  1/4 C honey may be substituted for sugar.  Place a fresh mint
leaf in bottom of cup before pouring in tea.
