Title: Potato Bake
Categories: Casserole, Potato
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Cream chicken soup
12 oz Sour cream
2 c  Grated cheddar
2 c  Crushed corn flakes
1/4 c  Melted butter
2 lb Bag Frozen hash brown
->
Potatoes, thawed
1/2 c  Melted butter/margarine
1 ts Salt
ds Pepper
1.  Combine first 8 ingredients in large bowl.

2.  Pour mixture into a greased 9 x 13 inch baking dish

3.  Top with corn flakes and drizzle with 1/4 c butter and sprinkle
with
paprika.

4.  Bake 350 degrees for 1 hour
