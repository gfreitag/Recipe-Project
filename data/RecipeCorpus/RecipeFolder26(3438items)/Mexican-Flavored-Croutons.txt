Title: Mexican Flavored Croutons
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salad, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Mexican seasoned mix
2 tb Corn oil
1/2 c  Cubed bread
1/2 c  Nacho chips crumbled
toss all and spread in a shallow pan  bake 20 minutes at 250. Will
keep in freezer 1 year Pantry several weeks
