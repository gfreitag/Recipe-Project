Title: Egg Harbor Hurry
Categories: Clam/oyster, Potato, Pork, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 lb Bacon
10 ea Potatoes
2 ea Onions
N.J. Sea Grant Extension
-Service
1 1/2 ea Dozen Chowder Clams
Open clams and cut in small pieces.  Save juice or liquor.  Peel and
slice potatoes and onions.  Cut bacon in small pieces and fry in a
large cast iron skillet. Add sliced potatoes and onions, cover with
clam juice, cover and simmer gently until potatoes are done. Add
clams and cook for 10 minutes. Serve with black pepper.  No salt
needed. Shared By: Pat Stockett
