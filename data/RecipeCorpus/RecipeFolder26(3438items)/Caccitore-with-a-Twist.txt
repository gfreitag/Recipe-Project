Title: Caccitore with a Twist
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Main dish, Turkey
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Turkey breast slices, cut
-into strips
15 oz Can tomato sauce
15 oz Can chick peas, drained
3/4 ts Dried oregano leaves
1 c  Uncooked rotini
2 ts Olive oil or oil
1 md Onion, sliced
1 sm Green pepper, seeded and cut
-into strips
2 cl Garlic, minced
Cook rotini to desired doneness. Drain and keep warm. Heat oil in a
large nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot. Add onion, pepper,
garlic, and turkey. Cook, stirring, 5-10 minutes until turkey is no
longer pink. Add tomato sauce, beans and oregano; mix well. Bring to
boiling; reduce heat and cover. Simmer 10 minutes or until thoroughly
heated. Serve over rotini.

Per serving: 280 cal., 21g pro., 42g carb., 6g fiber, 4g fat, 35mg
chol., 870mg sod., 740mg pot., 2 starch, 2 lean meat, and 1 vegetable
exchange.
