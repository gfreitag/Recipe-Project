Title: Rod's Chili Iii
Categories: Chili, Ground beef, Soup/stew, Chilies, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
-chopped fine
1 ea Habanero chili, seeded
-chopped fine
1 lg Clove garlic
2 ea 15 oz cn  stewed tomatoes
-(pureed in a blender)
1 ea 12 oz can beer
1 t  Beef base
3 t  Paprika, sweet
1 t  Coriander powder
1/4 t  Cayenne pepper
1 ea Shot Jim Beam Bourbon whisky
2 T  Olive oil
Masa Harisa (Corn Flour for
-Thickening)
1 3/4 lb Chili Grind Meat
3 sl Bacon
1 T  Cumin seed, roasted and
-ground
1 T  Chili powder - New Mexican
1 T  Ancho chili powder
1 T  California chili powder
1 t  Mexican oregano
1/4 t  Thyme
1/4 t  Allspice
1/4 t  Dried cilantro
1 lg Onion (chopped fine)
2 ea Stalks celery (chopped fine)
1 cn El Paso green chiles (mild)
3 ea Jalapeno chilies, seeded
Cook bacon and reserve grease for sauting onions and garlic.  In a
large chili pot, saute onion and garlic.  Remove onions and garlic
and set aside. Add olive oil and cook meat until grey in color, but
not browned.  Add onion and garlic back to chili pot. Add dry spices
and cook while stirring for 3 or 4 minutes.  Add stewed tomatoes,
bacon bits, chiles, beer, celery, whiskey, beef base.  Bring to a
boil and then simmer until done. 3 to 4 hours.
