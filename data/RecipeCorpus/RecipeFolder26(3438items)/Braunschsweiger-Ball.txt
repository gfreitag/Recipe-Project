Title: Braunschsweiger Ball
Categories: Appetizer, Pork, Sausage, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 15 servings
1 pk Cream cheese (small)
2 tb Milk
1 ds Garlic salt
1 pk Braunschweiger (large)
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Dry onion soup
2 tb Salad dressing
Blend the first 4 ingredients and form a ball.  Chill at least 2
hours or overnight.  Mix icing together, ice ball.  Chill until
served.

Icing:  1 small package cream cheese
2 tbsp. milk
dash of garlic salt Randy Rigg
