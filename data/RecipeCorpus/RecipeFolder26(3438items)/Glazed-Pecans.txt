Title: Glazed Pecans
Categories: Snack, Nuts/grains
Yield: 2 cups
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Vanilla
Butter or margarine
2 c  Pecan halves, walnut halves
-or cashews
Line a baking sheet with foil.  Butter the foil, then set baking sheet
aside.

In a heavy 10-inch skillet combine nuts, sugar, and butter or
margarine. Cook and stir over medium heat for 8 to 9 minutes or till
sugar melts and turns a rich brown color.

Remove skillet from heat.  Immediately stir in vanilla.  Spread nut
mixture onto the prepared baking sheet.  Cool.  Break up mixture into
clusters. Store tightly covered.  Makes 2 cups.
