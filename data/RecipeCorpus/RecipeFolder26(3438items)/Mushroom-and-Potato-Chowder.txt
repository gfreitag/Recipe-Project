Title: Mushroom and Potato Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Potato, Mushroom
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Diced peeled potatoes
1/2 c  Chopped carrots
1 c  Light cream
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 c  Chopped onion
4 c  Butter or margarine
2 T  All-purpose flour
1 t  Salt
1/2 t  Pepper
3 c  Water
In a large kettle, saute onion in butter until tender. Add flour,
salt and pepper; stir to make a smooth paste. Gradually add water,
stirring constantly. Bring to a boil; cook and stir 1 minute. Add the
mushrooms, celery, potatoes and carrots. Reduce heat; cover and
simmer for 30 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Add cream and
Parmesan cheese; heat through. Serves 4-6. SOURCE:* Taste of Home
Magazine Aug./Sept. 94 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 10/94
