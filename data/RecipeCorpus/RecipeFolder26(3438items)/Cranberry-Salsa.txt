Title: Cranberry Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Sugar
1 T  Crushed dried mint
6 T  Lime juice
1/4 t  Ground ginger
1    12 oz bag fresh cranberries
Chopped
4    Green onions, minced
2    Jalapeno peppers, minced
Combine ALL ingredients in a bowl Cover and chill 24 hours or up to 4
days Internet: jchappel@tyrell.net
