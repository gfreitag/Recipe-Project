Title: Sweet Sensation
Categories: Mixes, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Ground nutmeg
2 t  Ground cinnamon
2 t  Dried thyme
Makes about 1 cup
Use with tender cuts of pork
1/4 c  Ground allspice
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Onion powder
2 T  Salt
Mix the spices thoroughly in a bowl.  Store covered in a cool, dark
pantry. From: Smoke & Spice Shared By: Pat Stockett
