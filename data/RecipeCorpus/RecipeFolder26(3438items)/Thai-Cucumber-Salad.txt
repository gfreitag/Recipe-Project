Title: Thai Cucumber Salad
Categories: Fatfree, Thai, Vegetable, Salad
Yield: 5 servings
2 ts Sugar
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Freshly minced cilantro
2    Medium-sized cucumbers
1/2 c  Very finely minced red
Onions
1/2 ts Salt
MMMMM--------------------------OPTIONAL-------------------------------
Strips, or crushed
Red pepper, to taste
Sprigs of cilantro
1/2 c  Finely mined red or green
Bell pepper
1 sm Red serrano chili, seeded
And cut into very thin
1)  Peel and seed the cucumbers.  Cut into quarters lengthwise, then
into thin slices.

2)  Combine everything except the serrano chile and cilantro sprigs
into a medium-sized bowl.  Mix gently. Cover tightly, and let
marinate in the refrigerator at least 4 hours.

3)  Serve cold.  Top with thin strips of red serrano chili and sprigs
of fresh cilantro, if desired, shortly before serving.
