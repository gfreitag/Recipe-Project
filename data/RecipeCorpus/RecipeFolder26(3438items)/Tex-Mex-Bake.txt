Title: Tex Mex Bake
Categories: Main dish, Meat, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
4 oz Chopped Green Chilies*
3 oz Monterrey Jack Cheese**
8 oz Tomato Sauce
1 md Green Pepper, Chopped
2 c  Crushed Corn Chips
1 ea Large Egg Beaten
2 T  Water
1 ea Env. Onion Soup Mix
1 lb Lean Ground Beef
*  Green chilies should be drained. ** Cheese should be any mild
cheese, shredded. Should = 1 Cup.

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Combine corn chips, egg, and water;
press into 9-inch pie plate or casserole. Bake 10 minutes. Meanwhile,
in large bowl, combine onion recipe soup mix, ground beef, chilies,
and 1/2 C cheese; evenly press into prepared crust.  Top with tomato
sauce, then green pepper and bake 30 minutes.  Top with remaining
cheese, then bake an additional 5 minutes or until cheese is melted
and beef is done.
