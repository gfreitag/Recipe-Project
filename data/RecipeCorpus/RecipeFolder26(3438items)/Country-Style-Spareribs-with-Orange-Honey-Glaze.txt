Title: Country Style Spareribs with Orange Honey Glaze
Categories: Pork, Microwave, Bbq/grill, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2 T  Wine vinegar
1 ea Garlic clove, pressed
8 ea Spareribs
1/2 c  Orange juice
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
2 T  Minced onion
1 t  Worcestershire sauce
8 oz Tomato sauce
1/2 c  Honey
Cook ribs at 50 percent power, lightly covered, for 20 minutes. Drain.
Combine remaining ingredients.  Pour over cooked ribs, continue
cooking at 50 percent until ribs are tender.  Time will depend on the
size of the ribs.  Let stand 5 minutes. This is very good served with
scalloped potatoes.
