Title: Blueberry Pancakes
Categories: Breakfast, Fatfree, Pancakes, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 T  Sugar if blueberries are
-unsweetened
1/2 c  Soymilk or rice milk
1/2 c  Canned blueberries w/juice
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 t  Baking powder
1/4 t  Salt
1/4 t  Baking soda
Mix the dry ingredients togther; then mix in the soymilk and the
blueberries.  If the batter is too thick, mix in more blueberry juice
until it is the right consistency. Cook in a nonstick pan until they
look edible. They will be very blue!
