Title: Summer Pasta Salad with Fruit
Categories: Salad, Fruit, Pasta
Yield: 1 servings
-chicken
2 tb Chopped green onions (with
-tops)
1 cn (11 ounces) mandarin orange
-segments, drained
1 cn (8 ounces) pineapple
-chunks, drained
1 pk Suddenly Salad creamy
-macaroni salad mix
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Milk
1 pk (3 ounces) cream cheese,
-softened
1    L/2 cups cut-up cooked
Prepare salad as directed on package, except stir cream cheese
thoroughly into mayonnaise mixture. Stir remaining ingredients into
finished salad.

6 servings.
