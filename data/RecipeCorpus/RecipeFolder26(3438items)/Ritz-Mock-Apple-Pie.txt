Title: Ritz Mock Apple Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Dessert
Yield: 1 servings
2 t  Cream of tartar
20 ea Ritz crackers
Pastry
2 c  Water
1 1/4 c  Sugar
Apple pie spice
Bring to a boil the water, sugar, spice, and cream of tartar.  Add 20
ounces ritz crackers.  Boil about 8 minutes.  Pour into uncooked pie
shell and cover with pastry top.  Prick and bake at 425F for 30-35
minutes. Serve warm.  It really does taste like apple pie! Origin:
Found in recipe box bought at garage sale. Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
Jan/95
