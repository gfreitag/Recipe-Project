Title: Borscht 2
Categories: Soup/stew, German, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Tomato juice
1/2 c  Shredded cabbage
1/2 c  Chopped beet tops
ds Of dill
1 c  Diced potatoes
1 c  Thick sour cream
2 c  Water
2 c  Finely grated beets
1/2 c  Finely shredded carrots
1 c  Finely chopped onions
2 c  Beef stock
2 T  Butter
Combine water, beets, carrots, and onions into soup pot.  Bring to a
boil and cook for about 20 minutes.  Add the stock and remaining
ingredients except the potatoes and sour cream.  Simmer for 15
minutes, taste and season.  In a small saucpan, bring potatoes to a
boil and cook until tender, about 10-15 minutes.  Add potaotes and
sour cream to first mixture. Serve immediately.  Serves 10. Origin:
HomeStyle, Canadian Classics Shared by: Sharon Stevens. ++_ End
