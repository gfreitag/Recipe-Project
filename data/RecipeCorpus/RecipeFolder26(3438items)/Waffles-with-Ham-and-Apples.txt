Title: Waffles with Ham and Apples
Categories: Waffles, Pork, Apples
Yield: 4 servings
-sugar.  Allow an extra
-half hour preparation time
For the milk to soak into
-the waffles.
Serve this sweet and savory
-brunch dish with a drizzle
-of maple syrup or a
Sprinkling of confectioners
MMMMM-------------------PREP TIME:  10 MINUTES------------------------
MMMMM-------------------COOK TIME:  25 MINUTES------------------------
-waffles, unthawed
2 md Sized sweet apples, 1
-cored, peeled, and chopped,
The other cored and sliced
4 oz Baked or boiled ham, diced
1/2 c  Shredded low fat Cheddar
-cheese (2oz)
2 c  Low fat milk
2 lg Eggs
1 lg Egg white
1/3 c  Maple syrup
1/2 t  Vanilla extract
1/4 t  Salt
1 ea (10oz) package frozen
Whisk the milk, eggs, egg white, syrup, vanilla, and salt in a large
bowl until blender.  Arrange 4 of the waffles side by side in a
lightly greased 9x9x2 inch baking pan.  Sprinkle evenly with the
chopped apple and ham, then top with the remaining 4 waffles.  Pour
the milk mixture over all. Cover with foil, and let stand for 30
minutes at room temperature. Preheat the oven to 375F.  Bake on top
rack in oven, covered, for 15 minutes. Remove foil, sprinkle the
cheese on top, and bake 10 minutes more or until the cheese is
melted. Garnish with the remaining sliced apples. Serves 4. Shared
by: Rich Harper
