Title: Sourdough Cornmeal Cakes
Categories: Bread, Sourdough
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sourdough starter
3    Eggs
2 ts Salt
4 c  Flour
2 c  Cornmeal
1/2 c  Milk
Mix all ingredients together the night before and let rise in a warm
place. Bake on a griddle the next morning.

DATE: 08-08-93
FROM: NORMAN WILLIAMS
