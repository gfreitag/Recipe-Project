Title: Applesauce Christmas Cake
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Fruit, Holiday, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Applesauce
2 1/2 c  Flour, all-purpose
3/4 t  Salt
1 t  Cloves, ground
2 T  Molasses
1 c  Raisins
4 T  Butter (or marg.)
1/2 c  Shortening
1 ea Egg
1 t  Soda
1 t  Baking powder
2 t  Cinnamon, ground
2 t  Nutmeg, ground
1 t  Vanilla extract
1/4 c  Walnuts, chopped
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
3/4 t  Vanilla extract
1/4 c  Walnuts, chopped
1 T  Milk
1 1/2 c  Sugar, powdered
Cream shortening and sugar until light and fluffy.  Beat in egg.
Combine applesauce and soda; beat into creamed mixture.  Combine
flour, baking powder, salt, and spices; add to applesauce mixture.
Stir in molasses, vanilla, raisins, and walnuts. Line the bottom of a
10" tube pan with waxed paper; grease and flour sides of pan. Spoon
batter into pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 to 60 minutes. Let cool
in pan 10 minutes; then remove cake from pan. When completely cool,
spread top of cake with frosting. Frosting: Heat milk and butter in a
saucepan until hot, but not boiling.  Beat in sugar.  Stir in walnuts
and vanilla.  Yield: about 1 cup.
