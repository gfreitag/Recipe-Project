Title: Potato Candy
Categories: Candy, Dessert, Nuts/grains, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Coconut
1 pk Chocolate; [1 square]
1 ts Butter
1    Potato; large
2 ts Butter
1 lb Confectioners' sugar
1 ts Vanilla
Boil & mash potato. While hot add 2 t butter, sugar, vanilla &
coconut, minced in food processor or blender. Pat evenly into
well-buttered pan and set aside to cool. Melt chocolate and combine
with 1 t butter. Spread on top of candy. Cut into squares before
chocolate hardens. Ruth from PA
