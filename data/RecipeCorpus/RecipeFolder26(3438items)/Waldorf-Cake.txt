Title: Waldorf Cake
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Mom's, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 cake
2 c  Flour
3    Eggs, beaten separately
1 1/2 c  Sweet milk
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Butter or oleo
2 c  Sugar
2 oz Baking chocolate
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Nuts
Cream butter and sugar.  Add melted chocolate, egg yolks and vanilla.
Sift dry ingredients and add.  Beat egg whites and fold in.  Bake at
350 in two layers.

ICING:

Mix together:

1 pound powdered sugar 2 oz. melted chocolate - baking 1/4 pound oleo
1 egg 1 tsp. vanilla 1 tsp. lemon juice
