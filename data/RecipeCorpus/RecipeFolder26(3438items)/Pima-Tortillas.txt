Title: Pima Tortillas
Categories: Native amer, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------FROM: ARIZONA COOKBOOK, GOLD---------------------
1 t  Salt
1/2 c  Lard (for lightest results
-use lard not shortening)
3/4 c  Water
4113 ea N. Longview Avenue,
-Phoenix, AZ 85014, $5
2 c  Flour
1 t  Baking powder
Mix together flour, salt, and baking powder. Cut in lard, add water
sparingly, until soft dough forms. Flour board and roll out dough. Cut
cakes 6 inches across and bake on lightly greased griddle. Turn
frequently to brown on both sides.
