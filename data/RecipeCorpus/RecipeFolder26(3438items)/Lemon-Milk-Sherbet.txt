Title: Lemon Milk Sherbet
Categories: Mom's, Ice cream
Yield: 1 servings
rind
1/4 t  Salt
3/4 c  Lemon juice
1 1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1 T  Grated lemon
Combine lemon juice, sugar, rind- add very slowly to milk, stirring
constantly till sugar dissolves. Add salt and more sugar if desired.
Freeze in 2-qt crank freezer until difficult to turn, using 8 parts
crushed ice to 1 part ice-cream salt. Makes about 1 1/2 qt. Note:
This sherbet may curdle in the making, but when done it's nice and
creamy.
