Title: Wednesday's Chili
Categories: Chili, Ground beef, Vegetable, Beans, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ds Tabasco
21 oz Red Kidney Beans
6 oz Tomato Paste
8 oz Tomato Sauce
2 tb Oil
1 1/2 lb Ground Round
10 oz French Onion Soup
1 tb Chili Powder
2 ts Ground Cumin
Blend soup and spices in blender then add to browned meat. Heat 20
min on low. Mash meat to rice size. Add kidney beans, tomato paste
and tomato sauce. Heat thoroughly, about 30 min.
