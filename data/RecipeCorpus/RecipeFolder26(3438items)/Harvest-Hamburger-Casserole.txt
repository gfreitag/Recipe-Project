Title: Harvest Hamburger Casserole
Categories: Ground beef, Casserole, Tomato, Potato, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
1/3 c  All-purpose Flour
1 pk Frozen Corn, thawed (10 Oz.)
1 pk Frozen Lima Beans, thawed
-(10 Oz.)
1 ea Green Pepper, cut into
-strips
1 1/2 c  Cheddar Cheese, shredded (6
-Oz.)
1 lb Lean Ground Beef, browned
-and drained
1 c  Onion, minced
1 cn Tomatoes with liquid, cut
-up (28 Oz.)
1 T  Worcestershire Sauce
1 t  Salt
2 c  Potatoes, sliced
In a mixing bowl, combine ground beef, onion, tomatoes with liquid,
Worcestershire sauce, and salt. Spoon into a greased three quart
casserole. Layer the potatoes, corn, lima beans, and green pepper on
top. Bake, covered at 375 degrees for 45 minutes. Sprinkle with
cheese and continue baking, uncovered, for 30 minutes. Yield: 8
Servings SOURCE:*Grace Hagen, Raggen, CO, Reminisce Magazine Sept/Oct
92 SHARED BY: Jim Bodle 9/92, 1/95
