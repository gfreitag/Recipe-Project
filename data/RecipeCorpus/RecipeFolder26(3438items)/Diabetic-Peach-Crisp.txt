Title: Diabetic Peach Crisp
Categories: Diabetic, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
2 t  Reduced calorie margarine
3 ea Tb Firmly packed brown
-sugar OR
9 t  Sweetener(equiv)
Preheat oven to 350 F.
4 ea Ripe peaches,peel+slice 1/4"
3 ea Tb Flour
2 1/4 oz Quick-cooking oats,uncooked
1 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Nutmeg
2 ea Tb Reduced calorie margarine
Toss peaches with 1 tablespoon of the flour and place in a 6x11"
baking pan that has been sprayed with a nonstick cooking spray. In a
small bowl, combine remaining ingredients. Mix well until mixture is
crumbly. Sprinkle evenly over peaches. Bake 30-35 minutes, until
crisp. serve warm or cold. From Bridget Benjamin
