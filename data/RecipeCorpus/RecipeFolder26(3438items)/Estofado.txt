Title: Estofado
Categories: Tex/mexican, Beef, Soup/stew, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------FROM: ARIZONA COOKBOOK------------------------
3 T  Wine vinegar
1 t  Salt
1/2 t  Pepper
12 t  Oregano
3 lg Carrots, cut up
3 lg Potatoes, cut up
1 1/2 lb Stew beef (lean)
1 ea Onion, chopped
1 ea Clove garlic, chopped
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1 c  Water
Put all ingredients (except for carrots and potatoes) into 2-qt.
covered pan. Cover and simmer over low heat for 1 1/2-2 hrs. Add the
vegetables and continue simmering until meat is tender. This stew
will have a thin liquid. Looks easily adaptable to crock pot or
pressure cooker.
