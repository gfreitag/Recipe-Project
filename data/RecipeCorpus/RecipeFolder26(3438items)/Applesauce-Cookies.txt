Title: Applesauce Cookies
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 4 dozen
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Cloves
1 c  Seedless raisins
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
3/4 c  Soft shortening
1 c  Brown sugar, packed
1    Egg
1/2 c  Applesauce
2 1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Soda
Heat oven to 375 (quick mod.).  Mix shortening, sugar and egg
thoroughly. Stir in applesauce.  Measure flour by dip-level-pour
method or by sifting. Blend dry ingredients; stir in.  Mix in raisins
and nuts. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased baking sheet.  Bake 10 to
12 min.

Makes 4 dozen cookies.
