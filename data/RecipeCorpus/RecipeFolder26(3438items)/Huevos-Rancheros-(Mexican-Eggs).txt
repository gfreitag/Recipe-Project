Title: Huevos Rancheros (Mexican Eggs)
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Breakfast, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-------------------FROM: ARIZONA COOKBOOK------------------------
4 ea Eggs
Oil and butter for frying
1 c  Green chili salsa
4 oz Longhorn cheese
4 ea Corn tortillas
Dip tortillas in heated oil and remove quickly. Set tortillas on
baking pan to keep warm. In a frying pan, panfry eggs in butter until
the whites are set but the yolks are still soft. Put a friend egg on
each tortilla. Heat salsa and spoon over each egg. Sprinkle grated
cheese on top. Slip baking pan under broiler until cheese melts.
(Variation: try adding heated refried beans on the torilla, before
topping with eggs, salsa and cheese.)
