Title: Surf City Tilefish with Pasta
Categories: Fish, Vegetable, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
Fresh pepper
2 tb Fresh basil; minced
8 oz Spinach fettucine; cooked
1/2 c  Tomato juice
1 lb Tilefish fillets; cut in
- squares
2    Garlic cloves; minced
Cut fillets in 1-in squares.  Simmer covered in tomato juice with
garlic, pepper, and basil, about 8 minutes.  Toss with green noodles
and serve hot. Submitted By NICK LA ROCCA   On   12-30-94
