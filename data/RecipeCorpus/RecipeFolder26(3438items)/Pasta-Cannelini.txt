Title: Pasta Cannelini
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Main dish, Tomato, Beans, Pasta
Yield: 4 servings
28 oz Can whole tomatoes, cut up
-undrained
15 oz Can chick peas, rinsed
1 ts Dried oregano leaves
2 c  Uncooked pasta nuggets
-(radiatore)
2 ts Olive oil or oil
1 c  Thinly sliced onions
1 cl Garlic, minced
Cook pasta to desired doneness; drain and keep warm. heat oil in large
nonstick skillet until hot. Add onions and garlic; cook and stir
about 6 minutes or until onion is tender. Add tomatoes, beans and
oregano; mix well. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer 5-10 minutes
to blend flavors. Serve over pasta. If desired, sprinkle with
shredded Parmesan cheese.

Per serving: 300 cal., 12g pro., 55g carb., 7g fiber, 4g fat, 0mg
chol., 450mg sod., 720mg pot., 2 1/2 starch, 1/2 lean meat, 2
vegetable, and 1/2 fat exchanges.
