Title: Shrimp Dip (Heinz)
Categories: Appetizer, Dip, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 cups
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 cn 4 1/2 oz tiny shrimp
8 oz Cream Cheese Softened
1/2 c  Seafood cocktail sauce
2 tb Plain yogurt
2 tb Minced green onion
Drain and rinse shrimp. Combine first 6 ingredients, stir in shrimp
and chill overnight. Serve with chips, crackers or veggies.
