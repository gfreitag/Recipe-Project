Title: Hot Tomato Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Tomato, Chilies
Yield: 3 servings
-minced
2 T  Chopped cilantro
Salt and pepper to taste
2 lg Tomatoes chopped coarse
2 sl Thin scallions
2 ea Jalapeno peppers seeded and
In medium bowl, combine tomatoes, scallions, peppers and cilantro.
Season with salt and pepper. Cover and chill.
