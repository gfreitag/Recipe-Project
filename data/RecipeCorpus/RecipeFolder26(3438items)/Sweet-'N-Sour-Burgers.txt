Title: Sweet 'N Sour Burgers
Categories: Main dish, Ground beef, Sthrn/livng, Burgers
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 lb Beef, ground
Oil, salad; hot
2 tb Sugar, brown; firmly packed
1 ts Mustard, prepared
1 tb Vinegar
ds Pepper
8 oz Tomato sauce; divided
1/3 c  Onion; finely chopped
1/3 c  Gingersnaps; crushed
1    Egg; slightly beaten
1/4 c  Raisins, seedless
3/4 ts Salt
Combine 1/4 cup tomato sauce, onion, gingersnaps, egg, raisins, and
salt, blending well.  Add meat, mixing well.  Shape mixture into 6
patties. Brown on both sides in a small amount of hot salad oil;
drain.

Combine remaining tomato sauce, brown sugar, mustard, vinegar, and
pepper. Spoon sauce over patties.  Cover and simmer 20 minutes,
basting patties occasionally with the sauce.
