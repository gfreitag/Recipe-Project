Title: Cinnamon Flop
Categories: Bread, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------*****TOPPING*****--------------------------
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Brown sugar
4 tb Butter; softened
MMMMM----------------------*****DOUGH*****---------------------------
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Butter; softened
1    Egg; well beaten
1 c  Milk
2 c  All-purpose flour;
Sifted
2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
Recipe by: The American Heritage Cookbook - ISBN 0-8281-04-03-4 Make
Topping First: Work brown sugar and butter together with your finger
tips until well mixed. Then work in cinnamon.

To Makw Dough: Sift flour, baking powder, and salt together and set
aside. Mix sugar and butter with your finger tips until butter is
well dispersed. Stir in beaten egg thoroughly, then add flour and
milk alternately, beginning and ending with flour. Pour into a
greased 8-inch square pan, sprinkle topping over surface, and bake in
a preheated 425Â°F oven for 30 to 35 mins. Served warm, it makes a
delicious breakfast bread.
