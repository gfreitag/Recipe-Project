Title: Hawaiian Ribs
Categories: Crockpot, Fruit, Pork
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Catsup
1 T  Soy sauce
1 cn (9 oz) crushed pineapple
3 lb Spareribs, trimmed of fat
3 T  Cornstarch
3 T  Brown sugar
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 c  Vinegar
Combine all ingredients, except ribs, in pan. Bring to a boil until
thick. Arrange sauce and spareribs in crock pot and cook 6 to 8 hours
on Low. From: Steve Herrick Source: Mom
