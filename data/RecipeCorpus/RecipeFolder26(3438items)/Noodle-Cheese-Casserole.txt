Title: Noodle-Cheese Casserole
Categories: Hungarian, Casserole, Pork, Cheese/eggs, European
Yield: 8 servings
-temperature
1 c  Cottage cheese
1/3 c  Onion; minced
Salt and pepper; to taste
1/4 lb Bacon; sliced
1 pk Noodles, wide (8 ozs);
-cooked and drained
1 1/4 c  Sour cream; at room
Recipe by: The Eastern European Cookbook-ISBN 0-486-23562-9

Cook bacon until crisp. Drain, reserving 3 tbs of fat; crumble. Set
aside. Mix reserved fat with cooked noodles, sour cream, cottage
cheese and onion. Season with salt and pepper. Spoon into a buttered
casserole. Sprinkle with crumbled bacon. Bake in a preheated moderate
oven (350Â°F.) for about 30 mins. Serves 8.

NOTES : This Hungarian baked dish, Turos Csusza, may be served as an
accompaniment to meats or poultry or enjoyed as a supper dish.

From:  Dan Klepach
