Title: Sherried Baked Fruit (Modify)
Categories: Fruit, Fatfree
Yield: 20 servings
MMMMM----------------NECESSITIES AND TEMPTATIONS---------------------
Page 301
MMMMM------------------VIA BETTE LELAND CGVH43B-----------------------
2    15-oz jars spiced apple ring
1/2 c  Butter (modify!)
4 tb Flour
1 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Sherry
2 cn 20-oz chunk pineapple
1 cn 29-oz sliced freestone
Peaches
1 cn 17-oz can apricots, sliced
1 cn 16-oz pitted Bing cherries
Or blueberries
Heat oven to 350. Drain fruit. Arrange in layers in a 3-qt. baking
dish. Melt butter. Add flour and stir until well blended. Add brown
sugar and sherry. Cook until thick. Pour over fruit. Bake 30 min.
Elegant for buffet dinner parties, large or small.
