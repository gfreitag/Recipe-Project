Title: Green Peppercorn Butter
Categories: Condiment, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
2 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 c  Butter; softened
1/4 c  Sour cream
1    Bouillon cube, crushed
3 tb Shallots; minced
3 tb Butter
3 tb Brandy
2 tb Chopped green peppercorns
