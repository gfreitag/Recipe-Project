Title: White Chocolate Chip Macadamia Nut Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains, Chocolate, Mom's
Yield: 3 dozen
1 t  Vanilla
2 1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Macadamia nuts, coarsely
-chopped
1 ea 12 oz   pack of white
-baking chips
1 c  Butter
3/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Brown sugar, packed
2 ea Eggs
1 t  Salt
1 t  Baking soda
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Cream butter and sugars together. Add eggs and continue beating. Add
salt, baking soda and vanilla and beat until blended. Add flour a
little at a time while beating. Stir in chips and nuts. Drop by
tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 10
minutes, or until light golden brown. Courtesy of: Joann Pierce
