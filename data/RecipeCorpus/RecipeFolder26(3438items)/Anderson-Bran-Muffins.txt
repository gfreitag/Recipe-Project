Title: Anderson Bran Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1 qt Buttermilk
5 c  Flour
1 t  Salt
5 t  Baking soda
Raisins
2 c  Bran (natural)
2 c  Boiling water
1 lb Margarine
4 ea Eggs
2 c  Brown sugar
4 c  All bran cereal
Soak the 2 cups natural bran in the boiling water in a bowl.  Set
aside to cool.  Cream the margarine, eggs, and brown sugar together.
Add the buttermilk and mix.  Add the rest of the dry ingredients. Add
the soaked bran and mix thoroughly. Bake in muffins tins, or papers,
in a 350F oven for about 15-20 minutes.  Makes about 5-1/2 dozen. (
this is another recipe I 'acquired' when I was supervisor for a group
home. One of the staff would make it during night shift so that we
always seemed to have a bag in the freezer. This wasn't as fancy as
the decadent ones, but they are just as nice) Origin: John Weel,
Staff (1987) Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
