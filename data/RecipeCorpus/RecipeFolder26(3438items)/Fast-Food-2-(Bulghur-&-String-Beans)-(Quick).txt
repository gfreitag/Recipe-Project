Title: Fast Food 2 (Bulghur & String Beans) (Quick)
Categories: Snack, Fatfree, Information, Beans
Yield: 1 servings
x  Frozen string beans
x  Spices
x  Bughur wheat
x  Water
In a sealable pint bowl, put bulghur wheat, expansion water, and
frozen string beans.  Sprinkle on some spices (dry onion, garlic,
pepper, etc.) Take the bowl to work, put it in your desk drawer, eat
it for lunch.
