Title: Caribbean Surprise
Categories: Fatfree, Beans, Tomato, Curries, Caribbean
Yield: 1 servings
Chunks (more or less)
1/2 t  Ginger
1    2 teaspoons (to your taste)
Curry powder
1    15oz  can black beans,
Drained
1    15oz  can tomatoes, drained
1 c  Frozen corn
1 1/2 c  Canned or fresh pineapple
Save some of the drained tomato or pineapple juice in case it gets too
thick during heating.  Heat it all up in a saucepan and serve over
rice or pasta.  You can also add chopped peppers, waterchestnuts,
whatever.
