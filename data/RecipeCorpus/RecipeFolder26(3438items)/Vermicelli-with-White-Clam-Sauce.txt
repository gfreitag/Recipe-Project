Title: Vermicelli with White Clam Sauce
Categories: Pasta, Seafood, Crab/shrimp
Yield: 6 servings
4 tb Olive oil
1 cl Garlic
1/4 c  Dry white wine
1 ts Salt
Freshly ground pepper
-to taste
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
(PASTA):
6 qt Water
6 ts Salt
1 1/4 lb Vermicelli
(SAUCE):
2 lb Fresh cherrystone clams
-or-
10 oz Canned baby clams
Prepare clams as in the preceding recipe.
Flavor the olive oil with the garlic over medium heat, discarding the
garlic when golden.  Add the cleaned and chopped clams, their juice
and the dry white wine.  Cook over medium high heat for 2 to 3
minutes, or until

the wine has evaporated.  Add the salt, pepper and chopped parsley,
and

simmer for 5 minutes.

Cook and drain the pasta thoroughly, put it on a serving platter and
add the clams in white sauce.  Toss gently, and serve.  Please, no
cheese! From _The Romagnolis' Table_; Margaret & G. Franco Romagnoli;
Little, Brown and Company Pub.
