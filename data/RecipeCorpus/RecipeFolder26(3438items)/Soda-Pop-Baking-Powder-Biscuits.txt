Title: Soda Pop Baking Powder Biscuits
Categories: Bread
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 c  Shortening
3/4 c  7-up (soda pop)
2 c  Sifted all purpose flour
4 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
Sift flour, baking soda and salt in bowl.  Cut in shortening until
mixture is coarse and crumbly.  Add 7-up and stir with a fork only
until moistened. Turn dough out onto lightly floured surface and
knead 8-10 times. Roll out to 3/4 inch thickness. let rest a few
minutes. Cut into shapes and arrange on ungreased baking sheet.  Bake
at 450F for 10-12 minutesor until golden brown. Makes 12 biscuits.
Origin: My Mom, Shirley Whittaker. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
