Title: Treacle Scones
Categories: Bread, Breakfast, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Allspice
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Shortening
1 tb Molasses
2/3 c  Soya milk
2 c  Wholewheat flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 pn Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ginger powder
Mix together the dry ingredients in a bowl.  Rub in the shortening.
Soften the molasses a little & add it with the soya milk to the dry
ingredients to make a fairly soft dough.  Turn out onto a floured
board & roll out to about 1/2" thick.  Cut into rounds.

Place scones onto a lightly oiled baking sheet & bake at 425F for 10
to 15 minutes.

Leah Leneman, "365 + 1 Vegan Recipes"
