Title: Drizzles For Eclairs
Categories: Pastry, Chocolate
Yield: 12 eclairs
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
2 t  Strong brewed coffee
CHOCOLATE DRIZZLE
1    Square Semisweet Chocolate
COFFEE DRIZZLE
CHOCOLATE DRIZZLE: Melt chocolate, drizzle over glazed eclairs.

COFFEE DRIZZLE: Blend sugar and coffee until smooth. Drizzle over
glazed eclairs.
