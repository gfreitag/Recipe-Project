Title: Fruit Smoothie #2
Categories: Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Orange juice
1 c  Crushed ice
Garnish: 1 maraschino cherry
6 oz Plain yogurt
4 oz Papaya (about 1/2 medium); p
2 oz Banana (about 1/2 medium); p
2 oz Maraschino cherries
Recipe by: Smeraldi's, The Biltmore Hotel, Los Angeles Mix all
ingredients except garnish and blend until smooth. Pour into 20- oz
glass and garnish.
