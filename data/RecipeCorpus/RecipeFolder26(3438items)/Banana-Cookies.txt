Title: Banana Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 t  Baking soda
1 t  Salt (optional)
1/2 t  Nutmeg
1 c  Mashed banana
1/2 c  Nuts
3/4 c  Shortening
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
1 t  Vanilla
3/4 t  Cinnamon
1 c  Rolled oats
Cream shortening, sugar, egg, and vanilla.  Add the dry ingredients
and blend thoroughly.  Add mashed bananas and nuts.  Spoon onto
cookie sheet and bake at 350F for 10-15 minutes, depending on size.
