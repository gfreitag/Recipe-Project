Title: Easy Italian supper
Categories: Ground beef, Cheese/eggs, Italian, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
Vegetables
3/4 c  Spaghetti sauce
1/3 c  Mozzarella cheese; shredded
3/4 lb Lean ground beef
1    10 oz. pkg green giant pasta
Garden herb seasoning frozen
Recipe by: Green Giant ad Preparation Time: 0:20
In a large skillet, brown ground beef; drain.
Add Pasta Accents and spaghetti sauce.  cover; simmer 8 to 10
minutes
Sprinkle with cheese.
