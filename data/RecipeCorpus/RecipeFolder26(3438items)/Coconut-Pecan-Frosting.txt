Title: Coconut Pecan Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 t  Salt
1 t  Vanilla
1 1/4 c  Flaked coconut
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1/2 c  Evaporated milok
2/3 c  Sugar
2 T  Butter
1 ea Egg
In a saucepan, combine evaporate milk, sugar, butter, egg and salt.
Cook over medium heat until thickened.  Remove from heat and cool
slightly. Add vanilla, coconut and pecans. Cool completely before
spreading over favorite cake. Origin: Sharon's recipe files Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
