Title: Sourdough Pizza Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Sourdough, Pizza
Yield: 1 pizza
2 tb Butter; melted
1/2 ts Dried basil; crumbled
1/2 ts Dried oregano; crumbled
3 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Pepporoni; finely minced
3 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
-C-Thompson18 [Cat]
--For DAK/Welbilt:
1/2 c  Milk; PLUS
2 tb Milk
1 c  Sourdough starter
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1    Egg; beaten
A very unique bread, guaranteed to garner many compliments:

Combine all ingredients in bread machine.

For Sanyo/Seiko machine, use following proportions: 6 Tb plus 2 tsp
milk 2/3 cup sourdough starter 1 Tb plus 1 tsp sugar 1 egg yolk,
beaten 1/2 tsp salt 1 Tb plus 1 tsp melted butter 1/4 tsp plus a
generous pinch of basil, crumbled 1/4 tsp plus a generous pinch of
oregano, crumbled 2 Tb grated Parmesan cheese 2 Tb plus 2 tsp finely
minced pepperoni 2 cups flour 1 tsp yeast
