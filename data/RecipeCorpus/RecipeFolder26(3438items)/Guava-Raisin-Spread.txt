Title: Guava-Raisin Spread
Categories: Condiment, Fruit, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 tb Grated orange peel
1 c  Guava jelly
1 c  Raisins
2/3 c  Water
1 ts Dry mustard powder
Boil raisins and water until water is absorbed, mixing well. Stir in
other ingredients. Re-heat, mixing well. Cool, then store in
refrigerator.
