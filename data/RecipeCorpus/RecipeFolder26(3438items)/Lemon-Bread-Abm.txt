Title: Lemon Bread Abm
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker, Fruit
Yield: 24 servings
3/4 c  Milk; scalded
1    Egg + 1 yolk; room temp.
1/4 ts Lemon extract
2 ts Dried or freshly grated
-lemon peel
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Flour, bread
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Butter; room temperature
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order. Set "baking control" at 10 o'clock.  Select "white bread" and
push Start.

In hot & humid weather, use 1/8 c less water.
