Title: Cinnamon-Raisin Bread (ABM)
Categories: Fatfree, Breadmaker, Bread, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Vital wheat gluten
2 1/2 t  Yeast
2 t  Cinnamon
1/2 c  Raisins
1 c  Water
2 T  Honey
2 c  Flour, whole-grain wheat
1 t  Salt
This recipe was adapted for the Toastmaster 1152U (1-lb.) machine.
If you are using a 1.5-lb. machine, just multiply all amounts by 1.5.
Put first 6 ingredients in machine.  Set for Specialty cycle (mix-in
alarm). When mix-in alarm sounds, add cinnamon and raisins.

Serving Ideas : Toast and slather with apple butter!  :9

NOTES : If you leave out the cinnamon and raisins, this is the
(excellent) McDougall honey-whole wheat bread.

Only 3.8% CFF!
