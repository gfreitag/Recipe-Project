Title: Frosted Cauliflower
Categories: Vegetable, Microwave, Side dish
Yield: 5 servings
3/4 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
-cheese
x  Paprika - to taste
1 md Cauliflower
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 t  Salt
1 1/2 t  Prepared mustard
Remove woody base of cauliflower with sharp parring knife.  Place
whole head (of cauliflower) in glass dish with 2 tablespoons of
water. Loosely cover with waxed paper and microwave on high for 6 to
7 minutes a pound, rotating a quarter turn half way through cooking
time. Remove cauliflower and let stand while preparing the
"frosting". In a 2 cup glass measure, combine mayonnaise, salt, and
mustard. Microwave on high for 1 minute. Spread on cauliflower and
sprinkle with grated cheese. Microwave on high for 1 minute or until
cheese melts. Sprinkle with paprika and serve. Submitted By JEFF
