Title: Homemade Corn Chips
Categories: Vegetable, Snack, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
1/8 t  Salt
3 T  Jalapeno cheddar cheese dip
-(ie; frito lay)
1 c  Whole kernel corn, drained
1 T  Butter
1/4 t  Onion salt
Puree all together in food processor or blender.  Spread out to 1/8
inch thickness on a lightly oiled cookie sheet.  Dry for 3-4 hours at
140F in the oven, or until the sheet can be peeled off.  Flip over
and cut into corn chip sized strips.  Continue to dry for about 3-4
more hours, or until desired crispness You can also add garlic
powder, and extra salt if desired.
