Title: Sausage Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Sausage, Breakfast
Yield: 3 servings
2/3 c  Milk (to 1 cup)
1/4 ts Pepper (to 1/2 tsp)
1 lb Bulk sausage, mild pork
2 tb All-purpose flour
Recipe by: Fannie Flagg's Original Whistle Stop Cafe Cookbook Cook
sausage in a large heavy skillet until browned, stirring to crumble.
Remove from skillet using a slotted spoon, and drain well. Reserve 2
Tbs drippings in skillet. Add flour to drippings, stirring until
smooth. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, for 1 min.
Gradually add 2/3 cup milk, stirring constantly until smooth and
thickened. Add remaining 1/3 cup milk, if you want gravy thinner.
Stir in pepper and sausage; cook until hot, stirring constantly.
Serve over split biscuits. YIELD: 3 to 4 servings.

NOTES:  This has a little kick to it, and it is great over biscuits
--this and a cup of coffee makes a meal.
