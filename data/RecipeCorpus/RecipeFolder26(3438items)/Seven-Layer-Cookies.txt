Title: Seven Layer Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 dozen
1 pk (6 oz) butterscotch chips;
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk;
-(borden's)
1 c  Pecans; chopped (very fine)
1/4 c  Margarine;
1 c  Graham cracker crumbs;
1 cn (7 oz) angel flake coconut;
1 pk (6 oz) chocolate chips;
Melt margarine in a 9" X 12" pan, and add above ingredients by layers
as listed. Bake 325 degrees oven about 30 minutes.  Cool in pan.  Cut
into small squares.  Yield: 3 dozen. Source: Mrs. W. Kenneth Blaylock
from "The Stuffed Cougar". Brought to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion
and Her Meal-Master.
