Title: Custard Filling For Eclairs
Categories: Pastry, Chocolate, Pudding
Yield: 3 cups
2 T  Butter
3 t  Vanilla
FOR CHOCOLATE FILLING:
3    Sq. unsweetened chocolate
2 1/2 c  Milk
5    Egg yolks
3/4 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Flour
2 T  Flour
Heat milk to scalding.  Beat yolks and sugar in a large bowl until
thick. Beat in flour until well mixed.  Greadually beat in scalded
milk; pour mixture back into saucepan.  Cook, stirring constantly
until thick and boiling.  Lower heat, and cook 2-3 minutes, stirring
constantly until quite thick.  Remove from heat.  Stir in butter and
vanilla. Place a piece of plastic wrap directly on the filling and
chill 2 hours. If too thick, stir in a teaspoon of milk.

FOR CHOCOLATE FILLING:  Add unsweetened chocolate to milk while
scalding, stirring often until melted.
