Title: Avocado Salsa Recipe
Categories: Fatfree, Vegetable, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 T  Chopped cilantro
1 t  Ground cumin seed
1/2 t  Salt
1 lg Ripe avacado, diced
1 ds Cayenne pepper to taste
1 t  Grated orange peel
1 md Orange, peeled & diced
2 t  Grated lemon peel
1 T  Lemon juice
2 T  Minced onion
Combine all except avacado.  Gently stir avacado into salsa and add
cayenne to taste.  Serve w chips or NAUGHTY (c*****n)!!
