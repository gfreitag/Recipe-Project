Title: Basic Marinade
Categories: Marinade, Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 t  Onion salt
1/4 t  Garlic powder
Freshly ground black pepper
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
2 T  Vegetableoil
2 T  Soy sauce
2 T  Ketchup
Combine and use with beef, pork, or chicken. Marinate for 3-4 hours,
preferable in fridge. Origin: Tupperware instructions sheet, came with
marinating container. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
