Title: Creole Sauce 2
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Cajun/creol, Pork, Clam/oyster, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Parsley, finely chopped
1 T  Freeze-dried chives
1 ea Bay leaf
1 t  Brown sugar
1/2 t  Black pepper
1 t  Tabasco
1 t  Lemon juice
Ds Worcestershire sauce
1/4 lb Bacon, chopped
1 md Onion, chopped
3 ea Celery, rubs, chopped
1 ea Clove garlic, crushed
1 ea Bell pepper, chopped
8 oz Clam juice
1 c  Chicken broth
16 lb Tomato puree
Fry bacon in a heavy pot. Add onion, garlic, celery and bell pepper
and cook until onion is translucent. Add remaining ingredients, bring
to a boil, lower heat and simmer uncovered, stirring occasionally,
until sauce is thickened, 30 to 45 minutes.
