Title: Spicy Prunes
Categories: Mom's, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 cup
1 t  Ground cloves
1 t  Cinnamon
1 lb Large prunes
-water
1 c  Vinegar
Week or so ahead: Rinse prunes- cover with cold water- boil 10 to 12
min.; drain. Combine rest of ingredients; boil 1 min. Add prunes,
bring to boil. Cool; refrigerate. Nice with poultry or roasts, or as
garnish for fruit salads.
