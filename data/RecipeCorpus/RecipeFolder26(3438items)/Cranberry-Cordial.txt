Title: Cranberry Cordial
Categories: Beverage, Fruit, Alcohol
Yield: 3 pints
6 c  Sugar
1 l  Light or amber rum
8 c  Coarsley chooped fresh
-cranberries
Place the chopped cranberries in a gallon jar with a tight fitting
cover. Add the sugar and the rum; close tightly and shake gently to
blend. Store in a cool dark place for 6 weeks, shaking or stirring
every day. Strain into decorative bottles and seal with corks.
