Title: Barley With Vegetables (Cooked In A Rice Cooker)
Categories: Fatfree, Rice, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Vegan worstershire sauce
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1 t  Dried parsley
x  Pepper
1 c  Barley
2 c  Broth of your choice
1 lg Onion, chopped
2    Ripe tomatoes, diced
8 oz Can mushrooms, drained
Put all in rice cooker and stir.  Put on cover and turn to COOK.
Will be ready in about 45 minutes.  Can probably do this on the stove
as well. Just bring to a boil, turn down to low and simmer for 45
minutes.
