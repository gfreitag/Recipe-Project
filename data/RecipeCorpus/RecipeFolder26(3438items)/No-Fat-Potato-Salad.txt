Title: No Fat Potato Salad
Categories: Fatfree, Potato, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Vinegar - red or white
1 ct Egg beaters (equivalent to 4
Eggs)
Whip above ingredients and heat on stove until thick (scorches
easily).

Cook potatoes, let cool.  Add onion, celery, green pepper, or any
other vegetables and seasonings you want (no fat, of course).

Mix all together.
