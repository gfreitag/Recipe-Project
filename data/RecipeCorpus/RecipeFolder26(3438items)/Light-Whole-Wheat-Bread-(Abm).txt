Title: Light Whole Wheat Bread (Abm)
Categories: Fatfree, Breadmaker, Bread
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 T  Sugar
1 1/2 t  Salt
2 t  Active Dry Yeast
1 1/8 c  Water
1 c  Whole Wheat Flour
2 c  Unbleached White Flour
1 1/2 T  Non-fat Dry Milk (optional)
This is my 'basic' recipe. It never goes wrong, raises well, and my
SO who is not very excited about 100% WW loves it.

Use Whole Wheat mode. Timer ok.
