Title: Turkey Mac Skillet
Categories: Mom's, Low-cal/fat, Main dish, Turkey, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 1 c servng
1 c  Thinly sliced celery
1 md Tomato, chopped
1 lb Ground turkey
2 ts Oil
1 c  (4 oz) uncooked elbow macs
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
2 cn (8 oz ea) tomato sauce
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Thick and chunky salsa
Brown ground turkey in oil in large skillet; drain. Stir in all
remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low; cover and
simmer 8-10 minutes or until macaroni is of desired doneness.

Makes 6 1-cup servings. Dietary exchanges: 1 starch, 1 1/2 lean meat,
1 vegetable Calories per serving: 180 Fat - 3 gm Source: Pillsbury
