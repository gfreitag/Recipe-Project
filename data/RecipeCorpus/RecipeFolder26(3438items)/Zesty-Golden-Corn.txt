Title: Zesty Golden Corn
Categories: Vegetable, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Whole kernel corn, drained
1/4 t  Pepper
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1/3 c  Butter
1/4 c  Dijon mustard
1 ea Med. onion, cut in chunks
1/2 lb Small mushroom, halved
Melt butter in 10-inch skillet.  Blend in mustard.  Add onion and
mushrooms.  Cook, stirring constantly, over high heat until
tender-crisp, about 3 minutes.  Stir in corn and pepper; heat
through. Stir in parsley.
