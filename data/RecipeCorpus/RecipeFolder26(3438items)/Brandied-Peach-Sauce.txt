Title: Brandied Peach Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Brandy
1/2 c  Peach brandy
1/4 t  Nutmeg
18 oz Peach preserves
3/4 c  Light brown sugar, firmly
-packed
Combine ingredients in a bowl, blending well.  Makes about 3 cups.
