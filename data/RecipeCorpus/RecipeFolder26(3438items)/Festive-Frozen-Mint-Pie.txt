Title: Festive Frozen Mint Pie
Categories: Dessert, Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
-whipped
Sweetened condenced milk
1 1/2 ts Peppermint extract
Topping
1    Nabisco Oreo Pie Crust
1 cn Eagle Brand (reg or light)
10 dr Green food coloring
2 c  Whipping cream, or
In large mixer bowl, combine Eagle Brand, peppermint extract, and, if
desired, food coloring.  Fold in whipped cream.  Turn into crust;
cover. Freeze 6 hours or until firm.  Garnish as desired.

Prep time 8 minutes
