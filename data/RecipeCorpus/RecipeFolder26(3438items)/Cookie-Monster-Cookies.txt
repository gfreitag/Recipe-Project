Title: Cookie Monster Cookies
Categories: Nuts/grains, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Vegetable Oil
3 T  Water
1 ea Egg
1 1/2 c  Oatmeal
1 c  Dark brown sugar.
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Baking soda
1 t  Cinnamon
1/2 c  Raisins
Sift flour salt, baking soda, and cinnamon into bowl. Mix in veg.
oil, 3 tbsp. water, and egg. Mix in 1/2 C. raisins, 1-1/2 C. oatmeal
and 1 C. brown sugar. Drop small teaspoons onto greased cookie sheet.
Bake 10 minutes or more in 350 oven. Shared By: Pat Stockett
