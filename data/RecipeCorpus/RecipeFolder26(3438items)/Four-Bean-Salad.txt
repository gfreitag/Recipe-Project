Title: Four Bean Salad
Categories: Fatfree, Beans, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
2    Scallions, chopped thinly
1 c  Cider vinegar
1 c  Honey (or corn syrup)
1 T  Olive oil (optional - it
Cuts some of the acidity)
1 cn Garbanzon beans
1 cn Kidney beans
1 cn Green beans
1 cn Yellow/wax beans
1 md Onion, sliced very thin
Drain and rinse all beans.  Combine with onions and scallions. Blend
vinegar, oil, and honey thoroughly and pour over vegetables.
Refrigerate at least four hours.
