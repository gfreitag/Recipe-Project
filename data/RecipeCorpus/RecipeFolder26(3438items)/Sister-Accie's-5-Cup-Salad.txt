Title: Sister Accie's 5 Cup Salad
Categories: Salad, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM----------------KEYWORDS: MARSHAMLLOW, MINE---------------------
1 c  Pineapple chunks, drained
1 c  Miniature marshmallows,
-colored
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Coconut (long shred)
1 cn Mandarin oranges, drained
Mix all together and chill for at least 4 hours. Origin: My Aunt,
Dorothy (Dot) Hughes. (1974) Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
