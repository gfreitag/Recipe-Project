Title: No-Fat Italian Dressing (Lf)
Categories: Salad, Dressing, Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
1    Garlic clove; cut in half
1/2 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Basil
1/8 ts Thyme
1/8 ts Rosemary
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Apple juice; unsweetened
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Onion powder
Combine all ingredients.  Chill for an hour or two at least to allow
herbs to blend.  Remove garlic clove pieces before serving. Makes 12
tablespoons. :
