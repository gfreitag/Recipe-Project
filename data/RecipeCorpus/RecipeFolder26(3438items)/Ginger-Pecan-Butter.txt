Title: Ginger-Pecan Butter
Categories: Condiment, Herbs/spice, Nuts/grains, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Brown sugar
1/2 ts Allspice
3/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Pecans; finely ground
2 tb Ground crystallized ginger
Mix together and store in refrigerator.
