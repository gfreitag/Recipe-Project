Title: Sharon's Egg Rolls
Categories: Appetizer, Asian, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Chicken, cooked
1 c  Mushrooms
1 c  Bean sprouts
Salt and pepper to taste
Egg roll wrappers
1 c  Onions
2 c  Celery
1 c  Bok choy
Shred the onion, celery and bok choy.  Slice thinly the chicken and
mushrooms.  Mix all together and spoon into wrappers.  Fold over and
seal with egg whites.  Press edges with fork.  Deep fry in oil.
Serve hot with plum sauce or hot mustard as condiment.  Makes about
18-20. Origin: Sharon Stevens. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
