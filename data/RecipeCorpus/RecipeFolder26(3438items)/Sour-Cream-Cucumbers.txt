Title: Sour Cream Cucumbers
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
3 T  Cider vinegar
2 T  Sugar
Salt and pepper to taste
2 lg Cucumbers, peeled and sliced
1 lg Onion, sliced into rings
3/4 c  Sour cream
In a bowl, combine the cucumber and onion. Combine remaining
ingredients and pour over cucumbers. Mix well. Chill. Serves 6-8.
