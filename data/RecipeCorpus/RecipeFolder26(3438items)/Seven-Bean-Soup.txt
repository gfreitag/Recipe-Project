Title: Seven Bean Soup
Categories: Fatfree, Beans, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
3 t  Chopped green chili
3    Cloves garlic, chopped
1    28 oz & 1 16 oz can crushed
Tomatoes
1 1/2 t  Chili powder
6 T  Ketchup
6    Stalks celery, chopped
4 c  Dried Beans (as many types
As you can find, but try to
Include adzuki and anasazi
1 t  Fresh grated ginger
2    To 3 large onions, chopped
1 t  Lemon pepper
3 T  B-B-Q sauce
Wash, pick over, and soak beans in water to cover by 2 inches,
overnight. In the morning, drain & rinse the beans. Return to pot
with 18 cups cold water. Add ginger, and bring to a full boil. Reduce
to active simmer & Cook until tender, 1 1/2 to 2 hours. Add remaining
ingredients. Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer 3 hours more.
Stir and add water as needed. If you can wait until it's completely
done, you'll have plenty to freeze, and it freezes well.
