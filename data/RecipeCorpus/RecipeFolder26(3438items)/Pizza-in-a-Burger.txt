Title: Pizza-in-a-Burger
Categories: Main dish, Burgers, Sthrn/livng, Pizza
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 ts Pepepr
6 oz Tomato paste
8 sl Mozzarella cheese; about
-the size of patties
8 sl Tomato
8 sl Bread, French; toasted
1 1/2 lb Beef, ground
1/3 c  Cheese, Parmesan; grated
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
1/4 c  Olives, ripe; chopped
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Salt
Combine ground beef, Parmesan cheese, onion, olives, oregano, salt,
pepper, and tomato paste.  Shape mixture into 8 oval patties.  Broil
on one side 3 to 6 minutes.  Turn patties over; top each with cheese
and tomato. Broil 3 to 6 minute, depending on desired degree of
doneness. Serve each pattie on a slice of bread.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in 1977. Typos by Nancy
