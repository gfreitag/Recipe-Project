Title: English Wassail
Categories: Beverage, Holiday, British, Apples
Yield: 20 cups
2 T  Honey
1 t  Lemon rind
2 1/2 c  Pinapple juice, unsweetened
Orange slices
6 c  Apple cider
1    Cinnamon stick
1/4 t  Nutmeg
2 T  Lemon juice
Heat cider and cinnamon stick in a large pan. Bring to a boil and
simmer covered for 5 minutes. Add remaining ingredients and simmer
uncovered for 5 minutes. Keep warm over low heat.
