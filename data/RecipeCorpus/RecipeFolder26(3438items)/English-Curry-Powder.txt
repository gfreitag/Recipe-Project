Title: English Curry Powder
Categories: Herbs/spice, British, Curries
Yield: 4 cups
1 ts Dried powdered curry leaves
1 ts Asafoetida
1 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Red chile powder
-such as New Mexican
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Black pepper
-freshly ground
1 1/4 c  Coriander seeds
2/3 c  Cumin seeds
1/3 c  Fenugreek seeds
1/2 c  Besan (gram flour)
1/2 c  Garlic powder
1/3 c  Paprika
1/3 c  Turmeric
1/3 c  Garam masala
In a dry skillet, toast the coriander, cumin and fenugreek seeds, then
grind them together in a spice mill. combine with remaining
ingredients and mix well.

This powder improves as it becomes better blended, the longer it is
stored, It is best in about a month, but don't store it longer than
18 months.
