Title: Bacon Pancakes
Categories: Bread, Sourdough, Pork, Pancakes
Yield: 10 servings
2 tb Oil;bacon fat
2 tb Sugar
Flour;as needed
1/2 ts Baking soda
-Diana Lewis-VGWN37A
2 c  Starter
1    Egg;beaten
1/2 c  Bacon;fried and crumbled
add egg, bacon, oil and sugar to the starter and mix briefly. add
flour to make desired thickness and mix till lump free. just before
cooking dissolve baking soda into 1 Tablespoon of water mix gently
with batter then cook in a (400)hot griddle make 2 or 3 inch rounds
cook for 2 to 4 minutes turn and cook 2 more minutes. Converted by
MMCONV vers. 1.00
