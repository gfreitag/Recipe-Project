Title: Hot & Sweet Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Tomato, Chilies
Yield: 1 servings
-and chopped fine
1/4 c  Marukan Gourmet Seasoned
-rice vinegar
1 t  Mexican oregano, crushed
1 T  Ancho chile powder (plain)
1 T  New mexican chile powder
-(plain)
Olive oil
1 ea 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
1 ea 4 1/2 oz can tomato sauce
1 lg Onion
3 ea Cloves garlic
4 ea Jalapeno Peppers, seeded
-and chopped fine
2 ea Serrano Peppers, seeded and
-chopped fine
3 ea Habanero peppers, seeded
Saute onion, garlic, peppers in olive oil until onions are
translucent. Add liquid ingredients  and chile powder.  Bring to a
boil, and then simmer 10 minutes or until desired consistency is
reached. Allow flavors to blend for 24 hours before use. Yield:
Approximately 40 oz Source: Dennis Kantor/Rod Riplinger
